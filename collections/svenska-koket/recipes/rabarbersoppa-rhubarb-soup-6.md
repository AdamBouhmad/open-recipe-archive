## Ingredients

- 1 liter sliced fresh or 1 liter canned rhubarb
- 2 liters water
- 1.5-2 cups crushed sugar
- 0.33 lemon (optional)
- 1.25 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. If using fresh rhubarb, rinse it well and cut it into pieces.
2. Heat the water, add rhubarb and sugar.
3. When the soup comes to a boil, skim it and let it cook until the rhubarb is soft.
4. Mix the potato flour with a little cold water and use it to thicken the soup, which should then be given a good boil and tasted.
5. Serve with whipped cream if desired.
