## Ingredients

- 425 gr. butter
- 170 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 600 gr. wheat flour
- 2 tsk. ground cinnamon
- 1 kkp pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with sugar until white.
2. Add the egg, and finally work in the flour.
3. Roll out the dough into small wreaths, dip these in a mixture of cinnamon and pearl sugar and bake in moderate oven heat.
