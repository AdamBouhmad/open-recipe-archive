## Ingredients

- 2 large hens about 450 gr. scraped meat
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 2 hg. bread panade
- 2 hg. butter
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pluck the hens, clean out the insides, rinse very well, dry with a cloth and skin them.
2. Cut all the meat loose from the carcasses and scrape it thoroughly free from sinews and membranes.
3. Grind the meat thereafter 6 times through a meat grinder and pound it together with the spices using a wooden pestle in a stone mortar until the forcemeat becomes fine and smooth.
4. Remove it and pound the panade (see no. 626) thereafter with the butter to an even paste, add the forcemeat and pound the mixture very well.
5. Place it in a bowl, add the beaten eggs together with the yolks under continuous vigorous stirring.
6. Pass the forcemeat through a hair sieve and stir it again for about 1/2 hour.
7. Test it thereafter by cooking a small sample.
