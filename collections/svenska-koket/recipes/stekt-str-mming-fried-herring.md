## Ingredients

- 30 herring
- 1½ tablespoons salt
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 80 grams butter (for frying)
- 1½ tablespoons butter (30 grams) for sauce
- ½ tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, remove the backbone, scrape away the dark membrane carefully and rinse very well, then wrap in a fish cloth.
2. Rub with salt, dip in beaten egg and turn in a mixture of flour and crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Brown the butter, place herring in it, fry 7 minutes on each side and arrange on a warm serving dish.
4. For the sauce, brown the butter and parsley lightly, season with lemon juice and pour the sauce over the herring.
5. Serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
