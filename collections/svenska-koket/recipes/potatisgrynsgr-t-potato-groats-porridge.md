## Ingredients

- 2/3 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters milk
- 1 cup potato groats
- 1 tablespoon wheat flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a pot, pour in the milk and bring to a boil, shake in the groats, and let the porridge simmer under stirring for 1/2 hour, or until the groats are clear.
2. Finish the porridge with the flour stirred into a little cold milk, bring to a good boil, taste and serve.
