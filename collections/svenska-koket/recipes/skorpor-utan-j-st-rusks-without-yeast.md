## Ingredients

- 5 hg. prime wheat flour
- 2 tsp. baking powder
- 2 eggs
- 90 g. sugar
- 125 g. butter
- 6 crushed cardamom seeds
- 1 1/2 dcl. cold milk (scant)

## Directions

1. Sift the flour with the baking powder in a bowl. Beat the eggs well with the sugar and butter and add to the flour together with the cardamom and milk, then work the dough quickly with a spoon. Turn out onto a floured work surface and roll into small buns the size of a walnut, which are at once baked in a warm oven. Cut them while they are hot and dry in a not too warm oven, until they have a beautiful color and are soft.
