## Ingredients

- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters milk
- 0.5 cup semolina grains
- 1 piece cinnamon
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a pot, pour in milk and bring to boil, and add cinnamon.
2. Shake in the grains and let the gruel simmer gently for 0.5 hour with stirring.
3. Season and serve.
