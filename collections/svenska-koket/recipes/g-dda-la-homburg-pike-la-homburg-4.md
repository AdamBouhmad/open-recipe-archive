## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1.5 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 2 tablespoons clarified butter (40 g)
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g) for frying
- 1 ball crayfish butter (10 g)
- 3 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Fillet the pike. Rub the fillets with salt and let them rest for 1 hour.
2. Dry the fillets well with a fish cloth.
3. Brush both sides of the fillets with the clarified butter mixed with the beaten egg yolk.
4. Sprinkle the fillets with crushed breadcrumbs and grated cheese.
5. Butter a baking pan with part of the cold butter, making it large enough for the fillets. Place the fillets skin-side up and distribute the remaining butter in small flakes over them.
6. Mix the crayfish butter with the thick cream and pour this sauce over the fish.
7. Bake in a hot oven for approximately 20 minutes, basting often with the sauce.
8. Place on a warm platter. If desired, whip the sauce in the pan with 2 dcl cream and pour over the fish.
9. Garnish with parsley and serve with mashed potatoes.
10. The dish can also be served with mushroom sauce, in which case do not add cream to the pan sauce.
