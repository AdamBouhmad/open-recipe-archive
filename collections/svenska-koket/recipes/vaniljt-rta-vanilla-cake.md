## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 7 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 5 tablespoons potato flour
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 1/2 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy.
2. Crush the vanilla and add it.
3. Stir in the sifted potato flour at the same time as the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Immediately pour the batter into a well-buttered and rusk-sprinkled pan or mold and bake in moderate oven heat.
5. When the cake has cooled, cut it into two layers, and between them place vanilla cream or applesauce.
6. Glaze the cake and garnish with fruit and crystallized sugar.
