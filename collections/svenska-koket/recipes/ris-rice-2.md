## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups carolina rice grains
- 3 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains in lukewarm water, place in boiling salted water and let them cook until soft, or about 30 minutes.
2. Drain them in a colander and place aside from the fire to dry.
3. Occasionally stir the grains with a fork so they do not stick together.
