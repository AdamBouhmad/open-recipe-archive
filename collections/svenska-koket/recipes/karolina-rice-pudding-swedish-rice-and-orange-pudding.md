## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 dcl. karolina rice grains (125 gr.)
- 1 1/2 lit. water
- 4 oranges
- 1 1/2 dcl. sugar (128 gr.)
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- 5–6 leaves gelatin
- 3 tbsp. water
- 2 oranges in wedges for garnish

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, scald them in hot water, place in boiling water and let them cook until soft, about 20 minutes.
2. Strain the rice and rinse with cold water, letting it drain well.
3. Peel the oranges, remove the white pith carefully, then cut them into small cubes, remove the pits and mix with the sugar.
4. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
5. Rinse the gelatin with cold water, cut into pieces and dissolve in 3 tbsp. hot water.
6. Mix the rice grains with the orange cubes and cream, and lastly add the gelatin.
7. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed mold dusted with sugar and let it stand on ice about 3 hours, or until set.
8. Loosen the pudding around the edge with a pointed knife, unmold onto a flat glass dish with parchment paper, garnish with orange wedges and serve with orange sauce.
