## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg pike
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 2-3 chopped pickled onions (optional)
- 1 part boiling water
- 1/2 lemon
- 5 tablespoons butter (100 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2 parts cream
- 1/2-1 deciliter good white wine
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 g)

## Directions

1. When the pike is slaughtered, sprinkle it with salt and let it rest a while.
2. Skin it as you would an eel, trim the fins and carefully remove the pike, reserving the roe and liver.
3. Rinse it very thoroughly, dry it, rub it with salt and let it rest 1 hour, then dry it again and cut it into 5 cm wide pieces across the body.
4. Pour the juice of 1/2 lemon over these pieces.
5. Line a saucepan with half the butter, lay in the fish pieces close together, sprinkle flour over them, add the onion if desired, and place the remaining butter in flakes on top.
6. Add the water and cream carefully and let the fish cook slowly with a tightly closed lid 15-20 minutes.
7. Place it on a warm serving plate, whisk up the sauce, season with wine and sugar and pour over the fish.
8. Garnish the dish with the roe and liver, which have been rinsed, cooked in salted water and cut into pieces, as well as fluted lemon slices.
