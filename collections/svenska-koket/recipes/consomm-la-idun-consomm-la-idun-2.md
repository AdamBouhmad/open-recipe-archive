## Ingredients

- 40 gr tapioca pearls
- 1 1/2 cup water
- 2 1/2 liters clear consommé
- salt
- cayenne
- 1 hg cured, cooked ox tongue
- 1 can truffles (1/8 liter)
- 1 can fine mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 30 gr butter

## Directions

1. Let the tapioca pearls cook in water until they are clear, then strain them through muslin, and add the cooking liquid to the consommé.
2. Cut the tongue and truffles into fine strips and the mushrooms into thin slices.
3. Sauté the mushrooms in 30 gr butter until the butter is clear, then let it drain on a sieve.
4. Add the garnish to the consommé.
