## Ingredients

- Dough: 35 gr. butter
- 425 gr. sweet almond, 4 bitter almonds
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 425 gr. sugar
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 gr.)
- For filling: 2 liters apples
- 2 dcl. sugar (170 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. water
- For the form: 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The butter is melted, allowed to cool and stirred until it is white and fluffy.
2. The almonds are blanched, dried in the oven, ground through a mill and pounded in a stone mortar to a fine mass.
3. Egg yolks, egg whites and sugar are whipped for 1/2 hour with a steel whisk.
4. The egg mixture is then mixed with the butter and almonds, and finally the flour is added.
5. The apples should be cooked the day before. They are rinsed, dried, peeled, cut into fine wedges, freed from the cores and cooked with water and sugar until they fall apart, then they are strained and allowed to cool.
