## Ingredients

- 1 kg cooked, boneless salmon (leftovers)
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1 celery root
- 2 cooked beets
- 1 cauliflower head
- 1/2 can green beans
- 1/2 can fine peas
- 1 salad head
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 6 tbsp flour (60 g)
- 1/2 tsp English mustard
- 5 dcl water
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 tbsp fine salad oil
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 tbsp vinegar
- 2 dcl thick cream
- 3 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar, white pepper
- 1 small white cabbage head for garnish, parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon into slices and remove skin and bones.
2. Prepare and cook the vegetables as in no. 175 and let them drain well.
3. Prepare the mayonnaise as in no. 175.
4. Take the salmon slices with a fork and dip them in the mayonnaise and arrange them on a round platter in a pyramid.
5. Mix the vegetables and the rinsed salad cut into strips carefully with the rest of the mayonnaise.
6. Place the salad in small shells, cut from raw white cabbage leaves.
7. Arrange the shells in a tight ring around the salmon and between them garnish with beautiful curly parsley.
8. Serve as an intermediate course. In the same way leftovers of cooked pike-perch, pike and whitefish can be arranged.
