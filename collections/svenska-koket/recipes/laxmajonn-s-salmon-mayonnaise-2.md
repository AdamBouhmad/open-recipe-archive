## Ingredients

- 6 hg. cooked, boneless salmon (leftovers)
- 2 salad heads
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 1/4 tsp. paprika
- 1 tbsp. table oil
- 1 tbsp. vinegar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tsp. salt, cayenne
- 1/4 tsp. French mustard
- 1 1/2 dcl. fine table oil
- 1 tbsp. tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 tbsp. lemon juice
- 1 tbsp. boiling water
- 3 small salad heads
- 2 hard-boiled eggs, in wedges
- 2 tbsp. capers
- 4 pitted canned olives, in thin slices
- 6 radishes, in slices

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon into thin slices; remove skin and bones.
2. Clean the salad, rinse quickly and lay on linen so that the water drains well.
3. Cut it across, place it in a bowl and mix with the seasonings, oil and vinegar.
4. Place the salad on a round dish, preferably silver, and arrange the salmon slices in a pyramid on top.
5. Prepare the mayonnaise as in recipe 10, but omit the gelatin; when it is properly thick, pour it over the salmon.
6. Garnish the dish with the inner parts of small salad heads, egg wedges, capers, and slices of olives and radishes.
7. Serve as a breakfast or side dish.
