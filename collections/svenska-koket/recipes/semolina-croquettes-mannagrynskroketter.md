## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 deciliters semolina (114 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 7 deciliters milk
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 1-2 teaspoons grated cheese
- 1 tablespoon melted butter (20 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks
- grated cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the semolina in a sieve, rinse with cold water and drain. Butter a pot with 1/2 tablespoon of the butter, add the milk and bring to a boil. Whisk in the semolina, then stir the porridge with a spoon until it thickens. Move it to lower heat and let it cook slowly for about 20 minutes, stirring often.
2. Transfer the porridge, mix immediately with the rest of the butter and let cool. Then add the beaten eggs, seasonings and grated cheese.
3. Butter a flat dish, spread the mass on it about 2 cm. thick and let it stand 2-3 hours on ice to cool. Using a round measure, cut out small round cakes, place on a buttered sheet, brush with butter, sprinkle with a mixture of crushed rusks and grated cheese and bake a nice golden-brown in a warm oven. Serve with roasted meat, e.g. venison.
