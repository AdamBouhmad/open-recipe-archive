## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 hg. salt lax
- 1 lit. skummjölk
- 1 lit. kokt, kall potatis
- 3 ägg
- 2 tsk. mjöl (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. gräddmjölk
- 1/4 tsk. vitpeppar
- 1 msk. finhackad persilja
- 1/2 msk. smör (10 gr.) till formen
- 2 msk. stötta skorpor till formen

## Directions

1. Rinse the salmon, cut it into slices and let it lie in the skim milk for about 3 hours, then remove and wrap in a cloth or white paper.
2. Peel the potatoes, cut them into slices and layer alternately with the salmon in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pudding mold.
3. Whisk the eggs with the flour, milk, and pepper and pour the mixture into the mold.
4. Sprinkle the pudding with parsley and bake in the oven for about 40 minutes.
5. Serve with melted butter or currant sauce.
