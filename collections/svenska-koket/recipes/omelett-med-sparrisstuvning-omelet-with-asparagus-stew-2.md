## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 cups milk
- 1 teaspoon flour
- salt
- sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter for the pan
- 1 tablespoon butter for asparagus stew
- 2 tablespoons flour for asparagus stew
- 2 cups sour milk
- 1 can asparagus for omelets (7 deciliter)
- sugar
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Whisk all ingredients for the omelet well together, pour the batter into a buttered omelet pan and bake light brown in ordinary oven heat.
2. Immediately turn the omelet out, place the stew on half the omelet, and fold the other half over.
3. For the stew, fry butter and flour together, add the asparagus liquid and cream with frequent stirring, and let the sauce boil 10 minutes.
4. Add the asparagus and season the stew to taste.
