## Ingredients

- 1 liter water
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 grams)
- 2 deciliters French wine
- 1.5 deciliters sherry
- 4 egg yolks
- 150 grams granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Let the water boil for 5 minutes with the lemon peel.
2. Thicken it with the potato flour, mixed in 1/2 tablespoon cold water, then add the wine and let the soup boil for 2 minutes.
3. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar in a soup bowl until the mixture becomes white and frothy, then pour in the hot soup while whisking vigorously.
4. Serve immediately with biscuits, macaroons, or rusks.
