## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. water
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 30 gr. butter
- 75 gr. sifted flour
- 1 msk. butter for the dish (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Boil the water with salt and butter and whisk in the flour over a full boil. Stir the mixture with a ladle over a brisk fire until it releases from the pan and the ladle, then pour it onto a buttered dish and brush the top with a little butter so that it does not dry.
