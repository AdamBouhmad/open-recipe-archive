## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. sugar (170 gr.)
- 1 dcl. white wine
- 2 1/2 dcl. water
- 1 liter good pears
- 50 gr. butter
- 50 gr. flour
- 4 dcl. boiling milk
- 1/2 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Boil a clear syrup from the sugar, wine and water.
2. Rinse the pears, dry them, peel them, split them lengthwise, remove the cores, place them in the sugar syrup and let them cook until soft.
3. Remove them and place them in a buttered pie pan.
4. Reduce the syrup until it is thick and pour it over the pears.
5. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the mixture cook for 5 minutes.
6. Add the lemon zest and sugar, and let the mixture cool, then stir in the egg yolks one by one, and finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
7. Pour the cream immediately over the fruit and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour, or until the mass feels baked when tested with a whisk.
8. Serve somewhat cooled with or without whipped cream.
