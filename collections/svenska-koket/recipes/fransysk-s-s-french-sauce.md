## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 75 gr. caster sugar
- 150 gr. fine butter
- 3-4 tablespoons fine cognac
- a little ground nutmeg

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes.
2. Wash the butter and work out the water well, then stir until it is white and foamy, then blend in the egg mixture little by little, and finally add the cognac teaspoonwise, and if desired, a little finely ground nutmeg with continued even stirring.
3. Serve the sauce immediately with hot fruit dishes.
