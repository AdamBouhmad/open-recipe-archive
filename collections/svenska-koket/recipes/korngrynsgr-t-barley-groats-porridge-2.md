## Ingredients

- 2 cups crushed grains
- 2 liters milk
- 2 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the grains and place in cold water.
2. When it comes to a boil, pour in the milk, and let the porridge cook slowly with the lid closed for 3-4 hours.
3. During cooking, stir the porridge from time to time to prevent it from burning to the bottom, and thin with a little more milk if needed.
4. Taste and serve.
