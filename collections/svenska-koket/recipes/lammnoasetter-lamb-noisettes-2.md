## Ingredients

- 750 gr. lamb fillet
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1 1/2 hg. bacon
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 3 dcl. broth
- 1 hg. goose liver
- 1 can asparagus (1/4 lit.)
- 1/2 lit. baby potatoes, cooked in aspic

## Directions

1. Dry the fillets with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and cut into 12 slices, which are lightly flattened, rubbed with the seasonings, rolled up and wrapped in thinly cut bacon slices.
2. Brown them in the butter, dilute with the boiling broth and let them stew with a tight lid until tender, or approximately 45 minutes.
3. Remove the bacon slices and arrange the noisettes in a circle on a round serving platter and on each noisette place a small round slice of goose liver.
4. Place the asparagus in the middle of the platter, heated in a water bath, arrange the baby potatoes around it.
5. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve with truffle sauce (see no. 433).
