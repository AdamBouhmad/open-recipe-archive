## Ingredients

- 105 gr. butter
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 160 gr. wheat flour
- 1 knife tip ammonium carbonate salt
- 100 gr. sweet almonds
- 10 bitter almonds
- 110 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 tbsp. water
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. The pastry dough is prepared in the usual way.
2. The almonds are blanched, grated or driven through an almond mill, pounded finely together with sugar and a little water, and the egg is worked in well.
3. Small fluted molds are lined with pastry dough and filled with almond mixture.
4. A cross of pastry dough is laid on each pastry, after which they are placed in moderate oven heat to bake.
5. They are turned out as soon as they are done.
