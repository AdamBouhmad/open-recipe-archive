## Ingredients

- 400-500 g boneless lamb roast
- 1.5 tbsp butter (30 g)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 g)
- 300-400 ml stock
- salt
- white pepper
- 0.5 tbsp finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes.
2. Brown the butter and flour (see Sauces, p. 210); add the stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Warm the meat in the sauce but do not allow it to boil, as it will become tough.
4. Season the dish with spices and parsley and serve within a border of potato purée.
