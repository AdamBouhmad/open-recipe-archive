## Ingredients

- 3 hg. boneless, roasted meat
- 1 hg. butter
- 2 tsp. finely chopped onion
- 1 hg. flour
- 5-6 dcl. boiling milk
- 5 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Scrape and finely chop the meat.
2. Fry the butter with the onion and flour for 2 minutes.
3. Add the boiling milk and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes while stirring.
4. Cool and stir until the sauce has cooled.
5. Add the egg yolks one at a time and stir for 10 minutes.
6. Mix in the meat and season with spices.
7. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
8. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pudding mold with lid.
9. Cook in a water bath on the stove for approximately 2 hours.
10. Turn out onto a plate, garnish with slices of pickled cucumber or tomatoes and parsley.
11. Serve with brown sauce or with butter sauce mixed with finely chopped pickled onion, pickles, or pickled cucumber.
