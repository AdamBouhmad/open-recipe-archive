## Ingredients

- 1 liter green gooseberries or 1/2 liter preserved gooseberries
- 2 liters water
- peel from 1/4 lemon
- 1–1 1/2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato starch (30 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon caster sugar

## Directions

1. Top and tail the gooseberries, rinse them, place in boiling water with lemon peel and allow to cook until tender.
2. Strain the soup and cook and serve as in the previous recipe.
