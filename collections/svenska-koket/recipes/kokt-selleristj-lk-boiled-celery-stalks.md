## Ingredients

- Celery stalks
- Salted water
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the stalks as in the previous recipe.
2. Tie them in bundles like asparagus.
3. Place them in boiling salted water and boil for 10-15 minutes.
4. Drain them and place on a plate with a folded serviette.
5. Serve warm with melted butter.
