## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 dcl milk
- 60 gr butter
- 1/4 tsk salt
- 1/2 msk sugar (8 gr)
- 75 gr flour
- 5 whole eggs
- 1 kg cooking oil for poaching

## Directions

1. Bring butter, milk, and spices to a boil, add the flour with vigorous whisking, and let the batter cook until it releases from the pan and has a golden color.
2. Remove the pan from heat, work in the eggs, one at a time, and stir the mixture 5 minutes.
3. Shape the mixture with two teaspoons, dipped in the cooking oil, into round balls the size of a walnut, which are poached a nice light golden color in moderately hot oil and placed on absorbent paper to drain, then sprinkled with sugar.
4. Serve very hot, placed on parchment paper, with chocolate sauce, jam, or compote.
