## Ingredients

- 5—6 hg. boneless veal roast
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.)
- 3 msk. flour (30 gr.)
- 4 dcl. meat gravy
- 1/2 dcl. thick cream
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. The roast is cut into even slices.
2. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes, the meat gravy is thinned in and the sauce is allowed to boil for 5 minutes, after which the cream is added and the sauce is seasoned with the spices and brought to a boil.
3. The meat is warmed in the sauce but must not boil, is placed on a warm platter, garnished with fried potatoes and served with green salad, cucumbers and pickled lingonberries.
