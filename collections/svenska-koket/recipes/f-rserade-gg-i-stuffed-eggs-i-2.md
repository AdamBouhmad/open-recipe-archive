## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 5 gr. spinach or parsley
- 1/2 anchovy
- 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 6 tomato slices
- parsley for garnish
- salad greens or parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes, cool in cold water, peel and cut in half crosswise.
2. Carefully remove the yolks and pound in a mortar together with the butter, finely chopped spinach or parsley, the cleaned and boned anchovy, and the sugar.
3. Pass the egg filling through a fine sieve and pipe through a fluted piping tip into each egg half. Insert a small sprig of parsley at the edge as a handle.
4. Place the tomato slices on a plate and lay one egg half on each slice. Pipe the remaining filling in a ring around the edge of each slice. Garnish with salad greens or parsley and serve to the smörgåsbord.
