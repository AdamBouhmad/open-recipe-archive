## Ingredients

- 1 young hen
- 3 1/2 liters light broth
- 2 tbsp butter (40 g)
- 6 tbsp flour (60 g)
- salt
- cayenne
- 3-4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare the hen in the usual way, let it soak in water for 12 hours, dry it well, and cook it in the broth until tender. Remove the breast meat from the carcass, which is placed back in the broth and cooked for another 1 hour. Cut the meat into small, oblong pieces and place in the soup bowl. Strain the broth, skim it, and measure it — it should be 3 liters.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the broth little by little, and let it cook while stirring for 5 minutes. Season the soup with spices. Whisk the egg yolks and stir them into the soup, then pour it over the meat in the soup bowl.
