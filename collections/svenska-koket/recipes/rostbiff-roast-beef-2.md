## Ingredients

- 5 kg. roast beef or double sirloin
- 2 tbsp. salt (30 gr.)
- water

## Directions

1. Roast beef should hang for 1-2 weeks depending on the season and be dried daily with a dry towel.
2. Pound it on the skin side with a damp meat mallet, dry it with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and rub it with salt.
3. If no special roasting pan is available, place the meat with the fillet side up on a rack in a long pan, which is placed in a warm oven.
4. The oven should not be too hot from the start, as the fat should partially melt before the roast is browned.
5. Pour a little boiling water into the pan and increase the heat gradually.
6. When the roast is brown, baste it every 10 minutes with the gravy, which, if needed, is diluted with boiling water. (Note! Baste the roast first and dilute afterwards, not the other way around.)
7. Roasting time is calculated at 20-25 minutes per kg.
8. If the meat becomes too brown, place a sheet over it or cover it with a buttered paper.
9. Place roast beef on a warm platter and carve at the table.
10. Strain the gravy, skim it well, boil it together, taste and season, and serve undiluted.
11. Serve roast beef with French potatoes, potato croquettes or vegetables, with glazed onion and, if desired, with shavings of horseradish.
