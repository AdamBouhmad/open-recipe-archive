## Ingredients

- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 6 eggs
- 6 anchovies

## Directions

1. Grease a low omelette mould with butter.
2. Break the eggs carefully and let them slide into the mould close together.
3. Clean and bone the anchovies and cut each anchovy into four fillets. Lay two fillets in a ring around each egg and two crosswise over the egg.
4. Place the remaining butter in small flakes on the anchovies.
5. Fry the eggs in the oven until the white has set.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish.
