## Ingredients

- 2 medium cauliflower heads (1 kg)
- 1 1/4 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 40 g melted butter or 3 deciliters hollandaise sauce
- vinegar-mixed water for soaking

## Directions

1. The cauliflower heads should be white and firm
2. Remove the green leaves and cut away the bottom of the stem
3. Rinse the cauliflower well and let it soak in vinegar-mixed water for 1 hour
4. Rinse again and place in boiling, salted water with the florets facing down
5. Cook for 15-20 minutes, or until just tender, which is tested with a whisk
6. Place the cauliflower on a hot dish using a slotted spoon
7. Pour the melted butter or sauce over it
8. Serve very hot as a vegetable side dish or with roasted meat, salted beef brisket, or hamburger meat
9. The cauliflower can also be used as a garnish around meat together with other vegetables
10. Note: cauliflower becomes whiter if cooked in half milk and half water
