## Ingredients

- 5 hg powdered sugar
- 3 1/2 dcl water
- 1 litre juice or purée of fresh strawberries
- juice of 1 lemon (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix the powdered sugar with water.
2. Add the strawberry juice or purée.
3. Add lemon juice if desired.
4. Freeze as fruit glacé, standing approximately 1 hour longer in the mold with the salt mixture packed tightly around it before turning out.
