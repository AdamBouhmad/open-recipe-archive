## Ingredients

- 9 large, beautiful apples
- 75 gr. melted butter
- 1 dcl. finely crushed rusks
- 1 dcl. caster sugar (85 gr.)
- 75 gr. sweet almond
- 75 gr. icing sugar
- 1 small egg white
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Prepare the almond paste first. Blanch the almond, dry in the oven, grind through a mill and pound with the sugar into a fine mass, add the egg white, and pound the mass until it is smooth and supple.
2. Rinse the apples, dry, peel evenly, and remove the cores with a metal tube.
3. Divide the almond paste into nine portions, which are rolled out and filled into the apples, which are then dipped in the melted butter and rolled in a mixture of the rusks and sugar.
4. Butter a small long pan with cold butter, place the apples in it, place in a warm oven and bake until beautifully golden brown and tender.
5. Arrange when they are cold on a cake dish with cake paper and serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
