## Ingredients

- 3 liters cherries
- 1 1/2 liters vinegar
- 3/4 kg. sugar for each kg. of berries
- a few pieces of cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon whole cloves
- 15 cherry kernels

## Directions

1. Rinse the berries, pit them, weigh them and let them lie in the vinegar for 24 hours, then strain off the vinegar.
2. Layer the cherries with granulated sugar, spices and kernels in a jar, and pour over so much of the vinegar that it stands over the berries.
3. Cover the jar and let it stand in a cool place.
4. After 6 days strain off the liquid, cook it until it is syrupy, let it cool and pour it again over the berries.
5. Repeat this one more time, if the liquid seems thin.
