## Ingredients

- 10 eggs
- 2 hg. sugar
- 1/2 hg. pea flour
- 1/2 hg. potato flour
- 1 hg. sweet almonds
- 15 gr. bitter almonds
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the mold
- 4 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks and sugar for 1/2 hour.
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in quickly, then add the flour and finally the blanched and ground almonds.
3. Pour the batter into a large buttered and rusk-strewn decorative copper mold and bake in a not too hot oven for about 45 minutes.
