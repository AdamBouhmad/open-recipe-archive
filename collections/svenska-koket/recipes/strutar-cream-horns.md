## Ingredients

- 65 gr. butter
- 65 gr. powdered sugar
- 75 gr. fine flour
- 4 egg whites
- thick whipped cream
- jam to taste

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and add the sugar immediately, then stir in the flour and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
2. Heat a baking sheet, grease it well, and place the dough on it with a teaspoon, spread into oblong cakes with large spaces between them, as the dough spreads. Bake the cakes quickly in a hot oven until they have a nice golden-brown colour.
3. Remove them with a knife from the sheet while they remain in the oven and twist them while they are hot in the form of a horn. Just before serving, fill them with thick whipped cream, sweetened with jam to taste.
