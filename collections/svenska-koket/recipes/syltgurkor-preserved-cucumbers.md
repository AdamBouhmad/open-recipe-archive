## Ingredients

- 4 large white cucumbers
- 3 liters water
- 1 teaspoon salt (15 grams) for blanching
- 1 1/4 kilograms lump sugar
- 1 liter spirit vinegar
- 2 grams whole nutmeg flower
- 2 grams whole white pepper
- 3 pieces ginger (15 grams)

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut them in half lengthwise, remove the seeds with a silver spoon, and cut the cucumber halves crosswise into pieces.
2. Place a few cucumber pieces at a time into boiling salted water and let them cook slowly until they are soft, which is tested with a whisk.
3. Remove them with a slotted spoon, lay them on linen to drain and then layer them alternately in a jar.
4. Cook sugar, vinegar and spices, tied up in muslin, until a clear syrup forms, which is poured hot over the cucumbers.
5. Cover the jar with a folded towel and plate. Place the spice bag on top of the plate.
6. After 3 days, strain the syrup, boil it together with the spices and pour it cold over the cucumbers, which are carefully covered.
7. Repeat this procedure two more times with six-day intervals, after which the cucumbers are ready.
8. Bind the jar well.
