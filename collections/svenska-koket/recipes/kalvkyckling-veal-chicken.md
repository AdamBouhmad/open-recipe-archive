## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless veal leg
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 large bunch parsley
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 1 decilitre broth
- 3 decilitres thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal and treat it as in the previous recipe and sprinkle the veal slices with a mixture of salt and pepper.
2. Clean the parsley, rinse it, wring it in a cloth, chop it coarsely and work it into the butter.
3. Divide the parsley butter into 12 portions and place one portion on each veal slice, which is then rolled up and bound in two places with fine twine.
4. Cook the chicken and serve as in the previous recipe.
