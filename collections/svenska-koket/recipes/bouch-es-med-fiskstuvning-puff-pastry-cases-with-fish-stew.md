## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg. flour
- 1 part water
- 1 tablespoon brandy
- 1/2 egg
- 350 gr. butter (equal weight with the dough)
- 1 1/2-3/2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 parts fish stock
- 3 parts cream
- 1/2 litre cooked, boned and finely cut fish
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the puff dough as ordinary puff dough
2. When it is ready, roll it out to 1 cm. thickness, and with measure take out small round cakes
3. With a knife cut small lids in these, but not deeper than 1/2 cm. (half of the cake's thickness)
4. Brush the ring outside the lid with egg white, and fasten a strip of dough to it, edged with points
5. Bake the bouchées thereafter in very hot oven
6. Lift off the already cut lid, and fill the bouchées with fish stew, then place the lid back on
7. The bouchées should be served very hot
