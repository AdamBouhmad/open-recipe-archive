## Ingredients

- 1 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 1/2 liters mixed veal and beef broth
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the Jerusalem artichokes and form them to equal size
2. Place them in water mixed with vinegar so they do not darken
3. Cook them until soft in lightly salted water
4. Fry together the butter and flour, then add the boiling broth and as much of the cooking liquid from the artichokes as needed for good flavor
5. Let it cook for 10 minutes
6. Whisk the egg yolks in the soup bowl and pour the soup into it while whisking vigorously
7. Finally, add the Jerusalem artichokes to the soup, taste and adjust seasoning, and serve very hot
