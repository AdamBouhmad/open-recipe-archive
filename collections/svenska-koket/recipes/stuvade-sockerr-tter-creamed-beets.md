## Ingredients

- 12 fine, even beets
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoon flour (30 gr.)
- 2 deciliter of cooking water from beets
- 2 deciliter cream
- salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse, brush, and scrape the beets and place them gradually in vinegar-mixed water to prevent darkening
2. Rinse again, place in boiling water with salt and lemon juice, and let cook until tender, which is tested with a whisk
3. Remove with a slotted spoon and reserve the cooking water
4. Cut the beets into 3-4 pieces
5. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cooking water and cream, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes
6. Add the beets and heat in the sauce
7. Season the stew with spices and, if desired, bind with an egg yolk
8. Serve with roasted meat, in omelets, etc.
