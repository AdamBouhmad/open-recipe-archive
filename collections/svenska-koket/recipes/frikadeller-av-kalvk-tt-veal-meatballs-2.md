## Ingredients

- 150 g boneless veal
- 30 g butter
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 g)
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped mushrooms or truffle
- 1—1/2 decilitres cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat as in the previous recipe and pass together with the butter 4 times through the meat grinder
2. Pound the forcemeat with the flour in a mortar and work it together with the beaten egg, salt, onion and mushroom for about 1/2 hour, while adding the cream little by little
3. Taste and adjust the forcemeat and form and cook as in the previous recipe
