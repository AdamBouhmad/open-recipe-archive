## Ingredients

- 1 small carrot
- 1 small parsnip
- ½ celery
- 1 small leek
- 1½ tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- ½ teaspoon sugar
- 1½ liters clear broth
- 1 small head of lettuce
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Rinse, scrape, and cut the root vegetables into very fine strips and lightly brown them in the butter and sugar
2. Place them in the boiling broth along with the rinsed, cleaned lettuce head cut into fine strips
3. Let the soup cook with a tightly closed lid for approximately 15 minutes
4. Skim the soup and season with salt and cayenne
5. Serve, if desired, with cheese crackers or dumplings
