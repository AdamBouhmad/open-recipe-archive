## Ingredients

- 150 gr. oat flour
- 175 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tsk. bicarbonate (3 gr.)
- 50 gr. melted butter
- 3 msk. sugar (45 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 2 dcl. water

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and mix well with the bicarbonate.
2. Let the butter cool and mix it with the sugar until it is white and fluffy, then add it to the flour along with the whisked egg and water, and work the dough until it is even and smooth.
3. Roll out the dough to 2 mm. thickness, prick it well and cut out with a square mould into cakes, which are placed on a buttered sheet and baked in a not too warm oven.
