## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar
- 3 teaspoons salt (15 gr.)
- Water
- 7 gr. coarse salt per liter of water
- Parsley
- Melted butter
- Grated horseradish
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Do not scale the fish, clean it as described on page 171
2. When rubbed with salt, bind the fish by threading a needle with string through the head and tail so the fish lies bent when served, giving it the name kromgädda
3. Cook with the liver as in recipe n:o 327
4. When the fins loosen easily, it is done
5. Place on a fish platter with a sieve or on a broken napkin
6. Garnish with parsley and the creamed liver
7. Serve with melted butter, grated horseradish, and potatoes
