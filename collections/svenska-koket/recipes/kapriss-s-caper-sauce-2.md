## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 4 parts fish stock
- 2 tablespoons capers
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/5 part cream

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are cooked together
2. Fish stock and the coarsely chopped capers with some of their vinegar are added
3. The sauce is allowed to boil for 10 minutes
4. It is seasoned with the spices and finished with the egg yolk and cream
