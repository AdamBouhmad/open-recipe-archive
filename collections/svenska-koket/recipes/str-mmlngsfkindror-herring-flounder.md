## Ingredients

- 36 herring
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 bunch parsley
- 90 grams butter
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams) for breading
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon water
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs for breading
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams) or 200 grams lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the herring as in recipe 163, flatten them out, sprinkle with salt and let rest for 1 hour.
2. Clean the parsley, rinse, wrap, chop finely, wring out in a cloth and mix well with the butter.
3. Form the parsley butter into a roll, let it harden and cut into 18 slices.
4. Place two herring against each other with the skin side out and a slice of parsley butter between them.
