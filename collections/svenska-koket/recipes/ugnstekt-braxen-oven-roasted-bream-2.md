## Ingredients

- 1 bream weighing 2 kg
- 1 1/2 tbsp. salt (23 g)
- 3 parsley stems
- 1/2 tbsp. finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped mushrooms
- 3 tbsp. crushed breadcrumbs
- 1/2 egg
- 100 g butter
- 2 dcl. broth or water

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish as in recipe no. 338 and serve with mushroom or caper sauce.
