## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Various sauces
- Gelée

## Directions

1. Pour 1½ liters of water into a pot holding approximately 2 liters; add 1 tbsp. salt and 1 tbsp. vinegar.
2. Bring the water to a hard boil.
3. Carefully break the eggs and beat them into a cup. Let them slide into the boiling water; the rapid movement will cause the white to close around the firmer yolk and the egg becomes round.
4. Remove when the white has set sufficiently, trim, and serve as a breakfast dish with various sauces, in gelée, or as garnish to other dishes.
