## Ingredients

- 2 medium heads green or blue kale
- 3 liters water for pre-boiling
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 liters beef broth
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon ground fennel
- kale stalks
- fish quenelles

## Directions

1. The kale, which should be frozen, is placed in water to draw out the ice.
2. It is shredded and pre-boiled for 15 minutes as in recipe 257.
3. The kale is placed on a sieve, rinsed with cold water and allowed to drain well.
4. It is wrung in a cloth, chopped finely, passed through a sieve and cooked in broth for 1 hour.
5. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes, the soup is thinned and allowed to cook with stirring for 5 minutes.
6. It is seasoned with the spices and served with kale stalks cooked in cream sauce and fish quenelles.
7. The thickest kale stalks are pre-boiled, peeled, wrapped in a cloth, cut into 5 cm long pieces, which are rolled in flour, dipped in beaten egg and then in crushed breadcrumbs and cooked golden brown in hot cream sauce.
8. They are placed in the soup bowl along with the quenelles, the soup is poured in and served immediately.
9. Canned kale can be used with advantage.
