## Ingredients

- 1 12-öre wheat bread
- 1 kg. butter
- 4 eggs
- ¾—1 liter skim milk
- 1½ tsk. sugar
- 1 part punch
- ½ tsk. butter for the form

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into slices, spread these with butter and place them in a buttered pie form.
2. Whisk together the eggs, milk and sugar and pour over the bread in the form.
3. When the bread slices are thoroughly soaked, place the pudding in a not too hot oven to bake until it has taken on a nice brown color.
4. Just before serving, poke holes in the pudding with a fork and pour the punch over it.
5. Serve hot with cranberry jam.
