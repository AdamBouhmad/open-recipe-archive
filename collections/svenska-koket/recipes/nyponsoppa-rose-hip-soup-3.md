## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter rose hips
- 2 liters water
- 1 1/2-2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 25 gr potato flour
- 75 gr raisins or 1 hg prunes
- 15 gr sweet almonds (15 pieces)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rose hips well in lukewarm water and let them drain.
2. Place them in cold water and let them cook until soft for about 1 1/2 hours; meanwhile, whisk the soup often so that the rose hips break down.
3. Press them through a sieve, add the sugar, let the soup come to a boil, skim it, and thicken with the potato flour, stirred in 1/2 tablespoon cold water.
4. Let the soup cook for 2 minutes, flavor, and serve with the rinsed and cooked raisins or plums, the blanched and sliced almonds, and whipped cream.
