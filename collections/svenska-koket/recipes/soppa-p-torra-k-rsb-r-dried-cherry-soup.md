## Ingredients

- 2 cups dried cherries
- 2½ litres water
- 1–1½ cups crushed sugar
- 1½ tablespoons potato flour
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup cherry juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the cherries, crush them in a mortar and cook in the water for about ½ hour.
2. Strain out the berries, return the liquid to the pot and add sugar.
3. Let the soup come to a boil and thicken with potato flour mixed with a little cold water.
4. Rinse the raisins, cook them separately in a little liquid and add to the soup.
5. Taste the soup with cherry juice and serve, if desired, with rusks.
