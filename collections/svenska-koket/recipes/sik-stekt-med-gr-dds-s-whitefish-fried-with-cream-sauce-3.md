## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. fat whitefish
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1 hectogram butter
- 4 decilitres thick cream
- 3 tablespoons coarsely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, rinse well and dry it.
2. Cut it open along the backbone and remove this together with all small bones.
3. (If desired, the skin can be removed, but many consider it a delicacy.)
4. Rub the fillets with salt and let them rest 1 hour.
5. Dry them again and cut into 3 1/2 cm. wide pieces across, dip these in the beaten egg and turn them in the wheat flour.
6. Brown the fish quickly in half of the butter in a frying pan and layer it alternately with the parsley in a buttered pie form.
7. Lay the remaining butter in flakes on top and add the cream.
8. Place a lid on the form, which is then placed in the oven, and let the fish roast for about 20 minutes.
9. Serve it from the form with mashed potatoes. — Instead of parsley, chopped capers can be used.
