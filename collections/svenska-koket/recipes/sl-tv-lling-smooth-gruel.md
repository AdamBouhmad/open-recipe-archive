## Ingredients

- 1 small cup flour
- 2 liters milk
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix flour with 3 cups of milk and let it swell for about 1 hour.
2. Melt butter in a pot, pour in the milk and bring to boil.
3. Add cinnamon and the flour mixture, and let the gruel simmer for 10-15 minutes with stirring.
4. Season and serve.
