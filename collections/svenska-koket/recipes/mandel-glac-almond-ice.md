## Ingredients

- 100 grams sweet almonds
- 5 bitter almonds
- 1 liter boiling cream
- Water
- Base cream recipe

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, grind them through a mill and pound with a little water in a mortar until fine.
2. Place the almond mass in the boiling cream and let steep, covered, for 20 minutes.
3. Strain the cream through muslin.
4. Prepare the cream according to the base recipe.
5. Freeze in a machine as in recipe no. 929.
