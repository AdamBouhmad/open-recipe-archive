## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. water
- 65 gr. lump sugar
- the zest of 1 1/2 oranges
- the juice of 3 oranges
- 2-3 tbsp. pure or French wine
- 2 tsp. potato flour (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. From the water and sugar cook a simple sugar syrup, which is skimmed well.
2. Wash and dry the oranges.
3. Grate the zest from 1 1/2 orange and mix with the sugar syrup together with the pressed orange juice.
4. Add the wine, bring the juice to boil, taste and dilute if desired with a little water.
5. Thicken the sauce as in the previous recipe and can be served both warm and cold.
