## Ingredients

- Asp, Id, Mört, or similar bony fish
- Butter sauce
- Chives, parsley, or capers (for sauce)
- Carp
- Ruda (Crucian roach)
- Lobster sauce (for Ruda)

## Directions

1. Asp, Id, and Mört are very bony fish and therefore not highly valued
2. They can be prepared as braxen, boiled and served with butter sauce added with chives, parsley, or capers, or fried like other fish
3. Carp is prepared in the same way as braxen
4. Ruda is treated and prepared as braxen, can be stewed like pike, or served with lobster sauce poured over the fish before serving
