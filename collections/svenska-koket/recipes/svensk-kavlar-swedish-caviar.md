## Ingredients

- Roe from pike-perch, burbot or pike
- salt
- white pepper
- finely chopped Portuguese or red onion

## Directions

1. Clean the roe from membranes and blood.
2. Mix with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Place on a plate and garnish around with finely chopped onion.
