## Ingredients

- 50 small white or 75 small green cucumbers
- 1 hg. coarse salt, 3 1/2 liters water for brine
- 10 gr. whole white pepper
- 8 gr. cloves
- 4 gr. mace
- 3 gr. shelled cardamom
- pepperroot in small pieces (3 dcl.)
- cherry and currant leaves
- dill crowns
- 3 liters water, 1 liter wine vinegar, 3 hg. sugar

## Directions

1. Let the cucumbers lie for 24 hours in fresh water, then in brine for 48 hours, after which they are allowed to dry for about 5 hours on double linen.
2. They are then laid in alternating layers with the spices, pepperroot, leaves, and dill crowns in a large jar, but in such a way that they do not touch each other or the edge of the vessel, for then they become spotted.
3. Boil the water, wine vinegar, and sugar and pour it hot over the cucumbers, which shall be well covered by it.
4. Place a bottom and a light weight on top, and let the jar stand until the following day.
5. If the syrup is then too thin, pour it off, boil it up, and pour it cold over the cucumbers.
6. Bind with parchment paper.
