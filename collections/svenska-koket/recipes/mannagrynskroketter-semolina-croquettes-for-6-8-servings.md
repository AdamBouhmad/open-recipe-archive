## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 deciliters semolina (114 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 7 deciliters milk
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 1-2 teaspoon grated cheese
- 1 tablespoon melted butter (20 gr.) for coating
- 1 tablespoon breadcrumbs
- 1/2 tablespoon grated cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Sift the semolina, rinse with cold water and drain.
2. Grease a pot with 1/2 tablespoon of butter, pour in the milk and bring to a boil.
3. Whisk in the semolina, then stir the porridge with a spoon until thickened, then move to a lower heat and let simmer slowly for about 20 minutes, stirring often.
4. Remove the porridge from heat, immediately blend with the remaining butter and let cool.
5. Add the beaten eggs, spices and grated cheese.
6. Grease a flat dish with butter, spread the mass on it about 2 cm. thick and let stand for 2-3 hours on ice to chill.
7. With a round mold, cut out small round cakes, place on a greased baking sheet, brush with butter, dust with a mixture of breadcrumbs and grated cheese and bake until nicely golden-brown in a warm oven.
8. Serve with roasted meat, for example venison.
