## Ingredients

- 1 packet Moiré's jelly powder
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 3-4 parts boiling water
- 3 parts white French wine

## Directions

1. Crush the jelly powder together with the sugar and dissolve it in the boiling water.
2. When everything is completely melted, add the wine, pour the jelly into a jelly mold or rinsed form and set out to set.
