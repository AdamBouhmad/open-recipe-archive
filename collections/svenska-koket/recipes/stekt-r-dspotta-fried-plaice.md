## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg plaice
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 3 decilitres milk
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons food oil
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 1/2 cups crushed hardtack
- 1/2 kg butter
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the plaice as in the previous recipe, but cut off the head and remove the dark skin by scraping the tail fin upward with a pointed knife until enough skin loosens that you can wrap one turn around the knife blade; roll the knife thereafter toward the head and wrap the skin around it
2. Rub the fish with salt, let it rest 1 hour, dry it again and bread it as in recipe no. 376
3. Fry it as in recipe 329, place on a warm platter with parchment paper, garnish with lemon wedges and serve with browned butter and parsley and mashed potatoes
4. The fish can also be fried in butter as in recipe no. 330
