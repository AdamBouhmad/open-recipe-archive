## Ingredients

- 100 gr. butter
- 200 gr. granulated sugar
- 3 eggs
- 50 gr. bitter almonds
- 10 vanilla drops
- 1 knife point ammonium carbonate
- 275 gr. wheat flour
- 2 egg whites for brushing
- 1/2 cup pearl sugar
- 50 gr. sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and sift the flour with ammonium carbonate on the work table.
2. Distribute the butter in flakes and place on top of the flour along with sugar, the whisked egg, the blanched and grated bitter almonds, and vanilla drops.
3. Work all ingredients together into a smooth, cohesive dough.
4. Roll out quite thin and cut out small cakes with a measure.
5. Brush with egg white and sprinkle with almonds and sugar.
6. Bake until light golden brown in moderate oven heat.
