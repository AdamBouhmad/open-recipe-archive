## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 hg sugar
- 60 gr melted butter
- 260 gr wheat flour
- 3 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup thin cream
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold (10 gr)
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold
- 5 dcl warm, loose applesauce for filling
- 3 egg whites for meringue
- 105 gr powdered sugar for meringue

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar for 1/2 hour.
2. Add the melted and cooled butter, the flour which should be well sifted with the baking powder, and the cream milk.
3. Mix the batter well and immediately pour into a buttered tall timbal mold.
4. Bake in ordinary oven temperature for about 1 hour, or until the cake feels dry when tested with a whisk.
5. Turn out the cake, let it cool, and hollow it out so that the walls and bottom are only 2 cm thick.
6. Fill the cake with the applesauce and place a lid made from the cut-out cake on top.
7. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks, carefully fold in the sifted powdered sugar.
8. Pipe the meringue through a pastry bag over the cake and place in the oven to achieve a nice golden-brown color.
9. Serve warm, poured over with warm apricot sauce colored red with carmine.
