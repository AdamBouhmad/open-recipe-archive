## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. water (scant)
- 250 gr. fine butter
- 150 gr. sweet almonds
- 4 bitter almonds
- 150 gr. powdered sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 25 gr. sweet almonds
- 3 tbsp. pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as in no. 33.
2. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven and pass through an almond mill.
3. Pound it thereafter with the sugar and egg whites into a fine and supple mass.
4. Roll out the butter dough to 1 decimeter wide strip of approximately 3 mm. thickness.
5. Spread the almond mass thinly on the butter dough, whereupon roll the dough carefully along its length, and roll out again to 5 cm. width.
6. Cut the dough across into 2 1/2 cm. wide cakes, which are brushed with egg white and sprinkled with almonds and sugar.
7. Bake the cakes in a warm oven and serve fresh with coffee or tea.
