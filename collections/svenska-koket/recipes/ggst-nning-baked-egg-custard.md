## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 tbsp flour
- 2 cups sour milk
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 1 tsp grated cheese (or 1 tbsp chopped parsley)
- 1 tsp butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs and mix with the flour, milk, salt, grated cheese or chopped parsley
2. Butter a porcelain mold, pour in the mixture and bake in the oven until it takes on a nice, light brown color
3. Serve with clear soups
