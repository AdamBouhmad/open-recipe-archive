## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters strawberries, raspberries, lingonberries, cloudberries, or pitted cherries
- 1.5 deciliters granulated sugar (128 g)

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, which should be freshly picked, rinse quickly in a sieve, and drain well
2. Layer the berries alternately with the sugar in a compote bowl; sprinkle each layer of berries with fresh water
3. Let the berries stand for 2-3 hours to release their juice
4. Serve with whipped or frozen cream
