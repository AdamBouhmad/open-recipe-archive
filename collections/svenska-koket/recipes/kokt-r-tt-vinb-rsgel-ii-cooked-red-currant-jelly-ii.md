## Ingredients

- 3 kg red currants
- 3 kg sugar
- 7 parts water

## Directions

1. Rinse the currants and place them in the preserving pot, alternating with the sugar.
2. Bring to a boil over low heat, stir if necessary, and allow to cook for 2.5 minutes.
3. Pour the berries into a cheesecloth and allow the juice to drip directly into cleaned and warm glass jars.
4. Allow to stand for 4-5 days and then bind over with parchment paper.
