## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. karolina-risgryn (150 gr.)
- 1/2 lit. vatten
- 1/2 lit. kokande grädde
- 75-100 gr. sockrad blockchoklad
- socker

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains and scald them in hot water, then place in boiling water and let cook for 15 minutes, after which the cream is diluted in.
2. Melt the chocolate in a water bath and add it to the porridge, which then should slowly cook until the grains are completely soft, or approximately 1 hour.
3. Flavor the porridge with sugar, pour into a jelly mold and serve with whipped cream.
