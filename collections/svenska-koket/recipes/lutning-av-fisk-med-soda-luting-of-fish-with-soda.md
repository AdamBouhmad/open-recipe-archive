## Ingredients

- 4 kg spirling or pollock
- 15 liters water
- 850 grams slaked lime
- 6 hectograms soda

## Directions

1. The fish, which should be white in flesh, is cut into pieces and beaten well with a club.
2. Place the pieces in a tub with cold water (preferably seawater) and let them soak for a week, changing the water four times.
3. Dissolve the lime and soda in the cold water.
4. Place the fish in it with skin side up and place a weight on to keep the pieces down.
5. The fish should be turned often so that the pieces at the top come to the bottom.
6. After 3-4 days, take out the pieces as they become swollen.
7. Brush them with a root brush and place in cold water.
8. Change this water twice a day; during the first two days, brush the fish each time.
9. After about 8 days, the fish is ready to prepare.
