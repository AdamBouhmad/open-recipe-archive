## Ingredients

- 2-3 red cabbage heads
- water
- salt
- 4-6 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2.5 liters broth or pork stock
- white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place the cabbage (which should be frozen) in water so the ice thaws out.
2. Shred it, rinse well and blanch for 0.5 hour in salted water.
3. Place the cabbage on a sieve to drain and then chop it with the flour until very fine.
4. Melt the butter in a pot, add the cabbage and let it fry with frequent stirring.
5. Add the boiling broth and let the soup cook for 1.5 hours.
6. Season it and serve with meatballs or pork sausage.
