## Ingredients

- 5 dcl. apple sauce
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 6 leaves gelatin
- 3 tablespoons boiling water for the gelatin

## Directions

1. The apple sauce should be sieved, quite firm and moderately sweet.
2. Blanch the almonds, dry them well in the oven, pass through an almond grinder and mix with the apple sauce together with the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Finally add the rinsed gelatin that has been dissolved in the boiling water and allowed to cool.
4. Pour the mixture into a tin form that has been rinsed with water and sprinkled with sugar and let it stand on ice for about 3 hours.
5. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it with a pointed knife around the edge of the form and turn out onto a tart plate.
6. Garnish, if desired, with fruit and sponge biscuits.
