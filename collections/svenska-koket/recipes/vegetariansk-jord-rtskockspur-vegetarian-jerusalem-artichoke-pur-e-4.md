## Ingredients

- 1½ liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 small celery
- 2 liters water
- 2 tbsp. butter
- 3 tbsp. flour
- 4 parts cream
- salt, white pepper
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare the Jerusalem artichokes and celery in the usual way and cook soft in water with added salt.
2. When the Jerusalem artichokes are completely soft, pass them through a sieve.
