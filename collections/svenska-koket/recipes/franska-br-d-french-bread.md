## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg wheat flour
- 1 msk sugar
- 2 tsk salt
- 1 liter water
- 4 msk lard or margarine
- 50 gr pressed yeast

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm water.
2. Sift the wheat flour and set aside 2-3 kkp for shaping.
3. Place the rest in a bowl and mix with the sugar and salt; add the lukewarm water with vigorous stirring.
4. Stir in the melted and slightly cooled lard and the dissolved yeast, then beat the dough smooth and glossy.
5. The dough should be quite firm.
6. Place in a warm spot to rise to double size and then shape into French breads, place on well-buttered sheets to rise again.
7. Bake the breads in good oven heat.
