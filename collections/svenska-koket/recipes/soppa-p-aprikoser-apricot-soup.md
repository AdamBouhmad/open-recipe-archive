## Ingredients

- 250 gr. dried apricots
- 2 lit. water
- peel of 1/4 lemon
- 1-1 1/2 kkp crushed sugar
- 25 gr. rice or potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots very well in lukewarm water and let them soak overnight in the water in which they shall be cooked.
2. Set them on to boil with the lemon peel and sugar, and let them cook until they are soft.
3. Thicken the soup with the rice flour, stirred in 1/2 kkp cold water, and let it cook 5 minutes.
4. Season it with the juice of 1/4 lemon and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and yeast powder rusks.
