## Ingredients

- 2 hectograms scraped fish meat
- 80 grams butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 parts cream
- 1 egg
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the fish (do not scale), rinse and dry well, fillet and scrape finely (no scale or bone should go with it).
2. Pound it together with the butter in a stone mortar until the mass is fine and coherent, then mix with half of the cream and pass through a sieve.
3. In a bowl, mix the rest of the cream, egg yolks, salt, sugar, white pepper, and flour.
4. Add this mixture spoonful by spoonful to the fish mixture.
5. Work it for about 1.5 hours, then fold in the beaten egg whites.
6. Press the mixture through a pastry bag or paper cone into small oblong cakes on a buttered baking sheet.
7. Pour boiling water carefully over them, and let the quenelles cook slowly for 5-6 minutes.
