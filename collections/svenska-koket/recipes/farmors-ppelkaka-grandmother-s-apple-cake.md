## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters good, loose apples
- 50 gr. caster sugar
- 3 two-öre almond breads
- 1 1/2 dcl. lukewarm milk
- 120 gr. butter
- 120 gr. sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- Grated zest of 1/2 lemon
- 25 gr. sweet almond (about 25 pieces)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them, remove the cores with a pastry tube, and cut the apples into thin slices, which are placed alternately with the sugar in a bowl and left to rest for about 1 hour.
2. Cut the almond breads into pieces and place them in the milk.
3. Beat the butter and sugar to a foam, add the beaten eggs and egg yolks together with the lemon zest, the blanched and ground almond, and lastly work in the soaked, squeezed out bread.
4. Work the dough well and place it alternately with the apple slices in a buttered and rusk-sprinkled pie form - 3 layers of dough and 2 layers of apple slices.
5. Bake the pudding in ordinary oven heat for about 1 hour, or until the apples are soft and the dough, when tested with a whisk, feels dry.
6. Serve warm with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
