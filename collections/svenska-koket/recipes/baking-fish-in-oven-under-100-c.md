## Ingredients

- Fish cut into fillets
- Fish head, bones, and skin
- Butter
- Salt
- Fish broth
- Mushroom broth (optional)

## Directions

1. Make good fish broth from the head, bones, and skin, which may be mixed with a little mushroom broth if desired.
2. Grease a long pan or low saucepan with butter.
3. Rub the fillets with a very little salt and lay them in the pan, close together.
4. Pour in enough clear fish broth to reach just level with the fish.
5. Cover with a lid, or if lacking one, a baking sheet.
6. Place the pan in ordinary oven heat.
7. Baste the fish from time to time until it is done, which is tested with a whisk stick.
8. Let it drain, lay it on a platter and pour some sauce over it; it is usually then gratineed in the oven.
9. Boil down the cooking liquid and use it for the sauce.
