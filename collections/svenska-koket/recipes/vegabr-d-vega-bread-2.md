## Ingredients

- 2 kg. wheat flour
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 liter lukewarm milk
- 50 gr. pressed yeast
- 2-4 cup granulated sugar
- 800 gr. butter or margarine
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 1 egg for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- ½ cup pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm milk.
2. Sift the wheat flour with the salt, set aside 3-4 cups for rolling out, and pour the rest into a bowl.
3. Add the lukewarm milk with vigorous stirring, and stir in the dissolved yeast, then beat the dough until smooth and glossy.
4. Set it in a warm place to rise to double its size.
5. Work 200 gr. of the butter creamed with 2 cups of the sugar to a white foam into the dough.
6. Let the dough rise again.
7. Work it up and divide it into 10 parts. Roll each part out to an oblong rectangle ½ cm. thick and brush with the rest of the butter, clarified.
8. Blanch the sweet almonds, chop them and mix with 1 cup granulated sugar and sprinkle over the brushed dough, which is now rolled up into a long roll.
9. Cut this into 12-15 parts, press them in the middle with the handle of a table knife, turn them over and place on well-buttered baking sheets to rise.
10. When the breads have risen, brush them with beaten egg and sprinkle with blanched and finely chopped sweet almonds mixed with the pearl sugar.
11. Bake in good oven heat.
12. Instead of cutting the roll into small breads, you can shape it into a wreath and clip it.
