## Ingredients

- 3 liters cherries
- 1 1/2 liters vinegar
- 3/4 kilogram sugar per kilogram of berries
- some pieces of cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon whole cloves
- 15 cherry pits

## Directions

1. Rinse the berries, pit them, weigh them and let them soak in the vinegar for 24 hours, then drain the vinegar.
2. Layer the cherries with granulated sugar, spices and pits in a jar, and pour enough of the vinegar over so that it covers the berries.
3. Bind the jar and let it stand in a cool place.
4. After 6 days, strain the liquid, cook it until it is syrupy, let it cool and pour it back over the berries.
5. Repeat this once more if the liquid seems thin.
