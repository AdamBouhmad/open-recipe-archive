## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless inner loin
- 1 hg lard
- 3/4 liter vinegar
- 3/4 liter water
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 8 dcl boiling cream milk

## Directions

1. Pound the meat well with a damp meat mallet and lard it in dense rows with lard cut into strips.
2. Place it the day before it is to be served in a mixture of vinegar and water.
3. Dry it well with a cloth and tie it with kitchen twine so it has a nice shape.
4. Brown the butter in a cast iron pot, not larger than necessary to fit the meat.
5. Place the roast in and brown well on all sides, then sprinkle with spices, and add the boiling milk.
6. Let the meat braise with a tightly closed lid until tender, about 2 1/2 hours.
7. Slice the meat, place on a warm platter, pour a couple of tablespoons of sauce over, and garnish with browned potatoes, salad greens or parsley.
8. Serve with red currant jelly and pickled cucumber.
9. Serve the sauce reduced (see no. 429), mixed with a little soy sauce.
