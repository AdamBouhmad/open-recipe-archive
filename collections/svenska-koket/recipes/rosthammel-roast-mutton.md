## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg French cut
- 0.5 tbsp salt (8 gr.)
- 0.25 tsp white pepper
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 4 tbsp butter (80 gr.)
- 1 carrot
- 1 shallot
- 2 dcl gravy or broth

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung so that it is tender, is beaten with a damp club, dried with a cloth, wrung in hot water, rubbed with a mixture of salt and pepper and brushed with parsley butter, which is made of 2 tbsp of the butter as in the previous recipe.
2. In a small long roasting pan or roasting pan, the rest of the butter, the scraped and sliced carrot and the peeled and cut shallot are placed. The roast is placed in and roasted in a warm oven for about 1 hour, during which it is basted frequently with the butter in the pan so that it does not dry out or burn.
3. A quarter of an hour before it is done, the boiling gravy is added.
4. Placed on a warm platter and carved at table. Served with vegetable or potato pasties and the gravy, which is strained and skimmed.
