## Ingredients

- 3 liters strawberries
- 8 hectograms crushed sugar
- 1 liter water

## Directions

1. Clean the strawberries (which should be large, firm and meaty), rinse them very carefully, if necessary, and let them drain well.
2. Cook them as ordinary compote for 15 minutes.
