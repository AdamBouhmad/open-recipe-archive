## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg fine rye sifted flour
- 0.5 kg wheat flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 liter lukewarm milk
- 70 gr pressed yeast
- 1 hg butter or margarine
- 2.5 portions syrup
- 2 tbsp crushed fennel
- 1 tbsp crushed anise
- 2 tbsp finely cut orange peel

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm milk.
2. Pour the sifted rye flour into a bowl, add the lukewarm milk with vigorous stirring, and mix in the dissolved yeast, then beat the dough until smooth and shiny.
3. Set it in a warm place to rise to double its size.
4. Add the well-washed and stirred butter, syrup, the crushed spices and the finely chopped orange peel, and work in the wheat flour until the dough becomes quite firm.
5. Set it to rise again.
6. Finally, work the dough on the baking board and shape into oblong buns, place them on warmed and floured cloths to rise.
7. When they have risen well, place them on greased trays, brush with cold water and bake in a warm oven.
8. Twice during baking brush the buns with hot water, and again when the finished buns are removed from the oven.
9. Cover them well so the crust does not become too hard.
