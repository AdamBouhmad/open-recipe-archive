## Ingredients

- 8 liters plums
- 8 hectograms crushed sugar
- 1 liter water

## Directions

1. Clean the plums, rinse them and let them drain.
2. Peel them, split them with a silver knife and remove the pit.
3. The plums should be placed in the sugar syrup immediately, otherwise they will become dark.
4. Cook them as ordinary compote for 30 minutes.
