## Ingredients

- 2½ lit. clear consommé
- salt
- cayenne
- 2 tbsp. finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Let the consommé come to a boil, season with spices and add the parsley
2. Serve with small pastry shells filled with chicken forcemeat
