## Ingredients

- 15 liters raspberries
- 3 liters warm water
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 0.25 kg sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Crush the berries and place them in a crock. Pour the warm water and vinegar over them, and let the berries ferment for about 8 days, or until they float up. Stir the berries once a day.
2. Pour them into cheesecloth to drain. Place the juice with the sugar and let it cook until it is clear and glossy.
3. Bottle it hot or cold; cork the bottles and seal with resin.
