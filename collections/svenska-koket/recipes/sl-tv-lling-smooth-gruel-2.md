## Ingredients

- 5–6 tbsp. flour (50–60 gr.)
- 2 lit. milk
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)
- salt
- sugar
- 2 egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with 1/4 liter of the milk and let it swell for about 2 hours.
2. Melt the butter in a saucepan, pour in the rest of the milk, bring to a boil, and add the flour mixture while whisking.
3. Let the gruel boil with continued whisking for 8–10 minutes.
4. Season with salt and sugar and serve immediately.
5. If desired, add the whisked egg yolks and let the gruel simmer with vigorous whisking.
