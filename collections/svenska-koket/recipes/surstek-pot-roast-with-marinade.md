## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 kg inside or outside round beef
- 3 liters beer or ale
- 6 parts vinegar
- 3 teaspoons white peppercorns
- 3 parts sugar
- 2 hectograms lard
- 3 tablespoons butter for roasting
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 1/2-2 liters boiling water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water and pound it lightly
2. Place in a marinade made of beer, vinegar, sugar, and pepper mixed cold
3. Let the roast rest in this marinade for 12-14 days
4. Remove the roast, rinse quickly, and dry well
5. Lard the roast and cook as an ordinary pot roast
6. Strain and skim the meat gravy, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules
