## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg. kabeljo (salted long cod)
- 2 1/2 lit. water
- butter
- flour
- fish stock
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- fresh parsley
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into 4 pieces and soak in plenty of cold water for 24 hours, changing the water 2 times.
2. Scrape the skin well and place the fish in 2 1/2 lit. cold water and simmer for approximately 1 hour.
3. Lift the fish with a slotted spoon onto a warm dish.
4. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with sauce made of butter, flour, fish stock, 1 raw egg yolk and 2 hard-boiled eggs.
5. Serve with boiled potatoes.
