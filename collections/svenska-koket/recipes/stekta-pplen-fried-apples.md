## Ingredients

- 12 apples
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup finely crushed rusks
- 1 1/2 cups caster sugar
- 8 parts cream
- 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and hollow out the seed cavities, but not all the way through, so that the hollowing has a bottom.
2. Roll the apples in finely crushed rusks, fill them with sugar and place them in a buttered, heat-resistant pie form.
3. Place the remaining butter in small flakes over the apples.
4. Place the form in good oven heat and let the apples fry until they are soft without falling apart.
5. Remove them from the oven and let them cool.
6. Whip the cream with vanilla sugar until stiff peaks form and garnish the apples with it.
7. Serve either in the form or arranged on a glass dish.
