## Ingredients

- 300 gr. butter
- 300 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 3 cups apple sauce
- 250 gr. sweet almond
- 200 gr. granulated sugar
- 3-4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a regular shortcrust pastry from butter, flour, sugar and egg, and let it set.
2. Bake a thin layer of pastry in a ring form or baking pan and let it cool.
3. Line the edges of the form with pastry and fill with the apple sauce.
4. Blanch the almond, grate it and pound in a mortar together with the sugar and egg whites.
5. Spread the almond mass over the apple sauce.
6. Roll out the remaining pastry and cut it into thin strips, which are laid in a lattice over the almond mass.
7. Finally place a strip of pastry around the edge of the tart.
8. Bake at moderate oven heat and serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
