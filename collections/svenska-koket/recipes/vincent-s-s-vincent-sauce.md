## Ingredients

- 15 gr. parsley
- 15 gr. chervil
- 15 gr. tarragon
- 15 gr. chives
- 30 gr. watercress
- 30 gr. spinach
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 tsp salt
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- Real mayonnaise
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the vegetables, place in boiling salted water and let cook 2 minutes
2. Pour into a colander, lower into cold water and let drain well
3. Pound them together with the egg yolks in a mortar until a fine mass, which is passed through a hair sieve and mixed with mayonnaise, see recipe no. 442; which should be very thick
4. Finish the sauce with Worcestershire sauce, taste carefully and serve with cold meat in general and with à-la-daube
