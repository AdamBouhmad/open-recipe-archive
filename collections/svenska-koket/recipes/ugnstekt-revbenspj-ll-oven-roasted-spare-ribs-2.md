## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg spare ribs
- 1 1/2 tablespoon salt (23 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
- 2 tablespoon butter (40 gr.)
- 1/2 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. The spare ribs, which should be thinly cut, are cracked, dried with a cloth, wrung out in hot water, rubbed with a mixture of salt, white pepper and ginger and allowed to rest until the following day.
2. The butter is browned in a long pan, the spare ribs are placed in with the meaty side up and browned nicely, during which it is frequently basted with the fat in the pan.
3. The boiling broth or water is added and the spare ribs are allowed to roast under continued basting, until tender, or approximately 1 1/2 hours.
4. It is cut into nice pieces and served warm or cold, garnished with prunes and parsley.
5. The meat gravy is strained, skimmed, seasoned with meat extract and spices and served in a sauce bowl.
