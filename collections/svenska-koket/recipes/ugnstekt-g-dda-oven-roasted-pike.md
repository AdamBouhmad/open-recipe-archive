## Ingredients

- 2 kg pike
- 1½ tablespoons salt
- 1 egg
- ½ cup crushed rusks
- 2½ tablespoons butter or lard
- ½ liter boiling water or broth

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it and rinse well.
2. Trim the fins and dry the fish with a cloth.
3. Rub it with salt, brush with the beaten egg and sprinkle with the crushed rusks.
4. Heat the butter or lard in a roasting pan; place the fish in it and brown it well.
5. Add the boiling water and let the fish roast until done, basting it often with its sauce.
6. If the fish is roasted in butter, no other sauce is needed than the one the fish was roasted in, thinned with a little cream or broth and flavored with lemon juice.
7. For finer occasions, serve the whole roasted fish with mushroom or crayfish sauce.
8. This method is used for saithe, cod, whitefish, pike-perch and asp.
