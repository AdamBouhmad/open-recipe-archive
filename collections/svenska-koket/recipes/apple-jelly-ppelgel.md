## Ingredients

- 5 litres apples (preferably Gravensteins)
- 2 litres water
- sugar equal in weight to the juice

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the apples, cut them into pieces, removing all dark, damaged and worm-eaten parts.
2. Cook the apple pieces until soft in the water but do not let them fall apart, pour them into cheesecloth and let them drip until the following day.
3. Prepare the jelly thereafter as in the preceding recipe.
