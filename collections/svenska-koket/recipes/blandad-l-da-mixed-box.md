## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 2 hectograms fried or cooked meat
- 1/2 desalted herring
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- For the mold: 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g), 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs and milk well, add the crushed rusks and let the mixture stand for a while so that the rusks swell.
2. Cut the meat and herring into fine dice and stir into the egg mixture together with the parsley.
3. Season the mixture well with the spices, pour it into a well buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie mold and bake for approximately 45 minutes in regular oven heat.
4. Serve as a breakfast dish with clarified butter.
