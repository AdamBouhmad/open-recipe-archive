## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 kkp milk
- 2 msk. crumbled rusks
- 2 hg. fried or cooked meat
- 1/2 drained herring
- 1/2 msk. finely chopped parsley
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- For the mold: 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.), 2 msk. crumbled rusks

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs and milk well, add the crumbled rusks and let the mixture stand for a while so the rusks swell.
2. Cut the meat and herring into fine cubes and stir into the egg mixture together with the parsley.
3. Season the mixture well with the spices, pour into a well-buttered and breadcrumb-lined baking form and bake about 45 minutes in regular oven heat.
4. Serve as a breakfast dish with clarified butter.
