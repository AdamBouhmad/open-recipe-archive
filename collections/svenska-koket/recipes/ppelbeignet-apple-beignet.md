## Ingredients

- 280 gr. sweet almonds
- 12 bitter almonds (about 12 gr.)
- 250 gr. caster sugar
- 2 hg. fine butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 15 gr. wheat flour
- 2 egg whites
- For the apple compote: 2 hg. sugar
- 4 dcl. water
- 2 liters apples
- juice of 1/2 lemon or 1 tbsp. vinegar
- For the form: 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. finely crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven and pass them through an almond mill, then pound them very finely with the sugar in a stone mortar.
2. Melt the butter, let it cool and stir until it is white and creamy, then add the egg yolks one at a time together with the almond paste and flour, and mix the batter well.
3. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Butter a copper form or frying pan with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks, then line it on the bottom and around the edges with some of the batter and fill three-quarters with apple compote.
5. Spread the remaining batter as a lid over the apple compote.
6. Bake the pudding in ordinary oven heat for about 3/4 hour, remove it and let it stand for 1/2 hour before turning it out onto a silver platter or cake plate with paper.
7. Serve lukewarm with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
8. The apple compote is prepared as in no. 820.
