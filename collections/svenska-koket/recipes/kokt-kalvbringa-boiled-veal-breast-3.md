## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg veal breast
- 2 liters water
- 1 tbsp salt
- 6 allspice berries
- 1 piece ginger

## Directions

1. Break and wash the breast with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Place it in boiling water, skim, and add the spices.
3. Let the meat simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until tender, about 2 hours.
4. Remove, cut into appropriately sized pieces, and remove any loose bones.
5. Serve boiled veal breast with crayfish, mushroom, caper, or in the simplest case dill sauce. The sauce is always made from the broth in which the breast was cooked.
