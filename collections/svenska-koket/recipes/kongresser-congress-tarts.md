## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 decilitre water (scant)
- 150 gr. fine butter
- 1 hectogram unscalded sweet almond
- 4 eggs
- 2 hectograms granulated sugar
- 1 decilitre strawberry jam

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as in no. 34, roll out thin, cut with a round measure into cakes, and line small fluted pastry forms with it.
2. For the filling, dry the almond in a cloth and grind it. Whip the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes, then add the almond.
3. Place a little jam in each form, then fill the forms almost to the brim with almond mixture.
4. Bake in a hot oven until the almond mixture feels dry when tested with a whisk.
5. Turn out the pastries, let them cool and serve with coffee or tea.
