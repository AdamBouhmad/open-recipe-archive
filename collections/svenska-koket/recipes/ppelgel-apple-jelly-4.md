## Ingredients

- 5 liters apples, preferably Gravenstein
- 2 liters water
- sugar equal in weight to the juice

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the apples, cut them into pieces, removing all dark, damaged, and wormy parts
2. Cook the apple pieces until soft in the water, but do not let them fall apart
3. Strain through a sieve cloth and let drain until the following day
4. Weigh the juice and dissolve the sugar in the cold juice
5. Bring to a boil and cook rapidly for 15-20 minutes, skimming carefully
6. The jelly is ready when a drop of it, poured onto a plate, sets quickly
