## Ingredients

- 12 apples
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 1 1/2 cups caster sugar
- 2 tbsp butter
- 8 parts cream
- 1 tbsp vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and hollow out the core cavities, but not all the way through, so that the hollowing has a bottom.
2. Roll the apples in finely crushed rusks, fill with sugar and place them in a butter-brushed, heatproof pie mold.
3. Place the remaining butter in small pieces over the apples.
4. Place the mold in good oven heat and let the apples bake until they are soft without falling apart.
5. Remove from the oven and let them cool.
6. Whip the cream with vanilla sugar until stiff peaks form and garnish the apples with it.
7. Serve either in the mold or arranged on a glass dish.
