## Ingredients

- 2 large egg whites
- 150 grams granulated sugar
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks, and continue whipping for 1/4 hour
2. Flavor the meringue mass with a little lemon juice
3. On well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets, place small pointed cakes from the batter, or pipe the meringue mass through a piping bag
4. Bake the meringues in weak oven heat
