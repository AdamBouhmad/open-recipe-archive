## Ingredients

- 2 partridges
- 80 gr butter
- 4 dcl thick cream
- 3 eggs
- 2 tbsp flour (20 gr)
- 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 50 gr truffle
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp breadcrumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Pluck the partridges, remove the innards, rinse well and skin them.
2. Cut all meat from the carcasses and scrape thoroughly free of sinews and membranes.
3. Pass the meat 3 times through the meat grinder, mix with the butter and grind 3 more times.
4. Place the mixture in a bowl and add half the cream bit by bit while stirring vigorously.
5. Pass the mixture through a fine sieve and stir in the egg yolks one at a time, along with the flour and seasonings, and if desired, finely cut truffle.
6. Stir the mixture for 1/2 hour, during which time the remaining cream, whipped until stiff, is added bit by bit.
7. Finally, carefully fold in the egg whites beaten until stiff peaks form.
8. Taste and adjust seasonings.
9. Pour into a well-buttered and breadcrumb-lined ring mold and cook in a water bath in the oven for about 1 1/4 hours.
10. Turn out, garnish with fine vegetables or stuffed tomatoes and serve immediately as a middle course with gravy made from the poultry bones.
11. The same method can be used for soufflé of other game or domestic poultry.
