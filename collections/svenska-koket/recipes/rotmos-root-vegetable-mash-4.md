## Ingredients

- 1 large rutabaga
- 3/4 liter meat broth or pork stock
- 1 liter potatoes
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 1 dcl. cream
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 2 tsp. sugar
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper

## Directions

1. The rutabaga is peeled, cut into cubes, which are placed in cold broth and are allowed to cook until half soft or about 15 minutes.
2. The potatoes are peeled, cut into cubes and placed on top of the rutabaga, after which the root vegetables are allowed to cook until soft.
3. The excess broth is then poured off, and the roots are mashed with a fork.
4. The cold butter is added immediately along with the hot cream, while the mash is worked well with a fork.
5. It is seasoned carefully with spices and served with salt meat, pork or sausage.
