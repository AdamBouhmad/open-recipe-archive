## Ingredients

- 1 medium-sized cabbage head
- 300g boneless beef
- 200g fat/lard
- 3-4 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 1 small tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1 pork belly
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 liter bouillon

## Directions

1. Blanch the cabbage head whole, remove it and let it drain.
2. Hollow it out from the top and fill it with the prepared meat forcemeat, which should be fairly loose.
3. Cover with a few large cabbage leaves and tie it up.
4. Brown it in smoking hot butter.
5. When the cabbage head is browned, remove it, discard the butter, place a pork belly on the bottom of the pot, and lay the cabbage head on top.
6. Pour the boiling bouillon over it and let the dish simmer slowly for at least 2 hours.
7. Serve on a platter with the sauce in a separate sauce bowl.
