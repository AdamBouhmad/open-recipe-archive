## Ingredients

- 1 kg fruit juice
- 1 kg fine caster sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the sugar, warm it in the oven, and while it is still warm, add the juice (prepared as previously described), little by little, whereafter the mass is stirred very slowly in one direction, until the sugar is completely dissolved, or about 1½ hours.
2. Pour the jelly at once into jelly cups or small glass jars, previously rinsed with brandy or vinegar.
3. Bind the jars when the jelly has set.
