## Ingredients

- 125 grams Carolina rice
- 1 liter bouillon
- 0.5 Portuguese onion
- 3 cloves
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 eggs for breading
- 2 cups crushed rusks for breading
- 0.5 kilogram fat for cooking

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice and cook it in the bouillon together with onion, cloves, salt and butter.
2. When they are soft and the porridge fairly stiff, fill it into small water-rinsed molds and set aside to cool.
3. Turn out the pastries, bread them and fry golden brown in fat.
4. Cut out a lid, hollow out the pastry and fill with some ragout, place the lid on, and put the pastries in the oven until they are hot, then serve them.
