## Ingredients

- ½ dozen 2-ore wheat loaves
- 4 eggs
- 8 dl. unscummed milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 parts cognac
- 1 tsp. cinnamon
- ½ tsp. cardamom
- ¼ tsp. crushed cloves
- 1 tsp. salt
- 2 hg. bone marrow
- 1 tbsp. flour
- 1 cup sultana raisins
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks
- ½ tbsp. sugar
- ½ tbsp. butter for the mold
- For sauce: 1 part cognac, 1 part water

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into cubes and soften in a mixture of eggs and milk.
2. Add the spices.
3. Chop the bone marrow finely together with the flour and mix in; likewise the pre-boiled raisins.
4. Pour the batter into a buttered and bread-crumbed mold and bake in the oven for about 1 hour.
5. Mix the cognac, water and sugar together, heat and pour over the pudding, and ignite the sauce.
6. It can also be served in a separate sauce bowl, flaming.
