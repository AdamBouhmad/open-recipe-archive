## Ingredients

- 12 veal cutlets
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1½ cup sour milk
- 1 egg
- ¼ tsp white pepper
- 2 cups crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tbsp butter
- ¼ liter boiling broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the cutlets with a cloth dipped in hot water, trim them, and beat lightly on each side.
2. Sprinkle with pepper and salt, dip them in the beaten egg mixed with sour milk, and roll them in the crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Brown the cutlets in the heated butter, add the liquid, and let them roast slowly for 10 to 15 minutes with the lid closed.
4. Place them on a plate, pour the sauce over them, and garnish with parsley.
5. If desired, the breading (egg and crushed breadcrumbs) can be omitted.
