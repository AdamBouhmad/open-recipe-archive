## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liters apples
- 1 liter water
- 1 2/3 cups crushed sugar
- lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons potato flour
- 1 tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the cores, and cut into wedges.
2. To preserve their color, as they are peeled, place them in water with 1 tablespoon vinegar added.
3. Cook them in boiling water together with the sugar until the apple wedges are soft.
4. Thicken the cream with potato flour mixed in a little cold water, bring to a good boil, taste for seasoning, and pour into a jelly mold or carrot mold to cool.
