## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters good milk
- 1 cup wheat flour
- 1/2 cup potato flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 to 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a thickening paste from the flour and a portion of the milk; let it stand 1 hour to swell.
2. Bring the milk to a boil with the butter and stir in the paste.
3. Let the cream cook 10-15 minutes; add sugar, lemon peel, and the whisked egg yolks.
4. Serve cold, garnished with jam.
