## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. regular oat groats (112 gr.)
- 2 1/4 liters water
- 75 gr. sultana raisins
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Put the groats in the cold water and let boil 1 1/2 hours with a tight lid, without thinning.
2. Rinse the raisins well in lukewarm water, boil them in 2 cups water until they are soft, fully swollen and clear.
3. Pass the groats through a hair sieve, bring the strained soup to boil again with salt and sugar, add the raisins and taste the soup carefully.
4. Serve with the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
