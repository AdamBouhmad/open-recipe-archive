## Ingredients

- 3.5 kg ham
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1.5 tablespoons salt
- 8-10 cloves
- 10 peppercorns
- 10 white peppercorns
- 2 carrots
- 1/4 bunch parsley
- 1/2 liter broth

## Directions

1. Wash the ham with a cloth dipped in hot water, scrape the rind if necessary, and cut—but not too deeply—into diagonal squares and rub in the salt.
2. Insert the cloves into the cuts.
3. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the ham in it with the rind side up and let it brown in strong oven heat.
4. Add the peppercorns, scraped carrots and parsley to the pan, and add the boiling broth a little at a time.
5. Baste the roast every seven minutes and let it roast about 2 hours.
6. Strain the sauce and skim it and serve as gravy.
7. If desired, it can be thickened according to general rules for sauces.
8. The ham should be served with vegetables.
