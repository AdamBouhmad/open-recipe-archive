## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fat herrings
- 3 eggs
- 1 hectogram good butter

## Directions

1. Skin the herring, clean it, rinse it and fillet it, remove the backbone and soak the herring the day before it is to be used.
2. Wrap it in a fish towel and remove all small bones.
