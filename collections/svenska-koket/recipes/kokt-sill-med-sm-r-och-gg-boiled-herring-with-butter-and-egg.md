## Ingredients

- 8 salted herrings
- water
- 3 eggs
- 1.5 hg butter

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water the day before it is to be used.
2. Remove the herring, free it from skin and entrails, and split it down the middle, then remove the backbone and as many small bones as possible.
3. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes, peel and chop finely.
4. Lay the herring in boiling water and let it boil 3-5 minutes, then lay it on a warm platter, alternating with the chopped eggs. The top layer should be eggs.
5. Melt the butter and pour over, and serve the dish with boiled potatoes.
