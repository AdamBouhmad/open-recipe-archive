## Ingredients

- 1-2 salted herrings
- 1 onion
- 6 boiled potatoes
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Soak the herring the day before, clean and remove skin and bones
2. Butter an omelet dish
3. Cut the potatoes into slices and lay one layer on the bottom of the dish; on top of this lay the herring, then the onion, peeled and cut into slices, and on top again a layer of potatoes
4. Sprinkle the crushed rusks over and place the butter in flakes on top
5. Place the dish in the oven to achieve a nice color
