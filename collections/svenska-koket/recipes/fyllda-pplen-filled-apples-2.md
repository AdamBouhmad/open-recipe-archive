## Ingredients

- 12 apples
- 100 gr sweet almonds
- 100 gr caster sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1 1/2 cups crushed rusks
- 2 tbsp caster sugar
- 1 cup melted butter
- 1 tbsp butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them and grind them (or pass through an almond grinder) and pound together with the sugar in a mortar.
2. Beat the egg white until stiff peaks form and fold in.
3. Peel the apples, remove the cores and fill with the almond mixture.
4. Dip them in melted butter, sugar and crushed rusks.
5. Butter a pie mold with some of the butter, place the apples in it, sprinkle with sugar and cover with the remaining butter, distributed in small pieces.
6. Place in the oven and bake until they are light brown and tender.
7. Serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
