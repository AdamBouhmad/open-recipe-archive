## Ingredients

- 3.5 parts flour
- 8 parts water
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 parts clarified butter
- 6 parts cream

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix with sugar.
2. Add the cream with vigorous stirring, then add the clarified but cooled butter, and finally the water.
3. Heat a pancake pan slowly.
4. For each pancake, take approximately 2 tablespoons of batter.
5. When the pancake begins to turn golden brown, roll it on a stick made for that purpose.
