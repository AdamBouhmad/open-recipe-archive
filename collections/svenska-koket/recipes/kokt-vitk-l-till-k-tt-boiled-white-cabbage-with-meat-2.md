## Ingredients

- 1 medium white cabbage head
- 3 liters water, 1 tablespoon salt (15 g) for blanching
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 2 carrots
- 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped Portuguese onion
- 150 g fresh pork, in 1 slice
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 deciliters boiling water

## Directions

1. Remove all green leaves from the cabbage head; loosen the remaining leaves from the core and remove the thickest part of the central vein.
2. Blanch the cabbage leaves (place in boiling salted water and let cook 5–8 minutes without a lid), remove with a slotted spoon, and drain on cloth.
3. Roll the leaves into balls, which are twisted in a cloth.
4. Grease a pot with the butter, and cover the bottom with sliced carrots and onion and the pork slice.
5. Place the cabbage balls on top, sprinkle with seasonings, add the water, and let cook slowly with a lid until quite tender, or about 2.5 hours.
6. Serve as a garnish around meat or on a separate dish.
