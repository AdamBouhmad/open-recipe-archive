## Ingredients

- 2 liters green, unripe tomatoes
- 1 kg. granulated sugar
- 4 dcl. vinegar
- 3 dcl. vinegar
- 15 cloves
- 1/4 pod Spanish pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the tomatoes, prick them with a small silver fork, blanch in boiling water until they feel soft when tested with a whisk, then lay them on linen to drain.
2. When they are cold, place them in a jar.
3. From the sugar, vinegar and spices, cook a liquid which is strained hot over the tomatoes.
4. Cover the jar with a doubled cloth, place a plate and weight on top, and let the contents stand for three days, after which the liquid is strained off, brought to a boil and poured hot over the fruit again.
5. Proceed in this manner two more times with six days in between; the last time let the liquid cool before pouring it on.
6. The liquid should stand well over the tomatoes.
7. Bind the jar well closed.
