## Ingredients

- 1 old chicken
- 1 kg veal knuckle
- 1 kg veal neck
- 4 liters water
- 10 g salt
- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 1/4 celeriac
- 1 small leek
- 200 g boneless lean veal for clarification
- 1 egg white and rinsed eggshell for clarification

## Directions

1. Pluck the chicken, singe it, remove the entrails, rinse it and let it soak in water for 12 hours.
2. Dry it with a kitchen towel and cut it into smaller pieces.
3. Rinse the veal quickly, dry it, cut it into small pieces and crack the bones well.
4. Cook the broth as in recipe 222.
5. Clarify it in the same manner as described in recipe 223, and use it for light soups.
