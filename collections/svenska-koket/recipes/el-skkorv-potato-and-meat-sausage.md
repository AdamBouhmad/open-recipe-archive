## Ingredients

- 3 lit. halvkokt potatis (2 kg.)
- 1 kg. benfritt oxkött
- 3 kg. magert småfläsk
- 6 hg. späckfläsk
- 5 msk. salt (75 gr.)
- 2 tsk. vitpeppar (6 gr.)
- 1 liten msk. socker (15 gr.)
- 1/3 tsk. salpeter (1 gr.)
- 1 1/2 lit. kallt vatten
- omkr. 6 m. blå- eller krokskinn
- For rubbing: 4 msk. salt, 1 msk. socker, 1/4 tsk. salpeter

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes the day before they are to be used, but do not peel them then.
2. Peel the potatoes and pass them once through a meat grinder.
3. Dry the beef and pork with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and pass through the meat grinder 3 times, the last time together with the ground potatoes.
4. Scrape the fat and cut half of it into fine pieces.
