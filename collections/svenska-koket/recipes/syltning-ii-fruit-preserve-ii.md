## Ingredients

- 2 kg. berries
- 2 kg. loaf sugar
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Treat the berries as in the previous recipe.
2. Chop the sugar into small pieces, place in the pot, and add the water.
3. When the sugar is nearly melted, cook it with skimming to a clear syrup, which is ready when it threads between the fingers.
4. Remove the pot from heat, add the berries and shake them down well in the syrup, then return the pot to heat and let the preserve simmer gently, shaking the pot often and skimming the preserve until it is clear or about half an hour.
5. Treat it as in the previous recipe.
