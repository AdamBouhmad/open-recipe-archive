## Ingredients

- 210 gr. butter
- 210 gr. granulated sugar
- 2 eggs
- 225 gr. wheat flour
- 7 parts cream
- ½ vanilla pod
- 1 - 2 tbsp. granulated sugar
- 1 tsp. potato flour
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare the shortcrust pastry in the usual manner.
2. Boil the cream with the vanilla and sugar until it has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor.
3. Beat the cream with the egg yolks whisked together with the potato flour, and let the cream simmer over the heat until it thickens.
4. Continue whisking until the cream has cooled.
5. Line small fluted molds with shortcrust pastry and fill with vanilla cream.
6. Place the pastries in the oven to bake through, and as soon as they are done, turn them out immediately.
