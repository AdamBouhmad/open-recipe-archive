## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg cod (salted dried)
- 2 1/2 liters water
- butter
- flour
- fish stock
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- fresh parsley
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into 4 pieces and soak in cold water for 24 hours, changing the water 2 times
2. Scrape the skin well
3. Place the fish in 2 1/2 liters cold water and simmer for approximately 1 hour
4. Transfer with a skimmer to a warm platter
5. Garnish with fresh parsley
6. Prepare sauce from butter, flour, fish stock, 1 raw egg yolk and 2 hard-boiled eggs
7. Serve with boiled potatoes
