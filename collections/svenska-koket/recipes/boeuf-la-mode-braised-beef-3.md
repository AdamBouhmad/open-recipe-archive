## Ingredients

- 2 kg boneless inner loin
- 3 hg lard
- 1 tbsp salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr.)
- 1 1/4 liters beef broth
- 1 kg calf's feet
- 6 peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- 3 carrots
- 2 small Portuguese onions
- 1 dcl French wine
- 1/2 dcl cognac

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat, which should have hung until properly tender, and lard it with 2 hg of the lard (see page 232), rub it with the remaining spices, and tie it so it has a nice shape.
2. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, place the meat in and brown on all sides.
