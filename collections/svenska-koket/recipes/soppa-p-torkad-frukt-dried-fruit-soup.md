## Ingredients

- 1 hg. dried apples
- 1 hg. dried pears
- 1/2 hg. raisins
- 2 lit. water
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm.)
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 1 1/2-2 kkp. crushed sugar
- 25 gr. rice or potato flour
- 1/2 dcl. sherry or French wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit very well in lukewarm water and let it soak overnight in the water in which it shall be cooked.
2. Set it on to boil together with the cinnamon, lemon peel, and sugar, and let it cook until it is soft.
3. Thicken the soup with the rice flour, stirred in 1/2 kkp cold water, and let it cook 5 minutes.
4. Season it with the wine and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and rusks or biscuits.
