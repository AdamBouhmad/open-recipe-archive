## Ingredients

- 350 gr. flour
- 3 hg. butter
- 100-125 gr. sugar
- 1 egg
- 25 gr. sweet almond

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl, distribute the butter, which should be washed, in flakes in the flour, add the sugar, the beaten egg, the blanched and ground almond and work the dough quickly with the fingertips until it becomes smooth, then let it rest in a cold place for a couple of hours.
2. It can then be rolled out for cake bottoms or used for simpler cakes and fruit pies.
