## Ingredients

- 15 good apples
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 3 egg whites
- 6 tablespoons granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut into wedges; remove the yellow zest of the lemon neatly and press out the juice
2. Place the apples in a saucepan with the butter, sugar, lemon juice and lemon zest and shake over heat until the apples are soft
3. Place them in a buttered pie form, which should only be filled halfway
4. Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form, gently fold in the sugar, and spread the meringue batter over the apples
5. Place the pie form in a mild oven heat and let it stand until the meringue is light yellow
6. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream
