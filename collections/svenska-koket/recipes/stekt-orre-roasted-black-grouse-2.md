## Ingredients

- 2 black grouse
- 1 hectogram lard
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ¼-1 liter thin cream or broth
- ½ tablespoon butter
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- meat dripping
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck the birds, singe, clean out, rinse well and dry with a cloth. If the birds have been frozen, soak them in milk overnight. Lard them with lard cut into fine strips and truss.
2. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the birds in it with the breasts up and brown well on all sides. Then salt them and add cream or broth. Roasting takes about 1 hour.
3. Strain and skim the meat dripping, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules.
