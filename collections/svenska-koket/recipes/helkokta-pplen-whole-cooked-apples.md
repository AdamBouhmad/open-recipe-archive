## Ingredients

- 12 apples
- 2 cups caster sugar
- 6 cups water

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the blossom end, scrape the stem and remove it halfway.
2. Make a clear syrup from the sugar and water.
3. Place the apples in it and let them cook until they are clear and loose, then place them on a jelly dish.
4. Reduce the sugar syrup and pour it over the apples.
5. Serve preferably cold with whipped cream.
