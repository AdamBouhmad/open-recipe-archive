## Ingredients

- Approx. 2 kg. salmon
- 30 gr. coarse salt
- 2 gr. saltpeter
- 10 gr. granulated sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon coarsely crushed white pepper
- 1 small bunch dill

## Directions

1. From a salmon weighing 3–4 kg., cut a piece crosswise through the middle.
2. Clean and scrape the salmon piece well, carefully remove the backbone so that it becomes two equal pieces, and dry these with a cloth wrung out in cold water.
3. Pound the salt and saltpeter very finely and mix with the sugar.
4. Sprinkle the salmon pieces with the salt mixture and rub lightly with it.
5. If desired, add pepper and some dill sprigs.
6. Place the salmon pieces together with the meat sides against each other so that the thick side of the salmon is placed against the thin side.
7. Place it thereafter in a mild press between two cutting boards overnight and the salmon is then ready.
8. Cut into 1/2 cm thick slices, which are arranged beautifully on a platter, garnish with salmon skin, toasted on the stove or fried, and fresh dill sprigs.
9. Serve with oil, vinegar, granulated sugar, French mustard, salt and pepper.
