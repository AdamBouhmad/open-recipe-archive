## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 dcl. clear, simmering sugar syrup
- 1 dcl. champagne or white port wine
- 2-8 beautiful, good fresh apples
- 2-3 good, loose fresh pears
- 2-3 bananas
- 2 oranges
- 1 1/2 hg. blue or green grapes
- 6 cloudberries (compote)
- 3 tbsp. cherry jam or compote

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar syrup (see sugar syrup for compote) with the wine in a compote dish.
2. Peel the apples and pears, cut them into even, beautiful segments, remove the cores, and place them in the syrup.
3. Peel the bananas, cut them into 1/2 cm thick slices on the diagonal.
4. Peel the oranges, carefully remove the white membrane, divide them into segments or cut into slices, remove the seeds, and place together with the bananas in the compote dish.
5. Remove the grapes from the stems, rinse, dry in a cloth, and mix together with the cloudberries, plums, and cherries with the remaining fruit.
6. Gently stir the compote and let it stand on ice for approximately 2 hours.
7. Serve with biscuits or almond flakes.
