## Ingredients

- 1.5 deciliters granulated sugar (128 g)
- 2 deciliters water
- 1 liter blueberries

## Directions

1. Cook a thin, clear syrup from the water and sugar
2. Clean the blueberries, rinse in a sieve, and drain well
3. Place in the sugar syrup and cook for 4-5 minutes
4. Remove with a slotted spoon and place in a warm compote bowl
5. Cook the syrup until it is syrupy and pour immediately over the berries
6. Serve cold with whipped cream
