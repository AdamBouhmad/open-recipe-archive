## Ingredients

- 2 kg prime rib or English roast beef
- 1 hg lean smoked ham
- 1½ tbsp butter
- ⅛ tbsp salt
- ¾-1 liter broth
- 2 parts Madeira or Malaga wine

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and lard it thereafter with the smoked ham cut into fine strips.
2. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the meat in and brown it well on all sides, whereupon it is salted, and the boiling broth and wine are added.
3. Let the roast thereafter cook about 2-3 hours.
4. Serve with Spanish sauce.
