## Ingredients

- 1 kg. small pickling onions
- 2 liters water
- 2 tsp. salt for blanching
- 2 liters vinegar
- 1 hg. granulated sugar
- 3 gr. ginger (optional)
- 2 gr. mace (optional)
- 2 gr. white pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the unpeeled onions in boiling salted water, let them cook for 2 minutes, remove and place immediately in cold water.
2. Peel them and let them lie in salted water (2 liters water, 2 tbsp. salt) for about 6 hours.
3. Heat 1 liter of the vinegar, add the onions and let them cook—small onions 5 minutes, larger ones 8-10 minutes.
4. If using spices, cook them for a few minutes in the vinegar and strain the brine before adding the onions; however, the onions will be whiter if the spices are omitted.
5. Place the onions in a jar, cover with a quadruple cloth and a plate and let stand until the following day, when the vinegar is drained off.
6. Cook the remaining vinegar with the sugar and let it cool.
7. Fill the onions into glass jars, pour the cold vinegar over them and it should stand well above the onions.
8. Bind the jars well and store in a cool place.
