## Ingredients

- 1 twelve-öre wheat bread
- 100 grams butter

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into thin slices, and spread these with butter.
2. Put the sandwiches together two by two.
3. Spread thinly with butter on the outer sides as well, then brown them in the oven.
4. Serve with meat, fish and vegetarian soups.
