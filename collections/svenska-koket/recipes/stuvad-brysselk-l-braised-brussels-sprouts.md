## Ingredients

- 1 liter brussels sprouts
- 3/4 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 4-5 deciliters boiling broth
- 1-2 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 g)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 g)

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the cabbage as in the previous recipe and let it drain on cloth.
2. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes.
3. Add the broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
4. Stir in the beaten egg yolk, let the sauce simmer, add the cabbage, and season the braise with spices.
5. Serve garnished with browned potatoes as a main course or with fried meat.
6. Brussels sprouts may also be braised as white cabbage.
