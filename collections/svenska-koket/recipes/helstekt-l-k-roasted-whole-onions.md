## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg small Portuguese onions
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 60 g butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 g)

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, blanch them (place in boiling salted water and let cook 10 minutes), place on cloth, and let drain.
2. Fry the butter in a pan, add the onions, sprinkle with sugar, and let them slowly fry covered until they are beautifully brown and completely soft, which is tested with a whisk.
3. Serve with the smorgasbord or with roasted meat such as roast beef, roast mutton, and lamb saddle.
