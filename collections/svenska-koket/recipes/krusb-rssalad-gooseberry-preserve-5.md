## Ingredients

- 2 kg. gooseberries (approximately 3 liters)
- 1 1/2 kg. lump sugar
- 3 deciliters water
- 2 deciliters vinegar
- 10 cloves

## Directions

1. The gooseberries should be green, fully grown but unripe.
2. Clean them, rinse them and let them drain on linen.
3. Cook a clear, syrupy liquid from the sugar, water, vinegar and cloves.
4. Add the berries and cook as in no. 1031.
