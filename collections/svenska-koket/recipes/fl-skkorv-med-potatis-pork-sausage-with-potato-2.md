## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef (outside thigh)
- 1 kg pork
- 1 kg bacon
- 1.5 kg boiled, grated potato
- 1.5-2 liters boiled, cooled milk
- 3 tablespoons salt
- ½ tablespoon ground white pepper
- ½ tablespoon ground allspice pepper
- ¼ tablespoon grated ginger
- 5 meters intestines (curved casing)
- 2 tablespoons salt for rubbing
- 2 tablespoons sanding sugar
- 1 teaspoon potassium nitrate for rubbing

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water, cut into pieces removing sinews and membranes, and grind twice through a meat grinder
2. Cut the bacon into fine cubes, or grind it together with the meat
3. Half-cook the potato, peel and grate or press through a potato ricer
4. Boil and cool the milk
5. Work the meat well together with spices, potato and milk, which is added gradually, while the mixture is worked for 1 hour
6. Mix in the bacon cubes cut into pieces towards the end of the hour
7. Stuff the mixture into well-cleaned, narrow pig intestines
8. Rinse the sausages with cold water and rub with a mixture of salt, sugar and saltpeter
9. Let rest until the following day, then place in brine
