## Ingredients

- 1 liter leftover porridge of grain, oats, or rice
- 1.5 deciliters wheat flour or crushed rusks
- 2.5 tablespoons butter (50 g)

## Directions

1. Cut the porridge into 2 cm thick slices
2. Turn the slices in flour or crushed rusks
3. Fry slowly until beautifully golden brown in the butter
4. Arrange on a warm plate
5. Serve with milk
