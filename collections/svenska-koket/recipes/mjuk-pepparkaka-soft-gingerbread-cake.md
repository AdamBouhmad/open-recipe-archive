## Ingredients

- 4 cups wheat flour
- 4 cups brown sugar
- 2 tbsp. chopped orange peel
- 1 tbsp. ground cloves
- 1 tbsp. ground cinnamon
- 1 tsp. bicarbonate (or 1 1/2 tsp. baking powder)
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups buttermilk
- 8 tbsp. clarified butter
- 1/2 tbsp. washed butter for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Boil the orange peel, cut away the white part, and finely chop the outer part of the peel.
2. Mix the sifted flour, sugar, the finely ground spices, orange peel and bicarbonate well together.
3. Whisk the eggs, and add the buttermilk.
4. Stir the milk and eggs down into the dry mixture. One should stir slowly.
5. Last add the clarified butter.
6. When everything is well mixed, pour the batter into a rusk-strewn mold or baking pan and bake in not too strong oven heat for 30-40 minutes.
