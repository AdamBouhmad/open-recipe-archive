## Ingredients

- 1 rutabaga
- 1 tbsp. butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- 2½ liters broth
- salt
- sugar (optional)
- white pepper
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash, peel and cut the rutabaga into cubes. Fry butter and flour together, and dilute with 1 liter of the broth. Add the roots and let them cook until soft, after which they are passed through a sieve.
2. Pour the passed mass back into the soup pot and add the remaining broth. Bring the purée to a good boil, skim and season.
3. Whip the cream in the soup bowl, and pour in the soup with vigorous whisking.
