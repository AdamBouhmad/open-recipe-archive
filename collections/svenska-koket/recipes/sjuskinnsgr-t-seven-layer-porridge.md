## Ingredients

- 1 liter leftover rice grain or barley grain porridge
- 50 gr. sifted sugar
- 2 tsp. ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the dish

## Directions

1. Butter a fireproof dish with the butter.
2. Spread a layer of porridge on it and sprinkle thickly with sugar mixed with cinnamon.
3. Heat a pancake spatula until glowing and use it to brown the sugar, then spread another layer of porridge on top, sprinkle with sugar and brown.
4. Continue in the same way until there are 7 layers, which is where the dish gets its name.
5. Do not brown the sugar on the top layer with the pancake spatula; instead place the dish in a hot oven so that the sugar becomes nicely brown and the porridge is heated through.
6. Serve with milk.
