## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 g)
- 4 deciliters thin cream
- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 g)
- zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 deciliters sugar for the mold
- 2 tablespoons boiling water for the mold

## Directions

1. Place the sugar in a buttered, slightly warm frying pan and brown lightly, then add the boiling water, and use this mixture to coat a fireproof mold.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cream, and let the mixture boil for 5 minutes, then pour it out and let it cool.
3. Stir in the egg yolks one at a time together with the sugar, the finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice, and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
4. Finally, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the mixture into the coated mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
6. Serve the pudding immediately when it is ready with vanilla, lemon, or fruit sauce. If desired, the pudding can be turned out.
