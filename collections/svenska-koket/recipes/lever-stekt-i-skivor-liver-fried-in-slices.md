## Ingredients

- 1 small calf liver
- 1/2 kg. salted pork belly
- 1 liter milk
- 1 kkp wheat flour
- salt, white pepper
- 2 msk. butter
- 3 msk. butter for garnish
- 2 msk. chopped parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Wash the liver, remove the membranes and blood vessels, and slice it.
2. Place the slices in milk for 1 hour and then pat dry in a kitchen towel.
3. Turn them in a mixture of flour, salt and white pepper.
4. Fry the slices until nicely browned in butter and arrange on a serving platter.
5. Scrape the pork belly, cut it into thin slices and fry it.
6. Place a slice of pork on each liver slice.
7. Garnish the dish with parsley butter and serve.
