## Ingredients

- 60 gr butter
- 50 gr flour
- 1/4 liter unskinned milk
- 5 eggs
- 100 gr caster sugar
- 50 gr cocoa powder
- 1 tbsp butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and flour together, add the milk while stirring vigorously, and let the mixture come to a good boil.
2. Stir the egg yolks and sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy; blend in the cocoa along with the cooked mixture.
3. Lastly fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Immediately pour the mixture into a mold brushed with cold butter, cover with buttered paper, place in a water bath and let simmer slowly for about 1/3 hour.
5. Turn out and serve warm with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
