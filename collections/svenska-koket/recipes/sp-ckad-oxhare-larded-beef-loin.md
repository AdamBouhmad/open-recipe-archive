## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef loin
- 1-1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 liter broth or water
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 2 teaspoons tomato purée

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water
