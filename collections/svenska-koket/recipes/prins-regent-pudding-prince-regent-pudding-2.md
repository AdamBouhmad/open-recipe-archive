## Ingredients

- 125 grams butter
- 125 grams sugar
- 6 eggs
- 125 grams grated dark bread (pumpernickel)
- 50 grams sweet almonds
- ground cinnamon
- ground cloves
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy.
2. Add the egg yolks, grated bread, chopped almonds, and spices to taste.
3. Carefully fold in the beaten egg whites.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered and bread-crumbed mold and bake in a water bath on the stove for 2 hours.
5. Serve the pudding warm with foam sauce.
