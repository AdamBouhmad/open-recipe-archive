## Ingredients

- 8 hectograms coarse rye flour
- 4 hectograms wheat flour
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 tablespoons crushed fennel
- 1 liter cold milk
- 2 coffee cups syrup
- 80 grams pressed yeast

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little cold milk.
2. Sift the rye flour and wheat flour and mix together with the spices in a large bowl.
3. Stir the cold milk into the flour, and work the dough until it is supple and shiny.
4. Then add the syrup and the dissolved yeast, and work everything together thoroughly.
5. Sprinkle a little flour over the dough, cover it with a cloth and let it stand in ordinary room temperature to rise for about 24 hours.
6. Work the well-risen dough and place it in buttered and floured bread molds.
7. Fill the molds to just under half and place in a warm room to rise.
8. When the dough has risen to the edge of the mold, place the molds in a good oven heat, and let the breads bake for 2 1/2–3 hours.
9. After the bread has been in the oven for 1 hour, maintain the fire with only one piece of wood at a time.
10. Test the breads with a whisk and turn them out when they have cooled somewhat.
