## Ingredients

- 425 grams asparagus
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoon flour
- 2 liters good broth
- salt
- sugar (optional)
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Remove the asparagus tips and set aside to be blanched later and placed in the soup bowl.
2. Cut the asparagus, blanch it in salted water and let it drain.
3. Mix butter and flour together, add half of the broth, add the asparagus and let it simmer slowly until it is tender.
4. Strain the soup, add the rest of the broth, and let the soup come to a boil.
5. Season with spices and thicken with egg yolks and cream.
