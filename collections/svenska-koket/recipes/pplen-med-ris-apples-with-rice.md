## Ingredients

- 1 cup Carolina rice
- 3 liters boiling water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 1/2 liters apples
- 1 cup caster sugar
- 2 cups water
- 2 small egg whites
- 4 tablespoons caster sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice in lukewarm water and place it on the stove in the boiling water with salt.
2. Let the rice cook vigorously for about 30 minutes, then drain it in a colander.
3. Set it aside from the heat to dry.
4. Stir the rice from time to time with a fork so that it does not stick together.
5. Peel the apples, cut them into wedges and remove the seed cavities.
6. Make a clear syrup from the sugar and water, place the apples in it and let them cook until soft.
7. Carefully place them in the middle of a dish and reduce the sugar syrup for a few minutes, then pour it over the fruit.
8. Arrange the cooked rice in a ring around the apples.
9. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, mix with the sugar and spread over the apples.
10. Place the dish in weak oven heat and let it stand until the meringue has taken on a light yellow color.
11. Serve the dish preferably cold with whipped cream.
