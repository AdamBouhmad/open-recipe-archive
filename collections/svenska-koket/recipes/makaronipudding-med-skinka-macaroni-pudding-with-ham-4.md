## Ingredients

- 150 gr. ideal macaroni
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 4 hg. smoked ham
- 4 eggs, 3 teacups milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold, 2 tablespoons crushed bread crusts

## Directions

1. Rinse the macaroni, put it in boiling salted water and let it cook for 15-20 minutes.
2. Drain it in a colander and let the water drain well.
3. Cut the ham into small cubes, mix well with the macaroni and place the mixture in a buttered and bread-strewn pie mold.
4. Whisk the eggs well with the milk and spices, and pour the egg mixture over the dish in the mold.
5. Bake the pudding in ordinary oven heat for about 45 minutes.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish.
