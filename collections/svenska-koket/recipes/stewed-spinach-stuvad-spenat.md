## Ingredients

- 1 kg spinach
- 4 liters water for blanching
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 g)
- 2-3 deciliters cream, milk or broth
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 g)
- 2 hard-boiled eggs for garnish

## Directions

1. Treat and blanch the spinach as in the preceding recipe, place in a colander, immediately rinse with cold water, which is carefully pressed out with a spoon.
2. Chop the spinach; when it is half done, add the flour and finish chopping the spinach. (If desired, pass it afterwards through a sieve.)
3. Melt the butter in a pot, add the spinach and fry it well with the butter, pour on the boiling cream or broth, and let the stew simmer, with stirring, 5 minutes.
4. Season the spinach with sugar and more salt if needed, place on carrot, garnish with the hard-boiled eggs, cut into wedges or finely chopped, separating the white and yolk.
5. Serve with fried fish, boiled ham and fried meat.
