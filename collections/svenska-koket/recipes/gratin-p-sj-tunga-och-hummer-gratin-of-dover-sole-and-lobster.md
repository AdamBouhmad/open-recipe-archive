## Ingredients

- 4 Dover soles, approximately 5 hg each
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt (23 gr)
- 2 tbsp butter (40 gr) for cooking
- fish stock for cooking
- 2 tbsp butter (40 gr) for lobster stew
- 6 tbsp flour (60 gr)
- 6 dcl fish stock
- 3-4 egg yolks
- 1 jar lobster (1/2 liter)
- salt
- white pepper
- 3 balls crayfish butter (30 gr)
- 6 tbsp flour (60 gr)
- 7 dcl fish stock
- 2-3 egg yolks
- 4 tbsp grated cheese
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks
- 60 gr butter

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the Dover soles as in recipe no. 371
2. For the lobster stew, fry butter and flour for 2 minutes
3. Add fish stock and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes
4. Add the whisked egg yolks and let the sauce simmer while whisking
5. Carefully blend in the lobster cut into pieces, so that it does not fall apart, and season the stew with the spices
6. Butter one or two ovenproof dishes, or preferably silver dishes, with cold butter
7. Layer the fillets and stew alternately, with the first and last layer being fillets
8. Pour the sauce over and sprinkle the cheese, well mixed with the crushed rusks, on top, then pour the melted butter over everything
9. Gratinate the dish as in the previous recipe and serve with small boiled potatoes or mashed potatoes
10. The sauce is prepared as in recipe no. 371. For finer gratin, use fresh lobster
