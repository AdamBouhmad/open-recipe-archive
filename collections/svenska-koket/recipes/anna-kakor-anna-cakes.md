## Ingredients

- 210 grams sweet almond
- 315 grams granulated sugar
- 2 egg whites
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds, grate or grind them through an almond mill and pound with the sugar in a mortar
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and work into the almond mass
3. Roll the mass out to finger-thick sticks, which are placed on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets
4. Along each stick make a groove, which is filled with jelly
5. Bake in very weak oven heat and cut, while they are still warm, diagonally into pieces
