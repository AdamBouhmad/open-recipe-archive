## Ingredients

- 2 litres water
- 1 piece cinnamon
- ½ cup red sago grains
- 1–1½ cup crushed sugar
- 1 cup raisins
- 1½ cup sour lingonberry juice

## Directions

1. Bring the water to a boil with the cinnamon, rinse the sago grains and add them; likewise add the cleaned and rinsed raisins.
2. Add the sugar and juice, and let the soup cook until the grains are clear.
3. Taste and serve with rusks.
