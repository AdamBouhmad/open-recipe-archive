## Ingredients

- 1 small cabbage head
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1-2 tablespoons syrup
- 2.5 liters broth
- 12 allspice berries
- 6 white peppercorns
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves of the cabbage head, loosen the remaining leaves from the stalk, and cut away the thickest part of the central vein, then cut the cabbage into fine cubes.
2. Brown the butter in a pot, add the cabbage with the syrup and brown with frequent stirring until it has a nice light brown color.
3. Add the broth with the spices, and let the soup cook for about 3 hours with a tightly closed lid.
4. Season and serve with meatballs, sausage or pork.
