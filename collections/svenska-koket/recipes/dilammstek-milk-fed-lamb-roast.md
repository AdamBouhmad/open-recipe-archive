## Ingredients

- 1 1/2–2 kg milk-fed lamb roast
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1/2 liter meat stock or broth

## Directions

1. Milk-fed lamb—the still nursing young of sheep—has white, very fine-grained meat and requires short cooking time.
2. The loin and half kidney roast, attached together, should be used to obtain sufficient weight.
3. Treat, roast and serve as in recipe no. 531 but should not be larded.
4. The loin is prepared as lamb loin (see recipe no. 535).
