## Ingredients

- 4 oranges
- 1 dcl. caster sugar (85 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon fine jam
- 50 gr. biscuits

## Directions

1. Pour gradually, with vigorous stirring, into the cream.
2. Pour the cream into a compote bowl, let it set on ice and garnish with jam.
3. Serve with biscuits or almond shavings.
