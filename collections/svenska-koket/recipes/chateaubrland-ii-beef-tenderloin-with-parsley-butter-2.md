## Ingredients

- 6 hg. beef filet
- 3 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)

## Directions

1. Dry the filet with a cloth wrung out in hot water. Remove all outer sinews and membranes carefully, then cut the filet into 6 slices, which are beaten lightly with a damp mallet and rubbed on both sides with the spice mixture.
