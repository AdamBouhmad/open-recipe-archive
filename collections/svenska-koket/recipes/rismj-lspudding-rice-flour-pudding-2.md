## Ingredients

- 1 liter whole milk
- 100 grams rice flour
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 5 eggs
- 10 sweet almonds
- 5 bitter almonds
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar for the mold
- 2 tablespoons boiling water for the mold

## Directions

1. Caramelize the sugar in a frying pan, dilute with the boiling water, and use this to line a mold.
2. Bring the milk to a boil, whisk in the rice flour, and let the mixture cook for 10-15 minutes while stirring.
3. Pour the mixture into a bowl to cool.
4. Add the cold butter, whisked eggs, blanched and grated almonds, and sugar, and stir well.
5. Pour into the prepared mold, cover with buttered paper, and bake in a water bath in the oven.
6. When done, remove it, let it cool, and turn it out.
