## Ingredients

- 1 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g) for frying
- 1.5 deciliters finely crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rinse and brush the Jerusalem artichokes thoroughly, then scrape them and place gradually in vinegar-mixed water (see page 338) to prevent darkening.
2. Rinse again, place in boiling salted water, and simmer until tender, testing with a whisk.
3. Drain in a colander and cool on a cloth.
4. Shape into large olives with a knife or smooth spoon mold.
5. Dip in beaten egg, turn in crushed rusks, and fry to a light golden-brown in butter.
6. Serve as a garnish around roasted meat or together with other vegetables.
