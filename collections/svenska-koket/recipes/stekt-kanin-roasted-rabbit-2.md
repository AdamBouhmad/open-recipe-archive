## Ingredients

- 1 skinned rabbit weighing about 3 kg
- 1 1/2 hg lard
- 1 tbsp salt (15 gr)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 4 tbsp butter (80 gr)
- 2 dcl broth
- 5 dcl cream or milk
- 1 tbsp soy

## Directions

1. Rabbit is not considered game in our region but belongs to domestic animals. The meat has a pleasant firm, somewhat sweet taste and stands in nutritional value close to beef.
2. After the rabbit is slaughtered, it should hang 2-6 days, depending on the season, so the meat becomes tender.
