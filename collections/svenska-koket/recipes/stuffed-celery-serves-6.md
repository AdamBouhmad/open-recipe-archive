## Ingredients

- 2 heads of celery
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 2 hg beef
- 1/2 hg veal
- 1/2 hg lard
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 chopped pickled onions or 1/2 tablespoon chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 egg
- 2 dcl cream
- For browning: 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr), 3 dcl broth

## Directions

1. Peel the celery, rinse well, place in boiling salted water and let cook for about 1 hour, or until almost tender, then remove and let cool.
2. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and grind together with the lard 3 times through a meat grinder.
3. Add flour, egg, seasonings and cream little by little, and work the forcemeat for about 1/2 hour.
4. Stir in the onion and taste the forcemeat.
5. Cut a lid out from the top of the celery and hollow it out from above. Fill with forcemeat, put the lid on and tie the celery with twine previously dipped in hot water.
6. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the celery and brown nicely, dilute with broth and let braise slowly with the lid tightly closed for about 1 1/2 hours.
7. Place on a warm platter, cut off the twine and pour the sauce over it. Serve with the smörgåsbord.
