## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 hectogram boneless beef
- 1/2 hectogram boneless veal
- 30 gr. fat
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon pounded rusks
- 1 teaspoon salt
- white pepper
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped pickled onion
- 4 tablespoons cream
- 1 egg for coating
- 1 cup pounded rusks for coating
- 1/2 kilogram meat broth for cooking

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for 5 minutes, cool in cold water, and peel.
2. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut it together with the fat into pieces and pass 5 times through a meat grinder.
3. Pound it with a wooden pestle until the mixture becomes even and smooth.
4. Beat the egg yolks well with the cream and work them gradually into the mixture, together with the rusks, seasonings, and pickled onion. Taste and adjust seasoning.
5. Coat the peeled eggs with the meat mixture, roll in pounded rusks, then in the beaten egg, and again in pounded rusks. Flatten the mixture at both ends and let rest for 1 hour.
