## Ingredients

- 150 gr. semolina
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 1/4 lit. milk
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 2 msk. sugar (30 gr.)
- 20 sweet almonds, 10 bitter almonds
- 40 gr. raisins
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Treat the semolina and cook the porridge as in recipe 791, but it will be ready after 20 min. Pour out and add the remaining butter immediately along with the seasonings, the blanched and ground almonds, and the well-rinsed, blanched and pitted raisins.
2. When the porridge is cold, fold in the whisked egg yolks and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered and bread-crumb-lined mold and bake in regular oven heat for about 1 hour.
4. Serve immediately upon removal from the oven, with jam, compote or fruit sauce.
