## Ingredients

- 80 fine even sugar beets
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 1/2 lemon
- 2 liters water for soaking
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- butter for serving

## Directions

1. Scrape the sugar beets, rinse well and let them soak in vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar) for 1 hour.
2. Tie them together, 6 in each bundle, place in boiling salted water with the juice of 1/2 lemon and let them cook until soft, which is tested with a whisk handle.
3. Remove with a slotted spoon, arrange on an oblong plate with a broken napkin and serve very hot with stirred butter or hollandaise mixed with the juice of 1 blood orange.
