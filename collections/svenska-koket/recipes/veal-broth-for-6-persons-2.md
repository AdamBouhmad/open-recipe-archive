## Ingredients

- 1 veal knuckle
- 1.5 kg veal shoulder or brisket
- 4 liters water
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 1 carrot
- 1 small celery
- 0.25 leek

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut it into small pieces.
2. Place it in the cold water together with the salt, bring to a boil and skim well.
3. Add the cleaned vegetables, let the broth simmer for 3-4 hours, and clarify it afterwards in the usual way.
4. If a finer broth is desired, 1 kg of the veal meat may be omitted, but instead 1 chicken should be used.
