## Ingredients

- 1 twenty-five öre spiced bread
- 1½ liters apples
- 1½ cups sugar
- 2 hg. butter
- 7½ tbsp. butter (for the form)
- 3 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The bread, which should not be too fresh, is grated and fried in two-thirds of the butter.
2. Peel the apples, cut them into pieces and cook together with the sugar and a little water into a puree.
3. Grease a form or cast iron pan with butter and sprinkle it with the crushed rusks, and in it layer alternately the fried bread and the apple puree.
4. The first and last layer should be bread.
5. The last layer is also covered with the remaining butter, distributed in small flakes.
6. Place the cake in moderate oven heat to bake for about ½ hour.
7. Turn it out, sprinkle with sugar and serve with cream or vanilla sauce.
