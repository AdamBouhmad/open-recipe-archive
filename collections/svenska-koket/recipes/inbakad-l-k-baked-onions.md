## Ingredients

- 3 large Portuguese onions (3 hg.)
- 1 liter water
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 65 gr. flour
- 65 cl. cream
- 1 egg yolk
- 12 gr. butter
- 1/2 tsp. sugar
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 1 small egg white
- 1/2 kg. frying oil

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and blanch them (see recipe no. 713) for 15 minutes, place them on a cloth and let them cool.
2. Cut them thereafter into 1/2 cm. thick slices, and separate the rings from each other.
3. For the frying batter, sift the flour into a bowl, stir in the cream, egg yolk, the clarified butter and the spices. Last, fold in the egg white beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Take the onion rings with a fork and dip them in the batter, which must completely cover them. Cook them in hot frying oil until they have a beautiful light brown color, then place them on absorbent paper to drain. Serve hot with roasted meat, for example roast beef.
