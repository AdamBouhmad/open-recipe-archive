## Ingredients

- Ground meat as in recipe no. 632, no. 631 or no. 633
- 1.5 hectograms fine macaroni
- 1.5 liters water
- 3 tablespoons melted butter (60 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. The pudding can be prepared with beef, veal or ham ground meat according to the aforementioned descriptions.
2. Break the macaroni into 2 cm long pieces, rinse them quickly in cold water, place them in boiling salted water and let them cook until soft, then drain them in a colander and let them drip dry.
3. Melt the butter in a saucepan, add the macaroni and shake them well in the butter and season with salt and pepper if needed.
4. Butter a pudding mold with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs.
5. Layer the ground meat and macaroni alternately in the mold; the first and last layer should be meat.
6. Cook the pudding as in recipe no. 638, turn it out and serve with the same kind of sauce.
