## Ingredients

- 1 kg strawberries
- 1 kg (or 3/4 kg) granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the strawberries, rinse gently and let drain.
2. Layer the strawberries and granulated sugar alternately in a jam pot, and let them stand to release their juice.
3. Place the pot over heat, bring the jam to a rapid, brief boil, remove from heat, shake and skim.
4. Return to heat, bring to a boil, remove from heat, shake and skim.
5. Repeat this process 3-4 times, or until the berries are clear and the syrup is thick.
6. Shake the jam while it cools slightly, then immediately pour into prepared jars.
