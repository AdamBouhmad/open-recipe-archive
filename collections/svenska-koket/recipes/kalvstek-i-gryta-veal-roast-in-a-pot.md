## Ingredients

- 2 kg. knuckle
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- Salt
- Sugar
- White pepper

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until it is tender, is beaten on the skin side with a damp mallet, dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and rubbed with the seasonings. The butter is browned in a roasting pot that should be only just large enough to fit the meat. The roast is placed in and browned well on all sides, after which the lid is placed on and the roast is slowly roasted without adding liquid until it is tender, or about 3 hours. It is turned often and the lid must fit tightly on the pot. The roast is placed with the inner side up on a warm platter and is carved at the table. Most of the fat is skimmed from the gravy in the pot, after which the flour is mixed well with the meat gravy, the cream is added, and the sauce is allowed to boil for 8 minutes. It is seasoned with the seasonings and poured through a sauce strainer into a warm sauce bowl. The roast is served with vegetables or potatoes and cucumber, and salads.
