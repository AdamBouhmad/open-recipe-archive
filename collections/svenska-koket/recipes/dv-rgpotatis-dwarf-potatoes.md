## Ingredients

- 2 liters potatoes
- water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 4 tablespoons cream
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw and shape them with a knife or potato ball cutter into small round balls.
2. Give them a quick boil in water and let them drain.
3. Heat the cream in a baking pan, add the potatoes, shake them about and put them in the oven to bake through.
4. Remove them, pour off the cream, sprinkle the potatoes with salt and place them on a serving dish.
5. Pour the melted butter over the potatoes, and finally sprinkle with the chopped parsley.
