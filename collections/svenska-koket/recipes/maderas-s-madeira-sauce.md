## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr)
- 5 dcl strong meat stock
- 1/2 dcl madeira
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes.
2. Add the meat stock little by little and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add the wine and bring to a boil.
4. Season with salt and cayenne.
