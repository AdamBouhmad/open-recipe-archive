## Ingredients

- Small fish, fish slices, or small fillets
- Salt
- Milk
- Flour
- Egg, whisked
- Breadcrumbs, crushed
- Fat for frying (see recipes 43 and 44)
- Parsley
- Lemon

## Directions

1. If the fish is thick, after cleaning, make several deep crosswise cuts before breading to aid in cooking through.
2. For flatfish, partially loosen the two lower fillets from the backbone instead of scoring them.
3. Before frying, dip the fish first in salted milk and roll in flour, or bread them by turning in flour, dipping in whisked egg, and rolling in crushed breadcrumbs.
4. Pat with a knife blade so the breading adheres well, and score in a crosswise pattern with the back of a knife if desired for better appearance when fried.
5. Heat the fat in a frying or long pan, place the fish in and fry until a beautiful golden brown.
6. Place on absorbent paper so the fat drains off.
7. Place on a broken napkin or baking paper and garnish with parsley fried in fat and nicely cut lemon halves or fluted lemon slices.
