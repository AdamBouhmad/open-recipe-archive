## Ingredients

- 2 hg. butter
- 1 hg. sugar
- 2 hg. flour
- 10 gr. sweet almonds (10 pieces)
- 5 bitter almonds
- 1 egg
- 5 rhubarb stalks (about 7 hg.)
- 3 dcl. granulated sugar (255 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and work it together with the sugar, flour, the blanched and ground almonds and the egg into a smooth dough, which is allowed to rest in a cool place for about 2 hours.
2. Peel the rhubarb thinly and cut it into 3 cm. long pieces.
3. Roll out two thirds of the dough and use it to line a buttered baking sheet or pan, then add the rhubarb alternately with the sugar, and fold the dough edge over.
4. Roll out the remaining dough into a lid, cover the pie with it, and press the edges down well.
5. Bake the pie in ordinary oven heat for about 1 hour, or until the rhubarb is soft and the dough is thoroughly baked when tested with a whisk.
6. Remove it and let it rest for a quarter hour, then turn it out and serve warm or cold with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
