## Ingredients

- 4-5 hg. boneless, cooked or roasted meat
- 1 small red onion
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 g)
- 2 tsp. salt (10 g)
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 2 dcl. roasting sauce or broth
- 4 eggs for the custard
- 1 tsp. wheat flour
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. sugar
- 4 dcl. milk
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Chop the meat fairly finely.
2. Peel and finely chop the onion and brown it in the butter.
3. Add the meat and brown well, sprinkle with spices and mix with the sauce or broth.
4. For the egg custard, whip the egg yolks with flour, a little salt and sugar, and the milk.
5. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Place the meat in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined baking dish and pour the egg custard over it.
