## Ingredients

- 2 tjog kräftor
- 4 lit. vatten
- 90 gr. grovt salt
- smör, lika vikt med kräftskalen

## Directions

1. Cook the crayfish as in recipe no. 171. Clean them; tails, claws and all fat are used for stews or sauces, the remainder is weighed and an equal weight of butter is taken.
2. Pound the shells and butter in a stone mortar until a fine paste, which is allowed to melt in a water bath with stirring and strain through fine cloth into a bowl with cold water.
3. When the butter is half set, form it into balls, which should weigh 10 gr. These are stored well covered in brine and used for sauces and stews.
4. Alternatively, all the fat from the crayfish together with the shells can be pounded with the butter into a fine paste, which is passed through a hair sieve and wrung through muslin. This butter has an exquisite fine taste but should be used within 1-2 days, as it becomes rancid quickly.
5. Lobster butter is prepared from lobster shell in the same way.
6. Butter can be coloured green with spinach colouring or with pistachios. The pistachios, 120 gr., are scalded, driven through an almond mill, pounded in a stone mortar with 30 gr. butter and passed through a hair sieve.
