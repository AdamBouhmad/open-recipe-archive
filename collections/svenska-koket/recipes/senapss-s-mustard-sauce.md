## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1/2 liter fish stock
- 2 tsp powdered mustard
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry together the butter and flour, add the fish stock, stir in the mustard mixed with a little fish stock, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
2. If a milder sauce is desired, add a little milk.
