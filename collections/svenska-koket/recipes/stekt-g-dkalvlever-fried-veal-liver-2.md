## Ingredients

- 1 veal liver
- 1 hg. lard
- 1/4 msk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 caul
- 2 msk. butter
- 4 kkp heavy cream
- 1/2 msk. butter for sauce
- 1 msk. flour
- 2 msk. grated mesost cheese
- salt, white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the liver and let it soak in water for 1/4 hour.
2. Cut the lard into strips, roll these in pepper and salt, and lard the liver in dense rows with them.
3. Wrap it in the caul and brown it in the butter and then add the heavy cream.
4. Let the liver fry slowly for approximately 1 hour with a tightly fitting lid, stirring the sauce occasionally so it does not curdle.
5. When the liver is fried, remove the caul; slice the liver thinly and arrange on a platter.
6. Prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules and pour over the liver.
