## Ingredients

- 7 hectograms (250 gr) saithe
- 80 gr butter
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr)
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 tablespoons potato flour (23 gr)
- 4-5 deciliters good milk
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr)
- mace (optional)
- 1 1/2 liters fish stock for cooking

## Directions

1. Clean the saithe, rinse well and dry it
2. Cut off the head and remove the gills
3. Fillet the fish and remove the backbone
4. Scrape all meat from the bones and scrape the remaining fish meat finely
5. Weigh the meat, it should amount to 250 gr
6. Cook stock from the head, bones and skin in which the balls will later be cooked
7. Mix the fish meat with the butter and grind through a meat grinder 5 times
8. Pound the mixture in a mortar with a wooden pestle together with the salt
9. Blend in the beaten eggs and flour and pound again until the mixture is fine and cohesive
10. Work the farce with a spoon for 3/4 hour while gradually adding the milk
11. Season with salt, sugar and if desired a touch of mace
12. Form into small round balls using 2 tablespoons dipped in lukewarm water
13. Cook in the fish stock for 3-4 minutes
14. First test the farce by cooking a small sample; if it is too firm add a little more milk
