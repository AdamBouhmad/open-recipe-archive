## Ingredients

- 185 gr. sweet almonds
- 30 bitter almonds
- 240 gr. granulated sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 15 gr. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Dry the almonds well in a cloth and drive through an almond mill.
2. Whip the eggs with the sugar for 1/2 hour, then add the almonds and flour.
3. Place the dough with a teaspoon on buttered and floured baking sheet in the form of round or oblong cakes.
4. Bake the cakes light brown and half-dry in weak oven heat and remove immediately from the baking sheet.
