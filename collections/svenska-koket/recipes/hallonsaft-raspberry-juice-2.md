## Ingredients

- 30 liters raspberries
- 1.5 liters water
- 0.5 kg sugar per kg juice

## Directions

1. Clean the raspberries. They are put on with water and cooked with stirring for about 15 minutes.
2. The mass is then poured into a strainer cloth and allowed to drip until the next day.
3. The juice is then put on with sugar and cooked with careful skimming until it is clear and slightly sticky. It is bottled warm or cold, the bottles are corked and sealed with resin.
