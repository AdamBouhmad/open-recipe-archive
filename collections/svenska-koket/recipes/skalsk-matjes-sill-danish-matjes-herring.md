## Ingredients

- 2 kg salted fat herring
- 2 liters unskim med milk
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- 1 dessertspoon saltpeter

## Directions

1. Pack the salted herring into a porcelain jar.
2. Boil the milk with the sugar and the crushed saltpeter.
3. When the milk is completely cold, pour it over the herring and it should cover it well.
4. Let the herring lie in the milk for eight days, after which it is ready to use.
5. It can be served spiced, fried in paper, or marinated.
