## Ingredients

- 1 bg butter
- 1 bg flour
- 5 parts skimmed milk
- ½ liter fish (cooked and finely chopped)
- 4 eggs
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- ½ msk butter for the mold
- 2 msk crushed breadcrumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Brown the butter and flour together, and add the milk under vigorous stirring so that the batter does not become lumpy.
2. Let it cook for 10 minutes, then remove it to cool.
3. Add the finely chopped fish, together with the spices and egg yolks.
4. Lastly, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
