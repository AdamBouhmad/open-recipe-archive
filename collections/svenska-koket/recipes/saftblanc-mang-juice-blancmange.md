## Ingredients

- 4 leaves gelatin
- 16 cl. water
- 33 cl. sweet juice (not orange or lemon)

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut into pieces, and place in a saucepan with the water and juice.
2. Bring the mixture to a boil while whisking and then whisk vigorously until it forms stiff peaks, about 40 minutes.
3. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let it set on ice for 2-3 hours.
4. When serving, loosen from the edge with a pointed knife and unmold onto a glass dish with cake paper.
