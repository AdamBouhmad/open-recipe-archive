## Ingredients

- 250 gr. lump sugar
- 3 parts water
- juice of 1 lemon
- juice of 10 oranges (including 3 blood oranges)
- 2 oranges for garnish

## Directions

1. Make a clear, glossy syrup from the sugar and water.
2. Stir in the lemon and orange juice, and set the mixture out to cool.
3. When it is completely cold, pour it into an ice cream machine or ice cream freezer and freeze, but not too hard.
4. Pack it into an ice cream mold, which is in turn packed in ice.
5. Turn out and garnish with orange segments.
