## Ingredients

- 9 veal cutlets
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 3 deciliters finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 5 tablespoons butter (100 gr.)

## Directions

1. Dry the cutlet rack with a cloth wrung out in hot water.
2. Cut out the cutlets so that one rib bone remains in each.
3. Remove the outer membrane and backbone, scrape the meat from the tip of the rib bones and trim them evenly.
4. Beat the cutlets with a damp meat mallet, shape them and rub in a mixture of salt and pepper.
5. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter lightly in it, place the cutlets in and fry about 7 minutes on each side, or until they are nicely browned and tender.
6. Place them on a warm platter and pour the browned butter over them.
7. If desired, put paper frills on the bones.
8. Serve with fried potatoes or vegetables. If the cutlets are large, one can suffice for each person and the other ingredients should be reduced accordingly.
