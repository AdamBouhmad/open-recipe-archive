## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 g)
- 1 1/2 liters light broth
- 2-3 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the boiling broth with stirring and let it cook for 5 minutes. Beat the egg yolks and whisk them into the broth, which is then seasoned with spices and served with cooked vegetables, mushrooms, cheese croutons, quenelles of fish forcemeat or similar.
