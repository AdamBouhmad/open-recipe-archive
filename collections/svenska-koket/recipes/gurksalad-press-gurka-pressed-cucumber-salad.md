## Ingredients

- 1 firm, medium fresh cucumber of approximately 1/2 kg
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 1 deciliter vinegar or wine vinegar
- 4 teaspoons sugar (20 g)
- 2-3 tablespoons fine olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 teaspoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumber from the flower end to the stem end with not too thin peels, cut into 3 mm thick slices, which are sprinkled with salt and allowed to stand for approximately 1 hour.
2. Drain off the liquid, place the slices in a salad bowl, and pour over a mixture of vinegar, sugar, olive oil if desired, and white pepper, and sprinkle with parsley.
3. Serve with the sandwich board and with roasted meat.
