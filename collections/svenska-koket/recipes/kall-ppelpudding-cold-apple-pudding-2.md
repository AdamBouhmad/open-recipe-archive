## Ingredients

- 190 grams sugar
- 2 deciliters white wine
- 2 deciliters water
- 1.5 liters tart apples (approximately 800 grams)
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- juice of 1 lemon
- 6 sheets red gelatin
- 1 deciliter water for the gelatin

## Directions

1. Boil a clear, clear syrup from the sugar, wine and water.
2. Wash, dry and peel the apples, cut them into quarters, remove the cores and place directly in the sugar syrup.
3. Add the lemon peel and cook the apples to a pulp.
4. Pass the apple pulp through a hair sieve, season with lemon juice and more sugar if desired.
5. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces, dissolve it in hot water and mix well with the apple pulp.
6. Immediately fill the mixture into a rinsed and sugar-dusted mold of porcelain and let stand on ice for approximately 5 hours.
7. When the pudding is to be served, carefully loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a cake plate.
8. Garnish with biscuits and serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
9. It is best to cook the apple pulp the day before the pudding is to be prepared.
