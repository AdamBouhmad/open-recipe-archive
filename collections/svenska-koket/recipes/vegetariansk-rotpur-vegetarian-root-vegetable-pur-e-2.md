## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 celeriac
- 1 celeriac
- 1 leek
- 4 parsley stems
- 1 1/4 liters water
- 1 liter milk
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- salt
- sugar
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Prepare the roots in the usual way and cut into pieces.
2. Clean the leek, rinse, and cut into slices; clean the parsley, rinse, and tie together.
3. Put everything together to cook in the water and milk along with 1 tablespoon of butter.
4. When the roots are completely soft, pass through a sieve, and cook the purée as in recipe no. 266, season with salt, sugar, and, if desired, a little cayenne.
5. Place half a tablespoon of cold butter in the soup bowl and serve the purée with fried bread cubes or cheese croutons.
