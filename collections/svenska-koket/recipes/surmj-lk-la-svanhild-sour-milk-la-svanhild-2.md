## Ingredients

- 1 liter sour milk
- 1/2 liter thick, sour cream
- 1-1 1/2 deciliters fine red wine

## Directions

1. Whip the sour milk and thick sour cream to stiff peaks.
2. Season with red wine and serve immediately with sugar biscuits or rusks.
