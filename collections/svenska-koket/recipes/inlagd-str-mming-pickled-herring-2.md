## Ingredients

- 18 fried herrings
- 2 teaspoons allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 red onion
- 3 decilitres vinegar
- 2 tablespoons sugar

## Directions

1. Place the cold herrings in a jar, layering them alternately with the spices.
2. Peel and chop the onion finely, place in a saucepan together with the vinegar and sugar.
3. Bring the liquid to a boil and let cool, then pour over the herrings.
4. After 1 hour it is ready to be served at the smörgåsbord.
