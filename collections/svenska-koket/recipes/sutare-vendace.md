## Ingredients

- Fish (sutare)
- Salt
- Cream
- Horseradish

## Directions

1. Dip the fish for a couple of minutes in boiling water
2. Let it lie for 1/2 hour in cold water
3. Prepare and arrange as bream
4. Serve cold with cream and horseradish
