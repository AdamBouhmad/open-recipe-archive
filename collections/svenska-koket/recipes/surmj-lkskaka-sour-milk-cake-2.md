## Ingredients

- 600 grams rye sifted flour
- 2 small teaspoons bicarbonate
- 1 small teaspoon baking powder
- 0.5 liters sour milk
- 2 deciliters syrup
- 0.5 tablespoon caraway or 0.5 tablespoon fennel and 0.5 tablespoon anise
- 0.5 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan
- 2 tablespoons wheat flour (20 grams) for the pan

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix well with the bicarbonate and baking powder.
2. Whip the milk until stiff foam and fold into the flour along with the syrup and finely ground spices.
3. Pour the batter into a well-buttered and floured, oblong bread pan or frying pan.
4. Immediately place in ordinary oven heat for about 1 hour.
5. Test with a whisk to check if fully baked.
6. Use as open-faced sandwich bread.
