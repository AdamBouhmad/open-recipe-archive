## Ingredients

- For the pancakes: 60 gr. flour
- 1 kkp cream
- 1 kkp milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- For the forcemeat: 150 gr. boneless chicken or veal
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1/2 msk. flour (5 gr.)
- 1 1/2-2 dcl. thick cream
- 1 truffle

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl, add the cream, milk, egg and salt. Whisk the batter until smooth and without lumps and let it stand to swell for 2 hours.
2. Prepare the forcemeat as in recipe 318.
3. Heat the pancake pan slowly, grease it with butter and from the batter fry 3 pancakes, which are placed on a sheet and spread over with a thin layer of forcemeat. They are stacked on top of each other, the top layer shall be forcemeat, which is sprinkled with finely chopped truffle.
4. Place the arrangement in an ordinary oven heat 10-15 minutes or until the forcemeat is fried, which is tested with a whisk handle.
5. Remove the cake with a measure or glass about 2 1/2 cm. in diameter. The small cakes are placed in a soup bowl and when the soup is to be served the boiling consommé is poured over.
