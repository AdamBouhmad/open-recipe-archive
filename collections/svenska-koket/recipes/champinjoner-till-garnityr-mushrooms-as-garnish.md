## Ingredients

- 450 gr. small, fresh champinjoner (mushrooms)
- 1/2 lit. water
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- 30 gr. butter
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the champinjoner as soon as they are picked by gently removing the thin skin, avoiding removing the outer skin on tender mushrooms
2. Cut the stems even with the caps
3. Rinse the mushroom quickly and dry on cloth
4. Score the caps beautifully with a pointed knife
5. Place immediately in boiling water with salt, butter and lemon juice
6. Cook for 5-10 minutes
7. Remove with a slotted spoon and use as garnish for finer dishes
8. The mushrooms can be stored 2-3 days in a cold place if covered with butter paper
9. The liquid can be used for sauces and stews
