## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter extra thick cream, sweet or sour
- approximately 175 gr flour
- 40 gr melted butter
- 1/2 msk melted butter for the iron (10 gr)

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks.
2. Sift the flour and whip it in, then add the water and let the batter stand in a cold place for approximately 1 hour to rise, then carefully stir in the butter.
3. For the waffles to be crispy and beautiful, an even heat is required.
4. Heat the waffle iron gradually on both sides and grease it with melted butter or white wax.
5. Pour a spoonful of batter in, turn the iron, taking care that the hottest side of the iron is turned upward during baking.
6. Take the waffle out of the iron with a fork, trim with scissors, and place on a bread rack in the stove so it does not soften.
7. Arrange on pastry paper on a warm plate and serve with jam, compote, or with coffee and tea.
