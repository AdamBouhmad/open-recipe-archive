## Ingredients

- 1 liter cream
- 1 large vanilla pod
- 160 grams chocolate with sugar
- 2 deciliters water
- 7 egg yolks
- 200 grams sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the cream with 1 large vanilla pod and let steep, covered, for 20 minutes.
2. Grate the chocolate with sugar, mix with 2 deciliters water and blend with the cream.
3. Prepare the cream from 7 egg yolks and 200 grams sugar according to the base recipe description.
4. Freeze as in recipe no. 929.
