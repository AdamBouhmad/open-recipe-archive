## Ingredients

- For the sweet dough: 2 hg. flour, 2 hg. butter, 25 gr. caster sugar, 1 small egg
- For filling: 6 parts fresh rhubarb, 3½ parts caster sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter if it is salted.
2. Make an ordinary sweet dough from the butter, flour, egg and sugar, and set out to harden.
3. Roll out half the dough and line a sheet metal form or cast iron pan with it.
4. Peel the rhubarb, cut into pieces and place in layers with sugar in the lined mold.
5. Roll out the remaining dough, cut out a lid, and cover the pie with it.
6. Bake in good oven heat for ¾ to 1 hour, turn out and serve cold.
