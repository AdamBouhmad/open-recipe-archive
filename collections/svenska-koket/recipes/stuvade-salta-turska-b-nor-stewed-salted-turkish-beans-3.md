## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. salted beans
- 8 dcl. water
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.) [for white sauce]
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.) [for white sauce]
- 3 dcl. bean liquid [for white sauce]
- 3 dcl. cream or milk [for white sauce]
- 1 egg yolk [for white sauce]
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. The beans are placed in a colander, rinsed with cold water, and allowed to soak in plenty of water for approximately 12 hours.
2. They are rinsed again, placed in cold water and allowed to simmer slowly until completely tender, which takes approximately 2 hours.
3. The liquid is drained off and reserved. For the sauce, butter and flour are browned together, the bean liquid and cream are added, and the sauce is allowed to simmer 5 minutes.
4. The sauce is finished with the egg yolk and allowed to simmer. Then the beans are added and the stew is seasoned with sugar.
5. Served with boiled sausage, salted meat, etc.
