## Ingredients

- 3 salad heads
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon mustard
- 3 tablespoons oil
- 2 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the salad, rinse it and cut it into large pieces.
2. Mix the egg yolks together with salt, white pepper and mustard; add the oil and vinegar drop by drop while stirring constantly.
3. Mix the salad well with this sauce and serve immediately.
