## Ingredients

- 250 grams dried apricots
- 2 liters water
- Zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1 to 1.5 cups crushed sugar
- 25 grams rice or potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots very well in lukewarm water and let them soak overnight in the water in which they are to be cooked
2. Place them in water with the lemon zest and sugar and let them cook until soft
3. Thicken the soup with the rice flour, stirred in 1/2 cup cold water, and let it cook for 5 minutes
4. Flavor it with the juice of 1/4 lemon and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and yeast powder rusks
