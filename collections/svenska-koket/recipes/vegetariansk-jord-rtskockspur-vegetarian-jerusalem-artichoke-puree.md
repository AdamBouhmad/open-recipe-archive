## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 small celery
- 2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 4 parts cream
- salt
- white pepper
- (sugar)

## Directions

1. Peel the Jerusalem artichokes and celery in the usual way and cook them tender in the water mixed with salt.
2. When the Jerusalem artichokes are completely soft, strain them.
3. Brown the butter and flour together and mix with the puree and cream.
4. Let the soup simmer 10-15 minutes, then taste and adjust seasoning.
5. Serve with toasted bread or cheese sandwiches.
