## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized beautiful cauliflower heads
- 2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 100 grams dry flour
- 3 grams salt
- 1/2 tablespoon melted butter (10 grams)
- 1/2 deciliter of the cooking liquid
- 1 deciliter milk
- 1 egg
- 3/4 kilogram lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the cauliflower as in the previous recipe, let it drain on linen and then divide it into smaller pieces.
2. Prepare the batter as in recipe 218; dip the divided cauliflower in it and cook until golden brown in the hot lard.
3. Let it drain on gray paper, place on a platter with a folded napkin or parchment paper and serve very hot with creamed butter.
4. If desired, some mushroom stew can be placed in the middle of the platter and the cooked cauliflower in a ring around it.
