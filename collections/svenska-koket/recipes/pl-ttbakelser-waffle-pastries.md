## Ingredients

- 80 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 2 tbsp. pearl sugar
- 1 1/2 parts melted butter
- 100 gr. granulated sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tbsp. butter for the pan

## Directions

1. The butter is clarified and stirred white.
2. The eggs and sugar are stirred well together, and the butter is added.
3. Finally the sifted wheat flour is mixed in.
4. The almonds are blanched, chopped and mixed with the pearl sugar.
5. A waffle pan is placed in the oven to become hot and is then brushed with melted butter.
6. In each space 1/2 tbsp. of the batter is placed, and this is sprinkled with almonds and sugar.
7. The waffle pan is placed in the oven, and when the pastries are baked, they are taken out and bent over a stick.
