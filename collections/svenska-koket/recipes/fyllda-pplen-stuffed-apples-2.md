## Ingredients

- 9 large, beautiful apples
- 75 gr. melted butter
- 1 dcl. finely crushed rusks
- 1 dcl. granulated sugar (85 gr.)
- 75 gr. sweet almond
- 75 gr. icing sugar
- 1 small egg white
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Prepare the almond mixture first. Blanch the almond, dry it in the oven, grind it and pound it with the sugar to a fine mass, add the egg white, and pound the mixture until it is even and smooth.
2. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them evenly, and remove the cores with a metal tube.
3. Divide the almond mixture into nine parts, which are rolled out and filled into the apples, which are then dipped in the melted butter and rolled in a mixture of the rusks and sugar.
4. Grease a small baking pan with cold butter, place the apples in it, place in a warm oven and bake until they are beautifully golden brown and soft.
5. When they are cold, place them on a cake dish with cake paper and serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
