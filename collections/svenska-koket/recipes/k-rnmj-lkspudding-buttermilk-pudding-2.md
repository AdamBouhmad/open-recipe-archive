## Ingredients

- 6 parts buttermilk
- 2 teaspoons fine sugar
- 1 tablespoon coarse sugar
- 6 sheets red gelatin
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Flavor the buttermilk with the sugar and with grated lemon zest and juice.
2. Rinse the gelatin in cold water and dissolve it in a little hot water, then stir it into the milk.
3. If the milk is so cold that the gelatin does not blend properly with it and the milk appears streaky, place it over the fire with whisking for a moment until it has an even color.
4. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed mold and set it out to stiffen.
5. Turn it out and serve.
