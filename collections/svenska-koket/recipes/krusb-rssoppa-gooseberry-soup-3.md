## Ingredients

- 1 liter green gooseberries or 1/2 liter preserved gooseberries
- 2 liters water
- Zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1–1 1/2 coffee spoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 coffee spoon powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Top and tail the gooseberries, rinse them, and cook in boiling water with the lemon zest until soft.
2. Strain the soup.
3. Cook and serve as in the previous recipe (Rabarbersoppa).
