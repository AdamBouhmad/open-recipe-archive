## Ingredients

- 1 fresh ham, about 5 kg
- 40 gr. salt
- 1 tsp. white pepper
- 2 dcl. crushed juniper berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 red onions, in slices
- 1 celery, in cubes
- 2 liters weak broth
- water
- 1 bottle red wine
- 8 allspice berries
- 1 piece ginger

## Directions

1. Remove the rind neatly.
2. Rub the ham thoroughly with salt and pepper and place in a deep dish.
3. Sprinkle with juniper berries, bay leaves, red onion, and celery.
4. Pour over with weak broth and enough water to cover the meat.
5. Let the ham marinate about one week, turning it daily.
6. Cook it in wine and water with the spices as in recipe no. 556.
7. Serve warm or cold.
