## Ingredients

- 185 gr. butter
- 2 eggs
- 125 gr. flour sugar
- 90 gr. wheat flour
- 15 gr. pearl sugar to sprinkle over the pastries
- 15 gr. sweet almond to sprinkle over the pastries

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, let it cool and beat it until it is white and fluffy.
2. Thereafter beat in the eggs, one at a time, and the sugar and beat the mass for 10 minutes.
3. Last add the sifted flour.
4. Heat a waffle iron slightly, place 1/2 tbsp. of the batter in each cavity and beat it out so that the cakes become even and thin, whereupon they are sprinkled with sugar and finely chopped almond.
5. Bake the pastries in a not too hot oven until they are beautifully golden brown, when they are taken out and bent immediately over a narrow rod.
