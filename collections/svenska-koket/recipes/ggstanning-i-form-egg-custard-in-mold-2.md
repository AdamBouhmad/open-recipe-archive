## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 cups milk
- salt
- sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the mold

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs well with the milk, and add the seasonings.
2. Butter a tin mold or bowl, pour in the egg mixture, cover with a lid, and place the mold in a water bath on top of the stove.
3. Take care that the water does not boil but is only kept hot until the custard is set to the right firmness, otherwise it becomes watery.
4. Turn out onto a warm dish, garnish with parsley, and serve with boiled ham or sausage, fried meat, or fish.
5. The egg custard can also be baked in a water bath in the oven.
