## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef
- 1 kg pork
- 1½ kg lard
- 150 grams salt
- 18 grams ground strong pepper
- 1 small tablespoon potassium nitrate
- 1 generous tablespoon sugar
- 2 parts cognac
- 4 meters straight casings (ox casing)
- For rubbing in: 8 tablespoons salt
- 4 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 2 teaspoons potassium nitrate

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut into pieces.
2. Remove sinews and membranes and pass the meat 5 times through the meat grinder.
3. Cut the lard into fine, even cubes.
4. Work the ground meat alone for ½ hour.
5. Then mix in the finely ground spices, sugar, potassium nitrate (likewise finely ground), cognac, and pork cubes.
6. Work the mixture for another ½ hour.
7. Stuff the forcemeat as tightly as possible into straight ox casings with the tallow side turned out.
8. Tie the finished sausages, rub them with salt, sugar, and potassium nitrate and let them lie for 3 days.
9. Turn them morning and evening and rub in with salt, sugar, and potassium nitrate each time.
10. Smoke the sausages for 3 days.
11. Store them hanging in a cool place or packed in juniper branches.
