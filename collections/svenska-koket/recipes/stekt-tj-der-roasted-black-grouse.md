## Ingredients

- 1 black grouse
- 1 hg. lard
- 3-4 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. broth
- 5 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. If the bird is frozen, thaw it in a warm kitchen, clean it, and let it soak in lukewarm milk overnight.
2. Dry it well, lard it with fine lard strips, and truss it.
3. Roast the bird in a pot as you would a hazel grouse, but it requires 2-3 hours of roasting depending on whether it is a hen or a cock.
4. Cut it, garnish (see page 300), and serve with a reduced sauce (see no. 429), mixed with thick cream and cold butter, and if desired, with 1 tablespoon red currant jelly.
