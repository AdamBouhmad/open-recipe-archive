## Ingredients

- 2 kg zander
- 1¼ tbsp salt
- 1 tsk vinegar
- 2 tbsp butter
- fish stock
- Mushroom sauce: 1 tbsp butter, 2 tbsp flour, 2 parts cream, fish stock, 1 can mushrooms (at ½ liter), 2-3 egg yolks, pepper, salt, lemon juice
- Crayfish sauce: 1½ tbsp crayfish butter, 1½ tbsp flour, 2 parts cream, fish stock, 1½ hectograms crayfish tails, white pepper (sugar)
- For garnish: ½ can mushrooms (at ½ liter), ½ hectograms crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean and rinse it well.
2. Strip off the skin and cut the fish open along the back, remove the backbone along with all small bones.
3. Rub the fillets with salt and vinegar.
4. Butter a roasting pan, place the fish in it and put in the oven to roast, diluting it with fish broth (cooked from head, skin and bones).
5. Place it on a warm dish and pour over half the plate with the yellow mushroom sauce and on the other half with the pink crayfish sauce.
6. Garnish the arrangement with mushrooms and crayfish tails.
7. The sauces are prepared according to general rules.
