## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. regular oat groats (112 gr.)
- 1 1/4 liter water
- 15 almonds (3 bitter) (15 gr.)
- 1 liter milk
- 2 msk. sugar (30 gr.)
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1 1/2 hg. prunes

## Directions

1. Cook the groats in water as in the preceding recipe and strain.
2. Blanch the almonds, grind them through a mill and pound finely in a stone mortar.
3. Bring the milk to a boil with the almonds; add the oat purée, sugar and salt and let the soup boil while stirring for 5 minutes.
4. Rinse the prunes well in lukewarm water, cook them briefly in broth with a lid until they are soft and swollen, drain in a colander and add to the soup, which is tasted carefully.
