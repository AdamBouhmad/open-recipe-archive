## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg lamb brisket
- 1 head white cabbage
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 15 white peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 0.5 liter boiling water
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into even pieces, which are placed in 1 liter boiling water with 0.5 tablespoon salt and allowed to boil without a lid for approximately 10 minutes.
2. Remove the meat and rinse with cold water.
3. Free the cabbage head from the outer green leaves and cut into large wedges.
4. In an iron pot, layer the meat and cabbage alternately with spices between each layer.
5. Add the water and allow the dish to slowly boil until the meat is tender, or approximately 2 hours.
6. Place on warm serving dish and sprinkle with chopped parsley.
7. Serve very hot.
8. If desired, the cabbage can be browned in 3 tablespoons butter and 1 tablespoon syrup before cooking with the meat.
