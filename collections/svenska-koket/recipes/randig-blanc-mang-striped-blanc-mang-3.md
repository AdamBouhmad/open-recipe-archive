## Ingredients

- 95 gr. sweet almonds
- 3 bitter almonds
- 3 dcl. milk
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 125 gr. sugar
- peel and juice of 1 orange or a little orange essence
- 6 leaves gelatin
- 1 dcl. hot water
- purée of strawberries or raspberries
- maraschino liqueur
- cocoa

## Directions

1. Prepare the cream as in the preceding recipe and divide it into three equal parts.
2. Add a little purée or juice of strawberries or raspberries to one part, pour it into a nice tin mold, rinsed with cold water and dusted with sugar, and let it set on ice.
3. Add maraschino to the second part and pour it into the mold when the pink cream is set.
4. Add cocoa to the third part and pour it last into the mold.
5. Let the cream set on ice for 2-3 hours, turn it out and garnish with biscuits.
