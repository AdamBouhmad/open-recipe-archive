## Ingredients

- 2 liters thick cream
- 2 tablespoons raspberry jam
- 1 tablespoon other jam

## Directions

1. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form and then blend with the jam.
2. Pour it into a glacé mold and freeze, but not too hard.
3. Pack the frozen cream in a glacé form, place it on ice until it is to be served, then turn it out and garnish with fruit.
