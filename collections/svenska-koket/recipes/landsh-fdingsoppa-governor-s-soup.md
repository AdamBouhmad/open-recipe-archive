## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 parts barley groats
- 3 liters water
- 1 liter fresh apples
- 1 cup crushed sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 parts thick cream
- biscuits
- gooseberry jam

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats and place them in cold water.
2. Rinse the apples, cut into wedges, remove the cores, and add when the groats come to a boil.
3. When the groats are soft and the apples are broken down, strain the soup.
4. Pour it back into the saucepan, add the sugar and lemon juice, and bring to a boil.
5. Whip the egg yolks and cream together in the soup bowl and pour in the soup while whisking vigorously.
6. Serve with gooseberry jam and biscuits.
