## Ingredients

- 1 unflayed rabbit, approximately 3 kg
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 2 decilitres broth
- 5 decilitres cream or milk
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce

## Directions

1. After the rabbit has been slaughtered, it should hang for 2-6 days, depending on the season, so the meat becomes tender.
2. Skin and treat the rabbit and fry as with hare, but it requires somewhat shorter cooking time.
3. Instead of larding the rabbit, it can be covered with lard slices and bound with fine sailcloth.
4. Serve with browned potatoes and salads and prepared sauce.
5. Rabbit can also be braised as hare.
