## Ingredients

- 12 small sugar turnips
- water, salt
- 50 gr. butter
- 1 tsp. salt
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 4 dcl. boiling broth

## Directions

1. The turnips are peeled and cut out with a round vegetable cutter in the form of small onions, which are blanched 5 minutes in salted water, drained in a colander and allowed to drip dry.
2. The butter is browned in a saucepan, the turnips are added, sprinkled with salt and sugar and browned well in the butter.
3. The broth is added and is allowed to boil until the turnips are almost soft, then the lid is removed and the sauce is allowed to boil down so that it becomes thick as syrup.
4. The turnips are shaken gently in it until they are evenly glazed.
5. Served as garnish around roasted meat or together with boiled vegetables.
