## Ingredients

- 30 herrings
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
- 3 decilitres warm water

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings, scrape away the dark membrane, rinse well and wrap in a fish towel.
2. Rub the herrings inside and out with salt and layer them alternately in a buttered saucepan, sprinkling flour and parsley between each layer.
3. Distribute the remaining butter in flakes between the layers.
4. Add the warm water last and let the fish simmer slowly with a tight-fitting lid for approximately 10 minutes.
5. Arrange the herrings on carrots, whisk up the sauce, taste and adjust seasoning, and pour over.
6. Serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
