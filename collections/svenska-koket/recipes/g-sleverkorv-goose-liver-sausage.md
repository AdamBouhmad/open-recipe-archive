## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons rice grains
- 2 cups milk
- 1 goose liver
- 1 tablespoon sultana raisins
- 0.25 teaspoon marjoram
- 0.25 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 0.5 tablespoon chopped Portuguese onion
- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice grains, cook them soft in milk and beat until cool.
2. Pound the liver finely and mix in, along with the cleaned and blanched raisins and seasonings.
3. Brown the onion in butter and add last along with the egg yolk.
4. Fill the mixture into the well-rinsed and one end-sewn neck skin of a goose.
5. Sew the skin shut, and cook the sausage together with the goose giblets for about 1.5 hours.
6. Serve with black soup.
