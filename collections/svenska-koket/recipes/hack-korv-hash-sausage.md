## Ingredients

- 1 heart of young beef
- 1 beef leg
- 1 kg beef meat (chuck)
- 0.25 beef liver
- water
- 1 kg barley groats
- 4 medium red onions (or 2 tbsp marjoram)
- 10 tbsp salt
- 1.5 tbsp ground spice pepper
- 1 tsp ground white pepper
- 6-7 meters curved casings

## Directions

1. Soak the heart and liver in water the evening before
2. Wash the beef leg and chuck with a cloth dipped in cold water and place on the fire together with the liver and heart in enough cold water to just cover the meat
3. When the broth comes to a boil, skim it carefully, and let the meat cook until tender
4. Remove the meat, take out glands, sinews and bones, cut into pieces and pass through a meat grinder once
5. Boil down the meat broth and cook the barley groats in it until soft
6. Mix this porridge with the meat; add the finely chopped onion browned in a little butter and the spices, and work the mixture for approximately 1 hour
7. Stuff into well-cleaned curved casings to make small, short sausages
8. Blanch for a couple of minutes in boiling water and then hang to dry, or smoke them very gently
