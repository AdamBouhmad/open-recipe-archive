## Ingredients

- 3 beautiful fresh cucumbers
- 1 lit. water
- 2 msk. butter
- 1 tsk. sugar
- 1 msk. strong meat gravy or meat extract
- 1 1/2 liter broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/4 msk. cold butter

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut them into pieces, and remove the seeds well.
2. Boil water, add the cucumbers and let them cook until soft, then strain well.
3. Melt butter in a pan, add the cucumbers along with the sugar and brown them until they have a nice light brown color, then add the meat gravy.
4. Let the mixture cook a few minutes, then pass it through a sieve and pour it back into the pan.
5. Add the broth, bring the soup to a good boil and season it.
6. Finally stir in the cold butter, and serve the soup.
