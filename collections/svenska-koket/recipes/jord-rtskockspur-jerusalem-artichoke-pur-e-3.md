## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 1/4 root celery
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1 1/2 liters clear broth
- salt, white pepper, sugar
- 2–3 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter thick cream

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the Jerusalem artichokes (preferably under running water), wash again and let them soak for 1 hour in 1 1/2 liters of water with 2 tablespoons vinegar and 2 tablespoons wheat flour, to prevent them from darkening.
2. Peel the celery, cut into dice and place together with the Jerusalem artichokes.
3. Set aside twelve small round Jerusalem artichokes, cook them separately in a little broth and serve in the soup.
4. The rest are placed in cold water with the salt and cooked until completely soft, then passed through a sieve.
5. Prepare the purée thereafter as in recipe no. 266.
6. Serve with the Jerusalem artichokes and cheese croutons.
