## Ingredients

- 1/2 dcl rice grains (150 gr)
- 1 1/2 lit water
- 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 50 gr butter
- 5 hg smoked ham

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, add to boiling salted water and let cook until soft, approximately 15-20 minutes
2. Pour into a colander and let the water drain well
3. Peel the onion, chop finely and brown in butter in a frying pan
4. Add the rice grains to the pan and mix well with the onion
5. Break apart the ham, place in the center of a warm serving dish and arrange the rice grains in a ring around it
6. Garnish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish
