## Ingredients

- 2 pike perch, approximately 6 hg each
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 80 gr dry flour
- 3 gr salt
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr)
- 1 dcl water
- 1 egg
- 750 gr lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the pike perch as in recipe n:o 365, but do not flatten the fillets.
2. Rub with salt and let rest for 1 hour, then dry again with a fish towel.
3. Prepare the deep-fry batter and coat fillets.
4. Deep-fry in hot lard until golden brown.
