## Ingredients

- 7 hg. boneless veal
- 4 kkp milk
- 1 hg. cooked potato
- 2 tsk. salt
- 1 msk. chopped parsley
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1 egg for coating
- 1 1/2 kkp crushed rusks for coating
- 3 msk. butter for frying
- 2 kkp broth

## Directions

1. Rinse the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut into cubes and pass three times through a meat grinder, the last time together with the potato.
2. Add the parsley and seasonings, and work the forcemeat well.
3. Form small cutlets from the forcemeat, dip them in the beaten egg and turn them in the crushed rusks.
4. Heat the butter in a frying pan, add the cutlets and brown on both sides.
5. When all cutlets are browned, pour the boiling broth over them and let them cook slowly 5-10 minutes with a tightly fitting lid.
