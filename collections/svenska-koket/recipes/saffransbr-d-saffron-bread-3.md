## Ingredients

- 850 g premium wheat flour
- 0.5 liters unskim milk
- 35 g fresh yeast
- 2 g saffron
- 100 g butter
- 125 g sugar
- 1 egg
- 25 g sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 40 g sultana raisins or 50 g finely cut candied fruit
- 40 g sugar
- 1 egg for brushing
- 25 g sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as described on page 462 and let it rise to double its size.
2. Treat the saffron as in recipe 937.
3. Stir the butter with the sugar until it is fluffy and whisk the egg.
4. Scald the almonds (see recipe 937) and grind them; treat the raisins as in recipe 937.
5. When the dough has risen to double its size, work it with the above-mentioned additions and with more flour if needed, and let it rise again.
6. Place the dough on a floured work surface, knead it well, and divide it into 5 parts.
7. Bake these as in recipe 950 or shape them into braids or coils.
8. Place the breads on buttered baking sheets, let them rise, brush with egg, sprinkle with finely chopped almonds and sugar, and bake in a warm oven.
