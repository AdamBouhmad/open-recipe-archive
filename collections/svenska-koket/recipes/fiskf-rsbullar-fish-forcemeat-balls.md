## Ingredients

- 1 kg pike or coalfish (= 400 g scraped fish meat)
- 200 g butter
- 300 g wheat bread
- 5-6 parts unskimmed milk
- 3 eggs
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- sugar
- nutmeg
- fish stock cooked from skin and bones

## Directions

1. Remove the fish, rinse and dry well
2. Fillet it and scrape the fish meat from skin and bones; no scale or bone should be included
3. Force the fish meat together with butter 3 times through a meat grinder and then pound in a mortar until the mass is fine and cohesive
4. Soften the wheat bread in part of the milk and pass through a sieve
5. In a bowl mix the rest of the milk, egg yolks and seasonings and work this mixture into the forcemeat tablespoon by tablespoon, alternating with the passed bread
6. Work the forcemeat well and form balls, which are cooked in salted fish stock (cooked from skin and bones)
7. Serve the fish balls in regular fish sauce with cream added, or in crayfish sauce
