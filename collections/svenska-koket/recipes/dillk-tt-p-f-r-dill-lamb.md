## Ingredients

- 1.25 kg lamb breast
- 2 liters water
- 1.5 tablespoons salt
- a few dill stalks
- For sauce: 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 liter meat stock
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1.5 tablespoons vinegar
- 2 tablespoons chopped dill
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Break the breast and wash with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Place in boiling water, skim, salt, and add the dill stalks.
3. Let the meat simmer slowly with the lid closed until tender or about 2 hours.
4. Remove and cut into suitable pieces.
5. Prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces and serve in a separate sauce bowl.
