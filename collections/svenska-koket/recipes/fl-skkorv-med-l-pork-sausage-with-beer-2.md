## Ingredients

- 2.5 kg pork meat (preferably loin)
- 425 gr fatty pork
- 1⅓ liters water
- 2 parts beer
- 3 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp potassium nitrate
- 2 tsp ground white pepper
- 1 tsp ground strong pepper
- 5 meters casings (curved skin)
- For rubbing: 2 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp granulated sugar
- 1 tsp potassium nitrate

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water, cut into cubes and pass through a meat grinder 3 times
2. Cut the fat into fine cubes and mix with the meat along with beer, water and finely ground spices
3. Work the mixture for 1 hour and then stuff into casings
4. Rinse the sausages with cold water, rub with a mixture of salt, sugar and nitrate and let lie until the following day, then place in brine
