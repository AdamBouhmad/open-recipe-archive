## Ingredients

- 1 kg whitefish or pike
- 3 tbsp salt
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 6 white peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 can lobster (1 liter)
- 1 1/2 liters fish stock
- 15 leaves gelatin
- 3 egg whites
- 1 tbsp cold water
- 4 tbsp good white wine
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 truffle
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, remove scales and innards, rinse well and dry with a fish cloth.
2. Rub the fish with half the salt and let it rest for 30 minutes, then dry again.
3. Place the fish in cold water with the remaining salt and seasonings (peppercorns and bay leaf) and let it simmer slowly until cooked, about 15 minutes.
4. Remove the fish immediately, peel off the skin and divide into 2 fillets.
5. Place the bone back in the stock and let it cook for another 20 minutes.
6. Cut the fish into beautiful pieces and the lobster likewise.
7. Prepare aspic from the fish stock as in recipe no. 181.
8. When the aspic is cold, add the wine.
9. Arrange as in the previous recipe and serve for the smorgasbord.
10. Garnish with hard-boiled egg, truffle and parsley.
