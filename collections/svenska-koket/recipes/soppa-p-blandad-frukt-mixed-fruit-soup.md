## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hectograms prunes
- 1 hectogram dried apples
- 1/2 hectogram raisins
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 1 - 1 1/2 cups crushed sugar
- 1 1/2 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit and let it soak about 1 hour in the water in which it is to cook.
2. Place it in a covered pot together with the sugar and cinnamon and let cook until the fruit is soft.
3. Thicken the soup with the potato flour mixed with a little cold water, bring to a good boil, taste and adjust seasoning, and serve, preferably cold.
