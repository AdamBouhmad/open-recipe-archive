## Ingredients

- 200 g Russian peas
- water
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Clean the peas and soak in cold water the day before.
2. Place in cold water and cook slowly until completely soft.
3. Drain the water, season with salt, and stir in the cold butter.
