## Ingredients

- 2 liters juicy apples
- 2½ hg. sugar
- 3½ cups crushed rusks
- 2 tbsp. butter
- ½ tbsp. butter (for the form)

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut them into thin slices or wedges.
2. In the bottom of a buttered pie form, place a thick layer of the crushed rusks, on top of it the apples layered alternately with the sugar and lastly the rest of the crushed rusks.
3. On top place the butter, distributed in flakes.
4. Place the form in not too strong oven heat for about 45 minutes, or until the apple slices feel soft.
5. Serve in its form with whipped cream.
