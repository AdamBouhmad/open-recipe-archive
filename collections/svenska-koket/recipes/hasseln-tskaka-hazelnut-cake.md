## Ingredients

- 10 eggs
- 150 grams sugar
- 1/2 kilogram blanched, ground hazelnuts
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and sugar for 1/2 hour.
2. Add the blanched and ground nuts, then fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-strewn pan and bake in gentle oven heat for approximately 1 hour.
