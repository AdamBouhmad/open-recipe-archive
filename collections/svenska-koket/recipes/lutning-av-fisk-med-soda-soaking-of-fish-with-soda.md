## Ingredients

- 4 kg. spirlånga or gråsej (stockfish varieties)
- 15 lit. vatten (water)
- 850 gr. släckt kalk (slaked lime)
- 6 hg. soda (soda)

## Directions

1. The fish, which should have white flesh, cut into pieces and beat well with a club.
2. Place the pieces in a basin with cold water (preferably seawater) and let them lie for a week, during which time the water is changed four times.
3. Dissolve the lime and soda in the cold water, place the fish in it with skin side up, and place a weight on it to keep the pieces down.
4. The fish should be turned often, so that the pieces lying on top come to the bottom.
5. After 3-4 days take out the pieces as they become swollen.
6. Brush them with a stiff brush and place them in cold water.
7. This should be changed twice a day; during the first two days brush the fish each time the water is changed.
8. After about 8 days the fish is ready to serve.
