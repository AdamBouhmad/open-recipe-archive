## Ingredients

- 2 small cauliflower heads (3/4 kg)
- 1 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g)
- 4 eggs
- 2 teaspoons wheat flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 4 deciliters milk
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley (optional)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the cauliflower heads as in recipe 709, let drain on a sieve, divide into florets, and place them in a buttered and bread-crumb-covered ovenproof mold.
2. For the egg custard, whisk the egg yolks with flour, salt, sugar, and milk, then fold in the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the egg custard over the cauliflower and sprinkle with parsley if desired.
4. Place the mold in a regular oven and let it stand until the custard is baked and has a nice color.
5. Serve with sausages, roasted meat, or as a course dish. In the latter case, sprinkle the custard with 1 tablespoon grated cheese instead of parsley before putting it in the oven.
