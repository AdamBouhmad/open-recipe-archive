## Ingredients

- 220 gr. grated cheese (Roquefort, Gorgonzola, Stilton and Parmesan)
- 2 tablespoons grated mesost
- 80 gr. good butter
- 1-2 tablespoons fine cognac

## Directions

1. Mix the grated cheese varieties (leftovers of any cheese type can be used) and work well with the butter, after which add cognac to taste.
2. Pack the cheese spread in a porcelain jar with a lid and store in a cool place.
