## Ingredients

- 2 cups barley groats
- 1.5 liters pork broth
- 6-8 parts pig blood
- ½ Portuguese onion
- 8 tablespoons lard
- 2 cups raisins
- 1 ounce candied fruits
- 2 parts cream
- 1.5-2 cups syrup
- 1 teaspoon thyme
- 1 teaspoon marjoram
- 3-4 tablespoons salt
- sharp pepper
- allspice pepper
- cloves
- ginger
- to taste

## Directions

1. Place the groats in the pork broth with blood and let stand beside the fire to swell for a couple of hours but do not boil
2. Chop the onion and fry in lard, then strain this off and mix into the mixture along with raisins, candied fruit cut into fine cubes, cream and spices
3. Fill the mixture into pig stomach and wide pig intestines
4. Cook gently for about 1 hour
5. Serve either fresh, cut into slices with sugar, cinnamon and lingonberry jam, or fried in slices
