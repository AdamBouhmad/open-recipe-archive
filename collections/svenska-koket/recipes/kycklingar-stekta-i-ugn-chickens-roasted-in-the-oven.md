## Ingredients

- 3 chickens, approximately 1/2 kg each
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g)
- 2 deciliters good broth

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens, singe them (see page 299), cut them open down the back, draw them, rinse very well and dry.
2. Brown the butter in a long pan, add the chickens with breasts down and brown on top of the stove, while basting frequently with the butter in the pan.
3. Turn them, sprinkle with salt, thin with the hot broth and let them roast in the oven until tender, or 15-20 minutes. During this time baste them frequently.
4. Cut the chickens in half, arrange, garnish and serve as in the preceding recipe.
5. Serve the sauce as a gravy by straining it, skimming it, boiling it together and seasoning it, or else thicken it and add cream (see no. 429).
