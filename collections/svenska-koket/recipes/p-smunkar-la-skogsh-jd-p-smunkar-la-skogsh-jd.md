## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 dcl thin cream
- 1 1/2 msk sugar (23 gr)
- 6 bitter almonds
- 10 sweet almonds
- 3 1/2 msk butter (70 gr)
- 110 gr flour
- 3-4 eggs
- 1 kg cooking oil for poaching

## Directions

1. Butter a round-bottomed saucepan with 1/2 msk of the butter, pour in the cream, and add the sugar and the blanched and grated almonds.
2. Bring the cream to a boil, add the remaining butter, and whisk in the flour with a steel whisk.
3. Let the batter cook until it releases from the pan.
4. Remove the pan from heat, work in the eggs, one at a time, and stir the mixture 10 minutes.
5. Shape the mixture with two tablespoons into round balls the size of a small almond cake, which are poached a nice golden brown in the cooking oil.
6. The puffs should be shaped as smooth as possible, and no more than 3 at a time should be poached.
7. The cooking oil should not be too hot, as then the puffs cannot puff up.
8. Turn them with a slotted spoon, and when the puffs have a golden brown color on both sides, remove them with a slotted spoon and place them on absorbent paper on a hot baking sheet in the oven.
9. Sprinkle with sugar and serve warm with jam, as a dessert, or with coffee or tea.
