## Ingredients

- 20 ripe apricots
- 6 dcl sugar syrup
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Split the apricots and remove the pits, crushing the pits and saving the kernels.
2. Cook the kernels in the sugar syrup, then let it cool and strain.
3. Pass the apricots through a sieve and blend into the syrup along with lemon juice.
