## Ingredients

- 1 kg pork hare
- 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon ginger
- 3 beautiful, tart apples
- 1 hectogram prunes
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon solid meat extract (4 grams)

## Directions

1. Free the hare from most of the fat and dry it with a cloth wrung out in hot water.
2. Rub it with the lemon juice and spices, well blended together.
3. Peel the apples and cut them into thin slices.
4. Rinse the prunes well in lukewarm water, let them lie for 10 minutes in hot water, wrap them in a cloth and pit them.
5. Cut the hare open lengthwise, fill it with the apples and prunes and sew it together.
6. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, lay the hare in with the meat side down and brown it well on all sides.
7. Dilute first with hot prune stock and thereafter with water, mixed with meat extract and salt.
8. Let the hare roast slowly with the lid tightly closed until tender, approximately 1 3/4 hours, basting it over approximately every 10 minutes.
9. Serve with fried potatoes and peas.
