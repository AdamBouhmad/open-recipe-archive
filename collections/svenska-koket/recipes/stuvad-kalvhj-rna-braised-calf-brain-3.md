## Ingredients

- 1 calf brain
- water
- vinegar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 parts sour cream
- salt
- white pepper
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the calf brain in water so the blood is drawn out. Blanch it (that is, place it in cold water and bring to a boil) in vinegar-mixed water, take it up and let it drain. Place it again, but in boiling salted water, and let it simmer 20-30 minutes.
2. Take up the brain and cut into small cubes. Fry butter and flour together, and dilute the cream into it, then let the sauce simmer 10 minutes.
3. Add the brain, and season the stewing with pepper, salt and, if desired, with lemon juice.
