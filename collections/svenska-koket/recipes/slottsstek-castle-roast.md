## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg inner loin of beef
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1 liter water
- 8 allspice berries
- 4 white peppercorns
- 1/2 tbsp syrup
- 1 tbsp cognac
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 3 anchovies
- 1 small red onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 1/2 tbsp butter
- 1 1/2 tbsp flour
- meat gravy
- 1/2 deciliter cream

## Directions

1. Wash the roast with a cloth dipped in hot water
2. Brown in the heated butter, then salt and thin with boiling water
3. Add spices, cleaned anchovies, and the peeled but whole onion, and let the roast simmer slowly until done, about 3 hours
4. Strain the meat gravy and skim it; prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules
