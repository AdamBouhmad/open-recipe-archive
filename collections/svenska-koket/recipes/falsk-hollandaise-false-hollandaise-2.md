## Ingredients

- 60 gr. butter
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.)
- 2 gr. salt
- 1 gr. sugar
- 1/2 gr. white pepper
- 4 egg yolks
- 2 dcl. water
- 1 tsk. lemon juice

## Directions

1. Stir the butter in a lukewarm saucepan until it becomes white and frothy, then stir in the flour and the egg yolks one by one, the salt, sugar, and pepper.
2. Set the saucepan over low heat, add the cold water little by little with constant whisking, and let the sauce simmer until it is thick and glossy.
3. Remove from heat, season at once with lemon juice, and keep warm in a water bath.
