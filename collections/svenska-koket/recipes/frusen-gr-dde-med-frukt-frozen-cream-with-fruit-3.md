## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter heavy cream
- 2 cups raspberry jam or other jam

## Directions

1. The cream is whisked to stiff peaks and then mixed with the jam.
2. It is poured into a freezing mold and frozen, though not too hard.
3. The frozen cream is packed in an ice form, which is placed on ice until it is to be served, when it is turned out and garnished with fruit.
