## Ingredients

- 2 large salt fat herrings
- 3 eggs
- 1 hectogram good butter

## Directions

1. Skin the herring, remove the insides, rinse and flake it; remove the backbone and soak the herring the day before use
2. Wrap in a fish towel and carefully remove all small bones, then cut into small cubes
3. Hard-boil the eggs, cool in cold water and cut likewise into small cubes
4. Mix the herring and eggs in a bowl and pour the clarified butter over
5. Serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish
