## Ingredients

- 1 can chanterelles (1/4 liter)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1/2 deciliter chanterelle stock
- 3 deciliters cream
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 3 deciliters milk
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the mold

## Directions

1. Pat the chanterelles in a cloth, slice them and fry in butter until it becomes clear.
2. Squeeze the mushroom out of the butter with a spoon.
3. Fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the chanterelle stock and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Add the mushrooms, season the stewing with spices, place in a buttered omelet mold and whisk the egg mixture well and pour over.
5. Cook the omelet in the oven and serve at the smorgasbord.
6. Instead of the stewing, smoked ham, cut into cubes and fried in butter, or grated cheese can be used.
