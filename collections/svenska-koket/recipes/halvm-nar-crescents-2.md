## Ingredients

- 150 gr. butter
- 160 gr. granulated sugar
- 3 eggs
- 110 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tbsp. butter for the pan
- jelly or jam for filling
- 2 tbsp. granulated sugar for the glaze
- 1 tbsp. water for the glaze

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, let it cool and stir with the sugar until it becomes white and foamy.
2. Add the eggs alternately with the flour.
3. Place a plätt pan in the oven to heat, then brush with melted butter.
4. Put ½ tbsp. of the batter in each cavity.
5. Place the plätt pan in the oven, and when the cakes are baked, remove them.
6. Place two together on top of each other with jelly or jam between.
7. Glaze the cake with water glaze and cut into two halves.
