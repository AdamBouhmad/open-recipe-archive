## Ingredients

- 50 gr. unsweetened chocolate
- 150 gr. sugar
- 4 dcl. milk
- 15 gr. potato starch
- 5 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Whisk the chocolate, sugar, milk and starch in a saucepan over the fire until the mixture boils, then remove the saucepan and add the egg yolks immediately, one at a time, with vigorous whisking.
2. When the mixture is cold, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks, pour the mass into a buttered, oven-safe mold and bake in a water bath in ordinary oven heat for approximately 40 minutes.
3. Serve immediately from the mold with vanilla sauce.
