## Ingredients

- 6 hg. beef top round or boneless inner thigh
- 3 hg. smoked ham
- 6 eggs
- 2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.) for frying
- 1 dcl. boiling broth
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the eggs

## Directions

1. The meat is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and cut across the muscle fibers into approximately 1 cm. thick slices, which are beaten on both sides, peppered and salted.
2. Thin slices are cut from the smoked ham.
3. A frying pan is heated slowly, the butter is browned in it, the steaks are laid in and fried approximately 1 minute on each side, after which they are placed on a warm serving platter.
4. The pan is whisked out with the boiling broth and the sauce is poured over the steaks.
5. The pan is heated again and the ham slices are placed, one slice is laid on each steak.
6. Meanwhile the eggs are fried in a griddle and one egg is placed on each steak.
7. They are served with freshly boiled or fried potatoes as a breakfast dish.
