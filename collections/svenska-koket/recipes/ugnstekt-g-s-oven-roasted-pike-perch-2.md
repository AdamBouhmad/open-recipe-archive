## Ingredients

- 1 pike-perch of 1 1/2 kg.
- 1 msk. salt (15 gr.)
- 1 hg. butter
- 2 msk. chopped parsley
- 2 msk. chopped mushrooms
- 2 tomatoes in dice
- 1/2 egg
- 2 msk. crushed bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Scale the pike-perch, clean according to page 171; keep the head on but cut off the gills and fins.
2. Score with 2 cm. between each cut, rub with salt and let rest 1 hour, then dry again.
3. Cream 2 msk. (40 gr.) of the butter until white and fluffy, and mix with the parsley, mushrooms and tomatoes; fill the pike-perch with this mixture, then sew it shut.
4. Brush with egg and sprinkle with crushed bread crumbs.
5. Grease a long pan with some of the butter over an area as large as the fish occupies; place the pike-perch in it with the back up, arrange the remaining butter in flakes on top.
6. Put in a warm oven and let roast about 1/2 hour, basting often with the butter.
7. Arrange pike-perch on a hot plate, remove the thread, pour the butter from the pan over the fish and garnish with parsley and fluted lemon slices.
8. Serve with mushroom or tomato sauce.
