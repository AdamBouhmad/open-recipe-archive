## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg spinach
- 3 liters water for blanching
- 4 teaspoons salt (20 gr.) for blanching
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour (15 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 1 1/2–2 deciliters cream, milk, or broth
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1–2 teaspoons sugar
- 2–3 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the spinach as in the previous recipe, pour into a colander and let cool. Stir in the egg yolks one at a time.
2. When the spinach is cold, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pudding mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour.
4. Unmold and serve immediately as a side dish with melted butter, or garnish with small cooked carrots, fried potatoes, and peas and serve alongside roasted meat.
5. The mixture can also be baked in small pastry molds in a water bath in the oven for 20–30 minutes. Unmold and serve as garnish around roasted meat.
