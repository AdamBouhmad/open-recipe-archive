## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- Butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare, arrange and serve the duck as you would a goose, but roast for 1 1/2 to 2 hours. One duck is calculated for 4-6 people. They are best from September to February. At other times of year Russian ducks are available, which are cheaper and can be very good; they should be bought frozen.
