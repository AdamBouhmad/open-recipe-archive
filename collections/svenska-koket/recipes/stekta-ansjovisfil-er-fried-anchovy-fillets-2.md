## Ingredients

- 12 anchovies
- 4 dcl. milk
- 2 tbsp. flour (20 g)
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 g)
- 1 small bunch parsley

## Directions

1. Skin the anchovies, remove the insides, split down the middle, remove the bone and let the fillets soak in milk for 1 hour.
2. Take them up, wrap in white paper, turn in flour and fry beautifully golden brown in 2 tbsp. of the butter.
3. Lay up on a warm dish, garnish with parsley fried in the remaining butter and serve to the smorgasbord.
