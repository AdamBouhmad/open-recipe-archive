## Ingredients

- 600 grams fresh salmon
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg for breading
- 1 tablespoon water for breading
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams) for breading
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for breading
- 150 grams butter for frying and serving
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the salmon piece and dry with a cloth dipped in cold water.
2. Cut into 1.5 centimeter thick slices, flatten with a knife blade and rub with salt and pepper.
3. Dip in egg beaten with water, roll in a mixture of flour and rusks and fry until golden brown in half of the butter.
4. Serve the cutlets as a breakfast dish with the remaining butter, browned.
