## Ingredients

- 8 oranges
- 2 to 2.5 kg granulated sugar
- 40 grams tartaric acid
- 2.67 liters cold water

## Directions

1. Dry the oranges well with a damp cloth and then grate them against the sugar.
2. Blend the sugar, orange juice, crushed tartaric acid and water in a crock and let stand until everything is melted.
3. Stir the mixture occasionally.
4. When the juice is completely clear, strain it and bottle it; cork the bottles and seal with resin.
