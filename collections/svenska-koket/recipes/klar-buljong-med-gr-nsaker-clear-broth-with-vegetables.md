## Ingredients

- 1 carrot
- 1 small parsnip
- ¼ celery root
- ½ leek
- 12 small Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 small head of cauliflower
- ¾ liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon sugar
- 1¼ liters clear broth

## Directions

1. Scrape, rinse, and dry the roots, then cut into fine dice
2. Cut the leek into fine strips
3. Trim and shape the Jerusalem artichokes into rounds
4. Clean the cauliflower and divide into smaller pieces
5. Place the vegetables in boiling water with salt and sugar and let cook for 5 minutes
6. Add the cauliflower and let everything cook until tender
7. Bring the broth to a boil and add the cooked vegetables along with ¼ liter of the cooking liquid
8. Season the soup with salt and cayenne and serve very hot
