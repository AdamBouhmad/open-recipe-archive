## Ingredients

- 1 litre shelled sugar peas, approximately 5 litres unshelled
- 10 small fresh carrots
- 1/2 litre water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 4-4 1/2 decilitres pea cooking liquid
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the peas as in the previous recipe.
2. Rinse the carrots, scrape them, rinse again, dry and cut into fine dice.
3. Place the peas and carrots in boiling water with salt and sugar, and let them cook slowly until tender without falling apart.
4. Drain the cooking liquid and reserve it.
5. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the pea cooking liquid, and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
6. Add the peas and carrots together with the parsley, and taste the stew to adjust seasoning.
7. Serve with fried fish, boiled ham, farces, etc.
