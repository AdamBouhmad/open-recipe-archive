## Ingredients

- 2 hg. calf sweetbreads
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 1 dcl. sweetbread broth
- 4 dcl. cream
- 2—3 truffles
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. The calf sweetbreads should soak in water for 1 hour so that the blood is drawn out.
2. Blanch them (place in cold, lightly salted water and let simmer 10 minutes) and then cook in 1/2 liter boiling water with salt added for about 1/2 hour or until they become white and firm.
3. Place on a sieve so that the broth drains well.
4. Cut the sweetbreads into fine cubes and fry in the butter until it is clear.
5. Remove it from the butter with a spoon.
6. Fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the sweetbread broth and cream and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes.
7. Add the calf sweetbreads and the finely sliced truffle, and let the stew come to a boil and taste it.
8. Finally stir in the beaten egg yolk, let the stew simmer and fill immediately into the crusts.
9. Serve as a side dish.
