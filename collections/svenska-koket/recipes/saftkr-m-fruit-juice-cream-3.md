## Ingredients

- 4 dcl sweet or sour juice
- 6 dcl water (if juice is sour)
- 3 dcl sugar
- 50 gr mondamin flour or 50 gr potato flour

## Directions

1. The cream turns out best if two kinds of juice are used.
2. Mix juice and water and add sugar if the juice is sour.
3. Let the mixture come to a boil, taste it and add more sugar if needed, and thicken with the flour stirred in 1 dcl cold water.
4. After thickening, let the cream cook for 5 minutes if mondamin flour was used, but only 2 minutes if potato flour was used.
5. Pour the cream immediately into a compote bowl, rinsed with lukewarm water, and sprinkle with a little sugar so it does not form a skin.
