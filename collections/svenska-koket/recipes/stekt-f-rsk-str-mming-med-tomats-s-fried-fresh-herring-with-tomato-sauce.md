## Ingredients

- 30 herring
- 1½ tablespoons salt
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 80 grams butter (for frying)
- 6 tomatoes
- 1½ tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 3 decilitres stock
- 1 decilitre cream
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare and fry the herring as in recipe 163 and arrange on a warm serving dish.
2. For the sauce, peel the tomatoes and press through a sieve.
3. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the stock, tomato purée and cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
4. Finish with egg yolk, season with salt and pepper and pour over the herring.
5. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
