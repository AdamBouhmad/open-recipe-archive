## Ingredients

- 150 gr. idealmakaroner (ideal macaroni)
- 1 1/2 lit. vatten (water)
- 1/2 msk. salt (salt)
- 4 hg. rökt skinka (smoked ham)
- 4 ägg (eggs)
- 3 kkp mjölk (milk)
- 1/2 tsk. salt (salt)
- 1/4 tsk. socker (sugar)
- 1/2 msk. smör (butter)
- 2 msk. stötta skorpor (breadcrumbs)

## Directions

1. Rinse the macaroni, place in boiling salted water and let cook 15-20 minutes.
2. Strain in a colander and let water drain well.
3. Cut the ham into small cubes, mix well with the macaroni and place the mixture in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined baking dish.
4. Beat the eggs well with the milk and seasonings, and pour the egg mixture over the arrangement in the dish.
5. Bake the pudding in ordinary oven heat for approximately 45 minutes.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish.
