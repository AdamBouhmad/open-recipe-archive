## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 hectogrammes shrimp
- 12 pieces tender asparagus buds or canned asparagus
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 6 eggs
- 4 decilitres boiling milk

## Directions

1. Let the shrimp soak in milk for about 1 hour, then clean them.
2. Blanch the asparagus for a few minutes in lightly salted water, let it drain and fry well in half of the butter until the butter is clear.
3. Whisk the eggs well and add the boiling milk while whisking.
4. Melt the remaining butter in a pot and pour in the egg mixture.
