## Ingredients

- 2 parts carolina rice grains
- 1.5 liters unskimmed milk
- 2 to 4 eggs
- 1.5 cups powdered sugar
- 12 bitter almonds
- 75 grams butter
- 0.5 cup raisins
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains and cook them in the milk to a porridge, which is set aside to cool.
2. Mix in the sugar, eggs and butter, as well as the blanched and grated bitter almonds.
3. Pour the porridge into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated baking dish, tie over with buttered paper, bake in the oven for about 1 hour and serve warm with jam or fruit sauce.
