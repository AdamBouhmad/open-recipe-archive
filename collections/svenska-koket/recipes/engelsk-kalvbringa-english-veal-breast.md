## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg veal breast
- 50 grams kidney fat
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 1/4 cup crushed rusks
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 1/2 liters broth or water
- 1 carrot
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- the broth in which the breast was cooked

## Directions

1. Bone the breast and wash with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Chop the kidney fat with the parsley and mix with the crushed rusks, salt, and eggs.
3. Spread the mixture on the breast, roll it up tightly, and bind in three places.
4. Place in the boiling liquid and cook with the carrot and onion until tender, approximately 2 hours.
5. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the breast in it, and set in the oven to achieve a nice brown color.
6. Remove, cut into nice pieces, and arrange on the serving plate.
7. From the browned butter, flour, and strained broth, prepare a light sauce, taste and serve in a separate sauce bowl.
