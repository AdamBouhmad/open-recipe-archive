## Ingredients

- 1 kg fruit juice
- 1 kg lump sugar
- 2½ dcl water

## Directions

1. Prepare the juice as previously described.
2. Chop the sugar into small pieces, place it in a preserving pan, pour the water over it, and let the pan stand beside the stove until the sugar is dissolved.
3. Place the pan over the fire, and let the mixture cook, skimming it well, until all the water has evaporated and the sugar is dry.
4. Towards the end of cooking, stir the sugar continuously with a wooden spoon so it does not burn at the bottom of the pan.
5. Add the juice at once, little by little, otherwise the enamel of the pan will crack.
6. When the sugar has melted, let the jelly cook about 5 minutes; it is ready when a drop of it, poured on a plate, sets quickly.
7. Bind the jars when the jelly is set.
