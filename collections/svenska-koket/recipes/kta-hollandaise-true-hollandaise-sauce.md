## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp. fine vinegar
- 1 tbsp. water
- 2 gr. table salt
- 1 gr. sugar
- 1/2 gr. white pepper
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp. cold water
- 325 gr. melted butter
- 1/2 tsp. lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pour the vinegar, water, salt, sugar, and pepper into a well-cleaned sauce pot with a flat, thick bottom and let it boil down until 1/4 remains.
2. Move the pot to the side and add the whipped egg yolks and water, letting the sauce simmer with vigorous whisking.
3. Remove from heat and pour in the melted, cooled butter gradually while continuing to whisk.
4. Season with lemon juice, strain through a cloth, and keep warm in a water bath.
