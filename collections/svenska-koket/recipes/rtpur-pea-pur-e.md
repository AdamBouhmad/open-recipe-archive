## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter leftover pea soup
- 1.5 teaspoons solid meat extract (12 g)
- 1.5 liters boiling water
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 to 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The pea soup is heated and passed through a sieve
2. The meat extract is dissolved in 1/2 cup of the boiling water and added to the remaining water together with 2 teaspoons salt, after which the broth is brought to a good boil
3. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes, the purée and broth are added, little by little, and the soup is allowed to cook with stirring for 5 minutes
