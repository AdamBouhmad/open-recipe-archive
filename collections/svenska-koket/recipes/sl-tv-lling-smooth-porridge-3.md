## Ingredients

- 1 small cup flour
- 2 liters milk
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with 3 cups of the milk and let it swell for about 1 hour.
2. Melt the butter in a pot and add the milk and bring to a boil.
3. Add the cinnamon and flour mixture, and let the porridge simmer 10-15 minutes while stirring.
4. Season and serve.
