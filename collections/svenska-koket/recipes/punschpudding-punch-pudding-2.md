## Ingredients

- 1 twelve öre wheat bread
- 1 kg. butter
- 4 eggs
- ¾–1 liter unwhipped milk
- 1½ tbsp. sugar
- 1 part punch
- ½ tbsp. butter (for the form)

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into slices, spread them with butter and place in a buttered pie form.
2. Whisk the eggs, milk and sugar together and pour over the bread in the form.
3. When the bread slices are completely soaked through, place the pudding in not too hot an oven to bake until it has taken on a nice brown color.
4. Just before serving, prick the pudding with a fork, and pour the punch over it.
5. Serve warm with fruit jelly.
