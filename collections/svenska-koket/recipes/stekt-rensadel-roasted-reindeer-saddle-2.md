## Ingredients

- 2.5 kilograms reindeer saddle
- 4 liters drink (marinade liquid)
- 1 kilogram lard
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 0.75–1 liter broth or water
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for sauce
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat drippings
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Place the meat in drink overnight.
2. Remove it, rinse well and dry with a cloth.
3. Carefully remove all skin, and lard the reindeer saddle in close rows with the lard cut into fine strips.
4. Heat the butter in a roasting pan and brown the roast well in it.
5. Salt it and add the broth a little at a time.
6. Baste often and turn a couple of times.
