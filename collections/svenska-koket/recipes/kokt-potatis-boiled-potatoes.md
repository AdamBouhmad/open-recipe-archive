## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters potatoes
- water
- 1 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Brush the potatoes, peel with thin peels and rinse well, and let them soak in water until they are to be cooked.
2. Place in cold water, just enough so it comes to the surface of the potatoes, salt and let simmer slowly until tender (about 20-30 minutes).
3. Pour off the water, place a cloth over, and shake the potatoes.
4. Potatoes can also be cooked unpeeled, in which case they only need to be brushed and rinsed well.
5. New potatoes should be placed in warm water when cooking.
6. The potatoes turn out best if cooked in steam in a so-called potato cooker.
