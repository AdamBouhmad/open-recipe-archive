## Ingredients

- 3 salad heads
- juice of 1 lemon
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves and also the stalks.
2. Rinse each leaf, then lay them on cloth to drain.
3. Arrange the salad on a plate, the larger leaves on the outside, the small ones in the middle, so that they together form a head.
4. Mix the lemon juice, water and sugar well together, and pour the sauce over the salad head.
