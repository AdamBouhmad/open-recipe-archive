## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sago grains
- 2 litres water
- ½ lemon
- 1 cup raisins
- 2 egg yolks
- 4–5 tablespoons sugar
- 1½ parts wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the sago grains and place in water with the lemon peel.
2. Rinse the raisins and cook them separately in a pot and add to the soup when the grains are clear.
3. In the soup bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, sugar, lemon juice and wine.
4. Pour the soup in under vigorous whisking and taste.
5. Serve with rusks.
