## Ingredients

- 1½ litres fresh or ¼ litre preserved gooseberries
- 2 litres water
- 2 cups crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Place the gooseberries in cold water together with the sugar and let them cook until the berries are soft.
2. Press the berries through a sieve.
3. Thicken the soup with potato flour mixed with a little cold water and bring to a good boil.
4. Serve with cream and rusks.
