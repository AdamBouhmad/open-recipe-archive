## Ingredients

- 1.5 hectograms dried apricots
- 75 grams sugar
- 8 parts water

## Directions

1. Apricots are rinsed in lukewarm water and are then left to soak for 2-3 hours in the water in which they are to be cooked.
2. They are then placed on to cook together with the sugar until they are completely soft, after which they are strained.
3. The sauce is brought to a boil, thinned with a little water if necessary, is allowed to cool and is served.
