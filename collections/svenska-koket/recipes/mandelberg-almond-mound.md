## Ingredients

- 125 gr. sweet almond
- 2 egg whites
- 2 hg. granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. The almond is blanched, cut lengthwise into thin strips, which are lightly roasted in the oven and allowed to cool.
2. The egg whites are stirred together with the sugar and lemon juice for approximately 1 hour in the same direction, after which the almond is added.
3. The mass is placed with teaspoon in high heaps on well buttered and floured sheet.
4. Baked in not too warm an oven until they are half-dry and have a light yellow color, when they are at once loosened from the sheet.
5. Before the pastries are baked they can be sprinkled with 10 gr. blanched and finely pounded pistachios.
6. Instead of almond, 1 tablespoon cocoa can be added.
