## Ingredients

- 2 liters fish broth
- 1 1/2 tablespoon arrowroot flour or potato flour
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Bring the broth to a boil, thicken it with the flour stirred in a little cold water, let it come to a good boil and taste it.
2. Serve very hot with lobster patties.
