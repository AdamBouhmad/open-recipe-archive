## Ingredients

- 1 bream weighing 2 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 8 white peppercorns
- 5 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 5 dill sprigs

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish (see page 171); the head may remain but must be freed from gills.
2. Rub it with salt and let it rest for 1 hour.
3. Boil it afterwards either whole or in pieces as in recipe no. 327; when the fins come loose easily, it is done.
4. The bream can be served either hot or cold.
5. If served hot, it should be boiled whole, placed on a napkin and garnished with parsley.
6. If served cold, it is boiled in pieces and the stock is strained over the arranged fish and allowed to set into jelly.
7. Whether warm or cold, serve it with cold horseradish sauce.
