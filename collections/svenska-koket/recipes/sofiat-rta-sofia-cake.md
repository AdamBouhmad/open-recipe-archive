## Ingredients

- 7 eggs
- 300 grams granulated sugar
- 50 grams sweet almonds
- 8 bitter almonds
- 150 grams semolina
- 1/2 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar for 1/2 hour.
2. During beating, add water twice, 1 tablespoon each time.
3. Blanch the almonds, dry them well in the oven, and grate or press through an almond mill and blend with the semolina.
4. Stir the dry mixture into the egg batter, and last fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-strewn form or pan and bake for 1 hour in moderate oven heat.
6. This cake is particularly good with coffee and can, if desired, be glazed with chocolate glaze, or garnished like an ordinary cake.
