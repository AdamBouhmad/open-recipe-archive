## Ingredients

- 220 gr. grated cheese (Roquefort, Gorgonzola, Stilton and Parmesan)
- 2 tbsp. grated mesost
- 80 gr. good butter
- 1-2 tbsp. fine cognac

## Directions

1. Mix the grated cheese varieties (leftover cheese of any sort may be used) and work well with the butter.
2. Add cognac to taste.
3. Pack the cheese spread in a porcelain jar with a lid and store in a cool place.
