## Ingredients

- 120 gr. butter
- 2 tbsp. chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Brown the butter until it takes on a beautiful light brown color, but do not let it burn.
2. Add the parsley and pour the butter at once over the dish with which it is to be served, or serve it in a sauce bowl.
