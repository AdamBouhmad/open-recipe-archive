## Ingredients

- 80 gr butter
- 2 deciliters finely crushed rusks
- 1/2 liter finely chopped cooked lye fish (leftovers)
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 liter cream milk
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for mold (10 gr)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for mold

## Directions

1. Clarify the butter and mix with the crushed rusks
2. Add the lye fish along with the well-beaten eggs
3. Boil the milk and pour it hot over the mixture, then stir well
4. Season with salt, sugar and white pepper
5. Pour into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie form
6. Bake in oven until it has acquired a nice golden-brown color or approximately 40 minutes
7. Serve with clarified butter and small boiled potatoes
