## Ingredients

- 1/2 decilitre rice grains
- 3 1/2 decilitres milk
- 1 goose liver
- 30 g sultana raisins
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon marjoram
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 10 g butter, for the onion
- 1 small egg

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, blanch in hot water, rinse again, let drain, place in the boiling milk and let cook until soft, then remove the porridge and let it cool
2. Pound the liver, pass through a hair sieve and mix in together with the cleaned and blanched raisins, the spices and the lightly browned onion
3. Last stir in the egg and taste and adjust the forcemeat and fill it into the well-cleaned and sewn at one end goose neck skin
4. Do not fill the sausage more than 3/4, otherwise it will easily burst, sew it closed and let it cook slowly in salted water, without a lid, for about 1/2 hour
5. Allow to cool, cut into slices and serve with black soup
