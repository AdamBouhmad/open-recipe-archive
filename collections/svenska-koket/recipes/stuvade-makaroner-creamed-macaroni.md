## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hg good macaroni
- 2 litres boiling water
- 1 tbsp salt (15 g)
- 2 tbsp butter (40 g) for the sauce
- 4 tbsp flour (40 g)
- 4 dcl boiling milk
- salt
- sugar
- 1/2 tbsp grated cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into 4 cm long pieces, place in boiling salted water and let cook until tender, or approximately 20 minutes.
2. Pour into a colander, rinse with cold water and let drain.
3. For the sauce, fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
4. Add the macaroni and warm it in the sauce.
5. Season the creamed macaroni with salt, sugar and a little grated cheese, if desired.
6. Serve with meat dishes.
