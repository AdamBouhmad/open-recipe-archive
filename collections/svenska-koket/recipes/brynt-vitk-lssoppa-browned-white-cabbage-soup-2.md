## Ingredients

- 1 small cabbage head
- 3 lit. water for blanching
- 1 msk. salt for blanching
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 2 msk. syrup
- 3 lit. stock or pork broth
- 10 allspice berries
- 8 white peppercorns
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove all green leaves from the cabbage head, loosen the remaining leaves from the stem, cut away the thickest part of the main rib.
2. Blanch the cabbage (place in boiling, salted water and let it cook without a lid) 5 minutes, remove it with a slotted spoon, let it drain in a sieve, wrap it in a cloth and cut it into small cubes.
3. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the cabbage together with the syrup and brown it well with frequent stirring.
4. Add the boiling stock and spices and let the soup cook with a tightly closed lid about 3 hours.
5. Season with salt and serve with meatballs, sausage or pork.
