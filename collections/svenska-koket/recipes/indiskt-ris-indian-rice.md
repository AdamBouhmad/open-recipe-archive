## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. Patna rice grains (2 hg.)
- 3 l. water
- 1 1/2 tbsp. salt (23 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains well in cold water, let them drain, place them in boiling salted water and let them boil vigorously for 15 minutes; drain the water and fill the saucepan with cold water, in which the rice grains are shaken so that they separate from each other.
2. Pour them into a colander, let them drain, empty them onto a cloth laid on a sieve, and let them stand in a moderately warm oven for 15 minutes, during which they are often stirred with a fork, so that the water evaporates.
3. Serve with meat, garnished with parsley.
