## Ingredients

- 4 parts thick sour cream
- 6 eggs
- 150 gr. sugar
- grated zest of 1/2 lemon
- 2 1/2 parts dry wheat flour
- 1/2 tablespoon butter and 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. The cream is whipped to stiff peaks.
2. The egg yolks are stirred with the sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy; the well-dried flour is sifted in with constant stirring.
3. The lemon zest is added.
4. The egg mixture is blended into the cream, and the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks are folded in carefully.
5. The mixture is beaten into a buttered and crumb-strewn mold and placed in a fairly hot oven.
6. When the cake is done, it cracks.
7. It is sprinkled with sugar and served at once.
