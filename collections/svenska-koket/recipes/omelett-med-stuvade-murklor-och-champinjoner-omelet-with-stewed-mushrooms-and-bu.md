## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 0.5 liter sour milk
- 2 tablespoons wheat flour
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 teaspoon butter for the pan
- 5-6 large mushrooms (fresh or dried)
- 1 can button mushrooms (0.25 liter) or 4-5 medium fresh button mushrooms
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons wheat flour
- 1.5 deciliter cream
- button mushroom liquid
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs with salt for half an hour. Add the flour and milk.
2. Season the batter, pour into a well-buttered omelet pan and bake light yellow in ordinary oven heat.
3. Immediately turn the omelet out, place the stew on half the omelet, and fold the other half over.
4. For the stew: wash the mushrooms (and fresh button mushrooms if using) and place in cold water to boil until soft.
5. Fry butter and flour together, add the sour milk, and let the stew boil 10 minutes.
6. Cut the mushrooms and button mushrooms into appropriate-sized pieces and mix into the stew along with the button mushroom liquid and some of the mushroom liquid.
7. Season with salt and white pepper; finally stir in the egg yolk and let the stew simmer over heat.
