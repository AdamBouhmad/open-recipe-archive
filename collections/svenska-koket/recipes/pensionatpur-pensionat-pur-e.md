## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 rutabaga
- 1 small celery
- 1 small leek
- 4 parsley stalks
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 liter milk
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1 liter secondary broth
- salt, cayenne

## Directions

1. Prepare the vegetables in the usual way and cut into pieces.
2. Heat the water and milk, add the vegetables with 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter and let them cook until completely soft, then pass through a sieve.
3. Prepare the purée thereafter and serve as in recipe no. 266.
