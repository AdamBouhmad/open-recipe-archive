## Ingredients

- 210 gr. butter
- 75 gr. granulated sugar
- 10 bitter almonds
- 290 gr. flour
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 40 gr. sweet almond

## Directions

1. Wash the butter well and mix it with the sugar, the scalded and ground almond and the sifted flour.
2. Work the dough until it is smooth and even, then divide it into six equal parts, which are rolled out to finger-thick sticks and each of these is cut into 10 equally long pieces.
3. Brush the breads with lightly beaten egg white, turn in scalded and finely chopped almond and place on a baking sheet.
4. Bake the cakes beautifully golden brown in a not too warm oven.
