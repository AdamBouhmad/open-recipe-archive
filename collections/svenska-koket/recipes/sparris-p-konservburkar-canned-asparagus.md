## Ingredients

- Asparagus
- Cooked cooled salt brine (1 tablespoon salt per liter of water)

## Directions

1. Asparagus can be preserved either cut into pieces or whole. In the latter case, the asparagus stalks should be of equal length. Peel them and place them in the canning jars with the tips pointing upward. Pour on cooked cooled salt brine (1 tablespoon salt per liter of water) and screw the jars shut or seal them. Asparagus cut into pieces cooks for 1.5 hours; whole asparagus for 2.5 hours.
