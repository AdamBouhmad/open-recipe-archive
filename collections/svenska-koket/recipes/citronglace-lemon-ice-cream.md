## Ingredients

- 750 gr. sugar
- 1 liter water
- zest of 3 lemons
- juice of 8 lemons

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar syrup and let it cool
2. Remove all white pith from the lemon zest
3. Cut the zest into pieces and let it soak in the cold sugar syrup for approximately 3 hours
4. Add the lemon juice
5. Strain the mixture directly into the ice cream machine
