## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 4 parts strong meat stock
- 1 can mushrooms of 1/2 liter
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the mushrooms, fry them in the butter and remove.
2. Fry the flour thereafter with the butter, add the mushroom liquid and meat stock with frequent stirring, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
3. Add the fried mushrooms, and taste the sauce.
