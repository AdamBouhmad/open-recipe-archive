## Ingredients

- 500 g. veal without bone
- 50 g. butter
- 2 eggs
- 2.5 portions thin cream
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 small tablespoon salt
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 50 g. macaroni

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, free it from sinews and membranes, cut it into cubes and pass three times through the meat grinder
2. Pound it thereafter in a mortar together with the butter and egg yolks
3. Work the cream together with flour, salt and white pepper into the forcemeat
4. Grease a nice mold with butter and garnish it with blanched macaroni cut into rings and stars
5. Beat the egg whites until stiff and fold them last into the forcemeat, which is immediately poured into the prepared mold
6. Bind the mold over with buttered paper and bake the timbal in a water bath in the oven or on the stove for 1-1.5 hours
7. Turn it out and serve with preserved peas, covered with clarified butter
