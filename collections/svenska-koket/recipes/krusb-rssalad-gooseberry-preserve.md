## Ingredients

- 2 liters gooseberries
- 0.67 liters good vinegar
- 425 grams granulated sugar
- 4 cloves
- 1 blade of mace

## Directions

1. Top and rinse the berries.
2. Cook the vinegar with the sugar to a clear solution and let it cook long enough that a cooled sample threads between the fingers.
3. Add the berries with the spices, and let everything cook slowly for about 30 minutes, shaking the berries from time to time.
4. Pour the preserve into glass jars and cover them.
