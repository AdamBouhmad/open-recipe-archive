## Ingredients

- 1/2 litre milk
- 75 gr. butter
- 2 eggs
- 25 gr. granulated sugar
- peel and juice of 1/4 lemon
- 30 gr. pressed yeast
- 4 hectograms wheat flour
- apple sauce or currant jelly for filling
- 1/2 kg. oil for frying

## Directions

1. Warm the milk and blend it with the clarified butter, the beaten eggs, the sugar, the lemon peel and juice, and the yeast dissolved in a little warm milk.
2. Work in the flour, beat the dough smooth and glossy, and let it rise about 1 hour.
3. Work the dough and roll it out finger-thick, and cut small round cakes with a glass.
4. Place a little currant jelly or apple sauce in the middle of the cake and another cake on top; brush the edges with egg white and press them well together.
5. Let the doughnuts rise 1 hour.
6. Cook them in hot oil, place them on grey paper to drain, and roll in sugar.
7. Serve warm.
