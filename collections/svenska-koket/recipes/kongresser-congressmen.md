## Ingredients

- 200 grams wheat flour
- 200 grams butter
- 1 part water
- 1 tablespoon cognac
- 3 eggs
- 300 grams granulated sugar
- 250 grams sweet almond

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as simple butter dough (no. 549)
2. Roll out quite thin and line small pastry molds with it
3. Blanch the almond and grate it or pass it through an almond mill
4. Mix the eggs and sugar well together, blend in the almond, and fill the molds halfway with this mixture
5. Cut approximately 1 cm wide strips from the remaining butter dough and lay these in a cross over the molds
6. Bake the pastries in good oven heat
