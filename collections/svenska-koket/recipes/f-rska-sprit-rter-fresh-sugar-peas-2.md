## Ingredients

- 1 liter tender sugar peas, or about 5 liters unpodded
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Place the peas in a colander, rinse over with cold water and let drain.
2. Place them in boiling water, seasoned with salt and sugar, and let them cook slowly until they are tender.
3. Strain off the liquid, place the peas on a serving dish and mix well with the parsley and the cold butter.
4. The peas can also, after the liquid is strained off, be shaken in melted butter together with the parsley and served as garnish around roasted meat, such as filet, chops, calf saddle, etc.
