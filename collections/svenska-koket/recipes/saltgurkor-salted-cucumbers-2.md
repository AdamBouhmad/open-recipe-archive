## Ingredients

- 1 kg coarse salt
- 3 liters water
- 100 small cucumbers
- cherry and currant leaves
- dill flower clusters
- 2 to 3 tablespoons spice peppercorns
- For the brine: 3 liters vinegar
- 1.25 liters water

## Directions

1. Mix the salt and water cold together.
2. When the salt is completely dissolved, place the cucumbers in the brine and let them lie there for three days.
3. Remove the cucumbers and place them in a vat or stone crock layered with leaves, dill flower clusters and peppercorns.
4. Each cucumber should be wrapped with leaves so that they do not lie tightly packed.
