## Ingredients

- 2 kg meat bones
- 3 tablespoons lard
- 4 liters water
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 carrots
- 1 small bunch parsley stalks
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 teacup star pasta
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese (preferably Roquefort)
- 2 tablespoons sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Brown the good meat bones in the lard
2. Add them to cook in 4 liters water
3. Skim the liquid well when it comes to a boil
4. Add seasonings, carrots and the parsley bunch
5. Let it all cook with a tightly closed lid until the broth has good flavor, which takes 4-5 hours
6. Strain and skim the broth well
7. Brown the butter and flour together, add the broth and the rinsed star pasta
8. Let it cook about 20 minutes, or until the pasta is completely soft and swollen
9. Add the grated cheese and wine
10. Taste and adjust seasonings
