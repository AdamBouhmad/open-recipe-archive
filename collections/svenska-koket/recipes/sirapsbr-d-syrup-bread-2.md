## Ingredients

- 1 liter beer
- 1 kg coarse rye flour
- 2 liters unskim milk
- 100 gr pressed yeast
- 3 kg wheat flour
- 1 liter syrup
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp fennel
- 3 tbsp finely cut orange peel
- 2 hg butter or margarine

## Directions

1. Heat the beer but do not let it boil.
2. Pour the coarse rye flour into a bowl, add the beer, and work the dough for 1/4 hour.
3. Let it stand for approximately 12 hours in a warm place, then add the lukewarm milk, the yeast dissolved in a little lukewarm milk, and the wheat flour.
4. Set the dough to rise to double its size.
5. Add the syrup, salt, the crushed fennel and the finely chopped orange peel, and work in wheat flour until the dough becomes quite firm.
6. Set it to rise again.
7. Finally, work the dough on the baking board and shape into oblong buns, place them on warmed and floured cloths to rise.
8. When they have risen well, place them on greased trays, brush with cold water and bake in a warm oven.
9. Twice during baking brush the buns with hot water, and again when the finished buns are removed from the oven.
