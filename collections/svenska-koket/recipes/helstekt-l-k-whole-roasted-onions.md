## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg small Portuguese onions
- 1 litre water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 60 gr butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr)

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, blanch (place in boiling, salted water and let boil 10 minutes), place on cloth and let drain.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, add the onions, sprinkle with sugar and let slowly fry with a lid, until they are nicely brown and completely soft, which is tested with a whisk.
3. Serve to the smorgasbord or with roasted meat, such as roast beef, roast mutton and lamb saddle.
