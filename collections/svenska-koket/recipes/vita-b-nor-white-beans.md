## Ingredients

- 3 deciliters white beans
- 1/2 liter water
- 4 deciliters bouillon
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the beans and rinse them
2. Put them in cold, soft water (see page 338) and let them cook slowly until completely tender, which takes about 3 hours
3. When the water has boiled down, thin with bouillon
4. Melt the butter in a frying pan, add the onion and beans and fry well with the butter
5. Season with spices and serve preferably with roasted lamb
