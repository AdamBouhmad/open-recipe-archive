## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg wheat flour
- 1 part water
- 1 tbsp brandy
- 1/8 egg
- 425 gr butter
- 2 cups apple sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the puff pastry as fine puff pastry.
2. Roll out half of the dough to a round cake of approximately 2 cm thickness.
3. Roll out the other half somewhat thinner and cut into strips, arrange these together into a lattice, suitable for the base.
4. Bake the cake base and lattice separately in a warm oven.
5. When they are well baked, place them on top of each other with apple sauce between.
6. The cake is served with whipped cream.
