## Ingredients

- 3 kg veal breast
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 1 carrot
- 1 shallot
- 1 liter broth with 1 tsp meat extract
- 2 hg boneless veal
- 1 hg fresh pork
- 2 tbsp butter (40 g)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 g)
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1-1.5 parts cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the breast as in the preceding recipe and rub with half of the seasonings.
2. Cook broth on the bones to thin the roast.
3. Prepare the forcemeat as in recipe no. 325 and it should be quite firm.
4. Spread the forcemeat on the inside of the breast, which is then treated and roasted as in the preceding recipe.
5. The scraped carrot, cut into slices, and the peeled onion are browned and roasted with the meat.
6. Serve with cooked vegetables or vegetable pastries and salads and strained sauce, mixed with cream (see recipe no. 429).
