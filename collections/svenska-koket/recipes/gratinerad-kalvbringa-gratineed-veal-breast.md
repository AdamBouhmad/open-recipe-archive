## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg veal breast
- 1.5 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 gr)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 1 carrot
- 5 parsley stems
- 2 deciliters rice grains
- 1.5 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr)
- 30 gr butter
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 gr)
- 6 deciliters broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 3 egg yolks
- 0.5 tablespoon cold butter
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons melted butter (40 gr)

## Directions

1. Dry the veal breast with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into even, attractive pieces
2. Place the meat in boiling water so that it stands level with the meat; skim well and add the spices, the sautéed carrot and the rinsed parsley stems
3. Let the meat cook slowly with a tightly closed lid until tender, about 1.5 hours
4. Rinse the rice grains, scald them in hot water, place them in boiling salted water and cook for 15-20 minutes, then pour them into a colander, let them drain well and mix, while they are warm, with the cold butter
5. For the sauce, fry butter and flour for 2 minutes; add the broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes, then season with spices and bind with egg yolks
6. Grease a fireproof dish with cold butter and sprinkle with half of the crushed rusks, then layer the meat, rice and sauce alternately
7. The top layer should be sauce, sprinkled with the remaining crushed rusks mixed with the grated cheese
8. Finally pour the clarified butter over and place the dish in a warm oven and gratinee for 15-20 minutes
