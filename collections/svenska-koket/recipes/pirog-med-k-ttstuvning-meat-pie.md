## Ingredients

- 250 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tablespoon brandy
- 1/2 egg
- 250 gr. butter
- water
- 1/4 egg for brushing
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons wheat flour
- 3/4-1 cup bouillon
- 3 cups cooked meat cut into cubes
- 1 small tin peas
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as fine butter dough; roll out half of the dough to a bottom (suitable to the serving dish). Lay this on a baking sheet and bake light brown.
2. For the stewing, fry butter and flour together, dilute the bouillon into it, and let the sauce simmer 10 minutes.
3. Place the meat and peas into the sauce, and season the stewing with salt and white pepper.
4. Spread the stewing on the baked butter dough bottom. Roll out the rest of the dough to a lid, lay it over the stewing and press down at the edges.
5. Garnish the lid with squares or leaves of butter dough. Brush with egg, and bake the pirog thereafter about 10-15 minutes in good oven heat. Serve at once.
