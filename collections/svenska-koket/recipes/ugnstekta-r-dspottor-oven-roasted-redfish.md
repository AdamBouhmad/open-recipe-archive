## Ingredients

- 2 kg redfish
- 2 tsk vinegar
- 2 msk salt
- white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1/2 kkp crushed hardtack
- 3 msk butter
- 2 kkp fish stock or water

## Directions

1. Scale, scrape, clean, and rinse the fish thoroughly, then dry it well and rub it with a mixture of the vinegar, salt, and a little white pepper, allowing it to rest for a while.
2. Brush the fish with the beaten egg and sprinkle with the crushed hardtack, then place it in a buttered long pan with half of the butter.
3. Place the remaining butter in small flakes on top of the fish and set it in the oven.
4. When it has taken on a beautiful golden-brown color, add fish stock or water and continue to cook for approximately 30 minutes more.
