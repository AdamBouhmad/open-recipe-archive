## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 5-6 deciliters good milk
- salt
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel small, even potatoes raw and shape them oblong with a vegetable knife.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, pour in the milk and let it come to a boil, add the potatoes and let them cook slowly with a lid in the oven until tender, at which point the sauce should be quite creamy.
3. Season the stew with salt and with parsley if desired.
4. Serve with salted meat and boiled sausage.
