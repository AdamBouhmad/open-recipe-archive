## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg cooked lye fish (leftovers)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr)
- 2 teaspoons ground Scanian mustard
- 6 deciliters milk
- salt
- sugar
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- cream
- clarified butter
- small boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the lye fish into even pieces
2. Fry butter, flour and mustard for 2 minutes
3. Dilute the milk and let the sauce simmer while stirring for 5 minutes
4. Carefully blend in the fish so it does not fall apart
5. Taste the braised dish and dilute with a little cream if it is too thick
6. Transfer to a warm serving platter and garnish with peeled eggs cut into wedges
7. Serve with clarified butter and small boiled potatoes
