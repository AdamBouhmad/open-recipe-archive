## Ingredients

- 5 salted herrings
- water
- 3 eggs
- 1½ hectograms butter

## Directions

1. Soak the herring the day before it is to be used.
2. Take it up, free it from skin and innards, and split it in half, after which the backbone and as many small bones as possible are removed.
3. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes, shell and chop them finely.
4. Place the herring in boiling water and let it cook 3-5 minutes, after which it is placed on a warm plate, in layers with the chopped eggs. The top layer should be egg.
5. Melt the butter and pour it over, and the dish is served with boiled potatoes.
