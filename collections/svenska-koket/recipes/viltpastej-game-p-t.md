## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. leftover, scraped poultry
- 1 hg. lard
- 3 eggs
- 1 msk. flour (10 gr.)
- 3—4 dcl. thick cream
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (for the mold)
- 2 msk. finely chopped truffle (for the mold)

## Directions

1. The scraped poultry is ground together with the lard 5 times through a meat grinder and then pounded with a wooden pestle in a stone mortar until a smooth and supple forcemeat.
2. The egg yolks are whisked with the flour and cream and this mixture is added tablespoon by tablespoon to the forcemeat, while it is worked for about 1 hour.
3. The forcemeat is carefully seasoned with the spices and lastly the egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks, are folded in.
4. A timbale mold is greased with butter and sprinkled with finely chopped truffle, the forcemeat is poured in and cooked slowly in a water bath for about 2 hours.
5. It is turned out onto a round platter, garnished with peas and fried potatoes and served with poultry or truffle sauce as well as currant jelly.
