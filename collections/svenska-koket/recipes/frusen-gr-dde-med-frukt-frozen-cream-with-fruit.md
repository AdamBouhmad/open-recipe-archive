## Ingredients

- 2 liters thick cream
- 2 tablespoons raspberry jam
- 1 other jam

## Directions

1. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form
2. Mix in the jam
3. Pour into a glacé mold and freeze, but not too hard
4. Pack the frozen cream in a glacé form and place on ice until serving
5. Turn out of the form and garnish with fruit
