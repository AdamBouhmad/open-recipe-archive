## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 1 vanilla pod
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 tsk. potato flour (5 gr.)
- 1 hg. sugar
- 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream
- 2 dcl. milk
- 1 msk. vanilla sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix the eggs, egg yolks, sugar, milk, vanilla pod, and potato flour in a pot and whisk vigorously over heat until the cream is thick and frothy.
2. Pour out the cream and whisk until it is cold, then fold in the cream beaten to stiff peaks together with vanilla sugar, if desired.
3. Remove the vanilla pod, rinse it, store it in an airtight container and use it again.
4. Pour the cream into a compote bowl and serve with pastries or use as filling in cakes.
