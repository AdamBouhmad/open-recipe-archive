## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 425 gr. caster sugar
- 1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
- 425 gr. wheat flour
- 200 gr. coarsely cut almond

## Directions

1. Stir the eggs and sugar well; sift the flour with the baking powder and stir down into the egg mixture.
2. Last add the unblanced and coarsely cut almond.
3. From the batter, lay out on buttered baking sheets approximately 3 cm. wide lengths, which are baked a beautiful golden brown in moderate oven heat.
4. Cut the lengths, while they are still quite warm, into 1 cm. wide pieces on the diagonal.
