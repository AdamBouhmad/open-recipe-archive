## Ingredients

- 5 whole eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper
- 10 fresh asparagus tips
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for filling
- 1/2 tablespoon flour (5 g)
- 1 deciliter cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch the asparagus in salted water and let it drain.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cream, and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Taste the sauce with the seasonings and carefully add the asparagus.
4. Prepare the omelet as in recipe 24, and just before the eggs are fully bound, add the asparagus stewing.
5. Shape the omelet with a fork, turn it out onto a warm dish, and garnish, if desired, with a few blanched asparagus tips that have been browned in butter.
6. The blanched asparagus can also be mixed with a few tablespoons of hollandaise and filled into the omelet after it has been turned out onto the dish.
