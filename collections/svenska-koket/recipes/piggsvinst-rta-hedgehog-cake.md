## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 150 grams granulated sugar
- 70 grams potato flour
- zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks for the pan
- 50 grams icing sugar for glaze
- 1 part white French wine for glaze
- 50-100 grams sweet almonds for garnish

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and sugar for half an hour.
2. Add the lemon zest and juice, then the flour.
3. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-sprinkled pan and bake in weak oven heat.
5. Glaze with the icing sugar mixed with white French wine.
6. Blanch the almonds, cut them into fine strips, and insert them thickly into the cake so that it resembles a hedgehog.
