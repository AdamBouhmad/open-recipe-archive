## Ingredients

- 4 dcl. true hollandaise
- 2 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. To a hollandaise prepared according to the preceding recipe, add the cream whipped to stiff peaks just before the sauce is to be served.
2. Serve with fish and vegetable dishes.
