## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 dcl cream
- 160–180 g flour
- 7 dcl milk
- 1 tsp salt (5 g)
- 1½ msk butter (30 g)
- 2 msk butter for the pan (40 g)
- 2 kkp jam (raspberry or strawberry)
- 3 egg whites
- 150 g icing sugar or 3 dcl thick cream and ½ msk caster sugar (8 g)
- 50 g sweet almond

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks with a whisk, add cream, then whisk in flour which should preferably be sifted
2. Add milk, salt and butter, and let the batter rest 2 hours
3. Heat the pancake pan slowly, melt the butter in a water bath, and brush the pan with it
4. Stir the batter well and fry thin pancakes, stacking them on a warm plate; brush each pancake thinly with jam which has been warmed in a water bath
5. Cut the pancakes into 12 pieces and let cool
6. Beat egg whites to stiff peaks, carefully fold in the sugar, spread the meringue over the pancakes, sprinkle with blanched and finely chopped almond, and let the cake rest in a gentle oven heat until the meringue is half-dry and has a nice light yellow colour
