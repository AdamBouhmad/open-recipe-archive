## Ingredients

- 12-14 crusts
- 200 grams calf sweetbread
- 500 millilitres water
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 grams)
- 1 decilitre sweetbread stock
- 4 decilitres cream
- 2-3 truffles
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. The calf sweetbread should lie in water 1 hour for the blood to drain out.
2. Blanch it (place in cold, lightly salted water and let it cook 10 minutes) and then place in 1/2 litre boiling water, with salt added, to cook about 1/2 hour or until it becomes white and firm.
3. Place it on a sieve so the stock drains well.
4. Cut the sweetbread into fine cubes and fry in butter until it becomes clear.
5. Remove it from the butter with a spoon.
6. Fry the flour in the butter 2 minutes; add the sweetbread stock and cream, and let the sauce cook 5 minutes.
7. Add the calf sweetbread and finely cut truffle, bring the stew to a boil and taste it.
8. Finally stir in the whipped egg yolk, let the stew simmer and fill immediately into the crusts.
9. Serve as a middle course.
