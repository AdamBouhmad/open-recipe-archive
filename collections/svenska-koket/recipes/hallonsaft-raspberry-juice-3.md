## Ingredients

- 4 litres raspberries
- 8 decilitres water
- 750 grams sugar per kilogramme of juice

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, rinse them, crush them and place them with the cold water in a preserve pot.
2. Let the mass quickly come to a boil under stirring and boil for approximately 15 minutes.
3. Pour it into cheesecloth and let it drip until the following day.
4. Weigh the juice, add the sugar, and when it is dissolved, let the juice boil vigorously for approximately 10 minutes under careful skimming.
5. Bottle when it is cold.
