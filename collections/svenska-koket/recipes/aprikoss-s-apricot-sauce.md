## Ingredients

- 150 grams dried apricots
- 8 parts water
- 75 grams sugar

## Directions

1. The apricots are rinsed in lukewarm water and are then allowed to soak for 2-3 hours in the water in which they are to be cooked.
2. They are then brought to a boil together with the sugar until they are completely soft, after which they are pressed through a sieve.
3. The sauce is brought to a boil, diluted with a little water if necessary, is allowed to cool and is served.
