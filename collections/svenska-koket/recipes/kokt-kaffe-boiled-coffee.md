## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters water
- 75 grams (10 tablespoons) ground coffee of medium strength
- a small piece of isinglass

## Directions

1. Boil the water. Add the ground coffee, which should be the size of small oat grains, together with the isinglass, and let the coffee boil very slowly for 3-5 minutes.
2. Pour a little cold water into the pan, which is then set to the side of the stove, so that the grounds sink.
3. If serving the coffee in a pot, this should first be warmed with a little hot water.
