## Ingredients

- 1 litre fresh cherries or 5 decilitres dried cherries
- 2 litres water
- zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- 1-1 1/2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 g)

## Directions

1. Rinse the berries in a sieve and let them drain; crush half of them in a mortar until the pits are crushed
2. Place the crushed berries in 1 1/2 litres of water together with the lemon zest and let them cook 3/4 hour
3. Strain the berries, add sugar to the liquid, which gets a boil and is thickened with potato flour mixed in 1/2 tablespoon cold water, then let the soup cook 2 minutes
4. Remove the pits from the remaining cherries, cook the berries in the rest of the water with 2 tablespoons sugar and add them to the finished soup
5. Serve preferably cold with whipped cream and rusks or biscuits
6. If using dried cherries, rinse them in lukewarm water, crush them so the pits are crushed, and let them soak a few hours in the water in which they are to be cooked
