## Ingredients

- 2 kg eel
- 2 tablespoons salt
- a few allspice berries
- dill stalks
- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 liter water
- 3/4 liter fish stock
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Skin the eel, clean, rinse and wash well.
2. Rub it with salt, cut into pieces and place on the heat in hot water together with the spices and dill stalks and let simmer gently until done.
3. Remove the fish and place it in a beautiful à-la-daube mold.
4. Clarify the stock with the egg whites, strain through a cloth or cheesecloth and pour over the fish.
5. Place the à-la-daube in a cold place to set, turn out and garnish with dill.
