## Ingredients

- 90 grams cooked meat of chicken or game bird
- 3 tablespoons cold béchamel sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 gr.)
- a little white pepper or nutmeg
- 1-1 1/2 deciliters thick cream
- 1 egg
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces and force it 5 times through a meat grinder.
2. Pound it together with the sauce in a stone mortar with a wooden pestle until fine and smooth.
3. Add the spices and cream, little by little, and pass the mixture through a sieve.
4. Whisk the egg and egg yolks well and work them into the forcemeat tablespoon by tablespoon, then taste carefully for seasoning.
5. Bake the royal and serve as in the preceding recipe.
