## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 75 gr. caster sugar
- 2 tsk. potato flour (10 gr.)
- 1/2 liter milk

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar until the mixture becomes white and foamy.
2. Stir the potato flour with 2 tablespoons of the cold milk and blend it in.
3. Bring the remaining milk to a boil and pour it over the egg yolks while whisking vigorously.
4. Let the sauce simmer over the heat with continued whisking until thick; pour it out and whisk until cold.
5. This sauce may then be flavored with rum, arrack, fruit juice, lemon, orange, vanilla, caramel, coffee or cocoa to taste.
6. Serve both hot and cold. Keep hot in a water bath.
