## Ingredients

- 750 gr sugar
- 1 litre water
- zest of 3 lemons
- juice of 8 lemons

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar syrup and let it cool.
2. Remove all white pith from the lemon zest and cut into pieces.
3. Let the zest pieces steep in the cold sugar syrup for approximately 3 hours.
4. Add the lemon juice and strain the mixture directly into the glacé machine.
