## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg chuck or brisket
- 1 liter water
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 large rutabaga
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into oblong pieces of suitable size for serving.
2. Boil the water with the salt, add the meat and skim when it boils.
3. Add the pepper.
4. Let the meat cook slowly with the lid on until tender, approximately 2 1/2 hours, adding boiling water as needed.
5. Peel the rutabaga, cut into even cubes, and place on top of the meat 1/2 hour before it is done.
6. In a sauce pan, fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, pour in the meat cooking liquid and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes, then taste and adjust seasoning.
7. Transfer the meat and vegetables to a warm serving dish and pour the sauce over.
8. The butter may be omitted; in that case, stir the flour in a little cold water and add it to the stew, which should then boil for 8 minutes.
