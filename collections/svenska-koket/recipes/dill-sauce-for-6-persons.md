## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 5 dcl. meat stock
- 1-2 tbsp. finely minced dill
- 1-2 tbsp. vinegar
- 2-3 tsp. sugar (10-15 gr.)
- salt
- 1-2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Sauté 1 tbsp. of the butter with the flour for 2 minutes, add the stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Add the minced dill, season the sauce with vinegar and spices, add the whipped egg yolk and let the sauce simmer under vigorous whisking but do not boil, as it breaks easily.
3. Finish the sauce with the remaining cold butter.
