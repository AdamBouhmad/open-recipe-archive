## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg pork loin
- 3 litres water

## Directions

1. Soak the pork loin in water for 12-24 hours, depending on how heavily it is salted
2. Crack it, place in cold water and bring to a rapid boil, skim and let it boil for 15 minutes at 100°
3. Cool the broth to 82°-83° and let the pork remain at this heat level until tender, approximately 2 hours
4. Cut into attractive pieces, place on a warm serving dish, garnish with parsley and serve with root vegetable mash, potato purée or dumplings
