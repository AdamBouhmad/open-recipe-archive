## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes (Dutch)
- 1 kilogram fat
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush well clean, peel thinly and cut first into even squares and then into 3 mm thick slices.
2. The slices must be cut precisely evenly, otherwise the dish will not succeed.
3. Place in water as they are cut, and dry thoroughly afterwards in a cloth.
4. Heat half of the fat moderately in a frying pan, add the potato slices and let them cook until they float up, then place them in a wire basket or on absorbent paper to drain.
5. Heat the remaining fat in another pan until it is smoking hot, then quickly place the potato slices in it and let them puff up into small balloons.
6. Lift onto absorbent paper with a fat spoon, salt and serve hot as garnish around fried meat.
