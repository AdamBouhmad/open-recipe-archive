## Ingredients

- 9 large, beautiful apples
- 75 grams melted butter
- 1 deciliter finely crushed rusks
- 1 deciliter caster sugar (85 grams)
- 75 grams sweet almonds
- 75 grams caster sugar
- 1 small egg white
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams) for the pan

## Directions

1. First prepare the almond paste. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, pass them through a mill and grind with the sugar into a fine paste, add the egg white, and grind the paste until it is smooth and supple.
2. Wash and dry the apples, peel them evenly, and remove the cores with a tube.
3. Divide the almond paste into nine parts, roll them out and fill the apples, which are then dipped in the melted butter and rolled in a mixture of the rusks and sugar.
4. Grease a small roasting pan with cold butter, place the apples in it, put in a hot oven and bake until they are a nice golden brown and tender.
5. When they are cold, arrange them on a tart plate with tart paper and serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
