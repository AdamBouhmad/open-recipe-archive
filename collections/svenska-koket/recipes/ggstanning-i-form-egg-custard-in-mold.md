## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 tablespoons milk
- salt
- sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs well with the milk, and add the seasonings.
2. Butter a tin mold or metal bowl, pour in the egg mixture, cover with a lid, and place the mold in a water bath on top of the stove.
3. Ensure carefully that the water does not boil but only is kept hot until the custard is sufficiently firm, otherwise it easily becomes watery.
4. Turn out onto a warm dish, garnish with parsley and serve with cured ham or sausage, fried meat or fish.
5. The egg custard can also be baked in a water bath in the oven.
