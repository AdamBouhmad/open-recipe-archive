## Ingredients

- 1 liter cooked potatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crusts
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cut into 3 mm. thick slices or even dice.
2. Brown half the butter in a frying pan, add the potatoes and sprinkle with a mixture of the crushed bread crusts, salt and sugar.
3. Place the remaining butter on top and brown the potatoes while frequently shaking the pan.
4. Finally sprinkle parsley over and serve the potatoes immediately with fried fish and meat.
