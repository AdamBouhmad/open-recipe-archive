## Ingredients

- 5 parts heavy cream
- 1.5 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 0.5 vanilla bean
- 3-4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Half of the cream is boiled together with the vanilla and sugar until it has attained sufficient vanilla flavor, after which the pan is removed from the heat.
2. One egg yolk at a time is whisked in, and the sauce is placed back over the heat to simmer while whisking until it reaches the right thickness.
3. Whisking is continued until the sauce is cold.
4. The remaining cream is whisked to stiff peaks and folded into the cold sauce.
