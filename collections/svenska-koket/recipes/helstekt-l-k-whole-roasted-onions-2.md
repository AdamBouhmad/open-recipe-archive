## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg small Portuguese onions
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 60 g butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 g)

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and blanch them by placing them in boiling, salted water and cooking for 10 minutes
2. Place on a cloth and let drain
3. Fry the butter in a saucepan and add the onions
4. Sprinkle with sugar and cook slowly with a lid until they are nicely browned and completely tender, which is tested with a whisk
5. Serve with the sandwich table or with roasted meat such as roast beef, roast mutton, and lamb saddle
