## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2-3 teaspoons dry English or French mustard
- 6 deciliters fish stock
- salt
- sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter (10 g)

## Directions

1. Roux the butter, flour and English mustard for 2 minutes
2. Add the fish stock and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes, season with spices and finally stir in the cold butter
3. If using French mustard, mix it with a little fish stock and add it together with the spices
4. If desired, enrich the sauce with 1 egg yolk
5. The sauce can be made with milk or broth instead of fish stock
