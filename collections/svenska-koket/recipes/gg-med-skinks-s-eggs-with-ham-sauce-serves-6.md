## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- For the filling: 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 1.5 deciliters cream
- the hard-boiled egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 tablespoon clarified butter (20 gr.)
- For the ham sauce: 50 gr. lean, smoked ham
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1 deciliter broth
- 3 deciliters milk
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Shell the eggs and cut them in half lengthwise; loosen the yolk carefully and cut it into fine dice.
2. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes; add the thin cream and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes, after which carefully stir in the diced egg yolk and season the stew with spices.
3. Fill the egg halves with the stew and pour the clarified butter over.
4. Cut the ham into fine dice and fry it
