## Ingredients

- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 0.5 celery root
- 0.5 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 0.5 kohlrabi
- 1 cauliflower head
- 1 leek
- 3 teaspoons fresh or 0.25 liters canned peas
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 3 liters water
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)

## Directions

1. Prepare the roots and vegetables in the usual way, cut into pieces and place together with salt and butter in the cold water to cook slowly with a tight lid for 4-5 hours.
2. Strain the stock, season to taste, and color if desired with a little Japanese soy sauce.
3. Serve with rice or egg pasta, use for julienne soup, clear stock, or purée.
