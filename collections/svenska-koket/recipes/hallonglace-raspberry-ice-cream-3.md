## Ingredients

- 1 liter fresh raspberries (or 1-2 cups raspberry jam or compote)
- 100 g sugar
- 1/2 liter water
- 3 parts sherry
- 1 lemon
- carmine

## Directions

1. Pass the raspberries and mix with the water, sugar, the rubbed lemon peel, lemon juice and sherry.
2. If the mixture does not have a sufficiently red color, add a little carmine; then pour it into an ice cream machine or mold to freeze.
