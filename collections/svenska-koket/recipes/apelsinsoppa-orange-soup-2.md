## Ingredients

- 1¼ litres water
- 4 parts orange juice
- Sugar to taste
- 2 tablespoon potato flour
- 2 oranges

## Directions

1. Bring the water and juice to a boil, add sugar to taste, and thicken the soup with potato flour mixed with a little cold water.
2. Bring to a good boil and serve with the oranges cut into slices.
