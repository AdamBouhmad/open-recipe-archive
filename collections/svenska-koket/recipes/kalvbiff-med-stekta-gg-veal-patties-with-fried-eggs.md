## Ingredients

- 300 g boneless veal loin
- 100 g lard
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 egg white
- 1/2 tbsp water
- 1 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 2 dcl good broth
- 1 tbsp tomato puree
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 g)
- salt, white pepper
- 6-8 eggs
- 1 tbsp butter (20 g)

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and pass together with the lard 4 times through the meat grinder.
2. Mix the mixture with the beaten eggs and spices and form into round patties, which are brushed with the egg white, whisked with water, and turned in crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Heat a frying pan slowly, brown the butter in it, place the patties in and fry 2 minutes on each side, after which they are placed on a warm serving platter.
4. Pour the broth into the pan, which is whisked well, add the tomato puree, let the sauce come to a boil, taste it with the cold butter and the spices and pour over the patties.
5. Fry the eggs in a griddle and place one egg on each patty.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish.
