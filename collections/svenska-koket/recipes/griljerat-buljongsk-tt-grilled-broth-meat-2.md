## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. broth meat, preferably brisket
- 8 anchovies
- 2 eggs
- 3 dcl. grated breadcrumbs
- 5 tbsp. butter (1 hg.) for frying

## Directions

1. Slice the meat, which should not be too much cooked, into beautiful, even slices.
2. Clean the anchovies, remove the bones, chop them finely.
3. Beat the eggs well together with the anchovies.
4. Dip the meat slices in this mixture, turn them in grated breadcrumbs and fry them beautifully brown in butter.
5. Arrange on a hot dish, garnish with parsley and serve with clarified butter and stewed vegetables or potato puree.
