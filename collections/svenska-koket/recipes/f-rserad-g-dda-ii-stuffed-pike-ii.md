## Ingredients

- 6 hg pike (220 g scraped fish meat)
- 125 g butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp flour (15 g)
- 2.5-3 dcl cream
- 1 tsk sugar (5 g)
- 1 egg white
- 2 tbsp crushed breadcrumbs
- 1.5 tbsp butter (30 g)

## Directions

1. Scale the pike, cut it open along the back and fillet it without damaging the skin. Remove the innards, rinse well and dry with a fish towel.
2. Prepare the stuffing as in recipe no. 356.
3. Scrape the skin, rinse it, cut it in the shape of a turtle and spread it on a greased baking sheet with cold butter.
4. Spread the fish stuffing on top, smooth it even with a spoon dipped in lukewarm water, and brush over with egg white.
5. Bake in regular oven heat for approximately 20 minutes, test with a skewer if it is done.
6. Move carefully with a fish spatula onto a warm serving dish and garnish as in the previous recipe.
7. Serve with crayfish, lobster or mushroom sauce. — The stuffing can also be placed around the fish bone (the head and tail are then kept) and formed as a pike, arranged and served as mentioned in the previous recipe.
