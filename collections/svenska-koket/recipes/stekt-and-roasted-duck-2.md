## Ingredients

- 2 ducks
- 1 hg. bacon, in 2 slices
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- For the sauce: The butter in which the birds were browned
- 2 msk. flour
- 3 dcl. meat gravy or broth
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Duck should be prepared immediately after it is shot (see p. 299).
2. The bird is young if the webbing is thin and easily torn.
3. The broad-billed dabbling duck is best.
