## Ingredients

- 3 hg. flour
- 2 dcl. water (scarcely)
- 5 hg. fine butter (equal weight with the dough)
- 1/2 liter apple compote
- 3 dcl. extra thick cream
- 30 gr. finely chopped, roasted sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as in recipe no. 33 and roll out to 2 bottoms, one 2 cm., the other 3 cm. thick.
2. From the bowl or dish on which the cake is to be served, take a measurement. This is easiest done by cutting a pattern of paper, after which the dough is cut out with a pastry cutter.
3. The dough shrinks during baking and should therefore be 4 cm. wider in circumference than the pattern.
4. Place the thinnest bottom on a baking sheet and brush with egg white, but none of this must come down over the edge, as it prevents the butter dough from rising.
5. Cut the other bottom into a 3 cm. wide ring, which is placed on top of the bottom and carefully brushed with egg white.
6. Bake the cake in a hot oven and turn it often, so that it becomes evenly baked and gets a beautiful golden-brown color.
7. Roll out the remaining dough to 2 cm. thickness and cut with a cutter into strips, which are laid in diagonal squares, so that they form a lattice that fits inside the ring on the cake.
8. Brush the lattice and bake as before.
9. Fill the cake with apple compote or apple purée, spread the cream whipped to stiff peaks over it, and sprinkle with the roasted almonds.
10. Place the lattice on top and serve the cake at once.
