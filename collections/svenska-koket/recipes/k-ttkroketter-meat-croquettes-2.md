## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 3 cups bouillon
- 5 cups roasted or cooked meat cut into cubes
- salt, white pepper
- 2 eggs for breading
- 2 cups crushed hardtack for breading
- 3/4 kilogram lard for frying

## Directions

1. Melt butter and flour together, dilute with bouillon, add the meat, and let the stew cook 10 minutes, then season.
2. Spread the mixture on a flat dish to cool, and form small sugar-top-shaped croquettes from it.
3. Roll these in crushed hardtack, dip in beaten egg and roll again in crushed hardtack, then let them dry for 1/2 hour.
4. Heat the lard, cook the croquettes golden brown in it and place them on gray paper to drain.
5. Garnish with parsley and serve with mushroom or brown sauce, or together with stewed vegetables.
