## Ingredients

- 3 liters snow pea pods
- water
- salt
- 100 g butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the tender but fully mature pea pods well.
2. Place in boiling, salted water (short stock) and cook slowly until completely soft.
3. Remove and drain.
4. Serve with clarified butter.
