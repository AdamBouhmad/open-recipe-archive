## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters strawberries, raspberries, cloudberries, lingonberries or pitted cherries
- 1.5 deciliters sugar (128 grams)

## Directions

1. The berries should be freshly picked
2. Clean and rinse the berries quickly in a sieve and allow to drain well
3. Layer them alternately with the sugar in a compote bowl
4. Sprinkle each layer of berries with fresh water
5. Allow the berries to stand for 2-3 hours to release their juice
6. Serve with whipped or frozen cream
