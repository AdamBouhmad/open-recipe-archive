## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 210 gr. icing sugar
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1 hg. potato flour
- 3 egg whites
- 30 gr. melted butter for the molds
- 1 dcl. crushed bread crumbs
- 1/2 batch vanilla cream (recipe no. 871) or 1/2 batch lemon cream (recipe no. 869)
- 1 hg. icing sugar for glaze
- 1 tbsp. water
- 1/2 tsp. lemon or orange juice
- 3 candied orange peels for garnish
- 30 gr. pistachios

## Directions

1. Grease small fluted pastry molds with melted butter and allow to set, then dust with sifted bread crumbs.
2. Whip three whole eggs and three egg yolks for 45 minutes, then add the finely grated lemon zest or 15 gr. blanched and ground sweet almond and the flour.
3. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Fill the molds three-quarters full with batter and bake in a not-too-hot oven.
5. Turn out the pastries and allow to cool, split them open, and fill with cream.
6. Glaze (recipe no. 961), dust with finely chopped orange peels and pistachios, and serve as dessert or with coffee and tea.
