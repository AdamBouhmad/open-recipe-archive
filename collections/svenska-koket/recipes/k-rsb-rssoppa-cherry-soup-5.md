## Ingredients

- 1 liter fresh or 5 deciliters dried cherries
- 2 liters water
- Zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- 1 to 1.5 cups crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 grams)

## Directions

1. Rinse the berries in a sieve and let them drain; crush half of them in a stone mortar until the pits are crushed
2. Place the crushed berries in 1.5 liters of the water together with the lemon zest and let them cook for 3/4 hour
3. Strain the berries from the liquid, add the sugar to the juice, which is brought to a boil and thickened with the potato flour, stirred in 1/2 cup cold water, after which the soup is allowed to cook for 2 minutes
4. Remove the pits from the remaining cherries, cook the berries in the remaining water with 2 tablespoons sugar and add to the finished soup
5. Serve preferably cold with whipped cream and rusks or biscuits
6. If using dried cherries, rinse them in lukewarm water, crush so that the pits are crushed, and let them soak for a few hours in the water in which they are to be cooked
