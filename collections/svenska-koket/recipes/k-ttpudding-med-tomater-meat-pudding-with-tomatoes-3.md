## Ingredients

- Ground meat as in recipes 631 or 632
- 6 large tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 0.25 teaspoon white pepper
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 grams)
- 2 tablespoons breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the ground meat according to the aforementioned recipe.
2. Clean the tomatoes, rinse them and cut them into slices.
3. Sprinkle the slices with a mixture of salt and pepper.
4. Butter a pudding mold and coat it with breadcrumbs.
5. Alternate layers of ground meat and tomatoes in the mold; the first and last layer should be meat.
6. Cook or bake the pudding as in recipe 638 and serve with meat sauce (see recipe 429) or clarified butter and potatoes.
