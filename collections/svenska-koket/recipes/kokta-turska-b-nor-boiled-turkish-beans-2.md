## Ingredients

- 1 kg fresh Turkish beans
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 2-3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the beans and cut them diagonally into pieces, unless they are tender, in which case they are served whole.
2. Place in boiling, salted water (short stock) and cook slowly until soft.
3. Add the chopped parsley and cold butter, and mix well.
