## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg veal breast
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 6 allspice berries
- 1 piece ginger

## Directions

1. Break and wash the breast with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Place in boiling water, skim, and add the seasonings.
3. Let the meat cook slowly with a tightly fitting lid until tender, approximately 2 hours.
4. Remove, cut into appropriately sized pieces, and remove any loose bones.
5. Serve with crayfish, mushroom, caper, or dill sauce as desired. The sauce is always made from the broth in which the breast was cooked.
