## Ingredients

- 6 hectograms rye sieve flour
- 2 small teaspoons bicarbonate
- 1 small teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 liter sour milk
- 2 deciliters syrup
- 1/2 tablespoon caraway or 1/2 tablespoon fennel and 1/2 tablespoon anise
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan
- 2 tablespoons wheat flour (20 grams) for the pan

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix well with the bicarbonate and baking powder.
2. Whip the milk to stiff foam and fold into the flour along with the syrup and finely ground spices.
3. Pour the dough into a well-buttered and floured oblong bread pan or baking dish and place immediately to bake in regular oven temperature for approximately 1 hour.
4. Test with a whisk to see if it is baked through.
5. Use as sandwich bread.
