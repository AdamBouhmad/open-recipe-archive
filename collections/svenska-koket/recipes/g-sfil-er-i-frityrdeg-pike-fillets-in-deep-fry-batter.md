## Ingredients

- 2 pike of approximately 6 hg. each
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 80 gr. dry flour
- 3 gr. salt
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)
- 1 dcl. water
- 1 egg
- 750 gr. fat for frying

## Directions

1. Treat the pike as in recipe 365, but do not pound out the fillets.
2. Rub them with salt and let them lie for 1 hour, then dry them again with a fish towel.
3. Prepare the deep-fry batter according to the recipe.
