## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liters potatoes
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)
- water

## Directions

1. The potatoes are boiled either unpeeled or raw-peeled. If the potatoes are to be peeled, approximately 1 1/2 liters should be calculated for 6 persons. The potatoes are treated and cooked as described on page 337 et seq.
