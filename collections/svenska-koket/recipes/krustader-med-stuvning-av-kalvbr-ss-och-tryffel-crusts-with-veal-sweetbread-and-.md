## Ingredients

- 12-14 crusts
- 2 hg. veal sweetbread
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 1 deciliter sweetbread stock
- 4 deciliters cream
- 2-3 truffles
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. The veal sweetbread should lie in water for 1 hour so that the blood is drawn out.
2. Blanch it (place in cold, lightly salted water and let it cook 10 minutes) and then place it in 1/2 liter boiling water, added with salt, to cook about 1/2 hour or until it becomes white and firm.
3. Place it on a sieve so that the stock drains well.
4. Cut the sweetbread into fine cubes and fry in the butter until it becomes clear.
5. Remove it from the butter with a spoon.
6. Fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes; add the sweetbread stock and cream, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
7. Add the veal sweetbread and the finely cut truffle, and let the stew come to a boil and taste it.
8. Last, stir in the whipped egg yolk, let the stew simmer and fill immediately into the crusts.
9. Serve as an intermediate course.
