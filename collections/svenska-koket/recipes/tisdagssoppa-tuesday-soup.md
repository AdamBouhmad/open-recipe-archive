## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons pearl barley
- 2 liters broth from cooked pork or salted meat
- 0.25 turnip
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1 piece celery
- 0.5 liter potatoes
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 6 parts milk
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the barley and place it in the cold broth to cook until almost soft, after which add the prepared vegetables cut into strips and the potatoes cut into slices.
2. When these are cooked, thicken the soup with the flour mixed with the cold milk. Let the soup then cook for 10 minutes and season it.
