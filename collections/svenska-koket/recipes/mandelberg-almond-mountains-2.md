## Ingredients

- 125 gr. sweet almond
- 2 egg whites
- 2 hg. powdered sugar
- 1 tsp. lemon juice

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, cut them lengthwise into thin strips, which are lightly roasted in the oven and allowed to cool.
2. Beat the egg whites together with the sugar and lemon juice for approximately 1 hour in the same direction, whereupon the almonds are added.
3. Place the mass with a teaspoon on a well buttered baking sheet.
