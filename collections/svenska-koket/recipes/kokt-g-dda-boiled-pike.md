## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar
- 3 teaspoons salt (15 gr.)
- water
- 7 gr. coarse salt per liter of water

## Directions

1. Do not scale the fish; clean it as described on page 171
2. Rub the fish with salt and bind it by threading a needle with string through the head and tail so the fish curves when served, hence the name kromgädda (curved pike)
3. Cook with the liver as in recipe n:o 327; when the fins easily loosen, the fish is done
4. Place on a fish platter with a sieve or on a broken napkin
5. Garnish with parsley and the cooked liver
6. Serve with melted butter, grated horseradish, and potatoes
