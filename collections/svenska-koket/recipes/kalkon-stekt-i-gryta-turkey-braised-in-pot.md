## Ingredients

- 1 turkey cock, about 5 kg.
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 6 2-öre almond breads
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 3 dcl. milk
- 1 1/2 hg. lard, in 1 slice
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 1 1/2 lit. broth

## Directions

1. Treat the turkey as in the preceding recipe and rub inside and outside with the seasonings.
2. Split the almond breads, brush them with butter, dip them in milk and stuff them into the bird, which is then sewn closed with a running stitch, trussed (see page 300) and covered with the lard slice, which is fastened with fine twine.
3. Fry half of the butter in a braising pot, place the turkey in it with the breast up, and add the giblets.
