## Ingredients

- 2 kg. pears
- 1 kg. lump sugar
- 1/2 litre water

## Directions

1. Place the pears in boiling water and let them cook until they are almost soft.
2. From the sugar and water, cook a clear syrup and let it cool.
3. Peel the pears, scrape the stems, and cut a cross in each pear.
4. As each pear is peeled, place it immediately in the sugar syrup.
5. Place on the heat and let cook until the pears are clear and soft.
6. Remove the pears and place in a jar or crock.
7. Let the syrup cook down until it becomes syrupy and pour it hot over the fruit.
