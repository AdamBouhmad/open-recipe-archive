## Ingredients

- 1/2 tsp finely minced red onion
- 50 gr lean veal (in fine cubes)
- 60 gr butter
- 40 gr dry flour
- 7 dcl boiling milk
- salt
- white pepper
- grated mesost cheese

## Directions

1. Fry the onion and meat in butter until clear.
2. Add the flour and fry with the butter for 2 minutes.
3. Add the milk little by little.
4. Add salt and white pepper and let the sauce cook, stirring, for 15 minutes.
5. Strain through cloth, season, and heat in a water bath.
6. Serve with boiled chicken, veal, or lamb.
7. If desired, can be made with chopped mushrooms fried in butter.
8. Can be used for gratin and as royal sauce for fish and poultry.
