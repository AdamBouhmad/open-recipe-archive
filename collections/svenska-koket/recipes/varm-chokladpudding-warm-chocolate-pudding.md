## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 2 dcl. cream
- 2 dcl. milk
- 70 gr. sugar
- 35 gr. grated, unsweetened chocolate
- 4 eggs
- 2 dcl. sugar for the mold
- 2 tbsp. water for the mold

## Directions

1. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, thin with the cream and milk, add the sugar and chocolate, and let the mixture come to a boil, then pour it out and let it cool.
2. Add the egg yolks thereafter, one by one, under vigorous stirring, and finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mixture into a glazed mold (see previous recipe) and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
4. Turn out and serve warm with vanilla sauce.
