## Ingredients

- 2 litres water
- 1 stick cinnamon
- ½ cup red sago grains
- 1–1½ cups crushed sugar
- 1 cup raisins
- 1½ cups sour lingonberry juice

## Directions

1. Bring water to a boil with the cinnamon and add the rinsed sago grains and cleaned, rinsed raisins.
2. Add sugar and juice, and let the soup cook until the grains are clear.
3. Taste and serve with rusks.
