## Ingredients

- 10 liters ripe gooseberries
- 5 liters water
- 0.5 kg sugar per kg juice

## Directions

1. Rinse and trim the gooseberries. They are crushed and placed in a crock.
2. The water is boiled and poured over the berries. The crock is covered with a towel and a lid.
3. The next day the juice is strained off. It is boiled and poured over the berries again. This is repeated for three days in a row.
4. On the fourth day the juice is cooked with sugar. It is cooked with skimming until it becomes clear and sticky, after which it is bottled. The bottles are corked and sealed with resin.
