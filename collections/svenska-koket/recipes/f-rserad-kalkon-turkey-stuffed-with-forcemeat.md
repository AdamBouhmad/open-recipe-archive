## Ingredients

- 1 turkey cock approximately 5 kg
- 4-5 truffles
- 1 tbsp salt (15 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1 1/2 hg lard, in 1 slice
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 liter good broth
- 2 hens (approximately 450 g scraped meat) for forcemeat
- 2 tbsp salt (30 g)
- 1 tsp white pepper
- 1 1/2 tbsp flour (15 g)
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk
- 6 dcl thick cream
- 3-4 truffles

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey as in recipe 605 and rub it with a mixture of salt and pepper
2. For the forcemeat, scrape the meat from 2 hens and mix with salt, white pepper, flour, egg, egg yolk and thick cream, adding the truffles
3. Fill the turkey with this forcemeat and prepare and roast it as in the previous recipes
