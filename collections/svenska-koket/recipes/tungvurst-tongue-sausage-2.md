## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg veal meat
- 0.5 kg pork meat
- 1 cooked salted ox tongue
- 0.33 liters water
- 140 grams potato starch
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut it into pieces.
2. Remove sinews and membranes, and grind the meat 4 times through a meat grinder.
3. Cut the tongue into large cubes and mix it into the farce together with the water, potato starch, and seasonings to taste.
4. Work the farce very well and stuff it into a wet bag, sewn in the shape of casings.
5. Tie this tightly, cook the tongue sausage in water for 2 hours, and then place it under a press.
6. Serve it cut into thin slices for the sandwich board.
