## Ingredients

- 2 kg strawberries (approximately 4 liters) or 2 kg raspberries (approximately 3.8 liters)
- 40 grams tartaric acid
- 1 deciliter boiling water
- 1.3 liters fresh water
- 1 kg granulated sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, rinse them if necessary, and place them in a crock.
2. Dissolve the tartaric acid in 1 deciliter of boiling water, mix it with the cold water, and pour it over the berries.
3. Let the berries stand covered for 24 hours.
4. Pour the berries into a sieve cloth and let them drain.
5. Measure the juice and add the sugar, then stir slowly with an enameled spoon until the sugar has melted, approximately 1 hour.
6. Skim the juice and pour it into dry bottles, which are sealed with purified cotton dipped in salicylic acid powder, and then bound with parchment paper.
