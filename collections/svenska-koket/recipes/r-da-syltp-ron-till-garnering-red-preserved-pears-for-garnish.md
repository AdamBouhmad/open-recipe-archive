## Ingredients

- 2 kg pears
- 1 kg lump sugar
- 1/2 liter water
- carmine

## Directions

1. Place the pears in boiling water and allow them to cook until almost soft.
2. Meanwhile, cook a sugar syrup from the sugar and water.
3. Color the syrup with carmine.
4. Peel the pears, scrape the stems, and cut a cross into each pear.
5. Immediately place them in the sugar syrup and allow to cook until they are clear and soft.
6. Remove the pears and place in a jar or crock.
7. Cook the syrup down until it becomes thick and pour it over the pears.
