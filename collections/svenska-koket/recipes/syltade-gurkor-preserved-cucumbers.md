## Ingredients

- 5 large cucumbers with not too large seed cavities
- 3 liters water
- 3 teaspoons salt
- For the brine: 2 liters good vinegar
- 1.75 kg granulated sugar
- 15 grams whole mace
- 25 grams ginger
- 7 grams whole white pepper
- 3 pieces cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers and cut them in half lengthwise.
2. Carefully scoop out all the insides with a silver spoon and cut the cucumber halves in half widthwise.
3. Boil the water with the salt.
4. Place as many cucumbers as fit on the surface and let them cook until soft and clear.
5. Remove them with a skimmer and place on doubled linen to drain.
6. Cook the vinegar and sugar with the spices, tied in a small muslin bag, into a brine.
7. When the brine has a strong spice flavor, remove it from heat.
8. Place the cucumbers in a porcelain crock and strain the brine over.
9. Cover the crock with quadruple linen and a plate.
10. Save the spices.
11. After 3-4 days pour off the brine and cook it together with the spices for about 10 minutes.
12. Pour it to cool and then strain it again over the cucumbers.
13. Save the spices.
14. After 6 days repeat the same cooking.
15. Repeat again after eight days, and the cucumbers are ready.
16. If the brine is too strong, one should add a little water at the final cooking.
17. Melons and pumpkins are preserved in the same way.
