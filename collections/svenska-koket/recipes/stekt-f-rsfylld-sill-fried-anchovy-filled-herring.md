## Ingredients

- 6 fresh herrings (= 2 kg)
- 12 anchovies
- 1 cup crushed hardtack
- 1/2 cup milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 cup coarse rye flour
- 3 tbsp butter or lard

## Directions

1. Open the herring along the back and remove the innards and backbone; rinse the herring, dry well and rub with salt
2. Clean the anchovies and remove the bones
3. Make a forcemeat from the anchovies, bread, milk and seasonings, with which fill the herring
4. Turn the herring in the flour, place in a pan greased with butter or lard and cook slowly in the oven or on the stove for 1/2 hour
5. Serve with currant sauce
