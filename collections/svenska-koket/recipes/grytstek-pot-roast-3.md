## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 kg inside or outside round (French cut)
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter or 4 tablespoons lard
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 1 1/2-2 liters broth or water
- 24 allspice berries
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the roast with a cloth dipped in hot water and lard it
2. Brown it in the heated butter or hot lard
3. If using lard, remove the roast after browning and drain off all fat
4. Salt the roast, add liquid a little at a time, add spices, then let it roast slowly with a tightly fitting lid for 3-4 hours
5. Turn the roast a couple of times during roasting, but avoid adding liquid during the last half hour
6. Strain and skim the meat gravy, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules
