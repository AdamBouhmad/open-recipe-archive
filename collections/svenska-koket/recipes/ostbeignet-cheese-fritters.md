## Ingredients

- 40 grams butter
- 60 grams flour
- 1 part water
- 40 grams grated cheese
- eggs

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour together, add the water with frequent stirring, and let the batter cook until it leaves the pan.
2. Let the mass cool, then mix in the eggs, one at a time, well along with the cheese.
3. Form small round balls from the batter; these are cooked light brown in fat and placed on brown paper to drain.
