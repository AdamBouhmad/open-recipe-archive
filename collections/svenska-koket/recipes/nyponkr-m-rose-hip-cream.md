## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter rose hips
- 1 1/2 to 2 liters water
- 1 to 1 1/2 cups crushed sugar
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 3 to 4 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the rose hips, place them in cold water and let them cook slowly for about 2 hours.
2. During cooking, stir them vigorously a few times to extract the flavor.
3. Pass the rose hips, return the juice to the heat with sugar and cinnamon.
4. When it boils, skim carefully and thicken the cream with potato flour mixed in a little cold water, then give it a short boil.
5. Pour into a jelly mold to cool and serve with whipped cream.
