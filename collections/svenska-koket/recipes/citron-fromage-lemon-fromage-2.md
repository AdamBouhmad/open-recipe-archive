## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 250 grams sugar
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 11 sheets gelatin
- 4 tablespoons hot water
- 1/2 liter thick cream

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes and mix with the finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice.
2. Rinse the gelatin with cold water, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in the hot water.
3. Whip the cream to stiff foam; whip the egg whites likewise.
4. First stir the gelatin into the egg mixture, then the cream and finally the egg white foam.
5. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours.
6. When the fromage is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass dish with tart paper.
7. Serve with almond shavings.
