## Ingredients

- Vanilla ice: 0.5 liter cream, 6 egg yolks, 4 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- Raspberry ice: 2 cups raspberry jam, 3 parts sherry, 1 compote, 1 lemon, 100 gr. sugar, 0.5 liter water
- For garnish: 3 parts heavy cream, 1 cup strawberry jam

## Directions

1. Vanilla and raspberry ices are prepared in the usual manner and frozen (see pages 243-244).
2. When the punch is to be served, the ice is placed in wide champagne glasses, first a slice of vanilla ice, then a slice of raspberry ice, then a little whipped cream, and on top the strawberries with their sauce.
