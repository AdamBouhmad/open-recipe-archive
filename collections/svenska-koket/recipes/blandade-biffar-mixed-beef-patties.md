## Ingredients

- 600g boneless beef
- 1 egg
- 3 cups milk
- 1/2 cup crushed rusks
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 100g butter
- 1 Portuguese onion

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it together with the fat into cubes and pass it 3 times through a meat grinder.
2. Whip up the eggs and milk, add the crushed rusks and let them soak, then mix them into the forcemeat together with the spices.
3. Work the forcemeat until it becomes cohesive like a dough.
4. Form small thin patties from it.
5. Peel the onion and cut it into thin slices.
6. Brown the butter in a frying pan and fry the patties a beautiful brown in it, then lay them on a warm platter.
7. Whisk the pan with a little boiling water and pour the sauce over the patties.
8. Brown the onion in butter at the end and garnish the patties with it.
