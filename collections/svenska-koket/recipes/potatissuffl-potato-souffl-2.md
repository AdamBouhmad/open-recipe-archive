## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 liters potato
- 40 g butter
- 3 eggs
- 1-2 deciliters thick cream
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Treat the potato as described, peel thinly, cook and press through a purée press.
2. Stir the cold butter into the purée with a fork together with the beaten egg yolks and cream.
3. Add the spices and lastly fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Put the mixture in a buttered and bread-crumbed ovenproof mold, sprinkle with finely crushed rusks.
5. Bake the soufflé in good oven heat until it has taken on a nice golden-brown color, or about 40 minutes.
6. Serve immediately upon removing from the oven to the smorgasbord or with fried meat in general.
