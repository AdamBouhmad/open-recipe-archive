## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless inner loin
- 1 liter skim milk or beverage
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 dcl broth

## Directions

1. If the meat is frozen, thaw it in milk or beverage.
2. Dry the meat well with a cloth, trim it, and cut into 12 slices.
3. Lightly pound the slices, wrap them in cloth, sprinkle with spices, brush with 1 tbsp melted butter, and let them rest stacked for 1 hour.
4. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter in it, place the steaks in and fry approximately 3 minutes on each side.
5. Remove to a warm dish, deglaze the pan with broth, and keep the sauce on the dish.
6. Place a slice of maitre-d'hotel butter on each steak (see recipe no. 406); garnish the dish with parsley and serve with boiled or fried potatoes.
7. The steaks can also be garnished with fried Portuguese onion, in which case omit the maitre-d'hotel butter and deglaze the pan with 2 dcl thick cream, then pour the sauce over the steaks.
