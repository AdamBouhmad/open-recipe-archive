## Ingredients

- 600 g boneless veal loin
- 3 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cup finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 1 dcl broth
- 6 lemon slices
- 6 anchovy fillets
- 1 tbsp chopped capers

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into 6 slices, which are flattened to half their thickness, trimmed to even, oblong cutlets, which are seasoned with the spices.
2. Brush with the beaten eggs and turn in the finely crushed breadcrumbs and fry in the butter until they are beautifully golden brown or about 5 minutes on each side.
3. Place the schnitzels on a hot serving platter.
4. Whisk out the pan with the broth, let the sauce come to a boil, taste and pour carefully onto the platter.
5. On each schnitzel place a lemon slice, on top of this an anchovy fillet, in a ring, which is filled with capers.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve with browned potatoes as a breakfast course.
