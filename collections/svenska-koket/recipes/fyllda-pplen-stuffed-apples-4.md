## Ingredients

- 9 large, beautiful apples
- 75 grams melted butter
- 1 deciliter finely crushed rusks
- 1 deciliter granulated sugar (85 grams)
- For the almond paste: 75 grams sweet almonds, 75 grams caster sugar, 1 small egg white
- For the pan: 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams)

## Directions

1. First prepare the almond paste: blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, grind them through a mill and pound them with the sugar until a fine paste is formed. Add the egg white and pound the paste until it is smooth and pliable.
2. Wash and dry the apples, peel them evenly, and remove the cores with a corer.
3. Divide the almond paste into nine portions, roll them out and fill them into the apples, which are then dipped in the melted butter and rolled in a mixture of the rusks and sugar.
4. Butter a small roasting pan with cold butter, place the apples in it, insert into a warm oven and bake until beautifully golden brown and soft.
5. When the apples are cold, place them on a cake plate lined with baking paper and serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
