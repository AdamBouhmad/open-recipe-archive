## Ingredients

- 1 bream weighing 2 kg
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 g)
- 8 white peppercorns
- 5 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 5 dill sprigs

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, see page 171; the head may remain but should be freed from gills.
2. Rub the fish with salt and let it rest for 1 hour.
3. Cook it either whole or in pieces as in recipe no. 327; when the fins loosen easily, it is done.
4. The bream can be served either hot or cold.
5. If served hot, it should be cooked whole, placed on a napkin and garnished with parsley.
6. If served cold, cook it in pieces and strain the broth over the arranged fish and let it set into aspic.
7. Serve hot or cold with cold horseradish sauce.
