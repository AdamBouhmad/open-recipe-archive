## Ingredients

- 2–3 eggs
- 3 dcl cream
- approximately 160 g flour
- 7 dcl milk
- 1 tsp salt (5 g)
- 1½ msk melted butter (30 g)
- 2 msk butter for the pan (40 g)

## Directions

1. Beat eggs with a whisk, add cream, then whisk in flour which should preferably be sifted
2. Add milk, salt and butter, and let the batter rest 2 hours
3. Heat the pan slowly, melt the butter in a water bath, and brush the pan with it
4. Stir the batter well and fry thin, light pancakes, placing them on a warm plate
5. Serve hot with jam
