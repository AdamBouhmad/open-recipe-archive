## Ingredients

- 5 hg. boneless veal meat
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 3 hg. fresh pork meat, not too lean
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 3 dcl. finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 dcl. boiling water
- 4 gr. meat extract

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat (see p. 232), free from sinews and membranes, cut into pieces and pass 4 times through a meat grinder.
2. From the forcemeat form cutlets, which are chopped over with a knife so that they get a nice shape, sprinkle with a mixture of salt and white pepper, dip in the beaten eggs and turn in crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Brown the butter lightly in a frying pan, lay the cutlets in and fry about 3 minutes on each side, after which they are placed in a ring on a warm serving platter.
4. Whisk out the pan with the water, mixed with meat extract, let the sauce simmer for a couple of minutes, taste and pour over the cutlets.
