## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 gr.)
- 6 deciliters meat stock
- 2—3 egg yolks
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2—3 teaspoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Melt the butter with the flour for 2 minutes
2. Add the stock and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes
3. Add the beaten egg yolks and let the sauce simmer under stirring
4. Season the sauce with spices and lemon juice and pour it over the white meat
