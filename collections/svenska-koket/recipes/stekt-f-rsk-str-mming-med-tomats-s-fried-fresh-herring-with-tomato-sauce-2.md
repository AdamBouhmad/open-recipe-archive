## Ingredients

- 30 herrings
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs
- 80 gr. butter (for frying)
- 6 tomatoes
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 3 decilitres broth
- 1 decilitre cream
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare and fry the herrings as in recipe 163 and arrange on a warm serving dish.
2. For the sauce, peel and pass the tomatoes through a sieve.
3. Sauté butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the broth, tomato purée and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Finish with the egg yolk, season with salt and pepper and pour over the herrings.
5. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
