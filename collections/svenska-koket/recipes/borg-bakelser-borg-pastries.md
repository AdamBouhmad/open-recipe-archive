## Ingredients

- 210 gr. melted butter
- 210 gr. fine sugar
- 3 eggs
- zest of 1/2 lemon or 15 sweet almonds
- 150 gr. dry potato flour
- 20 gr. melted butter for the molds
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Grease small decorative pastry molds with melted butter and let it set, then sprinkle with crushed, sifted rusks.
2. Let the butter cool and stir until white and creamy, then add the sugar, egg yolks one at a time, the finely grated lemon zest or the blanched and ground almonds, and the potato flour.
3. Finally fold in the egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Fill the molds three-quarters full with batter, place on a baking sheet, and bake in a not-too-hot oven until dry when tested with a skewer.
5. Turn out when most of the heat has dissipated, and serve with cream or fruit or with coffee or tea.
