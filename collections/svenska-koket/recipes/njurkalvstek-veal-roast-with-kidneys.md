## Ingredients

- 2 kg. veal kidney roast
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.)
- 1 slice lard (1½ hg.)
- 1 lit. broth or water

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung as in the preceding recipe, is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and some of the fat is trimmed away, after which the roast is rubbed with a mixture of salt and pepper. The kidney with some of the fat is placed inside the roast by the bone, the flank is folded over, and the roast is tied with fine twine dipped in boiling water. It is wrapped in a buttered paper, placed on the lard slice in a long pan, and set in a hot oven where it stands for about ½ hour. The paper is removed, half of the boiling broth is added, and the meat is roasted until tender, or about 1½–2 hours, during which it is often basted and more broth is added as needed. The roast is sliced, garnished with browned potatoes, stuffed tomatoes or vegetables, and salad or parsley. Served with reduced sauce mixed with cream.
