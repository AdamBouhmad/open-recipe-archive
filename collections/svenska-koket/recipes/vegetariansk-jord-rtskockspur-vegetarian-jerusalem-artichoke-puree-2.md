## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 root celery
- 1 3/4 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3 deciliters cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Treat the Jerusalem artichokes and celery as in recipe no. 269.
2. Place them in cold water with salt and let cook until completely soft, then pass through a sieve.
3. Fry 2 tablespoons of the butter with the flour for 2 minutes.
4. Gradually add the puree and cream.
5. Let the soup cook for 5 minutes, then season with the spices.
6. Whip the egg yolks in the soup bowl, add the remaining butter, and pour in the soup while whisking.
7. Serve with toasted bread or cheese croutons.
