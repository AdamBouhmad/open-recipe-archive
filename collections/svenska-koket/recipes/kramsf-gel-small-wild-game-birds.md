## Ingredients

- Various small wild birds such as thrush, sparrow, quail, lark, and others
- thin lard slices
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Pluck, gut, rinse well, and dry the birds.
2. Truss them and wrap them in thin lard slices.
3. Brown in butter, moisten with cream, and roast for approximately 15 minutes, basting frequently.
4. Split the birds and arrange on croutons.
5. Whip the sauce with a little thick cream and cold butter, taste, and pour over the birds.
6. Garnish with parsley and, if desired, with mushroom caps fried in butter, or with small triangular croutons fried in butter and covered with apple sauce.
