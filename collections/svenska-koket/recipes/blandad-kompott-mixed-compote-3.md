## Ingredients

- Fruits cooked individually according to the above descriptions
- Mixed and combined sugar syrup
- Whipped or frozen cream for serving

## Directions

1. Cook fruits individually according to the above descriptions.
2. Arrange them attractively in a compote bowl.
3. Pour the mixed, combined sugar syrup over the fruit.
4. Serve with frozen or whipped cream.
