## Ingredients

- 1 liten knippa persilja
- 2 msk. finhackad persilja
- 120 gr. fint smör
- 2 gr. bordsalt
- 1 tsk. citronsaft
- 1/4 tsk. vitpeppar

## Directions

1. Rinse the parsley, wrap it in cloth, clean it, chop it finely and wring it hard in a cloth.
2. Stir the butter until it becomes white and fluffy, add the parsley, salt, lemon juice and pepper and stir the butter well.
3. Serve in a sauce boat with poached or fried fish or meat.
4. If the butter is used as a garnish, form it in a damp cloth into a roll and let it lie in the cloth on ice or in cold water until it is hard.
