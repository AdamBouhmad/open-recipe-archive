## Ingredients

- 1 part clarified butter
- 3 parts water
- 150 gr. flour
- 40 gr. unsweetened chocolate
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup strawberry jam for filling
- 3 parts thick cream
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Bring the butter and water to a boil, whisk in the flour, and let the batter cook under constant stirring until it leaves the sides of the pan.
2. Remove from heat and melt the chocolate into the batter.
3. When most of the heat has passed, stir in the egg yolks one at a time.
4. Lastly, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. On well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets, place large spoonfuls of the batter.
6. Bake in good oven heat, taking care not to open the oven door during the first 10 minutes.
7. When cooled, cut a lid from each pastry and place 1 tsp. of strawberry jam inside.
8. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, season with vanilla sugar, and fill the pastries.
