## Ingredients

- Bones from roast beef, beef and veal roasts
- Tendons and membranes trimmed from boneless meat
- Cold water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Chop the bones into pieces and place them in enough cold water to just cover them.
2. Add a little salt.
3. Let the broth slowly come to a boil and skim it well.
4. Then simmer gently with a tightly closed lid for 3-4 hours.
5. Strain it and use it for finished soups and purées as well as for thinning roasts and poultry, etc.
