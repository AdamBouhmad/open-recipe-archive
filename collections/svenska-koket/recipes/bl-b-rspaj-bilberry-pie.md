## Ingredients

- 200 gr. butter
- 200 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. sweet almond
- 50 gr. sugar
- 2 liters bilberries
- 1½ cups castor sugar

## Directions

1. Work all the ingredients together for the butter dough on the baking board.
2. Line a fireproof form with this dough.
3. Clean the bilberries, rinse them and place them in the form, in layers with the sugar.
4. Place a lid of dough over the berries.
5. Bake the pie in moderate oven heat.
6. Serve cold with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
