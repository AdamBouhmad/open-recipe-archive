## Ingredients

- 60 grams butter
- 1 1/2 cup crushed rusks
- 18 anchovies
- 3 eggs
- 7-8 decilitre unskimmed milk
- sugar
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter for the molds
- 4 tablespoon crushed rusks for garnish
- 2 eggs for garnish

## Directions

1. The butter is melted and mixed with the crushed rusks
2. The anchovies are cleaned, boned, finely chopped and stirred in
3. The eggs are whisked together with milk and a little sugar and mixed in
4. The batter is poured into buttered and breadcrumb-coated small pastry molds and baked in the oven for 15-20 minutes
5. The molds are turned out, the balls are placed on the serving platter, garnished with hard-boiled, chopped eggs and served with peas and clarified butter
