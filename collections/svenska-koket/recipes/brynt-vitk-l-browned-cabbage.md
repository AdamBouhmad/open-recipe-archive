## Ingredients

- 1 small white cabbage head
- 3 liters water, 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.) for blanching
- 2 tablespoon sugar (30 gr.)
- 2 teaspoon soy sauce
- 3 deciliter boiling broth
- 3 tablespoon butter (60 gr.)
- 1-2 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Remove the outer green leaves from the cabbage head, loosen the remaining leaves from the core and remove the thickest part of the main rib
2. Blanch the cabbage (place in boiling salted water and let cook 8-10 minutes without a lid), remove with a slotted spoon, let drain on cloth and cut into cubes or strips
3. Brown the butter and sugar in a pot, add the cabbage, pour the soy sauce over and brown with frequent stirring until it gets a nice color
4. Add the broth and let the cabbage cook slowly with a lid until completely tender, or about 3 hours
5. Season with salt and more sugar if desired
6. Serve with boiled or roasted pork and boiled sausage
