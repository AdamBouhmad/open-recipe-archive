## Ingredients

- 5 hg. boneless, roasted meat of beef, veal or mutton
- 8 dcl. bouillon or water
- 1 red onion
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 5 dcl. of the bouillon
- 2 msk. finely cut salt cucumber
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. The meat is cut from the bones in even, beautiful pieces.
2. The bones are crushed and cooked as in the preceding recipe.
3. The onion is peeled, chopped finely and lightly browned in the butter.
4. The flour is fried with the butter and onion for 2 minutes, the strained bouillon is thinned in and the sauce is allowed to boil for 5 minutes.
5. The meat and salt cucumber are added and warmed in the sauce but must not boil.
6. The preparation is seasoned with spices, placed on a warm platter and garnished with fried potatoes.
