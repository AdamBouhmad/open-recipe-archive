## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg boneless veal
- salt, white pepper
- 3.5 tablespoons butter
- 1 bunch parsley
- 2 cups broth or water
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into slices across the grain, pound lightly and sprinkle with pepper and salt.
2. Rinse, clean and chop the parsley coarsely and work it into 2 tablespoons of the butter.
3. Divide the parsley butter into as many parts as there are meat slices; place one part on each slice, which is then rolled up and tied with string (previously dipped in boiling water).
4. Brown the birds in the remaining butter, add boiling broth or water and let them fry on low heat until they feel tender, then remove and take off the strings.
5. Strain the sauce, skim, finish with cream and serve over the birds.
