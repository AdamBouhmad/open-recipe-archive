## Ingredients

- 1 ham of approximately 5 kg
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard, in flat slices
- 3/4 liter good meat stock or broth
- 5 parsley stems
- 1 bay leaf

## Directions

1. Dry the ham with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and score the skin 1 cm deep in diagonal squares.
2. Rub the underside and in the scores with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Spread the lard slices in a baking pan, place the ham on top with skin side up.
4. Set in a hot oven and roast until tender, or approximately 2 1/2 hours.
5. When the skin pieces get a nice yellow color, harden, and come loose, remove them and use for garnish.
6. After the ham has roasted approximately 1 1/2 hours, dilute it with the boiling meat stock, added with the parsley and bay leaf.
