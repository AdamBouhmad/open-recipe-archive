## Ingredients

- 1 hectogram butter
- 125 grams flour
- 125 grams grated cheese
- 1 egg yolk for brushing

## Directions

1. Soften the butter and mix with the cheese and flour.
2. Form finger-thick sticks from the dough, which are cut into approximately 5 cm long pieces.
3. Place them on a baking sheet, brush with the egg yolk, and bake in ordinary oven heat.
4. Serve with broth or finished soups.
