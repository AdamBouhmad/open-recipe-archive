## Ingredients

- 50 g butter
- 2 dcl. finely crushed rusks
- 12 anchovies
- 25 g veal roast
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 tsp. sugar
- 6 dcl. milk
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 g) for the forms
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the forms
- 2 hard-boiled eggs for garnish

## Directions

1. Clarify the butter and mix with the crushed rusks.
2. Clean the anchovies, remove the bones, chop finely and add together with the finely chopped veal roast.
3. Whisk the eggs with the sugar and add.
4. Bring the milk to a boil and pour hot over the mixture, which is then stirred well.
5. Fill into small, buttered and breadcrumbed pastry forms and bake in the oven for about 15 minutes.
6. Turn the balls out onto a round serving platter, garnish with the chopped eggs and serve as a breakfast dish with canned peas, mixed with 1 tbsp. cold butter.
