## Ingredients

- 2 liters tender nettles
- 1 small bunch chives
- 6 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 liters good broth
- white pepper
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the nettles thoroughly, rinse them and blanch in salted boiling water for about 15 minutes.
2. Drain the water and rinse the nettles with fresh water, then place on a sieve to drain.
3. Chop the nettles finely together with the rinsed and cleaned chives and sprinkle with flour.
4. Fry them in the butter, then add the hot broth.
5. Let the kale cook very slowly with the lid tightly closed for about 3-4 hours.
6. Skim well, season with spices and serve with poached eggs.
