## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg boneless veal
- 80 grams butter
- 2 eggs
- 4 decilitres thick cream
- 3 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams)
- 2 carrots for garnish
- 1/2 can haricots verts
- 1 hectogram macaroni
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams) for the mold

## Directions

1. Prepare the panade: boil the water with salt and butter, then add the flour.
2. Stir the mixture with a spoon over direct heat until it releases from the pan and spoon, then place it on a buttered platter and brush lightly with butter so it does not dry.
