## Ingredients

- 5 shallots
- 1/2 kg. red tomatoes
- 80 gr. butter
- 6 msk. flour (60 gr.)
- 3 lit. dark broth
- salt
- sugar
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Peel and chop the shallots coarsely. Fry them well in 1 msk. of the butter.
2. Clean and rinse the tomatoes, cut them into wedges and cook together with the shallots and 1/2 lit. of the broth with a tight lid about 3/4 hour.
3. Pass the tomatoes through a sieve.
4. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes. Add the purée and remaining broth and let the soup cook with stirring for 5 minutes.
5. Season with spices and serve with fried bread slices.
