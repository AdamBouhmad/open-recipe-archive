## Ingredients

- 0.5 liters thick cream
- grated zest of 0.5 lemon
- 4 parts apple sauce (sweetened)
- 7 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Mix the applesauce with the grated lemon zest and with the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water.
2. Last stir in carefully the cream beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugared mold and set it out to stiffen.
4. When it is to be served, dip the mold briefly in lukewarm water, turn out the pudding and garnish with fruit and biscuits.
