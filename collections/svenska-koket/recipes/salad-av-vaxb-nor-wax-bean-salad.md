## Ingredients

- 2-3 hectograms wax beans
- 6 deciliters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- a few grains white pepper
- 3-4 tablespoons fine table oil
- 1 1/2-2 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the beans, rinse them, let them drain, place in boiling water with salt and let them cook until soft.
2. Place them on a cloth with a slotted spoon, let them cool and cut into 1 cm. wide pieces on the slant.
3. Place the beans in a salad bowl and pour the sauce over them, which is prepared as in no. 448.
4. Garnish the salad with the parsley and serve with roasted meat.
