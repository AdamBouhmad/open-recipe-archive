## Ingredients

- 8 large egg whites
- 400 gr. powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 1 liter heavy cream
- Carmine coloring

## Directions

1. The egg whites are beaten to stiff peaks, and whisking is continued for 7 minutes.
2. Thereafter the mixture is further prepared (recipe continues on next page).
