## Ingredients

- 50 small white or 75 small green cucumbers
- 1 hectogram coarse salt
- 3 1/2 liters water for salt brine
- 10 grams whole white pepper
- 8 grams cloves
- 4 grams nutmeg flower
- 3 grams hulled cardamom
- Horseradish in small pieces (3 deciliters)
- Cherry and currant leaves
- Dill heads
- 3 liters water
- 1 liter vinegar
- 3 hectograms sugar

## Directions

1. Let the cucumbers soak for 24 hours in fresh water, then in salt brine for 48 hours, after which let them dry for about 5 hours on double linen.
2. Layer them alternately with the spices, horseradish, leaves and dill heads in a large jar, but so that they do not touch each other or the jar's edge, as they will then become spotted.
3. Heat the water, vinegar and sugar and pour it hot over the cucumbers, which should be well covered with it.
4. Place a weight and a light weight on top, and cover the jar with fourfold linen.
5. When the cucumbers are cold, bind the jar well.
6. Store in a cellar. They are ready after 5-6 weeks.
