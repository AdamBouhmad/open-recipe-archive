## Ingredients

- 1 kg veal knuckle
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 1 carrot
- 1/2 celeriac
- 4 parsley stems
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1 liter brown broth
- salt, 1 teaspoon sugar (5 g)
- cayenne
- 1/2 deciliter Madeira or sherry

## Directions

1. Wash the meat well in cold water, dry it and chop into small pieces, place in cold water which is allowed to come slowly to a boil and skim well.
2. Add the salt, the trimmed and cut-up vegetables and parsley and let everything simmer slowly for about 3 hours.
3. Remove the meat, strain the stock, skim well and measure—should be 1/2 liter.
4. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the stock and the hot beef broth and let the soup simmer while stirring for 5 minutes, then season with spices and wine and serve very hot with egg soufflé (see no. 309).
5. If desired, the meat can be cut into small strips and served in the soup.
