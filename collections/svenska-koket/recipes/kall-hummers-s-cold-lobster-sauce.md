## Ingredients

- shell and roe from 2 lobsters
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 1 msk. vinegar
- 2 msk. fine salad oil
- 2 dcl. thick cream
- salt, sugar, paprika

## Directions

1. Pound the shells, roe, fat from the lobster and butter in a stone mortar until fine, pass through a sieve and strain through muslin.
2. Add the vinegar and salad oil, little by little, while stirring.
3. Fold in the cream, whipped to stiff peaks, carefully and season the sauce with the spices.
4. Pour over chopped lobster or crayfish tails.
