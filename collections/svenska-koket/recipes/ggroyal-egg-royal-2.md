## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- 1 1/2 deciliters boiling cream or consommé
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. The egg yolks and whites are whisked quickly, the boiling cream or consommé is poured in while whisking and the egg mixture is skimmed.
2. It is poured into a well-buttered, smooth timbale mold with a lid, which is placed in a pot with boiling water.
3. The pot is placed to the side of the fire, so that the water stays hot without boiling, otherwise the egg mixture becomes watery and cannot be used.
4. When it has set, after approximately 35 minutes, it is done.
5. The mold is carefully removed and the royal is allowed to cool in its mold.
6. It is turned out onto a napkin, wiped on top and cut into 1/2 cm thick slices, which are cut into beautiful shapes or small balls with a fluted or smooth mold.
7. They are served as garnish in clear bouillon or consommé.
