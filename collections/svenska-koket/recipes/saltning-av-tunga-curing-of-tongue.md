## Ingredients

- 1 ox tongue
- 4 tablespoons salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon potassium nitrate
- 6 liters water
- 1 liter coarse salt
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 2 teaspoons potassium nitrate

## Directions

1. Rub the tongue with a mixture of salt, sugar and nitrate.
2. After one day, brine will form, and the tongue should remain in it for 3-14 days, depending on how salty you want it.
3. It should be turned twice daily during this time.
4. If curing multiple tongues and insufficient brine forms to cover them, cook a brine from water, coarse salt, sugar and potassium nitrate.
5. Skim the brine well during cooking and set aside to cool.
6. Only when completely cold, pour it over the tongue.
