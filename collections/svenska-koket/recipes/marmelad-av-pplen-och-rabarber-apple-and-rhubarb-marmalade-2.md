## Ingredients

- 1 kg. apples, cut in pieces
- 1/2 lit. water
- Rhubarb, equal weight with the apple sauce
- Sugar, equal weight with the mass

## Directions

1. The apples are treated and cooked as in the previous recipe.
2. The strained sauce is weighed, and raw rhubarb of equal weight is taken.
3. The stalks are rinsed, cut in small pieces and mixed with the sauce.
4. The mass is weighed and for each kg. thereof 625 gr. granulated sugar is taken.
5. The marmalade is cooked quickly under vigorous stirring toward the bottom, until bubbles form on the surface or it falls in flakes from the spoon.
6. It is then poured and treated as apple sauce (see previous recipe).
