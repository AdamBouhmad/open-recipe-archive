## Ingredients

- 3 kg wheat flour (or 2 kg wheat flour and 1 kg rye sifted flour)
- 2 liters lukewarm milk
- 80 gr pressed yeast
- 6 hg butter or margarine
- 2½ kkp syrup
- 2 tsk salt
- 1 msk crushed fennel
- 1 msk crushed anise
- 6-8 msk finely chopped orange peel

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm milk.
2. Sift the flour, set aside 3-4 kkp for baking, and place the rest in a bowl.
3. Add the milk with vigorous stirring, mix in the dissolved yeast, and beat the dough smooth and glossy.
4. Place in a warm spot to rise to double size.
5. Work the butter until it becomes white and fluffy, then work it into the dough together with the syrup and spices.
6. Let the dough rise again.
7. Bake out into long, narrow breads, place on buttered sheets to rise and then bake.
8. When the bread is cooled, cut it into approximately 3 cm thick slices, then divide each in half into upper and lower rusks and cut.
9. Place the rusks in medium oven heat to get a beautiful golden-brown color, remove and let cool.
10. Place them in weak oven heat to become completely dry and crispy.
