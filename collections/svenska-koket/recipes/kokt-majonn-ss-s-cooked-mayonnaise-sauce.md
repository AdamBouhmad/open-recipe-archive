## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 tablespoon mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 parts stock or fish stock
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons fine salad oil
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. The butter, flour, mustard and white pepper are cooked together in a pot
2. Stock is added, and the sauce is allowed to boil until it is thick as thin porridge
3. When it is cooked, it is strained through a sieve and stirred until it has cooled
4. Then the sugar and egg yolks are added one at a time and the salad oil and vinegar drop by drop with constant stirring
5. Finally the cream whipped to stiff peaks is folded in
