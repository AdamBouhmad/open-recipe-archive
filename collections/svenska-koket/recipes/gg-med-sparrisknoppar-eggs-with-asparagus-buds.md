## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 6 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 18 asparagus buds
- 2 tablespoons tomato purée

## Directions

1. Butter six small heatproof porcelain pans or regular scallop shells with half of the butter.
2. Carefully place one raw egg in each pan, season with salt and pepper, and cook in a water bath in the oven for approximately 6 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and let stand at the side of the stove for approximately 15 minutes.
4. Fry the asparagus buds in the remaining butter and use them to garnish the eggs.
5. Spray the tomato purée through a piping cone in a ring around each egg.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish.
