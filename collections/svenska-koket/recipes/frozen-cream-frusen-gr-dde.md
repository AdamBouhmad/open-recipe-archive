## Ingredients

- 4 dcl. extra thick heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks and flavor with vanilla sugar if desired
2. Freeze it in a glace machine, serve with a spoon in a silver bowl and serve with fruit
3. It can also be packed in a glace mold and left to stand for 1 hour in ice and salt
4. Turn out and garnish with cooked fruit
5. The cream can also be mixed with 3 dcl. fruit puree and frozen in the same way
