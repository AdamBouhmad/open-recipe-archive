## Ingredients

- 2 large celeriac roots
- 100 g beef
- 100 g veal
- 50 g lard
- 1 tablespoon crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 tablespoon chopped pickled onion
- 2 tablespoons butter for frying

## Directions

1. Scrape and rinse the celeriac well, then cook until half-soft in water.
2. Remove from water, cut out a lid, and hollow out the celeriac from the top.
3. Fill with well-worked and seasoned meat forcemeat and replace the lids.
4. Heat the butter in a saucepan and place the celeriac in it.
5. Slowly braise with a tight-fitting lid, adding good broth until the celeriac is completely soft and the forcemeat is cooked through.
6. Remove and serve with its sauce.
