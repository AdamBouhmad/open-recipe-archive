## Ingredients

- 2-3 hg. wax beans
- 6 dcl. water
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1 msk. finely chopped parsley
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- a few grains white pepper
- 3-4 msk. fine table oil
- 1 1/2-2 msk. vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the beans, rinse them, let them drain, place them in boiling water with salt and let them cook until soft.
2. Lay them out with a slotted spoon on a cloth, let them cool and cut them into 1 cm. wide pieces on the diagonal.
3. Place the beans in a salad bowl and pour the sauce over them, which is prepared as in no. 448.
4. Garnish the salad with parsley and serve with roasted meat.
