## Ingredients

- 1 part rice grains
- 1 to 1.5 liters skimmed milk
- 50 gr. caster sugar
- 1.5 drops vanilla essence
- 4 parts thick cream
- 3 to 4 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Rinse the grains and cook them in the milk to a porridge, which is set out to cool.
2. Fold the sugar, vanilla essence, the cream whipped to stiff peaks and the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water into the porridge.
3. Pour the pudding into a water-rinsed mold and set out to stiffen.
4. When it is to be served, dip the mold quickly in lukewarm water, turn out the pudding and serve with jam or plum sauce.
