## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hg. good macaroni
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- Béchamel sauce: 80 gr. butter
- 1 finely chopped red onion
- 50 gr. lean veal (in fine cubes)
- 40 gr. flour
- 8 dcl. boiling milk
- salt, white pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- For the form: 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 3 tablespoons ground breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into 4 cm. long pieces, place in boiling salted water and cook for about 20 minutes.
2. Strain in a colander, rinse with cold water and let drain.
3. For the sauce, fry the onion, meat, and flour (which should be dried in the oven) in the butter for about 10 minutes without browning.
4. Add the boiling milk, add the seasonings and let the sauce cook while stirring for 15 minutes, then strain through cloth and season.
5. Butter a fireproof form and sprinkle with ground breadcrumbs.
6. Layer the macaroni alternately with the sauce and grated cheese.
7. The top layer should be sauce, sprinkled with cheese and breadcrumbs.
8. Gratine the dish in a warm oven for about 15 minutes.
9. Serve as an intermediate course.
