## Ingredients

- 3–4 willow grouse
- 1 hg. lard, in 3–4 slices
- 3–4 tbsp. butter (60–80 gr.)
- 2 tsp. salt
- 3 dcl. broth
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. The willow grouse meat is white and beautiful with a distinctly pronounced flavor of game. They are treated and roasted as red partridge.
2. Willow grouse can also be roasted as pheasants (see no. 614).
3. They are split lengthwise, but only the breasts are served.
4. Garnish with browned potatoes, cooked apple halves, filled with currant jelly and salad or parsley.
5. Serve with reduced sauce, added with cream and cold butter (see no. 429) and, if desired, with 1 tbsp. currant jelly.
