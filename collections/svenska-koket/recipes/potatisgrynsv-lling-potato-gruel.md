## Ingredients

- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters milk
- 0.5 cup potato grains
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt butter in a pot, pour in milk and bring to boil.
2. Shake in the grains and let the gruel simmer for 0.5 hour with stirring, or until the grains are clear.
3. Season and serve.
