## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 5 gr. spinach or parsley
- 1/2 anchovy
- 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 6 tomato slices
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes, cool in cold water, peel, and cut in half crosswise.
2. Carefully remove the yolks and pound them in a mortar together with the butter, finely chopped spinach or parsley, cleaned and boned anchovy, and sugar.
3. Pass the egg mixture through a hair sieve and pipe through a fluted pastry bag into each egg half. Insert a small parsley sprig at the edge as a handle.
4. Place the tomato slices on a plate and lay one egg half on each slice. Pipe the remaining mixture in a ring around the edge of each slice.
5. Garnish with green salad or parsley and serve for the smorgasbord.
