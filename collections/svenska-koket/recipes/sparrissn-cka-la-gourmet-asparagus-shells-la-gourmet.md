## Ingredients

- 1 tin asparagus tips (1/2 liter)
- 1 liter water
- 10 grams salt
- 6 scallop shells
- White sauce: 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1 deciliter asparagus cooking liquid
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese

## Directions

1. Open the tin and treat the asparagus as in the previous recipe
2. Butter six scallop shells with cold butter and place the asparagus in them
3. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes
4. Add the asparagus cooking liquid and cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes
5. Season the sauce with the spices and bind it with the egg yolk
6. Pour the sauce over the asparagus in the shells and sprinkle a little grated cheese over
7. Pour a little clarified butter over the preparation
8. Place the shells in a roasting pan with coarse salt and gratine in a warm oven for 10-15 minutes
9. Serve immediately on a round platter with a folded napkin, garnished with salad or parsley
