## Ingredients

- 20 fried smelts
- 1 tsp allspice berries
- 4 bay leaves
- 1 chopped red onion
- 2 cups vinegar
- 3 tbsp sugar

## Directions

1. Place the fried, cold smelts in a jar, alternating with the spices
2. Mix the vinegar with a little sugar and pour over the smelts
3. The brine should cover them well
