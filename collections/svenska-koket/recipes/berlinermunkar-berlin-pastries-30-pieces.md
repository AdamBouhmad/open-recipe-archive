## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dl. milk
- 75 gr. butter
- 2 eggs
- 25 gr. caster sugar
- zest and juice of 1/4 lemon
- 30 gr. fresh yeast
- 4 hg. wheat flour
- apple sauce or currant jelly
- 1/2 kg. deep fat for cooking

## Directions

1. Warm the milk and mix it with the clarified butter, the beaten eggs, sugar, lemon zest and juice, and the yeast dissolved in a little warm milk.
2. Work in the flour, beat the dough smooth and shiny, and let it rise for approximately 1 hour.
3. Work the dough and roll it out finger-thick, and cut small round cakes with a glass.
4. Place a little currant jelly or apple sauce in the middle of each cake and place another cake on top; brush the edges with egg white and press them firmly together.
5. Let the pastries rise for 1 hour.
6. Cook them in deep fat, place them on gray paper to drain, and roll them in sugar.
7. Serve warm.
