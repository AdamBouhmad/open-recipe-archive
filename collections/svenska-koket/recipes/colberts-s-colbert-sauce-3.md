## Ingredients

- 200 g good butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 pinch nutmeg
- juice of 2 lemons
- 2 parts strong bouillon

## Directions

1. Work the butter well with the rinsed and coarsely chopped parsley.
2. Bring the bouillon to a boil and move it to the side, stir in the worked butter together with the lemon juice.
3. The sauce should not boil after this.
4. Add a tablespoon of cold water and serve immediately.
