## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters mixed veal and beef broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 part cream
- 6 tablespoons canned mushrooms with their liquid
- 0.5 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and flour together, gradually add the broth while stirring constantly, and let the soup simmer for 10 minutes, then season.
2. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl and pour the soup over while whisking vigorously.
3. Sauté the mushrooms in 0.5 tablespoon butter and add them with their liquid to the soup.
