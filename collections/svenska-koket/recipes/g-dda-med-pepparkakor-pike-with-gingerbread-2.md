## Ingredients

- 1 pike, 1 1/2 kg
- 1 small salted herring
- 1/2 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 3 decilitres boiling water
- 3 gingerbread cookies
- 2 decilitres cream

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike as described on page 171; the head may remain but remove the gills. Clean the unsalted herring, rinse, dry it and place it inside the pike, then brush it with egg and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
2. Grease a long pan with half of the cold butter over the area the fish occupies, place the pike in it and place the remaining butter in flakes on top. Roast the fish in a hot oven for about 1/2 hour, basting it often and adding water as needed.
3. Crush the gingerbread finely and mix with the cream. Remove the herring from the pike, place the pike on a hot serving dish and garnish with parsley.
4. Thicken the sauce in the pan with the dissolved gingerbread, let it boil and strain, then serve in a separate sauce bowl.
