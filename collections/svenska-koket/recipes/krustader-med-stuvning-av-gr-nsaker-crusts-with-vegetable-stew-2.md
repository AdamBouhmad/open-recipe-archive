## Ingredients

- 12-14 crusts
- 1 bunch tender carrots
- 1 small head of cauliflower
- 6 wax beans
- 1 1/2 cups fresh peas
- 12 asparagus tips
- 8 decilitres water, 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 grams)
- 1/2 litre vegetable stock
- 1/2 teaspoon meat extract
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the crusts as in recipe 46.
2. Clean the vegetables, place in boiling water with sugar added, and let them cook until tender.
3. Drain them in a colander and save the stock.
4. Divide the cauliflower into small pieces; cut the other vegetables neatly with a vegetable knife.
5. Fry butter and flour 2 minutes; stir in the vegetable stock mixed with meat extract and salt, and let the sauce cook 5 minutes.
6. Finish it with the whipped egg yolk and cream, let it simmer and taste with salt.
7. Mix the vegetables carefully with the sauce and fill the stew into the warm crusts.
8. Serve as a middle course or as a garnish to smoked salmon, fried fish, etc.
