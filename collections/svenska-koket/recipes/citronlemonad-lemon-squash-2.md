## Ingredients

- 2 lemons
- 2.5 kg. sugar
- 1 hectogram citric acid
- 2 liters boiling water

## Directions

1. Let the juice drip.
2. Weigh the juice, add the sugar, and when it is melted, cook the juice on a fresh fire with careful skimming until it is clear, or approximately 10 minutes.
3. Pour the juice hot into warm bottles.
