## Ingredients

- 2 liters lingonberries plus 1 1/2 liters water to make 1 liter lingonberry juice
- 750 gr crushed sugar
- 3 liters peeled pears

## Directions

1. The lingonberries are cleaned and rinsed, placed with the cold water and cooked slowly until they fall apart, then poured into a sieve cloth to drip naturally.
2. The lingonberry juice is cooked with the sugar to a clear syrup, which is well skimmed.
3. The pears are peeled, halved, the cores removed, the stems scraped, and the pears are placed directly in the syrup.
4. They are then cooked slowly without a lid until they appear clear, then removed with a slotted spoon onto a flat dish to cool before placing in the jar.
5. Let the syrup boil together somewhat, if needed, let it cool and pour it over the pears. Lingon apples are cooked the same way.
