## Ingredients

- 3 litres red, half-ripe gooseberries
- ½ litre water
- sugar equal in weight to the juice

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the berries and cook them very slowly with the water until they begin to burst, then pour them into cheesecloth and let them drip until the following day.
2. Weigh the juice and dissolve the sugar in the cold juice, which is then brought to the boil and cooked rapidly for 15-20 minutes with careful skimming.
3. The jelly is ready when a drop of it, poured on a plate, sets quickly.
4. Black currant, raspberry and lingonberry jelly are cooked in the same way.
