## Ingredients

- Approximately 2 kg. salmon
- 30 gr. coarse salt
- 2 gr. saltpeter
- 10 gr. caster sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon coarsely ground white pepper
- 1 small bunch dill

## Directions

1. From a salmon weighing 3–4 kg., cut a piece crosswise across the middle
2. Clean and scrape the salmon piece well, carefully remove the backbone so that it becomes two equal pieces, and dry these with a cloth wrung out in cold water
3. Pound the salt and saltpeter very finely and mix with the sugar
4. Sprinkle the salmon pieces with the salt mixture and rub them gently with it
5. Sprinkle with pepper and a few dill sprigs if desired
6. Place the salmon pieces together with the meat sides against each other so that the thick side of the salmon is placed against the thin side
7. Place it thereafter in a gentle press between two cutting boards overnight and the salmon is then ready
8. Cut into 1/2 cm. thick slices, which are arranged beautifully on a platter, garnished with salmon skin, toasted on the stove or fried, and fresh dill sprigs
9. Serve with salad oil, vinegar, caster sugar, French mustard, salt and pepper
