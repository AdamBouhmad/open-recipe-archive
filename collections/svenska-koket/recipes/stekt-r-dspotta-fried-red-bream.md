## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg red bream
- 1 tbsp salt (15 gr)
- 3 dcl milk for breading
- 4 tbsp flour (40 gr)
- 2 eggs
- 2 tsp cooking oil
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 1/2 cups crushed rusks
- 1/2 kg lard for frying
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the red breams as in the previous recipe, but cut off the head and remove the dark skin by scraping the tail fin with a pointed knife upward until enough skin is loosened to wind around the knife blade, then roll the knife toward the head and wind the skin around it
2. Rub the fish with salt, let it rest for 1 hour, dry it again and bread it as described
