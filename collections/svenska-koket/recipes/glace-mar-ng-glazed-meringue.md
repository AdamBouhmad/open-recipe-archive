## Ingredients

- 150 gr. powdered sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 4 dcl. vanilla glace

## Directions

1. Prepare the meringue mixture as in recipe no. 921 and place with a spoon on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets in the form of eggs, which are smoothed over with the spoon and dusted with sugar.
2. Bake in gentle oven heat until they are half-dry and release from the sheet, then remove them, form on an egg and let cool.
3. Fill the meringues with vanilla glace (see recipe no. 930) and serve in pairs.
