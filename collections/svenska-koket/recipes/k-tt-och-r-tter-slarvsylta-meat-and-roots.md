## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. rib or flank
- 1 liter water
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp. crushed coarse pepper
- 1 large rutabaga
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and cut into oblong pieces of suitable size for serving.
2. Boil the water with the salt, add the meat and skim when it boils.
