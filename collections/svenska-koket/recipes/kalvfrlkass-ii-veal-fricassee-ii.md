## Ingredients

- 1.5-2 kg veal breast
- 1.5 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 gr)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 2 carrots
- 5 parsley stems
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 gr)
- 6 deciliters broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the meat as in recipe number 524, place on a warm plate and cover while the sauce is being made
2. Strain the broth and skim well, and make the sauce as in recipe number 441, but omit the egg yolks and use parsley instead of lemon juice
3. Pour the sauce over the meat, which is served with boiled potatoes
