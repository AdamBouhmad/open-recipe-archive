## Ingredients

- 3 kg salted and smoked beef brisket
- water

## Directions

1. Brush the brisket well with lukewarm water, break it and let it soak in plenty of cold water for approximately 12 hours.
2. Place it in cold water, bring to a rapid boil, skim well, and cook as in recipe no. 497.
3. Let the brisket remain in its cooking liquid for 1/2 hour before serving, as this makes it more tender.
4. Serve sliced into even, beautiful slices, with vegetables, vegetable pastries, and some of the cooking liquid mixed with a little good broth, or as part of a mixed meat dish.
5. For a finer presentation (cold hamburger brisket): let it cool in the cooking liquid, slice into thin slices, arrange on a base of dark gelée, and garnish with gelée in molds or gelée croutons and parsley. Serve with potato or vegetable salad.
