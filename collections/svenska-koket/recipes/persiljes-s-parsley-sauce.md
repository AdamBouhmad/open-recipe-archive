## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1/2 liter fish stock
- 4 tbsp chopped parsley
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Fry together the butter and flour, add the fish stock, add the chopped parsley, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
2. Season with the spices.
