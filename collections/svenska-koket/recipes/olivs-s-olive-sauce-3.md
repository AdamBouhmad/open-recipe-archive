## Ingredients

- 9 preserved olives
- 6 dcl. good meat stock or bouillon
- 2 tbsp butter (40 gr.)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr.)
- salt, (sugar), white pepper
- (2 egg yolks, 1 dcl. thick cream)

## Directions

1. Peel the olives in even rings from the pits, boil these pits for 10 minutes in the meat stock so that the sauce will have a stronger olive flavor.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, strain the meat stock from the pits and add it little by little, add the olives and let the sauce cook while stirring for 10 minutes.
3. Season with spices and bind, if desired, with the beaten egg yolks and cream.
4. Serve with boiled veal or lamb breast.
