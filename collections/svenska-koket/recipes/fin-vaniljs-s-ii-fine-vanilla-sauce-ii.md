## Ingredients

- 6 parts heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 1 vanilla bean
- 5-6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The cream is boiled with the vanilla bean and sugar until it has attained sufficient vanilla flavor, after which it is strained through a fine sieve.
2. It is poured back into the pan, and the egg yolks are whisked in one at a time.
3. The pan is placed back over the heat, and the sauce is allowed to simmer while whisking until it reaches the right thickness.
4. Whisking is continued until the sauce is cold.
