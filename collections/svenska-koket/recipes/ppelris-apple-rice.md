## Ingredients

- 150 grams sugar
- 4 deciliters water
- 1 liter good, tart apples
- juice of 1/2 lemon or 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 1/2 deciliters Carolina rice grains
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 large egg whites
- 70 grams caster sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the apple compote as in recipe 820.
2. Treat and cook the rice grains as Indian rice (see recipe 795), but omit the salt.
3. When the grains are taken from the oven, mix them with the apple liquid.
4. Arrange the rice in a ring on a heat-proof dish or preferably on a silver dish.
5. Place the compote in a pyramid inside the rice.
6. Whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks form, gently fold in the sifted caster sugar.
7. Pipe the meringue mixture through a fluted piping bag over the apples.
8. Let the arrangement then stand in mild oven heat until the meringue is half-dry and has a light yellow color.
9. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream.
