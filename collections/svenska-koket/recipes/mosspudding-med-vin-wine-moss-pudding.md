## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 2 parts sherry
- 100 gr. sugar
- zest and juice of 2 lemons
- 6 sheets gelatin
- 3 parts thick cream
- biscuits for garnish

## Directions

1. The egg yolks, sugar, wine, grated lemon zest and juice are whipped in a pot over heat until the cream begins to simmer, then it is removed.
2. Whipping is continued until the cream has cooled.
3. Then the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water and the cream whipped to stiff peaks are added.
4. The cream is poured into a rinsed and sugared mold and set out to stiffen.
5. When it is to be served, the mold is held for a moment in lukewarm water, the pudding is turned out and garnished with biscuits.
