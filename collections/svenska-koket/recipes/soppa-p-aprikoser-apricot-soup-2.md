## Ingredients

- 250 g dried apricots
- 2 liters water
- zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1-1½ teaspoons crushed sugar
- 25 g rice or potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots very well in lukewarm water and let them soak overnight in the water in which they are to be cooked.
2. Place them in the pot with the lemon zest and sugar and let cook until soft.
3. Thicken the soup with the rice flour, stirred into ½ cup cold water, and let cook 5 minutes.
4. Season with the juice of ¼ lemon and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and yeast-raised rusks.
