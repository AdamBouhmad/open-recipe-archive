## Ingredients

- 1 calf brain
- 1 lit. water for blanching
- 2 tsp. salt for blanching
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. thick cream
- salt
- white pepper
- ginger
- croutons (fried bread slices)
- warm carrot for serving

## Directions

1. Let the calf brain soak in water, changing it often so that all blood is drawn out.
2. Blanch it as in recipe no. 47, for 45 minutes and place on a sieve.
3. When it is cold, remove all membranes and chop the brain with a knife on a cutting board.
4. Fry the butter in a pot, add the brain and fry well, then add the cream and seasonings and let the stew come to a good boil.
5. Serve on warm carrot and garnish with small croutons (fried bread slices).
6. Serve to the smorgasbord.
