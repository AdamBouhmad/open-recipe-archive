## Ingredients

- 2 kg. pike (1 fish)
- 2 tbsp. salt
- 2 tsp. vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the pike, but do not scale it.
2. Rinse it well, dry it with a cloth and rub it with the vinegar and salt.
3. Bend the fish so that head and tail meet, and place it with the back upward in hot, but not boiling water (short broth).
4. When the broth comes to a boil, skim it, and let the fish simmer slowly at the side of the fire until the fins come loose, or for approximately 1/2 hour.
5. Place the pike on the serving platter, garnish with a few sprigs of parsley and serve with clarified butter and grated horseradish or with simple fish sauce or milk sauce with horseradish.
6. The liver can be saved (except during spawning season) and cooked with the fish, then cut up and used as garnish for the fish.
