## Ingredients

- 200 grams sweet almond
- 200 grams granulated sugar
- 2 egg whites
- currant jelly for garnish

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds, grate or grind them through an almond mill and pound with the sugar in a mortar
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and work into the almonds and sugar
3. Place the almond mass with spoons onto well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets in small round cakes
4. Make a small indentation in the center of each cake, which is filled with jelly
5. Bake the kisses in very weak oven heat
