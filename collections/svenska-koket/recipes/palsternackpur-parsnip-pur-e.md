## Ingredients

- 5 medium-sized parsnips
- 2½ liters broth
- 1 tbsp. butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- salt, sugar
- white pepper
- 1-2 egg yolks
- 2 parts cream

## Directions

1. Wash, scrape and cut the parsnips into pieces. Fry butter and flour together, add 1 liter of the broth, and add the parsnips. When completely soft, pass the soup through a sieve.
2. Add the remaining broth, and bring the purée to a boil, skim and season. Whip the egg yolk and cream in the soup bowl, and pour in the purée with vigorous whisking.
3. Serve with toasted bread or fried bread.
