## Ingredients

- 1 large cauliflower head
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 4 eggs
- 3/4 liter milk
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 1 tablespoon butter and 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and place it in vinegar-mixed water for about 1/2 hour.
2. Place it in salted boiling water and let it simmer slowly until it feels tender, then remove it and let it drain.
3. Place the cauliflower in a buttered and breadcrumb-coated mold, pour the egg mixture over it and sprinkle with the grated cheese.
4. Place it in the oven to bake for about 1/2 hour.
