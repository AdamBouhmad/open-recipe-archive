## Ingredients

- 1 piece of salmon weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 6 spice peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 5 dill stems
- 1 1/2 liters of fish stock
- 10-12 gelatin leaves
- 3 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon cold water
- 4 tablespoons good white wine
- 5 dill stems
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- dill

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it, rinse well and dry with a fish towel.
2. Rub with half the salt and let rest for 1/2 hour, dry again and place in cold water with the remaining salt, spices and dill stems.
3. Let simmer slowly until done, about 15 minutes.
4. Remove immediately, peel off the skin and divide into 2 fillets.
5. Place the bone in the stock, which is allowed to cook for another 20 minutes.
6. Cut the fish into beautiful pieces.
7. Prepare aspic from the fish stock with gelatin, egg whites, water and wine.
8. Arrange the dish and serve at the smörgåsbord or as a course with real mayonnaise, garnished with chopped aspic, salad, parsley and dill.
