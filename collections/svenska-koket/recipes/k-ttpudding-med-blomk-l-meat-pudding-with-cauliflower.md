## Ingredients

- Meat mixture as in recipe no. 632
- 1 large cauliflower head
- 1 liter water
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 3 msk. butter (30 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 3-4 dcl. cooking liquid from cauliflower
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat mixture as in recipe no. 632.
2. Trim the cauliflower and soak it in vinegar-mixed water for 1 hour.
3. Place it in boiling water with salt and cook until tender but not falling apart.
4. Divide the cauliflower head into pieces and save the cooking liquid.
5. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, gradually add the cooking liquid, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
6. Add the cauliflower, season the braised vegetables with spices and let cool.
7. Grease a pudding mold with cold butter and dust with breadcrumbs.
8. Layer the meat mixture and the braised vegetables alternately in the mold; the first and last layer should be meat.
9. Cook the pudding in a water bath in the oven or on the stovetop for 1 1/2-2 hours.
10. Unmold, garnish with a ring of cooked cauliflower and serve with gravy (recipe no. 429) or with clarified butter.
11. Brussels sprouts may be used instead of cauliflower.
12. The pudding can also be baked in an ovenproof mold in the oven and is then ready in about 1 hour.
