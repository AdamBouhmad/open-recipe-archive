## Ingredients

- 3 grouse
- 1 hectogram lard
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 0.5–1 liter broth
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for sauce
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat drippings
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck the grouse, singe, clean, rinse well and dry with a cloth. (If the grouse have been frozen, soak them in milk overnight.)
2. Lard them with the lard cut into fine strips and truss.
3. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, place the grouse in breast-side up and brown well on all sides.
4. Salt them and add the boiling broth.
5. Roasting takes approximately 1 hour.
6. Strain and skim the meat drippings, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules.
