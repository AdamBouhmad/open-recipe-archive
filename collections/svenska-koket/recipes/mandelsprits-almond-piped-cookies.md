## Ingredients

- 280 g butter
- 210 g caster sugar
- 1 egg
- 35 g grated bitter almond
- 425 g wheat flour

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and mix it along with the other ingredients with the sifted flour on the work surface.
2. When the dough is well worked, press it through a piping bag and form into 'S' shapes or wreaths.
3. Bake the cookies in moderate oven heat on well-dried baking sheets.
