## Ingredients

- 3 deciliters sugar (255 grams)
- 1 deciliter water
- 1 liter half-ripe green thin-skinned gooseberries (approximately 500 grams)

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar and water together to make a clear, syrupy liquid
2. Top and tail the gooseberries, rinse well and allow to drain on cloth
3. Place them in the sugar liquid and cook until they begin to crack but do not cook until they fall apart
4. Remove the gooseberries with a slotted spoon and place in a compote bowl
5. Cook the syrup until it is clear and syrupy, then pour over the berries
6. Serve cold with whipped cream
