## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal breast
- salt
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 60 gr butter
- 1 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the veal breast with a cloth dipped in hot water and bone it out.
2. Rub it with salt, brush with egg, and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, place the meat in it, and brown on all sides.
4. Then moisten it with boiling water or broth and let it roast slowly until done, about 1 hour.
5. During the roasting baste the breast with its gravy every ten minutes.
6. Serve the sauce either as gravy or finish it according to general rules for sauces.
