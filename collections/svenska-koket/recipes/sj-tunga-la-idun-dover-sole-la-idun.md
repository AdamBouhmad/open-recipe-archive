## Ingredients

- 3 Dover soles of approximately 500 g each
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- For cooking: 1.5 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 5 deciliters fish stock
- For sauce: 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 5 deciliters fish stock
- 2-3 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon fine white wine
- salt
- white pepper
- For garnish: 1/2 liter good potato purée
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons melted butter (40 g)

## Directions

1. Remove the insides from the soles, rinse them thoroughly and dry them; trim the fins.
2. Make a deep cut with a pointed knife along the backbone.
