## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. salted salmon
- 2 bay leaves
- water
- a few allspice berries

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces and remove excess salt by soaking in water.
2. Place it on the fire in cold water together with the spices and let it simmer slowly until cooked through.
3. Remove the fish immediately and serve with currant sauce.
