## Ingredients

- 1 liter salted Turkish beans
- water
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 3 cups milk
- 2 parts cream
- 1 egg yolk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the beans with fresh water. If very salty, soak them the day before.
2. Place in cold water. When the water boils, pour it off and add fresh cold water. Repeat until the beans are properly salted.
3. Let them boil until tender, then strain the broth.
4. Fry the butter and flour together, add milk and cream, and let the sauce boil for about 10 minutes.
5. Remove from heat and finish with the egg yolk.
6. Add the beans and season the stew.
7. The cream and egg yolk can be omitted; instead use a little more milk.
