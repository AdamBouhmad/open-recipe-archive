## Ingredients

- 12 slices wheat bread
- 50 gr. butter
- 1-2 tbsp. egg white
- 40 gr. grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cut out the bread slices with a drinking glass into round cakes (dry and crush the edges), which are spread with butter on one side.
2. Beat the egg white a few times with a fork and add the grated cheese.
3. Mix the cheese mass well, spread on the sandwiches and place them on a buttered sheet.
4. Just before serving, toast them quickly in a hot oven.
5. Serve warm with bouillon, finished soups or purées.
6. The egg white can be omitted and the butter merely mixed with the cheese before spreading on the bread.
