## Ingredients

- Shortcrust pastry: 400 gr butter, 400 gr wheat flour, 90 gr sweet almond, 90 gr powdered sugar
- Apple compote: 3 liters apples, 3 cups sugar, 1-2 parts water

## Directions

1. Prepare a regular shortcrust pastry from the butter, flour, blanched and grated almond, and sugar, and place it out to harden.
2. Peel the apples, cut them into wedges and cook them with the sugar and water to a compote, which is allowed to cool.
3. Roll out the pastry to approximately 1/2 cm thickness.
4. In a form or baking pan, bake a thin bottom of pastry, which is allowed to cool.
5. Line the edges of the form with pastry, add the apple compote, and fold the pastry edges over the fruit.
6. Place the remaining dough as a lid on the fruit and press it well against the folded edge.
7. Bake the cake brown in moderate oven heat.
8. Turn it out only after it has almost cooled, and serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
