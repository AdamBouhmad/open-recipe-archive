## Ingredients

- 35 brussels sprout heads
- 3 liters water for blanching
- 1 tablespoon salt for blanching
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1.5 liters stock
- 150 g fresh lean pork
- 1 small red onion
- 250 ml milk
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the cabbage and blanch for 15 minutes as in the previous recipe
2. Cut the pork into dice, peel and finely chop the onion and sauté together in butter for about 10 minutes
3. Squeeze the cabbage in a cloth, chop finely and sauté well with the pork and flour
4. Add the boiling stock and milk and let the soup simmer while stirring for 15 minutes
5. Strain the soup if desired, season with spices and serve with chicken quenelles and fried bread cubes
