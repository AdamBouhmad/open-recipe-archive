## Ingredients

- 2 kg boneless beef brisket
- 3 hg lard
- 1 tbsp salt (15 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 1/4 liters beef stock
- 1 kg veal knuckles
- 6 allspice berries
- 2 cloves
- 3 carrots
- 2 small Portuguese onions
- 1 dcl French wine
- 1/2 dcl cognac

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until properly tender, is prepared and larded with 2 hg of lard (see page 232), rubbed with the remaining spices and tied so that it has a nice shape.
2. The butter is browned in a braising pot, the meat is placed in and browned on all sides until a nice brown crust is formed, then the rest of the lard with the marrow is placed under the meat, the stock is added and the well-cracked and washed veal knuckles are placed in.
3. The stock is brought to a boil and well skimmed, after which the whole spices, the browned carrots and the peeled onions, the wine and cognac are added and the meat is slowly braised with a tightly closed lid until tender, or approximately 3 hours, during which it is turned a few times.
4. The meat is sliced, placed on a warm dish, covered with a couple of tablespoons of sauce and garnished with the creamy veal knuckles, the beautifully sliced carrots and onions and parsley or salad.
5. Served with boiled potatoes, pickles or pressed cucumber and reduced sauce (see no. 429).
