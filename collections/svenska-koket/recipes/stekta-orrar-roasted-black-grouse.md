## Ingredients

- 2 black grouse
- 1 hectogram lard
- 3—4 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 decilitres bouillon
- 5 decilitres thick cream

## Directions

1. The black grouse hen has lighter meat and is considered finer than the cock.
2. They are treated and prepared as capercaillie but are roasted in 1—1½ hours.
3. The larding can be advantageously omitted, and the birds are instead covered with lard slices when they are trussed.
4. Cut, garnish (see page 300) and serve with strained sauce, mixed with thick cream (see recipe 429).
