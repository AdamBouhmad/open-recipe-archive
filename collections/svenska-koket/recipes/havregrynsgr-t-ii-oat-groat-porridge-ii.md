## Ingredients

- 5 deciliters steam-prepared oat groats (2 hectograms)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (10 grams)
- 1 liter water
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats and drain. Put the butter in a pot, pour in the water, add the groats and let stand overnight to swell. Let the porridge come to a boil while stirring and then let it cook slowly with a tightly closed lid until the groats are completely tender, or about 1 hour. Toward the end of cooking, thin with cream. Season the porridge with salt and serve with milk or cream. If the groats are not left to soak overnight, the porridge should cook for 1 1/2-2 hours. The cream can be omitted and the porridge cooked on plain water, or on plain milk, if desired.
