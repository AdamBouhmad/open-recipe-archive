## Ingredients

- 1 liter good, tart apples
- 5 eggs
- 100 grams granulated sugar
- 0.5 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, place them in a buttered frying pan, and fry in the oven until they are soft, then press them through a fine sieve.
2. Stir the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes, add the cold apple purée, and carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered pie mold and bake in regular oven heat for approximately 30 minutes. Serve immediately with whipped cream.
