## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 7 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1/2 part spirits
- 1/3 teaspoon grated lemon zest or 1 drop orange
- 250-300 grams wheat flour
- 1/2 kg butter for frying

## Directions

1. Work all ingredients together well
2. Place the dough in a cold place to rest for at least 2 hours
3. Roll out to approximately 1/2 cm thickness and cut out into klenäter shapes
4. Cook in hot butter
5. Sprinkle with sugar and serve with jam
