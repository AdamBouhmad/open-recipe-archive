## Ingredients

- 105 grams butter
- 50 grams granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 160 grams wheat flour
- 1 knife point baking soda salt
- 100 grams sweet almond
- 10 bitter almonds
- 110 grams granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Prepare the pastry dough in the usual way
2. Blanch the almond, grate it or pass it through an almond mill, pound it fine together with the sugar and a little water, and work in the egg well
3. Line small fluted molds with pastry dough and fill with almond paste
4. Place a cross of pastry dough on each pastry, after which they are placed in moderate oven heat to bake
5. Turn them out as soon as they are ready
