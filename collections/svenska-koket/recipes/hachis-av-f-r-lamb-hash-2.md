## Ingredients

- 400-500 g boned lamb roast
- 30 g butter (1½ tbsp)
- 30 g flour (3 tbsp)
- 300-400 ml broth
- salt
- white pepper
- ½ tbsp finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes.
2. Brown the butter and flour (see Sauces); add the broth and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Warm the meat in the sauce but do not boil, as it becomes tough.
4. Season the dish with spices and parsley and serve within a border of mashed potatoes.
