## Ingredients

- 200 g sugar
- 8 egg yolks
- 1.5 parts water
- 8 parts thick cream
- 0.5 vanilla pod

## Directions

1. A clear syrup is made from the sugar and water, and the vanilla is allowed to cook in it until it gives flavor to the syrup.
2. The pan is lifted to the side of the heat, the egg yolks are whisked in, and everything is allowed to simmer over the heat while whisking.
3. Whisking is continued until the frozen mixture is completely cold.
4. The cream, whipped to stiff peaks, is then folded in, after which the mixture is frozen.
5. When frozen, it is packed into molds and set to freeze again.
6. It is turned out and served.
