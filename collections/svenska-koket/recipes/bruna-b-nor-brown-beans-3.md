## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter brown beans
- 1.5 liters water
- 3-4 tablespoons syrup
- 3-4 tablespoons vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the beans and rinse them
2. Place in cold water and let simmer slowly until completely tender, which takes about 3 hours
3. Add warm water during cooking if needed
4. Season the beans with syrup, vinegar, and a little salt if desired
