## Ingredients

- 6 hg. pike = 220 gr. scraped fish meat
- 125 gr. butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp. flour (15 gr.)
- 2 1/2–3 dcl. cream
- 1 tsk. sugar (5 gr.)
- 1 egg white
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Scale the pike, cut it open along the back and flay it without damaging the skin. Remove the innards, rinse well and dry with a fish towel.
2. Prepare the stuffing as in recipe no. 356.
3. Scrape the skin, rinse it, cut it in the shape of a turtle and spread it on a greased baking sheet with cold butter.
4. Spread the fish stuffing over it, smooth it with a spoon dipped in lukewarm water, and brush over with egg white.
5. Bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 20 minutes, test with a skewer to see if it is done, carefully transfer with a fish spatula to a warm platter and garnish as in the previous recipe.
6. Serve with crayfish, lobster or mushroom sauce. — The stuffing can also be placed around the fish bone (the head and tail remain in place) and formed as a pike, arranged and served as previously described.
