## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless veal
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 200 gr. of the meat for the filling
- 50 gr. lard
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.) for the filling
- 1 tablespoon flour for the filling
- salt and white pepper for the filling
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped mushrooms or truffle
- 1 1/2 to 2 deciliters thick cream for the filling
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.) for browning
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 gr.) for browning
- 3 deciliters broth
- 2 deciliters thick cream for cooking

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water. Use 200 gr. of it for the filling. Cut the rest into 12 slices, which are beaten well with a damp meat mallet and rubbed in with the spices.
2. For the meat filling, grind the meat, lard and butter 4 times through a meat grinder.
3. Pound the filling together with the flour in a stone mortar until it becomes even and smooth, then mix in the spices, onion, mushroom and cream, a little at a time.
4. Spread the filling on the meat slices, which are then rolled up with the filling inside and tied in two places with fine twine dipped in boiling water.
5. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the rolls in and brown them nicely, sprinkle with flour, thin with boiling broth and cream and let them cook with a tightly closed lid until they are tender, or about 3/4 hour.
6. Remove them, cut off the twine, place in a warm carrot and whisk up the sauce, taste it and pour it over the rolls.
7. Serve with mashed potatoes or vegetables.
