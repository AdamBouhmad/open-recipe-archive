## Ingredients

- 175 grams fine prunes
- 75 grams fine, dried apricots
- 4 deciliters water
- 125 grams granulated sugar
- 5 egg whites
- 0.5 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes and apricots well in lukewarm water. Place the prunes in the cold water and let them cook until half-soft, then add the apricots and let the fruit cook soft but not fall apart.
2. Drain in a colander and let it drip off.
3. Pit the prunes, crush half of the pits in a mortar, and chop the almonds finely together with the fruit flesh and apricots in a chopping board.
4. Mix the fruit well with the sugar and carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the mixture into a buttered pie mold and bake in regular oven heat for approximately 30 minutes. Serve immediately from the mold with whipped cream.
