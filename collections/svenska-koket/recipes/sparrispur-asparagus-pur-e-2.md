## Ingredients

- 425 gr. fresh asparagus or 1 can preserved asparagus (1/2 lit.)
- 1/2 lit. boiling water
- 1 tsk. salt
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 1 1/2 lit. light broth
- white pepper, sugar
- 3 egg yolks, 1 dcl. thick cream
- 10 gr. cold butter

## Directions

1. If using fresh asparagus, treat it as in recipe no. 204 and cut off the tips with about 4 cm. of the stem. Place these in the boiling water with the salt and 1/2 msk. of the butter and cook until tender. Lift them out with a slotted spoon into a small bowl and cover.
2. In the same cooking liquid, cook the remaining asparagus about 3/4 hour, then pass it through a sieve and mix with the liquid.
3. Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes. Add the purée and boiling broth gradually and let the soup cook with stirring for 5 minutes. Season with spices.
4. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl, add the cold butter and pour in the purée while whisking vigorously. Finally add the asparagus tips and serve with fried bread if desired.
5. If using preserved asparagus, heat it only in a water bath. Place the tips in the soup bowl and prepare the purée from the remaining asparagus and its liquid in the same way as above.
