## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 hg. salt lax
- 1 lit. skummjölk
- 2 dcl. risgryn (2 hg.)
- 1 1/2 lit. oskummad mjölk
- 3 msk. kallt smör (60 gr.)
- 2-3 ägg
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 2 tsk. socker (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. vitpeppar
- 1/2 msk. smör (10 gr.) till formen
- 3 msk. stötta skorpor till formen

## Directions

1. Treat the salmon as in the preceding recipe and cut it into dice.
2. Rinse the rice grains, scald them in hot water, add to the boiling milk and let them cook, stirring, until almost soft; thin with a little more milk if needed.
3. Pour out and add the cold butter at once; when the grains have cooled, fold in the beaten egg yolks, the diced salmon and the seasonings.
4. Last fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Taste the mixture carefully, pour it into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pudding mold, sprinkle with 1 tablespoon of the breadcrumbs and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 1 hour.
6. Serve with clarified butter.
