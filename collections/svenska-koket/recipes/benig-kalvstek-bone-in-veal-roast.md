## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal breast
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 6 allspice berries
- 1 liter broth or water
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- meat stock
- 1/2 - 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the breast with a cloth dipped in hot water, then break it well.
2. Roast it as regular veal roast, either in the oven or in a pot.
3. Prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules.
