## Ingredients

- Celery stalks
- Butter
- Fine cheese

## Directions

1. Remove the outermost, coarse, green leaves from the celery stalks, but leave a little of the root intact.
2. Rinse them well and let them drain.
3. Serve standing in a tall glass with water, for lunch or dinner, with butter and some fine cheese.
