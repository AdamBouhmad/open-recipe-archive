## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 liter unskimmed milk
- 1 hg. chocolate
- 30 gr. cocoa
- 1 msk. mondamin flour (15 gr.)
- sugar to taste
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Heat the milk; when it reaches the boiling point, pour off 2 thirds of it and dissolve the chocolate broken into small pieces and the cocoa in the remaining milk.
2. Add the remaining milk and when the soup boils, thicken it with the mondamin flour, stirred in 1/2 kkp cold milk.
3. Let the soup boil while stirring for 5 minutes and add sugar to taste; pour it into the soup bowl and place the cream whipped to stiff peaks on top.
4. Instead of milk, water can be used.
