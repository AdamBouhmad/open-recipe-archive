## Ingredients

- Pastry dough: 1 part water, 0.5 egg, 1 tbsp brandy, 0.5 kg flour, 350 g butter
- Filling: 6 parts cream, 6 egg yolks, 2 tbsp sugar, 0.5 tsp salt, 6 bitter almonds, 45 g flour

## Directions

1. Prepare the pastry dough as fine pastry dough (recipe 546)
2. Roll out the dough to approximately 3 mm thickness, and line the pastry molds with it
3. For the filling, whisk all ingredients over heat until the cream begins to simmer and thicken
4. Continue whisking until the cream is cold
5. Fill the pastry molds halfway with the filling, and place a lid of dough on top
6. Bake the pastries in good oven heat
