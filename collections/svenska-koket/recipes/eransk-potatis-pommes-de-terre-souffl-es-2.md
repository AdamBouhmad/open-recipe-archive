## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes (Dutch)
- 1 kg fat/lard
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes, brush them clean, peel thinly and cut first into even squares and then into 3 mm thick slices.
2. The slices must be precisely uniform, otherwise the dish will not succeed.
3. Place them in water as they are cut, then dry them very well in a cloth.
4. Heat half of the fat moderately in a fat pan, add the potato slices and let them cook until they float up, then place them in a wire basket or on absorbent paper to drain.
5. Heat the remaining fat in another pan until it is smoking hot, then immediately place the potato slices in it and let them puff up into small balloons.
6. Place them on absorbent paper with a fat spoon, salt them and serve warm as a garnish around roasted meat.
