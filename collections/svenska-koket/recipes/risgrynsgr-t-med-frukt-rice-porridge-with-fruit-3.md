## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 parts Carolina rice grains (150 gr.)
- 1 liter water
- 1 1/2 liters fresh apples or 1 1/2 hectograms dried apples or 1 1/2 liters preserved gooseberries
- 1 1/2—2 parts crushed sugar
- 1—2 tablespoons punch (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, scald them in hot water, let them drain and place in boiling water.
2. Peel the apples, cut them into fine wedges and add together with the sugar when the porridge has cooked for about 15 minutes.
3. Let the porridge thereafter cook slowly until the grains and apples are completely soft, or about 1 1/2 hours, stirring it often so that it does not burn.
4. Taste the porridge and add more sugar if needed, and add the punch if desired.
5. Serve with cream or milk.
6. If using dried apples, rinse them and soak in 2 parts water for about 12 hours before cooking.
7. Instead of apples, preserved gooseberries may be used and the porridge is otherwise cooked in the same way.
8. Crushed grains may also be used for this porridge.
