## Ingredients

- 4 leaves gelatin
- 16 centiliters water
- 33 centiliters sweet juice (not orange or lemon)

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut into pieces and place in a saucepan together with the water and juice.
2. Let the mixture come to a boil while whisking and then whisk vigorously until it forms a stiff foam, or approximately 40 minutes.
3. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed form sprinkled with sugar and let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours.
4. When the blanc-mangé is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass dish with paper and serve.
