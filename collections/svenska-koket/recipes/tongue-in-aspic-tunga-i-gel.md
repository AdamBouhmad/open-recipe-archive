## Ingredients

- 1 salted, smoked ox tongue
- 3 liters water
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1/4 can green beans
- 1 1/2 liters good broth
- 12-15 sheets gelatin
- 3 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon cold water
- 1/2 deciliter good sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Let the tongue soak in water for approximately 12 hours, place it in cold water and let it cook slowly until tender, approximately 3 hours.
2. Remove it, immediately strip off the skin and let the tongue cool.
3. Cut it thereafter into 3 mm thick slices, which are trimmed so that they have a beautiful form and are of equal size. (The trimmed parts can be used for croquettes or braised dish.)
4. Lay the slices on large platters with small spacing between them.
5. Separate the egg white and yolk.
6. Cut the egg white into flowers and chop the yolk finely to form the center.
7. Cut the beans into stalks and leaves.
8. With these flowers and leaves, garnish the tongue slices beautifully and treat them then as in the preceding recipe.
9. Arrange them on a round platter and garnish with small rolls of cabbage leaf filled with vegetable salad and parsley, or with stuffed tomatoes and salad.
10. Serve as a course.
