## Ingredients

- 1½ kg beef tenderloin
- 1 hg beef marrow
- 1 Portuguese onion
- ½ tbsp salt
- ¼ tsk white pepper
- 5 tbsp butter
- 2 parts broth or water
- 1 bunch parsley
- juice of ½ lemon

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Remove the outer membranes from the tenderloin, and cut it into approximately 3 cm thick slices, which are lightly beaten.
3. Split the slices horizontally, however without separating the parts, only so that a small opening or pocket is formed.
4. The marrow, which should be prepared, is placed in the opening.
