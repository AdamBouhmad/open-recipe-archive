## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 dcl. Carolina rice grains (125 gr.)
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 4 oranges
- 1 1/2 dcl. sugar (128 gr.)
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- 5-6 leaves gelatin
- 3 tbsp. water
- 2 oranges in segments for garnishing

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, scald in hot water, add to boiling water and let cook until soft, or approximately 20 minutes.
2. Pour into a sieve and rinse with cold water, which should run off well.
3. Peel the oranges, remove the white membrane carefully, then cut into small cubes, remove the seeds and mix with the sugar.
4. Beat the cream to stiff peaks.
5. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut into pieces and dissolve in three tbsp. hot water.
6. Mix the rice grains with the orange cubes and cream, and finally add the gelatin.
7. Pour the mass into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let it stand on ice for approximately 3 hours, or until it has set.
8. Loosen the pudding around the edge with a pointed knife, turn out onto a flat glass dish with cake paper, garnish with orange segments and serve with orange sauce.
