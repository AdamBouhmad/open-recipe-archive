## Ingredients

- 300 g washed butter
- 150 g sugar
- 300 g rye sieve or coarse rye flour
- 300 g wheat flour

## Directions

1. Stir the butter for 10 minutes, then add the sugar and flour, which should be sifted (save 2 dcl of the wheat flour for rolling out). Roll out the dough to 3 mm thickness, prick well and cut out with a round mold into small cookies with a hole on one side. Lay the cookies on a buttered baking sheet and bake until beautifully golden brown in not too warm an oven.
