## Ingredients

- 125 gr. butter
- 2 1/2 dcl. water
- 210 gr. flour
- 4 eggs
- 5 dcl. firm potato mash
- salt
- sugar
- 1 kg. frying fat for cooking

## Directions

1. Butter and water are brought to a boil, flour is whisked in during full boiling and the batter is allowed to cook until it releases from the pan and has a yellow color.
2. It is poured out and allowed to cool, after which eggs are worked in one at a time and the batter is stirred for 10 minutes.
3. The potato mash is prepared as in recipe 677, allowed to cool and is added to the batter with vigorous stirring, after which the mass is seasoned with spices.
4. The mass is formed with two spoons, dipped in the frying fat, into round balls, which, if desired, can be filled with chopped truffle or peas, are cooked golden brown in hot frying fat and laid on blotting paper to drain.
5. Served as garnish around roasted meat. If the balls are not filled, they should be made very small.
