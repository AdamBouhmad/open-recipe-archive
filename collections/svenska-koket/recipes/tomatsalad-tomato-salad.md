## Ingredients

- 4-5 medium, firm tomatoes
- 2 tsk. chopped tarragon leaves
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- paprika
- 2-3 msk. fine cooking oil
- 1-1 1/2 msk. vinegar

## Directions

1. Dip the tomatoes for a moment in hot water, peel and cut in half crosswise.
2. Gently squeeze the tomato halves so that they are freed from juice and seeds.
3. Cut them into fine strips, place in a salad bowl and pour the sauce over, which is prepared as in recipe no. 448.
4. Sprinkle the salad with the chopped tarragon leaves and serve with finer meat dishes.
