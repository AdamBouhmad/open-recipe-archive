## Ingredients

- 8 parts heavy cream
- 100 gr. powdered sugar
- 2 vanilla pods
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Half of the cream, along with the vanilla and sugar, are brought to boil until the cream has sufficient vanilla flavor, after which the vanilla pod is removed.
2. The egg yolks are whisked in a bowl, the boiled cream is added with vigorous whisking, and the mixture is poured back into the saucepan.
3. It is then allowed to simmer over the heat until it thickens.
4. The saucepan is removed from heat, and whisking is continued until the cream is completely cold.
5. The remaining cream is whisked to stiff peaks and folded into the cooled mixture.
6. The ice is poured into a freezing mold and frozen, though not too hard.
