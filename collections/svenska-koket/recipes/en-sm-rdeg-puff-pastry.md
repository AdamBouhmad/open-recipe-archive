## Ingredients

- 150 grams flour
- 1 deciliter water (scant)
- 250 grams fine butter (equal weight to the dough)

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and mix with enough water to make a reasonably stiff dough, which is worked quickly, weighed and allowed to rest 20 minutes.
2. Wash the butter and squeeze out the water well, then pat it out into a round cake.
3. Roll out the dough into a round cake, somewhat thicker in the middle.
4. Place the butter in the middle of the dough and fold the edges of the dough over the butter so that it is completely covered, then let the dough rest 10 minutes.
5. Now lightly beat out the dough with a rolling pin into a square of approximately 1/2 cm thickness.
6. Fold this square together so that the edges of the dough meet in the middle of the square.
7. Then fold one half of the dough over the other, so the dough lies in four layers on top of each other.
8. Roll out the dough again into an oblong square.
9. Repeat this 4 times in succession, then let the dough rest 15 minutes.
10. Roll it out again 4 times and let it rest 15 minutes.
11. Proceed this way two more times with the dough, then let it rest 1 hour before baking it out into vol-au-vents, bouchées, pastries, puff pastry snails, tarts etc. — Puff pastry is baked in a warm oven.
