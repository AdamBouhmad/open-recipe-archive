## Ingredients

- ¾ kg cooked pike
- 75 gr butter
- 4 msk flour
- 2½ parts cream
- 2 parts fish stock
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 eggs for coating
- 2 cups crushed breadcrumbs for coating
- ¼ kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Free the cooked pike from skin and bones and cut into cubes.
2. Brown the butter and flour together, mix with the cream and fish stock and let it cook to a thick sauce; season with spices and bind with egg yolks.
3. Add the fish, and when the mixture is warm, spread it out on a dish to cool.
4. Form croquettes from the cooled mass, roll in crushed breadcrumbs, dip in beaten egg and roll again in crushed breadcrumbs.
5. Let the croquettes rest for 1 hour to harden and then fry golden brown in lard.
6. Place them on brown paper to drain and serve hot with mushroom sauce.
