## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 2 liters milk
- 1 deciliter semolina (76 g)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 g)
- 2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a pot; when melted, add the milk and bring to a boil.
2. Whisk in the semolina and let the porridge simmer slowly with a lid for about 3/4 hour, stirring often.
3. Season with spices and serve, if desired, with cinnamon.
