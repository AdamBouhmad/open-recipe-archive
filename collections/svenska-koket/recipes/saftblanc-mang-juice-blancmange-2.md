## Ingredients

- 4 sheets gelatin
- 16 cl. water
- 33 cl. sweet juice (not orange or lemon)

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut into pieces, and place in a saucepan with the water and juice.
2. Let the mixture come to a boil while whipping and then whip vigorously until it forms stiff peaks, or about 40 minutes.
3. Pour the mass into a mold rinsed with cold water and sprinkled with granulated sugar and let it set on ice for 2-3 hours.
4. When the blancmange is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a glass plate with parchment paper.
