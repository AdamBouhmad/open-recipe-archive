## Ingredients

- 150 gr. prune plums
- 5 dcl. water
- 50 gr. sugar
- 1 tsk. potato starch (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the plums in several changes of lukewarm water and wrap them in a cloth.
2. Crush half of them in a stone mortar so that the pits are broken.
3. Let the crushed and whole plums soak for several hours in the water in which they will be cooked.
4. Cook until completely soft, then press through a hair sieve.
5. Cook the fruit purée with the sugar and thicken as in no. 460.
6. Taste and season with a little lemon juice if desired.
7. Serve warm or cold. Sauce of dried cherries is prepared in the same way.
