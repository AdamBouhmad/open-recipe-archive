## Ingredients

- 1 liter Brussels sprouts
- 3/4 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 4-5 deciliters boiling stock
- 1-2 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the cabbage as in the previous recipe and let it drain on cloth.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the stock, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
