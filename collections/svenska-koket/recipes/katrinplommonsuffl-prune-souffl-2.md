## Ingredients

- 175 grams fine prunes
- 75 grams fine dried apricots
- 4 deciliters water
- 125 grams granulated sugar
- 5 egg whites
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the form

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes and apricots well in lukewarm water.
2. Place the prunes in the cold water and let them cook until half soft, then add the apricots and let the fruit cook until soft, but do not let it fall apart. Pour into a colander and let it drain.
3. Pit the prunes; crush half of the pits in a mortar and finely chop the kernels together with the fruit flesh and apricots in a chopping bowl.
4. Mix the fruit well with the sugar and carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks. Pour the mixture into a buttered pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 1/2 hour. Serve immediately from the form with whipped cream.
