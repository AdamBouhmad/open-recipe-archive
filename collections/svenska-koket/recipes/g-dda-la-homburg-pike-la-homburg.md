## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 2 tablespoons clarified butter (40 g)
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter for cooking (40 g)
- 1 ball crayfish butter (10 g)
- 3 decilitres thick cream

## Directions

1. Treat the pike as in the preceding recipe
2. Rub the fillets with salt and let them rest 1 hour
3. Dry them well with a fish towel
4. Brush both sides with the clarified butter mixed with the beaten egg yolk
5. Sprinkle with the crushed breadcrumbs and grated cheese
6. Butter a long pan with part of the cold butter, making a space large enough for the fillets
7. Lay the fillets in with skin side up and distribute the rest of the butter in small flakes
8. Mix the crayfish butter with the thick cream
9. Pour this mixture over the fish
10. Bake in a hot oven for about 20 minutes, basting often with the sauce
11. Transfer to a hot platter
12. If desired, whisk the pan sauce with 2 decilitres cream and pour over the fish
13. Garnish with parsley and serve with mashed potatoes
14. Can also be advantageously served with mushroom sauce, but then do not add cream to the pan sauce
