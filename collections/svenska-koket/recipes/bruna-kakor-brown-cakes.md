## Ingredients

- 225 gr. butter
- 210 gr. brown farinose sugar
- 1 hg. sweet almond
- 25 gr. bitter almond
- 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
- grated peel of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 tsp. hartshorn salt (1 1/2 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. milk
- 375 gr. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir it with the sugar until it is white and fluffy.
2. Add the scalded and ground almond, cinnamon, lemon peel and the hartshorn salt dissolved in the milk.
3. Lastly work in the flour, which should be sifted, and form the dough into small, round balls, which are dipped in egg white and granulated sugar, placed on a well buttered baking sheet and baked beautifully golden brown in a not too warm oven.
