## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. cod
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces and remove excess salt by soaking in water.
2. Remove it and scrape the skin until it becomes white.
3. Place the fish on the fire in cold water and let it simmer very slowly until it is cooked through.
4. Remove it immediately and serve with clarified butter and chopped eggs or with milk sauce.
