## Ingredients

- 6 hectograms fresh salmon
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon water
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 150 grams butter
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and dry the salmon piece with a cloth dipped in cold water
2. Cut into 1 1/2 centimeter thick slices
3. Flatten the slices with a knife blade
4. Rub with salt and pepper
5. Dip in the egg whisked with water
6. Turn in a mixture of flour and rusks
7. Fry to a beautiful golden brown in half of the butter
8. Serve as a breakfast dish with the remaining butter, browned and flavored with lemon juice
