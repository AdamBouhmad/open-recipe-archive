## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 4 dcl. water
- approximately 135 gr. flour
- 2 dcl. melted butter (2 hg.)
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 2 tbsp. butter for the pan (40 gr.)
- 2 cups jam (raspberry or strawberry)
- 3 egg whites
- 150 gr. powdered sugar or 3 dcl. thick cream and 1/2 tbsp. powdered sugar (8 gr.)
- 50 gr. sweet almond

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks with a steel whisk, pour in the water, then whisk in the flour, which should be sifted and well dried.
2. Add the butter and sugar, and lastly stir in the cream that has been whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Heat a pancake pan slowly and butter only the first pancake.
4. Make thin pancakes from the batter, which are fried on both sides, folded double and laid on a warm plate.
5. Serve with jam mixed with whipped cream, if desired.
