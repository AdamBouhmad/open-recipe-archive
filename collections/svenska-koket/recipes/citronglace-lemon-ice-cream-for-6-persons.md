## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter water
- 150 gr. sugar
- 2 lemons
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Dry the lemons, rub the yellow peel against the sugar, and squeeze out the juice.
2. Mix the water, lemon juice and sugar well, then strain it.
3. Mix in the egg white and pour everything into an ice cream machine to freeze.
