## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless veal
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 2 hg. of the meat for forcemeat
- 50 gr. lard
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.)
- 1 msk. flour
- salt and white pepper for forcemeat
- 1 tsk. finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 msk. finely chopped mushrooms or truffle
- 1 1/2–2 dcl. thick cream for forcemeat
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.) for frying
- 1 msk. flour (10 gr.)
- 3 dcl. broth
- 2 dcl. thick cream for cooking

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat (see page 232) and cut across into 12 even slices, which are lightly flattened.
2. Cut the lard into strips, which are dipped in a mixture of salt and pepper, and therewith lard the meat slices.
3. Roll them up and bind them in two places with fine twine dipped in boiling water.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the roulades in and brown them beautifully, sprinkle with the remainder of the spice mixture, dilute with boiling broth and thick cream.
