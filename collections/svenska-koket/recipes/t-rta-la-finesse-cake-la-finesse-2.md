## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 250 gr. fine butter (equal weight with the dough)
- 1 dcl. water (scarcely)
- 1/2 batch petits-choux (recipe no. 999)
- candied orange slices (recipe no. 1027)
- 1/2 batch vanilla cream with gelatin (recipe no. 872)
- 6 dcl. extra thick cream
- carmine, vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the fine butter dough and roll out to 2 bottoms.
2. Allow the bottoms to cool.
3. Place the vanilla cream between the bottoms.
4. Garnish the cake with petit-choux, candied orange slices, and whipped cream.
5. Decorate with carmine and vanilla sugar as desired.
