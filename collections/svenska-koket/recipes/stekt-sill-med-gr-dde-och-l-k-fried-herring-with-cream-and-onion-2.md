## Ingredients

- 2 large salted herrings
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 4 decilitres thick cream
- 1 large Portuguese onion for garnish
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter for garnish (30 g)

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in plenty of water for 24 hours, changing the water 2 times.
2. Remove the skin, fillet the herring, remove the backbone and all small bones, and wrap in a fish towel.
3. Coat the fillets in a mixture of flour and crushed rusks.
4. Fry until beautifully golden brown on both sides and place on a warm serving dish.
5. Whisk the pan with cream and bring the sauce to a boil.
6. Peel the onion, cut into thin slices and brown well in butter using a separate pan.
7. Place the herring on a warm serving dish, pour the sauce over, and garnish with the browned onion and parsley.
8. Serve as breakfast with freshly boiled unpeeled potatoes.
