## Ingredients

- 10 liters lingonberries
- 5 liters water
- 50 gr. tartaric acid
- 1 deciliter warm water
- 2.25 kg. granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, crush them with a wooden pestle and place them in a pot.
2. Dissolve the tartaric acid in the warm water and add it together with the cold water.
3. Stir the mass well, cover it and let it stand for 2 days, stirring it from time to time.
4. Strain the berries in cheesecloth and let them drip as long as any juice drops from the cloth, or approximately 1.5 days.
5. Add the sugar to the juice and stir it slowly for 1 hour.
6. Treat the juice as in the preceding recipe.
7. When serving, dilute with water to taste.
8. It is a good and healthy drink, especially suitable for meals.
9. From the remaining pulp, by adding 1.5 liters water, 15 gr. citric acid and 6 hectograms sugar, a simpler juice can be made, which is used first.
