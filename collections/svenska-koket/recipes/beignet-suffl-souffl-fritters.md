## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 dcl. milk
- 60 gr. butter
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 1/2 msk. sugar (8 gr.)
- 75 gr. flour
- 5 whole eggs
- 1 kg. lard for frying

## Directions

1. Heat the butter, milk, and seasonings, then add the flour with vigorous whisking, and let the batter cook until it leaves the pan and has a yellow color.
2. Remove the saucepan from heat and work in the eggs one at a time, and stir the batter for 5 minutes.
3. Form the batter with two teaspoons, dipped in the lard, into round balls about the size of a walnut, and cook them a beautiful light golden color in moderately hot lard and place them on absorbent paper to drain, then sprinkle with sugar.
4. Serve very hot, placed on pastry paper, with chocolate sauce, jam, or compote.
