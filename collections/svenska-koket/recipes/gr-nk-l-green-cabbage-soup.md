## Ingredients

- 2 medium heads green or blue cabbage
- 3 liters water for pre-boiling
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 liters meat broth
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon ground fennel
- cabbage stalks cooked in cream sauce
- fish forcemeat quenelles

## Directions

1. The cabbage, which should be frozen, is placed in water to remove the ice
2. It is grated and pre-boiled for 15 minutes as in recipe no. 257
3. The cabbage is placed on a sieve, rinsed with cold water and left to drain well
4. It is wrung in a cloth, chopped finely, passed through a sieve and cooked in broth for 1 hour
5. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes, the soup is thinned and allowed to cook with stirring for 5 minutes
6. It is seasoned with spices and served with cabbage stalks cooked in cream sauce and fish forcemeat quenelles
7. The thickest cabbage stalks are pre-boiled, peeled, wrapped in a cloth, cut into 5 cm long pieces, which are rolled in flour, dipped in beaten egg and then in crushed breadcrumbs and cooked golden brown in hot cream sauce
8. They are placed in the soup bowl together with the quenelles, the soup is poured in and served immediately
9. Preserved green cabbage can be used advantageously
