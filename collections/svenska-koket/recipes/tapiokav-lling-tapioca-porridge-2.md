## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup tapioca grits
- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters milk
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the tapioca grits.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, add the milk and bring to a boil.
