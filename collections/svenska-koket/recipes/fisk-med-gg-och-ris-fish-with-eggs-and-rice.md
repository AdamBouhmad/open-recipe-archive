## Ingredients

- 1.5 dcl. rice grains (150 grams)
- 1.5 liters water
- 1 tbsp. salt
- Leftovers of cooked fish, approximately 4 dcl.
- 3 eggs
- salt, white pepper
- 1.5 hg. clarified butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, place in boiling salted water and let them boil rapidly until soft, approximately 15 minutes.
2. Immediately pour into a strainer, rinse with cold water and place in a warm oven.
3. Stir often with a fork until they are separated and all water has evaporated.
4. Hard boil the eggs, let them cool and chop finely.
5. Grate the fish with a fork as fine as the rice grains.
6. Mix eggs and fish well with the grains and season with spices.
7. Serve with clarified butter as a breakfast dish.
