## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- juice of 1/8 lemon
- 200 grams granulated sugar
- 100 grams sweet almond
- 10 pistachio almonds for garnish

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds and cut into long strips, which are dried very well
2. Stir the egg whites, sugar and lemon juice slowly together with a wooden spoon in one direction for 1 hour
3. Then carefully stir in the almonds
4. Place the batter with spoons onto well-buttered or waxed baking sheets in pointed cakes, which are sprinkled with the scalded and finely chopped pistachio almonds
5. Bake the cakes in very weak oven heat
