## Ingredients

- 6 kg cherries
- 3 kg granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the cherries but do not remove the pits, rinse and let them drain.
2. Layer them alternately with the sugar in a stone crock.
3. Place a plate on top and seal it with a dough made from coarse rye flour and water so that it becomes completely airtight.
4. Place the crock in a water bath and let it cook for 6 hours, counted from the moment the water boils.
5. Strain the juice immediately and bottle it warm or cold, cork the bottles and seal with resin.
6. The berries can be dried and advantageously used for soups.
