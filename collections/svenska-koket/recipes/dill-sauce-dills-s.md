## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 5 decilitres meat stock
- 1-2 tablespoons finely chopped dill
- 1-2 tablespoons vinegar
- 2-3 teaspoons sugar (10-15 grams)
- salt
- 1-2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Fry one tablespoon of the butter with the flour for 2 minutes, add the stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Add the chopped dill, season the sauce with vinegar and spices.
3. Add the whisked egg yolk and let the sauce simmer under vigorous whisking but not boil, as it breaks easily.
4. Finish the sauce with the rest of the cold butter.
