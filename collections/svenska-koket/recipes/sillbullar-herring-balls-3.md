## Ingredients

- 2 salt herrings
- 1/2 liter cooked, cold potatoes
- 1.5 cups cooked, chopped meat
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter or lard for frying

## Directions

1. Soak the herrings the day before; clean them in the usual way and remove the skin.
2. Chop them finely together with the potatoes; mix in the meat, milk and pepper.
3. Form balls from the mixture, roll them in crushed rusks and fry golden brown in butter or lard.
4. Serve with currant sauce.
