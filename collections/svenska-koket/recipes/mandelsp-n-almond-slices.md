## Ingredients

- 2 sheets wafer (at 5 öre each)
- 175 gr. sweet almond
- 2 hg. fine sugar
- 2 egg whites
- Glaze: 1 large egg white
- 125 gr. fine sugar
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- For garnishing: 30 gr. sweet almond
- 15 gr. pistachios
- 50 gr. preserved orange peel
- 50 gr. candied fruit

## Directions

1. Each wafer sheet is divided into twelve equal oblong rectangles.
2. The almond is blanched, dried in the oven, driven through an almond mill.
