## Ingredients

- 6 two-öre almond bread
- 1 1/2 hg. melted butter
- 1 hg. coarsely crushed lump sugar
- 150 gr. sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 1 1/2 lit. good, tart apples
- 1 msk. lemon juice or vinegar
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Butter a copper or tin mold with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
2. Split the breads lengthwise, dip in the melted butter and turn in the sugar.
3. Line the mold with bread slices, fill with apple compote, and lay the remaining bread slices over as a lid.
4. Bake the pudding in ordinary oven heat for about 1/2 hour, turn out and serve warm with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
5. The apple compote is prepared as in recipe 820. If desired, the compote can be mixed with some jam.
