## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1/4 root celery
- 1 leek
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 1/2 liter shelled peas
- 2 small cauliflower heads
- 1 1/2 - 2 liters good milk
- 3 tablespoons flour
- salt
- sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 parts cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the carrots, scrape them, and cut into thin slices; peel and prepare the celery and leek and cut into fine strips.
2. Brown the vegetables and leek in the butter, thin with a little boiling water and cook tender in a short cooking liquid.
3. Clean the cauliflower and soak it for a while in water mixed with vinegar, then place it together with the peas to cook in a little salted water.
4. Boil the milk and thicken it with the flour mixed with a little cold milk, then let the soup simmer 10 minutes.
5. Add the finely chopped parsley together with the cooked vegetables and greens with their cooking liquid, and taste and adjust seasoning.
6. Whip the egg yolks and cream in the soup bowl and pour in the soup while whisking vigorously.
7. If using canned peas, heat them in their can and add last to the soup.
