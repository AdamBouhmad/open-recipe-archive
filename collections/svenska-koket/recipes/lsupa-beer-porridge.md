## Ingredients

- 5 tbsp. flour (50 gr.)
- 1 1/4 lit. milk
- 1/2 lit. good weak beer
- 2 tbsp. syrup
- 1/2 tbsp. sugar (8 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. ginger
- 1 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- 1–2 egg yolks
- 2 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Stir the flour with 1/4 lit. of the milk and let it swell for about 2 hours.
2. Bring the remaining milk to a boil, add the flour mixture, and let the porridge boil while whisking for 8–10 minutes.
3. Boil the beer with the syrup and spices and pour into the porridge while whisking steadily.
4. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in the soup bowl and stir the soup into them under vigorous whisking.
5. Serve at once with fried bread cubes.
6. In the simplest version, omit the egg yolks and cream.
