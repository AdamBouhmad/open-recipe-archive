## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg Baltic herring
- 2 tbsp salt
- 0.5 tbsp spiced pepper
- 2 bay leaves
- some dill stalks or whole dill
- 2 cups water

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the herring well.
2. Lay it in a pot, alternating with spices and dill stalks; add water and vinegar, and place the pot over heat.
3. When the broth boils up, skim it carefully, and let the herring simmer slowly until done.
4. Lay it up, pour the broth over it, and garnish the dish with dill.
