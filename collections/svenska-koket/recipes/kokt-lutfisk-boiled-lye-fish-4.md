## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 kg. lutfisk
- 1/2 lit. water
- 5 msk. salt (75 gr.)
- ground pepper
- milk sauce
- clarified butter
- Russian peas or green peas
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Well-drained fish is cut into large pieces and the meat is cut from the skin.
2. Scrape the skin well, dry it and use for clear skin.
3. Layer the fish alternately with salt in a fish towel, then tie it together.
4. In a round pot, pour the water, place a saucer or plate upside down.
5. Bring the water to a boil, place the bundle with the fish on the plate and let the fish simmer slowly for 15-20 minutes, or until it becomes suitably tender.
6. Place the bundle in a colander, let the fish drain, transfer to a serving dish and serve with ground pepper, milk sauce and clarified butter as well as Russian or green peas and potatoes.
