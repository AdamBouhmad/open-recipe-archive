## Ingredients

- 2-3 eggs
- 1 liter milk
- approximately 160-180 gr. flour
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- For the pan: 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)

## Directions

1. Prepare the pancake batter as in recipe no. 763 and let it stand to rise for 2 hours.
2. Heat a frying pan slowly; melt the butter in a water bath and grease the pan with it.
3. Stir the pancake batter well, and pour one third of the batter into the pan.
4. Fry the pancake in a warm oven until it has taken on a beautiful golden-brown color.
5. Place the pancakes on a warm dish, cut into four pieces and serve with jam.
