## Ingredients

- Carl Johanssvampar (porcini mushrooms)
- Butter
- Onion
- Parsley
- Bouillon
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms (on older mushrooms, remove the pores)
2. Rinse well and drain on a cloth
3. Cut into strips or pieces
4. Stew as mushrooms, or stew in butter, onion, parsley and bouillon and finish with egg yolks
