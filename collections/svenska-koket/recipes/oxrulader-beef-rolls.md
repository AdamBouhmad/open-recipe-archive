## Ingredients

- 1⅛ kg boneless beef sirloin
- 1½ hg lard
- ½ tbsp salt
- ⅙ tsk white pepper
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into thin slices, which are beaten well.
2. On each of them is placed a thin, well-seasoned lard slice, and the rolls are rolled up and bound tightly in two places.
3. They are browned in the butter, and flour is sprinkled over.
4. The rolls are thereafter diluted with the boiling liquid and are allowed to slowly roast with a tightly closed lid, until they feel tender when tested with a whisk.
5. The strings are removed, the rolls are laid up high on carrot, and the sauce is poured over.
