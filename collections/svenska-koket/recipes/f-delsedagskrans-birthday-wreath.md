## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. cold milk
- 3 eggs
- prime wheat flour
- 50 gr. yeast
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 2 hg. melted butter
- 3-4 cardamom pods or 2 tbsp. finely chopped candied orange peel
- 1 egg for brushing
- 15 gr. sweet almonds
- 50 gr. sanding sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough at 7 p.m. the evening before baking.
2. Whisk the milk with the eggs and mix into 425 gr. sifted wheat flour.
3. Add the yeast, dissolved with the sugar, the butter, and the finely crushed cardamom or orange peel, along with enough flour to make the dough quite stiff.
4. Work the dough until smooth and glossy, immediately wrap in a tight cloth that is tied securely, then place the bundle in a bucket of cold water.
5. Early the next morning, the bundle should have turned in the water; remove it at once.
6. Turn the dough onto a floured work surface and shape quickly, using as little flour as possible, into a wreath, which is placed on a well-buttered baking sheet.
7. Brush the wreath with beaten egg, sprinkle with coarsely chopped almonds, and let it rise quickly, preferably over steam.
8. Sprinkle generously with sugar and bake in a hot oven.
9. Instead of orange peel, 50 gr. finely cut candied citrus peel can be used.
