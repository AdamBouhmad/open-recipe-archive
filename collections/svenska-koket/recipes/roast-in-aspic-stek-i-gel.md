## Ingredients

- 6 hectograms leftover beef or veal roast
- 1 cooked carrot
- 1/4 can green beans
- 3/4 liter good broth
- 8 sheets gelatin
- 2 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon good Madeira
- 2 heads of lettuce
- 2 tomatoes

## Directions

1. The roast, which should be juicy and good, is cut into beautiful, even slices, which are laid on a large platter at even intervals.
2. The carrot is cut into slices and these are cut out into flowers with a vegetable cutter.
3. The beans are cut out into stalks and leaves.
4. With these flowers and leaves, garnish the meat slices.
5. Prepare the gelatin as in no. 181, and when it is cold, add the wine.
6. A little of the clear gelatin is poured onto the platter but should only just reach the garnish, otherwise it will flow around it.
7. When the first gelatin has set, pour on the remainder, which should cover the meat slices.
8. Place the platter in a cold place and after 2-3 hours, cut out the slices so that a border of gelatin extends beyond the slice.
9. Arrange a round platter with salad leaves and tomato wedges and lay the slices in a ring on the platter so that the flowers show.
10. Serve as a course with vegetable or potato salad.
