## Ingredients

- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
- 1/2 to 1 tablespoon powdered ginger
- 1 1/2 to 2 tablespoons cream
- 2 tablespoons clarified butter
- 1 1/2 hectograms wheat flour
- 1/2 kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Work all ingredients together into a loose dough
2. Set out to rest on a cold place at least 2 hours
3. Roll out the dough to approximately 1/2 cm thickness and cut out into klenäter shapes
4. Cook in lard
5. Sprinkle with sugar and serve with jam
