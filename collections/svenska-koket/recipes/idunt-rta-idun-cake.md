## Ingredients

- 212 gr. melted butter
- 75 gr. powdered sugar
- 25 gr. sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 1 small egg
- 210 gr. wheat flour
- 105 gr. potato flour
- 1/2 batch vanilla cream (recipe no. 871)
- 5 egg whites
- 175 gr. powdered sugar
- 50 gr. sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Let the butter cool and stir until it is white and fluffy.
2. Add the sugar, the blanched and ground almond, and both types of flour.
3. Bake the dough in a buttered ring form to make 2 bottoms, which are allowed to cool.
4. Place the vanilla cream between the bottoms.
5. Garnish the cake with the meringue mass and top with the blanched almonds cut into strips.
6. Let the meringue dry in a gentle oven heat.
7. Serve as a dessert or with coffee.
