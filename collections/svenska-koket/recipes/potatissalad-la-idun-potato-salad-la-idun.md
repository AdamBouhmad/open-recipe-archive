## Ingredients

- 1/2 litre cooked, cold potatoes
- 2 cooked beetroots
- 1 head of lettuce
- 2 tablespoons capers
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon English mustard
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1-2 tablespoons vinegar or vinegar
- 1 tablespoon caper vinegar
- 3 tablespoons fine oil
- 1 decilitre thick cream

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin slices.
2. Cut the beetroots into strips 1½ cm. long.
3. Clean the lettuce, rinse it, wrap it in a cloth and cut it into long, fine strips.
4. Mix all of this in a salad bowl.
5. Stir the egg yolks in a bowl with salt, mustard and sugar, whereupon the vinegar and oil are added.
6. Last, stir in the cream, and pour the sauce over the salad, which is carefully mixed and served immediately with cold meat dishes in general.
