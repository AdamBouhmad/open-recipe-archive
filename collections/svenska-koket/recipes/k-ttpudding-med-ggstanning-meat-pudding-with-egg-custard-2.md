## Ingredients

- 400-500 g boneless cooked or fried meat
- 1 small red onion
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 deciliters gravy or broth
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoon wheat flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 4 deciliters milk
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Chop the meat fairly finely. Peel and finely chop the onion and brown it in the butter, add the meat and brown it well, sprinkle with the seasonings and mix with the gravy or broth.
2. For the egg custard, whisk the egg yolks with the flour, a little salt and sugar, and the milk, then fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites.
3. Place the meat in a buttered and breadcrumb-coated pie mold, pour the egg custard over it and sprinkle with parsley.
4. Bake the pudding in the oven until it has a beautiful golden-brown color, about 1/2 hour. Serve with boiled potatoes and melted butter.
