## Ingredients

- 1 venison liver
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 red onions, in slices
- 4 dcl. meat stock or broth

## Directions

1. The liver should soak in water for a couple of hours to draw out the blood.
2. Wrap it in a cloth, remove the membrane and cut the liver into thin slices, which are sprinkled with salt and pepper and turned in 2 tbsp. of the flour.
3. Brown three tbsp. of the butter in a frying pan, place the liver slices in, fry quickly on both sides and place on a warm dish.
4. Brown the remaining butter with the onion in the frying pan, mix 1 tbsp. of the flour well with the butter and onion, add the broth and let the sauce boil 5 minutes, then taste and pour over the liver.
5. Serve with boiled potatoes.
