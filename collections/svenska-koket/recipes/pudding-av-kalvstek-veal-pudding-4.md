## Ingredients

- 4 hg. cooked, boneless veal or chicken meat
- 1 hg. raw, boneless veal meat
- 2 hg. bread panade
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 4 eggs
- salt, white pepper
- 1 msk. finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the onion
- 5 dcl. cream
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked meat finely; grind the raw meat 3 times through a meat grinder and mix with the cooked meat.
2. Lightly brown the panade in 1 msk. of the butter, place in a bowl and mix in the cold butter immediately.
3. Stir in the egg yolks, one at a time, along with the spices and the finely chopped onion fried in butter.
4. Mix the meat well with the panade and add enough cream so that the forcemeat becomes like thin porridge.
5. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
7. Serve with leftover roasting sauce, mushroom or morel sauce, or in the simplest case with clarified butter.
