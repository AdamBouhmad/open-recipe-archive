## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 7 decilitres good milk
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 g)
- 1/2 g white pepper
- 10 g cold butter

## Directions

1. Melt butter and flour together for 2 minutes
2. Add milk and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
3. Season with salt and white pepper, then stir in the cold butter, after which it must not boil
