## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized lettuce heads
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. The lettuce heads are separated, the stems removed, the leaves rinsed well and shaken in a cloth to become dry.
2. The largest leaves are cut into pieces, and the salad is placed in a crystal bowl.
3. The lemon juice is pressed out, mixed with the sugar and poured over the salad.
4. Served with roasted meat.
