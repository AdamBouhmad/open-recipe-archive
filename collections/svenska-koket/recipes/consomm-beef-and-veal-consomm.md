## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 kg. beef top round with bone
- 1 kg. veal neck
- 1 old hen
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 6 liters water
- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 1/4 celeriac
- 1 small red onion with 1 clove
- 10 gr. salt
- 1/2 kg. lean, boneless beef for clarification
- 2 egg whites and the rinsed eggshells for clarification

## Directions

1. Rinse the meat quickly and dry with a cloth.
2. Cut the meat from the bones into small pieces and crack the bones well.
3. Pluck the hen, singe it, remove it, rinse it and let it soak in water for about 6 hours.
4. Place the bones in cold water, bring to a rapid boil and skim well.
5. Add the prepared vegetables and the onion with the clove inserted into it, and let everything cook for 6 hours with a tightly closed lid.
6. Strain the broth through a fine sieve, let it cool and pour it back into the cleaned broth pot.
7. Dry the hen well with a cloth and brown it in butter until it has taken on a nice golden-brown color, then add it to the broth together with the ground meat and salt.
8. Let the consommé cook slowly for 4 hours, strain it and let it stand until the following day, when it is clarified as in recipe no. 223.
9. Measure it, bring to a boil, taste and adjust seasoning, and serve as clear broth.
