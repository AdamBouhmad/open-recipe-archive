## Ingredients

- 130 grams apples
- 130 grams currants
- 130 grams raisins
- 60 grams candied fruit
- 130 grams beef kidney suet
- 1 tablespoon wheat flour
- 130 grams crushed rusks
- 130 grams sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 4 eggs
- 1 part brandy
- 1-1.5 cups water
- 1 tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the cores, and chop them finely.
2. Clean and wash the currants, rinse the raisins, remove the pits, and chop them, and cut the candied fruit into thin slices.
3. Remove the membranes from the kidney suet and chop it finely together with the wheat flour.
4. Mix all the dry ingredients together well.
