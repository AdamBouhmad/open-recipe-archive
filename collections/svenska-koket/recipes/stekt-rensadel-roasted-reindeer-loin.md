## Ingredients

- 2.5 kg reindeer loin
- 4 liters drinking alcohol
- 1 kg lard
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 0.75-1 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Place the meat in drinking alcohol overnight, then remove, rinse well and dry with a cloth.
2. Remove all sinews carefully, and lard the reindeer loin in tight rows with lard cut into fine strips.
3. Heat the butter in a roasting pan and brown the roast well; then salt it and add the broth little by little, baste often and turn a couple of times during roasting.
4. Roasting takes 2-2.5 hours.
5. Remove the reindeer, cut into thin slices and garnish with vegetables.
6. Strain and skim the meat juices, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
