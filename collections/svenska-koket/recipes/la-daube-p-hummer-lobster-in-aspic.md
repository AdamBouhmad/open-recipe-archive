## Ingredients

- 3 fresh lobsters (or 1 can of preserved lobster of about 0.5 liter)
- 2.5 liters fish stock
- 2 tablespoons white French wine
- 10 sheets gelatin
- 2 tablespoons water
- 2 egg whites
- parsley for garnish
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Place the fish stock on the heat together with the wine and the gelatin dissolved in a little hot water.
2. When the mixture is warm, whisk in the egg whites beaten to soft peaks, and let the aspic come to a brief boil while whisking.
3. Strain the stock through a cloth or cheesecloth wrung out in hot water.
4. Pour the clear aspic onto the bottom of a rinsed à-la-daube mold and let set.
5. Garnish the mold with hard-boiled eggs and parsley.
6. Arrange the most beautiful pieces of lobster on the bottom and sides, and fill the mold with the remaining lobster meat.
7. Carefully pour the remaining aspic over and let the à-la-daube set in a cold place.
