## Ingredients

- 6 large canned artichoke bottoms
- 2 medium-sized Portuguese onions
- 3 tablespoon butter (60 grams)
- 2 tablespoon crushed crackers
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese

## Directions

1. Place the bottoms on a buttered ovenproof dish or a silver platter.
2. Peel the onion, chop it finely, and fry it in 2 tablespoons of the butter until it is golden brown.
3. Remove it and let it cool in the butter.
4. Fill the mixture into the bottoms and sprinkle with crushed crackers and a little grated cheese.
5. Melt the remaining butter and pour it over.
6. Place the platter in a roasting pan with coarse salt and gratinée in a warm oven for 10-15 minutes.
7. Serve very hot.
