## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 5 decilitres milk, broth or water
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs well with the spices, and the liquid, which should be boiling, is added with vigorous whisking.
2. Grease a pan with butter, pour in the egg mixture and cook in a water bath with stirring.
3. Serve with a flat spoon on a warm plate, garnish with parsley and serve with smoked salmon and herring, pickled ham, sausage, smoked pork tenderloin etc.
