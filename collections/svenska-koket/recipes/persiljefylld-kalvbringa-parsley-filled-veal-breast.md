## Ingredients

- 2 kg. veal breast
- 3 large bunches parsley
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 3 1/2 msk. butter (70 gr.)
- 3 msk. butter for roasting (60 gr.)
- 3/4 lit. broth

## Directions

1. Use the front part of the breast or the so-called breast and do not break the bones.
2. Bone the breast carefully and cook broth on the bones to dilute the roast with.
3. Prepare the meat and rub with the seasonings.
4. Rinse the parsley, wrap in a cloth, clean and chop coarsely and work into the butter.
5. Spread the parsley butter on the inside of the breast, then roll it up and sew it securely, then brown it in butter, sprinkle with the remaining seasonings, and let it roast in a pot or oven for about 3 hours.
6. During roasting baste the meat every 10 minutes and dilute with the boiling broth.
7. When the roast is tender, remove all threads, then cut into even slices, which are laid together so that the roast looks whole.
8. Place on a warm platter, garnish with salad or parsley, serve with vegetables or boiled potatoes, salads and strained sauce with cream added (see recipe 429).
9. If desired, the meat can be diluted with some cream and some broth.
