## Ingredients

- 2 liters good broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 12 sugar beets
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scrape and cut the sugar beets into 5–7 cm long pieces
2. Blanch them for a few minutes in boiling water
3. Cook them until done in the strained and skimmed broth
4. Whisk the egg yolks in a soup bowl
5. When the beets are cooked, pour the soup into the bowl with the egg yolks while whisking vigorously
6. Serve with fried bread
