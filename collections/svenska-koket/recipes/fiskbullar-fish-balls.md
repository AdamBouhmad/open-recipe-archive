## Ingredients

- 3 cups cooked fish (leftovers)
- 2 cups mashed potatoes
- 1 egg
- ⅛ tsp white pepper
- salt
- 2 eggs for coating
- 1½ cups crushed breadcrumbs for coating
- ¾ kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Free the cooked fish from skin and bones and chop finely.
2. Mix with the mashed potatoes, the beaten egg and the spices. The mixture should be done with a fork and not with a spoon, so that the forcemeat does not pack.
3. Form croquettes or small round balls from the forcemeat; these are rolled in crushed breadcrumbs, dipped in egg and rolled again in crushed breadcrumbs, then allowed to dry for ½ hour.
4. Heat the lard, fry the fish balls golden brown in it and place them on brown paper to drain.
5. Garnish with parsley and serve with mushroom or ordinary fish sauce.
