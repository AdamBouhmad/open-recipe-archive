## Ingredients

- Chicken forcemeat: 1 chicken, 100 g. butter, 2 eggs, 1 portion cream, panade made with twelve-öre wheat bread and 2 portions boiling milk, salt, white pepper, 0.175 glas truffle at kr. 1.50 in weight equal to the forcemeat
- Game bird forcemeat: 3 grouse, 100 g. butter, 2 eggs, 1 portion cream, panade made with twelve-öre wheat bread and 2 portions boiling milk, salt, white pepper, 0.05 glas truffle at kr. 1.50 in half the weight of the forcemeat
- Sauce: 1 tablespoon butter, 2 tablespoons flour, game bird stock, 1 portion thick cream, 0.125 glas truffle at kr. 1.50

## Directions

1. For chicken forcemeat: Pluck the chicken, clean it and rinse well
2. Remove the meat from the bones (boil stock on the carcass), cut it into cubes and pass three times through the meat grinder
3. Make panade as in the previous recipe and pass it through a sieve
4. Pound the meat and butter well together in a mortar and pass it through a hair sieve
5. Gradually mix the whisked egg yolks, cream and seasonings into the forcemeat, which is worked for 1 hour
6. Then add the finely cut truffle and fold in the egg whites beaten until stiff
7. If the forcemeat is too stiff, add a little cream; if too soft, add an egg yolk
8. Prepare game bird forcemeat in the same manner
9. Grease a nice pipe-shaped or à-la-daube form with butter and dust with breadcrumbs
10. Layer alternately the light and dark forcemeats in the form
