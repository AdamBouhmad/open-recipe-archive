## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and flour together, gradually add the broth while stirring constantly and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes, then skim and season.
2. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl and pour the broth over while whisking vigorously.
3. Serve with meatballs, quenelles or similar.
