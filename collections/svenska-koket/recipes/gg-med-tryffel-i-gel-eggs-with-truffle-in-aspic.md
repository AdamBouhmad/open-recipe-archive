## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter calf broth
- 5 leaves gelatin
- 1 tsp vinegar
- 2 egg whites and rinsed shells
- 1 tbsp cold water
- 1 large truffle
- 2 tbsp preserved peas
- 3 poached eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare the aspic as in recipe no. 10. Pour a little of the clear aspic on the bottom of a rinsed, oblong, smooth timbale mold and let set.
2. Cut the truffle into 3 even slices and arrange them lengthwise in the bottom of the mold with 5 cm spacing. Around each truffle slice place a ring of preserved peas and above these lay the poached eggs. Pour the remaining aspic into the mold and place on ice.
3. When ready to serve, loosen the aspic at the edges and turn the dish out onto an oblong platter. Garnish with parsley or salad and serve at the smörgåsbord.
