## Ingredients

- 1 liter leftover pea soup
- 1 1/2 liters broth
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- salt
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Bring the pea soup to a boil and strain, then add the broth.
2. Fry butter and flour together and mix with the pea purée, which then cooks for about 10 minutes.
3. Scrape the Jerusalem artichokes and cook until soft in lightly salted water and add with their liquid.
4. Taste and adjust seasoning.
5. Whisk the egg yolk in a soup bowl and add the soup with whisking.
