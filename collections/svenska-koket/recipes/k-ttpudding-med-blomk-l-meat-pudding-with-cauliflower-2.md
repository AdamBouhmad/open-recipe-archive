## Ingredients

- Meat forcemeat as in recipe n:o 632
- 1 large cauliflower head
- 1 liter water
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 3 msk butter (30 gr)
- 4 msk flour (40 gr)
- 3–4 dcl cauliflower cooking liquid
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1/2 msk butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 msk breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat forcemeat as in recipe n:o 632.
2. Trim the cauliflower and let it soak in vinegar-mixed water 1 hour.
3. Place it in boiling water with salt and let it cook until tender but not falling apart.
4. Divide the cauliflower head into pieces and reserve the cooking liquid.
5. Fry butter and flour 2 minutes, gradually add the cooking liquid and let the sauce cook 5 minutes.
6. Add the cauliflower, season the stew with spices and let it cool.
7. Butter a pudding mold with cold butter and sprinkle with breadcrumbs.
8. Layer the meat forcemeat and stew alternately in the mold; the first and last layer should be forcemeat.
9. Cook the pudding in a water bath in the oven or on the stovetop 1 1/2–2 hours.
10. Turn out, garnish with a wreath of cooked cauliflower and serve with meat gravy (see recipe n:o 429) or with clarified butter.
11. Brussels sprouts may be used instead of cauliflower.
12. The pudding may also be baked in a fireproof mold in the oven and will then be ready in about 1 hour.
