## Ingredients

- 2 liters milk
- 4-6 teaspoons medium-strength cocoa powder
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the milk, whisk in the cocoa, bring the chocolate to a boil and season with sugar.
2. Instead of cocoa powder, sweetened or unsweetened chocolate can be used, which should then be broken into smaller pieces and whisked into the milk.
