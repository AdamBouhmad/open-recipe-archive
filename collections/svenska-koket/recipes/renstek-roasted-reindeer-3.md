## Ingredients

- 3 kg of leg
- 3 liters skim milk or drinking liquid
- 3 hg lard
- 1 msk salt (15 gr.)
- 4 msk butter (80 gr.)
- 1/2 liter broth
- 1/2 liter cream or good milk

## Directions

1. If the meat is frozen, let it thaw in milk or drinking liquid until just thawed. If unfrozen, wash it very carefully with a cloth dipped in hot water and beat with a damp meat mallet.
2. Dry the meat well and remove all membranes, then lard it and rub with the remaining salt, and bind it so that it takes on a beautiful shape.
3. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, add the meat and brown well on all sides, then dilute first with the boiling broth, then with the boiling cream or milk, and let it roast with a tightly closed lid until tender, about 3 1/2 hours, turning it a few times and basting every 10 minutes.
4. Cut the roast into thin slices, arrange on a warm platter and pour over a couple of msk. sauce.
5. Garnish with browned potatoes, salad or parsley and serve with reduced sauce (see no. 429) mixed with thick cream, along with green salad and sour salads or lingonberry jelly.
