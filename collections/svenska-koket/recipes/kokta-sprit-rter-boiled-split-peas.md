## Ingredients

- 1 liter split peas (equivalent to 4 liters unsplit)
- water
- salt
- 1 bunch parsley
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the peas and place in boiling, salted water (short stock) together with the cleaned and rinsed parsley bunch.
2. Cook until completely soft.
3. Remove the parsley bunch and drain the water.
4. Pour the peas onto the serving dish and mix with the cold butter.
