## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized celery roots
- 2 parsnips
- 2 carrots
- water
- salt
- 1 tbsp. butter
- 1 tbsp. flour
- 2 liters light broth
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the vegetables in the usual way and cook soft in lightly salted water, after which they are passed through a sieve.
2. Fry butter and flour together, dilute with the purée and the boiling broth, and let the soup cook for 10 minutes, after which it is skimmed and seasoned.
3. Whip the egg yolks and cream in the soup bowl, and pour in the soup with vigorous whisking.
4. Serve with fried bread.
