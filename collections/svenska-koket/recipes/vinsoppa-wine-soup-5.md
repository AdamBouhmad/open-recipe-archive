## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters water
- 1 lemon
- 150 grams sugar
- 3 parts French wine
- 1.5 tablespoons potato starch
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the water for 1.5 hours together with thinly sliced lemon peel, well-pressed lemon juice, and a portion of the sugar.
2. Strain out the lemon peel and return the soup to the pot.
3. When it boils again, thicken it with potato starch dissolved in wine.
4. Just before serving, whisk the egg yolks together with 2 tablespoons of sugar in the soup bowl.
5. Pour the soup in gradually while whisking vigorously.
6. Serve foaming with biscuits.
