## Ingredients

- 0.5 kg lamb breast
- 0.25 kg salt pork
- 30 gr. butter
- 4-5 tablespoons tomato purée
- 1 tablespoon beef steak sauce
- 2 carrots
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 6 deciliters meat broth or broth
- 0.5 liter potatoes
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 0.5 can fine peas (0.25 liter)

## Directions

1. Treat the meat as in the previous recipe, cut into even beautiful pieces, which are browned well in hot salt pork.
2. Brown the butter and flour in a pot, add the meat, sprinkle with spices and add the boiling broth or meat broth and dilute with tomato purée and beef steak sauce.
3. Let it then simmer slowly covered about 1 hour.
4. Trim the carrots and potatoes and cut into oblong pieces, peel the onion and cut into wedges.
5. Place the vegetables and onion on top of the meat and let the stew simmer about another 30 minutes, or until everything is tender.
6. Then add the peas and let the stew come to a boil, taste and serve very hot.
