## Ingredients

- 7 hg potato puree
- 4 tbsp cooked, red tomato puree
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare the potato puree as in the preceding recipe and mix with thick, cooked, red tomato puree.
2. Pipe the mixture through a fluted piping nozzle onto a well-buttered baking sheet in the form of half eggs.
3. Brush with beaten egg and bake in a warm oven for about 10 minutes.
4. Serve immediately as a garnish around roasted meat.
