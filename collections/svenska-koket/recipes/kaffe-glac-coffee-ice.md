## Ingredients

- 50 grams freshly roasted and ground coffee beans
- 1 liter boiling cream
- Base cream recipe

## Directions

1. Place the roasted and ground coffee beans in the boiling cream.
2. Let it steep, well covered, in a warm place for about 20 minutes.
3. Strain through muslin.
4. Prepare the cream according to the base recipe.
5. Freeze in a machine as in recipe no. 929.
6. Serve with ice cream bread.
