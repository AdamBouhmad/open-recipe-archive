## Ingredients

- 30 brussels sprout heads
- 100 g lean pork
- 1 small red onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters broth
- 3 tablespoons flour
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the cabbage and blanch for about 10 minutes, remove and drain.
2. Cut the pork into slices, peel and chop the onion.
3. Fry the onion and pork slices in the butter over low heat for about 10 minutes.
4. Chop the cabbage finely together with the flour and add it together with the pork to the boiling broth, then let the soup cook for 20-30 minutes.
5. Strain, skim and season with spices, and serve with fried bread or quenelles.
