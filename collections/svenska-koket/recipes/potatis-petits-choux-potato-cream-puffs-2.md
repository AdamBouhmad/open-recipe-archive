## Ingredients

- 125 gr butter
- 2 1/2 dcl water
- 210 gr flour
- 4 eggs
- 5 dcl firm potato mash
- salt, sugar
- 1 kg cooking fat

## Directions

1. Boil butter and water together, whisk in flour while boiling and let the batter cook until it releases from the pan and has a yellow color
2. Pour out and let cool, then work in eggs one at a time and stir the batter 10 minutes
3. Prepare the potato mash as in recipe 677, let cool and add to the batter with vigorous stirring, then taste the mass with spices
4. Form the mass with two spoons dipped in cooking fat into round balls, which if desired can be filled with chopped truffle or peas, cook golden brown in hot cooking fat (see recipe 44) and place on absorbent paper to drain
5. Serve as garnish around fried meat. If the balls are not filled, they should be made very small
