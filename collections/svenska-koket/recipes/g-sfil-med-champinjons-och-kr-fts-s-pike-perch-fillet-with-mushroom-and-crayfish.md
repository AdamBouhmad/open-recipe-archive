## Ingredients

- 2 kg pike-perch
- 1¼ tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- fish broth
- Mushroom sauce: 1 tablespoon butter, 2 tablespoons flour, 2 parts cream, fish broth, 1 can mushrooms (½ liter), 2-3 egg yolks, pepper, salt, lemon juice
- Crayfish sauce: 1½ tablespoons crayfish butter, 1½ tablespoons flour, 2 parts cream, fish broth, 1½ hectograms crayfish tails, white pepper (sugar)
- For garnish: ½ can mushrooms (½ liter), ½ hectograms crayfish tails

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it and rinse well.
2. Remove the skin, cut it open along the back, remove the backbone together with all small bones.
3. Rub the fillets with salt and vinegar.
4. Butter a roasting pan, place the fish in it and put in the oven to roast, adding fish broth (cooked on the head, skin and bones).
5. Place it on a hot platter and pour the yellow mushroom sauce over half the platter and the pink crayfish sauce on the other half.
6. Garnish the dish with mushrooms and crayfish tails.
7. Prepare the sauces according to general rules.
