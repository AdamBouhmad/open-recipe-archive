## Ingredients

- 1½ litres water
- 60 grams red sago grains
- 1 stick cinnamon
- 1½ cups crushed sugar
- Peel of ¼ lemon
- 3 parts red wine
- 3 tablespoons redcurrant juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the sago grains and place in water with the sugar, cinnamon and lemon peel to cook.
2. When the grains are clear, add the wine and redcurrant juice.
3. Heat the soup but do not let it boil and taste with sugar.
4. Serve with rusks or rusks.
