## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg smoked beef brisket
- water
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Soak the meat in cold water overnight
2. Scrape with a knife and remove all blackened parts
3. Place in cold water, skim well during cooking, and let simmer slowly for 4-5 hours
4. Let the brisket rest in its broth for 1/2 hour before slicing; this makes it more tender
5. Serve sliced and garnished with parsley
