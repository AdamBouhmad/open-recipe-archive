## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless entrecôte
- 1 hg smoked ham
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 tsp salt (5 g)
- a little white pepper
- 5 dcl dark broth
- 1 1/2-2 dcl Madeira

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until properly tender, is prepared and larded with ham (see page 232), after which it is rubbed with a mixture of salt and pepper and tied so that it has a beautiful shape.
2. The butter is browned in an iron pot, which should not be larger than to just fit the roast, the roast is browned in it on all sides until a beautiful brown crust is formed, then the boiling broth and half of the wine are added.
3. The meat is then allowed to stew with the lid tightly closed, until tender, or about 1 1/2-2 hours, turning it a few times during cooking. Towards the end of cooking the remaining wine is added.
4. The roast is sliced into thin slices, placed on a hot platter, moistened with a couple of tablespoons of sauce, garnished with salad or parsley and small browned potatoes or potato pastries. Served with brown sauce (see no. 430).
