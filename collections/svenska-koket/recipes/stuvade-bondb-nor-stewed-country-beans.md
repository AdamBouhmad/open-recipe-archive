## Ingredients

- 8 deciliters sprouted beans
- 8 small fresh carrots
- 8 deciliters water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3 deciliters bean cooking liquid
- 3 deciliters milk
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 3 teaspoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the sprouts; if the beans are very mature, peel them as well.
2. Rinse the carrots, scrape them, rinse again, dry them and cut them into slices or cubes.
3. Bring the water to a boil with the salt, add the beans and let them cook approximately 15 minutes, then add the carrots and let the vegetables cook until they are soft.
4. Pour off the cooking liquid and reserve it.
5. Melt butter with flour for 2 minutes, add the cooking liquid and milk, and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes.
6. Add the beans with the parsley and season the stew with sugar.
7. Serve with braised ham or roasted pork.
