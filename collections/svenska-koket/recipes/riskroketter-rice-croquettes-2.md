## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. rice grains (150 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 1 l. milk
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 30 sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- zest of 1/2 lemon, finely grated
- 2 tbsp. flour for breading (20 gr.)
- 1 egg for breading
- 1/2 tsp. salt for breading
- 1 tsp. cooking oil for breading
- 1 1/2 dcl. crushed rusks for breading
- 1 kg. lard for frying

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains in cold water, blanch them in hot water, pour them into a sieve, rinse with cold water and let them drain.
2. Butter a saucepan with 1/2 tbsp. of the butter, pour in the milk and bring it to a boil, add the grains and let them cook slowly until they are soft, or about 1/2 hour.
3. Pour out the porridge, mix it immediately with the rest of the butter and let it cool, then add the beaten eggs, seasonings, the blanched and ground almonds.
4. Shape and cook the croquettes as in recipe no. 790. Sprinkle with sugar and serve with jam or compote as a dessert.
