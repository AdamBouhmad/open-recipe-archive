## Ingredients

- 150 gr. butter
- 75 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 50 gr. sweet almond
- 2 bitter almonds
- 180 gr. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Work all the ingredients well together on a pastry board to a smooth and coherent shortcrust pastry.
2. Line small fluted molds with this, and bake the pastries in moderate oven heat.
3. Turn out immediately.
