## Ingredients

- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 6 eggs
- 6 anchovies

## Directions

1. Butter a low omelette dish with butter.
2. Break the eggs carefully and let them slide into the dish close to each other.
3. Clean and bone the anchovies and cut each anchovy into four fillets.
4. Place two fillets in a ring around each egg and two in a cross over the egg.
5. Place the remaining butter in small flakes on the anchovies.
6. Fry the eggs in the oven until the whites are set.
7. Serve as a breakfast dish.
