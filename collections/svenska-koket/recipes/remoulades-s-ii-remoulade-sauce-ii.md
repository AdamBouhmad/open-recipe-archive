## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tsp salt (5 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1 tbsp tarragon vinegar or vinegar
- 3 tbsp fine salad oil
- 1/2 pickled cucumber
- 1 tbsp capers
- 2 tsp green French mustard
- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 tbsp beef sauce
- 1/2 tsp anchovy essence
- 2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with salt and pepper for 15 minutes
2. Add the vinegar little by little, with vigorous stirring
3. Add the oil in a fine stream, with steady stirring
4. Cut the pickled cucumber into pieces, pound in a mortar together with the capers and pass through a hair sieve along with the egg yolks
5. Mix this mass into the sauce together with the mustard, beef sauce, anchovy essence and the cream whipped to stiff peaks
6. Taste the sauce carefully
7. Serve as the previous recipe
