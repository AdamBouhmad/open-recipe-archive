## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 hectogram boneless beef
- 1/2 hectogram boneless veal
- 30 grams lard
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks
- 1 teaspoon salt
- white pepper
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped pickled onion
- 4 tablespoons cream
- 1 egg for breading
- 1 cup crushed rusks for breading
- 1/2 kilogram cooking fat

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for 5 minutes, cool in cold water and shell them.
2. Dry the meat with a cloth, wring it out in hot water, cut it together with the lard into pieces and pass through a meat grinder 5 times.
3. Pound with a wooden pestle until the mixture becomes even and smooth.
4. Whip the egg yolks well with the cream and work in by spoonfuls into the mixture, along with the rusks, spices and pickled onion.
5. Taste the mixture carefully.
6. Coat the shelled eggs with the meat mixture, roll in crushed rusks, then in the beaten egg and then again in crushed rusks.
7. Flatten the mixture at both ends and let it rest for 1 hour to harden.
8. Heat the cooking fat and cook the eggs in it until they are beautifully brown.
9. Place on gray paper so the fat can drain off.
10. Cut them in half crosswise and insert a parsley sprig as a handle.
11. Garnish with salad and serve with green salad in sharp sauce or with mayonnaise as a breakfast dish.
