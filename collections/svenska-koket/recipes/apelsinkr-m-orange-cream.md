## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 40 grams granulated sugar
- 2 parts thin cream
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- zest of 1 orange
- juice of 4 oranges
- 5 leaves gelatin
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks, sugar, thin cream and grated orange and lemon zests in a saucepan and simmer with vigorous whisking over heat until the cream thickens.
2. Pour out immediately and let cool with continued whisking.
3. Add the orange juice and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in a little warm water.
4. Last, stir in the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the cream into a mold rinsed with cold water and set it out to set.
6. When ready to serve, dip the mold briefly in warm water and turn out the cream.
7. Serve with pastries.
