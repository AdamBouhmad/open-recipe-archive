## Ingredients

- 18 chops
- 1 tbsp salt
- ½ tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter
- ½ liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the chops, chop, trim, and beat once on each side.
2. Pepper and salt them and brown in butter.
3. Add the broth and let the chops cook slowly until done in 5-10 minutes.
4. The chops can also be cooked in cream sauce, but must first be breaded.
