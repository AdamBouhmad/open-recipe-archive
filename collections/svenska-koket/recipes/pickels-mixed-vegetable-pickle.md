## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter small Västerås cucumbers
- 4 small tomatoes
- 1/2 liter sugar peas
- 1/2 liter small green beans
- 1 cauliflower head
- 1/2 liter small carrot pieces
- 2 dcl. cress seeds
- 2 dcl. apple cores
- 4 gr. whole white pepper
- 3 dcl. small pickled onions
- 2 gr. cayenne pepper
- 10 gr. ginger in small pieces
- 2 gr. mace
- 4 gr. cloves
- 2 pieces Spanish pepper
- dill
- 3 liters good vinegar
- 3 hg. sugar

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the cucumbers and tomatoes with 1 tbsp. salt and let them stand for one day.
2. Clean and rinse the vegetables; cut the peas, beans, cauliflower and carrots into nice pieces.
3. Blanch all the vegetables (except cucumbers and tomatoes) separately in lightly salted water (1 liter water, 1 tbsp. salt), rinse with cold water and let drain on cloth.
4. Dry the cucumbers and tomatoes on cloth and cover with quadruple linen.
5. When the cucumbers are cold, bind the jar well.
6. Store in the cellar.
7. Ready after 5-6 weeks.
