## Ingredients

- Orange peels
- Water
- 8 hg. sugar per kg. peels
- 2 dcl. water per kg. peels
- 2 gr. citric acid per kg. peels

## Directions

1. Let the orange peels lie in water for 4 days, changing the water every day.
2. Place them thereafter in boiling water and let them cook slowly until they are completely soft, then lay them on cloth to drain.
3. Weigh the peels — for each kg. of peels take 8 hg. sugar, 2 dcl. water and 2 gr. citric acid, which are cooked to a clear, syrupy liquid.
4. Add the peels and let them cook slowly for about 20 minutes, after which they are placed in a porcelain jar and the liquid is cooked until it is syrupy, and poured hot over them.
5. Bind the jar when the peels are cold.
