## Ingredients

- 750 gr. lamb fillet
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1.5 hectograms lard
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 3 deciliters bouillon
- 1 hectogram goose liver
- 1 can asparagus (1/4 liter)
- 1/2 liter small potatoes cooked in stock

## Directions

1. Dry the fillets with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into 12 slices, which are lightly flattened.
2. Rub with seasonings, roll up and wrap in thinly cut lard slices.
3. Brown in butter, add boiling bouillon and braise covered until tender, about 45 minutes.
4. Remove the lard slices and arrange the noisettes in a circle on a round serving dish.
5. Place a small round goose liver slice on each noisette.
6. Place the asparagus, reheated in a water bath, in the center of the dish.
7. Arrange the small potatoes around.
8. Garnish the arrangement with parsley and serve with truffle sauce (see recipe 433).
