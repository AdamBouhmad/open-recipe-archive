## Ingredients

- 3 litres red, half-ripe gooseberries
- 1/2 litre water
- sugar equal in weight to the juice

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, rinse them and cook them very slowly with the water until they begin to burst apart.
2. Pour them into cheesecloth and let them drip until the following day.
3. Weigh the juice and dissolve the sugar in the cold juice.
4. Then let it come to a boil and boil rapidly for 15-20 minutes under careful skimming.
5. The jelly is ready when a drop of it, poured onto a plate, quickly sets.
