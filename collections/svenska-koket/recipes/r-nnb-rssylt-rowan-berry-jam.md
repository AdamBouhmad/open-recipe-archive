## Ingredients

- 2 kg rowan berries from sweet rowan (approximately 3 litres)
- 1 1/2 kg sugar
- 4 dcl water

## Directions

1. Pick the berries when they are ripe and juicy.
2. Make the jam thereafter as in recipe 1031.
3. Pour the jam into small glass jars, which are to be bound the following day.
4. Serve as a salad or on butter and bread with tea.
