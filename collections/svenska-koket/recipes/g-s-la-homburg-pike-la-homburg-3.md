## Ingredients

- 1 pike of 1 1/2 kg.
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. clarified butter (40 gr.)
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tbsp. finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 2 balls crayfish butter (20 gr.)
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and prepare it as in recipe 353.
2. Garnish with mushroom caps sautéed in butter and serve with mushroom sauce and mashed potatoes.
3. If the fish is served at a dinner with several courses, 1 kg. of pike is sufficient for 6 persons.
