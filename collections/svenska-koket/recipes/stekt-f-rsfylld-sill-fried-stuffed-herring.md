## Ingredients

- 6 fresh herrings (= 2 kg)
- 12 anchovies
- 1 kkp crushed hardtack
- 1/2 kkp milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 kkp coarse rye flour
- 3 msk stock

## Directions

1. Open the herrings along the back, remove the entrails and backbone, rinse them thoroughly, dry them well, and salt them lightly.
2. Clean and bone the anchovies.
