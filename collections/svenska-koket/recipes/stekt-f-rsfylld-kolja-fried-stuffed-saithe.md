## Ingredients

- 2 kg saithe
- 1 1/2 msk salt
- 2 tsk vinegar
- Filling: 1 1/2 kkp grated wheat bread, 1 kkp cream milk, 1 egg, 20 anchovies, 1 msk chopped parsley
- For breading: 1 egg white, 1 kkp crushed hardtack
- For frying: 2 msk butter or stock, boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut the fish open along the back and remove the backbone and entrails.
2. Rinse it thoroughly, dry it, and rub it with salt and vinegar.
3. Clean the anchovies, bone them, and cut them into pieces.
4. Make a forcemeat from the bread, milk, egg, anchovies, and parsley.
5. Fill the fish with the forcemeat and sew it closed along the back.
6. Brush it with egg white and sprinkle with crushed hardtack.
7. Butter a long pan with stock, place the fish in it, and allow it to cook slowly in the oven for 20 minutes to 1/2 hour.
8. During cooking, add boiling water from time to time and baste it with its sauce.
