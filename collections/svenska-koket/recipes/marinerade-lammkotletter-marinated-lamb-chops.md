## Ingredients

- 12 lamb chops
- 50 gr. lard
- 50 gr. pickled, cooked beef tongue
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped red onion
- 4 parsley stems
- 2 bay leaves
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- 60 gr. melted butter
- 2 deciliters finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 60 gr. cold butter
- 1.5 deciliters meat stock or bouillon
- 2 cups applesauce with 30 gr. grated finely chopped horseradish, or mushrooms sliced and sautéed in butter
- parsley or salad for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops as in the previous recipe, lard with lard and tongue cut into fine strips, rub with the seasonings and place on a dish.
2. Sprinkle with onion, finely cut parsley stems and bay leaves, and pour over lemon juice and cooking oil.
3. Marinate for 1/2 hour, turning 4 times.
4. Remove (shake off the seasonings), wrap in cloth, dip in melted butter and turn in crushed breadcrumbs.
5. Fry the chops as in the previous recipe, arrange in a circle on a hot serving dish.
6. Whisk the pan with boiling bouillon and pour the sauce over the chops.
7. Garnish the center of the dish with applesauce mixed with grated finely chopped horseradish, or with mushrooms sliced and sautéed in butter.
8. Garnish around with parsley or salad.
9. Serve with French potatoes or potato mousse flowers.
