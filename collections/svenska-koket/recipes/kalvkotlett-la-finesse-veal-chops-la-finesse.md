## Ingredients

- 3-4 truffles
- 150 g cooked, smoked ham
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 6 veal chops
- 3 tsp. salt
- 1 tsp. white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp. water
- 3 tbsp. finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 g)
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 g)
- 3 dcl. good broth
- 1 tsp. tomato purée
- 6 wheat slices, cooked in cream
- French potatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the truffle, ham and mushrooms into small, even cubes. Mince the scraps finely and mix with the crushed breadcrumbs.
2. Dry the chops with a cloth wrung out in hot water, trim them, pound well on both sides and rub with the spice mixture.
3. Brush with the beaten egg mixed with the water, and turn in the ham, mushroom and breadcrumb mixture.
4. Brown the butter in a frying pan, place the chops in and let them fry about 5 minutes on each side.
5. Quickly sauté the mushroom and ham cubes in a little of the butter and arrange in the center of a round serving platter. Place the bread crouton around it and lay the chops on top.
6. Mix the flour well with the butter in which the chops were fried, add the broth and tomato purée and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes.
7. Taste and pour over the chops, garnish the dish with French potatoes and parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
