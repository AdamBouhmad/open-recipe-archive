## Ingredients

- 36 Baltic herrings
- 2 tbsp salt
- 1 bunch parsley
- 90 g butter
- 2 tbsp flour (20 g)
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp water
- 2 tbsp crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g) or 200 g lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the herrings as in recipe 163, spread them out, sprinkle with salt and let them rest for 1 hour.
2. Clean the parsley, rinse it, wrap it in cloth, chop it finely, wring it out in a cloth and mix well with the butter.
3. Form the parsley butter into a roll, let it set, and cut it into 18 slices.
4. Place two herrings against each other with skin side out and a slice of parsley butter between them.
5. Dip first in flour, then in egg beaten with water, and then roll in crushed breadcrumbs.
6. Fry the herrings in butter or lard for 8-10 minutes on each side, place on a warm plate, garnish with parsley and serve with mashed potatoes as a breakfast dish.
