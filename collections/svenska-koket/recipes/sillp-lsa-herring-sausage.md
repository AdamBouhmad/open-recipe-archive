## Ingredients

- 2 large or 3 small salted fat herrings
- 3/4 liter cooked cold potatoes
- 1 medium Portuguese onion
- 4 tbsp butter (80 gr.)
- 3 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the herrings: remove them, rinse well, and soak in plenty of water for 12 hours, changing the water 2 times.
2. Remove the skin, fillet the herrings, and remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets well, wrap in a fish towel, and cut into small cubes.
4. Peel and cut the potatoes into equally large cubes.
5. Peel, finely chop the onion, and fry well in the butter.
6. Add the herring and potatoes, and lightly brown the mixture with careful stirring.
7. Finally, stir in the cream and place the dish on a warm carrot.
8. Serve as a breakfast dish.
