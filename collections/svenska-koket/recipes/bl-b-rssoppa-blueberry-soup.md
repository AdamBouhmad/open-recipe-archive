## Ingredients

- 1 litre fresh or ½ litre preserved blueberries
- 2 litres water
- 1–1½ cups crushed sugar
- 1½ tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. If using fresh blueberries, clean and rinse them.
2. Bring the water to a boil, add the blueberries and sugar, and let the berries cook until soft.
3. Thicken the soup with potato flour mixed with a little cold water, bring to a good boil and taste.
4. Serve preferably cold. If desired, the berries may be strained out before adding the sugar.
