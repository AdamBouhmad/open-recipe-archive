## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fat herrings
- 3 deciliters vinegar
- 1 deciliter sugar (85 gr.)
- 2 red onions
- 5 white peppercorns
- 10 allspice berries
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 teaspoon table oil (optional)

## Directions

1. Let the herring soak in water for approximately 24 hours, changing the water three times.
2. Skin and clean the herring, then cut into fillets, carefully removing the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets, wrap in a fish towel, cut in half, and place in a stone crock alternating with peeled red onion sliced thin and the spices.
4. Boil the vinegar and sugar; when the brine is cold, add the table oil if desired, then pour the brine over the herring.
5. After 8-10 hours it is ready to serve.
6. Cut the fillets into 8 mm wide pieces crosswise, arrange in a glass salad dish, pour the brine over, and garnish with the onion and spices.
