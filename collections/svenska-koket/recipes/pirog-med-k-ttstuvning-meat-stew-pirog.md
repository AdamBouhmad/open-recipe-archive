## Ingredients

- 250 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tablespoon brandy
- 1/2 egg
- 250 gr. butter
- water
- 1/4 egg for brushing
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons wheat flour
- 3-4 parts bouillon
- 3 cups cooked meat cut into cubes
- 1 small can peas
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the puff dough as fine puff dough, roll out half of the dough to a bottom (suitable to the serving dish)
2. Place this on a baking sheet and bake light brown
3. For the stew, fry butter and flour together, add the bouillon, and let the sauce simmer 10 minutes
4. Add the meat and peas to the sauce, and season the stew with salt and white pepper
5. Spread the stew on the baked puff dough bottom
6. Roll out the rest of the puff dough to a lid, place it over the stew and press together at the edges
7. Garnish the lid with squares or leaves of puff dough
8. Brush with egg, and bake the pirog thereafter about 10-15 minutes in good oven heat
9. Serve at once
