## Ingredients

- 1 litre raw milk
- 1/2 litre cream
- 2 msk. sugar (30 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 15 almonds, 2 of them bitter (15 gr.) or 1 tsk. ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Whip together the milk, cream, sugar, salt, and the scalded, ground almonds or cinnamon
2. Test the mixture in a small saucepan over low heat; if it is too firm, add a little more cream; if too soft, add 1—2 beaten eggs
3. Pour into a buttered mold and bake in gentle oven heat or in a bain-marie, in the oven or on top of the stove
4. Serve with brûlée sauce, fruit sauce, or cream milk
