## Ingredients

- 210 gr. butter
- 2 eggs
- 8 dessert spoons caster sugar
- 6 heaping tbsp. wheat flour
- 1 tsp. washed butter
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks
- 1½ kg. sweet almond
- 10 bitter almonds
- 1 liter caster sugar
- ½ tsp. grated lemon zest
- 1 egg white
- 1½ liters fresh apples
- 2 kg. dried apricots
- 3 kg. prunes
- 6 kg. sugar
- 3 parts water
- 20 gr. pistachio almond

## Directions

1. Make a regular shortcrust pastry from butter, sugar, eggs and flour.
2. Grease a mold or cake pan with butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks. Roll out the dough to a round cake, place it in the mold and bake golden brown in good oven heat, then turn out and let it cool.
3. Blanch the almond, dry it and grate it or pass it through an almond mill and pound it with the sugar and lemon zest in a mortar. Cut the egg white, beaten to stiff peaks, fold it in, and pound everything until it becomes fine and cohesive.
4. Soak the apricots in water the day before. From the sugar and water, cook a clear syrup. In this, cook the peeled and quartered apples until they are clear. Remove the apples, and in the syrup cook the apricots and prunes until they are soft. Blanch the pistachio almond and chop it finely.
5. Form a ring of almond mass on the outer edge of the tart. Inside this ring, garnish the tart beautifully with the cooked fruit, and sprinkle pistachio almond over it.
6. Serve the tart with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
