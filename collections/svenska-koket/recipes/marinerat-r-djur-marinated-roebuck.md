## Ingredients

- 3 kg. of the leg or shoulder
- 3 hg. lard
- 1 msk. salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 2 dcl. vinegar
- 3 dcl. white wine
- 2 dcl. water
- 2 msk. cooking oil
- 6 cloves
- 1 small piece of ginger
- 1 red onion, in slices
- 4 parsley stems
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 boned anchovies
- 5 dcl. thick cream
- 2 tsk. sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the meat with a wet mallet, lard it (see page 232), season with the remaining spices and place in a deep dish.
2. Mix all ingredients for the marinade, then pour over the meat, which should rest in it for 2-3 days, turning it often.
3. Remove the meat, let it drip, dry it well.
4. Brown in butter in a roasting pan, add the strained marinade, cover with the anchovies and simmer for 1 hour.
5. Cook the cream with the sugar and add it, then let the meat roast until tender, or about 1 1/2 hours.
6. Cut and garnish with browned potatoes or vegetables.
7. Serve with reduced sauce (see no. 429), mixed with 1 msk. chopped capers.
