## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 2 dcl fine white wine
- 1/2 tsp salt (3 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 2-3 tbsp fine olive oil
- 1-2 dcl vinegar or wine vinegar
- 1 tbsp finely chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp chervil

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush them well and cook them in salted water.
2. Drain the water carefully, and let the potatoes cool slightly, then peel them and shape with a knife like corks.
3. Cut these immediately into thin slices, which are placed in a salad bowl and doused with the wine.
4. Add the spices, olive oil, vinegar, parsley and chervil, and stir the salad carefully so that the potato slices do not break apart.
5. Serve with roasted, cold meat and sausage.
