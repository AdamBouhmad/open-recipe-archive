## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. Carolina rice grains (2 hg.)
- 3 lit. water
- 1 1/2 msk. salt (23 gr.)
- 75 gr. butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains in cold water, place in boiling salted water and let them boil vigorously for 15 minutes, then pour them into a colander, rinse with cold water and let them drain. Place the grains in a saucepan, distribute the butter in flakes and mix it into the grains with a fork. Place the lid on the saucepan, which is then allowed to stand in a warm oven for about 15 minutes, during which time the grains are often stirred with a fork. Serve with eggs, cold fish, boiled meat and chicken. The rice grains can also be cooked in bouillon, treated in the same way.
