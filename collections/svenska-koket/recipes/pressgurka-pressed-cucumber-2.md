## Ingredients

- 1 pressing cucumber
- 2 tablespoons fine salt
- 0.5 cup vinegar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 0.5 teaspoon ground white pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumber and cut into thin slices from the stem downward.
2. Place the slices in alternating layers with salt between flat plates, press, and let lie for a couple of hours.
3. Pour off the liquid that forms.
4. Make a brine from the vinegar, water, sugar, and pepper.
5. Place the cucumber in it and sprinkle with chopped parsley.
