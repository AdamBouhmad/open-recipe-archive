## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups rice groats
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 liters milk
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice groats and pour them into a colander to drain.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, pour in the milk and bring to a boil, add the groats and let them cook in a closed pot for about 3 hours.
3. During cooking, the porridge should be stirred occasionally to prevent burning at the bottom and diluted, if necessary, with a little more milk.
4. Taste and serve with ground cinnamon.
