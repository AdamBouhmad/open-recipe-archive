## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 1/4 celery root
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 1 1/2 liters clear stock
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 2–3 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 2 tablespoons wheat flour

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the Jerusalem artichokes (preferably under running water), wash again, and let them soak for 1 hour in 1 1/2 liters water mixed with 2 tablespoons vinegar and 2 tablespoons wheat flour to prevent darkening.
2. Peel the celery root, cut it into cubes, and add it together with the Jerusalem artichokes.
3. Set aside twelve small, round Jerusalem artichokes; cook these separately in a little stock and reserve to serve in the soup.
4. Place the remaining artichokes in the cold salted water and let them cook until completely soft, then pass through a sieve.
5. Prepare the purée as in recipe no. 266 and serve with the reserved Jerusalem artichokes and cheese croutons.
