## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters water
- peel and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 hectogram sago grain
- 1 hectogram raisins
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- 1.5 dcl rhubarb juice
- 1 dcl sherry or French wine
- 3 egg yolks, 2 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the water with the lemon peel and whisk in the grain so that it does not lump.
2. Rinse the raisins well in lukewarm water and add along with the cinnamon and let the soup cook until the grain is clear.
3. Add the juice and wine.
4. Beat the egg yolks and whites with the sugar in a soup bowl until the mixture becomes white and frothy, then pour the soup in while stirring constantly.
5. Serve warm with small rusks or biscuits.
