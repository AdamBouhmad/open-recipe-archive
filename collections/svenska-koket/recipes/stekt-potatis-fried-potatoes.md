## Ingredients

- 1 liter boiled potatoes
- 3 tablespoons butter, cream or bacon fat
- 0.5 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Cut the potatoes into slices.
2. Brown the butter in a frying pan, add the potatoes, sprinkle with salt and fry with careful stirring until it gets a nice golden-brown color.
