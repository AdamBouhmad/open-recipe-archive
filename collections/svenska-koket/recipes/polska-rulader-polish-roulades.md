## Ingredients

- 1⅜ kg boneless beef in one connected piece
- ½ Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1½ hg lard
- ¼ tsk white pepper
- ½ tbsp salt
- 1 hg anchovy
- 2 tbsp butter
- ⅛ liter boiling water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into thin slices, which are well beaten, though not so that they fall apart.
2. Peel and chop the onion finely together with the parsley, and sprinkle this on the meat slices.
3. Cut the lard into thin slices, which are sprinkled with pepper and salt and placed on top of the meat slices.
4. On top place cleaned and boned anchovy.
5. Roll the meat slices up tightly and bind them in two places with fine sailcloth (previously dipped in hot water) and place them tightly next to each other in an iron roasting pan brushed with butter.
6. Set the roulades thereafter over good heat to brown.
7. Dilute them with the boiling water and let them cook 1-1½ hours.
8. Strain and skim the sauce and serve as gravy.
