## Ingredients

- 1 can of button mushrooms (1/4 liter) or 15 gr. dried morels
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 1 1/2 liters secondary broth
- 2—3 egg yolks
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. The button mushrooms or morels, which should first be well blanched, are cut into slices and fried in the butter until it is clear.
2. Press the mushroom out of the butter with a spoon and then fry the flour in the butter for 2 minutes.
3. Add the boiling broth gradually and let it boil for 5 minutes while stirring.
4. Beat the egg yolks and whisk them into the soup, then season with the spices and pour it over the mushroom in the soup bowl.
5. Serve with cheese croutons or toasted bread.
