## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hg. good macaroni
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.) for cooking
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese (optional)
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 4 deciliters milk
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tablespoon breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the macaroni as in the previous recipe, place in a pie mold, in layers with the butter distributed in small flakes, spices and cheese if desired.
2. For the egg mixture, whisk the egg yolks well with the spices and milk, then fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the egg mixture over the macaroni and bake the pudding in good oven heat until it is set and has a golden-brown color.
4. Serve with roasted meat or boiled ham.
