## Ingredients

- 210 grams sweet almonds
- 12 grams bitter almonds
- 250 grams granulated sugar
- peel of 1 lemon
- 10 tablespoons clarified butter
- 5 eggs
- 10 tablespoons thick cream
- 50 grams crushed rusks
- 1/2 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, grate or press through an almond mill, and pound with the sugar.
2. Clarify the butter, let it cool, and beat until white.
3. Add the egg yolks together with the cream, the grated lemon peel, and the almond paste, and beat the mixture for 1/2 hour.
4. Sift the crushed rusks and blend in together with the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-strewn form or pan and bake in not too warm an oven.
6. When it has risen and then fallen again, it is ready.
