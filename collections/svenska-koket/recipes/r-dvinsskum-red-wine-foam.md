## Ingredients

- 2 2/3 parts red wine
- 4 egg yolks
- 100 grams granulated sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest
- 1 tablespoon raspberry or red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Whisk all ingredients over heat until the liquid begins to simmer and the whole mixture is transformed into foam.
2. Continue whisking until the foam has cooled.
3. Pour into a jelly bowl or cream cups.
