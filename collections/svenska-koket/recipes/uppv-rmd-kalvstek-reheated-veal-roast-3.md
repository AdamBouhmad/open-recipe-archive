## Ingredients

- 500-600 g boned veal roast
- 20 g butter (1 tbsp)
- 30 g flour (3 tbsp)
- 400 ml meat stock
- 50 ml thick cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the roast into even slices.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes; add the meat stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes, then add the cream and season with spices and bring to a boil.
3. Warm the meat in the sauce but do not boil; place on a hot platter, garnish with fried potatoes and serve with green salad, cucumbers and lingonberries.
