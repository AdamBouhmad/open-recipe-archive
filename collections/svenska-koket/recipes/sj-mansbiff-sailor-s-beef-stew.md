## Ingredients

- 1 kg cut beef, beef chuck or boneless beef chuck
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 tbsp flour (10 g)
- 1 large Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 liter potatoes
- 5 dcl boiling water
- 1/2 tsk solid beef extract
- 1/2-1 dcl white wine or sherry

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat as for beef (see recipe no. 483). Sprinkle the beef patties with a mixture of salt, white pepper and flour.
2. Peel the onion, cut into slices and brown nicely in half of the butter.
3. Peel the potatoes and cut into 1/2 cm thick slices.
4. Grease a beef casserole with the remaining butter, and layer the beef patties, onion and potatoes alternately; sprinkle a little salt on the potatoes.
5. Pour on the water mixed with beef extract and wine, cover and let simmer slowly in the oven or on the stove for approximately 1 1/2-2 hours, or until the meat is tender.
6. Serve the beef from the casserole. The wine can be omitted if desired.
