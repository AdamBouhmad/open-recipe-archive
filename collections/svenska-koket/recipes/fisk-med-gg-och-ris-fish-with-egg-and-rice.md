## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. rice grains (150 gr.)
- 1 1/2 litre water
- 1 tbsp. salt
- Leftovers of cooked fish, approximately 4 dcl.
- 3 eggs
- salt, white pepper
- 1 1/2 hg. clarified butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, place in boiling salted water and let cook rapidly until soft, approximately 15 minutes.
2. Immediately drain in a colander, rinse with cold water and place in a warm oven.
3. Stir frequently with a fork until the grains are separated and all water has evaporated.
4. Hard boil the eggs, let them cool and chop finely.
5. Shred the fish with a fork as fine as rice grains.
6. Mix eggs and fish well with the grains and season with spices.
7. Serve with clarified butter as a breakfast dish.
