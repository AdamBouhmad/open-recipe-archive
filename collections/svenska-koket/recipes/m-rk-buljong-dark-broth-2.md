## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. beef bones or marrow pipe
- 1 kg. veal neck
- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 1/4 celeriac
- 1 red onion
- 80 gr. butter
- 4 liters water
- 3 parsley stems
- 1 clove
- 10 gr. salt
- 3 hg. lean, boneless beef (for clarification)
- 1 egg white and rinsed eggshells (for clarification)

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat as in recipe no. 222 and cut from the bones into small pieces. Crush the bones as finely as possible and brown them well in half of the butter in a frying pan together with the prepared and cut vegetables and the onion.
2. Place the browned bones in the broth pot and deglaze the frying pan with a little boiling water, which is then poured into the pot. Add the cold water and parsley and set the broth over fresh heat to boil rapidly. When the boiling point is reached, skim it well, wipe the edge, put on the lid and let the broth cook slowly for 4 hours. Meanwhile brown the meat in the remaining butter; when the bones have cooked 4 hours, add the meat and let everything cook for a further 3 hours.
3. Treat the broth as in the preceding recipe and serve with the same types of garnish.
