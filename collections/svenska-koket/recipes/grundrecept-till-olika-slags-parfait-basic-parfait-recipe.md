## Ingredients

- 125 gr. sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 dcl. water
- 6 dcl. extra thick cream

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water together.
2. Remove the saucepan from heat, add the egg yolks one at a time while whisking.
3. Let the cream simmer over heat with vigorous whisking until thick and foamy.
4. Remove the saucepan from heat and whisk the cream until completely cold.
5. Whip the cream to stiff peaks and carefully fold into the cream.
6. Then blend with the flavouring and colouring desired.
7. Fill the cream into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mould and freeze for 4-5 hours, as in recipe n:o 929.
