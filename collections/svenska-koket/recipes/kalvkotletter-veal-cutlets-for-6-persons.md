## Ingredients

- 12 veal cutlets
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1.75 cups sour milk
- 1 egg
- 0.25 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 cups crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 0.25 liter boiling broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the cutlets with a cloth dipped in hot water, trim and pound once on each side.
2. Sprinkle with pepper and salt, dip in the beaten egg mixed with the sour milk and turn in the crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Brown the cutlets in the heated butter, add the liquid and allow the cutlets to roast slowly for 10 to 15 minutes with the lid closed.
4. Place them on a platter, pour the sauce over and garnish with parsley.
5. If desired, the breading (the egg and crushed breadcrumbs) can be omitted.
