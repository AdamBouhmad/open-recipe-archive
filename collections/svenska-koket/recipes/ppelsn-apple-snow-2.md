## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters good, tart apples (approximately 8 hg.)
- 3 egg whites
- 135 gr. granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, place them in a small baking pan and bake in the oven until they are soft, then press them through a fine sieve.
2. Let the purée cool and then stir it with the egg whites and sugar until it is white and fluffy, or approximately 2 hours.
3. Place in a compote bowl and let it stand on ice for 1 hour.
4. Serve with sponge biscuits or macaroons. The mixture can also be baked in the oven as a soufflé.
