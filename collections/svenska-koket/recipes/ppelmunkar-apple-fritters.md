## Ingredients

- 7 beautiful apples
- 150 grams granulated sugar
- 125 grams butter
- 125 grams wheat flour
- 3 1/4-4 tablespoons water
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and remove the cores
2. Place them in a buttered pie form, fill with sugar, place in the oven to bake until they are half-soft, and then place them outside to cool
3. Prepare the butter dough as simple butter dough (no. 549) and roll out to approximately 1 cm thickness
4. Cut out into squares, place an apple on each square, and fold the corners over each other so that they form a bundle
5. Turn the fritters, place on a baking sheet, brush with egg, and bake in a hot oven
