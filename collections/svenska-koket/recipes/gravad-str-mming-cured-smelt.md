## Ingredients

- 20 smelts
- 1 tsp French mustard
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2-3 parts strong vinegar
- 1-2 parts vegetable oil
- 2 parts water
- 1 bunch dill

## Directions

1. Clean the smelts, rinse, scale and fillet them
2. From the spices, vegetable oil, vinegar and water make a strong brine
3. Place the smelts in this and sprinkle with chopped dill
4. After 1/2 hour it is ready to serve
