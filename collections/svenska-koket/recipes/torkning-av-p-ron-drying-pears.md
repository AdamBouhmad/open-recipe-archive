## Ingredients

- Pears
- Thin sugar syrup (2 kilograms sugar per liter of water)

## Directions

1. Dry these in the same way as apples but should be briefly blanched in thin sugar syrup (2 kilograms sugar per liter of water) before drying. If they are small, they can be dried whole.
