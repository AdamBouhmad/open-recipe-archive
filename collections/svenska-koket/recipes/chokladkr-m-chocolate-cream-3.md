## Ingredients

- 1 ägg
- 2 dcl. mjölk
- 35 gr. kakaopulver
- 1 1/2 dcl. strösocker (128 gr.)
- 5 blad gelatin
- 3 msk. vatten
- 3 dcl. tjock grädde

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg in a saucepan together with the milk, cocoa and sugar.
2. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, dissolve in 3 tablespoons hot water and add, then let the cream simmer over the fire with vigorous whisking.
3. Pour out the cream and whisk until it is cold, then carefully fold in the whipped heavy cream.
4. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let stand on ice 2-3 hours.
5. When the cream is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife and turn out onto a glass dish. Garnish with whipped cream and serve with it as well, if desired.
