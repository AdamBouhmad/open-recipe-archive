## Ingredients

- 3 liters half-cooked potatoes (2 kg.)
- 1 kg. boneless beef
- 3 kg. lean small pork
- 6 hg. fat pork
- 5 msk. salt (75 gr.)
- 2 tsk. white pepper (6 gr.)
- 1 small msk. sugar (15 gr.)
- 1/3 tsk. saltpeter (1 gr.)
- 1 1/2 liters cold water
- approximately 6 m. blue or hook casings
- For rubbing: 4 msk. salt, 1 msk. sugar, 1/4 tsk. saltpeter

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes the day before they are to be used, but do not peel them then.
2. Peel the potatoes and drive them once through a meat grinder.
3. Dry the beef and pork with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut it into pieces and drive it
