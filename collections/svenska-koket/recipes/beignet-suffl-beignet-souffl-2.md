## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 dcl milk
- 60 gr butter
- 1/4 tsk salt
- 1/2 msk sugar (8 gr)
- 75 gr flour
- 5 whole eggs
- 1 kg shortening for cooking

## Directions

1. Boil the butter, milk and seasonings, add the flour with vigorous whisking, and let the batter cook until it releases from the pan and has a golden color.
2. Remove the pan from heat, work in the eggs one at a time, and stir the mixture for 5 minutes.
3. Form the mixture with two teaspoons, dipped in the shortening, into round balls the size of a walnut, which are cooked a beautiful light golden color in moderately hot shortening and placed on absorbent paper to drain and then sprinkled with sugar.
4. Serve very hot, arranged on parchment paper, with chocolate sauce, jam or compote.
