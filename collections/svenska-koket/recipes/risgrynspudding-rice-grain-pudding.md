## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. Carolina rice grains (2 hg.)
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 1/2 lit. milk
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 2 msk. sugar (30 gr.)
- 20 sweet almonds
- 10 bitter almonds
- 40 gr. regular raisins
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 msk. butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the rice grains as in recipe 794. Pour the porridge and immediately blend with the cold butter, seasonings, the scalded and ground almonds, and the well-rinsed, blanched and pitted raisins.
2. When the porridge is cold, stir in the egg yolks and the egg whites beaten until stiff peaks form.
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered and bread-crumb-strewn baking mold and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 1 hour.
4. Serve warm with jam, compote, or fruit sauce.
