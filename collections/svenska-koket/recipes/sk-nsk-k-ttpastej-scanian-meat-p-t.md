## Ingredients

- 1.3 kg. boneless beef
- 212 gr. lean pork
- 650 gr. pork cubes
- 1 large red onion
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.) for the onion
- 3 eggs
- salt and white pepper to taste
- 1/2 liter good, clear broth
- 1 wine glass good vinegar
- 1 medium salt or vinegar cucumber
- 6 olives
- 750 gr. coarse rye flour
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Dry the beef and pork with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and run 6-8 times through a meat grinder.
2. Peel the onion, chop finely and fry in the butter.
3. Work the meat together with the pork cubes, onion, the beaten eggs and seasonings for about 1 hour, during which time add the broth and vinegar, a little at a time.
4. Pit and coarsely chop the olives together with the cucumber and mix well with the meat mixture.
5. Make a dough from the rye flour and water, with which line a large, fluted baking pan.
6. Place the meat mixture in and cover with a lid of dough and bake in the oven for about 3 hours.
7. Let it cool in the form, turn out, cut into beautiful slices and garnish with green salad, hard-boiled eggs and beets.
8. Serve as a middle course with mayonnaise.
