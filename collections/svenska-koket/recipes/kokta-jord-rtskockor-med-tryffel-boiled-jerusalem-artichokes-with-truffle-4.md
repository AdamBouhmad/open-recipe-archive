## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 3-4 truffles
- 2 liters water for soaking
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- butter for serving

## Directions

1. Brush the Jerusalem artichokes clean, scrape them, rinse well, shape them round and let them soak in vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar) for 1 hour.
2. Place them in boiling salted water and let them cook until soft.
3. Remove with a slotted spoon and arrange on a round plate with a broken napkin.
4. Sprinkle with truffle cut into fine strips and serve very hot with stirred butter.
