## Ingredients

- 2 kg pears
- 1 kg lump sugar
- 1/4 liter water

## Directions

1. Place the pears in boiling water and let them cook until they are almost soft.
2. From the sugar and water, boil a clear syrup, which is allowed to cool.
3. Peel the pears, scrape the stems, and cut a cross.
