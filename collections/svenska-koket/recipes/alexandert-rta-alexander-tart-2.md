## Ingredients

- 300 gr. butter
- 300 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. caster sugar
- 1 egg
- 3 cups applesauce
- 250 gr. sweet almond
- 200 gr. caster sugar
- 3-4 egg whites

## Directions

1. Make a regular shortcrust pastry from butter, flour, sugar and egg, and set it out to stiffen.
2. Bake a thin bottom of pastry in a ring mold or cake pan and let it cool.
3. Line the edges of the mold with pastry, and fill the mold with the applesauce.
4. Blanch the almond, grate it and pound it in a mortar together with the sugar and egg whites.
5. Spread the almond mass over the applesauce.
6. Roll out the remaining pastry and cut it into thin strips, which are laid in a lattice pattern over the almond mass.
7. Finally, lay a strip of pastry around the edge of the tart.
8. Bake in moderate oven heat and serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
