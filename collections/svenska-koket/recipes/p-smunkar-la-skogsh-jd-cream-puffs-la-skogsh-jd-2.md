## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 dcl. thin cream
- 1 1/2 tbsp. sugar (23 gr.)
- 6 bitter almonds
- 10 sweet almonds
- 3 1/2 tbsp. butter (70 gr.)
- 110 gr. flour
- 3—4 eggs
- 1 kg. lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Grease a saucepan with round bottom with 1/2 tbsp. of the butter, pour in the cream, and add the sugar and the blanched and grated almonds.
2. Let the cream come to a boil, add the rest of the butter, and whisk in the flour with a steel whisk.
3. Let the mixture cook until it leaves the sides of the pan.
4. Remove the saucepan from heat, work in the eggs one at a time, and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
5. Form the mixture with two tablespoons into round balls the size of a small crust, which are cooked a beautiful golden brown in lard.
6. Form the puffs as smooth as possible, and cook no more than 3 at a time.
7. The lard should not be too hot, or the puffs cannot puff up.
8. Turn them with a slotted spoon, and when the puffs have a golden brown color on both sides, remove them with a slotted spoon and place them on absorbent paper on a warm baking sheet in the oven.
9. Dust with sugar and serve warm with jam, as a dessert, or with coffee or tea.
