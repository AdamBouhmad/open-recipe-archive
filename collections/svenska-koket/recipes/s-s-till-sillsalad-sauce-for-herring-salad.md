## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 dcl. thick cream
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 2-3 tsk. sugar
- 2-3 msk. beetroot vinegar

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks and season with the spices and vinegar.
2. Many prefer to serve the cream unwhipped, but then 3 dcl. cream should be calculated.
