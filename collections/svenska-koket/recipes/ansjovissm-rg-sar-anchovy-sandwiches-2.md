## Ingredients

- 1 twelve-öre wheat bread
- 100 grams butter
- 9 anchovies
- 1 egg white

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into thin slices and on one side spread butter.
2. Clean the anchovies, fillet them, and remove skin and bones.
3. Place three anchovy halves diagonally on each sandwich.
4. Brush them with the lightly whisked egg white, place on a baking sheet and place in weak oven heat to get a nice golden brown color.
5. Serve mainly with vegetarian soups.
