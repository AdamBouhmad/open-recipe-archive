## Ingredients

- 1-2 salt herrings
- 1 onion
- 6 cooked potatoes
- 2 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoon crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The herring is soaked in water the day before, cleaned and freed from skin and bones
2. An omelette mold is greased with butter
3. The potatoes are cut into slices, and a layer of these is placed on the bottom of the mold; on top of this the herring is placed, then the onion, peeled and cut into slices, and on top again
