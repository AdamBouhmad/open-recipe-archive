## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters water
- peel and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- 125 grams red sago grains
- 1 tablespoon preserved plums
- 1 tablespoon preserved cherries or 2 tablespoons cherry, red currant, or raspberry juice
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Bring the water to a boil with the lemon peel and cinnamon.
2. Whisk in the grains so they do not lump, and let them cook until clear.
3. Add the fruit or one of the juice varieties and bring the soup to a boil, then season with sugar to taste.
4. Serve preferably cold with rusks.
