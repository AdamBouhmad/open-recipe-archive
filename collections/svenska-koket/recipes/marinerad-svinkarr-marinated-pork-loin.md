## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. loin with bone
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 bottle red wine
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. Free the loin from most of the fat, rub it with a mixture of salt and pepper and place it in a deep dish.
2. Add the red wine and let the loin soak in it about 12 hours, turning it several times.
3. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the loin in it with the meat side up and brown it in a hot oven, basting it frequently.
4. Add the marinade and let it roast until tender, or about 1 1/2 hours.
5. Cut as in recipe no. 561, serve with a roux-based sauce (see no. 429) mixed with cream and with browned potatoes and lingonberry jelly or preserved rowan berries.
