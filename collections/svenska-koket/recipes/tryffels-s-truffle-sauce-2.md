## Ingredients

- 1 can truffle (1/8 liter)
- truffle liquid
- 1 dcl. Madeira or sherry
- 1 tbsp mondamin starch (15 gr.)
- 5 dcl. strong, brown meat stock
- salt, cayenne

## Directions

1. Dice the truffle into small pieces and cook in the liquid and wine, covered, for approximately 10 minutes.
2. Mix the mondamin starch with 1/2 dcl. of the meat stock, add the remaining meat stock to the mushroom, bring to a boil and thicken with the starch.
3. Let the sauce simmer while stirring for 5 minutes and season with spices.
4. The sauce can also be thickened with butter and flour as in recipe no. 428.
