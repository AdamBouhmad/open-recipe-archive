## Ingredients

- Leftovers of poultry or game (about 250 gr. meat)
- 50 gr. tongue or ham
- 1 hg. mushrooms
- 50 gr. truffle
- 2 1/2 msk. butter (50 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 2 dcl. stock
- 2 dcl. cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 sheets gelatin
- salt, white pepper
- For breading: 4 msk. flour
- 2 eggs
- salt
- 2 tsk. table oil
- 1 kkp crushed bread crumbs
- For cooking: 1 kg. lard

## Directions

1. Free the leftovers of cooked or roasted poultry or game from skin and bones and cut into cubes.
2. Similarly cut the tongue or ham and the mushrooms into cubes.
3. Sauté the mushrooms in half the butter and remove.
4. Sauté the remaining butter with flour for 2 minutes.
5. Add the stock and cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
6. Stir in the meat and mushrooms along with the beaten egg yolks and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in 1 msk. hot water.
7. Season the mixture with spices and treat as in recipe no. 49.
8. Serve the croquettes as a course with peas and poultry sauce.
