## Ingredients

- 2 kg. berries
- 2 kg. powdered sugar
- 1/2 liter water

## Directions

1. Treat the berries as in recipe no. 1030 and place them in a deep bowl.
2. From sugar and water, cook a syrup (see previous recipe) and pour it hot over the berries.
3. Let everything stand until the next day.
4. Then let the jam cook slowly and cook while skimming and shaking until the berries are clear and sink.
5. See previous recipes for further details.
