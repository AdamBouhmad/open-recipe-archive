## Ingredients

- 150 gr. boneless veal
- 30 gr. butter
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped mushrooms or truffle
- 1—1/2 deciliters cream

## Directions

1. The meat is prepared as in the preceding recipe and ground together with the butter 4 times through a meat grinder
2. The forcemeat is then pounded, together with the flour, in a mortar and worked together with the whisked egg, salt, onion and mushroom for approximately 1/2 hour, during which the cream is added, a little at a time
3. The forcemeat is tasted, formed and cooked as in the preceding recipe
