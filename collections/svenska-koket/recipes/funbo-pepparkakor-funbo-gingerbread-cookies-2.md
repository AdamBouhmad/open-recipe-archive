## Ingredients

- 3 dcl. extra thick cream
- 450 gr. brown farina sugar
- 3 dcl. fine, brown syrup
- 2 tbsp. baking soda (20 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. ground ginger
- 1/2 tsp. citron oil
- 1 kg. 2 hg. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, then stir in the sugar, syrup, and baking soda; add the ginger and citron oil, and stir the dough for 15 minutes.
2. Finally work in the flour, and let the dough rest for about 2 hours.
3. Work it well on the work surface, roll out to 3-4 mm thickness and cut with a round cutter into cookies, place them on well-buttered sheets and brush with water just before putting them in the oven.
