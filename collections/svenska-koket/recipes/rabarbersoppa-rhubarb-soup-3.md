## Ingredients

- 3–4 stalks of rhubarb or 1/2 liter preserved rhubarb
- 2 liters water
- peel of 1/4 lemon
- 1–1 1/2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar

## Directions

1. If using fresh rhubarb, rinse it, dry it, and cut into 2 cm. long pieces.
2. Bring the water to a boil, add the rhubarb and lemon peel, and let cook until it falls apart.
3. Strain the soup, return it to the saucepan, and add the crushed sugar.
4. Bring to a boil again and thicken with the potato flour mixed with 1/2 tablespoon water.
5. Let cook for 2 minutes.
6. Whip the egg yolks and powdered sugar in the soup bowl until frothy, then pour in the soup while whisking constantly.
7. Serve with potato flour pastries or rusks. In simpler versions, omit the egg yolks but use more potato flour.
