## Ingredients

- 2 large, salted fat herrings
- 3 deciliters vinegar
- 1 deciliter sugar (85 grams)
- 2 red onions
- 5 white peppercorns
- 10 allspice berries
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 teaspoon oil (optional)

## Directions

1. Let the herring soak in water for about 24 hours, changing the water 3 times.
2. Skin it, remove it, cut it into fillets, and carefully remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets, wrap them in a fish towel, cut them in half, and place them in a stoneware container, alternating with the peeled red onion sliced thinly and the spices.
4. Bring the vinegar and sugar to a boil; when the liquid is cold, add the oil if desired, then pour the liquid over the herring.
5. After 8-10 hours it is ready to serve.
6. Cut the fillets into 8 mm wide pieces crosswise, arrange them in a glass salad dish, cover with the liquid, and garnish with the onion and spices.
