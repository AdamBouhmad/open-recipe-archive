## Ingredients

- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter) or 15 gr. dried morels
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 1 1/2 liters beef broth
- 2-3 egg yolks
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. The mushrooms or morels, which should have been blanched beforehand, are cut into slices and fried in the butter until clear.
2. The mushroom is pressed out of the butter with a spoon and the flour is then fried in the butter for 2 minutes.
3. The boiling broth is added and allowed to simmer with stirring for 5 minutes.
4. The egg yolks are beaten and whisked into the soup, which is then seasoned with the spices and poured over the mushrooms in the soup bowl.
5. Served with cheese croutons or toasted bread.
