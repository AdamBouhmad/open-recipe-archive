## Ingredients

- 110 g butter
- 110 g sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 egg white
- 180 g wheat flour
- 1 g ammonium carbonate
- 3 bitter almonds
- 2 cardamom pods
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 2 tbsp granulated sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with the sugar for 15 minutes, then work in the egg, egg white, the grated almond, the finely crushed cardamom, and lastly the flour, which should be sifted and well mixed with the ammonium carbonate. Roll out the dough to about 2 mm thickness and cut out with a round mold into small cookies, which are brushed with egg white, sprinkled with sugar and cinnamon, and baked golden brown in not too warm an oven.
