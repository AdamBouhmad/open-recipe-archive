## Ingredients

- 2 kg pike
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp vinegar
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tbsp Parmesan cheese (or other sharp cheese)
- 1/2 cup crushed hardtack
- 1 tbsp clarified crayfish butter
- 3 parts cream

## Directions

1. Clean, rinse, and dry the fish well, then fillet it.
2. Remove skin and bones carefully, rub the fish with vinegar and salt, and let it rest for a while.
3. Dry it well with a cloth.
4. Clarify one tablespoon of butter and mix with the egg yolk; brush the fish with this mixture and sprinkle with grated cheese and crushed hardtack.
5. Place it in a baking pan brushed with the remaining butter, pour the melted crayfish butter and cream over it, and bake quickly in a hot oven until golden brown.
6. Baste often with its sauce during cooking.
7. Serve with mushroom sauce mixed with the sauce in which the fish was cooked.
