## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef hare
- 1 hg lard
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 tsp salt (5 g)
- a little white pepper
- 1/2 liter boiling beef stock

## Directions

1. The beef hare, which should have hung 4-8 days according to the season, is prepared and larded (see page 232), rubbed with spices and tied so that it has a nice shape.
2. The butter is browned in an iron pot, the meat is placed in and browned nicely on all sides, diluted with half of the stock and placed in a hot oven to braise for approximately 3/4-1 hour, during which it is basted every 10 minutes and diluted as needed.
3. It is sliced thinly, placed on a warm dish, covered with a couple of tablespoons of sauce, garnished with parsley and vegetables or vegetable salad in shells and served with reduced sauce (see no. 429).
4. The hare can also be diluted with cream instead of stock, served then with browned potatoes and redcurrant jelly.
