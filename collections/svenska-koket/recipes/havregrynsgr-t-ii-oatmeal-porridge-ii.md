## Ingredients

- 5 parts steam-prepared oat groats (2 hg.)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)
- 1 liter water
- 3 parts cream
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats and let them drain.
2. Place the butter in a saucepan, pour in the water, add the groats and let them stand overnight to swell.
3. Bring the porridge to a boil while stirring and then let it simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until the groats are completely soft, or about 1 hour.
4. Toward the end of cooking, add the cream.
5. Season the porridge with salt and serve with milk or cream.
6. If the groats are not soaked overnight in water, the porridge should cook 1 1/2 to 2 hours. The cream may be omitted and the porridge cooked in plain water or plain milk if desired.
