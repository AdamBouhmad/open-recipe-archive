## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. melon
- 2 dcl. strösocker (170 gr.)
- 1 dcl. vatten
- 1—2 msk. fin rom
- saften av 1/2 citron

## Directions

1. The melon, which should be ripe, is cut into 1 cm thick slices and these are cut into 5 cm long pieces.
2. Place the cut melon in 1 liter of boiling water and cook for 2-3 minutes, then remove with a slotted spoon and drain on cloth.
3. Make a clear, syrupy syrup from the sugar, water, rum and lemon juice.
4. Add the melon and cook until it is soft and clear.
5. Place it in a compote bowl; if desired, cook down the syrup and pour it over.
6. Serve cold with biscuits.
