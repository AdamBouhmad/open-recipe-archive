## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg wheat flour
- 1 tbsp granulated sugar
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 liter water
- 4 tbsp butter or margarine
- 50 gr pressed yeast

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm water.
2. Sift the wheat flour and set aside 2–3 tbsp for baking.
3. Pour the rest into a bowl and mix with the sugar and salt; add the lukewarm water with vigorous stirring.
4. Stir in the melted and slightly cooled butter and the dissolved yeast, then beat the dough until smooth and shiny.
5. The dough should be quite firm.
6. Set it in a warm place to rise to double its size and then shape into French breads, place them on well-greased trays to rise again.
7. Bake the breads in good oven heat.
