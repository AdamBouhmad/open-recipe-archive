## Ingredients

- 300 gr. washed butter
- 150 gr. sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 100 gr. sweet almond
- 325 gr. flour

## Directions

1. Work butter and sugar until white and fluffy
2. Add the egg yolks one at a time
3. Add the blanched and ground almond
4. Lastly add the flour
5. Work the dough until it becomes even
6. Let it rest in a cool place for about 2 hours before use
7. This dough cannot be rolled out but is pressed directly into molds or piped through a pastry bag
