## Ingredients

- 1 kg small pickling onions
- 2 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt for blanching
- 2 liters vinegar
- 1 hectogram granulated sugar
- 3 grams ginger (optional)
- 2 grams mace (optional)
- 2 grams white pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the unpeeled onions in boiling salted water and let cook 2 minutes, then remove and place immediately in cold water.
2. Peel the onions and let them soak in salted water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons salt) for approximately 6 hours.
3. Heat 1 liter of the vinegar and add the onions, letting them cook 5 minutes for small onions or 8-10 minutes for larger ones.
4. If using spices, boil them for a few minutes in the vinegar and strain the liquid before adding the onions, though the onions will be whiter if spices are omitted.
5. Place the onions in a jar, cover with a quadruple layer of cloth and a plate, and let stand until the next day, then drain off the vinegar.
6. Cook the remaining vinegar with the sugar and let cool.
7. Fill clean glass jars with the onions, pour the cooled vinegar over them so it stands well above the onions, bind the jars well and store in a cool place.
