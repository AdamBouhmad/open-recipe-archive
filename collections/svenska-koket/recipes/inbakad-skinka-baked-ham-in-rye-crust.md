## Ingredients

- 1 lightly smoked ham, approximately 6 kg
- 1 kg coarse rye flour
- water

## Directions

1. Soak the ham in plenty of cold water for 1 day and then dry it well with a cloth.
2. Make a firm dough from the rye flour and water, and coat the ham evenly with it. The dough layer on the meat side should be thicker than on the skin side.
3. Place the ham in a roasting pan with the skin side up, set it in a hot oven and let it roast in even heat for 2 hours without opening the oven door.
4. Place the ham on a plate and immediately remove the charred dough.
5. Carefully collect the melted fat that accumulates on the plate and use it for frying potatoes to serve with the ham.
6. Ham prepared this way becomes very juicy and flavorful.
7. A salted ham can be prepared in the same way.
