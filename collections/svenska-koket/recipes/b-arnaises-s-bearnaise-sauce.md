## Ingredients

- 1 tsp minced shallot
- 1/2 gr white pepper
- 2 gr salt
- 4 tbsp good vinegar
- 5 egg yolks
- 180 gr melted butter
- cayenne
- 20 gr chopped tarragon
- 3 tbsp good meat stock or fish broth

## Directions

1. Mix the shallot, pepper, salt, and vinegar in a sauce pan and reduce until 1/3 remains.
2. Move the pan to the side and add the beaten egg yolks.
3. Let the sauce simmer over strong stirring.
4. Gradually add the melted, slightly cooled butter.
5. Strain the sauce through fine cloth.
6. Season with cayenne and finish with the chopped tarragon and, if desired, meat stock or fish broth.
7. Heat in a water bath until lukewarm and serve immediately.
8. If heated too much, it will break.
