## Ingredients

- 1 small cabbage head
- 3 liters water for blanching
- 1 tablespoon salt for blanching
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 2 tablespoons syrup
- 3 liters stock or pork broth
- 10 allspice berries
- 8 white peppercorns
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove all green leaves from the cabbage head, loosen the others from the core, cut away the thickest part of the main vein
2. Blanch the cabbage (place in boiling, salted water and cook without lid) for 5 minutes, remove with a slotted spoon, let drain on a sieve, wrap in a cloth and cut into small cubes
3. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the cabbage together with the syrup and brown well with frequent stirring
4. Add the boiling stock and spices and let the soup cook with the lid tightly closed for about 3 hours
5. Season with salt and serve with meatballs, sausage or pork
