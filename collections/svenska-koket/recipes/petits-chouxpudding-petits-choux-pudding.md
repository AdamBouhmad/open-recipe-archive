## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 5 tbsp. flour (50 gr.)
- 4 dcl. thin cream
- 4 eggs
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- Rind and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 dcl. sugar for the mold
- 2 tbsp. boiling water for the mold

## Directions

1. Place the sugar in a buttered, slightly warm frying pan, brown it lightly, then add the boiling water, and use this to line a fireproof mold. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, dilute with the cream, and let the mixture cook for 5 minutes, then pour it out and let it cool. Stir in the egg yolks one at a time together with the sugar, the finely grated lemon rind and lemon juice, and stir the mixture for 10 minutes. Finally, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks. Pour the mixture into the lined mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
