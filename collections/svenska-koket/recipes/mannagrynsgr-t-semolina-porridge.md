## Ingredients

- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters milk
- 1 1/2 cups semolina groats
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 4 bitter almonds
- 8 sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a pot, pour in the milk and bring to a boil
2. Shake in the groats and let the porridge cook with stirring about 1 hour
3. Season with salt, sugar and the scalded and grated almonds
