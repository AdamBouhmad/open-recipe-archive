## Ingredients

- celery stalks
- salt
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the stalks as in the previous recipe, bind them in bundles like asparagus, place them in boiling, salted water and let them cook for 10–15 minutes.
2. Let them drain, arrange them on a dish with a broken napkin and serve them warm with stirred butter.
