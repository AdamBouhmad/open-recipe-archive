## Ingredients

- 750 gr. spinach
- 3 liters water
- 4 tsp. salt (20 gr.)
- 80 gr. melted butter

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach (remove the coarse stalks), then rinse it very thoroughly, place on a sieve and let it drain.
2. Blanch it over fresh heat (place in boiling salted water) for 5-7 minutes, immediately place in cold water to preserve the color, place on a sieve and let it drain well.
3. Place on a warm platter, pour the hot butter over it and serve with fried fish or meat.
