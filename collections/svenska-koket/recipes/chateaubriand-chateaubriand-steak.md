## Ingredients

- 1¼ kg beef tenderloin
- ¼ tsk white pepper
- ⅛ tbsp salt
- 4 tbsp butter
- 2 parts broth
- 1 bunch parsley
- juice of ⅛ lemon

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Remove the outer membranes from the tenderloin, whereupon it is cut into approximately 3 cm thick slices; these are lightly beaten and sprinkled with pepper and salt.
3. Rinse the parsley, clean and chop it finely and work it together with the lemon juice into 1 tbsp softened butter.
4. Heat the remainder of the butter in a frying pan, fry the steaks quickly on both sides and place them on a warm platter.
5. Whisk out the frying pan with the boiling broth, and pour the sauce over the steaks.
6. Place a dab of parsley butter on each steak.
7. Chateaubriand should be served very hot.
