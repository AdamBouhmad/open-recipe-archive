## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef chuck
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 2 red onions or 1 Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 liter potatoes
- 4 dcl good beef broth
- 1 tbsp flour (10 g)
- Pie dough: 1 hg cooked, cold grated potato
- 1 hg butter
- 1 hg flour

## Directions

1. If a beef casserole is not available, the beef can be arranged in a heat-resistant pie dish with a dough cover, and is then called beef pie. It is prepared as in recipe 485, with or without potatoes. In the latter case, potatoes are served with the pie. It can also be supplemented with cooked ham or veal kidney, according to taste. Whisk the broth with flour and add last.
2. For the pie dough mix all ingredients on the work table, and roll out the dough to 1 cm thickness.
3. Cut a cover for the dish, brush the edges of the dish with water, and press the dough cover firmly on. With a fork or larding needle make 4-6 holes in the dough so that the gases formed during baking may escape.
4. Brush the pie with egg white and garnish with remaining dough cut into pretty figures, which are also brushed with egg white.
5. Bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 1 1/2 hours. Serve from the dish.
