## Ingredients

- 2 hg. spinach
- 1 msk. butter
- 3 msk. flour
- 1 1/2 lit. broth
- 1/4 lit. milk (or 3 tbsp. cream)
- salt, white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- (sugar)

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach, rinse it, let it drain, and chop it finely.
2. Melt butter and flour together, add the broth, add the spinach and let it cook until it is soft.
3. Pour in the hot milk or cream, and season the soup.
4. Whisk the egg yolks in a soup bowl, and pour the soup in while whisking vigorously.
