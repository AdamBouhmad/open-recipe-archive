## Ingredients

- 2 large or 3 smaller celeriac roots
- 1.5 liters water
- 1 tablespoon flour (10g)
- 1 egg
- 200ml cream
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 200g beef
- 50g veal
- 50g lard
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 chopped pickled onions or 1/2 tablespoon chopped Portuguese onion
- 3 tablespoons butter (60g) for frying
- 300ml broth

## Directions

1. Peel the celeriac, rinse well, place in boiling salted water and cook for about 1 hour, or until almost tender, then remove and let cool.
2. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and pass 3 times through a meat grinder together with the lard. Then add the flour, egg, seasonings and cream little by little, and work the forcemeat for about 1/2 hour. Stir in the onion and taste the forcemeat.
3. Cut a lid out of the celeriac and hollow it from above. Fill it with the forcemeat, place the lid on top and tie the celeriac with twine.
