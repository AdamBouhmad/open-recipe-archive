## Ingredients

- 1 kg. apples, cut into pieces
- 1/2 liter water
- Rhubarb, equal weight to the apple puree
- Sugar, equal weight to the mass

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the apples as in the preceding recipe.
2. Weigh the passed puree, and take equal weight of raw rhubarb.
3. Wash the stalks, cut them into small pieces, and mix with the puree.
4. Weigh the mass and add 625 gr. granulated sugar per kg.
5. Cook the marmalade quickly with vigorous stirring to the bottom until bubbles form on the surface or it falls in flakes from the spoon.
6. Pour into small warm jars and treat as apple sauce (see preceding recipe).
