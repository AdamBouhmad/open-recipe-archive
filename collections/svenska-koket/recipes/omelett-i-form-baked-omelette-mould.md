## Ingredients

- 1 tin chanterelles (1/4 litre)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1/2 decilitre chanterelle stock
- 3 decilitres cream
- salt, white pepper, sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 3 decilitres milk
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the mould

## Directions

1. Pat the chanterelles in a cloth, cut them into slices, and sauté in butter until clear.
2. Squeeze the mushroom out of the butter with a spoon.
3. Sauté the flour with butter for 2 minutes, then add the chanterelle stock and cream. Let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Add the mushroom back, season the ragout with the spices, place it in a buttered omelette mould.
5. Whisk the egg mixture well and pour it over the ragout.
6. Bake the omelette in the oven and serve at the smorgasbord. Instead of the ragout, smoked ham cut into cubes and sautéed in butter, or grated cheese can be used.
