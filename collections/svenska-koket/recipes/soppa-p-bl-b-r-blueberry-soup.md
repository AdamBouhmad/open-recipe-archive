## Ingredients

- 1 liter fresh blueberries or 4 deciliters dried blueberries
- 2 liters water
- peel from 1/4 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm.)
- 1 1/2–2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato starch (80 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, rinse in a colander and drain.
2. Place in boiling water with lemon peel and cinnamon and cook until loose.
3. Strain the berries from the juice, add sugar to the liquid, bring to a boil and thicken with potato starch mixed in 1/2 tablespoon cold water.
4. Cook for 2 minutes, season and allow to cool.
5. Serve with dumplings, whipped cream and rusks or biscuits.
6. If using dried blueberries, rinse them thoroughly in lukewarm water and let them soak overnight in the water in which they will be cooked.
