## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized salad heads
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 msk. sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Break apart the salad heads, remove the stems, rinse the leaves well and shake them in a cloth to dry them.
2. Cut the largest leaves into pieces, and place the salad in a crystal bowl.
3. Press out the lemon juice, mix it with the sugar, and pour it over the salad.
4. Serve with roasted meat.
