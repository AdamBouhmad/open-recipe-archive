## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter small potatoes
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 3-4 deciliters thin cream
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- a little Cayenne
- 1-2 tablespoons capers
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped chervil or cress

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush them clean and cook with skins on in salted water; peel and let cool, then cut into thin, even slices, which are placed in a salad bowl. The potatoes can also be cut into cubes.
2. Butter and flour are fried together for 2 minutes, the cream is added, and the sauce is allowed to boil for 5 minutes.
3. The sauce is seasoned with vinegar and spices and poured over the potato slices, which are mixed carefully with the sauce.
4. The salad is allowed to cool, garnished with the capers and chervil or cress.
5. Serve as the preceding salad.
