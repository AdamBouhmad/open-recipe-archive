## Ingredients

- sugar
- water
- egg yolks
- heavy cream
- 75 gr. ground pistachios

## Directions

1. Cook sugar and water together
2. Remove the pan from heat and add egg yolks one at a time while whisking
3. Let the cream simmer over the fire with vigorous whisking until it is thick and frothy
4. Remove the pan from heat and whisk the cream until it is completely cold
5. Whip the heavy cream to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the cream
6. Color the cream light green and mix with 75 gr. ground pistachios
7. Fill the cream into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and freeze for 4-5 hours
