## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. lamb breast or shoulder
- 1 litre raw potatoes
- 2 carrots
- 1—2 leeks
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 8 decilitres water
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Treat the meat and potatoes as in the previous recipe.
2. Peel the carrots and cut them into slices or oblong pieces.
3. Clean the leeks and cut them into fine strips.
4. Prepare the stew as in the previous recipe, place on a warm serving dish, sprinkle with parsley and serve very hot.
