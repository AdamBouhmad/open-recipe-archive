## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 dcl. milk
- 60 gr. butter
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tbsp. sugar (8 gr.)
- 75 gr. flour
- 5 whole eggs
- 1 kg. lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Boil together the butter, milk and seasonings.
2. Add the flour while whisking vigorously, and let the mixture cook until it leaves the sides of the pan and has a yellow color.
3. Remove the saucepan from heat, work in the eggs one at a time, and stir the mixture for 5 minutes.
4. Form the mixture with two teaspoons, dipped in the lard, into round balls the size of a walnut, which are cooked a beautiful light yellow in moderately hot lard and placed on absorbent paper to drain and dusted with sugar.
5. Serve very hot, placed on pastry paper, with chocolate sauce, jam or compote.
