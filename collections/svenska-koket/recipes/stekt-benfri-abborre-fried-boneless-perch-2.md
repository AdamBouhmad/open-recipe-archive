## Ingredients

- 2 kg perch
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 1 cup finely crushed hardtack
- 4 tbsp butter
- 3/4 liter boiling water
- fish stock
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, rinse, and dry the fish well.
2. Cut it open along the back and remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rub the fillets with vinegar and salt and roll them in finely crushed hardtack.
4. Butter a baking pan with half of the butter and place the perch fillets in it; place the remaining butter in flakes on top.
5. Put it in the oven, and when browned, add the fish stock (made from the head, skin, and bones) and let the perch cook for about 20 minutes.
6. Whisk the cream into the pan and pour the sauce over the fish.
