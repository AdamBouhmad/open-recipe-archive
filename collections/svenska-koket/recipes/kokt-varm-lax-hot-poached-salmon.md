## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 liters water
- 1 1/2 deciliters vinegar
- 40 grams coarse salt
- 1 carrot
- 1 red onion
- 1 bay leaf
- 4 parsley stems
- 7 white peppercorns
- 1 salmon weighing 2 kg
- 1 lemon
- parsley for garnishing

## Directions

1. Prepare the poaching liquid with water, vinegar, coarse salt, carrot, red onion, bay leaf, parsley stems, and white peppercorns
2. Place the cleaned salmon in the poaching liquid
3. Bring to a boil and skim well
4. Move to the side of the stove and allow the salmon to finish cooking gently with the water barely simmering
5. Cook until the salmon is done
6. Remove the salmon and place on a serving platter
7. Garnish with lemon and parsley
