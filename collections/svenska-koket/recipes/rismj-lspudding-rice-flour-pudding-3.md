## Ingredients

- 1/2 lit. good milk
- 60 gr. butter
- 1 dcl. rice flour (67 gr.)
- Peel of half a lemon or 6 bitter almonds
- 2 msk. sugar (30 gr.)
- 4-5 eggs
- 3 dcl. granulated sugar for the mold (255 gr.)
- 2 msk. boiling water for the mold

## Directions

1. Place the sugar in a somewhat warm frying pan, buttered with 1/2 tsk. butter, and stir with a wooden spoon until it melts and turns light brown, then add the water and use this to coat a baking form.
2. Boil the milk with the butter, whisk in the flour while at a rolling boil and let the mixture have a good boil, then pour it out and let it cool.
3. Add the grated lemon peel or the blanched and ground almonds, sugar, and egg yolks, one at a time.
4. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten until stiff.
5. Pour the mixture into the coated mold, cover with buttered paper, and let the pudding bake in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour.
6. Turn out of the mold and serve warm with vanilla or lemon sauce, if desired.
