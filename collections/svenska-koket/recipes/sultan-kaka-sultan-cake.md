## Ingredients

- 230 gr. butter
- 230 gr. icing sugar
- 6 eggs
- 1 hg. preserved orange peel
- 1 hg. candied fruit
- 130 gr. sultana raisins
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 75 gr. sweet almonds
- 50 gr. pistachios
- 275 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tsk. baking powder
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.) for the pan
- 3 msk. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, let it cool and beat until it becomes white and fluffy.
2. Add the sugar and egg yolks, one at a time.
3. Add the finely chopped orange peel and candied fruit, the cleaned and blanched raisins, the finely grated lemon peel, the blanched and ground sweet almonds, and the blanched and finely chopped pistachios.
4. Sift the flour and mix well with the baking powder, then fold in.
5. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Pour the batter into a large buttered and breadcrumb-lined ring mold and bake in a not-too-hot oven for approximately 1.5 hours.
