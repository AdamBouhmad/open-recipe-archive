## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter fresh or 1 liter canned gooseberries
- 1 liter water
- 1 1/2 to 2 cups crushed sugar
- 2 to 3 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the gooseberries and cook together with water and sugar until the berries are soft.
2. Thicken the cream with potato flour mixed in a little cold water, bring to a short boil, and pour out to cool.
