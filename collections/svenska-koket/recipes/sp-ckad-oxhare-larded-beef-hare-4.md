## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef hare
- 1 hg fat
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 tsp salt (5 g)
- a little white pepper
- 1/2 liter boiling broth

## Directions

1. The beef hare, which should have hung 4–8 days depending on the season, is prepared and larded (see page 232), rubbed with the seasonings and tied so it takes on a beautiful shape.
2. The butter is browned in an iron pot, the meat is placed in and browned beautifully on all sides, moistened with half the broth and set in a hot oven to braise approximately 3/4–1 hour, during which it is basted every 10 minutes and moistened as needed.
3. Sliced thin, laid on a warm platter, covered with a couple of tablespoons of sauce, garnished with parsley and vegetables or vegetable salad in shells and served with reduced sauce (see recipe 429).
4. The hare can also be moistened with cream instead of broth, then served with browned potatoes and currant jelly.
