## Ingredients

- 1 hectogram veal sweetbread
- 1 3/4 liters clear broth
- 4 parsley stems
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 g)
- 2 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1/2 deciliter madeira
- salt
- cayenne
- 2-3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The veal sweetbread should soak in water for 1 hour to draw out the blood, then blanch it (place in cold, lightly salted water and let it cook 10 minutes) and cool in cold water.
2. Heat the broth with the parsley, add the sweetbread and let it cook until it is white and firm, about 45 minutes. Remove it, let it cool and cut into fine dice, which are placed in the soup bowl. Strain the broth.
3. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the broth little by little and let it cook with stirring for 5 minutes. Season the soup with lemon juice, wine and spices. Beat the egg yolks and whisk them into the soup, which is then poured over the sweetbread in the soup bowl.
