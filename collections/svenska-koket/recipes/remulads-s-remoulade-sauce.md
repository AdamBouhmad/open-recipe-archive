## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 3 raw egg yolks
- 8 skinless and boneless anchovies
- 1/8 part salad oil
- 1 tablespoon chopped chives (optional)
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- 1 tablespoon French mustard
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar or wine vinegar
- 1 part strong meat stock
- 3 tablespoons pickles
- 2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. The hard-boiled egg yolks are pressed together with the anchovies, and this is mixed with the raw egg yolks
2. The salad oil is added slowly with constant stirring together with all the spices, the finely chopped chives and pickles
3. Finally the cream whipped to stiff peaks is stirred in
