## Ingredients

- 2 kg pike
- 1 1/2 msk salt
- 1 tsk vinegar
- 3 msk butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 msk Parmesan cheese (or other sharp cheese)
- 1/2 kkp crushed hardtack
- 1 msk clarified crayfish butter
- 3 del cream

## Directions

1. Clean, rinse, and dry the fish thoroughly, then fillet it.
2. Remove the skin and bones carefully and rub the fish with vinegar and salt, allowing it to rest for a while.
3. Dry it thoroughly with a cloth.
4. Clarify one tablespoon of butter and mix it with the egg yolk; brush the fish with this mixture and sprinkle with the grated cheese and crushed hardtack.
5. Place it in a long pan brushed with the remaining butter, pour the melted crayfish butter and cream over it, and fry quickly in a hot oven until it takes on a golden-brown color.
6. Baste it frequently with its sauce during cooking.
7. Serve with mushroom sauce mixed with the sauce in which the fish was cooked.
