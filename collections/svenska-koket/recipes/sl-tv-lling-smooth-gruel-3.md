## Ingredients

- 5-6 tbsp. flour (50-60 gr.)
- 2 liters milk
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)
- salt
- sugar
- 2 egg yolks (optional)

## Directions

1. Stir the flour with 1/4 liter of the milk and let it stand to swell for about 2 hours.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, pour in the rest of the milk, let it boil up and add the flour mixture while whisking, after which let the gruel boil with continued whisking for 8-10 minutes.
3. Season with salt and sugar and serve immediately.
4. If desired, add the whisked egg yolks and let the gruel simmer with vigorous whisking.
