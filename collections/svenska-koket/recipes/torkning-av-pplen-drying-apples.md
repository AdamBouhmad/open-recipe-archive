## Ingredients

- Apples
- Lemon solution (10-15 grams citric acid per liter of water)

## Directions

1. Peel the apples. To prevent them from losing their color, place them in lemon solution (10-15 grams citric acid per liter of water) as they are peeled. If they are large and attractive, remove the cores with an apple corer (made of tin) and cut the apples into rings. Otherwise, cut them into wedges and remove the cores. Spread the fruit on drying racks or, if these are not available, on trays covered with smooth paper. Place the racks or trays immediately into a very weak oven heat, preferably after a large baking, and let the fruit remain until it is completely dry, up to a full day.
