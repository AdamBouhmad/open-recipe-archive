## Ingredients

- 8 deciliters cooked beans
- 8 small fresh carrots
- 8 deciliters water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 3 deciliters bean cooking liquid
- 3 deciliters milk
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 3 teaspoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the sprouts; if the beans are very mature, peel them as well.
2. Rinse the carrots, scrape them, rinse again, dry and cut into slices or cubes.
3. Bring the water to a boil with salt, add the beans and let them cook about 15 minutes, then add the carrots and let the vegetables cook until they are tender.
4. Drain the liquid and save it.
5. Fry the butter and flour 2 minutes, add the liquid and milk, and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes, add the beans together with the parsley, and season the stew with sugar.
6. Serve with boiled ham or fried pork.
