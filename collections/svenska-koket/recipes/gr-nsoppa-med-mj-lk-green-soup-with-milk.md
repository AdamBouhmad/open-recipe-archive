## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 0.5 celery root
- 1 small leek
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams)
- 1 liter boiling water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons fresh peas
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 grams)
- 1 liter milk
- 0.5 hectogram spinach
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2–3 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 1 tablespoon cold butter (20 grams)

## Directions

1. Braise the roots in the usual way and cut them into small cubes, cut the leek into fine strips, clean the cauliflower, divide it into florets and let it soak in vinegar-mixed water for 1 hour.
2. Fry the vegetables and leek well in half the butter, place them in the boiling water with salt added, and let them cook until they are half tender; then add the rinsed cauliflower and peas and cook everything until done.
3. Place the vegetables in the soup bowl with a skimmer and use the broth for the soup.
