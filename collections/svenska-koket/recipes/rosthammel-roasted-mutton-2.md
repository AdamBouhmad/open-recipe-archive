## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. French mutton
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 1 carrot
- 1 shallot
- 2 deciliters meat gravy or broth

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung so that it is tender, is beaten with a wet club, dried with a cloth, wrung out in hot water, rubbed with a mixture of the salt and pepper, and brushed with parsley butter, which is made from 2 tablespoons of the butter as in the preceding recipe.
2. In a small roasting pan or frying pan, place the rest of the butter, the scraped carrot cut into slices, and the peeled shallot cut into wedges.
3. Place the meat in and roast in a warm oven for about 1 hour, during which it is basted frequently with the butter in the pan so that it does not dry or burn.
4. A quarter hour before it is done, add the boiling meat gravy.
5. Place on a warm platter and carve at the table. Serve with vegetable or potato pastries and the meat gravy, which is strained and skimmed.
