## Ingredients

- 7 hectograms potato puree
- 40-50 gr truffle
- 1 egg for brushing and breading
- 50 gr sweet almond
- 1 kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the potato puree as in recipe n:o 680 and mix with truffle cut into fine dice.
2. Treat the puree as in n:o 680 and roll into the shape of apricots.
3. Dip these in beaten egg and roll in almond that has been blanched and cut into the finest slivers.
4. Fry beautifully golden brown in hot lard, let drain on absorbent paper, and serve very hot as a garnish around roasted meat.
