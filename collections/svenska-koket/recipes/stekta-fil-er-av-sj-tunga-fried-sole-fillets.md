## Ingredients

- 3 sole fish, approximately 5 hg each
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- For breading: 1 1/2 dcl milk
- 3 tablespoon flour (30 g)
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons cooking oil
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 cup fine breadcrumbs
- For frying: 1/2 kg lard
- 1 lemon
- parsley
- remoulade sauce or maitre d'hôtel butter
- small boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the sole as in recipe 371
2. Rub the fillets with salt and pepper and let them lie on top of each other for 1 hour, then dry them well with a fish cloth
3. Dip in milk, turn in flour, then dip in the well beaten eggs mixed with cooking oil and seasoned with a little salt and pepper, and finally turn in the breadcrumbs
4. Press with a knife so the breading adheres and let them lie 1 hour to harden
5. Fry them in lard as in recipe 329, place on a broken napkin or parchment paper, pour over with a little melted butter mixed with lemon juice, garnish with nicely cut lemon wedges and fried parsley and serve with remoulade sauce or maitre d'hôtel butter and small boiled potatoes
