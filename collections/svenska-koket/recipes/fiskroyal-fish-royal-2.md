## Ingredients

- 120 grams fillet of sole or whiting
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon boiling water
- 2 tablespoons cold béchamel sauce
- 1 1/2 deciliters thick cream
- salt, white pepper
- 1 egg
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the fillets, wrap them in cloth, rub with salt and cut into cubes.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, add the fish cubes, pour in the water, let them cook gently with the lid on for 8-10 minutes, then remove and let cool.
3. Pound the fish in a stone mortar until fine, add the sauce and pound the mixture again.
4. Prepare the royal as in the preceding recipe.
5. Bake and serve as in recipe 306.
