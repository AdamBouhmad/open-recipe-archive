## Ingredients

- 80 grams butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 3 egg yolks
- sugar
- 2 parts water
- lemon juice
- salt

## Directions

1. Three quarters of the butter and the flour, egg yolks and a little sugar are whisked together cold
2. The mixture is placed over very low heat, and the cold water is added gradually, while the pot is occasionally lifted off—all with constant whisking
3. The sauce must not boil, only thicken
4. When it is smooth and glossy and sufficiently thick, it is lifted off, and the remaining butter is stirred in small portions together with the lemon juice
5. The sauce is carefully seasoned and served very hot
