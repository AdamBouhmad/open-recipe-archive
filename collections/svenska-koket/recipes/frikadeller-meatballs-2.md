## Ingredients

- 200 grams lean beef
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon crushed bread crumbs
- 0.25 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon potato flour
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small pieces and pass through a meat grinder three times and mix well together with the butter.
2. Mix the meat mixture with the crushed bread crumbs, milk, potato flour and seasonings and work until smooth.
3. Form small meatballs from the mixture, which are cooked in salted water or weak broth for about 10 minutes.
4. Serve with cabbage or strong broth soups in general.
