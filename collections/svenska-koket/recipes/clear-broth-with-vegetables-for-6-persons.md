## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters strong, clear broth
- 2 small carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 0.5 celery
- 1 leek
- 12 small Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 small cauliflower head
- water
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the roots, scrape and cut into fine strips and place in boiling lightly salted water along with the cauliflower.
2. Bring the broth to a boil, add the cooked roots and cauliflower, and season the soup.
