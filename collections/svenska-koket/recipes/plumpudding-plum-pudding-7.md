## Ingredients

- 1 hg. fresh apples
- 1 hg. malaga raisins
- 1 hg. sultana raisins
- 1 hg. currants
- 60 gr. preserved orange peel (3 dcl. maraschino liqueur)
- 225 gr. beef suet
- 225 gr. bread crumbs
- 120 gr. sugar
- juice and zest of 1/2 orange
- juice and zest of 1/4 lemon
- 10 gr. ground ginger
- 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp. cardamom
- 1/4 tsp. ground cloves
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. The milk is brought to a boil with the vanilla pod and sugar, the bread is soaked in it, and the mass is passed through a fine sieve and allowed to cool.
2. Then the whole eggs and egg yolks are added, and the mass is worked well, after which the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in.
3. The mass is poured into a buttered and breadcrumb-sprinkled ring mold and cooked in a water bath for about 1 hour.
4. Unmold and serve with wine, vanilla or fruit sauce.
