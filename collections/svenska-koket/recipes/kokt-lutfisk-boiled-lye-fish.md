## Ingredients

- 2 kg lye fish
- 4 tbsp salt
- 2 liters water

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the fish well.
2. Cut it into pieces, lay it in a cloth with salt between each layer, and tie the cloth closed.
3. Place it over heat in boiling water and let it simmer slowly without a lid until it becomes soft, or 10-15 minutes.
4. Serve with clarified butter and green peas or with milk sauce and potatoes.
