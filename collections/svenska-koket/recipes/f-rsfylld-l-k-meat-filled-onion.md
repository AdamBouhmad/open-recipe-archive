## Ingredients

- 3 large Portuguese onions
- 100 g beef
- 100 g veal
- 50 g lard
- 1 tablespoon crushed bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter for frying

## Directions

1. Peel the onion and parboil it until it is almost soft.
2. Remove it, cut out a lid, and hollow out the onion.
3. Fill it with well-worked and seasoned ground meat, and place the lid on.
4. Heat the butter in a pot, and let the onions fry until they are soft and the meat is cooked through.
5. Remove the onion and serve.
