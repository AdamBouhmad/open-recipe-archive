## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 2.5 deciliters cream
- 1 teaspoon powdered sugar
- jam
- 1 teaspoon butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks and whites.
2. Whisk the egg yolks together with the cream and sugar.
3. Fold in the egg whites, which have been beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Immediately pour the batter into a well-buttered omelet pan and bake until light yellow in ordinary oven heat.
5. Turn the omelet out immediately, spread jam on one half, and fold the other half over it.
