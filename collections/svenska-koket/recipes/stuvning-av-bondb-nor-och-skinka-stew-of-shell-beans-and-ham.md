## Ingredients

- 1 liter shelled beans
- 1 1/2 lit water
- 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 gr)
- 4 tbsp flour (40 gr)
- 3 dcl bean broth
- 3 dcl milk
- 3 hg smoked ham
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 2 tbsp finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the sprouts; if the beans are very mature, peel them as well
2. Bring water to a boil with salt, add beans and let cook until soft
3. Strain in a colander and reserve the broth
4. Brown butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the bean broth and milk and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes
5. Add the ham cut into cubes and bring the stew to a boil
6. Season with the spices and finely chopped parsley and serve as a breakfast dish
