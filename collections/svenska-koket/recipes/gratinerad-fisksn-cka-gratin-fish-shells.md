## Ingredients

- Leftovers of cooked fish, approximately 3 dcl.
- 1 can of lobster (1/4 litre)
- 6-8 mushrooms
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 1/2 litre boiling milk
- salt, white pepper
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.) for the shells
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Free the fish from all bones and cut into fine cubes.
2. Cut the lobster into small pieces and the mushrooms into thin slices.
3. Fry the mushrooms in the butter until clear, then remove with a spoon.
4. Fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes and season with spices.
5. Grease six shell dishes well with butter, then add fish, lobster and mushroom.
6. Pour the sauce over the contents in the shells, sprinkle with crushed rusks and drizzle a little clarified butter over.
7. Gratinize and serve as in the preceding recipe.
