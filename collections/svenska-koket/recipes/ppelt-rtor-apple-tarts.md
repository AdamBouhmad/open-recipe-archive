## Ingredients

- 125 grams butter
- 125 grams wheat flour
- 3 1/4-4 tablespoons water
- 1 liter apples
- 1/2-2/3 cup granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as simple butter dough (no. 549)
2. Roll out the dough to approximately 1 1/2 cm thickness, cut it into two equal parts, and place one part on a well-cleaned baking sheet
3. Peel the apples, remove the cores, slice the apples into thin slices and spread them out on the dough
4. Sprinkle the sugar over and place the other dough half on top
5. Brush the cake with water, sprinkle over with sugar, and bake in a hot oven
6. Cut into oblong squares and serve with whipped cream
