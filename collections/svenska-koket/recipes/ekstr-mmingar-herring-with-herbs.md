## Ingredients

- 1 barrel Baltic herring
- 1 large bunch parsley
- 1 bunch dill
- salt
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed hardtack
- 3 tbsp butter

## Directions

1. Clean the Baltic herring, fillet it and remove the backbone, and rub in a little salt
2. Chop the parsley and dill, and place approximately 1 tsp of it on each Baltic herring
3. Lay them together two by two with the skin side facing out
4. Bread the prepared herrings in egg and crushed hardtack and fry in butter
5. Place on a warm serving dish and serve
