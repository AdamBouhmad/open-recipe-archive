## Ingredients

- 105 gr. butter
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 160 gr. wheat flour
- 1 pinch potassium bicarbonate salt
- 100 gr. sweet almond
- 10 bitter almonds
- 110 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 tbsp. water
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Prepare the shortcrust pastry in the usual manner.
2. Blanch the almonds, grate or grind through an almond mill, pound finely together with the sugar and a little water, and work in the egg well.
3. Line small fluted molds with shortcrust pastry and fill with almond mixture.
4. Place a cross of shortcrust pastry on each pastry, then bake in moderate oven heat.
5. Turn out as soon as they are done.
