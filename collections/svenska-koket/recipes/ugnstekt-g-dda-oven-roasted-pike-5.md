## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs
- 80 gr. butter
- 3 dcl. boiling broth
- 1/2 dcl. white wine
- Mushroom sauce
- Pressed potatoes
- Applesauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike as described on page 171
2. Leave the head on but remove the gills
3. Rub with salt and let lie for 1 hour
4. Dry well, dip in beaten egg, coat with crushed bread crumbs, and fry in the oven for approximately 1/2 hour as in recipe n:o 338
5. Serve with mushroom sauce and pressed potatoes
6. Alternatively, cut the pike into fillets and remove the head, backbone, and skin (use these to cook broth for basting the fish), then fry in the same manner
7. Fried pike can also be served with applesauce
8. Pike can also be smoked in the oven using a special smoking plate available for purchase
