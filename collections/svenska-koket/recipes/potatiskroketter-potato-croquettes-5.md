## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram peeled potatoes (approximately 1 1/2 liters unpeeled)
- 30 grams butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- a little nutmeg
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams) for the pan
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams)
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon cooking oil
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 grams)
- 1 1/2 deciliters crushed rusks
- 1 kilogram lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the potatoes as described, peel thinly, cut into pieces, weigh them and cook quickly in salted water until soft, but not falling apart.
2. Drain them immediately in a colander and let them stand in the front part of the oven for a few minutes to dry.
3. Press the potatoes through a fine sieve.
4. Place the purée in a saucepan, add the cold butter and seasonings and stir the purée with a fork over heat until it is firm.
5. Move the pan off the heat and add the egg yolks one at a time while stirring the purée vigorously with a fork.
