## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups rice groats
- 3 liters milk
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/4 hectogram sugar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Scald the rice groats and cook them in the milk into a thick porridge
2. Grease a baking pan with cold butter
3. Lay a layer of porridge in the bottom, sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over it, and place small pieces of butter on top
4. Continue layering porridge, sugar, cinnamon and butter until the pan is full
5. Top with cinnamon and sugar
6. Place the pan in a hot oven to bake until golden brown
7. Serve with milk or cream
