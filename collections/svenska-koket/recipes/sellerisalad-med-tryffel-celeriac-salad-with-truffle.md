## Ingredients

- 2-3 root celerias
- 1 1/2 lit. water
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 50-75 gr. truffle
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- paprika
- 3-4 msk. fine cooking oil
- 1 1/2-2 msk. vinegar

## Directions

1. Rinse the celerias, brush, scrape, rinse again, place in cold salted water and let them cook until soft, which is tested with a whisk.
2. Place them on a cloth, let them cool and cut with a vegetable knife into oblong pieces or strips.
3. Cut the truffle into fine strips or cubes and mix with the celeriac strips in a salad bowl.
4. Prepare the sauce as in recipe no. 448 and pour over the salad.
5. Serve with roasted meat.
