## Ingredients

- 0.5 liters thick cream
- zest of 0.5 lemon
- 150 grams sugar
- 1.5 parts water
- 8-10 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Whisk the cream to stiff peaks and mix with the grated lemon zest.
2. Brown the sugar and thin it with boiling water, and when it has cooled somewhat, stir it into the cream.
3. Dissolve the gelatin in a little lukewarm water and stir it last into the cream.
4. Pour this into a water-rinsed mold and set it out to stiffen.
5. When it is to be turned out, dip the mold in a little lukewarm water.
6. Serve with pastries.
