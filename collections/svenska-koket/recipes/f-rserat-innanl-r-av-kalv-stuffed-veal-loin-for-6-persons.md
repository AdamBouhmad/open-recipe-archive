## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg boneless veal loin
- 100 grams larding fat
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 1 carrot
- 0.5 Portuguese onion
- 1 small bunch of parsley
- 1 liter broth
- 300 grams of the meat for the forcemeat
- 0.25 kg salt beef tongue
- 0.5 can of truffles
- 1 tablespoon crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 large carrots for vegetables
- 1 can of French peas
- 1 can of green beans
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for sauce
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 0.5 cup sour milk
- 1 egg
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 head of cauliflower
- asparagus
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and clean thoroughly.
2. Lard it densely with long strips of larding fat.
3. Cut open the veal loin on one side and remove about 300 grams of the meat.
4. From the removed meat, the crushed breadcrumbs, milk, egg and seasonings, make a forcemeat which should be quite firm.
5. Cut the beef tongue (and truffles) into small cubes and mix in last.
6. Fill the opening in the loin with the forcemeat and sew it shut.
7. Brown the roast in butter and salt it.
8. Add the trimmed and sliced carrot, the onion, parsley and broth.
9. Allow the meat to roast slowly for about 2 hours.
10. During roasting, baste the roast frequently with its sauce.
11. Cut it into slices and serve, if desired, on a bed of rice.
12. Garnish with cooked carrots, beautifully cut with a grocer's knife, peas, beans, cauliflower and asparagus.
13. Strain and skim the meat gravy, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
