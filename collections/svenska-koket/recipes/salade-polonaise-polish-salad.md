## Ingredients

- 50 gr. veal roast
- 50 gr. salted ox tongue
- 50 gr. salted meat
- 2 hg. cooked carrots
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 2 good apples
- 150 gr. canned green beans (haricots verts)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tsk. salt
- cayenne
- 1/4 tsk. French mustard
- 1 1/2 dcl. cooking oil
- 1 msk. tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 msk. lemon juice
- 1 msk. boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut the veal roast, tongue, salted meat, carrots, cucumber and apples into fine cubes.
2. Cut the beans into small square pieces.
3. Mix all ingredients with the mayonnaise, which is prepared as in recipe no. 10.
4. Fill the salad into hollowed-out tomatoes (see recipe no. 731) and serve with roast beef and ham, or serve it as a lunch dish.
