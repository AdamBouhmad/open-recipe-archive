## Ingredients

- calf brain
- 1 liter water for blanching
- salt
- butter for frying
- eggs beaten with water
- crushed rusks for coating
- peas for garnish

## Directions

1. Let the brain soak in water, changing frequently to draw out all blood.
2. Blanch the brain and place on a sieve.
3. When cold, remove all membranes and chop the brain with a knife on a cutting board.
4. Fry the brain well in butter and season with spices.
5. Pour onto a flat plate, let cool and form into oblong cutlets.
6. Brush the cutlets with eggs beaten with water and roll in crushed rusks.
7. Fry nicely golden brown in butter and arrange in a ring on a round plate.
8. Place peas in the center of the plate.
9. Serve as a breakfast or light meal dish.
