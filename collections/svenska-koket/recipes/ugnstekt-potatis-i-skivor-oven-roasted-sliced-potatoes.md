## Ingredients

- 1 liter evenly-sized, oblong potatoes
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. The potatoes are treated as described on page 337 and onward and cut into 1/2 cm. thick slices, which are laid together so that the potatoes retain their form. A frying pan or omelet form is buttered with half of the butter, the potatoes are added, close together, the rest of the butter is laid in flakes on top, and lastly a mixture of the crushed rusks and cheese is sprinkled over. The potatoes are fried in the oven until they are soft and beautifully golden brown. They are placed on a warm plate (preferably of silver) and around them are garnished with cooked vegetables. Served with roasted meat.
