## Ingredients

- 50 gr. veal roast
- 50 gr. salt beef tongue
- 50 gr. salt meat
- 2 hectograms cooked carrots
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 2 good apples
- 150 gr. canned green beans
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- cayenne
- 1/4 teaspoon French mustard
- 1 1/2 deciliters vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon boiling water

## Directions

1. The veal roast, tongue, salt meat, carrots, cucumber and apples are cut into fine cubes. The green beans are cut into small square pieces.
2. All ingredients are then mixed in the mayonnaise, which is prepared as in no. 10.
3. The salad is filled into hollowed-out tomatoes (see no. 731) and served with roast beef and ham, or it is served as a lunch dish.
