## Ingredients

- 240 g butter
- 1 tsp ground cloves
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 2 tbsp finely chopped candied orange peel
- 4 hg sugar
- 1 1/2 cups sour cream
- 6 eggs
- 450 g wheat flour
- 2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
- 1 tsp butter and 1 tbsp crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Cream the butter until white.
2. Then add the sugar, spices, eggs, and the flour, sifted with the bicarbonate of soda.
