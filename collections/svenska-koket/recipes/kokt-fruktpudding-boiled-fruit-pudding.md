## Ingredients

- 50 gr. dried ring apples
- 1 hg. dried apricots
- 1 1/2 hg. prunes
- 4 dcl. water
- 1 1/2 dcl. crushed sugar (143 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest
- 3 eggs
- 2 dcl. cream
- For the form: 2 dcl. granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons boiling water

## Directions

1. The fruit is rinsed very well in several lukewarm waters and is left to lie in cold water overnight.
2. From this water and the sugar, a clear, simmering syrup is cooked; in it the apples are cooked first, then the apricots and finally the prunes, after they have been pitted.
3. The syrup is cooked until it is simmering, and is poured over the fruit.
4. The granulated sugar is melted with stirring in a pan, diluted with boiling water, and with this a smooth, shallow baking form is lined.
5. When the fruit is cold, it is layered in the form.
6. The eggs and cream are whipped together and poured over the fruit.
7. The pudding is baked in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour, or until the egg mixture has set.
8. It is removed, loosened from the edge and turned out after 15 minutes.
9. Served preferably cold, if desired, with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
