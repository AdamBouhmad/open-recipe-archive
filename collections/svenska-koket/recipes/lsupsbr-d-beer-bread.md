## Ingredients

- 1/2 litre milk
- 1 kg wheat flour
- 1 litre malt drink
- 2 kg rye sieve flour
- 80 gr pressed yeast
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr)
- 3 decilitres syrup
- 2 tablespoons crushed fennel
- 1 tablespoon crushed anise
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped orange peel

## Directions

1. In the milk, whisk so much of the wheat flour that it becomes a thick batter
2. Boil the malt drink, add the batter and let the mixture boil well while stirring
3. Pour it into a baking pan and stir in so much rye sieve flour that it becomes a loose dough
4. When cooled to about 40°, add the yeast mixed with salt, and add so much flour that it becomes a fairly firm dough, which is set to rise to double its size
5. Boil the syrup with the spices in a saucepan, let it cool and work it into the dough together with wheat flour, so that the dough becomes firm
6. Let it rise again, then shape, unbake and bake as in recipe no. 939
