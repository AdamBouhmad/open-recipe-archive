## Ingredients

- 2 kg. pears
- 1 kg. lump sugar
- 1/2 litre water
- carmine

## Directions

1. Place the pears in boiling water and let them cook until they are almost soft.
2. In the meantime, cook a sugar syrup from the sugar and water.
3. Color the syrup with carmine.
4. Peel the pears, scrape the stems, and cut a cross in each pear.
5. Place them immediately in the sugar syrup and let cook until they are clear and soft.
6. Remove the pears and place in a jar or crock.
7. Let the syrup cook down until it becomes syrupy and pour it over the pears.
