## Ingredients

- 3 kg veal breast
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 4 tbsp butter (80 gr.)
- 1 carrot
- 1 shallot onion
- 1 liter broth with 1 tsp meat extract
- 2 hg boneless veal meat
- 1 hg fresh pork meat
- 2 tbsp butter (40 gr.)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1-1 1/2 dcl cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the breast as in the previous recipe and rub with half of the spices.
2. Cook broth on the bones to dilute the roast with.
3. Prepare the forcemeat as in recipe no. 325 and it should be quite firm.
4. Spread the forcemeat on the inside of the breast, which is then treated and roasted as in the previous recipe.
5. Brown the scraped carrot, cut into slices, and the peeled onion and roast with the meat.
6. Serve with cooked vegetables or vegetable pastries, salads and reduced sauce with cream (recipe no. 429).
