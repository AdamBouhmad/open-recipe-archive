## Ingredients

- 24 thin wheat bread slices
- 50 gr sweet almond
- 50 gr powdered sugar
- 1/2 to 1 msk water
- 2 dcl applesauce or tutti-frutti
- 3 dcl cream
- 2 dcl finely crushed rusk
- 4 msk butter (80 gr)

## Directions

1. The bread should be good, but can be a couple of days old.
2. Blanch the almond, dry it in the oven, pass through a almond mill, and pound in a mortar together with the sugar and water until a fine mass.
3. Spread the almond mass on one bread slice and the applesauce on the other, then press the slices together.
4. Dip in the cream, turn in the crushed rusk, and fry beautifully golden brown in the butter.
5. Serve hot with sugar.
