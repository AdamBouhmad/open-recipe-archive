## Ingredients

- 200 grams prunes
- 60 grams sugar
- Peel of ¼ lemon
- 1 stick cinnamon
- 2 litres water
- 1½ tablespoons potato flour
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes in lukewarm water; crush half of them in a mortar until the pits break.
2. Place the whole and crushed prunes in cold water together with lemon peel and cinnamon and let cook until soft.
3. Press through a sieve.
4. Dilute the pressed soup if needed, add sugar and thicken with potato flour mixed with a little cold water.
5. Bring to a good boil and taste.
6. Serve preferably cold with whipped cream and rusks.
