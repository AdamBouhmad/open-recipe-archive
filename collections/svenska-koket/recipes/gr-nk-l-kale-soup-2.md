## Ingredients

- 2 medium heads green or blue kale
- 3 liters water for blanching
- 1 tablespoon salt for blanching
- 2 liters stock
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 0.5 teaspoon finely ground fennel
- egg for coating
- breadcrumbs for coating

## Directions

1. The kale, which should be frozen, is placed in water so that the ice is drawn out
2. It is grated and blanched for 15 minutes as in recipe 257
3. Place the kale on a sieve, rinse with cold water and let drain well
4. Wring in a cloth, chop finely, pass and cook in the stock for 1 hour
5. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the soup and let cook with stirring for 5 minutes
6. Season with the spices and serve with kale stalks cooked in butter and fish quenelles
7. For the kale stalks: blanch the thickest stalks, peel, wrap in a cloth, cut into 5 cm pieces, roll in flour, dip in beaten egg and then in breadcrumbs, cook golden brown in hot butter
8. Place in the soup bowl together with the quenelles, pour in the soup and serve at once
