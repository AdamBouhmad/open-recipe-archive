## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef (outside leg)
- 1 kg pork meat
- 1 kg fatty pork
- 1.5 kg cooked, grated potato
- 1.5-2 liters cooked, cooled milk
- 3 tbsp salt
- 7.5 tbsp ground white pepper
- 0.5 tbsp ground spice pepper
- 0.25 tbsp grated ginger
- 5 meters casings (curved skin)
- For rubbing: 2 tbsp salt
- 2 tbsp granulated sugar
- 1 tsk potassium nitrate

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water, cut into pieces (removing sinews and membranes) and pass through a meat grinder twice
2. Cut the fatty pork into fine cubes, or grind it together with the meat
3. Half-cook the potato, peel and grate or press through a puree press
4. Boil the milk and cool it
5. Work the meat well together with the spices, potato and milk, which is added gradually while working the mixture for 1 hour
6. Add the cubed fatty pork near the end of the hour
7. Stuff the mixture into well-cleaned, narrow pork casings
8. Rinse the sausages with cold water, rub with a mixture of salt, sugar and nitrate and let lie until the following day, then place in brine
