## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg chestnuts
- 1/2 kg lard or fat
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 3-4 deciliters bouillon
- white pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cut a cross 1 1/2 cm deep into the pointed end of each chestnut
2. Boil them for 2 minutes in hot lard so the shells loosen
3. Remove both outer and inner shells while the chestnuts are still warm
4. Melt the butter in a pot, add the chestnuts, sprinkle with salt and sugar
5. Add just enough bouillon to cover them
6. Let simmer slowly with a lid until almost tender
7. Remove the lid and reduce the cooking liquid until it is thick as syrup
8. Shake the chestnuts gently in the glaze so they become evenly glazed
9. Season with more salt and sugar and a little white pepper if desired
10. Serve as a garnish around roasted meat or together with boiled Brussels sprouts as a side dish
