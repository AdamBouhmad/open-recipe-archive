## Ingredients

- 2 dcl red wine
- 1 1/2 dcl granulated sugar (128 gr)
- 5 egg yolks
- Zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tbsp red currant or gooseberry jelly

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients in a pot and whisk until the sugar has melted, then move the pot over the heat and let the cream simmer under vigorous whisking until it is quite thick.
2. Whisk the cream thereafter until it is cold, then pour it into a compote bowl and serve with biscuits or almond slices.
