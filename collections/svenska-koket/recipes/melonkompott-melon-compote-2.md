## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg melon
- 2 parts fine sugar (170 gr.)
- 1 dcl. water
- 1–2 tbsp. fine roe
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the melon, which should be ripe, into slices 1 cm. thick, and cut these into pieces 5 cm. long.
2. Place the cut melon in 1 liter of boiling water and let it boil for 2–3 minutes, then remove it with a slotted spoon and let it drain on cloth.
3. Cook a clear, syrupy syrup from sugar, water, roe, and lemon juice.
4. Add the melon and let it boil until it is soft and clear.
5. Place it in a compote bowl, then if desired boil down the syrup and pour it over.
6. Serve cold with lady fingers.
