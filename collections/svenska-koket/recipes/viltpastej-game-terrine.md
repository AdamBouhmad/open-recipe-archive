## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. leftover, scraped poultry
- 1 hg. lard
- 3 eggs
- 1 msk. flour (10 gr.)
- 3—4 dcl. thick cream
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- For the mold: 1 1/2 msk. butter
- 2 msk. finely chopped truffle

## Directions

1. The scraped poultry is ground together with the lard 5 times through a meat grinder and is thereafter pounded with a wooden pestle in a stone mortar until a smooth and supple forcemeat.
2. The egg yolks are whisked with the flour and cream and this mixture is added by spoonfuls into the forcemeat, while it is worked for approximately 1 hour.
3. The forcemeat is carefully seasoned with spices and lastly the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are cut in.
4. A timbale mold is buttered and sprinkled with finely chopped truffle, the forcemeat is poured in and cooked slowly in a water bath for approximately 2 hours.
5. It is turned out onto a round dish, garnished with peas and browned potatoes and served with poultry or truffle sauce and lingonberry jelly.
