## Ingredients

- 3 dcl. thick sugar syrup (3 hg. granulated sugar, 1 1/2 dcl. water)
- 8 egg yolks
- 5 dcl. extra thick heavy cream

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar syrup first, preferably the day before use
2. Chop the sugar into small pieces and place in an enameled iron pot, add water, and when the sugar is melted, cook it to a clear, syrupy consistency that should thread between the fingers when tested
3. Measure the sugar syrup and let it cool
4. Add the egg yolks one at a time while whisking, then let the cream simmer over heat with vigorous whisking
5. Pour the cream and whisk over ice until completely cold
6. Mix with the heavy cream, beaten to stiff peaks
7. The bomb cream can be flavored with vanilla, lemon, apricot, pineapple, kirsch, anisette, wild strawberries or strawberries, finely crushed nougat, finely chopped pistachios, cherry compote, etc.
8. Fill the cream into a bomb mold and freeze directly in it for 4-5 hours
9. Alternatively, the mold should preferably be lined inside with a thin layer of some kind of pre-frozen glace, filled with bomb cream, and frozen for 4-5 hours
