## Ingredients

- 5 hg lean salt pork
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 2 tbsp butter (40 gr)

## Directions

1. Cut the pork into slices, trim away the rind neatly and pound the pork slices well on both sides
2. Dip in the whisked egg and turn in the crushed rusks
3. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter in it, add the pork and let fry golden brown on both sides
4. Place on a warm dish, garnish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish with stewed cauliflower or artichokes
