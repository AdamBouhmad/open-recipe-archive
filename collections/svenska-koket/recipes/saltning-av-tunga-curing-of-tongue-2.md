## Ingredients

- 1 oxtunga (beef tongue)
- 4 msk. salt
- 2 msk. socker (sugar)
- 1 tsk. kalisalpeter (potassium nitrate)
- 6 lit. vatten (water)
- 1 lit. grovt salt (coarse salt)
- 4 msk. socker (sugar)
- 2 tsk. kalisalpeter (potassium nitrate)

## Directions

1. Rub the tongue with a mixture of salt, sugar and saltpeter.
2. After one day, brine is formed, and in this the tongue may lie 3-14 days, depending on how saltily one wishes to have it.
3. It should be turned twice daily during this time.
4. If salting down several tongues simultaneously and insufficient brine has formed to cover them, boil a brine from water, coarse salt, sugar and saltpeter.
5. Skim the brine well during cooking and then pour it up to cool.
6. Only when it is completely cold, pour it over the tongue.
