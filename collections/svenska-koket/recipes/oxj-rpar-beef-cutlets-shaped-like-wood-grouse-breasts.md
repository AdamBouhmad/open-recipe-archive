## Ingredients

- 500 g boneless beef
- 200 g lard
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons potato flour
- 3-4 parts milk
- For breading: 1 egg, 2 cups crushed breadcrumbs
- For frying: 3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it together with 100 g of the lard into cubes and pass three times through a meat grinder.
2. Add the whisked egg, spices, potato flour and milk, and work the forcemeat until it is smooth and cohesive.
3. Form small cutlets from the forcemeat in the shape of wood grouse breasts, lard them with the remaining lard cut into fine strips.
4. Bread the cutlets and then fry them in butter.
5. Serve with peas or other vegetables.
