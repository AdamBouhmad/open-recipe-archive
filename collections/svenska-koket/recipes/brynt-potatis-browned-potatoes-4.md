## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams)
- water
- 3 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 grams)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 grams)

## Directions

1. Treat the potatoes as described on page 337 and following, boil with the skin on and allow to cool.
2. Peel and shape with a smooth melon-baller or knife into small, even balls, which are shaken in a mixture of the crushed breadcrumbs and sugar; any potato that is cut off is fried separately or used for potato balls.
3. Brown the butter in a frying pan, add the potatoes; sprinkle with the breadcrumbs that have not adhered to them, and brown nicely while shaking.
4. Serve as garnish around meat or together with vegetables.
