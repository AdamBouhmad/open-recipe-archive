## Ingredients

- 1 kg. pork hare
- 1 hg. lard
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon ginger
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce

## Directions

1. Remove most of the fat and beat the hare with a damp mallet.
2. Cut the lard into strips, roll them in a mixture of salt, white pepper and ginger and let them sit 1 hour to harden.
3. Lard the hare in dense, even rows, rub it with the rest of the spices and tie it with fine sailcloth so that it takes on a beautiful shape.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the hare in it and brown it beautifully on all sides.
5. Add the milk mixed with the soy sauce and let the meat stew with a tight lid until tender, or about 1 3/4 hours, basting it every 10 minutes.
6. Cut the hare into thin slices, which are then reassembled on the platter.
7. Prepare the sauce and pour it over the meat.
8. Garnish the dish with browned potatoes and parsley.
9. Serve with lingonberry jelly and sour pickles.
