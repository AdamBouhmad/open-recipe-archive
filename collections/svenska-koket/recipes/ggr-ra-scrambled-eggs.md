## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 16 tablespoon milk
- salt
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Eggs, milk and salt are whisked well together
2. A saucepan is greased with butter, the egg mixture is poured in, and the saucepan is placed on low heat
3. As the scrambled eggs set, they are carefully lifted out with a spoon
