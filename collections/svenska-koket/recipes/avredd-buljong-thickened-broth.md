## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 g)
- 4 tbsp flour (40 g)
- 1 1/2 liters light broth
- 2-3 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the boiling broth while stirring and let it cook for 5 minutes. Whisk the egg yolks and stir them into the broth, then season with spices and serve with cooked vegetables, mushrooms, cheese croutons, quenelles of fish forcemeat, or the like.
