## Ingredients

- 1 score crayfish or 2 hg crayfish tails
- 5 dcl boiling milk
- 6 eggs
- 80 gr butter
- 80 gr flour
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp bread crumbs for the mold
- 40 gr coarse salt
- 2 dill stems

## Directions

1. Rinse the crayfish, put them in 2 liters boiling water with 40 gr coarse salt and 2 dill stems and let them cook for 10 minutes.
2. If using salted crayfish tails, let them soak in milk for a few hours.
3. Clean the crayfish, cut the tails and claws into pieces and scrape out the fat thoroughly.
4. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk while stirring and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
5. Pour into a dish; when cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
6. Mix in the crayfish meat and fat along with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
7. Taste the mixture, pour into a buttered and bread-crumbed pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 45 minutes.
8. If the soufflé becomes too brown on top, cover it with a butter-brushed paper.
9. Serve immediately when removed from the oven as a side course with crayfish sauce or creamed butter.
10. If using a baking sheet form, bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour and turn out.
