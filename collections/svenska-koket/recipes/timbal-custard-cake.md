## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 hg. sugar
- 60 gr. melted butter
- 260 gr. wheat flour
- 3 tsk. baking powder
- 1 kkp thin cream
- 1/2 msk. butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the mold
- 5 dcl. warm, loose applesauce for filling
- 3 egg whites for meringue
- 105 gr. powdered sugar for meringue

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar for 1/2 hour.
2. Add the melted, but cooled butter, the wheat flour which should be well sifted with the baking powder, and the cream milk.
3. Stir the batter well and pour immediately into a buttered tall timbal mold.
4. Bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 1 hour, or until the cake feels dry when tested with a whisk.
5. Remove the cake from the mold, let it cool, and hollow it out so that the walls and bottom are only 2 cm. thick.
6. Fill the cake with applesauce, and place a lid made from the cut-out cake on top.
7. Whip the egg whites until stiff, carefully fold in the sifted powdered sugar.
8. Pipe the meringue mixture through a pastry bag over the cake, then place in the oven to achieve a nice golden-brown color.
9. Serve warm, covered with warm apricot sauce colored red with carmine.
