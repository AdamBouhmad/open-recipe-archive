## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 dcl. clear, syrupy sugar syrup
- 1 dcl. champagne or white port wine
- 2-8 beautiful, good fresh apples
- 2-3 good, loose fresh pears
- 2-3 bananas
- 2 oranges
- 1 1/2 hg. blue or green grapes
- 6 prunes (compote)
- 3 tbsp. cherry preserve or compote

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar syrup (see sugar syrup for compote) with the wine in a compote bowl.
2. Peel the apples and pears, cut into even, beautiful wedges, remove the core and place in the sugar syrup.
3. Peel the bananas, cut into 1/2 cm thick slices on the bias, peel the oranges, carefully remove the white pith, then divide into wedges or cut into slices, remove the seeds and place together with the bananas in the compote bowl.
4. Trim the grapes and add to the compote along with the prunes and cherry preserve.
