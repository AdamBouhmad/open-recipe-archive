## Ingredients

- 2 kg Dover sole
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup crushed hardtack
- 3 tbsp butter
- Maitre-d'hôtel butter: 150 g butter, 1 tbsp chopped parsley, 1 tsp lemon juice, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp white pepper
- For sauce: 1 tbsp chopped parsley, juice of 1/2 lemon, 1 cup stock

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the fish well.
2. Make a deep cut from the head to the tail, and remove the backbone from top and bottom.
3. Salt the fish, dip it in the beaten egg and crushed hardtack, then fry it golden brown in butter.
4. While the fish fries, prepare the maitre-d'hôtel butter: rinse and finely chop the parsley and mix well with the butter, seasonings, and lemon juice.
5. When the fish is cooked, remove the backbone and place the maitre-d'hôtel butter in the opening.
6. Place the fish on a warm plate; add the parsley, lemon juice, and stock to the butter in which it was fried, and pour over the fish.
