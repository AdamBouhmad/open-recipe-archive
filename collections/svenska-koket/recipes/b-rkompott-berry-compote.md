## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lit. jordgubbar, hallon, åkerbär, hjortron ell. urkärnade körsbär
- 1 1/2 dcl. strösocker (128 gr.)

## Directions

1. The berries, which should be freshly picked, are cleaned and rinsed quickly in a sieve and drained well.
2. Place them in a compote bowl alternately with the sugar; each layer of berries is sprinkled with fresh water.
3. Let the berries stand for 2-3 hours to extract their juices.
4. Serve with whipped or frozen cream.
