## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 dcl. Carolina rice grains (125 gr.)
- 1 1/2 lit. water
- 4 oranges
- 1 1/2 dcl. sugar (128 gr.)
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- 5–6 leaves gelatin
- 3 tbsp. water
- 2 oranges in segments for garnish

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, scald them in hot water, add to boiling water and let them cook until soft, about 20 minutes.
2. Drain the rice in a sieve and rinse with cold water, letting it drain well.
3. Peel the oranges, carefully remove the white pith, cut them into small cubes, remove the seeds and mix with sugar.
4. Whip the cream into stiff peaks.
5. Rinse the gelatin with cold water, cut it into pieces and dissolve in three tbsp. hot water.
6. Mix the rice grains with the orange cubes and cream, and lastly add the gelatin.
7. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for about 3 hours, or until set.
8. Loosen the pudding around the edges with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish with pastry paper, garnish with orange segments and serve with orange sauce.
