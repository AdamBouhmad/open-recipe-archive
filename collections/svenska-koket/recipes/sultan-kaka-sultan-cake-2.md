## Ingredients

- 230 grams butter
- 230 grams castor sugar
- 6 eggs
- 1 hectogram candied orange peel
- 1 hectogram candied fruit
- 130 grams sultana raisins
- zest of 1/2 lemon
- 75 grams sweet almonds
- 50 grams pistachios
- 275 grams wheat flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams) for the pan
- 3 tablespoons finely crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, let it cool and beat until white and fluffy.
2. Add the sugar and egg yolks, one at a time.
3. Add the finely chopped orange peel, candied fruit, cleaned and pre-boiled raisins, finely grated lemon zest, blanched and ground sweet almonds, and blanched and finely chopped pistachios.
4. Fold in the flour, sifted and mixed well with baking powder.
5. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks last.
6. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-strewn large ring pan and bake in a not-too-hot oven for approximately 1 1/2 hours.
