## Ingredients

- 4 small salted herrings
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed hardtack
- 60 gr. butter (for frying)

## Directions

1. Skin, clean, rinse well, and fillet the herring.
2. Remove the backbone and let the herring soak in water for approximately 12 hours.
3. Rinse the fillets well and wrap in a fish towel.
4. Carefully remove all small bones and roll the fillets in a mixture of wheat flour and crushed hardtack.
5. Fry until nicely browned in the butter, arrange on a warm platter, garnish with parsley, and serve as a breakfast dish with freshly boiled unpeeled potatoes.
