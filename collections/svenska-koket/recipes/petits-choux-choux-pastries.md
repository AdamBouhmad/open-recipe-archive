## Ingredients

- 1 hg. butter
- 2 1/2 dcl. milk
- 25 gr. sugar
- 1/4 tsk. finely grated lemon zest
- 175 gr. flour
- 5 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan with a round bottom, pour in the milk and bring to a boil with sugar and lemon zest.
2. Whisk in the flour while at a rolling boil and let the mixture cook until it leaves the pan.
3. Remove from heat and let cool slightly, then stir in the eggs one at a time and beat the mixture for 10 minutes.
4. The mixture can either be piped through a fluted pastry bag onto a well-buttered baking sheet into oblong pastries (éclairs), which are split after baking and filled with some cream, or placed by spoon into round buns (choux); these are filled with whipped cream flavored with vanilla.
5. Bake immediately in a not-too-hot oven, taking care not to open the oven door before 8-10 minutes have passed.
