## Ingredients

- 1 liter pitted cherries
- 6 dcl sugar syrup
- 1/4 dcl kirsch
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Crush the cherry stones
2. Pound the kernels together with the pitted cherries
3. Allow the cherries and kernels to soak in the sugar syrup mixed with kirsch for approximately 1 hour
4. Pass the mixture through a sieve
5. Add lemon juice
