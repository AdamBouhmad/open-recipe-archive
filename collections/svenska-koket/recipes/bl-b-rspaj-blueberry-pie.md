## Ingredients

- 225 gr. flour
- 100 gr. butter
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)
- 20 gr. sweet almonds
- 1 dcl. water
- 1 egg yolk
- 3/4 liter blueberries
- 1 1/2 dcl. sugar (128 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl; the butter, which should be firm, is divided into flakes in the flour and quickly worked in with the fingertips until the mixture is even and light yellow in color. Then add the sugar, the blanched and ground almonds, water and egg yolk quickly, and knead the dough a couple of times, roll into a ball, place immediately in a napkin and set in a cold place to rest 1 hour.
2. Clean the blueberries, rinse them, let them drain, sprinkle with sugar and mix well by shaking them.
3. Roll out three-quarters of the dough and line a buttered baking sheet or pan with it. Add the blueberries and fold the dough edge over. Roll out the remaining dough into a cover and cover the pie with it. Bake in good oven heat approximately 1 hour, or until the dough is thoroughly baked when tested with a whisk. Turn the pie out and serve warm or cold with whipped cream.
