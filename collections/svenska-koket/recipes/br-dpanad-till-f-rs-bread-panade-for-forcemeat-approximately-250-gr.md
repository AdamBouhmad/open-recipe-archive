## Ingredients

- 1 hg. crumb of white bread
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. boiling milk
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Free the bread crumbs from the crust, weigh and place in a saucepan together with the salt. Pour the boiling milk over and when the bread has absorbed all the milk, place the pan over the heat and stir the panade with a wooden spoon or ladle until it is thick and even. Pour the panade onto a buttered dish and brush the top with a little butter so that it does not dry while cooling.
