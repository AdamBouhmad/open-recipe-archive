## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 250 g sugar
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 11 leaves gelatin
- 4 tbsp warm water
- 1/2 liter thick cream

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes and mix with the finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice.
2. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in the warm water.
3. Whip the cream until stiff; whip the egg whites likewise.
4. Stir the gelatin first into the egg mixture, then the cream and finally the egg white foam.
5. Fill the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours.
6. When the fromage is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass dish with cake paper.
7. Serve with almond slivers.
