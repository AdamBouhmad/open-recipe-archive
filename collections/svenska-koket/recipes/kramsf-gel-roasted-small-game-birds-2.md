## Ingredients

- Various small wild birds such as thrush, sparrow, quail, lark and similar
- Thin bacon slices
- Butter
- Cream

## Directions

1. The birds are plucked, drawn, well rinsed, dried, trussed, wrapped with thin bacon slices, browned in butter, thinned with cream and allowed to roast for approximately 15 minutes, during which they are basted frequently.
2. The birds are split and arranged on croutons.
3. The sauce is whisked up with a little thick cream and cold butter, tasted and poured over the birds.
4. Garnish with parsley and, if desired, with mushroom caps sautéed in butter, or with small triangular croutons fried in butter and topped with applesauce.
