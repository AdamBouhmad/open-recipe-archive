## Ingredients

- 12 slices good white bread
- 1 egg
- 2 1/2 dcl good milk
- 2 1/2 tbsp butter (50 gr)
- 4 dcl apple puree or apple compote
- 40 gr sultana raisins
- 3 eggs for the custard
- 1 tsp sugar (5 gr) for the custard
- 3 dcl thin cream
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Dip the bread slices in a mixture of the beaten egg and milk, fry them in butter and layer them alternately with the apple puree or compote and the raisins (cleaned and rinsed), if desired, in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie dish
2. For the custard, whisk the eggs and cream together, season with sugar and pour over the pudding
3. Bake in ordinary oven heat until it has a nice golden-brown color, or for approximately 1/2 hour
4. Serve cooled, with whipped cream if desired
