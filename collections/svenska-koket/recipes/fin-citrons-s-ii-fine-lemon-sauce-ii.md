## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- 2 parts white French wine
- 2 parts water
- 2 teacups castor sugar
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The lemon is zested so that only the yellow skin comes with it, and the juice is pressed out.
2. The wine, water, grated lemon zest, lemon juice, sugar and egg yolks are whisked well together in a pan and are allowed to simmer over the heat with vigorous whisking until the sauce thickens.
3. The pan is removed from the heat, and whisking is continued until the sauce has cooled.
