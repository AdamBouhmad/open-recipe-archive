## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr.)
- 4 deciliter cream
- 2 gelatin leaves
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 teaspoon anchovy sauce
- 4 tablespoon flour for breading
- 2 eggs for breading
- salt
- 2 teaspoon cooking oil
- 1 cup crushed bread crumbs
- 1 kg. cooking fat

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes, cool in cold water, shell and cut into fine dice.
2. Melt butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Carefully stir in the diced eggs together with the rinsed and in 1 tablespoon hot water dissolved gelatin.
4. Season the mixture with salt and white pepper and treat as in no. 49.
5. Form the croquettes into round shapes.
6. Serve as breakfast dish with stewed peas or spinach.
