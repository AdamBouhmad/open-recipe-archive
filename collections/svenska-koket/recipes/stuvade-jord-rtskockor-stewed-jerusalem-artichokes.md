## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 3-4 cups Jerusalem artichoke broth
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Scrape the Jerusalem artichokes, rinse and place in vinegar-mixed water.
2. To better preserve their whiteness, blanch them for a few minutes in boiling salted water with a little vinegar.
3. Drain this water and place the Jerusalem artichokes in new salted boiling water, enough so it can serve as broth for the stew.
4. Let them cook slowly with lid on until soft.
5. Brown the butter and flour together, add the broth, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
6. Add the Jerusalem artichokes and season the stew.
7. Sugar beets can be stewed the same way.
