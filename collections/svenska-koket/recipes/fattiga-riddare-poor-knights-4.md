## Ingredients

- 1 twelve-ounce white bread loaf
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 liter milk
- 0.5 teaspoon cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Cut the white bread into slices.
2. Whisk wheat flour, eggs and spices together with the milk.
3. Place the bread slices in this batter and let them lie until they are soft.
4. Fry them thereafter until beautifully brown on both sides.
5. Serve hot with jam.
