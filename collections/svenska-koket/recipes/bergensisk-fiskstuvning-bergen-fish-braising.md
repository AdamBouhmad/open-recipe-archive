## Ingredients

- 1 hg salted crayfish tails
- 0.75-1 liter cooked fish
- 1.5 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp wheat flour
- 1.5 cups cream milk
- 0.5 cup fish bouillon
- salt
- lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Lay the crayfish tails in milk the evening before they are to be used.
2. Clean the fish and cut into large dice.
3. Lay it on the serving platter alternating with the crayfish tails, and place the platter over a pot of boiling water to heat through.
4. Melt the butter and flour together, add milk and fish broth little by little, and let the sauce boil for 10 minutes.
5. Season with salt and lemon juice and finish with the egg yolks.
6. Pour the sauce over the fish and serve at once.
