## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups rice groats
- 3 liters milk
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 0.5 hectograms sugar
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice groats and cook them in the milk into a thick porridge.
2. Grease a baking pan with cold butter, lay a layer of porridge in the bottom, sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over it, and place small flakes of butter on top.
3. Continue to layer porridge, sugar, cinnamon and butter until the pan is full.
4. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar on top.
5. Place the pan in a hot oven to brown.
6. Serve with milk or cream.
