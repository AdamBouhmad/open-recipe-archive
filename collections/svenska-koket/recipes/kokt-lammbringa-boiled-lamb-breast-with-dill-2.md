## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. lamb breast
- 1 1/2 lit. water
- 1 1/2 msk. salt (23 gr.)
- 4 dill or parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Break the breast well, dry it with a cloth wrung out in hot water, place it in boiling water, skim well, salt it and add the dill or parsley.
2. Let it then boil slowly with a tightly closed lid for 1 1/2-2 hours.
3. Remove it and cut into beautiful, even pieces, which are arranged on a hot platter and garnished with dill or parsley.
4. Strain the broth, skim it well and use it for sauce and for simpler soups.
5. Boiled lamb breast is served with dill sauce (see no. 439) or with caper or light mushroom sauce (see no. 426 and no. 421). In the first case the breast is boiled with dill, in the latter case with parsley.
