## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- 100 grams sugar
- 1 deciliter French wine
- 1/2 deciliter water
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the form

## Directions

1. Rinse the lemon well and dry it. Grate the zest neatly and press out the juice.
2. In a well-scoured saucepan, blend the egg yolks, lemon zest, lemon juice, sugar, wine, and water and whisk over heat until the cream is thick and foamy, then remove from heat and whisk until cold.
3. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks, pour the cream into a buttered, heat-proof form and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1/2 hour. Serve immediately from the form.
4. If desired, you may place 1 tablespoon cloudberries and 1 tablespoon apple compote in the bottom of the form, alternated with 50 grams of biscuits, pour the cream over and bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for about 40 minutes.
