## Ingredients

- 60 gr. mondaminmjöl (mondamin flour)
- 5 dcl. mjölk (milk)
- 2 msk. smör (butter)
- 4-5 ägg (eggs)
- 75 gr. socker (sugar)
- 1/2 tsk. fmrivet citronskal (finely grated lemon zest)
- 2 syltade apelsinskal, i fina tärningar (candied orange zest, finely diced)
- 1/2 msk. smör för formen (butter for the mold)
- 2 msk. stötta skorpor (crushed rusks for the mold)

## Directions

1. Mix the mondamin flour with 2 dcl. of the milk.
2. Boil the remaining milk with the butter and add the mondamin mixture.
3. Let the porridge boil for 5 minutes while stirring.
4. Remove the pan from heat and add the egg yolks one at a time, along with the lemon zest and orange zest.
5. When the cream is cold, carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Pour the mixture immediately into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pie form.
7. Bake the pudding in regular oven heat for about 40 minutes.
8. Serve immediately with compote or fruit sauce.
