## Ingredients

- 1 kg. spinach
- 4 lit. water for blanching
- 2 msk. salt (30 gr.)
- 3 msk. flour (30 gr.)
- 2 1/2 msk. butter (50 gr.)
- 2-3 dcl. cream, milk or broth
- 2 tsk. sugar (10 gr.)
- 2 hard-boiled eggs, for garnish

## Directions

1. Treat and blanch the spinach as in the previous recipe, place in a colander, immediately pour cold water over it, which is carefully pressed out with a spoon.
2. Chop the spinach; when it is half chopped, add the flour and finish chopping the spinach. (If desired, pass it afterwards through a sieve.)
3. Melt the butter in a saucepan, add the spinach and brown it well with the butter, add the hot cream or broth, and let the stew cook, while stirring, for 5 minutes.
4. Season the spinach with sugar and more salt if needed, place on toast, garnish with the hard-boiled eggs, cut into wedges or finely chopped, separating the whites and yolks.
5. Serve with fried fish, boiled ham and fried meat.
