## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 kg. kalvbog (veal shoulder)
- 2 kalvläggar (veal legs)
- 2 lit. vatten (water)
- några vitpepparkom (some white peppercorns)
- salt
- stött vitpeppar (ground white pepper)
- riven ingefära (grated ginger)

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and place it on the fire in boiling water.
2. When the broth boils up, skim it well, add the peppercorns and 1 1/2 msk. salt.
3. Let it cook slowly until the meat is tender.
4. Take out the meat, let it cool and then chop it fairly finely.
5. Let the broth continue to cook down meanwhile.
6. Add the meat and let it all come to a boil.
7. Season the mixture quite strongly with salt, pepper and ginger and pour it.
