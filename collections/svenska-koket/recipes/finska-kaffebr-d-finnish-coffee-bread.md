## Ingredients

- 425 gr. butter
- 4 tbsp. granulated sugar
- 580 gr. wheat flour
- 12 bitter almonds
- 2 egg whites for brushing
- 3 tbsp. pearl sugar
- 25 gr. sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Wash the butter, sift the flour, and blanch and grate the bitter almonds.
2. Work all ingredients together well on the work table into a smooth dough.
3. Roll out elongated, finger-thick sticks from the dough, then cut into appropriately long pieces.
4. Brush with whisked egg white and sprinkle with almonds and sugar.
5. Bake until golden brown in good oven heat.
