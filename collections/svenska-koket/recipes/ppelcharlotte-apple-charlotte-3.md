## Ingredients

- 6 two-öre almond breads
- 1 1/2 hg. melted butter
- 1 hg. coarsely crushed granulated sugar
- 150 gr. sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 1 1/2 liters good, tart apples
- 1 tbsp. lemon juice or vinegar
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Butter a copper or tin pan with cold butter and sprinkle it with crushed rusks.
2. Split the breads lengthwise, dip them in the melted butter and turn them in the sugar.
3. Line the pan with bread slices, fill with apple compote, and place the remaining bread slices on top as a lid.
4. Bake the pudding for about 1/2 hour in ordinary oven heat, turn it out and serve warm with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
