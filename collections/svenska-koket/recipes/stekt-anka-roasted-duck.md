## Ingredients

- 1 young duck
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 0.5-1 liter broth
- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the duck, remove organs, rinse well and dry.
2. Rub the inside with half the salt and truss.
3. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the duck in breast-side up and brown well on all sides. Sprinkle with the remaining salt and add the broth.
4. Let the duck cook slowly for 1.5-2 hours. Strain the meat gravy, skim it and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
5. The duck can also be roasted in the oven like goose, filled with apples and prunes.
