## Ingredients

- 1 lit. fine, good apples
- 1 lit. good pears
- sugar, to taste (about 3 dcl.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit, dry it, peel it and grate it on a grater or grind through a mill and pass through a sieve.
2. Mix it then with sugar to taste and pack into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted ice cream mold.
3. Over the fruit place a so-called butter paper, put on the lid, pack the mold in ice and salt, and freeze the fruit for 4–5 hours.
4. Unmold, garnish with compote of fruit or berries and serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
