## Ingredients

- 2 kg oranges
- water
- 1.1 kg granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the oranges well and dry them.
2. Remove the peel so that each orange is divided into 8 parts.
3. Place the peels in plenty of water in a preserve pan and let them cook with a lid for approximately 1/2 hour, after which pour them into a colander and let them cool.
4. Divide the oranges into segments and carefully free them from membranes and pits.
5. Remove all the white from the peels, after which cut them crosswise into very fine strips, which are set to cook in 3 decilitres of water.
6. When the peels have cooked 15 minutes, add the fruit flesh and sugar; let the marmalade cook, while stirring and skimming well, until it is clear.
7. Pour it then into small warm glass jars, previously rinsed with brandy.
8. Bind the jars when the marmalade is cold.
