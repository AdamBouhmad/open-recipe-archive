## Ingredients

- 1 kg peeled potatoes, approximately 1 1/2 liters unpeeled
- 30 g butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- a little nutmeg
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g) for the pan
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 g)
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon cooking oil
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 g)
- 1 1/2 deciliters fine bread crumbs
- 1 kg lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes thinly, cut into pieces, weigh them and cook quickly in salted water until soft but not falling apart.
2. Drain immediately in a colander and let stand in the front part of the oven for a few minutes to dry.
3. Press the potatoes through a fine sieve.
4. Place the puree in a saucepan, add the cold butter and seasonings and stir the puree with a fork over the heat until it is firm.
5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and add the egg yolks one at a time while stirring vigorously with a fork.
