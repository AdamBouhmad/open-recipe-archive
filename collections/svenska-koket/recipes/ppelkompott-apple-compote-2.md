## Ingredients

- 2 hg. toppsocker
- 4 dcl. vatten
- 1 1/2 lit. goda, syrliga äpplen (omkr. 8 hg.)
- saften av 1/2 citron ell. 2 msk ättika

## Directions

1. Make a clear syrup from the sugar and water and let it cool.
2. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel thinly, cut into 4-6 pieces according to size, and remove the cores.
3. As the apples are cut, place them in 1 1/2 liters of water mixed with lemon juice or vinegar.
4. Remove only as many apple pieces as will fit on the surface of the sugar syrup, drain them on cloth, place in the sugar syrup and cook until just tender, testing with a whisk.
5. Remove with a slotted spoon onto a flat dish and let cool.
6. Add more sugar and water to the syrup if needed.
7. When all the apple pieces are cooked, cook down the syrup.
