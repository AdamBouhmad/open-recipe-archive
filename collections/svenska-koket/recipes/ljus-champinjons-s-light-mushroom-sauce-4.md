## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp. butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- 4 parts cream
- 1 can mushrooms on 1/4 liter
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are fried together, cream and mushroom broth are thinned in, and the sauce is allowed to simmer 10 minutes.
2. The mushrooms are cut in half and added, after which the sauce is seasoned with the spices.
3. Finally it is finished with the egg yolk, after which it must not boil further.
