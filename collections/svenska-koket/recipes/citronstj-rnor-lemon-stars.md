## Ingredients

- 425 gr. wheat flour
- 212 gr. sugar
- 212 gr. butter
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 tsp. ammonium carbonate (1 1/2 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp. brandy or cognac
- 1 tbsp. finely grated lemon peel
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 3 tbsp. pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl (1 1/2 dcl. is reserved for baking) and mix with the sugar, butter, the whipped eggs, the ammonium carbonate dissolved in brandy or cognac, and the grated lemon peel, and work the dough until it becomes smooth and even.
2. Roll it out to about 3 mm thickness and cut with a star cutter into cookies, brush with lightly beaten egg white, sprinkle with pearl sugar and bake golden-brown in a not too warm oven.
