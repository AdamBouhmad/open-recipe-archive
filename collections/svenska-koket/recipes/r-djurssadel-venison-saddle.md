## Ingredients

- 2 kg trimmed saddle
- 1.5 hg lard
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 3 dcl broth
- 3 dcl cream

## Directions

1. With a sharp knife remove all membranes from the back without any meat being lost.
2. Lard the saddle with finely cut lard strips dipped in a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Place in a roasting pan, arrange the butter in flakes over it, put in a warm oven, and let stand 15 minutes.
4. Add the boiling broth and cream and let roast until tender, or approximately 1 1/2 hours, basting every 10 minutes, otherwise it becomes dry.
5. Carve the saddle like lamb saddle, garnish with browned potatoes, small tartlets filled with red currant jelly, or with vegetable salad in small shells of raw cabbage leaves.
6. Serve with reduced sauce (see recipe no. 429), with 1 tbsp red currant jelly added.
7. Cutlets can be cut from the saddle, which are fried like veal cutlets, with or without breading. Serve with stewed mushrooms or champignons, potato croquettes, or chestnut puree.
