## Ingredients

- 12 good, large apples
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. sugar (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them if desired, and remove the cores.
2. Grease a frying pan or small baking pan with the butter, place the apples in it, sprinkle with sugar and bake in the oven until they are soft, but must not fall apart.
3. Place them on a glass dish and serve preferably cold with or without whipped cream.
