## Ingredients

- 425 gr. butter
- 4 egg whites
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 425 gr. wheat flour
- 25 gr. bitter almond
- 2 egg whites for brushing
- 1/4 kkp pearl sugar
- 50 gr. sweet almond

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and sift the flour on a baking board.
2. Divide the butter into flakes and place on top of the flour along with sugar, the blanched and finely grated almond, and egg whites (not beaten to peaks).
3. Work all ingredients together into a smooth, cohesive dough.
4. Roll out quite thick and cut small cakes with a measure.
5. Brush with egg white and sprinkle with almond and sugar.
6. Bake to a nice light brown in moderate oven heat.
