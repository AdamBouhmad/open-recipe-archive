## Ingredients

- 10 liters blueberries
- 5 liters water

## Directions

1. Clean the berries and cook them with water until they are completely broken down; pour them into cheesecloth to drain.
2. Place the juice again on to cook, during which it is skimmed very carefully.
3. Bottle it hot or cold; cork the bottles and seal with resin.
