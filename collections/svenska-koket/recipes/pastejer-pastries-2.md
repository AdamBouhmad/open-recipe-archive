## Ingredients

- Pâte à choux: 1 part water, 1/8 egg, 1 tablespoon brandy, 1/4 kilogram flour, 350 grams butter
- Filling: 6 parts cream, 6 egg yolks, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 6 bitter almonds, 45 grams flour

## Directions

1. Prepare the pastry dough as fine pâte à choux (recipe 546)
2. Roll out the dough to approximately 3 millimeters thickness and line the pastry molds
3. For the filling, whisk all ingredients over heat until the cream begins to simmer and thicken
4. Continue whisking until the cream is cold
5. Fill the pastry molds halfway with the filling and place a dough lid on top
6. Bake the pastries in good oven heat
