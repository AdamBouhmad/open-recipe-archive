## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp currants
- 3 tsp water
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1-2 tbsp syrup
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the currants well in lukewarm water and cook them soft in 3 tsp water.
2. Fry together the butter and flour, add the currant liquid, add the syrup, and let the sauce cook for 10-15 minutes.
3. Season with sugar, vinegar and a little salt.
