## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons flour (50 g)
- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 1/2 liter weak beer
- 2 tablespoons syrup
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar (8 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon salt (3 g)
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 deciliters thick cream

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with 1/4 liter of the milk and let it swell for about 2 hours.
2. Bring the rest of the milk to a boil, add the flour mixture and let the porridge simmer while whisking for 8-10 minutes.
3. Heat the beer with the syrup and spices and pour into the porridge while whisking evenly.
4. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in the soup bowl and add the soup with vigorous whisking.
5. Serve immediately with fried bread cubes. In the simplest version, omit the egg yolks and cream.
