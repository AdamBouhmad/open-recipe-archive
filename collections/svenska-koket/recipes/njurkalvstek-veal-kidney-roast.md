## Ingredients

- 2 kg kidney roast
- 2 tsk salt (10 gr.)
- ¼ tsk white pepper
- 1 msk butter (20 gr.)
- 1 slice lard (1½ hg.)
- 1 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung as in the previous recipe, is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and some of the fat is removed. The roast is then rubbed with a mixture of salt and pepper. The kidney with some of the surrounding fat is placed inside the roast at the bone, the flank side is folded over, and the roast is tied with fine sailing twine dipped in boiling water. Wrap it in buttered paper, place it on the lard slice in a long roasting pan, and put it in a hot oven for about ½ hour. Remove the paper, add half of the boiling broth, and let the meat roast until tender, about 1½–2 hours, basting often and adding more broth as needed. Slice the roast, garnish with browned potatoes, stuffed tomatoes, or legumes, and salad or parsley. Serve with reduced sauce mixed with cream (see no. 429).
