## Ingredients

- 12 lamb chops
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 1.5 deciliters meat stock or broth

## Directions

1. The lamb chops should be cut with long bones and be approximately 2 cm thick.
2. Dry them with a cloth wrung out in hot water, trim them, scrape the bones and pound the chops on both sides.
3. Rub them with a mixture of salt and pepper.
4. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter in it, place the chops in and fry 5-8 minutes on each side.
5. Remove them to a hot serving plate, whisk the pan with the boiling meat stock and pour the sauce over the chops.
6. Serve with fried potatoes, mashed potatoes or vegetables.
7. The chops can also be breaded and fried in the same way.
