## Ingredients

- 1 small veal liver
- 1/2 kg. rim-salted pork belly
- 1 liter milk
- 1 kkp wheat flour
- salt, white pepper
- 2 msk. butter
- 3 msk. butter for garnish
- 2 msk. chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the liver, remove the membranes and blood vessels and slice it.
2. Place the slices in milk for 1 hour and then pat dry with a kitchen towel.
3. Roll them in a mixture of the flour, salt and white pepper.
4. Fry the slices a nice brown in the butter and place on the serving platter.
5. Scrape the pork belly, cut into thin slices and fry.
6. Place one slice of pork belly on each liver slice.
7. Garnish the dish with parsley butter and serve.
