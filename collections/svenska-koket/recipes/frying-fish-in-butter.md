## Ingredients

- Fish (larger fish 1½–2 kg should be cut into fillets; small fish or slices of larger fish)
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper (optional)
- Flour
- Lemon juice
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Larger fish of 1½–2 kg should preferably be cut into fillets and fried in the oven; small fish and slices of larger fish are fried in a frying pan on top of the stove.
2. After the fish is cleaned and rinsed, dry it well, rub with a little salt and pepper if desired, and turn it in flour.
3. If frying in the oven, place it in a pan richly buttered with cold butter, put it in a warm oven and baste frequently with the butter until it is cooked through.
4. If frying on top of the stove, place it in a frying pan in very hot butter, turn it and fry on both sides until it has a beautiful golden brown color.
5. Lay it on a warm platter, pour over a few drops of lemon juice, garnish on top with chopped blanched parsley and pour over with browned butter just before serving.
