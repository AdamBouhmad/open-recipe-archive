## Ingredients

- 150 gr. boneless veal
- 30 gr. butter
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp. finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1/2 tbsp. finely chopped mushrooms or truffle
- 1—1/2 dcl. cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat as in the preceding recipe and pass it together with the butter 4 times through the meat grinder.
2. Pound the forcemeat thereafter, together with the flour, in a stone mortar and work together with the beaten egg, salt, onion and mushroom for about 1/2 hour, during which the cream is added, a little at a time.
3. Taste the forcemeat and form it with 2 tsp., dipped in warm water, into very small balls, which are placed in boiling broth or salted water (not too many at once) and allowed to cook 2—3 minutes.
4. Polish the meatballs before placing them in the soup bowl.
