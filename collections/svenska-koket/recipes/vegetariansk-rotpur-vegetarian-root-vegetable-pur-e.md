## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 celeriac root
- 1 celeriac
- 1 leek
- 4 parsley sprigs
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- salt
- sugar
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Prepare the roots in the usual manner and cut into pieces.
2. Clean the leek, rinse and cut into slices; clean and rinse the parsley and tie together.
3. Put everything to cook in the water and milk together with 1 tablespoon of butter.
4. When the roots are completely tender, strain them, and prepare the purée as in recipe no. 266, seasoning with salt, sugar and, if desired, a little cayenne.
5. Place half a tablespoon of cold butter in the soup bowl and serve the purée with fried bread cubes or cheese croûtons.
