## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg pork loin with bone
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 bottle red wine
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)

## Directions

1. Remove most of the fat from the loin
2. Rub with a mixture of salt and pepper and place in a deep dish
3. Pour red wine over it and let it marinate for about 12 hours, turning it a few times
4. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the loin in with meat side up and brown in a hot oven, basting it frequently
5. Add the marinade and let it cook until tender, or about 1 1/2 hours
6. Cut as in recipe 561 and serve with strained sauce (see recipe 429) mixed with cream, along with browned potatoes and red currant jelly or preserved rowan berries
