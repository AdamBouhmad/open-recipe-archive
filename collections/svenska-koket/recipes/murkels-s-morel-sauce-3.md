## Ingredients

- 15 gr. dried morels
- 1/2 liter cold water
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr.)
- 5 dcl. meat stock
- 1 dcl. truffle broth
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the morels and let them soak in plenty of water for a few hours.
2. Place them in the cold water and let them cook until they are soft, or about 1/2 hour, then strain them through a colander and save the broth.
3. Cut the mushroom into small dice, which are fried in the butter until it becomes clear.
4. The sauce is then prepared as in the preceding recipe.
