## Ingredients

- 1 fresh ox tongue
- water
- 3-4 tablespoons salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- ¼ teaspoon white pepper
- ½ liter boiling broth
- 3 tablespoons tomato puree
- 1 can mushrooms (¼ liter)

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the tongue as in the previous recipe, but do not cook it longer than until the skin can just barely be peeled off.
2. Brown the butter in a small long pan, place the tongue in it and brown it in a hot oven, basting it often with the butter.
3. Sprinkle with the spices, add the broth and let it roast for ½ hour, basting it about every 10 minutes.
4. Add the tomato puree and mushrooms and let the tongue roast another ½ hour, or until it is completely tender.
5. Slice and arrange as in the previous recipe, garnish with the mushrooms and parsley and serve with brown mushroom sauce, peas and browned potatoes.
