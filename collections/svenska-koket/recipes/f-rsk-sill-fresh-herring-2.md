## Ingredients

- Fresh herring
- Salt
- Flour
- Eggs
- Milk
- Parsley
- Butter
- Roe
- Liver
- Lemon juice
- Boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Fresh herring should be glossy with red gills and feel firm in the flesh.
2. Clean in the usual manner and place in boiling, heavily salted water to cook for 10-15 minutes.
3. Treat the fish as in recipe no. 384. Remove and discard the head and tail.
4. Rub the fish with salt and let it rest for 2 hours, then dry well.
5. Cut into 4.5 cm wide pieces crosswise and place in a butter-greased ovenproof dish, preferably with a lid, together with the roe and liver cut into pieces.
6. Whisk the eggs well with the milk, mix in the parsley and pour the egg mixture over the fish.
7. Place the lid on the dish or cover it with butter-greased double waxed paper.
8. Cook in a water bath in the oven for approximately 40 minutes or until the fish feels tender.
9. Serve from the dish with melted butter mixed with lemon juice and boiled potatoes.
