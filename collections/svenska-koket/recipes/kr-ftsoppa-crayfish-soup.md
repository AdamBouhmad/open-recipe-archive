## Ingredients

- water
- 2 handfuls coarse salt
- 40 medium-sized crayfish
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2 French breads
- 2 to 2 1/2 liters fish or meat broth

## Directions

1. Place the crayfish in boiling salted water and cook for 15-20 minutes, then remove with a slotted spoon and let drain.
2. Remove the tails and claws, and pound the shells with the butter.
3. Place the shells in a pot together with the bread, add the boiling broth (preferably fish broth), and let the soup simmer slowly over low heat for about 1 hour.
4. Strain the soup, add the crayfish tails and serve very hot.
5. If the soup needs to be colored, it should be done with crayfish butter and not with cochineal.
