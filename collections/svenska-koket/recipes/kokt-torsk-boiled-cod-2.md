## Ingredients

- 1 cod of approximately 3/4 kg
- 1/2 tbsp vinegar
- 2 1/2 tbsp salt (43 g)
- 10 g salt per liter of water

## Directions

1. Brush the cod with vinegar and scrape well from the tail upwards.
2. Clean it, rinse very thoroughly and dry.
3. Save the roe and liver, cook them separately in salted water and use for garnishing the fish.
4. Cut off the head (from which the gills have been removed) and the tail, as they easily fall apart during cooking. They can be cooked separately and used as garnish, or grilled and fried.
5. Make three incisions on each side of the thickest part of the fish and rub the fish with salt.
6. Let it lie for a couple of hours.
7. Cook it as in recipe no. 327 and requires great attention during cooking, which takes 10-15 minutes.
8. Place on a warm platter with a slotted spoon, garnish with the liver and roe cut into slices, as well as with parsley and small boiled potatoes.
9. Serve with melted butter and chopped eggs or mustard sauce or crayfish sauce.
