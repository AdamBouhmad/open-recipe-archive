## Ingredients

- 250 grams wheat flour
- 425 grams butter
- 1 part water
- 1 tablespoon spirits
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 cups apple sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as fine butter dough (no. 548)
2. Roll out to 1/2 cm thickness and cut into round cakes
3. Place a little apple sauce on one half of the cake and fold the other half over, pressing the edges well together
4. Cut the edge with a piping tool or knife into round points
5. Alternatively, cut out square cakes and make an incision in each corner with a piping tool, place a little apple sauce in the middle, fold every other of the eight flaps up over the apple sauce, and press them together against each other
6. Bake the pastries in a warm oven
