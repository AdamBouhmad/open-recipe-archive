## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 2 tablespoons chopped mushrooms
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1/2 egg for coating
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for coating

## Directions

1. Scale the pike, clean it (see page 171), keep the head but remove the gills and fins
2. Make cuts 2 cm apart, rub with salt and let rest 1 hour, then dry again
3. Cream 2 tablespoons (40 gr.) of the butter until white and fluffy, and blend with the parsley, mushrooms, and tomatoes
4. Stuff the pike with this mixture, sew it closed, brush with egg and coat with crushed rusks
5. Butter a long pan with some of the butter, covering an area the size of the fish, place the pike in it with the back up, dot with remaining butter
6. Place in a warm oven and roast for about 1/2 hour, basting often with the butter
7. Place on a warm platter, remove the thread, pour the butter from the pan over the fish and garnish with parsley and fluted lemon slices
8. Serve with mushroom or tomato sauce
