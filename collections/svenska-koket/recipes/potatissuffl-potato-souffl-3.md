## Ingredients

- 1¼ liters potatoes
- 40 grams butter
- 3 eggs
- 1–2 deciliters thick cream
- ¼ teaspoon white pepper
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- ½ tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Treat the potatoes as described on page 337, peel thinly, boil, and press through a purée press.
2. Stir the cold butter into the mash with a fork together with the beaten egg yolks and cream.
3. Add the seasonings and finally fold in the egg whites, beaten until stiff.
4. Place the mixture in a buttered and bread-crumbed ovenproof mold, and sprinkle with finely crushed rusks.
5. Bake the soufflé in good oven heat until it has taken on a nice golden-brown color, or about 40 minutes.
6. Serve immediately after removing from the oven to the smorgasbord or with fried meat in general.
