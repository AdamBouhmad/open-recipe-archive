## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 hg. salt salmon
- 1 lit. skimmed milk
- 1 lit. cooked cold potatoes
- 3 eggs
- 2 tsp. flour (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. sour milk
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley
- For the mold: 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.), 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rinse the salmon and cut it into slices. Let it soak in the skimmed milk for about 3 hours, then remove and wrap in a cloth or white paper.
2. Peel the potatoes, cut into slices and layer alternately with the salmon in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pudding mold.
3. Beat the eggs with the flour, milk and pepper and pour the egg mixture into the mold.
4. Sprinkle the pudding with parsley and bake in the oven for about 40 minutes.
5. Serve with melted butter or currant sauce.
