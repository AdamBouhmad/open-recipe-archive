## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 cups milk
- salt
- sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams)

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs well with the milk and add the seasonings.
2. Butter a tin mold or mold, pour in the egg mixture, cover with a lid and place the mold in a water bath on top of the stove.
3. Make sure the water does not boil but only remains hot, until the custard is sufficiently set, otherwise it will become watery.
4. Turn out onto a warm platter, garnish with parsley and serve with boiled ham or sausage, fried meat or fish.
5. The egg custard can also be baked in a water bath in the oven.
