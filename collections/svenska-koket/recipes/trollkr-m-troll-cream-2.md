## Ingredients

- 4 baked apples
- 2 egg whites
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 kilogram granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until quite stiff.
2. Pass the baked apples and fold into the egg white together with the sugar and lemon juice.
3. Stir the mixture for 1 hour with uneven speed, i.e., move the spoon quickly through the mixture one moment and slowly the next.
4. When the cream has turned into white foam, it is ready.
5. Place on a serving dish and can be garnished with carmine sugar if desired.
