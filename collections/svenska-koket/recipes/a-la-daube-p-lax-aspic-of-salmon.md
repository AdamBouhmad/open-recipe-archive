## Ingredients

- 1½ kg salmon
- 1½ msk salt
- a few allspice berries
- dill stalks
- 1½ liters water
- ¾ liter fish stock
- 2 egg whites
- dill for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon into pieces and place on the heat together with the spices and dill stalks in hot water and let it simmer gently until tender, but not so that it falls apart.
2. Remove the fish and place it in a beautiful aspic mold.
3. Place the most beautiful pieces on the bottom and on the sides.
4. Clarify the stock with egg whites, strain through a cloth or cheesecloth and pour over the fish.
5. Place the aspic in a cold place to set, turn out and garnish with dill.
