## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cooked broth
- 1 pound stewed chopped meat
- 2 small red onions
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 cup rice grains
- 1 liter milk
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/3 liter bouillon
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed hardtack for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel the onion, chop finely and brown together with the meat in butter, then add the seasonings.
2. Rinse the rice grains and place in the cold milk and cook while stirring until soft.
3. Pour out to cool, then add the beaten egg and salt.
4. Stir the browned meat and bouillon into the porridge, and fill the mixture into a buttered mold, sprinkle with crushed hardtack and bake in the oven.
5. Serve the pudding with meat gravy or clarified butter.
