## Ingredients

- 150 grams sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 6 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Blanch and dry the almonds, grate or grind them through a mill and pound them in a mortar together with the sugar
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in last
3. Pipe the almond mixture onto well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets into small mounds
4. Bake in very weak oven heat
5. If desired, they may be garnished with red and white icing
