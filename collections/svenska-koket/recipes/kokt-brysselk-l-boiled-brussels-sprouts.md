## Ingredients

- 2 liters Brussels sprouts
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Clean the sprouts and place them in vinegar-mixed water for about 1/2 hour.
2. Remove them, place them in boiling salted water (short broth) and let them cook until tender; then remove them immediately from the broth, place them on the serving plate and pour the melted butter over them.
