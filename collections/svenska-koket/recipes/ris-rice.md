## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups Carolina rice
- 3 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice in lukewarm water, place it in boiling salted water and let it cook until tender, or approximately 30 minutes.
2. Pour it into a colander and place it beside the fire to dry.
3. Stir the rice now and then with a fork so it does not stick together.
