## Ingredients

- 210 gr. sweet almonds
- 8 gr. bitter almonds
- 200 gr. sugar
- 160 gr. butter
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 egg white
- 15 gr. wheat flour
- 3 cups apple compote (made from 2 liters apples, 1 cup crushed sugar, water)

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds, dry in the oven and grate (or pass through an almond mill) and pound very finely together with the sugar.
2. Clarify the butter and stir until it becomes white, then mix with the egg yolks, egg white and wheat flour.
3. Add the almonds and stir the mixture well.
4. Butter a pie mold and line with the above mixture, then nearly fill the mold with apple compote.
5. Place a ring of dough around the edge of the mold.
6. Bake in mild oven heat and serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
