## Ingredients

- 125 gr. butter
- 2 1/2 dcl. water
- 210 gr. flour
- 4 eggs
- 5 dcl. firm potato mash
- salt, sugar
- 1 kg. fat for frying

## Directions

1. Butter and water are brought to a boil, the flour is whisked in under vigorous boiling and the batter is allowed to boil until it releases from the pan and has a golden color.
2. It is poured out and allowed to cool, whereupon the eggs are worked in one at a time and the batter is stirred for 10 minutes.
3. The potato mash is prepared as in recipe 677, allowed to cool and added to the batter with vigorous stirring, whereupon the mass is seasoned with spices.
4. The mass is formed with two spoons, dipped in the fat, into round balls, which, if desired, can be filled with chopped truffle or peas, are fried golden brown in hot fat and placed on blotting paper to drain.
5. Served as garnish around roasted meat. If the balls are not filled, they should be made very small.
