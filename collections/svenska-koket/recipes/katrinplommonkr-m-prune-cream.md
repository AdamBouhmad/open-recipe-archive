## Ingredients

- 250 gr fine prunes (about 23 pieces)
- 1 dcl caster sugar (85 gr)
- 7 dcl water
- 2 dcl French wine
- 1 1/2 tbsp potato flour (23 gr)

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes very well and layer alternately with the sugar in a bowl, add water, and let the prunes stand overnight.
2. Cut them in half lengthwise and remove the pits.
3. Crush half of the pits, scald the almonds and chop finely.
4. Put the prunes together with the almonds in the water they were soaked in, add the wine, and let the prunes boil until they are properly soft.
5. Thicken the cream with the potato flour mixed in 1/2 dcl cold water, let boil for 2 minutes, taste and pour into a compote bowl rinsed with warm water.
6. Serve cold with cream or milk.
