## Ingredients

- 3 hectograms macaroni
- water
- 3 eggs
- ½ liter unskimmed milk
- 1 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs
- 3 calf kidneys
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons wheat flour
- white pepper
- salt
- sugar
- ½ cup grated cheese
- broth
- pepper
- salt
- Madeira

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into 1 decimeter long pieces and blanch well in water.
2. Place in a buttered and bread-crumbed ring mold.
3. Whisk the eggs together with the milk and seasonings, and pour the mixture over the macaroni.
4. Place the pudding in the oven to bake for 1 hour.
5. Blanch the kidneys and cut into cubes; brown these in butter, add broth and let them stew until soft.
6. Combine butter and flour, add meat stock and broth, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
7. Add the kidneys and season the stew with seasonings and wine.
8. Turn out the pudding, sprinkle the grated cheese over, and place the stew in the middle.
