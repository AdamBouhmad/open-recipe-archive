## Ingredients

- 35 brussels sprout heads
- 3 liters water for blanching
- 1 tablespoon salt for blanching
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1.5 liters stock

## Directions

1. Blanch the brussels sprouts in boiling salted water
2. Make a roux with butter and flour
3. Bring the stock to a boil with the vegetable broth, pour in the roux while whisking vigorously
4. Let the soup simmer with continued whisking for about 8 minutes
5. Remove from heat, add the blanched brussels sprouts with their broth, season with spices and wine
6. Taste carefully and serve over meat in soup bowls, accompanied by goose sausage sliced and arranged on a separate dish
