## Ingredients

- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 50 gr. sugar
- 1 part milk
- 2 eggs
- 6 sheets gelatin
- 0.5 liters thick cream
- biscuits for garnish

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, grate them and pound together with the sugar to a fine paste.
2. Whip the almond paste, milk and eggs together in a saucepan, and let the mixture simmer over heat while whisking until the cream thickens.
3. Add the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water, and when the mixture is cold, fold in the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugared mold and set out to stiffen.
5. When it is to be served, dip the mold briefly in lukewarm water, turn out the pudding and garnish with jam and biscuits.
