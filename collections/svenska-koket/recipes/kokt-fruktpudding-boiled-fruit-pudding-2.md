## Ingredients

- 50 gr. dried ring apples
- 1 hg. dried apricots
- 1 1/2 hg. prunes
- 4 dcl. water
- 1 1/2 dcl. granulated sugar (143 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
- 3 eggs
- 2 dcl. cream
- 2 dcl. granulated sugar for the pan
- 2 tbsp. boiling water for the pan

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit very thoroughly in several lukewarm waters and let it soak in cold water overnight.
2. From this water and sugar, cook a clear, thick syrup; in it cook first the apples, then the apricots and last the prunes, after they have been pitted.
3. Cook the syrup together until it is thick, and pour it over the fruit.
4. Melt the granulated sugar with stirring in a frying pan, dilute with boiling water, and with this line a smooth, low sheet pan.
5. When the fruit is cold, layer it in the pan.
6. Whip the eggs and cream together and pour over the fruit.
7. Bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour, or until the egg mixture is set.
8. Remove it, loosen it from the edges and turn it out after 15 minutes.
9. Serve preferably cold, if desired, with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
