## Ingredients

- 150 gr. rice
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- approximately 3 hectograms cooked or fried white meat (leftovers)
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 2 deciliters milk
- 2 deciliters broth
- 1 egg yolk
- salt, white pepper
- For the mold: 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.), 3 tablespoons crushed rusks, 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice, add to boiling salted water and let cook vigorously for 15-20 minutes.
2. Drain in a colander, let run off and mix while hot with 30 gr. of the butter.
3. Cut the meat into fine cubes.
4. Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes, dilute with the milk and broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes, then finish with the egg yolk and season with the seasonings.
5. Butter a heat-resistant mold and sprinkle with crushed rusks, layer alternately rice, meat and sauce in it.
6. The top layer should be sauce, sprinkled with the grated cheese and the remaining crushed rusks.
7. Place the dish in a warm oven and gratinee as in no. 112.
8. Serve as a breakfast dish.
