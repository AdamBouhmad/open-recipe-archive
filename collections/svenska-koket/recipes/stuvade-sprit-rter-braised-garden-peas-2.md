## Ingredients

- 1 litre shelled garden peas (approximately 5 litres unshelled)
- 10 small fresh carrots
- 1/2 litre water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 2 tablespoon flour (20 gr.)
- 4-4 1/2 decilitres pea cooking liquid
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Treat the peas as in the previous recipe.
2. Rinse the carrots, scrape them, rinse again, dry and cut into fine cubes.
3. Place the peas and carrots in boiling water with salt and sugar, and let them simmer slowly until tender without falling apart.
4. Drain the cooking liquid and reserve it. Brown the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the pea liquid, and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Add the peas and carrots along with the parsley, and season to taste.
6. Serve with roasted fish, braised ham, forcemeat, etc.
