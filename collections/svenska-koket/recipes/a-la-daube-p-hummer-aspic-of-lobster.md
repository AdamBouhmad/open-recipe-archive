## Ingredients

- 3 fresh lobsters (or 1 can preserved lobster at ½ liter)
- 2¼ liters fish stock
- 2 msk white French wine
- 10 gelatin sheets
- 2 msk water
- 2 egg whites
- parsley for garnish
- 1 egg for garnish

## Directions

1. Place the fish stock on the heat together with the wine and the gelatin dissolved in a little hot water.
2. When the mixture is warm, whisk in the egg whites beaten to a light foam, and the aspic is given a short boil while whisking.
3. Strain through a cloth or cheesecloth wrung out in hot water.
4. Pour the clear aspic on the bottom of a rinsed aspic mold and place it to set.
5. Garnish the mold with hard-boiled eggs and parsley.
6. Place the most beautiful pieces of lobster on the bottom and sides, and fill the mold with the remaining lobster meat.
7. Carefully pour the remaining aspic over, and let the aspic set in a cold place.
