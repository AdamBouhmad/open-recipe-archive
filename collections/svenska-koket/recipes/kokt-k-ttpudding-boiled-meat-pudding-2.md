## Ingredients

- 400 g boneless cooked or fried meat
- 6 deciliters boiling milk
- 1 1/2 deciliters finely crushed rusks
- 3-4 eggs
- 100 g butter (melted)
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped salt cucumber
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 10 g butter for the onion
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Chop the meat very finely (do not grind) and mix with the milk, rusks and the beaten egg yolks, then let the mixture stand for 1 hour to swell; if it is then too firm, add a little more milk.
2. Add the butter, seasonings, the cucumber and the onion, fried in butter, and taste carefully. Finally fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites.
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated metal pan and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for about 1 1/2 hours. Turn out and garnish with slices of tomatoes or cucumber and parsley. Serve with gravy, meat sauce or melted butter. If desired, the cucumber and onion can be omitted.
