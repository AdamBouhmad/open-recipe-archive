## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. boneless veal from the loin
- 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter for frying
- 3 tablespoons crayfish butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- asparagus stock
- 4-5 parts thin cream
- 1 tin lobster (1/2-3/4 liter)
- 1 tin asparagus (1/4 liter)
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into 1 cm. thick slices, which are beaten lightly. Season with salt and pepper and fry in butter.
2. Fry butter and flour together and stir with asparagus stock and cream, and let the sauce simmer 10 minutes. Then add the lobster cut into pieces and the asparagus, and season the sauce and pour it over the meat.
3. Serve the arrangement at once.
