## Ingredients

- 0.75 kilograms lamb breast
- 2 liters broth
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 leek
- 3 medium-sized carrots
- 0.25 celery
- 1 parsnip
- 2 cups fresh or canned peas
- 8 small Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 cauliflower head
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Wipe the lamb breast with a cloth dipped in hot water, break it and place it to cook in 1.5 liters of broth. When the meat is half-cooked, remove it and cut it into square pieces, which are browned in 1 tablespoon of butter, together with the leek cut into strips.
2. Sprinkle the flour over, stir the meat well and thin it with broth and let it cook slowly until the meat is tender. Prepare the carrots, celery and parsnips in the usual way and cut them into fine strips and fry them in the remaining butter; add the peas and the rest of the broth, and let everything cook with the lid closed until done.
3. Cook the Jerusalem artichokes and cauliflower in salted water. When the vegetables are cooked, place them together with their cooking liquid in the soup tureen. Skim the broth carefully, season with pepper and salt and pour it together with the meat over the vegetables. Serve immediately.
