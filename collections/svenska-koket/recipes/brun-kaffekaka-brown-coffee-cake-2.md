## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- 3 hectograms brown sugar
- 3 deciliters thick, sour cream
- 1 hectogram melted butter
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
- 8 finely crushed cardamom seeds
- 1 teaspoon crushed cloves
- 1 small teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
- 4 hectograms wheat flour
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes.
2. Add the cream beaten to peaks, the melted butter, the spices, and last the flour.
