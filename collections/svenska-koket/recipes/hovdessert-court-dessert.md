## Ingredients

- 4 small egg whites
- 200 grams granulated sugar
- 1-2 tablespoons hot water
- 100 grams candied chocolate or 75 grams unsweetened chocolate and sugar to taste
- 1 hectogram sweet almond
- 1/2 liter thick cream
- 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form, and continue beating for 1 1/4 hours
2. Gently fold in the previously sifted granulated sugar
3. Arrange the batter on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets as large meringues
4. Place them immediately in a mild oven heat and bake very slowly
5. When they are dry and release from the sheet, remove them and let cool
6. Arrange them high on a dish
7. Dissolve the chocolate in a little hot water and let it cook to a proper consistency
8. When the chocolate is cold, pour it over the meringues
9. Blanch the almond, cut into strips and sprinkle over the chocolate
10. Just before serving, whip the cream to stiff peaks together with vanilla sugar, and garnish the pyramid with this
