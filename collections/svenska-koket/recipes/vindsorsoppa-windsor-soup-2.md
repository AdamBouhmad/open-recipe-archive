## Ingredients

- 2 veal knuckles
- 1 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1.5 liters beef broth
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 1 portion Madeira
- sugar (optional)
- salt
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch the knuckles, sauté them and cook them in the water for two hours, then remove them
2. Cut the meat into pieces and strain and skim the cooking liquid
3. Brown the butter and flour together
4. Add half a liter of the cooking liquid and the broth, as well as the cleaned and rinsed parsley, wine and seasonings
5. Let the soup cook for half an hour over low heat
6. Remove the parsley bunch
7. Serve the cut meat in the soup, which should have a distinct wine flavor
