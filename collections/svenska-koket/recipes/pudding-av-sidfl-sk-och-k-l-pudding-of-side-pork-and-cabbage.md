## Ingredients

- 750 g lean, cured side pork
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 medium head white cabbage
- water and salt for blanching

## Directions

1. Rinse the pork and place it in cold water and let it cook for 1/2 hour.
2. Remove the outer leaves of the cabbage head, loosen the remaining leaves from the core and cut them into coarse strips.
3. Bring water to a boil with a little salt, add the cabbage and let it cook 5 minutes uncovered, then place it on a sieve so the water drains well.
4. Cut the pork into slices and taste the liquid.
5. In a pudding mold with a lid, layer the cabbage and pork alternately, and add 1/2 liter of the liquid. Cover and let the pudding cook in a water bath on top of the stove for approximately 2 hours.
6. Serve with brown sauce.
