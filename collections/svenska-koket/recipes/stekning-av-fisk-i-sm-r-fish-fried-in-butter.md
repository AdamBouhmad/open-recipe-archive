## Ingredients

- Large fish 1.5-2 kg or small fish and slices of larger fish
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Cold butter
- Lemon juice
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Larger fish weighing 1.5-2 kg should preferably be cut into fillets and fried in the oven; small fish and slices of larger fish are fried in a pan on the stove.
2. After cleaning and rinsing the fish, dry it well, rub lightly with salt and pepper if desired, and dredge in flour.
3. To fry in the oven: place in a pan well buttered with cold butter, place in a warm oven and baste frequently with the butter until cooked through.
4. To fry on the stove: place in a frying pan in very hot butter, turn and fry on both sides until a beautiful golden-brown color is achieved.
5. Place on a warm dish, sprinkle with a few drops of lemon juice, garnish on top with chopped parsley that has been briefly blanched, and drizzle with browned butter just before serving.
