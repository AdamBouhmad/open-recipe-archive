## Ingredients

- 6 medium-sized Portuguese onions
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. finely chopped red onion
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 1 tbsp. fresh bread crumbs
- 1 tbsp. tomato puree
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- a little white pepper
- 1 tsp. finely chopped parsley
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. finely crushed croutons

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, cut away one quarter of the onion from the top, after which parboil them (place in boiling, salted water and let boil without cover) until they are tender but do not fall apart.
2. Lay them on cloth and let them drain, whereupon the interior is removed with a teaspoon, so that only about 8 mm. of the onion remains.
3. Chop the interior finely and fry it in the butter together with the red onion, the mushrooms cut into thin slices, the bread crumbs, tomato puree, seasonings and parsley. Fry the mixture until it becomes of suitable thickness, then fill it into the onions and sprinkle with finely crushed croutons.
4. Brown the butter in a casserole, add the onions, place the casserole in a warm oven, and gratinee the mixture until it gets a nice golden brown color, or about 10-15 minutes. Meanwhile baste the onions frequently with the butter in the pan.
5. Serve to the smorgasbord or as garnish around roasted meat. The onions can also be filled with risotto (see recipe no. 133), tomatoes or spinach souffle (see recipe no. 728).
