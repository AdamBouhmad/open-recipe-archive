## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg cooked fish
- 1.5 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp flour
- 0.25 liter fish broth
- 0.25 liter milk or cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Free the cooked fish from skin and bones and divide into suitable-sized pieces.
2. Melt the butter and flour together, add the fish broth and milk with careful stirring, and let the sauce boil for 10 minutes.
3. Add the fish, stir the braising gently and let it come to a brief boil, then taste and adjust seasoning.
