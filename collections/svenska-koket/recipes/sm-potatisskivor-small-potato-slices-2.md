## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 1 kg. fat
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Treat the potatoes as in the previous recipe and cut into 2 mm. thick slices, which are cut out with a round spoon into small, even slices, which are allowed to lie in cold water about 10 minutes.
2. Dry them well in a cloth, boil in hot fat until they are crispy, lay up on absorbent paper to drain and salt.
3. Serve warm or cold with game.
