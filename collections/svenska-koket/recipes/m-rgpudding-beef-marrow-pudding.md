## Ingredients

- 1 hg. beef marrow
- 30 gr. fine kidney fat
- 50 gr. bread crumbs
- 1/2 dcl. milk
- 1 hg. caster sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 hg. preserved fruit, in fine cubes
- 45 gr. sultana raisins
- 30 gr. pitted Malaga raisins
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. wheat flour (20 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. The marrow and fat are cleaned well, rinsed, wrapped in a cloth, placed in a colander, melted in a water bath, and the fat is allowed to cool.
2. Bread crumbs are weighed, soaked in the milk and wrung out.
3. The fat is worked well with the sugar, the wrung-out bread, the whipped eggs, the egg yolks, the fruit cubes, the cleaned and rinsed sultana raisins and the cleaned, rinsed, split and pitted Malaga raisins.
4. The mixture is poured into a buttered and floured smooth ring mold and baked in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
5. Serve warm, covered with hot wine sauce (see no. 457), mixed with rum or with flaming sauce (see no. 465), which is ignited when the pudding is brought to the table.
