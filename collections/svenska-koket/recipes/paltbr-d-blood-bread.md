## Ingredients

- 1 liter blood
- 6 parts weak beer
- 2 kg coarse rye flour
- 2 tbsp salt
- 100 gr compressed yeast
- 8 tbsp melted lard
- 0.5 tsp ground white pepper
- 0.5 tsk grated ginger
- 1 tsk finely ground marjoram
- 1 cup syrup

## Directions

1. Strain the blood and whisk well together with the beer
2. Add the flour, salt and dissolved yeast, work the dough well and let it rise
3. When it has risen to double its size, add the lard, spices and syrup, and let the dough rise once more
4. Form into oblong loaves or round cakes (in the latter case, make indentations with a knife)
5. Let rise again and bake in good oven heat
6. When the oblong loaves have cooled, slice them and dry well in the oven
7. Hang the cakes on sticks from the ceiling to dry
