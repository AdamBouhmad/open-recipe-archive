## Ingredients

- 1 kg cooked, boned fish meat or 1.25 kg pike
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g) for sauce
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 4 dcl fish stock
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 6 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the fish meat into beautiful, even slices.
2. Grease an ovenproof dish or silver dish with half the butter.
3. Lay the fish slices on top and distribute the remaining butter in flakes over the fish.
4. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes.
5. Dilute with fish stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
6. Season with salt, sugar, and white pepper, pour out and let cool.
7. Add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the sauce for 10 minutes.
8. Finally, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
9. Pour the sauce over the fish.
10. Sprinkle the grated cheese on top.
11. Place in a hot oven to bake for 15-20 minutes.
12. Serve, if desired, with lemon or mushroom sauce.
