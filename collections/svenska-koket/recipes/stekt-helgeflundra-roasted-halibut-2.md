## Ingredients

- 1½ kg halibut
- 1½ tsk vinegar
- 4 tbsp salt
- 2 liters water
- 2 tbsp butter
- 4 tbsp crushed hardtack
- 3-4 cups boiling water
- juice of ½ lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, rinse, and scald the dark side with hot water and scrape it.
2. Rub it with the vinegar and let it lie in the water, mixed with the salt, for 1 hour.
3. Remove the fish and dry it with a cloth, score on one side and place in a baking pan, buttered over an area as large as the fish occupies.
4. Divide the rest of the butter into small flakes and place on top of the fish, sprinkle with crushed hardtack and put in a fairly hot oven.
5. When it is brown, dilute with boiling water and baste every ten minutes.
6. The roasting takes ½ to ¾ hour.
7. When the fish is roasted, place it on a dish. Strain the sauce, add the lemon juice and taste it.
8. Garnish the fish with parsley and lemon sliced. Serve the sauce in a separate sauce bowl.
