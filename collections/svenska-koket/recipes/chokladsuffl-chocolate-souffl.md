## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 100 g caster sugar
- 50 g cocoa powder
- 60 g butter
- 50 g flour
- 1/4 liter unwhipped milk
- 1 tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Melt butter and flour together, add the milk gradually while stirring constantly, and let the mixture come to a good boil.
2. Stir the egg yolks and sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy; blend in the cocoa together with the cooked mixture.
3. Finally fold in the whites, beaten until stiff.
4. Pour the batter immediately into a mold buttered with cold butter, cover with parchment paper, place in a water bath and let cook slowly for about 1/3 hour.
5. Turn out onto a plate and serve warm with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
