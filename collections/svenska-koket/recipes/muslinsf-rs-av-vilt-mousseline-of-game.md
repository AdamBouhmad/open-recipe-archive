## Ingredients

- 1 large hare, approximately 450 gr. scraped meat
- 2 tsp. salt (30 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 1 whole egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 5-6 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the hare. Cut off the head, paws and flanks, then rinse the hare very well, dry it and remove all membranes.
2. Cut all the meat loose from the bones, scrape it free from tendons and membranes, mix with the spices and pass 6 times through the meat grinder.
3. Pound the forcemeat in a stone mortar until it becomes even and smooth, then add the whipped egg and egg yolk with vigorous stirring.
4. Pass the forcemeat through a hair sieve, place it in a mold or smaller pot, which is set in a bowl with ice and let it stand 1 hour, or until it is completely cooled.
5. Stir it then, let it remain on the ice and add the cream a little at a time with even and vigorous stirring.
6. Test the forcemeat as in the preceding recipe.
7. Forcemeat prepared in this way is particularly fine and delicate and is well suited to finer intermediate courses and garnishes.
