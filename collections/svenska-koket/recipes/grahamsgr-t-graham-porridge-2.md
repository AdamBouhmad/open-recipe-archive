## Ingredients

- 1.3 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 grams)
- 3 deciliters graham flour (165 grams)

## Directions

1. Bring the water to a boil with salt
2. Whisk in the flour
3. Allow the porridge to cook slowly with a lid for approximately 45 minutes
4. Adjust seasoning with more salt if desired
5. Serve with cream or milk
