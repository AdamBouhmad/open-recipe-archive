## Ingredients

- 1 poularde or 1 capon, approximately 3 kg
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1 hectogram lard, in 1 slice
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1/2 liter broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the bird in the usual way, salt it inside, truss it, and cover it with the slice of lard, which should be fastened with fine string.
2. Place it breast up on a rack in a roasting pan so that it does not rest in the fat that collects in the pan.
3. Place in a warm oven and roast until tender, about 40-50 minutes, basting frequently with melted butter and thinning with broth to reduce the heat.
4. Cut and garnish as in the previous recipe.
5. Serve with the strained and skimmed meat gravy or with reduced sauce (see recipe no. 429).
