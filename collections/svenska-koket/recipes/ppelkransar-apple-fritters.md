## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg. good, large apples
- 150 gr. crushed sugar
- 1 dcl. water
- 1 msk. white wine (optional)
- 125 gr. flour
- 1 1/4 dcl. thin cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 25 gr. melted butter
- 1 msk. sugar (15 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 2 cardamom seeds
- 1 egg white
- 1 kg. oil for frying
- 1 dcl. powdered sugar (85 gr.)

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water to make a clear syrup, which may be mixed with wine if desired.
2. Peel the apples and cut them into 1/2 cm. thick slices, remove the cores with a corer, and let them lie in the sugar syrup for about 2 hours, turning them carefully 3-4 times.
3. Dry the apple slices well in a cloth, otherwise the batter will not stick to them.
4. For the frying batter, sift the flour into a bowl, stir in the cream, egg yolks, melted butter, and spices. Lastly add the egg white beaten to stiff foam.
5. Take the apple slices with a fork and dip them in the batter, which must completely cover them.
6. Place them in hot oil and fry until they have a nice light brown color on both sides.
7. Remove the fritters with a fork, place them on absorbent paper to drain, dust with sugar, and serve very hot as a dessert or with coffee or tea.
