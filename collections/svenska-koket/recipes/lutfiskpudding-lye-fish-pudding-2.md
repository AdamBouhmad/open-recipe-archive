## Ingredients

- 80 gr. butter
- 2 dcl. finely crushed rusks
- 1/2 lit. finely chopped cooked lutfisk (leftovers)
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 lit. cream milk
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1/2 msk. butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Clarify the butter and mix with the crushed rusks.
2. Add the lye fish along with the well-beaten eggs.
3. Bring the milk to a boil and pour it hot over the mixture, then stir well.
4. Season with the spices, pour into a buttered and bread-crumbed baking dish and bake in the oven until it has taken on a beautiful, golden-brown color, or approximately 40 minutes.
5. Serve with clarified butter and small boiled potatoes.
