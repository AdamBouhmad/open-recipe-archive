## Ingredients

- 4-5 medium, firm tomatoes
- 2 teaspoons chopped tarragon leaves
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- a few grains paprika
- 2-3 tablespoons fine olive oil
- 1-1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar or wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Dip the tomatoes for a moment in hot water, peel them, and cut them in half crosswise.
2. Squeeze the tomato halves gently so that they are freed from juice and seeds.
3. Cut them into fine strips, place in a salad bowl, and pour the sauce over them.
4. Prepare the sauce as in recipe no. 448.
5. Sprinkle the salad with the chopped tarragon and serve with fine meat dishes.
