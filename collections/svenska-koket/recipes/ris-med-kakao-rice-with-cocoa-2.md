## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. Carolina rice grains (150 gr.)
- 1/2 lit. water
- 1/2 lit. boiling cream
- 75-100 gr. sweetened block chocolate
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the grains, scald them in hot water, place in boiling water and let cook 15 minutes, then dilute with cream.
2. Melt the chocolate in a water bath and add it to the porridge, which should then simmer slowly until the grains are completely soft, or about 1 hour.
3. Taste the porridge with sugar, pour into a jelly bowl and serve with whipped cream.
