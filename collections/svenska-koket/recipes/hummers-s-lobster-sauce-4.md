## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp. butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- 1/2 liter fish stock
- 1 can lobster on 1/4 liter
- salt
- white pepper
- 1-2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are fried together, fish stock is thinned in, and the sauce is allowed to simmer 10 minutes.
2. The lobster is cut into smaller pieces and added to the sauce, which is then finished with egg yolks and seasoned.
3. After the egg yolks are stirred in, the sauce must not boil further.
