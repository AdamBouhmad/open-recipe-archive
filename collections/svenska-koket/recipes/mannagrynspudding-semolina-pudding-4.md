## Ingredients

- 150 gr. semolina
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 1/2 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 20 sweet almonds
- 10 bitter almonds
- 40 gr. raisins
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 tbsp. butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Treat the semolina and cook the porridge as in recipe no. 791 but it is ready after 20 minutes, then it is poured out, and the rest of the butter is added immediately together with the seasonings, the blanched and ground almonds and the well-rinsed, parboiled and pitted raisins.
2. When the porridge is cold, the beaten egg yolks and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are stirred in.
3. The mass is poured into a buttered and breadcrumb-covered pie form and baked in regular oven heat for about 1 hour.
4. Serve immediately after removing from the oven, with jam, compote or fruit sauce.
