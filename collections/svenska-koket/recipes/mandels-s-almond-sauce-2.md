## Ingredients

- 50 gr. almonds, of which 6 bitter
- 5 dcl. cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 40 gr. powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, pass them through an almond grinder and pound in a mortar with 2 tsp. of the cream to a fine paste.
2. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes.
3. Heat the remaining cream with the almond paste and strain through muslin.
4. Heat the cream again and pour over the egg mixture with vigorous whisking.
5. Let the sauce then simmer with continued whisking until it is thick and fluffy.
6. Remove from heat and whisk until it is cold.
7. Taste the sauce and if desired, flavor with a little arrak, wine or liqueur, and it can also be colored green with spinach juice or red with carmine.
