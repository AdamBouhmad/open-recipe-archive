## Ingredients

- 1½ kilograms pike
- 1½ tablespoons salt
- 1½ teaspoons vinegar
- water
- 2 tablespoons crayfish butter
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- fish stock
- 2-3 parts cream
- 2 hectograms crayfish tails
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- 1 large cauliflower head
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the pike, rinse it well, rub it with vinegar and salt, and cook it in a short stock.
2. When it is cooked, take it up and pull off the skin. Free the fish meat from all bones and cut it into cubes.
3. Fry the crayfish butter and flour together, add the fish stock and cream, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
4. Stir the crayfish tails and the fish meat cut into cubes into the stew, and taste it.
5. Cook the cauliflower in salted water, place it in the middle of the serving plate, and pour the stew around it. Pour some of the clear sauce over the cauliflower, and serve the dish immediately.
