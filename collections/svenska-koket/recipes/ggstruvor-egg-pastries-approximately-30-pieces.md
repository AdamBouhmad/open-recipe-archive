## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 75 gr. caster sugar
- 2 1/2 dl. thick cream
- 200-225 gr. wheat flour
- 1/2 kg. deep fat for cooking

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar for approximately 1/2 hour.
2. Add the cream and sifted flour, and mix the batter well.
3. Heat the deep fat in an iron pot, no more than a small teacup full.
4. Through a pastry horn or ordinary funnel, drizzle a tablespoon of the batter back and forth into the fat so that it forms a latticed cake.
5. When the pastry is golden brown on the underside, turn it with a slotted spoon, and when it has achieved an even color, remove it and bend it over a stick and let it drain on gray paper.
6. Sprinkle the pastries with sugar before serving.
