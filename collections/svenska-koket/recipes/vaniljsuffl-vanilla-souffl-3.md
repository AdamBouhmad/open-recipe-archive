## Ingredients

- 50 gr butter
- 50 gr flour
- 1/4 liter unskinned milk
- 50 gr caster sugar
- 2 tbsp vanilla sugar
- 5 eggs
- preserved fruit
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and flour together, add the milk while stirring vigorously, and let the mixture come to a good boil.
2. Stir the egg yolks and sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy, then blend into the cooked mixture.
3. Lastly fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. In a buttered pie mold place a layer of preserved fruit and pour the mixture over it.
5. Bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for about 1/2 hour.
6. Serve warm.
