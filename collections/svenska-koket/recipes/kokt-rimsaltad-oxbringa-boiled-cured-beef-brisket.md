## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. cured brisket
- 2 1/2 liters water

## Directions

1. When the brisket is moderately salted (see salting of beef), rinse it, place in cold water and cook as in recipe 497.
2. Cut the meat into even pieces and serve with potato purée or browned turnips and a little of the broth, freed from fat, and French or English mustard.
