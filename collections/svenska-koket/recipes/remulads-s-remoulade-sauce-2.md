## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 3 raw egg yolks
- 8 skinned and boned anchovies
- 1/8 part olive oil
- 1 tbsp. chopped chives
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- 1 tbsp. French mustard
- 1 tbsp. tarragon vinegar or wine vinegar
- 1 part strong meat stock
- 3 tbsp. pickles
- 2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. The hard-boiled egg yolks are strained together with the anchovies, and this is mixed with the raw egg yolks.
2. The olive oil is added slowly with vigorous stirring along with all the spices, the finely chopped chives and pickles.
3. Finally the cream, whipped to stiff peaks, is stirred in.
