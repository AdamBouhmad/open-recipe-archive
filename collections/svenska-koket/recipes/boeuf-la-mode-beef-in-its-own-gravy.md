## Ingredients

- 2 kg boneless inside round
- 3 hg lard
- 1 tbsp salt (15 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 1/4 liters beef broth
- 1 kg veal bones
- 6 allspice berries
- 2 cloves
- 3 carrots
- 2 small Portuguese onions
- 1 dcl French wine
- 1/2 dcl cognac

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until properly tender, is prepared and larded with 2 hg of the lard (see page 232), rubbed with the remaining spices and tied so that it has a beautiful shape.
2. The butter is browned in a roasting pot, the meat is placed in it and browned on all sides until a beautiful brown crust is formed, then the remaining lard with the marrow is placed under the roast, the broth is added and the well-cracked and washed veal bones are placed in.
3. The broth is brought to a boil and well skimmed, after which the whole spices, the seared carrots and peeled onions, wine and cognac are added and the meat is allowed to stew slowly with the lid tightly closed, until tender, or about 3 hours, turning it a few times during cooking.
4. The roast is sliced, placed on a hot platter, moistened with a couple of tablespoons of sauce and garnished with the beautiful veal bones, the nicely cut carrots and onions as well as parsley or salad. Served with boiled potatoes, pickles or pressed cucumber and strained sauce (see no. 429).
