## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 decilitres thin cream or milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon finely grated lemon peel
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 cups apple sauce or jam for garnish

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk and let it boil up with the sugar.
2. Whisk the flour gently under full boiling, and let the mass boil for 3-4 minutes, then pour it out.
3. When most of the heat has subsided, stir in the egg yolks one at a time.
4. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the mass onto a well-buttered, heat-proof dish and bake until halfway done in the oven.
6. Remove the dish and quickly garnish the cake with apple sauce or jam, sprinkle with sugar, return to the oven and finish baking.
7. Serve immediately.
