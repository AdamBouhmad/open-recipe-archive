## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes (Dutch variety)
- 1 kg. fat
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush well clean, peel thinly and cut first into even squares and thereafter into 3 mm. thick slices.
2. The slices should be precisely evenly cut, otherwise the dish will not succeed.
3. Lay them in water as they are cut, and dry them afterwards very well in a cloth.
4. Heat half the fat moderately in a fat pan, add the potato slices and let boil until they float up, then lay them in a wire basket or on absorbent paper to drain.
5. Heat the remaining fat in another pan until it is smoking hot, then immediately place the potato slices therein and let them puff up into small balloons.
6. Lay them up with a fat spoon on absorbent paper, salt and serve hot as garnish around roasted meat.
