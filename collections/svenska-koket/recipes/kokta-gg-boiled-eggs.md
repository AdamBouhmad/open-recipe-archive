## Ingredients

- Fresh eggs
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Wash the eggs well before cooking; if the shells are spotted, rub them with salt or ash and rinse.
2. Place them in a steel basket or egg net and lower them into just enough boiling water to cover the eggs evenly. If too much water is used, the shells will crack easily.
3. Let the eggs boil slowly without a lid for approximately 3-3½ minutes; if soft-boiled eggs are desired with a hard white and runny yolk, boil for 5 minutes.
4. If the eggs are to be used for sauce or garnishing, boil them for 10 minutes.
5. Rinse the soft-boiled eggs quickly in cold water, dry them, and serve immediately. Leave the hard-boiled eggs in cold water for approximately 10 minutes so the shell will come off easily.
