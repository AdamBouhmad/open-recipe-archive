## Ingredients

- 1 kg preserved onions
- 2 liters water
- 4 tablespoons salt
- 2 liters vinegar (2 degrees spirit-vinegar per liter of water)
- 5 leaves nutmeg
- 2.5 hectograms sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and let them lie for one day in a brine of salt and water.
2. Boil half the vinegar together with the nutmeg and sugar.
3. Add the onions and let them cook slowly until clear and soft (test with a whisk).
4. Take up the onions with a skimming ladle and place them in a jar.
5. Pour the remaining vinegar over the onions, which is covered with a doubled towel and a plate.
6. When cold, bind it closed.
