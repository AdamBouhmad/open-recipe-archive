## Ingredients

- 8 parts cream
- 3 egg yolks
- 0.5 coffee spoon sugar
- 0.5 can pineapple
- 1 part sherry
- 6 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Bring 3 parts of the cream, the egg yolks, and the sugar to a boil over heat with whisking until the cream thickens.
2. Continue whisking until the cream becomes cold.
3. Beat the remaining cream to stiff peaks and stir in together with pineapple, cut into small pieces, and the sherry.
4. Last stir in the gelatin sheets dissolved in pineapple juice.
5. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugared mold and set it out to stiffen.
6. Dip the mold briefly in lukewarm water, turn out the pudding and garnish with pineapple.
