## Ingredients

- 6 large pork chops
- 1/2 lemon
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 2 deciliter finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 2 1/2 tablespoon butter for frying (50 gr.)
- 2 deciliter broth

## Directions

1. If the chops are small, one should calculate 2 per person for a meal.
2. The chop rack is dried with a cloth, wrung out in hot water, chopped so that a bone follows with each chop, which is then lightly pounded, trimmed and shaped.
3. The chops are moistened with the lemon juice, rubbed with the spices and allowed to rest on top of each other 1/4 hour, after which they are brushed with the butter and turned in a mixture of the crushed breadcrumbs and parsley.
4. The butter is browned in a frying pan, the chops are placed in and allowed to fry slowly without a lid approximately 7 minutes on each side.
