## Ingredients

- 50 gr. butter
- 60 gr. mondamin (mondamin is a flour made from corn)
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 4–5 eggs
- 40–50 gr. grated cheese
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1/6 tsp. white pepper
- 1/6 tsp. mustard
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. The butter is melted in a sauce pan, the mondamin is added and stirred until it is clear.
2. The milk is added little by little, and the sauce is allowed to boil 5 minutes.
3. The pan is removed from heat, and when most of the heat has subsided the egg yolks are added, one at a time, then the grated cheese and the seasonings.
4. Last the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in.
5. The mixture is poured into a buttered and breadcrumbed ovenproof form and baked in ordinary oven heat for approximately 45 minutes.
6. The soufflé is served at once as a middle course with stirred butter or to the smörgåsbord.
7. The mixture can also be baked in scallop shells or small forms and served then instead of cheese and crackers.
