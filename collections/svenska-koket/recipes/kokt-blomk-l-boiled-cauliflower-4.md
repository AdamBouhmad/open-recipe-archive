## Ingredients

- 3 medium cauliflower heads
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the stalk, remove all leaves, and place the cauliflower in water mixed with vinegar (1 tablespoon vinegar for each liter of water) for about 1/4 hour.
2. Remove the cauliflower and place it in boiling and salted water (short broth) and let it cook until tender.
3. Remove it immediately from the broth and place it on the serving dish.
4. Heat the butter and pour it over the cauliflower, which is then served immediately.
