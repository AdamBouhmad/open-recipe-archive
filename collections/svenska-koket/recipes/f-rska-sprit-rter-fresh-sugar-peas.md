## Ingredients

- 1 litre shelled sugar peas, approximately 5 litres unshelled
- 1/2 litre water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Place the peas in a colander, rinse with cold water and let drain.
2. Place them in boiling water with salt and sugar, and let them cook slowly until tender.
3. Drain the cooking liquid, place the peas on a serving dish and mix well with the parsley and cold butter.
4. Alternatively, after draining the liquid, shake the peas in melted butter together with the parsley and serve as a garnish around roasted meat such as filet, chops, calf saddle, etc.
