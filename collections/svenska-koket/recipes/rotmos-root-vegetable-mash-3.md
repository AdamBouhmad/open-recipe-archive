## Ingredients

- 1–2 rutabagas
- water
- salt
- 1 liter potatoes
- ½–1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter or cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the rutabaga and cut into small pieces, place in salted water and let boil for approximately ½ hour.
2. Peel the potatoes, cut into slices and add to boil with the rutabaga until everything is soft.
3. Drain the water, mash the potatoes and rutabaga with a fork.
4. Mix in the cold butter together with the milk.
5. Work the mash well and season with the spices.
