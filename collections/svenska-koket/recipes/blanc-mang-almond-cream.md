## Ingredients

- 5 parts cream
- 3 tablespoons fine sugar (or vanilla sugar)
- 4 sheets gelatin
- Garnish: jam, biscuits

## Directions

1. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks.
2. Dissolve the gelatin in a little lukewarm water and carefully stir it into the whipped cream until everything is well combined.
3. Pour the blanc-mangé into a gelatin mold to stiffen, then garnish with jam and biscuits.
