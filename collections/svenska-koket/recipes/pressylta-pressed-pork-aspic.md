## Ingredients

- 1 pig's head (4 kg)
- 1 large pork skin
- 2 kg veal shoulder
- 8 liters water
- 4 tablespoons salt
- 15 allspice berries
- 15 white peppercorns
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 tablespoons salt for the aspic
- 3 teaspoons white pepper for the aspic

## Directions

1. Split the pig's head, wash it well and soak it overnight in plenty of cold water, changing the water 2 times
2. Bring the water to a boil, add the head, skin and veal, skim well when it comes to a boil again, then add the salt and other seasonings
3. Allow the meat to cook until tender and easily comes away from the bones
4. Remove immediately and take off the skin in pieces as large as possible
5. Cut the meat and pork into slices or cubes
6. Dip a kitchen towel in hot water, wring it out and spread it in a dish, place the skin on the towel with the skin side down
7. Layer with veal, which is sprinkled with pepper and salt, then a layer of pork, and continue alternating layers with seasonings between each layer
8. Place a large pork skin on top and tie the towel tightly
9. Heat the aspic for 1/2 hour in the simmering broth and then place it under a press for approximately 12 hours
10. Store for shorter periods in a towel sprinkled with salt, for longer periods in salt brine
