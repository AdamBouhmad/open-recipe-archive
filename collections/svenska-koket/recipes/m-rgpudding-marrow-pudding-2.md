## Ingredients

- 1 hg. beef marrow
- 30 gr. fine beef suet
- 50 gr. bread crumbs
- 1/2 dcl. milk
- 1 hg. granulated sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 hg. preserved fruit, in fine dice
- 45 gr. sultana raisins
- 30 gr. pitted malaga raisins
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. wheat flour (20 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. The marrow and suet are cleaned well, rinsed, wrapped in cloth, placed in a colander, melted in a water bath, and the fat is allowed to cool.
2. Bread crumbs of wheat bread are weighed, soaked in milk and squeezed out.
3. The fat is worked well with the sugar, the squeezed-out bread, the beaten eggs, egg yolks, fruit dice, the cleaned and rinsed sultana raisins and the cleaned, rinsed, split and pitted malaga raisins.
4. The mass is poured into a buttered and floured, smooth ring mold and baked in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
5. Served warm, covered with warm wine sauce (see no. 457), mixed with rum or with flaming sauce (see no. 465), which is ignited when the pudding is brought to the table.
