## Ingredients

- Juice and zest of 2 oranges
- 150 grams brown sugar
- 1 egg, 3 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter milk
- 1 tablespoon sherry
- 2 grams citric acid
- 3 deciliters thick cream
- 5 sheets gelatin, 3 tablespoons water
- For garnish: 1 orange, 1/2 hectogram biscuits

## Directions

1. Wash the oranges well, dry them, and rub the zest against the sugar, which is placed in a well-scoured saucepan.
2. Press out the orange juice and strain it over the sugar, add the egg, egg yolks and milk, and whisk the mixture until the sugar is melted, then let it simmer over the fire under vigorous whisking until the cream is thick and fluffy.
3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold, then add the sherry and the crushed citric acid.
4. Rinse the gelatin, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in 3 tablespoons hot water.
5. Whip the cream to stiff foam.
6. Add the gelatin to the cream while whisking and thereafter the cream, very carefully.
7. Rinse a nice tin mold with cold water and dust it with granulated sugar, pour in the mixture and let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours.
8. When the cream is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish with tart paper and garnish with candied orange segments and biscuits.
