## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg lean lamb breast or shoulder
- 1 liter potatoes
- 4 medium red onions
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 parsley stems with roots
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 sprig thyme
- 8 deciliters water
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into small, even pieces, which are boned. Peel the potatoes and cut into slices. Peel the onions and chop coarsely.
2. In a heavy pot holding about 2 1/2 liters, place the meat on the bottom and around the sides and season with salt and pepper. On top of the meat place the potatoes and onions, layering everything with seasonings between each layer.
3. Clean the parsley stems, rinse them, wrap in a cloth and tie together with the bay leaf and thyme; place this bundle in the middle of the stew.
4. Add the water, cover with a lid and let the dish cook in the oven for about 1 1/2 hours.
5. Taste, transfer to a warm serving dish, sprinkle with parsley and serve very hot.
