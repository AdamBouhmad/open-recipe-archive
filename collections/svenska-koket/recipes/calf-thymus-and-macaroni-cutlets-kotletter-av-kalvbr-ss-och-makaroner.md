## Ingredients

- 1 hg. calf thymus
- 1 lit. water for blanching
- 2 tsp. salt for blanching
- 1 hg. macaroni
- 1 1/2 lit. water
- 1/2 tbsp. salt
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.) for sauce
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 2 dcl. calf thymus stock with 1 tsp. meat extract
- 3 dcl. milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 eggs for coating
- 1 tbsp. water
- 1 1/2 cups crushed rusks
- 80 gr. butter for frying
- peas for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the calf thymus as in recipe no. 47, clean and cut into fine dice.
2. Parboil the macaroni in salted water and cut into thin rings.
3. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the boiling stock and milk and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Add the thymus and macaroni to the sauce and season well with the seasonings.
5. Pour onto a flat platter, let cool and form into oblong cutlets.
6. Brush with eggs whisked with water and turn in crushed rusks.
7. Fry the cutlets a beautiful golden brown in the butter and arrange in a ring on a round platter.
8. Place peas in the middle of the platter as in the previous recipe.
9. Serve as a breakfast or side dish.
