## Ingredients

- 1 large cauliflower head
- 1 leek
- ¼ celery root
- 2 liters water
- 2 tbsp. butter
- 3 tbsp. flour
- ½ liter milk
- salt, sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the separated and prepared cauliflower, leek and celery in water with a little salt.
2. When the cauliflower is soft, pass the soup through a sieve. Fry butter and flour together, add the purée and milk, and let the soup cook approximately 15 minutes.
3. Season, and add 1 tsp. cold butter in the soup bowl.
