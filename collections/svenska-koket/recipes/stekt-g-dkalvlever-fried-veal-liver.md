## Ingredients

- 1 calf liver
- 1 hg. fat
- 1/4 msk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 caul membrane
- 2 msk. butter
- 4 kkp cream
- 1/2 msk. butter for sauce
- 1 msk. flour for sauce
- 2 msk. grated mesost cheese
- salt, white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the liver and let it soak in water for 1/4 hour.
2. Cut the fat into strips, roll these in pepper and salt, and lard the liver in close rows.
3. Wrap it in the caul membrane and brown it in butter, then add the cream.
4. Let the liver cook slowly for approximately 1 hour with a tightly fitting lid. Stir the sauce occasionally to prevent it from curdling.
5. When the liver is cooked, remove the caul membrane, slice the liver thinly and arrange on a platter.
6. Prepare the sauce according to the general rules for sauces and pour it over the liver.
