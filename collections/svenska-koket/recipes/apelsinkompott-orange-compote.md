## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 dcl. granulated sugar (213 gr.)
- 1 dcl. water
- 6 beautiful oranges

## Directions

1. From the sugar and water, cook a clear, syrupy syrup and let it cool.
2. Peel the oranges, carefully remove the white pith, then divide the fruit into wedges or cut into slices and remove the seeds.
3. Place the fruit in a compote bowl, pour the sugar syrup over it, and let the compote sit on ice for 1 hour before serving.
4. Serve with whipped cream.
