## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp. cream
- 2 tbsp. granulated sugar
- 1/2 part brandy
- 1/3 tsp. grated lemon peel or 1 drop orange essence
- 250-300 gr. wheat flour
- 1/2 kg. oil for frying

## Directions

1. Work all the ingredients well together, and set the dough on a cold place to rest at least 2 hours.
