## Ingredients

- 3 hg. butter
- 1 hg. powdered sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 25 gr. sweet almonds
- 5 bitter almonds
- 325 gr. wheat flour
- 1/2 liter apple purée
- 4 hg. powdered sugar
- 2 hg. sweet almonds
- 2–3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare the pastry dough as in recipe no. 974 and let it rest 2–3 hours.
2. The apple purée should preferably be cooked the day before, so that it is completely cold.
3. Blanch the almonds, dry them, grind them through a mill, and pound them with the sugar and egg whites in a mortar to a fine and smooth mass, which should be quite loose.
4. Divide the pastry dough into 3 parts. One part is rolled out on the bottom of a ring form and baked halfway.
5. When the bottom is cold, line the sides with dough, add the apple purée, and spread the almond mass evenly over it.
6. Roll out the rest of the dough into thin strips, which are laid in diagonal squares over the almond mass.
7. Finally, place the rest of the dough in a ring around the cake.
8. Bake in a not too hot oven until it is thoroughly tender, which is tested with a whisk.
9. Serve cooled with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
