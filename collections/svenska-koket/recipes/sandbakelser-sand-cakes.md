## Ingredients

- 210 gr. butter
- 185 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 360 gr. wheat flour

## Directions

1. All the ingredients are worked well together on the baking board to form an even and cohesive pastry dough.
2. With this, small fluted molds are lined, and the pastries are baked in moderate oven heat.
3. They are not removed from the molds until most of the heat has dissipated.
