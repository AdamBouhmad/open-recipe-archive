## Ingredients

- 1 large cauliflower head
- 0.5 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters broth
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower, rinse and place in the boiling water with salt, and let it cook until tender, then place it on a sieve to drain.
2. Save the cauliflower water.
3. Melt the butter and flour together, add the cauliflower water and broth, and let the soup simmer for 10 minutes.
4. Add the separated cauliflower, season and serve with toasted bread or cheese sandwiches.
5. If a simpler soup is desired, use 1.5 liters broth and 0.5 liters milk.
