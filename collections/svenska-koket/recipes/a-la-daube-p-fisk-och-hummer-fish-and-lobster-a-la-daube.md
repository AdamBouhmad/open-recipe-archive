## Ingredients

- 1 kg whitefish or pike-perch
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 6 white peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 can lobster (1 liter)
- 1 1/2 liters of fish stock
- 15 sheets gelatin
- 3 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon cold water
- 4 tablespoons good white wine
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 truffle
- parsley

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it, rinse well and dry with a fish cloth.
2. Rub it with half of the salt and let it rest for 1/2 hour, dry it again and place in the cold water with the remaining salt and spices, and let it simmer slowly until done or approximately 15 minutes.
3. Remove it immediately, pull off the skin and divide the fish into 2 fillets.
4. Place the bones in the stock, which should cook for another 20 minutes.
5. Cut the fish into beautiful pieces and cut the lobster similarly.
6. Prepare gelée from the fish stock as in recipe no. 181. When it is cold, add the wine.
7. Prepare the dish as in the previous recipe and serve to the smörgåsbord.
