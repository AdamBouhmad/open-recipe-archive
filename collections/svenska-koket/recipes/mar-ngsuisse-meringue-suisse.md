## Ingredients

- 8 large egg whites
- 400 grams powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- carmine
- 1 liter thick cream

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until stiff and continue beating for about 15 minutes.
2. Then carefully fold in the sifted powdered sugar.
3. Pour the mixture into piping bags with closed tips and pipe onto well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets, ring by ring, so that these together form wreaths.
4. Make the wreaths larger and smaller so that they, laid one upon the other, form a pyramid.
5. Place them in gentle oven heat and bake very slowly until they release from the sheet.
6. When they are cold, arrange them on a glass bowl, wreath upon wreath, into a pyramid.
7. Whip the cream with vanilla sugar until stiff peaks form.
8. Fill and coat the pyramid with whipped cream.
9. Divide the remaining cream in two; color one part with carmine to a beautiful pink color, and with this and the white cream garnish the pyramid through piping bags.
10. If the rings are made the same size, one can make a muff from them, fill it inside with pink cream and coat it with white cream. A little chocolate is dissolved in cream, and with this the muff is garnished, so that it resembles an ermine muff.
