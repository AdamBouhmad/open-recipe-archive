## Ingredients

- White glazed paper
- Glazed bread or thin slices of sponge cake, glazed with fondant icing in various colors (white, red, green, or brown)
- Lightly beaten egg white
- Vanilla cream with gelatin or Bavarian cream
- Thin slice of sponge cake, glazed with fondant icing and garnished with fruit

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a charlotte mold with a slice of white oiled paper
2. Garnish the sides with upright glazed bread or narrow slices of sponge cake, glazed with fondant icing in alternating colors
3. Arrange the breads closely together with alternating colors and brush the inside with lightly beaten egg white
4. Let the form stand on ice for 1 hour, then fill with vanilla cream with gelatin (flavored with vanilla or coffee) or Bavarian cream
5. Let the pudding set on ice for 2-3 hours and then turn it out onto a flat serving dish with tart paper
6. Remove the paper on top of the pudding and replace it with a thin slice of sponge cake, glazed with fondant icing and garnished with fruit
