## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes, water, salt
- 2 eggs
- 2-3 deciliters cream milk
- 0.5 hectogram cold butter
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 1-2 tablespoons sugar
- 0.5 tablespoon butter and 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw, cook them and mash them with a fork or press them through a potato ricer.
2. Whisk the egg yolks together with the milk and stir into the mash together with the cold butter and seasonings.
3. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in last.
4. Place the mash in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined mold, sprinkle with crushed rusks and crimp neatly.
5. Put the pudding in a good oven heat to bake through and get color.
6. It is served with meat dishes in general.
