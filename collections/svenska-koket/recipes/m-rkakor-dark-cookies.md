## Ingredients

- 400 gr. butter
- 200 gr. granulated sugar
- 600 gr. wheat flour
- 1 egg
- 7 egg whites for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almond
- 1/4 kkp pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and sift the flour on a baking board.
2. Divide the washed butter into flakes and place on top of the flour along with sugar and the whisked egg.
3. Work all ingredients together into a smooth, cohesive dough, and set to cool.
4. Roll out, not too thin, and cut small cakes with an oblong, crimped measure.
5. Brush with egg white and sprinkle with almond and sugar.
6. Bake in good oven heat.
