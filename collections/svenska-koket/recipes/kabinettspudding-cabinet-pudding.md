## Ingredients

- Leftover sponge cake cut into cubes (approximately 5 decilitres)
- 1 decilitre currants (60 gr.)
- 30 gr. candied fruit
- 4 eggs
- 50 gr. sugar
- 4 decilitres good milk
- 1 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Place the sponge cake, which should be cut into small cubes, in a buttered and rusk-strewn mold alternately with the well-rinsed currants and chopped candied fruit.
2. Whisk the eggs well with the sugar, add the milk and fill the mold with this mixture.
3. Bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour.
4. Turn out and serve very hot with warm vanilla sauce.
