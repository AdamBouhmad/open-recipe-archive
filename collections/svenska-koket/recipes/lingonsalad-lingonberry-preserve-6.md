## Ingredients

- 1 kg. large lingonberries
- 15 cloves
- 750 grams lump sugar
- 3 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Pick out the large lingonberries, rinse them, let them drain, prick them with needles, threaded through a cork, and layer them with the cloves in a jar.
2. Cook the sugar and water to a clear, syrupy liquid and pour it hot over the berries.
