## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless inner leg
- 1 liter skim milk or drinking liquid
- 2 tsk salt (10 gr.)
- 4 msk butter (80 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk white pepper
- 1 dcl broth

## Directions

1. If the meat is frozen, let it thaw in milk or drinking liquid. Dry it well with a cloth, trim and cut into 12 slices, which are lightly beaten, wrapped in a cloth, sprinkled with spices, brushed with 1 msk melted butter and left to lie on top of each other for 1 hour.
2. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter in it, place the steaks in and fry about 3 minutes on each side.
3. Arrange on a warm platter, whisk the pan with the broth and keep the sauce on the platter.
4. Place a slice of maitre-d'hôtel butter (see no. 406) on each steak; garnish the dish with parsley and serve with boiled or fried potatoes. The steaks can also be garnished with fried Portuguese onion, in which case the maitre-d'hôtel butter is omitted and the frying pan is whisked with 2 dcl thick cream, then the sauce is poured over the steaks.
