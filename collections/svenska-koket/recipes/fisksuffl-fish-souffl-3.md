## Ingredients

- 150 gr butter
- 150 gr flour
- 1 part cream
- 2 cups cooked fish
- 6 eggs
- salt, white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The butter is combined with flour and mixed with cream to make a thin sauce; this is given a good boil while whisking, after which it is set aside to cool.
2. The cooked fish is picked fine like rice grains and mixed into the cooled batter together with egg yolks added one at a time.
3. It is seasoned with pepper and salt, and egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in.
4. The batter is immediately poured into a buttered and bread-crumbed mold and baked for about 1 1/2 hours in moderate oven heat.
5. The soufflé is served immediately with a fine fish sauce.
