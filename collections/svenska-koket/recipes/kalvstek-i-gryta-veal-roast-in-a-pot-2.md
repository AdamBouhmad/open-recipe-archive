## Ingredients

- 2 kg French cut veal
- 2 tsk salt (10 gr.)
- ¼ tsk white pepper
- 4 msk butter (80 gr.)
- For the sauce: 2 msk flour (20 gr.)
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- Salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung so that it is tender, is beaten on the skin side with a damp mallet, dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and rubbed with the seasonings. Brown the butter in a roasting pot that is not larger than to just fit the meat snugly. Place the roast in and brown well on all sides, then cover and let it cook slowly without adding liquid until tender, about 3 hours. Turn it often and keep the lid tightly on the pot. Place the roast inner-side up on a warm platter and carve at the table. Skim most of the fat from the gravy in the pot, blend the flour well with the meat gravy, add the cream, and let the sauce simmer for 8 minutes. Season with the spices and pour through a sauce strainer into a warm sauce bowl. Serve the roast with vegetables or potatoes and cucumbers, and salads.
