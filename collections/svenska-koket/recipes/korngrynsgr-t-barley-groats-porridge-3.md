## Ingredients

- 2 cups crushed groats
- 1/2 liter water
- 2 liters milk
- 2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats and put them on in cold water.
2. When it comes to a boil, pour in the milk, and let the porridge cook slowly with a closed lid for 3–4 hours.
3. During cooking, the porridge should be stirred occasionally to prevent burning at the bottom and diluted, if necessary, with a little more milk.
4. Taste and serve.
