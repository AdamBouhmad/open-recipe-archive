## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. carrots (4-5 pieces)
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse and brush the carrots well clean, but do not scrape them. Put them in cold, salted water and let them boil until tender, which is tested with a whisk.
2. Remove the carrots and peel off the skin at once, then cut them into small pieces or slices with a vegetable knife.
3. Place them in a serving dish, sprinkle with the sugar, top with the cold butter and let them stand in a water bath until they are heated through, then serve as a garnish around roasted meat or together with cooked vegetables.
