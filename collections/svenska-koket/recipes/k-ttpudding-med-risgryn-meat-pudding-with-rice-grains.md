## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cooked rice grains
- 1 cup rice grains
- 2 small red onions
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 liter milk
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/3 liter broth
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold
- 1/2 liter fried minced meat

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, chop them finely and fry them together with the meat in butter, then add the seasonings.
2. Rinse the rice grains and place in cold milk and let cook while stirring until soft.
3. Pour out and let cool, then add the whipped egg and salt.
4. Fold the fried meat and broth into the porridge, and pour the mixture into a buttered mold.
5. Sprinkle with crushed rusks and bake in oven until golden brown.
6. Serve the pudding with meat gravy or clarified butter.
