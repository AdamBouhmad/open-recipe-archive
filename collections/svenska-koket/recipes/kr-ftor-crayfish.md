## Ingredients

- Medium-sized crayfish
- 2 liters water per 20 crayfish
- 45 gr. coarse salt per 20 crayfish
- Dill flowers to taste

## Directions

1. Rinse the crayfish in cold water.
2. Bring water to a boil with coarse salt and dill flowers.
3. Add the crayfish and cook for 10-15 minutes.
4. Transfer to a platter and let cool in enough of the cooking liquid to cover them evenly.
5. Arrange high on a round platter, garnish with dill, and serve to the smörgåsbord.
