## Ingredients

- 1 eider duck for 4-5 persons
- milk for soaking
- 1 day soaking time

## Directions

1. Clean the bird (see page 299). Remove the fatty skin. Parboil the bird and let it soak in milk for 1 day before serving. Prepare, roast and serve as duck.
