## Ingredients

- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 4 slices wheat bread
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 2 msk. sugar
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 4 dcl. milk
- the cooked egg whites
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- the hard-boiled egg yolks
- parsley

## Directions

1. Shell the eggs and carefully separate the white from the yolk.
2. Cut or chop the egg whites finely.
3. Cut the bread slices into triangular pieces, which are fried nicely golden brown in butter.
4. Melt the sugar in a frying pan.
5. Dip the bread pieces (croutons) by the point into the melted sugar and fasten them tightly together on the inner edge of an oblong plate.
6. Fry the butter and flour together 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce cook 5 minutes.
7. Stir in the chopped egg white, and the stew gets one boil and is carefully seasoned with the seasonings.
8. Arrange it inside the croutons on the plate.
9. Press the egg yolks through a sieve directly over the stew, taking care that none of the yolk falls on the bread.
10. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve to the smorgasbord.
