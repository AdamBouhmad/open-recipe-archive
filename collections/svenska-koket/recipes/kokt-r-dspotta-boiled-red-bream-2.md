## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg red bream
- 1 1/2 vinegar
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr)

## Directions

1. For cooking, choose large red breams
2. Place them on a plate, coat lightly with vinegar, let rest for 1/2 hour and then scrape well with a knife
3. Remove the gills and take out the innards through an incision made at the head
4. Scrape the dark membrane carefully and cut off the fins
5. Rinse the fish thoroughly, dry it, rub in the salt and let rest for 1 hour, then dry again
6. Cook as in recipe no. 327 and requires great attention during cooking, as it is cooked in a few minutes
7. Place on a warm plate with the white side up, garnish with parsley and serve with wine sauce, parsley sauce, or crayfish sauce
8. Note: Royal plaice is cleaned and cooked the same way but requires longer cooking time. Serve with melted butter, grated horseradish and boiled potatoes
