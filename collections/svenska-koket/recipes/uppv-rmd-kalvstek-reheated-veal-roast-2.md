## Ingredients

- 500-600 g boneless veal roast
- 1 tbsp butter (20 g)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 g)
- 400 ml meat stock
- 50 ml thick cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the roast into even slices.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes; add the meat stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes, then add the cream.
3. Season the sauce with spices and bring to a boil.
4. Warm the meat in the sauce but do not allow it to boil.
5. Arrange on a warm plate, garnish with browned potatoes and serve with green salad, cucumbers and lingonberry preserves.
