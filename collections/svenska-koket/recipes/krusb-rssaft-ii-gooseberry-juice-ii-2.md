## Ingredients

- half-ripe gooseberries
- water
- 1 kg sugar per kg juice

## Directions

1. Rinse and trim the gooseberries and place them in a stone crock.
2. Cover the berries with cold water, so much that it completely covers them.
3. Place a plate over the crock and seal it tight with a dough made of coarse rye flour and water, so that it becomes completely sealed.
4. Place the crock in a pot with hay, pour in enough water so that more than half of the crock stands under water.
5. Let it cook for 5-6 hours. Pour the mass into a strainer cloth and let the juice drip.
6. Let it then cook with sugar with skimming for 20 minutes, bottle warm or cold, the bottles are corked and sealed with resin.
