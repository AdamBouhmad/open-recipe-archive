## Ingredients

- 0.75 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters milk
- 0.75 cup semolina grits
- 1 piece cinnamon
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a pot and add the milk and bring to a boil with cinnamon.
2. Shake in the grits and let the porridge simmer gently while stirring for 0.75 hour.
3. Season and serve.
