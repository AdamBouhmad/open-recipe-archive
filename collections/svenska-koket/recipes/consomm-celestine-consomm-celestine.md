## Ingredients

- 40 gr. tapioca
- 1½ kkp water
- 2½ lit. clear consommé
- salt, cayenne
- 1 msk. finely chopped chives
- forcemeated pancakes

## Directions

1. Let the tapioca grains cook in water until they are clear, then strain through muslin and pour the liquid into the consommé, which is then brought to a boil and seasoned with the spices.
2. Cut the forcemeated pancakes (see no. 320) out with a round measure into cakes (approx. 2 cm. in diameter), which are placed in the soup bowl together with the chives, and the hot consommé is poured over.
