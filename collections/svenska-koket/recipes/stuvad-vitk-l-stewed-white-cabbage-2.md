## Ingredients

- 1 small white cabbage head
- 3 liters water, 1 tablespoon salt (15 g) for blanching
- ½ liter water for cooking
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 g)
- White sauce: 1½ tablespoons butter (30 g), 4 tablespoons flour (40 g), 2 deciliters cabbage cooking water, 2½–3 deciliters cream or milk, salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the cabbage head and blanch the leaves as in the previous recipe.
2. Cut them into large cubes, place in boiling salted water, and let cook slowly with a lid until completely soft, about 2 hours.
3. Drain the cabbage in a colander and reserve the cooking water.
4. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes; add the cabbage cooking water and cream, and let the sauce cook 5 minutes.
5. Add the cabbage, bring the stew to a boil, and season with the seasonings.
6. Serve with forcemeat, sausage, and roasted meat.
