## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 300 gr. granulated sugar
- 0.5 lemon
- 150 gr. sweet almond
- 6 bitter almonds
- 75 gr. potato flour
- 0.5 tbsp. washed butter
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks from the whites and stir together with the sugar for 0.5 hour.
2. During stirring, add 1 tbsp. water twice.
3. Blanch the almond, dry well in the oven and grate or run through an almond mill and mix with the grated lemon peel and potato flour.
4. Stir the dry mixture and lemon juice into the egg mixture, and finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Immediately pour the batter into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined form or baking pan and bake approximately 1 hour at not too strong oven heat.
6. When the tart has cooled, cut it into two bottoms and place vanilla cream or apple sauce between.
7. Glaze the tart and garnish with fruit and crystallized fruits.
