## Ingredients

- 2 wood grouse or 4 ptarmigans, approximately 450 gr. scraped meat
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 2 hg. flour panade
- 2 eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 3-4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. The birds are prepared and the meat is treated and pounded as in the preceding recipe.
2. The forcemeat is then pounded with the panade, until the mass becomes even and smooth, then it is placed in a dish, and the beaten eggs and egg yolks are added, little by little, under continuous vigorous stirring.
3. The forcemeat is passed through a hair sieve, placed in a small basin or smaller saucepan, which is set in a dish with ice (it will otherwise curdle when the cream is worked in) and is allowed to stand for 1 hour, or until it is completely cooled.
4. It is then stirred, allowed to remain on the ice and 2 dcl. of the cream is added, little by little, under even and vigorous stirring.
5. Last the rest of the cream, whipped into stiff peaks, is stirred in.
6. It is then tested as in the preceding recipe; if it is too loose, a little egg white is added, otherwise a little cream and more spices, if needed.
7. Last mixed with chopped truffle, if desired.
8. It is cooked as in the preceding recipe and served warm or cold with truffle sauce or brown mushroom sauce, or cold, in aspic, with vegetable salad.
