## Ingredients

- 2 liters juicy apples
- 2½ hg. sugar
- 3½ cups crushed hardtack
- 2 tsk. butter
- ½ tsk. butter for the form

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut them into thin slices or wedges.
2. In the bottom of a buttered pie form lay a thick layer of the crushed hardtack, on top of this the apples in layers with the sugar and finally the rest of the crushed hardtack.
3. On top lay the butter, distributed in flakes.
4. Place the form in not too strong oven heat for about 45 minutes, or until the apple slices feel soft.
5. Serve in its form with whipped cream.
