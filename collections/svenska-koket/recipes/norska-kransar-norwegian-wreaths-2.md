## Ingredients

- 200 g butter
- 200 g caster sugar
- 3–4 tbsp cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 420 g wheat flour
- 1 rounded tsp baking powder

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and cream it together with the sugar until white and fluffy.
2. Mix in the cream and then the egg yolks, one at a time, as well as the flour, sifted with the baking powder.
3. Press the batter through a piping bag and form into 'S' shapes or wreaths, which are baked golden brown in moderate oven heat.
