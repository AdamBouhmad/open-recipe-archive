## Ingredients

- 3 lobsters (weighing 4 hg. each)
- 5 lit. water
- 2 hg. coarse salt
- 1 lit. good fish stock or light meat stock
- 10 gelatin leaves
- 2 egg whites and the rinsed shells
- 1 msk. cold water
- 3 msk. good white wine
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 truffle
- parsley
- salad

## Directions

1. Cook the lobster as in recipe no. 170.
2. Cut the meat into beautiful pieces.
3. Prepare the aspic as follows: pour the stock into a pot together with the rinsed gelatin and the egg whites lightly beaten with water, mixed with the crushed eggshells.
4. Bring the mixture to a good boil while whisking, place the lid on the pot, remove from heat and let it stand to the side of the stove for about 15 minutes.
