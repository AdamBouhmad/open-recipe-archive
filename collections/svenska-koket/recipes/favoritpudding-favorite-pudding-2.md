## Ingredients

- 0.75 liter whole milk
- 70 grams baker's yeast
- 0.5 kilogram wheat flour
- 50 grams sugar
- grated zest of half a lemon
- 0.5 kilogram butter
- 7.5 teaspoon salt
- 6-8 eggs
- liqueur
- sugar
- water
- Glaze: 2 parts white wine, 0.5 kilogram powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Warm the milk and dissolve the yeast in a little of it.
2. Make a dough from the flour, milk, and yeast; work it well and let it rise.
3. When the dough has risen well, work it again; add the whisked eggs, sugar, grated lemon zest, salt, and the rest of the flour, then work in the washed and softened butter last.
4. Work the dough for at least half an hour, then pour it into a buttered and floured ring mold, let it rise again, and bake in a good oven heat.
5. When the cake is done, turn it out and soak it with a warm mixture of liqueur, sugar, and water, then let it cool.
6. Glaze the cake, place it on parchment paper on a platter, and fill the center high with glaze, preferably a red one such as strawberry or raspberry.
7. It can also be served with apple or pear compote and can be soaked with fruit juice instead of the liqueur mixture.
