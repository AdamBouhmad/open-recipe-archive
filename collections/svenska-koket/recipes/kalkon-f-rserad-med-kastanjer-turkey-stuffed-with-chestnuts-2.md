## Ingredients

- 1 turkey cock approximately 5 kg
- 1 tbsp salt (15 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 1/2 hg lard, in 1 slice
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 1/2 liters broth
- 1 1/2 kg chestnuts for filling
- 3/4 liter broth for filling
- 2 tbsp butter (40 g)
- 1/2 tbsp salt, 1 tsp sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey as in recipe 605 and rub it with a mixture of salt and pepper
2. Shell the chestnuts, let them lie in hot water until the inner thin skin can be removed with a cloth
3. Place the shelled chestnuts in the broth together with the butter and seasonings and cook them soft but do not let them fall apart
4. Drain them in a colander and let them cool
5. Fill the turkey with the chestnuts, then treat it as in the previous recipe and roast it either in the oven or in a pot
6. Carve it, garnish with small link sausages, browned potatoes and green salad
7. Serve with gravy or prepared sauce (see recipe 429)
