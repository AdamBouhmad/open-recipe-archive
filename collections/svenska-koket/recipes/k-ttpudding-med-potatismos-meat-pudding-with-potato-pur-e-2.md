## Ingredients

- ½ liter cooked or roasted chopped meat
- 2 small red onions
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ tablespoon salt
- ½ teaspoon white pepper
- 2 parts broth
- 1 liter potatoes
- ½ cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- sugar, salt
- ½ tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel and finely chop the onion and brown it together with the meat in the butter, then add the seasonings.
2. Peel the potatoes raw, cook and mash with a fork.
3. Heat the milk together with the butter and stir into the mash; season this and work well.
4. Grease a mold with butter, layer a layer of potato mash and then the browned meat.
5. Pour the broth over, and on top layer another layer of potato mash.
6. Sprinkle the pudding with crushed bread crumbs and place in the oven to get a nice brown color.
7. Serve with roast sauce.
