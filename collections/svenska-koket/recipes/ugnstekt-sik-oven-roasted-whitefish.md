## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. fat whitefish
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 3 parsley stems
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 3 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 1/2 egg
- 80 gr. butter
- 1 1/2 decilitres French wine
- 1 1/2 decilitres water
- 1 decilitre bouillon

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, see page 171, and rub with salt.
2. Clean the parsley, rinse it, wring it in a cloth, chop it finely and mix with the onion and crushed breadcrumbs, and if desired, with 1 tablespoon blanched, finely chopped mushrooms.
3. Brush the fish with egg and sprinkle on both sides with the breadcrumb mixture.
4. Butter a long pan with half of the cold butter in an area as large as the fish occupies.
5. Place the fish in with the back up and sides opened out, and lay the remaining butter in flakes on top of it; add half of the wine and water and place the pan in a warm oven.
6. Let the fish roast for about 20 minutes, basting it often and adding the rest of the wine and water.
7. Place it on a warm dish, bring the sauce to a boil with the bouillon and thin it, if too thick, with a little wheat flour, strain it and pour it over the fish.
8. Garnish with parsley and fluted lemon slices and serve with mashed potatoes.
