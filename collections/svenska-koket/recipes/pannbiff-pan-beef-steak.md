## Ingredients

- 750 gr boneless inner loin
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 4 tbsp butter (80 gr)
- 2–3 red onions or 1 large Portuguese onion
- 1–2 dcl broth

## Directions

1. The meat is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into pieces and ground 2 times through a meat grinder.
2. From the ground meat, thin or thick steaks are formed, depending on individual taste, which are finely chopped with a knife, evened well at the edges, and sprinkled with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. The steaks are fried and served as in recipe no. 483.
