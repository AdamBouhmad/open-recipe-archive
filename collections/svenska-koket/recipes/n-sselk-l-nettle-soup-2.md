## Ingredients

- 2.5-3 liters tender, fresh nettles
- 3 liters water for blanching
- 1 tablespoon salt for blanching
- 1 small bunch chives
- 1 handful chervil or 0.5 teaspoon finely ground fennel
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2 liters stock
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1 teaspoon cold butter

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the nettles extremely well
2. Blanch them for 15 minutes as in recipe 257, place on a sieve, rinse with cold water and let drain well
3. Clean the chervil and chives
