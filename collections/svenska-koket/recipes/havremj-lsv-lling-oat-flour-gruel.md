## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 cups prepared oat flour
- 0.5 liters water
- 1.5 liters milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with water and let it swell for about 1 hour.
2. Bring milk to boil, add the flour mixture, and let the gruel simmer for 0.5 hours with stirring.
3. Season and serve, preferably as a breakfast dish.
