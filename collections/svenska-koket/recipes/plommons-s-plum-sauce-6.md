## Ingredients

- 150 gr. prune plums
- 5 dcl. water
- 50 gr. sugar
- 1 tsk. potato flour (5 gr.)
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the plums in several lukewarm waters and wrap in a cloth.
2. Crush half of them in a stone mortar so that the pits are broken.
3. Let the crushed and whole plums soak for a few hours in the water in which they are to be cooked.
4. Cook them until completely soft, then press through a sieve.
5. Cook the fruit purée with the sugar and finish as in recipe no. 460.
6. Taste and add a little lemon juice if desired.
7. Serve warm or cold.
8. Sauce of dried cherries is prepared in the same way.
