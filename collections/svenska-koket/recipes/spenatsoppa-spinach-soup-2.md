## Ingredients

- 200 g spinach
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1.5 liters broth
- 0.5 liters milk or 3 parts cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean, rinse, and drain the spinach, then chop it finely.
2. Mix butter and flour together, add the broth, add the spinach and let it cook until tender.
3. Pour in the hot milk or cream and season the soup.
4. Whisk the egg yolks in a soup bowl and add the soup while whisking vigorously.
