## Ingredients

- 150 grams granulated sugar
- 1 liter water
- 1 lemon in slices
- 1/4 liter crushed ice
- 5 deciliters white or red wine
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar, water, lemon slices, and ice in a soup bowl and let it stand for 1/2 hour or until the sugar is melted.
2. Add the wine and lemon juice, season the punch to taste, and serve immediately with potato flour pastries or rusks.
