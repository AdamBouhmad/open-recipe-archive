## Ingredients

- 250 g. granulated or fine sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 12 good, evenly sized apples
- 25 g. sweet almond

## Directions

1. Make a clear syrup from the sugar and water, and let it cool.
2. Wash the apples, dry them, peel them, and remove the cores with a corer.
3. Place the apples directly in the syrup and let them cook until they are soft and clear, then remove them with a slotted spoon and arrange them in a pyramid on a jelly bowl.
4. If desired, fill the holes left by the cores with apple or red currant jelly.
5. Blanch the almond, cut it into fine cubes, lightly toast them in the oven and sprinkle over the pyramid.
6. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream. If the pyramid is served cold, it can be garnished with 3 dcl. cream, whipped to stiff foam, if desired.
