## Ingredients

- 3 litres red currants
- 4 parts water
- 3/4–1 kg. granulated sugar to each kg. juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the currants and let them drain.
2. Strip them from the stems and let them cook with the water on a very low heat for approximately 20 minutes, stirring constantly.
3. Pour the currants into cheesecloth and let the juice drip through without pressing.
4. Weigh the juice, add the sugar, and stir until the sugar has somewhat melted.
5. Bring slowly to a boil and let it cook slowly for 8–10 minutes.
6. Skim the jelly well during cooking.
7. Immediately pour into warm jars rinsed with brandy.
