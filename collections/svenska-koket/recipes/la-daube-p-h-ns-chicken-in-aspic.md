## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 5 white peppercorns
- 1 liter of cooking liquid
- a few drops soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons meat extract
- 8–10 sheets gelatin
- 2 egg whites
- 1 hard-boiled egg, beets, parsley

## Directions

1. Pluck the chicken, singe it, clean it, and cook it together with the spices in water until it feels completely tender.
2. Remove the skin.
3. Separate the meat from the bones and cut it into nice pieces.
4. Heat the broth with the meat extract, soy sauce, and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in a little cold water.
5. When the mixture is warm, whisk in the beaten egg whites, and bring the whole to a good boil while stirring and strain through a napkin or cheesecloth wrung out in cold water.
6. Pour the clear aspic onto the bottom of a rinsed aspic mold and set out to set.
7. Garnish the mold with hard-boiled eggs, beets, and parsley.
8. Place the finest pieces (the lightest) on the bottom and sides.
9. Fill the mold with the remaining meat, and carefully pour the rest of the aspic over.
10. Set the aspic in a cold place to set.
