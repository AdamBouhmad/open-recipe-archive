## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. water (scarcely)
- 250 gr. fine butter
- 150 gr. sweet almond
- 4 bitter almonds
- 150 gr. powdered sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 25 gr. sweet almond
- 3 msk. pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as in recipe no. 33.
2. Scald the almond, dry it in the oven and drive it through an almond mill.
3. Then pound it with the sugar and egg whites into a fine and smooth paste.
4. Roll out the butter dough into a 1 dm. wide strip of approximately 3 mm. thickness.
5. Spread the almond paste thinly on the butter dough, after which the dough is rolled up carefully, along its length, and rolled out again to 5 cm. width.
6. Cut the dough across into 2 1/2 cm. wide breads, which are brushed with egg white and sprinkled with almond and sugar.
7. Bake the cakes in a hot oven and serve fresh with coffee or tea.
