## Ingredients

- 1.4 kg pike (= 600 g scraped fish meat)
- 300 g butter
- 40 g flour
- 6 parts cream
- 4 eggs
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take the pike (do not scale), rinse and dry well, fillet it and scrape the fish meat from skin and bones
2. No scale or bone should be included
3. Force the scraped fish together with the butter three times through a meat grinder and then pound with the egg yolks in a mortar until the mass becomes fine and cohesive
4. In a bowl mix the cream, salt, sugar, white pepper and sifted flour
5. Work the mixture into the forcemeat tablespoon by tablespoon
6. Work the forcemeat for 1 hour, then pass it through a sieve
7. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
8. Test the forcemeat and pour into well-buttered and bread-crumbed molds
9. These should not be filled more than halfway and should preferably have lids
10. If the molds lack lids, cover with buttered paper
11. Cook the forcemeat in a water bath for 1 hour
12. Serve with mushroom or crayfish sauce
