## Ingredients

- 1 small carrot
- 1 small parsnip
- 1 small leek
- 1/4 celeriac
- 1 3/4 liters water
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 10 g salt
- 4 white peppercorns
- 2-3 teaspoons (16-24 g) solid meat extract

## Directions

1. Brown the vegetables in the usual way, cut them into pieces and add them to the cold water together with the butter and spices.
2. Let the vegetables cook with a tightly closed lid for about 2 hours or until they are completely soft.
3. Remove them with a slotted spoon and add the meat extract, dissolved in 1/2 cup of hot water, to the liquid.
4. Let the broth cook for about 1/2 hour or until it measures 1 1/2 liters.
5. Taste it, strain it through a cheesecloth wrung out in hot water and use it as clear or finished broth.
