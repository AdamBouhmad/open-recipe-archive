## Ingredients

- 1 small cup flour
- 1.5 liters milk
- 0.5 liter weak beer or malt beverage
- 1-2 tablespoons syrup or sugar
- ginger
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with 3 cups of the milk and let it swell for about 1 hour.
2. Bring the remaining milk to a boil, stir in the flour mixture, and let the porridge simmer 10-15 minutes while stirring.
3. Bring the beverage to a boil with the syrup and spices and pour into the porridge with vigorous whisking.
4. Serve the beer soup foaming.
