## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 300 grams granulated sugar
- 3 parts clarified butter
- 180 grams wheat flour
- 1/2 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks together with the sugar for half an hour.
2. Clarify the butter and set it out to cool.
3. Stir it until it becomes white and fluffy, then blend it into the egg mixture.
4. Add the sifted wheat flour and then the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-sprinkled pan or mold and bake in fairly good oven heat.
6. When the cake has cooled, cut it into two layers and fill with cream or applesauce.
7. Glaze and garnish with fruit and crystallized sugar.
