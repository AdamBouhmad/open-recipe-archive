## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups Carolina rice groats
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 3 parts thick cream
- 1/4 kg butter
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats and add to boiling water and let them cook until soft
2. Then add the cream whipped to soft peaks along with the butter
3. Season the porridge with sugar and salt
