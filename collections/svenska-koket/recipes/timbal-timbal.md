## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 hg. sugar
- 60 gr. melted butter
- 260 gr. wheat flour
- 3 tsk. baking powder
- 1 kkp thin cream
- For the mold: 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. crushed rusks
- For filling: 5 dcl. warm, loose applesauce
- For garnish, meringue: 3 egg whites
- 105 gr. powdered sugar

## Directions

1. The eggs and sugar are beaten for 1/2 hour, then the melted but cooled butter is added, the wheat flour which should be well sifted with the baking powder, and the cream.
2. The batter is mixed well, poured immediately into a buttered tall timbal mold and baked in ordinary oven heat.
