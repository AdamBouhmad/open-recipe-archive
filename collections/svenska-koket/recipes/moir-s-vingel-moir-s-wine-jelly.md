## Ingredients

- 1 package Moir's jelly powder (approximately 120 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. sugar (45 gr.)
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 4 dcl. boiling water
- 3 dcl. Rhine wine or sherry

## Directions

1. Stir the jelly powder, sugar, lemon juice and water in a bowl until the powder and sugar are dissolved, then add the wine and pour the mixture into a large or several small, water-rinsed and sugar-strewn molds and let set on ice for 4-5 hours.
2. This jelly can be advantageously colored red and used for garnishing cold rice pudding (see recipe no. 910). A light, fine sponge cake can be soaked with this jelly, which should then be warmed to approximately 60 degrees. The jelly is then allowed to set. The cake is served whole, or cut into slices, with whipped cream.
