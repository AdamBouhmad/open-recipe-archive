## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg veal brisket
- 1.5 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 1 carrot
- 5 parsley stems
- 2 deciliters rice grains
- 1.5 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 30 g butter
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 g)
- 6 deciliters meat broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 3 egg yolks
- 0.5 tablespoon cold butter
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons melted butter (40 g)

## Directions

1. Dry the brisket with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into even, attractive pieces
2. Place the pieces in boiling water so it comes level with the meat; skim well and add the spices, sautéed carrot, and rinsed parsley stems
3. Let the meat simmer slowly with the lid tightly closed until tender, approximately 1.5 hours
4. Rinse the rice grains, scald them in hot water, place in boiling salted water and let cook 15-20 minutes, then strain well, drain thoroughly, and while still warm blend with the cold butter
5. For the sauce, fry butter and flour for 2 minutes; add the broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes, then season with spices and finish with egg yolks
6. Grease a fireproof dish with cold butter and sprinkle with half of the crushed rusks, then layer the meat, rice, and sauce alternately
7. The top layer should be sauce, sprinkled with the remaining crushed rusks mixed with grated cheese
8. Finally pour the clarified butter over and place in a warm oven and gratinée for 15-20 minutes
