## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless beef
- ½ tbsp salt
- ¼ tsk white pepper
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2-3 parts broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it into cubes and pass 2-3 times through a meat grinder.
2. Form the ground meat into thin, round steaks, which are sprinkled with pepper and salt. Lightly chop them with a knife, and smooth the edges well, so that they retain their shape during frying.
3. Brown the butter in a smoking hot frying pan together with the peeled onion cut into slices. Remove the onion.
4. Place the steaks in the pan and fry 1 minute on each side. Place them on a warm dish with the onion in the middle.
5. Deglaze the frying pan with a little boiling broth or water, and pour the sauce over the steaks.
