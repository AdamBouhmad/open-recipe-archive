## Ingredients

- 5 parts thick cream
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 1 1/2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 3-4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Half of the cream is boiled together with the vanilla and sugar until it has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor, after which the pot is lifted off the heat
2. One egg yolk at a time is whisked in, and the sauce is placed again over heat to simmer with whisking
