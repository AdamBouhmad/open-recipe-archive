## Ingredients

- 6 hg. fresh salmon
- 1 msk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1 msk. water
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)
- 2 msk. crushed rusks
- 150 gr. butter
- 2 tsk. lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and dry the salmon piece with a cloth dipped in cold water
2. Cut into 1 1/2 cm. thick slices, which are flattened with a knife blade
3. Rub with salt and pepper
4. Dip in the egg whisked with water, turn in a mixture of flour and rusks
5. Fry a nice golden brown in half of the butter
6. Serve as a breakfast dish with the remaining butter, browned and flavored with lemon juice
