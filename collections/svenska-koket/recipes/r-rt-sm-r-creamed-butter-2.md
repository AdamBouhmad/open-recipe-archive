## Ingredients

- 120 gr. fint smör
- 2 gr. bordsalt

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a warmed bowl; if desired, add the salt, and stir the butter until it becomes white and fluffy. It is served with soufflés, vegetable dishes, roasted rice etc.
