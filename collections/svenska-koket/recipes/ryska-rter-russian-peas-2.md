## Ingredients

- 150 gr. dried Russian peas
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. The peas are rinsed very well in several waters and allowed to soak overnight in the water in which they are to be cooked.
2. They are placed with salt, sugar and butter and allowed to simmer slowly until completely tender, which takes approximately 2 hours.
3. Served with roasted meat, such as pork loin, ham, roasted veal, etc.
