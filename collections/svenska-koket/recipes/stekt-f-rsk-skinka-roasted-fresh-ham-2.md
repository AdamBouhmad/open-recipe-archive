## Ingredients

- 1 ham about 5 kg.
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard, in flat slices
- 3/4 liter good meat gravy or broth
- 5 parsley stems
- 1 bay leaf

## Directions

1. Dry the ham with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and score the skin 1 cm. deep in diagonal squares.
2. Rub the underside and in the scores with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Spread the lard slices in a baking pan, place the ham on top with skin side up, place in a warm oven and let it roast until tender, about 2 1/2 hours.
4. When the skin pieces get a nice golden color, harden and loosen, remove them and use them for garnish.
5. When the ham has roasted about 1 1/2 hours, thin it with the boiling meat gravy, add the parsley and bay leaf.
