## Ingredients

- 7 hg potato puree
- 40-50 gr truffle
- 1 egg for brushing and breading
- 50 gr sweet almond
- 1 kg cooking fat

## Directions

1. Prepare the potato puree as in recipe 680 and mix with truffle cut into fine dice
2. Treat the puree as in recipe 680 and roll into the shape of apricots
3. Dip these in whisked egg and roll in almond, blanched and cut into the finest shavings
4. Cook to a beautiful golden brown in hot cooking fat
5. Let drain on absorbent paper and serve very hot as garnish around fried meat
