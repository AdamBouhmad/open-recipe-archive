## Ingredients

- 3 kg spare ribs
- 2 tablespoon salt (30 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 6 fresh or 1 hectogram dried apples
- 2 teaspoon sugar (10 gr.)
- 1 1/2 hectogram prunes
- 2 tablespoon butter (40 gr.)
- 3/4 liter boiling broth or water
- 1 teaspoon meat extract

## Directions

1. The spare ribs are cut and treated as in the previous recipe and rubbed with the spices.
2. The apples are peeled, cored, cut into slices and sugared.
3. The spare ribs are spread out with the meaty side down and covered with the apple slices or the dried and well-rinsed apples and the rinsed, parboiled and pitted prunes.
4. It is rolled up not too tightly and sewn together well with fine sailcloth, previously dipped in boiling water.
5. The butter is browned in a roasting pot, the meat is placed in and browned well on all sides, after which it is moistened with the broth or water, combined with the meat extract, and allowed to roast until tender, or approximately 3 1/2 hours, during which it is basted every 10 minutes.
6. It is taken up, the thread is cut off; and the meat is cut into nice pieces, which are laid on a warm serving dish and garnished with the apples and prunes and curly kale or parsley.
7. Served with boiled potatoes and the meat gravy, treated as in the previous recipe.
