## Ingredients

- 3 dcl. thick sugar syrup (3 hg. granulated sugar, 1 1/2 dcl. water)
- 8 egg yolks
- 5 dcl. extra thick heavy cream

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar syrup first, preferably the day before it is to be used
2. Chop the sugar into small pieces and place in an enameled iron pot, add water, and when the sugar is melted, cook it to a clear, simmering syrup that should thread between the fingers when tested
3. Measure the sugar syrup and let it cool
4. Add the egg yolks one at a time while whisking, then let the cream simmer over the fire with vigorous whisking
5. Pour the cream out and whisk it over ice until it is completely cold, then mix with the heavy cream whipped to stiff peaks
6. The bomb cream can be flavored with vanilla, lemon, apricot, pineapple, kirsch, anisette, wild strawberry or strawberry, finely pounded nougat, finely chopped pistachios, cherry compote, etc.
7. Fill the cream into a bomb mold and freeze directly in it, or preferably line the mold inside with a thin layer of pre-frozen ice cream, fill with bomb cream (half syrup and half bomb cream is enough for 12 persons), and freeze for 4-5 hours
