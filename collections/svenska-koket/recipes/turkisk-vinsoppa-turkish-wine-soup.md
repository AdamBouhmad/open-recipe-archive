## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup rice grains
- ½ litre French wine
- 1½ litres water
- 1½ cups crushed sugar
- ½ lemon
- 1 cup raisins
- ½ cup currants

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains and cook soft in the water with 2 parts of the wine and the sugar.
2. Add the rest of the wine, the peel and juice of the lemon.
3. Heat the soup but do not let it boil.
4. Finally add the previously rinsed fruit cooked in a little liquid and taste.
5. Serve with rusks or rusks.
