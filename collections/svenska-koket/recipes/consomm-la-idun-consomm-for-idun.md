## Ingredients

- 40 gr. tapioca grains
- 1½ kkp water
- 2½ lit. clear consommé
- salt
- cayenne
- 1 hg. salted, cooked beef tongue
- 1 can truffles (1/8 lit.)
- 1 can fine mushrooms (1/4 lit.)
- 30 gr. butter

## Directions

1. Let the tapioca grains cook in the water until clear, then strain through muslin and add the strained portion to the consommé
2. Cut the tongue and truffles into fine strips and the mushrooms into thin slices
3. Fry the mushrooms in butter until the butter is clear, then let it drain on a sieve
4. Place the garnish in the soup bowl
5. Bring the consommé to a boil, season with spices and pour over the garnish
6. Serve very hot with cheese or butter pastry sticks
