## Ingredients

- 2 hg. toppsocker
- 1 1/2 dcl. vatten
- 1 1/2 lit. gula, röda ell. gröna plommon
- 2 msk. strösocker (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Make a clear, syrupy syrup from the sugar and water and let it cool.
2. Peel the plums, halve them, remove the pits, place them directly in the sugar syrup, sprinkle with powdered sugar and let stand 2-3 hours.
3. Crush half of the pits; blanch the kernels in boiling water and peel them.
4. Remove the plums with a slotted spoon and cook the syrup until it is syrupy, then add the plums and kernels and cook for 2-3 minutes.
5. Pour the compote into a warm compote bowl and serve cold with whipped cream.
