## Ingredients

- 1 liter leftover pea soup
- 1 1/2 liters broth
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoon flour
- 1/2 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- salt
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Bring the pea soup to a boil and strain it, then add the broth.
2. Mix butter and flour together and stir into the pea purée, then let it boil for about 10 minutes.
3. Scrape the Jerusalem artichokes and let them cook tender in lightly salted water and add them with their broth.
4. Taste the soup.
5. Whisk the egg yolk in the soup bowl, and add the soup with whisking.
