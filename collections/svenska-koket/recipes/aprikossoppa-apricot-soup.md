## Ingredients

- 3 hectograms apricots
- 2 liters water
- 1 3/4 - 2 cups crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour
- 3 parts cream for serving

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots and soak them in water for 1 hour.
2. Place them to cook in the water in which they soaked.
3. Add the sugar and let the apricots cook until completely soft.
4. Thicken the soup with the potato flour mixed with a little cold water, bring to a boil, and serve with whipped cream.
