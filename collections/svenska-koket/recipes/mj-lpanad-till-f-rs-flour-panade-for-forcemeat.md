## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. water
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 30 gr. butter
- 75 gr. sifted flour
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.) for the dish

## Directions

1. Boil the water with salt and butter and whisk in the flour over a full boil.
2. Stir the mixture with a spatula over fresh heat until it releases from the pan and spatula, then pour it onto a buttered dish and brush a little butter over the top so that it does not dry.
