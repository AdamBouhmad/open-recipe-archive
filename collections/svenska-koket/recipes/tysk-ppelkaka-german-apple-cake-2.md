## Ingredients

- 1 twelve öre wheat bread
- 5 parts milk
- 5 eggs
- 2 tbsp. butter
- 5 parts apple puree
- 2 tbsp. currants
- ½ tbsp. butter (for the form)

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into slices.
2. Whisk two eggs with half the milk, and dip the bread slices in it, after which fry them lightly in the butter.
3. Place them in a buttered form and cover with the apple puree and the rinsed and cleaned currants.
4. Make a custard from the remaining milk and eggs, which is poured over the cake, which is immediately placed in the oven to bake.
5. Turn it out, sprinkle with sugar and serve with whipped cream.
