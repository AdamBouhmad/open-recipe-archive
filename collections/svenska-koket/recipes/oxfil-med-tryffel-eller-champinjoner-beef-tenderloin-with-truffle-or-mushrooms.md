## Ingredients

- 1 kg. beef tenderloin
- 1 tin truffle (1/8 liter) or 1 tin mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 1/2 liter brown broth

## Directions

1. The tenderloin is treated as in the preceding recipe and larded in dense rows with half of the truffle or mushroom feet, cut into strips.
2. It is tied, roasted, and garnished as in the preceding recipe, but the shells are filled with browned potatoes, mixed vegetables, and some of the sautéed mushroom caps instead of vegetable salad.
3. Serve with truffle sauce or reduced sauce, supplemented with the rest of the mushroom caps, cut into cubes and sautéed in butter.
