## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. rice grains (150 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 1 liter milk
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 30 sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- zest of 1/2 lemon, finely grated
- For breading: 2 tbsp. flour (20 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1 tsp. oil
- 1 1/2 dcl. crushed rusks
- For cooking: 1 kg. lard

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains in cold water, blanch in hot water, strain through a sieve, rinse with cold water and let drain.
2. Butter a saucepan with 1/2 tbsp. of the butter, pour in the milk and let boil up, add the grains and let simmer slowly until soft, or for about 1/2 hour.
3. Strain the porridge, mix immediately with the rest of the butter and let cool, then add the beaten eggs, seasonings, the blanched and ground almonds.
4. The mass is treated and the croquettes are cooked as in recipe no. 790. Sprinkle with sugar and serve with jam or compote as a dessert.
