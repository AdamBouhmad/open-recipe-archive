## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fat herrings
- herring roe
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 red onions
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 2 tablespoons grated rusks
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the dish
- 1 tablespoon grated rusks for the dish

## Directions

1. Treat the herring as in the previous recipe.
2. Reserve the roe, rinse it and let it soak in milk.
3. Butter a fireproof dish with butter and sprinkle with rusks.
4. Lay the well-dried fillets in it and brush with the whisked egg yolk.
5. Cut the roe into slices and add along with the peeled onion sliced thinly.
6. Put butter in flakes on top and sprinkle with rusks.
7. Gratine in a warm oven about 10-15 minutes and serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
