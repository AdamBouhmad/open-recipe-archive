## Ingredients

- 1 unflayed rabbit, about 3 kg
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 5 deciliters broth or water
- 1/2 deciliter white wine

## Directions

1. The rabbit, which has hung so that the meat is tender, is flayed and treated as hare (recipe 590), chopped into smaller pieces, which are rubbed with a mixture of salt and pepper.
2. The lard is cut into slices and some of it is placed in the bottom of a pot.
3. The meat is placed in it; between each layer of meat sprinkle the flour, onion, parsley and the mushrooms cut into slices.
4. The rest of the lard is placed on top, then the broth and wine are added and the meat is allowed to simmer slowly with a lid until it is tender, or about 1 1/4 hours.
5. Place it on a warm carrot, whisk up the sauce, strain it, skim it, taste it and pour it over the meat.
6. Serve with boiled potatoes.
