## Ingredients

- Leftovers of cooked or roasted poultry or game (about 250 g meat)
- 50 g tongue or ham
- 1 hg. mushrooms
- 50 g truffle
- 2 1/2 tbsp. butter (50 g)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 g)
- 2 dcl. broth
- 2 dcl. cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 sheets gelatin
- salt, white pepper
- For breading: 4 tbsp. flour
- 2 eggs
- salt
- 2 tsp. table oil
- 1 cup finely crushed rusks
- For frying: 1 kg. cream

## Directions

1. Remove skin and bones from cooked or roasted poultry or game leftovers and cut into cubes.
2. Cut the tongue or ham and mushrooms likewise into cubes.
3. Fry the mushrooms in half of the butter and remove.
4. Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes.
5. Add the broth and cream and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
6. Stir in the meat and mushrooms together with the whisked egg yolks and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in 1 tbsp. hot water.
7. Season the mixture with spices and treat as in recipe no. 49.
8. Serve the croquettes as a course with peas and poultry sauce.
