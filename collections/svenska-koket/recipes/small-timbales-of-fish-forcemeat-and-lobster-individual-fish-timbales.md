## Ingredients

- 400 gr pike = 150 gr scraped fish meat
- 75 gr butter
- 15 gr flour
- 1 1/2 dcl cream
- 1 egg
- 2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr) for molds
- 2 tbsp crushed crackers
- 2 balls crayfish butter (20 gr)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr)
- 3 dcl cream
- 1 can lobster (1/4 liter)
- salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish forcemeat as in the previous recipe.
2. Butter small timbale molds holding about 2 dcl and sprinkle with crushed crackers.
3. Line them with a layer of forcemeat about 1 cm thick.
4. Fill with lobster stew, place a lid of forcemeat on top of the stew, and bind the molds over with butter-spread parchment paper.
5. Bake the timbales in a water bath in the oven about 1/2 hour.
6. Turn out after 10 minutes, place on a round dish, garnish with parsley and serve as an entrée.
7. The timbales can be filled with stew of crayfish, shrimp or chicken.
