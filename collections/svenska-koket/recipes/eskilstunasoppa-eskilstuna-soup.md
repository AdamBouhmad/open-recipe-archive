## Ingredients

- 1 deciliter crushed grain
- 2 liters water
- 1 liter fresh, tart apples
- 1–1½ tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2–3 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 1 tablespoon gooseberry jam

## Directions

1. Rinse the grain and place it in cold water and let it cook with a tight lid.
2. Rinse the apples, dry them, cut them into quarters and remove the cores.
3. Add them to the soup after it has cooked for ½ hour, and let them cook until they fall apart, then strain the soup.
4. Add the sugar and bring the soup to a boil.
5. In a soup bowl, blend the egg yolks and cream, whisk them up and pour the soup in while stirring constantly.
6. Add the gooseberry jam to the soup, which is served warm with biscuits.
