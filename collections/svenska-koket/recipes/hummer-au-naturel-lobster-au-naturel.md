## Ingredients

- 1 lobster of 4-5 hg.
- 2.5 liters water
- 1 hg. coarse salt
- Green salad or parsley for garnish
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Boil water with coarse salt.
2. Place the lobster head first into the boiling water—it dies immediately—and let it cook for 15-20 minutes.
3. Let the lobster lie on its back to cool.
4. Just before serving, split it in two halves.
5. Crush the claws carefully.
6. Remove the intestine and stomach sac located at the head.
7. Place the lobster on a platter, garnish with green salad or parsley, and serve with oil, vinegar, and pepper.
