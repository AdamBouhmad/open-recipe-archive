## Ingredients

- 6 large veal cutlets
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 tablespoons clarified butter (30 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.) for frying
- 150 gr. good macaroni
- 2 liters water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt (23 gr.) for macaroni
- 2 dcl. tomato purée or 30 gr. melted butter

## Directions

1. Treat the cutlets as in recipe no. 514, rub in with the spices, dip in the egg yolks mixed with the butter, turn in a mixture of breadcrumbs and cheese and fry as in no. 514.
2. Break the macaroni into 3 cm. long pieces, rinse in cold water, place in boiling salted water and allow to cook 15-20 minutes.
3. Pour into a colander, let drain and mix with the tomato purée (which has been heated in a water bath) or with the melted butter.
4. Place the macaroni in the middle of a warm serving dish, arrange the cutlets in a ring around and garnish the dish with parsley.
