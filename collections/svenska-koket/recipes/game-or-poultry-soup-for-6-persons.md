## Ingredients

- Scraps and bones of hare or carcass and scraps of poultry
- 2 carrots
- 1/2 root celery
- 1 small red onion
- 4 parsley stems
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- 2 liters beef stock without meat
- 4 white peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- 4 tbsp flour (40 gr)
- salt
- cayenne
- sugar
- 1/2 dcl sherry or Madeira
- 10 gr fine butter

## Directions

1. If raw bones and carcass are used, the soup will have a finer flavor. The best meat should then be removed and used for forcemeat or meatballs, which are served in the soup.
2. Cut the scraps and bones into cubes and brown in the butter.
3. Add the cleaned and cut vegetables and let them cook slowly in the stock for about 1 hour.
4. Brown the flour in butter for 2 minutes, thin with the stock little by little, add the soup and let it cook while stirring.
5. Strain the soup, skim carefully and season with salt, cayenne and sugar if desired.
6. Add the sherry or Madeira and finish with fine butter.
7. Serve very hot.
