## Ingredients

- Dough: 220 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. warm milk
- 25 gr. pressed yeast
- 220 gr. fine butter
- 2 msk. sugar (30 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 30 gr. sweet almonds or hazelnuts
- 1/2 tsk. finely grated lemon or orange zest
- 5 whole eggs
- For the mold: 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)
- Sugar syrup: 1/2 kg. sugar
- 5 dcl. water
- 3 dcl. maraschino liqueur or white wine or 1/2 dcl. arrack

## Directions

1. The flour is sifted, a portion of it is worked well together with the milk and the yeast mixed with 2 tsk. sugar.
2. The dough is worked until it becomes smooth and glossy, and is left on the oven door to rise.
3. The butter is worked well with the sugar, salt, the blanched and ground almonds or the ground nuts and lemon zest.
4. When the dough has risen well, the butter mixture and the rest of the flour are added along with the eggs, one at a time, and the dough is worked for approximately 1/2 hour, or until it is so elastic that it can be drawn out in long threads.
5. It is placed in a buttered and floured ring mold, which should not be filled more than halfway, and is left to rise to double its size.
6. The cake is baked in a warm oven for approximately 3/4 hour.
7. A clear syrup is made from the sugar and water, and maraschino liqueur, wine or arrack is added to it.
8. The cake is turned out of the mold, the mold is filled halfway with sugar syrup, the savarin is placed in and left until it has absorbed all the syrup, then it is turned out of the mold and placed on a flat cake dish with cake paper.
