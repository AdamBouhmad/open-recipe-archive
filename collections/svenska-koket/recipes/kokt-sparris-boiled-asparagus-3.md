## Ingredients

- 50 fresh asparagus spears
- 2 liters water
- 30 gr. salt

## Directions

1. Asparagus is harvested from mid-May to the end of June.
2. It can be preserved for 4-5 days in a cellar if bundled in bunches and covered with sand.
3. The largest are served boiled as a side dish; the smaller ones are used for soups and stewed dishes.
4. Scrape the asparagus carefully with a sharp knife against the tip without touching it; only remove the outermost skin.
5. Rinse it carefully in cold water, tie it together in five bunches with the tips all pointing the same way.
6. Bring the water to a boil with the salt and skim it well, then add the asparagus and let it boil for 15-20 minutes, depending on the quality of the asparagus.
7. Remove it immediately and let it drain on a sieve or cloth.
8. Place the asparagus on a serving plate with a folded napkin and cut the strings off.
9. Serve it very hot with melted butter.
