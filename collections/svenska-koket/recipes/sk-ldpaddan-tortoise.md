## Ingredients

- calf roast
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- wine
- seasonings
- eggs
- sugar
- milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the calf roast into fine cubes.
2. Brown the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the bouillon and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add the meat and warm it in the sauce, but do not let it boil; season the stew with the wine and seasonings.
4. For the omelette, whisk the eggs well with salt, sugar, flour and milk.
5. Heat the omelette pan and grease it well with butter.
6. Fry 2 omelettes from the egg mixture, which are done when they have a light brown color.
7. Turn the first omelette out onto a round dish, spread the meat stew on it, turn the second omelette out onto a butter lid and lay it over the stew.
8. Serve the Tortoise with clarified butter as a breakfast dish.
