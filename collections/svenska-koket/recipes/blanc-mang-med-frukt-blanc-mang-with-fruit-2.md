## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 1 hectogram fine sugar
- 1 teaspoon strawberry jam
- 1 hectogram biscuits
- Blanc-mangé: 3 parts thick cream
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 2 sheets gelatin
- Garnish: strawberry jam, biscuits

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges, cut them into slices, and lay them in a gelatin mold with a little fine sugar between each layer.
2. On top of the oranges, lay a layer of biscuits and above that strawberry jam.
3. Prepare the blanc-mangé according to the previous recipe and place it on top in the mold.
4. When it has set, garnish it with strawberries and biscuits.
