## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 1 liter weak ale
- 6 parts ale
- sugar
- lemon peel or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Bring ale and beer to boil together and pour boiling over the whisked egg yolks with vigorous whisking.
2. Season the egg ale with sugar, lemon peel, or cinnamon and serve with rusks.
