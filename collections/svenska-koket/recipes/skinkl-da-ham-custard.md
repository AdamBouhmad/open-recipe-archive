## Ingredients

- 1 hectogram smoked ham
- 3 eggs
- 3 small cups milk
- salt
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold

## Directions

1. An omelette mold is buttered, and the smoked ham, cut into cubes, is laid in it.
2. The eggs are whisked together with the milk and a little salt and poured over the ham.
3. The mold is placed in the oven to set and brown.
