## Ingredients

- 4 dcl. extra thick cream
- 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Whisk the cream with the sugar until stiff peaks form and flavor with vanilla sugar if desired.
2. Freeze it in an ice cream machine as described in no. 929, serve with a spoon in a silver bowl and serve with fruit.
3. It can also be packed in an ice mold, which then stands for 1 hour in ice and salt.
4. Unmold and garnish with cooked fruit.
5. The cream can also be mixed with 3 dcl. fruit purée and frozen in the same way.
