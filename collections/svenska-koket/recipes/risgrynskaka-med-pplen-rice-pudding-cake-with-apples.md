## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup rice
- 1 liter unwhipped milk
- 2 cups applesauce
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice, place it in boiling milk and let it cook until soft.
2. Butter a pie form; place half of the rice porridge in the form.
3. Place the applesauce on the porridge and cover with the remaining porridge.
4. For the custard, whisk eggs and milk together and blend.
