## Ingredients

- 750 gr. boneless veal top round
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 dcl. broth
- 2 msk. Madeira

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat (see p. 232), cut into 12 slices, which are well pounded, shaped with a knife, sprinkled with spices and turned in flour.
2. Brown the butter in a frying pan, lay the cutlets in and fry nicely brown on both sides, place on a warm platter.
3. Whisk out the pan with the boiling broth and wine, then pour the sauce over the cutlets.
4. Serve with braised vegetables.
