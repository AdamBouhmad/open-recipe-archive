## Ingredients

- 3 sea tongues of about 500 g each
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- For cooking: 1.5 tablespoons butter (30 g) and 5 deciliters fish broth
- For sauce: 2 tablespoons butter (40 g), 4 tablespoons flour (40 g), 5 deciliters fish broth, 2–3 egg yolks, 1 tablespoon fine white wine, salt, white pepper
- For garnish: 1/2 liter good potato purée, 3 tablespoons grated cheese, 2 tablespoons melted butter (40 g)

## Directions

1. Clean the sea tongues, rinse them well and dry them; cut off the fins.
2. Make a deep incision with a pointed knife along the backbone.
