## Ingredients

- 850 gr. prime wheat flour
- 1/2 liter skim milk
- 40 gr. pressed yeast
- 75 gr. sugar
- 2 hg. butter
- 20 gr. almonds, of which 5 bitter
- 1 egg for brushing
- Almond paste: 1 hg. sweet almonds
- 5 bitter almonds
- 2 hg. powdered sugar
- 1/2-1 tbsp. cold water

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as in recipe 950 and it should be quite loose.
2. When it has risen for the second time, work it well on the work surface and divide into 14-16 parts, which are shaped into round buns and placed on well-buttered baking sheets, brushed with beaten egg, and let rise quickly, preferably over steam.
3. Bake the semlor in a very hot oven.
4. When they are cold, cut a lid into each bun, remove some of the filling, and fill the semlor with almond paste.
5. For the almond paste: blanch the almonds, dry them, grind them in an almond mill, and pound with the sugar and a little water until smooth.
