## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 7 deciliters good milk
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1/2 gr. white pepper
- 10 gr. cold butter

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes.
2. Add the milk and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Season with the spices and stir in the cold butter, after which it should not boil.
4. If the sauce is used for lutfisk, season it with pepper; if served with paltbread, omit the pepper and the sauce can, if desired, be seasoned with 1 tablespoon grated mesost.
