## Ingredients

- 250 grams prunes
- 120 grams granulated sugar
- 5 egg whites
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the form

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes and cook soft in a short amount of water, then remove the pits, chop finely and mix with the sugar
2. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form and gently fold into the rest
3. Pour everything into a buttered pie form and bake in the oven for approximately 10 minutes
4. Serve immediately with whipped cream
