## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. sour cream milk
- 1 hg. melted butter
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 50 gr. caster sugar
- 25 sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 25 gr. compressed yeast
- approx. 280 gr. flour
- 1 tbsp. applesauce or red currant jelly
- 1 kg. lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Blend the milk, which should be lukewarm, with the butter, the beaten eggs, the sugar and the blanched and grated almonds.
2. Add the flour and the yeast dissolved in 1 tsp. sugar, and work the dough until it becomes smooth and shiny, then let it rise in a warm place for approx. 2 hours.
3. Work the dough on the baking board, roll it out to 1 cm. thickness and cut it out with a round cutter into cakes, which should be approx. 6 cm. in diameter.
4. Place a little applesauce or red currant jelly on the middle of each cake; brush the edges with egg white and press them well together.
5. Place the puffs on a floured baking sheet and let them rise for approx. 1 1/2 hours.
6. Cook them in moderately hot lard until they are thoroughly cooked and a beautiful brown, place them on absorbent paper and let them drain.
7. Dust with sugar and serve warm with coffee or tea.
