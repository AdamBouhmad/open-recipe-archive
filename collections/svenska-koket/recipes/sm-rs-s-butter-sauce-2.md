## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 4 deciliters boiling water with 3 g salt added
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 75 g finest butter, melted

## Directions

1. Sauté butter and flour for 5 minutes without browning.
2. Add the water gradually in small amounts, but the sauce should not boil.
3. Add the egg yolks whisked with cream and let the sauce simmer.
4. Season with lemon juice, strain through cloth and stir in the melted butter.
5. Serve with fish, meat and vegetables.
6. The sauce can also be cooked with fish stock and cream and in the simplest case with good milk.
7. Shrimp sauce is prepared in the same way.
