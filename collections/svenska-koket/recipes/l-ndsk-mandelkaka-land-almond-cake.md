## Ingredients

- 10 eggs
- 2 hectograms sugar
- 1/2 hectogram pea flour
- 1/2 hectogram potato flour
- 1 hectogram sweet almonds
- 15 grams bitter almonds
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams) for the pan
- 4 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and sugar for 1/2 hour.
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold in quickly.
3. Add the flour, then last the blanched and ground almonds.
4. Pour the batter into a large, buttered and rusk-strewn, fluted copper pan and bake in a not-too-hot oven for approximately 45 minutes.
