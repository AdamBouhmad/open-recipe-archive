## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. patna rice grains (2 hg.)
- 3 liters water
- 1 1/2 tbsp. salt (23 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains well in cold water, let drain, place in boiling salted water and let boil vigorously for 15 minutes; pour off the water, and fill the saucepan with cold water, in which the rice grains are shaken so they separate from each other.
2. Strain through a colander, let drain, turn out onto a cloth laid on a sieve, and let stand in moderately warm oven for 15 minutes, during which they are often stirred with a fork so the water evaporates.
3. Serve with meat, garnished with parsley.
