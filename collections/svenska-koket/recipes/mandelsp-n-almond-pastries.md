## Ingredients

- 2 sheets oblate
- 175 gr. sweet almond
- 200 gr. powdered sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 1 large egg white (for glaze)
- 125 gr. powdered sugar (for glaze)
- 1 tsp. lemon juice (for glaze)
- 30 gr. sweet almond (for garnish)
- 15 gr. pistachios (for garnish)
- 50 gr. candied orange peel (for garnish)
- 50 gr. candied fruit (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Each oblate sheet is divided into twelve equal, oblong rectangles.
2. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, pass through an almond mill and pound together with the sugar and egg whites into a fine and smooth mass, to which more egg white is added if it is too hard.
3. Carefully spread the almond mass on the oblate and place the pastries on almond pastry forms and put in not too warm an oven to become light yellow and dry on the surface, but soft inside.
4. Remove them, let them cool, brush with the glaze (see no. 961) and immediately sprinkle with the blanched and finely chopped almonds and pistachios, as well as the orange peel and candied fruit cut into fine cubes, all well mixed together.
5. Let the pastries remain on the forms and put back in the oven so that the glaze dries and gets a beautiful light yellow color.
