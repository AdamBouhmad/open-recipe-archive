## Ingredients

- Dough: 220 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. warm milk
- 25 gr. compressed yeast
- 220 gr. fine butter
- 2 msk. sugar (30 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 30 gr. sweet almonds or hazelnuts
- 1/2 tsk. finely grated lemon or orange zest
- 5 whole eggs
- For the mold: 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)
- Sugar syrup: 1/2 kg. sugar
- 5 dcl. water
- 3 dcl. maraschino liqueur or white wine or 1/2 dcl. arrack

## Directions

1. The flour is sifted, a portion of it is worked well together with the milk and the yeast stirred with 2 tsk. sugar.
2. The dough is worked until it becomes supple and shiny, and is allowed to stand on the oven door to rise.
3. The butter is stirred well with the sugar, salt, the blanched and ground almonds or the ground nuts and lemon zest.
4. When the dough has risen well, the butter mixture and the rest of the flour are added, as well as the eggs, one at a time, and the dough is worked for approximately 1/2 hour, or until it is so elastic that it can be drawn out into long strands.
5. It is then poured into a buttered and floured ring mold, which should not be filled more than halfway, and is allowed to rise to double its size.
6. The cake is baked in a hot oven for approximately 3/4 hour.
7. A clear syrup is boiled from the sugar and water, to which maraschino liqueur, wine or arrack is added.
8. The cake is turned out, the mold is filled halfway with sugar syrup, the savarin is placed in and is allowed to stand until it has absorbed all the syrup, then it is turned out of the mold and placed on a flat cake plate with cake paper.
