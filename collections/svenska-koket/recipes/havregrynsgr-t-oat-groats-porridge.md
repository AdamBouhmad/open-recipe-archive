## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups regular or 2 cups steam-prepared oat groats
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 liter milk
- 2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats and put them on in the cold water.
2. When it comes to a boil, pour in the milk, and let the porridge cook slowly until done (3 hours for regular groats, 1 1/2 hours for steam-prepared groats).
3. Taste the porridge with salt.
