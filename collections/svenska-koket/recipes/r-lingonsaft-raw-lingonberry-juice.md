## Ingredients

- 10 liters lingonberries
- 5 liters water
- 12 grams tartaric acid
- 7.5 kg granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Crush the lingonberries, blend with the tartaric acid and water and let stand for 24 hours.
2. Pour into cheesecloth to drain naturally and stir with the sugar for 1 hour.
3. Bottle the juice, cork and seal with resin.
