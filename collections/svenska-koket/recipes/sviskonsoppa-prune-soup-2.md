## Ingredients

- 200 grams prunes
- 60 grams sugar
- Peel of ¼ lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 2 litres water
- 1½ tablespoon potato flour
- 3 parts heavy cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes in lukewarm water.
2. Pound half of them in a mortar until the pits break apart.
3. Put both the whole and pounded prunes in the cold water together with lemon peel and cinnamon and let them cook until soft.
4. Press through a sieve.
5. Dilute the pressed soup if needed, add sugar, and thicken with potato flour mixed with a little cold water.
6. Bring to a good boil and taste.
7. Serve preferably cold, with whipped cream and rusks.
