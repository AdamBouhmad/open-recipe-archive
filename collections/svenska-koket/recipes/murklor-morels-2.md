## Ingredients

- Murklor (morel mushrooms) - two varieties: brown stone morel and top morel
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms thoroughly
2. Rinse extremely well so that all sand and dirt is removed
3. Blanch as with chanterelles
4. Sauté in butter for 15-20 minutes until the butter is clear
5. Season with salt and pepper and lemon juice if desired
6. Serve with open-faced sandwiches or in sauces and stews
7. Can also be stewed as champinjoner
