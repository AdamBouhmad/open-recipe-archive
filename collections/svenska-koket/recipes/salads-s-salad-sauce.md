## Ingredients

- 1/4 tsp salt
- A few grains paprika
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1-1/2 tbsp vinegar
- 4 tbsp fine salad oil
- (1 tsp French mustard)

## Directions

1. Place the spices in a bowl and mix with the vinegar, then add the oil little by little, just before the sauce is to be served
2. This sauce is used for green salad, potato salad and, with a little French mustard added, for lobster and fish salad
