## Ingredients

- 3 deciliters dried green peas
- 3 deciliters water
- 2 deciliters bouillon
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- a little white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the peas as in the previous recipe
2. When the water has boiled down, thin with bouillon
3. Pass the peas through a fine sieve
4. Heat the purée, season with spices and blend with the cold butter
5. Serve in croustades with roasted meat, together with other vegetables
