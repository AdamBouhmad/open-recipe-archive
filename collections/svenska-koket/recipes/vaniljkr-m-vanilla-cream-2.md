## Ingredients

- 4 parts thick cream
- 1/2 vanilla bean
- 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Heat the cream with the vanilla bean until it has sufficient vanilla flavor. Add the sugar and remove from heat. Whisk the egg yolks together in a bowl and fold the cream in with vigorous whisking. Return to heat and let simmer while whisking constantly, but do not boil. Cool the cream and whisk until completely cold.
