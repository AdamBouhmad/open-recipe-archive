## Ingredients

- 2½ kg mutton saddle
- 5 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp salt
- 3 large Portuguese onions

## Directions

1. Wash the mutton saddle with a cloth dipped in hot water and beat very well.
2. Brown 2 tbsp of the butter in a long pan, place the saddle in it and put in the oven.
3. When it has a nice brown color, salt it and continue roasting until tender.
4. Parboil the onion, peel and cut into large wedges, which are browned in the remaining butter and served with the roast.
5. Strain the sauce, skim, and serve as gravy.
