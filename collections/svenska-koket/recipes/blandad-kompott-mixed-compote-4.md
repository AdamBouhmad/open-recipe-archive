## Ingredients

- Various fruits, cooked separately according to above descriptions
- Mixed, boiled together sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Arrange the separately cooked fruits prettily in a compote dish.
2. Pour the mixed, boiled together syrup over.
3. Serve with frozen or whipped cream.
