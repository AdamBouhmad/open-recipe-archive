## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg carrots
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- 2 liters secondary broth
- 4 tbsp flour (40 gr)
- salt, cayenne
- 2-3 egg yolks
- 1 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Brush the carrots, rinse, scrape, rinse again, dry and cut into cubes, which are browned in half of the butter.
2. Add 1 liter of the boiling broth and let the carrots cook until they are quite soft, then strain.
3. Fry the rest of the butter with the flour 2 minutes.
4. Add the purée and the remaining broth and let the soup cook 5 minutes.
5. Season with the spices.
6. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl, pour in the purée while whisking and serve with toasted bread or cheese croutons.
