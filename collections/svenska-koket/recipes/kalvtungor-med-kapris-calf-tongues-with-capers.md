## Ingredients

- 2 large calf tongues
- 3½ tablespoons salt
- water
- 1 carrot
- 1 piece celery
- 1 leek
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2-3 tablespoons of the cooking broth
- 1½ tablespoons chopped capers
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 eggs for breading
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs for breading
- ¼ kg lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Let the tongues soak in cold water for 1 hour.
2. Scald them and scrape well.
3. Place them in boiling water and skim thoroughly when the broth comes to a boil.
4. Add salt and root vegetables, and let the tongues cook slowly until tender.
5. When done, remove the skin immediately and cut the meat into fine cubes.
6. Combine butter and flour, add the cooking broth, place the tongue and finely chopped capers in, and let the stew cook for 10 minutes, then taste for seasoning.
7. Spread the mixture on a flat dish to cool, and form small sugar-loaf shaped croquettes.
8. Roll in crushed bread crumbs, dip in whisked egg, and roll again in crushed bread crumbs.
9. Let stand to dry for ½ hour.
10. Heat the lard, cook the croquettes golden-brown in it, and place on brown paper to drain.
11. Garnish with parsley and serve with mushroom or brown sauce.
