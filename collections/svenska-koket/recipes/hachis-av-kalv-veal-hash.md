## Ingredients

- 400-500 g boneless roasted or cooked veal
- 1 red onion
- 1.5 tbsp butter (30 g)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 g)
- 300-400 ml meat stock or broth
- 2 tbsp tomato purée
- a little paprika
- 2 tbsp finely chopped pickled cucumber
- 1 tbsp Madeira
- 1 tsp cognac
- salt
- sugar
- 0.5 tbsp soy sauce

## Directions

1. Chop the meat finely (do not grind it).
2. Peel the onion, chop and brown in the butter together with the flour (see Sauces, p. 210); add the stock and tomato purée and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Add the chopped meat and season the stew with paprika, cucumber, wine, cognac, spices and soy sauce.
4. Heat the dish but do not allow it to boil; serve in tartlets or pastry shells or on a warm plate, garnished with fried or poached eggs, browned potatoes and parsley.
