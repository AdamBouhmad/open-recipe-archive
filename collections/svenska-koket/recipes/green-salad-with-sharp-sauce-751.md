## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized salad heads
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 2 tsk. sugar (10 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1/2 tsk. dry or French mustard
- 1 1/2 msk. vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as in no. 450. If desired, do not whip the cream to foam.
2. Prepare the salad just before it is to be served.
3. Break apart the salad heads, remove the stems, rinse the leaves well and shake them in a cloth to dry them; cut the largest leaves into pieces.
4. Place the leaves in a salad bowl, pour the sauce over, and stir the salad gently.
5. Garnish with the hard-boiled egg white, cut with a knife into beautiful strips, and serve with stuffed eggs and roasted meat.
