## Ingredients

- 2 kg. lamb roast (loin)
- 1 1/2 hg. lard
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 3/4 liter boiling broth or water

## Directions

1. The roast, which should have hung so it is tender, is dried, pounded, larded, and the shank bone is cut off.
2. It is larded most on the outer thigh and back, the knuckle is broken to facilitate carving, then the roast is rubbed with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. A roasting pan is heated, the butter is browned in it along with some of the lard, the roast is placed in it with the inner thigh up and browned for about 1/2 hour in a warm oven, during which it is frequently basted with the fat in the pan.
4. It is diluted with the boiling broth or water and allowed to roast until tender, or about 1 1/2-2 hours; during which it is basted every 10 minutes.
5. Place on a warm serving platter and carve at the table. Serve with white beans and vegetables or vegetable patties, and the strained, skimmed meat gravy or with thickened sauce.
