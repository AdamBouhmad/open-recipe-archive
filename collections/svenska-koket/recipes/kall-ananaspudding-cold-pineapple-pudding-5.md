## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple (about 750 gr.)
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 egg white
- 1 hg. sugar
- 1/2 dcl. sherry
- 1 dcl. pineapple juice
- 5 sheets gelatin
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Cut one quarter of the pineapple into small cubes, and press all the juice out of them through a cloth.
2. Whip the egg yolks, egg white, sugar, wine, and half of the pineapple juice in a saucepan over the fire until the mixture is thick and foamy, then remove the saucepan from heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
3. Rinse the gelatin with cold water and dissolve it in the rest of the pineapple juice, which has been heated beforehand.
4. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
5. First mix the pineapple cubes into the cream, then the gelatin, and finally fold in the whipped cream very carefully.
6. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for 3-4 hours.
7. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a sharp knife and turn it out onto a flat glass dish with parchment paper.
8. Garnish with the remaining pineapple, sliced thin. One pineapple suffices for 12 persons, as the slices with which the pudding is garnished can be divided.
