## Ingredients

- 1 suckling pig, approximately 4 kg
- 1 1/2 tablespoon salt (23 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- For stuffing: 1 hectogram prunes
- 1 liter apples
- 3 deciliters water
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 6 deciliters grated bread (limpa or wheat bread)
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2-3 eggs
- For roasting: 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 3/4 liter broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the pig as in the previous recipe and rub with the spices.
2. Rinse the prunes well in lukewarm water. Peel the apples, cut them into quarters and cook the fruit for 10 minutes in the water with the sugar added, after which pit the prunes.
3. Fry the bread in the butter, place it on a plate and let it cool. Mix it well with the fruit along with its liquid and the beaten eggs.
4. Stuff the pig with this mixture and sew it closed and prepare as in the previous recipe.
5. Serve with the stuffing and with potatoes, sour salads and strained sauce.
