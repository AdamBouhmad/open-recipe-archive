## Ingredients

- 2-3 red cabbage heads
- water
- salt
- 4-6 msk. flour
- 2 msk. butter
- 2 1/2 lit. broth or pork broth
- white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put the cabbage (which should be frozen) in water so that the ice is drawn out.
2. Grate it thereafter, rinse it well and blanch it 1/2 hour in salted water.
3. Lay the cabbage on a sieve to drain and chop it thereafter with flour until it becomes very fine.
4. Melt the butter in a pan, add the cabbage and let it fry with frequent stirring.
5. Add the boiling broth, and let the soup cook 1/2 hour.
6. Season it thereafter and serve with meatballs or pork sausage.
7. The thickest cabbage stems can be blanched, peeled, cut into slices, dipped in egg and breadcrumbs and fried golden brown in butter and served in the cabbage.
