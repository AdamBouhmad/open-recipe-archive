## Ingredients

- 1 kg carrots
- 3 shallots
- 5 tablespoons butter (1 hectoliter)
- 2 liters water
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 deciliters thick cream
- Garnish: 1 deciliter rice grains, 1 liter water, 1/2 tablespoon salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the carrots in the usual way, cut into cubes and brown in 2 tablespoons of butter together with the peeled and sliced shallot
2. Add water and cook the carrots until completely tender, about 2 hours, then pass through a sieve
3. Cook the purée as in recipe no. 266 and place 1 tablespoon of cold butter in the soup bowl
4. Rinse the rice grains, add to boiling salted water and cook until tender, about 15 minutes
5. Pour into a colander, rinse with hot water, let drain, place on a carrot and serve with the soup
