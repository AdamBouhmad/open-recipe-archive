## Ingredients

- 15 good apples
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 1.5 cups sugar
- zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- Meringue mass: 3 egg whites, 6 tablespoons powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and cut them into wedges; finely grate the yellow zest of the lemon and press out the juice.
2. Place the apples in a saucepan with the butter, sugar, lemon juice and lemon zest and shake over heat until the apples are soft.
3. Place them in a buttered pie form, which should be filled only halfway.
4. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, carefully fold in the sugar, and spread the meringue mass over the apples.
5. Place the pie form in gentle oven heat and let it stand until the meringue is light yellow.
6. Serve it warm or cold with whipped cream.
