## Ingredients

- 1 carrot
- 1 small parsnip
- 1/4 celeriac
- 1/2 leek
- 12 small Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 3/4 liter water
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1 1/4 liters clear broth

## Directions

1. Scrape, rinse, and dry the roots, then cut them into fine dice. Cut the leek into fine strips. Prepare the Jerusalem artichokes and form them round; clean the cauliflower and divide it into smaller pieces.
2. Place the roots in boiling water with salt and sugar, and let them cook for 5 minutes, then add the cauliflower and let everything cook until tender.
3. Bring the broth to a boil, add the cooked vegetables along with 1/4 liter of the cooking liquid. Season the soup with spices and serve very hot.
