## Ingredients

- 1 pressed cucumber
- 2 tablespoons fine salt
- 0.5 cup vinegar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 0.5 teaspoon crushed white pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumber and cut it into thin slices from the stem downward.
2. Layer the slices alternately with salt between flat plates, press them, and let them lie for a couple of hours.
3. Pour off the liquid that forms.
4. Make a brine from the vinegar, water, sugar, and pepper.
5. Place the cucumber in it and sprinkle with chopped parsley.
