## Ingredients

- 30 gr. mondaminmjöl (mondamin flour)
- 3 dcl. vatten (water)
- 3 1/2 dcl. franskt vin (French wine)
- 150 gr. strösocker (granulated sugar)
- skal och saft av 1/2 citron (zest and juice of 1/2 lemon)
- 4 äggulor (egg yolks)

## Directions

1. Mix the mondamin flour with 1 dcl. of the water.
2. Boil the remaining water with the sugar and the lemon zest and juice.
3. Add the wine and the mondamin mixture and let the cream boil for 5 minutes.
4. Remove the pan from heat and whisk in the egg yolks one at a time.
5. Let the cream simmer under vigorous whisking until thick, then remove from heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
6. Rinse a nice metal mold with cold water and sprinkle with granulated sugar.
7. Pour the cream into the mold and let it sit on ice for 2-3 hours.
8. Unmold the pudding onto a flat glass dish and garnish with ladyfingers or macaroons.
