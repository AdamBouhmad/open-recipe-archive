## Ingredients

- 3 kg. haunch of roe deer
- 1½ hg. lard
- 2 tbsp. butter
- 1 tbsp. salt
- 1½–2 liters boiling broth or water
- 1½ tbsp. butter for sauce
- 3 tbsp. flour
- meat stock
- 1 part sour or sweet cream

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, remove the outermost membrane, and lard the roast in close rows with the lard cut into fine strips. Brown the roast in the heated butter, salt and dilute with broth or water. The roasting takes 1½–2 hours.
2. Strain and skim the meat stock, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
