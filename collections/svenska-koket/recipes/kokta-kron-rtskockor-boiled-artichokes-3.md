## Ingredients

- 6 large artichokes
- 100 g butter
- water
- 1 lemon
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the lower leaves from the artichokes, and trim a small piece from the remaining leaves.
2. Rub the bottoms with lemon.
3. Place the artichokes in boiling, lightly salted water, just enough to cover them, and cook until the leaves come off easily.
4. Place immediately on a warm serving dish and serve with creamed butter or hollandaise sauce.
