## Ingredients

- 9 canned artichoke bottoms
- 50 gr. rice grains
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 3-4 deciliters broth
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- little white pepper
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped truffle

## Directions

1. Prepare the rice mixture first. Rinse the rice grains and let them drain well.
2. Fry them together with the onion in the butter until the butter is clear, then add the broth and let the grains cook while stirring until they are soft—add more broth if needed.
3. Pass the grains through a fine sieve, mix with the egg yolk, and let the mixture simmer, then season with the spices, and finally stir in the truffle.
4. Place the artichoke bottoms in boiling salted water, remove them when the water comes to a boil again, let them drain in a sieve and place on a warm platter.
5. On each bottom pipe some of the purée through a piping bag in the form of flowers or leaves.
6. Serve as a garnish around roasted meat.
