## Ingredients

- 500 gr. boneless veal
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 300 gr. fresh pork, not too lean
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 3 dcl. finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 dcl. boiling water
- 4 gr. solid meat extract

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat (see page 232), free from sinews and membranes, cut into pieces and put through the meat grinder 4 times
2. From the farce form cutlets, which are chopped over with a knife so they get a nice shape, sprinkle with a mixture of salt and white pepper, dip in the whisked eggs and turn in crushed breadcrumbs
3. Brown the butter lightly in a frying pan, lay the cutlets in and fry approx. 3 minutes on each side, after which they are placed in a ring on a warm serving dish
4. Whisk the pan with the water mixed with meat extract, let the sauce simmer a couple of minutes, taste and pour over the cutlets
