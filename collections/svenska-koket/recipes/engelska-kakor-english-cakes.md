## Ingredients

- 400 gr. wheat flour
- 2 tsp. baking powder
- 160 gr. butter
- 200 gr. caster sugar
- 200 gr. currants
- 30 gr. orange peel
- 2 tsp. grated ginger
- 3 eggs
- 2 tbsp. milk

## Directions

1. Wash the butter, sift the flour with the baking powder, and work in the butter.
2. Then mix in the sugar, the cleaned currants, the finely cut orange peel and the grated ginger.
3. Whisk the eggs together with the milk and stir down into the rest.
4. On well-buttered baking sheets, lay out small cakes from the dough, which are then baked golden brown in moderate oven heat.
