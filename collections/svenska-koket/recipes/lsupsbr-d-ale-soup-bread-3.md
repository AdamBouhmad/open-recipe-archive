## Ingredients

- 1/2 litre milk
- 1 kg wheat flour
- 1 litre drink
- 2 kg rye sifted flour
- 80 gr pressed yeast
- 1 tbsp salt (15 gr)
- 3 dcl syrup
- 2 tbsp crushed fennel
- 1 tbsp crushed anise
- 3 tbsp finely chopped orange peel

## Directions

1. Whisk so much wheat flour into the milk that it becomes a thick paste.
2. Boil the drink, add the paste and let the mixture come to a good boil while stirring.
3. Pour it into a baking vessel and stir in so much rye flour that it becomes a loose dough.
4. When this has cooled to about 40°, add the yeast mixed with the salt, and so much flour that it becomes a quite firm dough, which is set to rise to double its size.
5. Boil the syrup with the spices, let cool and work into the dough together with wheat flour so that the dough becomes stiff.
6. Let it rise again, then treat, shape and bake as in recipe no. 939.
