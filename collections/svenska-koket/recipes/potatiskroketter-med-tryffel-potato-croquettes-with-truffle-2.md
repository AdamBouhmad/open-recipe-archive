## Ingredients

- 7 hg potato puree
- 40–50 gr truffle
- 1 egg for brushing and coating
- 50 gr sweet almond
- 1 kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the potato puree as in recipe 680 and mix with the truffle, cut into fine dice.
2. Treat the puree as in recipe 680 and roll into the form of apricots.
3. Dip in beaten egg and roll in the almond, blanched and cut into the thinnest slivers.
4. Fry beautifully golden brown in hot lard, drain on absorbent paper and serve very hot as a garnish around roasted meat.
