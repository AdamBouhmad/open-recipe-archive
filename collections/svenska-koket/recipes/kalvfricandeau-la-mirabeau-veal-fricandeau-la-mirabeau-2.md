## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless veal loin
- 1 hg. lard
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 6 dl. dark broth
- 1 carrot
- 6 small pickled onions
- 12 small mushroom caps
- 2 olives
- 1 tablespoon tomato purée
- 1 teaspoon beef gravy

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat, lard it, and brown it as in the preceding recipe. When it is browned, add the boiling broth, and let the meat simmer slowly with a tight lid until it is completely tender.
2. Scrape the carrot and cut it with a vegetable knife into beautiful, thin pieces, which are placed in the roasting pan—when the meat has cooked 15 minutes—together with the pickled onions, mushrooms, the olives cut into strips, tomato purée, and beef gravy. Slice the roast into thin slices and place on a warm serving platter. Taste the meat gravy and pour it together with the garnish over the roast. Serve with small browned potatoes, peas, and salads.
