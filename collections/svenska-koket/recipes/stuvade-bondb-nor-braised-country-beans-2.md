## Ingredients

- 8 dcl. soaked beans
- 8 small, fresh carrots
- 8 dcl. water
- 2 tsk. salt
- 2 tsk. sugar (10 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 3 dcl. bean broth
- 3 dcl. milk
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 3 tsk. chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Remove sprouts; if the beans are very large, peel them as well.
2. Rinse the carrots, scrape them, rinse again, dry and cut into slices or cubes.
3. Bring the water to a boil with salt, add the beans and let them cook about 15 minutes, then add the carrots and let the vegetables cook until tender.
4. Drain the broth and save it.
5. Brown the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the broth and milk, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
6. Add the beans together with the parsley, and season the stew with sugar.
7. Serve with boiled ham or fried pork.
