## Ingredients

- ¼ kg macaroni
- 1½ liters milk
- salt and sugar
- ½ liter cooked or fried chopped meat and ham
- ½ medium Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 parts stock and leftover roast sauce
- 3-4 tablespoons grated strong cheese
- 3 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- ½ tablespoon butter (for the mold)

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni soft in the milk, season and let it cool.
2. Finely chop the onion and brown it together with the meat in butter.
3. Add stock or roast sauce, layer the macaroni and meat alternately in the buttered pie mold; the first and last layer should be macaroni.
4. Sprinkle the pudding with the grated cheese and crushed breadcrumbs and put it in moderate oven heat to get a nice brown color.
5. This takes about ¾ hour.
6. Serve with clarified butter or roast sauce.
