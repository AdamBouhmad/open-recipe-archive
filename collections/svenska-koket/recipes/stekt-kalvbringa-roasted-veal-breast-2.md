## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal breast
- 1 tsp salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr.)
- 3/4 liter boiling broth or water with 1 tsp meat extract

## Directions

1. Dry the breast with a cloth wrung out in hot water and crack the bones well.
2. Rub the meat with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Heat a roasting pan, fry the butter in it, place the breast in with the meat side up, set in a hot oven and brown beautifully, basting often with the butter in the pan.
4. When browned, add the boiling broth and let the meat roast until tender, about 2 hours, basting every 10 minutes.
5. Cut the roast into beautiful slices with a sharp knife, place on a hot platter, garnish with parsley and serve with vegetables or boiled potatoes, lingonberries and onion.
6. Prepare the sauce as in recipe no. 429.
