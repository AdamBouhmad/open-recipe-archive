## Ingredients

- 4 kg spirling or pollock
- 15 liters water
- 5 liters strong birch ash
- 2 parts slaked lime

## Directions

1. Soak the fish as in the previous recipe.
2. From the water, ash and lime, cook a lye.
3. Strain this into a clean tub and let it stand until the next day, when it should be completely clear.
4. Place the soaked fish in it with skin side up and let it remain for 6-8 days.
5. Treat it as in the previous recipe.
