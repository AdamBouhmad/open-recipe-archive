## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 100 gr. sugar
- 50 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. potato starch
- 5 parts thick cream
- 100 gr. butter
- 400 gr. pineapple
- 1/2 tablespoon butter and 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold
- pineapple juice
- white wine
- sugar
- 10 gr. potato starch for the sauce

## Directions

1. The egg yolks, sugar, flour and cream are simmered over heat with strong whipping until the cream thickens.
2. The pot is removed from heat, but whipping is continued, and the butter is blended in gradually.
3. The pineapple is cut into pieces and mixed in together with the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks.
4. The batter is beaten into a buttered and crumb-strewn mold and baked in not too strong heat for about 1 hour.
5. It is served warm with sauce made from pineapple juice, mixed with white wine and sugar to taste and finished with a little potato starch.
