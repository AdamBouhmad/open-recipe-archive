## Ingredients

- 6 partridges
- 1 hg. butter
- 3 tsp. salt
- 1/2 liter boiling broth
- Meat bones
- 2 liters good broth
- 12-15 sheets gelatin
- 3 egg whites and rinsed eggshells
- 1 tbsp. cold water
- 1/2 dcl. good Madeira or sherry
- 6-8 truffles
- Truffle aspic croûtons
- 2 heads of lettuce

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges, singe them, remove the innards and cut off wings and bones.
2. Rinse them very well, dry with a meat cloth and truss (see poultry).
3. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, place the partridges in breast-side up and roast in a warm oven for about 1/2 hour, basting frequently and adding broth.
4. Remove and let cool, then carefully loosen the breasts from the carcasses; cut each breast half into 3 slices.
5. Place the carcasses back in the roasting pan, add more broth and let it cook with the meat bones until reduced to about 1/2 liter.
6. Strain the broth, let cool and carefully skim off all fat.
7. Prepare the aspic as in recipe no. 181; when cold, add the wine.
8. Pour a little of the clear aspic into the bottom of 12 rinsed partridge molds and place on ice to set.
9. Decorate the molds with truffle cut into attractive shapes.
10. Pour a few drops of aspic over the garnish and let set so the garnish doesn't move when the meat is added.
11. Place half a partridge breast in each mold so it appears whole with the outer side against the garnish, then fill the molds with aspic.
12. Place the molds in a cold place to set.
13. Pour the remaining aspic onto a platter and let set.
14. Cut a cone-shaped croûton from day-old wheat bread, hollow out the top, wrap with foil and place in the center of a large round silver or porcelain platter.
15. Place flowers and greens in the top of the croûton.
16. Turn the partridge breasts out of the molds and stand them against the croûton.
17. Decorate between them with piped aspic and around the platter with small triangular aspic croûtons decorated with chopped truffle.
18. Garnish the arrangement with the inner light lettuce leaves and serve as an entrée with mayonnaise.
