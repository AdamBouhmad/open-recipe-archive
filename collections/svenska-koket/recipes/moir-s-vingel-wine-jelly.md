## Ingredients

- 1 packet Moir's gelatin powder (approximately 120 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons sugar (45 gr.)
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 4 dcl. boiling water
- 3 dcl. Rhine wine or sherry

## Directions

1. Stir the gelatin powder, sugar, lemon juice, and water in a bowl until the powder and sugar are dissolved.
2. Add the wine and pour the mixture into one large or several small water-rinsed and sugar-dusted molds.
3. Allow to set on ice for 4-5 hours.
4. This jelly can be colored red and used as garnish for cold rice pudding (see no. 910).
5. A light, fine sponge cake can be thoroughly soaked with this jelly, which should then be warmed to approximately 60 degrees.
6. Allow the jelly to set thereafter.
7. Serve the cake whole or sliced, with whipped cream.
