## Ingredients

- 400 g boneless fried meat of beef or veal
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 2½ tablespoons butter (50 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 4 deciliters broth
- salt, white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g) for breading
- 2 eggs
- salt, 2 teaspoons cooking oil
- 2 deciliters crushed rusks
- 1 kg fat for frying

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into fine dice.
2. Cut the mushrooms likewise into dice or thin slices and sauté in butter until clear, then squeeze the mushroom with a spoon.
3. Sauté the flour with the butter for 2 minutes.
4. Dilute with the broth and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Add the meat and mushroom together with the beaten egg yolk and let the stew simmer.
6. Season with spices.
7. Form the croquettes into oblong shapes and should weigh 50–60 g.
8. Serve with pan sauce or brown mushroom sauce and peas, if desired.
