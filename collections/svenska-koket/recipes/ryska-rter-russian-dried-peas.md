## Ingredients

- 150 gr. dried Russian peas
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the peas very well in several waters and let them soak overnight in the water in which they are to be cooked.
2. Place them with salt, sugar and butter and let them simmer slowly until they are completely tender, which takes approximately 2 hours.
3. Serve with roasted meat, such as pork loin, ham, veal roast etc.
