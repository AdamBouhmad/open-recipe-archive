## Ingredients

- veal roast, finely diced
- butter and flour
- broth
- wine and spices
- eggs, salt, sugar, flour and milk for omelette
- butter for the omelette pan

## Directions

1. Cut the veal roast into fine cubes.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, thin the broth into it and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add the meat and warm it in the sauce, but do not let it boil; season the stew with wine and spices.
4. For the omelettes, whisk the eggs well with salt, sugar, flour and milk.
5. Heat the omelette pan and grease it well with butter.
6. Fry 2 omelettes from the egg mixture, which are ready when they have a light brown color.
7. Tip the first omelette onto a round platter, spread the meat stew on it, tip the second omelette onto buttered paper and place it over the stew.
8. Serve Sköldpaddan with clarified butter as a breakfast dish.
