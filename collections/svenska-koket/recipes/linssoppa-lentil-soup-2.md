## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter lentils
- 30 grams smoked ham
- 1/4 Portuguese onion
- 1/4 celery
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters broth
- 1 1/2 tablespoon flour
- white pepper, salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 part cream
- 1 celery for serving
- 1/8 egg for serving
- 2 tablespoon crushed rusks for serving
- 30 grams butter for serving
- 1/2 hectogram smoked ham for serving

## Directions

1. Rinse the lentils and soak them the day before.
2. Scrape the ham and cut into pieces, scrape the celery, peel and blanch the onion, and chop everything fairly fine.
3. Fry in 1 tablespoon of butter together with the lentils.
4. When the butter is absorbed, add the broth, and let the soup simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid.
5. When the lentils are soft, strain the soup.
6. Fry the remaining butter with the flour, and add the lentil purée.
7. Let the soup boil for 10 minutes and taste it.
8. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in the soup bowl, and add the soup with vigorous whisking.
9. Scrape the celery and cook in salted water, cut into slices, bread and fry in butter.
10. Cook the ham and cut into strips.
11. Serve the celery and ham in the soup.
12. Alternatively, frankfurter sausage can be served in the soup if desired.
