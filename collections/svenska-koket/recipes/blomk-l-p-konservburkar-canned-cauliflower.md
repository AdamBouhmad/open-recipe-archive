## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Cold cooked salt brine (1 tablespoon salt per liter of water)

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and place it in canning jars as whole as possible. Pour on cold cooked salt brine (1 tablespoon salt per liter of water) and screw the jars shut or seal them. Cook for 0.5 hour.
