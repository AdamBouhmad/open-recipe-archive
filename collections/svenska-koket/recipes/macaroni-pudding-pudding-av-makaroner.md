## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hg. good macaroni
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- grated cheese (optional)
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 4 deciliters milk
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the macaroni as in the preceding recipe, place in a pie mold, layered alternately with the butter, distributed in small flakes, the seasonings and cheese, if desired.
2. For the egg mixture: whisk the egg yolks well with the seasonings and milk, then fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the egg mixture over the macaroni, and bake the pudding in good oven heat until it is set and has a golden-brown color. Serve with roasted meat or braised ham.
