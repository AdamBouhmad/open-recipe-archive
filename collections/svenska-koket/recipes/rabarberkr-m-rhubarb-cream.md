## Ingredients

- 3/4 kilogram fresh or 1 1/4 liters canned rhubarb
- 1/2 liter boiling water
- 1 1/2 cups crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the rhubarb, peel thinly, and cut into pieces approximately 1 1/2 centimeters long.
2. Place in boiling water and add the sugar.
3. When the rhubarb is soft, thicken the cream with potato flour mixed in a little cold water, bring to a short boil, and pour out to cool.
