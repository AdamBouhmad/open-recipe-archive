## Ingredients

- Butter dough: 1/4 kg wheat flour, 1 part water, 1 tablespoon brandy, 1/4 kg butter
- For filling: 2 cups apple sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as fine butter dough.
2. Roll out half of the dough to a round cake of approximately 2 cm thickness.
3. Roll out the other half somewhat thinner and cut it into strips, arranging these together to form a lattice, suitable to the bottom.
4. Bake the cake bottom and lattice separately in a hot oven.
5. When they are well baked, place them on top of each other with apple sauce between.
6. The butter dough can of course also be baked out to two equally large bottoms, which are baked separately and then placed on top of each other with apple sauce between.
7. Serve the cake with whipped cream.
