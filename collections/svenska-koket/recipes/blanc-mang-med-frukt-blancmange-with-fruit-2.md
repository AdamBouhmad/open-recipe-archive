## Ingredients

- 4 oranges
- 1 dcl. sugar (85 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon fine jam
- 50 gr. lady fingers
- 2.5 dcl. thick cream
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons vanilla sugar (10 gr.)
- 2 leaves gelatin
- 2 tablespoons warm water

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges and remove the white pith carefully, then cut into slices and remove the seeds.
2. Layer the orange slices, sugar, jam, and lady fingers in a compote bowl.
3. Prepare the blancmange as in recipe no. 876 and pour immediately over the fruit mixture.
4. Let it stand in a cool place for 2-3 hours.
5. Garnish with jam and lady fingers.
