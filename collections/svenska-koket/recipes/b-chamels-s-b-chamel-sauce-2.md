## Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped red onion
- 50 grams lean veal (in fine cubes)
- 60 grams butter
- 40 grams dry flour
- 7 deciliters boiling milk
- salt
- white pepper
- grated brown cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Brown the onion and meat in the butter until it becomes clear, then add the flour and brown it with the butter for 2 minutes.
2. Add the milk gradually, add the spices, and let the sauce cook, stirring, for 15 minutes.
3. Strain through a cloth, season, and warm in a water bath.
4. Serve with boiled chicken, veal or lamb, and may, if desired, be supplemented with a couple of tablespoons of chopped mushrooms, browned in butter.
5. Used for gratins and for royal on fish and poultry, etc.
