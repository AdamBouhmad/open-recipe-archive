## Ingredients

- 1 msk. sour cream
- 1 1/4 litre milk
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Coat the bottom of a milk vessel with 1 msk. sour cream
2. Pour in the milk and let it sour
3. Whip the thick cream until stiff peaks form
4. Add slices of the sour milk to the whipped cream
5. Serve with gingerbread cookies
