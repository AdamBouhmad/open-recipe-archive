## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- water, salt
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 5 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 cup warm water
- 1 1/2 teaspoons French mustard
- 1 tablespoon pickled onion

## Directions

1. Brush the potatoes, cook and peel them while still warm, and cut into thin slices and place in a bowl.
2. Mix the oil, vinegar, warm water, mustard and the cut-up onion well, and pour the mixture over the potatoes, which are carefully stirred.
3. The salad is advantageously served with cold meat.
