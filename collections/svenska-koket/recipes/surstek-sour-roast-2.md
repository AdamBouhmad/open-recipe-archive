## Ingredients

- 3 kg inside or outside round
- 2 tbsp salt (30 g)
- 2 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tsp crushed cloves
- For the brine: 2 1/2 dcl good vinegar
- 2 liters good weak drink
- 150 g granulated sugar
- 15 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 red onions, in slices
- For roasting: 3 hg lard
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1 liter boiling beef broth
- 1/2 liter sour cream
- 1 tbsp soya

## Directions

1. The meat is well pounded with a damp meat mallet and rubbed with a mixture of salt and crushed spices. It is placed in a crock and covered with a brine made of vinegar, weak drink, sugar, whole spices and onion, all well mixed together.
2. The meat is left to lie for 6-8 days and should be turned often during this time. It is removed, rinsed well, dried with a cloth, larded (see page 232), and tied so that it has a beautiful shape.
3. It is then roasted and served as in no. 471; first diluted with broth, then with the boiling milk mixed with soya, and allowed to roast 3 1/2-4 hours.
4. The sauce is served strained, mixed with cream (see no. 429).
