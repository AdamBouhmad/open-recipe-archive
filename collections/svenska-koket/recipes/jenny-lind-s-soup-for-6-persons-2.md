## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons pearl tapioca
- 2 liters mixed veal and beef broth
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 2 parts cream
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Rinse the tapioca in cold water, place it in the boiling broth and let it cook until it is clear.
2. Season the soup to taste.
3. Whisk the egg yolk with the cream in a soup bowl, and add the soup while whisking vigorously.
