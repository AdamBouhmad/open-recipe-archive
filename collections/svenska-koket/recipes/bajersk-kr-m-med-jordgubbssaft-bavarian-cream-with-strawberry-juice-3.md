## Ingredients

- 6 leaves gelatin
- 1/2 dcl boiling water
- 50 g granulated sugar
- 4 dcl strawberry juice
- 3 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in 1/2 dcl boiling water together with the sugar, then add the strawberry juice.
2. When the mixture is completely cold and begins to set, stir in the cream whipped until stiff.
3. Rinse a mold with cold water and dust with granulated sugar, pour in the mixture, and let the mold stand on ice for 3-4 hours.
4. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish with cake paper and garnish with preserved strawberries, biscuits or macaroons.
5. Instead of strawberry juice, raspberry, gooseberry or cherry juice may be used.
