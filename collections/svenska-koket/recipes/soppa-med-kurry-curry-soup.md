## Ingredients

- 2 liters broth
- 1 medium celery
- 2 small shallots
- 2 egg yolks
- curry powder to taste
- salt
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Sauté the celery and onion in the usual manner and cook until soft in the broth
2. Strain the broth, return it to the pot, and thicken with the egg yolks
3. Add curry powder to taste
4. Season the soup with salt and cayenne pepper
5. Serve with meatballs, fish forcemeat, or Jerusalem artichokes, parsnips, and pieces of the cooked celery
