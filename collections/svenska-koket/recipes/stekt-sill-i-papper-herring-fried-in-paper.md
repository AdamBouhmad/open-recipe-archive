## Ingredients

- 4-6 salt herrings
- 4 tbsp butter
- 3 sheets of paper

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water the day before, clean and rinse well
2. Remove the skin, fillet the herring and remove all bones
3. Wrap each herring fillet in a buttered sheet of paper and fry in the remaining butter until the paper is golden brown
