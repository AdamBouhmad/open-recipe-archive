## Ingredients

- 2 hg. wheat flour
- 2 hg. rye sift or coarse rye flour
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 75 gr. cold butter
- milk (approx. 2 dcl.)

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix with sugar and salt.
2. Distribute the butter in flakes in the flour and work it in with fingertips.
3. Add enough milk so that a reasonably firm dough forms, and work it until it becomes smooth and glossy and releases from the bowl.
4. Roll out the dough very thin, prick it lightly, and cut with a pastry wheel along a ruler into oblong rectangles.
5. Place on buttered baking sheets and bake light brown in a not too warm oven.
6. Serve as sandwich bread or bread for canapés.
