## Ingredients

- 1.5 to 2 kg veal brisket
- 1.5 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 g)
- 6 deciliters meat broth
- 2-3 egg yolks
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2-3 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 lemon in slices

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the meat as in the previous recipe, place on a warm plate and cover while the sauce cooks
2. Cook the sauce as in recipe no. 441 and pour over the meat
3. Garnish the dish with lemon slices and serve with rice
4. If desired, add 2 cooked carrots diced and 2 deciliters cooked peas to the sauce
5. The meat can also be cooked with 0.5 lemon in slices and the sauce finished with madeira instead of lemon juice; then omit the egg yolks
6. The sauce can, if desired, be made with curry instead of lemon and the rice sprinkled with a little dry curry
