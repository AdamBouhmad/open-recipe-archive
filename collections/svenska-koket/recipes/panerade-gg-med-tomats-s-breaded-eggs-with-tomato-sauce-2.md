## Ingredients

- 6 poached eggs
- 2 egg whites
- 2 cups crushed breadcrumbs
- 1/2 kilogram lard
- 1 sheet gray paper
- 4 large red tomatoes
- 4 deciliters stock
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 1 deciliter cream
- salt
- cayenne
- 6 slices of wheat bread
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)

## Directions

1. Dip the eggs in the egg whites lightly beaten with 1 tablespoon cold water, roll them in crushed breadcrumbs and let them lie for 1 hour in a cold place.
2. Rinse the tomatoes, clean them, cut them into pieces and cook them soft in the stock, then pass them through a sieve.
3. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the tomato purée and cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes, then season with the spices.
4. Fry the eggs golden brown in hot lard.
5. The bread slices, which should be 2 cm thick, cut into elongated pieces, hollow them out, fry them golden brown in the butter and place on a hot plate.
6. Place the eggs on the croutons and pour the tomato sauce around them.
