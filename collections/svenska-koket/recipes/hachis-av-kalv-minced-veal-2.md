## Ingredients

- 4—5 hg. boneless, roasted or cooked veal
- 1 red onion
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.)
- 3 msk. flour (30 gr.)
- 3—4 dcl. meat gravy or bouillon
- 2 msk. tomato puree
- a little paprika
- 2 msk. finely cut pickled gherkin
- 1 msk. Madeira
- 1 tsk. cognac
- salt, sugar, 1/2 msk. soy

## Directions

1. The meat is minced (not ground) finely.
2. The onion is peeled, chopped and browned in the butter together with the flour (see Sauces, p. 210), the meat gravy and tomato puree are thinned and the sauce is allowed to boil for 5 minutes.
3. The minced meat is added and the stew is seasoned with paprika, gherkin, wine, cognac, spices and soy.
4. The dish is heated.
