## Ingredients

- 300 gr. sweet almond
- 300 gr. butter
- 300 gr. caster sugar
- 300 gr. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Blanch the almond, grate it or pass it through an almond mill and pound it together with the sugar.
2. Wash the butter and stir it until it becomes white, and work the almond mass and flour into it.
3. Divide the dough into three parts, roll these out to round bottoms, which are baked on well-buttered baking sheets in quite good oven heat.
4. Let the cakes cool and place them on top of each other with marmalade or cream between each bottom.
5. Glaze the tart with water icing, garnish with fruit and candied fruit or with whipped cream.
