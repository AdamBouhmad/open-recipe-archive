## Ingredients

- 125 gr. sugar
- 1 hg. almonds
- 1 litre cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 150 gr. sugar

## Directions

1. Cook nougat from 125 gr. sugar and 1 hg. almond (see nougat recipe).
2. Pound 120 gr. of the nougat and pass it, then blend into the finished cream prepared for vanilla ice.
3. Use the remaining nougat for garnishing.
4. Freeze as in recipe n:o 929.
