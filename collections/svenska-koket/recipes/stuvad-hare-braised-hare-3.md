## Ingredients

- 1 skinned hare weighing 3 1/2 kg
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1 bottle red wine
- 1 bay leaf
- 6 allspice berries
- 1/4 tsp thyme
- 4 tbsp butter (80 gr)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr)
- 8 pickled onions, in wedges
- 10 mushrooms, in slices
- 8 pitted olives

## Directions

1. Treat the hare as in the previous recipe, cut into smaller pieces, which are seasoned with the spices and placed in a stoneware pot.
2. For the marinade, mix all the ingredients together, then pour it over the hare, which should rest in it until the next day.
3. Remove the meat, wrap it well in cloth and strain the marinade.
4. Brown the butter in a casserole, add the meat, brown it and sprinkle with flour. Add the marinade and let the meat simmer slowly with the lid tightly closed for about 1 hour, shaking the casserole often so the meat does not stick to the bottom.
5. Add the onions, mushrooms and olives cut into thin rings and let the stew simmer until everything is tender, about 1/2 hour.
6. Place the meat on a warm serving platter, whisk the sauce, taste and adjust seasoning, then pour it over. Garnish the dish with small triangular bread croutons and serve with boiled or fried potatoes.
