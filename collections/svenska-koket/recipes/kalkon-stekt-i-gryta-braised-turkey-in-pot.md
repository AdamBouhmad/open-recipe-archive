## Ingredients

- 1 turkey cock approximately 5 kg
- 1 tbsp salt (15 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 6 almond buns (2-öres mandelbröd)
- 2 tbsp butter (40 g)
- 3 dcl milk
- 1 1/2 hg lard, in 1 slice
- 4 tbsp butter for roasting (80 g)
- 1 1/2 liters broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey as in the previous recipe and rub it with the seasonings
2. Split the almond buns, brush them with butter, dip them in milk and stuff them inside the bird, which is then sewn shut with a needle and thread, truss it, and cover with the lard slice, which is tied fast with fine twine
3. Melt half of the butter in a roasting pot, place the turkey in it with breast up, add the giblets, add the broth and let it simmer slowly with the lid closed for 1 1/2-2 hours, or until the turkey is almost done, then drain off the broth and remove the lard
4. Melt the remaining butter and baste the bird with it
5. Add half of the broth, place the pot in a hot oven and let the turkey finish roasting while basting frequently, but let it only become light brown
6. Carve it as in the previous recipe, garnish with browned potatoes, small fine peas, stewed mushrooms, salad and parsley
7. Serve with prepared sauce with added cream (see recipe 429) and salads
