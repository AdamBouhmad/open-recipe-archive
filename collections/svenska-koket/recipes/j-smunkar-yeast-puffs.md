## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. sour milk
- 1 hg. melted butter
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 25 sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 25 gr. pressed yeast
- approximately 280 gr. flour
- 1 kkp applesauce or red currant jelly
- 1 kg. lard for frying

## Directions

1. The milk, which should be lukewarm, is mixed with the butter, the beaten eggs, the sugar, and the blanched and grated almonds.
2. Add the flour and the yeast stirred in 1 tsk. sugar, and work the dough until it becomes smooth and glossy, then let it stand in a warm place to rise for approximately 2 hours.
3. Work the dough on the baking board, roll it out to 1 cm. thickness, and cut it with a round measure into cakes about 6 cm. in diameter.
4. Place a little applesauce or red currant jelly in the middle of each cake; brush the edges with egg white and press them together well.
5. Place the puffs on a floured baking sheet and let them rise for approximately 1 1/2 hours.
6. Cook them in moderately hot lard until they are cooked through and a beautiful brown, place them on absorbent paper and let them drain.
7. Sprinkle with sugar and serve warm with coffee or tea.
