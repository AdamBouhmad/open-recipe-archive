## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 40 gr. sugar
- 1/2 liter cream milk
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 3 egg whites
- 3 dcl. sugar (255 gr.) for the mold
- 1/2 dcl. water for the mold

## Directions

1. Brylépudding is best if prepared the day before it is to be served.
2. Cook the sugar with water until it is dry, then stir until it becomes liquid again and has a beautiful light brown color.
3. Pour some of the caramel into a pie mold, which is turned around so that the caramel covers both the bottom and the sides.
4. Dilute the remaining caramel with water and serve as a sauce with the pudding.
5. Stir the egg yolks with sugar for 20 minutes.
6. Heat the cream milk (2 dcl. cream, 3 dcl. milk) with the vanilla pod for about 5 minutes and add it to the stirred egg yolks with vigorous whisking; continue whisking until the cream is cold, then remove the vanilla pod and fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
7. Pour the cream into the lined mold, and let the pudding cook in a water bath in ordinary oven heat for about 1 1/2 hours.
8. Let it stand in the mold until the following day, or until it is completely cold.
9. Unmold and serve with the remaining caramel sauce.
