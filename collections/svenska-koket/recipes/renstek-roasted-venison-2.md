## Ingredients

- 3 kg. from the hind leg
- 3 liters skim milk or drink
- 3 hectograms lard
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 1/2 liter broth
- 1/2 liter cream or good milk

## Directions

1. If the roast is frozen, it should lie in milk or drink until it is just thawed. (If it is unfrozen, it is washed very carefully with a cloth dipped in hot water and beaten with a damp meat mallet.) The roast is dried well and all membranes are cleaned away, after which it is larded (see page 232) and rubbed with the remaining salt and tied so that it gets a nice shape. The butter is browned in a roasting pan, the meat is placed in and browned well on all sides, after which it is diluted, first with the boiling broth, then with the boiling cream or milk, and is roasted with the lid tightly closed until tender, or approximately 3 1/2 hours, during which it is turned a few times and basted every 10 minutes. The roast is cut into thin slices, placed on a warm platter and poured over with a couple of tablespoons of sauce. Garnished with browned potatoes, salad or parsley and served with a prepared sauce (see no. 429), with thick cream added, and green salad and sour salads or red currant jelly.
