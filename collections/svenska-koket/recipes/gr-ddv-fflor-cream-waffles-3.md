## Ingredients

- 8 parts thick sour cream
- 2 to 3 parts water or snow
- 4 to 4.5 cups dry sifted flour

## Directions

1. Whisk the cream to stiff peaks, and sift in the flour.
2. Finally add the water (or even better snow).
3. Heat the waffle iron slowly, clean it well with white paper and brush with clarified butter.
4. Butter the iron only for the first waffles.
5. If the batter is too stiff, add more water or snow; if too thin, add more flour.
6. Place the waffles on a sieve, trim around the edges and place on a warm platter.
