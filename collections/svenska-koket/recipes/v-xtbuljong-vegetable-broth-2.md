## Ingredients

- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 celeriac
- 1/2 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 1/2 rutabaga
- 1 cauliflower head
- 1 leek
- 3 tablespoons fresh peas or 1/4 liter canned peas
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 3 liters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)

## Directions

1. Prepare the roots and vegetables in the usual way, cut into pieces and place together with salt and butter in cold water
2. Bring to a slow boil with a tight lid for 4-5 hours
3. Strain the broth, taste and adjust seasoning, and color if desired with a little Japanese soy sauce
4. Serve with rice or egg pasta, or use for julienne soup, refined broth or purée
