## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 2 parts water
- 110 gr. sugar
- 0.5 liter heavy cream
- 1 small can pineapple

## Directions

1. The egg yolks, water, and sugar are whisked over heat until the cream thickens, then the pot is removed from heat.
2. Whisking is continued until the mixture is cold.
3. The cream, whisked to stiff peaks, is then whisked in along with half of the pineapple, cut into small pieces, and the pineapple juice.
4. The mixture is frozen not too hard, placed on a gelatin mold, and garnished with the remaining pineapple cut into slices and small biscuits.
