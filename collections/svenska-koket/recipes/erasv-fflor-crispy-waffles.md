## Ingredients

- 1/2 lit. extra thick cream, sweet or sour
- approximately 175 gr. flour
- 40 gr. melted butter
- 1/2 msk. melted butter for the iron (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, sift the flour and whip it in, add water and let the batter rest in a cool place for approximately 1 hour to swell, then stir in the butter gently.
2. For the waffles to be crispy and beautiful, an even heat is required.
3. Heat the waffle iron gradually on both sides and grease with melted butter or white wax.
4. Pour one spoonful of batter onto it, turn the iron, taking care that the hotter side of the iron is turned upward during cooking.
5. Remove the waffle from the iron with a fork, trim with scissors, place on a bread rack in the oven so it does not soften.
6. Arrange on cake paper on a warm dish and serve with jam or compote or with coffee and tea.
