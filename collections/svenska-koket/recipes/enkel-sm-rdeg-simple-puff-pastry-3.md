## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. water
- 150 gr. butter

## Directions

1. This puff pastry is prepared in the same way as fine puff pastry, but need only be rolled out in two stages and thus need only rest four times.
2. It is used for simpler intermediate courses and simpler baked goods.
