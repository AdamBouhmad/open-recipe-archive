## Ingredients

- 6 parts buttermilk
- 2 coffee spoons granulated sugar
- 1 coarse sugar
- 6 sheets red gelatin
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Flavor the buttermilk with sugar and with grated zest and juice of lemon.
2. Rinse the gelatin in cold water and dissolve it in a little hot water, then stir it into the milk.
3. If the milk is so cold that the gelatin does not mix properly with it and the milk appears streaked, place it briefly under whisking over heat until it has an even color.
4. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed mold and set it out to stiffen.
5. Turn it out and serve.
