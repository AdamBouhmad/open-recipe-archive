## Ingredients

- 2 deciliters rice grains (2 hg.)
- 1 1/2 liters milk
- 3 hg. veal liver
- 2 hg. fresh pork
- 30 raisins (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons syrup
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt (23 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper (1/2 teaspoon marjoram)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rinse the grains, scald in hot water, add to the boiling milk and let simmer, stirring, until almost soft, then pour out and let cool.
2. Rinse the liver well, dry it, cut into pieces, grind 3 times through the meat grinder and pass through a hair sieve.
3. Grind the pork 2 times through the grinder.
4. Rinse the raisins in lukewarm water, scald in hot water, cut in half and pit them, then mix with the grains together with the liver, pork, syrup and spices. If desired, add a little marjoram.
5. Mix the mass well and fill into a buttered and bread-crumbed mold or frying pan and bake in regular oven heat for about 3/4 hour.
6. Serve with clarified butter and lingonberry jam.
