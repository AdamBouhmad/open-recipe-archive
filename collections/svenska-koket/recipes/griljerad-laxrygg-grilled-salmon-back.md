## Ingredients

- Approximately 1 1/2 kg. salted salmon back
- 1 liter milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 4 tablespoon crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoon butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon back (head can also be used) into pieces, rinse very well and let soak in milk overnight
2. Wrap them in a cloth, dip in the egg whisked with cream, turn in crushed rusks and fry beautifully brown in butter
3. If less fatty salmon is desired, place the pieces on absorbent paper as they become fried
4. Place on a warm platter, garnish with parsley, serve with currant sauce and potatoes or with stewed potatoes
