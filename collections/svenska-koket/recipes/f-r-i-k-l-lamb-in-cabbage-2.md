## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg lamb breast
- 1 head white cabbage
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 15 white peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 1/2 liter boiling water
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, chop it into even pieces, which are placed in 1 liter of boiling water, added with 1/2 tablespoon salt, and allowed to boil without a lid for about 10 minutes.
2. Remove the meat and pour cold water over it.
3. Free the cabbage head from the outer, green leaves and cut it into large wedges.
4. In an iron pot, place the meat and cabbage alternately with spices between each layer.
5. Add the water and then let the dish simmer slowly until the meat is tender, or about 2 hours, then place it on a warm platter and sprinkle with the chopped parsley.
6. Serve very hot.
7. If desired, the cabbage can be browned in 3 tablespoons butter and 1 tablespoon syrup before being cooked with the meat.
