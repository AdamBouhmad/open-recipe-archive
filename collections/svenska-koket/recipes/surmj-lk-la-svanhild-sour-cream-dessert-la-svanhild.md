## Ingredients

- 1 lit. sour milk
- 1/2 lit. thick sour cream
- 1-1 1/2 dcl. fine red wine

## Directions

1. Whip the sour milk and thick sour cream to stiff peaks
2. Season with 1-1 1/2 dcl. fine red wine
3. Serve immediately with sugar cookies or biscuits
