## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg rhubarb
- 125 grams crushed sugar
- 1/2 deciliter water
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- 5 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
- For the form: 1/2 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Peel the rhubarb, cut it into pieces and cook it quickly soft in the water together with the crushed sugar.
2. Mix the egg yolks with the cream, powdered sugar and lemon zest until the mixture is fluffy.
3. Beat the whites until stiff peaks form and fold in.
4. Pour half of the mixture into a buttered ovenproof form.
