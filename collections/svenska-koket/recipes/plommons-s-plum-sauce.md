## Ingredients

- 300 grams prunes
- 5 teacups water
- 1.5 teacups sugar

## Directions

1. The plums are rinsed and are then allowed to soak for 2-3 hours in the water in which they are to be cooked.
2. They are then brought rapidly to a boil with the sugar until the plums are completely disintegrated.
3. The pits are removed, and the plums together with their sauce are forced through a fine sieve.
4. The sauce is boiled up, tasted for seasoning and served.
