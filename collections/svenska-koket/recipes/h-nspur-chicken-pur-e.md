## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 3 liters veal broth or water
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons rice grains
- 1 tablespoon flour
- white pepper
- sugar
- 1 part cream
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pluck the chicken, singe it, clean it, rinse it well and dry it. Cook it in broth or, if lacking, in water with salt added. Scrape the meat off carefully, pass it through a meat grinder and pound it together with half of the butter.
2. Rinse the rice grains well, blanch them in water, cook them soft in a little broth and pound them well together with the chicken mixture. Add some broth so that the mixture can easily be passed through a sieve.
3. Strain and skim the broth. Fry the remaining butter with the flour, add the broth and purée, and let the soup cook for about 10 minutes, after which season it carefully.
4. Whisk the cream and egg yolks in a soup tureen and pour the purée into it while whisking vigorously.
