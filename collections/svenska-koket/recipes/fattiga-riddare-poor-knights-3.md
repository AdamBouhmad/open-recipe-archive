## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 8 dcl. milk
- approximately 60 gr. flour
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 18 slices wheat bread (not freshly baked)
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks with a steel whisk, dilute half of the milk into them, whisk in the flour, and add the remainder of the milk.
2. Let the batter stand for 2 hours to swell, after which the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks are folded in.
