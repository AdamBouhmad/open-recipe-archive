## Ingredients

- 4-5 eggs
- 1 hectogram boneless beef
- 1 hectogram boneless veal
- 1/2 hectogram lard
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 pickled gherkin, finely chopped
- 1/2 egg
- 1-1 1/2 cups cream milk
- 1 egg and 1 cup crushed rusks for breading
- 1/2 kg. lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Hard boil the eggs 5-6 minutes and shell them
2. Coat them thereafter with the well-worked and seasoned meat forcemeat, roll in crushed rusks, brush with egg and roll again in crushed rusks
3. Flatten the forcemeat slightly at both ends and cook them golden brown in lard
4. Cut them thereafter in half, across
5. Line the serving dish with salad or parsley, and place the small forcemeat halves on it
6. Fasten a parsley sprig at each one as a handle, so that the forcemeat half gets the appearance of a small basket
7. Forcemeat-stuffed eggs are best served with green salad and sharp sauce
