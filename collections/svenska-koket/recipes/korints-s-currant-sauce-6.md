## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons currants (50 g)
- 6 1/2 deciliters water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1 tablespoon sugar or syrup
- 1-2 tablespoons vinegar
- salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter (10 g)

## Directions

1. Clean the currants, rinse them very well in several tepid waters, place them in 6 1/2 deciliters cold water and let them cook until soft
2. Strain through a colander and use the currant cooking liquid for the sauce
3. Roux the butter and flour for 2 minutes
4. Add the currant liquid and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes
5. Add the currants, season the sauce with sugar or syrup, vinegar and salt and enrich with the beaten egg yolk if desired
6. Finally stir in the cold butter
