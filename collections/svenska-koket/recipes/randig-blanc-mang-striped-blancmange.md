## Ingredients

- sweet almond
- bitter almonds
- milk
- thick cream
- sugar
- orange zest and juice or orange essence
- gelatin
- warm water
- puree of strawberries or raspberries
- maraschino liqueur
- cocoa

## Directions

1. Prepare the cream as in the previous recipe and divide it into three equal parts.
2. To one part add a little puree or juice of strawberries or raspberries, pour into a nice metal mold, rinsed with cold water and dusted with caster sugar, and let it set on ice.
3. Add maraschino to the second part and pour into the mold when the pink cream is set.
4. Add cocoa to the third part and pour last into the mold.
5. Let the cream set on ice for 2-3 hours, unmold and garnish with biscuits.
