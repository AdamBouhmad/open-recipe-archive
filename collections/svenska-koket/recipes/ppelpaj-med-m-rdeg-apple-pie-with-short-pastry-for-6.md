## Ingredients

- For pastry: 200 gr. butter
- 2 eggs
- 200 gr. sugar
- Zest of 1/3 lemon
- 0.25 tsp. ammonium carbonate
- 150 gr. flour
- For filling: 2 cups apple sauce
- 1 tbsp. butter for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir until it becomes white.
2. Stir the eggs and sugar well together and then mix with the stirred butter.
3. Sift the wheat flour with the ammonium carbonate and work into the dough together with the finely grated lemon zest.
4. Grease a mold with butter and bread-crumb it.
5. Roll out the short pastry to about 0.5 cm. thickness, and with it line the bottom of the mold, then place in the oven to half-bake.
6. When it has cooled somewhat, line the edges of the mold with dough, place the apple sauce in, and place a dough lid on top.
7. Place it back in the oven to bake through, then turn out and serve.
