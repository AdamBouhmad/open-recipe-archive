## Ingredients

- 100 grams dried apricots
- 1 liter fresh apples
- 200 grams prunes
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1/4 liter water
- 3 egg whites
- 3-6 tablespoons granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots well and soak in water for about 1 hour
2. Cook a clear syrup from this water and sugar
3. Peel the apples, cut into 4 pieces, place directly in the syrup (to preserve color) and cook until clear
4. Remove with a slotted spoon, place the apricots in and let them cook until clear, then remove
5. Finally place the prunes in and let them cook soft
6. When the fruit is cold, arrange it in alternating layers, in pyramid form on a heat-resistant dish
7. Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form and gently fold in the sugar
8. Pipe the meringue batter through a fluted piping bag over the fruit and place the dish in a mild oven heat
9. When the meringue has dried and turned a light yellow color, remove it and serve cold with whipped cream
