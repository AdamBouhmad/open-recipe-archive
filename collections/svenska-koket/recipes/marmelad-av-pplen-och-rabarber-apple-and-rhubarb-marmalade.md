## Ingredients

- 1 kg apples, cut into pieces
- 1/2 litre water
- rhubarb, equal weight to the apple pulp
- sugar, equal weight to the mass

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the apples as in the previous recipe.
2. Weigh the strained pulp, and take rhubarb of equal weight.
3. Rinse the stalks, cut them into small pieces and mix with the pulp.
4. Weigh the mass and for each kg thereof take 625 grams of granulated sugar.
5. Cook the marmalade quickly with vigorous stirring to the bottom until bubbles form on the surface or it falls in flakes from the spoon.
6. Pour it out then and treat it as apple pulp (see previous recipe).
