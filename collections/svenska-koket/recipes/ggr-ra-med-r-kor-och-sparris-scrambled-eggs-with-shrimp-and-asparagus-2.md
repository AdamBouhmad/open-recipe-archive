## Ingredients

- 150 grams shrimp
- 12 tender asparagus tips or canned asparagus
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 6 eggs
- 4 decilitres boiling milk

## Directions

1. Let the shrimp soak in milk for about 1 hour, then clean them.
2. Blanch the asparagus a few minutes in lightly salted water, drain and fry well in half of the butter until the butter is clear.
3. Whisk the eggs well and add the boiling milk with whisking.
4. Melt the remaining butter in a pan, pour in the egg mixture and cook
