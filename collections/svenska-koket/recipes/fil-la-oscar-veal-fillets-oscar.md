## Ingredients

- 600 grams veal tenderloin or boneless veal loin
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 grams) for frying
- 2 balls crayfish butter (20 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1 deciliter asparagus puree
- 4 deciliters cream
- 1 can lobster (1/4 liter)
- 1 can asparagus (1/4 liter)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Dry the fillet with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into 1 centimeter thick slices, which are beaten on both sides and sprinkled with pepper and salt.
2. For the sauce, fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add asparagus puree and cream and let the sauce boil while stirring for 5 minutes.
3. Cut the lobster and asparagus into pieces (the finest pieces are set aside for garnish) and place them in the sauce, which is then brought to a boil and seasoned with the spices.
4. Heat a frying pan slowly, brown the butter in it, place the fillets in and fry quickly on both sides.
5. Place them on a warm serving dish and pour the finished sauce over the meat.
6. Garnish the dish with the lobster, asparagus and parsley and serve immediately with potatoes as the main course.
