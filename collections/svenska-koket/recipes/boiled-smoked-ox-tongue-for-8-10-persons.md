## Ingredients

- 1 cured, smoked ox tongue
- water
- 1 carrot

## Directions

1. The tongue, which should be lightly salted, is hot-smoked for 2 hours and is allowed to cool. It is then treated and coiled as in the previous recipe. When the broth has boiled up and been skimmed, the browned carrot is added. Smoked tongue is advantageously used for mixed meat dishes and for tongue in aspic, as well as for the sandwich table.
