## Ingredients

- 1 large rutabaga
- 3/4 liter beef broth or pork stock
- 1 liter potato
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 1 dcl. cream
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 2 tsp. sugar
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the carrots well, place in cold salted water and let cook almost soft, then the skin is immediately removed and the carrots are cut into fine strips.
