## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 kg unpeeled pears
- 1 1/2 kg granulated sugar
- 8 dcl water
- 2-3 pieces ginger

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, which should not be too large, scrape the stems, and cut a cross at the top of each pear.
2. Layer the pears and sugar alternately in a jam pot and add the water.
3. Crush the ginger slightly, tie it in a piece of muslin and add it.
4. Let the pears simmer slowly thereafter with a tight-fitting lid until they are completely clear, or approximately 4-5 hours.
5. Arrange them with a slotted spoon on a flat dish to cool and place them thereafter in jars rinsed with brandy.
6. Remove the ginger and cook the syrup down somewhat, let it cool and pour it over the pears.
