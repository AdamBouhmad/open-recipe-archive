## Ingredients

- 1 liter leftover porridge made from barley, oats or rice grains
- 1 1/2 deciliters wheat flour or crushed rusks
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cut the porridge into 2 centimeter thick slices, which are turned in flour or crushed rusks and fried slowly, beautifully golden brown in the butter.
2. Place the porridge on a warm dish and serve with milk.
