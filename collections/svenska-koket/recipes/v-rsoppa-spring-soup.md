## Ingredients

- 1 small bunch fresh carrots
- 1 small parsnip
- 1/4 celeriac
- 1/2 leek
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 3/4 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1 deciliter fine canned peas
- 1 can mushrooms (1/8 liter)
- 1 1/2 hectograms salted crayfish tails
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1 1/4 liters secondary broth
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 50 gr. spinach
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Scrape the vegetables, rinse them, dry them and cut them into fine cubes.
2. Cut the leek into strips and clean the cauliflower and divide it into florets.
3. Place the vegetables in boiling water with salt and sugar and let them cook for 5 minutes, then add the leek and cauliflower and cook everything until tender.
4. Remove the vegetables with a slotted spoon and place them in the soup bowl together with the peas, which have been warmed in a water bath.
5. Boil down the broth, without a lid, until it becomes about 1/2 liter.
6. Cut the mushrooms into slices and fry them in the butter together with the well-drained crayfish tails, then press them out of the butter with a spoon.
7. Fry the butter with the flour for 2 minutes, add the vegetable broth and the hot broth gradually, add the parsley and the cut-up spinach and let the soup cook for 5 minutes with stirring.
8. Beat the egg yolks and whisk them into the soup, season with the spices and pour it over the vegetables, mushrooms and crayfish tails in the soup bowl.
9. Serve very hot.
