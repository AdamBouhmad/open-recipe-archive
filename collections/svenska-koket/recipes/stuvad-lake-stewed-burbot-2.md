## Ingredients

- 1½ kilograms burbot
- 1½ tablespoons salt
- 1½ teaspoons vinegar
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 6 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 3 pickled onions
- 1-2 cups boiling water or milk
- ½ lemon
- French wine
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Skin the burbot, open it and gut it, rinse it well and dry it.
2. Cut off the fins, rub the fish with a mixture of the salt and vinegar and cut it into pieces.
3. Save the roe and liver, rinse them and cook them separately in a little salted water.
4. Butter a casserole with half of the butter, place the fish in it, and sprinkle flour between each layer.
5. On top place the rest of the butter, divided into small flakes, the pickled onions and the spices.
6. Last carefully add the water, and let the fish cook slowly for about 20 minutes.
7. Place it on a plate, taste the sauce with lemon juice and wine and finish it with an egg yolk and pour it over the fish.
8. Garnish the fish with the roe cut into slices, the liver and the lemon.
