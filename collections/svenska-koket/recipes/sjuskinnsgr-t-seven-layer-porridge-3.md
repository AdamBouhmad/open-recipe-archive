## Ingredients

- 1 liter leftover rice groat or barley groat porridge
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 2 tsp. ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the dish

## Directions

1. Grease a fireproof dish with the butter.
2. Spread a layer of porridge and sprinkle thickly with sugar mixed with cinnamon.
3. Heat a pancake turner until glowing and brown the sugar, then spread another layer of porridge, sprinkle with sugar and brown it.
4. Continue in this manner until there are 7 layers, which gives the dish its name.
5. Do not brown the top layer of sugar with the pancake turner; instead place the dish in a hot oven so that the sugar becomes nicely brown and the porridge is heated through.
6. Serve with milk.
