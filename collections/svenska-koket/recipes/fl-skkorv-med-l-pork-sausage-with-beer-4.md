## Ingredients

- 2.5 kg pork (preferably loin)
- 425 gr bacon
- 1⅓ liters water
- 2 parts beer
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon potassium nitrate
- 2 teaspoons ground white pepper
- 1 teaspoon ground sharp pepper
- 5 meters intestines (curved casing)
- 2 tablespoons salt for rubbing
- 1 tablespoon sanding sugar
- 1 teaspoon potassium nitrate for rubbing

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut into cubes
2. Grind the meat three times through a meat grinder
3. Cut the bacon into fine cubes and mix with the meat along with beer, water and finely ground spices
4. Work the mixture for 1 hour and then stuff into curved casing
5. Rinse the sausages with cold water and rub with a mixture of salt, sugar and saltpeter
6. Let rest until the following day, then place in brine
