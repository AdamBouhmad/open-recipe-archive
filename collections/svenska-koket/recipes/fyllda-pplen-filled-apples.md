## Ingredients

- 12 apples
- 1 part melted butter
- 1 1/2 cups finely crushed rusks
- 2 tablespoons caster sugar
- 100 g sweet almonds
- 100 g caster sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1 tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry and grate them (or pass them through an almond mill) and crush them together with the sugar in a mortar.
2. Beat the egg white until stiff peaks form and fold it in.
3. Peel the apples, remove the seed cavities and fill them with the almond mixture.
4. Dip them into melted butter, sugar and crushed rusks.
5. Butter a pie form with some of the butter, place the apples in it, sprinkle with sugar and cover with the remaining butter, divided into small flakes.
6. Place them in the oven and fry them until they are light brown and loose.
7. Serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
