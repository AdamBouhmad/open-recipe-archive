## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 slices wheat bread
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1-1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.)
- 3 msk. flour (30 gr.)
- 1 tsk. English mustard
- 5 dcl. milk
- salt
- sugar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs 5 minutes, dip in cold water and shell.
2. Cut the bread slices into oblong shapes, hollow them out and fry them nicely golden brown in the butter.
3. Arrange them on a hot plate and place one egg on each slice.
4. Fry the butter, flour and mustard together 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce cook 5 minutes.
5. Taste it with the seasonings and pour over the eggs.
6. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
