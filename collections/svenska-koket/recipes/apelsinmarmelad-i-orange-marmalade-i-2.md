## Ingredients

- 650 grams oranges
- 1 lemon
- 650 grams granulated sugar
- 1 liter water

## Directions

1. Wipe the oranges and lemon well with a damp cloth and peel them.
2. Carefully remove all the white from the peel.
3. Cut the peel into fine strips and the fruit flesh into thin slices, removing all pits.
4. Place everything in the cold water and allow it to stand for 24 hours.
5. Place it on the stove and allow it to cook for 1 hour.
6. Then add the sugar, and allow the marmalade to cook for a further 1 hour, or until it is clear and fairly thick.
7. During cooking skim it carefully.
8. When most of the heat has dissipated, pour it into warm glass jars.
