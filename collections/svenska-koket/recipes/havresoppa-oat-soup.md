## Ingredients

- 1 small cup regular oat groats
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 liter milk
- 18 prunes or 1 cup raisins
- sugar, salt
- 10 sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats, add to cold water and let them cook slowly with a tight-fitting lid until soft, about 2 hours
2. Rinse the prunes or raisins, add to cold water and let them cook slowly until soft
3. Pass the groats through a sieve
4. Bring the milk to a boil, pour in the passed soup and bring to a boil
5. Scald the almonds, cut into strips and add to the soup along with the fruit and its cooking liquid
6. Taste and serve
