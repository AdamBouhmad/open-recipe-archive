## Ingredients

- 1 fresh beef tongue
- water
- 3-4 tbsp salt
- For frying: 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 1/4 tsk white pepper
- 1/2 liter boiling bouillon
- 3 tbsp tomato puree
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue and soak it in water for a couple of hours.
2. Place it in boiling salted water and cook slowly until the skin can be easily pulled off.
3. Remove the skin and let the tongue cool.
4. Heat the butter, season the tongue with salt and pepper, and brown it on all sides in the butter.
5. Add the boiling bouillon and tomato puree, add the mushrooms, and let it simmer until the tongue is tender.
