## Ingredients

- 3 hg. boneless, cooked meat
- 1 hg. butter
- 2 tsp. finely chopped onion
- 1 hg. flour
- 5-6 dcl. boiling milk
- 5 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- For the mold: 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g.)
- 2 tbsp. crushed hardtack

## Directions

1. Scrape and chop the meat finely.
2. Sauté the butter with the onion and flour 2 minutes, add the boiling milk and let the sauce simmer while stirring 5 minutes; remove from heat and stir until cooled.
3. Then add the egg yolks, one at a time, and stir the mass 10 minutes, then mix in the meat and season the stew with spices.
4. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks, and pour the mass into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated pudding mold with a lid and let it simmer in a water bath on the stove for about 2 hours.
5. Turn out, garnish with slices of pickled cucumber or tomatoes and parsley.
6. Serve with brown gravy sauce (see p. 218) or with reduced sauce (see no. 429), mixed with finely chopped pickled onion, pickles or pickled cucumber.
