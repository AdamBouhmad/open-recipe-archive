## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless veal loin
- 1 hg. lard
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 6 dcl. dark broth
- 1 carrot
- 6 small pickled onions
- 12 small mushroom caps
- 2 olives
- 1 tbsp. tomato purée
- 1 tsp. beef steak sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat, lard it, and brown it as in the preceding recipe. When it is browned, add the boiling broth, and let the meat cook slowly with a tight lid until it is completely tender.
2. Scrape the carrot, cut it with a vegetable knife into beautiful, thin pieces, which are placed in the roasting pan when the meat has cooked 15 minutes, together with the pickled onion, mushrooms, the olives cut into strips, tomato purée and beef steak sauce.
3. Cut the meat into thin slices and place on a warm serving dish. Taste the meat gravy and pour it together with the garnish over the meat. Serve with small browned potatoes, peas and salads.
