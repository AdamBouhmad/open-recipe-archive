## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters mixed veal and beef broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 part cream
- 6 tablespoons preserved mushrooms with their liquid
- 0.5 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and flour together, add the broth with stirring, and let the soup simmer for 10 minutes, then season to taste.
2. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl, and add the soup while whisking vigorously.
3. Sauté the mushrooms in 0.5 tablespoon butter and add them with their liquid to the soup.
