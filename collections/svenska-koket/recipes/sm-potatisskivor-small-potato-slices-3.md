## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 1 kg fat/lard
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Treat the potato as in the previous recipe and cut into 2 mm thick slices, which are removed with a round cutter into small, even slices.
2. Let them lie in cold water for about 10 minutes.
3. Dry them well in a cloth, cook them in hot fat until they are crisp, place them on absorbent paper to drain and salt them.
4. Serve warm or cold with game.
