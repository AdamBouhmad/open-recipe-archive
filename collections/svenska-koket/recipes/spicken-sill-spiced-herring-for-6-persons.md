## Ingredients

- 4 small, salted fat herrings
- 1 teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Let the herring lie in water for 12 hours, changing the water once.
2. Fillet it, clean it, rinse it, dry it with a fish towel, rub it with pepper and let it lie for 1 hour.
3. Dry it again well, cut it into 1 1/2 cm wide pieces, across, arrange on a platter and garnish with parsley or dill.
4. Serve with fresh or stewed potatoes or stewed green peas as a breakfast or dinner course.
