## Ingredients

- 18 cutlets
- 1 tbsp salt
- 0.5 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter
- 0.5 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the cutlets, cut, trim, and beat once on each side. Pepper and salt them and brown in butter; add broth and let the cutlets simmer gently until done in 5-10 minutes.
2. The cutlets can also be cooked in aspic, but must then first be breaded.
