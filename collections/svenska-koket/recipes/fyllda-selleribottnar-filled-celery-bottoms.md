## Ingredients

- 2 large celeriac roots
- 2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 36 small Jerusalem artichokes
- 3 tablespoons melted butter (60 grams)
- 3-4 truffles

## Directions

1. Peel the celeriac, rinse and cut each into 6 slices.
2. Round off the edges so they are even and smooth, and hollow out slightly in the middle.
3. Place them in plenty of boiling, lightly salted water and boil until tender.
4. Remove with a slotted spoon and arrange in a ring on a round plate.
5. Place 3 small Jerusalem artichokes on each bottom, pour a little melted butter over, and finally sprinkle with finely chopped truffle.
6. The Jerusalem artichokes are prepared and cooked as in recipe 215.
