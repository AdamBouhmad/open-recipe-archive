## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon chopped shallot onion
- 1/2 gram white pepper
- 2 grams salt
- 4 tablespoons good vinegar
- 5 egg yolks
- 180 grams melted butter
- cayenne
- 20 grams chopped tarragon stems
- 3 tablespoons good meat stock or fish broth (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix the onion, pepper, salt, and vinegar in a sauce pan and cook together until 1/3 remains.
2. Move the pan to the side, add the beaten egg yolks, and let the sauce simmer under vigorous whisking.
3. Add the melted, slightly cooled butter gradually.
4. Strain the sauce through a fine cloth, season with cayenne, and finish with the chopped tarragon stems and, if desired, with meat stock or fish broth.
5. Warm in a water bath until lukewarm and serve immediately. If heated too much, it will break.
