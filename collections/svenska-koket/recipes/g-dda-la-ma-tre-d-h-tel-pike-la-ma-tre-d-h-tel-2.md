## Ingredients

- 3 pike at 425 gr. each
- 3 teaspoons salt (15 gr.)
- 3 1/2 tablespoons butter (70 gr.)
- 3 sheets white paper
- Maître d'hôtel butter: 1 small bunch parsley
- 100 gr. butter
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike as described on page 171; the head may remain but remove the gills
2. Rub the pike with salt and let rest 1 hour, then dry well
3. Cut the paper sheets lengthwise and butter them with the butter; wrap the fish well in them, place in a buttered long baking pan, and roast in a warm oven for approximately 20 minutes
4. Remove the paper only when the fish is placed on the serving platter, so that the formed sauce is not lost
5. Cover the fish thickly inside and outside with slices of maître d'hôtel butter, which is prepared as in recipe no. 406
6. Garnish with parsley and serve with mashed potatoes
