## Ingredients

- 2 msk. smör (butter)
- 5 msk. mjöl (flour)
- 4 dcl. tunn grädde (thin cream)
- 4 ägg (eggs)
- 2 msk. socker (sugar)
- skal och saft av 1/2 citron (zest and juice of 1/2 lemon)
- 2 dcl. socker för formen (sugar for the mold)
- 2 msk. kokande vatten (boiling water)

## Directions

1. Place the sugar for the mold in a buttered, slightly warm pan, brown it lightly, then add the boiling water to line an ovenproof mold.
2. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, dilute with the cream, and let the mixture boil for 5 minutes, then remove from heat and let cool.
3. Mix in the egg yolks one at a time along with the sugar, the finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice, and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
4. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the mixture into the lined mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
