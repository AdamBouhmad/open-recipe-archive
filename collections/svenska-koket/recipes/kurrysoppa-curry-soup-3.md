## Ingredients

- 1 small celery root
- 1 carrot
- 1 1/2 liters beef broth
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 2-3 egg yolks
- curry to taste
- salt
- cayenne
- 1 dcl. rice grains (1 hg.)
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 10 gr. salt

## Directions

1. The celery and carrot are prepared in the usual way, cut into fine strips and cooked soft in the broth, which is then strained.
2. The roots are placed in the soup bowl.
3. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes.
4. The broth is added and allowed to simmer with stirring for 5 minutes.
5. The egg yolks are beaten and whisked into the soup, which is then seasoned with curry powder, salt and cayenne pepper and poured over the vegetables in the soup bowl.
6. The rice grains are rinsed, placed in boiling salted water and allowed to cook until soft, approximately 15 minutes.
7. They are poured into a colander, rinsed with cold water and allowed to stand in the oven for a few minutes, during which they are stirred with a fork to become dry and warm.
8. Arranged on a carrot and served with the soup.
