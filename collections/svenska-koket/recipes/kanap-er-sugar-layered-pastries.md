## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. fine butter dough
- 1 1/2 dcl. granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as in recipe no. 33 and let it rest in a cool place 1-2 hours.
2. Roll it out into an oblong rectangle of 1/2 cm. thickness and sprinkle with sugar, then fold as with regular butter dough, but sprinkle sugar between each fold.
3. Fold 3 times in the same manner and sprinkle with sugar.
4. The last time the dough is rolled out, it should be approximately 4 dcm. wide and 8-10 cm. broad when the folding is finished, to make the kanapés the right size.
5. With a sharp knife, cut across the dough approximately 1/2 cm. wide strips, which are placed cut-side down on a well-cleaned baking sheet.
6. Bake immediately in a warm oven and turn once during baking so they become nicely golden-brown on both sides.
7. When cooled, place two together with jam between.
8. This recipe makes approximately 15 double pastries.
