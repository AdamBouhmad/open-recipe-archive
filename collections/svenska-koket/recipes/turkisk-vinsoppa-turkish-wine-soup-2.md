## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup rice grains
- ½ litre French wine
- 1½ litres water
- 1½ cup crushed sugar
- ½ lemon
- 1 cup raisins
- ½ cup currants

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains and cook them until soft in the water, mixed with 2 parts of the wine and the sugar.
2. Add the rest of the wine, the peel and juice of the lemon, and heat the soup, but do not let it boil.
3. Finally add the previously rinsed fruit that has been cooked in a short broth, and then taste the soup.
4. Serve with rusks or biscuits.
