## Ingredients

- 210 gr. flour
- 65 gr. sugar
- 210 gr. butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 10 gr. blanched and finely pounded pistachios (optional)
- 1 tbsp. cocoa (optional alternative)

## Directions

1. In high lumps on a well-buttered and floured baking sheet.
2. Bake in not too warm an oven until they are half-dry and have a light yellow color, then they are immediately loosened from the sheet.
3. Before the pastries are baked they can be sprinkled with 10 gr. blanched and finely pounded pistachios.
4. Instead of almond, 1 tbsp. cocoa can be added.
