## Ingredients

- 2 kg mutton roast (from the loin)
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon ginger
- 2 deciliters vinegar
- 1 deciliter water
- 1 deciliter white wine
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g)
- 1 small red onion, in slices

## Directions

1. The roast should have hung so that it is tender. The knuckle and the thin part are trimmed away and the roast is larded (see page 232) with strips of lard, dipped in a mixture of the salt, pepper and ginger, and placed in a deep dish.
2. For the marinade, blend all ingredients well, then pour it over the meat, which is then allowed to stand for 3-4 days, turning it 2 times daily.
3. The marinade is boiled with enough water so that it is level with the meat. The roast is placed in and allowed to simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until it is tender, or about 3 hours.
4. It is removed and allowed to cool, cut into thin slices and garnished with stuffed eggs (see no. 8) as well as green salad or parsley and served with mayonnaise.
