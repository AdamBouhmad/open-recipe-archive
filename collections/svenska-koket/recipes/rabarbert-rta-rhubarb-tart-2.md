## Ingredients

- 400 gr. butter
- 90 gr. sweet almond
- 400 gr. wheat flour
- 90 gr. caster sugar
- 1 kg. fresh rhubarb
- 200 gr. sugar
- 5 egg whites
- 8 tbsp. caster sugar
- 2 tbsp. vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Make a regular shortcrust pastry from the butter, flour, blanched and grated almond, and sugar, and set it out to stiffen.
2. Peel the rhubarb, cut it into pieces and cook together with the sugar until it becomes soft. Then remove it with a slotted spoon and let it drain.
3. Line the bottom of a ring mold and 30 cm. of the edge with the pastry, and bake this.
4. Spread the rhubarb on the tart, beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and then carefully mix with vanilla and caster sugar.
5. Garnish the tart with the meringue mixture and put it back in the oven to get a nice light golden color.
6. Serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
