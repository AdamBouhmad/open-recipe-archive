## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 200 grams granulated sugar
- 250 grams walnuts
- 70 grams potato flour
- 4 parts thick cream
- 1/2 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks and whites.
2. Beat the yolks rapidly together with the sugar for half an hour.
3. During beating, add 1 tablespoon water twice.
4. Shell the walnuts; grind half of them through an almond mill and stir into the batter along with the potato flour.
5. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-sprinkled pan or mold and bake in moderate oven heat.
7. When the cake is cold, divide it into two layers.
8. Whip the cream to stiff peaks and blend approximately half of it with 50 grams of finely chopped walnuts.
9. Fill the cake with this mixture.
10. Garnish with the remaining cream and walnuts.
