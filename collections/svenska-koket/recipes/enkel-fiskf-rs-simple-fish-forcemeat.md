## Ingredients

- 1.4 kg pike or coalfish (= 600 g scraped fish meat)
- 250 g butter
- 65 g flour
- 8 parts unskimmed milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare this fish forcemeat in the same way as the fine forcemeat, but the passing through a sieve can be omitted
