## Ingredients

- 1 cod of approximately 3/4 kg
- 1/2 tablespoon vinegar
- 2 1/2 tablespoons salt (43 g)

## Directions

1. Brush the cod with vinegar and scrape well from the tail upward
2. Clean it (see page 171), rinse very well and dry it
3. Reserve the roe and liver, cook them separately in salted water and use them for garnishing the fish
4. Cut off the head, from which the gills have been removed, together with the tail, since they easily fall apart during cooking; they can be cooked separately and used as garnish or grilled and fried
5. Make three slits on each side of the thickest part of the fish and rub the fish with salt
