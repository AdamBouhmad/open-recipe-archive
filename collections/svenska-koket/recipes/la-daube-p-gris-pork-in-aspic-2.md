## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram young pig or young pork shoulder
- 1/2 kilogram calf's feet
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 carrot
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 6 allspice berries
- 3 cloves
- 1 bay leaf
- 8 deciliters of broth
- 2–3 leaves gelatin
- 2 egg whites
- 1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 beet
- parsley

## Directions

1. Dry the calf's feet with a cloth wrung out in cold water, split them, and place them in cold water
2. Skim thoroughly when it comes to a boil
3. Dry the pork with a cloth wrung out in hot water and add it along with the peeled, cut carrot and spices
4. Skim again and simmer slowly until tender, about 1 hour
5. Remove the meat and when cooled, cut it into even pieces
6. Let the broth simmer with the calf's feet until it reduces to about 1 liter and let it cool; remove all fat carefully
7. Prepare the gelatin as in recipe no. 181
8. Garnish the bottom of the mold with hard-boiled egg and beet, cut into attractive figures, and parsley
9. Pour a few drops of gelatin over the garnish and let set so the garnish is not disturbed when the meat is added
10. Place the most attractive pieces at the bottom and on the sides; fill the mold with the remaining meat
11. Pour the remaining gelatin carefully over
12. Place the aspic in a cold place to set
13. Unmold as in the previous recipe and garnish with beets, egg, and parsley
14. Serve as a sandwich buffet dish
