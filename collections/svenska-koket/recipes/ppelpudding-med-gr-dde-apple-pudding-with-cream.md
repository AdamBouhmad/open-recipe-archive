## Ingredients

- 5 dcl. apple sauce
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 6 sheets gelatin
- 3 tbsp. boiling water for the gelatin

## Directions

1. The apple sauce should be strained, quite firm and moderately sweet.
2. Blanch the almonds, dry them well in the oven, pass them through an almond mill and mix with the apple sauce together with the cream beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Last, add the rinsed gelatin dissolved in boiling water, which should be cooled.
4. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled tin form and let it stand on ice for about 3 hours.
5. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it with a sharp knife around the edge of the form and turn it out onto a cake plate.
6. Garnish, if desired, with fruit and sponge cakes.
