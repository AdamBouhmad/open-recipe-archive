## Ingredients

- 1½ liters Jerusalem artichokes
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 3–4 cups Jerusalem artichoke cooking water
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Scrape the artichokes, rinse and place in vinegar-mixed water.
2. To better preserve their whiteness, blanch them for a few minutes in boiling and salted water with a little vinegar.
3. Drain this water and place the artichokes in fresh salted boiling water, enough so that it can later serve as liquid for the stew.
4. Let them cook slowly with the lid on until soft.
5. Make a roux of butter and flour, add the cooking liquid, and let the sauce simmer for 10 minutes.
6. Add the artichokes and season.
7. Root vegetables can be stewed the same way.
