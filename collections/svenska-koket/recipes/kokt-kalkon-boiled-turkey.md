## Ingredients

- 1 turkey hen of about 3 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 hectogram lard
- 1 1/2 liters broth or water

## Directions

1. Turkey hens or older turkeys that are not suitable for roasting can be boiled with advantage as poularde (see no. 600), but require longer cooking time.
2. Serve as chicken fricassée with rice or with sauce of mushrooms or oysters and boiled potatoes.
