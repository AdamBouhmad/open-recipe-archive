## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup rice grains
- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 2.5 liters milk
- 1 piece cinnamon
- salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains and pour into a colander.
2. Melt butter in a pot, pour in milk and bring to boil with cinnamon.
3. Add rice grains and let the gruel simmer slowly for about 2 hours.
4. Season and serve.
