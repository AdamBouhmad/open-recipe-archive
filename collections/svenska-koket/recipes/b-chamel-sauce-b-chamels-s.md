## Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped red onion
- 50 grams lean veal (in fine dice)
- 60 grams butter
- 40 grams dry flour
- 7 decilitres boiling milk
- salt
- white pepper
- a little grated mesost cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Fry the onion and meat in the butter until it becomes clear, then add the flour and fry with the butter for 2 minutes.
2. Add the milk, add the seasonings and let the sauce simmer under stirring for 15 minutes.
3. Strain it through a cloth, season to taste and heat in a water bath.
4. Serve with boiled chicken, veal or lamb, and can, if desired, be supplemented with a couple of tablespoons of chopped mushrooms, fried in butter.
5. Used for gratin and for royal on fish and poultry etc.
