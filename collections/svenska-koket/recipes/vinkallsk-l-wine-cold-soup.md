## Ingredients

- 1 bottle (6 parts) white or red French wine
- 1.5 liters water
- 200 grams sugar
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Mix wine, water, and sugar.
2. Slice the lemon and add it.
3. To cool the soup, ice may be added if desired.
4. Serve with rusks.
