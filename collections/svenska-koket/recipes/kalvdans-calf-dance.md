## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters raw milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 0.5 teaspoon salt
- 10 grated sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Whisk together milk, egg, sugar, salt, and almonds well.
2. Test a portion of the mixture in a pan over gentle heat.
3. If the test is too thin, dilute the mixture with cream milk; if too thick, whisk in one or two eggs.
4. Pour the tested mixture into a water-rinsed mold and bake in a water bath in the oven or on the stove.
5. In the latter case, the mold should be covered.
6. When the kalvdans is set and has turned a nice brown color, remove and serve with milk or fruit sauce.
