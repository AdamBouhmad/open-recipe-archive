## Ingredients

- 3 kg roast beef
- 1 tablespoon salt
- boiling water
- 2 Portuguese onions
- 3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. The roast beef should age 1-2 weeks depending on the season
2. Wash with a cloth dipped in hot water, pound lightly, and sprinkle with salt
3. Place in a hot roasting pan with a little boiling water beside it, then place in the oven
4. The oven should not be too hot at first, as some fat on the outside of the roast must melt before actual browning can begin
5. Gradually increase the heat in the oven, and baste the roast with its gravy, occasionally thinning with boiling water
6. Toward the end of roasting, cover the roast beef with butter or waxed paper to prevent burning
7. Allow 20-25 minutes per kilogram of meat
8. Peel the onions and cook them in butter
9. Arrange the roast beef on a warm platter and garnish with the onions
10. Strain the sauce, skim carefully, and serve as gravy
