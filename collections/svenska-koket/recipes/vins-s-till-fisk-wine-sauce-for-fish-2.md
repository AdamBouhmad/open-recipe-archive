## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 5 dcl. good fish stock
- 1-2 egg yolks
- salt, white pepper
- 1/2 dcl. white wine

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes
2. Add the fish stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
3. Whisk in the egg yolks and let the sauce simmer
4. Season with the spices and serve very hot
