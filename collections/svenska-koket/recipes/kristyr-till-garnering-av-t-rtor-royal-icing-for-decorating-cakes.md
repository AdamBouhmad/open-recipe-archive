## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- 120-150 gr finely sifted sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Whip the egg white, gradually stir in the sugar, and work the royal icing well until it is smooth and shiny and so stiff that if you squeeze it out with a spoon, the lines do not run out.
2. Put the icing in a paper piping bag, cut to points or with a round hole, and squeeze it out over the cake in the pattern you desire.
