## Ingredients

- 1 twelve-öre wheat bread
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into cubes.
2. Heat the butter in a frying pan, add the bread, and shake the pan over the fire until the bread has taken on a nice golden brown color.
3. Serve with purées and finished soups.
