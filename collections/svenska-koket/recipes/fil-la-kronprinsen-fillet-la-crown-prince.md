## Ingredients

- 500 grams beef fillet
- 3 slices salted beef tongue
- 100 grams calf sweetbread
- salt and white pepper for the sweetbread
- 1 egg white
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs
- 3 1/2 tablespoons butter (70 grams)
- 100 milliliters rice grains
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 teaspoon French mustard
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 2 1/2 tablespoons flour (25 grams)
- 400 milliliters strong meat stock
- salt and white pepper
- 1-2 truffles
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, add to boiling salted water and let cook until soft. Pour into a colander, rinse with cold water, let drain and mix well with the mustard.
2. Butter small brown, fireproof molds approximately 1 centimeter in cross-section, in each one place a bottom layer of rice. The bottom should be 1 centimeter high and 1 centimeter smaller than the mold.
3. On top of the rice bottom place a half slice of tongue, on top of the tongue a slice of grilled, fried calf sweetbread and on top a slice of fried or roasted beef fillet.
4. Place the molds in a warm oven 2-4 minutes to heat them.
5. Pour brown sauce flavored with chopped truffle over everything, and serve the dish very hot as a breakfast dish.
