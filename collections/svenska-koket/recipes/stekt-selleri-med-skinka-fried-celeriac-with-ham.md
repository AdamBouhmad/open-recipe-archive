## Ingredients

- 2 celeriac roots
- 1 1/2 lit water
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp water
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 150 gr smoked ham
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)

## Directions

1. Peel the celeriac thinly, rinse well, add to boiling salted water and let cook covered until half-soft
2. Let cool, cut into 1/2 cm thick slices, dip in the egg whisked with water, and turn in the crushed rusks
3. Fry golden brown in part of the butter and arrange in a ring on a warm serving dish
4. Cut the ham into strips, brown in the remaining butter and sprinkle over the celeriac
5. Garnish the dish with small browned potatoes and green salad and serve as a breakfast dish
