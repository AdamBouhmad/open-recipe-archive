## Ingredients

- 1 kg apples, cut into pieces
- 1/2 liter water
- Rhubarb of equal weight to the apple sauce
- Sugar of equal weight to the mixture

## Directions

1. The apples are treated and cooked as in the previous recipe.
2. The passed sauce is weighed, and raw rhubarb of equal weight is taken.
3. The stems are washed, cut into small pieces and mixed with the sauce.
4. The mixture is weighed and for each kg of it, 625 gr granulated sugar is taken.
5. The marmalade is cooked quickly with vigorous stirring toward the bottom until bubbles form on the surface or it falls in flakes from the spoon.
6. It is then poured and treated as apple sauce (see previous recipe).
