## Ingredients

- 1½ kg halibut
- 1½ teaspoons vinegar
- 4 tablespoons salt
- 2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 3-4 cups boiling water
- juice of ½ lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, rinse it, and scald the dark side with hot water and scrape it.
2. Rub it with vinegar and let it lie in water with the salt for 1 hour.
3. Remove the fish and dry it with a cloth, score it on one side and place it in a baking pan greased over as large an area as the fish occupies.
4. Divide the remaining butter into small flakes and place on top of the fish, sprinkle with crushed rusks and put in a fairly hot oven.
5. When brown, add boiling water and baste every ten minutes.
6. Roasting takes ½ to ¾ hour.
7. When the fish is roasted, place it on a platter.
8. Strain the sauce and add the lemon juice and taste.
9. Garnish the fish with parsley and lemon sliced into rounds.
10. Serve the sauce in a separate sauce bowl.
