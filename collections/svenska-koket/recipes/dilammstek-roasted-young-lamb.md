## Ingredients

- 1.5-2 kg young lamb roast
- 0.5 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 0.25 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 0.5 liter meat stock or broth

## Directions

1. Young lamb (suckling lamb) has white, very fine-grained meat and requires a short cooking time.
2. Use the loins and half of the kidney roast, which are connected together, to achieve sufficient weight.
3. Treat, roast and serve as in recipe 531 but should not be larded.
4. The saddle is prepared as lamb saddle (see recipe 535).
