## Ingredients

- 250 gr. prunes or sour plums
- 1 kkp crushed sugar
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 2 lit. water
- 25 gr. potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the plums very well in lukewarm water and let them drain.
2. Pound half of them in a mortar so that the pits are crushed.
3. Set the pounded and whole plums on to boil in cold water and let them cook until they are completely soft, then strain them.
4. Wash the lemon well, dry it, and rub the peel against the sugar.
5. Bring the strained soup to boil together with the sugar and thicken it with the potato flour, stirred in 1/2 kkp cold water.
6. Let it cook 2 minutes thereafter, season it, and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and small rusks or biscuits.
