## Ingredients

- Simpler or finer shortcrust pastry (see recipe above)
- 2 liters apples or 1 kg rhubarb stalks
- 250 gr. sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 4 egg whites
- 140 gr. powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Spread the shortcrust pastry into a bottom in a buttered ovenproof dish or ring form, about 3 cm higher around the edges
2. Bake the cake bottom, let it cool, and fill with apple or rhubarb compote
3. Prepare the meringue and pipe through a pastry bag in beautiful shapes over the fruit
4. Place the cake in the oven so the meringue gets a beautiful light yellow color
5. Serve cooled with vanilla sauce or whipped cream
