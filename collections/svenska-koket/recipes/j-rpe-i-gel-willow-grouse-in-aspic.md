## Ingredients

- 3 willow grouse
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 0.5 liter broth or water
- 1 kg marrowbone
- 1 kg beef neck
- 1 calf's foot
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2.5 liters water
- 2 eggs
- 10 leaves gelatin
- mayonnaise sauce
- poached eggs
- cooked beets or oysters

## Directions

1. Roast the grouse whole and loosely.
2. Cook broth from the beef and calf's foot, which when ready should make 1.5 liters.
3. Strain and skim the broth well and return it to heat.
4. Add the gelatin dissolved in a little water, and when the broth is warm, whisk in the eggs and let the broth come to a good boil while whisking, then strain through a cloth wrung out in hot water.
5. Pour half of the clear aspic onto a flat dish to set.
6. From the roasted, cold grouse, cut 4-5 slices on each side of the breastbone.
7. Place these on the set aspic, pour the remaining aspic over, and set everything aside to set.
8. Separate the meat slices with a knife from the set aspic.
