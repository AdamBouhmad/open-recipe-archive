## Ingredients

- 1 medium stand of kale
- 6-8 msk. flour
- 2 lit. bone broth
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the kale, rinse it and blanch it in salted boiling water for approximately 1/2 hour.
2. Drain the water, rinse the kale with cold water, and lay it on a sieve to drain.
3. Chop it finely together with the flour and put it in a pan with a little of the broth.
4. Dilute the rest of the broth while stirring frequently.
5. Let the kale cook 1/2 hour and season it thereafter with salt and white pepper.
6. Serve it with meatballs, sausage or poached eggs.
