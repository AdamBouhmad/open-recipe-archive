## Ingredients

- 4 liters raspberries
- 8 deciliters water
- 750 grams sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the berries, crush them and combine with the cold water in a preserving pot
2. Bring to a rapid boil while stirring and cook for approximately 15 minutes
3. Strain through a sieve cloth and let drain until the following day
4. Weigh the juice, add the sugar, and when dissolved, cook vigorously for approximately 10 minutes, skimming carefully
5. Bottle when cold
