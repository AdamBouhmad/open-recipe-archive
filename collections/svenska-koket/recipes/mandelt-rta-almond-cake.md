## Ingredients

- 150 gr. sweet almonds
- 8 bitter almonds
- 75 gr. potato flour
- 300 gr. fine sugar
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon water
- zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 15 gr. butter for the pan
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them and grind them. Dry the potato flour in a warm oven and let it cool. Sift the sugar and stir it with the egg yolks and water for about 1 hour, then add the almonds, the finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice as well as the flour. Last, carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks (see page 463), and fill the batter into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn baking pan or cake form, and bake the cake in a not too hot oven for about 40 minutes. Turn it out and let it cool, then cut it in half, between which vanilla or lemon cream or some fruit puree is placed. The cake is glazed (see no. 961), pricked with crystalized sugar and garnished with fruit and jelly.
