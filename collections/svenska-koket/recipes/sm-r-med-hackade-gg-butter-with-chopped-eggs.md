## Ingredients

- 180 gr. fine butter
- 2 gr. table salt
- 2 hard-boiled, chopped eggs

## Directions

1. Melt the butter as in recipe 407.
2. Season with a little salt, if desired.
3. Pour over the chopped eggs in the sauce bowl.
4. Serve with boiled fish.
