## Ingredients

- 2 large celeriac roots
- 2 liters water
- 15 gr salt
- 36 small Jerusalem artichokes
- 3 tbsp melted butter (60 gr)
- 3-4 truffles

## Directions

1. Peel and rinse the celeriac, then cut each into 6 slices
2. Even the edges of the slices so they become round and of equal size, and hollow out slightly in the middle
3. Place in abundant boiling lightly salted water and cook until tender
4. Remove with a slotted spoon and arrange in a ring on a round dish
5. Place 3 small Jerusalem artichokes on each bottom, pour a little melted butter over, and finally sprinkle with finely chopped truffle
6. Prepare and cook the Jerusalem artichokes as in recipe 215
