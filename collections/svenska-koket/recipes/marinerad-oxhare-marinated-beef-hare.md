## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef hare
- 1 hg fat
- For the marinade: 2 dcl white or red wine
- 1 dcl vinegar
- 1 dcl water
- 2 tbsp cooking oil
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 5 cloves
- 1 tsp coarsely crushed ginger
- 1 tbsp coarsely crushed juniper berries
- 1 red onion in slices
- 1–2 bay leaves
- For braising: 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 tsp sugar (5 g)
- 1 cleaned, boned anchovy
- 4 dcl cream

## Directions

1. The beef hare, which should have hung 4–8 days depending on the season, is prepared and larded (see page 232), and placed in a crock.
2. For the marinade all ingredients are mixed well, then poured over the meat, which is left to marinate 2–3 days and should be turned often during this time.
3. The meat is taken out, dried well, tied so it takes on a beautiful shape, and braised as in recipe 474.
4. It is moistened first with 2 dcl of the strained marinade, sprinkled with sugar, covered with the anchovy and thereafter the cream is added gradually.
5. Served with browned potatoes and reduced sauce (see recipe 429), mixed with 1 tbsp chopped capers or 2 tbsp currant jelly.
