## Ingredients

- 2 litres large, beautiful sugar snap peas
- 1/2 litre water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the peas, rinse well and let drain on cloth.
2. Place them in boiling water with salt and sugar, and let them cook slowly until completely tender.
3. Drain the cooking liquid and carefully mix the peas with the cold butter and parsley.
4. Serve with ham and roasted meat.
5. The pea pods can also be tied in bundles, cooked as above but in somewhat more water, placed on napkin on a warm dish and served with softened butter as a side dish.
6. Sugar snap peas can also be braised like sugar peas.
