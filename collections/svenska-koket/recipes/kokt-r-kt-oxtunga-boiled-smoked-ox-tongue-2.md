## Ingredients

- 1 salted smoked ox tongue
- water
- 1 carrot

## Directions

1. The tongue, which should be lightly salted, is hot smoked for 2 hours and allowed to cool
2. Treat and cook as in the previous recipe
3. When the broth has come to a boil and been skimmed, add the prepared carrot
