## Ingredients

- 40 gr. tapioca
- 1½ cups water
- 2½ liters clear consommé
- salt
- cayenne
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped chives
- prepared pancakes

## Directions

1. Let the tapioca grains cook in the water until they are clear, then strain through muslin and pour the liquid into the consommé
2. Let the consommé come to a boil and season with salt and cayenne
3. Cut out the prepared pancakes (see recipe 320) with a round measure into cakes (approximately 2 cm in diameter)
4. Place the pancakes and chives in the soup bowl and pour the hot consommé over
