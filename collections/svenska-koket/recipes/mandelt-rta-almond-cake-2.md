## Ingredients

- 150 gr. sweet almond
- 8 bitter almonds
- 75 gr. potato flour
- 3 hg. caster sugar
- 6 eggs
- 1 tbsp. water
- zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 15 gr. butter (for pan)
- 3 tbsp. crushed rusks (for pan)

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them and grind them. Dry the potato flour in a warm oven and let it cool.
2. Sift the sugar and stir with the egg yolks and water for about 1 hour, then add the almonds, the finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice, and the flour.
3. Last, carefully fold in the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks (see page 463), and fill the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated baking pan or cake form, and bake the tart in a not too hot oven for about 40 minutes.
4. Turn it up and let it cool, then split it into 2 bases, between which is placed vanilla or lemon cream or some fruit compote.
5. Glaze the tart (see recipe 961), prick with crystallized fruit and garnish with fruits and jelly.
