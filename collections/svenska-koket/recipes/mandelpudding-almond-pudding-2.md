## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 3.5 deciliters cream
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 150 grams sweet almonds
- 7-8 bitter almonds
- grated zest of half a lemon
- 1 small tablespoon potato flour
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar for the mold
- 2 tablespoons boiling water for the mold

## Directions

1. Caramelize the sugar in a pan, dilute with the boiling water, and use this to glaze a metal mold.
2. Whisk the egg yolks, cream, and sugar in a saucepan over heat until the mixture begins to simmer and thicken.
3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and continue whisking until the mixture cools.
4. Stir in the blanched and grated almonds, lemon zest, and potato flour; fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the mixture into the glazed mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for half an hour.
6. When the pudding has cooled, turn it out.
