## Ingredients

- 2 large or 3 small salted fatty herrings
- 3/4 liter cooked cold potatoes
- 1 medium Portuguese onion
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 grams)
- 3 deciliters thick cream

## Directions

1. Remove the herrings, rinse them well, and let them soak in plenty of water for 12 hours, changing the water 2 times.
2. Remove the skin and split the herrings; remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets well, wrap them in a fish towel, and cut them into small cubes.
4. Peel the potatoes and cut into similarly sized cubes.
5. Peel the onion, chop it finely, and fry it well in the butter.
6. Add the herring and potatoes, and brown the mixture lightly with careful stirring.
7. Finally stir in the cream and arrange on a warm platter.
8. Serve as a breakfast dish.
