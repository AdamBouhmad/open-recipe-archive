## Ingredients

- 165 gr. icing sugar
- 3 large egg whites
- 75 gr. sugared block chocolate
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 6 dcl. extra thick cream
- 1 tbsp. sugar
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- carmine

## Directions

1. Prepare the meringue mixture as in the previous recipe and shape into small rings or regular meringues on a well-cleaned and buttered sheet.
2. Dust lightly with sugar and bake in gentle oven heat until they are almost completely dry and release from the sheet.
3. Dissolve the chocolate in a water bath without adding water.
4. Blanch the almonds, cut them into thin slices, and lightly roast them with 2 tsp. sugar in the oven.
5. When the meringues are cold, layer them on a flat glass dish with parchment paper, alternating with the melted chocolate so that they form a pyramid.
6. Pour chocolate over the pyramid and sprinkle with the roasted almonds.
7. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks.
8. Flavor half of it with vanilla sugar; color the other half pink with carmine.
9. Pipe the white and pink cream separately through a fluted piping bag in beautiful garlands over the pyramid.
10. The cake should not be decorated until 1/2 hour before it is to be served.
