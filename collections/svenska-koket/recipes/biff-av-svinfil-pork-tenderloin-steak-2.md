## Ingredients

- 6 hectograms pork tenderloin
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 2 deciliters bouillon
- 1 large Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- parsley

## Directions

1. The fillets are trimmed, dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water. They are then cut across into 1 cm. thick slices, which are beaten lightly on both sides and seasoned. A frying pan is heated slowly, the butter is browned in it, the steaks are placed in and fried quickly.
