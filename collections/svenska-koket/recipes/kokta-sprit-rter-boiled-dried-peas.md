## Ingredients

- 1 liter dried peas (equivalent to 4 liters fresh peas)
- water
- salt
- 1 bunch parsley
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the peas and place in boiling, salted water (short broth) together with the cleaned and rinsed parsley.
2. Let boil until completely tender.
3. Remove the parsley bundle and pour off the water.
4. Pour the peas onto the serving dish and mix with the cold butter.
