## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup rice porridge
- 1 liter skim milk
- 2 cups applesauce
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1/2 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice porridge and place it in boiling milk, allowing it to cook until soft.
2. Grease a pie dish with butter; place half of the rice porridge in the dish.
3. Spread the applesauce over the porridge and cover with the remaining porridge.
4. Beat together the eggs and milk for the custard; pour this mixture over the porridge in the dish.
5. Place in moderate oven heat for half an hour.
6. Serve cold.
