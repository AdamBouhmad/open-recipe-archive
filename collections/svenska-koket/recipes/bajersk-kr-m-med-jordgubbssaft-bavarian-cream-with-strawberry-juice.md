## Ingredients

- 6 sheets gelatin
- 1/2 dcl. boiling water
- 50 gr. caster sugar
- 4 dcl. strawberry juice
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in 1/2 dcl. boiling water together with the sugar, then add the strawberry juice.
2. When the mixture is completely cold and begins to set, fold in the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Rinse a nice tin mold with cold water and sprinkle with caster sugar, pour in the mixture, and let the mold stand on ice for 3-4 hours.
4. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish with cake paper and garnish with preserved strawberries, sponge cakes or macaroons.
5. Instead of strawberry juice, raspberry, gooseberry or cherry juice may be used.
