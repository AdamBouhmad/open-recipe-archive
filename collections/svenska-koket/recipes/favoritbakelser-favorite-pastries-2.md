## Ingredients

- 210 gr. flour
- 65 gr. sugar
- 210 gr. butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 100 gr. sweet almonds
- 100 gr. fine sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1 deciliter jam
- 50 gr. fine sugar
- 1 teaspoon water
- 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice for glaze

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as simple shortcrust pastry and let it rest in a cold place for about 2 hours.
2. Blanch the almonds, dry them, grind them, and pound them with the sugar and a little of the egg white until fine and smooth, then carefully fold in the remaining egg white, beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Roll out the dough to 4 mm thickness and cut into round discs (6 cm in diameter) and place on a greased baking sheet.
4. Pipe the almond mixture onto each cake in the form of a ring (3 cm in diameter) and place a little jam or red currant jelly in the center of each ring.
5. Bake in a not-too-hot oven, and when cold, glaze the jam if desired with water glaze.
