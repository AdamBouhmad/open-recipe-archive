## Ingredients

- 1 veal loin
- 1 tablespoon butter
- hot broth or water
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Grease a small long pan with 1 tablespoon of the butter and place the loin in it.
2. Clarify the remaining butter and pour it hot over the loin.
3. Place the roast in the oven, brown it, dilute with the hot broth or water and baste with its gravy every tenth minute.
4. The roasting takes approximately 1.5 hours.
5. Strain and skim the meat gravy, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
