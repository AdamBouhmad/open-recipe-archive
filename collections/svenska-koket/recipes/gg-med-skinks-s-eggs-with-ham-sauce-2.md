## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.)
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)
- 1-1/2 dcl. cream
- the hard-boiled egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 msk. clarified butter (20 gr.)
- 1/2 hg. lean, smoked ham
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.)
- 3 msk. flour (30 gr.)
- 1 dcl. broth
- 3 dcl. milk
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Shell the eggs and cut them in half lengthwise; loosen the yolk carefully and cut it into fine cubes.
2. Fry the butter and flour together 2 minutes; add the thin cream and let the sauce cook 5 minutes, after which the finely chopped egg yolk is carefully mixed in and the stew is seasoned with the seasonings.
3. Fill the egg halves with the stew and pour the clarified butter over.
