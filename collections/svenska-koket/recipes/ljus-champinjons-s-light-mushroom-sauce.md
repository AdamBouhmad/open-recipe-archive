## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 4 parts cream
- 1 can mushrooms of 1/4 liter
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are cooked together
2. Cream and mushroom liquid are added
3. The sauce is allowed to boil for 10 minutes
4. The mushrooms are cut in half and added
5. The sauce is seasoned with the spices
6. Finally it is finished with the egg yolk, after which it must not boil further
