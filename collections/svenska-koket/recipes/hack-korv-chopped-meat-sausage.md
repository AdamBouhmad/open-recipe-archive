## Ingredients

- 1 heart of young beef
- 1 beef leg
- 1 kg beef (chuck)
- ¼ beef liver
- water
- 1 kg barley groats
- 4 medium red onions (or 2 tablespoons marjoram)
- 10 tablespoons salt
- 1⅓ tablespoons ground allspice pepper
- 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
- 6-7 meters curved intestine

## Directions

1. Soak the heart and liver in water the evening before
2. Wash the beef leg and chuck with a cloth dipped in cold water and place on heat together with liver and heart in enough cold water to cover the meat
3. When the broth boils, skim it thoroughly and let the meat cook until tender
4. Remove the meat, discard gristle, sinews and bone, cut into pieces and grind once through a meat grinder
5. Reduce the meat broth and cook the barley groats in it until soft
6. Mix this porridge with the meat
7. Add finely chopped and lightly browned onion in butter and spices
8. Work the mixture for about 1 hour
9. Stuff into well-cleaned curved intestines to make small, short sausages
10. Blanch the sausages for a couple of minutes in boiling water and then hang to dry, or smoke them very gently
