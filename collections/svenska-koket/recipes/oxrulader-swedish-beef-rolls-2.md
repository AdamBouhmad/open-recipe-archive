## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless inner round
- 1 hg. pork fatback, in 12 slices
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)
- 6 dcl. boiling broth or water

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until tender, is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into 12 slices, which are well tenderized and laid with the fatback slices.
2. These are sprinkled with a mixture of salt and pepper and the meat slices are rolled up tightly and tied in two places with fine sail twine, dipped in boiling water, or fastened together with toothpicks.
3. The rolls are well browned in the butter and sprinkled with flour, the boiling broth or water, mixed with a little meat extract or soya, is poured on, after which they are left to slowly braise with a tight lid, 2 1/2-3 hours.
4. During this time the pot is shaken often so that the rolls do not burn.
5. They are placed on a warm carrot, the strings are cut off and the sauce is whisked well, thinned if needed, tasted and poured over the rolls.
6. Served with boiled or mashed potatoes.
