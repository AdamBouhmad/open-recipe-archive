## Ingredients

- Orange peels
- 8 hektograms sugar per kilogram of peel
- 2 deciliters water per kilogram of peel
- 2 grams citric acid per kilogram of peel

## Directions

1. Let the orange peels lie in water for 4 days, changing the water daily.
2. Place them in boiling water and cook slowly until completely soft, then lay on cloth to drain.
3. Weigh the peels.
4. Cook sugar, water, and citric acid together to a clear, syrupy syrup.
5. Add the peels and cook slowly for approximately 20 minutes.
6. Place them in porcelain jars and cook the syrup until syrupy, then pour hot over them.
7. Bind the jar closed when the peels are cold.
