## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 1 ball crayfish butter (10 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 3 dcl. fish stock
- 3 dcl. cream
- 1 can lobster (1/4 liter)
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1-2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as in the previous recipe
2. Cut the lobster into small pieces; once it is added, stir the sauce very carefully so that the lobster does not fall apart
3. If using fresh lobster, allow one lobster for six persons
4. Make lobster butter from the shell and cook the sauce using the lobster roe
