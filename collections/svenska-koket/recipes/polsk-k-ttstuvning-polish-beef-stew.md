## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg boneless beef (round or flank)
- 2 cups crushed rusks
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped kidney fat
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 large carrots
- 1 onion and 1/2 Portuguese onion
- 3-4 cups broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into two large slices, approximately 1 cm thick.
2. Mix the crushed rusks, kidney fat, parsley, and seasonings with the beaten eggs and work into a smooth paste.
3. Divide the mixture in half and place on each meat slice.
4. Roll them up tightly and bind well with string.
5. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, brown the rolls, and add the boiling broth or water.
6. Scrub, scrape, and cut the carrots into thick slices. Peel the onions and cut into wedges, then add to the meat.
7. Let the stew simmer slowly with a tightly fitting lid for approximately 1 1/2 - 2 hours.
8. Remove the rolls, take off the string, and cut the meat into nice slices. Place on the center of a plate and garnish with the carrots and onion.
9. Strain and skim the sauce, then pour over the meat.
10. If desired, peeled potatoes can be added to the stew 1/2 hour before serving is complete, but more liquid must then be used.
