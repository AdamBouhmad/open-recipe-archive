## Ingredients

- 1 hectogram rice flour
- 1 liter milk or water
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 6 sweet almonds, 4 bitter almonds
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Stir the flour with 3 deciliters of the milk and let it stand 1 hour to swell.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, bring the rest of the milk to boil, pour 1 cup of it into the flour mixture, which is added to the boiling milk with vigorous whisking.
3. Let the porridge thereafter cook with stirring for about 10 minutes.
4. Thin with boiling milk if needed.
5. Season the porridge with the scalded and ground almonds, salt and sugar.
6. Serve with milk or fruit sauce.
