## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 small salt herring
- 1/2 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 3 dcl. boiling water
- 3 spice cookies
- 2 dcl. cream

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike as described on page 171; keep the head but remove the gills
2. Clean the unsalted herring, rinse, dry and place inside the pike
3. Brush the pike with egg and coat with crushed rusks
4. Grease a long pan with half the cold butter over an area the size of the fish, place the pike in it and dot the top with remaining butter
5. Roast in a warm oven about 1/2 hour, basting often and diluting with water
6. Crush the spice cookies finely and mix with cream
7. Remove the herring from the pike and place the pike on a warm platter, garnish with parsley
8. Whisk the pan sauce with the dissolved spice cookies, let boil, strain and serve in a separate sauce bowl
