## Ingredients

- 1 pike weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar
- 3 teaspoons salt (15 gr.)
- water
- 7 gr. coarse salt per litre of water

## Directions

1. Do not scale the fish, clean it as described on page 171
2. Rub the fish with salt and bind it by threading a needle with string through the head and tail so the fish lies bent during serving, hence the name kromgädda
3. Cook with the liver as in recipe no. 327
4. When the fins come loose easily, it is done
5. Place on a fish platter with a sieve or on a clean napkin
6. Garnish with parsley and the skimmed liver
7. Serve with melted butter, grated horseradish, and potatoes
