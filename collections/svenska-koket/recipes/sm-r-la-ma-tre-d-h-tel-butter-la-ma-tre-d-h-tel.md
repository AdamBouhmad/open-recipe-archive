## Ingredients

- 1 small bunch parsley, or 2 tbsp. finely chopped parsley
- 120 gr. fine butter
- 2 gr. table salt
- 1 tsp. lemon juice
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the parsley, wrap it in a cloth, clean it, chop it finely, and wring it hard in a cloth.
2. Stir the butter until it becomes white and fluffy.
3. Add the parsley, salt, lemon juice, and pepper, and stir the butter well.
4. Serve in a sauce boat with poached or fried fish or meat.
5. If the butter is to be used as a garnish, form it in a damp cloth into a roll and let it lie in the cloth on ice or in cold water until it is hard.
6. Then cut it into slices and let these lie in cold water until they are to be used.
