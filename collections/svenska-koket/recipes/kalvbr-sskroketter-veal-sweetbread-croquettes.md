## Ingredients

- 3 hg. veal sweetbread
- 1/2 lit. water
- 1/2 tsk. salt
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 lit.)
- 2 1/2 msk. butter (50 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 1 dcl. sweetbread stock
- 1 dcl. mushroom stock
- 2 dcl. cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 sheets gelatin
- salt, white pepper
- For breading: 4 msk. flour
- 2 eggs
- salt
- 2 tsk. table oil
- 1 kkp crushed bread crumbs
- For cooking: 1 kg. lard

## Directions

1. The veal sweetbread should soak in water for 1 hour to draw out the blood.
2. Blanch it, place in boiling water with salt added, and let cook 15 minutes, then drain on a sieve; save the stock.
3. Cut the sweetbread and mushrooms into cubes and sauté in half the butter until the butter is clear, then remove.
4. Sauté the remaining butter with flour for 2 minutes, add the sweetbread stock, mushroom stock and cream, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
5. Stir in the sweetbread and mushrooms along with the beaten egg yolks and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in 1 msk. hot water.
6. Season the mixture with spices and place on a flat dish well buttered, and brush with butter on the surface so it does not harden.
7. When the mass is cold, form portions of about 60 gr. with a spoon and place on a floured board.
8. Roll the croquettes in flour and form them as ovals.
9. Dip them in the well-beaten eggs mixed with table oil and seasoned with spices.
10. Then roll them in the finely crushed bread crumbs and let them sit for 1 hour to harden.
11. Fry them golden brown in hot lard and place on brown paper to drain.
12. Serve as a course with tomato or mushroom sauce.
