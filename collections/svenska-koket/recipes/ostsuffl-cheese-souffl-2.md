## Ingredients

- 80 gr. butter
- 80 gr. flour
- 1/2 litre boiling milk
- 5–6 eggs
- 60 gr. grated cheese
- salt
- white pepper
- (1/4 tsp. mustard)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mould
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mould

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are fried 2 minutes, the milk is added and the sauce is allowed to boil 5 minutes.
2. It is poured into a bowl; when it has cooled, the egg yolks are added, one at a time, and the mixture is stirred 10 minutes.
3. The grated cheese is mixed in along with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. The mixture is tasted, poured into a buttered and breadcrumbed pie mould and baked in ordinary oven heat for about 45 minutes.
5. The soufflé is served immediately as a course with browned butter or to the smorgasbord.
6. The mixture can also be baked in shells or small moulds and served instead of cheese and crackers.
