## Ingredients

- 210 grams sweet almonds
- 315 grams granulated sugar
- 2 egg whites
- redcurrant jelly

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, grate or grind them through an almond mill and pound with the sugar in a mortar
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and work them into the almond mixture
3. Roll this out into finger-thick sticks and place them on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets
4. Make a groove along each stick and fill it with jelly
5. Bake in very weak oven heat and cut, while still warm, into slanted pieces
