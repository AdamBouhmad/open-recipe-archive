## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sago grains
- 2 litres water
- ½ lemon
- 1 cup raisins
- 2 egg yolks
- 4–5 tablespoon sugar
- 1½ parts wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the sago grains and put them on to cook with the water and lemon peel.
2. Rinse the raisins and cook them separately in a saucepan and add them to the soup when the grains are clear.
3. Whisk the egg yolks, sugar, lemon juice and wine well together in a soup bowl, and pour the soup in under vigorous whisking, then taste.
4. Serve with rusks.
