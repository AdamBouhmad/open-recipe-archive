## Ingredients

- 1 hg. beef marrow
- 30 gr. kidney suet
- 50 gr. breadcrumbs
- 1/2 dcl. milk
- 1 hg. caster sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 hg. preserved fruit, in fine dice
- 45 gr. sultana raisins
- 30 gr. Malaga raisins
- 1/2 msk. butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. wheat flour for the mold (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the marrow and suet well, rinse, wrap in cloth, place in a strainer, melt in a water bath, and let the fat cool.
2. Weigh breadcrumbs, soak in milk and squeeze out.
3. Work the fat well with the sugar, the squeezed-out bread, the whisked eggs, egg yolks, fruit dice, cleaned and rinsed sultana raisins, and cleaned, rinsed, split and squeezed-out Malaga raisins.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered and floured smooth ring mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
5. Serve warm, covered with warm wine sauce (see no. 457), mixed with rum, or with flaming sauce (see no. 465), which is ignited when the pudding is brought to the table.
