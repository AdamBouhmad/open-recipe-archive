## Ingredients

- 2 kg berries
- 2 kg lump sugar
- 1/2 litre water

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, rinse quickly in a colander and let drain well, then place in a deep bowl.
2. Cook a syrup from sugar and water (see previous recipe) and pour it hot over the berries.
3. Let everything stand until the following day, then let the jam come slowly to a boil and cook while skimming and shaking until the berries are clear and sink.
4. See the previous recipes for further details.
5. This method is used with advantage for strawberries, large unripe raspberries, cloudberries and cloudberries.
