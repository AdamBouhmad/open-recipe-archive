## Ingredients

- 3 kg lingonberries
- 1 1/2 kg granulated sugar
- 5 - 6 parts water

## Directions

1. Clean the lingonberries, rinse and let drain.
2. Layer them alternately with the sugar in a jam pot, pour on the water, and place the pot over heat.
3. Bring the jam to a boil, remove from heat, shake and skim.
4. Return to heat, bring to a boil, remove from heat, shake and skim.
5. Repeat this 4-5 times, or until the berries are clear and the syrup is thick.
6. Immediately pour into prepared jars.
