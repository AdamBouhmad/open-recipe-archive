## Ingredients

- 5 hg. lean cured pork
- 1 egg
- 1 teacup crushed bread crusts
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cut the pork into slices, remove the rind neatly and pound the pork slices well on both sides.
2. Dip them in the whisked egg and turn them in the crushed bread crusts.
3. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter in it, add the pork and let it fry beautifully golden brown on both sides.
4. Place it on a warm dish, garnish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish with braised cauliflower or Jerusalem artichokes.
