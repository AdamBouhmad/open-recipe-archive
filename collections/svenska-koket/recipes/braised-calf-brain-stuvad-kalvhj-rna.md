## Ingredients

- 1 calf brain
- 1 lit. water for blanching
- 2 tsp. salt for blanching
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. thick cream
- salt
- white pepper
- ginger
- small croutons (fried bread slices)

## Directions

1. Let the calf brain soak in water, changing it often so all blood is removed.
2. Blanch it as in recipe no. 47, for 45 minutes and place on a sieve.
3. When it is cold, remove all membranes and chop the brain with a knife on a cutting board.
4. Fry the butter in a saucepan, add the brain and fry well.
5. Add the cream and seasonings and let the stew come to a good boil.
6. Place on warm toast and garnish with small croutons (fried bread slices).
7. Serve to the smörgåsbord (open sandwich table).
