## Ingredients

- 5 litres raspberries
- 1/2 litre boiling water
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 650 grams sugar per kilogramme of juice

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, crush them with a wooden pestle, place them in a crock, add the vinegar and water, stir the mass well and let it stand to ferment for approximately 5 days, stirring it from time to time.
2. Pour it into cheesecloth and let it drip.
