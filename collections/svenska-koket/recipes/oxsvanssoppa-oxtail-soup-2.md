## Ingredients

- 1 oxtail
- 1/2 kg beef neck
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 1-2 red onions
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/4 celery root
- 5 parsley stems
- 1 bay leaf
- 3 liters beef stock or water
- 15 g cornstarch flour
- salt
- sugar
- cayenne
- 1/2 deciliter dry sherry
- 1 tablespoon tomato puree (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the oxtail in cold water, scald in boiling water, dry with a cloth and chop into pieces at the joints.
2. Split the largest pieces crosswise.
3. Rinse the meat quickly, dry it and cut into smaller pieces.
4. Peel the onion, prepare the roots and cut them into pieces, rinse the parsley and bind it together with the bay leaf.
5. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the meat and vegetables and let them brown over gentle heat for about 1 hour.
6. Then add 1/4 liter of the boiling stock, which under even stirring is reduced to gelatin.
7. When the gelatin has taken on a nice dark brown color without burning, add the rest of the stock and let the soup cook slowly with a tightly closed lid for about 3 hours or until the meat is completely tender.
8. Remove it with a slotted spoon, trim the tail pieces, cut the meat into small pieces and place in the soup bowl.
9. Strain the soup, skim well, bring to a boil, bind with the cornstarch flour mixed in 1/2 cup cold water, and let it boil under stirring for 5 minutes.
10. Season with the spices, wine and tomato puree and pour over the meat in the soup bowl.
11. Serve very hot. If desired, a cooked carrot can be cut into nice figures and served as a garnish in the soup.
