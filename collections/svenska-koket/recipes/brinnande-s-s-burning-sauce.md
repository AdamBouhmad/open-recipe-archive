## Ingredients

- 2 hg. sugar
- 1 1/2 dcl. boiling water
- 1 1/2 dcl. cognac

## Directions

1. Place the sugar in a lukewarm frying pan and stir over heat until it has melted and taken on a light brown color.
2. Then add the water; when the sauce has cooled, carefully pour the cognac over it and set it alight.
3. Serve burning with marrow pudding and similar dishes.
