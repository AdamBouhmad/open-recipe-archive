## Ingredients

- 3 litres lingonberries
- 6 parts water
- 6 litres preserved pears
- 1.8 kg. crushed sugar
- 20 gr. cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clean the lingonberries, rinse them and cook with water until they break apart.
2. Pour them into cheesecloth to drip through naturally.
3. Peel the pears, cut a cross in each pear, scrape the stems and cut them off to half their length.
4. After peeling, place them in fresh water.
5. Cook the lingonberry juice with the sugar and cinnamon to a syrupy consistency.
6. Place the pears in and let them cook very slowly until they are soft and clear.
7. Place them in a crock or jar.
8. If the syrup is not sufficiently syrupy, cook it down and pour it over the pears.
