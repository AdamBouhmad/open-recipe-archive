## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. halibut
- 5 tbsp. salt
- 1 1/2 tsk. vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the fish, scald the dark side with hot water and scrape it well.
2. Rub it with the vinegar and 1 tbsp. salt, then let it lie in strongly salted water for 1 hour (about 2 tbsp. salt per liter of water).
3. Take the fish up and place it on the fire in hot, but not boiling water (short broth) and skim well when the broth comes to a boil.
4. Let it simmer slowly at the side of the fire for approximately 1/2 hour.
5. Place it immediately on the serving platter and garnish with parsley and lemon.
6. Halibut is served with hard-boiled, chopped eggs and clarified butter or with Dutch sauce.
