## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 cups milk
- 1 teaspoon flour
- salt
- sugar
- 1 teaspoon butter for the pan
- 1 tablespoon butter for stew
- 2 tablespoons flour for stew
- 2 cups sour cream
- 1 can omelet asparagus (7 deciliters)
- sugar
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. For the omelet, whisk all ingredients well together, pour the batter into a buttered omelet pan and bake light brown in ordinary oven heat.
2. Tip the omelet out immediately, place the stew on half of the omelet, and fold the other half over.
3. For the stew, fry the butter and flour together, add the asparagus liquid and cream with constant stirring, and let the sauce boil for 10 minutes.
4. Add the asparagus and season the stew to taste.
