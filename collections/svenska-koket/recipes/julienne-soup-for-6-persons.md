## Ingredients

- 2 small carrots
- 2 parsnips
- 1 small celery
- 1 leek
- 1 small head of lettuce
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 liters consommé or broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the lettuce head and cut into strips.
2. Rinse the vegetables, scrape and cut into fine strips.
3. Brown them well in the butter and sprinkle with sugar.
4. Add the consommé and let the soup simmer for about 20 minutes.
5. Skim, season to taste and serve very hot.
