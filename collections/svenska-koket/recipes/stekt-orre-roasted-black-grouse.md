## Ingredients

- 2 black grouse
- 1 hectogram lard
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 0.5-1 liter thin cream or broth
- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the birds, remove organs, rinse well and dry with a cloth. If the birds have been frozen, soak them in milk overnight.
2. Lard them with lard cut into fine strips and truss.
3. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the birds breast-side up and brown well on all sides, then salt and add cream or broth.
4. Roasting takes approximately 1 hour.
5. Strain the meat gravy and skim it, then prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
