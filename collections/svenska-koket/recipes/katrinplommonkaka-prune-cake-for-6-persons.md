## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 135 gr. sugar
- ½ liter thin cream
- 135 gr. butter
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 425 gr. prunes
- 1 hg. sweet almonds
- 1 tbsp. butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks and sugar until the egg mixture is white and fluffy.
2. Add the cream and butter, and let the mixture simmer over heat while whisking until it thickens.
3. Continue whisking until the cream is cold, then stir in the zest and juice of the lemon.
4. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Cook the prunes until soft in a little water, then remove the pits.
6. Butter a mold and line with buttered paper.
7. Place first a layer of prunes, over it sprinkle the chopped almonds, and finally pour the egg cream over.
8. Bake in mild oven heat for about 1 hour, turn out and serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
