## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless inner leg
- 1 litre skimmed milk or drinking milk
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 grams)
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 grams)
- ½ teaspoon white pepper
- 1 decilitre broth

## Directions

1. If the meat is frozen, let it thaw in milk or drinking milk.
2. Dry it well with a cloth, trim and cut into 12 slices, which are lightly beaten, wrapped in a cloth, sprinkled with seasonings, brushed with 1 tablespoon melted butter and left to rest on each other 1 hour.
3. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter in it, place the steaks in and fry about 3 minutes on each side.
4. Place on a warm platter, whisk the pan with broth and pour the sauce over the platter.
5. On each steak place a slice of maitre-d'hôtel butter (see no. 406); garnish the dish with parsley and serve with boiled or fried potatoes.
6. The steaks can also be garnished with fried Portuguese onion, in which case omit the maitre-d'hôtel butter and whisk the frying pan with 2 decilitres thick cream, then pour the sauce over the steaks.
