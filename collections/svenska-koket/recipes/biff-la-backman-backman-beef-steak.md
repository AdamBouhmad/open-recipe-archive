## Ingredients

- 750 gr. beef top round or boneless inner thigh
- 2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 dcl. boiling broth or water
- 1 small bunch parsley for garnish
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.) for garnish

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung 2-6 days depending on the season, is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into approximately 1 cm. thick slices, which are lightly beaten on both sides and sprinkled with spices.
2. A frying pan is heated until it becomes smoking hot, the butter is browned, the steaks are laid in and fried quickly on both sides.
3. They are placed on a warm serving platter, the pan is whisked out with the boiling broth or water and the sauce is poured onto the platter.
4. The steaks are garnished with the parsley, which has been cleaned, rinsed and well browned in butter.
5. They are served immediately with freshly boiled or fried potatoes as a breakfast dish.
