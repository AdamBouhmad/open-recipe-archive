## Ingredients

- 1¼ kg beef steak (loin or tenderloin)
- 1 hg smoked ham
- 2 calf kidneys
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- ½ tbsp salt
- ¼ tsp ground white pepper
- 1 tbsp flour
- 1 cup water
- 125 gr butter for dough
- 125 gr flour for dough
- 3-4 tbsp water

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as a simple butter dough.
2. Blanch the onion for 10 minutes. Blanch the calf kidneys and cut them into thin slices; likewise the onion and ham.
3. Prepare thin, well-pounded steaks from the beef steak, which are turned in a mixture of salt, white pepper and flour.
4. In a heatproof form of earthenware or porcelain, first place a layer of onion, over it a layer of steak slices, then a layer of ham and calf kidney, and so on until the form is filled.
5. Add the water, and place ¾ of the butter dough over the pie as a lid. Using a larding needle, make holes in a few places in the dough so that gases formed during baking can escape.
6. Brush the pie with egg white. Roll out the remaining butter dough thinly and cut it into narrow strips. Lattice the pie with these, or garnish it with small leaves cut out of the butter dough. Finally, also cover the edges of the pie with a strip of butter dough.
7. Brush over with beaten egg or water and bake in a hot oven for approximately 1½ hours.
