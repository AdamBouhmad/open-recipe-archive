## Ingredients

- 1 bream weighing 2 kg
- 1½ tablespoons salt (23 g)
- 3 parsley stems
- ½ tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped mushrooms
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks
- ½ egg
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2 deciliters broth or water

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish as in recipe no. 338 and serve with mushroom or caper sauce.
