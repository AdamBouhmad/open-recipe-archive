## Ingredients

- 2 cup dried cherries
- 2½ litres water
- 1–1½ cup crushed sugar
- 1½ tablespoon potato flour
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 cup cherry juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the cherries, pound them in a mortar and cook them in the water for approximately ½ hour.
2. Strain out the berries, return the liquid to the pot, add the sugar, and bring the soup to a boil.
3. Thicken with potato flour mixed with a little cold water.
4. Rinse the raisins, cook them separately in a short broth and add them.
5. Taste the soup with cherry juice and serve with biscuits if desired.
