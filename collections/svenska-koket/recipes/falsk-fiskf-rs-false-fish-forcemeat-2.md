## Ingredients

- 1 hg. butter
- 4 dcl. sour milk
- 1 hg. flour
- 6-7 eggs
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold
- lye-treated fish (lutfisk)

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and mix it with the crushed rusks. Add the lye-treated fish together with the well-beaten eggs.
2. Bring the milk to a boil and add it hot to the mixture, then stir it well.
3. Season with the spices, pour it into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined loaf mold which holds about 1 1/2 liters, put the lid on and cook the forcemeat in a water bath in the oven or on top of the stove for 1-1 1/2 hours.
4. Turn it out and serve immediately with crayfish or lobster sauce.
