## Ingredients

- 1 kg. beef fillet
- 1 can truffle (1/8 liter) or 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 1/2 liter brown broth

## Directions

1. The fillet is treated as in the previous recipe and larded in dense rows with half of the truffle or mushroom stems cut into strips.
2. Tie, roast, and garnish as in the previous recipe, but fill the shells with browned potatoes, mixed vegetables, and part of the sautéed mushroom caps instead of vegetable salad.
3. Serve with truffle sauce or reduced sauce, mixed with the remaining mushroom caps cut into cubes and sautéed in butter.
