## Ingredients

- 1 unflayed rabbit, about 3 kg
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 2 deciliters broth
- 5 deciliters cream or milk
- 1 tablespoon soy

## Directions

1. The rabbit, which should have hung for 2-6 days depending on the season so that the meat becomes tender, is flayed, treated and roasted as hare (recipe 590), but requires somewhat shorter roasting time.
2. Instead of larding the rabbit, it can be covered with lard slices and tied with fine sailcloth.
3. Serve with browned potatoes and salads and with a finished sauce (recipe 429).
4. Rabbit can also be stewed as hare (recipe 591).
