## Ingredients

- 75 gr. coffee beans
- 5 parts thick cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 hg. sugar
- 10 gr. gelatin
- sponge cake or slices of light and dark sponge cake
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. The coffee is roasted, and while it is still warm, 2 parts cream are poured over the ground coffee, after which it is left for 1/2 hour in a warm place.
2. The cream is strained off and whipped together with the eggs and sugar in a pot over heat until the egg mixture thickens.
3. The cream is removed from heat and allowed to cool.
4. The gelatin is dissolved in a little lukewarm water and stirred into the cream together with the remaining cream, whipped to stiff peaks.
5. A mold is buttered and lined with sponge cake or with light and dark sponge cake; the cream is filled in and set out to stiffen.
6. It is dipped for a moment in lukewarm water.
7. The pudding is turned out and served.
