## Ingredients

- 1 suckling pig, approximately 4 kg
- 1 1/2 tablespoon salt (23 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- For stuffing: 1 hectogram prunes
- 1 liter apples
- 3 deciliters water
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 6 deciliters grated bread (limpa or wheat bread)
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2-3 eggs
- For roasting: 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 3/4 liter broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the pig as in the preceding recipe and rub it with spices.
2. Wash the prunes well in lukewarm water. Peel the apples, cut them into quarters and cook the fruit for 10 minutes in water with sugar added, then pit the prunes.
3. Fry the bread in butter, place it in a bowl and let it cool. Mix it well with the fruit along with its liquid and the beaten eggs.
4. Fill the pig with this mixture and sew it closed and arrange as in the preceding recipe.
5. Serve with the stuffing as well as with potatoes, sour salads and reduced sauce.
