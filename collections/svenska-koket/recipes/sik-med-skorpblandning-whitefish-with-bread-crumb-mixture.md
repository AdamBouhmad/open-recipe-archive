## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg siklöja (whitefish)
- 1 msk salt (15 gr.)
- 4 msk flour (40 gr.)
- 1 hg butter
- parsley
- onion
- bread crusts, crushed
- morel mushrooms, optional

## Directions

1. Clean the fish as described on page 171 and score both sides with 1 1/2 cm between each score
2. Rub with salt
3. Pan-fry on the stove as in recipe no. 330
4. Clean and rinse the parsley, wring in a cloth, chop finely and mix with onion and crushed bread crusts, and if desired, with 1 msk blanched, finely chopped morel mushrooms
5. Brush the fish with egg and coat both sides with the bread crumb mixture
6. Grease a long pan with half the cold butter in a space as large as the fish
7. Place the fish in with the back up and sides spread out, and place the remaining butter in flakes on top
8. Add half the wine and water, and place the pan in a warm oven
9. Let the fish fry for about 20 minutes, basting it often and adding the remaining wine and water
10. Place on a warm dish, reduce the sauce with broth and thin with a little wheat flour if too thick, strain and pour over the fish
11. Garnish with parsley and sliced lemon and serve with mashed potatoes
