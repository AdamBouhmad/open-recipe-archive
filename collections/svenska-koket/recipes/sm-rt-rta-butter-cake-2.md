## Ingredients

- 3 hg. flour
- 2 dcl. water (scant)
- 5 hg. fine butter (equal weight to dough)
- 1/2 liter apple compote
- 3 dcl. extra thick cream
- 30 gr. finely chopped, roasted sweet almond

## Directions

1. Prepare the fine butter dough as in recipe 33 and roll out to 2 bases, one 2 cm. and the other 3 cm. thick.
2. Using the bowl or dish on which the tart is to be served, take a measurement. This is easiest done by cutting a paper pattern, according to which the dough is cut out with a pastry wheel.
3. The dough shrinks during baking and should therefore be 4 cm. wider in circumference than the pattern.
4. Place the thinnest base on a baking sheet and brush with egg white, but none of this may come down over the edge, as it prevents the butter dough from rising.
5. Cut the other base into a 3 cm. wide ring and place it on top of the base and brush carefully with egg white.
6. Bake the tart in a hot oven and turn it often so that it bakes evenly and gets a beautiful golden brown color.
7. Roll out the remaining dough to 2 cm. thickness and cut with a wheel into strips, which are laid in diagonal squares so that they form a lattice that fits inside the ring on the tart.
8. Brush the lattice and bake as before.
9. Fill the tart with apple compote or apple sauce, over which spread the cream whipped to stiff peaks, sprinkled with the roasted almond.
10. Place the lattice on top and serve the tart at once.
