## Ingredients

- 6 large canned artichoke bottoms
- 2 medium Portuguese onions
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese

## Directions

1. Place the bottoms on a buttered, fireproof dish or on a silver plate.
2. Peel the onion, chop it finely, and fry it in 2 tablespoons of the butter until it is golden brown, then pour it out and let it cool in the butter.
3. Fill the mixture into the bottoms and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs and a little grated cheese.
4. Melt the remaining butter and pour it over.
5. Place the dish in a baking pan with coarse salt and gratinée the dish in a hot oven for 10-15 minutes.
6. Serve very hot.
