## Ingredients

- 7 deciliters water
- 1 deciliter syrup
- 800 grams graham flour
- 50 grams pressed yeast
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 grams)
- wheat flour (about 400 grams)
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams) for the pans

## Directions

1. Boil the water with the syrup and mix with the graham flour.
2. Work the dough quickly, cover well and let stand overnight.
3. Early the next morning, add the yeast mixed with salt and 2 tablespoons lukewarm water.
4. Work the dough well and let it rise covered in a warm place until it begins to rise.
