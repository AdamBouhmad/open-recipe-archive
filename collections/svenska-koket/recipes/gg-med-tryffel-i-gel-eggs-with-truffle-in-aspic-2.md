## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter calf broth
- 5 sheets gelatin
- 1 tsp. vinegar
- 2 egg whites and their rinsed shells
- 1 tbsp. cold water
- 1 large truffle
- 2 tbsp. preserved peas
- 3 poached eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare the aspic as in recipe no. 10.
2. Pour a little of the clear aspic on the bottom of a rinsed, oblong, smooth timbale mold and let it set.
3. Cut the truffle into 3 even slices, which are laid lengthwise in the bottom of the mold with 5 cm. spacing.
4. Around each truffle slice place a ring of preserved peas and above lay the poached eggs.
5. Pour the rest of the aspic into the mold, which is placed on ice.
6. When the dish is to be served, loosen the aspic at the edges and turn the dish out onto an oblong platter, garnish with parsley or salad and serve to the smorgasbord.
