## Ingredients

- 500 grams calf liver
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 grams) for frying
- 1 red onion for sauce
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams) for sauce
- 2 1/2 tablespoons flour (25 grams) for sauce
- 4 deciliters broth or water
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. Let the liver soak in cold water for about 1 hour, dry with a meat cloth and remove the membrane.
2. Cut into 1 centimeter thick slices, sprinkle with salt and pepper and roll in flour.
3. Brown the butter, add the steaks and fry them nicely brown on both sides, then place on a warm plate.
4. Peel the onion, cut into fine dice and brown in butter, then fry with flour for 2 minutes.
5. Add the broth and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
6. Season with soy sauce and spices.
7. Pour over the steaks, which can be garnished with fried, rim-salted bacon slices.
8. Serve with potatoes as a breakfast dish.
