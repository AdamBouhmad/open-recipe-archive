## Ingredients

- 5 parts blood (pork blood)
- 8 parts weak beer
- 2 tablespoons syrup
- 3-4 tablespoons coarse rye flour
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped kidney fat
- ½ teaspoon white pepper
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1-1½ tablespoons salt
- 1/3 teaspoon finely ground marjoram
- 1 large red onion
- 1 tablespoon lard
- ½ tablespoon lard for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Strain the blood through a hair sieve and whisk well, then add the syrup and beer.
2. Whisk in the flour, add the finely chopped kidney fat and seasonings.
3. Peel and finely chop the onion, brown it in the lard and stir it last into the mixture.
4. Fry a little of the mixture to test if it is properly seasoned.
5. Grease a nice metal mold with lard and sprinkle with crushed bread crumbs; fill two-thirds with the mixture and bind over with buttered paper.
6. Place in boiling water and let cook for 2-3 hours.
7. Test with a whisk to see if the pudding is done.
