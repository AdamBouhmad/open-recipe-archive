## Ingredients

- 1½ liters potatoes
- water
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 2–2½ deciliters boiling milk

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush them well, peel thinly, and boil.
2. Drain the water well and press the potatoes through a purée press or mash with a fork.
3. Add the cold butter immediately while stirring vigorously, and then the boiling milk, a little at a time.
4. Season the mash with salt and a little sugar if desired.
5. Potato mash should be served as soon as it is prepared; if it stands for a long time it becomes tough and sticky.
