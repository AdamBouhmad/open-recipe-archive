## Ingredients

- 6 hg. raw fish (or 5 dcl. finely chopped fish)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 3 dcl. fish stock
- 2 msk. salt
- 1 tsk. vinegar
- 1 lit. water
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.)
- 2 dcl. boiling milk
- salt, white pepper
- 2 tsk. lemon juice
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (for shells)
- 2 msk. crushed rusks
- 1 msk. grated cheese

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the fish well and dry with a fish towel
2. Rub with some of the salt and vinegar and let sit a while
3. Place in hot water mixed with the remaining salt and let simmer slowly until done, about 10-15 minutes
4. Remove immediately from the stock, which is reserved
5. Remove skin, take out all bones and cut the fish meat into fine dice
6. Brown the butter and flour for 2 minutes
7. Add the fish stock and milk and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes
8. Season with spices and lemon juice and bind with the egg yolk
9. Gently mix the fish into the sauce and place the mixture in 6 buttered shells
10. Sprinkle with a mixture of rusks and cheese and drizzle with a little clarified butter
11. Place the shells in a long pan with coarse salt, put in a warm oven and gratinee for 10-15 minutes
12. Serve as a breakfast or intermediate course
