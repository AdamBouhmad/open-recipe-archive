## Ingredients

- 4-5 hg. boneless lamb roast
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 g.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 g.)
- 3-4 dcl. broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp. finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small cubes.
2. Brown the butter and flour (see Sauces, p. 210), add the broth and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes.
3. Warm the meat in the sauce but do not boil, as it will become tough.
4. Season the dish with spices and parsley and serve within a border of potato purée.
