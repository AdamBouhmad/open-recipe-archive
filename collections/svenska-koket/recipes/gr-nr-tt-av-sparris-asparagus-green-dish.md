## Ingredients

- 50 fresh asparagus or 1 can asparagus tips (1/2 liter)
- 2 liters water
- 30 grams salt
- 4 deciliters mousseline sauce
- 1 can fine mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 5-6 truffles

## Directions

1. Cook the asparagus as in recipe 204 and cut off the tips to a length of 5 cm.
2. Place the asparagus in a silver dish.
3. Pour the warm mousseline sauce over it.
4. Sprinkle the mushrooms (cut into strips and fried in a little butter) on top.
5. Sprinkle the truffle, cut into fine strips, on top.
