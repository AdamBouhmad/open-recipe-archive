## Ingredients

- 24 Baltic herrings
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 12 anchovies
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed hardtack
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 1 tablespoon crushed hardtack for the pan

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings, remove the bones, scrape away the dark membrane, rinse well and wrap in a fish towel.
2. Sprinkle salt inside and outside the herrings and let them rest for a while.
3. Clean and bone the anchovies and split them in half lengthwise.
4. Grease a fireproof omelet pan with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed hardtack.
5. Place half an anchovy in each herring, roll the herrings tightly and lay them in the pan close together.
6. Distribute the butter in flakes on top, then sprinkle the crushed hardtack over everything.
7. Place the pan in a good oven and fry the herrings until they are nicely browned, about 25 minutes.
8. Serve as a smörgåsbord dish or with fried potatoes as a breakfast course.
