## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg pike
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped red onion
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 1/2 deciliter milk for coating and frying
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 gr.) for coating
- 3 tablespoons wheat flour (30 gr.)
- 2 hectograms lard

## Directions

1. Scale the pike, clean it (see page 171), remove the backbone while keeping the head and tail, remove the gills and fins
2. Rub the fish with salt and white pepper, place on a platter and sprinkle with the onion
3. Mix the lemon juice and vegetable oil well and pour over the fish, let rest 2 hours, turning twice; then carefully remove the onion
4. Dissolve the salt in the milk, dip the fish in it and turn it in the flour
5. Heat the lard in a small long pan, place the pike in it, fry until nicely golden brown on both sides and place on absorbent paper so the fat drains off
6. Place on a serviette or parchment paper, garnish with fried parsley and nicely cut lemon wedges and serve with mushroom sauce and mashed potatoes
