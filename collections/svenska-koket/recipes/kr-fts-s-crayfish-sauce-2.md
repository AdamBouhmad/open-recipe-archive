## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 2 balls crayfish butter (20 gr.)
- 2 hg. canned crayfish tails
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 6 dcl. cream
- 1–2 egg yolks
- salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. If fresh crayfish are used, allow 1 score for this sauce
2. The crayfish butter is prepared as in recipe 404
3. If salted crayfish tails are used, let them soak in milk for a few hours and change the milk a couple of times
4. Drain the crayfish tails well in a colander
5. Melt the butter, fry the crayfish tails (the canned ones) in it until the butter is clear, then crush them with a spoon and fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes
6. Dilute with the cream and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes
7. Add the whipped egg yolks and crayfish tails and let the sauce simmer and season with the seasonings
8. The sauce can also be cooked on fish broth and cream and in the simplest case on good milk
