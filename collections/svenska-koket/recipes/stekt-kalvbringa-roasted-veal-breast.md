## Ingredients

- 2 kg. veal breast
- 1 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 3/4 lit. boiling broth or water, with 1 tsk. meat extract

## Directions

1. Dry the breast with a cloth wrung out in hot water, break the bones well and rub with a mixture of salt and pepper.
2. Heat a long pan, brown the butter in it, place the breast in with the meat side up, put in a warm oven and brown nicely, basting often with the butter in the pan.
3. When browned, add the boiling broth and let the meat roast until tender, or about 2 hours, basting every 10 minutes.
4. Cut the roast into nice slices with a sharp knife, place on a warm platter, garnish with parsley and serve with vegetables or boiled potatoes, lingonberries and onion.
5. Prepare the sauce as in recipe 429.
