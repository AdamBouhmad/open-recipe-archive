## Ingredients

- 1 kg large, juicy lingonberries
- 0.5 kg granulated sugar
- several cloves

## Directions

1. Clean the lingonberries and rinse them.
2. Prick them with a coarse needle all around and layer them alternately with the sugar and several cloves in a jar.
3. Cover the jar, and when the sugar is melted, the preserve is ready.
