## Ingredients

- 2 medium heads of lettuce
- 150 gr. fresh cucumber, in slices
- 2 tomatoes, in slices
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- a little paprika or white pepper
- 1 1/2 msk. vinegar
- 3-4 msk. fine table oil

## Directions

1. Treat the lettuce heads as in the preceding recipe.
2. Peel the cucumber, cut it into slices, rinse the tomatoes, dry them and cut them likewise into slices.
3. Prepare the salad shortly before it is served—preferably at the table.
4. In the salad bowl mix the seasonings, vinegar and oil, add the salad, cucumber and tomato slices and mix gently with the sauce.
5. Serve with roasted meat.
