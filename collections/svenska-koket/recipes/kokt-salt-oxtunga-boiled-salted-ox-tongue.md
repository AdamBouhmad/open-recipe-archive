## Ingredients

- 1 salted ox tongue
- water

## Directions

1. Rinse the ox tongue well and place it in cold water.
2. When it comes to a boil, remove all scum that rises to the surface, and let the tongue cook slowly until tender (approximately 3 hours).
3. Remove it from the pot and immediately strip off the skin.
4. Slice the tongue and serve with peas.
