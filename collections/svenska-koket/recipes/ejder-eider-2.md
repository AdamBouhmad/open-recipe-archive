## Ingredients

- 1 eider for 4-5 persons
- Milk

## Directions

1. After the bird is cleaned, the rank skin is removed, then it is blanched and allowed to soak in milk for 1 day before it is prepared.
2. Treat thereafter, roast and serve as duck.
