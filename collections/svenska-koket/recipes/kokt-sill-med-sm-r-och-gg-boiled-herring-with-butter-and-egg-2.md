## Ingredients

- 2 large or 4 small salt fat herrings
- 1 liter water
- dill
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2-3 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water for 12 hours, changing the water 2 times
2. Skin, remove the insides, fillet down the middle, then remove the backbone and all small bones
3. Rinse the fillets well and wrap in a fish towel
4. Hard-boil the eggs for 10 minutes, cool in water and peel; separate the whites and yolks and chop each separately
5. Place the herring in boiling water with a few sprigs of dill and boil 8-10 minutes
6. Place on a warm serving dish with a slotted spoon, pour the clarified butter over and garnish the herring with stripes of egg white and yolk
7. Serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish
