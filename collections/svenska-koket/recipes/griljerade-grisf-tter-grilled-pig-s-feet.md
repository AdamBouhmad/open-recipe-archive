## Ingredients

- 4 pig's feet
- 4 liters water
- 4 tablespoons salt (60 gr.)
- soda, the size of a pea
- 1 egg
- 2 dcl. finely crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. The pig's feet are cooked as in the previous recipe and as soon as they are done the large bone is removed, after which they are placed in a press between two cutting boards and allowed to cool.
2. They are dipped in whisked egg, turned in crushed rusks, fried a beautiful golden brown in butter and served with fried potatoes.
