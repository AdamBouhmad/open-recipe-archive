## Ingredients

- 10 white peppercorns
- 1 tbsp. wine vinegar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/2 kg. unsalted butter
- 1/2 cup water
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. The peppercorns are crushed and placed together with water and vinegar in a tinned copper saucepan (preferably with a flat bottom) and allowed to simmer until all the water has evaporated.
2. The saucepan is removed from heat, and when it is cool, the egg yolks are poured in; they are now allowed to simmer under strong whisking (with a steel whisk) over not too strong heat.
3. When they are fluffy, the saucepan is lifted to the side of the heat, and the clarified but cooled butter is added carefully alternating with 1 tbsp. water.
4. The sauce is worked for about 1/2 hour, seasoned with lemon juice, heated once more over the heat, strained through cloth and served.
