## Ingredients

- 2 deciliters rice groats (2 hectograms)
- 1.5 liters milk
- 3 hectograms calf's liver
- 2 hectograms fresh pork
- 30 raisins (30 g)
- 3 tablespoons syrup
- 1.5 tablespoons salt (23 g)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 0.5 teaspoon marjoram
- 0.5 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan
