## Ingredients

- 95 gr. sweet almonds
- 3 bitter almonds
- 3 dcl. milk
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 125 gr. sugar
- peel and juice of 1 orange or a little orange essence
- 6 leaves gelatin
- 1 dcl. hot water

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them in the oven, grind them through a mill and pound them well with a little of the milk in a stone mortar.
2. Mix the ground almonds, milk, cream, sugar, and the finely grated orange peel and orange juice in a well-scoured saucepan.
3. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces, dissolve it in the hot water and add it to the mixture.
4. Bring the mixture to a boil while whisking, strain it through muslin, pour it into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it set on ice.
5. The bottom of the mold can advantageously be garnished with red wine gelée.
6. Note: Instead of almonds, lemon, coffee, nuts etc. can be used.
