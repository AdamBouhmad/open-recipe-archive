## Ingredients

- 4 hg. fine prunes (approximately 36 pieces)
- 1/2 liter water
- 3 tbsp. sugar (45 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes very thoroughly in several portions of lukewarm water and let them soak in cold water overnight.
2. Drain the water and bring it to a boil with the sugar, add the prunes and cook covered until they are soft and swollen, or approximately 10 minutes.
3. Transfer them to a compote bowl, and cook the syrup until it is syrupy, or thicken it with 1 tsp. (5 gr.) potato starch and pour over the prunes.
4. Serve cold with whipped cream.
