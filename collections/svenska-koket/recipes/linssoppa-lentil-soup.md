## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter lentils
- 30 grams smoked ham
- 1/4 Portuguese onion
- 1/4 celery
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 2 liters broth
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- white pepper, salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 portion cream
- 30 grams butter
- 1/2 hectogram smoked ham
- 1 celery
- 1/4 egg

## Directions

1. Rinse the lentils and soak them the day before.
2. Scrape the ham and cut into pieces, scrape the celery, peel the onion and blanch it, then chop everything fairly finely.
3. Fry in 1 tablespoon of butter together with the lentils.
4. When the butter is absorbed, add the broth and let the soup slowly cook with a tightly closed lid.
5. When the lentils are soft, strain the soup.
6. Fry the remaining butter with the flour and add the lentil purée.
7. Let the soup cook for 10 minutes and taste and adjust seasoning.
8. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl and add the soup with vigorous whisking.
9. Scrape celery and cook in salted water, cut into slices, bread and fry in butter.
10. Cook the ham and cut into strips.
11. Serve the celery and ham in the soup. If desired, frankfurter sausage can be served in the soup instead.
