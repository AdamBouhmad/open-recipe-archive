## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 2 1/2 parts cream
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- jam
- 1 teaspoon butter for the pan

## Directions

1. The egg yolks and whites are separated.
2. The egg yolks are whipped together with the cream and sugar, after which the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks are folded in.
3. The batter is at once beaten into a well-buttered omelette pan and baked light yellow in ordinary oven heat.
4. It is at once turned out, the jam is placed on half of the omelette, and the other half is folded over.
