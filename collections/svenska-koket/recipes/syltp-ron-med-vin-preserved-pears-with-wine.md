## Ingredients

- 2.5 kg pears
- 1 kg lump sugar
- 3 parts water
- 2 parts white wine
- 3 parts vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, remove the blossom end, and cut a cross into each pear.
2. Scrape the stems and cut them down to half length.
3. After peeling, place the pears in fresh water so they do not darken.
4. From the sugar, vinegar and water, cook a thin syrup.
5. Remove the pears with a slotted spoon, place them in the syrup and allow to cook until they are completely soft, or approximately 3 hours.
6. Add the wine approximately 15 minutes before removing the preserving pot from heat.
