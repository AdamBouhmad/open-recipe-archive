## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 3 1/2 parts cream
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 150 gr sweet almonds
- 7-8 bitter almonds
- grated peel of 1/2 lemon
- 1 small tbsp potato flour
- 2 cups powdered sugar for the mold
- 2 tbsp boiling water

## Directions

1. Caramelize the sugar in a pan and dilute it with the boiling water, then glaze a baking form with this.
2. Whisk the egg yolks, cream and sugar in a pot over heat until the mixture begins to boil and thicken.
3. Lift the pot from the heat and continue whisking until the batter cools.
4. Then stir in the blanched and grated almonds, lemon peel and potato flour; lastly fold in the egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the batter into the glazed mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for 1/2 hour.
6. When the pudding cools, turn it out.
