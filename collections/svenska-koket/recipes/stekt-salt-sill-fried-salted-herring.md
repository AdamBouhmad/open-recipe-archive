## Ingredients

- 4-6 salted herrings
- 1 1/2 cups coarse rye flour
- 2 tablespoons butter or lard

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water the day before; clean, rinse and dry well
2. Remove the skin, fillet the fish, and carefully remove all bones
3. Coat it in coarse rye flour and fry in butter or lard
