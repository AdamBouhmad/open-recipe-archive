## Ingredients

- 1 small carrot
- 1 small parsnip
- 1 small leek
- 1/4 celery root
- 1 3/4 liters water
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr)
- 10 gr salt
- 4 white peppercorns
- 2-3 teaspoons (16-24 gr) solid meat extract

## Directions

1. Brown the root vegetables in the usual manner, cut them into pieces, and add them to cold water together with the butter and spices.
2. Let the vegetables cook with a tightly closed lid for approximately 2 hours or until they are completely soft.
3. Remove them with a slotted spoon and add the meat extract, dissolved in 1/2 cup hot water, to the broth.
4. Let the broth cook for approximately 1/2 hour or until it measures 1 1/2 liters.
5. Taste it, strain it through a muslin cloth wrung out in hot water, and use it as clear or reduced broth.
