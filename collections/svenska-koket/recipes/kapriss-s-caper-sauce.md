## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 4 parts fish stock
- 2 tbsp capers
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/3 part cream

## Directions

1. Fry together the butter and flour, add the fish stock and the coarsely chopped capers with some of its vinegar, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
2. Season with the spices and finish with the egg yolk and cream.
