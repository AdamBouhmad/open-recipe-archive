## Ingredients

- 1 liter pitted cherries
- 6 dcl. sugar syrup
- 1/4 dcl. kirsch
- Cherry pits and kernels

## Directions

1. Crush the pits and crush the kernels with the pitted cherries.
2. Mix with the sugar syrup and kirsch and freeze according to the basic fruit ice cream recipe (recipe no. 936).
