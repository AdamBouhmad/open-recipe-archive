## Ingredients

- 2 kkp linser (lentils)
- 1 1/2 lit. vatten (water)
- 1 portug. lök (Portuguese onion)
- 2 msk. smör (butter) - 40 gr.
- 1 kkp stötta skorpor (grated breadcrumbs)
- 1 kkp majsflingor (corn flakes)
- 2 ägg (eggs)
- 1 msk. salt
- 1/2 tsk. närsalt (seasoned salt)
- 4 msk. smör (butter) - 80 gr. for frying

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the lentils, place in soft cold water and let them cook until they form a mush.
2. Peel the onion, cut it into cubes and brown it in the butter.
3. Pass the lentil mush twice through a meat grinder together with the grated breadcrumbs and the onion.
4. Let the corn flakes cook for a few minutes in 1/2 liter water; when they have cooled, stir them into the lentil mush along with the whipped eggs.
5. Season the mixture and form into small cutlets, which are fried until nicely brown in the butter.
6. Serve with stewed parsnips or fried potatoes as a breakfast or main course dish.
