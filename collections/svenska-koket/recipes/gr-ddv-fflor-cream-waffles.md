## Ingredients

- 8 parts thick sour cream
- 2-3 parts water
- 1 snow
- 4-4.5 cups dried sifted flour

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, and sift down the flour.
2. Finally add the water (or even better snow).
3. Heat the waffle iron slowly, rub it well clean with white paper and brush with clarified butter.
4. The iron is only greased for the first waffles.
5. If the batter is too stiff, add more water or snow; if too thin, add more flour.
6. Place the waffles on a sieve, trim around the edges and place on a warm platter.
