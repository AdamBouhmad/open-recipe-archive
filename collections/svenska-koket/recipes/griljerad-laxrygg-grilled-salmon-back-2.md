## Ingredients

- Approx. 1 1/2 kg. salt salmon back
- 1 liter milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 4 tablespoon crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoon butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon back (head can also be used) into pieces, which are rinsed very well and allowed to soak in milk overnight.
2. Wrap them in a cloth, dip in the egg whisked with cream, turn in crushed rusks and fry beautifully brown in butter.
3. If the salmon is desired to be less fatty, place the pieces on blotting paper as they have been fried.
4. Arrange on a warm platter, garnish with parsley.
5. Serve with currant sauce and potatoes or with stewed potatoes.
