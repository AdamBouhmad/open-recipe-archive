## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons currants (50 g)
- 6 1/2 decilitres water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1 tablespoon sugar or syrup
- 1-2 tablespoons vinegar
- salt
- 1 egg yolk (optional)
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter (10 g)

## Directions

1. Clean the currants and rinse them very well in several warm waters
2. Put them in 6 1/2 decilitres cold water and let them simmer until soft
3. Strain through a colander and save the currant liquid for the sauce
4. Melt butter and flour together for 2 minutes
5. Add the currant liquid and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
6. Add the currants and season with sugar or syrup, vinegar and salt
7. Bind with the whisked egg yolk if desired
8. Stir in the cold butter last
