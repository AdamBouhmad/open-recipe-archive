## Ingredients

- 60 gr. butter
- 12 tbsp. sour milk
- 120 gr. wheat flour
- 20 gr. potato starch
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil butter and milk together, stir in the flour.
2. Let the batter cook until it leaves the pan, then remove from heat.
3. Stir in one egg yolk immediately, and once the batter has cooled, stir in the remaining egg yolks and last fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. For each doughnut, take one full teaspoon of the batter.
5. Cook the doughnuts in hot oil or bake them in a doughnut pan in the oven and dust with sugar as soon as they are done.
6. Serve with jam.
