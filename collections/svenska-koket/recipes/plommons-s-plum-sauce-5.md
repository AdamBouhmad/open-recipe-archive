## Ingredients

- 150 gr. prunes
- 5 dcl. water
- 50 gr. sugar
- 1 tsk. potato starch (5 gr.)
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes in several portions of lukewarm water and wrap them in a cloth.
2. Crush half of them in a mortar so that the pits are broken.
3. Let both the crushed and whole prunes soak for a few hours in the water in which they will be cooked.
4. Cook until the prunes are completely soft, then press through a fine sieve.
5. Boil the fruit purée with the sugar and thicken as in recipe no. 460.
6. Season with a little lemon juice if desired and serve warm or cold.
7. Sauce made from dried cherries is prepared in the same way.
