## Ingredients

- 1/4 tsk. salt
- a few grains paprika
- 1/2 tsk. sugar
- 1-1/2 msk. vinegar
- 4 msk. fine salad oil
- 1 tsk. French mustard (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the spices in a bowl and mix with the vinegar, then add the oil, a little at a time, just before the sauce is to be served.
2. This sauce is used for green salad, potato salad and, with the addition of a little French mustard, for lobster and fish salad.
