## Ingredients

- 1 young hen
- 3 1/2 liters light broth
- 2 tablespoon butter (40 g)
- 6 tablespoon flour (60 g)
- salt
- cayenne
- 3-4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Trim the hen in the usual way, let it soak in water 12 hours, dry well and cook in the broth until tender. Remove the breast meat from the carcass, which is returned to the broth and cooked for another 1 hour. Cut the meat into small, oblong pieces and place in the soup bowl. Strain the broth, skim and measure it — should be 3 liters.
2. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the broth little by little and let it cook with stirring for 5 minutes. Season the soup with spices. Beat the egg yolks and whisk them into the soup, which is then poured over the meat in the soup bowl.
