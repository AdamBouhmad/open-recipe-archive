## Ingredients

- 5 tbsp. flour (50 gr.)
- 1 1/4 lit. milk
- 1/2 lit. good weak ale
- 2 tbsp. syrup
- 1/2 tbsp. sugar (8 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. ginger
- 1 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- 1–2 egg yolks
- 2 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with 1/4 liter of the milk and let it swell for about 2 hours.
2. Bring the rest of the milk to a boil, add the flour mixture, and let the gruel boil while whisking for 8–10 minutes.
3. Boil the ale with the syrup and spices and pour it into the gruel with steady whisking.
4. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in the soup bowl and combine with the soup with vigorous whisking.
5. Serve immediately with fried bread cubes.
6. In the simplest version, omit the egg yolks and cream.
