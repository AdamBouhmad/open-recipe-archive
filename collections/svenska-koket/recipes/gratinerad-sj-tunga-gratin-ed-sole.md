## Ingredients

- 3 sole fish, approximately 5 hg each
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- For cooking: 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 g)
- fish broth
- For sauce: 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 g)
- 5 dcl fish stock
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper
- For garnish: 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 2 tablespoon butter (40 g)
- 2 tablespoon grated cheese
- 1 tablespoon fine breadcrumbs
- parsley
- small boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the sole as in recipe 371, let them drain and place on a butter-spread, ovenproof dish or preferably on a silver dish
2. Pour the sauce over it and garnish the arrangement in stripes or squares with the mushrooms, cut into fine cubes and sautéed in butter
3. Sprinkle the whole thing with the cheese, well mixed with the breadcrumbs, and finally pour over the butter in which the mushrooms were sautéed
4. Place the dish in a long pan with coarse salt, put the pan in a hot oven and gratinée the fish 10-15 minutes
5. Garnish with parsley and serve with small boiled potatoes
