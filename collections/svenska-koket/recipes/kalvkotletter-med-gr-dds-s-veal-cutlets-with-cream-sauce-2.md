## Ingredients

- 6 large veal cutlets
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 2 dcl good broth
- 1 tablespoon Madeira
- 1 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Treat the cutlets as in the previous recipe, rub in the spices but do not bread them.
2. Brown them nicely in the butter, dilute with the boiling broth, wine and cream and let them simmer slowly with the lid on for 5-8 minutes.
3. Place them on a warm plate and pour the sauce over them.
4. Serve with boiled or mashed potatoes and lingonberries.
