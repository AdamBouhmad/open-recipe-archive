## Ingredients

- ½ liter thick cream
- zest of ½ lemon
- 150 grams sugar
- 1½ parts water
- 8-10 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks and blend with the grated lemon zest.
2. Brown the sugar and dilute it with the boiling water, and when it has cooled somewhat, stir it into the cream.
3. Dissolve the gelatin in a little lukewarm water and stir it in last to the cream.
4. Pour this into a water-rinsed mold and set it out to stiffen.
5. When it is to be turned out, dip the mold in a little lukewarm water.
6. Serve with pastries.
