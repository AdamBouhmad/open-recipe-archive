## Ingredients

- 3–4 stalks rhubarb or 1/2 liter preserved rhubarb
- 2 liters water
- peel from 1/4 lemon
- 1–1 1/2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato starch (30 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon caster sugar

## Directions

1. If using fresh rhubarb, rinse, dry and cut into 2 cm. long pieces.
2. Bring water to a boil, add rhubarb and lemon peel and allow to cook until it falls apart.
3. Strain the soup and return to the saucepan, add crushed sugar.
4. Bring to a boil again, thicken with potato starch mixed in 1/2 tablespoon water and cook for 2 minutes.
5. Whisk the egg yolks and caster sugar in the soup bowl until frothy, and pour the soup in while whisking continuously.
6. Serve with potato starch pastries or rusks. In simpler versions, omit the egg yolks but use more potato starch.
