## Ingredients

- 3 hg. calf thymus
- 1 msk. butter
- 1 hg. salted ox tongue
- 1 hg. mushrooms
- 1 1/2 msk. butter
- 3 msk. flour
- 2 parts broth
- 2 parts cream
- salt, white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 eggs for coating
- 2 kkp crushed rusks for coating
- 3/4 kg. lard for frying

## Directions

1. Let the calf thymus soak in water for a couple of hours to remove the blood, then blanch it and let it drain.
2. Brown it in butter, remove it and let it cool, then cut it into small cubes.
3. Cut the tongue and mushrooms likewise into cubes.
4. Fry butter and flour together, add the broth and cream, and let the mixture cook for 5 minutes.
5. Remove the pan from heat, add the seasonings and egg yolks.
6. Add the thymus, tongue and mushrooms, and spread the mixture on a flat plate to cool.
7. Form oblong croquettes, roll them in crushed rusks, dip them in egg and roll them again in crushed rusks, then let them dry for 1/2 hour.
8. Heat the lard, cook the croquettes until golden brown and arrange on brown paper to drain.
9. Garnish with parsley and serve with mushroom or tomato sauce.
