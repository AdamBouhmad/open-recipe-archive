## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg boneless veal
- salt, white pepper
- 3.5 tablespoons butter
- 1 bunch parsley
- 2 cups broth or water
- 1 part heavy cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into slices across the grain, these are pounded lightly and sprinkled with pepper and salt.
2. Rinse the parsley, clean and chop coarsely and work into 2 tablespoons of the butter.
3. Divide the parsley butter into as many parts as there are meat slices; place one part on each slice, which is then rolled up and tied with string (previously dipped in boiling water).
4. Brown the roulades in the remaining butter, add boiling broth or water and let them cook on low heat until they feel tender, then remove and take off the strings.
5. Strain the sauce, skim, bind with cream and serve over the roulades.
