## Ingredients

- 75 gr. butter
- 50 gr. grated cheese
- 75 gr. wheat flour
- 1/2 egg yolk for brushing

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and pour it into a bowl.
2. Add the grated cheese and flour, and mix the mixture well.
3. Let the dough stand for a while to firm up, then roll it out on the work surface to a finger-thick length, which is cut into 5 cm. long sticks.
4. Twist them two turns or leave them straight, brush with egg yolk, place on a well-cleaned baking sheet and bake to a nice golden-brown color in a warm oven.
5. Serve lukewarm with broth, clear soups and purées.
