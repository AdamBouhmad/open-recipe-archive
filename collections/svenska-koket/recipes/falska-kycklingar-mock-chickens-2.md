## Ingredients

- 800 g pork tenderloin
- 4 tbsp chopped parsley
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter for frying (60 g)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 g)
- 4 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Clean the fillets, which weigh 2-4 hg each, dry with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and cut open lengthwise.
2. Clean the parsley, rinse, wring tightly in a cloth, chop and work into the butter.
3. Fill the fillets with the parsley butter, sew neatly with a basting stitch and rub with seasonings.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the fillets in, brown well on all sides and then let cook with a tight lid until tender, about 1/2 hour.
5. Cut and arrange as in the previous recipe.
6. Roux the flour well with the butter in the pot, add the cream, and let the sauce boil 5 minutes, taste and strain over the meat.
7. Serve with browned potatoes and salad.
