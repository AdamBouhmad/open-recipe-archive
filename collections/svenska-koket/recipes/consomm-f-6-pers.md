## Ingredients

- 1 kg marrow bone
- 1/4 kg veal breast
- 1 hen
- 4 liters broth
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 carrot
- 1 small parsnip

## Directions

1. The hen is prepared in the usual way and browned on strong heat, but must not be completely cooked through.
2. The beef and veal are washed with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut into small pieces.
3. It is then placed along with the hen and salt in the cold broth, brought slowly to a boil and skimmed, after which the roasted vegetables are added.
