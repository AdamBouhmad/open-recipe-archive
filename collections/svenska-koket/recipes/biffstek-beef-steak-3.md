## Ingredients

- 1¼ kg beef loin or tenderloin
- ⅛ tbsp salt
- ¼ tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 2-3 parts boiling broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into approximately 3 cm thick slices; these are lightly pounded on both sides, peppered and salted.
2. Place a frying pan on the heat to get hot, brown the butter in it, and place the steaks in to fry quickly for approximately one minute on each side.
3. Deglaze the pan with the boiling broth or water, and pour the sauce over the steaks.
4. Finally, brown the peeled onion cut into slices, and garnish the steaks with it.
