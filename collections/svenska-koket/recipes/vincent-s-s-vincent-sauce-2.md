## Ingredients

- 15 gr. parsley
- 15 gr. chervil
- 15 gr. tarragon
- 15 gr. chives
- 30 gr. watercress
- 30 gr. spinach
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 tsp salt
- 4 hard-boiled egg yolks
- True mayonnaise
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the vegetables, place them in boiling salted water and let them simmer for 2 minutes, strain in a colander, immerse in cold water and let drain well.
2. Pound them together
