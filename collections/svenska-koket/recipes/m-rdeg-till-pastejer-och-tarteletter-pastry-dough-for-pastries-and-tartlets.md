## Ingredients

- 225 gr. flour
- 1 hg. butter
- 5 gr. salt
- 10 gr. sugar
- 1 dcl. water
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Sift the flour on the pastry board; the butter, which should be cold, is distributed in flakes in the flour and worked in quickly with the fingertips, until the mixture becomes even and light yellow in color.
2. Then the seasonings, water and egg yolk are added quickly and the dough is kneaded a couple of times, rolled into a ball, placed immediately in a napkin and set in a cool place to rest for 1 hour.
3. Use in the same way as puff pastry for pastries, tartlets etc.
