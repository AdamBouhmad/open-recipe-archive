## Ingredients

- 2 hg. caster sugar
- 2 dcl. water
- 1 vanilla bean
- 1 hg. unsweetened chocolate
- 10 sheets gelatin
- 1 dcl. hot water for the gelatin
- 7 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar with the water and vanilla until a clear, simmering syrup forms, then remove the vanilla bean.
2. Melt the chocolate in a water bath, without the addition of water, and stir it into the sugar syrup.
3. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, dissolve it in the hot water and add it, then stir the cream until it is cold.
4. Then fold in the cream whipped to stiff peaks, and pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours to set.
5. When the cream is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass dish with cake paper.
6. The cream can also be poured into a mold with a lid and frozen, but then only 3 sheets of gelatin are used.
