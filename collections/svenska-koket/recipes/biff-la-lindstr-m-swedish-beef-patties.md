## Ingredients

- 3 potatoes
- 2 beets
- 2 red onions
- 6 hg. boneless beef
- 1 dcl. thick cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped capers
- 1 small tbsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 1 dcl. boiling broth

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes, let them cool and cut into fine dice. Cook the beets and cut into fine dice; peel the onions, blanch and chop finely. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and run 4 times through a meat grinder, whereupon it is mixed with the egg yolks, potatoes, beets, onions and spices. From this mixture form round patties, about 1 1/2 cm thick.
2. Heat a frying pan slowly until it becomes smoking hot, brown the butter in it, lay the patties in and let them fry about 2 minutes on each side. Place the patties on a warm serving dish, wipe out the pan with the broth and pour the sauce over the patties. Serve with fried potatoes as a breakfast dish.
