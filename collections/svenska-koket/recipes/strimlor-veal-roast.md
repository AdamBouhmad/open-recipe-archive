## Ingredients

- 1 small long roasting pan
- 1 tbsp of butter
- saddle of veal
- hot broth or water
- its own gravy

## Directions

1. Grease a small long roasting pan with 1 tbsp of the butter and place the saddle in it.
2. Cut the rest of the butter into small pieces and pour it hot over the saddle.
3. Place the roast in the oven and brown it, moisten with the hot broth or water, and baste with its own gravy every ten minutes.
4. The roasting takes approximately 1½ hours.
5. Strain and skim the meat gravy, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
