## Ingredients

- 3 chickens of 0.5 kg each
- 2-3 teaspoons salt
- 1 bunch parsley
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 0.5-1 liter water or broth
- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 1.5 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the chickens carefully, remove organs, rinse well and dry inside and outside with a towel.
2. Rinse a few parsley sprigs, coat with 1 tablespoon of butter and place inside the chickens, then sew them shut and truss.
3. Brown the remaining butter in a roasting pan and place the chickens in breast-side down. When nicely browned on all sides, salt them, add the boiling broth or water and cook slowly until tender with a tightly fitting lid.
4. Cut the chickens and serve on a warm platter, garnished with curly lettuce.
5. Strain the meat gravy, skim it, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
