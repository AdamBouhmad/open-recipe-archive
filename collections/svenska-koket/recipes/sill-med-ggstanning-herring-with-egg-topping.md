## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fat herrings
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp finely chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Prepare the herrings: remove them, rinse well, and soak in plenty of water for 12 hours, changing the water 2 times.
2. Remove the skin, fillet the herrings, and remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets well, wrap in a fish towel, and cut into small cubes.
4. Prepare the egg topping by mixing eggs, milk, sugar, and finely chopped parsley.
5. Butter a fireproof pan and arrange the prepared herring in it.
6. Pour the egg topping over the herring.
7. Bake until set and serve as a breakfast dish.
