## Ingredients

- 5 hg. boneless veal
- 50 gr. butter
- 2 eggs
- 2.5 parts thin cream
- 3 tbsp. flour
- 1 small tbsp. salt
- 1/8 tsp. white pepper
- 0.5 hg. macaroni

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, free it from sinews and membranes, cut into cubes and pass three times through a meat grinder.
2. Pound it in a stone mortar together with the butter and egg yolks.
3. Work the cream together with flour, salt and white pepper into the forcemeat.
4. Butter a fine metal mold and garnish it with blanched macaroni, cut into rings and stars.
5. Whisk the egg whites until stiff and fold them last into the forcemeat, which is immediately poured into the prepared mold.
6. Bind the mold over with butter paper and poach the timbal in a water bath in an oven or on the stove for 1-1.5 hours.
7. Turn it out and serve with preserved peas, poured over with clarified butter.
