## Ingredients

- 3 dcl. extra thick cream
- 450 gr. brown cane sugar
- 3 dcl. fine, brown syrup
- 2 msk. bicarbonate (20 gr.)
- 1 msk. ground ginger
- 1/2 tsk. citrus oil
- 1 kg. 2 hg. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form
2. Fold in the sugar, syrup, bicarbonate, ginger, and citrus oil
3. Stir the mixture for 15 minutes
4. Finally work in the flour, and let the dough rest for about 2 hours
5. Work the dough thoroughly on a baking board, roll out to 3-4 mm thickness
6. Cut out with a round mold into cookies, place on well-buttered baking sheets and brush with water just before placing in the oven
