## Ingredients

- 2 large salt fat herrings
- herring roe
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 red onions
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the form
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks for the form

## Directions

1. Prepare the herring as in the preceding recipe
2. Collect the roe, rinse and soak in milk
3. Grease a heat-proof form with butter and sprinkle with rusks
4. Place the well-dried fillets in it and brush with the whisked egg yolk
5. Cut the roe into slices and add along with the peeled and thinly sliced red onion
6. Place butter in flakes on top and sprinkle with rusks
7. Gratinée in a warm oven approximately 10-15 minutes and serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish
