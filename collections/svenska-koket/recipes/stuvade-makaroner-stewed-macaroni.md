## Ingredients

- 2 hectograms macaroni
- 1 liter milk
- 1.5 teaspoons salt
- 0.25 teaspoon white pepper
- 1.5 teaspoons grated cheese

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into pieces (unless "ideal macaroni" is used) and rinse them.
2. Bring the milk to a boil, add the macaroni and let it cook slowly until tender.
3. Stir often but gently so it does not break apart.
4. Season the stew with salt, white pepper and grated cheese.
