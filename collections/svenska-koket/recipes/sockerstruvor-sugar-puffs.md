## Ingredients

- 1 egg yolk
- 7 egg whites
- 150 gr. granulated sugar
- 150-160 gr. wheat flour
- 1/2 kg. oil for frying

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolk and four of the egg whites with the sugar until white and foamy.
2. Sift the flour and carefully stir into the batter.
3. Last, fold in the remaining egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Heat the oil in a pot, not more than a small teacup.
5. Through a rosette pipe or regular piping bag, squeeze a tablespoon of the batter back and forth into the oil so that it forms a perforated cake.
6. When the puff is golden brown on the underside, turn it with a slotted spoon, and when it has taken on an even, lovely colour, remove it, bend it over a stick, and let it drain on grey paper.
7. Dust the puffs with sugar before serving.
