## Ingredients

- 6 eggs (360 gr.)
- 360 gr. butter
- 360 gr. granulated sugar
- 360 gr. wheat flour
- red currant jelly for filling
- 100 gr. sifted sugar for the glaze
- 2 tbsp. water for the glaze

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar well.
2. Melt the butter, let it cool and stir until it becomes white, then blend with the egg mixture.
3. Finally add the flour.
4. Spread the batter quite thinly on a well-buttered baking sheet and place in the oven to bake.
5. When it is baked, cut it in half, and coat one half with jelly.
6. Lift the other half onto the first, glaze the cake and cut into slanting diamonds.
