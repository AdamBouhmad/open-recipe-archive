## Ingredients

- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 1 dcl. mushroom broth
- 5 dcl. cream
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1–2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut the mushrooms into pieces or slices and fry them in the butter until it is clear, then crush them with a spoon, and fry the butter with the flour for 2 minutes
2. Dilute with the mushroom broth and cream and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes
3. Add the mushrooms and let the sauce simmer
