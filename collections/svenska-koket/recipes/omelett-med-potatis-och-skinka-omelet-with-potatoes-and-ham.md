## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 10 tablespoons thick cream
- 0.5 liters cooked potatoes
- 200 g smoked ham
- 0.25 Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs and cream together.
2. Cut the potatoes into slices and the ham into oblong rectangular pieces.
3. Peel and chop the onion and fry it in butter.
4. Grease an omelet pan with butter, layer the potatoes, ham and onion in it, and pour the egg mixture over.
5. Shake the pan over the heat until the omelet has set, then tip it out first onto a lid and then onto the serving plate, so that the unbaked side comes up.
6. Sprinkle the omelet with chopped parsley and serve immediately.
