## Ingredients

- 750 gr sugar
- 1 litre water
- zest of 4 oranges
- juice of 8 oranges and 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar syrup.
2. Remove all white pith from the orange zest and cut into pieces.
3. Place the zest in the hot syrup and let it cool.
4. Prepare the mixture as in the lemon glacé recipe.
