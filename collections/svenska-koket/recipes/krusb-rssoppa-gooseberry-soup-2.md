## Ingredients

- 1 liter green gooseberries or 1/2 liter preserved gooseberries
- 2 liters water
- peel of 1/4 lemon
- 1–1 1/2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (30 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Top and tail the gooseberries, rinse them, place in boiling water with the lemon peel, and let cook until soft.
2. Strain the soup.
3. Cook and serve as in the rhubarb soup recipe.
