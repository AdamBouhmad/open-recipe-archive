## Ingredients

- half-ripe gooseberries
- water
- 1 kg loaf sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Rinse and top the gooseberries and place them in an earthenware crock. Pour cold water over the berries, enough to completely cover them.
2. Place a plate over the crock and seal it tightly with a dough made of coarse rye flour and water.
3. Place the crock in a pot with hay; pour in so much water that over half of the crock stands under water.
4. Let it cook for 5-6 hours. Pour the mass into cheesecloth and let the juice drain.
5. Then let it cook with the sugar with skimming for 20 minutes; bottle it hot or cold; cork the bottles and seal with resin.
