## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg. boneless beef (thigh, rib, or marrow bone)
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 3–4 red onions
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 8 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 6 dcl. boiling water
- 1/2 tbsp. soya

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until tender, is prepared (see p. 232) and cut across the grain into approximately 1 1/2 cm. thick slices, which are lightly pounded on both sides and turned in a mixture of the spices and flour.
2. Peel the onions, chop roughly, and brown in the butter in an iron pot, add the meat slices together with the allspice berries and bay leaves, and add the water mixed with soya.
3. Place the pot over a brisk fire so the liquid boils rapidly, then let the meat simmer slowly with a tight lid until tender, approximately 2 1/2–3 hours.
4. Remove, cut the string, slice thinly, and serve with the gravy strained and skimmed, or with truffle sauce (see no. 433).
