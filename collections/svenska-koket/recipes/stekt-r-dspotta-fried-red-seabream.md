## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg red seabream
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 3 deciliters milk
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons cooking oil
- salt
- white pepper
- 1.5 cups crushed rusks
- 0.5 kg butter
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the red seabream as in the preceding recipe, but cut off the head and remove the dark skin by scraping the tail fin upward with a sharp knife until enough skin loosens that you can wind it once around the knife blade; roll the knife toward the head and wrap the skin around it.
2. Rub the fish with salt, let it rest for 1 hour, dry it again and bread it as in recipe no. 376.
3. Fry as in recipe 329, place on a warm dish with parchment paper, garnish with lemon wedges and serve with browned butter and parsley and mashed potatoes.
4. The fish can also be fried in butter as in recipe no. 330.
