## Ingredients

- 125 g superfine sugar
- 3 dcl water
- peel and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 15 g gelatin (approximately 9 leaves)
- 1 dcl warm water for the gelatin
- 1 1/2 dcl French wine
- 1 1/2 dcl sherry

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar and water into a clear syrup, which is skimmed and immediately mixed with the finely grated lemon peel and lemon juice as well as the rinsed gelatin dissolved in the warm water and the wine
2. Stir the mixture well and pour it into a taut cheesecloth, previously wrung out in hot water, and let it drip through
3. As long as the jelly runs turbidly through, pour it back into the cheesecloth
4. The jelly can be beaten in a large bowl set on ice, after which the jelly is whisked until it resembles snow
5. Pour into a bowl or use for garnishing clear jellies
6. For this jelly, currant wine alone can advantageously be used
