## Ingredients

- 1 svinhuvud (pork head, = 4 kg.)
- 3 kg. kalvbog (veal shoulder)
- vatten (water)
- 12 kryddpepparkorn (allspice berries)
- 12 vitpepparkorn (white peppercorns)
- 7 msk. salt
- 3 tsk. stött vitpeppar (ground white pepper)

## Directions

1. Split the pig head, wash and scrape it and let it then lie in water for one day. Change the water a couple of times.
2. Then place the head and veal meat in so much boiling water that it stands over the meat.
3. After skimming, add 3 msk. of the salt and the peppercorns.
4. Let the meat cook until it is tender and falls away from the bones.
5. Wring a towel out in hot water and spread it out in a bowl.
6. Cut the skin in as large pieces as possible and spread them out on the cloth with skin side down.
7. Cut the meat and fat into slices while it is hot.
8. Nearest the skin, place a layer of veal, which is sprinkled with pepper and salt, and then a layer of fat, and so continue alternately with seasonings between each layer.
9. On top place a fat skin, then tie the towel tightly together.
10. Dip the sylta in the boiling broth and then place it in a press for about 24 hours.
11. It is preserved in brine.
