## Ingredients

- 6 grouse
- 1 hectogram butter
- 3 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 liter boiling broth
- Meat stock
- 2 liters good broth
- 12-15 sheets gelatin
- 3 egg whites and the rinsed egg shells
- 1 tablespoon cold water
- 1/2 deciliter good Madeira or sherry
- 6-8 truffles
- aspic croutons
- 2 heads of lettuce

## Directions

1. Pluck the grouse, singe them, remove the insides and chop off the wings and legs.
2. Rinse them very well, dry them with a kitchen towel and truss them (see poultry).
3. Brown the butter, place the birds in it with breasts up and let them roast in a warm oven for about 1/2 hour, basting them over and adding the broth.
4. Take them out and let them cool, after which the breasts are carefully loosened from the carcasses; each breast half is cut into 3 slices.
5. Put the carcasses back in the roasting pan and add more broth.
