## Ingredients

- 3 hg fresh asparagus or 1 can omelet asparagus (1/2 liter)
- 80 gr butter
- 80 gr flour
- 2 dcl asparagus broth
- 3 dcl boiling milk
- 5-6 eggs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Scrape the asparagus, rinse it, cut it into three parts and cook it soft in 4 dcl water with 1/2 tsp salt added. If using canned asparagus, cut it in half and place it in a sieve to drain.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the boiling asparagus broth and milk and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes. Pour it into a bowl; when it has cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time, and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
3. Mix in the well-drained asparagus together with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks. Taste the mixture, pour it into a buttered and bread-crumb-covered pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 45 minutes.
4. If using a sheet metal form, bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour and turn it out. Serve the soufflé immediately after removing it from the oven as a middle course with hollandaise or melted butter.
