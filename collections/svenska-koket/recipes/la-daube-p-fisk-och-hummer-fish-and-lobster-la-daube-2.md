## Ingredients

- 1 kg pike-perch or zander
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 6 white peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 can lobster (1 liter)
- 1 1/2 liters of fish stock
- 15 gelatin leaves
- 3 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon cold water
- 4 tablespoons good white wine
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 truffle
- parsley

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it, rinse well and dry with a fish towel.
2. Rub the fish with half the salt and let rest for 1/2 hour, dry again and place in cold water with the remaining salt and spices.
3. Let simmer slowly until done, about 15 minutes.
4. Remove immediately, peel off the skin and divide the fish into 2 fillets.
5. Place the bone in the stock, which is allowed to cook for another 20 minutes.
6. Cut the fish into beautiful pieces and the lobster likewise.
7. Prepare aspic from the fish stock as in recipe no. 181. When cool, add the wine.
8. Arrange the dish as in the preceding recipe and serve at the smörgåsbord.
