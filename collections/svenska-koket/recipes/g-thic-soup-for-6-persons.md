## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters strong, clear broth
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon cognac
- 0.5 part sherry
- salt
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Bring the broth to a boil and clarify with egg white if necessary.
2. Color with soy sauce and season with cognac, sherry and spices.
3. Serve with toasted bread.
