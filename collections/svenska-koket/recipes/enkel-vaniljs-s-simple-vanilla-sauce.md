## Ingredients

- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 3 parts milk
- 1 1/2 parts cream
- 1-2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon potato flour
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The milk and cream are allowed to boil with the vanilla pod and sugar until the liquid has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor
2. The vanilla pod is removed, the potato flour is stirred out with a little cold milk and added, after which the sauce is allowed a short boil
3. The pot is lifted off the heat, and the egg yolks are stirred in
4. The pot is then placed over the heat again, and the sauce is allowed to simmer with whisking, but not boil, until it becomes moderately thick
5. It is then whisked until it becomes cold
