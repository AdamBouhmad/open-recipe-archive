## Ingredients

- ½ liter cooked or fried chopped meat
- 2 small red onions
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ tablespoon salt
- ½ teaspoon white pepper
- 2 parts stock
- 1 liter potatoes
- ½ cup milk
- 1 tablespoon butter
- sugar
- salt
- ½ tablespoon butter (for the mold)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs (for the mold)

## Directions

1. Peel the onion, finely chop it and brown it together with the meat in butter, then add the spices.
2. Peel the potatoes raw, cook and mash them with a fork.
3. Boil the milk together with the butter and stir it into the purée; season this and work it well.
4. Butter a mold, put a layer of potato purée in it and then the browned meat.
5. Pour the stock over it, and place another layer of potato purée on top.
6. Sprinkle the pudding with crushed breadcrumbs and put it in the oven to get a nice brown color.
7. Serve with roast sauce.
