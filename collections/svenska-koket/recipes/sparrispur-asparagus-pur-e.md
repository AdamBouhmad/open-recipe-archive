## Ingredients

- 425 grams asparagus
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters good broth
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 portion cream

## Directions

1. Remove the asparagus tips and set aside to blanch later and place in the soup bowl.
2. Cut the asparagus, blanch in salted water and drain.
3. Fry butter and flour together, add half of the broth, add the asparagus and let it slowly cook until soft.
4. Strain the soup, pour in the remaining broth, and bring to a boil.
5. Taste and adjust seasoning with spices and thicken with egg yolks and cream.
