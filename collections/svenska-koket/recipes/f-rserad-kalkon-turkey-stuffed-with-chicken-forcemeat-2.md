## Ingredients

- 1 turkey cock of approximately 5 kg
- 4-5 truffles
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard, in 1 slice
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 1 liter good broth
- 2 chickens = approximately 450 g scraped meat
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour (15 g)
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk
- 6 deciliters thick cream
- 3-4 truffles

## Directions

1. The turkey is prepared and rubbed with a mixture of salt and pepper.
2. For the forcemeat: the chicken meat is finely scraped, mixed with salt, white pepper, flour, egg, egg yolk, thick cream and truffles.
3. The turkey is filled with this forcemeat and roasted in an oven or pot until tender.
4. The bird is basted frequently during roasting.
5. It is carved and served with gravy or reduced sauce.
