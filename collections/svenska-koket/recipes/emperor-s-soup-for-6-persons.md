## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters strong broth
- salt
- white pepper
- cayenne
- sugar
- 1 part sherry
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and flour together.
2. Add the broth while stirring constantly and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes.
3. Season the soup with wine and spices.
4. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl and pour the soup over while whisking.
5. It can be served with or without poached eggs.
