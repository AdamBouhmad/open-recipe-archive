## Ingredients

- Pears
- Thin sugar syrup (2 kg sugar per liter of water)

## Directions

1. Blanch the pears in thin sugar syrup (2 kg sugar per liter of water) before drying.
2. Dry them in the same way as apples.
3. Small pears can be dried whole.
