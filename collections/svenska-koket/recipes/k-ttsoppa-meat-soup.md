## Ingredients

- 2 kg marrow pipe, brisket, or neck
- 3 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1 leek
- 1 small celeriac
- 1 small bunch parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water
2. Chop it into two pieces and place it in cold water
3. Bring quickly to a boil, skim well, and salt
4. Let the soup cook slowly with a tight-fitting lid for 3–4 hours
5. After about 2 hours, add the sautéed vegetables
6. When the soup is done, cut the vegetables into pieces and serve them in the soup bowl
7. Finally, add the chopped parsley, bring the broth to a boil, and pour it into the soup bowl
8. The meat can either be served in the soup cut into pieces, or separately with horseradish sauce
