## Ingredients

- 8 hg pork fillet
- 4 tbsp chopped parsley
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- 2 tsk salt (10 gr)
- 1/4 tsk white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter for frying (60 gr)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 gr)
- 4 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. The fillets, weighing 2-4 hg each, are trimmed, dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and cut open lengthwise
2. Clean the parsley, rinse it, wring it hard in a cloth, chop it and work it into the butter
3. Fill the fillets with the parsley butter, sew them neatly with a larding needle and rub with seasoning
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the fillets, brown well on all sides and then let them cook with a tight lid until tender, or about 1/2 hour
5. Cut and plate as in the previous recipe
6. Fry the flour well with the butter in the pot, add the cream, and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes, taste and strain over the meat
7. Serve with browned potatoes and aaladers
