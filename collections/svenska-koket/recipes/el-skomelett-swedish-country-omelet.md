## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons wheat flour
- 4 deciliters sour cream
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped leek
- 3 hectograms salted side pork

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with salt and flour for 10 minutes, then add the milk and leek.
2. Cut the pork into thin slices and lightly fry in a hot pan.
3. Remove it, pour off the fat and clean the pan well with white paper so no salt remains.
4. Pour the fat back into the pan, which is again placed over heat to warm up, then pour in the egg mixture and place the pork on top.
5. When the omelet is fried on one side, turn it onto a lid, flip it back into the pan and fry on the other side.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish. Smoked ham can also be used.
