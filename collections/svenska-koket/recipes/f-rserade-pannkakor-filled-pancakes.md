## Ingredients

- Pancakes: 60 gr. flour
- 1 kkp cream
- 1 kkp milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- Forcemeat: 150 gr. boneless chicken or veal
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1/2 msk. flour (5 gr.)
- 1 1/2–2 dcl. thick cream
- 1 truffle

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl, add the cream, milk, egg and salt. Whip the batter until smooth and lump-free and let it stand 2 hours to rise.
2. Prepare the forcemeat as in recipe 318.
3. Heat the pancake pan slowly, grease with butter and from the batter fry 3 pancakes, which are placed on a sheet and spread with a thin layer of forcemeat. They are stacked on top of each other, the top layer should be forcemeat, which is sprinkled with finely chopped truffle.
4. Place the arrangement in ordinary oven heat 10–15 minutes or until the forcemeat is baked, which is tested with a whisk stick.
5. Remove the cake with a measure or glass about 2 1/2 cm. in diameter. The small cakes are placed in the soup bowl and when the soup is to be served, the boiling consommé is poured over.
