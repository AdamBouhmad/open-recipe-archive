## Ingredients

- 4 small salted fat herrings
- 1 teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Let the herring soak in water for 12 hours, changing the water once.
2. Skin, clean, and rinse the herring, then dry with a fish towel.
3. Rub with pepper and let sit for 1 hour.
4. Dry well again and cut into 1½ cm wide pieces crosswise.
5. Arrange on a platter and garnish with parsley or dill.
6. Serve with fresh or stewed potatoes or stewed green peas as a breakfast or main course.
