## Ingredients

- 3 willow grouse
- 1 hg. lard in 3 slices
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 dcl. broth
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Handle and prepare as partridge, but roast for approximately 1½ hours to make them tender.
2. May also be roasted as pheasant in a previously prepared sauce (see no. 614), and this sauce is mixed with a little thick cream and cold butter before serving.
3. Split the grouse in half, garnish with browned potatoes and salad or parsley.
4. Serve with currant jelly and green salad with lemon.
