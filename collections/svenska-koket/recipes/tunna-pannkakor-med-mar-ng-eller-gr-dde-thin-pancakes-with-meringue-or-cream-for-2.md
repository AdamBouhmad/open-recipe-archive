## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 3 dcl. cream
- 160-180 gr. flour
- 7 dcl. milk
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.)
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.) for the pan
- 2 kkp. jam (raspberry or strawberry)
- 3 egg whites
- 150 gr. powdered sugar or 3 dcl. thick cream and 1/2 msk. sprinkling sugar (8 gr.)
- 50 gr. sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with a steel whisk.
2. Pour in the cream.
3. Whisk in the flour, which should preferably be sifted.
4. Add the milk.
5. Add the salt and butter.
6. Let the batter stand for 2 hours.
7. Heat a pancake pan slowly.
8. Melt the butter in a water bath and brush the pan with it.
9. Stir the batter well.
10. Cook thin pancakes and layer them on a hot plate.
11. Coat each pancake thinly with some of the jam, which has been warmed in a water bath.
12. Let the pancakes cool.
13. Cut into 12 pieces.
14. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
15. Carefully fold in the sugar.
16. Spread the meringue over the pancakes.
17. Sprinkle with the blanched and finely chopped almonds.
18. Place the cake in gentle oven heat until the meringue is half dry and has a nice light yellow color.
