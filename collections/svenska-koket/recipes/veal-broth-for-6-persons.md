## Ingredients

- 1 veal shin
- 1.5 kg. veal shoulder or brisket
- 4 liters water
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 1 carrot
- 1 small celery
- 0.5 leek

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut into small pieces.
2. Place it in the cold water with salt, bring to a boil and skim well.
3. Add the cleaned vegetables, let the broth simmer for 3-4 hours, and then clarify in the usual way.
4. If a finer broth is desired, omit 1 kg. of the veal and use 1 hen instead.
