## Ingredients

- 8 hg. ox liver
- 3 hg. pork
- 1 msk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 3 msk. flour
- 2 kkp water
- 2 kkp milk

## Directions

1. Wash the liver and let it soak in water for 1/4 hour.
2. Then dry it well, and remove the membranes.
3. Slice the liver, and roll these in a mixture of flour, salt and white pepper.
4. Scrape the pork belly, remove the rind, and cut the pork into slices and fry in a hot pan; brown the liver slices in the fat.
5. When they are browned, pour water and milk on, and let the liver fry slowly for 1/2 hour, or until it feels tender.
6. Place it on a platter, arrange the pork slices around it, and pour the sauce over.
