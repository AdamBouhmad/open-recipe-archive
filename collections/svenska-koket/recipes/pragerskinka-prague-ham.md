## Ingredients

- 1 lightly smoked ham, about 5 kg
- 2 bottles Rhine wine
- water

## Directions

1. Let the ham soak in plenty of cold water for 1 day.
2. Brush the rind thoroughly with a soft brush and dry the ham with a cloth.
3. Place it in a pot that is not wider than necessary so the ham fits snugly.
4. Add the wine and enough water so it reaches level with the meat.
5. Cook the ham as in recipe no. 556, then place it in a deep dish.
6. Pour the cooking liquid over it and let the ham cool in the liquid.
7. Serve with the rind on and carve at the table.
8. Serve with red cabbage or other vegetables and potatoes.
