## Ingredients

- 2 kg Dover sole
- 1 1/2 msk salt
- 1 egg
- 1/2 kkp crushed hardtack
- 3 msk butter
- Maître-d'hôtel butter: 150 gr butter, 1 msk chopped parsley, 1 tsk lemon juice, 1/2 tsk salt, 1/4 tsk white pepper
- Sauce: 1 msk chopped parsley, juice of 1/2 lemon, 1 kkp stock

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the fish thoroughly.
2. Make a deep cut from the head to the tail, and remove the backbone from top and bottom.
3. Salt the fish, dip it in the beaten egg and crushed hardtack, then fry it golden-brown in butter.
4. While the fish is cooking, prepare the maître-d'hôtel butter: rinse and finely chop the parsley and mix it thoroughly with the butter, seasonings, and lemon juice.
5. Once the fish is cooked, remove the backbone and place the maître-d'hôtel butter in the opening.
6. Place the fish on a warm serving dish, add the chopped parsley, lemon juice, and stock to the cooking butter and pour it over the fish.
