## Ingredients

- 12 apples
- 2 cups caster sugar
- 6 cups water

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the blossom end, scrape the stems and reduce them to half their length.
2. Boil a clear syrup from the sugar and water.
3. Place the apples in it and let them cook until they are clear and tender, then place them on a jelly dish.
4. Reduce the syrup by boiling and pour it over the apples.
5. Serve preferably cold with whipped cream.
6. Note: The apples can also be cooked unpeeled. Rinse them then, dry them and prick with a silver fork.
