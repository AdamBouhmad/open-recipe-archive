## Ingredients

- 210 grams sweet almonds
- 12 grams bitter almonds
- 250 grams granulated sugar
- zest of 1 lemon
- 10 tablespoons clarified butter
- 5 eggs
- 10 tablespoons thick cream
- 50 grams crushed rusks
- 1/2 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, grind or pass them through an almond mill, and pound with the sugar.
2. Clarify the butter, let it cool, and stir until it becomes white.
3. Add the egg yolks along with the cream, grated lemon zest, and almond paste, and beat the mixture for half an hour.
4. Sift in the crushed rusks and fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-sprinkled pan or mold and bake in a not-too-hot oven.
6. The cake is ready when it has risen and then fallen again.
