## Ingredients

- 2 liters beautiful green tomatoes
- 1 liter water
- 4 parts wine vinegar
- For the brine: 1 kg granulated sugar
- 2 parts wine vinegar
- 8 parts water
- 10 cloves
- 1 piece cinnamon, 1 decimeter long

## Directions

1. Dry the tomatoes with a damp cloth, prick with a silver fork, and remove the flower end.
2. Cook the vinegar and water and pour everything over the tomatoes, which then let stand for one day.
3. Cook a clear brine from the sugar, vinegar, water and spices.
4. Place the tomatoes in it and let them cook slowly until soft.
5. Remove them and place in glass jars.
6. If the brine is not sufficiently syrupy, cook it a while longer and then pour it over the fruit.
