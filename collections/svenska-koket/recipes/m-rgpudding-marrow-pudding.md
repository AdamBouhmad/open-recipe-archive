## Ingredients

- 1 hg. ox marrow
- 30 gr. kidney fat
- 50 gr. bread crumbs
- 1/2 dcl. milk
- 1 hg. caster sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 hg. preserved fruit, in fine dice
- 45 gr. sultana raisins
- 30 gr. cleaned malaga raisins
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. wheat flour (20 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the marrow and fat well, wrap in cloth, place in a pot, melt in water bath, and let the fat cool.
2. Weigh bread crumbs, soak in milk and squeeze out.
3. Work the fat well with the sugar, the squeezed-out bread, the whipped eggs, egg yolks, diced fruit, the cleaned and rinsed sultana raisins and the cleaned, rinsed, split and squeezed-out malaga raisins.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered and floured smooth ring mold and bake in water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
5. Serve warm, poured over with warm wine sauce (see no. 457), mixed with rum, or with flaming sauce (see no. 465), which is lit when the pudding is brought to the table.
