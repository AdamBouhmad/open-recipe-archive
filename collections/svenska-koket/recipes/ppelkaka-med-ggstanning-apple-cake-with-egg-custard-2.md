## Ingredients

- 12 slices good white bread
- 1 egg
- 2 1/2 dcl. good milk
- 2 1/2 msk. butter (50 gr.)
- 4 dcl. apple sauce or apple compote
- 40 gr. sultana raisins
- 3 eggs
- 1 tsk. sugar (5 gr.)
- 3 dcl. thin cream
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Dip the bread slices in a mixture of the whipped egg and milk, fry in the butter and lay alternately with the apple sauce or compote and the raisins (cleaned and rinsed), if desired, in a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pie mold.
2. For the egg custard, whisk together the eggs and cream, season with the sugar and pour over the pudding.
3. Bake in ordinary oven heat until it has a nice golden brown color, or about 1/2 hour.
4. Serve cooled with whipped cream, if desired.
