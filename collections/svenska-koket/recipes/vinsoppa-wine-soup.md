## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters water
- 1 lemon
- 150 grams sugar
- 3 parts French wine
- 1.5 tablespoons potato flour
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the water for 1.5 hours together with thinly sliced lemon peel, well-pressed lemon juice, and some of the sugar.
2. Strain out the lemon peel and pour the soup back into the pot.
3. When it boils again, thicken it with potato flour mixed into the wine.
4. Just before serving, whisk the egg yolks together with 2 tablespoons of sugar in a soup bowl.
5. Pour the soup in little by little, whisking constantly, and serve foaming with biscuits.
