## Ingredients

- 4–5 good herring roe
- 1 litre skimmed milk
- 2 dcl. finely crushed rusks
- 5 dcl. cream milk
- 80 gr. butter, clarified
- 5 eggs
- 1 tsp. sugar
- a little nutmeg
- ½ tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mould
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mould

## Directions

1. Rinse the herring roe well and let soak in the skimmed milk for about 2 hours.
2. Wrap in a fish towel, chop finely and blend with the finely crushed rusks, cream milk, clarified butter, well-beaten eggs and spices.
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie mould and bake in the oven until it has taken on a beautiful, golden-brown colour, or about 45 minutes.
4. Serve with clarified butter as a breakfast dish.
