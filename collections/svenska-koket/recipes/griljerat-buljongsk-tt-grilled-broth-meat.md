## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg broth meat, preferably brisket
- 8 anchovies
- 2 eggs
- 3 dcl breadcrumbs
- 5 tbsp butter (100 g) for frying

## Directions

1. Cut the meat, which should not be overcooked, into beautiful, even slices.
2. Clean the anchovies, remove the bones, chop finely.
3. Whisk the eggs well together with the anchovies.
4. Dip the meat slices in the mixture, turn in breadcrumbs and fry beautifully brown in butter.
5. Arrange on a warm platter, garnish with parsley and serve with clarified butter and stewed vegetables or potato purée.
