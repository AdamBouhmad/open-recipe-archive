## Ingredients

- 3 grouse
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ½ liter broth or water
- 1 kg marrow bone
- 1 kg neck
- 1 calf's foot
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2½ liters water
- 2 eggs
- 10 sheets gelatin
- mayonnaise sauce, poached eggs, cooked beets or oysters

## Directions

1. Roast the grouse whole and let them cool.
2. Cook broth from the ox meat and calf's foot, which when done should make 1½ liters.
3. Strain the broth, skim it well, and return it to the heat.
4. Add the gelatin dissolved in a little water, and when the broth is warm, whisk in the eggs, and bring the broth to a good boil while stirring, then strain through a napkin or cheesecloth wrung out in hot water.
5. Pour half of the clear aspic onto a flat plate to set.
6. From the cooled roasted grouse, cut 4–5 slices on each side of the breastbone.
7. Place these slices on the set aspic, pour the remaining aspic over, and set everything out to set.
8. Separate the meat slices with a knife from the rest.
