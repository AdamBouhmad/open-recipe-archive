## Ingredients

- 0.75 liters strong, clear broth
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon cognac
- 0.25 part sherry
- salt
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Bring the broth to a boil and clarify it, if necessary, with egg white.
2. Color it with soy sauce and season with cognac, sherry and spices.
3. Serve with toasted bread.
