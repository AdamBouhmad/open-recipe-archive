## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites
- juice of 1/8 lemon
- 200 grams granulated sugar
- 100 grams sweet almonds
- 10 pistachio almonds for garnish

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and cut them into long strips, drying them very well
2. Stir together the egg whites, sugar and lemon juice slowly with a wooden spoon in one direction for 1 hour
3. Gently fold in the almonds
4. On well-buttered or waxed baking sheets, place spoonfuls of the batter in pointed cakes and sprinkle with blanched and finely chopped pistachio almonds
5. Bake the cakes in very weak oven heat
