## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. semolina grains (152 gr.)
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.)
- 1 1/4 lit. milk
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- 2 tsk. sugar (10 gr.)
- 4 bitter almonds
- 10 sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Place the semolina grains in a sieve, pour cold water over them and let drain.
2. Melt half of the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk and bring to a boil, whisk in the grains, and let the porridge cook while whisking until it thickens, then move it to a lower heat and let simmer slowly for about 1 hour.
3. Taste the porridge with the seasonings, the scalded and ground almonds, and the rest of the butter. Serve with cream or milk.
