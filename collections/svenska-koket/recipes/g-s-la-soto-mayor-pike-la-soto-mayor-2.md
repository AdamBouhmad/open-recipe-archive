## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg pike
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped red onion
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 1/2 deciliters milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 g)
- 3 tablespoons wheat flour (30 g)
- 2 hectograms butter for frying

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike, see page 171; the backbone is removed so that the head and tail remain
2. Remove the gills and fins, rub the fish with salt and white pepper, place on a dish and sprinkle with the onion
3. Mix the lemon juice and oil well and pour over the fish; let rest 2 hours, turning 2 times; carefully remove the onion afterwards
4. Dissolve the salt in the milk, dip the fish in it and turn it in the flour
5. Heat the butter in a small roasting pan, place the pike in it, fry until nicely browned on both sides and place on absorbent paper so the butter drains off
6. Place on a napkin or pastry paper, garnish with fried parsley and nicely cut lemon wedges and serve with mushroom sauce and mashed potatoes
