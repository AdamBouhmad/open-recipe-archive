## Ingredients

- 2 deciliters semolina (152 grams)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 grams)
- 4 bitter almonds
- 10 sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Place the semolina in a sieve, rinse with cold water and drain. Melt half of the butter in a pot, pour in the milk and bring to a boil, whisk in the semolina, and let the porridge cook while whisking until thickened, then move to lower heat and let simmer slowly for about 1 hour. Season the porridge with the spices, the blanched and ground almonds, and the remaining butter. Serve with cream or milk.
