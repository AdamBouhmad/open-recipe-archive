## Ingredients

- 2 parts rice groats (2 hg.)
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 2 1/2 liters milk
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice groats, scald them in hot water, rinse with cold water and let them drain.
2. Melt half of the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk and bring to a boil, stir in the groats, and then let the porridge cook slowly until the groats are completely soft, or about 3 hours.
3. Stir the porridge often so that it does not burn, and thin with more milk if needed.
4. Season the porridge with salt and the remaining butter and serve with milk.
