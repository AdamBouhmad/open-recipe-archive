## Ingredients

- 2 kg. wheat flour
- 1 tsk. salt
- 1 lit. lukewarm milk
- 50 gr. pressed yeast
- 5 gr. saffron
- 1 msk. brandy
- 300 gr. butter or margarine
- 2-4 kkp granulated sugar
- 2 kkp raisins
- 1 egg for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almonds for brushing
- ½ kkp pearl sugar for brushing

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm milk.
2. Sift the wheat flour with the salt, set aside 3-4 kkp for rolling out, and pour the rest into a bowl.
3. Add the lukewarm milk with vigorous stirring, mix in the yeast, and beat the dough until smooth and glossy.
4. Set it in a warm place to rise to double size.
5. Dry the saffron in a warm oven, pound it, and dissolve it in brandy and work it into the dough together with the beaten eggs, the butter beaten to white foam, the sugar, and the cleaned and rinsed raisins.
6. Let the dough rise again and shape it into wreaths or braids, which are placed on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets.
7. When the breads have risen, brush them with beaten eggs and sprinkle with scalded and chopped sweet almonds mixed with pearl sugar.
8. Bake in good oven heat.
