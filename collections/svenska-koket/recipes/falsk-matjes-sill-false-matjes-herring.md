## Ingredients

- 2 kg salt fat herring
- 2 liters un-skimmed milk
- 6 tablespoons caster sugar
- 1 dessert spoon saltpeter

## Directions

1. Pack the salted herring in a porcelain container.
2. Bring the milk to a boil with the sugar and crushed saltpeter.
3. When the milk is completely cold, pour it over the herring and it should stand well above it.
4. Let the herring lie in the milk for eight days, after which it is ready to be used.
5. It can be served smoked, fried in paper, or pickled.
