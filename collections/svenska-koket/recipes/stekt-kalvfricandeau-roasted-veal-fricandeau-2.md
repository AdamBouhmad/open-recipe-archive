## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless veal loin
- 2 hg lard, in 2 flat slices
- 1 carrot
- 1 small shallot onion
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 2 tbsp butter (40 g)
- 4 parsley stems
- 6 dcl good beef broth

## Directions

1. The meat is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, pounded with a damp meat mallet, larded with 1 hg of the lard, cut into fine strips, dipped in a mixture of salt and pepper, rubbed with the rest of the spices and tied so that it takes a nice shape.
2. The butter is browned in a suitable pot, which should not be larger than to just fit the meat. The meat is placed in it and browned nicely on all sides; when it is half browned, the prepared carrot, cut into slices, the peeled onion and parsley are added.
3. When the meat is browned, it is placed on the rest of the lard, half of the broth is added and the meat is allowed to cook for 1/2 hour, during which it is basted and turned once.
4. The rest of the broth is added and the meat is allowed to cook until it is so tender that it can be cut with a fork.
5. It is carefully removed and cut with a sharp knife into thin slices, which are arranged so that the roast retains its shape. Placed on a warm platter, garnished with mixed vegetables or vegetable patties and salad or parsley.
6. Served with reduced sauce, seasoned with cream and salads.
