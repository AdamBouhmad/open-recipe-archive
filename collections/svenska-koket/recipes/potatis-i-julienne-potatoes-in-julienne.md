## Ingredients

- 1 litre potatoes
- 1 kg fat
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. The potatoes should be oblong and of even size.
2. Wash them, brush them well clean, peel thinly and cut them into very thin strips, which are placed in water as they are cut.
3. Dry them well in a cloth, cook as in the previous recipe and they should be quite crispy.
4. Serve with poultry and game.
