## Ingredients

- 2 kg. pike-perch
- 2 tbsp. salt
- 2 tsk. vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Scale the pike-perch, clean it, wash it well, and trim the fins.
2. Rub it with the vinegar and salt and let it lie for a while.
3. Place the fish on the fire in hot, but not boiling water (short broth), skim well when the broth comes to a boil, and let it simmer slowly at the side of the fire until the fins loosen easily.
4. Place the fish immediately on the serving platter and garnish with parsley and lemon.
5. Serve with clarified butter, caper sauce, finer fish sauce or Dutch sauce.
