## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fat herrings
- 1 tablespoon table oil

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water for 12 hours.
2. Skin if desired, clean, rinse well, and dry with a fish towel.
3. Brush with the table oil, place in a double broiler that can be turned, and broil over coals on both sides.
4. Arrange on a warm platter, garnish with parsley, and serve with boiled unpeeled potatoes or unpeeled potatoes baked in the oven as a breakfast dish.
