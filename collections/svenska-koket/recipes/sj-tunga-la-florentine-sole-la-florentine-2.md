## Ingredients

- 3 sole, approximately 5 hg. each
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter for cooking
- 4 1/2 dcl. fish stock
- 3 hg. spinach
- 3 tablespoon butter (60 gr.)
- 4 dcl. hollandaise sauce
- 12 shrimp tails for garnish
- 2–3 slices truffle
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the sole as in the previous recipe.
2. Blanch the spinach in lightly salted water, let it drain, wring it in a cloth, chop it coarsely, and cook in butter for approximately 20 minutes.
3. Spread the spinach on the bottom of an oblong silver dish.
4. Place the fillets on top.
5. Pour 4 dcl. hollandaise sauce over and garnish the arrangement with shrimp tails, truffle cut into slices, and parsley.
6. Serve with small, boiled, mealy potatoes.
