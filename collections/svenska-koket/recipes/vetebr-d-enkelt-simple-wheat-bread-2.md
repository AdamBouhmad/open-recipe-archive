## Ingredients

- 850 gr. prime wheat flour
- 1/2 liter lukewarm milk
- 30 gr. pressed yeast
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 40 gr. butter or margarine
- 40 gr. sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as described (a little of the flour is reserved for dusting) and allow to rise to double its size.
2. Cream the butter with the sugar until white and fluffy and work into the risen dough, together with more flour if needed.
3. Work the dough on the work surface and divide it into 4 equal parts, which are rolled out into oblong buns or round cakes, which are well scored.
4. Place the breads on buttered baking sheets, allow to rise, brush with cold milk and bake in a hot oven until they have a lovely color and feel light.
5. The dough may also be baked out into rings, swirls, and braids, which are brushed with egg and sprinkled with sugar when they have risen.
