## Ingredients

- 1 oval slice of sponge cake, 1 cm thick
- Marmalade or sugar glaze
- 2 egg yolks
- 250 grams icing sugar
- 5 egg whites
- Finished vanilla ice

## Directions

1. Fasten a 1 cm thick oval slice of sponge cake to a silver platter with marmalade or sugar glaze.
2. Place the platter on ice.
3. Prepare a meringue mixture: whisk the egg yolks and two-thirds of the sugar for 20 minutes.
4. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and continue whisking for 15 minutes.
5. Carefully add the remaining sugar, then fold in the whisked egg yolks last.
6. Arrange the finished frozen vanilla ice high and oval on the sponge cake using a silver spoon dipped in warm water.
7. Spread a portion of the meringue smoothly over the ice.
8. Pipe the remaining meringue through a fluted piping bag in beautiful patterns over it.
9. Immediately place the platter in a roasting pan with crushed ice and coarse salt.
10. Place in a very hot oven for about 2-3 minutes until the meringue turns golden.
11. Dust with sugar and serve immediately with warm chocolate sauce if desired.
