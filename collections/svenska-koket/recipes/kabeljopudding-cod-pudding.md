## Ingredients

- 4 hg. kabeljo
- 2 dcl. risgryn (2 hg.)
- 1 1/2 lit. mjölk
- 3 msk. kallt smör (60 gr.)
- 2-3 ägg
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. vitpeppar
- 2 tsk. socker (10 gr.)
- 1/2 msk. smör (10 gr.) till formen
- 3 msk. stötta skorpor till formen

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the cod as in recipe 393, let it cool and chop finely or pass 2 times through a meat grinder.
2. Cook the rice grains and prepare and serve the pudding as in the preceding recipe.
3. Any kind of fish, boiled or fried, can be used for this pudding.
