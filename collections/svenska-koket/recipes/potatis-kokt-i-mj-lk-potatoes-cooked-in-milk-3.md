## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 5–6 deciliters good milk
- salt
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel small, evenly-sized potatoes raw and form them into oblong shapes with a vegetable knife.
2. Melt the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk and bring to a boil.
3. Add the potatoes and let them simmer slowly with a lid in the oven until soft, at which point the sauce should be quite thin.
4. Season the stew with salt and parsley if desired.
5. Serve with salted meat and boiled sausage.
