## Ingredients

- 2 large calf tongues
- 3 tablespoons salt
- water
- 1 carrot
- 1 piece celery
- 1 leek
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2-3 cups of stock
- 1½ tablespoons chopped capers
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 eggs (for breading)
- 2 cups crushed breadcrumbs (for breading)
- ¼ kg cream (for frying)

## Directions

1. Let the tongues soak in cold water for 1 hour.
2. Then scald them and scrape them well.
3. Put them in boiling water and skim carefully when the stock comes to a boil.
4. Add salt and root vegetables, and let the tongues simmer slowly until tender.
5. When done, remove the skin immediately and cut the meat into fine cubes.
6. Melt butter and flour together, add the stock in which the tongues cooked, add the tongue and finely chopped capers, and let the stew cook for 10 minutes, then taste for seasoning.
7. Spread the mixture on a flat dish to cool, and form small sugar-loaf-shaped croquettes from it.
8. Roll them in crushed breadcrumbs, dip in whisked egg, and roll again in crushed breadcrumbs, then let them dry for ½ hour.
9. Heat the cream, cook the croquettes golden brown in it, and place them on brown paper to drain.
10. Garnish with parsley and serve with mushroom or brown sauce.
