## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 dcl water
- 150 gr flour
- 70 gr melted butter
- 4 dcl thick cream
- 1 tsk sugar (5 gr)
- 1/2 msk butter for the iron (10 gr)

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg and water well, sift the flour and whisk it in, and let the batter stand 1 hour to swell.
2. Add the cooled butter together with the cream that has been whipped stiff with sugar.
3. Grease the waffle iron with butter for the first waffles only.
4. Bake the waffles and serve as in the preceding recipe.
