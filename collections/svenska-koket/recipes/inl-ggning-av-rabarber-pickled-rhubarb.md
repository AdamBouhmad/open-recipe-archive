## Ingredients

- Freshly cut rhubarb
- Fresh water

## Directions

1. Peel the rhubarb, dry it, and cut into pieces 2 cm long
2. Place the pieces in bottles with wide necks
3. Pour fresh water over them
4. Cork and seal the bottles with resin
