## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 250 grams sugar
- peel and juice of 1 lemon
- 11 leaves gelatin
- 4 tablespoons warm water
- 1/2 liter thick cream

## Directions

1. Stir egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes and mix with the finely grated lemon peel and lemon juice.
2. Rinse the gelatin with cold water, cut into pieces and dissolve in the warm water.
3. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form; do the same with the egg whites.
4. Stir the gelatin first into the egg mixture, then the cream and finally the egg white foam.
5. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed form sprinkled with sugar and let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours.
6. When the fromage is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass dish with paper.
7. Serve with almond slivers.
