## Ingredients

- 185 gr. butter
- 2 eggs
- 125 gr. fine sugar
- 90 gr. wheat flour
- 15 gr. pearl sugar to strew over the pastries
- 15 gr. sweet almond to strew over the pastries

## Directions

1. The butter is melted, allowed to cool and stirred until it is white and fluffy.
2. Thereafter the eggs are stirred in, one at a time, as well as the sugar and the mass is stirred for 10 minutes.
3. Last the sifted flour is added.
4. A waffle iron is heated slightly, 1/2 tablespoon of the batter is placed in each mold and stirred, so that the cakes become even and thin, after which they are sprinkled with sugar and finely chopped almond.
5. The pastries are baked in not too warm an oven until they are nicely golden brown, when they are taken out and bent at once over a narrow rod.
