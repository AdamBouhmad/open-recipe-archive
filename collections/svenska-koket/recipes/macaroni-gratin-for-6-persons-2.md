## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hectograms good macaroni
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 1 tbsp. salt
- Béchamel sauce: 80 gr. butter, 1 finely chopped red onion, 50 gr. lean veal (in fine cubes), 40 gr. flour, 8 deciliters boiling milk, salt, white pepper, a little grated nutmeg
- For the form: 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.), 3 tbsp. grated cheese, 3 tbsp. ground bread crusts

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into 4 cm. long pieces, place in boiling salted water and cook for approximately 20 minutes.
2. Drain in a colander, rinse with cold water and let drain.
3. For the sauce: fry the onion, meat and flour (which should be dried in the oven) in butter for approximately 10 minutes without browning.
4. Add the boiling milk, add the spices and let the sauce cook while stirring for 15 minutes, then strain through cloth and taste.
5. Butter an ovenproof form and sprinkle with ground bread crusts.
6. Layer the macaroni alternately with the sauce and grated cheese.
7. The top layer should be sauce, sprinkled with cheese and bread crusts.
8. Gratinée the dish in a warm oven for approximately 15 minutes.
9. Serve as an intermediate course.
