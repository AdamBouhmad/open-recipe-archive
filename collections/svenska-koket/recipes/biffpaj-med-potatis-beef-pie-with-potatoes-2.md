## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless French cut or inner loin
- 2 tsp salt
- ¾ tsp white pepper
- 2 tbsp butter
- 125 gr butter for dough
- 125 gr flour for dough
- 2 small red onions
- 1 liter potatoes
- 2 parts weak broth
- ½ part white French wine
- 3-4 tbsp water

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as a simple butter dough.
2. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into slices across the grain. Pound these, season and brown quickly in the butter.
3. Peel the onion, cut it into slices and brown it.
4. In a heatproof form of earthenware or porcelain, place the steaks in layers with the onion and, if desired, with peeled potatoes cut into slices.
5. Deglaze the frying pan with the broth, and pour this together with the wine over the meat.
6. Lay three-quarters of the butter dough over the pie as a lid. Using a larding needle, make holes in a few places in the dough so that gases formed during baking can escape.
7. Brush the pie lightly with egg white. Roll out the remaining butter dough thinly and cut it into narrow strips, and garnish the pie with these in a lattice pattern, or garnish it with small leaves cut out of the butter dough.
8. Brush over with beaten egg or water and bake in a hot oven for approximately 1 hour.
