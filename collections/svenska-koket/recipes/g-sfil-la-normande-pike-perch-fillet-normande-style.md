## Ingredients

- 2 pike perch, 5 hg each
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp butter (40 gr) for cooking
- fish stock
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 2 tbsp butter (40 gr) for stewing
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr)
- 4 dcl fish stock
- 1 hg shrimp
- 1 can mussels (1/4 liter)
- salt, white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 1.5 tbsp butter (30 gr) for sauce
- 4 tbsp flour (40 gr)
- 4 dcl fish stock
- 2 tbsp mushroom liquor
- 2 tbsp mussel liquor
- 2 tbsp thick cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 30 gr butter
- salt, cayenne
- 6 slices truffle for garnish
- 12 crayfish or shrimp tails
- 6 mushroom caps
- 6-8 small half-moons of puff pastry
- parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Scale the pike perch, clean and cut into fillets, removing backbone and all small bones.
2. Place fillets skin-side down on a cutting board and cut the meat from the skin with a pointed knife.
3. Cut fillets lengthwise and then across in half.
4. Gently pound with a damp mallet so they resemble sole fillets.
5. Rub with salt and let rest on top of each other for 1 hour.
6. Cook good fish stock from head, bones, and skin.
7. Cook fillets in this stock after drying them again, as in recipe n:o 328.
8. Arrange with slotted spoon in a ring on an oblong dish, preferably silver.
9. Place dish in the front of the oven for a few minutes and carefully pour off the liquid that collects.
10. For the stewing: cut mushrooms (excluding 6 caps for garnish) into smaller pieces.
11. Sauté cut mushrooms in butter until clear, press up with a spoon and sauté flour with butter for 2 minutes.
12. Dilute with fish stock and let sauce boil 5 minutes.
13. Add cleaned shrimp and mussels, let stewing come to a boil, season with spices, finish with whisked egg yolk and let simmer.
14. For the sauce: sauté butter and flour for 2 minutes, dilute with fish stock, mushroom liquor, mussel liquor and cream and let boil 5 minutes.
15. Add whisked egg yolks and cold butter, let simmer while whisking and season with spices.
16. Place stewing in center of dish, pour sauce over and garnish beautifully with truffle, crayfish tails, mushroom caps sautéed in butter, puff pastry half-moons and parsley.
17. Serve with small round boiled potatoes, cut out with a melon baller.
