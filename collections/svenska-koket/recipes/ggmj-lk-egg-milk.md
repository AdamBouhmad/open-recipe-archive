## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 50 gr. caster sugar
- 2 lit. milk
- lemon peel or vanilla
- 1/2 tbsp. potato flour (8 gr.)
- salt

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks in a soup bowl with the sugar until the mixture becomes white and frothy.
2. Boil the milk with a little lemon peel or vanilla to taste and thicken it with the potato flour, stirred into 1/2 cup cold milk.
3. Let the milk boil for 2 minutes, taste for seasoning, and pour into the soup bowl while whisking steadily.
4. Serve with rusks.
