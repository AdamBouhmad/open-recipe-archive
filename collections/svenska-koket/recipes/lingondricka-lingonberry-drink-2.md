## Ingredients

- 10 liters lingonberries
- 5 liters water
- 50 grams tartaric acid
- 1 deciliter warm water
- 2.25 kilograms granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, crush them with a wooden pestle, and place them in a crock.
2. Dissolve the tartaric acid in the warm water and add it together with the cold water.
3. Stir the mass well, cover it, and let it stand for 2 days, stirring it occasionally.
4. Pour the berries into sieve cloths and let them drain as long as any juice drips from the cloth, approximately 1.5 days.
5. Place the sugar in the juice, then stir slowly for 1 hour.
6. Treat the juice as in the previous recipe.
7. When serving, dilute with water according to taste.
8. It is a good and healthful drink, particularly suitable for meals.
9. From the remaining pulp, a simpler juice can be made by adding 1.5 liters of water, 15 grams of citric acid, and 6 hectograms of sugar, which is used first.
