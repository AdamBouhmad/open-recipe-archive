## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. water (scarcely)
- 250 gr. fine butter
- 1 beaten egg
- chopped sweet almonds
- granulated sugar
- 2 dcl. stiffly whipped, extra thick cream
- vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough using the same proportions and method as the previous recipe.
2. Roll out into an oblong cake of 1/2 cm. thickness and cut with a pastry wheel into 1 1/2 cm. wide strips, which are wound tightly (one edge should overlap the other by 3 mm.) around a cream horn mold.
3. Brush the pastries on top with beaten egg, sprinkle with finely chopped sweet almonds and granulated sugar, and bake in a not-too-hot oven.
4. When the pastries are baked through, remove them and take off the cream horn molds immediately.
5. After the pastries have cooled, fill them through a pastry bag with 2 dcl. stiffly whipped, extra thick cream, flavored with vanilla sugar if desired.
6. This recipe makes approximately 12 sharpshooter pastries.
