## Ingredients

- 9 veal cutlets
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 3 decilitres finely ground rusk
- 5 tablespoons butter (100 g)

## Directions

1. Dry the cutlet meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water.
2. Cut out the cutlets so that one rib remains in each. Remove the outer membrane and backbone, scrape the meat from the tip of the ribs and trim these even.
3. Pound the cutlets with a damp meat mallet, shape and rub in with a mixture of salt and pepper.
4. They can be fried with or without breading. If they are to be breaded, dip them in the beaten eggs and turn them in the crushed rusks.
5. Heat a frying pan, lightly brown the butter in it, place the cutlets in and fry approximately 7 minutes on each side, or until they are beautifully brown and tender.
6. Place them on a warm platter and pour the browned butter over them.
7. If desired, put paper frills on the bones.
8. Serve with fried potatoes or vegetables. If the cutlets are large, one can be enough for each person and the other ingredients are reduced accordingly.
