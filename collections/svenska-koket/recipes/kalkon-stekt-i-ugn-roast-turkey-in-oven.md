## Ingredients

- 1 turkey cock approximately 5 kg
- 1 tbsp salt (15 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1 1/2 hg lard, in 1 slice
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 liter broth

## Directions

1. Pluck the turkey immediately after slaughter and let it hang for several days so the meat becomes tender
2. Remove it, rinse, dry with a cloth, rub inside and outside with a mixture of salt and pepper, truss it, and cover the breast with a large slice of lard, which is tied fast with fine twine
3. Grease a long pan with butter large enough for the bird, place the bird on it with breast up, add the giblets and half of the broth
4. Place the turkey in a hot oven and let it roast until tender, approximately 2-3 hours, basting it approximately every 10 minutes and adding more broth as needed
5. If the bird becomes too brown, cover it with butter-brushed paper
6. Strain the gravy, skim it, boil it down, taste it and serve as gravy, or prepare it as in recipe 429
7. Carve the turkey neatly, arrange it on a warm platter so it appears whole, garnish with salad, parsley and browned potatoes
8. Serve with green salad and sour salads
