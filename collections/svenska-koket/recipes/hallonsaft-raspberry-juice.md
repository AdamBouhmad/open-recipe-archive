## Ingredients

- 30 liters raspberries
- 1.5 liters water
- 0.5 kg sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Clean the raspberries. Place them with water and let them cook with stirring for about 15 minutes.
2. Pour the mass into cheesecloth and let it drain until the next day.
3. Place the juice with the sugar and let it cook with careful skimming until it is clear and glossy.
4. Bottle it hot or cold; cork the bottles and seal with resin.
