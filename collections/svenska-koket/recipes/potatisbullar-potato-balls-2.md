## Ingredients

- 1 liter cooked, leftover potato
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 1 egg yolk
- 1-2 tablespoons thick cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- a little nutmeg
- 1 part crushed rusks for coating
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g) for frying

## Directions

1. Grate the potato on a grater or press through a purée press.
2. Stir the butter until fluffy and blend well with the purée together with the egg yolk, cream and spices.
3. Using two spoons dipped in flour, form round balls, which are flattened, turned in crushed rusks and fried until nicely brown in the butter.
4. Serve with fried fish or meat.
5. The egg yolk can be omitted if desired.
