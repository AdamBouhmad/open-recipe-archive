## Ingredients

- Fresh mackerel
- Melted butter
- Finely chopped parsley
- Capers or chives sauce
- Browned butter
- Potato puree or stewed vegetables
- Oil

## Directions

1. Mackerel should be purchased freshly caught as it spoils very quickly.
2. It can be cooked like salmon and served warm with melted butter and finely chopped parsley, capers or chives sauce.
3. It can also be baked in the oven, served with browned butter and potato puree or stewed vegetables.
4. When smoked and preserved in oil, it is served at the smorgasbord.
