## Ingredients

- 6 large pork chops
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoon butter (80 g)
- 9 beautiful, tart apples
- 1 deciliter broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops as in the previous recipe, rub with the seasonings, and brown quickly on both sides in half of the butter.
2. Peel the apples and cut them into thin slices, which are placed on a buttered, ovenproof platter or preferably a silver one.
3. Place the half-fried chops on top and coat with the remaining butter, distributed in flakes.
4. Whisk the frying pan with the broth, and pour the sauce onto the platter, which is then placed in a warm oven.
5. Let the chops and apples cook there until they are tender.
6. Serve with boiled or fried potatoes.
