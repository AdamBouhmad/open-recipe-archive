## Ingredients

- 300 g cooked or roasted boneless meat
- 100 g fat, smoked bacon
- 1 red onion
- 150 g cooked, grated potato
- 1-2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 g) for the onion
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 egg
- 1 egg white
- 2 dcl crushed bread crusts
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)

## Directions

1. Chop the meat finely together with the bacon. Peel the onion, chop it finely, sauté it in butter and work it into the forcemeat with a chopping knife, together with the potato, spices, the whisked egg and egg yolk.
2. Shape oblong, 1 cm thick cutlets from the forcemeat, which are dipped in the whisked egg and egg white and turned in crushed bread crusts.
3. Fry them in butter over low heat until they are nicely golden brown, garnish with parsley and serve with stewed vegetables or macaroni.
