## Ingredients

- 2.5 hectograms spinach
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1.5 liters stock
- 2.5 deciliters thin cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 2-3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach, rinse it, wrap it in a cloth and chop it
2. Fry it in butter until it becomes clear, then add the flour and mix well with the butter
3. Add the boiling stock little by little, together with the cream, and let the soup cook with stirring for 10 minutes
4. Whisk the egg yolks in the soup bowl and pour in the soup while whisking
5. Serve with croutons (fried white bread slices)
