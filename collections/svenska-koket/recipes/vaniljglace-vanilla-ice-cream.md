## Ingredients

- 8 parts cream
- 75 g caster sugar
- 1/2 vanilla bean
- 4-5 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the cream with the vanilla and sugar until it has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor, then remove the vanilla.
2. Whisk the egg yolks in a bowl, and pour the boiled cream over while whisking strongly.
3. Pour the cream back into the saucepan and let it simmer while whisking until it thickens.
4. Remove from heat and continue whisking until the cream is completely cold.
5. Pour it into the ice cream mold and freeze.
