## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 5 dcl. fish stock or broth
- 1 dcl. thick cream
- 2 msk. chopped capers
- 1—2 msk. caper vinegar
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1—2 egg yolks
- 1/2 msk. cold butter (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are fried 2 minutes.
2. Fish stock and cream are added and the sauce is allowed to boil 5 minutes.
3. Capers and vinegar are added and the sauce is brought to a boil.
4. Season with spices and finish with the whisked egg yolk.
5. If desired, the cold butter is stirred in last.
6. Served mainly with fish but also with boiled veal or lamb breast.
