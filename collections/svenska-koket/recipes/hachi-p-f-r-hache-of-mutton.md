## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram mutton roast (leftovers)
- 1/4 Portuguese onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1.5-2 tablespoons flour
- 4 parts bouillon cooked on mutton bones
- pepper, salt
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes. Peel the onion, chop finely and fry in butter, stir in the flour, and add the bouillon together with the seasonings.
2. Let the sauce cook about 10 minutes, then add the meat. Finish the stew with the egg yolk and cream.
3. Garnish, if desired, with poached eggs.
