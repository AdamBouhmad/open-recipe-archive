## Ingredients

- 1 kg whole redcurrants
- 1 kg caster sugar
- 1 dcl water

## Directions

1. Rinse the berries, place them alternately with the sugar in a preserving pan, and add the water.
2. Let the whole come slowly to the boil, then place the pan to the side for a few minutes, whereafter the contents are brought to the boil again.
3. Repeat this 3 times.
4. The last time, pour the mass at once into coarse cheesecloth, and let the jelly run directly into warm jelly cups or jars, previously rinsed with brandy.
5. Bind the jars as soon as the jelly has set.
6. Lingonberry jelly is prepared in the same way.
7. If the berries are strained, the mass is cooked thereafter about 10 minutes and becomes an excellent fine and good marmalade.
