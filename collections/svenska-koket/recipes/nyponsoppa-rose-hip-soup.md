## Ingredients

- 1/2 lit. rose hips
- 2 lit. water
- 1 1/2-2 kkp. crushed sugar
- 25 gr. potato flour
- 75 gr. raisins or 1 hg. prunes
- 15 gr. sweet almond (15 pieces)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rose hips well in lukewarm water and let them drain.
2. Set them on to boil in the cold water and let them cook soft for about 1 1/2 hours; during this time whisk the soup often so that the rose hips fall apart.
3. Strain them, add the sugar, bring the soup to boil, skim it, and thicken it with the potato flour, stirred in 1/2 kkp cold water.
4. Let the soup cook 2 minutes, season it, and serve with the rinsed and cooked raisins or plums, the blanched and thinly sliced almond, and whipped cream.
