## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 2 1/2 liters milk
- 2 1/2 deciliters fine crushed barley groats (2 hectograms)
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a pot, pour in the milk and bring to a boil, add the rinsed groats under vigorous whisking so the groats do not lump. Let the porridge cook slowly with a tightly closed lid until the groats are completely tender, or 3-4 hours, stirring often so it does not burn. Thin with boiling milk if needed. Season with salt and serve with milk.
