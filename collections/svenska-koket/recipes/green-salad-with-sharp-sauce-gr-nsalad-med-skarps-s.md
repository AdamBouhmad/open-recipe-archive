## Ingredients

- 2 medium heads of lettuce
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 2 tsk. sugar (10 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1/2 tsk. dry or French mustard
- 1 1/2 msk. vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the sauce as in no. 450. If desired, do not whip the cream into foam.
2. Prepare the salad shortly before it is to be served.
3. Separate the lettuce heads, remove the stalks, rinse the leaves well and shake them in a cloth to dry them; cut the largest leaves into pieces.
4. Place the leaves in a salad bowl, pour the sauce over, and mix the salad gently.
5. Garnish with the hard-boiled egg white, cut with a vegetable knife into beautiful strips and serve with stuffed eggs and roasted meat.
