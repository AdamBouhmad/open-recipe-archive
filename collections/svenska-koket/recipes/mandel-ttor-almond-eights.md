## Ingredients

- 150 grams sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 6 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 25 grams sweet almonds for garnish

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and grate or grind them through an almond mill and pound together with the sugar in a mortar
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and work them in
3. Form small cakes from the almond mixture in the shape of the number eight
4. Insert a blanched sweet almond, cut in half lengthwise, into each loop
5. Place the cakes on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets and bake in weak oven heat
