## Ingredients

- 2.5 liters sugar peas
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 0.5 tablespoon chopped parsley
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the pea pods, rinse and place in boiling, salted water together with 0.5 tablespoon of the butter.
2. When tender, remove them.
3. Fry the remaining butter with the flour, add the broth in which the peas were cooked, and let the sauce boil for 10 minutes.
4. Add the pea pods and the chopped parsley, and season the stew with sugar and salt.
