## Ingredients

- Carl Johanssvampar (porcini)
- Butter
- Onion
- Parsley
- Broth
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pick the mushrooms when half-developed if possible, as they are much favored by insects
2. Clean the mushrooms (remove the pores from older specimens)
3. Rinse well and drain on cloth
4. Cut into strips or pieces and stew as mushrooms
5. Alternatively, stew in butter, onion, parsley and broth, then finish with egg yolks
