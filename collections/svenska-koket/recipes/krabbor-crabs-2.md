## Ingredients

- Large crabs
- Water
- Coarse salt
- Cress
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook crabs the same way as lobster, but allow longer cooking time, about 30 minutes until done.
2. The main meat is in the claws; the shell contains only a white, loose mass resembling fish roe.
3. Loosen the loose meat with a fork and chop it finely with an equal amount of cress.
4. Mix with a few drops of oil and vinegar.
5. Fill the mixture back into the crab shells, arrange on a plate, garnish with the claws and parsley.
6. Serve without sauce.
