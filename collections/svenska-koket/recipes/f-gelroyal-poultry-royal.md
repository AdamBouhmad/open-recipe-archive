## Ingredients

- 90 gr. cooked meat from chicken or game bird
- 3 tablespoons cold béchamel sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 gr.)
- a little white pepper or nutmeg
- 1-1 1/2 deciliters thick cream
- 1 egg
- 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The meat is cut into pieces and driven 5 times through a meat grinder.
2. It is then pounded together with the sauce in a stone mortar with a wooden pestle to a fine and smooth mass.
3. The seasonings and cream are added, a little at a time, and the mass is passed through a hair sieve.
4. The egg and egg yolks are whisked well and worked into the force meat by tablespoons, after which it is carefully tasted.
5. The royal is then baked and served as in the preceding recipe.
