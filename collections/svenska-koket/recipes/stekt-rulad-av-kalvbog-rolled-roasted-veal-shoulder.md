## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal shoulder
- 1 hg lard
- ½ tbsp salt
- ½ tsp white pepper
- 2 tbsp butter
- ¾ to 1 liter boiling water
- ½ tbsp butter for sauce
- 1½ tbsp flour for sauce
- meat gravy
- ½ part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the shoulder with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Carefully cut out the shoulder blade and also the hip.
3. Spread the meat out; cut the lard into thin slices and cover the veal with it.
4. Mix pepper and salt and sprinkle over the lard.
5. Roll up the meat and tie it very loosely with string.
6. Brown the butter in a long roasting pan, place the roll in it, and put it in the roasting oven to brown on all sides.
7. Once it has achieved a nice brown color, add the water.
8. The roll should roast an additional hour, during which it is basted with its gravy every ten minutes.
9. Remove the roll and cut it into thin slices and garnish with parsley.
10. Strain the meat gravy, skim it, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
