## Ingredients

- 425 grams sweet almonds
- 425 grams granulated sugar
- 3 egg whites
- wafer cookies

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them, and grate or press through an almond mill.
2. Pound them quite finely together with the sugar in a stone mortar, and fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Cut a round cake from wafer cookie, and spread half of the dough on it.
4. Press the remaining dough through a piping bag in squares over the cake, and place the cake on a well-cleaned baking sheet and bake in weak oven heat.
5. Fill the squares with variously colored fruit or jelly.
