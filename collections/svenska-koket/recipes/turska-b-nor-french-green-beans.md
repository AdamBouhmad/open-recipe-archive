## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg tender French green beans
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 15 g salt

## Directions

1. Clean the beans, rinse them in cold water and let them drain on linen.
2. Put them in boiling, salted water and let them boil rapidly, without a lid, until they are tender, then remove them with a slotted spoon onto a dish with a broken napkin and serve with stirred butter or hollandaise.
