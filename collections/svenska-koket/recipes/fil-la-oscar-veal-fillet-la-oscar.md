## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. boneless veal from the roast
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter for frying
- 3 tablespoons crayfish butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- asparagus stock
- 4-5 parts thin cream
- 1 can lobster (of 1/2-3/4 litre)
- 1 can asparagus (of 1/4 litre)
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into 1 cm. thick slices, which are lightly pounded
2. Salt and pepper them and fry them in butter
3. Fry butter and flour together and mix with the asparagus stock and cream, and let the sauce simmer 10 minutes
4. Then add the cut up lobster and asparagus, and season the sauce and pour it over the meat
5. Serve the dish at once
