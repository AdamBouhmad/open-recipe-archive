## Ingredients

- 2 large salt fat herrings
- 3 anchovies
- 40 g butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 knife point cayenne
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped chives
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Fillet the herring, remove it, rinse it, cut it into fillets and remove the backbone and all small bones.
2. Soak the herring in lukewarm milk for about 12 hours.
3. Remove the fillets and wrap them well in a cloth or clean white paper.
4. Cut them into beautiful, oblong even pieces.
5. Place all the scraps (which should equal 1 fillet) in a stone mortar and pound together with the cleaned and deboned anchovies and butter.
6. Press the mixture through a fine sieve and mix well with the egg yolk, a small pinch of cayenne, the parsley and the chives.
7. Spread the mixture thinly on each herring piece and roll them up into small rolls.
8. Butter a fireproof mold with cold butter and place the rolls in the mold close together.
9. Sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs and pour the remaining melted butter over them.
10. Let the mold stand in a warm oven for 10-15 minutes.
11. Serve immediately as breakfast with freshly boiled potatoes.
