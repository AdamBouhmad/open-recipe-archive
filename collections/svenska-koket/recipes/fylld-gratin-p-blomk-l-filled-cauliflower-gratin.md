## Ingredients

- 2 large cauliflower heads
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons wheat flour
- 1-2 deciliters cream
- pepper
- salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower, place it in water mixed with vinegar and blanch it in boiling and salted water until almost tender, then remove it and let it drain.
2. Place it on a heat-resistant dish, and cut a lid from the cauliflower.
3. Fry the butter and flour together, gradually add the cream, let the sauce simmer a few minutes and season with salt and white pepper.
4. Stir in the egg yolks last, pour the stewing into the cauliflower, and place the lid on top.
5. Pour the melted butter over the cauliflower and sprinkle with the grated cheese and crushed rusks.
6. Place it in the oven to get a nice golden-brown color, remove it and serve immediately.
