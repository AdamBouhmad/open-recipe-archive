## Ingredients

- 2 deciliters rice grains (2 hectograms)
- 1.5 liters milk
- 3 hectograms calf's liver
- 2 hectograms fresh pork
- 30 raisins (30 g)
- 3 tablespoons syrup
- 1.5 tablespoons salt (23 g)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Rinse the grain, scald in hot water, place in the boiling milk and let cook, stirring, until nearly done.
