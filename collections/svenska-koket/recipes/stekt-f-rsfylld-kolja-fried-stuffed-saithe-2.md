## Ingredients

- 2 kg saithe
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 2 tsp vinegar
- For filling: 1 1/2 cups grated white bread, 1 cup cream milk, 1 egg, 20 anchovies, 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- For breading: 1 egg white, 1 cup crushed hardtack
- For frying: 2 tbsp butter or stock, boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut the fish open along the back, remove the backbone and entrails, then rinse well and dry.
2. Rub with salt and vinegar.
3. Clean and debone the anchovies and cut them into pieces.
4. Make a farce from the bread, milk, egg, anchovies, and parsley.
5. Stuff the fish with the farce and sew it closed along the back.
6. Brush with egg white and sprinkle with crushed hardtack.
7. Butter a baking pan with stock, place the fish in it, and let it cook slowly in the oven for 20 minutes to 1/2 hour.
8. During cooking, dilute it now and then with boiling water and baste it with its sauce.
