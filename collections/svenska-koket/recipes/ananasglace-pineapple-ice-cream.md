## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple (approximately 750 gr.)
- 4 hg. sugar
- 5 dcl water
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 tbsp. kirsch

## Directions

1. Cut the pineapple into small cubes
2. Cook the cubes with the sugar in the pineapple liquid and water for approximately 10 minutes
3. Pass the mixture through a fine sieve
4. Let it cool
5. Add lemon juice and kirsch
