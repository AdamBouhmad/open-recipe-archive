## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg veal breast
- 50 g kidney fat
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1/4 cup crushed hardtack
- 2 eggs
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 1/2 liters broth or water
- 1 carrot
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter
- broth in which the breast was cooked
- 2 tbsp flour

## Directions

1. Bone out the breast and wash with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Chop the kidney fat with the parsley and mix with the crushed hardtack, salt and eggs.
3. Spread the mixture on the breast, roll it up tightly and bind it in three places.
4. Place it in boiling liquid and let it simmer with the carrot and onion until tender, about 2 hours.
5. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, place the breast in it and put in the oven to get a nice brown color.
6. Remove, cut into nice pieces and arrange on the serving platter.
7. Make a smooth sauce from the browned butter, flour and the strained broth, taste it and serve in a separate sauce bowl.
