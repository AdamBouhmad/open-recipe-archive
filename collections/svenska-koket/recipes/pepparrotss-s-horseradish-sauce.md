## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1/2 liter meat stock or milk
- 3-4 tbsp grated horseradish
- salt
- (sugar)

## Directions

1. Soak the horseradish in water for a couple of hours, scrape and grate it.
2. Fry together the butter and flour, add the meat stock or milk, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
3. Add the grated horseradish, and taste the sauce.
4. The horseradish should not cook with the sauce, as this will give it a bitter taste.
