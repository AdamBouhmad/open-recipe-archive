## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- salt
- 2.5 deciliters cream
- sugar (optional)
- 1 teaspoon butter for the pan
- 1 tablespoon butter for lobster stew
- sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- salt
- 4 deciliters thin cream
- white pepper
- 1 can lobster (0.75 liter)
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks and whites. Whisk the yolks with the cream, salt (and sugar), and fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
2. Pour the batter into a well-buttered omelet pan and bake light yellow in ordinary oven heat.
3. Immediately turn the omelet out, place the stew on half the omelet, and fold the other half over.
4. For the stew, fry butter and flour together and add the cream with frequent stirring.
5. Let the sauce boil 10 minutes. Add the chopped lobster and season the stew to taste.
6. Finally stir in the beaten egg yolk.
