## Ingredients

- 2½ lit. clear consommé
- salt, cayenne
- 3–4 carrots
- ½ lit. broth
- egg royal
- ¼ can fine, preserved peas
- 1 can green asparagus tips (1/4 lit.)

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots, rinse, dry and cut them out with a spoon-shaped vegetable cutter into 36 small, oval balls, which are cooked tender in the broth.
2. Cut the royal (see no. 306) with a small round measure in the form of pastilles, which are placed in the soup bowl together with the carrot balls, peas and asparagus.
3. Bring the consommé to a boil, season with the spices and pour over the garnish.
4. Serve very hot.
