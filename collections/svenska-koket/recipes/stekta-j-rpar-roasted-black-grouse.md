## Ingredients

- 4 black grouse
- 1 hg. lard
- 2 tbsp. butter
- 2 tsp. salt
- ½–1 liter broth
- ½ tbsp. butter for sauce
- 1½ tbsp. flour
- meat stock
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck the black grouse, singe, remove innards, rinse well and dry. Truss them thereafter and cover with lard slices. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the black grouse in with breasts up and brown well on all sides, whereupon salt them. Dilute with broth, and let the black grouse roast slowly for approximately ¾ hour.
2. Strain the meat stock and skim, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
