## Ingredients

- 6 large canned artichoke bottoms
- 2 medium Portuguese onions
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese

## Directions

1. Place the bottoms on a buttered, fireproof dish or a silver dish.
2. Peel the onion, chop it finely and fry it in 2 tablespoons of butter until it is golden brown, then remove it and let it cool in the butter.
3. Fill the mixture into the bottoms and sprinkle with crushed rusks and a little grated cheese; melt the remaining butter and pour over.
4. Place the dish in a baking pan with coarse salt and gratinée the preparation in a hot oven for 10–15 minutes.
5. Serve very hot.
