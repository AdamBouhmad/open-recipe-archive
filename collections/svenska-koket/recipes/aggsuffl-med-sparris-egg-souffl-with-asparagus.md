## Ingredients

- 60 g butter
- 60 g flour
- 4 dl boiling milk
- 5 eggs
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp melted butter (20 g) for the mold
- 2 tbsp grated breadcrumbs
- Creamed asparagus: 0.5 tbsp butter (10 g), 1.5 tbsp flour (15 g), 1.5 dl cream, 1 can omelette asparagus (0.25 liters)
- 1 can asparagus spears
- 100 g unsalted butter

## Directions

1. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the mixture boil for 5 minutes. Pour it into a bowl; when it has cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the mixture for 10 minutes. Taste it with the seasonings and carefully mix in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
2. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn ring mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 40 minutes.
3. Prepare the asparagus creaming as in recipe no. 28. Turn the soufflé out onto a round plate, fill the hole with the creamed asparagus and garnish all around with the asparagus spears, which have been heated in a water bath. Serve the soufflé immediately with melted butter as a course.
