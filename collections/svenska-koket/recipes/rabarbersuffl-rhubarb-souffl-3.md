## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg rhubarb
- 125 gr crushed sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 6 eggs
- 1 tbsp sour cream
- 5 tbsp caster sugar
- 1 tsp grated lemon zest
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Peel the rhubarb, cut into pieces and cook quickly until soft in the water together with the crushed sugar.
2. Stir the egg yolks with the cream, caster sugar and lemon zest until the mixture is frothy.
3. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form and fold in gently.
4. Pour half of the mixture into a buttered, heatproof mold.
5. Drain the cooked and cooled rhubarb through a colander and place it in the mold.
6. Pour the remaining mixture over it.
7. Bake the soufflé in moderate oven heat for about 1 hour.
8. Serve immediately with whipped cream.
