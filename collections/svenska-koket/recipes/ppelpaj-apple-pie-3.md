## Ingredients

- For the pie dough: 2 1/2 dcl. flour (125 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr.)
- 125 gr. butter
- 3-4 tablespoons cold water
- For filling: 1 1/2 liters good, loose apples
- 2 tablespoons water
- 2 tablespoons white wine
- 1 1/4 dcl. sugar (108 gr.)
- For the form: 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Sieve the flour and sugar into a bowl, distribute the butter, which should be firm, in flakes in the flour and work it in with the fingertips until the mixture is even in color and without lumps.
2. Then add the water and work the mixture quickly.
