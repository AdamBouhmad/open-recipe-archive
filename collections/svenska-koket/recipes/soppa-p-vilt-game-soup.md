## Ingredients

- Bones and other leftovers from game
- 2 liters weak broth or water
- 1 small parsnip
- 0.25 celery
- 6 white peppercorns
- 1 clove
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- salt
- sugar
- 1 part Madeira
- 1 teaspoon butter in the soup tureen

## Directions

1. Bones of all kinds of game can be used. Chop the bones and cook them in the broth together with the vegetables and spices. When the broth has good flavor, strain it and skim off all fat carefully.
2. Mix butter and flour together, add the broth and let it cook for 10-15 minutes. Season the soup with wine, sugar and salt and serve with toasted bread.
