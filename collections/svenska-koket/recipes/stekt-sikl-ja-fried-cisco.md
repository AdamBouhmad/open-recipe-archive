## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg. cisco
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 1 hectogram butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, see page 171 and score on both sides, with 1 1/2 cm. between each score, rub with salt and fry on top of the stove as in recipe n:o 330.
