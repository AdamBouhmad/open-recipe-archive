## Ingredients

- 2 kg. ling or coalfish
- 1.4 liters lime
- 150 gr. soda
- water as needed

## Directions

1. Saw the fish into two or more pieces, depending on size, and let it lie in cold water for one week, changing the water twice during this time.
2. Brush it thoroughly clean with a root brush, especially on the skin side.
3. Spread a layer of lime on the bottom of a tub, then place the thickest fish pieces and sprinkle with lime, then fish again, and continue thus until all fish is laid down, but save approximately 4 dcl. of the lime to sprinkle over during the luring.
4. Crush the soda and dissolve it in 1 liter of lukewarm water.
5. Mix the soda solution with enough cold water so that it stands over the fish.
6. Add it carefully around the edge of the tub so that the lime is not rinsed off the fish.
7. As the fish swells, add more water and sprinkle a little more of the saved lime.
8. The smallest pieces are usually ready after 2 days, the large ones after 4 days.
9. Remove them as they become ready and let them lie in fresh water for approximately 4 days, changing the water once a day.
10. This batch is sufficient for approximately 30 persons.
