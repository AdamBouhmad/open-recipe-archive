## Ingredients

- 2 liters potatoes
- water
- 4 tablespoons whipped cream
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw and form with a knife or melon baller into small round balls.
2. Boil briefly.
