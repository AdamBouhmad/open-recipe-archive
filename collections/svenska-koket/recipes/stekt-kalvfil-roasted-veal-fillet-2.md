## Ingredients

- 750 g veal fillet
- 1/2 hg lard
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 2 dl broth
- 2 dl thick cream (1-2 truffle)

## Directions

1. Clean the fillets, dry with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and lard with fine lard strips in even rows.
2. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the fillets in, brown nicely on all sides and sprinkle with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Gradually add the boiling broth and cream and let the fillets braise slowly with a tightly closed lid until tender, approximately 30-40 minutes, basting often and turning.
4. Cut them, reassemble so they retain their shape, place on a warm serving dish and garnish with salad or parsley.
5. Strain the sauce, skim, heat and taste, and add, if desired, the truffle cut into strips, and pour over the meat.
6. Serve with peas or French potatoes.
7. If a thinner sauce is desired, thicken it with a little butter and flour.
8. The lard can be omitted and the fillets prepared otherwise in the same way.
