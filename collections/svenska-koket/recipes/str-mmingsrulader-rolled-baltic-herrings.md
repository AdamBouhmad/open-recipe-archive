## Ingredients

- 24 Baltic herrings
- 1 tbsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. sugar
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 cup water
- 5 allspice berries
- 5 white peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp. sugar
- 4 dill stalks
- The stock in which the herrings were cooked, 3 sheets gelatin
- 1 tbsp. hot water
- 1 egg white
- dill for garnish

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings, remove the spine, scrape away the dark membrane, then rinse well, wrap in a fish towel and rub with salt and sugar.
2. Bring the vinegar, water and the other spices to a boil.
3. Roll the herrings up tightly and lay them close together in a pot with the dill stalks between each layer and pour the liquid over them, then let the herrings cook slowly for about 1/2 hour.
