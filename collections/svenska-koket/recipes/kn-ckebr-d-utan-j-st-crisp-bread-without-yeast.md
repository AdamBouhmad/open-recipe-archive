## Ingredients

- 200 grams wheat flour
- 200 grams rye sifted or coarse rye flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 grams)
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)
- 75 grams cold butter
- milk (about 2 deciliters)

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix with sugar and salt.
2. Distribute the butter in flakes in the flour and work in with fingertips.
3. Add enough milk to make a reasonably firm dough that is worked until smooth, glossy and pulling away from the bowl.
4. Roll out the dough very thin, prick lightly, and cut with a pastry wheel along a ruler into oblong squares.
5. Place on buttered sheets and bake light brown in a not-too-hot oven.
6. Good for envelopes and open-faced sandwiches.
