## Ingredients

- 530 gr. butter
- 210 gr. sweet almond
- 425 gr. granulated sugar
- 750 gr. wheat flour
- 2 eggs
- raspberry jam for filling

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with sugar until white.
2. Work in the eggs, the blanched and grated sweet almond, and wheat flour, and set the dough to cool.
3. Roll out quite thin and cut round cakes with a measure.
4. Place a little raspberry jam on half of each cake and fold the other half over.
5. Make small points on the edge with a fork.
6. Bake in moderate oven heat.
