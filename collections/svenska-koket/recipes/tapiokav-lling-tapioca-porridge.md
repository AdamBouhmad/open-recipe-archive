## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup tapioca groats
- 2 liters milk
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- salt (sugar)

## Directions

1. Rinse the tapioca groats
2. Melt the butter in a pot
3. Pour in the milk and bring to a boil
4. Add the rinsed tapioca groats
5. Let the porridge cook until the groats are clear
6. Taste and serve
