## Ingredients

- 3 fine fresh cucumbers
- 1 liter water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon strong meat gravy or meat extract
- 1.5 liters broth
- salt
- white pepper
- 0.5 tablespoon cold butter

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers, cut into pieces, and remove the seeds well.
2. Boil the water, add the cucumbers and let them cook until soft, then drain well.
3. Melt the butter in a pot, add the cucumbers with the sugar and brown until they have a nice light brown color, then add the meat gravy.
4. Let the mass cook for a few minutes, then pass through a fine sieve and return to the pot.
5. Add the broth, bring the soup to a good boil and season it.
6. Finally stir in the cold butter and serve the soup.
