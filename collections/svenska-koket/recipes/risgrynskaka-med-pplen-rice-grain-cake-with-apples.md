## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup rice grains
- 1 liter unskimmed milk
- 2 cups apple sauce
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups milk for the custard
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice grains, place them in boiling milk and let them cook until soft.
2. Butter a pie mold; place half of the rice porridge in the mold.
3. Spread the apple sauce on the porridge and cover with the remaining porridge.
4. For the custard whisk the eggs and milk together and blend
