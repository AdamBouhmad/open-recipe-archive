## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg spareribs
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon mustard (optional)
- 1/2 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Break the spareribs, wash with a cloth dipped in hot water and rub in the spices.
2. Brown in good oven heat and add the boiling liquid.
3. Let the meat cook until tender (about 2 hours), basting it every tenth minute.
4. Cut the spareribs into nice pieces and garnish, if desired, with cooked prunes.
5. Strain the sauce, skim thoroughly and serve as gravy.
