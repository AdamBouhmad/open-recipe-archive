## Ingredients

- 2½ kg reindeer saddle
- 4 liters drink (marinade)
- 1 kg lard
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ½ tablespoon salt
- 3½–1 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Place the meat in drink overnight, then remove and rinse well and dry with a cloth.
2. Remove all sinews carefully, and lard the reindeer saddle in close rows with lard cut into fine strips.
3. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, and brown the roast well in it; then salt it and add the broth a little at a time, baste often and turn a couple of times during roasting.
4. Roasting takes 2–2½ hours.
5. Remove the reindeer, cut into thin slices and garnish with vegetables.
6. Strain the meat juices and skim, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
