## Ingredients

- 30 gr. mondamin flour
- 3 dcl. water
- 3 1/2 dcl. French wine
- 150 gr. granulated sugar
- Rind and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix the mondamin with 1 dcl. of the water. Boil the remaining water with the sugar and the lemon rind and juice. Add the wine and the mondamin mixture and let the cream cook for 5 minutes. Remove the saucepan from heat, whisk in the egg yolks one at a time, and let the cream simmer under vigorous whisking until thick, then pour it out and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
2. Rinse a nice metal mold with cold water and sprinkle with granulated sugar, pour in the cream, and let the mold stand on ice for 2-3 hours. Unmold the pudding onto a flat glass dish and garnish with ladyfingers or macaroons.
