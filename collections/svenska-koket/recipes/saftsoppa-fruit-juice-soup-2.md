## Ingredients

- 3–4 cup sour fruit juice
- 1¼ litres water
- 1–1½ cup crushed sugar
- 1 piece cinnamon (optional)
- 2 tablespoon potato flour
- 10 sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Bring the juice, water and cinnamon to a boil, add the sugar, let the mixture cook for 5–10 minutes and skim it.
2. Mix the potato flour with a little cold water and add it, then bring the soup to a good boil.
3. Blanch the almonds, cut them into strips and put them in the soup bowl.
4. Serve the soup preferably cold with rusks or biscuits.
5. If using sweet juice, omit the sugar.
