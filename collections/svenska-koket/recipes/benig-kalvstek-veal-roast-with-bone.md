## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal breast
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp salt
- 6 allspice berries
- 1 liter broth or water
- 1/2 tbsp butter
- 1 1/2 tbsp flour
- meat jus
- 1/2–1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the breast with a cloth dipped in hot water, then break it well.
2. Roast it as an ordinary veal roast, either in the oven or in a pot.
3. Prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
