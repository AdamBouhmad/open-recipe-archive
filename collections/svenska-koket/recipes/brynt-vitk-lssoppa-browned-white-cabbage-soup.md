## Ingredients

- 1 small cabbage head
- 2 msk. butter
- 1-2 msk. syrup
- 2 1/2 lit. broth
- 12 allspice berries
- 6 white peppercorns
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves of the cabbage head, loosen the rest from the stem, and cut away the thickest part of the head vein, then cut the cabbage into fine cubes.
2. Brown the butter in a pot, add the cabbage together with the syrup and brown it with frequent stirring until it has a nice light brown color.
3. Add the broth together with the spices, and let the soup cook approximately 3 hours with a tightly closed lid.
4. Season it thereafter and serve with meatballs, sausage or pork.
