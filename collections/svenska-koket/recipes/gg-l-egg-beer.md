## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 1 liter weak beer or malt beverage
- 6 parts beer
- sugar
- lemon peel or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Bring the beverage and beer to a boil together and pour hot over the whisked egg yolks while whisking vigorously.
2. Season the egg beer with sugar, lemon peel, or cinnamon and serve with rusks.
