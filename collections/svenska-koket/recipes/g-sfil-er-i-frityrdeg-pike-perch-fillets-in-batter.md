## Ingredients

- 2 pike perch weighing approximately 600 g each
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- For the batter: 80 g dried flour
- 3 g salt
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g)
- 1 decilitre water
- 1 egg
- For frying: 750 g lard

## Directions

1. Treat the pike perch as in recipe no. 365, but do not flatten the fillets.
2. Rub them with salt and let them rest for 1 hour, then dry them again with a fish cloth.
3. Prepare the batter as in recipe no. 218.
4. Heat the lard in an iron pot, take the fillets with a fork and dip them into the batter, which must completely cover them, and fry in the lard until they are nicely golden brown and cooked through.
5. Let them drain on absorbent paper, arrange on pastry paper, garnish with parsley fried in lard, and serve with creamed spinach or applesauce.
6. Note: Cream can be prepared and served in the same way.
