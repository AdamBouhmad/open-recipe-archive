## Ingredients

- 750 gr. lean, dry-salted pork belly
- 1 1/2 liter water
- 1 medium-sized white cabbage head
- water and salt for blanching

## Directions

1. Rinse the pork, place it in cold water and let it cook for 1/2 hour.
2. Remove the outer leaves of the cabbage head, loosen the remaining leaves from the core and cut them into coarse strips.
3. Bring water to a boil with a little salt, place the cabbage in it and let it cook 5 minutes without a lid, after which place it on a sieve so that the water drains well.
4. Cut the pork into slices and taste the broth.
5. In a pudding mold with a lid, layer the cabbage and pork alternately, and add 1/2 liter of the broth.
6. Put the lid on and let the pudding cook in a water bath on the stove for approximately 2 hours.
7. Serve with brown cream sauce.
