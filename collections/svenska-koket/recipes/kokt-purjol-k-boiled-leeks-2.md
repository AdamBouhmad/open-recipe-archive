## Ingredients

- 12 slender fine leeks
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 10 grams salt

## Directions

1. Trim the leeks in the usual manner and cut the green leaves back somewhat.
2. Rinse very thoroughly so that no sand or dirt remains between the leaves.
3. Place in boiling salted water and cook until soft, which is tested with a whisk.
4. Remove with a slotted spoon and serve very hot on a platter with a folded napkin.
5. Serve melted butter alongside.
