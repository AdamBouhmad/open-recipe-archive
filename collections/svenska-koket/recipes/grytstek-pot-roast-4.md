## Ingredients

- 2 kg boneless beef brisket
- 2 hectograms lard
- 2 hectograms fat
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 1 1/2 liters boiling broth
- 1 bay leaf
- 10 whole peppercorns
- 2 red onions

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat and lard it (see p. 232), then tie it, not too tight, with twine previously dipped in hot water, so that it gets a nice shape.
2. Heat the fat in an iron pot (not too large), brown the meat on all sides until a nice brown crust forms, then remove it and sprinkle with salt.
3. Remove the fat and wipe the pot well with white paper. Return the roast, sprinkle with flour and broth, add the spices and peeled onions.
4. Let it roast slowly with a tight lid for 3 1/2 to 4 hours, turning it a few times but do not add liquid.
5. Cut the roast, place on a warm platter, garnish with greens or parsley and serve with vegetables, potato croquettes or stewed macaroni.
6. Strain the sauce, skim well, let it reduce if it is too thin, or thin it if it is too thick, and add a little cream.
