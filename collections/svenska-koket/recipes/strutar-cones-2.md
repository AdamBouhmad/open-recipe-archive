## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- flour equal to the weight of 1 egg (scant)
- sugar equal to the weight of 2 eggs
- ¼ tsp. powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs together with the sugar, and add the flour and cinnamon.
2. Spread the batter on well-buttered
