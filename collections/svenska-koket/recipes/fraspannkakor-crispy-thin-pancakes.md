## Ingredients

- 4 dcl. water
- approximately 160 gr. flour
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 2 dcl. melted butter (2 hg.)
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Whisk the water and flour, which should be sifted, well together with a steel whisk, and let the batter rest for 2 hours.
2. Then add the melted and cooled butter, sugar, and the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Heat a pancake pan slowly and brush with butter for the first pancake.
4. Make thin pancakes from the batter, which are fried only on one side.
5. When the pancakes are light brown, roll them up on the pan with a fork.
6. Serve hot as a dessert with jam.
