## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hectograms star pasta
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 liters beef broth
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 deciliter sherry or Madeira
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter thick cream

## Directions

1. The pasta is put on to boil in salted water and allowed to cook until completely soft, then it is poured into a colander, rinsed over with cold water so it does not stick together and placed in the soup bowl
2. The broth is brought to a boil and seasoned with the cheese, spices and wine
3. The egg yolks are beaten with the cream and whisked into the soup, which is then poured over the pasta
4. Served very hot
