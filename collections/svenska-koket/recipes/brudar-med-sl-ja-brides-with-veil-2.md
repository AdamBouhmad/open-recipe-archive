## Ingredients

- 2 hg. prunes
- 1 hg. apricots
- 1½ hg. small apples
- 2 hg. sugar
- ¾–1 liter water
- 1 twenty-five öre spiced bread
- 75 gr. butter
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit and let it soak in the water for about 1 hour.
2. From the water and sugar cook a clear syrup, and in this cook first the prunes, then the apples and lastly the apricots.
3. Grate the bread and fry it in the butter.
4. Place the fruit and bread on a platter, layered so that they form a pyramid.
5. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, put it in a paper cornet and pipe it out as veils over the pyramid.
6. Serve with whipped cream.
