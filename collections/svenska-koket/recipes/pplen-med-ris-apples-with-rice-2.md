## Ingredients

- 1 cup Carolina rice grains
- 3 liters boiling water
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 liters apples
- 1 cup caster sugar
- 2 cups water
- 2 small egg whites
- 4 tbsp caster sugar for the meringue

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains in lukewarm water and place them on the fire in the boiling water with the salt.
2. Let the grains boil vigorously for about 30 minutes, then drain them in a colander.
3. Place them to the side of the fire to dry. Stir the grains occasionally with a fork so they do not stick together.
4. Peel the apples, cut into quarters and remove the cores.
5. Boil the sugar and water into a clear syrup, place the apples in it and let them cook until soft.
6. Carefully place them in the center of a dish and reduce the syrup by boiling for a few minutes, then pour it over the fruit.
7. Place the cooked rice grains in a ring around the apples.
8. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, mix with the sugar and spread over the apples.
9. Place the dish in low oven heat and let it stand until the meringue takes on a light yellow color.
10. Serve the arrangement preferably cold with whipped cream.
