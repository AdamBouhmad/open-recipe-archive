## Ingredients

- 3 partridges
- 1 hg. bacon, in 3 slices
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tsk. salt
- 3 dcl. broth
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Partridges are treated and prepared like partridge, but should roast approximately 1.5 hours to become tender.
2. They can also be roasted like pheasant in a previously prepared sauce (see no. 614) and this is mixed with a little thick cream and cold butter before serving.
3. The partridges are halved down the middle, garnished with browned potatoes and salad or parsley.
4. Served with lingonberry jelly and green salad with lemon.
