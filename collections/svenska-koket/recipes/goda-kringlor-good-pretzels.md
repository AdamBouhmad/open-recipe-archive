## Ingredients

- 3 hg. washed butter
- 3 hg. powdered sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 egg white
- 4 1/2 hg. wheat flour
- 1 egg for brushing
- 40 gr. sweet almond
- 40 gr. granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Stir the butter with the sugar until it is white and fluffy.
2. Add the egg yolks, egg white and the sifted flour, then cover the dough and let it stand until the following day.
3. Bake it then into small pretzels, which are brushed with whipped egg, dipped in scalded and ground almond and sugar, placed on a buttered baking sheet and baked beautifully golden brown in a warm oven.
