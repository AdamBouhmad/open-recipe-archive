## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters good, tart apples (approximately 800 grams)
- 3 egg whites
- 135 grams caster sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the apples, dry them, place them in a small roasting pan and bake in the oven until they are soft, then pass them through a hair sieve.
2. Let the puree cool and then stir it with the egg whites and sugar until it is white and fluffy, or for approximately 2 hours.
3. Place in a compote bowl and let stand on ice for 1 hour.
4. Serve with biscuits or macaroons. The mixture can also be baked in the oven as a soufflé.
