## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg spare ribs
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon mustard (optional)
- 1/2 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Break the spare ribs, wash them with a cloth dipped in hot water and rub them in with the spices.
2. Brown them in good oven heat and add the boiling liquid.
3. Let the meat roast until tender (about 2 hours), basting it every ten minutes.
4. Cut the spare ribs into nice pieces and garnish, if desired, with cooked prunes.
5. Strain the sauce, skim it carefully and serve as gravy.
