## Ingredients

- Sago grits
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- 3 parts red wine
- 3 tablespoons currant juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the sago grits and bring to a boil in water together with sugar, cinnamon, and lemon peel.
2. When the grits are clear, add the wine and currant juice.
3. Heat the soup but do not let it boil.
4. Season with sugar.
5. Serve with rusks or biscuits.
