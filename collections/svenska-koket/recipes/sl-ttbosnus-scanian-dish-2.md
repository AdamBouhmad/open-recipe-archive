## Ingredients

- 1/2 25-öre coarse spice bread
- 125 gr butter
- 1 1/2 cup raspberry jam
- 2-3 chocolate cakes with sugar coating (at 25 öre each)
- 4-5 dcl thick cream
- 1 tbsp vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. The bread, which should be 3-4 days old, is grated and fried in butter until completely cooked through and crispy, then allowed to cool
2. Grate the chocolate, and whip the cream until stiff
3. In a compote dish, layer the fried bread, jam, chocolate and cream alternately in the order named
4. The top layer should be cream, garnished with small dots of jam
