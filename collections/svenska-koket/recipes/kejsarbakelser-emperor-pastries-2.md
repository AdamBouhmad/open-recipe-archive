## Ingredients

- 106 gr. butter
- 30 gr. sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 106 gr. flour
- 2 hg. sweet almond
- 8 bitter almonds
- 2 hg. icing sugar
- 2 large egg whites
- 1/2 batch vanilla cream (recipe no. 871)
- 1 hg. icing sugar for glaze
- 1 tbsp. water
- 1/2 tsp. lemon or orange juice
- 15 gr. pistachios for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare the pastry dough as simple pastry dough (recipe no. 974) and let it rest in a cool place for about 2 hours.
2. Blanch the almonds, dry them, grind them through a mill, and pound with the sugar and egg whites in a mortar until a fine and smooth paste.
3. Prepare the cream as in recipe no. 871 and it should be quite firm.
4. Roll out the pastry dough to 3 mm thickness and cut out into oval cakes with a mold, place on a buttered sheet, and bake until nicely golden brown in a not-too-hot oven.
5. When cooled, place 1 tsp. cream on each cake and top with a layer of almond paste.
6. (The almond paste is pressed into oblong, fluted molds and turned over the cream.)
7. Glaze the almond paste (recipe no. 961), dust with blanched and finely chopped pistachios, then let the pastries stand a few minutes in gentle oven heat to dry.
