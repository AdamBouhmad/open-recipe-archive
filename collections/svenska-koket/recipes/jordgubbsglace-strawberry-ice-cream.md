## Ingredients

- 5 hg. powdered sugar
- 3 1/2 dcl water
- 1 liter juice or purée of fresh strawberries
- juice of 1 lemon (if desired)

## Directions

1. Mix the powdered sugar with water
2. Add the strawberry juice or purée
3. Add lemon juice if desired
4. Freeze as fruit ice cream, allowing approximately 1 hour longer in the mold with the salt mixture packed tightly around it before turning out
5. Serve immediately as it melts quickly
