## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg black currants
- 1/4 kg red currants
- 1/2 kg raspberries
- 1/2 kg red gooseberries
- 1 kg lump sugar
- 2 1/2 - 3 parts water

## Directions

1. Clean the berries.
2. From the sugar and water, boil a clear syrup, which should be thick enough that a cooled sample threads between the fingers or a drop on a plate does not spread out.
3. Add the berries, bring the jam to a boil, remove from heat, shake and skim.
4. Return to heat, bring to a boil, remove from heat, shake and skim.
5. Repeat this 4-5 times, or until the berries are clear and the syrup is thick.
6. Shake the jam until it has cooled somewhat, then immediately pour into prepared jars.
