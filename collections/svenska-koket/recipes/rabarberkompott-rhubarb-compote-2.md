## Ingredients

- 8 parts granulated sugar (255 g)
- 2 parts water
- 6 beautiful rhubarb stalks (about 900 g)

## Directions

1. Cook a clear, syrupy syrup from the sugar and water
2. Rinse the rhubarb stalks, dry them, peel thinly, and cut into 3 cm long pieces
3. Put the thickest pieces in the sugar syrup first and cook until soft but not falling apart
4. Remove pieces with a silver fork as they become soft and place in a compote bowl
5. The smaller pieces barely need to cook before they become soft
6. Cook the syrup until it is syrupy and pour over the rhubarb
7. Serve cold with whipped cream
