## Ingredients

- 100 grams butter
- 200-250 grams granulated sugar
- 3 eggs
- 150 grams cooked, cold, grated potato
- 200 grams sweet almonds
- 1 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and beat it with the sugar, egg yolks, and grated potato for half an hour.
2. Blanch the almonds, grind or pass them through an almond mill, and stir in along with the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-sprinkled pan and bake in fairly weak oven heat.
4. Remove the cake from the oven and let it stand 10-15 minutes before turning it out.
5. Serve it poured over with lemon cream.
