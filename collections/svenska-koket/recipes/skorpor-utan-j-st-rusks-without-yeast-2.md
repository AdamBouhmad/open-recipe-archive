## Ingredients

- 500 grams wheat flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 eggs
- 90 grams sugar
- 125 grams butter
- 6 crushed cardamom seeds
- 1.5 deciliters cold milk (generous)

## Directions

1. Sift the flour with baking powder into a bowl.
2. Beat the eggs well with the sugar and butter and add to the flour along with the cardamom and milk, then work the dough quickly with a spoon.
3. Place on a floured baking board and roll into small balls the size of a walnut, which are baked immediately in a hot oven.
4. Cut them while they are hot and dry in a not too hot oven until they have a nice color and are crispy.
