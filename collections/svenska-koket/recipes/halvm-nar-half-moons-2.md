## Ingredients

- 425 g butter
- 140 g granulated sugar
- 210 g potato flour
- 420 g wheat flour
- 1 egg for brushing
- 25 g sweet almond
- 1 dcl pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and work out the water well. Stir it until white and fluffy, then work in the sugar and both types of flour. Save 2 dcl of the wheat flour for rolling out. Roll out the dough thinly and cut out with a round mold into cookies, which are folded almost in half so that they resemble half moons. Brush the cookies with beaten egg, sprinkle with blanched and ground almond and pearl sugar, and bake until beautifully golden brown in not too warm an oven.
