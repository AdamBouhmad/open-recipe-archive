## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters cream milk
- 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
- 6 bitter almonds
- zest of half a lemon
- 6 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar for the mold
- 2 tablespoons boiling water for the mold

## Directions

1. Caramelize the sugar in a pan, dilute with the boiling water, and use this to glaze a mold.
2. Boil the milk with the sugar, almonds, lemon zest, and vanilla until the milk has absorbed a good flavor from the spices.
3. Strain the milk, and when most of the heat has dissipated, add the whisked eggs.
4. Pour the mixture into the prepared mold and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for approximately three-quarters of an hour.
5. When the pudding is cold, turn it out and serve with its sauce.
