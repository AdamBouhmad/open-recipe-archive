## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg pike
- 1.5 tbsp salt
- 1.5 tsp vinegar
- water
- 2 tbsp crayfish butter
- 1.5 tbsp flour
- fish broth
- 2-3 parts cream
- 2 hg crayfish tails
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- 1 large cauliflower head
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the pike well, rub it in with vinegar and salt, and boil it in a short broth.
2. When it is cooked, remove it and pull off the skin. Free the fish meat from all bones and cut into dice.
3. Melt the crayfish butter and flour together, add fish broth and cream, and let the sauce boil for 10 minutes.
4. Stir the crayfish tails and the diced fish meat into the braising and taste and adjust seasoning.
5. Boil the cauliflower in salted water, lay it in the middle of the serving platter, and pour the braising around it.
6. Pour some of the pale sauce over the cauliflower and serve the dish at once.
