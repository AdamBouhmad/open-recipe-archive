## Ingredients

- 2 kg pike
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 small red onion
- 1/4 lemon
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 1 part thin cream
- 3 tbsp flour
- 2 1/2 tbsp good stock
- fried parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Scale, rinse, and dry the fish.
2. Cut it open along the back, remove the entrails, and wash it well.
3. Remove the bones, but keep the head and tail.
4. Mix together the salt, white pepper, chopped onion, lemon juice, and olive oil, pour this over the fish, and let it rest for a couple of hours.
5. Remove the seasonings carefully, dip the fish in cream, roll it in flour, and fry it golden brown in stock.
6. Place it on brown paper to drain the excess fat, serve on fried bread or on a napkin, and garnish with fried parsley.
7. Serve as hot as possible with mushroom sauce.
