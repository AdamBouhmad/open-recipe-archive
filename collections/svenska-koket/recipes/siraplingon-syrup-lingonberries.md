## Ingredients

- 3 kg lingonberries
- 2 parts water
- 6 parts syrup
- 8 orange peels
- 1 piece cinnamon (about 1 inch)

## Directions

1. Clean the lingonberries, rinse and let drain.
2. Place them on the heat with the water, syrup and spices and let cook under constant skimming, until the berries are clear.
3. Pour the jam into well-cleaned crocks or jars.
