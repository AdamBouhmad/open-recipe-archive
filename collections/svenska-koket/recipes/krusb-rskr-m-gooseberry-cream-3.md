## Ingredients

- 8 decilitres fresh or 6 decilitres preserved gooseberries
- 8 decilitres water
- 2 1/2-3 decilitres crushed sugar
- 1 1/2 tablespoons mondamin flour (23 grams) or 1 1/2 tablespoons potato flour (23 grams)

## Directions

1. Top and tail the gooseberries, rinse them and let them drain.
2. Make a thin, clear syrup from the sugar and water.
