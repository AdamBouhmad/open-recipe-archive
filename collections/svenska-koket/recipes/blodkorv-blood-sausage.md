## Ingredients

- 2 cups barley groats
- 1.5 liters pork broth
- 6-8 parts pork blood
- 0.25 Portuguese onion
- 8 tbsp lard
- 2 cups sultana raisins
- 100 gr melted lard
- 2 parts cream
- 1.5-2 cups syrup
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1 tsp marjoram
- 3-4 tbsp salt
- strong pepper, spice pepper, cloves, ginger to taste

## Directions

1. Place the groats in the pork broth and blood and let stand beside the fire to swell for a couple of hours but do not boil
2. Chop the onion and fry in the lard, then strain this from the mixture and blend into the batter along with the raisins, lard cut into fine cubes, cream and spices
3. Fill the mixture into a pork stomach and into wide pork casings and cook gently for approximately 1 hour
4. Serve either fresh, sliced with sugar, cinnamon and lingonberry jam, or fried in slices
