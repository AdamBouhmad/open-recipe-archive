## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple (approximately 750 gr.)
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 egg white
- 1 hg. sugar
- 1/2 dcl. sherry
- 1 dcl. pineapple juice
- 5 leaves gelatin
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Cut one quarter of the pineapple into small cubes, from which all juice is pressed out into a linen cloth.
2. Whip the egg yolks, egg white, sugar, wine and half of the pineapple juice in a saucepan over the fire until the mixture is thick and foamy, then remove the saucepan and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
3. Rinse the gelatin with cold water and dissolve it in the rest of the pineapple juice, which has been heated beforehand.
4. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
5. First blend the pineapple pieces into the cream, then the gelatin, and last fold in the cream very carefully.
6. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it sit on ice for 3-4 hours.
7. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass plate lined with parchment paper. Garnish with the rest of the pineapple, sliced thinly. One pineapple is enough for 12 people, as the slices with which the pudding is garnished can be divided.
