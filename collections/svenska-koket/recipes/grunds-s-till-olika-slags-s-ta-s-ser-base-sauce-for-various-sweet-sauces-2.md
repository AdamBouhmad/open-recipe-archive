## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 75 gr. caster sugar
- 2 tsk. potato flour (10 gr.)
- 1/2 lit. milk

## Directions

1. Whip the egg yolks and sugar until the mixture becomes white and foamy.
2. Mix the potato flour with 2 msk. of the cold milk and blend in.
3. Boil the remaining milk and pour it onto the egg yolks with vigorous whisking.
4. Let the sauce thereafter simmer over the fire with continued whisking until it is thick; pour out and whip until it is cold.
5. This sauce is then mixed with either rum, arrack, fruit juice, lemon, orange, vanilla, caramel, coffee or cocoa to taste.
6. Serve both warm and cold. Keep warm in a water bath.
