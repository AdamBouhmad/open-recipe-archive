## Ingredients

- 2 eggs (= 120 gr.)
- 120 gr. butter
- 120 gr. granulated sugar
- 120 gr. wheat flour
- 1/4 tsp. baking powder
- 2 tbsp. cream or milk
- 4 tbsp. red currant jelly

## Directions

1. The butter is washed and stirred with the sugar until it becomes white, and the whisked eggs are added.
2. The flour is sifted together with the baking powder and stirred in alternately with the cream.
3. A small even baking sheet is greased with butter and lined with parchment paper; the batter is spread out on it and then placed in a warm oven to bake light yellow.
4. The cake is turned out onto sugared paper, brushed with some softened currant jelly and rolled up.
5. The roll is then cut into 1 cm. thick slices.
