## Ingredients

- 3 large celeriac roots
- 1 liter water
- salt
- 1 egg, beaten
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1.5 deciliters finely crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Wash, brush, and peel the celeriac roots. Place in cold water with salt and cook until soft, tested with a whisk.
2. Place on a cloth, let cool, then slice into 1/2 cm thick slices.
3. Dip the slices in beaten egg, turn in crushed rusks, and fry a beautiful golden-brown in butter.
4. Serve as a lunch dish or as garnish around fried meat.
5. Alternative: if desired, the celeriac roots can be cooked unpeeled, which gives them a stronger celery flavor.
