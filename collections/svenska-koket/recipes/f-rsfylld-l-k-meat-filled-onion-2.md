## Ingredients

- 3 large Portuguese onions
- 100 g beef
- 100 g veal
- 50 g lard
- 1 tablespoon crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter for frying

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and parboil until almost soft.
2. Remove from water, cut out a lid, and hollow out the onion.
3. Fill with well-worked and seasoned meat forcemeat and replace the lid.
4. Heat the butter in a saucepan and braise the onions until soft and the forcemeat is cooked through.
5. Remove and serve.
