## Ingredients

- 1 kg carrots
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- water
- 4 tablespoons chopped parsley
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots, wash and cut into approximately 5 cm long and 1 cm wide strips.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, fry the carrots in it but do not brown them, and sprinkle flour over.
3. Add water and let the carrots cook until soft.
4. Just before they are done, add the chopped parsley.
5. Season the stew and serve.
