## Ingredients

- 425 gr. butter
- 1 wine glass cognac
- 210 gr. sugar
- 635 gr. flour

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and sift the flour.
2. Work all ingredients together well on the work table into a smooth dough.
3. Roll out into thin sticks, twist them around each other, and bend into wreaths.
4. Bake in moderate oven heat.
