## Ingredients

- 3 whole eggs
- 3 hg. yellow sugar
- 3 dcl. thick, sour cream
- 1 hg. melted butter
- 1 1/2 tsk. ground cinnamon
- 1 1/2 tsk. ground ginger
- 8 crushed cardamom seeds
- 1 tsk. crushed cloves
- 1 small tsk. bicarbonate of soda
- 4 hg. wheat flour
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes.
2. Add the cream beaten to stiff peaks, the melted butter, the spices, and finally the sifted flour well mixed with the bicarbonate of soda.
3. Pour the batter into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined bread pan or skillet and bake in a not-too-hot oven for approximately 45 minutes.
