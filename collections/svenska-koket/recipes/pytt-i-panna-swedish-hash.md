## Ingredients

- 2-3 red onions
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- leftovers of cooked salted ham or beef brisket and beef or veal steak (about 1/2 lit. finely cut meat)
- 1/2 lit. cooked, finely cut potatoes
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the onion, chop finely and fry well in the butter in a frying pan.
2. Add the meat and potatoes together with the spices and stir the mixture carefully so it does not break apart and let it fry until it has taken on a nice light brown color.
3. Serve with beets as a breakfast dish.
