## Ingredients

- 1 liter unskim milk
- 1 hg rice flour
- 1 tbsp butter
- 5 eggs
- 10 sweet almonds
- 5 bitter almonds
- 2 tbsp powdered sugar
- 2 cups powdered sugar for the mold
- 2 tbsp boiling water

## Directions

1. Caramelize the sugar in a frying pan and dilute it with the boiling water, then use this to line a mold.
2. Bring the milk to a boil, whisk in the rice flour, and let the mixture cook for 10-15 minutes while stirring.
3. Pour the batter into a dish to cool.
4. Add the cold butter, the whisked eggs, the blanched and grated almonds, and the sugar, and stir well.
5. Pour it into the prepared mold, cover with buttered paper and bake in a water bath in the oven.
6. When done, remove it, let it cool and turn it out.
