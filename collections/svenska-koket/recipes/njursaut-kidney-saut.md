## Ingredients

- 8 calf kidneys
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 1 1/2 cups good bouillon
- 1/2 part white French wine or sherry

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys into 1 cm. thick slices and brown them in butter
2. Place them then in a pot, add the bouillon and let them cook slowly, until they are completely tender
3. Pour on the wine, and season the kidney sauté and serve
