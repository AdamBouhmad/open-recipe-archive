## Ingredients

- 225 gr. flour
- 1 hg. butter
- 5 gr. salt
- 10 gr. sugar
- 1 dcl. water
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 can omelette asparagus (1/2 liter)
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 6 tbsp. flour (60 gr.)
- 1 dcl. asparagus broth
- 6 dcl. cream
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the forms

## Directions

1. Prepare the shortcrust dough as in no. 35 and let it rest 1 hour.
2. Roll out the dough very thinly, cut out with a round mould into cakes, with which smooth tartlet forms are lined.
3. Cut pieces of butter paper large enough to size, round forms, which are placed inside the lined forms, which are then filled with peas and placed in the oven to bake.
4. Remove the peas and paper, turn out the tartlets and fill with the stew.
5. Prepare the stew as in no. 28. All the fillings mentioned for crusts can also be used for tartlets.
6. Serve as a course between courses.
