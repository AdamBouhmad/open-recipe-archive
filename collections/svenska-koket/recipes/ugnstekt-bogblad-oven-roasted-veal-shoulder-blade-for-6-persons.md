## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal shoulder
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 8 medium-sized apples
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 0.25 liter boiling water or broth

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into two equal parts, which are rubbed with salt and white pepper.
2. Dry the apples well, remove the blossom end and place them between the shoulder blades, which are then tied together with the meaty side facing out.
3. Place the roast in a butter-greased long pan and put it in the oven to brown, then dilute with boiling water or broth.
4. The roasting takes about 2 hours, during which the meat is basted every tenth minute.
5. Strain and skim the meat gravy and serve in a separate sauce bowl.
6. If desired, it can be prepared as ordinary roast sauce.
