## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal breast
- 3 large bunches parsley
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 3 1/2 tbsp butter (70 gr.)
- 3 tbsp butter for roasting (60 gr.)
- 3/4 liter broth

## Directions

1. Use the front part of the breast, the so-called breast, and do not crack the bones.
2. Bone the breast carefully and cook broth on the bones to dilute the roast with.
3. Prepare the meat and rub with the spices.
4. Rinse the parsley, wrap in a cloth, clean and chop coarsely and work into the butter.
5. Spread the parsley butter on the inside of the breast, which is then rolled up and sewn shut well.
6. Brown it in butter, sprinkle with the rest of the spices, and let it roast in a pot or oven about 3 hours.
7. During roasting, baste the meat every 10 minutes and dilute with the boiling broth.
8. When the roast is tender, remove all threads, then cut into even slices, which are laid together so the roast looks whole.
9. Place on a hot platter, garnish with salad or parsley, serve with vegetables or boiled potatoes, salads and reduced sauce with cream (recipe no. 429).
