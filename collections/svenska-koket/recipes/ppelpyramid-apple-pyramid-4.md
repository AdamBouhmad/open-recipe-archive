## Ingredients

- 250 gr. granulated or powdered sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 12 good, evenly-sized apples
- 25 gr. sweet almond

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar and water until a clear syrup is formed and let cool.
2. Rinse the apples, dry, peel, and remove the cores with a pastry tube.
3. Place the apples directly in the syrup and cook until soft and clear, then remove with a slotted spoon and arrange in a pyramid on a jelly dish.
4. If desired, fill the holes left by the cores with apple or red currant jelly.
5. Blanch the almond, cut into fine dice, lightly toast in the oven, and sprinkle over the pyramid.
6. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream.
7. If served cold, it can be garnished, if desired, with 3 dcl. cream, beaten until stiff peaks form.
