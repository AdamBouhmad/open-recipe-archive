## Ingredients

- 120 gr. butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 gr. salt
- 1/2 gr. white pepper

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a bowl together with the egg yolks and seasonings.
2. Set the bowl in boiling water and stir the sauce continuously until it is smooth and thick.
3. Serve immediately with fish, puddings, etc.
