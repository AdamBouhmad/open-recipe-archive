## Ingredients

- 5 egg yolks
- 125 gr. caster sugar
- 5 dcl. Rhine wine

## Directions

1. Carefully separate the egg yolks from the whites, place them in a well-scoured saucepan and whisk with the sugar for 15 minutes.
2. Pour in the wine and let the sauce simmer over heat with vigorous whisking until thick and foamy, then pour it out and serve immediately.
