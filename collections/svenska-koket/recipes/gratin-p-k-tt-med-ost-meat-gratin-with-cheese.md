## Ingredients

- 500 g boneless fried or cooked meat
- 3 msk grated cheese
- 1 msk melted butter (20 g)
- 1 1/2 msk butter for the sauce (30 g)
- 4 msk flour (40 g)
- 4 dcl meat sauce or broth
- salt, white pepper
- 1/2 msk butter for the mold
- 3 msk crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thin slices.
2. Butter a fireproof dish or pie form and sprinkle with half the rusks; layer the meat alternately with the cheese and sauce; the top layer should be sauce, sprinkled with the remaining rusks and cheese.
3. Finally pour over the clarified butter and place the dish in a hot oven to acquire a nice brown color.
4. Serve with mashed potatoes.
5. For the sauce: fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the meat sauce and let it boil for 5 minutes, then season with the spices.
