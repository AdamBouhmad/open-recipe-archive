## Ingredients

- 1 kg cooked, boned fish meat or 1.25 kg pike
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g) for the dish
- 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g) for the sauce
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 4 dcl fish stock
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 6 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked fish meat into neat, even slices.
2. Butter an ovenproof dish or silver dish with half the butter.
3. Arrange the fish slices on the dish and distribute the remaining butter in flakes over the fish.
4. For the sauce: fry butter and flour together for 2 minutes.
5. Add the fish stock and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
6. Season with salt, sugar, and white pepper to taste. Allow to cool slightly.
7. Add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the sauce for 10 minutes.
8. Finally, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
9. Pour the sauce over the fish.
10. Sprinkle the grated cheese on top.
11. Bake in a hot oven for 15-20 minutes.
12. Serve with lemon or mushroom sauce if desired.
