## Ingredients

- 5 hg. boneless, roasted meat of beef, veal or mutton
- 8 dcl. bouillon or water
- 1 red onion
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 2 msk. finely cut pickled cucumber
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. The meat is cut from the bones in even, beautiful pieces.
2. The bones are crushed and cooked as in the preceding recipe.
3. The onion is peeled, finely chopped and lightly browned in the butter.
4. The flour is fried with the butter and onion for 2 minutes, the strained bouillon is thinned and the sauce is allowed to boil for 5 minutes.
5. The meat and pickled cucumber are added and heated in the sauce but must not boil.
6. The dish is seasoned with spices, laid on a warm dish and garnished with browned potatoes.
