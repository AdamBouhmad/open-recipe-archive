## Ingredients

- 3 hg. beef
- salt, white pepper
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 1/2 liter cooked cold potatoes
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 1 msk. finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into small, even cubes, which are seasoned, fried brown and tender in some of the butter and placed in the center of a warm serving dish.
2. Finely chop the onion, fry it in butter in the same pan and place it in a ring around the meat.
3. Cut the potatoes into even cubes, fry them in a separate pan and place them on the sides of the onion, sprinkled with finely chopped parsley.
4. Pour a little browned butter over the dish, which is served as a breakfast dish.
