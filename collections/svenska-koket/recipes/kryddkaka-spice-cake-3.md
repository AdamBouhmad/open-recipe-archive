## Ingredients

- 240 gr. butter
- 1 tsp. ground cloves
- 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
- 2 tbsp. finely chopped orange peel
- 4 hg. sugar
- 1½ cup sour cream
- 6 eggs
- 450 gr. wheat flour
- 2 tsp. baking soda
- 1 tsp. butter for the pan
- 1 tbsp. crushed biscuits for the pan

## Directions

1. Cream the butter until white.
2. Add the sugar, spices, eggs, and wheat flour sifted with baking soda.
3. Last, fold in the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the batter into a buttered and bread-crumbed pan and bake in moderate oven heat.
