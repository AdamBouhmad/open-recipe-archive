## Ingredients

- 2 parts thin cream
- 50 gr. sugar
- 0.5 vanilla pod
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 parts heavy cream
- 40 gr. biscuits or preserved fruit

## Directions

1. The thin cream, sugar, and vanilla are brought to boil until the cream has vanilla flavor.
2. The egg yolks are added one at a time with vigorous whisking, and the cream is allowed to simmer over heat until it thickens.
3. The pan is removed from heat, and whisking is continued until the cream is completely cold.
4. The heavy cream is then whisked to stiff peaks and blended into the mixture.
5. This is poured into a freezing mold and frozen to half, then packed alternately with the crushed biscuits or fruit in a water-rinsed mold with a tight lid.
6. The mold is placed in crushed ice and salt.
7. The ice and salt should lie under, around, and over the mold for 2-3 hours.
8. The pudding is turned out and garnished with biscuits or fruit.
