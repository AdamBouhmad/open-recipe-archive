## Ingredients

- 2.5 to 3 cups flour
- 1 liter unskimmed milk
- 0.5 teaspoon salt
- 4 tablespoons clarified butter
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix with the salt.
2. Whisk the eggs with two cups of the milk and add to the flour with vigorous whisking.
3. When the batter is smooth and glossy, stir in half of the butter, and when this is well blended, add the remaining milk.
4. Let the batter rest for 1 hour so the flour can swell.
5. Give it a vigorous whisking.
6. Heat the pancake pan slowly, brush with clarified butter, and fry small pancakes from the batter.
7. Thin pancakes can also be fried from the same batter.
