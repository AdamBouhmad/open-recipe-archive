## Ingredients

- 4 parts thick, sour cream
- 6 eggs
- 150 gr. caster sugar
- zest of 0.5 lemon
- 2.5 parts dry wheat flour
- 0.5 tablespoon butter and 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks.
2. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar until the mixture becomes white and creamy; sift in the well-dried flour with constant stirring.
3. Add the lemon zest.
4. Fold the egg mixture into the cream, and carefully fold in the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the mixture into a buttered and rusk-strewn mold and place in a fairly hot oven.
6. When the cake is done, it will crack.
7. Sprinkle with sugar and serve at once.
