## Ingredients

- 3 hectograms plums
- 5 cups water
- 1.5 cups sugar

## Directions

1. Plums are rinsed and are then left to soak for 2-3 hours in the water in which they are to be cooked.
2. They are then placed on to cook quickly with the sugar until the plums are completely cooked down.
3. The pits are removed, and the plums together with their sauce are pushed through a hair sieve.
4. The sauce is boiled up, tasted for seasoning and served.
