## Ingredients

- 15 anchovies
- 4 cooked potatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Butter an omelette dish with butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
2. Lay slices of cooked, cold potato in it and over that a layer of cleaned anchovy and finally again a layer of potato.
3. Sprinkle the whole with crushed rusks and chopped parsley.
4. Place the rest of the butter on top, distributed in flakes, and place the dish in the oven to get a brown color.
