## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 kg inner or outer loin of beef
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 2 1/2 tbsp butter or 4 tbsp lard
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1 1/2-2 liters broth or water
- 24 allspice berries
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp flour
- meat gravy
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the roast with a cloth dipped in hot water, lard it, and brown in the browned butter or hot lard
2. If using lard, remove the roast after browning and drain off all fat
3. Salt the roast, thin the liquid a little at a time, add spices, and let roast finish cooking with a tightly fitting lid for 3-4 hours
4. Turn the roast a couple of times during cooking, but avoid thinning during the last half hour
5. Strain the meat gravy and skim it; prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules
