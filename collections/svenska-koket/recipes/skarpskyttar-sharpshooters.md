## Ingredients

- 250 grams wheat flour
- 425 grams butter
- 1 part water
- 1 tablespoon spirits
- 8 egg yolks
- 1 egg for brushing
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 hectogram sweet almond
- 3 parts thick cream
- vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as fine butter dough (no. 548)
2. Roll out the dough and cut out into narrow strips, which are wound around metal tubes
3. Brush them over with beaten egg and sprinkle with finely chopped almond and sugar
4. Bake the pastries in a hot oven
5. When they are ready, remove them and take off the metal tubes
6. After they have cooled, fill them with cream, whipped to stiff peaks and mixed with vanilla sugar
