## Ingredients

- 1 kg carrots
- 3 shallots
- 5 tablespoons butter (100 g)
- 2 liters water
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 deciliters thick cream
- 1 deciliter rice grains
- 1 liter water for rice
- 1/2 tablespoon salt for rice

## Directions

1. Prepare the carrots in the usual way, cut into cubes, and brown in 2 tablespoons of butter together with the peeled and sliced shallots.
2. Add the water and let the carrots cook until completely soft, about 2 hours, then pass through a sieve.
3. Prepare the purée as in recipe no. 266 and place 1 tablespoon of cold butter in the soup bowl.
4. Rinse the rice grains, place in boiling salted water and cook until soft, about 15 minutes, then drain through a colander, rinse with hot water, let drain, place on a carrot, and serve with the soup.
