## Ingredients

- 24 fine, even sugar beets
- 2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 80 grams dry flour
- 3 grams salt
- 1/2 tablespoon melted butter (10 grams)
- 1/2 deciliter of the cooking liquid
- 1/2 deciliter milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 kilogram lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the sugar beets as in the previous recipe and let them drain on linen.
2. For the batter, whisk together all ingredients into a thick pancake batter and let it rest and swell for approximately 2-3 hours.
3. Cut the sugar beets in half, take them with a fork and dip them into the batter, which must completely cover them, and cook in the hot lard until they are beautifully golden brown.
4. Let them drain on gray paper, place on a platter with a folded napkin or parchment paper and serve very hot with creamed butter.
