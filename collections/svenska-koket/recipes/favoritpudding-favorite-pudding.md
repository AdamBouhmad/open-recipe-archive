## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter unskim milk
- 70 gr pressed yeast
- 1/2 kg wheat flour
- 50 gr sugar
- grated peel of 1/2 lemon
- 50 gr sweet almonds (optional)
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 6-8 eggs
- 1 1/2 kg butter
- liqueur
- sugar
- water
- For glaze: 2 parts white wine, 1/2 kg sifted powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Warm the milk and dissolve the yeast in a little of it.
2. Make a dough of flour, milk and yeast, work it well and set it to rise.
3. When the dough has risen well, work it again; add the whisked eggs, sugar, grated lemon peel, (blanched and grated almonds), salt and the rest of the flour, and lastly knead in the washed and wrung butter.
4. Work the dough for at least 1/2 hour and then pour it into a buttered and floured ring mold, let it rise again and bake in good oven heat.
5. When the cake is done, turn it out and soak it thoroughly with a warm mixture of liqueur, sugar and water and let it cool.
6. Glaze the cake, place it on cake paper on a dish and fill the inside high with glace, preferably some red such as strawberry or raspberry glace. It can also be served with apple or pear compote and can in that case be soaked with the fruit juice instead of the liqueur mixture.
