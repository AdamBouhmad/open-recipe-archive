## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 250 gr. sugar
- 60 gr. wheat flour
- 60 gr. potato flour
- juice of 0.5 lemon or 6 bitter almonds or vanilla essence
- 0.5 tbsp. washed butter
- 3 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs with the sugar until the mixture becomes white and fluffy.
2. Add the potato flour little by little, and then the wheat flour.
3. Flavor the batter with lemon, almond or vanilla.
4. Pour into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined form or baking pan and bake at good oven heat.
