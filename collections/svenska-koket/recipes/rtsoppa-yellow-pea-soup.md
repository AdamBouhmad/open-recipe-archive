## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter yellow peas
- 1/2 kg pork
- 4 liters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ginger

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the peas and soak them in water the evening before use.
2. Place them in soft cold water (if soft water is not available, add a small amount of baking soda about the size of a pea) and bring quickly to a boil, skimming off the shells.
3. Add the cleaned and scraped pork and let the soup slowly cook until the peas fall apart, about 3 hours.
4. Taste and adjust seasoning, then serve with the cut-up pork.
