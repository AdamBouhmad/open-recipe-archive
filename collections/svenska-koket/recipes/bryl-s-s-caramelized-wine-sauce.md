## Ingredients

- 1 tsk. butter
- 4 hg. superfine sugar
- 2 dcl. boiling water
- 1 dcl. white wine

## Directions

1. Wipe a frying pan well and butter it with the cold butter, then place it at the side of the stove so that it becomes lukewarm.
2. Add the sugar, place the pan over the fire and stir until the sugar has melted and attained a beautiful brown color.
3. Add the water and wine, and let the sauce come to a boil.
4. Pour out and serve cold with mondamin pudding, etc.
