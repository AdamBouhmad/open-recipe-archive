## Ingredients

- 75 gr. butter
- 35 gr. icing sugar
- 1 egg yolk
- 90 gr. wheat flour
- 2 gr. baking powder
- 15 gr. butter for the molds
- 70 gr. sweet almond
- 2 eggs
- 120 gr. caster sugar
- 1 dcl. strawberry jam (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and beat it with the sugar until white and fluffy, then beat in the egg yolk; lastly add the flour sifted with the baking powder, then let the dough rest for about 2 hours.
2. Roll out the dough thinly and cut out into rounds with a mold to line buttered pastry molds.
3. For the filling, blanch the almond and grind through an almond mill.
4. Beat the eggs and sugar for 15 minutes, then add the almond.
5. If desired, place a little strawberry jam in the bottom of the molds.
6. Fill the molds almost to the brim with almond paste and bake in a not-too-hot oven until the almond paste, when tested with a whisk, feels dry.
7. If desired, the pastries may be glazed with water glaze (recipe no. 961).
