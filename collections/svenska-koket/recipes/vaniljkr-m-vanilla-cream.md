## Ingredients

- 4 parts thick cream
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the cream with vanilla until it has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor, then add the sugar and remove from heat.
2. Whisk the egg yolks together in a bowl and add the cream with vigorous whisking.
3. Return the cream to the heat and let it simmer with continued whisking, but do not let it boil.
4. Pour into a bowl and whisk until completely cooled.
