## Ingredients

- 25 g sweet almonds
- 4 bitter almonds
- zest of 1 lemon
- rice grits
- milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup crushed rusks for coating
- 1/2 kg cream for cooking

## Directions

1. Scald the rice grits and cook them in the milk until soft.
2. Beat up the porridge, and when most of the heat has gone, stir in the egg, the grated almond, and lemon zest.
3. Form small round balls with a spoon, turn them in egg and crushed rusks, and fry them golden brown in cream.
