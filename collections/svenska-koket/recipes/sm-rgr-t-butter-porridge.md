## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 small cups flour
- 1 liter boiling milk
- salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon cold butter

## Directions

1. Mix the butter and flour together, add the boiling milk a little at a time while stirring frequently
2. Let the porridge cook 15 minutes
3. Add salt and stir in the whipped egg yolks along with 1 teaspoon cold butter
4. Serve warm with milk
