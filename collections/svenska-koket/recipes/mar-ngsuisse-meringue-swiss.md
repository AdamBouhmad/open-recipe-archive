## Ingredients

- 8 large egg whites
- 400 grams granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 1 liter thick cream
- carmine

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form and continue beating for 1/4 hour
2. Gently fold in the previously sifted granulated sugar
3. Place the batter in piping bags and pipe onto well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets in rings, so that these together form wreaths
4. Make the wreaths in different sizes so that when laid on top of each other they form a pyramid
5. Place them in a mild oven heat and bake very slowly until they release from the sheet
6. When cool, arrange them on a glass dish, wreath upon wreath, into a pyramid
7. Whip the cream with vanilla sugar until stiff peaks form
8. Fill and coat the pyramid with whipped cream
9. Divide the remaining cream in two parts; color one part with carmine to a beautiful pink color, and with this and the white cream garnish the pyramid using piping bags
