## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg lamb breast
- 4 tablespoons salt (60 g)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 g)
- 2 g saltpeter

## Directions

1. Crack the ribs and rub the breast with a mixture of salt, saltpeter, and sugar.
2. Place it in a deep dish and let it stand 3-4 days, turning it often in the brine that forms.
3. Wash the meat thoroughly with a cloth dipped in cold water.
4. Place in cold water, bring quickly to a boil, skim well, and boil 15 minutes at 100°.
5. Cool the broth to 82°-83° and let the meat lie in it without boiling until tender, or 1 1/2-2 hours.
6. Serve with browned rutabaga, stewed vegetables, or mashed potatoes. The broth can be used for some prepared soup or puree.
7. Alternatively, after the meat is cooked, brush it with beaten egg, sprinkle with finely crushed rusks, place in a baking pan whose bottom is covered with broth, and set in a hot oven to gratinée 15-20 minutes. Serve as above.
