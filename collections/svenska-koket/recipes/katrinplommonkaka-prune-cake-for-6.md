## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 135 gr. sugar
- 0.5 liter thin cream
- 135 gr. butter
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 425 gr. prunes
- 100 gr. sweet almonds
- 1 tbsp. butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks and sugar until the egg mixture is white and fluffy.
2. Add the cream and butter, and the mixture is allowed to simmer over the fire while whisking until the cream thickens.
3. Continue whisking until the cream is cold, then stir in the zest and juice of the lemon.
4. Last, carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Cook the prunes until soft in a little water, then remove the pits.
6. Grease a mold with butter and line with buttered paper.
7. Place first a layer of prunes, sprinkle the chopped almonds over it, and finally pour the egg cream over.
8. Bake in gentle oven heat for about 1 hour, turn out and serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
