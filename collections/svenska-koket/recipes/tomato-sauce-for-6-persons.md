## Ingredients

- 6 tomatoes
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 4 dcl. broth
- 1 dcl. cream
- 1 egg yolk
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the tomatoes and pass through a sieve.
2. Sauté the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the broth, tomato purée and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Thicken with the whipped egg yolk, let it simmer and season with the spices.
4. Alternatively, the butter and flour can be omitted and the sauce thickened with 1 tbsp. cornstarch mixed in 1/2 dcl. cold meat gravy.
