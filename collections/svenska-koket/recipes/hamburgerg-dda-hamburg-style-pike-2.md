## Ingredients

- 2 kg pike
- 1¼ tbsp salt
- 1 tsp vinegar
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tbsp grated cheese
- 1¼ cups crushed hardtack
- 1 tbsp clarified crayfish butter
- 2 parts cream

## Directions

1. Clean the pike, rinse and dry it well and fillet it.
2. Remove skin and bones carefully, rub the fish with vinegar and salt and let it rest a while.
3. Dry it well with a cloth, brush with melted butter and the separated egg yolk, and sprinkle on both sides with grated cheese and crushed hardtack.
4. Place it in a roasting pan rubbed with cold butter, pour over the melted crayfish butter and cream, and roast quickly in a hot oven until it gets a golden-brown color.
5. Baste it frequently with its drippings during roasting.
6. Serve with mushroom sauce mixed with the sauce the fish was roasted in.
