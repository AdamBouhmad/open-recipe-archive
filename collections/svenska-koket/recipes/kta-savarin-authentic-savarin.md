## Ingredients

- 220 g flour
- 1 deciliter lukewarm milk
- 25 g compressed yeast
- 220 g fine butter
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 g)
- 30 g sweet almonds or hazelnuts
- 1/2 teaspoon finely grated lemon or orange zest
- 5 whole eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 g) for the mold
- Sugar syrup: 1/2 kg sugar
- 5 deciliters water
- 3 deciliters maraschino liqueur or white wine or 1/2 deciliter arrack

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, work part of it well together with the milk and the yeast dissolved with 2 teaspoons sugar
2. Work the dough until it becomes smooth and glossy, and let it rise on the oven door
3. Cream the butter well with the sugar and salt, and the blanched and ground almonds or hazelnuts
