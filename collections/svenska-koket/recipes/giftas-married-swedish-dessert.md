## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. extra thick cream
- 3 dcl. jam
- 3 dcl. pieces of small bread or 18 small sugar rusks
- 1 1/2 dcl. milk (for soaking)

## Directions

1. Whisk the cream to stiff peaks.
2. Stir in the jam and small bread or rusks (soaked in 1 1/2 dcl. milk).
3. Arrange the cream in a compote dish.
4. The small bread can be omitted, in which case the cream is instead piped into cones.
