## Ingredients

- 1 skinned rabbit weighing approximately 3 kg
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 grams)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 grams)
- 2 deciliters broth
- 5 deciliters cream or milk
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce

## Directions

1. Rabbit is not considered game in our country but belongs to domestic animals. The meat has a pleasant firm, somewhat sweet taste and in terms of nutritional value approaches beef. After the rabbit is slaughtered, it should hang for 2-6 days, depending on the season, so the meat becomes tender. Skin it, treat it and roast it as hare (see no. 590), but it requires somewhat shorter roasting time.
