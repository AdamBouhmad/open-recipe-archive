## Ingredients

- 1 cured, smoked ox tongue
- water
- 1 carrot

## Directions

1. The tongue, which should be lightly salted, is warm-smoked for 2 hours and allowed to cool.
2. Treat and cook thereafter as in the previous recipe.
3. When the broth has come to a boil and been skimmed, add the prepared carrot.
4. Smoked tongue is advantageously used for mixed meat dishes and for tongue in aspic, as well as for the smorgasbord.
