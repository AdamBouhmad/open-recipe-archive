## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2-3 teaspoons dry English or French mustard
- 6 decilitres fish stock
- salt
- sugar
- 1 egg yolk (optional)
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter (10 g)

## Directions

1. Melt butter, flour and English mustard together for 2 minutes
2. Add fish stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
3. Season with salt and sugar, then stir in the cold butter last
4. If French mustard is used, mix it with a little fish stock and add it at the same time as the seasonings
5. If desired, bind the sauce with 1 egg yolk
6. The sauce can be made with milk or broth instead of fish stock
