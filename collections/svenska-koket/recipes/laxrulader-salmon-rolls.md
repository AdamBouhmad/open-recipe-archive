## Ingredients

- 6 hectograms salted salmon
- 8 deciliters milk
- 2 beets
- Sharp sauce for garnish
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 head of lettuce

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon into thin slices and place them in milk for 6 hours.
2. Wrap them thereafter in a cloth.
3. Cut the beets into thick strips.
4. Roll up the salmon slices with a beet strip in each.
5. Place the rolls on an oblong platter, pour the sharp sauce over and garnish the dish with the hard-boiled eggs, cut into wedges, and green salad.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish.
