## Ingredients

- 8 hectograms raw fish or 5 deciliters cooked fish in dice
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr.)
- 2 deciliter fish broth
- 2 deciliter cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 sheets gelatin
- salt
- white pepper
- 4 tablespoon flour for breading
- 2 eggs for breading
- salt for breading
- 2 teaspoon cooking oil for breading
- 1 cup crushed rusks for breading
- 1 kg. lard for frying

## Directions

1. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the fish broth and cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
2. Carefully stir in the fish pieces together with the whisked egg yolks and rinsed gelatin dissolved in 1 tablespoon hot water.
3. Season the stew with spices and treat as in recipe no. 49.
4. Form the croquettes into elongated shapes.
5. Serve as a course with meat or mushroom sauce.
