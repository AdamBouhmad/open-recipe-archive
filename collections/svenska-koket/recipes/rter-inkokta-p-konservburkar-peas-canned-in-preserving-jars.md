## Ingredients

- Peas
- Cold boiled salt brine (1 tablespoon salt per liter of water)

## Directions

1. Spirit the peas, separate the smaller from the larger, and rinse the peas.
2. Place them in preserving jars and cover with cold boiled salt brine.
3. Screw the jars shut or solder them and cook according to general rules for 0.5-1 hour, depending on the size of the peas.
