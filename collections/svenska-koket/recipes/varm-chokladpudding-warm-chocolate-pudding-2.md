## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2 deciliters cream
- 2 deciliters milk
- 70 g sugar
- 35 g grated unsweetened chocolate
- 4 eggs
- 2 deciliters sugar for the mold
- 2 tablespoons water for the mold

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cream and milk, add the sugar and chocolate, and let the mixture boil well and pour it out and let it cool.
2. Add the egg yolks thereafter, one at a time, with vigorous stirring, and finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mixture into a glazed mold (see previous recipe) and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
4. Turn it out and serve warm with vanilla sauce.
