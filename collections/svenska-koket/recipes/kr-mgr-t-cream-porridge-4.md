## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 liter milk
- 2 dcl. wheat flour (1 hg.)
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Melt 2 tbsp. of the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk, whisk in the flour, and let the porridge cook 15 minutes while whisking.
2. Add the seasonings and the whisked egg yolks, and let the porridge simmer.
3. Finally stir in the rest of the butter, and serve the porridge with cream, milk or fruit sauce.
