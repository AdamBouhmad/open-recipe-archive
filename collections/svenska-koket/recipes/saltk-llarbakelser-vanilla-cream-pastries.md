## Ingredients

- 210 gr. butter
- 210 gr. granulated sugar
- 2 eggs
- 225 gr. wheat flour
- 7 parts cream
- 1/2 stick vanilla
- 1-2 tbsp. granulated sugar
- 1 tsp. potato flour
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The pastry dough is prepared in the usual way.
2. The cream is boiled with the vanilla and sugar until it has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor.
3. It is then stirred together with the egg yolks, whisked with the potato flour, and the cream is allowed to simmer over heat until it thickens.
4. The whisking is continued until the cream has cooled.
5. Small fluted molds are lined with pastry dough and then filled with vanilla cream.
6. The pastries are placed in the oven to bake through, and when they are done, they are turned out immediately.
