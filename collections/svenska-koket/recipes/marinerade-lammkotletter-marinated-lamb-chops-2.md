## Ingredients

- 12 lamb chops
- 50 gr. lard
- 50 gr. cooked salt ox tongue
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- Marinade: 2 teaspoons finely chopped red onion
- 4 parsley stems
- 2 bay leaves
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- For breading and frying: 60 gr. melted butter
- 2 deciliters finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 60 gr. cold butter
- 1.5 deciliters meat stock or broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops as in the previous recipe, lard them with the lard and tongue, cut into fine strips, rub with the spices and place on a plate.
2. Sprinkle with the onion, the finely cut parsley stems and bay leaves, and pour over the lemon juice and cooking oil.
3. Marinate for 0.5 hour, turning 4 times.
4. Remove them (shake off the spices), wrap in a cloth, dip in the melted butter and turn in the crushed breadcrumbs.
5. Fry the chops as in the previous recipe, arrange in a ring on a hot serving plate, whisk the pan with the boiling broth and pour the sauce over the chops.
6. Garnish the center of the plate with 2 tablespoons applesauce mixed with 30 gr. grated, finely chopped horseradish, or with mushrooms, cut into slices and sautéed in butter.
7. Garnish around with parsley or salad.
8. Serve with French potatoes or with potato mousse flowers.
