## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg pork loin with bone
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 grams)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 bottle red wine
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams)

## Directions

1. Free the loin from most of the fat and rub it with a mixture of salt and pepper and lay it in a deep dish.
2. Pour on the red wine and let the loin lie in it approximately 12 hours, turning it a few times.
3. Brown the butter in a long roasting pan, lay the loin in with the meat side up and brown it in a hot oven, while basting it frequently.
4. Dilute with the marinade and let it roast until tender, approximately 1 1/2 hours.
5. Cut as in recipe 561, serve with reduced sauce mixed with cream and with fried potatoes and red currant jelly or preserved rowan berries.
