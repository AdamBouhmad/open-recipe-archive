## Ingredients

- Medium-sized crayfish
- Water (2 liters per 20 crayfish)
- Coarse salt (45 grams per 20 crayfish)
- Dill crowns to taste

## Directions

1. Rinse the crayfish in cold water.
2. Bring water with coarse salt to a boil.
3. Add the crayfish and cook for 10-15 minutes.
4. Transfer to a serving dish and cool in some of the cooking broth until evenly cooled.
