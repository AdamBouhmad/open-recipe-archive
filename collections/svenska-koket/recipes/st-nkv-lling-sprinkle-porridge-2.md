## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters milk
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 0.25 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and whisk in the eggs and mix in the melted butter, salt, and sugar.
2. Work the mixture into a smooth dough.
3. Bring the milk to a boil with cinnamon and sugar, press the dough through a colander into the boiling milk.
4. Remove from heat and let stand covered with a lid at the side of the stove for about 10 minutes.
5. Season and serve.
