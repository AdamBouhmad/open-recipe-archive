## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 2 liters water
- 15 gr salt
- 3-4 truffles

## Directions

1. Brush the artichokes clean, scrape them, rinse well, shape round, and soak in vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tbsp vinegar) for 1 hour
2. Place in boiling salted water and cook until tender
3. Remove with a slotted spoon, place on a round dish lined with a clean napkin
4. Sprinkle over with truffle cut into fine strips
5. Serve very hot with melted butter
