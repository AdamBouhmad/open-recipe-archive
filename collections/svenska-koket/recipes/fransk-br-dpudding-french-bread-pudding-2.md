## Ingredients

- 1/2 lit. milk
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 120 gr. sugar
- 130 gr. bread crumbs
- 2 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. The milk is brought to the boil with the vanilla pod and sugar, the bread is soaked in it, and the mixture is passed through a fine sieve and allowed to cool.
2. Then the whole eggs and egg yolks are added, and the mixture is worked well, after which the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks are folded in.
3. The mixture is poured into a buttered and bread-crumb-lined ring mold and cooked in a water bath for about 1 hour.
4. Turn out and serve with wine, vanilla or fruit sauce.
