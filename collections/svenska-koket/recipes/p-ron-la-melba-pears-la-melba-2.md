## Ingredients

- 100 g sugar
- 2 dcl water
- 2 dcl white wine
- 6 French or other good, loose pears
- 3 egg yolks
- 100 g powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 4 dcl thin cream
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 150 g dried apricots
- 4 dcl water
- 40-50 g sugar
- carmine
- 1 teaspoon potato flour (5 g)
- spun sugar or 50 g roasted sweet almond

## Directions

1. Make a clear syrup from the sugar, water and wine, and let it cool.
2. Peel the pears, carefully remove the blossom end, scrape the stem and place the pears directly in the sugar syrup and cook until soft, but they must not break in the least.
3. Remove them with a slotted spoon and let them cool.
4. Pack the vanilla glace (see Vanilla Glace recipe) in the bottom of a beautiful compote bowl or silver dish.
5. Place the pears on the glace, pour cold red apricot sauce over them and garnish on top with plenty of spun sugar (see spun sugar recipe) or with almonds that have been blanched, cut into strips and roasted in the oven together with 1 tablespoon sugar.
6. The apricot sauce is prepared as in recipe 462, but coloured with carmine so that it becomes beautifully red, before it is thickened with potato flour.
7. Instead of apricot sauce, a sauce made from strawberry juice can be used.
