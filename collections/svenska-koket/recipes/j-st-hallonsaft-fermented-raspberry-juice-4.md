## Ingredients

- 5 liters raspberries
- 1/2 liter boiling water
- 3 tablespoons vinegar
- 650 grams sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Clean the berries and crush them with a wooden pestle, place in a crock
2. Add the vinegar and water, mix well and let ferment for approximately 5 days, stirring occasionally
3. Strain through a sieve cloth and let drain
4. Weigh the juice, add the sugar, and when melted, cook over a hot fire with careful skimming until clear, or for approximately 10 minutes
5. Bottle hot into warm bottles
