## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr)
- 2 dcl fine preserved peas
- 750 gr peeled potato, approximately 1 1/4 liter unpeeled
- 30 gr butter
- 1-1 1/2 dcl cooked cream
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar (little grated nutmeg)

## Directions

1. Rinse the carrots well, place in cold salted water and allow to cook almost soft, then immediately remove the skin and cut the carrots into fine strips
2. Butter small pastry molds well with cold butter, line the bottom of the molds thickly with peas and garnish the sides thickly, diagonally, with carrot strips
3. Treat the potato as described on page 337, peel thinly, cut into pieces and cook quickly
4. Drain the water well and let the potato stand about 5 minutes with the lid slightly open so the steam escapes
5. Drive the potato through a puree press or stir with a fork into a mash
6. Add the cold butter immediately and work the mash with a fork until white and fluffy, then add the cooked cream together with the whisked egg yolks
7. Finally fold in the egg white beaten to stiff peaks and taste the mash carefully with the spices
8. Fill the molds halfway with mash, lay a layer of peas, then fill the molds with mash
9. Cook the pastries immediately in a water bath in the oven about 15 minutes, turn out and serve as garnish around fried meat
