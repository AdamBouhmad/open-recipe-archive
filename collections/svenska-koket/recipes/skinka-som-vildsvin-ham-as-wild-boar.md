## Ingredients

- 1 fresh ham, about 5 kg
- Marinade: 40 gr. salt, 1 tsk. white pepper, 2 dcl. crushed juniper berries, 2 bay leaves, 2 red onions in slices, 1 celery in cubes, 2 lit. weak beer, water
- For cooking: 1 bottle red wine, water, 8 allspice corns, 2 bay leaves, 1 piece ginger

## Directions

1. Remove the rind neatly from the ham.
2. Rub the ham well with salt and pepper and place it in a deep dish.
3. Sprinkle with juniper berries, bay leaves, red onion, and celery.
4. Pour over the weak beer and enough water so it covers the meat.
5. Let the ham marinate for about one week, turning it daily.
6. Cook it in wine and water with the spices as in recipe no. 556.
7. Serve warm or cold.
