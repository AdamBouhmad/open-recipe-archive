## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 1 kilogram fat
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)

## Directions

1. Treat the potatoes as in the preceding recipe and cut into 2 mm thick slices, which are cut out with a round spoon into small, even slices, which are allowed to lie in cold water for about 10 minutes.
2. Dry well in a cloth, cook in hot fat until they are crisp, lay up on absorbent paper to drain and salt.
3. Serve hot or cold with game.
