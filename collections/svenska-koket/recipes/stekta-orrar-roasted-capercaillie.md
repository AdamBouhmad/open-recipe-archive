## Ingredients

- 2 capercaillie
- 1 hg. lard
- 3-4 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 dcl. broth
- 5 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Handle and prepare as black grouse but roast for 1-1½ hours.
2. The larding may be omitted, and the birds may instead be covered with lard slices when trussed.
3. Cut, garnish (see page 300), and serve with a reduced sauce mixed with thick cream (see no. 429).
