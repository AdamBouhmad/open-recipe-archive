## Ingredients

- 36 stickleback
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 1 cup wheat flour
- 2 cups milk
- 1/2 kilogram lard
- 1 lemon, in slices
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the stickleback and rinse very well
2. Rub with salt and let sit for 1/4 hour
3. Dry with a fish towel
4. Thread through the eyes on fine wooden sticks, 6 on each
5. Dip first in milk, then in flour
6. Let sit for a while to dry
7. Fry in hot lard
8. Let drain for a few moments on gray paper
9. Arrange on a warm platter without removing from the sticks
10. Garnish with fluted lemon slices and parsley
