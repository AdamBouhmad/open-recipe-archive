## Ingredients

- Rester av gås (goose leftovers) 3-4 hg. kött (meat)
- 1/2 lit. vatten (water)
- 1 tsk. salt (salt)
- 10 gr. torkade murklor (dried mushrooms)
- 2 msk. smör (butter)
- 3 msk. mjöl (flour)
- 2 dcl. överbliven sås (leftover sauce)
- 1 1/2 hg. förvällda katrinplommon (blanched prunes)
- 1 lit. goda äpplen (good apples)
- 1 kkp krossocker (crushed sugar)
- 2 kkp vatten (water)

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into nice pieces.
2. Chop the carcass, place in cold water with salt, and let cook approximately 1 1/2 hours to make a good broth.
3. Blanch the mushrooms for 1/2 hour, let drain, wrap in cloth and cut into fine cubes.
4. Sauté the mushrooms in butter until clear, then press them out with a spoon and fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes.
5. Add the sauce and enough broth to make a thickened gravy, let cook 5 minutes.
6. Add the meat along with the mushrooms and the blanched, pitted prunes.
7. The dish should then only simmer, not boil.
8. Peel the apples, cut into four wedges and cook in a mixture of sugar and water until soft but not falling apart.
9. Arrange the ragout on a warm serving dish, garnish with apple wedges and small browned potatoes and serve as a breakfast dish.
