## Ingredients

- 2 hg fried chicken meat (leftovers)
- 1 hg salted ox tongue
- 2 hg pale celery
- 1/2 hg truffle
- 1 can asparagus tips (1/4 liter)
- 1 small pickled cucumber, in slices
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tsp salt, cayenne
- 1/4 tsp French mustard
- 2 dcl fine salad oil
- 1 tbsp tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat, tongue, celery and truffle into narrow, beautiful strips and arrange them, separately, in small piles in a wreath on a round serving platter.
2. In the middle place the asparagus, which should be well drained.
3. Between the asparagus and the other ingredients decorate with a border of cucumber slices.
4. Prepare the mayonnaise as in no. 10 and pour it in a ring around the dish.
5. Serve to the smorgasbord or as an intermediate course.
