## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes, water, salt
- 2 eggs
- 2-3 parts cream milk
- 1/2 hectogram cold butter
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1-2 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold, 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw, cook and mash them with a fork or press them through a puree press.
2. Whisk the egg yolks together with the milk and stir into the puree along with the cold butter and seasonings.
3. Beat the whites to stiff peaks and fold in last.
4. Place the puree in a buttered and bread-crumb lined mold, sprinkle with crushed rusks and crimp neatly.
5. Put the pudding in a hot oven to cook through and brown.
6. It is served with meat dishes in general.
