## Ingredients

- 40 gr. sweet almonds
- 125 gr. granulated sugar
- 1/2 tsp. butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 50 gr. sugar
- 2 dcl. thin cream
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 5 sheets gelatin
- 3 tbsp. hot water
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- Nougat for garnish
- 1 hg. green grapes

## Directions

1. Blanch and finely chop the almonds. Place the granulated sugar in a slightly warm frying pan, greased with 1/2 tsp. butter, and stir with a wooden spoon over heat until melted. Add the almonds immediately, and let the mixture come to a boil and pour onto a well-cleaned and buttered baking sheet. As soon as the nougat has cooled slightly, cut it into even squares with a greased knife and loosen from the sheet. Crush half of the nougat finely in a mortar; save the other half for garnish.
2. For the cream, beat the egg yolks and sugar for 15 minutes. Heat the thin cream with the vanilla and pour it in with vigorous whisking onto the beaten egg yolks, then let the cream simmer over heat with continued whisking. Remove the pan and whisk the cream until it is cold, then add the rinsed gelatin dissolved in the hot water and the crushed nougat. Last, carefully fold in the cream beaten to stiff peaks. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let it set on ice for approximately 3 hours. When the cream is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass plate with parchment paper. Garnish with the remaining nougat and grapes, and macaroons if desired.
