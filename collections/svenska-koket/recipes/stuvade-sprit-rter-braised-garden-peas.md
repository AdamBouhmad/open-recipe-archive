## Ingredients

- 1 liter tender garden peas (or about 5 liters unshelled)
- 10 small fresh carrots
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. flour (20 gr.)
- 4-4 1/2 dcl. pea cooking liquid
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Treat the peas as in the preceding recipe.
2. Rinse the carrots, scrape them, rinse again, dry them and cut them into fine dice.
3. Add the peas and carrots to boiling water with salt and sugar, and let them cook slowly until they are soft. Drain the cooking liquid.
