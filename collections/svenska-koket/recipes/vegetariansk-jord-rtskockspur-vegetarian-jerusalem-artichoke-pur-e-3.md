## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 small celery root
- 2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 0.5 cup cream
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the Jerusalem artichokes and celery root in the usual manner and cook them soft in the water with salt.
2. When the Jerusalem artichokes are completely soft, pass them through a sieve.
3. Melt the butter and flour together and stir with the purée and cream.
4. Let the soup cook for 10-15 minutes, then taste and adjust seasoning.
5. Serve with toasted bread or cheese sandwiches.
