## Ingredients

- 250 grams wheat flour
- 250 grams butter
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- ice water
- 250 grams granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as fine butter dough (no. 548)
2. Roll out the dough into an oblong cake of 1 cm thickness
3. Sprinkle sugar over it and fold the bottom dough in three parts
4. Tap it out slightly lengthwise and fold it across the middle (from left to right), sprinkle with sugar and roll out again to a cake of 2 cm thickness and 15 cm width
5. Fold the dough double along the middle, from top to bottom
6. Tap the dough at the top edge and cut into 1 cm thick slices
7. Turn these in sugar and place on a well-dried baking sheet with the cut side down in rows with 10 cm between the rows
8. Place the canapés in a warm oven without a bottom baking sheet
9. When they have browned on the underside, turn them and bake beautifully brown also on the other side
10. Serve them either plain or two and two stacked on top of each other with marmalade between them
