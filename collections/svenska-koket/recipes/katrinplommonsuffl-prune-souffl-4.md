## Ingredients

- 250 grams prunes
- 120 grams powdered sugar
- 5 egg whites
- For the form: 1/2 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes and cook them soft in a little water, then pit them, chop them finely and blend with the sugar.
2. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form and carefully fold in with the rest.
3. Pour everything into a buttered pie form and bake in the oven for approximately 10 minutes.
4. Serve immediately with whipped cream.
