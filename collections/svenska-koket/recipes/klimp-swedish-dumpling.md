## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 g)
- 4 decilitres boiling milk
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 g)
- 6 almonds (2 bitter)
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Melt butter and flour together for 2 minutes
2. Add the milk and let the mixture cook while stirring for 10 minutes
3. Add the sugar, the blanched and grated almonds, and the beaten eggs and let the mass simmer over the fire until it becomes appropriately thick
4. Pour the klimp into a water-rinsed mold and let it cool
5. Carefully loosen it at the edge, turn it out onto a plate or round dish and serve with bouillon or meat soups
6. If desired, sprinkle it with 1 tablespoon chopped parsley when used with bouillon, otherwise garnish it with 20 g blanched sweet almonds, cut into strips
