## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- vinegar
- eggs

## Directions

1. In a pot holding about 2 liters, pour 1½ liters water, 1 tablespoon salt, and 1 tablespoon vinegar.
2. Bring the water to a strong boil. Carefully break the eggs and slide them into a cup. Let them slide into the boiling water; the rapid motion causes the white to enclose around the firmer yolk and the egg becomes round.
3. Remove once the white has set properly, trim, and serve as a breakfast dish with various sauces, in aspic, or as garnish for other dishes.
