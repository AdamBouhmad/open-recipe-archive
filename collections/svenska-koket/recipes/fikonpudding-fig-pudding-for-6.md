## Ingredients

- 300 gr. confection figs
- 300 gr. sweet almonds
- 240 gr. sugar
- 2.5 parts cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 0.5 tbsp. butter for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Cut the figs into strips, place in a bowl covered with a lid, and set into boiling water so the figs swell.
2. Blanch the almonds, grate or run through an almond mill, pound with the sugar and then mix with the cream and egg yolks.
3. Finally mix in the figs as well, and pour the batter into a buttered and bread-crumb-strewn mold and bake in gentle oven heat.
4. Turn the pudding out onto a platter and serve with vanilla or foam sauce.
