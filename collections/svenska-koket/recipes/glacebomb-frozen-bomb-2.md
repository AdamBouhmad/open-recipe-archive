## Ingredients

- 200 gr. sugar
- 1.5 parts water
- 0.5 vanilla pod
- 8 egg yolks
- 8 parts heavy cream

## Directions

1. A clear syrup is boiled from the sugar and water, and the vanilla is allowed to cook in it until it flavors the syrup.
2. The pot is moved to the side of the heat, the egg yolks are whisked in, and everything is allowed to simmer over heat with whisking.
3. Whisking is continued until the ice is completely cold.
4. The cream, whisked to stiff peaks, is then folded in, and the ice is frozen.
5. Once frozen, it is packed into molds and set to freeze again.
6. It is turned out and served.
