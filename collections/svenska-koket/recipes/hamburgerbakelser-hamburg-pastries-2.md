## Ingredients

- 150 grams sweet almonds
- 100 grams granulated sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1/2 egg white for icing
- 60 grams granulated sugar for icing

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, grate them and mix with the sugar in a bowl
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and blend into the almond mixture
3. Work the mixture on a pastry board and roll it out into sticks, then roll them out to 4 millimeters thickness
4. Cut out small cakes with a measure, ice them and then bake in weak oven heat
