## Ingredients

- 200 gr. butter
- 200 gr. caster sugar
- 3-4 tbsp. cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 420 gr. wheat flour
- 1 heaping tsp. baking powder

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir together with the sugar until it becomes white and fluffy.
2. Mix in the cream and thereafter the egg yolks, one at a time, as well as the wheat flour, sifted with the baking powder.
3. Press the batter through a pastry bag and form into S-shapes or wreaths, which are baked golden brown in moderate oven heat.
