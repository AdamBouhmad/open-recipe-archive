## Ingredients

- 1 liter Brussels sprouts
- 3/4 liter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons melted butter (40 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage, removing all yellow and wilted leaves. Rinse it very well, place it in boiling salted water and let it cook for 15-20 minutes, or until it is tender.
2. Drain the cabbage in a colander and serve as a garnish around roasted meat (especially veal) or together with other vegetables. When the cabbage is plated, pour the melted butter over it.
3. The cabbage can also be cooked in stock, browned nicely in butter and sugar, garnished with glazed chestnuts, and served as above or as a side dish.
