## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 liters water
- 3 deciliters fine crushed barley (240 grams)
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 grams)
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 grams)

## Directions

1. Bring the water to a boil.
2. Add the rinsed barley under vigorous whisking so that the barley does not become lumpy.
3. Let the porridge simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until the barley is completely soft, or 3-4 hours, stirring often so that it does not burn on the bottom.
4. Season with the salt and cold butter and serve with milk.
