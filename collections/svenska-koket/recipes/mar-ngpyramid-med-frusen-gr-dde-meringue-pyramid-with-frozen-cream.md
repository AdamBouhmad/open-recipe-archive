## Ingredients

- 8 large egg whites
- 400 grams granulated sugar
- 1/2 liter thick cream
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar
- 5 canned pears with their syrup
- 1/2 liter thick cream for garnish
- carmine

## Directions

1. Cut 6 strips from stiff paper and form them into rings of different sizes, the first being 12 cm in diameter, each of the others 1 cm smaller than the preceding one
2. Butter the paper rings with washed butter or with salad oil, and on the outer side press the meringue batter through a piping bag in the form of meringues side by side
3. The size of the meringues decreases according to the size of the papers
4. The last paper should be a round disk on which some leaves are formed with the piping bag
5. Sprinkle the meringues with sugar and bake in a mild oven heat, remove and let cool
6. Carefully remove the paper so that the meringues do not break
7. Then arrange wreath upon wreath until they form a pyramid
8. Cut the canned pears into pieces
9. Whip the cream together with sugar until stiff peaks form, mix in the pears with their syrup, and freeze the cream
10. Fill the pyramid with this and garnish with whipped cream beaten to stiff peaks and colored with a little carmine
