## Ingredients

- 1 hg calf sweetbread
- 1 3/4 liters clear broth
- 4 parsley stems
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 g)
- 4 tbsp flour (40 g)
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- 1/2 dcl Madeira
- salt
- cayenne
- 2-3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The calf sweetbread should soak in water for 1 hour to draw out the blood, then blanch it (place in cold, lightly salted water and let it cook for 10 minutes) and cool it in cold water.
2. Bring the broth to a boil with the parsley, add the sweetbread and let it cook until white and firm, about 45 minutes. Remove it, let it cool, and cut into fine dice, which are placed in the soup bowl. Strain the broth.
3. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the broth little by little, and let it cook while stirring for 5 minutes. Season the soup with lemon juice, wine, and spices. Whisk the egg yolks and stir them into the soup, then pour it over the sweetbread in the soup bowl.
