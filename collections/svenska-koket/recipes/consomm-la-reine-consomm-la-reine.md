## Ingredients

- 2½ lit. clear consommé
- salt, cayenne
- 2 msk. finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Bring the consommé to a boil, season with the spices and add the parsley.
2. Serve with small butter pastry shells filled with chicken forcemeat (see no. 321).
