## Ingredients

- 1/2 barrel Baltic herring
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 cup coarse rye flour
- 3 tbsp butter or lard

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the Baltic herring well and debone if desired, then cut
2. Rub in the salt and let rest for a while; then turn in the coarse rye flour and fry in butter or lard
