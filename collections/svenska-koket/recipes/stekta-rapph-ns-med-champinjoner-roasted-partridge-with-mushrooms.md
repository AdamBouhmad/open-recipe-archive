## Ingredients

- 3 partridges
- 3 tbsp. butter
- 1½ tbsp. flour
- 4 parts broth
- 1 part white wine
- white pepper
- salt
- juice of half a lemon
- 20 small pre-blanched mushrooms

## Directions

1. The birds, which should be young and tender, are plucked, singed, cleaned, rinsed well and dried inside and out with a cloth. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the partridges in and let them roast over brisk heat for ½–¾ hour, turning them now and then. The butter in which the birds roasted is fried with the flour; the broth and wine are diluted, and the sauce is allowed to boil 10–15 minutes, then it is seasoned, and the lemon juice and mushrooms are added. The partridges are cut in half and laid together again so that they appear whole.
2. The sauce should be served very hot.
