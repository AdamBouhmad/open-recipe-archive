## Ingredients

- 200 grams rice
- 1.5 liters milk
- 1 egg
- 25 grams sweet almonds
- 4 bitter almonds
- grated peel of 1 lemon
- 1 egg for breading
- 0.5 cup crushed rusks for breading
- 0.5 kilogram fat for cooking

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice and cook it in the milk until it is tender.
2. Pour out the porridge, and when most of the heat has dissipated, stir in the egg, the grated almonds and lemon peel.
3. Using a spoon, form small round balls, these are turned in egg and crushed rusks and fried golden brown in fat.
