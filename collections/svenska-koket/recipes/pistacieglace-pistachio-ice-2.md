## Ingredients

- 75 gr. pistachios
- 60 gr. sweet almonds
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1 litre cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 150 gr. sugar
- a little caramel colouring (optional)
- a couple of tablespoons of arrak (optional)

## Directions

1. Blanch the pistachios and almonds, grind through a mill and pound with 1 tablespoon milk to a fine paste.
2. Add this paste to the boiling cream and let it stand covered for 20 minutes to infuse.
3. Prepare the cream as described in the basic recipe, strain and whisk until cold.
4. If desired, colour green with a little caramel colouring and flavour with a couple of tablespoons of arrak.
5. Freeze as in recipe n:o 929.
