## Ingredients

- 6 hazel grouse
- 1 hectogram butter
- 3 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 liter boiling broth
- Meat stock
- 2 liters good broth
- 12-15 sheets gelatin
- 3 egg whites and the rinsed eggshells
- 1 tablespoon cold water
- 1/2 deciliter good Madeira or sherry
- 6-8 truffles
- gelatin croutons
- 2 heads of lettuce

## Directions

1. Clean the grouse, singe them, draw them and chop off the wings and legs.
2. Rinse them very well, dry them with a meat cloth and truss them (see fowl).
3. Brown the butter, place the birds in it with the breasts up and let them roast in a warm oven for approximately 1/2 hour, basting them over and adding broth.
4. Remove them and let them cool, after which the breasts are carefully loosened from the carcasses; each breast half is cut into 3 slices.
5. Place the carcasses back in the roasting pan, add more broth and continue the cooking.
