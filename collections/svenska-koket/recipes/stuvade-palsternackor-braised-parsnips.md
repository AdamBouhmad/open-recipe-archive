## Ingredients

- 750 gr. parsnips
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 4 deciliters boiling broth
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter cream
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the parsnips, brush them clean, scrape them, rinse again, dry them and cut them with a vegetable knife into beautiful pieces.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, add the parsnips, sprinkle with sugar and flour, dilute with broth and let them cook soft.
3. Finish them with the egg yolks, whisked with cream, and let the stew simmer.
4. Season with spices and serve with meat.
