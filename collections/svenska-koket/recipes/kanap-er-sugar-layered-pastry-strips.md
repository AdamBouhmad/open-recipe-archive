## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. fine puff pastry
- 1 1/2 dcl. granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the puff pastry as in no. 33 and let it rest in a cold place 1-2 hours.
2. Roll it out thereafter into an oblong square of 1/2 cm. thickness and sprinkle with sugar, fold thereafter as ordinary puff pastry, but between each fold sprinkle sugar.
3. Roll it 3 times in the same manner and sprinkle with sugar.
4. The last time the dough is rolled out, it should, in order that the canapés become the right size, be about 4 dcm. wide and 8-10 cm. wide when the dough is finished rolling.
5. With a sharp knife cut across the dough about 1/2 cm. wide strips, which are laid with the cut side down on a well-cleaned baking sheet.
6. Bake the canapés immediately in a warm oven and turn once during baking, so that they become nicely golden brown on both sides.
7. When they have cooled, lay two and two together with jam between.
8. From this batch there will be about 15 double pastries.
