## Ingredients

- 600 g boneless, fried beef
- 80 g dry flour
- 3 g salt
- 1 tbsp butter (20 g)
- 1-1.5 dcl cream
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Cut the roast crosswise into beautiful even slices.
2. For the frying batter, whisk all ingredients together into a thick batter and let it stand and swell for about 2 hours.
3. Dip the meat slices in the batter, which should completely cover them.
4. Place at once in hot deep fat and cook until beautifully golden brown.
5. Drain on absorbent paper.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve with browned butter and vegetables and salads.
