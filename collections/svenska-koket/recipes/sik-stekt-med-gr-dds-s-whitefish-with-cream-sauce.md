## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. fetsik (fat whitefish)
- 1 msk. salt (15 gr.)
- 1 ägg (egg)
- 3 msk. mjöl (30 gr.)
- 1 hg. smör (butter)
- 4 dcl. tjock grädde (thick cream)
- 3 msk. grovhackad persilja (coarsely chopped parsley)

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean it, rinse well and dry it.
2. Cut it in two along the backbone and remove the backbone together with all small bones.
3. (If desired, remove the skin, but many consider it a delicacy.)
4. Rub the fillets with salt and let them lie for 1 hour.
5. Dry them again and cut into 3 1/2 cm. wide pieces crosswise.
6. Dip these in the whisked egg and turn them in wheat flour.
7. Brown the fish quickly in half of the butter in a frying pan and layer it with the parsley in a buttered baking dish.
8. Lay the remaining butter in flakes on top and add the cream.
9. Cover the dish and place it in the oven, and let the fish cook for about 20 minutes.
10. Serve from the dish with mashed potatoes. Instead of parsley, chopped capers may be used.
