## Ingredients

- For the sweet dough: 2 hg. butter
- 65 gr. sugar
- 1 egg
- 10 sweet almonds
- 5 bitter almonds
- 2 hg. flour
- For the apple compote: 2 hg. sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 1 1/4 liters good, tart apples
- 1 tbsp. vinegar
- For the form: 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with the sugar until fluffy, then add the egg, the blanched and ground almonds and last the flour, which should be sifted.
2. Work the dough until it becomes smooth, then let it stand in a cool place for about 2 hours.
3. Prepare the apple compote as in no. 820, let it cool and mix with the reduced liquid.
4. Butter a tin form or frying pan and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
5. Roll out two-thirds of the dough and line the form on the bottom and up to the edge.
6. Add the apple compote, fold over the edge of the dough, roll out the rest of the dough to a lid, cover the pie with it, and press the edges well.
7. Bake the pie in good oven heat for 3/4 to 1 hour, or until the dough is baked through when tested with a whisk.
8. Remove the pie, let it stand for a quarter hour, turn it out onto a platter or bowl with paper, let it cool, garnish with apple jelly or whipped cream and serve with cream or vanilla sauce.
9. Instead of apple compote, 1/2 liter of firm apple sauce can be used.
