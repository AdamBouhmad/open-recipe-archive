## Ingredients

- 150 g boneless beef
- 20 g fat
- 1/2 tablespoon flour (5 g)
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1—1 1/2 decilitres cream

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and pass together with the fat 4 times through the meat grinder
2. Pound it well together with the flour and spices in a mortar and work the forcemeat for 1/2 hour, while adding the cream little by little
3. Taste and adjust the forcemeat and form with 2 teaspoons, which are dipped in hot water, into very small balls
4. Place them in boiling bouillon or salted water (not too many at once) and let them cook 2—3 minutes
5. Trim the meatballs before placing them in the soup bowl
