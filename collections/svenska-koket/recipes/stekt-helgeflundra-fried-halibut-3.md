## Ingredients

- 1 kg halibut
- 5 tablespoons salt (75 g)
- 3 liters water
- 0.5 lemon
- 0.5 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 100 g butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish piece, remove the dark skin and treat the fish as in the preceding recipe.
2. After rubbing the fish piece with lemon, brush it with the beaten egg on the side from which the skin has been removed, and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
3. Grease a baking pan with half of the butter over an area as large as the fish piece; place it in the pan with the breaded side up, cover with the remaining butter distributed in flakes, and fry in a moderately warm oven for about 40 minutes, basting frequently with the butter in the pan.
4. If desired, the butter can be diluted with 2 deciliters of boiling water.
5. Garnish as above, serve with lemon or mushroom sauce and boiled potatoes.
6. Alternatively, after rubbing with lemon juice, the fish piece can be cut crosswise into 1.5 cm thick slices, which are beaten with a damp meat mallet, dipped in beaten egg, turned in crushed rusks, fried in butter on the stovetop and served with lemon sauce, stewed vegetables or potato puree.
