## Ingredients

- 12 veal cutlets
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3/4 cup cream milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 cups crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 liter boiling broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the cutlets with a cloth dipped in hot water, trim and pound once on each side.
2. Sprinkle with pepper and salt, dip in the beaten egg mixed with cream milk and turn in the crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Brown the cutlets in the heated butter, add the liquid, and let them fry slowly for 10-15 minutes with the lid closed.
4. Place on a platter, pour the sauce over, and garnish with parsley.
5. If desired, the breading (egg and crushed breadcrumbs) can be omitted.
