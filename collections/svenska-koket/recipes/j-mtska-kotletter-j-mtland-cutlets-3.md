## Ingredients

- 300 g cooked or fried boneless meat
- 100 g fat smoked bacon
- 1 red onion
- 150 g cooked grated potatoes
- 1–2 teaspoons salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the onion
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk
- 1 egg
- 1 egg white
- 2 deciliters crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)

## Directions

1. Chop the meat finely together with the bacon.
2. Peel and chop the onion finely, sauté in butter and work into the forcemeat with a chopping knife, along with the potatoes, spices, the beaten egg and egg yolk.
3. Form oblong cutlets from the forcemeat, 1 cm thick.
4. Dip in the beaten egg and egg white and turn in crushed rusks.
5. Fry in butter over gentle heat until nicely golden brown.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve with stewed vegetables or macaroni.
