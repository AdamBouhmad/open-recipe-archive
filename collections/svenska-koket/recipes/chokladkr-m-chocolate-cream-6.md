## Ingredients

- 3 parts milk
- 1½ hectograms unsweetened chocolate (cocoa powder)
- 1½ teaspoons sugar
- 3 eggs
- 6 sheets gelatin
- ¾ liter thick cream

## Directions

1. Pour the milk, chocolate, sugar, and eggs into a saucepan and bring to a boil over the fire with vigorous whisking.
2. Remove the saucepan from the heat, but continue whisking until the cream has cooled.
3. Then add the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water and the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mixture into a mold rinsed with cold water and sprinkled with sugar, and let it set in a cold place for approximately 2½ hours.
5. When the cream is to be turned out, hold the mold for a moment in lukewarm water.
6. Serve with whipped cream.
