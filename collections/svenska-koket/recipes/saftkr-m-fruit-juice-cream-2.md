## Ingredients

- 4 dcl sweet or sour juice
- 6 dcl water (if using sour juice)
- 3 dcl sugar
- 50 gr mondamin flour or 50 gr potato flour

## Directions

1. The cream turns out best if two kinds of juice are used.
2. Mix juice and water and add sugar if the juice is sour.
3. Let the mixture boil up, taste and add more sugar if needed, and thicken with the flour mixed in 1 dcl cold water.
4. Let the cream boil for 5 minutes if thickened with mondamin flour, but only 2 minutes if thickened with potato flour.
5. Pour the cream immediately into a compote bowl rinsed with warm water and sprinkle with a little sugar so that it does not form skin.
