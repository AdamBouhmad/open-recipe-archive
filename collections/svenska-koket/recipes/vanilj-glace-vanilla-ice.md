## Ingredients

- 1 large vanilla pod
- 1 litre cream
- 8 egg yolks
- 150 gr. sugar
- 2 dcl. thick cream, whipped to stiff peaks (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil the cream with 1 large vanilla pod, then let the pod infuse in the cream for about 20 minutes.
2. Prepare the cream according to the basic recipe description.
3. When cold, if desired, fold in 2 dcl. thick cream whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Freeze as in recipe n:o 929.
5. Serve with ice cream bread.
