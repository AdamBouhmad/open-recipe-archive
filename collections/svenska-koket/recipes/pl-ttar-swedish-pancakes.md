## Ingredients

- 2.5-3 cups flour
- 1 liter unskim milk
- 0.5 teaspoon salt
- 4 tablespoons clarified butter
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix with salt.
2. Whip the eggs with two cups of the milk and add to the flour with vigorous whisking.
3. When the batter is smooth and glossy, stir in half the butter, and when it is well mixed, add the remaining milk.
4. Let the batter stand for 1 hour so the flour can swell.
5. Give it a vigorous whip.
6. Heat the pancake pan slowly, brush with clarified butter, and fry pancakes from the batter.
7. Thin pancakes can also be fried from the same batter.
