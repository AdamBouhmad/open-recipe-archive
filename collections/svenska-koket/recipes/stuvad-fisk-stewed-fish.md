## Ingredients

- 1½ kilograms cooked fish
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- ¼ liter fish stock
- ¼ liter milk or cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Free the cooked fish from skin and bones and divide it into appropriately sized pieces.
2. Fry the butter and flour together, gradually add the fish stock and milk with vigorous stirring, and let the sauce cook 10 minutes.
3. Add the fish, stir the stew gently and let it come to a brief boil, after which it is tasted and seasoned.
