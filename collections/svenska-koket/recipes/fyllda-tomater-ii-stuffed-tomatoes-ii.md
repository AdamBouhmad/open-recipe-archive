## Ingredients

- 6 large, beautiful tomatoes
- 1/2 liter polonaise salad
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped truffle for garnish

## Directions

1. Treat the tomatoes as in the previous recipe
2. Fill with polonaise salad (see recipe 744)
3. Sprinkle with finely chopped truffle
4. Serve with cold roast beef and ham
