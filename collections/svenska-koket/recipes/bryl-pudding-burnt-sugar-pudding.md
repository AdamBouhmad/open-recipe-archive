## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters cream milk
- 3 tbsp powdered sugar
- 6 bitter almonds
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 6 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 cups powdered sugar for the mold
- 2 tbsp boiling water

## Directions

1. Caramelize the sugar in a pan and dilute it with the boiling water, then use this to glaze a mold.
2. Cook the milk with the sugar, almonds, lemon peel and vanilla until it has absorbed a good flavor from the spices.
3. Strain the mixture, and when most of the heat has dissipated, add the whisked eggs.
4. Pour the mixture into the prepared mold and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for approximately 3/4 hour.
5. When the pudding is cold, turn it out and serve with its sauce.
