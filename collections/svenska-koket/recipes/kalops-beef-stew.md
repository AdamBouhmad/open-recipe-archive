## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless beef (thigh, rib or marrow bone)
- ½ tsp white pepper
- 1 tbsp salt
- 3 tbsp flour
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp butter
- 12 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 6 cups water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water. It is then cut across the grain into approximately 1½ cm thick slices. The slices are freed from coarser sinews and fat and beaten lightly.
2. White pepper, salt and flour are mixed on a plate, and in this the meat slices are turned.
3. The onion is peeled and cut into slices.
4. The meat and onion are browned quickly in the butter. The browned meat is placed in a pot, the frying pan is whisked out with a little boiling water, which is poured over the meat.
5. The allspice berries, bay leaves and the remaining water are added, and the kalops is allowed to simmer slowly with a tightly closing lid until the meat feels tender.
6. It is placed in a serving dish, and the sauce is skimmed.
