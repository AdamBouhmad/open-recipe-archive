## Ingredients

- 250 gr fine prunes (approximately 23 pieces)
- 1 dcl granulated sugar (85 gr)
- 7 dcl water
- 2 dcl French wine
- 1 1/2 tbsp potato flour (23 gr)

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes very well and layer them alternately with the sugar in a bowl, add the water and let the prunes soak overnight.
2. Cut them in half lengthwise and remove the pits.
3. Crush half of the pits, blanch the almonds and chop them finely.
4. Place the prunes together with the almonds in the water they soaked in, add the wine and let the prunes cook until they are quite loose.
5. Thicken the cream with the potato flour stirred in 1/2 dcl cold water, let it cook 2 minutes, taste it and pour it into a compote bowl, rinsed with lukewarm water.
6. Serve cold with cream or milk.
