## Ingredients

- 3 parts milk
- 1.5 hectograms unsweetened chocolate (cocoa powder)
- 1.5 coffee spoons sugar
- 3 eggs
- 6 sheets gelatin
- 0.25 liters thick cream

## Directions

1. Pour the milk, chocolate, sugar, and eggs into a saucepan and bring to a boil over heat with vigorous whisking.
2. Lift the saucepan from heat, but continue whisking until the cream has cooled.
3. Then add the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water and the cream beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mixture into a mold rinsed with cold water and sprinkled with sugar and let it set in a cold place for about 2.5 hours.
5. When the cream is to be turned out, hold the mold briefly in lukewarm water.
6. Serve with whipped cream.
