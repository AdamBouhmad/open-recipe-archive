## Ingredients

- 1 old hen
- 1 kg. veal topside
- 1 kg. veal neck
- 4 liters water
- 10 gr. salt
- 1 carrot
- 1 parsnip
- 1/4 celeriac
- 1 small leek
- 2 hg. boneless lean veal (for clarification)
- 1 egg white and rinsed eggshell (for clarification)

## Directions

1. Pluck the hen, singe it, remove, rinse and let it soak in water for 12 hours. Dry it with a kitchen towel and cut into smaller pieces. Rinse the veal quickly, dry it, cut into small pieces and crack the bones well.
2. Cook the broth as in recipe no. 222. Clarify it in the same way as described in recipe no. 223, and use it for light soups.
