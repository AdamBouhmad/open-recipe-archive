## Ingredients

- 1 pike perch weighing 1.5 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 2 tablespoons clarified butter (40 g)
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tablespoons finely crushed rusks
- For frying: 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 2 balls crayfish butter (20 g)
- 4 decilitres thick cream

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and prepare as in recipe no. 353.
2. Garnish with mushroom caps fried in butter.
3. Serve with mushroom sauce and pressed potatoes.
4. Note: If the fish is served at a multi-course dinner, 1 kg of pike perch is sufficient for 6 persons.
