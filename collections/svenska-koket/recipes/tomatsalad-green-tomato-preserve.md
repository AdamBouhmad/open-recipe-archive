## Ingredients

- 2 liters green, unripe tomatoes
- 1 kg. lump sugar
- 4 deciliters vinegar
- 3 deciliters wine vinegar
- 15 cloves
- 1/4 pod Spanish pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the tomatoes, rinse them, prick them with a small silver fork, blanch them in boiling water until they feel soft when tested with a whisk, then spread them on linen to drain.
2. When they are cold, place them in a jar.
3. Cook a liquid from the sugar, vinegar and spices, strain it hot over the tomatoes.
4. Cover the jar with a doubled kitchen towel, place a plate and weight on top, and let the contents stand for three days, after which strain off the liquid, bring it to the boil and pour it again hot over the fruit.
5. Repeat this two more times with six days in between; the last time let the liquid cool before pouring it on.
6. The liquid should stand well over the tomatoes.
7. Cover the jar well.
