## Ingredients

- Large fish of 1.5–2 kg or small fish and slices of larger fish
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Cold butter for baking
- Lemon juice
- Parsley, blanched and chopped

## Directions

1. Larger fish should preferably be cut into fillets and fried in the oven; small fish and slices of larger fish are fried in a pan on the stove.
2. After cleaning and rinsing the fish, dry it well, rub with a little salt and pepper if desired, and turn in flour.
3. For oven frying: place in a pan generously buttered with cold butter, put in a warm oven and baste often with the butter until cooked through.
4. For stovetop frying: place in a frying pan in very hot butter, turn and fry on both sides until a nice golden-brown color is achieved.
5. Place on a warm plate, pour over a few drops of lemon juice, garnish on top with chopped blanched parsley, and pour melted brown butter over just before serving.
