## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. water (scant)
- 2 hg. butter
- 5 gr. dried morels
- 150 gr. veal sweetbread
- 1/2 litre water
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1 tin champignons (1/4 litre)
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 1 dcl. sweetbread stock
- 1 dcl. asparagus stock
- 1 dcl. cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 sheets gelatin
- 1 egg for breading
- 1 cup crushed croutons
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare the puff pastry as in recipe no. 33, wrap in a napkin and let rest for several hours.
2. Prepare the stew as in recipe no. 47. When it has simmered with the egg yolk, immediately add the gelatin, rinsed in cold water and dissolved in 1 tbsp. hot water. Season the stew with spices, spread thinly on a flat dish and let cool.
3. Roll out the puff pastry to 2 mm thickness and cut small round cakes with a large round cutter (risole cutter). Place 1 dessertspoon of stew in the middle of each cake. Brush the edges with egg white and fold together so the cake forms a half-moon shape, then crimp the folded edge.
