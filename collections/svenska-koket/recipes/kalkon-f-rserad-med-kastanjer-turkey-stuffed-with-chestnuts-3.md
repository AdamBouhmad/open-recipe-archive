## Ingredients

- 1 turkey cock of approximately 5 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 1/2 hectograms lard, in 1 slice
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 1 1/2 liters broth
- 1 1/2 kg chestnuts
- 3/4 liter broth for filling
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 1/2 tablespoon salt, 1 teaspoon sugar

## Directions

1. The turkey is treated as in no. 605 and rubbed with a mixture of salt and pepper.
2. The chestnuts are peeled and allowed to lie in hot water until the inner thin skin can be removed with a cloth.
3. The peeled chestnuts are placed in the broth, together with the butter and spices and cooked until soft but must not fall apart.
4. They are drained in a colander and allowed to cool.
5. The turkey is filled with the chestnuts, treated thereafter as in the preceding recipe and roasted either in an oven or pot.
6. It is carved, garnished with small pork sausages, browned potatoes and green salad.
7. Served with gravy or reduced sauce (see no. 429).
