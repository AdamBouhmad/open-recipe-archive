## Ingredients

- 1 unflayed hare, approximately 3 1/2 kg
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 bottle red wine
- 1 bay leaf
- 6 allspice berries
- 1/4 teaspoon thyme
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 8 pickled onions, in halves
- 10 mushrooms, in slices
- 8 pitted olives

## Directions

1. Prepare the hare as in the previous recipe, cut into smaller pieces, which are rubbed with the spices and placed in a stone crock.
2. Mix all marinade ingredients and pour over the hare, which should rest until the following day.
3. Remove the meat, wrap well in cloth and strain the marinade.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the meat, brown it and sprinkle with flour.
5. Dilute the marinade and let the meat simmer slowly with the lid tightly closed for approximately 1 hour, shaking the pot often so the meat does not stick to the bottom.
6. Add the onions, mushrooms and olives, cut into thin rings, and let the braise simmer until everything is tender, approximately 1/2 hour.
7. Place the meat on a warm platter, whisk up the sauce, taste it and pour over.
8. Garnish the dish with small triangular bread croutons and serve with boiled or fried potatoes.
