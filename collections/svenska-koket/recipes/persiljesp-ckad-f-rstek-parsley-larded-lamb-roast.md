## Ingredients

- 2 kg lamb roast
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1 tbsp salt
- 6 allspice berries
- 1 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Crack the bones and wash the lamb roast with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Mix the parsley well with 1 tbsp of the butter, make holes in the roast with a pointed wooden stick, and insert some of the parsley butter in each hole.
3. Place the roast in a long pan, buttered with cold butter, and put in the oven to brown.
4. When well browned, salt it, add the allspice berries, and add the boiling liquid.
5. The roasting takes 1½-2 hours. During this time, baste the roast around every ten minutes.
6. Strain the meat drippings, skim, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
