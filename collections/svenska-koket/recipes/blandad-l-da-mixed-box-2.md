## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 kkp milk
- 2 msk. crushed rusks
- 2 hg. cooked or roasted meat
- 1/2 soaked herring
- 1/2 msk. finely chopped parsley
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs and milk well, add the crushed rusks and let the mixture stand a while so the rusks swell.
2. Cut the meat and herring into fine cubes and stir into the egg mixture together with the parsley.
3. Season well with the spices, pour into a well buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie pan and bake about 45 minutes in ordinary oven heat.
4. Serve as a breakfast dish with clarified butter.
