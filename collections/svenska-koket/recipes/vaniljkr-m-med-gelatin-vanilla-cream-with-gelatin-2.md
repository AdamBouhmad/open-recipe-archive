## Ingredients

- 6 sheets gelatin
- 3 msk. water
- 1 egg
- 110 gr. sugar
- 1 1/2 dcl. milk
- 1 vanilla pod
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 1 msk. vanilla sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin with cold water, cut it into pieces, dissolve it in 3 msk. boiling water and mix with the egg, egg yolks, sugar, milk, and vanilla in a well-scoured pot.
2. Whisk the mixture vigorously over heat until the cream is thick and frothy, then remove the pot from heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
3. Fold in the cream beaten to stiff peaks, and pour the cream into a mold rinsed with water and sprinkled with sugar, and let it stand on ice for 2-3 hours to set.
4. When the cream is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish with pastry paper and garnish with fruit or biscuits.
5. Alternatively, half of the cream can be colored green with pistachio paste, filled into the mold and allowed to set slightly on ice, then pour in the rest of the light yellow cream. Let the cream set on ice, turn it out, and serve with raspberry or strawberry sauce.
