## Ingredients

- 6 fine veal cutlets
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 3 1/2 tbsp. butter (70 gr.)
- 150 gr. boneless veal for the farce
- 40 gr. butter
- 1/2 tbsp. flour (5 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped truffle
- 1 dcl. thick cream
- 100 gr. flour
- approx. 3/4 dcl. water
- 175 gr. butter
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal cutlets as in recipe no. 514, fry quickly in butter, place on a dish and let cool
2. Prepare the veal farce as in recipe no. 325 and spread on the upper side of the cutlets
3. Prepare the butter dough as in recipe no. 33, roll out to 1/2 cm. thickness and cut out appropriately sized pieces, which are folded around the cutlets, but so that the bone remains free
4. The cutlets can be baked in the oven or cooked in fat. If baked in the oven, brush the butter dough with the whisked egg, place the cutlets on a baking sheet and bake golden brown in a warm oven. If cooked in fat, turn them after brushing with egg, in a mixture of finely crushed breadcrumbs and a little grated cheese, cook golden brown in hot fat and let drain on absorbent paper
5. Serve very hot, placed on pastry paper
