## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. chestnuts
- 4-5 dcl. broth
- 1 small celery stalk
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 g.)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 g.)
- 2-3 tbsp. thick cream
- salt
- a little white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut a 1/2 cm. deep cross in the pointed end of the chestnuts, then place them in boiling water and let them boil 2 minutes so the shells loosen.
2. Remove both the outer and inner shells while the chestnuts are warm; otherwise the shells stick easily.
3. Place the chestnuts together with the celery stalk and sugar in the cold broth and let them cook until they are completely soft, then pass them through a sieve.
4. Mix the purée with the butter and cream and heat it and season with the spices.
5. Serve preferably in pastry shells with goose, duck, pork loin, etc.
