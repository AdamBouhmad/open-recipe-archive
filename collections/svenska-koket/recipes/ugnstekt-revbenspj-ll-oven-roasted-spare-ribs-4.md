## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg spare ribs
- 1.5 tbsp salt (23 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tsp dry mustard
- ginger
- 2 tbsp butter (40 g)
- 1/2 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. The spare ribs, which should be thinly cut, break them, dry with a cloth wrung out in hot water, rub with a mixture of salt, white pepper and ginger and let stand until the next day.
2. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the spare ribs with the meaty side up and brown nicely, basting frequently with the fat in the pan.
3. Add the boiling broth or water and let the spare ribs cook with continued basting until tender, about 1.5 hours.
4. Cut into attractive pieces and serve warm.
