## Ingredients

- 3/4 kilogram fresh or 1 3/4 liters canned rhubarb
- 1/2 liter water
- 2 cups crushed sugar
- 1 1/2 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the rhubarb, peel thinly, and cut into pieces approximately 1 1/2 centimeters long.
2. Make a clear syrup from the sugar and water.
3. Place the rhubarb in the syrup and cook until soft.
4. Thicken the cream with potato flour mixed in a little cold water, bring to a short boil, and pour into a jelly mold or carrot mold to cool.
