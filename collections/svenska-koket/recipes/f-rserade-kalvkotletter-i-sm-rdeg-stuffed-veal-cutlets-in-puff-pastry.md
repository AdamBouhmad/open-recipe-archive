## Ingredients

- 6 fine veal cutlets
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 3 1/2 msk. butter (70 gr.)
- 150 gr. boneless veal meat
- 40 gr. butter
- 1/2 msk. flour (5 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 1 tsk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 msk. finely chopped truffle
- 1 dcl. thick cream
- 1 hg. flour
- 3/4 dcl. water
- 175 gr. butter
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal cutlets as in no. 514, quickly fry in butter, place on a platter and let cool.
2. Prepare the veal forcemeat as in no. 325 and spread on the upper side of the cutlets.
3. Prepare the puff pastry as in no. 33, roll out to 1/2 cm. thickness and cut out suitably sized pieces, which are folded around the cutlets, so that the bone remains free.
4. The cutlets can be baked in the oven or cooked in fat. If baked in the oven, brush the pastry with the beaten egg, place the cutlets on a sheet and bake golden brown in a warm oven. If cooked in fat, turn them after brushing with egg, in a mixture of finely crushed breadcrumbs and a little grated cheese, cook golden brown in hot fat and let drain on paper.
5. Serve very hot, placed on baking paper.
