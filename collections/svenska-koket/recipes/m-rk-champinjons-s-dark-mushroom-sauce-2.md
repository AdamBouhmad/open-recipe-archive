## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 4 parts strong meat stock
- 1 can of mushrooms at 1/2 liter
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Split the mushrooms, fry them in the butter and remove.
2. Then fry the flour with the butter, add the mushroom stock and meat stock with frequent stirring, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
3. Add the fried mushrooms and taste and adjust the sauce.
