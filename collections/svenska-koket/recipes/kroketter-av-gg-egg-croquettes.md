## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr.)
- 4 deciliter cream
- 2 sheets gelatin
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 teaspoon anchovy sauce
- 4 tablespoon flour for breading
- 2 eggs for breading
- salt for breading
- 2 teaspoon cooking oil for breading
- 1 cup crushed rusks for breading
- 1 kg. lard for frying

## Directions

1. Cook eggs for 10 minutes, cool in cold water, shell and cut into fine dice.
2. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Carefully stir in the diced eggs together with the rinsed gelatin dissolved in 1 tablespoon hot water.
4. Season the stew with spices and treat as in recipe no. 49.
5. Form the croquettes into round shapes.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish with stewed peas or spinach.
