## Ingredients

- 2 stora salta fetsillar
- 1 lit. kokt, kall potatis
- 3 ägg
- 2 tsk. mjöl (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. mjölk
- 1/2 tsk. vitpeppar
- 1-2 msk. finhackad persilja
- 1/2 msk. smör till formen
- 2 msk. stötta skorpor till formen

## Directions

1. Skin the herrings, remove the insides, rinse well, clean, remove the backbone and lay the fillets in water the night before they are to be used.
2. Wrap them in a fish towel or white paper, free them from all small bones and cut them into strips or 3 cm. wide pieces across.
3. Prepare and serve the pudding as in recipe 399.
