## Ingredients

- Juice and peel of 2 oranges
- 150 gr. lump sugar
- 1 egg, 3 egg yolks
- 1 dcl. milk
- 1 tablespoon sherry
- 2 gr. citric acid
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- 5 sheets gelatin, 3 tablespoons water
- 1 orange for garnish
- 1/2 oz. lady fingers

## Directions

1. Wash the oranges well, dry them, and grate the peel against the sugar, which is placed in a well-scoured saucepan.
2. Press out the orange juice and strain it over the sugar.
3. Add the egg, egg yolks, and milk, and whip the mixture until the sugar is melted, then let it simmer over the heat with vigorous whipping until the cream is thick and fluffy.
4. Remove the saucepan from the heat and continue whipping until the cream is cold, then add the sherry and the crushed citric acid.
5. Rinse the gelatin, cut into pieces, and dissolve in 3 tablespoons of hot water.
6. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
7. Add the gelatin to the cream while whipping and then add the cream very carefully.
8. Rinse a beautiful tin mold with cold water and sprinkle with granulated sugar, pour in the mass, and let it set on ice for 2-3 hours.
9. When the cream is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass plate with parchment paper, and garnish with candied orange segments and lady fingers.
