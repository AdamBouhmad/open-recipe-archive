## Ingredients

- Scraps of sponge cake, diced (approximately 5 deciliters)
- 1 deciliter currants (60 g)
- 30 g candied fruit (succate), chopped
- 4 eggs
- 50 g sugar
- 4 deciliters good milk
- 1 tablespoon butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Place the sponge cake, which should be cut into small dice, in a buttered and rusk-breadcrumbed timbale mold in layers with the well-rinsed currants and the chopped candied fruit
2. Whisk the eggs well with the sugar, add the milk and use this to fill the mold
3. Bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour
4. Turn out and serve very hot with warm vanilla sauce
