## Ingredients

- 150 grams sweet almond
- 100 grams granulated sugar
- 1 egg white
- 1/2 egg white for icing
- 60 grams granulated sugar for icing

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds, grate and mix with the sugar in a bowl
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and blend into the almond mass
3. Work the mass on a work surface and roll out into sticks, which are rolled out to 4 mm thickness
4. Cut out small cakes with a measure, which are iced and then baked in weak oven heat
