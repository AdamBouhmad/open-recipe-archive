## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg boneless veal from the loin
- 5 parts cream
- white pepper
- salt
- 4 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut into approximately 1 cm thick slices, straight across the fibers, and place in the cream 3-4 hours before it is to be served.
2. Pat the meat slices dry with a hand towel and sprinkle on both sides with pepper and salt.
3. Brown the butter in a frying pan, place the meat slices in and cook slowly on both sides.
4. Place on a warm platter or carrot.
5. Whisk out the pan with the cream, which is then poured over the meat.
