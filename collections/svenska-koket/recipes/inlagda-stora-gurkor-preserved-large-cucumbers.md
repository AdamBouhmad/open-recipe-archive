## Ingredients

- 4 large white cucumbers
- 6 teaspoons salt (30 grams)
- 2 liters boiling water
- 2 1/2 liters spirit vinegar
- 6 grams whole white pepper
- Horseradish in small pieces (1 deciliter)
- 4-5 dill heads
- 1 kilogram granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the cucumbers as in the previous recipe, place them in a bowl, sprinkle with salt and let them lie for 24 hours.
2. Remove them and lay them on double linen to drain.
3. Place the cucumbers back in a bowl and pour the boiling water over them.
4. After 5 minutes, remove them, wrap them well in dry linen, place them in a crock, and heat 1 1/2 liters of the vinegar and pour it over them.
5. Cover the crock with a folded towel and plate and let it stand in a cool place for 3 days.
6. Remove the cucumbers, wrap them in a cloth (discarding the vinegar) and layer them alternately with the white pepper, horseradish and dill heads in a jar.
7. Heat the remaining vinegar with the sugar and pour it hot over the cucumbers, which are covered with double linen and allowed to cool.
8. Bind the jar well. These cucumbers are strong but exceptionally good.
