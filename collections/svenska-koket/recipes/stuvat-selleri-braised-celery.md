## Ingredients

- 3 large root celerias
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt for blanching
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 4 deciliters boiling broth
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter cream
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the celerias, brush them clean, peel them, cut them into cubes and blanch for 7-8 minutes with the lid open.
2. Drain them in a colander and let them drip on a cloth.
3. Prepare them afterwards as braised parsnips, see recipe 693.
