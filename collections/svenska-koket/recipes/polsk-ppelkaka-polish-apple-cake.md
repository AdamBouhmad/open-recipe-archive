## Ingredients

- 8 good, tart apples
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. sugar (128 gr.)
- 1 dcl. white wine
- 1/2 dcl. water
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.)
- 1/2 liter good milk
- 3 eggs
- 4 tbsp. sugar (60 gr.)
- 25 sweet almonds (about 25 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them, cut them into 6-8 wedges, free them from the cores, place them in a preserving pan together with butter, sugar, wine and water and cook as compote, while shaking the pan often.
2. Place the apples in a buttered pie form, cook the liquid together until it is syrupy and pour it over them.
3. Whisk the flour and 1 dcl. of the milk into a slurry.
4. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar for 15 minutes.
5. Boil the rest of the milk, add the slurry, let the milk boil again and pour it over the beaten egg yolks with vigorous whisking.
6. Add the blanched and ground almond, let the cream simmer and whisk it thereafter until it is cold.
7. Season, if desired, with 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar, then fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
8. Pour the cream over the compote and bake the cake in ordinary oven heat for about 3/4 hour.
9. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream.
