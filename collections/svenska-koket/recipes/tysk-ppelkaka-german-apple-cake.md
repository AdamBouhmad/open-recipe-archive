## Ingredients

- 1 12-öre wheat bread
- 2 tsk. butter
- 5 parts milk
- 5 eggs
- 5 parts apple pulp
- 2 tsk. currants
- ½ tsk. butter for the form

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into slices.
2. Whisk two eggs with half the milk, and soak the bread slices in this, then fry them lightly in the butter.
3. Place them in a buttered form and cover with the apple pulp and the rinsed and cleaned currants.
4. Make a custard from the remaining milk and eggs, which is poured over the cake, which is immediately placed in the oven to bake.
5. Turn out, sprinkle with sugar and serve with whipped cream.
