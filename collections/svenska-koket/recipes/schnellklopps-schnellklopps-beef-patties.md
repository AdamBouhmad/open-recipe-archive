## Ingredients

- 750 gr beef loin, beef steak or boneless inner loin
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- For the sauce: 1 tbsp butter (20 gr)
- 1 tbsp finely chopped red onion
- 1.5 tbsp flour (15 gr)
- 4 dcl thick cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until properly tender, is treated and fried as in the previous recipe. The steaks are placed on a hot plate.
2. For the sauce, the butter is fried in the frying pan, the onion and flour are added, and everything is browned nicely together.
3. The cream is added, and the sauce is allowed to boil for a couple of minutes and is poured over the steaks.
4. Served with fried potatoes sprinkled with finely chopped parsley.
