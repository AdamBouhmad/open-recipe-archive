## Ingredients

- 1 calf brain
- water
- vinegar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 3 parts cream milk
- salt
- white pepper
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the calf brain in water so that the blood runs out
2. Blanch it (that is, place it in cold water and bring it to a boil) in vinegar-mixed water, take it up and let it drain
3. Place it again in boiling and salted water, and let it cook 20-30 minutes
4. Take up the brain and cut it into small cubes
5. Fry butter and flour together, and add the cream, after which let the sauce simmer 10 minutes
6. Add the brain, and season the stew with pepper, salt, and if desired, with lemon juice
