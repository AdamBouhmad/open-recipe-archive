## Ingredients

- 5 liters yellow gooseberries
- 5 liters green gooseberries
- 6 liters red gooseberries
- 8 liters water (or as much as it stands over the berries)
- 0.5 kg sugar per kg juice

## Directions

1. Preparation is the same as gooseberry juice I.
