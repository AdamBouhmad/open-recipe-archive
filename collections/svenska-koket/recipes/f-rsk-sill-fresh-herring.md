## Ingredients

- Fresh herring
- Salt
- Melted butter
- Lemon juice
- Boiled potatoes
- Eggs
- Milk
- Parsley
- Roe and liver

## Directions

1. The fish is treated as in recipe 384. The head and tail are cut off and not used
2. The fish is rubbed with salt and left for 2 hours, then dried well
3. Cut into 4 1/2 cm wide pieces across the grain and place in a butter-greased ovenproof dish, preferably with a lid, together with the roe and liver, cut into pieces
4. Beat the eggs well with the milk, mix in the parsley and pour the egg mixture over the fish
5. Place the lid on or cover the dish with buttered, double butter paper
6. Cook the dish in a water bath in the oven for approximately 40 minutes or until the fish feels tender
7. Serve from the dish with melted butter mixed with lemon juice and boiled potatoes
