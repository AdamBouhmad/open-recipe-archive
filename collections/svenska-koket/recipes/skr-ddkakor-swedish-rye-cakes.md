## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg rye sifted flour
- 325 gr wheat flour
- 1/2 liter skimmed milk
- 25 gr pressed yeast
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 1/2 tablespoon crushed fennel or anise (or 2 tablespoons syrup)

## Directions

1. Make a dough from the rye flour, some of the wheat flour, the lukewarm milk, and the yeast mixed with salt, as described on page 462, and let the dough rise to double its size.
2. Work in the fennel and syrup, if desired, along with wheat flour and let it rise again.
3. Work the dough well on the baking board and divide it into 5 parts, from which round cakes are formed, which are nicked, and a hole is made with a round measure in the middle of each cake.
4. From the removed dough a smaller cake is made.
5. Place the cakes on greased baking sheets and bake, when they are well risen, in a very hot oven.
