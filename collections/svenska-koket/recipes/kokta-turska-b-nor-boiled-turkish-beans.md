## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram fresh Turkish beans
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 2-3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Clean the beans, rinse and cut diagonally into pieces, unless they are tender, in which case serve them whole.
2. Place in boiling, salted water (short broth) and let simmer slowly until tender.
3. Add the chopped parsley and cold butter, and mix well.
