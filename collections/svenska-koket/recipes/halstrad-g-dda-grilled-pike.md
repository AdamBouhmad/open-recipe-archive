## Ingredients

- 2 pike weighing 5 hectograms each
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 1 hectogram clarified butter
- 1 lemon for garnish
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the pike, rinse well and dry.
2. Cut into fillets and loosen the flesh from the skin.
3. Remove the backbone and all small bones.
4. Rub the fillets with salt and vinegar and let sit for 1/2 hour, then dry well and cut each fillet into 2 pieces crosswise.
5. Roll the fish pieces in flour and dip in melted butter.
6. Heat a double grill over hot coals, place the fish pieces on it and grill over coals on both sides until they take on a golden-brown color and are cooked through.
7. Immediately place on a warm serving platter, garnish with fluted lemon slices and parsley.
8. Serve as a breakfast dish with maître d'hôtel butter and potatoes.
