## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg melon
- 2 parts caster sugar (170 g)
- 1 deciliter water
- 1-2 tablespoons fine roe
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the melon, which should be ripe, into 1 cm thick slices, and cut these into 5 cm long pieces.
2. Place the cut melon in 1 liter of boiling water and let it boil for 2-3 minutes, then remove it with a slotted spoon and let it drain on cloth.
3. Cook a clear, syrupy syrup from sugar, water, roe, and lemon juice.
4. Add the melon and let it cook until it is soft and clear.
5. Transfer to a compote bowl, then boil the syrup together if desired, and pour over.
6. Serve cold with biscuits.
