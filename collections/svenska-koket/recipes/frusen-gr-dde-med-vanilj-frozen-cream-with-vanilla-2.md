## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter heavy cream
- Vanilla sugar to taste

## Directions

1. The cream is whisked to stiff peaks and then flavored to taste with vanilla sugar.
2. It is poured into a freezing mold and frozen, though not too hard.
3. It is packed in an ice form, and this is placed on ice.
4. When the cream is to be served, it is turned out of the mold and garnished with fruit.
