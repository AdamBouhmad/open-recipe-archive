## Ingredients

- 750 gr. boneless pork loin
- 1 small msk. salt
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 hg. butter for maitre d'hôtel butter
- 2 tsk. finely chopped parsley
- 1 tsk. lemon juice
- French potatoes for garnish
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth, wring out in hot water, cut into 1 cm thick slices, which are beaten on both sides and sprinkled with pepper and salt.
2. Heat a frying pan slowly, brown the butter in it, lay in the steaks and fry over brisk heat and place on a warm serving dish.
3. Whisk out the pan with a little boiling broth or water and pour the sauce over the steaks.
4. On each steak place a small slice of maitre d'hôtel butter, which is prepared by mixing the butter with finely chopped parsley and lemon juice.
5. Garnish the dish with potatoes cooked in stock and parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
