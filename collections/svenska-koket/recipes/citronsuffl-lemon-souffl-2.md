## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- 1 hg. sugar
- 1 dcl. French wine
- 1/2 dcl. water
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the lemon well and dry it. Finely remove the zest and press out the juice.
2. In a well-scoured saucepan, mix the egg yolks, lemon zest, lemon juice, sugar, wine and water and whisk over the fire until the cream is thick and foamy, then remove it and whisk until it is cold.
3. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks, and pour the cream into a buttered, oven-safe mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1/2 hour.
4. Serve immediately from the mold.
