## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- eggs

## Directions

1. In a pot holding about 2 liters, pour 1.5 liters water, 1 tablespoon salt, and 1 tablespoon vinegar.
2. Bring the water to a strong boil.
3. Carefully break the eggs into a cup and let them slide into the boiling water.
4. The rapid motion will cause the white to wrap around the firmer yolk, making the egg round.
5. Remove them when the white has sufficient firmness, polish them, and serve as a breakfast dish with various sauces, in aspic, or as garnish for other dishes.
