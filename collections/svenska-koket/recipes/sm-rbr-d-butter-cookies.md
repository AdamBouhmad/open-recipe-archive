## Ingredients

- 210 gr. butter
- 1 egg
- 25 gr. granulated sugar
- 815 gr. wheat flour
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 50 gr. pearl sugar
- 50 gr. sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and sift the flour, then work all ingredients together well on the work table.
2. Roll out the dough to about 2 mm thickness and cut out small cakes with a measure.
3. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
4. Brush the cakes with egg white and sprinkle with chopped almonds and sugar.
5. Bake in mild oven heat.
