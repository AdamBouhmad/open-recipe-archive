## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 5 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoon crushed rusks
- 1 cup cream
- 1 can omelette asparagus (1/2 litre)
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are mixed together and stirred with cream to form a loose batter
2. When most of the heat has dissipated, egg yolks are added one by one with salt and pepper to taste
3. Last, the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in
4. The mass is poured into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated ring mold and baked in a water bath in a regular oven for about 20 minutes
5. Turn out onto a round platter
6. Fill the hole in the middle with stewed asparagus
7. Garnish the egg ring around with the heated rod asparagus and serve with melted butter
8. The asparagus stew is prepared as described elsewhere
