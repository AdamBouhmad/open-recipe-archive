## Ingredients

- 125 grams carolina rice grains
- 1 liter broth
- 0.5 portuguese onion
- 3 cloves
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 eggs for breading
- 2 cups crushed rusks for breading
- 0.5 kilogram lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Blanch the rice grains and cook them in the broth together with onion, cloves, salt and butter.
2. When they are soft and the porridge fairly firm, fill it into small water-rinsed molds and set out to cool.
3. Turn out the pastries, bread them and fry golden brown in lard.
4. Cut out a lid, hollow out the pastry and fill with some ragout, place the lid on, and place the pastries in the oven until they have become hot, then serve.
