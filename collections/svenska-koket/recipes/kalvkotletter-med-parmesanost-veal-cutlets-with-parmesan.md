## Ingredients

- 6 large veal cutlets
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 tablespoons clarified butter (30 g)
- 1 1/2 dcl fine breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 150 g good macaroni
- 2 liters water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt (23 g)
- 2 dcl tomato puree or 30 g melted butter

## Directions

1. Treat the cutlets as in recipe no. 514, rub in the spices, dip them in the egg yolks whisked with the clarified butter, turn them in a mixture of breadcrumbs and cheese and fry as in no. 514.
2. Break the macaroni into 3 cm long pieces, rinse in cold water, place in boiling salted water and let boil 15-20 minutes.
3. Drain in a colander, let drain and mix with the tomato puree (which has been heated in a water bath) or with the melted butter.
4. Place the macaroni in the middle of a warm serving plate, arrange the cutlets in a ring around them and garnish the dish with parsley.
