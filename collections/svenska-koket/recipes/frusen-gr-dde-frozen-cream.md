## Ingredients

- 4 dcl. extra thick cream
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Whisk the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks and flavor with vanilla sugar if desired.
2. Freeze it in an ice cream machine as in recipe no. 929, serve with a spoon in a silver bowl and serve with fruit.
3. It can also be packed in an ice mold, which should then stand 1 hour in ice and salt.
4. Unmold and garnish with cooked fruit.
5. The cream can also be mixed with 3 dcl. fruit purée and frozen in the same way.
