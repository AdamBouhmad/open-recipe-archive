## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 kilogram fat

## Directions

1. The potatoes are rinsed, brushed well clean, thinly peeled and cut into 1 cm. thick, even strips, which are placed in water as they are cut. They are dried well in a cloth, placed in hot fat.
