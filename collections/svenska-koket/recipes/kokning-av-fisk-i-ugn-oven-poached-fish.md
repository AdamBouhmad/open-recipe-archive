## Ingredients

- Fish fillets
- Fish stock made from head, bones, and skin
- Mushroom stock (optional)
- Butter for greasing
- Salt

## Directions

1. Make good fish stock from the head, bones, and skin; if desired, blend with a little mushroom stock
2. Grease a long pan or low saucepan with butter
3. Rub the fillets with just a little salt and place them in the pan, close together
4. Add enough clear fish stock to reach the level of the fish
5. Cover with a lid or, if unavailable, a sheet of metal and place in an ordinary oven at 100 degrees
6. Baste the fish from time to time until done, which can be tested with a whisk
7. Drain the fish, place on a platter, and pour sauce over it; usually it is then gratineed in the oven
8. Reduce the cooking liquid and use it for the sauce
