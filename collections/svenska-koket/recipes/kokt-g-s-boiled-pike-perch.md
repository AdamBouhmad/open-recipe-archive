## Ingredients

- 1 pike-perch weighing 1 1/2 kg
- 1 msk salt (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Scale the pike-perch, gut it and cut off the fins.
2. Leave the head on, but remove the gills.
3. Rinse very well, dry and rub in with salt, let rest 1 hour, dry again.
4. Place on a sieve in a fish kettle with back up and sides spread out and cook in a little water as in recipe no. 327.
5. Place on a plate with sieve or on a broken napkin as soon as it is done, garnish with parsley and serve with melted butter and chopped eggs, hollandaise, crayfish or oyster sauce and boiled potatoes.
