## Ingredients

- 1 young calf head
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 small piece ginger
- 2 onions
- 2 carrots
- 1 celery
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 liters veal and beef broth
- a pinch of cayenne pepper
- browned sugar
- 2 tablespoons sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Split the calf head, remove the brain and rinse the head well
2. Soak it in water for about 12 hours
3. Remove it and place it on the stove in enough cold water to cover the meat
4. Add the salt and when the liquid comes to a boil, skim it well
5. Add the seasonings and vegetables
6. Let the soup cook until the meat begins to loosen from the bones, then remove it
7. Strain the broth
8. Separate the meat from the bones, cut it and when completely cold, cut it into small pieces
9. Peel and finely chop the onion and brown it in the butter
10. Add the flour and broth and let the soup cook 10-15 minutes
11. Season with a little cayenne pepper and the wine
12. Serve with the cut meat
