## Ingredients

- 4 hg. fine prunes (about 36 pieces)
- 1/2 liter water
- 3 tbsp. sugar (45 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the plums very thoroughly in several lukewarm waters and let them soak in cold water overnight.
2. Pour off the water and cook it up with the sugar, add the plums and let them boil covered until they are soft and swollen, or about 10 minutes.
3. Place them in a compote bowl, and cook the syrup until it is syrupy, or thicken it with 1 tsp. (5 gr.) potato starch and pour it over the plums.
4. Serve cold with whipped cream.
