## Ingredients

- 75 gr. butter
- 4 dcl. milk
- 2 tbsp. flour (20 gr.)
- 40 gr. almonds, of which 2 bitter
- 125 gr. sugar
- 1/4 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
- 5 eggs
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan, pour in the milk and bring to a boil, whisk in the flour under full boiling, add the blanched and ground almonds, sugar and lemon zest, and let the mixture boil for 5 minutes.
2. Pour it out and let it cool, then work in the egg yolks one at a time.
3. When the mixture is cold, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mass into a buttered pie mold and bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 40 minutes.
5. Serve immediately from the mold with fruit sauce or wine sauce.
