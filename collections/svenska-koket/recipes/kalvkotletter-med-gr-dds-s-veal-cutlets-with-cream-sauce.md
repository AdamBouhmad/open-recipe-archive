## Ingredients

- 6 large veal cutlets
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.) for frying
- 2 dcl. good broth
- 1 tablespoon Madeira wine
- 1 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Treat the cutlets as in the previous recipe, rub in with the spices but do not bread them.
2. Brown them beautifully in the butter, dilute with the hot broth, wine and cream and allow to cook slowly with lid 5-8 minutes.
3. Plate them on a warm dish and pour the sauce over.
4. Serve with boiled or mashed potatoes and lingonberries.
