## Ingredients

- 1 kg marrow bone
- 1/2 kg veal brisket
- 1 chicken
- 4 liters broth
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 carrot
- 1 small parsnip

## Directions

1. Singe the chicken in the usual way and brown it over high heat, but do not cook it through
2. Wash the beef and veal with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut into small pieces
3. Place it together with the chicken and salt in the cold broth, let it come to a boil slowly and skim, then add the singed vegetables
4. Let the broth simmer
