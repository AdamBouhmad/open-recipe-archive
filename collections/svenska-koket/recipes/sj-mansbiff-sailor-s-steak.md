## Ingredients

- 1 kg inner thigh or French cut (boneless)
- 2 tsp salt
- ½ tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 small red onions
- 1 liter potatoes
- 3-4 parts weak bouillon
- ¾-1 part white French wine or beer

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into slices across the grain. The slices are beaten, seasoned and browned quickly in the butter.
2. The onion is peeled, cut into slices and browned likewise. The potatoes are peeled and cut into slices.
3. A form or pan with a tightly closing lid is buttered, in it a layer of potatoes is placed, then a layer of steaks and a little onion. Everything is laid in layers, the top layer should be potatoes.
4. The frying pan is whisked out with the bouillon, and this (together with the wine or beer) is poured over the meat. The lid is placed on, and the dish is allowed to simmer slowly for 2-3 hours.
