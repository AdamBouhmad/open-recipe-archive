## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks well with salt and sugar.
2. Add the cream and lastly gently fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Heat the omelet pan and butter it well with the butter.
4. Pour in the egg mixture and bake the omelet light brown in ordinary oven heat.
5. Turn it out onto a buttered lid, place some stewed filling on one half and fold the other half over.
6. The filling can also be placed on a round dish and the omelet turned out over it.
7. The omelet can be filled with stew made from various or mixed mushroom varieties, asparagus, salsify, calf sweetbreads, lobster, crayfish and fish, etc.
