## Ingredients

- 3/4 litre thick cream
- 10-12 tbsp. wheat flour
- 1 tbsp. butter for the pan
- 1 tbsp. water

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks.
2. Sift the flour and add alternately with the water.
3. Heat the doughnut pan slowly and grease it.
4. For each doughnut, take about 1 tbsp. of the batter.
5. Bake the doughnuts at strong heat on top of the stove.
6. Serve with jam.
