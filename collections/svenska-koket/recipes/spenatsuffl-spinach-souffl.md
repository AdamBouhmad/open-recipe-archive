## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg spinach
- 3 liters water for blanching
- 4 teaspoons salt (20 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour (15 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 1 1/2-2 deciliters cream, milk or broth
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1-2 teaspoons sugar
- 2-3 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the spinach as in the preceding recipe, pour into a colander and allow to cool, then stir in the egg yolks one at a time.
2. When the spinach is cold, fold in the egg whites which have been beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered and bread crumb-lined pudding mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
4. Turn out and serve immediately as a middle course with stirred butter, or garnish it with small cooked carrots, browned potatoes and peas and serve with roasted meat.
5. The mixture can also be baked in small pastry molds (in a water bath in the oven, 20-30 minutes). Turn out and serve as a garnish around roasted meat.
