## Ingredients

- 2 1/4 kg veal saddle
- 1 1/2 hg lard
- 1 tbsp salt
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1 liter broth or water
- lemon juice
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter
- 1 1/2 tbsp flour
- meat jus
- 1/2 part Madeira or sherry

## Directions

1. Wash the veal saddle with a cloth dipped in hot water and lard it with fine strips of lard turned in salt.
2. Grease a hot roasting pan with 1 tbsp of butter and place the saddle in it.
3. Clarify the remaining butter and pour it hot over the saddle.
4. Place the roast in the oven, brown it, dilute with the hot broth or water and baste it with its jus every ten minutes. The roasting takes about 1 1/2 hours.
5. Strain and skim the meat jus, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
