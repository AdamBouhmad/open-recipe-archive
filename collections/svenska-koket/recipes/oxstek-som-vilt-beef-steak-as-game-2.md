## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless beef chuck
- 1 hg lard
- 3/4 liter vinegar
- 3/4 liter water
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 8 dcl boiling buttermilk

## Directions

1. Pound the meat well with a damp meat mallet and lard it in tight rows with lard cut into strips.
2. Place it, the day before it is to be served, in a mixture of vinegar and water.
3. Dry it thoroughly with a cloth and tie it with twine so it has a beautiful shape.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, just large enough for the meat to fit.
5. Place the roast in and brown it well on all sides, then sprinkle with the spices and add the hot buttermilk.
6. Braise the meat with a tightly closed lid until tender, about 2.5 hours.
7. Carve the roast, place on a warm platter, pour a couple of tablespoons of sauce over it, garnish with browned potatoes, green salad or parsley and serve with red currant jelly and pickled cucumber.
8. Serve the sauce reduced (see no. 429), with a little soy added.
