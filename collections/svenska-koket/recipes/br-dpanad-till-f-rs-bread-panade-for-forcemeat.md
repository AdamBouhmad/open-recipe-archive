## Ingredients

- 1 hg. crumb of wheat bread
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. boiling milk
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Remove the crusts from the wheat bread, weigh it and place it in a saucepan together with the salt.
2. Pour the boiling milk over it and when the bread has absorbed all the milk, place the saucepan over the heat and stir the panade with a wooden spoon or spatula until it is thick and smooth.
3. Pour the panade onto a buttered dish and brush a little butter over the top so that it does not dry while it cools.
