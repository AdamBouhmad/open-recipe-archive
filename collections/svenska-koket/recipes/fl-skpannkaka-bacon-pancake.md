## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 liter milk
- approximately 150 gr. flour
- 350 gr. lightly salted side bacon

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with a steel whisk; add the water, and thereafter whisk in the flour, which should be sifted and well dried.
2. Add the butter and sugar, and finally fold in the cream beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Heat a pancake pan slowly and grease it only for the first pancake.
4. Make thin pancakes from the batter, which are fried on both sides, fold them double and place on a warm dish.
5. Serve with jam mixed with whipped cream, if desired.
