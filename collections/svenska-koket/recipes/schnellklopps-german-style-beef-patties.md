## Ingredients

- 750 gr. ox loin, cut beef or boneless inner loin
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr.)
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr.) for sauce
- 1 tbsp finely chopped red onion
- 1 1/2 tbsp flour (15 gr.)
- 4 dcl thick cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until properly tender, is treated and fried as in the previous recipe. The steaks are placed on a warm dish.
2. For the sauce, the butter is melted in the frying pan, the onion and flour are added and everything is browned nicely.
3. The cream is diluted in, and the sauce is allowed to boil for a couple of minutes and is poured over the steaks.
4. Served with fried potatoes, sprinkled with finely chopped parsley.
