## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 liters non-skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Whisk together flour, sugar, salt, and eggs in a saucepan.
2. Add the milk and let the gruel simmer over heat with vigorous whisking for 0.5 hours.
3. Serve immediately.
