## Ingredients

- 12 rusks
- 2 cups warm milk
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Place the rusks in a bowl
2. Mix the warm milk with one tablespoon of the vanilla sugar and pour over them
3. When the rusks have absorbed all the milk, whisk the cream with the remaining sugar to stiff peaks and place on top of the rusks
4. Vanilla rusk is served with rose hip soup and with sweet soups in general
