## Ingredients

- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 2 kkp. crushed sugar
- 2 dcl. thick cream
- 1 dcl. water
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 dcl. French wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the lemon well and dry it; rub the yellow zest against the sugar, cut the lemon in half and press out the juice.
2. Mix all ingredients except the cream in a well-scoured saucepan and whisk until the sugar is melted.
3. Place the saucepan over heat and let the cream simmer with vigorous whisking until thick and foamy.
4. Pour it out and whisk until cold, then fold in the cream whipped to stiff peaks and taste the sauce.
