## Ingredients

- 1 liter water
- 1 1/2 cups sweet fruit juice
- 1/2 cup semolina groats
- 1 tablespoon potato flour
- juice of 1 lemon
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the water and fruit juice together, whisk in the semolina and let it cook about 10 minutes
2. Finish the porridge with the potato flour dissolved in a little cold water and bring to a good boil
3. Pour immediately into a stone pot placed in cold water
4. Whip the porridge vigorously with a steel whisk for 20 minutes, or until it is light red and fluffy
5. Serve with good milk
