## Ingredients

- 2.5 kg veal brisket
- 2 veal knuckles
- 2 liters water
- white peppercorns
- salt
- ground white pepper
- grated ginger

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and place it in boiling water on the heat.
2. When the broth comes to a boil, skim it thoroughly, add the peppercorns and 1.5 tablespoons salt.
3. Let it simmer slowly until the meat is tender.
4. Remove the meat, let it cool, and then chop it fairly finely.
5. Let the broth reduce during this time.
6. Add the meat and bring everything to a boil.
7. Season the mixture quite strongly with salt, pepper, and ginger and pour it into water-rinsed molds.
8. Set the aspic out to set and then turn it out.
