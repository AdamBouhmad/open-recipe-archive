## Ingredients

- Leftover roast (3½ hectograms)
- leftover roast sauce
- 2 salt cucumbers
- 1½ liters cooked potatoes
- salt
- sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the roast into slices and place in a fireproof mold with a few thin slices of salt cucumber between each layer, until the mold is nearly full, then pour the warmed sauce over.
2. Mash the potatoes and season with salt and sugar.
3. Beat the egg whites until stiff and carefully fold into the mash.
4. Spread this as a cover on the mold, sprinkle with crushed bread crumbs and flute neatly.
5. Place the pudding in the oven to heat through and get a nice golden-brown color.
6. Instead of roast sauce, one can use broth or meat extract dissolved in boiling water.
