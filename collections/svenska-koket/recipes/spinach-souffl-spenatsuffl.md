## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg spinach
- 3 liters water for blanching
- 4 teaspoons salt (20 g)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour (15 g)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 1 1/2-2 deciliters cream, milk or broth
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1-2 teaspoons sugar
- 2-3 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for mold (10 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Treat and prepare the spinach as in the preceding recipe, place in a colander and let it cool, then stir in the egg yolks, one at a time.
2. When the spinach is cold, fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites, pour the mixture into a buttered and bread-crumbed pudding mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
3. Turn out and serve immediately as a side dish with creamed butter, or garnish it with small cooked carrots, browned potatoes and peas and serve with fried meat.
4. The mixture can also be baked in small pastry molds (in a water bath in the oven, 20-30 minutes). Turn out and serve as garnish around fried meat, together with other vegetables.
