## Ingredients

- 10 white peppercorns
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1/2 kilogram unsalted butter
- 1/2 cup water
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. The pepper is crushed and placed together with water and vinegar in a tinned copper pot (preferably with a flat bottom) and is allowed to boil until all water has evaporated
2. The pot is lifted off, and when it is cool, the egg yolks are poured in; they are now allowed to simmer over not too strong heat with vigorous whisking (with a steel whisk)
3. When they are foamy, the pot is moved to the side of the heat, and the clarified but cooled butter is added carefully alternately with 1 tablespoon water
4. The sauce is worked for about 1/2 hour, seasoned with lemon juice, heated once more over the heat, strained through cloth and served
