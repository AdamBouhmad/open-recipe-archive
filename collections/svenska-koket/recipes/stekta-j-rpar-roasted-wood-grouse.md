## Ingredients

- 3—4 wood grouse
- 1 hectogram lard, in 3—4 slices
- 3—4 tablespoons butter (60—80 grams)
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 decilitres bouillon
- 4 decilitres thick cream

## Directions

1. The wood grouse meat is white and beautiful with a distinctly strong flavor of game.
2. They are treated and roasted as partridges.
3. Wood grouse can also be roasted as pheasants (see recipe 614).
4. They are split lengthwise, but only the breasts are served.
5. Garnish with browned potatoes and cooked apple halves.
