## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fat herrings
- 3/4 liter raw potatoes
- 2 red onions
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 4 tbsp butter (80 gr.)
- 2 tbsp crushed hardtack
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 1 tbsp crushed hardtack for the pan

## Directions

1. Let the herring soak in water for 24 hours, changing the water 3 times.
2. Fillet the herring, remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets well, wrap in a fish towel, and cut each herring half into six pieces.
4. Rinse, peel, and cut the potatoes into thin slices.
5. Peel and finely chop the onion.
6. Butter a fireproof omelet pan and sprinkle with crushed hardtack.
7. On the bottom of the pan, layer potatoes, then herring, then onion and a little pepper.
8. Continue layering until there are 3 layers of potatoes and 2 layers of herring.
9. Place the butter in flakes on top and sprinkle with crushed hardtack.
10. Bake in good oven heat for approximately 45 minutes.
11. Serve as a breakfast dish or on a smorgasbord.
