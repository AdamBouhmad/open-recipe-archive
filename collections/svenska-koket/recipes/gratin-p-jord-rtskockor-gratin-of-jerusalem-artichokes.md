## Ingredients

- 1 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 3/4 liter boiling water
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)
- white pepper
- For the white sauce: 1.5 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3 deciliters Jerusalem artichoke broth
- 2 deciliters cream
- 1 teaspoon salt (20 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1-2 egg yolks
- For the dish: 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons finely crushed rusks
- 1/2 tablespoon melted butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the Jerusalem artichokes as in recipe 696, drain in a colander, let cool on a cloth and slice them.
2. Fry them well in butter together with sugar and pepper.
3. For the sauce, fry butter and flour 2 minutes, add the broth and cream; let the sauce cook 5 minutes.
4. Season the sauce with spices, blend with the beaten egg yolk and let simmer.
5. Butter an ovenproof dish or preferably a silver dish, sprinkle with cheese, pour a little of the white sauce over, and place a layer of Jerusalem artichokes, a little grated cheese and sauce. Continue until there are two layers of Jerusalem artichokes and three layers of sauce.
6. The top layer should be sauce, sprinkled with the remaining cheese and crushed rusks and poured over with the melted butter.
7. Serve for lunch or as an appetizer.
