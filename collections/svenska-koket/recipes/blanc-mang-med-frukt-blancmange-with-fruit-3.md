## Ingredients

- 4 oranges
- 1 dcl. granulated sugar (85 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon fine preserves
- 50 gr. lady fingers
- 2 1/2 dcl. thick cream
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar (15 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons vanilla sugar (10 gr.)
- 2 sheets gelatin
- 2 tablespoons hot water

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges, remove the white pith carefully, then cut into slices and remove the seeds.
2. Layer the orange slices, sugar, preserves, and lady fingers alternately in a compote bowl.
3. Prepare the blancmange as in recipe no. 876 and pour immediately over the fruit mixture, then let it stand in a cold place for 2-3 hours.
4. Garnish with preserves and lady fingers.
