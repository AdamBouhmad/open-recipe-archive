## Ingredients

- 1 cod of about 3/4 kg
- 1/2 tbsp vinegar
- 2 1/2 tbsp salt (43 gr.)

## Directions

1. Brush the cod with vinegar and scrape well from the tail upward.
2. Clean it (see page 171), rinse very well and dry.
3. Reserve the roe and liver, cook separately in salted water and use for garnishing the fish.
4. Cut off the head, from which the gills have been removed, together with the tail, as they easily fall apart during cooking.
5. They can be cooked separately and used as garnish or grilled and fried.
6. Make three slits on each side on the thickest part of the fish, rub the fish with the salt and let it lie for a couple of hours.
7. Cook it thereafter as in no. 327 and requires great attention during cooking, which takes place in 10-15 minutes.
8. Arrange on a warm platter with a sieve, garnish with the liver and roe, cut into slices, and with parsley and small boiled potatoes.
9. Serve with melted butter and chopped eggs or mustard sauce or with crayfish sauce.
