## Ingredients

- 6 large veal cutlets
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 tbsp. clarified butter (30 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 tbsp. Parmesan cheese
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 150 gr. good macaroni
- 2 liters water
- 1 1/2 tbsp. salt (23 gr.)
- 2 dcl. tomato puree or 30 gr. melted butter

## Directions

1. Treat the cutlets as in recipe no. 514, rub with spices, dip in the egg yolks whisked with butter, turn in a mixture of breadcrumbs and cheese and fry as in recipe no. 514
2. Break the macaroni into 3 cm. long pieces, rinse in cold water, place in boiling salted water and let cook 15-20 minutes
3. Drain in a colander, let drain and mix with the tomato puree (which has been heated in a water bath) or with the melted butter
4. Place the macaroni in the middle of a warm serving dish, the cutlets in a ring around and garnish with parsley
