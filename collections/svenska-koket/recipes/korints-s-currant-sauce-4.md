## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons currants
- 3 cups water
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1-2 tablespoons syrup
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the currants well in lukewarm water and then cook them soft in 3 cups water.
2. Fry butter and flour together, add the currant stock, add the syrup, and let the sauce cook for 10-15 minutes.
3. Taste and adjust with sugar, vinegar and a little salt.
