## Ingredients

- 850 gr. prime wheat flour
- 1/2 liter milk
- 30 gr. pressed yeast
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 hg. butter
- 125 gr. sugar
- 20 gr. sweet almond (20 pieces)
- 5 bitter almonds
- 1 egg
- 6-8 cardamom pods
- 1 egg for brushing
- 15 gr. sweet almond
- 50 gr. pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl, a little of it is reserved for dusting.
2. Heat the milk to about 40 degrees and stir into the flour together with the yeast, mixed with the salt and 1 tsp. of the sugar, after which the dough is worked until it is smooth and glossy.
3. The dough is covered and allowed to rise in a warm place to double its size.
4. Cream the butter with the remaining sugar and finely crushed almonds until fluffy and work into the risen dough along with the egg and finely crushed cardamom pods if desired.
5. Work the dough very well and divide it into 15 round buns.
6. Place the breads on buttered baking sheets, allow to rise about 1 1/2 hours, brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with the whole almonds and pearl sugar, and bake in a hot oven until golden brown.
