## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 6 deciliters fish stock
- 3 tablespoons chopped parsley or 2 tablespoons chopped chives or 2 hard-boiled chopped eggs or 1-2 onions or 4-6 anchovy fillets
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter (10 g)

## Directions

1. Roux the butter and flour for 2 minutes
2. Add the fish stock and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes
3. Add the parsley or chives or chopped eggs and season with spices; finally stir in the cold butter
4. If desired, enrich the sauce with 1 egg yolk before adding the cold butter
5. If using onion, peel and blanch it for 5 minutes in lightly salted water, drain, slice and brown in butter before adding the flour
6. If making the sauce with anchovies, pound the fillets in a mortar with 10 grams butter and add them along with 1 tablespoon chopped parsley when the sauce is finished cooking
