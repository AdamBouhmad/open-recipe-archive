## Ingredients

- 1 litre sour milk
- 1/2 litre thick sour cream
- 1—1 1/2 dcl. fine red wine

## Directions

1. Whip the sour milk and thick sour cream until stiff peaks form
2. Flavor with 1—1 1/2 dcl. fine red wine
3. Serve immediately with sugar crisps or biscuits
