## Ingredients

- 1 25-öres loaf of wheat bread, day-old
- 2 1/2 hg. butter
- 1/2 tsp. French mustard
- 1/2 hg. cooked ham
- 1/2 hg. tongue
- 1/2 hg. roast veal
- 1 salt cucumber
- 1 hg. smoked salmon
- 1 hg. Swiss cheese

## Directions

1. The bread should be baked the day before.
2. Cut the bread into thin slices lengthwise and trim the edges.
3. Mix the butter with the mustard until it becomes creamy.
4. Spread the butter mixture on both sides of the bread slices and layer them together with a layer of cold cuts between each pair.
5. Place thin slices of salt cucumber on top of the veal.
6. Spread butter on only one side of the first and last bread slices.
7. Place the sandwiches under pressure between two cutting boards; place a light weight on the top board.
8. After 2 hours, cut the bread into thin slices, shape these, and serve as in the previous recipe.
9. Dark rye bread can also be used together with the wheat bread for these sandwiches.
