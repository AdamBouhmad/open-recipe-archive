## Ingredients

- 150 gr. icing sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 4 dcl. vanilla glace

## Directions

1. Prepare the meringue mixture as in recipe no. 921 and place with a spoon onto well-cleaned and buttered sheets in the form of eggs, smooth them over with the spoon and dust with sugar.
2. Bake in gentle oven heat until they are half dry and release from the sheet, then remove them, shape onto an egg form and let cool.
3. Fill the meringues with vanilla glace and serve as pairs.
