## Ingredients

- 1 part clarified butter
- 3 parts water
- 150 gr. flour
- 40 gr. unsweetened chocolate
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup strawberry jam for filling
- 3 parts thick cream for filling
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar for filling

## Directions

1. Boil the butter and water together, whisk in the flour, and let the batter cook under constant stirring until it leaves the pan.
2. Remove from heat and melt the chocolate into it.
3. Once most of the heat has dissipated, stir in the egg yolks one by one.
4. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. On well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets, place large spoonfuls of the batter with a spoon.
6. Bake at good oven heat, taking care not to open the oven door during the first 10 minutes.
7. Once cooled, cut a lid out of each pastry and place 1 tsp. strawberry jam inside.
8. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, flavour with vanilla sugar, and fill the pastries with this mixture.
