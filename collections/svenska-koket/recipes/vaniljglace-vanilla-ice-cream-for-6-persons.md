## Ingredients

- 8 parts heavy cream
- 75 gr. granulated sugar
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 4-5 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the cream with the vanilla and sugar until it has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor, then remove the vanilla.
2. Whisk the egg yolks in a bowl, and add the boiled cream while whisking vigorously.
3. Pour the cream back into the saucepan and let it simmer while whisking until it thickens.
4. Remove it from the heat and continue whisking until the cream is completely cold.
5. Pour it into the ice cream freezer and freeze.
