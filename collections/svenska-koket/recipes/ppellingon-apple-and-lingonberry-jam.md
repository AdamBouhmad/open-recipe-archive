## Ingredients

- 4 liters lingonberries
- 2 liters apples
- 7-8 kg crushed sugar
- 1/4 liter water

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the lingonberries.
2. Rinse the apples, remove the blossom and stem ends, and cut each apple into approximately 8 wedges.
3. From the sugar and water, boil a clear syrup.
4. Add the apples and lingonberries and let them cook slowly under skimming, until they are clear.
5. Immediately pour the jam into prepared jars.
