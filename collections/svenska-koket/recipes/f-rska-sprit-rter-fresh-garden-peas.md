## Ingredients

- 1 liter tender garden peas (or about 5 liters unshelled)
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Place the peas in a colander, rinse them with cold water and let them drain.
2. Add them to boiling water with salt and sugar, and let them cook slowly until they are soft.
3. Drain the cooking liquid, place the peas on a serving dish and mix well with the parsley and the cold butter.
4. The peas can also, after the cooking liquid has been drained, be shaken in melted butter together with the parsley and served as a garnish around roasted meat, such as fillet, cutlets, veal saddle, etc.
