## Ingredients

- white paper for greasing
- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Grease a flat pan well with white paper and heat it.
2. Brown butter lightly in it; break the eggs carefully and let them slide into the pan.
3. When the white has set, remove the eggs with a spatula, polish and serve as a breakfast dish or as garnish to other dishes. — A regular frying pan can also be used, but more butter is needed then.
