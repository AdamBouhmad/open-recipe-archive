## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups cream
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs
- 1 cup cream
- 1 can omelet asparagus (0.5 liters)
- 1.5 tablespoons butter for the mold
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for stewed asparagus
- 1 tablespoon flour for stewed asparagus

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour together and mix with the cream so that it becomes a loose batter.
2. When most of the heat has passed, add the egg yolks one at a time with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten until stiff.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated ring mold and bake in a water bath in ordinary oven heat for about 20 minutes.
5. Tip it out onto a round plate.
6. Fill the hole in the middle with stewed asparagus.
7. Garnish the egg custard around with stewed asparagus.
