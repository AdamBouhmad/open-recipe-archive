## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. rice groats (100 gr.)
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.)
- 2 1/4 litre milk
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice groats in cold water
2. Scald them (let them lie 10 min. in hot water)
3. Pour into a colander and rinse again
4. Melt the butter in a saucepan, add the milk and bring to a boil
5. Add the groats and let them simmer slowly with a tight lid for approximately 2 hours, stirring often and thinning with more liquid as needed
6. Season the porridge with salt and serve with sugar and cinnamon
