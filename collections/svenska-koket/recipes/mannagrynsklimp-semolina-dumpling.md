## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter good milk
- 1/4 cup semolina
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 cup sultana raisins
- 2 egg whites
- 2 bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil, rinse the semolina well and add with constant stirring along with the sugar, cleaned and blanched sultana raisins and the grated almond
2. Once this has cooked to a thick porridge (or approximately 1/2 hour), lift the pan from the heat and fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
3. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed porcelain mold, let it cool and turn it out
4. Serve with fruit soup
