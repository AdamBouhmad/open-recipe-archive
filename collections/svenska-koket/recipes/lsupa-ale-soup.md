## Ingredients

- 1 small cup flour
- 1.5 liters milk
- 0.5 liters weak ale
- 1-2 tablespoons syrup or sugar
- ginger
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix flour with 3 cups of milk and let it swell for about 1 hour.
2. Bring the remaining milk to boil, stir in the flour mixture, and let the gruel simmer for 10-15 minutes with stirring.
3. Bring the ale to boil with the syrup and spices and pour into the gruel with vigorous whisking.
4. Serve the ale soup foaming.
