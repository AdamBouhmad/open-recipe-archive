## Ingredients

- Rester av gås (goose leftovers) - 3-4 hg. kött (meat)
- 1/2 lit. vatten (water)
- 1 tsk. salt
- 10 gr. torkade murklor (dried mushrooms)
- 2 msk. smör (butter) - 40 gr.
- 3 msk. mjöl (flour) - 30 gr.
- överbliven sås (leftover sauce) - 2 dcl.
- 1 1/2 hg. förvällda katrinplommon (blanched prunes)
- 1 lit. goda äpplen (good apples)
- 1 kkp krossocker (crushed sugar)
- 2 kkp vatten (water)

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into nice pieces. Break the carcass, place it in cold water with salt added, and let it cook for approximately 1 1/2 hours to make a good broth.
2. Blanch the mushrooms for 1/2 hour, drain them, wrap them in a cloth and cut into fine cubes.
3. Sauté them in the butter until it is clear, then press them up with a spoon and sauté the flour with the butter for 2 minutes.
4. Add the sauce and enough of the broth to make a simmering preparation, which should cook for 5 minutes.
5. Add the meat along with the mushrooms and the blanched, pitted prunes.
6. The preparation should not boil after this, only simmer.
7. Peel the apples, cut them into four sections and cook in a mixture of sugar and water until they are soft but do not fall apart.
8. Arrange the ragout on a warm serving platter, garnish with apple sections and small browned potatoes and serve as a breakfast dish.
