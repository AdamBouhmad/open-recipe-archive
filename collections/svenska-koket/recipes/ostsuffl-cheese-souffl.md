## Ingredients

- 80 g butter
- 80 g flour
- 0.5 liters boiling milk
- 5–6 eggs
- 60 g grated cheese
- salt
- white pepper
- 0.25 tsp mustard (optional)
- 0.5 tbsp butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tbsp grated breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes. Pour it into a bowl; when it has cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the mixture for 10 minutes. Mix in the grated cheese together with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks. Taste the mixture, pour it into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 45 minutes.
