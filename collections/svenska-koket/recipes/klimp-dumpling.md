## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 5 tbsp. flour (50 gr.)
- 4 dcl. boiling milk
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 6 almonds (2 bitter)
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes.
2. Dilute with the milk and let the mixture boil while stirring for 10 minutes.
3. Add the sugar, the blanched and grated almond and the whisked eggs and let the mass simmer over the fire until it becomes appropriately thick.
4. Pour the dumpling into a water-rinsed pudding mold and let it cool.
5. Loosen it carefully at the edge, turn it out onto a plate or round dish and serve with broth.
