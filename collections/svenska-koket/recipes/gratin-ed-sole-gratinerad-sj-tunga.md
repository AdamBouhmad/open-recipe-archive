## Ingredients

- 3 sole, approximately 500 g each
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- For cooking: 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- fish stock
- Sauce: 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 5 decilitres fish broth
- 2 egg yolks
- salt, white pepper
- For garnishing: 1 jar mushrooms (1/4 litre)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 1 tablespoon finely crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the sole as in recipe 371, let them drain and place on a butter-spread, heat-safe dish or preferably on a silver dish
2. Pour the sauce over and garnish the arrangement in stripes or squares with the mushrooms, cut into fine dice and sautéed in butter
3. Sprinkle cheese, well mixed with the crushed rusks, over the whole, and finally pour the butter in which the mushrooms were sautéed
4. Place the dish in a long pan with coarse salt, put the pan in a hot oven and let the fish gratinée for 10-15 minutes
5. Garnish with parsley and serve with small boiled potatoes
