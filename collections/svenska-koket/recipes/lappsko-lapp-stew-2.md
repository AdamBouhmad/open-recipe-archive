## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter potato
- 1-2 deciliters broth
- meat as in the preceding recipe
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the potato raw, cook until soft, mash with a fork and dilute with the boiling broth.
2. Mix in the meat, taste the dish with the seasonings and serve as a breakfast dish.
