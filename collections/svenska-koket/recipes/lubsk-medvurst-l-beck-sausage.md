## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 kg. saltat späck
- 5 kg. benfritt innanlår av oxe
- 2 1/2 kg. färskt, magert svinkött
- 150 gr. salt
- 1 hg. socker
- 32 gr. malen stark- och vitpeppar
- 4 ctl. rom
- omkr. 8 m. fetrader

## Directions

1. Cut the fat and meat the day before the sausage is to be made.
2. Cut the fat into 1 1/2 mm. cubes.
3. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces, free from membranes and sinews, mix with 75 gr. of salt and let stand in a cold place until the following day.
4. Grind the meat 6 times through a meat grinder together with the meat juice, but after each time it passes through the grinder, spread out the forcemeat and let it cool.
5. Work the forcemeat vigorously together with spices and roe for about 45 minutes, then add the fat and work the forcemeat further for about 1/2 hour.
6. Stuff the forcemeat firmly into straight beef casings, and let the sausages hang in a cold place 4-6 days depending on the season.
7. Then hang in ordinary room temperature — in summer 5-6 hours, in winter 24-30 hours — to dry and become uniformly red.
8. Then cold-smoke for about 14 days.
