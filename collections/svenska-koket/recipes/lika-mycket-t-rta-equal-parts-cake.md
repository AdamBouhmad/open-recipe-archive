## Ingredients

- 2 deciliters melted butter (180 gr.)
- 2 deciliters fine sugar (160 gr.)
- 3 eggs
- 2 deciliters wheat flour (100 gr.)
- 15 gr. butter for the pan
- 3 deciliters applesauce, jam, redcurrant jelly or orange marmalade for filling
- 3 deciliters whipped cream for garnish
- 2 tablespoons red redcurrant jelly for garnish

## Directions

1. Let the butter cool and stir it until it is white and fluffy. Stir the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes and mix with the butter, after which the sifted flour is stirred in. Bake the batter on pancake pans into 3 layers between which, when they are cold, are placed 3 deciliters applesauce, jam, redcurrant jelly or orange marmalade. Garnish the cake with 3 deciliters whipped cream and 2 tablespoons red redcurrant jelly, which is piped through a fine paper cone. The cake can also be garnished with meringue made from 3 egg whites and 105 gr. fine sugar, sprinkled with sugar, placed in the oven to achieve a golden brown color and served with vanilla sauce.
