## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg pike
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- Batter: 75 grams flour
- 1 decilitre lukewarm water
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tablespoon melted butter
- 1/2 kg aspic for cooking

## Directions

1. The pike is cleaned, rinsed and dried well
2. The skin is removed and the fish is cut into fillets
3. These are then cut into appropriate pieces and all bones are removed
4. The fillets are rubbed with salt and vinegar and allowed to rest for a while
5. The fish is dipped in batter and cooked in aspic
6. The batter is prepared as follows: fine, well-dried wheat flour is sifted with salt into a bowl, and in the middle of the flour water, egg yolks and lastly butter are stirred in
7. The batter is stirred until it is glossy and thick enough to coat a spoon
8. Just before use, egg whites are beaten to peaks and well blended in
