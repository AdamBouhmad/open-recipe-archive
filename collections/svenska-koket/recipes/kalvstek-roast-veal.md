## Ingredients

- 5 kg veal roast
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 12 allspice berries
- 2 - 2 1/2 liters broth or water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 5 tablespoons flour
- meat stock
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the roast with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Remove the flank and break the spine if the roast is so large that the kidney section is included.
3. Grease a roasting pan with butter, place the roast in it with the inside up, and put in a hot oven to brown.
4. When browned, remove it, salt it, and turn it over.
5. Add the boiling liquid, return the roast to the oven to roast through, and baste with its drippings every ten minutes.
6. Roasting takes 3-4 hours.
7. Strain and skim the meat stock, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules.
