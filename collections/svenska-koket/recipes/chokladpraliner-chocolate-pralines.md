## Ingredients

- 250 gr. toppsocker
- 1 1/4 dcl. vatten
- 8 gr. glykos
- 1/2 tsk. mjölk
- 2—3 droppar vaniljessens eller likör
- 150 gr. sockrad blockchoklad

## Directions

1. Cook the toppsocker, water, and glykos gently to soft ball stage, pour onto a lightly oiled baking sheet, and let cool.
2. Add the milk and 2—3 drops of vanilla essence or liqueur and work with a wooden spoon into a white and smooth mass called fondant.
3. Form the mass into small round balls, roll in powdered sugar, and let dry for a while.
4. Cut the sweetened block chocolate into pieces, melt in a water bath without adding water, and let cool.
5. Dip the sugar balls in the chocolate and place with a silver fork onto a sheet with baking paper.
6. Let the chocolate set before removing the pralines from the paper.
7. Alternatively, nut paste may be used instead of sugar cream, made from 1/2 kg. Spanish nuts (180 gr. shelled), 180 gr. powdered sugar, and 3—4 tbsp. egg white.
