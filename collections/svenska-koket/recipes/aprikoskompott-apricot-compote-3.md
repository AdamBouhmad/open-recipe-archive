## Ingredients

- 3 hectograms dried apricots
- 1 deciliter caster sugar (85 g)
- 1 1/2 deciliters water

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots well in several warm water baths and let them drain on cloth.
2. Layer them alternately with the sugar in an enameled preserving pan, add the water, and let the fruit stand in a warm place until the next day.
3. Bring the apricots to a boil and transfer with a slotted spoon to a compote bowl.
4. Add more sugar to the syrup if desired, cook until it is syrupy, and pour over the fruit.
5. Serve the compote cold with whipped cream.
