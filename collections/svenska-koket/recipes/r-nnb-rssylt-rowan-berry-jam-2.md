## Ingredients

- 2 kg. rönnbär av sötrönn (approx. 3 lit.)
- 1 1/2 kg. socker
- 4 dcl. vatten

## Directions

1. Pick the berries when they are ripe and juicy.
2. Prepare the jam as in recipe no. 1031.
3. Ladle the jam into small glass jars, which should be sealed the following day.
4. Serve as a salad or on butter and bread with tea.
