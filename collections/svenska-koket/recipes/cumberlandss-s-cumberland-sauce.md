## Ingredients

- 1 tsk. orange peel
- 1 tsk. lemon peel
- 1 tsk. finely chopped shallot onion
- 4 msk. red currant jelly
- 1 1/2 dcl. fine port wine
- 1 tsk. English or French mustard
- juice of 1 orange
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- a little cayenne
- a little ground ginger

## Directions

1. Remove all white pith from the orange and lemon peel, then cut into very fine strips, measure, blanch for 2 minutes in boiling water, wrap in cloth and allow to cool.
2. Blanch the chopped onion for 1 minute in boiling water and press well in a cloth.
3. Dissolve the currant jelly with the wine, mix with the peel, onion, mustard, orange and lemon juice and season carefully with the spices.
4. Serve with cold poultry and game.
