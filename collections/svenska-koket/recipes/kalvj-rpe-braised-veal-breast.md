## Ingredients

- 1 kg. boneless veal loin
- 1 hg. lard
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 dcl. broth
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat (see page 232) and cut across into 12 even slices, which are lightly flattened.
2. Cut the lard into strips, which are dipped in a mixture of salt and pepper, and therewith lard the meat slices.
3. Roll them up and bind them in two places with fine twine dipped in boiling water.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the rolls in and brown them beautifully, sprinkle with the remainder of the spice mixture, dilute with the boiling broth and cream and let them braise slowly with a tight lid until they are tender, or about 3/4 hour.
5. Remove them, cut off the strings, place them in a warm carrot, whip up the sauce, taste and adjust, and add a little thick cream if needed, and pour over the rolls.
6. Serve with boiled or pressed potatoes and lingonberries and onion.
7. If desired, the sauce can be made creamier by thinning it with a little butter and flour.
