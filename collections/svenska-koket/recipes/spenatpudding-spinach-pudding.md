## Ingredients

- 250 grams spinach
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 1/2 liter good milk
- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter and 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach, blanch it, let it drain and chop it fine.
2. Whisk the egg yolks together with the milk, and soak the crushed rusks in this.
3. Mix the chopped spinach into the mixture together with the melted butter and spices.
4. Beat the egg whites until stiff and fold them in last.
5. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated mold and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
