## Ingredients

- 3 kilograms leg of venison
- 1.5 hectograms lard
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1.5–2 liters boiling broth or water
- 1.5 tablespoons butter for sauce
- 3 tablespoons flour
- meat drippings
- 1 part sour or sweet cream

## Directions

1. Wipe the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, remove the outer membrane, and lard the roast in close rows with the lard cut into fine strips.
2. Brown the roast in the heated butter, salt it and add broth or water.
3. Roasting takes 1.5–2 hours.
4. Strain and skim the meat drippings, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules.
