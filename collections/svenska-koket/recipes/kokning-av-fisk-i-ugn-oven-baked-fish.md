## Ingredients

- Fish fillets
- Butter for greasing
- Salt
- Fish stock made from heads, bones, and skin
- Mushroom stock (optional)
- Sauce to serve

## Directions

1. This cooking method is suitable only for fish that is cut into fillets.
2. Cook good fish stock on the head, bones, and skin, which, if desired, can be mixed with a little mushroom stock.
3. Grease a long pan or low saucepan with butter.
4. Rub the fillets with very little salt and place them in the pan, close together, and add enough of the clear fish stock so that it reaches the level of the fish.
5. Place a lid, or if not available, a baking sheet, on top, and place the pan in the heat of a regular oven (under 100°C).
6. Baste the fish from time to time until it is done, which is tested with a whisk or skewer.
7. Let it drain, arrange it on a platter and pour some sauce over it; usually it is then gratinéed in the oven.
8. Reduce the cooking liquid and use it for the sauce.
