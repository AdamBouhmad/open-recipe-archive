## Ingredients

- 2 large salted herrings
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 red onions
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 1 sheet white writing paper
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 gr.) for frying

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water for 24 hours, changing the water 3 times. Remove the skin, cut off the head and tail.
2. Cut the herring up along the backbone, remove the bone along with the entrails, then rinse it well and dry it with a fish towel.
3. Sprinkle the herring inside with pepper and the peeled onion cut into thin slices. Put it back together so that it retains its form.
4. Wrap each herring well with 1/2 sheet of paper, spread with 2 tablespoons of the butter. Fry it in butter until the paper is brown or for approximately 15 minutes.
5. Serve the herring in the paper with creamed potatoes as a breakfast dish.
