## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 kg fish
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1 liter fish stock
- 4 tbsp white French wine
- 10 gelatin sheets
- 2 tbsp water
- 2 egg whites
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook the fish in a short stock in the usual way
2. Add the fish stock together with the wine and gelatin dissolved in a little hot water
3. When the mixture is warm, whisk in the egg whites beaten to light peaks and let the aspic boil briefly under whisking
4. Strain the stock through a cloth or fine sieve wrung out in hot water
5. Pour the clear aspic onto the bottom of a water-rinsed à-la-daube mold and let it set
6. Garnish the mold with parsley and fill with the fish cut into neat pieces
7. Pour the remaining aspic carefully over and let the à-la-daube set in a cool place
