## Ingredients

- 2 1/4 kg veal saddle
- 1 1/2 hectograms larding fat
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 liter broth or water
- lemon juice
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- meat stock
- 1/2 part Madeira or sherry

## Directions

1. Wash the veal saddle with a cloth dipped in hot water and lard with fine larding fat strips turned in salt.
2. Grease a hot roasting pan with 1 tablespoon of butter and place the saddle in it.
3. Melt the remaining butter and pour it hot over the saddle.
4. Put the roast in the oven, brown it, add the hot broth or water, and baste with its drippings every ten minutes.
5. Roasting takes approximately 1 1/2 hours.
6. Strain and skim the meat stock, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules.
