## Ingredients

- 125 gr. sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 dcl. water
- 6 dcl. extra thick cream
- 50 gr. blanched and ground sweet almonds
- 50 gr. shelled and ground walnuts

## Directions

1. Prepare the base cream according to the basic parfait recipe.
2. Blend the cream with 50 gr. blanched and ground sweet almonds and 50 gr. shelled and ground walnuts.
3. Fill into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mould and freeze for 4-5 hours.
4. Serve with curacao sauce made from 4 dcl. thick sugar syrup, coloured light green with caramel colouring, mixed with 1 dcl. curacao and 1/2 dcl. cognac.
