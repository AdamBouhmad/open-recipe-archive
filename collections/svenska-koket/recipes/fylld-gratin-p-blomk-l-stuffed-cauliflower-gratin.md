## Ingredients

- 2 large cauliflower heads
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons wheat flour
- 1-2 parts cream
- pepper
- salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower, place it in vinegar-mixed water and blanch it in boiling salted water until almost tender, then remove it and let it drain.
2. Place it on a fireproof dish, and cut a lid from the cauliflower.
3. Fry the butter and flour together, add the cream, let the sauce simmer for a few minutes and season with salt and white pepper.
4. Stir in the egg yolks last, pour the stew into the cauliflower and place the lid on it.
5. Pour the melted butter over the cauliflower and sprinkle with the grated cheese and crushed rusks.
6. Place it in the oven to get a nice golden brown color, remove it and serve immediately.
