## Ingredients

- Fresh beans
- Fine salt

## Directions

1. The beans are salted down either whole or cut, in which latter case they should only be cut quite coarsely, as the flavor is less extracted. Place them in a wooden or porcelain vessel with fine salt between each layer. Place the bottom and weight on top, and keep the vessel well covered in a cellar.
