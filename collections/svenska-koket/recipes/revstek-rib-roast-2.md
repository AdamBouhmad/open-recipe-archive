## Ingredients

- 3 kg. double rib or high rib
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 g)
- 2 hg. smoked ham, in slices
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 g)
- 2 carrots
- 3 shallots
- 1/2 liter boiling broth
- 1/2 liter Madeira

## Directions

1. The meat is chopped short before it is weighed. The rib bones are cut away, but the backbone is allowed to remain. Most of the fat on the outside is trimmed away, the meat is rubbed with salt and larded or covered with ham slices, which are fastened with kitchen twine, dipped in boiling water.
2. A long pan is buttered with the cold butter, the meat is placed on an area so large it fits, the roasted and thickly sliced carrots and the peeled and wedge-cut onions are laid in and the meat is placed on top with the outside up.
3. It is placed in a warm oven and basted often with the butter until it is beautifully browned (the vegetables must not be burnt) and is then gradually moistened with the broth and wine.
4. The meat is basted about every 10th minute and is allowed to roast until it is tender, which is tested with a whisk (see page 233); it is removed and the strings are cut away.
5. The roast is sliced, laid on a warm platter, garnished with vegetable pastries or potato croquettes and kale or parsley. Served with brown sauce (see no. 430).
6. Revstek can also be roasted in a pot on top of the stove.
