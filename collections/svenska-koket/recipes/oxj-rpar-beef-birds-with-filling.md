## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless beef chuck
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 200 g of the meat
- 50 g lard
- 2 cleaned, boned anchovies
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 1/2 decilitres thick cream
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 finely chopped red onion
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the onion
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 litre broth
- 2 decilitres thick cream

## Directions

1. Prepare and cut the meat as in the previous recipe — 200 g of it is used for the filling —, beat the meat slices well and sprinkle with the spices.
2. For the meat filling, grind the meat and lard 4 times through a meat grinder and pound with the anchovies and flour in a stone mortar to a fine filling.
3. Work the cream, parsley, the onion fried in butter, and a little salt and pepper into the filling, if needed.
4. Spread the filling on the meat slices, which are rolled up with the filling inside, then treated and fried as in the previous recipe.
5. Towards the end of cooking, add the cream.
6. Whip up the sauce and pour over the rolls; can, if desired, be seasoned with tomato puree.
7. Serve with pressed potatoes.
