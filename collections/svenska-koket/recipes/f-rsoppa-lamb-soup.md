## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg lamb neck
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1 celery root
- 4 potatoes
- 1 leek
- 1 white or red onion
- 4 tablespoons butter or drippings
- 2 tablespoons small oat groats
- little marjoram
- 3 liters water
- 15 g salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. The lamb neck is rinsed quickly, dried well and cut into pieces.
2. The roots and onion are prepared in the usual way, cut into slices and browned well in the butter or drippings together with the meat, groats and a little marjoram.
3. It is then placed in cold water with salt added.
4. The soup is brought to a boil, skimmed well and then allowed to cook slowly with a tight lid for 3-4 hours.
5. The meat is then removed, separated from the bones and cut into small pieces.
6. The soup is strained, skimmed and measured - should be 2 liters - is brought to a boil with the meat, seasoned with spices and served very hot.
