## Ingredients

- 4-5 eggs
- 1 hg. boneless beef
- 1 hg. boneless veal
- 1/2 hg. pork fat
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 pickled cucumber, finely chopped
- 1/2 egg
- 1-1 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1 egg and 1 cup crushed croutons for breading
- 1/2 kg. broth for cooking

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs hard 5-6 minutes and peel them.
2. Coat them thereafter with the well-worked and seasoned meat forcemeat, roll in crushed croutons, brush with egg and roll again in crushed croutons.
3. Flatten the forcemeat slightly at both ends and cook them golden brown in broth. Cut them thereafter in half, crosswise.
4. Lay the serving dish with salad or parsley, and place the small forcemeat halves on it. Fasten a parsley sprig to each one as a handle, so that the forcemeat half has the appearance of a small basket.
5. Stuffed eggs are best served with green salad and sharp sauce.
