## Ingredients

- 2 pike perch weighing approximately 600 g each
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 80 g dry flour
- 3 g salt
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g)
- 1 decilitre water
- 1 egg
- 750 g lard for frying

## Directions

1. Treat the pike perch as in recipe no. 365, but do not flatten the fillets
2. Rub with salt and let rest for 1 hour, then dry again with a fish towel
3. Prepare the frying batter as in recipe no. 218
4. Heat the lard in an iron pot
5. Take fillets with a fork and dip into the batter, which must completely cover them
6. Cook in the lard until beautifully golden brown and cooked through
7. Drain on absorbent paper and place on parchment paper
8. Garnish with parsley fried in lard
9. Serve with stewed spinach or apple sauce
