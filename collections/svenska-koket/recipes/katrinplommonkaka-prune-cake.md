## Ingredients

- 250 gr fine prunes (approximately 22 pieces)
- 2 tbsp sugar (80 gr)
- 3 dcl water
- 4 eggs
- 1 hg sugar
- 75 gr butter
- 4 dcl boiling cream
- 1/2 tsp finely grated lemon zest
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the pan

## Directions

1. The prunes are rinsed well in several lukewarm waters and placed in a stoneware pot in alternating layers with the sugar.
2. Water is added and the prunes are left to soak overnight.
3. They are then boiled for approximately 5 minutes, left to cool, pitted, cut into strips, which are placed in a greased pie pan.
4. The pits are crushed, the almonds are placed in boiling water, peeled, dried in the oven, chopped finely and sprinkled over the prunes.
5. Egg yolks, sugar and butter are stirred for 20 minutes, the boiling cream is added with strong whisking together with the lemon zest, and the whisking is continued until the cream is cold, then the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in carefully.
6. The cream is poured over the prunes.
