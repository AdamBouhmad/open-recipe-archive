## Ingredients

- 12 anchovies
- 4 deciliters milk
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1 small bunch parsley

## Directions

1. Skin the anchovies, clean them, split them down the middle, remove the bone and let the fillets soak in milk for 1 hour.
2. Take them up, wrap them in white paper, coat them in flour and fry them a beautiful golden-brown in 2 tablespoons of the butter.
3. Place them on a warm dish, garnish with parsley fried in the remaining butter and serve on the smorgasbord.
