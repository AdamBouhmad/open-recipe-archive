## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 liter fish stock
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Fry butter and flour together, add the fish stock, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes, then taste and adjust.
