## Ingredients

- 1 liter leftover rice grains or barley grains porridge
- 50 gr. sprinkle sugar
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the dish

## Directions

1. Butter a fireproof dish with the butter.
2. Lay a layer of porridge and sprinkle thickly with sugar mixed with cinnamon.
3. Heat a pancake spatula until glowing and use it to brown the sugar, then lay another layer of porridge, sprinkle with sugar and brown.
4. Proceed in the same way until there are 7 layers, from which the dish gets its name.
5. Do not brown the top layer of sugar with the pancake spatula; instead place the dish in a hot oven so that the sugar becomes beautifully brown and the porridge heats through.
6. Serve with milk.
