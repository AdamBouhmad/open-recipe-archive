## Ingredients

- 2 hg. sweet almond
- 2 hg. fine sugar
- 2-3 egg whites

## Directions

1. The almond is blanched, dried and driven through a mill.
2. The egg whites and sugar are stirred for 20 minutes, after which the almond is added.
3. If the mass is too stiff a little more egg white is added and the dough is worked for another 10 minutes.
4. The dough is piped through a quite coarse star in the form of S or kransar on well buttered sheet or on wafer.
5. The pastries are baked in not too warm an oven until they are half-dry and have a light yellow color.
6. Garnished, if so desired, when they are cold, with red and white crystal sugar.
