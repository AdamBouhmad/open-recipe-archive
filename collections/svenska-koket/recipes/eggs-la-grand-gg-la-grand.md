## Ingredients

- Leftovers of veal roast or poultry (1/2 litre finely cut meat)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 1 decilitre mushroom paste
- 1 tin mushrooms (1/4 litre)
- 1-2 truffles
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 3 decilitres thin cream
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cut or mince the leftovers of veal or poultry finely. Mince the mushrooms and, if desired, 1-2 truffles finely.
2. Melt the butter, add the mushrooms and fry until the butter is clear. Press them out of the butter with a spoon.
3. Fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the mushroom paste and cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
4. Mix the meat and mushrooms in the sauce and bring to a boil, then season carefully with the spices and pour into 6 small oven-proof porcelain pots or shells.
5. Fry the eggs in a frying pan, shape with a glass according to the size of the pot and place on top of the stewed mixture.
6. Serve as a breakfast dish.
