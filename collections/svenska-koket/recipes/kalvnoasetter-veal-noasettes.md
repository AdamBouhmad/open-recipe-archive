## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless veal (from the loin)
- 1.25 kg lard
- salt, white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4 parts cream milk

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, free it from membranes and cut straight across the fibers into 2-3 cm thick slices.
2. Pound the slices lightly on both sides.
3. Scrape and cut the lard into strips.
4. Each meat slice is then larded with two strips so that they form a cross, and sprinkle with a little salt and white pepper.
5. Brown the butter in a frying pan, place the noasettes in and fry them nicely brown on both sides.
6. Finally add the cream milk, and let the dish boil with the lid closed for 5 minutes. It is then ready to serve.
