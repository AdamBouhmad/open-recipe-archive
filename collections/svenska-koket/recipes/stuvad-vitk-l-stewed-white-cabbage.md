## Ingredients

- 1 medium-sized cabbage head
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 4-5 cups cabbage broth or milk
- salt, sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the stem and outer leaves, and cut the cabbage head into 6-8 pieces; these should sit in vinegar-mixed water for about 1/2 hour (allow 1 tablespoon vinegar per liter of water).
2. Remove the cabbage and blanch it in salted water without a lid for about 10 minutes; then remove it and place it in fresh boiling water (just over 1 liter) and let it cook until completely tender.
3. Fry the butter and flour together, add the cabbage broth or milk a little at a time, and let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, after which add the white cabbage and season the stew.
