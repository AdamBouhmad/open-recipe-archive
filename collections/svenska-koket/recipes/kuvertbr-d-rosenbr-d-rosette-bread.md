## Ingredients

- 5 hg. prime wheat flour
- 50 gr. butter
- 3 1/2 dcl. lukewarm milk
- 15 gr. pressed yeast
- 1/2 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 5-6 cardamom pods
- 1 egg for brushing
- 1 tbsp. poppy seeds

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl. Distribute the butter in flakes in the flour and work it in with the fingertips.
2. Stir in the milk and the yeast, dissolved with the salt and sugar and the finely crushed cardamom.
3. Work the dough until smooth, glossy, and reasonably firm.
4. Set in a warm place to rise until doubled in size.
5. Divide the dough and shape into rosette rolls.
6. Place on buttered baking sheets and let rise.
7. Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with poppy seeds.
8. Bake in a warm oven until golden brown.
