## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless sirloin
- 1 liter skim milk or drinking milk
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 deciliter broth

## Directions

1. If the meat is frozen, thaw it in milk or drinking milk.
2. Dry it thoroughly with a cloth, trim it, and cut it into 12 slices, which are beaten lightly.
3. Wrap in a cloth, sprinkle with spices, brush with 1 tablespoon melted butter, and let lie on top of each other for 1 hour.
4. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter in it, lay in the steaks and fry about 3 minutes on each side.
5. Place on a warm plate, whisk out the pan with the broth and keep the sauce on the plate.
6. Place a slice of maitre-d'hôtel butter on each steak; garnish the dish with parsley and serve with boiled or fried potatoes.
7. The steaks can also be garnished with fried Portuguese onion, in which case the maitre-d'hôtel butter is omitted and the frying pan is whisked out with 2 deciliters thick cream, after which the sauce is poured over the steaks.
