## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter blood
- 4 dcl. milk or weak beer
- 2-3 msk. syrup
- approximately 210 gr. ground rye flour
- 3 msk. finely chopped kidney fat, butter, or cream
- 1 1/2 msk. salt (23 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1/2 tsk. ginger
- 1/2 tsk. finely ground marjoram
- 1 finely chopped red onion
- 1 msk. butter for the onion
- 1/2 msk. butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 msk. ground rusks

## Directions

1. Strain the blood through a sieve and whisk it well.
2. Combine the blood with the milk or weak beer and the syrup.
3. Add the rye flour, chopped kidney fat or butter or cream, and the spices and onion.
4. Stir the mixture well.
5. Butter the mold and coat with ground rusks.
6. Fill with the mixture and bake or steam until set.
7. Turn out and serve.
