## Ingredients

- 1 kg. pork hare
- 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon ginger
- 3 beautiful, tart apples
- 1 hg. prunes
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 1/2 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon firm meat extract (4 gr.)

## Directions

1. Free the hare from most of the fat, dry it with a cloth wrung out in hot water, rub it with the lemon juice and spices, well mixed.
2. Peel the apples, cut them into thin slices.
3. Rinse the prunes well in lukewarm water, let them soak 10 minutes in hot water, wrap them in a cloth and pit them.
4. Cut the hare open lengthwise, fill it with the apples and prunes and sew it closed.
5. Roast it as in the preceding recipe, but it requires a shorter roasting time.
6. Serve with browned potatoes and peas.
