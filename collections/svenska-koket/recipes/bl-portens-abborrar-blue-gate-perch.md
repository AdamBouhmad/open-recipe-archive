## Ingredients

- 2 kilograms perch
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 2 teaspoons vinegar
- 2 hectograms butter
- 6 tablespoons coarsely chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoon chopped dill
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- 5-6 tablespoons water

## Directions

1. Scale the perch, gut them and rinse them well and rub them inside and out with a mixture of the salt and vinegar.
2. Butter a casserole with butter, and place the fish in layers; between each layer place butter, dill, parsley and flour.
3. Last add the water.
4. Let the fish only simmer with a tightly fitting lid, the liquid is thereby retained better.
5. When the fish is cooked, place it on a plate.
6. Whisk the sauce in the casserole and pour it over the fish, without straining or diluting it.
