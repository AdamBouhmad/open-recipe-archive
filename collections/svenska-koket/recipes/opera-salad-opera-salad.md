## Ingredients

- 2 hg. fried chicken meat (leftovers)
- 1 hg. salted beef tongue
- 2 hg. pale celery
- 1/2 hg. truffle
- 1 can asparagus tips (1/4 lit.)
- 1 small pickled cucumber, in slices
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tsk. salt, cayenne
- 1/4 tsk. French mustard
- 2 dcl. fine oil
- 1 msk. tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 msk. lemon juice
- 1 msk. boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat, tongue, celery and truffle into thin, beautiful strips and arrange, each separately, in small heaps in a ring on a round serving plate.
2. In the middle place the asparagus, which should be well drained.
3. Between the asparagus and the other ingredients decorate with a border of cucumber slices.
4. Prepare the mayonnaise as in recipe no. 10 and pour in a ring around the arrangement.
5. Serve to the smorgasbord or as a side course.
