## Ingredients

- Ground meat as in recipe no. 631 or no. 632
- 6 large tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 0.25 teaspoon white pepper
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat according to the aforementioned recipe.
2. Clean and rinse the tomatoes and slice them, then sprinkle them with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Butter a pudding mold with butter and sprinkle it with crushed breadcrumbs.
4. Layer the ground meat and tomatoes alternately in the mold; the first and last layer should be meat.
5. Cook or bake the pudding as in recipe no. 638 and serve with meat gravy (see recipe no. 429) or clarified butter and potatoes.
