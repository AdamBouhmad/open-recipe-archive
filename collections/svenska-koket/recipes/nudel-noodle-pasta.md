## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons good cream
- 1 1/2 tablespoons clarified butter (30 gr.)
- flour, enough for a firm dough

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs with the cream for 10 minutes, add the butter and mix in enough flour to make a really firm dough.
2. Roll out the dough to the thickness of a finger and let it rest in a cold place to stiffen overnight.
3. Then roll it out into 1 1/2 cm. wide strips, which are cut across into fine noodles.
4. Let these dry well and they can be stored for several weeks in a tin box with a tight-fitting lid.
