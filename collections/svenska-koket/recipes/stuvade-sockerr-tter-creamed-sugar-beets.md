## Ingredients

- 12 fine, even sugar beets
- 1 litre water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 2 decilitres of the cooking liquid
- 2 decilitres cream
- salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse the sugar beets, brush them, scrape them, and place them gradually in vinegar-mixed water (see page 338) to prevent darkening.
2. Rinse them again, place in boiling water with salt and lemon juice, and let them cook until tender, which is tested with a whisk.
3. Remove them with a slotted spoon and save the cooking liquid.
4. Cut the roots into 3-4 parts.
5. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cooking liquid and cream, and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
6. Add the sugar beets and heat them in the sauce.
7. Season the stew with spices and, if desired, finish with an egg yolk.
8. Serve with roast meat, in omelets, etc.
