## Ingredients

- 175 gr. fine prunes
- 75 gr. fine dried apricots
- 4 dcl. water
- 125 gr. caster sugar
- 5 egg whites
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes and apricots well in lukewarm water.
2. Place the prunes in cold water and cook until half soft, then add the apricots and let the fruit cook soft, but it must not fall apart.
3. Pour into a colander and let drain.
4. Pit the prunes; crush half of the pits in a mortar and chop the almonds finely together with the fruit flesh and apricots in a chopping trough.
5. Mix the fruit well with the sugar, and carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Pour the mixture into a buttered mold and bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 1/2 hour.
7. Serve immediately, turned out of its mold, with whipped cream.
