## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- water, salt
- 2.5 deciliters water
- 30 grams butter
- 125 grams flour
- 4 eggs
- salt
- sugar
- 0.5 kilogram cream for cooking

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw, cook them soft and press them through a potato ricer.
2. Boil water and butter, whisk in the flour, and let the batter cook until it leaves the pan.
3. When most of the heat has dissipated, add the eggs one by one and then the pressed potatoes, and season the batter with sugar and salt.
4. Form small balls with a spoon; these are cooked golden brown in cream.
5. Place them on grey paper and let them drain.
