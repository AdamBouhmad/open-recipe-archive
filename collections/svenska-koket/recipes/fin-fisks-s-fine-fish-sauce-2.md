## Ingredients

- 120 gr. butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 6 parts cream
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. The butter is stirred in a saucepan until it is soft and white.
2. The egg yolks are whisked with the cream.
3. The pan is placed over heat, the eggs and cream are added with vigorous whisking.
4. The whisking is continued until the sauce becomes frothy; then the pan is removed, and the sauce is seasoned with a few drops of lemon juice.
