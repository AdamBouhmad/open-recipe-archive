## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg boneless beef (thigh, rib or marrow pipe)
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 3–4 red onions
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 8 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 6 parts boiling water
- 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung until tender, is prepared and cut across the muscle fibers into slices approximately 1 1/2 cm thick, which are lightly beaten on both sides and turned in a mixture of the spices and flour.
2. The onion is peeled, chopped coarsely and browned in the butter in an iron pot; the meat slices are added together with the allspice berries and bay leaves, and the water mixed with the soy sauce is poured on.
3. The pot is placed over a fresh fire so that the liquid quickly comes to a boil; the meat is then allowed to simmer slowly with a tight lid until tender, or approximately 2 1/2–3 hours.
4. The kalops is placed on a warm platter, most of the fat is skimmed from the sauce, which is thinned if necessary, and poured over the meat.
5. Served with boiled potatoes.
