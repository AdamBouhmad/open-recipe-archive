## Ingredients

- 24 sour Baltic herrings
- 1 bottle beer
- 1 liter cooked potatoes
- 2 red onions
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 tbsp crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 pepper cookies
- 1 tbsp butter (20 g) for the dish
- 1 tbsp crushed breadcrumbs for the dish

## Directions

1. Remove the herrings from the brine, bone them, rinse them and let them soak in beer for 2 hours.
2. Wrap them in white paper.
3. Peel the potatoes and cut them into slices, peel the onion and chop it finely.
4. Grease a heat-resistant dish with butter and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs.
5. On the bottom of the dish place a layer of potatoes, then a layer of herring, then onion and flakes of butter.
6. Continue until there are 5 layers of potatoes and 4 layers of herring.
