## Ingredients

- 105 grams butter
- 50 grams granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 160 grams wheat flour
- 1 knife-tip baking soda salt
- 100 grams sweet almonds
- 10 bitter almonds
- 110 grams granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough in the usual way
2. Blanch the almonds, grate or pass through an almond mill, pound finely together with the sugar and a little water, and work in the egg well
3. Line small pleated forms with butter dough and fill with almond paste
4. Place a cross of butter dough on each pastry, then set in moderate oven heat to bake
5. Turn out as soon as they are done
