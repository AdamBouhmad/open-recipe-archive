## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram cooked or roasted meat
- 1 small red onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 liter bouillon or meat gravy
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into appropriately sized pieces. Peel the onion, chop finely and brown in butter. Fry the flour in it, and add the bouillon or meat gravy together with the pickled cucumber cut into thin slices.
