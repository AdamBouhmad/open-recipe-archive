## Ingredients

- 212 grams butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 212 grams castor sugar
- 212 grams currants
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped candied fruit or 1 teaspoon finely grated lemon zest
- 250 grams wheat flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams) for the pan
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, let it cool and beat until white and fluffy.
2. Beat the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes and blend with the butter.
3. Treat the currants as described on page 464, dust with 2 tablespoons of the flour and add along with the candied fruit or lemon zest.
4. Sift the flour with the baking powder and fold in.
5. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks last.
6. Pour the batter into a large or 2 small buttered and rusk-strewn ring pans and bake in a not-too-hot oven for approximately 1 1/4 hours.
