## Ingredients

- 1 kg veal shank
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 1 carrot
- 1/2 celeriac
- 4 parsley stems
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1 liter brown beef broth
- salt, 1 teaspoon sugar (5 g)
- cayenne
- 1/2 deciliter Madeira or sherry

## Directions

1. Wash the meat well in cold water, dry and cut into small pieces, place in cold water which should come slowly to a boil and be well skimmed.
2. Add the salt, the trimmed and cut vegetables and parsley and let everything cook slowly for approximately 3 hours.
3. Remove the meat, strain the broth, skim well and measure—should be 1/2 liter.
4. Brown the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the broth and the hot beef broth and let the soup cook under stirring for 5 minutes, after which it is seasoned with the spices and wine and served very hot with egg soufflé.
5. If desired, the meat can be cut into thin strips and served in the soup.
