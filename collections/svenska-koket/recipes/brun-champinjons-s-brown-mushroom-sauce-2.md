## Ingredients

- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 5 dcl. meat stock
- 1 dcl. mushroom liquor
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the mushrooms into slices or cubes and fry in the butter until it is clear
2. Press the mushroom through with a spoon
3. Fry the butter with the flour for 2 minutes
4. Dilute with the meat stock and mushroom liquor and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
5. Add the fried mushrooms and season with salt and white pepper
6. If desired, a little Madeira or white wine may be added
