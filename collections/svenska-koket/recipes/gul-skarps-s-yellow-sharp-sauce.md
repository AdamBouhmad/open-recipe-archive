## Ingredients

- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/3 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1-1 1/2 tablespoons vinegar
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. The egg yolks, sugar, pepper, salt, mustard and vinegar are ground well together with a wooden spoon
2. The cream is whipped to stiff peaks and added gradually, after which the sauce is seasoned
