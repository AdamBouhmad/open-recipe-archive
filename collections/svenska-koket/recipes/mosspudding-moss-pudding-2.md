## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 dcl. sherry
- 120 gr. sugar
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 5 sheets gelatin
- 3 tbsp. hot water
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks, wine, sugar, finely grated lemon zest, and lemon juice in a saucepan and whip until the sugar is melted.
2. Heat the cream over the fire with vigorous whisking until it is thick and foamy, then remove from heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
3. Rinse the gelatin in cold water and dissolve it in the hot water.
4. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form.
5. First fold the gelatin into the cream, then carefully fold in the whipped cream.
6. Pour the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for 3-4 hours.
7. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it from the edge with a sharp knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish with parchment paper and garnish with sponge fingers.
