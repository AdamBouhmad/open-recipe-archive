## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- 2 hectograms lard
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1.5-2 liters water
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- meat gravy
- 0.5 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the turkey, remove organs, rinse well and dry with a cloth.
2. Lard the turkey with fine strips of lard or cover with thin slices of lard, truss and season inside and outside with salt.
3. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the turkey in it and brown lightly. Add boiling water and baste every ten minutes. If the oven is too hot, cover the turkey with damp paper.
4. Roasting takes 2-3 hours.
5. Strain the meat gravy, skim it, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
