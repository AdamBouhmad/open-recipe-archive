## Ingredients

- 3 hg. large, beautiful raspberries or strawberries
- Sugar to taste
- 1/2 lit. extra thick cream

## Directions

1. The berries, which should be freshly picked, are passed through a fine sieve.
2. Add sugar to taste.
3. Whip the cream until stiff peaks form and fold gently into the fruit puree.
4. Place the mixture high in a compote dish and serve immediately with almond shavings, meringues, or macaroons.
