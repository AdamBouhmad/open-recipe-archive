## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg lamb breast
- water for cooking
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. If the lamb breast is too salty to cook directly, soak it the day before.
2. Break into appropriately sized pieces, which should however stay together, place in cold water (short broth), bring to a boil, skim and let cook with a tightly closed lid until the meat is tender, then remove.
3. Beat the egg with the milk, brush the breast with this and sprinkle with the finely crushed breadcrumbs.
4. Place in a long pan whose bottom is covered with the broth, and place in the oven to get a nice light golden color.
