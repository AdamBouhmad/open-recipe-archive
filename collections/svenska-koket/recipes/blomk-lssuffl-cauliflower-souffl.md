## Ingredients

- 1 small head of cauliflower
- 0.5 liters water
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 80 g butter
- 80 g flour
- 2 dl cauliflower stock
- 3 dl boiling milk
- 5–6 eggs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 0.5 tbsp butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tbsp grated breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and let it soak in 1 liter of water with 1 tbsp vinegar for 1 hour. Place it in boiling salted water and let it cook soft, then place it on a sieve to drain and divide it into smaller pieces.
2. Prepare the soufflé as in the asparagus recipe (Sparrissufflé), serve as a course with hollandaise or melted butter.
