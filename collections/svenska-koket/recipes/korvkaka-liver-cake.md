## Ingredients

- 5 hg. ox liver
- 3 hg. pork
- 1 1/2 kkp rice grains
- 1 1/2 liter milk
- 2 msk. syrup
- 1/4 msk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 kkp raisins
- 1/2 msk. butter for pan
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for pan

## Directions

1. Rinse the liver well and let it soak in water for approximately 1/4 hour.
2. Blanch it and let it cool, then grate or finely mince it.
3. Rinse the grains in hot water and let them cook soft in the milk.
4. Finely mince the pork and mix it together with the liver, seasonings, syrup and rinsed raisins into the cooled porridge.
5. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pan, sprinkle crushed rusks on top and bake in a good oven heat for approximately 1 hour.
6. Serve with clarified butter.
