## Ingredients

- 1 kg pork hare
- 1 hectogram lard
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon ginger
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 1 tablespoon soy

## Directions

1. Cut away most of the fat and beat the hare with a damp mallet.
2. Cut the lard into strips, which are rolled in a mixture of salt, white pepper and ginger and allowed to lie for 1 hour to harden.
3. Lard the hare in close, even rows, rub it with the rest of the spices and tie it with fine twine so that it gets a beautiful shape.
4. Brown the butter in an iron pot, lay the hare in and brown it beautifully on all sides.
5. Dilute with milk mixed with soy and let the meat roast with the lid tightly closed until tender, approximately 1 3/4 hours, basting it over every 10 minutes.
6. Cut the hare into thin slices, which are laid together again on the platter.
7. Make the sauce and pour it over the meat.
8. Garnish the dish with fried potatoes and parsley.
9. Serve with red currant jelly and tart cucumbers.
