## Ingredients

- 60 asparagus spears
- 1.5 liters water
- 35 grams salt
- 100 grams butter

## Directions

1. Peel the asparagus, rinse in cold water, and tie together in bundles with all heads pointing the same direction.
2. Place in boiling, salted water and let boil for 15-20 minutes.
3. When the asparagus is done, remove it immediately and let it drain.
4. Place it on an asparagus platter, napkin, or on a large slice of toasted bread dipped in the water in which the asparagus was cooked.
5. Serve with clarified butter.
