## Ingredients

- 1 liter sliced fresh or 1 liter preserved rhubarb
- 2 liters water
- 1 1/2 - 2 cups crushed sugar
- (1/3 lemon)
- 1 1/4 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. If using fresh rhubarb, rinse it well and cut into pieces.
2. Boil the water and add the rhubarb and sugar.
3. When the soup comes to a boil, skim it and let cook until the rhubarb is soft.
4. Mix the potato flour with a little cold water and thicken the soup with it, then bring to a good boil and taste and adjust seasoning.
5. Serve, if desired, with whipped cream.
