## Ingredients

- 1 kg cherries
- 3/4 kg lump sugar
- 1/2 part water

## Directions

1. Rinse the cherries and pit them. Rinse the pits and crush them, and save the kernels.
2. From the sugar and water, boil a clear syrup, thick enough that a drop on a plate does not spread out.
3. Add the berries, and let the jam cook slowly under constant skimming, until the berries are clear and the syrup is thick.
4. Just before the jam is done, add the kernels.
5. Shake the jam in the pot for a while so the berries fill with syrup.
6. Pour into prepared jars.
