## Ingredients

- 105 gr. butter
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 160 gr. wheat flour
- 1 knife tip ammonium carbonate salt
- 75 gr. sweet almonds
- 150 gr. granulated sugar
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. The pastry dough is prepared in the usual way.
2. The almonds are blanched and chopped fairly coarsely.
3. The eggs and sugar are stirred very well together, and the almonds are added.
4. Small pastry molds are lined with pastry dough and filled with the almond mixture.
5. The molds are placed in the oven to bake until the filling has completely set.
6. They are turned out immediately.
