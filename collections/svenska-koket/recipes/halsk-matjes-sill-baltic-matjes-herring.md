## Ingredients

- 2 kg. salt fat herring
- 2 liters un-skimmed milk
- 6 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1 dessert spoon saltpeter

## Directions

1. Pack the salted herring into a porcelain crock.
2. Boil the milk with the sugar and the crushed saltpeter.
3. When the milk is completely cold, pour it over the herring so that it covers well.
4. Let the herring sit in the milk for eight days, after which it is ready to use.
5. It can be served poached, baked in paper, or pickled.
