## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 5 tbsp. flour (50 gr.)
- 6 dcl. meat stock
- 2-3 egg yolks
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 2-3 tsp. lemon juice

## Directions

1. Sauté the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Add the whipped egg yolks and let the sauce simmer while whisking; season with spices and lemon juice and pour over the blanched meat.
