## Ingredients

- 3 lettuce heads
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tsp mustard
- 3 tbsp oil
- 2 tbsp vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the salad, rinse and cut into large pieces
2. Mix the egg yolks together with salt, white pepper and mustard; add the oil and vinegar drop by drop while stirring constantly
3. Mix the salad well with this sauce and serve immediately
