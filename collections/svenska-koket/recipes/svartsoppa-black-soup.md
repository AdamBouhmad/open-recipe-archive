## Ingredients

- Giblets of 1 goose (neck, wing tips, heart, stomach, liver)
- 1 liter goose or pork blood
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2.5 liters broth (including the broth in which the giblets were cooked)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 10 cloves (crushed)
- 2-3 teaspoons salt
- 6-8 tablespoons sugar
- 5-6 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 liter fresh or 0.5 hectogram dried apples
- 3 kilograms prunes
- 2 tablespoons port or Madeira

## Directions

1. Scald the goose giblets, rinse them well and let them soak in cold water overnight. Remove the neck skin and liver and use them for making goose liver sausage. Place the remaining giblets in cold water and let them cook with the lid closed.
2. When the meat is tender, remove it, cut it into pieces and place it in the soup tureen. Strain the broth and skim off all fat carefully.
3. In another pot, cook the peeled apples cut into wedges and the rinsed prunes. Strain the blood and whisk it vigorously; add the flour and 0.5 liter of the broth. Bring the remaining broth to a boil, add the blood while whisking vigorously, and continue whisking until the soup has cooked for 10 minutes.
4. Add the cooked fruit together with its broth and season the soup.
