## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters unskimmed milk
- 3 tablespoons sour cream

## Directions

1. Coat the bottoms of six small clay or glass jars with sour cream.
2. Pour in the milk and let it stand in a warm place (protected from flies) to curdle.
3. Serve with sugar and ground ginger.
