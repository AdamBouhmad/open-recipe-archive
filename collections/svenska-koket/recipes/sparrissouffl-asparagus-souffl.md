## Ingredients

- 3 hg fresh asparagus or 1 can omelet asparagus (1/2 liter)
- 80 gr butter
- 80 gr flour
- 2 dcl asparagus broth
- 3 dcl boiling milk
- 5-6 eggs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp breadcrumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Scrape the asparagus, rinse, cut into three pieces and cook until tender in 4 dcl water with 1/2 tsp salt.
2. If using canned asparagus, cut it in half and place on a sieve to drain.
3. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes.
4. Add the hot asparagus broth and milk and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
5. Pour into a bowl; when cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
6. Mix in the well-drained asparagus along with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten until stiff peaks form.
7. Taste and adjust seasonings.
8. Pour into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined mold and bake in regular oven heat for about 45 minutes.
9. If using a metal form, bake in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour and turn out.
10. Serve immediately upon removal from the oven as a middle course with hollandaise or stirred butter.
