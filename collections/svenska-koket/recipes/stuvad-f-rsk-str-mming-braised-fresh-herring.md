## Ingredients

- 30 herring
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams)
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
- 3 decilitres warm water

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, scrape away the dark membrane, rinse well and wrap in a fish cloth.
2. Rub the herring inside and out with salt and layer alternately in a butter-lined pot, sprinkling flour and parsley between each layer.
3. Distribute remaining butter in flakes between the layers.
4. Finally add the warm water and let the fish cook slowly with a tightly closed lid for about 10 minutes.
5. Arrange the herring on a serving dish, whisk up the sauce, taste and adjust seasoning, then pour over.
6. Serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
