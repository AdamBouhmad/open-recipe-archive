## Ingredients

- 150 gr. cooked potato
- 150 gr. cooked celeriac
- 1/2 small cooked leek
- 75 gr. salted ox tongue or salted meat
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 2 good apples
- 2 good pears
- 1/2 hg. truffle
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tsk. salt
- cayenne
- 1/4 tsk. French mustard
- 1 1/2 dcl. cooking oil
- 1 msk. tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 msk. lemon juice
- 1 msk. boiling water
- 2 dcl. thick, reduced tomato purée
- 2 tomatoes for garnish

## Directions

1. Cut all ingredients into fine cubes and mix with the mayonnaise, which is prepared as in recipe no. 10.
2. Line a smooth or fluted mold with lid with the tomato purée.
3. Add the salad, cover with 'butter paper' and lid and freeze in ice and salt.
4. When the salad is to be turned out, dip the mold for a moment in hot water.
5. Turn the salad out onto pastry paper in a crystal bowl, garnish with tomato slices and serve with cold, roasted poultry.
