## Ingredients

- 60 gr butter
- 60 gr flour
- 4 dcl boiling milk
- 5 eggs
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp melted butter (20 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the stewed asparagus
- 1 1/2 tbsp flour (15 gr)
- 1 1/2 dcl cream
- 1 can omelet asparagus (1/4 liter)
- 1 can asparagus spears
- 1 hg unsalted butter

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the mixture simmer for 5 minutes. Pour it into a bowl; when it has cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time, and stir the mixture for 10 minutes. Taste it with the seasonings, and carefully mix in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks. Pour the mixture into a buttered and bread-crumbed ring form and bake in a water bath in the oven for about 40 minutes.
2. Prepare the asparagus stew as in recipe no. 28. Turn the soufflé out onto a round platter, fill the hole with the stew and garnish all around with the asparagus spears, which have been warmed in a water bath. Serve the soufflé immediately with melted butter as a middle course.
