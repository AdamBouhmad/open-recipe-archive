## Ingredients

- 2 liters potatoes
- water, salt
- 2-3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 0.5 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes in their skins until they are almost done.
2. Peel them and shape them with a knife or potato ball cutter into small balls.
3. Brown the butter in a baking pan, add the potatoes and shake them about.
4. Mix the crushed rusks with the sugar and salt and sprinkle over the potatoes, then brown them.
