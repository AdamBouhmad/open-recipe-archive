## Ingredients

- 4 kilograms prune plums
- 1 liter water
- 1 cup sugar
- lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the plums and soak them in cold water for 1 hour before using.
2. Cook them in the water in which they soaked, adding the sugar; cook until soft.
3. Thicken the cream with potato flour mixed in a little cold water, bring to a short boil, taste for seasoning, and pour into a jelly mold or carrot mold to cool.
