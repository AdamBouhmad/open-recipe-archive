## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- flour equal to the weight of 1 egg (slightly more than)
- sugar equal to the weight of 2 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon pulverized cinnamon

## Directions

1. Whip the eggs together with the sugar
2. Add the flour and cinnamon
3. Spread the batter thinly on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets into very thin, round cakes, which are baked in moderate oven heat
4. When they are done, form them immediately into cone shapes, as otherwise they become hard
5. The cones are usually filled with jam and whipped cream
