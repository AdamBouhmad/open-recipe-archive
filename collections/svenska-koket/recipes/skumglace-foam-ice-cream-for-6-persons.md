## Ingredients

- 8 parts thick heavy cream
- 100 gr. granulated sugar
- 1/2 vanilla pod
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Let half of the cream, along with the vanilla and sugar, boil until the cream has acquired sufficient vanilla flavor, then remove the vanilla pod.
2. Whisk the egg yolks in a bowl and add the cooked cream.
