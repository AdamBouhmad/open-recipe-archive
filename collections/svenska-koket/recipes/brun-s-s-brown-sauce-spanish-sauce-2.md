## Ingredients

- 2 shallots
- 1 carrot
- 5 parsley stems
- 50 gr. lean, smoked ham
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 liter previously prepared meat stock
- 1 dcl. white wine
- 5 tbsp. flour (50 gr.)
- 6 dcl. good meat stock from the roast
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2-1 dcl. Madeira

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the onions and carrot into pieces
2. Clean, rinse and wring the parsley stems in a cloth
3. Cut the ham into cubes
4. Brown everything lightly in 1 tbsp. of the butter
5. Add the previously prepared meat stock and white wine
6. Simmer slowly with the lid on for about 2 hours
7. Strain the liquid through a fine sieve, skim and measure — should yield 8 dcl.
8. Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes
9. Gradually dilute with the stock cooked with the vegetables and the skimmed roast stock
10. Season with salt, white pepper and Madeira
11. Let simmer while stirring for 15 minutes
12. Season with additional Madeira if desired
13. If this sauce is used for game, omit the wine and season with 1-2 tbsp. black or red currant jelly
