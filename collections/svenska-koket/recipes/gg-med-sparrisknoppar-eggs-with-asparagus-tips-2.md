## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 6 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 18 asparagus tips
- 2 tablespoons tomato purée

## Directions

1. Grease six small heatproof porcelain pots or regular shells with half of the butter.
2. Carefully place an uncooked egg in each pot, season with salt and pepper and cook in a water bath in the oven for about 6 minutes.
3. Fry the asparagus tips in the remaining butter and use them to garnish the eggs.
4. Spray the tomato purée through a piping cone in a ring around the egg.
5. Serve as a breakfast course.
