## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
- 300 g butter
- 125 g sweet almond
- 50 g bitter almond
- 210 g brown flour
- 425 g wheat flour
- 1 tsp hartshorn salt
- 2 tbsp pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the egg with the spices.
2. Stir in the melted butter, the unpeeled and coarsely cut almond, and the sugar.
3. Sift the flour with the hartshorn salt and work in last.
4. Form small round balls from the dough, dip them in pearl sugar, and bake in moderate oven heat.
