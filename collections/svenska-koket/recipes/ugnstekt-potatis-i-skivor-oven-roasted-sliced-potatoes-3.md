## Ingredients

- 1 liter evenly-sized, oblong potatoes
- 1 tablespoon crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 grams)
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)

## Directions

1. Treat the potatoes as described on page 337 and following and cut into 1/2 centimeter thick slices, which are stacked so that the potato retains its form.
2. Grease a frying pan or omelet form with half the butter, lay in the potato slices close together.
3. Place the remaining butter in flakes on top, and finally sprinkle over a mixture of the crushed breadcrumbs and cheese.
4. Roast in the oven until the potato is soft and beautifully golden brown.
5. Place on a hot dish (preferably silver) and garnish around with cooked vegetables.
6. Serve with roasted meat.
