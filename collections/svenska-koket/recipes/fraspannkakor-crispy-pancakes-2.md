## Ingredients

- 4 dcl. water
- approximately 160 gr. flour
- 2 tsk. sugar (10 gr.)
- 2 dcl. melted butter (2 hg.)
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Whisk the water and flour, which should be sifted, well together with a steel whisk, and let the batter stand for 2 hours.
2. Thereafter add the melted and cooled butter, sugar, and the cream beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Heat a pancake pan slowly and brush with butter for the first pancake.
4. Make thin pancakes from the batter, which are fried on one side only.
5. When the pancakes are light brown, roll them up on the pan with a fork.
6. Serve warm as a dessert with jam.
