## Ingredients

- 4 pig's feet
- 4 lit. water
- 4 tbsp. salt (60 gr.)
- soda, of the size of a pea

## Directions

1. The pig's feet are left in plenty of cold water for 1 day.
2. They are then scalded with hot water and scraped well, placed in cold water with the salt and soda added, skimmed, and allowed to boil until a whisk passes easily through the meat.
3. They are taken out immediately from the broth and cooled in cold water to become white. For the same reason, soda is used during cooking.
4. Preserved in brine.
5. Served with beets.
