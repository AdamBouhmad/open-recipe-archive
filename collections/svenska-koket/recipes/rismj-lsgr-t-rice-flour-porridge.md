## Ingredients

- 1 hg. rice flour
- 1 liter milk or water
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 6 sweet almonds, 4 bitter almonds
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. Stir the flour with 3 dcl. of the milk and let it stand for 1 hour to swell.
2. Melt the butter in a saucepan, bring the rest of the milk to a boil, pour 1 cup of it into the flour mixture, which is then added to the boiling milk while whisking vigorously.
3. Let the porridge thereafter cook while stirring for about 10 minutes.
4. Thin with boiling milk if needed.
5. Season the porridge with the scalded and ground almonds, salt and sugar.
6. Serve with milk or fruit sauce.
