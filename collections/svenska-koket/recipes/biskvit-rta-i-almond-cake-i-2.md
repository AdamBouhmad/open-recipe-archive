## Ingredients

- 350 gr. sweet almond
- 8 bitter almonds
- 425 gr. flour sugar
- 4–5 egg whites

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry and grind them.
2. Pound them thereafter with the sugar and one egg white until a fine and smooth mass.
3. Beat the remaining egg whites to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the almond mass, which should be quite loose.
4. Cut a round base from wafer bread, as large as the dish or bowl on which the cake is to be served.
5. Spread half of the dough on it.
6. Press the remaining dough through a piping bag in squares over the base, and bake the cake in a not too hot oven until it is beautifully golden brown but must not be completely dry.
7. When the cake has cooled, fill the squares with cooked, pitted medlars or yellow plums and jelly in various colors.
