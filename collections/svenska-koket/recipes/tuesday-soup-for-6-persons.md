## Ingredients

- 30 gr pearl barley
- 1 1/2 liters pork or beef stock
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 rutabaga
- 1 leek
- 1/2 liter potatoes
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 gr)
- 1/2 liter milk
- 2 dcl thick cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Rinse the barley, add to the boiling stock and let cook for about 1 1/2 hours.
2. Clean the root vegetables and leek, cut into strips and add to the soup.
3. Peel the potatoes, cut into cubes and add, then let the soup cook until the vegetables are soft.
4. Brown the butter and flour for 2 minutes, thin with the milk and cream, add the soup and let it cook while stirring for 5 minutes.
5. Season with the spices and finally add the parsley.
6. If a finer soup is desired, omit the barley and whisk 2-3 egg yolks in the soup bowl.
