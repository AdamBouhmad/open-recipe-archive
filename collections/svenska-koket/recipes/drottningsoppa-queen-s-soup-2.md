## Ingredients

- 1 young hen
- 3 1/2 liters light broth
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 6 tablespoons flour (60 gr.)
- salt
- cayenne
- 3–4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the hen in the usual manner, let it soak in water for 12 hours, dry it well and cook it in the broth until tender.
2. Remove the breast meat from the carcass, return the carcass to the broth and let it cook for a further 1 hour.
3. Cut the meat into small, oblong pieces and place in the soup bowl.
4. Strain the broth, skim it and measure it—should be 3 liters.
5. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the broth gradually with stirring, and let it cook for 5 minutes.
6. Season the soup with the spices.
7. Beat the egg yolks and whisk them into the soup, then pour it over the meat in the soup bowl.
