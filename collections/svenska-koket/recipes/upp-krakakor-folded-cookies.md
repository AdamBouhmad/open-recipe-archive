## Ingredients

- 425 gr. butter
- 210 gr. potato flour
- 160 gr. granulated sugar
- 425 gr. wheat flour
- 2 eggs for brushing
- 1/4 kkp pearl sugar
- 50 gr. sweet almond

## Directions

1. Stir the washed butter with sugar, and work in both types of flour.
2. Roll out the dough to about 2 mm thickness, and cut round cakes with a measure.
3. Fold the cakes closed almost in half.
4. Brush with whisked egg and sprinkle with chopped almond and sugar.
5. Bake very well in good oven heat.
