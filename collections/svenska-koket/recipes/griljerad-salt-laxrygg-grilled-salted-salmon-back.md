## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg salted salmon back
- 1 egg
- 1 kkp crushed hardtack
- 2 msk butter or stock

## Directions

1. Cut the salted salmon back into pieces and soak out the salt.
2. Remove the pieces and dry them thoroughly, then brush with egg and dredge in crushed hardtack.
3. Fry them in butter or stock and place on gray paper so the excess fat may be absorbed.
4. Serve salted salmon back with currant sauce.
