## Ingredients

- 4 kg. spirlånga or gråsej (stockfish varieties)
- 15 lit. vatten (water)
- 5 lit. stark björkaska (strong birch ash)
- 2 del. släckt kalk (slaked lime)

## Directions

1. Soak the fish as in the previous recipe.
2. From the water, ash and lime, boil a lye. Strain this into a clean basin and let it stand until the following day, when it should be completely clear.
3. Place the soaked fish in it with skin side up and let it lie 6-8 days.
4. Treat it then as in the previous recipe.
