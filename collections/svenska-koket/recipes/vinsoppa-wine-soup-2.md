## Ingredients

- 1½ litres water
- 1 lemon
- 150 grams sugar
- 3 parts French wine
- 1½ tablespoons potato flour
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook the water together with the thinly sliced lemon peel, well-pressed lemon juice and some of the sugar for 1½ hours, then strain out the lemon peel and return the soup to the pot.
2. When it comes to a boil again, thicken it with potato flour mixed with a little cold water.
