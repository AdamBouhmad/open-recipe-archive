## Ingredients

- 4 kg lamb roast
- 3 hg lard
- 2 tbsp salt (30 gr.)
- 1 tsp white pepper
- 1 hg butter
- 1 litre broth or water

## Directions

1. From one roasted lamb, the two connected roasts are cut off. The legs are cut off—but roasted with—and some of the fat is trimmed off. The meat is treated, roasted and served as in the previous recipe.
2. The roast is carved, the slices are placed back together again so that it appears whole, and garnished with cooked vegetables (légumes).
