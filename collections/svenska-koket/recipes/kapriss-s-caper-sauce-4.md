## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 5 decilitres fish stock or broth
- 1 decilitre thick cream
- 2 tablespoons chopped capers
- 1-2 tablespoons caper vinegar
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1-2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter (10 g) (optional)

## Directions

1. Melt butter and flour together for 2 minutes
2. Add fish stock and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
3. Add the capers and vinegar and bring to a boil
4. Season with salt, sugar and white pepper, then bind with the whisked egg yolk
5. If desired, stir in the cold butter last
6. Serve mainly with fish but also with boiled veal or lamb breast
