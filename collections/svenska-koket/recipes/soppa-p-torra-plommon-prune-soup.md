## Ingredients

- 250 gr prunes or dried plums
- 1 tablespoon crushed sugar
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 2 liters water
- 25 gr potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the plums very well in lukewarm water and let them drain. Crush half of them in a mortar so that the pits are broken.
2. Place the crushed and whole plums in cold water and let them cook until completely soft, then press them through a sieve.
3. Wash the lemon well, dry it, and rub the peel against the sugar.
4. Boil the pressed soup together with the sugar and thicken with the potato flour, stirred in 1/2 tablespoon cold water.
5. Let it cook for 2 minutes, flavor, and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and small rusks or biscuits.
