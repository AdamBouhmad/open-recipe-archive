## Ingredients

- 2 kg. salt fat herring
- 2 liters skim milk
- 6 tbsp. granulated sugar
- 1 dessert spoon saltpeter

## Directions

1. Pack the salted herring in a porcelain jar.
2. Bring the milk to a boil with the sugar and the crushed saltpeter.
3. When the milk is completely cold, pour it over the herring and it should stand well above it.
4. Let the herring soak in the milk for eight days, after which it is ready to use.
5. It can be served marinated, fried in paper or pickled.
