## Ingredients

- 530 gr. butter
- 210 gr. sweet almonds
- 425 gr. granulated sugar
- 750 gr. wheat flour
- 2 eggs
- raspberry jam for filling

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with sugar until white.
2. Work in the eggs, the blanched and grated sweet almonds, and the wheat flour.
3. Set the dough aside to cool.
4. Roll out quite thin and cut out round cakes with a measure.
5. Place a little raspberry jam on half of each cake and fold the other half over.
6. Make small points at the edge with a piping tool.
7. Bake in moderate oven heat.
