## Ingredients

- 1 deciliter rice grains (100 g)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 g)
- 2 1/4 liters milk
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains in cold water, scald them (let them sit for 10 minutes in hot water), pour into a colander and rinse again.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, add the milk and bring to a boil.
3. Add the grains and let them simmer slowly with a tight lid for about 2 hours, stirring often and adding more liquid if needed.
4. Season the porridge with salt and serve with sugar and cinnamon.
