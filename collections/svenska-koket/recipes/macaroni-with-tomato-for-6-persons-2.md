## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 hectograms fine macaroni
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 1 tbsp. salt
- 2 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
- Tomato sauce: 2 small red onions, 4 tomatoes, 50 gr. butter, 6 deciliters light broth, 2 tbsp. flour (20 gr.), salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni as in the previous recipe but do not let it cool.
2. For the sauce: peel the onion and chop it finely; rinse the tomatoes, dry them and cut into slices.
3. Fry the onion in half of the butter, add the tomatoes and broth and let cook until a purée that is strained.
4. Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes.
5. Add the tomato purée, let the sauce cook for 5 minutes and season carefully with spices.
6. Place the macaroni on a warm serving dish or in shells, sprinkle cheese over it and serve the sauce in a sauce bowl.
7. The sauce can also be poured over the macaroni, but then the cheese should be served separately.
8. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve as an intermediate course.
