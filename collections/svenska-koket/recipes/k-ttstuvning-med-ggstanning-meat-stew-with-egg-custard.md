## Ingredients

- 4 cups sliced meat (leftovers from roast)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 4 cups broth or meat gravy
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 leek (optional)
- 3 eggs for the egg custard
- 3 cups milk for the egg custard
- salt and sugar for the egg custard
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes. Make a thick sauce from butter, flour and broth; season with spices, and add the finely chopped leek. Heat the meat in this sauce, and pour the stew into a buttered and breadcrumb-coated mold.
2. Whip the eggs, mix with milk, a little salt and sugar and pour into the mold over the pudding. Sprinkle with finely chopped parsley and place in oven to set and turn a beautiful light golden color.
