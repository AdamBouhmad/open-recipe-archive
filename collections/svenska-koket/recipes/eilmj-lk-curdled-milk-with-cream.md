## Ingredients

- 1 msk. sour cream
- 1 1/4 lit. milk
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Coat the bottom of a milk bowl with 1 msk. sour cream
2. Pour in the milk and let it sour
3. Whip the thick cream to stiff peaks
4. Layer slices of the soured milk into the whipped cream
5. Serve with pepper cookies
