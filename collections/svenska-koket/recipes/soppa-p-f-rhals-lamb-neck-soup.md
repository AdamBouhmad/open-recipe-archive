## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons barley grains
- 1 leek
- 3/4 kg lamb neck
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 6 potatoes

## Directions

1. Rinse the barley well, sauté the leek and cut it into strips
2. Rinse the meat, dry it with a cloth, and cut it into pieces, which are browned in the butter
3. Pour on the cold water, let the soup come to a boil and skim well
4. Add the barley, leek, and salt, and let the soup cook about 3 hours
5. Rinse the potatoes, peel them, cut them into slices, and add them to the soup 1/2 hour before it is done
6. Serve the meat in the soup
