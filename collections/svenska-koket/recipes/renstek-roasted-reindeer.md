## Ingredients

- 3 kg. reindeer roast
- 4 liters drink
- 2 kg. lard
- 3 tbsp. butter
- 1 tbsp. salt
- 1–1½ liters boiling broth or water
- 1½ tbsp. butter for sauce
- 3 tbsp. flour
- meat stock
- 1–2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. If the roast is frozen, it should lie in drink until it is completely thawed. Remove it, rinse well, dry with a cloth, and remove all membranes carefully. Lard the roast thereafter in close rows with the lard cut into fine strips. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, and brown the roast well therein, salt it then and dilute with broth little by little, baste often and turn a couple of times during roasting. The roasting takes 3–4 hours.
2. Strain and skim the meat stock, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
