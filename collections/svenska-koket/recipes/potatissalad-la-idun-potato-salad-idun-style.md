## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter cooked, cold potatoes
- 2 cooked beets
- 1 head of lettuce
- 2 tablespoons capers
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon English mustard
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1-2 tablespoons vinegar or vinegar
- 1 tablespoon caper vinegar
- 3 tablespoons fine vegetable oil
- 1 deciliter thick cream

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cut into thin slices. Cut the beets into 1 1/2 cm. long strips. Clean the lettuce, rinse, fold into a cloth and cut into long, fine strips. Mix all this in a salad bowl.
2. Stir the egg yolks in a small bowl with salt, mustard and sugar, then add the vinegar and vegetable oil.
3. Last, stir in the cream, and pour the sauce over the salad, which is stirred gently and served immediately with cold meat dishes in general.
