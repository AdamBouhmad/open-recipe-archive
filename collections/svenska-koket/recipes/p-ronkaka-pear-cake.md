## Ingredients

- 2 liters pears
- 1 cup crushed sugar
- 2 cups water
- ¾ liter milk
- 100 gr. wheat flour
- 100 gr. butter
- 100 gr. castor sugar
- grated peel of 1 lemon
- 4 eggs
- ½ tsk. butter for the form

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, cut them in half, and remove the cores.
2. From the sugar and water boil a clear syrup, and in this cook the pears until they are soft.
3. Place them on the bottom of a buttered pie form.
4. Bring the milk to a boil, stir in the sifted flour quickly, let the mixture cook 10 minutes and then cool.
5. Meanwhile mix in the cold butter, sugar, lemon peel and egg yolks one at a time.
6. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
7. Pour the mixture over the pears and place the cake in moderate oven heat to bake for a time.
8. Test with a whisk and serve after most of the heat has subsided.
