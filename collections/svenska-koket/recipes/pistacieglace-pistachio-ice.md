## Ingredients

- 0.5 liter thin cream
- 1 hectogram sweet almonds
- 10 bitter almonds
- 3 egg yolks
- 50-100 gr. sugar
- 0.5 parts arrack
- Green caramel coloring
- 30 pistachio almonds
- 3 parts heavy cream

## Directions

1. The sweet almonds and bitter almonds are blanched, passed through an almond grinder or grated, and cooked with the thin cream for approximately 10 minutes, after which the mixture is strained.
2. The cream is brought to a boil again and mixed with vigorous whisking into the egg yolks.
3. The mixture is then allowed to simmer over heat until it thickens, after which it is removed from heat.
4. It is flavored with sugar and arrack, colored, and beaten in an ice machine to freeze.
5. When the ice is half frozen, the blanched and chopped pistachio almonds and the cream whisked to peaks are mixed in, and freezing is continued.
6. The pistachio ice is served in glasses together with some white ice.
