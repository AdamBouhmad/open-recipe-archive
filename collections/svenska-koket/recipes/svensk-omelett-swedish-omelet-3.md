## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks well with salt and sugar.
2. Add the cream and carefully fold in the egg whites, which have been beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Heat the omelet pan and butter it well with the butter.
4. Pour in the egg mixture and cook the omelet light brown in ordinary oven heat.
5. Tip it onto a buttered lid, place some stewed filling on one half and fold the other half over.
6. The filling can also be placed on a round dish and the omelet tipped over it.
7. The omelet can be filled with stewing of different or mixed mushroom varieties, asparagus, sugar beets, sweetbreads, lobster, crayfish and fish, etc.
