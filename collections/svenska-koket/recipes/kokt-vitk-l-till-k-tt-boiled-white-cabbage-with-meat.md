## Ingredients

- 1 medium white cabbage head
- 3 liters water, 1 tablespoon salt (15 g) for blanching
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 2 carrots
- 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1½ hectograms fresh pork, in 1 slice
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- ¼ teaspoon white pepper
- 3 deciliters boiling water

## Directions

1. Remove all green leaves from the cabbage head; loosen the remaining leaves from the core and remove the thickest part of the main nerve.
2. Blanch the cabbage leaves (place in boiling salted water and let cook without a lid 5–8 minutes); lift out with a slotted spoon and let drain on cloth.
3. Roll the leaves into balls and twist them in a cloth.
4. Butter a saucepan and cover the bottom with the peeled and thickly sliced carrots, the onion, and the pork slice.
5. Place the cabbage balls on top, sprinkle with the seasonings, add the water, and let cook slowly with a lid until quite tender, about 2½ hours.
6. Serve as a garnish around meat or on a separate plate.
