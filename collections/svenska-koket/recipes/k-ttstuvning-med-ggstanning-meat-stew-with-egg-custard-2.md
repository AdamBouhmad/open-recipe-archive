## Ingredients

- 4 cups meat cut into cubes (leftovers from roast)
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 4 cups bouillon or meat gravy
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 leek (optional)
- 3 eggs for egg custard
- 3 cups milk for egg custard
- salt and sugar for egg custard
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed hardtack for the mold

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into cubes. Make a thick sauce from butter, flour and bouillon; season with the seasonings, and add the finely chopped leek. Warm the meat in this sauce, and pour the stew into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined mold.
2. Beat the eggs, mix with milk, a little salt and sugar and pour into the mold over the pudding. Sprinkle with finely chopped parsley and bake in the oven until set and a beautiful light golden color is achieved.
