## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- 1 part white French wine
- 2 parts water
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 2 teaspoons potato flour
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. The lemon is zested so that only the yellow skin comes with it, and the juice is pressed out.
2. All ingredients are whisked well together in a pan and are allowed to simmer over the heat with vigorous whisking until the mixture thickens.
3. The pan is removed from the heat, and whisking is continued until the sauce has cooled.
