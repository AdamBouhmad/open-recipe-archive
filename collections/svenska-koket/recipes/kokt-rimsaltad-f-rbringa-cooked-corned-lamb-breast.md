## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg lamb breast
- 4 tablespoons salt (60 g)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 g)
- 2 g saltpeter

## Directions

1. Crack the ribs and rub the breast with a mixture of salt, saltpeter and sugar. Place it in a deep dish and let it stand 3-4 days, turning it often in the brine that forms.
2. Wash the meat thoroughly with a cloth dipped in cold water, place in cold water, bring quickly to a boil, skim well and boil for 15 minutes at 100°, after which cool the broth to 82°-83° and let the meat lie in it without boiling until tender, about 1 1/2-2 hours.
3. Serve with browned turnips, stewed vegetables or potato purée. The broth can be used for soup or purée.
4. Alternatively, after cooking the meat may be brushed with beaten egg, sprinkled with finely ground breadcrumbs, placed in a long pan whose bottom is covered with broth, and put in a hot oven to gratinée for 15-20 minutes. Serve as above.
