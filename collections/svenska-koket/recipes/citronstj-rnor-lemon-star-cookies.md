## Ingredients

- 425 gr. wheat flour
- 212 gr. sugar
- 212 gr. butter
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 tsk. ammonium carbonate (1 1/2 gr.)
- 1/2 msk. brandy or cognac
- 1 msk. finely grated lemon peel
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 3 msk. pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour in a bowl (1 1/2 dcl. set aside for dusting)
2. Mix with the sugar, butter, whipped eggs, ammonium carbonate dissolved in brandy or cognac, and the grated lemon peel
3. Work the dough until it becomes smooth and even
4. Roll out to about 3 mm thickness
5. Cut out with a star-shaped mold into cookies
6. Brush with lightly beaten egg white, sprinkle with pearl sugar
7. Bake until golden brown in a not-too-hot oven
