## Ingredients

- 750 gr. sugar
- 1 liter water
- zest of 4 oranges
- juice of 8 oranges and 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar syrup
2. Remove all white pith from the orange zest
3. Cut the zest into pieces and place in the hot syrup and let cool
4. Prepare the mixture as in the lemon ice cream recipe
