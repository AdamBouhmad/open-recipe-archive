## Ingredients

- 1½ litres water
- 1 lemon
- 150 grams sugar
- 3 parts French wine
- 1½ tablespoon potato flour
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil the water for 1 hour together with the thinly sliced lemon peel, the well-pressed lemon juice and part of the sugar, then strain out the lemon peel and return the soup to the pot.
