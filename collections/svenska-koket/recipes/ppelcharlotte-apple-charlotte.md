## Ingredients

- 6 two-öre almond breads
- 1 1/2 hectograms melted butter
- 1 hectogram coarse crushed caster sugar
- 150 gr. sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 1 1/2 liters good, tart apples
- 1 tbsp. lemon juice or vinegar
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Grease a copper or tin form with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
2. Split the breads lengthwise, dip them in the melted butter and turn them in the sugar.
3. Line the form with bread slices, fill it with apple compote, and place the remaining bread slices over it as a lid.
4. Bake the pudding for about 1/2 hour in ordinary oven heat, turn it out and serve warm with vanilla sauce.
