## Ingredients

- 600 g boneless beef
- 1 egg yolk
- 3 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped Portuguese onion
- 2.5 cups cooked potato cut in cubes
- 1 cup cooked beets
- 3 tablespoons beet vinegar
- 100 g butter

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it into cubes and pass 3 times through a meat grinder.
2. Mix the egg yolk, milk, spices and the browned onion into the forcemeat, and work it very well.
3. Carefully mix in the potato, beets and vinegar.
4. Form round patties from the forcemeat and fry them beautifully brown in the butter.
