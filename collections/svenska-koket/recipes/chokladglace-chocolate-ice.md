## Ingredients

- 1 litre cream
- 1 large vanilla pod
- 160 gr. sugar-coated chocolate
- 2 dcl. water
- 7 egg yolks
- 2 hg. sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the cream with 1 large vanilla pod and let it stand covered for 20 minutes to infuse.
2. Grate the chocolate, dissolve it with 2 dcl. water and blend with the cream.
3. Prepare the cream from 7 egg yolks and 2 hg. sugar as described in the basic recipe.
4. Freeze as in recipe n:o 929.
