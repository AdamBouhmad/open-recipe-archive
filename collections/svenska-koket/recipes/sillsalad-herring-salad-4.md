## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 salt herring
- 10 medium potatoes
- 6 beets
- 2 apples
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 25 grams veal roast
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 2 hard-boiled eggs for garnish
- 1 beet in cubes for garnish
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Skin the herring, remove it from the water, rinse it well, and let it soak in water overnight.
2. Cut it into fillets and carefully remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets well and wrap them in a fish towel.
4. Cut them into fine cubes, as well as the potatoes, beets, apples, pickled cucumber, and veal roast.
5. Mix all these ingredients lightly together and season with vinegar and spices.
6. Pack the herring salad tightly into a water-rinsed dome mold, turn it out, and garnish with hard-boiled eggs and beets.
7. Serve with sharp sauce or whipped cream as a breakfast dish or with a smorgasbord.
