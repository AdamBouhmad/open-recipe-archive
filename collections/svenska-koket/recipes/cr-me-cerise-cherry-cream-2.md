## Ingredients

- ½ liter cherries
- 1 teaspoon fine sugar
- 2½ parts water
- 1 liter thick cream
- 1 teaspoon sifted sugar
- ½ part cognac
- 25 grams cocoa
- 15 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Cook the cherries with sugar and water until they are soft, then strain off the juice.
2. Dissolve five sheets of gelatin in a little lukewarm water and mix with the cherry juice.
3. Rinse a beautiful pipe mold with cold water and fill it one-third with cherry gelatin, then set it out to stiffen.
4. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks; flavor one half with cognac and the other half with cocoa.
5. Dissolve five sheets of gelatin in a little lukewarm water and stir into the white cream; fill this onto the set gelatin so that the mold is filled two-thirds.
6. Set the cream out to stiffen.
7. Dissolve the remaining gelatin in a little lukewarm water and stir into the cocoa cream, and fill the mold with this.
8. Set it out to stiffen.
9. When it is to be served, quickly dip the mold in lukewarm water and turn out the cream.
