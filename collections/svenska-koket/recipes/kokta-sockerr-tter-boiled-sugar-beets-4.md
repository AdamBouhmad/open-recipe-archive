## Ingredients

- 80 fine, even sugar beets
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 1/2 lemon
- vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar)

## Directions

1. Scrape and rinse the sugar beets well and let them soak in vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar) for 1 hour.
2. Tie them together in bundles of 6 each, place in boiling salted water with the juice of 1/2 lemon, and cook until soft, which is tested with a whisk.
3. Remove with a slotted spoon, arrange on an oblong platter with a folded napkin, and serve very hot with melted butter or hollandaise sauce, mixed with the juice of 1 blood orange.
