## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless veal roast
- 1 1/2 hectograms larding fat
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 2 carrots
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 liter broth

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, beat lightly with a damp mallet, and lard thickly with fine larding fat strips.
