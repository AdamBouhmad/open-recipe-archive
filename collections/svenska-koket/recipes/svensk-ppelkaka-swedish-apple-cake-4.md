## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lit. fresh or 1 1/2 hg. dried apples
- 1/2 twenty-five öre spiced bread loaf, not too fresh
- 2 hg. sugar
- 150 gr. butter
- 1-1 1/2 dcl. water
- 1/2 msk. butter for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them, cut them into fine pieces, free them from the cores and cook them with the sugar and a little water until it is a mush.
2. Grate the loaf and brown it lightly in 1 hg. of the butter.
3. Butter a mold or frying pan and sprinkle with crushed rusks, lay three layers of bread and two layers of apple mush in it.
4. Top the uppermost layer with the remaining butter, distributed in small flakes.
5. Bake the cake in ordinary oven heat for about 1/2 hour, then remove it and let it stand for 15 minutes.
6. Turn out, sprinkle with sugar and serve warm or cold with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
7. If dried apples are used, rinse them well and soak in 2 dcl. water for a few hours, then cook them with 150 gr. sugar until it is a mush.
8. Instead of apples, gooseberries or rhubarb can be used, but more sugar is needed then.
9. The cake can be made from 6 dcl. crushed rusks instead of from spiced bread loaf.
