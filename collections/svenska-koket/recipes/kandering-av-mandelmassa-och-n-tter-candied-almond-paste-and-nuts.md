## Ingredients

- 2 hg. sweet almonds
- 2 hg. powdered sugar
- 1-2 tbsp. water
- 4 hg. walnuts
- 1/2 kg. rock sugar
- 2 1/2 dcl. water
- 1 tbsp. glucose (15 gr.)
- carmine for coloring
- cocoa powder for coloring
- caramel coloring
- oil for preparation

## Directions

1. Prepare almond paste from sweet almonds, powdered sugar, and water. Divide into four equal parts.
2. Color one part bright red with carmine, one part light green with caramel coloring, one part brown with cocoa powder, and leave the last quarter white.
3. Gently crack walnuts, remove shells, and join walnut halves together with the uncolored almond paste. Roll the remaining almond paste into small balls.
4. Cook rock sugar, water, and glucose to caramel or strong crack stage (see sugar cooking). Cool rapidly by dipping the pan briefly in cold water.
5. Drop walnuts and almond paste balls one at a time into the sugar, lift out with two small silver forks dipped in food oil, and place on oiled marble or stone slab.
6. Similarly candy peeled brazil nuts (with inner brown skin scraped off, coloring the sugar layer red with carmine), peeled Spanish nuts, orange segments, grapes, etc.
