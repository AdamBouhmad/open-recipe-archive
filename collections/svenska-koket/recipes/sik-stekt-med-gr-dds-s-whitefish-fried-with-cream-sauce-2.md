## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg fat whitefish
- 1 msk salt (15 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 3 msk flour (30 gr.)
- 1 hg butter
- 4 dcl thick cream
- 3 msk coarsely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, rinse well and dry
2. Cut along the backbone and remove it along with all small bones
3. (If desired, remove the skin, but many consider it a delicacy)
4. Rub the fillets with salt and let rest 1 hour
5. Dry again and cut into 3 1/2 cm wide pieces across
6. Dip in the whipped egg and coat in wheat flour
7. Brown quickly in half the butter in a frying pan and layer in a buttered baking dish with the parsley
8. Place the remaining butter in flakes on top and add the cream
9. Cover with a lid and place in the oven
10. Let the fish fry for about 20 minutes
11. Serve from the dish with mashed potatoes
12. (Instead of parsley, chopped capers may be used)
