## Ingredients

- 1 kg beef fillet
- 3 tbsp preserved mushrooms
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp salt
- ½–1 liter broth or water
- ½ tbsp butter (for sauce)
- 1⅛ tbsp flour
- beef stock
- ½ part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, whereupon it is studded with the chopped mushroom stems (the caps are saved for garnish).
2. Brown the beef fillet in 1⅛ tbsp of the butter, salt it, and dilute the boiling liquid in a little at a time.
3. Let the fillet slowly roast with a tightly closed lid 1–1½ hours, or until the meat feels tender.
4. Cut the meat into thin slices and garnish with the mushroom caps fried in the rest of the butter.
5. Strain the meat stock and skim it, and prepare the sauce according to general rules.
