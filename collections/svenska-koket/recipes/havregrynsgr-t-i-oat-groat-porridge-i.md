## Ingredients

- 3 deciliters ordinary oat groats (225 grams)
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams)
- 1 liter water
- 1/2 liter cream
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)

## Directions

1. Place the groats in a sieve, rinse with cold water and drain. Put the butter in a pot, pour in the water, add the rinsed groats and let stand for about 12 hours to swell. Let the porridge come to a boil while stirring and then let it cook slowly with a tightly closed lid until the groats are completely tender, or about 3 hours. Toward the end of cooking, thin with cream. Season with salt and serve with milk.
