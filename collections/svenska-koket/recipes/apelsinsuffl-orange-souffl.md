## Ingredients

- 20 grams cold butter
- 50 grams sugar
- 3 deciliters cream
- 50 grams melted butter
- zest and juice of 1/2 orange
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the form

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan and stir with sugar and cream over heat until the mixture becomes thick, then pour into a bowl.
2. Add the cold butter and stir the mixture until it becomes cold, then add the finely grated orange zest and orange juice.
3. Stir in the egg yolks one at a time, while working the mixture for 1/2 hour.
4. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks and pour the mixture into a buttered pie form, sprinkle with sugar and bake for about 1/2 hour in ordinary oven heat. Serve immediately from the form.
