## Ingredients

- 1 kg cooked or fried meat
- 1 egg
- ¾ cup milk
- 1 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices.
2. Dip them in the whisked egg and milk and roll them in the crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Fry them golden brown in butter.
4. Serve with potato purée or vegetables.
