## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg rhubarb
- 125 grams granulated sugar
- 1/2 liter water
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- 5 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the form

## Directions

1. Peel the rhubarb, cut into pieces and cook quickly soft in the water together with the granulated sugar
2. Stir the egg yolks with the sour cream, granulated sugar and lemon zest until the mixture is foamy
3. Beat the whites until stiff peaks form and fold in
4. Pour half of the batter into a buttered heat-resistant form
