## Ingredients

- 1/3 liter syrup
- 635 gr. brown sugar
- 2 tbsp. pulverized soda
- 3 tbsp. grated ginger
- 1 tsp. cedrat oil
- 1/3 liter thick cream
- 1,275 kg. wheat flour

## Directions

1. Stir the syrup until it becomes light.
2. Then stir in the sugar, soda, spices and the cream whipped to stiff foam.
3. Last work in the flour.
4. Roll the dough out not too thinly, and with a measure cut out small round cookies, which are brushed over with water and baked in even oven heat.
