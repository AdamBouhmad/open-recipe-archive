## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 75 gr. caster sugar
- 150 gr. fine butter
- 3–4 tbsp. fine cognac
- a little ground nutmeg

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes.
2. Wash the butter and work out the water well, then stir it until it is white and fluffy, then add the egg mixture a little at a time, and finally add the cognac by the teaspoon and, if desired, a little finely ground nutmeg while continuing to stir evenly.
3. Serve the sauce immediately with warm fruit dishes.
