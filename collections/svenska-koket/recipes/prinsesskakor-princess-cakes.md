## Ingredients

- 425 gr. butter
- 4 egg whites
- 50 gr. granulated sugar
- 425 gr. wheat flour
- 25 gr. bitter almonds
- 2 egg whites for brushing
- 1/4 cup pearl sugar
- 50 gr. sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and sift the flour on the work table.
2. Distribute the butter in flakes and place on top of the flour along with sugar, the blanched and finely grated almonds, and egg whites (not beaten to peaks).
3. Work all ingredients together into a smooth, cohesive dough.
4. Roll out quite thick and cut out small cakes with a measure.
5. Brush with egg white and sprinkle with almonds and sugar.
6. Bake until light brown in moderate oven heat.
