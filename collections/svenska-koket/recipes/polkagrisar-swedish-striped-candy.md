## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. socker
- 2 1/2 dcl. vatten
- 1 msk. glykos
- 10 droppar pepparmyntolja
- karmin

## Directions

1. Cook the socker, water, and glykos to hard crack stage, then add 10 drops of peppermint oil.
2. Pour half of the sugar onto a lightly oiled marble slab and continuously fold the edges inward with an oiled knife until the sugar has cooled.
3. Pull the sugar between oiled hands or over a hook until it is white, then pull it out into a thin length.
4. Meanwhile, cook the remaining sugar to caramel stage, color it red with carmine, pour it out, roll it out, and place it on the white sugar which is twisted and cut diagonally into 2—3 cm. long candies.
