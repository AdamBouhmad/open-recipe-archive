## Ingredients

- 75 gr. biscuits
- 4 sheets gelatin
- 3 tbsp. boiling water
- 4 dcl. extra thick cream
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- 6 plums (compote) for garnish
- 2 tbsp. red currant jelly for garnish

## Directions

1. Dry the biscuits in a warm oven and pound them, not too finely, in a stone mortar.
2. Rinse the gelatin, cut it into pieces, and dissolve it in the water.
3. Whip the cream with 1 tbsp. of the sugar to stiff peaks.
4. Gradually fold in the cooled gelatin, the remaining sugar, vanilla sugar, and the pounded biscuits.
5. Pour the mixture into a rinsed and sugar-sprinkled mold and let it set on ice for 2-3 hours.
6. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it from the edges with a pointed knife and turn it out onto a flat glass dish with parchment paper.
7. Garnish with fruit and jelly.
