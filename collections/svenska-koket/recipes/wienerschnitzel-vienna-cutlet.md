## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless veal from the loin
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 2 cups cold water
- 1 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 18 cleaned and boned anchovies
- 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoons capers

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut across into approximately 1.5 cm thick slices, which are pounded well.
2. Dip the slices in strongly salted water (3 tablespoons salt and 2 cups water) and let them drain.
3. Turn them in crushed breadcrumbs and fry quickly on both sides in the butter.
4. Whisk the pan with a little boiling water, and pour the sauce carefully onto the serving platter.
5. On top of each schnitzel place a lemon slice, 2 halves of a boned and cleaned anchovy crosswise and a few chopped capers without their vinegar.
