## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 75 gr. powdered sugar
- 150 gr. fine butter
- 3-4 tbsp. fine cognac
- a little ground nutmeg

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes.
2. Wash the butter and work out the water well, then stir until it is white and fluffy.
3. Add the egg mixture a little at a time, and lastly add the cognac by the teaspoon, and if desired, a little finely ground nutmeg with continued even stirring.
4. Serve the sauce immediately with warm fruit dishes.
