## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 kg. oskalade päron
- 1 1/2 kg. strösocker
- 8 dcl. vatten
- 2—3 bitar ingefära

## Directions

1. The pears should not be too large; peel them and scrape the stems.
2. Cut a cross at the top of each pear.
3. Layer the pears with the sugar in a jam pot and add the water.
4. Crush the ginger somewhat, wrap it in a muslin cloth and add it.
5. Let the pears cook slowly with a tight-fitting lid until they are completely clear, or approx. 4-5 hours.
6. Lift out the pears with a slotted spoon onto a flat dish to cool.
7. Place them in jars that have been rinsed with cognac.
8. Remove the ginger, let the cooking liquid boil down slightly, cool it, and pour over the pears.
