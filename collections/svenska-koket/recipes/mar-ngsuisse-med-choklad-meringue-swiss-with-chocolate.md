## Ingredients

- 165 gr. powdered sugar
- 3 large egg whites
- 75 gr. sugared block chocolate
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 6 dcl. extra thick cream
- 1 tbsp. sugar
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- carmine

## Directions

1. Prepare the meringue mixture as in the previous recipe and place in the form of small wreaths or ordinary meringues on a well-cleaned and buttered baking sheet, which are dusted with a little sugar and baked in gentle oven heat until they are almost completely dry and release from the sheet.
2. Dissolve the chocolate in a water bath, without the addition of water. Blanch the almonds, cut them into thin slices, which are lightly roasted with 2 tsp. sugar in the oven.
3. When the meringues are cold, arrange them on a flat glass bowl with pastry paper, alternating with the melted chocolate, so that they form a pyramid. Pour chocolate over the pyramid and sprinkle with the roasted almonds.
4. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks. Half of it is flavored with vanilla sugar, the other half is colored pink with carmine. The white and pink cream are piped, each separately, through a fluted piping bag in beautiful garlands over the pyramid.
5. The Swiss meringue should not be garnished until 1/2 hour before it is to be served.
