## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potato
- water
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 2-2 1/2 deciliters boiling milk

## Directions

1. Rinse the potato, brush well, peel thinly and cook.
2. Drain the water well and press the potato through a purée press or stir with a fork into a purée.
3. Add the cold butter immediately with vigorous stirring and thereafter the boiling milk, a little at a time.
4. Season the purée with salt and a little sugar if desired.
5. Mashed potatoes should be served immediately after preparation; if it stands long it becomes thick and sticky.
