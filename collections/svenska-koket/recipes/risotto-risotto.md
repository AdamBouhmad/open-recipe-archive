## Ingredients

- 1 medium Portuguese onion
- 150 gr. butter
- 180 gr. Carolina rice
- 1 gr. crushed saffron
- 1/2 liter good broth
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 hectogram smoked ham
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the onion, chop it finely and sauté it well in 1 hectogram of the butter in a saucepan.
2. Add the rinsed rice together with the finely crushed saffron and stir over low heat until well mixed with the butter.
3. Add the broth gradually, stirring the grains frequently with a wooden spoon.
4. Cook the rice slowly with a lid, stirring often, until the grains are soft and begin to break apart.
5. Finish the dish with the remaining butter, Parmesan cheese and the ham cut into fine cubes, and salt.
6. Serve as a breakfast or intermediate course.
