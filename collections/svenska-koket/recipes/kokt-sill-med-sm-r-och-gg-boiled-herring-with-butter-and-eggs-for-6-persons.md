## Ingredients

- 8 salted herrings
- water
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/4 hg. butter

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water the day before it is to be used.
2. Take it up, remove the skin and entrails and split it down the middle, then remove the backbone and as many small bones as possible.
3. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes, peel and chop them finely.
4. Place the herring in boiling water.
