## Ingredients

- 400 gr butter
- 400 gr wheat flour
- 90 gr sweet almond
- 90 gr sugar
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Wash the butter.
2. Sift the flour on the work surface.
3. Blanch the almond, dry it and grate it or run it through an almond mill.
4. Work all the ingredients well together on the work surface to a smooth dough, and then place it out to cool.
