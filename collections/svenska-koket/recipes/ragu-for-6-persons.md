## Ingredients

- 1 kg. cooked or roasted meat
- 1 small red onion
- 1 tbsp. butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- 1/2 liter broth or pan drippings
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into appropriately sized pieces.
2. Peel the onion, chop it finely and brown it in the butter.
3. Fry the flour in it, and add the broth or pan drippings together with the cucumber cut into thin slices.
4. Let the sauce simmer for 10 minutes, then add the meat and bring it to a boil.
5. Season the dish with pepper and salt.
6. Note: If using roasted meat, do not let it simmer in the sauce, as it will become tough.
