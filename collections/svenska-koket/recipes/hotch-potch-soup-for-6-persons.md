## Ingredients

- 1 kg lamb breast
- 2 liters beef stock
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- 1 leek
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr)
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 celery root
- 1/4 liter preserved peas (or 2 cups fresh peas)
- 12 small Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 small cauliflower head
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Dry the lamb breast with a cloth, wring in hot water, crack it well and bring to boil in the stock.
2. When the meat is half-cooked, remove it and cut into small, even pieces.
3. Strain the stock, skim carefully and return to the pot.
4. Brown the meat in 2 tbsp of the butter together with the leek, cut into fine strips, sprinkle flour over and stir the meat well.
5. Add it to the stock, which should simmer slowly for about 1 hour.
6. Clean the root vegetables, cut into fine strips, brown in the remaining butter and add together with the peas to the soup 1/2 hour before it is ready.
7. Clean the Jerusalem artichokes and cauliflower in the usual way, place in 1/2 liter boiling water, added with 1/2 tsp salt, and let them cook until soft.
8. Put them in the soup bowl and reduce the cooking liquid until it becomes about 3 dcl, then pour it over the vegetables.
9. Skim the soup, season with the spices, pour into the soup bowl and serve very hot.
