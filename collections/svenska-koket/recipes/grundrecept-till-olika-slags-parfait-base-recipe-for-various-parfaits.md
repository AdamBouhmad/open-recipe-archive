## Ingredients

- 125 grams sugar
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter water
- 6 deciliters extra thick cream

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water together.
2. Remove the saucepan from heat and add the egg yolks one at a time while whisking.
3. Let the cream simmer over heat with vigorous whisking until thick and frothy.
4. Remove the saucepan from heat and whisk the cream until completely cold.
5. Whisk the cream to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the cream.
6. Blend with desired flavor and coloring.
7. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed form sprinkled with sugar and freeze for 4-5 hours as in recipe no. 929.
