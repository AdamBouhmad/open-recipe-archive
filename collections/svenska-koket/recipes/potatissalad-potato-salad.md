## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters potatoes
- water, salt
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 5 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 cup lukewarm water
- 1.5 teaspoons French mustard
- 1 tablespoon pickled onion

## Directions

1. Brush the potatoes, cook and peel them while still warm, and cut them into thin slices and place them in a bowl.
2. Mix the oil, vinegar, lukewarm water, mustard and the chopped onion well, and pour this mixture over the potatoes, which are gently stirred.
3. The salad is best served with cold meat.
