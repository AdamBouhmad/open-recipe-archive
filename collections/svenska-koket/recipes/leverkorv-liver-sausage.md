## Ingredients

- 1 pig liver
- 1 kg pork meat
- ½ kg lard pork
- 1 teaspoon ground marjoram
- 1 teaspoon ground thyme
- ¼ teaspoon grated ginger
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon strong pepper
- 2½ tablespoons salt
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 3 tablespoons lard
- 5 parts pork broth
- 5 meters straight pork casings

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into pieces and let it soak in cold water overnight.
2. Pour off the water and pour boiling water over the liver a couple of times.
3. Boil the pork meat and lard and let them cool.
4. Cut the pork meat into pieces and pass through the meat grinder.
5. Also grind the liver and pass it through a sieve.
6. Cut the lard into cubes and mix with the rest of the forcemeat.
7. Chop the lard and melt it in a pan.
8. Fry the chopped onion in it.
9. Strain off the liquid and mix it into the forcemeat along with all the spices and pork broth.
10. The mixture, which should be fairly loose, is filled into straight pork casings.
11. Prick the sausages carefully with a needle and cook slowly for ½ hour.
