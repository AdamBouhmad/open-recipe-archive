## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg reindeer steak
- 2 liters weak drinking alcohol
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2.5 tablespoons butter
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 2 parts broth
- 1.5 parts cream

## Directions

1. Place the meat in drinking alcohol overnight and wrap well in a cloth.
2. Cut into approximately 3 cm thick slices, which are beaten on both sides and seasoned with pepper and salt.
3. Heat a pan on the stove. Brown the butter in it, and place the steaks in to fry quickly about 2 minutes on each side.
4. Deglaze the pan with the boiling broth and cream, and pour the sauce over the steaks.
5. Finally, brown the peeled onion cut into slices, and garnish the steaks with it.
