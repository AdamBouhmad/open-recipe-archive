## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters good, loose apples
- 50 gr caster sugar
- 3 two-öre almond biscuits
- 1 1/2 dcl lukewarm milk
- 120 gr butter
- 120 gr sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- grated zest of 1/2 lemon
- 25 gr sweet almond (approximately 25 pieces)
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them, remove the cores with a tin tube, and cut the apples into thin slices, which are layered with the sugar in a bowl and left to stand for approximately 1 hour
2. Cut the almond biscuits into pieces and soak them in the milk
3. Cream the butter and sugar until fluffy, add the beaten eggs and egg yolks along with the lemon zest, the blanched and ground almonds, and finally fold in the soaked, squeezed-out bread
4. Work the batter well and layer it alternately with the apple slices in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie dish - 3 layers of batter and 2 layers of apple slices
5. Bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 1 hour, or until the apples are soft and the batter tests dry with a whisk
6. Serve warm with whipped cream or vanilla sauce
