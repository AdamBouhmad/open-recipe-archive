## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Bring water with the vinegar and salt to a boil in a frying pan
2. One egg at a time, crack it gently into a coffee cup and let it slide slowly into the water, which should only simmer gently
3. When the white has set, remove the egg with a slotted spoon, shape it with a glass and place it on the serving dish
4. Poached eggs can be served as a main course on fried bread slices
