## Ingredients

- 2 1/2-3 liters half-grown field peas
- 1 liter water
- 1 tbsp. salt
- 1/2 tbsp. sugar (8 gr.)
- 120 gr. butter, for serving

## Directions

1. The pea pods are rinsed well and allowed to drain on cloth.
2. They are placed in boiling water, seasoned with salt and sugar, and allowed to simmer slowly with a tight lid until completely tender, which takes approximately 2 hours.
3. They are arranged with a slotted spoon on a folded napkin, covered with a napkin and served very hot with creamed butter (see recipe no. 405).
