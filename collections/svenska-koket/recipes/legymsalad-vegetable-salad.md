## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 celeriac
- 1 parsnip
- 2-3 small sugar beets
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 1 hg. canned Turkish beans
- 1 hg. canned asparagus
- 1 hg. canned fine peas
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- paprika
- 1/2 tsk. sugar
- 6 msk. fine cooking oil
- 3 msk. vinegar
- 1 lettuce head for garnish
- 2 msk. finely chopped parsley
- 2 tsk. finely chopped watercress or chervil

## Directions

1. Prepare the root vegetables (see page 338), cook and cut into fine strips with a vegetable knife.
2. Prepare and cook the cauliflower head in the usual way (see page 338).
3. Cut the beans diagonally into smaller pieces, and cut the asparagus in half.
4. The vegetables, which should be freshly cooked and preferably lukewarm, are arranged in groups on a round silver platter, garnished with lettuce leaves; place the cauliflower head in the middle.
5. Prepare the sauce as in recipe no. 448 and pour over the vegetables.
6. Sprinkle with a mixture of the parsley and watercress.
7. Serve the salad with roasted meat.
8. Alternatively, the vegetables may be mixed together, covered with the sauce and served in small shells cut from raw white cabbage leaves.
9. If desired, the vegetables can be mixed with real or cooked mayonnaise (see recipes no. 442 and 443), served in a crystal bowl and sprinkled with finely chopped truffle.
