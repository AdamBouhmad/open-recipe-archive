## Ingredients

- 5 kg. roast beef or double beef
- 2 tbsp. salt (30 gr.)
- water

## Directions

1. Roast beef should hang 1-2 weeks depending on the season and be dried daily with a dry towel.
2. Beat it on the skin side with a damp meat mallet, dry it with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and rub it with salt.
3. If no special roasting pan is available, place the meat with the fillet up on a rack in a long pan, which is placed in a warm oven.
4. The oven should not be too hot from the beginning, as the fat should partially melt before the roast browns.
5. Pour a little boiling water into the pan and gradually increase the heat.
6. When the roast is brown, baste it every 10 minutes with the gravy, which, when needed, is thinned with boiling water. (Note well! Baste the roast first and thin it afterwards, not the other way around.)
7. Roasting time is calculated at 20-25 minutes for each kg.
8. If the meat becomes too brown, place a sheet over it or cover it with buttered paper.
9. Place the roast beef on a warm platter and carve it at the table.
10. Strain the gravy, skim it well, boil it down, taste it and serve unsweetened.
