## Ingredients

- 350 gr. flour
- 300 gr. butter
- 100-125 gr. sugar
- 1 egg
- 25 gr. sweet almond

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl
2. Distribute the butter, which should be washed, in flakes in the flour
3. Add the sugar, the beaten egg, and the blanched and ground almond
4. Work the dough quickly with fingertips until it becomes even
5. Let it rest in a cool place for a couple of hours
6. Roll out for cake bottoms or use for simpler cakes and fruit pies
