## Ingredients

- 2 liters fresh apples (or 150 grams dried apples)
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 2 liters water
- 1.5 cups crushed sugar
- 0.5 lemon (peel and juice)
- 1.5-2 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the cores, and cut them into wedges. (To preserve their color, place the peeled apples in water with 1 tablespoon vinegar.)
2. Put them on in boiling water together with sugar, lemon peel, and lemon juice and let them cook until the apple wedges are soft.
3. Thicken the soup with the potato flour mixed in a little cold water, give it a good boil, taste, and serve with rusks.
4. If using dried apples, rinse them and soak overnight in the water in which they will cook. In this case, use more water.
