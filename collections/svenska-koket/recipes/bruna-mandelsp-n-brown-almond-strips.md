## Ingredients

- 100 grams sweet almonds
- 3 bitter almonds
- 1 egg
- 100 grams granulated sugar
- 3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, dry them and grate them finely
2. Stir one egg with the sugar until the mixture becomes foamy
3. Fold in the almonds and the egg whites beaten to soft peaks
4. Dry a flat baking sheet carefully and brush it with washed butter
5. Spread the batter thinly on it with a spatula and place the sheet in weak oven heat
6. When the batter has set and taken on a beautiful brown color, take the sheet out at the oven door
7. Cut out strips of approximately 1 decimeter square and roll them up diagonally while they are still really hot, otherwise they will break
