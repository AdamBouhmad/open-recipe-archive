## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- 100 grams sugar
- 1 deciliter French wine
- 0.5 deciliter water
- 6 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 0.5 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the lemon well and dry it. Grate the zest carefully and press out the juice.
2. In a well-scoured saucepan, blend the egg yolks, lemon zest, lemon juice, sugar, wine, and water and whisk over the fire until the cream is thick and foamy, then remove from heat and whisk until cold.
3. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks, pour the cream into a buttered, oven-safe mold, and bake in a water bath in the oven for approximately 30 minutes. Serve immediately from the mold.
4. If desired, place 1 tablespoon cranberries and 1 tablespoon applesauce in the bottom of the mold, alternating with 50 grams ladyfingers, pour the cream over, and bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for approximately 40 minutes.
