## Ingredients

- 1 lit. raw milk
- 1/2 lit. cream
- 2 msk. sugar (30 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- 15 almonds, 2 bitter (15 gr.) or 1 tsk. ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Whip the milk, cream, sugar, salt and the scalded, ground almonds or cinnamon together well
2. Test the mixture in a small pan over gentle heat; if too thick add a little more cream, otherwise add 1-2 beaten eggs
3. Pour into a buttered mold and bake in gentle oven heat or in a water bath, in the oven or on the stovetop
4. Serve with caramel sauce, fruit sauce or cream milk
