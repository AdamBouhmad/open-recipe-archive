## Ingredients

- 0.5 hectogram sugar
- 0.25 part boiling water
- 1 part white French wine

## Directions

1. Sugar is placed in a cold, well-cleaned frying pan and is placed over gentle heat to melt under constant stirring and acquire a beautiful light brown color.
2. The boiling water and wine are then added little by little, as much as needed so that the sauce becomes appropriately thin.
