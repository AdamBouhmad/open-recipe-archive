## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg veal breast (gödkalvbringa)
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 10 white peppercorns

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal breast (see page 232), break it well, place it in boiling water and skim thoroughly, then add the spices.
2. Let it simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until it is tender and the outer bones loosen, about 1 1/2–2 hours.
3. Remove it, take away all loosened bones, and cut the veal breast into beautiful, even pieces, which are placed on a plate and kept warm.
4. Skim the broth thoroughly and use it for sauce.
5. For veal breast, one can serve crayfish sauce, oyster sauce, light mushroom sauce, morel sauce, caper sauce, olive sauce, tomato sauce, or dill sauce along with boiled potatoes.
6. If the veal breast is served with crayfish sauce, it should be garnished with fresh crayfish tails and fresh asparagus, boiled in the meat broth.
