## Ingredients

- 60 gr butter
- 1 1/2 cups crushed rusks
- 18 anchovies
- 3 eggs
- 7-8 parts unskimmed milk
- sugar
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter for the molds
- 4 tbsp crushed rusks for the molds
- 2 eggs for garnish

## Directions

1. The butter is melted and mixed with the crushed rusks.
2. The anchovies are cleaned, boned, finely chopped and stirred in.
3. The eggs are whisked together with milk and a little sugar and folded in.
4. The batter is poured into buttered and bread-crumbed small pastry molds and baked in the oven 15-20 minutes.
5. The molds are turned out, the balls are placed on the serving platter, garnished with hard-cooked, chopped eggs and served with peas and melted butter.
