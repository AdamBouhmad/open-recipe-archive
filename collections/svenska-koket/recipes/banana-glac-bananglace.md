## Ingredients

- 1 litre pounded fruit flesh of bananas (approximately 1 1/2 kg unpeeled fruit)
- 6 dcl sugar syrup
- 1/4 dcl maraschino liqueur
- juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Blend all ingredients together and pass through fine sieve.
