## Ingredients

- 15 liters raspberries
- 5 liters red currants
- 2 liters water
- 0.5 kg sugar per kg juice

## Directions

1. Clean the raspberries, rinse the currants and remove the stems.
2. The berries are then put on with water and cooked with stirring until they are completely broken down.
3. The mass is poured into a strainer cloth and allowed to drip until the next day.
4. The juice is cooked with sugar with careful skimming until it is clear and slightly sticky. It is bottled warm or cold, the bottles are corked and sealed with resin.
