## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. sugar
- 3 dcl. cream
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks well with salt and sugar.
2. Add the cream and carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Heat the omelet pan and butter it well with the butter.
4. Pour in the egg mixture and cook the omelet light brown in ordinary oven heat.
5. Turn it out onto a buttered lid, place some stewed filling on one half and fold the other half over.
6. The stewing can also be placed on a round dish and the omelet turned over it. The omelet can be filled with stewing of various or mixed mushroom types, asparagus, sugar beets, calf sweetbread, lobster, crayfish and fish, etc.
