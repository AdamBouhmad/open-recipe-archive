## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal from the hindquarters (fransyska)
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 2 tbsp flour (20 g)
- 4 dcl thick cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung so that it is tender, is pounded on the skin side with a damp mallet, dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and rubbed with the seasonings.
2. The butter is browned in a roasting pan, which should not be larger than to just fit the meat snugly.
3. The roast is placed in and browned well on all sides, after which the lid is placed on and the roast is allowed to cook slowly without adding liquid, until it is tender, or about 3 hours. It is turned often and the lid must lie tight on the pan.
4. The roast is placed with the inner side up on a warm platter and is carved at the table.
5. Most of the fat is skimmed off the gravy in the pan, then the flour is mixed well with the meat gravy, the cream is added and the sauce is allowed to boil for 8 minutes.
6. It is seasoned with the seasonings and poured through a sauce strainer into a warm sauce bowl.
7. The roast is served with vegetables or potatoes and cucumber and salads.
