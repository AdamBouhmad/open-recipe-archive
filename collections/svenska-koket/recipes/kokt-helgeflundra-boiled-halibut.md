## Ingredients

- 1 kg halibut
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 5 tablespoons salt (75 g)
- 3 litres ice-cold water
- 1/2 lemon
- 10 g coarse salt per litre water for cooking

## Directions

1. Clean the fish piece, pour vinegar on the dark side and scrape well
2. Dissolve the salt in the water and let the fish piece soak in it for 1 hour
3. Remove it, wrap it in cloth, extract through an incision made at the head, scrape off the dark membrane carefully and cut off the fins
4. Rinse the fish very well, dry it, rub it with salt and let it rest 1 hour, then dry it again
5. Rub it with the lemon and cook with 10 g salt per litre of water for approximately 1/2 hour as in recipe no. 327
6. Let it drain, place on a warm platter, garnish with fluted lemon slices and parsley and serve with caper sauce or hollandaise and boiled potatoes
