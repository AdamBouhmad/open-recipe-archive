## Ingredients

- 0.75 liters sweet juice
- 1.5 liters water
- 1 lemon
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Mix juice and water.
2. Slice the lemon and add it.
3. To cool the soup, ice may be added.
4. Serve with rusks.
