## Ingredients

- 50 gr. veal roast
- 50 gr. salt beef tongue
- 50 gr. salt meat
- 2 hectograms cooked carrots
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 2 good apples
- 150 gr. canned haricots verts (green beans)
- Genuine mayonnaise: 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon salt, cayenne
- 1/4 teaspoon French mustard
- 1½ decilitres oil
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut the veal roast, tongue, salt meat, carrots, cucumber and apples into fine cubes.
2. Cut the beans into small square pieces.
3. Mix all the ingredients thereafter in the mayonnaise, which is prepared as in no. 10.
4. Fill the salad into hollowed out tomatoes (see no. 731) and serve with roast beef and ham, or serve it as a lunch dish.
