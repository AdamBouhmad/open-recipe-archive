## Ingredients

- 30 brussels sprout heads
- 1 hg. lean pork
- 1 small red onion
- 1 msk. butter
- 2 lit. broth
- 3 msk. flour
- salt, white pepper
- (sugar)

## Directions

1. Rinse the cabbage and blanch it for approximately 10 minutes, remove and let it drain.
2. Cut the pork into slices, peel and chop the onion.
3. Fry the onion and pork slices in the butter over low heat for approximately 10 minutes.
4. Chop the cabbage finely together with the flour and add it together with the pork to the boiling broth, then let the soup cook 20-30 minutes.
5. Strain it, skim it and season it with the spices and serve with fried bread or quenelles.
