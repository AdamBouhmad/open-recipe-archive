## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 kg lye fish
- 1/2 liter water
- 5 tablespoons salt (75 gr)
- ground allspice pepper
- milk sauce
- clarified butter
- peas (Russian or green)
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Well-drained fish is cut into large pieces and the meat is separated from the skin
2. Scrape the skin well, dry it and use for clear skin
3. Layer the fish with salt in a fish cloth, which is then tied shut
4. Pour water into a round pot, place an inverted small plate or saucer in it
5. Bring water to a boil, place the tied fish bundle on the plate and let the fish simmer slowly for 15-20 minutes or until properly tender
6. Place the bundle in a colander, let the fish drain off and transfer to a serving platter
7. Serve with ground allspice pepper, milk sauce and clarified butter, as well as peas (Russian or green) and potatoes
