## Ingredients

- Ingredients from previous recipe
- Purée of strawberries or raspberries
- Maraschino liqueur
- Cocoa

## Directions

1. Prepare the cream as in the previous recipe and divide into three equal parts.
2. Add a little purée or juice of strawberries or raspberries to one part, pour into a clean tin mold rinsed with cold water and sprinkled with sugar, and let it set on ice.
3. Add maraschino to the second part and pour into the mold when the pink cream is set.
4. Add cocoa to the third part and pour last into the mold.
5. Let the cream set on ice for 2-3 hours, unmold, and garnish with lady fingers.
