## Ingredients

- 1 liter blood
- 6 parts weak beer
- 2 kg coarse rye flour
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 100 gr pressed yeast
- 8 tablespoons melted lard
- ½ teaspoon ground white pepper
- ½ teaspoon grated ginger
- 1 teaspoon finely ground marjoram
- 1 cup syrup

## Directions

1. Strain the blood and whisk well together with the beer
2. Add flour, salt and dissolved yeast and work the dough well and set to rise
3. When the dough has risen to double its size, add the lard, spices and syrup, and let the dough rise once more
4. Shape into oblong loaves or round cakes
5. In the latter case, cut a pattern on top
6. Set the loaves to rise again and bake in good oven heat
7. When the oblong loaves have cooled, cut them into slices
8. Dry the slices well in an oven
9. Hang the cakes on sticks in the ceiling to dry
