## Ingredients

- 300 grams large, beautiful raspberries or strawberries
- sugar, to taste
- 1/2 litre extra thick cream

## Directions

1. The berries, which should be freshly picked, are passed through a fine sieve.
2. Sugar to taste is added.
3. The cream is whipped until stiff peaks form and folded carefully into the fruit purée.
4. The mixture is piled high in a compote bowl and served at once with almond shavings, meringues or macaroons.
