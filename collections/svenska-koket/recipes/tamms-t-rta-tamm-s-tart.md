## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 300 gr. caster sugar
- 150 gr. sweet almond
- 6 bitter almonds
- ½ lemon
- 75 gr. potato flour
- ½ tbsp. washed butter
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks from the whites and stir together with the sugar. During stirring, add 1 tbsp. water twice.
2. Blanch the almond, dry it well in the oven and grate it or pass it through an almond mill and mix it with the grated lemon zest and potato flour.
3. Stir the dry mixture and lemon juice into the egg mixture, and finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Immediately pour the batter into a buttered and bread-crumb-coated mold or cake pan and bake about 1 hour in not too strong oven heat.
5. When the tart has cooled, cut it into two bottoms, and between these place vanilla cream or applesauce.
6. Glaze and garnish the tart with fruit and candied fruit.
