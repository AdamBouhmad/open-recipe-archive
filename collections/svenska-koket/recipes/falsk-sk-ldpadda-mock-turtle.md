## Ingredients

- Veal hash: 3 hectograms veal roast (leftovers), 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g), 4 tablespoons flour (40 g), 5 deciliters bouillon, 1 tablespoon white wine, salt, white pepper
- Omelet: 6 eggs, 1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon sugar, 2 teaspoons flour, 6 deciliters milk
- For the omelet pan: 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)

## Directions

1. Cut the veal roast into fine dice.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, thin with the bouillon and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add the meat and warm it in the sauce, but do not let it boil; season the stew with the wine and spices.
4. For the omelet, whisk the eggs well with the salt, sugar, flour and milk.
5. Heat the omelet pan and butter it well with the butter.
6. Fry 2 omelets from the egg mixture, which are done when they have a light brown color.
7. Turn the first omelet out onto a round plate, spread the meat stew on it, turn the second omelet out onto a buttered dish and place it on top of the stew.
8. Serve the mock turtle with clarified butter as a breakfast dish.
