## Ingredients

- 120 gr. fillet of sole or whiting
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon boiling water
- 2 tablespoons cold béchamel sauce
- 1 1/2 deciliters thick cream
- salt, white pepper
- 1 egg
- 3 egg yolks
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. The fillets are rinsed, wrapped in cloth, rubbed with salt and cut into dice.
2. The butter is melted in a pot, the fish dice are placed in, the water is added, they are allowed to cook slowly with a lid for 8-10 minutes, after which they are drained and allowed to cool.
3. The fish is pounded in a stone mortar to a fine mass, the sauce is added and the mass is pounded again.
4. The royal is then prepared as in the preceding recipe.
5. It is baked and served as in recipe 306.
