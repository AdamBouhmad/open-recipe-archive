## Ingredients

- 1 liter dried beans
- 2 carrots
- 2 liters water
- salt
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 3 cups milk
- salt
- sugar
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Sprout the beans and remove the sprouts.
2. Place in boiling, salted water, and when cooked, add the rinsed, scraped carrots cut into thin slices.
3. When everything is cooked, pour through a colander to drain.
4. Fry the butter and flour together, add 2 cups of the bean broth and the milk, and let the sauce boil for 10 minutes.
5. Add the beans and carrots, add the chopped parsley, and season the stew.
