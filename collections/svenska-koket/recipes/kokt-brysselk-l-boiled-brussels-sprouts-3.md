## Ingredients

- 1 litre brussels sprouts
- 3/4 litre water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons melted butter (40 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the sprouts, removing all yellow and wilted leaves.
2. Rinse very thoroughly.
3. Place in boiling salted water and cook 15-20 minutes, or until tender.
4. Drain in a colander and serve as a garnish around roasted meat (especially veal) or together with other vegetables.
5. When the sprouts are plated, pour the melted butter over them.
6. The sprouts may also be cooked in broth, browned nicely in butter and sugar, garnished with glazed chestnuts (see recipe 737) and served as previously described or as a side dish.
