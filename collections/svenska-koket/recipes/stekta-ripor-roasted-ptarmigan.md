## Ingredients

- 3 ptarmigan
- 1 hg. lard, in 3 slices
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tsp. salt
- 3 dcl. broth
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Ptarmigan are treated and prepared as red partridge, but should roast approximately 1½ hours to become tender.
2. They can also be roasted as pheasants in a previously prepared sauce (see no. 614) and this is added with a little thick cream and cold butter before it is served.
3. The ptarmigan are split in half, garnished with browned potatoes and salad or parsley.
4. Serve with currant jelly and green salad with lemon.
