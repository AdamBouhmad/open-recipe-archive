## Ingredients

- 4 1/2 kg veal roast
- 3 hg lard
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 gr)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr)
- 2 liters broth or water

## Directions

1. Use the shank with the french cut
2. Saw off the leg and cook to broth for basting the roast. The meat from it can be used for herring salad or gratin
3. The meat, which should have hung 3–10 days depending on season, is well tenderized, dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and all sinewy membranes are trimmed away
4. Lard the roast on the outer thigh and rub with a mixture of salt and pepper
5. Heat a roasting pan, melt the butter in it, place the roast with the inner thigh up, put in a hot oven to brown, basting frequently with the butter
6. When browned, add the boiling broth, but no more than about 1/2 cm high in the pan; roast until tender, about 3 hours, basting every 10 minutes and adding more broth as needed
7. If the roast becomes too brown, cover with a buttered paper
8. Serve with reduced sauce and mixed vegetables, small potatoes, cucumbers, and salads
