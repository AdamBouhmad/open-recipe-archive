## Ingredients

- 1 large rutabaga
- 3/4 liter beef broth or pork stock
- 1 liter potatoes
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 1 dcl. cream
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 2 tsp. sugar
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper

## Directions

1. The rutabaga is peeled (see page 338), cut into cubes, which are placed in the cold broth, and are allowed to boil until half soft or about 15 minutes.
2. The potato is peeled, cut into cubes and placed on top of the rutabaga, whereupon the root vegetables are allowed to boil until they are soft.
3. The excess stock is then drained off, and the roots are mashed with a fork.
4. The cold butter is added at once together with the boiled cream, while the mash is worked well with a fork.
5. It is seasoned carefully with spices and is served with salted meat, pork or sausage.
