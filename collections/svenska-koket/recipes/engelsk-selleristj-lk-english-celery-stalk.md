## Ingredients

- celery stalks
- butter
- fine cheese

## Directions

1. Remove the outermost, coarse, green leaves from the celery stalks, but leave a little of the root.
2. Rinse them well, let them drain and serve them standing in a tall glass with water, at lunch or dinner with butter and some fine cheese.
