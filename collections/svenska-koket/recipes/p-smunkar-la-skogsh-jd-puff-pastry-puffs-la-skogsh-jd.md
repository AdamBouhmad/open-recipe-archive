## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 dcl thin cream
- 1 1/2 msk sugar (23 gr)
- 6 bitter almonds
- 10 sweet almonds
- 3 1/2 msk butter (70 gr)
- 110 gr flour
- 3-4 eggs
- 1 kg shortening for cooking

## Directions

1. Butter a pot with a round bottom with 1/2 msk of the butter, pour in the cream, and add the sugar and the blanched and grated almond.
2. Let the cream boil up, add the rest of the butter, and whisk in the flour with a steel whisk.
3. Let the batter then cook until it releases from the pan.
4. Remove the pot from heat, work in the eggs one at a time, and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
5. Form the mixture with two tablespoons into round balls the size of a small crust, which are cooked a beautiful golden brown in shortening.
6. The puffs should be formed as smooth as possible, and no more than 3 are cooked at a time.
7. The shortening should not be too hot, as the puffs will not then be able to swell.
8. Turn them with a slotted spoon, and when the puffs have a golden brown color on both sides, remove them with a slotted spoon and place them on absorbent paper on a warm sheet in the oven.
9. Sprinkle with sugar and serve warm with jam as a dessert, or with coffee or tea.
