## Ingredients

- 10 liters apples
- half the weight of sugar against the peeled apples
- 3 parts water
- juice and zest of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into quarters and remove the cores.
2. Place them on heat with the water, sugar and lemon juice.
3. Allow to cook under constant stirring until the apples fall apart and form a pulp.
4. Pour it hot into the crock and cover, once it has cooled, with a thin layer.
