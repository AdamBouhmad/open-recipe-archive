## Ingredients

- 1.3 liters water
- 4 deciliters ground rye flour (2 hectograms)
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 grams)

## Directions

1. Boil the water with salt, whisk in the flour vigorously at full boil, and let the porridge cook slowly with a lid for about 1 hour. Season with more salt if desired, and serve with milk.
