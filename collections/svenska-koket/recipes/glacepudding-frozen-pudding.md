## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. thin cream
- 1 vanilla pod
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 hg. sugar
- 50 gr. biscuits
- 10 cloudberries or 6 pears
- 5 dcl. extra thick cream

## Directions

1. Heat the thin cream with the vanilla pod and let it stand covered for 20 minutes to infuse.
2. Whisk the egg yolks with the sugar in a saucepan for 15 minutes.
3. Add the cream while whisking and let the mixture simmer over heat while continuing to whisk until it is thick and creamy.
4. Pour the cream and whisk until it is cold.
5. Crush the biscuits finely; let the fruit drain, cut into pieces and remove the seeds.
6. Whisk the cream to stiff peaks and carefully fold into the cream mixture.
7. In a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted ice mold, layer the cream, crushed biscuits, and fruit alternately. The first and last layer should be cream.
8. Freeze the pudding as described in recipe no. 929.
