## Ingredients

- 3—4 capercaillies
- 1 hg. lard, in 3—4 slices
- 3—4 tbsp. butter (60—80 gr.)
- 2 tsk. salt
- 3 dcl. bouillon
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. The meat of capercaillies is white and beautiful with a particularly strong pronounced taste of game.
2. They are treated and roasted as partridges.
3. Capercaillies can also be roasted as pheasants (see no. 614).
4. They are split lengthwise, but only the breasts are served.
