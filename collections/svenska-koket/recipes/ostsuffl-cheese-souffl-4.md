## Ingredients

- 80 gr butter
- 80 gr flour
- 1/2 liter boiling milk
- 5-6 eggs
- 60 gr grated cheese
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/4 tsp mustard (optional)
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes. Pour it into a bowl; when it has cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time, and stir the mixture for 10 minutes. Mix in the grated cheese together with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks. Taste the mixture, pour it into a buttered and bread-crumb-covered pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 45 minutes.
