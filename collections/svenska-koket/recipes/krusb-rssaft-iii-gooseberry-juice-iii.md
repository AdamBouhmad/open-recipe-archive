## Ingredients

- 10 liters ripe gooseberries
- 5 liters water
- 0.5 kg sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Rinse and top the gooseberries. Crush them and place them in a crock. Boil the water and pour it over the berries. Cover the crock with a towel and a lid.
2. The next day strain off the juice. Boil it and pour it back over the berries. Repeat this for three days in a row.
3. On the fourth day cook the juice with the sugar. Let it cook with skimming until it is clear and glossy, after which bottle it. Cork the bottles and seal with resin.
