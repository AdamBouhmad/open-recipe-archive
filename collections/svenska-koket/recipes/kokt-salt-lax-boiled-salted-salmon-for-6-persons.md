## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. salted salmon
- water
- 2 bay leaves
- a few allspice berries

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces and soak out the salt.
2. Place it on the fire in cold water together with the spices and let it simmer slowly until cooked.
3. Take the fish up immediately and serve with currant sauce.
