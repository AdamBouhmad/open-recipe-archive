## Ingredients

- 3 small cups flour
- 6 cups sour milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- raspberry jam
- 3 parts thick cream
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 kg sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Sift the flour in a bowl, whisk the eggs with the milk and stir into the flour; lastly add the clarified but cooled butter, then let the batter stand and swell for 1 hour.
2. Heat the pancake griddle slowly and brush with clarified butter.
3. Pour in just enough batter to cover the pan bottom.
4. Bake the pancakes on one side only, place on a warm plate with the light side up and brush thinly with warm jam.
5. When all pancakes are baked, pour the cream over the whole, mixed with the sugar and whisked to stiff peaks.
6. Finally sprinkle over the blanched and finely chopped sweet almonds.
