## Ingredients

- 500 g boneless veal bottom round
- 200 g cooked cold potatoes
- 4 tsp finely chopped parsley
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 dcl heavy cream
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp milk
- 2 dcl crushed rusks
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 2 dcl stock

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat, cut into pieces and grind 4 times through a meat grinder, the last time together with the potatoes.
2. Mix the parsley, spices and cream into the meat mixture, which is stirred well and formed into 15 oblong cutlets.
3. Even out the cutlets with a knife, brush with the egg whisked with milk, and turn in the crushed rusks.
4. Brown the butter in a frying pan, add the cutlets, brown them quickly in the butter and place in a pot.
5. Whisk out the frying pan with the boiling stock, pour the sauce over the cutlets, which then cook with a tightly closed lid for about 10 minutes.
6. Place on a warm serving dish, whisk up the sauce and pour over the cutlets, which are garnished with parsley and served with green peas, spinach or mashed potatoes.
