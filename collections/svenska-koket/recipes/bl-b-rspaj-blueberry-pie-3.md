## Ingredients

- 225 gr. flour
- 1 hg. butter
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)
- 20 gr. sweet almonds
- 1 dcl. water
- 1 egg yolk
- 3/4 liter blueberries
- 1 1/2 dcl. granulated sugar (128 gr.)
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl; distribute the cold butter in flakes into the flour and work it in quickly with fingertips until the mixture is even and light yellow in color.
2. Add the sugar, the blanched and ground almonds, the water and the egg yolk quickly, and knead the dough a couple of times, roll it into a ball, place it immediately in a cloth and let it rest in a cool place for 1 hour.
3. Grease a pan or baking dish with the butter mixed with flour, and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
4. Clean the blueberries, rinse them, let them drain, sprinkle with sugar and mix well by shaking.
5. Roll out three-quarters of the dough and use it to line a greased sheet pan or baking dish. Add the blueberries and fold the edge of the dough over.
6. Roll out the remaining dough into a lid and cover the pie with it.
7. Bake in a good oven temperature for about 1 hour, or until the dough tests done with a whisk.
8. Turn the pie out and serve warm or cold with whipped cream.
