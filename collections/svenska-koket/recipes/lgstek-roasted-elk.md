## Ingredients

- 5 kg. elk roast
- 2 tbsp. salt
- 1 tsp. white pepper
- 3 liters water
- 3 tbsp. vinegar
- 2 hg. lard
- 3 tbsp. butter
- 2–2½ liters broth or water
- 2½ tbsp. butter for sauce
- 5 tbsp. flour
- meat stock
- 2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. The meat should be chosen from young animals. Rub it well with the seasonings and let it lie 2–3 days in water, diluted with vinegar. Remove it and dry well with a cloth. Lard the roast thereafter with the lard cut into fine strips, brown it in the butter, season further if desired, and dilute with the boiling broth or water. Let the roast roast slowly until done, for which approximately 3–4 hours are required, while it is frequently diluted.
2. Strain and skim the meat stock, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
