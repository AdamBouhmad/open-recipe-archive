## Ingredients

- 1½ kg reindeer steak
- 2 liters weak drink (marinade)
- ½ teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2½ tablespoons butter
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 2 parts broth
- 1½ parts cream

## Directions

1. Place the meat in drink overnight and then wrap it well in a cloth.
2. Cut it into approximately 3 cm thick slices, which are pounded on both sides and seasoned with pepper and salt.
3. Heat a pan. Brown the butter in it, and place the steaks in to brown quickly about 2 minutes on each side.
4. Deglaze the pan with the boiling broth and cream, and pour the sauce over the steaks.
5. Finally brown the peeled onion cut into slices, and garnish the steaks with it.
