## Ingredients

- 1 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 10 grams salt
- 3-4 truffles
- For the white sauce: 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1 deciliter Jerusalem artichoke broth
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- For the shells: 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions
