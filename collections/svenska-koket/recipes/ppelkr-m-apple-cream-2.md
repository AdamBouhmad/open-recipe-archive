## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters apples
- 1 liter water
- 1 3/4 cups crushed sugar
- lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, remove the cores and cut them into wedges.
2. As they are peeled, place them in water mixed with 1 tablespoon vinegar to preserve their color.
3. Place them in boiling water together with the sugar and let them cook until the apple wedges are soft.
4. Thicken the cream with potato flour mixed in a little cold water, bring to a good boil, taste and pour into a jelly mold or carrot to cool.
