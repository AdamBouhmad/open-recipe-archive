## Ingredients

- 210 gr. butter
- 8 dessert spoons granulated sugar
- 2 eggs
- 6 heaping tbsp. wheat flour
- 1 tsp. washed butter
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks
- 1.5 kg. sweet almond
- 10 bitter almonds
- 1 liter granulated sugar
- 0.5 tsp. grated lemon peel
- 1 egg white
- 1.5 liter fresh apples
- 2 kg. dried apricots
- 3 kg. prunes
- 6 kg. sugar
- 3 parts water
- 20 gr. pistachio almond

## Directions

1. Make a regular shortcrust pastry from butter, sugar, eggs and flour.
2. Grease a form or baking pan with butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
3. Roll out the dough to a round cake, place in the form and bake golden brown in good oven heat, turn out and let cool.
4. Blanch the almond, dry and grate or run through an almond mill and pound with the sugar and lemon peel in a mortar.
5. Mix in the beaten egg white and pound until fine and cohesive.
6. Soak the apricots in water the day before.
7. Make a clear syrup from the sugar and water.
8. Cook the peeled apples cut into wedges in this syrup until clear.
9. Remove the apples and cook the apricots and prunes in the syrup until soft.
10. Blanch the pistachio almond and chop finely.
11. Form a ring of almond mass on the outer edge of the tart.
12. Inside this ring garnish the tart beautifully with the cooked fruit and sprinkle pistachio almond over.
13. Serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
