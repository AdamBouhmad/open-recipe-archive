## Ingredients

- 150 gr butter
- 150 gr flour
- 1 liter unskimmed milk
- 8 eggs
- salt, sugar
- white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are combined, milk is added, and the mixture is brought to a boil until it leaves the sides of the pan.
2. When the batter has cooled somewhat, egg yolks are stirred in one at a time, and the forcemeat is well seasoned with spices.
3. Last, egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in, the batter is poured into a buttered and bread-crumbed mold and baked in a water bath in the oven or on top of the stove 40-50 minutes.
4. Served with crayfish or mushroom sauce.
