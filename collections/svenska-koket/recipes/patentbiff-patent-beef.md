## Ingredients

- 6 hg. beef tenderloin or boneless sirloin
- 3 hg. smoked ham
- 6 eggs
- 2 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.) for frying
- 1 dcl. boiling broth
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the eggs

## Directions

1. The meat is dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and cut crosswise across the muscle fibers into approximately 1 cm. thick slices, which are pounded on both sides, peppered and salted. From the smoked ham, thin slices are cut.
2. Heat a frying pan slowly, brown the butter in it, add the steaks and let them fry for approximately 1 minute on each side, after which they are placed on a warm serving dish.
3. Whisk out the pan with the boiling broth and pour the sauce over the steaks. Heat the pan again and break the ham slices, place one slice on each steak.
4. Meanwhile fry the eggs in a pancake pan and place one egg on each steak.
5. Serve with newly boiled or fried potatoes as a breakfast course.
