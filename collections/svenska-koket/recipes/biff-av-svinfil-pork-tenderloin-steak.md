## Ingredients

- 6 hg. pork tenderloin
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1/2 msk. salt
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.) for frying
- 2 dcl. broth
- 1 large Portuguese onion for garnish
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.) for garnish
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the filets, dry with a cloth, wring out in hot water.
2. Cut them crosswise into 1 cm thick slices, which are lightly beaten on both sides and seasoned.
3. Heat a frying pan slowly, brown the butter in it, lay in the steaks and fry quickly on both sides and place on a warm serving dish.
4. Whisk out the pan with the boiling broth and pour the sauce over the steaks.
5. Peel the onion, cut it into slices and brown in the butter, and garnish the steaks with it together with the parsley.
6. Serve with potatoes or peas as a breakfast dish.
