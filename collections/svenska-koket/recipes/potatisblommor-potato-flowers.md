## Ingredients

- 1 kg peeled potatoes, approximately 1 1/2 liters unpeeled
- 30 g butter
- 1-2 tablespoons thick cream
- salt
- white pepper
- a little nutmeg
- 3 egg yolks
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Rinse the potatoes, brush them, peel thinly, cut into pieces and cook quickly in salted water until soft but not falling apart.
2. Drain them immediately in a colander and let stand in the front part of the oven for a few minutes to dry, then press through a fine sieve.
3. Place the puree in a saucepan, add the butter, cream and seasonings and stir the puree with a fork over the heat until it becomes firm.
4. Remove from the heat and add the egg yolks one at a time while stirring vigorously.
5. If desired, season the mash with sugar.
6. Let the puree cool and then press it through a pastry bag with a large star nozzle onto a well-buttered baking sheet in the shape of flowers.
7. Brush these with beaten egg and bake in a warm oven until they have a beautiful golden yellow color.
8. Serve as a garnish around roasted meat.
