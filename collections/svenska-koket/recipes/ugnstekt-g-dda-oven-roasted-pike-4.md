## Ingredients

- 2 kg pike
- 1½ tbsp salt
- 1 egg
- ½ cup crushed hardtack
- 2½ tbsp butter or lard
- ½ liter boiling water or broth

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, clean and rinse it well.
2. Even out the fins and dry the fish with a cloth.
3. Rub it with salt, brush with the beaten egg and sprinkle with the crushed hardtack.
4. Heat the butter or lard in a roasting pan; place the fish in it and brown well.
5. Add the boiling water and let the fish roast until done, basting it occasionally with its sauce.
