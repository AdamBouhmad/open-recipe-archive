## Ingredients

- 750 grams fresh or 3 hectograms dried apples
- 2 liters water
- Zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- 1.5 to 2 cups crushed sugar
- 25 grams rice or potato flour
- 1/2 deciliter French wine or sherry

## Directions

1. The apples for this should be tart and not fully ripe. Rinse and dry them, do not peel, cut into 6 quarters; remove the blossom, stem, and core
2. Place the apples in cold water together with the lemon zest and cinnamon and let them cook until they fall apart, then pass them through a coarse sieve
3. Let the fruit purée come to a boil with the sugar and thicken with the rice flour, stirred in 1/2 cup cold water, after which it is allowed to cook while stirring for 5 minutes
4. Flavor the soup with the wine and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and rusks or toasted bread
5. If using dried apples, rinse them very well in lukewarm water and let them soak overnight in the water in which they are to be cooked
