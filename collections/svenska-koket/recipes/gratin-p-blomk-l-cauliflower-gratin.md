## Ingredients

- 3 medium-sized cauliflower heads
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup grated Swiss cheese
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower, place it in vinegar-mixed water and blanch it in boiling salted water until almost tender, then remove it and let it drain.
2. Place it on a fireproof dish, pour the melted butter over it and sprinkle with the grated cheese and finally with the crushed rusks.
3. Place the cauliflower in the oven to get a nice golden brown color, remove it and serve immediately.
