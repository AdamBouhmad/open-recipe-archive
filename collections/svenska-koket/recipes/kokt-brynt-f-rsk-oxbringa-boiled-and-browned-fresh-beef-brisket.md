## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg beef brisket (front brisket)
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- water
- 6 allspice berries
- 1 piece of celery
- 1 large carrot

## Directions

1. Wash the brisket with a cloth dipped in hot water, rub in the salt, and let sit until the next day
2. Break the meat well and place in enough boiling water to cover it well
3. When the water comes to a boil, remove all floating foam, add spices and vegetables, and let the brisket simmer slowly until completely tender (2-3 hours)
4. Remove, place in a roasting pan with the broth on the bottom, and put in the oven to brown
5. Cut the brisket into nice pieces and garnish with sliced carrot
6. Strain the sauce and skim it
