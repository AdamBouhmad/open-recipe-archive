## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter brown beans
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 3-4 tablespoons syrup
- 3-4 tablespoons vinegar
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the beans
2. Place in cold water and let simmer slowly until completely tender, which takes approximately 3 hours
3. During cooking, add warm water as needed if necessary
4. Season the beans with syrup, vinegar, and a little salt if desired
5. Serve with braised ham, fried pork, meatballs, etc. The beans cook more easily if soaked in water the evening before, but will then have a grey color
