## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg boneless veal
- 2-3 cups cream milk
- 1 tablespoon potato flour
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg (for breading)
- 1.5 cups crushed rusks (for breading)
- 3 tablespoons butter (for frying)
- 2 cups bouillon

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it into cubes and pass it three times through a meat grinder.
2. Mix the forcemeat with the cream milk, potato flour and spices.
3. Form fairly long round cutlets from the forcemeat, brush them with egg and sprinkle with finely crushed rusks.
4. Heat the butter in a frying pan, lay the cutlets in it and brown them on both sides.
5. When all the cutlets are browned, pour the boiling bouillon over them and let them simmer slowly for 10-15 minutes with a tightly fitting lid.
