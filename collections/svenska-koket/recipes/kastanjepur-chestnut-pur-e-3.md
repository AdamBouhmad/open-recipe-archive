## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg chestnuts
- 4-5 deciliters bouillon
- 1 small celery stalk
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 2-3 tablespoons thick cream
- salt
- a little white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut a cross 1/2 cm deep into the pointed end of each chestnut
2. Place in boiling water and let boil for 2 minutes so the shells loosen
3. Remove both outer and inner shells while the chestnuts are still warm; otherwise the shells stick easily
4. Place the chestnuts together with the celery stalk and sugar in the cold bouillon and let cook until completely tender
5. Pass through a fine sieve
6. Mix the purée with butter and cream, heat it through and season with the spices
7. Serve preferably in pastry shells
