## Ingredients

- 1 packet Moir's jelly powder (approximately 120 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. sugar (45 gr.)
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 4 dcl. boiling water
- 3 dcl. Rhine wine or sherry

## Directions

1. Stir the jelly powder, sugar, lemon juice and water in a bowl until the powder and sugar are dissolved, then add the wine and pour the mixture into a large or several small molds rinsed with water and sprinkled with sugar and let it set on ice for 4-5 hours.
2. This jelly can advantageously be colored red and used as garnish for cold rice pudding. A light, fine sponge cake can be soaked through with this jelly, which should then be heated to approximately 60 degrees. The jelly then sets. The cake is served whole, or cut into slices, with whipped cream.
