## Ingredients

- 1 kg. hallon (raspberries)
- 1 kg. blåbär (blueberries)
- 1 kg. toppsocker (loaf sugar)
- 2½—3 del. vatten (water)

## Directions

1. Beredning: lika med föregående recept. (Preparation: the same as the previous recipe.)
2. Vackrast blir sylten om var sort syltas för sig och blandas kall. (The preserve will look most beautiful if each variety is preserved separately and mixed when cold.)
