## Ingredients

- 3 pigeons
- 1 1/2 hg. lard
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 liter broth, water or cream

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the pigeons and clean them as other poultry. Lard the breasts or cover them with lard and tie them. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, add the pigeons, brown them nicely together with the giblets, salt them, moisten with broth, water mixed with meat extract or cream, and let them roast until tender, or 40-50 minutes, during which they are basted about every 10 minutes. Cut and garnish as chicken. Serve with thickened sauce and salad and red currant jelly.
