## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 liter milk
- approximately 150 gr. flour
- 350 gr. lightly salted side of pork

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks with a steel whisk; add half of the milk, whisk in the flour (which should be sifted and well dried), and add the remaining milk.
2. Let the batter rest for 2 hours to rise, then fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Cut the pork into small cubes, fry them, and remove them from the fat.
4. Pour a little of the pork fat into another frying pan, whisk up the batter, pour it into the pan, and sprinkle some of the pork cubes over it.
5. Bake in a warm oven until beautifully golden-brown, approximately 20 minutes.
6. Serve with sugar. This recipe yields three large pancakes.
