## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg pork loin (with bone)
- 1-2 lemons
- 1/2 cup prunes
- 3 parts water
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the loin with a cloth dipped in hot water and rub it with the lemon until it has released all its juice.
2. Rinse the prunes and parboil them (save the liquid for basting during roasting), pit them and cut into four parts; lard the loin with these in tight rows.
3. Place it in good oven heat to brown in the butter, salt it and add the warm prune liquid, then let it cook slowly for about 1 hour.
4. Add the hot broth during roasting and baste the loin every tenth minute.
5. Serve with its own sauce, which is strained and skimmed thoroughly.
6. If the sauce is to be thickened, prepare it according to general sauce rules and a little cream may be added.
