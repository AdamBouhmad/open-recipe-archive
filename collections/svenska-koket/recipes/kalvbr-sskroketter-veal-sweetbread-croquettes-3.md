## Ingredients

- 3 hg. veal sweetbread
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 2 1/2 tbsp. butter (50 g)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 g)
- 1 dcl. sweetbread stock
- 1 dcl. mushroom stock
- 2 dcl. cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 sheets gelatin
- salt, white pepper
- For breading: 4 tbsp. flour
- 2 eggs
- salt
- 2 tsp. table oil
- 1 cup finely crushed rusks
- For frying: 1 kg. cream

## Directions

1. Soak the veal sweetbread in water for 1 hour to draw out the blood.
2. Blanch it, place in boiling water with salt added, and let it boil for 15 minutes, then lay it on a sieve to drain; save the stock.
3. Cut the sweetbread and mushrooms into cubes and fry in half of the butter until the butter is clear, then remove.
4. Fry the remaining butter with the flour for 2 minutes, add the sweetbread stock, mushroom stock and cream, and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
5. Stir in the sweetbread and mushrooms together with the whisked egg yolks and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in 1 tbsp. hot water.
6. Season the mixture with spices and transfer to a flat buttered dish, then brush butter over the top so it does not harden.
7. When the mixture is cold, form portions of about 60 grams, place them on a floured board.
8. Roll the croquettes in flour and form them into oblong shapes.
9. Dip them in the stiffly whisked eggs mixed with table oil and seasoned with spices.
10. Roll them in the finely crushed rusks and let them harden for 1 hour.
11. Fry them golden brown in hot cream and place on blotting paper to drain.
12. Serve as a course with tomato or mushroom sauce.
