## Ingredients

- Leftover roast (3½ hectograms)
- leftover roast sauce
- 2 pickled cucumbers
- 1½ liters cooked potatoes
- salt
- sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the roast into slices and place them in an ovenproof mold with a few thin slices of pickled cucumber between each layer until the mold is nearly full, then pour the heated sauce over it.
2. Mash the potatoes and season with salt and sugar.
3. Beat the egg whites until stiff and fold them carefully into the purée.
4. Spread this as a lid on the mold, sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs and crimp neatly.
5. Put the pudding in the oven to be heated through and get a nice golden brown color.
6. Instead of roast sauce, you can use stock or meat extract dissolved in boiling water.
