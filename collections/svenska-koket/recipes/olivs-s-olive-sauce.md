## Ingredients

- 9 preserved olives
- 6 dcl good meat stock or broth
- 2 tbsp butter (40 gr)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr)
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Peel the olives in even rings from the pits.
2. Simmer the pits in the meat stock for 10 minutes so the sauce will have a stronger olive flavor.
3. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes.
4. Strain the meat stock from the pits and add little by little.
5. Add the olives and let the sauce cook, stirring, for 10 minutes.
6. Season with salt, sugar, and white pepper.
7. If desired, thicken with the beaten egg yolks and cream.
8. Serve with boiled veal or lamb brisket.
