## Ingredients

- 6 hg. beef tenderloin
- 3 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.) for parsley butter

## Directions

1. Dry the tenderloin with a cloth wrung out in hot water. Carefully remove all outer sinews and membranes, then cut the tenderloin into 6 slices, which are lightly pounded with a damp mallet and rubbed on both sides with the spice mixture. Clarify 1 tbsp. of the butter and brush the steaks with it.
