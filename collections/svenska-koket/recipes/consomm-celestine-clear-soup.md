## Ingredients

- 40 gr. tapioca
- 1½ cup water
- 2½ liters clear consommé
- salt
- cayenne
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped chives
- stuffed pancakes

## Directions

1. Let the tapioca grains cook in the water until they are clear, then strain through muslin and pour the liquid into the consommé, which is then brought to a boil and seasoned with spices.
2. Cut the stuffed pancakes (see no. 320) with a round measure into cakes (approximately 2 cm in diameter), which are placed in the soup bowl together with the chives, and the hot consommé is poured over.
