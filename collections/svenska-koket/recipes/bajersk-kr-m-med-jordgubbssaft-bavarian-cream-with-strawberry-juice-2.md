## Ingredients

- 6 sheets gelatin
- 1/2 deciliter boiling water
- 50 grams granulated sugar
- 4 deciliters strawberry juice
- 3 deciliters thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in 1/2 deciliter boiling water together with the sugar, after which add the strawberry juice.
2. When the mixture is completely cold and begins to set, fold in the cream whipped to stiff foam.
3. Rinse a nice tin mold with cold water and dust it with granulated sugar, pour in the mixture, and let the mold stand on ice for 3-4 hours.
4. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish with tart paper and garnish with preserved strawberries, biscuits or macaroons.
5. Instead of strawberry juice, raspberry, gooseberry or cherry juice may be used.
