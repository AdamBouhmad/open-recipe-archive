## Ingredients

- 8 calf kidneys
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 cup good bouillon
- 1/2 part white French wine or sherry

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys into 1 cm. thick slices and brown them in butter. Place them then in a pot, dilute with bouillon and let them simmer slowly until they are completely tender.
2. Pour on the wine, season the kidney sauté and serve.
