## Ingredients

- 400 gr. butter
- 90 gr. sweet almond
- 400 gr. wheat flour
- 90 gr. granulated sugar
- 1 kg. fresh rhubarb
- 200 gr. sugar
- 5 egg whites
- 8 tbsp. granulated sugar
- 2 tbsp. vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Make a regular shortcrust pastry from the butter, flour, blanched and grated almond, and sugar, and let it set.
2. Peel the rhubarb, cut it into pieces and cook together with the sugar until soft.
3. Remove with a slotted spoon and let it drain.
4. Line the bottom of a ring form and 30 cm. of the edge with the pastry, and bake it.
5. Spread the rhubarb on the tart.
6. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and then carefully mix with vanilla and granulated sugar.
7. Garnish the tart with the meringue mixture and put it in the oven again to get a nice light yellow color.
8. Serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
