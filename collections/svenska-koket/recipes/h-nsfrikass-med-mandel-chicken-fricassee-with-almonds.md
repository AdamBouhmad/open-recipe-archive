## Ingredients

- 2 young chickens
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1.5 liters water or light broth
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 8 parts chicken broth
- 80 grams sweet almonds
- 2 parts milk

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the chickens, cut off wings and legs. Open them, remove organs, rinse very well and dry. Rub inside and outside with vinegar diluted with a little water.
2. Place the chickens in 1.5 liters boiling water or light broth. When the liquid comes to a boil, skim carefully and add the salt. When the chickens are tender, remove them and cut into nice pieces.
3. Strain the broth and skim off the fat.
4. Blanch the almonds, grind them through an almond mill, pound very fine in a mortar and cook in the milk.
5. Mix butter and flour together, add the broth and milk with the almonds, and let the sauce simmer for 10 minutes. Finally add the meat.
