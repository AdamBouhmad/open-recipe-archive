## Ingredients

- 4 dcl. extra thick heavy cream
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks and flavor with vanilla sugar if desired
2. Freeze in an ice cream machine, serve with a spoon in a silver bowl with fruit
3. Can also be packed in a mold and left for 1 hour in ice and salt, unmolded and garnished with cooked fruit
4. The cream can also be mixed with 3 dcl. fruit puree and frozen the same way
