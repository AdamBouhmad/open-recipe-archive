## Ingredients

- 2 eggs (120 gr.)
- 120 gr. butter
- 120 gr. granulated sugar
- 120 gr. wheat flour
- ¼ tsp. baking powder
- 2 tbsp. cream or milk
- 4 tbsp. red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with the sugar until it becomes white, and add the beaten eggs.
2. Sift the flour together with the baking powder and blend alternately with the cream.
3. Grease a small even baking sheet with butter and cover with baking paper, spread the batter on it and place in a hot oven to bake light yellow.
4. Turn the cake out onto sugared paper, coat with some liquefied currant jelly and roll up.
5. Cut the roll into 1 cm. thick slices.
