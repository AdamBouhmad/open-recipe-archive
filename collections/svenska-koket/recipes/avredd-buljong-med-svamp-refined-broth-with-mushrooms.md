## Ingredients

- 1 can of mushrooms (1/4 liter) or 15 gr. dried morels
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 1 1/2 liters secondary broth
- 2–3 egg yolks
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. The mushrooms or morels, which should first be well blanched, are cut into slices and fried in the butter until it is clear.
2. Press the mushroom out of the butter with a spoon and then fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes.
3. Add the hot broth gradually and let it cook for 5 minutes with stirring.
4. Beat the egg yolks and whisk them into the soup, then season with the spices and pour it over the mushrooms in the soup bowl.
5. Serve with cheese croutons or toasted bread.
