## Ingredients

- 10 coffee cups unskinned milk
- 3 heaping tablespoons cornstarch flour
- 1/2 vanilla bean
- 4–6 egg yolks
- 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Whisk one coffee cup of milk with the sugar and cornstarch flour. Heat the remaining milk with the vanilla bean. Stir the mixture into the hot milk and let the cream cook for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and fold in the whisked egg yolks. Remove the vanilla bean and pour the cream into a rinsed mold. When completely cold, turn out and serve with jam or fruit sauce.
