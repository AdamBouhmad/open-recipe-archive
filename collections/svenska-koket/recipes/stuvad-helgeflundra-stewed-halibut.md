## Ingredients

- 1½ kilograms halibut
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1½ teaspoons vinegar
- water
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 2½ tablespoons flour
- fish stock
- 3 parts cream
- pepper
- salt
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Free the fish from skin and bone and cut it into 2 centimeter thick slices, which are rubbed with salt and vinegar.
2. Place it in warm, but not boiling water, let it cook slowly until soft and place it afterwards on a serving plate.
3. Fry the butter and flour together, gradually add the fish stock and cream with vigorous stirring, and let the sauce cook for 10 minutes.
4. Taste it and pour it over the fish on the plate.
5. Garnish the dish with hard-boiled eggs, cut into wedges.
