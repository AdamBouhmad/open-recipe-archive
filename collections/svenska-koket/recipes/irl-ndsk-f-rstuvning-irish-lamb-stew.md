## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. lean lamb breast or shoulder
- 1 litre potatoes
- 4 medium red onions
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 parsley stalks with roots
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 sprig thyme
- 8 decilitres water
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into small, even pieces, and bone them.
2. Peel and slice the potatoes.
3. Peel and coarsely chop the onions.
4. In a heavy pot holding about 2 1/2 litres, place the meat on the bottom and around the sides and season with salt and pepper.
5. Layer the potatoes and onions on top of the meat, layering everything with seasonings between each layer.
6. Clean and rinse the parsley stalks, wrap them in a cloth and tie together with the bay leaf and thyme; place this bundle in the middle of the stew.
7. Add the water, cover with a lid and let it cook in the oven for about 1 1/2 hours.
8. Taste for seasoning, place on a warm serving dish, sprinkle with parsley and serve very hot.
