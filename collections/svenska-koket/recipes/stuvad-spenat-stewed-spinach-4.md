## Ingredients

- 1 kg. spinach
- 4 liters water for blanching
- 2 tbsp. salt (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 2 1/2 tbsp. butter (50 gr.)
- 2-3 dcl. cream, milk or broth
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 2 hard-boiled eggs for garnish

## Directions

1. Treat and blanch the spinach as in the previous recipe, place in a colander, immediately pour cold water over it, and press out the liquid carefully with a spoon.
2. Chop the spinach finely.
