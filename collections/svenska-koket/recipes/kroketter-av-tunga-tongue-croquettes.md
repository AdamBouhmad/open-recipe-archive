## Ingredients

- 2 large calf tongues
- 3/4 msk. salt
- water
- 1 carrot
- 1 piece celery
- 1 leek
- 1 1/2 msk. butter
- 3 msk. flour
- 2-3 kkp of the cooking broth
- 1 1/2 msk. chopped capers
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 eggs for coating
- 2 kkp crushed rusks for coating
- 1/4 kg. lard for frying

## Directions

1. Let the tongues soak in cold water for 1 hour, then scald and scrape them well.
2. Place them in boiling water and skim thoroughly when the broth comes to a boil.
3. Add salt and vegetables, and let the tongues cook slowly until tender.
4. When done, immediately remove the skin and cut the meat into fine cubes.
5. Fry butter and flour together, add the broth in which the tongues cooked, add the tongue and finely cut vegetables along with the capers.
