## Ingredients

- 5 large tomatoes
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 hectogram butter
- 1 large Portuguese onion

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the tomatoes and cut them into slices.
2. Season the tomato slices and dip them in the beaten egg, then turn them in the crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Peel the onion and cut it into thin slices.
4. Heat half of the butter in a frying pan, place the tomato slices in, fry them nicely brown on both sides and place on a warm serving platter.
5. Brown the onion in the rest of the butter and garnish the beef patties with it.
6. Serve with fried potatoes for the smörgåsbord.
