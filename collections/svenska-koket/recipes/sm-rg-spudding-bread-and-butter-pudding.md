## Ingredients

- 1 twelve öre wheat bread
- 2 tbsp. butter
- 3 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 2 cups apple compote or other fruit
- 2 eggs (for the custard)
- 1½ cups milk (for the custard)
- ½ tbsp. butter (for the form)
- 3 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into slices, and spread butter on one side.
2. Whisk the milk and egg together, and dip the bread lightly in it.
3. Grease a form with washed butter and sprinkle it with finely crushed rusks; on the bottom of the form place a layer of the sandwiches, also around the edges of the form — then a layer of apple compote or other fruit.
4. Continue until the form is filled; the top layer should be sandwiches.
5. Whisk the eggs and milk together and pour over the pudding, place it in gentle oven heat.
6. When it has taken on a nice, golden-brown color, it is ready.
7. Serve either in its form, or turn it out after most of the heat has subsided.
