## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Fried bread slices

## Directions

1. Pour water to a depth of 2 cm into a well-cleaned frying pan; add 1/2 tsp. salt and 1/2 tsp. vinegar.
2. When the water boils, carefully break one egg at a time into a cup and let it slide slowly into the water, which should barely be boiling.
3. When the white has set, remove the eggs with a slotted spoon and lower them quickly into cold water to remove the scum.
4. Trim them with a drinking glass and serve as a breakfast dish, arranged on fried bread slices, or as garnish to other dishes.
