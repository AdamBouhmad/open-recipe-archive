## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 celery root
- 1 parsnip
- 2-3 small sugar beets
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 1 hg. canned Turkish beans
- 1 hg. canned asparagus
- 1 hg. canned fine peas
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- a few grains paprika
- 1/2 tsk. sugar
- 6 msk. fine cooking oil
- 3 msk. vinegar or wine vinegar
- 1 head of lettuce for garnish
- 2 msk. finely chopped parsley
- 2 tsk. finely chopped watercress or chervil

## Directions

1. Clean the root vegetables (see page 338), boil them and cut into attractive strips with a vegetable knife.
2. Clean the cauliflower head and boil in the usual way (see page 338).
3. Cut the beans diagonally into smaller pieces and cut the asparagus in half.
4. Arrange the vegetables, which should be freshly boiled and preferably lukewarm, in groups on a round silver platter garnished with lettuce leaves; place the cauliflower head in the center.
5. Prepare the sauce as in recipe no. 448 and pour it over the vegetables.
6. Sprinkle with a mixture of parsley and watercress.
7. Serve with roasted meat.
