## Ingredients

- 150 grams sweet almond
- 6 bitter almonds
- 6 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Scald the almonds and grate or grind them through an almond mill and pound with the sugar in a mortar
2. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and work into the almond mass
3. Place the mass with spoons onto small cakes, which are baked in very weak oven heat
