## Ingredients

- 2 kg red bream
- 2 tsp vinegar
- 2 tbsp salt
- white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup crushed hardtack
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2 cups fish stock or water

## Directions

1. Scale, scrape, clean, rinse, and dry the fish well, then rub it with a mixture of vinegar, salt, and a little white pepper and let it rest for a while.
2. Brush it with the beaten egg and sprinkle with the crushed hardtack, then place it in a buttered baking pan with half of the butter.
3. Place the remaining butter in small flakes on top of the fish and set it in the oven.
4. When it has taken on a nice golden-brown color, dilute it with fish stock or water and let it continue cooking for about 20 minutes.
