## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg boneless veal (from the loin)
- 1 hectogram lard bacon
- white pepper
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2-3 cups boiling water
- For sauce: 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat stock
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut into slices, which are pounded lightly.
2. Lard each slice with three bacon strips and season.
3. Brown them in the butter, add the boiling water, and let the dish boil with the lid closed until the meat is tender.
4. Serve the meat on carrot with the sauce over.
5. The sauce is prepared according to general rules for sauces.
