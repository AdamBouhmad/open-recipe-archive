## Ingredients

- 1 cauliflower head
- 100 grams tender carrots
- 300 grams small green cucumbers
- 100 grams preserved onions
- 100 grams tender fresh beans
- Water
- Salt
- 15 grams whole white pepper
- 7 grams nutmeg
- 2 slices Spanish pepper
- 1.5 liters vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook all vegetables and root vegetables separately in salted water until just tender.
2. Drain them well and place them in glass preserving jars with the spices.
3. Boil the vinegar, let it cool, and pour it over.
