## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon chopped shallot
- 1/2 gram white pepper
- 2 grams salt
- 4 tablespoons good vinegar
- 5 egg yolks
- 180 grams melted butter
- cayenne
- 20 grams chopped tarragon
- 3 tablespoons good meat stock or fish bouillon

## Directions

1. Mix the shallot, pepper, salt and vinegar in the sauce pan and reduce by boiling until 1/3 remains.
2. Move the pan to the side, add the whisked egg yolks and let the sauce simmer under vigorous whisking.
3. Add the melted, slightly cooled butter gradually.
4. Strain the sauce through a fine cloth, season with cayenne and finish with the chopped tarragon and, if desired, the meat stock or fish bouillon.
5. Heat in a water bath until lukewarm and serve at once. If overheated, it will break.
