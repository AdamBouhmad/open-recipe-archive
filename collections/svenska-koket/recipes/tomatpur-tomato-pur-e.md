## Ingredients

- 8 pickle onions
- 2 tbsp. butter
- 6 ordinary tomatoes
- 2 liters broth
- 2 tbsp. tapioca pearls
- salt
- white pepper
- 1-2 tbsp. sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Lightly brown the onions in the butter, and add the coarsely cut tomatoes (fresh or preserved) together with ½ liter of the broth, and let everything cook approximately 20 minutes, after which the mass is passed through a sieve.
2. Cook the remaining broth with the tapioca pearls, which are then strained out so that the broth is only slightly thickened; add the passed purée, and bring the soup to a boil and season with the spices and wine.
3. Serve with fried bread.
