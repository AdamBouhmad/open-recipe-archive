## Ingredients

- 20 grams cold butter
- 50 grams sugar
- 3 deciliters cream
- 50 grams melted butter
- zest and juice of 0.5 orange
- 5 egg yolks
- 3 egg whites
- 0.5 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the mold

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan and stir with the sugar and cream over the fire until the mixture becomes simmering, then pour it into a bowl.
2. Add the cold butter and stir the mixture until it becomes cold, then add the finely grated orange zest and orange juice.
3. Stir in the egg yolks one at a time, while working the mixture for 30 minutes.
4. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks and pour the mixture into a buttered pie mold, sprinkle with sugar, and bake for approximately 30 minutes in regular oven heat. Serve immediately from the mold.
