## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- a few grains cayenne
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar or vinegar
- 2 deciliter fine oil
- 1/2—1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon boiling water

## Directions

1. Place the egg yolks in a large bowl together with the seasonings and stir with a wooden spoon for 10 minutes.
2. Gradually pour the vinegar over the yolks with vigorous stirring.
3. When the vinegar is blended with the yolks, add the oil in a thin stream with constant stirring.
4. Finish the sauce with lemon juice and the boiling water—the latter to prevent it from breaking.
5. Mayonnaise prepared in this way is quite thin and should therefore stand in a cool (not too cold) place for 4—5 hours to thicken.
6. It can be stored for 3—4 days without damage, provided it is well covered and does not stand on ice.
7. If the sauce breaks, this can be remedied by gradually stirring it into 1 egg yolk and 1/2 tablespoon vinegar.
8. The sauce becomes lighter if mustard is omitted and only lemon juice is used instead of vinegar.
