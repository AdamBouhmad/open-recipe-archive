## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup rice grits
- 0.75 tablespoon butter
- 2.5 liters milk
- 1 piece cinnamon
- salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grits and pour into a colander.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, add the milk and bring to a boil with cinnamon.
3. Add the rice grits and let the porridge simmer gently for about 2 hours.
4. Season and serve.
