## Ingredients

- 1.5 deciliters regular oat grains (112 grams)
- 2.25 liters water
- 75 grams sultana raisins
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 grams)
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)
- 3 deciliters thick cream

## Directions

1. Put the grains into cold water and let them boil for 1.5 hours, with a tight lid, without thinning.
2. Rinse the raisins well in warm water, cook them in 2 tablespoons water until they are soft, fully swollen, and clear.
3. Strain the grains through a fine sieve, bring the strained soup to a boil again with salt and sugar, add the raisins, and season the soup carefully.
4. Serve with the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
