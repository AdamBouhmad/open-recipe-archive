## Ingredients

- 1 kg half-ripe medlars
- 1 kg (or 3/4 kg) lump sugar
- 3 parts water

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit and prick it with a silver fork so the skin does not burst during cooking.
2. Cut off the stems somewhat.
3. Cook the medlars the same way as plums (see previous recipe).
4. After the fruit is poured into jars, sprinkle the top layer with granulated sugar (otherwise the berries will turn black).
