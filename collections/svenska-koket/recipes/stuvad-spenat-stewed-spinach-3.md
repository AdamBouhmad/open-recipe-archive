## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg spinach
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 cups broth
- 1 hard-boiled egg

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach, rinse it quickly and let it drain.
2. Blanch it in very little boiling and salted water, then remove it and let it drain.
3. Chop it finely together with the flour.
4. Fry the butter, and add the chopped spinach.
5. Add broth, milk or water, and let the stewing simmer for 10 minutes.
6. Season the spinach with salt and sugar and garnish with the hard-boiled egg, finely chopped or cut into wedges.
