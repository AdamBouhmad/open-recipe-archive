## Ingredients

- 18 Baltic herrings
- 1/2 tbsp. salt
- 1/2 tbsp. sugar
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 1/2 tsp. French mustard
- 2 tbsp. cooking oil
- 1 dcl. vinegar
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped dill

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, rinse well, skin, remove the bones, and scrape away the dark skin carefully.
2. Rinse the fillets, wrap in a fish towel and lay in an earthenware bowl in alternating layers with the spices.
3. Stir the mustard with the oil and vinegar and pour this marinade over the herring.
4. Sprinkle the dill over and let the herring stand in a cold place for 1 hour. It is then cured.
5. Serve to the smorgasbord.
