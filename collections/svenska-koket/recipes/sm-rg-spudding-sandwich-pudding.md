## Ingredients

- 1 12-öre wheat bread
- 2 tsk. butter
- 3 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 2 cups apple compote or other fruit
- 2 eggs for the custard
- 1¼ cups milk for the custard
- ½ tsk. butter for the form
- 3 tsk. crushed hardtack

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into slices and spread butter on one side.
2. Whisk together the milk and egg, and lightly soak the bread in this.
3. Butter a form and sprinkle with finely crushed hardtack; on the bottom of the form place a layer of sandwiches, also around the edges of the form—then a layer of apple compote or other fruit.
4. Continue thus until the form is filled; the top layer should be sandwiches.
5. Whisk together the egg and milk and pour over the pudding; place it in a weak oven heat.
6. When it has taken on a nice golden-brown color, it is done.
7. Serve either in its form, or turn it out after most of the heat has subsided.
