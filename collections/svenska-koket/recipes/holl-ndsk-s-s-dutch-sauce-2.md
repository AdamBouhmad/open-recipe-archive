## Ingredients

- 80 gr. butter
- 1 tbsp. flour
- 3 egg yolks
- sugar
- 2 parts water
- lemon juice
- salt

## Directions

1. Three quarters of the butter and the flour, egg yolks and a little sugar are whisked together cold.
2. The mixture is placed on very low heat, and the cold water is added little by little, while the saucepan is occasionally lifted off—all under constant whisking.
3. The sauce must not boil, only thicken.
4. When it is smooth and glossy and sufficiently thick, it is removed from heat, and the rest of the butter is stirred in in small portions together with the lemon juice.
5. The sauce is tasted carefully and served very hot.
