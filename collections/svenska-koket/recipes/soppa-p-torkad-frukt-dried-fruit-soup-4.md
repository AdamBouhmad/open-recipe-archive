## Ingredients

- 1 hg dried apples
- 1 hg dried pears
- 1/2 hg raisins
- 2 liters water
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- peel of 1/2 lemon
- 1 1/2-2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 25 gr rice or potato flour
- 1/2 dcl sherry or French wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit very well in lukewarm water and let it soak overnight in the water in which it will be cooked.
2. Place the fruit in the water together with the cinnamon, lemon peel, and sugar and let it cook until soft.
3. Thicken the soup with the flour, stirred in 1/2 tablespoon cold water, and let it cook for 5 minutes.
4. Flavor with the wine and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and rusks or biscuits.
