## Ingredients

- 1 cod of about 0.75 kg
- 0.5 tablespoon vinegar
- 2.5 tablespoons salt (43 g)

## Directions

1. Brush the cod with vinegar and scrape well from the tail upward.
2. Clean it (see page 171), rinse very well and dry it.
3. Set aside the roe and liver, boil them separately in salted water and use them as garnish for the fish.
4. Cut off the head, from which the gills have been removed, together with the tail, as they easily fall apart during cooking. These can be boiled separately and used as garnish or grilled and fried.
5. Make three scores on each side of the thickest part of the fish; rub the fish with salt.
