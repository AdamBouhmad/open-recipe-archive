## Ingredients

- 2 cups dried green peas
- 3 liters water
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1 leek
- 1 small celery root
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 0.25 cup cream
- salt
- sugar (optional)
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Sort the dried peas and soak them in cold water the evening before.
2. Put them on in fresh cold water to cook until completely soft. If the water is hard, add a small pinch of soda about the size of a pea during cooking.
3. Peel the root vegetables in the usual manner, cut them into strips, and cook them separately in salted water.
4. When the peas are cooked, pass them through a sieve.
5. Melt the butter and flour together, add the purée, and let the soup cook for 10 minutes.
6. Add the cooked root vegetables together with their liquid and taste the soup.
7. Whip the cream in the soup bowl and mix it in.
