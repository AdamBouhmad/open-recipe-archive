## Ingredients

- 1½ liters water
- Peel and juice of ½ lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- 125 grams red sago grain
- 1 tablespoon preserved plums
- 1 tablespoon preserved cherries or 2 tablespoons cherry, red currant, or raspberry juice
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Bring water to a boil with lemon peel, whisk in the cinnamon and grain so that it does not lump, and let it cook until the grain is clear.
2. Then add the fruit or one of the juice varieties and bring the soup to a boil and season with sugar.
3. Serve preferably cold with rusks.
