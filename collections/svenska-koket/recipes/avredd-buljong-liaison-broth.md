## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 1 1/2 liters light broth
- 2—3 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes.
2. Add the boiling broth gradually while stirring, and let it boil for 5 minutes.
3. Beat the egg yolks and whisk them into the broth.
4. Season with the spices and serve with cooked root vegetables, mushrooms, cheese croutons, quenelles of fish forcemeat or similar.
