## Ingredients

- 0.5 loaf of twelve-öre wheat bread
- 4 eggs
- 0.8 liters whole milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 parts cognac
- 1 tsp. cinnamon
- 0.5 tsp. cardamom
- 0.25 tsp. ground cloves
- 1 tsp. salt
- 200 gr. marrow
- 1 tbsp. flour
- 1 cup sultana raisins
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks
- 0.5 tbsp. sugar
- 0.5 tbsp. butter for the mold
- For sauce: 1 part cognac
- 1 part water
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into cubes and soften in a mixture of eggs and milk.
2. Add the spices.
3. Chop the marrow finely together with the flour and mix in; also add the pre-soaked raisins.
4. Pour the batter into a buttered and bread-crumb-strewn mold and bake in the oven for about 1 hour.
5. Mix the cognac, water and sugar together, warm and pour over the pudding, and ignite the sauce.
6. It can also be served in a separate sauce bowl, flaming.
