## Ingredients

- 1 kg pike or coalfish (= 400 gr scraped fish meat)
- 200 gr butter
- 300 gr wheat bread
- 5-6 parts unskimmed milk
- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- sugar
- nutmeg
- fish stock, made from skin and bones

## Directions

1. Remove the fish, rinse and dry well
2. Fillet it, and scrape the fish meat from the skin and bones; no scale or bone should be included
3. Pass the fish meat together with the butter 3 times through the meat grinder and then pound in a mortar until the mass is fine and cohesive
4. Soften the wheat bread in part of the milk and pass it
5. In a bowl mix the rest of the milk, egg yolks and seasonings, and work this mixture gradually into the forcemeat with a tablespoon, alternating with the passed bread
6. Work the forcemeat well, and form balls from it, which are cooked in salted fish stock (made from skin and bones)
7. Serve the fish balls in regular fish sauce with added cream, or in crayfish sauce
