## Ingredients

- 4 cup broken macaroni
- water
- salt
- 2 cup grated cheese
- 6 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoon flour
- 3-4 decilitre cream
- 2 tablespoon crushed rusks

## Directions

1. The macaroni is broken into 5 cm long pieces and boiled in salted water, then drained
2. It is then placed in a buttered heatproof mold, alternated with the grated cheese
3. A sauce is made from 1 tablespoon butter, flour and cream, and poured over the macaroni
4. The mold is sprinkled with crushed rusks, and the remaining butter is placed in flakes on top
5. The mold is placed in the oven
6. When the dish has a golden brown color, it is removed and served
