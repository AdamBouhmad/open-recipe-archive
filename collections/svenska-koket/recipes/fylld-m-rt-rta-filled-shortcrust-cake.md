## Ingredients

- 300 gr. butter
- 100 gr. powdered sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 25 gr. sweet almond
- 5 bitter almonds
- 325 gr. wheat flour
- 1/2 liter applesauce
- 400 gr. powdered sugar
- 200 gr. sweet almond
- 2-3 egg whites

## Directions

1. Prepare the shortcrust pastry and let it rest 2-3 hours
2. The applesauce should preferably be cooked the day before so it is completely cold
3. Blanch, dry and grind the almonds, pound with the sugar and egg whites in a mortar until a fine and smooth mass is formed, which should be quite loose
4. Divide the dough into 3 parts
5. Roll out one part on the bottom of a ring form and bake until half done
6. When the bottom is cold, line the sides with dough, add the applesauce and spread the almond mass evenly over it
7. Roll out the rest of the dough into thin strips and lay them in diagonal squares over the almond mass
8. Place the remaining dough in a ring around the cake
9. Bake in a not-too-warm oven until it is completely soft, which is tested with a whisk
10. Serve cooled with vanilla sauce or whipped cream or with coffee and tea
