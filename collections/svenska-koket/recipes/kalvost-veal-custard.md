## Ingredients

- 1 liter raw milk
- 1/2 liter cream
- 2 tablespoons sugar (30 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 gr.)
- 15 almonds (2 bitter) (15 gr.) or 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Whisk together the milk, cream, sugar, salt and the blanched, ground almonds or cinnamon thoroughly.
2. Test the mixture in a small pan over gentle heat; if it is too firm add a little more cream, otherwise 1-2 beaten eggs.
3. Pour it into a buttered mold and bake in gentle oven heat or in a water bath, in the oven or on top of the stove.
4. Serve with brûlée sauce, gravy sauce or cream milk.
