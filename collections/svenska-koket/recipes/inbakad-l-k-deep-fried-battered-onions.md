## Ingredients

- 3 large Portuguese onions (3 hg)
- 1 litre water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr)
- 65 gr flour
- 65 centilitres cream
- 1 egg yolk
- 12 gr butter
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 small egg white
- 1/2 kg lard

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and blanch (see no. 713) 15 minutes, place on cloth and let cool.
2. Cut them thereafter into 1/2 cm thick slices, and separate the rings from each other.
3. For the batter, sift the flour in a bowl, stir in the cream, egg yolk, the melted butter and the seasonings. Last fold in the egg white beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Take the onion rings with a fork and dip in the batter, which must completely cover them.
5. Fry in hot lard, until they have a nice light brown colour, then place on absorbent paper to drain.
6. Serve hot with roasted meat, for example roast beef.
