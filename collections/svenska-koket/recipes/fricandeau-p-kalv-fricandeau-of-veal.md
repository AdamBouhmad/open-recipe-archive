## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless veal roast
- 1½ hg lard
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 2 carrots
- ½ tbsp salt
- ¼ tsp white pepper
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 liter broth

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and beat lightly with a damp mallet, then lard densely with fine strips of lard.
2. Line a roasting pan with slices of lard and place the roast in it.
3. Add the scraped carrots cut into thick slices, the peeled and chopped onion, the seasonings, and the butter.
4. Pour in half the broth and let it boil in carefully so the meat does not burn.
5. When the liquid on the bottom becomes thick and murky, add the remaining broth and let the meat roast slowly for 1½ to 2 hours, turning it a couple of times during roasting.
6. Strain the sauce, skim it, and serve as gravy.
