## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- 1 liter boiling cream or milk
- 150 grams sugar

## Directions

1. Stir the egg yolks with sugar in a saucepan for about 20 minutes.
2. Add the boiling cream slowly, a little at a time.
3. Let the cream simmer over heat with vigorous whisking until it is thick and frothy.
4. Remove the saucepan from heat and continue whisking until the cream is completely cold.
5. Note: Unskimmed milk can be used instead of cream, but then use 10 egg yolks and 180 grams sugar.
6. The proportions of egg, sugar and cream are the same for all cream ices, and the preparation is the same; they differ only in various flavor additions.
