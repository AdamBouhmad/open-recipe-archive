## Ingredients

- 4 small salted herrings
- 80 gr. butter
- 2 sheets white writing paper
- 3 hectograms fatty broth (for frying)

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water for 24 hours, changing the water 2 times. Skin it, clean it, rinse it well and dry it with a fish towel.
2. Cut off the head and tail and also the backbone, if desired. Divide the sheets of paper in half, cut them oval and brush them with butter on as large an area as the herring occupies.
3. Place the herring on the paper, fold the edges tightly over one another, then place the herring in a frying pan with hot fatty broth and fry for approximately 5 minutes.
4. Place the pan in a warm oven until the paper puffs up like a balloon. Serve with boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
