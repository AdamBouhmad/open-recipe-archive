## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg lamb meat
- 2 red onions
- 1 tbsp salt
- 0.5 tsp white pepper
- 6 cups water
- 1.5 liters potatoes

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut into small pieces and place in the bottom of a pot. Peel and parboil the onions, cut into slices and place with the seasonings on top of the meat. Pour on the boiling water and let the meat simmer slowly for 0.25 hour. Then add the peeled potatoes cut into slices and let the stew simmer further for approximately 0.5 hour.
2. Serve the meat in the middle of the plate, potatoes around, and pour the sauce over.
