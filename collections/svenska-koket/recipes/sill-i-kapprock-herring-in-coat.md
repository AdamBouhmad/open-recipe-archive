## Ingredients

- 2 large salted herrings
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 red onions
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 1 sheet white writing paper
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 gr.) for frying

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water for 24 hours, changing the water 3 times. Remove the skin, cut off head and tail.
2. Cut open along the back, remove the bones and innards, then rinse well and dry with a fish towel.
3. Sprinkle the inside with pepper and the peeled onion cut into thin slices. Fold together again so that the herring retains its shape.
4. Wrap each herring well with 1/2 sheet of paper, spread with 2 tablespoons of the butter. Fry in butter until the paper is brown or approximately 15 minutes.
5. Serve the herring in the paper with stewed potatoes as a breakfast dish.
