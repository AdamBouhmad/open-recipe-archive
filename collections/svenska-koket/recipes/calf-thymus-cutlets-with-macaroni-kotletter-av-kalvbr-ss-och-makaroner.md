## Ingredients

- 1 hg. calf thymus
- 1 lit. water for blanching
- 2 tsp. salt for blanching
- 1 hg. macaroni
- 1 1/2 lit. water for cooking macaroni
- 1/2 tbsp. salt for cooking macaroni
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.) for sauce
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 2 dcl. thymus broth, mixed with 1 tsp. meat extract
- 3 dcl. milk
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 eggs for breading
- 1 tbsp. water for breading
- 1 1/2 cups crushed breadcrumbs
- 80 gr. butter for frying
- 1 can peas for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the calf thymus as in recipe no. 47, clean it and cut it into fine dice.
2. Parboil the macaroni in salted water and cut into thin rings.
3. Make a roux by frying butter and flour for 2 minutes.
4. Add the hot broth and milk and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
5. Add the thymus and macaroni and stew well, seasoning to taste with the seasonings.
6. Pour onto a flat plate, let cool, and then form into oblong cutlets.
7. Brush the cutlets with eggs beaten with water and turn in crushed breadcrumbs.
8. Fry the cutlets a beautiful golden brown in butter and arrange in a ring on a round plate.
9. Place peas in the middle of the plate, see previous recipe.
10. Serve as a breakfast or light meal dish.
