## Ingredients

- 1 kg veal liver
- 50 gr lard
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 caul
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr)
- 6 deciliters boiling milk
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr)
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr)
- liver sauce
- 1-2 deciliters thick cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 0.5 tablespoon cold butter (10 gr)

## Directions

1. The liver, which should be light and tender, should soak in water for about 0.5 hour, then dry it well, clean it and lard it with fine lard strips rolled in a mixture of the salt and pepper
