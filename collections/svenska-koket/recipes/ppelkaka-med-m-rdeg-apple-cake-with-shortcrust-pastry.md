## Ingredients

- For the shortcrust pastry: 2 hg. butter
- 65 gr. sugar
- 1 egg
- 10 sweet almonds
- 5 bitter almonds
- 2 hg. flour
- For the apple compote: 2 hg. sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 1 1/4 liters good, tart apples
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- For the form: 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and mix with the sugar to a foam, then add the egg, the blanched and ground almonds and finally the flour, which should be sieved.
2. Work the dough until it becomes even, then let it stand in a cool place for about 2 hours.
3. Prepare the apple compote as in no. 820, let it cool and mix with the reduced syrup.
4. Butter a tin form or frying pan and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
5. Roll out two-thirds of the dough and line the form on the bottom and up to the edge.
6. Add the apple compote, fold the dough edge over, roll out the remaining dough as a lid, cover the pie with it and press the edges down well.
7. Bake the pie in a good oven heat for 3/4 to 1 hour, or until the dough is baked through when tested with a whisk.
8. Remove the pie, let it stand a quarter hour, turn it out onto a platter or bowl with tart paper, let it cool, garnish with apple jelly or whipped cream and serve with cream or vanilla sauce.
9. Instead of apple compote, 1/2 liter of firm apple sauce can be used.
