## Ingredients

- ½ kg veal meat
- ½ kg pork meat
- 1 cooked salted ox tongue
- ⅓ liter water
- 140 grams potato starch
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water and cut into pieces.
2. Remove sinews and membranes and pass the meat 4 times through the meat grinder.
3. Cut the tongue into large cubes and mix into the forcemeat along with the water, potato starch, and spices to taste.
4. Work the forcemeat very well and stuff it into a wet bag, sewn in the form of a casing.
5. Tie this up tightly, cook the tongue sausage in water for 2 hours, and then press it.
6. Serve sliced in thin slices for the sandwich board.
