## Ingredients

- 2 kg. short-chopped saddle
- 3 liters skim milk or drink
- 1 1/2 hg lard
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 2 dcl. finely crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. The saddle is prepared according to the method for roasted venison, larded and seasoned with salt, then roasted until tender.
2. Once cooked and cooled, it is cut into serving pieces, dipped in whisked egg, turned in crushed rusks, fried a beautiful golden brown in butter and served with fried potatoes.
