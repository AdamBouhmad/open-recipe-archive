## Ingredients

- 35 brussels sprout heads
- 3 liters water for blanching
- 1 tablespoon salt for blanching
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 1.5 liters stock
- 1.5 hectograms fresh, lean pork
- 1 small red onion
- 0.25 liters milk
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the cabbage, blanch for 15 minutes as in the previous recipe
2. Cut the pork into cubes, peel the onion, chop it finely and fry in butter together with the pork for about 10 minutes
3. Wring the cabbage in a cloth, chop it finely and fry well with the pork and flour
4. Add the boiling stock and milk and let the soup cook with stirring for 15 minutes
5. Strain the soup if desired, season with the seasonings and serve with chicken quenelles and fried bread cubes
