## Ingredients

- 1 kg carrots
- 2.5 liters water
- 1 shallot
- 1 leek
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the carrots and parboil them. When most of the heat has escaped, scrape off the thin skin, then grate the carrots and cook them in the water together with the sliced onions and salt for 1.5-2 hours.
2. Pass the soup through a sieve.
3. Melt the butter and flour together, add the carrot purée, and let the soup cook for 10-15 minutes.
4. Skim and taste carefully, pour into soup bowls, and stir in 1 teaspoon cold butter.
5. Serve the purée with cheese sandwiches.
