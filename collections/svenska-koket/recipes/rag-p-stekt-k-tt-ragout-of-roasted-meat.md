## Ingredients

- 500 g boneless roasted meat of beef, veal or lamb
- 800 ml stock or water
- 1 red onion
- 2 tbsp butter (40 g)
- 4 tbsp flour (40 g)
- 500 ml of the stock
- 2 tbsp finely chopped pickled cucumber
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the bones into even, attractive pieces.
2. Crush the bones and cook as in the previous recipe.
3. Peel the onion, chop finely and brown lightly in the butter.
4. Fry the flour with the butter and onion for 2 minutes; add the strained stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
5. Add the meat and pickled cucumber and warm in the sauce but do not allow it to boil.
6. Season the dish with spices, arrange on a warm plate and garnish with browned potatoes.
