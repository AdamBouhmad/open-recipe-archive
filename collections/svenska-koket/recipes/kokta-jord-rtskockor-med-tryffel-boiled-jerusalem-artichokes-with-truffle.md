## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 2 liters water
- 15 grams salt
- 3-4 truffles

## Directions

1. Brush the Jerusalem artichokes clean, scrape, rinse well, shape them round and let them soak in vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar) for 1 hour.
2. Place them in boiling salted water and boil until tender.
3. Remove with a slotted spoon and place on a round plate with a folded serviette.
4. Sprinkle with the truffle, cut into fine strips, and serve very hot with melted butter.
