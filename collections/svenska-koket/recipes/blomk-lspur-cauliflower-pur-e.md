## Ingredients

- 1 large cauliflower head
- 1/2 liter boiling water
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 gr)
- 4 tbsp flour (40 gr)
- 1 1/2 liters light broth
- salt, sugar
- white pepper
- 2-3 egg yolks
- 1 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower in the usual way and divide into wedges, which should soak in vinegar-mixed water (1 liter water, 1 tbsp vinegar) 1 hour.
2. Put them in the boiling water, with salt added, and let cook until they are quite soft, then strain.
3. The purée is then cooked as in the previous recipe.
4. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl and pour in the purée while whisking.
5. If desired, cook a small cauliflower head and serve divided in the purée, or serve it with cheese croutons or toasted bread.
