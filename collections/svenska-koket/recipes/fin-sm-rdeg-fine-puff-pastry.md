## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg wheat flour
- 3 parts water
- 1/4 part brandy
- 1 egg
- 1 kg butter (equal weight to the remaining dough)

## Directions

1. From the flour, water, brandy and egg make a smooth dough, which is rolled out in a square to approximately 15 mm thickness.
2. In the middle place the well-washed butter.
3. Now fold all four corners of the dough over the butter, and gently clap out the dough with a rolling pin to a square of approximately 10-15 mm thickness.
4. Fold this square so that 1/4 is folded inward from the left and 1/4 is folded inward from the right.
5. The edges of these two folds meet in the middle of the square.
6. Fold one half of the dough over the other along the length.
7. The dough now lies in four layers on top of each other.
8. The long, narrow dough is then folded into three parts on top of each other (so that you get layers).
9. Work the dough until all the butter is well mixed, then place it on ice to rest for 20 minutes.
10. Roll out the dough again to a square of approximately 5-7 mm thickness, which is folded together in the same way as before and allowed to rest on ice for 20 minutes.
11. Roll out again and fold as before.
12. Then place the dough with the tripled side toward the worker and roll out in one direction from bottom to top, but then fold together only into three parts.
13. Repeat this twice with 20 minutes rest between each time.
14. The puff pastry is now ready, but should preferably rest a while longer before being rolled out and baked.
