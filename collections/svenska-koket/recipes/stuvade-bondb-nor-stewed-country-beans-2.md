## Ingredients

- 8 dcl. sprouted beans
- 8 small fresh carrots
- 8 dcl. water
- 2 tsp. salt
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 4 tbsp. flour (40 gr.)
- 3 dcl. bean cooking liquid
- 3 dcl. milk
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 3 tsp. chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the sprouts; if the beans are very large, peel them as well.
2. Rinse the carrots, scrape them, rinse again, dry them and cut them into slices or cubes.
3. Bring the water to a boil with salt, add the beans and let them cook approximately 15 minutes, then add the carrots and let the vegetables cook until soft.
4. Drain the cooking liquid and reserve it.
5. Brown the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the cooking liquid and milk, and let the sauce simmer 5 minutes, then add the beans with parsley, and season the stew with sugar.
6. Serve with boiled ham or fried pork.
