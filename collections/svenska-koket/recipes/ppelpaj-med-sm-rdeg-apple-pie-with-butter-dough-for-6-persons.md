## Ingredients

- For the butter dough: 2 cups flour, 8 tbsp. butter, 1½ tbsp. brandy
- For filling: 2 liters apples, 1½ cups sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour on the pastry board, wash the butter and distribute finely in the flour, working it in with the fingertips.
2. Add the brandy quickly, and as soon as the dough is cohesive, set it out to become cold.
3. Peel the apples, core them and cut into thin slices and place in a fireproof mold with sugar between each layer.
4. The last layer should be fruit.
5. If the apples are not juicy, add ½ cup water.
6. Roll out the pie dough to ½ cm. thickness.
7. Cut out a lid for the mold, brush the mold's edge with water and cover with a thick strip of dough, which is pressed firmly on the edge and brushed with water.
8. Place the dough lid on and press firmly against the mold's inner edge, then smooth the outer edges of the lid and strip with a sharp knife.
9. Crimp the edge with the back of the knife and flute with the point of the knife.
10. Make four holes in the lid with a coarse whisk.
11. Brush the pie with beaten egg and place in good oven heat to bake to a lovely golden brown for about 1 hour.
12. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream.
