## Ingredients

- 1 salted, smoked ox tongue
- 3 liters water
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1/4 can green beans
- 1 1/2 liters good broth
- 12-15 sheets gelatin
- 3 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon cold water
- 1/2 deciliter good sherry or Madeira
- small rolls of white cabbage leaves, filled with vegetable salad
- parsley
- filled tomatoes and salad

## Directions

1. Let the tongue soak in water for about 12 hours, place it in cold water and let it cook slowly until tender or about 3 hours.
2. Remove it, pull off the skin at once and let the tongue cool.
3. Cut it into 3 millimeter thick slices, which are trimmed so that they have a nice shape and are of equal size. (The trimmed pieces can be used for croquettes or stewed preparations.)
4. Lay the slices on large platters with a little space between them.
5. Separate the egg white from the yolk.
6. Cut the egg white into flowers and finely chop the yolk into a center.
7. Cut the beans into stems and leaves.
8. Garnish the tongue slices beautifully with these flowers and leaves and treat them as in the previous recipe.
9. Arrange them on a round platter and garnish with small rolls of white cabbage leaves, filled with vegetable salad, and parsley, or with filled tomatoes and salad.
10. Serve as a middle course.
