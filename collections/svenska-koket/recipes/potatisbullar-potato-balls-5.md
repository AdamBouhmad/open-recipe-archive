## Ingredients

- 1 liter cooked leftover potatoes
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams)
- 1 egg yolk
- 1–2 tablespoons thick cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- a little nutmeg
- 1 part crushed rusks for breading
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams) for frying

## Directions

1. Grate the potatoes on a grater or press them through a purée press.
2. Stir the butter until foamy and blend well with the potato mash together with the egg yolk, cream, and seasonings.
3. With two spoons dipped in flour, form round balls, which are flattened, turned in crushed rusks, and fried until nicely brown in the butter.
4. Serve with fried fish or meat.
5. The egg yolk can be omitted if desired.
