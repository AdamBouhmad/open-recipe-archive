## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg veal breast
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 10 white peppercorns

## Directions

1. Prepare the breast (see page 232), break it well, place it in boiling water and skim thoroughly, then add the spices.
2. Let it cook slowly with a tightly closed lid until it is tender and the outer bones loosen, or 1 1/2–2 hours.
3. Remove it, take away all loosened bones and cut the breast into beautiful, even pieces, which are placed on a plate and kept warm.
4. Skim the broth thoroughly and use it for sauce.
5. Serve the veal breast with crayfish, oyster, light mushroom, morel, caper, olive, tomato, or dill sauce, as well as boiled potatoes.
6. If the breast is served with crayfish sauce, it should be garnished with fresh crayfish tails and fresh asparagus, cooked in the meat broth.
