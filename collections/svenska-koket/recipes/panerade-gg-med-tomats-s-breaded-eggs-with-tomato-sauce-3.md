## Ingredients

- 6 poached eggs
- 2 egg whites
- 2 cups crushed rusks
- ½ kg clarified butter
- 1 sheet gray paper
- 4 large red tomatoes
- 4 deciliters stock
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 g)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 1 deciliter cream
- salt
- cayenne
- 6 slices of wheat bread
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)

## Directions

1. Dip the eggs in the egg whites that have been lightly beaten with 1 tablespoon cold water, roll them in crushed rusks, and let them rest for 1 hour in a cold place.
2. Rinse the tomatoes, clean them, cut them into pieces, and cook them soft in the stock, then press them through a sieve.
3. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, add the tomato purée and cream, and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes, then season with spices.
4. Fry the eggs golden brown in hot clarified butter.
5. The bread slices, which should be 2 cm thick, are cut into oblong shapes, hollowed out, fried golden brown in butter, and placed on a warm platter.
6. Place the eggs on the croutons and pour the tomato sauce around them.
