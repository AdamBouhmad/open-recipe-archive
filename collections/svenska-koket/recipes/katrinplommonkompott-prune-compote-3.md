## Ingredients

- 4 hectograms fine prunes (about 36 pieces)
- 1/2 liter water
- 3 tablespoons sugar (45 g)

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes very well in several warm water baths and let them soak in cold water overnight.
2. Pour off the water and cook it up with the sugar, add the prunes, and let them cook covered until they are soft and swollen, or about 10 minutes.
3. Place them in a compote bowl, and cook the syrup until it is syrupy, or thicken it with 1 teaspoon (5 g) potato starch and pour over the prunes.
4. Serve cold with whipped cream.
