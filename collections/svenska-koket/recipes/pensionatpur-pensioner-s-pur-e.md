## Ingredients

- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1/2 rutabaga
- 1 small celery root
- 1 small leek
- 4 parsley stalks
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 liter milk
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 1 liter stock
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the vegetables in the usual way and cut them into pieces.
2. Bring the water and milk to a boil; add the vegetables along with 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter and let them cook until completely soft, then pass through a sieve.
3. Prepare and serve the purée as in recipe no. 266.
