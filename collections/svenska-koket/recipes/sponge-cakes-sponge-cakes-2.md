## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- wheat flour equal to the weight of 3 small eggs (180 gr.)
- granulated sugar equal to the weight of 4 eggs (240 gr.)
- grated peel of 1 lemon or 3 drops almond essence
- 2 tbsp. washed butter for the molds
- granulated sugar for the molds

## Directions

1. Dry the flour and sift it.
2. Beat the egg yolks with the sugar until the mixture becomes white.
3. Add the flour together with the lightly grated lemon peel or almond essence.
4. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the batter.
5. Grease sponge cake molds or ordinary smooth pastry molds with washed butter, sprinkle with sugar and fill three-quarters with batter.
6. Place the cakes in a fairly hot oven to bake through and acquire a beautiful golden-brown color.
7. Turn out of the molds while still warm, sprinkle with sugar and let cool.
