## Ingredients

- 800 g pork tenderloin
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 3 dcl broth
- 1 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Clean the fillets, which weigh 2-4 hg each, and dry with a cloth wrung out in hot water.
2. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the fillets in, brown well on all sides and sprinkle with seasonings.
3. Add the boiling broth and cream, and let the meat cook slowly with a tight lid until tender, about 1/2 hour.
4. Cut the fillets into thin slices, arrange them on a warm platter so they appear whole.
5. Garnish with Russian peas and browned potatoes or with legumes.
6. Prepare the sauce (see recipe 429) and pour over the meat.
7. Serve with red currant jelly.
