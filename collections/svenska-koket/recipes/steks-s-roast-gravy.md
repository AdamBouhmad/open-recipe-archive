## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 5 dcl. meat stock
- 1 dcl. thick cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes
2. Dilute with the meat stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
3. If desired, add the cream and let the sauce come to a boil
4. Season with salt and white pepper
