## Ingredients

- 2½ cups flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 liter milk
- 3 hectograms salted pork or smoked ham

## Directions

1. Cut the pork into cubes and fry quite hard in a frying pan, then pour off with its fat.
2. Wipe the pan with clean paper so all salt is removed, then return the clear fat.
3. Prepare the pancake batter as above and pour into the pan.
4. Add the pork cubes and bake the pancake in a warm oven.
