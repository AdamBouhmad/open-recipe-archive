## Ingredients

- 600 g boneless veal loin
- 3 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 1 dcl broth
- 6 lemon slices
- 6 anchovy fillets
- 1 tbsp chopped capers

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and cut into 6 slices, which are flattened to half their thickness, trimmed to even, oblong cutlets, which are rubbed with the spices.
2. Brush them with the beaten eggs and turn them in the finely crushed breadcrumbs and fry in the butter until they are beautifully golden brown, or about 5 minutes on each side.
3. Place the schnitzel on a warm serving platter, whisk the pan with the broth, let the sauce come to a boil, taste it and pour carefully onto the platter.
4. On each schnitzel place a lemon slice, on top of this an anchovy fillet, in a ring, which is filled with capers.
5. Garnish with parsley and serve with browned potatoes as a breakfast dish.
