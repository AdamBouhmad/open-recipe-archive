## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal breast
- 1 tsp salt
- 1½ tbsp butter
- 2 hg boneless meat from the shoulder
- 1 red onion
- 1 liter broth (cooked from the bones)
- ½ tbsp butter for forcemeat
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ hg kidney fat
- 1 egg
- 2 parts thin cream
- ½ tbsp butter for sauce
- 1½ tbsp flour for sauce
- meat gravy
- ½ tsp soy sauce
- ¼ tsp white pepper
- 2 tbsp crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Wash the breast with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Carefully cut out the shoulder blade and ribs so the meat remains as whole as possible.
3. (Cook broth from the bones to use for moistening the roast during cooking.)
4. Cut 2 hg of the meat into cubes and grind 3 times through a meat grinder together with the crumbled kidney fat.
5. Pound the meat in a mortar with butter and blend with the seasonings, beaten eggs, cream, and crushed breadcrumbs.
6. Work the forcemeat until it becomes smooth and cohesive.
7. Rub the breast with salt, fill with the forcemeat, and sew it shut well.
8. Brown it together with the peeled onion in the heated butter, moisten with the boiling broth, and let it roast slowly with a tightly closed lid for 1 to 1½ hours.
9. Strain the meat gravy, skim it, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
