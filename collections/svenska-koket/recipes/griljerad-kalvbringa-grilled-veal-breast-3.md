## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg veal breast
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tablespoons salt (30 g)
- 10 white peppercorns
- 1 egg
- 1 deciliter crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the meat as in the preceding recipe, bone it out, brush it with the beaten egg and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs.
2. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the breast in with the outside up and brown it in a warm oven, while it is frequently basted with the butter in the pan.
3. Serve with browned butter, boiled potatoes and salads, or with cooked or stewed vegetables or with potato purée.
