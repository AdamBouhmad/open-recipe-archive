## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 250 gr. fine butter (equal weight to dough)
- 1 dcl. water (scant)
- 1/2 batch petits-choux (see recipe 999)
- candied orange segments (see recipe 1027)
- 1/2 batch vanilla cream with gelatin (see recipe 872)
- 6 dcl. extra thick cream
- carmine
- vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the fine butter dough as in recipe 33 and bake to a base with ring as in recipe 978. Bake the tart base beautifully golden brown in a hot oven.
2. Allow small petits-choux of walnut size to cool and fill with whipped cream, colored pink with carmine if desired.
3. Dip these petits-choux in sugar glaze and place them tightly against each other on the outer edge of the tart.
4. Between each petits-choux, on the inner edge of the ring, stand up the orange segments after dipping them in sugar glaze.
5. Fill the tart with the vanilla cream and garnish with cream whipped to stiff peaks.
6. If desired, half of the cream can be colored pink with carmine and the other half flavored with vanilla sugar.
7. Pipe the cream through a smooth pastry tube in beautiful peaks over the cream.
