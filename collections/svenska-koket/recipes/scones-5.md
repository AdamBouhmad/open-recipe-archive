## Ingredients

- 450 g. wheat flour
- 3 tsp. baking powder
- 70 g. cold butter
- 1 1/2 tsp. salt (8 g.)
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 g.)
- 3 dcl. cold milk

## Directions

1. Sift the flour with the baking powder in a bowl, distribute the butter in flakes in the flour and work it in with the fingertips. Salt, sugar and milk are added and the dough is worked quickly with a spoon. Turn out onto a floured work surface, cut into 5 parts. Each part is formed into a round, about 1 cm. thick cake, which is pinched and cut with a knife into 4—6 parts. The breads are laid on a buttered pan and baked at once in a warm oven. Served with butter for coffee or tea.
