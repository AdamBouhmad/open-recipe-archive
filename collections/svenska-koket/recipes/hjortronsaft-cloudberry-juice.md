## Ingredients

- 10 liters cloudberries
- 4 liters water
- 0.25 kg sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the berries. Place them with water and let them cook until they are completely broken down.
2. Pour the mass and let it cool somewhat. Strain the juice through cheesecloth and cook it with the sugar with skimming until it becomes glossy and is free from foam.
3. Bottle it hot or cold; cork the bottles and seal with resin.
