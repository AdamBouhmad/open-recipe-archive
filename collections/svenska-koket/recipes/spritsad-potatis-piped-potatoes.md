## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters potatoes, water, salt
- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons cream
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw, cook them and mash them with a fork or press them through a potato ricer.
2. Whisk the egg yolks with the cream and stir into the mash together with the seasonings and the cold butter.
3. Then press the purée through a piping bag with a coarse star tip onto the serving dish.
4. Piped potatoes are used mainly as a garnish for meat dishes, such as larded ox hare, cutlets etc.
5. The potato mash can also be piped out into an omelette pan, sprinkled with finely crushed rusks and put in the oven to get a nice brown color. It is then served with the smorgasbord.
