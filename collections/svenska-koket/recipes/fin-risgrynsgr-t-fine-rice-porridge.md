## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups rice groats
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3 liters milk
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons sugar

## Directions

1. Scald the rice groats and pour into a colander to drain
2. Melt the butter in a pot, pour in the milk and bring to a boil
3. Add the groats and let them cook in a covered pot about 3 hours
4. During cooking, stir the porridge occasionally to prevent burning and dilute if needed with a little more milk
5. Season and serve with crushed cinnamon
