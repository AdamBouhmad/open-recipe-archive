## Ingredients

- 212 gr. sweet almonds
- 2 whole eggs
- 212 gr. granulated sugar
- 50 gr. ground cinnamon
- 40 gr. granulated sugar for baking
- wheat flour (sifted)

## Directions

1. Dry the almonds well in a cloth and pass through an almond mill.
2. Pound it thereafter in a stone mortar with the eggs, one at a time.
3. Add the sugar and cinnamon and pound the mass.
4. Until it becomes even and supple. Place it on granulated sugar on the work surface and roll out into sticks of a finger's thickness; cut these into 15 cm long pieces.
5. Place the cakes on buttered and floured paper on a baking sheet and bake half-dry in a not too hot oven.
