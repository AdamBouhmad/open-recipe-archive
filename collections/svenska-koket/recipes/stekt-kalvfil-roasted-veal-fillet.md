## Ingredients

- 750 g veal fillet
- 1/2 hectogram lard
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 2 deciliters broth
- 2 deciliters thick cream (1-2 truffles)

## Directions

1. Clean the fillets, dry them with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and lard with fine lard strips in even rows.
2. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the fillets in, brown nicely on all sides and sprinkle with a mixture of salt and pepper.
3. Gradually add the boiling broth and cream and let the fillets roast slowly with the lid tightly closed until tender, or 30-40 minutes, during which time they should be basted often and turned.
4. Cut them, lay them together again so they retain their form, place on a warm serving platter and garnish with salad or parsley.
5. Strain the sauce, skim it, heat it and taste it, and if desired add the truffle cut into strips and pour over the meat.
6. Serve with peas or French potatoes.
7. If a thinner sauce is desired, it can be thickened with a little butter and flour.
8. The lard can be omitted and the fillets prepared in the same way otherwise.
