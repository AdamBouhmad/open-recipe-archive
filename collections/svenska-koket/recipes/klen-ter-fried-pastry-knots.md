## Ingredients

- 15 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 egg white
- 250 gr. powdered sugar
- 2 1/2 dcl. extra thick cream
- 1/2 wine glass cognac
- 750 gr. wheat flour
- 1 1/2 kg. melted lard for frying
- 1 tbsp. brandy for frying

## Directions

1. Stir the eggs and sugar for 1 hour in the same direction.
2. Thereafter add the cream beaten to stiff peaks, the cognac, and lastly the flour.
3. Work the dough very well so that it becomes tough.
4. Cover it and let it rest overnight, otherwise it is very difficult to roll out.
5. Roll out the dough to 2 mm. thickness and cut with a pastry cutter into squares, which are folded at the corners, strips which are twisted, or into oblong cakes, on whose middle a slit is made, and thereby one end of the cake is drawn through so that a knot is formed in the middle.
6. Heat the lard together with 1 tbsp. brandy, whereupon a raw potato slice is placed in it (when the potato slice becomes dark brown, a new one is placed).
7. Place the pastries—not too many at once—and cook until light yellow, lay them on gray paper to drain and fan them with a wing or with a bellows, if you have one.
