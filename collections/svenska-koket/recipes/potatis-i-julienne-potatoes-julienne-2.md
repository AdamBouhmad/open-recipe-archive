## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 1 kilogram fat
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)

## Directions

1. The potatoes should be oblong and of even size.
2. Rinse, brush well clean, peel thinly and cut into thread-fine strips, which are placed in water as they are cut.
3. Dry well in a cloth, cook as in the preceding recipe and should be properly crisp.
4. Serve with poultry and game.
