## Ingredients

- 5 kg loaf sugar
- 2.5 liters water
- 5 kg pears
- 5 kg apples
- 5 kg plums

## Directions

1. From the sugar and water cook a clear syrup, which is set out to cool.
2. Peel the pears and apples, cut them into four parts, and remove the cores.
3. Place them in the sugar syrup and allow them to cook 30-40 minutes under constant stirring.
4. Place the plums in boiling water, remove the skins, remove the pits.
5. Add them to the other fruit, and allow everything to cook 2.5-3 hours, all under constant stirring.
