## Ingredients

- 1 large, fattened young goose
- 1 lemon
- 2 small tablespoons salt
- 2 hectograms prunes
- 2 liters fresh or 3 hectograms dried apples
- 1.5-2 liters broth or water

## Directions

1. Open and clean the goose, rinse well and dry with a cloth. Rub inside and outside with the lemon; sprinkle a little salt inside.
2. Rinse the fruit (remove blossom ends) and fill the goose with it, then sew it shut neatly and fasten the legs well along the sides.
3. Place on a long pan and put in a hot oven to brown slowly under frequent basting with the fat that forms. When well browned, sprinkle with the remaining salt, add the boiling liquid and roast for 3-4 hours, basting every ten minutes.
4. Cut the goose and place it whole again on the serving platter, garnished with the apples and prunes.
5. Strain the sauce, skim it and serve as gravy. It can also be thickened like a regular sauce if desired.
