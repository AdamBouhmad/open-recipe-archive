## Ingredients

- 1 pail strömming (Baltic herring)
- 1 large bunch parsley
- 1 bunch dill
- salt
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Clean the strömming, fillet it, remove the backbone, and rub it lightly with salt
2. Chop the parsley and dill, and place approximately 1 teaspoon of this mixture on each strömming
3. Lay them against each other two by two with the skin side out
4. Coat the prepared strömming in egg and crushed rusks and fry in butter
5. Place on a warm serving dish and serve
