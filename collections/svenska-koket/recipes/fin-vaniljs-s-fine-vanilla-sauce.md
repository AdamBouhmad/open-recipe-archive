## Ingredients

- 1 vanilla pod
- 1 dcl. water
- 4 egg yolks
- 50 gr. caster sugar
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Place the vanilla pod in a saucepan, add water, cover with a lid and let the vanilla simmer slowly until the water is almost completely reduced.
2. Place the egg yolks and sugar in a bowl and whisk for 20 minutes.
3. Whip the cream in the saucepan, bring to a boil, remove the vanilla pod and pour the cream over the eggs while whisking vigorously.
4. Let the sauce simmer over the heat with continued whisking until thick and foamy; pour it out and whisk until cold.
