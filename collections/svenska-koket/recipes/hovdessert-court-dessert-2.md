## Ingredients

- 4 small egg whites
- 200 grams powdered sugar
- 1-2 tablespoons hot water
- 100 grams sweetened chocolate (or 75 grams unsweetened chocolate and sugar to taste)
- 1 hectogram sweet almonds
- 1/2 liter thick cream
- 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, and continue beating for about 1.5 hours.
2. Then carefully fold in the sifted powdered sugar, and shape the mixture on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets into large meringues.
3. Place them immediately in gentle oven heat and bake very slowly.
4. When they are dry and release from the sheet, remove them and let them cool.
5. Arrange them high on a bowl.
6. Dissolve the chocolate in a little hot water and let it boil to a proper thickness.
7. When the chocolate is cold, pour it over the meringues.
8. Blanch the almonds, cut them into strips and sprinkle over the chocolate.
9. Just before serving, whip the cream until stiff peaks form together with the vanilla sugar, and garnish the pyramid with this.
