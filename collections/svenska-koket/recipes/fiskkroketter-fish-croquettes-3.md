## Ingredients

- 8 hectograms raw fish or 5 deciliters cooked fish in cubes
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr.)
- 2 deciliter fish stock
- 2 deciliter cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 sheets gelatin
- salt, white pepper
- 4 tablespoon flour for breading
- 2 eggs for breading
- salt for breading
- 2 teaspoon cooking oil
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 1 kg. lard for frying

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the fish stock and cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
2. Gently stir in the fish pieces together with the whipped egg yolks and the gelatin that has been rinsed and dissolved in 1 tablespoon hot water.
3. Season the mixture with spices and treat as in recipe no. 49.
4. Form the croquettes into oblong shapes.
5. Serve as a side course with beef or mushroom sauce.
