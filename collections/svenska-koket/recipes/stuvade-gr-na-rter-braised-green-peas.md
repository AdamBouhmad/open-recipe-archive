## Ingredients

- 3 cups dried green peas
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Clean the peas and soak the day before.
2. Place in cold, soft water and cook slowly until soft.
3. Season with salt and stir in the cold butter.
