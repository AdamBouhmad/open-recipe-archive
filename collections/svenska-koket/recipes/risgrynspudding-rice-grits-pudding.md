## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. Carolina rice grits (2 hg.)
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 1/2 liters milk
- 1/2 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 20 sweet almonds
- 10 bitter almonds
- 40 gr. common raisins
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 tbsp. butter for the mold (10 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the rice grits as in no. 794.
2. Pour the grits and immediately mix with the cold butter, seasonings, the blanched and ground almonds, and the well-rinsed, parboiled and pitted raisins.
3. When the grits are cold, stir in the egg yolks and the stiffly beaten egg whites.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pie mold and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 1 hour.
5. Serve warm with jam, compote or fruit sauce.
