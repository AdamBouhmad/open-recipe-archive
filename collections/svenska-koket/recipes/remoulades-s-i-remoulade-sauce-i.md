## Ingredients

- Real mayonnaise
- 2 tsp French mustard
- 1/2 tbsp finely chopped pickled cucumber
- 1/2 tbsp chopped capers
- 1 tsp finely chopped parsley
- 1 tsp chervil
- 1 tsp tarragon
- 1/2 tsp anchovy essence

## Directions

1. Prepare real mayonnaise as in recipe no. 442 and add the mustard, pickled cucumber, capers, parsley, chervil, tarragon and anchovy essence
2. Mix the sauce well and taste carefully
3. Serve with lobster, fried fish, cold meat and chicken
4. If desired, can be colored green with spinach coloring
