## Ingredients

- 1 grouse
- water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 5 white peppercorns
- 1 liter of broth
- 8–10 sheets gelatin
- 2 teaspoons soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons meat extract
- 2 eggs
- 1 hard-boiled egg, parsley

## Directions

1. Pluck the bird, singe it, clean it, and cook it in just enough water to cover it.
2. When the liquid comes to a boil, skim it carefully, and add the spices.
3. Let the bird cook until it feels completely tender.
4. Separate the meat from the bones and cut it into nice pieces.
5. Heat the broth; add the soy sauce, meat extract, and dissolved gelatin.
6. When the mixture is warm, whisk in the eggs, and bring the broth to a good boil while stirring.
7. Strain through a napkin or cheesecloth wrung out in hot water.
8. Pour the clear aspic onto the bottom of a rinsed aspic mold and set out to set.
9. Garnish the mold with hard-boiled eggs and parsley.
10. Place the finest pieces on the bottom and sides.
11. Fill the mold with the remaining meat, and carefully pour the rest of the aspic over.
12. Set the aspic in a cold place to set.
