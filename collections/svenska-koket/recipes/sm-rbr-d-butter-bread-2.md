## Ingredients

- 210 grams butter
- 25 grams granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 315 grams wheat flour
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 50 grams pearl sugar
- 50 grams sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Wash the butter, sift the flour and work all ingredients well together on a pastry board
2. Roll the dough out to approximately 2 millimeters thickness and cut out small cakes with a measure
3. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks
4. Brush the cakes with this and sprinkle with chopped almonds and sugar
5. Bake in weak oven heat
