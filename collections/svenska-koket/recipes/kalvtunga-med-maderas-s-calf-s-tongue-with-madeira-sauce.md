## Ingredients

- 1 calf's tongue
- 2 liters water
- 1 msk. salt
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 tsk. salt
- white pepper
- 3 kkp good broth
- 1 tsk. soy
- 4 dcl. meat gravy for sauce
- 1 tsk. mondamin flour
- 1 msk. Madeira
- 2 msk. finely cut, browned mushrooms

## Directions

1. Brush the tongue well, rinse it, place it in the boiling water with the salt added, and let it simmer until the skin can be easily removed.
2. Brown the butter in an iron pot, place the tongue in and brown beautifully on all sides, salt and dilute with the broth, adding the soy.
3. Let it simmer slowly until it is tender, about 1 hour, and baste every ten minutes so it does not dry out.
4. Cut the tongue and place on a serving dish.
