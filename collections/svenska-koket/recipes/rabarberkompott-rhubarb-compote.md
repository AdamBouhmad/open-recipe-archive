## Ingredients

- 8 parts sugar (255 grams)
- 2 parts water
- 6 beautiful rhubarb stalks (approximately 900 grams)

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar and water together to make a clear, syrupy liquid
2. Rinse and dry the rhubarb stalks, peel thinly, and cut into 3 centimeter pieces
3. Place the thickest pieces first in the sugar liquid and cook until tender but not falling apart
4. Remove pieces with a silver fork as they become soft and place in a compote bowl
5. The smaller pieces require barely any cooking before becoming tender
6. Cook the syrup until it is clear and syrupy, then pour over the rhubarb
7. Serve cold with whipped cream
