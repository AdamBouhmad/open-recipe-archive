## Ingredients

- 2 liters red currants
- 2 kg sugar
- rhubarb (amount not specified)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rhubarb and dry it, then cut into pieces.
2. Rinse the red currants and remove the stems.
3. Place the rhubarb and currants together in water and allow to cook with stirring until completely soft.
4. Pour the mass into cheesecloth to drain naturally.
5. Return the clear juice to the heat together with the sugar and allow to cook with constant skimming until completely clear and somewhat syrupy.
6. Bottle the juice, cork the bottles and seal with resin.
