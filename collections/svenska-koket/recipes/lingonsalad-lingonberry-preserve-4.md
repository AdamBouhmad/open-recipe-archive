## Ingredients

- 1 kg large lingonberries
- 15 cloves
- 750 gr. loaf sugar
- 3 dcl. water

## Directions

1. Sort through the large lingonberries, rinse them, let them drain, then prick them with sewing needles threaded through a cork, and lay them in alternating layers with the cloves in a jar.
2. Boil the sugar and water to a clear, simmering syrup, which is poured hot over the berries.
3. Cover the jar with a quadruple cloth and plate and let it stand until the following day.
4. If the syrup is then too thin, pour it off, boil it up, and pour it cold over the berries.
5. Bind with parchment paper.
