## Ingredients

- 1 litre brussels sprouts
- 3/4 litre water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 4-5 decilitres boiling broth
- 1-2 egg yolks
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon sugar (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the sprouts as in the preceding recipe and let drain on cloth.
2. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes.
3. Add the broth and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
4. Add the whisked egg yolk, let the sauce simmer, add the sprouts, and season the stew with spices.
5. Serve garnished with browned potatoes as a single dish or with roasted meat.
6. Brussels sprouts may also be stewed like cabbage.
