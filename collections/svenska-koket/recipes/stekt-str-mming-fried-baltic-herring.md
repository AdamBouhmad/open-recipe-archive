## Ingredients

- 30 Baltic herrings
- 1 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 80 grams butter for frying
- For the sauce: 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 grams)
- 1/2 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, remove the backbone, scrape away the dark skin carefully and rinse very well and wrap in a fish towel.
2. Rub it with salt, dip in the beaten egg and turn in a mixture of the flour and crushed rusks.
3. Heat the butter and fry the herrings until golden.
4. Make the sauce with butter, parsley and lemon juice and serve with the fried herring.
