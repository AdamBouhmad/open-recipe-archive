## Ingredients

- 1 fresh lobster weighing 4-5 hectograms
- 2.5 liters water
- 1 hectogram coarse salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Greensalad or parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Bring the water with coarse salt to a boil.
2. Place the lobster head-first into the boiling water (it dies immediately) and cook for 15-20 minutes.
3. Let the lobster cool on its back.
4. Just before serving, split the lobster in half lengthwise.
5. Carefully crush the claws.
6. Remove the intestine and stomach sac, which are located near the head.
7. Arrange the lobster on a platter and garnish with greensalad or parsley.
8. Serve with oil, vinegar, and pepper.
