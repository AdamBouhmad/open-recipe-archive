## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lit. good, loose apples
- 50 gr. sprinkled sugar
- 3 two-öre almond breads
- 1 1/2 dcl. lukewarm milk
- 120 gr. butter
- 120 gr. sugar
- 2 whole eggs
- 2 egg yolks
- grated peel of 1/2 lemon
- 25 gr. sweet almonds (about 25 pieces)
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them, remove the cores with a tin tube, and cut them into thin slices, which are laid alternately with the sugar in a bowl and allowed to stand for about 1 hour.
2. Cut the almond breads into pieces and place in the milk.
3. Stir butter and sugar until foamy, add the whipped eggs and egg yolks along with the lemon peel, the blanched and ground almonds, and finally stir in the soaked, squeezed-out bread.
4. Work the dough well and lay it alternately with the apple slices in a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pie mold - 3 layers of dough and 2 layers of apple slices.
5. Bake the pudding in ordinary oven heat for about 1 hour, or until the apples are soft and the dough feels dry when tested with a whisk.
6. Serve warm with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
