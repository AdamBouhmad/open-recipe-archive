## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg. flour
- 1 part water
- 1 tablespoon brandy
- 1/2 egg
- 350 gr. butter (equal weight to the dough)
- 1 1/2-3/2 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 parts fish stock
- 3 parts cream
- 1/2 liter cooked, deboned and finely cut fish
- white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as ordinary butter dough. When it is ready, roll it out to 1 cm. thickness, and with a measure cut out small round cakes. With a knife cut small lids in these, but not deeper than 1/2 cm. (half the cake's thickness).
2. Brush the ring outside the lid with egg white, and to this fasten a strip of dough, cut with points. Bake the bouchées thereafter in a very hot oven.
3. Lift off the already cut lid, and fill the bouchées with fish stewing, whereafter the lid is laid on again.
4. The bouchées should be served very hot.
