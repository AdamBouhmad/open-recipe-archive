## Ingredients

- 450 grams wheat flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 70 grams cold butter
- 1.5 teaspoons salt (8 grams)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 grams)
- 3 deciliters cold milk

## Directions

1. Sift the flour with baking powder into a bowl, distribute the butter in flakes in the flour and work it in with the fingertips.
2. Add salt, sugar and milk and work the dough quickly with a spoon.
3. Place on a floured baking board, divide into 5 parts. Shape each part into a round cake about 1 centimeter thick, prick with a fork and cut into 4-6 pieces.
4. Place the breads on a buttered baking sheet and bake immediately in a hot oven.
5. Serve with butter for coffee or tea.
