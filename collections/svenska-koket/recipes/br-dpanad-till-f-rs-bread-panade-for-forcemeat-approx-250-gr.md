## Ingredients

- 1 hg. crumb of white bread
- 1/2 tbsp. salt (8 gr.)
- 1 1/2 dcl. boiling milk
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Free the white bread from the crusts, weigh it and place it in a saucepan together with the salt.
2. Pour the boiling milk over it and when the bread has absorbed all the milk, place the pan over the fire and stir the panade with a wooden spoon or ladle until it is thick and even.
3. Transfer the panade to a buttered dish and brush lightly with butter on top so that it does not dry while cooling.
