## Ingredients

- 18 Baltic herrings
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon French mustard
- 2 tablespoons food oil
- 1 deciliter vinegar
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped dill

## Directions

1. Clean the herring, rinse well, fillet, remove the backbone, and scrape away the dark skin carefully.
2. Rinse the fillets, wrap in a fish towel and place in a bowl layered with the spices.
3. Mix the mustard with the oil and vinegar and pour this marinade over the herring.
4. Sprinkle the dill over and let the herring stand in a cold place for 1 hour. It is then cured.
5. Serve at the smorgasbord.
