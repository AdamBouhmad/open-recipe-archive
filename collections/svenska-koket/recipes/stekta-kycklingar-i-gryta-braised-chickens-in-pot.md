## Ingredients

- 3 chickens about 1/2 kg. each
- 1 bunch parsley
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.) for roasting
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 4 deciliters thick cream
- salt
- sugar (optional)
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Heat the 3 tablespoons butter in a pot and brown the chickens with the bunch of parsley and salt until golden on all sides.
2. For the sauce, make a roux with the 2 tablespoons flour and butter, then add the thick cream.
3. Season the sauce with salt, sugar if desired, and white pepper to taste.
4. Serve the braised chickens with the sauce.
