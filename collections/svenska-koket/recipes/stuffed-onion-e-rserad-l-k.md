## Ingredients

- 2 large or 3 smaller Portuguese onions
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 2 hectograms beef
- 1/2 hectogram veal
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 grams)
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 deciliters cream
- 1 tablespoon chopped mushrooms
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 grams) for frying
- 3 deciliters broth

## Directions

1. Peel the onion and blanch about 15 minutes, or until almost tender.
2. Prepare the dish as in the preceding recipe and serve at the smörgåsbord.
