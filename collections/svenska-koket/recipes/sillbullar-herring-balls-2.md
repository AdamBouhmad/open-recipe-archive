## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 large salted herring
- 3/4 lit. potatoes
- 2 hg. cooked or fried meat
- 1 tsk. potato flour (5 gr.)
- 1 dcl. cream or milk
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 4 msk. crushed rusks
- 1 hg. butter or fine lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the herring as in the preceding recipe and chop finely.
2. Cook the potatoes, allow to cool, peel and chop.
3. Chop the meat finely and mix with the herring, potatoes, potato flour, cream and pepper.
4. Work the mixture until it becomes even and smooth, and form with two spoons into small round balls, which are flattened slightly and rolled in crushed rusks.
5. Fry golden brown in the butter or lard and serve with currant sauce.
