## Ingredients

- 3 kilograms rhubarb
- 1 liter red currants
- 2 liters water
- 650 grams sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the rhubarb, cut it into pieces
2. Combine the rhubarb and rinsed currants in the water and cook until soft
3. Strain and let drain until the following day
4. Weigh the juice, dissolve the sugar in the juice
5. Proceed as in recipe 1046
