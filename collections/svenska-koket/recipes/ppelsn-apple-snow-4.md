## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters good, tart apples (approximately 800 grams)
- 3 egg whites
- 135 grams granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and dry the apples, place them in a small roasting pan and bake in the oven until they are soft.
2. Pass the apples through a hair sieve.
3. Let the pulp cool, then stir it together with the egg whites and sugar until it is white and fluffy, or for approximately 2 hours.
4. Place in a compote bowl and let stand on ice for 1 hour.
5. Serve with biscuits or macarons. The mixture can also be baked in the oven as a soufflé.
