## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg tender French beans
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 15 grams salt

## Directions

1. Clean the beans and rinse in cold water, then drain on linen.
2. Place them in boiling salted water and boil rapidly, without a lid, until they are tender.
3. Remove them with a slotted spoon and place on a plate with a folded serviette.
4. Serve with melted butter or hollandaise sauce.
5. Note: Canned French beans can be used in the same manner: place them in boiling salted water and remove them as soon as it returns to a boil. Wax beans are prepared and served in the same way.
