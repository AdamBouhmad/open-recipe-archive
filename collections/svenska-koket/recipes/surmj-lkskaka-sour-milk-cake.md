## Ingredients

- 6 hg. rye sift flour
- 2 small tsp. bicarbonate
- 1 small tsp. baking powder
- 1/2 liter sour milk
- 2 dcl. syrup
- 1/2 tbsp. caraway or 1/2 tbsp. fennel and 1/2 tbsp. anise
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 tbsp. wheat flour (20 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix well with the bicarbonate and baking powder.
2. Whisk the milk to stiff foam and mix into the flour together with the syrup and the finely ground spices.
3. Pour the dough into a well-buttered and floured, oblong bread form or baking pan and place immediately to bake in regular oven heat for about 1 hour.
4. Test with a whisk to see if it is thoroughly baked.
5. Use as sandwich bread.
