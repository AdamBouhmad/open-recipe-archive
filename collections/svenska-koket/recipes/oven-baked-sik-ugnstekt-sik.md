## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg fat sik
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 3 parsley sprigs
- 1/2 tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1/2 egg
- 80 g butter
- 1 1/2 deciliter French wine
- 1 1/2 deciliter water
- 1 deciliter bouillon
- Pepper and dill sprigs, if desired

## Directions

1. If desired, add pepper and some dill sprigs.
2. The salmon pieces are arranged together with the flesh sides against each other in such a way that the thick side of the salmon is placed against the thin side.
3. It is then placed in a mild press between two cutting boards overnight and the salmon is thereafter ready.
4. It is cut into slices 1/2 cm thick, which are arranged beautifully on a platter, garnished with salmon skin, lightly heated on the stove or fried, and fresh dill sprigs.
5. Serve with cooking oil, vinegar, powdered sugar, French mustard, salt and pepper.
6. Gravlax can be cut into thin slices and lightly fried in an almost dry frying pan. Serve as above.
