## Ingredients

- 1 can chanterelles (1/4 liter)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1/2 deciliter chanterelle broth
- 3 deciliters cream
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 3 deciliters milk
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the chanterelles in cloth, cut into slices and sauté in butter until clear.
2. Squeeze the mushroom out of the butter with a spoon.
3. Sauté the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, then add the chanterelle broth and cream, and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
4. Place the mushroom in and season the stew with the spices, then place it in a buttered omelet mold.
5. Whisk the egg mixture well and pour it over.
6. Bake the omelet in the oven and serve at the smorgasbord.
7. Alternative: instead of the stew you can use smoked ham cut into cubes and sautéed in butter, or grated cheese.
