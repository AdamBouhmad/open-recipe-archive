## Ingredients

- 2 large rutabagas
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.) for blanching
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 1 tsp. salt (5 gr.)
- 4 dcl. boiling secondary broth
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the rutabagas and cut them into fairly large cubes or oblong pieces. Blanch them for 3-4 minutes with the lid off.
2. Drain them in a colander and let them dry on a cloth.
3. Brown the butter in an iron saucepan, add the rutabagas, sprinkle with sugar and salt and brown them, stirring often, until they get a nice color.
4. Then add the broth and let the rutabagas simmer slowly until they are completely tender, about 1 1/2 hours.
5. Season with a little pepper if desired and serve with salt meat.
