## Ingredients

- 120 grams butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 6 parts cream
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. The butter is stirred in a pot until it is soft and white
2. The egg yolks are whisked with the cream
3. The pot is placed over heat, the eggs and cream are added with constant whisking
4. The whisking is continued until the sauce becomes foamy
5. Then the pot is lifted off, and the sauce is seasoned with a few drops of lemon juice
