## Ingredients

- 2 1/2-3 liters half-grown field peas
- 1 liter water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar (8 gr.)
- 120 gr. butter for serving

## Directions

1. Rinse the pea pods well, let them drain on a cloth, place them in boiling water seasoned with salt and sugar, and let them simmer slowly with a tight lid until they are completely tender, which takes approximately 2 hours.
2. Arrange them with a slotted spoon on a torn napkin, cover with a napkin and serve very hot with stirred butter (see recipe no. 405).
