## Ingredients

- 3 liters cherries
- 6-8 hectograms crushed sugar
- 1 liter water

## Directions

1. Clean the berries and remove the stems.
2. Rinse the fruit carefully and let it drain very well.
3. Cook it as ordinary compote for 20 minutes.
