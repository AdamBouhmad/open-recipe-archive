## Ingredients

- 1 litre large, beautiful clusters of red or white currants
- 2 egg whites
- 1 1/2 decilitre caster sugar (128 grams)

## Directions

1. Place the clusters in a colander, pour cold water over them and let them drain on cloth.
2. Whip the egg whites until light foam forms.
3. Dip the currant clusters first in the egg white, then in the sugar, until they are evenly glazed.
4. Place on a sieve and let them stand in gentle oven heat until they are dry.
5. Serve as dessert or at a buffet.
