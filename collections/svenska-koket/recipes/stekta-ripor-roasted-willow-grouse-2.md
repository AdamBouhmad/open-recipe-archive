## Ingredients

- 3 willow grouse
- 1 hectogram lard
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ½-1 liter broth
- ½ tablespoon butter
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- meat dripping
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck, singe, clean out and dry the grouse. Lard them with lard cut into fine strips and truss. Heat the butter in a roasting pan, place the birds in it and brown well. Salt them and add broth.
2. Strain and skim the meat dripping, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules.
