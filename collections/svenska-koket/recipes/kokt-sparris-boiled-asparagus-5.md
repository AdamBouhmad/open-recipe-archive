## Ingredients

- 50 fresh asparagus spears
- 2 liters water
- 30 gr. salt
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Asparagus is harvested from mid-May to the end of June.
2. It can be stored for 4-5 days in a cellar if bundled in bundles and covered with sand.
3. The largest are served boiled as a side dish; the smaller ones are used for soups and stews.
4. Scrape the asparagus carefully with a sharp knife toward the tip without touching it; only the outermost skin is removed.
5. Rinse the asparagus gently in cold water and bind it into five bundles with the tips pointing in the same direction.
6. Boil the water with the salt and skim well.
7. Place the asparagus in the boiling water and cook for 15-20 minutes, depending on the quality of the asparagus.
8. Remove immediately and drain on a sieve or cloth.
9. Place the asparagus on a plate with a folded napkin and cut the strings.
10. Serve very hot with melted butter.
