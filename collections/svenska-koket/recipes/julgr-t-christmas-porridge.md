## Ingredients

- 2 parts Carolina rice groats (2 hg.)
- 1 1/4 liters water
- 75 gr. butter
- 4 parts thick cream
- 1 tbsp. salt (15 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice groats, scald them, place them in boiling water and let them cook until soft, or about 1 hour.
2. Then add the cold butter and the cream beaten to stiff peaks, after which the porridge is heated but must not boil.
3. Season the porridge with salt and sugar and serve with good milk.
