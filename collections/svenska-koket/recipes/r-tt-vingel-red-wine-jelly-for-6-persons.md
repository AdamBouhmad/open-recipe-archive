## Ingredients

- 8 parts water
- 2 parts red wine
- 8 parts port wine
- juice of 1 lemon
- 250 gr. sugar
- 20 gr. white gelatin
- 20 gr. red gelatin

## Directions

1. Pour the water, wine, lemon juice and sugar into a saucepan and place over heat.
2. When the mixture has become warm, stir in the rinsed gelatin sheets, and allow everything to come to a brief boil while whisking.
3. Strain the jelly through double cheesecloth into a slightly warmed jelly mold and set out to set.
4. If fine wines are used, no clarifying agent is needed. Otherwise, clarify the jelly as in the preceding recipe.
