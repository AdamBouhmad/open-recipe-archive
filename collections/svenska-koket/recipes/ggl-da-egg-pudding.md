## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 cup milk
- salt
- sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. The eggs are whisked together with the milk and seasonings, and the mixture is poured into a buttered baking pan or heatproof porcelain mold
2. The egg pudding is placed in a water bath in the oven to set for 20-30 minutes, or until completely coagulated
