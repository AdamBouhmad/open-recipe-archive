## Ingredients

- 3 parts syrup
- 350 gr. butter
- 400 gr. brown sugar
- 3 parts thick cream
- 2 tbsp. ground ginger
- 1 tbsp. bicarbonate
- 30 drops cedrat oil
- 1 1/8 kg. wheat flour
- Brown sugar for baking

## Directions

1. Stir the syrup cold together with the sugar, spices and the washed butter until a smooth mass, or about 1/2 hour.
2. Whip the cream to stiff foam and stir in gently.
3. Sift the flour with the bicarbonate and work in.
4. When the dough is so firm that it can be handled, knead in the remaining flour on the baking board with the rolling pin.
5. Let the dough then stand until the next day.
6. Roll it out to 1 cm. thickness.
7. Now sprinkle brown sugar on the baking board under the cake, and then roll this out to about 4 mm. thickness.
8. From the dough cut out small round cookies, which are brushed with water and baked a beautiful brown.
