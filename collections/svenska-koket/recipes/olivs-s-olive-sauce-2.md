## Ingredients

- 9 preserved olives
- 6 deciliters good meat stock or broth
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- salt
- sugar (optional)
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks (optional)
- 1 deciliter thick cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel the olives into even rings from the pits; cook these pits in the meat stock for 10 minutes so that the sauce will have a stronger olive flavor.
2. Brown the butter and flour together for 2 minutes.
3. Strain the meat stock from the pits and add it gradually to the roux.
4. Add the olives and let the sauce cook, stirring, for 10 minutes.
5. Season with the spices and thicken, if desired, with the beaten egg yolks and cream.
6. Serve with boiled veal or lamb breast.
