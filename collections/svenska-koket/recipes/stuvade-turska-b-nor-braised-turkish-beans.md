## Ingredients

- 1 liter salted Turkish beans
- water
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 3 cups milk
- 2 parts cream
- 1 egg yolk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the beans with fresh water. If very salty, soak in water the day before.
2. Place in cold water. When the water boils, drain it and add fresh cold water. Repeat until the beans are properly salted.
3. Let them cook until soft, then strain the stock.
4. Fry together the butter and flour, thin with milk and cream, and let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes.
5. Remove from heat and finish with the egg yolk.
6. Add the beans and season the stew.
7. The cream and egg yolk can be omitted; use a little more milk instead.
