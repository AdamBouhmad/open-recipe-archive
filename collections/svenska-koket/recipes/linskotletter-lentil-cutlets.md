## Ingredients

- 2 cups lentils
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 1 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 cup corn flakes
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon seasoning salt
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.) for frying

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the lentils, place them in soft, cold water and cook until mushy.
2. Peel the onion, cut it into cubes and brown it in the butter.
3. Pass the lentil mash twice through a meat grinder together with the crushed breadcrumbs and onion.
4. Cook the corn flakes for a few minutes in 1/2 liter of water; when cooled, stir them into the lentil mash together with the beaten eggs.
5. Season the mass and form into small cutlets, which are fried until nicely brown in the butter.
6. Serve with stewed pálstemackor or fried potatoes as a breakfast or dinner dish.
