## Ingredients

- 15 egg yolks
- 1 1/2 egg whites
- 250 gr. icing sugar
- 2 1/2 dcl. extra thick cream
- 1/2 glass cognac
- 750 gr. wheat flour
- 1 1/2 kg. melted lard
- 1 tbsp. brandy

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar for 1 hour in the same direction, then add the cream beaten to stiff peaks, the cognac, and finally the flour.
2. Work the dough very well so that it becomes tough.
3. Cover and let rest overnight, otherwise it is very difficult to roll.
4. Roll out to 2 mm. thickness and cut with a pastry wheel into squares, which are folded at the corners, bands that are twisted, or into oblong cakes, on the center of which a slit is made, and through this one end of the cake is drawn so that a knot is formed in the middle.
5. Heat the lard together with 1 tbsp. brandy, then place a raw potato slice in it (when the potato slice becomes dark brown, place a new one in).
