## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp butter
- 6 tbsp flour
- 4 cups milk
- 8 bitter almonds
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 eggs
- 0.5 hg sweet almonds for garnish
- 1.5 bunches chopped parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Mix butter and flour together, gradually add the milk a little at a time, and let the mixture cook for 15 minutes
2. Remove the pot from the side of the fire; add the blanched and grated bitter almond, sugar, and beaten eggs
3. Let the klimp simmer over the fire until it becomes thick
4. Cool it in a water-rinsed mold and turn it out once cooled
5. If serving with fruit soup, garnish with blanched sweet almonds cut into strips
6. If using with broth, garnish with chopped parsley
