## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 200 grams sweet almonds
- 1/2 cup potato flour
- 1/2 tablespoon washed butter for the pan
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Separate the eggs.
2. Beat the yolks with the sugar for two hours.
3. Add the blanched and finely chopped almonds along with the sifted potato flour, a little at a time with gentle stirring.
4. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Pour the batter immediately into a buttered and rusk-sprinkled pan or mold and bake in moderate oven heat.
6. When the cake is done, turn it out and let it cool.
7. Cut it into two layers, place lemon cream or applesauce between them.
8. Glaze and finally garnish with fruit and crystallized sugar.
