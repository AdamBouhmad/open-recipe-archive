## Ingredients

- 2 hg flour
- 3 dcl water
- 1-2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tsk salt
- 1/2 tsk sugar
- 2 tsk vegetable oil
- 3/4 liter vegetable oil or 3/4 kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, water, egg yolks, salt, sugar, and vegetable oil.
2. Heat the oil or lard for frying to approximately 160 degrees C.
3. Drop small portions of the batter into the hot fat and fry until golden brown.
4. Remove and drain on paper.
