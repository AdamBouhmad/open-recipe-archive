## Ingredients

- 350 gr. dried apricots
- 1.3 litres water
- 125 gr. sugar
- 3 1/2 decilitres of the strained liquid
- 3-4 leaves gelatin
- the strained apricots

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots well and cook in the water, with sugar added, until soft.
2. Strain off the liquid and pass the apricots through a sieve.
3. Rinse the gelatin in cold water and cut into pieces.
