## Ingredients

- 5 hg. boneless beef
- 1 1/2 dcl. meat sauce
- 1 hg. flour
- 3/4 dcl. water (scant)
- 150 gr. butter

## Directions

1. Slice the beef into thin, even slices and heat the sauce somewhat.
2. Prepare the butter pastry as in recipe no. 34, roll out to 1/2 cm. thickness and line a smooth or fluted baking pan with it.
3. The dough should hang 2 cm. over the edge of the pan.
4. Place the slices in the pan and pour the meat sauce over them.
5. Fold the dough edge that hangs over the pan over the meat and place a lid of dough on top.
6. Garnish the dough lid with figures of dough and brush with beaten egg white.
7. Place the pastry in a hot oven and it is ready when the butter pastry is baked.
8. Serve with meat sauce.
