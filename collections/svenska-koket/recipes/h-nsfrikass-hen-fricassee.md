## Ingredients

- 2 young hens of about 3 kg each
- 1 lemon
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt (23 gr.)
- 1 bay leaf
- 6 white peppercorns
- 1 clove
- 1 small piece Portuguese onion
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter for sauce (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 6 deciliters hen broth
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 2–3 egg yolks
- salt, white pepper, and sugar to taste

## Directions

1. The hens are plucked, singed, and drawn (see p. 299 et seq.), rinsed very thoroughly, and each hen is cut into 8 pieces, which are left to lie in fresh water for 3–4 hours.
2. They are taken out, wrapped in a cloth, put into boiling water, which is well skimmed when it comes to a boil, after which the spices and onion are added and the meat is allowed to simmer slowly with a tight lid until tender, or 1 1/2–2 hours.
