## Ingredients

- 1 medium cabbage head
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 4-5 cups cabbage broth or milk
- salt, sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the cabbage core and also the outer leaves, and cut the cabbage head into 6-8 pieces; let these soak in water mixed with vinegar for about 1/2 hour (calculating 1 tablespoon vinegar for each liter of water).
2. Remove the cabbage and blanch it in salted water without a lid for about 10 minutes; then remove it and place it in new boiling water (just over 1 liter) and let it cook until completely tender.
3. Fry the butter and flour together, gradually add the cabbage broth or milk, and let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes, then add the white cabbage and season the stewing.
