## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless veal from the inner thigh
- 1 hg lard
- 1/2 tbsp salt (8 g)
- 1/4 tsk white pepper
- 6 dcl dark broth
- 1 carrot
- 6 small pickled onions
- 12 small mushroom caps
- 2 olives
- 1 tbsp tomato purée
- 1 tsp beef steak sauce

## Directions

1. The meat is prepared, larded and browned as in the previous recipe.
2. When it is browned, the hot broth is added and the meat is allowed to cook slowly with a tight lid until it is completely tender.
3. The carrot is scraped, cut with a vegetable knife into nice, thin pieces, which are placed in the roasting pan when the meat has cooked for 15 minutes, together with the pickled onion, the mushrooms, the olives cut into strips, the tomato purée and the beef steak sauce.
4. The roast is cut into thin slices and placed on a warm serving platter. The meat gravy is seasoned and poured together with the garnish over the roast.
5. Served with small browned potatoes, peas and salads.
