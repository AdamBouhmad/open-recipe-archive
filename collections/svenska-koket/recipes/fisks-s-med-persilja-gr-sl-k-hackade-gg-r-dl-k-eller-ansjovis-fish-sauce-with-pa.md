## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 6 decilitres fish stock
- 3 tablespoons chopped parsley, or 2 tablespoons chopped chives, or 2 hard-boiled chopped eggs, or 1-2 onions, or 4-6 anchovy fillets
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 1 egg yolk (optional)
- 1/2 tablespoon cold butter (10 g)

## Directions

1. Melt butter and flour together for 2 minutes
2. Add fish stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
3. Add the parsley, chives, chopped eggs, or other chosen ingredient and season with salt, sugar and white pepper
4. Stir in the cold butter last
5. If desired, bind the sauce with 1 egg yolk before adding the cold butter
6. If using onion, peel and blanch it for 5 minutes in lightly salted water, drain, slice, and brown in butter before adding the flour
7. If using anchovy, pound the fillets in a mortar with 10 g butter and add along with 1 tablespoon chopped parsley when the sauce is finished cooking
