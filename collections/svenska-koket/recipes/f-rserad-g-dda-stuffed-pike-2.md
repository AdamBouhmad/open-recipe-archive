## Ingredients

- 1.4 kg pike (= 600 gr scraped fish meat)
- 300 gr butter
- 40 gr flour
- 6 parts cream
- 4 eggs
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- (sugar)
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 2 tablespoons butter
- fish stock

## Directions

1. Scale the pike and cut it open along the back, remove the entrails, and wash the fish well
2. Remove the backbone, and scrape the meat carefully from the skin
3. Sew the skin together with a surgical stitch, leaving a small opening at the head
4. Pass the finely scraped fish meat together with the butter three times through the meat grinder and then pound with the egg yolks in a mortar until the mass is fine and cohesive
5. In a bowl mix the flour, cream and seasonings
6. When all is well blended, work the mixture gradually into the forcemeat with a tablespoon
7. Work the forcemeat for about 1 hour, then fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites
8. Test the forcemeat and fill it through a pastry horn into the prepared fish skin
9. Do not fill the pike too full, as the forcemeat will swell due to the beaten egg white and the fish will burst easily
10. Brush the pike with egg, sprinkle with crushed rusks and fry in the oven for about 1 1/2 hours
11. Serve with mushroom, crayfish or lobster sauce
