## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 5 deciliters meat or fish stock or 6 deciliters good milk
- 2—3 tablespoons grated horseradish
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Melt 1 tablespoon of the butter with the flour for 2 minutes
2. Add the stock or milk and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes
3. Add the horseradish together with the remainder of the butter and season the sauce with spices
4. Prepare the horseradish by scraping and rinsing it, let it soak in cold water for 1 hour, dry it with a cloth and grate it on a grater
