## Ingredients

- 12 rusks
- 2 cups lukewarm milk
- 2 tbsp vanilla sugar
- 2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Place the rusks in a bowl
2. Mix the lukewarm milk with one tablespoon of vanilla sugar and pour over them
3. When the rusks have absorbed all the milk, whisk the cream with the remaining sugar to stiff peaks and place on top of the rusks
4. Vanilla rusks are served with rose hip soup and with sweet soups in general
