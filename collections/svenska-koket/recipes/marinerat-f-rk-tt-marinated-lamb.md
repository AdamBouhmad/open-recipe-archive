## Ingredients

- 2 kg lamb roast (from the leg)
- 1.5 hg larding fat
- 0.5 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 0.25 teaspoon ginger
- 2 deciliters vinegar
- 1 deciliter water
- 1 deciliter white wine
- 3 tablespoons food oil
- 0.5 tablespoon salt (8 gr.)
- 1 small red onion, in slices

## Directions

1. The roast should have hung so that it is tender.
2. Remove the fat and thin parts and lard the roast with strips of larding fat dipped in a mixture of salt, pepper, and ginger.
3. Place in a deep dish.
4. Mix all marinade ingredients thoroughly and pour over the meat.
5. Allow to stand 3–4 days, turning twice daily.
6. Cook the marinade with enough water so it is level with the meat.
7. Place the roast in and allow to slowly boil with a tightly closed lid until tender, or approximately 3 hours.
8. Remove and allow to cool.
9. Cut into thin slices and garnish with deviled eggs and green salad or parsley and serve with mayonnaise.
