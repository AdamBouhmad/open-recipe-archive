## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg salmon
- 1.5 tablespoons salt
- a few allspice berries
- dill stalks
- 1.5 liters water
- 3/4 liter fish stock
- 2 egg whites
- white wine (from previous recipe)
- gelatin (from previous recipe)

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon into pieces and place on the heat together with the spices and dill stalks in hot water and let simmer gently until tender, but not so that it falls apart.
2. Remove the fish and place it in a beautiful à-la-daube mold. Place the most beautiful pieces on the bottom and sides.
3. Clarify the stock with the egg whites, strain through a cloth or cheesecloth and pour over the fish.
4. Place the à-la-daube in a cold place to set, turn out and garnish with dill.
