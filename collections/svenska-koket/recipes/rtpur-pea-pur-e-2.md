## Ingredients

- 3/4 lit. leftover pea soup
- 1 1/2 tsk. solid meat extract (12 gr.)
- 1 1/2 lit. boiling water
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 3 msk. flour (30 gr.)
- salt
- white pepper
- 1-2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Heat the pea soup and pass it through a sieve.
2. Dissolve the meat extract in 1/2 cup of the boiling water and add it to the remaining water along with 2 tsk. salt. Bring the broth to a good boil.
3. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes. Add the purée and broth gradually and let the soup cook with stirring for 5 minutes.
4. Season with spices. Whisk the egg yolks in a soup bowl, pour in the purée while whisking, and serve with fried bread cubes or small Jerusalem artichokes cooked in salted water.
