## Ingredients

- 150 gr. cooked potatoes
- 150 gr. cooked celery
- 1/2 small cooked leek
- 75 gr. salt beef tongue or salt meat
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 2 good apples
- 2 good pears
- 1/2 hectogram truffles
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- cayenne
- 1/4 teaspoon French mustard
- 1 1/2 deciliters vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon boiling water
- 2 deciliters thick, reduced tomato purée
- 2 tomatoes, in slices

## Directions

1. All ingredients are cut into fine cubes and mixed in the mayonnaise, which is prepared as in no. 10.
2. A smooth or fluted mold with a lid is lined with tomato purée, the salad is added, covered with butter paper and lid and frozen in ice and salt.
3. When the salad is to be turned out, dip the mold for a moment in hot water.
4. Turn the salad out onto wax paper in a crystal bowl, garnish with tomato slices and serve with cold roasted poultry.
