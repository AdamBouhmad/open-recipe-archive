## Ingredients

- 6 hg. pork tenderloin
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1/2 msk. salt
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 2 dcl. broth
- 1 large Portuguese onion
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the fillets, dry with a cloth, wring out in hot water.
2. Slice crosswise into 1 cm. thick slices, which are lightly pounded on both sides and seasoned.
3. Heat a frying pan slowly, brown the butter in it, place the steaks in and fry quickly on both sides and place on a warm serving dish.
4. Whisk out the pan with the boiling broth and pour the sauce over the steaks.
5. Peel the onion, cut into slices and brown in butter, and garnish the steaks with it along with the parsley.
6. Serve with potatoes or peas as a breakfast dish.
