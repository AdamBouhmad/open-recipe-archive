## Ingredients

- Fresh mackerel
- Melted butter
- Finely chopped parsley
- Capers or chives sauce
- Browned butter
- Potato purée or stewed vegetables
- Oil

## Directions

1. Cook as you would salmon, or serve warm with melted butter and finely chopped parsley, capers or chives sauce
2. It can also be baked in the oven and served with browned butter and potato purée or stewed vegetables
3. Smoked and preserved in oil, it is served at the smörgåsbord
