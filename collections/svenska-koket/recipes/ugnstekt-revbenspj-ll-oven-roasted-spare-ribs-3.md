## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg spare ribs
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt (23 gr)
- 1/2 tsk white pepper
- 1/2 tsk dry mustard
- 2 tbsp butter (40 gr)
- 1/2 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. The spare ribs, which should be thinly cut, are cracked, dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water, rubbed with a mixture of salt, white pepper and ginger and left to stand until the following day
2. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the ribs in with the meaty side up and brown nicely, basting frequently with the fat in the pan
3. Add the boiling broth or water and let the ribs cook with continued basting until tender, or about 1 1/2 hours
4. Cut into attractive pieces and serve warm
