## Ingredients

- 4 capercaillies
- 1 hectogram lard
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 0.5–1 liter broth
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for sauce
- 1.5 tablespoons flour
- meat drippings
- 1 part thick cream

## Directions

1. Pluck the capercaillies, singe, clean, rinse well and dry.
2. Truss them and cover with lard slices.
3. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, place the capercaillies in breast-side up and brown well on all sides.
4. Salt them.
5. Add the broth and let them roast slowly for approximately 45 minutes.
6. Strain and skim the meat drippings, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules.
