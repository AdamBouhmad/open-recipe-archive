## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. cooked lutfisk (leftovers)
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 4 msk. flour (40 gr.)
- 2 tsk. ground Skåne mustard
- 6 dcl. milk
- salt
- sugar
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- clarified butter
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the lye fish into even pieces.
2. Fry butter, flour and mustard for 2 minutes, add the milk and let the sauce boil while stirring for 5 minutes.
3. Gently blend in the fish so it does not fall apart.
4. Taste the stew and thin with a little cream if it is too thick.
5. Pour onto a warm serving dish and garnish with peeled eggs cut into wedges.
6. Serve with clarified butter and small boiled potatoes.
