## Ingredients

- 3 grouse
- 1 hg. (100 g) bacon, in 3 slices
- 3 msk. (60 g) butter
- 2 tsk. (10 g) salt
- 3 dcl. (300 ml) broth
- 4 dcl. (400 ml) thick cream

## Directions

1. Grouse are prepared and cooked as partridge, but should roast for approximately 1.5 hours to become tender.
2. They can also be roasted as pheasant in a previously prepared sauce and this is added with a little thick cream and cold butter before serving.
3. The grouse are split down the middle, garnished with browned potatoes and salad or parsley.
4. Serve with lingonberry jelly and green salad with lemon.
