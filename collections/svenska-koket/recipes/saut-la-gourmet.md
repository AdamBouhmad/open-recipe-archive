## Ingredients

- 1 veal kidney
- 1 liter water for blanching
- 1 teaspoon salt for blanching
- 1 pork fillet
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1/2 liter raw potato
- 2 deciliters brown broth
- 2 tablespoons Madeira
- 1 hectogram butter for the form
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 egg yolks for mayonnaise
- 1/2 teaspoon salt for mayonnaise
- cayenne
- 1/4 teaspoon French mustard
- 2 deciliters fine salad oil
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon hot water

## Directions

1. Blanch the kidney as in the previous recipe.
2. Cut it into slices and season.
3. Dry the fillet with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into thin slices, which are pounded out and rubbed with seasonings.
4. Peel the potatoes and cut into slices.
5. Peel the onion and chop finely.
6. Grease an ovenproof form with some of the butter.
7. On the bottom of the form place a layer of potatoes, sprinkle a little salt over, then a layer of fillet and kidney slices.
8. Continue until there are 3 layers of potatoes and 2 layers of meat.
9. On each layer of meat sprinkle the onion, and place the rest of the butter in flakes on top.
10. Add the broth and sprinkle the crushed breadcrumbs over.
11. Place the dish in ordinary oven heat to cook for approximately 1 1/4 hours.
12. A quarter of an hour before it is done, pour in the wine.
13. Serve the sauté as a breakfast dish.
