## Ingredients

- 4 hg. roasted, boneless veal or chicken meat
- 1 hg. raw, boneless veal meat
- 2 hg. bread panade
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 g)
- 4 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g) for the onion
- 5 dcl. cream
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Finely chop the roasted meat.
2. Grind the raw meat 3 times through a meat grinder and mix with the roasted meat.
3. Lightly brown the panade in 1 tbsp. of butter, place in a bowl and immediately mix in the cold butter.
4. Stir in the egg yolks one at a time, along with spices and the finely chopped onion fried in butter.
5. Mix the meat well with the panade and add enough cream so the forcemeat becomes like thin porridge.
6. Fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
7. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined mold.
8. Bake in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour.
9. Serve with leftover roasting sauce, mushroom sauce, or simply with clarified butter.
