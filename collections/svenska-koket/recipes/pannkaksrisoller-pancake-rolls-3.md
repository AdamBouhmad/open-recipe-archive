## Ingredients

- Thin pancakes: 3 eggs, 600 ml milk, 90-100 g flour, 5 g salt (1 tsp)
- For the pan: 40 g butter (2 tbsp)
- For the stew: 300 g boned roasted meat, 1 can mushrooms (250 ml), 40 g butter (2 tbsp), 40 g flour (4 tbsp), 400 ml broth, 1 egg yolk, 100 ml thick cream, salt, white pepper
- For frying: 200 ml fine breadcrumbs, 60 g butter (3 tbsp)

## Directions

1. For the pancake batter, whisk the ingredients well with a whisk, then let the batter rest for 1 hour to rise.
2. Cut the meat into fine cubes.
3. Cut the mushrooms likewise into cubes and fry in butter until clear, then press them up with a spoon; fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the broth and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Whisk the egg yolk with the cream and add to the sauce, then let it simmer.
5. Add the meat and mushrooms and season the stew with spices.
6. Heat a pancake pan slowly, melt the butter in a water bath and brush the pan with it.
7. Make thin pancakes from the batter, which are only fried on one side.
8. Place them on a cutting board, sprinkle with the meat mixture, roll up, coat with breadcrumbs and fry in butter until golden brown.
