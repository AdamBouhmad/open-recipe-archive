## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple (approximately 750 gr)
- 4 hg sugar
- 5 dcl water
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 tbsp kirsch

## Directions

1. Cut the pineapple into small cubes and cook with the sugar in the pineapple juice and water for approximately 10 minutes.
2. Pass the mixture through fine sieve and let cool.
3. Add lemon juice and kirsch.
