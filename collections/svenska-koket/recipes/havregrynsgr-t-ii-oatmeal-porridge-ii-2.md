## Ingredients

- 5 deciliters steam-prepared oat groats (2 hectograms)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (10 grams)
- 1 liter water
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats and let them drain.
2. Place the butter in a pot, pour in the water, add the groats and let them swell overnight.
3. Bring the porridge to a boil while stirring and then let it simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid until the groats are completely soft, or about 1 hour.
4. Toward the end of cooking, thin with the cream.
5. Season the porridge with salt and serve with milk or cream.
6. If the groats are not left to swell overnight, the porridge should cook for 1 1/2-2 hours. The cream can be omitted and the porridge cooked on plain water or plain milk if desired.
