## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. water
- 150 gr. butter

## Directions

1. Prepare this butter dough in the same way as fine butter dough, but it need only be rolled out in two sessions and thus need rest only four times.
2. It is used for simpler intermediate courses and simpler baked goods.
