## Ingredients

- 5 kg ham
- 4 tbsp salt
- 2 tbsp sugar
- ½ tbsp saltpeter
- 12 liters water
- 1.3 kg salt butter
- 25 gr saltpeter
- 160 gr granulated sugar
- water for cooking
- 24 allspice berries
- ½ tbsp mustard
- ½ tbsp sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rub the ham with salt, sugar, and saltpeter.
2. Make a brine from the water, salt, saltpeter, and sugar, skim it well and let cool.
3. When the brine is cold, place the ham in it. It should remain in the salt brine for 14 days.
4. When the ham is to be cooked, remove it from the brine, dry with a cloth and place in a pot with boiling water.
5. When the broth boils up, skim carefully, add the allspice berries, and let the ham cook slowly for 4-5 hours. If the ham is not sufficiently salted, add salt during cooking.
6. When cooked, remove the ham from the broth and remove the rind immediately.
7. Coat the ham with the above breading and sprinkle with finely crushed rusks.
8. Put in the oven and let stand until it has a nice brown color, which takes about 1 hour.
9. When serving, wrap the shank with curled paper and garnish the ham with green, cooked prunes and apple sauce.
