## Ingredients

- 9 slices white bread
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cut the bread slices into cubes.
2. Melt the butter in a frying pan, add the bread and fry, shaking the pan often, until it has taken on an even, golden-brown color.
3. Serve to clear soups or purées.
4. If the bread is to be served with sweet soups, mix the butter with 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.) before adding the bread.
