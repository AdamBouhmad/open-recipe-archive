## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 litre unskinned milk
- 3 msk. sour cream

## Directions

1. Coat the bottoms of six small earthenware or glass pots with sour cream
2. Pour in the milk and let it stand in a warm place (protected from flies) to sour
3. Serve with sugar and ground ginger
