## Ingredients

- 1/2 hg. butter
- 3 egg yolks
- 3 dcl. thick cream
- 2 gr. salt
- 2 gr. sugar
- zest and juice of 1/2 small lemon
- 2 tbsp. blanched, finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, add the whipped egg yolks, cream and seasonings and let the sauce simmer until thick and creamy
2. Season immediately with lemon zest and juice and more seasonings if needed
3. Finally add the parsley and keep the sauce warm in a water bath
