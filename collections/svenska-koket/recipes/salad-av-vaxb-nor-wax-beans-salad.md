## Ingredients

- 2-3 hectograms wax beans
- 6 deciliters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 g)
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- a few grains white pepper
- 3-4 tablespoons fine olive oil
- 1 1/2-2 tablespoons vinegar or wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the beans, rinse them, let them drain, place in boiling water with salt and let cook until soft.
2. Lay them out with a slotted spoon on a cloth, let them cool, and cut into 1 cm wide pieces on the bias.
3. Place the beans in a salad bowl and pour the sauce over them.
4. Prepare the sauce as in recipe no. 448.
5. Garnish the salad with parsley and serve with roasted meat.
