## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 5 white peppercorns
- 1 liter of cooking liquid
- a few drops soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons meat extract
- 8-10 leaves gelatin
- 2 egg whites
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- beets
- parsley

## Directions

1. Pluck the chicken, singe it, clean it and cook together with the spices in water until fully tender.
2. Peel off the skin. Remove the meat from the bones and cut into beautiful pieces.
3. Heat the broth with the meat extract, soy sauce and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in a little hot water.
4. When the mixture is warm, whisk in the beaten egg whites, and let the whole come to a good boil while whisking, then strain through a cloth wrung out in hot water.
5. Pour the clear aspic into the bottom of a rinsed à-la-daube mold and set aside to set.
6. Garnish the mold with hard-boiled egg, beets and parsley.
7. Place the most beautiful pieces (the lightest) on the bottom and on the sides.
8. Fill the mold with the remaining meat, and carefully pour the rest of the aspic over.
9. Set the à-la-daube on a cold place to set.
