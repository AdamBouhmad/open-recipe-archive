## Ingredients

- Beef kidneys
- 200 grams boneless meat
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 small Portuguese onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1.5 tablespoons rice grains
- 0.5 bunch parsley
- 2 liters broth

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys and meat into small cubes and season with pepper and salt. Peel the onion, chop it finely and fry it together with the meat in butter for about 10 minutes.
2. Add the flour together with the rice grains, parsley bunch and broth. When the meat is completely tender, strain the soup and skim off the fat carefully.
3. Press the meat and kidneys through a fine sieve. Mix the pressed mixture into the soup, which is then brought to a boil.
4. If desired, add a little wine or curry powder to the soup. Serve with toasted bread.
