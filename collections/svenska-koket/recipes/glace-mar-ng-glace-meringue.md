## Ingredients

- 150 gr. icing sugar
- 3 egg whites
- 4 dcl. vanilla ice cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the meringue mixture as in recipe no. 921 and place with a spoon on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets in the form of eggs, which are smoothed over with the spoon and sprinkled with sugar.
2. Bake them in weak oven heat until they are half-dry and release from the sheet, then they are removed, shaped on an egg and left to cool.
3. Fill the meringues with vanilla ice cream (see recipe no. 930) and serve them in pairs.
