## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 105 gr. powdered sugar
- zest of 1/4 lemon or 4 bitter almonds
- 55 gr. dry potato flour
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the pan
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and the sifted powdered sugar vigorously for about 1 hour, then add the finely grated lemon zest or the blanched and ground almond and the potato flour.
2. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-breadcrumbed ring pan or frying pan and bake in a not too hot oven for about 1/2 hour.
