## Ingredients

- 2 hectograms macaroni
- 1 liter milk
- 1.5 teaspoons salt
- 0.25 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese
- 3 eggs
- 3 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into pieces and rinse them.
2. Bring the milk to a boil, add the macaroni and let it cook slowly until tender.
3. Stir often but gently so it does not break apart.
4. Season the macaroni with pepper and salt and place in a buttered mold, and sprinkle the grated cheese over it.
5. Whip the eggs and milk together, and pour the mixture over the macaroni, and place the pudding in the oven to turn golden brown.
