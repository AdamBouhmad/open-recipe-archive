## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless French cut or inner thigh
- 2 tsp salt
- ¾ tsp white pepper
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 small red onions
- 1 liter potatoes
- 2 parts weak bouillon
- ½ part white French wine
- 3-4 tbsp water
- Butter dough: 125 g butter, 125 g flour

## Directions

1. The butter dough is prepared as simple butter dough.
2. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and cut it into slices across the grain. These are beaten, seasoned and browned quickly in the butter.
3. The onion is peeled, cut into slices and browned. In a fireproof form of clay or porcelain, the steaks are laid in alternating layers with the onion and, if desired, with peeled potatoes cut into slices.
4. The frying pan is whisked out with the bouillon, and this together with the wine is poured over the meat.
5. Three-quarters of the butter dough is placed over the pie as a lid. With a larding needle, holes are made in a few places in the dough so that gases formed during baking can escape.
6. The pie is brushed lightly with egg white. The remaining butter dough is rolled out thin and cut into narrow strips, and with it the pie is garnished in squares, or it is garnished with small leaves cut from the butter dough.
7. It is brushed with beaten egg or water and baked in a hot oven for approximately 1 hour.
