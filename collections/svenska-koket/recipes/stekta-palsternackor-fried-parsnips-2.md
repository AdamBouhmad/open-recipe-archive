## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg. parsnips (4-5 pieces)
- 1 litre water
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the parsnips, brush well, but do not scrape, place in cold water with salt and let boil until they are soft, which is tested with a whisk.
2. Place them on a cloth and peel the skin immediately, then let them cool.
3. Cut them thereafter into long or round slices, which are turned in flour and fried a nice golden brown in butter.
4. Serve with finished soups or as garnish around fried meat.
