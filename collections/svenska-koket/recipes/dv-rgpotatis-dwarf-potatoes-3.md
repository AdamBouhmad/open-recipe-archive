## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- 2 hectograms bouillon fat
- water
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. The potatoes are rinsed, brushed well clean and raw peeled. They are then formed with a smooth vegetable cutter into small round balls, which are boiled in water with salt until they are half soft, then they are poured into a colander and allowed to drain. The fat is heated in a frying pan, the potatoes are added and allowed to fry in the oven, while the pan is shaken now and then, until the potatoes are soft and beautifully golden brown. The fat is poured off, the potatoes are placed as garnish around meat and sprinkled with the parsley.
