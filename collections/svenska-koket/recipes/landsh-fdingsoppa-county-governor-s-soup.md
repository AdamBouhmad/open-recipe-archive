## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups barley groats
- 3 liters water
- 1 liter fresh apples
- 1 cup crushed sugar
- juice of 1 lemon
- 2 egg yolks
- 0.5 cup thick cream
- biscuits
- gooseberry preserve

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats and put them on in cold water.
2. Rinse the apples, cut them into wedges, remove the cores, and add them when the groats come to a boil.
3. When the groats are soft and the apples are broken down, pass the soup through a sieve.
4. Return the soup to the pot, add the sugar and lemon juice, and give it a boil.
5. Whip the egg yolks and cream together in the soup bowl and mix in the soup with vigorous whisking.
6. Serve with gooseberry preserve and biscuits.
