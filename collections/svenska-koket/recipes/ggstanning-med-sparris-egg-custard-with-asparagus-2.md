## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs
- 1 cup cream
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups cream
- 1.5 tablespoons butter for the mold
- 1 can asparagus for omelets (0.25 liter)
- 1 can asparagus (7 deciliter)
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for stewed asparagus
- 1 tablespoon flour for stewed asparagus
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for asparagus

## Directions

1. Fry butter and flour together and mix in with the cream to make a loose batter. When most of the heat has dissipated, add the egg yolks one at a time with salt and pepper to taste. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
2. Pour the mixture into a buttered ring mold dusted with breadcrumbs and bake in a water bath in ordinary oven heat for about 20 minutes.
3. Turn out onto a round plate. Fill the hole in the middle with stewed asparagus. Garnish the egg custard around with asparagus.
