## Ingredients

- 3-4 sheets gelatin, 2 tbsp. warm water
- 5 dcl. extra thick cream
- 3 tbsp. granulated sugar (45 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar or 1 tsp. vanilla essence

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut it into pieces and dissolve it in the warm water.
2. Whisk the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks, add the vanilla sugar or essence, and gradually add the gelatin with vigorous whisking.
3. Pour the cream into a compote dish, let it set on ice and garnish with jam.
4. Serve with rusks or sliced almonds.
