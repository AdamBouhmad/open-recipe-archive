## Ingredients

- 2 cups crushed groats
- 1/2 liter water
- 2 liters milk
- 2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the groats and add to cold water
2. When it comes to a boil, pour in the milk and let the porridge cook slowly with a tight lid for 3–4 hours
3. During cooking, stir the porridge occasionally to prevent burning and dilute if needed with a little more milk
4. Season and serve
