## Ingredients

- 12 pork chops
- 1 lemon
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 cups bread crumbs (sifted)
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 3 tablespoons butter for frying

## Directions

1. Trim the pork chops and pound them well, pour lemon juice over them and let them lie on top of each other for 1.5 hours.
2. Brush them with the beaten eggs and turn them in a mixture of salt, white pepper, bread crumbs and parsley.
3. Heat the butter in a frying pan, place the chops in and brown them well.
4. Cover with a lid and let the chops cook through about 1.5 hours.
5. Place them on a warm platter, whisk the frying pan with a little boiling water, and pour the sauce over the chops.
6. If desired, add a little lemon juice to the sauce.
