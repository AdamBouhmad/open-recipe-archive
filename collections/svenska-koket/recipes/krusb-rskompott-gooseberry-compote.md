## Ingredients

- 3 dcl. strösocker (255 gr)
- 1 dcl. vatten
- 1 lit. halvmogna, gröna, tunnskaliga krusbär (omkr. 5 hg.)

## Directions

1. Make a clear, syrupy syrup from the sugar and water.
2. Top and rinse the gooseberries well and drain on cloth.
3. Place them in the sugar syrup and cook until they begin to crack, but do not cook until they fall apart.
4. Remove the gooseberries with a slotted spoon and place in a compote bowl.
5. Cook the syrup until it is syrupy and pour it immediately over the berries.
6. Serve cold with whipped cream.
