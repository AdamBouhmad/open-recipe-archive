## Ingredients

- 1 liter fresh bilberries or 4 deciliters dried bilberries
- 2 liters water
- peel of 1/4 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm.)
- 1 1/2–2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (80 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the berries and rinse them in a colander, then let them drain.
2. Place them in boiling water with the lemon peel and cinnamon and let cook until they are loose.
3. Strain the berries from the liquid, add the sugar to the juice, bring to a boil, and thicken with the potato flour mixed with 1/2 tablespoon cold water.
4. Let cook for 2 minutes, season to taste, and set aside to cool.
5. Serve with dumplings, whipped cream, and rusks or biscuits.
6. If using dried bilberries, rinse them thoroughly in lukewarm water and let them soak overnight in the water in which they will be cooked.
