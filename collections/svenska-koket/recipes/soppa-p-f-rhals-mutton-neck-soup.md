## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons barley grains
- 1 leek
- 0.75 kg mutton neck
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 6 potatoes

## Directions

1. Rinse the barley grains well
2. Sauté the leek and cut it into strips
3. Rinse the meat, dry it with a cloth and cut it into pieces
4. Brown the pieces in the butter
5. Pour in the cold water and bring the soup to a boil
6. Skim it well
7. Add the barley, leek and salt and let the soup cook about 3 hours
8. Rinse the potatoes, peel them, cut them into slices and add them to the soup a quarter hour before it is done
9. Serve the meat in the soup
