## Ingredients

- 400-500 g boned roasted or cooked veal
- 1 red onion
- 30 g butter (1½ tbsp)
- 30 g flour (3 tbsp)
- 300-400 ml meat stock or broth
- 2 tbsp tomato purée
- a little paprika
- 2 tbsp finely chopped pickled cucumber
- 1 tbsp Madeira wine
- 1 tsp cognac
- salt
- sugar
- ½ tbsp soy sauce

## Directions

1. Chop the meat (do not grind) finely.
2. Peel the onion, chop and brown in the butter together with the flour (see Sauces), add the stock and tomato purée and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Add the chopped meat and season the stew with paprika, cucumber, wine, cognac, spices and soy sauce.
4. Heat the dish but do not boil; serve in tartlets or vol-au-vents or on a hot platter, garnished with fried or poached eggs, fried potatoes and parsley.
