## Ingredients

- 2 hg. flour
- 25 gr. castor sugar
- 2 hg. butter
- 1 small egg
- 6 parts fresh rhubarb
- 3½ parts castor sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter if it is salted.
2. Make a regular pie dough from the butter, flour, egg and sugar, and set it aside to stiffen.
3. Roll out half the dough and line a baking sheet or cast-iron pan with it.
4. Peel the rhubarb, cut it into pieces and lay it in layers with the sugar in the lined form.
5. Roll out the remaining dough, cut out a lid, and cover the pie with it.
6. Bake in a good oven heat for ¾—1 hour, turn out and serve cold or warm with whipped cream.
