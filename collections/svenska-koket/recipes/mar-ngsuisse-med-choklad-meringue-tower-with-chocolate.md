## Ingredients

- 165 gr. icing sugar
- 3 large egg whites
- 75 gr. glazed block chocolate
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 6 dcl. extra thick cream
- 1 tbsp. sugar
- 1 tbsp. vanilla sugar
- carmine

## Directions

1. Prepare the meringue mixture as in the previous recipe and pipe it in the form of small rings or regular meringues onto a well-cleaned and buttered sheet, dust with a little sugar and bake in gentle oven heat until they are almost completely dry and release from the sheet.
2. Dissolve the chocolate in a water bath, without adding water.
3. Blanch the almonds, cut into thin slices, which are lightly roasted with 2 tsp. sugar in the oven.
4. When the meringues are cold, layer them up on a flat glass dish with parchment paper, alternating with the melted chocolate, so that they form a pyramid.
5. Pour chocolate over the pyramid and sprinkle with the roasted almonds.
6. Whip the cream with the sugar to stiff peaks.
7. Flavor half of it with vanilla sugar, color the other half pink with carmine.
8. Pipe the white and pink cream, separately, through a fluted piping tube in beautiful garlands over the pyramid.
9. Do not decorate the tower until 1/2 hour before it is to be served.
