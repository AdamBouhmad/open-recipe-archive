## Ingredients

- 2 liters good broth
- 12 beets
- salt
- white pepper
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scrape the beets and cut into 5-7 cm. long pieces and blanch for a few minutes in boiling water, then cook until done in the strained and skimmed broth.
2. Whisk the egg yolks in a soup bowl, and when the beets are cooked, pour the soup over while whisking vigorously.
3. Serve with fried bread.
