## Ingredients

- 0.5 tablespoon butter
- 2 liters milk
- 0.5 cup potato starch
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a pot and add the milk and bring to a boil.
2. Shake in the starch and let the porridge simmer while stirring for 0.5 hour or until the starch is clear.
3. Season and serve.
