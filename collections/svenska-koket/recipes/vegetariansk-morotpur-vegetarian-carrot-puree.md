## Ingredients

- 1 kg carrots
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 1 shallot onion
- 1 leek
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the carrots and blanch them. When most of the heat has subsided, peel off the thin skin, then grate the carrots and cook them in the water together with the onions sliced and salt for 1 1/2 - 2 hours.
2. Strain the soup.
3. Brown the butter and flour together, add the carrot puree, and let the soup simmer 10-15 minutes.
4. Skim the soup and taste carefully, pour into soup bowls, and add 1 teaspoon of cold butter while stirring.
5. Serve the puree with cheese sandwiches.
