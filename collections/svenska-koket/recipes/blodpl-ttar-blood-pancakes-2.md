## Ingredients

- 60–70 g finely crushed rusks
- 4 dcl milk
- 1/2 litre blood
- 2 tbsp syrup
- 3 tbsp melted butter (60 g)
- 1 tbsp salt (15 g)
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1/2 tsp finely ground marjoram
- 1 finely chopped red onion (optional)
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the onion
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g) for the griddle

## Directions

1. Soak the rusks in the milk and let stand 1 hour to swell.
2. Strain the blood through a sieve, whisk well and mix with the rusk mass.
3. Add the syrup, melted butter and spices along with the onion, if desired, and stir the batter well.
4. Heat a griddle slowly, melt the butter in a water bath, and brush the griddle with it.
5. Stir the batter well, and fry thin pancakes from it, which are laid on a warm serving dish.
6. Serve with melted butter and lingonberry jam.
