## Ingredients

- 4 hg. boneless beef
- 1 hg. boneless veal or pork
- 50 gr. fat
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 dcl. breadcrumbs
- 3-4 dcl. cream, milk or broth
- 1 msk. finely chopped Portuguese onion (optional)
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the onion (optional)
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.) for frying
- 4 dcl. broth for frying

## Directions

1. If desired, beef alone may be used.
2. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, remove sinews and membranes, cut into cubes, mix with the fat and spices and run 6 times through a meat grinder.
3. Mix the meat mixture with the whipped egg, egg yolk and breadcrumbs and work for 1 hour, during which the cream is added by the tablespoon.
4. If desired, add at the end the finely chopped onion, fried in the butter.
5. Test the mixture to see if it is sufficiently seasoned and of the right consistency by frying a small ball of it.
6. Heat a frying pan slowly and brown the butter in it.
7. Form the mixture with two wooden spoons, dipped in lukewarm water, into small balls, which are browned nicely in the butter and placed in a pot.
8. Whisk out the frying pan with the broth and pour the sauce over the meatballs, which then fry with a tight lid for 10-15 minutes.
9. Arrange on a warm serving plate, whisk up the sauce, taste and adjust seasoning, and pour over the balls.
10. The sauce can also be prepared as in recipe no. 429.
