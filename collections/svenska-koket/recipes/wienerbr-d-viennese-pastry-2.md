## Ingredients

- 750 gr. prima wheat flour
- 80 gr. granulated sugar
- 1/2 liter lukewarm milk
- 35 gr. compressed yeast
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 6-8 cardamom pods
- 250 gr. washed butter
- 1 egg for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 75 gr. granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl and mix with the sugar.
2. Add the milk little by little together with the compressed yeast, mixed with salt and 1 teaspoon sugar.
3. Add the finely crushed cardamom.
4. Work the dough until it is smooth and glossy.
5. Roll out the dough to a thickness of 1 1/2 cm.
6. Place the butter in flakes on it and fold the dough in thirds, then fold again in thirds the same way as folding a napkin.
7. Roll out the dough again and fold in the same way, repeating this 3-4 times until the butter is well incorporated.
8. Let the dough rest about 1 hour in a cool place until it becomes firm.
9. Roll out to 1 cm thickness.
10. Cut into 1 1/2 cm wide strips with a pastry cutter, twist and form as rings, snails or braids and place on buttered baking sheets.
11. Brush with beaten egg, sprinkle with blanched, finely chopped almonds and let rise slowly.
12. When well risen, sprinkle with sugar and bake in a very hot oven.
13. The dough can also be cut into diagonal squares.
14. When baked, the pastries can be brushed with water glaze flavored with vanilla or lemon.
