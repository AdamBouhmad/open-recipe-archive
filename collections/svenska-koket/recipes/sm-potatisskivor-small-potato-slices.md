## Ingredients

- 1 litre potatoes
- 1 kg fat
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Treat the potatoes as in the previous recipe and cut them into 2 mm thick slices, which are cut out with a round vegetable cutter into small, even slices, which are left to soak in cold water for about 10 minutes.
2. Dry them well in a cloth, cook in hot fat until they are crispy, place on absorbent paper to drain and salt them.
3. Serve warm or cold with game.
