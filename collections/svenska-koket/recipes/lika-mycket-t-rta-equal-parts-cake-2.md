## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. melted butter (180 gr.)
- 2 dcl. powdered sugar (160 gr.)
- 3 eggs
- 2 dcl. wheat flour (1 hg.)
- 15 gr. butter for the pan
- 3 dcl. applesauce, jam, red currant jelly or orange marmalade for filling
- 3 dcl. whipped cream for garnish
- 2 tbsp. red currant jelly for garnish

## Directions

1. Let the butter cool and stir until it is white and fluffy.
2. Stir the eggs and sugar for 20 minutes and mix with the butter.
3. Stir in the sifted flour.
4. Bake the batter on pancake pans to make 3 layers.
5. When the layers are cold, place 3 dcl. applesauce, jam, red currant jelly or orange marmalade between them.
6. Garnish the cake with 3 dcl. whipped cream and 2 tbsp. red currant jelly, which is piped through a fine paper cone.
7. Alternatively, the cake can be garnished with meringue made from 3 egg whites and 105 gr. powdered sugar, sprinkled with sugar, placed in the oven to turn golden brown and served with vanilla sauce.
