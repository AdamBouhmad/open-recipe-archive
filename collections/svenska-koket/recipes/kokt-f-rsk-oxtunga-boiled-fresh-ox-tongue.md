## Ingredients

- 1 ox tongue
- 2 msk. salt
- 5 white peppercorns
- water

## Directions

1. Let the tongue soak in cold water for 1 hour, then scald and scrape it well.
2. Place it in boiling water, skim thoroughly when the broth comes to a boil, and add the seasonings.
3. Let the tongue cook slowly until tender, approximately 8 hours.
4. When done, immediately remove the skin, slice the tongue and serve with vegetables or with a piquant sauce, such as tomato sauce.
