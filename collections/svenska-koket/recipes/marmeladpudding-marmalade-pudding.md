## Ingredients

- 125 gr. butter
- 1 tsk. sugar
- 1 egg
- 3 tsk. baking powder
- 250 gr. wheat flour
- 2 cups apple marmalade

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and sift the flour with the baking powder.
2. Work butter, sugar, egg and flour well together on the baking board to a smooth dough.
3. Roll this out to a cake ¼ inch thick, spread with marmalade and roll up.
4. Bend the roll in the shape of a horseshoe, place it on a buttered sheet and bake in moderate oven heat.
5. Cut into slices and serve with whipped cream or vanilla sauce.
