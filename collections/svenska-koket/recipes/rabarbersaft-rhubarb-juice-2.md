## Ingredients

- 3 kg rhubarb
- 1 liter red currants
- 2 liters water
- 650 grams sugar per kilogram of juice

## Directions

1. Rinse the rhubarb, dry it, and cut it into pieces. Place it together with the rinsed currants in the water and let it cook until soft.
2. Pour the mass and let it drain until the following day.
3. Weigh the juice, dissolve the sugar in the juice, and then treat it as in recipe no. 1046.
