## Ingredients

- 36 strömmingar (Baltic herring)
- 2 msk. salt
- 1 knippa persilja (bunch of parsley)
- 90 gr. smör (butter)
- 2 msk. mjöl (20 gr.) (flour)
- 1 ägg (egg)
- 1 msk. vatten (water)
- 2 msk. stötta skorpor (crushed rusks)
- 3 msk. smör (60 gr.) eller 2 hg. flottyr (butter or lard for frying)

## Directions

1. Prepare the herring as in recipe no. 163, lay them out flat, sprinkle with salt and let sit for 1 hour.
2. Clean the parsley, rinse it, wrap it up, chop it finely, wring it out in a cloth and mix well with the butter.
3. Form the parsley butter into a roll, let it harden and cut into 18 slices.
4. Place two herring against each other with skin side out and one slice of parsley butter between them.
5. Dip first in flour, then in egg whisked with water, and then turn in crushed rusks.
6. Fry the flounder in butter or lard for 8-10 minutes on each side.
7. Place on a warm serving dish, garnish with parsley and serve with mashed potatoes as breakfast.
