## Ingredients

- 2 hg. cooked chicken meat (leftovers)
- 1 hg. salted beef tongue
- 1 small pickled cucumber
- 3 medium-sized, cooked potatoes
- 2 tomatoes
- 2 small apples
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 tsk. salt, cayenne
- 2 dcl. fine oil
- 1 msk. tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 msk. lemon juice
- 1 msk. boiling water
- 1/2 lit. good stock
- 5 gelatin leaves
- 1 tsk. vinegar
- 1 egg white and the rinsed shells
- 1 msk. cold water

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken meat and tongue into strips.
2. Cut the cucumber, the peeled potatoes, tomatoes and apples into small, even cubes.
3. Prepare the mayonnaise as in recipe no. 10 and let it stand in a cold place for a few hours, so that it becomes properly firm.
4. Gently mix the meat and the other ingredients into the mayonnaise.
5. Prepare the aspic as in recipe no. 10 and let it set to halfway.
6. Glaze twelve small timbal molds with it and place on ice to set.
7. Fill the molds with the salad and pour a little aspic over.
8. Place the molds on ice again, until the aspic is firm, then turn out and garnish with remaining salad and aspic as well as parsley.
9. Serve to the smorgasbord or as a side course.
