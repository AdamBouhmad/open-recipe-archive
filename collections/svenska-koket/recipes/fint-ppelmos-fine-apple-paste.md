## Ingredients

- 3 kg. apples
- 1 kg. lump sugar
- 4 parts water
- juice and zest of 1 lemon
- 1 gr. salicylic acid (optional)

## Directions

1. From the sugar and water, cook a clear syrup and pour it out to cool.
2. Peel the apples and cut them into very thin sections; remove the cores.
3. As each apple is peeled, place it in the cold syrup (to preserve its color).
4. Place on the heat and let cook until they fall apart and form a paste.
5. If the paste is not sufficiently tart, add the lemon.
6. When the paste is ready, stir in the salicylic acid if using.
7. Place the apple paste warm in a crock.
8. To prevent the paste from molding, once it has cooled, cover it with a thin layer of lingonberry preserve, or cover it with parchment paper dipped in brandy.
