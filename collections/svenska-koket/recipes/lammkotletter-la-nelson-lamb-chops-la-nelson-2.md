## Ingredients

- 12 lamb chops
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 0.5 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- Rice mixture: 1 hectogram rice grains
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped shallot onion
- 50 gr. butter
- 0.5 liter broth
- 1-2 egg yolks
- 1-2 deciliters thick cream
- salt
- white pepper
- 3 tablespoons grated cheese
- 50 gr. melted butter

## Directions

1. Treat and season the chops as in recipe 538, lightly fry them in the butter and place on a plate.
2. Rinse the rice grains and let them drain well.
3. Sauté them together with the onion in the butter until it is clear, then add the broth and let the grains simmer with stirring until they are soft — add more broth if needed.
4. Pass the grains through a sieve, blend with the egg yolks and a little thick cream and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper.
5. Pipe the rice mixture beautifully through a piping bag onto the top of the chops, sprinkle with the grated cheese and pour the melted butter over it.
6. Place the chops in a long pan and gratinée in a warm oven for 8-10 minutes, place on a hot plate with parchment paper, garnish with parsley and serve with brown sauce (see recipe 430) and dwarf potatoes.
7. The chops will be juicier if they are braised over charcoal.
