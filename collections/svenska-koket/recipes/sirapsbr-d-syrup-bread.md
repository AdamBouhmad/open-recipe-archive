## Ingredients

- 1 liter beer
- 1 kg coarse rye flour
- 2 liters unskinned milk
- 100 gr pressed yeast
- 3 kg wheat flour
- 1 liter syrup
- 2 tsk salt
- 2 msk fennel
- 3 msk finely cut orange peel
- 2 hg lard or butter

## Directions

1. Heat the beer, but do not let it boil.
2. Place the coarse rye flour in a bowl, add the beer, and work the dough for 1/4 hour.
3. Let it then stand about 12 hours in a warm place.
4. Then add the lukewarm milk, the yeast dissolved in a little lukewarm milk, and the wheat flour.
5. Place the dough to rise to double size.
6. Add the syrup, salt, the crushed fennel and the finely chopped orange peel, and work in wheat flour until the dough becomes quite firm.
7. Let it rise again.
8. Finally work the dough on the baking board and shape into oblong buns, place on heated and floured cloths to rise.
9. When well risen, place on buttered sheets, brush with cold water and bake in a warm oven.
10. During baking, brush the buns twice with hot water, likewise when the finished buns are removed from the oven.
