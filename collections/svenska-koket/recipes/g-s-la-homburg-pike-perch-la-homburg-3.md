## Ingredients

- 1 pike perch weighing 1.5 kg
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 2 tablespoons clarified butter (40 g)
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tablespoons finely crushed breadcrumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter for frying (40 g)
- 2 balls crayfish butter (20 g)
- 4 decilitres thick cream
- mushroom caps
- mushroom sauce
- pressed potatoes

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and prepare as in recipe no. 353
2. Garnish with mushroom caps that have been sautéed in butter
3. Serve with mushroom sauce and pressed potatoes
