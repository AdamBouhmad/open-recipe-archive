## Ingredients

- 1 tsk. butter
- 4 hg. granulated sugar
- 2 dcl. boiling water
- 1 dcl. white wine

## Directions

1. Rub a frying pan very well and butter it with the cold butter, then place it to the side of the stove so it becomes lukewarm.
2. Add the sugar, place the pan over the heat and stir the sugar until it melts and takes on a beautiful brown color.
3. Add the water and wine, and let the sauce come to a boil.
4. Pour it up and serve cold with mondamin pudding, etc.
