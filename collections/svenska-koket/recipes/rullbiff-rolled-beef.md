## Ingredients

- 1¾ kg boneless beef (flank)
- ½ tsp white pepper
- ½ tbsp salt
- 1 red onion
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 liter broth or water
- ¼ tbsp butter (for sauce)
- 1¼ tbsp flour
- beef stock
- 1 part thin cream

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Cut it thereafter to a larger oblong slice, which is sprinkled with pepper and salt and with the peeled and finely chopped red onion.
3. Roll the meat together and bind it well with twine (previously dipped in boiling water) and brown it in the butter.
4. Add boiling broth or water, and let the meat cook 3-4 hours. During cooking it should be frequently diluted and basted with the broth.
5. Strain and skim the beef stock, and prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces.
