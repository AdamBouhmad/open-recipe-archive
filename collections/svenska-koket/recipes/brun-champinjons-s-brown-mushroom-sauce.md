## Ingredients

- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr.)
- 5 dcl. meat stock
- 1 dcl. mushroom liquid
- salt, white pepper

## Directions

1. Slice or dice the mushrooms and fry them in butter until clear, then squeeze the mushrooms with a spoon and fry the butter with the flour for 2 minutes.
2. Add the meat stock and mushroom liquid and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes, add the fried mushrooms and season with spices.
3. If desired, a little Madeira or white wine may be added.
