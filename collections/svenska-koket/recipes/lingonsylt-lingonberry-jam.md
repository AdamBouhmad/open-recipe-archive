## Ingredients

- 6 litres lingonberries (approximately 3 kg)
- 1 1/2 litres water
- 2 1/2 kg lump sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the lingonberries, preferably rinse with warm water and let drain well.
2. Cook the sugar and water approximately 15 minutes while skimming frequently, thereafter pour in the berries and let the jam simmer slowly while skimming until the berries appear clear.
3. If the lingonberries cook too long, the jam becomes dark.
4. Pour the jam into warm, dry jars and let cool.
5. Bind the jars with parchment paper (see page 505).
6. For simpler lingonberry jam, the sugar can be reduced or replaced with syrup.
