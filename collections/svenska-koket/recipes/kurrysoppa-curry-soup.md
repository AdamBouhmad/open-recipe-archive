## Ingredients

- 1 small celery root
- 1 carrot
- 1 1/2 liters beef stock
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 2-3 egg yolks
- curry powder to taste
- salt
- cayenne
- 1 deciliter rice grains (1 hg)
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 10 g salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the celery root and carrot in the usual way, cut into fine strips and cook soft in the beef stock, which is then strained.
2. Place the roots in the soup bowl.
3. Brown the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the strained stock and let it boil under stirring for 5 minutes.
4. Beat the egg yolks and whisk them into the soup, which is then seasoned with curry powder, salt and cayenne pepper and poured over the vegetables in the soup bowl.
5. Rinse the rice grains, place them in boiling salted water and let them cook until soft, about 15 minutes.
6. Drain them in a colander, rinse with cold water and let them stand in the oven for a few minutes, stirring with a fork until they are dry and warm.
7. Arrange on a platter and serve with the soup.
