## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 kg salted cod (long pieces)
- 2 1/2 liters water
- butter
- flour
- fish stock
- 1 raw egg yolk
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- fresh parsley
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the fish piece into 4 pieces and soak them in plenty of cold water for 24 hours, changing the water twice
2. Scrape the skin well
3. Place the fish in 2 1/2 liters cold water and let it simmer for about 1 hour
4. Lift it out with a slotted spoon onto a warm dish
5. Garnish with fresh parsley
6. Prepare a sauce from butter, flour, fish stock, 1 raw egg yolk and 2 hard-boiled eggs
7. Serve with boiled potatoes
