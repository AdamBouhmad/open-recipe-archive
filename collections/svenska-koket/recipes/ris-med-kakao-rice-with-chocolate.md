## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. karolina rice grains (150 gr.)
- 1/2 litre water
- 1/2 litre boiling cream
- 75-100 gr. sweetened block chocolate
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse the grains, scald them in hot water, place them in boiling water and let them cook for 15 minutes, then dilute the cream into it.
2. Melt the chocolate in a water bath and add it to the porridge, which then should simmer slowly until the grains are completely soft, or about 1 hour.
3. Taste the porridge with sugar, pour it into a jelly mould and serve with whipped cream.
