## Ingredients

- Heart and lungs of veal, lamb, or pork
- Water
- 1 1/2 tablespoon salt (23 gr.)
- 1 red onion
- 20 white peppercorns
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoon flour (40 gr.)
- 3/4 liter of broth
- 1-2 tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut open the heart and lungs, rinse them, and let them soak in water for 12-15 hours, changing the water twice.
2. Place them in enough cold water to just cover the meat, skim well, and add the spices; let cook until tender.
3. Remove the windpipe and air tubes, and chop the meat very finely.
4. Brown the butter and flour together for 2 minutes; add the well-skimmed broth, then add the meat and vinegar, and let the ragout simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
5. Taste carefully and serve with boiled potatoes and vinegar.
6. If the ragout is too thick, thin it with a little more broth; if too thin, thicken it with a little potato flour mixed in cold water, then let it cook for 2 minutes.
