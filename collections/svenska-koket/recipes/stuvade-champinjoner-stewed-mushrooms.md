## Ingredients

- 450 gr. fresh champinjoner (mushrooms)
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. salt (3 gr.)
- little white pepper
- 3-4 dcl. cream or broth
- 2 msk. flour (20 gr.)

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms as in the previous recipe, but do not cut off the stems; instead trim and scrape them
2. If the mushroom is large, cut it into pieces
3. Rinse quickly and let drain on cloth
4. Melt the butter and sauté the mushroom in it until the butter is clear
5. Season the mushrooms and add the cream or broth little by little while the mushroom simmers until soft
6. If a thicker stew is desired, sauté 15 gr. butter with the flour, add to the stew and cook while stirring for 5 minutes
7. Taste and adjust seasoning
8. Serve with open-faced sandwiches, in omelettes, tarts, tartlets, etc.
9. If uncertain whether the mushroom is genuine, blanch it before cooking
