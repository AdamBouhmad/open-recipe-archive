## Ingredients

- 1 beef tongue
- water
- 3-4 tablespoons salt
- 10 white peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- 1 carrot

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue and soak it in water for a couple of hours.
2. Place it in boiling water, which should come just to the top of it, skim well, and add the spices and the prepared carrot.
3. Let the tongue simmer slowly for 2 1/2 to 3 hours, or until the skin can be easily pulled off.
4. After removing the skin, place the tongue back in the cooking liquid to keep it warm until serving.
5. Slice the tongue diagonally into 1/2 cm thick slices, arranging them so that the tongue appears whole on the plate when served.
6. Garnish with parsley or salad and serve with boiled or stewed vegetables or with a sauce, such as brown mushroom, morel, or olive sauce, and potatoes.
7. Leftover boiled tongue can be sliced, dipped in egg and breadcrumbs, and fried in butter.
8. Alternatively, serve in brown caper sauce made from 4 dl meat broth, 1-2 teaspoons cornstarch, 1 tablespoon finely chopped capers, 1/2 tablespoon Madeira, salt, and sugar. After the sauce has boiled for 5 minutes, add the tongue slices and boil for 5 minutes. Serve with browned potatoes as a breakfast dish.
