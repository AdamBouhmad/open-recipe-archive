## Ingredients

- 1 kg peeled potato, approximately 1 1/2 liter unpeeled
- 30 gr butter
- 1 tsp salt, 2 tsp sugar
- little white pepper (little nutmeg)
- 3 egg yolks
- 60 gr butter for filling
- 25 gr grated cheese
- 1 egg yolk for filling (1/4 tsp sugar)
- 1 egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Treat the potatoes as described on page 337, peel thinly, cut into pieces, weigh and cook quickly in salted water until soft but not falling apart
2. Drain immediately in a colander and let stand in the front of the oven for a few minutes to dry
3. Pass the potato through a hair sieve
4. Place the puree in a saucepan, add cold butter and spices and stir with a fork over heat until firm
5. Remove the saucepan from heat and add egg yolks one at a time while stirring constantly with a fork, then taste carefully and let cool
6. Pipe onto a buttered sheet in ring upon ring to fairly high basket cases with bottom
7. Small lids are piped separately, brushed with whisked egg and baked separately
8. For the filling, stir the butter until white and fluffy, mix with cheese and the whisked egg yolk and sugar if desired
9. Fill the basket cases, brush with whisked egg and bake in warm oven until they achieve a beautiful golden color, or about 10 minutes
10. Remove, place lids on top and serve the basket cases as garnish around fried meat
