## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 5 deciliters strong meat stock
- 1/2 deciliter Madeira
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Brown the butter and flour together for 2 minutes.
2. Add the meat stock gradually and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add the wine and bring to a boil.
4. Season with the spices.
