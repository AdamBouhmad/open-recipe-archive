## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 dcl. semolina (114 gr.)
- 1 msk. butter (20 gr.)
- 7 dcl. milk
- 1 egg, 1 egg yolk
- 1 tsk. salt (5 gr.)
- 1 tsk. sugar (5 gr.)
- 1–2 tsk. grated cheese
- 1 msk. melted butter (20 gr.) for coating
- 1 msk. crushed rusks (1/2 msk. grated cheese) for coating

## Directions

1. Sift the semolina, rinse with cold water and let drain.
2. Butter a saucepan with 1/2 msk. of the butter, pour in the milk and bring to a boil. Whisk in the semolina, then stir the porridge with a spoon until it thickens. Move it to a gentler heat and let it simmer slowly for about 20 min., stirring often.
3. Pour out the porridge, blend immediately with the remaining butter and let cool. Then add the whisked eggs, seasonings and grated cheese.
4. Butter a flat dish, spread the mixture on it about 2 cm. thick and let it stand 2–3 hours on ice to cool.
5. Using a round measure, cut out small round cakes, place them on a buttered baking sheet, brush with butter, sprinkle with a mixture of the crushed rusks and grated cheese and bake until nicely golden-brown in a hot oven.
6. Serve with roasted meat, for example venison.
