## Ingredients

- 150 gr. flour
- 1 dcl. water (scant)
- 225 gr. fine butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 3 dcl. cream
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.)
- 1 tbsp. sugar (15 gr.)
- 6 sweet almonds
- 2 bitter almonds
- 1 egg yolk for brushing

## Directions

1. Prepare the butter dough as in recipe no. 33.
2. Roll out the dough to approximately 3 mm thickness and cut with a round measure into cakes, with which small, smooth pastry forms are lined.
3. For the filling, blend the eggs, cream, flour, sugar and the blanched and ground almonds in a saucepan and whisk over heat until the cream simmers and is thick.
4. Remove from heat and stir until it cools.
5. Fill the pastries three-quarters full with cream, place on a baking sheet and bake in a warm oven without a base sheet.
6. When they are half-baked, brush the dough edges with egg yolk to achieve a nice color.
7. When the pastries are well baked, carefully turn them out, flip them over, place on pastry paper and serve warm with consommé or clear broth.
8. The pastries can also be filled with chicken forcemeat (see quenelles of chicken forcemeat) and baked in the same way.
