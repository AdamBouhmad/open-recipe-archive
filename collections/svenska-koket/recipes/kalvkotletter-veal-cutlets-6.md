## Ingredients

- 9 veal cutlets
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 3 dcl fine breadcrumbs
- 5 tablespoons butter (100 g)

## Directions

1. Dry the cutlet rack with a cloth wrung out in hot water.
2. Cut out the cutlets so that one rib bone remains in each.
3. Remove the outer membrane and backbone, scrape the meat from the tip of the ribs and trim them even.
4. Pound the cutlets with a damp meat mallet, shape them and rub in a mixture of salt and pepper.
5. They can be fried with or without breading.
6. If breading is desired, dip them in the beaten eggs and turn them in the breadcrumbs.
7. Heat a frying pan, brown the butter lightly in it, place the cutlets in and fry about 7 minutes on each side, or until they are nicely browned and tender.
8. Place them on a warm plate and pour the browned butter over them.
9. If desired, paper frills can be put on the bones.
10. Serve with fried potatoes or vegetables. If the cutlets are large, one can serve each person and reduce the other ingredients accordingly.
