## Ingredients

- 5 dcl thick cream
- 1 tsk sugar (5 gr)
- approximately 160 gr flour
- 1 dcl water
- 1 msk butter for the pan (20 gr)

## Directions

1. Whip the cream with sugar to stiff peaks.
2. Sift the flour and add it together with the water, little by little, and let the batter stand 1 hour in a cool place so the flour will swell.
3. Melt the butter in a water bath, heat the puff pan slowly until it is hot, brush it with butter, add 1 1/2 msk of the batter, and bake the puffs a nice golden brown on both sides (turn with a fork).
4. Place on parchment paper on a pastry board and serve warm with jam, as a dessert, or with coffee or tea.
