## Ingredients

- 1 kg raspberries
- 1 kg blueberries
- 1 kg lump sugar
- 2 1/2 - 3 parts water

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as the previous recipe. The jam looks most beautiful if each type of berry is cooked separately and then mixed cold.
