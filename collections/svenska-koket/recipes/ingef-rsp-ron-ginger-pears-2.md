## Ingredients

- 2.5 hectograms pears
- 1.8 kg. lump sugar
- 6 pieces of ginger
- 1/2 litre water

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, remove the blossom end, and cut a cross in each pear.
2. Scrape the stems and cut them off to half their length.
3. After peeling, place the pears in fresh water.
4. From the sugar and water, cook a clear syrup.
5. Place the pears in the syrup together with the crushed ginger, tied in a thin cloth, and let them cook slowly for 3–4 hours, or until they are completely soft and clear.
6. Place the pears in jars and pour the hot syrup over them.
7. If the syrup does not have a sufficiently strong ginger flavor, add a little grated ginger.
