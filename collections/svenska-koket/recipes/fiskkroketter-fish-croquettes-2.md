## Ingredients

- 3/4 kg cooked pike
- 75 grams butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 2.5 parts cream
- 2 parts fish stock
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 eggs for coating
- 2 cups crushed rusks for coating
- 1/4 kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Free the cooked pike from skin and bones and cut into cubes.
2. Brown the butter and flour together, stir in the cream and fish stock and let cook to a thick sauce; season with spices and bind with egg yolks.
3. Add the fish and when the mixture is warm, spread it on a dish to cool.
4. Form croquettes from the cooled mixture, roll in crushed rusks, dip in beaten egg and roll again in crushed rusks.
5. Let the croquettes sit for 1 hour to harden and then fry golden brown in lard.
6. Place on gray paper to drain and serve hot with mushroom sauce.
