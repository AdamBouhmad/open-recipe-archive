## Ingredients

- 1 part rice grains
- 1 to 1 1/2 liters whole milk
- 50 gr. sugar
- 1.5 drops vanilla essence
- 4 parts thick cream
- 3 to 4 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. The grains are rinsed and cooked in milk to a porridge, which is spread out to cool.
2. The sugar, vanilla essence, the cream whipped to stiff peaks and the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water are stirred into the porridge.
3. The pudding is poured into a rinsed mold and set out to stiffen.
4. When it is to be served, the mold is dipped quickly in lukewarm water, the pudding is turned out and served with jam or plum sauce.
