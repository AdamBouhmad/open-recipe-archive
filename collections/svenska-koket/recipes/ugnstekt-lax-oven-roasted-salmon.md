## Ingredients

- 1 salmon weighing 1 3/4–2 kg
- 1 1/2 tablespoon salt (23 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- For breading: 1 egg
- For breading: 3 tablespoon crushed rusks
- For frying: 1 hg. butter

## Directions

1. Clean the salmon as in recipe no. 331 and score both sides with 2 cm. between each score
2. Rub with the spices and let rest in a cool place for a couple of hours
3. Dry it again, brush with the beaten egg and sprinkle with finely crushed rusks
4. Grease a long pan with cold butter in an area as large as the fish occupies
5. Place the salmon in with the back up and sides spread out and let fry in good oven heat for 35–45 minutes, basting it often with the butter
6. Place on a warm fish platter, garnish with parsley, fluted lemon slices and crayfish tails, if desired
7. Serve with Colbert or caper sauce and small boiled potatoes
