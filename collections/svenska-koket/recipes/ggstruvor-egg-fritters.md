## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 egg yolk
- 70 gr. caster sugar
- 125 gr. flour
- 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream
- 1 kg. lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs and sugar with a steel whisk for 20 minutes, then add the cream and the flour, which should be sifted, and stir the mixture well.
2. Heat the lard in a small iron saucepan, not larger than a saucer.
3. At the same time as the lard is placed in the saucepan, place the frying iron in it to warm it, otherwise the mixture will not stick to the iron.
4. When the lard is hot, dip the iron in the mixture and pour it into the hot lard until the fritter has a beautiful golden brown color, then loosen it with a larding pin and place it upside down on absorbent paper to drain.
5. Serve warm and dusted with sugar, if desired, with coffee or tea or as a dessert with jam or compote.
6. If there is no frying iron, pour 2 tbsp. of the mixture into a funnel and ring it back and forth in the lard, so that it forms a broken cake according to the size of the saucepan.
7. When the fritter has a beautiful golden brown color on the underside, turn it with a lard spoon, and when it is even in color, place it on absorbent paper, over a stick, if desired, so that the fritter becomes bent.
