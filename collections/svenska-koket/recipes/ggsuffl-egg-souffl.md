## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 3 deciliters boiling milk
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon salt (3 gr.)
- a little white pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes, the milk is added, a little at a time, and the batter is allowed to boil for 5 minutes.
2. It is poured into a bowl; when it has cooled, the egg yolks are added, one at a time, and the batter is stirred for 10 minutes.
3. It is seasoned with the seasonings, and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are gently folded in.
4. The mass is poured into a well-buttered ring mold and baked in a water bath in weak oven heat for approximately 40 minutes.
5. It is turned out onto a warm platter and served immediately with clear or finished soups.
