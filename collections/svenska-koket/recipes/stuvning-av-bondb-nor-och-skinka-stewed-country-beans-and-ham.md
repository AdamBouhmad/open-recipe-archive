## Ingredients

- 1 lit. sprit, bondbönor (shelled country beans)
- 1 1/2 lit. vatten (water)
- 1/2 msk. salt
- 1 1/2 msk. smör (butter) - 30 gr.
- 4 msk. mjöl (flour) - 40 gr.
- 3 dcl. bönspad (bean broth)
- 3 dcl. mjölk (milk)
- 3 hg. rökt skinka (smoked ham)
- salt, socker (sugar), vitpeppar (white pepper)
- 2 msk. finhackad persilja (finely chopped parsley)

## Directions

1. Remove the sprouts; if the beans are very overgrown, peel them as well.
2. Bring the water to a boil with salt, add the beans and let them cook until they are soft.
3. Drain in a colander and reserve the broth.
4. Sauté the butter and flour for 2 minutes, dilute with the bean broth and milk and let the preparation cook for 5 minutes.
5. Add the ham, cut into cubes, and let the stew come to a boil.
6. Season with spices and finely chopped parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
