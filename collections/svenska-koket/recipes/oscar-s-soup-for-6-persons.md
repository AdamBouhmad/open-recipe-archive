## Ingredients

- 2 liters strong broth
- 2 carrots
- 1 small parsnip
- white pepper
- salt
- meatballs made from veal forcemeat
- 3 tablespoons canned mushrooms

## Directions

1. Clarify the broth in the usual way.
2. Scrape the roots and cook them in lightly salted water, then cut into neat slices with a vegetable knife.
3. Place them in the hot broth along with the meatballs and mushrooms.
4. Finally, season the soup to taste.
