## Ingredients

- 4 small, salted herrings
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 grams)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 60 grams butter (for frying)

## Directions

1. Skin the herring, remove it, rinse it well, and fillet it.
2. Remove the backbone and let the herring soak in water for about 12 hours.
3. Rinse the fillets well and wrap them in a fish towel.
4. Carefully remove all small bones and coat the fillets in a mixture of wheat flour and crushed rusks.
5. Fry them to a beautiful brown in the butter, arrange on a warm platter, garnish with parsley, and serve as a breakfast dish with freshly boiled unpeeled potatoes.
