## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. white or red wine
- 1 dcl. vinegar
- 1 dcl. water
- 2 tbsp. cooking oil
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 5 cloves
- 1 tsp. coarsely crushed ginger
- 1 tbsp. coarsely crushed juniper berries
- 1 red onion in slices
- 1–2 bay leaves
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 tsp. sugar (5 gr.)
- 1 cleaned, boned anchovy
- 4 dcl. cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the hare, which should have hung 4–8 days depending on the season, and lard it (see page 232), and place it in a crock.
2. Mix all marinade ingredients well, then pour over the meat, which should lie in it 2–3 days and must be turned frequently during this time.
3. Remove the meat, dry it well, tie it so that it takes a nice form, and roast as in no. 474. First dilute with 2 dcl. of the strained marinade, sprinkle with sugar, cover with the anchovy, and then gradually add the cream.
4. Serve with browned potatoes and reduced sauce (see no. 429), supplemented with 1 tbsp. chopped capers or 2 tbsp. lingonberry jelly.
