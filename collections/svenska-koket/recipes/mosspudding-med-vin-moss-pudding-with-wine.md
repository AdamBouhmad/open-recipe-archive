## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 2 parts sherry
- 100 gr. caster sugar
- zest and juice of 2 lemons
- 6 sheets gelatin
- 3 parts thick cream
- biscuits for garnish

## Directions

1. Whip the egg yolks, sugar, wine, grated lemon zest and lemon juice in a saucepan over heat until the cream begins to simmer, then remove from heat.
2. Continue whisking until the cream has cooled.
3. Then add the gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water and the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugared mold and set out to stiffen.
5. When it is to be served, dip the mold briefly in lukewarm water, turn out the pudding and garnish with biscuits.
