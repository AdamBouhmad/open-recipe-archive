## Ingredients

- 1 small red cabbage head
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr.)
- juice of 1 lemon or 3 tablespoons beet vinegar or regular vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves of the cabbage head, loosen the remaining leaves from the core, cut away the thickest part of the main vein, and cut the cabbage into fine strips.
2. Melt the butter in a pot, add the cabbage, and add the seasonings together with the lemon juice. Let the cabbage cook slowly with a closed lid for about 2 hours, stirring it often.
3. Let the cabbage cook for 15 minutes without a lid and with constant stirring.
4. Taste and season with more butter, spices and lemon juice if needed. Serve with fatty meat dishes such as Prague ham, goose and duck. If desired, the cabbage can be cooked with 1/2 teaspoon caraway and seasoned with a little red wine or red currant juice.
