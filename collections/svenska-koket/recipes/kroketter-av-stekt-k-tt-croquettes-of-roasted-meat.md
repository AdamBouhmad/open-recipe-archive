## Ingredients

- 400 g boneless roasted beef or veal
- 1 tin mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 2.5 tbsp butter (50 g)
- 4 tbsp flour
- 4 dcl bouillon
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 4 tbsp flour (40 g) for breading
- 2 eggs
- salt
- 2 tsp cooking oil
- 2 dcl crushed bread crusts
- 1 kg cream for frying

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into fine dice. Cut the mushrooms likewise into dice or thin slices and sauté in butter until clear, then squeeze the mushroom with a spoon and sauté the flour with the butter for 2 minutes.
2. Thin with the bouillon and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes. Add the meat and mushroom together with the whisked egg yolk and let the stew simmer, then season with spices and proceed as in no. 49.
3. Shape the croquettes oblong and they should weigh 50-60 g. Serve with gravy or brown mushroom sauce and peas, if desired.
