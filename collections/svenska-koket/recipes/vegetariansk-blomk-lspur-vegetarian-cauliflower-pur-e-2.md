## Ingredients

- 2 medium cauliflower heads
- 1/2 celeriac root
- 1 leek
- 1 3/4 liters water
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 deciliters thick cream
- 1 liter water
- 1 tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Trim the cauliflower in the usual manner and divide into florets, which should be soaked in vinegar-mixed water (1 liter water, 1 tablespoon vinegar) for 1 hour.
2. Peel the celeriac and cut into cubes; clean the leek, rinse and cut into pieces.
3. Cook the vegetables in water with a little salt until completely tender, then strain.
4. Prepare the purée as in recipe no. 266 and serve as directed.
