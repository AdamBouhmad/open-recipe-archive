## Ingredients

- 1 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the Jerusalem artichokes, brush them thoroughly clean, scrape them and place them gradually in vinegar-diluted water to prevent them from darkening.
2. Rinse them again, place them in boiling salted water and let them cook soft, which is tested with a whisk, drain in a colander and let them cool on a cloth.
3. Shape them afterwards like large olives with a knife or smooth vegetable knife and fry a beautiful light brown in the butter.
4. Serve as garnish around fried meat or together with other vegetables.
