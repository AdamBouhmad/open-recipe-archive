## Ingredients

- 3 kg. red currants
- 3 kg. granulated sugar
- 7 parts water

## Directions

1. Rinse the currants and place them in the preserve pot, alternating with the sugar.
2. Let them come to a boil over low heat, stir if necessary, and then let cook for 2.5 minutes.
3. Pour the currants into cheesecloth and let the juice run directly into cleaned and warm glass jars.
4. Let them stand for 4–5 days and then bind over with parchment paper.
