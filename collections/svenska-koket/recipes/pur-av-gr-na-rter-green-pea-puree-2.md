## Ingredients

- 3 deciliter dried green peas
- 3 deciliter water
- 2 deciliter broth
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)
- a little white pepper
- 1.5 tablespoons butter (30 grams)

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the peas as in the previous recipe
2. When the water has cooked down, dilute with broth
3. Pass the peas through a fine sieve
4. Heat the puree, season with spices, and blend with cold butter
5. Serve in pastry shells with roasted meat, together with other vegetables
