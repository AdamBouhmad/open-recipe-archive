## Ingredients

- 1½ tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 4 deciliters boiling water with 3 gr. salt
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 75 gr. finest butter, melted

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour for 5 minutes without browning.
2. Add the water gradually, but the sauce should not boil.
3. Add the egg yolks whipped with the cream and let the sauce simmer.
4. Season with the lemon juice, strain through cloth, and stir in the melted butter.
5. Serve with fish, meat, and vegetables.
6. The sauce can also be cooked on fish stock and cream, and in the simplest case on good milk.
7. Shrimp sauce is prepared in the same way.
