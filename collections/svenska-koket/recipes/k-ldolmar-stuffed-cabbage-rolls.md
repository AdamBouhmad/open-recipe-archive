## Ingredients

- 1 small cabbage head
- 1/5 cup rice grains
- 4 cups milk
- 200g boneless beef
- 100g boneless veal
- 100g fat pork
- 1 egg
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 liter bouillon
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves of the cabbage head and carefully loosen the remaining leaves from the core so they stay whole.
2. Blanch them in boiling water for a few minutes, then lay them on cloth to drain.
3. Rinse the rice grains in cold water and scald them in boiling water.
4. Add 3 cups milk and let the grains cook until soft, then pour them out to cool.
5. Prepare the meat and pork as for making meat forcemeat.
6. Mix the egg, 1 cup milk, spices and the cooled grains together.
7. Remove the thickest part of the main vein from the cabbage leaves.
8. Place 1 tablespoon of forcemeat on each cabbage leaf, roll it up and tie it with fine twine previously dipped in hot water.
9. Brown the rolls in butter, add the bouillon and let them simmer slowly with the lid on for at least 1 hour, shaking them occasionally.
10. Remove them, take off the twine, skim the sauce, add the cream and bring it to a boil while stirring.
11. Lay the cabbage rolls on a carrot and pour the sauce over them.
