## Ingredients

- 2 kg veal breast
- salt
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 60 grams butter
- 1 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the veal breast with a cloth dipped in hot water and bone it.
2. Rub it with salt, brush with egg and sprinkle with crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Brown the butter in a roasting pan, place the meat in it and brown on all sides.
4. Then dilute with boiling water or broth and allow it to roast slowly until done, or about 1 hour.
5. During roasting, baste the breast with its gravy every tenth minute.
6. Serve the sauce either as gravy or prepare it according to general rules for sauces.
