## Ingredients

- 4 kg lamb saddle
- 2 hectograms lard
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 hectogram butter
- 3/4 liter meat gravy or broth
- 1 can mushrooms (1/2 liter)
- 1 shallot
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 5 tablespoons flour (50 gr.)
- 3 deciliters meat gravy
- 3 deciliters cream
- salt and white pepper
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 3 tablespoons melted butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. The saddle is treated as in the previous recipe, but the outer membrane is not scored but removed carefully and the saddle is larded in even, dense rows with finely cut lard strips, rolled in a mixture of salt and pepper. The saddle is rubbed with the rest of the seasonings. It is then bound and roasted as in the previous recipe. It must not roast too long, as it will become dry.
2. For the stew, chop the mushrooms and shallot finely and sauté in the butter until it becomes clear, then press them up with a spoon and sauté the flour with the butter for 2 minutes. Dilute with the broth and cream and let the sauce boil for 5 minutes, after which the mushroom and shallot are added and the stew is seasoned with the seasonings.
3. Remove the saddle, loosen the meat neatly on both sides of the backbone; place on a cutting board and cut into thin slices.
