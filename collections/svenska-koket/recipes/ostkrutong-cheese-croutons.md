## Ingredients

- 12 slices white bread
- 50 gr. butter
- 1–2 tbsp. egg white
- 40 gr. grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cut out the bread slices with a drinking glass into round cakes (wipe and brush the edges).
2. Spread butter on one side.
3. Whisk the egg white a few times with a fork and add the grated cheese.
4. Mix the cheese mixture well and spread it on the buttered bread rounds.
5. Place them on a buttered baking sheet.
6. Just before serving, toast them quickly in a hot oven.
7. Serve warm with broth, clear soups or purées.
8. The egg white can be omitted and the butter merely mixed with the cheese before spreading on the bread.
