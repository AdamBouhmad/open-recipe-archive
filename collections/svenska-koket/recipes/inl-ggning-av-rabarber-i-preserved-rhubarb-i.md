## Ingredients

- Rhubarb
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Rinse the rhubarb, peel it thinly, and cut it into pieces, which are placed in well-cleaned and dry bottles.
2. Fill the bottles with fresh water, cork them, and bind them, storing in the cellar.
