## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg rye sifted flour
- 325 gr wheat flour
- 1/2 liter skimmed milk
- 25 gr fresh yeast
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 1/2 tbsp crushed fennel or anise (or 2 tbsp syrup)

## Directions

1. From the rye flour, a portion of the wheat flour, the lukewarm milk, and the yeast mixed with salt, make a dough as described on page 462 and let the dough rise to double its size.
2. Work it over with the fennel and syrup, if desired, and wheat flour, and let it rise again.
3. Work the dough well on the work surface and divide into 5 parts, from which shape round cakes, prick them, and make a hole with a round measure in the middle of each cake.
4. From the removed dough make a smaller cake.
5. Place the cakes on buttered baking sheets and bake, when they are well risen, in a very hot oven.
