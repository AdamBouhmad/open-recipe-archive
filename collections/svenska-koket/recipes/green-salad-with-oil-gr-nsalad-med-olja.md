## Ingredients

- 2 medium heads of lettuce
- 150 g. fresh cucumber, sliced
- 2 tomatoes, sliced
- Salad dressing: 1/4 tsp. salt
- a little paprika or white pepper
- 1 1/2 tbsp. vinegar or acetic acid
- 3-4 tbsp. fine table oil

## Directions

1. Treat the lettuce heads as in the previous recipe.
2. Peel the cucumber, cut it into slices, rinse the tomatoes, dry them and cut them likewise into slices.
3. Prepare the salad just before serving—preferably at the table.
4. In the salad bowl mix the spices, vinegar and table oil, add the salad, cucumber and tomato slices and mix gently with the dressing.
5. Serve with roasted meat. In the same way salad of endive, chicory, cress, tender dandelion leaves, etc. can be prepared.
