## Ingredients

- 5 large tomatoes
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed bread crumbs
- 1 hectogram butter
- 1 large Portuguese onion

## Directions

1. Wash the tomatoes, dry them and cut them into slices.
2. Season the tomato slices and dip them in the beaten egg, then turn them in the crushed bread crumbs.
3. Peel the onion and cut it into thin slices.
4. Brown half of the butter in a frying pan, place the tomato slices in, fry them beautifully brown on both sides and arrange them on a hot serving platter.
5. Brown the onion in the rest of the butter and garnish the cutlets with it.
6. Serve with fried potatoes for the smorgasbord.
