## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg lamb breast
- water for boiling
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. If the lamb breast is too salty to boil directly, soak it the day before.
2. Break into suitable pieces which should stay together, place in cold water (short broth), let boil, skim, and let simmer with a tight-fitting lid until the meat is tender, then remove.
3. Beat the egg with the milk, brush the breast with this and sprinkle with the finely crushed breadcrumbs.
4. Place in a long pan whose bottom is covered with the broth, and put in the oven to get a nice light yellow color.
