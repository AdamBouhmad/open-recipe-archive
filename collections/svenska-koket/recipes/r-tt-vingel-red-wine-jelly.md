## Ingredients

- 8 parts water
- 2 parts red wine
- 8 parts port wine
- juice of one lemon
- 250 grams sugar
- 20 grams white gelatin
- 20 grams red gelatin

## Directions

1. The water, wine, lemon juice and sugar are poured into a pan and placed over heat.
2. When the mixture has become warm, the rinsed gelatin leaves are stirred in, and the whole is brought to a boil with whisking.
3. The jelly is strained through double cheesecloth into a somewhat warm jelly bowl and set out to set.
