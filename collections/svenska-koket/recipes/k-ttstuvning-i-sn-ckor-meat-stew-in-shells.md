## Ingredients

- 75 gr. veal sweetbread
- 1/2 litre water for blanching
- 1 tsp. salt
- Leftovers of roast or poultry (approximately 250 gr. meat)
- 1 can of mushrooms (1/4 litre)
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 1 dcl. mushroom broth
- 1 dcl. bouillon
- 2 dcl. cream
- salt, white pepper
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the shells
- 2 eggs for the egg mixture
- 1 tsp. flour for the egg mixture
- 2 dcl. milk for the egg mixture
- salt, sugar
- 1 can of mushrooms for garnish (1/4 litre)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. butter for garnish (30 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into fine cubes.
2. Let the veal sweetbread soak in water, changing the water until all blood is drawn out.
3. Blanch it for 10 minutes, rinse with cold water and let drain in a sieve.
4. Carefully remove cartilage and membranes and cut the sweetbread into small cubes.
5. Cut the mushrooms likewise into cubes and save the broth.
6. Fry the sweetbread and mushroom pieces in the butter until clear, then remove with a spoon.
7. Fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the various liquids and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
8. Add the meat, mushrooms and sweetbread and season the stew with spices.
9. Grease six to eight shell dishes with butter and place the meat stew in them.
10. For the egg mixture, beat the eggs well with the flour, milk and spices.
11. Heat a griddle and grease it with butter.
12. Make small omelets from the egg mixture and place one on each shell.
