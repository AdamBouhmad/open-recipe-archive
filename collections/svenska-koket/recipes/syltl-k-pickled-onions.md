## Ingredients

- 1 kg. pickling onions
- 2 liters water
- 4 tablespoons salt
- 2 liters vinegar (2 degrees spirit-vinegar per liter of water)
- 5 blades of mace
- 2.5 hectograms sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the onion and let it lie for one day in a brine of the salt and water.
2. Boil half the vinegar together with the mace and sugar.
3. Place the onion in and let it cook slowly until it is clear and soft (test with a whisk).
4. Take up the onion with a slotted spoon and place in a jar.
5. Pour the remaining vinegar over the onion, which is covered with a double-folded towel and a plate.
6. When it is cold, bind it over.
