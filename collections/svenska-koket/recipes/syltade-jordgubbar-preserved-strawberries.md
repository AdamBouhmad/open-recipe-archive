## Ingredients

- 1 kg strawberries
- 1 kg (or 3/4 kg) granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the strawberries, rinse them gently, and let them drain.
2. Layer them alternately with granulated sugar in a preserving pan, and let everything stand to "release its juice".
3. Then place the pan over the heat, let the preserve come to a quick, short boil, lift off, shake and skim.
4. Set it back on, let it come to a boil, lift off, shake and skim.
5. Repeat this 3 to 4 times, or until the berries are clear and the syrup is shimmering.
6. Shake the preserve until it has cooled somewhat, then pour it immediately into the prepared jars.
