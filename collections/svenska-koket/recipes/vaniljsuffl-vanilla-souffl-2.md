## Ingredients

- 5 dcl. good milk
- 1 vanilla pod
- 1 1/2 msk. butter (30 gr.)
- 2 msk. wheat flour (20 gr.)
- 1 msk. potato flour (15 gr.)
- 4 whole eggs
- 50 gr. sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with the vanilla, covered, for about 8 minutes, or until the vanilla flavor is sufficiently strong.
2. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the boiling milk and let the mixture boil for 2 minutes, pour out and let cool.
3. Whisk the eggs with the sugar for 10 minutes and add gradually while stirring.
4. Lastly fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks and pour the mixture into a buttered pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 40 minutes.
5. Serve immediately from the form with jam or fruit sauce.
