## Ingredients

- ½ pail strömming (Baltic herring)
- 2 tablespoons salt
- ½ tablespoon allspice
- 2 bay leaves
- some dill stems or whole dill
- 2 cups water

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the strömming well.
2. Place it in a pot, in layers with spices and dill stems; add the water, and place the pot over the heat.
3. When the liquid comes to a boil, skim it carefully, and let the strömming cook slowly until done.
4. Place it on a plate, pour the liquid over, and garnish the dish with dill.
5. Fresh herring can be prepared in the same way.
