## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters cooked potatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3-4 tablespoons flour
- 8-9 deciliters milk
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- sugar
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked potatoes into cubes or slices.
2. Brown the butter and flour together, dilute with milk, and let the sauce cook 10 minutes.
3. Add the potatoes and chopped parsley, and season the stew with the spices.
