## Ingredients

- 12 oranges
- 1 litre orange juice
- sugar, equal weight to the mass

## Directions

1. Wash the oranges well, dry them, place them whole in plenty of cold water and let them cook until they are so soft that they can be pierced with a fine whisk.
2. When they have cooled, push the fruit flesh out of the peel with a silver spoon.
3. Remove the pits carefully, and cut the peels into very fine strips.
4. Scrape the fruit flesh somewhat, whereby the coarsest membranes are removed.
5. Mix peel, fruit flesh and 1 litre of the juice in which the oranges were cooked together, weigh it, and take an equal weight of granulated sugar.
6. Let the mass cook for 1 hour before the sugar is added, and let the marmalade thereafter cook approximately 45 minutes, or until it is clear and of proper consistency.
7. Pour it out then and treat it as in the previous recipe.
8. If the marmalade is desired less bitter, the peel of 3 oranges is omitted.
