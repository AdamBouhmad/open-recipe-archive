## Ingredients

- Various fruits, cooked separately according to aforementioned descriptions
- Mixed, boiled sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Arrange the fruits, cooked separately, neatly in a compote dish and pour over with the mixed, boiled sugar syrup.
2. Serve with frozen or whipped cream.
