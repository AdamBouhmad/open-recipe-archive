## Ingredients

- 1 kg halibut
- 5 tbsp salt (75 gr.)
- 3 liters water
- 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 egg
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks
- 1 hg. butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish piece, remove the dark skin and treat the fish as in the previous recipe.
2. Once the fish piece is rubbed with lemon juice, brush it with the beaten egg on the side from which the skin has been removed, and coat with crushed rusks.
3. Grease a baking pan with half of the butter on a surface as large as the fish piece takes up, place it in with the breaded side up, cover with the rest of the butter, distributed in flakes, and fry in a moderately warm oven for about 40 minutes, during which it is often basted with the butter in the pan.
4. If desired, the butter can be diluted with 2 dcl. boiling water.
5. Garnish as before, serve with lemon or mushroom sauce and boiled potatoes.
6. Alternatively, the fish piece can, after being rubbed with lemon juice, be cut crosswise into 1 1/2 cm. thick slices, which are pounded with a damp meat mallet.
7. Dip the fish slices in beaten egg, coat in crushed rusks, fry in butter on the stovetop and serve with lemon sauce, stewed vegetables or mashed potatoes.
