## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. perch
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp. wheat flour
- 1 tbsp. coarse rye flour
- 4 tbsp. butter (80 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. white wine
- 1 1/2 dcl. fish broth
- 1 1/2 dcl. thick cream
- 2 tsp. lemon juice
- 1/2 dcl. white wine
- 1 tsp. sugar coloring
- 1 tbsp. finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Treat the perch as in the previous recipe.
2. Boil fish broth from the head, bones and skin, to be used for the sauce.
3. Dip the fillets in the beaten egg and turn them in the flour, which has previously been well mixed.
4. Fry them in the butter as in recipe no. 330.
