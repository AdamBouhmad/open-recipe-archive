## Ingredients

- 4 small, salted fat herrings
- 1 teaspoon white pepper

## Directions

1. Let the herring soak in water for 12 hours, changing the water once.
2. Skin it, clean it, rinse it, dry it with a fish towel.
3. Rub it with the pepper and let it lie for 1 hour.
4. Dry it again well, cut it into 1.5 cm wide pieces, crosswise, arrange on a platter and garnish with parsley or dill.
5. Serve with fresh or braised potatoes or braised green peas as a breakfast or dinner dish.
