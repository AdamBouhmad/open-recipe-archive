## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup rice grains
- 1 liter whole milk
- 2 cups apple sauce
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1/2 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains and place them in boiling milk, let them cook until soft.
2. Grease a pie form with butter; place half of the rice porridge in the form.
3. Spread the apple sauce over the porridge and cover with the remaining porridge.
4. Beat eggs and milk together, pour the mixture over the porridge in the form.
5. Place in moderate oven heat for half an hour.
6. Serve cold.
