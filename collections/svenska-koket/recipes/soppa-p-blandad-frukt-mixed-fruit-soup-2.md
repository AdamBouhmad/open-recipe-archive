## Ingredients

- 150 grams prunes
- 100 grams dried apples
- 50 grams raisins
- 1 piece cinnamon
- 2.5 liters water
- 1-1.5 cups crushed sugar
- 1.5 tablespoons potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit and let it soak for about 1 hour in the water in which it will cook.
2. Put it on with the sugar and cinnamon and let it cook in a covered pot until the fruit is soft.
3. Thicken the soup with the potato flour mixed in a little cold water, give it a good boil, taste, and serve, preferably cold.
