## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 celeriac
- 1 3/4 liters water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 3 deciliters cream
- salt
- sugar
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Treat the Jerusalem artichokes and celeriac as in recipe no. 269.
2. Place them in cold water with the salt and cook until completely soft, then pass through a sieve.
3. Rub 2 tablespoons of butter with the flour for 2 minutes, then thin the purée and cream with it, a little at a time, and let the soup cook for 5 minutes, then season with the spices.
4. Whisk the egg yolks in the soup bowl, add the remaining butter, and hold the soup over while whisking.
5. Serve with toasted bread or cheese croutons.
