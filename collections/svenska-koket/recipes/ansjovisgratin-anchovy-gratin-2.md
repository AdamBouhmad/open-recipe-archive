## Ingredients

- 18 anchovies
- 1/2 liter milk
- 1/2 liter raw potatoes
- 1/2 tbsp. finely chopped red onion
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 g)
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g) for the form
- 1 tbsp. crushed rusks for the form

## Directions

1. Skin the anchovies, remove the insides, fillet them in half, remove the bone, let the fillets soak in milk for 1/2 hour, then wrap in white paper.
2. Prepare the gratin as herring gratin, see no. 151, and serve to the smorgasbord.
