## Ingredients

- 6 liters lingonberries (about 3 kg)
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 2 1/2 kg granulated sugar

## Directions

1. The lingonberries are cleaned and rinsed, preferably with warm water, which is allowed to drain well.
2. The sugar and water are cooked about 15 minutes with frequent skimming, then the berries are added and the jam cooks slowly with skimming until the berries appear clear.
3. If the lingonberries cook too long, the jam becomes dark.
4. The jam is poured into warm, dry jars and is allowed to cool. The jars are then covered with parchment paper.
5. For simpler lingonberry jam, the sugar can be reduced or replaced with syrup.
