## Ingredients

- Morel mushrooms (brown stone morel or top morel)
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. There are two types of morels: the brown stone morel and the top morel
2. Clean and rinse very thoroughly to remove all sand and soil
3. Blanch as for chanterelles
4. Sauté in butter for 15-20 minutes or until the butter is clear
5. Season with salt and pepper and lemon juice if desired
6. Serve with smorgasbord or in sauces and stews
7. They can also be braised like champignons
