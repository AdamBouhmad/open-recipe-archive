## Ingredients

- 3 kg wheat flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 liters lukewarm milk
- 80 gr pressed yeast
- 2 hg butter or margarine
- 1–2 tbsp granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough according to the previous recipe.
2. Bake out into small round buns.
3. When they have risen, bake them in good oven heat.
4. When they have cooled, cut them in half to make upper and lower rusks.
5. Place them in moderate oven heat to achieve a nice golden-brown color.
6. Remove them, let them cool and place them in weak oven heat to become thoroughly dried and crispy.
