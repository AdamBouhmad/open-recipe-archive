## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters weak ale
- 4 parts water
- 3 tablespoons syrup
- 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Mix ale, water, syrup, and the juice of 1 lemon.
2. Slice the other lemon and add it.
3. To cool the soup, ice may be added.
4. Serve with rusks.
