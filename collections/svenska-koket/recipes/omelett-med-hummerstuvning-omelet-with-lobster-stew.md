## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- salt
- 2.5 deciliters cream
- sugar (optional)
- 1 teaspoon butter for the pan
- 1 tablespoon butter for stew
- 2 tablespoons flour for stew
- 4 deciliters thin cream
- 1 can lobster (3.5 liters)
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks and whites.
2. Whisk the yolks together with the cream, salt and sugar (if using), and fold in the egg whites beaten until stiff.
3. Pour the batter into a well-buttered omelet pan and bake light yellow in ordinary oven heat.
4. Tip the omelet out immediately, place the stew on half of the omelet, and fold the other half over.
5. For the stew, fry the butter and flour together, and add the cream with constant stirring.
6. Let the sauce boil for 10 minutes.
7. Add the chopped lobster and season the stew.
8. Finally stir in the whisked egg yolk.
