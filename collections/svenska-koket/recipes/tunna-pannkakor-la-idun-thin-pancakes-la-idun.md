## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 4 dcl. water
- approximately 135 gr. flour
- 2 dcl. melted butter (2 hg.)
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.) for the pan
- 2 cups jam (raspberry or strawberry)
- 3 egg whites
- 150 gr. powdered sugar or 3 dcl. thick cream and 1/2 tbsp. sprinkle sugar (8 gr.)
- 50 gr. sweet almond

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks with a steel whisk, add the cream, then whisk in the flour, which should preferably be sifted.
2. Dilute with the water, add the salt and butter, and let the batter rest for 2 hours.
3. Heat a pancake pan slowly, melt the butter in a water bath, and brush the pan with it.
4. Stir the batter well and fry thin pancakes, placing them on top of each other on a warm plate.
5. Coat each pancake thinly with some of the jam, which has been warmed in a water bath.
6. Cut the pancakes into 12 pieces and let them cool.
7. Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
8. Carefully sift the sugar into the egg white foam, spread the meringue over the pancakes, sprinkle with the blanched and finely chopped almond, and let the cake stand in gentle oven heat until the meringue is half-dry and has a beautiful light yellow color.
9. The pancakes can also be garnished with cream, whipped until stiff and flavored with sugar, sprinkled with almond and served immediately.
10. Vanilla cream can also be spread between the thin pancakes, after which the pancakes are poured with warm cherry or strawberry juice.
