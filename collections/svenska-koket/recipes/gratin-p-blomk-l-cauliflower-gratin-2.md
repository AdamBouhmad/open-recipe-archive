## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized, beautiful cauliflower heads
- 1.5 liters water
- 10 grams salt
- 1.5 tablespoons butter (30 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1 deciliter cauliflower cooking liquid
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower, rinse it and let it soak in vinegar-mixed water (2 liters water, 2 tablespoons vinegar) for 1 hour.
2. Remove it, rinse again and place in boiling salted water and cook until a whisk easily passes through it.
3. Remove the cauliflower immediately with a slotted spoon, let it drain and place it on a buttered, oven-safe dish or a silver platter.
4. Fry the butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the cauliflower cooking liquid and cream and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
5. Season with the spices, bind with the egg yolk and pour over the cauliflower, which is then sprinkled with the grated cheese and crushed rusks.
6. Pour a little clarified butter over, place the dish in a roasting pan with coarse salt and gratinée the preparation in a warm oven for approximately 15 minutes.
7. Serve immediately when removed from the oven.
