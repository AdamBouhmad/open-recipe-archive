## Ingredients

- 300 g washed butter
- 300 g caster sugar
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 egg white
- 450 g wheat flour
- 1 egg for brushing
- 40 g sweet almond
- 40 g granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Stir the butter with the sugar until white and fluffy, then add the egg yolks, egg white, and the sifted flour, after which the dough is covered and allowed to stand until the following day. It is then rolled out into small rings, which are brushed with beaten egg, dipped in blanched and ground almond and sugar, laid on a buttered baking sheet, and baked until beautifully golden brown in a warm oven.
