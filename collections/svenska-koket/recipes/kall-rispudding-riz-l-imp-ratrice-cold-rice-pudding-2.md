## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 dcl. rice grains (125 gr.)
- 1 1/4 lit. milk
- 1/2 stick vanilla or the grated peel of 1/4 lemon
- 60 gr. granulated sugar
- 4 sheets gelatin
- 2 msk. warm water
- 3 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice grains, blanch in hot water, place in the boiling milk and let them cook with the vanilla or lemon peel, stirring, until they are completely soft.
2. Thin with more milk if needed.
3. Pour out the porridge, which should be thick, and let it cool, then add the sugar.
4. Rinse the gelatin, cut into pieces, dissolve in the warm water and fold into the porridge together with the cream beaten until stiff.
5. Fill the mixture into a water-rinsed and sugar-sprinkled timbal mold and let it set on ice for 3-4 hours.
6. Turn out of the mold, garnish with red currant jelly and serve with apricot sauce or strawberry juice.
