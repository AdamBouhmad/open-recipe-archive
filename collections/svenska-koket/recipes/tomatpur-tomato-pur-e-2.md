## Ingredients

- 5 shallot onions
- 1/2 kg red tomatoes
- 80 gr butter
- 6 tbsp flour (60 gr)
- 3 liters dark broth
- salt
- sugar
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, chop coarsely and fry well in 1 tbsp of the butter.
2. Clean the tomatoes, rinse, cut into wedges and let cook together with the onion and 1/2 liter of the broth, with a tight lid, about 3/4 hour.
3. Strain the tomatoes.
4. Fry butter and flour 2 minutes.
5. Add the purée and the rest of the broth gradually and let the soup cook while stirring 5 minutes.
6. Season with the spices and serve with fried bread slices.
