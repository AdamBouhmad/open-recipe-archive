## Ingredients

- peel of 2 oranges
- juice of 4 oranges
- 7 parts thick cream
- 5 egg yolks
- 115 g sugar
- 1.25 parts water

## Directions

1. The oranges are rinsed, dried well and peeled.
2. All the white pith is removed, and the peel is cut into strips.
3. Half of the cream is brought to a boil, the orange peel is added, and the cream is covered and allowed to cool.
4. The egg yolks are whisked in a saucepan with 1 tablespoon of the sugar; the boiled and strained cream is added gradually, after which the mixture is allowed to simmer over the heat to a thick cream.
5. Meanwhile, the orange juice is boiled with the remaining sugar and water to a syrupy consistency, which is then blended with the cream.
6. The cream whipped to stiff peaks is folded in, and the frozen mixture is frozen.
