## Ingredients

- Meat forcemeat as in recipe n:o 632
- 1 1/2 liters Jerusalem artichokes
- 3/4 liter water
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 3 msk butter
- 4 msk flour (40 gr)
- 4 dcl cooking liquid
- salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat forcemeat as in recipe n:o 632.
2. Brush the Jerusalem artichokes well clean, scrape, rinse and let them soak in vinegar-mixed water 1 hour to keep them white.
3. Place them in boiling water with salt and let them cook until tender, then drain in a colander and reserve the cooking liquid.
4. Prepare and bake the stew and pudding as in the preceding recipe.
