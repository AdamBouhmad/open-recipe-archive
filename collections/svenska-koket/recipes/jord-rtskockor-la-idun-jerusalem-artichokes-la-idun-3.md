## Ingredients

- 1 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 10 gr salt
- 1 1/2 tbsp butter (30 gr) for the sauce
- 3 tbsp flour (30 gr)
- 1 dcl Jerusalem artichoke cooking water
- 3 dcl cream
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1 egg yolk
- 3 tbsp butter for the shells
- 1 tbsp grated cheese
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the Jerusalem artichokes as in the previous recipe
2. Remove with a slotted spoon, let cool, and cut into 3 mm thick slices
3. Cut the truffle into thin slices and mix with the artichokes
4. Place in 6 small shell dishes buttered with butter
