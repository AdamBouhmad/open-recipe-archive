## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- water, salt
- 1/2 hectogram cold butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup crushed rusks
- 4 tablespoons butter or cream for cooking

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes, peel and mash them with a fork or press them through a puree press.
2. Add the cold butter, salt, sugar and egg yolk; lastly fold in the egg white, beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Form small round balls with a floured spoon, flatten them, roll in crushed rusks and fry golden brown in butter or cream.
4. In simpler cases, the cold butter and egg yolk can be replaced with milk.
