## Ingredients

- 1 kg. coarse salt
- 3 liters water
- 100 small Västerås cucumbers
- cherry leaves
- currant leaves
- dill crowns
- 2–3 tablespoons spice peppercorns
- 3 liters vinegar
- 1.5 liters water

## Directions

1. Mix the salt and water cold together.
2. When the salt is completely dissolved, place the cucumbers in the brine and let them lie there for three days.
3. Take up the cucumbers and place them in a barrel or stone crock in alternating layers with leaves, dill crowns, and peppercorns. Each cucumber should be wrapped with leaves so that they do not lie tightly together (otherwise they become spotted). The first and last layers should be leaves.
4. Boil a brine from the vinegar and water, which is poured hot over the cucumbers.
5. Place a weighted bottom on top and bind the crock closed.
6. The brine should stand above the cucumbers. When they are clear when cut in two, they are ready.
7. To keep the cucumbers better, you can cover them with mustard sewn into a small bag.
