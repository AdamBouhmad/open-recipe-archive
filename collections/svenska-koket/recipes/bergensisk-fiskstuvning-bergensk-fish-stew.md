## Ingredients

- 1 hectogram salted crayfish tails
- ¾-1 liter cooked fish
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons wheat flour
- 1½ cups cream milk
- ½ cup fish broth
- salt
- lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place the crayfish tails in milk the evening before they are to be used.
2. Clean the fish and cut it into large cubes.
3. Place it on a serving plate in layers with the crayfish tails, and set the plate over a pot with boiling water to heat through.
4. Fry the butter and flour together, gradually add the milk and fish stock, and let the sauce cook 10 minutes.
5. Season it with salt and lemon juice and finish it with the egg yolks.
6. Pour the sauce over the fish and serve it immediately.
