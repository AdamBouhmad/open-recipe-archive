## Ingredients

- 6 leaves gelatin
- 1/2 deciliter boiling water
- 50 grams granulated sugar
- 4 deciliters strawberry juice
- 3 deciliters thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin in cold water, cut into pieces and dissolve in 1/2 deciliter boiling water together with the sugar, after which the strawberry juice is added.
2. When the mixture is completely cold and begins to set, fold in the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Rinse a nice tin form with cold water and sprinkle with granulated sugar, pour in the mixture and let the form stand on ice for 3-4 hours.
4. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish with paper and garnish with preserved strawberries, biscuits or macaroons.
5. Instead of strawberry juice, raspberry, gooseberry or cherry juice can be used.
