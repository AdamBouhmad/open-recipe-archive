## Ingredients

- 0.33 kg kidney tallow
- 0.33 kg pork intestinal fat
- water
- 1 potato
- 1 apple

## Directions

1. Cut the tallow and fat into fine cubes and let soak in water for approximately 24 hours
2. Squeeze out the cubes well and place in fresh, cold water to boil
3. Peel and halve the potato or apple and add to the lard, which becomes clearer thereby
4. Let the lard boil until a thin, bluish smoke rises from the surface and the cubes (the "greaves") sink to the bottom
5. Strain into a clean stone crock and store in a cool place
6. Note: Lard can also be prepared from kidney tallow alone but will be harder
