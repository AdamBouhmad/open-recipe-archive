## Ingredients

- 6 leaves gelatin
- 3 tablespoons water
- 1 egg
- 110 grams sugar
- 1.5 deciliters milk
- 1 vanilla pod
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 deciliters thick cream
- 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Rinse the gelatin with cold water, cut it into pieces, dissolve it in 3 tablespoons of boiling water and mix it with the egg, egg yolks, sugar, milk and vanilla in a well-scoured saucepan.
2. Whisk the mixture vigorously over the fire until the cream is thick and foamy, then remove the saucepan from the heat and continue whisking until the cream is cold.
3. Add the cream whipped to stiff peaks, and pour the cream into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and let it stand on ice for 2–3 hours to set.
4. When the cream is to be served, loosen it around the edge with a pointed knife, turn it out onto a flat glass dish with cake paper and garnish with fruit or biscuits.
5. Half of the cream can be colored green with pistachio paste, poured into the mold and allowed to set somewhat on ice, after which the rest of the light yellow cream is poured in.
6. The cream is allowed to set on ice, turned out and served with raspberry or strawberry sauce.
7. The vanilla can be omitted and the cream flavored instead with crushed hazelnuts, cocoa, liqueur, punch, coffee, lemon or fruit.
