## Ingredients

- 30 Baltic herrings
- 1 1/2 tbsp salt
- 75 g flour
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 dcl lukewarm water
- 10 g melted butter
- 1 egg white
- 1/2 kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the herrings as in recipe 163.
2. For the batter: dry the flour well in the oven and sift it with salt in a bowl.
3. Add the water together with the butter, and stir the batter as little as possible so it does not become tough.
4. Just before using, fold in the egg white beaten to stiff peaks.
5. Wrap the herrings in a fish cloth and roll them up with the flesh side out.
6. Dip them in the batter and fry in hot lard until they are brown and cooked through, which is tested with a whisk.
7. Place them on paper to drain.
8. Arrange on a warm plate, garnish with parsley cooked in lard, and serve with cooked mayonnaise as a breakfast dish.
