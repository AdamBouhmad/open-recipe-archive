## Ingredients

- 2 kg. wheat flour
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 liter lukewarm milk
- 50 gr. pressed yeast
- 5 gr. saffron
- 1 tbsp. brandy
- beaten eggs
- butter creamed to a white foam
- sugar
- cleaned and rinsed raisins
- 1 egg for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- ½ cup pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm milk.
2. Sift the wheat flour with the salt, set aside 3-4 cups for rolling out, and pour the rest into a bowl.
3. Add the lukewarm milk with vigorous stirring, stir in the yeast, and beat the dough until smooth and glossy.
4. Set it in a warm place to rise to double its size.
5. Dry the saffron in a warm oven, pound it and dissolve it in the brandy and work it into the dough together with the beaten eggs, the butter creamed to a white foam, the sugar, and the cleaned and rinsed raisins.
6. Let the dough rise again and shape it into wreaths or braids, which are placed on well-cleaned and buttered baking sheets.
7. When the breads have risen, brush them with the beaten eggs and sprinkle with the blanched and chopped sweet almonds mixed with the pearl sugar.
8. Bake in good oven heat.
