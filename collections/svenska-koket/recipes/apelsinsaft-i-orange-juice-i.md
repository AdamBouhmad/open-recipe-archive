## Ingredients

- Peel of 6 oranges
- 1.33 liters boiling water
- 2 kg granulated sugar
- 40 grams citric acid

## Directions

1. Carefully cut away all the white from the inside of the peels, and cut the peels into fine strips.
2. Place these in a porcelain crock and pour the boiling water over.
3. Cover the crock with a lid and let it stand for two days.
4. Strain the peels away, and add the sugar and citric acid.
5. When this is well dissolved and the juice is clear, pour it into bottles, which are corked and sealed with resin.
