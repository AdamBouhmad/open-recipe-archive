## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram butter
- 100 grams flour
- 200 grams cooked, grated potato
- 1 egg
- 0.5 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon caraway
- 1 egg yolk for brushing

## Directions

1. Work all the ingredients together on a pastry board.
2. Form finger-thick sticks from the dough, which are cut into 0.5 decimeter long pieces; these are brushed with egg yolk, sprinkled with salt and caraway and baked in ordinary oven heat.
3. Serve with broth or finished soups.
