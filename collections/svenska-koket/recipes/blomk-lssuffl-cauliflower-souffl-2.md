## Ingredients

- 1 small cauliflower head
- 1/2 liter water
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 80 gr butter
- 80 gr flour
- 2 dcl cauliflower broth
- 3 dcl boiling milk
- 5-6 eggs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold
- 1 liter water with 1 tbsp vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the cauliflower and let it soak in 1 liter water with 1 tbsp vinegar for 1 hour. Place it in boiling salted water and let it cook soft, then place it on a sieve to drain and divide it into smaller pieces.
2. The soufflé is prepared as in the preceding recipe, served as a middle course with hollandaise or melted butter.
