## Ingredients

- 2 hg. prunes
- 1 hg. apricots
- 1½ hg. ring apples
- 2 hg. sugar
- ¾—1 liter water
- 125 gr. spice bread
- 75 gr. butter
- 3 parts thick cream for garnish

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit and let it soak in the water for about 1 hour.
2. From the water and sugar boil a clear syrup, and in this cook first the prunes, then the apples and finally the apricots.
3. Grate the bread and fry it in the butter.
4. Place the fruit and bread on a platter in layers so that they form a pyramid.
5. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, place it in a paper cone and pipe out as veils over the pyramid.
6. Serve with whipped cream.
