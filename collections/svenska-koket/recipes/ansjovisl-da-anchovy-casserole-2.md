## Ingredients

- 12 anchovies
- 2 eggs
- 2 dcl. cream
- 1 tsp. finely chopped parsley
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 g) for the form
- 1 tbsp. crushed rusks for the form

## Directions

1. Skin the anchovies, remove the insides, fillet them in half and remove the bone.
2. Grease an ovenproof form with butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
3. Lay the fillets in it, pour over with the eggs, well whisked with the cream, and sprinkle with the parsley.
4. Bake the casserole in good oven heat and serve to the smorgasbord.
