## Ingredients

- 2 kg. wheat flour
- 1 tsk. salt
- 1 lit. lukewarm milk
- 50 gr. pressed yeast
- 2-4 kkp granulated sugar
- 800 gr. butter or margarine
- 50 gr. sweet almonds
- 1 egg for brushing
- 50 gr. sweet almonds for brushing
- ½ kkp pearl sugar for brushing

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little of the lukewarm milk.
2. Sift the wheat flour with the salt, set aside 3-4 kkp for rolling out, and pour the rest into a bowl.
3. Add the lukewarm milk with vigorous stirring, and mix in the dissolved yeast, then beat the dough until smooth and glossy.
4. Set it in a warm place to rise to double size.
5. Work 200 gr. of the butter mixed with 2 kkp of the sugar beaten to white foam into the dough.
6. Let the dough rise again.
7. Divide it into 10 portions.
8. Roll each portion out to an oblong square of 1 cm thickness and brush with the remaining melted butter.
9. Scald and chop the almonds and mix with 1 kkp granulated sugar, then sprinkle over the brushed dough, which is now rolled up into a long roll.
10. Cut the roll into 12-15 portions, press them in the middle with the handle of a table knife, turn them over, and place on well-buttered baking sheets to rise.
11. When the breads have risen, brush them with beaten egg and sprinkle with scalded and finely chopped almonds mixed with pearl sugar.
12. Bake in good oven heat.
