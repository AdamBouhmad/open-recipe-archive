## Ingredients

- 400 g wheat flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 160 g butter
- 200 g caster sugar
- 200 g currants
- 30 g candied orange peel
- 2 tsp grated ginger
- 3 eggs
- 2 tbsp milk

## Directions

1. Wash the butter, sift the flour with the baking powder, and work the butter in.
2. Then mix in the sugar, the cleaned currants, the finely cut candied orange peel, and the grated ginger.
3. Whisk the eggs together with the milk and stir into the rest.
4. On well-buttered baking sheets, place small cookies from the dough, which are then baked golden brown in moderate oven heat.
