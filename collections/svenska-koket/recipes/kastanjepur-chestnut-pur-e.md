## Ingredients

- 350 gr. chestnuts
- water
- 1 tbsp. butter
- 2 tsp. finely chopped Portuguese onion
- 1 tbsp. flour
- 2 liters broth
- sugar (optional)
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Free the chestnuts from the outer shell and place them for a few minutes in boiling water, after which the inner membrane is easily removed.
2. Place the chestnuts in cold water together with half of the butter and 1½ tbsp. salt and let them cook slowly until soft enough to pass through a sieve, approximately 1½-2 hours.
3. Fry the remaining butter with the onion and flour, add the purée, and dilute with the broth, after which the soup is allowed to cook 10-15 minutes, skimmed and seasoned.
4. Serve either with small round carrots cooked in salted water, or with Graham bread.
