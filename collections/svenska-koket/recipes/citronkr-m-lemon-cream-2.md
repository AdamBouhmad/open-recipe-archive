## Ingredients

- Peel and juice of 1 small lemon
- 2 dcl crushed sugar (190 gr)
- 1 dcl French wine
- 1 1/2 dcl water
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. Rinse the lemon well and dry it, rub the peel against the sugar, which is then placed in a well-scoured pot.
2. Press out the lemon juice and mix with the sugar.
3. Add the wine, water and egg yolks, and whisk the mixture until the sugar has melted.
4. Move the pot over the fire, and let the cream simmer under vigorous whisking until it is thick and fluffy.
5. Pour the cream and whisk until it is cold.
6. If desired, then add the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
7. Serve in a compote bowl with pastries. Half the batch is enough to fill a large cake. The cream should then be omitted.
