## Ingredients

- 12 slices dark bread
- 12 slices wheat bread
- 1 1/2 hg. butter
- 6 slices smoked, cooked ham
- 6 slices tongue
- French mustard

## Directions

1. The bread slices should be about 3 mm. thick.
2. Spread the dark bread slices with butter, cover with ham or tongue, then spread a little mustard; on top of these place the butter-spread wheat slices.
3. The sandwiches should rest under light pressure between two cutting boards for about 1 hour.
4. Then cut into rectangles, about 7 cm. long and 3 1/2 cm. wide, and arrange in a pyramid on a napkin.
5. Serve with tea, breakfast, at the sandwich table at dinners and at supper.
