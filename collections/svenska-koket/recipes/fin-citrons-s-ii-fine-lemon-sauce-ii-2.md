## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 2 parts white French wine
- 6 egg yolks
- 2 parts water

## Directions

1. The lemon is zested so that only the yellow peel comes off, and the juice is pressed out.
2. The wine, water, zested lemon peel, lemon juice, sugar and egg yolks are whisked well together in a pan and are simmered over the heat with vigorous whisking until the sauce thickens.
3. The pan is removed from the heat, and whisking is continued until the sauce has cooled.
