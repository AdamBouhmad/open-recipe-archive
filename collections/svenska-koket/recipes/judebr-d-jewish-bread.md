## Ingredients

- 110 gr. butter
- 110 gr. sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 egg white
- 180 gr. wheat flour
- 1 gr. hartshorn salt
- 3 bitter almonds
- 2 cardamom
- 1 egg white for brushing
- 2 tbsp. granulated sugar (30 gr.)
- 1 tsp. cinnamon (3 gr.)

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir it with the sugar for 15 minutes.
2. Work in the egg, egg white, the grated almond, the finely crushed cardamom and lastly the flour, which should be sifted and well mixed with the hartshorn salt.
3. Roll out the dough to approximately 2 mm thickness and cut with a round measure into small cakes.
4. Brush the cakes with egg white, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and bake golden brown in a not too warm oven.
