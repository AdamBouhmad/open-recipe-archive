## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 parts cream
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1/2 kg. broth for cooking
- 1 1/2 tablespoon butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- mushroom stock
- 1/2 liter thin cream
- 1 tin mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- pepper
- salt
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Sift the dry ingredients into a bowl and stir with half of the cream, whisked with the egg. Let the butter cool slightly and add it together with the rest of the cream, and stir everything well.
2. Heat the broth in an iron pot, and warm the mold for the crusts in it. Dip the mold thereafter in the batter, taking care that the batter does not reach over the edge of the mold. Cook the crusts in the broth until they are golden brown.
3. Fry the mushrooms in butter and take them up, then mix the flour together with the butter. Add the mushroom stock and cream with frequent stirring, and let the sauce simmer 10 minutes.
4. Add the fried mushrooms, and season the stewing with pepper and salt. Lastly stir in the whisked egg yolk, and fill the stewing into the crusts.
