## Ingredients

- 4 soles, approximately 5 hg each
- 1 1/2 tablespoon salt (23 gr)
- 2 tablespoon butter (40 gr) for cooking
- fish broth for cooking
- 2 tablespoon butter (40 gr) for lobster stew
- 6 tablespoon flour (60 gr)
- 6 dcl fish broth
- 3-4 egg yolks
- 1 can lobster (1/2 litre)
- salt, white pepper
- 3 balls crayfish butter (30 gr) for sauce
- 6 tablespoon flour (60 gr) for sauce
- 7 dcl fish broth for sauce
- 2-3 egg yolks for sauce
- 4 tablespoon grated cheese
- 2 tablespoon crushed rusks
- 60 gr butter

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the soles as in recipe no. 371
2. For the lobster stew, fry butter and flour for 2 minutes
3. Dilute with fish broth and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
4. Add the beaten egg yolks and let the sauce simmer while whisking
5. Carefully mix in the lobster, cut into pieces, so it does not break apart, and season the stew with spices
6. Grease one or two fireproof dishes or preferably silver dishes with cold butter
7. Layer the fillets and stew alternately, with the first and last layer being fillets
8. Pour the sauce over and sprinkle the cheese, well mixed with the crushed rusks
9. Finally pour over the 60 gr melted butter
10. Gratinee as in the preceding recipe and serve with small boiled potatoes or mashed potatoes
11. Note: If fresh lobster is used, the gratin will be finer
