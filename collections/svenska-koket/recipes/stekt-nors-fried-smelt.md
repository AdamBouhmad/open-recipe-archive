## Ingredients

- 36 smelt
- 1 1/2 msk. salt
- 1 kkp wheat flour
- 2 kkp milk
- 1/2 kg. lard
- 1 lemon, in slices
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the smelt, rinse very well, rub with salt and let sit 1/4 hour
2. Dry with a fish towel
3. Thread through the eyes on fine wooden sticks, 6 on each
4. Dip first in milk, then in flour and let sit a while to dry
5. Fry in the hot lard and let drain a few moments on gray paper
6. Arrange on a warm dish without removing from the sticks
7. Garnish with fluted lemon slices and parsley
