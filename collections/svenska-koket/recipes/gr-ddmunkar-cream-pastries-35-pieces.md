## Ingredients

- 3/4 liter thick cream
- 10-12 tbsp. wheat flour
- 1 tbsp. water
- 1 tbsp. butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks.
2. Sift the flour and add it alternately with the water.
3. Heat the pastry pan slowly and grease it.
4. Take approximately 1 tbsp. of the batter for each pastry.
5. Bake the pastries over high heat on top of the stove.
6. Serve with jam.
