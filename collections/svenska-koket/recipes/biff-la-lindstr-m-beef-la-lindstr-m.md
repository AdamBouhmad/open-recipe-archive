## Ingredients

- 3 potatoes
- 2 beets
- 2 red onions
- 6 hg. boneless beef
- 1 dcl. thick cream
- 6 egg yolks
- 1 msk. finely chopped capers
- 1 small msk. salt
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 1 dcl. boiling broth

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes, let them cool and cut into fine cubes.
2. Cook the beets and cut into fine cubes; peel the onion, blanch and chop finely.
3. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and pass 4 times through a meat grinder, then mix with the egg yolks, potatoes, beets, onion and seasonings.
4. Form round beef patties, approximately 1 1/2 cm thick.
5. Heat a frying pan slowly until it becomes smoking hot, brown the butter in it, add the beef patties and fry for about 2 minutes on each side.
6. Place the beef patties on a warm serving dish, whisk the pan with the broth and pour the sauce over the beef patties.
7. Serve with fried potatoes as a breakfast dish.
