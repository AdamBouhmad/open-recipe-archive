## Ingredients

- 1 lit. fine, good apples
- 1 lit. good pears
- sugar to taste (about 3 dcl.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit, dry it, peel it and grate it on a grater or grind it through a mill and strain it.
2. Mix it with sugar to taste and pack it into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted ice cream mold.
3. Place a so-called butter paper on top of the fruit, put the lid on, pack the mold in ice and salt, and freeze the fruit for 4–5 hours.
4. Turn it out, garnish with compote of fruit or berries and serve with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
