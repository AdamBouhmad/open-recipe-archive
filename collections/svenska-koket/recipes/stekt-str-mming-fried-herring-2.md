## Ingredients

- 30 herrings
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 2 tablespoons crushed bread crumbs
- 80 gr. butter (for frying)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.) for sauce
- 1/2 tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings, remove the backbone, scrape away the dark membrane carefully and rinse very well, then wrap in a fish towel.
2. Rub the herrings with salt, dip in the beaten egg and roll in a mixture of flour and crushed bread crumbs.
3. Brown the butter, add the herrings, fry 7 minutes on each side and arrange on a warm serving dish.
4. For the sauce, brown the butter and parsley lightly, season with lemon juice and pour the sauce over the herrings.
5. Serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
