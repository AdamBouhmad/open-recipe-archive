## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter thick cream
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1 hg. biscuits
- 5 sheets gelatin
- redcurrant jelly for garnish, biscuits

## Directions

1. The cream is whipped with sugar to stiff peaks, and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in a little lukewarm water and the pounded biscuits are stirred in.
2. The cream is beaten into a rinsed and sugared mold and set out to stiffen.
3. When it is to be served, the mold is dipped for a moment in lukewarm water, and the pudding is turned out.
4. It is garnished with biscuits and redcurrant jelly.
