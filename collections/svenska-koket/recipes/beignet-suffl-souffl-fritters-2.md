## Ingredients

- 80 gr. butter
- 1/4 litre milk
- 150-200 gr. wheat flour
- 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil butter and milk together, carefully stir in the flour, and let the batter cook until it leaves the pan, then remove from heat.
2. Add the egg yolks immediately one by one, and when the batter is cold, carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Form small balls from the batter using teaspoons and cook them in hot oil.
4. When the pastries have taken on a nice light brown colour, place them on grey paper to drain and dust with fine sugar.
5. Serve warm or cold with jam.
