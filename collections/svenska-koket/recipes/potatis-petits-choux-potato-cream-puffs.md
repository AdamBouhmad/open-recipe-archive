## Ingredients

- 125 gr. butter
- 2 1/2 dcl. water
- 210 gr. flour
- 4 eggs
- 5 dcl. firm potato purée
- salt, sugar
- For cooking: 1 kg. lard

## Directions

1. Bring the butter and water to a boil, whisk in the flour over a full boil and let the batter cook until it releases from the pan and has a yellow color.
2. Pour it out and let it cool, then incorporate the eggs one at a time and stir the batter 10 minutes.
3. Prepare the potato purée as in recipe no. 677, let it cool and add it to the batter with vigorous stirring, then taste the mixture with the spices.
4. Form the mixture with two spoons, dipped in the lard, into round balls, which, if desired, can be filled with chopped truffle or peas, cook golden-brown in hot lard (see recipe no. 44) and place on blotting paper to drain.
5. Serve as a garnish around fried meat. If the balls are not filled, they should be made very small.
