## Ingredients

- 1 black grouse
- 1 hg. lard
- 3–4 tbsp. butter
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. broth
- 5 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. The black grouse hen has the finest meat and is calculated for 6 persons. A young black grouse cock also has delicious meat and serves 8–10 persons.
2. If the bird is frozen, it should be thawed in a warm kitchen, cleaned (see p. 299) and allowed to lie in lukewarm milk overnight.
3. Dry it thoroughly, lard it with fine strips of lard and truss it.
4. Roast the bird in a pot as with wood grouse, but it requires 2–3 hours of roasting, depending on whether it is a hen or cock.
5. Cut it, garnish (see p. 300) and serve with reduced sauce (see no. 429), added with thick cream and cold butter and, if desired, with 1 tbsp. red currant jelly.
