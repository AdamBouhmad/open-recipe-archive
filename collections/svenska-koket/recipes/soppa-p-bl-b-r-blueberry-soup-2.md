## Ingredients

- 1 litre fresh blueberries or 4 decilitres dried blueberries
- 2 litres water
- zest of 1/4 lemon
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- 1 1/2-2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 2 tablespoons potato flour (80 g)

## Directions

1. Clean the berries, rinse them in a sieve and let them drain
2. Place them in boiling water together with the lemon zest and cinnamon and let them cook until loose
3. Strain the berries, add sugar to the liquid, which then gets a boil and is thickened with potato flour mixed in 1/2 tablespoon cold water
4. Let the soup cook 2 minutes, taste it and pour it up to cool
5. Serve with klimp, whipped cream and rusks or biscuits
6. If using dried blueberries, rinse them very well in lukewarm water and let them soak overnight in the water in which they are to be cooked
