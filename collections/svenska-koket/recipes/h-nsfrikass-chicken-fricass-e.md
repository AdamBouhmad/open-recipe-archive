## Ingredients

- 2 young chickens, approximately 3 kg each
- 1 lemon
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt (23 g)
- 1 bay leaf
- 6 white peppercorns
- 1 clove
- 1 small wedge Portuguese onion
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 g) for the sauce
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 6 deciliters chicken broth
- 1 deciliter thick cream
- 2-3 egg yolks
- Salt and white pepper (sugar, optional)

## Directions

1. The chickens are plucked, singed, and cleaned (see page 299 onwards), rinsed very thoroughly, and each chicken is cut into 8 pieces, which are left to soak in fresh water for 3-4 hours.
2. They are removed, wrapped in cloth, placed in boiling water, which is skimmed well when it comes to a boil, after which the spices and onion are added and the meat is allowed to simmer slowly with the lid tightly closed until tender, approximately 1 1/2-2 hours.
