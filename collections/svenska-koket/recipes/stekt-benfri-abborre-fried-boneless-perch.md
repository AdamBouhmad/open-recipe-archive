## Ingredients

- 2 kg perch
- 1 1/2 msk salt
- 1 msk vinegar
- 1 kkp finely crushed hardtack
- 4 msk butter
- 3/4 liter boiling water
- 3 msk butter
- fish stock
- 1 del cream

## Directions

1. Scale, clean, rinse, and dry the fish thoroughly.
2. Cut it open along the back and remove the backbone along with all small bones.
3. Rub the fillets with vinegar and salt and dredge them in the finely crushed hardtack.
4. Butter a long pan with half of the butter and lay the perch in it; place the remaining butter in flakes on top of the fish.
5. Set it in the oven, and when it is browned, add the fish stock (made from the head, skin, and bones), and allow the perch to cook for approximately 20 minutes.
6. Whisk the pan with cream and pour the sauce over the fish.
