## Ingredients

- shell and roe of 2 lobsters
- 2 msk. butter (40 gr.)
- 1 msk. vinegar
- 2 msk. fine table oil
- 2 dcl. thick cream
- salt, sugar, paprika

## Directions

1. Pound the shells, roe, fat from the lobster and butter in a mortar to a fine paste, pass through a sieve and strain through muslin.
2. Add the vinegar and oil little by little to the strained paste, stirring constantly.
3. Carefully fold in the cream beaten to stiff peaks and season the sauce with spices.
4. Pour over chopped lobster or crayfish tails.
