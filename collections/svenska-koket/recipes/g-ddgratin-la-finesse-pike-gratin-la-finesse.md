## Ingredients

- 1 kg cooked, deboned fish meat or 1 1/4 kg pike
- 3 tablespoons butter for the dish (60 g)
- 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
- 3 tablespoons butter for the sauce (60 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 4 decilitres fish stock
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 6 whole eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the fish meat into beautiful, even slices
2. Butter a fireproof dish or silver dish with half of the butter
3. Lay the fish slices on it
4. Distribute the remaining butter in flakes over the fish
5. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes
6. Dilute with the fish stock and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes
7. Season to taste with salt, sugar, and white pepper and remove from heat to cool
8. Add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the sauce for 10 minutes
9. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
10. Pour the sauce over the fish
11. Sprinkle the grated cheese on top
12. Place in a hot oven to brown for 15-20 minutes
13. Serve, if desired, with lemon or mushroom sauce
14. Pike can also be prepared in the same way
