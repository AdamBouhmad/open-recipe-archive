## Ingredients

- 1 liter heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
- 15 gelatin leaves
- 1 cup strawberry jam
- Carmine coloring

## Directions

1. The cream is whisked to stiff peaks.
2. Half of it is mixed with the vanilla sugar and frozen in an ice machine.
3. The strawberries are strained and mixed with the remaining cream, and the mixture is colored with a little carmine.
4. The gelatin is rinsed with cold water, dissolved in a little hot water, and stirred in.
5. A bomb mold is lined with the frozen cream, filled with the pink mixture, and set to freeze further in ice and salt.
6. Between the cream and the lid, a layer of buttered paper is placed to prevent water or salt from entering.
