## Ingredients

- 3 hg fresh asparagus or 1 can omelet asparagus (1/2 liter)
- 80 gr butter
- 80 gr flour
- 2 dcl asparagus stock
- 3 dcl boiling milk
- 5-6 eggs
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the mold
- 2 tbsp bread crumbs for the mold

## Directions

1. Scrape the asparagus, rinse, cut into three pieces and cook until soft in 4 dcl water with 1/2 tsp salt.
2. If using canned asparagus, cut it in half and place on a sieve to drain.
3. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the boiling asparagus stock and milk and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
4. Pour into a dish; when cooled, add the egg yolks one at a time and stir the mixture for 10 minutes.
5. Mix in the well-drained asparagus along with the seasonings and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Taste the mixture, pour into a buttered and bread-crumbed pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 45 minutes.
7. If using a baking sheet form, bake the soufflé in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour and turn out.
8. Serve immediately when removed from the oven as a side course with hollandaise or creamed butter.
