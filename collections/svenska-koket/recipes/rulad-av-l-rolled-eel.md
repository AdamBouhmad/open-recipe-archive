## Ingredients

- 2 kg eel (in one piece)
- 2 msk salt
- a few allspice berries
- 4 msk vinegar
- 1½ liters water
- dill stalks
- 1 liter fish stock
- 8 gelatin sheets
- 2 egg whites

## Directions

1. Skin the eel and cut it open along the back.
2. Remove the backbone and innards, rinse and dry the eel well and rub it with salt.
3. Roll it up fairly tightly from the head and tie it with string.
4. Place the roll in hot water together with the dill stalks, allspice berries and vinegar and let it simmer slowly until done, then remove it and let it cool.
5. Add gelatin to the stock and clarify with egg whites and strain through a cloth or cheesecloth.
6. Pour part of the clarified aspic onto a flat dish to set.
7. Slice the roll, place these slices on the set aspic, and pour the remaining aspic over, then set it again.
8. Separate the slices with a knife from each other so that a little aspic remains around each slice.
9. Arrange them in a ring on a platter and garnish with dill.
