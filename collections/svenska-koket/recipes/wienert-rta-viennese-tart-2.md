## Ingredients

- 250 gr. butter
- 4 eggs
- 200 gr. caster sugar
- 100 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tbsp. butter
- 3 cups applesauce
- 3 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Clarify the butter and set it out to cool.
2. Then stir it together with the sugar until it becomes white and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time and finally the sifted wheat flour.
3. Bake the batter on a pancake griddle to 3 bottoms.
4. When these are cold, place them on top of each other with applesauce in between.
5. Garnish the tart with whipped cream.
