## Ingredients

- beets
- salt
- granulated sugar
- raw vinegar
- caraway seed (optional)

## Directions

1. Do not rinse the beets, but rub them only with a rough towel
2. Place in cold water with a little salt and let cook slowly until tender, which takes 3-4 hours
3. Drain the water immediately and let the beets cool
4. Peel and cut into thin slices
5. Layer the slices with granulated sugar in a jar
6. Pour raw vinegar over and it should stand well above the beets
7. Do not cover the jar, as the beets will easily mold
8. If desired, add a little caraway seed
9. Beets can also be roasted in the oven like potatoes and treated as described above
