## Ingredients

- 1 kg plums
- 1 kg lump sugar
- 3 parts water

## Directions

1. Blanch the plums very quickly, peel and pit them.
2. From the sugar and water, boil a clear syrup, thick enough that a drop on a plate does not spread out.
3. Add the plums, bring the jam to a boil, remove from heat, shake and skim.
4. Return to heat, bring to a boil, remove from heat, shake and skim.
5. Repeat this 4-5 times, or until the plums are clear and the syrup is thick.
6. Shake the jam until it has cooled, then immediately pour into prepared jars.
