## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups rice groats
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1 1/2 cups fresh or 1 1/2 hectograms dried apples
- 1–1 1/2 cups crushed sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice groats in lukewarm water.
2. Bring the water to a boil; add the groats, the peeled and chopped apples, and the sugar, and let the porridge cook for about 2 hours.
3. Taste and serve.
4. If using dried apples, rinse them and soak them for a few hours before cooking.
