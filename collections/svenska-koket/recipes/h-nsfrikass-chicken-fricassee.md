## Ingredients

- 2 young chickens, approximately 3 kg
- 1 lemon
- 1.5 liters water
- 1.5 tablespoons salt (23 g)
- 1 bay leaf
- 6 white peppercorns
- 1 clove
- 1 small wedge Portuguese onion
- 1.5 tablespoons butter (30 g)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 g)
- 6 deciliters chicken broth
- 1 part thick cream
- 2-3 egg yolks
- salt, white pepper, (sugar)

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the chickens, remove innards (see page 299 onwards), rinse very thoroughly and cut each chicken into 8 pieces
2. Let the pieces soak in fresh water for 3-4 hours
3. Remove the pieces, wrap in cloth and place in boiling water, which is skimmed well when it comes to a boil
4. Add the spices and onion and let the meat simmer slowly with a tight lid until tender, about 1.5-2 hours
5. Remove the pieces that are done first and keep them warm in a water bath
6. Reduce the broth and prepare sauce (see recipe no. 441), add the meat and shake together
7. On a round serving platter, arrange a ring of cooked rice (see garnish for meat), place the meat high in the middle and pour sauce over
8. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve very hot
