## Ingredients

- 8 hg pork fillet
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr)
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 3 dcl broth
- 1 dcl thick cream

## Directions

1. The fillets, weighing 2-4 hg each, are trimmed and dried with a cloth wrung out in hot water
2. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the fillets, brown well on all sides and sprinkle with seasoning
3. Add the boiling broth and cream, and let the meat cook slowly with a tight lid until tender, or about 1/2 hour
4. Cut the fillets into thin slices, which are placed together on a warm platter so they appear whole
5. Garnish with Russian peas and browned potatoes or with legumes
6. Strain the sauce (see recipe 429) and pour it over the meat
7. Serve with red currant jelly
