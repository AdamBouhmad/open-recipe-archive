## Ingredients

- 1 can pineapple (approximately 750 gr.)
- 4 eggs
- 75 gr. sugar
- 50 gr. wheat flour
- 15 gr. mondamin or cornstarch
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 50 gr. butter
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks for the mold
- 1 1/2 dcl. pineapple juice
- 1 1/2 dcl. French wine
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.) for the sauce
- 1 tsp. mondamin flour (5 gr.) for the sauce

## Directions

1. Half of the pineapple is sufficient for this pudding. The remainder can be used for cold pudding or mixed into compote.
2. Cut the pineapple into small cubes, which are wrapped in a cloth.
3. Whisk the egg yolks well with the sugar in a saucepan, add the flour and cream, and let the cream simmer over the fire with vigorous whisking until it is thick and fluffy.
4. Remove the saucepan from the heat, add the butter in small flakes and whisk the cream until it is cold.
5. Then stir in the drained pineapple and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie mold and bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 40 minutes.
7. Serve the pudding immediately upon removing it from the oven.
8. For the sauce, bring the pineapple juice to a boil with the wine and sugar, and thicken with the mondamin mixed in 1 tbsp. cold water, then let the sauce boil for 5 minutes.
9. Taste and serve warm.
