## Ingredients

- 750 gr. boneless veal loin
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons flour (20 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.)
- 2 dcl. broth
- 2 tablespoons Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat, cut it into 12 slices, which are pounded well, formed with a knife, sprinkled with spices and turned in flour.
2. Brown the butter in a frying pan, lay in the cutlets and fry beautifully brown on both sides, plate on a warm dish.
3. Whisk out the pan with the hot broth and wine, then pour the sauce over the cutlets.
4. Serve with braised vegetables.
