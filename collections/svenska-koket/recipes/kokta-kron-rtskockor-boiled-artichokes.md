## Ingredients

- 6 large artichokes
- water
- salt
- 100 grams butter
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Remove the lower leaves from the artichokes, and cut off a small piece from the remaining ones; rub the bottoms with lemon.
2. Place the artichokes in boiling, lightly salted water, enough to just cover them, and let them cook until the leaves come off easily.
3. Place them immediately on a warm serving plate and serve with melted butter.
