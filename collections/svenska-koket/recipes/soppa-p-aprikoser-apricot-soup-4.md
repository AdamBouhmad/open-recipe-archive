## Ingredients

- 250 gr dried apricots
- 2 liters water
- peel of 1/4 lemon
- 1-1 1/2 tablespoons crushed sugar
- 25 gr rice or potato flour

## Directions

1. Rinse the apricots very well in lukewarm water and let them soak overnight in the water in which they will be cooked.
2. Place them in the water with the lemon peel and sugar and let them cook until soft.
3. Thicken the soup with the flour, stirred in 1/2 tablespoon cold water, and let it cook for 5 minutes.
4. Flavor with the juice of 1/4 lemon and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and yeast rusks.
