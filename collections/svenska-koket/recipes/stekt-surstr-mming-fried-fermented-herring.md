## Ingredients

- 24 fermented herrings
- 1 bottle beer
- 1 liter cooked potatoes
- 2 red onions
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- 1 tablespoon crushed hardtack
- 2 gingerbread cookies
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 gr.) for the pan
- 1 tablespoon crushed hardtack for the pan

## Directions

1. Clean the herrings, remove the bones, rinse them and let them soak in beer for 2 hours.
2. Wrap the herrings in white paper.
3. Peel and slice the potatoes; peel and chop the onion finely.
4. Grease a fireproof pan with butter and sprinkle with crushed hardtack.
5. Layer the bottom of the pan with a layer of potatoes, then a layer of herring, then onion and butter flakes.
6. Continue layering until there are 5 layers of potatoes and 4 layers of herring.
7. Place the remaining butter in flakes on top, then sprinkle the crushed hardtack mixed with the crushed gingerbread cookies.
8. Fry the herring in a good oven for about 30 minutes.
9. Serve as a breakfast or supper course.
10. Note: The potatoes can be omitted and the herring fried in the same way. It can also be eaten directly from the barrel au naturel, rinsing the herring and serving with raw chopped onion and freshly boiled potatoes.
