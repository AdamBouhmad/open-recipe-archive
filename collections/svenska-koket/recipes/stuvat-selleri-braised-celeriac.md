## Ingredients

- 3 large celeriac roots
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt for blanching
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 g)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 g)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 4 deciliters boiling broth
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 deciliter cream
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Rinse, brush, and peel the celeriac roots, then place in cold salted water and simmer until tender, testing with a whisk.
2. Lay on a cloth to cool, then cut into cubes and blanch for 7–8 minutes with the lid open.
3. Drain in a colander and allow to drip dry on a cloth.
4. Prepare as braised celeriac (stuvade palstemackor, recipe no. 693).
