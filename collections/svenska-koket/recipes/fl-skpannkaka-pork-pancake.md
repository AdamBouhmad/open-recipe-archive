## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 liter milk
- 3 hectograms salt pork or smoked ham

## Directions

1. Cut the pork into cubes and fry quite hard in a frying pan and then pour off with its fat.
2. Wipe the pan with clean paper so all salt comes off, then pour the clear fat back in.
3. Prepare the pancake batter as before and pour into the pan.
4. Add the pork cubes and bake the pancake in a warm oven.
