## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ parts melted butter
- 100 gr. granulated sugar
- 2 eggs
- 80 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. sweet almond
- 2 tbsp. pearl sugar
- 1 tbsp. butter for the pan

## Directions

1. Whisk the butter and beat white.
2. Beat the eggs and sugar very well together, and add the butter.
3. Finally blend in the sifted wheat flour.
4. Blanch the almonds, chop them and mix with the pearl sugar.
5. Place a plätt pan in the oven to heat, then brush with melted butter.
6. Put ½ tbsp. of the batter in each cavity and sprinkle with almond and sugar.
7. Place the plätt pan in the oven, and when the pastries are baked, remove them and bend over a stick.
