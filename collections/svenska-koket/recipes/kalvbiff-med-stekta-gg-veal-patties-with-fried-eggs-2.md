## Ingredients

- 300 g boneless veal loin
- 100 g lard
- 1 egg
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/2 tbsp salt
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1 egg white
- 1/2 tbsp water
- 1 cup crushed breadcrumbs
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 2 dcl good broth
- 1 tbsp tomato purée
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 g)
- salt, white pepper
- 6-8 eggs
- 1 tbsp butter (20 g)

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, cut into pieces and grind together with the lard 4 times through a meat grinder.
2. Mix the forcemeat with the beaten eggs and spices and form into round patties, which are brushed with the egg white, whisked with water, and turned in crushed breadcrumbs.
3. Heat a frying pan slowly, brown the butter in it, place the patties in and fry 2 minutes on each side, then place them on a hot serving platter.
4. Pour the broth into the pan, which is whisked out well, the tomato purée is added, the sauce comes to a boil, is seasoned with the cold butter and spices and poured over the patties.
5. Fry the eggs in a griddle and place one egg on each patty.
6. Serve as a breakfast course.
