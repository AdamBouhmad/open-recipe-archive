## Ingredients

- 10 liters cloudberries
- 4 liters water
- 0.25 kg sugar per kg juice

## Directions

1. Clean and rinse the berries. They are put on with water and cooked until they are properly broken down.
2. The mass is then poured and allowed to cool somewhat. The juice is then pressed through a strainer cloth and cooked with sugar with skimming until it becomes sticky and is free from froth. It is bottled warm or cold, the bottles are corked and sealed with resin.
