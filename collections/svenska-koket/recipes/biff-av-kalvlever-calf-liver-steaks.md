## Ingredients

- 5 hectograms calf liver
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 gr.) for frying
- 1 red onion
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 2 1/2 tablespoons flour (25 gr.)
- 4 deciliters broth or water
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- salt, sugar, white pepper

## Directions

1. Let the liver soak in cold water for about 1 hour, dry with a meat cloth and remove the membrane.
2. Cut into slices 1 cm thick, sprinkle with salt and pepper and dredge in flour.
3. Brown the butter, add the steaks and fry until nicely brown on both sides, then place on a warm plate.
4. Peel the onion, cut into fine dice, brown in butter, which is then fried with flour for 2 minutes.
5. Pour in the broth and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes, then season with soy sauce and spices.
6. Pour the sauce over the steaks, which can be garnished with fried, salted bacon slices.
7. Serve with potatoes as a breakfast dish.
