## Ingredients

- 2.5 kg calf meat
- 2 calf legs
- 2 liters water
- white peppercorns
- salt
- ground white pepper
- grated ginger

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water and place it in boiling water.
2. When the broth comes to a boil, skim it well and add the peppercorns and 1.5 tablespoons salt.
3. Let it simmer slowly until the meat is tender.
4. Remove the meat, let it cool and then chop it fairly finely.
5. Let the broth reduce during this time.
6. Add the meat and bring everything to a boil.
7. Season strongly with salt, pepper and ginger.
