## Ingredients

- 0.5 liters cherries
- 1 coffee spoon granulated sugar
- 2.5 parts water
- 1 liter thick cream
- 1 coffee spoon sifted sugar
- 0.5 parts cognac
- 25 grams cocoa
- 15 sheets gelatin

## Directions

1. Cook the cherries with sugar and water until they are soft, then strain off the juice.
2. Dissolve five sheets of gelatin in a little lukewarm water and mix with the cherry juice.
3. Rinse a pretty mold with cold water and fill it one-third with cherry jelly, then set it out to stiffen.
4. Whisk the cream with sugar to stiff peaks, flavor one half with cognac and the other half with cocoa.
5. Dissolve five sheets of gelatin in a little lukewarm water and stir into the white cream, then pour this onto the set jelly so that the mold is two-thirds filled.
6. Set the cream out to stiffen.
7. Dissolve the remaining gelatin in a little lukewarm water and stir into the cocoa cream, and fill the mold with this.
8. Set it out to stiffen.
9. When it is to be served, dip the mold quickly in lukewarm water and turn out the cream.
