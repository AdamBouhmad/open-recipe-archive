## Ingredients

- 225 gr. flour
- 1 hg. butter
- 5 gr. salt
- 10 gr. sugar
- 1 dcl. water
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 can omelet asparagus (1/2 liter)
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 6 tbsp. flour (60 gr.)
- 1 dcl. asparagus stock
- 6 dcl. cream
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tbsp. butter (20 gr.) for the forms

## Directions

1. Prepare the shortcrust dough as in recipe no. 35 and let it rest 1 hour.
2. Roll out the dough very thinly and cut with a round mold into cakes, with which smooth pastry forms are lined.
3. Cut appropriately large round forms from so-called butter paper, which are placed inside the lined forms, which are then filled with peas and placed in the oven to bake.
4. Remove the peas and paper, unmold the tartlets and fill with the stew.
5. Prepare the stew as in recipe no. 28. All the fillings mentioned for crusts can also be used for tartlets.
6. Serve as a middle course.
