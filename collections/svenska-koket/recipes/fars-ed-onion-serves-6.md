## Ingredients

- 2 large or 3 smaller Portuguese onions
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 2 hg beef
- 1/2 hg veal
- 1 tablespoon flour (10 gr)
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 dcl cream
- 1 tablespoon chopped mushrooms
- For browning: 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr), 3 dcl broth

## Directions

1. Peel the onion and blanch for about 15 minutes, or until almost tender.
2. Prepare the dish as in the preceding recipe and serve with the smörgåsbord.
