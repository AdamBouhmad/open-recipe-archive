## Ingredients

- 20 g gelatin (approximately 12 leaves)
- pineapple juice and water together 1 liter
- juice of 1 lemon
- 225 g sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 dcl white wine
- 1 can pineapple (approximately 750 g)

## Directions

1. Open the pineapple can first, carefully save the juice, and cut the pineapple into beautiful slices
2. Prepare the jelly as in no. 924 and when it is clear, lay the pineapple slices in the mold in the same manner as is described for grapes in the same number
