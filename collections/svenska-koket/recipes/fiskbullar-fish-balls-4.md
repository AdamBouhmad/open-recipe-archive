## Ingredients

- 3 cups cooked fish (leftovers)
- 2 cups mashed potatoes
- 1 egg
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- salt
- 2 eggs for coating
- 1.5 cups crushed rusks for coating
- 3/4 kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Free the cooked fish from skin and bones and chop finely.
2. Mix with the mashed potatoes, the beaten egg and the spices. The mixing should be done with a fork and not with a spoon, so that the mixture does not pack.
3. Form croquettes or small round balls from the mixture; roll these in crushed rusks, dip in egg and roll again in crushed rusks, then let dry for 1/2 hour.
4. Heat the lard, fry the fish balls golden brown in it and place on gray paper to drain.
5. Garnish with parsley and serve with mushroom or regular fish sauce.
