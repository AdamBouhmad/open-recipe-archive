## Ingredients

- 1 liter shelled garden beans
- 1 1/2 liters water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter (30 gr.)
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 gr.)
- 3 dcl. bean broth
- 3 dcl. milk
- 3 hg. smoked ham
- salt, sugar, white pepper
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the sprouts; if the beans are very developed, peel them as well.
2. Boil the water with salt, add the beans and let them cook until soft.
3. Drain them in a colander and save the broth.
4. Fry butter and flour for 2 minutes, add the bean broth and milk and let the sauce cook for 5 minutes.
5. Add the ham, cut into cubes, and bring the stew to a boil.
6. Taste and season with the spices and the finely chopped parsley and serve as a breakfast dish.
