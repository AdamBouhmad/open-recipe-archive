## Ingredients

- 350 gr. wheat flour
- 210 gr. butter
- 1 1/2 dcl. milk

## Directions

1. Sift the flour. Work the butter into half of the flour with fingertips, then add the milk and finally the rest of the flour except 1 kkp, which is set aside for baking.
2. Work the dough until it becomes even and smooth, then cover it and let it stand in a cold place for one day.
3. Roll out the dough thinly, prick it well and cut out with a large round mould into cookies, which are baked light yellow in a warm oven.
4. Serve with marmalade or butter.
