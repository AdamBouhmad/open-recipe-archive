## Ingredients

- 210 gr. powdered sugar
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 egg yolks
- zest of 1/2 lemon or 10 bitter almonds
- 55 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. potato flour
- 3 egg whites
- 15 gr. butter for the pan
- 3 tbsp. crushed rusks for the pan

## Directions

1. Sift the sugar and whip with the whole eggs and egg yolks for about 1 hour
2. Add the finely grated lemon zest or the blanched and ground almonds
3. Add the sifted wheat flour and potato flour
4. Lastly, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks
5. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-strewn ring mold or baking pan
6. Bake in a not-too-warm oven for about 40 minutes
7. The cake can advantageously be split, filled with cream or fruit, glazed, decorated with crystallized fruit, and garnished with fruit
