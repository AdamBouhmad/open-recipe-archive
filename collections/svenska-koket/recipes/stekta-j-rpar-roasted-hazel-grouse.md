## Ingredients

- 3-4 hazel grouse
- 1 hg. lard in 3-4 slices
- 3-4 tablespoons butter (60-80 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 3 dcl. broth
- 4 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Handle and roast as partridge.
2. May also be roasted as pheasant (see no. 614).
3. Split lengthwise, but only the breasts are served.
4. Garnish with browned potatoes, cooked apple halves filled with currant jelly, and salad or parsley.
5. Serve with a reduced sauce mixed with cream and cold butter (see no. 429) and, if desired, with 1 tablespoon currant jelly.
