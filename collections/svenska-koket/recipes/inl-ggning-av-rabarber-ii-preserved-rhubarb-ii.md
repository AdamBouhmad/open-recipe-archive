## Ingredients

- Rhubarb
- Boiling water

## Directions

1. Rinse the rhubarb, peel it thinly, and cut it into pieces, which are placed in well-cleaned and dry bottles.
2. Place these in a cold oven, which is gradually heated.
3. When the rhubarb is soft, fill the flasks with boiling water, after which they are immediately corked and bound.
