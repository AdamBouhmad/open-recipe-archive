## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg veal breast
- 2 liters water
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1/2 bunch parsley
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1–2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Break and wash the breast with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Place it in boiling water, skim, and salt.
3. Let the meat simmer slowly with the lid on until tender, about 2 hours.
4. Remove and cut into appropriately sized pieces.
5. Prepare the sauce according to general rules for sauces and serve over the meat.
