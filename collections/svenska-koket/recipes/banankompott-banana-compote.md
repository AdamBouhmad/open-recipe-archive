## Ingredients

- 3 hg. sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 1/2 vanilla bean
- 9 bananas

## Directions

1. Cook the sugar and water with the vanilla in an enameled iron pot until a clear, thin syrup forms.
2. Peel the bananas, cut into halves if desired, place in the sugar syrup, cover with a lid, and let the bananas rest in the syrup, which should be kept at 90°, until they are clear and soft, but do not allow them to boil.
3. Transfer to a compote bowl, reduce the syrup if desired, and pour over.
4. Serve with biscuits or sliced almonds.
