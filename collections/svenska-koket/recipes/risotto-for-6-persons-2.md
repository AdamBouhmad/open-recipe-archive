## Ingredients

- 1 medium Portuguese onion
- 150 gr. butter
- 180 gr. Carolina rice grains
- 1 gr. ground saffron
- 1/2 liter good broth
- 2 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 hectogram smoked ham
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel the onion, chop it finely and fry well in 1 hectogram of butter in a saucepan.
2. Add the rinsed rice grains together with the finely ground saffron and stir over gentle heat until well mixed with the butter.
3. Add the broth a little at a time while stirring the grains frequently with a wooden spoon.
4. Allow the rice grains to cook slowly with a lid, stirring often, until the grains are soft and begin to break apart.
5. Season the dish with the remaining butter, Parmesan cheese, and the ham cut into fine cubes, plus salt.
6. Serve as a breakfast or intermediate course.
