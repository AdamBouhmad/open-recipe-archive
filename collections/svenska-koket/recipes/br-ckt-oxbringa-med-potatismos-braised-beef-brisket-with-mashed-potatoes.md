## Ingredients

- 3 hg. cooked, salted beef brisket (leftovers)
- 1/2 lit. mashed potatoes (leftovers)
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)

## Directions

1. Cut the beef brisket while it is warm into fine strips and mix with the warm mashed potatoes.
2. Cut the mixture into slices and fry in the butter and place on a warm platter.
3. Garnish with parsley and serve as a breakfast dish. Cooked, salted lamb brisket can also advantageously be used.
