## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 100 gr. sugar
- 50 gr. wheat flour
- 50 gr. potato starch
- 5 parts thick cream
- 100 gr. butter
- 400 gr. pineapple
- 0.5 tablespoon butter and 2 tablespoons crushed rusks for the mold
- pineapple juice, white wine, caster sugar, and 10 gr. potato starch for the sauce

## Directions

1. Whip the egg yolks, sugar, flour and cream together over heat with vigorous whisking until the cream thickens.
2. Remove the pan from heat, but continue whisking, gradually blending in the butter.
3. Cut the pineapple into pieces and fold in together with the egg whites whipped to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the batter into a buttered and rusk-strewn mold and bake in a not too hot oven for about 1 hour.
5. Serve warm with a sauce made from the pineapple juice, seasoned with white wine and sugar to taste and thickened with a little potato starch.
