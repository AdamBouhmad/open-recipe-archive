## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 5 decilitres meat or fish stock or 6 decilitres good milk
- 2-3 tablespoons grated horseradish
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Fry one tablespoon of the butter with the flour for 2 minutes, add the stock or milk and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Add the horseradish along with the rest of the butter and season the sauce with spices.
3. To prepare horseradish: scrape it, rinse it, let it soak in cold water for 1 hour, dry it with a cloth and grate it on a grater.
