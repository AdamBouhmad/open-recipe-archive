## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 2½ liters broth
- 1 knife point celery seed
- ½ tbsp. butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- white pepper
- salt
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Raw peel the potatoes and cook them in 1 liter of the broth until soft, after which they are passed through a sieve.
2. Fry butter and flour together, dilute with the purée and the remaining broth, and let the soup cook for 10 minutes. Season, whip the egg yolks in the soup bowl, and pour in the soup with vigorous whisking.
