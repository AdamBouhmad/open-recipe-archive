## Ingredients

- 12 veal cutlets
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 1.5 cups crushed breadcrumbs
- 1/2 cup grated cheese (preferably Parmesan)
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 liter broth or water
- 2 cups "ideal macaroni"
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons tomato purée or melted butter

## Directions

1. Wash the cutlets with a cloth dipped in hot water, trim and pound once on each side.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, brush over with the beaten eggs and turn in the crushed breadcrumbs mixed with cheese.
3. Brown them in the butter, add the broth, and let the cutlets fry until done in 1/4 hour.
4. Rinse the macaroni and place in salted boiling water. When tender, strain and rinse with water to prevent sticking.
5. Mix in the tomato purée or melted butter with careful stirring.
6. Serve the veal cutlets in the middle of the serving platter and the macaroni around them.
