## Ingredients

- Large crabs (or medium-sized for finer flavor)
- Water
- Coarse salt
- Watercress
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Cook the crabs the same way as lobster, but allow approximately 1/2 hour until they are ready.
2. Serve cold.
3. Alternatively: loosen the loose meat with a fork and chop it finely with an equal amount of watercress, then blend with a few drops of oil and vinegar.
4. Fill this mixture into the crab shells, place them on a platter, and garnish with the claws and parsley.
5. Serve without sauce.
