## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters water
- 100 grams finely ground coffee

## Directions

1. For brewing coffee, special apparatuses are usually used, but a coffee strainer made of some thin, tight fabric (such as raw silk) and two coffee makers or fireproof pots can be used. Pour the coffee into the strainer, pour the boiling water through the coffee, and the filtering proceeds in this way, continuously with boiling liquid, until the coffee is sufficiently strong.
