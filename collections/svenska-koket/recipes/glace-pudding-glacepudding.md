## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. thin heavy cream
- 1 vanilla pod
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 hg. sugar
- 50 gr. biscuits
- 10 cloudberries or 6 pears
- 5 dcl. extra thick heavy cream

## Directions

1. Cook the thin heavy cream with the vanilla pod and let it stand covered for 20 minutes to infuse
2. Stir the egg yolks with the sugar in a pot for 15 minutes
3. Add the cream while whisking and let the cream simmer over heat with continued whisking until thick and fluffy
4. Pour the cream and whisk until cold
5. Crush the biscuits finely, drain the fruit, cut it in half and remove the seeds
6. Whip the heavy cream to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the cream
7. In a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted glace mold, layer the cream, crushed biscuits and fruit alternately
8. The first and last layer should be cream
9. Freeze the pudding as described
