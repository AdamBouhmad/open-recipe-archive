## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg halibut
- 3 tbsp salt
- 1.5 tsp vinegar
- water
- 1.5 tbsp butter
- 2.5 tbsp flour
- fish broth
- pepper
- 3 parts cream
- salt
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Free the fish from skin and bones and cut into 2 cm thick slices, which are rubbed in with salt and vinegar.
2. Place it in warm, but not boiling water, let it simmer slowly until it is soft and lay it on the serving platter.
3. Melt the butter and flour together, add the fish broth and cream with careful stirring, and let the sauce boil for 10 minutes.
4. Taste the sauce and pour it over the fish on the platter.
5. Garnish the dish with hard-boiled eggs, cut into wedges.
