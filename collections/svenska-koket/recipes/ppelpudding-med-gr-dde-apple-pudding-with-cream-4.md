## Ingredients

- 5 deciliters apple pulp
- 50 grams sweet almonds
- 4 deciliters thick cream
- 6 sheets gelatin
- 3 tablespoons boiling water for the gelatin

## Directions

1. The apple pulp should be sifted, fairly firm and pleasantly sweet.
2. Blanch the almonds, dry them well in the oven, grind them through an almond mill and mix with the apple pulp together with the cream whipped to stiff peaks.
3. Finally add the rinsed gelatin that has been dissolved in the boiling water and allowed to cool.
4. Fill the mixture into a rinsed and sugar-dusted tin mold and let stand on ice for approximately 3 hours.
5. When the pudding is to be served, loosen it with a pointed knife around the edge of the mold and turn it out onto a cake plate.
6. Garnish, if desired, with fruit and biscuits.
