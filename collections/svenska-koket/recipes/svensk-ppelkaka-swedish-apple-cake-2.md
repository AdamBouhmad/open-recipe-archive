## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters fresh or 1 1/2 hg dried apples
- 1/2 25-öre spice bread, not too fresh
- 2 hg sugar
- 150 gr butter
- 1-1 1/2 dcl water
- 1/2 tbsp butter for the mold
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, peel them, cut into fine wedges, remove the cores and cook with the sugar and a little water to a puree
2. Grate the spice bread and lightly brown it in 1 hg of the butter
3. Grease a mold or frying pan with butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks, layer three layers of bread and two layers of apple puree. Top layer is covered with the remaining butter, divided into small flakes
4. Bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 1/2 hour, then remove and let stand for 15 minutes
5. Turn out, sprinkle with sugar and serve warm or cold with vanilla sauce or whipped cream
6. If using dried apples, rinse them well and let them soak in 2 dcl water for a few hours, then cook with 150 gr sugar to a puree
7. Instead of apples, gooseberries or rhubarb can be used, but more sugar is needed then
8. The cake can be made from 6 dcl crushed rusks instead of spice bread
