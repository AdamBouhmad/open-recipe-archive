## Ingredients

- 4 kg. lamb roast
- 3 hg. lard
- 2 tablespoon salt (30 gr.)
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 1 hg. butter
- 1 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. From a young lamb, cut away the two connected roasts.
2. The legs are removed but roasted with, and some of the fat is trimmed away.
3. The meat is treated, roasted, and served as in the preceding recipe.
4. The roast is carved, the slices are placed back together so that it appears whole, and garnished with cooked vegetables.
