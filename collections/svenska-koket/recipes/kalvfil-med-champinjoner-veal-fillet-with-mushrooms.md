## Ingredients

- 600 g veal fillet or boneless veal loin
- 3 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 egg white
- 1 dcl cream or broth
- salt, 1 tsp butter
- 3 tbsp butter (60 g)
- 1 1/2 dcl broth
- 1 can mushrooms (1/4 liter)
- 1 tbsp butter (20 g)

## Directions

1. Dry the meat with a cloth wrung out in hot water, and cut into 6 slices, which are flattened to half their thickness and rubbed with the spices.
2. For the egg custard, beat the egg yolks and egg white well with the cream or broth and a little salt. Pour the mixture into a well-buttered mold or bowl with a lid and place in a pot with boiling water. The water should be level with the egg mixture but must not boil, as then the egg custard becomes watery and cannot be used. When it has set after about 1/2 hour it is done and is then cut into 6 squares.
3. Fry the fillets about 3 minutes on each side and place on a warm serving platter. Whisk out the frying pan with the broth and pour the sauce over the meat. Fry the mushrooms in the butter until it is clear, place one egg square on each fillet and the mushrooms around it.
4. Garnish with parsley and serve very hot with browned potatoes as a breakfast dish.
