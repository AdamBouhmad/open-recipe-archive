## Ingredients

- 1 liter evenly-sized, oblong potatoes
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter (50 grams)
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 1 teaspoon salt (5 grams)

## Directions

1. The potatoes are treated as described on page 337 et seq. and cut into 1/2 cm thick slices, which are arranged so that the potato retains its form. A frying pan or omelet form is buttered with half of the butter, the potato slices are placed in it, tightly next to each other, the rest of the butter is placed in flakes on top, and finally a mixture of the crushed rusks and cheese is sprinkled over. The potatoes are baked in the oven until soft and beautifully golden brown. It is placed on a warm platter (preferably silver) and boiled vegetables are garnished around it. Served with roasted meat.
