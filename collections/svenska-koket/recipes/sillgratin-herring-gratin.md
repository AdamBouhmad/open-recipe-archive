## Ingredients

- 2 large salted fatty herrings
- 3/4 litre raw potatoes
- 2 red onions
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 4 tablespoons butter (80 g)
- 2 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold (10 g)
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Soak the herring in water for 24 hours, changing the water 3 times.
2. Skin, remove, fillet the herring down the middle, then remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets well, wrap in a fish towel, and cut each herring half into six pieces.
4. Rinse the potatoes, peel and cut into thin slices.
5. Peel the onion and chop finely.
6. Butter a fireproof omelet mold with cold butter and sprinkle with crushed rusks.
7. Layer on the bottom of the mold: a layer of potatoes, then a layer of herring, then onion and a little pepper. Continue until there are 3 layers of potatoes and 2 layers of herring.
8. Place the butter in flakes on top and sprinkle rusks over it.
9. Bake in good oven heat for about 45 minutes and serve as breakfast or with the smörgåsbord.
