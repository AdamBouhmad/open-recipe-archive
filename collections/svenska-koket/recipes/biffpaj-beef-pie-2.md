## Ingredients

- 1 kg. beef chuck
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsp. white pepper
- 2 red onions or 1 Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 liter potatoes (optional)
- 4 dcl. good broth
- 1 tbsp. flour (10 gr.)
- 1 hg. cooked, cold grated potato
- 1 hg. butter
- 1 hg. flour

## Directions

1. If a beef cooker is lacking, the beef can be arranged in a fireproof so-called pie form with a pastry lid, and is then called beef pie.
2. Prepare as in recipe 485, with or without potatoes. In the latter case, serve the potatoes with the pie. It can also be supplemented with cooked ham or calf kidney, according to taste.
3. Whisk the broth with the flour and add last.
4. For the pie dough, mix all ingredients on the work bench and roll out the dough to 1 cm. thickness.
5. Cut a lid for the form, brush the edges of the form with water, and press the dough lid firmly.
6. With a fork or larding needle, make 4-6 holes in the dough so that the gases formed during baking may escape.
7. Brush the pie with egg white and garnish with the rest of the dough, cut into beautiful figures, which are also brushed with egg white.
8. Bake in ordinary oven heat for approximately 1 1/2 hours. Serve from the form.
9. The pie can also be baked in a tinned copper form or sheet form, but then double the amount of dough is needed, and the form is lined with it. Otherwise prepare as above.
