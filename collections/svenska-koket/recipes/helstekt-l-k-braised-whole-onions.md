## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg small Portuguese onions
- 1 liter water
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 60 grams butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 gr)

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, blanch them (place in boiling salted water and let cook 10 minutes), lay on cloth and let drain.
2. Melt the butter in a saucepan, add the onions, sprinkle with sugar and let slowly fry covered until they are beautifully brown and completely soft, which is tested with a whisk.
3. Serve with the sandwich table or with fried meat, such as roast beef, roast lamb, and lamb saddle.
