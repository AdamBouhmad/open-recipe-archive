## Ingredients

- 3 sole fillets of approximately 5 hg. each
- 1 tablespoon salt (15 g.)
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter for cooking
- 4 1/2 dcl. fish stock
- 3 hg. spinach
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g.)
- 4 dcl. hollandaise sauce
- 12 shrimp tails for garnish
- 2-3 truffles
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the sole as in the previous recipe.
2. Blanch the spinach in lightly salted water, drain, squeeze in a cloth, chop coarsely, and cook in butter for approximately 20 minutes and spread on the bottom of an oblong silver dish.
3. Lay the fillets on top, pour 4 dcl. hollandaise sauce over, and garnish the dish with shrimp tails, truffle sliced, and parsley. Serve with small, boiled, floury potatoes.
