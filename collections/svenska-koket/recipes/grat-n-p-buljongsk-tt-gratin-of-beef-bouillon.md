## Ingredients

- 400 g boneless beef bouillon meat
- 1-2 red onions
- 4 tbsp butter (80 g)
- 2 tsp salt (10 g)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 3 dcl beef stock or bouillon
- 1 liter potatoes
- 1 tbsp butter (20 g)
- 1.5-2 dcl boiling milk
- 2 eggs
- salt
- sugar
- 1 tbsp finely crushed bread crusts
- 1-2 tbsp grated cheese
- 2 tbsp melted butter (20 g)
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 g) for the dish

## Directions

1. Chop the meat finely (do not grind). Peel the onion, chop it finely and brown it in butter, then add the meat and brown it well, sprinkle with spices and thin it with the boiling beef stock or bouillon.
2. Peel potatoes raw, cut into pieces, boil quickly, drain well and press through a potato ricer.
3. Place the mash in a saucepan, add the cold butter immediately and work the mash with a fork, thin it with the boiling milk, add the whisked egg yolks and stir until it is white and fluffy. Season with salt and sugar and finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Grease an ovenproof dish or pie pan with butter, place the meat on it, spread the potato mash over the meat, sprinkle with bread crusts mixed with cheese, and finally pour the melted butter over it.
5. Place the dish in a hot oven and gratinee for 20-30 minutes. Serve with gravy or melted butter. If desired, the meat can be browned in cream instead of butter; in the potato mash, butter and eggs can be omitted.
