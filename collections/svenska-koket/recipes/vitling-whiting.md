## Ingredients

- Fresh whiting
- Unsalted butter
- Chopped parsley
- Lemon slices
- Browned butter
- Maitre-d'hôtel butter
- Parsley sauce
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Cook the fresh whiting for a very short time, 3-4 minutes
2. Serve with parsley sauce or melted butter and chopped parsley
3. Best pan-fried in unsalted butter, garnish with lemon slices and serve with browned butter or maitre-d'hôtel butter
