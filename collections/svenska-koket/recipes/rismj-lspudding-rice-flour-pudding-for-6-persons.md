## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter good milk
- 60 gr butter
- 1 dcl rice flour (67 gr)
- Peel of half a lemon or 6 bitter almonds
- 2 tbsp sugar (30 gr)
- 4-5 eggs
- 3 dcl granulated sugar for the mold (255 gr)
- 2 tbsp boiling water

## Directions

1. Place the sugar in a somewhat warm skillet buttered with 1/2 tsp butter, and stir with a wooden spoon until melted and light brown in color.
2. Add the water and use this to coat a baking pan.
3. Bring the milk to a boil with the butter, whisk in the flour under full boiling and allow the porridge to achieve a good boil, then pour it out and let it cool.
4. Add the grated lemon peel or the blanched and ground almonds, sugar, and egg yolks, one at a time.
5. Finally fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
6. Pour the batter into the coated mold, cover it with buttered paper, and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for about 1 hour.
7. Turn out and serve warm with vanilla or lemon sauce, if desired.
