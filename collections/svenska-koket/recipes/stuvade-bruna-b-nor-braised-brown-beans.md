## Ingredients

- 3 cups brown beans
- 2 liters water
- 4 tablespoons syrup
- 2-4 tablespoons vinegar
- 2 teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Clean the beans and soak the day before use.
2. Place in cold, soft water and cook slowly until soft.
3. Season with syrup, vinegar, and salt.
