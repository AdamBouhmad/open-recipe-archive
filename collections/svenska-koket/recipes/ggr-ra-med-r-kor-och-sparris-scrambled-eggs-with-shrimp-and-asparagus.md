## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 hg. shrimp
- 12 sprigs tender asparagus buds or canned asparagus
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 6 eggs
- 4 dcl. boiling milk

## Directions

1. Let the shrimp soak in milk for about 1 hour, then clean them.
2. Blanch the asparagus for a few minutes in lightly salted water, let it drain, and fry it well in half of the butter until the butter is clear.
3. Whisk the eggs well and add the boiling milk while whisking.
4. Melt the remaining butter in a saucepan, pour in the egg mixture and cook in a water bath while stirring.
5. When it is half set, add the shrimp and asparagus and let the scrambled eggs finish cooking.
6. Arrange on a hot plate, garnish with parsley and serve to the sandwich board.
