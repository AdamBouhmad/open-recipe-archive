## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 liters potatoes
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 gr.)
- water
- 3 tablespoons crushed rusks
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 gr.)
- 2 teaspoons sugar (10 gr.)

## Directions

1. The potatoes are treated as described on page 337 and onward, boiled with skin on and allowed to cool. They are peeled and formed with a smooth vegetable cutter or knife into small, even balls, which are shaken in a mixture of the crushed rusks and sugar; what is cut off from the potatoes is fried separately or used for potato balls. The butter is browned in a frying pan, the potatoes are added; sprinkled with the rusks that have not stuck to them, and browned nicely with shaking. Served as garnish around meat or together with vegetables.
