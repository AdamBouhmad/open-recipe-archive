## Ingredients

- 150 gr ideal macaroni
- 1 1/2 lit water
- 1/2 tbsp salt
- 4 hg smoked ham
- 4 eggs
- 3 cups milk
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp sugar
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr)
- 2 tbsp crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Rinse the macaroni, add to boiling salted water and let cook 15-20 minutes
2. Strain in a colander and let the water drain well
3. Cut the ham into small cubes, mix well with the macaroni and place the mass in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined pie form
4. Whisk the eggs well with the milk and spices, and pour the egg mixture over the dish in the form
5. Bake the pudding in ordinary oven heat approximately 45 minutes
6. Serve as a breakfast dish
