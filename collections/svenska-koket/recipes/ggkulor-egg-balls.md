## Ingredients

- 2 fresh eggs
- 25 gr. fine butter

## Directions

1. Hard boil the eggs for 10 minutes and let them lie in cold water for a few minutes so the shell comes off easily.
2. Shell them, let them cool, and carefully separate the white from the yolk.
3. Finely chop the yolk with a knife and work it into the butter.
4. Place the egg mixture in a cold place to harden.
5. Then roll it with two teaspoons, which are dipped in water, into small balls, which are arranged pyramidically in a salad bowl.
6. Serve to clear broth soups.
