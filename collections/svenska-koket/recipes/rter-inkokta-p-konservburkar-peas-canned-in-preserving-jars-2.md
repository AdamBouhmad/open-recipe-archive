## Ingredients

- Peas
- Salt
- 1 tablespoon salt per liter water

## Directions

1. Shell the peas, separate the smaller from the larger, and rinse the peas.
2. Place them then on preserving jars and pour over with cold, boiled salt brine (1 tablespoon salt to each liter of water).
3. Screw the jars shut or solder them and cook according to general rules 0.5–1 hour, depending on the size of the peas.
4. The peas can be pre-blanched 3 minutes in lightly salted water.
