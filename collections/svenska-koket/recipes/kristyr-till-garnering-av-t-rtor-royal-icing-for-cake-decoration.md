## Ingredients

- 1 egg white
- 120-150 gr sifted powdered sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Whip the egg white, add the sugar little by little, and work the royal icing well until it is smooth and shiny and so firm that if you pipe it out with a spoon, the lines do not flow out.
2. Place the icing in a paper cone, cut at the point or with a round hole, and pipe out over the cake in the pattern you desire.
