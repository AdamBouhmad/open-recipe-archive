## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp. ground cardamom
- 300 gr. butter
- 125 gr. sweet almond
- 50 gr. bitter almond
- 210 gr. brown sugar
- 425 gr. wheat flour
- 1 tsp. ammonium carbonate
- 2 tbsp. pearl sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the egg with the spices.
2. Stir in the melted butter, the unblached and coarsely cut almond and the sugar.
3. Sift the flour with the ammonium carbonate and work in last.
4. Form small round balls from the dough, which are dipped in pearl sugar.
5. Bake the cookies in moderate oven heat.
