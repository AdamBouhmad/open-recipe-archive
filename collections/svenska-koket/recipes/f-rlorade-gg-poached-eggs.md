## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- water
- 1 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp vinegar

## Directions

1. Bring water with vinegar and salt to a boil in a frying pan
2. Break one egg at a time carefully into a coffee cup and let it slowly slide into the water, which should only simmer gently
3. When the white has set, remove the egg with a slotted spoon, shape it with a glass, and place it on the serving plate
