## Ingredients

- 1 hectogram dried apples
- 1 hectogram dried pears
- 1/2 hectogram raisins
- 2 liters water
- 1 piece cinnamon (5 cm)
- Zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1.5 to 2 cups crushed sugar
- 25 grams rice or potato flour
- 1/2 deciliter sherry or French wine

## Directions

1. Rinse the fruit very well in lukewarm water and let it soak overnight in the water in which it is to be cooked
2. Place it in water together with the cinnamon, lemon zest, and sugar and let it cook until soft
3. Thicken the soup with the rice flour, stirred in 1/2 cup cold water, and let it cook for 5 minutes
4. Flavor it with the wine and serve preferably cold with whipped cream and rusks or biscuits
