## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup regular oat groats
- 2 1/2–3 liters water
- 24 prunes
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar
- salt
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 parts thick cream

## Directions

1. Cook the groats about 2 hours in water, without salt and without diluting, in a pot with a tight-fitting lid
2. When the groats are fully soft, pass them through a sieve
3. Rinse the prunes and cook them in a short cooking liquid with the sugar added
4. Bring the puree to a boil, add the prunes along with their cooking liquid
5. Whip the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl
6. Pour the soup into the bowl while whisking
