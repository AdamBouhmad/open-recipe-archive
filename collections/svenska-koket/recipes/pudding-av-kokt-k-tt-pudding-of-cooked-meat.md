## Ingredients

- 7 parts (5 cups) cooked chopped meat
- 3 eggs
- 7 parts (5 cups) cream milk
- 10 tablespoons crushed hardtack
- 1 part clarified butter
- 1 tablespoon currants
- 1/2 tablespoon sugar
- 3 teaspoons salt
- a little grated nutmeg
- 1/2 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 2 tablespoons crushed hardtack for the mold

## Directions

1. Chop the meat finely and mix with the beaten eggs and cream milk, the crushed hardtack, the clarified butter, the cleaned and blanched currants and the seasonings.
2. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-lined mold, and bake the pudding in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1.5 hours.
3. Serve with clarified butter.
