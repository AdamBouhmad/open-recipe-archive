## Ingredients

- 225 gr. butter
- 210 gr. brown farinose sugar
- 1 hg. sweet almond
- 25 gr. bitter almond
- 1 tsk. ground cinnamon
- grated peel of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 tsk. hartshorn salt (1 1/2 gr.)
- 1 msk. milk
- 375 gr. wheat flour
- egg white
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and stir with the sugar until it is white and fluffy, then add the scalded and ground almond, cinnamon, lemon peel, and the hartshorn salt dissolved in milk.
2. Last, work in the flour, which should be sifted, and form the dough into small, round balls, which are dipped in egg white and granulated sugar, placed on a well-buttered baking sheet and baked a beautiful golden brown in a not too hot oven.
