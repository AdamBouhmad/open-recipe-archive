## Ingredients

- 1 liter potatoes
- 1/4 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 1/4 celery
- 3 liters water
- 2 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- salt
- (sugar)
- white pepper
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 part cream

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw; peel and prepare the Jerusalem artichokes and celery in the usual way and place in cold, salted water.
2. When the vegetables are tender, strain them.
3. Brown 1 1/2 tablespoons of the butter with the flour, add the strained mixture, and let the soup simmer 10-15 minutes, then taste and adjust seasoning.
4. Whip the egg yolks and cream together in the soup bowl, add the cold butter, and pour in the puree while whisking vigorously.
5. Serve with toasted bread.
