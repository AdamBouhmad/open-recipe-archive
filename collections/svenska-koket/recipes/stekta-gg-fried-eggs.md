## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Brown the butter in a frying pan.
2. Crack the eggs carefully into a cup and let them slowly slide into the pan.
3. When the white has set, remove the egg with a pancake turner.
4. The eggs can also be fried in a griddle pan.
