## Ingredients

- 2 dcl sugar (170 gr)
- 1 dcl white wine
- 2 1/2 dcl water
- 1 litre good pears
- 50 gr butter
- 50 gr flour
- 4 dcl boiling milk
- 1/2 tsp finely grated lemon zest
- 2 tsp sugar (10 gr)
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 tbsp butter (10 gr) for the pan

## Directions

1. From the sugar, wine and water, a clear syrup is boiled.
2. The pears are rinsed, dried, peeled, split lengthwise, freed from the cores, placed in the sugar syrup and left to cook until soft.
3. They are taken out and placed in a greased pie pan.
4. The syrup is boiled down until it is thick, and poured over the pears.
5. Butter and flour are fried for 2 minutes, the milk is added and the mixture is left to boil for 5 minutes.
6. The lemon zest and sugar are added, and the mixture is left to cool, after which the egg yolks are stirred in one at a time, and finally the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks are folded in.
7. The cream is poured immediately over the fruit, and the pudding is baked in a water bath in the oven for approximately 1 hour, or until the mass is baked through when tested with a whisk.
8. Served slightly cooled with or without whipped cream.
9. Instead of pears, compote of apples or plums can be used.
