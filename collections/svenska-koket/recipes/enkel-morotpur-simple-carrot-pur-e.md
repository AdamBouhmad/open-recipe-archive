## Ingredients

- 450 gr. carrots
- 2 leeks
- 2 tbsp. butter
- 3 liters broth
- 1 twelve-øre wheat bread
- salt
- sugar
- pepper (preferably cayenne)

## Directions

1. Wash, scrape and cut bright red carrots into cubes; clean the leeks and cut into pieces.
2. Fry the vegetables in the butter but do not let them brown, add 1 liter of the broth together with half of the bread, cut into cubes, and let the soup cook with a closed lid for 1 hour, after which it is passed through a sieve.
3. Add the remaining broth, and bring the soup to a good boil. Skim and season with the spices.
4. Serve either with the remaining bread toasted or with anchovy butter sandwiches.
