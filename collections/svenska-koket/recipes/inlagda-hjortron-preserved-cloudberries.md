## Ingredients

- Half-ripe red cloudberries
- Fully ripe cloudberries

## Directions

1. Clean the half-ripe red berries, rinse them, and place them in well-cleaned and dry glass preserving jars.
2. Press the ripe berries in a cloth, and pour the thick juice over the unripe berries.
3. The juice should well cover the berries.
4. Screw the jars shut and store in a cold place.
5. Serve with sugar as a compote.
