## Ingredients

- 5 parts blood (pork blood)
- 8 parts weak broth
- 2 tablespoons syrup
- 3-4 cups coarse rye flour
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped kidney fat
- ½ teaspoon white pepper
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 1-1½ tablespoons salt
- ⅙ teaspoon finely ground marjoram
- 1 large red onion
- 1 tablespoon cream
- ½ tablespoon cream (for the mold)
- 2 tablespoons crushed breadcrumbs (for the mold)

## Directions

1. Strain the blood through a fine sieve and whisk it well, then add the syrup and broth.
2. Whisk in the flour, add the finely chopped kidney fat and spices.
3. Peel and finely chop the onion, brown it in cream and stir it last into the mixture.
4. Fry a little of the mixture to taste if it is properly seasoned.
5. Butter a beautiful metal mold and dust it with crushed breadcrumbs, fill it two-thirds with the mixture and bind it over with buttered paper, then place it in boiling water and let it cook for 2-3 hours.
6. Test with a whisk to see if the pudding is done.
