## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 3 deciliters thick cream

## Directions

1. Coat the bottom of a milk bowl with sour cream.
2. Pour in the milk and let it sour.
3. Whip the thick cream to stiff peaks and add slices of the soured milk to it.
4. Serve with gingerbread cookies.
