## Ingredients

- 3 hg. boneless, cooked meat
- 1 hg. butter
- 2 tsk. finely chopped onion
- 1 hg. flour
- 5–6 dcl. boiling milk
- 5 eggs
- salt
- white pepper
- 1/2 msk. butter (10 gr.) for the mold
- 2 msk. crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Scrape and chop the meat finely.
2. Fry the butter with the onion and flour for 2 minutes; add the boiling milk and let the sauce simmer under stirring for 5 minutes; pour out and stir until cooled.
3. Add the egg yolks, one at a time, and stir the mixture for 10 minutes, then blend in the meat and season the mixture with spices.
4. Last, fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks, and pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn pudding mold with lid and let it cook in a water bath on the stove for about 2 hours.
5. Turn out onto a platter, garnish with slices of pickled cucumber or tomatoes and parsley.
6. Serve with brown gravy sauce or with a reduced sauce mixed with finely chopped pickled onion, pickles, or pickled cucumber.
