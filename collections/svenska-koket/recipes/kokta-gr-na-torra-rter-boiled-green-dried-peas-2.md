## Ingredients

- 1/2 liter peas
- 8 deciliter water
- 1/2 tablespoon salt (8 grams)
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)

## Directions

1. Clean the peas and rinse them
2. Place in cold, soft water (see page 338) and let simmer slowly until completely tender, which takes 2.5-3 hours
3. Add warm water during cooking if needed
4. Season with salt and stir in cold butter at the end
5. Serve with pickled herring, fried bacon, and meat
6. Note: Peas cook more easily if soaked in water the evening before, but will have poor color
