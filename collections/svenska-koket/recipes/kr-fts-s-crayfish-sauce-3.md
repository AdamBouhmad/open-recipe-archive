## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp. butter or 2 tbsp. crayfish butter
- 2 tbsp. flour
- 1/2 liter thin cream
- 2 hg. crayfish tails
- sugar
- salt
- white pepper
- 1 egg yolk

## Directions

1. Butter and flour are fried together, cream is thinned in, and the sauce is allowed to simmer 10 minutes.
2. The crayfish tails are added, and the sauce is seasoned with sugar and salt.
3. Finally it is finished with the egg yolk, after which it must not boil.
4. If salted crayfish tails are used, these must first be well rinsed.
