## Ingredients

- Heart offal of veal (lungs and heart)
- water
- 1 red onion
- 24 allspice berries
- 4 bay leaves
- salt
- 2 cups pearl barley

## Directions

1. Rinse the heart offal (lungs and heart) well and let it soak in cold water for about 12 hours so that the blood drains out.
2. Change the water at least three times.
3. Put the heart offal in cold water; when the stock comes to a boil, skim it carefully, then add the spices and the halved onion, and let the meat simmer slowly.
4. When it feels tender, remove it and strain the stock.
5. Cook the barley in the stock, and when it is almost done, add the finely chopped meat, and let everything cook until the barley is soft.
6. Season the purée to taste.
