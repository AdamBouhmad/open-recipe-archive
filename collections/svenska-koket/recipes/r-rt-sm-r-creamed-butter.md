## Ingredients

- 120 gr. fine butter
- 2 gr. table salt

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a warmed bowl; if desired, add the salt, and stir the butter until it becomes white and fluffy. Serve with soufflés, vegetable dishes, roasted rice, etc.
