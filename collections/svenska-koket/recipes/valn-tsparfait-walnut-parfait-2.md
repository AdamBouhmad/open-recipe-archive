## Ingredients

- sugar
- water
- egg yolks
- heavy cream
- 50 gr. blanched and ground sweet almonds
- 50 gr. shelled and ground walnuts

## Directions

1. Cook sugar and water together
2. Remove the pan from heat and add egg yolks one at a time while whisking
3. Let the cream simmer over the fire with vigorous whisking until it is thick and frothy
4. Remove the pan from heat and whisk the cream until it is completely cold
5. Whip the heavy cream to stiff peaks and fold carefully into the cream
6. Mix the cream with 50 gr. blanched and ground sweet almonds and 50 gr. shelled and ground walnuts
7. Fill into a water-rinsed and sugar-dusted mold and freeze for 4-5 hours
8. Serve with Curaçao sauce: 4 dcl. thick sugar syrup colored light green with caramel coloring, mixed with 1 dcl. Curaçao and 1/2 dcl. cognac
