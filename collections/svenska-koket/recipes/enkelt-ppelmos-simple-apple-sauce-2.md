## Ingredients

- 10 liters apples
- 3 parts water
- half the weight of sugar against the peeled apples
- peel and juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, cut them into wedges, and remove the cores.
2. Place them on the fire together with the water, sugar, and lemon, and let them cook under constant stirring until the apples fall apart and form a sauce.
3. Pour it hot into the jar and cover, after it has cooled, with a thin layer of lingonberry jam or with parchment paper dipped in cognac.
