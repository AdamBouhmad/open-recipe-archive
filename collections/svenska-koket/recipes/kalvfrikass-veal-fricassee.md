## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg veal breast
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 cup parsley
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1-2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Break and wash the breast with a cloth dipped in hot water.
2. Place in boiling water, skim, and salt.
3. Let the meat cook slowly with the lid on until tender, approximately 2 hours.
4. Remove and cut into appropriately sized pieces.
5. Prepare the sauce according to general sauce rules and serve over the meat.
