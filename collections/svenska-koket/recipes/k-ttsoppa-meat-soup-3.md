## Ingredients

- 2 kg marrow pipe, rib or neck of meat
- 3 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 carrots
- 1 parsnip
- 1 leek
- 1 small celery root
- 1 small bunch parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in cold water
2. Cut it into two pieces and place it in cold water
3. Bring it quickly to a boil, skim well and salt it
4. Let the soup cook slowly with a tightly closed lid for 3-4 hours
5. After about 2 hours, add the sautéed vegetables
6. When done, cut the vegetables into pieces and serve them in the soup bowl
7. Finally add the chopped parsley, bring the broth to a boil and pour it into the soup bowl
8. The meat can be served in the soup, cut into pieces, or separately with horseradish sauce
