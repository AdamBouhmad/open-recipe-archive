## Ingredients

- 1 liter Jerusalem artichokes
- 0.5 liters water
- 0.5 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1.5 liters mixed veal and beef broth
- 2 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the Jerusalem artichokes and shape them evenly, and place them in water mixed with vinegar so they do not discolor.
