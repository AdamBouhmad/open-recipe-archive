## Ingredients

- 2 hard-boiled egg yolks
- 2 raw egg yolks
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 2—3 tsk. sugar
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 tsk. dry or French mustard
- 1—1/2 msk. vinegar
- 2 1/2 dcl. thick cream

## Directions

1. Pass the hard-boiled egg yolks through a sieve and mix well with the raw egg yolks, spices, mustard and vinegar.
2. Thereafter add the cream whipped to stiff peaks and taste the sauce carefully.
3. Use for green salad and other cold dishes for those who do not tolerate cooking oil. Can be mixed with finely chopped dill or chives if served with cold fish.
