## Ingredients

- 4 kg salmon
- 4 tbsp salt
- parsley and lemon for garnish

## Directions

1. Brush, skin, and wash the salmon well, then score it.
2. Rub it with salt and let it rest for a while.
3. Heat butter in a roasting pan, place the salmon in it, and brown it in good oven heat.
4. Add boiling water and let the salmon finish cooking, basting it now and then with its sauce.
5. Place it on the serving plate, pour the sauce over it, and garnish with parsley and lemon.
