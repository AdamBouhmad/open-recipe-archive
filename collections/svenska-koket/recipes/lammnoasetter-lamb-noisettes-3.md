## Ingredients

- 750 gr. lamb fillet
- 2 tsk. salt (10 gr.)
- 1/2 tsk. white pepper
- 1 1/2 hg. lard
- 3 msk. butter (60 gr.)
- 3 dcl. broth
- 1 hg. goose liver
- 1 jar asparagus (1/4 lit.)
- 1/2 lit. dwarf potatoes, cooked in aspic
- parsley
- truffle sauce (see no. 433)

## Directions

1. Dry the fillets with a cloth wrung out in hot water and cut into 12 slices, which are gently flattened and rubbed with the seasonings.
2. Roll them up and wrap in thinly cut lard slices.
3. Brown them in the butter, thin with the boiling broth and let them braise with a tight lid until tender, or approximately 45 minutes.
4. Remove the lard slices and arrange the noisettes in a ring on a round serving platter and on each noisette place a small round slice of goose liver.
5. Place the asparagus in the middle of the platter, heated in a water bath, and arrange the dwarf potatoes around it.
6. Garnish the dish with parsley and serve with truffle sauce (see no. 433).
