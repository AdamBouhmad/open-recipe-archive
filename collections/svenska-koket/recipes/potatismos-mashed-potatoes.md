## Ingredients

- 2 liters potatoes
- water, salt
- 2-3 cups milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes, cook them and mash them with a fork or press them through a potato ricer.
2. Heat the milk and add it to the mash with vigorous stirring.
3. Stir in the cold butter, and season the mash with sugar and salt; work it until it resembles foam.
