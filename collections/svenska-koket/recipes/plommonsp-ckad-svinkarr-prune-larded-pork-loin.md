## Ingredients

- 1.5 kg loin (with bone)
- 1-2 lemons
- 1/2 cup prunes
- 3 parts water
- 1.5 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1/2 liter broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash the loin with a cloth dipped in hot water and rub it with the lemon until all its juice is extracted.
2. Rinse the prunes and parboil them (the water is used for basting during roasting), remove the pits and cut them into four parts; lard the loin with these in dense rows.
3. Place it in good oven heat to brown in the butter, salt it and add the cooled prune water, then let it cook slowly about 1 hour.
4. Add the hot broth during roasting, and baste the loin every ten minutes.
5. Serve with its own sauce, which is strained and carefully skimmed.
6. If the sauce is to be thickened, prepare it according to general rules for sauces, and a little cream can be added in that case.
