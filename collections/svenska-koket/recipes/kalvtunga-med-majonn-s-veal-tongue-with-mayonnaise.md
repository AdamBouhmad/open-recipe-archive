## Ingredients

- 1 veal tongue
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon cream
- 1/2 cup finely crushed hardtack
- 3 tablespoons butter for frying
- 2 egg yolks
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- cayenne
- 1/4 teaspoon French mustard
- 2 deciliters fine olive oil
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon hot water

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the tongue in the same way as in the previous recipe.
2. Split it lengthwise into three slices, which are cut in half, dipped in the egg whisked with cream, and turned in the crushed hardtack.
3. Fry the slices beautifully golden brown in butter, arrange on a platter, garnish with mayonnaise and parsley and serve as a breakfast course.
4. The mayonnaise is prepared as in recipe no. 10.
