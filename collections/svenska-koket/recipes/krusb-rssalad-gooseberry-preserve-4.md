## Ingredients

- 2 kg. gooseberries (approximately 3 liters)
- 1 1/2 kg. granulated sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- 2 dcl. vinegar
- 10 clove spices

## Directions

1. The gooseberries should be green, fully grown but unripe.
2. Clean and rinse them, and let them drain on linen.
3. Cook a syrupy liquid from sugar, water, vinegar and cloves.
4. Add the berries and cook as in recipe no. 1031.
