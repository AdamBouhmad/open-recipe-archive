## Ingredients

- 3 kg rhubarb
- 2 liters red currants
- 8 liters water
- 2 kg sugar

## Directions

1. Rinse and dry the rhubarb and cut it into pieces. Rinse and strip the currants.
2. Place these together with the rhubarb in the water and let them cook with stirring until they are completely soft.
3. Pour the mass into cheesecloth to drain. Place the clear juice on the fire again together with the sugar and let it cook with constant skimming until it is completely clear and somewhat glossy.
4. Bottle the juice; cork the bottles and seal with resin.
