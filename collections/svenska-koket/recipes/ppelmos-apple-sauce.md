## Ingredients

- 2 kg. apples, cut in pieces
- 1 lit. water
- 750 gr. crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Tart apples of the same sort are rinsed, dried, cut in pieces, freed from wormy and damaged parts and weighed.
2. Place them in the cold water and let them cook until they are completely soft, then pass them through a hair sieve.
3. Cook the sauce thereafter with the sugar while stirring, until it is appropriately firm, or 1/2—1 hour.
4. Pour hot into warm jars rinsed with cognac, preferably not larger than what can be used all at once.
5. When the sauce is cold, cover it with a thin layer of melted paraffin or sheep fat, or pour over it 1 tbsp. cognac, mixed with 1/4 gram salicylic acid.
