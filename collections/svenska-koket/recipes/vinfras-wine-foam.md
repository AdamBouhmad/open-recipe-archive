## Ingredients

- 125 gr. loaf sugar
- 3 dcl. water
- zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 15 gr. gelatin (approximately 9 sheets)
- 1 dcl. warm water
- 1 1/2 dcl. French wine
- 1 1/2 dcl. sherry

## Directions

1. Cook a clear syrup from the sugar and water, skim it and immediately mix with the finely grated lemon zest and lemon juice and the rinsed gelatin dissolved in the warm water and the wine.
2. Stir the mixture well and pour it into a stretched cheesecloth, previously wrung out in hot water, and let it drain by itself. As long as the jelly runs cloudy through, pour it back into the cheesecloth.
3. The jelly can be poured into a large bowl, which is placed on ice, then the jelly is whipped until it resembles snow and poured into a dish or used as garnish for clear jellies.
