## Ingredients

- 1 liter good, tart apples
- 5 eggs
- 100 grams granulated sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the form

## Directions

1. Rinse the apples, dry them, place in a buttered baking pan and bake in the oven until soft, then press through a fine sieve.
2. Stir the egg yolks and sugar for 20 minutes, add the cold apple puree and carefully fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
3. Pour the mixture into a buttered pie form and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 1/2 hour. Serve immediately with whipped cream.
