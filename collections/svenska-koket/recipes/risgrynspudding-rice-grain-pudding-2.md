## Ingredients

- 2 dcl. karolina rice grains (2 hg.)
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 gr.)
- 1 1/2 litre milk
- 1/2 tsp. salt (3 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. sugar (30 gr.)
- 20 sweet almonds, 10 bitter almonds
- 40 gr. ordinary raisins
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 tbsp. butter for the mould (10 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. crushed rusks

## Directions

1. Treat and cook the rice grains as in recipe no. 794.
2. Pour the porridge and immediately blend it with the cold butter, the spices, the scalded and ground almonds, and the well-rinsed, blanched and pitted raisins.
3. When the porridge is cold, stir in the egg yolks and the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
4. Pour the mixture into a buttered and breadcrumb-strewn cake tin and bake in ordinary oven heat for about 1 hour.
5. Serve warm with jam, compote or fruit sauce.
