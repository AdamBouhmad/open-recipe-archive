## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons flour (50 g)
- 1 1/4 liters milk
- 1/2 liter good weak beer
- 2 tablespoons syrup, 1/2 tablespoon sugar (8 g)
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- 1 teaspoon salt (3 g)
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 deciliters thick cream

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with 1/4 liter of the milk and let it swell for about 2 hours.
2. Boil the rest of the milk, add the mixture and let the porridge boil while whisking for 8-10 minutes.
3. Boil the beer with the syrup and spices and pour it into the porridge while whisking evenly.
4. Whisk the egg yolks and cream in a soup bowl and add the soup while whisking vigorously.
5. Serve immediately with fried bread cubes.
6. In the simplest version, the egg yolks and cream are omitted.
