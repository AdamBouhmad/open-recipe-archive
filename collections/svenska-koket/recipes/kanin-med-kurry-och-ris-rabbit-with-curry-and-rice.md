## Ingredients

- 1 unflayed rabbit, approximately 3 kg
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 1 red onion in slices
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 g)
- 1-2 teaspoons curry powder
- 1/2 litre broth
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice
- 3 tablespoons thick cream

## Directions

1. Treat and cut the rabbit as in the previous recipe.
2. Brown the butter in an iron pot, add the meat, brown it nicely and remove with a slotted spoon.
3. Add the onion, flour and curry powder and fry well with the butter in the pot, dilute with broth, and let the sauce come to a boil.
4. Place the meat in the sauce, add the lemon juice and let the meat simmer slowly with the lid tightly closed until it is tender, approximately 1 hour.
5. Place it on a warm platter, whisk up the sauce with the cream, let it come to a boil, taste it and pour over the meat.
6. Around the meat garnish with boiled rice, sprinkled with 1-2 teaspoons curry powder.
