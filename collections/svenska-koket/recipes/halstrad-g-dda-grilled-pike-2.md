## Ingredients

- 2 pike, 500 grams each
- 2 tablespoons salt
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 4 tablespoons flour (40 grams)
- 100 grams clarified butter
- 1 lemon for garnish
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Clean the pike thoroughly and dry it.
2. Cut into fillets and separate the meat from the skin.
3. Remove the backbone and all small bones.
4. Rub the fillets with salt and vinegar and let rest for 30 minutes, then dry thoroughly.
5. Cut each fillet into 2 pieces crosswise.
6. Roll the fish pieces in flour and dip in the melted butter.
7. Heat a double grilling screen over coals, place the fish pieces in it and grill over coals on both sides until golden brown and cooked through.
8. Place immediately on a warm serving plate and garnish with fluted lemon slices and parsley.
9. Serve as a breakfast dish with maitre d'hotel butter and potatoes.
