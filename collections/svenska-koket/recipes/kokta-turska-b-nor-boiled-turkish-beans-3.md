## Ingredients

- 750 gr. fresh beans
- 7 dcl. water
- 2 tsp. salt (10 gr.)
- 3 tsp. finely chopped parsley
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 2 tbsp. flour (20 gr.) [optional]

## Directions

1. The beans are cleaned and rinsed, allowed to drain on cloth, and cut into 4 mm. thick pieces, cut across (slightly on the diagonal).
2. They are placed in boiling, salted water and allowed to cook until tender. Excess liquid is then drained off and the beans are mixed with the parsley and cold butter.
3. If a thicker sauce is desired, it is thickened with flour, which is browned with half of the butter. The liquid is added, and the sauce is allowed to simmer 5 minutes, after which the beans are added and mixed with the parsley and the remaining butter.
4. Served with braised ham, boiled sausage and roasted meat.
