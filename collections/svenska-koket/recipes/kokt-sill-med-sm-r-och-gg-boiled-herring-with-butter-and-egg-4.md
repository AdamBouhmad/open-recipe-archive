## Ingredients

- 2 large or 4 small salted fat herrings
- 1 liter water
- dill
- 1 hectogram butter
- 2-3 eggs

## Directions

1. Let the herring soak in water for 12 hours, changing the water 2 times.
2. Skin it, remove the innards, fillet it in half, then remove the backbone and all small bones.
3. Rinse the fillets well and wrap in a fish towel.
4. Hard boil the eggs for 10 minutes, cool in water and shell; separate the white and yolk and chop each separately.
5. Place the herring in boiling water with some dill sprigs and boil 8-10 minutes.
6. Lift out with a slotted spoon onto a warm serving dish, pour the clarified butter over and garnish the herring with egg white and yolk in close rows.
7. Serve with freshly boiled potatoes as a breakfast dish.
