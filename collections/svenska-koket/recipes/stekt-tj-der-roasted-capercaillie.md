## Ingredients

- 1 capercaillie
- 1 hectogram lard
- 3—4 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 grams)
- 4 decilitres bouillon
- 5 decilitres thick cream

## Directions

1. The capercaillie hen has the finest meat and is calculated for 6 persons. A young capercaillie cock also has flavorful meat and serves 8—10 persons.
2. If the bird is frozen, it should be thawed in a warm kitchen, cleaned (see page 299) and allowed to lie in lukewarm milk overnight.
3. Dry it thoroughly, lard it with fine strips of lard and truss it.
4. Roast the bird in a pot as with grouse, but it requires 2—3 hours of roasting, depending on whether it is a hen or cock.
5. Cut, garnish (see page 300) and serve with strained sauce (see recipe 429), mixed with thick cream and cold butter, and if desired, with 1 tablespoon of red currant jelly.
