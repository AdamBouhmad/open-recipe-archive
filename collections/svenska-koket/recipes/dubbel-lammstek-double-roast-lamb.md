## Ingredients

- 4 kg. lamb roast
- 3 hg. lard
- 2 msk. salt (30 gr.)
- 1 tsk. white pepper
- 1 hg. butter
- 1 lit. broth or water

## Directions

1. From a young lamb the two connected roasts are separated.
2. The shanks are cut off — but roasted with — and some of the fat is trimmed away.
3. The meat is treated, roasted and served as in the previous recipe.
4. The roast is carved, the slices are placed together again so that it looks whole, and is garnished with cooked vegetables (legumes).
