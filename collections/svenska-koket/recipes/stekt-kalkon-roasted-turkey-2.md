## Ingredients

- 1 turkey
- 2 hectograms lard
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1½-2 liters water
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- ¼ part thick cream
- meat dripping

## Directions

1. Pluck the turkey, singe, clean out, rinse well and dry with a cloth. Lard it with fine strips of lard or cover with thin slices of lard, truss and sprinkle inside and outside with salt.
2. Brown the butter in a long pan, place the turkey in it and brown it lightly. Then add boiling water and baste every ten minutes. If the oven is too hot, cover the turkey with a damp paper. Roasting takes 2-3 hours.
3. Strain and skim the meat dripping, and prepare the sauce according to general sauce-making rules.
