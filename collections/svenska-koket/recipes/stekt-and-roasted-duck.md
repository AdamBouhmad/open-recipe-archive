## Ingredients

- 2 ducks
- 3 tbsp. butter
- 2 tsp. salt
- ½–1 liter broth
- lemon juice
- soy sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck the ducks, singe, remove innards, rinse very well and dry and truss them. Rub them in with the liver until they have taken on a reddish-brown color, then brown them in the butter and salt them. Pour the boiling broth in little by little, and let the ducks roast about 1 hour, while they are frequently basted with their stock. Serve the ducks very hot.
2. Strain and skim the sauce, season it with lemon juice and soy sauce and serve as stock. If desired thickened, prepare it according to general rules for sauces.
