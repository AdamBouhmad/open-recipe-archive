## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 4 dcl. water approximately
- 135 gr. flour
- 2 dcl. melted butter (2 hg.)
- 2 tsp. sugar (10 gr.)
- 4 dcl. thick cream
- 2 tbsp. butter for the pan (40 gr.)
- 2 cups fine jam (raspberry or cloudberry)
- 50 gr. powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg yolks with a steel whisk, pour in the cream, then whisk in the flour, which should preferably be sifted.
2. Dilute with the water, add the salt and butter, and let the batter rest for 2 hours.
3. Heat a pancake pan slowly, melt the butter in a water bath, and brush the pan with it.
4. Stir the batter well, and fry thin pancakes from it, which are placed on top of each other on a warm plate.
5. Brush each pancake thinly with some of the jam, which has been warmed in a water bath.
6. Cut the pancakes into 12 pieces and let them cool.
7. Fry very thin pancakes from the batter, which are brushed with warm jam, cut diagonally at both ends, cut in half lengthwise, placed with the rounded side up on a baking sheet, sprinkled with powdered sugar, glazed in a hot oven and served laid on pastry paper.
