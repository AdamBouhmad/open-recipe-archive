## Ingredients

- Sheep polypore mushrooms
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms and place in boiling water for 1-2 minutes
2. Cut into slices and fry like calf sweetbreads
3. Alternatively, cut into pieces and braise like champignons
4. They can also be advantageously dried or salted
