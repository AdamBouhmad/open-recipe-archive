## Ingredients

- 1-2 rutabagas, water, salt
- 1 liter potatoes
- 1/2-1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter or cream
- salt, sugar
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the rutabaga and cut it into small pieces and put it on in salted water to cook for about 1/2 hour.
2. Peel the potatoes, cut them into slices and add them to cook with the rutabaga, until everything is soft.
3. Drain off the water, mash the potatoes and rutabaga with a fork.
4. Stir in the cold butter along with the milk.
5. Work the puree well and season with the spices.
