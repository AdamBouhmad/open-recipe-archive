## Ingredients

- 2 cups noodles
- 2 1/2 liters water
- 1 tbsp. salt
- 3 tbsp. butter (60 g.)
- 8 dcl. cream
- 2 tsp. salt
- 1/4 tsp. white pepper
- 2 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Bring the water to a boil with salt in a large pot and add the noodles, letting them cook about 15 minutes.
2. Pour into a colander and let the water drain well.
3. Melt the butter in the pot, add the noodles and add the cream gradually while the stew cooks for about 1 hour.
4. Season with the spices, arrange on plates, sprinkle with cheese and serve very hot.
5. Mushroom stew can also be mixed in. The mixture can also be placed in a buttered and breadcrumb-lined mold, gratinéed in a water bath in the oven and turned out onto a platter, though the stew should then be slightly firmer.
