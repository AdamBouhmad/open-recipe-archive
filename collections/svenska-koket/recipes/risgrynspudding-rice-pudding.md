## Ingredients

- 2 parts Carolina rice
- 1.5 liters unskim milk
- 2-4 eggs
- 1.5 cups powdered sugar
- 12 bitter almonds
- 75 grams butter
- 0.5 cup raisins
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for the mold
- 1 tablespoon crushed rusks for the mold

## Directions

1. Rinse the rice and cook it in the milk to a porridge, which is set aside to cool.
2. Mix in the sugar, eggs and butter, and the blanched and grated bitter almonds.
3. Pour the porridge into a buttered and bread-crumbed pie mold, cover with buttered paper, bake in the oven for approximately 1 hour and serve hot with jam or fruit sauce.
