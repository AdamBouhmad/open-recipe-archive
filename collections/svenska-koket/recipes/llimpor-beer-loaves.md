## Ingredients

- 1.5-1.75 liters good beer
- 5 hg wheat flour
- 2 kkp milk
- 2 kkp syrup
- 2 tsk salt
- 2 msk crushed fennel
- 2 tsk crushed anise
- 2 msk finely cut orange peel
- 2-2.5 kg rye sifted flour
- 100 gr pressed yeast

## Directions

1. Boil the beer.
2. Stir half of the wheat flour with the milk and add with vigorous stirring.
3. Let the mixture boil well and remove from heat.
4. Add the syrup and spices, and when the mixture has cooled, work in the sifted rye flour and the yeast dissolved in a little lukewarm milk.
5. Work the dough smooth and glossy and place in a warm spot to rise to double size.
6. Then work it up with the rest of the wheat flour and let it rise again.
7. Then shape with wheat flour in the form of oblong or round loaves.
8. Place on heated and floured cloths to rise.
9. When well risen, place on buttered sheets, brush with cold water and bake in a warm oven.
10. During baking, brush the buns twice with hot water, likewise when the finished buns are removed from the oven.
11. Cover them well afterwards so that the crust does not become hard.
