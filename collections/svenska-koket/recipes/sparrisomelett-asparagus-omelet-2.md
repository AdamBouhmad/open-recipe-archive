## Ingredients

- 1 can omelet asparagus (1/4 liter)
- 1 tablespoon butter (20 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1/2 deciliter asparagus stock
- 3 deciliters cream
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon sugar
- 3 deciliters cream
- 1/2 tablespoon butter (10 grams) for the pan

## Directions

1. Fry the butter and flour together for 2 minutes, thin the asparagus stock and cream with stirring and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Add the asparagus and let the stewing come to a boil and season with spices.
3. Prepare the omelet as in the preceding recipe, fill with the stewing, tip it out, garnish with parsley and serve at the smorgasbord.
