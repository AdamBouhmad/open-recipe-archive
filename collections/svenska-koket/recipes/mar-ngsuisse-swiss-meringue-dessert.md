## Ingredients

- 165 grams fine sugar
- 3 large egg whites
- 8 deciliters extra thick cream
- 1 tablespoon sugar (15 grams)
- 1 tablespoon vanilla sugar
- carmine

## Directions

1. Sift the sugar through a fine sieve.
2. Whip the egg whites to stiff peaks (see page 463) and carefully blend in the sugar.
