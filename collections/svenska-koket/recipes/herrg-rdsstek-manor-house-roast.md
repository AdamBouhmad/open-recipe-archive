## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg chuck steak or inner loin
- 1 1/2 hg lard
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr.)
- 1/4 tsp white pepper
- 3 tbsp butter (60 gr.)
- 1 liter boiling beef broth
- 1 bay leaf
- 2 small red onions
- 6 peppercorns
- 2 anchovies
- 2 tbsp cognac
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tsp syrup

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat and lard it (see page 232), rub it with a mixture of white pepper and salt, and tie it with kitchen twine (previously dipped in hot water) so it has a nice shape.
2. Brown the butter in a roasting pan that should not be larger than necessary to fit the meat.
3. Place the roast in and brown until a nice brown crust forms, then dilute with broth.
4. Place the bay leaf, peeled onions, peppercorns, cleaned and deboned anchovies on top of the roast, and finally add the cognac, vinegar, and syrup.
5. Cover and let the meat braise slowly until tender, about 2 1/2-3 hours, turning it several times.
6. Place the roast on a warm platter and slice at the table.
7. Serve with vegetable pastries and salads along with reduced sauce mixed with cream.
