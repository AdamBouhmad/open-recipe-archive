## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons good cream
- 1 1/2 tablespoons clarified butter (30 g)
- flour, as much as needed for a firm dough

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs with the cream for 10 minutes.
2. Add the butter and stir in as much flour as needed to make a really firm dough.
3. Roll out the dough to a thickness of one-fifth of an inch and let it lie in a cold room to stiffen overnight.
4. Spread it out into strips 1 1/2 cm wide, which are cut across into fine strips.
5. Let these dry well and they can be stored for several weeks in a tin box with a tightly closed lid.
