## Ingredients

- 1 kg boneless beef
- ½ tbsp salt
- ¼ tsk white pepper
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 3 tbsp butter
- 2-3 parts bouillon or water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water, cut it into cubes and pass it 2-3 times through a meat grinder.
2. From the ground meat, thin round steaks are formed, which are sprinkled with pepper and salt. They are chopped lightly with a knife, and the edges are evened well, so that they retain their shape during frying.
3. The butter is browned in a smoking hot frying pan together with the peeled onion cut into slices. The onion is removed.
4. The steaks are placed in the pan and fried 1 minute on each side. They are placed on a hot plate with the onion in the middle.
5. The frying pan is whisked out with a little boiling bouillon or water, and the sauce is poured over the steaks.
