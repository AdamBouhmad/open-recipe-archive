## Ingredients

- 40 gr. tapioca grains
- 1½ kkp water
- 2½ lit. clear consommé
- salt, cayenne
- 1 hg. cured, cooked ox tongue
- 1 can truffle (1/8 lit.)
- 1 can fine mushrooms (1/4 lit.)
- 30 gr. butter

## Directions

1. Let the tapioca grains cook in water until they are clear, then strain through muslin and pour the liquid into the consommé.
2. Cut the tongue and truffle into fine strips and the mushrooms into thin slices.
3. Sauté the mushrooms in 30 gr. butter until the butter is clear, then let it drain on a sieve.
4. Place the garnish in the soup bowl.
5. Bring the consommé to a boil, season with the spices and pour over the garnish.
6. Serve very hot with cheese or butter pastry sticks (see no. 318 and 314).
