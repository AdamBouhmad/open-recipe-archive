## Ingredients

- 3 kg beef (French cut)
- 3 handfuls of lard with rind
- 1 calf's foot
- 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
- 8 white peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- 1 Portuguese onion
- 3 carrots
- 2 parts French wine
- 1 part cognac
- 1 1/2 liters weak broth
- 5 large fried onions for garnish

## Directions

1. Wash the meat with a cloth dipped in hot water
2. Cut the lard into strips, roll in salt and pepper, and lard the meat in tight rows
3. Tie the meat with twine (first dipped in boiling water) and place in a pot with the blanched pork rind, blanched calf's foot, remaining salt, white peppercorns, cloves, wine, cognac, peeled onion, scraped carrots, and broth
4. Bring to a boil and skim well
5. Cover tightly and let the meat simmer slowly for 3-4 hours
6. When tender, remove the meat and take off the twine
7. Slice the meat and arrange on a warm platter, sprinkle with a little salt, and garnish with sliced carrots and fried onions
8. Serve with French potatoes
9. Strain the broth, skim well, taste, and serve as gravy with the meat
10. If desired, the sauce can be thickened according to general sauce-making rules
