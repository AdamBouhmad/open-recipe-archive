## Ingredients

- 250 gr. fine prunes (about 22 pieces)
- 2 tbsp. sugar (80 gr.)
- 3 dcl. water
- 4 eggs
- 1 hg. sugar
- 75 gr. butter
- 4 dcl. boiling cream
- 1/2 tsp. finely grated lemon zest
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the pan

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes well in several warm waters and place them in a bowl alternately with the sugar.
2. Add the water and let the prunes soak overnight.
3. Cook them thereafter for about 5 minutes, let them cool, pit them, cut them into strips, which are placed in a buttered pie pan.
4. Crush the pits, place the almonds in boiling water, peel them, dry them in the oven, chop them finely and sprinkle over the prunes.
5. Stir the egg yolks, sugar and butter for 20 minutes, add the boiling cream while whisking vigorously together with the lemon zest, and continue whisking until the cream is cold, then fold in the egg whites beaten to stiff peaks carefully.
6. Pour the cream over the prunes.
