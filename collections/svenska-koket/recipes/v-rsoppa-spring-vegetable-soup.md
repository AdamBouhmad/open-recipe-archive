## Ingredients

- 1 small bunch fresh carrots
- 1 small parsnip
- 1/4 root celery
- 1/2 leek
- 1 small cauliflower head
- 3/4 liter water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1 deciliter canned peas
- 1 can mushrooms (1/8 liter)
- 1 1/2 hectograms salted crayfish tails
- 2 tablespoons butter (40 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- 1 1/4 liters beef broth
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 50 grams spinach
- 3 egg yolks
- salt
- white pepper

## Directions

1. The roots are scraped, rinsed, dried and cut into fine cubes
2. The leek is cut into strips and the cauliflower is cleaned and divided into florets
3. The roots are put on to boil in salted water with sugar and allowed to cook for 5 minutes, after which the leek and cauliflower are added and everything is allowed to cook until soft
4. The vegetables are removed with a slotted spoon and placed in the soup bowl together with the peas, which have been warmed in a water bath
5. The broth is cooked down, without a lid, until it becomes approximately 1/2 liter
6. The mushrooms are cut into slices and fried in the butter together with the well-drained crayfish tails, after which they are pressed out of the butter with a spoon
7. The butter is then fried with the flour for 2 minutes, the root broth and the boiling beef broth are added, the parsley and the chopped spinach are added and the soup is allowed to cook while stirring for 5 minutes
8. The egg yolks are beaten and whisked into the soup, which is then seasoned with spices and poured over the vegetables, mushrooms and crayfish tails in the soup bowl
9. Served very hot
