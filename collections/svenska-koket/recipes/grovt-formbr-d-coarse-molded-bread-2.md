## Ingredients

- 6 dcl sour milk or drink
- 70 gr pressed yeast
- 525 gr coarsely ground rye flour
- 6 hg wheat flour
- 2 tsp salt (10 gr)
- 2 dcl syrup
- 1 tbsp crushed fennel
- 2 tbsp finely chopped orange peel
- 1 tbsp butter (20 gr) for the pans

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl.
2. Whisk the sour milk, skimmed milk or drink into the flour.
3. Mix the yeast with the salt and add along with the syrup and spices, and work the dough until it is smooth and shiny, then place it in two buttered bread pans and let it rise overnight.
4. Place the pans in a not too warm oven and bake the bread for about 1 hour, at the end somewhat
