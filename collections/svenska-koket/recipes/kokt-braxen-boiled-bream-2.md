## Ingredients

- 1 bream weighing 2 kg
- 1 msk salt (15 gr.)
- 8 white peppercorns
- 5 allspice berries
- 2 bay leaves
- 5 dill sprigs

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish as described on page 171; the head may remain but remove the gills
2. Rub with salt and let rest 1 hour
3. Boil either whole or in pieces as in recipe no. 327; when the fins loosen easily, it is done
4. Bream may be served either hot or cold
5. If served hot, boil whole, arrange on a napkin and garnish with parsley
6. If served cold, boil in pieces, strain the broth over the arranged fish and let set into jelly
7. Whether warm or cold, serve with cold horseradish sauce
