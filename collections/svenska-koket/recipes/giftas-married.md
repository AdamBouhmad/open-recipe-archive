## Ingredients

- 1 dcl. extra tjock grädde
- 3 dcl. sylt
- 3 dcl. bitar av småbröd eller 18 små sockerskorpor

## Directions

1. Whip the cream to stiff peaks, fold in the jam and small bread or rusks (soaked in 1 1/2 parts milk), and spread the cream in a compote bowl.
2. The small bread can be omitted, in which case the cream is instead piped into cones.
