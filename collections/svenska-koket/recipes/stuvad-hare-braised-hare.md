## Ingredients

- 1 skinned hare weighing 3 1/2 kg
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 grams)
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- Marinade: 1 bottle red wine
- 1 bay leaf
- 6 allspice berries
- 1/4 teaspoon thyme
- For braising: 4 tablespoons butter (80 grams)
- 3 tablespoons flour (30 grams)
- the marinade
- 8 pickled onions, in wedges
- 10 mushrooms, in slices
- 8 pitted olives

## Directions

1. Treat the hare as in the preceding recipe, cut it into smaller pieces, which are seasoned with the spices and placed in a stoneware pot. For the marinade, mix all ingredients, then pour it over the hare, which is left to marinate until the next day. Remove the meat, wrap it well in cloth and strain the marinade. Brown the butter in a saucepan, add the meat, brown it and sprinkle with flour. Pour in the marinade and let the meat simmer slowly with a tightly closed lid for about 1 hour, shaking the pot frequently so the meat does not stick to the bottom. Add the onions, mushrooms and olives, cut into thin rings, and let the braise simmer until everything is tender, or about 1/2 hour. Place the meat on a warm plate, whisk up the sauce, taste for seasoning and pour over. Garnish the dish with small triangular bread croutons and serve with boiled or fried potatoes.
