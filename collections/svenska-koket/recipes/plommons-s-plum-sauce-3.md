## Ingredients

- 150 gr. prunes
- 5 dcl. water
- 50 gr. sugar
- 1 teaspoon potato flour (5 gr.)

## Directions

1. Rinse the prunes in several changes of lukewarm water and wrap them in a cloth.
2. Crush half of them in a stone mortar so that the pits are crushed.
3. Let the crushed and whole prunes soak for a few hours in the water in which they are to be cooked.
4. Cook until completely soft, then press through a fine sieve.
5. Bring the fruit purée to a boil with the sugar and thicken as in recipe 460.
6. Season with a little lemon juice if desired and serve warm or cold.
7. Sauce of dried cherries is prepared in the same way.
