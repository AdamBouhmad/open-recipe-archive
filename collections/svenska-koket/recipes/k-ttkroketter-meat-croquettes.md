## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 3 cups broth
- 5 cups fried or boiled meat cut into cubes
- salt and white pepper
- 2 eggs for coating
- 2 cups crushed rusks for coating
- 3/4 kg lard for frying

## Directions

1. Melt butter and flour together, thin with broth, add the meat, and let the stew cook 10 minutes, then season.
2. Spread the mixture on a flat plate to cool, and form small sugar-top-shaped croquettes.
3. Roll them in crushed rusks, dip in whipped egg and roll again in crushed rusks, then let them dry for 1/2 hour.
4. Heat the lard, cook the croquettes golden brown in it and place on gray paper to drain.
5. Garnish with parsley and serve with mushroom or brown sauce, or together with stewed vegetables.
