## Ingredients

- 1 25-öre spice bread
- 1½ liters apples
- 1½ cups sugar
- 2 hg. butter
- 7½ tsk. butter for the form
- 3 tsk. crushed hardtack

## Directions

1. The bread, which should not be too fresh, is grated and fried in two-thirds of the butter.
2. Peel the apples, cut them into pieces and cook together with the sugar and a little water to a pulp.
3. Butter a form or cast-iron pan and sprinkle with the crushed hardtack, and in this lay alternately the fried bread and the apple pulp.
4. The first and last layer should be bread.
5. The last layer is also covered with the remaining butter, distributed in small flakes.
6. Place the cake in moderate oven heat to bake for about ½ hour.
7. Turn out and sprinkle with sugar and serve with cream or vanilla sauce.
