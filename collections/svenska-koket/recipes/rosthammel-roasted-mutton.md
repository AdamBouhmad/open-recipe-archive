## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 kg. French cut
- 1/2 msk. salt (8 gr.)
- 1/4 tsk. white pepper
- 1 small bunch parsley
- 4 msk. butter (80 gr.)
- 1 carrot
- 1 shallot onion
- 2 dcl. meat gravy or broth

## Directions

1. The meat, which should have hung so that it is tender, is beaten with a damp mallet, dried with a cloth, wrung out in hot water, rubbed with a mixture of the salt and pepper and brushed with parsley butter, which is made from 2 msk. of the butter as in the previous recipe.
2. In a small roasting pan or frying pan the rest of the butter is placed, the scraped carrot cut into slices and the peeled onion cut into wedges.
3. The roast is placed in and roasted in a warm oven for approximately 1 hour, during which it is frequently basted with the butter in the pan so that it does not dry out or become burnt.
4. A quarter of an hour before it is done the boiling meat gravy is added.
5. Placed on a warm platter and carved at the table.
6. Served with vegetable or potato pastries and meat gravy, which is strained and skimmed.
