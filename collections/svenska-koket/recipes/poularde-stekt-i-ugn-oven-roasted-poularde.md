## Ingredients

- 1 poularde or 1 capon, approximately 3 kg
- 2 teaspoons salt (10 g)
- 1 hectogram fat, in 1 slice
- 3 tablespoons butter (60 g)
- 0.5 liters broth

## Directions

1. Prepare the bird in the usual manner (see page 299 onwards), salt it inside, truss it and lay the fat slice on top, which is fastened with fine twine
2. Place it breast up on a grate in a roasting pan so that it does not need to lie in the fat that collects in the pan
3. Place in a warm oven and roast until tender, about 40-50 minutes, basting frequently with melted butter and diluting with broth to reduce the heat
4. Cut and garnish as in the previous recipe
5. Serve with the strained and skimmed meat gravy or with a prepared sauce (see recipe no. 429)
