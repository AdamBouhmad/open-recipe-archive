## Ingredients

- 1 12-penny wheat bread
- 1 hectogram butter

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into thin slices, and spread these with butter.
2. Place the sandwiches two by two together.
3. Spread thinly with butter on the outer sides as well, then brown in the oven.
4. Serve with meat, fish, and vegetarian soups.
