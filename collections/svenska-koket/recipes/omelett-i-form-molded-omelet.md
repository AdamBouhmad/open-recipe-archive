## Ingredients

- 1 can chanterelles (1/4 lit.)
- 2 tbsp. butter (40 gr.)
- 3 tbsp. flour (30 gr.)
- 1/2 dcl. chanterelle liquid
- 3 dcl. cream
- salt
- white pepper
- sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 tsk. salt
- 1/4 tsk. sugar
- 3 dcl. milk
- 1/2 tbsp. butter (10 gr.) for the mold

## Directions

1. Pat the chanterelles dry in a cloth, slice them and fry in butter until it becomes clear. Squeeze the mushroom out of the butter with a spoon.
2. Fry the flour with the butter for 2 minutes, add the chanterelle liquid and cream and let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Add the mushroom, season the stewing with the spices, place it in a buttered omelet mold and whisk the egg mixture well and pour over.
4. Bake the omelet in the oven and serve for the smorgasbord.
5. Instead of stewing, smoked ham, cut into cubes and fried in butter, or grated cheese can be used.
