## Ingredients

- 12 veal cutlets
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/4 cups crushed breadcrumbs
- 1/4 cup grated cheese (preferably Parmesan)
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 liter broth or water
- 2 cups 'ideal macaroni'
- 2 liters water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3 tablespoons tomato purée or melted butter

## Directions

1. Wash the cutlets with a cloth dipped in hot water, clean and pound lightly on each side.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, brush with the beaten eggs and turn in the crushed breadcrumbs mixed with cheese.
3. Brown in butter, add broth, and let the cutlets cook until done for 1/4 hour.
4. Rinse the macaroni and place in salted boiling water. When soft, strain and rinse with water so they don't stick together.
5. Add the tomato purée or melted butter with careful stirring.
6. Serve the veal cutlets in the middle of the platter and the macaroni around them.
