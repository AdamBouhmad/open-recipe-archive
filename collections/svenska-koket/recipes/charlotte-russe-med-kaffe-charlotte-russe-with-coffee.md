## Ingredients

- 75 gr. coffee beans
- 5 parts thick cream
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 hg. sugar
- 10 gr. gelatin
- sponge fingers or slices of light and dark sponge cake
- 0.5 tablespoon butter for the mold

## Directions

1. Roast the coffee, and while it is still warm, pour 2 parts cream over the unground coffee, then let it stand for 0.5 hour in a warm place.
2. Strain the cream off and whip it together with the eggs and sugar in a saucepan over heat until the egg mixture thickens.
3. Remove the cream from heat and let it cool.
4. Dissolve the gelatin in a little lukewarm water and fold it into the cream together with the remaining cream, whipped to stiff peaks.
5. Butter a mold and line it with sponge fingers or with light and dark sponge cake; fill in the cream and set out to stiffen.
6. Dip it briefly in lukewarm water.
7. Turn out the pudding and serve.
