## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound chicken
- 1/4 pound lean pork
- 1 pint water
- chicken blood

## Directions

1. Chop the meat into small pieces.
2. Cook slowly for 2½ hours, or until about half of the liquid has evaporated.
3. Put into the mixture a bowl of chicken blood in order to do away with any oil which may exist.
4. Strain through a thick cloth until the liquid is as clear as water. Should there be any oil remaining on top, skim it off.
5. Let the soup cool. Keep in the refrigerator to be used as needed.
