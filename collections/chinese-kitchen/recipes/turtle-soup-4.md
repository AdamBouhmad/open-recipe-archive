## Ingredients

- 8 turtles
- 2 cups mushrooms
- 2 cups chestnuts
- ¾ pound Chinese roast pork
- ¾ cup Fun Wine
- 1 teaspoonful ginger root juice
- 25 cents' worth of Bug Kay

## Directions

1. Put the live turtles into a cooking-pan of cold water. Bring to a boil slowly.
2. Remove the shell and interior. Wash. Cut the meat into pieces 1 inch by 1 inch by 1¼ inches.
3. Have a hot fire. Put 2 tablespoonfuls of oil into a frying-pan. When the oil is very hot put in the meat, Fun Wine, and ginger root juice. Fry for 10 minutes. Turn constantly.
4. Cut the bamboo shoots into pieces ¾ inch by 1½ inches by 1/16 inch. Cut the pork into pieces ¾ inch by 1¼ inches by ¾ inch.
5. Add to the meat in the frying-pan, the mushrooms, chestnuts, bamboo shoots, pork and enough primary soup to cover. Bring to a boil.
6. Put into a suitable bowl, add the Bug Kay, Dong Sum, Gay Gee, Yen York and steam for 1½ hours.
7. Skim off any oil which may be on the top. Salt to taste. Serve the soup in bowls.
