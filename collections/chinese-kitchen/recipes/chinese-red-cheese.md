## Ingredients

- Bean cake made of Chinese red beans
- Fun Wine
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wrap up the cakes in a piece of cloth in any desired size
2. Put pressure on top for 5 days
3. Take off the weight and then the cloth
4. Scrape off the mold on top
5. Place in a jar
6. Fill the jar 1/2 full of Fun Wine, and add plenty of salt
7. Cover air-tight, and set away for not less than two weeks; the longer, the better, provided the jar is kept air-tight
