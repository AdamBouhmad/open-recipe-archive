## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1/2 pound sugar
- 3/4 pound lard
- 5 eggs
- 3/4 teaspoonful alkaline solution
- almond

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, sugar, lard, eggs, and solution well on a suitable board. Add a little quantity of lard at a time until every particle of flour will contain an equal amount of each substance.
2. Make into a cake of any desired size. In the center of each place an almond.
3. Put into a suitable pan and bake in the oven until nicely browned. The length of time depends on the temperature of the oven and the amount of cake.
