## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 1 pound pork
- 2 onions
- 2 cups bamboo shoots
- 2 cups water chestnuts
- A little oil
- Salt
- Chinese ham dice
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well and put them in a bowl with oil and salt.
2. Cut the onions, pork, bamboo shoots, and water chestnuts into threads 1 1/2 inches long.
3. Mix together all of the ingredients and fry in boiling oil until done.
4. Put all in a bowl and add Chinese gravy.
