## Ingredients

- 9 dozen oysters
- 9 cups bamboo shoots
- 2 cups water chestnuts
- 2 cups celery
- A few pieces of pork
- 1 tablespoonful Fun Wine
- 1 teaspoonful ginger juice
- primary soup
- Chinese gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the oysters, bamboo shoots, chestnuts, celery, and pork into fragments.
2. Put the oysters into boiling water for 10 minutes. Then fry the oysters in a hot oiled pan.
3. Add the Fun Wine and the ginger juice. Continue to cook for 5 minutes.
4. Fry the bamboo shoots, water chestnuts, celery, and pork in an oiled pan for 5 minutes. Add to oysters.
5. Add 1 cup primary soup and boil until about 1 cup of liquid remains.
6. Add Chinese gravy.
