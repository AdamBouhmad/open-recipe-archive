## Ingredients

- pork
- bean cakes
- Chinese vegetables

## Directions

1. Cut 1 pound pork into pieces 1 inch by 1 inch by 1 1/2 inches.
2. Use about 10 bean cakes. Cut each bean cake into 4 pieces. Fry until yellow.
3. Cut about 3 pounds of any Chinese vegetables into pieces the same size as the pork.
4. Cook the pork, bean cakes, and vegetables in plain water in an oiled pan for 3/4 hour.
5. Add Chinese gravy.
