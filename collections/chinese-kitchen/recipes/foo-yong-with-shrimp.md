## Ingredients

- one-half cupful fresh shrimp meat
- one-fourth cupful bamboo shoots
- a half cupful celery
- a half cupful Chinese potatoes
- a little shredded onion
- a half dozen eggs
- One teaspoonful of each of the two sauces
- a small half teaspoonful of sugar
- a teaspoonful of rice flour or cornstarch
- a little water

## Directions

1. Remove the shells from fresh shrimps and use one-half cupful fresh shrimp meat.
2. One-fourth cupful bamboo shoots, a half cupful each of celery and Chinese potatoes, and a little shredded onion.
3. After all is cut, put in bowl and break over it a half dozen eggs.
4. Mix all together and fry in small cakes that are flat like pancakes.
5. Serve on an oval platter and pour over it the following sauce.
6. Mix one teaspoonful of each of the two sauces, a small half teaspoonful of sugar, a teaspoonful of rice flour or cornstarch and a little water in pan in which cakes were fried.
7. Pour sauce over cakes in platter.
8. Serve with rice and tea.
