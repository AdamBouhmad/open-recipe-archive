## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound beef
- 4 cups celery cut into pieces 3/4 inch long
- 2 cups bean sprouts
- 3/4 teaspoonful cornstarch
- 3/4 teaspoonful Fun Wine
- A few pieces sesamum-seed oil
- A few threads ginger root
- salt to taste
- Chinese sauce
- Chinese gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into pieces 1/32 inch by 3/4 inch by 1 3/4 inches. Mix thoroughly with the cornstarch, sesamum-seed oil, Fun Wine, and a little salt and Chinese sauce.
2. Put the celery and bean sprouts into a hot, oiled pan and fry for 5 minutes.
3. Add water enough to cover. Spread the beef on top. Cover tightly and cook until nearly dry.
4. Add Chinese gravy and mix well.
