## Ingredients

- one or more eggs
- pinch of salt
- flour
- small spoonful of clear soup stock

## Directions

1. Beat one or more eggs and add a pinch of salt
2. Add as much flour as the egg will absorb
3. Add a small spoonful of clear soup stock and mix with the fingers into a soft, smooth dough, adding more flour as needed
4. Roll out as thin as possible and let stand for a few minutes
5. Roll over and over carefully into a long thin roll
6. Cut thin slices from the end of the roll
