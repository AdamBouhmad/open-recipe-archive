## Ingredients

- six Mandarin oranges
- two small cucumbers
- two good sized preserved ginger roots
- a small piece of preserved citron
- one-half cupful of the syrup of the preserved ginger root
- an equal amount of lemon juice
- salted almonds

## Directions

1. Slice six Mandarin oranges, removing all skin and seeds
2. Peel and cut into cubes two small cucumbers
3. Mince fine two good sized preserved ginger roots
4. Boil a small piece of preserved citron till tender
5. Mix together all the prepared ingredients
6. Mix a half cupful of the syrup of the preserved ginger root with an equal amount of lemon juice
7. Pour the syrup mixture over the salad
8. Garnish with salted almonds and serve
