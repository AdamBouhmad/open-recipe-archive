## Ingredients

- Young sucking pig
- Seasoning Sauce
- finely ground imported spice

## Directions

1. The pig is carefully dressed, but is not stuffed.
2. Rub inside and out with the Seasoning Sauce until the meat is well seasoned.
3. Rub with the finely ground imported spice.
4. Hang on a spit over a clear wood fire.
5. Turn frequently and cook slowly till brown and crisp.
