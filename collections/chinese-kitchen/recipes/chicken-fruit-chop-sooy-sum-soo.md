## Ingredients

- One-half pound lean pork from the shoulder
- One-half pound white meat of cooked chicken
- Seven Chinese potatoes, peeled
- Six Chinese mushrooms, previously steamed with stems removed
- Scant cupful bamboo shoots
- One cupful celery
- One level tablespoon olive oil
- One cupful weak and hot chicken stock
- One teaspoon of each sauce
- One dessertspoon rice flour, wheat flour, or cornstarch
- One teaspoon chicken stock
- A pinch sugar

## Directions

1. Cut fine one-half pound of lean pork from the shoulder, one-half pound white meat of cooked chicken, seven Chinese potatoes, six Chinese mushrooms, scant cupful bamboo shoots, and one cupful celery.
2. Place pork in kettle with one level tablespoon olive oil already heated to prevent meat from sticking, and stir frequently till done.
3. Add the cooked chicken meat and the vegetables prepared as directed above, adding a little more oil if necessary.
4. Draw the mixture carefully from the sides of the kettle to the center and pour around the sides of the kettle a cupful of weak and hot chicken stock.
5. Cover tightly and let cook slowly till the vegetables are done for about fifteen minutes.
6. Mix one teaspoon of each sauce with one dessertspoon rice flour, wheat flour, or cornstarch, a teaspoon chicken stock, and a pinch sugar to thicken gravy.
7. Add a little more stock to the kettle if necessary.
8. Serve with bowl of rice and pot of tea.
