## Ingredients

- Chinese white beans
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil Chinese white beans slowly for 6 hours.
2. Strain off the beans and expose the bean soup in a big pot placed under the hot sun.
3. The surface of the liquid turns brown and has a top layer. Remove this layer of brown.
4. A little later take off the other layers, and so on until there is no brown layer.
5. Add salt to this, and boil.
