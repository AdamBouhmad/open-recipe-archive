## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- 1 can of pineapple
- 2 cups green peppers
- 2 cups celery
- 4 pieces of canned sour ginger
- 3/4 cup Fun Wine
- 2 eggs
- cornstarch
- salt
- primary soup
- vinegar
- sugar
- Chinese gravy

## Directions

1. Cut into small oblong pieces the duck, pineapple, peppers, celery, and ginger.
2. Break the eggs into the duck and mix well together with a little cornstarch and salt.
3. Put the pineapple, pepper, celery, and ginger into a hot, oiled pan and fry for 2 minutes.
4. Add the fried duck. Mix well and add the Fun Wine. Have a hot fire. After 5 minutes add enough primary soup to cover the substances and cook until about one cup of liquid is left.
5. Add the vinegar and sugar and mix with Chinese gravy.
