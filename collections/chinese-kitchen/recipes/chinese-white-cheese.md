## Ingredients

- Bean cake made of Chinese white beans
- Fun Wine
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut bean cake into half-inch squares 1/4 inch thick
2. Put into a jar provided with an air-tight cover
3. Fill the jar 1/4 full of Fun Wine
4. Salt to taste
5. Cover air-tight, and put away for not less than two weeks
