## Ingredients

- 1 duck (about 7 pounds)
- The same amount of skinned potatoes
- A cake of Chinese red cheese
- 1 small piece of ginger, pounded
- 3/8 cup Fun Wine
- A piece of green carrot
- water
- oil

## Directions

1. Put the duck into an oiled frying-pan and fry until brown. Turn frequently, so that every part of the duck receives the same amount of heat.
2. Add the ginger and Fun Wine and fry for 2 minutes. Take out and put into a bowl. The liquid left in the frying-pan is to be put into a separate bowl.
3. Add 2 cups of water to the cheese and mash. Rub this well into the duck, inside and outside.
4. Put the potatoes into the cooking-pan. Place the duck on top. Add enough water to cover all. Over this pour any cheese liquid which remains. Add the carrot.
5. Cover the pan and cook until tender.
