## Ingredients

- 2 pounds beef
- 2 cups green peppers cut into pieces 1 inch by 1 inch
- 1 cup celery, chopped
- 1 cup bean sprouts
- 3/4 teaspoonful cornstarch
- 1/2 teaspoonful Fun Wine
- A few pieces sesamum-seed oil
- A few threads ginger root
- Chinese sauce
- Chinese gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into pieces 1/32 inch by 3/4 inch by 1 3/4 inches. Mix with the cornstarch oil, sesamum-seed oil, and Chinese sauce.
2. Put the celery, bean sprouts, and green peppers into a hot, oiled pan and fry for 5 minutes.
3. Add water enough to cover. Spread the beef on top. Cover tightly and cook until nearly dry.
4. Add Chinese gravy and mix well.
