## Ingredients

- 2 cups lily-root powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 6 cups water

## Directions

1. Dissolve the lily-root powder in a small quantity of cold water. Mash well.
2. To this add the 6 cups of water and the sugar. Stir well.
3. Put into a suitable pan. Steam until done (about 1 hour).
