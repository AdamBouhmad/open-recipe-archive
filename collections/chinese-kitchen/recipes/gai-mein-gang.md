## Ingredients

- noodles
- salted water
- good chicken stock
- cooked white meat of chicken
- sliced and cooked Chinese mushrooms
- half cupful of bean sprouts
- fine strips of Chinese cured pork

## Directions

1. Boil noodles till done in salted water and drain in colander
2. Put noodles in bowl and pour over plenty of good chicken stock
3. Have ready cooked white meat of chicken with half as much sliced and cooked Chinese mushrooms
4. Cook bean sprouts for six minutes
5. Put chicken and mushrooms over the noodles in bowl
6. Garnish with fine strips of Chinese cured pork in center, and five thin strips of white meat of chicken each side
