## Ingredients

- 1 cup lily flower
- 1 cup fungus
- 2 cups dry mushrooms
- 1 chicken
- A small piece of ginger root, without the skin
- 3/4 cup of Fun Wine

## Directions

1. Soak the lily flower, fungus, and the mushrooms in cold water for 3/4 hour.
2. Wash the chicken inside and outside with Chinese sauce. Put in a pan with oil and fry for 10 minutes. Add the Fun Wine and salt. Add 2 cups of water.
3. Now put all into a small pot and add the lily flower, fungus, mushrooms, and ginger. Keep cover on tight and cook for 3/4 hour over a slow fire.
