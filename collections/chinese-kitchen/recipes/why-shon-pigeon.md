## Ingredients

- 6 pigeons
- A few pieces of pork
- 10 cents' worth of Kee Zee
- 25 cents' worth of Dong Chong Chow
- 10 cents' worth of Why Shon

## Directions

1. When the pigeons have been washed, rub inside and out with salt. Put in a bowl.
2. Wash the Why Shon, Kee Zee, Dong Chong Chow, and pork. Add to the pigeons.
3. Cover with primary soup and steam for 1½ hours in a double-boiler.
4. Remove the oil from the top of the liquid. Salt to taste. Use the liquid for soup. Serve the pigeon with oil and Chinese sauce.
