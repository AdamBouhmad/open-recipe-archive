## Ingredients

- small white mushrooms
- oil
- powdered spice
- Chinese Sauces

## Directions

1. Open the squab down the back with a very sharp knife
2. Remove the entire skeleton of the bird from the meat without breaking the skin
3. Carefully tie the ends of the wings, the legs and the neck
4. Sew up the back of the bird except one small opening
5. Coat the small white mushrooms with oil and rub a small portion of powdered spice over them
6. Insert the mushrooms through the small opening
7. Place the birds in a pan
8. Cook in a hot oven, frequently basting with a mixture of the Chinese Sauces
9. Allow twenty minutes for cooking
