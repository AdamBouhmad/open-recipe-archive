## Ingredients

- 6 soft-shell crabs
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup red vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful cornstarch

## Directions

1. Mix the crab with the cornstarch and eggs. Salt to taste.
2. Fry in boiling oil until golden brown. Serve with the red vinegar.
