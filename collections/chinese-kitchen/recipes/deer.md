## Ingredients

- 2 pounds deer
- 2 cups water chestnuts
- 2 cups mushrooms
- 2 cups bamboo shoots
- 1 pound chicken meat
- 3/8 teaspoonful ginger juice
- 1 tablespoonful Fun Wine
- A few pieces of sugar-cane about 3 inches long, unskinned
- salt
- primary soup
- oil
- Chinese sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the deer into pieces 1 1/2 inches by 1 1/2 inches by 1 inch. Cut the secondary vegetables into small pieces.
2. Fry the deer in a hot, oiled pan. Turn constantly.
3. Add the ginger juice, sugar-cane, and a little salt. Add the secondary vegetables and the chicken and cook for 5 minutes.
4. Add enough primary soup to cover and bring to a boil.
5. Transfer to a suitable bowl. Put the Fun Wine on top. Steam for 2 hours.
6. Serve with oil and Chinese sauce.
