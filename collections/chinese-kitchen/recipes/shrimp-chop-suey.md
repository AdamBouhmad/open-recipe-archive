## Ingredients

- 9 pounds shrimp with the shells off
- 2 cups dry mushrooms
- 2 cups bean sprouts
- 1 piece of onion cut into threads
- 3/4 teaspoonful Fun Wine
- primary soup
- Chinese gravy

## Directions

1. Fry mushrooms, bean sprouts, and onion in an oily pan for 10 minutes. Add enough water to cover. Boil for 15 minutes.
2. Fry the shrimp in a hot, oiled pan and add the Fun Wine. Keep frying until the shrimp turns red.
3. Mix the shrimp with the mushrooms, bean sprouts, and onion, and add 2 cups of primary soup. When it boils, add Chinese gravy.
