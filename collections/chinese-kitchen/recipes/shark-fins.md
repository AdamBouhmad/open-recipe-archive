## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of dry shark fins
- a few pieces of dry garlic
- 2 pieces of ginger root
- 2 tablespoonfuls of lard
- primary soup
- 1 cup of chicken starch
- whites of 3 eggs
- Chinese ham dice
- cornstarch
- salt
- 1 tablespoonful red vinegar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Buy 2 pounds of dry shark fins from a Chinese grocery store. Soak in cold water for 3 hours.
2. Boil the fins with a few pieces of dry garlic and 2 pieces of ginger root. Change the water several times when boiling.
3. Put into a suitable pan. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of lard, and twice more than enough primary soup to cover. Boil slowly for 3/4 hour. Drain off the liquid and throw it away.
4. Put into another pan, containing 6 pints of primary soup, and boil.
5. Change again into a third pan of primary soup. Add gravy which consists of 1 cup of chicken starch, the whites of 3 eggs, Chinese ham dice and a little cornstarch and salt. Use 1 tablespoonful red vinegar to improve the taste. Garnish with parsley and serve hot.
