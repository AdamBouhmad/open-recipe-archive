## Ingredients

- 1 chicken cut into pieces about 1 1/2 inches long
- 2 cups mushrooms
- A few slices of ginger root
- 4 pieces of red dates
- 1 Chinese onion
- Cornstarch, sauce, salt, and oil

## Directions

1. Mix well the chicken, oil, salt, cornstarch, and sauce.
2. Add mushrooms, ginger root, and red dates. Put on a plate and steam for 1 1/2 hour.
3. Add Chinese onion cut into threads 1 1/2 inches long. Garnish with parsley. Serve with mustard, sauce, and oil.
