## Ingredients

- 2½ dozen oysters
- Lard-skin
- 1 teaspoonful ginger juice
- 1 tablespoonful Fun Wine
- Whites of 2 eggs
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Wash the oysters. Boil them in water for 10 minutes. Then fry in hot oil.
2. Add the Fun Wine and ginger juice. Fry for 7 minutes.
3. Cut the lard-skin into pieces as wide as the oysters and about 4 inches long. Wrap each oyster with a piece of lard-skin, using the white of egg for paste.
4. Put the wrapped oysters into boiling oil and fry until they are brown.
5. Now set them in a bowl and steam for 1½ hours. Serve on lettuce.
