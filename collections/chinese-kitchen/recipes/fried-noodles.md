## Ingredients

- 2 pounds Chinese noodles
- 1½ cups onions cut into threads
- 1 cup raw pork cut into threads 1½ inches long
- ¾ cup roast pork cut into threads
- 1 egg fried and cut into threads 1½ inches long
- 2 tablespoonfuls lard
- 1 tablespoonful Chinese sauce
- 1 tablespoonful cornstarch
- 3 cups primary soup
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Put the lard on the noodles, and steam for ¾ hour.
2. After the noodles have been steamed, they tend to stay together; therefore it is necessary to loosen them up with a fork.
3. Place the steamed noodles in a pan of boiling oil and fry until nicely brown. Then put on a dish.
4. Cook the onion and the pork until done.
5. Make a gravy of the primary soup, cornstarch (first dissolved in water), Chinese sauce, and salt and pepper. Add the roast pork and egg threads.
6. Put the onion and pork over the noodles. Pour over this the gravy.
