## Ingredients

- Any kind of vegetables washed thoroughly and cut into pieces one inch long
- Small piece of root ginger
- 9 cups primary soup
- Salt
- Oil

## Directions

1. Heat the cooking pan for 1/2 minute
2. Then spread one teaspoonful of oil all over the surface of the pan and let it heat for from 1 to 2 minutes
3. Add the salt, then the vegetables and ginger
4. Turn over frequently until the volume of the vegetables is reduced to 1/2
5. Add the primary soup, and let it cook until it boils
6. Keep the soup boiling slowly until done; the length of time depends upon the kind of vegetables
7. It is best to keep the pan uncovered
