## Ingredients

- 8 pounds lobster
- 12 eggs
- 2 cups bamboo shoots
- 2 cups water chestnuts
- 2 cups mushrooms
- 2 onions

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well in a bowl with oil and salt.
2. Cut the onions, lobster, bamboo shoots, water chestnuts, and mushrooms into threads 1 1/2 inches long. Cook until done.
3. Add beaten eggs and mix well. Continue to cook until eggs are done.
