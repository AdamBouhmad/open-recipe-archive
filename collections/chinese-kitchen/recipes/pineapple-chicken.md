## Ingredients

- 1 chicken
- 1 can pineapple
- 2 cups green peppers
- 2 cups celery
- 1 cup canned sour ginger
- 3/8 cup vinegar
- 3/8 cup Fun Wine
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken, pineapple, peppers, celery, and ginger into pieces 1 1/2 inches long.
2. Break the eggs into the chicken and mix well together with a little cornstarch and salt. Then put into boiling oil and fry until all becomes yellow.
3. Put the pineapple, pepper, celery, and ginger into a hot, oiled pan and fry for 2 minutes. Add the fried chicken. Mix well and add the Fun Wine. Cook over a hot fire for 5 minutes; then add enough primary soup to cover and cook until about one cup of liquid is left.
4. Add the vinegar and a little sugar.
5. Pour Chinese gravy over the chicken.
