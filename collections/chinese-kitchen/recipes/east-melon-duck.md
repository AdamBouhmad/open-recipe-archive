## Ingredients

- 1 boneless duck cut into small dice
- 1 east melon (8 inches in diameter)
- 1 handful skinned water chestnuts cut into small dice
- 1 handful lotus nuts
- 1 handful mushrooms cut into small dice
- 1 small piece of ginger without the skin, pounded
- 1 teaspoonful Fun Wine
- primary soup
- salt
- oil
- cold water

## Directions

1. Cut off the top of the melon as shown in the picture. Take out the seeds.
2. Oil the cooking-pan and heat.
3. Fry the duck for 10 minutes.
4. Add the ginger and Fun Wine. Salt. Fry for 1 minute more. Turn frequently.
5. Add chestnuts, lotus nuts, mushrooms, and enough primary soup to fill the melon. Cook until the soup boils.
6. Transfer all into the melon. Cover with the piece you cut from the melon. Steam for about 3½ hours; the size of melon decides the length of time necessary for steaming.
7. Remove from the stove. Add cold water to stop the steam. Take the melon out of the pan. Take off the cover of the melon. By means of a spoon take off any oil which has formed on the top.
8. Scoop out the melon and mix well with the duck and other ingredients. Be careful not to break the rind. Serve hot.
