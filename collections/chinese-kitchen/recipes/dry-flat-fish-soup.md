## Ingredients

- 1 pound dry flat fish
- 3/4 teaspoonful alkaline solution
- 12 cups primary soup
- sesamum-seed oil

## Directions

1. Cover the dry flat fish with cold water. Add the alkaline solution. Soak for 15 minutes. Wash thoroughly, take out the bones.
2. Cook the fish in plain water for 3 hours. Change the water several times while cooking. Then cook for 15 minutes in the primary soup.
3. Serve with the soup. Put into it a few drops of sesamum-seed oil.
