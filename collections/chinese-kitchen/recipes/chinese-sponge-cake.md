## Ingredients

- 10 eggs
- 1 pound sugar
- 5/8 pound flour
- A few drops of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs in a suitable bowl. Mix well with sugar. Beat for an hour, being careful always to beat in one direction.
2. Mix with the flour and lemon juice.
3. Put into a suitable pan and steam for 3/4 hour.
