## Ingredients

- One pound lean young pig pork, cut into slices one inch long, one-quarter inch wide and one-quarter inch thick
- Two chicken livers, gizzards and hearts, sliced thin
- One-half ounce green ginger roots
- One tablespoon olive oil
- One pound cooked white meat of chicken, cut fine
- One cupful hot chicken stock
- One tablespoon Seasoning Sauce
- Two cups celery, cut fine
- One small can white mushrooms, sliced
- One tablespoon shredded onion
- One-half cup bean sprouts

## Directions

1. Cut one pound lean young pig pork into slices one inch long, one-quarter inch wide and one-quarter inch thick.
2. Slice thin two chicken livers, gizzards and hearts, and one-half ounce green ginger roots.
3. Put all in kettle with one tablespoon olive oil previously heated, and brown evenly, stirring frequently till done, using care that it does not scorch.
4. Add one pound cooked white meat of chicken cut fine.
5. Pour around sides of kettle one cupful hot chicken stock, add one tablespoon Seasoning Sauce and stir carefully.
6. Have ready two cups celery cut fine, one small can white mushrooms sliced, a tablespoon shredded onion and put all in kettle, cooking for twelve minutes.
7. Put in one-half cup bean sprouts, adding a little as directed.
