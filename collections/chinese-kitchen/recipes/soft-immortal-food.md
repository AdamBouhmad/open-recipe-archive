## Ingredients

- 12 pieces bean cake
- 2 cups dry mushrooms
- 4 ounces Chinese vermicelli
- A few bean sticks

## Directions

1. Cut each piece of bean cake into 4 pieces.
2. Soak the mushrooms, vermicelli, and bean sticks in water for 1½ hour. Cut the vermicelli into pieces 3 inches long.
3. Put all of the ingredients in cold water and cook for 1 hour. Put in plenty of oil and red cheese.
4. Add Chinese gravy.
