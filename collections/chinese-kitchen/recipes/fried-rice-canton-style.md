## Ingredients

- small stalk of celery
- one small onion
- two Chinese potatoes
- one cupful Chinese cured pork
- one good-sized bowl of cooked rice
- well beaten egg
- olive or peanut oil

## Directions

1. Mince fine small stalk of celery, one small onion, two Chinese potatoes, one cupful Chinese cured pork, and mix with one good-sized bowl of cooked rice.
2. Fry in oblong cakes in olive or peanut oil, using sufficient well beaten egg to mold the other ingredients into shape.
