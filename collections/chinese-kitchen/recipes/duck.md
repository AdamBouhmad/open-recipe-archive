## Ingredients

- one-half pound partly fat young pork
- tablespoon olive or peanut oil
- one pound cooked breast of duck
- one tablespoon shredded onion
- one cup fine cut Chinese potatoes
- one cup celery
- one cup tops of white mushrooms
- one cup hot chicken stock
- one tablespoon Seasoning and Flavoring Sauce
- one tablespoon Flavoring Sauce
- one dessertspoon corn starch or flour
- one-half ounce green ginger
- two green peppers

## Directions

1. Slice the pork in thin strips
2. Brown the pork in a kettle with tablespoon olive or peanut oil
3. Add one pound cooked breast of duck cut in long, fine strips, one tablespoon shredded onion and let cook five minutes
4. Add one cup fine cut Chinese potatoes, one cup celery cut fine, one cup tops of white mushrooms
5. Pour around sides one cup hot chicken stock and let cook fifteen minutes
6. Season with one tablespoon each of Seasoning and Flavoring Sauce and a dessertspoon corn starch, or flour for thickening
7. Add one-half ounce green ginger if liked, or two green peppers seeded and cut very fine
