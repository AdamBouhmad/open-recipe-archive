## Ingredients

- noodles
- salted water
- chicken stock or beef or mutton broth
- one-half a hard boiled egg for each bowl
- five or six fine strips of Chinese cured pork
- small pieces of cooked white meat of chicken
- Seasoning Sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the noodles till done in salted water and drain in colander
2. Put noodles in the necessary number of bowls to serve with chicken stock, or beef, or mutton broth, enough to thin somewhat
3. Use for garnish one-half a hard boiled egg for each bowl with five or six fine strips of the Chinese cured pork and a few small pieces of the cooked white meat of chicken around the sides of bowl
4. Additional seasoning may be supplied at the table by using a small portion of the Seasoning Sauce, according to the taste of the individual
