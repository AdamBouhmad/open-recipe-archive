## Ingredients

- 1 medium-sized duck without bones
- 1 1/2 cups Chinese mushrooms
- 2 cups bamboo shoots
- 4 cups celery
- 2 teaspoonfuls oil
- A few drops sesamum-seed oil
- 1 teaspoonful Chinese sauce
- 1/4 teaspoonful cornstarch
- Salt
- Primary soup
- Chinese ham dice

## Directions

1. Chop all into dice
2. Heat the cooking pan for 3/4 minute
3. Then grease it thoroughly with oil
4. Add salt and all other ingredients except duck
5. Keep turning for about 5 minutes
6. Add primary soup and boil very slowly
7. Mix together well the duck (cut into dice), the oil, sesamum-seed oil, Chinese sauce, and cornstarch
8. When primary soup has boiled 3/4 hour, add the duck mixture
9. Boil slowly for another 3/4 hour
10. Use Chinese ham dice as a garnish
