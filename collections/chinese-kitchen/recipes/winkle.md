## Ingredients

- 8 winkles
- 2 cups water chestnuts, unshelled
- 8 cups mushrooms
- 2 cups bamboo shoots
- 1 cup celery
- A few pieces of pork
- 3/8 teaspoonful ginger root juice
- 1 tablespoonful Fun Wine
- 1 cup primary soup
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the winkles carefully. Break the shell. Take the meat out. Cut into pieces 1/16 inch by 1 1/4 inches by 1 5/16 inches.
2. Cut the secondary vegetables and the pork into pieces and fry in a hot, oiled pan. Add enough water to cover. Cover the pan and cook until done.
