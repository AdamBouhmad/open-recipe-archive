## Ingredients

- 12 pieces bean cake
- East melon (twice the amount of bean cake)
- 2 cups white nuts

## Directions

1. Cut each bean cake into 4 pieces. Cut the melon into pieces 1 inch by 1 inch by 1½ inches.
2. Fry all in an oiled pan.
3. Add enough water to cover. Add ¼ cake of red cheese and plenty of oil. Mix. Cook for 1 hour.
