## Ingredients

- rice
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the rice according to the previous directions
2. Put the rice in a kettle and pour over it sufficient boiling water to cover it at least two inches above the rice
3. Cover the kettle tightly so no steam can escape and set where it will keep very hot but not actually boil
4. Allow from half to three-quarters of an hour for this method of cooking
