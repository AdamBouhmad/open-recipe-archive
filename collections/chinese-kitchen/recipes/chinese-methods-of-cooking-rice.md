## Ingredients

- rice
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the rice in many waters, rubbing the rice well between the hands in each water until it is entirely free from the white powdery substance that gives the water a milky appearance
2. Continue washing until the water remains perfectly clear
3. Throw the washed rice into a good sized kettle in which a considerable quantity of water is boiling rapidly
4. Allow this rapid boiling to continue for at least a quarter of an hour, or more
5. Take one kernel of rice from the kettle and rub between the thumb and finger to test doneness; if it rubs away easily, the rice is done; if not quite done boil a few minutes longer
6. Pour the contents of the kettle into a colander with fine holes set in a shallow pan and let the water drain off
7. Put the rice back into the kettle, pour over it a small cup of boiling water
8. Cover the kettle tightly and set near, but not over the fire till time to serve
