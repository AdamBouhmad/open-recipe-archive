## Ingredients

- one pound lean pork cut fine
- one tablespoon olive oil
- one cup of the tops of French mushrooms cut very fine
- two green peppers cut fine with seeds removed
- one cup celery
- one-half cup bamboo shoots cut fine
- one tablespoon minced Spanish onion
- one teaspoon peanut oil
- one cup hot water
- one tablespoon Seasoning Sauce
- one rounded teaspoon corn starch or flour
- one teaspoon Flavoring Sauce
- four drops Sesamun oil

## Directions

1. Brown one pound lean pork cut fine in Chop Sooy kettle with one tablespoon olive oil
2. When done add one cup of the tops of French mushrooms cut very fine, two green peppers cut fine with seeds removed, one cup celery and one-half cup bamboo shoots cut fine and one tablespoon minced Spanish onion
3. When the vegetables are mixed with the meat, add one teaspoon peanut oil and one cup hot water, remembering to pour it carefully around sides of the kettle
4. Cover tightly and let all cook for fifteen minutes, very slow
5. Use one tablespoon Seasoning Sauce thickened with one rounded teaspoon corn starch or flour and add when vegetables are done
6. Then add one teaspoon Flavoring Sauce and four drops Sesamun oil
7. Serve with bowls of rice and tea
