## Ingredients

- two pounds of any kind of meat
- two handfuls Chinese mushrooms
- two Spanish onions
- Seasoning Sauce
- one pound peeled and sliced Chinese potatoes
- two cupfuls noodles
- one egg
- cooked ham
- green onion tops

## Directions

1. Cut meat into small square pieces
2. Soak Chinese mushrooms for two hours, remove the stems and cut in fine pieces
3. Cut Spanish onions fine
4. Put all in pan with enough Seasoning Sauce to cover bottom of pan about one inch deep
5. When boiling, cover tightly and set on back of stove to cook slowly about two hours
6. When nearly done, put in one pound peeled and sliced Chinese potatoes and let cook till done, about ten minutes
7. Add a little boiling water if needed, but not too much
8. Cook noodles in boiling water and drain in colander
9. Put meat mixture in large oval dish and cover over top with two cupfuls cooked noodles
10. Beat one egg very light, cook in flat pan spreading very thin, and let cool
11. Slice egg in thin, fine strips
12. Slice green onion tops and a piece of cooked ham about three inches long
13. Put egg strips over center of noodles with ham each side and green onion at ends of dish
