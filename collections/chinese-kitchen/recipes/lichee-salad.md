## Ingredients

- ten Mandarin oranges
- ten Lichee nuts
- preserved ginger syrup
- a piece of preserved citron
- water
- two small cucumbers
- lemon juice
- sugar
- rice wafers

## Directions

1. Slice ten Mandarin oranges and remove the skin, seeds, and inner skin
2. Cook the kernels of ten Lichee nuts in preserved ginger syrup for ten minutes
3. Cook a piece of preserved citron in water till tender and mince enough to make two tablespoonfuls
4. Peel and cut two small cucumbers into cubes
5. Mix the cucumbers with the other ingredients
6. Pour over them lemon juice well sweetened with sugar
7. Serve cold with rice wafers
