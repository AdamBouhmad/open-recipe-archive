## Ingredients

- 1 pound peanuts
- 3/4 pound sugar

## Directions

1. Fry the nuts in a hot pan for 10 minutes. Take off the skins.
2. Put 1 bowl of water in a hot, oiled pan. To this add the sugar. Cook, stirring constantly, until there is no water left.
3. Mix the peanuts with the sugar on a board. Roll while hot until the mixture is 1/8 inch thick. Let cool.
4. Cut to desired size.
