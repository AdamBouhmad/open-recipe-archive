## Ingredients

- 6 pounds pike
- 1 1/2 cups salted almonds or peanuts
- 3/4 cup Chinese ham
- 6 pounds vegetables in season
- 1 teaspoonful cornstarch
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- Chinese gravy

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the pike and take out the bones. Run through the grinder three times.
2. Cut into small pieces the almonds, ham, and the vegetables.
3. Put the pike into a big bowl and mix with it the cornstarch, 1 cup of water and 1 teaspoonful salt. Stir well for an hour. Be sure to stir in the same direction all the time.
4. Add the nuts and ham. Mix well.
5. Wash your hands as clean as possible. Hold the mixture in your hand and squeeze gently; a small ball will come through the top of the fingers. Take off with a spoon. Wash the spoon each time with cold water.
6. Place the ball in a pan of boiling water. When it comes to the top it indicates it is done and can be placed in a bowl.
7. Cook the vegetables until done.
8. Mix together the fish balls, the vegetables and Chinese gravy.
