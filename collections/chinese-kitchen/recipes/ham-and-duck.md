## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- Chinese ham
- 2 cups primary soup
- 1 cup Fun Wine

## Directions

1. Take the bones out of 1 duck. Then boil the duck for 15 minutes.
2. Boil Chinese ham for 15 minutes and then cut into pieces 1/16 inch by 3/4 inch by 1 1/8 inches. There should be 3/4 as much ham as duck.
3. Put in a bowl 1 piece of duck, 1 piece of ham, and so on, until the bowl is filled.
4. Add 2 cups primary soup and 1 cup Fun Wine, and steam for 2 1/2 hours.
