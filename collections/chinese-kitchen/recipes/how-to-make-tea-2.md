## Ingredients

- 1 level teaspoonful of tea
- 1 cup of water

## Directions

1. Heat the water until it just reaches the boiling-point, but no more
2. Insert a thermometer graduated with centigrade degrees; when it reaches 99° remove from the fire at once
3. Pour immediately into a half-pint teapot which contains 1 teaspoonful of Chinese tea
4. Keep covered for three minutes
5. Serve
