## Ingredients

- one teaspoonful each of olive oil and peanut oil
- one-half pound lean young pig pork cut in long thin strips
- one pound cooked chicken meat both dark and white
- one cupful bamboo shoots cut fine
- one cup white mushroom tops
- one-half ounce green ginger root
- one cup chicken stock
- one cup bean sprouts
- one teaspoon of each sauce
- one teaspoon rice flour
- six drops Sesamun oil

## Directions

1. Have a teasponful each of olive oil and peanut oil hot in the kettle
2. Put in first a half pound lean young pig pork cut in long thin strips and brown carefully
3. When done add one pound cooked chicken meat both dark and white
4. Add one cupful bamboo shoots cut fine, one cup white mushroom tops, one-half ounce green ginger root and pour around dish one cup chicken stock
5. Cook for ten minutes and add one cup bean sprouts
6. Cook six minutes longer, no more, or bean sprouts cook too long
7. Add one teaspoon of each sauce with one teaspoon rice flour for thickening
8. Add six drops Sesamun oil just before serving
9. Cut bamboo shoots and mushroom tops very fine
