## Ingredients

- two eggs
- small piece smoked pork
- one teaspoonful onion
- one tablespoonful celery
- one medium sized bowl of cooked rice
- peanut oil

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs light and pour over greased pan with a flat bottom, spreading eggs very thin.
2. Take small piece smoked pork, minced fine, one teaspoonful onion minced fine, one tablespoonful celery minced fine.
3. When eggs are cooked, cut in fine pieces.
4. Add all to one medium sized bowl of cooked rice, mix well and fry all together in pan, with peanut oil.
