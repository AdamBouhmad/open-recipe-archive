## Ingredients

- 12 bean cakes
- 3 pounds pike
- ¾ cup Chinese ham dice
- ¾ cup Chinese onion dice
- ¾ cup salted almonds
- boiling oil
- primary soup
- Chinese sauce
- sesamum-seed oil
- sweet sauce
- cornstarch
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut each bean cake into two triangles. Cut the triangles and remove the pieces to leave a hole as shown in the instructions.
2. Grind or pound the pike. Stir up in a small amount of salt water for ¾ hour.
3. Mix with the ham, onions, and almonds.
4. Fill the hole in the bean cake with this mixture. Fry in boiling oil until yellow.
5. Cook the fried bean cake triangles in primary soup for ¾ hour.
6. Add a gravy made of Chinese sauce, a few drops of sesamum-seed oil, sweet sauce, and cornstarch.
7. Garnish with parsley.
