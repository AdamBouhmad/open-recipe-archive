## Ingredients

- 1 cup primary soup
- 1 teaspoonful cornstarch
- 3/4 teaspoonful Chinese sauce
- salt
- sugar
- a few drops of sesamum-seed oil

## Directions

1. Mix the cornstarch well in a little cold water.
2. Stir into the boiling primary soup, and let boil until it thickens.
3. Add the Chinese sauce, salt, sugar, and sesamum-seed oil, and stir well.
