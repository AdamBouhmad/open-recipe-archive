## Ingredients

- noodles
- salted water
- cooked white meat of chicken
- sliced, canned white mushroom tops
- half cup fine cut Chinese potatoes
- bamboo shoots
- small cup hot chicken stock
- half hard boiled egg
- Chinese cured pork

## Directions

1. Boil noodles till done in salted water and drain in colander
2. Have ready cooked white meat of chicken with sliced, canned white mushroom tops, half cup fine cut Chinese potatoes and bamboo shoots mixed and cooked till tender
3. Put over top of noodles in small bowls, adding small cup hot chicken stock to each bowl
4. Serve with garnish of half hard boiled egg and Chinese cured pork in small thin strips on each side
