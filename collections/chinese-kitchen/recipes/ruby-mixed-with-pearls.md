## Ingredients

- 9 bean cakes
- 1 pound Chinese ham
- 1 pound shrimp
- primary soup
- Chinese gravy
- parsley

## Directions

1. Remove the thin outer layer from the bean cakes.
2. Cut the ham and the shrimp into ¼ inch cubes.
3. Boil all together in a pan of primary soup until the shrimp turns red.
4. Add Chinese gravy.
5. Garnish with parsley.
