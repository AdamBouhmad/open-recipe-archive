## Ingredients

- 4 pounds pigs' ribs
- 1 cup vinegar
- 2 teaspoonfuls sugar
- 1 cup Fun Wine
- 2 teaspoonfuls cornstarch
- 1 teaspoonful Chinese sauce
- 1 egg
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the ribs into pieces 3/4 inch by 3/4 inch by 1 3/4 inches, and mix well with the egg, Chinese sauce, salt, 1 teaspoonful cornstarch and 1 teaspoonful sugar. Fry in boiling oil until brown.
2. Add to the mixture the vinegar, Fun Wine, 2 cups of water, and the remaining sugar and cornstarch. Cook until nearly dry.
