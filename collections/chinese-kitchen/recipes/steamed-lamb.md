## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of lamb cut into pieces 3/8 inch by 1 3/8 inches by 3/8 inch
- 2 cups dry mushrooms
- 2 cups unskinned chestnuts
- 3 red dates
- 1 cup Fun Wine
- salt and sweet sauce (Chinese name Chew Tout)
- primary soup

## Directions

1. Use water to boil the lamb for 10 minutes.
2. Take out the lamb and dip into a bowl of salt and sweet sauce (Chinese name Chew Tout).
3. Put all into a suitable bowl. Add enough primary soup to cover and add the chestnuts, red dates, and Fun Wine. Steam until tender.
4. Remove any oil from the liquid, and serve.
