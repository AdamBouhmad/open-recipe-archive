## Ingredients

- 5 pounds pike not less than 2 1/2 inches in diameter
- 2 cups mushrooms
- A few pieces of ginger cut into pieces 1/32 inch by 3/4 inch by 1 inch
- 3 cups bamboo shoots cut into pieces 3/4 by 1 3/4 inches by 1/32 inch
- 2 cups bean sprouts
- Chinese gravy
- parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the pike and take out the bones. Cut into pieces 1/16 inch thick.
2. Fry the mushrooms, ginger, bamboo shoots, and bean sprouts in a hot, oiled pan for 5 minutes.
3. Add water enough to cover all. Cook for 15 minutes.
4. Add Chinese gravy.
5. Add the pike and mix well. Garnish with parsley, and serve hot.
