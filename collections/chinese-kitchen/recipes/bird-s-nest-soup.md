## Ingredients

- two ounces of bird's nests
- one fat chicken or one lean chicken with two small fat pork chops
- two quarts of water
- onion
- white meat of the chicken

## Directions

1. Soak the bird's nests in water all night
2. Remove any trace of feathers and wash carefully in several waters
3. Boil a fat chicken in two quarts of water, or if a lean chicken, add two small fat pork chops
4. Cook till a good rich stock is secured and season lightly
5. Boil a small amount of onion with the chicken
6. Strain the stock
7. Boil the soaked bird's nests in this stock for twenty minutes
8. Cut a little of the white meat of the chicken in fine pieces
9. Serve in each bowl
