## Ingredients

- 4 partridges
- 2 cups water chestnuts
- 5 cups mushrooms
- 2 cups celery
- 2 cups primary soup
- Chinese gravy

## Directions

1. Take the bones out of the partridges and cut the meat into thin pieces. Put into a hot, oiled pan and fry for 5 minutes.
2. Cut the secondary vegetables into thin pieces. Put into a hot pan and fry for 2 minutes. Add water and cook for 15 minutes.
3. Add the secondary vegetables to the partridge fragments. Mix well.
4. Add primary soup and cook for 5 minutes.
5. Add Chinese gravy.
