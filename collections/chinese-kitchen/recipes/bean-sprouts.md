## Ingredients

- beans
- water

## Directions

1. Soak some beans in water in a flat dish.
2. Spread the beans out; do not let one cover another.
3. Cover with a piece of wet cloth, and water every morning.
4. The sprouts will appear in two nights.
5. They are ready to eat when they are 2 inches long.
