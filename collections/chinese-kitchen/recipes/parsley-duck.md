## Ingredients

- 2 ducks
- 2 cups mushrooms
- 2 small pieces ginger root, mashed
- 1 tablespoonful Fun Wine
- Parsley enough to cover the dish 1 inch thick
- primary soup
- cornstarch
- sauce
- sesamum-seed oil
- salt
- Chinese ham

## Directions

1. Open the ducks with one 4-inch cut in the back of each. Take out the interior. Wash thoroughly and dry. Now wash the ducks inside and out with sweet sauce.
2. Cook the duck in a pan of boiling oil until yellow.
3. Put the duck into a frying-pan. Cover with the mushrooms, ginger, and Fun Wine. Add 1/2 more than enough primary soup to cover all. Cook until the duck is done.
4. Make a gravy of the liquid left, with cornstarch, sauce, sesamum-seed oil, and salt.
5. Place the duck on top of the parsley. Pour the gravy over all, and garnish with Chinese ham.
