## Ingredients

- 20 pairs of duck feet
- 2 cups chopped bamboo shoots
- 2 cups mushrooms
- 2 cups water chestnuts cut into thin pieces
- salt
- primary soup or water
- Chinese gravy

## Directions

1. Wash thoroughly 20 pairs of duck feet and plunge them into boiling water for 3 minutes. Take off the skin. Take out all bones without spoiling the shape of the feet. Cut each foot into 2 pieces.
2. Cut into small pieces the bamboo shoots, mushrooms, and water chestnuts.
3. Have a hot stove. Oil the frying-pan and fry the feet for 5 minutes. Add salt and the bamboo shoots, chestnuts and mushrooms. Continue to fry for 5 minutes after having mixed well.
4. Add enough primary soup or water to cover all. Cook until tender.
5. Add Chinese gravy and mix well.
