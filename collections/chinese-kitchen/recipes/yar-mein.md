## Ingredients

- three large cupfuls of noodles
- salted water or chicken stock with a little onion cut from the green tops
- good chicken stock
- one-half as much freshly cooked Chop Sooy as there are noodles
- shredded green onion

## Directions

1. Cook noodles till done in salted water or chicken stock with a little onion cut from the green tops
2. Drain in fine colander and place in five small bowls or one large one
3. Pour over a little good chicken stock already heated
4. Add one-half as much freshly cooked Chop Sooy as there are noodles over top
5. Garnish with shredded green onion
