## Ingredients

- pork
- spicery salt
- salt
- sugar
- sauce residue
- sweet sauce
- Fun Wine
- Chinese tissue-paper
- bean cakes
- gray potatoes

## Directions

1. To each pound of pork use 2 ounces of spicery salt, 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls of salt, 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls of sugar. Mix and rub over the pork. Expose to the hot sun for 1 day. Dip into 5 ounces of sauce residue. Mash. Add 1 1/2 ounces of sweet sauce, 2 ounces of good Fun Wine. Mix well.
2. Wrap up with Chinese tissue-paper, and put in a dry place where it can stay until dry. It is now ready for cooking.
3. Cut 1 pound of dry pork into pieces 1 inch by 1 inch by 1 1/2 inches.
4. Use about 2 pounds of bean cakes. Cut each cake into 4 pieces. Fry until yellow.
5. Cut about 3 pounds of gray potatoes into pieces the same size as the pork.
6. Cook the pork, bean cakes, and gray potatoes in plain water in an oiled pan for 3/4 hour.
