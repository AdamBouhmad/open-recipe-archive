## Ingredients

- 1 chicken cut into fragments
- 3/4 cups water chestnuts
- 2 cups mushrooms
- 2 cups bamboo shoots
- 2 cups celery cut into thin pieces

## Directions

1. Put the water chestnuts, mushrooms, bamboo shoots, and celery into a hot pan containing oil. Fry for 2 minutes. Add water and cook for 15 minutes.
2. Put the chicken in a frying-pan containing oil and fry for 5 minutes.
3. Add the secondary vegetables and mix well.
4. Add enough primary soup to cover, and cook for 5 minutes.
5. Add Chinese gravy and stir. Remove from the stove at once.
