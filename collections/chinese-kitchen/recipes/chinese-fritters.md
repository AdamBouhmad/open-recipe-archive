## Ingredients

- two cups of rice flour
- one-half cup sugar
- hot water

## Directions

1. Scald the rice flour and sugar together with hot water
2. Mix like bread, kneading lightly
3. Roll into little balls
4. Cook in deep fat as you would ordinary fritters or doughnuts
