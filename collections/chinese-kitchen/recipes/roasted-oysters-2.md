## Ingredients

- 2½ dozen oysters
- 1 cup red vinegar
- Sesamum-seed oil
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Dry the oysters with a clean, dry cloth. Then dip in oil.
2. String the oysters through a wire and roast until done over a charcoal fire. Have them about 1 foot above the fire. Serve with a sauce made of the red vinegar, sesamum-seed oil, sugar, and salt.
