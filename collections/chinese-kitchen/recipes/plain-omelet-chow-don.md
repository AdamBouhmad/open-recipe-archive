## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- A little oil
- Salt
- Chinese ham dice
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Break the eggs in a bowl. Add oil and salt. Beat well.
2. Put plenty of oil in a hot frying-pan. Be sure that every part of the pan is covered with oil.
3. Have a medium fire. Place the eggs in the pan and keep turning with a cooking-shovel.
4. When the omelet is done add the Chinese ham dice and parsley for garnish.
