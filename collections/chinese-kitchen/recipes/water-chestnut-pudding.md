## Ingredients

- 5 cups water chestnut powder
- 1 cup sugar
- 6 cups water

## Directions

1. Dissolve the water chestnut powder in a little cold water. Mash well.
2. Now add the sugar and the 6 cups of water. Stir well.
3. Put into a suitable pan. Steam until done (about 1 hour).
