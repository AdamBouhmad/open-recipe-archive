## Ingredients

- noodles
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Do not allow the noodles to become too dry after making
2. If too much time is needed, place noodles in ice box till ready
3. Put olive oil in pan having the oil about one inch deep when heated
4. Spread the noodles over bottom of pan and let fry slowly till a golden color
5. Turn over with wide bladed knife or pancake turner and let fry on the other side till done, perhaps ten to fifteen minutes
6. Remove noodles and drain carefully to remove oil
7. Put on platter and serve with any one of the Chop Sooy dishes preferred
8. The Chop Sooy must be ready and hot, and the noodles served immediately when done
