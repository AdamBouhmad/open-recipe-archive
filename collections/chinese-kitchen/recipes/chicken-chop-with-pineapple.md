## Ingredients

- one-half pound young pig pork very fine cut
- peanut oil
- one small teaspoon shredded onion
- one pound cooked white meat of chicken cut very fine
- one-half cup Chinese potatoes
- one-half cup centers of celery stalks cut very fine
- one cupful weak chicken stock
- one teaspoon rice flour
- one dessertspoon Seasoning Sauce
- one-half cup imported Chinese Pineapple cut in thin strips

## Directions

1. Use one-half pound young pig pork, very fine cut, and browned in kettle with peanut oil
2. Add one small teaspoon shredded onion and one pound cooked white meat of chicken cut very fine
3. Add one-half cup Chinese potatoes, one-half cup centers of celery stalks, cut very fine, both
4. Pour around one cupful weak chicken stock and let all cook for twelve minutes
5. When done thicken with teaspoon rice flour and dessertspoon Seasoning Sauce
6. Just before removing from fire add one-half cup imported Chinese Pineapple cut in thin strips
7. Serve with small bowls of rice and tea
