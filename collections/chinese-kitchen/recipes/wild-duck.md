## Ingredients

- 1 duck
- 2 cups chestnuts without skins
- 2 cups water chestnuts
- 1 cup chopped pork
- 1 cup barley
- 3/4 cup white nuts without skins
- Chinese sauce
- salt
- Fun Wine

## Directions

1. Soak the chestnuts and the barley in cold water for 1 hour.
2. Open the back of the duck with one cut about 4 inches long. Take out the bones without tearing the meat.
3. Wash the duck inside and out with Chinese sauce. Salt thoroughly and put into an oiled pan and fry for 10 minutes. Add the Fun Wine. Turn the duck frequently. Take from the stove and put into a bowl a little larger than the duck.
4. Fill the duck with the chestnuts, barley, nuts and any juice left in the frying-pan. Steam for 2 1/4 hours.
5. Get a bowl larger than the one holding the duck and place that on top of the other one, mouth to mouth. Use a cloth to protect the hand from getting burned. Carefully pour the gravy off into a third bowl and take off any oil. Turn the duck upside down so it will fall into the new bowl.
6. Pour the gravy over the duck and serve.
