## Ingredients

- one-half pound pork with little fat
- one tablespoon olive oil
- one pound lean veal cut in small dice
- one cup Chinese potatoes cut fine
- one-half cup bamboo shoots
- one-half cup Chinese mushrooms cut in fine pieces
- one cup weak chicken stock
- one tablespoon each kind of sauce
- one cupful bean sprouts
- one dessertspoon wheat or rice flour

## Directions

1. Cut one-half pound pork with little fat, not too much, fine and brown in kettle with one tablespoon olive oil
2. Brown for five minutes, then add one pound lean veal cut in small dice and brown all together till veal is done
3. Cover kettle tightly, taking off cover two or three times to stir meat
4. When done, have ready one cup Chinese potatoes cut fine, one-half cup bamboo shoots, one-half cup Chinese mushrooms all cut in fine pieces
5. Pour around one cup weak chicken stock, add the vegetables and cook for twelve minutes
6. Put in one tablespoon each kind of sauce, and one cupful bean sprouts
7. Cook for six minutes longer, thicken with one dessertspoon wheat or rice flour and serve with rice
