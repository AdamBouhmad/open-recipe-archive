## Ingredients

- 3 cups Chinese mushrooms chopped into small dice
- 3/4 cup chicken chopped to same size
- 9 cups primary soup
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 teaspoonful cornstarch
- 1 teaspoonful Chinese sauce
- 3/4 teaspoonful oil
- Salt and a few drops of sesamum-seed oil

## Directions

1. Cook the mushrooms for 3/4 hour, and then drain off and put into the primary soup
2. Boil for 15 minutes
3. Add the chopped chicken, the eggs well beaten, and all the other ingredients
4. Take off the stove 1/2 minute after the cornstarch is added
5. Serve in bowls
6. Garnish the top of each bowl with 1 teaspoonful of Chinese ham cut into dice
