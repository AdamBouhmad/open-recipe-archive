## Ingredients

- 2 cups lean pork cut into fragments
- 2 teaspoonfuls cornstarch
- 2 teaspoonfuls Chinese sauce
- 5 cups primary soup
- 3 cups Chinese mushrooms
- A few drops sesamum-seed oil
- 3 cups star melon cut into small pieces after the rind is removed
- Salt

## Directions

1. Mix the pork, cornstarch, Chinese sauce, and salt
2. Bring the primary soup to a boil
3. Then add the pork mixture and the mushrooms, and cook for 1/2 hour
4. Ten minutes before taking soup off stove, add the melon
