## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 1 cup dried mushrooms
- 1 1/2 pounds chicken
- A few Chinese onions
- A piece of ginger root

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs until hard. Take off the shell without destroying the shape of the eggs. Now fry them in boiling oil.
2. Cut the mushrooms, chicken, bamboo shoots, and ginger root into threads 1 1/2 inches long. Fry for 5 minutes in a hot, oiled pan.
3. Pour this over the eggs. Add twice more than enough primary soup to cover and cook until 2 cups of liquid remain.
4. Add Chinese gravy.
5. Serve with parsley as a garnish.
