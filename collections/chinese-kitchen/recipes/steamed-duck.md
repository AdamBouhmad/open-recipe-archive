## Ingredients

- 1 duck (about 8 pounds)
- 2 cups chestnuts without skins
- 1 cup barley
- 1/2 cup white nuts without skins
- 1 tablespoonful Fun Wine
- cold water
- Chinese sauce
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Soak the chestnuts and barley in cold water for 1 hour.
2. Open the back of the duck with one cut about 4 inches long. Take out the bones without tearing the meat.
3. When the duck has been cleaned wash it inside and out with Chinese sauce. Salt thoroughly and put into a hot, oiled pan and fry for 10 minutes.
4. Add the Fun Wine. Turn the duck frequently.
5. Now put the duck, with the open side upward, in a bowl a little larger than the duck. Fill with the chestnuts, barley, and white nuts and any juice left in the frying-pan.
6. Steam for 2½ hours.
7. Pour off the gravy into another bowl. Skim off the oil.
8. Transfer the duck carefully into a larger bowl by holding the bowls mouth to mouth.
9. Pour the gravy over the duck and serve.
