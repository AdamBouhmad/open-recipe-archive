## Ingredients

- 1 3/8 pounds lamb
- 25 threads skinless ginger root
- 2 cups bamboo shoots
- 2 cups dry mushrooms
- 1 cup fried noodles
- Chinese gravy
- primary soup

## Directions

1. Cut all the ingredients except the noodles into threads 1 1/2 inches long.
2. Fry the ginger root, bamboo shoots, and mushrooms in a hot, oiled pan for 10 minutes.
3. Add the lamb. Mix well. Cook for 5 more minutes.
4. Add one bowl of primary soup and cook until 1 cup of liquid is left.
5. Add Chinese gravy.
6. Use the fried noodles for a garnish. Serve when hot.
