## Ingredients

- 1 chicken cut into threads
- 2 cups bamboo shoots
- 2 cups Chinese mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cook the bamboo shoots and mushrooms for 20 minutes. Then mix with the chicken.
2. Add enough primary soup to cover and cook for 5 minutes.
3. Add Chinese gravy and remove from the stove at once.
