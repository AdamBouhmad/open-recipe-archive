## Ingredients

- 6 young pigeons or squabs
- Chinese sauce
- boiling oil
- spicery salt

## Directions

1. Clean 6 young pigeons, or squabs, and put them into a bowl of Chinese sauce.
2. Fry in boiling oil until yellow.
3. Serve when hot with spicery salt.
