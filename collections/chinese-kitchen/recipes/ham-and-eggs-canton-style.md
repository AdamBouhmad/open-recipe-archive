## Ingredients

- lean ham
- three eggs
- Chinese cured pork
- one tablespoonful Seasoning sauce
- one teaspoonful cornstarch
- one tablespoonful hot water

## Directions

1. Cut lean ham into strips about two inches long and one-quarter inch thick.
2. Beat up three eggs very light, add one cupful strips of ham.
3. Fry in small cakes like pancakes and serve on oblong platter.
4. Garnish with strips of Chinese cured pork.
5. Make a sauce of one tablespoonful Seasoning sauce, mixed with one teaspoonful cornstarch and one tablespoonful hot water.
6. Pour into pan and heat till thickened, and put around sides of platter.
