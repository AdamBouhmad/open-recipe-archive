## Ingredients

- 2 pounds shrimp
- 2 pounds green peppers
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful cornstarch
- salt
- primary soup
- Chinese gravy
- parsley

## Directions

1. Mix up the shrimp, eggs and cornstarch. Add salt to suit the taste. Put one by one into a pan of boiling oil. Fry until brown.
2. Cut the green peppers into pieces the same size as the shrimp. Fry in an oiled pan for 2 minutes. Then add the shrimp and mix well.
3. Add enough primary soup to cover. Cook for 10 minutes.
4. Add Chinese gravy. Garnish with parsley and serve.
