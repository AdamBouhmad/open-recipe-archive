## Ingredients

- 3/4 pounds pork (uncut)
- 3/4 pounds string beans cut 2 inches long
- 3/4 cup mustard
- 1 cup vinegar
- salt to taste
- parsley and sage for garnish

## Directions

1. Boil the pork until done and place in cold water. Punch several small holes with a large needle. Gently squeeze the oil out of it. Change water until no oil is on the top.
2. Cut the pork into thin pieces 1/32 inch by 3/4 inch by 1 3/4 inches. Mix with salt to suit the taste, and with the mustard and vinegar.
3. Boil the string beans for 10 minutes. Put into a bowl, and add salt and enough vinegar to cover.
4. Cover and keep air tight for 2 hours.
5. Place the pork on top of the string beans and serve. Use parsley and sage cut into threads for a garnish.
