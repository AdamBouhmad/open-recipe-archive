## Ingredients

- Winkles
- 2 cups water chestnuts, unshelled
- 3 cups mushrooms
- 2 cups bamboo shoots
- 1 cup celery
- A few pieces of pork
- 3/8 teaspoonful ginger root juice
- 1 tablespoonful Fun Wine
- 1 cup primary soup

## Directions

1. Wash the winkles carefully. Break the shell. Take the meat out. Cut into pieces 1/16 inch by 1 1/4 inches by 1 3/16 inches.
2. Cut the secondary vegetables and the pork into pieces and fry in a hot, oiled pan. Add enough water to cover. Cover the pan and cook until done.
3. Fry the meat over a quick fire in a hot, oiled pan. Add the ginger root juice and the Fun Wine.
4. Now add the secondary vegetables and mix well.
5. Add the primary soup and Chinese gravy.
6. Serve with Chinese ham dice and parsley for garnish.
