## Ingredients

- spiced pork
- gray potatoes
- Chinese onions

## Directions

1. Cut the pork and the potatoes into pieces 1 inch by 1 inch by 1 1/2 inches.
2. Cook the pork, the potatoes and the onions in plain water in an oiled pan for 3/4 hour.
3. Add Chinese gravy.
