## Ingredients

- dry flat fish
- 1/2 teaspoonful ginger juice
- 1 tablespoonful Fun Wine
- 1 teaspoonful crab juice
- 2 cups dry mushrooms
- 1 cup celery
- 1 pound any vegetable
- a few pieces of pork
- 1 Chinese onion
- primary soup
- Chinese gravy

## Directions

1. Soak the dry flat fish for 3/4 hour. Wash thoroughly. Take off a piece of bone on the center of one side of it. On the bone side put knife-marks one-half as deep as it is thick. Then cut into 2 inches by 1 1/4 inches.
2. Put into the cut flat fish 1/2 teaspoonful of ginger juice, 1 tablespoonful of Fun Wine, 1 teaspoonful of crab juice.
3. Soak 2 cups of dry mushrooms for 3/4 hour.
4. Cut 1 cup of celery, 1 pound of any vegetable, a few pieces of pork, and a Chinese onion into pieces 1 1/2 inches long.
5. Cook the mushrooms, celery, vegetables, and pork in an oiled pan with primary soup until done.
6. Put the fish into a very hot, oiled pan. Keep turning until it is rolled up like a piece of cigarette. Add the other ingredients and mix well.
7. Add Chinese gravy.
