## Ingredients

- 2 cups of bird-nest
- 1 piece of ginger
- 6 pints of primary soup
- 6 teaspoonfuls of Chinese ham and chicken dice
- 3 teaspoonfuls of cornstarch
- A few drops of sesamum-seed oil
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Soak in cold water for one hour 2 cups of bird-nest, then wash gently
2. Cook in water for 1 hour, with a piece of ginger
3. Strain off and put into 6 pints of primary soup
4. Let simmer for 1/2 hour
5. Serve in bowls or soup-plates, and use 6 teaspoonfuls of Chinese ham and chicken dice for garnish
6. For gravy use 3 teaspoonfuls of cornstarch, a few drops of sesamum-seed oil, and salt and pepper to taste
