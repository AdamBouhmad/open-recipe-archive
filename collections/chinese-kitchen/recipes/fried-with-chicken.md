## Ingredients

- one cup fine cut white meat of cooked chicken
- one small cup inside stalk celery, minced very fine
- one teaspoon finely shredded onion
- two Chinese potatoes peeled and finely cut
- two eggs beaten light
- two tablespoons olive oil
- rice flour
- Chinese sauces

## Directions

1. Spread the beaten eggs over the bottom of a flat pan and cook not too much
2. Cut the cooked eggs in small strips
3. Mix the eggs with the chicken, celery, onion, and potatoes
4. Heat two tablespoons olive oil in a pan until very hot
5. Spread all the ingredients evenly in the pan and fry slowly till light brown
6. Turn with a broad knife and fry on the other side
7. Make sauce by mixing the two sauces and thickening with a little rice flour
8. Serve on platter with sauce
