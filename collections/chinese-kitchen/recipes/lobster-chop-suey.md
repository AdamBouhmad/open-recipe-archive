## Ingredients

- 1 lobster (about 6 pounds)
- 9 cups bamboo shoots
- 2 cups water chestnuts
- 3 cups celery
- A few pieces of Chinese onion
- 1 tablespoonful Fun Wine
- 1 teaspoonful ginger root juice
- water

## Directions

1. Boil the lobster in water until it turns red. Take off the shell and cut the meat into fragments not longer than 1½ inches.
2. Fry the lobster with the Fun Wine, and ginger root juice for 5 minutes over a hot fire.
3. Cut the bamboo shoots, water chestnuts, celery, and onion into pieces the size of the lobster fragments. Fry these in a hot, oiled pan for 3 minutes. Add water enough to cover and cook for 10 minutes. Then mix with the lobster.
