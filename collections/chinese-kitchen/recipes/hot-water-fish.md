## Ingredients

- 10 pounds cod
- 1 can Chinese Chow Chow
- 1 cup vinegar
- 2 teaspoonfuls salt

## Directions

1. Clean the cod but do not cut it.
2. Pour into a bowl, boiling water equal to 5 times the weight of the fish. Add the salt. Place the fish in this and keep covered until the water gets lukewarm.
3. Put the fish on a suitable plate and pour over it the gravy made of the Chow Chow, vinegar, and a little sugar, cornstarch, and salt. Garnish with parsley and serve.
