## Ingredients

- one pound lean leg of lamb cut in cubes or small dice
- one dessertspoon peanut oil
- one small cup fine cut French mushrooms
- one cup celery cut fine
- one cup Spanish onions sliced thin
- one cup hot water
- one tablespoon Seasoning Sauce
- one teaspoon rice flour or cornstarch
- one teaspoon Flavoring Sauce
- a few drops Sesamun oil

## Directions

1. Cut one pound lean leg of lamb in cubes or small dice
2. Brown and cook carefully on one dessertspoon peanut oil
3. When done mix with lamb in kettle one small cup fine cut French mushrooms, one cup celery cut fine, one cup Spanish onions sliced thin
4. Add carefully one cup hot water, cover and let cook fifteen minutes
5. Use one tablespoon Seasoning Sauce with one teaspoon rice flour or cornstarch for thickening
6. When done, add one teaspoon Flavoring Sauce and a few drops Sesamun oil
