## Ingredients

- pork, preferably from the shoulder of the pig
- Seasoning Sauce
- Flavoring Sauce
- good brandy

## Directions

1. Remove the fat from the pork, cut preferably from the shoulder of the pig, and cut into strips.
2. Rub into the pork the Seasoning Sauce, and a little of the Flavoring Sauce.
3. Rub over it some good brandy and place in the oven.
4. Mix a small cup of the two sauces, add a little brandy, and baste frequently till done.
5. This cured pork is used for a garnish for many of the dishes, and is sliced in thin strips about two inches long.
