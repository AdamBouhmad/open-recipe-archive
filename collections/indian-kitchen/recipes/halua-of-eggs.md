## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- sugar
- ghee
- a little powdered garam-masala
- very little saffron

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and ghee in equal quantities, each being double of the quantity of eggs
2. Add a little powdered garam-masala and very little saffron
3. Heat the mass stirring constantly till it begins to form red crystals
4. Take it down and serve
