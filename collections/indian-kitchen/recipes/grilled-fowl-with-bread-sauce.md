## Ingredients

- fowl
- butter
- water-cress

## Directions

1. Bone your fowl, and make a nice strong broth with the bones
2. Flatten out the fowl like a steak, butter it and grill it smartly over a bright fire
3. Serve with bread sauce and fried potatoes a la Lyonnaise
4. Garnish with water-cress
