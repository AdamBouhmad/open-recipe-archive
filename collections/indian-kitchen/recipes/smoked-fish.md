## Ingredients

- mango fish, becky, or hilsa
- salt
- bran
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Cut down the back, spread open, and well wash and salt the fish
2. Have a bright charcoal fire, and sprinkle over it some bran, with brown sugar
3. Cover the fire with an open-work bamboo basket, having over it a coarse duster
4. Arrange the fish over the duster, and allow them to smoke
5. When one side has browned, turn and brown the other side
6. As the smoke decreases, add more bran, and fan up the fire
7. A duster thrown over the fish while smoking will facilitate the operation
