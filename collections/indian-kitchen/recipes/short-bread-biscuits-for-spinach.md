## Ingredients

- three ounces of flour
- two ounces of butter liquefied
- a tea-spoonful of salt
- a salt-spoonful of sugar
- an ounce of grated cheese

## Directions

1. Put three ounces of flour into a bowl and mix into it two ounces of butter liquefied, a tea-spoonful of salt, a salt-spoonful of sugar and an ounce of grated cheese
2. Roll out the paste
3. Cut it into heart-shapes one-third of an inch thick
4. Bake
