## Ingredients

- half a head of ox-cheek
- boiled carrots
- boiled turnips

## Directions

1. Wash half a head very clean
2. Let it lie in cold water for some hours
3. Break the bone in two, taking care not to break the flesh
4. Put it into a pot of boiling water, and let it boil from two to three hours
5. Take out the bone
6. Serve it with boiled carrots and turnips
