## Ingredients

- moog flour 1 seer
- fine flour 1 powa
- sugar 1 powa
- middlings 1 powa
- scraped and pasted cocoa-nut kernel 1 powa
- entire black sesames 1 chhatak
- powdered garam-masalla 4 tola
- a half pinch of camphor

## Directions

1. Mix together all ingredients
2. Make a stiff paste with hot water
3. Make balls of the paste
4. Fry in ghee or oil
5. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar for 12 to 24 hours
