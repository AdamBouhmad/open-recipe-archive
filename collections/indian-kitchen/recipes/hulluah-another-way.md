## Ingredients

- half a seer of soojee
- half a seer of ghee
- half a seer of sugar
- half a seer of almonds
- half a seer of raisins
- a few white cardamoms
- sticks of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of the sugar and set it aside
2. Roast or brown the soojee and set it aside
3. Melt the ghee and fry the soojee with the spices in it
4. Put in the almonds and raisins, stirring all the time
5. Last of all add the syrup and continue to cook and stir it until it thickens
6. Remove into moulds or shapes while hot
