## Ingredients

- white Patna onion
- fresh green chilies
- fresh lime juice
- two fine young brinjals of large size
- salt
- mustard oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the sliced onions, chilies, and lime-juice in the manner directed for potato burta
2. Carefully and thoroughly roast the brinjals in a quick ash fire
3. Remove the ashes and burnt parts of the skin if any
4. Open the brinjals and with a clean spoon remove the contents to as near the skin as possible
5. Add a good teaspoonful of salt and a teaspoonful of mustard oil to the brinjal contents
6. Work these with a spoon to a perfect pulp, throwing away the lumps or shreds if any
7. Mix with it all the onions, chilies, and lime-juice
8. If not to your taste, add more salt or lime-juice according to fancy
