## Ingredients

- ounce of butter
- garlic
- onion the size of a racquet ball
- two breakfast-cupfuls of well boiled rice
- couple of heaped up table-spoonfuls of grated Parmesan or Gruyere
- strips of anchovies

## Directions

1. Melt an ounce of butter at the bottom of a sauce-pan which ought to have been previously well rubbed with a piece of garlic.
2. Shred an onion the size of a racquet ball very finely and fry it in the butter.
3. Stir into this, when of a golden yellow colour, two breakfast-cupfuls of well boiled rice.
4. Work it vigorously with a wooden spoon while an assistant shakes into the pan a couple of heaped up table-spoonfuls of grated Parmesan or Gruyere.
5. Garnish the dish with strips of anchovies, and serve it piled upon a flat dish.
