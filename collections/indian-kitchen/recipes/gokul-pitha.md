## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of khowa kheer
- 1 or 1 1/2 powa of sabeda
- hot milk (if necessary)
- flour 1 powa
- sugar 1/2 powa
- milk about 3 chhataks
- ghee for frying
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Make stiff dough of 1/2 seer of khowa kheer and 1 or 1 1/2 powa of sabeda with a little hot milk if necessary
2. Make small balls of it, press them flat and round like luchi
3. Dip them into a thick paste of flour 1 powa sugar 1/2 powa and milk about 3 chhataks
4. Fry in plenty of ghee
5. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar
