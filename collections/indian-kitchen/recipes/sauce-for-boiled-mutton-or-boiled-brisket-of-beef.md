## Ingredients

- two chittacks or four ounces of butter
- a tablespoonful of finely-sliced onions
- two tablespoonfuls of finely-sifted flour
- eight chittacks or sixteen ounces of pure milk
- two wineglasses of vinegar
- finely-pounded white pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Warm a saucepan and melt in it two chittacks or four ounces of butter free of water
2. Fry in it a tablespoonful of finely-sliced onions
3. When half browned, put in gradually two tablespoonfuls of finely-sifted flour, taking care to keep stirring it the whole time
4. Add gradually eight chittacks or sixteen ounces of pure milk
5. Add lastly two wineglasses of vinegar
6. Season with finely-pounded white pepper and salt to taste
