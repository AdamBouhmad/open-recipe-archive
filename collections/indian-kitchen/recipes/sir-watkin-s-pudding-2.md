## Ingredients

- two ounces of chopped candied peel
- four ounces of suet finely minced
- four ounces fine white crumbs
- one table-spoonful of flour
- three ounces of sugar
- two ounces of apricot jam
- a liqueur-glass of curacao
- one dessert-spoonful of milk
- four fresh eggs
- butter
- half a pot of apricot jam
- half a pint of water
- a glass of brown sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Mix together in a bowl two ounces of chopped candied peel, four ounces of suet finely minced, four ounces fine white crumbs, one table-spoonful of flour, three ounces of sugar, two ounces of apricot jam, a liqueur-glass of curacao, one dessert-spoonful of milk, and four fresh eggs
2. When thoroughly mixed, put the ingredients into a buttered mould, and steam for three hours
3. To make apricot sauce: Put half a pot of apricot jam into a sauce-pan with half a pint of water, and a glass of brown sherry or Madeira
4. Boil together, stirring well, then strain, and serve with the pudding
