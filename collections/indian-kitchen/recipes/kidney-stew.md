## Ingredients

- a dozen mutton kidneys
- pepper
- salt
- juice of onions
- ginger
- garlic
- three dessertspoonfuls of ghee or lard
- half a clove of garlic
- eight large Patna onions
- hot stock

## Directions

1. Steep in lukewarm water for a few minutes a dozen mutton kidneys, and remove the white skin or coat which will become perceptible
2. Cut into halves or quarter them, wash in three or four waters, and allow them to remain as long as possible in pepper, salt, and the juice of onions, ginger, and garlic
3. Boil up three dessertspoonfuls of ghee or lard in a deep frying-pan, throw in the kidneys with the juice, put in half a clove of garlic, and cover over the whole with eight large Patna onions sliced each into eight slices, and separated so as to cover over the whole surface of the pan
4. Pour over it as much hot stock as will keep all the onions under, and simmer over a slow fire until the onions disappear
5. Serve up quite hot
