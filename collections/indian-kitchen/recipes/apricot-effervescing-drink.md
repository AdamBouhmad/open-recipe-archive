## Ingredients

- one pint juice of bruised apricots
- half a pound sugar
- one ounce tartaric acid
- water
- two tablespoonfuls syrup
- one scruple carbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Filter until clear the juice of bruised apricots
2. Make into a syrup with half a pound of sugar
3. Add one ounce of tartaric acid
4. Bottle and cork well
5. To a tumbler three parts full of water add two tablespoonfuls of the syrup and a scruple of carbonate of soda
6. Stir well and drink while effervescing
