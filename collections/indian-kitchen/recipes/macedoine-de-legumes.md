## Ingredients

- equal portions of boiled carrot
- French beans
- turnip or knolkhol
- a few peas
- asparagus points
- haricot beans
- sauce blonde

## Directions

1. Cut equal portions of boiled carrot, French beans, turnip or knolkhol, peas, asparagus points, and haricot beans into small dice
2. Gently heat in sauce blonde
3. Be careful not to mash the vegetables
