## Ingredients

- patols
- egg contents
- powdered turmeric
- ginger juice
- salt
- powdered bread or biscuits
- ghee
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Dip skinned patols into liquid egg contents beaten with powdered turmeric, ginger juice and salt
2. Roll on powdered bread or biscuits
3. Fry in ghee
4. Thin slices of peeled potatoes may be fried in the same way
