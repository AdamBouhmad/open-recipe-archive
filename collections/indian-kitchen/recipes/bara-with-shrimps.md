## Ingredients

- shrimps
- rice or husked grams
- water
- powdered chillies
- salt
- ghee or oil

## Directions

1. Scale the shrimps and shred off the flesh portions
2. Grind them to a paste with some rice or husked grams left moistened for several hours
3. Add powdered chillies and salt, mix thoroughly
4. Form cakes, and fry in ghee or oil
