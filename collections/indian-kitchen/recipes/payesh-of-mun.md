## Ingredients

- mun (arum)
- the juice of a lemon
- 1 seer of mun
- 1/2 powa of ghee
- a few cassia leaves, cloves and cardamoms
- 1 1/2 seer of milk
- 2 1/2 powas of sugar
- raisins, almonds and pistachios
- very little camphor

## Directions

1. Peel thickly a non-irritating mun (arum) and cut in very small and very thin pieces.
2. Boil 1 seer of them in water mixed with the juice of a lemon.
3. Then drain out the water and wash again with fresh water.
4. Fry them with 1/2 powa of ghee heated with a few cassia leaves, cloves and cardamoms, all entire.
5. Then add 1 1/2 seer of milk.
6. Stir constantly.
7. Add sugar 2 1/2 powas, raisins, almonds and pistachios as desired when the milk is reduced to 1 seer.
8. Take down when thick and mix with very little camphor.
