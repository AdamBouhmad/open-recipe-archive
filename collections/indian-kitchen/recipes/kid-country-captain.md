## Ingredients

- 4 ounces ghee
- half a teaspoonful ground chilies
- one teaspoonful salt
- a quarter of a teaspoonful ground turmeric
- twenty onions
- half a cup of water

## Directions

1. Before cutting up the kid, a four-quarter, let it be partially broiled or roasted, and then make it into country captain in accordance with the above directions.
2. Or, instead of partially roasting the kid, add half a cup of water to assist the meat to dissolve.
