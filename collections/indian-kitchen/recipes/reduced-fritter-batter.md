## Ingredients

- two ordinary eggs
- one table-spoonful of brandy
- a dessert-spoonful of salad oil
- two or three table-spoonfuls of water
- one and a half table-spoonful of flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat up two ordinary eggs with one table-spoonful of brandy, a dessert-spoonful of salad oil, and two or three table-spoonfuls of water.
2. Incorporate with this mixture one and a half table-spoonful of flour.
3. Work to a smooth paste and beat for at least ten minutes.
4. Add water if needed until consistency is satisfactory.
5. Whip the egg whites to a stiff froth and stir in at the time of using.
6. For sweet fritters, use sugar instead of salt.
