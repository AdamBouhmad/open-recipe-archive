## Ingredients

- fish
- powdered chillies
- cumin
- pepper
- coriander
- turmeric
- salt
- fried pieces of onions

## Directions

1. Slightly brown pieces of any kind of fish and add water covering them, with powdered chillies, cumin, pepper, coriander and turmeric.
2. Add a little more salt and fried pieces of onions if desired.
3. The soup must be thick and scanty.
