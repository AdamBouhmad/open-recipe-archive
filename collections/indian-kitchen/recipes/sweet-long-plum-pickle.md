## Ingredients

- long plums
- salt
- fresh chilies
- cloves of garlic
- ginger finely sliced
- peppercorns
- vinegar
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make according to the Long Plum Pickle recipe
2. Add a syrup in the proportion of a quarter of a pound of sugar to every quart of vinegar
3. Add a few sticks of cinnamon
