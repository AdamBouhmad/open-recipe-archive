## Ingredients

- Half a pound of flour
- a dessert-spoonful of butter
- one small egg
- two tea-spoonfuls of Yeatman's powder
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- four table-spoonfuls of milk

## Directions

1. Work the butter thoroughly into the flour
2. Beat the egg up briskly with the milk, and strain it into another cup
3. Dust the salt over the flour
4. Gradually add the eggy-milk till the dough is formed
5. Mix the baking powder into it thoroughly
6. Form the dough into two nice oblong rolls
7. Place them on a sheet of well buttered paper, on the baking tin
8. Set them in the oven
9. Look at them after twenty minutes' baking
10. Take them out as soon as their colour indicates that they are done
