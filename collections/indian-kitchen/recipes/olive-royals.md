## Ingredients

- one pound of potatoes
- four ounces of flour
- one ounce of butter
- cold roasted meat
- pepper
- salt
- fresh dripping

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of potatoes, and when nearly cold rub them perfectly smooth with four ounces of flour and one ounce of butter
2. Knead all together till it becomes a paste
3. Roll it out about a quarter of an inch thick
4. Cut it into rounds, and lay upon one side any sort of cold roasted meat cut into thin small bits, and seasoned with pepper and salt
5. Put a small bit of butter over the meat
6. Wet the edges of the paste, and close it in the form of half-circles
7. Fry them in boiling fresh dripping till of a light brown colour
8. Lay them before the fire, on the back of a sieve, to drain
9. Serve them with or without gravy in the dish
