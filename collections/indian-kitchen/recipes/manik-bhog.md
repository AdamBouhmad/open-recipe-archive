## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 powas of khowa kheer
- 1 1/2 powas of sugar
- pastes of almonds and pistachios
- a drop or two of essence of rose
- 5 or 6 raisins
- 5 or 6 cardamom seeds
- 8 or 10 drops of rose water
- ghee
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1 1/2 powas of khowa kheer and drop it into a boiling syrup of 1 1/2 powas of sugar
2. Stir constantly on mild heat
3. On boiling again add pastes of almonds and pistachios as desired and a drop or two of essence of rose
4. Take down when the mass is a very stiff paste
5. Scrape it together
6. When cool make balls of it stuffed with 5 or 6 raisins, 5 or 6 cardamom seeds and 8 or 10 drops of rose water after smearing the hands with ghee
7. Roll them on sugar and serve
