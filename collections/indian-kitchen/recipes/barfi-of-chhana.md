## Ingredients

- 1 seer of sugar
- 1 seer of paste of hard chhana
- 1 tola each of pieces of almonds and pistachios
- a pinch of powdered cardamoms

## Directions

1. Heat a syrup of 1 seer of sugar
2. When warm add 1 seer of a paste of hard chhana
3. Stir constantly and vigorously
4. Add pieces of almonds and pistachios 1 tola each and a pinch of powdered cardamoms when thick
5. Take the pan off fire when the mass is very thick
6. Stir as before
7. Transfer the mass in a wooden plate when a little cooled
8. Cut in cubes when solid
