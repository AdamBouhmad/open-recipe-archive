## Ingredients

- mango or apple
- minced onion
- green chilli
- vinegar
- sugar
- salad oil
- salt
- black pepper
- a tea-spoonful of chopped green ginger

## Directions

1. Make like cucumber chutney with the addition of a tea-spoonful of chopped green ginger.
