## Ingredients

- tender pumpkin
- pumpkin shoots and leaves
- mustard oil
- red chillies
- poppy seed 1 powa or more
- ginger 2.5 tolas or more
- salt
- browned broken bori
- water

## Directions

1. Peel and chop a very tender pumpkin into fine pieces.
2. Boil them and drain off the water.
3. Boil some mustard oil with a few red chillies and pour in the boiled mass with 1 powa or more of poppy seed and 2.5 tolas or more of ginger, both pasted.
4. Add a little water if necessary.
5. When it boils add salt and browned broken bori.
6. Cooking finishes when the soup dries up.
