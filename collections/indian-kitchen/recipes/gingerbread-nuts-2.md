## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- a pound of sugar
- three pounds and a half of treacle
- half an ounce of caraway-seeds
- half an ounce of allspice
- two ounces of butter
- half an ounce of candied lemon-peel
- three ounces of ground ginger
- half an ounce of coriander
- the yolks of three eggs
- a wineglassful of brandy

## Directions

1. Work the butter to a cream, then the eggs, spice, and brandy, then flour, sugar, and then hot treacle
2. If not stiff enough, a little more flour must be added in rolling out, but the less the better
