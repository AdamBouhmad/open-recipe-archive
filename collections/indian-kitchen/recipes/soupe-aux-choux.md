## Ingredients

- a cabbage or two
- a good sized slice of bacon
- some slices of a Bologna sausage
- sweet herbs
- a clove of garlic
- pepper
- a little spice
- water
- croutons of fried bread

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage into quarters
2. put them into a sauce-pan with a good sized slice of bacon
3. add some slices of a Bologna sausage
4. add a bag containing sweet herbs, a clove of garlic, pepper, and a little spice
5. add water enough to cover the whole
6. let the soup simmer till the cabbage is done
7. serve with croutons of fried bread
