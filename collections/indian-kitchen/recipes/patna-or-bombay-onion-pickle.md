## Ingredients

- small or button onions
- vinegar
- fresh red chilies
- black peppercorns
- finely-sliced green ginger
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the outer coat from the onions, wash and dry them thoroughly
2. Throw them into a pan with some vinegar, and parboil them
3. Set the vinegar aside, after filtering it, for shrimp, cucumber, and other salads, or for the preparation of mustard
4. Put the parboiled onions when cold into wide-mouthed bottles, laying them alternately with fresh red chilies, a few black peppercorns, some finely-sliced green ginger, and a little salt
5. Fill the bottles with vinegar, and cork them
