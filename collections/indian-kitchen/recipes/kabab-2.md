## Ingredients

- salt
- ginger paste
- onion paste
- garlic paste
- chilli paste
- minced mutton
- ghee
- besam of grams

## Directions

1. Mix salt, pastes of ginger, onions, garlic and chillies with minced mutton and keep aside for some time.
2. Mix it thoroughly with a little ghee and besam of grams to a stiff mass.
3. Form balls of it, attach them at the end of a pointed iron stick and roast on charcoal flame till brown dropping ghee on them at intervals.
