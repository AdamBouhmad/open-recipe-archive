## Ingredients

- potatoes
- beef dripping or frying medium

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes well and peel them
2. Slice them carefully to a uniform thickness about half that of a rupee
3. Spread them on a clean cloth to get rid of the moisture
4. Wipe them thoroughly and spread a sheet of blotting paper ready for draining
5. Dissolve a goodly allowance of beef dripping in your friture-pan or a shallow stew-pan
6. When quite boiling, drop in your potato slices with enough fat to completely cover them
7. Watch them as they are cooking, turning and moving them about continually
8. As soon as they assume a golden tint, a nice rich yellow, lift them quickly from the fat
9. Let them drain on the blotting paper for a minute or two
10. When quite dry, turn them into a very hot silver dish and serve
