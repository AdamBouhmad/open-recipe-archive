## Ingredients

- one pint of chicken broth
- one ounce of butter
- one ounce of flour
- pepper and salt to taste
- the yolks of three eggs
- a pat of butter
- a couple of table-spoonfuls of chopped mushrooms

## Directions

1. Make an ordinary thin sauce blonde with one pint of chicken broth, one ounce of butter, one ounce of flour, pepper and salt to taste
2. Stir well for a quarter of an hour, and it will be a thin white sauce
3. Add en bain-marie one by one the strained and well beaten yolks of three eggs
4. Finish off with a pat of butter, and a couple of table-spoonfuls of chopped mushrooms
