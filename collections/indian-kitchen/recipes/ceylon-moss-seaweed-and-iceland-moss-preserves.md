## Ingredients

- moss or weed
- fresh water
- a wineglass of the best vinegar per seer or two pounds of the weed
- clarified sugar or syrup
- cochineal (optional)

## Directions

1. Steep the moss or weed for two or three days in fresh water, changing the water two or three times a day
2. Wash it well once before boiling it
3. To every seer or two pounds of the weed add a wineglass of the best vinegar
4. Allow it to simmer over a gentle fire until it thickens, so as to congeal in a glass
5. Strain the moss or weed through a towel
6. Pour the liquid into clarified sugar or syrup, and boil them together for half an hour
7. Pour the jelly into large wide dishes, and when quite cold cut it into cakes
8. If desired, the jelly may be coloured or tinted with cochineal
