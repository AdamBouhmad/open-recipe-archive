## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- butter
- green butter
- prawn or lobster meat
- Nepaul pepper
- lettuce
- beetroot
- mayonnaise sauce
- chopped olive or chopped capers

## Directions

1. Cut slices of bread a quarter of an inch thick, and two inches long if heart-shaped, two inches in diameter if round, and two inches square if rectangular
2. Fry these a pale golden colour in butter
3. Set them on a dish to get cold
4. Spread a layer of green butter over each piece of fried bread
5. Place a layer of prawn or lobster meat pounded with butter and slightly seasoned with Nepaul pepper
6. Smooth this with a dessert-knife
7. Place a leaf of lettuce cut from the golden heart upon the top of the prawn meat
8. Place a piece of beetroot shaped with your cutter on top
9. Pour a dessert-spoonful of rich, thickly worked, mayonnaise sauce, iced, over each canape when thus prepared and placed in the dish for serving
10. Sprinkle a little chopped olive, or chopped capers, or the two mixed over each cap of mayonnaise dressing
11. The dish should stand on ice before serving
