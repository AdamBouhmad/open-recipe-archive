## Ingredients

- twelve drops of oil of neroli
- twelve drops of oil of citron
- twelve drops of oil of bergamot
- twelve drops of oil of orange
- twelve drops of oil of rosemary
- a drachm of cardamom-seeds
- a pint of spirits of wine

## Directions

1. Put twelve drops each of oil of neroli, citron, bergamot, orange, and rosemary, and a drachm of cardamom-seeds, into a pint of spirits of wine
2. Let it stand for a week
