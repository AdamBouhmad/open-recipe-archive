## Ingredients

- 1 tola of powdered cardamoms
- 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose
- 1 seer of khowa kheer
- 1 seer of sugar
- 1/2 powa of broken pieces of sugar-candy

## Directions

1. Mix 1 tola of powdered cardamoms and 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose with 1 seer of khowa kheer.
2. Boil a syrup of 1 seer of sugar, add 1/2 powa of broken pieces of sugar-candy and the kheer, and stir till very thick.
3. Transfer it in a flat plate and cut in cubes when slightly warm.
