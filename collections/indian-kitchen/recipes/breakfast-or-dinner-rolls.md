## Ingredients

- eight ounces of flour
- one dessert-spoonful of good butter
- two tea-spoonfuls of Yeatman's powder
- one salt-spoonful of salt
- four table-spoonfuls of milk

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the flour with one of the wooden spoons after having spread the latter in the enamelled pan
2. Sprinkle the salt over it
3. Mix your dough as lightly as you can, using both wooden spoons, and shaking the milk into the flour by degrees
4. When nicely formed, add the baking powder (last thing of all mark) and stir it well into the dough
5. Divide it into four equal portions
6. Pat them into shape with the spoons
7. Place them in four patty-pans well buttered
8. Put them on the baking-sheet
9. Slip into the oven, which should have been heated to such a degree that you can hardly bear your hand inside it
10. Bake until the rolls brown very slightly, having risen into nice round forms
