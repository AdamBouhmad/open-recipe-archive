## Ingredients

- neck of veal or leg of veal
- two well-beaten eggs
- pounded mace
- nutmeg
- salt
- pepper
- finely-chopped sweet marjoram
- lemon
- thyme
- parsley
- grated bread
- clarified butter
- white sauce
- forcemeat balls
- small mushrooms
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Cut a neck of veal into cutlets, or take them off a leg.
2. Season two well-beaten eggs with pounded mace, nutmeg, salt, pepper, and finely-chopped sweet marjoram, lemon, thyme, and parsley.
3. Dip the cutlets into it.
4. Sift over them grated bread, and fry them in clarified butter.
5. Serve with a white sauce, forcemeat balls, and small mushrooms.
6. Garnish with fried parsley.
