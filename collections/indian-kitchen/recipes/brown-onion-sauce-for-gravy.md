## Ingredients

- one chittack or two ounces of butter, free of water
- half a dozen well-selected white Patna onions finely sliced
- half a chittack or one ounce of flour
- stock
- pepper
- salt
- a tablespoonful of port wine
- a tablespoonful of mushroom catsup
- lemon-juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Heat one chittack or two ounces of butter, free of water, in which fry to a light brown half a dozen well-selected white Patna onions finely sliced
2. Stir into it gradually half a chittack or one ounce of flour
3. Add a little stock and some pepper and salt, boil up for a few minutes
4. Strain through a sieve
5. Add a tablespoonful of port wine, and the same of mushroom catsup
6. Lemon-juice or vinegar may be added if a sharper gravy be required
