## Ingredients

- cabbage
- thick slice of bacon
- shallot
- parsley
- marjoram
- thyme
- sugar
- pepper
- salt
- gravy
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Thoroughly clean the cabbage and cut into quarters
2. Steam the quarters or plunge them into boiling hot water and boil for a quarter of an hour, then take them out and drain
3. Mince a thick slice of bacon and a little shallot, parsley, marjoram, and thyme, with a pinch of sugar, and pepper and salt to taste
4. Put all in a stew-pan and set it on the fire
5. As soon as the bacon melts, lay your cabbage quarters in it and pour round them sufficient gravy to half cover them
6. Let this simmer gently till the cabbage is done
7. Lift out the quarters and place them in a hot dish and cover them up
8. Strain the gravy, thicken it with flour and butter, and pour it over the cabbage
