## Ingredients

- clear gravy soup
- eggs
- dried tarragon
- grated Parmesan

## Directions

1. Carefully poach the eggs (one for each person), neither too lightly nor too hard
2. Trim the eggs neatly leaving as little margin of white as possible
3. Heat the soup plates and place the poached eggs in them
4. Pour the soup over the eggs
5. Flavour the consomme with a few leaves of dried tarragon
6. Serve with grated Parmesan handed round on a separate plate
