## Ingredients

- rice
- heart of a tender cabbage
- couple of ounces of butter
- Bombay onion
- two cloves of garlic
- broth or consomme
- grated cheese
- bacon

## Directions

1. Boil some rice and keep it ready in a dish.
2. Cut up the heart of a tender cabbage.
3. Melt a couple of ounces of butter in a roomy stew-pan, cast into it a finely sliced Bombay onion, and two cloves of garlic minced as small as possible.
4. Let the onion turn yellow, and then put in the shred cabbage.
5. Stir it about for three minutes with the butter and onions, and then pour over it enough broth or consomme to keep it from burning.
6. Stew gently until the cabbage is cooked, then add the rice which should be vigorously stirred about for five minutes with the cabbage.
7. Turn it out upon a flat dish, and smother it with grated cheese.
