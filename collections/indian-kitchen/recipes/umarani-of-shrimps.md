## Ingredients

- big pieces of potatoes
- minced onions
- ghee or oil
- turmeric
- cumin
- pepper
- chillies
- coriander
- salt
- water
- Mayarani pieces
- cassia leaves
- red chillies
- ghee
- powdered garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Brown some big pieces of potatoes and minced onions in ghee or oil.
2. Add turmeric, cumin, pepper, chillies coriander, all powdered, and salt and water.
3. On boiling add the fried pieces called Mayarani.
4. Season the curry after a time with ghee boiled with a few cassia leaves and red chillies.
5. Add ghee and powdered garam-masalla when the soup is thick and take down.
