## Ingredients

- two tablespoonfuls pearl barley
- one quarter pound lump sugar
- rather more than two quarts boiling water
- peel of one fresh lemon

## Directions

1. Combine pearl barley, lump sugar, boiling water, and lemon peel
2. Let it stand all night
3. Strain the next morning
