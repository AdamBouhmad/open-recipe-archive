## Ingredients

- ordinary beef consomme
- a dozen good-sized prawns
- pepper corns
- a dessert-spoonful of dried basil
- a bunch of parsley
- celery leaves
- half a glass of Madeira

## Directions

1. Prepare your ordinary beef consomme from your daily allowance of soup meat
2. Boil a dozen good-sized prawns, save the liquor in which you have cooked them, and reduce that a little
3. Throw into it the meat which you pick from the shells, and the shells pounded
4. Add the beef consomme, some pepper corns, a dessert-spoonful of dried basil tied up in a muslin bag, a bunch of parsley, and some celery leaves
5. Let this simmer gently
6. Strain after three hours' simmering
7. Clarify it, and add half a glass of Madeira
8. Quenelles of the prawn meat may be used as garnish
