## Ingredients

- eight large and perfectly sound white Patna onions
- water
- half a chittack or one ounce of butter
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in halves eight large and perfectly sound white Patna onions, and steep them in water for half an hour
2. Boil them until quite tender
3. Drain them of all water
4. Chop and bruise them fine, and put them into a saucepan, with half a chittack or one ounce of butter, half a teaspoonful of salt, and some milk
5. Put the mixture over a brisk fire, and keep stirring till it boils
6. Rub the whole through a sieve
7. Add sufficient milk to make the sauce of the consistency required
