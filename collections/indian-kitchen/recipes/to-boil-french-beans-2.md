## Ingredients

- French beans
- water
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the stalk and string them
2. If not very young, cut them in four, or into very thin slices
3. Put them into water as they are done, and put them on in boiling water, with a little salt
4. Let them boil for half an hour
5. If they are old they will require a longer time to boil
6. Melted butter in a sauce-tureen is served with them
