## Ingredients

- round of beef
- large quantities of finely-powdered pepper and hot spices
- saltpetre
- salt
- suckee
- lemon-rind
- juice of lemon
- two tablespoonfuls of finely-powdered spices and pepper
- skins of fat
- strong coarse pie-crust

## Directions

1. Corn a round of beef as per recipe No. 123 with the addition of large quantities of finely-powdered pepper and hot spices
2. Rub some of the pepper and spice well in with the saltpetre
3. Puncture the beef well the whole time with a needle to insure the saltpetre and spices penetrating
4. After the dry saltpetre and spices have been well rubbed in, prepare a mixture of salt, saltpetre, suckee, lemon-rind, pepper, and spice
5. Rub in one-fourth of the mixture, continuing to puncture the beef
6. Add subsequently to the brine the juice of lemon
7. Observe closely all the instructions given in recipe No. 123
8. On the seventh day remove the beef from the brine
9. Rub it well with two tablespoonfuls of finely-powdered spices and pepper
10. Inclose it thoroughly in skins of fat, and then in a strong coarse pie-crust
11. Bake it in a good oven, preferably a baker's oven
