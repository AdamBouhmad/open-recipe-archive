## Ingredients

- gravy from tinned beef
- fat from the gravy
- dried sweet herbs
- onion
- vegetables
- pepper-corns
- spice
- salt

## Directions

1. Skim the fat that may rise to the surface of the gravy
2. Put fat into a sauce-pan with a bag of dried sweet herbs, an onion cut into quarters, any vegetables you can spare, some pepper-corns, a pinch of spice, and salt according to the quantity
3. Simmer this gently to extract the flavour of the things you have added
4. In about a couple of hours you will have an excellent consommé, quite fit to be served as soup
