## Ingredients

- tin of oysters
- bread-crumbs
- milk or stock
- spiced pepper
- lime peel
- anchovies

## Directions

1. Drain oysters well and cut them up
2. Soak bread-crumbs in milk or stock
3. Mix oysters with bread-crumbs, spiced pepper, a little chopped very finely pared lime peel, and a couple of minced anchovies
4. Stir all together and bind with a couple of eggs
