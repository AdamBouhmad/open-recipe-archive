## Ingredients

- contents of a tin of petits pois
- a large spoonful of fresh butter
- a tea-spoonful of sifted sugar
- a tea-spoonful of salt
- a little bundle of mint leaves

## Directions

1. Wash the contents of a tin of petits pois in cold water by emptying the tin into your strainer and pouring a jug of water over them
2. Put them into a jar with a close fitting cover with a large spoonful of fresh butter, a tea-spoonful of sifted sugar, and a tea-spoonful of salt with a little bundle of mint leaves
3. Secure the top of your jar and immerse it within a large sauce-pan with water that should half cover the jar
4. Steam the peas until thoroughly hot
5. Stir in a little more butter, pick out the mint, and serve on a very hot silver dish
