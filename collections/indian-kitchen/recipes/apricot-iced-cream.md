## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of pounded loaf sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of apricot jam
- the juice of a lemon
- half an ounce of blanched bitter almonds pounded with a little rose-water
- a pint of cream

## Directions

1. Mix a tablespoonful of pounded loaf sugar with two of apricot jam, the juice of a lemon, and half an ounce of blanched bitter almonds pounded with a little rose-water
2. add a pint of cream, stir all well together before putting it into the freezing-pot, and freeze it as directed above
