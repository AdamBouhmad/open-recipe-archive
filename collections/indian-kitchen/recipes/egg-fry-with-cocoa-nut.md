## Ingredients

- ripe cocoa nut kernel
- salt
- turmeric
- red chillies
- raisins
- almonds
- moistened pulses of grams
- beaten egg contents

## Directions

1. Scrape out kernel of ripe cocoa nut
2. Mix with salt, turmeric, red chillies, raisins, almonds and moistened pulses of grams all powdered or pasted
3. Beat thoroughly
4. Form cakes
5. Dip into beaten egg contents
6. Fry
