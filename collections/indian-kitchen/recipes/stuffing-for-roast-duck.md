## Ingredients

- heart or inside of a young tender cabbage
- pepper
- salt
- 3 cloves of garlic
- 2 chittacks or 4 ounces of butter free of water

## Directions

1. Slice into fine long strips or ribbons as much of the heart or inside of a young tender cabbage as will suffice for stuffing
2. Wash and dry it through a colander, and shake it up in a napkin, without crushing or destroying the crispness of the leaves
3. Take for the stuffing as much only as will suffice to stuff the number of ducks intended to be dressed
4. Add for each duck a teaspoonful of finely-pounded pepper, and one of salt, with three cloves of garlic, and two chittacks or four ounces of butter free of water
5. Stuff your birds and bake or roast as you please
