## Ingredients

- artichoke bottoms
- beef marrow
- coquilles or pastry cases
- Espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the fonds till tender in blanc
2. Prepare beef marrow by cutting it into pieces and blanching them in scalding water
3. Take as many coquilles as you have fonds
4. Put a little of the marrow into each shell
5. Place a fond over that marrow
6. Fill the cavity of the fond with marrow
7. Heat the coquilles hot
8. Just before serving, pour over each a little Espagnole sauce
