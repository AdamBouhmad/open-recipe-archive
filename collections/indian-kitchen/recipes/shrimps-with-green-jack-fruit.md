## Ingredients

- pieces of jack-fruit 1 seer
- potatoes 1/2 seer
- shrimps 1 seer
- oil 1 powa
- onions 1 chhatak
- ginger 2.5 tolas
- salt 2.5 tolas
- coriander 1/2 tola
- cumin 2 tolas
- pepper 1/2 tola
- turmeric 1.5 tola
- ghee
- garam-masalla
- chillies
- cassia leaves
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare a green and tender jack-fruit, cut in small pieces, boil in water mixed with a little pasted turmeric and drain off the water.
2. Prepare the shrimps, cut in suitable pieces (keep entire if small) and fry.
3. Paste together the onions, ginger, coriander, cumin, pepper, turmeric and a few chillies.
4. Brown some pieces of potatoes and then add the boiled pieces of jack-fruit and stir for a few minutes.
5. Add the pasted spices, stir again for a time and add a little water.
6. On boiling add fish.
7. Season the curry with ghee boiled with chillies and cassia leaves.
8. Add ghee and pasted garam-masalla.
9. Keep a little very thick soup.
