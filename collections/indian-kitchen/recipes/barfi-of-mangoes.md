## Ingredients

- sweet ripe mangoes
- cow's ghee
- sugar syrup
- powdered cardamoms

## Directions

1. Peel some sweet ripe mangoes
2. Strain off the juice through a clean piece of linen
3. Boil the juice in an enamelled pot
4. Stir constantly adding cow's ghee little by little till very thick
5. Take it off fire
6. Mix with an adequate quantity of very concentrated syrup of sugar and powdered cardamoms
7. Pour it in a flat plate
8. Cut in cubes when solid
