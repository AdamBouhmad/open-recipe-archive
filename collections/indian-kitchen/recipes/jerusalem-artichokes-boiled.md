## Ingredients

- Jerusalem artichokes
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the artichokes, peel and shape them nicely, dropping each one into salt and water at once to prevent its turning black
2. When all are ready, put them into a saucepan with a gallon of cold water, and two table-spoonfuls of salt
3. Boil till tender (which will take about twenty minutes after boiling-point has been attained) and drain
4. Serve them with a nice sauce blanche
