## Ingredients

- 1/2 powa of lemon-juice
- 1/2 seer of sugar
- 1 seer of water
- salt
- fragments of ice

## Directions

1. Thoroughly mix together 1/2 powa of lemon-juice, 1/2 seer of sugar, 1 seer of water, a generous pinch of salt and fragments of ice as required
2. Use pots and glasses of porcelain, glass or enamel
