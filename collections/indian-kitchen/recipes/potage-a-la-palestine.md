## Ingredients

- Jerusalem artichokes
- butter
- flour
- stock
- cream or milk and egg yolk
- pepper corns
- parsley
- sweet onion
- bacon or ham
- bread for croutons

## Directions

1. Wash, peel, and boil a nice dish of artichokes
2. Pass them through the sieve and save the pulp
3. Obtain sufficient nice clear stock from soup meat for the puree
4. Melt an ounce of butter at the bottom of a sauce-pan
5. Incorporate therewith a dessert-spoonful of flour
6. Add stock and pulp of artichoke by degrees
7. When the puree comes to the boil, it will be ready to serve
8. On its way to the table, it may be enriched by a table-spoonful of cream, or a little fresh milk into which the yolk of an egg has been stirred
9. Hand round fried croutons of bread with it
