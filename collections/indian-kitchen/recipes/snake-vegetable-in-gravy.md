## Ingredients

- snake vegetable
- brown gravy
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cut the vegetable into convenient lengths
2. Boil and drain it
3. Remove its seeds
4. Heat the pieces in a good brown gravy
5. A dust of grated cheese over each piece may be given without fear
