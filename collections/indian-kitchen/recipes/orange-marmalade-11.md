## Ingredients

- twenty-four oranges
- six lemons
- sugar equal to the weight of the fruit
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Grate the rinds of the oranges and lemons
2. Mark or cut into quarters and strip off the rinds without hurting the pulps
3. Stew the rinds until they become perfectly tender, changing the water two or three times
4. Drain them, scrape out a little of the inside, and cut them into very fine slices or chips
5. Separate the pips, skin, and fibrous parts from the pulps
6. Pour some water over the pulps and strain it off
7. With this and a little more water prepare a syrup in a preserving-pan
8. Add to it the whites of two eggs well beaten up
9. Skim it well, and the moment it begins to boil take it off the fire
10. Continue to remove the scum, add a little more water, boil, and strain until the syrup is perfectly clear
11. Throw in the chips and boil until they are quite transparent
12. Put in all the pulp and juice, and boil until it thickens
13. To ascertain if it has been sufficiently cooked, drop a little on a plate and see if it congeals
