## Ingredients

- fowl
- onion paste
- ginger paste
- chilli paste
- coriander
- cumin
- garam-masala
- curd
- ghee
- salt
- minced meat
- powdered pepper
- almonds
- boiled potatoes
- white of boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare a fowl and thrust the neck, legs, etc. into the body to make it a single lump of flesh.
2. Make a paste of onions, ginger, chillies, coriander, cumin and garam-masala, and mix with it curd, ghee and salt.
3. Smear the lump with the pasted spices and keep aside for half an hour.
4. Stuff its bowels with these spices or with minced meat fried with salt, powdered pepper, pieces of almonds, boiled potatoes and the white of boiled eggs.
5. Pass a thin iron rod through the lump lengthwise and roast all the sides on charcoal fire dropping ghee at intervals till deep brown.
