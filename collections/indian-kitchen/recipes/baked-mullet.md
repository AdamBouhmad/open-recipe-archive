## Ingredients

- a good sized mullet
- a dozen prawns
- butter
- the crumb of a roll
- milk
- one anchovy
- curly parsley
- chopped marjoram
- a cold boiled onion
- pepper
- salt
- two raw eggs
- a breakfast cupful of broth
- maitre d'hotel butter
- hock or light wine like chablis

## Directions

1. Clean the mullet carefully
2. Boil the prawns
3. Shell and clean the boiled prawns, wash them well, and dry them
4. Pound the prawns thoroughly in a mortar with a little butter and the crumb of a roll soaked in milk
5. Pass through the sieve
6. Season with anchovy wiped free from oil and chopped fine, minced curly parsley, chopped marjoram, and shredded cold boiled onion
7. Dust with pepper and a little salt
8. Work together thoroughly with two raw eggs
9. Lay the mullet on a flat dish and wipe it dry
10. Fill it with the prawn stuffing
11. Sew up the fish securely
12. Butter a pie-dish and place the mullet therein
13. Pour a breakfast cupful of broth round it
14. Spread a little butter upon the top
15. Bake for about ten minutes or a quarter of an hour in a quick oven
16. Place a pat of maitre d'hotel butter on top of the mullet before serving
17. Pour a spoonful or two of hock or light wine like chablis round it during baking
18. Baste the fish now and then during cooking
