## Ingredients

- plain clear jelly
- nice liqueur
- greengages
- apricots
- cherries
- strawberries
- pears
- vanilla blanc-manger
- preserved apples
- prunes

## Directions

1. Make enough plain clear jelly, flavoured with any nice liqueur, to fill a pretty border mould
2. Get ready a mixed collection of dessert fruits such as greengages, apricots, cherries, strawberries, pears, &c., cut them into pieces
3. Garnish the mould as tastefully as you can
4. When the garnish has set, complete the jelly with layers of the remaining fruit, and set it in ice
5. Make a breakfast-cupful of vanilla blanc-manger, dipping slices of preserved apples into it to counterfeit fillets of chicken masked in white sauce
6. Set them on ice for the blanc-manger to congeal, and then pack the centre of the jelly with them
7. Garnish the white fillets with slices of prunes to represent truffles, and a few plain slices of apple cut with fretted edges to imitate cocks-combs
