## Ingredients

- bael fruit
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select fruit that is half ripe
2. Remove all the seeds and gum carefully
3. Pass the pulp through a coarse sieve into a preserving-pan with the help of a little water
4. Add sugar to taste
5. Simmer over a slow fire for half an hour or until the fruit and sugar have acquired the consistency of jam
6. Let it cool and then bottle
