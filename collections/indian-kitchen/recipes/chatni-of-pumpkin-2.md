## Ingredients

- 1/2 powa of seedless ripe tamarind
- 1 1/2 seer of water
- tender pumpkin
- mustard seeds
- broken chillies
- panch-forons
- 5 chhatak of oil
- 1/2 seer of pumpkin pieces
- 1/2 powa of cleansed raisins
- salt
- 1 1/2 powa of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix 1/2 powa of seedless ripe tamarind with 1 1/2 seer of water and throw away the refuse
2. Peel a tender pumpkin and cut in very small thin and long pieces
3. Fry a few mustard, broken chillies and panch-forons in 5 chhatak of oil and pour in the tamarind solution
4. Add 1/2 seer of the pieces of pumpkin, 1/2 powa of cleansed raisins and a generous pinch of salt
5. A little later add 1 1/2 powa of sugar and take down when the soup is thick
