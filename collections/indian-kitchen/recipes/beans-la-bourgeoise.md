## Ingredients

- beans
- table-spoonful of tinned butter
- table-spoonful of flour
- water from cooking beans
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil and skin the beans
2. Turn beans into a stew-pan over a slow fire with a table-spoonful of tinned butter
3. Mix with them a table-spoonful of flour
4. Moisten with some of the water in which the beans were boiled
5. Season with pepper and salt
6. When nice and creamy, serve
