## Ingredients

- cabbage
- pounded quenelle meat of chicken and ham or tongue
- anchovy
- egg
- white tape
- brown gravy
- bacon

## Directions

1. Boil the head of cabbage till the leaves become pliant
2. Take it from the water and gently detach a number of leaves whole and dry them on a clean cloth
3. Have ready some pounded quenelle meat of chicken and ham or tongue with an anchovy or any artistic mixture of savoury meats bound with an egg
4. Arrange a dessert-spoonful of this on a cabbage leaf and roll it carefully up in the form of a sausage
5. Wrap two or three more leaves round this and tie them up with white tape
6. Make six or eight of these rolls
7. Simmer them gently in some good brown gravy till the leaves are done
8. Pick out your rolls and untie the tapes and dispose them tastefully in the hot dish
9. Thicken the gravy and pour it over them
10. Garnish with sippets of crisply fried bacon and serve
