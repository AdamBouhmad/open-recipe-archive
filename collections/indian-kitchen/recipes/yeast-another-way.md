## Ingredients

- two pounds of soojee or flour
- a quarter of a pound of brown sugar or suckur
- half a drachm of hops
- water

## Directions

1. Dry the hops in the sun, and then reduce them to fine powder, by pounding in a mortar
2. Mix the soojee or flour and powdered hops with a little water, just sufficient to make a stiff dough
3. then add the sugar and knead all well together
4. Roll the leaven into a ball, wrap it lightly in a clean cloth, then in a blanket, and put it away for three days, when it will be ready for use
