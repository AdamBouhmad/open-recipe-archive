## Ingredients

- Three large lemons
- Three large apples
- One pound of stoned raisins
- One pound of currants
- One pound of suet
- Two pounds of moist sugar
- One pound of sliced candied orange-peel
- One ounce of sliced candied citron
- One ounce of lemon-peel
- One teacupful of brandy
- Two tablespoonfuls of orange marmalade

## Directions

1. Grate the rinds of the lemons, squeeze out the juice, strain it, and boil the remainder of the lemons until tender enough to pulp or chop very finely
2. Add to this pulp the apples, which should be baked, and their skins and cores removed
3. Put in the remaining ingredients one by one, and as they are added mix everything thoroughly together
4. Put the mincemeat into a stone jar with a closely-fitting lid, and in a fortnight it will be ready for use
5. This should be made the first or second week in December
