## Ingredients

- one pound carrots
- one pound onions
- half a pound butter
- bunch of parsley
- couple of ounces celery
- two quarts hot water
- half an ounce salt
- quarter ounce black peppercorns
- pint measure shelled green peas

## Directions

1. Throw carrots and onions into a stew-pan with butter, parsley, and celery
2. Fry until the vegetables begin to take colour
3. Moisten with two quarts of hot water
4. Boil and skim
5. Put into the pan half an ounce of salt, a quarter ounce of black peppercorns, and a pint measure of shelled green peas
6. Simmer for three hours
7. Skim off any oil that may rise from the butter
8. Strain the broth into a basin through a tamis
