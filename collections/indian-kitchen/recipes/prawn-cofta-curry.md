## Ingredients

- thirty to forty of the best prawns
- salt
- water
- bread-crumbs
- lard, ghee, or mustard oil
- ground onions
- ground chillies
- ground turmeric
- ground peppercorns
- ground garlic
- garden herbs
- unroasted coriander-seed
- bay-leaves
- lemon-grass

## Directions

1. Get thirty to forty of the best prawns, and remove the heads and shells
2. Wash the prawns well with salt and water, then pound them to a pulp
3. Mix with it all the ingredients as directed for the beef cofta
4. Make into balls, roll them in bread-crumbs, and set aside
5. After washing the heads, remove the shells, and bruise the contents with a dessertspoonful of unroasted coriander-seed
6. Take all the juice, and fry it with the ground condiments
7. Put in the balls, brown them, add salt to taste, a cup of water, and simmer until they are cooked
