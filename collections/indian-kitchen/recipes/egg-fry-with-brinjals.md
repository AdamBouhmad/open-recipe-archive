## Ingredients

- tender brinjals
- salt
- powdered turmeric
- egg contents
- middlings
- ghee or oil

## Directions

1. Cut tender brinjals in slices of medium thickness
2. Rub with salt and powdered turmeric
3. Leave for some time until soft
4. Dip into beaten egg contents with salt
5. Roll on middlings
6. Fry in plenty of ghee or oil
