## Ingredients

- two pounds of well-selected meat
- one-inch square pieces

## Directions

1. Cut it up into one-inch square pieces, rejecting all the scraggy parts
2. Cook it in every respect according to the instructions given in recipe No. 16 for cooking a gravy chicken curry
3. Allow it to simmer for a much longer time than any other curry, or until the beef becomes tender
