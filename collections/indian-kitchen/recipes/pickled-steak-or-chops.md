## Ingredients

- a steak
- a couple of onions
- a clove of garlic
- pepper corns
- salt
- leaves of thyme and marjoram
- a bunch of parsley
- lime peel
- oil
- vinegar
- butter
- made-gravy

## Directions

1. Place a steak in a deep dish with a couple of onions sliced, a clove of garlic, pepper corns, salt, some leaves of thyme and marjoram, a bunch of parsley, and some lime peel
2. Add oil and vinegar (two table-spoonfuls of former to one of latter) sufficient to soak the meat well without actually covering it
3. Let it soak all day
4. Lift it when wanted from the marinade, and fry lightly in butter
5. When coloured on both sides nicely, pour in the marinade, with a breakfast-cup of made-gravy, and stew the steak gently till thoroughly done
6. Strain the liquor, free it from fat, reduce it a little over the fire, pour over the steak, and serve
