## Ingredients

- the yolks of three eggs
- a little water
- a salt-spoonful of pounded allspice
- salt to taste
- about three ounces of fresh butter
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Beat up the yolks of three eggs in a little water in which a salt-spoonful of pounded allspice has been dissolved
2. add salt to taste, and about three ounces of fresh butter
3. Put this mixture into a small sauce-pan, and plunge it into a bain-marie, or stew-pan large enough to receive it, full of boiling water
4. steam your mixture in this way till it thickens, and stir in your lime juice to finish with
