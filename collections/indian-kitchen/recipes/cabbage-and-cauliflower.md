## Ingredients

- cabbage
- cauliflower
- salt
- mustard oil
- powdered mustard

## Directions

1. Take only the interior tender portion of the cabbage
2. Tie it in a clean piece of cloth
3. Drop it into the boiling rice only a few minutes before taking down from the oven
4. Treat the boiled cabbage with salt, mustard oil, and powdered mustard if desired
5. Use the same method with cauliflowers
