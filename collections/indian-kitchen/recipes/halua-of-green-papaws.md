## Ingredients

- one or two big green papaws
- 1/2 seer of papaw pieces
- aniseeds
- cardamom seeds
- 1/2 powa of ghee
- 1/2 powa or 1 chhatak of cleansed raisins
- salt
- 3 chhataks of sugar
- juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Peel one or two big green papaws and cut in pieces throwing away the seeds
2. Boil 1/2 seer of the pieces in water and when fully boiled drain out the water
3. Make a paste of the boiled pieces as far as practicable
4. Fry a few aniseeds and cardamom seeds in about 1/2 powa of ghee and then add the paste, 1/2 powa or 1 chhatak of cleansed raisins and salt as required
5. A little later add 3 chhataks of sugar and the juice of a lemon
6. Take down when the mass is like a stiff dough
