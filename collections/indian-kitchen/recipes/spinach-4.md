## Ingredients

- two pounds of spinach leaves
- one ounce of butter
- three quarters of an ounce of flour
- a pinch of salt
- a pinch of sugar
- a coffee-cupful of milk, gravy, or stock
- a breakfast-cupful of milk or stock
- butter or cream or the yolk of an egg dissolved in a little milk

## Directions

1. Having selected two pounds of leaves carefully, wash them well
2. Blanch them by plunging them for a couple of minutes in scalding water
3. Drain and chop them up
4. Put into a stew-pan one ounce of butter, three quarters of an ounce of flour, a pinch of salt, and one of sugar
5. Stir this over the fire for three minutes
6. Add the spinach leaves
7. Stir round for five minutes
8. Moisten with a coffee-cupful of milk, gravy, or stock
9. Stir for two minutes more
10. Add a breakfast-cupful of the milk or stock
11. Stir for five minutes and take the pan from the fire
12. Mingle a little butter with the spinach, or give it a spoonful of cream, or the yolk of an egg dissolved in a little milk
13. Turn it out upon a good hot dish
14. Garnish it with sippets of fried bread, fleurons of puff pastry, or short-bread biscuits specially baked for the dish
15. Serve
