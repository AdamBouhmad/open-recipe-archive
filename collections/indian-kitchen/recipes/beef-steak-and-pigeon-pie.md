## Ingredients

- a slice of good steak
- two pounds of beef
- one chittack or two ounces of ghee
- a teaspoonful of salt
- two fresh limes
- four young pigeons
- twelve oysters
- twelve curry onions
- a teaspoonful of ground pepper
- some sweet herbs
- a dessertspoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Cut up the steak into pieces three inches long, and two inches or two and a half wide, by half an inch thick.
2. Cut and divide each pigeon into four pieces.
3. Put up two pounds of beef with sufficient water to make a good strong gravy, throwing in all the scraggy parts and other rejections of the steak and pigeons.
4. Warm the ghee, and fry in it the sliced onions.
5. Throw in, well dredged with the flour, the steaks and pigeons, and after frying a while add the pepper, salt, soup herbs, and some of the rind of the limes, and about half the beef gravy.
6. Set the whole on a slow fire, and simmer until the meat is tender.
7. Allow to cool.
8. Then add the oysters and the remaining gravy, with the juice of two limes.
9. Put into a dish lined with pastry, cover over the whole with a pastry crust, and bake.
