## Ingredients

- ½ seer ghee
- 1 seer coarse flour (atta)
- 1 powa curd
- ½ tola cardamom seeds
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Beat ½ seer of ghee vigorously till it is perfectly white
2. Mix with 1 seer of coarse flour (atta), 1 powa of curd and ½ tola of cardamom seeds
3. Knead the mass to a dough
4. Make balls of it and press them a little flat
5. Fry in plenty of boiling ghee to a light brown colour
6. Keep them immersed in a very concentrated syrup of sugar till saturated
7. Take them out in a dish and dry them in air
