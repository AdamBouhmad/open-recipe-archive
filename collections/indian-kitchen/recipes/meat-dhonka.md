## Ingredients

- Meat halua
- Potatoes big pieces
- Onions
- Ginger
- Cumin
- Pepper
- Turmeric
- Chillies
- Curd or asafetida solution
- Water
- Salt
- Garam-masalla
- Egg beaten
- Ghee

## Directions

1. Prepare meat halua as before
2. Press it flat on a plate
3. Cut in pieces
4. Dip into beaten egg and fry in ghee
5. Brown some big pieces of potatoes
6. Add to them onions, ginger, cumin, pepper, turmeric and chillies all pasted together
7. Stir for a few minutes and add curd or a little asafetida solution
8. A few minutes later add sufficient water
9. On boiling add salt and the fried meat
10. Finish with garam-masalla
11. Keep a little very thick soup
