## Ingredients

- 1/2 powa of rice
- water
- two eggs
- 1.5 powa of milk
- 1/2 powa of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 1/2 powa of rice in water till quite soft.
2. Beat thoroughly two eggs in a pot.
3. Add 1.5 powa of milk, 1/2 powa of sugar and the boiled rice, and stir thoroughly.
4. Heat on charcoal fire for about an hour.
