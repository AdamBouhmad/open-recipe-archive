## Ingredients

- a dozen onions
- three-quarters of a chittack of ghee
- mussoor dal
- ground condiments
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the onions into fine slices lengthways
2. Warm the three-quarters of a chittack of ghee
3. Fry and set aside the sliced onions
4. Fry all the ground condiments
5. Wash the dal well, soak it in water for about a quarter of an hour, and drain it through a colander
6. Fry the dal until thoroughly fried and browned
7. Add only a little water, barely sufficient to cover the fried dal
8. Allow to simmer from ten to fifteen minutes, or until the dal has dissolved
9. Serve up, strewing over it the fried onions
