## Ingredients

- nice sized prawns
- melted butter
- salt
- white pepper
- red pepper
- a few drops of anchovy sauce
- a little pounded mace
- freshly made butter

## Directions

1. Select some nice sized prawns
2. Boil them
3. Pick out very carefully all the grit and that black line which runs straight down the back and underneath every prawn
4. Wash them afterwards in cold water
5. Pour a lot of water over them as they lie on the top of your sieve
6. Dry them
7. Toss them in a little melted butter in a frying-pan until they have absorbed the butter
8. Pass through the mincing machine
9. Pound them thoroughly in the mortar
10. Press them through the wire sieve
11. Season the paste with salt, white and red pepper, a few drops of anchovy sauce, and a little pounded mace
12. Work the whole together with some nice freshly made butter till thoroughly incorporated
13. Set it in an earthenware jar
14. Pour a spoonful of melted butter over the surface
