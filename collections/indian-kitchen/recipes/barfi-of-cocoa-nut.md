## Ingredients

- a few half-ripe cocoa-nut kernels
- 1/2 seer of khowa kheer
- 1 tola of powdered cardamoms
- 1 seer of sugar

## Directions

1. Scrape a few half-ripe cocoa-nut kernels and make a paste of them.
2. Mix 1/2 seer of this paste with 1/2 seer of khowa kheer and fry the mixture a little without ghee or oil.
3. Then add 1 tola of powdered cardamoms and a syrup of 1 seer of sugar.
4. Stir constantly till very thick and gluey.
5. Take the pan off fire and transfer the mass in a flat plate.
6. Cut in cubes when cold.
