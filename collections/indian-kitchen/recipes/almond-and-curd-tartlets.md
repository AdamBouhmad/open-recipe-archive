## Ingredients

- puff paste
- half pound sugar
- yolks of six eggs
- two ounces of blanched sweet almonds
- three bitter almonds
- two table-spoonfuls of orange flower water
- juice of four limes
- two potatoes
- a quart of milk
- four ounces of good butter
- one glass of brandy
- one glass of maraschino
- finely sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Line six tart-let tins with puff paste
2. Beat half pound sugar with yolks of six eggs in a basin
3. Pound together in a mortar two ounces of blanched sweet almonds, three bitter ones, two table-spoonfuls of orange flower water, the juice of four limes, and two potatoes (mealy ones) boiled, drained, and passed through a hair sieve
4. Mix the eggs, sugar, and almond paste
5. Turn a quart of milk to curd, crumble it, and beat it up with four ounces of good butter till quite smooth
6. Mix it thoroughly with the eggs, almonds, and other ingredients, adding a glass of brandy and one of maraschino
7. Fill the patties with this mixture
8. Bake till thoroughly hot in a moderate oven and serve with finely sifted sugar dusted over them
