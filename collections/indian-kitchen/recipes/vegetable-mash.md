## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- cauliflower
- carrots
- turnips
- green peas
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Mash down the potatoes with plenty of butter, pepper, and salt
2. Mince small the cauliflower, carrots, and turnips
3. Add them with the peas to the mashed potatoes
4. Mix them all well together and serve up
