## Ingredients

- flowers
- til or sesamum seed, wetted or damped

## Directions

1. Place on a large tray a layer of the flowers, about four inches thick and two feet square
2. Put some of the til or sesamum seed, wetted or damped, about two inches thick on this
3. Place another layer of flowers, four inches thick on top
4. Cover the whole with a sheet, held down by weights at the sides
5. Allow to remain for eighteen hours
6. Remove the flowers and replace by layers of fresh flowers
7. Repeat the operation three times, each layer of fresh flowers being allowed to remain eighteen hours
8. After the last process, take the seeds in their swollen state and place them in a clean mill
9. Express the oil, which will possess the scent of the flowers
10. Keep in prepared skins, called dubbers
