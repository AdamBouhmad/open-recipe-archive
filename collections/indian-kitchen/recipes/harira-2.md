## Ingredients

- 1/4 seer of fine flour
- 1/4 seer of ghee
- 1 seer of milk
- 2 1/2 powas of sugar
- a pinch of powdered cloves
- very little saffron
- raisins
- pieces of almonds and pistachios

## Directions

1. Fry 1/4 seer of fine flour with 1/4 seer of ghee till brown
2. Add a seer of milk and when it boils add a very concentrated syrup of 2 1/2 powas of sugar
3. Add a pinch of powdered cloves and very little saffron
4. Add raisins, pieces of almonds and pistachios as desired
5. Stir constantly throughout the whole preparation
6. Take down when very thick
