## Ingredients

- artichoke bottoms
- salad oil
- six cloves of garlic
- pepper
- salt
- lime

## Directions

1. Prepare the artichokes as usual without the farce
2. Place them in a pie-dish with enough salad oil to keep them moist and safe from burning
3. Add six cloves of garlic, pepper, and salt
4. Put the dish into the oven and bake until the fonds are tender
5. Remove the garlic
6. Give them a dust of pepper and the squeeze of a lime
7. Dish up and serve
