## Ingredients

- half a seer of soojee
- one seer of water
- half a seer of sugar
- three chittacks or six ounces of ghee
- a few white cardamoms
- a few small sticks of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Steep half a seer of soojee in one seer of water for twelve hours, or, if the hulluah be made in the winter, let it soak for eighteen hours
2. Strain the milk of soojee through a coarse duster, rejecting only such impurities as remain unstrained
3. Add to the milk half a seer of sugar
4. Boil it, stirring it all the time, and as it thickens add three chittacks or six ounces of ghee, warmed with a few white cardamoms and a few small sticks of cinnamon
5. Continue stirring it
