## Ingredients

- tipparees
- water
- fine clean white sugar
- juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Clean and prick the tipparees
2. Put them into a clean well-tinned stewpan with as much water as will entirely cover them
3. Boil them until all the juice has run out
4. Strain the latter into a preserving-pan through fine muslin without crushing the fruit
5. Allow it to simmer for a while, removing the scum
6. Add to it fine clean white sugar to taste in small quantities at a time, skimming it well all the while
7. When nearly ready, put in the juice of two lemons strained through muslin
8. When the scum has ceased to rise and the jelly is clear, remove the pan from the fire
9. Bottle the jelly while it is warm and cork when it is quite cold
