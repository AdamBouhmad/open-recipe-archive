## Ingredients

- half a dozen sweet and ripe oranges
- 1 seer milk
- pasted almonds
- ½ powa sugar
- essence of rose

## Directions

1. Peel half a dozen sweet and ripe oranges and further skin them separating the grains
2. Squeeze the grains in a bag of linen bringing out the juice
3. Boil 1 seer of milk till it is reduced to 1½ powa
4. Mix with the juice of the oranges, some pasted almonds and ½ powa of sugar
5. Take down when thick and mix with 2 drops of essence of rose
