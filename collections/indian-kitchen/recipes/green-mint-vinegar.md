## Ingredients

- fresh, clean mint-leaves
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. Put into a wide-mouthed bottle enough fresh, clean mint-leaves to fill it loosely
2. Fill it up with good vinegar
3. After it has been stopped close for two or three weeks, pour off the vinegar clear into another bottle
4. Keep it well corked for use
5. Serve with lamb or kid when fresh mint cannot be obtained
