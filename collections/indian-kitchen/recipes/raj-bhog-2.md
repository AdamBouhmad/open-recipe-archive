## Ingredients

- hard chhana 1/2 seer
- khowa kheer 1/2 seer
- middlings 1/2 powa
- paste of almonds 1 chhatak
- powdered aniseeds 1/4 tola
- a little powdered cardamom seeds
- milk if necessary
- broken pieces of sugar-candy 1/2 powa
- raisins 1 chhatak
- 2 or 3 drops of essence of rose
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1/2 seer of hard chhana
2. Mix thoroughly with khowa kheer, middlings, a paste of almonds, powdered aniseeds and a little powdered cardamom seeds
3. Make a paste soft but stiff enough to be made into balls with a little milk if necessary
4. Make big balls (10 or 12 in number)
5. Stuff them with a mixture of broken pieces of sugar-candy, raisins and 2 or 3 drops of essence of rose
6. Drop them into a boiling syrup of sugar
7. Remove from heat when they sink down after boiling
8. Serve a few hours later
