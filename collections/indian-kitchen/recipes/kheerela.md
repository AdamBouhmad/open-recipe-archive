## Ingredients

- 2 seers of milk
- sugar
- cardamom seeds
- ghee

## Directions

1. Boil 2 seers of milk.
2. Add a table spoonful of sugar when half reduced.
3. Stir the more carefully and vigorously the more it is thick.
4. Take down when the milk becomes a stiff paste.
5. Scrape together the paste that sticks to the sides of the pan and beat thoroughly to a fine muddy one.
6. Prepare finger-like round and long forms stuffed with a few seeds of cardamom and fry in plenty of ghee till reddish brown.
