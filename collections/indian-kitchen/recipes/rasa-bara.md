## Ingredients

- 0.5 seer of pulses of Kalai
- 1 powa of hard chhana
- entire cubebs
- seeds of big cardamoms
- ghee or oil
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Make fine pastes of 0.5 seer of pulses of Kalai, moistened for a few hours, and 1 powa of hard chhana separately
2. Then mix them together adding a few entire cubebs and seeds of a few big cardamoms
3. Beat thoroughly
4. Fry this paste in medium balls till deep brown in ghee or oil
5. Keep them in a syrup of sugar till saturated
