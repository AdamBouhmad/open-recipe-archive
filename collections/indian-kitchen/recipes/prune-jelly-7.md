## Ingredients

- half a pound of prunes
- two ounces of white sugar
- a slice or two of a lime
- a little cinnamon
- claret and water, mixed half and half

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of prunes into a sauce-pan with two ounces of white sugar, a slice or two of a lime, a little cinnamon, and sufficient claret and water, mixed half and half, to cover them
2. Stew gently till the fruit is quite tender
3. Lift the sauce-pan from the fire and drain off the liquor
4. Stone the prunes and pass the fruit through the sieve, saving the pulp in a basin
5. Crack the stones and throw the prune kernels into the pulp
