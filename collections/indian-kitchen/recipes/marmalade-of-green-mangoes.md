## Ingredients

- full sized green mangoes
- lime water
- salt
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Peel some full sized green mangoes and cut them in big pieces
2. Keep them in lime water for about half an hour
3. Wash them with fresh water and keep aside for a little while more after smearing them with salt
4. Boil them in water for a few minutes
5. Drop them in a hot syrup of sugar
6. Apply mild heat
7. Take down when the syrup being thick sticks to the sides of the pieces of mangoes
