## Ingredients

- three ounces fine Spanish chocolate with vanilla flavour
- water
- two pints pure milk
- powdered loaf sugar
- salt
- yolks of eight eggs

## Directions

1. Rasp three ounces of fine Spanish chocolate, which has the vanilla flavour
2. Make a paste of it with the smallest possible quantity of water
3. Put two pints of pure milk over the fire, and let it boil
4. Add powdered loaf sugar to your taste, and a little salt
5. Beat up the chocolate with some of the milk as it boils, and mix it well
6. Pour it into the boiling milk, which you must keep in motion
7. Add the yolks of eight eggs well beaten up
8. Keep stirring in, or rather milling the mixture, until of sufficient consistency
9. When cool enough put the custards into glasses
