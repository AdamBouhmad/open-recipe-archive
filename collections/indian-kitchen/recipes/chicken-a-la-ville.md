## Ingredients

- pieces of chicken
- egg yolks
- bread crumbs
- boiling fat
- potato duchesses
- parsley
- minced mushrooms

## Directions

1. Stew pieces of chicken in their own broth
2. Drain them and make a rich veloute with the broth, thickening it with yolks of egg like custard
3. Dip the pieces of chicken in the thick sauce, and let them get cold
4. Bread-crumb them, and fry them a golden yellow in boiling fat
5. Drain them, and pile them on a napkin, garnished with small potato duchesses, and fried parsley
6. Send the rest of the sauce round in a boat after adding to it a heaped-up tablespoonful of minced mushrooms
