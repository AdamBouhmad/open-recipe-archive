## Ingredients

- capon
- strips of fat bacon
- sweet onions
- broth
- carrot
- celery leaves and stalks
- onions
- pepper corns
- bunch of parsley
- dried thyme
- glass of sherry
- liver
- tomato pulp
- maccaroni
- puree of young turnips
- potatoes a la maitre d'hotel

## Directions

1. Lard the capon with strips of fat bacon, and fill the cavity of its carcass with a couple of sweet onions
2. Choose a deep stew-pan, and place the bird therein, with a carrot cut into slices, celery leaves and stalks, two onions, pepper corns, a bunch of parsley, some dried thyme in a muslin bag, and a glass of sherry
3. Braise the bird in this till done
4. Meanwhile pound the liver you boiled in the broth, and get ready some tomato pulp
5. Lift out the capon, and dish it upon some previously boiled maccaroni
6. Strain off the gravy in which it was braised, thicken it, add the liver, and tomato pulp
7. Give it a rapid boil up, and pour it over the bird
8. Serve with puree of young turnips, and potatoes a la maitre d'hotel
