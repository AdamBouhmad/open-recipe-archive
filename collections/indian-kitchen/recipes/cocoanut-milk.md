## Ingredients

- a cocoanut
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Scrape or rasp the nut with the aid of the khoornee into very fine particles.
2. Put it into a deep vessel, and pour boiling water over it until the whole of the scraped cocoanut is covered.
3. Allow it to steep for ten or fifteen minutes.
4. Carefully strain through a clean napkin into another vessel or cup.
5. Return the pulp into the original vessel, and pour more boiling water over it.
6. Continue this operation of steeping in boiling water and straining until you have obtained the required quantity of the extracted milk of the cocoanut.
7. Throw away the pulp.
