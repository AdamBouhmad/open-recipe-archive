## Ingredients

- ghee 1 chhatak
- flour 1 powa
- powdered rice 3 chhataks
- middlings 1 chhatak
- moistened and pasted pulses of kalai 0.5 powa
- husked sesame 1 powa
- pasted almonds
- salt
- aniseeds
- cardamom seeds
- ginger-juice
- powdered pepper
- water
- milk 2.5 seers
- sugar
- rose water
- camphor

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly ghee with flour, powdered rice and middlings
2. Add moistened and pasted pulses of kalai, double the quantity of husked sesame and pasted almonds
3. Add adequate quantities of salt, aniseeds, cardamom seeds, ginger-juice, powdered pepper and water to make a not-so-stiff paste
4. Beat thoroughly
5. Make squares as before and fry in ghee
6. Boil say 2.5 seers of milk till it is less than half
7. Add sugar as desired, a little rose water, very little camphor and the fried squares
8. Boil a few minutes more and serve in cups when cool
