## Ingredients

- Jerusalem artichokes
- cream
- pepper
- salt
- mild cheese
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the artichokes until tender and drain
2. Make a puree of plain boiled artichoke, slightly diluted with cream, and seasoned with pepper, and salt
3. Turn into a well-buttered pie-dish
4. Dust the surface over with finely grated mild cheese
5. Bake until the top takes colour
