## Ingredients

- intestines of big fresh fish
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- oil
- besam

## Directions

1. Remove the dark portions containing the undigested foods and wash
2. Mix with powdered turmeric and salt, and fry in oil
3. This is taken with rice or used to season sak chach-chari of every kind
4. Or, cut in big pieces, mix with salt and turmeric, dip into a well-beaten paste of besam and fry
