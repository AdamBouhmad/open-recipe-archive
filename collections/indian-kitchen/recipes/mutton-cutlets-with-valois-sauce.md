## Ingredients

- mutton cutlets
- mashed potatoes a la G.C.
- large sweet onion
- vinegar
- butter
- yolks of five eggs
- chicken glaze
- chopped curled parsley

## Directions

1. Grill the cutlets most delicately
2. Serve them round a socle of mashed potatoes a la G.C.
3. Boil a large sweet onion, cut up into dice, in a coffee-cupful of vinegar
4. Let it boil till the vinegar is entirely reduced
5. Let the onion get cold, then pound it and mix into it an ounce of butter, and the yolks of five eggs
6. Stir this over a low fire for a couple of minutes only
7. Take it off, add another ounce of butter, mix it well, and re-place the sauce-pan on the fire
8. Add half a pint of chicken glaze and another ounce of butter
9. Mix thoroughly, adding finally a table-spoonful of chopped curled parsley
10. Pour this into the centre of your ring of cutlets, and serve
11. This sauce should be made early, and kept hot en bain-marie, for the moment the cutlets are cooked, they must be sent up, and the sauce will spoil if allowed to get cold
