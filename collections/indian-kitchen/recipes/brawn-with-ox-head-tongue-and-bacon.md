## Ingredients

- ox-head
- well cured tongue
- thick slice of bacon

## Directions

1. Stew the ox-head as in the foregoing recipe
2. Boil the tongue
3. Cut up both the tongue and bacon whilst they are quite hot
4. Season as before explained
5. Stir the chopped meats well together
6. Press the whole tightly down in a brawn tin
7. Let it remain three or four hours with a heavy weight above it
8. When required, dip the tin into hot water to loosen the sides of the brawn
9. It will slip out fully formed and ready for the table
