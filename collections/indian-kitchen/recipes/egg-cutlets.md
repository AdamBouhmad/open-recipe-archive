## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- besam
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- ghee
- ginger juice
- onion juice
- powdered biscuit or suji

## Directions

1. Shell and slice hard boiled eggs into four pieces
2. Dip into thick solution of besam with powdered turmeric and salt
3. Fry in plenty of ghee until brown
4. Dip again into beaten egg contents with powdered turmeric, ginger juice and onion juice
5. Roll on powdered biscuit or suji
6. Fry as before
