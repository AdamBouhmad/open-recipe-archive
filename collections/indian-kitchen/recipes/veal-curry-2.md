## Ingredients

- small shoulder of veal
- sixteen or eighteen one-inch square pieces of meat

## Directions

1. Cut off from the shoulder sixteen or eighteen one-inch square pieces of the best part of the meat
2. Curry it in every particular the same as a chicken
3. Allow it to simmer half to three-quarters of an hour
