## Ingredients

- ghee 1 chhatak
- flour 1 seer
- water
- besam of grams
- ginger-juice
- powders of pepper
- cumin
- garam-masalla
- salt
- minced meat

## Directions

1. Mix ghee with flour at the rate of one chhatak to one seer, add water and knead.
2. Mix a little ghee with some besam of grams and fry.
3. Mix together this fried besam, ginger-juice, powders of pepper, cumin, garam-masalla, salt and minced meat and fry with ghee.
4. Form medium balls of the kneaded flour, flatten, stuff with a little meat and make balls again.
5. Press these balls into flat and round discs of moderate thickness and roast them with a little ghee or heat them 2 or 3 at a time in a pan, roast on fire without ghee, and then smear with ghee to make soft.
