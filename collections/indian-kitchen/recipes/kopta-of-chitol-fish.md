## Ingredients

- big sized chitol fish
- turmeric
- salt
- powdered turmeric
- pepper
- cumin
- ginger-juice
- a little flour
- ghee

## Directions

1. Flake and skin a big sized chitol fish
2. Cut in pieces and boil some of them with turmeric and salt
3. Drain off the water
4. Remove the bones and mix powdered turmeric, pepper, cumin, ginger-juice, salt and a little flour
5. Form balls and fry them till deep brown in ghee
