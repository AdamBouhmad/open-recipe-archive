## Ingredients

- three partridges
- soup meat
- Madeira
- sugar
- chilli vinegar

## Directions

1. Buy three partridges in addition to your customary soup meat
2. Pluck the birds, draw them, and cut them up, breaking all bones of any size with a mallet
3. Set the soup meat for consomme as usual
4. When you have obtained as strong a stock as possible therefrom, strain it, and let it get cool
5. Remove all fat that may rise to the surface
6. When quite clear, pour it into a large sauce-pan, adding all the pieces of partridge, including the livers of the birds
7. Set this on the fire to come slowly to the boil, skimming it very carefully
8. After it has boiled, slack off the fire, and let the contents of your sauce-pan simmer slowly for an hour or more
9. Strain the soup from the bones, clarify
10. Give it half a glass of Madeira, a pinch of sugar, and a few drops of chilli vinegar to finish with
