## Ingredients

- four good sized Jerusalem artichokes
- four hard-boiled eggs
- buttered dish
- veloute au Parmesan or melted maitre d'hotel butter
- spiced salt

## Directions

1. Choose four good sized Jerusalem artichokes, trim, boil, and set them to cool
2. Take four hard-boiled eggs, and cut them in halves
3. Out of the artichokes prepare eight flat slices, and place half an egg upon each slice with the rounded end uppermost
4. Set them on a buttered dish, heat them thoroughly in the oven
5. Just before serving, pour over them some thickly worked veloute au Parmesan, or some melted maitre d'hotel butter
6. Give a dusting of spiced salt on taking the eggs from the oven
