## Ingredients

- 0.5 chhatak of ghee
- 1 powa of middlings
- 3 powas or more of hard chhana
- khowa kheer
- sugar syrup
- dry crystalline white sugar

## Directions

1. Mix 0.5 chhatak of ghee with 1 powa of middlings
2. Mix this again with 3 powas or more of hard chhana
3. Make a fine paste with the hands
4. Make big spherical balls of it
5. Stuff each with a little pasted khowa kheer
6. Fry them in boiling ghee till red
7. Pick them out and keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated
8. When taking them out smear all over with dry crystalline white sugar
