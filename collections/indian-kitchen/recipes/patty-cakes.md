## Ingredients

- one ounce of flour
- one ounce of bread crumbs
- essence to flavour
- two ounces of sugar
- two ounces of butter
- half pint of milk
- two eggs
- powdered sugar
- sauce a la Royale

## Directions

1. Mix flour, bread crumbs, essence, sugar, butter melted in half pint of milk, and two eggs well beaten up
2. Bake in small, buttered patty-pans till browned
3. Turn them out, and serve on a napkin
4. Dust powdered sugar over all
5. Serve sauce a la Royale in a boat
