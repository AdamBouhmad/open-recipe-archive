## Ingredients

- a shin and a half of beef
- an oxtail
- two Bombay onions
- two carrots
- a stick of celery
- sweet herbs
- parsley
- a dozen pepper corns
- a clove of garlic
- a wine-glass of mushroom ketchup
- a teaspoonful of caramel
- Madeira
- lime
- sugar

## Directions

1. Buy a shin and a half of beef, and make the best gravy you can as usual
2. Commence boiling the shin as soon as you can
3. When the shin consomme is ready early in the afternoon, separate the tail at the joints, and throw the pieces into your stock pot with two Bombay onions, two carrots, a stick of celery, and a muslin bag containing sweet herbs, parsley, a dozen pepper corns, and a clove of garlic
4. Add a wine-glass of mushroom ketchup and a teaspoonful of caramel, and simmer the soup for a couple of hours skimming it carefully
5. Strain and clarify the liquor, saving a few joints of the tail as a garnish
6. Add a table-spoonful of Madeira with a squeeze of a lime, and a pinch of sugar
