## Ingredients

- ½ powa of seedless ripe tamarind
- 1½ seer of water
- tender pumpkin
- mustard seeds
- broken chillies
- panch-forons
- 5 chhataks of oil
- ½ seer of pumpkin pieces
- ½ powa of cleansed raisins
- salt
- ½ powa of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix ½ powa of seedless ripe tamarind with 1½ seer of water and throw away the refuse
2. Peel a tender pumpkin and cut in very small thin and long pieces
3. Fry a few mustard, broken chillies and panch-forons in 5 chhataks of oil and pour in the tamarind solution
4. Add ½ seer of the pieces of pumpkin, ½ powa of cleansed raisins and a generous pinch of salt
5. A little later add ½ powa of sugar and take down when the soup is thick
