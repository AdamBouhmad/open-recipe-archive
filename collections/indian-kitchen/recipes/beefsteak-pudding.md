## Ingredients

- beef
- paste
- cold cooked bacon
- spiced pepper
- strong gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thin collops
2. place a thin slice of cold cooked bacon over each collop
3. season this with spiced pepper, and roll each collop up
4. Line the pudding basin with paste
5. fill it with layers of collops
6. pour in among the collops a little strong gravy
7. close the paste securely and boil for three hours
