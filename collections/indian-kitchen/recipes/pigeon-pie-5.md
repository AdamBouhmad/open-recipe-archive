## Ingredients

- pound and a half of tender lean beef
- three good pigeons
- half a pound of leanish bacon
- pound of gravy beef
- spiced pepper
- salt
- grated lime peal
- chopped bacon
- finely chopped parsley
- Madeira or white wine

## Directions

1. Make the best gravy possible from the pound of gravy meat, the pigeon trimmings, and any scraps at hand
2. Cut the tender beef into neat collops and blend with the bacon cut into thin strips
3. Prepare the pigeons whole as if for roasting
4. Put some chopped bacon seasoned with pepper, salt, or spiced pepper, salt, and grated lime peal, inside each bird
5. Place the pigeons upon the tender beef
6. Rub the pie-dish with a shallot before it is packed
7. Sprinkle finely chopped parsley over the bottom of the dish before the beef
8. Half a glass of Madeira or any sound white wine may be mingled with the gravy
