## Ingredients

- some large sweet onions
- milk
- sauce blonde
- a spoonful of cream

## Directions

1. Simmer some large sweet onions in milk till tender
2. Pass through the sieve
3. Work the pulp into sauce blonde
4. Add a spoonful of cream for high days and holidays
