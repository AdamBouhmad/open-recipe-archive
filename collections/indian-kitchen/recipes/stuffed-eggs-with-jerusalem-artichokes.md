## Ingredients

- four eggs
- four large Jerusalem artichokes
- melted butter
- anchovy butter
- maitre d'hotel butter or prawn butter

## Directions

1. Boil four eggs hard and put them into a basin of cold water
2. When cold, cut them in halves crosswise with a dessert-knife dipped in melted butter
3. Slice off a piece from each rounded end so that the halves will sit upright
4. Prepare the yolks as previously described
5. Boil four large Jerusalem artichokes until just done and not overdone
6. Lift them out of the water and let them get cold
7. Trim with a silver dessert-knife two thick flat slices from each artichoke, each capable of holding a half egg
8. Butter the eight slices and lay them upon a flat silver dish well buttered
9. Set half an egg upon each of them
10. Put on the top of each egg a little pat of anchovy butter
11. Set the dish in the oven till the steam rises freely and serve piping hot
12. If desired, pour melted maitre d'hotel or prawn butter over them
