## Ingredients

- undercut of sirloin of beef
- coarser meat for gravy
- boiled bacon
- spiced pepper
- shallot
- butter
- strong gravy
- hard boiled egg
- light crust

## Directions

1. Buy a nice undercut of a sirloin of beef and some coarser meat for gravy
2. Slice the undercut into thin slices crosswise, not lengthwise
3. Place the slices on a flat dish and upon each slice lay a thin one of boiled bacon
4. Give the whole a dusting of spiced pepper
5. Roll them up (each separately) and give them an external dusting of spiced pepper
6. Take a pie-dish, rub it well with a shallot, butter it, and place a layer of the rolled slices over the bottom of it
7. Pour gently round the layer a cupful of strong gravy made from the coarser meat
8. Repeat the process adding another layer of rolled beef slices, and more gravy, till the dish is packed
9. Garnish the surface with pieces of hard boiled egg
10. Cover the whole with a good light crust
11. Bake in the usual way and serve
