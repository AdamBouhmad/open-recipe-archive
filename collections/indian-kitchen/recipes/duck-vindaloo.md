## Ingredients

- a young, full-grown, but tender duck
- vinegar
- salt
- ground condiments

## Directions

1. Take a young, full-grown, but tender duck; cut it up as for a curry, and put it through the same course of pickling from eighteen to twenty-four hours before being cooked.
