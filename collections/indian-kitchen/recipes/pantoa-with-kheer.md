## Ingredients

- ghee 1 chhatak
- sabeda 5 chhataks
- cold water
- 1/2 seer khowa kheer
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Mix 1 chhatak of ghee thoroughly with 5 chhataks of sabeda
2. Make a stiff paste with a little cold water
3. Mix this again with 1/2 seer of khowa kheer
4. Make a fine paste with the hands
5. Proceed exactly in the same way as before
