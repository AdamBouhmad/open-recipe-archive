## Ingredients

- yolks of eight fresh eggs
- one dessertspoonful corn-flour
- pounded loaf sugar
- quarter of a pound Jordan almonds
- one seer good cold milk
- essence of almonds or orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Beat up in a large deep bowl the yolks of eight fresh eggs
2. Dredge into it while beating up a dessertspoonful of corn-flour
3. Sweeten to taste with the best pounded loaf sugar
4. Add to it a quarter of a pound of Jordan almonds well bruised in a marble mortar
5. Pour the mixture into a clean newly-tinned copper pan
6. Stir into it a seer of good cold milk
7. Put the pan on the fire
8. Never cease stirring it, keeping the spoon as much as possible in the centre of the pan
9. Reduce the flame after it has boiled for fifteen minutes, and continue to boil for a few minutes longer, until the custard is of the consistency required
10. Fill the custard cups or glasses, leaving about half an inch space to fill up with the whites of the eggs, beaten up to a very light froth
11. Flavour it with a little essence of almonds, or orange-flower water
