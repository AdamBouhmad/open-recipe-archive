## Ingredients

- tender fillet of beef
- bacon
- mushroom
- parsley
- shallot
- pepper
- thyme
- marjoram
- lime peel
- garlic
- sweet onion
- carrot
- butter
- tomato puree
- beef gravy
- glazed onions
- haricots verts
- Brussels sprouts
- maccaroni
- cheese

## Directions

1. Take a tender fillet of beef and preserve it whole after trimming it into shape
2. Make an incision in it lengthways
3. Insert a long strip of bacon, fat and lean in equal parts, previously rolled in a fines herbes mince composed of a table-spoonful each of finely chopped mushroom and parsley, a dessert-spoonful of minced shallot, and some pepper
4. Tie up the fillet carefully with tape
5. Take a good slice of bacon, mince it very small with thyme, marjoram, lime peel, a clove of garlic, half a sweet onion and a carrot
6. Shake this mixture in a little butter at the bottom of a stew-pan
7. When it melts, place the fillet upon it and turn it gently till it browns nicely
8. Pour in a pint of tomato puree diluted with beef gravy to the consistency of ordinary pea-soup
9. Simmer your fillet in this till it is done, it ought to be kept at least a couple of hours at a gentle heat
10. When ready to serve, strain off the sauce
11. Place the fillet upon a very hot dish and remove the tape
12. Garnish with glazed onions, haricots verts, Brussels sprouts, or any nice vegetable
13. Pour a little of the sauce over it
14. Have ready some hot boiled and drained maccaroni in a sauce-pan
15. Empty all the sauce that remains amongst the maccaroni
16. Shake into it a table-spoonful of finely grated cheese
17. Toss the whole over the fire for a minute and dish it separately in a very hot dish
18. Serve the two together immediately
