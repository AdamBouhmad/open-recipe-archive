## Ingredients

- two ounces of sweet almonds
- six bitter almonds
- rose-water
- three-fourths of an ounce of isinglass
- water
- a quart of good rich milk
- the peel of a small lemon
- a stick of cinnamon
- good clean sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound with a little rose-water two ounces of sweet and six bitter almonds
2. Dissolve three-fourths of an ounce of isinglass in a little water
3. When nearly cool, mix it into a quart of good rich milk
4. Mix in the almonds the peel of a small lemon and a stick of cinnamon
5. Sweeten to taste with good clean sugar
6. Let it stand for two or three hours
7. Put it into a pan and let it boil for six or eight minutes, stirring constantly
8. Strain through muslin and keep stirring until nearly cold
9. Pour it into moulds
