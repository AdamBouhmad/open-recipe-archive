## Ingredients

- Khowa kheer
- Cocoa-nut kernel
- Almonds
- Pistachios
- Sugar
- Ghee
- Rose essence
- Flour or kalai pulses
- Milk
- Water

## Directions

1. Make a paste of khowa kheer, the kernel of a half ripe cocoa-nut, almonds, and pistachios in any quantity
2. Mix with the necessary amount of sugar
3. Fry it with a little ghee on mild heat
4. Add 2 or 3 drops of essence of rose when sticky and take down
5. Make a thick solution of flour or a paste of moistened pulses of kalai, sugar and milk
6. Heat a frying pan, smear with a little ghee
7. Add two table-spoonfuls of the flour solution
8. Tilt the pan spreading it in a thin circle
9. When sufficiently baked put a little of the preparation of kheer shaped like a cigar at one end
10. Roll the disc around it
11. Fry a little with ghee and take down
12. Continue in this way
