## Ingredients

- maccaroni
- garlic clove
- cold water
- butter
- fresh tomato pulp
- salt
- black pepper
- grated Parmesan cheese or mild powdered dry cheese

## Directions

1. Boil the maccaroni in boiling water in a sauce-pan (which may be rubbed with a clove of garlic) until it is tender
2. The moment it is tender, stop the boiling by adding a cup of cold water
3. Drain it carefully, as you do rice, and let it remain in the hot sauce-pan
4. Stir into it a table-spoonful of the best fresh, or preserved butter
5. Work this about over the fire whilst an assistant adds by degrees a breakfast-cupful of fresh tomato pulp, a little salt and black pepper
6. Add lastly, a heaped up table-spoonful of either grated Parmesan from the bottle, or any mild thoroughly powdered dry cheese
7. Serve steaming hot without delay. The cheese should form long threads when lifted from the dish with the maccaroni
