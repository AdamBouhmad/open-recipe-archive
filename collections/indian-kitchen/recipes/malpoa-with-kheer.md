## Ingredients

- 1 powa of milk
- 1.5 powa of middlings
- 0.5 seer of thoroughly pasted khowa kheer
- milk
- powders of aniseed
- powders of cardamom
- powders of pepper
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Mix 1 powa of milk with 1.5 powa middlings
2. Keep aside for about an hour
3. Then mix it with 0.5 seer of thoroughly pasted khowa kheer and adequate quantity of milk to make the mass a moderately thick paste
4. Also mix powders of aniseed, cardamom and pepper
5. Fry as before
6. Drop into a syrup of sugar
