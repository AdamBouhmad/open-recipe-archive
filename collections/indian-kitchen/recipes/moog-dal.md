## Ingredients

- Fried moog-dal 0.5 seer
- Ghee 1 chhatak
- Turmeric 0.5 tola
- Pepper with cumin seeds 0.5 tola
- Coriander 0.5 tola
- Cassia leaves a few
- Red chillies 3 or 4
- Salt 1.5 tola
- Sugar 1 tola
- Water 1 seer
- Panch-foron 1 pinch

## Directions

1. Entire moog pulses are generally heated in a pot for a few minutes and then they are grinded and husked. These are called fried moog-dal.
2. Heat water in a pan. Wash the moog-dal thoroughly and add them in the water when it boils.
3. Put the lid on keeping a small opening.
4. Clear off the scum formed on the surface from time to time throughout the entire run.
5. Add the powdered turmeric and the sugar when the pulses are soft.
6. Add the pepper with cumin and the coriander, all pasted or powdered, when the pulses melt.
7. Add salt and then take the pot down from the oven.
8. Take the ghee in another pan and heat with the cassia leaves, red chillies broken in pieces, and the panch-foron.
9. Pour the boiled liquid dal when the red chillies turn black.
10. Boil it for a few minutes more and take down.
