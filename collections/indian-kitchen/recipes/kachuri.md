## Ingredients

- salt
- sodium bicarbonate
- powders of fried pepper
- cumin
- garam-masala
- coriander
- minced meat
- ghee
- water
- flour
- boiled and pasted green peas
- cauliflowers

## Directions

1. Mix salt, a pinch of sodium bicarbonate, powders of fried pepper, cumin, garam-masala and coriander with minced meat and fry with ghee, adding a little water if necessary to boil the meat.
2. Knead some flour into a dough, make small balls of it, flatten, stuff with a little meat and make balls again.
3. Press them into small, round, flat discs of moderate thickness and fry in plenty of ghee.
4. Boiled and pasted green peas and cauliflowers may be mixed with the meat.
