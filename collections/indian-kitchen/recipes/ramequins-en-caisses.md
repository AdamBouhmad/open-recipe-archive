## Ingredients

- two ounces of mild grated cheese
- two ounces of white bread-crumbs
- milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- one tea-spoonful of mustard powder
- yolks of three eggs
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Soak the crumbs in milk
2. Pound them in a mortar with the cheese and a little butter till the whole is well mixed
3. Season the mixture with pepper and salt, adding a tea-spoonful of mustard powder, and the yolks of three eggs
4. Beat up the egg whites to a stiff froth
5. Mingle the egg whites with the mixture
6. Fill your paper cases, which should be well buttered to prevent their burning outside
7. Bake them from ten minutes to a quarter of an hour
8. Serve them as soon as they have raised their heads and have slightly taken colour
