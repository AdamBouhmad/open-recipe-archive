## Ingredients

- pastry
- prawn doopiaja
- ghee

## Directions

1. Prepare pastry the same as Dal Pittas
2. Enclose in the pastry a tablespoonful of the prawn doopiaja
3. Mince the prawns before being put into the pastry
4. Fry in ghee
