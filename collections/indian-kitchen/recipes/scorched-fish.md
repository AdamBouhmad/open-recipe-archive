## Ingredients

- fishes with scales
- chopped onions
- green chillies
- mustard oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the scales from fishes, wash and scorch them equally on both sides till they are soft.
2. Remove the black crusts and the bones.
3. Mix them with chopped onions, green chillies, mustard oil and salt.
4. Serve.
