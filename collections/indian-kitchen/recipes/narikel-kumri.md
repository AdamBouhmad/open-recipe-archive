## Ingredients

- sweet gourd
- cocoanut kernel
- oil
- milk 1 powa or more
- sugar 2.5 tolas
- salt 2 tolas
- coriander 3 tolas
- cumin 1 tola
- ghee 0.5 powa
- cassia leaves
- red chillies
- garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Peel and cut a small ripe sweet gourd into small and thin pieces throwing away the interior portion.
2. Scrape out a ripe cocoanut kernel.
3. Brown 1.5 seer of the pieces of gourd in oil and add cocoanut, 1 powa or more of milk, sugar 2.5 tolas, salt 2 tolas, powdered coriander 3 tolas and cumin 1 tola.
4. When no soup remains, season the curry with 0.5 powa of ghee heated with a few cassia leaves, red chillies and powdered garam-masalla.
5. Take care that the curry may not turn black at the slightest delay or over-heating.
