## Ingredients

- 1 seer of sugar
- 1 powa of very fine flour
- sesames

## Directions

1. Prepare a very thick syrup of 1 seer of sugar and beat thoroughly adding 1 powa of very fine flour and a handful of sesames.
2. Pour it in a round flat dish to half inch thickness.
3. Sprinkle a few more sesames on the surface if you like.
4. Take the mass out when cold and solid in the form of a big disc.
