## Ingredients

- potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- milk
- butter
- nicely-browned mutton, beef, or chicken stew

## Directions

1. Boil and mash down some potatoes, with pepper, salt, milk, and butter
2. Line a pie-dish a quarter of an inch thick with the mash
3. Arrange in it a nicely-browned mutton, beef, or chicken stew, cover it over with a thick coat of the mashed potatoes, and bake for a quarter of an hour
