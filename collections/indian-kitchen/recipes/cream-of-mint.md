## Ingredients

- a quart of spirits of wine
- twenty-five drops of oil of mint
- three drops of oil of citron
- a quart of syrup
- green colouring tincture

## Directions

1. Drop into a quart of spirits of wine twenty-five drops of oil of mint and three of oil of citron
2. shake it well
3. add a quart of syrup and as much green colouring tincture as may be necessary
4. filter and bottle
