## Ingredients

- mutton fat
- powdered or pasted turmeric
- ginger
- onions
- chillies
- salt
- besam
- oil or ghee
- livers
- hearts

## Directions

1. Cleanse some fat, mix it thoroughly with powdered or pasted turmeric, ginger, onions, chillies and salt
2. Form balls
3. Dip into a moderately thick solution of besam
4. Fry in plenty of oil or ghee
5. Boil small pieces of livers and hearts
6. Mix onions, turmeric, chillies, ginger, ground to a paste, and salt with the boiled pieces
7. Fry with oil or ghee
