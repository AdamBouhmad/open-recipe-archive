## Ingredients

- one pint of water
- one and a half ounces of butter
- salt
- pepper
- four and a half ounces of flour
- one ounce of grated Parmesan
- eggs
- one ounce of lean cooked ham

## Directions

1. Put one pint of water in a stew-pan with one and a half ounces of butter, season with salt and pepper
2. Boil, and add four and a half ounces of flour and one ounce of grated Parmesan
3. Stir over the fire for three minutes
4. Add sufficient eggs to turn the mixture to a smooth paste
5. Add to the paste one ounce of lean cooked ham finely chopped
6. When mixed, form the paste into fritters
7. Fry in plenty of hot fat
