## Ingredients

- consomme
- Julienne tablet
- water or weak stock
- sugar
- table-spoonful of Madeira

## Directions

1. Make a good, strong, and clear consomme sufficient for your party
2. Cut off a portion of the Julienne tablet
3. Place it in a sauce-pan with a large allowance of boiling water or weak stock
4. Allow it to cook until the pieces of vegetable detach themselves and appear nice and tender
5. Drain them when thus ready
6. Add them to the consomme with a pinch of sugar and a table-spoonful of Madeira
