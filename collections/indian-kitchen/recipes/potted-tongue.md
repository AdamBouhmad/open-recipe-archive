## Ingredients

- one pound weight of tongue, home-cured
- four ounces of the best butter
- a salt-spoonful of pounded mace
- a coffee-cup of beef gravy
- a tea-spoonful of spiced pepper

## Directions

1. Cut off the hard skin of the tongue, and pass the meat through your mincing machine.
2. Pound it thoroughly in your mortar, adding the butter and gravy during the operation.
3. Press the meat through your wire-sieve to get rid of gristle and lumps.
4. Work the spiced pepper and mace into it, and pat it down tightly in a jar.
5. Smooth the surface.
6. Melt a table-spoonful of the same sort of butter in a sauce-pan, and pour it over the surface.
7. Let it get cold, and the work will be completed.
