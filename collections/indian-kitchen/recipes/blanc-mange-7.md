## Ingredients

- three-fourths of an ounce of isinglass
- water
- a quart of good rich milk
- a stick of cinnamon
- lemon-peel
- a few bitter almonds well pounded
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Boil isinglass till dissolved in as much water as will cover it
2. When lukewarm, add to it gradually a quart of good rich milk, with a stick of cinnamon, some lemon-peel, and a few bitter almonds well pounded
3. Sweeten to taste
4. Boil for five or six minutes, stirring all the while
5. Strain through muslin into moulds
6. Place in a pan of cold water to congeal
