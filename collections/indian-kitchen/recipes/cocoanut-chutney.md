## Ingredients

- pounded cocoanut
- minced onion
- green chilli
- green ginger
- an atom of garlic
- tamarind juice
- red pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix pounded cocoanut with minced onion and green chilli, green ginger, and an atom of garlic, moistened with tamarind juice, and seasoned with red pepper and salt.
