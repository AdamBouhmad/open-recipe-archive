## Ingredients

- six pigeons
- sausage-meat
- jelly
- parsley

## Directions

1. Bone six pigeons
2. Remove the heads and feet, stuff with sausage-meat, and roast
3. Lay the pigeons in a mould with the breasts down
4. Fill up the mould with jelly
5. When cold, turn out
6. Garnish with parsley, and some of the jelly cut fine, and sprinkled round the dish
