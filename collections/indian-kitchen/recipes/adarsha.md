## Ingredients

- 3 powas sugar
- 1 seer besam of any kind of pulse
- ghee

## Directions

1. Boil a syrup of 3 powas of sugar
2. Add a seer of besam of any kind of pulse
3. Stir vigorously till it is a very stiff dough
4. Take the pan down
5. Prepare cakes of the mass
6. Fry in plenty of ghee till brown
