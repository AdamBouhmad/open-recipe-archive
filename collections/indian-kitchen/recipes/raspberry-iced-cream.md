## Ingredients

- One tablespoonful of pounded loaf sugar
- Two tablespoonfuls of raspberry jelly or jam
- A little cochineal
- Juice of a large lemon
- Ice broken small
- Three or four handfuls of coarse salt

## Directions

1. Mix a tablespoonful of pounded loaf sugar, two tablespoonfuls of raspberry jelly or jam, and a little cochineal to heighten the colour, with the juice of a large lemon
2. Strain, and put into the freezing-pot
3. Cover it closely and place it in a bucket which has a small hole near the bottom, and a spigot to let the water run off, with plenty of ice broken small, and mixed with three or four handfuls of coarse salt
4. Press the ice closely round the freezing-pot, turn it round and round for about ten minutes
5. Take off the cover, and remove with a spoon the frozen cream to the centre
6. Cover it again, and turn it till all be equally iced
7. Serve it in china ice-pails in block, or put it into moulds, cover them securely, and replace them in the bucket, with ice and salt as before, for an hour or more
8. Dip the moulds into cold water before turning out, and serve immediately
