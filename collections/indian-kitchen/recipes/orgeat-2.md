## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound sweet almonds
- thirty bitter almonds
- one tablespoonful water
- two pints water
- three pints milk
- half a pound loaf sugar
- one pint water
- two tablespoonfuls orange-flower water
- one teacupful good brandy

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound sweet and bitter almonds with one tablespoonful of water
2. Stir in by degrees two pints of water and three pints of milk
3. Strain the whole through a cloth
4. Dissolve half a pound of loaf sugar in one pint of water
5. Boil and skim well
6. Mix with the almond-water
7. Add two tablespoonfuls of orange-flower water and one teacupful of good brandy
