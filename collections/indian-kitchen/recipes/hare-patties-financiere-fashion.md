## Ingredients

- best puff-paste
- hare
- Espagnole sauce
- oysters
- chicken
- rabbit
- tongue
- sweet-breads
- liver
- cocks-combs
- truffles
- mushrooms
- game

## Directions

1. Make enough of the best puff-paste to form eight or nine pastry cases of the usual patty shape
2. Bake them in the oven, and put them aside when done
3. As serving time approaches, fill each with a share of thoughtfully composed ragout a la financiere of hare
4. Heat in a quick oven for a minute or two, and serve
