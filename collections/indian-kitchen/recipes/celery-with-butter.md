## Ingredients

- four heads of fine white celery
- salt
- water or milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Trim four heads of fine white celery very neatly
2. Wash them carefully, and when convinced that no earth remains hidden in the leaves, boil them in salt and water, or in milk if you can afford it
3. When tender, drain, split each piece in half
4. Serve as hot as possible upon a silver dish like asparagus
5. Butter plainly melted, as for Dutch sauce, should alone accompany the vegetable thus delicately dressed
