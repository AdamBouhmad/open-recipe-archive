## Ingredients

- one perfectly ripe sweet bael
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take a perfectly ripe sweet bael, and scoop out the whole contents into a bowl
2. make a paste of it with a little water
3. then add sugar to taste, and as much water as will bring it to the consistency of good honey
4. then pass it through a fine sieve, leaving all the fibres and seeds behind
