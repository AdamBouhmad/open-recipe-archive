## Ingredients

- six ounces of lemon-peel, cut very thin and without any particle of the white skin
- eight ounces of spirits of wine

## Directions

1. Steep six ounces of lemon-peel, cut very thin and without any particle of the white skin, in eight ounces of spirits of wine well corked
