## Ingredients

- Sweet potatoes
- Salt
- Powdered rice
- Cocoa-nut
- Sugar
- Khowa kheer
- Water
- Ghee or oil

## Directions

1. Boil some sweet potatoes, peel and make a paste of them
2. Mix with salt and some powdered rice so that the paste is stiff enough to make balls of it
3. Press the balls flat
4. Stuff with a preparation of cocoa-nut and sugar as in Chandrapuli or with khowa kheer and sugar boiled in equal quantities with a little water till the paste is sticky and stiff
5. Give them a flat elongated shape when closing up
6. Fry 4 to 6 at a time with a little ghee or oil till deep brown
