## Ingredients

- pounded veal or mutton
- minced beef suet or fat of veal
- grated bread-crumbs
- fat bacon
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- beaten yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Take an equal quantity of meat, suet, and grated bread-crumbs
2. Add a bit of fat bacon chopped
3. Season with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg
4. Mix all well together with the beaten yolk of an egg
