## Ingredients

- eggs
- thin shavings of cheese
- melted butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Eggs broken gently over very thin shavings of cheese, (which have been placed in melted butter in an omelette-pan over the fire) and allowed to set, are called oeufs au fromage.
2. They should be dusted with pepper, and salt before serving.
