## Ingredients

- one seer and a quarter of soojee
- half a seer of suet
- a teaspoonful of salt
- a little yeast
- half a seer of flour for dredging and rolling

## Directions

1. To one seer and a quarter of soojee add half a seer of suet and a teaspoonful of salt
2. Thoroughly clean the suet, remove all the skin and other objectionable particles, chop, mince, and pound fine in a mortar
3. Damp the soojee for half an hour before kneading it
4. Knead it with the suet and a little of the yeast
5. Divide it into parts, dredge it with flour, and roll in layers
6. Repeat the operation two or three times, and the pastry when baked will be light and flaky
