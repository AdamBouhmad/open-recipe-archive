## Ingredients

- two tea-spoonfuls of flour
- half an ounce of butter
- a cupful of boiling water
- a table-spoonful of jam
- the juice of a lime
- a spoonful of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix flour with half an ounce of butter
2. Add a cupful of boiling water
3. Stir in jam, lime juice, and brandy
4. Simmer for five minutes
5. Mix with a little pat of butter
6. Strain and pour over and round the pudding
