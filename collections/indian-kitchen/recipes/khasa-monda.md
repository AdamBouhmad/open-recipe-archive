## Ingredients

- 1 seer of hard chhana
- 1/2 seer of sugar
- khowa kheer 2 chhatak
- 1/2 to 1 chhatak of pieces of pistachios

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1 seer of hard chhana and drop it into a boiling syrup of 1/2 seer of sugar
2. Stir vigorously
3. After a time add khowa kheer 2 chhatak
4. Take down when the mass is very thick and gluey
5. Mix with 1/2 to 1 chhatak of pieces of pistachios
6. Make balls of it when cold and serve
