## Ingredients

- 1.5 powa of sabeda
- 1 chhatak of middlings
- 1 powa of water
- 0.5 seer of hard chhana
- a pinch of saffron
- water
- ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Mix 1.5 powa of sabeda and 1 chhatak of middlings with 1 powa of water
2. Keep aside for 10 or 12 hours
3. Then mix with a fine paste of 0.5 seer of hard chhana, a pinch of saffron if desired and an adequate quantity of water making a thick paste
4. Fry Amrithi or Jilapi with this paste as before
5. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated
