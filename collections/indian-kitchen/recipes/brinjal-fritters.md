## Ingredients

- young brinjals
- salt and water
- batter
- fat
- Dutch sauce

## Directions

1. Parboil some young brinjals in salt and water, let them get cold, and slice them in longitudinal pieces of a convenient size and thickness for fritters — two inches long, and a quarter of an inch thick
2. Pick out the seeds
3. Prepare the batter, dipping the pieces of brinjal in it, and frying them a crisp golden yellow in abundance of fat
4. Drain them quite dry, dust a little salt over them, and serve them with Dutch sauce
