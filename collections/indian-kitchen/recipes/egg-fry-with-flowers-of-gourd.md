## Ingredients

- gourd flowers
- salt
- egg contents
- minced onions
- ground turmeric
- besam or rice flour

## Directions

1. Remove stalks, placentas and sepals of flowers
2. Dip into beaten egg contents with salt, minced onions, ground turmeric and besam or rice flour
3. Fry
4. Serve while hot and crisp
