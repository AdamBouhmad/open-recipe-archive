## Ingredients

- lime-juice
- double-refined sugar
- silver spoon

## Directions

1. Squeeze and strain a pint of lime-juice
2. Put into a basin one pound of double-refined sugar finely pounded and sifted
3. Add the lime-juice
4. Stir it with a silver spoon till the sugar is perfectly dissolved
5. Bottle
