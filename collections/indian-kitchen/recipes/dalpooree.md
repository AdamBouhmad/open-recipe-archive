## Ingredients

- dal chur churree
- ordinary pie pastry
- ghee

## Directions

1. Prepare a dal chur churree
2. Put it into a marble mortar, and reduce it to a fine paste
3. Prepare an ordinary pie pastry
4. Take two pieces of the prepared dough, each of the size of a walnut
5. Shape them into two small bowls
6. Take as much of the dal paste as will make a ball of the size of a walnut
7. Put it into one of the bowls of dough, and cover it over with the other bowl
8. Roll out the whole very carefully to the size of a dinner-plate
9. Fry in ghee of a very light yellow colour
10. Serve hot
