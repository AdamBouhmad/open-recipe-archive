## Ingredients

- not-very-ripe pine-apples
- sugar (equal weight to pine-apple pieces)
- cassia leaves
- cinnamon pieces

## Directions

1. Peel thickly a few not-very-ripe pine-apples, cut in very big pieces and remove the eyes and the central stems
2. Cover the bottom of a pan with cassia leaves and a few pieces of cinnamon
3. Put half the sugar on them and on the sugar put the pieces of pine-apples in a layer
4. Sprinkle the rest of the sugar above
5. Continue to heat the pan on mild heat
6. Take down when the thick syrup sticks to the sides of the pieces
