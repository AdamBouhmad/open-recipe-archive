## Ingredients

- short-bread paste
- butter
- truffles
- rich Espagnole sauce
- Madeira

## Directions

1. Line as many little open tartlet pans as you have guests with some carefully made short-bread paste, using an oval shape not much larger than the bowl of a table spoon.
2. Butter them well first to keep them from burning.
3. Preserve their hollow centres while baking them by a piece of stale bread, to be removed afterwards.
4. Cut up some truffles in dice, using enough to yield a good dessert-spoonful per head.
5. Warm the minced truffles gently in some rich Espagnole sauce flavoured with a dash of Madeira, and keep it en bain-marie.
6. When wanted, fill the crisp croustades with a spoonful each of the truffles and sauce.
7. Dish them en serviette, and send them round with the bird.
