## Ingredients

- tipparees
- sugar
- orange marmalade
- melted butter or sweet oil

## Directions

1. Take the fruit which had been boiled for jelly and pass it through a fine sieve, leaving the skins behind
2. Clean and prick a few more tipparees and add them to the strained fruit
3. Put the whole into a preserving-pan with sugar and simmer until of a sufficient consistency to make into cheese
4. Add some orange marmalade in the proportion of a tablespoonful to every mould
5. With a feather damp the moulds with melted butter or sweet oil
6. Pour into them the cheese while quite hot
7. Place them in cold water and turn out the cheeses as soon as they are cool enough to retain their shape
