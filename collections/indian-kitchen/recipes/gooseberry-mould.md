## Ingredients

- gooseberries
- gelatine
- water
- maraschino or curaçoa
- custard

## Directions

1. Turn out a basin of liquid jelly of gooseberries
2. Sweeten them to taste in their own syrup
3. Melt an ounce of gelatine in water and stir into the basin containing the fruit
4. Place this in a mould and set it to get firm in the ice-box
5. A glass of maraschino or curaçoa may be stirred into the syrup with manifest advantage
6. Serve with the best creamy custards, also set in the ice-box, in glasses
