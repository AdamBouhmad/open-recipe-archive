## Ingredients

- partridges
- bacon
- chopped mushrooms
- partridge livers
- minced shallot
- spiced pepper
- rich gravy made from their bones
- paste

## Directions

1. bone the partridges
2. lay them out flat
3. put a few thin slices of cooked bacon over them
4. strew some chopped mushrooms over that
5. add their livers chopped and a little minced shallot
6. dust with spiced pepper
7. roll the birds up and put them into the pudding basin
8. pour in a little rich gravy made from their bones
9. close the paste over them, and boil for three hours
