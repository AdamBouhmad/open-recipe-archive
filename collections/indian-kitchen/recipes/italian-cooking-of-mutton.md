## Ingredients

- meat containing enough fat
- curd
- salt
- ghee
- pastes of ginger
- onions
- garlics
- chillies
- coriander
- garam-masalla
- entire cumins
- broken chillies
- cassia leaves
- pieces of onions
- hot water
- fried pieces of almonds

## Directions

1. Mix curd, salt, ghee, pastes of ginger, onions, garlics, chillies, coriander and garam-masalla with pieces of meat, preferably from the thighs, weighing about 1 powa each
2. Keep covered for 2 or 3 hours
3. Boil enough ghee and fry some entire cumins, garam-masalla, broken chillies, cassia leaves and sufficient pieces of onions in it
4. Add the meat to these and fry for some time
5. Add hot water so that the meat is just boiled while no soup is left
6. Add a little ghee before taking down
7. Serve with fried pieces of almonds and onions
