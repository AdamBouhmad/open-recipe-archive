## Ingredients

- gravy soup or stock
- 2 carrots
- 1 turnip
- 1 onion
- 1 stick of celery
- pepper
- salt
- 1 ounce of butter
- 1 dessert-spoonful of flour
- 1 egg yolk
- milk
- fried croutons of bread

## Directions

1. Prepare early in the day the usual gravy soup, or stock
2. Take it off the fire in the afternoon, and add two carrots, a turnip, an onion, a stick of celery, and a little pepper and salt
3. Boil together
4. When thoroughly done, drain off the soup and pass the boiled vegetables through the wire sieve
5. Mix over the fire in a saucepan an ounce of butter, and a dessert-spoonful of flour
6. Gradually add vegetable pulp and stock, stirring well, until you have exhausted the whole
7. Let it come to the boil
8. Take the soup off the fire and stir in the yolk of an egg beaten up in a little milk
9. Accompany with fried croutons of bread
