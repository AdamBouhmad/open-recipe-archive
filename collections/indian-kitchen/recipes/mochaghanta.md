## Ingredients

- Banana flowers
- Water
- Salt
- Cumin
- Coriander
- Pepper
- Ginger
- Turmeric
- Milk
- Sugar
- Flour
- Bori
- Ghee
- Cassia leaves
- Powdered garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Remove the sepals and other cap-like covers of the undeveloped bunches of bananas, and the thin stick-like hard substances
2. Chop the rest throwing away the extreme ends of the bananas and the central stem
3. Chop one or two banana flowers into very fine pieces and keep them in water for a few hours
4. Boil them in fresh water and drain the water off
5. Add adequate quantities of salt, cumin, coriander, and a little pepper, ginger and turmeric, all pasted or powdered, plus a little milk, sugar and flour
6. Rub the mass thoroughly
7. Mix some browned bori broken in pieces
8. Season the mass with a little ghee heated with a few cassia leaves, add a little water and boil gently for a few minutes
9. Mix a little powdered garam-masalla when the soup is scanty, and take down
