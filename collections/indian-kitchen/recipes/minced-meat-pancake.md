## Ingredients

- minced meat
- chopped olive
- anchovy
- sausage meat
- good sauce
- eggs
- pan-cake batter
- bacon
- spiced pepper
- bread-crumbs

## Directions

1. Make your mince and flavour it with a little chopped olive, anchovy, sausage meat, &c.
2. Bind it with a little good sauce thickened with a couple of eggs
3. Let it get cold
4. Make a good sized thin pan-cake
5. Take it from the pan when almost done and put it on a dish
6. Arrange some slices of cold cooked bacon upon it
7. Lay the mince upon the bacon
8. Give it a dust of spiced pepper
9. Fold the pan-cake over it
10. Brush it over with an egg and bread-crumb it
11. Bake it a golden brown in your oven
