## Ingredients

- one seer of soojee
- four tablespoonfuls of ghee
- half a seer of sugar
- a quarter of a seer of milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix one seer of soojee with four tablespoonfuls of ghee
2. Add half a seer of sugar
3. Mix well together
4. Pour in gradually a quarter of a seer of milk
5. Add as much flour as will make a good dough
6. Knead it well, and then allow it to stand for two or three hours
7. Have some ghee melted; take the dough of the size of walnuts
8. Shape them like shells and fry them in the melted ghee until they acquire a rich brown colour
