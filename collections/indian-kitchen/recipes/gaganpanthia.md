## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of besam of gram
- 1 tola each of powders of pepper, white cumin and coriander
- 1/2 tola of salt
- water
- plenty of oil or ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Mix together thoroughly 1/2 seer of besam of gram, powders of pepper, white cumin and coriander 1 tola each and salt 1/2 tola
2. Make a stiff dough with water
3. Form finger-like thick and long sticks
4. Fry them in plenty of oil or ghee
5. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar
