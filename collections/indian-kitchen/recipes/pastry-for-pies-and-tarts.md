## Ingredients

- three ounces flour
- one ounce soojee
- two ounces beef suet
- salt

## Directions

1. Pick and clean the suet, pound it in a mortar, and make a flat circular cake of it
2. Make a dough of the flour and soojee, knead it well
3. Divide it into two equal parts, and make them into two flat circular cakes quite as large as the suet cake
4. Roll the three together, placing the suet cake between the two flour cakes
5. Double the whole up twice, and roll it out again
