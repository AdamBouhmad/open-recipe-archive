## Ingredients

- one full-sized curry chicken
- six cucumbers or a quarter of a white pumpkin
- ghee
- ground condiments
- ground hot spices
- salt
- two cups of cocoanut milk
- lemon-grass

## Directions

1. Divide the chicken as before directed
2. Remove the green skin and the part containing the seeds from the pumpkin or cucumber, then cut it up into sixteen pieces of about two inches square, and steep in water
3. Fry in the ghee all the ground condiments, including the ground hot spices
4. When brown, add the cut-up chicken and salt
5. Fry to a fine bright light brown
6. Put in the pumpkin, having previously allowed all the water to drain away through a colander
7. Pour in the two cups of cocoanut milk, the lemon-grass, and hot spices
8. Allow the whole to simmer over a slow fire for about half an hour, when the curry will be ready
9. The blade of lemon-grass is not dished up
