## Ingredients

- strong beef soup
- onions
- a chicken
- curry condiments
- two chittacks or four ounces of ghee
- tamarind
- half a dozen kurreeah fool leaves

## Directions

1. Prepare a strong beef soup
2. Slice some onions, and cut up a chicken
3. Take curry condiments as directed above, omitting the coriander and cumin seed
4. Melt two chittacks or four ounces of ghee
5. Fry and set aside the sliced onions, then fry the condiments, add the cut-up chicken, and fry that also
6. In a part of the beef soup boil a spoonful of tamarind, so as to separate the stocks and stones
7. Strain and stir it into the fried chicken
8. After a while add the remainder of the beef soup, with half a dozen kurreeah fool leaves, and the fried onions
9. Close up the pot, and continue to simmer the whole until the chicken is quite tender
10. Serve up hot
