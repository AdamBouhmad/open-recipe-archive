## Ingredients

- potatoes
- turmeric
- salt
- oil
- fishes
- coriander
- chillies
- water
- fried bori

## Directions

1. Peel some potatoes and cut them in big pieces.
2. Smear with turmeric and salt, and brown in oil.
3. Prepare fishes and fry in sufficient oil.
4. Add turmeric, coriander and a good number of chillies, all pasted, and water.
5. On boiling add salt and the potatoes.
6. Fried bori may be added if desired.
7. Take down when the soup is quite thick.
