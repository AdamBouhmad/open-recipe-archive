## Ingredients

- a quart of water
- a pound of dark brown sugar-candy
- a hundred and twenty drops of oil of bitter orange
- a quart of spirits of wine sixty-two degrees overproof

## Directions

1. Boil a quart of water in a very clean pan
2. add to it, bit by bit, a pound of dark brown sugar-candy
3. when the latter is dissolved, increase the fire and let the syrup boil up
4. pour it into a deep dish to cool
5. dissolve a hundred and twenty drops of oil of bitter orange in a quart of spirits of wine sixty-two degrees overproof
6. mix with the syrup when quite cold
7. filter and bottle the liqueur
