## Ingredients

- tamarinds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Rid the tamarinds of all the stones
2. Put a layer of sugar in a wide-mouthed bottle
3. Over it put a layer of stoned tamarinds, then another layer of sugar, and so on alternately until the bottle is full
4. Make the final layer a deep one of sugar
5. Tie the stopper down with oiled bladder
