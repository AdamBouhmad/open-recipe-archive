## Ingredients

- a dozen large tomatoes
- bread-crumb
- eggs
- pounded anchovies
- minced olives
- capers
- grated Parmesan
- butter

## Directions

1. Empty a dozen large tomatoes preserving the cases as well as you can.
2. To the pulp of the vegetable add sufficient bread-crumb to thicken it somewhat, and beat up eggs in the proportion of one egg to two cases.
3. Mix the whole thoroughly, flavour with a couple of pounded anchovies, a tea-spoonful of minced olives, and one of capers.
4. Stuff the cases, dust them over with grated Parmesan, lay them on a well-buttered dish that will stand the oven, and bake for ten minutes just before they are wanted.
5. Serve them in their own dish buried in a napkin.
