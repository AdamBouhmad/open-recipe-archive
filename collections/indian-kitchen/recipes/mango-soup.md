## Ingredients

- small and tender mangoes
- mustard seeds
- panch-forons
- oil
- 2 1/2 powas of water
- 1/2 chhatak of mustard paste
- 1 powa of mango pieces
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel small and tender mangoes and cut in small thin pieces throwing away the seeds
2. Fry a small pinch of mustards and panch-forons with a little oil and pour in 2 1/2 powas of water
3. Add a paste of 1/2 chhatak of mustard, 1 powa of the pieces of mangoes and a generous pinch of salt
4. Take down when the pieces are boiled
5. Keep the soup very thin
