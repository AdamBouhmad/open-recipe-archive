## Ingredients

- guavas (boiled for jelly)
- lime-juice
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. After all the water or juice has drained from the guavas boiled for jelly, pass the fruit or pulp through a sieve, rejecting the seeds
2. Add lime-juice and sugar to taste
3. Boil over a slow fire to a consistency stiff enough for it to remain unmoved in a spoon
4. Rub a little butter in a mould
5. Fill it with the cheese while hot
6. Place it in a heat, or in an expiring oven, to dry
7. The colour may be improved with the aid of cochineal
