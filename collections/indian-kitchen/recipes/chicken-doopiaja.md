## Ingredients

- full-sized curry chicken
- one chittack and a half or three ounces of ghee
- one breakfast-cupful of water
- one teaspoonful and a half of salt
- four teaspoonfuls of ground onions
- one teaspoonful each of ground turmeric and chilies
- half a teaspoonful of ground ginger
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground garlic
- twelve onions cut lengthways, each into six or eight slices
- half a teaspoonful of ground coriander-seed

## Directions

1. Take a full-sized curry chicken and divide it into sixteen or eighteen pieces
2. Melt the ghee in a warm or heated pot
3. Fry brown the sliced onions and set aside
4. Fry the ground condiments, stirring the whole
5. When brown, add the cut-up chicken with the salt, and fry to a rich brown
6. Chop the fried onions and put into the pot with one cup of water
7. Allow to simmer over a slow fire for about one hour, when the chicken will be perfectly tender, and the liquid reduced to a thick consistency, and to half its original quantity
