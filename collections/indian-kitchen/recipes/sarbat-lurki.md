## Ingredients

- 1 seer of curd
- 1 seer of water
- 1 1/2 powa of orange-juice
- 1/2 seer of sugar
- 4 table-spoonfuls of rose water
- fragments of ice

## Directions

1. Beat 1 seer of curd thoroughly with the hands or churn with a churn-staff
2. Strain through a clean piece of linen
3. Mix it with 1 seer of water, 1 1/2 powa of orange-juice, 1/2 seer of sugar, 4 table-spoonfuls of rose water and fragments of ice as required
