## Ingredients

- meat or bird or birds
- jelly

## Directions

1. Clean the meat or the bird or birds
2. Fully roast, bake, or stew in the usual way
3. Place the meat in the mould, or if birds, arrange them with their breasts downwards
4. Fill the mould quite full with the jelly
5. Set it to cool till the next day
6. Turn it on a dish, breasts upwards
7. Garnish the dish with curled parsley, and some of the jelly cut fine, and sprinkled about
