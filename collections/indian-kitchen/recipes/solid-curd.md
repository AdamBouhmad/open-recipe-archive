## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- sour curd

## Directions

1. Boil milk till it is reduced to half.
2. Pour it in an earthen, stone or porcelain pot.
3. Add sugar and a little sour curd when slightly warm and mix up.
4. Place the pot on another containing hot water.
5. The milk will be transformed into solid curd in about twelve hours if kept undisturbed.
