## Ingredients

- a capon
- a pint of good stock
- an onion
- carrots, sliced
- half a glass of sherry
- pepper and salt
- a few slices of fat bacon
- a couple of tablespoonfuls of minced truffle
- sippets of dry fried bacon
- slices of lime

## Directions

1. Braise the capon with a pint of good stock round it, an onion and carrots sliced, half a glass of sherry, and pepper and salt to taste
2. Pin a few slices of fat bacon over the breast and use the larding needle
3. Braise gently till done
4. Lift the capon out of the pan, strain and reduce the gravy in which it was cooked
5. Add a couple of tablespoonfuls of minced truffle and pour this over the bird before serving
6. Garnish the dish with sippets of dry fried bacon and slices of lime
