## Ingredients

- Tender pumpkin
- Water
- Cumin
- Coriander
- Mustard
- Pepper
- Ginger
- Garam-masalla
- Salt
- Milk
- Flour
- Sugar
- Ghee
- Cassia leaves
- Bori

## Directions

1. Peel and chop a very tender pumpkin into fine pieces, and boil them with a little water
2. Add suitable quantities of cumin, coriander, mustard and a little pepper, ginger and garam-masalla, all pasted together, with salt
3. Add some milk with a little flour and sugar mixed in it, and stir the mass thoroughly
4. Heat a little ghee in a pan with a few cassia leaves in it and pour in the mass and boil
5. Add browned broken bori if desired
6. Take down when a little or no soup remains
