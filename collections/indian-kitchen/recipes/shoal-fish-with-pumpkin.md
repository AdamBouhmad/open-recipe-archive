## Ingredients

- shoal fishes
- oil
- tender pumpkin
- cassia leaves
- chillies
- entire cumins
- salt
- powdered chillies
- coriander
- cumin
- pepper
- water
- ghee

## Directions

1. Throw away the heads of the shoal fishes as they are not edible.
2. Prepare the flesh portions and fry with oil.
3. Peel a tender pumpkin and chop it in pieces.
4. Heat oil, fry cassia leaves, chillies and a pinch of entire cumins in it, add the chopped pumpkin and stir.
5. A few minutes later add salt, powdered chillies, coriander, cumin, pepper and water.
6. On boiling add fish.
7. Add a little ghee when the soup is scanty and take down after stirring a little.
