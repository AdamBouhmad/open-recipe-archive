## Ingredients

- Almonds ½ powa
- Pistachios ½ powa
- Powdered sugar candy 1 powa
- Powdered cardamoms 1 tola
- Fine flour ¾ seer
- Besam of grams ½ seer
- Ghee 1 chhatak
- Water

## Directions

1. Make a paste of ½ powa of almonds and ½ powa of pistachios
2. Mix thoroughly with 1 powa powdered sugar candy and 1 tola of powdered cardamoms
3. Mix thoroughly ¾ seer of fine flour, ½ seer of besam of grams and 1 chhatak of ghee
4. Knead to a stiff paste with water
5. Make balls, stuff them with the above preparation
6. Press into round thin discs
7. Fry in plenty of ghee like Luchi
