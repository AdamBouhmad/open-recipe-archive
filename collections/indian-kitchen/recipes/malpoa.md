## Ingredients

- 0.5 seer of fine flour
- 1 powa of curd
- 1 tola of entire seeds of big cardamoms
- water
- 1 powa or a little more of ghee or oil
- 0.5 powa of paste
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Make a thick paste with fine flour 0.5 seer, curd 1 powa, entire seeds of big cardamoms 1 tola, and an adequate quantity of water
2. Boil ghee (or oil) 1 powa or a little more till the bubbles die out
3. Fry in it 0.5 powa of the paste at a time till light brown
4. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated
