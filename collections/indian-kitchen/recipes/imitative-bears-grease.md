## Ingredients

- hogs' lard, eight ounces
- flowers of benzoin, one-eighth of an ounce
- palm oil, one-eighth of an ounce

## Directions

1. Melt together hogs' lard and flowers of benzoin and palm oil until combined
2. Stir until cold
3. Scent at pleasure
