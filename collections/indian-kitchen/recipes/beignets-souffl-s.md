## Ingredients

- one pint water
- pinch of salt
- butter the size of an egg
- one tablespoonful sugar
- rasped peel of three lemons
- sufficient flour to form a thick paste
- two eggs complete
- yolks only of two more eggs
- whites of two eggs
- boiling fat
- powdered white sugar

## Directions

1. Put about a pint of water in a sauce-pan with a pinch of salt, a piece of butter the size of an egg, a tablespoonful of sugar, and the rasped peel of three lemons
2. When the whole boils, throw in gradually sufficient flour to form a thick paste
3. Let it remain ten minutes
4. Work into it, off the fire, two eggs complete, and the yolks only of two more, reserving the whites for whisking to a froth
5. Add the froth and let it rest awhile
6. Proceed to fry by dropping pieces of it the size of a walnut into boiling fat
7. The paste will swell in the process of frying, and if the fat be sufficiently heated hollow balls of a fine golden colour will be produced
8. Serve them piled upon a dish, with a plentiful dusting of powdered white sugar
