## Ingredients

- two pounds of fat beef
- one teaspoonful of ground onion
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground turmeric
- one-eighth of a teaspoonful of ground garlic
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground chilies
- half a teaspoonful of ground peppercorns
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground ginger
- half a teaspoonful of ground hot spices
- one tablespoonful of tyre or dhye
- salt
- one egg
- ghee
- plantain-leaf

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of fat beef, wash it, cut it into small pieces, and pound it to a pulp, remove all fibres.
2. Add to it one teaspoonful of ground onion, a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground turmeric, one-eighth of a teaspoonful of ground garlic, a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground chilies, half a teaspoonful of ground peppercorns, a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground ginger, half a teaspoonful of ground hot spices, and one tablespoonful of tyre or dhye.
3. Mix the whole well together, add salt to your taste, and the yolk and white of an egg well beaten up.
4. Form into balls of equal sizes; flatten them.
5. Pass them on iron or plated skewers about eighteen inches long.
6. Rub them well over with ghee, wrap them in plantain-leaf, and roast or broil them over a charcoal fire.
7. Serve them up hot, removed from the skewers.
