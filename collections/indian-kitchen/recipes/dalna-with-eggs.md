## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- ghee
- potatoes
- powdered pepper
- cumin
- coriander
- turmeric
- salt
- water
- chopped onions
- red chillies
- cassia leaves
- powdered garam masala

## Directions

1. Shell hard boiled eggs and brown entire or halved with ghee
2. Brown big pieces of potatoes
3. Add powdered pepper, cumin, coriander, turmeric and salt
4. Stir and add water
5. Add eggs when boiling
6. Fry chopped onions, red chillies and cassia leaves in ghee
7. Season curry with fried mixture
8. Boil for some time more
9. Add powdered garam masala before taking down
