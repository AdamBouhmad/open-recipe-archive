## Ingredients

- almonds
- tender minced meat
- fried and powdered khowa kheer
- besam of grams
- ginger-juice
- powdered cardamom seeds
- salt
- saffron
- water
- ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Make pastes of 1/2 powa of almonds and 1/2 seer of tender minced meat after boiling it
2. Mix these with 1 powa of fried and powdered khowa kheer, 1 seer of besam of grams, ginger-juice 1/2 chhatak, a little powdered cardamom seeds, salt, a pinch of saffron and about 3 powas of water
3. Beat thoroughly
4. Put this semi-liquid mass in a pot or linen-bag with a small hole at the bottom shutting it with a thumb
5. Bring it above a pan of boiling ghee and let the liquid fall in a stream in the pan in round spirals making any design
6. Fry both sides by turning them
7. Drop them in a syrup of sugar
8. Serve after about an hour when they are saturated with the syrup
