## Ingredients

- half a pound of curd free of the whey
- six ounces of butter
- four yolks of eggs
- sugar and nutmeg to the taste
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients well
2. Line patty-pans with puff paste
3. Fill them with the mixture
4. Bake in a quick oven
