## Ingredients

- sweet ripe mangoes
- middlings 1.5 powa
- sabeda 0.25 powa
- besam 0.5 powa
- powdered cardamom seeds
- ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Peel some sweet ripe mangoes of the best qualities and strain the juice in an enamelled pot up to 1.5 seers
2. Mix with it middlings 1.5 powa, sabeda 0.25 powa, besam 0.5 powa and a little powdered cardamom seeds
3. Beat thoroughly
4. Fry big Bonde with it and keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated
