## Ingredients

- ripe papaws
- ghee
- milk
- sugar
- raisins
- almonds
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Peel a few ripe papaws, remove the seeds, etc., and make a liquid-like paste of them.
2. Strain it through a very fine piece of linen if necessary.
3. Heat a little ghee and when the bubbles die out add the paste of papaws and stir constantly.
4. A little later add adequate quantities of milk, sugar, pastes of raisins, almonds and pistachios.
5. Take down when the mass begins to crystallise when properly thick.
