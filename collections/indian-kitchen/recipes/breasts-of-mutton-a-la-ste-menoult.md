## Ingredients

- breasts of mutton
- carrots
- onions
- spices
- gravy
- egg yolk
- grated bread
- clarified butter
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Stew them with carrots, onions, and spices in gravy, and when done drain them and take out the bones
2. Flatten the meat between two dishes, and when cold cut it into the form of cutlets or hearts
3. Brush them with the beaten yolk of an egg
4. Roll them in grated bread, then in clarified butter, and again in the grated bread
5. Bake them in an oven till of a fine brown colour, and serve them with an Italian or any other sauce
