## Ingredients

- crabs of big size
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- oil
- pumpkin
- powdered pepper
- cumin
- mustard
- cassia leaves
- red chillies
- panch-foron

## Directions

1. Shell a few crabs of big size and cut their fleshes in pieces
2. Thrash their edible legs
3. Rub them with powdered turmeric and salt and fry in oil
4. Peel and chop a very tender pumpkin into very fine pieces
5. Fry them with the brains of the crabs in some oil
6. Add salt and water will come out of the vegetable
7. When this water dries up add a little more water with powdered pepper, cumin and mustard and the fried crabs
8. After boiling for some time season the curry with some oil boiled with a few cassia leaves, red chillies and panch-foron
9. Take down when the curry is moderately thick
