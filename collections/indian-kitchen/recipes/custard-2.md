## Ingredients

- one seer milk
- one stick cinnamon
- sugar
- yolks of four eggs
- one tablespoonful rice flour
- rose water or orange-flower water or vanilla

## Directions

1. Take a seer of milk and a stick of cinnamon, and boil down to half the quantity
2. Add sugar to taste
3. Beat up quickly the yolks of four eggs, and add them gradually to the milk, stirring it continually
4. Thicken with a tablespoonful of rice flour
5. Take it off the fire, and flavour with rose water, orange-flower water, or vanilla
