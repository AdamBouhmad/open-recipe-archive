## Ingredients

- eight or ten lobsters
- red coral from the head
- bread-crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- herbs
- egg
- unroasted coriander-seed
- mustard oil
- ground onions
- ground chillies
- ground turmeric
- ground peppercorns
- ground garlic
- bay-leaves
- lemon-grass
- water

## Directions

1. According to their size, take eight or ten lobsters
2. Clean them thoroughly
3. Remove the heads and shells
4. Pull the flesh to pieces and pound to a pulp
5. Add to it some of the red coral from the head
6. Mix into it the bread-crumbs, salt, pepper, herbs, and an egg well beaten up, and make into balls
7. Bruise down the remains of the heads and the contents of the long legs with unroasted coriander-seed
8. Take all the juice and fry it in mustard oil with the ground condiments omitting the ginger, and cook the balls in the same way as the prawn balls, with the addition of bay-leaves and a few blades of lemon-grass
