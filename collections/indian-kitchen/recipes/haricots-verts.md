## Ingredients

- French beans
- good butter
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Remember not to allow your cook to cut the beans into vermicelli-like strips
2. Peel off the external fibre which runs round the outside edge of the pod, leaving the pods so peeled intact
3. Put the beans into a roomy vessel with a table-spoonful of good butter, a dessert-spoonful of sugar, and a salt-spoonful of salt
4. Cover the vessel tightly and steam it for an hour in boiling water
5. When done, drain the bean pods, and serve them with a spoonful of melted maitre d'hotel butter or a coffee-cupful of poulette sauce
