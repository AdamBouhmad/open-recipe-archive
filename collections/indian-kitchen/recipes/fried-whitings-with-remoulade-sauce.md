## Ingredients

- whitings
- butter
- shallots
- flour
- chicken stock
- marjoram
- garden cress
- parsley
- capers
- French mustard
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- salad oil

## Directions

1. Fry the whitings
2. Toss in butter a couple of shallots, finely minced
3. Add a dessert-spoonful of flour and half a pint of chicken stock
4. Take a table-spoonful of mixed marjoram, garden cress, parsley, and capers, all very finely minced, and add them to the sauce
5. Add a dessert-spoonful of French mustard, pepper, and salt to taste, and a little grated nutmeg
6. Add a dessert-spoonful of salad oil (optional)
7. When the sauce has boiled a minute or two, it is ready
8. Serve with the sauce in a boat
