## Ingredients

- pulp of mangoes (boiled for jelly)
- plenty of clean white sugar

## Directions

1. Pass through a sieve the pulp of the mangoes which had been boiled for jelly
2. Add plenty of clean white sugar, without quite destroying the acidity of the fruit
3. Boil it over a slow fire until it acquires the thickness of guava cheese
4. Bottle while it is yet warm
