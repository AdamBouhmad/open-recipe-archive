## Ingredients

- two chittacks or four ounces of soojee
- four eggs, well beaten up
- four chittacks or eight ounces of milk
- three chittacks or six ounces of ghee
- crushed crystallized sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the soojee and eggs, beating them well together
2. Gradually add the milk
3. Melt down three chittacks or six ounces of ghee in a small but deep pan
4. Pour into the boiling ghee in one spot the mixture, a dessertspoonful at a time
5. Fry until of a rich brown colour
6. Serve up hot, sprinkled with crushed crystallized sugar
