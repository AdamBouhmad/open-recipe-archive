## Ingredients

- Panifal 1 seer
- potato 1.5 powa
- ripe coconut
- handful of grams
- bori 1 chhatak
- ghee 0.5 powa
- coriander 1 tola
- salt 1 tola
- turmeric 1/4 tola
- pepper 1/4 tola
- cumin 1/4 tola
- garam-masalla 1/4 tola
- cassia leaves
- pinch of sugar
- water about 1 powa
- little milk

## Directions

1. Peel the vegetables, cut the potatoes in medium pieces and half the kernel of the coconut in small pieces
2. Slightly brown the panifal, potato, coconut and moistened grams in ghee
3. Add water with powdered turmeric, coriander, pepper, cumin and salt
4. After a time add browned bori, a little milk and a pinch of sugar
5. Season the curry when it is boiled with ghee heated with a few cassia leaves and cardamom seeds
6. Add powdered garam-masalla and take down when the soup is thick
