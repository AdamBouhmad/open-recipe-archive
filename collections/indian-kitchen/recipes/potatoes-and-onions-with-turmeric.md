## Ingredients

- potatoes
- onions
- ground turmeric
- salt
- green chillies
- oil

## Directions

1. Mix ground turmeric, salt and a few minced green chillies with the chopped vegetables.
2. Fry them in oil.
3. Stir the vegetables so that they may brown uniformly.
