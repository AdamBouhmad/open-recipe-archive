## Ingredients

- a cupful of root shavings from the moringa or drum-stick tree
- half a pint of chicken broth
- the yolks of three eggs
- a dessert-spoonful of tarragon vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- a very little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Scrape as finely as you can a cupful of the root shavings.
2. Simmer them in half a pint of chicken broth.
3. When done, thicken the broth custard-wise with the yolks of three eggs beaten up with a dessert-spoonful of tarragon vinegar.
4. Add pepper, salt, and a very little grated nutmeg, and serve in a sauce-boat.
