## Ingredients

- 1 chhatak of ripe tamarind without seeds
- 1 powa of water
- salt
- powdered turmeric
- ginger-juice
- onion-juice
- powdered pepper
- mustard oil
- 1/2 seer of small pieces of meat
- broken chillies
- cassia leaves
- ghee
- 1/2 seer of water
- browned pieces of vegetables
- sugar

## Directions

1. Keep 1 chhatak of ripe tamarind without seeds immersed in 1 powa of water for about 15 minutes
2. Squeeze them in the water and throw away the refuse
3. Mix salt, powdered turmeric, ginger-juice, onion-juice, a little powdered pepper and mustard oil with 1/2 seer of small pieces of meat
4. Fry broken chillies and cassia leaves in enough ghee
5. Fry the meat in it for about 15 minutes
6. Add about 1/2 seer of water and when this water nearly dries up add the tamarind solution
7. Add browned pieces of vegetables if desired
8. Add sugar as desired about 5 minutes before taking down
9. Keep as thick soup
