## Ingredients

- mature yet green mangoes
- sugar - 1.5 times the weight of the pulp
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Remove the stalks of a few mature yet green mangoes, wash and boil them.
2. Squeeze the boiled mangoes and strain the pulp through a clean piece of linen.
3. Mix it with 1.5 times its weight of sugar or add it to a boiling thick syrup of the sugar.
4. Stir constantly till the mass is thick when taking down mix with a little lemon-juice.
