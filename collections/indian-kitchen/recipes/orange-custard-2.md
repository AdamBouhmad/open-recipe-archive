## Ingredients

- rind of half an orange
- one spoonful brandy
- juice of one orange
- four ounces loaf sugar
- yolk of four eggs
- one pint boiling milk
- preserved orange-peel

## Directions

1. Boil very tender the rind of half an orange, and beat it in a mortar until it is very fine
2. Put to it a spoonful of the best brandy, the juice of an orange, four ounces of loaf sugar, and the yolk of four eggs
3. Beat them altogether for ten minutes
4. Pour in by degrees a pint of boiling milk
5. Beat them until cold
6. Put them in custard-cups into a dish of hot water
7. Let them stand till they are set
8. Take them out, and stick preserved orange-peel on the top
