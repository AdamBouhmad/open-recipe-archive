## Ingredients

- an ounce and a half of isinglass
- three-quarters of a pound of fine white sugar
- a pint of water
- orange and lemon peel
- juice of ten oranges
- juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Melt an ounce and a half of isinglass and three-quarters of a pound of fine white sugar in a pint of water
2. Add some orange and lemon peel
3. Boil until it is a good syrup
4. While warm, add the juice of ten oranges and two lemons
5. Strain the whole through flannel
6. Put it into moulds
