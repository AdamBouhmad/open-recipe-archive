## Ingredients

- half a dozen tender cobs of Indian corn
- a large pat of fresh butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Choose half a dozen tender cobs of Indian corn, they should be still greenish otherwise they will be too tough
2. Boil them, and then with a silver fork, strip the corn from the cob
3. When wanted for the table, melt a large pat of fresh butter at the bottom of a sauce-pan
4. Empty the corn into the pan, stir it about adding butter, pepper and salt, till it is smoking hot
5. Serve
