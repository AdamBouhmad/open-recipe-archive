## Ingredients

- bruised fresh-gathered mint-leaves
- a bottle of beer
- a soda-water bottle of sparkling lemonade

## Directions

1. Put some bruised fresh-gathered mint-leaves into a large tankard
2. pour over them a bottle of beer well iced, and a soda-water bottle of sparkling lemonade, also well iced
