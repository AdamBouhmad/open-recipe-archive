## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- fine stale bread-crumbs
- olives
- capers
- anchovy
- hot melted butter or salad oil

## Directions

1. Arrange your pieces of cauliflower as before explained
2. Strew over them some fine stale bread-crumbs, with some olives, a few capers, and an anchovy chopped up small
3. Pour over this a cupful of hot melted butter
4. Bake for ten minutes
5. Serve
