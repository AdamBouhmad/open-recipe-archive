## Ingredients

- meat from breast
- bread crumbs
- salt
- powdered pepper
- cumin
- garam-masala
- butter
- mustard

## Directions

1. Tie up some big pieces of meat from the breast in a piece of cloth and boil.
2. Mix the boiled pieces with bread crumbs, salt, powdered pepper, cumin and garam-masala, and fry them with butter.
3. Stir constantly till brown.
4. Serve with mustard.
