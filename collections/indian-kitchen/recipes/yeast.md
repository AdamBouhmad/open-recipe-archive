## Ingredients

- one pound of good flour
- a quarter of a pound of brown sugar
- a little salt
- two gallons of water

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of good flour, a quarter of a pound of brown sugar, and a little salt in two gallons of water for one hour
2. when milk-warm, bottle it close
3. it will be fit to use in twenty-four hours
