## Ingredients

- pomfret
- prawns
- anchovy
- spice
- pepper
- salt
- melted butter
- flour
- broth

## Directions

1. Boil the pomfret
2. Boil and clean a dozen fair sized prawns
3. Pound the prawns to a paste in a mortar
4. Flavour with an anchovy, a little spice, pepper, and salt
5. Work the mixture with melted butter, flour, and a little broth to the consistency of mayonnaise sauce
6. Serve as hot as possible
