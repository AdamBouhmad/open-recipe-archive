## Ingredients

- 1 seer of hard chhana
- 1/2 to 1 powa of khowa kheer
- 1 seer of sugar syrup
- 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose
- a little powdered cardamom seeds
- pieces of pistachios

## Directions

1. Prepare sandesh with hard chhana 1 seer, khowa kheer 1/2 to 1 powa and a syrup of 1 seer of sugar
2. When it is prepared mix with 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose and a little powdered cardamom seeds
3. Add pieces of pistachios as desired
4. Form balls of it
5. Throw them with force on a flat plate
6. Join two together on the flattened sides while they are yet warm
7. They will stick to each other when cold
