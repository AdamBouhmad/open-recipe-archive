## Ingredients

- Whites of four eggs
- Three-quarters of a pound of double-refined sugar, pounded and sifted through a lawn sieve
- Juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Beat till very light the whites of four eggs, and add gradually three-quarters of a pound of double-refined sugar, pounded and sifted through a lawn sieve
2. Mix in the juice of half a lemon
3. Beat it till very light and white
4. Place the cake before the fire, pour over it the icing, and smooth over the top and sides with the back of a spoon
