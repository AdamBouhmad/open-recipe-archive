## Ingredients

- one pound loaf
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a pound loaf
2. When the bread is all but done, take it from the oven
3. Tear the crumb from the inside with a fork in irregular pieces
4. Place them on a buttered baking tin
5. Crisp them in the oven
