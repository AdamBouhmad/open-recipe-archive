## Ingredients

- thick pieces of a fish weighing 1 or 2 seers
- powdered turmeric
- mustard
- salt
- enough chillies
- mustard oil
- banana leaf

## Directions

1. Rub some thick pieces of a fish weighing 1 or 2 seers with powdered turmeric, mustard, salt, enough chillies and mustard oil
2. Arrange them in one layer on a piece of banana leaf smeared with mustard oil
3. Cover with another banana leaf and bind with a thread
4. Heat it in a frying pan on charcoal fire
5. Turn it over when one side is cooked
6. Use plenty of oil
