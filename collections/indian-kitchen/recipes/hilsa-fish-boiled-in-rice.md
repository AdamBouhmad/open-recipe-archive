## Ingredients

- hilsa fish
- salt
- powdered mustard
- turmeric
- mustard oil
- green chillies
- banana leaves

## Directions

1. Do not wash the fish after cutting it to pieces, wash after flaking
2. Rub the pieces of fish with salt, powdered mustard, turmeric, a quantity of mustard oil and a few green chillies cut lengthwise
3. Wrap them up with banana leaves and boil
4. Tender leaves of gourd or pumpkin may be substituted for banana leaves and may be eaten with fish
5. Or, break the stone of a half-ripe cocoa-nut in two and drain off the water
6. Stuff it with the pieces of fish rubbed with the spices, etc.
7. Join the two parts with flour-paste and heat uniformly on all sides on charcoal fire
8. Or, wrap the pieces, after rubbing them with spices, etc., with banana leaves and put the bundle in the middle of a mass of hot boiled rice whose starchy water has just been drained off
9. Take out after 15 or 20 minutes
10. Or, put the pieces in a shallow pot after preparing them
11. Close it with a lid and place on boiling water
