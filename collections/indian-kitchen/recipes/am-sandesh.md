## Ingredients

- one seer of brownish powder-like sugar (kasir chini)
- one seer of hard chhana
- cardamom-seeds
- pieces of almonds
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Prepare a syrup of one seer of brownish powder-like sugar (kasir chini) and cool it.
2. Drive out the whey from chhana keeping it under high pressure for a few hours.
3. Make a fine paste of one seer of hard chhana without whey by pasting with the hands on a wooden plate.
4. Heat the syrup in a thick iron pot and when it is warm add the pasted chhana.
5. Stir constantly and vigorously with a wooden ladle for the whole sandesh will taste bitter if only slightly burned.
6. When the mass is sticky, take the pot off fire, place on a circular stand of straw and stir as before, adding cardamom-seeds, pieces of almonds and pistachios if desired, till cold.
7. Smear the interior of a mango-shaped cast with the juice of a kind of ginger scented like green mangoes each time as a ball of the sandesh is pressed into the cast to give the desired shape.
8. Transfer on a wooden plate when moulded into shapes.
9. Keep them in earthen pots.
