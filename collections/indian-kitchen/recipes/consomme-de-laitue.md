## Ingredients

- clear consomme
- one large or two small cabbage lettuces
- a couple of slices of bacon
- stock
- two cloves
- an onion
- a tea-spoonful of sugar
- one tea-spoonful of salt
- a tea-spoonful of dried herbs

## Directions

1. Make a clear consomme as usual
2. Pick and wash one large or two small cabbage lettuces
3. Dip them into boiling water for a few minutes
4. Take them out and cut them into quarters
5. Tie them together again
6. Butter a stew-pan and place a couple of slices of bacon at the bottom
7. Lay the lettuces on them and cover them with stock
8. Add two cloves, an onion, a tea-spoonful of sugar, one tea-spoonful of salt, and a tea-spoonful of dried herbs
9. Simmer the lettuces until done
10. Take them out, drain them, and when dry, cut them into shreds with a dessert-knife
11. Put the shredded pieces at the bottom of your tureen
12. Pour the consomme, boiling hot, over them and serve
