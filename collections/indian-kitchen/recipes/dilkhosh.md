## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 seers pure milk
- crystalline sugar
- cardamom seeds
- essence of rose

## Directions

1. Boil 3 or 4 seers of pure milk
2. Make the heat very mild so that the milk may no longer boil
3. Fan the surface of the milk with a small hand fan when creams will form
4. Remove the creams constantly to the sides of the boiling pan with a thin stick till the formation of creams ceases
5. Remove the pan off fire
6. Transfer the creams on a plate
7. Beat thoroughly adding requisite quantities of crystalline sugar, cardamom seeds and essence of rose
8. Form balls of the paste and serve
