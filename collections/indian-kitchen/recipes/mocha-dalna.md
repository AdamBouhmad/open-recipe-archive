## Ingredients

- banana flowers
- coriander
- red chillies
- pepper
- cumin
- turmeric
- salt
- ghee
- oil
- cassia leaves
- potatoes
- grams
- cocoanut kernel
- browned broken bori
- milk
- sugar
- garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Chop one or two banana flowers into very fine pieces, boil them and drain off the water.
2. Mix powdered coriander, red chillies, pepper, cumin, turmeric and salt in adequate quantities with the boiled mass.
3. Boil some ghee or oil with a few cassia leaves and a pinch of cumin, and add pieces of potatoes and a handful of moistened grams.
4. Add the mocha when the potatoes are slightly browned, stir for a few minutes, and pour in water.
5. When it boils add scraped out cocoanut kernel, browned broken bori, a little milk and sugar.
6. Take down after adding powdered garam-masalla.
