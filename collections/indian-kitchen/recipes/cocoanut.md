## Ingredients

- cocoanut kernel
- turmeric
- salt
- oil
- potatoes
- pepper
- cumin
- coriander
- ginger
- water
- ghee
- red chillies
- cassia leaves
- garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Take out the kernel of a ripe cocoanut, peel off the skin, and cut in small pieces.
2. Mix turmeric and salt, and brown a little with oil.
3. Add an equal quantity of browned pieces of peeled potatoes.
4. Add powdered pepper, cumin, coriander, ginger, salt and water in adequate quantities.
5. Season the curry when it is boiled with ghee, or oil heated with a few red chillies and cassia leaves.
6. Add powdered garam-masalla and take down when no soup is left.
