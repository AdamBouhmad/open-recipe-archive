## Ingredients

- 1 seer of the pieces of a big fish
- salt
- turmeric
- ginger
- turmeric
- cumin
- pepper
- coriander
- garam-masala
- a pinch of sugar
- ghee
- 0.5 powa of ghee
- 2 seers of flour
- water

## Directions

1. Boil 1 seer of the pieces of a big fish with salt and turmeric
2. Remove the bones
3. Add ginger, turmeric, cumin, pepper, coriander, garam-masala, salt and a pinch of sugar, all powdered
4. Beat thoroughly and brown a little with ghee
5. Mix 0.5 powa of ghee with 2 seers of flour
6. Add water and knead it to a moderately soft lump
7. Form small balls of it
8. Flatten them a little and stuff with a little of the spiced fish
9. Press these stuffed balls into thick round discs
10. Fry in plenty of ghee
11. Serve while hot
