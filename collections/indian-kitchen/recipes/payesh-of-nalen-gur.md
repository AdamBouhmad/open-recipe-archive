## Ingredients

- 1/2 powa of rice
- 1 chhatak of ghee
- 1 seer of boiling milk
- 1 powa of finest nalen gur
- raisins, pieces of almonds and pistachios
- powdered cardamoms
- very little camphor

## Directions

1. Fry 1/2 powa of rice with 1 chhatak of ghee and drop them in 1 seer of boiling milk.
2. Stir constantly.
3. When the rice are fully boiled add 1 powa of finest nalen gur prepared from the juice of date palm.
4. Then add raisins, pieces of almonds and pistachios as desired.
5. Take down when thick and mix with a pinch of powdered cardamoms and very little camphor.
