## Ingredients

- loaf
- water
- eggs
- powdered pepper
- salt
- pasted onions
- green chillies
- meat
- powdered biscuits
- ghee
- mustard
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Keep 3 or 4 slices of loaf immersed in water for 1 or 2 minutes
2. Squeeze the water out and make a paste of these with eggs, powdered pepper, salt, pasted onions and green chillies
3. Cut 1 seer of meat into 10 or 12 slices
4. Thrash both sides of all the slices with a stone and keep them mixed up with the pasted spices for about an hour
5. Roll them on powdered biscuits and fry in plenty of ghee
6. Serve with mustard, slices of boiled potatoes and onions
