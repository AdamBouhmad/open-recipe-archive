## Ingredients

- contents of a few eggs
- salt
- ginger-juice
- onion-juice
- powdered red chillies
- turmeric
- pepper
- cumin
- cardamom
- fried pieces of almonds
- flour
- beaten egg-contents
- ghee
- mustard
- chopped onions
- pieces of cucumber

## Directions

1. Take the contents of a few eggs and mix salt, ginger-juice, onion-juice, powdered red chillies, turmeric, pepper, cumin and cardamom, and fried pieces of almonds.
2. Add a little flour, if necessary, and beat thoroughly to make it a muddy paste.
3. Form cakes, dip them into beaten egg-contents and fry in plenty of ghee.
4. Serve while hot with mustard, chopped onions, pieces of cucumber, etc.
