## Ingredients

- spinach
- salt
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. After being nicely picked and well washed, put it into a saucepan, with no more water than adheres to it
2. Add a little salt
3. Cover the pan closely, and boil it till tender, frequently shaking it
4. Beat it quite smooth, adding butter and cream, and make it quite hot
5. Spinach may be served with poached eggs, or fried sausages laid on it
