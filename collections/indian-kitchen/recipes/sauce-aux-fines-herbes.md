## Ingredients

- a pint of milk
- a minced Bombay onion
- a tiny bit of garlic
- a handful of parsley
- a couple of ounces of butter
- two ounces of flour
- a table-spoonful of minced curled parsley
- a dessert-spoonful of chopped garden cress
- half a dessert-spoonful of chopped green stem of spring onion
- a squeeze of a lime

## Directions

1. Flavour a pint of milk by boiling up in it a minced Bombay onion, a tiny bit of garlic, and a handful of parsley
2. When well flavoured, strain the milk through muslin
3. Stir it by degrees into a sauce-pan in which a couple of ounces of butter and two of flour have been mixed
4. Thicken gently by bringing the mixture to the boil
5. Strain
6. Add, just before serving, a table-spoonful of minced curled parsley, a dessert-spoonful of chopped garden cress, and half one of chopped green stem of spring onion
7. A squeeze of a lime may be judiciously added to this sauce
