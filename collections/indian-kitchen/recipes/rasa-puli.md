## Ingredients

- Powdered rice
- Boiling water
- Kheer or cocoa-nut preparation
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough of powdered rice with boiling water, adding the necessary amount of water at a time
2. Make medium balls
3. Stuff them with kheer or the preparation of cocoa-nut
4. Give them the shape of an almond
5. Fry in ghee
6. Immerse in a syrup of sugar till they are saturated
