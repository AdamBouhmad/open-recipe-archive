## Ingredients

- a dozen good-sized tomatoes
- ground chilies
- ginger
- salt
- half a teaspoonful of good mustard oil
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Bake in an oven a dozen good-sized tomatoes until the skin cracks
2. Break them down and mix with them a little ground chilies, ginger, salt, and half a teaspoonful of good mustard oil
3. A small squeeze of lemon juice may be added if desired
