## Ingredients

- ghee
- cassia leaves
- cloves
- chopped ginger
- pieces of meat without bones
- salt
- pasted coriander
- water
- pasted poppy seeds
- almonds
- fried and powdered khowa kheer
- curd
- sugar
- boiling ghee
- powdered garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Heat enough ghee, fry cassia leaves, cloves and chopped ginger in it and add pieces of meat without bones.
2. Stir till the meat turns reddish brown.
3. Add salt, pasted coriander, and sufficient water.
4. On softening add pasted poppy seeds, almonds, fried and powdered khowa kheer, curd and sugar.
5. Boil till the soup is very thick.
6. Finish by adding boiling ghee and powdered garam-masalla.
