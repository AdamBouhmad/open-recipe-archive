## Ingredients

- boiled and peeled potatoes
- powdered pepper
- white of eggs
- salt
- eggs
- ginger-juice
- onion-juice
- powdered turmeric
- chillies
- pieces of almonds
- raisins
- minced meat
- fried and powdered khowa kheer
- curd
- sugar
- lemon-juice
- powders of garam-masalla
- powders of cumin
- ghee
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Make a paste of some big boiled and peeled potatoes and mix thoroughly with powdered pepper, the white of eggs and salt
2. Mix eggs, ginger-juice, onion-juice, powdered turmeric, chillies, pieces of almonds and raisins with minced meat, and keep aside for an hour
3. Fry the spiced meat with ghee, form flat oval cakes of it
4. Dip into beaten eggs and fry in plenty of ghee
5. Give a thick coating of the pasted potatoes over these chops, dip again into beaten eggs, roll on bread crumbs and fry in plenty of ghee
