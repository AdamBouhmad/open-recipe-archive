## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of pulses of grams
- 1 1/2 powa of ghee
- saffron
- powdered cardamoms
- 1/2 seer of sugar

## Directions

1. Keep 1/2 seer of pulses of grams moist for 5 or 6 hours and make a paste of them.
2. Fry the paste with 1 1/2 powa of ghee to a reddish brown colour.
3. Then add a pinch of saffron, another of powdered cardamoms and syrup of sugar 1/2 seer.
4. Stir incessantly till quite thick when transfer it in a flat plate smeared with ghee and cut in cubes when congealed.
