## Ingredients

- a pound of puff-paste
- butter
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Make very carefully a pound of puff-paste, following the directions given at page 263
2. Give the paste six turns, and roll it out three-quarters of an inch thick
3. Cut out of this as neatly as possible an oval piece the size you wish your vol-au-vent to be
4. Turn it over upon a buttered baking-sheet
5. Brush the surface and side with a beaten egg, and mark out the interior oval, leaving an inch margin all round
6. Let the knife cut this tracing to a depth of a quarter of an inch
7. Put the sheet in the oven, and when the paste is baked, remove the inner oval (for a cover) which you will find has risen
8. Scoop out the uncooked paste inside the case
9. Brush the whole case thus formed with egg again, and bake it for about five minutes
