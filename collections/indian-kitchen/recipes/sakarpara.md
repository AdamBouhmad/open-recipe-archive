## Ingredients

- 1 powa almonds
- water
- 1 powa cream of milk
- ½ powa ghee
- 1 seer fine flour
- ghee
- 1 powa sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Keep 1 powa of almonds moist for a couple of hours
2. Peel them and cut in pieces
3. Make a paste of them with the necessary amount of water
4. Mix this paste with 1 powa of cream of milk and ½ powa of ghee with 1 seer of fine flour
5. Beat to a stiff dough adding a little water if necessary
6. Prepare small cubes of it
7. Fry in plenty of boiling ghee to a light brown colour
8. Smear them with a very concentrated syrup of 1 powa of sugar
