## Ingredients

- young full-sized bringals
- mustard oil
- ground turmeric
- ground chilies
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the bringals thoroughly and slice them about an eighth of an inch thick
2. Dry them
3. Steep them for half an hour in the ground condiments and salt
4. Fry in oil
5. Serve up hot
