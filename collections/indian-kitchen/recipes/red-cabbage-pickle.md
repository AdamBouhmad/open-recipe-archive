## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- boiling vinegar
- red beetroot

## Directions

1. Slice the cabbage, and sprinkle salt over each layer
2. After twenty-four hours remove it into a colander, and allow all the salt water to drain
3. Put the cabbage into a pan, pour in sufficient boiling vinegar to cover it, and add a few slices of red beetroot
4. When cold, put it into glass bottles and cork down
