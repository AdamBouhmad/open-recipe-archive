## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- sugar
- green mint
- shelled peas
- butter
- finely-pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Put one quart of water with a teaspoonful of salt, one of sugar, and half an ounce of green mint on the fire
2. When it boils, pour in a pint measure of shelled peas
3. Boil quickly
4. When done, drain and turn them out upon a frying-pan with an ounce of butter
5. Sprinkle a little salt and finely-pounded sugar over them
6. Work the pan till the butter melts and is blended with peas
7. Empty them upon a hot dish and serve
