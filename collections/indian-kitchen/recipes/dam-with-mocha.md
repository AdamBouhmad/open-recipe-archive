## Ingredients

- Chopped mocha 0.5 seer
- ghee 1 chhatak
- curd 1 chhatak
- salt 1 tola
- coriander 1/4 tola
- cumin 1/4 tola
- ginger 1/4 tola
- poppy-seed 1/4 tola
- garam-masalla 1/4 tola
- sugar 1/4 tola
- pepper 1/2 tola
- turmeric 1/2 tola
- cassia leaves

## Directions

1. Keep the chopped mocha in water for a few hours
2. Bind them with a few cassia leaves in a clean piece of cloth and suspend the ball in a pan half full of water so that it does not touch the bottom
3. Cover the lid and boil for half an hour
4. Boil some ghee in a pan, add ground poppy-seed, ginger, coriander, pepper, cumin and last of all the boiled mocha in it
5. Stir for a few minutes
6. Add turmeric, salt, curd, sugar and a little water
7. Cover the lid and boil on mild heat
8. Boil some ghee and fry the powdered garam-masalla in it, and season the curry with this
9. Stir thoroughly and take down
