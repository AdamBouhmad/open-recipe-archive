## Ingredients

- pastry
- bandecai forcée
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Line four little patty pans with pastry as laid down for cheese fingers
2. Bake and cool them
3. When ready, fill each case with a spoonful of bandecai forcée as explained for bandecai toast
4. Heat thoroughly in the oven dusting a little grated cheese over the surface of each croustade
5. Serve on a napkin
