## Ingredients

- Fried moog-dal i seer
- Rice |- seer
- Ghee i powa
- Potato ^ seer or more
- Onion |- seer
- Raisins ^ powa
- Almonds i chhatak
- Ginger 1 chhatak
- Pistachios ^ chhatak
- Salt ^ chhatak
- Cumin i tola
- Turmeric i tola
- Pepper i tola
- Garam-masalla ^ tola
- A few cassia leaves
- Red chillies
- One ripe cocoa-nut (if desired)
- Seeds of cardamoms
- Pieces of cinnamon
- Entire cloves

## Directions

1. Drop rice when the water boils
2. Drop pulse when the rice are half boiled
3. Then add salt and browned pieces of onions and potatoes
4. After this add browned pieces of pistachios, almonds and raisins
5. The pasted kernel of a ripe cocoa-nut or its milk may be added now if desired
6. Add pepper, cumin, turmeric and some red chillies, all powdered, when the boiling is almost complete
7. Then heat the ghee with broken red chillies, seeds of cardamoms, pieces of cinnamon, entire cloves and cassia leaves
8. Add this to the khechuri when the smell evolves
9. Pour in the juice of the ginger just before taking the boiling pot off the fire
10. Stir continually after adding the spices
