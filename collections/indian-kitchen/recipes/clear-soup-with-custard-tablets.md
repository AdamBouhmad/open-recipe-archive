## Ingredients

- consomme for eight covers
- yolks of four eggs
- water
- salt
- cochineal
- spinach-greening
- butter
- parsley
- shallot
- Parmesan

## Directions

1. Mix the yolks of four eggs with a little water and a pinch of salt
2. Strain the mixture
3. Divide it into three equal portions
4. Colour one with cochineal, one with spinach-greening, and leave the third plain
5. Pour them into three little moulds previously buttered
6. Dip them into a pan of hot water
7. Steam just long enough to set the custards
8. Take them off the fire, and when cold, turn the moulds out on a napkin
9. Cut them up into dice or any pretty shapes with your vegetable cutter as gently as possible
10. Add them to the soup just before serving
11. Hand round grated Parmesan
