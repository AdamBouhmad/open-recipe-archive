## Ingredients

- salsify roots
- cream
- a tea-spoonful of anchovy sauce
- bread-crumbs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Boil the roots until they peel easily
2. Mash with cream, and a tea-spoonful of anchovy sauce
3. Strew bread-crumbs over it, and add a little melted butter
4. Bake till brown, and serve in coquilles
