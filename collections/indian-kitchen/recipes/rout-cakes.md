## Ingredients

- one pound of ground almonds
- one pound of powdered sugar
- yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Add one pound of powdered sugar to one pound of ground almonds
2. Mix them together with yolks of eggs to a stiff, yet flexible paste
3. Form it into small biscuits in the shape of coronets, shells, filberts, birds' nests, rings, or any other fancy shapes
4. Let them remain five or six hours, or all night, upon the baking-tin in a warm oven
