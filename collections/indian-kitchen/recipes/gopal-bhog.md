## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of fine besam of moog
- 1 powa of fine flour
- 1 powa of sugar
- 1/2 powa of middlings
- 1/2 powa of scraped and pasted cocoa-nut kernel
- 1 chhatak of entire black sesames
- 4 tola of powdered garam-masala
- 1/2 pinch of camphor
- hot water
- ghee or oil
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take fine besam of moog 1/2 seer, fine flour 1 powa, sugar 1 powa, middlings 1/2 powa, scraped and pasted cocoa-nut kernel 1/2 powa, entire black sesames 1 chhatak, powdered garam-masala 4 tola and a half pinch of camphor, and mix together.
2. Make a stiff paste with hot water.
3. Make balls of it.
4. Fry in ghee or oil.
5. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar for 12 to 24 hours.
