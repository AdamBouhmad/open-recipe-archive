## Ingredients

- coffee berries
- water

## Directions

1. Roast the berries with care
2. Cast the berries into a large metal mortar and pound them to a very fine powder
3. Carefully sift the powder through a fine sieve, rejecting all coarse particles
4. Boil the required amount of water in a small copper can with a narrow top and broad bottom
5. When the water boils, add powdered coffee according to requirements and replace the can on the fire
6. Permit the liquid to come to the boil three times, taking the can off the fire for a while after each boiling
7. After the third boiling, place the can for a minute in a shallow vessel containing cold water to precipitate the grounds
8. Pour the clear, black, and sparkling coffee into the cup
