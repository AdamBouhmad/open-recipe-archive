## Ingredients

- three seers or six pounds of peeled and sliced mangoes
- two pounds of ground raisins
- a pound of ground garlic
- half a pound of ground chilies
- a pound and a half of ground ginger
- a pound of sugar
- two pounds of salt
- a quart of lime-juice
- six quarts of vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix all the above well together, put it into stone jars, and expose it to the sun for twenty days or a month
2. After which drain away the liquid, which is the sauce
3. Boil it for ten to fifteen minutes, and when cold bottle and cork it
