## Ingredients

- a couple of brinjals
- butter
- cream
- white pepper
- salt
- anchovy sauce
- grated Parmesan or any dry mild cheese

## Directions

1. Boil a couple of nice brinjals till tender
2. Cut them in halves lengthways
3. Scoop out the inside with a silver spoon
4. Pass it through the sieve to get rid of the seeds and put it into a bowl
5. Butter the now empty cases or pods
6. Stir into the inside part that you scooped out a good spoonful of cream
7. Season with white pepper, salt, and few drops of anchovy sauce
8. Mix thoroughly and re-fill your cases
9. Shake over the surface a layer of grated Parmesan or any dry mild cheese
10. Bake for seven or eight minutes and serve
