## Ingredients

- crabs
- salt
- onion
- ginger juice
- lime-juice
- pepper
- mushroom catsup
- butter
- stock
- bread-crumbs
- coral

## Directions

1. Clean and boil the crabs in salt
2. Remove them out of the shells and pick and clean them well, and reserve the coral for dressing
3. Chop and mince fine the crabs
4. Add some onion and ginger juice, a little lime-juice, pepper, and salt, and a little mushroom catsup
5. Melt some butter, and fry the mixture in it until the butter be absorbed
6. Add a little stock, and remove from the fire immediately the stock begins to dry
7. Butter the shells, and fill with the mixture
8. Take some finely-sifted bread-crumbs and grind down the coral, and put it over the mixture on the shells, with the bread-crumbs, and bits of butter
9. Bake for a few minutes
