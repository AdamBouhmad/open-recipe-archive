## Ingredients

- 1 chhatak of rose petals
- 2.5 powas of water
- 1 seer of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 1 chhatak of rose petals in 2.5 powas of water
2. Throw away the petals when they are fully boiled
3. Add 1 seer of sugar
4. Take down when the syrup is prepared
