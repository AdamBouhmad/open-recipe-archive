## Ingredients

- kheer 1/2 seer
- sugar 3 chhataks
- powdered cardamoms 1/4 tola
- powdered sugar-candy 1/2 powa
- pastes of almonds and pistachios 1/2 chhatak each
- minced almonds and pistachios
- saffron
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Heat kheer and 3 chhataks of sugar
2. Stir till the mass is non-sticky
3. Take down and mix with powdered cardamoms, powdered sugar-candy and pastes of almonds and pistachios
4. Make small balls of it
5. Stuff with minced almonds and pistachios
6. Immerse in a syrup of sugar coloured with saffron
