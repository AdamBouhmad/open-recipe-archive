## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer hard chhana
- 1/2 seer khowa kheer
- 1/2 powa middlings
- 1/4 chhatak almonds
- powdered aniseeds 1/4 tola
- powdered cardamom seeds
- milk
- sugar-candy 1/2 powa
- raisins one chhatak
- essence of rose 2 or 3 drops
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1/2 seer of hard chhana and mix thoroughly with khowa kheer 1/2 seer, middlings 1/2 powa, a paste of 1/4 chhatak of almonds, powdered aniseeds 1/4 tola and a little powdered cardamom seeds
2. Make a paste soft but stiff enough to be made into balls with a little milk if necessary
3. Make big balls (10 or 12 in number)
4. Stuff them with a mixture of broken pieces of sugar-candy 1/2 powa, raisins one chhatak and 2 or 3 drops of essence of rose
5. Drop them into a boiling syrup of sugar
6. Take the pan off fire when they are well boiled and sink down
7. Serve a few hours later
