## Ingredients

- above three pints of clear consommé
- two ounces coriander seed
- one ounce cummin seed
- one ounce fenugreek or maythee
- half an ounce mustard seed
- two cloves garlic
- dozen black peppercorns
- four or five leaves hurreapliool or Izodia neem

## Directions

1. Put coriander seed, cummin seed, fenugreek, mustard seed, garlic cloves, black peppercorns, and hurreapliool leaves into a muslin bag without pounding or bruising
2. Boil the bag with the soup
3. Remove as soon as the flavour is satisfactory
4. Finish with a few drops of tabasco or chilli-vinegar if tabasco be unobtainable
