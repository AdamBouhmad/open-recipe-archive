## Ingredients

- best dried Patna chilies
- salt
- vinegar
- muslin

## Directions

1. Pick one chittack or two ounces of the best dried Patna chilies
2. Expose them to a light sun for an hour
3. Pound them to as fine a powder as possible
4. Put the powder into a stoppered bottle with a teaspoonful of salt
5. Pour over it as much vinegar only as will form a limp paste
6. Expose it to the sun for a few days
7. Pass it through muslin, adding to it as much more vinegar as will reduce it to the consistency of some thick sauce
