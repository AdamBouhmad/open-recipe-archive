## Ingredients

- two table-spoonfuls of vinegar
- salt and pepper
- two table-spoonfuls of water
- two yolks of eggs
- four ounces of butter

## Directions

1. reduce two table-spoonfuls of vinegar on the fire with a little salt and pepper added to it, till about a tea-spoonful remains
2. strain, and add to it two table-spoonfuls of water, and two yolks of eggs
3. put this on the fire and heat it thoroughly, stirring it well with a wooden spoon, and add four ounces of butter ounce by ounce by degrees, with a little water now and then to prevent its curdling
4. This process had better be carried out in a bain-marie, for you thus obtain the amount of gentle heat which is necessary to preserve the sauce in a velvety condition without risk of any kind
