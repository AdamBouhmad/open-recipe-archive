## Ingredients

- turnips 1/2 seer
- potatoes 1 powa
- small shrimps 1/2 seer
- oil 1/8 powa
- ginger 1/2 chhatak
- turmeric 1 tola
- cumin 1 tola
- pepper 1 tola
- salt 1/8 tolas
- panch-foron
- cassia leaves
- chillies
- ghee
- garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Peel the turnips and potatoes, cut in small pieces and brown them.
2. Prepare the shrimps and fry them.
3. Add water and the spices pasted together to the fried shrimps.
4. On boiling add salt and the browned vegetables.
5. When fully boiled season the curry with ghee boiled with panch-foron, cassia leaves and chillies.
6. Add pasted garam-masalla and boil a few minutes more.
7. Keep a little very thick soup.
