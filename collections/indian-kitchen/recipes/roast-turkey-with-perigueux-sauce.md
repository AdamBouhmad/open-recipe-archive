## Ingredients

- turkey
- Perigueux sauce
- cauliflower
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Choose a nice turkey
2. Prepare the bird for roasting, stuffing it very carefully as directed on page 100
3. Let sauce Perigueux (page 97) steaming hot, be passed round in a boat
4. Accompany with cauliflower, and potatoes
