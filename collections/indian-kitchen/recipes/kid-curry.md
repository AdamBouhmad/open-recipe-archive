## Ingredients

- a hind-quarter or a fore-quarter of kid
- ground onions
- ground turmeric
- ground chilies
- ground ginger
- ground garlic
- ghee
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the kid into sixteen or eighteen pieces
2. Take condiments in the proportion given in recipe No. 16
3. Cook it in every particular the same as the chicken curry
4. Allow it to simmer three-quarters of an hour
