## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- potatoes
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- ghee
- red chillies
- peas
- ground red chillies
- pepper
- cumin
- water

## Directions

1. Shell some hard-boiled eggs and cut in small pieces.
2. Cut some potatoes in small pieces.
3. Smear powdered turmeric and salt with them separately.
4. Brown the potatoes fully and the eggs slightly in ghee.
5. Boil ghee with a few red chillies and add fully moistened big sized peas.
6. Add ground red chillies, turmeric, pepper and cumin, and stir.
7. Add water and salt after a time.
8. Add potatoes and eggs on boiling.
9. Add powdered garam-masalla and take down when no soup is left.
10. Serve while hot.
