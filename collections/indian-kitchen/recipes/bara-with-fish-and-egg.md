## Ingredients

- pieces of fish
- boiled potato
- powdered pepper
- chillies
- salt
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- ghee

## Directions

1. Boil pieces of fish and remove the bones.
2. Mix boiled and pasted potato about a third of the quantity of fish, proper quantities of powdered pepper, chillies and salt with the boiled fishes.
3. Form cakes, dip into beaten egg-contents, roll on bread crumbs and fry in ghee.
