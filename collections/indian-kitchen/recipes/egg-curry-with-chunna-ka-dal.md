## Ingredients

- chunna ka dal, half a cupful
- eggs, hard-boiled

## Directions

1. Parboil the dal
2. Curry the dal first
3. When about nearly cooked, throw in the hard-boiled eggs
4. Finish the simmering immediately the dal is soft or tender
