## Ingredients

- Jerusalem artichokes
- batter
- fat

## Directions

1. Boil half a dozen large artichokes until three parts done
2. Cut into long strips about a quarter of an inch thick
3. Dip them in batter
4. Fry them a golden tint
5. Serve alone as an entremets, or pile them in a pyramid as the central garnish of an entree
