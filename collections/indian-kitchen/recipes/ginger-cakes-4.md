## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- three-quarters of a pound of good moist sugar
- one ounce of the best Jamaica ginger
- three-quarters of a pound of lard melted
- four eggs well beaten

## Directions

1. In two pounds of flour well mix three-quarters of a pound of good moist sugar and one ounce of the best Jamaica ginger
2. Have ready three-quarters of a pound of lard melted, and four eggs well beaten
3. Mix the lard and eggs together and stir into the flour, which will form a paste
4. Roll out in thin cakes and bake in a moderately heated oven
