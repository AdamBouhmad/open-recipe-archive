## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer hard chhana
- essence of rose
- powdered cardamoms
- khowa kheer
- sugar syrup
- white sugar crystals

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1/2 seer of hard chhana and make balls of it
2. Stuff each of them with a drop of essence of rose, a pinch of powdered cardamoms and a little khowa kheer
3. Slightly flatten them
4. Drop them in a boiling syrup of sugar
5. Take them out when they are a little brownish in colour
6. Roll on white sugar crystals
7. Serve when cool
