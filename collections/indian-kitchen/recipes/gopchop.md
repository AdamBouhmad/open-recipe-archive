## Ingredients

- 1 seer fine flour
- ½ seer sugar syrup
- ½ seer ghee
- essence of rose

## Directions

1. Mix together fine flour 1 seer, a syrup of ½ seer of sugar, ghee ½ seer and 3 or 4 drops of essence of rose
2. Prepare balls of the mass
3. Arrange them in a big metal dish
4. Cover it by another of the same size
5. Close the join with some flour paste
6. Place the whole thing on burning coals
7. Surround it on all sides and above with more burning coals
8. Remove the ashes when the coals are burnt up
9. Serve the balls when cool
