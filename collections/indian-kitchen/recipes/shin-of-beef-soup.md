## Ingredients

- a shin of beef
- pepper
- salt
- water
- soup herbs
- white of an egg
- a tablespoonful of Lea & Perrin's Worcestershire sauce
- half a wineglassful of sherry

## Directions

1. Take a shin of beef, cut it up small, wash it thoroughly, and boil with pepper and salt in sufficient water to well cover the meat
2. Let it boil over a brisk fire, taking away the black scum
3. Add a little cold water, and skim off the white scum
4. Reduce the fire, and allow the soup to simmer until it somewhat thickens
5. Strain the soup, cut away all the fat, season with soup herbs, and add more pepper and salt if necessary
6. Give it a good boil up, and then clear it with the white of an egg well beaten up
7. After which add a tablespoonful of Lea & Perrin's Worcestershire sauce, and half a wineglassful of sherry
