## Ingredients

- almonds
- pistachios
- khowa kheer
- rose essence
- flour
- ghee

## Directions

1. Make a stiff paste with almonds and pistachios of khowa kheer without adding water
2. One or two drops of essence of rose may be added
3. Make very small balls of it and roll them into thin discs
4. Prepare a bit bigger discs of flour as in the preparation of luchi
5. Place a disc of kheer between two of flour and close the ends of the flour discs
6. Fry as luchi in plenty of ghee
