## Ingredients

- four ounces of fine stale crumbs
- half a pint of boiling milk
- four ounces more of crumbs
- four ounces of dry, finely minced suet
- pinch of salt
- three ounces of crushed ratafias
- three ounces of candied peel
- lemon essence
- five eggs
- four ounces of sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Grate four ounces of fine stale crumbs and put them in a basin
2. Pour over them half a pint of boiling milk
3. Cover the basin with a plate and let the liquid soak into the crumbs and get cold
4. Stir into the basin four ounces more of crumbs, four ounces of dry, finely minced suet, a pinch of salt, three ounces of crushed ratafias, three ounces of candied peel sliced thin, and a few drops of lemon essence
5. Whisk five eggs well with four ounces of sugar, continue to beat them together until the sugar is dissolved
6. Pour the eggs into the basin with the other ingredients and mix well
7. Turn all into a buttered basin and boil for two hours
