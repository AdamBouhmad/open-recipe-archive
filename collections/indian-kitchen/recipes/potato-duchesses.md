## Ingredients

- potatoes
- good milk
- butter
- yolks of two eggs
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- parsley or celery leaves
- marjoram leaves
- a beaten egg

## Directions

1. Mash potatoes up with a little good milk and butter and the yolks of two eggs
2. Add pepper, salt, some grated nutmeg, and some parsley or celery leaves chopped fine (a few marjoram leaves are an improvement)
3. Roll the mixture into balls the size of a pippin, brush them over with a beaten egg, set them on a buttered tin, and bake for a few minutes till they turn a golden yellow colour
