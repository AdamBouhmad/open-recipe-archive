## Ingredients

- crabs
- salt
- onions
- butter
- ginger-juice
- lemon-juice
- powdered pepper
- water
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Shell a few crabs and keep their fleshes aside for a while after mixing with salt
2. Keep the brains in a pot and thoroughly clean the shells
3. Cut the fleshes in small pieces and fry them in butter with slices of onions, ginger-juice, lemon-juice, powdered pepper, salt and the brains
4. Add a little water and take down when boiled
5. Stuff the shells with this mass, bread crumbs and butter
6. Apply heat under the shells for some time
