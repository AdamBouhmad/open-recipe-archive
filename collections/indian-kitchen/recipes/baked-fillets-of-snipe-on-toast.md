## Ingredients

- eight snipes
- stock
- vinegar
- red currant jelly
- sherry
- eight pieces of fried bread
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- crisply fried bread-crumbs
- Nepaul pepper

## Directions

1. Slightly roast eight snipes
2. Fillet them, saving the trails
3. Make the richest sauce you can of the bones, moistened with stock and helped up with vinegar, red currant jelly, and a little sherry
4. Prepare eight pieces of fried bread for the eight breasts and butter them
5. Spread the trails over them, pepper and salt them
6. Place a breast of snipe upon each trail toast
7. Bake till quite hot
8. Just before serving, pour your thick rich sauce, reduced almost to a glaze, over them
9. Let crisply fried bread-crumbs surround your toasts
10. Use Nepaul pepper
