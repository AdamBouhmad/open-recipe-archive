## Ingredients

- beef
- paste
- kidney
- cold cooked bacon
- spiced pepper
- gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into thin collops
2. cut the kidney into strips
3. place a thin slice of cold cooked bacon and kidney over each collop
4. season with spiced pepper and roll each collop up
5. Line the pudding basin with paste
6. fill it with layers of collops
7. pour in among the collops a little gravy
8. close the paste securely and boil for three hours
