## Ingredients

- ½ seer khowa kheer
- ½ seer sabeda
- ½ powa besam
- powders of pepper ½ tola
- powders of cardamom ½ tola
- saffron
- water
- ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Make a moderately thick paste of khowa kheer ½ seer, sabeda ½ seer, besam ½ powa, powders of pepper and cardamom ½ tola each and very little saffron with water
2. Beat thoroughly
3. Fry this paste in very fine drops in plenty of boiling ghee till reddish brown in the same way as Mihidana
4. Take them out with the same perforated ladle
5. Drop in a syrup of sugar
6. Take them out again when saturated
7. Form into big balls like Mihidana
