## Ingredients

- green tender ends of lau plant with leaves
- salt
- mustard oil
- posta (poppy-seed)

## Directions

1. Tie green tender ends of lau plant with leaves together with a thread.
2. Boil in rice.
3. Keep posta moistened in water for some time and paste.
4. Take in a paste condition with salt and mustard oil.
5. Add a little pasted posta if desired.
