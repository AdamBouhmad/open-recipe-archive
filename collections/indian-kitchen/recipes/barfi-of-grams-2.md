## Ingredients

- 1.5 seer of pulses of grams
- 1.5 powa of ghee

## Directions

1. Keep 1.5 seer of pulses of grams moist for 5 or 6 hours
2. Make a paste of them
3. Fry the paste with 1.5 powa of ghee
