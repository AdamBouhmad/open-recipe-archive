## Ingredients

- cocoa-nut and shrimps ball
- ghee
- liquid egg
- salt
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare a ball of cocoa-nut and shrimps, cut it in rectangular pieces and brown them in ghee.
2. Dip the browned pieces into liquid egg-contents beaten with salt, roll on bread crumbs and fry to crispness in ghee.
