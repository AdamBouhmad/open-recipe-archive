## Ingredients

- lobsters
- salt
- powdered turmeric
- ginger-paste
- browned pieces of onions
- powdered garam-masalla
- pepper
- lemon-juice
- patols
- flour-paste
- eggs
- besam
- rice-flour
- cream of milk

## Directions

1. Scale some lobsters, wash, rub with salt and powdered turmeric, and fry
2. Make a paste of them on a curry-stone
3. Mix ginger-paste and again fry with some browned pieces of onions
4. Mix powdered garam-masalla, pepper and lemon-juice with them
5. Remove the soft skin off some patols by rubbing with a knife
6. Cut one of their ends and remove the seeds with a pin
7. Stuff them with the pasted spiced fish or meat
8. Close the ends with a little flour-paste or stitch them up
9. Dip them into eggs beaten with a little besam, rice-flour, cream of milk and salt
10. Fry to crispness in plenty of ghee
