## Ingredients

- four young, full-grown pigeons
- salt
- finely-sifted pepper
- two tablespoonfuls of ghee or lard
- cupful of beef stock
- tin of petit pois with their gravy

## Directions

1. Kill and feather four young, full-grown pigeons without plunging into hot water, taking care not to break their skins
2. Singe them to destroy any remaining feathers
3. Wash them in three or four cold waters
4. Cut them in halves
5. Dredge them well with salt and finely-sifted pepper
6. Allow to remain for an hour
7. Boil up two tablespoonfuls of ghee or lard
8. Fry the birds to a rich brown, turning them over
9. When sufficiently browned, put in a cupful of beef stock
10. Allow to simmer until the birds are quite tender
11. Pour over them a tin of petit pois with their gravy
12. Serve up hot
