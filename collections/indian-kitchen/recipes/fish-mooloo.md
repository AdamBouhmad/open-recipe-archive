## Ingredients

- fish
- cocoanut
- hot water
- green chilies
- onions
- garlic
- ghee
- vinegar
- ginger
- whole pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry the fish and let it cool
2. Scrape a cocoanut and put a teacupful of hot water into it
3. Rub it well and strain it and put aside
4. Then put two spoonfuls more of water and strain this also
5. Cut up three or four green chilies and as many onions as you like, with half a garlic
6. Fry them with a little ghee
7. Whilst frying put the last straining of the cocoanut water in with the ingredients till it is dry
8. Then add the first water of the nut
9. Pour the whole over the fish, with some vinegar, ginger, whole pepper, and salt to your taste
