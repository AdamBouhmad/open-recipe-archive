## Ingredients

- young turnips
- water
- salt
- butter
- milk
- flour
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash, peel, and boil them till tender in water with a little salt
2. Serve them with melted butter poured over them, or
3. They may be stewed in a pint of milk thickened with a bit of butter rolled in flour, and seasoned with salt and pepper, and served with the sauce
