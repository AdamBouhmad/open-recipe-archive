## Ingredients

- sugar
- eggs
- ghee

## Directions

1. Mix some sugar with the contents of a few eggs and beat thoroughly
2. Smear the interior surface of an enamelled pot with ghee and pour the liquid into it
3. Place this pot on hot water to coagulate the liquid
4. Then cut it into cubes and immerse in a syrup of sugar
