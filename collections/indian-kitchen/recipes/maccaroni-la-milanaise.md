## Ingredients

- two ounces of maccaroni
- a breakfast-cupful of good chicken broth
- an onion
- sweet herbs
- black pepper corns
- an ounce of butter
- a dessert-spoonful of flour
- two ounces of grated cheese or Parmesan from the bottle
- a tea-spoonful of powdered mustard
- salt
- spiced pepper
- a table-spoonful of cream or the yolks of two raw eggs beaten separately

## Directions

1. Boil two ounces of maccaroni, and keep it hot in its own pan after draining.
2. Make a breakfast-cupful of good chicken broth flavoured with an onion, sweet herbs, and black pepper corns.
3. Melt an ounce of butter in a sauce-pan, stir into it a dessert-spoonful of flour, mix them to a paste, and by degrees pour in the hot chicken broth.
4. As this is thickening, add to it two ounces of grated cheese or Parmesan from the bottle, a tea-spoonful of powdered mustard, salt, and spiced pepper, at discretion.
5. Continue to stir the sauce until it reaches a creamy thickness, when you can finish it off the fire by a table-spoonful of cream, or the yolks of two raw eggs beaten separately.
6. Stir the sauce into the hot boiled maccaroni, and serve immediately.
