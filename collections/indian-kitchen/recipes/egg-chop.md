## Ingredients

- potatoes
- egg contents
- minced onions
- besam
- ginger juice
- salt
- powdered pepper
- red chillies
- beaten egg
- powdered biscuits or bread crumbs
- ghee

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes, peel and rub to paste
2. Mix with equal quantity of egg contents
3. Add minced onions, besam, ginger juice, salt, powdered pepper and red chillies
4. Mix thoroughly
5. Form cakes
6. Dip into beaten egg
7. Roll on powdered biscuits or bread crumbs
8. Fry in plenty of boiling ghee
