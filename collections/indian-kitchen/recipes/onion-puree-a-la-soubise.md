## Ingredients

- three onions
- stock or milk
- coffee-cupful of cream or milk
- egg yolk
- gravy
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Simmer three onions in sufficient stock or milk to cover them till tender
2. Mash them, and pass them through the sieve
3. Work into the pulp a coffee-cupful of cream, or milk enriched with the yolk of an egg, with a little gravy
4. Flavour with a little pepper, and salt
5. Heat it up as hot as possible, and serve
