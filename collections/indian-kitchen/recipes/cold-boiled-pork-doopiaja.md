## Ingredients

- remains of cold boiled pork
- sixteen one-inch square pieces

## Directions

1. Cut from the remains of cold boiled pork sixteen one-inch square pieces
2. Doopiaja it in the way directed for a chicken
3. The time required to simmer will not exceed that allowed for the chicken doopiaja
