## Ingredients

- fish
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- oil
- saks
- potato
- patol
- brinjal
- bean
- radish
- gourd
- cauliflower
- squash
- jhinga
- chichinga
- bori
- powdered mustard
- chillies
- coriander
- pepper
- cumin
- ghee
- powdered garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Fry the waste portions of big fishes in the same way as in other curries
2. Prepare 1 or 2 kinds of saks
3. Cut some other unpeeled vegetables in pieces and wash
4. Mix with powdered turmeric
5. After some time brown them in oil
6. Add sak and fish and stir
7. A little later add water, powdered mustard, chillies, coriander, pepper, cumin and salt
8. Take down when no soup is left
9. Ghee and powdered garam-masalla may be added if desired
