## Ingredients

- rolled rib meat
- egg
- dry cheese
- bread-crumbs
- maccaroni
- melted butter
- breakfast-cupful of rich tomato puree
- two table-spoonfuls of grated Parmesan or Gruyere
- butter
- flour
- bouillon
- one spoonful of Marsala
- one tea-spoonful of red currant jelly

## Directions

1. After draining the rolled rib meat and letting it get cold, place it on a dish that will stand the oven
2. Brush it over with egg, and dredge over it a coating of very finely grated dry cheese and bread-crumbs in equal proportions
3. Set it in the oven to heat thoroughly, and brown the outside of the crumbs with a hot iron used salamander fashion
4. Put the meat on a dish, and trim round it a circle of hot maccaroni freshly boiled, drained, and tossed in melted butter
5. Pour a breakfast-cupful of rich tomato puree with two table-spoonfuls of grated Parmesan or Gruyere over the maccaroni
6. Make a nice thick brown gravy with butter, flour, and some of the bouillon, flavoured with a spoonful of Marsala and a tea-spoonful of red currant jelly, and serve in a boat
