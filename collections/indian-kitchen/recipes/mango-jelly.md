## Ingredients

- a hundred green mangoes
- weak lime-water
- cold water
- good clean white sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and stone a hundred green mangoes, and cut each into four
2. Throw them as they are ready into a solution of weak lime-water, strained of all sediment
3. When all have been peeled and stoned, remove them into a large vessel
4. Pour in as much cold water as will entirely cover them
5. Boil them until they are quite dissolved
6. Carefully strain the liquid without pressing the fruit
7. Let it drip all night
8. Boil the juice again in an open preserving-pan, and cut away the scum as it rises
9. Add gradually good clean white sugar until it is sweetened to taste
10. Continue to boil steadily until the scum has ceased to rise, and the jelly is quite clear and transparent
11. Allow some of it to drop on a plate and cool
12. If it congeals, remove the pan and fill the bottles while the jelly is slightly warm
13. Cork down when quite cold
