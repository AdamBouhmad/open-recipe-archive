## Ingredients

- chicken
- ox tongue
- six mutton cutlets from the neck
- chopped parsley
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Cut up a chicken as if for curry
2. Slice up a cold-boiled, home-cured tongue
3. Trim six nice mutton cutlets from the neck as if for an entree
4. Throw the chicken scraps, the tongue skin and trimmings, and all the remnants of mutton left after shaping the cutlets, into a large sauce-pan with the materials for gravy flavouring
5. Make a nice broth with them
6. When the broth is nearly ready, give it half a glass of Madeira, and strain when it is finished
7. Pack the pie thus: a dust of chopped parsley at the bottom, then the mutton cutlets, above them the slices of tongue, and the chicken at the top
8. Pour in the gravy, garnish the top with hard-boiled eggs
9. Cover the pie with a good crust, and bake
