## Ingredients

- 1 powa middlings
- 1/2 powa ghee
- liquid contents of 8 ducks' eggs
- 1/2 powa sugar
- very little saffron
- powdered garam-masalla
- very little camphor

## Directions

1. Brown 1 powa of middlings with 1/2 powa of ghee and cool them a little.
2. Add the liquid contents of 8 ducks' eggs beaten with 1/2 powa of sugar and very little saffron.
3. Heat on mild charcoal fire.
4. Add powdered garam-masalla and very little camphor when the mass is thick, and stir on till the desired thickness is reached.
5. Egg-halua is now ready to be served.
