## Ingredients

- twenty-five Barcelona nuts
- a dozen Jordan almonds
- two chittacks or four ounces of butter free of water
- four tablespoonfuls of finely-sliced onions
- one dozen oysters
- half a dozen boiled potatoes
- a pork or beef sausage
- the rind of a fresh lemon
- the crumb of a slice of stale bread
- some garden herbs
- grated nutmeg
- finely-pounded pepper
- salt
- stock
- the juice of a lemon
- a little mushroom catsup
- port wine
- an egg

## Directions

1. Break, blanch, and slice up very fine twenty-five Barcelona nuts and a dozen Jordan almonds, and set aside
2. Fry, in two chittacks or four ounces of butter free of water, four tablespoonfuls of finely-sliced onions
3. Add to it one dozen oysters, half a dozen boiled potatoes broken up small, but not mashed, a pork or beef sausage broken up small, the rind of a fresh lemon finely sliced and chopped, the crumb of a slice of stale bread grated fine, some garden herbs, grated nutmeg, plenty of finely-pounded pepper, and salt to taste
4. Add the sliced nuts, and as much stock as will cover the whole of the mixture
5. Allow it to simmer over a slow fire until it is reduced to the consistency of stuffing
6. Add the juice of a lemon and a little mushroom catsup and port wine
7. Fix the stuffing with an egg and stuff the bird
