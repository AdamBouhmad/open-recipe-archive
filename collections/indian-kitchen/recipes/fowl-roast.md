## Ingredients

- fowl
- curd
- salt
- onion paste
- ginger paste
- chilli paste
- coriander
- cumin
- garam-masala
- minced meat
- powdered pepper
- almonds
- boiled potatoes
- white of boiled eggs
- ghee
- onion pieces
- cassia leaves
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare a fowl, domestic or wild, and thrust the neck, legs, etc. into the body.
2. Smear it with curd, salt and a paste of onions, ginger, chillies, coriander, cumin and garam-masala.
3. Its bowels may be stuffed, if desired, with these spices or with minced meat fried with salt, powdered pepper, pieces of almonds, boiled potatoes and the white of boiled eggs.
4. Boil some ghee, fry a few pieces of onions and cassia leaves in it and then the fowl till deep brown.
5. Add a little hot water and cover up.
6. Take down when the meat is soft and the soup is very thick.
