## Ingredients

- one quart of spirits of wine sixty-two degrees overproof
- twenty drops of good essential oil of bitter almonds
- six drops of oil of orange
- one quart of syrup

## Directions

1. To a quart of spirits of wine sixty-two degrees overproof add twenty drops of good essential oil of bitter almonds and six drops of oil of orange
2. Shake it well
3. Add a quart of syrup
4. Filter it through paper until it is quite clear
