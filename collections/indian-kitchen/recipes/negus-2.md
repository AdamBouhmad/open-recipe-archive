## Ingredients

- two quarts claret or one quart port
- one wineglassful brandy
- two limes
- slight grating of nutmeg
- few cloves
- cardamoms
- sticks of cinnamon
- two teacupfuls boiling water
- two tablespoonfuls sugar

## Directions

1. To claret or port add brandy, limes cut into thin slices, nutmeg, cloves, cardamoms, and cinnamon
2. Add boiling water and sugar
