## Ingredients

- 3 powas of sugar
- 1/2 seer of hard chhana
- 1 powa of khowa kheer
- 1 chhatak of almonds
- 1/2 chhatak of pistachios
- 1/2 tola of powdered cardamom seeds
- sugar

## Directions

1. Heat syrup of 3 powas of sugar and when slightly warm add a paste of 1/2 seer of hard chhana.
2. Stir vigorously till sticky.
3. Add pastes of khowa kheer 1 powa, almonds 1 chhatak, pistachios 1/2 chhatak and powdered cardamom seeds 1/2 tola, and mix thoroughly.
4. Take off the fire.
5. Mix a handful of sugar and stir as before.
6. Make balls and press in casts when cold.
