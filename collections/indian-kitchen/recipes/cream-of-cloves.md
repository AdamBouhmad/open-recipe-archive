## Ingredients

- one quart of spirits of wine of the strength of sixty-two degrees overproof
- forty drops of oil of cloves
- one quart of syrup
- yellow colouring matter

## Directions

1. To a quart of spirits of wine of the strength given in the foregoing recipe add forty drops of oil of cloves
2. Shake it well
3. Mix with it a quart of syrup, and as much yellow colouring matter as will give it a good colour
4. Filter through filtering-paper
5. Bottle immediately
