## Ingredients

- forty tomatoes
- soup herbs
- salt
- stock
- a dessertspoonful of arrowroot or corn or other flour
- a teaspoonful of good English vinegar

## Directions

1. Take forty tomatoes (halved)
2. Add some soup herbs and salt
3. Boil them in a little stock
4. Strain through a sieve
5. Replace on the fire
6. Thicken with the addition, more or less, of a dessertspoonful of arrowroot or corn or other flour, to obtain any required consistency
7. Finally add a teaspoonful of good English vinegar
8. If a sharper gravy sauce be desired, instead of the vinegar add either a dessertspoonful of tapp sauce
