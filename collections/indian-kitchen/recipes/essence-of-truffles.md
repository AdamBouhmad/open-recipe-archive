## Ingredients

- the contents of a small bottle of truffles
- a pint of clear chicken broth
- a glass of Madeira
- some sweet herbs
- pepper
- salt
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take the contents of a small bottle of truffles, and boil them for twenty minutes briskly in a pint of clear chicken broth, flavoured with a glass of Madeira, some sweet herbs, pepper, salt, and a little nutmeg.
2. Remove the sauce-pan from the fire, and when the liquid is cold, pick out the truffles (they will do for any other dish), and strain the cold essence.
