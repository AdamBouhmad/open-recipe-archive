## Ingredients

- two anchovies
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- half a teaspoonful of flour
- yolks of three eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of tarragon vinegar
- parsley
- green onions
- a bay-leaf
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash and bone two anchovies
2. Rub them up in a mortar with a quarter of a pound of butter and half a teaspoonful of flour
3. Put these into a small saucepan
4. Add to the yolks of three eggs well beaten up
5. Add two tablespoonfuls of tarragon vinegar
6. Add a small bunch of sweet herbs, consisting of parsley, green onions, and a bay-leaf
7. Add a little salt, pepper, and nutmeg
8. Stir these over the fire until the sauce is thick, being careful not to let it boil
9. Serve it up in a sauce-tureen
