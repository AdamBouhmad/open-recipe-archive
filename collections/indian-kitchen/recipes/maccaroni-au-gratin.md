## Ingredients

- maccaroni
- butter
- black pepper
- salt
- one large cupful of sauce blonde
- two ounces of grated cheese

## Directions

1. First boil the maccaroni in boiling water, or in milk or stock if preferred
2. Well butter a pie-dish
3. Arrange the maccaroni therein neatly
4. Give it a dusting with black pepper and salt
5. Pour round it a large cupful of good sauce blonde in which you have mixed two ounces of grated cheese
6. Let this run well in amongst the bed of maccaroni
7. Shake over the surface a liberal coating of grated cheese
8. Make this thoroughly hot in the oven
9. Brown the surface of the cheese by passing a hot iron about half an inch above it
10. Send it up
