## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- butter
- salt
- sweet onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Mash the potatoes thoroughly
2. Work them through a wire sieve to get them smooth
3. Add a little milk, as much butter as you can spare, and a little salt
4. Form with the wooden spoon
5. Brown the outside in the oven
