## Ingredients

- a goose
- apple or tomato sauce
- potatoes
- Brussels-sprouts
- salt
- Nepaul pepper
- mixed mustard
- a glass of portwine

## Directions

1. Pick, draw, singe, truss, and stuff the goose
2. Roast it before a clear fire
3. When done, cut a slice into the breast and let the gravy run out for a moment
4. Add to the gravy a tea-spoonful of salt, a salt-spoonful of Nepaul pepper, a tea-spoonful of mixed mustard, and a glass of portwine
5. Stir these ingredients into the gravy
6. Baste the bird well with it and continue with the carving
7. Serve with either apple or tomato sauce in a boat
8. Serve with potatoes and Brussels-sprouts if in season
