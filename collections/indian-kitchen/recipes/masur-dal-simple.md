## Ingredients

- Masur dal 1/2 seer
- ghee 1 chhatak
- curd 1 chhatak
- ginger 1 tola
- pepper 1/2 tola
- cumin 1/4 tola
- turmeric 1/4 tola
- cassia leaves 4 in number
- panch-foron 1 pinch
- salt 1 1/2 tola
- water 1 1/2 seer
- fenugreek 1 pinch

## Directions

1. Boil the washed dal in water.
2. Add powdered turmeric and cassia leaves when it starts boiling.
3. Add ginger, pepper and cumin, all of them pasted or powdered, and curd and salt when the pulses are boiled.
4. Churn the liquid so that the pulses melt thoroughly.
5. Take the pan down.
6. Heat the ghee in another pan with the panch-foron and fenugreek in it.
7. When they are fried pour in the liquid dal.
8. Boil for a few minutes more and take down.
