## Ingredients

- young moringa pods
- a table-spoonful of cream or a coffee-cupful of milk
- yolks of two eggs
- salt and pepper
- a few drops of anchovy essence
- Parmesan or mild dry cheese

## Directions

1. Buy enough young moringa pods to yield seeds enough to fill a little pie-dish
2. Boil them and scrape out the seeds and tender flesh inside the pods into a basin
3. Stir into this a table-spoonful of cream or a coffee-cupful of milk in which the yolks of two eggs have been well beaten
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Add a few drops of anchovy essence
6. Pass this into a well buttered pie-dish
7. Grate over the surface a good layer of Parmesan or any nice mild dry cheese
8. Bake for a quarter of an hour and serve
