## Ingredients

- fine rice, 1 seer
- pure cow-milk, 1½ seers
- ghee, ½ seer
- meat, 1 seer
- cloves, ½ anna
- cinnamon, 1 anna
- cardamom, 1 tola
- cardamom big quality, 8 in number
- mace, 1 tola
- coriander, ½ chhatak
- cassia leaves, 1 tola
- white pepper, ½ tola
- cubebs, ½ tola
- pepper with cumin seeds, ½ tola
- raisins, 1½ chhatak
- pistachios, 1 chhatak
- almonds, ½ powa
- sugar, 1 chhatak
- curd, ½ powa
- milk condensed, ½ powa
- salt, 1 chhatak
- saffron, ½ tola
- water, 4½ seers
- ginger, ½ powa

## Directions

1. Wash the rice in water and dry them up.
2. Keep the rice in the milk for about 1½ hours till they absorb the milk to the maximum.
3. Tie the swelled rice loosely in a piece of clean cloth and allow to cook.
