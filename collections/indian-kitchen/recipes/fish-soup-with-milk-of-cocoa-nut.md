## Ingredients

- cocoa-nut
- water
- fish
- oil
- flour solution
- cassia leaves
- salt
- ghee
- cardamom seeds
- ginger
- chillies
- cloves

## Directions

1. Scrape out the kernel of a ripe cocoa-nut and make a paste of it
2. Add sufficient boiling water and churn
3. Strain out the milk and throw away the refuse
4. Fry the pieces of fish in oil
5. Add the vegetable milk, a little solution of flour and cassia leaves
6. Add salt when the fishes are boiled
7. Season the soup with boiling ghee with cardamom seeds, minced ginger, chillies and cloves fried in it
