## Ingredients

- thin pancake
- bacon
- mince
- egg
- cold sauce
- butter
- egg for brushing
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Picture to yourself a nicely-made thin pancake
2. spread it out upon a flat dish, and cut it into pieces two inches wide, and three inches long
3. Upon the surface of each piece, place a thin slice of bacon slightly smaller each way than the crepe
4. over the bacon put a tablespoonful of any nice mince, well worked with an egg or two, and a little cold sauce to give it moisture and cohesion
5. roll up your crepes, put them on a buttered tin
6. brush them with a whipped egg
7. bread-crumb them, and bake brown in the oven
