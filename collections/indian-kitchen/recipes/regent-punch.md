## Ingredients

- a quart of sparkling champagne
- a claret glassful of brandy
- a wineglassful of old Jamaica rum
- a pint of very strong pure green tea
- capillaire or any other syrup

## Directions

1. Mix a quart of sparkling champagne, a claret glassful of brandy, a wineglassful of old Jamaica rum, and a pint of very strong pure green tea
2. sweeten to taste with capillaire or any other syrup
