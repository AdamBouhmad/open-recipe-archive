## Ingredients

- moistened pulses of kalai
- salt
- ghee or oil
- ghee
- mustard seeds
- curd

## Directions

1. Grind some moistened pulses of kalai to a paste.
2. Add salt and beat thoroughly.
3. Prepare cakes of it and fry in ghee or oil till brown.
4. Boil a little ghee with a few mustard seeds and add some curd.
5. Stir and take down.
6. Keep the fried baras immersed in the curd for several hours, and then serve.
