## Ingredients

- butter
- sweet onion
- tomato
- garlic
- Parmesan cheese
- Grujere cheese

## Directions

1. Put an ounce of butter into a small stew-pan
2. throw into it a table-spoonful of finely minced sweet onion
3. put the pan on the fire and lightly fry the onion
4. before the pieces take color, put into the vessel four or five large, or a dozen small tomatoes, cut up into small pieces
5. Stir well over the fire until the tomatoes are thoroughly cooked
6. rub an au gratin dish with a piece of garlic, butter it
7. pour into it the contents of the stew-pan
8. dust over the surface a layer of Parmesan, Grujere, or other mild cheese
9. bake for eight or ten minutes
10. serve hot
