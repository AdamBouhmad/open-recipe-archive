## Ingredients

- minced onions
- cassia leaves
- ghee
- minced meat
- water
- loaf
- milk
- eggs
- salt
- powders of pepper
- powders of garam-masalla
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Fry minced onions and cassia leaves with enough ghee
2. Fry 1/2 seer of minced meat with these for about 10 minutes and add 1/2 seer of water
3. When the water dries up add 1/2 powa of loaf pasted with 1/2 powa of milk, 3 or 4 eggs, salt, powders of pepper and garam-masalla
4. Stir thoroughly and take down when the mass is a stiff paste
5. Make about 25 coorkits of round and long shape, dip into beaten eggs and fry in plenty of ghee
