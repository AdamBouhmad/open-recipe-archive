## Ingredients

- a breakfastcupful of fine rice
- water
- a hard coconut
- boiling-hot water
- date jagree or treacle to taste
- a few grains of aniseed
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak a breakfastcupful of fine rice in water until quite soft
2. Scoop out the contents of a hard coconut
3. Extract all the milk with a little boiling-hot water
4. Boil the rice in it and sweeten it to taste with some date jagree or treacle
5. Put in a few grains of aniseed
6. Pour the mixture into a buttered pudding-dish and bake it slightly
