## Ingredients

- two ounces of maccaroni
- salt
- pepper
- one ounce of butter
- one table-spoonful of flour
- four table-spoonfuls of gravy
- a coffee-cupful of cream, or milk enriched with the yolk of an egg
- two ounces of grated cheese
- one table-spoonful of mustard

## Directions

1. Throw maccaroni into boiling water with a salt-spoonful of salt and a pinch of pepper
2. Boil, and as soon as tender, drain it well
3. Put into a sauce-pan one ounce of butter, mix it well with one table-spoonful of flour
4. Moisten with four table-spoonfuls of gravy and a coffee-cupful of cream or milk enriched with the yolk of an egg
5. Add two ounces of grated cheese, one table-spoonful of mustard, salt and pepper to taste
6. Place the maccaroni in a shallow well buttered pie-dish
7. By degrees pour your mixture amongst it
8. Dust a thick layer of grated cheese over the surface
9. Send it to table as soon as it browns nicely in the oven
