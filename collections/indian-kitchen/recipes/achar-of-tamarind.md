## Ingredients

- ripe tamarinds
- salt
- mustard oil
- powders of chillies, mustard, aniseed and other spices (except turmeric)

## Directions

1. Shell some ripe tamarinds, mix with salt and dry them thoroughly in the sun.
2. Mix with enough mustard oil, powders of chillies, mustard, aniseed and any or every other spices except turmeric, and put them in an earthen pot.
3. Put the pot in the sun at intervals of 2 or 3 days with the lid open allowing the sunbeams to fall directly into it.
