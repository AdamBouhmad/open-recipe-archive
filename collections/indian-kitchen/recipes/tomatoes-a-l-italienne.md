## Ingredients

- tomato
- salad oil
- grated ham
- bread-crumbs
- minced shallot
- parsley
- marjoram
- thyme
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the tomatoes in halves
2. scoop out the pulp and seeds with a silver spoon
3. place them on a baking dish upon which you have poured a little of the best salad oil
4. Make a mixture of grated ham, bread-crumbs, some finely minced shallot, parsley, marjoram, and thyme, seasoned with pepper and salt
5. mix this with the tomato pulp
6. fill the cases, covering them completely
7. shake an allowance of salad oil in drops over all
8. bake for a few minutes
9. serve the dish intact as it comes from the oven
