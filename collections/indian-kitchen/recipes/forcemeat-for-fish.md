## Ingredients

- meat of a large beckty, hilsa, or any sort of white fish
- minced suet
- grated bread
- chopped oysters
- boiled parsley chopped
- pounded onion
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- lemon-peel
- well-beaten yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Pick from the bones the meat of a large beckty, hilsa, or any sort of white fish
2. Mince it finely
3. Add the same proportions of minced suet and grated bread
4. Add a few chopped oysters and some boiled parsley chopped
5. Season with a little pounded onion, cayenne pepper, salt, nutmeg, and lemon-peel
6. Mix all well together
7. Bind it with the well-beaten yolks of eggs
8. Roll it into small balls
9. Fry them
