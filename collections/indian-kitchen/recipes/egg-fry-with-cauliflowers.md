## Ingredients

- cauliflower flowers
- water
- salt
- ground turmeric
- egg contents
- powdered bread or biscuit
- ghee or oil
- poppy seeds
- minced onions
- green chillies

## Directions

1. Cut flowers in small pieces
2. Boil in water with salt and ground turmeric
3. Dip into beaten egg contents with salt and ground turmeric
4. Roll on powdered bread or biscuit
5. Fry in plenty of ghee or oil
6. Poppy seeds, minced onions and green chillies may be mixed with egg contents if desired
