## Ingredients

- well-boiled ham
- butter
- table-spoonful of Madeira

## Directions

1. Obtain a few nice slices of a good, well-boiled ham
2. Toss them in butter in a frying-pan, with a table-spoonful of Madeira
3. Pass them from the pan to the plate as hot as possible
