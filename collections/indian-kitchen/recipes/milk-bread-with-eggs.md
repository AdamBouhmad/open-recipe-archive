## Ingredients

- 3 seers milk
- 1 1/2 powa sugar
- 1/2 seer loaf
- contents of a few fresh eggs
- a little rose water

## Directions

1. Boil about 3 seers of milk with 1 1/2 powa of sugar till it is boiled.
2. Add a loaf weighing 1/2 seer, the contents of a few fresh eggs and a little rose water, and mix thoroughly.
3. Heat it on charcoal fire till thick when milk-bread will be ready to be served.
