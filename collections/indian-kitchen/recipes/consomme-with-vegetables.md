## Ingredients

- equal parts carrots, turnips, leeks, onions, and celery
- two ounces fresh butter
- one tea-spoonful powdered sugar
- pepper
- salt
- broth from the consomme
- lettuce leaves
- sorrel leaves

## Directions

1. Cut carrots, turnips, leeks, onions, and celery into thin strips not more than the eighth of an inch across, and an inch long
2. Put them into a sauce-pan with two ounces of fresh butter, a tea-spoonful of powdered sugar, a little pepper, and a pinch of salt
3. Toss them lightly on the fire until they take colour slightly, say for five minutes or so
4. Cover them with a little broth from the consomme and carefully remove the grease thrown up by the butter
5. Let the vegetables thus cooked remain in the gravy near the fire, nice and hot, until the time of serving
6. Add the vegetables with a few leaves of lettuce and of sorrel finely shredded to the rest of the consomme
7. Bring to the boil, finally skim, and serve
