## Ingredients

- raisins 2 tolas
- pieces of almonds 2 tolas
- pieces of pistachios 1 tola
- cardamom seeds 1/2 tola
- powdered aniseed 1/4 tola
- cows' ghee 1 teaspoonful
- khowa kheer 1 chhatak
- crystals of sugar-candy 1 chhatak
- very little camphor
- sugar 3 powas
- scraped and pasted kernel of not-so-ripe cocoa-nut 3 powas
- pasted hard chhana 1 powa
- 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose

## Directions

1. Fry raisins, pieces of almonds, pieces of pistachios, cardamom seeds and powdered aniseed with a teaspoonful of cows' ghee
2. Mix with khowa kheer, crystals of sugar-candy and very little camphor
3. Boil a syrup of three powas of sugar in a brass pot
4. Add 3 powas of scraped and pasted kernel of not-so-ripe cocoa-nut and 1 powa of pasted hard chhana
5. Stir vigorously on mild heat till the mass sticks to the sides of the pot and ladle
6. Take off the fire and mix thoroughly with 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose
7. Make balls of it when slightly warm
8. Stuff with the spiced kheer
9. Press in casts
10. Eat them up within a week
