## Ingredients

- a table-spoonful of tamarind pulp
- one table-spoonful of green ginger
- salt
- a tea-spoonful of minced green chillies
- one tea-spoonful of mustard seed
- butter

## Directions

1. Pound together a table-spoonful of tamarind pulp and one of green ginger.
2. Season it with salt, a tea-spoonful of minced green chillies, and one of mustard seed roasted in butter.
3. Mix thoroughly and serve.
