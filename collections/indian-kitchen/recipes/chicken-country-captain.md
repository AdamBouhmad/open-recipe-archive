## Ingredients

- 4 ounces ghee
- half a teaspoonful ground chilies
- one teaspoonful salt
- a quarter of a teaspoonful ground turmeric
- twenty onions

## Directions

1. Cut up in the usual way an ordinary curry chicken.
2. Warm the ghee and fry the sliced onions, which when brown set aside.
3. Fry the ground turmeric and chilies, then throw in the chicken and salt, and continue to fry, stirring the whole, until the chicken is tender.
4. Serve it up, strewing over it the fried onions.
