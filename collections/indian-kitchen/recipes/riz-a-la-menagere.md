## Ingredients

- six ounces of rice
- a quarter of a pound of streaky bacon
- a pint and a half of broth
- a salt-spoonful of pepper
- half a pint of tomato puree or sauce
- sausages
- fried bacon
- croquettes of fish
- savoury mixture

## Directions

1. Wash and blanch six ounces of rice in boiling water for five minutes, using a roomy stew-pan
2. Cool and drain it on a sieve
3. Weigh a quarter of a pound of the best streaky bacon, dip it into scalding water for a couple of minutes, and then cut it into inch dice
4. Fry these in a stew-pan till they turn yellow
5. Add the rice, and a pint and a half of broth, with a salt-spoonful of pepper
6. Simmer for twenty minutes, stirring the rice every now and then to prevent its catching at the bottom of the pan
7. Take it off the fire, and add half a pint of tomato puree or sauce
8. Mix thoroughly, and put the rice on a dish
9. Garnish with sausages, curls of fried bacon, croquettes of fish, or any savoury mixture you like, worked into small shapes, and fried a golden yellow
