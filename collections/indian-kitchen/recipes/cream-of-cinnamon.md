## Ingredients

- one quart of spirits of wine
- two drops of oil of cinnamon
- two drops of oil of roses
- one quart of syrup
- red tincture

## Directions

1. To a quart of spirits of wine add two drops of oil of cinnamon and two of oil of roses
2. Shake it well until the oil has thoroughly dissolved
3. Add a quart of syrup and a sufficient quantity of red tincture to produce a bright full colour
4. Filter and bottle it
