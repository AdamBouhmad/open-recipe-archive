## Ingredients

- pieces of fish
- salt
- powdered pepper
- minced onions
- boiled and pasted potatoes
- eggs
- ghee

## Directions

1. Boil pieces of fish and remove the bones
2. Add salt, powdered pepper, minced onions, boiled and pasted potatoes equal in quantity with the fish, and eggs
3. Mix them to a thick paste
4. Spread it on a plane with a uniform thickness of 0.5 inch
5. Cut it in small squares
6. Fry with ghee
