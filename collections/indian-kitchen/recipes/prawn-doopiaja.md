## Ingredients

- mustard oil, one chittack and a half
- ground onions, four teaspoonfuls
- ground turmeric, one teaspoonful
- ground chilies, one teaspoonful
- ground garlic, a quarter of a teaspoonful
- curry onions, twelve, cut lengthways into six or eight slices each
- water, one cupful
- large prawns, twelve
- unroasted coriander-seed

## Directions

1. Clean and thoroughly wash the prawns, rejecting the heads or taking only their substance pounded and squeezed out with unroasted coriander-seed
2. Parboil the prawns
3. Make the doopiaja in all respects according to the ordinary mode
