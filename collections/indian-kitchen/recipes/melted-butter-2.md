## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound of butter
- flour
- about a wineglass of water

## Directions

1. Dust a little flour over a quarter of a pound of butter, and put it into a saucepan, with about a wineglass of water
2. Stir it one way constantly till it is melted, and let it just boil
