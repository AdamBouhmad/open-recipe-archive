## Ingredients

- perfectly ripe plums
- salt
- cloves of garlic
- green or fresh red chilies
- sliced ginger
- peppercorns
- ground mustard-seed
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Get the perfectly ripe fruit
2. Put them into a damp cloth, and roll them about to free them of dust
3. Sprinkle them well with salt
4. Stand them in the sun for three or four days
5. Drain away all the water
6. Bottle the plums alternately with cloves of garlic, green or fresh red chilies, sliced ginger, peppercorns, and ground mustard-seed
7. Add a little salt
8. Fill up the bottles with vinegar, and cork
9. Expose them to the sun for fifteen to twenty days
