## Ingredients

- two ounces of chocolate
- half a pint of milk
- a few drops of vanilla essence
- two tea-spoonfuls of potato-flour or corn-flour
- yolks of four eggs
- a glass of liqueur
- whites of six eggs

## Directions

1. Grate the chocolate into half a pint of milk
2. Add a few drops of vanilla essence
3. Boil the milk so that the chocolate may become thoroughly dissolved
4. Thicken the flavoured milk with two tea-spoonfuls of potato-flour or corn-flour
5. Strain and set to get cold
6. Beat up the yolks of four eggs and a glass of liqueur with the cold batter
7. Add the whites of six eggs in the shape of a stiff froth just before baking
8. Pour into a proper souffle case or tin
9. Bake until done
