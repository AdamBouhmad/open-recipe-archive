## Ingredients

- eggs, six or eight
- ghee
- ground condiments
- sliced fried onions
- water
- English green peas

## Directions

1. Boil eggs hard, shell, and cut into halves
2. Use ghee, ground condiments, and sliced fried onions in the same manner as recipe No. 71
3. Allow the curry to simmer until the peas are quite tender
