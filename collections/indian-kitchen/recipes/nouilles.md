## Ingredients

- half a pound of sifted flour
- three eggs
- half an ounce of butter
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of sifted flour
2. Put it on the paste board
3. Make a hole in the centre of the flour
4. Break three eggs in it
5. Add half an ounce of butter and a pinch of salt
6. Mix all into a nice smooth paste
7. Roll the paste out very thin, say about the sixteenth of an inch
8. Let it dry
9. Cut it into ribbons an inch and a half broad
10. Put five of these ribbons above one another, sprinkling a little flour between each
11. With a knife cut through them crosswise, making thin shreds like vermicelli
12. Shake them in a cloth with a little flour to prevent them adhering to one another
13. Throw them into two quarts of boiling water for six minutes
14. Use nouilles exactly as you would maccaroni
