## Ingredients

- Fish pieces
- Turmeric powdered
- Salt
- Molten butter
- Garam-masalla powdered
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the pieces of fish
2. Wipe them with a clean piece of linen
3. Smear with powdered turmeric and salt
4. Fry in molten butter and remove the bones as far as possible
5. Mix molten butter, salt, powdered garam-masalla and pepper to a paste
6. Mix it with the pieces of fish
7. Keep air tight in a dry and cool cellar for long preservation
