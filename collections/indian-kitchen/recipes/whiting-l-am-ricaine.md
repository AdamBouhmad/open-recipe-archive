## Ingredients

- three nice whitings
- egg
- white bread crumbs
- boiling fat
- one dessert-spoonful of butter
- one dessert-spoonful of flour
- half a pint of warm fish gravy
- juice of a lime
- salt
- sugar
- tabasco
- one heaped dessert-spoonful of chopped capsicums

## Directions

1. Cut and trim three nice whitings in fillets
2. Brush them over with egg and bread-crumb them with some finely sifted white crumbs
3. Fry them a golden yellow in boiling fat
4. Drain and serve them with sauce
5. To make sauce: Melt a dessert-spoonful of butter in a small sauce-pan
6. Stir in a dessert-spoonful of flour
7. Add half a pint of warm fish gravy and let it thicken
8. Finish it with the juice of a lime, a little salt, a pinch of sugar, a few drops of tabasco, and a heaped up dessert-spoonful of chopped capsicums
