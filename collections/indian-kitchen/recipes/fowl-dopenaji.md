## Ingredients

- fowl 0.5 seer
- ghee 0.5 powa
- onions 1 powa
- pieces of garlic
- salt 1 tola
- cassia leaves
- turmeric 0.5 tola
- garam-masalla 0.5 tola
- pepper 0.5 tola
- cumin 0.5 tola
- coriander 0.5 tola
- water 0.5 seer

## Directions

1. Cut the fowl in big pieces and mix with salt.
2. Make a paste of all the spices together and mix with 0.5 seer of water.
3. Cut the onions in slices, fry half of them with a little salt and keep aside.
4. Boil ghee and fry a few cloves, cassia leaves and garlic in it, add the other half of onions and brown them slightly.
5. Add the fowl and stir constantly for about 20 minutes adding the spiced water little by little if necessary.
6. Add the whole of the spiced water and cover the pan.
7. Take down when no soup is left.
8. Place the fried onions on the fowl.
