## Ingredients

- pasted ginger
- chillies
- turmeric
- garam-masala
- salt
- minced meat
- oil
- boiled potatoes
- powdered pepper
- big sized hilsa fish
- mustard oil
- powdered turmeric
- mustard
- green chillies
- green banana leaves

## Directions

1. Mix pasted ginger, chillies, turmeric, garam-masala and salt with some minced meat and fry with oil
2. Flake a big sized hilsa fish, preferably without roes
3. Wash and remove the head, entrails and tail
4. Cut deep lengthwise on both sides of the fish
5. Smear it well with a paste of mustard oil, powdered turmeric, mustard, green chillies and salt
6. Stuff it full with the meat and stitch the opening
7. Wrap it up 8 or 10 fold with green banana leaves
8. Roast both sides alternately on glowing charcoal till the outermost leaves are burnt up
9. Remove the leaves and serve
