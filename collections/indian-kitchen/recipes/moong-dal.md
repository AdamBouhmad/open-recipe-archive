## Ingredients

- half a pound of raw dal or half a cupful
- three-quarters of a chittack of ghee
- four teaspoonfuls of ground onions
- one teaspoonful of ground chilies
- half a teaspoonful of ground turmeric
- half a teaspoonful of ground ginger
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground garlic
- one teaspoonful and a half of salt
- half a dozen onions

## Directions

1. Clean, pick, and roast the dal
2. Mix it up with all the ground condiments and salt
3. Put into a pot and pour water over the whole, some two inches above the dal
4. Boil it well until the dal has quite dissolved, allowing it to cake as it were en masse
5. Churn the dal by twirling it in a wooden instrument called a phootnee
6. Warm the ghee in a separate pot and fry the onions
7. Chop the onions and throw into the churned dal
8. Pour the dal into the pot of melted ghee and keep stirring until the dal and ghee have well mixed
9. Put the cover on and allow to simmer over a slow fire for about a quarter of an hour
