## Ingredients

- green mangoes
- salt
- powders of chillies
- pepper
- treacle or concentrated syrup of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the mangoes in halves and dry them in the sun after smearing with salt
2. Mix them with powders of chillies and pepper and enough treacle or concentrated syrup of sugar to saturate them
3. Keep them in an airtight jar and keep the jar in the sun for several days
