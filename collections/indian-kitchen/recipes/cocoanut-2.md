## Ingredients

- ripe cocoanut
- ground turmeric
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Break the stone of a ripe cocoanut and bring out the kernel with a knife.
2. Remove the brown skin of the kernel and cut it in small pieces.
3. Smear ground turmeric and salt.
4. Brown them in oil.
