## Ingredients

- Rice ^ seer
- Fried moog-dal ^ seer
- Potato |- seer or more
- Onion i powa
- Ghee ^ powa
- Salt 2 tolas
- Ginger i tola
- Turmeric ^ tola
- Cumin ^ tola
- Pepper ^ tola
- Garam-masalla ^ tola
- A few cassia leaves
- Red chillies
- Almonds (optional)
- Pistachios (optional)
- Raisins (optional)
- Rose-water

## Directions

1. Heat the cassia leaves and red chillies with half the ghee and when the chillies turn black drop rice and pulse, washed and dried, and stir for about 5 minutes
2. Then pour in water and cover the boiling pot
3. Add salt, turmeric, cumin, pepper and ginger, all pasted or powdered, when the rice are half-boiled
4. Brown pieces of potatoes and onions in ghee or oil and drop them in the khechuri
5. Add ghee and powdered garam-masalla before finish
6. Almonds, pistachios and raisins, peeled and cut in pieces, may be added if desired after browning them in ghee
7. Mix adequate quantities of rose-water with the khechuri in every dish before serving
