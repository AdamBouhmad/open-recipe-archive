## Ingredients

- eight parts powdered sulphur
- one and a half parts powdered charcoal
- a quarter part powdered saltpetre
- gum water

## Directions

1. Mix the sulphur, charcoal, and saltpetre together
2. Add just enough gum water to make pastiles
3. Shape them like pyramids
4. Dry them in the sun
5. Suspend the meat in an air-tight box furnished with hooks, a closely fitting door, and all crevices filled with putty and pasted over with strips of strong paper
6. Place two or three pastiles below the meat
7. Light the pastiles
8. Close the door securely and leave for two or three hours
9. The meat will keep perfectly for thirty-six hours and be found deliciously tender
