## Ingredients

- shallow pie-dish
- butter
- cream a quarter of an inch deep
- well grated cheese a quarter of an inch deep
- six eggs
- black pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Choose a shallow pie-dish and butter it liberally
2. Pour over the bottom of the dish a layer of cream a quarter of an inch deep
3. Over that shake a coating of well grated cheese a quarter of an inch deep
4. Slip in one by one as carefully as you can six eggs to avoid breaking a single yolk
5. Give them a dust of black pepper and salt
6. Gently pour a little more cream over the surface
7. Coat it over again with grated cheese
8. Let the dish remain in the oven until the eggs are set without being hard
9. Brown the surface by passing a red hot iron backwards and forwards over it, about an inch above the cheese
10. Serve
