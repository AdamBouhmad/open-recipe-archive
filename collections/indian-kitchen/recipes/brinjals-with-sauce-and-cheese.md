## Ingredients

- eight very young brinjals
- seeds
- butter
- grated mild cheese
- thick brown gravy

## Directions

1. Parboil eight very young brinjals, the size of a small hen's egg
2. Cut them in halves lengthways
3. Pick out the seeds with the point of a vegetable-knife
4. Butter them, and dredge over the surface of each a layer of grated mild cheese
5. Arrange them neatly on a silver dish, well buttered, or any dish that will stand the oven
6. Bake for ten minutes
7. Pour round them a cupful of well made thick brown gravy, and serve in the dish in which they were cooked, placed on a napkin
