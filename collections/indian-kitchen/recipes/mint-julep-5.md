## Ingredients

- about one dozen young sprigs of mint
- one tablespoonful white sugar
- half a wineglassful peach
- half a wineglassful common brandy
- pounded ice

## Directions

1. Put about a dozen young sprigs of mint into a tumbler
2. Add one tablespoonful of white sugar
3. Add half a wineglassful of peach and the same of common brandy
4. Fill up the tumbler with pounded ice
