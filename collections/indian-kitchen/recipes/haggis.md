## Ingredients

- heart
- lights
- one pound of minced suet
- two or three large onions
- two small handfuls of oatmeal
- pepper
- salt
- bag

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the heart and lights.
2. Parboil and mince them very small.
3. Add one pound of minced suet, two or three large onions minced, and two small handfuls of oatmeal.
4. Season highly with pepper and salt, and mix all well together.
5. The bag being perfectly clean and sweet, put in the ingredients.
6. Press out the air, sew it up, and boil it for three hours.
