## Ingredients

- good fresh ripe oranges
- sugar

## Directions

1. Stew good fresh ripe oranges till perfectly tender, changing the water several times
2. Drain them, and cut and remove the rinds without breaking them or wounding the pulps
3. Weigh the pulps, having previously removed all the pips, skin, and seeds
4. To every six pounds of fruit add seven of sugar
5. Pour boiling water over the pips, seeds, &c., strain them, and take the liquor for the preparation of syrup
6. Skim it well while boiling
7. When clear, add to it the rind, having first scraped and thrown away some of the inside and then cut it up into thin slices or chips
8. After a while add the pulp and juice, and boil it up again until it acquires the consistency of jelly
