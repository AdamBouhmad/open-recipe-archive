## Ingredients

- two pounds of fat pork
- vinegar
- salt
- ground condiments
- ghee or lard
- peppercorns
- bay-leaves

## Directions

1. Cut up two pounds of fat pork into large squares, and curry according to the directions given in the foregoing recipe, omitting the cloves, cardamoms, and cinnamon.
