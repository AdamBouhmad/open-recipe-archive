## Ingredients

- four ounces of butter
- yolks of four eggs
- four ounces of finely pounded loaf sugar
- juice of three limes
- liqueur-glass of noyeau or curaçoa
- puff-pastry
- finely pounded loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Melt four ounces of butter in an enamelled sauce-pan.
2. Stir into it the yolks of four eggs, and four ounces of finely pounded loaf sugar, and when dissolved, the juice of three limes.
3. Mix the syrup well, and add a liqueur-glass of noyeau or curaçoa.
4. Line eight or nine round patty pans with puff-paste.
5. Fill them half full with the mixture, leaving room for it to rise in the baking.
6. When done, dust over the cheesecakes some finely pounded loaf sugar, and serve them upon a napkin, hot.
