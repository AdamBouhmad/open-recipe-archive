## Ingredients

- nearly half a pound of flour
- five well-beaten eggs
- a quart of good milk
- fresh lard
- pounded loaf sugar

## Directions

1. With nearly half a pound of flour mix five well-beaten eggs
2. Add, by degrees, a quart of good milk
3. Fry them in fresh lard
4. Serve them with pounded loaf sugar strewed between each
