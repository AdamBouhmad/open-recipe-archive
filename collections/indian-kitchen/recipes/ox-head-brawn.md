## Ingredients

- ox-head, skinned and cleaned
- water
- vegetables
- flavouring
- salt
- spiced pepper

## Directions

1. Skin and clean an ox-head, or purchase one already skinned.
2. Split it in two, cut out the eyes, break the bones of the jaws, and remove the brains.
3. Let the whole soak for an hour or two in cold water.
4. Put it into a stew-pan with water enough to cover it.
5. Boil very slowly, then add vegetables and flavouring as if for soup.
6. When the meat is quite tender and you can pick the bones away from it easily, strain the meat from the broth and vegetables.
7. Free it from every fragment of bone.
8. Cut up the meat whilst it is hot and juicy, en masse rather small, seasoning it with salt and spiced pepper whilst doing so.
