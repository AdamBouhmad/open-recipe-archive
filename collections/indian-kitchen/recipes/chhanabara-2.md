## Ingredients

- 1 seer of chhana (not hard)
- 2.5 powas of sabeda
- water

## Directions

1. Mix 1 seer of chhana (not hard) with 2.5 powas of sabeda and make a moderately stiff yet fine paste of the mixture adding a little water if necessary
2. Make spherical balls of it
3. Fry them in ghee
4. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated
