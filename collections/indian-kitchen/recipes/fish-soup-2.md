## Ingredients

- fish
- oil
- vegetables (peeled and cut in big pieces)
- brinjals
- water
- coriander
- cumin
- pepper
- turmeric
- chillies
- onions
- salt
- fried bori
- cassia leaves
- panch-foron

## Directions

1. Prepare fish and fry in oil
2. Brown all the vegetables, peeled and cut in big pieces, except brinjals
3. Add water with coriander, cumin, pepper, turmeric, chillies and onions, all pasted together
4. On boiling add salt, brinjals and fried fishes
5. Add fried bori if desired
6. When the vegetables are boiled season the soup with boiling oil with a few cassia leaves, chillies and a pinch of panch-foron browned in it
7. Keep the soup thin
