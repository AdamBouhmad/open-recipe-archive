## Ingredients

- butter
- Bombay onion
- bunch of mint
- parsley
- salt
- peas
- broth
- flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put a lump of butter into a stew-pan with a Bombay onion sliced, a bunch of mint and parsley, and a tea-spoonful of salt
2. Cook this awhile till the onions take a pale colour
3. Add the peas with as much broth as will just float them
4. Simmer this patiently till the peas are thoroughly tender
5. Take up the pan and strain the liquor
6. Spread out the peas on a dish and pick out the pieces of onion
7. Thicken the liquor with butter and flour, adding a tea-spoonful of sugar
8. Add the peas again
9. Stir well, bring the sauce-pan to steaming point, and serve
