## Ingredients

- Tender stalks of arum
- Ground pepper
- Cumin
- Coriander
- Turmeric
- Red chillies
- Salt
- Grams
- Oil
- Cumin seeds
- Kernel of cocoanut
- Milk
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Skin the tender stalks of arum and cut into pieces an inch long
2. Boil them and drain off the water
3. Add ground pepper, cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chillies and salt, and beat thoroughly
4. Fry this with a handful of moistened grams in oil heated with a few cumin seeds
5. Add some minced kernel of cocoanut and stir
6. Take down when the soup dries up
7. Add a little milk and a pinch of sugar if desired
