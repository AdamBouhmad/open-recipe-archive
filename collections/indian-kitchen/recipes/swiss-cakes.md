## Ingredients

- butter, flour, and sugar, of each the weight of four eggs
- yolks of eggs
- grated lemon-peel or ten drops of essence of lemon
- one large teaspoonful of rose-water or orange-flower water
- whites of eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks with the sugar and some grated lemon-peel or ten drops of essence of lemon
2. Add one large teaspoonful of rose-water or orange-flower water if preferred
3. Add the butter just melted
4. Slowly shake in the flour, beating it until well mixed
5. Beat the whites of the eggs to a froth
6. Mix the whole together and beat on for a few minutes after the whites are added
7. Butter a tin and bake the cake half an hour
