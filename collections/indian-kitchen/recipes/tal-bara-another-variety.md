## Ingredients

- Ripe tal fruit pulp
- Sugar
- Powdered rice
- Banana leaves

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and powdered rice with the pulp of a ripe tal fruit so that the paste is just stiff to be made a big ball
2. Press the ball flat
3. Cover it all round with a few layers of banana leaves
4. Burn it on mild heat till well boiled
5. Remove the burnt leaves when cold and serve
