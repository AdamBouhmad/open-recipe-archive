## Ingredients

- whites of four eggs
- three-quarters of a pound of finely-powdered white sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Take the whites of four eggs, beat them up well
2. Add three-quarters of a pound of finely-powdered white sugar
3. Flavour with vanilla
4. Beat up well
5. Drop it on buttered paper
6. Bake in a cool oven
