## Ingredients

- pounded mint
- minced onion
- green chilli
- green ginger
- an atom of garlic
- tamarind juice
- red pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Scald the mint leaves before pounding them.
2. Make in the same way as cocoanut chutney, substituting pounded mint for cocoanut.
