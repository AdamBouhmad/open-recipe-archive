## Ingredients

- cabbage
- rice
- gravy
- cheese

## Directions

1. Par-boil the cabbage and cut it up into pieces the size of an egg
2. Put them with an equal quantity of half-boiled rice into as much gravy as will cover them
3. Simmer till done then serve
4. Hand round grated cheese with this dish
