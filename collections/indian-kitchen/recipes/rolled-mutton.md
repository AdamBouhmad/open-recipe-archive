## Ingredients

- shoulder of mutton
- pepper
- nutmeg
- clove
- parsley
- lemon
- thyme
- sweet marjoram
- onion
- egg yolk
- bones and parings
- flour
- butter
- vinegar
- mushroom catsup
- soy
- lemon pickle
- port wine
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Bone a shoulder of mutton carefully, so as not to injure the skin.
2. Cut all the meat from the skin, mince it small, and season it highly with pepper, nutmeg, and a clove, some parsley, lemon, thyme, sweet marjoram chopped, and a pounded onion, all well mixed, together with the well-beaten yolk of an egg.
3. Roll it up very tightly in the skin.
4. Tie it round, and bake it in an oven for two or three hours, according to the size of the mutton.
5. Make a gravy of the bones and parings.
6. Season with an onion, pepper, and salt.
7. Strain and thicken it with flour and butter.
8. Add a tablespoonful each of vinegar, mushroom catsup, soy, and lemon pickle, and a teacupful of port wine.
9. Garnish with forcemeat balls made of grated bread, and part of the mince.
