## Ingredients

- ripe pine-apple
- ½ seer of pine-apple pieces
- lime water
- ½ powa of raisins
- ghee
- panch-forons
- mustard seeds
- cardamom seeds
- sugar 1 powa
- salt
- lemon-juice 1 table-spoonful

## Directions

1. Peel thickly a ripe pine-apple, remove the eyes, cut in thin and small pieces and keep ½ seer of the pieces in lime water for a few minutes
2. Wash with fresh water and boil them with ½ powa of raisins with a little water
3. On softening remove the pan off fire
4. Heat a little ghee in another pan and fry a few panch-forons, mustard and cardamom seeds in it
5. Add the mass, sugar 1 powa, a pinch of salt and a table-spoonful of lemon-juice
6. Boil for some time and take down when the juice is thick
