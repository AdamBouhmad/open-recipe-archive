## Ingredients

- butter
- mild grated cheese
- cream
- four fresh eggs

## Directions

1. Butter a little pie-dish well
2. Strew a good layer of mild grated cheese at the bottom of it
3. Pour over the cheese a coffee-cupful of cream
4. Break four fresh eggs very carefully and pass them into the cream without breaking them
5. Dust a light layer of cheese over the surface
6. Bake for about seven minutes until the surface slightly colours
7. Serve with eggs just set in a creamy sauce with a little delicate cheese flavouring
