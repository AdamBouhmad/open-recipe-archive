## Ingredients

- cabbage
- 2 seers of milk
- 2 1/2 or 3 powas of pieces of cabbage
- 1 1/2 powas of sugar
- raisins, pistachios and almonds
- very little camphor

## Directions

1. Remove the upper leaves of a cabbage till the tender white leaves appear.
2. Then chop it in small very fine pieces.
3. Boil 2 seers of milk till it is reduced to 1 1/2 seers.
4. Then add 2 1/2 or 3 powas of the pieces of cabbage.
5. When well boiled add 1 1/2 powas of sugar, raisins, pistachios and almonds as desired.
6. Take down when thick and mix with very little camphor.
