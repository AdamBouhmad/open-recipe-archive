## Ingredients

- two ounces of cheese
- a dessert-spoonful of anchovy sauce
- a dessert-spoonful of made-mustard
- one dessert-spoonful of vinegar
- a pinch of Nepaul pepper
- a little salt
- the yolks of two eggs
- a tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Pound two ounces of cheese with a dessert-spoonful of anchovy sauce, a dessert-spoonful of made-mustard, and one of vinegar, a pinch of Nepaul pepper, and a little salt, the yolks of two eggs, and a tablespoonful of butter.
2. Mix thoroughly in a basin, and proceed as directed for Welsh rarebit.
