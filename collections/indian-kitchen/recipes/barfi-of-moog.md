## Ingredients

- 2 seers of pulses of moog
- 1 seer of ghee
- 1.5 tolas of pieces of almonds
- 2 tolas of raisins
- 1 tola of pieces of pistachios
- 2 tolas of powders of pepper
- 1 tola of powders of coriander
- 0.5 tola of powders of aniseed
- 0.5 tola of saffron
- 2 seers of sugar

## Directions

1. Keep 2 seers of pulses of moog moistened for 2 or 3 hours
2. Make a paste of them
3. Beat 1 seer of ghee till very white
4. Mix it with the pasted moog, pieces of almonds 1.5 tolas, raisins 2 tolas, pieces of pistachios 1 tola, powders of pepper 2 tolas, coriander 1 tola, aniseed 0.5 tola and saffron 0.5 tola
5. Heat the mass in a pan and stir constantly till red
6. Pour in a syrup of 2 seers of sugar
7. Continue stirring till the mass is very thick
8. Pour it in a flat plate
9. Cut it in cubes when cold
