## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer hard chhana
- 1/4 chhatak sabeda
- cardamom seeds
- 3/4 seer ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Make a fine paste of 1/2 seer of hard chhana and mix thoroughly with 1/4 chhatak of sabeda
2. Make slightly elongated balls of it with one cardamom seed at the centre of each
3. Boil 3/4 seer of ghee till the bubbles die out
4. Fry the balls in it till reddish brown
5. Pick them up and keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated with it
