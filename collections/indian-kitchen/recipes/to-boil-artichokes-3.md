## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the stalks close to the bottom, wash them well
2. let them lie for some hours in cold water
3. put them on in boiling water with a little salt in it
4. cover the pan closely
5. boil them an hour and a half
6. If they are old, and have not been freshly gathered, they will take a longer time to boil
7. Melted butter is served with them in a sauce-tureen
