## Ingredients

- ten or twelve crabs full of the red coral
- salt
- pepper
- herbs
- bread-crumbs
- egg
- mustard oil
- ground onions
- ground chillies
- ground turmeric
- ground peppercorns
- ground garlic
- bay-leaves
- lemon-grass
- water

## Directions

1. Select ten or twelve glieewalla kakaralis, or crabs full of the red coral
2. Wash them thoroughly, then boil them
3. Remove all the meat and coral out of the shells, pound to a pulp
4. After mixing all the ingredients and fixing them with an egg well beaten up, make into balls
5. Cook them in all respects according to the directions for lobster cofta curry
6. Simmer for about half an hour
