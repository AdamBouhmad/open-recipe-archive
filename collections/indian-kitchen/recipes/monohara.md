## Ingredients

- ghee 1 chhatak
- flour 1 powa
- powdered rice 3 chhataks
- middlings 1 chhatak
- moistened and pasted pulses of kalai 0.5 powa
- husked sesame 0.5 powa
- salt
- aniseeds
- cardamom seeds
- ginger-juice
- powdered pepper
- water
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly 1 chhatak of ghee with flour 1 powa, powdered rice 3 chhataks and middlings 1 chhatak
2. Add moistened and pasted pulses of kalai 0.5 powa, husked sesame 0.5 powa and adequate quantities of salt, aniseeds, cardamom seeds, ginger-juice, powdered pepper and water to make a not-so-stiff paste
3. Beat thoroughly
4. Make small squares of it about 1/4 inch thick
5. Fry in ghee
6. Keep them in a very concentrated syrup of sugar for say half an hour
7. Take them out and roll on dry sugar
