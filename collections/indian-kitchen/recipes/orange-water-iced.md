## Ingredients

- a pint of water
- the strained juice of three oranges and one lemon
- the grated peel of one orange
- syrup

## Directions

1. Mix with a pint of water the strained juice of three oranges and one lemon, also the grated peel of one orange
2. sweeten it well with syrup, and freeze it
