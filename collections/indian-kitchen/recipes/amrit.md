## Ingredients

- sugar
- water
- milk
- egg yolks
- powdered garam-masalla
- saffron
- ghee

## Directions

1. Mix equal weights of sugar and water, and boil
2. If a little milk is added the impurities will rise to the surface in the form of scum, which is to be removed from time to time
3. Take down when the syrup is a bit sticky
4. Take the yolks of some eggs in a cup, add powdered garam-masalla, a little saffron and sugar, and beat thoroughly
5. Put the liquid in a clean thick linen piece with a small round hole in the middle and tie it up into a bag putting a thumb under the hole
6. Remove the thumb, press the bag with the fingers and allow the liquid to fall in a thin stream on plenty of boiling ghee
7. Take them out as soon as they are fried hard on both sides and put them in the syrup of sugar
