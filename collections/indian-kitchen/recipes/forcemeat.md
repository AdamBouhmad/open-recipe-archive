## Ingredients

- Three ounces beef suet
- one ounce fat bacon
- three ounces raw or dressed veal
- two ounces grated bread
- grated lemon-peel
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- finely-minced parsley
- beaten yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Mince very finely the beef suet, fat bacon, raw or dressed veal, grated bread, lemon-peel, nutmeg, pepper, salt, and finely-minced parsley
2. Mix all well together
3. Bind with the beaten yolks of eggs
4. Make it into croquets or balls, the size of large nutmegs
5. Fry them in ghee or clarified beef dripping, or use it for stuffing
