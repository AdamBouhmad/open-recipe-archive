## Ingredients

- nice fish
- equal amount of prawns
- chopped mushrooms
- rich velouté
- couple of eggs
- flour for pancakes
- melted butter
- eggs for coating
- bread-crumbs
- spiced pepper
- Dutch sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the fish and prawns
2. When cold, make a nice salpicon, or coarse mince of them, with some chopped mushrooms
3. Mix the mince in a sauce-pan with some rich velouté, and bind it with a couple of eggs
4. Let it get cold again, and divide it into six nice portions
5. Make a large pancake, or two small ones, and when not quite done, take them from the pan
6. Spread them on a flat dish, and from them cut six pieces about four inches square
7. Place your salpicon portions in the centre of each, and wrap them up neatly
8. Set the folded pancakes on a well-buttered flat silver dish
9. Egg them, bread-crumb them, pour some melted butter over them
10. Bake until a nice golden brown
11. Serve in the same dish, laid upon a napkin
