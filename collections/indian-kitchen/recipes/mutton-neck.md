## Ingredients

- mutton 1 seer
- curd 0.5 powa
- butter 0.5 powa
- bread crumbs 0.5 powa
- eggs 5
- garam-masalla 1 tola
- cumin 2 tolas
- pepper 1 tola
- salt 1.5 tola

## Directions

1. Make pastes of cumin, pepper and garam-masalla separately.
2. Cut the meat in small pieces and mix with curd and parts of pepper, cumin and salt.
3. Boil sufficient water and boil the meat in it under cover.
4. Add the liquid contents of the eggs, bread crumbs, salt and all the pasted spices when the meat is boiled and stir thoroughly.
5. Add hot butter and take down after boiling a little more.
6. Keep a little soup.
