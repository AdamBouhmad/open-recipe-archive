## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- butter
- lime juice
- curled parsley
- broth

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes and slice them moderately thickly
2. Toss them in plenty of butter in your frying-pan till they colour nicely
3. Pour the brown butter over them
4. Add a few drops of lime juice
5. Add a heaped up table-spoonful of very finely chopped curled parsley
6. Add half a cupful of broth
