## Ingredients

- young turnips
- salt
- water
- melted butter
- powdered sugar
- clear stock
- pepper

## Directions

1. Trim the turnips into shapes like small pears or cones
2. Boil them till nearly done in salt and water
3. Drain them
4. Put them into a saucepan with plenty of melted butter
5. Sprinkle them bountifully with powdered sugar
6. Stir gently over the fire until they begin to brown
7. Add a spoonful or two of clear stock
8. Give pepper and salt
9. Let them simmer till quite tender and serve them in their own sauce
