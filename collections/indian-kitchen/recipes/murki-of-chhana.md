## Ingredients

- chhana
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Remove the whole quantity of whey from any quantity of chhana by very high pressure
2. Cut the chhana when very hard in very small cubes of say 0.5 inch at each side with a sharp knife
3. Boil them in a syrup of sugar for say half an hour
4. Take them out, spread on a flat surface and serve when dry
