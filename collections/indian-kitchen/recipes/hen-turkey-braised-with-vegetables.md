## Ingredients

- hen turkey
- gravy
- white wine
- onions
- carrot
- turnip
- French beans
- peas
- head of celery
- potatoes a la Duchesse
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Braise a hen turkey in gravy and a little white wine, with a couple of onions, a carrot, a turnip, a handful of French beans, a breakfast-cupful of peas and a head of celery
2. When done, dish the turkey
3. Strain the gravy
4. Thicken it, and cut up the vegetables into dice
5. Add them to the gravy again
6. Heat this in a sauce-pan quickly, and pour it over the bird
7. Serve with potatoes a la Duchesse and a cauliflower
