## Ingredients

- Husked kalai pulses 1½ powa
- Ghee
- Curd ¾ powa
- Pasted ginger ½ chhatak
- Powdered garam-masala ½ tola
- Powdered pepper
- Salt
- Flour 1 seer
- Water

## Directions

1. Half boil 1½ powa of husked pulses of kalai
2. Fry them with ghee
3. Bind them with a clean piece of linen
4. Boil in steam above boiling water in a pot with the lid on
5. Make a paste of them when they are soft
6. Mix with curd ¾ powa, pasted ginger ½ chhatak, powdered garam-masala ½ tola, a little powdered pepper and salt
7. Again boil a little in steam
8. Mix 1 chhatak of ghee with 1 seer of flour
9. Make a stiff paste with water
10. Make medium balls, stuff them with the spiced paste of pulses
11. Roll into discs of medium thickness
12. Fry both sides of each on mild heat sprinkling ghee little by little
13. Take down when browned
