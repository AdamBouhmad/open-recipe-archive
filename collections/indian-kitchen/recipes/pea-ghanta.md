## Ingredients

- green peas 1/2 seer
- potatoes 1 seer
- ghee 1 powa
- pepper 1 tola
- ginger 2 tolas
- salt 2 tolas
- coriander 2 tolas
- sugar 2.5 tolas
- flour 1 tola
- water 1 seer
- milk
- garam-masalla 0.5 tola
- cassia leaves

## Directions

1. Brown the peas and pieces of potatoes separately in two thirds of the ghee.
2. Add the pasted pepper, ginger and salt in the water and boil.
3. Add the peas and potatoes.
4. When well boiled season them with the rest of the ghee heated with a few cassia leaves.
5. Mix the powdered coriander, sugar and flour with the milk and pour in.
6. Boil for a few minutes more.
7. Finish by adding the powdered garam masalla.
