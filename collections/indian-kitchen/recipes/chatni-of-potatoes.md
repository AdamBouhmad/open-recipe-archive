## Ingredients

- 1 chhatak of seedless ripe tamarind
- 1 powa of water
- potatoes
- 2 or 3 broken chillies
- mustards
- panch-foron
- 1 chhatak of mustard oil
- 1 powa of potato slices
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix 1 chhatak of seedless ripe tamarind thoroughly with 1 powa of water and throw away the refuse
2. Peel and cut some potatoes in round and thin slices
3. Fry 2 or 3 broken chillies, a pinch of mustards and another of panch-foron in 1 chhatak of mustard oil
4. Fry 1 powa of the slices of potatoes in it
5. When the slices are half browned add the tamarind water and a generous pinch of salt
6. Take down when the soup is thick after mixing with a little sugar
