## Ingredients

- Rice 1 seer
- Minced meat 1 seer
- Ghee 1 seer
- Onions 1.5 powa
- Almonds 0.5 powa
- Raisins 0.5 powa
- Salt 4 tolas
- Curd 0.5 powa
- Ginger 0.5 chhatak
- Coriander seeds 1 tola
- Cloves 0.5 tola
- Cardamoms 0.5 tola
- Cinnamon 0.5 tola
- Pepper 0.5 tola
- Saffron 0.5 tola
- Garlic 1 in number
- Lemon 1 in number
- Cassia leaves a few
- Rose-water 1 tablespoonful
- Eggs 5 or 6 in number
- Fowl 1 in number

## Directions

1. Make a paste of a few pieces of raw onions, a few pieces of garlic, half the ginger, all the coriander seeds and a few cloves.
2. Boil this paste with some salt, half the saffron and half the cassia leaves in about 3 seers of water till it is halved.
3. Strain the akhni water out and keep it covered.
4. Make a paste of a few cloves, cardamoms, almonds and mix it with the curd, the juice of the lemon and the rose-water with the rest of saffron dissolved in it.
5. Cut the onions into pieces and fry them, excepting only some pieces, in about 0.5 powa of ghee and keep them with the ghee in another pot.
6. Take about 0.5 powa of ghee and fry in it the raisins and almonds, peeled and cut into pieces and take them out of the ghee.
7. Fry the minced meat in the same ghee for 2 or 3 minutes more after the water coming out of the meat dries up.
8. Make a paste of 0.75 of the meat, some fried onions, 4 or 5 cloves, 2 or 3 cardamoms, a piece of cinnamon, a pinch of salt, and the minced liver of the fowl if desired.
9. Mix some fried almonds and raisins, a few drops of lemon-juice and a pinch of powdered pepper with the paste and stuff it into the bowels of the fowl, and stitch it up.
10. Make a paste of the rest of the ginger, garlic, the pieces of raw onions, 0.5 chhatak of ghee and some salt, and smear this paste on the body of the fowl.
11. Roast the fowl in 0.5 chhatak of ghee. When the water coming out of the fowl dries up, roast it by constantly sprinkling the curd with spices mixed in it till the fowl is fully boiled.
12. Take down after adding some powdered garam-masalla.
13. Make a paste of the fried minced meat and half the fried onions, and mix it with salt, some powdered garam-masalla, the rest of the powdered peppers and the white of the eggs.
14. Make 8 or 10 round thick balls of this mass and fry them in ghee.
15. Make 2 big omelettes with the yolks of the eggs after mixing them with thin slices of ginger, raw onions, some powdered peppers and salt.
16. Cut the omelettes in long stripes.
17. Take about 0.5 powa of ghee in a pan and heat with some entire garam-masalla.
18. Add rice when the smell evolves and stir till the rice become white and begin to burst.
19. Add the akhni water and take down after about 25 minutes when the water dries up and the rice are boiled.
20. Arrange the layers of in a boiler as follows: cassia leaves, fried onions with the ghee, rice, fried almonds and raisins, rice, roasted fowl with its juice, rice, fried balls of meat, rice, pieces of omelettes, and rice.
21. Shut the mouth of the boiler and heat very slowly for about three quarters of an hour. Then it is ready for the dish.
