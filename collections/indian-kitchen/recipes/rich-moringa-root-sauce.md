## Ingredients

- moringa root shavings
- chicken broth
- yolks of eggs
- a coffee-cupful of cream
- tarragon vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Scrape and simmer the moringa root shavings in chicken broth
2. Beat the yolks of the eggs with a dessert-spoonful of tarragon vinegar
3. Add a coffee-cupful of cream with the yolks of the eggs
4. Let the sauce remain on the fire en bain-marie, stirring well until it is very hot but not boiling
5. Serve it in a hot sauce-boat
