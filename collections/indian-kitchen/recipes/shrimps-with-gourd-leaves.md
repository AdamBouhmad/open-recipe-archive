## Ingredients

- lobsters
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- potatoes
- onions
- water
- cumin
- pepper
- chillies
- coriander
- gourd leaves
- ghee or oil
- cassia leaves
- panch-foron
- garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Prepare some lobsters and cut in pieces
2. Fry them after smearing with powdered turmeric and salt
3. Brown some potatoes and onions peeled and cut in pieces
4. Add water, salt, powdered turmeric, cumin, pepper, chillies, coriander and some chopped and thoroughly washed tender gourd leaves
5. When fully boiled season the curry with ghee or oil boiled with a few cassia leaves and panch-foron
6. Garam-masalla may be added
7. Leave little soup
