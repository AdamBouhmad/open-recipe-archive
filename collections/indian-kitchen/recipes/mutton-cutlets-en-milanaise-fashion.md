## Ingredients

- neck chops
- bacon for larding
- finely sifted crumbs
- minced parsley
- shallot
- grated cheese
- fat for frying
- savoury rice
- maccaroni à l'Italienne

## Directions

1. Trim the neck chops nicely
2. Lard with bacon
3. Set en marinade all day
4. Bread-crumb secundum artem, with finely sifted crumbs, some minced parsley, a very little shallot, and a little grated cheese, all shaken together, the crumbs and cheese in equal proportions
5. Fry a golden yellow in abundance of fat
6. Serve round a hollow mould of savoury rice, with some maccaroni à l'Italienne in the hollow in the centre
