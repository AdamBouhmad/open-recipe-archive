## Ingredients

- brisket of beef
- salt
- saltpetre
- suckur or brown sugar
- lime-juice
- hot spices
- pepper
- chutnee
- sauces
- skin of fat
- coarse pastry

## Directions

1. The process is the same as Collared Brisket
2. If the beef is required to keep for any lengthened time double the quantity of salt
3. Keep the beef in the brine for seventy-two hours
4. Add hot spices, pepper, chutnee, and sauces
5. Roll the beef
6. Pack it in the skin of fat, then in a coarse pastry instead of in plaintain-leaf
7. Bake it in a baker's oven
