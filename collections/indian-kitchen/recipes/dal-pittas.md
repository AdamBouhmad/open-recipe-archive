## Ingredients

- ordinary pie-crust
- dal chur churree
- ghee

## Directions

1. Prepare an ordinary pie-crust and the dal chur churree
2. Roll out the pastry, cut into circles of the size of saucers
3. Put into them a tablespoonful of the dal, and close them
4. Fry in ghee of a light brown colour
5. Serve hot
