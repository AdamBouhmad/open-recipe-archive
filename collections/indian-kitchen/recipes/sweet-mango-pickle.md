## Ingredients

- hundred green mangoes
- salt
- a seer or two pounds of sliced ginger
- half a seer of chilies finely sliced
- sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and quarter a hundred green mangoes
2. Steep them in salt for thirty-six hours
3. Drain off the salt water
4. Wipe the mangoes dry
5. Put them into a preserving-pan, with a seer or two pounds of sliced ginger, and half a seer of chilies finely sliced
6. Pour in a syrup made of sugar and vinegar (half a seer of the former to two quarts of the latter)
7. Allow the whole to simmer for ten to fifteen minutes
8. Bottle when quite cold
