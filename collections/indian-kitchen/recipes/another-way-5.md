## Ingredients

- some green mangoes
- cold milk which has been previously boiled

## Directions

1. Boil to a pulp some green mangoes without peel or stones
2. Pass through a sieve, and sweeten to taste
3. Mix into it very gradually some cold milk, which has been previously boiled
4. Keep stirring until it has acquired the thickness of an ordinary cream custard
5. Fill into glass cups
6. Grate a little cinnamon or nutmeg over them
