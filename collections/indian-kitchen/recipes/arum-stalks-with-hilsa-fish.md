## Ingredients

- hilsa fish heads, tails, gills and entrails
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- arum stalks
- ghee or oil
- cumin
- powdered cumin
- pepper
- coriander
- chillies
- water

## Directions

1. Break or cut the hilsa fish portions piecemeal and thoroughly wash them
2. Fry after smearing with powdered turmeric and salt
3. Peel fresh and tender arum stalks and cut in pieces an inch long
4. Boil and drain off the water
5. Boil ghee or oil with a few entire cumins
6. Add salt, powdered cumin, pepper, coriander and chillies and stir for 2 or 3 minutes
7. Add the boiled vegetable and stir for a few minutes more
8. Add water and on boiling add fish
9. Take down when no soup is left
