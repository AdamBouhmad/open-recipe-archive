## Ingredients

- half a pound of fresh butter melted
- a pound and a half of dried and sifted flour
- the same quantity of brown sugar
- a quarter of a pound of pounded ginger
- nine eggs, the yolks and whites separately beaten
- one glass of rose-water
- one glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Mix all well together, and beat for an hour
2. With a spoon spread it over flat tin pans, about the thickness of a penny-piece
3. Bake it of a light brown, and while warm cut it into oblong pieces, and place them on end till cool, when they will be very crisp
