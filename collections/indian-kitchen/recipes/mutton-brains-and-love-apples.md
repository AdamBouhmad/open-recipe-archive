## Ingredients

- six brains
- sixteen to twenty large tomatoes
- two chittacks or four ounces of butter
- eight biscuits
- good stock
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the brains well; clean, boil, and halve, or cut each into three pieces
2. Thoroughly butter the dish which will be put on the table; dredge it well with finely-powdered biscuit
3. Lay in the brains; cut the tomatoes, and lay them in the dish between the brains, the cut ends upwards
4. Add a small cupful of good stock, and, after sprinkling a sufficient quantity of pepper and salt as a seasoning, dredge thickly over with the ground biscuit-powder, and bake of a rich brown
5. Serve up hot
