## Ingredients

- 1 powa of water
- 1 powa of middlings
- 1 seer of sabeda
- a pinch of saffron
- water
- 0.5 seer or 3 powas of ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Mix 1 powa of water with 1 powa of middlings
2. Keep aside for 5 or 6 hours
3. Then mix with 1 seer of sabeda, a pinch of saffron and an adequate quantity of water making a thick paste
4. Boil 0.5 seer or 3 powas of ghee in a pan till vapour rises
5. Put the paste in a coconut shell or a linen bag with a small hole at the bottom
6. Drop it in the pan in a thin stream
7. Make suitable designs by manipulating the hand, the common one being a spiral of two circumferences crossed by one straight line
8. Fry both sides till hard and red
9. Take them out and keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated
