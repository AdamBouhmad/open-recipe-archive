## Ingredients

- four ounces of pistachio nuts
- six ounces of sugar
- a few drops of rose water
- a pint of new milk
- the yolks of ten eggs
- spinach-greening
- a coffee-cupful of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Blanch four ounces of pistachio nuts
2. Pound them in a mortar with six ounces of sugar and a few drops of rose water
3. When quite a smooth paste, add a pint of new milk very gradually
4. When the milk and paste have amalgamated, make a rich custard by placing the mixture en bain-marie, and adding the strained yolks of ten eggs
5. When the custard has formed nicely, strain it through a fine sieve
6. Whisk it well adding enough spinach-greening to tint the mixture a very pale green
7. Freeze it, working into it a coffee-cupful of whipped cream in the usual way when half frozen
