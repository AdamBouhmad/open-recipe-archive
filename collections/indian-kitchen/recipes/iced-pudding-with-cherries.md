## Ingredients

- preserved cherries
- liqueur-glass of kirsch

## Directions

1. Make a rich custard, retaining the pale yellow colour.
2. Add the preserved cherries to the partly frozen custard, with a liqueur-glass of kirsch.
3. In all other respects follow the directions given in Menus No. I and III.
