## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of khowa kheer
- 1 seer of milk
- 1 1/2 powa of sugar
- almonds
- pistachios
- essence of rose

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1/2 seer of khowa kheer and drop it into 1 seer of boiling milk.
2. Add 1 1/2 powa of sugar and stir constantly till the mass is thick when taking down mix with some pasted almonds, some pieces of pistachios and 3 or 4 drops of essence of rose.
3. Serve when cool.
