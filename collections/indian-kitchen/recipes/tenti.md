## Ingredients

- 3 powas ghee
- ½ seer besam of kalai
- 1 powa besam of grams
- 1 seer sugar
- 1 tola powdered ginger
- camphor

## Directions

1. Boil 3 powas of ghee
2. Mix together besam of kalai ½ seer, besam of grams 1 powa, sugar 1 seer, powdered ginger 1 tola and half a pinch of camphor
3. Add to the boiling ghee
4. Stir till the mass is a very stiff dough
5. Take it off fire
6. Prepare balls of it and serve when cool
