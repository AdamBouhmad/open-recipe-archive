## Ingredients

- a slice of seer
- a parboiled carrot
- a parboiled onion
- powdered dried thyme and marjoram
- chopped parsley
- three table-spoonfuls of salad oil
- buttered paper
- a dessert-spoonful of butter
- a dessert-spoonful of flour
- a cupful of broth

## Directions

1. Cut a parboiled carrot and one parboiled onion into thin slices
2. Add powdered dried thyme and marjoram and chopped parsley
3. Mix this up with three table-spoonfuls of salad oil
4. Cover your slice of seer with the mixture
5. Wrap the fish carefully in a sheet of buttered paper
6. Bake it for half an hour
7. Remove the paper very carefully and place the slice upon a hot dish
8. Melt a dessert-spoonful of butter and add a dessert-spoonful of flour
9. Add a cupful of broth and the vegetables in which the slice was cooked
10. Boil this for three or four minutes
11. Strain and pour it over the fish
