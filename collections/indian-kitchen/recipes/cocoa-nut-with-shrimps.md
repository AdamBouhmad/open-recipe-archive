## Ingredients

- a few big sized lobsters or shrimps
- powdered turmeric
- chillies
- pepper
- cumin
- ginger
- mustard
- salt
- mustard oil
- minced onions
- half-ripe cocoa-nut
- flour paste

## Directions

1. Remove the tips of the heads and all the scales of a few big sized lobsters or shrimps and wash.
2. Rub them with powdered turmeric, chillies, pepper, cumin, ginger, mustard, salt and mustard oil in small quantities.
3. Add minced onions if desired.
4. Stuff them into a half-ripe cocoa-nut stone broken in two with the kernel inside and the water drained off.
5. Close the two parts with a little flour paste and boil in water for an hour or more.
