## Ingredients

- 1 pound and a half of tender carrots
- 1 ounce of butter
- a pinch of salt
- 1 pinch of sugar
- a coffee-cupful of water
- 2 yolks of eggs
- a coffee-cupful of cream
- half an ounce of butter
- a table-spoonful of chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Choose a pound and a half of tender carrots, blanch them in scalding water, scrape off their tough skin, and trim them in slices the eighth of an inch thick
2. Put the pieces into a stew-pan with one ounce of butter, a pinch of salt and one of sugar, and a coffee-cupful of water
3. Cover the pan, and simmer for twenty minutes, shaking the pan occasionally to ensure even cooking
4. When done, remove the pan, let its contents cool a little, and then stir in two yolks of eggs, a coffee-cupful of cream, and half an ounce of butter
5. Add a table-spoonful of chopped parsley and serve
