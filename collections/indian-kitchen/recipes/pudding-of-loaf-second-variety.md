## Ingredients

- butter
- slices of a loaf of bread
- raisins
- milk
- sugar
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Smear the bottom and sides of a pan with some butter.
2. Arrange slices of a loaf of bread and raisins in alternate layers up to the brim.
3. Add milk minced with adequate quantities of sugar, the yolks of eggs and a pinch of salt.
4. Heat for about an hour.
