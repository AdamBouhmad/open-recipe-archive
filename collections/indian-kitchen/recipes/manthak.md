## Ingredients

- 1 chhatak of ghee
- 1/4 seer of fine flour
- water
- plenty of ghee
- sugar syrup
- 1 or 2 pinches of powdered cardamoms
- very little camphor

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly 1 chhatak of ghee with 1/4 seer of fine flour
2. Make a moderately stiff dough of it with water
3. Prepare a little flattened balls of it
4. Fry them in plenty of ghee
5. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar mixed with 1 or 2 pinches of powdered cardamoms and very little camphor till saturated
