## Ingredients

- 25 Barcelona nuts
- 12 Jordan almonds
- 2 chittacks or 4 ounces of butter free of water
- 4 tablespoonfuls of finely-sliced onions
- 12 oysters
- 6 boiled potatoes
- pork or beef sausage
- fresh lemon rind
- crumb of a slice of stale bread
- garden herbs
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Break, blanch, and slice up very fine twenty-five Barcelona nuts and a dozen Jordan almonds, and set aside
2. Fry, in two chittacks or four ounces of butter free of water, four tablespoonfuls of finely-sliced onions
3. Add to it one dozen oysters, half a dozen boiled potatoes broken up small, but not mashed, a pork or beef sausage broken up small, the rind of a fresh lemon finely sliced and chopped, the crumb of a slice of stale bread grated fine, some garden herbs, grated nutmeg
