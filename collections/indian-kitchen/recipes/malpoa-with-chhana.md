## Ingredients

- 1.5 seer of hard chhana
- 1.5 seer of sabeda
- powdered pepper
- powdered cubebs
- entire seeds of big cardamoms
- entire aniseed
- water
- ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Make a thick paste with 1.5 seer of hard chhana pasted with the hands, 1.5 seer of sabeda, powdered pepper, powdered cubebs, entire seeds of big cardamoms, entire aniseed 1 tola each and adequate quantity of water
2. Fry 0.5 powa of the paste at a time in plenty of boiling ghee
3. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated
