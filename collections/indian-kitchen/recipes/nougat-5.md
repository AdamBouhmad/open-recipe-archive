## Ingredients

- almonds
- loaf sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Blanch a pound of almonds and cut each lengthwise into thin narrow pieces
2. Lay them on a dish in front of the fire or in the oven (with the door open) to get perfectly dry
3. Melt in a sugar boiler one pound of loaf sugar
4. When the sugar is a rich brown, put in the almonds
5. Mix them well but carefully together and you will have a soft paste, which will harden when cold
6. Dip some small moulds into some very hot water, slightly but thoroughly oil them with melted butter
7. Put some of the mixture in one of the moulds
