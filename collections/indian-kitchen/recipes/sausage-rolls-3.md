## Ingredients

- equal portions of cold roast veal and ham, or cold fowl and tongue
- a teaspoonful of powdered sweet herbs
- a spoonful of mixed salt and cayenne pepper
- pie-crust pastry (recipe No. 199)

## Directions

1. Take equal portions of cold roast veal and ham, or cold fowl and tongue; chop them together very small.
2. Season with a teaspoonful of powdered sweet herbs, and a spoonful of mixed salt and cayenne pepper.
3. Mix well together.
4. Put three tablespoonfuls of the meat well rolled together into enough pastry to cover it.
5. When you have used up the whole of your materials, bake them for half an hour in a brisk oven.
