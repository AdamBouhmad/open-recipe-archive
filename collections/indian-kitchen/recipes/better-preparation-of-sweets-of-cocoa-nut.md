## Ingredients

- scraped and pasted cocoa-nut kernel 1 powa
- khowa kheer or pasted hard chhana 1 powa
- pasted almonds 1 chhatak
- sugar syrup 5 powas
- pieces of pistachios
- powdered cardamom seeds

## Directions

1. Heat together scraped and pasted cocoa-nut kernel, khowa kheer or pasted hard chhana, pasted almonds and a syrup of sugar in a brass pot
2. Stir continuously and vigorously till the mass sticks to the sides of the pot and ladle
3. Take care that it does not burn
4. Take it off fire
5. Mix with pieces of pistachios and powdered cardamom seeds
6. Make balls of it when slightly warm
7. Press in flat semi-circular or other shapes
