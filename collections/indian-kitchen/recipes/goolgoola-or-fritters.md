## Ingredients

- half a seer or one pound of flour
- half a seer or one pound of sugar
- half a seer or one pound of milk
- half a dozen small sticks of cinnamon
- a little yeast
- half a seer of ghee
- water

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with the yeast and a little milk
2. Add water sufficient to bring it to a thick consistency
3. Put into it gradually the sugar and the remainder of the milk
4. Place it on the fire, adding the cinnamon
5. Keep stirring it with a large spoon until it is again reduced to a thick consistency
6. Remove it from the fire, and when it has cooled make it up into small balls
7. Fry them in ghee
