## Ingredients

- cooked French beans
- flour
- butter
- water from cooking beans
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- egg yolks
- milk or cream
- mild grated cheese

## Directions

1. Make a sauce blanche with flour and melted butter, some of the water in which the beans were cooked, salt, pepper, and a little nutmeg
2. Enrich it with the yolks of two eggs and a little milk or cream
3. Dredge into it a table-spoonful of mild grated cheese
4. Pour it over the beans piping hot
