## Ingredients

- ripe cocoa-nut kernel
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- black cumin
- aniseed

## Directions

1. Break the stone of a ripe cocoa-nut and scrape out the kernel with a toothed iron grater
2. Grind them again into a paste
3. Add powdered turmeric, salt, a few entire black-cumin and aniseed
4. Mix thoroughly
5. Form cakes of the paste and fry
