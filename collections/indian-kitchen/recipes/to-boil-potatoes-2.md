## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Wash and pare them, throwing them into cold water as they are pared
2. Put them into a saucepan, cover them with cold water, and throw in a little salt
3. Cover the saucepan closely, and let them boil quickly for half an hour
4. Pour off the water immediately, and set the pan by the side of the fire to dry the potatoes
