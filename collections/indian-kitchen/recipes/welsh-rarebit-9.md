## Ingredients

- cheese
- butter
- made-mustard
- salt
- Nepaul pepper
- egg yolks
- bread
- white paper

## Directions

1. Grate two ounces of the cheese and mix with it an ounce of butter, a dessert-spoonful of made-mustard, a little salt, a pinch of Nepaul pepper, and the yolks of two eggs.
2. Mix well together in a basin, and work the mixture thoroughly smooth.
3. Toast a couple of neat slices of bread very carefully and butter them on both sides.
4. Place them on a dish that will stand the oven and spread the cheese mixture over them pretty thickly.
5. Place your toasts in a buttered pie-dish and spread a sheet of common white paper over them.
6. Bake for ten minutes in a really hot oven, then remove the paper and serve.
