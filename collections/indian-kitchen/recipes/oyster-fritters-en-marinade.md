## Ingredients

- oysters
- lime juice
- onion
- whole peppers
- cloves

## Directions

1. Drain the oysters from their liquor in the tin
2. Wipe them
3. Set them en marinade all the day in a soup plate with the juice of three limes, an onion sliced, some whole peppers, and a few cloves
4. Turn them occasionally till they are wanted for the friture-pan
5. Dip in batter and fry according to the previously described method
