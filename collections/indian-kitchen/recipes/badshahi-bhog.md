## Ingredients

- whites of 15 to 20 eggs
- 3 powas of sugar-candy
- a table-spoonful of rose water
- 1/2 tola of musk

## Directions

1. Take the whites of 15 to 20 eggs in a deep pan and churn the mass with a churn-staff till the pan is filled with white foams
2. Add a syrup of 3 powas of sugar-candy and churn again till thoroughly mixed up
3. Heat the mass on mild heat and stir constantly
4. Take down when broken pieces of the whites of the eggs float on the surface
5. Mix with a table-spoonful of rose water and 1/2 tola of musk
6. Keep covered till it is served
