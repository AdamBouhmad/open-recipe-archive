## Ingredients

- powdered lump sugar the weight of twelve eggs in their shells
- half that weight of potato flour
- twelve eggs
- a few drops of essence of lemon
- apricot jam
- chocolate icing
- bonbons or sweet-meats

## Directions

1. Separate the whites from the yolks of the eggs
2. Beat up the yolks with the sugar, adding a few drops of essence of lemon
3. Whisk the whites to a stiff froth
4. Mix the two together and incorporate the potato flour, beating it all the time with the egg whisk
5. Pour into a plain mould, buttered
6. Bake in a quick oven until done
7. When cold, cut the cake in horizontal slices half an inch thick
8. Spread half the slices of cake with apricot jam, and half with chocolate icing
9. Arrange the slices one over the other to form the cake again
10. Trim the slices neatly
11. Ice the cake completely over with chocolate icing, keeping it hot
12. Ornament the cake with bonbons or sweet-meats before the icing has time to cool
