## Ingredients

- six eggs
- butter
- tasty trifles for farce
- finely minced olives
- capers
- anchovies
- mushrooms
- truffles
- finely grated ham
- bruised liver of a chicken
- pate de foie gras
- sausage meat
- spiced pepper
- bread
- melted butter
- nicely fried bread-crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil six eggs for half an hour
2. Take them out and plunge them into cold water
3. When quite cold, peel off their shells
4. With a dessert-knife rubbed in butter, divide each egg in half, slicing a little piece of the rounded ends to admit of each half sitting upright upon a dish
5. Pick out the yolks
6. Pound them with butter in a mortar
7. Dress them with any tasty trifles at your command
8. Season the composition delicately
9. Fill the egg cases therewith, trimming the farce neatly with a dessert-knife dipped in melted butter in a convex-shape over each case
10. Fry a little square of bread for each one, as for canapes, and place them thereon
11. Arrange them on a flat silver dish slightly buttered
12. Pour a little melted butter over each egg
13. Bake for five minutes
14. Strew some nicely fried bread-crumbs over the dish when going to table
