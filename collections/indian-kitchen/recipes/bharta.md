## Ingredients

- fish of medium size
- clean linen piece
- fine mud
- ginger
- cumin
- pepper
- chillies
- cardamom
- flour
- salt
- ginger-juice
- ghee

## Directions

1. Flake a fish of medium size, remove its intestines and wash.
2. Wrap it up with a clean linen piece, coat with fine mud and burn on fire to a brown colour.
3. Remove the earth and the linen piece carefully.
4. Remove the bones, mix with ginger, cumin, pepper, chillies, cardamom, pasted or powdered, a little flour, salt and ginger-juice.
5. Fry with ghee.
