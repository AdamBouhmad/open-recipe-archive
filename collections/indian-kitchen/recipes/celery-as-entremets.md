## Ingredients

- celery
- water
- butter
- Dutch sauce

## Directions

1. trim the heads neatly and cut short, say five or six inches in length
2. split lengthwise in two or four pieces according to the thickness of the head
3. plunge the pieces into fast boiling water
4. as soon as tender, drain, turn out upon a hot silver dish
5. serve like asparagus with a pat of butter melting over them
6. send a piping hot Dutch sauce round in a boat
