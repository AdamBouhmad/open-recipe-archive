## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer middlings
- 1/2 seer sabeda
- water
- saffron
- ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Make a thin paste of 1/2 seer of middlings and 1/2 seer of sabeda with water
2. It may be coloured with a pinch of saffron
3. Boil ghee in a pan and hold on it a ladle with big perforations
4. Pour the solution in the ladle and shake it up and down that big drops may fall in the ghee
5. Take them out when fried hard and red
6. Keep in a syrup of sugar till saturated
