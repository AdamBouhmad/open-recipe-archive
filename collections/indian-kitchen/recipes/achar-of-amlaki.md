## Ingredients

- ripe and fresh amlaki fruits
- sugar
- powders of chillies
- pepper
- aniseed
- cardamom seeds
- pasted or powdered ginger
- mustard oil

## Directions

1. Make a few deep cuts on each of some ripe and fresh amlaki fruits and remove the seeds if you can
2. Boil them in plenty of water till soft
3. Drain out the water and wash with fresh cold water
4. Drop them in a boiling syrup of sugar and boil for 20 or 25 minutes
5. Make a paste of powders of chillies, pepper, aniseed, cardamom seeds, pasted or powdered ginger in adequate quantities with little mustard oil
6. Smear the boiled fruits with it and preserve in an airtight jar
