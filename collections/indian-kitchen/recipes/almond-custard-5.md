## Ingredients

- quarter of a pound almonds
- two tablespoonfuls orange-flower water
- more than one pint milk
- one teaspoonful corn-flour
- yolks of six eggs
- pounded loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound almonds with two tablespoonfuls of orange-flower water
2. Add more than a pint of milk, thickened with a teaspoonful of corn-flour, and the well-beaten-up yolks of six eggs
3. Sweeten to taste with pounded loaf sugar
4. Stir it over a slow fire till it thickens, but do not allow it to boil
5. Serve up in glass custard-cups
