## Ingredients

- khowa kheer
- ghee
- eggs
- sugar
- cardamom seeds
- raisins
- almonds
- rose water
- flour

## Directions

1. Fry a half cap of khowa kheer in ghee and grind it finely
2. Mix it with two cups of the contents of eggs, some sugar, cardamom seeds, raisins, pasted almonds and a little rose water, and beat thoroughly
3. Take a few casting moulds for shaping and smear their interior surfaces with a little ghee
4. Stuff them with the mixture, close them with a little flour-paste and boil in water for about 15 minutes
5. Then open the joints of the moulds and the food is ready to be served
