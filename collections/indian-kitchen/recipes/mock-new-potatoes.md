## Ingredients

- waxy potatoes
- flour
- butter
- mace
- milk
- egg yolk
- parsley

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes as usual
2. When nearly done, cut them into pieces the shape and size of a pigeon's egg
3. Make a flour and butter sauce blanche slightly flavoured with mace
4. Put the pieces of potato into it
5. Simmer the potatoes in the sauce, and when thoroughly done
6. Add chopped parsley, a coffee-cupful of milk in which the yolk of an egg has been stirred, and a lump of butter at the last moment and serve
