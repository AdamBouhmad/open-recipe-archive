## Ingredients

- one good sized rabbit
- half a pound of bacon
- forcemeat
- pound of gravy beef
- spiced pepper
- rabbit liver and kidneys

## Directions

1. Skin and wash a fine rabbit
2. Cut it up in the usual way as if for a stew, and put the pieces to soak in cold water
3. When quite clean, drain them, wipe them dry with a clean cloth, and set them aside
4. Put the head, the lower joints of the legs, and all scraps of the rabbit, with the beef, and the usual ingredients for flavouring a gravy into a large sauce-pan, and make the best gravy you can with them for the pie
5. When this has been done to your satisfaction, and the fat has been skimmed off the surface of the liquor, make a plain forcemeat as described for turkey, mingling with it the liver and kidneys of the rabbit very finely chopped
6. Spread a thin layer of forcemeat at the bottom of the pie-dish
7. Immediately above it put a layer of bacon slices
8. Then put the coarser joints of the rabbit, dusting them with spiced pepper, and filling the interstices between the pieces with forcemeat
9. Put a second layer of bacon over the rabbit, and rabbit again above that
10. Repeat the forcemeat dressing
11. When the pie-dish is full, pour in the gravy till it almost reaches the level of the topmost layer
12. Garnish as usual, cover the pie with paste, and bake for about one hour and a quarter
