## Ingredients

- besam
- middlings
- water
- saffron
- ghee
- sugar syrup
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Make a thin paste of equal quantities of besam and middlings with water
2. Take half of the solution in another pot and mix with saffron
3. Fry bonde of medium size separately with the two solutions
4. Take them out of the ghee before they are hard
5. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated
6. Mix the red and yellow bonde thoroughly and take them out
7. Mix with raisins, pieces of almonds and a little hot ghee
8. Make balls of it
