## Ingredients

- choplets from a neck of mutton
- fat bacon
- pieces of truffle
- pieces of tongue
- gravy made from the bones, flap, and trimmings
- reduced bechamel or Espagnole sauce

## Directions

1. For this dish select the nicest choplets you can from a neck of mutton
2. Trim them neatly
3. Lard them with fat bacon
4. Introduce some pieces of truffle judiciously here and there by making incisions with a sharp, pointed root knife
5. Pieces of tongue may also be inserted in this manner; they should be cut square at the head and tapering to a point like nails
6. When ready, stew the cutlets very tenderly in gravy made from the bones, flap, and trimmings
7. Take them out, and set them to get cold with a weight above them
8. When cold, mask the cutlets on one side only with reduced bechamel, or Espagnole sauce
9. When this mask or glaze has set, trim the edges neatly, and the cutlet is ready
