## Ingredients

- two cocoanuts
- three-quarters of a pound of sugar
- half a pound of rice-flour, finely sifted
- butter

## Directions

1. Extract a cupful of milk from two cocoanuts, and set it aside
2. Make a syrup of three-quarters of a pound of sugar
3. Mix into the syrup half a pound of rice-flour finely sifted, and the cocoanut milk
4. Boil over a good fire, stirring the whole while until it thickens
5. Pour it into a buttered pudding-dish, and bake it of a rich light-brown colour
