## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts
- butter
- parsley
- lime
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the Brussels sprouts
2. Toss them in butter in a saucepan with some minced parsley and the juice of a lime sprinkled over them, and salt and pepper to taste
