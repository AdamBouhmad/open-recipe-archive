## Ingredients

- peelings of potato
- sweet gourd
- jhinga
- green banana
- tender pumpkin
- oil
- salt
- ground turmeric
- red chillies
- mustard or cumin

## Directions

1. Cut them in small pieces and wash thoroughly.
2. Boil in water and drain.
3. Fry them with oil.
4. Add the water in which they were boiled with salt, ground turmeric, red chillies, and mustard or cumin.
5. Stir and take down when no soup is left.
