## Ingredients

- 1 seer of milk
- 1 powa of sugar-candy
- 1/2 powa of ghee
- 1/2 powa of almonds

## Directions

1. Boil the milk on mild heat.
2. Add the syrup and ghee to it in four instalments at intervals.
3. Last of all add the pasted almonds.
4. Take down when thick.
5. Stir all along to prevent the formation of cream.
