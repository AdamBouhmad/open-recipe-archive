## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef or a small but good leg of mutton or two legs of a kid
- sliced onions
- ginger
- salt
- water
- fried onions
- hard-boiled eggs
- fried bacon

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of beef, and cut up as for a curry, or take a small but good leg of mutton, or two legs of a kid, rejecting the loin
2. Make a good, strong gravy with seasoning of sliced onions, ginger, and salt, with water, which when cooked down will be reduced to about sufficient only to cover the rice
3. Proceed to make the pellow in all respects as directed in the chicken pellow recipe
4. If using beef, it is not further used for the table
5. If using kid or mutton, treat the legs the same as the chicken, and serve up with fried onions, hard-boiled eggs, and fried bacon
