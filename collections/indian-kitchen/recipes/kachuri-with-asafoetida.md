## Ingredients

- husked pulses of kalai
- water
- asafoetida
- entire aniseeds
- black cumin
- salt
- ghee

## Directions

1. Keep some husked pulses of kalai under water for two hours
2. Grind them to a paste
3. Mix thoroughly with a little asafoetida melted with water, entire aniseeds, black cumin and salt
4. Fry this mass with ghee for 15 to 20 minutes
5. Prepare kachuri as in Kachurl recipe stuffed with this new preparation
