## Ingredients

- 5/8 seer of fine flour
- 1/2 seer of boiled milk
- 2 seers of sugar
- 1 seer or more of ghee
- a pinch of saffron
- seeds of a few cardamoms
- raisins
- a paste or pieces of pistachios

## Directions

1. Mix 5/8 seer of fine flour with 1/2 seer of boiled milk and heat
2. Stir constantly
3. After a time add a concentrated syrup of 2 seers of sugar
4. When partly thick add 1 seer or more of ghee little by little
5. Last of all add a pinch of saffron, the seeds of a few cardamoms, raisins and a paste or pieces of pistachios as desired
6. Take down when very thick
7. Pour in a flat dish
8. Cut in cubes when cold and solid
