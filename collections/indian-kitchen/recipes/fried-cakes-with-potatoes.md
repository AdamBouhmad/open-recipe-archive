## Ingredients

- potatoes
- powdered pepper
- red chillies
- salt
- powdered turmeric
- besam
- eggs
- oil

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes and then peel them
2. Rub them into a paste and mix powdered pepper, red chillies and salt
3. Form thin cakes and dip them into a thick solution of besam with salt or into the contents of eggs beaten with salt, and fry them in plenty of oil
4. Or slice the boiled potatoes and dip them into either of the two and fry them similarly
