## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound of butter
- 4 ounces of powdered loaf sugar
- eggs
- 1/2 ounce of fine flour
- buttered flat tin
- apricot jam
- chocolate icing
- powdered loaf sugar
- grated chocolate
- vanilla essence
- water

## Directions

1. Take 1/4 pound of the freshest butter, put it in a bowl, and warm it until it can be beaten with a spoon
2. Add to it 4 ounces of powdered loaf sugar, and beat the two together until a smooth white cream is obtained
3. Add one egg, and keep on beating the mixture till it is smooth again
4. Add three more eggs in the same manner
5. Remove the germ of the eggs
6. Incorporate quickly 1/2 ounce of fine flour with the mixture
7. As soon as it is smooth, pour it out to the thickness of 1/4 inch on a buttered flat tin
8. Put it into the oven at once
9. When done (in about ten to fifteen minutes), turn out the slab of Genoise, and put it to cool, underside uppermost, on a sieve
10. Take a slab of Genoise, spread on the top of it the thinnest possible coating of apricot jam
11. Apply a coating of chocolate icing
12. Put it into a very hot oven for rather less than a minute
13. Take it out, and place it in a cold place to get cool
14. Cut it up with a sharp knife in any shapes you like
15. For chocolate icing: Put into a sauce-pan 1/4 pound of powdered loaf sugar, 2 ounces of grated chocolate, a tea-spoonful of vanilla essence, and about a gill of water
16. Stir on the fire until the mixture assumes the consistence of a thick smooth cream
