## Ingredients

- fine flour 1 seer
- ghee 1 powa
- entire black sesames 1 1/2 chhatak
- some entire aniseeds
- ghee for frying
- sugar for concentrated syrup

## Directions

1. Mix together fine flour, ghee, entire black sesames and some entire aniseeds
2. Make a very stiff paste with as little water as possible
3. Make balls of it or cut with a knife in cubes
4. Fry hard in plenty of ghee
5. Keep immersed in a very concentrated syrup of sugar till saturated
6. Take them out and dry in the air
