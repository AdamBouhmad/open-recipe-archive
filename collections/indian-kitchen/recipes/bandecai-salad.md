## Ingredients

- young bandecais
- anchovy fillets
- iced salad dressing
- crushed ice

## Directions

1. Gather bandecais young and boil till tender.
2. Arrange the bandecais on a flat dish placed over some crushed ice.
3. With a dessert-knife slit each one open longitudinally.
4. In the slit put a fillet of anchovy.
5. Pour a little iced salad dressing over all.
