## Ingredients

- green mint
- 1 tablespoonful of the young leaves
- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful of brown sugar

## Directions

1. Pick and wash some green mint
2. Mince it and add a tablespoonful of the young leaves to four of vinegar
3. Put it into a sauce-tureen, with a teaspoonful of brown sugar
