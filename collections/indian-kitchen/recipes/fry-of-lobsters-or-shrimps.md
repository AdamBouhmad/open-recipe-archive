## Ingredients

- shrimps
- powdered pepper
- salt
- minced potatoes
- eggs
- powdered turmeric
- flour

## Directions

1. Remove the scales except that on the head and wash thoroughly
2. Remove that on the head, make a deep cut lengthwise and stuff it with powdered pepper, salt and minced potatoes
3. Dip it into the liquid contents of eggs beaten with salt and powdered turmeric
4. Roll on flour and fry on mild heat
