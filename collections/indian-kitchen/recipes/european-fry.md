## Ingredients

- big pieces of fish
- minced or ground ginger
- onion
- turmeric
- salt
- eggs
- powdered turmeric
- bread crumbs
- ghee or butter

## Directions

1. Smear big pieces of fish with minced or ground ginger, onion, turmeric and salt
2. Dip them into egg-contents beaten with powdered turmeric and salt
3. Roll on bread crumbs and fry in hot ghee or butter
