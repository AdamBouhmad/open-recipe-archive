## Ingredients

- hard chhana 1 seer
- seeds of a few cardamoms
- 4 drops of essence of rose
- sabeda 1 powa (optional)
- sugar candy
- kasir chini for syrup or white sugar

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1 seer of hard chhana
2. Mix with the seeds of a few cardamoms and 4 drops of essence of rose
3. Optionally mix 1 powa of sabeda
4. Make balls of it
5. Stuff each with a piece of sugar candy to make them hollow
6. Drop into a boiling syrup of kasir chini
7. Boil till they are brownish in colour
8. If using white sugar syrup boil till stiff
9. Keep them in the syrup till they are served
10. Eat them up within 3 or 4 days
