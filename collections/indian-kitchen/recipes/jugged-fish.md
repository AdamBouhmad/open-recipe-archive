## Ingredients

- fish fillets
- pepper
- salt
- Bombay onion
- pepper corns
- cloves
- black pepper
- tomatoes
- curled parsley
- capsicums
- fish bones and trimmings
- chablis, sauterne, or hock
- garlic
- lime rind

## Directions

1. Trim the fish in fillets, season them with pepper and salt.
2. Cover the bottom of the tin with slices of Bombay onion, dotting in a dozen pepper corns, and two cloves.
3. Put a layer of fillets over the onion, and pepper them with black pepper.
4. Put in a layer of sliced tomatoes, sprinkle some roughly-chopped curled parsley over them, and a table-spoonful of chopped capsicums.
5. Pour in a little broth made from the fish bones and trimmings, with a glass of chablis, sauterne, or hock.
6. A clove of garlic may be introduced in this dish by those who appreciate it, and the fine rind of a lime also.
7. Cover the tin closely, and boil.
