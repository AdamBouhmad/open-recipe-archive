## Ingredients

- tin of lobster
- Bombay onion
- ounce of butter
- sherry glass of chablis or sauterne
- breakfast-cupful of rich thick brown sauce
- breakfast-cupful of tomato puree
- Nepaul pepper

## Directions

1. Open a tin of lobster
2. Choose all the larger pieces and put all the mashed fragments aside to be used in bouchees or croquettes for some other meal
3. Wash and drain the firm pieces, dry them, and cut them up into quarter inch collops
4. Pile them in the centre of a dish that will stand the oven
5. Cut up a good sized Bombay onion and fry it in an ounce of butter
6. Add off the fire a sherry glass of chablis or sauterne
7. When the onion seems cooked, stir in a breakfast-cupful of rich thick brown sauce and the same quantity of tomato puree
8. Add a strong suspicion of Nepaul pepper and reduce the mixture for five minutes
9. When nice and thick, pour the sauce over the lobster
10. Put the dish into the oven and when thoroughly hot, serve
