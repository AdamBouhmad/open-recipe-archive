## Ingredients

- two fresh eggs
- finely-sifted flour

## Directions

1. Take the yolks and white of two fresh eggs, and as much finely-sifted flour as will make a good dough of the consistency of dough for pie-crusts without the addition of any water
2. Roll it out to its full extent on a large board to about the thickness of an eight-anna piece
3. Cut it up into small squares, diamonds, or circles, or into any shape or design you please
4. Do this quickly, as within an hour of its being rolled out the pastry will harden
5. It may be used immediately, or in the winter it may be kept good for a few days
