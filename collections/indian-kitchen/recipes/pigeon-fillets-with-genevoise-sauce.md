## Ingredients

- eight young pigeons
- oil
- vinegar
- minced parsley
- shallot
- breakfast-cupful of gravy
- onion
- clove of garlic
- butter the size of an hen's egg
- two or three anchovies
- pepper
- salt
- juice of a lime
- one glass of claret
- flour
- caramel
- bacon
- petits pois verts

## Directions

1. Lightly roast eight young pigeons
2. Slice the breasts of the birds off whole
3. Place the eight portions so obtained en marinade in oil, vinegar, minced parsley, and shallot
4. Take the bones, trimmings, livers, &c., and put them into a sauce-pan with a good breakfast-cupful of gravy
5. Simmer this until you have extracted the essence of your pigeon scraps
6. Strain it
7. Chop up an onion, and one clove of garlic very small
8. Stir a piece of butter the size of an hen's egg at the bottom of a sauce-pan over the fire
9. Throw in your chopped onion, &c., let it slightly brown
10. Add the gravy you previously made, with two or three anchovies chopped into dice, pepper and salt to taste, and the juice of a lime, with one glass of claret
11. Bring this sauce to boiling point
12. Let it simmer awhile
13. Strain it
14. Replace it in the sauce-pan
15. Thicken it with butter and a little flour
16. Colour it with caramel
17. Place your fillets in it to gently heat up without coming to the boil
18. When quite hot, place the fillets in their dish, pouring the sauce round them and serve, with a crisply fried curl of bacon, between each of them
19. Fill the centre of the dish with petits pois verts
