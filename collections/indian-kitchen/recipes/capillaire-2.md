## Ingredients

- a quart of water
- three pounds of lump sugar
- one pound of soft sugar
- the whites and yolks of two eggs well beaten up
- two ounces of the best orange-flower water

## Directions

1. To a quart of water add three pounds of lump sugar, one pound of soft sugar, and the whites and yolks of two eggs well beaten up
2. Boil it gently, and skim well
3. On the scum ceasing to rise, remove the pan from the fire
4. Add two ounces of the best orange-flower water
5. Strain through flannel
