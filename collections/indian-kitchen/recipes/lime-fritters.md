## Ingredients

- four ounces of flour
- one ounce of fresh butter
- one coffee-cup of milk
- one ounce of finely sifted sugar
- rasped peel of three nice limes
- a few drops of lemon essence
- four eggs
- boiling fat for frying
- powdered sugar for dusting
- white sugar
- water
- a glass of brandy or Madeira
- juice of a lime

## Directions

1. Mix together in a sauce-pan four ounces of flour and an ounce of fresh butter
2. Add a coffee-cup of milk and stir to the consistency of a thick batter; if too thick dilute with a little more milk
3. Stir in one ounce of finely sifted sugar and the rasped peel of three nice limes with a few drops of lemon essence
4. Stir the whole mixture over the fire till thoroughly heated, but on no account let it boil
5. When well mixed and hot, place the batter on one side to get cool
6. Break four eggs into a bowl separating the yolks from the whites
7. Beat the former and whisk the latter to a froth
8. Add the yolks to the cool batter and stir thoroughly
9. At the last add the whisked whites
10. Prepare a deep bath of boiling fat and set your frying basket therein
11. Drop the batter into it by dessert-spoonfuls at a time
12. Each spoonful will form itself into a ball and turn gradually a golden yellow
13. When they are all done, drain and serve them in a napkin, dusted over with well powdered sugar
14. For the sauce: Make a nice syrup with white sugar and water, stir in a glass of brandy or Madeira, give it the juice of a lime, and serve hot
