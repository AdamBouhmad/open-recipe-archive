## Ingredients

- half a pound or a coonkeeful of rice
- water

## Directions

1. Wash half a pound or a coonkeeful of rice
2. put it to boil in a large quantity of water, over a brisk fire
3. Keep the cover of the pot partially open, and stir the rice to prevent an entire overflow of the water
4. On the subsiding of the water or the bubbling, reduce the fire, until the grains of rice, without being pappy, are quite soft
5. Remove the pot from the fire and add a quart of cold water
6. Drain all the liquid, which is conjee
7. Replace the pot over a gentle charcoal heat, to allow all moisture to evaporate, assisting the process by occasionally shaking the pot, or stirring its contents gently with a wooden spoon
8. Time to boil: half an hour
