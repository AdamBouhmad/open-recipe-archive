## Ingredients

- eggs, six or eight
- ghee
- ground condiments
- sliced fried onions
- water

## Directions

1. Boil eggs hard, shell, and cut into halves
2. Set them aside
3. Use ghee, ground condiments, and sliced fried onions in all respects the same as for a chicken doopiaja
4. Observe precisely the same method of cooking, keeping in mind that a smaller quantity of water and a shorter time to simmer will suffice, as the eggs are already cooked or boiled
