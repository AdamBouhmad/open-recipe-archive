## Ingredients

- two pounds of fat beef
- half a cup of good vinegar
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- ground condiments
- ghee or lard
- peppercorns
- bay-leaves

## Directions

1. Cut up two pounds of fat beef into large squares, and steep them in the vinegar, together with half a teaspoonful of salt and all the ground condiments, from eighteen to twenty-four hours.
2. Warm the ghee or lard and throw in the meat, together with the condiments and vinegar in which it had been steeped, adding a few peppercorns and bay-leaves.
3. Allow to simmer gently over a slow fire for two hours, or until the meat is perfectly tender, and serve up hot.
