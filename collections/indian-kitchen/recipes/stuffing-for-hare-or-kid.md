## Ingredients

- liver
- grated bread
- fat bacon
- bit of butter the size of a walnut
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- chopped lemon thyme
- parsley
- 1 egg beaten

## Directions

1. Parboil the liver, and mince it
2. Add an equal quantity of grated bread, double the quantity of fat bacon chopped, and a bit of butter the size of a walnut
3. Season with pepper, salt, nutmeg, chopped lemon thyme, and parsley
4. Bind with an egg beaten
