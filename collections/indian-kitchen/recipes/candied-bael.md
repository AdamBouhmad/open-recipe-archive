## Ingredients

- bael fruit
- boiling clarified sugar or syrup

## Directions

1. Select fruit as for the preserve
2. Cut it into slices and remove the seeds and gum
3. After steeping it in cold water, drain it
4. Put it in a preserving-pan with sufficient boiling clarified sugar or syrup to cover it
5. Simmer it over a slow fire for half an hour or until it becomes quite tender
6. Remove the pan from the fire
7. Lay the fruit on some fresh oiled paper spread on tin trays
8. Expose it to the sun where it will crystallize in a few hours, and the oil will prevent it adhering to the paper
