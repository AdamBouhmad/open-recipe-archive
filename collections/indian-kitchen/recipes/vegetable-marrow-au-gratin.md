## Ingredients

- cold boiled vegetable marrow
- butter
- eggs
- mild cheese
- gravy
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Slice up a cold boiled vegetable marrow
2. Butter the bottom of a pie-dish and lay a layer of slices thereon
3. Grate a little mild cheese over them
4. Place another layer of slices above the first and strew grated cheese over that also
5. Melt some butter in a small sauce-pan and add a little gravy to it, together with some finely powdered cheese
6. When you have enough to half cover your layers of marrow, pour the liquid round them
7. Salt and pepper should be dusted over each layer, before the cheese gratings are added
8. Bake for ten minutes or so in a fast oven
