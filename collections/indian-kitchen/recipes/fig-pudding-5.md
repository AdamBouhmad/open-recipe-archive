## Ingredients

- finely grated breadcrumbs
- minced dried figs
- sugar
- chopped suet
- grated nutmeg
- Yeatman's baking powder
- flour
- eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Take a quarter of a pound of finely grated breadcrumbs, half a pound of minced dried figs, three ounces of sugar, six ounces of chopped suet, and a little grated nutmeg
2. Mix a teaspoonful of Yeatman's baking powder with an ounce of flour
3. Add this to the other ingredients with four eggs well beaten
4. Moisten with a little milk sufficiently to form a firm paste
5. Turn the mixture into a buttered mould and boil for three hours
