## Ingredients

- mutton 1 seer
- onions 0.5 powa
- carrot 1
- turnip 1
- cumin 2 tolas
- pepper 1 tola
- garam-masalla 1 tola
- butter 3 chhataks
- salt 1.5 tola
- flour

## Directions

1. Mince the meat and fry it with butter till brown.
2. Add water and boil under cover for an hour.
3. Add all the spices pasted together, salt and flour mixed with a little water when the meat is boiled.
4. Meanwhile cut the carrot, turnip and onions in small cubes and boil in water.
5. Heat the rest of the butter in another pan and brown the boiled vegetables in it.
6. Add the boiled meat with soup in it.
7. Boil a few minutes more.
8. Take down with soup as required.
