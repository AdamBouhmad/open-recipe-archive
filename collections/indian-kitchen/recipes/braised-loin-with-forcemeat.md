## Ingredients

- a loin
- slices of bacon
- the meat of the undercut
- kidney
- the nicest fat
- herbs
- spiced pepper
- chopped bacon
- a little onion
- mushroom or truffle parings
- four eggs
- gravy made from the loin's own bones and trimmings
- a glass of Madeira
- a clove of garlic
- scraps of vegetables of all kinds
- boiled maccaroni

## Directions

1. Bone the loin and introduce a number of slices of bacon under the flap
2. Fill the inside with a forcemeat made with the meat of the undercut chopped up small, with the kidney (scalded first), and some of the nicest fat, seasoned with herbs, spiced pepper, chopped bacon, a little onion, and any mushroom or truffle parings
3. Bind the salpicon with four eggs, and spread it thickly over the inside of the boned loin
4. Roll the joint up, and tie it firmly into shape, do not skewer it
5. Braise the loin in a gravy made from its own bones and trimmings, with a glass of Madeira, a clove of garlic, and scraps of vegetables of all kinds arranged round it
6. When done and nicely browned, lift up the loin and remove the string
7. Dish it on a layer of boiled maccaroni
8. Strain off the gravy, throw away the garlic, and pass all the vegetables through the sieve
9. Thicken the gravy, add the vegetable pulp, and when thoroughly incorporated, pour it round the loin and serve
