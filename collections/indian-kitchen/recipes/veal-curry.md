## Ingredients

- a small shoulder of veal
- ghee
- water
- ground onions
- ground turmeric
- ground chilies
- ground ginger
- ground garlic
- salt

## Directions

1. Select a small shoulder of veal
2. Cut off sixteen or eighteen one-inch square pieces of the best part of the meat
3. Curry it in every particular the same as a chicken
4. Allow it to simmer half to three-quarters of an hour
