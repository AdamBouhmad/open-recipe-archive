## Ingredients

- two quarts barley-water
- juice and rind of one lemon
- honey, capillaire, or sugar

## Directions

1. Pour while hot two quarts of barley-water on the juice and rind of a lemon very thinly cut
2. Add honey, capillaire, or sugar according to taste
3. Let it stand one hour and strain
