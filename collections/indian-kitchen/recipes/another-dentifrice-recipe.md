## Ingredients

- two ounces of borax
- three pints of boiling water
- a teaspoonful of tincture of myrrh
- a tablespoonful of spirits of camphor

## Directions

1. Dissolve two ounces of borax in three pints of boiling water
2. Before quite cold, add a teaspoonful of tincture of myrrh and a tablespoonful of spirits of camphor
3. Bottle the mixture for use
4. One wineglassful of the solution, with half a pint of tepid water, is sufficient for each application
