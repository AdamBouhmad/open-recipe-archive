## Ingredients

- a pound of dried and sifted flour
- half a pound of fresh butter
- seven ounces of sifted loaf sugar
- seven ounces of cleaned and dried currants
- a little grated nutmeg
- a little water
- two tablespoonfuls of rose or orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Rub into a pound of dried and sifted flour half a pound of fresh butter
2. Add seven ounces of sifted loaf sugar
3. Add seven ounces of cleaned and dried currants
4. Add a little grated nutmeg
5. Make it into a paste with a little water and two tablespoonfuls of rose or orange-flower water
6. Roll it out and cut it into round cakes
7. Prick them
8. Bake them upon tins dusted with flour
