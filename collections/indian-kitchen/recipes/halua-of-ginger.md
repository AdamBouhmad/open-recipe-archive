## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of ginger
- water
- flour
- 1/2 seer of ghee
- 3 powas of sugar

## Directions

1. Skin 1/2 seer of ginger, cut them in pieces and boil in about 1/2 seer of water till the water is reduced to 1/2 powa or less.
2. Then make a paste of the boiled pieces and mix with 1 1/2 powa of flour and the water in which they were boiled.
3. Fry this mass with about 1/2 seer of ghee and then pour in a syrup of 3 powas of sugar.
4. Stir constantly and take down when thick.
