## Ingredients

- handful of pudina leaves
- 2 or 3 green chillies
- tamarind pulp
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a paste of a handful of pudina leaves with 2 or 3 green chillies
2. Mix with tamarind pulp, salt and sugar adequate quantities
3. Serve
