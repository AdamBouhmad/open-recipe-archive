## Ingredients

- 2 seers of dried paddy
- 1 seer of sugar
- 1 seer of fresh khai
- 1/2 tola of cardamom seeds
- 1/2 tola of powdered cinnamon
- 1 tola of powdered pepper
- 1 tola of dried and powdered ginger
- very little camphor
- pasted almonds and pistachios
- 1/2 powa of pure cows' ghee
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Keep 2 seers of dried paddy out on a cold moist floor for 2 to 3 hours
2. Heat a quantity of sands in a pan till they are very hot
3. Throw a handful of the paddy on the hot sands and stir them
4. The paddy will burst into khai in half a minute
5. Remove them immediately to avoid burning
6. Fry the whole quantity of paddy in this way
7. Remove the chaffs
8. Boil a syrup of 1 seer of sugar
9. Add 1 seer of fresh khai and stir constantly
10. When the khai are powdered and mixed up with the syrup add seeds of small cardamoms 1/2 tola, powdered cinnamon 1/2 tola, powdered pepper 1 tola and dried and powdered ginger 1 tola and very little camphor
11. Add pasted almonds and pistachios as desired
12. Stir till the mass is gluey
13. Taking down mix with 1/2 powa of pure cows' ghee
14. Make balls when it is yet warm
15. Roll the ball on powdered sugar and serve when cold
