## Ingredients

- four partridges
- stock
- carrot
- onion
- piece of celery
- sweet herbs
- glass of Madeira
- red currant jelly
- lime peel
- aspic jelly
- tarragon
- white of hard-boiled egg
- slices of truffle
- cornichons
- sauce ravigote
- dry toast

## Directions

1. Gently stew four partridges in stock with a carrot, an onion, a piece of celery, sweet herbs, and a glass of Madeira
2. When done, set the birds to get cold and strain the gravy in which they were done
3. Cut off the fillets of the partridges and pick all the other meat from the bones as well
4. Put the fillets aside
5. Simmer the skin, bones pounded, and scraps in the gravy again with a little red currant jelly and lime peel
6. Strain and reduce it to a glaze
7. Pound the meat picked from the backs and thighs with the livers and make a rich savoury paste with your glaze
8. Prepare your aspic jelly slightly flavoured with tarragon
9. Decorate a plain mould with white of hard-boiled egg, slices of truffle and cornichons
10. Pour in a little jelly and set the garnish
11. Pack your mould with the fillets of partridge between layers of the paste and slices of truffle
12. Ice this and present it with sauce ravigote also iced in a boat, and dry toast
