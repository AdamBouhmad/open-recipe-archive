## Ingredients

- mustard oil, one chittack or two ounces
- water, two cups
- ground onions, four teaspoonfuls
- ground turmeric, one teaspoonful
- ground chilies, one teaspoonful
- ground garlic, a quarter of a teaspoonful
- fish

## Directions

1. Thoroughly clean, rub, and wash the fish in salt and water before cooking
2. Use the condiments as specified, cooking the fish in the oil with the ground spices and water
