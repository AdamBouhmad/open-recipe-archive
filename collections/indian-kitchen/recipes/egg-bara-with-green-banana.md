## Ingredients

- green banana
- eggs
- minced onions
- besam
- salt
- ginger-juice
- powdered turmeric
- aniseed
- black-cumin
- ghee or oil

## Directions

1. Boil a developed green banana and peel it
2. Make a muddy paste with the boiled banana, the contents of 3 eggs, minced onions, a little besam, salt, ginger-juice, powdered turmeric, aniseed and black-cumin by beating them thoroughly
3. Form small cakes of it and fry to crispness in ghee or oil
4. Serve while hot
