## Ingredients

- cooked French beans
- flour
- butter
- water from cooking beans
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- raw egg yolk or cream
- lime-juice

## Directions

1. Make a sauce blanche with flour and melted butter, some of the water in which the beans were cooked, salt, pepper, and a little nutmeg
2. Stir into this the yolk of a raw egg or a spoonful of cream
3. Give it two or three drops of lime-juice
4. Add over the beans
