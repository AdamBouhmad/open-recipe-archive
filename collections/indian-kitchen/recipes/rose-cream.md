## Ingredients

- a quart of spirits of wine
- twelve drops of the oil of roses
- three drops of oil of nutmeg
- a quart of syrup
- a sufficient quantity of pink tincture

## Directions

1. Into a quart of spirits of wine put twelve drops of the oil of roses and three of oil of nutmeg
2. shake the mixture well until the oils are dissolved
3. add a quart of syrup and a sufficient quantity of pink tincture to produce a fine rose-colour
4. filter and bottle
