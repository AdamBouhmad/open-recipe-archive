## Ingredients

- yolks of eight eggs
- pint of fresh milk
- vanilla
- coffee-cupful of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Mix the yolks of eight eggs well with a pint of fresh milk
2. Strain it and sweeten it to taste
3. Flavour it with vanilla
4. Set it in a sauce-pan in your bain-marie to thicken
5. When you have got a nice thick custard, whip it well and let it get cold
6. Pour it into your ice pot and freeze it
