## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- pint of gravy
- slice of lean ham
- onion
- cloves
- parsley
- thyme
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Pare the cucumbers, and let them lie in vinegar and water with a little salt in it
2. drain them
3. put them into a saucepan with a pint of gravy, a slice of lean ham, an onion stuck with one or two cloves, and a bunch of parsley and thyme
4. let them stew, closely covered, till tender
5. Take out the cucumbers, strain and thicken the gravy with a piece of butter rolled in flour
6. boil it up, and pour it over the cucumbers
