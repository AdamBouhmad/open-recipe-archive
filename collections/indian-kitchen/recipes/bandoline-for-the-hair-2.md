## Ingredients

- olive oil, two ounces
- spermaceti, one drachm
- oil of bergamot, one drachm
- otto of roses, six drops
- annatto, half a drachm

## Directions

1. Mix two ounces of olive oil with one drachm each of spermaceti and oil of bergamot
2. Heat and strain
3. Beat in six drops of otto of roses
4. If colour be desired, add half a drachm of annatto
