## Ingredients

- one entire fish, 1 or 1.5 seers
- ghee, one powa
- besam, one powa
- garam-masalla, half tola
- pepper, one tola
- coriander, 2 tolas
- curd, half powa
- salt, 2 tolas
- a few cassia leaves

## Directions

1. Flake the fish and remove the fins and intestines.
2. Wrap with a linen piece and give a coating of clay all over its surface.
3. Burn it with a sand-bath in a pan.
4. Then remove the clay, wash the fish and remove the bones.
