## Ingredients

- onions
- pasted ginger
- salt
- powdered black cumin
- broken chillies
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel some onions and half boil them.
2. Then smear them with pasted ginger, salt, powdered black cumin and broken chillies in adequate quantities, and keep them immersed in vinegar in a covered jar.
