## Ingredients

- Husked moog pulses
- Ghee
- Flour
- Salt
- Kheer or cocoa-nut preparation

## Directions

1. Keep some husked pulses of moog under water for two hours
2. Fry them, if desired, with a little ghee for a few minutes
3. Make a paste of them
4. Add salt and a little flour, if necessary, to make the paste stiff
5. Prepare Puli cakes as before stuffing them with kheer or the preparation of cocoa-nut
6. Fry till reddish brown with ghee
