## Ingredients

- half a pint lemon ice
- one wineglassful Jamaica rum
- one bottle iced ginger beer

## Directions

1. Mix lemon ice with Jamaica rum
2. Pour over it, stirring briskly, a bottle of iced ginger beer
3. Drink it while it is effervescing
