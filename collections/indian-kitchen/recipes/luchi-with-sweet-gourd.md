## Ingredients

- ripe sweet gourd
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut a ripe sweet gourd in two and remove the seeds
2. Close it again as before binding with a thread
3. Give a very thick coating of mud all round it
4. Burn well on charcoal or wood fire so that the interior portion is made a pulp
5. Break the fruit when cold and take out the pulp
6. Add salt and prepare a stiff dough with it and flour without adding water
7. Proceed as usual with the making of Luchi
