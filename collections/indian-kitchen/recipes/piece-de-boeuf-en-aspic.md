## Ingredients

- a nice piece of fresh brisket of beef
- plenty of fat bacon
- stock
- vegetables
- a glass of white wine
- its own gravy reduced
- aspic
- hard-boiled eggs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Tie a nice piece of fresh brisket of beef into a compact shape
2. Lard it with plenty of fat bacon
3. Braise it in stock and vegetables with a glass of white wine
4. When done, take it out and remove the string
5. Place the meat in an oblong shape with a heavy weight upon it
6. When thoroughly cold and set, trim it all round with a sharp knife
7. Glaze it with its own gravy reduced
8. Set it in aspic in the ice-box using a plain oblong mould
9. When you turn it out, garnish with hard-boiled eggs and parsley
10. Serve with the best salad you can devise and a good mayonnaise sauce
