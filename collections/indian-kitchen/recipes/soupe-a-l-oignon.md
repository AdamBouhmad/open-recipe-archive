## Ingredients

- a couple of Bombay onions
- flour
- butter
- water
- pepper
- salt
- croutons of fried bread
- Grated Parmesan

## Directions

1. Slice a couple of Bombay onions
2. powder them well with flour
3. let them fry awhile in a stew-pan with plenty of butter
4. before they begin to brown at all, add water, pepper, and salt
5. let the whole boil till the onions are well done
6. serve with croutons of fried bread
7. Grated Parmesan should accompany
