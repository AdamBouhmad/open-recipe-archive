## Ingredients

- pomfret fish
- garlic
- mushrooms
- flour
- glass of chablis, sauterne, or hock
- gravy
- thyme
- marjoram
- parsley
- lime peel
- sugar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the fish
2. Put into a sauce-pan a coffee-cupful of the fish stock, a clove of garlic, and a table-spoonful of mushrooms, all finely chopped up
3. When this has been on the fire a few minutes, add a table-spoonful of flour, stir it well
4. Pour in a glass of chablis, sauterne, or hock, with half a pint of gravy
5. Add a dessert-spoonful of finely minced thyme, marjoram, parsley, and lime peel, all mixed together, a pinch of sugar, with salt and pepper to taste
6. Let the sauce simmer slowly for half an hour, then strain
7. Serve with the boiled fish
