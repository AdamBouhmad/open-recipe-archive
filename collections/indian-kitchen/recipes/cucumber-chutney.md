## Ingredients

- three heaped up table-spoonfuls cucumber strips
- a tea-spoonful of finely-minced onion
- one tea-spoonful of chopped green chilli
- one tea-spoonful of parsley
- a dessert-spoonful of vinegar
- sugar
- a dessert-spoonful of salad oil
- salt
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the cucumber into thin strips an inch long; say three heaped up table-spoonfuls.
2. Mix with them a tea-spoonful of finely-minced onion, one of chopped green chilli, and one of parsley.
3. Moisten with a dessert-spoonful of vinegar in which a pinch of sugar has been dissolved, a dessert-spoonful of salad oil, and dust over it salt and black pepper at discretion.
