## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of pulses of moog
- water
- 1 powa of ghee
- a few aniseeds
- 1 1/2 powa of milk
- 1 powa of sugar
- cardamom seeds
- raisins
- almonds
- pistachios
- camphor

## Directions

1. Keep 1/2 seer of pulses of moog in water for 3 or 4 hours and then make a paste of them.
2. Heat 1 powa of ghee with a few aniseeds and when the bubbles die out fry the paste with it stirring constantly to prevent the mass becoming hard.
3. Then add 1 1/2 powa of milk, 1 powa of sugar, pinch of powdered cardamom, raisins, pieces of almonds and pistachios.
4. Take down when thick and mix with very little camphor.
