## Ingredients

- new loaf
- milk or cream
- favourite essence
- boiling fat or butter
- apricot preserve

## Directions

1. Take a new loaf and cut it into slices three quarters of an inch thick
2. Trim off all crust, cut into convenient slices
3. Marinade in milk, or in cream if available, flavoured with your favourite essence
4. Take up the slices, drain them
5. Fry them in a deep bath of boiling fat, or butter, till a golden yellow
6. Spread apricot or any nice preserve over them, and serve hot
