## Ingredients

- four chittacks or eight ounces of butter free of water
- six chittacks or twelve ounces of white sugar
- twelve fresh eggs
- four wineglassfuls of lemon or lime juice
- four tablespoonfuls of finely-grated bread-crumbs

## Directions

1. Mix the butter and the sugar
2. Add the yolks of the eggs
3. Add the lime-juice and bread-crumbs
4. When the oven is ready add the whites of the eggs well beaten up
5. Put the whole into a buttered pudding-dish
6. Bake it immediately
