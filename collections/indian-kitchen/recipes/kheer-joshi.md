## Ingredients

- ½ powa fine flour
- ½ seer khowa kheer
- water
- ghee
- 2 seers milk
- raisins
- pasted almonds
- 1½ powa sugar
- cardamom seeds
- essence of rose

## Directions

1. Mix ½ powa of fine flour with ½ seer of khowa kheer
2. Prepare threads of it as fine as possible rolling it little by little with the hands, adding a little water if necessary
3. Cut them in pieces of 1 inch in length
4. Fry them in plenty of ghee
5. Boil 2 seers of milk till it is reduced to half
6. Drop the fried pieces into it
7. Add raisins, pasted almonds and 1½ powa of sugar
8. Stir at intervals and take down when thick after mixing with cardamom seeds and 3 or 4 drops of essence of rose
