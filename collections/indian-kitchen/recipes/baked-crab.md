## Ingredients

- a nice crab
- black pepper
- maccaroni
- a tea-cupful of mild cheese
- butter
- a coffee-cupful of well made white sauce
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Choose a nice crab, have it boiled, cleaned, and picked
2. Place the meat in a soup plate and give it a dust of black pepper
3. Boil till tender sufficient maccaroni to line the bottom of a small pie-dish, and grate a tea-cupful of mild cheese
4. Rub the bottom of the pie-dish with butter, place the maccaroni thereon, and pour a coffee-cupful of well made white sauce over the maccaroni
5. Shake a dust of grated cheese over its surface
6. Place the crab meat, well worked with melted butter, over the maccaroni, and, as you arrange it, dust it with black pepper
7. Over the crab dredge a nice dressing of the grated cheese, about one-eighth of an inch deep, and pour a little melted butter over the surface
8. Bake till the top takes colour, and serve
