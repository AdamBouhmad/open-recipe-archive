## Ingredients

- ½ seer khowa kheer
- ½ powa sugar
- 1 powa milk
- 1 tola powdered cardamom
- powdered aniseed
- 1 chhatak ghee
- 1 chhatak pasted almonds
- 1 chhatak pasted pistachios
- essence of rose
- clove
- mango-scented ginger juice
- entire poppy-seed

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly khowa kheer ½ seer, sugar ½ powa, milk 1 powa, powdered cardamom 1 tola, a pinch of powdered aniseed, ghee 1 chhatak, pasted almonds and pistachios 1 chhatak each, together
2. Heat the mass and stir till it is a stiff paste
3. Take down and scrape together the paste sticking to the sides of the pan
4. Mix with 2 or 3 drops of essence of rose
5. Beat the mass to a very fine paste
6. Make balls of it and give them the colour of the fruits to be made
7. Press them in its cast
8. Fix a clove in the place of the stalk
9. For mangoes, smear the balls also with the juice of mango-scented ginger
10. For lichies, fry some entire poppy-seed without ghee or oil and then give them a deep red colour
11. Sprinkle them on the lichies of kheer giving the look of real fruits
12. The fruits may be decorated with their respective leaves and branches
