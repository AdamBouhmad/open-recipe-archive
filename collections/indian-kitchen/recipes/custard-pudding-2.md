## Ingredients

- a pint of cream or milk
- six eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of finely-sifted flour
- half a small nutmeg grated, or an equal quantity of pounded cinnamon
- a tablespoonful of pounded loaf sugar
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Mix with a pint of cream or milk six well-beaten eggs, two tablespoonfuls of finely-sifted flour, half a small nutmeg grated, or an equal quantity of pounded cinnamon, a tablespoonful of pounded loaf sugar, and a little salt
2. Put it into a cloth or buttered basin, that will exactly hold it
3. Boil it for half an hour
4. Serve with wine sauce
