## Ingredients

- cabbage, interior tender portion
- cauliflower
- salt
- mustard oil
- powdered mustard

## Directions

1. Take only the interior tender portion of the cabbage and tie it in a clean piece of cloth.
2. Drop it into the boiling rice only a few minutes before taking down from the oven.
3. Treat the boiled cabbage with salt, mustard oil, and powdered mustard if desired.
4. Use the same method with cauliflowers.
