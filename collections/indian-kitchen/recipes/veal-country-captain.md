## Ingredients

- 4 ounces ghee
- half a teaspoonful ground chilies
- one teaspoonful salt
- a quarter of a teaspoonful ground turmeric
- twenty onions
- half a cup of water

## Directions

1. Partially broil or roast a shoulder of veal before cutting it up.
2. Or make the country captain as directed by adding half a cup of water instead of partially broiling the meat.
