## Ingredients

- white mutton suet, one pound
- hot water, one quart
- fresh lard, half a pound
- bees' wax, a quarter of a pound
- oil of cloves or any essential oil, fifteen drops

## Directions

1. Well boil white mutton suet in a quart of hot water
2. Wash to free it from salt
3. When dried, melt it with half a pound of fresh lard and a quarter of a pound of bees' wax
4. Pour it into an earthen vessel
5. Stir till it is cold
6. Beat into it fifteen drops of oil of cloves, or any essential oil whose scent is preferred
7. If too hard, use less wax
