## Ingredients

- cabbage
- sauce blanche
- cream

## Directions

1. Half-boil your cabbage and take it out and drain it
2. Divide it into quarters
3. Make a nicely flavoured sauce blanche, adding a little cream if you can spare it
4. Place the quarters in this and complete their cooking therein
5. Serve, pouring the sauce over them
