## Ingredients

- equal parts of carrots, turnips, leeks, onions, and celery
- couple of ounces of fresh butter
- tea-spoonful of powdered sugar
- pepper
- salt
- broth from the consomme

## Directions

1. Take equal parts of carrots, turnips, leeks, onions, and celery
2. Cut them all into thin strips not more than the eighth of an inch across and an inch long
3. Put them into a sauce-pan with a couple of ounces of fresh butter, a tea-spoonful of powdered sugar, a little pepper, and a pinch of salt
4. Toss them lightly on the fire until they take colour slightly, say for five minutes or so
5. Cover them with a little broth from the consomme and carefully remove the grease thrown up by the butter
