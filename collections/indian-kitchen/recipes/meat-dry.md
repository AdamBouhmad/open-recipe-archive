## Ingredients

- meat without bones
- water
- besam
- powdered or pasted poppy-seeds
- almonds
- chillies
- garam-masala
- coriander
- ginger
- turmeric
- salt
- white of eggs
- ghee

## Directions

1. Boil pieces of meat without bones in water.
2. Add this juice to besam, powdered or pasted poppy-seeds, almonds, chillies, garam-masala, coriander, ginger, turmeric, salt and the white of eggs, and beat thoroughly to a thick paste.
3. Dip the boiled pieces of meat into this paste and fry in enough boiling ghee.
