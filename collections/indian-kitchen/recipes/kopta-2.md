## Ingredients

- minced meat
- water
- ginger-juice
- onion-juice
- salt
- curd
- powders of chillies
- cumin
- coriander
- garam-masala
- fried almonds
- raisins
- pasted garlics
- eggs
- besam of grams
- beaten eggs
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil some minced meat with a little water, add in adequate quantities of ginger-juice, onion-juice, salt, curd, powders of chillies, cumin, coriander, garam-masala, fried almonds and raisins, pasted garlics, eggs and besam of grams about 0.5 of the weight of raw meat, and beat thoroughly to a stiff paste.
2. Form balls of it, dip into beaten eggs, roll on bread crumbs and fry in plenty of ghee.
