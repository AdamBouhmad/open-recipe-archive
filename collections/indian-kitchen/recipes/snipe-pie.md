## Ingredients

- eight snipes
- two pounds of gravy meat
- two pounds of tender rib-meat or undercut beef
- beef gravy
- shallot
- tender beef collops
- strips of bacon
- spiced pepper
- slices of lean bacon, ham, tongue, or Bologna sausage
- paste

## Directions

1. Prepare six snipes as if for roasting for the pie
2. Sacrifice two snipes for the gravy
3. Make a good beef gravy
4. Throw the two snipes into the gravy and simmer slowly
5. Make a puree by pounding the meat of the birds to a paste and blending it with the gravy
6. Rub the pie-dish with a shallot
7. Fill the bottom of the pie-dish with little collops of tender beef rolled with strips of bacon
8. Dust spiced pepper over each layer until all beef is used
9. Lay the six snipes on the surface of the beef with slices of lean bacon, ham, tongue, or Bologna sausage between each bird
10. Garnish as usual
11. Baptize the pie with the puree, saving some for the final process
12. Cover with a nice light paste and bake
