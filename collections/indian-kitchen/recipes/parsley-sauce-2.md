## Ingredients

- fresh green crisp parsley
- a tablespoonful of chopped capers
- a teaspoonful of good English vinegar
- a dessertspoonful of curry onions
- two chittacks or four ounces of butter, free of water
- a cup of good white stock, free of fat
- crumb of stale bread finely grated
- a teaspoonful of salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pick, clean, and mince fine some fresh green crisp parsley, and put it into a tureen with a tablespoonful of chopped capers and a teaspoonful of good English vinegar
2. Fry to a nice light brown a dessertspoonful of curry onions in two chittacks or four ounces of butter, free of water
3. Add a cup of good white stock, free of fat, and thicken with crumb of stale bread finely grated, a teaspoonful of salt, and a little pepper
4. Allow to simmer until of a sufficient consistency
5. Pour it over the minced parsley and capers, mix well together, and it is ready for use
