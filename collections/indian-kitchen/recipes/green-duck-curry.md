## Ingredients

- young tender duck
- half a teaspoonful of coriander seeds
- half a teaspoonful of cumin seeds

## Directions

1. Cut it up exactly as you would a chicken
2. Curry it in the same manner
3. Introduce half a teaspoonful each of coriander and cumin seeds in this curry
4. Allow it to simmer for an hour and a half
