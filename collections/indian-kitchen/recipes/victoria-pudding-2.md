## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of bread-crumbs
- three ounces of shredded suet
- a quarter of a pound of any jam
- the juice of a lime
- the peel of a lime, grated
- two ounces of sifted sugar
- two eggs
- a pinch of salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix bread-crumbs, suet, jam, lime juice, lime peel, sugar, eggs, and salt thoroughly
2. Place in a buttered mould
3. Boil for three hours
