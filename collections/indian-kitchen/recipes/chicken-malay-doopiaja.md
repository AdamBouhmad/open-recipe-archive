## Ingredients

- one chittack and a half or three ounces of ghee
- one teaspoonful and a half of salt
- four teaspoonfuls of ground onions
- one teaspoonful of ground turmeric
- one teaspoonful of ground chilies
- half a teaspoonful of ground ginger
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground garlic
- one cup of strong cocoanut milk
- one dozen onions

## Directions

1. Cut up the chicken in the usual manner
2. Warm the ghee
3. Fry and set aside the sliced onions
4. Fry brown the ground condiments
5. Add the chicken and salt
6. When fried brown, pour in the cocoanut milk and the fried onions finely chopped
7. Allow to simmer over a slow fire
8. The Malay doopiaja will be ready in an hour
