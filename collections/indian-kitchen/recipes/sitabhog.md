## Ingredients

- 1/4 powa ghee
- flour 3 powas
- 1 seer hard chhana or khowa kheer
- milk or water
- ghee for frying
- sugar syrup
- kasir chini

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly 1/4 powa of ghee with 3 powas of flour of superfine quality
2. Make a thin paste of it with 1 seer of hard chhana or khowa kheer pasted with the hands and sufficient quantity of milk or water
3. Hold a finely perforated ladle over a pan of boiling ghee (preferably cow's ghee) of excellent quality
4. Pour the solution in the ladle and shake it up and down when the solution will fall in the ghee in broken streams
5. Take them out before they are even slightly browned
6. Keep them in a syrup of sugar (preferably of kasir chini) previously prepared till saturated
