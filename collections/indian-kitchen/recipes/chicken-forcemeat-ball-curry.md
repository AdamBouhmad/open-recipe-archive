## Ingredients

- a good fat chicken
- a quarter of a pound of beef suet
- salt
- pepper
- garden herbs
- bread-crumbs
- egg
- lard or ghee
- water or broth

## Directions

1. Procure a good fat chicken and a quarter of a pound of beef suet
2. Put the suet into a mortar with all the fleshy parts of the chicken, and pound to a pulp
3. Make a stock of gravy of the bones
4. Mix with the pounded meat all the several ingredients named in the beef forcemeat ball curry recipe, with the addition of an egg well beaten up
5. Make into balls, roll in bread-crumbs, and curry as directed for beef forcemeat ball curry
