## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 eggs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Boil three or four eggs about a quarter of an hour
2. Put them into cold water, take off the shells
3. Cut three of the whites and four yolks in small pieces
4. Mix them with melted butter, and heat it well
