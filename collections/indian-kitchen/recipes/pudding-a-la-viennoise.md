## Ingredients

- twelve ounces of crumb of bread cut into small dice
- two glasses of Madeira
- a dessert spoonful of minced citron
- two ounces sweet almonds pounded
- half an ounce bitter almonds pounded
- six ounces of raisins
- six yolks of eggs
- one pint of cream
- two ounces of burnt sugar
- six ounces loaf-sugar
- candied peel

## Directions

1. Put the bread, almonds, raisins and citron into a basin, and pour the wine over them
2. Put the two ounces of sugar into a stew-pan to brown, then pour in the cream, stir it round, and add the yolks of eggs previously beaten
3. Thicken in the bain-marie, and strain the custard into the mixture in the basin
4. Butter a mould and ornament it with candied peel
5. Fill it with the mixture, and steam one and a half hours
6. Serve with caramel custard, or sauce Royale
