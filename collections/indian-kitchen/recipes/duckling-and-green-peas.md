## Ingredients

- duckling
- green peas
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Roast the duckling and do not stuff it
2. Let the basting be frequent and froth the breast up nicely to finish with
3. Serve accompanied by green peas and cook the potatoes according to any of the recipes given at page 136
