## Ingredients

- 1/4 seer of middlings
- 1 1/2 powas of water
- a little lime-water
- 1 seer of sabeda
- 3/4 seer of fine flour
- 1 seer of hot water
- sugar syrup
- a little ghee

## Directions

1. Keep 1/4 seer of middlings moist with 1 1/2 powas of water and a little lime-water for about 12 hours
2. Mix it with 1 seer of sabeda, 3/4 seer of fine flour and 1 seer of hot water
3. Fry Bonde with this paste
4. Take them out when they are fried but yet soft
5. Drop them in a syrup of sugar
6. Take them out again when saturated
7. Make a paste of them
8. Mix with a little ghee and make balls of it
