## Ingredients

- fowl
- salt
- powdered pepper
- pasted onions
- yolks of eggs
- powdered biscuits
- ghee

## Directions

1. Prepare the fowl and cut it in 5 or 6 slices
2. Remove the bones as far as possible and thrash both sides of the slices with a stone
3. Mix them with salt, powdered pepper, pasted onions and the yolks of eggs
4. Roll on powdered biscuits and fry in plenty of ghee
