## Ingredients

- pieces of a big fish
- pasted ginger
- onions
- turmeric
- salt
- raisins
- pieces of almonds
- powdered turmeric
- chillies
- ginger-juice
- minced onions
- a little paste of moistened grams
- ghee
- eggs beaten with salt
- powdered turmeric
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil pieces of a big fish with pasted ginger, onions, turmeric and salt
2. Take out the boiled pieces and remove the bones
3. Add raisins, pieces of almonds, powdered turmeric, chillies, ginger-juice, salt, minced onions and a little paste of moistened grams
4. Beat thoroughly
5. Heat it with a little ghee to evaporate some water so that small balls may be made of the paste
6. Dip the balls into eggs beaten with salt and powdered turmeric
7. Roll on bread crumbs
8. Fry in plenty of ghee
