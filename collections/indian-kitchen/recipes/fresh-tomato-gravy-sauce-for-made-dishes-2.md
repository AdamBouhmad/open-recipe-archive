## Ingredients

- forty tomatoes, halved
- some soup herbs
- salt
- stock
- a dessertspoonful of arrowroot or corn or other flour
- a teaspoonful of good English vinegar

## Directions

1. Take forty tomatoes (halved), some soup herbs, and salt; boil them in a little stock
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Replace on the fire, and thicken with the addition, more or less, of a dessertspoonful of arrowroot or corn or other flour, to obtain any required consistency
4. Finally add a teaspoonful of good English vinegar; if a sharper gravy sauce be desired, instead of the vinegar add either a dessertspoonful of tapp sauce or a teaspoonful of chili vinegar
