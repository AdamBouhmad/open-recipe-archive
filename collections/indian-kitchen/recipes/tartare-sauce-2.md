## Ingredients

- raw egg yolks
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Use raw yolks alone without hard-boiled eggs
2. Add oil and vinegar in the following proportions: a tea-spoonful of vinegar to two tablespoonfuls of oil
3. Beat together well, and often times repeat till enough sauce is made
