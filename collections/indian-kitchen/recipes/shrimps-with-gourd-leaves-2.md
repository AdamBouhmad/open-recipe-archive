## Ingredients

- lobsters
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- potatoes
- onions
- water
- cumin
- pepper
- chillies
- coriander
- chopped and thoroughly washed tender gourd leaves
- ghee or oil
- cassia leaves
- panch-foron
- garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Prepare some lobsters, cut in pieces and fry.
2. Fishes are always to be smeared with powdered turmeric and salt before frying.
3. Then brown some potatoes and onions peeled and cut in pieces.
4. Add water, salt, powdered turmeric, cumin, pepper, chillies, coriander and some chopped and thoroughly washed tender gourd leaves.
5. When fully boiled season the curry with ghee or oil boiled with a few cassia leaves and panch-foron.
6. Garam-masalla may be added.
7. Leave little soup.
