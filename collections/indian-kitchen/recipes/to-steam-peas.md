## Ingredients

- peas
- lettuce-salad leaves

## Directions

1. Shell and close-pack the peas securely in a large quantity of lettuce-salad leaves
2. Put the package into a stewpan over a moderate fire for the ordinary time required to boil peas, say half an hour, when they will be ready
