## Ingredients

- Karola 1.5 seer
- pepper 1/4 tola
- cumin 1/4 tola
- garam-masalla 1/4 tola
- coriander 1 tola
- salt 1 tola
- ginger 1/2 tola
- turmeric 1/4 tola
- curd 0.5 powa
- sugar 0.5 powa
- ghee 0.5 powa
- ripe coconut
- cassia leaves
- flour paste

## Directions

1. Scrape out the kernel of the coconut, mix them with sugar, pasted ginger and garam-masalla and fry them with ghee
2. Remove the seeds from the tender karolas by cutting off one end of each
3. Fill the hollows with this stuff and close the ends with a little flour paste
4. Fry them in ghee
5. Boil some ghee in another pan with the cassia leaves and powdered pepper, cumin, coriander and turmeric
6. Add the curd, sugar and a little hot water
7. Add the karolas and salt when it boils
8. Take down when the soup is thick after adding powdered garam-masalla
