## Ingredients

- champa-note sak 1 seer
- ghee 1 powa
- garam-masalla 0.5 tola
- minced ginger 1 chhatak
- coriander 1 tola
- salt 1 tola
- pepper 0.5 tola
- panch-foron a pinch
- water

## Directions

1. Dissolve salt in a tea-cup of water and boil the sak in it.
2. Boil ghee in a pan with the minced ginger and panch-foron, and add into it the boiled sak and powdered coriander and pepper, and stir thoroughly for a few minutes till the sak are non-sticky.
3. Add powdered garam-masalla and take down.
