## Ingredients

- besam 2.5 powas
- sabeda 1.5 powas
- water
- saffron
- ghee
- sugar syrup
- kasir chini
- cardamom seeds
- raisins
- pistachios
- almonds

## Directions

1. Make a moderately thick paste of besam 2.5 powas and sabeda 1.5 powas with water
2. Mix with very little saffron if desired to give a reddish colour
3. Hold a finely perforated iron ladle over a pan of boiling ghee
4. Pour some of the solution in the ladle and shake it up and down when the solution will fall in the ghee in very fine drops
5. Take them out when they are fried
6. Keep them in a concentrated syrup of sugar (preferably of kasir chini) till saturated with it
7. Mix with cardamom seeds, raisins, pieces of pistachios and almonds and a little hot ghee
8. Make balls of it if desired
