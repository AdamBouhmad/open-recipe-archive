## Ingredients

- a large onion
- a tablespoonful of finely-chopped parsley
- some good gravy
- one ounce of butter
- flour
- a spoonful of cut capers

## Directions

1. Mince a large onion, parboil it, and drain off the water
2. Put the onion into a saucepan, with a tablespoonful of finely-chopped parsley, some good gravy, and one ounce of butter dredged with a little flour
3. Let it boil nearly ten minutes, and add a spoonful of cut capers
4. The sauce must be thoroughly heated before being served up
