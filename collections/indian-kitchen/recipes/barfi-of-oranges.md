## Ingredients

- oranges
- 1 1/2 seers of milk
- 3 powas of sugar
- 4 drops of essence of rose
- powdered cardamoms
- 1 tola each of pieces of almonds, pistachios and raisins

## Directions

1. Peel some oranges and further skin them separating the grains.
2. Boil 1 1/2 seers of milk till it reduces to only 3 powas and then add 1 1/2 seers of the grains of oranges.
3. Boil on mild heat and stir constantly till the mass is very thick.
4. Remove the pan off fire and mix thoroughly with a very concentrated syrup of 3 powas of sugar.
5. Add 4 drops of essence of rose, a little powdered cardamoms, pieces of almonds, pistachios and raisins 1 tola each.
6. Boil again if necessary till very thick and gluey when remove it in a flat plate and cut in cubes when solid.
