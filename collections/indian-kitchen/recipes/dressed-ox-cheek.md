## Ingredients

- ox-cheek head
- quart of good gravy
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- vinegar
- catsup or white wine
- forcemeat balls

## Directions

1. Prepare it as directed for stewing
2. Cut the meat into square pieces
3. Make a sauce with a quart of good gravy, thickened with butter mixed with flour
4. Season with salt and pepper, a little cayenne, and a tablespoonful of vinegar
5. Put in the head, and simmer it till quite tender
6. A few minutes before serving add a little catsup or white wine
7. Forcemeat balls may be added
