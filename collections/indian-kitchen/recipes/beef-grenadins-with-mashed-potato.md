## Ingredients

- undercut of beef
- marinade
- clarified dripping
- mashed potato
- fried bread
- butter
- flour
- beef gravy
- sherry
- caramel
- mushroom ketchup
- Harvey sauce
- Anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Cut and trim a nice undercut of beef in slices as for grenadins.
2. Steep them all day en marinade and drain.
3. Either grill, or fry them very carefully in clarified dripping.
4. Drain again, and serve in a circle round a ring of mashed potato, with a heart-shaped crouton of fried bread between each slice of meat.
5. For the sauce: Melt half an ounce of butter in a small sauce-pan, mix into it half an ounce of flour.
6. Stir in, when the butter and flour have amalgamated, a breakfast-cupful of beef gravy, half a glass of sherry, a tea-spoonful of caramel, a table-spoonful of mushroom ketchup, one of Harvey, and one and a half of Anchovy sauce.
7. Stir well, and pour piping hot into the centre of the potato ring.
