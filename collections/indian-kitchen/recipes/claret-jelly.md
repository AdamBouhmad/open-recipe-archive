## Ingredients

- a bottle of sound claret
- six ounces of white sugar
- a glass of brandy
- the rind of one well-washed lime
- the juice of three limes
- one tea-cupful of raspberry syrup or half a pound of raspberry jam
- an ounce of isinglass
- crystallized cherries
- iced cream

## Directions

1. Boil all together claret, white sugar, brandy, lime rind and lime juice, and raspberry syrup or jam
2. Add an ounce of isinglass, and strain through muslin
3. Decorate a jelly mould with crystallized cherries, set it upon ice
4. Pour some of the liquid jelly into the mould, and set the cherries
5. Gradually add the liquid till the mould is completed
6. Thoroughly consolidate the jelly in ice, and serve with iced cream
