## Ingredients

- khesari pulse, peas or grams
- red chillies
- onions
- aniseed
- salt
- powdered turmeric
- oil

## Directions

1. Wash and keep some pulse of khesari, peas or grams under water for a few hours
2. Grind the moistened pulse into a paste with red chillies and onions
3. Add entire aniseed, salt, and powdered turmeric
4. Beat well, form cakes of it and fry in plenty of oil
