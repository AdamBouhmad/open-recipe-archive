## Ingredients

- two or three brinjals
- a clove of garlic
- a tea-spoonful of minced onion
- one tea-spoonful of green chilli
- one tea-spoonful of vinegar
- a very little green ginger
- salt
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Boil two or three brinjals, let them get cold, scrape out the whole of the inside of the pods, pass this through the sieve to get rid of the seeds.
2. Rub a soup-plate with a clove of garlic, empty the brinjal pulp therein, dress it with a tea-spoonful of minced onion, one of green chilli, one of vinegar, and a very little green ginger.
3. Season with salt and black pepper, pat the mixture into a little mould, and serve in a saucer.
