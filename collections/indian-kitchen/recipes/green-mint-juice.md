## Ingredients

- half a dozen large stalks of good fresh mint
- 1 dessertspoonful of water or brandy

## Directions

1. Pick and clean half a dozen large stalks of good fresh mint
2. Pound the leaves in a mortar with a dessertspoonful of water or with brandy
3. Put them into muslin and squeeze out all the liquor
4. Juice may be extracted a second time by a little more water or brandy being added, and the leaves rebruised and pressed through muslin
