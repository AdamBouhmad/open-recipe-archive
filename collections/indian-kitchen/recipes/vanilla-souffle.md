## Ingredients

- one table-spoonful of flour
- a small piece of fresh butter
- half a pint of new milk
- a dessert-spoonful of powdered sugar
- yolks of four eggs
- a few drops of vanilla flavoring
- a table-spoonful of any liqueur
- whites of six eggs

## Directions

1. Mix together in a sauce-pan one table-spoonful of flour, a small piece of fresh butter, half a pint of new milk, and a dessert-spoonful of powdered sugar
2. Stir this over the fire to get thoroughly warm but no more
3. Put it on one side to get cool, stirring to prevent any scum forming on the surface
4. When quite cold, stir into it the yolks of four eggs
5. Add a few drops of vanilla flavoring
6. Mix this thoroughly
7. Throw in a table-spoonful of any liqueur you may have open
8. Add the whites of six eggs whipped to a froth
9. Mix the whole together
10. Pour it into a souffle tin
11. Bake in an oven at moderate heat
12. Serve the moment your eye tells you that the souffle is ready
