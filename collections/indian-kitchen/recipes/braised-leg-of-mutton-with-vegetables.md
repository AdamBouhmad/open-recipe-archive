## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- turkey stuffing
- broth made from the bone
- liqueur-glass of brandy
- sliced onions
- carrot
- bunch of mixed sweet herbs
- dried herbs
- whole peppers
- bunch of parsley
- clove of garlic
- glass of port
- red currant jelly
- anchovy vinegar
- turkey forcemeat
- haricots verts
- mashed potato

## Directions

1. Remove the bone from a leg of mutton and fill its place with turkey stuffing
2. Tie the meat back in shape and set it in a stewing-pan
3. Add as much broth made from the bone as will half cover it
4. Throw in a liqueur-glass of brandy, a couple of sliced onions, a carrot cut up, a bunch of mixed sweet herbs or two dessert-spoonfuls of dried herbs, some whole peppers, a bunch of parsley, a clove of garlic, and one glass of port
5. Set the pan on the fire with some live coals on the lid as well
6. Let this stew till done very slowly, keeping it from absolutely boiling point
7. When ready, strain off the sauce in which it has been cooked
8. Thicken the sauce by stirring in a table-spoonful of red currant jelly, half one of anchovy vinegar, and a glass of port
9. Serve the mutton garnished with balls of turkey forcemeat fried in butter
10. Pour the rich brown gravy round it and let haricots verts and mashed potato accompany this dish
