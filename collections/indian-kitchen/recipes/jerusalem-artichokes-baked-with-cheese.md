## Ingredients

- Jerusalem artichokes
- cream or milk
- egg yolk
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Boil the artichokes till quite tender
2. Mash them with a silver fork, moistening them with cream, or milk with the yolk of an egg
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Place the artichoke in a buttered pie-dish, or in buttered coquilles
5. Give the surface a layer of grated cheese
6. Bake till it takes colour
