## Ingredients

- palang sak
- pumpkin leaves
- flour
- salt
- turmeric
- pepper
- cumin
- red chillies
- fried fishes
- ghee
- cassia leaves
- green peas
- beans
- potatoes
- brinjals
- browned broken bori

## Directions

1. Chop the sak fine and wash thoroughly.
2. Boil them and drain off the water.
3. Mix flour, salt and powdered turmeric, pepper, cumin and red chillies in suitable quantities.
4. Boil some fried fishes in water, pick up the bones and add the mixture of flesh and water, if desired.
5. Boil some ghee with a few cassia leaves and cumin, and add the mass.
6. Stir constantly so that the mass may not be scorched.
7. Add green peas and some pieces of beans, potatoes and brinjals if you like.
8. Add browned broken bori after a while.
9. The curry is ready when the soup is thick and the mass is completely boiled.
