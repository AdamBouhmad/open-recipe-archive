## Ingredients

- butter
- a little pepper
- a little salt
- the squeeze of a lemon

## Directions

1. Melt butter plainly in a sauce-pan, flavoured with a little pepper, a little salt, and the squeeze of a lemon.
2. Allow this to settle over the fire.
3. Pour free from the sediment at the bottom of the pan into a piping hot metal sauce-boat.
