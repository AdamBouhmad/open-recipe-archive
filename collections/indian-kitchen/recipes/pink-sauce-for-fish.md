## Ingredients

- one quart of good vinegar
- half a pint of port wine
- half an ounce of cayenne
- one large tablespoonful of walnut catsup
- two of anchovy liquor
- a quarter of an ounce of cochineal
- six cloves of garlic

## Directions

1. Put into a pan, or wide-mouthed jar, one quart of good vinegar, half a pint of port wine, half an ounce of cayenne, one large tablespoonful of walnut catsup, two of anchovy liquor, a quarter of an ounce of cochineal, and six cloves of garlic
2. Let it remain forty hours, stirring it two or three times a day
3. Run it through a flannel bag, and put it into half-pint bottles
