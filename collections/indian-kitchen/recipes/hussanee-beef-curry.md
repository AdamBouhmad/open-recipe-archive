## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef
- 1.5 chittacks ghee
- 1.5 teaspoonful of salt
- four teaspoonfuls of ground onions
- one teaspoonful of turmeric
- half a teaspoonful of ginger
- half a cupful of water
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground garlic
- one teaspoonful of chilies
- half a cupful of tyre or dhye
- finely-sliced ginger
- small curry onions

## Directions

1. Cut up two pounds of beef into small squares not exceeding one inch, and pass them on the silver or bamboo pins alternately with half an onion and a slice of ginger.
2. Warm the ghee and brown the ground condiments.
3. Put in the sticks of meat, and brown, stirring the whole.
4. Add the tyre and a little water, and allow to simmer over a slow fire for nearly two hours, when the curry will be ready.
5. Serve up on a curry-dish without removing the sticks.
