## Ingredients

- rich custard
- salt
- Nepal pepper
- grated Parmesan or Gruyere
- dissolved gelatine
- whipped cream
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make half a pint of rich custard
2. Season it with salt instead of sugar, and a little Nepal pepper
3. Whip it well
4. Stir in three ounces of grated Parmesan or Gruyere, and half an ounce of dissolved gelatine
5. Set the mixture in a plain mould on ice
6. While setting, stir in a coffee-cupful of whipped cream
7. Turn out the mould
8. Garnish with sprigs of parsley
9. Serve cold
