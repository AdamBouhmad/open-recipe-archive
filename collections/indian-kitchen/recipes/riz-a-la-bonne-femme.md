## Ingredients

- well boiled rice
- butter
- onion
- garlic
- grated Parmesan or Gruyere
- finely rasped ham or corned beef
- crisply fried bacon

## Directions

1. Proceed as for riz a l'Italienne, but stir into the mixture some finely rasped ham, or corned beef.
2. Garnish with curls of crisply fried bacon.
