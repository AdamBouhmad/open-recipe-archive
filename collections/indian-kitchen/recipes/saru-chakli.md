## Ingredients

- Husked kalai pulses
- Powdered rice
- Cocoa-nut kernel
- Salt
- Water
- Ghee

## Directions

1. Keep some husked pulses of kalai under water for 2 hours and make a paste of them
2. Add powdered rice up to half the quantity of pulses
3. Scraped and pasted kernel of cocoa-nut may be added if desired up to half the quantity of pulses
4. Add salt and make a thick solution with water
5. Heat a frying pan, smear with a little ghee
6. Pour in two table-spoonfuls of the paste at a time
7. Spread it in a thin circle with a small thin rectangular plate of iron
8. Bake the other side when it is hard enough to be turned
