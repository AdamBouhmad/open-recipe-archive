## Ingredients

- tipparees
- sugar

## Directions

1. Shell or remove the pods of the tipparees and wipe away all dust
2. Prick each with a bamboo or other wooden pin
3. Put them into a preserving-pan
4. Strew some sugar over each layer of fruit, making the final layer of sugar thicker than the others
5. Simmer the whole until all the juice has been extracted and the syrup has acquired such a consistency that it will congeal if dropped on a plate
6. Remove the preserve quickly from the fire and bottle while warm
