## Ingredients

- eight or ten good-sized bagda prawns
- a good hard cocoanut
- rice
- onions
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry and set aside the sliced onions
2. Fry the rice in cocoanut milk (vide recipe No. 54)
3. Observe in all particulars the instructions given for the chicken pellow (recipe No. 12)
4. Dish up the pellow and strew over it the fried onions
5. Garnish with the prawns finely boiled and two hard-boiled eggs cut in halves or in some device
