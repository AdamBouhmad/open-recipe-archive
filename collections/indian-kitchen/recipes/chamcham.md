## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer hard chhana
- 1/4 chhatak middlings or sabeda
- cardamom seeds
- rose-water one tea-spoonful
- milk
- sugar syrup
- crystalline sugar

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1/2 seer of hard chhana and mix thoroughly with 1/4 chhatak of middlings or sabeda, seeds of a few cardamoms, a tea-spoonful of rose-water and a little milk if necessary to make a mud-like paste
2. Make round and long finger-like cakes of it
3. Drop them in a boiling syrup of sugar
4. Pick them out when a little brownish in colour
5. Roll on crystalline sugar
