## Ingredients

- egg contents
- ghee or oil
- ripe cocoa nut kernel
- ground turmeric
- salt
- potatoes
- water
- coriander
- pepper
- cumin
- ginger
- onions
- cassia leaves
- minced onions
- red chillies
- powdered garam masala

## Directions

1. Smear interior surface of cup with ghee
2. Pour egg contents into it
3. Place cup on hot water covered with lid
4. Take down when hard boiled
5. Cut in small squares and fry in ghee or oil
6. Cut cocoa nut kernel in small pieces
7. Smear with ground turmeric and salt and fry
8. Brown pieces of potatoes
9. Add water with coriander, pepper, cumin, turmeric, ginger, onions and salt ground to paste
10. Add cocoa nut pieces and eggs when boiling
11. Fry cassia leaves, minced onions and red chillies in ghee and season curry with them
12. Add powdered garam masala before finish
