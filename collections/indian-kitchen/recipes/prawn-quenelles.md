## Ingredients

- a dozen and a half well-cleaned prawns
- half their bulk of crumb of bread
- stock
- two whole raw eggs
- a pinch of salt
- a dusting of white or Nepaul pepper
- a tea-spoonful of anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Pound the well-cleaned prawns with half their bulk of crumb of bread soaked in stock
2. Work in with the paste two whole raw eggs
3. Season with a pinch of salt, a dusting of white or Nepaul pepper, and a tea-spoonful of anchovy sauce
4. When thoroughly blended and of the right consistency, form tiny olive-shaped quenelles between two tea-spoons
5. Poach them for about five minutes in boiling stock or water
6. Drain them and add them to the soup at the last moment
