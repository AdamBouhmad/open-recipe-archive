## Ingredients

- tender amrita mun leaves
- water
- besam
- salt
- powdered red chillies
- turmeric
- egg contents
- ghee or oil

## Directions

1. Cut tender amrita mun leaves in big squares
2. Boil in water
3. Dip into thick solution of besam beaten with salt, powdered red chillies and turmeric
4. Fry in ghee or oil
5. Dip again into beaten egg contents with salt and ground turmeric
6. Fry
