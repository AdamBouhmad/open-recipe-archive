## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef
- one-inch square pieces

## Directions

1. Cut two pounds of beef into one-inch square pieces
2. Follow all the instructions given in recipe No. 23
3. Allow it to simmer for a much longer time over a slow fire, until the beef is perfectly tender
