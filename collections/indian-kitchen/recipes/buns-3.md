## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- six ounces of butter
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- one egg
- nearly a quarter of a pint of milk
- a few drops of essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Mix together one pound of flour, six ounces of butter, two teaspoonfuls of baking powder, a quarter of a pound of sugar, one egg, nearly a quarter of a pint of milk, and a few drops of essence of lemon
2. Bake immediately
