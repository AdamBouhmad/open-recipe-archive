## Ingredients

- Powdered rice
- Salt
- Scraped cocoa-nut kernel
- Water
- Oil

## Directions

1. Make a thick paste of powdered rice, salt and some scraped kernel of cocoa-nut, if desired, with water
2. Heat an earthen frying pot, smear with a little oil
3. Add a small tea-cupful of the paste and cover up
4. Take out when properly boiled using mild heat
