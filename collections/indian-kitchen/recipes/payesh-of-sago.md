## Ingredients

- 1 seer of milk
- 1 chhatak of sago
- water
- 1/2 powa of sugar-candy
- raisins
- almonds
- pistachios
- cardamom seeds
- camphor

## Directions

1. Boil a seer of milk and when it is reduced to half add 1 chhatak of sago, after washing with water.
2. Stir incessantly.
3. A little later add 1/2 powa of sugar-candy, raisins, almonds and pistachios as desired.
4. Take down when thick and mix with a pinch of powdered cardamom seeds and very little camphor.
