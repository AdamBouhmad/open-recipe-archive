## Ingredients

- green mangoes
- 1 powa of salt
- 1 seer of mango pieces
- powders of chillies
- powdered mustard
- salt
- mustard oil

## Directions

1. Remove the stalks of a few green mangoes and cut them in halves
2. Throw away the seeds, mix 1 powa of salt with 1 seer of the pieces and dry them perfectly in the sun
3. Mix them with powders of chillies mustard and salt 1 chhatak each and enough mustard oil to saturate them, and keep in an airtight jar
4. Keep the jar in the sun for several days
