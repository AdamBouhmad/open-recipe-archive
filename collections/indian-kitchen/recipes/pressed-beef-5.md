## Ingredients

- nice fleshy piece of the flank with fat and lean pretty equally balanced
- salt
- spiced pepper
- brine (without saltpetre)

## Directions

1. Rub it carefully with salt and spiced pepper.
2. Let it rest in brine without saltpetre for forty-eight hours.
3. Drain it dry and wipe it.
4. Stew it gently till nice and tender.
5. Set it on a dish with a weight above it and let it get quite cold.
6. Remove the weight and trim the joint neatly in a rectangular form with a sharp knife.
7. Glaze its surface and serve.
