## Ingredients

- a few hard-boiled eggs
- ghee
- a few cloves
- curd
- pasted ginger
- pepper
- coriander
- salt
- water
- garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Shell a few hard-boiled eggs and cut them into 4 or 5 slices each.
2. Boil some ghee with a few cloves and singe the eggs in it a little.
3. Add curd, pasted ginger, pepper and coriander.
4. Add salt and water a little later.
5. Add ghee and garam-masalla, and take down when the soup is thick.
