## Ingredients

- eight fairly-sized potatoes
- yolks of five eggs
- finely-minced parsley
- marjoram
- cream
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- whites of three eggs
- boiling fat

## Directions

1. Mash eight fairly-sized potatoes
2. Pass the puree through the sieve
3. Work into it the yolks of five eggs
4. Season it with a little finely-minced parsley and marjoram
5. Moisten it with enough cream to bring it to the consistency of thin paste
6. Add salt, pepper, and a dust of nutmeg
7. Add the whites of three of the eggs whipped to a stiff froth
8. Prepare a bath of boiling fat
9. Fry the boulettes by passing tea-spoonfuls of the paste one after the other into the fat
10. When each little spoonful reaches the fat, it will swell up
11. As soon as it turns a rich golden yellow, it is done
