## Ingredients

- ox-cheek head
- liquor from boiling
- butter
- flour
- carrots
- turnips
- two or three onions
- salt
- pepper
- port wine

## Directions

1. Clean the head as before directed, and parboil it
2. Take out the bone
3. Stew it in part of the liquor in which it was boiled, thickened with a piece of butter mixed with flour, and browned
4. Cut into dice, or into any fancy shape, as many carrots and turnips as will fill a pint basin
5. Mince two or three onions, add the vegetables, and season with salt and pepper
6. Cover the pan closely, and stew it two hours
7. A little before serving, add a glassful of port wine
