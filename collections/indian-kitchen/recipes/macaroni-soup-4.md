## Ingredients

- a shin of beef
- pepper
- salt
- water
- soup herbs
- white of an egg
- macaroni

## Directions

1. Prepare a shin of beef soup as directed above, omitting the sauce and wine
2. Boil some macaroni until perfectly tender
3. Clear the soup with the white of an egg, then add the boiled macaroni, and warm up before serving
