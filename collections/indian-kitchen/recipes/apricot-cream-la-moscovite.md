## Ingredients

- apricot jam
- pint and a half of milk
- yolks of ten eggs
- ounce and a half of dissolved gelatine
- sugar
- half a pint of whipped cream
- a glass of noyeau

## Directions

1. Pass a pot of apricot jam through a fine sieve
2. Boil a pint and a half of milk
3. Mix into it when cool the yolks of ten eggs and make a rich custard
4. Stir into the custard when cool, an ounce and a half of dissolved gelatine, and then the strained jam
5. If not sweet enough, add a little sugar
6. Set the liquid in a mould upon ice
7. When you perceive that it is beginning to congeal, add half a pint of whipped cream, and a glass of noyeau
8. Stir the contents of the mould together
9. Bury it in ice for at least two hours
10. Serve as cold as possible
