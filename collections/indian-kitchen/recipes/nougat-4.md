## Ingredients

- 1 pound of almonds
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Blanch a pound of almonds, and cut each lengthwise into thin narrow pieces
2. Lay them on a dish in front of the fire, or in the oven (with the door open), to get perfectly dry
3. Melt in a sugar boiler 1 lb. of loaf sugar
4. When the sugar is a rich brown, put in the almonds, mix them well but carefully together
5. You will have a soft paste, which will harden when cold
6. Dip some small moulds into some very hot water, slightly but thoroughly oil them with melted butter
7. Put some of the mixture in one of them, and with the handle of a tea-spoon previously buttered, spread it out so as completely to line the mould
8. Trim the edges, and when cold, turn out the nougat
9. Serve with whipped cream or sweetmeats inside each
10. The nougat should be very thin
