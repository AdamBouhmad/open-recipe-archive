## Ingredients

- 1/4 seer of khowa kheer
- 1 powa of sugar
- raisins
- pieces of almonds and pistachios
- a handful of broken sugar-candy
- a pinch of powdered cardamoms
- 2 or 3 drops of essence of rose
- a few entire cardamom seeds

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1/4 seer of khowa kheer and drop it into a boiling syrup of 1 powa of sugar
2. Stir on mild heat
3. Add raisins, pieces of almonds and pistachios, a handful of broken sugar-candy and a pinch of powdered cardamoms when the mass is thick
4. Take down when it is stiff
5. Scrape the paste together
6. Mix with 2 or 3 drops of essence of rose and a few entire cardamom seeds
7. Form small balls of it
