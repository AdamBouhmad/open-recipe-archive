## Ingredients

- half a pound of fresh butter, washed in rose-water and beaten to a cream
- half a pound of dried and sifted flour
- seven ounces of pounded and sifted loaf sugar
- half an ounce of caraway-seeds
- two well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Mix the butter, flour, sugar, caraway-seeds, and eggs together into a paste
2. Roll it thin
3. Cut it into round cakes
4. Prick them
5. Bake them upon floured tins
