## Ingredients

- slices of seer
- chicken bones
- fish trimmings
- slices of carrot
- onion
- table-spoonful of dried sweet herbs
- glass of chablis
- butter
- flour
- fillets of cucumber

## Directions

1. Put fish trimmings, chicken bones, slices of carrot, onion, a table-spoonful of dried sweet herbs, and a glass of chablis into a clear broth
2. Stew neatly trimmed slices of seer plainly in the broth
3. Simmer gently until done
4. Drain the fish and strain the gravy
5. Thicken the gravy with butter and flour
6. Add previously cooked fillets of cucumber about an inch long and half an inch thick to the gravy
7. Add the pieces of fish to the gravy
8. Heat altogether until the stew steams freely and serve
