## Ingredients

- Entire grams ^ seer
- Potato ^ seer
- Ghee i powa
- Poppy-seed ^ powa
- Garam-masalla ^ tola
- Pepper i tola
- Cumin i tola
- Coriander i tola
- Some red chillies
- A ripe cocoa nut
- Ginger i chhatak
- Turmeric i tola
- Salt i tola
- A pinch of aniseed
- Almonds (if desired)
- Pistachios (if desired)

## Directions

1. Keep the grams under water for about 6 hours and then boil them in water till they are quite soft
2. Take the husks out that come out easily and drain off the water
3. Heat the garam-masalla, pepper, cumin and coriander in a pot without ghee or oil for a few minutes and then powder them with turmeric
4. Similarly heat the poppy-seed and pulverize them
5. Mix them together
6. Brown the pieces of potatoes and those of the kernel of the cocoa-nut in half a powa of ghee or oil and then add the boiled grams
7. Stir for 5 minutes, add the powdered spices, and stir for 5 minutes more
8. Then add a little water and salt, and stir
9. Take down when the water dries up and the mass is quite soft
10. Now heat half a powa of ghee (not oil) with the aniseed and red chillies
11. Brown the pieces of almonds and pistachios in it
12. Then add the boiled mass and stir
