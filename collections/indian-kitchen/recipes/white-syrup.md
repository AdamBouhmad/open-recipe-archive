## Ingredients

- 1 quart of water
- 1 pound of the best loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Put a quart of water over the fire in a well-tinned and clean copper stewpan
2. When on the boil, drop into it lump by lump one pound of the best loaf sugar
3. Let it well boil up
4. After all the sugar is thoroughly dissolved, pour it into a broad dish to cool
5. When cold it is fit for use
