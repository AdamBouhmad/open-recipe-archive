## Ingredients

- Ripe sweet tal fruit
- Salt
- Powdered rice or fine flour
- Almonds
- Rose essence
- Ghee or oil

## Directions

1. Mix the pulp of a ripe sweet tal fruit with salt and powdered rice or fine flour so that the mass becomes a thick paste
2. Chopped almonds and drops of essence of rose may be added
3. Heat a round bottomed pan with a little ghee or oil
4. Add a table-spoonful of the paste and bake till it is solid
5. Bake the other side also
