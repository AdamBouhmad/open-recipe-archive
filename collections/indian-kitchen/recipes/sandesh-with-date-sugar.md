## Ingredients

- 1.5 seer of solid date-sugar (khejuri gur)
- 1/2 seer of hard chhana
- 1/2 powa of ordinary sugar
- pieces of pistachios
- 6 or 7 drops of essence of rose

## Directions

1. Prepare a syrup of 1.5 seer of solid date-sugar (khejuri gur).
2. When it is slightly warm add a paste of 1/2 seer of hard chhana.
3. Stir vigorously.
4. When it is sticky and boiling take it off fire.
5. Add about 1/2 powa of ordinary sugar and mix thoroughly which will render the mass thick very quickly.
6. Remove the mass on a wooden plate.
7. Add pieces of pistachios and 6 or 7 drops of essence of rose and rub thoroughly.
8. Make balls and press in casts.
