## Ingredients

- plovers
- buttered cases
- thick glaze

## Directions

1. Roast the plovers (the grey bird will do for this dish)
2. Proceed as you did to compose the salpicon de gibier, fill your buttered cases with pieces of the plovers
3. Pour round and over them your thick glaze
4. Bake for five or ten minutes, and serve
