## Ingredients

- undiluted milk 5 seers
- cows' ghee
- powdered sugar-candy
- a little powdered cardamom seeds
- milk for baking

## Directions

1. Boil five seers of undiluted milk in two pans
2. Stir for a few minutes
3. Make the heat very mild and cease stirring altogether when creams will form
4. When the creams are quite thick remove one on the other pan
5. Cream will again appear on the milk from which it is removed
6. Remove this again on the other pan
7. Continue in this way 4 or 5 times
8. Remove one fourth of the milk from the pan containing the cream to the other pan
9. Again proceed in the same way till the cream is quite thick
10. Remove it on a plate and cut in big squares
11. Boil some pure cows' ghee in another pan with some powdered sugar-candy and a little powdered cardamom seeds
12. Smear the squares with this spiced ghee
13. Arrange one on another till the desired thickness is reached
14. Bake both sides of them for a little while with a little milk using very mild heat
