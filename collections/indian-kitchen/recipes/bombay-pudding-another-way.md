## Ingredients

- a good sweet custard
- a coconut
- butter
- grated nutmeg
- a wineglassful of brandy
- good puff paste

## Directions

1. Make a good sweet custard and set it aside
2. Rasp fine a coconut and fry it in a little butter with grated nutmeg
3. Pour into it gradually a wineglassful of brandy, stirring all the time
4. Have a pudding-dish lined with a good puff paste
5. Pour the fried coconut gradually into the custard, stirring well all the while
6. Fill the pudding-dish with the mixture
7. Bake it in a gentle oven for fifteen to twenty minutes, or until the pudding is cooked
