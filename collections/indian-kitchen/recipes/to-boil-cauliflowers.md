## Ingredients

- cauliflowers
- water
- salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Trim them neatly, and let them lie an hour or two in cold water
2. Rinse them in fresh cold water, and put them with a very little salt into boiling water
3. Boil them twenty minutes, or half an hour if very large
4. They may be boiled in milk and water, and require to be skimmed with particular attention
