## Ingredients

- a large tablespoonful of finely-grated horseradish
- a dessertspoonful of made mustard
- half a dessertspoonful of brown sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix well together a large tablespoonful of finely-grated horseradish, a dessertspoonful of made mustard, and half a dessertspoonful of brown sugar
2. Add vinegar till it be as thick as made mustard
3. Serve in a sauce-tureen
