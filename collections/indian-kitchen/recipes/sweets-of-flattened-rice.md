## Ingredients

- flattened rice 1 powa
- milk
- hard chhana 1 seer
- middlings 1/4 powa
- khowa kheer
- pieces or paste of almonds and pistachios
- powdered sugar-candy
- entire seeds of cardamom
- ghee for frying
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Wash the flattened rice and keep moistened with milk for 15 or 20 minutes
2. Take the moistened flattened rice out and make a paste with 1 seer of hard chhana
3. Mix with middlings up to 1/4 powa in weight
4. Make balls of it
5. Stuff them with a mixture of khowa kheer, pieces or paste of almonds and pistachios, powdered sugar-candy and a few entire seeds of cardamom
6. Form oval cakes
7. Fry in ghee
8. Immerse in a syrup of sugar
