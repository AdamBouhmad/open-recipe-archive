## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- two ounces of Parmesan or any mild grated cheese
- one ounce of butter
- one and a quarter ounce of flour
- one and a half pint of water
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil or steam the head first till all but done, which you must test with a skewer
2. Drain it thoroughly
3. Dissolve two ounces of Parmesan or any mild grated cheese in a sauce composed of one ounce of butter, one and a quarter ounce of flour, one and a half pint of water, pepper, and salt
4. Arrange the flower in a neat pie-dish, either whole if large enough, or in pieces with the green leaves introduced between each piece
5. Pour the sauce well round this
6. Dust a layer of cheese over the surface
7. Bake and serve as soon as the top takes colour
8. A red-hot iron passed closely over the surface of the dish will brown it nicely
