## Ingredients

- small shoulder of mutton
- sixteen or eighteen one-inch square pieces

## Directions

1. Obtain a small shoulder
2. Cut it up into sixteen or eighteen one-inch square pieces, rejecting all the bones
3. Curry it the same as a chicken
4. Allow it to simmer for half an hour longer, or until the meat is tender
