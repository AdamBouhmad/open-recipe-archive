## Ingredients

- bael fruit
- strong syrup

## Directions

1. Select fruit that is rather less than half ripe
2. Cut it into firm slices a quarter of an inch thick
3. Carefully remove the seeds together with the gum by which they are surrounded
4. Throw the slices into cold water
5. When all the bael is ready, remove it from the water
6. Simmer it in a strong syrup over a slow fire for half an hour or until it has become of a rich light brown colour
7. Bottle it when cool, taking care that the fruit is well covered with syrup
