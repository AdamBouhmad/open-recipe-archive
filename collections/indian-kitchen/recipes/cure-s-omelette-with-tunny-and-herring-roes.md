## Ingredients

- half a slice of preserved tunny
- soft roes of two herrings a la sardine
- a little shallot
- a dessert-spoonful of parsley
- butter
- six well mixed eggs
- a few drops of lime juice
- minced shallot and parsley

## Directions

1. Finely mince together half a slice of preserved tunny and the soft roes of two herrings a la sardine with a little shallot and a dessert-spoonful of parsley.
2. Toss the mixture in butter awhile.
3. Stir into a basin containing six well mixed eggs.
4. Cook the mixture as already explained.
5. Turn it out, when ready, into a hot dish containing a little melted butter, a few drops of lime juice and some minced shallot and parsley.
