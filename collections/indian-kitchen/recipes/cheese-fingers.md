## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of puff paste
- a pinch of salt
- two ounces of grated Parmesan or other mild cheese
- a very little cayenne, Nepaul pepper, or a few drops of tabasco

## Directions

1. Work the ingredients together
2. Roll the paste out about half an inch thick
3. Cut it into oblong shapes, about three inches long
4. Bake and serve as hot as possible on a napkin
