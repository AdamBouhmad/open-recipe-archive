## Ingredients

- salt
- turmeric paste
- onion paste
- garlic paste
- meat cubes
- ghee
- ginger-juice
- powdered pepper
- garam-masala
- water

## Directions

1. Mix salt and a paste of turmeric, onions and garlics with small cubes of meat and fry them with ghee.
2. 15 or 20 minutes later add ginger-juice, powdered pepper and garam-masala, and more ghee if necessary.
3. Sprinkle water at intervals to make them soft.
4. Take down when deep brown.
