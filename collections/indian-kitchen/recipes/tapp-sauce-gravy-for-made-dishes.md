## Ingredients

- a good seasoned stock
- arrowroot or corn-flour
- a tablespoonful of tapp sauce per cup of thickened stock

## Directions

1. Thicken a good seasoned stock with arrowroot or corn-flour
2. Add to every cup of the thickened stock a tablespoonful of tapp sauce
3. Pour it while hot over chicken, veal, beef, or prawn cutlets, or other made dishes
