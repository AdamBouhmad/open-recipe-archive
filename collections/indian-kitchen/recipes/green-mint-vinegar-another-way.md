## Ingredients

- fresh, full-grown, green mint-leaves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fill a wide mouthed bottle with fresh, full-grown, green mint-leaves
2. Pour in a quart of vinegar
3. After ten or fifteen days strain away the liquor
4. Re-fill the bottle with fresh leaves
5. Pour back the liquor
6. After it has steeped for ten or fifteen days longer, strain and bottle for use as required
