## Ingredients

- ½ powa khowa kheer
- ghee
- ½ powa raisins
- 1 chhatak pieces of almonds
- 1 chhatak pieces of pistachios
- 1 tola cardamom seeds
- powdered cloves ½ tola
- powdered cinnamon ½ tola
- sugar syrup
- 1 powa ghee
- 1 seer fine flour
- water
- ½ seer boiling ghee

## Directions

1. Fry ½ powa of khowa kheer with a little ghee and powder it
2. Fry ½ powa of raisins and pieces of almonds and pistachios 1 chhatak each with a little more ghee
3. Mix all with 1 tola of cardamom seeds, powdered cloves and cinnamon ½ tola each and a little syrup of sugar
4. Mix 1 powa of ghee with 1 seer of fine flour
5. Knead to a stiff dough with water
6. Make balls of it and prepare hollow cones of them
7. Stuff them with the above spiced kheer and close up the base
8. Fry them in ½ seer of boiling ghee and keep immersed in a very concentrated syrup of sugar for about half an hour
9. Take them out and roll on dry crystalline sugar
