## Ingredients

- snipes
- hot buttered toast
- crisply fried breadcrumbs
- salad
- butter
- Nepaul pepper

## Directions

1. Roast the birds properly over a bright fire (or bake them if possible, or fry them in a frying-pan).
2. Serve them on hot buttered toast, smothered with crisply fried breadcrumbs.
3. A nice salad, butter, and Nepaul pepper should go round.
