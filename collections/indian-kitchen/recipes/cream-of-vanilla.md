## Ingredients

- twelve drops of tincture of vanilla
- a quart of spirits of wine
- a quart of syrup

## Directions

1. Put twelve drops of tincture of vanilla into a quart of spirits of wine
2. shake it well
3. add a quart of syrup
4. when well mixed, let it stand for a quarter of an hour
5. filter it two or three times through filtering paper, but do not filter again if it comes out bright and clear the first time
