## Ingredients

- butter
- bandecais
- white pepper
- grated cheese
- milk
- egg yolk
- cream

## Directions

1. Butter a small pie-dish liberally
2. Fill it with the pulp and seeds of some well boiled, tender bandecais
3. Dust the pulp with white pepper, and some grated cheese
4. Pour a little milk in which the yolk of an egg has been beaten (or cream if you can allow it) in amongst the pulp
5. Dust a layer of cheese over the surface
6. Bake for ten or twelve minutes
7. Alternatively, the bandecai mixture can be baked in coquille shells, which should be served on a napkin
