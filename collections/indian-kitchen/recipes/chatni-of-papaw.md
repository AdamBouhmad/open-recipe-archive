## Ingredients

- 1 chhatak of seedless ripe tamarind
- 1½ powa of water
- big and green papaw
- 1 powa of papaw slices
- mustard seeds
- panch-forons
- broken chillies
- ½ chhatak of oil
- 1 chhatak of cleansed raisins
- salt
- mango-scented ginger juice 1 table-spoonful
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix 1 chhatak of seedless ripe tamarind thoroughly with 1½ powa of water and throw away the refuse
2. Peel a big and green papaw and cut in thin and long slices after removing the seeds
3. Boil 1 powa of the slices in water and drain out the water when they are fully boiled
4. Fry a few mustard, panch-forons and broken chillies in ½ chhatak of oil
5. Add the boiled pieces of papaw, 1 chhatak of cleansed raisins, the tamarind water and a generous pinch of salt
6. Mix with a table-spoonful of the juice of mango-scented ginger and a little sugar when the soup is thick, and take down
