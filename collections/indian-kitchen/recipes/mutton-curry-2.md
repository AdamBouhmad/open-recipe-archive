## Ingredients

- a small shoulder of mutton
- ghee
- water
- ground onions
- ground turmeric
- ground chilies
- ground ginger
- ground garlic
- salt

## Directions

1. Obtain a small shoulder
2. Cut it up into sixteen or eighteen one-inch square pieces, rejecting all the bones
3. Curry it according to the directions for chicken curry
