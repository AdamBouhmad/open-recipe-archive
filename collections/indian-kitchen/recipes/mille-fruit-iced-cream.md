## Ingredients

- the juice of three lemons
- the peel of one lemon
- one dessertspoonful of orange marmalade
- one dessertspoonful of dried cherries
- one dessertspoonful of preserved angelica
- half a pint of syrup
- a pint and a half of cream, or a pint of water
- a few drops of prepared cochineal

## Directions

1. Strain the juice of three lemons, and grate the peel of one
2. mince finely a dessertspoonful each of orange marmalade, dried cherries, and preserved angelica
3. add to these half a pint of syrup, and mix the whole with a pint and a half of cream, or a pint of water, and then drop in here and there a few drops of the prepared cochineal
4. Put it into a mould, and freeze as above directed
