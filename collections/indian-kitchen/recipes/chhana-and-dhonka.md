## Ingredients

- chhana
- ghee or oil
- flour
- potato
- coriander
- pepper
- turmeric
- ginger
- salt
- curd
- water
- cassia leaves
- red chillies
- garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Take some chhana and drain off the water under high pressure
2. Cut the chhana in rectangular pieces half inch thick and brown them in ghee or oil
3. Mix some flour with the chhana if it fails to take the form
4. Brown some medium pieces of peeled potatoes in oil or ghee
5. Add pasted coriander, pepper, turmeric, ginger and salt in adequate quantities
6. Add a little curd and water after a time
7. Add browned chhana when it boils
8. Season the curry with a little ghee boiled with a few cassia leaves and red chillies
9. Add some more ghee and powdered garam-masalla before taking down
10. Keep a little thick soup
