## Ingredients

- lard, four ounces
- castor oil, an ounce
- spermaceti, a quarter of an ounce
- salad oil, an ounce and a half
- white wax, a quarter of an ounce
- tincture of lytse, a drachm and a half
- oil of roses, verbena, bergamot, or cloves, twenty drops

## Directions

1. Melt the wax, spermaceti, and lard with the oils in a glazed earthen pipkin
2. When nearly cold add the scent
