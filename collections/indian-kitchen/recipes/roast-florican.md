## Ingredients

- florican
- Bombay onion
- fat boiled bacon
- bacon slice
- melted butter
- plain gravy
- fried crumbs
- bread sauce

## Directions

1. Pick, draw, singe, and truss the florican placing a Bombay onion, rolled in fat boiled bacon, inside its carcass
2. Tie a thin slice of bacon over the breast
3. Roast the bird over a moderate fire, basting it frequently with melted butter
4. A few minutes before the florican is done, remove the slice of bacon, so as to let the breast take colour
5. Serve with plain gravy, fried crumbs, and bread sauce
6. Time, about thirty minutes
