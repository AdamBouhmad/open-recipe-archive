## Ingredients

- finely-scraped horseradish
- vinegar
- cayenne
- black pepper
- celery-seeds
- bruised onions

## Directions

1. To three ounces of finely-scraped horseradish add a quart of vinegar and a drachm of cayenne, some black pepper and celery-seeds, and one ounce of bruised onions
2. After eight or ten days filter the vinegar
3. It will serve as an excellent relish for cold beef, salads, and for the preparation of mustard
