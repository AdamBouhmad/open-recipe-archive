## Ingredients

- One entire fish 1 or 1.5 seers
- ghee 1 powa
- besam 1 powa
- garam-masala 0.5 tola
- pepper 1 tola
- coriander 2 tolas
- curd 0.5 powa
- salt 2 tolas
- a few cassia leaves
- cloves
- panch-foron
- almonds
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Flake the fish and remove the fins and intestines
2. Wrap with a linen piece and give a coating of clay all over its surface
3. Burn it with a sand-bath in a pan
4. Remove the clay, wash the fish, remove the bones
5. Mix thoroughly with besam and powdered garam-masala and form small cakes
6. Heat water in a pan covering its bottom with banana leaves
7. Put the cakes carefully on them and boil till the cakes harden a little
8. Boil ghee with cassia leaves, cloves and panch-foron and brown the cakes in it
9. Add salt, water, curd, powdered coriander, pepper
10. Add almonds and a little flour when boiled
11. Take down after adding ghee and garam-masala
