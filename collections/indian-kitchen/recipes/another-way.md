## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- one pound of butter
- four eggs
- twelve ounces of loaf sugar powdered very finely
- candied peel
- caraway comfits

## Directions

1. Rub the butter and sugar into the flour with the hand, and by means of the eggs convert it into a stiff paste
2. Roll it out half an inch thick, and cut into square or round cakes
3. Pinch up the edges to the height of about an inch, and on the top of each cake place some slices of candied peel and some large caraway comfits, pressed down so as to imbed about half of each in the cake
4. Bake in a warm oven upon iron plates
