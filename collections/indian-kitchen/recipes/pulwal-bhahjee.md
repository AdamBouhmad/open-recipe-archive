## Ingredients

- a dozen or more pulwals
- ground turmeric
- ground chilies
- salt
- melted mustard oil

## Directions

1. Scrape or pare away very finely the upper green coating
2. Divide them lengthways into two pieces
3. Clear away all the seeds
4. Wash and drain away all the water
5. Steep them in ground turmeric, chilies, and salt for half an hour or longer
6. Fry them quite crisp in melted mustard oil
