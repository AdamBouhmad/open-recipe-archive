## Ingredients

- ground onions, two teaspoonfuls
- ground chilies, one teaspoonful
- salt, two teaspoonfuls
- ground turmeric, half a teaspoonful
- ground garlic, a quarter of a teaspoonful
- mustard oil, one chittack
- hilsa fish

## Directions

1. Slice a hilsa in the manner directed for a curry
2. Thoroughly clean and wash it with salt
3. Rub into the slices all the ground condiments and the remaining salt
4. Allow them to remain for at least an hour
5. Warm the oil and fry the slices of fish of a very light and bright brown
6. Serve up hot
