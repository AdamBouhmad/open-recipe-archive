## Ingredients

- pulses of kalai
- aniseed
- cumin
- turmeric
- salt
- ghee
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Fry some moistened pulses of kalai with powders of aniseed, cumin, turmeric and salt with ghee or oil
2. Make a stiff paste of them
3. Prepare flour dough as in the preparation of Luchi
4. Make big balls of it and flatten them a little
5. Put some spiced pulse in each and close up again in the form of a ball
6. Roll them into big discs of moderate thickness
7. Fry in plenty of ghee
