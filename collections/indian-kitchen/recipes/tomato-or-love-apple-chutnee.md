## Ingredients

- two hundred large ripe love-apples
- four ounces of raisins
- seven ounces of salt
- four ounces of sugar
- eight ounces of chilies, finely sliced
- four ounces of ground garlic
- seven ounces of ground mustard-seed
- a quart of vinegar

## Directions

1. Parboil the tomatoes in a quart of vinegar
2. Add the other ingredients, and allow the whole to stand for ten to twelve hours
3. Boil it for twenty to thirty minutes over a slow fire
4. When cold, bottle it
