## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of milk
- 5 powa of a loaf of bread
- two eggs
- 1/2 powa of sugar
- jelly of any fruit
- powdered mace

## Directions

1. Heat 1/2 seer of milk and as soon as it boils add 5 powa of a loaf of bread broken into fragments, and take down.
2. When cold add the yolks of two eggs, 1/2 powa of sugar, some jelly of any fruit and a little powdered mace, and mix up.
3. Heat for about half an hour.
