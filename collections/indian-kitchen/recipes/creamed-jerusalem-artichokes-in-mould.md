## Ingredients

- two pounds of artichokes
- milk
- half a pint of cream
- four eggs
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil the artichokes in milk
2. Mash the artichokes, and pass them through the hair sieve
3. Add the cream and the eggs well beaten up whites and all
4. Season with pepper and salt
5. Put the mixture into a well-buttered mould, and steam it for one hour
6. Turn it out, and garnish it with tomato puree, Parmesan sauce, or a rich veloute
