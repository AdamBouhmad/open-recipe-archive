## Ingredients

- small quantities of potatoes
- kutchoo or artichoke
- sweet potatoes or suckercund
- carrots
- red and white pumpkins
- tomatoes
- four teaspoonfuls of ground onions
- one teaspoonful of ground turmeric
- one teaspoonful of ground chilies
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground garlic
- one teaspoonful of roasted and ground coriander-seed
- two large cups of tamarind water
- jaggry
- fresh chilies
- one chittack or two ounces of mustard oil
- one teaspoonful of coUinga or onion-seeds

## Directions

1. Cut the vegetables into large pieces and boil in water with the ground onions, turmeric, chilies, garlic, and coriander-seed
2. Prepare two large cups of tamarind water slightly sweetened with jaggry and strain through a sieve
3. Add the strained water to the boiled vegetables with a few fresh chilies
4. Melt one chittack or two ounces of mustard oil in a separate pot
5. While the oil is bubbling, fry in it a teaspoonful of the coUinga
6. When sufficiently fried pour it over the boiled vegetables including the tamarind water
7. Close up the pot and allow it to simmer for fifteen to twenty minutes
8. Serve cold
