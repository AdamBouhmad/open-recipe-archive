## Ingredients

- beetroot
- pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley
- cress
- butter
- vinegar
- poulette sauce

## Directions

1. Bake the beetroot
2. After having thus cooked it, cut it into slices
3. season them with pepper, salt, chopped parsley, and cress
4. give them a turn or two in a pan with a pat of butter and a few drops of vinegar
5. Dish up
6. pour a poulette sauce over them
