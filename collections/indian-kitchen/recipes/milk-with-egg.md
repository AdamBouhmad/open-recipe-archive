## Ingredients

- contents of a fresh egg
- 1 cup hot milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pour the contents of a fresh egg in a cup of hot milk and mix thoroughly.
2. Add a little sugar to taste.
3. This diet is an excellent body-builder and should be taken every morning.
