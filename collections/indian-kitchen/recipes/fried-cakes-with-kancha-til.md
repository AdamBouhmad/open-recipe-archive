## Ingredients

- kancha-til sesame seeds
- powdered rice or flour or pulse-meal
- salt
- aniseed

## Directions

1. Wash and keep them under water for a while
2. Grind into a paste
3. Add either powdered rice or flour or pulse-meal, and salt and entire aniseed
4. Beat thoroughly, form into cakes and fry
