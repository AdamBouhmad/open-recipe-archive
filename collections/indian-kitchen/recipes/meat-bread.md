## Ingredients

- rabbit or hare
- salt
- water
- eggs
- sugar
- powdered pepper
- ginger-juice
- lemon-juice
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare a rabbit or hare, cut in big pieces and keep them immersed in saline water for half an hour.
2. Boil the pieces of salted meat in fresh water.
3. Take out the boiled pieces and remove the bones.
4. Mix them with 2 or 3 eggs, sugar, powdered pepper, ginger-juice, a few drops of lemon-juice, a small quantity of flour, salt and a little water in which meat had been boiled, if necessary.
5. Form rectangular cakes of moderate thickness, cover with white paper and fry with butter on mild heat for about half an hour.
