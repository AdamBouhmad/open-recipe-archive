## Ingredients

- one and a half chittack of mustard oil
- four teaspoonfuls of ground onions
- one teaspoonful of ground chilies
- half a teaspoonful of ground turmeric
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of ground garlic
- one teaspoonful and a half of ground salt
- one cupful of water
- omra
- saug

## Directions

1. Peel the omra and half fry if large
2. Thoroughly clean and wash the greens
3. Put them into a colander and allow all the water to drain away
4. Warm the oil and fry the ground condiments
5. Fry the saug and omra until crisp
6. Add the water and cook over a slow fire until the greens and omra are tender
