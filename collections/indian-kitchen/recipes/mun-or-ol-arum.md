## Ingredients

- arum
- tamarind or other acidic substance
- ground turmeric
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Peel the arum thickly and cut in big pieces.
2. Dry them in the sun.
3. Boil in a solution of tamarind or other acidic substance so that they may not irritate the mucous membranes of the mouth and throat.
4. Smear the boiled pieces with ground turmeric and salt.
5. Fry in boiling oil.
