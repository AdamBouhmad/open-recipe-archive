## Ingredients

- three eggs
- salt-spoonful of salt
- table-spoonful of cream or milk
- dust of pepper
- piece of butter the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Break three eggs into a bowl with a salt-spoonful of salt, a table-spoonful of cream or of milk, and a dust of pepper
2. Mix them well
3. Melt a piece of butter the size of an egg in your omelette-pan
4. Pour in your mixture
5. Stir it about unceasingly until it is lightly set
6. Turn it out
