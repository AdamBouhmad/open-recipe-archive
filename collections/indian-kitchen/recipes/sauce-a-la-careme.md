## Ingredients

- yolks of three eggs
- two ounces of sugar
- claret-glassful of Madeira

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of three eggs with two ounces of sugar and a claret-glassful of Madeira
2. When well mixed, set the sauce in the bain-marie and let it thicken like a custard
3. When smooth and creamy, serve it in a boat, hot
