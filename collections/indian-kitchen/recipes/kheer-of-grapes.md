## Ingredients

- 1 seer of grapes
- water
- 1 powa of sugar
- 3 or 4 egg yolks
- essence of rose

## Directions

1. Wash 1 seer of grapes, remove the stems and boil with a little water.
2. Then crush them with the hands in the water bringing out the juice.
3. Throw away the skins and seeds.
4. Heat the juice.
5. Add 1 powa of sugar when it boils.
6. Add the heated yolks of 3 or 4 eggs when much reduced.
7. Take down when thick and mix with 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose.
8. Serve when cool.
