## Ingredients

- yolks of four hard-boiled eggs
- a teaspoonful of very finely sifted flour
- tender leaves of parsley, finely chopped
- white pepper
- salt
- yolk of a raw egg

## Directions

1. Grind down to a powder or paste the yolks of four hard-boiled eggs
2. Add a teaspoonful of very finely sifted flour
3. Add some tender leaves of parsley, finely chopped
4. Add a little white pepper and salt
5. Grind and mix all well together with the yolk of a raw egg
6. Roll into small balls
7. Boil for two or three minutes
