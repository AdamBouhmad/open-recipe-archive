## Ingredients

- a couple of ounces of butter
- a dessert-spoonful of flour
- a heaped up tea-spoonful of French mustard
- a pinch of salt
- half a pint of broth or water

## Directions

1. Melt a couple of ounces of butter in a small sauce-pan
2. blend with it a dessert-spoonful of flour, and a heaped up tea-spoonful of French mustard with a pinch of salt
3. when thoroughly mixed, add half a pint of broth or water
4. let it come to the boil, then strain through the pointed strainer into a hot sauce-boat
