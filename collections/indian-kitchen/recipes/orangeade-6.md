## Ingredients

- one orange
- peel of orange
- boiling water
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Squeeze out the juice of an orange
2. Pour boiling water on a little of the peel and cover it close
3. Boil water and sugar to a thin syrup and skim it
4. When cold, mix all together with as much water as will make a rich drink
5. Strain through a jelly-bag and ice
