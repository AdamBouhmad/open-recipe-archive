## Ingredients

- yolks of six eggs
- white sugar
- lemon essence or vanilla essence or ratafia essence
- whites of six eggs
- finely sifted sugar
- preserve

## Directions

1. Beat up the yolks of six eggs with a tablespoonful of white sugar
2. Flavour the mixture with lemon, vanilla, or ratafia essence
3. Whisk the whites to a stiff froth independently
4. Blend the two thoroughly
5. Put this into a small circular souffle tin
6. Bake it in a very quick oven
7. Send it to table the moment it is ready, dusted over with finely sifted sugar
8. Serve immediately, close to the dining-room door
