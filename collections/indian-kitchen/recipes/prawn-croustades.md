## Ingredients

- two or three small dinner rolls
- butter
- boiled prawns
- mace
- pepper
- salt
- white sauce
- bread-crumb

## Directions

1. Choose two or three small dinner rolls which have been baked in tins and will stand upright
2. Scoop out all the crumb
3. Fry the cases so obtained a golden yellow colour in melted butter
4. Drain them
5. Prepare enough boiled prawns to fill them
6. Cut them into quarter inch pieces
7. Toss them in melted butter with a little mace, pepper and salt
8. Fill your cases, moistening the salpicon with a little white sauce
9. Shake a little bread-crumb over the surface of each
10. Place them on a buttered baking tin
11. Heat them for five minutes in the oven
12. Serve as soon as the tops take colour
