## Ingredients

- two or three ripe tomatoes
- a quarter their bulk of white onion
- salt
- two green chillies
- a little bit of celery
- black pepper
- a tea-spoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove the seeds and watery juice from two or three ripe tomatoes, chop them up with a quarter their bulk of white onion, and season the mince with a little salt.
2. Add a pinch of salt, two green chillies chopped small, and a little bit of celery also chopped.
3. Give the whole a dust of black pepper, and moisten it with a tea-spoonful of vinegar.
