## Ingredients

- a fine sweet onion
- an ounce of butter
- an ounce of flour
- half a pint of gravy
- pepper and salt
- a pinch of sugar
- a table-spoonful of vinegar
- one of mixed mustard

## Directions

1. Chop up a fine sweet onion, throw the mince into a sauce-pan with an ounce of butter
2. Let it take colour, then add an ounce of flour by degrees, and when that has been well worked, half a pint of gravy, pepper and salt at discretion, and a pinch of sugar
3. When thoroughly mixed, and piping hot, pass the sauce through the tin strainer, catching up all lumps
4. at the last moment, stir in a table-spoonful of vinegar, and one of mixed mustard
