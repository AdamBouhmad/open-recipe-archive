## Ingredients

- enough young moringa pods to yield seeds enough to fill a little pie-dish
- a tablespoonful of cream, or a coffee-cupful of milk
- yolks of two eggs
- salt
- pepper
- a few drops of anchovy essence
- butter
- Parmesan or any nice mild dry cheese

## Directions

1. Boil the moringa pods
2. Scrape out the seeds and the tender flesh inside the pods into a basin
3. Stir into this a tablespoonful of cream, or a coffee-cupful of milk in which the yolks of two eggs have been well beaten
4. Season with salt and pepper, and add a few drops of anchovy essence
5. Pass this into a well buttered pie-dish, and grate over the surface a good layer of Parmesan or any nice mild dry cheese
6. Bake for a quarter of an hour, and serve
