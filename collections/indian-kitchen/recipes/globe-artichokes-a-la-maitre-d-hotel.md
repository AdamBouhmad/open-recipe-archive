## Ingredients

- trimmed globe artichoke quarters
- butter
- lime juice
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare artichoke quarters as directed and boil until nearly done
2. Toss in butter
3. Serve on a hot silver dish with the melted butter, a squeeze of lime juice and a sprinkling of very finely chopped curled parsley
