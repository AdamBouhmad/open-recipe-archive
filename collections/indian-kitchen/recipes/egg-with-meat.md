## Ingredients

- pieces of meat
- salt
- curd
- turmeric
- pepper
- cumin
- coriander
- red chillies
- garam masala
- ginger
- onions
- ghee
- egg contents
- powdered biscuit

## Directions

1. Wash big pieces of meat
2. Mix with salt, curd, turmeric, pepper, cumin, coriander, red chillies, garam masala, ginger and onions all ground to paste
3. Boil ghee and add meat
4. Allow to simmer until water evaporates
5. Beat egg contents with salt, powdered turmeric, pasted onion and ginger juice
6. Dip each piece of meat into egg mixture
7. Roll on powdered biscuit
8. Fry in plenty of ghee
