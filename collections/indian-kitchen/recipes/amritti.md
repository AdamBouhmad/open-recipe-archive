## Ingredients

- rice-flour
- water
- eggs
- saffron
- ghee

## Directions

1. Keep some rice-flour moistened with water for several hours
2. Mix it with double its quantity of the contents of eggs and beat thoroughly
3. Add a little saffron for colouring and proceed as with Amrit
