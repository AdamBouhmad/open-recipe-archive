## Ingredients

- six coffee-cupfuls of broth or consomme
- tomato pulp
- salt
- black pepper
- three coffee-cupfuls of well cleansed raw rice
- ounce of fresh butter
- nutmeg
- raisins

## Directions

1. Put into a sauce-pan six coffee-cupfuls of broth or consomme into which sufficient tomato pulp has been stirred to slightly thicken it, flavour this with salt and black pepper to taste.
2. Set the sauce-pan on the fire.
3. As soon as the liquid boils, cast into it three coffee-cupfuls of well cleansed raw rice.
4. Reduce the heat, and let the rice stew gently in the tomato-flavoured gravy.
5. Watch it narrowly, and as soon as it has sucked up the whole of it, stir into it an ounce of fresh butter.
6. Just before serving, a dust of nutmeg should be added, and some raisins carefully picked and cleaned may be thrown in as a garnish.
