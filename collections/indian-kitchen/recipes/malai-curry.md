## Ingredients

- fish (1 seer)
- cocoa-nuts (1 or 2)
- cumin (1 tola)
- pepper (1/2 tola)
- coriander (1/2 tola)
- turmeric (2 tolas)
- ginger (2 1/2 tolas)
- onions (1 chhatak)
- oil (1 powa)
- ghee (1 chhatak)
- garam-masalla (1/2 tola)
- flour (1/2 chhatak)
- chillies
- cassia leaves
- salt (1 1/2 tola)

## Directions

1. Prepare lobsters and big fishes and cut in big pieces, keeping lobsters entire
2. Fry them in oil
3. Bring out the vegetable milk from the cocoa-nuts
4. Make a paste of all the spices
5. Make a paste of the garam-masalla separately
6. Boil oil and fry cassia leaves and the pasted spices in it for about 5 minutes, adding water little by little if necessary
7. Add the vegetable milk
8. On boiling add salt, the fishes and a little thin solution of flour
9. When properly boiled season the curry with boiling ghee with chillies and a few entire cumins fried in it
10. Add garam-masalla and take down when the soup is fairly thick
