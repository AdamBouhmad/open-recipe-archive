## Ingredients

- cucumber
- ground turmeric
- salt
- oil
- water

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into two halves a well developed cucumber.
2. Remove the seeds and cut into slices.
3. Smear with ground turmeric and salt.
4. Fry in oil.
5. Sprinkle water from time to time to make them soft.
