## Ingredients

- Half a pound of flour
- two tea-spoonfuls of Yeatman's powder
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- four or five table-spoonfuls of water

## Directions

1. Work this as above, reserving the baking powder to the last
2. Set the dough in a tin, or form it in the well-known cottage shape
3. Bake
