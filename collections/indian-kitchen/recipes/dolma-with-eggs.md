## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- salt
- ginger juice
- minced onions
- powdered turmeric
- red chillies
- pepper
- almonds
- raisins
- aniseeds
- cardamom seeds
- ghee
- sugar
- patols
- flour paste
- liquid beaten egg contents
- bread crumbs or powdered biscuits

## Directions

1. Shell hard boiled eggs and mince finely
2. Add salt, ginger juice, minced onions, powdered turmeric, red chillies, pepper, almonds, raisins, aniseeds and cardamom seeds
3. Mix thoroughly
4. Boil ghee with pinch of sugar and fry this mixture in it
5. Skin patols by rubbing against blunt edge of knife
6. Cut off one or both ends and remove seeds with pin
7. Fill hollows with prepared egg mixture
8. Close ends with flour paste
9. Dip into liquid beaten egg contents
10. Roll on bread crumbs or powdered biscuits
11. Fry in plenty of ghee
