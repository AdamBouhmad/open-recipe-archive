## Ingredients

- mature yet green mangoes
- 1 chhatak of pasted turmeric
- 1 seer of mango pieces
- 1 powa of salt

## Directions

1. Remove the stalks of some mature yet green mangoes and cut them in four pieces without peeling them
2. Remove the seeds and smear 1 chhatak of pasted turmeric with 1 seer of the pieces
3. Wash them after a few hours and again mix 1 powa of salt with them and dry them perfectly in the sun for several days
