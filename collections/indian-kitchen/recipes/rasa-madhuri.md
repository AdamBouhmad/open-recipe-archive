## Ingredients

- 1.5 seer of hard chhana
- 1 seer of sabeda
- water
- sugar syrup
- white sugar
- sugar-candy

## Directions

1. Make a fine paste of 1.5 seer of hard chhana
2. Mix with 1 seer of sabeda and a little water making a dough
3. Make small balls of it
4. Boil in a syrup of sugar
5. Take them out and roll on white sugar
6. When cold roll again on crystals of sugar-candy after dipping them for a moment, if necessary, into a cold syrup of sugar
