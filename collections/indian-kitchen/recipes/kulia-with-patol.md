## Ingredients

- Patol 1 seer
- ghee 1/2 powa
- coriander 1 tola
- pepper 1/4 tola
- cumin 1/4 tola
- garam-masalla 1/4 tola
- turmeric 1/4 tola
- salt 1 tola
- curd 1 chhatak
- cassia leaves

## Directions

1. Skin the patols very thin and cut both ends longitudinally and vertically to one another to a quarter of their lengths so that salt and spices may enter into them
2. Brown them in ghee
3. Boil some ghee in another pan with a few cassia leaves and then pour in water with powdered turmeric, pepper, cumin, coriander and salt
4. When it boils add patol and curd and cover the pan
5. Add powdered garam-masalla and take down when the soup is thick
