## Ingredients

- Meat 1 seer
- Potato 1/2 seer
- Ghee 1/4 powa
- Oil 1 chhatak
- Curd 1 powa
- Salt 2 1/2 tolas
- Turmeric 1 tola
- Ginger 2 1/2 tolas
- Cumin 1 tola
- Pepper 1 tola
- Coriander 1 tola
- Onions 1/2 powa
- Garlic pieces
- Chillies
- Cassia leaves
- Garam-masalla 1 tola
- Sugar a pinch

## Directions

1. Make a paste of all the spices including onions and garlics
2. Keep garam-masalla entire or paste separately
3. Mix thoroughly some oil, a little salt and a part of the pasted spices with the meat cut in big pieces, and keep aside
4. Peel the potatoes, cut in big pieces and brown them with oil
5. Boil ghee, fry cassia leaves and a few cloves in it and add the rest of the pasted spices with curd
6. Stir for a few minutes and add the meat
7. Stir thoroughly and cover up the pot
8. Add hot water when the water coming out of the meat dries up
9. On boiling add potatoes and salt
10. Add sugar, garam-masalla, and ghee before taking down
11. Keep a little thick soup
