## Ingredients

- minced onions
- green chillies
- powdered turmeric
- pepper
- salt
- contents of an egg
- ghee

## Directions

1. Mix minced onions, green chillies, powdered turmeric, pepper and salt thoroughly with the contents of an egg.
2. Pour it on a little hot ghee in a pan.
3. Tilt the pan so that it spreads evenly thick and fry both sides till light brown.
4. Roll it up, press flat and fry till golden brown.
