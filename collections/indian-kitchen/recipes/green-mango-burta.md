## Ingredients

- quarter of a teaspoonful of ground chilies
- half a teaspoonful of ground fresh mint-leaves
- half a teaspoonful of ground ginger
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- a teaspoonful of sugar
- two ordinary large-size green mangoes
- clean water

## Directions

1. Peel the mangoes, divide, and throw them into clean water
2. Remove the stones
3. Bruise them to a perfect pulp with the aid of the curry-stone and muller, ensuring the stone is perfectly clean
4. Mix the sugar well with the pulp
5. If the mango is very acid, add a little more sugar
6. Mix it with the salt and ground condiments
7. Add more salt or sugar if required
