## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil your potatoes
2. Slice them moderately thickly
3. Toss them in plenty of butter in your frying-pan till they colour nicely
4. Pour the brown butter over them
5. Give them a dust of salt
