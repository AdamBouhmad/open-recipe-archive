## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 1/2 seer potatoes
- 1/4 powa onions
- 1/2 powa peas, green or moistened
- 1/2 powa ghee or oil
- 1 tola coriander
- 1 tola ginger
- 1 tola salt
- 1/2 tola turmeric
- 1/2 tola pepper
- 1/2 tola cumin
- 1/2 tola garam-masalla
- a few cassia leaves
- red chillies
- cloves
- water

## Directions

1. Shell 8 hard-boiled eggs and cut in halves.
2. Cut the potatoes in big pieces and the onions in small ones after peeling them.
3. Brown the eggs and onions separately.
4. Brown potatoes and peas together.
5. Add turmeric, coriander, pepper, cumin, ginger, red chillies and salt, all pasted together, with water.
6. Add eggs and onions when the curry is half-boiled.
7. Season it with ghee or oil with a few cassia leaves, red chillies and cloves fried in it.
8. Add ghee and powdered garam-masalla and take down while a little soup remains.
