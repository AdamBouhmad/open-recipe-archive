## Ingredients

- Khova kheer
- Ghee
- Onions
- Ginger
- Coriander
- Turmeric
- Garam-masalla
- Salt
- Minced meat
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Besam of grams
- Water
- Eggs white
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Fry some khova kheer in ghee and powder them
2. Make a paste of onions, ginger, coriander, turmeric and garam-masalla
3. Mix this paste and salt with minced meat
4. Heat some ghee and slightly brown some raisins and pieces of almonds
5. Add to them minced meat and fry till dry
6. Add besam of grams about 1/4 of the meat in quantity 2 or 3 minutes before the dryness is reached
7. Add water and boil for about 15 minutes
8. Add the fried and powdered khova kheer, the white of eggs and sugar to taste
9. Boil a few minutes more and take down before too dry
