## Ingredients

- ghee
- red chillies
- raw potatoes
- powdered pepper
- turmeric
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Heat some ghee with a few red chillies and brown a little in it pieces of some raw potatoes
2. Add powdered pepper, turmeric and salt
3. Stir a little and add water
4. Add a little more ghee when the water dries up, stir for a while and take down
