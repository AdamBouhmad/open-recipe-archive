## Ingredients

- 1 good sized cucumber or 2 small ones
- plenty of boiling water
- half an ounce of butter
- a tea-spoonful of salt
- half a pint of poulette sauce

## Directions

1. Take a good sized cucumber or two small ones; cut them lengthwise into quarters, remove the seeds, and peel off the green skin
2. Cut them into pieces two inches long and one inch thick
3. Put them into a stew-pan with plenty of boiling water, half an ounce of butter, and a tea-spoonful of salt
4. Simmer them until three parts done
5. Drain the liquor off, and turn the pieces of cucumber out upon a clean dish
6. Cut each piece in half and cover them up
7. Make half a pint of poulette sauce, put the pieces of cucumber into it
8. Warm gently in the bain-marie, and serve
