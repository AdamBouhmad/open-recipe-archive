## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer khowa kheer
- milk
- rose-water 2 or 3 tea-spoonfuls
- cardamom seeds
- powdered aniseeds
- pistachios
- camphor
- sugar syrup
- crystalline sugar

## Directions

1. Make a muddy paste of 1/2 seer of khowa kheer with milk and 2 or 3 tea-spoonfuls of rose-water
2. Mix with the seeds of a few cardamoms, a little powdered aniseeds, some pieces of pistachios and very little camphor
3. Prepare cakes as before
4. Boil in a syrup of sugar on mild heat till hard and well boiled
5. Pick them out and roll on crystalline sugar
6. Serve when cool
