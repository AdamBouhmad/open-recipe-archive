## Ingredients

- 1 seer of sweet potatoes
- 1/2 powa of ghee
- a pinch of aniseeds
- 1 seer of milk
- 1 1/2 powa of sugar
- raisins, almonds and pistachios
- very little camphor

## Directions

1. Boil 1 seer of sweet potatoes, skin them, rub to a pulp and remove the fibres, if any.
2. Boil 1/2 powa of ghee with a pinch of aniseeds and fry the pulp with the ghee.
3. Then add 1 seer of milk, 1 1/2 powa of sugar, raisins, almonds and pistachios as desired.
4. Stir constantly all the while.
5. Mix with very little camphor when thick and take down.
