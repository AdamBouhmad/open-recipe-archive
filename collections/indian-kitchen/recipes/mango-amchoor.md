## Ingredients

- green mangoes
- salt
- dry pounded turmeric
- chilies
- dry ginger

## Directions

1. Peel and quarter some green mangoes
2. Sprinkle with salt, and expose them to the sun until they begin to dry up
3. Rub them with dry pounded turmeric, chilies, and dry ginger
4. Sprinkle more salt, and expose them to the sun again, until they are quite dried up
5. Bottle and keep for use
