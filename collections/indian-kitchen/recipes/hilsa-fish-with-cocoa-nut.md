## Ingredients

- pieces of hilsa fish
- salt
- mustard
- turmeric
- chillies
- mustard oil
- half-ripe cocoa-nut
- flour paste

## Directions

1. Smear some pieces of hilsa fish with adequate quantities of salt, mustard, turmeric, chillies, all powdered, and sufficient mustard oil.
2. Stuff the stone of a half-ripe cocoa-nut breaking it in two and join the two parts water-tight with a little paste of flour.
3. Boil it for an hour.
4. Then open it, take the lump out and serve.
