## Ingredients

- tomatoes, twenty
- tamarinds, two or three
- mustard oil
- ground onions
- ground chilies
- ground turmeric
- ground garlic
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Chahkee twenty tomatoes with the pulp of two or three tamarinds as directed for red pumpkin chahkee
