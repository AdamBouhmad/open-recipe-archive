## Ingredients

- moog dal
- salt
- mustard oil

## Directions

1. Wash moog dal thoroughly.
2. Tie very loosely in a piece of clean cloth as they increase four or five times in volume when boiled.
3. Drop the bundle into the rice when the latter start boiling.
4. Mix salt and mustard oil with the boiled moog dal, pressed to a paste, before eating.
