## Ingredients

- breakfast-cupful of gravy
- minced shallot or sweet herb
- eggs
- bread-crumbs
- minced anchovy or remains of cold fish

## Directions

1. Choose a little pie-dish and pour some of the gravy into it to cover the bottom well
2. Flavour it with a little minced shallot or any sweet herb
3. Set it in the oven and when it is hot, break into it as many eggs as will fill the dish nicely without crowding
4. Shake some bread-crumbs over the eggs and some little pieces of minced anchovy or the remains of any cold fish
5. Return the dish to the oven for three or four minutes so that its contents may partly set
6. Pour the rest of the gravy evenly over the surface
7. Add another layer of fine crumbs
8. Bake for five or six minutes, being careful not to let the eggs harden
