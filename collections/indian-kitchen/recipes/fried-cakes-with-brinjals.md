## Ingredients

- tender brinjals
- water or rice
- minced green chillies
- onions
- salt
- rice-flour
- oil

## Directions

1. Boil a few tender brinjals in water or in rice
2. remove the stalks and peel
3. Mix with minced green chillies, onions, salt and rice-flour and beat
4. Form into cakes and fry in oil
