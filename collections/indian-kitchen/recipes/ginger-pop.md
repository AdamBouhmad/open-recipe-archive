## Ingredients

- one ounce well-bruised green ginger cleaned of all rind
- one ounce cream of tartar
- one pound white sugar
- some toddy
- rind and juice of one large lime
- four quarts water
- one claretglassful good fresh toddy

## Directions

1. Boil the ginger, cream of tartar, sugar, toddy, and lime rind and juice in four quarts of water for twenty minutes
2. When nearly cold, add one claretglassful of good fresh toddy
3. Let it stand for six hours
4. Put into soda-water bottles. It will fill eight or nine bottles.
