## Ingredients

- Bombay onions
- sheep's kidneys
- anchovies
- parsley
- lime
- butter
- spiced pepper
- brown sauce

## Directions

1. Take six large Bombay onions and have ready ten sheep's kidneys, scalded and skinned, but uncooked.
2. Boil the onions till three parts done and take them out and drain them.
3. Slice off the top of each one (as you would treat an egg) and carefully remove the inside, leaving a hollow big enough to hold two small kidneys cut into eight pieces.
4. Take two anchovies, pick out their spines and cut the fish into little squares.
5. Have some minced parsley handy, a lime, and some of the inside of the onion that you scooped out, minced and peppered.
6. Put a lump of butter into the onion case first, then a little of the minced onion peppered.
7. Add your pieces of kidney, with little bits of anchovy here and there.
8. Crown the top again with minced onion and a pat of butter.
9. A drop or so of lime juice should be given during the packing, and an occasional dust of spiced pepper.
10. Place the cap on again, and when the six onions are stuffed, lay them in a buttered baking dish.
11. Bake in a slow oven for an hour.
12. When done, pour a brown sauce (separately made) over them and serve in their own dish.
