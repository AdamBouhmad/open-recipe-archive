## Ingredients

- four ounces of flour
- an ounce of fresh butter
- a coffee-cup of milk
- one ounce of finely sifted sugar
- the rasped peel of three nice limes
- a few drops of lemon essence

## Directions

1. Mix together in a sauce-pan four ounces of flour and an ounce of fresh butter
2. Add a coffee-cup of milk and stir to the consistency of a thick batter
3. If too thick, dilute with a little more milk
4. Stir in one ounce of finely sifted sugar
5. Add the rasped peel of three nice limes with a few drops of lemon essence
6. Stir the whole mix
