## Ingredients

- Moog pulses
- Ghee
- Scraped cocoa-nut kernel
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Make a preparation of scraped cocoa-nut kernel and sugar as in Chandrapuli
2. Keep some pulses of moog under water for 2 hours
3. Fry them with ghee and make a stiff paste of them without water
4. Make medium balls of it, press a little flat
5. Stuff each with a little of the above preparation and make balls again
6. Fry them in plenty of ghee
