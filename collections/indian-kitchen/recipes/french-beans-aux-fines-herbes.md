## Ingredients

- cooked French beans
- butter
- parsley
- common garden cress
- shallot
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Turn the cooked beans out into a hot dish
2. Melt a lump of butter in a little sauce-pan with some finely-chopped parsley, some common garden cress, some finely-minced shallot if approved, some pepper, and a pinch of grated nutmeg
3. Pour over the beans and serve
