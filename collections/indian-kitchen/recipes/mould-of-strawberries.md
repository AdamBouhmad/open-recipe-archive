## Ingredients

- pot of strawberry jam
- water
- cochineal
- glass of liqueur or brandy
- ounce of gelatine
- almonds
- fresh strawberries

## Directions

1. Dilute a pot of strawberry jam with sufficient water to make enough syrup to fill the mould
2. Strain it and colour it a rosy pink with a drop or two of cochineal
3. Add a glass of liqueur or brandy
4. Melt an ounce of gelatine and strain it when cold into the syrup, stirring well
5. Decorate a plain mould with almonds and put it on ice
6. Pour the strawberry syrup into it by degrees
7. Cover it over and set it in ice for two hours
8. Turn it out and serve it with cold custard in glasses or iced cream
9. If in season, you can make the syrup with fresh strawberries
10. When filling the mould, you may add to its attractiveness by setting layers of whole fruit in it in the style of a jelly
