## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- tomato sauce or sauce blanche or bechamel or sauce piquante
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil or steam the cauliflower carefully
2. Cut the stalk flat so that the cauliflower can sit up with the flower in the centre and the leaves round it
3. Pour about it a good tomato sauce or a plain sauce blanche, bechamel, or sauce piquante
4. Dust some finely rasped crumbs over the whole
