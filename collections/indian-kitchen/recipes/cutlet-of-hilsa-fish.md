## Ingredients

- big pieces of hilsa fish
- pasted ginger
- onions
- salt
- curd
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- ghee

## Directions

1. Rub some big pieces of hilsa fish with pasted ginger, onions and salt
2. Keep them merged in a sufficient quantity of curd for 3 or 4 hours
3. Dip them into eggs beaten with salt
4. Roll on bread crumbs
5. Fry till brown in ghee
6. Serve while hot
