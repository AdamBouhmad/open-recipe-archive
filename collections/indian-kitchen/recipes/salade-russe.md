## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- salted water
- butter
- beetroot
- truffles
- fresh haricot beans
- asparagus points
- capers
- French pickled gherkins
- anchovies
- olives
- parsley
- shallot
- raw yolks of eggs
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs
- caviare
- prawns
- olives farcies
- pickles
- sweet capsicums

## Directions

1. Boil some carrots and some turnips in salted water with a small piece of butter, but do not let them be overdone.
2. When cold, cut out of them, with a vegetable scoop, a number of pieces the size of an olive.
3. Cut some beetroot in the same way, and likewise some truffles.
4. Take equal parts of each of the above (say a cupful), and a similar quantity of fresh haricot beans ready cooked, and of asparagus points prepared in the same way.
5. Add two table-spoonfuls respectively of capers, of French pickled gherkins cut into the shape of capers, and of anchovies, perfectly cleaned, and cut into small pieces.
6. Add a couple of dozen or more olives stoned, one table-spoonful of parsley minced fine, and one of shallot, also minced.
7. Mix the whole lightly together with a sauce, made with raw yolks of eggs, oil, vinegar, pepper, and salt.
8. Ornament with hard-boiled eggs, caviare, prawns, olives farcies, pickles, truffles, and sweet capsicums.
