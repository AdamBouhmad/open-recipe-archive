## Ingredients

- cold boiled or fried fish
- salt
- pepper
- herbs
- bread-crumbs
- egg
- mustard oil

## Directions

1. Use cold boiled or fried fish which is best adapted for making coftas
2. Follow the instructions as already given at length in the foregoing recipes
3. Use mustard oil which is best adapted for fresh-fish curries
