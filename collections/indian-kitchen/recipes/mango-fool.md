## Ingredients

- six green mangoes
- clean water
- good pure milk

## Directions

1. Remove every particle of the green peel from six green mangoes, cut them into four, and steep them in clean water
2. Throw the stones away
3. Boil the fruit perfectly tender, pulp and pass it through a sieve
4. Sweeten to your taste
5. Add to it very gradually, stirring all the while, as much good pure milk as will reduce it to the consistency of custard
6. Eat on the day it is made
