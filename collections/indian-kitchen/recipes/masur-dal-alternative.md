## Ingredients

- Masur dal 1/2 seer
- ghee 1/4 powa
- curd 1 chhatak
- ginger-juice 1 tola
- pepper 1/4 tola
- cumin 1/4 tola
- turmeric 1/4 tola
- cassia leaves a few
- salt 1 1/2 tola
- cardamom, cinnamon and clove 1/4 tola altogether
- panch-foron 1 pinch
- water 1 seer
- green chillies a few

## Directions

1. Wash the dal and keep them under water for about 20 minutes, and then drain off the water.
2. Heat half the ghee in a pan with the cassia leaves.
3. Add the dal when the ghee is sufficiently hot and stir till they become light brown.
4. Then add hot water with salt and powdered turmeric.
5. Add curd, ginger-juice and powdered pepper and cumin when the pulses are well boiled.
6. The pulses must be thoroughly melted.
7. Take the pan down.
8. Heat the rest of the ghee in another pan with the panch-foron and the green chillies cut lengthwise.
9. Pour in the liquid dal when the ghee is sufficiently hot.
10. Boil for a few minutes more.
11. Add powdered cardamom, cinnamon and clove, and stir before taking off the fire.
12. Keep it covered.
