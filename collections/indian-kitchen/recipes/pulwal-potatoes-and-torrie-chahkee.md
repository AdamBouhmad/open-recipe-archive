## Ingredients

- pulwal
- potatoes
- torrie
- mustard oil
- ground onions
- ground chilies
- ground turmeric
- ground garlic
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Clean as much of the three kinds of vegetables as will overfill a vegetable-dish
2. Make the chahkee in all respects as the seam, potato, and peas chahkee recipe
