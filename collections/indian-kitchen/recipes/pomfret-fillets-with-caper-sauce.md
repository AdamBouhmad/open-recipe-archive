## Ingredients

- pomfret
- onion
- celery
- butter
- flour
- capers
- yolks of two eggs
- melted butter
- aromatic vinegar

## Directions

1. Fillet the pomfret, and make a fish gravy with the bones and trimmings, flavoured with onion and a little celery
2. Simmer your fillets, nicely rolled in little curls, in this gravy
3. Drain them, and dish them on a hot dish
4. Make a sauce blonde with butter and flour, moistened with the liquor from the fish
5. When smooth, add a dessert-spoonful of finely minced capers
6. Off the fire, just as you serve, add the yolks of two eggs, well mixed with some melted butter
7. Pour the sauce over the fillets
8. A few drops of any aromatic vinegar will improve this sauce
