## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- one pound of butter free of water
- twelve ounces of sugar
- four eggs, well beaten
- candied peel
- caraway-seeds
- caraway comfits

## Directions

1. Warm before the fire two pounds of flour and one pound of butter free of water
2. Rub the butter, with twelve ounces of sugar, into the flour with the hand and make it into a stiff paste with four eggs, well beaten
3. Roll out to the required thickness with as little use of the rolling-pin as possible; either take small pieces, and roll them into oblong cakes, or roll out a large piece and cut it into squares or rounds
4. Prick a pattern round the edge of each cake with the back of a knife, and arrange slices of candied peel, caraway-seeds, and caraway comfits in a pattern
5. Bake for about twenty minutes in an oven that is not too quick
6. The mixing of flour, sugar, and butter, and afterwards of the eggs, must be done very thoroughly and smoothly
