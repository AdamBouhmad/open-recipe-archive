## Ingredients

- two parboiled Bombay onions
- an ounce of butter
- a pinch of sugar
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- a table-spoonful of previously boiled rice, or pearl barley
- a breakfast-cupful of broth
- a table-spoonful of finely grated mild cheese (Parmesan for choice)
- half a pint of rich sauce blonde, or boiling cream

## Directions

1. Cut up two parboiled Bombay onions, and put them into a sauce-pan with an ounce of butter, a pinch of sugar, and a salt-spoonful of salt
2. add a table-spoonful of previously boiled rice, or pearl barley, and moisten with a breakfast-cupful of broth
3. let them cook slowly, and when the onions are done, add a table-spoonful of finely grated mild cheese (Parmesan for choice)
4. stir the mixture, pass it through a sieve, and mingle it with half a pint of rich sauce blonde, or with boiling cream
