## Ingredients

- eight calves'-feet
- pepper
- salt
- two onions
- a head of celery
- two carrots
- three or four quarts of water
- a dozen cloves
- the juice and rind of a lemon
- a tablespoonful of soy or any other dark-coloured, rich, and well-flavoured sauce
- the whites of four eggs
- a wineglass of brown sherry
- flannel

## Directions

1. Boil down eight calves'-feet, with some pepper and salt, two onions, a head of celery, and two carrots, in three or four quarts of water
2. When perfectly boiled down strain it lightly without bruising the onions or carrots
3. Let it cool, and remove all the fat
4. With a dozen cloves and the juice and rind of a lemon, boil it again, adding a tablespoonful of soy or any other dark-coloured, rich, and well-flavoured sauce
5. Beat up to a light froth the whites of four eggs, and clear the soup or jelly
6. Add a wineglass of brown sherry, and run or drip it through flannel
7. Pour what you require over the ready cooked or dressed meat into moulds
8. Let the rest cool in some large flat dish, and cut it up small for garnishing the meat or bird when served up
