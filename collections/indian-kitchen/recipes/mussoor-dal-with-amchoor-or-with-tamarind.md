## Ingredients

- mussoor dal
- amchoor or tamarind

## Directions

1. Put the amchoor, or if preferred tamarind, into the pot with the dal
2. Allow it to dissolve
3. When the dal is going through the process of being churned remove the hard stones of the amchoor or seeds of the tamarind
