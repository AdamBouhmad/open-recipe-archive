## Ingredients

- boiled and peeled potatoes
- salt
- powdered pepper
- white of eggs
- ginger-juice
- minced onions
- powdered chillies
- eggs
- pasted grams
- minced meat
- ground almonds
- raisins
- fried and powdered khowa kheer
- ghee
- water
- bread crumbs or powdered biscuits

## Directions

1. Make a paste of some boiled and peeled potatoes and mix thoroughly with salt, powdered pepper and the white of eggs
2. Mix ginger-juice, salt, minced onions, powdered chillies, eggs or a little pasted grams with minced meat
3. Fry the spiced meat with ghee adding a little water to make soft
4. Take a handful of the paste of potatoes, make a flat disc of it with the hands, put some meat at the centre, close the disc over the meat and give the shape of an almond
5. Dip them into beaten eggs, roll on bread crumbs or powdered biscuits and fry in plenty of ghee till deep brown
