## Ingredients

- asparagus
- water
- salt
- bread

## Directions

1. Wash them well, scrape, and tie them up in small bundles
2. Cut them all even at the bottom, and as they are done put them into cold water
3. Put them on in boiling water, with a little salt, and let them boil twenty or twenty-five minutes
4. Take them up, lay them upon a slice of toasted bread cut in four, and the crusts pared off, with the tops meeting in the middle of the dish, and cut off the strings
