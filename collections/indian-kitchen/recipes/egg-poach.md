## Ingredients

- contents of an egg
- ghee or oil
- ground pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Pour the contents of an egg on hot ghee or oil in a pan so that the yolk falls entire and in the centre.
2. Trim the sides, if necessary, round the yolk.
3. Take it out instead of turning over when the lower side is browned.
4. Serve with ground pepper and salt.
5. Alternatively, drop the contents of an egg keeping the yolk entire on boiling water.
6. Tilt the pan to give it a plump appearance.
7. Take the egg out as soon as the white is set.
8. Serve with ground pepper and salt.
