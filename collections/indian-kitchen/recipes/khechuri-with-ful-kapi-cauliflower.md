## Ingredients

- Rice ^ seer
- Fried moog-dal ^ seer
- Potato |- seer or more
- Onion i powa
- Ghee ^ powa
- Salt 2 tolas
- Ginger i tola
- Turmeric ^ tola
- Cumin ^ tola
- Pepper ^ tola
- Garam-masalla ^ tola
- A few cassia leaves
- Red chillies
- Cauliflower
- Entire grams

## Directions

1. Prepare khechuri exactly in the same process as in the case of Golapi-khechuri
2. Only add browned pieces of cauliflowers instead of onions or along with onions
3. Entire grams, after keeping them under water for a few hours, are generally browned and added to the khechuri along with the cauliflowers
