## Ingredients

- ol or mun
- lemon juice
- powdered red chillies
- aniseeds
- salt
- powdered rice or flour

## Directions

1. Peel and dry them in the sun for 2 or 3 days.
2. Boil in water with a little lemon juice.
3. Grind them to a paste.
4. Add powdered red chillies, aniseeds, salt and powdered rice or flour.
5. Beat thoroughly.
6. Form into cakes and fry.
