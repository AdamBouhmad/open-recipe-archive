## Ingredients

- a cupful of moringa or horse-radish root scrapings
- mayonnaise or tartare sauce
- cream

## Directions

1. Rasp the moringa or horse-radish root till you have a cupful of fine scrapings
2. Mingle them with an ordinary mayonnaise or tartare sauce, iced
3. Add cream if desired, or use the usual mixture of eggs, oil, mustard, and vinegar
