## Ingredients

- two eggs
- 1 powa of middlings
- milk 1/2 seer
- sugar 1/2 powa
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat thoroughly the white portions of two eggs.
2. Mix thoroughly with 1 powa of middlings, milk 1/2 seer, sugar 1/2 powa, the yolks of the two eggs and a pinch of salt.
3. Heat on charcoal fire for about an hour.
