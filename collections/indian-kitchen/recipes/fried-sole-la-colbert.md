## Ingredients

- sole
- egg
- fine bread-crumbs
- fat
- fine salt
- maitre d'hotel butter

## Directions

1. Make an incision along the back-bone on the upper side of the sole, from and up to within an inch of the head and tail.
2. Slip a knife under the flesh on each side of the cut, and loosen it from the bones.
3. Egg and bread-crumb the fish with finely sifted crumbs.
4. Fry it in plenty of fat, with the side bearing the incision uppermost.
5. The edges of this will curl outwards in the process of frying, and the opening thus made should be filled at the time of serving with plenty of maitre d'hotel butter.
6. Sprinkle the sole with fine salt.
7. While the fish is being drained in front of the fire, the back-bone may be removed carefully, but this is not essential.
