## Ingredients

- 6 boiled potatoes
- 1 chittack or 2 ounces of butter free of water
- 2 white Patna onions
- finely chopped suet
- finely-grated crumbs of a slice of stale bread
- 1 teaspoonful of ground pepper
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- soup herbs
- 1 dessertspoonful of tomato or tapp sauce
- stock or gravy of the giblets

## Directions

1. Break up, but not mash, six boiled potatoes with one chittack or two ounces of butter free of water
2. Cut into fine slices two white Patna onions
3. Take a little finely chopped suet, finely-grated crumbs of a slice of stale bread, a teaspoonful of ground pepper, a teaspoonful of salt, all kinds of soup herbs, and a dessertspoonful of tomato or tapp sauce
4. Add a little of the stock or gravy of the giblets, &c., and stuff the pig, kid, or bird
