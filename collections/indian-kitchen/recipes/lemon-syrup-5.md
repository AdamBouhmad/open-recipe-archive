## Ingredients

- 1 chhatak of lemon skin
- 9 chhataks of lemon juice
- 1 seer of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 1 chhatak of lemon skin in 9 chhataks of lemon juice on mild heat and throw them away when they are fully boiled
2. Add 1 seer of sugar
3. Boil a few minutes more after the sugar is dissolved and take down when a little gluey
4. Strain the syrup through a fine strainer and bottle it
