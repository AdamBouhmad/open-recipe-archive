## Ingredients

- six or eight good Patna onions
- two chittacks or four ounces of butter, free of water
- a dessertspoonful of finely-sliced curry onions
- a tablespoonful of finely-sifted flour
- half a seer of milk
- a teaspoonful of salt
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and boil six or eight good Patna onions
2. Allow the water to drain away
3. Fry to a light brown colour, in two chittacks or four ounces of butter, free of water, a dessertspoonful of finely-sliced curry onions
4. Gradually mix into it a tablespoonful of finely-sifted flour and half a seer of milk, taking care through the whole operation to keep stirring the sauce to prevent its lumping
5. Add a teaspoonful of salt and a quarter of a teaspoonful of pepper
6. Last of all add the boiled onions, and in a few minutes the sauce will be ready
