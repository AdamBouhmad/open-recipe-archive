## Ingredients

- chicken
- water 1.5 seer
- salt
- pieces of onions
- powdered turmeric
- pepper
- powdered arrowroot
- cassia leaves
- cloves
- aniseeds
- ghee

## Directions

1. Kill and prepare a chicken and cut in 4 or 5 pieces.
2. Boil the pieces in about 1.5 seer of water with salt, a few pieces of onions, a little powdered turmeric and pepper under cover.
3. If the juice is to be made thick add a little powdered arrowroot.
4. Take down when the soup is only 1.5 powa and strain it out.
5. Fry a few cassia leaves, cloves and aniseeds in a little ghee, and add the soup.
6. Take down when it boils.
7. Strain the soup out through a clean piece of linen.
8. This soup is given to patients on medical advice.
9. The fowl may be utilised in preparing chops and cutlets.
