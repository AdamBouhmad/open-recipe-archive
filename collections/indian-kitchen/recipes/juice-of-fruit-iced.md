## Ingredients

- the juice of a pint of currants or raspberries, or other fruit preserved for tarts
- four or five ounces of pounded loaf sugar
- a little lemon-juice
- a pint of cream

## Directions

1. Press through a sieve the juice of a pint of currants or raspberries, or other fruit preserved for tarts
2. add to it four or five ounces of pounded loaf sugar, a little lemon-juice, and a pint of cream
3. It may be whisked previous to freezing, and a mixture of the juice in which the fruit was preserved may be used
