## Ingredients

- spinach puree
- cream
- nutmeg
- puff pastry
- buttered egg

## Directions

1. Prepare a puree of spinach as described at page 146, drain it very dry, and then moisten it with cream, adding a very little nutmeg
2. Make eight bouchees (miniature oyster patty shapes) of puff pastry
3. Fill each bouchee with spinach puree
4. Give them a cap of buttered egg
5. Heat up in the oven, and serve on a napkin
