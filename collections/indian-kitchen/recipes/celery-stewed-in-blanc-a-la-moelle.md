## Ingredients

- celery
- blanc
- beef marrow
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Stew celery in blanc as described
2. Pile the celery in the centre of the dish
3. Thicken the blanc in which it was cooked, and pour it over the pile
4. Break the bone, take out the raw marrow, cut it into dice
5. Blanch them for five minutes in scalding water, drain them, and heat them up gently in a little of the sauce en bain-marie
6. Put a dessert-spoonful each of this into little croustades of fried bread, and serve round the celery as a garnish
7. Cut your croustades square, out of stale bread, and hollow a little space in each to hold the marrow
