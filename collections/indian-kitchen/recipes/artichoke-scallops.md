## Ingredients

- globe artichokes
- silver plated coquille shells
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- thick cream
- very finely sifted bread-crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil the artichokes
2. When cold, strip them of their leaves, scraping off all the pulp which adheres to them with a silver dessert-knife
3. Extract the chokes, and add the bottoms of the artichokes to the leaf pulp
4. Mash the whole together with a silver fork, dust it with salt and pepper
5. Mix a coffee-cupful of thick cream with it, stir it well
6. Butter the coquille shells and fill them with the artichoke puree
7. Dust over the surface a layer of very finely sifted bread-crumbs
8. Sprinkle some little bits of butter over the crumbs
9. Bake till thoroughly hot, brown the surface with a hot iron, and serve on a napkin
