## Ingredients

- yolks of three eggs
- two table-spoonfuls of brandy
- one table-spoonful of the best salad oil
- four or five table-spoonfuls of cold water
- three table-spoonfuls of flour
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Beat up the yolks of three eggs with two table-spoonfuls of brandy, one table-spoonful of the best salad oil, and four or five table-spoonfuls of cold water.
2. Incorporate with this mixture three table-spoonfuls of flour and a salt-spoonful of salt.
3. Work this, with care, to a smooth paste, and continue to beat it for at least ten minutes.
4. If the batter appear too thick, add a little water until its consistency be satisfactory, i.e.: it should cover the spoon when lifted out of it with a coating about the eighth of an inch thick.
5. Take the whites of the three eggs, and whip them to a stiff froth.
6. Stir this into your batter at the time of using.
