## Ingredients

- veal
- ham
- bread-crumb sippets
- butter
- mashed potatoes
- gravy

## Directions

1. Make a good rich veal mince, mixed with a little ham, and some sippets of bread-crumb cut into small squares, diamonds, &c., and fried in butter
2. Line the pie-dish with mashed potatoes as above directed
3. Fill into it the veal mince, with plenty of gravy
4. Arrange the sippets, cover over with a thick crust of the mashed potatoes, and bake for a quarter of an hour
