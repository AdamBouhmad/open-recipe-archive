## Ingredients

- an ounce and a half of spermaceti
- nine drachms of white wax
- twelve ounces of oil of sweet almonds
- two ounces of alkanet-root
- one drachm of otto of roses

## Directions

1. Digest the first four ingredients with the heat of boiling water for four hours
2. Strain through flannel
3. Add the otto of roses
