## Ingredients

- two ounces and a half of butter
- two ounces of flour
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- a pint of broth, or milk and water
- a pinch of sugar

## Directions

1. Melt your lump of butter at the bottom of your sauce-pan
2. Add the flour, which soon amalgamates with the melted butter
3. By degrees add the water, or milk and water (boiling) with a pinch of salt
4. Work this well with a wooden spoon till it is soft and creamy to look upon
5. Pass it through your tin strainer into a hot sauce boat
6. As you serve it, add a pat of fresh butter the size of a rupee, which will melt of its own accord
