## Ingredients

- Guinea-fowl
- thin slices of bacon
- water-cresses
- bread sauce
- fried bread crumbs
- brown gravy
- liver
- half a glass of red wine
- 1 tea-spoonful of red-currant jelly
- juice of a lime

## Directions

1. Pluck, singe, draw and truss the bird for roasting
2. Do not allow the cook to put the liver and gizzard under the wings
3. Tie a few thin slices of bacon over the breast
4. Put it to roast over a very clear fire
5. Baste frequently
6. When done, put it on a dish garnished with water-cresses
7. Send round the best bread sauce, fried bread crumbs, and a rich brown gravy containing the liver pounded, half a glass of red wine, a tea-spoonful of red-currant jelly, and the juice of a lime
8. Do not stuff a Guinea-fowl like a capon
9. Serve with pommes de terre à la maître d'hôtel, and haricots verts panaches
