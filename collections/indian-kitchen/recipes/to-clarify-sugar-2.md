## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of loaf sugar
- 1 beaten white of egg
- 1 pint and a half of water

## Directions

1. To every three pounds of loaf sugar allow the beaten white of one egg and a pint and a half of water
2. Break the sugar small and put it into a nicely cleaned brass pan
3. Pour the water over it and let it stand some time before it be put upon the fire
4. Add the beaten white of the egg and stir till the sugar be entirely dissolved
5. When it boils up pour in a quarter of a pint of cold water
6. Let it boil up a second time
7. Remove it from the fire and let it settle for fifteen minutes
8. Carefully take off all the scum
9. Put it again on the fire and boil till sufficiently thick or till candy high
10. To ascertain this, drop a little from a spoon into a cup of cold water, and if it become quite hard it is ready
