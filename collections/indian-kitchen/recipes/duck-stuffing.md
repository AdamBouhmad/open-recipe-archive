## Ingredients

- three Bombay onions the size of Badminton balls
- eight tender looking sage leaves
- five ounces of bread-crumbs
- spiced pepper
- salt
- one ounce of butter or suet
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Take three Bombay onions the size of Badminton balls, wash, peel, and boil them in two waters to extract the acrid flavour
2. Whilst these are boiling, take eight tender looking sage leaves, and scald them in boiling water for five minutes
3. Take them out, and when the onions are tender, turn them out and drain them dry
4. Mince them with the sage leaves, very fine
5. Add to this, five ounces of bread-crumbs
6. Dust over the mixture a liberal allowance of spiced pepper and salt
7. When nicely worked together, add an ounce of butter or suet cut into dice
8. Bind the ingredients with three eggs, it will now be ready for use
