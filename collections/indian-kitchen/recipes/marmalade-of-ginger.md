## Ingredients

- 1 seer of ginger
- 1 chhatak of pasted leaves of Indian black berry (kala jam)
- 2 seers of water
- 1 seer of sugar
- powders of cardamoms, cinnamon and camphor - 1 pinch each
- 5 or 6 drops of essence of rose

## Directions

1. Peel 1 seer of ginger and perforate them all over with an iron pin.
2. Keep them immersed in lime water for 3 days and then wash them with plain water.
3. Boil them with 1 chhatak of pasted leaves of Indian black berry (kala jam) in about 2 seers of water till fully boiled.
4. Wash them 3 or 4 times with plain water and drop them in a boiling syrup of 1 seer of sugar.
5. Mix powders of cardamoms, cinnamon and camphor 1 pinch each and 5 or 6 drops of essence of rose with the mass when the syrup is very thick.
6. Then take down, serve one small piece to each when cold and preserve in bottles.
