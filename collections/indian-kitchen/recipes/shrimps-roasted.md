## Ingredients

- a few big sized shrimps
- salt
- mustard oil
- powdered turmeric
- chillies
- minced green chillies
- onions

## Directions

1. Scale a few big sized shrimps
2. Smear them with salt, mustard oil, powdered turmeric and chillies
3. Fix them to an iron stick
4. Hold on fire and heat all the sides till deep brown
5. Drop oil from time to time on the fishes
6. Alternative method: Scorch the shrimps on charcoal fire
7. Scale them and wash
8. Smear them with minced green chillies, onions, mustard oil and salt
9. Serve
