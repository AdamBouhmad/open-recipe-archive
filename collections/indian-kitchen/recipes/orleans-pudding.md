## Ingredients

- ounce of gelatine
- cold water
- ten egg yolks
- three-quarters of a pound of sugar
- quart of boiled milk
- ounce of candied orange peel
- ounce of citron
- ounce of raisins
- ounce of currants
- small tumbler of rum
- crushed ratafias
- ice

## Directions

1. Steep an ounce of gelatine in cold water
2. Make a rich custard with ten yolks of eggs, three-quarters of a pound of sugar, and a quart of boiled milk
3. Add the gelatine to the custard while the latter is hot, and stir it until it is dissolved
4. Strain it into a bowl
5. Cut up one ounce of candied orange peel, one ounce of citron, one ounce of raisins, and one ounce of currants, wash them well, dry them
6. Toss the minced confitures in a frying-pan with a small tumbler of rum
7. As soon as the rum is absorbed, stop and take the pan off the fire
8. Put a mould upon ice, pour a layer of custard into it first with some of the minced fruit
9. When set, put a layer of crushed ratafias, then another layer of custard with fruit, again crushed ratafias, and so on till the mould is filled
10. Cover it in ice and let it rest for an hour
11. Turn it out and serve
