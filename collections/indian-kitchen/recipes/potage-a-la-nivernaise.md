## Ingredients

- soup meat for stock
- four good-sized carrots
- one table-spoonful of butter
- a salt-spoonful of powdered sugar
- pepper
- salt
- stock

## Directions

1. Set your soup meat for stock as usual for a clear soup
2. Take four good-sized carrots, wash them well
3. Cut out of them a number of thickish pieces the size and shape of a pigeon's egg, or a little smaller
4. When you have cut a couple of dozen such pieces, throw all the scraps and trimmings of the carrots into the stock pot to flavour the soup well
5. Shortly before serving time, melt a table-spoonful of butter in a frying-pan
6. Put into it the carrot pieces, which should have been all but boiled till soft in the soup
7. Toss them about over the fire adding a salt-spoonful of powdered sugar with a dusting of pepper and of salt
8. Moisten the pan with a spoonful of stock as the butter is absorbed
9. When done, the pieces of carrot will be almost glazed
10. Put the glazed carrots into the tureen and pour the soup over them
