## Ingredients

- 1/2 powa of ghee
- a few cassia leaves
- 1 powa of middlings
- 1/2 powa of raisins
- 1 powa of sugar
- 1 1/2 powa of water
- almonds
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Heat 1/2 powa of ghee with a few cassia leaves and when the bubbles die out fry 1 powa of middlings and 1/2 powa of raisins with the ghee till brown.
2. Then add 1 powa of sugar and stirring for about a minute pour in 1 1/2 powa of water.
3. Add pieces of almonds and pistachios if desired.
4. Stir constantly and take down when thick.
