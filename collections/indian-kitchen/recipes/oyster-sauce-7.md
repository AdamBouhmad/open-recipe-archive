## Ingredients

- oysters
- flour
- butter
- lemon-juice
- three tablespoonfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Beard and scald the oysters
2. Strain the liquor, and thicken it with a little flour and butter
3. Squeeze in a little lemon-juice, and add three tablespoonfuls of cream
4. Heat it well, but do not let it boil
