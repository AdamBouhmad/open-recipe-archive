## Ingredients

- fillet
- minced anchovies
- olives
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Lard the fillet carefully, and tie it up into a convenient shape for roasting
2. Roast it over a clear fire, and when sufficiently done, serve it with some minced anchovies and olives, tossed in melted butter, poured over it at the last moment
