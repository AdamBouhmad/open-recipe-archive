## Ingredients

- 1/2 powa of ghee
- salt
- soda
- black cumins
- 1 1/2 seer of fine flour
- water

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly 1/2 powa of ghee, a little salt, a half-pinch of soda, and a few entire black cumins with 1 1/2 seer of fine flour
2. Knead into a stiff dough with water
3. Make small balls of it and roll them into very thin discs
4. Fold the discs twice and fry in plenty of ghee till brown
5. Or make big balls, roll into big discs, cut them into pieces of parallelogram shape and fry
