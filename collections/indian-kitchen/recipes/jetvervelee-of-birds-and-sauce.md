## Ingredients

- eight fat quails
- four sheep's tongues
- half a pound of lamb's liver
- couple of ounces of gravy meat
- bacon
- spiced pepper
- onion

## Directions

1. Buy eight fat quails, four sheep's tongues, half a pound of lamb's liver, and a couple of ounces of gravy meat
2. Stew the tongues
3. Bone the quails and throw the bones into the gravy with all scraps of bacon
4. Trim your cold sheep's tongues and cut eight nice fillets the size of a walnut from the centre of them
5. Place your boned-birds breast downwards on a board and dust them over with spiced pepper
6. Melt a couple of thick slices of fat bacon in a frying-pan
7. When melted, throw in three table-spoonfuls of the lamb's liver and those of the birds too, cut into dice, and one onion shredded finely
8. Cook the liver in this until done
