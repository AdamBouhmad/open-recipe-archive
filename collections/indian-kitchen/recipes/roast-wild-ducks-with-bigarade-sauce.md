## Ingredients

- wild ducks
- two oranges (sweet limes)
- an ounce of butter
- a dessert-spoonful of flour
- a breakfast-cupful of common stock
- pepper
- salt
- juice of the oranges
- a pinch of sifted sugar
- a tablespoonful of Marsala
- a tea-spoonful of chilli vinegar

## Directions

1. Roast the wild ducks
2. Pare as thin as possible the rind of two oranges
3. Cut them into thin shreds and boil them in water for five minutes, then drain
4. Melt an ounce of butter in a sauce-pan
5. Stir into it a dessert-spoonful of flour and a breakfast-cupful of common stock made with scraps and the giblets of the ducks
6. Add pepper, salt, and the juice of the oranges, with a pinch of sifted sugar, a tablespoonful of Marsala, and a tea-spoonful of chilli vinegar
7. Add the boiled rinds, stir till the sauce boils, and serve in a boat
