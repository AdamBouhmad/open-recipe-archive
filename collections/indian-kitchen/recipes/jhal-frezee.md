## Ingredients

- cold mutton, beef, or veal
- a large quantity of sliced onions
- some chilies
- a teaspoonful of salt
- 2 ounces ghee

## Directions

1. Cut up into small squares, of less than an inch, either cold mutton, beef, or veal, rejecting the bones.
2. Add a large quantity of sliced onions, some chilies cut up, and a teaspoonful of salt.
3. Warm 2 ounces of ghee, and throw into it the meat, onions, chilies, and salt, and allow to simmer, or fry, stirring the whole while, until the onions are quite tender.
