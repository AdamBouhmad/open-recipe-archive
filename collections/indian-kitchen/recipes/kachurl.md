## Ingredients

- pulses of grams
- ghee
- entire aniseeds
- black cumin
- salt
- fish
- meat
- green peas
- almonds
- spices
- soda
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Keep some pulses of grams moist under water for two hours
2. Fry them with ghee and grind to a paste
3. Mix entire aniseeds, black cumin and salt with it
4. Fish, meat, green peas, almonds, fried with spices may also be mixed with it
5. Mix a pinch of soda, one of salt, and ghee with flour
6. Knead it to a dough with water
7. Make small balls of it
8. Press each ball with the fingers and stuff with a little of the pulse preparation
9. Make balls again
10. Press these stuffed balls into flat discs of moderate thickness
11. Fry in plenty of boiling ghee
