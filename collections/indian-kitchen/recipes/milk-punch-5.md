## Ingredients

- six quarts of rum
- one quart of brandy
- one quart of lime-juice
- two seers of soft sugar
- three quarts of cold water
- two seers of pure milk
- the rinds of forty limes
- three nutmegs

## Directions

1. Steep for two days in a bottle of the rum the peels of the forty limes
2. boil in the three quarts of water the two seers of soft sugar, and grate in the nutmeg
3. pour all the rum and syrup into a large vessel
4. add gradually the quart of lime-juice and two seers of milk, boiling hot, stirring the whole time
5. let it stand for an hour or two
6. strain through flannel several times until it drips clear
7. bottle
