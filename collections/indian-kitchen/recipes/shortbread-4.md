## Ingredients

- two pounds of sifted flour
- one pound of butter
- a quarter of a pound of candied orange and lemon peel
- a quarter of a pound of pounded loaf sugar
- a quarter of a pound of blanched sweet almonds
- a quarter of a pound of caraway comfits

## Directions

1. Cut the lemon, the orange-peel, and almonds into small thin bits, and mix them with a pound and a half of the flour, a few of the caraway comfits, and the sugar
2. Melt the butter, and when cool, pour it into the flour, at the same time mixing it quickly with the hands
3. Form it into a large round nearly an inch thick, using the remainder of the flour to make it up with
4. Cut it into four, and with the finger and thumb pinch each bit neatly all round the edge
5. Prick them with a fork, and strew the rest of the caraway comfits over the top
6. Put the pieces upon white paper dusted with flour, and then upon tins
7. Bake them in a moderate oven
