## Ingredients

- one teaspoonful Robinson's patent barley
- one tablespoonful cold spring water
- one quart boiling water

## Directions

1. Mix smoothly the barley and cold spring water into a smooth paste
2. Gradually add one quart of boiling water
3. Boil it gently for ten minutes, stirring constantly
4. Strain when cold
