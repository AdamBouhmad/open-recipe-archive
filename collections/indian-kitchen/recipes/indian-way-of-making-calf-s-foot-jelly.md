## Ingredients

- twelve large or full-sized calves'-feet
- one pound or half a seer of sugar
- eight limes
- two oranges
- half a dozen blades of lemon-grass
- one tablespoonful of mixed spices
- six eggs
- a handful of isinglass
- a claretglassful of sherry

## Directions

1. Thoroughly wash the feet, break them up and boil them
2. Allow all the meat to dissolve over a slow fire
3. Skim away every particle of fat
4. Strain the liquid through a coarse napkin
5. Add the sugar, all the hot spices, and the rinds of two lemons and one orange
6. Simmer the whole for some time
7. Squeeze in the juice of the eight limes and the two oranges, together with the isinglass and lemon-grass
8. When it begins to thicken strain it
9. Reboil until it is reduced to the required quantity, skimming all the fat
10. Beat the whites of the six eggs to a good light froth
11. Add this to the jelly, and pour it from one pan into another several times, until it clears
12. Add the sherry and strain it through flannel, returning it quickly two or three times until it runs perfectly bright and clear
13. Fill into glasses or moulds before it congeals
