## Ingredients

- hen turkey
- turkey stuffing
- bacon sliced
- sheep's trotters
- Bombay onion sliced
- carrots
- chopped sweet herbs
- whole pepper corns
- lime peel
- clove of garlic
- gravy made from giblets, trimmings, and scraps
- wine-glassful of Madeira
- minced truffles
- potatoes
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Truss the bird nicely
2. Lard its breast
3. Stuff it with very carefully made turkey stuffing
4. Place it in a deep stew-pan upon a bed of sliced bacon
5. Put a couple of sheep's trotters, cut into small pieces, round it, with a sliced Bombay onion, two carrots, some chopped sweet herbs, whole pepper corns, lime peel, and a clove of garlic
6. Pour into the pan a good pint of gravy made from the giblets, trimmings, and any scraps, with a wine-glassful of Madeira
7. Cook the bird gently with live coals on the stew-pan lid, as well as under the vessel, for about three hours
8. Brown the larding on the breast by passing a red hot iron close to it
9. Strain the gravy, thicken it, add some minced truffles, and send it round in a boat
10. Serve with potatoes and cauliflower accompanying
