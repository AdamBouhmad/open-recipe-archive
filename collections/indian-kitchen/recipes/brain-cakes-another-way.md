## Ingredients

- brains
- salt
- nutmeg
- pepper
- raw egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Take the brains and remove any veins
2. Chop well with a knife
3. Add salt, nutmeg, and pepper
4. Add a little raw egg and flour enough to make them stick together
5. Mix well
6. Make into cakes about the size of the top of liqueur glasses
7. Fry them brown on both sides
