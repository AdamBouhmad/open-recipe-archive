## Ingredients

- soup meat
- six or eight good sized tomatoes
- a tea-spoonful of dried basil
- an onion
- a tea-spoonful of salt
- two cloves of garlic
- a table-spoonful of butter
- an ounce of butter
- a dessert-spoonful of flour
- croutons of fried bread

## Directions

1. Soup meat as usual for two
2. Make your stock from the soup meat
3. Choose an enamel-lined sauce-pan and throw in the tomatoes cut into quarters, dried basil, shredded onion, salt, garlic cloves, and a table-spoonful of butter
4. Cook the pieces of tomato until quite soft, stirring to prevent catching
5. Turn them out upon a sieve, pick out the garlic, and work the tomatoes through the sieve, saving the pulp in a bowl
6. When dinner time is at hand, melt an ounce of butter at the bottom of a sauce-pan
7. Work a dessert-spoonful of flour into it till smooth
8. Add a little stock and a little puree by degrees till exhausted
9. Let the soup come to the boil to thicken properly
10. Serve with croutons of fried bread
