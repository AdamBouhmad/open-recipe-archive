## Ingredients

- pasted pulses of peas or grams
- tender leaves of amrita mun
- ghee
- oil
- cassia leaves
- water
- coriander
- ginger
- pepper
- cumin
- turmeric
- salt
- garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Make a thick solution of pasted pulses of peas or grams and dip pieces, 1 or 1.5 inch square, of very tender leaves of amrita mun into it, and fry them in ghee or oil.
2. Boil some ghee with a few cassia leaves and pour in water with powdered coriander, ginger, pepper, cumin and turmeric.
3. When it boils add the fried pieces and salt.
4. Take down after adding powdered garam-masalla.
