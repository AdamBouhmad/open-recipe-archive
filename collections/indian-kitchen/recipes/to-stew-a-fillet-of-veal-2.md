## Ingredients

- fillet of veal
- two quarts of white stock
- one teaspoonful of lemon pickle
- one teaspoonful of mushroom catsup
- butter
- flour
- cayenne
- salt
- pickled mushrooms
- forcemeat balls
- lemon

## Directions

1. Bone, lard, and stuff a fillet of veal.
2. Half roast, and then stew it with two quarts of white stock, a teaspoonful of lemon pickle, and one of mushroom catsup.
3. Before serving, strain the gravy.
4. Thicken it with butter rolled in flour.
5. Add a little cayenne, salt, and some pickled mushrooms.
6. Heat it, and pour it over the veal.
7. Have ready two or three dozens of forcemeat balls to put round it and upon the top.
8. Garnish with cut lemon.
