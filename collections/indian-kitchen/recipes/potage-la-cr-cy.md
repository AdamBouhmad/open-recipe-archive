## Ingredients

- carrots
- stock made from bones, meat, scraps (bones of ham and bacon especially valuable)
- dried sweet herbs
- onion
- parsley
- carrot
- celery
- salt and pepper to taste
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil as many carrots as you think will suffice for the quantity of soup you have to make in the stock
2. When thoroughly done, drain them, and pass them through the sieve
3. Mix the pulp so obtained with sufficient of the stock to make a puree a little thinner than you wish your soup eventually to be
4. Melt a piece of butter at the bottom of a sauce-pan, and work a little flour into it
5. Gradually add the puree, and stir without ceasing till the soup comes to the boil
6. When it will be found of the proper consistency, skim if necessary, and serve
