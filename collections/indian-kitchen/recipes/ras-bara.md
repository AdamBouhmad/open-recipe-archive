## Ingredients

- almonds
- raisins
- cardamom seeds
- aniseeds
- bread crumbs
- contents of a few eggs
- ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Mix almonds, raisins, cardamom seeds, aniseeds and bread crumbs, all powdered or pasted, with the contents of a few eggs and beat thoroughly.
2. Form balls, fry in plenty of ghee and drop them into a syrup of sugar.
3. Keep them there for half an hour or so till the syrup reaches its centre.
4. Then serve.
