## Ingredients

- four tablespoonfuls of finely-sifted ground rice-flour
- cold milk
- a quart of boiling milk
- one-eighth of an ounce of isinglass
- a stick of cinnamon
- the peel of half a small lemon
- sugar to taste
- a tablespoonful of pounded almonds

## Directions

1. Mix to a stiff smooth paste four tablespoonfuls of finely-sifted ground rice-flour with a little cold milk
2. Stir it into a quart of boiling milk in which had been dissolved one-eighth of an ounce of isinglass, a stick of cinnamon, and the peel of half a small lemon
3. Sweeten to taste
4. Boil from ten to fifteen minutes, stirring carefully all the while
5. Remove from the fire and mix into it briskly a tablespoonful of pounded almonds
6. Pour it while scalding hot into moulds previously dipped in cold water
