## Ingredients

- prawns
- butter
- cayenne pepper
- powdered mace
- iced butter

## Directions

1. Boil the prawns, clean them carefully, pound them to a paste in your mortar, mixing a little butter with them to assist the operation.
2. Melt some butter in a sauce-pan, mix your pounded prawn meat in it, and as soon as it appears to have absorbed the butter (having been well stirred during the process and flavoured with a very little cayenne pepper, and powdered mace), take it out.
3. Let it get cold and mix it with your iced butter in the proportion of about half and half.
4. The pounding must be thorough; there should be no granulated particles of prawn meat in the butter.
