## Ingredients

- 1 seer of very ripe blackberries
- 1 seer of water
- salt
- 1 or 1 1/2 powa of sugar
- fragments of ice

## Directions

1. Make a paste of the soft pulp of 1 seer of very ripe blackberries and strain it through a clean piece of linen throwing away the seeds
2. Mix it with 1 seer of water, a little salt, 1 or 1 1/2 powa of sugar and fragments of ice as required
