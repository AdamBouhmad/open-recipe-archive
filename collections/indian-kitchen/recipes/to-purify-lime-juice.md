## Ingredients

- 1 quart of strained lime-juice
- 1 ounce of well-burnt and finely pounded animal charcoal

## Directions

1. Add an ounce of well-burnt and finely pounded animal charcoal to a quart of strained lime-juice
2. In twelve hours filter it through blotting-paper
3. Put it into small phials
4. Cork these tightly and keep them in a cool place
