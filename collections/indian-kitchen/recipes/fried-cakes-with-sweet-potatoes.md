## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- powdered rice or flour
- salt
- sugar
- powdered black-cumin
- aniseed
- oil
- khowa kheer

## Directions

1. Boil them in water, peel and rub into a paste
2. Add some powdered rice or flour, salt, sugar and powdered black-cumin and aniseed, and mix thoroughly
3. Form cakes and fry in oil
4. A little khowa kheer mixed with sugar may be put inside each cake
