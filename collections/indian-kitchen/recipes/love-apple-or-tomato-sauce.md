## Ingredients

- five hundred tomatoes
- two pounds of green ginger, ground fine
- a pound and a half of garlic, ground fine
- one pound of chilies, ground fine
- one pound of salt
- three pounds of tamarinds
- three quarts of vinegar

## Directions

1. Steep the tamarinds for twelve hours in a quart of the vinegar
2. Strain them through a sieve, rejecting the stones, and add the other two quarts of vinegar, all the ground condiments, and salt
3. Break the tomatoes into the mixture, and boil the whole, stirring it all the time until it thickens
4. Remove it from the fire, and when cold strain it carefully and bottle the liquid, which is the sauce
