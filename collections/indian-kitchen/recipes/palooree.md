## Ingredients

- one seer of finely-sifted flour of the chunna ka dal, which has been previously parched
- six large Patna onions
- eight fresh green chilies
- a tablespoonful each of finely-chopped soa mattee, saug, and parsley
- a dessertspoonful of salt
- a teaspoonful of finely-ground green ginger
- water

## Directions

1. Take of the finely-sifted flour of the chunna ka dal, which has been previously parched, one seer
2. Slice and chop six large Patna onions finely
3. Slice very fine eight fresh green chilies
4. Prepare a tablespoonful each of finely-chopped soa mattee, saug, and parsley
5. Prepare a dessertspoonful of salt and a teaspoonful of finely-ground green ginger
6. Put the seer of dal-flour into a large deep pan, and mix into it all the above condiments
7. Keep adding to it water, very gradually and in small quantities at a time, mixing it briskly the whole while
