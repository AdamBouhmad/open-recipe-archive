## Ingredients

- pieces of boiled fish
- boiled and minced potatoes
- salt
- powdered almonds
- pepper
- butter
- eggs
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from pieces of boiled fish and mix with a third of its quantity of boiled and minced potatoes.
2. Add adequate quantities of salt, powdered almonds, pepper.
3. Add butter and eggs.
4. Beat thoroughly.
5. Form cakes, roll on bread crumbs and fry with butter.
6. Serve with lemon-juice.
