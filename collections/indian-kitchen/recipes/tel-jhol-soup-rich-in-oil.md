## Ingredients

- fish
- oil
- coriander
- chillies
- turmeric
- cumin
- pepper
- onions
- cassia leaves
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Brown some big pieces of fish in oil
2. Boil sufficient oil in a new pot and add coriander, chillies, turmeric, cumin, pepper and onions, all pasted together, and cassia leaves
3. Stir for about 5 minutes
4. Add a quantity of water, fish and salt
5. Take down when fishes are boiled and the soup is moderately thick
