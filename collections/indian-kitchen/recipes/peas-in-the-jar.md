## Ingredients

- shelled peas
- tinned butter
- salt
- powdered sugar
- mint leaves
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Shell half a pint of peas
2. Put them into a two-pound jam jar with a screw lid or a block tin can with a closely fitting top
3. Put in with them a table-spoonful of tinned butter, a salt-spoonful of salt, and a tea-spoonful of powdered sugar
4. Add a dozen mint leaves and a very little black pepper
5. Cover the vessel down tightly
6. Immerse it in a stew-pan or bain-marie half full of boiling water
7. Set the latter on the fire and boil briskly
8. Examine the peas in half an hour by which time, if very young, they should be done
