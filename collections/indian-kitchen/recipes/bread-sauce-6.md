## Ingredients

- a pint of water
- the crumb of a French roll or of a slice of bread
- a minced onion
- whole pepper
- a gill of cream

## Directions

1. Boil in a pint of water the crumb of a French roll or of a slice of bread, a minced onion, and some whole pepper
2. When the onion is tender drain off the water, pick out the peppercorns, and rub the bread through a sieve
3. Put it into a saucepan, with a gill of cream
