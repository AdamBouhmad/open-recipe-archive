## Ingredients

- fish, half seer
- potato, one and a half powa
- milk, one chhatak
- butter, one chhatak
- 3 or 4 hen's eggs
- flour, about one powa
- salt, one tola
- syrup of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil pieces of a big fish and remove the bones.
2. Add boiled and pasted potatoes, then all the other ingredients and lastly flour little by little while beating the mass thoroughly.
3. Form small balls and fry in ghee.
4. Serve while hot or prepare as above with only half the amount of salt and keep the fried balls immersed in a syrup of sugar till they are saturated, then serve.
