## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 powa of flour
- 1 powa of bananas
- 1 powa of milk
- 1/2 powa of sugar
- cardamom seeds
- ghee

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly flour or powdered rice, ripe bananas, milk, sugar and a pinch of powdered cardamom seeds to a thick paste
2. Heat a little ghee or oil in a round bottomed pan
3. Add 2 or 3 table-spoonfuls of the paste and cover the pan up completely
4. Remove the cakes when they are solid and boiled after about 1 or 1 1/2 minutes
5. Continue in this way
