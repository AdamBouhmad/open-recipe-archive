## Ingredients

- partridges
- Bombay onion
- milk
- spiced pepper
- salt
- boiled bacon
- giblets of the birds
- scraps
- slice of lean bacon
- onion
- pepper corns
- four large Bombay onions
- melted butter
- cream
- curls of crisply fried bacon

## Directions

1. Prepare partridges as for roasting
2. Fill each partridge with a chopped Bombay onion previously boiled in milk seasoned with spiced pepper, salt, and rolled in a slice of boiled bacon
3. Make a broth with the giblets of the birds, any scraps you may have, a slice of lean bacon, an onion cut into quarters, a few pepper corns, and a seasoning of salt and pepper
4. Simmer the partridges in the broth until perfectly tender, about three-quarters of an hour
5. Take them out and drain them, replacing them in the hot pan in which they were done, with the cover on
6. Strain the liquor in which the birds have been cooked
7. Simmer four large Bombay onions in milk till tender, drain them, chop them up very fine, and pass them through the sieve
8. Make a rich puree with melted butter and the stock aforesaid
9. When boiling hot, dish the birds and pour the onion puree over them
10. Serve garnished with curls of crisply fried bacon, with a spoonful of cream added if possible
