## Ingredients

- clear, well-flavoured consomme for eight
- small tin of asparagus

## Directions

1. Open the tin of asparagus and place the pieces on a dish, draining all the liquor from the tin
2. Choose American asparagus if you can, because it is so much greener than either the French or English
3. When drained, cut the tender ends of the asparagus into pieces half an inch long using a dessert-knife
4. Put them away carefully, and throw the tough ends into the consomme
5. When strained and ready to serve, add to the soup the chopped green points
6. Beat it up to concert pitch, and serve
