## Ingredients

- 1/2 powa of pistachios
- 1/2 powa of almonds
- 1 seer of fresh unboiled milk
- 1 powa of water
- 1 or 2 table-spoonfuls of rose water
- 1/2 seer of sugar
- fragments of ice

## Directions

1. Peel pistachios and almonds 1/2 powa each after keeping them moistened in water for a few hours
2. Cut them in pieces and make a paste of them
3. Mix the paste with fresh unboiled milk 1 seer, water 1 powa, 1 or 2 table-spoonfuls of rose water, sugar 1/2 seer and fragments of ice as required
