## Ingredients

- khowa kheer 1 seer
- white sugar 1 1/2 seer
- powdered cardamom seeds
- pieces of almonds and pistachios
- 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose

## Directions

1. Heat khowa kheer and white sugar in a brass pot
2. Stir continuously and vigorously till perfectly blended together
3. Mix with powdered cardamom seeds, pieces of almonds and pistachios and 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose
4. Take down from heat
5. Make balls of it
6. Give a flat semi-circular or any other form to them through casts
