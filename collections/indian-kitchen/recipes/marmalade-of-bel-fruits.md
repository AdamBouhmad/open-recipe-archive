## Ingredients

- green bel fruits
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the shells of a few green bel fruits, cut into thin circular or semicircular pieces, remove the seeds with a pin and keep in water for an hour.
2. Then wash them with plain water, boil in a syrup of sugar till soft, take them out and serve when cold.
3. If they are to be preserved for a long time, instead of boiling them at once, boil them each day in degrees ranging over 5 or 6 days.
