## Ingredients

- one cauliflower
- butter
- white pepper
- salt
- breakfast-cupful of veloute au fromage
- grated Parmesan

## Directions

1. Choose a nice cauliflower and boil it carefully; it should be under rather than over-done.
2. Slice the stem evenly so that the cauliflower will sit up straight.
3. Place it in a neat dish that will stand the fire, well buttered, with the flower in the centre and the tender leaves neatly arranged round it.
4. Give it a dust of white pepper and a cream of salt.
5. Pour round, so that all the crevices may be filled, a breakfast-cupful of veloute au fromage.
6. Sprinkle a layer of grated Parmesan over the surface.
7. Bake the dish in a quick oven for ten minutes or a quarter of an hour.
8. By passing a red hot iron just over the top, brown the surface of the cheese.
