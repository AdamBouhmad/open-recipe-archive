## Ingredients

- Varieties of vegetables, as jhinga, potato, patol, brinjal, radish, beans, figs, green bananas, bori (dried cakes of pasted pulse)
- Kernel of a ripe cocoa-nut scratched out
- Sajina khara, kachu
- Tender jute leaves and neem leaves, uch-chhe, karala, palta
- Oil
- Powdered turmeric
- Pasted ginger
- Salt
- Powdered mustard
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Ghee
- Mustard seeds
- Milk or sugar

## Directions

1. Brown the boris in oil first and keep them aside
2. Fry the bitter vegetables in sufficient oil and add the other pieces of peeled vegetables
3. When everything is properly browned by constant stirring, add water with very little powdered turmeric and pasted ginger, and sufficient quantities of salt, powdered mustard, pepper and cumin
4. Add the browned boris when the mass is boiled
5. Season it with some ghee heated with a few mustard seeds
6. Add a little milk or sugar if desired
7. Take down with a quantity of soup
8. Add a little thin solution of flour if the soup is too thin
