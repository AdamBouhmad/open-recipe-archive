## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of melted butter
- 2 wineglasses of sherry
- 1 tablespoonful of pounded loaf sugar
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix with half a pint of melted butter two wineglasses of sherry and 1 tablespoonful of pounded loaf sugar
2. Make it quite hot, and serve in a sauce-tureen, with grated nutmeg on the top
