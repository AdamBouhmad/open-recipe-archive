## Ingredients

- a minced onion
- a little butter
- a little broth made from scraps
- a tea-spoonful of vinegar
- melted butter and flour
- a pinch of salt
- a pinch of sugar
- a little pepper
- some minced parsley

## Directions

1. Fry a minced onion in a little butter until it assumes a golden brown tint.
2. Pour in a little broth made from scraps, with a tea-spoonful of vinegar.
3. Give this a boil, and then strain it by degrees into another sauce-pan containing melted butter and flour.
4. Work this well with a wooden spoon and add a pinch of salt, one of sugar, a little pepper, and some minced parsley.
