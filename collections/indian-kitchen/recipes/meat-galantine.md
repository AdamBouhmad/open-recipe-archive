## Ingredients

- meat without bones
- bread-crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- onions
- eggs
- butter
- flour
- parsley
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly 1 seer of small pieces of meat without bones, 1/4 powa of bread-crumbs and pepper, salt, chopped onions and beaten eggs as required to make a stiff paste.
2. Smear a pudding-bag or a thick piece of cloth with butter and flour respectively.
3. Tie the mass in it and boil for 2 hours.
4. Keep the mass pressed between two large plates till cold.
5. Remove the cloth when set firmly, cut in suitable pieces, decorate with pieces of parsley or pistachios, and serve.
