## Ingredients

- teal
- water-cress
- butter
- limes
- Nepaul pepper

## Directions

1. Let the teal be sent up most carefully roasted straight from the fire
2. Garnish each portion with a bunch of water-cress
3. Send round butter, limes cut in quarters, and Nepaul pepper
