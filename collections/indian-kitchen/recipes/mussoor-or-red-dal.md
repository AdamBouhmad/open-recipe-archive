## Ingredients

- mussoor dal
- large clove of garlic

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the red dal in the same manner as moong dal
2. Instead of fried sliced onions, cut up a large clove of garlic small and fry it
3. The fried garlic takes the place of the onion
