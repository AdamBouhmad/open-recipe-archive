## Ingredients

- three ounces of beef suet
- one ounce of fat bacon
- three ounces of raw or dressed veal
- two ounces of green bread
- a little grated lemon-peel
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- finely-minced parsley
- beaten yolks of eggs
- ghee or clarified beef dripping

## Directions

1. Mince very finely the following ingredients: three ounces of beef suet, one of fat bacon, three of raw or dressed veal, two of green bread, a little grated lemon-peel, nutmeg, pepper, salt, and finely-minced parsley
2. mix all well together, and bind with the beaten yolks of eggs
3. make it into croquets or balls, the size of large nutmegs, and fry them in ghee or clarified beef dripping, or use it for stuffing
