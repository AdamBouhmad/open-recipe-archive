## Ingredients

- eight juicy limes
- four lemons or oranges
- two pounds of the best loaf sugar
- whites of ten fresh eggs
- two seers of cutcha, or raw ice
- two quarts of champagne

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice out of eight juicy limes and four lemons or oranges
2. strain it through muslin
3. well mix with it two pounds of the best loaf sugar
4. beat to a light froth the whites of ten fresh eggs
5. add gradually to the sugared juice
6. pour the whole into a pewter vessel
7. place it in a tub containing two seers of cutcha, or raw ice, stirring it frequently to make it congeal
8. Ice two quarts of champagne
9. when required add it to the contents of the pewter vessel
10. mix all well together
11. serve in green or amber-coloured hock glasses
