## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of treacle
- one egg
- four ounces of moist sugar
- an ounce of powdered ginger
- a quarter of an ounce each of mace, cloves, allspice, and nutmeg powdered
- a pound of oiled butter
- sufficient flour to make a stiff paste

## Directions

1. Mix well, and make into thick pieces, which should be brushed over the top with white of egg and baked for an hour in a moderate oven
