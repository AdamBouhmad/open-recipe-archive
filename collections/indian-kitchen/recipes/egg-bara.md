## Ingredients

- eggs
- fried and powdered garam-masalla
- cumin
- onions
- ginger
- besam
- ghee

## Directions

1. Take the contents of a few fresh eggs in a pot
2. Add fried and powdered garam-masalla and cumin, finely chopped onions, minced ginger and a little besam, and beat thoroughly
3. Form small thin cakes of it and fry in boiling ghee
4. Take them out with a spoon when they are brown
