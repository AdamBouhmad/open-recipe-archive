## Ingredients

- 8 lbs of turmeric
- 2 lbs of coriander-seed
- 1 lb of cummin-seed
- 2 lbs of poppy-seed
- 1 lb of fenugreek
- 1/2 lb of dry-ginger
- 1/2 lb of mustard-seed
- 1 lb of dried chillies
- black pepper corns
- salt, a quarter of a bottle

## Directions

1. Weigh everything most carefully, and even after the various ingredients have been cleaned, weigh them again, and also weigh the husks that have been removed.
2. Parch the coriander-seed and fenugreek very carefully, i.e., roasted like coffee berries, before being pounded.
3. Clean and dry each of the other ingredients separately.
4. Pound each ingredient separately and sift well.
5. Mix the whole of the powders, sprinkling in salt by degrees during the process.
6. Thoroughly cleanse and dry bottles in the sun.
7. Fill bottles and cork tightly down, with tops securely waxed over.
8. Some recommend that when the powder has been mixed, it should be browned in melted butter over the fire, then dried in the sun, and powdered again, in order to tone down the strong flavour of the cummin-seed.
