## Ingredients

- a shin of beef
- pepper
- salt
- water
- a chicken
- two chittacks or four ounces of ghee
- onions
- ground onions
- ground turmeric
- ground chilies
- ground ginger
- ground garlic
- roasted and ground coriander-seed
- roasted and ground cumin-seed
- two teaspoonfuls of salt
- two chittacks or four ounces of roasted and ground chunna ka or gram dal
- strong coconut milk
- half a dozen of the kurreeah fool leaves
- limes

## Directions

1. Prepare a shin of beef as directed above
2. Cut up a chicken; wash it and set it aside
3. Heat a pot and melt in it two chittacks or four ounces of ghee
4. After frying in it and setting aside some finely-sliced onions, fry the condiments in the proportions given in the foregoing recipe
5. Then add the cut-up chicken with two teaspoonfuls of salt
6. Brown it nicely
7. Have ready two chittacks or four ounces of roasted and ground chunna ka or gram dal, which mix thoroughly in a cup of strong coconut milk, and pour over the chicken just as it has become brown
8. Stir it well, and add the fried onions and the soup, with half a dozen of the kurreeah fool leaves
9. Close the pot, and allow the whole to simmer for three-quarters of an hour
10. Serve up hot, with limes, either whole or cut in slices, on a separate plate
