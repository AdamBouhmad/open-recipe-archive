## Ingredients

- ten bitter almonds
- two ounces of shelled sweet almonds
- rose water
- breakfast cupful of milk
- pint of fresh milk
- five ounces of sugar
- vanilla essence
- one ounce of dissolved gelatine
- coffee-cupful of cream

## Directions

1. Blanch ten bitter almonds and two ounces of shelled sweet almonds
2. Pound them to a paste with a little rose water
3. Dilute with a breakfast cupful of milk
4. Let the liquid stand for an hour and then strain it
5. Put into a pint of fresh milk five ounces of sugar and vanilla essence to taste
6. Pour it into an enamelled sauce-pan and boil slowly till the sugar is dissolved
7. Stir in an ounce of dissolved gelatine
8. Pour the liquid through a strainer into a basin
9. Add the almond liquor and a coffee-cupful of cream
10. Pour the mixture into a mould
11. Set it in ice and turn it out when firmly set
