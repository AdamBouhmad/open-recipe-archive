## Ingredients

- pieces of hilsa, chitol or any other big fish
- water
- powdered turmeric
- besam or rice-flour
- powdered turmeric
- red chillies
- ginger
- salt
- minced onions
- boiling oil or ghee

## Directions

1. Boil pieces of hilsa, chitol or any other big fish in water with a little powdered turmeric
2. Remove the bones
3. Add some besam or rice-flour, a little powdered turmeric, red chillies, ginger, salt and some minced onions, if desired
4. Beat thoroughly
5. Form cakes and drop them in boiling oil or ghee after the bubbles die out and the vapour arises
6. Fry till golden-brown
