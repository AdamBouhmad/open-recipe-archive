## Ingredients

- bread
- prawn doopiaja or prawn cofta curry
- gravy

## Directions

1. Pare away very finely all the outer brown crust of the bread, without injuring the inner crust
2. Cut out of the top of the loaf a small square sufficiently large to extract from within all the crumb, leaving the shell complete
3. Fill the loaf up to the top either with some prawn doopiaja minced, or with the prawn cofta curry, and as much gravy as it will take
4. Replace the square bit at the top
5. Bake it to a light brown
6. Serve up hot
