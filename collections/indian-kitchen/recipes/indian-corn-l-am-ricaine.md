## Ingredients

- Indian corn or maize, young
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Strip corn from the young pod
2. Boil like peas
3. Drain thoroughly
4. Toss in melted butter
5. Season with pepper and salt
6. Serve with plenty of butter
