## Ingredients

- breast of mutton
- juice of onions
- ginger
- garlic
- pepper
- salt
- two tablespoonfuls of ghee or lard
- stock
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut up a breast of mutton in the usual way for a stew; wash and dry the meat
2. Take of the juice of onions one tablespoonful, of ginger half a tablespoonful, and of garlic a quarter of a tablespoonful; mix with the meat, add pepper and salt, and allow to stand for any time from one to four hours
3. Fry in a large stewpan two tablespoonfuls of ghee or lard, and when on the boil fry to a nice brown all the meat only
4. Afterwards pour in the liquor in which the meat had been steeped, and allow to simmer for fifteen or twenty minutes
5. Thicken some stock with a teaspoonful of flour, and add it to the stew
6. Allow to simmer until the meat is perfectly tender
