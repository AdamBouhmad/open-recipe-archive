## Ingredients

- well boiled rice
- butter
- tomato pulp
- finely grated Parmesan, Gruyere, or other mild dry cheese

## Directions

1. Empty the well boiled rice into a hot sauce-pan with plenty of butter.
2. Stir till thoroughly hot and well lubricated, add tomato pulp enough to moisten the whole nicely, and finish with finely grated Parmesan, Gruyere, or other mild dry cheese.
3. Serve piping hot.
