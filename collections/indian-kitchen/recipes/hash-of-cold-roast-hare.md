## Ingredients

- cold roast hare
- large onion
- pepper-corns
- celery
- any vegetable
- spice
- any sauce that may have been left
- a couple of glasses of red wine
- water
- butter and flour
- a dessert-spoonful of red currant jelly
- a table-spoonful of mushroom ketchup
- half a glass of vinegar
- a little more red wine

## Directions

1. Trim off as much of the meat as you can find in slices
2. Cut out what remains of the stuffing
3. Break up all the bones and put them with the skin and scraps into a stew-pan
4. Add a large onion cut up, pepper-corns, a bit of celery if possible, any vegetable, a little spice, any sauce that may have been left, a couple of glasses of red wine and enough water to cover the bones
5. Simmer these ingredients for an hour and a half
6. Strain off the gravy
7. Thicken it with butter and flour
8. Flavour it with a dessert-spoonful of red currant jelly, a table-spoonful of mushroom ketchup, half a glass of vinegar, and a little more red wine
9. Heat up the slices of hare in this sauce taking care that they do not boil
10. Serve with the stuffing sliced and fried in butter as a garnish
