## Ingredients

- two partridges
- two quails
- four snipes
- back fillets of a good hare
- spiced pepper
- livers of the birds
- liver of the hare
- spare meat of the hare
- a quarter of a pound of ham
- half a pound of fat bacon
- finely chopped shallot
- eight fine truffles
- an ounce of butter
- a claret-glassful of Madeira
- liver and bacon forcemeat
- marjoram leaves
- bones and trimmings of the game
- two sheep's feet
- truffle liquor

## Directions

1. Bone two partridges, two quails, and four snipes
2. Cut out the back fillets of a good hare
3. Give the meat a dusting of spiced pepper, and cover it up
4. Take the livers of the birds and that of the hare, and mince them finely
5. Mince the spare meat of the hare, and a quarter of a pound of ham
6. Throw the whole of the mince into a frying-pan, in which half a pound of fat bacon cut into dice has been tossed with a finely chopped shallot
7. Work the mince about for ten minutes
8. Empty the contents of the frying-pan into a mortar, and pound the mixture to a paste
9. Cut eight fine truffles into dice the size of a pea
10. Melt an ounce of butter in the frying-pan, and throw in the truffles
11. Toss for a couple of minutes, and add a claret-glassful of Madeira
12. Let the dice boil in this, and then pour them with the liquor into a bowl
13. Spread a layer of liver paste over the inside of each bird
14. Put a spoonful of truffles upon it, and roll the bird up
15. Cut the hare into slices, and treat them in the same way
16. Put a layer of the liver paste over the bottom of the pie
17. Dot it over with truffles
18. Arrange the rolls of hare meat over that
19. Spread some liver paste over them, a few truffles, and some thin slices of fat bacon
20. Arrange a layer of snipe, and continue until the pie is tightly filled
21. Cover the topmost layer with fat bacon, sprinkled over with marjoram leaves
22. Cover the pie closely and bake
23. When done, pour into it a little very strong jelly made of the bones and trimmings of the game, two sheep's feet, and the truffle liquor
