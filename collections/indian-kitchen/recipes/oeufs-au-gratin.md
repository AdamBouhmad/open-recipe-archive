## Ingredients

- pie-dish
- butter
- shallot
- maccaroni boiled in milk
- half a pint of sauce blonde
- grated Parmesan or other mild cheese
- hard-boiled eggs
- finely minced anchovy
- pepper and salt or spiced salt
- bread-crumbs
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Butter the bottom of a pie-dish previously well rubbed with shallot
2. Line it with some maccaroni already boiled in milk
3. Pour round it half a pint of sauce blonde in which you have dissolved some grated Parmesan or other mild well-rasped cheese
4. Dispose a complete layer of hard-boiled eggs, sliced
5. Sprinkle a finely minced anchovy over the eggs with pepper and salt or with a judicious dressing of spiced salt
6. Apply a nice coating of bread-crumbs and grated cheese mixed in equal proportions
7. Drop a number of little bits of butter the size of a pea over the surface
8. Bake the dish till the top takes a golden brown tint
