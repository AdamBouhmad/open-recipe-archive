## Ingredients

- fillets cut from the undercut of a sirloin
- bacon for larding
- six anchovies from the tin
- half a pint of good veloute
- good cream

## Directions

1. Lard the fillets with bacon and marinate all day
2. Broil the fillets over a bright fire
3. Serve them round a chaplet of mashed potato, in the centre of which you may place a puree of spinach, or a bunch of water-cress
4. Take six anchovies from the tin, wipe them dry, free them from the oil, and pass them through the sieve
5. Add the pulp to about half a pint of good veloute
6. Heat it up, and as you serve, enrich the sauce with a ladleful of good cream
7. Serve the sauce round very hot, in a boat
