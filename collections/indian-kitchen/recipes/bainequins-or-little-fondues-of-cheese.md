## Ingredients

- one ounce of butter
- a quarter of a pint of water
- a pinch of salt
- black pepper
- two ounces of flour
- two ounces of grated Parmesan
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Put one ounce of butter in a roomy sauce-pan, with a quarter of a pint of water, a pinch of salt, and a dust of black pepper
2. Boil it, and add two ounces of flour
3. Stir over the fire for four minutes
4. Mix with it two ounces of grated Parmesan, and two eggs, one after the other
5. Put the paste thus formed on a buttered baking sheet in lumps the size of a hen's egg
6. Flatten them slightly
7. Brush them over with an egg
8. Bake in the oven
9. Serve on a napkin very hot
