## Ingredients

- boiled and peeled potatoes
- salt
- black cumins
- powders of chillies
- garam-masalla
- aniseed
- 1 chhatak ghee
- 1/2 seer of fine flour
- water
- meat

## Directions

1. Break some boiled and peeled potatoes and mix with salt, a few entire black cumins, powders of chillies, garam-masalla and aniseed
2. Mix 1 chhatak ghee and a little salt with half seer of fine flour
3. Prepare a stiff dough with water
4. Make very small balls of it so that when rolled make discs of diameter 3 to 4 inches
5. Form hollow cones of these
6. Stuff each with a little spiced potato and close up
7. Fry in plenty of ghee
8. Small pieces of meat fried with spices may be substituted for potatoes
