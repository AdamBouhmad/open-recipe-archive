## Ingredients

- mature yet green mangoes
- water
- cleansed raisins
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel a few mature yet green mangoes, take out the edible portions and throw away the stones
2. Thrash them thoroughly with a stone and keep in water for about half an hour
3. Mix them thoroughly and drain out the water
4. Wash them again with fresh water
5. Boil them with a little water
6. Add cleansed raisins and salt as required
7. When they are boiled add sugar equal to half the quantity of mangoes
8. Take down when the mass is like a stiff dough
9. Serve after a day
