## Ingredients

- French beans
- butter
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Trim the French beans as explained
2. Put them into a jar or steamer with a table-spoonful of butter, a dessert-spoonful of sugar, and a large tea-spoonful of salt
3. Steam the jar as described for peas
4. When done, drain the beans and serve with a pat of butter boiling hot
