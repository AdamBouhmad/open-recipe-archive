## Ingredients

- six or eight slices of fried bread
- caviare roe
- ounce of butter
- Nepaul pepper
- juice of a lime
- mayonnaise sauce
- olive

## Directions

1. Prepare six or eight neatly shaped slices of fried bread
2. Take from a tin of caviare enough roe to cover each piece nicely
3. Choose a small sauce-pan, melt an ounce of butter in it
4. Stir in the caviare, dust it well with Nepaul pepper, and add the juice of a lime
5. When piping hot, spread the caviare over the fried bread (which should have been kept in the oven) and send up the canapes without delay
6. Alternatively, spread the caviare cold over the cold fried bread, mask the surface with thick mayonnaise sauce, and garnish with a turned olive upon each
