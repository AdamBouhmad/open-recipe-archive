## Ingredients

- damsons that have been bottled for tarts
- loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Take damsons that have been bottled for tarts
2. Pass them through a sieve and reject the skins and stones
3. To every pound of the strained pulp add half a pound of loaf sugar broken small
4. Boil the whole until it has thickened
5. Pour it into buttered moulds
6. Put it in an oven or warm place to dry
7. When quite firm, remove it from the moulds and serve up
