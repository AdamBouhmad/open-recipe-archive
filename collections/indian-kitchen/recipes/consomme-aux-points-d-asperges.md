## Ingredients

- clear, well-flavoured consomme
- a small tin of asparagus

## Directions

1. Prepare a clear, well-flavoured consomme for eight
2. Take a small tin of asparagus, open it, and turn the pieces out upon a dish, draining from them all the liquor of the tin
3. Choose American asparagus if you can, because it is so much greener than either the French or English
4. When drained, cut the tender ends of the asparagus into pieces half an inch long using a dessert-knife
5. Put them away carefully, and throw the tough ends into the consomme
6. When strained, and ready to serve, add to the soup the chopped green points
7. Beat it up to concert pitch, and serve
