## Ingredients

- seams, twenty
- new potatoes, four
- green peas, a quarter of a seer
- mustard oil, one chittack and a half
- ground onions, four teaspoonfuls
- ground chilies, one teaspoonful
- ground turmeric, half a teaspoonful
- ground garlic, a quarter of a teaspoonful
- salt, one teaspoonful and a half
- water, one cupful
- cauliflower (optional for a change)

## Directions

1. Divide each seam into three pieces and throw into a bowl of water
2. Divide each potato into four pieces and throw into water
3. Shell the peas and wash all thoroughly
4. Put into a colander to drain
5. Warm the oil and let it bubble well
6. Fry the ground condiments until quite brown
7. Put in the vegetables and salt
8. Let the whole fry, stirring it well
9. Add the water and allow it to simmer over a slow fire until the vegetables are quite tender
