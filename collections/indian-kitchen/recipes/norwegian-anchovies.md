## Ingredients

- tin of best sardines
- white pepper
- Bombay onion
- cinnamon leaf
- pepper corn
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Open a tin of the best sardines, take the fish out one by one, and place them on a dish
2. Wipe them free from oil, carefully pick off their skins and divide each sardine in half lengthwise
3. Give the fillets thus obtained a dusting of white pepper
4. Take an oval earthenware pot, see that it is dry and clean, slice a Bombay onion up, and put a layer of the rings at the bottom of the pot, with a cinnamon leaf, and a pepper corn or two
5. Arrange over this a layer of sardine fillets, and continue the process until the pot is filled, or the fillets are exhausted
6. Pour over the layers a marinade of oil and vinegar (one spoonful of vinegar to four of oil)
7. In a few hours the sardines may be eaten
