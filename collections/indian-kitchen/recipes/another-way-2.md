## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- one pound and a quarter of treacle
- half a pound of sugar
- two ounces of ginger
- three-quarters of a pound of butter (melted)
- a small quantity of cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Mix all together and roll out to about the thickness of half an inch, or not quite so much
2. Cut into cakes, and bake in a moderate oven
