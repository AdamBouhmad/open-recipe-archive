## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of turmeric
- 8 lbs. of coriander-seed
- 2 lbs. of cummin-seed
- 1 lb. of poppy-seed
- 2 lbs. of fenugreek
- 1 lb. of dry-ginger
- 1/2 lb. of mustard-seed
- 1/2 lb. of dried chillies
- 1 lb. of black pepper corns
