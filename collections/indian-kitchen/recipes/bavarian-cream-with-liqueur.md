## Ingredients

- three-quarters of an ounce of gelatine
- half a liqueur-glass of brandy or sherry
- a few drops of vanilla essence
- one ounce of cocoa powder
- one pint of boiling milk
- two ounces of sugar
- yolks of eight eggs
- one ounce of dissolved gelatine
- one coffee-cupful of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Melt three-quarters of an ounce of gelatine in a stew-pan over the fire, with half a liqueur-glass of brandy or sherry, and a few drops of vanilla essence
2. Strain it through a tamis cloth, till clear, and set it to get cool
3. Take one ounce of cocoa powder, and stir it into a pint of boiling milk, adding two ounces of sugar
4. When thoroughly mixed, strain the milk through a piece of muslin
5. Let it get quite cold, and then add to it the strained yolks of eight eggs, making therewith a rich custard
6. Set the custard upon ice, having stirred an ounce of dissolved gelatine into it
7. When it begins to set, add a coffee-cupful of whipped cream, mix thoroughly
8. Place a mould on ice and pour into it a layer of the vanilla jelly half an inch thick
9. When set, pour in about an inch of the cream, then a layer of jelly when the cream has set, and so on until the mould is filled
10. Serve after it has rested on ice for an hour
