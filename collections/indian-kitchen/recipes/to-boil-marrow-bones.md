## Ingredients

- marrow-bones
- butter
- flour
- linen
- thin slices of dry toast
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Saw them even at the bottom.
2. Butter and flour some bits of linen, and tie a piece over the top of each bone.
3. Boil them for an hour or two.
4. Take off the linen, and serve them with thin slices of dry toast cut into square bits.
5. At table the marrow should be put upon the toast, and a little pepper and salt sprinkled over it.
