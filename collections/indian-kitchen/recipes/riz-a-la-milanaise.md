## Ingredients

- rice
- butter
- shred onion
- stock
- saffron
- flour
- grated Parmesan

## Directions

1. Parboil the rice for five minutes.
2. Fry it in butter with a shred onion until it begins to take colour.
3. Add stock without tomato, but coloured with saffron.
4. When the rice is cooked, the gravy may be thickened with flour.
5. Serve hot with grated Parmesan.
6. Add the stock by degrees, allowing the rice to drink it up as it were.
7. The grains gradually distend, and when ready to burst are fit to serve.
