## Ingredients

- cold mashed potatoes
- chopped parsley
- shallot
- chopped thyme or marjoram
- spices
- fat

## Directions

1. Work the cold mashed potatoes very much as previously described
2. Flavour them with a little chopped parsley, a very little shallot, a little chopped thyme or marjoram, or spices if you like
3. Form them into rolls or tablets
4. Fry them a golden brown
