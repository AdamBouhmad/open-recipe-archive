## Ingredients

- six ounces of fresh beef suet
- two ounces of moist sugar
- a quarter of a pound of well-grated bread-crumbs
- half a pint of new milk
- the well-beaten yolks of three eggs

## Directions

1. Shred six ounces of fresh beef suet and chop it up fine
2. Mix it with two ounces of moist sugar and a quarter of a pound of well-grated bread-crumbs
3. Stir in half a pint of new milk
4. When these are all mixed, add the well-beaten yolks of three eggs
5. Whisk all together for a quarter of an hour
