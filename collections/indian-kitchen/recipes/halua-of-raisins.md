## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of raisins
- 3 chhataks of ghee
- a few cassia leaves
- 1/2 seer of sugar
- cardamom seeds
- camphor

## Directions

1. Remove the stems of 1/2 seer of raisins and wash them.
2. Heat 3 chhataks of ghee with a few cassia leaves and when the bubbles die out fry the raisins with the ghee till they begin to melt.
3. When they begin to melt pour in a syrup of 1/2 seer of sugar and stir constantly.
4. Take down when thick and mix with the seeds of a few cardamoms and very little camphor.
