## Ingredients

- French bean pods
- soda
- salt
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Gather the pods young
2. Peel all round to get rid of the delicate fibre
3. Nip off the ends
4. Plunge into boiling water with a pinch of soda, at least a tea-spoonful of salt and one of sugar mixed in
