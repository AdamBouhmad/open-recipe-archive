## Ingredients

- 1 powa besam of moog
- water
- ghee
- asafoetida ½ tola
- powdered cumin ½ tola
- pepper ½ tola
- pasted ginger 1 chhatak
- lemon juice 1 table-spoonful
- ½ seer besam of moog
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1 powa of besam of moog with some water and fry it in small cakes in boiling ghee till very hard
2. Powder them
3. Mix the powder with fried and powdered asafoetida ½ tola, powdered cumin and pepper ½ tola each, pasted ginger 1 chhatak and a table-spoonful of lemon juice
4. Make a stiff paste of ½ seer of besam of moog with water
5. Make balls of it, press them flat, stuff with the above spiced preparation and close them again making a little flat and elongated cakes
6. Fry them in plenty of ghee and keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated
