## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- milk
- onion
- spice
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- flour
- yolk of an egg
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut up your boiled potatoes into thick slices
2. Flavour a little milk with onion, spice, pepper, and salt
3. Strain and thicken it with butter and flour till you have a nice sauce blanche
4. Place your slices of potato in this sauce
5. Heat them up to boiling point
6. Take the sauce-pan off the fire
7. Stir in the yolk of an egg
8. Add a large spoonful of chopped parsley and a pat of butter the size of a rupee
9. Serve
