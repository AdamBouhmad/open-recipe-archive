## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound of fresh butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- salt
- 1/2 gill of water
- 1/2 spoonful of white vinegar
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix in a stewpan, with a quarter of a pound of fresh butter, a tablespoonful of flour, a little salt, half a gill of water, half a spoonful of white vinegar, and a little grated nutmeg
2. Put it on the fire, stir it, and let it thicken, but do not allow it to boil, lest it should taste of the flour
