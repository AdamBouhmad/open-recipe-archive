## Ingredients

- asparagus
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolk
- butter
- bread

## Directions

1. Boil it, and chop small the heads and tender parts of the stalks, together with a boiled onion
2. Add a little salt and pepper, and the beaten yolk of an egg
3. Heat it up
4. Serve it on sippets of toasted bread, and pour over it a little melted butter
