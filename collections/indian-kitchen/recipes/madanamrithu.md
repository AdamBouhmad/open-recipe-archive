## Ingredients

- 1 powa of pulses of moog
- water
- 1/2 powa of curd
- plenty of ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Keep 1 powa of pulses of moog in water for 3 or 4 hours
2. Make a paste of moistened moog
3. Beat the paste with 1/2 powa of curd with the hands or churn with a staff till a drop of the mass when thrown on water float on it
4. Fry Jilapi with this paste in plenty of ghee on mild heat
5. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till saturated
