## Ingredients

- 4 boiled potatoes
- 1 or 2 chittacks or 2 ounces of butter free of water
- pepper
- salt
- grated bread-crumb
- 8 or 10 olives stoned
- stock or gravy

## Directions

1. Take four boiled potatoes; break them into pieces while hot
2. Add a chittack or two ounces of butter free of water, some pepper and salt, a little grated bread-crumb, and some eight or ten olives stoned, and cut or chopped small
3. Moisten with a little stock or gravy before stuffing the fowls
