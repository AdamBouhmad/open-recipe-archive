## Ingredients

- 2 seers of milk
- 1 powa of sugar
- 1 seer of hard chhana
- 3 powas of fine flour
- milk
- plenty of ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Boil 2 seers of milk with 1 powa of sugar stirring constantly till it is a stiff paste
2. Scrape together this kheer from the pan and beat thoroughly
3. Make a paste of 1 seer of hard chhana and mix it thoroughly with 3 powas of fine flour
4. Make a stiff dough of them with milk
5. Prepare elongated cakes of it stuffed with the previously prepared kheer
6. Fry them in plenty of ghee
7. Drop them in a syrup of sugar
8. Serve when saturated with syrup
