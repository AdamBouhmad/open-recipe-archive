## Ingredients

- white Patna onion
- fresh green chilies
- fresh lime juice
- cold corned beef

## Directions

1. Steep sliced onions and chilies in lime-juice
2. Have the red well-corned part of a cold round of beef nicely pounded
3. Add to it the onions, chilies, and as much of the lime-juice as may be desirable
