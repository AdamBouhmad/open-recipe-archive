## Ingredients

- dry prawns
- red pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take a seer of dry prawns
2. Wash them well, dry over the fire until crisp
3. Pound fine, with some red pepper and nutmeg
4. Pass through a sieve, and bottle for use
