## Ingredients

- three ounces cream of tartar
- one ounce bruised ginger
- one pound and a half white sugar
- one ounce lemon-juice
- one gallon and a half boiling water
- two tablespoonfuls yeast

## Directions

1. Pour the boiling water on the cream of tartar, ginger, sugar, and lemon-juice
2. Add two tablespoonfuls of yeast
3. Mix
4. Bottle and tie down the corks as usual
