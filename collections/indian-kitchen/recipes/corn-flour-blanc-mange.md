## Ingredients

- two tablespoonfuls of corn-flour per quart of milk
- a stick of cinnamon
- lemon-peel
- sugar to taste
- pounded almonds

## Directions

1. Use the rice blanc mange recipe
2. Use the proportion of two tablespoonfuls of corn-flour to every quart of milk
