## Ingredients

- finger biscuits
- couple of ounces of sugar
- quarter of a pound of blanched Jordan almonds
- pint and a half of very rich custard
- half an ounce of dissolved gelatine
- breakfast-cupful of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Line a mould with finger biscuits as described in Menu No. IV.
2. Put a couple of ounces of sugar in a copper-pan; melt it till it is very hot.
3. Stir into it a quarter of a pound of blanched Jordan almonds for a few minutes.
4. Spread the almonds on a dish to cool.
5. When cold, pound them to a puree saving a few, which should only be minced roughly, and stirred into the ice when nearly formed.
6. Mix a pint and a half of very rich custard and stir the burnt almond puree into it while quite hot.
7. Strain, whip, and add half an ounce of dissolved gelatine.
8. Put the custard into the pail, adding a breakfast-cupful of whipped cream, and finish as explained in the menu previously quoted.
