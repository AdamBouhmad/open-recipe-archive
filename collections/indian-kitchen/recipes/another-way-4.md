## Ingredients

- Whites of three new-laid eggs
- One pound of sifted white sugar
- Flour for the cake

## Directions

1. Beat to a stiff froth the whites of three new-laid eggs, and add to them one pound of sifted white sugar
2. Flour the cake, and then wipe it off
3. Apply the icing by means of a knife smoothly
4. Then bake in a slow oven
