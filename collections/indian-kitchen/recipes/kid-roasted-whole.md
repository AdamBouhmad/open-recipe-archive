## Ingredients

- whole kid with its head on
- stuffing
- beef suet melted down
- hot water
- salt
- lime

## Directions

1. Bespeak from a butcher a whole kid, with its head on
2. Prepare a stuffing as per recipe No. 323 or 325, and after cleaning the kid, stuff into it the stuffing
3. Break the joints of the legs, and fold and truss them like a pig
4. Put it up to roast, basting it the whole time with beef suet melted down, to which add hot water and salt
5. Serve up in a sitting posture like a pig, and with a lime in the mouth
