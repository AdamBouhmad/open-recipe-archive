## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 big green mangoes
- 1 seer of water
- 1 powa of sugar
- salt
- table-spoonful of rose water
- fragments of ice

## Directions

1. Burn or boil 4 or 5 big green mangoes till they are soft
2. Peel them and strain the pulp through a clean piece of linen
3. Mix it with 1 seer of water, 1 powa of sugar, a pinch of salt, a table-spoonful of rose water and fragments of ice as required
