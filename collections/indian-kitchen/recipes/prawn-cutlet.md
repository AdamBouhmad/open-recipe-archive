## Ingredients

- prawns
- pepper
- salt
- finely-minced soup herbs
- yolk of eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. Shell and wash the prawns
2. Remove the heads, but leave the tails
3. Slit them down in the centre, and gently beat them flat with a rolling-pin
4. Sprinkle them with pepper and salt, and some finely-minced soup herbs
5. Rub them over with yolk of eggs, and dredge with flour
6. Fry over a very moderate fire to a rich light brown colour
7. Garnish the dish with fried green parsley, or serve up with tomato sauce gravy
