## Ingredients

- a large red beetroot
- yolks of five eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of flour
- four tablespoonfuls of cream
- plenty of pounded loaf sugar
- half a nutmeg grated
- a wineglassful of brandy
- melted butter, ghee, or lard
- green candied sweetmeats

## Directions

1. Boil a large red beetroot until it is very tender
2. Peel it, cut it into thin slices
3. Pound it to a pulp in a marble mortar and strain through muslin
4. Add the yolks of five eggs, two tablespoonfuls of flour, four of cream, plenty of pounded loaf sugar, half a nutmeg grated, and a wineglassful of brandy
5. Rub the whole into a batter
6. Fry the pancakes with melted butter, ghee, or lard
7. Serve them up hot, garnished with green candied sweetmeats
