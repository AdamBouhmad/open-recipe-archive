## Ingredients

- eels
- batter
- oil or fat
- plain sharp sauce

## Directions

1. Slightly boil eels first to get rid of their oiliness
2. Cut them into fillets
3. Dip fillets in batter
4. Fry in oil or fat (lots of it)
5. Serve with a plain sharp sauce
