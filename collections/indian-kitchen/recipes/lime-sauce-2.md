## Ingredients

- sugar
- water
- lime rind
- lime juice
- liqueur, brandy, or rum
- cochineal

## Directions

1. Put three table-spoonfuls of sugar into a sauce-pan with a breakfast-cupful of water and the very finely peeled rind of a lime
2. Simmer for twenty minutes
3. Add the juice of two limes and a liqueur glass of any liqueur, brandy, or rum
4. Strain and serve
5. A drop or two of cochineal will improve the colour of the sauce
