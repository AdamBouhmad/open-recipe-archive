## Ingredients

- the weight of eight eggs in their shells of finely-powdered sugar
- that of two eggs of potato flour
- the same weight of pistachio nuts blanched and skinned
- eight eggs
- four eggs (whites)
- four eggs (whites)
- two eggs (whites)
- a little lime juice
- six ounces of sugar
- strips of citron
- preserved cherries

## Directions

1. Beat up the sugar and the yolks of eight eggs well together with an egg whisk or with a fork until the mixture assumes a white creamy appearance
2. Sprinkle in half the potato flour while beating the mixture all the time
3. Add the whites of four eggs whisked to a stiff froth
4. Put in the rest of the flour in the same manner
5. Add the remaining four whites beaten to a froth
6. Lastly add the pistachio nuts pounded to a paste in a mortar
7. Bake in a slow oven
8. Meanwhile put the whites of two eggs into a basin with a little lime juice and six ounces of sugar
9. Well work the mixture with a wooden spoon
10. As it gets thin, keep on adding more sugar until you get a smooth paste of the consistency of batter
11. Lay the icing evenly on the cake with a spatula
12. Put it into the oven for a minute to set the icing
13. Ornament it quickly with strips of citron and preserved cherries
14. Put it aside to get cold
