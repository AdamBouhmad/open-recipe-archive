## Ingredients

- Bellary onion
- pint of milk
- pepper corns
- nutmeg
- cloves
- grated bread-crumbs
- cream

## Directions

1. Simmer till tender a Bellary onion in a pint of milk with a dozen pepper corns, a pinch of nutmeg, and four cloves
2. Strain when the onion is quite pulpy
3. Pour the strained milk over the grated bread-crumbs, and heat it up again
4. Add a spoonful of cream before serving
