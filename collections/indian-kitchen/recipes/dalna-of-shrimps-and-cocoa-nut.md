## Ingredients

- flesh portions of shrimps
- kernel of a ripe cocoa-nut
- pasted onions
- powdered turmeric
- chillies
- salt
- besam or flour
- ghee
- big pieces of potatoes
- patois or cauliflowers
- oil
- water
- powdered cumin
- pepper
- coriander
- cassia leaves
- red chillies
- powdered garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Take the flesh portions of some shrimps and the kernel of a ripe cocoa-nut in the proportion 2 to 1 respectively and grind them to a paste.
2. Add some pasted onions, a little powdered turmeric, chillies, salt and besam or flour in a quantity such that when beaten the mass forms a very thick paste.
3. Spread the mass on a flat dish making uniform thickness and cut it in squares.
4. Fry the pieces in ghee.
5. Brown some big pieces of potatoes and patois or cauliflowers with oil and add water with powdered cumin, pepper, coriander, turmeric and salt.
6. Add the fried pieces on boiling.
7. Season the curry when it is fully boiled with some ghee boiled with a few cassia leaves and red chillies.
8. Add ghee and powdered garam-masalla before finish.
