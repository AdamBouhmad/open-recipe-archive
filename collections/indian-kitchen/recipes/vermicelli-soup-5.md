## Ingredients

- a shin of beef
- pepper
- salt
- water
- soup herbs
- white of an egg
- vermicelli

## Directions

1. Prepare a shin of beef soup as directed above, omitting the sauce and sherry
2. Parboil some vermicelli
3. After clearing the soup with the white of an egg, add to it the parboiled vermicelli, and give it a good boil up before serving
