## Ingredients

- alubokharas 1 powa
- raisins 1 powa
- dates 1 powa
- ½ powa of oil
- panch-foron
- broken chillies
- 3 powas of water
- 1 powa of sugar
- salt
- pasted turmeric

## Directions

1. Wash alubokharas, raisins and dates 1 powa each and keep the raisins and alubokharas under water for about a quarter of an hour in an enamelled pot
2. Heat ½ powa of oil and fry a generous pinch of panch-foron and some broken chillies in the oil
3. Fry a little the alubokharas, raisins and dates in it
4. Add 3 powas of water and when it boils add 1 powa of sugar, a generous pinch of salt and a little pasted turmeric
5. Take down when the desired thickness of the soup is reached
