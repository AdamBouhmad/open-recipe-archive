## Ingredients

- Seven pounds of currants well picked and cleaned
- Half a pound of finely-chopped beef suet
- Half a pound of the lean of sirloin of beef minced raw
- Half a pound of citron, lemon, and orange peel cut small
- Two pounds of fine moist sugar
- One ounce of mixed spice
- The rinds of four lemons and four oranges
- One bottle of brandy
- White wine
- The juice of the four lemons and oranges

## Directions

1. Mix well the currants, suet, beef, citron, lemon and orange peel, sugar, mixed spice, and rinds of lemons and oranges
2. Put in a deep pan
3. Mix the bottle of brandy and white wine and the juice of the four lemons and oranges
4. Pour half over, and press down tight with the hand
5. Then add the other half and cover closely
6. It may be made one year, to use the next
