## Ingredients

- 1.5 seer of syrup of sugar
- 1 seer of hard chhana

## Directions

1. Heat syrup of sugar 1.5 seer and when slightly warm add a paste of hard chhana 1 seer.
2. Stir continuously and vigorously till sticky.
3. Take it off fire and stir as before till cold.
4. Make balls of it and serve.
