## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- meat dripping

## Directions

1. After being boiled, lay them on a dish, and place it in the dripping-pan
2. Baste them now and then with a little of the meat dripping
3. When one side is browned turn the other; they should all be of an equal colour
