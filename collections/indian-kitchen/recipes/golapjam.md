## Ingredients

- hard chhana 1/2 seer
- sabeda (powdered atap rice) 1/2 seer
- water if necessary
- sugar for syrup
- dry sugar for coating

## Directions

1. Make a paste of 1/2 seer hard chhana
2. Mix thoroughly with 1/2 seer of sabeda with a little water if necessary
3. Make medium balls of it
4. Boil in a syrup of sugar till stiff
5. Take them out and smear with dry sugar
