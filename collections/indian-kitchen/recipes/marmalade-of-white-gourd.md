## Ingredients

- 1 ripe and old white gourd
- 1 seer of white gourd pieces
- 1 chhatak of sabeda
- 1 tola of powdered alum
- water
- 1 seer of sugar
- 1 tola of powdered cardamoms

## Directions

1. Peel a ripe and old white gourd, cut in big pieces, remove the seeds, perforate them all over with an iron pin and keep 1 seer of such pieces in cold water for about an hour.
2. Then smear them with 1 chhatak of sabeda and 1 tola of powdered alum, and boil in water.
3. Then wash them with plain water and drop in a boiling syrup of 1 seer of sugar.
4. Mix with 1 tola of powdered cardamoms when the syrup becomes very thick.
5. Then take down.
