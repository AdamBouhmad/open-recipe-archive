## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- anchovy
- cloves
- nutmeg
- mace
- pepper
- salt
- white wine
- butter
- flour
- cream

## Directions

1. Clean them as for pickling, and, after washing them, put them into a saucepan, with an anchovy, two cloves, some nutmeg sliced, mace, whole pepper, and salt
2. let them stew in their own liquor till tender
3. In this way they will keep for some time
4. when required to be dressed, pick out the spice, and to a dishful put two large tablespoonfuls of white wine
5. add part of their own liquor, and let them just boil
6. stir in a bit of butter dredged with flour, and two tablespoonfuls of cream
