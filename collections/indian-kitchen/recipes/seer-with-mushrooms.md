## Ingredients

- a good deep cut of seer fish
- a pint of thin brown gravy
- a little onion
- a carrot
- a bit of celery
- some parsley
- a tablespoonful of chablis or sauterne
- one tablespoonful of mushroom ketchup
- a teaspoonful of red currant jelly
- a little lime juice
- butter
- flour
- a dozen black Leicestershire mushrooms

## Directions

1. Choose a good deep cut of seer fish
2. Take about a pint of thin brown gravy, a little onion, a carrot cut up, a bit of celery, and some parsley
3. Flavour it with a tablespoonful of chablis or sauterne, and one of mushroom ketchup, a teaspoonful of red currant jelly, and a little lime juice
4. Stew the fish in this liquor very gently
5. When done, strain it, thicken the gravy with butter and flour, adding a dozen black Leicestershire mushrooms
6. Heat up without boiling, and serve
