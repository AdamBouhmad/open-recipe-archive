## Ingredients

- A hundred green mangoes
- half a pound each of ground chilies, ginger, and garlic
- half a pound of bruised mustard-seed
- two pounds of tamarinds without the stones
- salt
- warmed or cooked mustard oil

## Directions

1. Peel and slice fine a hundred green mangoes
2. Steep them in salt for twelve hours
3. Put them under a heavy pressure for two hours, and drain away all the water
4. Mix with them half a pound each of ground chilies, ginger, and garlic, half a pound of bruised mustard-seed, two pounds of tamarinds without the stones, and some salt
5. Pour over it as much warmed or cooked mustard oil as will entirely cover it
6. Cook it for ten to fifteen minutes over a brisk fire
7. When cold bottle it, taking care that it is kept several inches well under the oil, and that it is well corked
