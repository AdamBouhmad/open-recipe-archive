## Ingredients

- Peas, husked and broken in two, 1 seer
- Ghee 1/2 powa
- Ginger 2 tolas
- Cumin 1 tola
- Coriander 2 tolas
- Salt 1/2 chhatak
- Pepper 1/2 tola
- Garam-masalla 1/2 tola
- Water 2 seers
- A few cassia leaves

## Directions

1. Make a paste of the ginger, cumin, coriander and pepper and dilute it in the water with salt.
2. Brown the dal in half of the ghee and pour in the spiced water.
3. Take down when the dal are thoroughly boiled.
4. Heat the remaining ghee in another pan with the cassia leaves and the powdered garam-masalla.
5. Pour in the boiled dal when the smell of the spices evolves, and stir.
6. Take down when the desired degree of thickness is obtained.
