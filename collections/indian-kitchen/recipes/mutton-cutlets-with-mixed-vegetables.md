## Ingredients

- eight cutlets from a neck of mutton
- mashed potato
- sauce an macedoine de legumes
- French beans
- a carrot
- a turnip
- a piece of cucumber
- sauce blonde
- green peas
- boiled celery

## Directions

1. Take the eight cutlets and trim them neatly
2. Grill them over a brisk, clear fire
3. When done, arrange them round a circle of nicely mashed potato
4. Place a sauce an macedoine de legumes in the centre
5. The sauce should contain French beans, a carrot, a turnip, and a piece of cucumber, previously boiled and cut into small dice
6. Heat the vegetables up in well made sauce blonde
7. Add a few green peas and some pieces of boiled celery if available
