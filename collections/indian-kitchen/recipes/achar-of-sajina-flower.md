## Ingredients

- 1.5 powa of seedless ripe tamarinds
- water
- 1 powa of washed and perfectly dried sajina flowers
- 1 powa of salt
- 1 powa of mustard
- 0.5 chhatak of powdered chillies
- 1 powa of mustard oil
- pieces of green chillies (optional)
- dried pieces of brinjals (optional)

## Directions

1. Make a thick paste of 1.5 powa of seedless ripe tamarinds with a little water and mix it with 1 powa of washed and perfectly dried sajina flowers.
2. Also mix with 1 powa of salt and a paste of 1 powa of mustard and keep in an airtight jar for a day.
3. Then mix with 0.5 chhatak of powdered chillies and 1 powa of mustard oil, dry them in the sun for a whole day and keep in the jar.
4. Pieces of green chillies and dried pieces of brinjals may be added if desired.
