## Ingredients

- shrimps of medium size
- ginger-juice
- onion-juice
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- ghee

## Directions

1. Remove the scales except those at the ends of the tails
2. Remove the heads and the black threads at the backs of a few shrimps of medium size
3. Flatten them with a hammer and wash
4. Add ginger-juice, onion-juice, powdered turmeric and salt
5. Keep them covered for some time
6. Dip them holding the tail into eggs beaten with turmeric and salt
7. Roll on bread crumbs
8. Fry in plenty of ghee
9. Serve while hot
