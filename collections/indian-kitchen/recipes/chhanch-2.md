## Ingredients

- half cup khowa kheer
- ghee
- egg contents
- sugar
- cardamom seeds
- raisins
- pasted almonds
- rose water
- flour paste

## Directions

1. Fry half cup of khowa kheer in ghee and grind finely
2. Mix with two cups of egg contents, sugar, cardamom seeds, raisins, pasted almonds and rose water
3. Beat thoroughly
4. Smear interior surfaces of casting moulds with ghee
5. Stuff moulds with mixture
6. Close with flour paste
7. Boil in water for about 15 minutes
8. Open joints of moulds and serve
