## Ingredients

- four or five large potatoes
- beef dripping
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash four or five large potatoes, scrape them, and cut them into thin strips round and round, keeping as nearly to one width as possible
2. Throw them into cold water as they are cut
3. Fry them of a light brown, in very hot or boiling beef dripping
4. Strew over them a little salt and pepper
5. Before serving, drain them upon a dish turned up before the fire
