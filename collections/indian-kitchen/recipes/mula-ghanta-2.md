## Ingredients

- radishes
- oil
- cassia leaves
- til
- pepper
- cumin
- coriander
- water
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- browned broken bori
- ghee

## Directions

1. Peel and chop a few radishes into small pieces and boil them in water.
2. Drain off the water and again wash them with cold water to weaken the disagreeable smell.
3. Brown the boiled pieces in some oil heated with a few cassia leaves.
4. Add some til and a little pepper, cumin and coriander, all powdered, with water and a little milk.
5. When it begins to boil add salt, sugar and browned broken bori, and mix thoroughly.
6. Add some ghee before taking down.
