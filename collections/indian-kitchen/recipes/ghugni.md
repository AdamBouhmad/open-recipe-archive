## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- potatoes
- powdered turmeric
- salt

## Directions

1. Shell hard boiled eggs and cut in small pieces
2. Cut potatoes in small pieces
3. Smear powdered turmeric and salt with them separately
4. Brown the potatoes and eggs
