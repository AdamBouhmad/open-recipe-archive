## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash them very clean, put them on in cold water, cover the saucepan, and let them boil quickly
2. As soon as the water boils pour it off, and cover them with cold water
3. Add a little salt, and when the water boils pour it off instantly
4. Dry them, and take off the skins before serving
