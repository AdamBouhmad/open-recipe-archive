## Ingredients

- young, full-grown, tender ducks
- beef stock
- sliced onions
- bottle of olives stoned

## Directions

1. Choose young, full-grown, tender ducks; feather and singe them as directed in the foregoing recipe, after which wash them in three or four cold waters
2. Stuff the ducks according to recipe No. 325, and bake in a deep dish in a moderate oven until brown
3. Add a good beef stock with sliced onions, and bake until the stock is reduced
4. Remove the ducks, and put into the pan the contents of a bottle of olives stoned, and allow to bake for ten or fifteen minutes to soften the olives
5. Place the ducks on a clean dish, arrange the olives round the ducks, and pour the gravy over
6. Serve up hot
