## Ingredients

- Twenty grains of cochineal
- Twenty grains of alum
- Twenty grains of cream of tartar
- One gill of water

## Directions

1. Boil very slowly in a gill of water, till reduced to one half, twenty grains of cochineal, and the same quantity of alum and cream of tartar finely pounded
2. Strain, and keep it in a small phial
