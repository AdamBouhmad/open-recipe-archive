## Ingredients

- white Patna onion
- fresh green chilies
- fresh lime juice
- Arabian dried beckty
- mustard oil

## Directions

1. Prepare onions, chilies, and lime-juice as before
2. Take a part of the Arabian dried beckty and well broil it
3. Remove all the bones and pound the fish to nearly a powder
4. Mix it thoroughly with a teaspoonful of mustard oil
5. Add the onions, chilies, and lime-juice
