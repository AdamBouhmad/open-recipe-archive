## Ingredients

- half a pint of pearl barley
- a quart of clear stock
- additional stock uncoloured and well flavoured
- yolk of an egg
- a gill of milk or cream
- bread
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil half a pint of pearl barley in a quart of clear stock till it is reduced to a pulp
2. Pass it through a hair sieve, and add sufficient additional stock uncoloured, and very well flavoured, to bring the puree to the consistency of cream
3. Put it now in a sauce-pan on the fire till it comes to the boil
4. Stir into it off the fire the yolk of an egg thoroughly beaten up with a gill of milk (or cream if you can spare it)
5. Serve with dice of bread, dipped in stock, and crisped in the oven
