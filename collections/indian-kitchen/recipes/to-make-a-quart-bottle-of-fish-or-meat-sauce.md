## Ingredients

- half a bottle of vinegar
- one ounce of cayenne
- two cloves of garlic
- one tablespoonful of soy
- two of walnut catsup
- two of mushroom catsup
- the remaining half of the bottle of vinegar

## Directions

1. To half a bottle of vinegar put one ounce of cayenne, two cloves of garlic, one tablespoonful of soy, two of walnut, and two of mushroom catsup
2. Let it stand six days, shaking it frequently
3. Add the remaining half of the bottle of vinegar, let it stand another week
4. Strain, and put it into small bottles
