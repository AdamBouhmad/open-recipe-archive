## Ingredients

- red pumpkin, a quarter
- tamarinds, two or three
- mustard oil
- ground onions
- ground chilies
- ground turmeric
- ground garlic
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Dissolve the pulp of the tamarind in the water
2. Fry the pumpkin with the ground condiments and oil
3. Put the tamarind water into the curry after the pumpkin has been fried
4. Simmer until the pumpkin is quite tender
