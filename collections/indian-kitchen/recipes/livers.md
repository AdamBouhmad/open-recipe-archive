## Ingredients

- livers
- minced onions
- butter
- water
- salt
- powdered cumin
- pepper
- flour
- lemon-juice
- pasted garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Wash and boil the livers before cooking.
2. Cut the boiled livers in small cubes.
3. Fry them with minced onions in sufficient butter and add water, salt, powdered cumin and pepper.
4. Add a little flour solution to make the soup thick if it is too thin.
5. Add a little lemon-juice and pasted garam-masalla in the end and take down.
6. Serve with bread.
