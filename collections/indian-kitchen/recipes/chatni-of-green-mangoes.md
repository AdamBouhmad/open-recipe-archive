## Ingredients

- mature yet green mangoes
- mustard seeds
- panch-forons
- broken chillies
- 1 chhatak of oil
- 1 1/2 seer of water
- 1 seer of mango pieces
- 1 powa of cleansed raisins
- 1/2 powa of salt
- 1 1/2 powa of sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the stems of a few mature yet green mangoes and cut them in two or four pieces without peeling them
2. Throw away the seeds
3. Fry some mustards, panch-forons and broken chillies in 1 chhatak of oil and then add 1 1/2 seer of water
4. When it boils add 1 seer of the pieces of mangoes, 1 powa of cleansed raisins and 1/2 powa of salt
5. Add 1 1/2 powa of sugar when the pieces are well boiled and take down when the soup is thick
