## Ingredients

- big pieces of fish
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- oil
- ginger-juice
- minced chillies
- onions

## Directions

1. Smear powdered turmeric and salt with big pieces of fish and fry a little with oil
2. Break the pieces in small fragments while frying and remove the bones
3. Add ginger-juice, minced chillies and onions
4. Fry to crispness
5. Or, boil the pieces, remove the bones, mix with powdered or pasted chillies, onions, ginger, turmeric and salt, and fry in oil
