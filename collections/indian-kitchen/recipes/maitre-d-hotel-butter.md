## Ingredients

- two ounces of iced fresh butter
- the juice of one lime
- a dessert-spoonful of chopped parsley free from moisture
- a little white pepper
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. To two ounces of iced fresh butter, add the juice of one lime, a dessert-spoonful of chopped parsley free from moisture, a little white pepper, and a pinch of salt
2. Form it with your butter bat
3. Set it in the ice box
