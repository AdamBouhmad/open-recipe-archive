## Ingredients

- half a pint of tarragon vinegar
- one table-spoonful of minced garden-cress
- one table-spoonful of minced marjoram
- one clove of garlic
- two small green capsicums
- one minced shallot

## Directions

1. To half a pint of tarragon vinegar, add a table-spoonful of minced garden-cress, a table-spoonful of minced marjoram, a clove of garlic, two small green capsicums shred, and one minced shallot
2. Cork your bottle down tightly, seal it with wax, and set it in the sun
3. In a week or two, strain the liquid and take it into use
