## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 powa of fresh chhana
- 1/2 seer of sugar
- 1 seer of water
- lemon-juice
- 1 or 2 table-spoonfuls of rose water
- fragments of ice

## Directions

1. Make a very fine paste of 1 1/2 powa of fresh chhana
2. Dissolve 1/2 seer of sugar in 1 seer of water and strain it through a clean piece of linen
3. Mix the pasted chhana thoroughly with the sugar solution and churn with the hands till nearly all the particles of chhana float
4. Mix with a little lemon-juice, 1 or 2 table-spoonfuls of rose water and fragments of ice as required
