## Ingredients

- brisket of beef
- saltpetre
- suckur or brown sugar
- one seer of salt
- lime-juice

## Directions

1. Bone a brisket of beef
2. Rub into it saltpetre, suckur or brown sugar, and one seer of salt with some lime-juice
3. Keep it in the brine for thirty-six hours, rubbing it continually
4. Remove it from the brine and clear away all the salt
5. Roll the beef tightly into a collar and secure it well
6. Inclose it in a stout duster
7. Boil it
