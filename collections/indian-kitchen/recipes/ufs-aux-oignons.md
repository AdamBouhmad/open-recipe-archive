## Ingredients

- one good sized Bombay onion
- one tablespoonful of butter
- flour
- one breakfast-cupful of cream or fresh milk
- salt
- spiced pepper
- four hard-boiled eggs
- crisply fried bacon
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Slice up a good sized Bombay onion, and fry the rings in a tablespoonful of butter till they are nice and yellow
2. Add a little flour to the butter, and when it is mixed, pour in a breakfast-cupful of cream or fresh milk
3. Give this a dasting with salt and spiced pepper
4. Put into the sauce four hard-boiled eggs cut into slices
5. Simmer the sauce-pan till its contents are thoroughly hot
6. Serve garnished with curls of crisply fried bacon, alternated with neatly cut pieces of fried bread
