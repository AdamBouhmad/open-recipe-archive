## Ingredients

- fish roes
- besam
- powdered turmeric
- pepper or chillies
- salt
- onion-juice or minced onion
- oil

## Directions

1. Beat the roes of fishes with some besam, a little powdered turmeric, pepper or chillies, and salt
2. A little onion-juice or minced onion may be added
3. Form small balls or flat cakes and fry them in oil
