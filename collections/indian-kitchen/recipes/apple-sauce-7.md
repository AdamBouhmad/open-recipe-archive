## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- bit of lemon-peel
- bit of butter the size of a walnut
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and slice some apples
2. Boil them in water with a bit of lemon-peel
3. When tender, mash them
4. Add to them a bit of butter the size of a walnut, and some brown sugar
5. Heat, and serve in a sauce-tureen
