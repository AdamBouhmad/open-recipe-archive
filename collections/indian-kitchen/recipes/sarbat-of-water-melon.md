## Ingredients

- big very ripe water-melon
- 1 or 1 1/2 powa of sugar
- two table-spoonfuls of rose water
- half a table-spoonful of lemon-juice
- fragments of ice

## Directions

1. Place a big very ripe water-melon on an enamelled pot and cut it in two with a knife
2. Bring out the red pulp of the fruit with its juice and squeeze the pulp with the hands
3. Remove all the seeds
4. Mix it with 1 or 1 1/2 powa of sugar, two table-spoonfuls of rose water, half a table-spoonful of lemon-juice and fragments of ice as required
