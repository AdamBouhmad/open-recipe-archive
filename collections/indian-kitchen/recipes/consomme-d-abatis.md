## Ingredients

- giblets of the goose
- an ounce of butter
- an onion
- a glass of Marsala
- beef consomme
- a clove of garlic
- sweet herbs
- a stick of celery
- a carrot
- a dozen pepper corns
- a dessert-spoonful of sugar
- salt
- caramel
- half a glass of Madeira
- a drop or two of tabasco

## Directions

1. Clean the giblets carefully and cut them up small
2. Put them with an ounce of butter into a stew-pan with an onion shredded finely
3. Fry them a pale golden colour
4. Add a glass of Marsala and a little beef consomme
5. Reduce to a glaze
6. Pour in the remainder of the consomme
7. Throw in an onion, a clove of garlic, a muslin bag of sweet herbs, a stick of celery, a carrot, a dozen pepper corns, a dessert-spoonful of sugar, and one of salt
8. Simmer for two hours
9. Strain the liquor
10. Colour it with caramel
11. Let it get cold and skim off all grease
12. Clarify if necessary
13. Add half a glass of Madeira and a drop or two of tabasco
14. Serve very hot
