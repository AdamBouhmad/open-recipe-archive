## Ingredients

- Ol
- Potatoes
- Lemon-juice
- Oil
- Powdered turmeric
- Coriander
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Red chillies
- Salt
- Ghee
- Panch-foron
- Cassia leaves
- Powdered garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Peel the skin thickly off the ol
2. Cut the ol and potatoes in big pieces
3. Boil the pieces of ol in water with a few drops of lemon-juice and drain off the water
4. Brown the ol and take down
5. Similarly brown the potatoes in oil and then add water with powdered turmeric, coriander, pepper, cumin, red chillies and salt
6. When it boils add browned ol
7. Season the curry when it is fully boiled with oil or ghee heated with a few panch-foron, cassia leaves and red chillies
8. Add ghee and powdered garam-masalla before taking down
