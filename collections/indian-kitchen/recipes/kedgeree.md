## Ingredients

- boiled rice
- chopped hard-boiled egg
- cold minced fish
- fresh butter
- pepper
- salt
- minced garden herb such as cress, parsley, or marjoram

## Directions

1. Toss together boiled rice, chopped hard-boiled egg, cold minced fish, and a lump of fresh butter in the frying-pan
2. Flavour with pepper, salt, and any minced garden herb such as cress, parsley, or marjoram
3. Serve smoking hot
