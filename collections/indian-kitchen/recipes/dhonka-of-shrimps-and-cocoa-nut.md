## Ingredients

- cocoa-nut and shrimps ball
- ghee or oil
- big pieces of potatoes
- minced onions
- ground turmeric
- coriander
- pepper
- cumin
- ginger
- salt
- water
- cassia leaves
- red chillies
- powdered garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Prepare a ball of cocoa-nut and shrimps, cut it in small cubes and fry in ghee or oil.
2. Brown some big pieces of potatoes and minced onions.
3. Add ground turmeric, coriander, pepper, cumin, ginger, salt and just enough water to cover the potatoes.
4. On boiling add the fried pieces of cocoa-nut and shrimps.
5. Season the curry with ghee boiled with a few cassia leaves and red chillies.
6. Add powdered garam-masalla before taking down.
