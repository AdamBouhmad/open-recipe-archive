## Ingredients

- 2 seers of the juice of date palm
- 1 powa of rice
- 1 seer of milk
- nalen gur
- very little camphor

## Directions

1. Boil 2 seers of the juice of date palm and constantly remove the scum formed on the surface.
2. When scum no longer appears add 1 powa of rice.
3. Add 1 seer of milk when the rice are fully boiled and stir incessantly till thick.
4. Take down after mixing with a little nalen gur and very little camphor.
