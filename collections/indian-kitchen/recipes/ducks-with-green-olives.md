## Ingredients

- young, full-grown, tender ducks
- good beef stock
- sliced onions
- bottle of olives stoned

## Directions

1. Choose young, full-grown, tender ducks
2. Feather and singe them as directed in recipe No. 129
3. Wash them in three or four cold waters
4. Stuff the ducks according to recipe No. 325
5. Bake in a deep dish in a moderate oven until brown
6. Add a good beef stock with sliced onions
7. Bake until the stock is reduced
8. Remove the ducks
9. Put into the pan the contents of a bottle of olives stoned
10. Allow to bake for ten or fifteen minutes to soften the olives
