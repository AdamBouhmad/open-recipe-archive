## Ingredients

- a shin of beef
- pepper
- salt
- water
- soup herbs
- white of an egg
- sauce
- sherry
- forcemeat
- egg balls

## Directions

1. Prepare a shin of beef soup in all respects according to the above directions
2. Clear with an egg well beaten up, add to it sauce, sherry, forcemeat, and egg balls
