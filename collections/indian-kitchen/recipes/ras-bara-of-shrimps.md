## Ingredients

- flesh portions of shrimps
- flour
- turmeric
- lemon-juice
- salt
- mustard oil
- ghee
- syrup of sugar

## Directions

1. Make a paste of only the flesh portions of shrimps and add some flour, a little turmeric, lemon-juice, salt and mustard oil.
2. Beat thoroughly.
3. Make small balls of it and fry in plenty of ghee taking care that they do not get scorched.
4. Drop the fried balls in a syrup of sugar.
5. Serve when they are saturated with syrup.
