## Ingredients

- garam-masala
- pepper
- cumin
- onion paste
- meat cubes
- ghee
- oil
- potato pieces
- cassia leaves
- ginger pieces
- salt
- water
- barabatees
- peas

## Directions

1. Mix garam-masala, pepper, cumin and onions, pasted together, with small cubes of meat and fry to crispness with ghee or oil.
2. Add an equal quantity of small pieces of potatoes fried with cassia leaves and very small pieces of ginger.
3. Boil with a little water, add salt and take down when no soup is left.
4. Barabatees and peas, moistened, boiled to softness and fried, may be added in place of or along with fried potatoes.
