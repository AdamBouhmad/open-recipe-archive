## Ingredients

- 1 chhatak of ghee
- salt
- 1 seer of fine flour
- 1/2 seer of water

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly 1 chhatak of ghee and a little salt with 1 seer of fine flour
2. Add about 1/2 seer of water and knead to a stiff dough
3. Rub and beat it continually with the hands for at least half an hour till it is softened to some extent
4. Make 16 balls of the mass
5. Press each ball with a little ghee to a round flat disc on a polished flat piece of marble and a handy roller of wood
6. Smear the upper surface with ghee and fold it
7. Smear with ghee again and fold
8. Press it again with the roller to a triangular form of uniform moderate thickness
9. Fry both sides on mild heat with ghee added little by little till it is crisp and properly browned
10. Serve while hot
