## Ingredients

- Dried shrimps flaked
- Saffron
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wash some dried shrimps which have been flaked before drying
2. Fry them a little without any kind of fat
3. Powder them with saffron, pepper and salt
4. Strain through a sieve
5. Bottle it and serve with bread and butter
