## Ingredients

- tender meat
- onion paste
- ginger paste
- chilli paste
- garam-masala
- cumin
- curd
- ghee
- chopped onions
- powdered pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Smear a lump of tender meat with pasted onions, ginger, chillies, garam-masala and cumin.
2. Thrust the sharp end of an iron rod into the meat and roast on charcoal fire turning round and round till deep brown dropping a mixture of curd and ghee at intervals.
3. Cut it, when prepared, into pieces and serve with chopped onions, powdered pepper and salt.
