## Ingredients

- Fish 1 seer
- ghee 0.5 powa
- curd 0.5 powa
- ginger 2.5 tolas
- coriander 2.5 tolas
- a few chillies
- cloves
- cardamoms
- 2 lemons
- 1 or 2 handfuls of besam

## Directions

1. Prepare the middle portion of a big fish
2. Make holes with a sharp point in it and cut in pieces
3. Rub them with besam and wash
4. Make a mixture of all the other ingredients after pasting or powdering them where necessary
5. Smear the pieces of fish with it
6. Fix the pieces closely at the tip of a pointed iron stick, with thread if necessary
7. Roast them till golden-brown keeping about a foot above the flame
8. Rotate the stick slowly and drop the mixture on them at intervals
