## Ingredients

- whites of six eggs
- three ounces of sugar
- one pint and a half of milk

## Directions

1. Beat up the whites of six eggs to a stiff froth with three ounces of sugar
2. Have a pint and a half of milk previously sweetened in a sauce-pan on the fire
3. When it boils, drop your egg-froth in separate table-spoonfuls upon its surface
4. A few seconds will cook each spoonful of froth on one side, then turn it over, and when cooked on the other side, serve
