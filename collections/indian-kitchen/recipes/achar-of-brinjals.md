## Ingredients

- 1 seer of brinjals
- 1.5 powa of thrashed green mangoes
- pasted ginger
- powdered mustard - 1 chhatak
- salt - 1 chhatak
- powdered black cumin - 0.5 chhatak
- flour-paste
- oil

## Directions

1. Remove the stalks of a few brinjals weighing 1 seer and make hollows within them by removing the cores with a knife.
2. Stuff them with thrashed green mangoes 1.5 powa mixed with pasted ginger, powdered mustard and salt 1 chhatak each and powdered black cumin 0.5 chhatak.
3. Close the openings with the stalks with a little flour-paste and dry them in the sun till the natural colour of the brinjals fades away and the surface becomes rough.
4. Then keep them immersed in oil.
