## Ingredients

- consomme
- carrots
- turnips
- celery
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- powdered loaf-sugar
- chopped onion
- Madeira

## Directions

1. Make consomme enough for your party.
2. Cut some carrots, turnips and celery into dice.
3. Melt a piece of butter in a sauce-pan, add pepper, salt, and a tea-spoonful of powdered loaf-sugar.
4. First put in the dice of carrots and toss them on the fire till they begin to brown.
5. Next add the celery, and lastly, the turnips with a little chopped onion.
6. Work them all together, and after a few minutes, add some of the consomme.
7. Set the sauce-pan by the side of the fire, and let it simmer.
8. Skim off the butter that is thrown up to the surface in the form of scum very carefully.
9. When clear, add the rest of the consomme.
10. Let it come to the boil, and the soup will be ready to serve.
11. Add a dessert-spoonful of Madeira to the whole tureen.
