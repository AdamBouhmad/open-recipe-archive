## Ingredients

- bright, clear consomme
- a little dried basil
- a table-spoonful of Madeira
- prawn quenelles

## Directions

1. Make a bright, clear consomme according to directions
2. Add a little dried basil with the vegetables
3. Add a table-spoonful of Madeira to finish
4. Add the poached quenelles to the soup at the last moment
