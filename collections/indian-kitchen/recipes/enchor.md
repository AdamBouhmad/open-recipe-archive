## Ingredients

- tender green jack-fruits
- water
- oil
- potatoes
- coriander
- pepper
- cumin
- turmeric
- red chillies
- salt
- bori
- ghee
- cassia leaves
- panch-foron
- milk
- sugar
- garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Rub the hands with a little oil so that the gum may not stick
2. Peel the skin thickly off one or two tender green jack-fruits and cut in small pieces throwing away the central stem
3. Boil them with water
4. Boil some oil and brown pieces of peeled potatoes in it
5. Add the boiled enchor and stir for a minute
6. Add water with powdered coriander, pepper, cumin, turmeric, red chillies and salt in adequate quantities
7. Add browned bori when it boils and put the lid on
8. Season the curry with ghee boiled with a few cassia leaves, red chillies and panch-foron
9. Add a little milk and sugar may be added
10. Take down when a little soup remains after adding a little more ghee and powdered garam-masalla
