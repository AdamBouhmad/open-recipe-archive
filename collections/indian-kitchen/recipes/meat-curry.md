## Ingredients

- Meat 1 seer
- Onions
- Ghee 1/4 powa
- Curd 1/4 powa
- Turmeric 1/4 tola
- Pepper 1/4 tola
- Cumin 1/4 tola
- Garam-masalla 1/4 tola
- Ginger 2 tolas
- Coriander 2 tolas
- Salt 1 1/2 tola
- Chillies
- Cassia leaves

## Directions

1. Make a paste of chillies, ginger, turmeric, coriander and onions
2. Mince some onions
3. Boil ghee and slightly brown the minced onions in it
4. Add the pasted spices and curd
5. Stir for about 5 minutes and add the pieces of meat
6. Brown the meat for about half an hour adding water little by little at intervals
7. Add some water or milk and a few cassia leaves when the meat is properly browned
8. Add powdered garam-masalla at the end
9. Keep a little soup if milk is added, otherwise ordinarily no soup should be left in the curry
