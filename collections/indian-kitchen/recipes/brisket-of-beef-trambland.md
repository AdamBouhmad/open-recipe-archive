## Ingredients

- two chittacks of butter
- one tablespoonful of finely-sliced onions
- one tablespoonful and a half of flour
- half a teaspoonful of ground pepper
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one large cupful of pure milk
- two wineglassfuls of vinegar
- half a dozen pickled gherkins or French beans
- six hard-boiled eggs
- fresh brisket of beef

## Directions

1. Heat or melt in a saucepan two chittacks of butter free of water.
2. Fry to a light brown a tablespoonful of finely-sliced onions.
3. Add a tablespoonful and a half of flour, which must be put in very gradually, stirring the whole time.
4. Add half a teaspoonful of ground pepper, and one teaspoonful of salt.
5. When these have been well mixed, pour in gradually a large cupful of pure milk, and lastly two wineglassfuls of vinegar.
6. Keep stirring to prevent the sauce lumping.
7. Mince fine half a dozen pickled gherkins or French beans, and mince up also the yolks and whites of six hard-boiled eggs.
8. Boil a fresh brisket of beef, and dish up quite hot.
9. Pour over it the sauce, over which sprinkle the minced pickle, and then cover the whole with the minced eggs.
