## Ingredients

- two cocoanuts
- half a pound of sugar
- half a pound of finely-sifted rice-flour
- egg yolk
- half a pound of jagree
- some grains of aniseed
- butter

## Directions

1. Extract a cupful of milk from two cocoanuts, and set it aside
2. Make a syrup of half a pound of sugar
3. Mix into it half a pound of finely-sifted rice-flour, and set aside
4. Fry with the yolk of an egg all the scrapings of the two cocoanuts, half a pound of jagree, and some grains of aniseed
5. Mix the whole thoroughly together
6. After the oven is well heated and ready to receive the pudding, pour the mixture into a well-buttered pudding-dish, and bake over a slow fire until it is perfectly set
