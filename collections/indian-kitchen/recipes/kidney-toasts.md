## Ingredients

- kidney
- surrounding fat
- pepper
- salt
- grated lemon-peel
- nutmeg
- one egg yolk
- thin toasted bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Pound, in a marble mortar, the kidney and the surrounding fat.
2. Season with pepper, salt, grated lemon-peel, and nutmeg.
3. Mix with it the yolk of an egg well beaten.
4. Lay it upon thin toasted bread cut into square bits.
5. Put a little butter into a dish, lay in it the kidney toasts, and brown them in an oven.
6. Serve them very hot.
