## Ingredients

- the spawn of a lobster
- a quarter of a pound of melted butter
- the meat of the lobster

## Directions

1. Pound very finely the spawn of a lobster, rub it through a sieve
2. Mix it with a quarter of a pound of melted butter
3. Add the meat of the lobster cut into small bits
4. Make it quite hot, but do not allow it to boil
