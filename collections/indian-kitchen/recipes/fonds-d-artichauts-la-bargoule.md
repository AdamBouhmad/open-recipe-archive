## Ingredients

- six artichoke bottoms
- salt
- pepper
- four ounces of finely minced bacon
- a quarter ounce of butter
- a quarter ounce of flour
- a coffee-cupful of broth
- one tablespoonful of very finely minced parsley
- one tablespoonful of finely minced mushrooms
- one dessert-spoonful of finely chopped white onion
- a very thin slice of bacon
- a breakfast-cupful of good gravy

## Directions

1. Scoop out the chokes and drain six artichoke bottoms of a fair size
2. Give them a dust of salt and pepper and put them on a clean dish
3. Prepare the farce by combining four ounces of finely minced bacon, a quarter ounce of butter and the same weight of flour, a coffee-cupful of broth, one tablespoonful of very finely minced parsley, one tablespoonful of finely minced mushrooms, and one dessert-spoonful of finely chopped white onion
4. Stir the mixture over the fire for five minutes
5. Fill the hollows of the artichoke bottoms with the farce
6. Tie a very thin slice of bacon over each fond
7. Put them in a stew-pan with a breakfast-cupful of good gravy
8. Put the stew-pan into the oven and bake for twenty minutes
9. Ascertain if tender, then dish up and serve
