## Ingredients

- beef marrow, eight ounces
- tincture of cantharides, twenty-two drops
- sugar of lead, sixty grains
- spirits of wine, an ounce
- oil of bergamot, twenty drops

## Directions

1. Boil the marrow in the bone
2. Mix the prescribed quantity, free of bone and fibre, with the other ingredients, excepting the scent
3. Add the scent last of all
4. If any other scent be preferred, the bergamot may be omitted
