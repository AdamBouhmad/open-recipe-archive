## Ingredients

- cholum birds
- bacon
- vine leaf
- tape
- buttered toast
- water cress
- sauce bigarade

## Directions

1. Truss the cholum birds, put a slice of bacon inside them
2. Wrap a vine leaf round each bird and a piece of bacon over the vine leaf
3. Tie the leaf and bacon in their place with a tape
4. Roast over a brisk fire
5. Serve each little bird on a square of hot buttered toast, garnished with water cress
6. Sauce bigarade should go round in a boat
