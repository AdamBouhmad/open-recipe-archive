## Ingredients

- 1 powa of ghee
- 1 seer of besam of grams
- 1 seer of ghee
- 1 powa of sugar
- 1 tola of powdered pepper
- powdered garam-masalla (if desired)

## Directions

1. Mix 1 powa of ghee thoroughly with 1 seer of besam of grams and heat
2. Stir constantly
3. Add 1 seer of ghee more in 2 or 3 instalments
4. Add 1 powa of sugar when the besam is fried red
5. Take down when it is thoroughly mixed up
6. Mix with 1 tola of powdered pepper
7. Powdered garam-masalla may also be added if desired
8. Make balls while the mass is yet warm
