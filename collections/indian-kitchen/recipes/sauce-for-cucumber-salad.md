## Ingredients

- two large Patna onions
- a couple of fresh chilies
- ground pepper
- salt
- two tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- sliced cucumbers

## Directions

1. Slice into a soup-plate two large Patna onions and a couple of fresh chilies
2. Sprinkle over with ground pepper and a little salt
3. Add two tablespoonfuls of vinegar, and allow to stand for two or three hours before adding to it the sliced cucumbers
