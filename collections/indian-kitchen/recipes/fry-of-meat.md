## Ingredients

- curd
- salt
- turmeric paste
- cumin paste
- chilli paste
- onion paste
- ginger paste
- garam-masala paste
- minced meat
- ghee
- garlic pieces
- cassia leaves
- water

## Directions

1. Mix curd, salt, pastes of turmeric, cumin, chillies, onions, ginger and garam-masala with minced meat.
2. Boil some ghee, fry a few pieces of garlic and cassia leaves in it and then the meat till the water coming out of it completely dries up.
3. A little water may be added once or twice, if necessary, to boil the meat to softness.
