## Ingredients

- twelve ounces of pounded loaf sugar
- a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
- one pound of dried flour
- two ounces of pounded ginger
- a quarter of an ounce of cloves
- a quarter of an ounce of cinnamon
- a small teacupful of water

## Directions

1. Mix the ginger and the spice with the flour
2. Put the sugar and a small teacupful of water into a saucepan
3. When it is dissolved add the butter
4. As soon as it is melted mix it with the flour and other things
5. Work it up, form the paste into cakes or nuts, and bake them upon tins
