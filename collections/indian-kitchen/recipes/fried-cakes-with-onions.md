## Ingredients

- pulse-meal
- salt
- red chillies
- onions
- oil

## Directions

1. Make a paste of pulse-meal, salt and ground red chillies
2. Add some onions chopped into fine pieces and beat thoroughly
3. Form cakes and fry them in plenty of oil
