## Ingredients

- pieces of a big fish
- curd
- ginger
- coriander
- chillies
- cloves
- cardamoms
- lemon juice
- besam of grams
- a little water
- ghee
- salt
- powdered garam-masala
- beaten yolks of eggs
- bread crumbs or middlings

## Directions

1. Boil some pieces of a big fish and remove the bones
2. Add curd, ginger, coriander, chillies, cloves, cardamoms, lemon juice, besam of grams and a little water
3. Mix to a paste
4. Fry it in ghee till dry
5. Grind it and make a muddy paste by adding curd, salt and powdered garam-masala
6. Form balls of it
7. Dip them into a cup containing beaten yolks of eggs
8. Roll on bread crumbs or middlings
9. Fry in ghee
10. Serve while hot
