## Ingredients

- big ripe white gourd
- 1 1/2 seer of sugar

## Directions

1. Peel a big ripe white gourd, cut in big pieces and remove all the seeds.
2. Make perforations all over the pieces with an iron pin.
3. Boil them in a syrup of 1 1/2 seer till it is very thick and scanty.
4. Then take the pan off fire and shake it till the syrup is mixed up with the pieces.
5. Arrange the pieces on a plate and fan them to cool.
