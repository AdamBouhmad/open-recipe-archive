## Ingredients

- the nicest meat of a full sized cold roast chicken
- two ounces of butter
- a table-spoonful of minced ham
- about half a pint of rich white sauce
- almonds
- pepper
- salt
- broth made of the bones and trimmings
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Mince the chicken meat very finely
2. Put it in a frying-pan with two ounces of butter and minced ham
3. Toss till the butter is absorbed
4. Make about half a pint of rich white sauce flavoured with almonds, pepper, salt, and broth from the bones
5. Throw the mince into a mortar and pound to a paste, moistening with the sauce
6. Pass the puree through a sieve
7. Stir in the yolks of four eggs lightly
8. Add last the whites of the eggs whipped to a stiff froth
9. Pour into a souffle tin with a paper round the top
10. Bake in a very quick oven
11. Serve quickly in the tin with a white napkin pinned round it
