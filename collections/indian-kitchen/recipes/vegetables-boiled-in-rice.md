## Ingredients

- potato, brinjal, patol, Uch-chhe, karala, beans, mun, sweet potato, sweet gourd, green banana, lady's fingers, seeds of jack fruit, radish, carrot, cauliflower, olkochu, jhinga
- salt
- mustard oil
- green chillies
- powdered mustard

## Directions

1. Wash vegetables clean, peel and cut in suitable pieces where necessary before boiling
2. If need be tie them in a clean piece of cloth
3. Boil vegetables in rice instead of water
4. Take the boiled things out of the rice with a spoon
5. Squeeze and rub them into a smooth paste separately
6. Add salt, mustard oil, and a few fragmented pieces of green chillies if liked, and mix thoroughly
7. Mix mun, ol and olkochu with powdered mustard and a few drops of lemon if irritability be suspected
8. Take together with rice
