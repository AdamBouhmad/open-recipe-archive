## Ingredients

- scraped and pasted cocoa-nut kernel
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Heat equal quantities of scraped and pasted cocoa-nut kernel and a syrup of sugar
2. Stir vigorously with a ladle till the mass sticks to the sides of the pot and the ladle
3. Take the mass off the fire
4. Make into balls
5. Press in casts
