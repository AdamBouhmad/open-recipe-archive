## Ingredients

- two oranges
- six lemons
- one quart hot water
- one pound and a quarter loaf sugar
- three pints water
- juice of six oranges
- juice of one dozen lemons

## Directions

1. Pare two oranges and six lemons as thin as possible
2. Steep them four hours in one quart of hot water
3. Boil one pound and a quarter of loaf sugar in three pints of water
4. Skim it and add to the two liquors the juice of six oranges and a dozen lemons
5. Stir well
6. Strain through a jelly-bag and ice
