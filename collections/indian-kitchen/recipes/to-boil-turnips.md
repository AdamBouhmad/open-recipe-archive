## Ingredients

- turnips
- water
- salt
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Wash, pare, and throw them into cold water
2. Put them on in boiling water with a little salt, and boil them from two hours to two and a half
3. Drain them in a colander, put them into a saucepan, and, mixing in a bit of butter, with a beater mash them very smoothly
4. Add half a pint of milk, mix it well with the turnips, and make them quite hot before serving
