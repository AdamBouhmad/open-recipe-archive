## Ingredients

- seer fish
- maccaroni
- ripe tomatoes
- Parmesan cheese
- black Leicestershire mushrooms
- butter
- sauce blonde
- pepper
- salt
- white wine (chablis, hock, or sauterne)
- gravy

## Directions

1. Divide a good cut of seer fish into slices half an inch thick and cut into portions about large enough for each guest.
2. Boil some maccaroni till tender.
3. Slice up some nice ripe tomatoes.
4. Turn out the mushrooms only (not the gravy) of a tin of black Leicestershire mushrooms onto a plate.
5. Butter a nice sized pie-dish (not too deep in the sides).
6. Arrange the maccaroni on the bottom of the pie-dish.
7. Dust over the maccaroni a little of the Parmesan.
8. Put a layer of the sliced tomatoes.
9. Arrange your portions of fish thereon with a piece of mushroom between each of them, and a slice of tomato in the centre of each.
10. Shake a little pepper and salt gently over each layer as you pack the dish.
11. Pour a breakfast-cupful of sauce blonde over all.
12. Give a light surface dressing of Parmesan.
13. Add only a very little white wine like chablis, hock, or sauterne, or gravy as a finishing touch.
14. Bake the dish for about a quarter of an hour in a quite hot oven.
