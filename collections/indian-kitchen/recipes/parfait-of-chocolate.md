## Ingredients

- a pint and a half of strong chocolate
- boiling milk
- two ounces of the best chocolate
- the yolks of ten eggs
- half an ounce of dissolved gelatine
- a wine-glass of very cold syrup flavoured with vanilla
- a pint of well-whipped cream

## Directions

1. Make a pint and a half of strong chocolate, using boiling milk, and two ounces of the best chocolate
2. Sweeten it to taste, and let it get cool
3. Take an enamelled-pan and break into it the yolks of ten eggs
4. Strain the made chocolate, and add it to the eggs, stirring continually over a low fire, or in the bain-marie until the custard thickens satisfactorily
5. Mix half an ounce of dissolved gelatine into the custard, strain, and whip it well while warm and let it get cold
6. Set a mould in the ice-pail, and when the custard has cooled nicely, pour it into the mould
7. Freeze in the usual way, using the spatula frequently
8. When the custard seems nearly frozen, add a wine-glass of very cold syrup flavoured with vanilla, continue the working
9. Pour in a pint of well-whipped cream
10. Finish the freezing thoroughly, close the mould securely, and bury it in ice for a couple of hours
11. Turn the parfait out on a napkin, and serve
