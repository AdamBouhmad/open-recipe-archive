## Ingredients

- duck
- half a teaspoonful of ground coriander
- half a teaspoonful of ground cumin seed

## Directions

1. Divide as you would a chicken
2. Cook the duck in the same manner as chicken doopiaja
3. Allow it to simmer a little longer than the chicken doopiaja
4. Mix half a teaspoonful each of ground coriander and cumin seed with the condiments
