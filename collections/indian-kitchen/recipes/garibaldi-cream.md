## Ingredients

- a pint of cream
- a pint of milk
- 1 oz. of isinglass
- sugar to taste
- yolks of eight eggs
- cochineal
- spinach greening

## Directions

1. Make a rich custard with a pint of cream and a pint of milk, 1 oz. of isinglass, sugar to taste, and the yolks of eight eggs
2. Flavour it with any essence you like
3. Strain it, and divide it into three basins
4. Colour the first a bright red with cochineal, the second green with spinach greening, and leave the third its original colour
5. Whip each separately to a froth
6. Lay a mould on ice, pour some of the red cream into it to the thickness of about an inch
7. When this is set, pour in a similar layer of the plain cream, and when this is set, pour in a layer of the green cream
8. Go on pouring in layers in the same way until the mould is filled
9. When the cream is quite set, turn it out and serve
