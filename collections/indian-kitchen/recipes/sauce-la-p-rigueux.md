## Ingredients

- half the contents of a small bottle of truffles
- melted butter
- a coffee-cupful of clear gravy
- a glass of Madeira
- pepper
- a pinch of sugar
- salt
- half a pint of Espagnole

## Directions

1. Chop up half the contents of a small bottle of truffles, and toss them awhile in some melted butter at the bottom of a light sauce-pan
2. Add a coffee-cupful of clear gravy, and a glass of Madeira, and simmer for ten minutes
3. Give it a little pepper, a pinch of sugar, and salt
4. Slowly stir in half a pint of Espagnole to complete the sauce
5. When thoroughly hot, it is ready
