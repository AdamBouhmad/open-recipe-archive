## Ingredients

- Chinre |- seer
- Fried moog-dal |- seer
- Curd i powa
- Adequate quantities of ghee
- Spices
- Salt
- Vegetables
- A little tamarind
- Cassia leaves
- Red chillies
- Entire cumin
- Cauliflowers
- Potatoes
- Green peas
- Powdered turmeric
- Boiled dal
- Fried fish (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash chinre and mix them with curd, tamarind and powdered garam-masalla
2. Prepare moog-dal as in the alternative method, only do not make it so thick
3. Heat sufficient ghee and add cassia leaves, red chillies and a pinch of entire cumin
4. Add pieces of cauliflowers, potatoes, green peas and other vegetables according to taste when the red chillies turn black, and brown them
5. Then add powdered turmeric, salt and the boiled dal
6. Add chinre when the vegetables are boiled, and boil for a few minutes more
7. Add ghee and garam-masalla before taking down
8. Fried fish may be added if desired along with the boiled dal
