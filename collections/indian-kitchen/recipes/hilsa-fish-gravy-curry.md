## Ingredients

- hilsa fish
- salt
- tamarind acid or amchoor (sliced green mango dried in salt)

## Directions

1. Throw away the head and tail
2. Cut the fish into slices of rather more than half an inch thick
3. Wash the slices in several waters with salt to rid them of all 'besine' before currying
4. Cook with the acid of tamarind or amchoor as an improvement
