## Ingredients

- good mealy potatoes
- butter
- cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel and boil some good mealy potatoes, pound them, and mix with them some butter, cream, and a little salt
2. put them about an inch and a half high upon a dish, and leave an opening in the centre
3. bake it of a light brown colour, and take out as much more from the centre as will admit of a ragout, fricassee cutlet, or macaroni being put in
