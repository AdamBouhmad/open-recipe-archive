## Ingredients

- flowers of sweet gourd
- pulse-meal or rice-flour
- salt
- black-cumin
- oil

## Directions

1. Remove the stalks and sepals of the flowers.
2. Dip them in a thick solution of pulse-meal or rice-flour mixed with a little salt and some black-cumin.
3. Fry them one by one or a few at a time in excess of oil.
