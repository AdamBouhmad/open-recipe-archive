## Ingredients

- glass jar of best French plums (prunes)
- bottle of good cherry brandy

## Directions

1. Buy a glass jar of the best French plums (prunes) and a bottle of good cherry brandy
2. Take out a few plums
3. Pour as much cherry brandy into the jar as the plums will admit
4. The next day you can add more, for the plums will absorb the brandy
5. Continue for a day or two
6. Finally cork it down for a fortnight or so
7. Serve at dessert
8. Never let the jar be empty, but re-fill it as the plums are eaten
