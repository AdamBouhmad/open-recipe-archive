## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- ghee
- cassia leaves
- cloves
- cardamom seeds
- minced meat
- minced onions
- ginger juice
- powdered turmeric
- coriander
- garam masala
- salt
- sugar
- flour paste
- pasted mustard
- cucumber
- sliced onions
- boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Shell hard boiled eggs and remove yolks by cutting on one side
2. Boil ghee and add cassia leaves, cloves and cardamom seeds
3. When spice smell evolves add minced meat mixed with minced onions, ginger juice, powdered turmeric, coriander, garam masala and salt
4. Singe meat until water evaporates
5. Mix with sugar before taking down
6. Stuff hollows of boiled eggs with spiced meat
7. Close openings with flour paste
8. Brown eggs in ghee
9. Serve with pasted mustard, cucumber pieces, sliced onions and boiled potatoes
