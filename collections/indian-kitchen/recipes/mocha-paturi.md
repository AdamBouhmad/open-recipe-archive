## Ingredients

- chopped mocha 0.5 seer
- poppy seed 1 powa
- mustard oil 1.5 powa
- sesame 1 chhatak
- salt 1.5 tolas
- green chillies
- banana leaves

## Directions

1. Keep the chopped mocha for a couple of hours in water.
2. Boil them in fresh water and squeeze off the water.
3. Mix pasted poppy seed, oil, entire sesame, salt and pieces of chillies with them.
4. Place this mass in a flat round shape, one inch thick, on 3 or 4 layers of banana leaves and cover it also in the same way.
5. Bind it, place it on a frying pan and apply mild heat alternately on both sides of the cake for about half an hour.
