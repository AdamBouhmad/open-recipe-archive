## Ingredients

- teal
- bones and giblets of the birds
- lime juice
- oranges

## Directions

1. Prepare the teal, preserving the breast of each bird entire
2. Make a game gravy with the bones and the giblets of the birds
3. Proceed as already directed to make bigarade sauce, adding a little lime juice to give it piquancy
4. Broil the teal breasts upon a gridiron, and serve them full of juice straight from the fire
