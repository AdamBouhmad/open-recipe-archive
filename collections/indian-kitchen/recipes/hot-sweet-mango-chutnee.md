## Ingredients

- A hundred green mangoes
- the syrup of four pounds of sugar and three quarts of vinegar
- four pounds of tamarinds, stoned and strained
- three quarts of vinegar
- eight or ten bay-leaves
- one pound of ground chilies
- two pounds of sliced ginger
- one pound of cloves of garlic
- one pound of raisins
- two pounds of salt

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the mangoes into fine slices, and steep them in salt for twenty-four to thirty-six hours
2. Remove the mangoes from the salt water, and boil them in three quarts of vinegar
3. When quite cool, lay them in a preserving-pan
4. Sprinkle over them the remaining salt, add all the condiments, tamarinds, raisins, &c.
5. Allow the whole to simmer for half an hour, stirring all the time, with the syrup
6. It should not be bottled until quite cold
