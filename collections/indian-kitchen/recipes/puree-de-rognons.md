## Ingredients

- daily allowance of soup meat
- six mutton kidneys
- salt
- dried thyme
- marjoram
- spice

## Directions

1. Make a strong consomme with your daily allowance of soup meat
2. Wash the six mutton kidneys and dry them
3. Slit them in halves
4. Plunge them immediately into boiling water well salted and let them remain in this bath for one minute only
5. Take them out and dry them in a napkin
6. Boil them gently with some dried thyme, marjoram, and a bag containing spice, etc., till they are very tender
7. Take them out
