## Ingredients

- three well-beaten eggs
- a pint of new milk
- three tablespoonfuls of flour
- some sugar
- a little pounded cinnamon
- butter

## Directions

1. Add to three well-beaten eggs a pint of new milk, three tablespoonfuls of flour, some sugar, and a little pounded cinnamon
2. Mix all well together
3. Fry in butter
4. Brown the upper side for a minute before the fire
5. Serve it, cut into four, with pounded sugar strewed over it
