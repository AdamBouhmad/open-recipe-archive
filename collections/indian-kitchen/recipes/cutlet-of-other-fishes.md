## Ingredients

- ginger-juice
- onion-juice
- salt
- fish pieces of big-size
- water
- eggs
- turmeric
- bread crumbs
- ghee
- mustard

## Directions

1. Mix a little ginger-juice, onion-juice and salt with a few pieces of fish of big-size
2. Boil in water and remove the bones keeping the pieces intact
3. Again add ginger-juice, onion-juice and salt
4. Leave them for some time
5. Dip them into eggs beaten with salt and turmeric
6. Roll on bread crumbs
7. Fry in plenty of ghee
8. Serve with mustard while hot
