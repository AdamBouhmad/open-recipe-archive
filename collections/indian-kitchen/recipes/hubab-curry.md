## Ingredients

- tender mutton or veal
- bacon
- white onion
- green ginger
- butter
- salt
- sugar
- curry sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton into thickish pieces, about an inch square and half an inch thick.
2. Cut out of some slices of good bacon some pieces an inch square also, but about a quarter the thickness of the mutton.
3. Cut up some pieces of parboiled white onion upon the same pattern as the bacon, and some thin slices of green ginger to match.
4. Impale these mixed pieces upon small plated or silver skewers, or upon thinly-cut wooden ones, maintaining the order: first a piece of mutton, then a piece of bacon, then a bit of onion, and lastly, the thin slice of green ginger.
5. Repeat this until the skewer is filled, then go on with another.
6. Lightly toss the hubabs in butter in a saute-pan with an onion sliced, a tea-spoonful of salt, and one of sugar.
7. Simmer the hubabs in a good curry sauce as recommended for chicken curry.
