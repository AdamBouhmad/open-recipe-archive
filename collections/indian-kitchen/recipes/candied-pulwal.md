## Ingredients

- pulwals
- hot or boiling clarified sugar or syrup

## Directions

1. Observe the same process as directed for pulwal preserve
2. Use hot or boiling clarified sugar or syrup
3. Expose the preserve to the sun spread out on fresh oiled paper to dry
