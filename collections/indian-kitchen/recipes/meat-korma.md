## Ingredients

- meat 1 seer
- onions 1.5 powa
- curd 0.5 powa
- ghee 0.5 powa
- ginger 1 chhatak
- salt 1.5 tolas
- poppy seeds 1 tola
- chillies

## Directions

1. Make a paste of ginger, poppy seeds, chillies and half of the onions.
2. Mix this paste and the curd with big pieces of meat.
3. Chop the other onions.
4. Heat ghee, slightly brown the chopped onions in it and add the meat.
5. When the water coming out of the meat completely dries up, add a little more water and boil under cover on very mild heat.
6. Take down when this water dries up.
