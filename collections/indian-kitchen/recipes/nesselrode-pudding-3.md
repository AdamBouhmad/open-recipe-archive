## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds (shelled)
- two bitter almonds
- a pint of syrup flavoured with vanilla
- ten yolks of eggs
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- a pint and a half of cream or good milk
- a glass of maraschino
- two ounces of raisins
- two ounces of currants
- two ounces of citron
- a breakfast-cupful of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Blanch and peel a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds (shelled) and two bitter ones
2. Put them into a stew-pan with a pint of syrup flavoured with vanilla
3. Simmer till the almonds are soft, then drain them
4. Pound them in a mortar, and pass them through a fine sieve
5. Put ten yolks of eggs with a quarter of a pound of sugar into a pint and a half of cream (or good milk)
6. Stir over the fire till the custard thickens, then add the almond puree
7. Strain it into a bowl, whip it, and give the liquid a glass of maraschino
8. Stone two ounces of raisins, pick and wash two ounces of currants, and cut up two ounces of citron
9. Cook them in the syrup saved from the almonds, drain and let them get cool
10. Freeze the custard, and work it with the spatula
11. When partly frozen, add a breakfast-cupful of whipped cream
12. When the cream is nearly set, work in the raisins, currants and citron
13. Close the mould, and bury it in ice for a couple of hours
