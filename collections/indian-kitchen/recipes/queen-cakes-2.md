## Ingredients

- eight ounces of fresh butter
- six ounces of pounded and sifted loaf sugar
- half a pound of dried and sifted flour
- half a pound of cleaned and dried currants
- four well-beaten eggs
- a little grated nutmeg
- pounded cinnamon
- a few pounded bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Prepare eight ounces of fresh butter beaten to a cream
2. Add six ounces of pounded and sifted loaf sugar to the butter
3. Put in the eggs by degrees
4. Add the flour and the other ingredients
5. Beat all well together for half an hour
6. Put it into small buttered tins, nearly filling them
7. Strew over the top finely-powdered loaf sugar
8. Bake them in a pretty brisk oven
