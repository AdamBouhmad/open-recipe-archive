## Ingredients

- nice sized chicken or small fowl
- fowl meat
- cold boiled tongue
- truffles
- ounce of butter
- table-spoonful of flour
- stock
- salt
- pepper
- powdered thyme
- two eggs
- juice of a lime
- mashed potato
- crispy fried curl of bacon
- tin of asparagus
- chicken broth
- almond
- cream
- anchovy vinegar

## Directions

1. Lightly roast a nice sized chicken or small fowl
2. Cut off the best meat and put it aside
3. Save all bones, skin and scraps and make a nice clear white broth with them
4. Take of the fowl meat two parts, of cold boiled tongue one part, and of truffles one part
5. Mince all very finely and mix them together
6. Melt an ounce of butter in a sauce-pan and stir into it a table-spoonful of flour
7. Moisten it with some stock and then add the mince
8. Flavour it with salt, pepper, and a little powdered thyme to taste
9. Stir it over the fire for three or four minutes
10. Take it off the fire and add two eggs beaten up with the juice of a lime and strained
11. Spread the mince out upon a large dish and let it get cold
12. Divide it into six or eight egg-shaped croquettes
13. Introduce in the centre of each a piece of truffle the size of a shilling
14. Bread-crumb them and fry them a very light gold colour
15. Prepare a circle of mashed potato and place it in the dish
16. Dispose the croquettes carefully round it
17. Between every croquette put a crispy fried curl of bacon
18. Place a slice of truffle upon each croquette
19. For the asparagus points, cut off the green ends of the stalks of a tin of asparagus
20. Heat them gently up in veloute made with the chicken broth, slightly flavoured with almond, and enriched with a good spoonful of cream
21. Give them a few drops of anchovy vinegar
22. Pour them into the middle of your potato circle
