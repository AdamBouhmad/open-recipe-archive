## Ingredients

- new loaf
- milk or cream
- favourite essence
- boiling fat or butter
- apricot or any nice preserve

## Directions

1. Cut the loaf into slices three quarters of an inch thick
2. Trim off all crust and cut into convenient slices
3. Marinade the slices in milk, or in cream if available, flavoured with your favourite essence
4. Take up the slices and drain them
5. Fry them in a deep bath of boiling fat, or butter, till a golden yellow
6. Spread apricot or any nice preserve over them
7. Serve hot
