## Ingredients

- quart of good peas
- lettuce
- small onion
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- egg
- mint
- gravy

## Directions

1. Put a quart of good peas into a stewpan, with a lettuce and small onion sliced small, but not any water
2. add a piece of butter the size of an orange, pepper and salt to taste
3. stew gently for two hours
4. Beat up an egg, and stir into them (or a lump of butter will do as well)
5. mint should be stewed (if it can be procured) with them, and ought to be chopped fine, and stirred in with some good gravy
