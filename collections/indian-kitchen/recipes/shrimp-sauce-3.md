## Ingredients

- shrimps
- melted butter
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon pickle
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Pick some shrimps nicely from the shell
2. Put them into melted butter, and add a tablespoonful of lemon pickle and vinegar
3. Heat it
