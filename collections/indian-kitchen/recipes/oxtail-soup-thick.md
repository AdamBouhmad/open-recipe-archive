## Ingredients

- oxtail tail joints
- butter
- flour
- red currant jelly
- anchovy vinegar
- Madeira

## Directions

1. Strain the soup, saving all the tail joints, and leave it in a bowl for a while.
2. Pick all the meat from the tail, pound it in a mortar, pass the paste through the sieve, and then skim the fat off the bowl of soup.
3. Take a roomy sauce-pan, melt an ounce of butter at the bottom of it, stir in a table-spoonful of flour, and gradually add soup and paste till all has been expended.
4. Let the puree come to the boil (by which time it will be thick enough).
5. Add a dessert-spoonful of red currant jelly, a tea-spoonful of anchovy vinegar, and a glass of Madeira.
6. Stir vigorously for a minute and serve.
