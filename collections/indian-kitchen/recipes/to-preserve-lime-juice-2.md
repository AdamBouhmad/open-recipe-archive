## Ingredients

- 1 pint of lime-juice
- 1 pound of double-refined sugar

## Directions

1. Squeeze and strain a pint of lime-juice
2. Put into a basin one pound of double-refined sugar finely pounded and sifted
3. Add the lime-juice and stir it with a silver spoon till the sugar is perfectly dissolved
4. Bottle it and cork it tightly
5. Seal the cork or tie bladder over it
6. Keep it in a dry, cool place
