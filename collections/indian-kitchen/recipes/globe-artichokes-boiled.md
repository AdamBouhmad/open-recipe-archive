## Ingredients

- globe artichoke
- salt
- water
- soda
- Dutch sauce
- anchovy vinegar or lime-juice

## Directions

1. Soak the globe artichoke well in salt and water to get rid of the insects which may be hidden between the leaves
2. Set it head downwards in boiling water, with soda and salt
3. Boil till the leaves part easily from the core
4. Drain it, and dish it hot
5. Serve with Dutch sauce in which a few drops of anchovy vinegar, or lime-juice have been introduced
