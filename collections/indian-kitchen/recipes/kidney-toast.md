## Ingredients

- four ordinary kidneys
- bones or scraps for broth
- butter
- flour
- breakfast-cupful of gravy
- kidney-paste
- one table-spoonful port wine
- one tea-spoonful red currant jelly
- one dessert-spoonful anchovy vinegar
- a few drops of chilli vinegar
- four squares of hot fried toast

## Directions

1. Blanch the kidneys in scalding water
2. Lift them out and dry them in a cloth
3. Make a strong broth or gravy out of any bones or scraps
4. Stew the kidneys in the gravy till they are nice and tender
5. Take them out, drain them, and pour the gravy into a bowl
6. Cut up and pound the kidneys to a paste in a mortar with some butter and pass it through a sieve
7. Skim any grease from the top of the gravy
8. Melt a dessert-spoonful of butter at the bottom of a sauce-pan
9. Stir into it a dessert-spoonful of flour
10. When creamy, add by degrees a breakfast-cupful of the gravy
11. Add kidney-paste until all is expended
12. Flavour the puree with one table-spoonful port wine, one tea-spoonful red currant jelly, one dessert-spoonful anchovy vinegar, and a few drops of chilli vinegar
13. Let the contents of the sauce-pan thicken properly by coming to the boil
14. Pour the puree over four squares of hot fried toast
15. Serve without delay
