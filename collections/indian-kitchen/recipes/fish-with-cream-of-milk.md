## Ingredients

- butter
- big fish
- water
- 1 or 1-1/2 powa of water
- 1/2 chhatak of butter
- soda
- 1/2 powa or more of cream of milk
- flour
- salt
- powdered pepper
- egg milk

## Directions

1. Smear butter on both sides of a big piece of paper
2. Wrap the middle portion of a big fish, after washing it, with the paper and fix it with pins
3. Boil it for about an hour on mild heat in a covered pan with 1 or 1-1/2 powa of water mixed with 1/2 chhatak of butter
4. Take care that the paper does not get scorched
5. Drop a pinch of soda in a cup containing 1/2 powa or more of cream of milk and heat it on boiling water
6. Add to it a pinch of flour, salt, a little butter and powdered pepper
7. Take off the paper and pour this cream of milk on the fish
