## Ingredients

- eight or ten large cucumbers
- flour
- butter
- teacupful of gravy
- cayenne
- salt
- mushroom catsup
- port wine

## Directions

1. Pare cucumbers, and cut them into thick slices
2. flour them well, and fry them in butter
3. put them into a saucepan with a teacupful of gravy
4. season it highly with cayenne, salt, mushroom catsup, and a little port wine
5. Let them stew for an hour, and serve them hot
