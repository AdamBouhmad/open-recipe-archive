## Ingredients

- large leg of veal
- knuckle-bone removed

## Directions

1. Take a large leg of veal and remove the knuckle-bone
2. Corn and lard it in all respects like a beef a la mode, reducing the ingredients in proportion to the difference in size and weight between a round of beef and the leg of veal
3. Boil, baste, and glaze it well in the liquor in which it is boiled
4. Serve up with all sorts of boiled and glazed vegetables
