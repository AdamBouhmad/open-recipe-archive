## Ingredients

- boiled pieces of fish
- boiled potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- cumin
- ginger
- garam-masala
- butter
- fried onions

## Directions

1. Remove the bones from boiled pieces of fish
2. Mince an equal quantity of boiled potatoes
3. Mix salt, pepper, cumin, ginger and garam-masala, all powdered, with both of them separately
4. Knead the fish and make thin circular discs of it
5. Arrange the discs of fish and potatoes in alternate layers in a baking pan with some butter in it
6. Bake on very mild heat with the lid on
7. Add fried onions to add zest to their taste
