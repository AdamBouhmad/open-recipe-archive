## Ingredients

- pine-apples
- cassia leaves
- cloves
- cardamom seeds
- powdered coriander
- ghee
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the pine-apples in small pieces
2. Fry a few cassia leaves, cloves, cardamom seeds and a little powdered coriander with a little ghee
3. Add the pieces of pine-apples and the sugar
4. Stir from time to time
5. Take down and preserve when the syrup is very thick and sticks to the sides of the pieces
