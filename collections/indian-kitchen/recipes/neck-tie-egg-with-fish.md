## Ingredients

- a few pieces of fresh fish
- water
- ground turmeric
- salt
- boiled and peeled potatoes
- powdered pepper
- turmeric
- juice of ginger and onion
- fried pieces of almonds and raisins
- contents of a few eggs
- rice-flour
- beaten egg-contents
- middlings or powdered biscuit
- ghee

## Directions

1. Boil a few pieces of fresh fish in water mixed with ground turmeric and salt.
2. Remove the bones and rub some boiled and peeled potatoes with the fish.
3. Add salt, powdered pepper and turmeric, juice of ginger and onion, fried pieces of almonds and raisins, and the contents of a few eggs.
4. Add a little rice-flour, if necessary, and beat thoroughly.
5. Form flat cakes, dip into beaten egg-contents, roll on middlings or powdered biscuit, and fry in plenty of ghee.
6. Serve while hot.
