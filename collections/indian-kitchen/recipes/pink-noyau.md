## Ingredients

- one quart of spirits of wine sixty-two degrees overproof
- fifteen drops of essential oil of bitter almonds
- three drops of oil of roses
- four drops of oil of aniseed
- one drop of tincture of vanilla
- one quart of syrup
- pink colouring matter

## Directions

1. To a quart of spirits of wine sixty-two degrees overproof add fifteen drops of essential oil of bitter almonds, three drops of oil of roses, four drops of oil of aniseed, and one drop of tincture of vanilla
2. Shake it well
3. Mix with it a quart of syrup and a sufficient quantity of pink colouring matter to make it of a delicate pink colour
4. Bottle it after filtering
