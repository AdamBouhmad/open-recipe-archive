## Ingredients

- long plums
- salt
- fresh chilies
- cloves of garlic
- ginger finely sliced
- peppercorns
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take the long plums, or what the natives call nar kollee bhyar
2. Remove the peel
3. Keep them in salt in the sun for a day or two
4. Drain away the salt water
5. Put them into bottles, in layers alternately with fresh chilies, cloves of garlic, ginger finely sliced, and peppercorns
6. Add a little more salt
7. Pour in as much vinegar as will cover the whole
8. Cork and expose to the sun for fifteen to twenty days
9. The plums will not be fit for use until they have pickled for six months
