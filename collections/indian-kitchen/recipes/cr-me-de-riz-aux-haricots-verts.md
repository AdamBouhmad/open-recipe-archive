## Ingredients

- stock
- rice
- French beans
- butter
- flour
- milk
- egg yolk
- sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Make your stock for soup as usual
2. Boil a tea-cupful of rice till tender, work it through a sieve
3. Amalgamate the pulp so obtained with the soup gravy by butter and flour as described for purees
4. Cut into dice a handful of jugged French beans
5. Stir the dice into the soup as it thickens
6. Add a coffee-cupful of milk, with which the yolk of an egg has been mixed, off the fire, just before serving
7. The milk of a few pounded sweet almonds may be mixed with the rice pulp if the flavour be liked
