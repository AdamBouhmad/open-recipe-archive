## Ingredients

- bruised almonds
- rose-bloom

## Directions

1. Make in every respect like the foregoing custard
2. Add some bruised almonds and a little rose-bloom to tint the custard
3. Tint the froth of the white of the eggs with a few drops of the rose-bloom
