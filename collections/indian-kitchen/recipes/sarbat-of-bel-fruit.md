## Ingredients

- big very ripe bel fruit
- water
- curd
- 1/2 powa of sugar
- salt
- half a table-spoonful of rose water
- lemon juice or tamarind pulp
- fragments of ice

## Directions

1. Break a big very ripe bel fruit and bring out the whole of the pulp
2. Squeeze it with a little water and strain out the pulp with a clean piece of linen
3. Throw away the seeds and other refuses
4. Mix the pulp with curd and sugar 1/2 powa each, a pinch of salt, half a table-spoonful of rose water, a little lemon juice or tamarind pulp, fragments of ice and water as required
