## Ingredients

- lard, a quarter of a pound
- salad oil, three tablespoonfuls
- palm oil, enough to give it a colour
- white wax, a little

## Directions

1. Dissolve a quarter of a pound of lard in a basin of boiling water
2. When cold, strain off the water and squeeze the lard dry in a cloth
3. Melt it in a pipkin
4. Mix well with it three tablespoonfuls of salad oil and enough palm oil to give it a colour
5. When cold, or nearly so, scent it and put it into pots
6. A little white wax may be added to make it thicker or stiffer
