## Ingredients

- butter
- bread
- apples
- apricot jam
- lime juice
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Butter a small but deep pie-dish or plain mould
2. Cut a thin piece of bread to form the top of the charlotte the size and shape of the top of the mould, butter it on both sides, and put it into the mould
3. Line the side of the mould with moderately thin strips of bread and butter, buttered on both sides
4. Cover both the inverted top and sides of the mould, making a case of bread and butter
5. Within the case place layers of apples cut small with apricot jam spread between each layer, some lime juice sprinkled over them all, and a good allowance of white sugar
6. When packed, cover the bottom of the mould with slices of bread and butter, the same as that used for the top and sides
7. Bake till the bread browns nicely
8. Use plenty of good butter
9. Turn it out very carefully
10. Serve with cold custards accompanying the charlotte
