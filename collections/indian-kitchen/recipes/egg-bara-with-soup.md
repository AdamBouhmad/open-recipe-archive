## Ingredients

- potatoes
- ghee
- powdered pepper
- cumin
- turmeric
- ginger
- salt
- water
- egg-baras
- minced onions
- red chillies
- cloves
- powdered garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Brown some pieces of potatoes in ghee
2. Add powdered pepper, cumin, turmeric, ginger and salt with water
3. Add fried egg-baras on boiling
4. Season the curry with boiling ghee with minced onions, red chillies and cloves fried in it
5. Boil for some time more
6. Add ghee and powdered garam-masalla, and take down with some soup
