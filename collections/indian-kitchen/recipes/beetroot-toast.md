## Ingredients

- tender leaves and stalks of the beetroot
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- hot fried toast

## Directions

1. Boil the beetroot leaves and stalks
2. Drain them like spinach
3. Chop them up
4. Heat them in a sauce-pan with some butter, salt and pepper
5. Spread them upon hot fried toast
6. Serve with as little delay as possible
