## Ingredients

- six cold boiled or tinned fonds d'artichauts
- cold boiled or tinned pointes d'asperges
- cold sliced truffles stewed in champagne or Madeira
- rich mayonnaise dressing

## Directions

1. Take six cold boiled or tinned fonds d'artichauts, fill their hollows with cold boiled or tinned pointes d'asperges.
2. Cover the surface with cold sliced truffles that have been stewed in champagne or Madeira.
3. Smother each dressed fond with a rich mayonnaise dressing.
4. Serve straight from the ice-box.
