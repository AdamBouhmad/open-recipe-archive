## Ingredients

- two parboiled Bombay onions
- an ounce of butter
- a pinch of sugar
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- a table-spoonful of previously boiled rice, or pearl barley
- a breakfast-cupful of broth

## Directions

1. Cut up two parboiled Bombay onions
2. Put them into a sauce-pan with an ounce of butter, a pinch of sugar, and a salt-spoonful of salt
3. Add a table-spoonful of previously boiled rice, or pearl barley
4. Moisten with a breakfast-cupful of broth
5. Let them cook slowly
