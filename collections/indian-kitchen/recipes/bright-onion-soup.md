## Ingredients

- shoulder of veal
- pepper
- salt
- water
- soup herbs
- white of an egg
- pure silvery white Patna onions

## Directions

1. Cut up the shoulder of veal small, breaking all the bones
2. Wash it thoroughly
3. Put it into a pan with pepper, salt, and water
4. Boil it well and remove all the scum as it rises
5. Reduce the fire and let it simmer until the meat is perfectly dissolved
6. Strain it and cut away all the fat
7. Add soup herbs and more pepper and salt if required
8. Give it a boil up and clear it with the white of an egg well beaten up
9. Slice very fine some pure silvery white Patna onions
10. Steep them in boiling water, changing the water three or four times, every ten minutes
11. Drain away all the water and add the onions to the soup
12. Boil and serve up hot
