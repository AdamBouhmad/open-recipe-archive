## Ingredients

- 1 chhatak of ghee
- salt
- 1 seer of fine flour
- 1/2 seer of water

## Directions

1. Prepare the mass as in Parota
2. Make only 4 balls of it
3. Press each ball to a round disc and fold it twice, smearing ghee at each fold
4. Repeat the process 10 or 12 times till the disc is very soft
5. Fry both sides one after the other with enough ghee on very mild heat till it is crisp and brown
6. Serve while hot
