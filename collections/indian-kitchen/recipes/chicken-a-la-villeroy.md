## Ingredients

- a couple of nice chickens
- egg yolks
- bread-crumbs
- boiling fat
- small potato duchesses
- parsley
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut up and cook a couple of nice chickens as explained for poulet a la St. Lambert
2. When the pieces of chicken have been stewed in their own broth, drain them
3. Make a rich veloute with the latter, thickening it with yolks of egg like custard
4. Dip the pieces of chicken in the thick sauce, and let them get cold
5. Bread-crumb them, and fry them a golden yellow in boiling fat
6. Drain them, and pile them on a napkin, garnished with small potato duchesses, and fried parsley
7. Send the rest of the sauce round in a boat after adding to it a heaped-up table-spoonful of minced mushrooms
