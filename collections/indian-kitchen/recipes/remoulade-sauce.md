## Ingredients

- mayonnaise sauce
- chopped gherkins
- parsley
- chives
- capers
- French mustard
- garlic-vinegar

## Directions

1. Make a mayonnaise sauce
2. Add chopped gherkins, parsley, chives and capers
3. Use French mustard
4. Add a drop or two of garlic-vinegar
