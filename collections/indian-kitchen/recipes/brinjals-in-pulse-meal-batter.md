## Ingredients

- brinjals
- pulse-meal (besam)
- salt
- poppy-seeds
- black-cumin
- aniseeds
- oil or ghee

## Directions

1. Cut the brinjals in thin slices.
2. Dip them in a thick solution of pulse-meal beaten with salt, poppy-seeds, black-cumin and aniseeds.
3. Fry them in plenty of boiling oil or ghee till brown.
4. Eat while hot.
