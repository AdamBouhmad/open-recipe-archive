## Ingredients

- table-spoonful of salad oil
- table-spoonful of brandy
- yolk of one egg
- flour
- water
- white of one egg

## Directions

1. Beat up equal parts of salad oil and brandy, say a table-spoonful of each.
2. Add the yolk of an egg, and incorporate with this sufficient flour to make a thick paste.
3. Thin to the required consistency by the addition of water.
4. Reserve the whipped white of the egg to finish with.
