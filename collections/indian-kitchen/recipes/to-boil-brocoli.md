## Ingredients

- brocoli
- water
- salt
- butter
- bread

## Directions

1. Wash it, cut off all the outside leaves and stalks, throw it into cold water as it is trimmed
2. Put it on in boiling water with a little salt, and boil it for twenty-five minutes or half an hour
3. It is sometimes served upon bits of toasted bread, and a little melted butter poured round it
