## Ingredients

- asparagus
- fresh butter or Denmark tinned butter
- lime
- maitre d'hotel or anchovy butter

## Directions

1. Gently warm the asparagus in its own tin in the bain-marie-pan (immersed in a bath of hot water)
2. Drain it from its liquor as soon as it is hot
3. Turn it carefully into a hot dish
4. Put a pat of fresh, or of the Denmark tinned butter on the top of it
5. Give it a squeeze of a lime
6. Serve
