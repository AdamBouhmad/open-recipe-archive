## Ingredients

- savoy or good cabbage
- salt
- water
- thick slice of bacon
- shallot
- parsley
- marjoram
- thyme
- pinch of sugar
- pepper
- salt
- gravy

## Directions

1. Take a savoy or any good sort of cabbage, pick it carefully, and let it soak in salt and water for an hour
2. If a large head, divide it into quarters, and even a small head had better be similarly treated
3. When satisfied that the cabbage is thoroughly clean, either steam the quarters, or plunge them into boiling hot-water, and after boiling for a quarter of an hour, take them out and drain them
4. Mince a thick slice of bacon, and a little shallot, parsley, marjoram, and thyme, with a pinch of sugar, and pepper and salt to taste
5. Put all in a stew-pan, and set it on the fire
6. As soon as the bacon melts, lay your cabbage quarters in it, and pour round them sufficient gravy to half cover them
7. Let this simmer gently till the cabbage is done
8. Lift out the quarters
