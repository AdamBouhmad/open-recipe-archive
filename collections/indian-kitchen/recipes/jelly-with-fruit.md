## Ingredients

- two ounces of gelatine
- three quarters of a pound of sugar
- three whites of eggs
- one quart of water
- the juice of one lime
- maraschino
- a wine-glass of maraschino
- preserved cherries
- strawberries
- apricots
- greengages
- raspberries
- iced cream en bloc

## Directions

1. Put two ounces of gelatine in an enamelled stew-pan with three quarters of a pound of sugar.
2. Beat three whites of eggs, moisten them with one quart of water and the juice of one lime.
3. Pour the whole into the stew-pan containing the gelatine.
4. Put it on the fire, whisking the liquid continually until it boils.
5. Take the pan from the fire, let the liquid cool, and strain it through a jelly bag, pouring it back and straining again until it is quite clear.
6. When cold, flavour the jelly with maraschino, a wine-glass will be sufficient.
7. Let it rest awhile.
8. Prepare a macedoine of fruits: preserved cherries, strawberries, apricots, greengages, and raspberries.
9. Fill the jelly mould as follows: first set it upon ice, and pour into it about one-eighth thickness of the jelly.
10. Arrange some fruit tastefully in this layer, cover it with jelly.
11. Continue layers of fruit and of jelly until the mould is nearly full.
12. Finish it with jelly only.
13. Cover the mould with ice, and turn it out when finally set and quite firm.
14. Iced cream en bloc may accompany.
