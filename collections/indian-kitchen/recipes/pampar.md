## Ingredients

- 3 powas of husked pulses of moog, peas or grams
- 1 powa of fine besam
- entire aniseeds
- black cumin
- powdered pepper
- ghee or oil

## Directions

1. Boil 3 powas of husked pulses of moog, peas or grams till saturated with water.
2. Grind them to a paste.
3. Mix with 1 powa of fine besam and beat till the mass is sticky.
4. Mix with entire aniseeds, black cumin and powdered pepper.
5. Make balls of it and roll into thin discs.
6. Fry in enough ghee or oil.
