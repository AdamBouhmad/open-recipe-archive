## Ingredients

- birds'-eye chilies
- vinegar
- blotting-paper

## Directions

1. Pick, clean, and put into a glass-stoppered bottle one chittack or two ounces of birds'-eye chilies
2. Pour over them a pint and half of the best vinegar
3. After a month's time filter through blotting-paper a pint of the vinegar
4. Add to what remains half a pint more of vinegar
5. Expose it to the sun for a few days, when the second portion will also be ready for use
