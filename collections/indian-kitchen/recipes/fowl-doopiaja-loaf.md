## Ingredients

- bread
- fowl doopiaja or chicken cofta curry

## Directions

1. Prepare the shell of the bread the same way as the prawn loaf
2. Stuff the shell with either a fowl doopiaja or with the chicken cofta curry
3. Remove all the bones of the fowl before the bread is stuffed with the curry
4. Bake and serve hot
