## Ingredients

- Ghee
- Cassia leaves
- Cloves
- Ginger chopped
- Meat pieces without bones
- Salt
- Coriander pasted
- Water
- Poppy seeds pasted
- Almonds
- Khova kheer fried and powdered
- Curd
- Sugar
- Garam-masalla powdered

## Directions

1. Heat enough ghee
2. Fry cassia leaves, cloves and chopped ginger in it
3. Add pieces of meat without bones
4. Stir till the meat turns reddish brown
5. Add salt, pasted coriander, and sufficient water
6. On softening add pasted poppy seeds, almonds, fried and powdered khova kheer, curd and sugar
7. Boil till the soup is very thick
8. Finish by adding boiling ghee and powdered garam-masalla
