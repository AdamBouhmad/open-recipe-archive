## Ingredients

- potatoes
- onions
- boiled livers
- ghee
- pepper
- salt
- pieces of ginger
- green chillies
- water

## Directions

1. Cut potatoes, onions and the boiled livers in slices.
2. Brown the potatoes in ghee.
3. Heat ghee and fry the onions with powdered pepper, salt, pieces of ginger and green chillies.
4. After a time add the livers and fry for about 10 minutes.
5. Add a little water and when it boils add the browned potatoes and salt.
6. Take down when this water dries up.
7. A little more powdered pepper may be added now.
