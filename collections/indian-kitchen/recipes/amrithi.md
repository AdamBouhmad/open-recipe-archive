## Ingredients

- 0.5 seer of sabeda
- 0.5 seer of middlings
- 3 powas of water
- a tea-spoonful of lime-water
- a pinch of saffron
- water
- ghee
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Mix 0.5 seer of sabeda and 0.5 seer of middlings with 3 powas of water and a tea-spoonful of lime-water
2. Keep aside for 10 or 12 hours
3. Add a pinch of saffron and a little more water if necessary to make a thin paste
4. Fry as before in ghee in suitable designs, the common one being a big circle with a continuous spiral rings round its circumference
5. Keep immersed into a syrup of sugar
