## Ingredients

- very ripe plantains
- egg yolk
- flour
- melted ghee
- crushed crystallized sugar

## Directions

1. Slice or divide very ripe plantains lengthways into two
2. Brush them slightly with the yolk of an egg
3. Dredge with flour
4. Fry in melted ghee
5. Serve up hot, sprinkled with crushed crystallized sugar
