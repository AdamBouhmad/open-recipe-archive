## Ingredients

- three tablespoonfuls of gravy
- two of vinegar
- a blade of mace
- pepper
- salt
- a large onion sliced

## Directions

1. Take three tablespoonfuls of gravy, two of vinegar, a blade of mace, a little pepper and salt, and a large onion sliced
2. Boil and strain
