## Ingredients

- karolas
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut the karolas in thin slices and rub powdered turmeric and a little salt.
2. Heat some oil in a pan till the foams die out.
3. Add the slices a few at a time so that they may remain merged in oil.
4. Brown the other side by turning the slices over with any flat utensil like the wrong end of a spoon when one side is browned.
5. Use slow heat, or the slices will parch in the surface while the interior will remain uncooked.
6. Alternatively, slices of karolas may be first boiled in water and then fried to make them soft and not crisp.
