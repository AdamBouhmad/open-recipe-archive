## Ingredients

- 1 seer of myrobalans
- water
- 1.5 seer of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 1 seer of myrobalans with water till quite soft and drop them in a boiling syrup of 1.5 seer of sugar.
2. Take down when the syrup is very thick.
