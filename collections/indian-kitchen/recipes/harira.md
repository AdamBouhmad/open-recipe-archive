## Ingredients

- ½ seer fine flour
- ½ seer ghee
- 1 seer milk
- 2½ powas sugar
- powdered cloves
- saffron
- raisins
- pieces of almonds
- pieces of pistachios

## Directions

1. Fry ½ seer of fine flour with ½ seer of ghee till brown
2. Add a seer of milk
3. When it boils add a very concentrated syrup of 2½ powas of sugar
4. Add a pinch of powdered cloves and very little saffron
5. Raisins, pieces of almonds and pistachios may be added as desired
