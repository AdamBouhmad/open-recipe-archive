## Ingredients

- pulses of grams
- peas
- khesari
- salt
- aniseeds
- black cumin
- ghee or oil

## Directions

1. Keep the pulses moistened under water for a few hours.
2. Grind them to a paste.
3. Mix salt and bind it in a clean piece of cloth.
4. Drop it in boiling rice or boiling water.
5. Remove it when boiling is complete.
6. Mix some entire aniseeds and black cumin.
7. Press it into a flat disc half inch thick.
8. Cut into small pieces and fry them in ghee or oil.
