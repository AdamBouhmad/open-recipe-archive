## Ingredients

- three ordinary eggs
- a table-spoonful of grated and finely sifted cheese
- salt
- black pepper
- a dessert-spoonful of rich cream or new milk

## Directions

1. Incorporate the cheese, salt, black pepper, and cream or milk with the eggs.
2. Proceed as recorded in the previous directions for making an omelette.
3. Serve just before all the juicy mixture on the surface quite sets, so that there may be an exudation of creamy moisture in the dish.
4. Dust over the surface a canopy of grated cheese.
5. Serve immediately from the fire to the plate.
