## Ingredients

- husked pulses of grams
- water
- salt
- powdered chillies
- ghee or oil

## Directions

1. Keep some husked pulses of grams under water for 2 or 3 hours.
2. Fry the moistened pulses with salt and powdered chillies with sufficient ghee or oil on gentle heat, stirring all the while, till they are brittle to eat.
