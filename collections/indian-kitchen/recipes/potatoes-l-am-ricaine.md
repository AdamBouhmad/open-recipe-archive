## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- milk
- onion
- spice
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- flour
- egg yolk
- parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut up your boiled potatoes into thick slices
2. Flavour a little milk with onion, spice, pepper, and salt
3. Strain and thicken it with butter and flour till you have a nice sauce blanche
4. Place your slices of potato in this sauce and heat them up to boiling point
5. Take the sauce-pan off the fire and stir in the yolk of an egg
6. Add a large spoonful of chopped parsley with a pat of butter the size of a rupee and serve
