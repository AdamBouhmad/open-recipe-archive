## Ingredients

- salt 1 tola
- a table-spoonful of oil
- powdered mustard
- pepper 1 tola
- a pinch of sugar
- vinegar 1 chhatak
- yolks of a few boiled eggs
- 0.5 seer of boiled fish
- beaten eggs
- ghee

## Directions

1. Mix salt, a table-spoonful of oil, powdered mustard and pepper, a pinch of sugar, vinegar with the yolks of a few boiled eggs
2. Mix it with 0.5 seer of boiled fish after removing the bones
3. Form rectangular blocks of it 0.5 inch thick
4. Dip them into beaten eggs
5. Fry in ghee
