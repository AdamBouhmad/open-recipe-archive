## Ingredients

- Kachu
- Potatoes
- Ghee
- Ground turmeric
- Red chillies
- Coriander
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Minced onions
- Ginger-juice
- Salt
- Water
- Cassia leaves
- Powdered garam-masalla

## Directions

1. First boil the kachu and then peel it off
2. Peel some potatoes and cut into small pieces
3. Brown the vegetables in ghee and add ground turmeric, red chillies, coriander, pepper, cumin, minced onions, ginger-juice, salt and water
4. Season the curry with ghee boiled with a few cassia leaves, red chillies and minced onions
5. Add powdered garam-masalla when the soup thickens, and take down
