## Ingredients

- brains
- a teaspoonful of finely-sifted flour
- grated nutmeg
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Previously boil down the brains
2. Bruise them
3. Add a teaspoonful of finely-sifted flour
4. Add some grated nutmeg, pepper, and salt
5. Roll out like piecrust to the thickness of a rupee
6. Cut into cakes of the size of Spanish dollars
7. Fry them
