## Ingredients

- trimmed globe artichoke quarters
- gravy
- bread-crumbs
- mushroom
- parsley
- shallot
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare artichoke quarters as directed and boil until nearly done
2. Neatly dispose the pieces upon a silver dish, with a little gravy round them to keep them from burning
3. Dust over with very finely sifted bread-crumbs, chopped mushroom, parsley, and a little shallot
4. Season with pepper and salt, and place a piece of butter on the top of each piece
5. Bake for ten minutes and serve hot
