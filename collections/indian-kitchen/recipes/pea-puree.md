## Ingredients

- peas
- butter
- black pepper
- salt
- sugar
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Boil the peas as previously described
2. Work them through the sieve
3. Add butter, a little black pepper, salt, a very little sugar
4. Add a spoonful of cream or good milk
5. Serve in a small mould
