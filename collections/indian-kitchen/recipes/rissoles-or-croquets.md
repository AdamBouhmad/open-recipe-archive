## Ingredients

- cold roast meat or fowl
- a small bit of bacon
- grated nutmeg
- salt
- cream
- yolks of eggs
- finely-grated bread
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Mince very finely some cold roast meat or fowl and a small bit of bacon
2. season it with grated nutmeg and salt
3. moisten it with cream, and make it up into good-sized balls
4. dip them into yolks of eggs beaten up, and then into finely-grated bread
5. Bake them in an oven, or fry of a light brown colour
6. Before serving, drain them before a fire on the back of a sieve
7. Garnish with fried parsley
