## Ingredients

- 2 seer of ripe plums
- 0.5 powa of salt
- pieces of ginger
- powders of pepper, mustard and aniseed
- 1.5 powa or 0.75 seer of mustard oil

## Directions

1. Wash 2 seer of ripe plums, rub with a cloth to soak the water, remove the stalks and crack each of them a little.
2. Mix with 0.5 powa of salt and dry them in the sun for several days till thoroughly dried.
3. Put the dried plums with pieces of ginger, powders of pepper, mustard and aniseed and 1.5 powa or 0.75 seer of mustard oil in an airtight jar.
4. Shake the jar to mix them thoroughly.
5. Keep it in the sun for 3 weeks.
