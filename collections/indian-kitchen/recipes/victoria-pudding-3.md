## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of bread-crumbs
- three ounces of shredded suet
- a quarter of a pound of jam
- the juice of a lime
- lime peel grated
- two ounces of sifted sugar
- two eggs
- a pinch of salt
- flour
- butter
- boiling water
- brandy

## Directions

1. Mix bread-crumbs, shredded suet, jam, lime juice, grated lime peel, sifted sugar, eggs and salt thoroughly
2. Place it in a buttered mould, and boil for three hours
3. For the sauce, mix two tea-spoonfuls of flour with half an ounce of butter, and add a cupful of boiling water
4. Stir in a table-spoonful of the jam you use for your pudding, the juice of a lime and a spoonful of brandy
5. Simmer for a few minutes
