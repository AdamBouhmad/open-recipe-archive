## Ingredients

- 1/2 table-spoonful of lime water
- 5 seers of milk
- powdered sugar-candy 1 powa
- powdered cardamoms 1 tola
- 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose

## Directions

1. Mix 1/2 table-spoonful of lime water with 5 seers of milk and boil it
2. Make the heat very mild so that the milk may no longer boil
3. Fan the surface of the milk and remove the creams constantly as they are formed to the sides of the pan with a very thin stick
4. Stir the milk from time to time and continue the process till the milk is reduced to one seer
5. Take the pan down
6. Scrape the creams together from the sides of the pan and mix with the milk
7. Mix powdered sugar-candy 1 powa, powdered cardamoms 1 tola and 4 or 5 drops of essence of rose with the milk
8. Serve when cold
