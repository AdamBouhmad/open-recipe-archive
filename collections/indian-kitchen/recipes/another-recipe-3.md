## Ingredients

- beef marrow, three-quarters of a pound
- spring water
- rose-water
- storax, half an ounce
- gum benjamin, half an ounce
- odoriferous cyprus-powder, half an ounce
- cinnamon, two drachms
- cloves, a drachm
- white of egg
- flour
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Clean beef marrow well from bone and fibre
2. Wash it in water fresh from the spring, which must be changed night and morning for ten days
3. Steep it in rose-water for twenty-four hours
4. Drain it
5. Take half an ounce each of storax, gum benjamin, and odoriferous cyprus-powder, two drachms of cinnamon, and a drachm of cloves
6. Let these ingredients be all powdered and well mixed with the marrow
7. Put them in a pewter pot which holds about a pint and a half
8. Make a paste of white of egg and flour, and lay it on a piece of linen
9. Place a second linen to cover the pot very tight and keep in the steam
10. Place the pot in a copper vessel of water, and keep it steady, so that the water may not reach or touch the covering
11. As the water evaporates, add more, boiling hot
12. Keep it boiling four hours without ceasing
13. Strain the pomade into small jars or boules
14. Cork when quite cold
15. Take care to touch it only with silver
