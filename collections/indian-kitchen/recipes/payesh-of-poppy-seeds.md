## Ingredients

- 1 powa of poppy seeds
- water
- a few cassia leaves
- cardamom seeds
- 1 chhatak of ghee
- 1 seer of milk
- 1 powa of sugar
- raisins
- pistachios
- almonds

## Directions

1. Keep 1 powa of poppy seeds moist in water for an hour and then make a paste of them adding water little by little.
2. Fry a few cassia leaves and cardamom seeds in 1 chhatak of ghee and then fry the pasted poppy seeds till light brown.
3. Add 1 seer of milk, 1 powa of sugar, raisins, pistachios and almonds as desired.
4. Stir constantly and take down when thick.
