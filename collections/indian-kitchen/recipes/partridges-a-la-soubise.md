## Ingredients

- partridges
- Bombay onion
- milk
- spiced pepper
- salt
- boiled bacon
- giblets
- lean bacon
- onion
- pepper corns

## Directions

1. Prepare the partridges as for roasting
2. Fill each of them with a chopped Bombay onion previously boiled in milk
3. Season with spiced pepper and salt
4. Roll in a slice of boiled bacon
5. Make a broth with the giblets of the birds, any scraps you may have, a slice of lean bacon, an onion cut into quarters, a few pepper corns, and a seasoning of salt and pepper
6. Simmer the partridges in the broth until perfectly tender
