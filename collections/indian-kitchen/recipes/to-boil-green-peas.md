## Ingredients

- green peas
- salt
- pounded loaf sugar
- butter
- mint

## Directions

1. After being shelled, wash them, drain them in a colander
2. put them on in plenty of boiling water, with a teaspoonful of salt, and one of pounded loaf sugar
3. boil them till they become tender, which, if young, will be in less than half an hour; if old, they will require more than an hour
4. drain them in a colander
5. put them immediately into a dish with a slice of fresh butter in it
6. Some people think it an improvement to boil a small bunch of mint with the peas; it is then minced finely, and laid in small heaps at the end or sides of the dish
7. If peas are allowed to stand in the water after being boiled they lose their colour
