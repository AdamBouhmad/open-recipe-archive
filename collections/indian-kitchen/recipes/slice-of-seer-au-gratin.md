## Ingredients

- a nice slice of seer fish
- small cupful of maccaroni
- butter
- 1 dessert-spoonful of minced shallot
- 2 table-spoonfuls of grated cheese
- 1 breakfast-cupful of broth
- chablis
- paper
- tomatoes

## Directions

1. Flatten out on a board a nice slice of seer fish
2. Prepare a small cupful of maccaroni boiled till tender
3. Butter a small pie-dish
4. Sprinkle a dessert-spoonful of minced shallot over the butter
5. Place a layer of maccaroni on it
6. Dust over the maccaroni a couple of table-spoonfuls of grated cheese
7. Put the slice of seer on the top of the maccaroni
8. Moisten with a breakfast-cupful of broth, in which a little chablis may be mixed
9. Put a little butter on the fish
10. Spread a piece of paper over it
11. Bake for twelve minutes
12. Remove the paper and serve
13. Tomatoes, sliced, may be laid on the top of the fish and baked with it if available
