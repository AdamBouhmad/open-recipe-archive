## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- butter
- finely pared lime-peel
- chopped parsley
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lime juice

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onion in butter till it begins to brown
2. Add broth with pieces of potato
3. Toss them till coloured
4. Add a little finely pared lime-peel, some chopped parsley, an atom of garlic, salt, pepper, and a dust of grated nutmeg
5. When serving, sprinkle a little lime juice over the potatoes
