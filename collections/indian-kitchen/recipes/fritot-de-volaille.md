## Ingredients

- a well grown chicken
- salad oil
- juice of a lime
- an onion
- pepper
- salt
- milk
- flour
- hot fat
- crisply fried parsley

## Directions

1. Cut up a well grown chicken as if for fricassee
2. Marinade the pieces in salad oil with the juice of a lime, an onion sliced in rings, pepper and salt
3. Half an hour before serving, take out the onion
4. Wipe the pieces of chicken on a cloth
5. Dip them in milk
6. Flour them well
7. Fry in plenty of hot fat accelerating the heat during the process
8. When the pieces are cooked and fried a golden yellow colour, pile them on a napkin garnished with crisply fried parsley
9. Serve with a nice sauce such as bread sauce, poivrade, or Robert
