## Ingredients

- fish roes
- water
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- besam

## Directions

1. Take out the roes of the fish and wash gently with water without damaging them
2. Smear powdered turmeric and salt, and fry them entire or piecemeal
3. Or, dip the pieces into egg-contents beaten with powdered turmeric and salt, roll on bread crumbs and fry
4. Or, beat with salt, powdered turmeric and a little besam, and fry in cakes like bara
