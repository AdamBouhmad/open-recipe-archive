## Ingredients

- one pound of liver
- one pound of udder
- pepper
- salt
- herbs
- bread-crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Get one pound each of liver and udder
2. Thoroughly wash and parboil them
3. Cut them into pieces, put into a mortar, and pound them to a pulp
4. Mix with pepper, salt, herbs, bread-crumbs, and an egg
5. Make into balls, and curry them in the same manner as any of the foregoing forcemeat ball curries
