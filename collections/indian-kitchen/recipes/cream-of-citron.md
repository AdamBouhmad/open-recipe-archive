## Ingredients

- sixty drops of the oil of citron
- one quart of spirits of wine of the strength of sixty-two degrees overproof
- one quart of syrup
- two ounces of yellow colouring matter

## Directions

1. Put sixty drops of the oil of citron into a quart of spirits of wine of the strength of sixty-two degrees overproof
2. Shake it well
3. Mix with it a quart of syrup and two ounces of yellow colouring matter
4. Filter the whole through filtering-paper
5. If not sufficiently bright, filter it a second time through some fresh paper
6. Bottle it
