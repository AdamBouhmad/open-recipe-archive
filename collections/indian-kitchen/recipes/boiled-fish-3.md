## Ingredients

- pieces of fish
- powdered turmeric
- salt
- water
- powdered mustard
- mustard oil
- onions

## Directions

1. Rub powdered turmeric and salt on pieces of fish and boil in the minimum quantity of water
2. Remove the bones and mix to a paste with powdered mustard, mustard oil and a little more salt, if necessary
3. Serve with sliced onions
