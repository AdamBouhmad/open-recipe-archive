## Ingredients

- rolled rib meat
- egg
- dry cheese
- bread-crumbs
- maccaroni

## Directions

1. After draining the rolled rib meat and letting it get cold, place it on a dish that will stand the oven.
2. Brush it over with egg.
3. Dredge over it a coating of very finely grated dry cheese, and bread-crumbs, in equal proportions.
4. Set it in the oven to heat thoroughly, and brown the outside of the crumbs with a hot iron used salamander fashion.
5. When ready, put the meat on a dish, and trim round it a circle of hot maccaroni freshly boiled.
