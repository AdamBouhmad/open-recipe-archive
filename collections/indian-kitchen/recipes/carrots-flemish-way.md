## Ingredients

- carrots
- parsley
- young onions
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Prepare (after boiling) in the form of dice, balls, stars, crescents, &c.
2. stew with chopped parsley, young onions, salt and pepper, in plain melted butter, or good brown gravy
