## Ingredients

- 1 seer of shelled almonds
- 1 powa of khowa kheer
- 0.5 tola of powdered cardamom seeds
- 1 chhatak of ghee
- 0.5 seer of sugar

## Directions

1. Keep 1 seer of shelled almonds moist for about an hour
2. Skin them and make a paste of them
3. Mix the paste with 1 powa of khowa kheer and 0.5 tola of powdered cardamom seeds
4. Boil 1 chhatak of ghee in a pan
5. Add the paste and stirring a little add a concentrated syrup of 0.5 seer of sugar
6. Continue stirring till thick
7. Smear a flat plate with a little ghee
8. Pour the mass in it
9. Cut in cubes when solid
