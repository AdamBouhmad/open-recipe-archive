## Ingredients

- two seers or four pounds of good large fresh pulwals
- bruised green ginger
- half a seer of sugar

## Directions

1. Thoroughly grate the outer surface of the pulwals
2. Half slit them and remove the seeds
3. Throw them into water
4. Parboil them in clean water
5. Remove them into a colander and allow them to drain and dry
6. Stuff each pulwal with some bruised green ginger
7. Tie or bind them with fine cotton
8. Put them into a strong syrup made of half a seer of sugar
9. Allow them to simmer until they change colour
10. Remove them and continue to boil the syrup until it thickens
11. Return them into the syrup
12. In two or three days reboil the syrup if it has become thin or appears inclined to ferment
