## Ingredients

- hare
- bacon
- forcemeat
- gravy beef
- spiced pepper
- pounded mace
- port
- vinegar
- red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Make like the rabbit pie exactly, with two slight variations
2. Mix a glass of port, half a glass of good vinegar, with a dessertspoonful of red currant jelly into the gravy
3. Sprinkle a little pounded mace over the meat in addition to the ordinary spiced pepper
