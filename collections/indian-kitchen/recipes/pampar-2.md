## Ingredients

- Husked moog pulses, peas or grams 3 powas
- Water
- Fine besam 1 powa

## Directions

1. Boil 3 powas of husked pulses of moog, peas or grams till saturated with water
2. Grind them to a paste
3. Mix with 1 powa of fine besam
4. Beat till the mass is sticky
