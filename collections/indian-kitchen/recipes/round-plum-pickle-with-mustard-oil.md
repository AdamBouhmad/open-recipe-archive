## Ingredients

- perfectly ripe plums
- salt
- cloves of garlic
- green or fresh red chilies
- sliced ginger
- peppercorns
- ground mustard-seed
- vinegar
- mustard oil

## Directions

1. Make like Round Plum Pickle
2. Pour some mustard oil over the vinegar
3. Allow it to float about an eighth of an inch thick over the surface
