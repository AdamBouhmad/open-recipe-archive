## Ingredients

- cumin
- pepper
- coriander
- chillies
- turmeric
- onions
- ginger
- ghee
- meat
- salt
- curd
- powdered spices
- water
- lemon-juice
- pasted garam-masalla
- almonds
- milk

## Directions

1. Powder cumin, pepper, coriander and chillies all together.
2. Make a paste of turmeric, onions, ginger and chillies.
3. Cut some other onions in slices.
4. Heat enough ghee, slightly brown the slices of onions in it and then add the pasted spices.
5. Add meat and salt when the spices are fried.
6. Fry the meat to a reddish brown colour for about half an hour adding water little by little.
7. Add curd and powdered spices.
8. Add water after stirring for a few minutes.
9. Add a little lemon-juice and pasted garam-masalla in the end.
10. Take down when the soup is very thick.
11. For the variant with almonds: make a paste of the almonds, mix it with some milk and condense it if necessary.
12. Add this milk along with curd and powdered spices.
