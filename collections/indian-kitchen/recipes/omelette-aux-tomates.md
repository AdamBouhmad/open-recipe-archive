## Ingredients

- three or four ripe tomatoes
- a sweet onion
- a table-spoonful of butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut three or four ripe tomatoes into quarters, pick out the seeds, and let the watery juice run off.
2. Cut a sweet onion into the thinnest slices possible.
3. Melt a table-spoonful of butter in a small saucepan, cast into it the onion slices; let them cook without browning.
4. Add the drained tomato quarters, pepper and salt.
5. Toss the sauce-pan about till the tomatoes are cooked which will be in about ten minutes.
6. Keep the mixture hot, and pour it over the surface of an ordinary omelette just as you are on the point of turning it out of the frying-pan.
7. The omelette will roll over of its own accord enveloping the tomatoes within it as it passes into the dish.
