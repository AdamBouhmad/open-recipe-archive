## Ingredients

- kernel of a ripe cocoa-nut
- hot water
- pieces of fish
- oil
- pieces of onions
- pieces of garlic
- red chillies
- ghee
- milk of cocoa-nut
- pasted almonds
- vinegar
- pasted ginger
- pepper
- salt
- powdered turmeric
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Make a paste of the kernel of a ripe cocoa-nut, add hot water and strain out the vegetable milk.
2. Fry pieces of fish in oil.
3. Then fry some pieces of onions, a few pieces of garlic and red chillies with ghee, and add the milk of cocoa-nut with fried pieces of fish and pasted almonds.
4. Add vinegar, pasted ginger, pepper and salt.
5. Take down when the soup is thick.
6. Alternatively, mix the pieces of fish with powdered turmeric, egg and bread crumbs, fry in ghee and boil with milk of cocoa-nut.
7. Add salt, pasted ginger and pepper and make the soup thick.
