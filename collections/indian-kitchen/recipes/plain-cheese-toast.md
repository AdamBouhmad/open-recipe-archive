## Ingredients

- cheese
- butter
- Nepaul pepper
- toast

## Directions

1. Cut a few very thin slices of a nice fresh cheese, or grate two ounces of a hard dry one.
2. Put the cheese upon a small well buttered baking tin, and place it in the oven.
3. Watch it carefully, and when it begins to dissolve, stir some butter into it.
4. Give it a dust of Nepaul pepper, and serve it upon crisply fried toasts quickly.
