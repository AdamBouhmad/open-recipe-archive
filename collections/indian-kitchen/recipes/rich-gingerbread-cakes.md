## Ingredients

- one pound of dried and sifted flour
- half a pound of pounded loaf sugar
- three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter washed in rose-water
- one pound of treacle
- one nutmeg grated
- the weight of a nutmeg of pounded mace
- as much of pounded cinnamon
- one ounce of pounded ginger
- one and a half of candied orange and lemon peel cut small
- half an ounce of blanched sweet almonds cut into long thin bits
- two well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Melt the butter with the treacle, and when nearly cold stir in the eggs and the rest of the ingredients
2. Mix all well together, make it into round cakes, and bake them upon tins
