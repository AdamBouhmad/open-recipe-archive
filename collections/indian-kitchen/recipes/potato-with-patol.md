## Ingredients

- patol
- potato
- green peas
- ghee or oil
- powdered turmeric
- coriander
- pepper
- cumin
- red chillies
- salt
- cassia leaves
- powdered garam-masalla

## Directions

1. Skin the vegetables very thin and cut the patols in two and the potatoes in big pieces
2. Brown the pieces of vegetables and some green peas in ghee or oil and then pour in water with powdered turmeric, coriander, pepper, cumin, red chillies and salt
3. Cover the pan
4. Season the curry with ghee boiled with a few cassia leaves and red chillies when the vegetables are boiled
5. Add powdered garam-masalla and take down when the soup is thick
