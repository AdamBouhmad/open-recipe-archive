## Ingredients

- two large shoulders of veal
- white pepper
- white salt
- white stocks of celery
- whites of two eggs
- calf's head
- calf's tongue
- fancy macaroni
- pale sherry

## Directions

1. Cut up the two shoulders of veal small, bones and all
2. After washing thoroughly, boil over a brisk fire with white pepper and the best white salt
3. Skim well the scum that rises
4. Reduce the fire and allow it to simmer until the meat falls off the bones
5. Strain the soup and let it cool
6. Thoroughly free it of all fat
7. Return it into a clean digester and add more salt and white pepper if necessary, and some white stocks of celery
8. Boil it and clear it with the whites of two eggs well beaten up
9. Strain through flannel and set aside
10. Take the best and most transparent parts of a calf's head and the tongue, and boil perfectly tender without reducing them to shreds
11. Remove all the scum that rises to the surface
12. Lay the boiled tongue and meat out on a clean dish
13. Slice the tongue fine and cut out all manner of devices, such as diamonds, squares, circles, hearts, stars, etc.
14. Do the same with the best and cleanest parts of meat selected from the head
15. Take care that no particles of scum or other impurities be adhering to them
16. Where any does adhere, rinse it off in a little of the cleared soup
17. Place them carefully into the tureen in which it is purposed to serve up the soup
18. If fancy macaroni be procurable, boil a tablespoonful tender, free of all particles of dust or powder, and add to the cut-up meat and tongue
19. Over the meat and macaroni pour the boiling-hot soup
20. Add to it a wineglassful of the palest sherry and serve up hot
