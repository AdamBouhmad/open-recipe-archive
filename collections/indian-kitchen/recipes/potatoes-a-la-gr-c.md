## Ingredients

- Bombay onion
- potato
- butter
- cream
- pepper
- yolks of two eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil a Bombay onion very soft
2. Beat it hot with four times its bulk of potato
3. Add butter, cream, pepper, the yolks of two eggs, and salt
4. Pass the whole through the sieve
5. Roll this mixture into balls
6. Treat them as laid down for the Duchesse
