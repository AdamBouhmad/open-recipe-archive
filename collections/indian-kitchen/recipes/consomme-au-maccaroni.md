## Ingredients

- maccaroni
- milk or weak stock
- consomme
- sherry
- tabasco or chilli vinegar
- grated Parmesan

## Directions

1. Proceed to make a bright clear consomme in the usual way
2. Boil till tender in milk or weak stock some pieces of maccaroni
3. When done, drain them and let them get cold
4. Cut them into thin rings
5. Add them to your consomme just before serving with a dessert-spoonful of sherry to which a few drops of tabasco or chilli vinegar have been added
6. Let a plate of grated Parmesan be handed round with the soup
