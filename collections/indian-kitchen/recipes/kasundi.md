## Ingredients

- mature yet green mangoes
- 1 powa of salt
- 1 seer of mango pieces
- pasted ginger 1 chhatak
- powdered chillies 1½ chhataks
- powdered mustard 3 chhataks
- seedless ripe tamarind 1 chhatak
- salt 1 chhatak
- 1 powa of mustard oil

## Directions

1. Peel some mature yet green mangoes and cut them in thin long pieces throwing away the seeds if possible
2. Mix 1 powa of salt with 1 seer of such pieces and keep in the sun for a day or two till the pieces are quite soft and some juice is gathered at the bottom
3. Drain out the juice and mix the pieces with pasted ginger 1 chhatak, powdered chillies 1½ chhataks, powdered mustard 3 chhataks, seedless ripe tamarind 1 chhatak and salt 1 chhatak
4. Heat 1 powa of mustard oil in an aluminium pan, add the spiced pieces, mix thoroughly and take down
5. Preserve them when cold in an airtight stone, porcelain, glass or earthen jar
