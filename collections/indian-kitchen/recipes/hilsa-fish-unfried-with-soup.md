## Ingredients

- hilsa fish
- salt
- turmeric
- mustard
- water
- cassia leaves
- green chillies
- ginger

## Directions

1. Flake a fresh hilsa fish and remove the fins
2. Wash clean so that it does not require any more washing after being cut in pieces
3. Smear the pieces with salt, pasted turmeric and enough pasted mustard
4. Boil water with cassia leaves, green chillies cut in halves and pasted ginger
5. Add the fish mixed with spices
6. Take down after properly boiling
7. Keep thin soup
