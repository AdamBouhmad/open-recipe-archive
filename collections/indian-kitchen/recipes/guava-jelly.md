## Ingredients

- ripe guavas
- fresh clean water
- good clean sugar
- lime-juice (ten large juicy limes to every hundred guavas)

## Directions

1. Select ripe guavas, and as they are peeled and quartered throw them into a large bowl of fresh clean water
2. Boil them in as much other clean water as will only cover the fruit
3. When perfectly tender, so as to dissolve to the touch, strain through a fine sieve or towel without breaking or pressing the fruit
4. Allow it to drip through for twelve to eighteen or twenty-four hours if necessary
5. Put the juice on the fire again without a cover to the preserving-pan
6. Boil and skim well
7. Add gradually good clean sugar to your taste
8. When nearly done, add lime-juice in the proportion of ten large juicy limes to every hundred guavas
9. After it has boiled until no more scum rises, and the jelly is quite clear, pour it while warm into glass or stone jars
10. Cork them down when quite cold
