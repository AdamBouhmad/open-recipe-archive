## Ingredients

- a coonkee or half a pound of bassmuttee or other fine-grain rice
- a chicken
- sliced ginger
- sliced onions
- a few bay-leaves
- some peppercorns
- a few hot spice
- a dessertspoonful of salt
- one chittack or two ounces of butter
- water

## Directions

1. Pick and wash in several waters a coonkee or half a pound of the bassmuttee or other fine-grain rice
2. Add to it, cleaned and cut up, a chicken, some sliced ginger, sliced onions, a few bay-leaves, some peppercorns, a few hot spice, a dessertspoonful of salt, one chittack or two ounces of butter, and water sufficient to cover the whole
3. Simmer over a slow fire until the chicken becomes perfectly tender and the rice quite pappy
4. Serve up hot
