## Ingredients

- Potato 0.5 seer
- ghee 1 chhatak
- curd 1 chhatak
- almonds 2.5 tolas
- coriander 1 tola
- salt 1 tola
- pepper 1/4 tola
- turmeric 1/4 tola
- sugar 1/4 tola
- garam-masalla 1/4 tola
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Boil ghee in a pan and brown the pieces of potatoes in it
2. Add to them almonds, coriander, pepper, turmeric, salt and lemon-juice, all pasted together
3. Stir for a few minutes
4. Add curd and sugar and stir for two minutes more
5. Add water
6. Add powdered garam-masalla when the soup is thick
7. Take down
