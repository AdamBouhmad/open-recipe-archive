## Ingredients

- a breakfast-cupful of rich cream
- a tea-spoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Mix a tea-spoonful of salt with a large breakfast-cupful of rich cream
2. Stir it well
3. Pour the cream into a slop basin in which a clean piece of soft linen has been laid
4. When the cream has been thus turned into the cloth, draw the ends of the cloth together, holding the cream as it were in a bag
5. Tie it tightly with string
6. Hang the bag in a cool place to drip
7. When the dripping of moisture from the bag ceases, the cheese is ready
8. Take the bag down, turn the contents out into a clean cloth
9. Mould it into a circular form, or shape it in a neat square
10. Serve it on a dish garnished with green leaves
11. Let it stand in the ice-box till wanted
12. Use a porous sort of cloth for the operation, so as to encourage the escape of the whey from the cheese
13. It is sometimes advisable to change the cloth during the draining process
