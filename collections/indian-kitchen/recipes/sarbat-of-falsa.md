## Ingredients

- 1 seer of very ripe falsa fruits
- 1 powa of water
- 3 or 4 green legumes of tamarind
- 1/2 seer of water
- 1 1/2 powa of sugar
- salt
- 3 or 4 tea-spoonfuls of rose water
- fragments of ice

## Directions

1. Squeeze 1 seer of very ripe falsa fruits with the hands, mix with 1 powa of water and strain through a clean piece of linen
2. Boil fully 3 or 4 green legumes of tamarind and squeeze with the hands
3. Mix with 1 powa of water and strain out the juice
4. Mix the two together with 1/2 seer of water, 1 1/2 powa of sugar, a pinch of salt, 3 or 4 tea-spoonfuls of rose water and fragments of ice as required
