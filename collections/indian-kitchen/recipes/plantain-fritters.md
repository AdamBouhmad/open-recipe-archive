## Ingredients

- twelve ripe plantains
- four tablespoonfuls of finely-sifted flour
- half a cupful of milk
- sugar to taste
- cardamom seeds
- caraway seeds
- two eggs, beaten up
- melted ghee

## Directions

1. Prepare a batter of twelve ripe plantains, four tablespoonfuls of finely-sifted flour, half a cupful of milk, sugar to taste, and cardamom and caraway seeds, with a couple of eggs beaten up
2. Mix the whole well together
3. Make into small cakes by pouring a tablespoonful at a time of the mixture into melted ghee
4. Fry them on both sides to a good brown colour
5. Serve up hot
