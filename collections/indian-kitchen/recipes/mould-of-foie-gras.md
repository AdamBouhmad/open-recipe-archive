## Ingredients

- small tin of Pate de foie gras
- thick slice of fat bacon
- spiced pepper
- minced shallot
- parsley
- marjoram
- finely minced liver
- crumb of a roll
- stock

## Directions

1. Cut a thick slice of fat bacon into dice, and try it with a seasoning of spiced pepper, some minced shallot, parsley, and marjoram
2. When the bacon is melted, add some finely minced liver
3. Fry it in the hot bacon, and then turn the contents of your saide-pan into a bowl to cool
4. Break up the pate, pick out the truffles, and foie gras, and place them on one side
5. Pound the pate forcemeat, and the cooled liver and bacon, together with the crumb of a roll, soaked in a stock, say about a quarter the bulk of the meat
6. Pass this through the sieve
7. Choose a nice sized plain mould, butter it, and pack it with alternate layers of forcemeat, and foie gras, dotting the truffles in according to fancy
8. Steam the mould in your bain-marie for three-quarters of an hour
9. Let it get cold, turn it out, glaze, and ice it
10. Serve with dry toast
