## Ingredients

- potatoes
- yolks of two or three eggs
- a gill of cream
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- butter
- egg
- very fine stale bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Well work potatoes through the sieve
2. Enrich with the yolks of two or three eggs and a gill of cream
3. Give a delicate flavouring of salt, pepper, nutmeg, and chopped parsley
4. Roll into balls
5. Either fry gently in butter, or broom in the oven on a buttered tin
6. Brush over with egg and bread crumb with very fine stale crumbs
