## Ingredients

- cold meat
- ready-boiled peas
- cauliflower
- cabbage
- potatoes
- turnips
- carrots
- pepper
- salt
- sliced ginger
- good stock

## Directions

1. Put into a pot cold meat cut into thin slices two inches square, with ready-boiled peas, cauliflower, cabbage, potatoes, turnips, and carrots cut up, with pepper, salt, and sliced ginger, and with as much good stock as will cover the meat and vegetables.
2. Allow the whole to simmer until the meat and vegetables have absorbed half the stock.
3. Serve it up bubbling and squeaking.
