## Ingredients

- tender mutton
- green ginger
- salt
- butter
- white onions

## Directions

1. Cut up about a pound of very tender mutton without any bone, and stir the pieces about in a big bowl with a dessert-spoonful of pounded green ginger, and a sprinkling of salt.
2. Melt a quarter of a pound of butter in a stewpan, and throw into it a couple of white onions cut into rings.
