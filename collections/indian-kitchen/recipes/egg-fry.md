## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- powdered or pasted turmeric
- salt
- flour or powdered rice

## Directions

1. Shell hard boiled eggs
2. Cut lengthwise and crosswise on both ends
3. Smear with powdered or pasted turmeric and salt
4. Dip into thick solution of flour or powdered rice mixed with salt
5. Fry in plenty of ghee
6. Eggs may also be halved and fried
