## Ingredients

- 1 chhatak of ghee
- 1 seer of flour or middlings
- 1/2 seer of sugar
- 2 1/2 powas of water

## Directions

1. Mix 1 chhatak of ghee with 1 seer of flour or middlings and again mix with 1/2 seer of sugar.
2. Then make a very stiff paste with about 2 1/2 powas of water.
3. Make balls of it, press them a little flat and fry in plenty of ghee till brown.
