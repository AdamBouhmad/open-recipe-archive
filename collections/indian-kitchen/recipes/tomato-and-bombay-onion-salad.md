## Ingredients

- raw ripe tomatoes
- Bombay onion
- oil
- tarragon or aromatic vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- minced fines herbes
- strips of green capsicum

## Directions

1. Slice raw ripe tomatoes with a Bombay onion
2. Apply the plain salad dressing, using an abundant allowance of oil
3. Use a freer allowance of vinegar than with lettuce, as tomatoes are sweet
4. Sprinkle minced fines herbes over the whole
5. Add strips of green capsicum
