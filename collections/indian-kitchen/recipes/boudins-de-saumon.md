## Ingredients

- butter
- a good rich custard with savoury seasoning of salt and pepper
- a tin of salmon
- Hollandaise sauce

## Directions

1. Butter China ramaquin cases or small dariole moulds, one for each guest
2. Make a good rich custard with salt and pepper seasoning instead of sugar
3. Empty a tin of salmon and choose a nice piece for each mould
4. Place the pieces in a colander and remove all oily liquid with cold water
5. Dry with a clean cloth
6. Put them into the moulds, pouring the custard round them
7. Steam the moulds until set
8. Turn out the boudins carefully
9. Send round hot with Hollandaise sauce
