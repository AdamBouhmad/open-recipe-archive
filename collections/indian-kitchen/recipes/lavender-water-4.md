## Ingredients

- two drachms of oil of lavender
- half a drachm of oil of bergamot
- a drachm of essence of musk
- thirteen ounces of spirits of wine
- five ounces of water

## Directions

1. Mix two drachms of oil of lavender, half a drachm of oil of bergamot, a drachm of essence of musk, thirteen ounces of spirits of wine, and five ounces of water
2. Let it stand a week
