## Ingredients

- a quart of claret
- a bottle of soda-water
- a wineglassful of curaçao
- sugar
- a handful of picked and bruised mint-leaves
- a seer of crushed ice
- a quart of champagne

## Directions

1. Empty into a punchbowl a quart of claret and a bottle of soda-water
2. add a wineglassful of curaçao
3. sweeten to taste with sugar
4. throw in a handful of picked and bruised mint-leaves, with a seer of crushed ice
5. add a quart of champagne
6. stir briskly
7. serve up
