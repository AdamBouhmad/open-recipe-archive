## Ingredients

- two seers of water
- one seer of rice

## Directions

1. Heat water in a pan with two seers of water for every seer of rice
2. When water boils pour the rice, washed clean, in it and close the lid
3. After a few minutes when the boiling water becomes practically unmanageable let the surplus steam escape from the pan either by removing the lid entirely or by pushing it to one side
4. Take the minimum amount of water which will be enough for boiling them properly and yet not in excess to be removed
5. If in the middle of the preparation the water runs short add a little more water to prevent the rice from being scorched
6. Use slow heat in this method
