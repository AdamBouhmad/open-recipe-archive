## Ingredients

- oysters
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- brown sauce
- minced mushroom
- shallot
- truffle
- egg yolks
- lime juice
- batter

## Directions

1. Split each oyster open almost as wide as a kidney
2. Toss on the fire in butter with pepper, salt and a spoonful of rich brown sauce, a dessert-spoonful each of minced mushroom, shallot, and truffle
3. Thicken this with the yolks of two eggs
4. Give it the juice of a lime and let it get cold
5. Put a small allowance of this in each oyster
6. Shut the sides together
7. Dip it in the batter
8. Fry immediately
