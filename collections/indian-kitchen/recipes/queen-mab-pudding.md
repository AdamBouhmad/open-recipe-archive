## Ingredients

- a pint and a half of milk
- a few drops of lemon essence
- sugar
- six yolks
- an ounce of gelatine
- a little milk
- two ounces of preserved cherries or finely chopped apricots
- one ounce of citron peel or preserved ginger
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil a pint and a half of milk with a few drops of lemon essence and sugar to taste
2. Strain, and let it get cold
3. Beat up six yolks, and pour the flavoured milk upon them
4. Put this into the bain-marie, and stir gently over the fire until it thickens
5. Whip it well
6. Dissolve an ounce of gelatine in a little milk, add it to the above
7. Stir the custard until nearly cold
8. Pour the mixture by degrees into a buttered mould, adding two ounces of preserved cherries or finely chopped apricots and one ounce of citron peel or preserved ginger cut very small in layers, setting them carefully
9. When the mould is quite set, turn it out
