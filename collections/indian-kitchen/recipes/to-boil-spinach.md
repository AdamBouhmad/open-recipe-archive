## Ingredients

- spinach
- water
- salt
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Pick it very carefully, and wash it thoroughly two or three times in plenty of cold water
2. Put it on in boiling water with a little salt
3. Let it boil nearly twenty minutes
4. Put it into a colander, hold it under the water-cock, and let the water run on it for a minute
5. Put it into a saucepan, beat it perfectly smooth with a beater or wooden spoon
6. Add a bit of butter and three tablespoonfuls of cream, mix it well together, and make it hot before serving
7. When dished, it is scored in squares with the back of a knife
