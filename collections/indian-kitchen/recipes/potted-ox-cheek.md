## Ingredients

- leftover ox-cheek meat
- liquor from boiling the cheek
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- vinegar
- mustard

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into small bits, and heat up with a little of the liquor in which the cheek was boiled, seasoned with pepper, salt, nutmeg, and a little vinegar
2. Put into a mould, and turn out when required for use
3. Eat with mustard and vinegar
