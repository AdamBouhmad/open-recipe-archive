## Ingredients

- a full grown rabbit
- bacon
- butter
- sweet salad oil
- bread-crumbs
- grated mild cheese
- spiced pepper
- salt
- egg
- savouiry rice or maccaroni an parmesam
- puree of vegetable or petit pois
- Sauce Milanaise

## Directions

1. Detach from either side of the back bone the deep strips of meat which run from the shoulder to the end of the loin
2. Cut out each strip whole, and remove the small nerves or sinews of each
3. Cut each strip into three equal portions, cross-wise
4. Flatten them with a bat
5. Trim them neatly
6. Lard them with bacon
7. Lay them upon a buttered tin in the oven sufficiently long to stiffen them
8. Take them out, place them under a weight, and when quite cold and firm, dip them into some melted butter or sweet salad oil
9. Roll them in a mixture of bread-crumbs and grated mild cheese, in equal proportions
10. Dust over them some spiced pepper, and a little salt and let them rest to set thoroughly
11. Dip them carefully in a soup plate containing an egg beaten up
12. Roll them in very fine bread-crumbs
13. When wanted, fry them in abundance of fat
14. Serve a pale golden yellow, round a circle of savoury rice, or maccaroni an parmesam, with the centre of the circle containing any nice puree of vegetable, or petit pois
15. Accompany with Sauce Milanaise
