## Ingredients

- a tin of foie gras
- chicken chaud-froid glaze
- ground rice
- butter
- points d'asperges, flageolets, or a macedoine
- pure cream

## Directions

1. Open a tin of foie gras and slice it very carefully in slices about half an inch thick
2. Out of these slices trim some nicely shaped fillets, Cashmere shawl patterns, or ovals
3. Put them on a flat dish and mask them with chicken chaud-froid glaze
4. When the glaze has set, trim each fillet neatly
5. Set them in a circle with their masked sides outwards upon a socle of ground rice which should be spread with butter for them to adhere to it
6. Fill the centre of the circle with points d'asperges, flageolets, or a macedoine, moistened with pure cream
7. Serve very cold
