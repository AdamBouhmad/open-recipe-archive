## Ingredients

- whiting
- two ounces of camphor
- a few drops of brandy
- half an ounce of powdered myrrh

## Directions

1. Scrape as much whiting to a fine powder as will fill a pint pot
2. Moisten two ounces of camphor with a few drops of brandy
3. Rub it into a powder
4. Mix with the whiting half an ounce of powdered myrrh
5. Bottle it, and keep it well corked down, taking small quantities out in a separate bottle for daily use
