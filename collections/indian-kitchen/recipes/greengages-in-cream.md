## Ingredients

- greengages
- one ounce of gelatine
- liqueur-glass of maraschino
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Turn the greengages out into a glass dish.
2. Dissolve an ounce of gelatine and stir it with a liqueur-glass of maraschino into the syrup.
3. Set the dish in the ice-box to get firm and cold.
4. Serve with a canopy of whipped cream resting on the surface of the congealed fruit.
