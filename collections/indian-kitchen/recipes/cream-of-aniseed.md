## Ingredients

- twenty drops of essential oil of aniseed
- one quart of spirits of wine
- one quart of syrup

## Directions

1. Put twenty drops of essential oil of aniseed in a quart of spirits of wine
2. After shaking it well, mix with it a quart of syrup
3. Filter and put it in bottles
