## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
- a handful of spinach
- pepper
- soft roes of two herrings a la sardine from the tin
- a large bunch of curly parsley
- a dozen capers

## Directions

1. Make a quarter of a pound of fresh butter from cream that you have set at home, and put it in ice
2. Boil a handful of spinach till tender, and then pass it through the sieve
3. Save the pulp carefully and give it a dust of pepper
4. Take the roes (soft) of two herrings a la sardine from the tin, wipe them carefully to get rid of the oil
5. Pound them in a mortar, and pass them through the hair sieve
6. Mince very finely a large bunch of curly parsley, so as to have at least a heaped up table-spoonful of it when minced
7. Mince a dozen capers
8. Mix the whole of the ingredients together with a wooden butter bat, and shape it as you like
9. Set it again in ice till wanted
