## Ingredients

- fresh quicklime, the size of a walnut
- water, one pint
- vinegar, a quarter of a pint

## Directions

1. Drop a lump of fresh quicklime the size of a walnut into a pint of water
2. Let it stand all night
3. Pour the water off clear from sediment
4. Add a quarter of a pint of the best vinegar
5. Wash the head with the mixture, wetting only the roots of the hair
