## Ingredients

- peaches
- strong syrup
- brandy

## Directions

1. Clean the peaches, slit them with a silver or plated knife, and remove the stones
2. Have a very strong syrup ready, and while it is boiling hot throw in the peaches, and let them stand over a slow fire for six to eight hours
3. Remove them from the fire, and twelve hours after drain off the syrup and reboil it
4. Return the fruit into the syrup, and if it shows any disposition to ferment, boil it again
5. When satisfied it will not ferment any more, add a little brandy, say a wineglassful to every fifty peaches, and boil the whole over a slow fire for two hours
6. Bottle when quite cold
7. The kernels from the stones may be put in if desired
