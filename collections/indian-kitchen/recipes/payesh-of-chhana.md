## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 powa of sugar
- 1/2 seer of pasted fresh chhana
- 2 seers of milk
- 1 chhatak of raisins
- 1/2 chhatak of pieces of almonds
- 1 1/2 tola of pieces of pistachios
- half a table-spoonful of rose water or 3 or 4 drops of essence of rose

## Directions

1. Heat a syrup of 1 1/2 powa of sugar.
2. When it is warm add 1/2 seer of pasted fresh chhana and stir constantly.
3. Take down when they are perfectly blended together and keep covered.
4. Boil 2 seers of milk till it is reduced to half and then add the mixture of chhana and sugar.
5. Boil on mild heat and stir constantly.
6. A little later add 1 chhatak of raisins, pieces of almonds 1/2 chhatak and pieces of pistachios 1 1/2 tola.
7. Take down when thick after mixing with half a table-spoonful of rose water or 3 or 4 drops of essence of rose.
