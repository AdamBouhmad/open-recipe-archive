## Ingredients

- fish (1 seer, weighing between 3 to 8 seers)
- potato (1/2 seer)
- onions (1/2 powa)
- oil (3 chhataks)
- ghee (1/2 powa)
- curd (1/2 powa)
- salt (2 tolas)
- ginger (1 1/2 chhatak)
- turmeric (1 1/2 tola)
- cumin (2 tolas)
- pepper (1 tola)
- coriander (1/2 tola)
- garam-masalla (1/2 tola)
- chillies
- cassia leaves

## Directions

1. Make a paste of all the spices including onions except half the turmeric and garam-masalla which are to be pasted separately
2. Smear the pasted turmeric and half the salt with the pieces of fish
3. Cut the potatoes in big pieces and brown them separately in oil
4. Boil half the ghee and add the pasted spices, stirring constantly
5. Add curd when the spices turn reddish brown and stir again, adding water little by little when necessary (about 15 minutes)
6. Add the potatoes and stir for a couple of minutes
7. Add sufficient water and when it boils add fish and salt
8. Season the curry when it is boiled with ghee boiled with cassia leaves
9. Add garam-masalla before taking down
10. Keep a little fairly thick soup
