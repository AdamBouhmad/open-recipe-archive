## Ingredients

- turmeric paste
- pepper powder
- cumin powder
- ginger paste
- onion paste
- garam-masala powder
- curd
- salt
- meat slices
- ghee
- water

## Directions

1. Make a paste of turmeric, pepper, cumin, ginger, onions and garam-masala, and mix it with curd and salt.
2. Smear slices of meat 3 inches by 2 inches by 0.5 inch with this mixture.
3. Place them in a layer on hot ghee in a pan and heat slowly placing some weights on them so that they may not shrink.
4. Sprinkle water at intervals to make them soft.
