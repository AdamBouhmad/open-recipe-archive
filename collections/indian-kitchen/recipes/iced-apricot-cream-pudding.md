## Ingredients

- finger biscuits or slices of sponge cake
- jam
- cold custard
- liqueur
- half an ounce of dissolved gelatine
- chopped crystallized apricots
- coffee-cupful of rich cream

## Directions

1. Make a cake case with finger biscuits or slices of sponge cake and jam and cold custard
2. Flavour it with a dash of liqueur and mix into it half an ounce of dissolved gelatine
3. Work the mixture in the ice pail until it begins to freeze well
4. Stir into it a good allowance of chopped crystallized apricots and a coffee-cupful of rich cream well whipped
5. Mix well in the ice pail and then fill a mould with the frozen mixture
6. Completely cover it with lumps of ice
7. Leave it thus for an hour
8. Turn it out and serve
