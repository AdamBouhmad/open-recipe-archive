## Ingredients

- arum
- 1 seer of arum pieces
- 1 seer of green tamarind leaves or 1/2 seer of tamarinds
- 1 chhatak of salt
- 1/2 powa of mustard paste
- mustard oil

## Directions

1. Peel an arum and cut in big pieces
2. Boil 1 seer of the pieces with 1 seer of green tamarind leaves or 1/2 seer of tamarinds till soft
3. Wash them with fresh water
4. Smear with 1 chhatak of salt and a paste of 1/2 powa of mustards
5. Dry them in the sun for 2 or 3 days
6. Keep them immersed in plenty of mustard oil
7. Let at least a week elapse before it is taken
