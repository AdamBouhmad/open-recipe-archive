## Ingredients

- lettuce
- finest oil
- tarragon vinegar
- salt
- coarse freshly ground black pepper

## Directions

1. Pull the leaves of the lettuce from the stalk with your hand, rejecting all that are bruised and discoloured
2. Turn those at all muddy into a basin, wash them well, and drain them thoroughly on a sieve
3. Toss them lightly in a cloth to get rid of every drop of water
4. When dry, put the leaves into the bowl
5. Work them about with the wooden spoon and fork whilst an assistant sprinkles over them a libation of the finest oil you can buy
6. As soon as every leaf is thoroughly anointed, shake over them a few drops of tarragon vinegar
7. Dust them with salt and some coarse freshly ground black pepper
8. Keep the spoon and fork going during the addition of the vinegar drops and whilst the pepper and salt are being dusted into the bowl
9. Avoid creating a sediment of dressing to maintain the crispness of the leaves
