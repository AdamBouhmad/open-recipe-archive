## Ingredients

- onions
- milk
- a coffee-cupful of cream or milk enriched with the yolk of an egg
- pepper
- salt
- grated cheese
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Boil the onions in milk till tender
2. Cut them up as finely as possible and mash them, pass through a wire sieve
3. Mix the pulp well, adding a coffee-cupful of cream or milk enriched with the yolk of an egg, pepper, and salt
4. Butter a shallow pie-dish and put the puree into it
5. Strew a layer of grated cheese over the surface
6. Sprinkle a little melted butter over the cheese
7. Bake for a few minutes till the top takes colour
