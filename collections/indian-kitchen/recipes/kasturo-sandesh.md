## Ingredients

- 1 seer of hard chhana
- 1/2 powa of almonds
- 3 powas of syrup of white sugar
- 1 chhatak of pieces of pistachios
- 4 drops of essence of rose

## Directions

1. Mix pastes of hard chhana 1 seer and almonds 1/2 powa with syrup of white sugar 3 powas, and heat on fire.
2. Stir continuously and vigorously till sticky.
3. When taking off the fire add pieces of pistachios 1 chhatak and 4 drops of essence of rose, and stir as before till cold and hard.
4. Make balls of it and give any shape with the help of casts.
