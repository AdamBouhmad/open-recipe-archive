## Ingredients

- pomfret
- egg
- curled parsley
- broth from bones and trimmings
- one ounce of butter
- table-spoonful of flour
- half a pint of cooking liquor
- table-spoonful of finely chopped curled parsley
- yolk of an egg
- juice of a lime

## Directions

1. Divide your pomfret into nice fillets
2. Egg them on one side
3. Shake over the egg some finely minced curled parsley
4. Simmer the fillets, neatly rolled up and skewered, in broth made from their own bones and trimmings
5. Dish them on a hot dish as soon as they are done
6. Melt an ounce of butter in a small sauce-pan
7. Work into it a table-spoonful of flour
8. Moisten with half a pint of the liquor in which the fillets were cooked
9. Throw in a table-spoonful of finely chopped curled parsley
10. Finish the sauce, off the fire, with the yolk of an egg beaten up with the juice of a lime
11. Pour over the fillets and serve
