## Ingredients

- the best parts of a leg or shoulder of mutton
- salt
- pepper
- garden herbs
- bread-crumbs
- egg
- lard or ghee
- water or broth

## Directions

1. Take the best parts of a leg or shoulder of mutton
2. Cut them up, wash, and pound well down
3. Make a gravy of the bones and rejections
4. Mix with the pounded mutton all the ingredients mentioned in the recipe for making beef balls, and cook exactly as the beef cofta curry
