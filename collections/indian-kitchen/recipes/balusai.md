## Ingredients

- butter 1 powa
- fine flour 1 seer
- a little powdered cardamoms
- very little camphor or essence of rose
- water or milk
- ghee for frying
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly butter, fine flour, a little powdered cardamoms and very little camphor or essence of rose
2. Make a stiff paste with water or milk
3. Heat thoroughly
4. Make balls of it
5. Press them in the middle with a thumb
6. Fry hard in plenty of ghee
7. Keep immersed in a very concentrated syrup of sugar for a few hours
