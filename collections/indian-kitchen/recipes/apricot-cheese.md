## Ingredients

- Cabool apricots or those preserved for tarts
- sugar
- apricot stones blanched
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the apricots thoroughly in several waters
2. Parboil and reduce them to a pulp
3. Pass them through a sieve, rejecting all the skin
4. Add sugar as directed in the foregoing recipe and a handful or two of the apricot stones blanched
5. Boil the whole until it has thickened sufficiently
6. Pour it into buttered moulds
7. Put it into an expiring oven or some warm place to dry
8. When quite firm turn it out of the moulds
