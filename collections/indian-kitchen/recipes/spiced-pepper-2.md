## Ingredients

- savory
- ounce nutmeg
- ounce cloves
- ounce whole black pepper
- ounce Nepaul pepper

## Directions

1. Pound the above ingredients thoroughly in a mortar
2. When ground to powder, pass it through a fine sieve
3. Bottle it and cork it down securely
