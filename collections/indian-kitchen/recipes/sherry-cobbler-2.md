## Ingredients

- two wineglassfuls sherry
- half a wineglassful rum
- half a wineglassful maraschino
- half an orange
- crushed ice

## Directions

1. Pour sherry, rum, and maraschino into a tumbler
2. Add half an orange sliced fine
3. Fill the tumbler with crushed ice
4. Take the preparation through a reed, quill, or common straw
