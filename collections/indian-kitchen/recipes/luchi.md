## Ingredients

- 1 chhatak of ghee
- salt
- 1 seer of flour
- 1/2 seer of water

## Directions

1. Mix 1 chhatak of ghee and a pinch of salt with 1 seer of flour
2. Knead into a dough with about 1/2 seer of water
3. Make small balls of it
4. Roll into thin round discs
5. Fry in plenty of ghee till they expand
6. Fry also the other sides
7. It takes about half a minute to fry a luchi
8. They are not at all browned
