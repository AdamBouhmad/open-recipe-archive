## Ingredients

- a quart of spirits of wine
- twelve drops of oil of aniseed
- six drops of oil of cinnamon
- three drops of oil of roses
- eight drops of oil of citron
- a quart of syrup
- a few squares of leaf-gold

## Directions

1. To a quart of spirits of wine add twelve drops of oil of aniseed, six of oil of cinnamon, three of oil of roses, and eight of oil of citron
2. shake it well until the oils dissolve
3. add a quart of syrup
4. filter through filtering-paper
5. before bottling the liqueur, stir into it a few squares of leaf-gold cut into very little bits
