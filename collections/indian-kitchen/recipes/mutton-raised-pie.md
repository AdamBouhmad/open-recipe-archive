## Ingredients

- first class neck of mutton
- kidney fat from the loin
- salt
- black pepper
- chopped curled parsley
- white of egg
- bones and trimmings of the neck
- couple of sheep's feet

## Directions

1. Choose a first class neck of mutton, cut the meat from the bones in one piece
2. Divide that into slices an inch thick, and cut them into squares
3. Keep the fat and lean separate
4. If the neck be a lean one, take a few pieces of nice kidney fat from the loin to assist the pie
5. Thoroughly season the meat when it is cut up, with salt, black pepper, and a table-spoonful of chopped curled parsley
6. Pack the pie as closely as possible, in alternate layers of fat and lean
7. Do not pour in any gravy with the meat
8. When satisfactorily filled, put on the oval cover
9. Cement the edges with white of egg, and pinch them together firmly
10. Brush the top over with a well-beaten egg
11. Bake the pie in a slow oven
12. While baking, simmer the bones and trimmings of the neck, with a couple of sheep's feet cleaned and cut up, and make a clear yet strong broth
13. When cold, the broth should solidify as jelly
14. Flavour it with pepper and salt
15. After the pie has been baked, pour a little of it through an opening in the top of the cover
16. Set the pie in the ice-box, and when quite cold, serve
