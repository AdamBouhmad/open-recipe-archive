## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of fine flour
- 1/2 powa of middlings
- 1/2 powa of besam
- salt
- black cumins
- sesames
- water
- ghee

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough of fine flour 1/2 seer, middlings 1/2 powa, besam 1/2 powa, a little salt, some entire black cumins and sesames with water
2. Cut it in rectangular pieces half inch thick
3. Fry in plenty of ghee
