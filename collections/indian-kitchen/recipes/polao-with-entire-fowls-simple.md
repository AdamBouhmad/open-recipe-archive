## Ingredients

- Rice ½ seer
- ghee 1 powa
- onions ½ powa
- a few cassia leaves
- cloves
- cardamoms
- cinnamon ½ tola
- salt 2 tolas
- fowl 1 in number
- water 1 seer
- turmeric ½ tola

## Directions

1. Wash the rice thoroughly and keep them moist with a small quantity of water.
2. Cut the onions in pieces and fry them in ghee and take out.
3. Take about ½ powa of ghee in the same pan and when it is hot, roast the fowl, cleaned and washed and smeared with a paste of the turmeric, a few raw onions and half the salt, in it for 7 or 8 minutes till all the water coming out of the fowl dries up.
4. Take the fowl out of the pan.
5. Take the rest of the ghee in the pan and add the cassia leaves, cloves, cardamoms and cinnamons, all entire in it.
6. When the smell of the spices comes out, pour the moist rice in it and stir for about 5 minutes.
7. Add the roasted fowl and the fried onions, stir them for 2 or 3 minutes.
8. Pour water with the rest of the salt in the pan.
9. Lower the temperature of the fire when the mass begins to boil and allow it to simmer for quite a long time.
10. Take down when the water dries up, and the fowl and the rice are non-sticky. Add a little of hot water if water runs short at any time.
