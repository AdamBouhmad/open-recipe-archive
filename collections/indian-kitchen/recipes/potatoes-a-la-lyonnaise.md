## Ingredients

- Bombay onion
- butter
- broth
- potatoes
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry a Bombay onion (chopped small) in butter till it begins to brown
2. Add a wine-glass of broth with your pieces of potato
3. Toss them till coloured
4. Give them a dust of pepper and salt
