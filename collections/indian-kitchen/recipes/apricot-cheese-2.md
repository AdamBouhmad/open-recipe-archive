## Ingredients

- Cabool apricots or apricots preserved for tarts
- sugar
- apricot stones

## Directions

1. Take the Cabool apricots or those preserved for tarts
2. If the former, wash them thoroughly in several waters
3. Parboil and reduce them to a pulp
4. Pass them through a sieve, rejecting all the skin
5. Add sugar as directed in the damson cheese recipe
6. Add a handful or two of the apricot stones blanched
7. Boil the whole until it has thickened sufficiently
8. Pour it into buttered moulds
9. Put it into an expiring oven or some warm place to dry
10. When quite firm turn it out of the moulds
