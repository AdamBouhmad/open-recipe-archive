## Ingredients

- 1/2 seer of fried moog dal
- scraped kernels of 2 ripe cocoa-nuts
- khowa kheer 3 powas
- fine flour 1/2 powa
- sugar 1 powa
- water 1/2 seer
- 1 tola of powdered cardamoms
- ghee for frying
- sugar for syrup
- 6 or 7 drops of essence of rose

## Directions

1. Boil 1/2 seer of fried moog dal in water till saturated with it and then make a paste of them with the scraped kernels of 2 ripe cocoa-nuts
2. Mix this paste with khowa kheer 3 powas, fine flour 1/2 powa, sugar 1 powa and water 1/2 seer
3. Heat the mass and stir till it is a stiff gluey paste
4. Take it down, scrape together the paste and beat thoroughly after mixing with 1 tola of powdered cardamoms
5. Prepare round and long finger-like cakes of it
6. Fry in plenty of ghee
7. Drop them while hot in a syrup of sugar
8. Serve while cold after mixing with 6 or 7 drops of essence of rose
