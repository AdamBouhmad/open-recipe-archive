## Ingredients

- neck of pork
- black pepper
- salt
- liquid jelly made from pettitoes and pork scraps

## Directions

1. Choose the neck of pork
2. Omit the parsley
3. Season with black pepper and salt, in the proportion of two-thirds salt to one of pepper
4. Cut the meat into pieces as for a dry curry, keeping the fat and lean separate
5. Pack the pieces of meat as closely as you can
6. Bake the pie very slowly
7. A little liquid jelly made from pettitoes and pork scraps may be poured in after the baking
8. No gravy should be added before the baking operation
