## Ingredients

- two ounces of olive oil
- one drachm of spermaceti
- one drachm of oil of bergamot
- six drops of otto of roses
- half a drachm of annatto (if colour be desired)

## Directions

1. Mix two ounces of olive oil with one drachm each of spermaceti and oil of bergamot
2. Heat and strain
3. Beat in six drops of otto of roses
4. If colour be desired, add half a drachm of annatto
