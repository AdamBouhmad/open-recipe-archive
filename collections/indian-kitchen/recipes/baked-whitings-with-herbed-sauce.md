## Ingredients

- whitings
- sauce blanche
- parsley
- shallot
- marjoram
- garden cress
- green chilli
- fresh mushrooms

## Directions

1. Bake the whitings very gently.
2. Make sauce blanche in which a bunch of parsley minced fine, a very little shallot, a little marjoram, some garden cress, and a little green chilli (if liked) have been mixed.
3. For true fines herbes if fresh mushrooms are available: use equal portions (say a table-spoonful) of chopped mushroom and parsley, with a tea-spoonful of chopped shallot.
4. Serve the whitings with the herbed sauce.
