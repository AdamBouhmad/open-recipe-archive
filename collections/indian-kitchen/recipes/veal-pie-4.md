## Ingredients

- a leg of veal or a breast
- water
- pepper
- salt
- a little mace
- a little bacon
- a little flour
- pie-crust

## Directions

1. Cut a leg of veal into small pieces, or a breast into chops, and parboil in water enough to fill the pie-dish.
2. When about half stewed take the veal out.
3. Season the gravy with pepper, salt, a little mace, and a little bacon.
4. Dredge in a little flour.
5. Line the sides of the dish with a pie-crust.
6. Arrange the meat, pour in the gravy, cover it with a pie-crust, and bake it for an hour.
