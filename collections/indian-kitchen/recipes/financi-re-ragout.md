## Ingredients

- a nice tender fowl
- an onion
- a bit of celery
- a spoonful of dried herbs
- a sliced carrot
- two or three pepper corns
- equal portions of mushrooms and truffles
- melted butter
- a glass of sherry
- a pint of Espagnole
- whole button mushrooms
- sliced truffles
- cocks-combs
- sweet-breads
- tongue
- grated ham
- bacon

## Directions

1. Choose a nice tender fowl, lightly roast it, and save the choicest fillet meat for your ragout
2. Take the legs, thighs, skin, bones, liver, giblets, scraps, and trimmings, and break up the bones
3. Make the strongest broth you can with the remnants, flavoured with an onion, a bit of celery, a spoonful of dried herbs, a sliced carrot, and two or three pepper corns
4. Reduce it as strong as possible, and then strain
5. Take equal portions of mushrooms and of truffles; cut them up, and toss them in melted butter at the bottom of a sauce-pan
6. Work them well for two or three minutes, then add your fowl-essence, with a glass of sherry
7. Complete the sauce with your pint of Espagnole
8. Garnish the ragout with whole button mushrooms, sliced truffles, cocks-combs, sweet-breads, tongue, grated ham, and sippets of crisply fried bacon
9. Carefully stew the fowl fillets in the sauce
10. Gently heat up the fillets in the sauce
