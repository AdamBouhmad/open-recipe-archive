## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- butter
- cheese
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Dispose the pieces of cauliflower in a well-buttered dish that will stand the oven
2. Pour over them some melted butter
3. Dust some grated cheese over them, pepper and salt
4. Bake for ten minutes and serve
