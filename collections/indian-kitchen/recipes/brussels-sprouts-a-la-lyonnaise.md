## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts
- Bombay onion
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the Brussels sprouts
2. Fry a Bombay onion cut into thin rings in some butter
3. When a golden colour, add the sprouts and toss them together in the pan for a minute
4. Serve hot
