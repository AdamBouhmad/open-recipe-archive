## Ingredients

- lemons
- 1 powa of salt
- 1 seer of lemons

## Directions

1. Rub some lemons against a piece of blanket till all the minute oily particles on the skin of the lemons are completely removed
2. Make a few cuts on each, smear 1 powa of salt on 1 seer of the lemons and keep in an airtight glass jar
3. Keep the jar in the open atmosphere
4. Achar will be ready after about a month and can be preserved for about a year
