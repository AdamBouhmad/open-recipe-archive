## Ingredients

- 1/4 pound of beef suet
- crumb of a thick slice of stale bread
- soup herb
- Patna onions
- lemon-peel
- grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful of white pepper
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 6 oysters
- 1 anchovy
- minced ham or tongue
- 1 chittack or 2 ounces of butter
- stock

## Directions

1. Mince a quarter of a pound of beef suet, and grate fine the crumb of a thick slice of stale bread
2. Add a good quantity of soup herb, finely sliced and chopped Patna onions, lemon-peel, some grated nutmeg, a teaspoonful of white pepper, a teaspoonful of salt, half a dozen oysters, and an anchovy, or in the absence of anchovies a little minced ham or tongue
3. Melt down one chittack or two ounces of butter
4. Throw in the above ingredients and warm up well
5. Moisten with stock, and then stuff the bird
