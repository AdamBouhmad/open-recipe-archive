## Ingredients

- six ounces of fresh beef suet
- two ounces of moist sugar
- a quarter of a pound of well-grated bread-crumbs
- half a pint of new milk
- three eggs
- about one pound of marmalade
- loaf sugar
- orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Shred six ounces of fresh beef suet, and chop it up fine
2. Mix it with two ounces of moist sugar, a quarter of a pound of well-grated bread-crumbs, and then stir in half a pint of new milk
3. Add the well-beaten yolks of three eggs
4. Whisk all together for a quarter of an hour
5. Set it to stand on a cold stone for an hour
6. Butter a pudding-dish or mould thickly
7. Place a layer of the above mixture in it, then a layer of marmalade, another layer of mixture, and so on alternately until the mixture is exhausted
8. Whisk the whites of the eggs with a little loaf sugar and orange-flower water
9. Place the froth at the top of the pudding
10. Bake for an hour and a half in a moderate oven
