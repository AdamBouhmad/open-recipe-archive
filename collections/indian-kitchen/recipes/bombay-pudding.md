## Ingredients

- two pounds or one seer of soojee
- butter
- the yolk of an egg
- finely-sifted flour
- ghee
- lemon-juice
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Half roast the soojee, then boil it in water until it becomes very thick
2. Butter a soup-plate or any other dish of about the same depth
3. Pour the boiled soojee into it
4. When it has cooled and congealed, cut it into eight or more cakes
5. Rub the cakes over with the yolk of an egg and dredge with finely-sifted flour
6. Fry in ghee until they acquire a rich brown colour
7. Arrange them in a dish and pour over them a thick syrup flavoured with lemon-juice
