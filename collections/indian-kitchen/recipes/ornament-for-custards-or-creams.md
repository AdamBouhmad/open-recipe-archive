## Ingredients

- Whites of two eggs
- Two tablespoonfuls of syrup or thin jelly

## Directions

1. Whisk for an hour the whites of two eggs, together with two tablespoonfuls of some syrup or thin jelly
2. Lay it in any form upon a custard or cream, piled up to imitate rock, or it may be served in a dish with cream round it
3. The ornament may be coloured, if desired, with cochineal, saffron, spinach, &c.
