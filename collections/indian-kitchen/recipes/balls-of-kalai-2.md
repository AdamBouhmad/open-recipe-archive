## Ingredients

- 1 seer of pulses of mash-kalai
- whites of 2 or 3 eggs (if desired)
- ghee for frying
- sugar for syrup

## Directions

1. Keep 1 seer of pulses of mash-kalai under water for 7 or 8 hours and then make a paste of them
2. Mix with the whites of 2 or 3 eggs if desired, and beat the paste thoroughly
3. Fry small balls of it in plenty of ghee
4. Keep immersed in a syrup of sugar till served
