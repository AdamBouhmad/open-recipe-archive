## Ingredients

- boiled prawns
- butter
- weak gravy
- batter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Pound a dish of boiled prawns in a mortar with some butter and weak gravy
2. When quite worked to a puree, incorporate it with the batter
3. Drop the mixture by dessert-spoonfuls at a time into your friture-pan
4. Let the fritters cook till they turn a rich golden colour and are as crisp as biscuits
5. Drain and serve them on a napkin with crisply fried parsley
