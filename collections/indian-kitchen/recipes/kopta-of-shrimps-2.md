## Ingredients

- 1 powa of shrimps
- 1 chhatak of powdered biscuit
- 1/2 tola of powdered pepper
- a pinch of powdered mace
- 1/2 tola of salt
- a little pasted green chillies
- 1/2 chhatak of ghee
- eggs
- powdered biscuit for rolling

## Directions

1. Remove the scales, etc. from 1 powa of shrimps and make a paste of them
2. Add 1 chhatak of powdered biscuit, 1/2 tola of powdered pepper, a pinch of powdered mace, 1/2 tola of salt, a little pasted green chillies and 1/2 chhatak of ghee
3. Mix thoroughly and prepare small balls of it
4. Dip them into eggs beaten with salt
5. Roll on powdered biscuit
6. Fry in plenty of ghee
