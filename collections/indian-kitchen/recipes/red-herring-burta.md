## Ingredients

- white Patna onion
- fresh green chilies
- fresh lime juice
- red herring

## Directions

1. Take onions, chilies, and lime-juice
2. Place the herring with its original paper packing on a gridiron or on a frying-pan and warm it well
3. Clear it of all skin and very carefully pick out all the bones
4. Bruise the herring and mix it thoroughly with the sliced onions, chilies, and lime-juice
