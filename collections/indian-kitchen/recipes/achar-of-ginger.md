## Ingredients

- 1 seer of ginger
- 1 powa of salt
- 0.5 chhatak of powdered pepper
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Peel 1 seer of ginger and wash them.
2. Then cut them in pieces, smear with 1 powa of salt and 0.5 chhatak of powdered pepper and keep aside for a few hours.
3. Then keep them immersed in lemon-juice for a week, keeping all the while in the open atmosphere.
4. Then pick the pieces out and keep covered in a stone or earthen pot.
