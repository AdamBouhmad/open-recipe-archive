## Ingredients

- egg contents
- green bananas
- potatoes
- ghee or oil
- coriander
- pepper
- cumin
- turmeric
- onions
- salt
- cassia leaves
- minced onions
- red chillies
- powdered garam masala

## Directions

1. Prepare egg baras with green bananas
2. Brown pieces of potatoes in ghee or oil
3. Cook as Dhonka with Eggs without ginger
