## Ingredients

- chhola, khesari or peas
- salt
- mustard oil

## Directions

1. Wash cleanly and keep any of the dals moist under water for about two hours.
2. Grind them into a paste when they are soaked with water.
3. Tie the paste loosely in a piece of cloth.
4. Drop it into the rice when the latter start boiling.
5. Mix salt and mustard oil.
