## Ingredients

- six anchovies in oil
- yolks of two raw eggs
- four slices of bread
- butter
- table-spoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Wipe anchovies free from oil
2. Split them open and remove the spines
3. Pass the fish through a hair sieve
4. Put the pulp in a bowl and stir into it the yolks of two raw eggs
5. Cut four nice rounds or slices of bread and fry them in butter till of a bright golden tint
6. Arrange them on a very hot silver dish and cover them up
7. Melt a table-spoonful of butter at the bottom of a sauce-pan which should be dipped into a bain-marie or any vessel containing boiling water
8. Stir into the melted butter the anchovy pulp mixture and let it thicken
9. When quite hot, pour it over the four toasts and send the dish up immediately
