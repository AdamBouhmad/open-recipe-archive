## Ingredients

- ox head
- water
- vegetables
- flavouring
- salt
- spiced pepper
- powdered dried thyme
- marjoram
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut out the eyes, break the bones of the jaws, and remove the brains
2. Let the whole soak for an hour or two in cold water
3. Put it into a stew-pan with water enough to cover it
4. Boil very slowly, then add vegetables and flavouring as if for soup
5. When the meat is quite tender and you can pick the bones away from it easily, strain the meat from the broth and vegetables
6. Free the meat from every fragment of bone
7. Cut up the meat whilst it is hot and juicy, en masse rather small
8. Season with salt and spiced pepper whilst doing so (or if you have no spiced pepper, mix a table-spoonful of powdered dried thyme and marjoram blended with a tea-spoonful of pepper, and dust it freely into the meat)
9. Press the meat tightly down in a round brawn tin
10. Let it get cold
11. Turn out whole and slice for breakfast or lunch
