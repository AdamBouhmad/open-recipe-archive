## Ingredients

- contents of 3 or 4 fresh eggs
- 1/2 seer flour
- water
- ghee
- honey or sugar

## Directions

1. Add the contents of 3 or 4 fresh eggs to 1/2 seer of flour and rub thoroughly.
2. Add some water and knead the flour into a dough.
3. Form small balls of it and roll them into very thin and round discs.
4. Heat them a little in a shallow pan 2 or 3 at a time and then bake them on charcoal fire.
5. Smear ghee and again heat them a little in a pan.
6. Serve with honey or sugar.
