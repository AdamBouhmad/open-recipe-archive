## Ingredients

- thin slices of steak
- Oxford or Bologna sausage meat
- hot butter
- onions
- flour
- sweet herbs
- mushroom trimmings
- pepper
- salt
- good gravy or stock

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of steak two inches wide by six inches long.
2. Put on each at one end a piece of Oxford or Bologna sausage meat the size of a pigeon's egg.
3. Roll up each olive tightly and neatly, and tie it up with a piece of thread.
4. Fry them in hot butter until they begin to take colour.
5. Take them out, remove the string from each olive, and lay them by.
6. Fry some onions a gold colour in butter, add a very little flour, sweet herbs, a few mushroom trimmings, pepper and salt.
7. Moisten with some very good gravy or stock; let the sauce boil, then strain it.
8. Carefully lay the olives in it to simmer till done and ready to be served; the sauce should cover them in the sauce-pan.
