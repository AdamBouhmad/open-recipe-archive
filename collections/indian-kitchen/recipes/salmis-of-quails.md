## Ingredients

- eight nice quails
- boiled bacon
- butter
- rich gravy
- butter
- flour
- caramel
- Madeira
- livers of the birds
- red currant jelly
- chilli vinegar
- mushrooms
- dry toast

## Directions

1. Choose eight nice quails and clean the birds carefully
2. Put a roll of boiled bacon inside each bird and sew up their vents
3. Truss the birds as for roasting
4. Melt some butter in a frying-pan, and give the quails a turn or two therein till they colour nicely
5. Put them in a stew-pan and cover them with a rich gravy
6. Stew them till they are done
7. Lift them out
8. Thicken the gravy with butter and flour
9. Colour it with caramel
10. Add half a glass of Madeira, the livers of the birds pounded, a tea-spoonful of red currant jelly, a dash of chilli vinegar, and some finely minced mushrooms
11. Heat the quails up in this sauce, and serve as hot as possible, dry toast accompanying
