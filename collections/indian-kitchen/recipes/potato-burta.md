## Ingredients

- moderate or middling sized white Patna onion
- two hot green chilies
- fresh lime juice
- eight or ten well-boiled potatoes
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- a teaspoonful of good mustard oil

## Directions

1. Remove the outer coats of the onion and slice very fine
2. Slice or cut up the two hot green chilies
3. Squeeze the lime juice over the onion and chilies and allow to soak
4. Bruise the potatoes down with a large silver or plated fork
5. When the potatoes are half bruised, add the onions and chilies, with as much of the lime-juice as may be agreeable
6. Mix all well together with a light hand, so that the potatoes may not cake, and yet be well and thoroughly mashed and mixed
