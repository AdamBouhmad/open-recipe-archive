## Ingredients

- three eggs
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- a tablespoonful of cream or of milk
- a dust of pepper
- a piece of butter the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Break three eggs into a bowl with a salt-spoonful of salt, a tablespoonful of cream or of milk, and a dust of pepper.
2. Mix them well.
3. Melt a piece of butter the size of an egg in your omelette-pan.
4. Pour in your mixture, stir it about unceasingly until it is lightly set, and turn it out.
5. Tomato pulp may be mixed with the eggs, and any nice meat such as ham, tongue, corned beef, game, &c., may be minced up, and added to them.
