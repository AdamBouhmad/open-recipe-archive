## Ingredients

- besam
- water
- salt
- powdered chillies
- boiling oil or ghee

## Directions

1. Prepare a thick solution of besam and water.
2. Add salt and powdered chillies, and beat thoroughly.
3. Drop it on boiling oil or ghee through a finely perforated ladle and fry till brown.
