## Ingredients

- minced meat
- khowa kheer
- besam of grams
- powdered rice
- ghee
- powders of pepper
- cumin
- garam-masala
- salt
- sugar
- ginger-juice
- fried and powdered almonds
- raisins
- water
- eggs

## Directions

1. Mix together to a stiff paste 1.5 seer of minced meat, 0.5 powa of slightly fried khowa kheer, 0.5 powa of besam of grams or powdered rice, 1 chhatak of ghee, powders of pepper, cumin and garam-masala 1 tola each, salt 0.5 tola, sugar, ginger-juice, fried and powdered almonds and raisins as desired, and water.
2. Beaten eggs may be added if desired.
3. Make balls of it and roast on slow heat on an iron fork adding ghee at intervals.
