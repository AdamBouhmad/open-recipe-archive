## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- four tablespoonfuls of rice flour
- a little milk
- one quarter of a pound of butter
- the grated peel with the juice of two lemons
- the yolks and beaten whites of four eggs
- sugar to taste
- one wine-glassful of wine

## Directions

1. Put on to boil one quart of milk
2. When it simmers stir in four tablespoonfuls of rice flour that has been moistened in a little milk
3. Let it come to a boil and remove from the fire
4. Add one quarter of a pound of butter
5. When cool, add the grated peel with the juice of two lemons and the yolks and beaten whites of four eggs
6. Sweeten to taste
7. Add one wine-glassful of wine in the last thing
