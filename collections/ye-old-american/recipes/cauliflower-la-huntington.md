## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- one and one-half teaspoons mustard
- one and one-fourth teaspoons salt
- one teaspoon powdered sugar
- one-fourth teaspoon paprika
- yolks three eggs
- one-fourth cup olive oil
- one-half cup vinegar
- one-half teaspoon finely chopped shallot
- one-half tablespoon curry powder
- two tablespoons melted butter
- one teaspoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare cauliflower as for boiled cauliflower, and steam until soft.
2. Separate in pieces and pour over the following sauce:
3. Mix one and one-half teaspoons mustard, one and one-fourth teaspoons salt, one teaspoon powdered sugar, and one-fourth teaspoon paprika.
4. Add yolks three eggs slightly beaten, one-fourth cup olive oil, and one-half cup vinegar in which one-half teaspoon finely chopped shallot has infused five minutes.
5. Cook over hot water until mixture thickens.
6. Remove from range, and add one-half tablespoon curry powder, two tablespoons melted butter, and one teaspoon finely chopped parsley.
