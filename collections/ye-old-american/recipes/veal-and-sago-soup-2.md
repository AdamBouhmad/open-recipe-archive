## Ingredients

- 2½ lbs. lean veal
- 3 quarts cold water
- ¼ lb. pearl sago
- 2 cups scalded milk
- Yolks 4 eggs
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Order meat from market, very finely chopped.
2. Pick over and remove particles of fat.
3. Cover meat with water, bring slowly to boiling-point, and simmer two hours, skimming occasionally; strain and reheat.
4. Soak sago one-half hour in enough cold water to cover, stir into hot stock, boil thirty minutes, and add milk; then pour mixture slowly on yolks of eggs, slightly beaten.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
