## Ingredients

- cod sounds
- salt
- milk
- water
- egg sauce

## Directions

1. Steep your sounds as you do the salt cod
2. Boil them in a large quantity of milk and water
3. When they are very tender and white, take them up
4. Drain the water out and skin them
5. Pour the egg sauce boiling hot over them
6. Serve them up
