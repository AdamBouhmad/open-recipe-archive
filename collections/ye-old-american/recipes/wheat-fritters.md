## Ingredients

- Three eggs
- One and a half cups of milk
- Three teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Salt
- Flour enough to make quite stiff, thicker than batter cakes

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, milk, baking powder, salt, and flour together
2. Drop into hot lard and fry like doughnuts
