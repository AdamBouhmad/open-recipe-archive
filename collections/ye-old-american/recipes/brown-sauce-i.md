## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ slice onion
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup Brown Stock
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Cook onion in butter until slightly browned; remove onion and stir butter constantly until well browned.
2. Add flour mixed with seasonings, and brown the butter and flour.
3. Add stock gradually.
