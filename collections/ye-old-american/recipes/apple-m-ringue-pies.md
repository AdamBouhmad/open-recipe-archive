## Ingredients

- Ripe, juicy apples
- Sugar
- Nutmeg
- Lemon-peel
- 3 eggs—whites beaten to stiff froth
- 1 tablespoonful powdered sugar for each egg
- Rose-water or vanilla
- Crust
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and slice apples
2. Stew and sweeten apples
3. Mash smooth
4. Season with nutmeg
5. If you like the flavor, stew some lemon-peel with the apple, and remove when cold
6. Fill your crust, and bake until just done
7. Make a thick méringue by whipping to a stiff froth the whites of three eggs for each pie
8. Sweeten with a tablespoonful of powdered sugar for each egg
9. Flavor with rose-water or vanilla
10. Beat until it will stand alone
11. Spread over the apple and cover the pie three-quarters of an inch thick
12. Set back in the oven until the méringue is well "set"
13. If it colors too darkly, sift powdered sugar over it when cold
14. Eat cold
