## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- lemon

## Directions

1. Slice cold boiled potatoes a quarter of an inch thick and put into a saucepan with four or five tablespoonfuls of milk, two or three of butter, pepper, salt, and some chopped parsley.
2. Heat quickly, stirring all the time until ready to boil.
3. When ready to boil, stir in the juice of half a lemon.
4. Pour into a deep dish and serve very hot.
