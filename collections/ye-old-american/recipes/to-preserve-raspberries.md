## Ingredients

- raspberries (not too ripe)
- sugar (the weight of them)
- water (a little water)

## Directions

1. Choose raspberries that are not too ripe
2. Take the weight of them in sugar
3. Wet your sugar with a little water
4. Put in your berries, and let them boil softly
5. Take heed of breaking them
6. When they are clear, take them up
7. And boil the sirrup till it be thick enough
8. Then put them in again
9. And when they are cold, put them up in glasses
