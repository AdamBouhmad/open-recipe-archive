## Ingredients

- four small tomatoes
- Mayonnaise
- chives
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Remove skins from four small tomatoes, and cut in halves crosswise
2. Cover with Mayonnaise, and sprinkle with finely chopped chives
3. Serve on lettuce leaves
