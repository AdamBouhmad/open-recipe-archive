## Ingredients

- salt shad
- fresh water
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak the fish six or seven hours in warm water, changing it several times.
2. Wipe off all the salt and immerse in ice-cold water.
3. When it has lain in this an hour, put into a fish-kettle with enough fresh water to cover it, and boil from fifteen to twenty minutes, in proportion to the size.
4. Serve in a hot dish, with a large lump of butter spread over the fish.
