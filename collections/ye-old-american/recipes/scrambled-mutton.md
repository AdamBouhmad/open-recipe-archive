## Ingredients

- cold mutton
- hot water
- butter
- eggs
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop cold mutton into two cups
2. Add two tablespoonfuls of hot water and a piece of butter as large as an English walnut
3. When the meat is hot, break in three eggs
4. Constantly stir until the eggs begin to stiffen
5. Season with pepper and salt
