## Ingredients

- ½ box gelatine or 2 tablespoons granulated gelatine
- ½ cup cold water
- 1⅔ cups boiling water
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup Sherry or Madeira wine
- ⅓ cup orange juice
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine twenty minutes in cold water, dissolve in boiling water; add sugar, wine, orange juice, and lemon juice; strain, mould, and chill.
2. If a stronger jelly is desired, use additional wine in place of orange juice.
