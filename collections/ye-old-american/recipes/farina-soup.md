## Ingredients

- 4 cups White Stock III
- ¼ cup farina
- 2 cups scalded milk
- 1 cup cream
- Few gratings of nutmeg
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Heat stock to boiling-point, add farina, and boil fifteen minutes; then add milk, cream, and seasonings.
