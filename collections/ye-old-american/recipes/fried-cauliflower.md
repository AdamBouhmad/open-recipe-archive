## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- flour
- eggs
- water
- salt
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Boil the cauliflower till about half done
2. Mix two tablespoonfuls of flour with two yolks of eggs
3. Add water enough to make a rather thin paste
4. Add salt to taste
5. Beat the two whites till stiff and then mix with the yolks, flour and water
6. Dip each branch of the cauliflower into the mixture
7. Fry them in hot fat
8. When done, take them off with a skimmer, turn into a colander
9. Dust salt all over and serve warm
