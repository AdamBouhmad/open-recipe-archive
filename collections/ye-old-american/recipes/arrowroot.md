## Ingredients

- 1 cup of boiling water
- 1 cup fresh milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls best Bermuda arrowroot, wet with cold water
- 1 small pinch of salt
- 2 even teaspoonfuls white sugar, dissolved in the milk

## Directions

1. Stir the arrowroot paste into the salted boiling water
2. Stir and boil five minutes or until it is clear
3. Add the sweetened milk
4. Boil ten minutes, slowly, still stirring
