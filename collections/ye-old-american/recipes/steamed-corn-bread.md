## Ingredients

- 2 cups Indian meal
- 1 cup flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 2½ cups loppered milk or buttermilk
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 heaping tablespoonful lard, melted

## Directions

1. Beat very hard and long
2. Put in buttered mould
3. Tie a coarse cloth tightly over it
4. If you have no steamer, fit the mould in the top of a pot of boiling water, taking care it does not touch the surface of the liquid
5. Lay a close cover over the cloth tied about the mould, to keep in all the heat
6. Steam one hour and a half
7. Set in an oven ten minutes
8. Turn out upon a hot plate, and eat while warm
