## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup flour
- ⅓ cup cream
- ⅓ cup water in which vegetables were cooked
- 1 cup cooked vegetables rubbed through a sieve, carrots, turnips, or onions
- Yolks 3 eggs
- Whites 3 eggs
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add flour, and pour on gradually cream and water.
2. Add vegetable, yolks of eggs beaten until thick and lemon-colored, and fold in whites of eggs beaten until stiff.
3. Add seasonings.
4. Turn in a buttered baking-dish and bake in a slow oven.
