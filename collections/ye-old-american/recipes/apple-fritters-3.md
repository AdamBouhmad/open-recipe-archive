## Ingredients

- A batter according to the Fritters (No. 2) receipt
- 3 large juicy apples, pared and quartered
- 1 glass brandy
- 1 tablespoonful white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put the brandy, a very little water, the sugar, and the spice into a covered saucepan with the apples.
2. Stir gently until half done; drain off the liquor, every drop; mince the apple when cold, and stir into the batter.
