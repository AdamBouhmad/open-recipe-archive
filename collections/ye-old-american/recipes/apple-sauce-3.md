## Ingredients

- apples
- sugar
- a little gelatine dissolved in warm water
- a tablespoonful in a pint of sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the apples until they are very tender
2. Stir them thoroughly so there will be no lumps at all
3. Add the sugar and a little gelatine dissolved in warm water, a tablespoonful in a pint of sauce
4. Pour the sauce into bowls, and when cold it will be stiff like jelly, and can be turned out on a plate
