## Ingredients

- clams
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Roast in a pan over a hot fire, or in a hot oven, or, at a "Clam Bake," on hot stones
2. when they open, empty the juice into a saucepan
3. add the clams, with butter, pepper and a very little salt
