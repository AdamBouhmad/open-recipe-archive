## Ingredients

- Neck or breast of veal in small pieces
- A little salt pork cut fine
- Pepper and salt
- A small piece of celery chopped coarsely
- Yellow top of celery picked
- A tablespoonful of flour
- The yolk of one egg
- Milk
- Light, flaky crust

## Directions

1. Cut portions of the neck or breast of veal into small pieces
2. With a little salt pork cut fine, stew gently for ten or fifteen minutes
3. Season with pepper and salt, and a small piece of celery chopped coarsely, also of the yellow top, picked
4. Stir in a paste made of a tablespoonful of flour, the yolk of one egg, and milk to form a thin batter
5. Let all come to a boil
6. Make the patties of a light, flaky crust, as for tarts, cut round, the size of a small sauceplate
7. Cut the centre of each, for about three inches, half way through, to be raised and serve as a cover
8. Put a spoonful of the stew in each crust, lay on the top and serve
