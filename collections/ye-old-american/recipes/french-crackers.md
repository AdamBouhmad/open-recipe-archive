## Ingredients

- six eggs
- twelve tablespoonfuls of sweet milk
- six tablespoonfuls of butter
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix six eggs, twelve tablespoonfuls of sweet milk, six tablespoonfuls of butter, half a teaspoonful of soda
2. Mold with flour, pounding and working half an hour
3. Roll it thin
4. Bake with rather quick fire
