## Ingredients

- fresh salmon
- salt
- pepper
- powdered mace
- butter
- flour
- horseradish
- parsley
- lobster sauce

## Directions

1. Clean a small or moderate sized salmon.
2. Season it with salt, pepper, and powdered mace rubbed on it both outside and in.
3. Skewer it with the tail turned round and put to the mouth.
4. Lay it on a stand or trivet in a deep dish or pan.
5. Stick it over with bits of butter rolled in flour.
6. Put it into the oven, and baste it occasionally while baking with its own drippings.
7. Garnish it with horseradish and sprigs of curled parsley, laid alternately round the edge of the dish.
8. Send to table with it a small tureen of lobster sauce.
