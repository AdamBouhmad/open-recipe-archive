## Ingredients

- 1 quart of elderberries
- 1 quart of vinegar
- 6 anchovies, soaked and pulled to pieces
- Half a teaspoonful mace
- A pinch of ginger
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful whole peppers

## Directions

1. Scald the vinegar and pour over the berries, which must be picked from the stalks and put into a large stone jar
2. Cover with a pane of glass, and set in the hot sun two days
3. Strain off the liquor, and boil up with the other ingredients, stirring often, one hour, keeping covered unless while stirring
4. Let it cool; strain and bottle
