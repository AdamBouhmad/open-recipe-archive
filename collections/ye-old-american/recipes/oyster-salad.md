## Ingredients

- a quart of fresh oysters
- hot vinegar
- cold water
- two pickled cucumbers
- a quart of celery
- salt and pepper
- Mayonnaise dressing
- celery tips
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Drain the liquor from a quart of fresh oysters
2. Put them in hot vinegar enough to cover them placed over the fire
3. Let them remain until plump, but not cooked
4. Drop them immediately in cold water and drain off
5. Mix with them two pickled cucumbers cut fine
6. Also add a quart of celery cut in dice pieces, some seasoning of salt and pepper
7. Mix all well together, tossing up with a silver fork
8. Pour over the whole a Mayonnaise dressing
9. Garnish with celery tips and slices of hard-boiled eggs arranged tastefully
