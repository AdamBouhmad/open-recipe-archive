## Ingredients

- The juice of two lemons
- grated peel of one lemon
- one pint of cream
- sugar
- one cupful of sherry
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Let sugar, lemon juice and peel lie together two hours
2. Add wine and nutmeg
3. Strain through double tarlatan
4. Whip gradually into the frothed cream
5. Serve very soon heaped in small glasses
