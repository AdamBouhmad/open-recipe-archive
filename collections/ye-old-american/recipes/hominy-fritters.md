## Ingredients

- one pint of hot boiled hominy
- two eggs
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- one tablespoonful of flour
- cold milk
- one teaspoonful of baking powder
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Take one pint of hot boiled hominy, two eggs, half a teaspoonful of salt and a tablespoonful of flour
2. Thin it a little with cold milk
3. When cold add a teaspoonful of baking powder and mix thoroughly
4. Drop tablespoonfuls of it into hot fat and fry to a delicate brown
