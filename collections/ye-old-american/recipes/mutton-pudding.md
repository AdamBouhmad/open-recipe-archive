## Ingredients

- beef suet paste
- mutton cutlets
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- thyme
- onion
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Line a two-quart pudding basin with some beef suet paste
2. Fill the lining with thick mutton cutlets, slightly trimmed, or if preferred, with steaks cut from the leg
3. Season with pepper and salt some parsley, a little thyme and two slices of onion chopped fine
4. Between each layer of meat, put some slices of potatoes
5. When the pudding is filled, wet the edges of the paste around the top of the basin
6. Cover with a piece of paste rolled out the size of the basin
7. Fasten down the edge by bearing all around with the thumb
8. With the thumb and forefinger twist the edges of the paste over so as to give it a corded appearance
9. This pudding can be set in a steamer and steamed, or boiled
10. Cook for about three hours
11. When done, turn it out carefully on a platter and serve with a rich gravy under it
