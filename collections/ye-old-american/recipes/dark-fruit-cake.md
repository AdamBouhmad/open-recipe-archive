## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- ¾ cup raisins, seeded and cut in pieces
- ¾ cup currants
- ½ cup citron, thinly sliced and cut in strips
- ½ cup molasses
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- ½ teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon allspice
- ½ teaspoon mace
- ¼ teaspoon clove
- ½ teaspoon lemon extract

## Directions

1. Follow directions for mixing butter cake mixtures.
2. Bake in deep cake pans one and one-quarter hours.
