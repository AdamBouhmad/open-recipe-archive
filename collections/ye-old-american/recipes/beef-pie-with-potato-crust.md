## Ingredients

- rare roast beef or cold corned beef
- pepper
- salt
- mashed potato
- butter
- large cupful of mashed potato
- two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- well-beaten egg
- two cups of milk
- flour
- warm water
- catsup
- cold gravy or dripping
- beaten white of egg

## Directions

1. Mince some rare roast beef or cold corned beef
2. Season with pepper and salt, and spread a layer in the bottom of a pudding-dish
3. Over this put one of mashed potato, and stick bits of butter thickly all over it
4. Then another of meat, and so on until you are ready for the crust
5. To a large cupful of mashed potato add two tablespoonfuls of melted butter, a well-beaten egg, two cups of milk, and beat all together until very light
6. Work in enough flour to enable you to roll out in a sheet—not too stiff
7. Add to the meat and potato in the dish a gravy made of warm water, butter, milk, and catsup, with what cold gravy or dripping remains from roast
8. Cover the pie with a thick, tender crust, cutting a slit in the middle
9. Brush over with beaten white of egg before it goes to table
