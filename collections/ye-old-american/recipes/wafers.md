## Ingredients

- six eggs
- a pint of flour
- two ounces of melted butter
- as much milk as will make a thin batter
- pounded loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs, add a pint of flour, two ounces of melted butter, with as much milk as will make a thin batter
2. Put in pounded loaf sugar to your taste
3. Pour it in the wafer irons, bake them quickly without browning
4. Roll them while hot
