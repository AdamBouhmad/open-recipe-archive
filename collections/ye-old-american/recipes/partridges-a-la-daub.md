## Ingredients

- six partridges
- thin slices of fat bacon
- jelly

## Directions

1. Truss six partridges neatly, cover them with thin slices of fat bacon taken from the top of a middling
2. This keeps them white, and gives a good flavour
3. They must be wrapped entirely in it
4. Roast them, and when done, take off the bacon
5. Let them get cold, and use jelly as for the pheasants
