## Ingredients

- cinnamon
- a quart of milk
- a pint of molasses
- a quart or more of Indian meal
- grated peel and juice of a large lemon or orange

## Directions

1. Boil some cinnamon in a quart of milk, and then strain it
2. While the milk is hot, stir into it a pint of molasses
3. Add by degrees a quart or more of Indian meal so as to make a thick batter
4. Improve it by the grated peel and juice of a large lemon or orange
5. Tie it very securely in a thick cloth, leaving room for it to swell, and pasting up the tying-place with a lump of flour and water
6. Put it into a pot of boiling water, having ready a kettle to fill it up as it boils away
7. Hang it over a good fire, and keep it boiling hard for four or five hours
8. Eat it warm with molasses and butter
