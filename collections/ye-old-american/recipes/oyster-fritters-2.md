## Ingredients

- oysters
- oyster liquor
- milk
- eggs
- salt
- flour
- lard or half lard and half butter

## Directions

1. Drain the liquor from the oysters.
2. To a cupful of oyster liquor add the same quantity of milk, three eggs, a little salt, and flour enough for a thin batter.
3. Chop the oysters and stir into the batter.
4. Have ready in the frying-pan a few spoonfuls of lard, or half lard, half butter.
5. Heat very hot.
6. Try a spoonful first, to satisfy yourself that the lard is hot enough, and that the fritter is of the right size and consistency.
7. Drop the oyster-batter in by the tablespoonful.
8. Take rapidly from the pan as soon as they are done to a pleasing yellow brown.
9. Send to table very hot.
10. Alternatively, fry the oyster whole, enveloped in batter, one in each fritter, with batter thicker than if chopped oysters were added.
