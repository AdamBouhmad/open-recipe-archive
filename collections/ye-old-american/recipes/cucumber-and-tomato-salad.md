## Ingredients

- Sliced tomatoes
- Lettuce leaves
- Cucumber
- French or Mayonnaise Dressing

## Directions

1. Arrange sliced tomatoes on a bed of lettuce leaves.
2. Pile on each slice, cucumber cubes cut one-half inch square.
3. Serve with French or Mayonnaise Dressing.
