## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- two cupfuls of bread crumbs
- four eggs
- butter the size of an egg
- one cupful of white sugar
- one large lemon (juice and grated rind)
- four tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Heat the milk and pour over the bread crumbs
2. Add the butter, cover and let it get soft
3. When cool, beat the sugar and yolks and add to the mixture, also the grated rind
4. Bake in a buttered dish until firm and slightly brown, from a half to three-quarters of an hour
5. When done, draw it to the door of the oven and cover with a meringue made of the whites of the eggs, whipped to a froth with four tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar and the lemon juice
6. Put it back in the oven and brown a light straw color
7. Eat warm, with lemon sauce
