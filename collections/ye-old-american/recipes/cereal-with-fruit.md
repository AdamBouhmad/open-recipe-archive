## Ingredients

- ¾ cup Wheat Germ
- ¾ cup cold water
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ lb. dates, stoned, and cut in pieces
- cream

## Directions

1. Mix cereal, salt, and cold water; add to boiling water placed on front of range.
2. Boil five minutes, steam in double boiler thirty minutes; stir in dates, and serve with cream.
3. To serve for breakfast, or as a simple dessert.
