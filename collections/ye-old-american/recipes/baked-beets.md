## Ingredients

- beets
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Beets retain their sugary, delicate flavor to perfection if they are baked instead of boiled.
2. Turn them frequently while in the oven, using a knife, as the fork allows the juice to run out.
3. When done remove the skin, and serve with butter, salt and pepper on the slices.
