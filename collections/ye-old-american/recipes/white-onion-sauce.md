## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- water
- butter
- milk or cream
- turnip

## Directions

1. Peel a dozen onions and throw them into salt and water to keep them white
2. Boil them tender
3. When done, squeeze the water from them and chop them
4. Have ready some butter that has been melted rich and smooth with milk or cream instead of water
5. Put the onions into the melted butter and boil them up at once
6. If you wish to have them very mild, put in a turnip with them at the first boiling
