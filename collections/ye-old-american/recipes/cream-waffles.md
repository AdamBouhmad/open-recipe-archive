## Ingredients

- One pint of sour cream
- Two eggs
- One pint of flour
- One tablespoonful of corn meal
- One teaspoonful of soda
- Half a teaspoonful of salt
- A little sweet milk

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs separately
2. Mix the cream with the beaten yolks
3. Stir in the flour, corn meal and salt
4. Add the soda dissolved in a little sweet milk
5. Add the whites beaten to a stiff froth
