## Ingredients

- one cupful of sugar
- half a cupful of cold butter
- a pint of milk
- two cupfuls of sifted flour
- five eggs
- grated lemon rind

## Directions

1. Make the milk hot
2. Stir in the butter and let it cool before the other ingredients are added to it
3. Stir in the sugar, flour and eggs, which should be well whisked and omit the whites of two
4. Flavor with a little grated lemon rind
5. Beat the mixture well
6. Butter some small cups, rather more than half fill them
7. Bake from twenty minutes to half an hour according to the size of the puddings
8. Serve with fruit, custard or wine sauce, a little of which may be poured over them
9. They may be dropped by spoonfuls on buttered tins and baked if cups are not convenient
