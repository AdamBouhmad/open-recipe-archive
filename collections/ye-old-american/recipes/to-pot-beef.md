## Ingredients

- ten pounds of beef
- three teaspoonfuls of salt
- one teaspoonful of pepper
- one teaspoonful of cloves
- one teaspoonful of mace
- one teaspoonful of cinnamon
- one teaspoonful of allspice
- one teaspoonful of thyme
- one teaspoonful of sweet basil
- a piece of fat salt pork
- three tablespoonfuls of Tarragon vinegar or half a pint of Madeira wine
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Take ten pounds of beef and remove all the fat
2. Cut the lean into square pieces, two inches thick
3. Mix together three teaspoonfuls of salt, one of pepper, one of cloves, one of mace, one of cinnamon, one of allspice, one of thyme, and one of sweet basil
4. Put a layer of the pieces of beef into an earthen pot
5. Sprinkle some of this spice mixture over this layer
6. Add a piece of fat salt pork, cut as thin as possible
7. Sprinkle a little of the spice mixture over the pork
8. Make another layer of the beef with spices and pork, and so on, until the pot is filled
9. Pour over the whole three tablespoonfuls of Tarragon vinegar, or, if you prefer it, half a pint of Madeira wine
10. Cover the pot with a paste made of flour and water, so that no steam can escape
11. Put the pot into an oven, moderately heated
12. Let it stand there eight hours
13. Set it away to use when wanted
