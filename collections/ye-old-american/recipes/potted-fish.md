## Ingredients

- fish
- allspice
- cloves
- muslin bags
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- sage
- cider vinegar
- dough

## Directions

1. Take out the backbone of the fish
2. For one weighing two pounds take a tablespoonful of allspice and cloves mixed
3. Put these spices into little bags of not too thick muslin
4. Put sufficient salt directly upon each fish
5. Roll in cloth, over which sprinkle a little cayenne pepper
6. Put alternate layers of fish, spice and sage in an earthen jar
7. Cover with the best cider vinegar
8. Cover the jar closely with a plate, and over this, put a covering of dough, rolled out to twice the thickness of pie crust
9. Make the edges of paste, to adhere closely to the sides of the jar, so as to make it air tight
10. Put the jar into a pot of cold water and let it boil from three to five hours, according to quantity
11. Ready when cold
