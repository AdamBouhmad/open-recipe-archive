## Ingredients

- fine puff paste, or the best plain paste, or sweet paste
- stewed fruit (cold)
- or sweetmeats

## Directions

1. Roll out paste in a circular form
2. Spread it over the bottom, sides, and edges of buttered dishes or patty-pans
3. Bake empty for about half an hour in a rather quick oven of equal heat throughout
4. Bake to a light brown
5. When cool, take out of the dishes and transfer to plates
6. Fill with the fruit
