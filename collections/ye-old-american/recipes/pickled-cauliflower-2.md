## Ingredients

- cauliflower heads
- salt and water
- hot vinegar
- a liberal supply of whole cloves, pepper, allspice and white mustard
- two teaspoonfuls of French mustard to each quart of vinegar
- half a cup of white sugar

## Directions

1. Break the heads into small pieces and boil ten or fifteen minutes in salt and water
2. remove from the water and drain carefully
3. When cold, place in a jar, and pour over it hot vinegar, in which has been scalded a liberal supply of whole cloves, pepper, allspice and white mustard
4. Tie the spices in a bag, and, on removing the vinegar from the fire, stir into each quart of it two teaspoonfuls of French mustard, and half a cup of white sugar
5. Cover tightly and be sure to have the vinegar cover the pickle
