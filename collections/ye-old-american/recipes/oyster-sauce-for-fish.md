## Ingredients

- a pint of oysters
- anchovy liquor
- half a lemon
- two blades of mace
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald a pint of oysters and strain them through a sieve
2. Wash some more oysters in cold water and take off their beards
3. Put them in a stew-pan and pour the liquor over them
4. Add a large spoonful of anchovy liquor, half a lemon, and two blades of mace
5. Thicken it with butter rolled in flour
6. Put in half a pound of butter and boil it till it is melted
7. Take out the mace and lemon and squeeze the lemon juice into the sauce
8. Boil it and stir it all the time
9. Put it in a boat
