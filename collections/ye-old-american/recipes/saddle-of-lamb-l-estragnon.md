## Ingredients

- lamb
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- four tablespoons butter
- four tablespoons flour
- two cups bouillon
- one-half cup stock
- one tablespoon tarragon

## Directions

1. Wipe meat
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Place on rack in dripping-pan
4. Dredge meat and bottom of pan with flour
5. Bake in hot oven one and one-fourth hours, basting every fifteen minutes
6. Remove to hot serving dish
7. Brown four tablespoons butter
8. Add four tablespoons flour which has been previously browned
9. Pour on gradually while stirring constantly two cups bouillon and one-half cup stock which has infused with one tablespoon tarragon one hour
10. Pour around meat
