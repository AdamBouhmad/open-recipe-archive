## Ingredients

- skimmed milk
- cream
- powdered sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Set a china or glass dish of skimmed milk away in a warm place, covered.
2. When it turns—i. e., becomes a smooth, firm, but not tough cake, like blanc-mange—serve in the same dish.
3. Cut out carefully with a large spoon, and put in saucers, with cream, powdered sugar, and nutmeg to taste.
4. It is better, if set on the ice for an hour before it is brought to table.
5. Do not let it stand until the whey separates from the curd.
