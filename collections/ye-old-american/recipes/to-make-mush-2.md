## Ingredients

- a lump of butter the size of an egg
- a quart of water
- corn meal
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Put a lump of butter the size of an egg into a quart of water
2. Make it sufficiently thick with corn meal and a little salt
3. Mix it perfectly smooth
4. Stir it constantly till done enough
