## Ingredients

- bread crumbs
- fat of bacon
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- yelks of eggs
- lard
- water
- wine
- mushroom catsup
- forcemeat balls

## Directions

1. Take the bone out of the fillet, wrap the flap around and sew it
2. Make a forcemeat of bread crumbs, the fat of bacon, a little onion chopped, parsley, pepper, salt, and a nutmeg pounded
3. Wet it with the yelks of eggs
4. Fill the place from which the bone was taken
5. Make holes around it with a knife and fill them also
6. Lard the top
7. Put it in a Dutch oven with a pint of water
8. Bake it sufficiently
9. Thicken the gravy with butter and brown flour
10. Add a gill of wine and one of mushroom catsup
11. And serve it garnished with forcemeat balls fried
