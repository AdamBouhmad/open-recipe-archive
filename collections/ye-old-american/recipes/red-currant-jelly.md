## Ingredients

- ripe currants
- best loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Select currants that are perfectly ripe and gathered on a dry day
2. Strip them from the stalks and put into a stone jar
3. Cover the jar and set it up to the neck in a kettle of boiling water
4. Keep the water boiling round the jar till the currants are all broken, stirring occasionally
5. Put them into a jelly-bag and squeeze out all the juice
6. To each pint of juice allow a pound and a quarter of the best loaf-sugar
7. Put the sugar into a porcelain kettle and pour the juice over it
8. Stir frequently till all is melted
9. Set the kettle over a moderate fire and let it boil twenty minutes or till the jelly congeals in the spoon when held in the air
10. Skim carefully all the time
11. When done, pour it warm into tumblers
12. Cover each with two rounds of white tissue paper, cut to fit exactly the inside of the glass
