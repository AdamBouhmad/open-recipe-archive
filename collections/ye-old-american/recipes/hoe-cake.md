## Ingredients

- whites of three eggs
- a quart of wheat flour
- a salt-spoon of salt
- two table-spoonfuls of the best fresh yeast

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of three eggs to a stiff froth
2. Sift into a pan a quart of wheat flour, adding a salt-spoon of salt
3. Make a hole in the middle, and mix in the white of egg so as to form a thick batter
4. Add two table-spoonfuls of the best fresh yeast
5. Cover it, and let it stand all night
6. In the morning, take a hoe-iron and prop it before the fire till it is well heated
7. Flour a tea-saucer, and fill it with batter, shake it about, and clap it to the hoe (which must be previously greased)
8. The batter will adhere till it is baked
9. Repeat this with each cake
10. Keep them hot, and eat them with butter
