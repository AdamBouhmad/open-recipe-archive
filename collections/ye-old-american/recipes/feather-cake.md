## Ingredients

- one egg
- one cup of sugar
- one tablespoonful of cold butter
- half a cup of milk
- one and one-half cups of flour
- one teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- half a teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Combine one egg, one cup of sugar, one tablespoonful of cold butter, half a cup of milk, one and one-half cups of flour, one teaspoonful of cream of tartar, half a teaspoonful of soda
2. Bake
3. Eat while it is fresh
