## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 3¼ cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon mace

## Directions

1. Put butter and sugar in a bowl, and stir until well mixed; add eggs well beaten, then milk, and flour mixed and sifted with baking powder and mace.
2. Bake in individual tins.
3. Cover with Chocolate Frosting.
