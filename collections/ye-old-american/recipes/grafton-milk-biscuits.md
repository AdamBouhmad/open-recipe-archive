## Ingredients

- two white potatoes
- two teaspoonfuls of brown sugar
- boiling water
- one small teacupful of yeast
- three ounces of butter
- one pint of milk
- salt
- one-third of a teaspoonful of soda
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil and mash two white potatoes
2. Add two teaspoonfuls of brown sugar
3. Pour boiling water over these, enough to soften them
4. When tepid, add one small teacupful of yeast
5. When light, warm three ounces of butter in one pint of milk with a little salt and one-third of a teaspoonful of soda
6. Add flour enough to make stiff sponge
7. When risen, work it on the board
8. Put it back in the tray to rise again
9. When risen, roll into cakes and let them stand half an hour
10. Bake in a quick oven
