## Ingredients

- Three cups chestnuts
- Three cups highly seasoned chicken stock
- Two tablespoons butter
- One and one-half tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Remove shells from chestnuts, put in a casserole dish, and pour over chicken stock.
2. Cover, and cook in a slow oven three hours.
3. Thicken chicken stock with butter and flour cooked together.
4. Send to table in casserole dish.
