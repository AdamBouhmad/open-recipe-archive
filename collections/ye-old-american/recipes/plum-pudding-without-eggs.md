## Ingredients

- one cupful of finely-chopped beef suet
- two cupfuls of fine bread crumbs
- one cupful of molasses
- one cupful of chopped raisins
- one cupful of well-washed currants
- one spoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Stir thoroughly together one cupful of finely-chopped beef suet, two cupfuls of fine bread crumbs, one cupful of molasses, one cupful of chopped raisins, one cupful of well-washed currants, and one spoonful of salt
