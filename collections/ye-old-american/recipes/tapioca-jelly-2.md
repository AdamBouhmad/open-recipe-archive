## Ingredients

- A cupful of tapioca
- A quart of cold water
- Juice of a lemon
- Grated lemon peel
- A pinch of salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the tapioca thoroughly two or three times
2. Soak it in a quart of cold water for three or four hours
3. Simmer it in a stewpan until it becomes quite clear, stirring often
4. Add the juice of a lemon and a little of the grated peel
5. Add a pinch of salt
6. Sweeten to taste
