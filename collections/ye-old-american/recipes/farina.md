## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 cup fresh milk
- 1 large tablespoonful Hecker's Farina, wet up with cold water
- 2 teaspoonfuls white sugar
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Stir the farina into the boiling water (slightly salted) in the farina kettle (one boiler set within another, the latter filled with hot water)
2. Boil fifteen minutes, stirring constantly until it is well-thickened
3. Add the milk, stirring it in gradually
4. Boil fifteen minutes longer
5. Sweeten and give to the child so soon as it is cool enough
