## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- ½ cup sugar
- 2½ cups flour
- 3½ teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients and work in butter with tips of fingers
2. Beat egg, add milk, and combine mixtures
3. Turn into buttered mould
4. Cover and steam two hours
5. Serve with warm Apple Sauce and Hard Sauce
