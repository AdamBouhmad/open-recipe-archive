## Ingredients

- fore-quarter of lamb
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- flour
- cold milk
- fresh parsley

## Directions

1. Take off the shoulder and lay it upon the gridiron with the breast
2. Cut in two parts to facilitate its cooking
3. Put a tin sheet on top of the meat and a weight upon that
4. Turn the meat around frequently to prevent its burning
5. Turn over as soon as cooked on one side
6. Renew the coals occasionally that all parts may cook alike
7. When done, season with butter, pepper and salt
8. Heat a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan for cream sauce
9. Add a teaspoonful of flour and stir until perfectly smooth
10. Add slowly, stirring in, a cup of cold milk
11. Let it boil up once
12. Season to taste with salt and pepper and a teaspoonful of finely chopped fresh parsley
13. Serve in a gravy boat, all hot
