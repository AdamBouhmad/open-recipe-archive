## Ingredients

- 1 quart fresh celery, chopped fine, or 1/4 pound celery seed
- 1 quart best vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful white sugar

## Directions

1. Put the celery or seed into a jar
2. Heat the vinegar, sugar and salt
3. Pour it boiling hot over the celery
4. Let it cool and cover it tightly
5. Set away for two weeks
6. Strain and bottle
