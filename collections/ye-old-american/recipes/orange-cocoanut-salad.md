## Ingredients

- a dozen oranges
- cocoanut
- pineapple
- sweetened wine
- small cakes

## Directions

1. Peel and slice a dozen oranges, grate a cocoanut and slice a pineapple
2. Put alternate layers of each until the dish is full
3. Pour over them sweetened wine
4. Serve with small cakes
