## Ingredients

- half a pint of new milk
- one teaspoonful of wheat flour
- four ounces of sugar
- the yolks of three eggs
- one lemon

## Directions

1. Put half a pint of new milk on the fire
2. When it boils stir into it one teaspoonful of wheat flour, four ounces of sugar and the well-beaten yolks of three eggs
3. Remove it from the fire and add the grated rind and the juice of one lemon
4. Stir it well and serve hot in a sauce tureen
