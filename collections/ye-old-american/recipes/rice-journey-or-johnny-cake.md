## Ingredients

- a pint of rice
- a tea-spoonful of salt
- a large spoonful of butter
- a pint of rice flour
- half a pint of milk

## Directions

1. Boil a pint of rice quite soft, with a tea-spoonful of salt
2. Mix with it while hot a large spoonful of butter, and spread it on a dish to cool
3. When perfectly cold, add a pint of rice flour and half a pint of milk
4. Beat them all together till well mingled
5. Take the middle part of the head of a barrel, make it quite clean, wet it, and put on the mixture about an inch thick
6. Smooth with a spoon, and baste it with a little milk
7. Set the board aslant before clear coals
8. When sufficiently baked, slip a thread under the cake and turn it
9. Baste and bake that side in a similar manner
10. Split it, and butter while hot
