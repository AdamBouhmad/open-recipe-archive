## Ingredients

- a teacupful of rice
- a tablespoonful of cold butter
- a cupful of sugar
- a teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
- one of ground cinnamon
- four eggs
- a quart of sweet milk

## Directions

1. Pick over, wash and boil a teacupful of rice
2. When soft drain off the water
3. While warm, add to it a tablespoonful of cold butter
4. When cool, mix with it a cupful of sugar, a teaspoonful of grated nutmeg and one of ground cinnamon
5. Beat up four eggs very light, whites and yolks separately
6. Add them to the rice
7. Stir in a quart of sweet milk gradually
8. Butter a pudding-dish and turn in the mixture
9. Bake one hour in a moderate oven
10. Serve warm with sweet wine sauce
