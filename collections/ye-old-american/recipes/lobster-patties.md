## Ingredients

- boiled lobster
- lobster claws
- spawn
- gravy or broth
- bread crumbs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- lemon
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Cut some boiled lobster in small pieces
2. Take the small claws and the spawn, put them in a suitable dish, and jam them to a paste with a potato masher
3. Add to them a ladleful of gravy or broth, with a few bread crumbs
4. Set it over the fire and boil
5. Strain it through a strainer or sieve to the thickness of a cream
6. Put half of it to your lobsters, and save the other half to sauce them with after they are baked
7. Put to the lobster the bigness of an egg of butter, a little pepper and salt
8. Squeeze in a lemon, and warm these over the fire enough to melt the butter
9. Set it to cool
10. Sheet your patty pan or a plate or dish with good puff paste
11. Put in your lobster, and cover it with a paste
12. Bake it within three-quarters of an hour before you want it
13. When it is baked, cut up your cover, and warm up the other half of your sauce with a little butter to the thickness of cream
14. Pour it over your patty, with a little squeezed lemon
15. Cut your cover in two, and lay it on the top, two inches distant
