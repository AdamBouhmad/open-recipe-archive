## Ingredients

- 1/4 box gelatine or 1 tablespoon granulated gelatine
- 1/4 cup cold water
- 2 cups scalded milk
- Yolks of 3 eggs
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 tablespoon brandy
- 5 lady fingers
- 6 macaroons

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cold water
2. Add to custard made of milk, eggs, sugar, salt
3. Strain and cool slightly
4. Flavor
5. Place a mould in pan of ice-water
6. Decorate with candied cherries and angelica
7. Cover with mixture, added carefully by spoonfuls
8. When firm, add layer of lady fingers (first soaked in custard)
9. Then layer of macaroons (also soaked in custard)
10. Repeat, care being taken that each layer is firm before another is added
11. Garnish and serve with Cream Sauce I and candied cherries
