## Ingredients

- thin slices of bread
- melted cheese
- prepared mustard

## Directions

1. Toast thin slices of bread an even, crisp brown
2. Place on a warm plate, allowing one small slice to each person
3. Pour on enough melted cheese to cover them
4. Serve while warm
5. Many prefer a little prepared mustard spread over the toast before putting on the cheese
