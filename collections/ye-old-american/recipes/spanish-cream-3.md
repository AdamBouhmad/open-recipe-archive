## Ingredients

- 1/4 box gelatine or 1 tablespoon granulated gelatine
- 3 cups milk
- Whites of 3 eggs
- Yolk of 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup sugar (scant)
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla or 3 tablespoons wine

## Directions

1. Scald milk with gelatine
2. Add sugar
3. Pour slowly on yolks of eggs slightly beaten
4. Return to double boiler and cook until thickened, stirring constantly
5. Remove from range
6. Add salt, flavoring, and whites of eggs beaten stiff
7. Turn into individual moulds, first dipped in cold water
8. Chill
9. Serve with cream
