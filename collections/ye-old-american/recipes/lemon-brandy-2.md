## Ingredients

- lemon peels
- brandy
- kernels of peach and plum stones

## Directions

1. Cut lemon peels in small pieces--the thin yellow outside--and put them in a glass jar or bottle of brandy
2. Keep for use the kernels of peach and plum stones, pounding them slightly before putting them into the brandy
