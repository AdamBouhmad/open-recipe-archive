## Ingredients

- five cents' worth of bay rum
- five cents' worth of magnesia snowflake
- five cents' worth of bergamot
- five cents' worth of oil of lemon
- rain-water

## Directions

1. mix in a pint bottle and fill up with rain-water
2. Shake well
3. apply with a soft sponge or cloth
