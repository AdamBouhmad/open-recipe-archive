## Ingredients

- medium-sized sheepshead
- salt
- parsley
- lemon
- two ounces of butter
- flour
- a tablespoonful of lemon juice
- half a tablespoonful of chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Select a medium-sized fish, clean it thoroughly, and rub a little salt over it
2. Wrap it in a cloth and put it in a steamer
3. Place this over a pot of fast-boiling water and steam one hour
4. Lay it whole upon a hot side-dish
5. Garnish with tufts of parsley and slices of lemon
6. Serve with drawn butter prepared as follows: Take two ounces of butter and roll it into small balls, dredge these with flour
7. Put one-fourth of them in a saucepan, and as they begin to melt, whisk them
8. Add the remainder, one at a time, until thoroughly smooth
9. While stirring, add a tablespoonful of lemon juice, half a tablespoonful of chopped parsley
10. Pour into a hot sauce boat and serve
