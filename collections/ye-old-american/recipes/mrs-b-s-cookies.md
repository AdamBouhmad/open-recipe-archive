## Ingredients

- 6 eggs, whites and yolks separately
- 1 cup butter
- 3 cups sugar
- Flour to make batter just stiff enough to be moulded with well-floured hands
- Lemon for flavor

## Directions

1. Make into round cakes and bake in a quick oven.
