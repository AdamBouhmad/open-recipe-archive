## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- ½ teaspoon soda
- ½ cup sour milk
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Flour
- Currant jelly

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, egg well beaten, soda mixed with milk, salt and flour to make a soft dough.
2. Chill and shape, using a round cutter.
3. On the centres of one-half the pieces put currant jelly.
