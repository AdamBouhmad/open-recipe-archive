## Ingredients

- two large oranges
- seeds removed
- half a peeled lemon
- one cup of sugar
- white of an egg

## Directions

1. Peel two large oranges, remove the seeds, chop them fine
2. Add half a peeled lemon and one cup of sugar
3. Beat the white of an egg well and add to the mixture
4. Spread between the layers of Silver Cake
