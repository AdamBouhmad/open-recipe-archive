## Ingredients

- White Part: whites of four eggs
- one cup of white sugar
- half a cup of butter
- half a cup of sweet milk
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- one teaspoonful of vanilla or lemon
- two and a half cups of sifted flour
- Dark Part: yolks of four eggs
- one cup of brown sugar
- half a cup of cooking molasses
- half a cup of butter
- half a cup of sour milk
- one teaspoonful of ground cloves
- one teaspoonful of cinnamon
- one teaspoonful of mace
- one nutmeg grated
- one teaspoonful of soda
- one and a half cups of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Drop a spoonful of each kind in a well-buttered cake-dish, first the light part, then the dark, alternately
2. Try to drop it so that the cake shall be well-streaked through, so that it has the appearance of marble
