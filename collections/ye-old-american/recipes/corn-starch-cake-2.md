## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup corn-starch
- 2 cups flour
- 4½ teaspoons baking powder
- Whites 5 eggs
- ¾ teaspoon vanilla or ½ teaspoon almond extract

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for mixing butter cakes.
2. This mixture makes two loaves.
