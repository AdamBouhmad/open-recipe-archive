## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 cups flour
- 5 eggs
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoonful soda dissolved in hot water
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- 1 qt. ripe, fresh huckleberries, thickly dredged with flour

## Directions

1. Stir the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add the beaten yolks
3. Add the milk, the flour, and spice
4. Add the whites whipped stiff and the soda
5. At the last stir in the huckleberries with a wooden spoon or paddle, not to bruise them
6. Bake in a loaf or card, in a moderate but steady oven, until a straw comes out clean from the thickest part
