## Ingredients

- one quart of southern corn meal, sifted
- a teaspoonful of salt
- a tablespoonful of butter or lard melted
- cold water

## Directions

1. Mix with cold water into a soft dough one quart of southern corn meal, sifted, a teaspoonful of salt, a tablespoonful of butter or lard melted
2. Mold into oval cakes with the hands
3. bake in a very hot oven, in well-greased pans
4. To be eaten hot
5. The crust should be brown
