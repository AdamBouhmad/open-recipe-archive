## Ingredients

- large fine potatoes
- cold butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Take large fine potatoes; wash and dry them
2. Either lay them on the hearth and keep them buried in hot wood ashes, or bake them slowly in a Dutch oven
3. They will not be done in less than two hours
4. It will save time to half-boil them before they are roasted
5. Send them to table with the skins on, and eat them with cold butter and salt
