## Ingredients

- parsnips
- cream
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil and scrape them, mash smooth with the back of a wooden-spoon, or a potato beetle, picking out the fibres.
2. Mix in three or four spoonfuls of cream, a great spoonful of butter, pepper and salt to taste.
3. Heat to boiling in a saucepan, and serve.
4. Heap in a mound as you would potato cooked in the same way.
