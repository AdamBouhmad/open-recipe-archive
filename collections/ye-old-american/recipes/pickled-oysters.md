## Ingredients

- oysters
- clean water
- salt
- blades of mace
- whole black pepper
- pale vinegar

## Directions

1. Select the largest oysters
2. Drain off their liquor and wash them in clean water
3. Pick out the pieces of shells that may be left
4. Put them in a stew pan with water proportioned to the number of oysters, some salt, blades of mace, and whole black pepper
5. Stew them a few minutes
6. Put them in a pot
7. When cold, add as much pale vinegar as will give the liquor an agreeable acid
