## Ingredients

- two cups of raised dough
- one cup of sugar
- half a cup of butter
- two well-beaten eggs
- flour
- damp sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take two cups of raised dough, one cup of sugar and half a cup of butter
2. Add two well-beaten eggs
3. Use flour enough to make a stiff dough
4. Set to rise
5. When light mold into high biscuit and let rise again
6. Rub damp sugar and cinnamon over the top
7. Place in the oven and bake about twenty minutes
