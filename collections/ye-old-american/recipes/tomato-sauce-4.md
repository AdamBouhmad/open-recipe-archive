## Ingredients

- Four tablespoons butter
- Five tablespoons flour
- One and one-half cups Brown Stock
- One and one-half cups stewed and strained tomatoes
- One slice onion
- One slice carrot
- A bit of bay leaf
- A sprig of parsley
- Four cloves
- Three-fourths teaspoon salt
- One-fourth teaspoon pepper
- A few grains cayenne

## Directions

1. Brown butter, add flour, and pour on gradually Brown Stock and stewed and strained tomatoes.
2. Add onion, carrot, bay leaf, parsley, cloves, salt, pepper, and cayenne.
3. Cook ten minutes, and strain.
