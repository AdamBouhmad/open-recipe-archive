## Ingredients

- firm, sound, green peppers
- a few red peppers
- chopped cabbage
- green tomatoes
- salt
- mustard seed
- ground cloves
- vinegar
- a cup of brown sugar

## Directions

1. Select firm, sound, green peppers, and add a few red ones as they are ornamental and look well upon the table
2. With a sharp knife remove the top, take out the seed
3. soak over night in salt water
4. fill with chopped cabbage and green tomatoes, seasoned with salt, mustard seed and ground cloves
5. Sew on the top
6. Boil vinegar sufficient to cover them, with a cup of brown sugar, and pour over the mangoes
7. Do this three mornings, then seal
