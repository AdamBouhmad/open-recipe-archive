## Ingredients

- winter squash
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- cold water

## Directions

1. Pare, take out the seeds, cut into small pieces, and stew until soft and tender.
2. Drain, press well, to rid it of all the water, and mash with butter, pepper, and salt.
3. It will take much longer to cook than the summer squash, and before you put it into hot water, should lie in cold at least two hours.
