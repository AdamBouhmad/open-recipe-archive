## Ingredients

- Pint or three cups peas
- Wooden spoon
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter
- Two whipped eggs
- Cupful of milk
- Quarter teaspoonful soda
- Half teaspoonful cream tartar
- Half a cup of flour

## Directions

1. Cook a pint or three cups more peas than you need for dinner.
2. Mash while hot with a wooden spoon, seasoning with pepper, salt, and butter.
3. Put by until morning.
4. Make a batter of two whipped eggs, a cupful of milk, quarter teaspoonful soda, a half teaspoonful cream tartar, and half a cup of flour.
5. Stir the pea-mixture into this, beating very hard.
6. Cook as you would ordinary griddle-cakes.
