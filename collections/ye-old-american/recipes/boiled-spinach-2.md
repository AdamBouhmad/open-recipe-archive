## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Piece of butter the size of an egg
- Pepper
- Hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Pick it over very carefully; it is apt to be gritty.
2. Wash in several waters, and let it lie in the last half an hour at least.
3. Take out with your hands, shaking each bunch well, and put into boiling water, with a little salt.
4. Boil from fifteen to twenty minutes.
5. When tender, drain thoroughly, chop very fine.
6. Put into a saucepan with a piece of butter the size of an egg, and pepper to taste.
7. Stir until very hot, turn into a dish and shape into a flat-topped mound with a silver or wooden spoon.
8. Slice some hard-boiled eggs and lay on top.
