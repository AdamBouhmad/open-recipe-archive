## Ingredients

- lamb
- water
- salt
- shelled peas
- pepper
- potatoes
- butter
- flour
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash a small quarter of lamb in cold water, and put it into a soup-pot with six quarts of cold water
2. Add to it two tablespoonfuls of salt, and set it over a moderate fire
3. Let it boil gently for two hours, then skim it clear
4. Add a quart of shelled peas, and a teaspoonful of pepper
5. Cover it, and let it boil for half an hour
6. Having scraped the skins from a quart of small young potatoes, add them to the soup
7. Cover the pot and let it boil for half an hour longer
8. Work quarter of a pound of butter and a dessertspoonful of flour together, and add them to the soup ten or twelve minutes before taking it off the fire
9. Serve the meat on a dish with parsley sauce over it, and the soup in a tureen
