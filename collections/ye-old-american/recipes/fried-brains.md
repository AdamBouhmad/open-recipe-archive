## Ingredients

- brains
- flour
- egg
- bread crumbs
- butter or well-clarified drippings
- gravy

## Directions

1. Well wash the brains and soak them in cold water until white.
2. Parboil them until tender in a small saucepan for about a quarter of an hour.
3. Thoroughly drain them and place them on a board.
4. Divide them into small pieces with a knife.
5. Dip each piece into flour, and then roll them in egg and bread crumbs.
6. Fry them in butter or well-clarified drippings.
7. Serve very hot with gravy.
