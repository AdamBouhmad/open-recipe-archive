## Ingredients

- lobster meat
- salt
- cayenne
- made mustard
- lemon juice
- salad dressing
- crisp lettuce leaf

## Directions

1. Remove lobster meat from shell, and chop.
2. Season with salt, cayenne, made mustard, and lemon juice; or moisten with any salad dressing.
3. Spread mixture on a crisp lettuce leaf, and prepare as other sandwiches.
