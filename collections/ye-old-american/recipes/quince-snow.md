## Ingredients

- five fair-looking quinces
- water
- sugar
- the whites of three or four eggs

## Directions

1. Quarter five fair-looking quinces and boil them till they are tender in water
2. Peel them and push them through a coarse sieve
3. Sweeten to the taste and add the whites of three or four eggs
4. Beat all to a stiff froth with an egg-whisk
5. Pile with a spoon upon a glass dish and set away in the ice box
