## Ingredients

- fresh salmon
- salt
- horseradish
- parsley
- lobster sauce
- whole pepper
- whole cloves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, handling it as little as possible, and cutting it open no more than is absolutely necessary.
2. Place it on the strainer of a large fish-kettle and fill it up with cold water.
3. Throw in a handful of salt.
4. Let it boil slowly. Allow a quarter of an hour to each pound.
5. Skim it well or the colour will be bad.
6. When it is completely boiled, lift up the strainer and rest it across the top of the kettle, that the fish may drain.
7. Cover it with a soft napkin or flannel several folds double, to keep it firm by absorbing the moisture.
8. Send it to table on a hot dish.
9. Garnish with scraped horseradish and curled parsley.
10. Have ready a small tureen of lobster sauce to accompany the salmon.
