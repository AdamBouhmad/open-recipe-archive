## Ingredients

- nine tablespoonfuls of fine white sugar
- nine eggs
- nine tablespoonfuls of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Put nine tablespoonfuls of fine white sugar into a bowl and put the bowl into hot water to heat the sugar
2. When the sugar is thoroughly heated, break nine eggs into the bowl and beat them quickly until they become a little warm and rather thick
3. Take the bowl from the water and continue beating until it is nearly or quite cold
4. Stir in lightly nine tablespoonfuls of sifted flour
5. With a paper funnel, or something of the kind, lay this mixture out upon papers, in biscuits three inches long and half an inch thick, in the form of fingers
6. Sift sugar over the biscuits and bake them upon tins to a light brown
7. When they are done and cold, remove them from the papers by wetting them on the back
8. Dry them and they are ready for use
