## Ingredients

- 2 gallons vinegar
- 1 pint white mustard seed
- 4 oz. ginger
- 3 oz. pepper-corns
- 1 oz. allspice
- 2 oz. cloves
- 1 oz. mace
- 1 oz. nutmeg
- 2 oz. turmeric
- 1 large handful of garlic, chopped
- 1 handful scraped horseradish
- 4 lbs. sugar
- 2 oz. celery seed
- 3 lemons, sliced thin
- cabbage
- brine
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix all spices, garlic, horseradish, sugar, celery seed, and lemons and set in the sun for three days.
2. Cut cabbage in quarters—leaving off the outer and green leaves—and put in a kettle of boiling brine.
3. Cook three minutes.
4. Take out, drain, and cover thickly with salt.
5. Spread out in the sun to dry; then shake off the salt, and cover with cold vinegar in which has been steeped enough turmeric to color it well.
6. Leave it in this two weeks, to draw out the salt and to plump the cabbage.
7. Pack down in the seasoned vinegar.
8. Do not use under six weeks or two months.
