## Ingredients

- nice steaks
- boiled ham
- puff paste
- gravy

## Directions

1. Cut nice steaks, and stew them till half done
2. Put a puff paste in the dish
3. Lay in the steaks with a few slices of boiled ham
4. Season the gravy very high
5. Pour it in the dish
6. Put on a lid of paste and bake it
