## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- ½ cup corn meal
- 1½ tablespoons baking powder
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- ⅓ cup sugar
- 1½ cups boiling water
- 1¼ cups milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Add meal to boiling water, and boil five minutes; turn into bowl, add milk, and remaining dry ingredients mixed and sifted, then the egg well beaten, and butter.
2. Cook same as other griddle-cakes.
