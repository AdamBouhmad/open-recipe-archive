## Ingredients

- six eggs
- half a cupful of sugar
- one quart of new milk
- extract or flavoring
- nutmeg or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and the sugar and milk, and any extract or flavoring you like.
2. Fill your custard cups, sift a little nutmeg or cinnamon over the tops.
3. Set them in a moderate oven in a shallow pan half filled with hot water.
4. In about twenty minutes try them with the handle of a teaspoon to see if they are firm.
5. Serve cold with fresh fruit sugared and placed on top of each. Strawberries, peaches or raspberries, as preferred.
