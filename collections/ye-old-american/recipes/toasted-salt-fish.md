## Ingredients

- salt codfish
- butter

## Directions

1. Pick salt codfish in long thin strips.
2. If very salt, it may need to be freshened by standing for a short time in lukewarm water.
3. Place on a greased wire broiler, and broil until brown on one side; turn, and brown the other.
4. Remove to platter, and spread with butter.
