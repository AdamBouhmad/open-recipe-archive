## Ingredients

- one pound of arrowroot
- quarter of a pound of pounded white sugar
- half a pound of butter
- whites of six eggs
- essence of almonds, or vanilla, or lemon (flavoring to taste)

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Stir in the sugar and arrowroot gradually, at the same time beating the mixture
3. Whisk the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
4. Add them to the other ingredients and beat well for twenty minutes
5. Put in whichever of the above flavorings may be preferred
6. Pour the cake into a buttered mold or tin and bake it in a moderate oven from one to one and a half hours
