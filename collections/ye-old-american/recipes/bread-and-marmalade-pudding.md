## Ingredients

- Bread and butter
- Marmalade or jam
- 4 eggs
- 3 cups milk
- ¾ cup sugar
- Vanilla or other extract
- Nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of bread (stale), spread thickly with butter and marmalade or jam
2. Fit them neatly and closely into a buttered pudding-dish until it is half full
3. Lay a small, heavy plate upon them to prevent them from floating, and saturate them gradually with a hot custard made of the milk, heated almost to boiling, then taken from the fire, and the beaten eggs and sugar stirred in with the seasoning
4. Let the bread soak in this fifteen minutes or so, adding by degrees all the custard
5. Just before you put the pudding in the oven, take up the plate gently
6. If the bread still rise to the top, keep down with a silver fork or spoon, laid upon it from the side of the dish, until the custard thickens, when slip it out
7. Eat cold
