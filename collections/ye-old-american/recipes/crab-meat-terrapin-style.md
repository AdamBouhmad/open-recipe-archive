## Ingredients

- 1 cup crab meat
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ small onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons Sherry wine
- ⅓ cup heavy cream
- Yolks of 2 eggs
- Salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. Cook butter and onion until yellow; remove onion, add crab meat and wine.
2. Cook three minutes, add cream, yolks of eggs, salt, and cayenne.
