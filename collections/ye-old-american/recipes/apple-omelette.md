## Ingredients

- 6 large pippins
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 8 eggs
- 5 or 6 tablespoonfuls sugar
- Nutmeg to taste
- 1 teaspoonful rose-water

## Directions

1. Stew the apples, when you have pared and cored them, as for apple-sauce
2. Beat them very smooth while hot, adding the butter, sugar, and nutmeg
3. When perfectly cold, put with the eggs, which should be whipped light, yolks and whites separately
4. Put in the yolks first, then the rose-water, lastly the whites
5. Pour into a deep bake-dish, which has been warmed and buttered
6. Bake in a moderate oven until it is delicately browned
7. Eat warm—not hot—for tea, with Graham bread
