## Ingredients

- ten large, tart apples
- half a cupful of water
- yolks of six eggs, or three whole ones
- juice and grated outside rind of two lemons
- half a cup of butter
- one and a half cups of sugar
- puff paste
- whites of three eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Pare, quarter, core and boil in half a cupful of water, until quite soft, ten large, tart apples
2. beat until very smooth and add the yolks of six eggs, or three whole ones, the juice and grated outside rind of two lemons, half a cup of butter; one and a half of sugar
3. beat all thoroughly, line patty-pans with a puff paste and fill
4. bake five minutes in a hot oven
5. If desired very nice, cover them when removed from the oven with the meringue made of the whites of three eggs remaining, mixed with three tablespoonfuls of sugar
6. return to the oven and delicately brown
