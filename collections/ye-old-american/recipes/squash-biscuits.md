## Ingredients

- ½ cup squash (steamed and sifted)
- ¼ cup sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup scalded milk
- ¼ yeast cake dissolved in ¼ cup lukewarm water
- ¼ cup butter
- 2½ cups flour

## Directions

1. Add squash, sugar, salt, and butter to milk; when lukewarm, add dissolved yeast cake and flour
2. Cover, and let rise over night
3. In morning shape into biscuits, let rise, and bake
