## Ingredients

- sugar-corn
- salt
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Choose young sugar-corn, full grown, but not hard; test with the nail.
2. Clean by stripping off the outer leaves, turn back the innermost covering carefully, and pick off every thread of silk.
3. Recover the ear with the thin husk that grew nearest it and tie at the top with a bit of thread.
4. Put into boiling salted water and cook fast from twenty minutes to half an hour, in proportion to size and age.
5. Cut off the stalks close to the cob and send whole to table wrapped in a napkin.
6. Or, cut from the cob while hot and season with butter, pepper and salt, sending to table in a vegetable dish.
