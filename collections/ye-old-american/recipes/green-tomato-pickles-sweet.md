## Ingredients

- 1 peck green tomatoes, sliced
- salt
- 1 dozen good-sized onions, coarsely sliced
- 6 red peppers, chopped coarsely
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoonful ground allspice
- 1 tablespoonful ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful cloves
- 1 tablespoonful mustard
- 3 pints good vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice the tomatoes the day before you are ready for pickling
2. Sprinkle them through and through with salt, not too heavily
3. In the morning drain off the liquor
4. Have a dozen good-sized onions rather coarsely sliced
5. Take a suitable kettle and put in a layer of the sliced tomatoes, then of onions
6. Between each layer sprinkle the spices: red peppers, sugar, allspice, cinnamon, cloves, and mustard
7. Turn over three pints of good vinegar or enough to completely cover them
8. Boil until tender
