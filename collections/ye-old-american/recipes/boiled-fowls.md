## Ingredients

- fowls
- flour
- cold water
- white sauce or melted butter

## Directions

1. Dust the fowls well with flour
2. Put them in a kettle of cold water
3. Cover it close and set it on the fire
4. When the scum begins to rise, take it off
5. Let them boil very slowly for twenty minutes
6. Take them off and cover them close
7. Let the heat of the water stew them enough in half an hour
8. When you take them up, drain them
9. Pour over them white sauce or melted butter
