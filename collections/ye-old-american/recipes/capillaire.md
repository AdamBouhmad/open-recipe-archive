## Ingredients

- eight pounds of loaf-sugar
- three pints of water
- three eggs well beaten with their shells
- two wine glasses of orange flower water
- lemon juice and water

## Directions

1. Powder the loaf-sugar, and wet it with water and beaten eggs with their shells
2. Stir the whole mass very hard, and boil it twice over, skimming it well
3. Strain it, and stir in orange flower water
4. Bottle it, and use it for a summer draught, mixed with lemon juice and water; or sweeten punch with it
