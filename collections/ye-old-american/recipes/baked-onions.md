## Ingredients

- large Spanish or Bermuda onions
- salt
- water
- butter
- tissue-paper
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash clean, but do not remove the skins.
2. Boil in boiling water for an hour, slightly salted.
3. Change the water twice during this time, always replenishing with more boiling-hot water.
4. Turn off the water and lay the onions upon a cloth to absorb or evaporate the moisture.
5. Roll each in a round piece of buttered tissue-paper, twisting it at the top to keep it closed.
6. Bake in a slow oven nearly an hour.
7. When tender all through, peel them.
8. Put them into a deep dish and brown slightly, basting with butter freely for perhaps a quarter of an hour more.
9. Serve in a vegetable dish and pour the melted butter over them when you have sprinkled with pepper and salt.
