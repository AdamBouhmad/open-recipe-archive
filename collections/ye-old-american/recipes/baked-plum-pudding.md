## Ingredients

- 1¼ lb. of flour
- 1 lb. raisins seeded, cut in two, and dredged with flour
- ½ lb. suet, freed from strings and powdered
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 oz. citron, shred fine
- 5 eggs—whites and yolks beaten separately
- Nutmeg, cinnamon, and cloves—one teaspoonful each
- Milk to make a thick batter of the flour, beginning with two cups and adding more if necessary

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and sugar together
2. Add the suet and spice, then the flour, moistening the mixture gradually with milk until you can move the spoon in it
3. Dredge the fruit and put in by degrees
4. Finally, stir in the beaten whites
5. Beat all very hard and long before baking in a buttered mould
6. Bake at least an hour and a half in a moderate oven
7. Turn out, and eat with rich sweet sauce
