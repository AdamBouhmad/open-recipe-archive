## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ¾ cup milk
- 1½ squares Baker's chocolate
- ⅓ cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons hot water
- 3 eggs
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, add flour, and pour on gradually, while stirring constantly, milk
2. Cook until boiling-point is reached
3. Melt chocolate in a small saucepan placed over hot water
4. Add sugar and water, and stir until smooth
5. Combine mixtures, and add yolks of eggs well beaten
6. Cool
7. Fold in whites of eggs beaten stiff, and add vanilla
8. Turn into a buttered baking-dish, and bake in a moderate oven twenty-five minutes
9. Serve with Cream Sauce I
