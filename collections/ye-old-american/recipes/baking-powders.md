## Ingredients

- 1 ounce super-carbonate soda
- 7 drachms tartaric acid (in powder)

## Directions

1. Roll smoothly and mix thoroughly.
2. Keep in a tight glass jar or bottle.
3. Use one teaspoonful to a quart of flour.
