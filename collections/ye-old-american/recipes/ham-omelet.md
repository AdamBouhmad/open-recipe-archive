## Ingredients

- six ounces of cold boiled ham
- pepper
- six eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Mince the ham very fine, adding a little pepper
2. Beat separately the whites and yolks of six eggs, then mix them together
3. Add to them gradually the minced ham
4. Beat the whole very hard and do not let it stand a moment after it is thoroughly mixed
5. Have ready some boiling lard in a frying-pan and put in the omelet immediately
6. Fry it about ten minutes or a quarter of an hour
7. When done, put it on a hot dish, trim off the edges, and fold it over in a half moon
8. Send it to table hot and covered
