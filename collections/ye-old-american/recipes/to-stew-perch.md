## Ingredients

- perch
- salt
- pepper
- chopped onion
- water
- a gill of wine
- one gill of catsup
- lemon pickle
- spice

## Directions

1. Lay the perch in a deep pan with the heads on
2. sprinkle salt, pepper, and a little chopped onion over each layer
3. when they are all in, take as much water as will be sufficient to fill the pan less than half full
4. add a gill of wine, one of catsup, a little lemon pickle and spice
5. cover the pan, and let it stew gently till done
6. take out the fish without breaking, put them in a deep dish, pour the gravy on, and neatly turn them out
