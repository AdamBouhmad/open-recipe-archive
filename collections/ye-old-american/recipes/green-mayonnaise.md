## Ingredients

- Mayonnaise Dressing I
- Parsley
- Watercress

## Directions

1. Color Mayonnaise Dressing I with juices expressed from parsley and watercress, using one-half as much parsley as watercress.
2. To obtain coloring, break greens in pieces, pound in a mortar until thoroughly macerated, then squeeze through cheese-cloth.
