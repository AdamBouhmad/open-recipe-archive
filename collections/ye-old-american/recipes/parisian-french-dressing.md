## Ingredients

- ½ cup olive oil
- 5 tablespoons vinegar
- ½ teaspoon powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped Bermuda onion
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
- 4 red peppers
- 8 green peppers
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in the order given.
2. Let stand one hour, then stir vigorously for five minutes.
3. This is especially fine with lettuce, romaine, chiccory, or endive. The red and green peppers are the small ones found in pepper sauce.
