## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 heaping teaspoonfuls arrowroot
- 2 heaping teaspoonfuls white sugar
- 1 tablespoonful brandy or 3 tablespoonfuls of wine

## Directions

1. Prepare and serve as arrowroot jelly, adding the brandy or wine.
