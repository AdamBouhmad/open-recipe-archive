## Ingredients

- 1 quart new unskimmed milk
- 3 eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately, and very stiff
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- Rice flour

## Directions

1. Mix the beaten yolks with the milk
2. Add the salt, then rice flour to make a batter as thick as that for flannel cakes
3. Lastly, whip in the stiffened whites very lightly, and bake immediately
