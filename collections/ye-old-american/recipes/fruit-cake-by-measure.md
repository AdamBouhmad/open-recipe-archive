## Ingredients

- Two scant teacupfuls of butter
- three cupfuls of dark brown sugar
- six eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately
- one pound of raisins, seeded
- one pound of currants, washed and dried
- half a pound of citron cut in thin strips
- half a cupful of cooking molasses
- half a cupful of sour milk
- half a grated nutmeg
- one tablespoonful of ground cinnamon
- one teaspoonful of cloves
- one teaspoonful of mace
- a wine-glass of brandy
- four cupfuls of sifted flour
- one level teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Stir the butter and sugar to a cream
2. add to that half a grated nutmeg, one tablespoonful of ground cinnamon, one teaspoonful of cloves, one teaspoonful of mace
3. add the molasses and sour milk
4. Stir all well; then put in the beaten yolks of eggs, a wine-glass of brandy
5. stir again all thoroughly, and then add four cupfuls of sifted flour alternately with the beaten whites of eggs
6. Dissolve a level teaspoonful of soda and stir in thoroughly
7. Mix the fruit together and stir into it two heaping tablespoonfuls of flour
8. stir it in the cake
9. Butter two common-sized baking tins carefully, line them with letter paper well buttered
10. bake in a moderate oven two hours
11. After it is baked, let it cool in the pan
12. Afterward put it into a tight can, or let it remain in the pans and cover tightly
