## Ingredients

- 1 grouse
- lardoons
- salt
- melted butter
- flour
- trimmings of fat salt pork
- Bread Sauce
- coarse brown bread crumbs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean, remove pinions, and if it be tough the skin covering breast.
2. Lard breast and insert two lardoons in each leg.
3. Truss, and place on trivet in small shallow pan.
4. Rub with salt, brush over with melted butter, dredge with flour, and surround with trimmings of fat salt pork.
5. Bake twenty to twenty-five minutes in a hot oven, basting three times.
6. Arrange on platter, remove string and skewers, pour around Bread Sauce, and sprinkle bird and sauce with coarse brown bread crumbs.
7. Garnish with parsley.
