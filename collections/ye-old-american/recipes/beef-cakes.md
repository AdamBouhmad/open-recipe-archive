## Ingredients

- cold roast beef that has been under-done
- grated bread crumbs
- chopped onion
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- beef-dripping
- walnut or onion pickle
- scraped cold tongue or ham
- mashed potato
- butter

## Directions

1. Take some cold roast beef that has been under-done, and mince it very fine.
2. Mix with it grated bread crumbs, and a little chopped onion and parsley.
3. Season it with pepper and salt, and moisten it with some beef-dripping and a little walnut or onion pickle.
4. Add some scraped cold tongue or ham if desired.
5. Make it into broad flat cakes, and spread a coat of mashed potato thinly on the top and bottom of each.
6. Lay a small bit of butter on the top of every cake, and set them in an oven to warm and brown.
