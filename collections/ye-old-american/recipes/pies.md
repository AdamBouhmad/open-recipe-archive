## Ingredients

- any sort of paste
- fruit or other ingredients
- sugar

## Directions

1. Divide the paste into two sheets
2. Spread one sheet over the bottom and sides of a deep dish well buttered
3. Put in the fruit or other ingredients, heaping it higher in the centre
4. Place the other sheet of paste on top as a lid or cover
5. Press the edges closely down and crimp or notch them with a sharp small knife
6. Bake until done
