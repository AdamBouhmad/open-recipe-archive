## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls green mint, chopped fine
- 1 tablespoonful powdered sugar
- Half a teacupful cider vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop the mint, put the sugar and vinegar in a sauce boat, and stir in the mint. Let it stand in a cool place fifteen minutes before sending to table.
