## Ingredients

- salt
- loppered cream

## Directions

1. Stir a little salt into a pan of "loppered" cream.
2. Pour into a linen bag, and let it drain three days, changing the bag every day.
3. Then pack into a wooden cup or mould with holes in the bottom, and press two hours.
4. Wet the mould with cold water before putting in the cream-curd.
5. Wrapped in soft white paper—two or three folds of tissue paper will do—to exclude the air, they will keep in a cool place for a week.
