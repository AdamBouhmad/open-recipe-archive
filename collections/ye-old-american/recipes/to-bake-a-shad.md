## Ingredients

- a large fat shad
- forcemeat
- a pint of water
- a gill of red wine
- one gill of mushroom catsup
- pepper
- vinegar
- salt
- cloves of garlic
- six cloves
- butter
- brown flour

## Directions

1. Prepare the shad nicely
2. put some forcemeat inside
3. lay it at full length in a pan with a pint of water, a gill of red wine, one of mushroom catsup, a little pepper, vinegar, salt, a few cloves of garlic, and six cloves
4. stew it gently till the gravy is sufficiently reduced
5. use a fish-slice with holes to lay the fish on for the convenience of dishing without breaking it
6. when the fish is taken up, slip it carefully into the dish
7. thicken the gravy with butter and brown flour
8. pour over it
