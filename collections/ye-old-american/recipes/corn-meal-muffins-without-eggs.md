## Ingredients

- One cup of flour
- One cup of corn meal
- Two tablespoonfuls of sugar
- Water or sour milk

## Directions

1. Mix flour, corn meal and sugar with water or sour milk to make a thick batter at night
2. In the morning add two tablespoonfuls of melted butter and one teaspoonful of soda
3. Bake in cake rounds
