## Ingredients

- popped corn
- salt
- one ounce of gum arabic
- half a pound of sugar
- two quarts of water

## Directions

1. Pop your corn, salt it and keep it warm
2. Dissolve an ounce of gum arabic and a half pound of sugar in two quarts of water
3. Boil all a few minutes
4. Sprinkle the mixture over the corn with a whisk broom
5. Stir the corn with the hands or large spoon thoroughly
6. Mold into balls with the hands
