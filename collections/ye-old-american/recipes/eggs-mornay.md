## Ingredients

- Egg
- Butter
- White Sauce II
- ⅓ cup grated cheese
- Paprika
- Yolks two eggs

## Directions

1. Break egg and slip into buttered egg-shirrers, allowing one or two eggs to each shirrer, according to size.
2. Cover with White Sauce II, seasoned with one-third cup grated cheese, paprika, and yolks two eggs; cover with grated cheese and bake until firm.
