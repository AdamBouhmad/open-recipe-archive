## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Choose those that are close and white, and of a middle size
2. Trim off the outside leaves
3. Cut off the stalk flat at the bottom
4. Let them lie in salt and water an hour before you boil them
5. Put them in boiling water, with a handful of salt in it
6. Skim it well
7. Let it boil slowly till done, which a small one will be in fifteen minutes, a large one in twenty
8. Take it up the moment it is enough
