## Ingredients

- Spanish onions
- butter
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- brown gravy
- flour
- port or claret
- mushroom catchup

## Directions

1. Slice some large mild Spanish onions
2. Cover them with butter and set them over a slow fire to brown
3. Add salt and cayenne pepper to your taste and some good brown gravy of roast meat, poultry or game
4. Thicken with a bit of butter rolled in flour that has first been browned by holding it in a hot pan or shovel over the fire
5. Give it a boil and skim it well
6. Just before you take it off, stir in a half glass of port or claret and the same quantity of mushroom catchup
