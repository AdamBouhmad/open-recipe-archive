## Ingredients

- lumps of loaf-sugar
- fine ripe lemons

## Directions

1. Rub lumps of loaf-sugar on fine ripe lemons till the yellow rind is all grated off
2. Scrape up the sugar in a tea-spoon, and put it on a plate, as you proceed
3. When you have enough, press it down into a little glass or china jar
4. Cover it closely
