## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- ½ lb. butter
- ¾ lb. sugar
- 4 eggs
- ½ lb. currants, well washed and dredged
- ½ teaspoonful soda dissolved in hot water
- ½ lemon, grated rind and juice
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon

## Directions

1. Drop from a spoon upon well buttered paper, lining a baking-pan.
2. Bake quickly.
