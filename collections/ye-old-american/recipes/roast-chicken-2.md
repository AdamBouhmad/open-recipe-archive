## Ingredients

- one chicken
- salt
- three tablespoons butter
- two tablespoons flour
- flour
- one-fourth cup butter
- two-thirds cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Dress, clean, stuff, and truss a chicken
2. Place on its back on rack in a dripping-pan, rub entire surface with salt, and spread breast and legs with three tablespoons butter, rubbed until creamy and mixed with two tablespoons flour
3. Dredge bottom of pan with flour
4. Place in a hot oven, and when flour is well browned, reduce the heat, then baste
5. Continue basting every ten minutes until chicken is cooked
6. For basting, use one-fourth cup butter, melted in two-thirds cup boiling water, and after this is gone, use fat in pan, and when necessary to prevent flour burning, add one cup boiling water
7. During cooking, turn chicken frequently, that it may brown evenly
8. If a thick crust is desired, dredge bird with flour two or three times during cooking
9. If a glazed surface is preferred, spread bird with butter, omitting flour, and do not dredge during baking
10. When breast meat is tender, bird is sufficiently cooked
