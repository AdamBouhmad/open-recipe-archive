## Ingredients

- mashed potato
- roast beef
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- gravy

## Directions

1. Cover bottom of a small greased baking-dish with hot mashed potato
2. add a thick layer of roast beef, chopped or cut in small pieces (seasoned with salt, pepper, and a few drops onion juice) and moistened with some of the gravy
3. cover with a thin layer of mashed potato
4. bake in a hot oven long enough to heat through
