## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- ¾ cup sugar
- Yolks 6 eggs
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 2½ cups cream
- 2 tablespoons rum
- 1 tablespoon brandy
- 1 cup mixed fruit
- glacé cherries
- Sultana raisins
- sliced citron
- candied pineapple

## Directions

1. Prepare same as Frozen Tom and Jerry. Freeze to a mush, add one cup mixed fruit which has been soaked in brandy to cover for twelve hours, using glacé cherries, Sultana raisins, sliced citron, and candied pineapple; then finish freezing. Serve in small beer jugs, and garnish with cream, whipped, sweetened, and flavored.
