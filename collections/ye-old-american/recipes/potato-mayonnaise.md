## Ingredients

- Very small baked potato
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- ¾ cup olive oil

## Directions

1. Remove and mash the inside of potato.
2. Add mustard, salt, and powdered sugar.
3. Add one tablespoon vinegar, and rub mixture through a fine sieve.
4. Add slowly oil and remaining vinegar.
5. By the taste one would hardly realize eggs were not used in the making.
