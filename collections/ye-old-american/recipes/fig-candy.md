## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- one-third cup of water
- one-fourth teaspoonful cream of tartar
- figs

## Directions

1. Do not stir while boiling
2. Boil to amber color
3. Stir in the cream of tartar just before taking from the fire
4. Wash the figs, open and lay in a tin pan
5. Pour the candy over them or dip them in the syrup
