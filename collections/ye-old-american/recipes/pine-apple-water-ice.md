## Ingredients

- very ripe pine-apples
- powdered loaf-sugar
- loaf-sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Pare and slice a sufficient number of very ripe pine-apples
2. Cut the slices into small bits and put them into a deep dish or a tureen
3. Sprinkle among them powdered loaf-sugar, cover them and let them set several hours in a cool place
4. Make a syrup of loaf-sugar dissolved in a little water, allowing to every two pounds of sugar a pint of water beaten with half the white of an egg
5. Boil and skim the syrup till quite clear
6. Get as much pine-apple juice as you can by squeezing through a sieve the bits of pine-apple after they have stood some hours in the tureen
7. Measure the juice and to each pint of the boiled syrup allow a pint of juice
8. Mix them together while the syrup is warm from the fire
9. Put it into a freezer and proceed in the usual manner
