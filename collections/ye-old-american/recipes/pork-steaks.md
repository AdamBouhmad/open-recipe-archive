## Ingredients

- pork steaks or chops from the neck or loin
- pepper
- salt
- powdered sage-leaves or sweet marjoram
- trimmings or coarse pieces of pork
- water
- chopped onions
- sage
- apple-sauce

## Directions

1. Cut them about half an inch thick, remove the skin, trim them neatly, and beat them.
2. Season them with pepper, salt, and powdered sage-leaves or sweet marjoram.
3. Broil them over a clear fire till quite done all through, turning them once.
4. When you think they are nearly done, take up one on a plate and try it.
5. If it is the least red inside, return it to the gridiron.
6. Have ready a gravy made of the trimmings, or any coarse pieces of pork stewed in a little water with chopped onions and sage, and skimmed carefully.
7. When all the essence is extracted, take out the bits of meat, &c. and serve up the gravy in a boat to eat with the steaks.
8. Accompany them with apple-sauce.
