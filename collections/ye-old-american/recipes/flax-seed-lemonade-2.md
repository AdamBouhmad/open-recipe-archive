## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls flax-seed (whole)
- 1 quart boiling water
- Juice of two lemons
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Steep three hours in a covered pitcher.
2. If too thick, put in cold water with the lemon-juice and sugar.
3. Ice for drinking.
