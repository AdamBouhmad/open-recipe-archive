## Ingredients

- a handful of hops
- two gallons and a half of water
- a table-spoonful of ground white ginger
- a pint of molasses
- a table-spoonful of essence of spruce
- half a pint of yeast
- three or four raisins per bottle

## Directions

1. Boil a handful of hops in two gallons and a half of water, till they fall to the bottom
2. Strain the water, and when it is lukewarm, stir into it a table-spoonful of ground white ginger
3. Add a pint of molasses and a table-spoonful of essence of spruce
4. Mix the whole well together in a stone jug with half a pint of yeast
5. Let it ferment for a day and a half, or two days
6. Put it into bottles, with three or four raisins in the bottom of each, to prevent any further fermentation
7. It will then be fit for immediate use
