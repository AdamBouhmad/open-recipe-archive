## Ingredients

- uncooked French Cream
- chocolate

## Directions

1. Mold the uncooked French Cream into cone-shape forms with the fingers
2. Lay them on oiled paper until the next day, to harden, or make them in the morning and leave them until afternoon
3. Melt some chocolate in a basin set in another basin of boiling water
4. When melted, and the creams are hard enough to handle, take one at a time on a fork and drop into the melted chocolate
5. Roll it until well covered, then slip from the fork upon oiled or waxed paper, and set them aside to harden
