## Ingredients

- a dozen large turnips
- four ounces of butter
- salt
- veal gravy

## Directions

1. Pare a dozen large turnips and slice them
2. Put them into a stew-pan with four ounces of butter and a little salt
3. Set the pan over a moderate fire
4. Turn them often with a wooden spoon
5. When they look white, add a ladle full of veal gravy
6. Stew them till it becomes thick
7. Skim it, and pass it through a sieve
8. Put the turnips in a dish, and pour the gravy over them
