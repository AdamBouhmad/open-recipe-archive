## Ingredients

- quinces
- water (one cup per five pounds of fruit)

## Directions

1. Pare and slice the quinces, and add for every five pounds of fruit a cup of water.
2. Put peelings, cores, and all into a stone jar; set this in a pot of boiling water.
3. When the fruit is soft and broken, proceed as with other jellies.
