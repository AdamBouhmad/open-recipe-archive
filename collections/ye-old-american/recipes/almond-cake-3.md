## Ingredients

- one-half cupful butter
- two cupfuls sugar
- four eggs
- one-half cupful almonds, blanched and cut in fine shreds
- one-half teaspoonful extract bitter almonds
- one pint flour
- one and one-half teaspoonful baking powder
- one glass brandy
- one-half cupful milk

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar to a smooth white cream
2. Add eggs, one at a time, beating three or four minutes between each
3. Sift flour and powder together
4. Add to the butter with almonds, extract of bitter almonds, brandy and milk
5. Mix into a smooth, medium batter
6. Bake carefully in a rather hot oven twenty minutes
