## Ingredients

- one quart of boiling water
- one-third of a six cent loaf of bread
- sugar
- lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Measure a quart of boiling water and set it away to get cold
2. Take one-third of a six cent loaf of bread, slice it, and pare off the crust
3. Toast the crumb nicely of a light brown
4. Put it into the boiled water and set it on hot coals in a covered pan
5. Boil it gently till you find by putting some in a spoon to cool that the liquid has become a jelly
6. Strain it through a thin cloth and set it away for use
7. When it is to be taken, warm a tea-cupful, sweeten it with sugar, and add a little grated lemon-peel
