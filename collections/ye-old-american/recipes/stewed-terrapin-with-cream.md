## Ingredients

- two heaping tablespoonfuls of butter
- one tablespoonful of dry flour
- one pint of cream
- teaspoonful of salt
- quarter of a teaspoonful of white pepper
- grated nutmeg
- cayenne pepper
- one pint of terrapin meat
- four eggs
- gill of good Madeira
- tablespoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place in a saucepan two heaping tablespoonfuls of butter and one of dry flour
2. Stir it over the fire until it bubbles
3. Gradually stir in a pint of cream, a teaspoonful of salt, a quarter of a teaspoonful of white pepper, the same of grated nutmeg, and a very small pinch of cayenne
4. Put in a pint of terrapin meat and stir all until it is scalding hot
5. Move the saucepan to the back part of the stove or range, where the contents will keep hot but not boil
6. Stir in four well-beaten yolks of eggs
7. Do not allow the terrapin to boil after adding the eggs
8. Pour it immediately into a tureen containing a gill of good Madeira and a tablespoonful of lemon juice
9. Serve hot
