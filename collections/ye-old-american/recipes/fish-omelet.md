## Ingredients

- a cupful of any kind of cold fish
- cream
- a tablespoonful of butter
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Make a plain omelet, and when ready to fold, spread over it fish prepared as follows
2. Add to a cupful of any kind of cold fish, broken fine, cream enough to moisten it
3. Season with a tablespoonful of butter; then pepper and salt to taste
4. Warm together
