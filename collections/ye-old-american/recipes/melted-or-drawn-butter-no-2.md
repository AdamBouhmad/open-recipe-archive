## Ingredients

- 1½ teaspoonful of flour
- 2 ounces butter
- 1 teacupful (small) hot water

## Directions

1. Wet the flour to a thin smooth paste with cold water, and stir into the hot, which should be in the inner vessel.
2. When it boils, add the butter by degrees, and stir until well mixed.
3. Boil one minute.
