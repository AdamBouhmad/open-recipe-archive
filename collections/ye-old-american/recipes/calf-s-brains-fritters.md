## Ingredients

- brains
- one-half teaspoon salt
- one tablespoon lemon juice
- three cloves
- two slices onion
- one-half bay leaf
- one-half cup flour
- one teaspoon baking powder
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- pepper
- one egg well beaten
- one-fourth cup milk

## Directions

1. Clean brains, and cook twenty minutes in boiling water, to which is added one-half teaspoon salt, one tablespoon lemon juice, three cloves, two slices onion, and one-half bay leaf.
2. Remove from range, and let stand in water until cold; drain, dry between towels, and separate into pieces.
3. Make a batter of one-half cup flour, one teaspoon baking powder, one-fourth teaspoon salt, a few grains pepper, one egg well beaten, and one-fourth cup milk.
4. Add brains, and drop mixture by spoonfuls into greased muffin rings, placed in a frying-pan in which there is a generous supply of hot lard.
5. Cook on one side until well browned, turn, and cook other side.
6. Arrange on serving dish and pour around Sauce Finiste.
