## Ingredients

- ¼ cake chocolate
- ½ cup sweet milk
- 1 tablespoonful corn-starch
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla
- powdered sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Mix together chocolate, sweet milk, and corn-starch.
2. Boil it two minutes after it has fairly come to a boil.
3. Flavor with 1 teaspoonful vanilla, and then sweeten to taste with powdered sugar, taking care to make it sweet enough.
