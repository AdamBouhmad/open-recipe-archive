## Ingredients

- brook trout
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- fat of fried salt pork or lard
- butter
- pork

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and dry the fish
2. Split them to the tail
3. Salt and pepper them, and flour them nicely
4. If you use lard instead of the fat of fried salt pork, put in a piece of butter to prevent their sticking, and which causes them to brown nicely
5. Let the fat be hot; fry quickly to a delicate brown
6. They should be sufficiently browned on one side before turning on the other
7. Lay them side by side on a heated platter, garnish and send hot to the table
