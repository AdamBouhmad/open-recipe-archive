## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons caramel sauce
- Few grains salt
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- Three-fourths tablespoon butter
- Shredded almonds

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored, add caramel, salt, and vanilla, and cut and fold in whites of eggs beaten until stiff and dry.
2. Put three-fourths tablespoon butter in a hot omelet pan, cover bottom of pan with shredded almonds, turn in mixture, and cook and fold same as Plain Omelet.
3. Pour around.
