## Ingredients

- one pint of small homony
- corn meal
- eggs
- milk
- butter
- molasses

## Directions

1. Wash a pint of small homony very clean, and boil it tender
2. add an equal quantity of corn meal
3. make it into a batter with eggs, milk, and a piece of butter
4. bake it like batter cakes on a griddle
5. eat it with butter and molasses
