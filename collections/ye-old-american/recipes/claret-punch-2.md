## Ingredients

- 1 quart cold water
- ½ cup raisins
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 inch piece stick cinnamon
- Few shavings lemon rind
- 1⅓ cups orange juice
- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- 1 pint claret wine

## Directions

1. Put raisins in cold water, bring slowly to boiling-point, and boil twenty minutes.
2. Strain, add sugar, cinnamon, lemon rind, and boil five minutes.
3. Add fruit juice, cool, strain, pour in claret, and dilute with ice-water.
