## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- half a pound of sugar
- half a pound of butter
- mace and nutmeg powdered
- a glass of brandy or wine
- milk

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour, add half a pound of sugar, half a pound of butter, some mace and nutmeg powdered, and a glass of brandy or wine
2. Wet it with milk
3. When well kneaded, roll it thin
4. Cut it in shapes
5. Bake it quickly
