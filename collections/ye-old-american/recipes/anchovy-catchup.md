## Ingredients

- two dozen anchovies
- ten shalots, or very small onions
- a handful of scraped horseradish
- a quarter of an ounce of mace
- a lemon
- twelve cloves
- twelve pepper-corns
- a pint of port
- a pint of madeira
- a pint of anchovy liquor

## Directions

1. Bone two dozen anchovies, and then chop them
2. Put to them ten shalots, or very small onions, cut fine, and a handful of scraped horseradish, with a quarter of an ounce of mace
3. Add a lemon, cut into slices, twelve cloves, and twelve pepper-corns
4. Mix together a pint of port, a pint of madeira, and a pint of anchovy liquor
5. Put the other ingredients into the liquid, and boil it slowly till reduced one-half
6. Then strain it, and when cold put it into small bottles, securing the corks with leather
