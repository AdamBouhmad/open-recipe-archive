## Ingredients

- six peeled tomatoes
- cold water
- pepper
- salt
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Cut in slices and place in a stewpan six peeled tomatoes
2. Add a tablespoonful of cold water, a little pepper and salt
3. When they begin to simmer, break in six eggs
4. Stir well, stirring one way, until the eggs are cooked, but not too hard
5. Serve warm
