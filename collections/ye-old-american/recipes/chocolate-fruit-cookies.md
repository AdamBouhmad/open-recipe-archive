## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons grated chocolate
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon boiling water
- 1 egg
- ½ cup nut meats, finely chopped
- ½ cup seeded raisins, finely chopped
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, and add sugar, gradually.
2. Melt chocolate, add remaining sugar and water, and cook one minute.
3. Combine mixtures, and add remaining ingredients.
4. Chill, shape, and bake same as Chocolate Cookies.
