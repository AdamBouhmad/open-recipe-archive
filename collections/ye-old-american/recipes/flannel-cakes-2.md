## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls yeast
- 1 tablespoonful butter, melted
- 2 eggs, well beaten
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- Flour to make a good batter

## Directions

1. Set the rest of the ingredients as a sponge over night
2. In the morning add the melted butter and eggs
