## Ingredients

- one pound of nice sugar
- two pounds of flour
- pounded spice of any kind
- four eggs
- three quarters of a pound of melted butter

## Directions

1. Put one pound of nice sugar into two pounds of flour
2. Add pounded spice of any kind, and pass them through a sieve
3. Beat four eggs, pour them on with three quarters of a pound of melted butter
4. Knead all well together
5. Bake them
