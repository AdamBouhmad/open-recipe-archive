## Ingredients

- ½ cup scalded milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ yeast cake dissolved in 2 tablespoons lukewarm water
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- 1 egg
- Few gratings from rind of lemon
- Flour

## Directions

1. Add sugar and salt to milk
2. When lukewarm, add dissolved yeast cake and three-fourths cup flour
3. Cover and let rise
4. Add butter, egg well beaten, grated rind of lemon, and enough flour to knead
5. Let rise again
6. Roll to one-half inch thickness
7. Shape with small biscuit-cutter
8. Place in buttered pan close together
9. Let rise again
10. Bake
