## Ingredients

- medium-sized tomatoes
- paste made as biscuit dough
- nutmeg
- half a cup of butter
- medium cup of sugar
- small handful of flour
- half a cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Take medium-sized tomatoes, pare and cut out the stem end
2. Line your pie-pan with paste made as biscuit dough
3. Slice the tomatoes very thin, filling the pan somewhat heaping
4. Grate over it a nutmeg
5. Put in half a cup of butter and a medium cup of sugar
6. Sprinkle a small handful of flour over all
7. Pour in half a cup of vinegar before adding the top crust
8. Bake half an hour in a moderately hot oven
9. Serve hot
