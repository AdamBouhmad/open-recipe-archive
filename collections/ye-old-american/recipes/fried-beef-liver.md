## Ingredients

- beef liver
- boiling water
- flour or bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- hot fat mixed with one-third butter

## Directions

1. Cut the liver in rather thin slices, say a quarter of an inch thick
2. Pour boiling water over it, which closes the pores of the meat
3. Roll in flour or bread crumbs, seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Dip in egg
5. Fry in hot fat mixed with one-third butter
