## Ingredients

- Two teaspoonfuls of tea
- One large cupful of boiling water

## Directions

1. Scald the teapot
2. Put in the tea
3. Pour on about a cupful of boiling water
4. Set it on the fire in a warm place, where it will not boil, but keep very hot, to almost boiling
5. Let it steep or draw ten or twelve minutes
6. Fill up with as much boiling water as is required
7. Send hot to the table
8. Pour boiling water on the leaves directly it boils
9. Allow the water to remain on the leaves from ten to fifteen minutes
