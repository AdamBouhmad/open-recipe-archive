## Ingredients

- 1 lb. soft maple sugar
- ¾ cup thin cream
- ¼ cup boiling water
- ⅔ cup English walnut or pecan meat, cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Break sugar in pieces; put into a saucepan with cream and water.
2. Bring to boiling-point, and boil until a soft ball is formed when tried in cold water.
3. Remove from fire, beat until creamy, add nut meat, and pour into a buttered tin.
4. Cool slightly, and mark in squares.
