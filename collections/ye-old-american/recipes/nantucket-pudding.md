## Ingredients

- one quart of berries or any small fruit
- two tablespoonfuls of flour
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Simmer together and turn into molds
2. Cover with frosting as for cake, or with whipped eggs and sugar, browning lightly in the oven
3. Serve with cream
