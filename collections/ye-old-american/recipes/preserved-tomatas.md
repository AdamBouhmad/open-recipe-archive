## Ingredients

- large fine tomatas
- white sugar
- lemon
- ginger

## Directions

1. Take large fine tomatas, not too ripe, and scald them to make the skins come off easily
2. Weigh them and to each pound allow a pound of the best white sugar and the grated peel of half a lemon
3. Put all together into a preserving kettle and boil it slowly for three hours, skimming it carefully
4. Add the juice of the lemons and boil it an hour longer
5. Put the whole into jars and when cool cover and tie them up closely
6. It may be improved by boiling a little ginger with the other ingredients
