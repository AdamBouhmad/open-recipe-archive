## Ingredients

- pint of fresh shelled Lima beans or any large fresh beans
- twelve ears of young sweet corn
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Take a pint of fresh shelled Lima beans, or any large fresh beans, put them in a pot with cold water, rather more than will cover them.
2. Scrape the kernels from twelve ears of young sweet corn; put the cobs in with the beans, boiling from half to three-quarters of an hour.
3. Take out the cobs and put in the scraped corn; boil again fifteen minutes.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste, a piece of butter the size of an egg and half a cup of cream.
5. Serve hot.
