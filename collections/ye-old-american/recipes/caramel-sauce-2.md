## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ¾ cup hot water

## Directions

1. Pour one cup sugar in omelet pan, and stir constantly, over hot part of range, until melted to a light brown syrup.
2. Add three-fourths cup hot water, and let simmer ten minutes.
