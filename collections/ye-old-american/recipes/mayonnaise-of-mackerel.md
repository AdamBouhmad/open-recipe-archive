## Ingredients

- two medium-sized mackerel
- one-third cup water
- one-third cup cider vinegar
- one-third cup tarragon vinegar
- twelve cloves
- one teaspoon peppercorns
- one teaspoon salt
- bit of bay leaf
- buttered paper
- Mayonnaise thickened with gelatine
- sliced cucumbers
- lemon baskets
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean two medium-sized mackerel, put in baking-dish with one-third cup each water, cider vinegar, and tarragon vinegar, twelve cloves, one teaspoon each peppercorns and salt, and a bit of bay leaf.
2. Cover with buttered paper and cook in a moderate oven.
3. Arrange on serving dish, remove skin, cool, and mask with Mayonnaise thickened with gelatine.
4. Let stand until thoroughly chilled, and garnish with sliced cucumbers, lemon baskets filled with Mayonnaise sprinkled with finely chopped parsley, and sprigs of parsley.
