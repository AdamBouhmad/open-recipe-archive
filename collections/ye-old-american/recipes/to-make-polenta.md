## Ingredients

- large spoonful butter
- quart of water
- corn meal
- cold water
- salt
- cheese
- butter

## Directions

1. Put a large spoonful of butter in a quart of water
2. Wet your corn meal with cold water in a bowl
3. Add some salt and make it quite smooth
4. Put it in the buttered water when it is hot
5. Let it boil, stirring it continually till done
6. As soon as you can handle it, make it into a ball and let it stand till quite cold
7. Cut it in thin slices
8. Lay them in the bottom of a deep dish so as to cover it
9. Put on it slices of cheese and on that a few bits of butter
10. Then mush, cheese and butter, until the dish is full
11. Put on the top thin slices of cheese and butter
12. Put the dish in a quick oven
13. Bake twenty or thirty minutes
