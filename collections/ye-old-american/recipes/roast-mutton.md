## Ingredients

- a breast (cauled)
- a leg (stuffed or not)
- scraped horse radish
- potatoes
- beans
- colliflowers
- water-cresses
- boiled onion
- caper sauce
- mashed turnip
- lettuce

## Directions

1. If a breast let it be cauled, if a leg, stuffed or not, let be done more gently than beef, and done more.
2. The chine, saddle or leg require more fire and longer time than the breast, &c.
3. Garnish with scraped horse radish.
4. Serve with potatoes, beans, colliflowers, water-cresses, or boiled onion, caper sauce, mashed turnip, or lettuce.
