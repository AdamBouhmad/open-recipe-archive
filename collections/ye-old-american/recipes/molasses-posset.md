## Ingredients

- a pint of the best West India molasses
- a tea-spoonful of powdered white ginger
- a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
- the juice of two lemons
- two table-spoonfuls of vinegar

## Directions

1. Put into a sauce-pan a pint of the best West India molasses; a tea-spoonful of powdered white ginger; and a quarter of a pound of fresh butter.
2. Set it on hot coals, and simmer it slowly for half an hour; stirring it frequently.
3. Do not let it come to a boil.
4. Stir in the juice of two lemons, or two table-spoonfuls of vinegar.
5. Cover the pan and let it stand by the fire five minutes longer.
