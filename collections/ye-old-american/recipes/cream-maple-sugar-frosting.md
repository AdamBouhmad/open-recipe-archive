## Ingredients

- 1 lb. soft maple sugar
- 1 cup cream

## Directions

1. Break sugar in small pieces, put in saucepan with cream, and stir occasionally until sugar is dissolved. Boil without stirring until a ball can be formed when mixture is tried in cold water. Beat until of right consistency to spread.
