## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- salt
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs slightly
2. Add one-fourth cup milk
3. Add gradually to one cup flour mixed and sifted with one teaspoon salt
4. Place colander over a kettle of boiling water
5. Turn in one-third mixture
6. Force through colander into water, using a potato masher
7. As soon as buttons come to top of water, remove with skimmer to hot vegetable dish
8. Sprinkle with salt and grated cheese
9. Repeat until mixture is used
10. Let stand in oven five minutes
11. Serve
