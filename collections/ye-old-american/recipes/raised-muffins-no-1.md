## Ingredients

- One pint of sweet milk
- One teaspoonful of sugar
- One teaspoonful of salt
- One tablespoonful of butter or sweet lard
- Half a cup of yeast
- Flour
- One or two well-beaten eggs
- Half a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little warm water

## Directions

1. Make a batter of milk, sugar, salt, butter and yeast
2. Add flour enough to make it moderately thick
3. Keep it in a warm, not hot, place until it is quite light
4. Stir in one or two well-beaten eggs
5. Add half a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little warm water
6. Let the batter stand twenty-five or thirty minutes longer to rise a little
7. Turn into well-greased muffin-rings or gem-pans
8. Bake in a quick oven
9. Serve hot and torn open instead of cut with a knife
