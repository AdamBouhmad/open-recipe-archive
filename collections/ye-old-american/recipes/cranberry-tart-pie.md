## Ingredients

- Cranberries
- water
- sugar
- thin puff paste

## Directions

1. Wash and pick over the berries
2. Stew them well in a little water, just enough to cover them
3. When they burst open and become soft, sweeten them with plenty of sugar
4. Mash them smooth
5. Line your pie-plates with thin puff paste
6. Fill them and lay strips of paste across the top
7. Bake in a moderate oven
