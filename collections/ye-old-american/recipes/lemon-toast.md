## Ingredients

- the yolks of six eggs
- three cupfuls of sweet milk
- baker's bread
- melted butter
- the whites of six eggs
- a large cupful of white sugar
- the juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Take the yolks of six eggs, beat them well and add three cupfuls of sweet milk
2. Take baker's bread, not too stale, and cut into slices
3. Dip them into the milk and eggs and lay the slices into a spider, with sufficient melted butter, hot, to fry a delicate brown
4. Take the whites of the six eggs and beat them to a froth, adding a large cupful of white sugar
5. Add the juice of two lemons, heating well and adding
