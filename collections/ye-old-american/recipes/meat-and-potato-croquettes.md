## Ingredients

- an ounce of butter
- a slice of onion minced fine
- a level tablespoonful of sifted flour
- half of a cupful of milk
- salt
- pepper
- a cupful of cold meat chopped fine
- a cupful of cold or hot mashed potato
- beaten egg
- cracker or bread crumbs
- hot lard
- brown paper

## Directions

1. Put in a stewpan an ounce of butter and a slice of onion minced fine
2. When this simmers add a level tablespoonful of sifted flour
3. Stir the mixture until it becomes smooth and frothy
4. Add half of a cupful of milk, some seasoning of salt and pepper
5. Let all boil, stirring it all the while
6. Add a cupful of cold meat chopped fine and a cupful of cold or hot mashed potato
7. Mix all thoroughly and spread on a plate to cool
8. When it is cool enough, shape it with your hands into balls or rolls
9. Dip them in beaten egg and roll in cracker or bread crumbs
10. Drop them into hot lard and fry about two minutes a delicate brown
11. Take them out with a skimmer and drain them on a piece of brown paper
12. Serve immediately while hot
