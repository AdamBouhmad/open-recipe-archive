## Ingredients

- a white onion
- a turnip
- a pared potato
- a head of celery
- a large tea-spoonful of celery seed
- a quart of water
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Put the vegetables whole into a quart of water, (adding a little salt,) and boil it slowly till reduced to a pint.
2. Make a slice of nice toast; lay it in the bottom of a bowl, and strain the soup over it.
