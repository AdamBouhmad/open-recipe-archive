## Ingredients

- fresh, crisp lettuce leaves
- thin slices of buttered bread
- teaspoon of Mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Wash and thoroughly dry fresh, crisp lettuce leaves.
2. Put lettuce leaves between thin slices of buttered bread prepared as for Bread and Butter Folds, having a teaspoon of Mayonnaise on each leaf.
