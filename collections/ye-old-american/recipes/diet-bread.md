## Ingredients

- one pound sugar
- 9 eggs
- 14 ounces flour
- spoonful rose water
- one do. cinnamon or coriander

## Directions

1. beat for an hour
2. add to 14 ounces flour
3. spoonful rose water, one do. cinnamon or coriander
4. bake quick
