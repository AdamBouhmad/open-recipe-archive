## Ingredients

- oysters (two quarts)
- water
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- milk
- oyster or cream crackers

## Directions

1. Drain the liquor from two quarts of firm, plump oysters.
2. Mix the liquor with a small teacupful of hot water.
3. Add a little salt and pepper.
4. Set over the fire in a saucepan.
5. Let it boil up once.
6. Put in the oysters and let them boil for five minutes or less—not more.
7. When they "ruffle," add two tablespoonfuls of butter.
8. The instant it is melted and well stirred in, put in a large cupful of boiling milk.
9. Take the saucepan from the fire.
10. Serve with oyster or cream crackers, as soon as possible.
