## Ingredients

- 1½ tablespoons prepared cocoa
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 2 cups boiling water
- 2 cups milk
- Few grains salt

## Directions

1. Scald milk.
2. Mix cocoa, sugar, and salt, dilute with one-half cup boiling water to make smooth paste, add remaining water, and boil one minute.
3. Turn into scalded milk and beat two minutes, using Dover egg-beater, when froth will form, preventing scum, which is so unsightly; this is known as milling.
