## Ingredients

- 1 pint Graham flour
- Nearly a quart of boiling water or milk
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Scald the flour, when you have salted it, into as soft dough as you can handle
2. Roll it nearly an inch thick
3. Cut in round cakes
4. Lay upon a hot buttered tin or pan
5. Bake them in the hottest oven you can get ready
6. Split and butter while hot
