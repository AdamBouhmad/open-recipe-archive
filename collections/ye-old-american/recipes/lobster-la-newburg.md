## Ingredients

- one whole lobster
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- heavy cream sauce
- egg yolk
- sherry wine
- flour
- boiling cream

## Directions

1. Cut lobster up in pieces about as large as a hickory nut
2. Put in the same pan with a piece of butter size of a walnut
3. Season with salt and pepper to taste
4. Thicken with heavy cream sauce
5. Add the yolk of one egg and two oz. of sherry wine
6. For cream sauce: melt 1 oz. butter in saucepan
7. Mix 2 oz. flour with butter
8. Thin down to proper consistency with boiling cream
