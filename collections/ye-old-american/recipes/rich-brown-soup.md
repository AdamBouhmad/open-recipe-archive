## Ingredients

- six pounds lean fresh beef
- four dozen cloves
- tea-spoonful salt
- tea-spoonful pepper
- tea-spoonful mace
- beaten nutmeg
- half a dozen onions
- butter
- five quarts water
- tumbler and a half or six wine glasses claret or port wine

## Directions

1. Take six pounds of the lean of fresh beef, cut from the bone.
2. Stick it over with four dozen cloves.
3. Season it with a tea-spoonful of salt, a tea-spoonful of pepper, a tea-spoonful of mace, and a beaten nutmeg.
4. Slice half a dozen onions; fry them in butter; chop them, and spread them over the meat after you have put it into the soup-pot.
5. Pour in five quarts of water, and stew it slowly for five or six hours; skimming it well.
6. When the meat has dissolved into shreds, strain it, and return the liquid to the pot.
7. Then add a tumbler and a half, or six wine glasses of claret or port wine.
8. Simmer it again slowly till dinner time.
9. When the soup is reduced to three quarts, it is done enough.
10. Put it into a tureen, and send it to table.
