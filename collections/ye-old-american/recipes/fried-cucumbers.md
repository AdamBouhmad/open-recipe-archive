## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Having pared your cucumbers, cut them lengthways into pieces about as thick as a dollar.
2. Then dry them in a cloth.
3. Season them with pepper and salt, and sprinkle them thick with flour.
4. Melt some butter in a frying-pan, and when it boils, put in the slices of cucumber, and fry them of a light brown.
5. Send them to table hot.
