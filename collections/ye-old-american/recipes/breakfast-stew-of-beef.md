## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef
- water
- pepper
- salt
- sweet marjoram or summer savory
- chopped onion
- parsley
- teaspoonful of sauce or catsup
- tablespoonful of browned flour
- cold water
- half a glass of wine

## Directions

1. Cut up two pounds of beef—not too lean—into pieces an inch long
2. Put them into a saucepan with just enough water to cover them, and stew gently for two hours
3. Set away until next morning
4. Season with pepper, salt, sweet marjoram or summer savory, chopped onion, and parsley
5. Stew half an hour longer
6. Add a teaspoonful of sauce or catsup, and a tablespoonful of browned flour wet up with cold water
7. If you wish to have it very good, add half a glass of wine
8. Boil up once, and pour into a covered deep dish
