## Ingredients

- 1 large juicy pippin
- 1 quart of water
- 1 glass of fine old whiskey
- lemon-juice
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Boil a large juicy pippin in a quart of water, and when it has broken to pieces strain off the water.
2. While it is still boiling-hot, add a glass of fine old whiskey, a little lemon-juice, and sweeten to taste.
3. Take hot at bed-time for influenza.
