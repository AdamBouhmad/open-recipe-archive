## Ingredients

- butter
- slices of stale cake (sponge cake)
- large pineapple
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Butter a pudding-dish and line the bottom and sides with slices of stale cake
2. Pare and slice thin a large pineapple
3. Place in the dish first a layer of pineapple, then strew with sugar, then more pineapple, and so on until all is used
4. Pour over a small teacupful of water
5. Cover with slices of cake which have been dipped in cold water
6. Cover the whole with a buttered plate
7. Bake slowly for two hours
