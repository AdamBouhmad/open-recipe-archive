## Ingredients

- One cup flour
- One and one-half teaspoons baking powder
- One-fourth teaspoon salt
- Few grains pepper
- One-third cup milk
- One egg well beaten

## Directions

1. Mix and sift one cup flour, one and one-half teaspoons baking powder, one-fourth teaspoon salt, and a few grains pepper
2. Add one-third cup milk and one egg well beaten
