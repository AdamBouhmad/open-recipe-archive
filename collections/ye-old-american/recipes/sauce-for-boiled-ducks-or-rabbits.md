## Ingredients

- onions
- water
- flour
- butter
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Peel some onions and boil them in plenty of water
2. Change the first water and boil them two hours
3. Take them up and put them in a colander to drain
4. Chop them on a board
5. Put them in a sauce-pan and sprinkle a little flour over them
6. Put in a large piece of butter with a little milk or cream
7. Set them over the fire, and when the butter is melted, they will be done enough
8. Pour boiled onions over your ducks or rabbits
