## Ingredients

- blueberries
- molasses
- cotton cloth

## Directions

1. Pick over your berries, using only sound ones
2. Fill your jars or wide-mouthed bottles to within an inch of the top
3. Pour in molasses enough to settle down into all the spaces
4. As it settles, pour in more until the berries are covered
5. Tie over the top a piece of cotton cloth to keep the flies and other insects out
6. Set away in the preserve closet
