## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- powdered white sugar
- powdered cinnamon or nutmeg
- the juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Stir together, as for a pound-cake, equal quantities of fresh butter and powdered white sugar.
2. When quite light and creamy, add some powdered cinnamon or nutmeg, and the juice of a lemon.
3. Send it to table in a small deep plate with a tea-spoon in it.
