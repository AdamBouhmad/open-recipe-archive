## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 1 cup sugar
- Juice 3 lemons
- 2 cups ice-water
- 1 can grated pineapple or 1 pineapple shredded

## Directions

1. Make a syrup by boiling water and sugar fifteen minutes; add pineapple and lemon juice; cool, strain, add ice-water, and freeze to a mush, using equal parts ice and salt.
2. If fresh fruit is used, more sugar will be required.
