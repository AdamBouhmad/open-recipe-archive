## Ingredients

- One tablespoonful of butter
- one of flour
- one teaspoonful of curry powder
- one large slice of onion
- one large cupful of stock
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the onion fine, and fry brown in the butter
2. Add the flour and curry powder
3. Stir for one minute, add the stock and season with the salt and pepper
4. Simmer five minutes; then strain and serve
