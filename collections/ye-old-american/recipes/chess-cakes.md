## Ingredients

- one cocoanut
- one pound of sugar
- two-thirds of a pint of water
- one quarter of a pound of butter
- yolks of seven eggs
- rich paste

## Directions

1. Peel and grate one cocoanut.
2. Boil one pound of sugar fifteen minutes in two-thirds of a pint of water.
3. Stir in the grated cocoanut and boil fifteen minutes longer.
4. While warm, stir in a quarter of a pound of butter.
5. Add the yolks of seven eggs well beaten.
6. Bake in patty-pans with rich paste.
