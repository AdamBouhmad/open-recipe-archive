## Ingredients

- ¾ cup thick cream
- ¼ cup milk
- ¼ cup powdered sugar
- White one egg
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Dilute cream with milk and beat until stiff, using Dover egg-beater.
2. Add sugar, white of egg beaten until stiff, and vanilla.
