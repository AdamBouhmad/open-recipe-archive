## Ingredients

- a cup of cold boiled rice
- a cupful of warm milk
- a tablespoonful of butter melted
- a level teaspoonful of salt
- a dash of pepper
- three well-beaten eggs
- a tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Take a cup of cold boiled rice, turn over it a cupful of warm milk
2. Add a tablespoonful of butter melted, a level teaspoonful of salt, a dash of pepper
3. Mix well, then add three well-beaten eggs
4. Put a tablespoonful of butter in a hot frying pan, and when it begins to boil pour in the omelet
5. Set the pan in a hot oven
6. As soon as it is cooked through, fold it double
7. Turn it out on a hot dish, and serve at once
