## Ingredients

- very young oval shaped musk-melons
- salt and water
- vine leaves or cabbage leaves
- alum, a little piece
- horseradish, scraped
- white mustard seed
- mace
- nutmeg
- race ginger
- pepper
- turmeric
- sweet oil
- garlic cloves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take very young oval shaped musk-melons
2. Cut a round piece out of the top or side of each, saving the piece to put on again
3. Extract the seeds
4. Tie on the pieces with packthread and put them into strong salt and water for two days
5. Afterwards drain and wipe them
6. Put them into a kettle with vine leaves or cabbage leaves under and over them, and a little piece of alum
7. Hang them on a slow fire to green, keeping them closely covered to retain the steam, which will greatly accelerate the greening
8. When they are quite green, have ready the stuffing, which must be a mixture of scraped horseradish, white mustard seed, mace and nutmeg pounded, race ginger cut small, pepper, turmeric and sweet oil
9. Fill your mangoes with this mixture, putting a small clove of garlic into each
10. Replace the pieces at the openings and tie them with a packthread crossing backwards and forwards round the mango
11. Put them into stone jars and pour boiling vinegar over them, and cover them well
