## Ingredients

- rich biscuit dough
- one cup and a quarter of water
- one cup and a half of sugar
- two lemons
- three crackers
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a rich biscuit dough
2. While baking, take a cup and a quarter of water, a cup and a half of sugar, and two lemons, peel, juice and pulp, throwing away the tough part of the rind
3. Boil this for some little time
4. Stir in three crackers rolled fine
5. Split the short-cakes while hot
6. Spread with butter, then with the mixture
7. Eat warm
