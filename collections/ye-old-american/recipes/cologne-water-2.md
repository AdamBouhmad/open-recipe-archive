## Ingredients

- one drachm of oil of lavender
- one drachm of oil of lemon
- one drachm of oil of rosemary
- one drachm of oil of cinnamon
- two drachms of oil of bergamot
- a pint of spirits of wine

## Directions

1. Procure at a druggists the oils
2. Shake the oils well, and pour them into a pint of spirits of wine
3. Cork the bottle tightly, shake it hard, and it will be fit for immediate use
