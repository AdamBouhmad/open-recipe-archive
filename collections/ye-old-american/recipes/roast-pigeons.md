## Ingredients

- pigeons
- water
- butter
- gravy

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and stuff as you would chickens
2. Lay them in rows if roasted in the oven, with a little water in the pan to prevent scorching
3. Unless they are very fat, baste with butter until they are half done, afterwards with their own gravy
4. Thicken the gravy that drips from them, and boil up once; then pour into a gravy-boat
5. The pigeons should lie close together in the dish
