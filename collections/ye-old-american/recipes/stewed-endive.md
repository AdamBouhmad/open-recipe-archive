## Ingredients

- six heads of endive
- salt
- water
- one pint of broth
- butter
- flour
- one tablespoonful of lemon juice
- a small lump of sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and free the endive thoroughly from insects
2. Remove the green part of the leaves
3. Put it into boiling water, slightly salted
4. Let it remain for ten minutes
5. Take it out and drain it till there is no water remaining
6. Chop it very fine
7. Put it into a stewpan with the broth
8. Add a little salt and a lump of sugar
9. Boil until the endive is perfectly tender
10. When done, add a thickening of butter and flour and the lemon juice
11. Let the sauce boil up and serve
