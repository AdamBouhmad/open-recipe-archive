## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- milk
- pepper
- salt
- boiled onion
- flour
- gravy
- roast beef or mutton

## Directions

1. Mash some boiled potatoes smoothly with a little milk, pepper, salt, and a boiled onion (minced)
2. Make into small cones or balls
3. Flour well
4. Skim off all the fat from the gravy before putting them in
5. Put under or beside the meat, half an hour or so before you take it up
6. Drain them dry when brown, and lay around the meat when dished
