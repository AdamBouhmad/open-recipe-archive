## Ingredients

- 1 quart clear Brown Soup Stock
- ¼ cup carrot, cut in thin strips one and one-half inches long, previously cooked in boiling salted water
- ¼ cup turnip, cut in thin strips one and one-half inches long, previously cooked in boiling salted water
- 2 tablespoons cooked peas
- 2 tablespoons string beans

## Directions

1. To one quart clear Brown Soup Stock, add one-fourth cup each carrot and turnip, cut in thin strips one and one-half inches long, previously cooked in boiling salted water, and two tablespoons, each, cooked peas and string beans.
2. Heat to boiling-point.
