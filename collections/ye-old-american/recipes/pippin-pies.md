## Ingredients

- 12 fine ripe pippins, pared and grated
- 1 lb. white sugar
- ½ lb. butter
- 6 eggs—whites and yolks separately beaten
- 1 lemon—grated peel and juice, with nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. Stir in the beaten yolks
3. Add the lemon, nutmeg, and apple
4. Lastly add the whites, very lightly
5. Bake in paste, with cross-bars of the same on top
