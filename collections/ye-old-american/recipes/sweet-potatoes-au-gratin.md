## Ingredients

- five medium-sized cold boiled sweet potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- three tablespoons brown sugar
- one tablespoon butter
- buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut five medium-sized cold boiled sweet potatoes in one-third inch slices.
2. Put a layer in buttered baking-dish, sprinkle with salt, pepper, and three tablespoons brown sugar, dot over with one tablespoon butter.
3. Repeat, cover with buttered cracker crumbs, and bake until the crumbs are brown.
