## Ingredients

- pure white vinegar
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Take some pure white vinegar and mix with it granulated sugar until a syrup is formed quite free from acidity
2. Pour this syrup into earthen jars and put in it good, perfectly ripe fruit, gathered in dry weather
3. Cover the jars tight and put them in a dry place
4. The contents will keep for six or eight months
