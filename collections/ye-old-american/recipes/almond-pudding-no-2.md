## Ingredients

- four ounces of crumbs of bread, sliced
- one and one-half pints of cream
- half a pound of blanched almonds
- two teaspoonfuls of orange-flower water
- eight eggs yolks
- four eggs whites
- a quarter of a pound of loaf sugar
- three or four ounces of melted butter

## Directions

1. Steep four ounces of crumbs of bread, sliced, in one and one-half pints of cream, or grate the bread
2. Beat half a pound of blanched almonds very fine till they become a paste, with two teaspoonfuls of orange-flower water
3. Beat up the yolks of eight eggs and the whites of four
4. Mix all well together
5. Put in a quarter of a pound of loaf sugar and stir in three or four ounces of melted butter
6. Put it over the fire, stirring it until it is thick
7. Lay a sheet of paper at the bottom of a dish and pour in the ingredients
8. Bake half an hour
9. Use the remaining four whites of eggs for a meringue for the top
