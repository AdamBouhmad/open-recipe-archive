## Ingredients

- water cress
- hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- pepper
- stale loaf
- fresh butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash well some water cress and then dry them in a cloth, pressing out every atom of moisture as far as possible
2. Mix with the cress hard-boiled eggs chopped fine, and seasoned with salt and pepper
3. Have a stale loaf and some fresh butter, and with a sharp knife cut as many thin slices as will be required for two dozen sandwiches
4. Cut the cress into small pieces, removing the stems
5. Place it between each slice of bread and butter, with a slight sprinkling of lemon juice
6. Press down the slices hard, and cut them sharply on a board into small squares, leaving no crust
