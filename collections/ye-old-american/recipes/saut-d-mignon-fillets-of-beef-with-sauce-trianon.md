## Ingredients

- small fillets of beef
- fried potato balls
- parsley
- Sauce Trianon

## Directions

1. Wipe and sauté small fillets in hot omelet pan.
2. Arrange in a circle around a mound of fried potato balls sprinkled with parsley.
3. Put Sauce Trianon on each fillet.
