## Ingredients

- leg of lamb
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Remove caul from leg of lamb as usually sent from market.
2. Wipe meat, sprinkle with salt and pepper, place on rack in dripping-pan, and dredge meat and bottom of pan with flour.
3. Place in hot oven, and baste as soon as flour in pan is brown, and every fifteen minutes afterwards until meat is done, which will take about one and three-fourths hours.
4. It may be necessary to put a small quantity of water in pan while meat is cooking.
5. Make gravy, following directions for Roast Beef Gravy on page 202, or serve with Currant Jelly Sauce.
