## Ingredients

- Squirrels
- Melted butter
- Pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Clean and soak to draw out the blood.
2. Wipe dry and broil over a hot, clear fire, turning often.
3. When done, lay in a hot dish and anoint with melted butter, seasoned with pepper and salt.
4. Use at least a tablespoonful for each squirrel, and let it lie between two hot dishes five minutes before sending to table.
