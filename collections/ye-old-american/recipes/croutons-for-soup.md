## Ingredients

- Boiling fat
- Stale bread

## Directions

1. Have the depth of an inch of boiling fat in a frying pan
2. Cut stale bread into little half-inch squares
3. Drop into the frying pan enough of these bits of bread to cover the surface of the fat
4. When browned, remove with a skimmer and drain
5. Add to the hot soup and serve
