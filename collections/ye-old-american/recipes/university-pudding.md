## Ingredients

- Frozen Tom and Jerry base
- 1 cup mixed fruit soaked in brandy for twelve hours
- glacé cherries
- Sultana raisins
- sliced citron
- candied pineapple
- whipped cream
- sweetener
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Prepare same as Frozen Tom and Jerry
2. Freeze to a mush
3. Add one cup mixed fruit which has been soaked in brandy to cover for twelve hours, using glacé cherries, Sultana raisins, sliced citron, and candied pineapple
4. Finish freezing
5. Serve in small beer jugs
6. Garnish with cream, whipped, sweetened, and flavored
