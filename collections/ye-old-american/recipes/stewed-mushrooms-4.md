## Ingredients

- button mushrooms
- salt
- butter
- flour
- cream or egg yolk
- water

## Directions

1. Choose buttons of uniform size
2. Wipe them clean and white with a wet flannel
3. Put them in a stewpan with a little water
4. Let them stew very gently for a quarter of an hour
5. Add salt to taste
6. Work in a little flour and butter, to make the liquor about as thick as cream
7. Let it boil for five minutes
8. When ready to dish it up, stir in two tablespoonfuls of cream or the yolk of an egg
9. Stir it over the fire for a minute, but do not let it boil
10. Serve
