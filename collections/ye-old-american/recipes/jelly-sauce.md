## Ingredients

- ½ cup currant jelly
- 1 tablespoonful butter, melted
- ½ dessert spoonful arrowroot or corn-starch; wet with cold water
- 1 glass pale Sherry
- 3 tablespoonfuls boiling water

## Directions

1. Stir the arrowroot into the boiling water and heat, stirring all the time, until it thickens; add the butter, and set aside until almost cool, when beat in, spoonful by spoonful, the jelly to a smooth pink paste.
2. Pour in the wine, stir hard, and heat in a tin vessel, set within another of boiling water, until very hot.
3. Pour over and around Neapolitan, bread-and-marmalade puddings, cake fritters, and Queen's toast.
