## Ingredients

- 1 quart raspberries
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Sprinkle raspberries with sugar, cover, and let stand two hours
2. Mash, squeeze through cheese-cloth, add water and lemon juice to taste, then freeze
