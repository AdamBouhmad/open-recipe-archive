## Ingredients

- halibut
- cold salt and water
- egg-sauce
- mashed potato
- milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Lay in cold salt and water for an hour.
2. Wipe dry and score the skin in squares.
3. Put into the kettle with cold salted water enough to cover it.
4. Let it heat gradually, and boil from half to three-quarters of an hour, in proportion to the size of the piece.
5. Drain and accompany by egg-sauce—either poured over the fish, or in a sauce-boat.
6. Save the cold remnants of the fish and what sauce is left until next morning.
7. Pick out as you would cod, mix with an equal quantity of mashed potato, moisten with the sauce, or with milk and butter if you have no sauce.
8. Put it into a skillet, and stir until it is very hot.
9. Do not burn. Season with pepper and salt.
