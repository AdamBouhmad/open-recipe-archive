## Ingredients

- four potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- one-half tablespoon butter
- hot milk

## Directions

1. Mash, pare, soak, and cut four potatoes in one-fourth inch slices
2. Put a layer in buttered baking-dish
3. sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. dredge with flour, and dot over with one-half tablespoon butter
5. repeat
6. Add hot milk until it may be seen through top layer
7. bake one and one-fourth hours or until potato is soft
