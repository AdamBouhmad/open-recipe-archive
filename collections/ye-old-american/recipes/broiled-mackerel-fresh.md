## Ingredients

- fresh mackerel
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean the mackerel, wash, and wipe dry.
2. Split it open, so that when laid flat the backbone will be in the middle.
3. Sprinkle lightly with salt, and lay on a buttered gridiron over a clear fire, with the inside downward, until it begins to brown.
4. Turn the other side.
5. When quite done, lay on a hot dish and butter it plentifully.
6. Turn another hot dish over the lower one, and let it stand two or three minutes before sending to table.
