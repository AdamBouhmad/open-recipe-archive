## Ingredients

- Green or ripe apples
- Sugar
- Butter
- Nutmeg
- Crust
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and core apples
2. Stew apples
3. Mash to a smooth compote
4. Sweeten to taste
5. While hot, stir in a teaspoonful butter for each pie
6. Season with nutmeg
7. When cool, fill your crust
8. Either cross-bar the top with strips of paste, or bake without cover
9. Eat cold, with powdered sugar strewed over it
