## Ingredients

- One cup grated cocoanut
- one and one-half cups sugar
- three-fourths cup butter
- one-half cup milk
- two eggs
- one large teaspoonful baking powder
- one-half teaspoonful extract of vanilla
- flour enough to roll out

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients
2. roll out
