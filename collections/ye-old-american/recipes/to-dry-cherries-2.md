## Ingredients

- fine and largest red cherries
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Choose the finest and largest red cherries for this purpose.
2. Stone them, and spread them on large dishes in the sun, till they become quite dry, taking them in as soon as the sun is off, or if the sky becomes cloudy.
3. Put them up in stone jars, strewing among them some of the best brown sugar.
