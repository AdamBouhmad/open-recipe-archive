## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup macaroni
- 2 quarts boiling water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- grated cheese
- 1 1/2 cups White Sauce
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups scalded milk
- buttered crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water twenty minutes or until soft
2. Drain in strainer
3. Pour over it cold water to prevent pieces from adhering
4. Put a layer of boiled macaroni in buttered baking dish
5. Sprinkle with grated cheese
6. Repeat
7. Pour over White Sauce
8. Cover with buttered crumbs
9. Bake until crumbs are brown
