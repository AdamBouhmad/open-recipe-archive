## Ingredients

- fresh meat or fowl
- mashed potatoes
- butter
- salt
- black pepper
- prepared mustard
- cayenne pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Take any kind of fresh meat or fowl, chop very fine, add an equal quantity of smoothly mashed potatoes, mix, and season with butter, salt, black pepper, a little prepared mustard, and a little cayenne pepper.
2. Make into cakes, dip in egg and bread crumbs and fry a light brown.
