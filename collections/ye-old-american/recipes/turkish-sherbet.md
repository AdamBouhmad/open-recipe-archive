## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds of loaf-sugar
- 1 quart of clear cold water
- 1 dozen large fine ripe oranges
- ice

## Directions

1. Put into a large pitcher a pound and a half of the best loaf-sugar, broken small
2. Pour on it a quart of clear cold water
3. Crush and stir the sugar till it is all melted
4. Take a dozen large fine ripe oranges, and roll every one under your hand on a table, to increase the juice
5. Take off the yellow rind in large thin pieces, and cut them neatly into round shapes, the size of a half-dollar
6. Squeeze the juice of the oranges through a strainer upon the melted sugar, and stir it well
7. Set the pitcher on ice till the sherbet is wanted
8. Serve it up in lemonade-glasses, placing in the bottom of each, one of the round pieces of orange-rind, and lay a lump of ice upon it
9. Fill the glasses with the sherbet
