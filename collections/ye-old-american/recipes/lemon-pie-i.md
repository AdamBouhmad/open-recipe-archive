## Ingredients

- ½ cup chopped apple
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 beaten egg
- ¼ cup rolled common crackers
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- Grated rind 1 lemon
- 1 teaspoon melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given and bake with two crusts.
