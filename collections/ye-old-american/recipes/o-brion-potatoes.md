## Ingredients

- three cups potato cubes or balls
- deep fat
- salt
- one slice onion
- one and one-half tablespoons butter
- three canned pimentoes
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Fry three cups potato cubes or balls in deep fat, drain on brown paper, and sprinkle with salt.
2. Cook one slice onion in one and one-half tablespoons butter three minutes, remove onion, and add to butter three canned pimentoes cut in small pieces.
3. When thoroughly heated add potatoes; stir until well mixed, turn into serving dish, and sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
