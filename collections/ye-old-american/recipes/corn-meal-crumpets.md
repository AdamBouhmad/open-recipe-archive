## Ingredients

- 1 quart Indian meal
- 1 quart boiled milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls yeast
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 2 heaping tablespoonfuls lard or butter or half-and-half
- 1 saltspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Scald the meal with the boiling milk, and let it stand until lukewarm
2. Stir in the sugar, yeast, and salt
3. Leave it to rise five hours
4. Add the melted shortening
5. Beat well
6. Put in greased muffin-rings
7. Set these near the fire for fifteen minutes
8. Bake half an hour in a quick oven
