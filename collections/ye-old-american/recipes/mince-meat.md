## Ingredients

- two pounds of lean
- one pound of fat
- fine juicy apples, three pounds
- four pounds of raisins
- a large table-spoonful of powdered cloves
- a large table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon
- a pound of brown sugar
- a quart of bottled or sweet cider
- grated peel and juice of an orange

## Directions

1. Boil the meat, and when it is quite cold, chop it fine. Or you may substitute cold roast beef.
2. Pare and core the apples, cut them in pieces, and chop them.
3. Stone the raisins, and chop them also.
4. Add the powdered cloves, powdered cinnamon, and brown sugar.
5. Mix all thoroughly, moistening it with a quart of bottled or sweet cider.
6. You may add the grated peel and the juice of an orange.
7. Bake it in good common paste.
