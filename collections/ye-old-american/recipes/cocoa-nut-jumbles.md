## Ingredients

- a large cocoa-nut, grated
- half a pound of butter
- a pound of sifted flour
- three beaten eggs
- a little rose water
- loaf-sugar for grating

## Directions

1. Grate a large cocoa-nut.
2. Rub half a pound of butter into a pound of sifted flour.
3. Wet it with three beaten eggs and a little rose water.
4. Add by degrees the cocoa-nut, so as to form a stiff dough.
5. Flour your hands and your paste-board.
6. Divide the dough into equal portions and make the jumbles with your hands into long rolls.
7. Curl them round and join the ends so as to form rings.
8. Grate loaf-sugar over them.
9. Lay them in buttered pans, not so near as to run into each other.
10. Bake them in a quick oven from five to ten minutes.
