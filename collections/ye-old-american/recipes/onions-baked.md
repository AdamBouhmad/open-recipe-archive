## Ingredients

- large Spanish onions
- salt
- buttered tissue paper
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash them clean, but do not peel
2. Put into a saucepan with slightly salted water
3. Boil an hour, replacing the water with more boiling hot as it evaporates
4. Turn off the water and lay the onions on a cloth to dry them well
5. Roll each one in a piece of buttered tissue paper, twisting it at the top to keep it on
6. Bake in a slow oven about an hour, or until tender all through
7. Peel them and place in a deep dish
8. Brown slightly, basting well with butter for fifteen minutes
9. Season with salt and pepper and pour some melted butter over them
