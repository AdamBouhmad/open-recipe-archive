## Ingredients

- milk
- prepared rennet
- ladle
- thin cloth
- water
- light press
- thread
- fine salt
- board
- cold water
- sweet grass

## Directions

1. First scald the quantity of milk desired; let it cool a little, then add the rennet
2. When the curd is formed, take it out on a ladle without breaking it; lay it on a thin cloth held by two persons
3. Dash a ladleful of water over each ladleful of curd, to separate the curd
4. Hang it up to drain the water off, and then put it under a light press for one hour
5. Cut the curd with a thread into small pieces; lay a cloth between each two, and press for an hour
6. Take them out, rub them with fine salt, let them lie on a board for an hour, and wash them in cold water
7. Let them lie to drain, and in a day or two the skin will look dry
8. Put some sweet grass under and over them, and they will soon ripen
