## Ingredients

- 2 squares chocolate
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 3 tablespoons hot water
- Confectioners' sugar
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate over boiling water, add butter and hot water.
2. Cool, and add sugar to make of right consistency to spread.
3. Flavor with vanilla.
