## Ingredients

- four pounds of beef
- one gallon of cold water
- a quart of tomatoes
- one chopped onion
- salt and pepper to taste
- a teaspoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Place in a kettle four pounds of beef.
2. Pour over it one gallon of cold water.
3. Let the meat and water boil slowly for three hours, or until the liquid is reduced to about one-half.
4. Remove the meat and put into the broth a quart of tomatoes, and one chopped onion; salt and pepper to taste.
5. Dissolve a teaspoonful of flour and stir in.
6. Allow to boil half an hour longer.
7. Strain and serve hot.
8. Canned tomatoes in place of fresh ones may be used.
