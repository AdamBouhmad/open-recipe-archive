## Ingredients

- Same ingredients as for Parker House Rolls
- One-fourth cup butter

## Directions

1. Shape in small biscuits
2. Place in rows on a floured board
3. Cover with cloth and pan
4. Let rise until light and well puffed
5. Flour handle of wooden spoon and make a deep crease in middle of each biscuit
6. Take up and press edges together
7. Place closely in buttered pan
8. Cover and let rise
9. Bake twelve to fifteen minutes in hot oven
