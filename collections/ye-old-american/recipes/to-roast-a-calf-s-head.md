## Ingredients

- calf's head
- veal
- suet
- brains
- tongue
- sage
- parsley
- salt
- eggs
- bacon
- gravy
- oysters
- mushrooms
- capers
- white wine

## Directions

1. Wash and pick the head very nicely
2. Take out the brains and tongue, prepare a good quantity of forced meat, with veal and suet well seasoned
3. Fill the hole of the head with this forced meat, skewer and tie it together upon the spit
4. Roast it for an hour and a half
5. Beat up the brains with a little sage and parsley shred fine, a little salt, and the yelks of two or three eggs
6. Boil the tongue, peel, and cut it into large dice, fry that with the brains, also some of the forced meat made up into balls, and slices of bacon
7. Let the sauce be strong gravy, with oysters, mushrooms, capers, and a little white wine thickened
