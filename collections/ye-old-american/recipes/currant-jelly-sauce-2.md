## Ingredients

- 1 cup Brown Sauce, from which onion has been omitted
- one-fourth tumbler currant jelly
- 1 tablespoon Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Add one-fourth tumbler currant jelly and one tablespoon Sherry wine to one cup Brown Sauce, from which onion has been omitted; or, add currant jelly to one cup gravy made to serve with roast lamb.
2. Currant Jelly Sauce is suitable to serve with lamb.
