## Ingredients

- sound, solid cabbage
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a sound, solid cabbage and with a large sharp knife shave it very fine
2. Put it in a saucepan and pour in half a teacupful of water, or just enough to keep it from burning
3. Cover it very tightly, so as to confine the steam
4. Watch it closely and add a little water now and then, until it begins to be tender
5. Put into it a large tablespoonful of butter
6. Add salt and pepper to taste
7. Dish it hot
8. If you prefer to give it a tart taste, just before taking from the fire add a third of a cup of good vinegar
