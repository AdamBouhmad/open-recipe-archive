## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup raw halibut
- White 1 egg
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cayenne
- ½ cup heavy cream

## Directions

1. Chop fish finely, or force through a meat chopper.
2. Pound in mortar, adding gradually white of egg, and working until smooth.
3. Add seasonings, rub through a sieve, and then add cream.
