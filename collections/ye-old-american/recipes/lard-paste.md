## Ingredients

- half a pound of nice lard
- half a pound of fresh butter
- two pounds and a quarter of flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of nice lard, and half a pound of fresh butter; rub them together into two pounds and a quarter of flour.
2. Mix it with a little cold water to a stiff dough.
3. Roll it out twice.
