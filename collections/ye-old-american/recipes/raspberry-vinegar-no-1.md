## Ingredients

- a quart of raspberries
- a quart of good vinegar
- loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Put a quart of raspberries into a suitable dish
2. Pour over them a quart of good vinegar
3. Let it stand twenty-four hours
4. Strain through a flannel bag
5. Pour this liquor on another quart of berries
6. Do this for three or four days successively and strain it
7. Make it very sweet with loaf sugar
8. Bottle and seal it
