## Ingredients

- a pint of water
- a tablespoonful of cornstarch or flour
- a little cold water
- a cupful of sugar
- a tablespoonful of vinegar
- a piece of butter as large as a small egg
- a tablespoonful of vanilla or lemon extract

## Directions

1. Into a pint of water stir a paste made of cornstarch or flour rubbed smooth with a little cold water
2. Add a cupful of sugar and a tablespoonful of vinegar
3. Cook well for three minutes
4. Take from the fire and add a piece of butter as large as a small egg
5. When cool, flavor with a tablespoonful of vanilla or lemon extract
