## Ingredients

- a haunch of venison
- water
- butter or clarified drippings
- flour
- salt and pepper
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Wash the haunch slightly in tepid water and dry it thoroughly by rubbing it with a clean, soft cloth
2. Lay over the fat side a large sheet of thickly-buttered paper
3. Apply a paste of flour and water about three-quarters of an inch thick
4. Cover this again with two or three sheets of stout paper
5. Secure the whole well with twine
6. Put down to roast with a little water in the dripping-pan
7. Let the fire be clear and strong
8. Baste the paper immediately with butter or clarified drippings
9. Roast the joint from three to four hours according to its weight and quality
10. About twenty minutes before the joint is done, remove the paste and paper
11. Baste the meat in every part with butter and dredge it very lightly with flour
12. Let it take a pale brown color and serve hot with unflavored gravy made with a thickening in a tureen and good currant jelly
