## Ingredients

- half a cupful of grated chocolate
- a pint of water
- a pint of milk
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Rub the chocolate smooth in a little cold water and stir into the boiling water
2. Boil twenty minutes
3. Add the milk and boil ten minutes more, stirring it often
4. Sweeten to your taste
