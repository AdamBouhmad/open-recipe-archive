## Ingredients

- Two pints of cream
- Six ounces of powdered loaf sugar
- The juice of two large lemons
- Two glassfuls of white wine
- Two ounces of isinglass or gelatine
- Four small teacupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Put two pints of cream into two bowls
2. With one bowl mix six ounces of powdered loaf sugar, the juice of two large lemons and two glassfuls of white wine
3. Add the other pint of cream
4. Stir the whole very hard
5. Boil two ounces of isinglass or gelatine with four small teacupfuls of water till reduced to one-half
6. Stir the mixture luke-warm into the other ingredients
7. Put them in a glass dish to congeal
