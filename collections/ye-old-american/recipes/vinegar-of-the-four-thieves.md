## Ingredients

- lavender, a large handful
- rosemary, a large handful
- sage, a large handful
- wormwood, a large handful
- rue, a large handful
- mint, a large handful
- four quarts of very strong vinegar
- clove of garlic

## Directions

1. Put the herbs in a pot of earthen ware
2. Pour on them four quarts of very strong vinegar
3. Cover the pot closely and put a board on the top
4. Keep it in the hottest sun two weeks
5. Strain and bottle it, putting in each bottle a clove of garlic
6. When it has settled in the bottle and become clear, pour it off gently
7. Repeat until you get it all free from sediment
