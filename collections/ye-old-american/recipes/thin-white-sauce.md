## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- 1 cup scalded milk
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains pepper

## Directions

1. Put butter in saucepan and stir until melted and bubbling
2. Add flour mixed with seasonings and stir until thoroughly blended
3. Pour on gradually the milk, adding about one-third at a time, stirring until well mixed
4. Beat until smooth and glossy
