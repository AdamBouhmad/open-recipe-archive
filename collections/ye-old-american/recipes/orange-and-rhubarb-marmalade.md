## Ingredients

- eight oranges
- five pounds rhubarb
- four pounds cut sugar

## Directions

1. Remove peel in quarters from oranges and prepare as for Orange Marmalade
2. Divide oranges in sections and remove seeds and tough part of skin
3. Put into a preserving kettle and add five pounds rhubarb, skinned and cut in one-half inch pieces
4. Heat to boiling-point and boil one-half hour
5. Add four pounds cut sugar and cut rind
6. Cook slowly two hours
7. Turn into glasses
