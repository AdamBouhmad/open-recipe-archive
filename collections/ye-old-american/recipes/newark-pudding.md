## Ingredients

- 1 cup fine bread-crumbs soaked in a pint of the milk
- 1 quart of milk
- 5 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls rice-flour
- ½ lb. raisins seeded, cut in two, and dredged with flour
- Vanilla or bitter almond extract
- 2 tablespoonfuls melted butter
- ½ teaspoonful soda

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks light
2. Add the soaked bread-crumbs and milk
3. Stir to a smooth batter
4. Put in the rice-flour, wet up first with cold milk
5. Add the reserved pint of milk, the seasoning, butter, the fruit
6. Lastly add the whites whipped stiff
7. Bake an hour in a buttered mould
8. Turn out and pour sauce over it, serving hard sauce also with it
