## Ingredients

- thin slices of bread
- butter
- thin slices of cold boiled ham
- mustard

## Directions

1. Trim the crusts from thin slices of bread
2. Butter them
3. Lay between every two some thin slices of cold boiled ham
4. Spread the meat with a little mustard if liked
