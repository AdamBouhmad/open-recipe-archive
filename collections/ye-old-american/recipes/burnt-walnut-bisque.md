## Ingredients

- 2 cups scalded milk
- Yolks 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- ⅔ cup chopped walnut meats
- 1 cup heavy cream
- ¾ tablespoon vanilla
- Few grains salt

## Directions

1. Make custard of milk, eggs, one-third of the sugar, and salt.
2. Caramelize remaining sugar, add nut meats, and turn into a slightly buttered pan.
3. Cool, pound, and pass through a purée strainer.
4. Add to custard, cool, then add one cup heavy cream, beaten until stiff, and vanilla.
5. Freeze and mould.
