## Ingredients

- four table-spoonfuls of fresh strong yeast
- a pint of lukewarm water
- a small tea-spoonful of salt
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. Put four table-spoonfuls of fresh strong yeast into a pint of lukewarm water
2. Add a little salt; about a small tea-spoonful
3. Stir in gradually as much sifted flour as will make a thick batter
4. Cover the pan, and set it in a warm place to rise
5. When it is quite light, and your griddle is hot, grease and set your muffin rings on it; having first buttered them round the inside
6. Dip out a ladle full of the batter for each ring, and bake them over a quick fire
7. Send them to table hot, and split them by pulling them open with your hands
