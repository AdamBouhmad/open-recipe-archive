## Ingredients

- One dozen thoroughly cleaned birds
- one oyster per bird
- two ounces of boiled salt pork
- three raw potatoes
- a pint of oyster liquor
- an ounce of butter
- salt and pepper
- a crust

## Directions

1. Stuff each bird with an oyster
2. Put the birds into a yellow dish
3. Add two ounces of boiled salt pork and three raw potatoes cut into slices
4. Add a pint of oyster liquor and an ounce of butter
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Cover the dish with a crust and bake in moderate oven
