## Ingredients

- Calves' hearts
- Stuffing
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Pork fat
- One stalk celery
- One tablespoon chopped onion
- Two sprigs parsley
- Four slices carrot
- Turnip
- Bay leaf
- Two cloves
- One-fourth teaspoon peppercorns
- One and one-half cups Brown Stock
- Cooked carrots
- Cooked turnips

## Directions

1. Clean and wash calves' hearts
2. Stuff and skewer into shape
3. Lard
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Dredge with flour
6. Sauté in pork fat, adding to fat one stalk celery, one tablespoon chopped onion, two sprigs parsley, four slices carrot cut in pieces, half the quantity of turnip, a bit of bay leaf, two cloves, and one-fourth teaspoon peppercorns
7. Turn hearts occasionally until well browned
8. Add one and one-half cups Brown Stock
9. Cover and cook slowly one and one-half hours
10. Serve with cooked carrots and turnips cut in strips or fancy shapes
