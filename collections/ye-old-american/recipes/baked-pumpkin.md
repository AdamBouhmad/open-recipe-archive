## Ingredients

- pumpkin
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Choose the richest pumpkin you can find; take out the seeds, cut in quarters or eighths, pare, and slice lengthwise half an inch thick.
2. Arrange in layers—not more than two or three slices deep—in a shallow but broad baking-dish.
3. Put a very little water in the bottom, and bake very slowly until not only done, but dry.
4. It requires a long time, for the heat should be gentle.
5. Butter each strip on both sides when you dish, and eat hot with bread and butter for tea.
