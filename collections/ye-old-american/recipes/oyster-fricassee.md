## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- Milk or cream
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Clean oysters, heat oyster liquor to boiling-point, and strain through double thickness of cheese-cloth
2. Add oysters to liquor and cook until plump
3. Remove oysters with skimmer and add enough cream to liquor to make a cupful
4. Melt butter, add flour, and pour on gradually hot liquid
5. Add salt, cayenne, parsley, oysters, and egg slightly beaten
