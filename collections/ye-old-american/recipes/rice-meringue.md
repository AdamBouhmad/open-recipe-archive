## Ingredients

- one cupful of carefully sorted rice
- water
- one quart of new milk
- the well-beaten yolks of three eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of white sugar
- a little nutmeg, or lemon or vanilla for flavoring
- the whites of the eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil one cupful of carefully sorted rice in water until it is soft
2. When done, drain it so as to remove all the water
3. Cool it and add one quart of new milk, the well-beaten yolks of three eggs, three tablespoonfuls of white sugar and a little nutmeg, or flavor with lemon or vanilla
4. Pour into a baking dish and bake about half an hour
5. Let it get cold
6. Beat the whites of the eggs and add two tablespoonfuls of sugar, flavor with lemon or vanilla
7. Drop or spread it over the pudding and slightly brown it in the oven
