## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- ¼ cup Mocha coffee
- Yolks 3 eggs
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 cups thin cream
- Italian Meringue

## Directions

1. Scald milk with coffee, and add one-half the sugar
2. Without straining, use this mixture for making custard, with eggs, salt, and remaining sugar
3. Add one cup cream and let stand thirty minutes
4. Cool, strain through double cheese-cloth
5. Add remaining cream
6. Freeze
7. Line a mould
8. Fill with Italian Meringue
9. Cover, pack in salt and ice, using two parts crushed ice to one part rock salt
10. Let stand three hours
