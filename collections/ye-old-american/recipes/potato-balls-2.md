## Ingredients

- mashed potatoes
- egg yolk
- flour
- bread crumbs
- clean dripping

## Directions

1. Mix mashed potatoes with the yolk of an egg
2. Roll them into balls
3. Flour them, or cover them with egg and bread crumbs
4. Fry them in clean dripping, or brown them in a Dutch oven
