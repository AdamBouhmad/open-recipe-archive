## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped onion
- 3 tablespoons flour
- ⅔ tablespoon curry powder
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 1 cup crab meat
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook butter with onion three minutes
2. Add flour mixed with curry powder and chicken stock
3. When boiling-point is reached add crab meat and season with salt
