## Ingredients

- raisins seeded
- currants
- figs
- citron
- uncooked French Cream
- nuts
- chopped almonds
- hickory nuts
- butternuts
- English walnuts

## Directions

1. Chop raisins seeded, currants, figs and citron fine
2. Mix with the uncooked French Cream, while soft, before the sugar is all mixed in
3. Nuts may be mixed with this cream, stirring into it chopped almonds, hickory nuts, butternuts, or English walnuts
4. Form them into balls, bars or squares
5. Several kinds of nuts may be mixed together
