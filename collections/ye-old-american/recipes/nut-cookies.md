## Ingredients

- Yolks 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup chopped nut meats
- Whites 2 eggs
- 6 tablespoons flour
- Few grains salt

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored, add sugar gradually, nut meats, whites of egg beaten until stiff, and flour mixed with salt.
2. Drop from tip of spoon on buttered sheet, spread, and bake in a moderate oven.
