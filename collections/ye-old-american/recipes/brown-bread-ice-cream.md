## Ingredients

- 3 pints cream
- 1¼ cups dried brown bread crumbs
- ⅞ cup sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Soak crumbs in one quart cream, let stand fifteen minutes, rub through sieve, add sugar, salt, and remaining cream; then freeze.
