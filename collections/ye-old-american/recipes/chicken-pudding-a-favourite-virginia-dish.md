## Ingredients

- ten eggs
- quart of rich milk
- quarter of a pound butter
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- four young chickens
- water
- bundle of thyme and parsley
- white gravy

## Directions

1. Beat ten eggs very light
2. Add to them a quart of rich milk with a quarter of a pound of butter melted and some pepper and salt
3. Stir in as much flour as will make a thin good batter
4. Take four young chickens and clean them nicely
5. Cut off the legs, wings, &c.
6. Put them all in a sauce pan with some salt and water and a bundle of thyme and parsley
7. Boil them till nearly done
8. Take the chicken from the water and put it in the batter
9. Pour it in a dish and bake it
10. Send nice white gravy in a boat
