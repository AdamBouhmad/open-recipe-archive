## Ingredients

- three cups of sweet milk
- one cup of butter and lard, mixed in equal proportions
- one-half cup of good yeast, or half a cake of compressed yeast
- one teaspoonful of salt
- flour
- two well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Add flour enough to make a stiff dough
2. Let it rise over night
3. In the morning, add two well-beaten eggs
4. Knead thoroughly and let it rise again
5. With the hands, make it into balls as large as an egg
6. Roll between the hands to make long rolls (about three inches)
7. Place close together in even rows on well-buttered pans
8. Cover and let them rise again
9. Bake in a quick oven to a delicate brown
