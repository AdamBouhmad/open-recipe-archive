## Ingredients

- shoulder of veal
- salt
- pepper
- butter half pound
- slices of raw salt pork
- biscuit or biscuit dough
- water

## Directions

1. Boil a shoulder of Veal, and cut up, salt, pepper, and butter half pound, and slices of raw salt pork, make a layer of meat, and a layer of biscuit, or biscuit dough into a pot, cover close and stew half an hour in three quarts of water only.
