## Ingredients

- ¾ cup butter
- ⅞ cup sugar
- Yolks 5 eggs
- 4 squares Baker's chocolate
- 1½ cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- Whites 5 eggs
- Apricot or Orange Marmalade

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given, and bake in small tins.
2. Remove from tins, cool, take out a small portion of cake from the centre of each, and fill cavity with marmalade.
3. Cover tops of cake with Marshmallow Frosting or Chocolate Frosting IV.
