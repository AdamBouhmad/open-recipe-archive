## Ingredients

- 3 pound fillet of beef
- 1/2 pound butter
- 1 cup cooked peas
- 1 cup carrots cut in fancy shapes
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms
- 1/4 cup fat from frying-pan
- 5 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup Brown Soup Stock
- 1/3 cup mushroom liquor
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wipe a three-pound fillet, trim, and remove fat.
2. Put one-half pound butter in hot frying-pan and when melted add fillet, and turn frequently until the entire surface is seared and well browned; then turn occasionally until done, the time required being about thirty minutes.
3. Remove to serving dish and garnish with one cup each cooked peas and carrots cut in fancy shapes, both well seasoned, one-half cup raisins seeded and cooked in boiling water until soft, and the caps from one-half pound fresh mushrooms sautéd in butter five minutes.
4. Pour off one-fourth cup fat from frying-pan, add five tablespoons flour, and stir until well browned; then add one cup Brown Soup Stock, one-third cup mushroom liquor, and the caps from one-half pound mushrooms cut in slices and sautéd in butter three minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper, and just before serving add gradually, while stirring constantly, the butter remaining in frying-pan.
6. To obtain mushroom liquor, scrape stems of mushrooms, break in pieces, cover with cold water, and cook slowly until liquid is reduced to one-third cup.
