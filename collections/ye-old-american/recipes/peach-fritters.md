## Ingredients

- Peaches
- Powdered sugar
- Batter made from three eggs, a gill of milk or half cupful, a saltspoonful of salt, four ounces of flour
- Butter
- Sugar
- Yolks of two eggs
- Nutmeg
- Cold water
- Rum

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches, split each in two and take out the stones
2. Dust a little powdered sugar over them
3. Dip each piece in the batter and fry in hot fat
4. For sauce: Put an ounce of butter in a saucepan and whisk to a cream
5. Add four ounces of sugar gradually
6. Beat the yolks of two eggs and add a dash of nutmeg and a gill each of cold water and rum
7. Stir this into the luke-warm batter and allow it to heat gradually
8. Stir constantly until of a smooth, creamy consistency and serve
