## Ingredients

- a potato
- flour
- spice
- sugar
- a spoonful of yeast
- a piece of butter

## Directions

1. Boil and mash a potato
2. Rub into it as much flour as will make it like bread
3. Add spice and sugar to your taste, with a spoonful of yeast
4. When it has risen well, work in a piece of butter
5. Bake it in small rolls, to be eaten hot with butter, either for breakfast or tea
