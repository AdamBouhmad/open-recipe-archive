## Ingredients

- one and half pound sugar
- half pound butter
- two pounds flour
- one glass wine
- one do. rose water
- 18 eggs
- a nutmeg

## Directions

1. rub one and half pound sugar, half pound butter, into two pounds flour
2. add one glass wine, one do. rose water, 18 eggs and a nutmeg
