## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups water
- ⅓ cup seeded and finely cut raisins
- ¾ cup orange juice
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 cup fruit syrup
- ¼ grated nutmeg
- ¼ cup port wine
- Whites 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Make syrup by boiling water and sugar ten minutes
2. Pour over raisins and cool
3. Add fruit syrup and nutmeg
4. Freeze to a mush
5. Add wine and whites of eggs beaten stiff and continue freezing
6. Serve in glasses
