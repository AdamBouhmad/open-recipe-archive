## Ingredients

- a little salt
- one egg beaten
- four ounces of butter
- a quart of flour
- new milk

## Directions

1. Put a little salt, one egg beaten, and four ounces of butter, in a quart of flour
2. Make it into a paste with new milk
3. Beat it for half an hour with a pestle
4. Roll the paste thin, and cut it into round cakes
5. Bake them on a gridiron, and be careful not to burn them
