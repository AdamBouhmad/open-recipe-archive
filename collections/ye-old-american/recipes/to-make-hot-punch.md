## Ingredients

- half a pint of rum
- half a pint of brandy
- quarter of a pound of sugar
- one large lemon
- half a teaspoonful of nutmeg
- one pint of boiling water

## Directions

1. Rub the sugar over the lemon until it has absorbed all the yellow part of the skin
2. Put the sugar into a punch bowl
3. Add the lemon juice (free from pips) and mix these two ingredients well together
4. Pour over them the boiling water, stir well together
5. Add the rum, brandy and nutmeg; mix thoroughly and the punch will be ready to serve
