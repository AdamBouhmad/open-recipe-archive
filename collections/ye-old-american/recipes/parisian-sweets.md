## Ingredients

- 1 lb. figs
- 1 lb. dates
- 1 lb. English walnut meat
- Confectioners' sugar

## Directions

1. Pick over and remove stems from figs and stones from dates.
2. Mix fruit with walnut meat, and force through a meat chopper.
3. Work, using the hands, on a board dredged with confectioners' sugar, until well blended.
4. Roll to one-fourth inch thickness, using confectioners' sugar for dredging board and pin.
5. Shape with a small round cutter, first dipped in sugar, or cut with a sharp knife in three-fourth inch squares.
6. Roll each piece in confectioners' sugar, and shake to remove superfluous sugar.
7. Pack in layers in a tin box, putting paper between each layer.
8. These confections may be used at dinner in place of bonbons or ginger chips.
9. A combination of nut meat (walnut, almond, and filbert) may be used in equal proportions.
