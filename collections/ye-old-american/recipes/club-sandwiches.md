## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- thin slices of cooked bacon
- slices of cold roast chicken
- Mayonnaise Dressing

## Directions

1. Arrange on slices of bread thin slices of cooked bacon.
2. Cover with slices of cold roast chicken, and cover chicken with Mayonnaise Dressing.
3. Cover with slices of bread.
