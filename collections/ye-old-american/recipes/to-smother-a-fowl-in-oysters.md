## Ingredients

- dry Oysters
- water (just sufficient to cover the bird)
- salt and season to your taste
- a pint of stewed oysters
- butter
- pepper
- parsley
- celery

## Directions

1. Fill the bird with dry Oysters, and sew up and boil in water just sufficient to cover the bird, salt and season to your taste--when done tender, put into a deep dish and pour over it a pint of stewed oysters, well buttered and peppered, garnish a turkey with sprigs of parsley or leaves of cellery: a fowl is best with a parsley sauce.
