## Ingredients

- 1 quart of blackberry juice
- 1 lb. white sugar
- ½ oz. grated nutmeg
- ½ oz. powdered cinnamon
- ¼ oz. allspice
- ¼ oz. cloves
- 1 pint best brandy

## Directions

1. Tie the spices in thin muslin bags
2. Boil juice, sugar, and spices together fifteen minutes, skimming well
3. Add the brandy
4. Set aside in a closely covered vessel to cool
5. When perfectly cold, strain out the spices, and bottle, sealing the corks
