## Ingredients

- one-half cupful of butter
- two cupfuls of sugar
- one cupful of milk
- five eggs
- one teaspoonful of soda
- two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- four cups of sifted flour
- a small cocoanut grated

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar until very light.
2. Add the beaten yolks, then the milk, the beaten whites of eggs.
3. Add the flour by degrees.
4. After beating all well together, add a small cocoanut grated.
5. Line the cake-pans with paper well buttered, fill rather more than half full.
6. Bake in a moderate oven.
7. Spread over the top a thin frosting, sprinkled thickly with grated cocoanut.
