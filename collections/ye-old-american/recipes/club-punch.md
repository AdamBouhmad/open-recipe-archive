## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 quart Burgundy
- 1 cup rum
- ⅓ cup brandy
- ⅓ cup Benedictine
- 1 quart Vichy
- 3 sliced oranges
- ½ can pineapple
- Juice 2 lemons
- 1 cup tea infusion
- Ice

## Directions

1. Make a syrup by boiling water and sugar ten minutes.
2. Mix remaining ingredients, except ice, sweeten to taste with syrup, and pour into punch-bowl over a large piece of ice.
