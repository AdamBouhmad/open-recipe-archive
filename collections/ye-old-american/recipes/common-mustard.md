## Ingredients

- flour of mustard
- salt
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Take good flour of mustard; put it in a plate, add to it a little salt, and mix it by degrees with boiling water to the usual consistence, rubbing it for a long time with a broad-bladed knife or a wooden spoon.
2. It should be perfectly smooth.
