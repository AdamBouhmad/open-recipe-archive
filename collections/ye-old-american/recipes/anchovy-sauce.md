## Ingredients

- eight anchovies
- water
- two glasses of red wine
- about half a pint of melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak eight anchovies for three or four hours, changing the water every hour
2. Put them into a sauce-pan with a quart of cold water
3. Set them on hot coals and simmer them till they are entirely dissolved and till the liquid is diminished two-thirds
4. Strain it
5. Stir in two glasses of red wine and add about half a pint of melted butter
6. Heat it over again and send it to table
