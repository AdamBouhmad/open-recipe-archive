## Ingredients

- large selected mushrooms
- butter
- large oysters
- salt
- pepper
- Brown or Béchamel Sauce

## Directions

1. Wash large selected mushrooms.
2. Remove stems, peel caps, and sauté caps in butter.
3. Place in a small buttered shallow pan, cap side being up.
4. Place on each a large oyster, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and place on each a bit of butter.
5. Cook in a hot oven until oysters are plump.
6. Serve with Brown or Béchamel Sauce.
