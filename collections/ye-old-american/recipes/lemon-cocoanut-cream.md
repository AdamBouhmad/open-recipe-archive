## Ingredients

- Juice and grated rind 1 lemon
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- Yolks 2 eggs
- 1 cup shredded cocoanut

## Directions

1. Mix lemon juice and rind with sugar and yolks of eggs slightly beaten.
2. Cook ten minutes in double boiler, stirring constantly.
3. Add cocoanut.
4. Cool, and use as a filling for Corn-starch Cake, or any cake made from the whites of eggs.
