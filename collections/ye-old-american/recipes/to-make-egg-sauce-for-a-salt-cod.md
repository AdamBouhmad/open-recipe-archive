## Ingredients

- eggs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Boil four eggs hard
2. First half chop the white
3. Put in the yolks and chop them both together, but not very small
4. Put them into half a pound of good melted butter
5. Let it boil up
6. Pour it on the fish
