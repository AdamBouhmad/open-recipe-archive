## Ingredients

- shad roe
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Drop into boiling water and cook gently for twenty minutes
2. Take from the fire and drain
3. Butter a tin plate and lay the drained roe upon it
4. Dredge well with salt and pepper and spread soft butter over it
5. Dredge thickly with flour
6. Cook in the oven for half an hour, basting frequently with salt, pepper, flour, butter and water
