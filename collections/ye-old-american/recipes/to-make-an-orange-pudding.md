## Ingredients

- two oranges
- two lemons
- five quarts of water
- a pound of loaf sugar
- a pint of water
- twelve pippins
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Put two oranges and two lemons into five quarts of water
2. Boil them till the rinds are quite tender
3. Take them out, and when cold, slice them thin, and pick out the seeds
4. Put a pound of loaf sugar into a pint of water
5. When it boils, slice into it twelve pippins pared and cored
6. Lay in the lemons and oranges, stew them tender
7. Cover the dish with puff paste, lay the fruit in carefully, in alternate layers
8. Pour on the syrup, put some slips of paste across, and bake it
