## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds
- an ounce and a half of bitter almonds
- rose water
- three pints of rich milk
- half a pound of loaf-sugar
- a glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound in a mortar with rose water the sweet and bitter almonds
2. Sweeten three pints of rich milk with half a pound of loaf-sugar
3. Stir the almonds gradually into the milk
4. Boil it over hot coals
5. As soon as it comes to a boil, take it off and stir it frequently till it gets cold
6. Strain it, add a glass of brandy and put it into decanters
7. When you pour it out for drinking dilute it with water
