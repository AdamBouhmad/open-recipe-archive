## Ingredients

- biscuit finely broken, rusk and spiced cake
- wine
- boiled custard (not too thick)
- syllabub
- jelley
- flowers

## Directions

1. Fill a dish with biscuit finely broken, rusk and spiced cake, wet with wine
2. then pour a good boil'd custard, (not too thick) over the rusk
3. and put a syllabub over that
4. garnish with jelley and flowers
