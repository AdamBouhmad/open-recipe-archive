## Ingredients

- 1 quart ripe huckleberries
- flour for dredging
- ½ pint milk
- ½ pint molasses
- ½ pint powdered sugar
- ½ pound butter
- 1 large table-spoonful powdered cloves and cinnamon mixed
- 5 eggs
- sifted flour
- 1 small tea-spoonful pearl-ash or sal-aratus

## Directions

1. Spread a quart of ripe huckleberries on a large dish, and dredge them thickly with flour
2. Mix together half a pint of milk; half a pint of molasses; half a pint of powdered sugar; and half a pound of butter
3. Warm them by the fire till the butter is quite soft
4. Stir them all together, and set them away till cold
5. Prepare a large table-spoonful of powdered cloves and cinnamon mixed
6. Beat five eggs very light, and stir them gradually into the other ingredients
7. Add by degrees, sufficient sifted flour to make a thick batter
8. Stir in a small tea-spoonful of pearl-ash or dissolved sal-aratus
9. Add by degrees the huckleberries
10. Put the mixture into a buttered pan, or into little tins, and bake it in a moderate oven
