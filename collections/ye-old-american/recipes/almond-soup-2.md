## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 4 tablespoons cold water
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 3 cups White Stock III
- 1 small onion
- 3 stalks celery
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups scalded milk
- 1 cup cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch, chop, and pound almonds in a mortar
2. Add gradually water and salt; then add stock, sliced onion, and celery
3. Let simmer one hour, and rub through a sieve
4. Melt butter, add flour, and pour on gradually the hot liquor; then add milk, cream, and salt and pepper to taste
