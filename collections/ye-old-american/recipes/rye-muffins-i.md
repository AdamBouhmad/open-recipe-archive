## Ingredients

- 1 cup rye meal
- 1 cup flour
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 4 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Make as Graham Muffins II, substituting rye meal for Graham flour.
