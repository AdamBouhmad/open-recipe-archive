## Ingredients

- 1 head of fine white cabbage, minced fine
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls salad oil
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful pepper
- 1 teaspoonful made mustard
- 1 teacupful vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix as for lettuce and pour upon the chopped cabbage.
