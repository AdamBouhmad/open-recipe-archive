## Ingredients

- a brisket of beef
- a sharp knife
- fat bacon
- parsley
- oysters
- pounded cloves
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- a pint of red wine
- a large spoonful of lemon pickle

## Directions

1. Bone a brisket of beef, and make holes in it with a sharp knife about an inch apart, fill them alternately with fat bacon, parsley and oysters, all chopped small and seasoned with pounded cloves and nutmeg, pepper and salt, dredge it well with flour, lay it in a pan with a pint of red wine and a large spoonful of lemon pickle; bake it three hours, take the fat from the gravy and strain it; serve it up garnished with green pickles.
