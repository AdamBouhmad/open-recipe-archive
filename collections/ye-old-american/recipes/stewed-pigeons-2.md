## Ingredients

- pigeons
- onion dressing
- thyme
- corned pork
- hot water
- pepper
- parsley
- flour

## Directions

1. Clean and stuff with onion dressing and thyme, do not sew up
2. Take five or more slices of corned pork, let it fry a while in a pot so that the fat comes out and it begins to brown a little
3. Lay the pigeons all around in the fat, leaving the pork still in
4. Add hot water enough to partially cover them
5. Cover tightly and boil an hour or so until tender
6. Turn off some of the liquid, and keep turning them so they will brown nicely
7. Heat and add the liquor poured off
8. Add extra thyme and pepper, and keep turning until the pigeons and gravy are nicely browned
9. Thicken with a little flour, and serve with the gravy poured over them
10. Garnish with parsley
