## Ingredients

- three cocoanuts, the meats grated
- yolks of five eggs
- half a cupful of white sugar
- a wine-glass of milk
- butter
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Take three cocoanuts, the meats grated, the yolks of five eggs, half a cupful of white sugar, season, a wine-glass of milk
2. put the butter in cold and bake in a nice puff paste
