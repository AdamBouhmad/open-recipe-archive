## Ingredients

- green or summer squash
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash them, cut them into pieces, and take out the seeds.
2. Boil them about three quarters of an hour, or till quite tender.
3. When done, drain and squeeze them well till you have pressed out all the water.
4. Mash them with a little butter, pepper and salt.
5. Put the squash thus prepared into a stew-pan, set it on hot coals, and stir it very frequently till it becomes dry.
6. Take care not to let it burn.
