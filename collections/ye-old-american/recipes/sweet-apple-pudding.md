## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 4 eggs
- 3 cups chopped apple
- 1 lemon
- Nutmeg and cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little vinegar
- Flour for a stiff batter

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks very light, add the milk, seasoning, and flour.
2. Stir hard five minutes, and beat in the apple, then the whites, lastly the soda, well mixed in.
3. Bake in two square shallow pans one hour, and eat hot, with sweet sauce.
4. Cover with paper when half done, to prevent hardening.
