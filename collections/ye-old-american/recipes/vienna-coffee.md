## Ingredients

- Equal parts of Mocha and Java coffee
- One heaping tablespoonful of coffee for each person and two extra
- One egg
- Boiling water
- One pint of cream
- White of an egg, well beaten
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one egg with grounds
2. Pour on coffee half as much boiling water as will be needed
3. Let it froth, then stir down grounds, and let boil five minutes
4. Let it stand where it will keep hot, but not boil, for five or ten minutes
5. Add rest of water
6. To one pint of cream add the white of an egg, well beaten
7. Put this in cups with sugar, and hot coffee added
