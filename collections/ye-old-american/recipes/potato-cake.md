## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- peel and pound them
- yolks of eggs
- wine
- melted butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes, peel and pound them, add yolks of eggs, wine and melted butter work with flour into paste, shape as you please, bake and pour over these melted butter, wine and sugar
