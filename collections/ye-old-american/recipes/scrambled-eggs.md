## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Put a good piece of butter in a frying-pan, and when it is hot drop in the eggs, which should be broken whole into a bowl.
2. Stir in with them a little chopped parsley, some pepper and salt, and keep stirring to and fro, up and down, without cessation, for three minutes.
3. Turn out at once into a hot dish, or upon buttered toast and eat without delay.
