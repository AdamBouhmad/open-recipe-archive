## Ingredients

- One quart of sifted flour
- Three teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- One teaspoonful of salt
- One teaspoonful of sugar
- A tablespoonful of melted butter
- Six well-beaten eggs
- A pint of sweet milk

## Directions

1. Put into one quart of sifted flour three teaspoonfuls of baking powder, one teaspoonful of salt, one of sugar, all thoroughly stirred and sifted together
2. Add a tablespoonful of melted butter, six well-beaten eggs and a pint of sweet milk
3. Cook in waffle-irons heated and well greased
4. Serve hot
