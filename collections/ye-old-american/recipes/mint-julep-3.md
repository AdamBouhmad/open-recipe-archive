## Ingredients

- Green mint sprigs
- 1 teaspoon
- 1 generous teaspoonful white sugar
- Water
- Pounded ice
- 2 tablespoonfuls fine brandy

## Directions

1. Slightly bruise green mint sprigs in a tumbler with a teaspoon
2. Put in a generous teaspoonful of white sugar
3. Add gradually, stirring and rubbing lightly, enough water to fill the glass three-quarters of the way to the top
4. Fill up with pounded ice
5. Stir hard
6. Pour into a larger glass that you may shake up well
7. Put in two tablespoonfuls fine brandy
