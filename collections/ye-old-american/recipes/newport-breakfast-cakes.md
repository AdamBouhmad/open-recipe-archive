## Ingredients

- one quart of bread dough
- three eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- one tablespoonful of sugar
- one teaspoonful of soda
- warm milk

## Directions

1. Take one quart of dough from the bread at an early hour in the morning
2. Break three eggs, separating yolks and whites, both to be whipped to a light froth
3. Mix them into the dough
4. Gradually add two tablespoonfuls of melted butter, one of sugar, one teaspoonful of soda, and enough warm milk until it is a batter the consistency of buckwheat cakes
5. Beat it well and let it rise until breakfast time
6. Have the griddle hot and nicely greased
7. Pour on the batter in small round cakes and bake a light brown, the same as any griddle cake
