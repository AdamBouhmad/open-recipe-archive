## Ingredients

- four ounces of the very best mustard powder
- four salt-spoons of salt
- a large table-spoonful of minced tarragon leaves
- two cloves of garlic chopped fine
- vinegar
- salad oil

## Directions

1. Mix together four ounces of the very best mustard powder, four salt-spoons of salt, a large table-spoonful of minced tarragon leaves, and two cloves of garlic chopped fine.
2. Dilute it to the proper consistence by adding alternately equal portions of vinegar and salad oil.
3. It will probably require about four wine-glassfuls or half a pint.
4. Mix it well, using for the purpose a wooden spoon.
5. When done, put it into a wide-mouthed bottle or into little white jars.
6. Cork it very closely, and keep it in a dry place.
