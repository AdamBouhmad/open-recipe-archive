## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 yeast cake dissolved in ¼ cup lukewarm water
- Flour
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- Yolk one egg
- ⅛ teaspoon mace
- ¼ cup melted butter

## Directions

1. Scald milk
2. When lukewarm, add dissolved yeast cake and one and one-half cups flour
3. Beat well, cover, and let rise until light
4. Add sugar, salt, eggs well beaten, mace, and butter, and enough more flour to knead
5. Knead and let rise again
6. Shape and bake same as Salad Rolls, or roll in a long strip to one-fourth inch in thickness
7. Spread with butter, roll up like jelly roll, and cut in one-inch pieces
8. Place pieces in pan close together, flat side down
9. A few gratings from the rind of a lemon or one-half teaspoon lemon extract may be substituted in place of mace
