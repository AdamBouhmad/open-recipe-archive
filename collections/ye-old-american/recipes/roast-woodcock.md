## Ingredients

- bread-crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- melted butter
- butter
- water
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Fill the birds with a rich force-meat of bread-crumbs, peppered and salted, shortened with melted butter.
2. Sew them up and roast, basting with butter and water, from twenty minutes to half an hour.
3. When half done, put circular slices of buttered toast beneath, and serve upon these when you take them up.
