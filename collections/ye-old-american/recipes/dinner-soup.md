## Ingredients

- 3½ lbs. lean beef from round
- 2 lbs. marrow-bone
- 2 qts. cold water
- 1 can tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon peppercorns
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tablespoon lean raw ham, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ⅓ cup carrot
- ⅓ cup turnip
- onion cut in small pieces
- celery cut in small pieces
- 1 sprig parsley
- ½ bay leaf

## Directions

1. Wipe meat and cut in inch cubes.
2. Put one-half in kettle with marrow-bone, water, and tomatoes.
3. Brown remaining half in hot frying-pan with some marrow from bone, then turn into kettle.
4. Heat slowly to boiling-point, and cook at temperature just below boiling-point five hours.
5. Cook ham and vegetables with butter five minutes, then add to soup with peppercorns, salt, parsley, and bay leaf.
6. Cook one and one-half hours, strain, cool quickly, remove fat, and clear.
