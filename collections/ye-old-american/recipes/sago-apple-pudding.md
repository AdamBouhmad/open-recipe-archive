## Ingredients

- one cupful of sago
- one quart of tepid water
- pinch of salt
- six or eight apples
- water

## Directions

1. Soak the sago in a quart of tepid water with a pinch of salt for one hour
2. Pare and core, or quarter six or eight apples, and steam them tender and put in the pudding-dish
3. Boil and stir the sago until clear, adding water to make it thin, and pour it over the apples
4. Bake one hour
