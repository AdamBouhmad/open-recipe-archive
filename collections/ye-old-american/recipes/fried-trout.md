## Ingredients

- brook trout
- flour
- butter
- clarified dripping
- lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and dry the fish, roll lightly in flour, and fry in butter or clarified dripping, or butter and lard.
2. Let the fat be hot, fry quickly to a delicate brown, and take up the instant they are done.
3. Lay for an instant upon a hot folded napkin, to absorb whatever grease may cling to their speckled sides; then range side by side in a heated dish, garnish, and send to the table.
4. Use no seasoning except salt, and that only when the fish are fried in lard or unsalted dripping.
