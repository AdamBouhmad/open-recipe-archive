## Ingredients

- 1 quart oysters
- 4 cups milk
- 1 slice onion
- 2 blades mace
- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅓ cup flour
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Make same as Oyster Soup, adding yolks of eggs, slightly beaten, just before serving
2. Garnish with Fish Quenelles
