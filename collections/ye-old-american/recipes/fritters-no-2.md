## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 quart milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful soda dissolved in hot water
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar sifted into the flour
- A little salt

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and whites separately, of course; stir the milk in with the former, then the soda, the flour, and salt, finally the whites.
2. Beat very hard, and fry at once, in great ladlefuls.
