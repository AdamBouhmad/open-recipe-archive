## Ingredients

- a quart of cyder
- double refined sugar
- nutmeg
- milk
- cream

## Directions

1. Sweeten a quart of cyder with double refined sugar, grate nutmeg into it, then milk your cow into your liquor, when you have thus added what quantity of milk you think proper, pour half a pint or more, in proportion to the quantity of syllabub you make, of the sweetest cream you can get all over it.
