## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of very fine stale biscuit or bread crumbs
- one cupful of rich milk, half cream if you can get it
- five eggs beaten very light
- half a teaspoonful of soda stirred in boiling water
- one cupful of sweet jelly, jam or marmalade
- sweetened cream

## Directions

1. Scald the milk and pour over the crumbs
2. Beat until half cold and stir in the beaten yolks, then whites, finally the soda
3. Fill large cups half full with the batter
4. Set in a quick oven and bake half an hour
5. When done, turn out quickly and dexterously
6. With a sharp knife make an incision in the side of each
7. Pull partly open and put a liberal spoonful of the conserve within
8. Close the slit by pinching the edges with your fingers
9. Eat warm with sweetened cream
