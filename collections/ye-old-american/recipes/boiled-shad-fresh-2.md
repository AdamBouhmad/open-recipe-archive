## Ingredients

- shad with roe
- salt
- cloths
- fish-kettle
- salted water
- drawn butter

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and wipe the fish.
2. A roe shad is best for this purpose.
3. Cleanse the roes thoroughly, and having sprinkled both shad and eggs with salt, wrap in separate cloths and put into a fish-kettle, side by side.
4. Cover with salted water, and boil from half an hour to three-quarters, in proportion to the size.
5. Serve the shad upon a hot dish, with a boat of drawn butter mingled.
