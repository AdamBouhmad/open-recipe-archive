## Ingredients

- cold boiled ham
- Sauce Tartare
- thin slices of buttered bread

## Directions

1. Finely chop cold boiled ham, and moisten with Sauce Tartare.
2. Spread between thin slices of buttered bread prepared as for Bread and Butter Folds.
