## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- 1 cup milk
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- Anchovy essence

## Directions

1. Make a thin white sauce of butter, flour, milk, and seasonings.
2. Add eggs finely chopped, and season with Anchovy essence.
3. Serve same as Welsh Rarebit I.
