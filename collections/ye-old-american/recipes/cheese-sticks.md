## Ingredients

- Bread sticks
- Butter
- Grated cheese
- Salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. Cut bread sticks in halves lengthwise, spread thinly with butter.
2. Sprinkle with grated cheese seasoned with salt and cayenne, and bake until delicately browned.
