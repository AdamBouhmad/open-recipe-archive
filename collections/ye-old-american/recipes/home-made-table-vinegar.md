## Ingredients

- four gallons of warm rain-water
- one gallon of common molasses
- two quarts of yeast
- thin muslin

## Directions

1. Put in an open cask four gallons of warm rain-water
2. Add one gallon of common molasses and two quarts of yeast
3. Cover the top with thin muslin
4. Leave it in the sun, covering it up at night and when it rains
5. In three or four weeks it will be good vinegar
