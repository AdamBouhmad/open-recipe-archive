## Ingredients

- Two cups of rich milk
- Four tablespoonfuls of butter
- One gill of yeast
- One teaspoonful of salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Mix warm milk, butter, yeast and salt
2. Add flour enough to make a light dough
3. When light, roll thin and cut in long pieces three inches wide
4. Prick well with a fork
5. Bake in a slow oven
