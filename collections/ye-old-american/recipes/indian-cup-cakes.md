## Ingredients

- a pint and a half of yellow Indian meal
- half a pint wheat flour
- two eggs
- almost a quart of sour milk
- a tea-spoonful of sal-aratus or a very small tea-spoonful of pearl-ash

## Directions

1. Sift a pint and a half of yellow Indian meal, and mix it with half a pint wheat flour
2. Beat two eggs very light
3. Stir them gradually into the meal, in turn with almost a quart of sour milk
4. Dissolve a tea-spoonful of sal-aratus, or a very small tea-spoonful of pearl-ash in a little of the sour milk reserved for the purpose
5. Make the batter as thick as that for a pound-cake, adding more Indian meal if necessary
6. Stir the foaming mixture at the last into the batter
7. Put into buttered cups, or little tin pans
8. Set immediately into an oven, brisk but not too hot
9. When well baked, turn out the cakes, and send them warm to the breakfast-table
10. Eat them with butter
