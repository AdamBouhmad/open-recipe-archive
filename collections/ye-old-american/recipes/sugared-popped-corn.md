## Ingredients

- 2 quarts popped corn
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 cups brown sugar
- ½ cup water

## Directions

1. Put butter in saucepan, and when melted add sugar and water.
2. Bring to boiling-point, and let boil sixteen minutes.
3. Pour over corn, and stir until every kernel is well coated with sugar.
