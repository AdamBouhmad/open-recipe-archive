## Ingredients

- fruit such as cling-stone peaches, damsons, plums, cherries, apricots
- white sugar
- cider vinegar
- stick cinnamon
- whole cloves
- whole allspice

## Directions

1. To every quart of fruit, allow a cup of white sugar and a large pint of good cider vinegar
2. Add half an ounce of stick cinnamon, one tablespoonful of whole cloves, the same of whole allspice
3. Let it come to a boil, and pour it hot over the fruit
4. Repeat this two or three days in succession
5. Seal hot in glass jars if you wish to keep it for a long time
6. For damsons and plums, prick with a needle
7. For peaches, wash with a weak lye, and then rub with a coarse cloth to remove the fur
