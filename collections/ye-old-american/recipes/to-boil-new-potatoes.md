## Ingredients

- new potatoes
- salt
- cream or new milk
- butter

## Directions

1. If very young, rub the skin off with a rough towel. If almost ripe, scrape with a blunt knife.
2. Lay in cold water an hour and cover with cold water slightly salted.
3. Boil half an hour.
4. Drain and salt, and dry for two or three minutes.
5. Send to table plain.
6. Alternatively, crack each by pressing lightly upon it with the back of a wooden spoon, lay them in a deep dish, and pour over them a cupful of cream or new milk heated to a boil, in which a great spoonful of butter has been dissolved.
