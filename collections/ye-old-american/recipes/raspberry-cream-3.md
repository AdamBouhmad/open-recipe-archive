## Ingredients

- raspberry marmalade
- a quart of cream

## Directions

1. Stir as much raspberry marmalade into a quart of cream, as will be sufficient to give a rich flavour of the fruit
2. Strain it, and fill your glasses, leaving out a part to whip into froth for the top
