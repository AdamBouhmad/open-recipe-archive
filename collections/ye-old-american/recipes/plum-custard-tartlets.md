## Ingredients

- One pint of greengage plums, after being rubbed through a sieve
- One large cup of sugar
- Yolks of two eggs well beaten
- Two tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- Puff paste for patty-pan shells

## Directions

1. Whisk all together until light and foamy
2. Bake in small patty-pan shells of puff paste a light brown
3. Fill with the plum paste
4. Beat the two whites until stiff, add two tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
5. Spread over the plum paste and set the shells into a moderate oven for a few moments
