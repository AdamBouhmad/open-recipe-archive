## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread flour
- 1 tablespoon lard
- Cold water
- ⅞ cup butter

## Directions

1. Work lard into flour, first using knife then tips of fingers
2. Moisten to a dough with cold water
3. Pat and roll out same as Puff Paste
4. Dot paste with small pieces of butter, using one-third the quantity
5. Dredge with flour
6. Fold from ends toward centre, then double, making four layers
7. Pat and roll out
8. Repeat until butter is used
9. Roll, shape, chill, and bake in a hot oven
