## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 great spoonful butter
- ¾ cup white sugar
- Juice and grated peel of lemon

## Directions

1. Cream the sugar and butter, stir in the beaten yolks and the lemon, and bake.
2. Beat the whites to a stiff méringue with three tablespoonfuls powdered sugar and a little rose-water.
3. When the pies are done, take from the oven just long enough to spread the méringue over the top, and set back for three minutes.
4. Eat cold.
