## Ingredients

- one quart of flour
- three eggs
- a quart of milk
- a gill of yeast
- a large spoonful of melted butter

## Directions

1. Make a batter of one quart of flour, three eggs, a quart of milk, and a gill of yeast
2. When well risen, stir in a large spoonful of melted butter
3. Bake them in muffin hoops
