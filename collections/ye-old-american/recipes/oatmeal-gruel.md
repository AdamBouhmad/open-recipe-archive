## Ingredients

- four table-spoonfuls of the best grits
- one pint of boiling water
- butter
- wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put four table-spoonfuls of the best grits into a pint of boiling water
2. Let it boil gently and stir it often till it becomes as thick as you wish it
3. Strain it and add to it while warm, butter, wine, nutmeg, or whatever is thought proper to flavour it
