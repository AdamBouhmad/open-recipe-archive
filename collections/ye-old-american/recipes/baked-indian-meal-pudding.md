## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- two gills and a half of corn meal
- seven eggs
- one gill of molasses
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil one quart of milk
2. mix in it two gills and a half of corn meal very smoothly
3. add seven eggs well beaten
4. add a gill of molasses, and a good piece of butter
5. bake it two hours
