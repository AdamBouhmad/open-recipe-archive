## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1½ cups sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon cream of tartar
- Whites 5 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for mixing butter cakes. Bake in shallow pans, and put Marshmallow Cream between the layers and on the top.
