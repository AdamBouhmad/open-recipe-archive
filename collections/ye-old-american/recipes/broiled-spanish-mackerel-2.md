## Ingredients

- Spanish mackerel
- salt
- butter
- lemon juice
- pepper

## Directions

1. Split the fish down the back, take out the backbone, wash it in cold water, dry it with a clean, dry cloth
2. Sprinkle it lightly with salt and lay it on a buttered gridiron, over a clear fire, with the flesh side downward, until it begins to brown
3. Turn the other side
4. Have ready a mixture of two tablespoonfuls of butter melted, a tablespoonful of lemon juice, a teaspoonful of salt, some pepper
5. Dish up the fish hot from the gridiron on a hot dish, turn over the mixture and serve it while hot
