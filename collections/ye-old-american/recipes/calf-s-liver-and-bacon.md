## Ingredients

- calf liver
- bacon
- water
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Slice the liver a quarter of an inch thick.
2. Pour hot water over it and let it remain for a few minutes to clear it from blood.
3. Dry it in a cloth.
4. Take a pound of bacon, or as much as you require, and cut the same number of thin slices as you have of liver.
5. Fry the bacon to a nice crisp.
6. Take it out and keep it hot.
7. Season the liver with pepper and salt and dredge in a little flour.
8. Lay it in the hot bacon fat and fry it a nice brown.
9. Serve it with a slice of bacon on the top of each slice of liver.
10. If you wish a gravy with it, pour off most of the fat from the frying pan.
11. Put in about two ounces of butter and a tablespoonful of flour well rubbed in.
12. Add a cup of water, salt and pepper.
13. Give it one boil and serve in a gravy boat.
