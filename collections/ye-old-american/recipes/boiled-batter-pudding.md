## Ingredients

- a pint of flour
- a teaspoonful of baking powder
- salt
- a tablespoonful of melted butter
- a pint of milk
- four eggs
- fruit

## Directions

1. Sift together a pint of flour and a teaspoonful of baking powder into a deep dish
2. Sprinkle in a little salt, adding also a tablespoonful of melted butter
3. Stir into this gradually a pint of milk; when quite smooth, add four eggs, yolks and whites beaten separately
4. Add enough more flour to make a very stiff batter
5. If liked, any kind of fruit may be stirred into this; a pint of berries or sliced fruit
6. Boil two hours
7. Serve with cream and sugar, wine sauce, or any sweet sauce
