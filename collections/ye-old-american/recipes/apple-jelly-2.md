## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- lemon juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select apples that are rather tart and highly flavored
2. Slice them without paring
3. Place in a porcelain preserving kettle and cover with water
4. Let them cook slowly until the apples look red
5. Pour into a colander and drain off the juice
6. Let this run through a jelly-bag
7. Return to the kettle, which must be carefully washed
8. Boil half an hour
9. Measure it and allow to every pint of juice a pound of sugar and half the juice of a lemon
10. Boil quickly for ten minutes
