## Ingredients

- 1 quart oysters
- Water
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3½ tablespoons flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Paprika
- Celery salt
- 1 cup cream

## Directions

1. Clean, pick over, chop, and parboil oysters; drain and add to liquor enough water to make one quart liquid
2. Brown butter, add flour, and pour on gradually, while stirring constantly, oyster liquor
3. Let simmer one-half hour
4. Season with salt, paprika, and celery salt, and just before serving add cream
