## Ingredients

- 2 heaping tablespoonfuls rice flour
- 1 large cup boiling water
- Same quantity of milk
- 1 saltspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Stir the wet flour into the boiling water, slightly salted.
2. Boil fifteen minutes, stirring almost constantly.
3. Add the milk and cook, after it has come again to a boil, ten minutes longer.
4. Give with sugar and milk for breakfast.
