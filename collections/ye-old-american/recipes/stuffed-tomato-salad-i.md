## Ingredients

- medium-sized tomatoes
- cucumbers
- Mayonnaise Dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Peel medium-sized tomatoes
2. Remove thin slice from top of each and take out seeds and some of pulp
3. Sprinkle inside with salt, invert, and let stand one-half hour
4. Fill tomatoes with cucumbers cut in small cubes and mixed with Mayonnaise Dressing
5. Arrange on lettuce leaves
6. Garnish top of each with Mayonnaise Dressing forced through a pastry bag and tube
