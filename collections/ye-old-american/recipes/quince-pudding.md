## Ingredients

- six large ripe quinces
- half a pint of cream
- half a pound of powdered sugar
- seven eggs
- two wine glasses of rose water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare the quinces, and cut out all the blemishes.
2. Scrape them to a pulp, and mix the pulp with half a pint of cream, and half a pound of powdered sugar, stirring them together very hard.
3. Beat the yolks of seven eggs, omitting all the whites except two, and stir them gradually into the mixture, adding two wine glasses of rose water.
4. Stir the whole well together, and bake it in a buttered dish three quarters of an hour.
5. Grate sugar over it when cold.
