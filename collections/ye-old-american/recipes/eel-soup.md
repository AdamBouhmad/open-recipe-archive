## Ingredients

- eel
- salt pork
- milk
- eggs
- celery
- chopped sweet herbs
- chopped onion
- butter

## Directions

1. Make in precisely the same manner as cat-fish, only boiled longer
2. A chopped onion is no detriment to the flavor and will remove the muddy taste which these fish sometimes acquire from turbid streams
