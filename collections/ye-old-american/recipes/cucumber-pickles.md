## Ingredients

- medium, small-sized cucumbers
- salt for brine
- vinegar
- 1 quart brown sugar
- 1 pint white mustard seed
- small handful whole cloves
- small handful cinnamon sticks
- piece of alum the size of an egg
- 1/2 cup celery seed

## Directions

1. Select the medium, small-sized cucumbers
2. For one bushel make a brine that will bear up an egg
3. Heat it boiling hot and pour it over the cucumbers
4. Let them stand twenty-four hours, then wipe them dry
5. Heat some vinegar boiling hot and pour over them
6. Stand again twenty-four hours
7. Change the vinegar, putting on fresh vinegar with brown sugar, mustard seed, cloves, cinnamon sticks, alum, and celery seed
8. Heat it all boiling hot and pour over the cucumbers
