## Ingredients

- red cabbage
- cider vinegar
- fresh butter
- flour
- salt
- cayenne
- eggs

## Directions

1. Take a red cabbage, wash, drain, and shred it finely
2. Put it into a deep dish, cover it closely, and set it on the top of a stove, or in a bake oven, till it is warm all through
3. Make a dressing as in the receipt for cold slaw
4. Pour it hot over the cabbage
5. Cover the dish, and send it to table as warm as possible
