## Ingredients

- a quart of cream or rich milk
- fine flour
- peach leaves or bitter almonds
- eight yolks and four whites of eggs
- a glass of brandy or white wine
- powdered nutmeg
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- butter
- lemon juice or orange

## Directions

1. Take five table-spoonfuls out of a quart of cream or rich milk, and mix them with two large spoonfuls of fine flour.
2. Set the rest of the milk to boil, flavouring it with half a dozen peach leaves, or with bitter almonds broken up.
3. When it has boiled hard, take it off, strain it, and stir it in the cold milk and flour.
4. Set it away to cool.
5. Beat well eight yolks and four whites of eggs.
6. Add them to the milk.
7. Stir in, at the last, a glass of brandy or white wine, a powdered nutmeg, and a quarter of a pound of sugar.
8. Butter a large bowl or mould; pour in the mixture.
9. Tie a cloth tightly over it.
10. Put it into a pot of boiling water, and boil it two hours, replenishing the pot with hot water from a tea-kettle.
11. When the pudding is done, let it get cool before you turn it out.
12. Eat it with butter and sugar stirred together to a cream, and flavoured with lemon juice or orange.
