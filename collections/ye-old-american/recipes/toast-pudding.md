## Ingredients

- thin slices of stale bread
- butter
- hot stewed fruit

## Directions

1. Toast several thin slices of stale bread, removing the crust, butter them well
2. Pour over them hot stewed fruit in alternate layers
3. Serve warm with rich hot sauce
