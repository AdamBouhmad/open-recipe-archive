## Ingredients

- one pound of beef suet
- two quarts of flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and stringy fibres from a pound of beef suet, and chop it as fine as possible.
2. Sift two quarts of flour into a deep pan, and rub into it one half of the suet.
3. Make it into a round lump of dough with cold water, and then knead it a little.
4. Lay the dough on your paste-board, roll it out very thin, and cover it with the remaining half of the suet.
5. Flour it, roll it out thin again, and then roll it into a scroll.
6. Cut it into as many pieces as you want sheets of paste, and roll them out half an inch thick.
