## Ingredients

- turnip shoots
- cold water
- salt
- bacon

## Directions

1. Put them in cold water an hour before they are dressed
2. When the water boils, put in a small handful of salt, and then your vegetables
3. Boil them, the more water they are boiled in, the better they will look
4. If boiling with bacon in the Virginia style, boil until done in about twenty minutes for fresh and young turnip tops
5. Drain them on the back of a sieve, and put them under the bacon
