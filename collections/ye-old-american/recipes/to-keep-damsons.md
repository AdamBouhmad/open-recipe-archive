## Ingredients

- damsons (when they are first ripe)
- snuff bottles
- plumbs
- water

## Directions

1. Take damsons when they are first ripe, pick them off carefully, wipe them clean
2. Put them into snuff bottles, stop them up tight so that no air can get to them, nor water
3. Put nothing into the bottles but plumbs
4. Put the bottles into cold water, hang them over the fire
5. Let them heat slowly, let the water boil slowly for half an hour
6. When the water is cold, take out the bottles
7. Set the bottles into a cold place
8. They will keep twelve months if the bottles are stopped tight, so as
