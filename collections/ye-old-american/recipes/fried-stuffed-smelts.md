## Ingredients

- smelts
- crumbs
- egg
- deep fat
- Sauce Tartare

## Directions

1. Stuff smelts as for Baked Stuffed Smelts
2. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs
3. Fry in deep fat
4. Serve with Sauce Tartare
