## Ingredients

- sliced fillets cut from the tenderloin
- Maître d'Hôtel Butter or Mushroom Sauce

## Directions

1. Wipe sliced fillets, place in greased broiler, and broil four or five minutes over a clear fire.
2. Serve with Maître d'Hôtel Butter or Mushroom Sauce.
