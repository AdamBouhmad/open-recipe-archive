## Ingredients

- common batter
- one cupful chopped tomatoes
- one onion
- minced parsley
- salt
- pepper
- young tender chickens
- butter
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Make common batter; mix into it a cupful of chopped tomatoes, one onion chopped, some minced parsley, salt and pepper.
2. Cut up young, tender chickens, dry them well and dip each piece in the batter.
3. Fry brown in plenty of butter in a thick-bottomed frying pan.
4. Serve with tomato sauce.
