## Ingredients

- large close bunches of fine ripe grapes
- a quarter of a pound of bruised sugar candy to each bunch
- French brandy

## Directions

1. Allow to each bunch a quarter of a pound of bruised sugar candy
2. Put the grapes and the sugar candy into large jars, about two-thirds full
3. Fill them up with French brandy
4. Tie them up closely, and keep them in a dry place
