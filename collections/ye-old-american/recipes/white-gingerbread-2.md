## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- three quarters of a pound of butter
- a pound of common white sugar powdered
- three table-spoonfuls of the best white ginger
- four eggs
- a small tea-spoonful of pearl-ash

## Directions

1. Sift two pounds of flour into a deep pan, and rub into it three quarters of a pound of butter.
2. Mix in a pound of common white sugar powdered and three table-spoonfuls of the best white ginger.
3. Beat four eggs very light, and mix them gradually with the other ingredients in the pan.
4. Add a small tea-spoonful of pearl-ash.
