## Ingredients

- a pint of fine yellow Indian meal
- half a pint of wheat flour
- a nutmeg
- a table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon
- half a pound of powdered white sugar
- half a pound of fresh butter
- a glass of white wine
- a glass of brandy
- eight eggs

## Directions

1. Sift a pint of fine yellow Indian meal, and half a pint of wheat flour, and mix them well together.
2. Prepare a nutmeg beaten, and mixed with a table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon.
3. Stir together till very light, half a pound of powdered white sugar; and half a pound of fresh butter; adding the spice, with a glass of white wine, and a glass of brandy.
4. Having beaten eight eggs as light as possible, stir them into the butter and sugar, a little at a time, in turn with the meal.
5. Give the whole a hard stirring at the last; put it into a well-buttered tin pan, and bake it about two hours.
