## Ingredients

- radish pods
- salt and water
- mace
- whole pepper
- turmeric
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut them in nice bunches as soon as they are fully formed
2. They must be young and tender
3. Pour boiling salt and water on them
4. Cover with a thick cloth and pewter plate to keep in the steam
5. Repeat this every day till they are a good green
6. Then put them in cold vinegar with mace and whole pepper
7. Mix a little turmeric with a small portion of oil and stir it into the vinegar to make the pods of a more lively green
