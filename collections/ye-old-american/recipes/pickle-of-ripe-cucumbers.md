## Ingredients

- thirty old, full grown cucumbers
- salt
- vinegar
- horse-radish
- mustard seed
- garlic
- onions
- one quart of vinegar
- one ounce of race ginger
- half an ounce of allspice
- half an ounce of turmeric

## Directions

1. Sun-dry thirty old, full grown cucumbers, which have first been pared and split, had the seeds taken out, been salted and let stand twenty-four hours
2. Allow the sun to dry them, not simply drain them
3. When they are moderately dry, wash them with vinegar
4. Place them in layers in a jar, alternating them with a layer of horse-radish, mustard seed, garlic and onions for each layer of cucumbers
5. Boil in one quart of vinegar, one ounce of race ginger, half an ounce of allspice and the same of turmeric
6. When cool pour this over the cucumbers, tie up tightly and set away
7. Allow several months to mature before using
