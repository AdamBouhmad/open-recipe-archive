## Ingredients

- eels
- salt
- pepper
- corn meal
- pork fat

## Directions

1. Clean eels
2. Cut in two-inch pieces
3. Parboil eight minutes
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
5. Dip in corn meal
6. Sauté in pork fat
