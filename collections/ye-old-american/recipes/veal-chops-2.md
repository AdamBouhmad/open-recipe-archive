## Ingredients

- veal chops
- beaten egg
- cracker-crumbs
- parsley
- shallot
- gravy

## Directions

1. Trim the bone as with mutton chops.
2. Fry, dipping in beaten egg and cracker-crumbs.
3. Add a little parsley and a minced shallot to the gravy.
