## Ingredients

- a piece of sturgeon with the skin on (the piece next to the tail)
- bread crumbs
- butter
- chopped parsley
- pepper and salt
- a pint and a half of water
- a gill of red wine
- one gill of mushroom catsup
- brown flour

## Directions

1. Get a piece of sturgeon with the skin on, the piece next to the tail
2. scrape it well
3. cut out the gristle
4. boil it about twenty minutes to take out the oil
5. take it up and pull off the large scales
6. when cold, stuff it with forcemeat made of bread crumbs, butter, chopped parsley, pepper and salt
7. put it in a Dutch oven just large enough to hold it with a pint and a half of water, a gill of red wine, one of mushroom catsup, some salt and pepper
8. stew it gently till the gravy is reduced to the quantity necessary to pour over it
9. take up your sturgeon carefully
10. thicken the gravy with a spoonful of butter rubbed into a large one of brown flour
11. see that it is perfectly smooth when you put it in the dish
