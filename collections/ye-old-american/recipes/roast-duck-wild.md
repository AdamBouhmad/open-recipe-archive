## Ingredients

- wild ducks
- carrot or onion
- bread-crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- sage
- onion
- butter
- water
- currant jelly
- cayenne
- flour

## Directions

1. Parboil with a small carrot, peeled, put within each, to absorb the unpleasant taste.
2. Throw away the carrot or onion, lay in fresh water half an hour.
3. Stuff with bread-crumbs seasoned with pepper, salt, sage, and onion, and roast until brown and tender, basting for half the time with butter and water, then with the drippings.
4. Add to the gravy, when you have taken up the ducks, a tablespoonful of currant jelly, and a pinch of cayenne.
5. Thicken with browned flour and serve in a tureen.
