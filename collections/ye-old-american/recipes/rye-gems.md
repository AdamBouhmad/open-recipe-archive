## Ingredients

- 1⅔ cups rye flour
- 1⅓ cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup molasses
- 1¼ cups milk
- 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients, add molasses, milk, eggs well beaten, and butter.
2. Bake in hot oven in buttered gem pans twenty-five minutes.
