## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds
- six ounces of fine white sugar
- yolks of four eggs
- one ounce of patent gelatine
- one quart of boiling milk

## Directions

1. Blanch a quarter of a pound of sweet almonds
2. Pound them with six ounces of fine white sugar
3. Mix them well with the yolks of four eggs
4. Dissolve one ounce of patent gelatine in one quart of boiling milk
5. Strain it through a sieve and pour into the mixture
6. Stir the whole over the fire until it thickens and is smooth
7. Pour it into your mold and keep it upon ice, or in a cool place, until wanted
8. When ready to serve dip the mold into warm water, rub it with a cloth and turn out the cream carefully upon your dish
