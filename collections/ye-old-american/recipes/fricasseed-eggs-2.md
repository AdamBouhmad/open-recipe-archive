## Ingredients

- eggs
- ground tongue or ham or cold fowl
- parsley
- butter
- mustard
- veal gravy or chicken broth
- cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs hard, cut in half crosswise, and take out the yolks.
2. Chop these fine, or rub to a paste, with a little ground tongue or ham or cold fowl, some minced parsley, some melted butter, and a very little made mustard.
3. Work well together and fill the whites with it, setting them close together in a deep covered dish, the open ends up.
4. Have ready some veal gravy or chicken broth; heat to boiling in a saucepan with a half teaspoonful chopped parsley, salt, pepper, and lastly three tablespoonfuls of cream to a cup of broth.
5. Boil up; pour smoking hot over the eggs, let them stand five minutes, closely covered, and send to table.
