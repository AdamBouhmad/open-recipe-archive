## Ingredients

- a little mace
- a quartered nutmeg
- a quart of cream
- rice (well boiled)
- orange or rose water

## Directions

1. Boil a little mace, a quartered nutmeg in a quart of cream, add rice (well boiled) while boiling sweeten and flavor with orange or rose water, putting into cups or dishes, when cooled, set to serve up.
