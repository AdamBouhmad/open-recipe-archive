## Ingredients

- reed-birds or ortolans
- oyster
- butter
- bread-crumbs
- flour
- lard or fresh butter
- vine leaves

## Directions

1. Put into every bird, an oyster, or a little butter mixed with some finely sifted bread-crumbs.
2. Dredge them with flour.
3. Run a small skewer through them, and tie them on the spit.
4. Baste them with lard or with fresh butter.
5. They will be done in about ten minutes.
6. A very nice way of cooking these birds is, (having greased them all over with lard or with fresh butter, and wrapped them in vine leaves secured closely with a string,) to lay them in a heated iron pan, and bury them in ashes hot enough to roast or bake them.
7. Remove the vine leaves before you send the birds to table.
