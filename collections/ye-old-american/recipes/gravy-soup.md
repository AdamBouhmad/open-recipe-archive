## Ingredients

- eight pounds of coarse lean beef
- the same ingredients as for the shin soup
- the same quantity of water
- a sieve
- two table-spoonsful of mushroom catsup

## Directions

1. Wash it clean and lay it in your pot, put in the same ingredients as for the shin soup, with the same quantity of water, and follow the process directed for that;
2. Strain the soup through a sieve, and serve it up clear, with nothing more than toasted bread in it;
3. Two table-spoonsful of mushroom catsup will add a fine flavour to the soup.
