## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- ½ cup milk
- 1 sweetbread, parboiled and cut in dice
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Beat eggs slightly, using a silver fork, add salt, pepper, milk, and sweetbread.
2. Put butter in hot chafing-dish; when melted, pour in the mixture.
3. Cook until of creamy consistency, constantly stirring and scraping from bottom of the pan.
