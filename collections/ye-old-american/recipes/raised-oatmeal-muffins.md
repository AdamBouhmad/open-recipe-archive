## Ingredients

- ¾ cup scalded milk
- ¼ cup sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ yeast cake dissolved in ¼ cup lukewarm milk
- 1 cup cold cooked oatmeal
- 2½ cups flour

## Directions

1. Add sugar and salt to scalded milk; when lukewarm, add dissolved yeast cake
2. Work oatmeal into flour with tips of fingers, and add to first mixture
3. Beat thoroughly, cover, and let rise over night
4. In morning, fill buttered iron gem pans two-thirds full, let rise on back of range that pan may gradually heat and mixture rise to fill pan
5. Bake in moderate oven twenty-five to thirty minutes
