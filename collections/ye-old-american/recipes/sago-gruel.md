## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 2 tablespoonfuls sago
- 3 teaspoonfuls white sugar
- 1 glass of wine
- 1 tablespoonful lemon juice
- nutmeg to taste
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Put the sago in the water while cold, and warm by setting in a saucepan of boiling water.
2. Stir often, and let it soften and heat for one hour.
3. Then boil ten minutes, stirring all the while.
4. Add the sugar, wine, and lemon, and pour into a bowl or mould to cool.
5. Eat warm, if preferred. The wine and nutmeg should be omitted if the patient be feverish.
