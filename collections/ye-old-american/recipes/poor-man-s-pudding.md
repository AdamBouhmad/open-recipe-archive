## Ingredients

- 4 cups milk
- ½ cup rice
- ⅓ cup molasses
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Wash rice, mix and bake same as Rice Pudding. At last stirring, add butter.
