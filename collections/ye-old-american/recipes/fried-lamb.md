## Ingredients

- leg
- loin
- shank
- flour
- parsley
- butter
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Separate the leg from the loin, cut off the shank and boil the leg
2. Divide the loin in chops, dredge and fry them a nice brown
3. Lay the leg in the middle of the dish, and put the chops around
4. Pour over parsley and butter
5. And garnish with fried parsley
6. The leg cut into steaks and the loin into chops will make a fine fricassee, or cutlets
