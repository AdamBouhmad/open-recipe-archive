## Ingredients

- Boiled Onions
- buttered baking-dish
- White Sauce I
- buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut Boiled Onions in quarters.
2. Put in a buttered baking-dish, cover with White Sauce I, sprinkle with buttered cracker crumbs, and place on centre grate in oven to brown crumbs.
