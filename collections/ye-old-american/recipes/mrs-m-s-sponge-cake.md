## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- The weight of the eggs in sugar
- Half their weight in flour
- 1 lemon, juice and rind

## Directions

1. Beat yolks and whites very light.
2. Add the sugar into the yolks when they are smooth and stiff.
3. Add the juice and grated peel of the lemon.
4. Add the beaten whites.
5. Lastly, add the flour, very lightly.
6. Bake in tins, covering the cake with paper to prevent burning.
7. Line the pans in which sponge-cake is baked with buttered paper, fitted neatly to the sides and bottom.
