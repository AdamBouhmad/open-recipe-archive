## Ingredients

- French Cream
- walnut meats
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Take a piece of French Cream, put it into a cup and setting the cup into a vessel of boiling water, heating it until it turns like thick cream
2. Drop the walnut meats into it, one at a time, taking them out on the end of a fork and placing on buttered paper
3. Continue to dip them until all are used, then go over again, giving them a second coat of candy
4. They look nice colored pink and flavored with vanilla
