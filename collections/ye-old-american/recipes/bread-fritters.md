## Ingredients

- bread
- white wine
- four eggs
- lard
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut your bread of a convenient size
2. pour on it some white wine, and let it stand a few minutes
3. drain it on a sieve
4. beat four eggs very light
5. add four spoonsful of wine, beat all well together
6. have your lard boiling
7. dip the bread in the egg, and fry it a light brown
8. sprinkle sugar on each, and glaze them
