## Ingredients

- boiled crabs
- pepper
- salt
- melted butter
- milk or cream
- bread or cracker crumbs
- eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Pick the meat of boiled crabs and chop it fine
2. Season to taste with pepper, salt and melted butter
3. Moisten it well with rich milk or cream
4. Stiffen it slightly with bread or cracker crumbs
5. Add two or three well-beaten eggs to bind the mixture
6. Form the croquettes
7. Egg and bread crumb them
8. Fry them delicately in boiling lard using a wire frying basket
