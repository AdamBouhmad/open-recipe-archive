## Ingredients

- 1 quart buttermilk, or "loppered" sweet milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- Flour to make good batter

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well and stir them into the milk, beating hard all the while
2. Add the flour and salt
3. At the last add the soda
4. Bake at once in a quick oven
