## Ingredients

- an ounce of oil of peppermint
- a pint of alcohol
- leaves of green mint

## Directions

1. Mix an ounce of oil of peppermint with a pint of alcohol
2. Colour it by putting in some leaves of green mint
3. Let it stand till the colour is a fine green; then filter it through blotting paper
4. Drop it on sugar when you take it
