## Ingredients

- rabbits
- bread
- parsley
- sweet marjoram
- thyme
- nutmeg
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- cream
- eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Case the rabbits, skewer their heads with their mouths upon their backs, stick their fore-legs into their ribs, skewer the hind-legs doubled
2. Make a pudding for them of the crumb of half a loaf of bread, a little parsley, sweet marjoram and thyme, all shred fine, nutmeg, salt and pepper to your taste
3. Mix them up into a light stuffing, with a quarter of a pound of butter, a little good cream, and two eggs
4. Put it into the body, and sew them up
5. Dredge and baste them well with lard
6. Roast them near an hour
7. Serve them up with parsley and butter for sauce, chop the livers, and lay them in lumps round the edge of the dish
