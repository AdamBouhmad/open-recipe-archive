## Ingredients

- 10 lbs. grease
- 6 lbs. soda (washing)
- 8 gallons hot water

## Directions

1. Let it stand for several days until the grease is eaten up
2. If too thick, add more water
3. Stir every day
4. If wood-ashes are used instead of soda, boil the mixture
