## Ingredients

- rabbit
- four hard-boiled eggs
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Make the pie the same as Game Pie
2. Scatter through it four hard-boiled eggs cut in slices
3. Cover with puff paste and cut a slit in the middle
4. Bake one hour, laying paper over the top should it brown too fast
