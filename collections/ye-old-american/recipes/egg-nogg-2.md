## Ingredients

- 6 eggs—whites and yolks beaten separately and very stiff
- 1 quart rich milk
- ½ cup of sugar
- ½ pint best brandy
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stir the yolks into the milk with the sugar, which should first be beaten with the yolks
2. Next add the brandy
3. Lastly whip in the whites of three eggs
4. Flavor with nutmeg
