## Ingredients

- 1 handful moss, washed in five waters, and soaked an hour
- 1 quart boiling milk
- vanilla or rose-water

## Directions

1. Soak the washed moss in a very little cold water.
2. Stir into the boiling milk, and simmer until it is dissolved.
3. Sweeten and flavor with vanilla or rose-water.
4. Strain into moulds.
