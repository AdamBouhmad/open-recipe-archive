## Ingredients

- 2 oranges
- Batter II, III, IV, or V

## Directions

1. Peel oranges and separate into sections.
2. Make an opening in each section just large enough to admit of passage for seeds, which should be removed.
3. Dip sections in batter, and fry and serve same as other fritters.
