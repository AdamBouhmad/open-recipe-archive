## Ingredients

- three cupfuls of white sugar
- half a cupful of water
- half a cupful of vinegar
- half a teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- a tablespoonful of butter
- eight drops of extract of lemon
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Boil without stirring till it will snap and break
2. Just before taking from the fire, add a quarter of a teaspoonful of soda
3. Pour into well-buttered biscuit tins, a quarter of an inch thick
4. Mark off into inch squares when partly cold
