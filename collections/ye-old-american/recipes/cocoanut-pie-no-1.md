## Ingredients

- one-half cup desiccated cocoanut
- one cupful of milk
- two eggs
- one small cupful of sugar
- butter the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Soak one-half cup desiccated cocoanut in one cupful of milk
2. Mix with two eggs, one small cupful of sugar, and butter the size of an egg
3. This is for one small-sized pie
4. Nice with a meringue on top
