## Ingredients

- 2 ozs. sweetened chocolate
- 4 cups milk
- Few grains salt
- Whipped cream

## Directions

1. Scald milk, add chocolate, and stir until chocolate is melted.
2. Bring to boiling-point, mill, and serve in chocolate cups with whipped cream sweetened and flavored.
