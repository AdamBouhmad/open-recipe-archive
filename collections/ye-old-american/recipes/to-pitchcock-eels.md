## Ingredients

- eels
- pepper
- salt
- dried sage
- beef suet
- melted butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Skin and wash your eels
2. Dry them with a cloth
3. Sprinkle them with pepper, salt, and a little dried sage
4. Turn them backward and forward and skewer them
5. Rub a gridiron with beef suet
6. Broil them a nice brown
7. Put them on a dish with good melted butter
8. Lay around fried parsley
