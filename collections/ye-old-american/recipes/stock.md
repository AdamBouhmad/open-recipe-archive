## Ingredients

- Six pounds of shin of beef, or six pounds of knuckle of veal
- Any bones, trimmings of poultry, or fresh meat
- One-quarter pound of lean bacon or ham
- Two ounces of butter
- Two large onions
- Cloves
- One turnip
- Three carrots
- One head of celery
- Two ounces of salt
- One-half teaspoonful of whole pepper
- One large blade of mace
- One bunch of savory herbs except sage
- Four quarts and one-half-pint of cold water

## Directions

1. Cut up the meat and bacon, or ham, into pieces of about three inches square
2. Break the bones into small pieces
3. Rub the butter on the bottom of the stewpan
4. Put in one-half a pint of water, the broken bones, then meat and all other ingredients
5. Cover the stewpan, and place it on a sharp fire, occasionally stirring its contents
6. When the bottom of the pan becomes covered with a pale, jelly-like substance, add the four quarts of cold water, and simmer very gently for five or six hours
7. When nearly cooked, throw in a tablespoonful of salt to assist the scum to rise
8. Remove every particle of scum whilst it is doing, and strain it through a fine hair sieve
9. When cool remove all grease
