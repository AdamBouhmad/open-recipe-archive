## Ingredients

- Half a raw chicken
- about a quart of cold water
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Pound the raw chicken with a mallet, bones and meat together
2. Heat slowly in a covered vessel with the cold water
3. Let it simmer until the meat is in white rags and the liquid reduced one half
4. Strain and press, first through a cullender, then through a coarse cloth
5. Salt to taste, and pepper if desired
6. Return to the fire, and simmer five minutes longer
7. Skim when cool
8. Give to the patient cold—just from the ice—with unleavened wafers
9. Keep on the ice
10. Can make into sandwiches by putting the jelly between thin slices of bread spread lightly with butter
