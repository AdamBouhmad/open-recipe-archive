## Ingredients

- one and one-half tablespoons butter
- three tablespoons flour
- one-half cup highly seasoned Brown Stock
- one-fourth cup chopped canned mushrooms
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Brown one and one-half tablespoons butter, add three tablespoons flour, and stir until well browned.
2. Add one-half cup highly seasoned Brown Stock.
3. Add one-fourth cup chopped canned mushrooms, and season with salt and pepper.
