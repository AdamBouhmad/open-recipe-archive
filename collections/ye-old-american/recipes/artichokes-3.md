## Ingredients

- artichokes
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Strip off the outer leaves, and cut the stalks close to the bottom.
2. Wash well and lay in cold water two hours.
3. Immerse in boiling water, the stalk-ends uppermost, with an inverted plate upon them to keep them down.
4. Boil an hour and a half, or until very tender.
5. Arrange in circles upon a dish, the tops up, and pour drawn butter over them.
