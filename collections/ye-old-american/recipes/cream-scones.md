## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking power
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 eggs
- ⅓ cup cream
- white of egg

## Directions

1. Mix and sift together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
2. Rub in butter with tips of fingers; add eggs well beaten, and cream.
3. Toss on a floured board, pat, and roll to three-fourths inch in thickness.
4. Cut in squares, brush with white of egg, sprinkle with sugar, and bake in a hot oven fifteen minutes.
