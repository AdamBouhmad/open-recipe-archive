## Ingredients

- one-quarter cup of butter
- one-half cupful of sugar
- two cupfuls of jam
- six cupfuls of soft bread crumbs
- four eggs

## Directions

1. Rub the butter and sugar together
2. Beat the eggs, yolks and whites separately
3. Mash the raspberries, add the whites beaten to a stiff froth
4. Stir all together to a smooth paste
5. Butter a pudding dish, cover the bottom with a layer of the crumbs, then a layer of the mixture
6. Continue the alternate layers until the dish is full, making the last layer of crumbs
7. Bake one hour in a moderate oven
8. Serve in the dish in which it is baked and serve with fruit sauce made with raspberries
