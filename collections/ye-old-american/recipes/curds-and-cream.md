## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- cream
- sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Turn one quart of milk as for the slip
2. Let it stand until just before it is to be served
3. Then take it up with a skimming dish, and lay it on a sieve
4. When the whey has drained off, put the curds in a dish, and surround them with cream
5. Use sugar and nutmeg
