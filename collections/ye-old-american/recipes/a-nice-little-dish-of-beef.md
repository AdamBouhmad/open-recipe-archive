## Ingredients

- cold roast beef, fat and lean
- chopped onion
- pepper
- salt
- good gravy
- scollop shells
- potatos
- cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Mince cold roast beef, fat and lean, very fine
2. Add chopped onion, pepper, salt, and a little good gravy
3. Fill scollop shells two parts full
4. Fill them up with potatoes mashed smooth with cream
5. Put a bit of butter on the top
6. And set them in an oven to brown
