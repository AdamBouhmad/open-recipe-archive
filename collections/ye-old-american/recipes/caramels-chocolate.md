## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 tablespoonful (heaping) of butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls flour
- half a pound of grated chocolate
- half a cup of sweet milk
- a teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil together 2 cups brown sugar, 1 cup molasses, 1 tablespoonful (heaping) of butter, and 3 tablespoonfuls flour for twenty-five minutes.
2. Stir in half a pound of grated chocolate wet in half a cup of sweet milk, and boil until it hardens on the spoon, with which you must stir it frequently.
3. Flavor with a teaspoonful of vanilla.
