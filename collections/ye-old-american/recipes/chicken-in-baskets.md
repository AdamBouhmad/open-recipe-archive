## Ingredients

- Three cups hot mashed potatoes
- Three tablespoons butter
- One teaspoon salt
- Yolks of three eggs, slightly beaten
- Milk
- White of egg, slightly beaten
- Creamed Chicken
- Parsley

## Directions

1. To mashed potatoes add butter, salt, yolks of eggs, and enough milk to moisten.
2. Shape in form of small baskets, using a pastry bag and tube.
3. Brush over with white of egg slightly beaten, and brown in oven.
4. Fill with Creamed Chicken.
5. Form handles for baskets of parsley.
