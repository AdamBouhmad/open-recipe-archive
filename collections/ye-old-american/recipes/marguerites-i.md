## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ cup flour
- ¼ teaspoon baking powder
- ⅓ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup pecan nut meats, cut in small pieces

## Directions

1. Beat eggs slightly, and add remaining ingredients in the order given.
2. Fill small buttered tins two-thirds full of mixture, and place pecan nut meat on each.
3. Bake in a moderate oven fifteen minutes.
