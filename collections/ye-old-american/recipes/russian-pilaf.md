## Ingredients

- ½ cup rice
- 1 cup highly seasoned chicken stock
- ¾ cup stewed and strained tomato
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Wash one-half cup rice.
2. Mix one cup highly seasoned chicken stock with three-fourths cup stewed and strained tomato, and heat to boiling-point.
3. Add rice, and steam until rice is soft.
4. Add two tablespoons butter, stirring lightly with a fork that kernels may not be broken, and season with salt.
