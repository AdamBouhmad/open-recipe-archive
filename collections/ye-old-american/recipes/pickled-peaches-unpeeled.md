## Ingredients

- peaches
- water
- For every 7 lbs. of fruit: 3 lbs. of sugar
- 3 pints of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful allspice
- 1 tablespoonful mace
- 1 tablespoonful cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful celery-seed
- 1 teaspoonful cloves

## Directions

1. Rub the fur off with a coarse cloth, and prick each peach with a fork.
2. Heat in just enough water to cover them until they almost boil; take them out.
3. Add to the water sugar in the following proportions: for every 7 lbs. of fruit, 3 lbs. of sugar.
4. Boil fifteen minutes; skim, and add 3 pints of vinegar.
5. Add 1 tablespoonful (each) of allspice, mace, and cinnamon.
6. Add 1 teaspoonful celery-seed and 1 teaspoonful cloves.
7. Put the spices in thin muslin bags.
8. Boil all together ten minutes, then put in the fruit, and boil until they can be pierced with a straw.
9. Take out the fruit with a skimmer, and spread upon dishes to cool.
10. Boil the syrup until thick.
11. Pack the peaches in glass jars, and pour the syrup over them scalding hot.
