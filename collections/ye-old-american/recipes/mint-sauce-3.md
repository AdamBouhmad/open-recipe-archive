## Ingredients

- fresh, young spearmint leaves
- finely powdered cut-loaf sugar
- three tablespoonfuls of mint
- two tablespoonfuls of finely powdered cut-loaf sugar
- six tablespoonfuls good cider or white-wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Strip spearmint leaves from stems
2. Wash and drain them or dry on a cloth
3. Chop very fine
4. Put in a gravy tureen
5. Add two tablespoonfuls of finely powdered cut-loaf sugar to three tablespoonfuls of mint
6. Mix and let it stand a few minutes
7. Pour over it six tablespoonfuls good cider or white-wine vinegar
8. Make some time before dinner so that the flavor of the mint may be well extracted
