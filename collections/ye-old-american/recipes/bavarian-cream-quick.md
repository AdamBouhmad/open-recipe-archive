## Ingredients

- ½ lemon, grated rind and juice
- ½ cup white wine
- ⅓ cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon granulated gelatine
- 1 tablespoon cold water

## Directions

1. Mix lemon, wine, sugar, and yolks of eggs
2. Stir vigorously over fire until mixture thickens
3. Add gelatine soaked in water
4. Pour over whites of eggs beaten stiff
5. Set in pan of ice-water and beat until thick enough to hold its shape
6. Turn into a mould lined with lady fingers, and chill
