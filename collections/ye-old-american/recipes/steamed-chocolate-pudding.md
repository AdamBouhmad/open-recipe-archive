## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- ⅔ cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 2¼ cups flour
- 4½ teaspoons baking powder
- 2½ squares Baker's chocolate
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, and egg well beaten
2. Mix and sift flour with baking powder and salt
3. Add alternately with milk to first mixture
4. Add chocolate, melted
5. Turn into a buttered mould
6. Cover and steam two hours
7. Serve with Cream Sauce
