## Ingredients

- one large chicken
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- loaf-sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken up into very small pieces and bruise the bones
2. Put the whole into a stone jar with a cover that will make it water tight
3. Set the jar in a large kettle of boiling water and keep it boiling for three hours
4. Strain off the liquid and season it slightly with salt, pepper, and mace; or with loaf-sugar and lemon juice, according to the taste of the person for whom it is intended
5. Return the fragments of the chicken to the jar and set it again in a kettle of boiling water
6. Collect the jelly from the second boiling
