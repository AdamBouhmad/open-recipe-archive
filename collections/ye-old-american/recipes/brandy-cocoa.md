## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons cocoa
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1½ cups boiling water
- 4 cups milk
- 3 teaspoons cooking brandy

## Directions

1. Prepare as Reception Cocoa, and add brandy before milling.
