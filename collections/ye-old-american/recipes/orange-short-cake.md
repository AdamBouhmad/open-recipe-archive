## Ingredients

- two large oranges
- half a peeled lemon
- one cup of sugar
- short-cake

## Directions

1. Peel two large oranges
2. Chop them fine
3. Remove the seeds
4. Add half a peeled lemon and one cup of sugar
5. Spread between the layers of short-cake while it is hot
