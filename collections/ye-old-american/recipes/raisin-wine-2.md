## Ingredients

- two pounds of raisins
- a lemon
- a pound of white sugar
- about two gallons of boiling water

## Directions

1. Seed and chop the raisins
2. Pour into a stone jar and stir daily for six or eight days
3. Strain and bottle
4. Put in a cool place for ten days or so, when the wine will be ready for use
