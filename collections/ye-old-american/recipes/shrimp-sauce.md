## Ingredients

- half a pint of shrimps
- a spoonful of anchovy liquor
- a pound of thick melted butter
- half a lemon

## Directions

1. Wash half a pint of shrimps very clean
2. Mince and put them in a stew-pan with a spoonful of anchovy liquor and a pound of thick melted butter
3. Boil it up for five minutes
4. Squeeze in half a lemon
5. Toss it up and put it in a sauce-boat
