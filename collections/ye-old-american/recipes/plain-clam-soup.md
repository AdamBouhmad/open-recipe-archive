## Ingredients

- hundred clams
- quart of water or milk
- butter
- flour
- small bunch of sweet marjoram
- large table-spoonful of whole pepper
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Wash clams well and put them into a large pot of boiling water
2. As they open take them out and extract the clams, taking care to save the liquor
3. Mix with the liquor a quart of water, or a quart of milk
4. Thicken it with butter rolled in flour
5. Add a small bunch of sweet marjoram and a large table-spoonful of whole pepper
6. Put the liquid into a pot over a moderate fire
7. Make some little round dumplings about the size of a hickory nut of flour and butter, and put them into the soup
8. When it comes to a boil, put in the clams, and keep them boiling an hour
9. Take them out before you send the soup to table
10. When the soup is done, take out the sweet marjoram
11. Have ready some toasted bread cut into small squares or dice and put it into the soup before serving
