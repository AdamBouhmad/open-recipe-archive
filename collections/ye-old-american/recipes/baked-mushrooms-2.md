## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- chopped parsley
- butter
- cream
- cayenne pepper
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare them the same as for stewing
2. Place them in a baking-pan in a moderate oven
3. Season with salt, pepper, lemon juice and chopped parsley
4. Cook in the oven fifteen minutes
5. Baste with butter
6. Arrange on a dish and pour the gravy over them
7. Make sauce by heating a cup of cream, two ounces of butter, a tablespoonful of chopped parsley, a little cayenne pepper, salt, a tablespoonful of white sauce and two tablespoonfuls of lemon juice
8. Put in a saucepan and set on the fire
9. Stir until thick, but do not let boil
10. Serve with sauce
