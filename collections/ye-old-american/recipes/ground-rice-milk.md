## Ingredients

- two table-spoonfuls of ground rice
- milk
- sugar
- lemon-peel
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix in a bowl two table-spoonfuls of ground rice with sufficient milk to make a thin batter
2. Stir it gradually into a pint of milk and boil it with sugar, lemon-peel or nutmeg
