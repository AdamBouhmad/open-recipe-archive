## Ingredients

- six kidneys
- salt and pepper
- two tablespoons butter
- flour
- two-thirds cup boiling water or hot Brown Stock
- lemon juice, onion juice, or Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Soak, pare, and cut kidneys in slices.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Melt two tablespoons butter in hot frying-pan.
4. Put in kidneys and cook five minutes.
5. Dredge thoroughly with flour.
6. Add two-thirds cup boiling water or hot Brown Stock.
7. Cook five minutes and add more salt and pepper if needed.
8. Lemon juice, onion juice, or Madeira wine may be used for additional flavor.
