## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup thick cream
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1½ tablespoons caraway seeds

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for Cocoanut Cream Cookies, using one and one-half tablespoons caraway seeds in place of cocoanut.
2. Chill thoroughly, toss on a floured board, pat, and roll one-half inch thick.
3. Sprinkle with caraway seeds, roll one-fourth inch thick, and shape with a small round cutter, first dipped in flour.
4. Bake on a buttered sheet in a moderate oven.
