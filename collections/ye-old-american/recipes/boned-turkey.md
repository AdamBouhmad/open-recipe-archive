## Ingredients

- turkey
- stuffing
- fat bacon
- salt
- water
- butter
- port wine
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the fowl as usual
2. With a sharp and pointed knife begin at the extremity of the wing and pass the knife down close to the bone, cutting all the flesh from the bone and preserving the skin whole
3. Run the knife down each side of the breast bone and up the legs, keeping close to the bone
4. Split the back half way up and draw out the bones
5. Fill the places whence the bones were taken with a stuffing, restoring the fowl to its natural form
6. Sew up all the incisions made in the skin
7. Lard with two or three rows of slips of fat bacon on the top
8. Baste often with salt and water and a little butter
9. Carve across in slices and serve with tomato sauce
