## Ingredients

- cold, cooked salmon
- a pint of salmon dice
- half a pint of cream
- cayenne pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut cold, cooked salmon into dice
2. Heat about a pint of the dice in half a pint of cream
3. Season to taste with cayenne pepper and salt
4. Fill the shells and serve
