## Ingredients

- one half pound of maccaroni
- salt
- five yelks of eggs
- two whites of eggs
- one half pint of cream
- white meat
- boiled ham
- grated cheese
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- rich gravy

## Directions

1. Simmer half a pound of maccaroni in a plenty of water, with a table-spoonful of salt, till tender, but not broke
2. strain it
3. beat five yelks, two whites of eggs, half a pint of cream together
4. mince white meat and boiled ham very fine
5. add three spoonsful of grated cheese, pepper and salt
6. mix these with the maccaroni
7. butter the mould, put it in
8. steam it in a pan of boiling water for an hour
9. serve with rich gravy
