## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- salt and water
- bread-crumbs
- fat salt pork
- shallot
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- sweet marjoram
- thyme
- sage
- butter
- hot water
- raw eggs
- strips of salt pork

## Directions

1. Soak the liver in salt and water an hour to draw out the blood
2. Wipe perfectly dry
3. Make a force-meat of bread-crumbs, two slices of fat salt pork chopped small, a shallot, pepper, salt, nutmeg, sweet marjoram, thyme, and if desired, a little sage
4. Moisten the force-meat with butter melted in a very little hot water and two raw eggs, well beaten
5. Make an incision with a narrow sharp knife and enlarge the cavity inside by moving the point dexterously to and fro
6. Stuff the cavity full of the force-meat
7. Sew or skewer up the outer orifice
8. Lard with strips of salt pork
9. Roast for an hour, basting twice with butter and water, afterward with the gravy in the dripping-pan
10. Pour the gravy over the liver when done
