## Ingredients

- two quarts of superfine flour
- one pound of fresh butter
- cold water

## Directions

1. Sift two quarts of superfine flour into a pan.
2. Divide one pound of fresh butter into two equal parts, and cut up one half in the flour, rubbing it fine.
3. Mix it with a very little cold water, and make it into a round lump.
4. Knead it a little.
5. Flour your paste-board, and roll the dough out into a large thin sheet.
6. Spread it all over with the remainder of the butter.
7. Flour it, fold it up, and roll it out again.
8. Then fold it again, or roll it into a scroll.
9. Cut it into as many pieces as you want sheets of paste, and roll each not quite an inch thick.
10. Butter your pie-dish.
