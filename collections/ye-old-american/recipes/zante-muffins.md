## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ¾ cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1½ cups milk
- 2 cups corn meal
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 5 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ cup currants

## Directions

1. Cream the butter; add sugar, gradually, eggs well beaten, and milk; then add dry ingredients mixed and sifted, and currants.
2. Bake in buttered individual tins.
