## Ingredients

- partridges or small birds
- bacon
- bread
- butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Lard them with slips of bacon, put them on a skewer, tie it to the spit at both ends, dredge and baste them
2. Let them roast ten minutes
3. Take the grated crumb of half a loaf of bread, with a piece of butter, the size of a walnut, put it in a stew pan, and shake it over a gentle fire till it is of a light brown
4. Lay it between your birds, and pour over them a little melted butter
