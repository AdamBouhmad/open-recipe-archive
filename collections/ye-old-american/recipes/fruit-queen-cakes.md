## Ingredients

- fourteen ounces of the finest flour
- a table-spoonful of beaten cinnamon
- a tea-spoonful of mace
- two beaten nutmegs
- half a glass of white wine
- half a glass of brandy
- half a glass of rose water
- a pound of loaf-sugar
- a pound of fresh butter
- ten eggs
- a pound of currants
- juice and grated peel of two large lemons

## Directions

1. Make them in the manner described for Queen Cake, with the addition of a pound of currants, (picked, washed, dried, and floured,) and the juice and grated peel of two large lemons, stirred in gradually at the last.
2. Instead of currants, you may put in sultana or seedless raisins, cut in half and floured.
3. You may substitute oranges for lemons.
