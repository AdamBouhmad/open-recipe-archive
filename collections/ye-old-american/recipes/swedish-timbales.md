## Ingredients

- ¾ cup flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients, add milk gradually, and beaten egg; then add olive oil.
2. Shape, using a hot timbale iron, fry in deep fat until crisp and brown.
3. Take from iron and invert on brown paper to drain.
