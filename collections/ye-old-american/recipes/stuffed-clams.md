## Ingredients

- Rock salt
- 2 quarts large-sized soft-shelled clams
- Clam liquor
- Cream
- Lemon juice
- Cayenne
- Grated cheese
- Buttered soft stale bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cover bottom of dripping-pan with rock salt
2. Arrange clams on salt, in such a manner that liquor will not run into pan as clam-shells open
3. As soon as shells begin to open, remove clams from shells, and chop
4. Reserve liquor, strain, and use in making a thick sauce following directions for thick White Sauce for Croquettes, making one-half rule, and using one-fourth cup each clam liquor and cream
5. Season highly with lemon juice and cayenne
6. Moisten clams with sauce, fill shells, sprinkle with grated cheese, cover with buttered soft stale bread crumbs, and bake in a hot oven until crumbs are brown
