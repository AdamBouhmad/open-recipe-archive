## Ingredients

- butter
- one cupful of chopped citron
- raisins
- currants
- stale sponge cake
- cinnamon
- cloves
- nutmeg
- one quart of milk
- four eggs
- a pinch of salt
- two tablespoonfuls of melted butter

## Directions

1. Butter well the inside of a pudding-mold
2. Sprinkle some of the chopped citron, raisins and currants on the bottom of the mold
3. Add slices of stale sponge cake
4. Shake over this cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg
5. Continue layering fruit and cake until the mold is nearly full
6. Make a custard of one quart of milk, four eggs, a pinch of salt, and two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
7. Pour this custard over the cake without cooking it
8. Let it stand and soak one hour
9. Steam one hour and a half
10. Serve with wine sauce or a custard seasoned with wine
