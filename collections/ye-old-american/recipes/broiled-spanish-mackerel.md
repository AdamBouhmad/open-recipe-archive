## Ingredients

- spanish mackerel
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Split the fish down the back, take out the backbone
2. Wash it in cold water, dry it with a clean, dry cloth
3. Sprinkle it lightly with salt and lay it on a buttered gridiron, over a clear fire, with the flesh side downward, until it begins to brown
4. Then turn the other side
