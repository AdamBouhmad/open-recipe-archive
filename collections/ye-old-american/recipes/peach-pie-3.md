## Ingredients

- peaches
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove skins from peaches. This may be done easily after allowing peaches to stand in boiling water one minute.
2. Cut in eighths, cook until soft with enough water to prevent burning; sweeten to taste.
3. Cool, and fill crust previously baked.
4. Cover with whipped cream, sweetened and flavored.
