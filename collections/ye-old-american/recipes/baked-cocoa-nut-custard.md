## Ingredients

- a pound of grated cocoa-nut
- half a pound of powdered white sugar
- milk of the cocoa-nut or a pint of cream
- two table-spoonfuls of rose water
- a pint of rich milk
- whites of eight eggs
- a tea-spoonful of powdered nutmeg and cinnamon
- loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Grate as much cocoa-nut as will weigh a pound
2. Mix half a pound of powdered white sugar with the milk of the cocoa-nut, or with a pint of cream; adding two table-spoonfuls of rose water
3. Stir in gradually a pint of rich milk
4. Beat to a stiff froth the whites of eight eggs, and stir them into the milk and sugar, a little at a time, alternately with the grated cocoa-nut
5. Add a tea-spoonful of powdered nutmeg and cinnamon
6. Put the mixture into cups, and bake them twenty minutes in a Dutch oven half filled with boiling water
7. When cold, grate loaf-sugar over them
