## Ingredients

- half a pound of white sugar
- one pint of fine sweet cider or of white wine
- one nutmeg
- three pints milk
- brandy (optional if using cider)

## Directions

1. Mix half a pound of white sugar with a pint of fine sweet cider or of white wine and grate in a nutmeg
2. Prepare them in a large bowl just before milking time
3. Have about three pints milked into it, stirring it occasionally with a spoon
4. Let it be eaten before the froth subsides
5. If you use cider, a little brandy will improve it
