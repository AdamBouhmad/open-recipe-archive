## Ingredients

- a pound of tripe
- a small cup of water or milk
- a bit of butter the size of an egg
- a large teaspoonful of flour
- pepper
- salt
- a bunch of parsley

## Directions

1. Cut a pound of tripe in narrow strips
2. Put a small cup of water or milk to it, add a bit of butter the size of an egg
3. Dredge in a large teaspoonful of flour, or work it with the butter
4. Season with pepper and salt
5. Let it simmer gently for half an hour
6. Serve hot with a bunch of parsley cut small as an improvement
