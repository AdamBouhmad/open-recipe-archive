## Ingredients

- lobster meat
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- crumbs
- egg
- Sauce Tartare

## Directions

1. Remove lobster meat from shell.
2. Use tail meat, divided in fourths, and large pieces of claw meat.
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
4. Dip in crumbs, egg, and again in crumbs.
5. Fry in deep fat, drain, and serve with Sauce Tartare.
