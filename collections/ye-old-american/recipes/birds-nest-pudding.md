## Ingredients

- 8 apples
- sugar
- grated nutmeg
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 2 teacupfuls flour
- 3 even teaspoonfuls baking powder
- 1 pint milk
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- whites of 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Core and peel apples and put in dish
2. Fill cored places with sugar and grated nutmeg
3. Cover and bake
4. Beat egg yolks light and add flour sifted with baking powder
5. Add milk with salt, then add well beaten egg whites
6. Pour over apples and bake one hour in moderate oven
7. Serve with sauce
