## Ingredients

- fresh green leaves of burnet
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Nearly fill a wide-mouthed bottle with the fresh green leaves of burnet, cover them with vinegar, and let them steep two weeks.
2. Then strain off the vinegar, wash the bottle, put in a fresh supply of burnet leaves, pour the same vinegar over them, and let it infuse a fortnight longer.
3. Then strain it again and it will be fit for use.
