## Ingredients

- 1 sweetbread, parboiled ten minutes
- ⅓ cup breast meat of a raw chicken
- 1 white of egg
- ¾ cup heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon corn-starch
- ¼ cup White Stock
- ⅓ cup parboiled sweetbread cut in cubes
- 1 tablespoon Sherry wine
- Salt and pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup rich chicken stock
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon Sherry wine
- ¼ teaspoon beef extract

## Directions

1. Parboil a sweetbread ten minutes, chop, and rub through sieve; there should be one-half cup.
2. Mix with one-third cup breast meat of a raw chicken, and rub through sieve.
3. Pound in mortar, add gradually white of one egg, and work until smooth, then add three-fourths cup heavy cream.
4. Line buttered timbale moulds with mixture, fill centres with filling, cover with mixture, place in a pan of hot water, cover with buttered paper and bake until firm.
5. Remove to serving dish, and pour around sauce.
6. For filling: Melt one tablespoon butter, add one tablespoon corn-starch, and pour on gradually one-fourth cup White Stock; then add one-third cup parboiled sweetbread cut in cubes, one tablespoon Sherry wine, and salt and pepper to taste.
7. For sauce: Melt three tablespoons butter, add three tablespoons flour, and pour on one cup rich chicken stock and one-half cup heavy cream. Season with one tablespoon Sherry wine, one-fourth teaspoon beef extract, and salt and pepper to taste.
