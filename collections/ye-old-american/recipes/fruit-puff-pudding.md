## Ingredients

- one pint of flour
- two teaspoonfuls baking powder
- salt
- milk
- berries or steamed apples
- two eggs
- half a cup butter
- a cup of sugar
- a cup of boiling milk
- one cup of strawberries

## Directions

1. Stir two teaspoonfuls baking powder and a little salt into one pint of flour
2. Sift and stir the mixture into milk, until very soft
3. Place well-greased cups in a steamer
4. Put in each a spoonful of the batter, then add one of berries or steamed apples
5. Cover with another spoonful of batter
6. Steam twenty minutes
7. For sauce: beat two eggs, half a cup butter, a cup of sugar thoroughly with a cup of boiling milk and one cup of strawberries
