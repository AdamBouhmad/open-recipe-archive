## Ingredients

- loaf-sugar
- water
- three quarts of ripe strawberries

## Directions

1. To each pound of loaf-sugar allow half a pint of water
2. You may allow to each pint of water half the white of an egg beaten into the water
3. Break up the sugar and put it into a preserving-kettle and pour on it the water in the above proportion
4. When the sugar has melted and been well stirred in the water, put the kettle over the fire and boil and skim it till the scum ceases to rise
5. Put the strawberries into a linen bag and squeeze the liquid into a deep pan
6. As soon as you take the kettle of syrup from the fire, stir into it the strawberry juice
7. Put it into a freezer surrounded with ice broken small and mixed with salt
8. Twirl it round by the handles for ten minutes and then let it freeze, frequently stirring it hard
9. When done, turn it out into a glass bowl and serve it on saucers
10. Or you may give it a second freezing in a pyramid mould
