## Ingredients

- finnan haddie
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Broil in a greased broiler until brown on both sides.
2. Remove to a pan, and cover with hot water; let stand ten minutes, drain, and place on a platter.
3. Spread with butter, and sprinkle with pepper.
