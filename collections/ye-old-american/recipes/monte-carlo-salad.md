## Ingredients

- four large grape fruits
- finely cut celery
- apple
- Mayonnaise
- lettuce leaves
- green Mayonnaise
- canned pimentoes
- truffles
- cold cooked carrot
- cold cooked turnip

## Directions

1. Remove pulp from four large grape fruits, and drain.
2. Add an equal quantity of finely cut celery, and apple cut in small pieces.
3. Moisten with Mayonnaise, pile on a shallow salad dish, arrange around a border of lettuce leaves, and mask with Mayonnaise.
4. Outline, using green Mayonnaise, four oblongs to represent playing cards, and denote spots on cards by canned pimentoes or truffles; pimentoes cut in shapes of hearts and diamonds, truffles cut in shapes of spades and clubs.
5. Garnish with cold cooked carrot and turnip, shaped with a small round cutter to suggest gold and silver coin.
