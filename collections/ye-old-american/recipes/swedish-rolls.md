## Ingredients

- Recipe for Salad Rolls
- Butter
- Two tablespoons sugar
- One-third teaspoon cinnamon
- One-third cup stoned raisins finely chopped
- Two tablespoons chopped citron
- White of egg slightly beaten, diluted with one-half tablespoon water

## Directions

1. Use recipe for Salad Rolls
2. Roll to one-fourth inch thickness
3. Spread with butter and sprinkle with two tablespoons sugar mixed with one-third teaspoon cinnamon, one-third cup stoned raisins finely chopped, and two tablespoons chopped citron
4. Roll up like jelly roll and cut in three-fourths inch pieces
5. Place pieces in pan close together, flat side down
6. Let rise again
7. Bake in a hot oven
8. When rolls are taken from oven, brush over with white of egg slightly beaten, diluted with one-half tablespoon water
9. Return to oven to dry egg and glaze top
