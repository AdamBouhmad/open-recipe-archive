## Ingredients

- One egg well beaten
- One tablespoonful of butter
- One tablespoonful of sugar
- One teaspoonful of salt
- One cup of milk
- Three cups of sifted flour
- Three teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat together the egg, butter, sugar and salt until very light
2. Add the milk, flour and baking powder
3. Drop on well-greased patty-pans
4. Bake twenty minutes in a rather quick oven, or bake on a griddle in muffin-rings
