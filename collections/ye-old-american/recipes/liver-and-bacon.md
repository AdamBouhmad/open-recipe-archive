## Ingredients

- liver
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- bacon fat
- bacon

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Broiled Liver, cut in pieces for serving, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dredge with flour, and fry in bacon fat.
2. Serve with bacon.
