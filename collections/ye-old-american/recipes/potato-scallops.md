## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Boil and mash the potatoes soft with a little milk
2. Beat up light with melted butter—a dessertspoonful for every half-pint of the potato—salt and pepper to taste
3. Fill some patty-pans or buttered scallop shells with the mixture
4. Stamp a pattern on the top of each
5. Brown in an oven
6. Glaze, while hot, with butter, and serve in the shells
7. If you like, you can strew some grated cheese over the top
