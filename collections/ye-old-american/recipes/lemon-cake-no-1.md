## Ingredients

- 1 lb. sugar
- 12 eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately
- ¾ lb. flour
- Juice and rind of a lemon

## Directions

1. Baked in small square tins, and iced on sides and top with icing flavored with lemon juice and rind, these are sometimes called biscuits glacés.
