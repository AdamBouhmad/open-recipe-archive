## Ingredients

- five eggs
- five tablespoonfuls of sugar
- one quart of scalded milk
- flavoring
- bright jelly

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of five eggs and the whites of two very light
2. Sweeten with five tablespoonfuls of sugar and flavor to taste
3. Stir them into a quart of scalded milk and cook it until it thickens
4. When cool pour it into a glass dish
5. Whip the whites of the three remaining eggs to a stiff froth, adding three tablespoonfuls of sugar and a little flavoring
6. Pour this froth over a shallow dish of boiling water
7. When sufficiently cooked, take a tablespoon and drop spoonfuls of this over the top of the custard
8. Drop a teaspoonful of bright jelly on the top or centre of each island
