## Ingredients

- Slices of stale bread cut in rounds or stale cake
- Hot lard
- Boiling milk
- Powdered sugar or preserves
- Sweet wine sauce

## Directions

1. Fry the bread slices in hot lard like crullers to a light brown
2. Dip each slice when fried in boiling milk to remove the grease
3. Drain quickly
4. Dust with powdered sugar or spread with preserves
5. Pile on a hot plate and serve
6. Pour sweet wine sauce over them
