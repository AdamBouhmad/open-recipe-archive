## Ingredients

- butter
- salt
- cayenne
- button onion
- giblets
- necks
- fat pork
- water
- buttered toast
- flour
- lemon juice
- wine

## Directions

1. Clean and half-roast the birds; cut in quarters, and put in a saucepan with gravy made of the giblets, necks, and some bits of fat pork, stewed in a little water.
2. Add a minced button onion, salt, and a pinch of cayenne, and stew fifteen minutes or until tender, closely covered.
3. Take out the birds, and pile neatly upon buttered toast in a chafing-dish.
4. Strain the gravy and return to the fire, adding some small pieces of butter rolled in flour, the juice of a lemon and a little wine.
5. Boil up, and pour over the salmi.
