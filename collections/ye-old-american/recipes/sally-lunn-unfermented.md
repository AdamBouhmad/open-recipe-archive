## Ingredients

- butter as large as an egg
- one quart of flour
- one tumbler of milk
- two eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar
- three tablespoonfuls of baking powder
- one teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Rub a piece of butter as large as an egg into a quart of flour
2. Add a tumbler of milk, two eggs, three tablespoonfuls of sugar, three tablespoonfuls of baking powder and a teaspoonful of salt
3. Scatter the baking powder, salt and sugar into the flour
4. Add the eggs, the butter melted, and the milk
5. Stir all together and bake in well-greased round pans
6. Eat warm with butter
