## Ingredients

- 6 fresh lemons
- 1 quart and a half milk
- 1 and a half quarts French brandy
- 3 pounds powdered loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Cut six fresh lemons in thin slices
2. Put them into a quart and a half of milk
3. Boil it until the whey is very clear
4. Pass it through a sieve
5. Put to this whey, one and a half quarts of French brandy, and three pounds of powdered loaf sugar
6. Stir it till the sugar is dissolved
7. Let it stand to refine, and bottle it
8. Pare some of the yellow rind of the lemons very thin, and put a little in each bottle
