## Ingredients

- ¼ box gelatine or 1 tablespoon granulated gelatine
- ¼ cup cold water
- 1 cup boiling water
- ⅔ cup sugar
- ½ cup Sauterne
- ¼ cup orange juice
- 1½ tablespoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make same as other jellies, cool slightly, and beat until frothy and firm enough to mould.
2. Turn into mould and chill.
