## Ingredients

- wild ducks
- carrot or onion
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- giblets
- necks
- lean veal
- shallot
- sweet herbs
- cream or rich milk
- egg
- wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Parboil ten minutes, when you have drawn them, and put in a raw carrot or onion.
2. Lay in very cold water half an hour.
3. Cut into joints, pepper, salt, and flour them.
4. Have ready some butter in a frying-pan, and fry them a light brown.
5. Put them in a saucepan and cover with gravy made of the giblets, necks, and some bits of lean veal.
6. Add a minced shallot, a bunch of sweet herbs, salt, and pepper.
7. Cover closely and stew half an hour, or until tender.
8. Take out the duck, strain the gravy when you have skimmed it; put in a half-cup of cream or rich milk in which an egg has been beaten, thicken with browned flour, add a tablespoonful of wine and the juice of half a lemon, beaten in gradually not to curdle the cream; boil up and pour over the ducks.
