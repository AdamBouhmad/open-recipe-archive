## Ingredients

- stale bread, rolled fine
- fruit
- sugar
- milk
- custard ingredients

## Directions

1. Place layer of stale bread rolled fine in bottom of pudding-dish
2. Add layer of fruit and sprinkle with sugar
3. Alternate layers of bread crumbs and fruit until dish is full, top layer being crumbs
4. Make custard as for pies, add pint of milk and mix
5. Pour over pudding and bake until fruit is cooked
