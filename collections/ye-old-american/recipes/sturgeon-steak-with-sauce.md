## Ingredients

- sturgeon steaks
- butter
- boiling water
- browned flour
- cold water
- salt
- Worcestershire sauce or anchovy
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Skin the steaks carefully and lay in salted water (cold) for an hour, to remove the oily taste.
2. Then wipe each steak dry, salt, and broil over hot coals on a buttered gridiron.
3. Put a tablespoonful of butter into a frying-pan, and stir until it is brown—not burned.
4. Add a half-teacupful of boiling water in which has been stirred a tablespoonful of browned flour previously wet with cold water.
5. Add salt, a teaspoonful Worcestershire sauce or anchovy, the juice of a lemon, and let it boil up well.
6. Pour over the steaks when you have arranged them in the dish.
