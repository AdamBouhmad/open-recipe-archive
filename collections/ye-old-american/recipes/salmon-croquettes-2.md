## Ingredients

- 1¾ cups cold flaked salmon
- 1 cup Thick White Sauce
- Few grains cayenne
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- Salt

## Directions

1. Add sauce to salmon, then add seasonings.
2. Spread on a plate to cool.
3. Shape, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again, fry in deep fat, and drain.
