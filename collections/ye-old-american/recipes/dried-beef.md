## Ingredients

- dried or smoked beef
- boiling water
- butter
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Put the slices of uncooked beef into a frying-pan with just enough boiling water to cover them
2. Set them over the fire for ten minutes
3. Drain off all the water
4. With a knife and fork cut the meat into small bits
5. Return to the pan, which should be hot, with a tablespoonful of butter and a little pepper
6. Have ready some well-beaten eggs, allowing four to a half-pound of beef
7. Stir them into the pan with the minced meat
8. Toss and stir the mixture for about two minutes
9. Send to table in a covered dish
