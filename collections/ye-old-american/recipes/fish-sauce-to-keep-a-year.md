## Ingredients

- twenty-four anchovies
- two shallots
- handful of scraped horse radish
- four blades of mace
- one quart of white wine
- one pint of anchovy liquor
- one pint of claret
- twelve cloves
- twelve pepper corns

## Directions

1. Chop twenty-four anchovies, bones and all
2. Chop two shallots
3. Gather a handful of scraped horse radish
4. Combine with four blades of mace, one quart of white wine, one pint of anchovy liquor, one pint of claret, twelve cloves, and twelve pepper corns
5. Boil them together till reduced to a quart
6. Strain it off into a bottle for use
