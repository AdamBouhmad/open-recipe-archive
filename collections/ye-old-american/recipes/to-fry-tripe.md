## Ingredients

- tripe
- yolk of egg
- grated bread crumbs
- beef-dripping
- onion sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the tripe the day before, till it is quite tender, which it will not be in less than four or five hours.
2. Then cover it and set it away.
3. Next day cut it into long slips, and dip each piece into beaten yolk of egg, and afterwards roll them in grated bread crumbs.
4. Have ready in a frying-pan over the fire, some good beef-dripping.
5. When it is boiling hot put in the tripe, and fry it about ten minutes, till of a light brown.
6. You may serve it up with onion sauce.
