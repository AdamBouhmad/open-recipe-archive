## Ingredients

- rhubarb stalks
- water
- sugar
- freshly-baked shells

## Directions

1. Skin the stalks with care and cut into small pieces.
2. Put into a saucepan with very little water, and stew slowly until soft.
3. Sweeten while hot, but do not cook the sugar with the fruit.
4. Have ready some freshly-baked shells.
5. Fill up with the fruit and they are ready to serve.
