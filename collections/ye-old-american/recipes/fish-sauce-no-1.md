## Ingredients

- one pint of drawn butter
- one tablespoonful of pepper sauce or Worcestershire sauce
- salt
- six hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Make a pint of drawn butter
2. Add one tablespoonful of pepper sauce or Worcestershire sauce and a little salt
3. Chop six hard-boiled eggs fine and add them
4. Pour over boiled fish and garnish with sliced lemon
