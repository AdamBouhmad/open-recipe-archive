## Ingredients

- frogs' hind legs
- salt
- pepper
- crumbs
- egg
- deep fat

## Directions

1. Trim and clean
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again
4. Fry three minutes in deep fat
5. Drain
