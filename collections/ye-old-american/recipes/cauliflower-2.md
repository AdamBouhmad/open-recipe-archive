## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- flour or bread slice
- melted butter
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- vinegar

## Directions

1. When cleaned and washed, drop them into boiling water, into which you have put salt and a teaspoonful of flour, or a slice of bread
2. Boil till tender
3. Take off, drain and dish them
4. Serve with a sauce spread over and made with melted butter, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, chopped parsley and vinegar
