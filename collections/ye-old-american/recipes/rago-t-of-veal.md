## Ingredients

- two cups cold roast veal
- one and one-half cups Brown Sauce
- one teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
- onion juice
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Reheat two cups cold roast veal, cut in cubes, in one and one-half cups Brown Sauce seasoned with one teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce, few drops of onion juice, and a few grains of cayenne.
