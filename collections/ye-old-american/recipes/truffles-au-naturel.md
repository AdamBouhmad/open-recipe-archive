## Ingredients

- fine truffles
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Select some fine truffles
2. Cleanse them by washing them in several waters with a brush until not a particle of sand or grit remains on them
3. Wrap each truffle in buttered paper
4. Bake in a hot oven for quite an hour
5. Take off the paper
6. Wipe the truffles
7. Serve them in a hot napkin
