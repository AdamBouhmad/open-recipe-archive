## Ingredients

- Whites of 4 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoon granulated gelatine
- 3 tablespoons boiling water
- 1/4 cup powdered sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon lemon extract

## Directions

1. Beat whites of four eggs until stiff
2. Add one-half tablespoon granulated gelatine dissolved in three tablespoons boiling water
3. Beat until thoroughly mixed
4. Add one-fourth cup powdered sugar
5. Flavor with one-half teaspoon lemon extract
6. Pile lightly on dish
7. Serve with Boiled Custard
