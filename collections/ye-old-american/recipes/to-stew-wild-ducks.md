## Ingredients

- wild ducks
- salt
- pepper
- powdered cloves
- shallot
- butter
- vinegar
- water
- lemon peel
- sweet herbs
- bread

## Directions

1. Prepare the fowls, rub the insides with salt, pepper, and a little powdered cloves
2. Put a shallot or two with a lump of butter in the body of each
3. Lay them in a pan that will just hold them, putting butter under and over them, with vinegar and water, and add pepper, salt, lemon peel, and a bunch of sweet herbs
4. Cover the pan close, and let them stew till done
5. Pass the liquor through a sieve, pour it over the ducks, and serve them up hot, with a garnish of lemon sliced, and raspings of bread fried
