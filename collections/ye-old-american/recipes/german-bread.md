## Ingredients

- one pint of milk well boiled
- one teacupful of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of nice lard or butter
- two-thirds of a teacupful of baker's yeast
- flour

## Directions

1. Make a rising with the milk and yeast
2. when light, mix in the sugar and shortening with flour enough to make as soft a dough as can be handled
3. Flour the paste-board well and roll out about one-half inch thick
4. put this quantity into two large pans
5. make about a dozen indentures with the finger on the top
6. put a small piece of butter in each and sift over the whole one tablespoonful of sugar mixed with one teaspoonful of cinnamon
7. Let this stand for a second rising
8. when perfectly light, bake in a quick oven fifteen or twenty minutes
