## Ingredients

- neck of veal
- onion, finely chopped
- pepper
- salt
- parsley, chopped
- butter
- suet
- scraps and trimmings of veal
- hot water
- catchup

## Directions

1. Cut a neck of veal into thin steaks, and beat them to make them tender.
2. For seasoning, mix together some finely chopped onion sprinkled with pepper and salt, and a little chopped parsley.
3. Add some butter, and put it with the parsley and onion into a small sauce-pan, and set it on hot coals to stew till brown.
4. Put the steaks on a hot gridiron (the bars of which have been rubbed with suet) and broil them well, over a bed of bright clear coals.
5. When sufficiently done on one side turn them on the other.
6. After the last turning, cover each steak with some of the seasoning from the sauce-pan, and let all broil together till thoroughly done.
7. Have ready a gravy made of the scraps and trimmings of the veal, seasoned with pepper and salt, and boiled in a little hot water in the same sauce-pan in which the parsley and onions have been previously stewed.
8. Strain the gravy when it has boiled long enough, and flavour it with catchup.
