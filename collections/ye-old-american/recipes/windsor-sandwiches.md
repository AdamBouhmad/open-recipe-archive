## Ingredients

- one-third cup butter
- one-half cup finely chopped cold boiled ham
- one-half cup cold boiled chicken
- salt
- paprika
- thin slices of bread

## Directions

1. Cream one-third cup butter, and add one-half cup each of finely chopped cold boiled ham and cold boiled chicken.
2. Season with salt and paprika.
3. Spread mixture between thin slices of bread.
