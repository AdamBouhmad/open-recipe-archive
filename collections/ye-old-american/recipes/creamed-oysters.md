## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- 1½ cups White Sauce II
- ⅛ teaspoon celery salt

## Directions

1. Clean, and cook oysters until plump and edges begin to curl
2. Drain, and add to White Sauce seasoned with celery salt
3. Serve on toast, in timbale cases, patty shells, or vol-au-vents
