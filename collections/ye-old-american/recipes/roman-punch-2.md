## Ingredients

- 3 coffee cups of lemonade—(strong and sweet)
- 1 glass champagne
- 1 glass rum
- 2 oranges—juice only
- 2 eggs—whites only—well whipped
- ½ lb. powdered sugar, beaten into the stiffened whites

## Directions

1. Whip the egg whites well
2. Beat powdered sugar into the stiffened whites
3. Combine all ingredients
4. Ice abundantly—or, if you prefer, freeze
