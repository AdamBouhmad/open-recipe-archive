## Ingredients

- one quart of strained honey
- half a pound of fresh butter
- a small tea-spoonful of pearl-ash dissolved in a little sour milk
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. Take a quart of strained honey and half a pound of fresh butter
2. Add a small tea-spoonful of pearl-ash dissolved in a little sour milk
3. Add by degrees as much sifted flour as will make a stiff paste
4. Work the whole well together
5. Roll it out about half an inch thick
6. Cut it into cakes with the edge of a tumbler or with a tin cake-cutter
7. Lay them on buttered tins
8. Bake them with rather a brisk fire, but see that they do not burn
