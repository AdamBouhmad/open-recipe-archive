## Ingredients

- 4½ lbs. salt
- 1 lb. brown sugar
- 1 oz. saltpetre
- 3 gallons of water

## Directions

1. Put into a large saucepan and boil for half an hour, skimming off the scum
2. When cold, pour over the meat, and let it lie for a few days
