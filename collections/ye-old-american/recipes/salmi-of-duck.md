## Ingredients

- Cold roast duck
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
- 1 stalk celery
- 2 slices carrot, cut in pieces
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped lean raw ham
- ¼ cup flour
- 2 cups Consommé
- Bit of bay leaf
- Sprig of parsley
- Blade of mace
- 2 cloves
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- Sherry wine
- Stoned olives
- Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut cold roast duck in pieces for serving
2. Melt butter, add onion, celery, carrot, and ham
3. Cook until butter is brown, then add flour
4. When well browned add Consommé, bay leaf, parsley, mace, cloves, salt, and pepper
5. Cook five minutes and strain
6. Add duck and when reheated add Sherry wine, stoned olives, and mushrooms cut in quarters
7. Arrange on dish for serving and garnish with olives and mushrooms
