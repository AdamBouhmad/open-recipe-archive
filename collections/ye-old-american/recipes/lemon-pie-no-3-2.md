## Ingredients

- a heaping tablespoonful of cornstarch
- a little cold water
- a cupful of boiling water
- a teaspoonful of butter
- a cupful of sugar
- one egg
- the juice and grated rind of a fresh lemon
- crust

## Directions

1. Moisten a heaping tablespoonful of cornstarch with a little cold water
2. Add a cupful of boiling water
3. Stir over the fire till it boils and cook the cornstarch, say two or three minutes
4. Add a teaspoonful of butter and a cupful of sugar
5. Take off the fire and, when slightly cooled, add an egg well beaten and the juice and grated rind of a fresh lemon
6. Bake with a crust
