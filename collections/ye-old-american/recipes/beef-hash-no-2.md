## Ingredients

- cold roast beef or pieces of beefsteak
- onion
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- beef gravy or water
- poached eggs

## Directions

1. Chop cold roast beef, or pieces of beefsteak.
2. Fry half an onion in a piece of butter; when the onion is brown, add the chopped beef.
3. Season with a little salt and pepper.
4. Moisten with the beef gravy, if you have any, if not, with sufficient water and a little butter.
5. Cook long enough to be hot, but no longer, as much cooking toughens the meat.
6. Some prefer to let a crust form on the bottom and turn the hash brown side uppermost.
7. Serve with poached eggs on top.
