## Ingredients

- salted or pickled tongue
- mashed potato
- sliced carrot

## Directions

1. Put it into boiling water, and let it boil three hours or more, according to its size.
2. When you take it out peel and trim it, and send it to table surrounded with mashed potato, and garnished with sliced carrot.
