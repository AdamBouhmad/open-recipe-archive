## Ingredients

- 2 rabbits
- Cold salt and water
- Salt
- Half a dozen young onions
- Water
- Gill of drawn butter
- Pepper
- Juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Clean a pair of nice rabbits; soak in cold salt and water for an hour, to draw out the blood.
2. Put on in a large saucepan with cold water enough to cover them, salt slightly, and stew until tender.
3. Slice into another pot half a dozen young onions, and boil in a very little water until thoroughly done.
4. Drain off the water, and stir the onions into a gill of drawn butter, pepper to taste, and when it simmers, add the juice of a lemon.
5. Cut off the heads of the hares, lay in a hot dish and pour over them the onion-sauce.
6. Let the dish stand in a warm place, closely covered, five minutes before sending to table.
