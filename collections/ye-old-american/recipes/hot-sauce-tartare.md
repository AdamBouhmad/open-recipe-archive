## Ingredients

- ½ cup White Sauce I
- ⅓ cup Mayonnaise
- ½ shallot, finely chopped
- ½ teaspoon vinegar
- ½ tablespoon capers, finely chopped
- ½ tablespoon pickles, finely chopped
- ½ tablespoon olives, finely chopped
- ½ tablespoon parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. To white sauce add remaining ingredients.
2. Stir constantly until mixture is thoroughly heated, but do not let it come to the boiling-point.
3. Served with boiled, steamed, or fried fish.
