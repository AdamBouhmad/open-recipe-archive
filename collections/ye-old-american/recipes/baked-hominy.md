## Ingredients

- cupful cold boiled hominy
- two cups milk
- heaping teaspoonful butter
- teaspoonful white sugar
- salt
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs very light, yolks and whites separately.
2. Work the yolks first into the hominy, alternately with the melted butter.
3. When thoroughly mixed, put in sugar and salt, and continue beating while you soften the batter gradually with the milk.
4. Be careful to leave no lumps in the hominy.
5. Stir in the whites and bake in a buttered pudding-dish until light, firm, and delicately browned.
