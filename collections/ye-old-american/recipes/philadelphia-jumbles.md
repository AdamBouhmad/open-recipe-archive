## Ingredients

- two cups of sugar
- one cup of butter
- eight eggs
- essence of bitter almond or rose
- flour
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Stir the sugar and butter to a light cream
2. Add the well-whipped eggs, the flavoring and flour
3. Mix well together
4. Roll out in powdered sugar in a sheet a quarter of an inch thick
5. Cut into rings with a jagging-iron and bake in a quick oven on buttered tins
