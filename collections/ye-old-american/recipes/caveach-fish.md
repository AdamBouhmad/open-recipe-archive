## Ingredients

- fish
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- onion
- vinegar
- water
- oil
- pounded mace
- whole black pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the fish in pieces the thickness of your hand
2. Wash it and dry it in a cloth
3. Sprinkle on some pepper and salt
4. Dredge it with flour and fry it a nice brown
5. When it gets cold, put it in a pot with a little chopped onion between the layers
6. Take as much vinegar and water as will cover it
7. Mix with it some oil, pounded mace, and whole black pepper
