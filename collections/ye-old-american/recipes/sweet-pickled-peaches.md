## Ingredients

- ½ peck peaches
- 2 lbs. brown sugar
- 1 pint vinegar
- 1 oz. stick cinnamon
- cloves
- hot water

## Directions

1. Boil sugar, vinegar, and cinnamon twenty minutes.
2. Dip peaches quickly in hot water, then rub off the fur with a towel.
3. Stick each peach with four cloves.
4. Put into syrup, and cook until soft, using one-half peaches at a time.
