## Ingredients

- 3 tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter for sautéing
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- few grains cayenne
- yolk 1 hard-boiled egg
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Wipe, peel, and cut tomatoes in slices.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dredge with flour.
3. Sauté in butter.
4. Place on a hot platter.
5. Make dressing by creaming the butter, adding dry ingredients, yolk of egg rubbed to a paste, egg beaten slightly, and vinegar.
6. Cook dressing over hot water, stirring constantly until it thickens.
7. Pour over the tomatoes.
