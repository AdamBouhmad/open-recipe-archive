## Ingredients

- rabbits
- letter paper
- butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the rabbits
2. Wipe them dry and split them down the back lengthwise
3. Pound them flat
4. Wrap them in letter paper well buttered
5. Place them on a buttered gridiron and broil over a clear, brisk fire, turning them often
6. When sufficiently cooked, remove the papers and lay them on a very hot platter
7. Season with salt, pepper and plenty of butter, turning them over and over to soak up the butter
8. Cover and keep hot in a warming oven until served
