## Ingredients

- boiled custard
- four whites of eggs
- eight or ten drops of oil of lemon
- fresh rose leaf

## Directions

1. Make a boiled custard as in the preceding receipts
2. When it is done and quite cold, put it into a deep glass dish
3. Beat to a stiff froth the four whites of eggs that have been omitted in the custard, adding eight or ten drops of oil of lemon
4. Drop the froth in balls on the top of the dish of custard, heaping and forming them with a spoon into a regular size and shape
5. Do not let them touch each other
6. You may lay a fresh rose leaf on the top of every one
