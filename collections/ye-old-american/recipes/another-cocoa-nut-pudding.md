## Ingredients

- a cocoa-nut, one pound grated
- two stale rusks or small sponge-cakes
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of powdered white sugar
- a glass of white wine
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Peel and cut up the cocoa-nut, and wash and wipe the pieces.
2. Weigh one pound, and grate it fine.
3. Mix with it two stale rusks or small sponge-cakes, grated also.
4. Stir together till very light half a pound of butter and half a pound of powdered white sugar, and add a glass of white wine.
5. Beat six whole eggs very light, and stir them gradually into the butter and sugar in turn with the grated cocoa-nut.
6. Having stirred the whole very hard at the last, put it into a buttered dish and bake it half an hour.
7. Send it to table cold.
