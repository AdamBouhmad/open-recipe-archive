## Ingredients

- one pound of fresh beef-dripping
- two pounds and a quarter of flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. To a pound of fresh beef-dripping that has been nicely clarified, allow two pounds and a quarter of flour.
2. Put the flour into a large pan, and mix the dripping with it, rubbing it into the flour with your hands till it is thoroughly incorporated.
3. Make it into a stiff dough with a little cold water.
4. Roll it out twice.
