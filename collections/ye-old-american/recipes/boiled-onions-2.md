## Ingredients

- onions
- boiling water
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- drawn butter

## Directions

1. Cut off tops and tails, and skin them.
2. Lay in cold water half an hour.
3. Put into a saucepan with enough boiling water to cover them.
4. Cook fifteen minutes and drain off the water.
5. Re-cover them with more water from the boiling tea-kettle.
6. Boil until a straw will pierce them.
7. Drain and put into a dish with pepper, salt, and plenty of butter.
8. Send around drawn butter with them.
9. Never cook onions in an iron pot.
