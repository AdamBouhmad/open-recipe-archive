## Ingredients

- 1 pint very strong made coffee—fresh and hot
- 1 pint boiling milk

## Directions

1. Pour off the coffee from the grounds through a fine strainer (thin muslin is the best material) into the table coffee-pot.
2. Add the milk.
3. Set the pot where it will keep hot for five minutes before pouring it out.
