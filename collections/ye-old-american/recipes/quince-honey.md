## Ingredients

- five large quinces
- five pounds sugar
- one pint boiling water

## Directions

1. Pare and grate five large quinces
2. To one pint boiling water add five pounds sugar
3. Stir over fire until sugar is dissolved
4. Add quince and cook fifteen or twenty minutes
5. Turn into glasses
