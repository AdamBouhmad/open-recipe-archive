## Ingredients

- small-sized head of cabbage
- boiling water
- new milk or part milk and cream
- wheat or rice flour
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the outer leaves from a solid, small-sized head of cabbage
2. Cut the remainder as fine as for slaw
3. Heat a spider or deep skillet
4. Put in the cut cabbage and pour over it a pint of boiling water
5. Cover closely and allow it to cook rapidly for ten minutes
6. Drain off the water and add half a pint of new milk, or part milk and cream
7. When it boils, stir in a large teaspoonful of either wheat or rice flour moistened with milk
8. Add salt and pepper
9. As soon as it comes to a boil, serve
