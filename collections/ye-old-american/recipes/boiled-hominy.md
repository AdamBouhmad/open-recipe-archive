## Ingredients

- large hominy
- cold water
- water for boiling
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak the large hominy over night in cold water.
2. Next day put it into a pot with at least two quarts of water to a quart of the hominy.
3. Boil slowly three hours, or until it is soft.
4. Drain in a cullender, heap in a root-dish, and stir in butter, pepper, and salt.
