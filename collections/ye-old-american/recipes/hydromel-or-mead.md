## Ingredients

- 36 ounces honey
- 4 quarts warm water

## Directions

1. Mix your mead in the proportion of thirty-six ounces of honey to four quarts of warm water
2. When the honey is completely held in solution, pour it into a cask
3. When fermented, and become perfectly clear, bottle and cork it well
