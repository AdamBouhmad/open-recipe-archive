## Ingredients

- Stale bread
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut stale bread in one-third inch slices and remove crusts.
2. Spread thinly with butter.
3. Cut slices in one-third inch cubes, put in pan and bake until delicately brown, or fry in deep fat.
