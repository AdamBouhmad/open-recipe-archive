## Ingredients

- one cupful of sugar
- half a cupful of butter
- one egg
- one lemon
- half a cupful of boiling water

## Directions

1. Put sugar, butter, one egg beaten light, one lemon with juice and grated rind, and half a cupful of boiling water in a tin basin
2. Thicken over steam
