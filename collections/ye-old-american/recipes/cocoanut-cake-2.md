## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a cup of butter
- two cups of white sugar
- one cup of sweet milk
- four eggs, whites and yolks separately beaten
- lemon or vanilla (flavoring)
- three heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- three cups of sifted flour
- For Filling: whites of three eggs
- one cup of powdered sugar
- grated cocoanut

## Directions

1. Cream together three-quarters of a cup of butter and two cups of white sugar
2. Add one cup of sweet milk
3. Add the yolks of four eggs first
4. Add the whites of four eggs
5. Flavor with lemon or vanilla
6. Mix three heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder in three cups of sifted flour and add last
7. Bake in jelly pans
8. For filling, make an icing by beating the whites of three eggs and a cup of powdered sugar to a stiff froth
9. When the cake is cooled, spread a thick layer of this frosting over each cake
10. Sprinkle very thickly with grated cocoanut
