## Ingredients

- 1 quart of water, boiling
- 2 ozs. prepared cocoa—Baker's is best
- 1 quart of milk

## Directions

1. Make as you do chocolate—only boil nearly an hour before you add the milk
2. Afterward heat almost to boiling
3. Sweeten to taste
