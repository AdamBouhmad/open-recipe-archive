## Ingredients

- 1 lb. cocoa-nut, grated
- ½ lb. powdered sugar
- 1 quart milk, unskimmed
- 6 eggs beaten to a froth
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg
- 2 teaspoonfuls vanilla or rose-water

## Directions

1. Boil the milk, take it from the fire, and whip in gradually the beaten eggs.
2. When nearly cold, season; add the cocoa-nut, and pour into paste-shells.
3. Do not boil the egg and milk together.
4. Bake twenty minutes.
