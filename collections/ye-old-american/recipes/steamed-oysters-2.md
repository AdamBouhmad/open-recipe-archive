## Ingredients

- oysters
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and drain a quart of counts or select oysters.
2. Put them in a shallow pan and place in a steamer over boiling water.
3. Cover and steam till they are plump, with the edges ruffled, but no longer.
4. Place to a heated dish, with butter, pepper, and salt, and serve.
