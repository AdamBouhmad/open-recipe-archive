## Ingredients

- a pint of rich milk
- a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
- a small table-spoonful of flour
- half a dozen slices of toast

## Directions

1. Boil a pint of rich milk
2. Take it off, and stir into it a quarter of a pound of fresh butter, mixed with a small table-spoonful of flour
3. Let it again come to a boil
4. Have ready two deep plates with half a dozen slices of toast in each
5. Pour the milk over them hot, and keep them covered till they go to table
