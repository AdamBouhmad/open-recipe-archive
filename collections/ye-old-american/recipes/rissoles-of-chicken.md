## Ingredients

- cold chicken
- lean cold ham
- white sauce
- light paste
- lard

## Directions

1. Mince up finely the remains of a cold chicken together with half the quantity of lean, cold ham
2. Mix them well, adding enough white sauce to moisten them
3. have light paste rolled out until about a quarter of an inch or a little more in thickness
4. Cut the paste into pieces, one inch by two in size
5. lay a little of the mixture upon the centres of half of the pieces
6. cover them with the other halves
7. press the edges neatly together and form them into little rolls
8. Have your frying pan ready with plenty of boiling hot lard, or other frying medium
9. fry until they become a golden-brown color
10. drain them well
11. serve immediately on a napkin
