## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped beef (cut from top of round)
- ⅓ cup rice
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- Few grains cayenne
- Cabbage
- Tomato Sauce

## Directions

1. Mix beef and rice, and add salt, pepper, and cayenne.
2. Cook cabbage leaves two minutes in boiling water to cover.
3. In each leaf put two tablespoons mixture, and fold leaf to enclose mixture.
4. Cook one hour in Tomato Sauce.
