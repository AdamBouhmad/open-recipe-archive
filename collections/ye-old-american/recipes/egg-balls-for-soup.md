## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 hard-boiled eggs
- melted butter
- pepper
- salt
- 2 raw eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. Rub the yolks of three or four hard-boiled eggs to a smooth paste with a very little melted butter, pepper, and salt
2. Add two raw ones, beaten light
3. Add enough flour to hold the paste together
4. Mince into balls with floured hands and set in a cool place until just before your soup comes off
5. Put in carefully and boil one minute
