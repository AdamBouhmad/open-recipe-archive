## Ingredients

- Sardines
- Batter III
- Hot Tartare Sauce

## Directions

1. Drain fish and pour over boiling water to free from oil, then remove skins.
2. Dip in batter, fry in deep fat, and drain on brown paper.
3. Serve with Hot Tartare Sauce.
