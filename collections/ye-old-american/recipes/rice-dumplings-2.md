## Ingredients

- 1 lb. rice boiled without stirring, until soft, and at the top dry
- 12 pippins, pared and cored
- strawberry marmalade or crab-apple jelly

## Directions

1. Let the rice cool upon a sieve or coarse cloth, that it may dry at the same time
2. Dip your dumpling cloths in hot water; wring them out and flour well inside
3. Put a handful of the cold rice upon each, spreading it out into a smooth sheet
4. Lay in the centre an apple
5. Fill the hole left by the core with marmalade or jelly
6. Draw up the cloth carefully to enclose the apple with a coating of rice
7. Tie and boil one hour
8. Turn over with care
9. Pour sweet sauce or rich sweetened cream over them, and send around more in a boat with them
