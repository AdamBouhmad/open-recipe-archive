## Ingredients

- peaches
- sugar (to half a dozen peaches, a quarter of a pound of sugar)
- brandy

## Directions

1. Put your peaches in boiling water, just give them a scald, but don't let them boil
2. Take them out, and put them in cold water
3. Then dry them in a sieve
4. Put them in long wide mouthed bottles
5. To half a dozen peaches take a quarter of a pound of sugar
6. Clarify it, pour it over your peaches, and fill the bottles with brandy
7. Stop them close, and keep them in a close place
