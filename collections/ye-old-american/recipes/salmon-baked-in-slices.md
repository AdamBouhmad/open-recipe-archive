## Ingredients

- salmon
- cayenne
- salt
- butter
- flour
- water
- port wine
- catchup
- soy
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take out the bone and cut the flesh into slices.
2. Season them with cayenne and salt.
3. Melt two ounces of butter that has been rolled in flour, in a half pint of water.
4. Mix with it two large glasses of port wine, two table-spoonfuls of catchup, and two of soy.
5. You may add the juice of a large lemon.
6. Mix all well.
7. Strain it and pour it over the slices of salmon.
8. Tie a sheet of buttered paper over the dish, and put it into the oven.
