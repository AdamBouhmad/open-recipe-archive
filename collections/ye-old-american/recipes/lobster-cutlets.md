## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped lobster meat
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- Few gratings nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- Yolk 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 cup Thick White Sauce

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given, and cool.
2. Shape in form of cutlets, crumb, and fry same as croquettes.
3. Make a cut at small end of each cutlet, and insert in each the tip end of a small claw.
4. Stack around a mound of parsley.
5. Serve with Sauce Tartare.
