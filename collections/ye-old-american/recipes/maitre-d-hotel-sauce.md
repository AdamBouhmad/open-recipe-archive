## Ingredients

- 1 teacupful drawn butter
- 1 teaspoonful minced parsley
- 1 lemon
- Cayenne and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Draw the butter (No. 2); boil the parsley three minutes; take it out and lay in cold water five minutes, to cool; chop and stir into the butter; squeeze in the lemon-juice, the pepper and salt; beat hard with an egg-whip, return to the fire, and boil up once.
