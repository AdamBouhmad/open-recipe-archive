## Ingredients

- two pounds of sal soda
- two pounds of common bar soap
- ten quarts of water

## Directions

1. Cut the soap in thin slices and boil together two hours
2. strain and it will be fit for use
3. Put the clothes in soak the night before you wash
4. to every pailful of water in which you boil them add a pound of soap
5. They will need no rubbing, but merely rinsing
