## Ingredients

- medium-sized fresh codfish
- salt
- one pound of salt fat pork
- corn meal
- lemon juice
- freshly ground pepper

## Directions

1. Select a medium-sized fresh codfish and cut it in steaks crosswise of the fish, about an inch and a half thick
2. Sprinkle a little salt over them, and let them stand two hours
3. Cut into dice a pound of salt fat pork
4. Fry out all the fat from them and remove the crisp bits of pork
5. Put the codfish steaks in a pan of corn meal, dredge them with it
6. When the pork fat is smoking hot, fry the steaks in it to a dark brown color on both sides
7. Squeeze over them a little lemon juice
8. Add a dash of freshly ground pepper
9. Serve with hot, old-fashioned, well-buttered Johnny Cake
