## Ingredients

- a coffee cup of cold veal
- cold ham
- two or three slices of onion
- a pinch of mace
- powdered parsley
- pepper
- salt
- a pint of milk or cream
- a tablespoonful of cold butter
- two eggs
- a teaspoonful of cornstarch or flour
- egg
- bread crumbs
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Mince a coffee cup of cold veal in a chopping bowl, adding a little cold ham and two or three slices of onion, a pinch of mace, powdered parsley and pepper, some salt.
2. Let a pint of milk or cream come to the boiling point, then add a tablespoonful of cold butter, then the above mixture.
3. Beat up two eggs and mix with a teaspoonful of cornstarch or flour, and add to the rest.
4. Cook it all about ten minutes, stirring with care.
5. Remove from the fire, and spread it on a platter.
6. Roll it into balls, when cooled flatten each.
7. Dip them in egg and bread crumbs, and fry in a wire basket, dipped in hot lard.
