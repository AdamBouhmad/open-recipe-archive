## Ingredients

- one broiler
- two and one-half tablespoons melted butter
- one cup stock
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon flour
- one-half cup cooked potato balls
- one-third cup canned string beans
- one-third cup cooked carrot
- six sautéd mushroom caps

## Directions

1. Dress, clean, and truss a broiler.
2. Put in a casserole dish and brush over with two and one-half tablespoons melted butter.
3. Put on cover and bake twenty minutes.
4. Add one cup stock and cook until chicken is tender.
5. Thicken stock with one tablespoon each butter and flour cooked together.
6. Add one-half cup cooked potato balls, one-third cup canned string beans cut in small pieces, one-third cup cooked carrot cut in fancy shapes, and six sautéd mushroom caps.
