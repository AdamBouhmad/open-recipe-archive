## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- parsley
- sweet marjoram
- green onion
- black fish
- cayenne
- salt
- powdered cloves
- powdered mace
- wine
- water

## Directions

1. Flour a deep dish, and lay in the bottom a piece of butter rolled in flour.
2. Then sprinkle it with a mixture of parsley, sweet marjoram, and green onion; all chopped fine.
3. Take your black fish and rub it inside and outside with a mixture of cayenne, salt, and powdered cloves and mace.
4. Place skewers across the dish, and lay the fish upon them.
5. Then pour in a little wine, and sufficient water to stew the fish.
6. Set the dish in a moderate oven, and let it cook slowly for an hour.
