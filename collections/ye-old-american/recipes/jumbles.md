## Ingredients

- two cups of sugar
- one cup of butter
- three eggs
- six tablespoonfuls of sweet milk
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Cream together two cups of sugar and one of butter
2. Add three well-beaten eggs and six tablespoonfuls of sweet milk, two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
3. Flavor to taste
4. Add flour enough to make into a soft dough
5. Do not roll it on the paste-board, but break off pieces of dough the size of a walnut and make into rings by rolling out rolls as large as your finger, and joining the ends
6. Lay them on tins to bake, an inch apart, as it rises and spreads
7. Bake in a moderate oven
