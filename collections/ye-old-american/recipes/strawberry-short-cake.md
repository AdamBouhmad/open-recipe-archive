## Ingredients

- baking powder biscuit dough
- shortening
- soft butter
- one quart of strawberries
- sugar
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Make a rule of baking powder biscuit, with a little more shortening
2. Divide the dough in half
3. Lay one-half on the molding board
4. Divide this half again, and roll each piece large enough to cover a biscuit-tin, or a large sized pie-tin
5. Spread soft butter over the lower one and place the other on top of that
6. Proceed with the other lump of dough the same, by cutting it in halves, and putting on another tin
7. Set them in the oven
8. When sufficiently baked take them out, separate each one by running a large knife through where the cold soft butter was spread
9. Butter plentifully each crust
10. Lay the bottom of each on earthen platters or dining-plates
11. Cover thickly with a quart of strawberries that have been previously prepared with sugar
12. Lay the top crusts on the fruit
13. If there is any juice left pour it around the cake
14. Send to the table with a pitcher of sweet cream
