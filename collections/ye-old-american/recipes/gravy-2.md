## Ingredients

- liquid from roasting pan
- six tablespoons fat
- six tablespoons flour
- three cups stock
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Pour off liquid in pan in which turkey has been roasted.
2. From liquid skim off six tablespoons fat and return fat to pan.
3. Brown with six tablespoons flour.
4. Pour on gradually three cups stock in which giblets, neck, and tips of wings have been cooked, or use liquor left in pan.
5. Cook five minutes and season with salt and pepper.
6. Strain.
