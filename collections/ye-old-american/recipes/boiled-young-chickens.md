## Ingredients

- young chickens
- scalding water
- skimmed milk
- flour
- cold water
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Put the chickens in scalding water
2. As soon as the feathers will slip off, take them out
3. When you have drawn them, lay them in skimmed milk for two hours
4. Truss and dust them well with flour
5. Put them in cold water
6. Cover them close and set them over a very slow fire
7. Take off the scum
8. Let them boil slowly for five or six minutes
9. Take them off the fire and keep them closely covered in the water for half an hour
10. When you are going to dish them, set them over the fire to make them hot
11. Drain them and pour over white sauce made the same way as for the boiled fowls
