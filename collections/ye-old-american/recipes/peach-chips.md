## Ingredients

- peaches
- sugar
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Slice them thin, and boil them till clear in a syrup made with half their weight of sugar
2. Lay them on dishes in the sun, and turn them till dry
3. Pack them in pots with powdered sugar sifted over each layer
4. Should there be syrup left, continue the process with other peaches
