## Ingredients

- mangoes
- vine leaves or cabbage leaves
- alum
- scraped horseradish
- white mustard seed
- mace
- nutmeg
- race ginger
- pepper
- tumeric
- sweet oil
- garlic
- packthread
- boiling vinegar

## Directions

1. Put mangoes into a kettle with vine leaves or cabbage leaves under and over them, and a little piece of alum, and hang them on a slow fire to green
2. Keep them closely covered to retain the steam, which will greatly accelerate the greening
3. When they are quite green, prepare the stuffing which must be a mixture of scraped horseradish, white mustard seed, mace and nutmeg pounded, race ginger cut small, pepper, tumeric and sweet oil
4. Fill your mangoes with this mixture, putting a small clove of garlic into each
5. Replace the pieces at the openings and tie them with a packthread crossing backwards and forwards round the mango
6. Put them into stone jars and pour boiling vinegar over them, and cover them well
7. Before you put them on the table remove the packthread
