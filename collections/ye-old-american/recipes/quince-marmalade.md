## Ingredients

- quinces
- water
- sugar
- cochineal

## Directions

1. Boil the quinces in water until soft, let them cool
2. Rub all the pulp through a sieve
3. Put two pounds of it to one of sugar
4. Pound a little cochineal, sift in through fine muslin, and mix it with the quince to give a colour
5. Pick out the seeds, tie them in a muslin bag, and boil them with the marmalade
6. When it is a thick jelly, take out the seeds, and put it in pots
