## Ingredients

- 1 cup chestnuts
- Butter
- Tabasco Sauce or few grains paprika

## Directions

1. Shell chestnuts, cut in thin slices, and fry until well browned, using enough butter to prevent chestnuts from burning.
2. Season with Tabasco Sauce or few grains paprika.
