## Ingredients

- a cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- a half cup of milk
- four eggs
- one teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
- one teaspoonful of cinnamon
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- flour
- one quart of huckleberries

## Directions

1. Beat a cup of butter and two cups of sugar together until light.
2. Add a half cup of milk, four eggs beaten separately, the yolks to a cream and the whites to a stiff froth.
3. Add one teaspoonful of grated nutmeg, the same of cinnamon and two teaspoonfuls of baking powder, rubbed into the flour.
4. Rub one quart of huckleberries well with some flour and add them last, but do not mash them.
5. Pour into buttered pans, about an inch thick.
6. Dust the tops with sugar and bake.
7. It is better the day after baking.
