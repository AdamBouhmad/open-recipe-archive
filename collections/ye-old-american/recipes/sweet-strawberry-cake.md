## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one cupful of sugar
- two cupfuls of flour
- one tablespoonful of butter
- a teaspoonful, heaped, of baking powder
- three pints of strawberries
- a cupful of sugar
- white of an egg
- a tablespoonful of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar together and add the eggs well beaten.
2. Stir in the flour and baking powder well sifted together.
3. Bake in deep tin plate in four plates.
4. With three pints of strawberries mix a cupful of sugar and mash them a little.
5. Spread the fruit between the layers of cake.
6. The top layer of strawberries may be covered with a meringue made with the white of an egg and a tablespoonful of powdered sugar.
7. Save out the largest berries and arrange them around in circles on the top in the white frosting.
