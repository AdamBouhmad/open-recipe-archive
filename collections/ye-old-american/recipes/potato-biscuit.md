## Ingredients

- 8 potatoes of medium size, mashed very fine
- 4 tablespoonfuls butter, melted
- 2 cups milk, blood-warm
- 1 cup yeast
- flour to make a thin batter
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Stir all ingredients together except the butter
2. Let the sponge rise until light (four or five hours)
3. Add the melted butter with a little salt and flour, enough to make soft dough
4. Set aside for four hours longer
5. Roll out in a sheet three-quarters of an inch thick
6. Cut into cakes and let these rise one hour
7. Bake
