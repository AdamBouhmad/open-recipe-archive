## Ingredients

- pigeons or squabs
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- salt pork

## Directions

1. Split them down the back and broil the same as chicken
2. Season well with salt, pepper and plenty of butter
3. Broil slices of salt pork, very thin
4. Place a slice over each bird and serve
