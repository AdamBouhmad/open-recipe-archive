## Ingredients

- butter
- one pint of water
- one pint of flour
- five or six eggs
- one pint of lard

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter the size of an egg into a pint of water
2. let it boil a few minutes
3. thicken it very smoothly with a pint of flour
4. let it remain a short time on the fire, stir it all the time that it may not stick to the pan
5. pour it in a wooden bowl
6. add five or six eggs, breaking one and beating it in--then another, and so on till they are all in, and the dough quite light
7. put a pint of lard in a pan, let it boil
8. make the fritters small, and fry them of a fine amber colour
