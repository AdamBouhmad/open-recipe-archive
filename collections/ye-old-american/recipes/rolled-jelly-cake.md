## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one teacup of fine sugar
- one teacup of flour
- two tablespoonfuls of water
- a pinch of salt
- a heaping teaspoonful of baking powder
- jelly for filling

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks until light
2. Add the sugar
3. Add two tablespoonfuls of water and a pinch of salt
4. Stir in the flour gradually, in which there should be a heaping teaspoonful of baking powder
5. Bake in long, shallow biscuit-tins, well greased
6. Turn out on a damp towel on a bread-board
7. Cover the top with jelly and roll up while warm
