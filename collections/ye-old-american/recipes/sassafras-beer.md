## Ingredients

- 2 gallons of soft water
- 1 quart of wheat bran
- a large handful of dried apples
- half a pint of molasses
- a small handful of hops
- half a pint of strong fresh yeast
- a piece of sassafras root the size of an egg

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients (except the molasses and yeast) at once into a large kettle
2. Boil it till the apples are quite soft
3. Put the molasses into a small clean tub or a large pan
4. Set a hair sieve over the vessel, and strain the mixture through it
5. Let it stand till it becomes only milkwarm, and then stir in the yeast
6. Put the liquor immediately into the keg or jugs, and let it stand uncorked to ferment
7. Fill the jugs quite full, that the liquor in fermenting may run over
8. Set them in a large tub
9. When you see that the fermentation or working has subsided, cork it, and it will be fit for use next day
