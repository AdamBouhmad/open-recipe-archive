## Ingredients

- 1 pint large oysters
- toast
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- lemon juice
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Clean one pint large oysters.
2. Place in dripping-pan small oblong pieces of toast, put an oyster on each piece, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and bake until oysters are plump.
3. Serve with Lemon Butter.
4. For Lemon Butter: Cream three tablespoons butter, add one-half teaspoon salt, one tablespoon lemon juice, and a few grains cayenne.
