## Ingredients

- 1½ cups brown sugar
- ¾ cup thin cream
- ½ tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Boil ingredients together in a smooth granite saucepan until a ball can be formed when mixture is tried in cold water. It takes about forty minutes for boiling. Beat until of right consistency to spread.
