## Ingredients

- whole boiled potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut whole boiled potatoes lengthwise, into slices a quarter of an inch thick
2. Lay upon a gridiron over a hot, bright fire
3. Brown on both sides
4. Sprinkle with pepper and salt
5. Lay a bit of butter upon each
6. Eat very hot
