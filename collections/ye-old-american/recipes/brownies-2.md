## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅓ cup powdered sugar
- ⅓ cup Porto Rico molasses
- 1 egg, well beaten
- ⅞ cup bread flour
- 1 cup pecan meat, cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given.
2. Bake in small shallow fancy cake tins, garnishing top of each cake with one-half pecan.
