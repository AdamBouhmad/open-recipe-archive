## Ingredients

- half a pint of noyau
- a pint of sherry or other white wine
- the yellow peel of four lemons, pared thin
- half an ounce of mace
- half a pint of capillaire or strong sugar syrup or Curaçoa

## Directions

1. Mix together half a pint of noyau; a pint of sherry or other white wine; the yellow peel of four lemons, pared thin; and half an ounce of mace.
2. Put the whole into a large bottle, and let it stand for two or three weeks.
3. Strain it, and add half a pint of capillaire or strong sugar syrup; or of Curaçoa.
4. Bottle it, and it will keep two or three years.
5. It may be used for various sweet dishes, but chiefly for pudding-sauce mixed with melted butter.
