## Ingredients

- rice croquette mixture
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- grated rind ½ lemon

## Directions

1. To rice croquette mixture add two tablespoons powdered sugar and grated rind one-half lemon.
2. Shape in cylinder forms, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again, fry in deep fat, and drain.
