## Ingredients

- thin slices of steak from the upper part of the round or one large thin steak
- one cupful fine bread crumbs
- half a teaspoonful salt
- pepper
- one tablespoonful butter
- half a teaspoonful sage
- half a teaspoonful powdered summer savory
- milk
- thin slices of salt pork
- half a pint water
- onion

## Directions

1. Lay the meat out smoothly and wipe it dry
2. Prepare a dressing using bread crumbs, salt, pepper, butter, sage, powdered summer savory, and enough milk to moisten into a stiff mixture
3. Spread the dressing over the meat
4. Roll it up carefully and tie with a string, securing the ends well
5. Fry a few thin slices of salt pork in the bottom of a kettle or saucepan
6. Place the roll or rolls of beef into the fat and brown on all sides, turning until a rich color all over
7. Add half a pint of water
8. Stew until tender
9. If desired, chop a slice of onion fine and add to the dressing
10. When cooked sufficiently, take out the meat and thicken the gravy
11. Turn the gravy over the meat
12. Carve by cutting crosswise in slices through beef and stuffing
