## Ingredients

- sweet pears
- water
- sugar
- cloves, cinnamon, or ginger

## Directions

1. Sweet pears may be baked just as sweet apples are—steamed without being pared or cored
2. Alternatively, if large, cut in half, put into a deep dish, with a very little water
3. Sprinkle them with sugar, and put a few cloves, or bits of cinnamon, or a pinch of ginger among them
4. Cover closely, and bake until tender
