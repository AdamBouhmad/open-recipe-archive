## Ingredients

- canvas-back duck
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. After dressing the duck in the usual way by plucking, singeing, and drawing, wipe it with a wet towel
2. Truss the head under the wing
3. Place it in a dripping-pan
4. Put it in the oven, basting often, and roast it half an hour
5. It is generally preferred a little underdone
6. Place it when done on a hot dish, season well with salt and pepper
7. Pour over it the gravy it has yielded in baking and serve it immediately while hot
