## Ingredients

- the inwards (tender, chopped fine)
- double quantity of grated bread
- 4 ounces butter
- pepper
- salt
- sweet herbs (if you like)
- 2 eggs (moulded into the stuffing)
- 4 onions (parboiled and chopped)
- wine

## Directions

1. Boil the inwards tender, chop them fine, put double quantity of grated bread, 4 ounces butter, pepper, salt, (and sweet herbs if you like) 2 eggs moulded into the stuffing, parboil 4 onions and chop them into the stuffing, add wine, and roast the bird.
2. The above is a good stuffing for every kind of Water Fowl, which requires onion sauce.
