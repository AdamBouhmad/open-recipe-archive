## Ingredients

- knuckle of veal
- salt
- whole pepper
- sweet marjoram
- parsley
- onions
- butter
- flour
- water
- ham

## Directions

1. Lay four wooden skewers across the bottom of your stew-pan and place the meat upon them
2. Carefully wash the meat and rub it with salt
3. Add a table-spoonful of whole pepper, the leaves from a bunch of sweet marjoram, a sprig of parsley leaves chopped, two onions peeled and sliced, and a piece of butter rolled in flour
4. Pour in two quarts of water
5. Cover it closely and after it has come to a boil, lessen the fire and let the meat only simmer for two hours or more
6. Before you serve it up, pour the liquid over it
