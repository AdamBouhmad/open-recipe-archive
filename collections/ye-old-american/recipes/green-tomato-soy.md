## Ingredients

- 2 gallons tomatoes, green, and sliced without peeling
- 12 good-sized onions, also sliced
- 2 quarts vinegar
- 1 quart sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls ground mustard
- 2 tablespoonfuls black pepper, ground
- 1 tablespoonful allspice
- 1 tablespoonful cloves

## Directions

1. Mix all together, and stew until tender, stirring often lest they should scorch.
2. Put up in small glass jars.
