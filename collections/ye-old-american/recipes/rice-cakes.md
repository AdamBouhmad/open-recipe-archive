## Ingredients

- half a pint of rice
- a pint and a half of flour
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- five eggs
- a quart of milk
- butter, honey, or molasses

## Directions

1. Pick and wash half a pint of rice, and boil it very soft
2. Drain it, and let it get cold
3. Sift a pint and a half of flour over the pan of rice, and mix in a quarter of a pound of butter that has been warmed by the fire, and a salt-spoonful of salt
4. Beat five eggs very light, and stir them gradually into a quart of milk
5. Beat the whole very hard, and bake it in muffin rings, or in waffle-irons
6. Send them to table hot, and eat them with butter, honey, or molasses
