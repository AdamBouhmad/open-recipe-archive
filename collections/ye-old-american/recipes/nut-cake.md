## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup cold water
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 2 teaspoonfuls cream-tartar
- 2 cupfuls kernels of hickory-nuts or white walnuts, carefully picked out

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients, adding the nut kernels last of all
