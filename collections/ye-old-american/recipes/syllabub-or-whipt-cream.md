## Ingredients

- four large lemons
- one pint of white wine
- half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- one quart of cream
- fruit jelly

## Directions

1. Pare off very thin the yellow rind of four large lemons and lay it in the bottom of a deep dish
2. Squeeze the juice of the lemons into a large bowl containing a pint of white wine and sweeten it with half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
3. By degrees mix in a quart of cream
4. Pour the whole into the dish in which you have laid the lemon-peel and let the mixture stand untouched for three hours
5. Beat it with rods to a stiff froth, first taking out the lemon-peel
6. Put into each of your glasses a table-spoonful or more of fruit jelly and heap the syllabub upon it so as to stand up high at the top
