## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- ⅓ pound sugar dates
- 2 eggs
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few gratings nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook dates with milk twenty minutes in top of double boiler.
2. Strain, and rub through sieve, then add eggs and salt.
3. Bake same as Custard Pie.
