## Ingredients

- Oyster crabs
- Flour
- Cold milk
- Crumbs
- Bouchée cases

## Directions

1. Pick over oyster crabs, dip in flour, cold milk, and crumbs
2. Fry in deep fat, and drain on brown paper
3. Fill bouchée cases with crabs
