## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- a wine-glassful of brandy or rum
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make one pint of milk very sweet
2. Add a wine-glassful of brandy or rum, well stirred together
3. Grate a little nutmeg over the top of the glasses
4. Serve with a straw in each glass
