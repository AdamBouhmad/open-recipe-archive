## Ingredients

- four-pound haddock
- salt
- lemon juice
- oysters
- butter
- cracker crumbs
- pepper
- egg
- Hollandaise Sauce I
- one pint oysters
- one cup cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Remove skin, head, and tail from the haddock.
2. Bone, leaving in large bones near head, to keep fillets in shape of the original fish.
3. Sprinkle with salt, and brush over with lemon juice.
4. Lay one fillet on greased fish-sheet in a dripping-pan, cover thickly with oysters, cleaned and dipped in buttered cracker crumbs seasoned with salt and pepper.
5. Cover oysters with other fillet, brush with egg slightly beaten, cover with buttered crumbs, and bake fifty minutes in a moderate oven.
6. Serve with Hollandaise Sauce I.
