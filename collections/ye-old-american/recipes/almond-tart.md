## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- ⅓ cup grated chocolate
- ½ cup Jordan almonds, blanched and finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ¾ cup cracker dust
- whipped cream, sweetened and flavored
- angelica
- candied cherries

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored; add sugar gradually, then fold in white of eggs beaten until stiff and dry.
2. Add chocolate, almonds, baking powder, and cracker dust.
3. Bake in a round pan.
4. Cool, split, and put whipped cream, sweetened and flavored, between and on top.
5. Garnish with angelica and candied cherries.
