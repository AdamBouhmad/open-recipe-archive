## Ingredients

- young beans
- salt water
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- olive oil or melted butter

## Directions

1. String young beans
2. Break into half-inch pieces or leave whole
3. Wash and cook soft in salt water
4. Drain well
5. Add finely chopped onions, pepper, salt and vinegar
6. When cool, add olive oil or melted butter
