## Ingredients

- two-thirds of a cupful of sugar
- a pint of raspberries or strawberries
- a tablespoonful of melted butter
- a cupful of hot water

## Directions

1. Boil all together slowly, removing the scum as fast as it rises
2. Then strain through a sieve
