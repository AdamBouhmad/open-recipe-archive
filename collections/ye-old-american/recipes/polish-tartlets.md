## Ingredients

- Puff or plain paste
- Two-thirds quince marmalade
- One-third currant jelly

## Directions

1. Roll puff or plain paste one-eighth inch thick, and cut in two and one-half inch squares.
2. Wet the corners, fold toward the centre, and press lightly.
3. Bake on a sheet.
4. When cool, press down the centres and fill, using two-thirds quince marmalade and one-third currant jelly.
