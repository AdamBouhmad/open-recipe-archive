## Ingredients

- four cold boiled potatoes
- six hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- pepper
- two cups Thin White Sauce
- buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut four cold boiled potatoes and six hard-boiled eggs in one-fourth inch slices.
2. Put layer of potatoes in buttered baking-dish, sprinkle with salt and pepper, cover with layer of eggs; repeat, and pour over two cups Thin White Sauce.
3. Cover with buttered cracker crumbs and bake until the crumbs are brown.
