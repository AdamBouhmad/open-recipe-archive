## Ingredients

- two boxes of sardines
- hot water
- pepper
- salt
- tiny pieces of lettuce
- buttered bread

## Directions

1. Take two boxes of sardines and throw the contents into hot water, having first drained away all the oil
2. A few minutes will free the sardines from grease
3. Pour away the water and dry the fish in a cloth
4. Scrape away the skins and pound the sardines in a mortar till reduced to paste
5. Add pepper, salt and some tiny pieces of lettuce
6. Spread on the sandwiches, which have been previously cut
7. Alternatively, chop the sardines up fine and squeeze a few drops of lemon juice into them, and spread between buttered bread or cold biscuits
