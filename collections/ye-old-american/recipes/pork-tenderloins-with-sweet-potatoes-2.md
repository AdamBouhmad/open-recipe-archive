## Ingredients

- pork tenderloins
- salt
- pepper
- sweet potatoes

## Directions

1. Wipe tenderloins, put in a dripping-pan, and brown quickly in a hot oven.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and bake forty-five minutes, basting every fifteen minutes.
3. Pare six potatoes and parboil ten minutes, drain, put in pan with meat, and cook until soft, basting when basting meat.
