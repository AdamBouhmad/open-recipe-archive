## Ingredients

- a quart of stewed pumpkin
- eight eggs
- a quart of rich cream
- a pound of sugar
- a quarter of an ounce of powdered mace
- two powdered nutmegs
- a table-spoonful of ground ginger
- thin paste
- puff-paste

## Directions

1. Take a quart of stewed pumpkin and put it into a sieve and press and strain it as dry as possible
2. Set it away to get cold
3. Beat eight eggs very light and stir them gradually into the pumpkin, a little at a time, in turn with a quart of rich cream and a pound of sugar
4. Mix together a quarter of an ounce of powdered mace, two powdered nutmegs, and a table-spoonful of ground ginger and stir them into the other ingredients
5. When all is mixed, stir the whole very hard
6. Cover the bottom of your pie-dishes with a thin paste and fill them nearly to the top with the mixture
7. Cut out narrow stripes of paste with your jagging-iron and lay them across the tops of your pies
8. Bake them from an hour to an hour and a quarter
9. Send them to table cool
