## Ingredients

- half pound butter
- one pound sugar
- ten eggs
- one glass wine
- half gill rose-water
- spices to your taste
- one and a quarter pound flour

## Directions

1. whip half pound butter to a cream
2. add 1 pound sugar, ten eggs, one glass wine, half gill rose-water
3. and spices to your taste
4. all worked into one and a quarter pound flour
5. put into pans, cover with paper
6. and bake in a quick well heat oven, 12 or 16 minutes
