## Ingredients

- good, dry flour
- milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix good, dry flour to a stiff dough with milk and salt.
2. Roll out thin.
3. Cut into round cakes and roll these again almost as thin as letter-paper.
4. Bake very quickly.
