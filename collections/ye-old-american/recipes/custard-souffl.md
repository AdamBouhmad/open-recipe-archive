## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- ¼ cup flour
- 1 cup scalded milk
- 4 eggs
- ¼ cup sugar

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add flour, and gradually hot milk
2. When well thickened, pour on to yolks of eggs beaten until thick and lemon-colored, and mixed with sugar
3. Cool, and cut and fold in whites of eggs beaten stiff and dry
4. Turn into buttered pudding-dish, and bake from thirty to thirty-five minutes in slow oven
5. Take from oven and serve at once,—if not served immediately it is sure to fall
6. Serve with Creamy or Foamy Sauce
