## Ingredients

- bread crumbs
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt
- brown gravy
- pickle

## Directions

1. Cut the loin in four pieces, take off the skin
2. Rub each piece with salt, wash them with the yelk of an egg, and cover them thickly with bread crumbs, chopped parsley, pepper and salt
3. Wrap them up securely in paper, put them on a bird spit, and roast them
4. Put a little brown gravy in the dish, and garnish with pickle
