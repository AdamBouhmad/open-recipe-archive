## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of powdered sugar
- half a cupful of butter
- one wine-glassful of brandy
- cinnamon
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Warm the butter slightly and work it to a light cream with the sugar
2. Add the brandy and spices
3. Beat it hard and set aside until wanted
4. Put into a mold to look nicely and serve on a flat dish
