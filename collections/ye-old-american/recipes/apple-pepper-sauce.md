## Ingredients

- 1 quart sifted apple
- 1 teacupful sugar
- 1 teaspoonful pepper
- 1 teaspoonful cloves
- 1 teaspoonful mustard
- 2 teaspoonfuls cinnamon
- 2 medium-sized onions, chopped very fine
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 1 pint vinegar

## Directions

1. Stir all together
2. Place over the fire and boil one hour
3. Bottle while hot and seal very tight
4. It should be about as thick as tomato catsup, so that it will just run from the bottle
