## Ingredients

- two cups of sugar
- half a cup of butter
- yolks of six eggs and one whole egg
- the grated rind and juice of a lemon or orange
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- half a cup of sweet milk
- four cups of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar.
2. Add the beaten yolks and the flour, beating hard for several minutes.
3. Dissolve half a teaspoonful of soda in half a cup of sweet milk and add.
4. Lastly, add the lemon or orange and bake, frosting if liked.
