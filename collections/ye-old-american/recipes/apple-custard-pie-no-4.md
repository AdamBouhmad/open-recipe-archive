## Ingredients

- sour apples
- three eggs per pie
- one cupful of butter per three pies
- one cupful of sugar per three pies
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel sour apples and stew until soft, and not much water left in them
2. rub through a colander
3. beat three eggs for each pie to be baked
4. put in at the rate of one cupful of butter and one of sugar for three pies
5. season with nutmeg
