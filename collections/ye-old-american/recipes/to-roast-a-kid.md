## Ingredients

- kid
- grated bread
- butter or suet
- sweet herbs
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- grated lemon-peel
- beaten egg
- lard or sweet oil
- salt and water
- cream
- butter
- flour
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Wash the kid well, wipe it dry, and truss it.
2. Stuff the body with a force-meat of grated bread, butter or suet, sweet herbs, pepper, salt, nutmeg, grated lemon-peel, and beaten egg; and sew it up to keep the stuffing in its place.
3. Put it on the spit and rub it over with lard, or sweet oil.
4. Put a little salt and water into the dripping-pan, and baste the kid first with that, and afterwards with its own gravy.
5. Or you may make it very nice by basting it with cream.
6. It should roast about three hours.
7. At the last, transfer the gravy to a small sauce-pan; thicken it with a little butter rolled in flour, give it a boil up, and send it to table in a boat.
8. Garnish the kid with lumps of currant jelly laid round the edge of the dish.
