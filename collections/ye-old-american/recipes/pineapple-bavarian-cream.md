## Ingredients

- ½ box gelatine or 2 tablespoons granulated gelatine
- ½ cup cold water
- 1 can grated pineapple
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Whip from 3 cups cream

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cold water
2. Heat pineapple, add sugar, lemon juice, and soaked gelatine
3. Chill in pan of ice-water, stirring constantly
4. When it begins to thicken, fold in whip from cream, mould, and chill
