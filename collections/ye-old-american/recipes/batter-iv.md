## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1½ teaspoons baking powder
- 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup milk
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients, add milk gradually, and egg well beaten.
