## Ingredients

- cranberries
- water
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Wash a quart of ripe cranberries and put them into a pan with about a wine-glass of water
2. Stew them slowly and stir them frequently, particularly after they begin to burst
3. They require a great deal of stewing and should be like a marmalade when done
4. After you take them from the fire, stir in a pound of brown sugar
5. When they are thoroughly done, put them into a deep dish and set them away to get cold
