## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- ¼ cup heavy cream

## Directions

1. Cream the butter
2. Add sugar gradually
3. Add vanilla and cream beaten until stiff
