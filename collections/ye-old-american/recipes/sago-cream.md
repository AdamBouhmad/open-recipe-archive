## Ingredients

- sago
- a stick of cinnamon
- water
- rich boiled custard
- sugar
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash the sago clean, and put it on the fire with a stick of cinnamon, and as much water as will boil it thick and soft
2. Take out the cinnamon, and add rich boiled custard till it is of a proper thickness
3. Sweeten it, and serve in glasses or cups, with grated nutmeg on the top
