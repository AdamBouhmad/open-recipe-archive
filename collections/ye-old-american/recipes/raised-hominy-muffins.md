## Ingredients

- 1 cup warm cooked hominy
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 cup scalded milk
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ yeast cake
- ¼ cup lukewarm water
- 3¼ cups flour

## Directions

1. Mix first five ingredients; when lukewarm add yeast cake, dissolved in lukewarm water and flour
2. Cover, and let rise over night
3. In the morning cut down, fill hot buttered gem pans two-thirds full, let rise one hour, and bake in a moderate oven
4. Unless cooked hominy is rather stiff more flour will be needed
