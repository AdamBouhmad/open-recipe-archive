## Ingredients

- hominy
- water
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Wash the hominy very clean through three or four waters.
2. Then put it into a pot (allowing two quarts of water to one quart of hominy) and boil it slowly five hours.
3. When done, take it up, and drain the liquid from it through a cullender.
4. Put the hominy into a deep dish, and stir into it a small piece of fresh butter.
