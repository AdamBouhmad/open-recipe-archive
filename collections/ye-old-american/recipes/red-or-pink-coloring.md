## Ingredients

- cochineal
- alcohol

## Directions

1. Take two cents' worth of cochineal
2. Lay it on a flat plate and bruise it with the blade of a knife
3. Put it into half a teacupful of alcohol
4. Let it stand a quarter of an hour
5. Filter it through fine muslin
6. Cork the bottle tight
