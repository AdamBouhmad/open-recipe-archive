## Ingredients

- fore-quarter of shote
- rich forcemeat
- pint of water
- two cloves of garlic
- pepper
- salt
- two gills of red wine
- two gills of mushroom catsup
- batter
- brown flour
- burnt sugar
- balls

## Directions

1. Make several incisions between the ribs, and stuff it with rich forcemeat
2. Put it in a pan with a pint of water, two cloves of garlic, pepper, salt, two gills of red wine, and two of mushroom catsup
3. Joint it, and the ribs cut across before it is cooked
4. Bake it, and thicken the gravy with batter and brown flour
5. Lay it in the dish with the ribs uppermost
6. If it be not sufficiently brown, add a little burnt sugar to the gravy
7. Garnish with balls
