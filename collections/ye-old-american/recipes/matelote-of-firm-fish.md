## Ingredients

- firm fish
- onion
- parsley
- thyme
- mushrooms
- spice
- pepper
- salt
- red wine
- water
- butter
- flour
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut the fish in pieces six inches long
2. Put it in a pot with onion, parsley, thyme, mushrooms, a little spice, pepper and salt
3. Add red wine and water enough for gravy
4. Set it on a quick fire and reduce it one-third
5. Thicken with a spoonful of butter and two of flour
6. Put it in a dish with bits of bread fried in butter
7. Pour the gravy over it
