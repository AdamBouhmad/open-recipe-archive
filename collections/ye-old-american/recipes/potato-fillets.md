## Ingredients

- potatoes
- cold water
- boiling lard

## Directions

1. Pare and slice the potatoes thin; cut them if you like in small fillets about a quarter of an inch square, and as long as the potato will admit.
2. Keep them in cold water until wanted.
3. Drop them into boiling lard.
4. When nearly done, take them out with a skimmer and drain them.
5. Boil up the lard again, drop the potatoes back and fry till done.
