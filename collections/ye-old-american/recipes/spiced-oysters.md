## Ingredients

- four hundred large oysters
- a pint of cider vinegar
- four grated nutmegs
- sixteen blades of whole mace
- six dozen of whole cloves
- three dozen whole pepper corns
- a salt-spoonful of cayenne

## Directions

1. Put the liquor into a porcelain kettle, and boil and skim it
2. When it has come to a hard boil, add the vinegar and put in the oysters with the seasoning of spices, &c.
3. Give them one boil up, for if boiled longer they will shrivel and lose their flavour
4. Put them into a stone or glass jar, cover them closely, and set them in a cool place
5. They must be quite cold when eaten
6. You may give them a light reddish tint by boiling in the liquor a little prepared cochineal
