## Ingredients

- 1 terrapin
- ¾ cup White Stock
- 1½ tablespoons wine
- Cayenne
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- Salt and pepper
- Yolks 2 eggs

## Directions

1. To stock and wine add terrapin meat, with bones cut in pieces and entrails cut in smaller pieces
2. Cook slowly until liquor is reduced one-half
3. Add liver separated in pieces, eggs, butter, salt, pepper, and cayenne
