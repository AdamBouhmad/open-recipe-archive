## Ingredients

- one cupful of finely-chopped beef suet
- two cupfuls of fine bread crumbs
- one heaping cupful of sugar
- one cupful of seeded raisins
- one cupful of well-washed currants
- one cupful of chopped blanched almonds
- half a cupful of citron, sliced thin
- a teaspoonful of salt
- one teaspoonful of cloves
- two teaspoonfuls of cinnamon
- half a grated nutmeg
- four well-beaten eggs
- a level teaspoonful of soda
- a tablespoonful of warm water
- a pint of flour
- one cupful of milk
- wine or brandy or any well-flavored sauce

## Directions

1. Flour the fruit thoroughly from a pint of flour
2. In a large bowl put the well-beaten eggs, sugar, spices and salt in one cupful of milk
3. Stir in the fruit, chopped nuts, bread crumbs and suet, one after the other, until all are used
4. Dissolve a level teaspoonful of soda in a tablespoonful of warm water and put in last
5. Add enough flour to make the fruit stick together, which will take all the pint
6. Boil or steam four hours
7. Serve with wine or brandy or any well-flavored sauce
