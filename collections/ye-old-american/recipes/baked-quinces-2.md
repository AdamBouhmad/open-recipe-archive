## Ingredients

- ripe quinces
- loaf sugar
- clear water

## Directions

1. Take ripe quinces, pare and quarter them, cut out the seeds
2. Stew them in clear water until a straw will pierce them
3. Put into a baking dish with half a cupful of loaf sugar to every eight quinces
4. Pour over them the liquor in which they were boiled
5. Cover closely and bake in the oven one hour
6. Take out the quinces and put them into a covered dish
7. Return the syrup to the saucepan and boil twenty minutes
8. Pour over the quinces and set them away to cool
