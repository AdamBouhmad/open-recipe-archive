## Ingredients

- One cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- a small teacupful of sweet milk
- half a grated nutmeg
- five cups of sifted flour
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix into a soft dough and cut into round cakes
2. roll the dough as thin as pie crust
3. Bake in a quick oven a light brown
