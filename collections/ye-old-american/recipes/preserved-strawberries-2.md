## Ingredients

- fruit
- refined sugar

## Directions

1. For every pound of fruit weigh a pound of refined sugar
2. Put them with the sugar over the fire in a porcelain kettle, bring to a boil slowly about twenty minutes
3. Take them out carefully with a perforated skimmer and fill your hot jars nearly full
4. Boil the juice a few minutes longer and fill up the jars
5. Seal them hot
6. Keep in a cool, dry place
