## Ingredients

- Apple sauce
- Stale bread
- Softened butter
- Sugar
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Cover bottom of a shallow baking-dish with apple sauce
2. Cut stale bread in one-third inch slices
3. Spread with softened butter, remove crusts, and cut in triangular-shaped pieces
4. Arrange closely together over apple
5. Sprinkle generously with sugar, to which is added a few drops vanilla
6. Bake in a moderate oven and serve with cream
