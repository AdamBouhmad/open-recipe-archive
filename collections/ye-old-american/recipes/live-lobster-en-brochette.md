## Ingredients

- Live lobster
- Small slices bacon
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Mustard
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Split a live lobster, remove meat from tail and large claws, cut in pieces, and arrange on skewers, alternating pieces with small slices of bacon.
2. Fry in deep fat and drain.
3. Cook liver of lobster with one tablespoon butter three minutes, season highly with mustard and cayenne, and serve with lobster.
