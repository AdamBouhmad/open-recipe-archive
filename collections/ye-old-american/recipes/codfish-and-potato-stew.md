## Ingredients

- Salt or fresh codfish
- Mashed potatoes
- Butter
- Milk
- Boiling water
- Pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Soak, boil, and pick the fish, if salt, as for fish-balls, or if fresh, boil and pick into bits
2. Add an equal quantity of mashed potatoes
3. Add a large tablespoonful of butter and milk, enough to make it very soft
4. Put into a skillet, and add a little boiling water to keep it from burning
5. Turn and toss constantly until it is smoking hot but not dry
6. Add pepper and parsley, and dish
