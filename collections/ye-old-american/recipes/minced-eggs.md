## Ingredients

- four or five hard-boiled eggs
- milk
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- savory
- flour
- sippets of toast

## Directions

1. Chop up four or five hard-boiled eggs; do not mince them too fine
2. Put over the fire in a suitable dish a cupful of milk, a tablespoonful of butter, salt and pepper, and some savory chopped small
3. When this comes to a boil stir into it a tablespoonful of flour, dissolved in a little cold milk
4. When it cooks thick like cream put in the minced eggs
5. Stir it gently around and around for a few moments and serve, garnished with sippets of toast
