## Ingredients

- collards or cabbage-sprouts
- salt
- pork

## Directions

1. Pick over carefully and lay in cold water, slightly salted, for half an hour
2. Shake in a cullender to drain
3. Put into boiling water, keeping at a fast boil until tender
4. Put the meat on first if using pork, adding the greens when it is parboiled, and cooking them together
5. Boil in an uncovered vessel
6. Drain very well, chop and heap in a dish, laying the meat on top
