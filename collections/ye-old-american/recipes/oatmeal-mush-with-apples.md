## Ingredients

- apples
- sugar
- water
- oatmeal mush
- cream

## Directions

1. Core apples, leaving large cavities; pare, and cook until soft in syrup made by boiling sugar and water together, allowing one cup sugar to one and one-half cups water.
2. Fill cavities with oatmeal mush; serve with sugar and cream.
3. The syrup should be saved and re-used.
