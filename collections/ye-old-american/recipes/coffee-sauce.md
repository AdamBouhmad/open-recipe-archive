## Ingredients

- 1½ cups milk
- ½ cup ground coffee
- ⅓ cup sugar
- ¾ tablespoon arrowroot
- Few grains salt

## Directions

1. Scald milk with coffee, and let stand twenty minutes.
2. Mix remaining ingredients, and pour on gradually the hot infusion which has been strained.
3. Cook five minutes, and serve hot.
