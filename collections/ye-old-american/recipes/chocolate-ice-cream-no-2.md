## Ingredients

- two eggs
- two cupfuls of sugar
- a pint of milk
- half a cupful of grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs very light and cream them with two cupfuls of sugar
2. Scald a pint of milk and turn on by degrees, mixing well with the sugar and eggs
3. Stir in half a cupful of grated chocolate
4. Return to the fire and heat until it thickens, stirring briskly
5. Take off and set aside to cool
6. When thoroughly cold, freeze
