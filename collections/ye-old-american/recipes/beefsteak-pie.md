## Ingredients

- rump or flank steak
- bone
- water
- cold boiled potatoes
- pie paste
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- butter
- brown flour
- egg

## Directions

1. Cut up rump or flank steak into strips two inches long and about an inch wide
2. Stew them with the bone in just enough water to cover until partly cooked
3. Slice half a dozen cold boiled potatoes
4. Line a baking-dish with pie paste
5. Put in a layer of the meat with salt, pepper, and a little thinly-sliced onion
6. Add a layer of sliced potatoes with bits of butter dotted over them
7. Alternate layers of steak and potatoes until the dish is full
8. Add the gravy or broth, first thickened with brown flour
9. Cover with a top crust, making a slit in the middle
10. Brush a little beaten egg over it
11. Bake until quite brown
