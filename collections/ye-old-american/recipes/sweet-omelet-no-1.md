## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of butter
- two of sugar
- one cupful of milk
- four eggs
- flour
- creamy sauce

## Directions

1. Let the milk come to a boil
2. Beat the flour and butter together
3. Add to them gradually the boiling milk and cook eight minutes, stirring often
4. Beat the sugar and the yolks of the eggs together
5. Add to the cooked mixture and set away to cool
6. When cool, beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth and add to the mixture
7. Bake in a buttered pudding-dish for twenty minutes in a moderate oven
8. Serve immediately with creamy sauce
