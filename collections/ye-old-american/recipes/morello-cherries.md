## Ingredients

- Morello cherries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take out the stones with a quill over a deep dish, to save the juice that runs from them
2. Put to the juice a pound of sugar for each pound of cherries, weighed after they are stoned
3. Boil and skim the syrup
4. Put in the fruit, and stew till quite clear
