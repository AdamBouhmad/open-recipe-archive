## Ingredients

- one large Boston cracker
- one teacupful of cold water
- one teacupful of fine white sugar
- juice and pulp of one lemon
- half a lemon rind grated
- a little nutmeg
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Crush finely with a rolling pin one large Boston cracker
2. put it into a bowl
3. pour upon it one teacupful of cold water
4. add one teacupful of fine white sugar, the juice and pulp of one lemon, half a lemon rind grated and a little nutmeg
5. line the pie-plate with half puff paste
6. pour in the mixture
7. cover with the paste
8. bake half an hour
