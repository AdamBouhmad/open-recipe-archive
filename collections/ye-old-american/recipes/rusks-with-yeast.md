## Ingredients

- one large coffeecup of warm milk
- half a cake of compressed yeast, or three tablespoonfuls of home-made yeast
- three well-beaten eggs
- one small cup of sugar
- one teaspoonful of salt
- flour
- dried currants

## Directions

1. Dissolve half a cake of compressed yeast, or three tablespoonfuls of home-made yeast, in one large coffeecup of warm milk
2. Add three well-beaten eggs, a small cup of sugar and a teaspoonful of salt
3. Beat these together
4. Use flour enough to make a smooth, light dough
5. Let it stand until very light
6. Knead it in the form of biscuits
7. Place them on buttered tins and let them rise until they are almost up to the edge of the tins
8. Pierce the top of each one
9. Glaze the top of each with sugar and milk, or the white of an egg, before baking
10. Bake in a quick oven
