## Ingredients

- 2 quarts clams
- ¼ cup cold water
- 4 cups consommé

## Directions

1. Wash two quarts clams in shell.
2. Put in kettle with one-fourth cup cold water, cover, and cook until shells open.
3. Strain liquor through double thickness cheese-cloth, add to four cups consommé, and clear.
