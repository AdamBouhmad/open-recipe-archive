## Ingredients

- dough from wheat bread
- 1 tea-cup powdered sugar
- 1 tea-cup butter
- 1 tea-cup warm milk
- 1 beaten egg
- flour for dredging

## Directions

1. When you are making wheat bread, and the dough is quite light and ready to bake, take out as much of it as would make a twelve cent loaf
2. Mix with it a tea-cup full of powdered sugar, and a tea-cup full of butter that has been softened and stirred about in a tea-cup of warm milk
3. Add also a beaten egg
4. Knead it very well, put it into a square pan, dredged with flour
5. Cover it, and set it near the fire for half an hour
6. Bake it in a moderate oven, and wrap it in a thick cloth as soon as it is done
