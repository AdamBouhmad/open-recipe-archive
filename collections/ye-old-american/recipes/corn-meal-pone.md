## Ingredients

- 1 quart Indian meal
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- A little lard, melted
- Cold water to make a soft dough

## Directions

1. Mould with the hands into thin oblong cakes
2. Lay in a well-greased pan
3. Bake very quickly
4. Alternatively mould into oval mounds, higher in the middle than at the ends, shaping these rapidly and lightly with the hands, by tossing the dough over and over
5. Break, not cut, and eat very hot
