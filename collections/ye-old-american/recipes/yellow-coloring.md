## Ingredients

- saffron
- cold soft water
- alcohol

## Directions

1. Take a little saffron, put it into an earthen vessel with a very small quantity of cold, soft water
2. Let it steep till the color of the infusion is a bright yellow
3. Strain it, add half alcohol to it
