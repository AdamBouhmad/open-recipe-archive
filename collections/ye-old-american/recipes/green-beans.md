## Ingredients

- young green or French beans
- salt and water
- vine or cabbage leaves
- alum, a little piece
- cider vinegar
- mace
- pepper, whole
- ginger, sliced

## Directions

1. String the beans, but do not cut them in pieces
2. Put them in salt and water for two days, stirring them frequently
3. Put them into a kettle with vine or cabbage leaves under, over, and all round them, adding a little piece of alum
4. Cover them closely to keep in the steam, and let them hang over a slow fire till they are a fine green
5. Drain them in a sieve
6. Make for them a pickle of cider vinegar, and boil in it for five minutes, some mace, whole pepper, and sliced ginger tied up in a thin muslin bag
7. Pour it hot upon the beans, put them into a stone jar, and tie them up
