## Ingredients

- one turtle
- two onions
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- juice of one lemon
- five quarts of water
- a glass of Madeira
- four quarts of water
- pepper and salt
- brown flour
- turtle eggs

## Directions

1. After removing the entrails, cut up the coarser parts of the turtle meat and bones.
2. Add four quarts of water.
3. Stew four hours with the herbs, onions, pepper and salt.
4. Stew very slowly, do not let it cease boiling during this time.
5. At the end of four hours strain the soup.
6. Add the finer parts of the turtle and the green fat, which has been simmered one hour in two quarts of water.
7. Thicken with brown flour.
8. Return to the soup-pot, and simmer gently for an hour longer.
9. If there are eggs in the turtle, boil them in a separate vessel for four hours, and throw into the soup before taking up.
10. Add the juice of the lemon, and the wine.
11. Beat up at once and pour out.
