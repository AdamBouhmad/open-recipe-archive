## Ingredients

- package of gelatine
- pint of cold water
- juice of four lemons
- rind of one lemon
- pint of boiling water
- pinch of cinnamon
- three cups of sugar

## Directions

1. To a package of gelatine add a pint of cold water, the juice of four lemons and the rind of one
2. Let it stand one hour
3. Add one pint of boiling water, a pinch of cinnamon, three cups of sugar
4. Let it all come to a boil
5. Strain through a napkin into molds, set away to get cold
6. Nice poured over sliced bananas and oranges
