## Ingredients

- 1 cup scalded milk
- ½ cup white corn meal
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Add salt to corn meal, and pour on gradually milk.
2. Turn into a buttered shallow pan to the depth of one-fourth inch.
3. Bake in a moderate oven until crisp.
4. Split and spread with butter.
