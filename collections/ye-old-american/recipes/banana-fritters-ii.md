## Ingredients

- 3 bananas
- 1 cup bread flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients.
2. Beat egg until light, add milk, and combine mixtures; then add lemon juice and banana fruit forced through a sieve.
3. Drop by spoonfuls, fry in deep fat, and drain.
4. Serve with Lemon Sauce.
