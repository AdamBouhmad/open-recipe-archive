## Ingredients

- 6 Boston crackers, split
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- A good pinch of salt
- a little nutmeg
- boiling water to cover them well

## Directions

1. Split the crackers
2. Pile in a bowl in layers, salt and sugar scattered among them
3. Cover with boiling water
4. Set on the hearth with a close top over the bowl for at least one hour
5. The crackers should be almost clear and soft as jelly, but not broken
6. Eat from the bowl, with more sugar sprinkled in if desired
