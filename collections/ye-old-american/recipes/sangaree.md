## Ingredients

- 1/3 wine, ale, or porter
- 2/3 water
- loaf-sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix in a pitcher or in tumblers one-third of wine, ale, or porter, with two-thirds of water either warm or cold
2. Stir in sufficient loaf-sugar to sweeten it
3. Grate some nutmeg into it
