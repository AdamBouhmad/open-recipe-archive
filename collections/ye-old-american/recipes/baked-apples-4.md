## Ingredients

- nice fruit, a little tart and juicy, but not sour
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Get nice fruit, a little tart and juicy, but not sour; clean them nicely, and bake in a moderate oven--regulated so as to have them done in about an hour
2. When the skin cracks and the pulp breaks through in every direction they are done and ready to take out
3. Serve with white sugar sprinkled over them
