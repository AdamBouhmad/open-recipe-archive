## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- half a cup of butter
- three eggs
- one teaspoonful of vanilla extract
- four cups of sifted flour
- one cup of raisins
- one cup of currants
- half a teaspoonful of baking soda
- milk

## Directions

1. Beat one cup of sugar and half a cup of butter to a smooth cream
2. Add three well-beaten eggs, a teaspoonful of vanilla extract, four cups of sifted flour, one cup of raisins and one of currants, half a teaspoonful of baking soda dissolved in a little water, and milk enough to make a stiff batter
3. Drop this batter in drops on well-buttered tins and bake in a quick oven
