## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter (scant)
- 1½ cups flour
- 2 tablespoons yellow ginger
- 5 eggs
- 1½ cups powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, and add flour gradually, mixed and sifted with ginger.
2. Beat the yolks of the eggs until thick and lemon-colored, and add sugar gradually.
3. Combine mixtures, add whites of eggs, beaten until stiff, and sift over baking powder.
4. Beat thoroughly, turn into a buttered deep cake pan, and bake one hour in a moderate oven.
