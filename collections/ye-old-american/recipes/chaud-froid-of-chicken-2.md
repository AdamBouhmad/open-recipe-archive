## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup White Stock
- yolk one egg
- 2 tablespoons cream
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- salt and pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon granulated gelatine
- one tablespoon hot water
- aspic jelly
- truffles
- 6 pieces cooked chicken

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of butter, flour, and stock.
2. Add egg yolk diluted with cream, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
3. Add dissolved gelatine.
4. Dip chicken in sauce which has been allowed to cool.
5. When chicken has cooled, garnish upper side with truffles cut in shapes.
6. Brush over with aspic.
