## Ingredients

- cucumbers suitable for the table
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take cucumbers suitable for the table; peel and grate them, salt a little, and put in a bag to drain over night.
2. In the morning season to taste with salt, pepper and vinegar, put in small jars and seal tight for fall or winter use.
