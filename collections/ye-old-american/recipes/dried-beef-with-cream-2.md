## Ingredients

- ¼ lb. smoked dried beef, thinly sliced
- 1 cup scalded cream
- 1½ tablespoons flour
- cold water
- White Sauce I

## Directions

1. Remove skin and separate meat in pieces
2. cover with hot water
3. let stand ten minutes
4. drain
5. Dilute flour with enough cold water to pour easily, making a smooth paste
6. add to cream
7. cook in double boiler ten minutes
8. Add beef
9. reheat
10. One cup White Sauce I may be used in place of cream, omitting the salt
