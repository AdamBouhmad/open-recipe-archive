## Ingredients

- a peck of tomatos
- salt
- half a pint of chopped onions
- half a quarter of an ounce of mace
- one table-spoonful of whole black pepper

## Directions

1. Gather a peck of tomatos, pick out the stems, and wash them
2. Put them on the fire without water, sprinkle on a few spoonsful of salt
3. Let them boil steadily an hour, stirring them frequently
4. Strain them through a colander, and then through a sieve
5. Put the liquid on the fire with half a pint of chopped onions, half a quarter of an ounce of mace broke into small pieces
6. Add salt if not sufficiently salt, and one table-spoonful of whole black pepper
7. Boil all together until just enough to fill two bottles
8. Cork it tight
9. Make it in August, in dry weather
