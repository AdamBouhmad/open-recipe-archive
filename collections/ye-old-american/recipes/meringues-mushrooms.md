## Ingredients

- Meringue Glacée mixture
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Shape Meringue Glacée mixture in rounds the size of mushroom caps, using pastry bag and tube.
2. Sprinkle with grated chocolate.
3. Shape stems like mushroom stems.
4. Bake.
5. Remove from paper and place caps on stems.
