## Ingredients

- ripe berries
- 1 quart of water to 2 quarts of berries
- 1 lb. sugar for 3 quarts of liquor

## Directions

1. Mash ripe berries to a pulp; put into a stone jar
2. Add water
3. Stir well and let it stand two days
4. Strain through a double flannel bag
5. Mash a second supply of berries, equal in quantity to the first, and cover with this liquid
6. Steep two days more
7. Strain and add sugar
8. Boil five minutes
9. Let it ferment in lightly covered jars
10. Rack off and bottle
