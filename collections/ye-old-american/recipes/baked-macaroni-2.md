## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup macaroni
- 2 quarts boiling water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups White Sauce
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups scalded milk
- buttered crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water twenty minutes or until soft
2. Drain in strainer
3. Pour over it cold water to prevent pieces from adhering
4. Reheat in White Sauce
5. Put in buttered baking dish
6. Cover with buttered crumbs
7. Bake until crumbs are brown
