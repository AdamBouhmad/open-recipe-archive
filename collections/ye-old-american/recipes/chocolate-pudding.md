## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- Yolks 2 eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 1⅜ cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- Whites 2 eggs
- 1⅓ squares Baker's chocolate
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, and add one-half the sugar gradually
2. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored, and add, gradually, remaining sugar
3. Combine mixtures, and add milk alternately with flour mixed and sifted with baking powder and salt
4. Add whites of eggs beaten until stiff, melted chocolate, and vanilla
5. Bake in an angel-cake pan, remove from pan, cool
6. Fill the centre with whipped cream, sweetened and flavored
