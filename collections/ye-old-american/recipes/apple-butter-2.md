## Ingredients

- cider
- juicy apples
- spices (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil down a kettleful of cider to two-thirds the original quantity
2. Pare, core, and slice juicy apples, and put as many into the cider as it will cover
3. Boil slowly, stirring often with a flat stick, and when the apples are tender to breaking, take them out with a perforated skimmer, draining well against the sides of the kettle
4. Put in a second supply of apples and stew them soft, as many as the cider will hold
5. Take from the fire, pour all together into a tub or large crock; cover and let it stand twelve hours
6. Return to the kettle and boil down, stirring all the while until it is the consistency of soft soap, and brown in color
7. You may spice to taste if you please
8. Keep in stone jars in a dry, cool place
