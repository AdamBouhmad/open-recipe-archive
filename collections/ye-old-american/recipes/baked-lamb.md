## Ingredients

- shank bone
- joints of the loin
- kidney
- pepper
- salt
- cloves of garlic
- water
- ripe tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the shank bone from a hind-quarter, separate the joints of the loin
2. Lay it in a pan with the kidney uppermost
3. Sprinkle some pepper and salt
4. Add a few cloves of garlic, a pint of water and a dozen large ripe tomatoes with the skins taken off
5. Bake it but do not let it be burnt
6. Thicken the gravy with a little butter and brown flour
