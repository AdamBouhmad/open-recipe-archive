## Ingredients

- whites of three eggs
- a pint of thick sweet cream
- four tablespoonfuls of sweet wine
- three tablespoonfuls of fine white sugar
- a teaspoonful of the extract of lemon or vanilla
- jelly or jam
- sliced sponge cake

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of three eggs to a stiff froth
2. Add a pint of thick sweet cream (previously set where it is very cold)
3. Add four tablespoonfuls of sweet wine, three tablespoonfuls of fine white sugar and a teaspoonful of the extract of lemon or vanilla
4. Mix all the ingredients together on a board platter or pan
5. Whip it to a standing froth
6. As the froth rises, take it off lightly with a spoon and lay it on an inverted sieve with a dish under it to catch what will drain through
7. What drains through can be beaten over again
8. Serve in a glass dish with jelly or jam and sliced sponge cake
9. This should be whipped in a cool place and set in the ice box
