## Ingredients

- one dozen pods of pepper
- three pints of vinegar

## Directions

1. Get one dozen pods of pepper when ripe, take out the stems, and cut them in two
2. Put them in a kettle with three pints of vinegar
3. Boil it away to one quart, and strain it through a sieve
