## Ingredients

- small fresh button mushrooms
- one quarter of a pound of butter
- two whole cloves
- a saltspoonful of salt
- a tablespoonful of lemon juice
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe free from grit the small fresh button mushrooms
2. Put into a frying pan a quarter of a pound of the very best butter
3. Add to it two whole cloves, a saltspoonful of salt and a tablespoonful of lemon juice
4. When hot add a quart of the small mushrooms
5. Toss them about in the butter for a moment only
6. Put them in jars
7. Fill the top of each jar with an inch or two of the butter and let it cool
8. Keep the jars in a cool place
9. When the butter is quite firm add a top layer of salt
10. Cover to keep out dust
