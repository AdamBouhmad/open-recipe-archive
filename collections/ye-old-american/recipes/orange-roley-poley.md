## Ingredients

- light paste
- sweet oranges, peeled, sliced, and seeded
- white sugar
- grated yellow peel

## Directions

1. Make a light paste as for apple dumplings or valise pudding, roll in an oblong sheet, and lay oranges, thickly all over it.
2. Sprinkle with white sugar; scatter a teaspoonful or two of the grated yellow peel over all and roll up closely, folding down the end to secure the syrup.
3. Boil in a pudding-cloth one hour and a half.
4. Eat with lemon sauce.
