## Ingredients

- half a pound suet
- one and a quarter pound flour
- salt
- half a pint milk
- butter
- beef steaks or mutton-chops
- rich gravy

## Directions

1. Chop half a pound of suet very fine
2. Add one and a quarter pound of flour and a little salt
3. Mix it up with half a pint of milk
4. Knead it till it looks light
5. Take a bowl of proper size and rub the inside with butter
6. Roll out the paste and lay it in
7. Parboil beef steaks, mutton-chops, or any kind of meat you like
8. Season it and lay it in the bowl
9. Fill it with rich gravy
10. Close the paste over the top
11. Get a very thick cloth that will keep out the water
12. Wet and flour it
13. Place it over the top of the bowl
14. Gather it at bottom and tie it very securely
15. Put it in boiling water
16. When done, dip the top in cold water for a moment, that the cloth may not stick to the paste
17. Untie and take it off carefully
18. Put a dish on the bowl and turn it over
19. Have gravy in a boat to eat with it
