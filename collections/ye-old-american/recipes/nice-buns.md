## Ingredients

- four ounces of sugar
- three quarters of a pound of flour
- two spoonsful of yeast
- half a pint of milk
- four ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Put four ounces of sugar with three quarters of a pound of flour
2. make it up with two spoonsful of yeast, and half a pint of milk
3. when well risen, work into it four ounces of butter
4. make it into small buns
5. bake them in a quick oven
6. do not burn them
