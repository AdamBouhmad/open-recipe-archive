## Ingredients

- smelts
- flour
- fresh lard or butter

## Directions

1. Before they are cooked, cut off the heads and tails.
2. Sprinkle the smelts with flour, and have ready in a frying pan over the fire plenty of fresh lard or butter.
3. When it boils, put in the fish and fry them.
