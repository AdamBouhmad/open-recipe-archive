## Ingredients

- potatoes
- cold water

## Directions

1. Pare the potatoes, throw them into cold water as they are peeled.
2. Put them in a steamer.
3. Place the steamer over a saucepan of boiling water.
4. Steam the potatoes from twenty to forty minutes, according to the size and sort.
5. When the fork goes easily through them, they are done; then take them up, dish and serve very quickly.
