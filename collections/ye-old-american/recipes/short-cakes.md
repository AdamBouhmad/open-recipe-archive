## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound of fresh butter
- a pound and a half of sifted flour
- a little cold water

## Directions

1. Rub three quarters of a pound of fresh butter into a pound and a half of sifted flour
2. Make it into a dough with a little cold water
3. Roll it out into a sheet half an inch thick, and cut it into round cakes with the edge of a tumbler
4. Prick them with a fork; lay them in a shallow iron pan sprinkled with flour
5. Bake them in a moderate oven till they are brown
6. Send them to table hot; split and butter them
