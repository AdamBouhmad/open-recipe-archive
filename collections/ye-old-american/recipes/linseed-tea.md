## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of linseed
- half a pint of cold water
- lemon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put one tablespoonful of linseed into a stewpan with half a pint of cold water
2. Place the stewpan over a moderate fire, and when the water is quite warm, pour it off
3. Add to the linseed half a pint of fresh cold water
4. Let the whole boil three or four minutes
5. Season it with lemon and sugar
