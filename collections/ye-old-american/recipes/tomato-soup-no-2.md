## Ingredients

- a quart of peeled tomatoes
- a pinch of soda
- a quart of hot boiled milk
- salt and pepper
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- three tablespoonfuls of rolled cracker

## Directions

1. Place over the fire a quart of peeled tomatoes.
2. Stew them soft with a pinch of soda.
3. Strain it so that no seeds remain.
4. Set it over the fire again.
5. Add a quart of hot boiled milk.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Add a piece of butter the size of an egg.
8. Add three tablespoonfuls of rolled cracker.
9. Serve hot.
10. Canned tomatoes may be used in place of fresh ones.
