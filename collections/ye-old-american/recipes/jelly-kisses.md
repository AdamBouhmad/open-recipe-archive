## Ingredients

- kiss mixture
- currant jelly or jam

## Directions

1. Having made the kisses, heap them in the shape of half an egg, placed upon stiff letter paper lining the bottom of a thick baking pan
2. Put them in a moderate oven until the outside is a little hardened
3. Take one off carefully, take out the soft inside with the handle of a spoon, and put it back with the mixture, to make more
4. Lay the shell down and take another and prepare it likewise
5. Fill the shells with currant jelly or jam
6. Join two together, cementing them with some of the mixture
7. Continue until you have enough
8. Make kisses the day before they are wanted
9. Tint half of them pale pink, then unite white and pink
10. Serve on a high glass dish
