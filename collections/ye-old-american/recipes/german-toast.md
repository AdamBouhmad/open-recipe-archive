## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 6 slices stale bread

## Directions

1. Beat eggs slightly, add salt, sugar, and milk; strain into a shallow dish.
2. Soak bread in mixture until soft.
3. Cook on a hot, well-greased griddle; brown on one side, turn and brown other side.
4. Serve for breakfast or luncheon, or with a sauce for dessert.
