## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- ½ tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- ¾ tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put butter in a bowl, and with small wooden spoon work until creamy
2. Add salt, pepper, and parsley, then lemon juice very slowly
