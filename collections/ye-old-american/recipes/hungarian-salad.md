## Ingredients

- shredded fresh pineapple
- bananas
- tangerines
- French dressing
- paprika
- lettuce leaves
- banana skins

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts shredded fresh pineapple, bananas cut in pieces, and sections of tangerines, and marinate with French dressing.
2. Fill banana skins with mixture, sprinkle generously with paprika, and arrange on lettuce leaves.
