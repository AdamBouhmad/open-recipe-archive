## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- The weight of the eggs in sugar
- Half their weight in flour
- ¼ teaspoonful soda
- ½ teaspoonful cream-tartar
- caramel icing

## Directions

1. Sift ¼ teaspoonful soda and ½ teaspoonful cream-tartar well with the flour.
2. Prepare small tins, about five inches long and two wide, round at the bottom, and kept firm by strips of tin connecting them (or tack stiff writing-paper into the same shape, stitching each little canoe to its neighbor after the manner of a pontoon bridge).
3. Have these made and buttered before you mix the cake.
4. Put a spoonful of batter in each, and bake in a steady oven.
5. When nearly cold, cover the rounded side with a caramel icing, made according to the foregoing receipt.
