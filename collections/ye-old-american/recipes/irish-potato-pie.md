## Ingredients

- 1 lb. mashed potato, rubbed through a cullender
- ½ lb. butter—creamed with the sugar
- 6 eggs—whites and yolks separately
- 1 lemon—squeezed into the potato while hot
- 1 cup of milk
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful mace
- 2 cups white sugar

## Directions

1. Mix as you do sweet potato pudding, and bake in open shells of paste.
2. To be eaten cold.
