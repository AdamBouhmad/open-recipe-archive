## Ingredients

- seven eggs
- a pint of cream
- spinach juice
- juice of tansey
- a quarter of a pound of powdered crackers or pounded rice
- a glass of wine
- nutmeg
- sugar
- paste

## Directions

1. Beat seven eggs very light
2. Mix with them a pint of cream, and nearly as much spinach juice, with a little juice of tansey
3. Add a quarter of a pound of powdered crackers or pounded rice made fine, a glass of wine, some grated nutmeg and sugar
4. Stir it over the fire to thicken
5. Pour it into a paste and bake it, or fry it like an omelette
