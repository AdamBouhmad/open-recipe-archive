## Ingredients

- fowls
- celery
- Mayonnaise dressing
- celery tips
- cold hard-boiled eggs
- lettuce leaves
- cold boiled beets
- capers
- olives

## Directions

1. Boil the fowls tender and remove all the fat, gristle and skin
2. Mince the meat in small pieces, but do not hash it
3. To one chicken put twice and a half its weight in celery, cut in pieces of about one-quarter of an inch
4. Mix thoroughly and set it in a cool place--the ice chest
5. Prepare a Mayonnaise dressing
6. When ready for the table pour this dressing over the chicken and celery, tossing and mixing it thoroughly
7. Set it in a cool place until ready to serve
8. Garnish with celery tips, or cold hard-boiled eggs, lettuce leaves from the heart, cold boiled beets or capers, olives
