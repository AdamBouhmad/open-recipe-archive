## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. knuckle of veal
- 1 lb. lean beef
- 3 quarts boiling water
- 1 onion
- 6 slices carrot
- 1 large stalk celery
- ½ teaspoon peppercorns
- ½ bay leaf
- 2 sprigs thyme
- 2 cloves

## Directions

1. Wipe veal, remove from bone, and cut in small pieces.
2. Cut beef in pieces.
3. Put bone and meat in soup kettle, cover with cold water, and bring quickly to boiling-point; drain, throw away the water.
4. Wash thoroughly bones and meat in cold water; return to kettle.
5. Add vegetables, seasonings, and three quarts boiling water.
6. Boil three or four hours; the stock should be reduced one half.
