## Ingredients

- fish
- cheese-cloth
- small lobster claw
- thin slice of canned mushroom
- parsley
- thin circular slice of truffle
- one and one-half pound lobster
- cold water
- one-half small onion
- sprig of parsley
- bit of bay leaf
- one-fourth teaspoon peppercorns
- three tablespoons butter
- three tablespoons flour
- one-half cup heavy cream
- yolks of two eggs
- salt
- lemon juice
- paprika
- Lobster Sauce III

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook fish same as for Halibut à la Martin.
2. Insert tip of small lobster claw in each fillet, and garnish with a thin slice of canned mushroom sprinkled with parsley and a thin circular slice of truffle.
3. For Lobster Sauce III: Remove meat from a one and one-half pound lobster and cut claw meat in cubes.
4. Cover remaining meat and body bones with cold water.
5. Add one-half small onion, sprig of parsley, bit of bay leaf, and one-fourth teaspoon peppercorns, and cook until stock is reduced to one cup.
6. Melt three tablespoons butter, add three tablespoons flour, and pour on gradually the stock.
7. Then add one-half cup heavy cream and yolks of two eggs.
8. Season with salt, lemon juice, and paprika; then add lobster cubes.
9. Serve with Lobster Sauce III.
