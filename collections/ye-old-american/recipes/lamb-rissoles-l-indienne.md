## Ingredients

- puff paste
- prepared lamb mixture
- egg yolk
- cold water
- butter
- onion juice
- flour
- lamb stock
- salt
- paprika
- curry powder
- roast lamb

## Directions

1. Roll puff paste one-eighth inch thick and shape, using circular cutters of different sizes.
2. On the centres of smaller pieces put one tablespoon prepared lamb mixture, wet edges, cover with large pieces, press edges firmly together.
3. Prick upper paste in several places, brush over with yolk of egg diluted with one teaspoon cold water.
4. Bake in hot oven.
5. For lamb filling: Cook three tablespoons butter, with a few drops onion juice, until well browned.
6. Add one-fourth cup flour, and brown butter and flour.
7. Add one cup lamb stock and season highly with salt, paprika, and curry powder.
8. To one-half the sauce, add two-thirds cup cold roast lamb cut in one-third inch cubes.
9. Add stock to remaining sauce, and pour around rissoles just before sending to table.
