## Ingredients

- 1 large cup of sugar
- Nearly half a cup of butter
- 1 egg
- 1 lemon—all the juice and half the grated peel
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg
- 3 tablespoonfuls boiling water

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar and beat in the egg whipped light; the lemon and nutmeg.
2. Beat hard ten minutes, and add, a spoonful at a time, the boiling water.
3. Put in a tin pail and set within the uncovered top of the tea-kettle, which you must keep boiling until the steam heats the sauce very hot, but not to boiling.
4. Stir constantly.
