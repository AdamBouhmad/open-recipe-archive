## Ingredients

- chopped ham or ground ham
- fine bread-crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- minced parsley
- melted butter
- milk
- patty-pans or scallop-shells
- egg
- powdered cracker

## Directions

1. Make a force-meat of chopped ham—ground is better—fine bread-crumbs, pepper, salt, a little minced parsley, and some melted butter.
2. Moisten with milk to a soft paste, and half fill some patty-pans or scallop-shells with the mixture.
3. Break an egg carefully upon the top of each, dust with pepper and salt, and sift some very finely powdered cracker over all.
4. Set in the oven, and bake until the eggs are well set—about eight minutes.
5. Eat hot.
