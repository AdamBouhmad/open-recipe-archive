## Ingredients

- white of one egg
- powdered sugar
- walnut meats
- two tablespoonfuls of maple sugar
- one tablespoonful of water

## Directions

1. Beat the white of one egg to a stiff froth
2. Stir in enough powdered sugar to make it like hard frosting
3. Make a syrup by boiling for two or three minutes two tablespoonfuls of maple sugar in one of water
4. Dip the walnut meats in the syrup
5. Press some of the hard frosting between the two halves of the walnut and let it harden
