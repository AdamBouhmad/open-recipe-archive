## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- lemon-peel
- brown sugar
- butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and slice some fine apples
2. Put them into a sauce-pan with just sufficient water to keep them from burning and some grated lemon-peel
3. Stew them till quite soft and tender
4. Mash them to a paste
5. Make them very sweet with brown sugar
6. Add a small piece of butter and some nutmeg
