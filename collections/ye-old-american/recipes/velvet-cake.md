## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1½ cups sugar
- Yolks 4 eggs
- ½ cup cold water
- 1½ cups flour
- ½ cup corn-starch
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- Whites 4 eggs
- ⅓ cup almonds, blanched, and shredded
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, yolks of eggs well beaten, and water.
2. Mix and sift flour, corn-starch, and baking powder, and add to first mixture; then add whites of eggs beaten until stiff.
3. After putting in pan, cover with almonds and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
4. Bake forty minutes in a moderate oven.
