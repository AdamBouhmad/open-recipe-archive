## Ingredients

- Young turnips
- Tablespoonful of butter
- White sauce made of butter drawn in milk

## Directions

1. Pare smoothly, and trim all into the same size and shape.
2. Lay in cold water half an hour.
3. Put on in boiling water, with a tablespoonful of butter, and stew until tender.
4. Drain dry, without crushing or breaking them.
5. Pile in a deep dish, and cover with a white sauce made of butter drawn in milk.
6. Serve very hot.
