## Ingredients

- Potato Croquette mixture
- Creamed Chicken, shrimp, or peas
- crumbs
- egg
- deep fat

## Directions

1. Make Potato Croquette mixture, omitting parsley.
2. Shape in small nests and fill with Creamed Chicken, shrimp, or peas.
3. Cover nests with Croquette mixture, then roll in form of croquettes.
4. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again; fry in deep fat, and drain on brown paper.
