## Ingredients

- 5 gallons of water
- 2 gallons of whiskey
- 1 quart of strong yeast
- 2 pounds of powdered charcoal

## Directions

1. Put into a cask a mixture composed of five gallons of water, two gallons of whiskey, and a quart of strong yeast
2. Stir in two pounds of powdered charcoal
3. Place it where it will ferment properly
4. Leave the bung loose till the fermentation is over, but cover the hole slightly to keep out the dust and insects
5. At the end of four months draw it off
6. You will have a fine vinegar, as clear and colourless as water
