## Ingredients

- boiling water
- salt
- eggs
- toasted bread
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Have one quart of boiling water and one tablespoonful of salt in a frying pan
2. Break the eggs, one by one, into a saucer, and slide carefully into the salted water
3. Dash with a spoon a little water over the egg, to keep the top white
4. Cook until the white is firm
5. Lift out with a griddle cake turner and place on toasted bread
6. Serve immediately
