## Ingredients

- salt fish
- mashed potatoes or cold boiled chopped potatoes
- butter
- warm milk
- sifted flour
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Soak, boil and pick the fish, the same as for fish-balls
2. Add an equal quantity of mashed potatoes, or cold, boiled, chopped potatoes
3. Add a large piece of butter, and warm milk enough to make it quite soft
4. Put it into a buttered dish
5. Rub butter over the top
6. Shake over a little sifted flour
7. Bake about thirty minutes, and until a rich brown
8. Make a sauce of drawn butter, with two hard-boiled eggs sliced, served in a gravy boat
