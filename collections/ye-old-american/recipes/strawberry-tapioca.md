## Ingredients

- a large teacupful of tapioca
- cold water
- sugar
- a quart of berries
- water

## Directions

1. Soak over night a large teacupful of tapioca in cold water
2. In the morning, put half of it in a buttered yellow-ware baking-dish, or any suitable pudding-dish
3. Sprinkle sugar over the tapioca
4. Put a quart of berries and sugar and the rest of the tapioca on this
5. Fill the dish with water, which should cover the tapioca about a quarter of an inch
6. Bake in a moderately hot oven until it looks clear
7. Eat cold with cream or custard
