## Ingredients

- fine firm cabbage of a deep red or purple colour
- salt
- cider vinegar
- cloves
- allspice
- mace
- cochineal

## Directions

1. Take a fine firm cabbage of a deep red or purple colour, strip off the outer leaves, and cut out the stalk
2. Quarter the cabbage lengthways, and then slice it crossways
3. Lay it in a deep dish, sprinkle a handful of salt over it, cover it with another dish, and let it lie twenty-four hours
4. Drain it in a cullender from the salt, and wipe it dry
5. Make a pickle of sufficient cider vinegar to cover the cabbage well, adding to it equal quantities of cloves and allspice, with some mace and a little cochineal to give it a good red colour
6. Boil the vinegar and spices hard for five minutes
7. Put the cabbage into a stone jar and pour the vinegar over it boiling hot
8. Cover the jar with a cloth till it gets cold, then put in a large cork, and tie a leather over it
