## Ingredients

- ten gallons of water
- a quarter of a pound of hops
- a tea-cupful of ginger
- six quarts of molasses
- three ounces and a half of the essence of spruce
- half a pint of good strong yeast

## Directions

1. Put into a large kettle ten gallons of water, a quarter of a pound of hops, and a tea-cupful of ginger
2. Boil them together till all the hops sink to the bottom
3. Dip out a bucket full of the liquor, and stir into it six quarts of molasses, and three ounces and a half of the essence of spruce
4. When all is dissolved, mix it with the liquor in the kettle
5. Strain it through a hair sieve into a cask
6. Stir well into it half a pint of good strong yeast
7. Let it ferment a day or two
8. Bung up the cask, and you may bottle the beer the next day
9. It will be fit for use in a week
