## Ingredients

- sound large cherries
- vinegar
- sugar
- whole cloves
- blades of mace

## Directions

1. Select sound, large cherries, as large as you can get them
2. To every quart of cherries allow a large cupful of vinegar, two tablespoonfuls of sugar, a dozen whole cloves, and half a dozen blades of mace
3. Put the vinegar and sugar on to heat with the spices and boil five minutes
4. Turn out into a covered stoneware vessel and let it get perfectly cold
5. Pack the cherries into jars, and pour the vinegar over them when cold
6. Cork tightly and set away
7. They are fit for use almost immediately
