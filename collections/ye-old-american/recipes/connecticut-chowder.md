## Ingredients

- 4 lb. cod or haddock
- 4 cups potatoes cut in ¾-inch cubes
- 1½-inch cube fat salt pork
- 1 sliced onion
- 2½ cups stewed and strained tomatoes
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ⅔ cup cracker crumbs
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare same as Fish Chowder, using liquor drained from bones for cooking potatoes, instead of additional water.
2. Use tomatoes in place of milk and add cracker crumbs just before serving.
