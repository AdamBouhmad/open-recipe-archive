## Ingredients

- equal quantities of mashed potatoes and of flour, one quart of each
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- a little salt
- milk
- half a teacupful of fresh yeast
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- warm water

## Directions

1. Potato-cakes to be served with roast lamb or with game, are made of equal quantities of mashed potatoes and of flour, say one quart of each, two tablespoonfuls of butter, a little salt and milk enough to make a batter as for griddle-cakes
2. to this allow half a teacupful of fresh yeast
3. let it rise till it is light and bubbles of air form
4. dissolve half a teaspoonful of soda in a spoonful of warm water and add to the batter
5. bake in muffin tins
6. take them from the tins and drop in the gravy just before sending to the table
