## Ingredients

- two quarts of lemonade made with pure lemon juice
- one tablespoonful of extract of lemon
- half a pint of brandy per quart of ice
- half a pint of Jamaica rum per quart of ice

## Directions

1. Make two quarts of lemonade, rich with pure juice lemon fruit
2. Add one tablespoonful of extract of lemon
3. Work well and freeze
4. Just before serving, add for each quart of ice half a pint of brandy and half a pint of Jamaica rum
5. Mix well and serve in high glasses
