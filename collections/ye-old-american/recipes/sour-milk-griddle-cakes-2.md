## Ingredients

- 2½ cups flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 cups sour milk
- 1¼ teaspoons soda
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix and sift flour, salt, and soda; add sour milk, and egg well beaten.
2. Drop by spoonfuls on a greased hot griddle; cook on one side.
3. When puffed, full of bubbles, and cooked on edges, turn, and cook other side.
4. Serve with butter and maple syrup.
