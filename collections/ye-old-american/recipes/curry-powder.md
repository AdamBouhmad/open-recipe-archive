## Ingredients

- One ounce turmeric
- one ounce coriander seed
- one ounce cummin seed
- one ounce white ginger
- one ounce nutmeg
- one ounce mace
- one ounce Cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Pound all together, and pass them through a fine sieve
2. Bottle and cork it well
3. One tea-spoonful is sufficient to season any made dish
