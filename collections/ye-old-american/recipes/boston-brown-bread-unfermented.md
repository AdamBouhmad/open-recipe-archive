## Ingredients

- one cupful of rye flour
- two cupfuls of corn meal
- one cupful of white flour
- half a teacupful of molasses or sugar
- a teaspoonful of salt
- sour milk
- one level teaspoonful of soda
- one tablespoonful of water
- baking powder
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Stir all together thoroughly, and wet up with sour milk
2. add a level teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a tablespoonful of water
3. The same can be made of sweet milk by substituting baking powder for soda
4. The batter to be stirred as thick as can be with a spoon, and turned
