## Ingredients

- one quart of fresh mushrooms
- salt
- a blade of mace
- butter
- half a pint of cream
- the yelks of two eggs
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Clean and wash one quart of fresh mushrooms
2. Cut them in two and put them into a stew-pan with a little salt, a blade of mace, and a little butter
3. Stew them gently for half an hour
4. Add half a pint of cream and the yelks of two eggs beat very well
5. Keep stirring it till it boils up
6. Put it over the fowls or turkies, or put it on a dish with a piece of fried bread first buttered, then toasted brown, and just dipped into boiling water
