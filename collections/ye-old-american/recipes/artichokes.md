## Ingredients

- artichokes
- cold water
- salt
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak them in cold water
2. Wash them well
3. Put them into plenty of boiling water with a handful of salt
4. Let them boil gently till they are tender, which will take an hour and a half, or two hours
5. Draw out a leaf to know when they are done enough
6. Trim them, and drain them on a sieve
7. Send up melted butter with them, with some put into small cups, so that each guest may have one
