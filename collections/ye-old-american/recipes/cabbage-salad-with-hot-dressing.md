## Ingredients

- 1 head of cabbage
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls sour cream
- A pinch of pepper
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Shred the head of cabbage fine.
2. Put the vinegar, with all the ingredients for the dressing, except the cream, in a saucepan, and let them come to a boil.
3. Pour while scalding over the cabbage, and set away until perfectly cold.
4. Add the cream just before serving, stirring in with a silver fork.
