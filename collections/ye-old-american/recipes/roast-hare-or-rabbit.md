## Ingredients

- one hare or rabbit
- dressing
- butter
- flour
- salt and pepper
- vinegar
- a pint of boiling water
- a heaping tablespoonful of flour
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Skin and wash the hare or rabbit well in cold water and rinse thoroughly in lukewarm water
2. If musty, rub the insides with vinegar and afterward remove all taint of the acid by a thorough washing in lukewarm water
3. Wipe well with a soft cloth and put in a dressing as usual
4. Sew the animal up and truss it
5. Roast for half or three-quarters of an hour until well browned, basting constantly with butter and dredging with flour just before taking up
6. After roasting, pour nearly all the fat out of the pan but do not pour the bottom or brown part of the drippings
7. Put the pan over the fire and stir in a heaping tablespoonful of flour, stirring until the flour browns
8. Stir in a pint of boiling water
9. Season the gravy with salt and pepper and let it boil for a moment
10. Send hot to the table in a tureen with the hot rabbits and serve with currant jelly
