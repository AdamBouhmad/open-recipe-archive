## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- ½ cup thick cream
- ¼ cup milk
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat white of egg until stiff; add yolk of egg well beaten, and sugar gradually; dilute cream with milk, beat until stiff, combine mixtures, and flavor.
