## Ingredients

- one half pound of macaroni
- boiling salted water
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Divide half a pound of macaroni into four-inch pieces
2. Put it into boiling salted water enough to cover it
3. Boil from fifteen to twenty minutes then drain
4. Arrange it neatly on a hot dish
5. Pour tomato sauce over it, and serve immediately while hot
