## Ingredients

- pie crust
- strawberries, blackberries, raspberries, or whatever berries are in season
- sugar
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Line small pie-tins with pie crust and bake
2. Just before ready to use fill the tarts with strawberries, blackberries, raspberries, or whatever berries are in season
3. Sprinkle over each tart a little sugar
4. add to each tart a tablespoonful of sweet cream
