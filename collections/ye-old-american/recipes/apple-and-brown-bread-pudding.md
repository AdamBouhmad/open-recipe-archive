## Ingredients

- 1 pint brown bread crumbs
- 1 pint bowl chopped apples
- 2/3 cupful finely-chopped suet
- 1 cupful raisins
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful salt
- 1/2 pint milk
- sauce flavored with lemon

## Directions

1. Mix bread crumbs and apples
2. Add suet, raisins, egg, flour and salt
3. Mix with milk
4. Boil in buttered molds about two hours
5. Serve with lemon-flavored sauce
