## Ingredients

- three pounds of shin of beef or neck of mutton
- salt
- water
- sweet marjoram
- sweet basil
- black pepper-corns
- onion
- turnips
- carrots
- two sets of goose-giblets or four of duck
- celery
- butter
- flour
- mushroom catchup
- force-meat balls or little dumplings

## Directions

1. Cut off the meat and break the bones
2. Put the meat with the bones into a soup-pot with a tea-spoonful of salt and three quarts of water
3. Add a bunch of sweet marjoram, one of sweet basil, and a quarter of an ounce of black pepper-corns, all tied in a thin muslin rag
4. Add a sliced onion and six or eight turnips and carrots, cut small
5. Let the whole boil slowly for two or three hours, skimming it well
6. Scald the giblets and well wash them in warm water
7. Cut off the bills and split the heads
8. Cut the necks and gizzards into mouthfuls
9. Take the meat and bones out of the soup and put in the giblets with a head of celery chopped
10. Boil it slowly an hour and a half or more, taking care to skim it
11. Make a thickening of an ounce and a half of butter and a large table-spoonful of flour, mixed together with a little of the soup
12. Stir it into the pot, adding a large table-spoonful of mushroom catchup and some small force-meat balls or little dumplings
13. Boil the soup half an hour longer
14. Send it to table with the giblets in the tureen
