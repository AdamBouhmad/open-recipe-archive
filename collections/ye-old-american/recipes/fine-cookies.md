## Ingredients

- five large tea-cupsful of flour
- one tea-cup of fresh butter
- two cups of powdered white sugar
- a handful or two of carraway seeds
- an egg
- a little rose-water
- a small tea-spoonful of sal-aratus
- lukewarm water

## Directions

1. Sift into a pan five large tea-cupsful of flour
2. Rub into it one tea-cup of fresh butter
3. Add two cups of powdered white sugar, and a handful or two of carraway seeds
4. Wet it with an egg well beaten, and a little rose-water
5. Dissolve a small tea-spoonful of sal-aratus in a very little lukewarm water and add at the last
6. Knead the whole well
7. Roll it out into a sheet
8. Cut it into cakes with a stamp or a tumbler edge
9. Put them into a buttered pan
10. Bake them about fifteen minutes
