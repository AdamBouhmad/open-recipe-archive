## Ingredients

- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup thin cream
- 2 cups orange juice
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Add cream slowly to orange juice, sweeten to taste, and freeze.
2. Serve with canned strawberries or fresh fruit mashed and sweetened.
