## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- one cup of sour cream or milk
- three eggs
- one teaspoonful of soda
- a tablespoonful of warm water
- one tablespoonful of ginger
- one teaspoonful of ground cinnamon
- five cups of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in a tablespoonful of warm water.
2. Cream together butter and sugar.
3. Add sour cream or milk, eggs, ginger, cinnamon, and soda mixture.
4. Mix in five cups of sifted flour, or enough to roll out soft.
5. Cut out rather thick like biscuits.
6. Brush over the tops while hot with the white of an egg, or sprinkle with sugar while hot.
