## Ingredients

- cod sounds and tongues
- fresh water
- milk
- water
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- new milk
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Soak them all night in fresh water
2. Take off the skins
3. Cut them in two pieces
4. Boil them in milk and water till quite tender
5. Drain them in a colander
6. Season with nutmeg, pepper, and a little salt
7. Take as much new milk as will make sauce for it
8. Roll a good lump of butter in flour
9. Melt it in the milk
10. Put the fish in
11. Set it over the fire and stir it till thick enough
12. Serve it up
