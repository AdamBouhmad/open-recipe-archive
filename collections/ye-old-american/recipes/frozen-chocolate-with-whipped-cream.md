## Ingredients

- 2 squares Baker's chocolate
- 1 cup sugar
- Few grains salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- 3 cups rich milk

## Directions

1. Scald milk
2. Melt chocolate in small saucepan placed over hot water, add one-half the sugar, salt, and gradually boiling water
3. Boil one minute, add to scalded milk with remaining sugar
4. Cool, freeze, and serve
