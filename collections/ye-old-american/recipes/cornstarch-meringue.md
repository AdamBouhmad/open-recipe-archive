## Ingredients

- a quart of milk
- four heaping teaspoonfuls of cornstarch
- a little cold milk
- the yolks of five eggs
- three-fourths of a cupful of sugar
- lemon, vanilla or bitter almond
- the whites of five eggs
- a half cupful of jelly
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Heat a quart of milk until it boils
2. Add four heaping teaspoonfuls of cornstarch which has previously been dissolved in a little cold milk
3. Stir constantly while boiling for fifteen minutes
4. Remove from the fire, and gradually add while hot the yolks of five eggs, beaten together with three-fourths of a cupful of sugar, and flavored with lemon, vanilla or bitter almond
5. Bake this mixture for fifteen minutes in a well-buttered pudding-dish or until it begins to set
6. Make a meringue of the whites of five eggs, whipped stiff with a half cupful of jelly, and spread evenly over the custard, without removing the same farther than the edge of the oven
7. Use currant jelly if vanilla is used in the custard, crab apple for bitter almond and strawberry for lemon
8. Cover and bake for five minutes
9. Take off the lid and brown the meringue a very little
10. Sift powdered sugar thickly over the top
11. Eat cold
