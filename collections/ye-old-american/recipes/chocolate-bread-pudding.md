## Ingredients

- 2 cups stale bread crumbs
- 4 cups scalded milk
- 2 squares Baker's chocolate
- ⅔ cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Soak bread in milk thirty minutes
2. Melt chocolate in saucepan placed over hot water
3. Add one-half sugar and enough milk taken from bread and milk to make of consistency to pour
4. Add to mixture with remaining sugar, salt, vanilla, and eggs slightly beaten
5. Turn into buttered pudding-dish and bake one hour in a moderate oven
6. Serve with Hard or Cream Sauce I
