## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- boiling water
- salt

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the tomatoes to loosen the skins.
2. Remove the skins.
3. Drain off all the juice that will come away without pressing hard.
4. Put them into a kettle and heat slowly to a boil.
5. Remove all the hard parts before putting them on the fire, and rub the pulp soft with your hands.
6. Boil ten minutes.
7. Dip out the surplus liquid.
8. Pour the tomatoes, boiling hot, into the cans, and seal.
9. Keep in a cool, dark place.
