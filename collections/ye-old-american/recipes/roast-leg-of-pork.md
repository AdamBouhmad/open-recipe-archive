## Ingredients

- One leg of pork weighing about seven pounds
- very little water
- brown flour
- pepper
- salt
- juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Score the skin in squares, or parallel lines running from side to side, for the convenience of the carver.
2. Put it down to roast with a very little water in the pan below.
3. Heat gradually until the fat begins to ooze from the meat, when quicken the fire to a red, steady glow.
4. Baste only with its own gravy, and do this often, that the skin may not be hard or tough.
5. When done take it up, skim the gravy thoroughly, put in half a cup of boiling water, thicken with brown flour, add pepper, salt, and the juice of a lemon, and serve in a boat.
