## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- salt
- whole black pepper
- allspice
- sliced ginger
- cloves
- mace

## Directions

1. Take mushrooms that have been freshly gathered, and examine them carefully to ascertain that they are of the right sort
2. Pick them nicely, and wipe them clean, but do not wash them
3. Spread a layer of them at the bottom of a deep earthen pan, and then sprinkle them well with salt
4. Add another layer of mushrooms, and another layer of salt, and so on alternately
5. Throw a folded cloth over the jar, and set it by the fire or in a very cool oven
6. Let it remain thus for twenty-four hours, and then mash them well with your hands
7. Squeeze and strain them through a bag
8. To every quart of strained liquor add an ounce and a half of whole black pepper, and boil it slowly in a covered vessel for half an hour
9. Then add a quarter of an ounce of allspice, half an ounce of sliced ginger, a few cloves, and three or four blades of mace
10. Boil it with the spice fifteen minutes longer
11. When it is done, take it off, and let it stand awhile to settle
12. Pour it carefully off from the sediment, and put it into small bottles, filling them to the top
13. Secure them well with corks dipped in melted rosin, and leather caps tied over them
