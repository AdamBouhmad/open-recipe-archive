## Ingredients

- Breast and second joints of uncooked chicken weighing 4 lbs
- 4 eggs
- 1⅓ cups thick cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Force chicken through a meat chopper, or chop very finely
2. Beat eggs separately, add one at a time, stirring until mixture is smooth
3. Add cream, and season with salt and pepper
4. Turn into slightly buttered Dario moulds, and bake same as Lobster Timbales, allowing thirty minutes for baking
5. Serve with Suprême or Béchamel Sauce
