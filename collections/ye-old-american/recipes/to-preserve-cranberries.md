## Ingredients

- large ripe cranberries
- best loaf sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Select large and ripe cranberries and wash them
2. To six quarts of cranberries allow nine pounds of the best loaf sugar
3. Take three quarts of the cranberries and put them into a stew-pan with a pint and a half of water
4. Cover the pan and boil or stew them till they are all to pieces
5. Squeeze the juice through a jelly-bag
6. Put the sugar into a preserving kettle and pour the cranberry juice over it
7. Let it stand till all is melted, stirring frequently
8. Place the kettle over the fire and put in the remaining three quarts of whole cranberries
9. Let them boil till they are tender, clear, and of a bright colour, skimming frequently
10. When done, put them warm into jars with the syrup, which should be like a thick jelly
