## Ingredients

- four tablespoons fat
- four tablespoons flour
- two cups stock
- salt
- pepper
- giblets

## Directions

1. Pour off liquid in pan in which chicken has been roasted
2. From liquid skim off four tablespoons fat; return fat to pan, and brown with four tablespoons flour
3. Add two cups stock in which giblets, neck, and tips of wings have been cooked
4. Cook five minutes, season with salt and pepper, then strain
5. Add giblets (heart, liver, and gizzard) finely chopped
