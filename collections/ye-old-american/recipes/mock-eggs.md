## Ingredients

- Yolks of three hard-boiled eggs
- One raw egg
- One teaspoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Rub the boiled eggs into a paste with a teaspoonful of butter
2. Bind with the raw egg
3. Roll into pellets the size and shape of turtle-eggs
4. Lay in boiling water for two minutes before dropping into the soup
