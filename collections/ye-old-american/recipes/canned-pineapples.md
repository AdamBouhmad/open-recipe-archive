## Ingredients

- pineapples
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Remove skin and eyes from pineapples
2. Cut in half-inch slices and slices in cubes, at the same time discarding the core
3. Follow Directions for Canning
4. Pineapples may be shredded and cooked in one-half their weight of sugar without water and then put in jars
