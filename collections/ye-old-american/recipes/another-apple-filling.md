## Ingredients

- one coffeecup of sugar
- one egg
- three large apples grated
- one lemon grated, juice and outside of the rind

## Directions

1. Beat together the sugar, egg, grated apples, and grated lemon
2. Cook till quite thick
3. Cool before putting on the cake
4. Spread between layers of cake
