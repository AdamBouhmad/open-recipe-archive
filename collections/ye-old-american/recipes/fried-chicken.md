## Ingredients

- young chicken
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- beaten egg
- cracker crumbs
- butter
- sweet lard
- cream
- milk
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash and cut up a young chicken
2. wipe it dry
3. season with salt and pepper
4. dredge it with flour, or dip each piece in beaten egg and then in cracker crumbs
5. Have in a frying pan one ounce each of butter and sweet lard made boiling hot
6. Lay in the chicken and fry brown on both sides
7. Take up and drain it
8. set aside in a covered dish
9. Stir into the gravy left, if not too much, a large tablespoonful of flour
10. make it smooth
11. add a cup of cream or milk
12. season with salt and pepper
13. boil up and pour over the chicken
14. Some like chopped parsley added to the gravy
15. Serve hot
