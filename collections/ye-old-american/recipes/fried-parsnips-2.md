## Ingredients

- parsnips
- hot water
- salt
- flour
- hot lard or dripping or butter and lard mixed

## Directions

1. Boil tender in a little hot water salted
2. Scrape and cut into long slices
3. Dredge with flour
4. Fry in hot lard or dripping, or in butter and lard mixed
5. Fry quite brown
6. Drain off fat and serve
