## Ingredients

- whites of three eggs
- three cups of fine white sugar
- one pound of sweet almonds
- vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Beat whites of three eggs
2. Blanch sweet almonds and pound them in a mortar with a little sugar until a fine paste
3. Add the whites of eggs, sugar and vanilla extract to the almond paste
4. Pound a few minutes to thoroughly mix
5. Cover the cake with a very thick coating of this frosting
6. Set in a cool oven to dry
7. Afterwards cover with a plain icing
