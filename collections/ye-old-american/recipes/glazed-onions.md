## Ingredients

- small silver skinned onions
- boiling water
- cheese-cloth
- buttered baking-dish
- brown stock
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel small silver skinned onions, and cook in boiling water fifteen minutes.
2. Drain, dry on cheese-cloth, put in a buttered baking-dish, add highly seasoned brown stock to cover bottom of dish, sprinkle with sugar, and bake until soft, basting with stock in pan.
