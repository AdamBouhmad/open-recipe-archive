## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ⅞ cup flour
- 2 cups milk
- Grated rind one lemon
- 5 eggs
- ⅓ cup powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add flour gradually
2. Scald milk with lemon rind
3. Add to first mixture and cook five minutes in double boiler
4. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored
5. Add sugar gradually
6. Add to cooked mixture
7. Cool and cut and fold in whites of eggs beaten stiff
8. Turn into buttered mould
9. Cover and steam one and one-fourth hours
10. While steaming, be sure water surrounds mould to half its depth, and never reaches a lower temperature than the boiling-point
