## Ingredients

- button mushrooms of uniform size
- cold water
- salt
- tablespoonful of butter
- flour
- three or four tablespoonfuls of cream
- egg

## Directions

1. Choose button mushrooms of uniform size.
2. Wipe clean and white with a wet flannel cloth, and cut off the stalks.
3. Put into a porcelain saucepan, cover with cold water, and stew very gently fifteen minutes.
4. Salt to taste; add a tablespoonful of butter, divided into bits and rolled in flour.
5. Boil three or four minutes; stir in three or four tablespoonfuls of cream whipped up with an egg, stir two minutes without letting it boil, and serve.
