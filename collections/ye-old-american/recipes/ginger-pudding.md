## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 2¼ cups flour
- 3½ teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- 1 cup milk

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, and egg well beaten
2. Mix and sift dry ingredients
3. Add alternately with milk to first mixture
4. Turn into buttered mould
5. Cover and steam two hours
6. Serve with Vanilla Sauce
