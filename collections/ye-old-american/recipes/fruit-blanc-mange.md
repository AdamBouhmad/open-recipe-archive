## Ingredients

- Fresh fruit such as cherries, raspberries and strawberries, or canned ones
- Sugar to taste
- Cornstarch
- Cold water
- Cream and sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Stew nice fresh fruit or canned ones
2. Strain off the juice and sweeten to taste
3. Place it over the fire in a double kettle until it boils
4. While boiling, stir in cornstarch wet with a little cold water, allowing two tablespoonfuls of cornstarch to each pint of juice
5. Continue stirring until sufficiently cooked
6. Pour into molds wet in cold water
7. Set away to cool
8. Serve with cream and sugar
