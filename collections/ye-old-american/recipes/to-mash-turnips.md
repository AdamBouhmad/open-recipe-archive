## Ingredients

- turnips
- butter

## Directions

1. When they are boiled quite tender, squeeze them as dry as possible
2. Put them into a sauce pan
3. Mash them with a wooden spoon
4. Rub them through a colander
5. Add a little bit of butter
6. Keep stirring them till the butter is melted and well mixed with them
