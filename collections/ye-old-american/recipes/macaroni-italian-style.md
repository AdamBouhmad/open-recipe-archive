## Ingredients

- 1 cup macaroni
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 1/2 cups scalded milk
- 2/3 cup grated cheese
- salt and paprika
- 1/4 cup finely chopped cold boiled ham

## Directions

1. Break macaroni in one-inch pieces
2. Cook in boiling salted water
3. Drain
4. Reheat in sauce made of butter, flour, and milk
5. Add cheese to sauce
6. As soon as cheese is melted, season with salt and paprika
7. Turn on to a serving dish
8. Sprinkle with ham
9. Garnish with parsley
