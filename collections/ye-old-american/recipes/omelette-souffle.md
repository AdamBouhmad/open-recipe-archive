## Ingredients

- six eggs
- four table spoonsful of powdered sugar
- grated lemon peel
- quarter of a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Break six eggs
2. Beat the yelks and whites separately till very light
3. Mix them together
4. Add four table spoonsful of powdered sugar and a little grated lemon peel
5. Put a quarter of a pound of butter in a pan
6. When melted, pour in the eggs and stir them
7. When they have absorbed the butter, turn it on a plate previously buttered
8. Sprinkle some powdered sugar
9. Set it in a hot Dutch oven
10. When a little brown, serve it up for a desert
