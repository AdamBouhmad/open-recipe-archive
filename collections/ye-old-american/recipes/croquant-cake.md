## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound of almonds (of which two ounces or more should be the bitter sort)
- three quarters of a pound of fine white sugar
- three quarters of a pound of flour
- half a pound of citron
- six eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Blanch and slice the almonds
2. Powder the fine white sugar
3. Sift the flour
4. Slice the citron
5. Mix together the almond and citron on a flat dish, and sprinkle among them flour from the dredging-box, till they are white all over
6. Beat six eggs as light as possible, till they are very thick and smooth
7. Mix the eggs gradually with the sugar, almond, and citron, stirring very hard
8. Stir in the sifted flour by degrees
9. Butter a tin pan or pans, and put in the mixture about an inch deep
10. Bake it
11. When cool, cut it into narrow slices about an inch wide, and five inches long
12. For long keeping, lay them on shallow tins, and give them a second baking
13. Put the cakes into a stone jar, and they will keep a year or more after this double baking
