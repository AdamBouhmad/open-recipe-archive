## Ingredients

- Three cups of sour milk
- One teaspoonful of soda
- One teaspoonful of salt
- One tablespoonful of brown sugar
- One tablespoonful of melted lard or butter
- One or two beaten eggs
- Graham flour

## Directions

1. Add to the egg the milk, then the sugar and salt
2. Mix in the Graham flour with the soda mixed in, together with the lard or butter
3. Make a stiff batter so that it will drop, not pour, from the spoon
4. Have the gem-pans very hot
5. Fill and bake fifteen minutes in a hot oven
