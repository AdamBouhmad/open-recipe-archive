## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- Thick cream
- Flour
- Lard
- Butter
- White Sauce I
- 1 tablespoon meat extract

## Directions

1. Remove fillets from two chickens.
2. Dip each in thick cream, roll in flour, and sauté in lard three minutes.
3. Place in a pan, dot over with butter, and bake ten minutes.
4. Serve with White Sauce I, to which is added one tablespoon meat extract.
