## Ingredients

- 1 lb. white sugar
- 2 lbs. raisins, seeded and chopped
- 1 lemon—all the juice and half the grated peel
- 2 gallons boiling water

## Directions

1. Put all into a stone jar, and stir every day for a week
2. Strain, then, and bottle it
3. It will be fit for use in ten days
