## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- salt water
- calf's tongue
- melted butter
- cayenne pepper
- white or black pepper
- nutmeg
- cloves
- Worcestershire sauce
- salt
- made mustard
- boiling water
- onion

## Directions

1. Boil a calf's liver until very tender in slightly salted water
2. In another vessel, boil a nice calf's tongue
3. Allow both to cool until not only cold, but firm
4. Cut the liver into bits
5. Rub these gradually to a smooth paste in a Wedgewood mortar, moistening with melted butter as you go
6. Work into the paste a quarter-teaspoonful of cayenne pepper or twice the quantity of white or black pepper
7. Add half a grated nutmeg, a little cloves, a teaspoonful of Worcestershire sauce, salt to taste, a full teaspoonful of made mustard
8. Add a tablespoonful of boiling water in which a minced onion has been steeped until the flavor is extracted
9. Work all together
