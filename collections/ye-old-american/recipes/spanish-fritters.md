## Ingredients

- one quart of flour
- one egg
- yeast
- milk
- two spoonsful of melted butter
- boiling lard
- wine
- sugar
- molasses

## Directions

1. Make up a quart of flour, with one egg well beaten, a large spoonful of yeast, and as much milk as will make it a little softer than muffin dough
2. mix it early in the morning
3. when well risen, work in two spoonsful of melted butter
4. make it in balls the size of a walnut
5. fry them a light brown in boiling lard
6. eat them with wine and sugar, or molasses
