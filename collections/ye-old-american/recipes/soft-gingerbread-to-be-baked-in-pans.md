## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- two pounds of butter
- four pounds of flour
- 20 eggs
- 4 ounces ginger
- 4 spoons rose water

## Directions

1. rub three pounds of sugar, two pounds of butter, into four pounds of flour
2. add 20 eggs, 4 ounces ginger, 4 spoons rose water
3. bake as No. 1
