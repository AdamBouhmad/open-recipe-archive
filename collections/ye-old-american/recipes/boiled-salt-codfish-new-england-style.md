## Ingredients

- salt codfish cut into square pieces
- cold water
- drawn-butter gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into square pieces
2. Cover with cold water and set on the back part of the stove
3. When hot, pour off water and cover again with cold water
4. Let it stand about four hours and simmer, not boil
5. Put the fish on a platter
6. Cover with a drawn-butter gravy and serve
