## Ingredients

- eight sweet apples
- one-third cup sugar
- molasses
- two-thirds cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Wipe and core eight sweet apples.
2. Put in a baking-dish, and fill cavities with sugar, allowing one-third cup, or sweeten with molasses.
3. Add two-thirds cup boiling water.
4. Cover, and bake three hours in a slow oven, adding more water if necessary.
