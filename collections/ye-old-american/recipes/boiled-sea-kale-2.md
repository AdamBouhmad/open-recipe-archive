## Ingredients

- sea-kale
- salt
- water
- buttered toast
- butter

## Directions

1. Tie up in bunches when you have picked it over carefully, and lay in cold water for an hour.
2. Put into salted boiling water, and cook twenty or thirty minutes until tender.
3. Lay some slices of buttered toast in the bottom of a dish, clip the threads binding the stems of the sea-kale, and pile upon the toast, buttering it abundantly.
4. Or, you can send around with it a boat of drawn butter.
