## Ingredients

- four fine sweet-breads
- butter
- beaten egg
- grated bread-crumbs
- veal gravy
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Take four fine sweet-breads, and having trimmed them nicely, parboil them, and then lay them in a pan of cold water till they become cool.
2. Afterwards dry them in a cloth.
3. Put some butter into a sauce-pan, set it on hot coals, and melt and skim it.
4. When it is quite clear, take it off.
5. Have ready some beaten egg in one dish, and some grated bread-crumbs in another.
6. Skewer each sweet-bread, and fasten them on a spit.
7. Then glaze them all over with egg, and sprinkle them with bread-crumbs.
8. Spread on some of the clarified butter, and then another coat of crumbs.
9. Roast them before a clear fire, at least a quarter of an hour.
10. Have ready some nice veal gravy flavoured with lemon-juice, and pour it round the sweet-breads before you send them to table.
