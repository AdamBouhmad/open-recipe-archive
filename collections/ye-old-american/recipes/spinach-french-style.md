## Ingredients

- one peck spinach
- boiling salted water
- four tablespoons butter
- three tablespoons flour
- two-thirds cup Chicken Stock
- one teaspoon powdered sugar
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lemon rind

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash one peck spinach, and cook in boiling salted water twenty-five minutes.
2. Drain, and finely chop.
3. Reheat in hot pan with four tablespoons butter to which have been added three tablespoons flour and two-thirds cup Chicken Stock.
4. Season with one teaspoon powdered sugar, salt, pepper, and a few gratings each of nutmeg and lemon rind.
