## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of flour
- one-half teaspoonful of salt
- one teaspoonful of baking powder
- one cup of chopped suet, freed of skin, and chopped very fine
- one cupful of water

## Directions

1. Place the flour, sifted with the powder in a bowl.
2. Add suet and water; mix into smooth, rather firm dough.
