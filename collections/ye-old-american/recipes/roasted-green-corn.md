## Ingredients

- corn
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Turn back the husks upon the stalk and pick off the silk.
2. Recover with the husks as closely as possible.
3. Roast in the hot ashes of a wood-fire.
4. Eat with butter, salt, and pepper, out of doors, in the forest, or on the beach.
