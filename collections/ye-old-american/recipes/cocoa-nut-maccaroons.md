## Ingredients

- whites of six eggs
- a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- a pound of grated cocoa-nut
- buttered white paper
- loaf-sugar for grating

## Directions

1. Beat to a stiff froth the whites of six eggs.
2. Beat into it very hard a pound of powdered loaf-sugar.
3. Mix with it a pound of grated cocoa-nut, or sufficient to make a stiff paste.
4. Flour your hands, and make it up into little balls.
5. Lay them on sheets of buttered white paper.
6. Grate loaf-sugar over each.
7. Bake them in a brisk oven for a few minutes.
