## Ingredients

- ham slices
- scalding water
- boiling water
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut in slices.
2. Wash well, and soak in scalding water in a covered vessel for half an hour.
3. Pour off the water, and add more boiling water.
4. Wipe dry when the ham has stood half an hour in the second water, and lay in cold for five minutes.
5. Wipe again and broil over or under a clear fire.
6. Pepper before serving.
