## Ingredients

- juice of twelve lemons
- rind of six lemons, grated
- six pounds of white sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the rind of six lemons into the juice of twelve lemons, let it stand over night
2. Take six pounds of white sugar and make a thick syrup
3. When it is quite cool, strain the juice into it, and squeeze as much oil from the grated rind as will suit the taste
4. Put in bottles, securely corked, for future use
