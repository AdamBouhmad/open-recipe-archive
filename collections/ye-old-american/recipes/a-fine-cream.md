## Ingredients

- a pint of cream
- to your palate
- a little nutmeg
- a spoonful of orange flower water
- rose water
- two spoonsful of wine
- four eggs
- two whites

## Directions

1. Beat up four eggs and two whites
2. stir it all together one way over the fire till it is thick
3. have cups ready and pour it in
