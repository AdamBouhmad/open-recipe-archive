## Ingredients

- one cup of bread-crumbs
- an ounce of suet
- a bunch of parsley
- teaspoonful of powdered sage
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- a little thyme
- half a glass Madeira or Sherry
- juice of a lemon
- two tablespoonfuls melted butter
- a cup of oyster-liquor
- two well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together to create a rich and savory force-meat for a pig.
