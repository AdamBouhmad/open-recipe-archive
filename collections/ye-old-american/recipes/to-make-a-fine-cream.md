## Ingredients

- a pint of cream
- sweetening to your palate
- nutmeg
- orange flower water
- rose water
- wine
- four eggs
- two whites

## Directions

1. Take a pint of cream, sweeten it to your palate, grate a little nutmeg, put in a spoonful of orange flower water and rose water, and two sponfuls of wine; beat up four eggs and two whites, stir it all together one way over the fire till it is thick, have cups ready and pour it in.
