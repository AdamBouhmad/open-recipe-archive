## Ingredients

- 1 lb. sugar
- 1 lb. flour
- ¾ lb. butter
- 9 eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls cream-tartar
- 1 teaspoonful soda

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar with great care.
2. Beat the yolks and whites separately.
3. Sift the cream-tartar well through the flour.
4. Add the flour last.
