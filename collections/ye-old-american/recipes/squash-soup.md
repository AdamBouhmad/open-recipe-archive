## Ingredients

- ¾ cup cooked squash
- 1 quart milk
- 1 slice onion
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Few grains pepper
- ¼ teaspoon celery salt

## Directions

1. Rub squash through a sieve before measuring.
2. Scald milk with onion, remove onion, and add milk to squash; season, and bind.
