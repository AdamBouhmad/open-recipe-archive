## Ingredients

- ripe cling-stone peaches
- peach brandy
- water
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Gather ripe cling-stone peaches, wipe off the down, cut them to the stone in several places, and put them in a cask
2. When filled with peaches, pour on as much peach brandy as the cask will hold
3. Let it stand six or eight weeks, then draw it off
4. Put in water until reduced to the strength of wine
5. To each gallon of this, add one pound of good brown sugar
6. Dissolve it, and pour the cordial into a cask just large enough to hold it
7. When perfectly clear, it is fit for use
