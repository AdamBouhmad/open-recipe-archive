## Ingredients

- frog quarters
- butter
- flour
- parsley
- powdered summer savory
- bay leaf
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- hot water
- cream
- eggs

## Directions

1. Wash and skin the quarters, parboil them about three minutes, drain them
2. put into a stewpan two ounces of butter
3. When it is melted, lay in the frogs, and fry about two minutes, stirring them to prevent burning
4. shake over them a tablespoonful of sifted flour and stir it into them
5. add a sprig of parsley, a pinch of powdered summer savory, a bay leaf, three slices of onion, salt and pepper, a cup of hot water and one of cream
6. Boil gently until done
7. remove the legs, strain and mix into the gravy the yolks of two eggs, well beaten to a cream
8. put the legs in a suitable dish, pour over the gravy and serve
