## Ingredients

- Salt codfish
- One pint of rich, sweet milk
- Three eggs
- One tablespoonful of butter
- Chopped parsley
- Pepper
- Buttered toast

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish as for balls
2. Heat almost to boiling a pint of rich, sweet milk
3. Stir into it, gradually and carefully, three eggs, well beaten
4. Add a tablespoonful of butter, a little chopped parsley and butter, with pepper
5. Add the fish
6. Boil up once and turn into a deep covered dish, or chafing dish lined with buttered toast
7. Eat hot for breakfast or supper
