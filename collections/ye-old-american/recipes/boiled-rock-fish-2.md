## Ingredients

- rock-fish
- salt
- eggs
- celery sauce
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Clean the rock-fish
2. Put it into a fish-kettle with water enough to cover it well, having first dissolved a handful of salt in the water
3. Set it over a moderate fire, and do not let it boil too fast
4. Skim it well
5. When done, drain it, and put it on a large dish
6. Have ready a few eggs boiled hard
7. Cut them in half, and lay them closely on the back of the fish in a straight line from the head to the tail
8. Send with it in a boat, celery sauce flavoured with a little cayenne
