## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 large cup sour cream or very rich loppered milk
- 1 egg
- 4 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- strawberries
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Proceed in mixing and baking as with the huckleberry short-cake.
2. Instead of putting the berries between the crust, lay one sheet of paste smoothly upon the other, and bake until done.
3. While warm—not hot—separate these.
4. Lay upon the lower a thick coating several deep, of strawberries.
5. Sprinkle powdered sugar among and over them.
6. Cover with the upper crust.
7. It is best to bake strawberry shortcake in round jelly-cake tins, or round pans a little deeper than these.
