## Ingredients

- two ounces of dried hoarhound
- one pint and a half of water
- three and a half pounds of brown sugar

## Directions

1. Boil two ounces of dried hoarhound in a pint and a half of water for about half an hour
2. Strain and add three and a half pounds of brown sugar
3. Boil over a hot fire until sufficiently hard
4. Pour out in flat, well-greased tins
5. Mark into sticks or small squares with a knife as soon as cool enough to retain its shape
