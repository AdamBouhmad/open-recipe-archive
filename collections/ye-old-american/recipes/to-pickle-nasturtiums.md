## Ingredients

- nasturtium berries
- salt and water
- vinegar
- mace
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Gather the berries when full grown but young
2. Put them in a pot
3. Pour boiling salt and water on them
4. Let them stand three or four days
5. Drain off the water
6. Cover them with cold vinegar
7. Add a few blades of mace and whole grains of black pepper
