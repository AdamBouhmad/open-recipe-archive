## Ingredients

- crumb of a loaf of bread
- cold water
- pepper corns
- salt
- onion
- quarter of a pound of butter
- two spoonsful of thick cream

## Directions

1. Cut the crumb of a loaf of bread in thin slices
2. Put it in cold water with a few pepper corns, a little salt and onion
3. Boil it till the bread is quite soft
4. Beat it well
5. Put in a quarter of a pound of butter and two spoonsful of thick cream
6. Put it in the dish with the turkey
