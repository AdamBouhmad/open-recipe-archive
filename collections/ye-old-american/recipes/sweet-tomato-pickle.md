## Ingredients

- 7 lbs. ripe tomatoes, peeled and sliced
- 3½ lbs. sugar
- 1 oz. cinnamon and mace mixed
- 1 oz. cloves
- 1 quart of vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix all together and stew an hour.
