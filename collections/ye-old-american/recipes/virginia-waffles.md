## Ingredients

- 1½ cups boiling water
- ½ cup white corn meal
- 1½ cups milk
- 3 cups flour
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1¼ tablespoons baking powder
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Whites 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Cook meal in boiling water twenty minutes
2. Add milk, dry ingredients mixed and sifted, yolks of eggs well beaten, butter, and whites of eggs beaten stiff
3. Cook same as Waffles
