## Ingredients

- one quart of cranberries
- two cupfuls of sugar
- a pint of water

## Directions

1. Wash the cranberries
2. Put them on the fire with the water in a covered saucepan
3. Let them simmer until each cranberry bursts open
4. Remove the cover of the saucepan
5. Add the sugar and let them all boil twenty minutes without the cover
6. The cranberries must never be stirred from the time they are placed on the fire
