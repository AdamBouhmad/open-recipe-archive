## Ingredients

- mackerel
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the mackerel well, and let them lie a short time in vinegar and water.
2. Then put them into the fish-kettle with cold water and a handful of salt.
3. Boil them slowly.
4. If small, they will be sufficiently cooked in twenty minutes.
5. When the eye starts and the tail splits they are done.
6. Take them up immediately on finding them boiled enough.
7. Serve them up with parsley sauce, and garnish the dish with lumps of minced parsley.
