## Ingredients

- Cinnamon Bar mixture
- frosting colored with fruit red
- chocolate frosting

## Directions

1. Use Cinnamon Bar mixture.
2. Cover with frosting colored with fruit red.
3. Cut in strips six inches long by one-half inch wide.
4. As soon as cut, shape quickly, at the same time carefully, in form of horseshoes.
5. Bake same as Cinnamon Bars.
6. When cool, make eight dots with chocolate frosting to represent nails.
