## Ingredients

- one quart scallops
- salt
- pepper
- fine crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Clean one quart scallops, turn into a saucepan, and cook until they begin to shrivel.
2. Drain, and dry between towels.
3. Season with salt and pepper, roll in fine crumbs, dip in egg, again in crumbs, and fry two minutes in deep fat.
4. Drain on brown paper.
