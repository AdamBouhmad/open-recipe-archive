## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 5 eggs
- 5 tablespoonfuls sugar
- Nutmeg
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Scald but not boil the milk
2. Add by degrees to the beaten yolks, and when well mixed, stir in the whites
3. Flavor
4. Pour into a deep dish, or custard-cups of white stone-china
5. Set these in a pan of hot water, grate nutmeg upon each, and bake until firm
6. Eat cold from the cups
