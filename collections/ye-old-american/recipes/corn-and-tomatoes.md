## Ingredients

- green corn
- tomatoes
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of green corn cut from the cob and tomatoes sliced and peeled.
2. Stew together half an hour; season with pepper, salt, and a very little sugar.
3. Stew fifteen minutes longer and stir in a great lump of butter.
4. Pour out and serve five minutes later.
