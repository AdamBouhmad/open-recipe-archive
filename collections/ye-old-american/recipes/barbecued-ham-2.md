## Ingredients

- thin slices of ham
- three tablespoons vinegar
- one and one-half teaspoons mustard
- one-half teaspoon sugar
- one-eighth teaspoon paprika

## Directions

1. Soak thin slices of ham one hour in lukewarm water; drain, wipe, and cook in a hot frying-pan until slightly browned.
2. Remove to serving dish and add to fat in pan three tablespoons vinegar mixed with one and one-half teaspoons mustard, one-half teaspoon sugar, and one-eighth teaspoon paprika.
3. When thoroughly heated pour over ham and serve at once.
