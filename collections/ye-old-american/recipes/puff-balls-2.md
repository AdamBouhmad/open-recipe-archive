## Ingredients

- butter as large as an egg
- three well-beaten eggs
- pinch of salt
- half a teacupful of sour cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Stir butter until soft
2. Add three well-beaten eggs, a pinch of salt and half a teacupful of sour cream
3. Stir well together, then add enough flour to make a very thick batter
4. Drop a spoonful of this into boiling water
5. Cook until the puffs rise to the surface
6. Dish them hot with melted butter turned over them
