## Ingredients

- ingredients from Dressing or Stuffing for Fowls
- half a can of oysters drained and slightly chopped

## Directions

1. Make with the same ingredients as Dressing or Stuffing for Fowls, with the exception of half a can of oysters drained and slightly chopped and added to the rest
2. This is used mostly with boiled turkey and chicken, and the remainder of the can of oysters used to make an oyster sauce to be poured over the turkey when served
