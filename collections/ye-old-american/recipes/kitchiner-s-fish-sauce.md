## Ingredients

- a pint of claret
- a pint of mushroom catchup
- half a pint of walnut pickle
- four ounces of pounded anchovy
- an ounce of fresh lemon-peel pared thin
- an ounce of shalot or small onion
- an ounce of scraped horseradish
- half an ounce of black pepper
- half an ounce of allspice mixed
- a quantity of cayenne and celery-seed

## Directions

1. Mix together a pint of claret, a pint of mushroom catchup, and half a pint of walnut pickle, four ounces of pounded anchovy, an ounce of fresh lemon-peel pared thin, and the same quantity of shalot or small onion.
2. Also an ounce of scraped horseradish, half an ounce of black pepper, and half an ounce of allspice mixed, and the same quantity of cayenne and celery-seed.
3. Infuse these ingredients in a wide-mouthed bottle (closely stopped) for a fortnight, shaking the mixture every day.
4. Then strain and bottle it for use.
5. Put it up in small bottles, filling them quite full.
