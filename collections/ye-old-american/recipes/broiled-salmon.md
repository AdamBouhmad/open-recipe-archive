## Ingredients

- salmon slices
- salt
- pepper
- sifted flour
- suet

## Directions

1. Cut slices from an inch to an inch and a half thick
2. Dry them in a cloth
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Dredge them in sifted flour
5. Broil on a gridiron rubbed with suet
