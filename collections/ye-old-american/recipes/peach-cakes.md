## Ingredients

- five eggs
- half a pound of pulverized and sifted loaf sugar or crushed sugar
- half a pound of fine flour
- peach jam
- icing
- cochineal water

## Directions

1. Take the yolks and whites of five eggs and beat them separately, the whites to a stiff froth
2. Mix the beaten yolks with half a pound of pulverized and sifted loaf or crushed sugar, and beat the two together thoroughly for fifteen minutes
3. Add half a pound of fine flour, dredging it in a little at a time
4. Put in the whites of the eggs and beat the whole together for four or five minutes
5. Drop the batter upon a baking tin which has been buttered and floured, making cakes as nearly the same size as possible and resembling in shape the half of a peach
6. Have a quick oven ready and bake the cakes about ten minutes, watching closely so that they only come to a light brown color
7. Take them out and spread the flat side of each with peach jam
8. Stick them together in pairs and cover the outside with a thin coat of icing
9. When dry, brush over on one side of the cake with a little cochineal water
