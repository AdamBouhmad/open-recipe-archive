## Ingredients

- two dozen full ears of sweet green corn
- a pint and a half or one quart of milk
- four eggs well beaten
- a half teacupful of flour
- a half teacupful butter
- a tablespoonful of sugar
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Score the kernels and cut them from the cob
2. Scrape off what remains on the cob with a knife
3. Add milk according to the youngness and juiciness of the corn
4. Add four eggs well beaten, flour, butter, sugar, and salt to taste
5. Bake in a well-greased earthen dish in hot oven two hours
6. Place on the table browned and smoking hot
7. Eat with plenty of fresh butter or serve with a sweet sauce as dessert
