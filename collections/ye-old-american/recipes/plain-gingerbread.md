## Ingredients

- 2 cups molasses
- ½ cup lard
- ½ cup butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls soda dissolved in hot water
- 2 tablespoonfuls ginger
- 1 cup sour milk
- Flour to thicken to a soft dough

## Directions

1. Warm the molasses, lard, butter, and ginger, and beat them ten minutes before adding the milk, soda, and flour
2. Roll out, cut into shapes, and bake in a quick, but not too hot oven
3. Keep in a tight tin box
4. Brush over with white of egg while hot
