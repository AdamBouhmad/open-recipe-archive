## Ingredients

- stale bread
- poultry or fresh meat broth
- plums or dried currants
- butter or cream

## Directions

1. Put some grated crumbs of stale bread into a sauce-pan
2. Pour over them some of the liquor in which poultry or fresh meat has been boiled
3. Add some plums or dried currants that have been picked and washed
4. Simmer till the bread is quite soft and the currants well plumped
5. Add melted butter or cream
