## Ingredients

- peaches (large, white freestone)
- sugar (heaping tablespoonful per quart of fruit)
- water (one cupful optional)

## Directions

1. Pare, cut in half and stone, taking care not to break the fruit.
2. Drop each piece in cold water so soon as it is pared.
3. Allow a heaping tablespoonful of sugar to each quart of fruit, scattering it between the layers.
4. Fill your kettle and heat slowly to a boil.
5. Boil three minutes, just to assure yourself that every piece of fruit is heated through.
6. Can and seal.
7. It is safe to put a cupful of water in the bottom of the kettle before packing it with fruit, lest the lower layer should burn.
