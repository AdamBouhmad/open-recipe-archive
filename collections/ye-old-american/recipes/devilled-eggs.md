## Ingredients

- six or eight eggs
- cold water
- melted butter
- cayenne pepper
- mustard
- vinegar
- chopped cresses
- pepper
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil six or eight eggs hard; leave in cold water until they are cold.
2. Cut in halves, slicing a bit off the bottoms to make them stand upright, à la Columbus.
3. Extract the yolks, and rub to a smooth paste with a very little melted butter, some cayenne pepper, a touch of mustard, and just a dash of vinegar.
4. Fill the hollowed whites with this, and send to table upon a bed of chopped cresses, seasoned with pepper, salt, vinegar, and a little sugar.
5. The salad should be two inches thick, and an egg be served with a heaping tablespoonful of it.
