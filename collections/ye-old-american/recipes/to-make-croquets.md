## Ingredients

- cold fowl or fresh meat
- slices of ham
- stale bread
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- tea-spoonful made mustard
- table-spoonful catsup
- lump of butter
- egg yolk
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Take cold fowl or fresh meat of any kind with slices of ham, fat and lean
2. Chop them together very fine
3. Add half as much stale bread grated, salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, a tea-spoonful of made mustard, a table-spoonful of catsup, and a lump of butter
4. Knead all well together till it resembles sausage meat
5. Make them in cakes
6. Dip them in the yolk of an egg beaten
7. Cover them thickly with grated bread
8. Fry them a light brown
