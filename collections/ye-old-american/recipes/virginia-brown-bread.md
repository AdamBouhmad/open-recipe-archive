## Ingredients

- one pint of corn meal
- boiling water
- one pint of light, white bread sponge
- one cupful of molasses
- Graham flour

## Directions

1. pour boiling water over corn meal to thoroughly scald it
2. when cool add one pint of light, white bread sponge and mix well together
3. add one cupful of molasses and Graham flour enough to mold
4. when light, bake in a moderate oven one and a half hours
