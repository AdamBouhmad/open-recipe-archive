## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- salt
- flour
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Wash and put the leg in a steamer and cook it until tender, then place in a roasting pan, salt and dredge well with flour and set it in a hot oven until nicely browned.
2. The water that remains in the bottom of the steamer may be used for soup.
3. Serve with currant jelly.
