## Ingredients

- one quart of dried peas
- five quarts of water
- three or four large onions
- two heads of celery
- a carrot
- two turnips
- pepper
- salt
- one tablespoonful of cold butter
- small pieces of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Put a quart of dried peas into five quarts of water.
2. Boil for four hours.
3. Add three or four large onions, two heads of celery, a carrot, two turnips, all cut up rather fine.
4. Season with pepper and salt.
5. Boil two hours longer, and if the soup becomes too thick add more water.
6. Strain through a colander and stir in a tablespoonful of cold butter.
7. Serve hot, with small pieces of toasted bread placed in the bottom of the tureen.
