## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- made mustard

## Directions

1. Broil tomatoes upon an oyster gridiron for a few minutes until done.
2. Have ready in a cup hot butter seasoned with pepper, salt, a little sugar, and a half a teaspoonful of made mustard.
3. As soon as the tomatoes are done, dip each piece in this mixture and lay upon a hot chafing-dish.
4. When all are dished, heat what remains of the seasoning to a boil, pour upon them, and serve at once.
