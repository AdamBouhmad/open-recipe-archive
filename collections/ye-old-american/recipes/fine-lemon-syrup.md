## Ingredients

- lemons, large ripe
- loaf-sugar, best
- white of egg
- water, clear soft

## Directions

1. The best time for making lemon syrup is early in the spring
2. Before using the lemons for any purpose, wipe each lemon well and then roll it hard under your hand upon a table to soften it and increase the juice
3. Two dozen large ripe lemons will generally yield about a quart of juice if pressed with a wooden lemon-squeezer
4. To a quart of juice allow six pounds of the best loaf-sugar, broken up
5. Rub off the yellow rind or zest of the lemons on pieces of sugar
6. Put all the sugar into a large porcelain preserving-kettle
7. Beat to a stiff froth the whites of two eggs and mix it gradually with a quart of clear soft water
8. Add it to the sugar
9. Stir the sugar while it is melting in the water
10. When all is dissolved, place the kettle over the fire and boil and skim it till perfectly clear
