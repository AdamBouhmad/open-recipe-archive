## Ingredients

- two quarts of milk
- two-thirds of a cupful of rice
- a cupful of sugar
- a piece of butter as large as a walnut
- a teaspoonful of cinnamon
- a little nutmeg
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Put into a deep pudding-dish, well buttered
2. Set into a moderate oven
3. Stir it once or twice until it begins to cook
4. Let it remain in the oven about two hours until it is the consistency of cream
5. Eat cold
