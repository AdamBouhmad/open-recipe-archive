## Ingredients

- 1 teacupful tapioca
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 pint and a half water
- 6 tart apples
- sugar
- grated nutmeg
- lemon peel
- 1 teaspoonful melted butter
- 1 cupful cold milk
- 1/2 cupful sugar

## Directions

1. Put tapioca and salt into water and let stand several hours in warm place but not cook
2. Peel apples, take out cores, fill with sugar mixed with grated nutmeg and lemon peel
3. Put apples in pudding-dish
4. Mix tapioca with melted butter, cold milk and sugar
5. Pour over apples and bake one hour
6. Eat with sauce
7. When cool, serve with sugar and cream
