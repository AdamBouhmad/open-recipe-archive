## Ingredients

- yelks of two eggs
- raspings of bread sifted
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- mace powdered
- brains cut in pieces
- thick butter
- white wine
- water
- gravy

## Directions

1. Divide the calf's head, wash it clean
2. Having the yelks of two eggs well beaten, wash the outside of the head all over with them
3. On that strew raspings of bread sifted, pepper, salt, nutmeg and mace powdered
4. Also, the brains cut in pieces and dipped in thick butter
5. Then cover the head with bits of butter
6. Pour into the pan some white wine and water, with as much gravy
7. Cover it close
8. Let it be baked in a quick oven
9. When it is served up, pour on some strong gravy
10. And garnish with slices of lemon, red beet root pickled, fried oysters and fried bread
