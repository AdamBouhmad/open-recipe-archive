## Ingredients

- 9 oranges
- 6 lemons
- 1 cup grated pineapple
- 1 cup raspberry syrup
- 1½ cups tea infusion
- 1¼ cups sugar
- 1 cup hot water
- 1 quart Apollinaris

## Directions

1. Mix juice of oranges and lemons with pineapple, raspberry syrup, and tea; then add a syrup made by boiling sugar and water fifteen minutes.
2. Turn in punch-bowl over a large piece of ice.
3. Chill thoroughly, and just before serving add Apollinaris.
