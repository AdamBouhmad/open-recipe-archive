## Ingredients

- perfect, fresh peaches
- sugar
- blanched peach kernels
- whipped cream
- home-made sponge cakes

## Directions

1. Select perfect, fresh peaches, peel and core and cut in quarters
2. Well sugar them
3. Arrange in a trifle dish with a few of their own blanched kernels among them
4. Heap with whipped cream
5. Set on the ice for at least an hour before serving
6. Serve with home-made sponge cakes
