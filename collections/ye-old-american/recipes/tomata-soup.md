## Ingredients

- one fore-leg of beef
- one gallon of water
- pepper
- salt
- half a peck of ripe tomatas
- six onions
- crusts of bread
- bread cut into dice

## Directions

1. Cut up the fore-leg of beef into small pieces
2. Put the meat with the bones into a soup-pot, and cover it with a gallon of water
3. Season it with pepper, and a little salt
4. Boil and skim it well
5. Have ready half a peck of ripe tomatas cut up small; and when the soup is boiling thoroughly, put them in with all their juice
6. Add six onions sliced, and some crusts of bread cut small
7. Boil the soup slowly for six hours or more
8. When done, strain it through a cullender
9. Put into the tureen some pieces of bread cut into dice or small squares, and pour the soup upon it
