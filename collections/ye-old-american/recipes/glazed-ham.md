## Ingredients

- cold boiled ham
- beaten egg
- 1 cup of powdered cracker
- rich milk or cream
- salt
- 1 teaspoonful of melted butter

## Directions

1. Brush the ham well, all over with beaten egg
2. To a cup of powdered cracker allow enough rich milk or cream to make into a thick paste, salt, and work in a teaspoonful of melted butter
3. Spread this evenly a quarter of an inch thick over the ham, and set to brown in a moderate oven
