## Ingredients

- tablespoonful of cold lard and butter mixed
- mutton chops
- wheat flour or rolled cracker
- beaten egg
- salt
- pepper
- water or milk

## Directions

1. Put in a frying-pan a tablespoonful of cold lard and butter mixed; have some fine mutton chops without much fat; trim off the skin.
2. Dip into wheat flour, or rolled cracker, and beaten egg, then lay them into the hot grease, sprinkle with salt and pepper, fry on both sides a fine brown.
3. When done, take them up and place on a hot dish.
4. If you wish a made gravy, turn off the superfluous grease, if any, stir into the hot gravy remaining a heaping spoonful of cold water or milk; season with pepper and salt, let it boil up thick.
5. You can serve it in a separate dish or pour it over the chops.
