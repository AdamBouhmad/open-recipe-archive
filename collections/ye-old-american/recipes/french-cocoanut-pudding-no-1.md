## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- three tablespoonfuls of cornstarch
- the yolks of four eggs
- half a cupful of sugar
- a little salt
- a cupful of grated cocoanut
- vanilla
- the whites of four eggs
- lemon flavoring

## Directions

1. Put part of the milk, salt and sugar on the stove and let it boil
2. Dissolve the cornstarch in the rest of the milk
3. Stir into the milk and while boiling add the yolks and a cupful of grated cocoanut
4. Flavor with vanilla
5. Beat the whites of four eggs to a stiff froth with half a cupful of sugar and flavor with lemon
6. Spread the frosting on the pudding and put it into the oven to brown, saving a little of the frosting to moisten the top
7. Put on grated cocoanut to give it the appearance of snowflake
