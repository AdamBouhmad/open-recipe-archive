## Ingredients

- salsify
- egg
- milk
- flour
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- lard

## Directions

1. Stew the salsify as usual till very tender; then with the back of a spoon or a potato jammer mash it very fine.
2. Beat up an egg, add a teacupful of milk, a little flour, butter and seasoning of pepper and salt.
3. Make into little cakes, and fry a light brown in boiling lard.
