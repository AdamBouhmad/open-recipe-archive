## Ingredients

- two quarts of new milk
- two tablespoonfuls of prepared rennet
- two tablespoonfuls of powdered loaf sugar
- a small wine-glassful of pale brandy
- sugar and rich cream for serving

## Directions

1. Warm two quarts of new milk on the stove to about blood heat
2. Pour it into a glass or china bowl
3. Stir into it two tablespoonfuls of prepared rennet, two tablespoonfuls of powdered loaf sugar, and a small wine-glassful of pale brandy
4. Let it stand till cold
5. Eat with sugar and rich cream
