## Ingredients

- Two cups of flour
- Two cups of sweet milk
- Two eggs
- One teaspoonful of butter
- One teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Mix two cups of flour, two cups of sweet milk, two eggs, one teaspoonful of butter, one teaspoonful of salt
2. Bake in cups in a quick oven fifteen minutes
3. Serve hot with a sweet sauce
