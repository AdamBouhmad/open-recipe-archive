## Ingredients

- a pint of milk
- a large glass of sherry or Madeira
- a glass of currant wine

## Directions

1. Boil a pint of milk; and when it rises to the top of the sauce-pan, pour in a large glass of sherry or Madeira.
2. Add a glass of currant wine also.
3. Let it again boil up, and then take the sauce-pan off the fire, and set it aside to stand for a few minutes, but do not stir it.
4. Remove the curd, (if it has completely formed,) and pour the clear whey into a bowl and sweeten it.
