## Ingredients

- steak
- bone
- water
- potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- chopped onion
- butter
- browned flour
- paste

## Directions

1. Cut the steak into pieces an inch long
2. Stew with the bone (cracked) in just enough water to cover the meat until it is half-done
3. At the same time parboil a dozen potatoes in another pot
4. If you wish a bottom crust, line a pudding-dish with a good paste
5. Put in a layer of the beef, with salt and pepper, and a very little chopped onion
6. Then one of sliced potatoes, with a little butter scattered upon them
7. Continue layering until the dish is full
8. Pour over all the gravy in which the meat is stewed, having first thrown away the bone and thickened with browned flour
9. Cover with a crust thicker than the lower, leaving a slit in the middle
