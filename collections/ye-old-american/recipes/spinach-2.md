## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Examine and pick the spinach carefully, as insects are frequently found among it and it is often gritty
2. Wash it through three or four waters
3. Drain it and put it in boiling water
4. Boil for fifteen to twenty minutes
5. Be careful to remove the scum
6. When it is quite tender, take it up, and drain and squeeze it well
7. Chop it fine
8. Put it into a saucepan with a piece of butter and a little pepper and salt
9. Set it on the fire and let it stew five minutes, stirring it all the time, until quite dry
10. Turn it into a vegetable dish and shape it into a mound
11. Slice some hard-boiled eggs and lay around the top
