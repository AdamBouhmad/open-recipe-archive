## Ingredients

- blackberries
- cold water
- one gallon of juice and three pounds of common brown sugar
- brown paper

## Directions

1. Cover your blackberries with cold water
2. Crush the berries well with a wooden masher
3. Let them stand twenty-four hours
4. Strain, and to one gallon of juice put three pounds of common brown sugar
5. Put into wide-mouthed jars for several days, carefully skimming off the scum that will rise to the top
6. Put in several sheets of brown paper and let them remain in it three days
7. Skim again and pour through a funnel into your cask
8. Let it remain undisturbed till March
9. Strain again and bottle
