## Ingredients

- 1 pound of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- A little salt
- Sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix with sweet milk into a stiff dough
2. Roll out very thin
3. Cut into round cakes
4. Roll these as thin as they can be handled
5. Lift them carefully
6. Lay in a pan
7. Bake very quickly
8. Flour the baking-pan instead of greasing
