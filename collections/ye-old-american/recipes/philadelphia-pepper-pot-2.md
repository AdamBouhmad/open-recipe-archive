## Ingredients

- ¼ cup sliced onion
- ¼ cup chopped celery
- ¼ cup chopped green peppers
- 4 tablespoons butter
- 3½ tablespoons flour
- 5 cups hot White Stock III
- ½ lb. honeycomb tripe, cut in cubes
- 1½ cups potato cubes
- ½ teaspoon peppercorns, finely pounded
- ¾ tablespoon salt
- ½ cup heavy cream

## Directions

1. Cook vegetables in three tablespoons butter fifteen minutes; add flour, and stir until well mixed; then add remaining ingredients except cream.
2. Cover, and let cook one hour.
3. Just before serving, add cream and remaining butter.
