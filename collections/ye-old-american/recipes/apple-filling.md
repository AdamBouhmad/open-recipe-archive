## Ingredients

- green tart apples
- sugar
- a small piece of butter

## Directions

1. Peel and slice green tart apples
2. Put them on the fire with sugar to suit
3. When tender, remove, rub them through a fine sieve and add a small piece of butter
4. When cold, use to spread between the layers
5. Cover the cake with plenty of sugar
