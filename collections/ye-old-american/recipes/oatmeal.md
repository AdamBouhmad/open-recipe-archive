## Ingredients

- one cup of oatmeal
- one quart of water
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak one cup of oatmeal in a quart of water over night
2. Boil half an hour in the morning, salted to taste
3. It is better to cook it in a dish set into a dish of boiling water
