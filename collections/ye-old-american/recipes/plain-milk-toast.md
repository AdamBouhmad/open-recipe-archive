## Ingredients

- a thin slice from a loaf of stale bread
- salt
- three tablespoonfuls of boiling milk or cream

## Directions

1. Cut a thin slice from a loaf of stale bread, toast it very quickly
2. Sprinkle a little salt over it, and pour upon it three tablespoonfuls of boiling milk or cream
