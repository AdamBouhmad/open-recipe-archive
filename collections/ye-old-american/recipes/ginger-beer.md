## Ingredients

- 2 gallons boiling water
- 2 pounds brown sugar
- 1 and a half ounce cream of tartar
- 1 and a half ounce pounded ginger
- half a pint good yeast

## Directions

1. Pour two gallons of boiling water on two pounds brown sugar, one and a half ounce of cream of tartar, and the same of pounded ginger
2. Stir them well, and put it in a small cask
3. When milk warm, put in half a pint of good yeast
4. Shake the cask well, and stop it close
5. In twenty-four hours it will be fit to bottle
6. Cork it very well, and in ten days it will sparkle like Champaigne
