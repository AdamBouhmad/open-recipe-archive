## Ingredients

- one coffeecupful of finely powdered sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Upon a coffeecupful of finely powdered sugar pour just enough lemon juice to dissolve it
2. Boil it to the consistency of thick syrup, and so that it appears brittle when dropped in cold water
3. Drop this on buttered plates in drops
4. Set away to cool and harden
