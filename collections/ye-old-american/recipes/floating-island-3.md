## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 5 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls (heaping) white sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls extract bitter almond or vanilla
- ½ cup currant jelly

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks well, stir in the sugar, and add the hot, not boiling milk, a little at a time
2. Boil until it begins to thicken
3. When cool, flavor and pour into a glass dish, first stirring it up well
4. Heap upon it a méringue of the whites into which you have beaten, gradually, half a cup of currant, cranberry, or other bright tart jelly
5. Dot with bits of jelly cut into rings or stars, or straight slips laid on in a pattern
