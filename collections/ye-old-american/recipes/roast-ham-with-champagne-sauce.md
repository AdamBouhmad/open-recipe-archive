## Ingredients

- whole baked ham
- Champagne Sauce

## Directions

1. Place a whole baked ham in the oven fifteen minutes before serving time, that outside fat may be heated.
2. Remove to a hot platter, garnish bone end with a paper ruffle, and serve with Champagne Sauce.
