## Ingredients

- white sponge cake
- yolks of six eggs
- three ounces of sugar
- a saltspoonful of salt
- a quart of milk
- two teaspoonfuls of vanilla
- whites of four eggs
- four tablespoonfuls of sugar
- lemon or vanilla

## Directions

1. Make a rule of white sponge cake and bake in narrow shallow pans
2. Wet a saucepan with cold water to prevent the milk that will be scalded in it from burning
3. Pour out the water and put in a quart of milk and boil and partly cool
4. Beat up the yolks of six eggs and add three ounces of sugar and a saltspoonful of salt and mix thoroughly
5. Add the lukewarm milk
6. Stir and pour the custard into a porcelain or double saucepan and stir while on the range until of the consistency of cream
7. Do not allow it to boil, as that would curdle it
8. Strain, and when almost cold add two teaspoonfuls of vanilla
9. Having arranged your cake (cut into inch slices) around the sides and on the bottom of a glass dish, pour over the custard
10. If you wish a meringue on the top, beat up the whites of four eggs with four tablespoonfuls of sugar and flavor with lemon or vanilla
11. Spread over the top and brown slightly in the oven
