## Ingredients

- cream cheese
- cream
- salt
- cayenne
- six olives
- lettuce
- one-half a can pimento
- Mayonnaise Dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Mash a cream cheese, moisten with cream, and season with salt and cayenne
2. Add six olives finely chopped, lettuce finely cut, and one-half a can pimento cut in strips
3. Press in original shape of cheese and let stand two hours
4. Cut in slices, separate in pieces, and serve on lettuce leaves with Mayonnaise Dressing
