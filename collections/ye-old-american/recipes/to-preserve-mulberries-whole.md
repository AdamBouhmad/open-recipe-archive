## Ingredients

- mulberries
- three pounds of sugar
- one pint of juice
- two pounds of ripe mulberries

## Directions

1. Set some mulberries over the fire in skillet or preserving pan
2. Draw from them a pint of juice when it is strained
3. Take three pounds of sugar beaten very fine, wet the sugar with the pint of juice
4. Boil up your sugar and skim it
5. Put in two pounds of ripe mulberries, and let them stand in the sirrup till they are thoroughly warm
6. Then set them on the fire, and let them boil very gently
7. Do them but half enough, so put them by in the sirrup till next day
8. Then boil them gently again
9. When the sirrup is pretty thick, and will stand in round drops when it is cold, they are done enough
10. So put all into a gallipot for use
