## Ingredients

- a handful of hops
- about three quarts of water

## Directions

1. Let it boil about fifteen minutes, then make a thickening as you do for starch, strain the liquor, when cold put a little emptins to work them, they will keep well cork'd in a bottle five or six weeks.
