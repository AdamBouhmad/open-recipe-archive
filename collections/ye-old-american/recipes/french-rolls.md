## Ingredients

- a quart of flour
- a little salt
- a spoonful of yeast
- two eggs well beaten
- half a pint of milk
- an ounce of butter

## Directions

1. Sift a quart of flour, add a little salt, a spoonful of yeast, two eggs well beaten, and half a pint of milk
2. Knead it, and set it to rise
3. Next morning, work in an ounce of butter
4. Make the dough into small rolls, and bake them
5. The top crust should not be hard
