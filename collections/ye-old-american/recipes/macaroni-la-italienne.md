## Ingredients

- one quarter of a pound of macaroni
- boiling water
- salt
- strong soup stock
- one ounce of grated cheese

## Directions

1. Divide a quarter of a pound of macaroni into four-inch pieces
2. Simmer fifteen minutes in plenty of boiling water, salted
3. Drain
4. Put the macaroni into a saucepan and turn over it a strong soup stock, enough to prevent burning
5. Strew over it an ounce of grated cheese
6. When the cheese is melted, dish
7. Put alternate layers of macaroni and cheese
8. Turn over the soup stock and bake half an hour
