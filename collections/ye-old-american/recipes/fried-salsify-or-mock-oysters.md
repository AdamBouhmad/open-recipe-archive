## Ingredients

- salsify roots
- water
- milk
- butter
- egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Scrape the roots thoroughly, and lay in cold water ten or fifteen minutes.
2. Boil whole until tender, drain, and when cold, mash with a wooden spoon to a smooth paste, picking out all the fibres.
3. Moisten with a little milk; add a tablespoonful of butter, and an egg and a half for every cupful of salsify.
4. Beat the eggs light.
5. Make into round cakes, dredge with flour, and fry brown.
