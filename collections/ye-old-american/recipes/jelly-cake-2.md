## Ingredients

- 1 lb. sugar
- 1 lb. flour
- ½ lb. butter
- 6 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- ½ teaspoonful soda
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar

## Directions

1. Bake in shallow tins, and when cold put jelly between.
