## Ingredients

- 1 quart Brown Soup Stock
- 1 can tomatoes
- ½ teaspoon peppercorns
- 1 small bay leaf
- 3 cloves
- 3 sprigs thyme
- 4 tablespoons butter
- ⅓ cup flour
- ¼ cup onion, cut in dice
- ¼ cup carrot, cut in dice
- ¼ cup celery, cut in dice
- ¼ cup raw ham, cut in dice
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook onion, carrot, celery, and ham in butter five minutes.
2. Add flour, peppercorns, bay leaf, cloves, and thyme, and cook three minutes.
3. Add tomatoes, cover, and cook slowly one hour.
4. When cooked in oven it requires less watching.
5. Rub through a strainer, add hot stock, and season with salt and pepper.
