## Ingredients

- parsnips
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- cream or milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil tender, scrape and slice lengthwise.
2. Put over the fire with two tablespoonfuls of butter, pepper and salt and a little minced parsley.
3. Shake until the mixture boils.
4. Dish the parsnips, add to the sauce three tablespoonfuls of cream or milk in which has been stirred a quarter of a spoonful of flour.
5. Boil once and pour over the parsnips.
