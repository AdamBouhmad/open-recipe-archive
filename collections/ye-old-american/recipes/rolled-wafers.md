## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup powdered sugar
- ¼ cup milk
- ⅞ cup bread flour
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, and milk drop by drop; then add flour and flavoring.
2. Spread very thinly with a broad, long-bladed knife on a buttered inverted dripping-pan.
3. Crease in three-inch squares, and bake in a slow oven until delicately browned.
4. Place pan on back of range, cut squares apart with a sharp knife, and roll while warm in tubular or cornucopia shape.
5. If squares become too brittle to roll, place in oven to soften.
6. If rolled tubular shape, tie in bunches with narrow ribbon.
7. If rolled cornucopia shape, they may be filled with whipped cream just before sending to table.
