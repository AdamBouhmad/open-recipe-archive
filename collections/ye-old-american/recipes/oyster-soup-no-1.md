## Ingredients

- 2 qts. of oysters
- 1 qt. of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 teacupful water
- pepper and salt
- sliced lemon
- oyster or cream crackers

## Directions

1. Strain the liquor from the oysters, add to it the water, and set it over the fire to heat slowly in a covered vessel
2. When it is near boiling, season with pepper and salt; add the oysters, and let them stew until they ruffle on the edge, about five minutes
3. Put in the butter with the milk which has been heated in a separate vessel, and stir well for two minutes
4. Serve with sliced lemon and oyster or cream crackers
