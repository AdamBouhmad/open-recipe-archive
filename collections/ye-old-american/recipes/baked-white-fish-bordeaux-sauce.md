## Ingredients

- white fish
- butter
- flour
- claret
- brown flour
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Clean and stuff the fish
2. Put it in a baking pan and add a liberal quantity of butter, previously rolled in flour, to the fish
3. Put in the pan half a pint of claret
4. Bake for an hour and a quarter
5. Remove the fish and strain the gravy
6. Add to the latter a gill more of claret, a teaspoonful of brown flour and a pinch of cayenne
7. Serve with the fish
