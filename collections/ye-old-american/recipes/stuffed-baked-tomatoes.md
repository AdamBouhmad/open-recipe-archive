## Ingredients

- large smooth tomatoes
- grated bread
- green corn
- salt
- pepper
- white sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Choose large, smooth tomatoes and cut a thin slice from the blossom end of each, laying it aside for further use.
2. Scoop out the inside and chop fine with a little grated bread, some green corn, salt, pepper, a teaspoonful white sugar, and a tablespoonful butter.
3. Mix well and stuff the hollowed tomatoes.
4. Fit the tops on neatly.
5. Place in circular rows in a deep dish.
6. Bake three-quarters of an hour, to a light brown.
7. Fill the interstices with the force-meat if you have any left, before you bake.
8. Do not peel them.
