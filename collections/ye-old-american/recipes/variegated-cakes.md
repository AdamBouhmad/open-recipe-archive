## Ingredients

- one cup powdered sugar
- one-half cup of butter
- one-half cup of milk
- four eggs, whites only
- two and one-half cups prepared flour
- bitter almond flavoring
- spinach juice
- cochineal
- pistachio nuts

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. Add the milk, flavoring, the whites and flour
3. Divide the batter into three parts
4. Bruise and pound a few leaves of spinach in a thin muslin bag until you can express the juice
5. Put a few drops of spinach juice into one portion of the batter
6. Color another with cochineal, leaving the third white
7. Put a little of each into small, round pans or cups, giving a light stir to each color as you add the next to vein the cakes prettily
8. Put the white between the pink and green so the tints show better
9. If available, pound up pistachio nuts for the green
10. Ice on sides and top
