## Ingredients

- two endive
- chives
- Mayonnaise dressing

## Directions

1. Take two endive, cut away the root, remove the dark green leaves, and pick off all the rest
2. Wash and drain well
3. Add a few chives
4. Dress with Mayonnaise dressing
