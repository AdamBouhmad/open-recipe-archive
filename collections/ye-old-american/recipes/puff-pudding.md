## Ingredients

- six eggs
- six spoonsful of milk
- six spoonsful of flour
- butter
- sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs
2. Add six spoonsful of milk, and six of flour
3. Butter some cups
4. Pour in the batter, and bake them quickly
5. Turn them out, and eat them with butter, sugar and nutmeg
