## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 unbeaten eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Heat omelet pan, put in two tablespoons butter, and when melted turn in four unbeaten eggs.
2. Cook until white is partially set, then stir until cooking is completed, when whites will be thoroughly set.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
