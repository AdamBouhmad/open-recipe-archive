## Ingredients

- Brioche dough
- white of one egg
- 1-half tablespoon cold water
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Shape Brioche dough in sticks similar to Bread Sticks.
2. Place on a buttered sheet, cover, and let rise fifteen minutes.
3. Brush over with white of one egg slightly beaten and diluted with one-half tablespoon cold water.
4. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and bake ten minutes.
