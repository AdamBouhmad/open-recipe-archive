## Ingredients

- Live lobster
- 1 tablespoon butter
- Salt
- Cayenne
- Worcestershire Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare lobster same as for Broiled Live Lobster and place in a dripping-pan.
2. Cook liver of lobster with one tablespoon butter three minutes.
3. Season highly with salt, cayenne, and Worcestershire Sauce.
4. Spread over lobster, and bake in a hot oven fifteen minutes.
5. Remove to platter and serve at once, allowing over one and one-half pound lobster to each person.
