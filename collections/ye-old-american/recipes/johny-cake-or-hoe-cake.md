## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 3 pints of Indian meal
- half pint of flower
- salt
- molasses
- shortening

## Directions

1. Scald 1 pint of milk and put to 3 pints of Indian meal, and half pint of flower—bake before the fire. Or scald with milk two thirds of the Indian meal, or wet two thirds with boiling water, add salt, molasses and shortening, work up with cold water pretty stiff, and bake as above
