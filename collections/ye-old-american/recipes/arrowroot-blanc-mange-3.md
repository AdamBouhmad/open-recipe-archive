## Ingredients

- One large cupful of boiling milk
- One even tablespoonful of arrowroot
- Cold water
- Two teaspoonfuls of white sugar
- A pinch of salt
- Rose-water
- Cream
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Rub one even tablespoonful of arrowroot to a paste with cold water
2. Add the arrowroot to the boiling milk with two teaspoonfuls of white sugar and a pinch of salt
3. Flavor with rose-water
4. Boil and stir eight minutes
5. Turn into a wet mold and let firm
6. Serve with cream and powdered sugar
