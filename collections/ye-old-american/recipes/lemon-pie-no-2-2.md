## Ingredients

- one coffee cupful of sugar
- three eggs
- one cupful of water
- one tablespoonful of melted butter
- one heaping tablespoonful of flour
- the juice and a little of the rind of one lemon

## Directions

1. Reserve the whites of the eggs
2. Cook the mixture in a double boiler or dish
3. Pour into the crust or bake first
4. Bake from thirty-five to forty minutes
5. After the pie is baked, spread the whites over the top beaten lightly with a spoonful of sugar
6. Return to the oven until it is a light brown
