## Ingredients

- round of beef
- equal quantity of cloves and mace
- pickle

## Directions

1. The best part for this purpose is the round, which you must desire the butcher to cut into four pieces.
2. Wash the meat and dry it well in a cloth.
3. Grind or beat to powder an equal quantity of cloves and mace, and having mixed them together, rub them well into the beef with your hand.
4. Have ready a pickle made precisely as that in the preceding article.
5. Boil and skim it, and (the meat having been thoroughly rubbed all over with the spice) pour on the pickle as before directed.
6. Keep the beef in the pickle at least six weeks, and then smoke it about three weeks.
7. Corn cobs make a good fire for smoking meat.
