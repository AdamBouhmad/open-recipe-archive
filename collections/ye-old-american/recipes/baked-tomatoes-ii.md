## Ingredients

- six small, selected tomatoes
- sauce for Devilled Tomatoes
- one tablespoon heavy cream

## Directions

1. Wipe six small, selected tomatoes and make two one-inch gashes on blossom end of each, having gashes cross each other at right angles.
2. Place in granite-ware pan and bake until thoroughly heated.
3. Serve with sauce for Devilled Tomatoes, adding, just before serving, one tablespoon heavy cream.
