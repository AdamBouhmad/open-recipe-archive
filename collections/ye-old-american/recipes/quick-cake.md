## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup soft butter
- 1⅓ cups brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 1¾ cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon grated nutmeg
- ½ lb. dates, stoned and cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Put ingredients in a bowl and beat all together for three minutes, using a wooden cake spoon.
2. Bake in a buttered and floured cake pan thirty-five to forty minutes.
