## Ingredients

- half a cup of butter
- one tablespoonful of mixed mustard
- one tablespoonful of salad oil
- a little red or white pepper
- a pinch of salt
- the yolk of an egg
- chopped ham
- thin slices of bread

## Directions

1. Rub the butter to a cream
2. Add the mustard, salad oil, pepper and salt
3. Mix thoroughly
4. Stir in as much chopped ham as will make it consistent
5. Spread between thin slices of bread
6. Omit salad oil and substitute melted butter if preferred
