## Ingredients

- one cup of well-washed rice
- one pint of milk
- two ounces of butter
- two ounces of sugar
- salt
- yolks of two eggs well beaten
- beaten eggs
- fine cracker or bread crumbs
- tablespoonful each of butter and lard mixed

## Directions

1. Boil for thirty minutes one cup of well-washed rice in a pint of milk
2. Whip into the hot rice two ounces of butter, two ounces of sugar, some salt, and when slightly cool add the yolks of two eggs well beaten
3. If too stiff pour in a little more milk
4. When cold, roll into small balls and dip in beaten eggs, roll in fine cracker or bread crumbs, and fry same as doughnuts
5. Or they may be fried in the frying pan, with a tablespoonful each of butter and lard mixed, turning and frying both sides brown
6. Serve very hot
