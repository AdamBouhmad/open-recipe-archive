## Ingredients

- potatoes
- deep fat
- salt
- small fillets of fried fish or fried smelts

## Directions

1. Wash, pare, and cut potatoes in thin strips, using same slicer as for Lattice Potatoes
2. Soak in cold water fifteen minutes, drain, and dry between towels
3. Line a fine wire strainer of four-inch diameter, and having a wire handle, with potatoes
4. place a similar strainer, having a two and one-half inch diameter, in larger strainer, thus holding potatoes in nest shapes
5. Fry in deep fat, taking care that the fat does not reach too high a temperature at first
6. Keep the small strainer in place during frying with a long handled spoon
7. Carefully remove nests from strainers
8. Drain on brown paper, and sprinkle with salt
9. Fill with small fillets of fried fish or fried smelts
