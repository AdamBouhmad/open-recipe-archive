## Ingredients

- 2 oranges
- 3 bananas
- ½ lb. Malaga grapes
- 12 English walnut meats
- 1 head lettuce
- French Dressing

## Directions

1. Peel oranges, and remove pulp separately from each section.
2. Peel bananas, and cut in one-fourth inch slices.
3. Remove skins and seeds from grapes.
4. Break walnut meats in pieces.
5. Mix prepared ingredients and arrange on lettuce leaves.
6. Serve with French Dressing.
