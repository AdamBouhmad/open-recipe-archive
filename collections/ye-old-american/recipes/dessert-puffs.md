## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- one pint of cream
- the whites of four eggs
- one heaping cupful of sifted flour
- one scant cupful of powdered sugar
- a little grated lemon peel
- a little salt
- pulverized sugar
- sauce flavored with lemon

## Directions

1. Take one pint of milk and cream each, the whites of four eggs beaten to a stiff froth, one heaping cupful of sifted flour, one scant cupful of powdered sugar, add a little grated lemon peel and a little salt
2. Beat these all together till very light
3. Bake in gem-pans
4. Sift pulverized sugar over them and eat with sauce flavored with lemon
