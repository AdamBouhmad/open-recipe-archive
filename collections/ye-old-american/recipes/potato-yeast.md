## Ingredients

- half a dozen middle-sized potatoes
- a quart of soft water
- a handful of hops
- a handful of wheat flour
- four table-spoonfuls of strong yeast

## Directions

1. Pare half a dozen middle-sized potatoes, and boil them in a quart of soft water, mixed with a handful of hops, till quite soft
2. Mash the potatoes smooth, not leaving in a single lump
3. Mix with them a handful of wheat flour
4. Set a sieve over the pan in which you have the flour and mashed potatoes, and strain into them the hop-water in which they were boiled
5. Stir the mixture very hard, and afterwards pass it through a cullender to clear it of lumps
6. Let it stand till it is nearly cold
7. Stir in four table-spoonfuls of strong yeast, and let it stand to ferment
8. When the foam has sunk down in the middle, it is done working
9. Put it into a stone jug and cork it
10. Set it in a cool place
