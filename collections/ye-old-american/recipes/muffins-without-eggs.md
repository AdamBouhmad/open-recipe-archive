## Ingredients

- One quart of buttermilk
- One teaspoonful of soda dissolved in the milk
- Salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in the buttermilk
2. Add a little salt and flour enough to make a stiff batter
3. Drop in hot gem-pans
4. Bake in a quick oven
