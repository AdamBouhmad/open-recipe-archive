## Ingredients

- six good-sized potatoes
- one tablespoonful of butter
- one egg
- one pint of sweet milk
- half a cup of yeast
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil six good-sized potatoes with their jackets on
2. Take them out with a skimmer, drain and squeeze with a towel to ensure being dry
3. Remove the skin and mash them perfectly free from lumps
4. Add a tablespoonful of butter, one egg and a pint of sweet milk
5. When cool, beat in half a cup of yeast
6. Put in just enough flour to make a stiff dough
7. When this rises, make into small cakes
8. Let them rise the same as biscuit and bake a delicate brown
