## Ingredients

- sturgeon
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- butter
- lemon
- bread crumbs
- sweet herbs
- pepper
- beaten yolk of egg
- white paper

## Directions

1. Carefully take off the skin, as its oiliness will give the fish a strong and disagreeable taste when cooked
2. Cut from the tail-piece slices about half an inch thick
3. Rub them with salt, and broil them over a clear fire of bright coals
4. Butter them, sprinkle them with cayenne pepper, and send them to table hot, garnished with sliced lemon
5. Alternatively, make a seasoning of bread crumbs, sweet herbs, pepper and salt
6. First dip the slices of sturgeon in beaten yolk of egg, then cover them with seasoning
7. Wrap them up closely in sheets of white paper well buttered
8. Broil them over a clear fire, and send them to table either with or without the papers
