## Ingredients

- one quart of sweet cream
- the yolks of four eggs
- one cupful of sugar
- half an ounce of gelatine or isinglass
- half a teacupful of warm water
- one pint of boiling hot cream
- one pint of cold cream
- vanilla or lemon for seasoning
- the whites of the eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of four eggs together with a cupful of sugar
2. Dissolve half an ounce of gelatine or isinglass in half a teacupful of warm water
3. When it is dissolved stir in a pint of boiling hot cream
4. Add the beaten yolks and sugar and cook all together until it begins to thicken
5. Remove from the fire and add the other pint of cold cream whipped to a stiff froth, adding a little at a time and beating hard
6. Season with vanilla or lemon
7. Whip the whites of the eggs for the top
8. Dip the mold in cold water before filling
9. Set it in a cold place
