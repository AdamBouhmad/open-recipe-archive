## Ingredients

- one pound soft bread or biscuit
- one quart milk
- 7 eggs
- three quarters of a pound sugar
- one quarter of a pound butter
- nutmeg or cinnamon
- one gill rose-water
- one pound stoned raisins
- half pint cream

## Directions

1. soaked in one quart milk
2. run thro' a sieve or cullender
3. add 7 eggs
4. three quarters of a pound sugar
5. one quarter of a pound butter
6. nutmeg or cinnamon
7. one gill rose-water
8. one pound stoned raisins
9. half pint cream
10. bake three quarters of an hour
11. middling oven
