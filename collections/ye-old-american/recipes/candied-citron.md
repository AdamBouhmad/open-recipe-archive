## Ingredients

- citron slices
- ginger water
- sugar
- lemon parings
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put the citron slices into a preserving-kettle, and pour the ginger water over it.
2. When the sugar is all melted, set it over the fire, add the lemon parings, and boil and skim it, till no more scum rises.
3. Then take out the lemon peel, stir in the juice, and put in the citron slices.
4. Boil them in the syrup till they are transparent and soft, but not till they break.
5. When done, put the citron slices and syrup into a large tureen, set it in a dry, cool, dark place, and leave it uncovered for two or three days.
6. Then put the slices carefully into wide-mouthed glass jars, and gently pour in the syrup.
7. Lay inside the top of each jar a double white tissue paper cut exactly to fit, and close the jars carefully with corks and cement.
