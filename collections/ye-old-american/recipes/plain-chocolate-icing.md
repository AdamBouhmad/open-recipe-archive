## Ingredients

- four tablespoonfuls of scraped chocolate
- three tablespoonfuls of milk or cream
- one tablespoonful of water
- one scant teacupful of sugar

## Directions

1. Put chocolate into a shallow pan and place where it will melt gradually, but not scorch
2. When melted, stir in milk or cream and water
3. Mix all well together and add sugar
4. Boil about five minutes
5. While hot, spread some evenly over the surface of one cake when the cakes are nearly cold
6. Put a second cake on top, alternating the mixture and cakes
7. Cover top and sides
8. Set in a warm oven to harden
