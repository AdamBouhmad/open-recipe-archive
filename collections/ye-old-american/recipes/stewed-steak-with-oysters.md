## Ingredients

- two pounds rump steak
- one pint oysters
- one tablespoonful lemon juice
- three tablespoonfuls butter
- one tablespoonful flour
- salt
- pepper
- one cupful water

## Directions

1. Wash the oysters in the water and drain into a stewpan
2. Put this liquor on to heat
3. As soon as it comes to a boil, skim and set back
4. Put the butter in a frying pan and when hot, put in the steak
5. Cook ten minutes
6. Take up the steak and stir the flour into the butter remaining in the pan
7. Stir until a dark brown
8. Add the oyster liquor and boil one minute
9. Season with salt and pepper
10. Put back the steak and cover the pan
11. Simmer half an hour or until the steak seems tender
12. Add the oysters and lemon juice
13. Boil one minute
14. Serve on a hot dish with points of toast for a garnish
