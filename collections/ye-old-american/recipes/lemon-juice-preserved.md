## Ingredients

- lemons
- 1 pound good loaf sugar per pint of juice
- French brandy

## Directions

1. Get lemons quite free from blemish
2. Squeeze them, and strain the juice
3. To each pint of it, put a pound of good loaf sugar pounded
4. Stir it frequently until the sugar is completely dissolved
5. Cover the pitcher closely, and let it stand till the dregs have subsided, and the syrup is transparent
6. Have bottles perfectly clean and dry
7. Put a wine glass full of French brandy into each bottle
8. Fill it with syrup
9. Cork it, and dip the neck into melted rosin or pitch
10. Keep them in a cool dry cellar
