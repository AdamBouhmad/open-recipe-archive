## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Peel the finest and freshest you can get, score the under side, and cut the stems close.
2. Put into a deep dish and anoint well, once and again, with melted butter.
3. Salt and pepper, and let them lie in the butter an hour and a half.
4. Then broil over a clear, hot fire, using an oyster-gridiron, and turning it over as one side browns.
5. Serve hot, well buttered, pepper and salt, and squeeze a few drops of lemon-juice upon each.
