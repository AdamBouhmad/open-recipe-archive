## Ingredients

- two pounds of nice brown sugar
- three quarts of flour
- a large cup full of powdered ginger
- one pound of butter
- twelve eggs
- a glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of the nicest brown sugar, dry and pound it
2. Put it into three quarts of flour
3. Add a large cup full of powdered ginger
4. Sift the mixture
5. Wash the salt out of a pound of butter, and cream it
6. Have twelve eggs well beaten
7. Work into the butter first, the mixture, then the froth from the eggs, until all are in, and it is quite light
8. Add a glass of brandy
9. Pour it into butter shallow moulds
10. Bake in a quick oven
