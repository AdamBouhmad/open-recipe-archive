## Ingredients

- currants
- sugar (the weight of the currants)
- half a pint of water

## Directions

1. Take the weight of the currants in sugar
2. Pick out the seeds
3. Take to a pound of sugar, half a pint of water, let it melt
4. Then put in your currants and let them do very leisurely
5. Skim them, and take them up
6. Let the sirrup boil
7. Then put them on again
8. And when they are clear, and the sirrup thick enough, take them off
9. And when they are cold, put them up in glasses
