## Ingredients

- half a pound of grated cocoanut
- half a cupful of stale sponge cake, crumbled fine
- half a cupful of butter
- one cupful of sugar
- a coffee cupful of rich milk or cream
- six eggs
- two teaspoonfuls of vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix the grated cocoanut with half a cupful of stale sponge cake, crumbled fine
2. Stir together until very light half a cupful of butter and one cupful of sugar
3. Add a coffee cupful of rich milk or cream
4. Beat six eggs very light and stir them gradually into the butter and sugar in turn, with the grated cocoanut
5. Having stirred the whole very hard, add two teaspoonfuls of vanilla
6. Stir again, put into a buttered dish and bake until set, or about three-quarters of an hour
