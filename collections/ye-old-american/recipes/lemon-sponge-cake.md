## Ingredients

- one level cup of flour
- one level teaspoonful of baking powder
- one lemon
- four eggs
- one scant cup of white granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Put a level teaspoonful of baking powder into one level cup of flour and sift it
2. Grate off the yellow rind of a lemon
3. Separate the whites from the yolks of four eggs
4. Measure a scant cup of white granulated sugar and beat it to a cream with the yolks
5. add the grated rind and a tablespoonful of the juice of the lemon
6. Stir together until thick and creamy
7. beat the whites to a stiff froth
8. quickly and lightly mix without beating a third of the flour with the yolks
9. then a third of the whites
10. then more flour and whites until all are used
11. The mode of mixing must be very light, rather cutting down through the cake batter than to beating it
12. Bake immediately until a straw run into it can be withdrawn clean
