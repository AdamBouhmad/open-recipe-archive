## Ingredients

- one cup powdered sugar
- one-fourth cup hot water
- white of an egg
- one-half cup chopped raisins
- one-half cup chopped walnuts
- one tablespoonful of grated cocoanut

## Directions

1. Let the sugar and water simmer
2. Beat white of an egg and mix with the above
3. When cold, add chopped raisins, chopped walnuts, and grated cocoanut
