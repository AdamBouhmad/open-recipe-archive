## Ingredients

- One coffeecupful of white sugar
- the grated rind and juice of one large lemon
- the yolks of three eggs
- the white of one egg
- a tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Put into a basin the sugar and butter, set it in a dish of boiling water over the fire
2. While this is melting, beat up the eggs, and add to them the grated rind from the outside of the lemon
3. Add this to the sugar and butter
4. Cook and stir it until it is thick and clear like honey
5. Put into a tight preserve jar
