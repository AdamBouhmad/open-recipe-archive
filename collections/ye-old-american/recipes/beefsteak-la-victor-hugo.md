## Ingredients

- porterhouse steak
- one-half teaspoon finely chopped shallot
- one tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- one-third cup butter
- yolks of two eggs
- one teaspoon lemon juice
- one teaspoon meat extract
- one-half tablespoon grated horseradish

## Directions

1. Wipe a porterhouse steak and broil.
2. Cook one-half teaspoon finely chopped shallot in one tablespoon tarragon vinegar five minutes.
3. Wash one-third cup butter, and divide in thirds.
4. Add one piece butter to mixture, with yolks two eggs, one teaspoon lemon juice, and one teaspoon meat extract.
5. Cook over hot water, stirring constantly; as soon as butter is melted, add second piece, and then third piece.
6. When mixture thickens, add one-half tablespoon grated horseradish.
7. Serve steak with sauce.
