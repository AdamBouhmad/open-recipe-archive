## Ingredients

- apricots
- sugar (the weight of your apricots)
- water (as needed)
- plumbs (before they have stones in them)
- pint of water

## Directions

1. Take your apricots and pare them, then stone what you can whole
2. Give them a light boiling in a pint of water, or according to your quantity of fruit
3. Take the weight of your apricots in sugar, and take the liquor which you boil them in, and your sugar, and boil it till it comes to a sirrup
4. Take the scum as it rises
5. When the sirrup jellies, it is enough
6. Take up the apricots, and cover them with the jelly
7. Put cut paper over them, and lay them down when cold
8. Or, take your plumbs before they have stones in them, which you may know by putting a pin through them
9. Codle them in many waters till they are as green as grass
10. Peel them and codle them again
11. You must take the weight of them in sugar and make a sirrup
12. Put to your sugar a pint of water
13. Then put them in, set them on the fire to boil slowly till they be clear
14. Skimming them often, and they will be very green
15. Put them up in glasses, and keep them for use
