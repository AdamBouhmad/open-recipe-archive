## Ingredients

- pigeons
- butter
- brocoli
- parsley

## Directions

1. Scald the pigeons, draw them, take the craw out, wash them in several waters, cut off the pinions, turn the legs under the wings, dredge them, and put them in soft cold water
2. Boil them slowly a quarter of an hour
3. Dish them up, pour over them good melted butter, lay round a little brocoli in bunches, and send butter and parsley in a boat
