## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of boiled or fried ham
- yolks of two eggs
- a tablespoonful of butter
- cream or rich milk
- pepper
- toast
- salt
- water
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Take a quarter of a pound of either boiled or fried ham, chop it fine, mix it with the yolks of two eggs, well beaten, a tablespoonful of butter, and enough cream or rich milk to make it soft, a dash of pepper.
2. Stir it over the fire until it thickens.
3. Dip the toast for an instant in hot salted water; spread over some melted butter, then turn over the ham mixture.
4. Serve hot.
