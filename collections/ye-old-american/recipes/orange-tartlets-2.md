## Ingredients

- juice of two large oranges
- grated peel of one orange
- three-fourths of a cup of sugar
- a tablespoonful of butter
- a good teaspoonful of cornstarch
- juice of half a lemon
- tart shells

## Directions

1. Take the juice of two large oranges and the grated peel of one
2. add three-fourths of a cup of sugar and a tablespoonful of butter
3. stir in a good teaspoonful of cornstarch into the juice of half a lemon and add to the mixture
4. Beat all well together and bake in tart shells without cover
