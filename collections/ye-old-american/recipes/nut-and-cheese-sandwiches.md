## Ingredients

- grated Gruyère cheese
- chopped English walnut meat
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts of grated Gruyère cheese and chopped English walnut meat.
2. Season with salt and cayenne.
3. Prepare as other sandwiches.
