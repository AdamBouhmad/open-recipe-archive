## Ingredients

- field peas
- lard
- fried bacon

## Directions

1. have them young and newly gathered
2. shell and boil them tender
3. pour them in a colander to drain
4. put some lard in a frying pan
5. when it boils, mash the peas
6. fry them in a cake of a light brown
7. put it in the dish with the crust uppermost
8. garnish with thin bits of fried bacon
