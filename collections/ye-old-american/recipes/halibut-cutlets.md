## Ingredients

- halibut
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- yolk of egg
- grated bread crumbs
- fresh lard or clarified beef dripping

## Directions

1. Cut your halibut into steaks or cutlets about an inch thick.
2. Wipe them with a dry cloth, and season them with salt and cayenne pepper.
3. Have ready a pan of yolk of egg well beaten, and a large flat dish of grated bread crumbs.
4. Put some fresh lard or clarified beef dripping into a frying pan, and hold it over a clear fire till it boils.
5. Dip your cutlets into the beaten egg, and then into the bread crumbs.
6. Fry them of a light brown.
7. Serve them up hot, with the gravy in the bottom of the dish.
