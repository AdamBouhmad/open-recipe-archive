## Ingredients

- ducks
- onion
- sage leaves
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- lard
- gizzards
- pinions
- mace
- pepper corns
- catsup
- lemon pickle

## Directions

1. Draw the ducks
2. Shred one onion and a few sage leaves
3. Put them into the ducks with pepper and salt
4. Spit and dust them with flour
5. Baste them with lard
6. Roast in twenty minutes if your fire is very hot
7. Just before you take them from the spit, dust them with flour and baste them
8. Get ready some gravy made of the gizzards and pinions, a large blade of mace, a few pepper corns, a spoonful of catsup, a tea-spoonful of lemon pickle
9. Strain it and pour it on the ducks
10. Send onion sauce in a boat
