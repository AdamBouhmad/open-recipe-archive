## Ingredients

- six pounds of washing soda
- three pounds of unslaked lime
- four gallons of boiling water
- six pounds of clean fat
- two gallons of cold water
- a handful of salt

## Directions

1. Pour on four gallons of boiling water, let it stand until perfectly clear
2. drain off, and put in six pounds of clean fat
3. Boil it until it begins to harden, about two hours, stirring most of the time
4. While boiling, thin it with two gallons of cold water, which you have previously poured on the alkaline mixture, after draining off the four gallons
5. This must be settled clear before it is drawn off
6. Try the thickness by cooling a little on a plate
7. Put in a handful of salt just before taking from the fire
8. Wet a tub to prevent sticking; turn in the soap and let it stand until solid
9. Cut into bars, put on a board and let it dry
