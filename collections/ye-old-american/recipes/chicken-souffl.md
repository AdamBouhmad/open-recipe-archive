## Ingredients

- 2 cups scalded milk
- ⅛ cup butter
- ⅛ cup flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- ½ cup stale soft bread crumbs
- 2 cups cold cooked chicken, finely chopped
- Yolks 3 eggs, well beaten
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- Whites 3 eggs, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of first five ingredients, add bread crumbs, and cook two minutes
2. Remove from fire, add chicken, yolks of eggs, and parsley
3. Fold in whites of eggs
4. Turn in a buttered pudding-dish, and bake thirty-five minutes in a slow oven
5. Serve with White Mushroom Sauce
