## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 1 cup sugar
- Yolks 6 eggs
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup sherry
- 2½ cups cream
- ¾ cup candied fruit
- ½ cup almonds, blanched and chopped
- ⅓ cup Sultana raisins
- ½ cup pounded macaroons

## Directions

1. Make custard of milk, one-half the sugar, egg yolks, and salt.
2. Caramelize the remaining sugar and add.
3. Strain, cool, add remaining ingredients, freeze, and mould.
