## Ingredients

- one pound of figs
- one teacupful of water
- half a cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Chop figs fine
2. Put into a stewpan on the stove
3. Pour over them a teacupful of water and add a half cup of sugar
4. Cook all together until soft and smooth
5. When cold spread between layers of cake
