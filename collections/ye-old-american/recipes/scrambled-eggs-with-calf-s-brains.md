## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- ½ cup milk
- calf's brains, parboiled and cut in dice
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Soak calf's brains one hour in cold water to cover.
2. Remove membrane, and parboil twenty minutes in boiling, salted, acidulated water.
3. Drain, put in cold water; as soon as cold, drain again, and separate in small pieces.
4. Beat eggs slightly, using a silver fork, add salt, pepper, milk, and calf's brains.
5. Put butter in hot chafing-dish; when melted, pour in the mixture.
6. Cook until of creamy consistency, constantly stirring and scraping from bottom of the pan.
