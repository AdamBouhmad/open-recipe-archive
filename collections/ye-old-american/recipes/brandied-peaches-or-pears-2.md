## Ingredients

- four pounds of fruit
- four pounds of sugar
- one pint of best white brandy
- water

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of the sugar and enough water to dissolve it.
2. Let this come to a boil and put the fruit in and boil five minutes.
3. Remove the fruit carefully and let the syrup boil fifteen minutes longer, or until it thickens well.
4. Add the brandy and take the kettle at once from the fire.
5. Pour the hot syrup over the fruit and seal.
6. If a reddish liquor oozes from the fruit, drain this off before adding the clear syrup.
7. Put up in glass jars.
8. Peaches and pears should be peeled for brandying.
9. Plums should be pricked and watched carefully for fear of bursting.
