## Ingredients

- peas
- water
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Shell the peas and wash in cold water.
2. Put in boiling water just enough to cover them well and keep them from burning; boil from twenty minutes to half an hour, when the liquor should be nearly boiled out.
3. Season with pepper and salt and a good allowance of butter; serve very hot.
