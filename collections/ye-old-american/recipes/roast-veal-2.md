## Ingredients

- leg, cushion, or loin of veal
- salt
- pepper
- stuffing
- strips of fat salt pork
- flour
- one-third cup butter
- one-half cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Wipe meat, sprinkle with salt and pepper, stuff, and sew in shape.
2. Place on rack in dripping-pan, dredge meat and bottom of pan with flour, and place around meat strips of fat salt pork.
3. Bake three or four hours in moderate oven, basting every fifteen minutes with one-third cup butter melted in one-half cup boiling water, until used, then baste with fat in pan.
4. Serve with brown gravy.
