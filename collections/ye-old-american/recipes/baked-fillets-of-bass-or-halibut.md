## Ingredients

- bass or halibut
- salt
- pepper
- buttered paper
- rice
- parsley
- Hollandaise Sauce II

## Directions

1. Cut bass or halibut into small fillets, sprinkle with salt and pepper, put into a shallow pan, cover with buttered paper, and bake twelve minutes in hot oven.
2. Arrange on a rice border, garnish with parsley, and serve with Hollandaise Sauce II.
