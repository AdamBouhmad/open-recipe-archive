## Ingredients

- Two cups of boiled hominy
- Three cups of sour milk
- Half a cup of melted butter
- Two teaspoonfuls of salt
- Two tablespoonfuls of sugar
- Three eggs well beaten
- One teaspoonful of soda dissolved in hot water
- Two cups of flour

## Directions

1. Beat the hominy smooth
2. Stir in sour milk, butter, salt and sugar
3. Add eggs well beaten
4. Add soda dissolved in hot water
5. Add flour
6. Bake quickly
