## Ingredients

- one or two round slices of pine-apple
- a thick layer of loaf-sugar
- a glass or more of the best brandy
- cold water
- pounded ice
- a bunch of fresh green mint

## Directions

1. Lay at the bottom of a large tumbler, one or two round slices of pine-apple nicely pared
2. Cover them with a thick layer of loaf-sugar, powdered or well-broken
3. Pour on it a glass or more of the best brandy
4. Add cold water till the tumbler is two-thirds full
5. Finish with a thick layer of pounded ice till it nearly reaches the top
6. Stick down to one side a bunch of fresh green mint, the sprigs full and handsome, and tall enough to rise above the edge of the tumbler
7. Place, in the other side, one of the small tubes or straws used for drawing in this liquid
