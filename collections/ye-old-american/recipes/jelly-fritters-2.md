## Ingredients

- three eggs
- a pint of milk
- a pint bowl of wheat flour
- a tablespoonful of lard or beef fat
- a saltspoonful of salt
- firm jelly or jam

## Directions

1. Make a batter of three eggs, a pint of milk and a pint bowl of wheat flour or more, beat it light
2. Put a tablespoonful of lard or beef fat in a frying or omelet pan, add a saltspoonful of salt, making it boiling hot
3. Put in the batter by the large spoonful, not too close
4. When one side is a delicate brown, turn the other
5. When done, take them on to a dish with a d'oyley over it
6. Put a dessertspoonful of firm jelly or jam on each and serve
