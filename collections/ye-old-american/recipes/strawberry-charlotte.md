## Ingredients

- One quart of milk
- Yolks of six eggs
- Three-quarters of a cupful of sugar
- Slices of sponge cake
- Sweet cream
- Ripe strawberries
- Whites of six eggs
- One tablespoonful of sugar per egg

## Directions

1. Make a boiled custard of one quart of milk, the yolks of six eggs and three-quarters of a cupful of sugar
2. Flavor to taste
3. Line a glass fruit-dish with slices of sponge cake dipped in sweet cream
4. Lay upon this ripe strawberries sweetened to taste
5. Add a layer of cake and strawberries as before
6. When the custard is cold pour over the whole
7. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
8. Add a tablespoonful of sugar to each egg and put over the top
9. Decorate the top with the largest berries saved out at the commencement
