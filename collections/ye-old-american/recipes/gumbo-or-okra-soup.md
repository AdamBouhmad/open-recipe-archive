## Ingredients

- a slice of bacon or fat ham
- a large onion
- two quarts fresh tomatoes
- one quart okra
- a little chopped parsley
- about three quarts of hot broth
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Fry out the fat of a slice of bacon or fat ham.
2. Drain it off.
3. In it fry the slices of a large onion brown.
4. Scald, peel and cut up two quarts fresh tomatoes (use canned tomatoes if out of season).
5. Cut thin one quart okra.
6. Put them together with a little chopped parsley in a stew-kettle with about three quarts of hot broth of any kind.
7. Cook slowly for three hours.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Serve hot.
