## Ingredients

- Coffee-sugar, four pounds
- three pints of water
- three nutmegs, grated
- the whites of ten eggs, well beaten
- gum arabic, one ounce
- twenty drops of oil of lemon, or extract equal to that amount
- eight ounces of bicarbonate of soda
- six ounces of tartaric acid

## Directions

1. Mix all and place over a gentle fire, and stir well about thirty minutes
2. Remove from the fire and strain, and divide into two parts
3. Into one-half put eight ounces of bicarbonate of soda, into the other half put six ounces of tartaric acid
4. Shake well, and when cold they are ready for use by pouring three or four spoonfuls from both parts into separate glasses, each one-third full of water
5. Stir each and pour together, and you have a nice glass of cream soda which you can drink at your leisure, as the gum and eggs hold the gas
