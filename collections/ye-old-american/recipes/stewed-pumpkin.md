## Ingredients

- pumpkin
- water
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut in two, extract the seeds, slice, and pare.
2. Cover with cold water for an hour; put over the fire in a pot of boiling water and stew gently, stirring often, until it breaks to pieces.
3. Drain and squeeze, rub through a cullender, then return to the saucepan with a tablespoonful of butter, pepper, and salt to taste.
4. Stir rapidly from the bottom until very hot, when dish, rounding into a mound, with "dabs" of pepper on the top.
