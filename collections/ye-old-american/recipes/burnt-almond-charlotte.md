## Ingredients

- one cupful of sweet almonds
- half a box of gelatine
- half a cupful of cold water
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar
- a cup and a half of milk
- the yolks of two eggs
- a cupful of sugar
- two quarts of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Blanch and chop fine one cupful of sweet almonds
2. Soak half a box of gelatine for two hours in half a cupful of cold water
3. Put three tablespoonfuls of sugar into a saucepan over the fire and stir until it becomes liquid and looks dark
4. Add the chopped almonds to it and stir two minutes more
5. Turn it out on a platter and set aside to get cool
6. When they become cool enough break them up in a mortar and put them in a cup and a half of milk and cook again for ten minutes
7. Beat together the yolks of two eggs with a cupful of sugar and add to the cooking mixture
8. Add also the gelatine and stir until smooth and well dissolved
9. Take from the fire and set in a basin of ice-water and beat it until it begins to thicken
10. Add to that two quarts of whipped cream and turn the whole carefully into molds
11. Set away on the ice to become stiff
