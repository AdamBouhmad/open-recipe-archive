## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- hot water

## Directions

1. Take a quarter of a pound of the best fresh butter, cut it up, and mix with it about one tablespoonful of flour.
2. When it is thoroughly mixed, put it into a covered saucepan lined with porcelain.
3. Add to it half a teacupful of hot water.
4. Cover the saucepan and set it in a large tin pan of boiling water.
5. Shake it round continually (always moving it the same way) till it is entirely melted and begins to simmer.
6. Let it rest till it boils up.
