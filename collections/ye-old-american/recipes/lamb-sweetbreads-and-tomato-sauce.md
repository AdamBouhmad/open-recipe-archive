## Ingredients

- lamb sweetbreads
- salted water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Wash lamb sweetbreads well in salted water
2. Parboil fifteen minutes
3. When cool, trim neatly and put them in a pan with just butter enough to prevent their burning
4. Toss them about until a delicate color
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Serve, surrounded with tomato sauce
