## Ingredients

- 4 common crackers, rolled
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- ⅓ cup lemon juice or vinegar
- 1 cup raisins, seeded and chopped
- ½ cup butter
- 2 eggs well beaten
- Spices

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given, adding spices to taste.
2. Bake between crusts. This quantity will make two pies.
