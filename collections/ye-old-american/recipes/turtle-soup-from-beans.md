## Ingredients

- black beans
- water
- thyme
- summer savory
- parsley
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- force meat balls
- lemon
- wine

## Directions

1. Soak over night one quart of black beans
2. Next day boil them in the proper quantity of water, say a gallon
3. Dip the beans out of the pot and strain them through a colander
4. Return the flour of the beans, thus pressed, into the pot in which they were boiled
5. Tie up in a thin cloth some thyme, a teaspoonful of summer savory and parsley, and let it boil in the mixture
6. Add a tablespoonful of cold butter, salt and pepper
7. Have ready four hard-boiled yolks of eggs quartered, and a few force meat balls
8. Add this to the soup with a sliced lemon, and half a glass of wine just before serving the soup
