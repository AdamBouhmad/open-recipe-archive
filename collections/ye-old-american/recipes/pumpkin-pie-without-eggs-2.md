## Ingredients

- One quart of properly stewed pumpkin pressed through a colander
- Good, rich milk, sufficient to moisten it enough to fill two good-sized earthen pie-plates
- One teaspoonful of salt
- Half a cupful of molasses or brown sugar
- One tablespoonful of ginger
- One teaspoonful of cinnamon or nutmeg

## Directions

1. Bake in a moderately slow oven three-quarters of an hour
