## Ingredients

- green tender corn
- egg
- wheat flour
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- lard

## Directions

1. Grate the corn, while green and tender, with a coarse grater, into a deep dish
2. For two ears of corn, allow one egg
3. Beat the whites and yolks separately and add them to the corn
4. Add one tablespoonful of wheat flour and one of butter, a teaspoonful of salt and pepper to taste
5. Drop spoonfuls of this batter into a frying pan with hot butter and lard mixed
6. Fry a light brown on both sides
