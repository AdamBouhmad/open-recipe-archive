## Ingredients

- Terrapin à la Baltimore
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- ½ cup cream
- Yolks 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon Sherry wine
- Toast or puff paste points

## Directions

1. Add to Terrapin à la Baltimore one tablespoon each butter and flour creamed together, one-half cup cream, yolks two eggs slightly beaten, and one teaspoon lemon juice
2. Add, just before serving, one tablespoon Sherry wine
3. Pour in a deep dish and garnish with toast or puff paste points
