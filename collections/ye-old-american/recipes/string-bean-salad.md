## Ingredients

- two cups cold string beans
- French Dressing
- one teaspoon finely cut chives
- radishes

## Directions

1. Marinate two cups cold string beans with French Dressing.
2. Add one teaspoon finely cut chives.
3. Pile in centre of salad dish and arrange around base thin slices of radishes overlapping one another.
4. Garnish top with radish cut to represent a tulip.
