## Ingredients

- French beans
- spring water
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut off the stalk end first, and then turn to the point and strip off the strings
2. If not quite fresh, have a bowl of spring water with a little salt dissolved in it, standing before you
3. As the beans are cleansed and trimmed, throw them in
4. When all are done, put them on the fire in boiling water with some salt in it
5. When they have boiled fifteen or twenty minutes, take one out and taste it
6. As soon as they are tender, take them up, and throw them into a colander to drain
7. To send up the beans whole, when they are young, is much the best method
