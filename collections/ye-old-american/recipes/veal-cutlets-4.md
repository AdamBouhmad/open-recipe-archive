## Ingredients

- Slices of veal from leg, cut one-half inch thick
- Salt and pepper
- Flour
- Egg
- Crumbs
- Salt pork fat or butter
- One and one-half cups Brown Sauce

## Directions

1. Wipe veal slices, remove bone and skin, then cut in pieces for serving.
2. Roll long, irregular-shaped pieces and fasten with small wooden skewers.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper; dip in flour, egg, and crumbs.
4. Fry slowly, until well browned, in salt pork fat or butter.
5. Remove cutlets to stewpan and pour over one and one-half cups Brown Sauce.
6. Place on back of range and cook slowly forty minutes, or until cutlets are tender.
7. Arrange on hot platter, strain sauce and pour around cutlets, and garnish with parsley.
