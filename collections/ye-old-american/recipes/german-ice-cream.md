## Ingredients

- one and one-fourth cups sugar
- one tablespoon flour
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- two eggs slightly beaten
- two cups scalded milk
- two squares melted chocolate
- three cups cream
- one tablespoon vanilla
- three cups zweiback dried and broken in small pieces

## Directions

1. Mix one and one-fourth cups sugar, one tablespoon flour, and one-fourth teaspoon salt
2. Add two eggs slightly beaten and two cups scalded milk
3. Cook over hot water until mixture thickens
4. Add two squares melted chocolate, and cool
5. Add three cups cream and one tablespoon vanilla
6. Strain and freeze
7. Just before serving add three cups zweiback dried and broken in small pieces
