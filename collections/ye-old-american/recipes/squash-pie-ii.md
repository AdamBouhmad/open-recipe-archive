## Ingredients

- 1 cup squash, steamed and strained
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs, slightly beaten
- 4 tablespoons brandy
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- ¾ teaspoon ginger
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon mace
- puff paste
- white of egg
- stale bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Line a deep pie plate with puff paste.
2. Brush over paste with white of egg slightly beaten, and sprinkle with stale bread crumbs.
3. Fill with mixture and bake in a moderate oven.
4. Serve warm.
