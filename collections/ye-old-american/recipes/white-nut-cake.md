## Ingredients

- ¾ cup butter
- 1½ cups sugar
- Whites 8 eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 2½ cups flour
- ½ teaspoon cream of tartar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup walnut meat cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for mixing butter cakes. This mixture makes two loaves.
