## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 4 tablespoons oil
- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- ½ tablespoon sugar
- ½ teaspoon mustard
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- White 1 egg

## Directions

1. Force yolks of hard-boiled eggs through a strainer, then work, using a silver or wooden spoon, until smooth.
2. Add sugar, mustard, salt, and cayenne, and when well blended add gradually oil and vinegar, stirring and beating until thoroughly mixed.
3. Then cut and fold in white of egg beaten until stiff.
