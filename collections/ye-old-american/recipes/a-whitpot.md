## Ingredients

- half a loaf of bread
- 2 quarts milk
- 6 eggs
- rose-water
- nutmeg
- half pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut half a loaf of bread in dices
2. pour thereon 2 quarts milk
3. 6 eggs
4. rose-water
5. nutmeg and half pound of sugar
6. put into a dish and cover with paste, No. 1
7. bake slow 1 hour
