## Ingredients

- large ripe black currants
- a tumbler of water to each quart of currants
- about a pound of loaf-sugar to each pint of juice

## Directions

1. Strip the currants from the stalks, and mash them with the back of a ladle
2. Put them into a preserving kettle with a tumbler of water to each quart of currants
3. Cover it closely, set it over a moderate fire
4. When the currants have come to a boil, take them out, and squeeze them through a jelly-bag
5. To each pint of juice allow about a pound of loaf-sugar
6. Having washed the preserving kettle perfectly clean, put in the sugar with the juice
7. Stir them together till well mixed and dissolved
8. Boil it not longer than ten minutes
