## Ingredients

- firm fish
- salt
- pepper
- salt pork
- onion
- crackers
- milk
- white wine
- water
- flour
- butter
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Take any kind of firm fish
2. Cut it in pieces six inches long
3. Sprinkle salt and pepper over each piece
4. Cover the bottom of a small Dutch oven with slices of salt pork about half boiled
5. Lay in the fish, strewing a little chopped onion between
6. Cover with crackers that have been soaked soft in milk
7. Pour over it two gills of white wine and two of water
8. Put on the top of the oven and stew it gently about an hour
9. Take it out carefully and lay it in a deep dish
10. Thicken the gravy with a little flour and a spoonful of butter
11. Add some chopped parsley
12. Boil it a few minutes and pour it over the fish
13. Serve it up hot
