## Ingredients

- butter
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- cream or rich milk
- flour
- cold boiled potatoes

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan three tablespoonfuls of butter, a small handful of parsley chopped small, salt and pepper to taste.
2. Stir up well until hot, add a small teacupful of cream or rich milk, and thicken with two teaspoonfuls of flour.
3. Stir until it boils.
4. Chop some cold boiled potatoes, put into the mixture, and boil up once before serving.
