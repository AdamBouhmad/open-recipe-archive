## Ingredients

- cold chicken
- suet
- parsley
- sweet marjoram
- grated lemon-peel
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- yolk of egg
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a fine mixture of the same ingredients as croquets, well mixed, and beaten smooth in a mortar.
2. Make a fine paste, roll it out, and cut it into round cakes.
3. Then lay some of the mixture on one half of the cake, and fold over the other upon it, in the shape of a half-moon.
4. Close and crimp the edges nicely, and fry the rissoles in butter.
5. They should be of a light brown on both sides.
6. Drain them and send them to table dry.
