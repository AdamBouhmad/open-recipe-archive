## Ingredients

- two chickens
- eighteen tiny young onions
- butter
- flour
- heavy cream
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Dress, clean, and cut in pieces for serving, two chickens.
2. Cook in a small quantity of water with eighteen tiny young onions.
3. Remove chicken to serving dish as soon as tender.
4. When onions are soft drain from stock and reduce stock to one and one-half cups.
5. Make sauce of three tablespoons butter, four tablespoons flour, stock, and one-half cup heavy cream.
6. Add yolks of three eggs, salt, pepper, and lemon juice to taste.
7. Pour sauce over chicken and onions.
