## Ingredients

- grated chocolate
- one egg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put some grated chocolate into a basin and place on the back of the stove and let it melt (do not add any water to it)
2. Beat one egg and some sugar in it
3. When melted, spread this on the top of a custard pie
