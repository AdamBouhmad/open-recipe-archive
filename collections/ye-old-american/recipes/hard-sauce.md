## Ingredients

- 1 cup of butter
- 3 cups of powdered sugar
- 3/4 teacup wine
- Juice of a lemon
- 2 teaspoonfuls nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stir to a cream the butter and powdered sugar.
2. When light, beat in the wine, lemon juice, and nutmeg.
3. Beat long and hard until several shades lighter in color than at first, and creamy in consistency.
4. Smooth into shape with a broad knife dipped in cold water, and stamp with a wooden mould, first scalded and then dipped in cold water.
5. Set upon the ice until the pudding is served.
