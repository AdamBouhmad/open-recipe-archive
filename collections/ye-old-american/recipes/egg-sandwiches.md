## Ingredients

- very fresh eggs
- bread
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Hard boil some very fresh eggs
2. When cold cut them into moderately thin slices
3. Lay them between some bread and butter cut as thin as possible
4. Season them with pepper, salt and nutmeg
