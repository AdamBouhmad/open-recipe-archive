## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- bread-crumbs
- melted butter
- cream or milk
- pepper
- salt
- egg

## Directions

1. Boil until tender and clip into neat clusters
2. Pack—the stems downward—in a buttered pudding-dish
3. Beat up a cupful of bread-crumbs to a soft paste with two tablespoonfuls of melted butter and six of cream or milk
4. Season with pepper and salt and bind with a beaten egg
5. Cover the cauliflower with this mixture
6. Cover the dish closely and bake six minutes in a quick oven
7. Brown in five more and serve very hot in the dish in which they were baked
