## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 slice carrot
- 1 slice onion
- 1 tablespoon lean raw ham, finely chopped
- 5 tablespoons flour
- 1 1/4 cups brown stock
- 1/2 lb. mushrooms
- 1 cup cold water
- 1 teaspoon beef extract
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook butter with vegetables and ham until brown
2. Add flour and when well browned add stock gradually, then strain
3. Clean mushroom stems, break in pieces, cover with water, and cook slowly until stock is reduced to one-third cup
4. Strain and add to sauce with beef extract and seasonings
5. Just before serving add mushroom caps peeled, cut in slices lengthwise, and sautéd in butter five minutes
