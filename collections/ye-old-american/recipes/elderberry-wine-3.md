## Ingredients

- 8 quarts of berries
- 4 quarts of boiling water poured over the berries
- 3 lbs. of sugar to 4 quarts of juice
- 1 oz. powdered cinnamon
- ½ oz. powdered cloves

## Directions

1. Let it stand twelve hours, stirring now and then
2. Strain well, pressing out all the juice
3. Add sugar and spices
4. Boil five minutes, and set away to ferment in a stone jar, with a cloth thrown lightly over it
5. When it has done fermenting, rack it off carefully, not to disturb the lees
6. Bottle and cork down well
