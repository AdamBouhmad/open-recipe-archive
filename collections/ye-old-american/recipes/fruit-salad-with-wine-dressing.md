## Ingredients

- sliced pineapples
- sliced bananas
- sliced oranges
- grapes
- Wine Dressing

## Directions

1. Arrange alternate layers of sliced fruit, using pineapples, bananas, oranges, and grapes.
2. Pour over all Wine Dressing, and let stand one hour in a cold place.
