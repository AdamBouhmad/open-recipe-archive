## Ingredients

- Green peas
- loaf sugar
- mint
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Shell peas and lay in cold water
2. Boil with two or three lumps of loaf-sugar and a sprig of mint
3. Remove mint before dishing
4. Drain peas and mix with butter and pepper
