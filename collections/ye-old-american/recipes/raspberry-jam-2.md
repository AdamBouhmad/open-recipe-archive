## Ingredients

- fine ripe raspberries
- fine loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Weigh the raspberries
2. To each pound of fruit allow three quarters of a pound of fine loaf-sugar
3. Mash the raspberries and break up the sugar
4. Mix them together and put into a preserving kettle over a good fire
5. Stir frequently and skim them
6. The jam will be done in half an hour
7. Put it warm into glasses
8. Lay on the top a white paper cut exactly to fit the inside and dipped in brandy
9. Tie on another cover of very thick white paper
