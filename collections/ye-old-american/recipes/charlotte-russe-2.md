## Ingredients

- Stale sponge cake
- One and a half pints of cream, or one pint of cream and a half pint of milk
- Two-thirds of a cup of powdered sugar
- One teaspoonful of vanilla
- Half of a box of gelatine
- Cold water
- Boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut stale sponge cake into slices about half an inch thick
2. Line three molds with them, leaving a space of half an inch between each slice
3. Set the molds where they will not be disturbed until the filling is ready
4. Take a deep tin pan and fill about one-third full of either snow or pounded ice
5. Into this set another pan that will hold at least four quarts
6. Into a deep bowl or pail put one and a half pints of cream if the cream is very thick take one pint of cream and a half pint of milk
7. Whip it to a froth
8. When the bowl is full, skim the froth into the pan which is standing on the ice
9. Repeat until the cream is all froth
10. With a spoon draw the froth to one side and turn the cream that has gone back to milk into the bowl again
11. Whip as before until the cream is all whipped
12. Stir into it two-thirds of a cup of powdered sugar, one teaspoonful of vanilla and half of a box of gelatine which has been soaked in cold water for one hour
13. Put the gelatine in boiling water enough to dissolve it
