## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1½ cups sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 2¼ cups flour
- ¼ teaspoon soda
- ¾ teaspoon cream of tartar
- Whites 5 eggs
- 2 squares Baker's chocolate, grated

## Directions

1. Cream the butter; add sugar gradually, milk, and flour mixed and sifted with soda and cream of tartar.
2. Beat whites of eggs, and add to first mixture; then add chocolate, and beat thoroughly.
3. Bake forty-five minutes in a moderate oven.
