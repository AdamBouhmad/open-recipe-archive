## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains pepper
- 1 cup stock obtained by cooking in water bones and skin of a roast turkey
- 1½ cups cold roast turkey, cut in small pieces
- Seasoned cracker crumbs
- Buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Make one cup of sauce using butter, flour, salt, pepper, and stock
2. Cut remnants of cold roast turkey in small pieces
3. Sprinkle bottom of buttered baking-dish with seasoned cracker crumbs
4. Add turkey meat and pour over sauce
5. Sprinkle with buttered cracker crumbs
6. Bake in a hot oven until crumbs are brown
