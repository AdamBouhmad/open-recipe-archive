## Ingredients

- One pint of grated bread crumbs
- Half a cupful of chopped suet
- Half a cupful of moist sugar
- A little salt
- A small tablespoonful of flour
- Grated rind of a lemon
- Whites and yolks of two eggs
- Juice of the lemon
- Butter
- Cloth
- Sifted sugar for serving
- Wine sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Mix together a pint of grated bread crumbs, half a cupful of chopped suet, half a cupful of moist sugar, a little salt and a small tablespoonful of flour, adding the grated rind of a lemon.
2. Beat the whites and yolks of two eggs well.
3. Strain the juice of the lemon.
4. Moisten the mixture with the beaten eggs and the juice of the lemon.
5. Stir it all well together.
6. Put the mixture into small cups well buttered.
7. Tie them down with a cloth dipped in flour.
8. Boil three-quarters of an hour.
9. Turn them out on a dish, strew sifted sugar over them and serve with wine sauce.
