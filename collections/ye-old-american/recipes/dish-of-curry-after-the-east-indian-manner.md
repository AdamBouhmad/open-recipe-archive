## Ingredients

- chickens
- water
- salt
- butter
- garlic
- onion
- curry powder
- lemon or orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut two chickens as for fricassee, wash them clean, and put them in a stew pan with as much water as will cover them
2. Sprinkle them with a large spoonful of salt, and let them boil till tender, covered close all the time, and skim them well
3. When boiled enough, take up the chickens, and put the liquor of them into a pan
4. Put half a pound of fresh butter in the pan, and brown it a little
5. Put into it two cloves of garlic, and a large onion sliced, and let these all fry till brown, often shaking the pan
6. Put in the chickens, and sprinkle over them two or three spoonsful of curry powder
7. Cover the pan close, and let the chickens do till brown, often shaking the pan
8. Put in the liquor the chickens were boiled in, and let all stew till tender
9. If acid is agreeable squeeze the juice of a lemon or orange in it
