## Ingredients

- fore-quarter of shote
- melted butter
- caper vinegar
- mint sauce

## Directions

1. Joint it for the convenience of carving
2. Roast it before a brisk fire
3. When done, take the skin off, dredge and froth it
4. Put a little melted butter with some caper vinegar over it, or serve it with mint sauce
