## Ingredients

- A large tablespoonful of flax-seed
- One and one-half tumblers of cold water
- A quarter of a pound of pulverized sugar
- An ounce of pulverized gum arabic
- Juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Boil a large tablespoonful of flax-seed with a tumbler and a half of cold water together till the liquid becomes very sticky
2. Strain it hot over a quarter of a pound of pulverized sugar and an ounce of pulverized gum arabic
3. Stir it till quite dissolved
4. Squeeze into it the juice of a lemon
5. Take a wine-glass of it as often as the cough is troublesome
