## Ingredients

- 1 cup scalded milk
- 1 cup soft stale bread crumbs
- ¼ lb. mild cheese, cut in small pieces
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Yolks 3 eggs
- Whites 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix first five ingredients, add yolks of eggs beaten until lemon-colored.
2. Cut and fold in whites of eggs beaten until stiff.
3. Pour in a buttered baking-dish, and bake twenty minutes in a moderate oven.
