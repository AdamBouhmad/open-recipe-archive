## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ¾ cup water
- Whites 3 eggs
- 1 pint heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water until syrup will thread when dropped from tip of spoon
2. Pour slowly on the beaten whites of eggs
3. Continue the beating until mixture is cool
4. Add cream beaten until stiff, and vanilla
5. Freeze
