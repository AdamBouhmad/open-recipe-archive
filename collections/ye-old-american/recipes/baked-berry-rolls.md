## Ingredients

- rich biscuit dough
- berries
- butter
- spices
- sugar
- hot water

## Directions

1. Roll rich biscuit dough thin
2. Cut it into little squares four inches wide and seven inches long
3. Spread over with berries
4. Roll up the crust
5. Put the rolls in a dripping-pan just a little apart
6. Put a piece of butter on each roll and spices if you like
7. Strew over a large handful of sugar and a little hot water
8. Set in the oven and bake like dumplings
9. Serve with sweet sauce
