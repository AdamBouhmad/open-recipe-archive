## Ingredients

- shad roe
- boiling salted water
- one-half tablespoon vinegar
- cold water
- buttered pan
- three-fourths cup Tomato Sauce I or II

## Directions

1. Cook shad roe fifteen minutes in boiling salted water to cover, with one-half tablespoon vinegar.
2. Drain, cover with cold water, and let stand five minutes.
3. Remove from cold water, and place on buttered pan with three-fourths cup Tomato Sauce I or II.
4. Bake twenty minutes in hot oven, basting every five minutes.
5. Remove to a platter, and pour around three-fourths cup Tomato Sauce.
