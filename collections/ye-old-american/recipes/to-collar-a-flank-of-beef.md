## Ingredients

- a nice flank of beef
- a large portion of saltpetre
- common salt
- parsley
- sage
- thyme
- pepper
- pounded cloves

## Directions

1. Rub it well with a large portion of saltpetre and common salt
2. Let it remain ten days
3. Then wash it clean
4. Take off the outer and inner skin with the gristle
5. Spread it on a board
6. Cover the inside with the following mixture: parsley, sage, thyme chopped fine, pepper, salt and pounded cloves
7. Roll it up
8. Sew a cloth over it
9. Bandage that with tape
10. Boil it gently five or six hours
11. When cold, lay it on a board without undoing it
12. Put another board on the top, with a heavy weight on it
13. Let it remain twenty-four hours
14. Take off the bandages
15. Cut a thin slice from each end
16. Serve it up garnished with green pickle and sprigs of parsley
