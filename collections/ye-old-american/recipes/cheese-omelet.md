## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one tablespoonful of milk
- one tablespoonful of grated cheese

## Directions

1. Beat up three eggs
2. Add to them a tablespoonful of milk and a tablespoonful of grated cheese
3. Add a little more cheese before folding
4. Turn it out on a hot dish
5. Grate a little cheese over it before serving
