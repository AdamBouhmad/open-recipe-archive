## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- four tablespoonfuls of sugar
- nutmeg or vanilla
- beaten whites of four eggs
- pinch of salt
- quart of sweet milk
- paste for lining

## Directions

1. Beat together until very light the yolks of four eggs and four tablespoonfuls of sugar
2. Flavor with nutmeg or vanilla
3. Add the four beaten whites
4. Add a pinch of salt
5. Lastly, add a quart of sweet milk
6. Mix well and pour into tins lined with paste
7. Bake until firm
