## Ingredients

- peaches
- peach kernels
- water
- lemon juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare the peaches and take out the stones
2. Slice them and add to them about a quarter of the kernels
3. Place them in a kettle with enough water to cover them
4. Stir them often until the fruit is well cooked
5. Strain and to every pint of the juice add the juice of a lemon
6. Measure again, allowing a pound of sugar to each pint of juice
7. Heat the sugar very hot and add when the juice has boiled twenty minutes
8. Let it come to a boil and take instantly from the fire
