## Ingredients

- one quart can of tomatoes
- one slice of onion
- two cloves
- pepper and salt
- one ounce of butter
- one tablespoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Put one quart can of tomatoes over the fire in a stewpan
2. Add one slice of onion and two cloves, a little pepper and salt
3. Boil about twenty minutes
4. Remove from the fire and strain it through a sieve
5. Melt one ounce of butter in another pan, and as it melts, sprinkle in one tablespoonful of flour
6. Stir it until it browns and froths a little
7. Mix the tomato pulp with it, and it is ready for the table
