## Ingredients

- shad
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- parsley
- beaten yolk of egg
- water
- lemon
- water cress

## Directions

1. Stuff the shad with bread crumbs, salt, pepper, butter and parsley, and mix this up with the beaten yolk of egg
2. Fill the fish with it, and sew it up or fasten a string around it
3. Pour over it a little water and some butter, and bake as you would a fowl
4. Bake from an hour to an hour and a quarter
5. Garnish with slices of lemon, water cress, etc.
