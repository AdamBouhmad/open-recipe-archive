## Ingredients

- large chicken
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- flour, two tablespoonfuls
- butter the size of an egg
- nice light bread-dough

## Directions

1. Cut and joint a large chicken, cover with cold water, and let it boil gently until tender
2. Season with salt and pepper, and thicken the gravy with two tablespoonfuls of flour, mixed smooth with a piece of butter the size of an egg
3. Have ready nice light bread-dough, cut with the top of a wine-glass about a half an inch thick
4. Let them stand half an hour and rise
5. Drop these into the boiling gravy
6. Put the cover on the pot closely, wrap a cloth around it, in order that no steam shall escape
7. Boil three-quarters of an hour
