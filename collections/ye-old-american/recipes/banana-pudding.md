## Ingredients

- sponge cake
- bananas
- soft custard
- wine
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut sponge cake in slices
2. In a glass dish, put alternately a layer of cake and a layer of bananas sliced
3. Make a soft custard and flavor with a little wine
4. Pour over it
5. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth and heap over the whole
