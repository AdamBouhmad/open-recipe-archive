## Ingredients

- Whites 8 eggs
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1 cup sugar
- ¾ cup flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¾ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat whites of eggs until frothy; add cream of tartar, and continue beating until eggs are stiff
2. Add sugar gradually
3. Fold in flour mixed with salt and sifted four times, and add vanilla
4. Bake forty-five to fifty minutes in an unbuttered angel-cake pan. After cake has risen and begins to brown, cover with a buttered paper
