## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- ½ tablespoon lemon juice
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains pepper
- ¼ teaspoon finely chopped parsley
- Bread
- ¼ cup heavy cream
- Sherry wine
- Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add lemon juice drop by drop, salt, pepper, and parsley.
2. Cut bread in circular pieces three-eighths inch thick, then toast.
3. Put one-half of the sauce on the under side of toast; put toast on a small baking-dish, pile mushroom caps cleaned and peeled in conical shape on toast, and pour over cream.
4. Cover with glass and bake about twenty-five minutes, adding more cream if necessary.
5. Just before serving add one teaspoon Sherry wine.
