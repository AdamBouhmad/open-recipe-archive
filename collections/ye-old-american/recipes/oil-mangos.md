## Ingredients

- melons a size larger than a goose egg
- salt and water
- vinegar
- a pound of white race ginger
- one pound of horse-radish
- one pound of mustard seed
- one pound of chopped onion
- one ounce of mace
- one ounce of nutmeg
- two ounces of turmeric
- a handful of whole black pepper
- a quarter of a pound of mustard
- a large cup full of sweet oil
- a clove of garlic

## Directions

1. Gather the melons a size larger than a goose egg and put them in a pot
2. Pour boiling salt and water made strong upon them, and cover them up
3. Next day, cut a slit from the stem to the blossom end, and take out the seeds carefully
4. Return them to the brine, and let them remain in it eight days
5. Then put them in strong vinegar for a fortnight
6. Wipe the insides with a soft cloth
7. Stuff them and tie them
8. Pack them in a pot with the slit uppermost
9. Strew some of the stuffing over each layer, and keep them covered with the best vinegar
10. For the stuffing: wash a pound of white race ginger very clean and pour boiling water on it, letting it stand twenty-four hours
11. Slice it thin and dry it
12. Combine with one pound of horse-radish scraped and dried, one pound of mustard seed washed and dried, one pound of chopped onion, one ounce of mace, one ounce of nutmeg pounded fine, two ounces of turmeric, and a handful of whole black pepper
13. Make these ingredients into a paste with a quarter of a pound of mustard and a large cup full of sweet oil
14. Put a clove of garlic into each mango
