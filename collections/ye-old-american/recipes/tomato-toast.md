## Ingredients

- a quart of ripe tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- a tablespoonful of butter
- one cup sweet cream
- flour
- dipped toast
- butter

## Directions

1. Pare and stew a quart of ripe tomatoes until smooth.
2. Season with salt, pepper and a tablespoonful of butter.
3. When done, add one cup sweet cream and a little flour.
4. Let it scald, but not boil; remove at once.
5. Pour over slices of dipped toast, well buttered.
