## Ingredients

- three cupfuls of flour
- one cupful of molasses
- half a cupful of milk
- a teaspoonful of salt
- a little cloves and cinnamon
- a teaspoonful of soda
- a quart of huckleberries

## Directions

1. Combine flour, molasses, milk, salt, cloves and cinnamon with a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little of the milk
2. Stir in a quart of huckleberries, floured
3. Boil in a well-buttered mold two hours
4. Serve with brandy sauce
