## Ingredients

- cabbage
- boiling water
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Pick off the outer green leaves, quarter, examine carefully to be sure there are no insects in it
2. Lay for an hour in cold water
3. Put into a pot with plenty of boiling water, and cook fifteen minutes
4. Throw away the water, and fill up the pot from the boiling tea-kettle
5. Cook until tender all through
6. Drain well, chop, and stir in a tablespoonful of butter, pepper, and salt
7. Serve very hot
