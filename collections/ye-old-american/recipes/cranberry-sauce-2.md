## Ingredients

- a quart of ripe cranberries
- a teacupful of water
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and pick a quart of ripe cranberries, and put into a saucepan with a teacupful of water. Stew slowly, stirring often until they are thick as marmalade. They require at least an hour and a half to cook. When you take them from the fire, sweeten abundantly with white sugar. Put them into a mould and set aside to get cold.
