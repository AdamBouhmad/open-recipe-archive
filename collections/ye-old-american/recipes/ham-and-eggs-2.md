## Ingredients

- eggs
- salted lard
- ham
- parsley

## Directions

1. Fry the eggs in a little very nice salted lard; drain off every drop of grease, and lay them upon a hot dish, with neat slices of fried ham around the edges, half the size of the slice as first carved from the ham.
2. Trim off the rough edges of the eggs, and cut the ham evenly in oblong pieces before dishing.
3. Garnish with parsley.
