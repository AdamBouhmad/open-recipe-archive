## Ingredients

- radish pods, young and tender
- salt and water
- vinegar
- alum
- mace, whole
- pepper, whole
- turmeric
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Gather sprigs or bunches of radish pods while they are young and tender, but let the pods remain on the sprigs
2. Put them into strong salt and water, and let them stand two days
3. Then drain and wipe them and put them into a clean stone jar
4. Boil an equal quantity of vinegar and water
5. Pour it over the radish pods while hot, and cover them closely to keep in the steam
6. Repeat this frequently through the day till they are very green
7. Then pour off the vinegar and water, and boil for five minutes some very good vinegar, with a little bit of alum
8. Pour it over them
9. Put them into a stone jar, having added some whole mace, whole pepper, a little turmeric and a little sweet oil
10. Cork it closely, and tie over it a leather or oil-cloth
