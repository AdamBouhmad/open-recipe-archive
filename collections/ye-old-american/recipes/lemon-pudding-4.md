## Ingredients

- lemons
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of powdered white sugar
- a wine-glass of mixed wine and brandy
- six eggs
- puff-paste
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Make precisely in the same manner as the Orange Pudding, substituting lemons for oranges.
