## Ingredients

- large full grown green gooseberries
- cold water
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Select large and full grown but quite green gooseberries
2. Top and tail them
3. Put them into wide-mouthed bottles as far up as the beginning of the neck
4. Cover the bottom of a large boiler or kettle with saw-dust or straw
5. Stand the bottles of gooseberries slightly corked upright in the boiler
6. Pour cold water round them as far up as the fruit
7. Put a brisk fire under the boiler
8. When the water boils up, instantly take out the bottles
9. Fill them up to the mouth with boiling water from a tea-kettle
10. Cork them again slightly
11. When quite cold put in the corks very tight and seal them
12. Lay the bottles on their sides in a box of dry sand
13. Turn them every day for four or five weeks
