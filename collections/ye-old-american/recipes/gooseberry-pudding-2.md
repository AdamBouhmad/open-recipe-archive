## Ingredients

- gooseberries
- rich milk
- nutmeg
- eggs

## Directions

1. Stir cold gooseberries into a quart of rich boiling milk
2. Grate in a nutmeg
3. Cover the pan and let the gooseberries simmer in the milk for five minutes
4. Stir in the beaten yolks of two or three eggs
5. Immediately remove from the fire
6. Keep the cover on a few minutes longer
7. Turn out the mixture into a deep dish or glass bowl
8. Set away to get cold before serving
9. Eat with sponge-cake
10. Add additional sugar if required
