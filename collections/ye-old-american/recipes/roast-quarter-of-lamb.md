## Ingredients

- hind-quarter of lamb
- salt
- flour
- water
- lettuce
- green peas
- mint sauce

## Directions

1. Procure a nice hind-quarter, remove some of the fat that is around the kidney
2. Skewer the lower joint up to the fillet
3. Place it in a moderate oven and let it heat through slowly
4. Dredge it with salt and flour
5. Quicken the fire
6. Put half a pint of water into the dripping-pan, with a teaspoonful of salt
7. With this liquor baste the meat occasionally
8. Serve with lettuce, green peas and mint sauce
