## Ingredients

- short stemmed bright red cherries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Get the short stemmed bright red cherries in bunches
2. Make a syrup, with equal quantities of sugar and cherries
3. Scald the cherries, but do not let the skins crack, which they will do if the fruit be too ripe
