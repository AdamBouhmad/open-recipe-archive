## Ingredients

- Crab meat
- Mustard
- Cayenne
- Salt
- Pungent sauce
- Hot water
- Vinegar
- Pulverized cracker
- Rich cream
- Cream crackers
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare according to the receipt for devilled lobster
2. Substitute for the coral in the vinegar some pulverized cracker moistened first with a tablespoonful of rich cream
3. Serve up in the back-shell of the crab if you like
4. Send in with cream crackers and stick a sprig of parsley in the top of each heap, arranging the shells upon a large flat dish
