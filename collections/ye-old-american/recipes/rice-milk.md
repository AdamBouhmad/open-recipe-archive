## Ingredients

- half a pint of rice
- a quart of water
- a quart of rich milk
- half a pound of whole raisins
- two beaten eggs
- four large table-spoonfuls of brown sugar

## Directions

1. Pick and wash half a pint of rice, and boil it in a quart of water till it is quite soft.
2. Then drain it, and mix it with a quart of rich milk.
3. You may add half a pound of whole raisins.
4. Set it over hot coals, and stir it frequently till it boils.
5. When it boils hard, stir in alternately two beaten eggs, and four large table-spoonfuls of brown sugar.
6. Let it continue boiling five minutes longer; then take it off, and send it to table hot.
7. If you put in raisins you must let it boil till they are quite soft.
