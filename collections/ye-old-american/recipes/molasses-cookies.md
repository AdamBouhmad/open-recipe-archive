## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups molasses
- 1 teaspoonful cloves
- 1 tablespoonful ginger
- Sufficient flour to make soft dough

## Directions

1. Mould with the hands into small cakes, and bake in a steady rather than quick oven, as they are apt to burn.
