## Ingredients

- One quart of flour, sifted twice
- Three eggs with whites and yolks beaten separately
- Three teacups of sweet milk
- One teaspoonful of salt
- One tablespoonful of sugar
- One large tablespoonful of lard or butter
- Two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, sugar, salt and baking powder
2. Rub in the lard cold
3. Add the beaten eggs and milk
4. Mix quickly into a smooth batter, a little firmer than for griddle-cakes
5. Grease well some muffin-pans and fill them two-thirds full
6. Bake in a hot oven fifteen or twenty minutes
