## Ingredients

- four calf's feet
- four quarts of water
- one pound of loaf sugar
- rind and juice of two lemons
- two eggs
- one gill of Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Wash and prepare four calf's feet, place them in four quarts of water
2. Let them simmer gently five hours
3. Take them out and pour the liquid into a vessel to cool
4. When cold, remove every particle of fat
5. Replace the jelly into the preserving-kettle, and add one pound of loaf sugar, the rind and juice of two lemons
6. When the sugar has dissolved, beat two eggs with their shells in one gill of water, which pour into the kettle and boil five minutes, or until perfectly clear
7. Add one gill of Madeira wine and strain through a flannel bag into any form you like
