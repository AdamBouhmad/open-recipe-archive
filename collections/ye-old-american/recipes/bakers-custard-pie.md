## Ingredients

- the yolks of three eggs
- a tablespoonful of sifted flour
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- a teaspoonful of vanilla
- a little grated nutmeg
- the well-beaten whites of the eggs
- a pint of scalded milk
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Beat up the yolks of three eggs to a cream
2. Stir thoroughly a tablespoonful of sifted flour into three tablespoonfuls of sugar; this separates the particles of flour so that there will be no lumps
3. Add it to the beaten yolks, put in a pinch of salt, a teaspoonful of vanilla and a little grated nutmeg
4. Add the well-beaten whites of the eggs
5. Add a pint of scalded milk (not boiled) which has been cooled
6. Mix this in by degrees and turn all into a deep pie-pan lined with puff paste
7. Bake from twenty-five to thirty minutes
