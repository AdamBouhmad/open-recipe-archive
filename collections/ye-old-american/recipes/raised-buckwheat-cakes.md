## Ingredients

- one quart of warm water or half water and milk
- one heaping teaspoonful of salt
- buckwheat flour
- half a cup of yeast
- one small level teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Put into a crock or large earthen pitcher one quart of warm water or half water and milk, and one heaping teaspoonful of salt
2. Stir in as much buckwheat flour as will thicken it to rather a stiff batter
3. Add half a cup of yeast
4. Make it smooth, cover it up warm to rise over night
5. In the morning add a small, level teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little warm water
