## Ingredients

- Puff paste
- Fruit
- Sugar
- White of an egg

## Directions

1. Make a nice puff paste; roll it out the usual thickness, as for pies
2. Cut it out into circular pieces about the size of a small tea saucer
3. Pile the fruit on half of the paste, sprinkle over some sugar
4. Wet the edges and turn the paste over
5. Press the edges together, ornament them and brush the turnovers over with the white of an egg
6. Sprinkle over sifted sugar and bake on tins, in a brisk oven, for about twenty minutes
