## Ingredients

- three eggs
- a quart of cream
- a quart of sifted flour
- one wine glass of strong yeast
- a salt-spoon of salt
- a large table-spoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs very light, stir them into a quart of cream alternately with a quart of sifted flour
2. Add one wine glass of strong yeast, and a salt-spoon of salt
3. Cover the batter, and set it near the fire to rise
4. When it is quite light, stir in a large table-spoonful of butter that has been warmed by the fire
5. Bake the cakes in muffin rings, and send them to table hot, split with your fingers, and buttered
