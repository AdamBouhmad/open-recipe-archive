## Ingredients

- One quart of stewed pumpkin pressed through a sieve
- Nine eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately
- Two scant quarts of milk
- One teaspoonful of mace
- One teaspoonful of cinnamon
- One teaspoonful of nutmeg
- One and one-half cupfuls of white sugar, or very light brown

## Directions

1. Beat all well together and bake in crust without cover
