## Ingredients

- salmon slices
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- white paper
- anchovy or caper sauce
- chopped herbs
- spice

## Directions

1. Cut the slices one inch thick
2. Season them with pepper and salt
3. Butter a sheet of white paper
4. Lay each slice on a separate piece
5. Envelop them in it with their ends twisted
6. Broil gently over a clear fire
7. Serve with anchovy or caper sauce
8. When higher seasoning is required, add a few chopped herbs and a little spice
