## Ingredients

- 1½ cups flour
- 1 yeast cake dissolved in ½ cup lukewarm water
- ⅔ cup butter
- 4 eggs
- ½ cup sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Apricot Marmalade
- Wine Sauce

## Directions

1. Make sponge of one-half cup flour and dissolved yeast cake; cover and let rise.
2. Mix remaining flour with butter, two eggs, sugar, and salt.
3. Beat thoroughly, and add, while beating, remaining eggs, one at a time, then beat until mixture is perfectly smooth.
4. As soon as sponge has doubled its bulk, combine mixtures, beat thoroughly, and half fill buttered individual tins.
5. Let rise, and bake in a moderate oven.
6. Remove from tins, cut a circular piece from top of each, and scoop out a small quantity of the inside.
7. Fill centres thus made with Apricot Marmalade, replace circular pieces, and serve with Wine Sauce.
