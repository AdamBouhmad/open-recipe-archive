## Ingredients

- two cups of sugar
- half a cup of butter
- half a cup of sweet milk
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- whites of six eggs
- one heaping teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- three cups of sifted flour
- two cups of sweet milk
- two tablespoonfuls of cornstarch or flour
- half a cup of sugar
- yolks of four eggs
- a little cold milk
- flavoring, either vanilla or lemon

## Directions

1. Cream together sugar and butter
2. Add half a cup of sweet milk in which is dissolved half a teaspoonful of soda
3. Beat the whites of six eggs to a stiff froth and add to the mixture
4. Stir cream of tartar thoroughly into sifted flour and add quickly
5. Bake in a moderate oven in layers like jelly cake
6. For the Custard: Put two cups of sweet milk into a clean suitable dish and set it in a dish of boiling water on the range or stove
7. When the milk comes to a boil add cornstarch or flour stirred into half a cup of sugar, adding the yolks of four eggs and a little cold milk
8. Stir this into the boiling milk and when cooked thick enough set aside to cool
9. Add the flavoring, either vanilla or lemon
10. Spread custard between the cake layers
