## Ingredients

- one-half recipe Milk and Water Bread made with entire wheat flour
- two tablespoons molasses
- one cup English walnut meats or pecan nut broken in small pieces
- butter
- orange marmalade
- nut meats

## Directions

1. Use one-half recipe for Milk and Water Bread made with entire wheat flour, and add two tablespoons molasses and one cup English walnut meats or pecan nut broken in small pieces.
2. Let stand twenty-four hours.
3. Slice as thinly as possible.
4. Spread sparingly and evenly with butter.
5. Put between slices orange marmalade.
6. Remove crusts.
7. Cut in fancy shapes.
8. Garnish with nut meats.
