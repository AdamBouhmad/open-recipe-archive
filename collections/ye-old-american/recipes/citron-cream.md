## Ingredients

- citron melons
- powdered sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut the finest citron melons when perfectly ripe
2. Take out the seeds, and slice the nicest part into a China bowl in small pieces, that will lie conveniently
3. Cover them with powdered sugar, and let them stand several hours
4. Drain off the syrup they have made, and add as much cream as it will give a strong flavour to, and freeze it
