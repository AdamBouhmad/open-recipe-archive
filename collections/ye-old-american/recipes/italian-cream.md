## Ingredients

- two pints of cream
- six ounces of powdered loaf-sugar
- juice of two large lemons
- two glasses of white wine
- two ounces of isinglass
- four small tea-cups full of water

## Directions

1. Put two pints of cream into two bowls.
2. With one bowl mix six ounces of powdered loaf-sugar, the juice of two large lemons, and two glasses of white wine.
3. Add the other pint of cream, and stir the whole very hard.
4. Boil two ounces of isinglass with four small tea-cups full of water, till it is reduced to one half.
5. Stir the isinglass lukewarm into the other ingredients, and put them into a glass dish to congeal.
