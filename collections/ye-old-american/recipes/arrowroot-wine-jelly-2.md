## Ingredients

- One cupful of boiling water
- One scant tablespoonful of arrowroot
- Cold water
- One tablespoonful of sugar
- A pinch of salt
- One tablespoonful of brandy or three tablespoonfuls of wine

## Directions

1. Mix one scant tablespoonful of arrowroot with a little cold water
2. Add to one cupful of boiling water with one tablespoonful of sugar and a pinch of salt
3. Add one tablespoonful of brandy or three tablespoonfuls of wine
4. Boil and stir until clear
