## Ingredients

- macaroni
- milk
- water
- salt
- cheese
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil as much macaroni as will fill your dish in milk and water, till quite tender
2. Drain it on a sieve
3. Sprinkle a little salt over it
4. Put a layer in your dish then cheese and butter as in the polenta
5. Bake it in the same manner
