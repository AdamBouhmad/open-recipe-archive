## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- one and a half pounds of pounded sugar
- one tea-spoonful of cloves
- one tea-spoonful of mace
- one tea-spoonful of ginger
- four spoonsful of good yeast
- twelve eggs
- milk
- one pound of butter
- two gills of brandy
- two pounds of raisins

## Directions

1. Take three pounds of flour, one and a half pounds of pounded sugar, a tea-spoonful of cloves, one of mace, and one of ginger, all finely powdered
2. Pass the whole through a sieve
3. Put to it four spoonsful of good yeast, and twelve eggs
4. Mix it up well, and if not sufficiently soft, add a little milk
5. Make it up at night, and set it to rise
6. When well risen, knead into it a pound of butter, and two gills of brandy
7. Have ready two pounds of raisins stoned
8. Mix all well together
9. Pour it into a mould of proper size
10. Bake it in an oven heated as for bread
11. Let it stand till thoroughly done
12. Do not take it from the mould until quite cold
