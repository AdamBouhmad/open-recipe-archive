## Ingredients

- mashed boiled sweet potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- Sherry wine
- cream
- two tablespoons molasses
- one teaspoon butter

## Directions

1. Season mashed boiled sweet potatoes with butter, salt, pepper, and Sherry wine.
2. Moisten with cream, and beat five minutes.
3. Put in a buttered baking-dish, leaving a rough surface.
4. Pour over a syrup made by boiling two tablespoons molasses and one teaspoon butter five minutes.
