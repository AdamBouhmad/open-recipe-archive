## Ingredients

- six spoonsful of flour
- three spoonsful of corn meal
- a little salt
- four eggs
- a sufficient quantity of rich milk

## Directions

1. Take six spoonsful of flour and three of corn meal, with a little salt
2. Sift them, and make a thin batter with four eggs, and a sufficient quantity of rich milk
3. Bake it in little tin moulds in a quick oven
