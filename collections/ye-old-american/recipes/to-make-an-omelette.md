## Ingredients

- six or eight eggs
- parsley
- chives
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Break six or eight eggs in a dish
2. Beat them a little
3. Add parsley and chives chopped small, with pepper and salt
4. Mix all well together
5. Put a piece of butter in a pan
6. Let it melt over a clear fire till nearly brown
7. Pour in the eggs
8. Stir it in
9. In a few minutes it will be done sufficiently
10. Double it and dish it quite hot
