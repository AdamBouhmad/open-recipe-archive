## Ingredients

- cold roast of beef
- gravy
- butter
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- water
- flour
- potatoes
- milk
- egg
- tomato sauce or Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Cut off enough cold roast beef to half fill a baking-dish
2. Put sliced beef into a stewpan with saved gravy, a lump of butter, sliced onion, pepper and salt, and enough water to make plenty of gravy
3. Thicken by dredging in a tablespoonful of flour
4. Cover and stew gently on the fire without burning
5. Boil a sufficient quantity of potatoes to fill the baking-dish
6. Mash the potatoes smooth and beat with milk and butter
7. Transfer stewed meat to the baking-dish
8. Place a thick layer of mashed potatoes on top of the meat
9. Brush over with egg
10. Place in an oven and let remain until brown
11. Serve with tomato sauce, Worcestershire sauce or other preferred sauce
