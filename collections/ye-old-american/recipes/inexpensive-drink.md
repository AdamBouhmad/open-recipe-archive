## Ingredients

- one cupful of pure cider vinegar
- half a cupful of good molasses
- one quart pitcher of ice-water
- a tablespoonful of ground ginger

## Directions

1. Put one cupful of pure cider vinegar and half a cupful of good molasses into one quart pitcher of ice-water
2. A tablespoonful of ground ginger added makes a healthful beverage
