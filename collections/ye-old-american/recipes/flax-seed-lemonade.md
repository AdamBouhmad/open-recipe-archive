## Ingredients

- a large table-spoonful of flax-seed
- a tumbler and a half of cold water
- a quarter of a pound of pulverized sugar candy
- an ounce of pulverized gum arabic
- the juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. To a large table-spoonful of flax-seed allow a tumbler and a half of cold water.
2. Boil them together till the liquid becomes very sticky.
3. Strain it hot over a quarter of a pound of pulverized sugar candy, and an ounce of pulverized gum arabic.
4. Stir it till quite dissolved, and squeeze into it the juice of a lemon.
