## Ingredients

- one-fourth cup butter
- one small onion
- three cups cold boiled potato cubes
- one-half to three-fourths cup White Stock
- one half tablespoon curry powder
- one half tablespoon lemon juice
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook one-fourth cup butter with one small onion, finely chopped, until yellow; add three cups cold boiled potato cubes, and cook until potatoes have absorbed butter, then add from one-half to three-fourths cup White Stock, one half tablespoon each curry powder and lemon juice, and salt and pepper to taste.
2. Cook until potatoes have absorbed stock.
