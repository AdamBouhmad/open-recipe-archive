## Ingredients

- 2 cups of sugar
- 1 cup butter
- The yolks of five eggs and whites of two
- 1 cup of milk
- 3½ cups flour
- ⅓ teaspoonful soda
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar
- Whites of three eggs
- 1½ cup sugar
- 3 tablespoonfuls grated chocolate
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix together 2 cups of sugar, 1 cup butter, the yolks of five eggs and whites of two, 1 cup of milk, 3½ cups flour, ⅓ teaspoonful soda, and 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar sifted into the flour.
2. Bake in jelly-cake tins.
3. For the filling: Beat together whites of three eggs, 1½ cup sugar, 3 tablespoonfuls grated chocolate, and 1 teaspoonful vanilla well together.
4. Spread between the layers and on top of cake.
