## Ingredients

- crust
- apples
- custard made of four eggs and one quart of milk

## Directions

1. Lay a crust in your plates
2. slice apples thin and half fill your plates
3. pour over them a custard made of four eggs and one quart of milk, sweetened and seasoned to your taste
