## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- White of 1 egg
- ⅔ cup breast raw chicken
- Salt
- Few grains cayenne
- Slight grating nutmeg
- Bread and milk

## Directions

1. Cook bread and milk to a paste
2. Add butter, white of egg beaten stiff, and seasonings
3. Add chicken pounded and forced through purée strainer
