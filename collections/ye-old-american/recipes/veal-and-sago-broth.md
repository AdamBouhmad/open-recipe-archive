## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. knuckle of veal, cracked all to pieces
- 2 quarts of cold water
- 3 tablespoonfuls best pearl sago, soaked in a cup of cold water
- 1 cup cream, heated to boiling
- yolks of two eggs, beaten light
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil the veal and water in a covered saucepan very slowly until reduced to one quart of liquid.
2. Strain, skim, season with salt, and stir in the soaked sago (having previously warmed it by setting for half an hour in a saucepan of boiling water, and stirring from time to time.)
3. Simmer half an hour, taking care it does not burn.
4. Beat in the cream and eggs.
5. Give one good boil up, and turn out.
