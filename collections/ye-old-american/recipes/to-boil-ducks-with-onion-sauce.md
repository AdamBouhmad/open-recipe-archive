## Ingredients

- ducks
- water
- milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald and draw your ducks
2. Put them in warm water for a few minutes
3. Take them out and put them in an earthen pot
4. Pour over them a pint of boiling milk
5. Let them lie in it two or three hours
6. Dredge them well with flour
7. Put them in a copper of cold water
8. Cover and let them boil slowly twenty minutes
9. Take them out, and smother them with onion sauce
