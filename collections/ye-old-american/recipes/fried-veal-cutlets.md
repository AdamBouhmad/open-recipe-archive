## Ingredients

- two or three tablespoonfuls of lard or beef drippings
- veal cutlets
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- cold water
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Put into a frying pan two or three tablespoonfuls of lard or beef drippings.
2. When boiling hot lay in the cutlets, well seasoned with salt and pepper and dredged with flour.
3. Brown nicely on both sides, then remove the meat, and if you have more grease than is necessary for the gravy put it aside for further use.
4. Reserve a tablespoonful or more and rub into it a tablespoonful of flour, with the back of the spoon, until it is a smooth, rich brown color.
5. Then add gradually a cup of cold water and season with pepper and salt.
6. When the gravy is boiled up well return the meat to the pan and gravy.
7. Cover it closely and allow it to stew gently on the back of the range for fifteen minutes.
