## Ingredients

- salmon slices
- flour
- egg
- crumbs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the slices three-quarters of an inch thick
2. Dredge them with flour, or dip them in egg and crumbs
3. Fry a light brown
4. Season well with salt and pepper
