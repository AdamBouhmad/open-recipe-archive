## Ingredients

- oysters
- water
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Drain the liquor from two quarts of oysters; mix with it a small teacupful of hot water, add a little salt and pepper and set it over the fire in a saucepan.
2. Let it boil up once, put in the oysters, let them come to a boil, and when they "ruffle" add two tablespoonfuls of butter.
3. The instant it is melted and well stirred in, put in a pint of boiling milk and take the saucepan from the fire.
4. Serve with oyster or cream crackers.
5. Serve while hot.
