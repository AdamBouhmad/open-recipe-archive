## Ingredients

- capers
- melted butter
- liquor from capers

## Directions

1. Mix a sufficient quantity of capers and add them to the melted butter with a little of the liquor from the capers
2. Alternatively, use pickled nasturtiums or green pickle minced and put with the butter
