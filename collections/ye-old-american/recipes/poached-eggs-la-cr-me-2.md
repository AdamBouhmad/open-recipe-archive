## Ingredients

- hot water
- vinegar
- salt
- eggs
- cream
- rich milk
- butter
- pepper
- cornstarch
- toast

## Directions

1. Put a quart of hot water, a tablespoonful of vinegar and a teaspoonful of salt into a frying pan
2. Break each egg separately into a saucer
3. Slip the egg carefully into the hot water
4. Simmer three or four minutes until the white is set
5. With a skimmer lift them out into a hot dish
6. Empty the pan of its contents, put in half a cup of cream, or rich milk; if milk, a large spoonful of butter; pepper and salt to taste
7. Thicken with a very little cornstarch
8. Let it boil up once, and turn it over the dish of poached eggs
9. It can be served on toast or without
