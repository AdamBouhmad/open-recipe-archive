## Ingredients

- boiled lobster
- coral
- mustard
- cayenne
- salt
- table sauce
- water
- vinegar
- butter

## Directions

1. Take out all the meat from a boiled lobster, reserving the coral
2. Season highly with mustard, cayenne, salt and some kind of table sauce
3. Stew until well mixed and put it in a covered saucepan, with just enough hot water to keep from burning
4. Rub the coral smooth, moistening with vinegar until it is thin enough to pour easily
5. Stir the coral into the saucepan
6. Stir in a heaping teaspoonful of butter
7. When it boils again it is done and should be taken up at once
