## Ingredients

- three slices of bread
- butter
- a quarter of a pound of cheese
- salt and pepper to taste
- four eggs
- three cups of milk

## Directions

1. Take three slices of bread well-buttered, first cutting off the brown outside crust.
2. Grate fine a quarter of a pound of any kind of good cheese.
3. Lay the bread in layers in a buttered baking-dish.
4. Sprinkle over it the grated cheese, some salt and pepper to taste.
5. Mix four well-beaten eggs with three cups of milk.
6. Pour it over the bread and cheese.
7. Bake it in a hot oven as you would cook a bread pudding.
