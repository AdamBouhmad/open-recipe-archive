## Ingredients

- three ounces of dry cheese
- yolks of two eggs
- four ounces of grated bread
- three ounces of butter
- a dessertspoonful of made mustard
- salt and pepper
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Grate three ounces of dry cheese and mix it with the yolks of two eggs.
2. Put four ounces of grated bread and three of butter.
3. Beat the whole together in a mortar with a dessertspoonful of made mustard, a little salt and some pepper.
4. Toast some slices of bread, cut off the outside crust, cut it in shapes and spread the paste thick upon them.
5. Put them in the oven, let them become hot and slightly browned.
6. Serve hot as possible.
