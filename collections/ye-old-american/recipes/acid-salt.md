## Ingredients

- an ounce of salt of sorrel
- an ounce of the best cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Pound together in a marble mortar an ounce of salt of sorrel, and an ounce of the best cream of tartar, mixing them thoroughly
2. Put it in little wooden boxes or covered gallipots
3. Rub it on your hands when they are stained, washing them in cold water, and using the acid salt instead of soap
4. For linen or muslin that is spotted with ink or fruit juice, hold the stained part tightly stretched over a cup or bowl of boiling water
5. With your finger rub on the acid salt till the stain disappears
6. Do this before the article is washed
