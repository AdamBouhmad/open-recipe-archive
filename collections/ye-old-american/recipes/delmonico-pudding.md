## Ingredients

- three tablespoonfuls of cornstarch
- yolks of five eggs
- six tablespoonfuls of sugar
- a little cold milk
- a little salt
- one quart of milk
- canned peaches or other fruit
- whites of eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of white sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs light, then add the sugar and beat again till very light
2. Mix the cornstarch with a little cold milk
3. Mix all together and stir into one quart of milk just as it is about to boil, having added a little salt
4. Stir it until it has thickened well
5. Pour it into a dish for the table and place it in the oven until it will bear icing
6. Place over the top a layer of canned peaches or other fruit (and it improves it to mix the syrup of the fruit with the custard part)
7. Beat the whites to a stiff froth with two tablespoonfuls of white sugar to an egg
8. Put it into the oven until it is a light brown
