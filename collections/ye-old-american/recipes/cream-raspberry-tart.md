## Ingredients

- Paste
- Raspberries
- Powdered sugar
- 1 small cup of milk—half cream, if you can get it
- Whites of two eggs
- 1 tablespoonful white sugar
- ½ teaspoonful corn-starch

## Directions

1. Line a dish with paste and fill with raspberries, made very sweet with powdered sugar.
2. Cover with paste, but do not pinch it down at the edges.
3. When done, lift the top crust, which should be thicker than usual, and pour upon the fruit the following mixture: heat the milk to boiling, beat the egg whites light and stir into the boiling milk, add the white sugar and corn-starch wet in cold milk.
4. Boil these ingredients three minutes; let them get perfectly cold before you put them into the tart.
5. Replace the top crust, and set the pie aside to cool.
6. Sprinkle sugar over the top before serving.
