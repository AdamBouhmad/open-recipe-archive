## Ingredients

- peppers
- salt and water
- alum, a small piece
- vinegar, best quality
- cloves
- mace
- mustard seed

## Directions

1. Extract the seeds from the peppers
2. Proceed in the same manner as cucumbers
3. Do not put peppers into the same jar with cucumbers, as the former will destroy the latter
