## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1⅓ cups sour milk
- 4 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 teaspoons soda
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon grated nutmeg
- Flour

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given; shape, fry, and drain.
