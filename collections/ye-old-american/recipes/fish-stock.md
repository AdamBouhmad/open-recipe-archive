## Ingredients

- A good-sized piece of sweet butter
- A sliced onion
- Sliced tomatoes
- Different kinds of fish (oysters, clams, smelts, prawns, crabs, shrimps and all kinds of pan-fish)
- A bunch of sweet herbs
- Salt and pepper
- Water

## Directions

1. Place a saucepan over the fire with butter and a sliced onion
2. Put in sliced tomatoes
3. Add as many different kinds of fish as you can get
4. Cook all together until the onions are well browned
5. Add a bunch of sweet herbs, salt and pepper, and sufficient water to make the required amount of stock
6. Cook for half an hour
7. Pound it with a wooden pestle
8. Strain and cook again until it jellies
