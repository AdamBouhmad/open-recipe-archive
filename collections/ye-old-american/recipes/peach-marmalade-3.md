## Ingredients

- peaches
- sugar
- lemon juice
- kernels
- water
- pineapple

## Directions

1. Pare, stone, and weigh the fruit
2. Heat slowly to draw out the juice, stirring up often from the bottom with a wooden spoon
3. After it is hot, boil quickly, still stirring, three-quarters of an hour
4. Add the sugar, allowing three-quarters of a pound to each pound of the fruit
5. Boil up well for five minutes, taking off every particle of scum
6. Add the juice of a lemon for every three pounds of fruit, and a very little water in which one-fourth of the kernels have been boiled and steeped
7. Stew all together ten minutes, stirring to a smooth paste, and take from the fire
8. Put up hot in air-tight cans, or, when cold, in small stone or glass jars, with brandied tissue-paper fitted neatly to the surface of the marmalade
