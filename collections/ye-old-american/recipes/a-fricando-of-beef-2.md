## Ingredients

- a few slices of beef six inches long, two or three wide, and one thick
- bacon
- gravy

## Directions

1. Lard them with bacon
2. Dredge them well
3. Make them a nice brown before a brisk fire
4. Stew them half an hour in a well seasoned gravy
5. Put some stewed sorrel or spinage in the dish
6. Lay on the beef
7. Pour over a sufficient quantity of gravy
8. Garnish with fried balls
