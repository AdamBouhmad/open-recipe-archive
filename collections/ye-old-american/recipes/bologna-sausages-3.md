## Ingredients

- ten pounds beef
- four pounds pork
- six ounces fine salt
- one ounce black pepper
- half an ounce cayenne
- one table-spoonful powdered cloves
- one clove garlic
- large beef skins
- salt and vinegar
- packthread or fine twine
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Take ten pounds of beef, and four pounds of pork
2. Chop it very fine, and mix it well together
3. Season it with six ounces of fine salt, one ounce of black pepper, half an ounce of cayenne, one table-spoonful of powdered cloves; and one clove of garlic minced very fine
4. Have ready some large skins nicely cleaned and prepared, (they should be beef-skins,) and wash them in salt and vinegar
5. Fill them with the above mixture, and secure the ends by tying them with packthread or fine twine
6. Make a brine of salt and water strong enough to bear up an egg
7. Put the sausages into it, and let them lie for three weeks, turning them daily
8. Take them out, wipe them dry, hang them up and smoke them
9. Before you put them away rub them all over with sweet oil
10. Keep them in ashes
11. You may fry them or not before you eat them
