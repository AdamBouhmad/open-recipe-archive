## Ingredients

- three tablespoonfuls of sugar
- a cupful of water
- six whole cloves
- a piece of stick cinnamon
- the yellow rind of a lemon
- the juice of the lemon
- a cup of brandy

## Directions

1. Brown over the fire three tablespoonfuls of sugar
2. Add a cupful of water, six whole cloves and a piece of stick cinnamon, the yellow rind of a lemon cut very thin
3. Let the sauce boil, strain while hot
4. Pour it into a sauce bowl containing the juice of the lemon and a cup of brandy
5. Serve warm
