## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- half a cup of butter
- one cup and a half of flour
- half a cup of wine
- one cup of raisins
- two eggs
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- whites of two eggs
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Put ingredients together with care as if it were a very rich cake
2. Bake it in three layers
3. Make frosting from the whites of two eggs with enough powdered sugar to make it thick
4. Put frosting between layers
5. The top of the cake may be frosted if you choose
