## Ingredients

- two cups hot riced potatoes
- two tablespoons butter
- one-half teaspoon salt
- yolks of three eggs
- beaten egg
- one teaspoon water

## Directions

1. To two cups hot riced potatoes add two tablespoons butter, one-half teaspoon salt, and yolks of three eggs slightly beaten
2. Shape, using pastry bag and tube, in form of baskets, pyramids, crowns, leaves, roses, etc.
3. Brush over with beaten egg diluted with one teaspoon water
4. brown in a hot oven
