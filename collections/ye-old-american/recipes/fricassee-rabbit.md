## Ingredients

- two young rabbits
- salt and water
- a pint of cold water
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- an onion
- a pinch of mace
- half a nutmeg
- a pinch of pepper
- half a pound of salt pork
- a cup of cream or milk
- two eggs
- a tablespoonful of butter
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a little milk
- the juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Clean two young rabbits and cut into joints
2. Soak in salt and water for half an hour
3. Put into a saucepan with a pint of cold water, a bunch of sweet herbs, an onion finely minced, a pinch of mace, half a nutmeg, a pinch of pepper and half a pound of salt pork cut in small thin slices
4. Cover and stew until tender
5. Take out the rabbits and set in a dish where they will keep warm
6. Add to the gravy a cup of cream or milk, two well-beaten eggs stirred in a little at a time, a tablespoonful of butter, and a thickening made of a tablespoonful of flour and a little milk
7. Boil up once and remove the saucepan from the fire
8. Squeeze in the juice of a lemon while stirring
9. Pour over the rabbits
