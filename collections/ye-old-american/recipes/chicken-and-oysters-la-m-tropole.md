## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- 2 cups cream
- 2 cups cold cooked chicken, cut in dice
- 1 pint oysters, cleaned and drained
- ⅓ cup finely chopped celery

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of butter, flour, salt, pepper, and cream.
2. Add chicken dice and oysters.
3. Cook until oysters are plump.
4. Serve sprinkled with celery.
