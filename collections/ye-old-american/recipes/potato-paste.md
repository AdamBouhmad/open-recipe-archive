## Ingredients

- mealy potatos
- water
- one quart of flour
- apples or other fruit
- butter
- sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil mealy potatos quite soft, first taking off the skins
2. rub them while hot through a sieve
3. put them in a stew pan over the fire, with as much water as will make it the consistence of thick mush
4. sift one quart of flour, and make it into a paste
5. with this mush, knead it till light
6. roll it out thin, make the dumplins small
7. fill them with apples, or any other fruit
8. tie them up in a thick cloth
9. boil them nicely
10. eat them with butter, sugar, and nutmeg
