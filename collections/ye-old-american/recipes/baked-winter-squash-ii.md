## Ingredients

- squash
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut squash in halves, remove seeds and stringy portion, place in a dripping-pan, cover.
2. Bake two hours, or until soft, in a slow oven.
3. Remove from shell, mash, and season with butter, salt, and pepper.
