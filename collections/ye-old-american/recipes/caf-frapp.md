## Ingredients

- White 1 egg
- ½ cup cold water
- ½ cup ground coffee
- 4 cups boiling water
- 1 cup sugar

## Directions

1. Beat white of egg slightly, add cold water, and mix with coffee; turn into scalded coffee-pot, add boiling water, and let boil one minute; place on back of range ten minutes; strain, add sugar, cool, and freeze same as Pineapple Frappé.
2. Serve in frappé glasses, with whipped cream, sweetened and flavored.
