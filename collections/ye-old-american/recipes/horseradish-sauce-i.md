## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons grated horseradish root
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- 4 tablespoons heavy cream

## Directions

1. Mix first four ingredients, and add cream beaten stiff
