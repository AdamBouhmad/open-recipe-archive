## Ingredients

- one knuckle of veal
- one pound lean veal
- one onion
- boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- hard-boiled eggs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Separate a knuckle of veal in pieces by sawing through bone.
2. Wipe, put in kettle with one pound lean veal and one onion; cover with boiling water, and cook slowly until veal is tender.
3. Drain, chop meat finely, and season highly with salt and pepper.
4. Garnish bottom of a mould with slices of hard-boiled eggs and parsley.
5. Put in layer of meat, layer of thinly sliced hard-boiled eggs, sprinkle with finely chopped parsley, and cover with remaining meat.
6. Pour over liquor, which should be reduced to one cupful.
7. Press and chill, turn on a dish, and garnish with parsley.
