## Ingredients

- cold turkey or chicken
- butter
- bread or biscuit
- salted water
- milk
- gravy
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- dark meat

## Directions

1. Warm up pieces of cold turkey or chicken with a little butter in a frying pan
2. Place it on a warm platter and surround it with pieces of small thick slices of bread or biscuit halved
3. Dip the bread or biscuit in hot salted water
4. Place the platter in a warm oven with the door open
5. Into the frying pan put a large spoonful of butter, one or two cupfuls of milk, and any gravy that may be left over
6. Bring it to a boil and add sufficient flour wet in a little cold milk or water to make it the consistency of cream
7. Season with salt and pepper and add a little of the dark meat chopped very fine
8. Let the sauce cook a few moments then pour over the biscuit and fowl
