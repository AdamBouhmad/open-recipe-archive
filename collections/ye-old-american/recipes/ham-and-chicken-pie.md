## Ingredients

- tender young chicken
- pie-crust
- cold boiled ham slices
- hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- pepper
- veal or chicken gravy

## Directions

1. Cut up and parboil a tender young chicken.
2. Line a deep dish with a good pie-crust.
3. Cut some thin slices of cold boiled ham, and spread a layer next the crust.
4. Arrange pieces of the fowl upon the ham.
5. Cover this, in turn, with slices of hard-boiled eggs, buttered and peppered.
6. Proceed in this order until your materials are used up.
7. Pour in enough veal or chicken gravy to prevent dryness.
8. Bake one hour and a quarter for a large pie.
