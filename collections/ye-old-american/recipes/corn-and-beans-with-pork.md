## Ingredients

- A good piece of pork, either salt or fresh
- Corn
- Beans, either dried or green
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the pork by itself till quite tender
2. Boil also the corn and beans separately
3. If string-beans, they must be cut in three
4. When the corn is well boiled, cut it from the cob, and mix it with the boiled beans
5. Put it into a pot with the boiled pork, and barely sufficient water to cover it
6. Season with pepper, and stew the whole together till nearly dry
