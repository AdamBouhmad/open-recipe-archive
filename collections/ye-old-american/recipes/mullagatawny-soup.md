## Ingredients

- four onions
- one carrot
- two turnips
- one head of celery
- three quarts of liquor in which one or two fowls have been boiled
- one tablespoonful of currie powder
- one tablespoonful of flour
- pieces of roast chicken
- boiled rice
- a teaspoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Cut four onions, one carrot, two turnips, and one head of celery into three quarts of liquor, in which one or two fowls have been boiled.
2. Keep it over a brisk fire till it boils.
3. Place it on a corner of the fire, and let it simmer twenty minutes.
4. Add one tablespoonful of currie powder, and one tablespoonful of flour.
5. Mix the whole well together.
6. Let it boil three minutes.
7. Pass it through a colander.
8. Serve with pieces of roast chicken in it.
9. Add boiled rice in a separate dish.
10. If too thick, add a little boiling water and a teaspoonful of sugar.
