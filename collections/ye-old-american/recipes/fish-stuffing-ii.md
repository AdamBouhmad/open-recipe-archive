## Ingredients

- Cracker crumbs, 1 cup
- Melted butter, ¼ cup
- Salt, ¼ teaspoon
- Pepper, ⅛ teaspoon
- Onion juice, few drops
- Parsley, 1 teaspoon finely chopped
- Capers, 1 teaspoon finely chopped
- Pickles, 1 teaspoon finely chopped

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given. This makes a dry, crumbly stuffing.
