## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of grated cocoanut
- one cupful of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of flour
- the whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Soak the cocoanut, if desiccated, in milk enough to cover it
2. Beat the whites of the eggs, add gradually the sugar, cocoanut and flour
3. With your fingers make, by rolling the mixture, into cone shapes
4. Place them on buttered sheets of tin covered with buttered letter paper
5. Bake in a moderate heat about fifteen or twenty minutes
6. They should cool before removing from the tins
