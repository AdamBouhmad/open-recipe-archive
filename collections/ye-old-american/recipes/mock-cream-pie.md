## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one pint of milk
- cupful of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of cornstarch or three of flour
- pinch of salt
- teaspoonful of butter
- pie crust
- nutmeg
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar, cornstarch and yolks of the eggs together
2. After the milk has come to a boil, stir in the mixture
3. Add a pinch of salt and about a teaspoonful of butter
4. Make crust the same as any pie and bake
5. Fill with the custard
6. Grate over a little nutmeg and bake again
7. Take the whites of the eggs and beat to a stiff froth with two tablespoonfuls of sugar
8. Spread over the top and brown in a quick oven
