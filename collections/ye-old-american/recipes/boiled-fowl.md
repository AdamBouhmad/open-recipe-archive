## Ingredients

- four-pound fowl
- salt
- Egg Sauce or Oyster Sauce or Celery Sauce

## Directions

1. Dress, clean, and truss a four-pound fowl.
2. Tie in cheese-cloth, place on trivet in a kettle, half surround with boiling water, and cover.
3. Cook slowly until tender, turning occasionally.
4. Add salt the last hour of cooking.
5. Serve with Egg, Oyster, or Celery Sauce.
