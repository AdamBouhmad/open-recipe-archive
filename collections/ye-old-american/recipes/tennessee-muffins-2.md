## Ingredients

- One pint of corn meal
- One pint of flour
- One tablespoonful of sugar
- One teaspoonful of salt
- Three teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- One tablespoonful of lard or butter
- Two eggs
- One pint of milk

## Directions

1. Sift together corn meal, flour, sugar, salt and powder
2. Rub in lard or butter cold
3. Add eggs beaten and milk
4. Mix into batter of consistency of cup-cake
5. Have muffin-rings cold and well greased
6. Fill two-thirds full
7. Bake in hot oven fifteen minutes
