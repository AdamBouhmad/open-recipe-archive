## Ingredients

- two quarts of flour
- one pint of warm water or new milk
- one teaspoonful of salt
- half a cup of melted lard or butter
- half a cupful of yeast
- nearly a teaspoonful of soda
- warm butter or sweet lard

## Directions

1. Sift flour in a mixing-pan and make a hole in the middle
2. Pour in water or milk, salt, and melted lard
3. Stir in a little flour, then add yeast
4. Stir in as much flour as you can conveniently
5. Let it rise over night
6. In the morning add soda and more flour to make a soft dough
7. Mold for fifteen to twenty minutes
8. Let it rise until light again
9. Roll out about half an inch thick and cut with a biscuit-cutter, or make into little balls
10. Cover and set in a warm place to rise
11. When light, bake a light brown in a moderate oven
12. Rub a little warm butter or sweet lard on the sides of biscuits to prevent sticking
