## Ingredients

- plain cake
- frosting
- melted chocolate

## Directions

1. Have a plain cake baked in rather thin sheets
2. Cut into small oblong pieces the size and shape of a domino, a trifle larger
3. Frost the top and sides
4. When the frosting is hard, draw the black lines and make the dots with a small brush dipped in melted chocolate
