## Ingredients

- Coarser parts of turtle-meat with entrails and bones
- Four quarts of water
- Sweet herbs
- Onions
- Pepper
- Salt
- Finer parts of turtle and green fat
- Two quarts of water
- Browned flour
- Turtle eggs or force-meat balls
- Juice of one lemon
- A glass of Madeira

## Directions

1. Chop up the coarser parts of the turtle-meat, with the entrails and bones
2. Add to them four quarts of water, and stew four hours with the herbs, onions, pepper, and salt
3. Stew very slowly, but do not let it cease to boil during this time
4. At the end of four hours strain the soup, and add the finer parts of the turtle and the green fat, which has been simmered for one hour in two quarts of water
5. Thicken with browned flour
6. Return to the soup-pot, and simmer gently an hour longer
7. If there are eggs in the turtle, boil them in a separate vessel for four hours, and throw into the soup before taking it up, or put in force-meat balls
8. Add the juice of the lemon and the wine
9. Beat up once and pour out
