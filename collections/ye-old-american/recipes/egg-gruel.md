## Ingredients

- Yolk of an egg
- One tablespoonful of sugar
- One teacupful of boiling water
- White of an egg
- Any seasoning or spice desired

## Directions

1. Beat the yolk of an egg with one tablespoonful of sugar
2. Pour one teacupful of boiling water on it
3. Add the white of an egg, beaten to a froth, with any seasoning or spice desired
4. Take warm
