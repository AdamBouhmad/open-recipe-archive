## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. flour
- 1 lb. best butter
- ½ lb. powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Chop the flour and butter together, having made the latter quite soft by setting it near the fire.
2. Knead in the sugar, roll into a sheet half an inch thick, and cut in shapes with a cake-cutter.
3. Bake upon buttered paper in a shallow tin until crisp and of a delicate yellowish brown.
