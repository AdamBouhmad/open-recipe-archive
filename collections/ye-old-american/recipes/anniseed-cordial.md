## Ingredients

- a pound of loaf-sugar
- two quarts of water
- two quarts of white brandy
- a table-spoonful of oil of anniseed

## Directions

1. Melt a pound of loaf-sugar in two quarts of water.
2. Mix it with two quarts of white brandy, and add a table-spoonful of oil of anniseed.
3. Let it stand a week; then filter it through white blotting paper, and bottle it for use.
