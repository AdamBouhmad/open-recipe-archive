## Ingredients

- lambs' kidneys
- lukewarm water
- salt and pepper
- flour
- butter
- one sliced onion
- one-half shallot, finely chopped
- three tablespoons butter
- three tablespoons flour
- one and one-fourth cups Brown Stock
- one-half cup mushroom caps
- white wine

## Directions

1. Soak lambs' kidneys one hour in lukewarm water.
2. Drain, clean, cut in slices, season with salt and pepper, and dredge with flour.
3. Sauté in butter.
4. Fry one sliced onion and one-half shallot, finely chopped, in three tablespoons butter until yellow.
5. Add three tablespoons flour and one and one-fourth cups Brown Stock.
6. Cook five minutes and strain.
7. Add one-half cup mushroom caps peeled and cut in quarters.
8. Season with salt and pepper and add kidneys.
9. Serve as soon as heated. White wine may be added if desired.
