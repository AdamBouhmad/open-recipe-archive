## Ingredients

- green corn
- Lima beans
- string beans
- butter beans
- water
- milk
- butter
- flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Have a third more corn than beans, when the corn has been cut from the cob and the beans shelled.
2. Put into boiling water enough to cover them—no more—and stew gently together until tender, perhaps half an hour, stirring now and then.
3. Pour off nearly all the water and add a large cupful of milk.
4. Stew in this, watching to prevent burning, for an hour.
5. Stir in a great lump of butter, a teaspoonful of flour wet with cold milk, pepper and salt to taste.
6. Boil up once and pour into a deep vegetable-dish.
7. If using string-beans, string and cut up into half-inch lengths before cooking.
