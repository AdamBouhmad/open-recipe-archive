## Ingredients

- leaf lard
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Skin the leaf lard carefully, cut it into small pieces, and put it into a kettle or saucepan.
2. Pour in a cupful of water to prevent burning.
3. Set it over the fire where it will melt slowly.
4. Stir it frequently and let it simmer until nothing remains but brown scraps.
5. Remove the scraps with a perforated skimmer, throw in a little salt to settle the fat.
6. Watch it constantly, stirring it from the bottom until the salt is thrown in to settle it.
7. Set it back on the range until clear.
8. Strain through a coarse cloth into jars.
