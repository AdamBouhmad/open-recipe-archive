## Ingredients

- Half a cup cold boiled rice
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups Indian meal
- 1 tablespoonful lard or butter
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 pint milk

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs light, and the rice to a smooth batter in the milk
2. Melt the shortening
3. Stir all together very hard
4. Bake in shallow tins very quickly
