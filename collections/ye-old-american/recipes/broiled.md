## Ingredients

- Game birds
- Cold water
- Salt and pepper
- Butter

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and split down the back.
2. Lay in cold water half an hour.
3. Wipe carefully, season with salt and pepper, and broil on a gridiron over a bright fire.
4. When done, lay in a hot dish, butter on both sides well, and serve at once.
