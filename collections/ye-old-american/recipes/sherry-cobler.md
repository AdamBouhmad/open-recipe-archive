## Ingredients

- the yellow rind of an orange or lemon
- a heaping table-spoonful of powdered loaf-sugar
- pounded ice
- sherry wine
- ice-water

## Directions

1. Lay in the bottom of a tumbler some pieces of the yellow rind of an orange or lemon, pared off very thin
2. Add a heaping table-spoonful of powdered loaf-sugar
3. Upon this, place some pounded ice
4. Pour on sherry wine till the tumbler is one-third, or half full
5. Hold an empty tumbler inverted upon the top of that which contains the ingredients
6. Place the glasses so that their edges exactly meet, and leave no opening for any portion of the contents to escape
7. Keep your hands fast on the two tumblers, one above and one below
8. Turn them up and down, back and forwards, till the articles inside are thoroughly mixed
9. Take off the upper tumbler, and let the lower one stand still a few moments
10. Fill it up with ice-water
