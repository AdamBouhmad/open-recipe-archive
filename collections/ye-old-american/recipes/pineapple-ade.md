## Ingredients

- very ripe pineapples
- powdered white sugar
- boiling water
- ice

## Directions

1. Pare and slice some very ripe pineapples
2. Cut the slices into small pieces
3. Put them with all their juice into a large pitcher
4. Sprinkle among them plenty of powdered white sugar
5. Pour on boiling water, allowing a small half pint to each pineapple
6. Cover the pitcher and let it stand till quite cool
7. Occasionally press down the pineapple with a spoon
8. Set the pitcher for a while in ice
9. Strain the infusion into another vessel
10. Transfer it to tumblers, putting into each glass some more sugar and a bit of ice
