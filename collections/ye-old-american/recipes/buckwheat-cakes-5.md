## Ingredients

- half a pint of buckwheat flour
- a quarter of a pint of corn meal
- a quarter of a pint of wheat flour
- a little salt
- two eggs beaten very light
- one quart of new milk
- one tablespoonful of butter or sweet

## Directions

1. Mix together half a pint of buckwheat flour, a quarter of a pint of corn meal, a quarter of a pint of wheat flour, and a little salt
2. Warm new milk and mix with two eggs beaten very light before the flour is put in
3. Add one tablespoonful of butter or sweet
