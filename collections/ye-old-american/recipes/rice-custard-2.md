## Ingredients

- rice
- milk
- preserved quince or peach, or fruit jelly
- raisins or currants

## Directions

1. Boil some rice in milk till it is quite dry
2. Put it into small tea-cups, pressing it down hard
3. When it is cold and has taken the shape of the cups, turn it out into a deep dish
4. Pour a boiled custard round it
5. Lay on the top of each lump of rice a piece of preserved quince or peach, or a piece of fruit jelly
6. In boiling the rice, you may mix with it raisins or currants; if so, omit the sweetmeats on the top
7. Ground rice is best
