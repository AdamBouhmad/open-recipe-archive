## Ingredients

- 1 lb. lean beef, cut into small pieces
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put into a jar without a drop of water; cover tightly, and set in a pot of cold water.
2. Heat gradually to a boil, and continue this steadily for three or four hours, until the meat is like white rags, and the juice all drawn out.
3. Season with salt to taste, and when cold, skim.
4. Serve ice-cold or hot with Albert biscuit or thin wafers, unleavened.
