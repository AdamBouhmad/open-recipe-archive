## Ingredients

- halibut
- salt water
- butter
- water
- walnut catsup
- Worcestershire sauce
- lemon
- brown flour

## Directions

1. Take a nice piece of halibut weighing five or six pounds and lay it in salt water for two hours
2. Wipe it dry and score the outer skin
3. Set it in a dripping pan in a moderately hot oven and bake an hour, basting often with butter and water heated together in a sauce pan or tin cup
4. When a fork will penetrate it easily, it is done
5. Take the gravy in the dripping pan, add a little boiling water, should there not be enough
6. Stir in a tablespoonful of walnut catsup, a teaspoonful of Worcestershire sauce, the juice of a lemon
7. Thicken with brown flour, previously wet with cold water
8. Boil up once and put in a sauce boat
