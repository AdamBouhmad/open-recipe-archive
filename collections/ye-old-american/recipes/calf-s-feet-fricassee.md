## Ingredients

- calf's feet
- white gravy
- white wine
- lemon pickle
- salt
- curry powder
- eggs
- milk
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil the feet till very tender, cut them in two and pull out the large bones.
2. Have half a pint of good white gravy, add to it a spoonful of white wine, one of lemon pickle, and some salt, with a tea-spoonful of curry powder.
3. Stew the feet in it fifteen minutes, and thicken it with the yelks of two eggs, a gill of milk, a large spoonful of butter, and two of white flour.
4. Let the thickening be very smooth, shake the stew pan over the fire a few minutes, but do not let it boil lest the eggs and milk should curdle.
