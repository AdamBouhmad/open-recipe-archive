## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 apples
- 1/4 pound bread crumbs
- 1 lemon
- 3 ounces sugar
- 3 ounces currants
- 1/2 wine-glassful wine
- nutmeg
- butter and sugar for sauce

## Directions

1. Pare, core and mince apples and mix with bread crumbs, nutmeg, sugar, and currants
2. Add juice of lemon and half the rind grated
3. Beat eggs well and moisten mixture with them, beating all together
4. Add wine last
5. Put pudding in buttered mold and tie down with cloth
6. Boil one hour and a half
7. Serve with sweet sauce
