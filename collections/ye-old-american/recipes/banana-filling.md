## Ingredients

- whites of two eggs
- one cup and a half of powdered sugar
- bananas
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Make an icing of the whites of two eggs and one cup and a half of powdered sugar
2. Spread this on the layers, then cover thickly and entirely with bananas sliced thin or chopped fine
3. Simply frost the top
