## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- one egg
- one cup sweet milk
- two cups flour
- one tablespoonful butter
- two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- flavoring
- one egg
- one-half cup sugar
- one-quarter cup flour
- a very little milk
- one-half pint of boiling milk

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, egg, sweet milk, flour, butter, and baking powder to taste
2. Divide into three parts and bake in round shallow pans
3. For Cream: Beat one egg and one-half cup sugar together
4. Add one-quarter cup flour, wet with a very little milk
5. Stir this mixture into one-half pint of boiling milk, until thick
6. Flavor to taste
7. Spread the cream when cool between the cakes
