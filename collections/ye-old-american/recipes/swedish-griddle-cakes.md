## Ingredients

- One pint of white flour, sifted
- Six eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately
- One saltspoonful of salt
- One saltspoonful of soda dissolved in vinegar
- Milk to make a thin batter

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks light, add the salt, soda, and two cupfuls of milk
2. Add the flour and beaten whites alternately
3. Thin with more milk if necessary
