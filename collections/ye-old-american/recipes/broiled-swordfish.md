## Ingredients

- Swordfish
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Cucumber Sauce I or Horseradish Sauce I

## Directions

1. Clean and broil fish.
2. Spread with butter.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Serve with Cucumber Sauce I, or Horseradish Sauce I.
