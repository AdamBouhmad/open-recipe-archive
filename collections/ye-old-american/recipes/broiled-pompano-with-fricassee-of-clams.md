## Ingredients

- Pompano
- Butter, melted
- Mashed potatoes
- Clam-shells, 10 halves
- Clams, 1 pint
- Butter, 2 tablespoons
- Flour, 2 tablespoons
- Cream, one-third cup
- Salt and cayenne
- Egg yolk, 1

## Directions

1. Clean and broil fish as directed in Ways of Cooking Fish.
2. When nearly cooked, slip from broiler onto a hot platter and brush over with melted butter.
3. Surround with two borders of mashed potatoes, one-inch apart, forced through a pastry bag and tube.
4. Arrange ten halves of clam-shells between potato borders, at equal distances.
5. Fill spaces between shells with potato roses.
6. Place in oven to finish cooking fish and to brown potatoes.
7. For Fricassee of Clams: Clean one pint clams, finely chop hard portions and reserve soft portions.
8. Melt two tablespoons butter, add chopped clams, two tablespoons flour, and pour on gradually one-third cup cream.
9. Strain sauce, add soft part of clams, cook one minute, season with salt and cayenne, and add yolk of one egg slightly beaten.
10. Just before serving, fill clam-shells with Fricassee of Clams.
