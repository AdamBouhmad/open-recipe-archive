## Ingredients

- two quarts milk
- 3 pound of sugar
- 3 pound of shortning, warmed hot
- a quart of sweet cyder
- 18 eggs
- allspice
- orange
- fennel
- carroway
- coriander seeds
- 9 pounds of flour
- 3 pints emptins

## Directions

1. To two quarts milk, 3 pound of sugar, 3 pound of shortning, warmed hot, add a quart of sweet cyder, this curdle, add 18 eggs, allspice and orange to your taste, or fennel, carroway or coriander seeds; put to 9 pounds of flour, 3 pints emptins, and bake well
