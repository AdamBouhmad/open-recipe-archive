## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ cup molasses
- Yolks 4 eggs
- 1 cup sour milk
- 2½ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon clove
- ¼ nutmeg, grated
- 1 cup raisins, seeded and cut in pieces
- ½ cup currants
- ½ cup English walnut meats, cut in pieces
- 1½ teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in the order given.
2. This recipe makes two loaves.
