## Ingredients

- porterhouse or cross-cut of rump steak
- anchovies
- stoned olives
- green butter
- chopped parsley
- stuffed tomatoes
- fried potato balls
- noodle mixture
- two tablespoons butter
- two and one-half tablespoons browned flour
- one cup Chicken Stock
- one tablespoon tomato catsup
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Broil porterhouse or cross-cut of rump steak.
2. Garnish with anchovies, and stoned olives stuffed with green butter and chopped parsley.
3. Arrange around steak stuffed tomatoes, and fried potato balls served in shells made from noodle mixture.
4. Melt two tablespoons butter, add two and one-half tablespoons browned flour, then add one cup Chicken Stock.
5. Season with one tablespoon tomato catsup and salt and pepper.
6. Pour around the steak.
