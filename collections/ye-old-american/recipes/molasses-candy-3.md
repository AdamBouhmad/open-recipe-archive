## Ingredients

- one quart of West India molasses
- one cupful of brown sugar
- a piece of butter the size of half an egg
- half a teaspoonful of baking soda

## Directions

1. Put molasses, brown sugar, and butter into a six-quart kettle
2. Let it boil over a slack fire until it begins to look thick, stirring it often to prevent burning
3. Test it by taking some out and dropping a few drops in a cup of cold water; if it hardens quickly and breaks short between the teeth it is boiled enough
4. Put in half a teaspoonful of baking soda, and stir it well
5. Pour it out into well-buttered flat tins
6. When partly cooled, take up the candy with your hands well buttered then pull and double, and so on, until the candy is a whitish yellow
7. Cut in strips and roll or twist
8. If flavoring is desired, drop the flavoring on the top as it begins to cool and when it is pulled, the whole will be flavored
