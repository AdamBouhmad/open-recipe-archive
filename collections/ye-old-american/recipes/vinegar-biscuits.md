## Ingredients

- two quarts of flour
- one large tablespoonful of lard or butter
- one tablespoonful and a half of vinegar
- one teaspoonful of soda
- two eggs
- warm water

## Directions

1. Put the soda in the vinegar and stir it well
2. Stir in the flour
3. Beat two eggs very light and add to it
4. Make a dough with warm water stiff enough to roll out
5. Cut with a biscuit-cutter one inch thick
6. Bake in a quick oven
