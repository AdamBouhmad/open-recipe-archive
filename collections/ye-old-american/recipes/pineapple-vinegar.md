## Ingredients

- sliced pineapples
- pure cider vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cover sliced pineapples with pure cider vinegar
2. Let them stand three or four days
3. Mash and strain through a cloth as long as it runs clear
4. To every three quarts of juice add five pounds of sugar
5. Boil it altogether about ten minutes
6. Skim carefully until nothing rises to the surface
7. Take from the fire and when cool, bottle it
