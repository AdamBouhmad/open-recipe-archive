## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons carrot, cut into cubes
- 2 tablespoons onion, cut into cubes
- 2 tablespoons celery, cut into cubes
- 2 sprigs parsley
- 2 sprigs thyme
- 1 sprig savory
- 2 cloves
- ½ teaspoon peppercorns
- 1 bay leaf
- ⅞ cup white wine
- 1 box gelatine
- 1 quart White Stock or Brown Stock
- juice 1 lemon
- 3 egg whites
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Put vegetables, seasonings, and wine (except two tablespoons) in a saucepan; cook eight minutes, and strain, reserving liquid.
2. Add gelatine to stock, then add lemon juice.
3. Heat to boiling-point and add strained liquid.
4. Season with salt and cayenne.
5. Beat whites of eggs slightly, add two tablespoons wine, and dilute with one cup hot mixture.
6. Add slowly to remaining mixture, stirring constantly until boiling-point is reached.
7. Place on back of range and let stand thirty minutes.
8. Strain through a double cheese-cloth placed over a fine wire strainer, or through a jelly bag.
