## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of lady's-fingers
- 1 quart of rich sweet cream
- ¾ cup powdered sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls vanilla or other extract

## Directions

1. Split and trim the cakes, and fit neatly in the bottom and sides of two quart moulds
2. Whip the cream to a stiff froth in a syllabub-churn when you have sweetened and flavored it
3. Fill the moulds, lay cakes closely together on the top, and set upon the ice until needed
