## Ingredients

- fresh rose leaves
- best white wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Fill a stone or china jar with fresh rose leaves put in loosely
2. Pour on them as much of the best white wine vinegar as the jar will hold
3. Cover it, and set it in the sun, or in some other warm place for three weeks
4. Strain it through a flannel bag, and bottle it for use
