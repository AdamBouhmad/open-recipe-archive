## Ingredients

- venison meat
- trimmings and bones
- water
- bits of butter
- flour
- gravy from the venison
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Cut the meat in nice small slices
2. Put the trimmings and bones into a saucepan with barely water enough to cover them
3. Let them stew for an hour
4. Strain the liquid into a stewpan
5. Add to it some bits of butter rolled in flour and whatever gravy was left of the venison the day before
6. Stir in some currant jelly and give it a boil up
7. Put in the meat and keep it over the fire just long enough to warm it through
8. Do not allow it to boil, as it has been once cooked already
