## Ingredients

- 1 quart ripe sweet berries
- 1 lb. sugar
- 1 quart fresh cream

## Directions

1. Scatter half the sugar over the berries and let them stand three hours
2. Press and mash them, and strain them through a thin muslin bag
3. Add the rest of the sugar, and when dissolved beat in the cream little by little
4. Freeze rapidly, opening the freezer (if it is not a patent one) several times to beat and stir
