## Ingredients

- three quarts of sifted flour
- nearly a quart of warm water
- a level tablespoonful of salt
- an ounce of compressed yeast
- butter for baking tins

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a pint of lukewarm water
2. Stir into it enough flour to make a thick batter
3. Cover the bowl containing the batter or sponge with a thick folded cloth and set it in a warm place to rise
4. When the sponge is foamy and light in half an hour, stir into this sponge the salt dissolved in a little warm water
5. Add the rest of the flour and sufficient warm water to make the dough stiff enough to knead
6. Knead it from five to ten minutes
7. Divide it into loaves, knead again each loaf and put them into buttered baking tins
8. Cover them with a double thick cloth and set again in a warm place to rise twice their height
9. Bake the same as any bread
