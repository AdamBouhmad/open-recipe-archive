## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1½ cups sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 2¼ cups flour
- ¼ teaspoon soda
- ¾ teaspoon cream of tartar
- Whites 5 eggs
- 2 squares Baker's chocolate, grated
- marshmallows
- Chocolate Fudge Frosting

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for Chocolate Cake II.
2. As soon as cake is removed from pan, cover bottom with marshmallows pulled apart with tips of fingers, but not quite separated into halves.
3. The exposed soft surface will quickly adhere to hot cake.
4. Pour over Chocolate Fudge Frosting.
