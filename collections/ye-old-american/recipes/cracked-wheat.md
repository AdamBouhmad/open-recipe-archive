## Ingredients

- wheat
- cold water
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Soak the wheat over night in cold water, about a quart of water to a cup of wheat
2. Cook it as directed for oatmeal
3. Should be thoroughly done
4. Eat with sugar and cream
