## Ingredients

- apple
- celery
- Mayonnaise Dressing
- canned pimentoes
- red or green apples
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Mix equal quantities of finely cut apple and celery, and moisten with Mayonnaise Dressing.
2. Garnish with curled celery and canned pimentoes cut in strips or fancy shapes.
3. An attractive way of serving this salad is to remove tops from red or green apples, scoop out inside pulp, leaving just enough adhering to skin to keep apples in shape.
4. Refill shells thus made with the salad, replace tops, and serve on lettuce leaves.
