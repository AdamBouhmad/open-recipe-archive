## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- yolks of two eggs
- six ounces of sugar
- one tablespoonful of cornstarch
- whites of two eggs
- one pint of cream
- vanilla or lemon flavoring

## Directions

1. Scald but do not boil the milk with yolks of two eggs, six ounces of sugar and one tablespoonful of cornstarch
2. Put the whites of the two eggs into a pint of cream and whip it
3. Mix the milk and cream
4. Flavor with one teaspoonful of vanilla or lemon
5. Freeze
