## Ingredients

- a dozen square or oblong sponge-cakes or Naples biscuits
- raspberry jam
- whites of six eggs
- six table-spoonfuls of raspberry or currant jelly
- cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Take a dozen of the square or oblong sponge-cakes that are commonly called Naples biscuits, they should be quite fresh
2. Spread over each a thick layer of raspberry jam
3. Place them in the bottom and round the sides of a glass bowl
4. Take the whites of six eggs and mix with them six table-spoonfuls of raspberry or currant jelly
5. Beat the egg and jelly with rods till very light
6. Fill up the bowl with it, using cream if you can conveniently procure it as it is better than white of egg
