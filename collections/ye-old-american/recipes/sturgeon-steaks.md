## Ingredients

- sturgeon
- flour
- melted butter
- chopped parsley
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Cut them as for the cutlets
2. dredge them
3. fry them nicely
4. dish them quickly lest they get cold
5. pour over melted butter with chopped parsley
6. garnish with fried parsley
