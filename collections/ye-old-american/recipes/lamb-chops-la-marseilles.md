## Ingredients

- six French chops
- Mushroom Sauce
- butter
- parsley
- paper frills

## Directions

1. Pan broil, on one side, six French chops.
2. Cover cooked side with Mushroom Sauce, place in a buttered baking-dish, and bake in a hot oven eight minutes.
3. Remove to serving dish, place a paper frill on each chop, and garnish with parsley.
