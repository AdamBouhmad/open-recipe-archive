## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- ½ cup milk
- 1¾ cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- Strawberries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Mix same as Cottage Pudding, and bake twenty-five minutes in shallow pan
2. Cut in squares and serve with strawberries (sprinkled with sugar and slightly mashed) and Cream Sauce I
3. Sliced peaches may be used in place of strawberries
