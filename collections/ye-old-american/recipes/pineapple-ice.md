## Ingredients

- 1 juicy ripe pineapple—peeled and cut small
- Juice and grated peel of 1 lemon
- 1 pint of sugar
- 1 pint water, or a little less

## Directions

1. Strew the sugar over the pineapple and let it stand an hour
2. Mash all up together, and strain out the syrup through a hair-sieve
3. Add the water and freeze
