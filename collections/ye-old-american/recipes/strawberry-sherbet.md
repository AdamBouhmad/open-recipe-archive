## Ingredients

- 1 quart of strawberries
- 3 pints of water
- 1 lemon—the juice only
- 1 tablespoonful orange-flower water
- ¾ lb. white sugar

## Directions

1. The strawberries should be fresh and ripe
2. Crush to a smooth paste
3. Add the rest of the ingredients (except the sugar), and let it stand three hours
4. Strain over the sugar, squeezing the cloth hard
5. Stir until the sugar is dissolved
6. Strain again and set in ice for two hours or more before you use it
