## Ingredients

- asparagus
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- milk or cream
- warm butter

## Directions

1. Boil a bunch of asparagus twenty minutes; cut off the tender tops and lay them in a deep-pie plate, buttering, salting and peppering well.
2. Beat up four eggs, the yolks and whites separately to a stiff froth; add two tablespoonfuls of milk or cream, a tablespoonful of warm butter, pepper and salt to taste.
3. Pour evenly over the asparagus mixture.
4. Bake eight minutes or until the eggs are set.
