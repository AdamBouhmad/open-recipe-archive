## Ingredients

- two tablespoonfuls of flour
- half a teacupful of butter
- one pint of boiling milk
- three heads of celery
- water

## Directions

1. Mix two tablespoonfuls of flour with half a teacupful of butter
2. Have ready a pint of boiling milk and stir the flour and butter into the milk
3. Take three heads of celery, cut into small bits, and boil for a few minutes in water
4. Strain off the water
5. Put the celery into the melted butter, and keep it stirred over the fire for five or ten minutes
