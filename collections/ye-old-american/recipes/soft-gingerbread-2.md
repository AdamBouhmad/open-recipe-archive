## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup sour or buttermilk
- 1 teaspoonful soda dissolved in boiling water
- 1 tablespoonful ginger
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- 2 eggs
- About five cups of flour

## Directions

1. Stir butter, sugar, molasses, and spice together to a light cream, set them on the range until slightly warm
2. Beat the eggs light
3. Add the milk to the warmed mixture, then the eggs, the soda, and lastly the flour
4. Beat very hard ten minutes, and bake at once in a loaf, or in small tins
