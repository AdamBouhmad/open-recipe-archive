## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup macaroni
- 2 quarts boiling water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 cup cream
- 1 1/2 cups Tomato Sauce I
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water twenty minutes or until soft
2. Drain in strainer
3. Pour over it cold water to prevent pieces from adhering
4. Add cream
5. Reheat in one and one-half cups of Tomato Sauce I
6. Sprinkle with grated cheese
7. Serve; or prepare as Baked Macaroni, using Tomato in place of White Sauce
