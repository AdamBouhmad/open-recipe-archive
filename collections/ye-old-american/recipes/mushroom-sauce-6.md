## Ingredients

- a pint of small button mushrooms
- veal gravy or milk or cream
- an onion
- pepper
- salt
- a little butter rolled in flour

## Directions

1. Wash a pint of small button mushrooms
2. Remove the stems and outside skins
3. Stew them slowly in veal gravy or milk or cream, adding an onion, and seasoning with pepper, salt and a little butter rolled in flour
4. Heighten their flavor by salting a few the night before, to extract the juice
5. If there is a poisonous one among them, the onion in the sauce will turn black and the whole should be thrown away
