## Ingredients

- ⅓ can salmon
- 1 quart scalded milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- Few grains pepper

## Directions

1. Drain oil from salmon, remove skin and bones, rub through a sieve.
2. Add gradually the milk, season, and bind.
