## Ingredients

- one-half a coffeecupful of molasses
- one-quarter of a cupful of butter
- one egg
- one-half a cupful of milk
- one-half a teaspoonful of pure soda
- one and one-half cupfuls of good Graham flour
- one small teacupful of raisins
- spices to taste

## Directions

1. Mix well together molasses, butter, egg, milk, soda, Graham flour, raisins, and spices to taste
2. Steam four hours
3. Serve with brandy or wine sauce, or any sauce that may be preferred
