## Ingredients

- three fresh eggs
- one quart of clear, cold rain-water
- half an ounce of sulphate of zinc (white vitrol)

## Directions

1. break the eggs into one quart of clear, cold rain-water
2. stir until thoroughly mixed
3. bring to a boil on a slow fire, stirring often
4. add half an ounce of sulphate of zinc (white vitrol)
5. continue the boiling for two minutes, then set it off the fire
6. Take the curd that settles at the bottom of this and apply to the eye at night with a bandage
7. Strain the liquid through a cloth and use for bathing the eyes occasionally
