## Ingredients

- paste
- apples, pared and cored

## Directions

1. Cut paste in squares
2. Put in the centre of each an apple, pared and cored
3. Bring the corners together
4. Enclose each dumpling in a small square cloth, tied up bag-wise, leaving room to swell
5. Dip each cloth in hot water, wring out and flour on the inside before the apple is put in
6. Boil one hour
