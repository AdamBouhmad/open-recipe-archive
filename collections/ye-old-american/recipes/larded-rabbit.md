## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit
- Slips of fat pork
- Strained gravy made of veal or beef
- Bunch of sweet herbs
- 1 minced onion
- Pepper
- Tablespoonful of butter
- Juice of a lemon
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and divide the body into joints.
2. Lard with slips of fat pork; put into a clean hot frying-pan and fry until half done.
3. Have ready some strained gravy made of veal or beef; put the pieces of rabbit into a saucepan, with a bunch of sweet herbs, a minced onion, and some pepper.
4. Stew, closely covered, half an hour, or until tender; take out the rabbits and lay in a hot covered dish.
5. Strain the gravy, add a tablespoonful of butter, the juice of a lemon, and thicken with flour.
6. Boil up and pour over the meat.
