## Ingredients

- new potatoes
- butter
- cream
- green parsley
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and rub new potatoes with a coarse cloth or scrubbing-brush.
2. Drop into boiling water and boil briskly until done, and no more.
3. Press a potato against the side of the kettle with a fork; if done, it will yield to a gentle pressure.
4. In a saucepan have ready some butter and cream, hot, but not boiling, a little green parsley, pepper and salt.
5. Drain the potatoes, add the mixture, put over hot water for a minute or two, and serve.
