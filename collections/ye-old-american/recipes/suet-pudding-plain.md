## Ingredients

- one cupful of chopped suet
- one cupful of milk
- two eggs beaten
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Combine chopped suet, milk, beaten eggs, salt and enough flour to make a stiff batter, but thin enough to pour from a spoon
2. Put into a bowl and cover with a cloth
3. Boil three hours
