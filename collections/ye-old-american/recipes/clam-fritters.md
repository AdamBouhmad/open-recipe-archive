## Ingredients

- 12 clams, minced fine
- 1 pint of milk
- 3 eggs
- clam liquor
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Add the liquor from the clams to the milk.
2. Beat up the eggs and put to this, with salt and pepper, and flour enough for thin batter.
3. Add the chopped clams lastly.
4. Fry in hot lard, trying a little first to see that fat and batter are right.
5. A tablespoonful will make a fritter of moderate size.
6. Alternatively, you can dip the whole clams in batter and cook in like manner.
7. Fry quickly, or they are apt to be too greasy.
