## Ingredients

- sirloin steak, cut one and one-half inches thick
- butter
- salt and pepper
- one pint oysters

## Directions

1. Wipe a sirloin steak, cut one and one-half inches thick, and broil five minutes.
2. Remove to platter.
3. Spread with butter and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Clean one pint oysters, cover steak with same.
5. Sprinkle oysters with salt and pepper and dot over with butter.
6. Place on grate in hot oven, and cook until oysters are plump.
