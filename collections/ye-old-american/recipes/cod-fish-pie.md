## Ingredients

- soaked fish
- stale bread grated
- new milk
- minced parsley
- nutmeg
- pepper
- made mustard
- melted butter
- good paste

## Directions

1. Soak the fish
2. Boil it and take off the skin
3. Pick the meat from the bones and mince it very fine
4. Take double the quantity of your fish of stale bread grated
5. Pour over it as much new milk, boiling hot, as will wet it completely
6. Add minced parsley, nutmeg, pepper, and made mustard
7. Add as much melted butter as will make it sufficiently rich
8. Beat these together very well
9. Add the minced fish and mix it all
10. Cover the bottom of the dish with good paste
11. Pour the fish in
12. Put on a lid and bake it
