## Ingredients

- calf's head
- sweetbreads
- tongue
- veal knuckle
- beef
- onions
- thyme
- parsley
- cloves
- mace
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- red wine
- mushroom catsup
- walnut catsup
- butter
- brown flour
- forcemeat balls
- green pickles

## Directions

1. Have the head nicely cleaned, divide the chop from the skull, take out the brains and tongue
2. And boil the other parts till tender
3. Take them out of the water and put into it a knuckle of veal or four pounds of lean beef, three onions chopped, thyme, parsley, a tea-spoonful of pounded cloves, the same of mace, salt, and cayenne pepper to your taste
4. Boil these things together till reduced to a pint
5. Strain it, and add two gills of red wine, one of mushroom and one of walnut catsup
6. Thicken it with butter and brown flour
7. The head must be cut in small pieces and stewed a few minutes in the gravy
8. Put a paste round the edge of a deep dish, three folds, one on the other, but none on the bottom
9. Pour in the meat and gravy, and bake it till the paste is done
10. Pick all strings from the brains, pound them, and add grated bread, pepper and salt
11. Make them in little cakes with the yelk of an egg
12. Fry them a nice brown
13. Boil six egg's hard, leave one whole and divide the others
