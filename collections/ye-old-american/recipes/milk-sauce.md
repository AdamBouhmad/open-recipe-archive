## Ingredients

- a tablespoonful of flour
- cold milk
- an ounce of butter
- a cupful of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- half a pint of milk
- one egg
- a dash of nutmeg, or any flavor

## Directions

1. Dissolve the flour in cold milk and see that it is free from lumps
2. Whisk the butter and sugar to a cream and add to it a pinch of salt
3. Mix together half a pint of milk, one egg and the flour
4. Stir this into the butter and add a dash of nutmeg, or any flavor
5. Heat until near the boiling point and serve
