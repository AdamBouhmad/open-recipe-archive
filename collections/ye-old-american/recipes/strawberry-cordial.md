## Ingredients

- ripe strawberries
- white brandy
- powdered loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Hull a sufficient quantity of ripe strawberries, and squeeze them through a linen bag.
2. To each quart of the juice allow a pint of white brandy, and half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar.
3. Put the liquid into a glass jar or a demijohn, and let it stand a fortnight.
4. Then filter it through a sieve, to the bottom of which a piece of fine muslin or blotting paper has been fastened; and afterwards bottle it.
