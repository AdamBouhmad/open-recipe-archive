## Ingredients

- two table-spoonfuls of extract of lead
- a bottle of rain or river water
- two table-spoonfuls of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix two table-spoonfuls of extract of lead with a bottle of rain or river water
2. add two table-spoonfuls of brandy, and shake it well
