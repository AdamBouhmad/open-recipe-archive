## Ingredients

- One teacupful of butter
- one teacupful of brown sugar
- two teacupfuls of cooking molasses
- one cupful of milk
- one teaspoonful of soda
- one tablespoonful of ginger
- one tablespoonful of cinnamon
- one teaspoonful of cloves
- a little grated nutmeg
- four eggs well beaten
- five cups of sifted flour
- one cup of raisins
- one cup of currants

## Directions

1. Work butter and brown sugar well together
2. add two teacupfuls of cooking molasses, one cupful of milk with a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in it
3. add one tablespoonful of ginger, one tablespoonful of cinnamon and one teaspoonful of cloves, a little grated nutmeg
4. add four eggs well beaten and five cups of sifted flour, or enough to make a stiff batter
5. Flour a cup of raisins and one of currants
6. add last
7. Bake in a very moderate oven one hour
