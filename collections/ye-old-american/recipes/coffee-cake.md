## Ingredients

- one cup of brown sugar
- one cup of butter
- two eggs
- one-half cup of molasses
- one cup of strong, cold coffee
- one teaspoonful of soda
- two teaspoonfuls of cinnamon
- one teaspoonful of cloves
- one cup of raisins or currants
- five cups of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Combine one cup of brown sugar, one cup of butter, two eggs, one-half cup of molasses, one cup of strong cold coffee, one teaspoonful of soda, two teaspoonfuls of cinnamon, one teaspoonful of cloves
2. Add one cup of raisins or currants last, rubbed in a little of the flour
3. Bake about one hour
