## Ingredients

- slices of raw beef
- water
- catsup
- a clove of garlic
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- brown flour

## Directions

1. Cut slices of raw beef
2. Put them in a stew pan with a little water, some catsup, a clove of garlic, pepper and salt
3. Stew them till done
4. Thicken the gravy with a lump of butter rubbed into brown flour
5. A hash may be made of any kind of meat that has been cooked
6. But it is not so good
7. And it is necessary to have a gravy prepared and seasoned
8. And keep the hash over the fire only a few minutes to make it hot
