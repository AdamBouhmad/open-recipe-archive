## Ingredients

- winter squash
- salt
- melted butter
- water
- pork chops

## Directions

1. cut the crooked neck in slices an inch thick
2. take off the rind
3. boil them with salt in the water
4. drain them well before they are dished
5. pour melted butter over
6. serve them up very hot
7. slice and pare the large part containing the seeds
8. cut it in small pieces
9. stew it till soft, with just water enough to cover it
10. pass it through a sieve and stew it again, adding some butter, pepper, and salt
