## Ingredients

- A calf's head
- Water to cover
- Pepper and salt
- A heaping tablespoonful of salt
- A teaspoonful of pepper
- A tablespoonful of finely chopped sweet herbs

## Directions

1. Boil a calf's head in water enough to cover it, until the meat leaves the bones
2. Take it with a skimmer into a wooden bowl or tray
3. Take from it every particle of bone
4. Chop it small
5. Season with pepper and salt, a heaping tablespoonful of salt and a teaspoonful of pepper
6. If liked, add a tablespoonful of finely chopped sweet herbs
7. Lay in a cloth in a colander, put the minced meat into it, then fold the cloth closely over it
8. Lay a plate over, and on it a gentle weight
9. When cold it may be sliced thin for supper or sandwiches
10. Spread each slice with made mustard
