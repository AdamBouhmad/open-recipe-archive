## Ingredients

- 2 cups lukewarm water
- 1 yeast cake
- ½ tablespoon salt
- ½ cup molasses
- 1 cup rye flour
- 1 cup granulated corn meal
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast cake in water, add remaining ingredients, and mix thoroughly
2. Let rise, shape, let rise again, and bake as Entire Wheat Bread
