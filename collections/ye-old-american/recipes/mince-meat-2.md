## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. raisins, seeded and chopped
- 1 lb. sultana raisins, washed and picked over
- 2 lbs. currants, washed and carefully picked over
- ¾ lb. citron, cut up fine
- 2 tablespoonfuls cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful powdered nutmeg
- 2 tablespoonfuls mace
- 1 tablespoonful cloves
- 1 tablespoonful allspice
- 1 tablespoonful fine salt
- 2½ lbs. brown sugar
- 1 quart brown Sherry
- 1 pint best brandy

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Keep in stone jars, tied over with double covers
3. Let the mixture stand at least twenty-four hours after it is made before it is used
4. Add a little more liquor if it should dry out when you make up a batch of pies
5. Lay strips of pastry, notched with a jagging-iron, in a cross-bar pattern upon the pie, instead of a top-crust
