## Ingredients

- beefsteak or chops
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take a smooth, thick-bottomed frying pan and scald it out with hot water
2. Wipe it dry and set it on the stove or range
3. When very hot, rub the bottom with a rag dipped in butter
4. Place your steak or chops in it
5. Turn often until cooked through
6. Take up on a warm platter
7. Season both sides with salt, pepper and butter
8. Serve hot
