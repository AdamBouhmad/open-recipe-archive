## Ingredients

- Puff paste
- Chicken Force-meat
- Egg
- Crumbs

## Directions

1. Roll puff paste very thin, and spread with Chicken Force-meat.
2. Roll like a jelly roll, and cut in pieces four inches long and a little larger round than a cigarette.
3. Brush over with egg, roll in crumbs, fry in deep fat, and drain on brown paper.
4. Arrange log-cabin fashion on a folded doily, and serve while hot.
