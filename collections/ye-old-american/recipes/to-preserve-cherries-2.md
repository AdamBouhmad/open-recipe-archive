## Ingredients

- large ripe morella cherries
- a pound of loaf-sugar to each pound of cherries

## Directions

1. Weigh the cherries, and to each pound allow a pound of loaf-sugar
2. Stone the cherries, opening them with a sharp quill, and save the juice that comes from them
3. Throw them into a large pan or tureen, and strew about half the sugar over them
4. Let them lie in it an hour or two after they are all stoned
5. Put them into a preserving kettle with the remainder of the sugar
6. Boil and skim them till the fruit is clear and the syrup thick
