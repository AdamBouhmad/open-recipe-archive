## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour
- 1/3 cup white stock
- 1/3 cup milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- Grated cheese
- 3/4 cup buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of the butter, flour, stock, and milk; add eggs finely chopped and salt and cayenne.
2. Fill buttered ramequin dishes with mixture, sprinkle with grated cheese, cover with cracker crumbs, and bake in a moderate oven until crumbs are brown.
