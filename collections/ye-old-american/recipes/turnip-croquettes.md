## Ingredients

- new French turnips
- salt
- pepper
- yolks of two eggs
- crumbs

## Directions

1. Wash, pare, and cut in quarters new French turnips.
2. Steam until tender, mash, pressing out all water that is possible.
3. This is best accomplished by wringing in cheese-cloth.
4. Season one and one-fourth cups with salt and pepper, then add yolks of two eggs slightly beaten.
5. Cool, shape in small croquettes, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again, fry in deep fat, and drain.
