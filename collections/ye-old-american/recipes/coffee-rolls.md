## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 1½ yeast cakes
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup lard
- ½ cup sugar
- flour
- 1 egg
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon salt
- melted butter
- confectioners' sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Scald milk, when lukewarm add yeast cakes, and as soon as dissolved add three and one-half cups flour
2. Beat thoroughly, cover, and let rise
3. Then add butter, lard, sugar, egg unbeaten, cinnamon, salt, and flour enough to knead
4. Knead until well mixed, cover, and let rise
5. Turn mixture on a floured cloth
6. Roll into a long, rectangular piece one-fourth inch thick
7. Brush over with melted butter, fold from ends toward centre to make three layers and cut off pieces three-fourths inch wide
8. Cover and let rise
9. Take each piece separately in hands and twist from ends in opposite directions, then shape in a coil
10. Place in buttered pans, cover, again let rise, and bake in a moderate oven twenty minutes
11. Cool slightly, and brush over with confectioners' sugar moistened with boiling water and flavored with vanilla
