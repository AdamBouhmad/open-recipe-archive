## Ingredients

- one quart of warm milk
- half a cup of yeast
- one teaspoonful of salt
- flour
- half a cupful of melted butter
- one teaspoonful of soda
- water

## Directions

1. Mix one quart of warm milk, half a cup of yeast, one teaspoonful of salt and flour enough to make a stiff batter
2. When light, add half a cupful of melted butter, a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little water and a very little more flour
3. Let it stand twenty minutes or until light
4. Grease some muffin-rings, place them on a hot griddle and fill them half full of the batter
5. When done on one side turn and bake the other side
6. Butter them while hot; pile one on another and serve immediately
