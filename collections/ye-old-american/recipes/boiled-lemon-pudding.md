## Ingredients

- half a cupful of chopped suet
- one pint of bread crumbs
- one lemon
- one cupful of sugar
- one cupful of flour
- one teaspoonful of salt
- two eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Mix the suet, bread crumbs, sugar and flour well together
2. Add the lemon peel, which should be the yellow grated from the outside, and the juice, which should be strained
3. When these ingredients are well mixed, moisten with the eggs and sufficient milk to make the pudding of the consistency of thick batter
4. Put it into a well-buttered mold and boil for three and a half hours
5. Turn it out, strew sifted sugar over and serve warm with the lemon sauce, or not, at pleasure
