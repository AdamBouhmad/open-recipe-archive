## Ingredients

- a pint of good, rich, boiled custard
- an ounce of sweet almonds, blanched, roasted and pounded to a paste
- half an ounce of pine-nuts or peanuts, blanched, roasted and pounded
- a small quantity of candied citron

## Directions

1. Cook the custard as usual
2. Add the blanched, roasted and pounded almonds
3. Add the blanched, roasted and pounded pine-nuts or peanuts
4. Add candied citron cut into the thinnest possible slips
5. Set it on the ice for some hours before using
