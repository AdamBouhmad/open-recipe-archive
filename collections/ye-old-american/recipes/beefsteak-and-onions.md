## Ingredients

- beefsteak
- a dozen onions
- beef drippings or butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the steak in the usual way
2. Have ready in a frying pan a dozen onions cut in slices and fried brown in a little beef drippings or butter
3. Dish your steak and lay the onions thickly over the top
4. Cover and let stand five minutes
5. Send to the table hot
