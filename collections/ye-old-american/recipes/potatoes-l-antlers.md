## Ingredients

- potatoes
- two tablespoons butter
- salt
- paprika
- cream

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes with jackets on, drain, and let stand twenty-four hours.
2. Peel, and cut in small cubes.
3. Put into a saucepan with two tablespoons butter to each two cups potatoes.
4. Sprinkle with salt, and generously with paprika.
5. Add cream to cover, and cook slowly, forty minutes.
