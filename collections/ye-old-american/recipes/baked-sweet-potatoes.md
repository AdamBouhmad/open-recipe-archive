## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape them, split them lengthwise
2. Steam or boil them until nearly done
3. Drain, and put them in a baking dish
4. Place over them lumps of butter, pepper and salt
5. Sprinkle thickly with sugar
6. Bake in the oven to a nice brown
