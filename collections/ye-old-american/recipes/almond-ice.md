## Ingredients

- two pints of milk
- eight ounces of cream
- two ounces of orange-flower water
- eight ounces of sweet almonds
- four ounces of bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Pound all in a marble mortar, pouring in from time to time a few drops of water
2. When thoroughly pounded add the orange-flower water and half of the milk
3. Pass this, tightly squeezed, through a cloth
4. Boil the rest of the milk with the cream and keep stirring it with a wooden spoon
5. As soon as it is thick enough, pour in the almond milk
6. Give it one boiling, take it off and let it cool in a bowl or pitcher before pouring it into the mold for freezing
