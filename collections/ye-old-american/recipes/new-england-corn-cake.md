## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- one pint of corn meal
- one teacupful of wheat flour
- a teaspoonful of salt
- two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- half cup of yeast
- two well-beaten eggs
- a half teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Scald the milk and gradually pour it on the meal
2. when cool add the butter and salt, also a half cup of yeast and let stand overnight
3. in the morning beat thoroughly and add two well-beaten eggs and a half teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a spoonful of water
4. Pour the mixture into buttered deep earthen plates
5. let it stand fifteen minutes to rise again
6. bake from twenty to thirty minutes
