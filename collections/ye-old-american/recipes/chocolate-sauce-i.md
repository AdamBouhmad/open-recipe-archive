## Ingredients

- 1½ cups water
- ½ cup sugar
- 6 tablespoons grated chocolate
- 1 tablespoon arrowroot
- ½ cup cold water
- Few grains salt
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil water and sugar five minutes
2. Mix chocolate with arrowroot to which water has been added
3. Combine mixtures and add salt
4. Boil three minutes
5. Flavor with vanilla and serve hot
