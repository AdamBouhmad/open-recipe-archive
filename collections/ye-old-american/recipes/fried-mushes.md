## Ingredients

- Mush left over from breakfast
- flour

## Directions

1. Pack mush in greased, one pound baking-powder box, and cover, which will prevent crust from forming.
2. The next morning remove from box, slice thinly, dip in flour, and sauté.
3. Serve with maple syrup.
