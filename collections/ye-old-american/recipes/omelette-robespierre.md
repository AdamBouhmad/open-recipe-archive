## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons hot water
- 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- 1½ tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Beat eggs slightly, and add remaining ingredients.
2. Put one and one-half tablespoons butter in a hot omelet pan.
3. Turn in mixture and cook same as French Omelet.
4. Fold, turn on a hot platter.
5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar, and score with a hot poker.
