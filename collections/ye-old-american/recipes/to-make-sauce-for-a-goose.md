## Ingredients

- apples
- water
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare, core and slice some apples
2. Put them in a sauce pan, with as much water as will keep them from burning
3. Set them over a very slow fire
4. Keep them closely covered till reduced to a pulp
5. Put in a lump of butter, and sugar to your taste
6. Beat them well
7. Send them to the table in a china bowl
