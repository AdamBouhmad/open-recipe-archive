## Ingredients

- large duck
- pint of beef-gravy or dripping of roast-beef
- boiled onions
- sage leaves
- mint leaves
- pepper
- salt
- quart of young green peas
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Half roast a large duck.
2. Cut it up, and put it into a stew-pan with a pint of beef-gravy, or dripping of roast-beef.
3. Have ready two boiled onions, half a handful of sage leaves, and two leaves of mint, all chopped very fine and seasoned with pepper and salt.
4. Lay these ingredients over the duck.
5. Stew it slowly for a quarter of an hour.
6. Then put in a quart of young green peas.
7. Cover it closely, and simmer it half an hour longer, till the peas are quite soft.
8. Then add a piece of butter rolled in flour; quicken the fire, and give it one boil.
9. Serve up all together.
