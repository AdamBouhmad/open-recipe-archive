## Ingredients

- one cupful of sugar
- boiling water
- cream of tartar
- oil of peppermint

## Directions

1. Crush one cupful of sugar fine, and just moisten with boiling water, then boil five minutes
2. Take from the fire and add cream of tartar the size of a pea
3. Mix well and add four or five drops of oil of peppermint
4. Beat briskly until the mixture whitens, then drop quickly upon white paper
5. Have the cream of tartar and oil of peppermint measured while the sugar is boiling
6. If it sugars before it is all dropped, add a little water and boil a minute or two
