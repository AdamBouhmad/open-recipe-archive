## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of beef, cut up small
- 2 quarts of water
- 1 cup of sago, soaked soft in a little lukewarm water
- yolks of three eggs
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Stew the beef until it falls to pieces.
2. Strain it out, salt the liquid and stir in the sago.
3. Simmer gently one hour, stirring often.
4. Add the beaten yolks: boil up once and serve.
5. Eat with dry toast.
