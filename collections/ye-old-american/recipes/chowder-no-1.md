## Ingredients

- One pound of salt pork
- Four pounds of cod or sea-bass
- Onions
- Parsley
- Summer savory
- Pepper, black or cayenne
- Boston, butter, or whole cream crackers
- Cold water
- Flour
- Butter
- Sliced lemon
- Pickles
- Stewed tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cut salt pork into strips and soak in hot water five minutes
2. Cover the bottom of a pot with a layer of pork
3. Cut cod or sea-bass into pieces two inches square and lay enough on the pork to cover it
4. Follow with a layer of chopped onions, parsley, summer savory, and pepper
5. Add a layer of split crackers soaked in warm water until moist but not ready to break
6. Lay a stratum of pork and repeat the order of onions, seasoning, crackers, and pork until materials are exhausted
7. Let the topmost layer be buttered crackers, well soaked
8. Put in enough cold water to cover all barely
9. Cover the pot and stew gently for an hour, watching that water does not sink too low
10. Replenish water from tea-kettle if upper layer becomes exposed
11. Take out with a perforated skimmer and put into a tureen
12. Thicken the gravy with a tablespoonful of flour and about the same quantity of butter
13. Boil up and pour over the chowder
14. Send sliced lemon, pickles, and stewed tomatoes to the table
