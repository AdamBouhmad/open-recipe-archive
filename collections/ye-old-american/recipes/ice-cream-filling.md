## Ingredients

- three cups of sugar
- one cup of water
- whites of three eggs
- lemon or vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water to a thick, clear syrup, or until it begins to be brittle
2. Pour this, boiling hot, over the well-beaten whites of three eggs
3. Stir the mixture very briskly, and pour the sugar in slowly
4. Beat it, when all in, until cool
5. Flavor with lemon or vanilla extract
6. Spread between any white cake layers
