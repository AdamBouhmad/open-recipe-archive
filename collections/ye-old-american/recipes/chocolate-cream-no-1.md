## Ingredients

- Three ounces of grated chocolate
- one-quarter pound of sugar
- one and one-half pints of cream
- one and one-half ounces of clarified isinglass or gelatine
- the yolks of six eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs well
2. Put them into a basin with the grated chocolate, the sugar and one pint of the cream
3. Stir these ingredients well together
4. Pour them into a basin and set this basin in a saucepan of boiling water
5. Stir it one way until the mixture thickens, but do not allow it to boil, or it will curdle
6. Strain the cream through a sieve into a basin
7. Stir in the isinglass and the other one-half pint of cream, which should be well whipped
8. Mix all well together
9. Pour it into a mold which has been previously oiled with the purest salad oil
10. If at hand, set it in ice until wanted for table
