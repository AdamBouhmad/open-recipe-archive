## Ingredients

- three teacupfuls of sugar
- one cupful of butter
- five eggs
- a level teaspoonful of soda
- a cup of sweet milk
- four full cups of sifted flour
- the grated peel and juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Mix together three teacupfuls of sugar and one cupful of butter.
2. Add five eggs.
3. Dissolve a level teaspoonful of soda in a cup of sweet milk and add.
4. Add four full cups of sifted flour.
5. Add the grated peel and juice of a lemon, the juice to be added the very last.
6. Bake in two shallow tins.
7. When cold ice with lemon icing and cut into squares.
