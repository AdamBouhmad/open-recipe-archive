## Ingredients

- almonds
- hickory nuts
- butternuts
- English walnuts
- French Cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Chop almonds, hickory nuts, butternuts or English walnuts quite fine
2. Make the French Cream, and before adding all the sugar, while the cream is quite soft, stir into it the nuts
3. Form into balls, bars or squares
4. Several kinds of nuts may be mixed together
