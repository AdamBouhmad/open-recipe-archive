## Ingredients

- half a pound of sago
- a quart of milk
- a stick of cinnamon
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of sugar
- a gill of wine
- six eggs
- four ounces of currants
- paste

## Directions

1. Wash half a pound of sago in several waters
2. Put it on to boil in a quart of milk, with a stick of cinnamon
3. Stir it very frequently, for it is apt to burn
4. When it becomes quite thick, take out the cinnamon
5. Stir in half a pound of butter, and an equal quantity of sugar, with a gill of wine
6. When cold, add six eggs and four ounces of currants that have been plumped in hot water
7. Bake it in a paste
