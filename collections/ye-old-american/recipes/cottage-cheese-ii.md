## Ingredients

- one quart sweet milk
- one junket tablet
- cheese-cloth

## Directions

1. Heat one quart sweet milk to 100° F., and add one junket tablet reduced to a powder
2. Let stand in warm place until set
3. Beat with a fork to break curd, turn into a bag made of cheese-cloth, and let hang until whey has drained from curd
4. Proceed as with Cottage Cheese I
