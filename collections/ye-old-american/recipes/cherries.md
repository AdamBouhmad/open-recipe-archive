## Ingredients

- carnation or common light red cherries
- double refined sugar

## Directions

1. Select the finest that are not too ripe, with short stems
2. Take an equal weight with the cherries of double refined sugar, make it into a syrup
3. Preserve them without stoning, and with the stems on
4. If done carefully, and the directions for preserving closely attended to, the stems will not come off, and they will be so transparent that the stones may be seen
