## Ingredients

- 6 quail breasts
- very rich brown stock
- corn meal or hominy mush
- parsley
- mashed seasoned sweet potato
- creamed mushrooms
- sweetbread
- toasted bread points
- white of egg
- parsley
- 1 1/2 cups rich brown sauce
- tomato catsup
- mashed sweet potato

## Directions

1. Remove breasts from six quail, lard, and bake in a hot oven twenty minutes
2. Baste every five minutes with very rich brown stock, that breasts may have a glazed appearance
3. Mould corn meal or hominy mush in cone shape
4. When firm remove from mould and sprinkle with finely chopped parsley
5. Arrange breasts on cone around base
6. Make six nests of mashed seasoned sweet potato around base of cone at equal distances, using a pastry bag and rose tube
7. Fill nests with creamed mushrooms and sweetbread
8. Garnish between nests with toasted bread points, the tips of which have been brushed with white of egg, then dipped in finely chopped parsley
9. Insert a stab frill in each nest and one in top of cone
10. Serve with one and one-half cups rich brown sauce seasoned with tomato catsup and mashed sweet potato
