## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 1 quart of cream
- Yolks of 5 eggs—beaten light with the sugar
- 3 cups of sugar
- 1 lemon—juice and grated peel
- 1 glass of pale Sherry
- ½ lb. crystallized fruits, chopped

## Directions

1. Heat the milk almost to boiling; pour by degrees over the eggs and sugar, beating all together well
2. Return to the fire, and boil ten minutes, or until set into a good custard
3. When cold, beat in the cream, and half freeze before you stir in half a pound of crystallized fruit—peaches, apricots, cherries, limes, etc., chopped very fine
4. Beat in with these the lemon and wine
5. Cover again, and freeze hard
