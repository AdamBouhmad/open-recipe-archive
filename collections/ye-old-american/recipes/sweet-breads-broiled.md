## Ingredients

- sweet-breads
- butter

## Directions

1. Parboil.
2. Rub them well with butter.
3. Broil on a clean gridiron.
4. Turn frequently, and now and then roll over in a plate containing some hot melted butter.
5. This will prevent them from getting too dry and hard.
