## Ingredients

- ¾ lb. butter
- 1 lb. sugar
- 1 lb. flour
- 6 eggs
- 2 cups sour cream or milk
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful powdered cinnamon
- ¼ lb. citron
- 1 tablespoonful rose-water
- 1 teaspoonful soda dissolved in hot water

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. Put with them the yolks whipped light
3. Add the cream and spice
4. Next add the flour
5. Stir in the rose-water
6. Add a double-handful of citron cut in slips and dredged
7. Finally stir in the beaten whites of the eggs
8. Stir all well
9. Bake in a loaf or in a card, using a square shallow baking-pan
