## Ingredients

- half a pound of shelled sweet almonds
- a quarter of a pound of bitter almonds
- a jill of rose or orange-flower water
- half a pound of clear strained honey
- orange-flower or rose-water

## Directions

1. Blanch half a pound of shelled sweet almonds, and a quarter of a pound of bitter ones, and beat them in a mortar to a smooth paste--adding by degrees a jill of rose or orange-flower water.
2. Beat in, gradually, half a pound of clear strained honey.
3. When the whole is well incorporated, put it into gallipots, pouring on the top of each some orange-flower or rose-water.
4. Keep it closely covered.
