## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- half a pound of pounded sugar
- half a pound of fresh butter melted
- half a nutmeg grated
- puff paste
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat eight eggs very light
2. Add half a pound of pounded sugar, the same of fresh butter melted, and half a nutmeg grated
3. Sit it on a stove, and keep stirring till it is as thick as buttered eggs
4. Put a puff paste in a shallow dish, pour in the ingredients, and bake it half an hour in a moderate oven
5. Sift sugar over it, and serve it up hot
