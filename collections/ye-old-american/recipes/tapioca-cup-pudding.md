## Ingredients

- An even tablespoonful of tapioca
- Nearly a cup of new milk
- Yolk of a fresh egg
- A little sugar
- A grain of salt
- A little jelly

## Directions

1. Soak an even tablespoonful of tapioca for two hours in nearly a cup of new milk
2. Stir into this the yolk of a fresh egg
3. Add a little sugar and a grain of salt
4. Bake it in a cup for fifteen minutes
5. A little jelly may be eaten with it
