## Ingredients

- corn, boiled
- six eggs
- one quart milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Having boiled the corn, grate it, as if for a pudding.
2. Beat six eggs very light, and stir them gradually into a quart of milk.
3. Then stir in, by degrees, the grated corn, till you have a moderately thick batter.
4. Add a salt-spoon of salt.
5. Butter the inside of your muffin-rings.
6. Place them on a hot griddle, over a clear fire, and nearly fill them with the batter.
7. Bake the muffins well, and send them to table hot.
8. Eat them with butter.
