## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons boiling water or cream
- Confectioners' sugar
- Flavoring

## Directions

1. To liquid add enough sifted sugar to make of right consistency to spread.
2. Add flavoring.
3. Fresh fruit juice may be used in place of boiling water.
