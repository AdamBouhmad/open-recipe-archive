## Ingredients

- cabbages
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Pick cabbages very clean, and wash them thoroughly
2. Look them carefully over again
3. Quarter them if they are very large
4. Put them into a sauce pan with plenty of boiling water
5. If any scum rises, take it off
6. Put a large spoonful of salt into the sauce pan
7. Boil them till the stalks feel tender
8. A young cabbage will take about twenty minutes, or half an hour; when full grown, nearly an hour
9. See that they are well covered with water all the time
10. For strong old cabbages, boil them in two waters: when they are half done, take them out, and put them into another sauce pan of boiling water
