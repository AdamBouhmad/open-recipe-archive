## Ingredients

- ½ cup whole rice, well-washed and soaked two hours in a little warm water
- 3 pints cold water
- 1 small pinch of salt
- Loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Add the soaked rice with the water to the kettle containing the cold water with salt
2. Simmer the rice half an hour
3. Boil it until it is a smooth paste, and the water is reduced one-half
4. Strain through double tarlatan
5. Sweeten and give to the child
