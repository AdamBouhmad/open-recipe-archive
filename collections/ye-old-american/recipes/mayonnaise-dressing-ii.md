## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon powdered sugar
- Few grains cayenne
- Yolks 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- 1½ cups olive oil
- Mashed yolk of a hard-boiled egg

## Directions

1. Use same ingredients as for Mayonnaise Dressing I, adding mashed yolk of a hard-boiled egg to dry ingredients.
