## Ingredients

- hard boiled eggs
- cold chicken, lamb, veal or any tender roasted meat
- bread soaked in milk
- parsley, onion, celery
- grated cheese
- olive oil
- drawn butter
- beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- boiling lard

## Directions

1. Boil hard several eggs
2. Halve them lengthwise
3. Remove the yolks and chop them fine with cold chicken, lamb, veal or any tender roasted meat; or with bread soaked in milk and any salad, as parsley, onion, celery, the bread being half of the whole; or with grated cheese, a little olive oil, drawn butter, flavored
4. Fill the cavity in the egg with either of these mixtures, or any similar preparation
5. Press the halves together
6. Roll twice in beaten egg and bread crumbs
7. Dip into boiling lard
8. When the color rises delicately, drain them and they are ready for use
