## Ingredients

- flour
- forcemeat
- bacon
- onion
- celery
- red wine
- mushroom catsup
- curry powder

## Directions

1. Take the flank off, but leave all the fat, cut out the bone
2. Stuff the place with a rich forcemeat
3. Lard the top and sides with bacon
4. Put it in a pan with a pint of water, some chopped onion and cellery cut small
5. A gill of red wine, one of mushroom catsup and a tea-spoonful of curry powder
6. Bake it and serve it up with the gravy
7. Garnish with forcemeat balls fried
