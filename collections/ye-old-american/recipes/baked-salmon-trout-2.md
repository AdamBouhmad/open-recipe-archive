## Ingredients

- salmon trout
- butter
- water
- cream
- flour
- parsley

## Directions

1. Bake precisely as shad or white fish
2. Bake slowly, basting often with butter and water
3. When done have ready in a saucepan a cup of cream, diluted with a few spoonfuls of hot water, for fear it might clot in heating
4. Stir in two tablespoonfuls of melted butter, a scant tablespoonful of flour, and a little chopped parsley
5. Heat this in a vessel set within another of boiling water
6. Add the gravy from the dripping-pan, boil up once to thicken
7. When the trout is laid on a suitable hot dish, pour this sauce around it
8. Garnish with sprigs of parsley
