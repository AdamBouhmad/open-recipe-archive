## Ingredients

- 8 small slices stale bread
- Lemon mixture
- 1 cup milk
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 eggs
- Grated rind 1 lemon
- ⅛ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Spread bread with lemon mixture, and arrange in buttered pudding mould
2. Beat eggs slightly, add sugar, salt, and milk
3. Strain, add lemon rind, and pour mixture over bread
4. Cover, set in pan of hot water, and bake one hour
