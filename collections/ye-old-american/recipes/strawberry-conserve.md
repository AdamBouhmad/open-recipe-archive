## Ingredients

- fruit
- half a pound of loaf sugar to one pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Prepare the fruit as for preserving
2. Sprinkle the sugar over the fruit at night
3. In the morning, put it on the fire in a kettle and boil until the berries are clear
4. Spread on dishes and put in the sun until dry
5. Roll the fruit in sugar and pack in jars
