## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 quart of rich milk or thin cream
- 1/2 pound of sugar
- 1/2 pint of rum or brandy
- 1 grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat separately the yolks and whites of six eggs
2. Stir the yolks into a quart of rich milk, or thin cream, and add half a pound of sugar
3. Mix in half a pint of rum or brandy
4. Flavour it with a grated nutmeg
5. Stir in gently the beaten whites of three eggs
6. Mix it in a china bowl
