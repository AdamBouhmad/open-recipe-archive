## Ingredients

- one quart of rich cream
- loaf-sugar
- currant jelly
- sliced almond-sponge cake or lady cake
- raspberry jam or marmalade
- juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Take a quart of rich cream and divide it in half
2. Sweeten one pint of it with loaf-sugar and stir into it sufficient currant jelly to colour it of a fine pink
3. Put it into a glass bowl
4. Place in the centre a pile of sliced almond-sponge cake or of lady cake, every slice spread thickly with raspberry jam or marmalade and laid evenly one on another
5. Have ready the other pint of cream, flavoured with the juice of two lemons
6. Beat it with rods to a stiff froth and heap it all over the pile of cake so as entirely to cover it
