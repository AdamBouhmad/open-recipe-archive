## Ingredients

- 4 eggs (the whites only), whipped stiff, with 1 lb. powdered sugar
- Lemon or vanilla flavoring
- 1 teaspoonful arrowroot

## Directions

1. When very stiff, heap in the shape of half an egg upon stiff letter-paper lining the bottom of your baking-pan
2. Have them half an inch apart
3. Do not shut the oven-door closely, but leave a space through which you can watch them
4. When they are a light yellow-brown, take them out and cool quickly
5. Slip a thin-bladed knife under each; scoop out the soft inside, and fill with cream whipped as for Charlotte Russe
