## Ingredients

- Two-thirds cup rice
- One cup boiling water
- One and one-third cups hot milk
- One teaspoon salt
- One-fourth cup sugar
- Cooked peaches
- Macaroon dust
- Candied cherries
- Angelica
- Fruit red

## Directions

1. Wash rice, add boiling water, and steam until rice has absorbed water.
2. Then add hot milk, salt, and sugar.
3. Cook until rice is soft.
4. Turn into a slightly buttered round shallow mould.
5. When shaped, remove from mould to serving dish, and arrange on top sections of cooked peaches drained from their syrup and dipped in macaroon dust.
6. Garnish between sections with candied cherries and angelica cut in leaf-shapes.
7. Angelica may be softened by dipping in hot water.
8. Color peach syrup with fruit red, and pour around mould.
