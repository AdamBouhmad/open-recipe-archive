## Ingredients

- A quart of sound, ripe peaches
- Sugar
- The whites of three eggs
- The yolks of the eggs
- A pint of milk

## Directions

1. Pare and quarter the peaches, removing stones
2. Place them all in a dish that it will not injure to set in the oven and yet be suitable to place on the table
3. Sprinkle the peaches with sugar
4. Cover them well with the beaten whites of three eggs
5. Stand the dish in the oven until the eggs have become a delicate brown
6. Remove, and when cool enough, set the dish on ice, or in a very cool place
7. Take the yolks of the eggs and add to them a pint of milk
8. Sweeten and flavor
9. Boil same in a custard kettle, being careful to keep the eggs from curdling
10. When cool pour into a glass pitcher and serve with the meringue when ready to use
