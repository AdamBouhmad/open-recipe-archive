## Ingredients

- ¼ lb. suet
- ½ lb. figs (finely chopped)
- 1 large sour apple (cored, pared, and chopped)
- ¼ lb. brown sugar
- ¼ lb. bread crumbs
- ¼ cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 3 oz. flour

## Directions

1. Cream the suet, and add figs, apple, and sugar
2. Pour milk over bread crumbs, and add yolks of eggs, well beaten
3. Combine mixtures, add flour and whites of eggs beaten until stiff
4. Turn into buttered pudding mould, and steam four hours
5. Serve with Lemon Sauce III
