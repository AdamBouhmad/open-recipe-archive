## Ingredients

- Puff or plain paste
- White of egg
- Shredded cocoanut

## Directions

1. Roll puff or plain paste to one-fourth inch in thickness.
2. Shape with a lady-finger cutter and bake on a tin sheet in a hot oven.
3. When nearly done remove from oven, cool slightly, brush over with beaten white of egg, sprinkle with shredded cocoanut, and return to oven to finish the cooking.
