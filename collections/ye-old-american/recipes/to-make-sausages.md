## Ingredients

- tender pieces of fresh pork
- leaf fat
- pepper
- salt
- dried sage
- skins

## Directions

1. Take the tender pieces of fresh pork, chop them exceedingly fine
2. Chop some of the leaf fat, and put them together in the proportion of three pounds of pork to one of fat
3. Season it very high with pepper and salt
4. Add a small quantity of dried sage rubbed to a powder
5. Have the skins nicely prepared, fill them and hang them in a dry place
6. Sausages are excellent made into cakes and fried, but will not keep so well as in skins
