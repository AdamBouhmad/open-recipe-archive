## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- water
- acid

## Directions

1. Cut off stem close to leaves, remove outside bottom leaves, trim artichoke, cut off one inch from top of leaves, and with a sharp knife remove choke
2. Tie artichoke with a string to keep its shape
3. Soak one-half hour in cold water
4. Drain, and cook thirty to forty-five minutes in boiling, salted, acidulated water
5. Remove from water, place upside down to drain, then take off string
6. Serve with Béchamel or Hollandaise Sauce
