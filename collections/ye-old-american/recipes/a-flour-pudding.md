## Ingredients

- seven eggs
- one quarter of a pound of sugar
- a tea spoon of salt
- one quart milk
- 5 spoons of flour
- cinnamon and nutmeg to your taste

## Directions

1. beat and put to one quart milk
2. 5 spoons of flour
3. cinnamon and nutmeg to your taste
4. bake half an hour
5. and serve up with sweet sauce
