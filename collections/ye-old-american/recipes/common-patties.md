## Ingredients

- veal
- slices of boiled ham
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- parsley
- thyme
- gravy

## Directions

1. Take some veal, fat and lean, and some slices of boiled ham
2. Chop them very fine
3. Season it with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, and a small quantity of parsley and thyme minced very fine
4. With a little gravy make some paste
5. Cover the bottoms of small moulds
6. Fill them with the meat
7. Put thin lids on
8. Bake them crisp
