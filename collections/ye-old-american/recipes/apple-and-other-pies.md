## Ingredients

- fine juicy acid apples
- paste
- brown sugar
- lemon rind
- lemon juice or essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Take fine juicy acid apples; pare, core, and cut them into small pieces.
2. Have ready a deep dish that has been lined with paste.
3. Fill it with the apples; strewing among them layers of brown sugar, and adding the rind of a lemon pared thin, and also the juice squeezed in, or some essence of lemon.
4. Put on another sheet of paste as a lid; close the edges well, and notch them.
5. Bake the pie in a moderate oven, about three quarters of an hour.
6. Eat it with cream and sugar, or with cold boiled custard.
