## Ingredients

- Yolks 3 eggs
- 1 egg
- ½ cup Consommé
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- Slight grating nutmeg
- Few grains cayenne

## Directions

1. Beat eggs slightly, add Consommé and seasonings.
2. Pour into a small buttered tin mould, place in pan of hot water, and bake until firm.
3. Cool, remove from mould, and cut in fancy shapes.
