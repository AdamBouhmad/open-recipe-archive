## Ingredients

- largest strawberries
- loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Get the largest strawberries before they are too ripe
2. Have the best loaf sugar, one pound to each of strawberries
3. Stew them very gently, taking them out to cool frequently, that they may not be mashed
4. When they look clear, they are done enough
