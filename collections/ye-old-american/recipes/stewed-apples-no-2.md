## Ingredients

- firm round greenings apples
- boiling water
- a cup of sugar to every six apples
- lemon

## Directions

1. Select firm round greenings, pare neatly and cut in halves
2. Place in a shallow stewpan with sufficient boiling water to cover them and a cup of sugar to every six apples
3. Each half should cook on the bottom of the pan and be removed from the others so as not to injure its shape
4. Stew slowly until the pieces are very tender
5. Remove to a glass dish carefully
6. Boil the syrup a half hour longer, pour it over the apples and eat cold
7. A few pieces of lemon boiled in the syrup add to the flavor
