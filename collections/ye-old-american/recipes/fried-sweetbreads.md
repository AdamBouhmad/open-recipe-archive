## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- hot fat
- butter
- egg
- cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the sweetbreads by soaking for half an hour in tepid water.
2. Throw into hot water to whiten and harden.
3. Draw off the outer casing, remove the little pipes, and cut into thin slices.
4. Put them into hot fat and butter, and fry the same as lamb chops, first rolling them in egg and cracker crumbs.
