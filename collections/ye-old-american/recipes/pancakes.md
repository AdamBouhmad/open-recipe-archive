## Ingredients

- 1 pint of flour
- 6 eggs
- 1 saltspoonful salt, and same of soda dissolved in vinegar
- Milk to make a thin batter, beginning with two cups

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks light, add the salt, soda, and two cups of milk, then the flour and beaten whites alternately, and thin with more milk.
