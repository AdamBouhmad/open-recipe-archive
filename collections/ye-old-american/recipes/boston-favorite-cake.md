## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 3½ cups flour
- 5 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, eggs beaten until light, then milk and flour mixed and sifted with baking powder.
2. This recipe makes two loaves, or one-half the mixture may be baked in individual tins.
