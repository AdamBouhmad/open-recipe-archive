## Ingredients

- six fresh lemons
- three fresh lemons
- sixteen eggs
- sixteen table-spoonsful of powdered loaf sugar
- sixteen table-spoonsful of melted butter
- four crackers or rice flour or six ounces of corn meal
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Grate the rind from six fresh lemons
2. Squeeze the juice from three, and strain it
3. Beat the yelks of sixteen eggs very light
4. Put to them sixteen table-spoonsful of powdered loaf sugar, not heaped up
5. Add the same of melted butter
6. Add the grated rind, and the juice
7. Add four crackers finely pounded, or an equal quantity of rice flour
8. Or for change, six ounces of corn meal which is excellent
9. Beat it till light
10. Put a puff paste in your dish, pour the pudding in
11. Bake it in a moderate oven--it must not be very brown
