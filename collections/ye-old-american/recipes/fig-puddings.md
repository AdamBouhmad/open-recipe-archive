## Ingredients

- half a pound of good dried figs
- two cupfuls of fine, dry bread crumbs
- three eggs
- half a cupful of beef suet, powdered
- two scant cupfuls of sweet milk
- half a cupful of white sugar
- a little salt
- half a teaspoonful of baking powder
- half a cupful of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Wash, wipe and mince the figs
2. Soak the crumbs in milk, add the eggs, beaten light, with sugar, salt, suet, flour and figs
3. Beat three minutes
4. Put in buttered molds with tight top, set in boiling water with weight on cover to prevent mold from upsetting
5. Boil three hours
6. Eat hot with hard sauce or butter, powdered sugar, one teaspoonful of extract of nutmeg
