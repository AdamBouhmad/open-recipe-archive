## Ingredients

- grapes
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Squeeze the pulp from the skin
2. Boil the pulp in one kettle until the seeds begin to loosen
3. Boil the skins in a little water hard in another kettle
4. When the pulp seems tender, put it through the sieve
5. Add the skins, if tender, with the water they boil in, if not too much
6. Use a large coffeecupful of sugar for a quart can
7. Boil until thick and can in the usual way
