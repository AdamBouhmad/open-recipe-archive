## Ingredients

- paper (especially for fat parts)
- a clear good fire
- salt and water for basting
- green-parsley
- sliced lemon

## Directions

1. As it is more tender than beef or mutton, and easily scorched, paper it, especially the fat parts.
2. Lay it some distance from the fire a while to heat gently.
3. Baste it well.
4. A 15 pound piece requires one hour and a quarter roasting.
5. Garnish with green-parsley and sliced lemon.
