## Ingredients

- potatoes
- cold water
- deep fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and pare potatoes.
2. Shape in balls, using a French vegetable cutter.
3. Soak fifteen minutes in cold water; take from water and dry between towels.
4. Fry in deep fat, drain, and sprinkle with salt.
