## Ingredients

- six eggs
- pepper
- salt
- two ounces of fresh butter
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- sprigs of curled parsley

## Directions

1. Leave out the whites of two eggs.
2. Beat the eggs very light, and strain them through a sieve.
3. Add pepper and salt to your taste.
4. Divide two ounces of fresh butter into little bits, and put it into the egg.
5. Have ready a quarter of a pound of butter in a frying-pan, or a flat stew-pan.
6. Place it on hot coals, and have the butter boiling when you put in the beaten egg.
7. Fry it gently till of a light brown on the under side.
8. Do not turn it while cooking as it will do better without.
9. You may brown the top by holding a hot shovel over it.
10. When done, lay it in the dish, double it in half, and stick sprigs of curled parsley over it.
