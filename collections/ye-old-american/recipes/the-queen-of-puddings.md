## Ingredients

- 1½ cup white sugar
- 2 cups fine dry bread-crumbs
- 5 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- Vanilla, rose-water, or lemon seasoning
- 1 quart fresh rich milk
- one half cup jelly or jam

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into a cup of sugar; beat the yolks very light, and stir these together to a cream.
2. Add the bread-crumbs, soaked in milk, then the seasoning.
3. Bake this in a buttered pudding-dish—a large one and but two-thirds full—until the custard is "set."
4. Draw to the mouth of the oven, spread over with jam or other nice fruit-conserve.
5. Cover this with a méringue made of the whipped whites and half a cup of sugar.
6. Shut the oven and bake until the méringue begins to color.
7. Eat cold, with cream.
