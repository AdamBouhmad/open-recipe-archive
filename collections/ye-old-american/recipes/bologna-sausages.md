## Ingredients

- one pound of bacon (fat and lean)
- one pound of veal
- one pound of pork
- one pound of suet
- salt and pepper for seasoning
- sausage skins

## Directions

1. Chop all fine
2. season highly
3. fill the skins
4. prick and boil them an hour
5. hang them to dry
6. clean the skins with salt and vinegar
