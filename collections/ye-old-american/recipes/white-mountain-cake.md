## Ingredients

- 3 cups sugar
- 1 cup butter
- ½ cup sweet milk
- Whites of ten eggs
- ½ teaspoonful soda
- 1 teaspoonful cream tartar
- 4 cups flour
- essence of bitter almond
- whites of three eggs
- 1 lb. powdered sugar
- lemon-juice
- grated cocoanut
- a quarter of its weight of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Sift soda and cream tartar with the flour. Flavor with essence of bitter almond. Bake in jelly-cake tins.
2. Make icing with whites of three eggs and 1 lb. powdered sugar. Flavor with lemon-juice.
3. Fill with grated cocoanut, sweetened with a quarter of its weight of powdered sugar, or with icing such as is made for Lee cake, only flavored with lemon entirely.
