## Ingredients

- A chicken
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook a chicken in enough water to little more than cover it
2. Let it stew gently until the meat drops from the bones and the broth is reduced to about a pint
3. Season it to taste with a little salt and pepper
4. Strain and press, first through a colander, then through a coarse cloth
5. Set it over the fire again and cook a few minutes longer
6. Turn it into an earthen vegetable dish to harden
7. Set it on the ice in the refrigerator
8. Eat cold in slices or made into sandwiches with thin slices of bread lightly spread with butter
