## Ingredients

- one and one-half cupfuls of white sugar
- two cupfuls of fine, dry bread crumbs
- five eggs
- one tablespoonful of butter
- vanilla, rose-water or lemon flavoring
- one quart of fresh rich milk
- half a cupful of jelly or jam
- cream

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into a cupful of sugar
2. Beat the yolks very light, and stir these together to a cream
3. Add the bread crumbs soaked in milk, then the flavoring
4. Bake in a buttered pudding-dish--a large one and but two-thirds full--until the custard is "set"
5. Draw to the mouth of the oven, spread over with jam or other nice fruit conserve
6. Cover this with a meringue made of the whipped whites and half a cupful of sugar
7. Shut the oven and bake until the meringue begins to color
8. Eat cold with cream
