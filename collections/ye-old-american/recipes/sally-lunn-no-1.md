## Ingredients

- 1 quart of flour
- 4 eggs
- ½ cup melted butter
- 1 cup warm milk
- 1 cup warm water
- 4 tablespoonfuls yeast
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- ½ teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs to a stiff froth
2. Add the milk, water, butter, soda, and salt
3. Stir in the flour to a smooth batter and beat the yeast in well
4. Set to rise in a buttered pudding-dish, in which it must be baked and sent to table, or if you wish to turn it out, set to rise in a well-buttered mould
5. It will not be light under six hours
6. Bake steadily three-quarters of an hour, or until a straw thrust into it comes up clean
7. Eat while hot
