## Ingredients

- Three eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of melted lard or butter
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. Mix very hard with sifted flour, as hard as can be rolled
2. Roll very thin like pie crust
3. Cut in squares three inches long and two wide
4. Cut several slits or lines lengthwise to within a quarter of an inch of the edges of the ends
5. Run your two forefingers through every other slit
6. Lay them down on the board edgewise and dent them
7. Fry in hot lard a light brown
