## Ingredients

- rich old cheese
- eggs
- butter
- milk
- chopped parsley
- thyme

## Directions

1. Grate some rich old cheese
2. Having mixed the omelette as usual, stir in the cheese with a swift turn or two of the whisk, and at the same time some chopped parsley and thyme
3. Cook at once
4. Alternatively, make the omelette in the usual way, grate cheese upon it and fold it over
