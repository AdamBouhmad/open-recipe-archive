## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1 egg
- ¾ cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Cream the butter; add sugar and egg well beaten; sift baking powder with flour, and add to the first mixture, alternating with milk.
2. Bake in buttered tin gem pans twenty-five minutes.
