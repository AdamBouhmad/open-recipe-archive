## Ingredients

- stale cake
- good sweet cream
- fresh butter
- preserves

## Directions

1. Cut a stale cake into slices an inch and a half in thickness
2. Pour over them a little good sweet cream
3. Fry lightly in fresh butter in a smooth frying pan
4. When done, place over each slice of cake a layer of preserves or serve with a rich sauce
