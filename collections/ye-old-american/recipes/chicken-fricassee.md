## Ingredients

- one fowl
- boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter or pork fat
- dry toast
- White or Brown Sauce
- three tablespoons butter
- four tablespoons flour
- one and one-half cups stock
- one-half cup cream

## Directions

1. Dress, clean, and cut up a fowl
2. Put in a kettle, cover with boiling water, and cook slowly until tender, adding salt to water when chicken is about half done
3. Remove from water, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dredge with flour, and sauté in butter or pork fat
4. Arrange chicken on pieces of dry toast placed on a hot platter, having wings and second joints opposite each other, breast in centre of platter, and drumsticks crossed just below second joints
5. Pour around White or Brown Sauce
6. Reduce stock to two cups, strain, and remove the fat
7. Melt three tablespoons butter, add four tablespoons flour, and pour on gradually one and one-half cups stock
8. Just before serving, add one-half cup cream, and salt and pepper to taste; or make a sauce by browning butter and flour and adding two cups stock, then seasoning with salt and pepper
