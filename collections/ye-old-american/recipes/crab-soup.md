## Ingredients

- 6 hard-shelled crabs
- 3 cups White Stock III
- ⅔ cup stale bread crumbs
- 1 slice onion
- 1 sprig parsley
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup cream
- Salt
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Remove meat from crabs, and chop finely.
2. Add stock, bread crumbs, onion, and parsley, and simmer twenty minutes.
3. Rub through a sieve, bind with butter and flour cooked together, then add cream and seasonings.
4. Serve with Pulled Bread.
