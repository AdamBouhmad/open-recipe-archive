## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- sweet clarified dripping
- lard
- butter

## Directions

1. Pare cucumbers and lay in ice-water half an hour.
2. Cut lengthwise into slices nearly half an inch thick and lay in ice-water ten minutes longer.
3. Wipe each piece dry with a soft cloth, sprinkle with pepper and salt, and dredge with flour.
4. Fry to a delicate brown in sweet clarified dripping, nice lard, or butter.
