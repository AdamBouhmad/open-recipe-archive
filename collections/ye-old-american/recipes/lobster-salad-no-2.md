## Ingredients

- canned lobsters
- six heads of celery
- a teaspoonful of mustard
- vinegar
- yolks of two fresh eggs
- a tablespoonful of butter
- a small teaspoonful of salt
- a small teaspoonful of pepper
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of cayenne pepper
- a gill of vinegar
- mashed yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- green tops of celery
- a hard-boiled egg

## Directions

1. Take a can of lobster, skim off all the oil on the surface, and chop the meat up coarsely on a flat dish
2. Prepare the same way six heads of celery
3. Mix a teaspoonful of mustard into a smooth paste with a little vinegar
4. Add yolks of two fresh eggs and a tablespoonful of butter, creamed
5. Add a small teaspoonful of salt, the same of pepper, a quarter of a teaspoonful of cayenne pepper, a gill of vinegar, and the mashed yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
6. Mix a small portion of the dressing with the celery and meat
7. Turn the remainder over all
8. Garnish with the green tops of celery and a hard-boiled egg, cut into thin rings
