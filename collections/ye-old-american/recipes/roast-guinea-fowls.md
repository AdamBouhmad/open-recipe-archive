## Ingredients

- a pair of young Guinea fowls
- butter
- chopped shallot
- parsley
- summer savory
- minced giblets
- browned flour
- currant jelly or other tart jelly
- ham, minced fine

## Directions

1. Stuff the Guinea fowls
2. Roast them, basting with butter until they are half done
3. Season the gravy with a chopped shallot, parsley, or summer savory, not omitting the minced giblets, and thicken with browned flour
4. Send around currant or other tart jelly with the fowl
