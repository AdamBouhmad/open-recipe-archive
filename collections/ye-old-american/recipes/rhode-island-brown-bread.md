## Ingredients

- two and one-half cupfuls of corn meal
- one and one-half cupfuls of rye meal
- one egg
- one cup of molasses
- two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- one teaspoonful of soda
- a little salt
- one quart of milk

## Directions

1. Bake in a covered dish, either earthen or iron, in a moderately hot oven three hours
