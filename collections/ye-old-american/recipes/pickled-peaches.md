## Ingredients

- peaches
- cold vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Take fine large peaches that are not too ripe
2. Wipe off the down with a clean flannel, and put the peaches whole into a stone jar
3. Cover them with cold vinegar of the best kind, in which you have dissolved a tea-spoonful of salt to a quart of vinegar
4. Put a cork in the jar and tie leather or oil-cloth over it
