## Ingredients

- three pounds lean fresh pork
- two pounds fat
- two ounces and a half salt
- half an ounce pepper
- twelve cloves
- a dozen blades of mace powdered
- three grated nutmegs
- six table-spoonfuls powdered sage
- two tea-spoonfuls powdered rosemary
- beaten yolk of egg
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Take some fresh pork, (the leg is best,) and clear it from the skin, sinews, and gristle
2. Mince it all very fine, and season it with two ounces and a half of salt, half an ounce of pepper, twelve cloves, and a dozen blades of mace powdered, three grated nutmegs, six table-spoonfuls of powdered sage, and two tea-spoonfuls of powdered rosemary
3. Mix all well together
4. Put it into a stone jar, and press it down very hard
5. Cover it closely, and keep it in a dry cool place
6. When you use this sausage-meat, mix with it some beaten yolk of egg, and make it into balls or cakes
7. Dredge them with flour, and fry them in butter
