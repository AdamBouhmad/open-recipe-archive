## Ingredients

- Parsnips
- Salt
- Drawn Butter Sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape parsnips, and cut in pieces two inches long and one-half inch wide and thick.
2. Cook five minutes in boiling salted water, or until soft.
3. Drain, and to two cups add one cup Drawn Butter Sauce.
