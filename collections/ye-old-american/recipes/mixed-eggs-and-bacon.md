## Ingredients

- mild bacon
- eggs
- pepper
- hot pickles

## Directions

1. Take a nice rasher of mild bacon; cut it into squares no larger than dice
2. Fry it quickly until nicely browned; but on no account burn it
3. Break half a dozen eggs into a basin, strain and season them with pepper
4. Add them to the bacon, stir the whole about and, when sufficiently firm, turn it out into a dish
5. Decorate with hot pickles
