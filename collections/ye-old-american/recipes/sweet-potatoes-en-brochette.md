## Ingredients

- potatoes
- melted butter
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and pare potatoes, and cut in one-third inch slices.
2. Arrange on skewers in groups of three or four, parboil six minutes, and drain.
3. Brush over with melted butter, sprinkle with brown sugar, and bake in a hot oven until well browned.
