## Ingredients

- Tripe
- Salt
- Pepper
- Batter
- Hot fat

## Directions

1. Wipe tripe and cut in pieces for serving
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in batter
4. Fry in a small quantity of hot fat
5. Drain
