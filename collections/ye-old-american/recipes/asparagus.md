## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- bass or tape
- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Set a stew-pan with plenty of water on the fire
2. Sprinkle a handful of salt in it and let it boil
3. Skim it
4. Scrape all the stalks till they are perfectly clean
5. Throw them into a pan of cold water as you scrape them
6. Tie them in little bundles, of a quarter of a hundred each, with bass or tape
7. Cut off the stalks at the bottom, that they may be all of a length
8. Boil from twenty to thirty minutes until tender at the stalk
9. Take them just at that instant when they are tender
10. While the asparagus is boiling, toast a slice of bread, about a half an inch thick
11. Brown it delicately on both sides
12. Dip it lightly in the liquor the asparagus was boiled in
13. Lay it in the middle of a dish
14. Pour some melted butter on the toast
15. Lay the asparagus upon it
16. Send butter in a boat separately
