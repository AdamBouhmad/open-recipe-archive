## Ingredients

- peaches
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. To one pound of peaches allow half a pound of sugar.
2. To six pounds of sugar add half a tumbler of water.
3. Put in the kettle a layer of sugar and one of peaches until the whole of both are in.
