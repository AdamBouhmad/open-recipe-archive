## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Buy the fruit when not too ripe, pick over immediately, wash if absolutely necessary and put in glass jars, filling each one about two-thirds full
2. Put in the preserving kettle a pound of sugar and one cupful of water for every two pounds of fruit, and let it come slowly to a boil
3. Pour this syrup into the jars over the berries, filling them up to the brim
4. Set the jars in a pot of cold water on the stove, and let the water boil and the fruit become scalding hot
5. Take them out and seal perfectly tight
