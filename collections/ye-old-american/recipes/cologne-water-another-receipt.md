## Ingredients

- a pint of alcohol
- sixty drops or two large tea-spoonfuls of orange-flower water
- essential oils of lemon, lavender, and bergamot

## Directions

1. Mix with a pint of alcohol the orange-flower water and essential oils in equal quantities
