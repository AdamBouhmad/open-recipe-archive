## Ingredients

- cold mutton, lean
- pepper
- salt
- mutton bones and trimmings
- water
- onions, sliced
- carrots, boiled
- turnips, boiled
- potatoes, boiled
- onions, boiled
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Cut into small pieces the lean of some cold mutton that has been underdone, and season it with pepper and salt.
2. Take the bones and other trimmings, put them into a sauce-pan with as much water as will cover them, and some sliced onions, and let them stew till you have drawn from them a good gravy.
3. Skim the gravy well, and strain it into a stew-pan, and put the mutton into it.
4. Have ready-boiled some carrots, turnips, potatoes and onions. Slice them, and add them to the meat and gravy.
5. Set the pan on hot coals, and let it simmer till the meat is warmed through, but do not allow it to boil, as it has been once cooked already.
6. Cover the bottom of a dish with slices of buttered toast.
7. Lay the meat and vegetables upon it, and pour over them the gravy.
