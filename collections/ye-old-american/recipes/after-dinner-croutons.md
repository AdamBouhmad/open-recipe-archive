## Ingredients

- sandwich bread
- butter
- rich creamy cheese
- a very little minced parsley

## Directions

1. Cut sandwich bread into slices one-quarter of an inch thick
2. Cut each slice into four small triangles
3. Dry them in the oven slowly until they assume a delicate brownish tint
4. Serve either hot or cold
5. A nice way to serve them is to spread a paste of part butter and part rich creamy cheese, to which may be added a very little minced parsley
