## Ingredients

- calf's brains
- powdered sage
- pepper
- salt
- fine bread-crumbs
- milk
- beaten egg
- cracker-crumbs
- butter or veal-drippings

## Directions

1. Wash the brains very thoroughly until free from membranous matter and perfectly white
2. Beat them smooth
3. Season with a pinch of powdered sage, pepper, and salt
4. Add two tablespoonfuls fine bread-crumbs moistened with milk and a beaten egg
5. Roll into balls with floured hands
6. Dip in beaten egg, then cracker-crumbs
7. Fry in butter or veal-drippings
