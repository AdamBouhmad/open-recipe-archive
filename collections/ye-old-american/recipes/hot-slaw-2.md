## Ingredients

- one-half cabbage
- yolks of two eggs
- one-fourth cup cold water
- one tablespoon butter
- one-fourth cup hot vinegar
- one-half teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Slice cabbage as for Cole-Slaw, using one-half cabbage.
2. Heat in a dressing made of yolks of two eggs slightly beaten, one-fourth cup cold water, one tablespoon butter, one-fourth cup hot vinegar, and one-half teaspoon salt, stirred over hot water until thickened.
