## Ingredients

- 1 quart thin cream
- 1 cup boiled coffee
- 1 cup sugar
- ¼ box gelatine or 1¼ tablespoons granulated gelatine
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- 3 tablespoons hot water

## Directions

1. Make same as Strawberry Mousse, using one cup boiled coffee in place of fruit juice
