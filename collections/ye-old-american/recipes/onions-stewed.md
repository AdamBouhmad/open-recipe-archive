## Ingredients

- onions
- milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Cook the same as boiled onions
2. When quite done, turn off all the water
3. Add a teacupful of milk, a piece of butter the size of an egg, pepper and salt to taste
4. Add a tablespoonful of flour stirred to a cream
5. Let all boil up once and serve in a vegetable dish hot
