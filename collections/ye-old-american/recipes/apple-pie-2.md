## Ingredients

- apples
- lemon
- cinnamon
- mace
- rose-water
- sugar
- paste No. 3

## Directions

1. Stew and strain the apples, to every three pints, grate the peal of a fresh lemon, add cinnamon, mace, rose-water and sugar to your taste--and bake in paste No. 3.
2. Every species of fruit such as peas, plums, raspberries, black berries may be only sweetened, without spices--and bake in paste No. 3.
