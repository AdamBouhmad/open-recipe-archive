## Ingredients

- Two boiled potatoes, strained through a kitchen sieve
- One spoon of mordant mustard
- A double quantity of salt
- Oil of Lucca
- Vinegar
- The pounded yellow of two well-boiled eggs
- Onions
- A magic spoonful of anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Strain two boiled potatoes through a kitchen sieve
2. Take a single spoon of mustard and add a double quantity of salt
3. Crown four times the spoon with oil of Lucca
4. Add twice the spoon with vinegar procured from town
5. Add the pounded yellow of two well-boiled eggs
6. Let onions' atoms lurk within the bowl, scarce suspected
7. Toss in a magic spoonful of anchovy sauce
8. Mix all ingredients together
