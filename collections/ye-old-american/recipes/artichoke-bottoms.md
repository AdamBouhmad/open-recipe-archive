## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- water
- acid

## Directions

1. Remove all leaves and the choke
2. Trim bottoms in shape
3. Cook until soft in boiling, salted, acidulated water
4. Serve with Hollandaise or Béchamel Sauce
