## Ingredients

- two young chickens
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- celery
- flour
- milk
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut up two young chickens
2. put them in a stewpan with just enough cold water to cover them
3. Cover closely and let them heat very slowly
4. stew them over an hour, or until tender
5. If old chickens, require long, slow boiling, often from three to four hours
6. When tender, season with salt and pepper
7. add a piece of butter as large as an egg, and a little celery, if liked
8. Stir up two tablespoonfuls of flour in a little water or milk and add to the stew
9. add two well-beaten yolks of eggs
10. let all boil up one minute
11. arrange the chicken on a warm platter
12. pour some of the gravy over it and send the rest to the table in a boat
