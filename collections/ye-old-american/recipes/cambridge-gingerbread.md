## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅔ cup boiling water
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 egg
- 2¾ cups flour
- 1½ teaspoons soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- ¼ teaspoon clove

## Directions

1. Melt butter in water, add molasses, egg well beaten, and dry ingredients mixed and sifted.
2. Bake in a buttered shallow pan.
