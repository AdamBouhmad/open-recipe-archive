## Ingredients

- ducks
- livers
- bacon
- butter
- green onions
- sweet herbs
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- mushrooms
- gravy
- orange juice
- shallots

## Directions

1. Singe, pick, and draw the ducks
2. Mince the livers with a little scraped bacon, some butter, green onions, sweet herbs and parsley, seasoned with salt, pepper, and mushrooms
3. Put them into the bodies of the ducks
4. Roast them, covered with slices of bacon, and wrapped up in paper
5. Put a little gravy, the juice of an orange, a few shallots minced, into a stew pan, and shake in a little pepper
6. When the ducks are roasted, take off the bacon, dish them, and pour your sauce with the juice of oranges over them, and serve them up hot
