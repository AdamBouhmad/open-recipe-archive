## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound sweet almonds
- 1 large spoonful rose-water
- 6 eggs
- 3 spoonfuls fine white sugar
- 1 quart milk
- 3 spoonfuls pounded crackers
- 4 ounces melted butter
- 4 ounces citron, cut into bits
- pastry for lining and rim

## Directions

1. Turn boiling water on almonds and let sit until skin comes off easily
2. Rub with dry cloth and when dry, pound fine with rose-water
3. Beat eggs to stiff froth with sugar
4. Mix with milk, crackers, butter, citron and almonds
5. Stir altogether and bake in pudding-dish with pastry lining and rim
6. Bake half an hour in quick oven
7. Serve cold
