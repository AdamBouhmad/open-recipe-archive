## Ingredients

- ½ can tomatoes or 1¾ cups fresh stewed tomatoes
- 1 slice onion
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Cook onion with tomatoes fifteen minutes, rub through a strainer, and add to butter and flour (to which seasonings have been added) cooked together.
2. If tomatoes are very acid, add a few grains of soda.
3. If tomatoes are to retain their red color it is necessary to brown butter and flour together before adding the tomatoes.
