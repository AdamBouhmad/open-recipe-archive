## Ingredients

- five cupfuls of sifted flour
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- one-half a cupful of chopped suet
- half a pound of currants
- milk
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Sift two teaspoonfuls of baking powder into five cupfuls of sifted flour
2. Wash the currants, dry them thoroughly and pick away any stalks or grit
3. Chop the suet finely
4. Mix all the ingredients together and moisten with sufficient milk to make the pudding into a stiff batter
5. Tie it up in a floured cloth and put it into boiling water
6. Boil for three hours and a half
7. Serve with jelly sauce made very sweet
