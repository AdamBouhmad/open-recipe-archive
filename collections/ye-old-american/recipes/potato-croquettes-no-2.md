## Ingredients

- cold mashed potatoes (two cups)
- salt
- pepper
- butter (a tablespoonful)
- eggs (two)
- flour or cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Take two cups of cold mashed potatoes, season with a pinch of salt, pepper and a tablespoonful of butter.
2. Beat up the whites of two eggs, and work all together thoroughly.
3. Make it into small balls slightly flattened.
4. Dip them in the beaten yolks of the eggs.
5. Roll either in flour or cracker crumbs.
6. Fry the same as fish-balls.
