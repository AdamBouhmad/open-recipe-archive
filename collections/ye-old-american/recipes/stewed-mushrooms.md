## Ingredients

- grown mushrooms
- salt
- butter
- brown flour

## Directions

1. gather grown mushrooms, but such as are young enough to have red gills
2. cut off that part of the stem which grew in the earth
3. wash them carefully
4. take the skin from the top
5. put them into a stew pan with some salt, but no water
6. stew them till tender
7. thicken them with a spoonful of butter, mixed with one of brown flour
