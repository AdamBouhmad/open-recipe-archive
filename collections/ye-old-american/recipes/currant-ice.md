## Ingredients

- currants or raspberries
- a pint of juice
- a pint of water
- a pint of sugar
- whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Squeeze enough fruit in a jelly-bag to make a pint of juice
2. Add a pint each of the water and sugar
3. Pour the whole, boiling hot, onto whites of three eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
4. Whip the mixture thoroughly
5. When cool, freeze in the usual manner
