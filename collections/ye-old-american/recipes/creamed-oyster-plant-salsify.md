## Ingredients

- oyster plant
- cold acidulated water
- boiling salted water
- White Sauce I

## Directions

1. Wash, scrape, and put at once into cold acidulated water to prevent discoloration.
2. Cut in inch slices, cook in boiling salted water until soft, drain, and add to White Sauce I.
