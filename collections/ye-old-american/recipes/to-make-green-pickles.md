## Ingredients

- articles to pickle
- salt and water
- turmeric
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the articles you intend to pickle in a pot
2. Cover them with boiling salt and water
3. Put a thick cloth on the top and then a plate that will fit it
4. Let it stand till the next morning
5. Pour off the salt and water and boil it again
6. Cover them as before
7. Repeat this until your pickles are a good green
8. Then put them in plain cold vinegar with some turmeric in it
9. At the end of a fortnight, put them up as you do the yellow pickle
