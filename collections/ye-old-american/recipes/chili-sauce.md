## Ingredients

- two dozen ripe tomatoes
- three small green peppers or half a teaspoonful of cayenne pepper
- one onion
- half a cup of sugar
- two cups of vinegar
- one tablespoonful of salt
- one teaspoonful each of ginger, allspice, cloves and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil together two dozen ripe tomatoes, three small green peppers or a half teaspoonful of cayenne pepper, one onion cut fine, and half a cup of sugar
2. Boil until thick
3. Add two cups of vinegar
4. Strain the whole and set back on the fire
5. Add one tablespoonful of salt and one teaspoonful each of ginger, allspice, cloves and cinnamon
6. Boil all five minutes
7. Remove and seal in glass bottles
