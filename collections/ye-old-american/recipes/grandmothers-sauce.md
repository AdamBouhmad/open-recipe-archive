## Ingredients

- a cupful of sifted sugar
- half a cupful of butter
- a teaspoonful of ground cinnamon
- an egg
- a teacupful of milk

## Directions

1. Cream together a cupful of sifted sugar and half a cupful of butter
2. Add a teaspoonful of ground cinnamon and an egg well beaten
3. Boil a teacupful of milk and turn it, boiling hot, over the mixture slowly, stirring all the time
4. This will cook the egg smoothly
5. It may be served cold or hot
