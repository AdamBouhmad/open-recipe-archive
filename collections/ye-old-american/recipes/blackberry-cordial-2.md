## Ingredients

- berries
- one pint of juice
- one pound of white sugar
- one-half ounce of powdered cinnamon
- one-fourth ounce of mace
- two teaspoonfuls of cloves
- a glass of French brandy per pint

## Directions

1. Warm and squeeze the berries
2. Add to one pint of juice one pound of white sugar, one-half ounce of powdered cinnamon, one-fourth ounce of mace, and two teaspoonfuls of cloves
3. Boil all together for one-fourth of an hour
4. Strain the syrup, and to each pint add a glass of French brandy
