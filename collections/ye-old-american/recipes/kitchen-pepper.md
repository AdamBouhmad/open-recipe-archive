## Ingredients

- two ounces of the best white ginger
- an ounce of black pepper
- an ounce of white pepper
- an ounce of cinnamon
- an ounce of nutmeg
- two dozen cloves

## Directions

1. Mix together two ounces of the best white ginger, an ounce of black pepper, an ounce of white pepper, an ounce of cinnamon, an ounce of nutmeg, and two dozen cloves
2. They must all be ground or pounded to a fine powder, and thoroughly mixed
3. Keep the mixture in a bottle, labelled, and well corked
