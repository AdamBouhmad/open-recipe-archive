## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- lard
- butter

## Directions

1. Pare them and cut lengthwise in very thick slices.
2. Wipe them dry with a cloth.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dredge with flour.
4. Fry in lard and butter, a tablespoonful of each mixed.
5. Brown both sides and serve warm.
