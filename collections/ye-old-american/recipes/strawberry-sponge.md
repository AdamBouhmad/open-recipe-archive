## Ingredients

- One quart of strawberries
- Half a package of gelatine
- One cupful and a half of water
- One cupful of sugar
- The juice of a lemon
- The whites of four eggs

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine for two hours in half a cupful of the water
2. Mash the strawberries and add half the sugar to them
3. Boil the remainder of the sugar and the water gently twenty minutes
4. Rub the strawberries through a sieve
5. Add the gelatine to the boiling syrup
