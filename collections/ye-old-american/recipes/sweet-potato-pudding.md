## Ingredients

- one pound of sweet potatos
- six eggs well beaten
- three quarters of a pound of powdered sugar
- three quarters of butter
- grated nutmeg
- lemon peel
- a glass of brandy
- paste
- sugar
- bits of citron

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of sweet potatos very tender
2. Rub them while hot through a colander
3. Add six eggs well beaten, three quarters of a pound of powdered sugar, three quarters of butter, and some grated nutmeg and lemon peel, with a glass of brandy
4. Put a paste in the dish, and when the pudding is done, sprinkle the top with sugar, and cover it with bits of citron
