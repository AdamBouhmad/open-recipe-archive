## Ingredients

- slice of raw ham pickled but not smoked
- veal or chicken broth
- oysters
- oyster liquor
- onion
- parsley
- sweet marjoram
- pepper
- cornstarch
- milk
- butter
- egg
- lemon
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a slice of raw ham which has been pickled but not smoked and soak in boiling water for half an hour
2. Cut it in quite small pieces and put in a saucepan with two-thirds of a pint of veal or chicken broth, well strained
3. Add the liquor from a quart of oysters, one small onion minced fine, a little chopped parsley, sweet marjoram, and pepper
4. Let them simmer for twenty minutes and then boil rapidly for two or three minutes
5. Skim well and add one scant tablespoon of cornstarch mixed smoothly in one-third cup of milk
6. Stir constantly, and when it boils add the oysters and one ounce of butter
7. Just let it come to a boil and remove the oysters to a deep dish
8. Beat one egg and add to it gradually some of the hot broth
9. When cooked, stir it into the pan
10. Season with salt and pour the whole over the oysters
11. When placed upon the table, squeeze the juice of a lemon over it
