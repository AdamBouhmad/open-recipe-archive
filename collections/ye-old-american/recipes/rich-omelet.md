## Ingredients

- 2½ tablespoons flour
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Mix salt and flour, and add gradually milk.
2. Beat eggs until thick and lemon-colored, then add to first mixture.
3. Heat iron frying-pan and put in two-thirds of the butter.
4. When butter is melted, pour in mixture.
5. As it cooks, lift with a griddle-cake turner so that uncooked part may run underneath.
6. Add remaining butter as needed, and continue lifting the cooked part until it is firm throughout.
7. Place on hotter part of range to brown.
8. Roll, and turn on hot platter.
