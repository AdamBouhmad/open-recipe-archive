## Ingredients

- three cups of flour sifted
- three teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- one teaspoonful of salt
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- three eggs
- half a nutmeg grated
- one teaspoonful of ground cinnamon
- two small cups of milk
- sugar and water
- dry sugar

## Directions

1. Sift together salt, flour, sugar and baking powder
2. Rub in the butter cold
3. Add the milk, beaten eggs and spices
4. Mix into a soft dough
5. Break off pieces about as large as an egg
6. Roll them under the hands into round balls
7. Rub the tops with sugar and water mixed
8. Sprinkle dry sugar over them
9. Bake immediately
