## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- The weight of the eggs in sugar and in flour
- Half their weight in butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls milk
- ¼ teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water

## Directions

1. Rub the sugar and butter together; beat the yolks light, and add then the milk and soda; lastly the flour and beaten whites alternately.
2. Fill six small cups, well buttered, and bake twenty minutes, or until a nice brown.
3. Eat warm.
