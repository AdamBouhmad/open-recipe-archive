## Ingredients

- grapes
- sugar
- quince seeds

## Directions

1. Mash well the berries so as to remove the skins
2. Pour all into a preserving kettle and cook slowly for a few minutes to extract the juice
3. Strain through a colander, and then through a flannel jelly-bag, keeping as hot as possible
4. Measure the juice, allowing a pound of sugar to every pint of juice
5. Boil fast for at least half an hour
6. Try a little, and if it seems done, remove and put into glasses
