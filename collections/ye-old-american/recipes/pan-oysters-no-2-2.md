## Ingredients

- oysters
- oyster liquor
- toast

## Directions

1. Lay in a thin pie tin or dripping-pan, half a pint of large oysters, or more if required.
2. Have the pan large enough so that each oyster will lie flat on the bottom.
3. Put in over them a little oyster liquor, but not enough to float.
4. Place them carefully in a hot oven and just heat them through thoroughly--do not bake them--which will be in three to five minutes, according to fire.
5. Take them up and place on toast; first moistened with the hot juice from the pan.
