## Ingredients

- celery
- water
- powdered mace
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil celery in water till quite tender
2. Add a little powdered mace and nutmeg, and a very little pepper and salt
3. Take a tolerably large piece of butter, roll it well in flour, and stir it into the sauce
4. Boil it up again and it is ready to send to table
