## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 teacupful sugar
- ½ teacupful butter
- 3 teaspoonfuls milk
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar
- ½ teaspoonful soda
- 2 small lemons, juice of two and grated rind of one

## Directions

1. Mix rather stiff.
2. Roll and cut out with a cake-cutter.
