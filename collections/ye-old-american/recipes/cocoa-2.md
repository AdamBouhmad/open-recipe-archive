## Ingredients

- Six tablespoonfuls of cocoa
- a pint of water
- as much milk as water
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Rub cocoa smooth in a little cold water
2. Have ready on the fire a pint of boiling water
3. Stir in grated cocoa paste
4. Boil twenty minutes
5. Add milk and boil five minutes more, stirring often
6. Sweeten in cups so as to suit different tastes
