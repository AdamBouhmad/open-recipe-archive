## Ingredients

- a good piece of fresh beef
- salt
- water
- potatoes
- parsnips
- turnips (optional)

## Directions

1. Take a good piece of fresh beef. It must not be too fat.
2. Wash it, rub it with salt, and put it into a pot with barely sufficient water to cover it.
3. Set it over a slow fire, and after it has stewed an hour, put in some potatoes pared and cut in half, and some parsnips, scraped and split.
4. Let them stew with the beef till quite tender.
5. Turn the meat several times in the pot.
6. When all is done, serve up the meat and vegetables together, and the gravy in a boat, having first skimmed it.
