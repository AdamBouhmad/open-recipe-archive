## Ingredients

- shad roe
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- crumbs
- egg
- deep fat

## Directions

1. Parboil and cook shad roe as for Baked Shad Roe
2. Cut in pieces
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
4. Brush over with lemon juice
5. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs
6. Fry in deep fat
7. Drain
