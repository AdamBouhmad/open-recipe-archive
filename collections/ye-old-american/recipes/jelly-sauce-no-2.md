## Ingredients

- ½ cup currant jelly
- 2 tablespoonfuls melted butter
- 1 lemon—juice and half the grated peel
- ½ teaspoonful nutmeg
- 2 glasses wine, and a tablespoonful powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Heat the butter a little more than blood-warm; beat the jelly to a smooth batter and add gradually the butter, the lemon, and nutmeg.
2. Warm almost to a boil, stirring all the while; beat hard, put in the sugar, lastly the wine.
3. Set in a vessel of hot water stirring now and then, until it is wanted.
4. Keep it covered to hinder the escape of the wine flavor.
5. Stir well before pouring out.
