## Ingredients

- vinegar
- four spoonfuls of mustard
- half of a teaspoonful of sugar
- a saltspoonful of salt
- a tablespoonful of melted butter

## Directions

1. Boil some vinegar
2. Take four spoonfuls of mustard, half of a teaspoonful of sugar, a saltspoonful of salt, a tablespoonful of melted butter; mix well
