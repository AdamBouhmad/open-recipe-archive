## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4½ tablespoons flour
- ½ cup chicken stock
- ½ cup cream
- ½ tablespoon vinegar
- ¾ tablespoon lemon juice
- 2 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon grated horseradish
- 2 tablespoons capers
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Parboil oysters, drain, and reserve liquor; there should be one-half cup.
2. Make sauce of butter, flour, stock, oyster liquor, and cream.
3. Add yolks of eggs, seasonings, and salt and pepper to taste.
4. Add oysters, and as soon as oysters are heated, fill patty shells.
