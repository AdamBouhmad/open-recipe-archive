## Ingredients

- three heads of lettuce
- two teaspoonfuls of green mustard leaves
- a handful of water cresses
- five tender radishes
- one cucumber
- three hard-boiled eggs
- two teaspoonfuls of white sugar
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one teaspoonful of pepper
- one teaspoonful of made mustard
- one teacupful of vinegar
- half a teacupful of oil

## Directions

1. Mix all well together
2. Serve with a lump of ice in the middle
