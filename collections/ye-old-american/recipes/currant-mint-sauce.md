## Ingredients

- two-thirds tumbler currant jelly
- one and one-half tablespoons mint leaves
- one-fourth orange

## Directions

1. Separate currant jelly in pieces, but do not beat it
2. Add finely chopped mint leaves
3. Add shavings from the rind of one-fourth orange
