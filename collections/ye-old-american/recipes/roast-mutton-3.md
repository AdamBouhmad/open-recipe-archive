## Ingredients

- mutton
- water
- salt
- flour
- butter
- brown flour
- currant jelly or other tart sauce

## Directions

1. Trim off every part that cannot be eaten.
2. Wash well and dry with a clean cloth.
3. Lay it in your dripping-pan and put in a little water to baste it with at first.
4. Allow, in roasting, about twelve minutes to the pound.
5. It should not be salted at first, as that tends to harden it.
6. Salt soon after it begins to roast well.
7. If there is danger of its browning too fast, cover it with a sheet of white paper.
8. Baste it often, and about a quarter of an hour before you think it will be done dredge the meat very lightly with flour and baste it with butter.
9. Skim the gravy well and thicken very slightly with brown flour.
10. Serve with currant jelly or other tart sauce.
