## Ingredients

- woodcocks or snipes
- lard
- bread slices
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Pluck, but do not draw them
2. Put them on a small spit
3. Dredge and baste them well with lard
4. Toast a few slices of bread and put them on a clean plate
5. Set it under the birds while they are roasting
6. If the fire be good, they will take about ten minutes
7. When you take them from the spit, lay them upon the toasts on the dish
8. Pour melted butter round them
9. Serve them up
