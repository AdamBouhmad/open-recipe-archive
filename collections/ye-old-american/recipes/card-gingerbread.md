## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅓ cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- ½ cup molasses
- 1¾ cups flour
- ½ tablespoon ginger
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon soda
- ¼ teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, egg well beaten, molasses, and flour mixed and sifted with ginger, salt, soda, and cinnamon.
2. Chill, roll in sheets to one-fourth inch in thickness, bake on a buttered sheet, and cut in squares.
