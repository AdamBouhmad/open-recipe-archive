## Ingredients

- 1 quart berries
- 1 pint sugar—dissolved in the juice
- 1 lemon—juice only
- ½ pint of water

## Directions

1. Extract the juice and strain
2. Dissolve the sugar in the juice
3. Add the lemon juice and water, and freeze
