## Ingredients

- Leg of pork
- sage
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- water
- apple sauce
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Choose a small leg of fine young pork
2. Cut a slit in the knuckle with a sharp knife
3. Fill the space with sage and onion chopped, and a little pepper and salt
4. When half done, score the skin in slices, but do not cut deeper than the outer rind
5. Make the gravy by turning off all the superfluous fat and adding a spoonful of flour stirred with a little water
6. Add water to make the right consistency
7. Serve in a gravy boat
8. Serve apple sauce and potatoes with it
