## Ingredients

- fowls
- salt
- water
- salt pork, half a pound
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- milk, one teacupful
- eggs, two
- flour, two tablespoonfuls
- butter, a great spoonful

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and cut up the fowls
2. Lay them in salt and water for half an hour
3. Put them in a pot with enough cold water to cover them, and half a pound of salt pork cut into thin strips
4. Cover closely, and let them heat very slowly
5. Stew for over an hour, if the fowls are tender
6. When tender, add a chopped onion or two, parsley, and pepper
7. Cover closely again, and when it has heated to boiling, stir in a teacupful of milk to which have been added two beaten eggs and two tablespoonfuls of flour
8. Boil up fairly; add a great spoonful of butter
9. Arrange the chicken neatly in a deep chafing-dish, pour the gravy over it, and serve
