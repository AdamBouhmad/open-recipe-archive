## Ingredients

- three pound sugar
- half pound butter
- quarter of a pound of ginger
- one doz. eggs
- one glass rose water
- three pounds flour

## Directions

1. rub three pound sugar, half pound butter, quarter of a pound of ginger, one doz. eggs, one glass rose water, into three pounds flour
2. bake as No. 1
