## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Few grains pepper
- ⅔ cup milk
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix flour, salt, and pepper.
2. Add milk gradually, and eggs well beaten.
