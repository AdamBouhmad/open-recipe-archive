## Ingredients

- Swedish Bread mixture
- melted butter
- sugar
- blanched almonds, chopped or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take one-third Swedish Bread mixture and shape, using the hands, in a long roll
2. Put on an unfloured board and roll, using a rolling-pin, as thinly as possible
3. Spread with melted butter, sprinkle with sugar and chopped blanched almonds or cinnamon
4. Roll like a jelly roll, cut a piece from each end and join ends to form ring
5. Place on a buttered sheet, and cut with scissors and shape
6. Let rise, and proceed as with Tea Ring I
