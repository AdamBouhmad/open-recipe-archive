## Ingredients

- Two cups of sugar
- Two-thirds cup of butter
- Whites of seven eggs
- Two-thirds cup of sweet milk
- Two cups of flour
- One cup of cornstarch
- Two teaspoonfuls baking powder
- Whites of three eggs
- Sugar
- Grated cocoanut

## Directions

1. Beat whites of seven eggs well
2. Mix sugar, butter, and beaten egg whites together
3. Add sweet milk
4. Mix flour and cornstarch with baking powder
5. Stir into cake mixture
6. Bake in jelly-cake tins
7. Make frosting by beating whites of three eggs with sugar not quite as stiff as usual
8. Spread frosting over cakes and add grated cocoanut
9. Put cakes together with cocoanut and frosting
10. Add frosting on top
