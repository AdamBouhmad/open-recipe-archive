## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅔ cup strawberries
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, egg beaten until stiff, and strawberries.
2. Beat until fruit is mashed.
