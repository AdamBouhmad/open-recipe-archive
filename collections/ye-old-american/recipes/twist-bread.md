## Ingredients

- bread dough
- flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Let the bread be made as directed for wheat bread
2. take three pieces as large as a pint bowl each
3. strew a little flour over the paste-board or table
4. roll each piece under your hands to twelve inches length, making it smaller in circumference at the ends than in the middle
5. lay one part on a baking-tin
6. joint one end of each of the other two to it
7. braid them together the length of the rolls and join the ends by pressing them together
8. dip a brush in milk and pass it over the top of the loaf
9. after ten minutes or so, set it in a quick oven and bake for nearly an hour
