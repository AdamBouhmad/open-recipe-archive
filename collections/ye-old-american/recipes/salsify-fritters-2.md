## Ingredients

- Oyster plant
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cook oyster plant as for Creamed Oyster Plant.
2. Mash, season with butter, salt, and pepper.
3. Shape in small flat cakes, roll in flour, and sauté in butter.
