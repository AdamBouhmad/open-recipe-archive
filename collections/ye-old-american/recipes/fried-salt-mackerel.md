## Ingredients

- salt mackerel
- cold water
- flour
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Select as many salt mackerel as required
2. Wash and cleanse them well
3. Put them to soak all day in cold water, changing them every two hours
4. Put them into fresh water just before retiring
5. In the morning drain off the water, wipe them dry, roll them in flour
6. Fry in a little butter on a hot, thick-bottomed frying pan
7. Serve with a little melted butter poured over, and garnish with a little parsley
