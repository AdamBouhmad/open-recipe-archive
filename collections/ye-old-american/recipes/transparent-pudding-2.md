## Ingredients

- small cupful of fresh butter
- one cupful of sifted sugar
- teaspoonful of nutmeg, grated
- eight eggs, yolks and whites beaten separately
- vanilla, almond, peach or rose-water flavoring
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar light
2. Add the nutmeg and the beaten eggs, which should be stirred in gradually
3. Flavor with vanilla, almond, peach or rose-water
4. Stir hard
5. Butter a deep dish and line with puff paste
6. Bake half an hour
7. Make a meringue for the top and brown
8. Serve cold
