## Ingredients

- fillet of veal
- grated stale bread
- butter
- sweet marjoram
- grated lemon-peel
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- beaten yolk of egg
- cold boiled ham
- water
- green peas

## Directions

1. Wipe the fillet well and with a sharp knife make deep incisions all over the surface, the bottom as well as the top and sides
2. Make a stuffing of grated stale bread, butter, chopped sweet marjoram, grated lemon-peel, nutmeg, pepper and salt, mixed up with beaten yolk of egg to bind and give it consistency
3. Fill the holes or incisions with the stuffing, pressing it down well with your fingers
4. Reserve some of the stuffing to rub all over the outside of the meat
5. Have ready some very thin slices of cold boiled ham, the fatter the better
6. Cover the veal with them, fastening them on with skewers
7. Put it into a pot and stew it slowly in a very little water, just enough to cover it
8. It will take at least five hours to stew, or more in proportion to its size
9. When done, take off the ham and lay it round the veal in a dish
