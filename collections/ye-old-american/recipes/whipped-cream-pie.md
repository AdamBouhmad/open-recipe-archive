## Ingredients

- rich crust
- jelly or jam
- one cupful of thick sweet cream
- powdered sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Line a pie plate with a rich crust and bake quickly in a hot oven
2. When done, spread with a thin layer of jelly or jam
3. Whip one cupful of thick sweet cream until it is as light as possible
4. Sweeten with powdered sugar and flavor with vanilla
5. Spread over the jelly or jam
6. Set the cream where it will get very cold before whipping
