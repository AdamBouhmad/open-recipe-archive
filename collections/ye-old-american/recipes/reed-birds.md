## Ingredients

- reed birds
- salt
- flour
- butter
- pepper
- toast
- oyster
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Pick and draw them very carefully
2. Salt and dredge with flour
3. Roast with a quick fire ten or fifteen minutes
4. Serve on toast with butter and pepper
5. Alternatively, put in each one an oyster dipped in butter and then in bread crumbs before roasting
6. They are also very nice broiled
