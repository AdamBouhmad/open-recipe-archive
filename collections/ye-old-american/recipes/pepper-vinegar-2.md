## Ingredients

- 6 pods red peppers broken up
- 3 dozen black pepper-corns
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 quart of best vinegar

## Directions

1. Scald the vinegar in which the sugar has been dissolved; pour over the pepper, put into a jar, and steep a fortnight
2. Strain and bottle
