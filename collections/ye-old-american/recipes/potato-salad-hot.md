## Ingredients

- six or eight large potatoes
- three large onions
- breakfast bacon
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Pare six or eight large potatoes, and boil till done, and slice thin while hot
2. Peel and cut up three large onions into small bits and mix with the potatoes
3. Cut up some breakfast bacon into small bits, sufficient to fill a teacup and fry it a light brown
4. Remove the meat, and into the grease stir three tablespoonfuls of vinegar, making a sour gravy
5. Pour the gravy and bacon over the potato and onion
6. Mix lightly
7. Eat when hot
