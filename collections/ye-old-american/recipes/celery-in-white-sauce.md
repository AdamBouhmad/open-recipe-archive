## Ingredients

- celery stalks
- salt
- one cup White Sauce I

## Directions

1. Wash, scrape, and cut celery stalks in one-inch pieces.
2. Cook twenty minutes or until soft in boiling salted water.
3. Drain.
4. To two cups celery add one cup White Sauce I.
