## Ingredients

- strawberries
- pounded sugar
- half a package of Cox's gelatine per pint of juice
- half a pint of water per pint of juice
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pick the strawberries and put them into a pan
2. Squeeze them well with a wooden spoon
3. Add sufficient pounded sugar to sweeten them nicely
4. Let them remain for one hour that the juice may be extracted
5. Add half a pint of water to every pint of juice
6. Strain the strawberry juice and water through a napkin
7. Measure it and to every pint allow half a package of Cox's gelatine dissolved in a teacupful of water
8. Mix this with the juice
9. Put the jelly into a mold
10. Set the mold on ice
