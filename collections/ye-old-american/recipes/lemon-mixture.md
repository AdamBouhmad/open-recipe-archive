## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- Grated rind 1 lemon
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoon brandy

## Directions

1. Cook three tablespoons lemon juice, grated rind one lemon, and one-fourth cup butter two minutes
2. Add one cup sugar and three eggs slightly beaten
3. Cook until mixture thickens
4. Cool, and add one tablespoon brandy
