## Ingredients

- puff paste
- white of an egg
- sugar
- apricot jam, marmalade, or red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Roll puff paste out thin
2. cut it into two and a half inch squares
3. brush each square over with the white of an egg
4. fold down the corners, so that they all meet in the middle of each piece of paste
5. slightly press the two pieces together
6. brush them over with the egg
7. sift over sugar
8. bake in a nice quick oven for about a quarter of an hour
9. When they are done, make a little hole in the middle of the paste and fill it up with apricot jam, marmalade, or red currant jelly
10. Pile them high in the centre of a dish on a napkin and garnish with the same preserves the tartlets are filled with
