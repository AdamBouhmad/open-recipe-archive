## Ingredients

- two quarts of boiling water
- three pounds and a half of the best brown sugar
- a pint and a half of good West India molasses
- a quarter of a pound of tartaric acid
- a tea-spoonful of essence of sassafras
- a quarter of a pound of carbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Mix gradually with two quarts of boiling water, three pounds and a half of the best brown sugar, a pint and a half of good West India molasses, and a quarter of a pound of tartaric acid
2. Stir it well
3. When cool, strain it into a large jug or pan
4. Mix in a tea-spoonful (not more) of essence of sassafras
5. Transfer it to clean bottles, cork it tightly, and keep it in a cool place
6. It will be fit for use next day
7. Put into a box or boxes a quarter of a pound of carbonate of soda to use with it
