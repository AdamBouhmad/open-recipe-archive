## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 1¼ cups flour
- 2½ teaspoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in the order given, and bake in individual buttered cake tins.
