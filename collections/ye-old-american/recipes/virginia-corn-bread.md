## Ingredients

- three cups of white corn meal
- one cup of flour
- one tablespoonful of sugar
- one teaspoonful of salt
- two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- one tablespoonful of lard
- three cups of milk
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Sift together the flour, corn meal, sugar, salt and baking powder
2. rub in the lard cold
3. add the eggs well beaten and then the milk
4. Mix into a moderately stiff batter
5. pour it into well-greased, shallow baking pans (pie-tins are suitable)
6. Bake from thirty to forty minutes
