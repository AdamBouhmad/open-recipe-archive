## Ingredients

- Shad Roe
- Salt and pepper
- Maître d'Hôtel Butter

## Directions

1. Wipe.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Put on greased wire broiler.
4. Broil five minutes on each side.
5. Serve with Maître d'Hôtel Butter.
