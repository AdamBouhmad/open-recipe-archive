## Ingredients

- 1 crab, boiled and cold
- 4 tablespoonfuls of milk
- 1 teacupful drawn butter
- Cayenne, mace, and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Make the drawn butter as usual, and stir in the milk.
2. Pick the meat from the crab, chop very fine, season with cayenne, mace, and salt to taste; stir into the drawn butter.
3. Simmer three minutes, but do not boil.
