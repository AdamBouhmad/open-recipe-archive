## Ingredients

- two thin slices of stale bread
- salt
- butter
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Toast well, but not too brown, two thin slices of stale bread
2. Put them on a warm plate, sprinkle with a pinch of salt, and pour upon them some boiling water
3. Quickly cover with another dish of the same size, and drain off the water
4. Put a very small bit of butter on the toast and serve at once while hot
