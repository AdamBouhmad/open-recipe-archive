## Ingredients

- perch
- salt
- flour
- lard or fresh beef-dripping
- melted butter
- soy or catchup

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and dry them with a cloth
2. Lay them, side by side, on a board or large dish
3. Sprinkle them with salt, and dredge them with flour
4. After a while turn them, and salt and dredge the other side
5. Put some lard or fresh beef-dripping into a frying-pan, and hold it over the fire
6. When the lard boils, put in the fish and fry them of a yellowish brown
7. Skim the lard or dripping always before you put in the fish
8. After they have fried five minutes on one side, turn them and fry them five minutes on the other
9. Send to table with them in a boat, melted butter flavoured with soy or catchup
