## Ingredients

- neck chine
- salt
- pint of water
- sweet potatos

## Directions

1. Take the neck chine, rub it well with salt
2. Lay it in a pan, put it in a pint of water, and fill it up with sweet potatos nicely washed, but not peeled
3. Cover it close and bake it till done
4. Serve it up with the potatos, put a little of the gravy in the dish
