## Ingredients

- canned mushrooms
- cream
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Open the can and pour off every drop of the liquid found there
2. Let the mushrooms drain
3. Put them in a saucepan with a little cream and butter, pepper and salt
4. Let them simmer gently for from five to ten minutes
5. When the meat is on the platter pour the mushrooms over it
