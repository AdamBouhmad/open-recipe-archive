## Ingredients

- Pickled honeycomb tripe
- Boiling water
- Cold water
- Milk
- Salt
- Pepper
- Melted butter
- Batter
- Deep fat
- Brown paper
- Lemon slices
- Chili Sauce

## Directions

1. Cut pickled honeycomb tripe in pieces for serving
2. Wash, cover with boiling water, and simmer gently twenty minutes
3. Drain, and again cover, using equal parts cold water and milk
4. Heat to boiling-point, again drain
5. Wipe as dry as possible
6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
7. Brush over with melted butter
8. Dip in batter
9. Fry in deep fat and drain on brown paper
10. Serve with slices of lemon and Chili Sauce
