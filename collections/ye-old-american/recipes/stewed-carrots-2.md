## Ingredients

- carrots
- water
- salt
- butter (two tablespoons)
- flour
- pepper
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the carrots and divide them into strips
2. Put them into a stewpan with water enough to cover them
3. Add a spoonful of salt
4. Let them boil slowly until tender
5. Drain and replace them in the pan
6. Add two tablespoons of butter rolled in flour
7. Shake over a little pepper and salt
8. Add enough cream or milk to moisten the whole
9. Let it come to a boil and serve hot
