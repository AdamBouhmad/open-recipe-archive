## Ingredients

- eggs
- lard
- butter

## Directions

1. Break the eggs, one at a time, into a saucer, and then slide them carefully off into a frying pan of lard and butter mixed
2. Dip over the eggs the hot grease in spoonfuls, or turn them over, frying both sides without breaking them
3. They require about three minutes' cooking
