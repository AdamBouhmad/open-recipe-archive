## Ingredients

- three or four good-sized parsnips
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- sifted flour
- boiling drippings or lard

## Directions

1. Boil parsnips until tender
2. Mash and season with a little butter, a pinch of salt and a slight sprinkling of pepper
3. Have ready a plate with some sifted flour on it
4. Drop a tablespoonful of the parsnip in the flour and roll it about until well coated and formed into a ball
5. When you have a sufficient number ready, drop them into boiling drippings or lard, as you would a fritter
6. Fry a delicate brown and serve hot
7. Do not put them in a covered dish, for that would steam them and deprive them of their crispness
