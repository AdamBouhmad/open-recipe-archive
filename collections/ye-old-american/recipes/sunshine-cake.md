## Ingredients

- Whites 10 eggs
- 1½ cups powdered sugar
- Yolks 6 eggs
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Beat whites of eggs until stiff and dry, add sugar gradually, and continue beating
2. Add yolks of eggs beaten until thick and lemon-colored, and extract
3. Cut and fold in flour mixed and sifted with cream of tartar
4. Bake fifty minutes in a moderate oven in an angel-cake pan
