## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold roast turkey, cut in small dice
- ⅓ cup soft stale bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup stock obtained by cooking bones and skin of a roast turkey
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion juice
- Small pieces of toast
- Poached eggs
- Toast points

## Directions

1. Add bread crumbs to turkey
2. Make one cup sauce using butter, flour, and stock
3. Season with salt, pepper, and onion juice
4. Heat turkey and bread crumbs in sauce
5. Serve on small pieces of toast and garnish with poached eggs and toast points
