## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Strip off the coarse outer leaves
2. Cut off the stalks close to the bottom
3. Wash the artichokes well
4. Let them lie two or three hours in cold water
5. Put them with their heads downward into a pot of boiling water, keeping them down by a plate floated over them
6. Boil steadily from two to three hours, replenishing the pot with additional boiling water as needed
7. When tender all through, drain them
8. Serve up with melted butter
