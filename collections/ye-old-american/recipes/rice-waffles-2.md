## Ingredients

- 1¾ cups flour
- ⅔ cup cold cooked rice
- 1½ cups milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients
2. Work in rice with tips of fingers
3. Add milk, yolk of egg well beaten, butter, and white of egg beaten stiff
4. Cook same as Waffles
