## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- ¾ lb. butter
- 1 egg; use the yolk only
- Ice-water

## Directions

1. Chop half the butter into the flour.
2. Stir the beaten egg into half a cup ice-water, and work the flour into a stiff dough.
3. Roll out thin, baste with one-third the remaining butter, fold closely, roll out again, and so on until the butter is used up.
4. Roll very thin, and set the last folded roll in a very cold place ten or fifteen minutes before making out the crust.
5. Wash with beaten egg while hot.
