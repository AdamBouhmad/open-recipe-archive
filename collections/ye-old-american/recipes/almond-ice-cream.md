## Ingredients

- 6 ounces of bitter almonds
- rose water
- 1 pint of cream
- 1 pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- 3 pints of cream

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, and pound them in a mortar, adding by degrees a little rose water.
2. Boil them gently in a pint of cream till you find that it is highly flavoured with them.
3. Pour the cream into a bowl, stir in a pound of powdered loaf-sugar, cover it, and set it away to cool gradually.
4. When it is cold, strain it, and then stir it gradually and hard into three pints of cream.
5. Put it into the freezer, and proceed as directed in the first ice cream receipt.
6. Freeze it twice.
