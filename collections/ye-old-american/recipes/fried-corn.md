## Ingredients

- corn
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- rich cream

## Directions

1. Cut the corn off the cob, taking care not to bring off any of the husk with it and to have the grains as separate as possible.
2. Fry in a little butter--just enough to keep it from sticking to the pan; stir very often.
3. When nicely browned, add salt and pepper and a little rich cream.
4. Do not set it near the stove after the cream is added, as it will be apt to turn.
