## Ingredients

- Florida oranges
- lemons
- sugar
- water
- gelatine

## Directions

1. Grate the yellow rind of two Florida oranges and two lemons
2. Squeeze the juice into a porcelain-lined preserving kettle, adding the juice of two more oranges
3. Remove all the seeds
4. Put in the grated rind a quarter of a pound of sugar, or more if the fruit is sour, and a gill of water
5. Boil these ingredients together until a rich syrup is formed
6. Dissolve two ounces of gelatine in a quart of warm water, stirring it over the fire until it is entirely dissolved
7. Add the syrup and strain the jelly
8. Cool it in molds wet in cold water
