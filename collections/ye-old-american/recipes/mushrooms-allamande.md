## Ingredients

- mushroom caps
- butter
- crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Clean mushroom caps and sauté in butter.
2. Put together in pairs, cover with Allamande Sauce, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again, fry in deep fat, and drain on brown paper.
