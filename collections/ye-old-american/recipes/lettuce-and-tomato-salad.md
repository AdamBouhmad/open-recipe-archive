## Ingredients

- Three tomatoes
- Lettuce leaves
- Mayonnaise Dressing

## Directions

1. Peel and chill three tomatoes.
2. Cut in halves crosswise, arrange each half on a lettuce leaf.
3. Garnish with Mayonnaise Dressing forced through a pastry bag and tube.
4. If tomatoes are small, cut in quarters, and allow one tomato to each lettuce leaf.
