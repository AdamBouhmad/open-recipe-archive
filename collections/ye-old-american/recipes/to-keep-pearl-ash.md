## Ingredients

- three ounces of pearl-ash
- a pint and a half of soft water

## Directions

1. Take three ounces of pearl-ash, and put it into a clean black bottle with a pint and a half of soft water
2. Cork it very tightly, shake it, and it will be fit for use as soon as all the pearl-ash is dissolved
