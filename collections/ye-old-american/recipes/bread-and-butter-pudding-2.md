## Ingredients

- 1 small baker's stale loaf
- Butter
- 3 eggs
- ½ cup sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 quart milk

## Directions

1. Remove end crusts from bread, cut loaf in one-half inch slices
2. Spread each slice generously with butter
3. Arrange in buttered pudding-dish, buttered side down
4. Beat eggs slightly, add sugar, salt, and milk
5. Strain, and pour over bread
6. Let stand thirty minutes
7. Bake one hour in slow oven, covering the first half-hour of baking
8. The top of pudding should be well browned
9. Serve with Hard or Creamy Sauce
10. Three-fourths cup raisins, parboiled in boiling water to cover and seeded, may be sprinkled between layers of bread
