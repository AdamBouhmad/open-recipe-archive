## Ingredients

- 1 quart cream
- 2 cups milk
- 1⅓ cups sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon flour
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 1½ tablespoons vanilla

## Directions

1. Prepare same as Vanilla Ice Cream II, using one-half sugar in custard; remaining half caramelize, and add slowly to hot custard.
