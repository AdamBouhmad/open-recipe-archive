## Ingredients

- 2½ cups flour
- ½ cup cold cooked rice
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1½ cups milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients.
2. Work in rice with tips of fingers; add egg well beaten, milk, and butter.
3. Cook same as other griddle-cakes.
