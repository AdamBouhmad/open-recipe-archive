## Ingredients

- one pound of cooked salmon
- one cup of cream
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- one tablespoonful of flour
- three eggs
- one pint of crumbs
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop the salmon fine
2. Mix the flour and butter together
3. Let the cream come to a boil, and stir in the flour and butter, salmon and seasoning
4. Boil one minute
5. Stir in one well-beaten egg, and remove from the fire
6. When cold make into croquettes
7. Dip in beaten egg, roll in crumbs and fry
