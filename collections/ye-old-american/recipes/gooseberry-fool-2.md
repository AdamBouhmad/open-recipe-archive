## Ingredients

- two quarts of stewed gooseberries
- one quart of rich boiling milk
- nutmeg
- beaten yolks of two or three eggs

## Directions

1. Having stewed two quarts of gooseberries, stir them as soon as they are cold into a quart of rich boiling milk.
2. Grate in a nutmeg, and covering the pan, let the gooseberries simmer in the milk for five minutes.
3. Stir in the beaten yolks of two or three eggs, and immediately remove it from the fire.
4. Keep on the cover a few minutes longer, then turn out the mixture into a deep dish or a glass bowl, and set it away to get cold before it goes to table.
5. Eat it with sponge-cake.
