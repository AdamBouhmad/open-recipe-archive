## Ingredients

- twelve small beets
- one-half cup sugar
- one-half tablespoon corn-starch
- one-half cup vinegar
- two tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Wash twelve small beets, cook in boiling water until soft, remove skins, and cut beets in thin slices, small cubes, or fancy shapes, using French vegetable cutter.
2. Mix one-half cup sugar and one-half tablespoon corn-starch.
3. Add one-half cup vinegar and let boil five minutes.
4. Pour over beets, and let stand on back of range one-half hour.
5. Just before serving add two tablespoons butter.
