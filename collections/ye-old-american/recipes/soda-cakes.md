## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- a pint of milk
- a tea-spoonful of soda
- two pounds of flour
- half a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Dissolve half a pound of sugar in a pint of milk
2. add a tea-spoonful of soda
3. pour it on two pounds of flour
4. melt half a pound of butter
5. knead all together till light
6. put it in shallow moulds
7. bake it quickly in a brisk oven
