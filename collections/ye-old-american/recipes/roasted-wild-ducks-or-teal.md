## Ingredients

- wild ducks or teal
- small onion
- pepper
- salt
- spoonful of red wine
- necks and gizzards
- half an anchovy
- blade or two of mace
- cayenne pepper
- onion sauce
- raspings of bread

## Directions

1. When the ducks are ready dressed, put in them a small onion, pepper, salt, and a spoonful of red wine
2. If the fire be good, they will roast in twenty minutes
3. Make gravy of the necks and gizzards, a spoonful of red wine, half an anchovy, a blade or two of mace, one onion, and a little cayenne pepper
4. Boil it till it is wasted to half a pint
5. Strain it through a hair sieve
6. Pour it on the ducks
7. Serve them up with onion sauce in a boat
8. Garnish the dish with raspings of bread
