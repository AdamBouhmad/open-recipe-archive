## Ingredients

- two or three pounds lean fresh pork
- puff-paste
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- mace
- juicy apples
- white sugar
- sweet cider
- wine
- butter
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Cut two or three pounds of lean fresh pork into strips as long and as wide as your middle finger.
2. Line a buttered dish with puff-paste.
3. Put in a layer of pork seasoned with pepper, salt, and nutmeg or mace.
4. Next add a layer of juicy apples, sliced and covered with about an ounce of white sugar.
5. Alternate more pork and apples until ready for the paste cover.
6. Pour in half a pint of sweet cider or wine and stick bits of butter all over the top.
7. Cover with a thick lid of puff-paste, cut a slit in the top, and brush over with beaten egg.
8. Bake an hour and a half.
