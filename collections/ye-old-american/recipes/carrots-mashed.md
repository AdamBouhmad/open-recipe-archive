## Ingredients

- carrots
- boiling water
- salt
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Scrape and wash the carrots
2. Cook them tender in boiling water salted slightly
3. Drain well and mash them
4. Work in a good piece of butter
5. Season with pepper and salt
6. Heap up on a vegetable dish and serve hot
