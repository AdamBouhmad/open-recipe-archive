## Ingredients

- 1 quart prepared flour
- 2½ tablespoonfuls lard and butter mixed
- 2 cups of milk, or enough to make a soft dough
- damson, cherry, or other tart preserve

## Directions

1. Roll out a quarter of an inch thick
2. Cut into oblong pieces, rounded at the corners
3. Put a great spoonful damson, cherry, or other tart preserve in the middle and roll into a dumpling
4. Bake three-quarters of an hour
5. Brush over with beaten egg while hot
6. Set back in the oven three minutes to glaze
7. Eat hot with brandy or wine sauce
