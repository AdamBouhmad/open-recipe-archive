## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped cooked mutton
- Yolks 6 hard-boiled eggs
- ¾ teaspoon mixed mustard
- Salt
- Cayenne
- 1 cup of cream
- ¼ cup wine

## Directions

1. Mash the yolks, and season with mustard, salt, and cayenne.
2. Add cream and mutton.
3. When thoroughly heated add wine.
4. Serve on toast.
