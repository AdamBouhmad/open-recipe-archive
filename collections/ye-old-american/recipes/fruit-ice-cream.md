## Ingredients

- fruit juice
- cream
- sugar
- pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Let the fruit be well ripened
2. Pick it off the stalks and put it into a large earthen pan
3. Stir it about with a wooden spoon, breaking it until it is well mashed
4. Rub it through a hair-sieve with the back of the spoon
5. Sweeten it nicely with pounded sugar
6. Whip the cream for a few minutes
7. Add it to the fruit and whisk the whole again for another five minutes
8. Put the mixture into the freezer and freeze
