## Ingredients

- medium-sized potatoes
- small wooden skewers
- butter or some other fat

## Directions

1. Wash and pare medium-sized potatoes
2. Cut lengthwise in one-fourth inch slices, and fasten in fan shapes, with small wooden skewers, allowing five slices of potato to each skewer
3. Parboil ten minutes
4. drain, then place in a dripping-pan, and bake in a hot oven until soft, basting every three minutes with butter or some other fat
