## Ingredients

- One cup chopped cold cooked fowl
- Salt
- Celery salt
- Cayenne
- Lemon juice
- Onion juice
- Sauce
- One pint selected oysters

## Directions

1. Season chopped fowl with salt, celery salt, cayenne, lemon juice, and onion juice; moisten with sauce, and cool.
2. Parboil oysters, drain, and cover each oyster with chicken mixture.
3. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs; fry in deep fat, and drain.
