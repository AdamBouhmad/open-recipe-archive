## Ingredients

- a pound of bacon
- a large onion chopped small
- some pepper and salt
- a few blades of mace
- a handful of parsley, cut up very fine
- two quarts of water

## Directions

1. Boil it gently for three hours
2. tie up a small bunch of thyme
3. and let it boil in it half an hour
4. then take it out
5. Thicken your soup with a large spoonful of butter rubbed into two of flour
6. the yelks of two eggs
7. and half a pint of milk
8. Be careful not to let it curdle in the soup
