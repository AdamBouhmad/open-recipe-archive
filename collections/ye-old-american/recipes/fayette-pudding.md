## Ingredients

- one loaf of bread
- sugar
- nutmeg
- butter
- one quart of good boiled custard sweetened

## Directions

1. Slice a loaf of bread tolerably thick
2. lay the slices in the bottom of a dish, cutting them so as to cover it completely
3. sprinkle some sugar and nutmeg, with a little butter, on each layer
4. when all are in, pour on a quart of good boiled custard sweetened
5. serve it up cold
