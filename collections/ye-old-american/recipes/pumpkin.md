## Ingredients

- pumpkin
- water
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut it in half and take out all the seeds.
2. Then cut it in thick slices, and pare them.
3. Put it into a pot with a very little water, and stew it gently for an hour, or till soft enough to mash.
4. Then take it out, drain, and squeeze it till it is as dry as you can get it.
5. Afterwards mash it, adding a little pepper and salt, and a very little butter.
