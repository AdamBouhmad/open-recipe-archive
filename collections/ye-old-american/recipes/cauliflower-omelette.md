## Ingredients

- cold cauliflower
- eggs
- butter
- milk
- cayenne pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop some cold cauliflower very fine
2. Mix in when your omelette is ready to go into the pan
3. Season highly with cayenne pepper and salt
