## Ingredients

- ½ cup sugar
- ½ cup chopped blanched filberts
- ¾ cup hot caramel syrup
- Yolks 4 eggs
- 1⅓ cups heavy cream
- ½ tablespoon vanilla
- Few grains salt

## Directions

1. Caramelize sugar
2. Add nut meats, turn into a buttered pan
3. Cool, then pound in mortar and put through a purée strainer
4. Beat egg yolks until thick
5. Add gradually caramel syrup
6. Cook in double boiler until mixture thickens
7. Beat until cold
8. Fold in cream beaten until stiff
9. Add prepared nut meats, vanilla, and salt
10. Line melon mould with ice
11. Turn in mixture
12. Pack in salt and ice
13. Let stand three hours
