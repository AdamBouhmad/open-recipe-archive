## Ingredients

- 2 calves' feet
- 2 quarts cold water
- 1 egg, beaten up with two tablespoonfuls milk for each cupful of broth
- Pepper and salt
- thin, crisp toast
- lemon-juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil the feet to shreds
2. Strain the liquor through a double muslin bag
3. Season to taste
4. Set by for use as needed
5. Warm by the small quantity, allowing to each cupful a beaten egg and two tablespoonfuls of milk
6. Give a good boil up to cook these
7. Serve with thin, crisp toast
8. If the patient can take it, a dash of lemon-juice improves the broth
