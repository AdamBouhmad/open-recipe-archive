## Ingredients

- half a pint of milk
- two well-beaten eggs
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. To half a pint of milk put two well-beaten eggs, and as much wheat flour as will make a smooth, rather thick batter free from lumps.
2. Drop this batter, a tablespoonful at a time, into boiling soup.
