## Ingredients

- 1 quart of flour
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- ½ teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- 2 cups sweet milk

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the flour, or cut it up with a knife or chopper, as you do in pastry
2. Add the salt, milk, and soda, mixing well
3. Work into a ball
4. Lay upon a floured board
5. Beat with a rolling-pin half an hour, turning and shifting the mass often
6. Roll into an even sheet, a quarter of an inch thick, or less
7. Prick deeply with a fork
8. Bake hard in a moderate oven
9. Hang them up in a muslin bag in the kitchen for two days to dry
