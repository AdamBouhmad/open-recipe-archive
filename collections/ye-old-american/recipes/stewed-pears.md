## Ingredients

- small ripe pears
- water
- sugar
- ginger or cloves

## Directions

1. If small and ripe, cut out the blossom-end, without paring or coring
2. Put into a saucepan, with enough water to cover them, and stew until tender
3. Add a half cupful of sugar for every quart of pears, and stew all together ten minutes
4. Take out the pears, lay in a covered bowl to keep warm
5. Add to the syrup a little ginger or a few cloves, boil fifteen minutes longer, and pour over the fruit hot
