## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 1 quart cream
- 6 eggs, and three cups of sugar beaten up with the yolks
- 1 pint fresh peaches, cut up small, or fresh ripe berries

## Directions

1. Heat the quart of milk almost to boiling, and add gradually to the beaten yolks and sugar
2. Whip in the frothed whites, return to the custard-kettle, and stir until it is a thick, soft custard
3. Let it get perfectly cold, beat in the cream and freeze
4. If you let it freeze itself, stir in the fruit after the second beating; if you turn the freezer, when the custard is like congealed mush
