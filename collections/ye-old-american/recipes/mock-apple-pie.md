## Ingredients

- one large Boston cracker
- one teacupful of cold water
- one teacupful of fine white sugar
- the juice and pulp of one lemon
- half a lemon rind
- a little nutmeg
- half puff paste

## Directions

1. Crush finely with a rolling pin, one large Boston cracker
2. Put it into a bowl and pour upon it one teacupful of cold water
3. Add one teacupful of fine white sugar, the juice and pulp of one lemon, half a lemon rind grated and a little nutmeg
4. Line the pie-plate with half puff paste
5. Pour in the mixture, cover with the paste and bake half an hour
