## Ingredients

- a pound of shelled sweet almonds
- a quarter of a pound of shelled bitter almonds
- rose water
- a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- whites of seven eggs
- a table-spoonful of mixed spice (nutmeg, mace, and cinnamon)
- white sugar for grating
- buttered white paper

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds in scalding water.
2. Mix the sweet and bitter almonds together, and pound them, one or two at a time, in a mortar to a very smooth paste, adding frequently a little rose water to prevent them from oiling and becoming heavy.
3. Prepare a pound of powdered loaf-sugar.
4. Beat the whites of seven eggs to a stiff froth.
5. Beat into the froth gradually the powdered sugar, adding a table-spoonful of mixed spice (nutmeg, mace, and cinnamon).
6. Mix in the pounded almonds.
7. Stir the whole very hard.
8. Form the mixture with a spoon into little round or oval cakes upon sheets of buttered white paper, and grate white sugar over each.
9. Lay the paper in square shallow pans or on iron sheets.
10. Bake the maccaroons a few minutes in a brisk oven, till of a pale brown.
11. When cold, take them off the papers.
