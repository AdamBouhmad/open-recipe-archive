## Ingredients

- Mashed Potatoes
- Butter
- Creamed meat or fish

## Directions

1. Place a buttered mould on platter, build around it a wall of hot Mashed Potatoes, three and one-half inches high by one inch deep, smooth, and crease with case knife.
2. Remove mould, fill with creamed meat or fish, and reheat in oven before serving.
