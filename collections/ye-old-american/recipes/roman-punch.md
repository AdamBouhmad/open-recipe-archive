## Ingredients

- four lemons
- two oranges
- two pounds of loaf-sugar
- a bottle of champagne
- the whites of eight eggs

## Directions

1. Grate the yellow rinds of four lemons and two oranges upon two pounds of loaf-sugar
2. Squeeze on the juice of the lemons and oranges
3. Cover it, and let it stand till next day
4. Strain it through a sieve
5. Add a bottle of champagne, and the whites of eight eggs beaten to a froth
6. You may freeze it or not
