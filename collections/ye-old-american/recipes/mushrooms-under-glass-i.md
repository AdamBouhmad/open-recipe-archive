## Ingredients

- toasted bread
- mushroom caps
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- hot cream

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of an individual baking-dish with circular pieces of toasted bread.
2. Arrange mushroom caps on toast, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dot over with butter, and pour over a small quantity of hot cream.
3. Cover, and bake twenty minutes.
