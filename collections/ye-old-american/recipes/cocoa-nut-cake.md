## Ingredients

- a cocoa-nut
- a pound of grated cocoa-nut
- a pound of loaf-sugar
- fifteen eggs
- a handful of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Cut up and wash a cocoa-nut, and grate as much of it as will weigh a pound
2. Powder a pound of loaf-sugar
3. Beat fifteen eggs very light; and then beat into them, gradually, the sugar
4. Add by degrees the cocoa-nut; and lastly, a handful of sifted flour
5. Stir the whole very hard, and bake it either in a large tin pan, or in little tins
6. The oven should be rather quick
