## Ingredients

- fish
- half a pint of vinegar
- pepper and salt
- parsley
- beet

## Directions

1. Secure the tail of the fish in its mouth, the body in a circle
2. Pour over it half a pint of vinegar, seasoned with pepper and salt
3. Let it stand an hour in a cool place
4. Pour off the vinegar, and put it in a steamer over boiling water, and steam twenty minutes, or longer for large fish
5. When the meat easily separates from the bone it is done
6. Drain well and serve on a very clean white napkin, neatly folded and placed on the platter
7. Decorate the napkin around the fish with sprigs of curled parsley, or with fanciful beet cuttings, or alternately with both
