## Ingredients

- String Beans
- salt
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove strings, and snap or cut in one-inch pieces
2. Wash, and cook in boiling water from one to three hours, adding salt last half-hour of cooking
3. Drain, season with butter and salt
