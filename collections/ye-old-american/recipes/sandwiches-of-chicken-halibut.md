## Ingredients

- chicken halibut
- Fish or Chicken Force-meat
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- one-fourth cup white wine
- parsley
- Tomato Jelly
- Hollandaise Sauce

## Directions

1. Cut chicken halibut in thin fillets
2. Put together in pairs, with Fish or Chicken Force-meat between
3. Dip fillets in melted butter seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Brush over with lemon juice
5. Place in shallow pan with one-fourth cup white wine
6. Bake twenty minutes in hot oven
7. Arrange on hot platter for serving
8. Sprinkle with finely chopped parsley
9. Garnish with Tomato Jelly
10. Serve with Hollandaise Sauce
