## Ingredients

- three quarts of flour
- three quarters of a pound of brown sugar
- a large spoonful of pounded ginger
- one tea-spoonful of powdered cloves
- half a pound of butter
- a quart of rich molasses

## Directions

1. Sift three quarts of flour, three quarters of a pound of brown sugar, a large spoonful of pounded ginger, one tea-spoonful of powdered cloves
2. Melt half a pound of butter in a quart of rich molasses
3. Wet the flour with it
4. Knead it well
5. Bake it in a slack oven
