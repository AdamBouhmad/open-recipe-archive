## Ingredients

- Lettuce
- Eggs
- Water
- Sweet oil
- Salt
- Mustard
- Powdered loaf-sugar
- Vinegar
- Red beet

## Directions

1. Strip off the outer leaves, wash the lettuce, split it in half, and lay it in cold water till dinner time
2. Drain it and put it into a salad dish
3. Boil two eggs hard for ten minutes and lay in a basin of cold water for five minutes to prevent the whites from turning blue
4. Cut them in half and lay them on the lettuce
5. Put the yolks of the eggs on a large plate and with a wooden spoon mash them smooth, mixing with them a table-spoonful of water and two table-spoonfuls of sweet oil
6. Add by degrees a salt-spoonful of salt, a tea-spoonful of mustard, and a tea-spoonful of powdered loaf-sugar
7. When these are all smoothly united, add very gradually three table-spoonfuls of vinegar
8. Cut the lettuce up fine on another plate, put it to the dressing, and mix it well
9. Decorate the top of the salad with slices of red beet and with the hard white of the eggs cut into rings
