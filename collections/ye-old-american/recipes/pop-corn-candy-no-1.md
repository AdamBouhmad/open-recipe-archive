## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of butter
- three tablespoonfuls of water
- one cupful of white sugar
- three quarts nicely popped corn

## Directions

1. Put into an iron kettle one tablespoonful of butter, three tablespoonfuls of water and one cupful of white sugar
2. Boil until ready to candy
3. Throw in three quarts nicely popped corn
4. Stir vigorously until the sugar is evenly distributed over the corn
5. Take the kettle from the fire and stir until it cools a little
6. Stir constantly to prevent scorching
