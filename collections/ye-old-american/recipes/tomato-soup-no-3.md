## Ingredients

- two quarts of tomatoes
- an onion
- other soup vegetables
- a level tablespoonful of flour
- a third of a cup of melted butter
- pepper and salt
- little squares of bread

## Directions

1. Peel two quarts of tomatoes.
2. Boil them in a saucepan with an onion, and other soup vegetables.
3. Strain.
4. Add a level tablespoonful of flour dissolved in a third of a cup of melted butter.
5. Add pepper and salt.
6. Fry little squares of bread brown and crisp in butter.
7. Serve very hot over the fried bread.
