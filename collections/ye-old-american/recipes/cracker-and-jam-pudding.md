## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- ½ cup cracker-crumbs
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 teacup milk
- ½ lemon—juice and grated rind
- 3 tablespoonfuls jam

## Directions

1. Soak the cracker in the milk; rub the butter and sugar together, adding the lemon, and beating to a cream; then stir in the beaten yolks; next the cracker and milk; lastly, the whites.
2. Butter a deep dish, and put the jam, which should be pretty stiff, at the bottom.
3. Fill up with the mixture, and bake about half an hour.
4. Eat cold, with sugar sifted over the top.
