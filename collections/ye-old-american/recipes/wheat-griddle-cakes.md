## Ingredients

- Three cups of flour
- One teaspoonful of salt
- Three teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- Three eggs
- Three cupfuls of sweet milk
- A tablespoonful of melted butter

## Directions

1. Sift together three cups of flour, one teaspoonful of salt, three teaspoonfuls of baking powder
2. Beat three eggs and add to three cupfuls of sweet milk
3. Add a tablespoonful of melted butter
4. Mix all into a smooth batter, as thick as will run in a stream from the lips of a pitcher
5. Bake on a well-greased, hot griddle, a nice light brown
