## Ingredients

- a quart of bran
- a handful of wheat flour
- a level tea-spoonful of salt
- milk-warm water
- two table-spoonfuls of brewer's yeast or three if home-made
- a salt-spoonful of soda
- warm water

## Directions

1. Mix a quart of bran with a handful of wheat flour, and a level tea-spoonful of salt
2. Pour in sufficient milk-warm water to make a thick batter
3. Add two table-spoonfuls of brewer's yeast, or three, if home-made
4. Stir it very hard
5. Cover it, and set it by the fire to rise
6. Half an hour before you begin to bake, you may add a salt-spoonful of soda, melted in a little warm water
7. Bake it like buckwheat cakes, on a griddle
