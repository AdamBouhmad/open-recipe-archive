## Ingredients

- four large ripe lemons
- cold water
- sugar
- twelve eggs
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take four large ripe lemons, and roll them under your hand on the table to increase the juice
2. Squeeze them into a bowl, and mix with the juice a very small tea-cup full of cold water
3. Use none of the peel
4. Add gradually sufficient sugar to make it very sweet
5. Beat twelve eggs till quite light, and then stir the lemon juice gradually into them, beating very hard at the last
6. Put the mixture into cups, and bake it ten minutes
7. When done, grate nutmeg over the top of each, and set them among ice, or in a very cold place
