## Ingredients

- a dozen blades of mace
- half a dozen pepper-corns
- water
- three quarters of a pound of butter
- flour
- a cold boiled lobster
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Boil a dozen blades of mace and half a dozen pepper-corns in about a jill and a half of water, till all the strength of the spice is extracted
2. Strain it
3. Cut three quarters of a pound of butter into little bits and melt it in this water, dredging in a little flour as you hold it over the fire to boil
4. Toss it round and let it just boil up and no more
5. Take a cold boiled lobster and pound the coral in a mortar, adding a little sweet oil
6. Stir it into the melted butter
7. Chop the meat of the body into very small pieces and rub it through a cullender into the butter
8. Cut up the flesh of the claws and tail into dice and stir it in
9. Give it another boil up and it will be ready for table
