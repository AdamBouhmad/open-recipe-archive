## Ingredients

- cold roast fowl
- butter
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- pounded mace
- nutmeg
- ham
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Strip the meat from the bones of a cold roast fowl.
2. To every pound of meat allow a quarter of a pound of butter, salt and cayenne pepper to taste; one teaspoonful of pounded mace, half a small nutmeg.
3. Cut the meat into small pieces, pound it well with the butter, sprinkle in the spices gradually and keep pounding until reduced to a perfectly smooth paste.
4. Pack it into small jars and cover with clarified butter, about a quarter of an inch in thickness.
5. Two or three slices of ham minced and pounded with the above will be an improvement.
6. Keep in a dry place.
