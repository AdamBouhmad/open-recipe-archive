## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- cream

## Directions

1. Set a common white dish into the oven until quite hot, large enough to hold the number of eggs to be cooked, allowing plenty of room for each
2. Melt a small piece of butter in it
3. Breaking the eggs carefully in a saucer, one at a time, slip them into the hot dish
4. Sprinkle over them a small quantity of pepper and salt
5. Allow them to cook four or five minutes
6. Add a tablespoonful of cream for every two eggs when the eggs are first slipped in
