## Ingredients

- 1½ cups sugar
- 4 cups water
- 1 quart sweet cider
- 2 cups orange juice
- ½ cup lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make a syrup by boiling sugar and water twenty minutes.
2. Add cider, orange juice, and lemon juice.
3. Cool, strain, and freeze to a mush.
