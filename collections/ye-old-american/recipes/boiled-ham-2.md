## Ingredients

- ham
- sugar
- fine cracker crumbs
- cloves

## Directions

1. Soak several hours or over night in cold water to cover.
2. Wash thoroughly, trim off hard skin near end of bone, put in a kettle, cover with cold water, heat to boiling-point, and cook slowly until tender.
3. Remove kettle from range and set aside, that ham may partially cool; then take from water, remove outside skin, sprinkle with sugar and fine cracker crumbs, and stick with cloves one-half inch apart.
4. Bake one hour in a slow oven.
5. Serve cold, thinly sliced.
