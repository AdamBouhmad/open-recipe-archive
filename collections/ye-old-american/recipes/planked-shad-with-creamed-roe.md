## Ingredients

- roe shad
- salt
- acidulated water
- three tablespoons butter
- one teaspoon finely chopped shallot
- one and one-half tablespoons flour
- one-third cup cream
- two egg yolks
- pepper
- lemon juice
- buttered crumbs
- mashed potatoes
- small tomatoes
- lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Select and prepare the roe shad as Planked Shad.
2. Parboil roe in salted, acidulated water twenty minutes.
3. Remove outside membrane, and mash.
4. Melt three tablespoons butter, add one teaspoon finely chopped shallot, and cook five minutes; add roe, sprinkle with one and one-half tablespoons flour, and pour on gradually one-third cup cream.
5. Cook slowly five minutes, add two egg yolks and season highly with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
6. Remove shad from oven, spread thin part with roe mixture, cover with buttered crumbs, and return to oven to brown crumbs.
7. Garnish with mashed potatoes forced through a pastry bag and tube, small tomatoes, slices of lemon and parsley.
