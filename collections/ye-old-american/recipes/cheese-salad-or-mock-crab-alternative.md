## Ingredients

- ½ lb. old cheese, grated
- 1 hard-boiled egg
- ½ teaspoonful cayenne
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful made mustard
- 1 tablespoonful onion vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful salad oil

## Directions

1. Rub the yolk of the egg to a paste with the oil, adding in order the salt, pepper, sugar, and mustard, lastly the cheese.
2. Work all well together before putting in the vinegar.
3. Serve in a crab-shell.
