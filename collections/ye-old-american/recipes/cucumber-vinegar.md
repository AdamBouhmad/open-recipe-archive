## Ingredients

- Ten large cucumbers, or twelve smaller ones
- one quart of vinegar
- two onions
- two shallots
- one tablespoonful of salt
- two tablespoonfuls of pepper
- a quarter of a teaspoonful of cayenne

## Directions

1. Pare and slice the cucumbers, put them in a stone jar, or wide-mouthed bottle, with the vinegar
2. Slice the onions and shallots, and add them, with all the other ingredients, to the cucumbers
3. Let it stand four or five days
4. Boil it all up, and when cold, strain the liquor through a piece of muslin, and store it away in small bottles well sealed
