## Ingredients

- two or three large handfuls of the fresh-gathered leaves of the Jamestown weed
- lard

## Directions

1. pound the fresh-gathered leaves of the Jamestown weed in a mortar till you have extracted the juice
2. put the juice into a tin sauce-pan, mixed with sufficient lard to make a thick salve
3. Stew them together half an hour, and then put the mixture into gallipots and cover it closely
