## Ingredients

- sardines
- hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- cayenne
- lemon juice
- olive oil or melted butter
- thin slices of buttered bread

## Directions

1. Remove skin and bones from sardines, and mash to a paste.
2. Add to an equal quantity of yolks of hard-boiled eggs rubbed through a sieve.
3. Season with salt, cayenne, and a few drops of lemon juice.
4. Moisten with olive oil or melted butter.
5. Spread mixture between thin slices of buttered bread prepared as for Bread and Butter Folds.
