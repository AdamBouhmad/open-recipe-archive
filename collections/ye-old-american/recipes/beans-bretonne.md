## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups pea beans
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Soak pea beans over night in cold water to cover
2. Drain and parboil until soft
3. Again drain, put in earthen-ware dish or bean-pot
4. Add tomato sauce, cover, and cook until beans have nearly absorbed sauce
