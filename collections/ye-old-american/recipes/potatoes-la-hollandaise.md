## Ingredients

- potatoes
- White Stock
- one-third cup butter
- one tablespoon lemon juice
- one-half teaspoon salt
- few grains of cayenne
- one-half tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Mash, pare, soak, and cut potatoes in one-fourth inch slices
2. shape with French vegetable cutters; or cut in one-half inch cubes
3. Cover three cups potato with White Stock
4. cook until soft, and drain
5. Cream one-third cup butter
6. add one tablespoon lemon juice, one-half teaspoon salt, and few grains of cayenne
7. Add to potatoes
8. cook three minutes
9. add one-half tablespoon finely chopped parsley
