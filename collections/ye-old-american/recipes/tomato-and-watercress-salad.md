## Ingredients

- large tomatoes
- watercress
- French Dressing

## Directions

1. Peel and chill large tomatoes, cut in slices one-third inch thick, and slices in strips one-third inch wide
2. Arrange on a flat dish to represent lattice work
3. Fill in the spaces with watercress
4. Serve with French Dressing
