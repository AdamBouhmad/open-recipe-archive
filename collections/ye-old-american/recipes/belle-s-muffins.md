## Ingredients

- 3 pints of flour
- 1 quart of milk
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls cream tartar
- 1 tablespoonful soda
- 1 tablespoonful salt

## Directions

1. Sift the cream tartar with the flour
2. Beat the eggs very light
3. Dissolve the soda in hot water
4. Bake in rings in a quick oven
