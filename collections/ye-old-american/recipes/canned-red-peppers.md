## Ingredients

- 1 peck red peppers
- boiling water
- ice-water
- 1 quart vinegar
- 2 cups sugar

## Directions

1. Wash one peck red peppers, cut a slice from stem end of each, and remove seeds; then cut in thin strips by working around and around the peppers, using scissors or a sharp vegetable knife.
2. Cover with boiling water, let stand two minutes, drain, and plunge into ice-water.
3. Let stand ten minutes, again drain, and pack solidly into pint glass jars.
4. Boil one quart vinegar and two cups sugar fifteen minutes.
5. Pour over peppers to overflow jars, cover, and keep in a cold place.
