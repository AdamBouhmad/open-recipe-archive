## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- ice
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- oil
- onion

## Directions

1. Pare cucumbers neatly from end to end and lay in ice-water one hour.
2. Wipe them and slice thin.
3. Season with pepper, salt and vinegar—and oil, if you wish—laying some bits of ice among them, with thin slices of onion.
