## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful Indian meal
- 1 tablespoonful white flour
- 2 cups boiling water
- 2 cups milk
- A good pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Boil the paste in the hot water twenty minutes; add the milk and cook ten minutes more, stirring often.
2. Eat with sugar and milk, stirred in while hot.
