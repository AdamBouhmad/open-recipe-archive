## Ingredients

- sour apples
- rich biscuit dough or raised biscuit dough

## Directions

1. Peel, core and slice sour apples
2. Make a rich biscuit dough, or raised biscuit dough may be used if rolled thinner
3. Roll not quite half an inch thick
4. Lay the slices on the paste
5. Roll up and tuck in the ends
6. Prick deeply with a fork
7. Lay it in a steamer and steam hard for an hour and three-quarters
8. Or wrap it in a pudding-cloth well floured, tie the ends, baste up the sides, plunge into boiling water and boil continually an hour and a half, perhaps more
