## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of raised dough
- two-thirds of a cup of butter
- two cups of sugar creamed together
- three eggs
- one even teaspoonful of soda
- two tablespoonfuls of milk
- half a nutmeg grated
- one tablespoonful of cinnamon
- one teaspoonful of cloves
- one cup of raisins
- beaten whites of eggs
- half a glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar into the raised dough
2. Add three well-beaten eggs
3. Dissolve soda in milk and add
4. Add grated nutmeg, cinnamon, and cloves
5. Add raisins and beaten whites of eggs
6. Beat all hard for several minutes
7. Put in buttered pans and let it stand half an hour to rise again before baking
8. Bake in a moderate oven
