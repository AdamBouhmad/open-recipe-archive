## Ingredients

- Six large onions
- Salt pork
- Five pounds of bass or cod
- Salt
- Black peppers
- Clove
- Thyme
- Parsley
- Tablespoonful tomato or mushroom catsup
- Six oysters
- Oyster crackers
- Milk
- Butter
- Water
- Capers
- Sliced lemon
- Oyster liquor

## Directions

1. Slice six large onions and fry them in the gravy of fried salt pork
2. Cut five pounds of bass or cod into strips three inches long and one thick
3. Line the bottom of a pot with fish
4. Scatter a few slices of onion upon them, salt, half a dozen whole black peppers, a clove or two, a pinch of thyme and one of parsley, a tablespoonful tomato or mushroom catsup, and six oysters
5. Add a layer of oyster crackers well-soaked in milk and buttered thickly
6. Add another layer of fish, onions, seasoning, and crackers, continuing until all are used up
7. Cover with water and boil slowly for an hour
8. Pour out and serve with capers and sliced lemon
9. Add a cup of oyster liquor to the chowder while boiling to improve it
