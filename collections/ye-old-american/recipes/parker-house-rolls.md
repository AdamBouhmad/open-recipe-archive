## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- one-half cupful of fresh yeast
- one tablespoonful of sugar
- one pinch of salt
- two quarts of sifted flour
- half of a teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Boil and cool milk
2. Melt butter in warm milk
3. Add sugar, salt, and flour
4. Let it rise over night and mix rather soft
5. In the morning add soda dissolved in a spoonful of water
6. Mix in enough flour to make the same stiffness as any biscuit dough
7. Roll out not more than a quarter of an inch thick
8. Cut with a large round cutter
9. Spread soft butter over the tops and fold one-half over the other
10. Place them apart a little so there will be room to rise
11. Cover and place them near the fire for fifteen or twenty minutes before baking
12. Bake in rather a quick oven
