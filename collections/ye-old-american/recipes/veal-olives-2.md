## Ingredients

- veal slices
- pepper
- salt
- oysters
- boiling water
- melted butter
- oyster-liquor
- browned flour
- tin or wooden skewers
- pack-thread
- cranberry jelly

## Directions

1. Season veal slices with pepper and salt
2. Spread chopped oysters over each slice, about three to a good-sized slice of veal
3. Roll them up carefully and closely
4. Pin each with two small tin or wooden skewers
5. Lay them in a dripping-pan
6. Dash a teacupful of boiling water over them
7. Roast, basting at least twice with melted butter
8. When brown, remove to a chafing-dish and cover
9. Add a little oyster-liquor to the gravy left in the dripping-pan
10. Let this simmer for three or four minutes
11. Thicken with a teaspoonful of browned flour and boil up at once
12. Withdraw the skewers cautiously
13. Pour the gravy over and around the olives and serve
14. Serve with cranberry jelly
