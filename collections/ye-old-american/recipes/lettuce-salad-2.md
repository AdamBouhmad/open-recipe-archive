## Ingredients

- yolks of three hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- mustard
- a dessertspoonful of olive oil or melted butter
- a teacupful of vinegar
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Take the yolks of three hard-boiled eggs, and salt and mustard to taste
2. Mash it fine
3. Make a paste by adding a dessertspoonful of olive oil or melted butter
4. Mix thoroughly, and then dilute by adding gradually a teacupful of vinegar
5. Pour over the lettuce
6. Garnish by slicing another egg and laying over the lettuce
