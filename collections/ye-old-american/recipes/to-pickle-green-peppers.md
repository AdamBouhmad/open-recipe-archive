## Ingredients

- bell peppers
- salt
- water
- cold vinegar
- alum

## Directions

1. Gather bell peppers when quite young
2. Slit one side, and carefully take out the core, so as not to injure the shell of the pepper
3. Put them into boiling salt and water, changing the water every day for one week, and keeping them closely covered in a warm place near the fire
4. Stir them several times a day
5. When they are a fine green put them into a jar, and pour cold vinegar over them, adding a small piece of alum
