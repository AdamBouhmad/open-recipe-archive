## Ingredients

- six large potatoes
- three pints of water
- a handful of hops
- flour
- half a cup of sugar
- half a tablespoonful of ginger
- two of salt
- a teacupful of yeast

## Directions

1. Tie a handful of hops in a small muslin bag
2. Boil six large potatoes in three pints of water with the hops
3. When thoroughly cooked drain the water on enough flour to make a thin batter
4. Set this on the stove or range and scald it enough to cook the flour
5. Remove it from the fire and when cool enough, add the potatoes mashed
6. Add half a cup of sugar, half a tablespoonful of ginger, two of salt and a teacupful of yeast
7. Let it stand in a warm place, until it has thoroughly risen
8. Put it in a large mouthed jug and cork tightly; set away in a cool place
9. The jug should be scalded before putting in the yeast
