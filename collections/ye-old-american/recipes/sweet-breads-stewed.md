## Ingredients

- sweet-breads
- water
- fat salt pork
- butter
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt
- cream
- wine

## Directions

1. Wash them, and remove all bits of skin and fatty matter.
2. Cover with cold water, and heat to a boil.
3. Pour off the hot water, and cover with cold until the sweet-breads are firm.
4. If you desire to have them very rich, lard as for frying before you put in the second water.
5. Stew in a very little water the second time.
6. When they are tender, add for each sweet-bread a heaping teaspoonful of butter, and a little chopped parsley, with pepper, and salt, and a little cream.
7. Let them simmer in this gravy for five minutes, then take them up.
8. Send to table in a covered dish, with the gravy poured over them.
9. If you lard the sweet-breads, substitute for the cream in the gravy a glass of good wine.
10. In this case, take the sweet-breads out before it is put into the gravy.
11. Boil up once and pour over them.
