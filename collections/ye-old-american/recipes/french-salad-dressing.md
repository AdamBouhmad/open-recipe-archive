## Ingredients

- one saltspoon of pepper
- one saltspoon of salt
- three tablespoonfuls of olive oil
- one tablespoonful of onion scraped fine
- one tablespoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix one saltspoon of pepper with one of salt
2. Add three tablespoonfuls of olive oil and one even tablespoonful of onion scraped fine
3. Then add one tablespoonful of vinegar
4. When well mixed, pour the mixture over your salad and stir all till well mingled
