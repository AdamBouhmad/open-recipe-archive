## Ingredients

- Three cupfuls of milk
- four eggs
- one cupful of sugar
- two cupfuls of thick stewed apples, strained through a colander
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks of the eggs lightly
2. mix the yolks well with the apples, flavoring with nutmeg
3. beat into this the milk
4. lastly, beat in the whites
5. Let the crust partly bake before turning in this filling
6. Bake with only the one crust, like all custard pies
