## Ingredients

- corn
- water
- milk
- butter
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- potatoes
- beef-dripping

## Directions

1. Cut corn from the cob and stew fifteen minutes in boiling water.
2. Turn off most of this water and cover with cold milk.
3. Stew until very tender, adding before you take it up a large lump of butter cut into bits and rolled in flour.
4. Season with pepper and salt to taste.
5. Boil five minutes and serve.
6. Cold corn left from dinner should be cut from the cob and stewed a few minutes in a little milk, adding seasoning as above.
7. Or, mix it with chopped cold potatoes—Irish or sweet; heat a piece of butter or beef-dripping in a frying-pan and stir in the mixture until smoking-hot.
