## Ingredients

- one quart of sour milk
- sifted flour
- two beaten eggs
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one tablespoonful of melted butter
- one level teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Make a batter of a quart of sour milk and as much sifted flour as is needed to thicken so that it will run from the dish
2. Add two beaten eggs, a teaspoonful of salt, a tablespoonful of melted butter, and a level teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little milk or cold water, added last
3. Bake on a hot griddle, well greased, brown on both sides
