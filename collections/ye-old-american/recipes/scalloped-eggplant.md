## Ingredients

- eggplant
- boiling water
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- buttered crumbs

## Directions

1. Pare an eggplant and cut in two-thirds inch cubes.
2. Cook in a small quantity of boiling water until soft, then drain.
3. Cook two tablespoons butter with one-half onion, finely chopped, until yellow, add three-fourths tablespoon finely chopped parsley and eggplant.
4. Turn into a buttered baking-dish.
5. Cover with buttered crumbs and bake until crumbs are brown.
