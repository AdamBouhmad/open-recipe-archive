## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- soft butter
- Anchovy essence
- thin slices of buttered bread

## Directions

1. Rub the yolks of hard-boiled eggs to a paste.
2. Moisten with soft butter and season with Anchovy essence.
3. Spread mixture between thin slices of buttered bread prepared as for Bread and Butter Folds.
