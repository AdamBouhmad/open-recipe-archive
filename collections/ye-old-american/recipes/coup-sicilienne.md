## Ingredients

- 1 shredded pineapple
- 3 oranges (pulp)
- 3 bananas sliced
- 2 tablespoons Maraschino
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Few grains salt
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients and sweeten to taste
2. Chill
3. Serve in champagne glasses having glasses two-thirds full
4. Cover fruit to fill glasses with Strawberry Ice II and garnish with strawberries and angelica
