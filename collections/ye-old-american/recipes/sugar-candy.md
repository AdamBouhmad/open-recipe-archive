## Ingredients

- 6 cups of sugar
- 1 cup of vinegar
- 1 cup of water
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 teaspoonful saleratus dissolved in hot water

## Directions

1. Boil fast without stirring, an hour, or until it crisps in cold water.
2. Pull white with the tips of your fingers.
3. Flavor to taste.
