## Ingredients

- eight selected smelts
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- Fish Force-meat
- white wine
- buttered paper
- lobster coral
- three tablespoons butter
- three tablespoons flour
- one and one-half cups cream
- one tablespoon meat extract
- cayenne
- one-half cup lobster dice
- Aurora Sauce

## Directions

1. Split and bone eight selected smelts.
2. Cut off tails, and from tail ends of fish turn meat over one inch onto flesh side.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and brush over with lemon juice.
4. Garnish with Fish Force-meat forced through a pastry bag and tube, and fasten heads with skewers to keep in an upright position.
5. Arrange in a buttered pan, and pour around white wine.
6. Cover with buttered paper, and bake from fifteen to twenty minutes.
7. Just before taking from oven, sprinkle with lobster coral forced through a strainer.
8. For Aurora Sauce: Melt three tablespoons butter, add three tablespoons flour, and pour on gradually one and one-half cups cream and one tablespoon meat extract.
9. Season with salt and cayenne, and add lobster coral and one-half cup lobster dice.
10. Serve with Aurora Sauce.
