## Ingredients

- acetate of potash
- sulphuric acid
- oil of lavender

## Directions

1. Put a portion of acetate of potash, (sal diureticus,) into a smelling bottle
2. Mix gradually with it half its weight of sulphuric acid, and add a few drops of oil of lavender
