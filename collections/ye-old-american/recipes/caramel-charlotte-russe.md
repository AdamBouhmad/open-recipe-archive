## Ingredients

- ¼ box gelatine or 1 tablespoon granulated gelatine
- ¼ cup cold water
- ½ cup scalded cream
- ⅓ cup sugar, caramelized
- ¼ cup powdered sugar
- 1½ teaspoons vanilla
- Whip from 3½ cups cream
- 6 lady fingers

## Directions

1. Make same as Charlotte Russe, adding caramelized sugar to scalded cream before putting into gelatine mixture
