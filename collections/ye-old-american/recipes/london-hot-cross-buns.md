## Ingredients

- three cups of milk
- one cup of yeast, or one cake of compressed yeast dissolved in a cup of tepid water
- flour
- half a cup of melted butter
- one cup of sugar
- half a nutmeg grated
- one saltspoonful of salt
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- white of egg
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Use flour enough to make a thick batter with milk and yeast
2. Set this as a sponge over night
3. In the morning add half a cup of melted butter, one cup of sugar, half a nutmeg grated, one saltspoonful of salt, and half a teaspoonful of soda
4. Add flour enough to roll out like biscuit
5. Knead well and set to rise for five hours
6. Roll the dough half an inch thick
7. Cut in round cakes and lay in rows in a buttered baking-pan
8. Let the cakes stand half an hour, or until light
9. Put them in the oven, having first made a deep cross on each with a knife
10. Bake a light brown and brush over with white of egg beaten stiff with powdered sugar
