## Ingredients

- three ounces of salt
- a quart of boiling vinegar
- two ounces of scraped horseradish
- flour of mustard

## Directions

1. Dissolve three ounces of salt in a quart of boiling vinegar, and pour it hot upon two ounces of scraped horseradish.
2. Cover the jar closely and let it stand twenty-four hours.
3. Strain it and then mix it by degrees with the best flour of mustard.
4. Make it of the usual thickness, and beat it till quite smooth.
5. Then put it into wide-mouthed bottles and stop it closely.
