## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla or ½ teaspoon vanilla and 1 teaspoon brandy

## Directions

1. Beat eggs until very light, add sugar gradually and continue beating; then flavor.
