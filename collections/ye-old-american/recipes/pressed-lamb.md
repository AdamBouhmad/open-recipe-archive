## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder or leg
- water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put the meat to boil in the morning with water just enough to cover it
2. When tender, season with salt and pepper
3. Keep it over the fire until very tender and the juice nearly boiled out
4. Remove it from the fire-place in a wooden chopping bowl
5. Season more if necessary and chop it up like hash
6. Place it in a bread-pan and press out all the juice
7. Put it in a cool place to harden by placing a dish over the meat and putting a flat-iron upon that
8. Cut up cold into thin slices and serve with the broth left from the meat made into soup with vegetables and spices
