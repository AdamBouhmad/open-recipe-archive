## Ingredients

- 1 cup Brown Sauce
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- ½ small shallot, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon chopped capers
- 1 tablespoon chopped pickle
- Few grains of cayenne

## Directions

1. To one cup Brown Sauce add one tablespoon vinegar, one-half small shallot finely chopped, one tablespoon each chopped capers and pickle, and a few grains of cayenne.
