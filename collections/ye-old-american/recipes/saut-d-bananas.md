## Ingredients

- bananas
- flour
- clarified butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Remove skins from bananas, cut in halves lengthwise, and again cut in halves crosswise.
2. Dredge with flour, and sauté in clarified butter.
3. Drain, and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
