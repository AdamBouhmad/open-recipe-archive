## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Very ripe tomatoes are the best for the purpose
2. Put them in a large pan and cover with boiling water
3. This loosens the skin, which is easily removed
4. Put the tomatoes into the preserving kettle, set over a moderate fire without the addition of water or any seasoning
5. Bring to a boil
6. Boil slowly one-half hour
7. Put into the jars while boiling hot and seal tightly
8. Fill the jars to the brim to prevent air from getting in
9. Set in a cool, dark closet
