## Ingredients

- One pint of boiled rice
- A tablespoonful of dry flour
- Two-thirds of a teaspoonful of salt
- Two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- Three eggs
- A cup and a half of milk
- An ounce of melted butter

## Directions

1. Rub through a sieve one pint of boiled rice
2. Add it to a tablespoonful of dry flour, two-thirds of a teaspoonful of salt, two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
3. Beat separately the yolks and whites of three eggs
4. Add to the yolks a cup and a half of milk, work it into the flour, then add an ounce of melted butter
5. Beat the whites of eggs thoroughly
6. Mix the whole together
7. Heat the waffle-iron and grease it evenly
8. Pour the batter into the half of the iron over the range until nearly two-thirds full
9. Cover, allow to cook a moment, then turn and brown slightly on the other side
