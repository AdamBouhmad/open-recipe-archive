## Ingredients

- crabs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- water
- flour

## Directions

1. Take out the meat from the shell, put it into a saucepan with butter, pepper, salt, a pinch of mace and a very little water.
2. Dredge with flour and let simmer five minutes over a slow fire.
3. Serve hot; garnish the dish with the claws laid around it.
