## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 3 tablespoons Sherry or Madeira wine
- Slight grating nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, and wine slowly; pile on glass dish, and sprinkle with grated nutmeg.
