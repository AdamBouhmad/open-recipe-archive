## Ingredients

- a box of strawberries
- one cup of powdered sugar
- one pint of whipped cream
- one ounce and a half of clarified isinglass or gelatine

## Directions

1. Pick off the hulls of a box of strawberries
2. Bruise them in a basin with a cup of powdered sugar
3. Rub this through a sieve
4. Mix with it a pint of whipped cream and one ounce and a half of clarified isinglass or gelatine
5. Pour the cream into a mold previously oiled
6. Let it in rough ice
7. When it has become firm turn out on a dish
