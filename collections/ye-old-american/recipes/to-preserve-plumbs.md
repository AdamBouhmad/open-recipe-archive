## Ingredients

- plumbs (before they have stones in them)
- sugar (the weight of them)
- a pint of water

## Directions

1. Take your plumbs before they have stones in them, which you may know by putting a pin through them
2. Codle them in many waters till they are as green as grass
3. Peel them and codle them again
4. You must take the weight of them in sugar
5. A pint of water
6. Then put them in
7. Set them on the fire, to boil slowly till they be clear
8. Skimming them often, and they will be very green
9. Put them up in glasses and keep them for use
