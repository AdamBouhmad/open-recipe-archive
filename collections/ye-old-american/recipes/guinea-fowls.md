## Ingredients

- young Guinea fowls
- butter
- shallot
- parsley
- summer savory
- giblets
- browned flour
- currant or other tart jelly
- ham
- dressing

## Directions

1. A pair of young Guinea fowls should be stuffed and roasted, basting them with butter until they are half done.
2. Season the gravy with a chopped shallot, parsley, or summer savory, not omitting the minced giblets, and thicken with browned flour.
3. Send around currant, or other tart jelly, with the fowl.
4. A little ham, minced fine, improves the dressing.
