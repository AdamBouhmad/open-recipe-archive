## Ingredients

- a pound of sweet almonds
- hot water
- orange flower water or rose water
- four crackers or two gills of rice flour
- six eggs
- a pint of cream
- a pound of sugar
- half a pound of butter
- four table-spoonsful of wine
- paste

## Directions

1. Put a pound of sweet almonds in hot water till the skin will slip off them
2. Pound them with a little orange flower or rose water, to keep them from oiling
3. Mix with them four crackers, finely pounded, or two gills of rice flour
4. Add six eggs, a pint of cream, a pound of sugar, half a pound of butter, and four table-spoonsful of wine
5. Put a nice paste in the bottom of your dish, garnish the edges
6. Pour in the pudding
7. Bake it in a moderate oven
