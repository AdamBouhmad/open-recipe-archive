## Ingredients

- one teacupful of butter
- two teacupfuls of fine white sugar
- one lemon
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat to a cream one teacupful of butter and two teacupfuls of fine white sugar
2. Stir in the juice and grated rind of one lemon
3. Grate nutmeg upon the sauce and serve on a flat dish
