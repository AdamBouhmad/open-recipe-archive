## Ingredients

- a fine fat round weighing about twenty-five pounds
- three ounces saltpetre
- one ounce of cloves
- half an ounce of alspice
- a large nutmeg
- a quart of salt
- suet
- water

## Directions

1. Pound them all together very fine
2. Take the bone out
3. Rub it well with this mixture on both sides
4. Put some of it at the bottom of a tub just large enough to hold the beef
5. Lay it in and strew the remainder on the top
6. Rub it well every day for two weeks
7. Spread the mixture over it
8. At the end of this time, wash the beef
9. Bind it with tape, to keep it round and compact
10. Filling the hole where the bone was with a piece of fat
11. Lay it in a pan of convenient size
12. Strew a little suet over the top
13. Pour on it a pint of water
14. Cover the pan with a coarse crust and a thick paper over that
15. It will take five hours baking
16. When cold take off the tape
17. It is a delicious relish at twelve o'clock, or for supper, eaten with vinegar, mustard, oil, or sallad
18. Skim the grease from the gravy and bottle it
19. It makes an excellent seasoning for any made dish
