## Ingredients

- half a pound of shelled sweet almonds
- rose-water
- a pound of loaf-sugar finely powdered
- a tea-spoonful of mixed spice (mace, nutmeg, and cinnamon)
- three-quarters of a pound of sifted flour
- fourteen eggs
- the juice of a large lemon

## Directions

1. Blanch, and pound in a mortar, half a pound of shelled sweet almonds till they are a smooth paste, adding rose-water as you pound them
2. They should be done the day before they are wanted
3. Prepare a pound of loaf-sugar finely powdered, a tea-spoonful of mixed spice (mace, nutmeg, and cinnamon), and three-quarters of a pound of sifted flour
4. Take fourteen eggs, and separate the whites from the yolks
5. Leave out seven of the whites, and beat the other seven to a stiff froth
6. Beat the yolks till very thick and smooth, and then beat the sugar gradually into them, adding the spice
7. Stir in the white of egg, then the flour, and lastly the almonds
8. Add the juice of a large lemon
9. Put the mixture into a square tin pan (well buttered), or into a copper or tin turban-mould, and set it immediately in a brisk oven
10. Ice it when cool
