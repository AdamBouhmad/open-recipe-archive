## Ingredients

- cold turkey
- gravy
- hot water
- butter
- pungent sauce
- nutmeg
- salt
- cranberry or currant jelly
- browned flour
- cold water
- brown Sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Cut the cold turkey from the bones and into bits an inch long with knife and fork, tearing as little as possible.
2. Put into a skillet or saucepan the gravy left from the roast, with hot water to dilute it should the quantity be small.
3. Add a lump of butter the size of an egg, a teaspoonful of pungent sauce, a large pinch of nutmeg, with a little salt.
4. Let it boil, and put in the meat.
5. Stew very slowly for ten minutes—not more.
6. Stir in a tablespoonful of cranberry or currant jelly, another of browned flour which has been wet with cold water.
7. Add a glass of brown Sherry or Madeira.
8. Boil up once, and serve in a covered dish for breakfast.
