## Ingredients

- carp
- flour
- lard
- water
- red wine
- lemon pickle
- walnut catsup
- mushroom powder
- cayenne
- onion
- cloves
- horse-radish
- pickled mushrooms
- scraped horse-radish

## Directions

1. Gut and scale your fish
2. Wash and dry them well with a clean cloth
3. Dredge them with flour
4. Fry them in lard until they are a light brown
5. Put them in a stew pan with half a pint of water and half a pint of red wine
6. Add a meat spoonful of lemon pickle, the same of walnut catsup, a little mushroom powder and cayenne to your taste
7. Add a large onion stuck with cloves and a stick of horse-radish
8. Cover your pan close up to keep in the steam
9. Let them stew gently over a stove fire till the gravy is reduced to just enough to cover your fish in the dish
10. Take the fish out and put them on the dish you intend for the table
11. Set the gravy on the fire and thicken it with flour and a large lump of butter
12. Boil it a little and strain it over your fish
13. Garnish them with pickled mushrooms and scraped horse-radish
14. Send them to the table
