## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 pint milk
- salt
- flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls baking powder
- apples, chopped fine

## Directions

1. Make batter with milk, salt, eggs, and flour thickened as waffle batter with baking powder
2. Fill teacups alternately with a layer of batter and then of chopped apples
3. Steam one hour
4. Serve hot with flavored cream and sugar
