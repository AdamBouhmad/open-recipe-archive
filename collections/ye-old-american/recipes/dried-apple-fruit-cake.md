## Ingredients

- Three cupfuls of dried apples
- Cold water
- Three cups of molasses
- One cupful of raisins
- Three cupfuls of flour
- One cupful of butter
- Three eggs
- One teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Soak three cupfuls of dried apples over night in cold water enough to swell them
2. Chop them in the morning and put them on the fire with three cups of molasses
3. Stew until almost soft
4. Add a cupful of nice raisins and stew a few moments
5. When cold, add three cupfuls of flour, one cupful of butter, three eggs and a teaspoonful of soda
6. Bake in a steady oven
7. This will make two good-sized panfuls
