## Ingredients

- apples
- one glass of brandy
- white wine
- one quarter of a pound of pounded sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon rind
- two eggs
- one quarter of a pound of flour
- one table-spoonful of melted butter
- cold water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare some apples, and cut them in thin slices
2. put them in a bowl, with a glass of brandy, some white wine, a quarter of a pound of pounded sugar, a little cinnamon finely powdered, and the rind of a lemon grated
3. let them stand some time, turning them over frequently
4. beat two eggs very light
5. add one quarter of a pound of flour, a table-spoonful of melted butter, and as much cold water as will make a thin batter
6. drip the apples on a sieve, mix them with the batter
7. take one slice with a spoonful of batter to each fritter
8. fry them quickly of a light brown
9. drain them well
10. put them in a dish, sprinkling sugar over each
11. glaze them nicely
