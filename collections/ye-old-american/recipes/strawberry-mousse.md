## Ingredients

- 1 quart thin cream
- 1 box strawberries
- 1 cup sugar
- ¼ box gelatine or 1¼ tablespoons granulated gelatine
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- 3 tablespoons hot water

## Directions

1. Wash and hull berries, sprinkle with sugar, and let stand one hour
2. Mash, and rub through a fine sieve
3. Add gelatine soaked in cold and dissolved in boiling water
4. Set in pan of ice-water and stir until it begins to thicken
5. Fold in whip from cream, put in mould, cover, pack in salt and ice, and let stand four hours
