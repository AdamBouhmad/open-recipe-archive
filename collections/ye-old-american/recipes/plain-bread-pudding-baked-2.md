## Ingredients

- 1 pint stale bread
- 1 quart boiling milk
- butter
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- 1/2 cupful sugar

## Directions

1. Break up stale bread after cutting off crust
2. Pour boiling milk over it
3. Add butter the size of a small egg
4. Cover dish tight and let stand until cool
5. Mash with spoon until fine
6. Add cinnamon, nutmeg and sugar
