## Ingredients

- 1 cup of butter
- 2 cups of sugar
- 1 cup sour cream or milk
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful soda dissolved in hot water
- 2 teaspoonfuls ginger
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- 5 cups of flour, or enough to roll out soft

## Directions

1. Cut in shapes
2. Brush over with white of egg while hot, and bake
