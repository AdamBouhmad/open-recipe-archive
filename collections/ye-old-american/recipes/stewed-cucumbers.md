## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- water
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- milk
- flour
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Pare cucumbers and lay in ice-water an hour.
2. Slice a quarter of an inch thick and pick out the seeds with a penknife.
3. Put into a saucepan with enough boiling water to cover them.
4. Stew fifteen minutes and drain off the water.
5. Add enough water from the boiling tea-kettle to keep them from burning; season with salt and pepper.
6. Stir carefully in a tablespoonful of butter—or two, should the quantity of cucumber be large.
7. Stew gently ten minutes and add half a cupful of rich milk.
8. Thicken with a little flour, boil up, and squeeze some lemon-juice in at the last.
9. Serve in a deep dish.
