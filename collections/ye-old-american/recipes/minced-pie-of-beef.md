## Ingredients

- boiled beef
- apples
- beef suet
- wine or rich sweet cyder
- mace
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- raisins
- paste No. 3

## Directions

1. Four pound boild beef, chopped fine; and salted; six pound of raw apple chopped also, one pound beef suet, one quart of Wine or rich sweet cyder, one ounce mace, and cinnamon, a nutmeg, two pounds raisins, bake in paste No. 3, three fourths of an hour.
