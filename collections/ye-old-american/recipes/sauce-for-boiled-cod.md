## Ingredients

- one gill of boiling water
- milk
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- one tablespoonful of flour
- chopped yolk of one boiled egg
- one raw egg
- pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Add as much milk to one gill of boiling water
2. Stir into this while boiling two tablespoonfuls of butter gradually
3. Add one tablespoonful of flour wet up with cold water
4. As it thickens, add the chopped yolk of one boiled egg and one raw egg beaten light
5. Take directly from the fire and season with pepper, salt, a little chopped parsley and the juice of one lemon
6. Set covered in boiling water (but not over fire) five minutes, stirring occasionally
7. Pour part of the sauce over fish when dished; the rest in a boat
