## Ingredients

- cold roast lamb
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- celery salt
- hot water or stock
- flour
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Remove dry pieces of skin and gristle from remnants of cold roast lamb.
2. Chop meat.
3. Heat in well-buttered frying-pan.
4. Season with salt, pepper, and celery salt.
5. Moisten with a little hot water or stock, or after seasoning, dredge well with flour, stir, and add enough stock to make thin gravy.
6. Pour over small slices of buttered toast.
