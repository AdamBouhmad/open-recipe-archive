## Ingredients

- crabs
- salt
- pepper
- crumbs
- egg
- deep fat
- Sauce Tartare

## Directions

1. Clean crabs
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs
4. Fry in deep fat
5. Drain
6. Being light, they will rise to top of fat, and should be turned while frying
7. Serve with Sauce Tartare
