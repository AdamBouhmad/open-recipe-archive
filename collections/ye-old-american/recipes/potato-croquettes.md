## Ingredients

- cold mashed potato
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- butter
- eggs
- parsley
- bread-crumbs
- lard or drippings

## Directions

1. Season cold mashed potato with pepper, salt, and nutmeg.
2. Beat to a cream with a tablespoonful of melted butter to every cupful of potato.
3. Bind with two or three beaten eggs and add some minced parsley.
4. Roll into oval balls, dip in beaten egg, then in bread-crumbs, and fry in hot lard or drippings.
5. Pile in a pyramid upon a flat dish and serve.
