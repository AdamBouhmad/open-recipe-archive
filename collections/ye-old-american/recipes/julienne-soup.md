## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- celery
- boiling water
- a teaspoonful of salt
- half a teaspoonful pepper
- two quarts of boiling stock

## Directions

1. Cut carrots and turnips into quarter-inch pieces the shape of dice; also celery into thin slices
2. Cover them with boiling water; add a teaspoonful of salt, half a teaspoonful pepper, and cook until soft
3. In another saucepan have two quarts of boiling stock
4. Add the cooked vegetables, the water and more seasoning if necessary
5. Serve hot
