## Ingredients

- small green tomatoes
- sugar
- lemons
- ginger and mace

## Directions

1. Heat all together slowly, and boil until the fruit is clear.
2. Take it from the kettle in a perforated skimmer, and spread upon dishes to cool.
3. Boil the syrup thick.
4. Put the fruit into jars and cover with hot syrup.
