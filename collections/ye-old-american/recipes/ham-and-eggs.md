## Ingredients

- ham slices
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut your slices of ham of a uniform size and shape.
2. Fry quickly, and take them out of the pan as soon as they are done.
3. Have the eggs ready, and drop them, one at a time, in the hissing fat.
4. Have a large pan for this purpose, that they may not touch and run together.
5. In three minutes they will be done.
6. The meat should be kept hot, and when the eggs are ready, lay one upon each slice of ham.
7. Do not use the gravy.
