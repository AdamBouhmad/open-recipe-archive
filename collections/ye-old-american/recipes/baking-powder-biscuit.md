## Ingredients

- two pints of flour
- butter the size of an egg
- three heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- one teaspoonful of salt
- sweet milk or water

## Directions

1. Make a soft dough of sweet milk or water
2. Knead as little as possible
3. Cut out with the usual biscuit-cutter
4. Bake in rather a quick oven
