## Ingredients

- one cup of cold, cooked, lean beef
- half a cup of fat
- half a cup of cold boiled or fried ham or cold pork
- a slice of onion
- a teaspoonful of salt
- half a teaspoonful of pepper
- a teaspoonful of powdered sage or parsley
- half a cup of stock or milk
- a beaten egg
- bread crumbs or flour
- hot lard or beef drippings
- sprigs of parsley

## Directions

1. Chop fine one cup of cold, cooked, lean beef
2. Chop fine half a cup of fat and half a cup of cold boiled or fried ham or cold pork
3. Mince up a slice of onion
4. Season all with a teaspoonful of salt, half a teaspoonful of pepper, and a teaspoonful of powdered sage or parsley if liked
5. Heat together with half a cup of stock or milk
6. When cool add a beaten egg
7. Form the mixture into balls, slightly flattened
8. Roll in egg and bread crumbs, or flour and egg
9. Fry in hot lard or beef drippings
10. Serve on a platter and garnish with sprigs of parsley
