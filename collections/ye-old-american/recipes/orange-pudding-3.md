## Ingredients

- sixteen yolks
- half a pound butter melted
- grated rinds of two Seville oranges
- half pound of fine Sugar
- two spoons orange water
- two of rose-water
- one gill of wine
- half pint cream
- two naples biscuit or the crumbs of a fine loaf, or roll soaked in cream

## Directions

1. Put sixteen yolks with half a pound butter melted, grate in the rinds of two Seville oranges, beat in half pound of fine Sugar, add two spoons orange water, two of rose-water, one gill of wine, half pint cream, two naples bisc, or the crumbs of a fine loaf, or roll soaked in cream, mix all together, put it into rich puff-paste, which let be double round the edges of the dish; bake like a custard.
