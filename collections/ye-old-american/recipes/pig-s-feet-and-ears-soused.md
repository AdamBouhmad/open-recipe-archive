## Ingredients

- pig's feet and ears
- salt
- vinegar
- mace
- beaten yolk of egg
- bread-crumbs
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Clean them properly, and remove the skin
2. Boil them slowly till they are quite tender, and then split the feet
3. Put them with the ears into salt and vinegar, flavoured with a little mace
4. Cover the jar closely, and set it away
5. When you use them, dry each piece well with a cloth
6. Dip them first in beaten yolk of egg, and then in bread-crumbs, and fry them nicely in butter or lard
