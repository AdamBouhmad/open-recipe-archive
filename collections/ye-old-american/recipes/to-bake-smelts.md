## Ingredients

- smelts
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- bread or cracker crumbs
- fried parsley
- lemon

## Directions

1. Wash and dry them thoroughly in a cloth
2. Arrange them nicely in a flat baking-dish; the pan should be buttered, also the fish
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Cover with bread or cracker crumbs
5. Place a piece of butter over each
6. Bake for fifteen or twenty minutes
7. Garnish with fried parsley and cut lemon
