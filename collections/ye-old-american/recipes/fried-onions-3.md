## Ingredients

- four medium-sized onions
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove skins from four medium-sized onions.
2. Cut in thin slices and put in a hot omelet pan with one and one-half tablespoons butter.
3. Cook until brown, occasionally shaking pan that onions may not burn, or turn onions, using a fork.
4. Sprinkle with salt one minute before taking from fire.
