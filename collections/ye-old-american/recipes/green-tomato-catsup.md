## Ingredients

- one peck of green tomatoes
- two large onions sliced
- salt
- a quarter of a pound of mustard seed
- one ounce allspice
- one ounce cloves
- one ounce ground mustard
- one ounce ground ginger
- two tablespoonfuls black pepper
- two teaspoonfuls celery seed
- a quarter of a pound of brown sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place green tomatoes and two large onions sliced in layers, sprinkling salt between; let them stand twenty-four hours and then drain them.
2. Add a quarter of a pound of mustard seed, one ounce allspice, one ounce cloves, one ounce ground mustard, one ounce ground ginger, two tablespoonfuls black pepper, two teaspoonfuls celery seed, a quarter of a pound of brown sugar.
3. Put all in preserving-pan, cover with vinegar and boil two hours; then strain through a sieve and bottle for use.
