## Ingredients

- small black wild grapes
- brown sugar
- cold molasses

## Directions

1. Gather the small black wild grapes late in the season, after they have been ripened by a frost
2. Pick them from the stems
3. Put them into stone jars, two-thirds full, with layers of brown sugar
4. Fill them up with cold molasses
5. If they incline to ferment in the jars, give them a boil with additional sugar
