## Ingredients

- cochineal
- cream of tartar
- alum
- soft water

## Directions

1. Take twenty grains of cochineal and fifteen grains of cream of tartar finely powdered
2. Add to them a piece of alum the size of a cherry stone
3. Boil them with a gill of soft water in an earthen vessel, slowly, for half an hour
4. Strain it through muslin
5. Keep it tightly corked in a phial
