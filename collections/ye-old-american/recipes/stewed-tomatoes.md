## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- scalding water
- salt
- pepper
- white sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Loosen the skins by pouring scalding water upon them.
2. Peel and cut them up, extracting the cores or hard parts of the stem end, and removing all unripe portions.
3. Stew in a saucepan (tin or porcelain) half an hour.
4. Add salt and pepper to taste, a teaspoonful of white sugar, and a tablespoonful of butter.
5. Stew gently fifteen minutes longer.
6. Serve.
