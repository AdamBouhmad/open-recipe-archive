## Ingredients

- a pint of sifted Indian meal
- a handful of wheat flour
- a small tea-spoonful of salt
- four eggs
- a quart of milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Have ready a pint of sifted Indian meal, mixed with a handful of wheat flour, and a small tea-spoonful of salt.
2. Beat four eggs very light, and stir them by degrees into a quart of milk, in turn with the meal.
3. They can be made in a very short time, and should be baked as soon as mixed, on a hot griddle; allow a large ladle full of batter to each cake, and make them all of the same size.
4. Send them to table hot, buttered and cut in half.
