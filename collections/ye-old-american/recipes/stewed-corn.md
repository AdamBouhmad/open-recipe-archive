## Ingredients

- dozen ears of green sweet corn
- water
- milk or cream
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a dozen ears of green sweet corn, very tender and juicy; cut off the kernels, cutting with a large sharp knife from the top of the cob down; then scrape the cob.
2. Put the corn in a saucepan over the fire with just enough water to make it cook without burning; boil about twenty minutes.
3. Add a teacupful of milk or cream, a tablespoonful of cold butter, and season with pepper and salt.
4. Boil ten minutes longer and dish up hot in a vegetable dish.
