## Ingredients

- yolk one hard-boiled egg
- two tablespoons butter
- one sprig parsley
- one sprig tarragon
- one small shallot
- one-half teaspoon anchovy paste
- one teaspoon capers
- one teaspoon chopped gherkins
- salt and pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix yolk one hard-boiled egg, two tablespoons butter, one sprig parsley, one sprig tarragon, one small shallot, one-half teaspoon anchovy paste, one teaspoon capers, and one teaspoon chopped gherkins, and pound in a mortar.
2. Rub through a very fine sieve.
3. Season with salt and pepper, and add a few drops vinegar.
