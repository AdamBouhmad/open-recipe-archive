## Ingredients

- one teacupful of wheat flour
- one-half teacupful of Porto Rico molasses
- one-half cupful of good yeast
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one pint of warm water
- Graham flour
- one teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. add sufficient Graham flour to make the dough as stiff as can be stirred with a strong spoon
2. this is to be mixed at night
3. in the morning, add one teaspoonful of soda, dissolved in a little water
4. mix well, and pour into two medium-sized pans
5. let it stand in a warm place until it rises to the top of the pans
6. bake one hour in a pretty hot oven
7. This should be covered about twenty minutes when first put into the oven with a thick brown paper, or an old tin cover
