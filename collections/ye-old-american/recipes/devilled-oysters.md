## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup flour
- ⅔ cup milk
- Yolk 1 egg
- ½ tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- Buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean, drain, and slightly chop oysters
2. Make a sauce of butter, flour, and milk
3. Add egg yolk, seasonings, and oysters
4. Arrange buttered scallop shells in a dripping-pan, half fill with mixture, cover with buttered crumbs, and bake twelve to fifteen minutes in a hot oven
5. Deep oyster shells may be used in place of scallop shells
