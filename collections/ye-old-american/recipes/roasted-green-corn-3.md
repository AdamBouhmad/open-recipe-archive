## Ingredients

- green corn
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Strip off all the husk from green corn and roast it on a gridiron over a bright fire of coals, turning it as one side is done.
2. Or, if a wood fire is used, make a place clean in front of the fire, lay the corn down, turn it when one side is done; serve with salt and butter.
