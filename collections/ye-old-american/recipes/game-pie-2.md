## Ingredients

- small birds
- quail
- snipe
- woodcock
- water
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onion
- cloves
- salt pork
- browned flour
- butter
- potatoes
- rich crust

## Directions

1. Clean well, inside and out, a dozen small birds such as quail, snipe, woodcock, etc., and split them in half
2. Put them in a saucepan with about two quarts of water
3. When it boils, skim off all scum that rises
4. Add salt and pepper, a bunch of minced parsley, one onion chopped fine, and three whole cloves
5. Cut up half a pound of salt pork into dice, and let all boil until tender
6. Use care that there be enough water to cover the birds
7. Thicken this with two tablespoonfuls of browned flour and let it boil up
8. Stir in a piece of butter as large as an egg
9. Remove from the fire and let it cool
10. Have ready a pint of potatoes cut as small as dice, and a rich crust made
11. Line the sides of a buttered pudding dish with the crust
12. Lay in the birds, then some of the potatoes, then birds and so on, until the dish is full
13. Pour over the gravy
14. Put on the top crust, with a slit cut in the centre, and bake
15. The top can be ornamented with pastry leaves in a wreath about the edge, with any fancy design placed in the centre across the slit
