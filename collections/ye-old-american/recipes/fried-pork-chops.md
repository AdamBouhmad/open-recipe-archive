## Ingredients

- pork chops
- sage
- summer savory
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Fry them the same as mutton chops
2. If a sausage flavor is liked, sprinkle over them a little powdered sage or summer savory, pepper and salt
3. If a gravy is liked, skim off some of the fat in the pan and stir in a spoonful of flour
4. Stir it until free from lumps
5. Season with pepper and salt and turn in a pint of sweet milk
6. Boil up and serve in a gravy boat
