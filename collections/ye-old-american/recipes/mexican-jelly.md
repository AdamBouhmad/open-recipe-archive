## Ingredients

- four large cucumbers
- one cup cold water
- two and one-half tablespoons granulated gelatine
- three-fourths cup boiling water
- onion juice
- one tablespoon vinegar
- cayenne
- salt
- pepper
- leaf green
- Tomato Mayonnaise
- truffle
- small tomatoes

## Directions

1. Peel four large cucumbers and cut in thin slices
2. Put in saucepan with one cup cold water, bring to boiling-point, and cook slowly until soft
3. Force through a purée strainer
4. Add two and one-half tablespoons granulated gelatine dissolved in three-fourths cup boiling water, few drops onion juice, one tablespoon vinegar, few grains cayenne, and salt and pepper to taste
5. Color with leaf green, strain through cheese-cloth, and mould same as Fruit Chartreuse
6. After removing small mould fill space with Tomato Mayonnaise
7. Garnish sides of mould with thin slices of cucumber shaped with a small round fluted cutter, and on the centre of each slice place a circular piece of truffle
8. Garnish around base of mould with small tomatoes peeled, chilled, and cut in halves crosswise
9. On each slice of tomato place a circular fluted slice of cucumber, and over all a circular piece of truffle
10. Serve with Tomato Mayonnaise
