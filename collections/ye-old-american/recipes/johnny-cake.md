## Ingredients

- a quart of Indian meal
- a pint of warm water
- a small tea-spoonful of salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Sift a quart of Indian meal into a pan; make a hole in the middle, and pour in a pint of warm water.
2. Mix the meal and water gradually into a batter, adding a small tea-spoonful of salt.
3. Beat it very hard, and for a long time, till it becomes quite light.
4. Spread it thick and even on a stout piece of smooth board.
5. Place it upright on the hearth before a clear fire, with a flat iron or something of the sort to support the board behind, and bake it well.
6. Cut it into squares, and split and butter them hot.
