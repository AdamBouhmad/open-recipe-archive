## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- grated bread crumbs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- sweet cream or milk

## Directions

1. Hard-boil twelve eggs
2. Slice them thin in rings
3. In the bottom of a large well-buttered baking-dish place a layer of grated bread crumbs, then one of eggs
4. Cover with bits of butter and sprinkle with pepper and salt
5. Continue thus to blend these ingredients until the dish is full, being sure that the crumbs cover the eggs upon top
6. Pour a large teacupful of sweet cream or milk over the whole and brown nicely in a moderately heated oven
