## Ingredients

- chops from loin of mutton
- potatoes
- turnips
- onions
- water

## Directions

1. Take some chops from loin of mutton, place them in stewpan in alternate layers of sliced potatoes and chops
2. Add turnips and onions cut into pieces
3. Pour in nearly a quart of cold water
4. Cover stewpan closely
5. Let it stew gently till vegetables are ready to mash and the greater part of the gravy is absorbed
6. Place in a dish and serve it up hot
