## Ingredients

- turkey neck
- turkey heart
- turkey liver
- turkey gizzard
- pint of water
- flour

## Directions

1. When you put the turkey in to roast, put the neck, heart, liver and gizzard into a stewpan with a pint of water
2. Boil until they become quite tender; take them out of the water
3. Chop the heart and gizzard, mash the liver and throw away the neck
4. Return the chopped heart, gizzard and liver to the liquor in which they were stewed
5. Set it to one side, and when the turkey is done it should be added to the gravy that dripped from the turkey, having first skimmed off the fat from the surface of the dripping-pan
6. Set it all over the fire, boil three minutes and thicken with flour
