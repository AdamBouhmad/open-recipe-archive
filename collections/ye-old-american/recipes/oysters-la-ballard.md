## Ingredients

- oysters on the half shell
- melted butter
- lemon juice
- Sauterne
- Tabasco
- parsley
- salt
- paprika
- garlic clove

## Directions

1. Arrange oysters on the half shell in a dripping-pan, and bake in a hot oven until edges curl.
2. Allow six to each serve, pouring over the following sauce: Mix three-fourths tablespoon melted butter, three-fourths teaspoon each lemon juice and Sauterne, few drops Tabasco, one-fourth teaspoon finely chopped parsley, and salt and paprika to taste.
3. Before putting ingredients in bowl, rub inside of bowl with a clove of garlic.
