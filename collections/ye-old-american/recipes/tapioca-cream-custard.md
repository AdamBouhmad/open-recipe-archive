## Ingredients

- Three heaping tablespoonfuls of tapioca
- A teacupful of water
- A quart of milk
- A good pinch of salt
- A cupful of sugar
- The beaten yolks of three eggs
- The whites beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Soak three heaping tablespoonfuls of tapioca in a teacupful of water over night
2. Place over the fire a quart of milk; let it come to a boil
3. Stir in the tapioca and a good pinch of salt, stir until it thickens
4. Add a cupful of sugar and the beaten yolks of three eggs
5. Stir it quickly and pour it into a dish
6. Stir gently into the mixture the whites beaten stiff, the flavoring and set it on ice, or in an ice chest
