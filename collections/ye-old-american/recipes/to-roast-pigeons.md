## Ingredients

- pigeons
- butter
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- asparagus

## Directions

1. Dress your pigeons
2. Roll a good lump of butter in chopped parsley, with pepper and salt, put it in your pigeons
3. Spit, dust and baste them
4. If the fire be good, they will roast in twenty minutes
5. When they are through, lay round them bunches of asparagus, with parsley and butter for sauce
