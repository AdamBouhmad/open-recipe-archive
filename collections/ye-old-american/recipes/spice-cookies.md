## Ingredients

- ½ cup molasses
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 1½ tablespoons lard
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 2 cups flour
- ½ teaspoon soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon clove
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon nutmeg

## Directions

1. Heat molasses to boiling-point.
2. Add sugar, shortening, and milk.
3. Mix and sift dry ingredients, and add to first mixture.
4. Chill thoroughly, and proceed as with Ginger Snaps.
