## Ingredients

- Siberian crab-apples
- water (one cup for every six pounds of fruit, if apples are very dry)

## Directions

1. Cut Siberian crab-apples to pieces, but do not pare or remove the seeds.
2. Put into a stone jar, set in a pot of hot water, and let it boil eight or nine hours.
3. Leave in the jar all night, covered closely.
4. Next morning, squeeze out the juice, allow pound for pint, and manage as you do currant jelly.
