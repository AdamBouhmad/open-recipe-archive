## Ingredients

- 2¼ cups flour
- ¾ cup hot cooked rice
- 5 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix and sift flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder; add one-half milk, egg well beaten, the remainder of the milk mixed with rice, and beat thoroughly; then add butter.
2. Bake in buttered muffin rings placed in buttered pan or buttered gem pans.
