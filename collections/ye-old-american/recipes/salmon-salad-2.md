## Ingredients

- cold boiled salmon
- French Mayonnaise
- Cream Dressing
- lettuce leaves
- hard-boiled egg yolk
- hard-boiled egg white

## Directions

1. Flake remnants of cold boiled salmon.
2. Mix with French Mayonnaise, or Cream Dressing.
3. Arrange on nests of lettuce leaves.
4. Garnish with the yolk of a hard-boiled egg forced through a potato ricer, and white of egg cut in strips.
