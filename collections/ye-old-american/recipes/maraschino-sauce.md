## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup boiling water
- ⅓ cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons corn-starch
- ¼ cup Maraschino cherries, cut in halves
- ½ cup Maraschino syrup
- ½ tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and corn-starch, add gradually to boiling water, stirring constantly.
2. Boil five minutes, and add cherries, syrup, and butter.
