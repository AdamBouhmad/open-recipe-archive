## Ingredients

- fine and not too ripe berries
- sugar

## Directions

1. To each pound of berries, allow three-quarters of a pound of sugar.
2. Put them into a preserving pan and stir gently, not to break up the fruit.
3. Simmer for one-half hour and put into pots air-tight.
4. Cut round covers from writing paper a half-inch too large for the tops, smear the inside with the unbeaten white of an egg, tie over with a cord, and it will dry quickly and be absolutely preservative.
5. A circular paper dipped in brandy and laid over the contents before covering will prevent any dampness from affecting the flavor.
