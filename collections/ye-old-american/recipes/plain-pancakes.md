## Ingredients

- half a pound or a pint of flour
- seven eggs
- a quart of rich milk
- a spoonful of lard
- powdered sugar
- nutmeg
- wine

## Directions

1. Sift half a pound or a pint of flour
2. Beat seven eggs very light, and stir them gradually into a quart of rich milk
3. Then add by degrees the flour, so as to make a thin batter
4. Mix it very smooth, pressing out all the lumps with the back of a spoon
5. Set the frying-pan over the fire, and when it is hot, grease it with a spoonful of lard
6. Then put in a ladle full of the batter, and fry it of a light brown, turning it with care to prevent its breaking
7. Make each pancake large enough to cover the bottom of a dessert plate; greasing the pan every time
8. Send them to table hot, accompanied by powdered sugar and nutmeg mixed in a small glass bowl
9. Have wine with them also
