## Ingredients

- Two chickens' livers
- Onion juice
- Butter
- Yolks of four hard-boiled eggs
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Tabasco Sauce
- Grated cheese
- Tomato Purée
- Toast rings

## Directions

1. Clean and chop two chickens' livers, sprinkle with onion juice, and sauté in butter.
2. Add the yolks of four hard-boiled eggs rubbed through a sieve, one teaspoon chopped parsley, and salt, pepper, and Tabasco Sauce to taste.
3. Refill whites of eggs with mixture, cover with grated cheese, and bake until cheese melts.
4. Serve in toast rings and pour around Tomato Purée.
