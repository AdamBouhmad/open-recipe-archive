## Ingredients

- fresh bread
- butter, creamed
- baby ribbon

## Directions

1. Cut fresh bread, while still warm, in as thin slices as possible, using a very sharp knife.
2. Spread evenly with butter which has been creamed.
3. Roll slices separately, and tie each with baby ribbon.
