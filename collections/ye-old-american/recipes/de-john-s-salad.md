## Ingredients

- six Bartlett pears
- lettuce leaves
- French Dressing

## Directions

1. Pare six Bartlett pears, care being taken not to remove stems.
2. Cut in thin slices, and serve in original shapes on lettuce leaves.
3. Serve with French Dressing.
