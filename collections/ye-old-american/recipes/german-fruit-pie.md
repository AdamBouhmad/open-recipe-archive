## Ingredients

- a heaping teaspoonful of baking powder
- a pint of flour
- a piece of butter as large as a walnut
- a pinch of salt
- one beaten egg
- sweet milk
- quartered juicy apples
- a little cinnamon
- molasses
- sugar

## Directions

1. Sift together a heaping teaspoonful of baking powder and a pint of flour
2. add a piece of butter as large as a walnut, a pinch of salt, one beaten egg and sweet milk enough to make a soft dough
3. Roll it out half an inch thick
4. butter a square biscuit tin and cover the bottom and sides with the dough
5. fill the pan with quartered juicy apples, sprinkle with a little cinnamon and molasses
6. Bake in rather quick oven until the crust and apples are cooked a light brown
7. Sprinkle a little sugar over the top five minutes before removing from the oven
