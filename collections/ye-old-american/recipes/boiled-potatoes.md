## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Select potatoes that are as nearly as possible of the same size
2. Wash, but do not pare them
3. Put them into a pot with water enough to cover them about an inch, and do not put on the pot-lid
4. When the water is very near boiling, pour it off, and replace it with the same quantity of cold water, into which throw a good portion of salt
5. Potatoes of a moderate size will require about half an hour boiling; large ones an hour
6. Try them with a fork
7. When done, pour off the water, cover the pot with a folded napkin, or flannel, and let them stand by the fire about a quarter of an hour to dry
8. Peel them and send them to table
