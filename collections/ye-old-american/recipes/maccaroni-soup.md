## Ingredients

- clear gravy soup
- quarter of a pound maccaroni
- butter
- Parmesan cheese or other rich cheese

## Directions

1. Cut up and boil the maccaroni by itself in a very little water, allowing a quarter of a pound to a quart of soup.
2. The pieces should be about an inch long.
3. Put a small piece of butter with it.
4. It must boil till tender, but not till it breaks.
5. Throw it into the soup shortly before it goes to table, and give it one boil up.
6. While the maccaroni is boiling, take care that it does not get into lumps.
7. Send to table with it a plate or glass of rasped Parmesan or other rich cheese, with a dessert spoon in it, that those who like it may put it into their soup on the plate.
