## Ingredients

- Whites 10 eggs
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅞ teaspoon cream of tartar
- Yolks 7 eggs
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1 teaspoon almond extract
- 1 cup pastry flour

## Directions

1. Add salt to whites of eggs and beat until light
2. Sift in cream of tartar and beat until stiff
3. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored and add two heaping tablespoons beaten whites
4. To remaining whites add gradually sugar measured after five siftings
5. Add almond extract and combine mixtures
6. Cut and fold in flour, measured after five siftings
7. Bake in angel-cake pan, first dipped in cold water, in a slow oven one hour. Have a pan of hot water in oven during the baking
