## Ingredients

- Four pounds loin of veal
- Salt and pepper
- Flour
- One-fourth cup butter
- About three cups hot water
- Two cups boiled turnips, cut in half-inch cubes
- Two cups boiled carrots, cut in half-inch cubes
- Potatoes cut in balls

## Directions

1. Wipe four pounds loin of veal, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and dredge with flour.
2. Put one-fourth cup butter in deep stewpan; when melted, add veal and brown entire surface of meat, watching carefully and turning often, that it may not burn.
3. Add one cup hot water, cover closely, and cook slowly two hours, or until meat is tender, adding more water as needed, using in all about three cups.
4. Remove meat, thicken stock remaining in pan with flour diluted with enough cold water to pour easily.
5. Surround the meat with two cups each boiled turnips and carrots, cut in half-inch cubes, and potatoes cut in balls.
6. Serve gravy in a tureen.
