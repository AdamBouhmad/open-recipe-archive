## Ingredients

- four ounces of butter
- half a teacup of milk
- four ounces of white sugar
- eight ounces of sifted flour
- yolk of one egg
- one tablespoonful of orange-flower water
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Dissolve four ounces of butter in half a teacup of milk
2. Stir together sugar, flour, and egg yolk
3. Add gradually the butter and milk, orange-flower water and salt
4. Mix well
5. Heat the wafer-irons and butter their inner surfaces
6. Put in a tablespoonful of the batter and close the irons immediately
7. Put the irons over the fire and turn them occasionally until the wafer is cooked
8. When all cooked, roll them on a small round stick
9. Stand them upon a sieve and dry them
10. Serve with ices
