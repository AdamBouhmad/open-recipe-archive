## Ingredients

- button mushrooms
- veal gravy or milk or cream
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Wash a pint of small button mushrooms
2. Remove the stems and the outside skin
3. Stew them slowly in veal gravy or in milk or cream, seasoning them with pepper and salt
4. Add a piece of butter rolled in a large proportion of flour
5. Stew them till quite tender, now and then shaking the pan round
