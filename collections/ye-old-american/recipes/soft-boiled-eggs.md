## Ingredients

- eggs
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Put the eggs into a dish with a cover, or a tin pail
2. Pour boiling water upon them--two quarts or more to a dozen of eggs
3. Cover and set them away where they will keep hot and not boil for ten to twelve minutes
