## Ingredients

- sturgeon
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- green fennel

## Directions

1. Take small sturgeons, about four feet long without the head
2. Split through the back bone
3. Take a piece with the skin on
4. Cut off the gristle
5. Scrape the skin well, wash it, and salt it
6. Let it lie twenty-four hours
7. Wipe off the salt
8. Roll it and tie it around with twine
9. Put it in a good deal of cold water
10. Let it boil till you can run a straw easily into the skin
11. Take it up and pull off the large scales
12. When cold, put it in a pot and cover it with one part vinegar and two of salt and water
13. Keep it closely stopped
14. When served, garnish with green fennel
