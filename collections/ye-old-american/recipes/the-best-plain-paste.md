## Ingredients

- superfine flour
- fresh butter
- cold water

## Directions

1. Sift three pints of superfine flour, by rubbing it through a sieve into a deep pan.
2. Divide a pound of fresh butter into four quarters.
3. Cut up one quarter into the flour, and rub it fine with your hands.
4. Mix in, gradually, as much cold water as will make a tolerably stiff dough, and then knead it slightly.
5. Sprinkle a little flour on your paste-board, lay the lump of dough upon it, and knead it a very short time.
6. Flour it, and roll it out into a very thin sheet, always rolling from you.
7. Flour your rolling-pin to prevent its sticking.
8. Take a second quarter of the butter, and with your thumb, spread it all over the sheet of paste.
9. When you have put on the layer of butter, sprinkle it with a very little flour, and with your hands roll up the paste as you would a sheet of paper.
10. Then flatten it with a rolling-pin, and roll it out a second time into a thin sheet.
11. Cover it with another layer of butter, as before, and again roll it up into a scroll.
12. Flatten it again and continue the process.
