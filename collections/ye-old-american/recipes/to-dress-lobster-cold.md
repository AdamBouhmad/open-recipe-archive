## Ingredients

- cold water
- lobster coral or scarlet meat
- salt
- cayenne
- sweet oil
- made mustard
- vinegar
- powdered white sugar

## Directions

1. Put a table-spoonful of cold water on a clean plate, and with the back of a wooden spoon mash into it the coral or scarlet meat of the lobster, adding a salt-spoonful of salt, and about the same quantity of cayenne.
2. On another part of the plate mix well together with the back of the spoon two table-spoonfuls of sweet oil, and a tea-spoonful of made mustard.
3. Mix the whole till they are well incorporated and perfectly smooth, adding, at the last, one table-spoonful of vinegar, and two more of oil.
4. Extract the meat of the body and claws of the lobster from the shell and mince very small.
5. When the dressing is smoothly and thoroughly amalgamated mix the meat with it.
6. Pile it in a deep dish, and smooth it with the back of a spoon.
7. Stick a bunch of the small claws in the top, and garnish with curled parsley.
