## Ingredients

- 1 boiled egg
- 1 raw egg
- 1 tablespoonful salad oil
- 1 teaspoonful white sugar
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- 1 saltspoonful pepper
- 4 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful made mustard
- celery

## Directions

1. Prepare the dressing as for tomato salad.
2. Cut the celery into bits half an inch long, and season.
3. Eat at once, before the vinegar injures the crispness of the vegetable.
