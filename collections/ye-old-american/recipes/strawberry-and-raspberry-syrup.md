## Ingredients

- fresh fruit
- three and a half pounds of granulated sugar per quart of juice

## Directions

1. Mash the fresh fruit, express the juice and to each quart add three and a half pounds of granulated sugar
2. Heat the juice to 180° Fahrenheit, and strain or filter previous to dissolving the sugar
3. Can hot in glass jars, it will keep for an indefinite time
