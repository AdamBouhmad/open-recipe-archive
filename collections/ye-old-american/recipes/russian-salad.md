## Ingredients

- a dozen anchovies
- one herring
- an equal quantity of Bologna or Lyons sausage, or of smoked ham and sausages
- an equal quantity of the breast of cold roast fowl, or veal
- beet-roots
- pickled cucumbers
- cold boiled potatoes
- a tablespoonful of capers
- yolks and whites of some hard-boiled eggs
- a dozen stoned olives
- oil
- Tarragon vinegar
- white pepper
- French mustard

## Directions

1. Wash, split and bone a dozen anchovies, and roll each one up
2. Wash, split and bone one herring, and cut it up into small pieces
3. Cut up into dice an equal quantity of Bologna or Lyons sausage, or of smoked ham and sausages
4. Cut up into dice an equal quantity of the breast of cold roast fowl, or veal
5. Cut into dice beet-roots, pickled cucumbers, cold boiled potatoes cut in larger dice, in quantity according to taste, but at least thrice as much potato as anything else
6. Add a tablespoonful of capers, the yolks and whites of some hard-boiled eggs, minced separately, and a dozen stoned olives
7. Mix all the ingredients well together, reserving the olives and anchovies to ornament the top of the bowl
8. Beat up together oil and Tarragon vinegar with white pepper and French mustard to taste
9. Pour the dressing over the salad and serve
