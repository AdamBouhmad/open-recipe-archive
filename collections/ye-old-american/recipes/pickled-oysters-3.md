## Ingredients

- 100 large oysters
- 1 pt. white wine vinegar
- 1 doz. blades of mace
- 2 doz. whole cloves
- 2 doz. whole black peppers
- 1 large red pepper broken into bits
- salt

## Directions

1. Put oysters, liquor and all, into a porcelain or bell-metal kettle.
2. Salt to taste.
3. Heat slowly until the oysters are very hot, but not to boiling.
4. Take them out with a perforated skimmer, and set aside to cool.
5. To the liquor which remains in the kettle add the vinegar and spices.
6. Boil up fairly, and when the oysters are almost cold, pour over them scalding hot.
7. Cover the jar in which they are, and put away in a cool place.
8. Next day put the pickled oysters into glass cans with tight tops.
9. Keep in the dark, and where they are not liable to become heated.
