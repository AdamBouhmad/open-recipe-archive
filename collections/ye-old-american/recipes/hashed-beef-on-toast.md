## Ingredients

- cold roast beef
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- soup-stock or water
- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop a quantity of cold roast beef rather fine and season it well with pepper and salt.
2. For each pint of meat add a level tablespoonful of flour.
3. Stir well and add a small teacupful of soup-stock or water.
4. Put the mixture into a small stewpan and, after covering it, simmer for twenty minutes.
5. Meanwhile, toast half a dozen slices of bread nicely and at the end of the twenty minutes spread the meat upon them.
6. Serve at once on a hot dish.
7. In case water be used instead of soup-stock, add a tablespoonful of butter just before spreading the beef upon the toast.
