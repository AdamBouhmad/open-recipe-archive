## Ingredients

- 6 anchovies
- A teacupful drawn butter
- A wineglass pale Sherry

## Directions

1. Soak the anchovies in cold water two hours; pull them to pieces, and simmer in just enough water to cover them for half an hour.
2. Strain the liquor into the drawn butter (No. 3), boil a minute, add the wine; heat gradually to a boil, and stew five minutes longer.
