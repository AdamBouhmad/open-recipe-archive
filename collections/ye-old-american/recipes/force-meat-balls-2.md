## Ingredients

- Six tablespoonfuls turtle-meat
- Yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- Tablespoonful of butter
- Oyster-liquor
- Cayenne
- Mace
- Half a teaspoonful of white sugar
- One well-beaten egg
- Powdered cracker

## Directions

1. Chop the turtle-meat very fine
2. Rub to a paste with the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs and tablespoonful of butter, and if convenient, a little oyster-liquor
3. Season with cayenne, mace, and half a teaspoonful of white sugar
4. Bind with a well-beaten egg
5. Shape into balls
6. Dip in egg, then powdered cracker
7. Fry in butter, and drop into the soup when it is served
