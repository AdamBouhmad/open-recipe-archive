## Ingredients

- 1¼ cups raspberries
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- White 1 egg

## Directions

1. Put ingredients in bowl and beat with wire whisk until stiff enough to hold in shape; about thirty minutes will be required for beating.
2. Pile lightly on dish, chill, surround with lady fingers, and serve with Boiled Custard.
