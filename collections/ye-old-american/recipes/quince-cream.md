## Ingredients

- ripe quinces
- cochineal finely powdered
- cream

## Directions

1. Wash ripe quinces and boil them whole till quite tender
2. Let them stand to drain and cool
3. Rub them through a hair sieve
4. Mix with the pulp as much cochineal finely powdered, as will make it a pretty colour
5. Add an equal quantity of cream, and sweeten it
