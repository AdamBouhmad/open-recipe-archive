## Ingredients

- one cupful of cooked chicken
- a teacupful of cream or rich milk
- a tablespoonful of butter
- salt and pepper
- a large tablespoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Mince rather fine one cupful of cooked chicken
2. Warm in a teacupful of cream or rich milk a tablespoonful of butter, salt and pepper
3. Thicken with a large tablespoonful of flour
4. Make a plain omelet, then add this mixture just before turning it over
