## Ingredients

- five scruples of lunar caustic
- one drachm of gum arabic
- one scruple of sap-green
- one ounce of rain water
- a quarter of an ounce of salt of soda
- a heaped table-spoonful of powdered gum arabic
- two ounces of hot water

## Directions

1. For the marking liquid, rub together in a small mortar five scruples of lunar caustic with one drachm of gum arabic, one scruple of sap-green and one ounce of rain water
2. Keep the bottle three days in the sun
3. For wetting the linen, mix together a quarter of an ounce of salt of soda, a heaped table-spoonful of powdered gum arabic, and two ounces of hot water
