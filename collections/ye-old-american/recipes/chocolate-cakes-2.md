## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup sugar
- 3 squares Baker's chocolate
- 1 cup stale bread crumbs
- 3 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs until light.
2. Cream the butter, add sugar, combine mixtures, then add chocolate melted, bread crumbs, and flour.
3. Spread mixture in a shallow buttered pan and bake in a slow oven.
4. Shape with a tiny biscuit-cutter and put together in pairs with White Mountain Cream between and on top.
