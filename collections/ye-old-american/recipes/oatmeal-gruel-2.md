## Ingredients

- four tablespoonfuls of the best grits (oatmeal coarsely ground)
- one pint of boiling water
- butter
- wine
- nutmeg
- salt

## Directions

1. Put four tablespoonfuls of the best grits into a pint of boiling water
2. Let it boil gently, and stir it often, till it becomes as thick as you wish it
3. Strain it, and add to it while warm, butter, wine, nutmeg, or whatever is thought proper to flavor it
4. Salt to taste
5. If you make a gruel of fine oatmeal, sift it, mix it first to a thick batter with a little cold water, and then put it into the saucepan of boiling water
6. Stir it all the time it is boiling, lifting the spoon gently up and down, and letting the gruel fall slowly back again into the pan
