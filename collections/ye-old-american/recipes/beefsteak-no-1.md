## Ingredients

- beefsteak, about three-quarters of an inch in thickness
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Have a clear, glowing bed of coals
2. Lay the steak on a buttered gridiron
3. Turn it often as it begins to drip
4. Do not season it until it is done, which will be in about ten to twelve minutes
5. Remove it to a warm platter
6. Pepper and salt it on both sides
7. Spread a liberal lump of butter over it
8. Serve at once while hot
