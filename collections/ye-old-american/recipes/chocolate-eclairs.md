## Ingredients

- half a cup of butter
- one cup of hot water
- a pint of sifted flour
- five eggs
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- a teaspoonful of salt
- chocolate icing

## Directions

1. Make the mixture exactly like the recipe for Boston Cream Cakes.
2. Spread it on buttered pans in oblong pieces about four inches long and one and a half wide, to be laid about two inches apart.
3. Bake in a rather quick oven about twenty-five minutes.
4. As soon as baked ice with chocolate icing.
5. When this is cold split them on one side and fill with the same cream as Boston Cream Cakes.
