## Ingredients

- 1 lb. halibut
- ⅓ cup thick cream
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- 1½ teaspoons lemon juice
- Whites 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook halibut in boiling salted water, drain, and rub through a sieve.
2. Season with salt, cayenne, and lemon juice.
3. Add cream beaten until stiff, then beaten whites of eggs.
4. Turn into small, slightly buttered moulds, put in a pan, half surround with hot water, cover with buttered paper, and bake twenty minutes in a moderate oven.
5. Remove from moulds, arrange on a serving dish, pour around Béchamel Sauce or Lobster Sauce II, and garnish with parsley.
