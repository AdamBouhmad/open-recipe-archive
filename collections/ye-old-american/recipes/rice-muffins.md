## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold boiled rice
- 1 pint of flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 quart of milk, or enough to make thin batter
- 1 tablespoonful lard or butter
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Beat hard and bake quickly
