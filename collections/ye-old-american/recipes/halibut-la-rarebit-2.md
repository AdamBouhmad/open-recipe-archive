## Ingredients

- two small slices halibut
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- melted butter
- Welsh Rarebit

## Directions

1. Sprinkle two small slices halibut with salt, pepper, and lemon juice
2. Brush over with melted butter
3. Place in dripping-pan on greased fish-sheet
4. Bake twelve minutes
5. Remove to hot platter for serving
6. Pour over it a Welsh Rarebit
