## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅔ cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1¾ cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ⅓ cup raisins, stoned and cut in small pieces
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon clove
- ¼ teaspoon mace
- ¼ teaspoon nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, then raisins, egg well beaten, and milk.
2. Mix and sift dry ingredients and add to first mixture.
3. Roll mixture a little thicker than for Vanilla Wafers.
