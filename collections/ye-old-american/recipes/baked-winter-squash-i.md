## Ingredients

- squash
- salt
- pepper
- molasses
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut in pieces two inches square, remove seeds and stringy portion, place in a dripping-pan.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and allow for each square one-half teaspoon molasses and one-half teaspoon melted butter.
3. Bake fifty minutes, or until soft, in a moderate oven, keeping covered the first half-hour of cooking.
4. Serve in the shell.
