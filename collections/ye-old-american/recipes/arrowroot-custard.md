## Ingredients

- 2 cups of boiling milk
- 3 heaping teaspoonfuls arrowroot
- cold milk for wetting the arrowroot
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 egg very well beaten
- vanilla or rose-water for flavoring

## Directions

1. Wet up the arrowroot with a little cold milk
2. Mix the arrowroot paste with the boiling milk
3. Stir three minutes
4. Take from the fire and whip in the egg and sugar
5. Boil two minutes longer
6. Flavor with vanilla or rose-water
7. Pour into moulds
