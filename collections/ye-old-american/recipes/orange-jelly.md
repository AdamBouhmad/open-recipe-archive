## Ingredients

- twenty large ripe oranges
- isinglass
- warm water
- loaf-sugar
- saffron

## Directions

1. Grate the yellow rind from seven of the oranges
2. Dissolve an ounce of isinglass in as much warm water as will cover it
3. Mix the juice with a pound of loaf-sugar broken up
4. Add the grated rind and the isinglass
5. Put it into a porcelain pan over hot coals and stir it till it boils
6. Skim it well
7. Boil it ten minutes and strain it through a jelly-bag till it is quite clear
8. Put it into a mould to congeal
9. When you want to turn it out dip the mould into luke-warm water, or put it into glasses at once
10. A few grains of saffron boiled with the jelly will improve the colour without affecting the taste
