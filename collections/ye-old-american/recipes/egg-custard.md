## Ingredients

- Yolks 2 eggs
- Few grains salt
- 2 tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Beat eggs slightly, add milk and salt.
2. Pour into small buttered cup, place in pan of hot water, and bake until firm.
3. Cool, remove from cup, and cut in fancy shapes with French vegetable cutters.
