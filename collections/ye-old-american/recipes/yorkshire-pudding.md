## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- four eggs
- one teaspoonful of salt
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- two cups of flour
- drippings from the dripping-pan

## Directions

1. Sift baking powder through two cups of flour.
2. Beat egg whites and yolks separately.
3. Mix milk, eggs, salt, and flour mixture very smooth, about the consistency of cream.
4. Regulate your time so that the pudding will be done half an hour or forty minutes before dishing up.
5. Take two common biscuit tins and dip some of the drippings from the dripping-pan into these tins.
6. Pour half of the pudding into each tin.
7. Set them into the hot oven and keep them in until the dinner is dished up.
8. Take these puddings out at the last moment and send to the table hot.
