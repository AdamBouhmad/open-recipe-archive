## Ingredients

- young chickens
- water
- butter, half of a cup
- beaten egg
- salt
- pepper
- powdered thyme, a teaspoonful
- two hard-boiled eggs
- small glass of wine
- jelly

## Directions

1. Select young chickens, clean and cut them into pieces
2. Put them into a stewpan with just enough water to cook them
3. When tender stir into it half of a cup of butter and one beaten egg
4. Season it with salt and pepper, a teaspoonful of powdered thyme
5. Add two hard-boiled eggs coarsely minced and a small glass of wine
6. Boil up once and serve with jelly
