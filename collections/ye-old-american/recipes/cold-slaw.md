## Ingredients

- a head of bleached cabbage
- salt
- pepper
- dressing for cold slaw
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Select the finest head of bleached cabbage
2. Cut up enough into shreds to fill a large vegetable dish or salad bowl
3. Shave very fine and after that chop up, the more thoroughly the better
4. Put this into a dish in which it is to be served, after seasoning it well with salt and pepper
5. Turn over it a dressing made as for cold slaw
6. Mix it well and garnish with slices of hard-boiled eggs
