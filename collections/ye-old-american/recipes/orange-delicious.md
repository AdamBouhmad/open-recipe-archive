## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup water
- 2 cups orange juice
- 1 cup cream
- Yolks two eggs
- 1 cup heavy cream
- ¼ cup shredded candied orange peel

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water eight minutes, then add orange juice
2. Scald cream, add yolks of eggs, and cook over hot water until mixture thickens
3. Cool, add to first mixture with heavy cream beaten stiff
4. Freeze; when nearly frozen, add orange peel
5. Line a melon mould with Orange Ice, fill with Orange Delicious, pack in salt and ice, and let stand one and one-half hours
