## Ingredients

- two cups of sifted meal
- one cup of wheat flour
- one teaspoonful of salt
- three well-beaten eggs
- sour milk
- one teaspoonful of baking soda

## Directions

1. Scald two cups of sifted meal, mix with a cup of wheat flour and a teaspoonful of salt
2. Add three well-beaten eggs
3. Thin the whole with sour milk enough to make it the right consistency
4. Beat the whole till very light and add a teaspoonful of baking soda dissolved in a little water
