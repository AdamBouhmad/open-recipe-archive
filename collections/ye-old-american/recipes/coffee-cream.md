## Ingredients

- two gills raw coffee
- one quart rich, perfectly sweet milk
- eight egg yelks

## Directions

1. Toast two gills of raw coffee till it is a light brown, and not a grain burnt
2. Put it hot from the toaster without grinding it, into a quart of rich, and perfectly sweet milk
3. Boil it, and add the yelks of eight eggs
4. When done, strain it through a sieve, and sweeten it
5. If properly done, it will not be discoloured
