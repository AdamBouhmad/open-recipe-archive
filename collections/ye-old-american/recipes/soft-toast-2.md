## Ingredients

- Two thin slices of stale bread
- A pinch of salt
- Boiling water
- A very small bit of butter

## Directions

1. Toast well, but not too brown, two thin slices of stale bread
2. Put them on a warm plate and sprinkle with a pinch of salt
3. Pour boiling water upon them
4. Quickly cover with another dish of the same size
5. Drain off the water
6. Put a very small bit of butter on the toast
