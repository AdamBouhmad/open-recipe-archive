## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon peppercorns
- 4 cloves
- 3 sprigs thyme
- 1 sprig marjoram
- 2 sprigs parsley
- ½ bay leaf
- 3 quarts cold water
- beef
- marrow from marrow-bone
- veal
- bones
- fowl broth
- carrot
- turnip
- onion
- celery

## Directions

1. Cut beef in one and one-half inch cubes, and brown one-half in some of the marrow from marrow-bone
2. Put remaining half in kettle with cold water, add veal cut in pieces, browned meat, and bones
3. Let stand one-half hour
4. Heat slowly to boiling-point, and let simmer three hours, removing scum as it forms on top of kettle
5. Add one quart liquor in which a fowl was cooked, and simmer two hours
6. Cook carrot, turnip, onion, and celery in butter five minutes; then add to soup, with remaining seasonings
7. Cook one and one-half hours, strain, cool quickly, remove fat, and clear
