## Ingredients

- Two cupfuls of the best Graham meal
- Two cupfuls of water, fresh and cold, or milk and water
- A little salt

## Directions

1. Stir briskly for a minute or two
2. Have the gem-pan hot and well greased, on the top of the stove while pouring in the batter
3. Place in a very hot oven and bake forty minutes
4. Check the heat a little when they are nearly done
