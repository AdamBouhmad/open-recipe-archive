## Ingredients

- syrup made as for Glacé Nuts
- grapes, strawberries, sections of mandarins and oranges, and candied cherries

## Directions

1. Take grapes separately from clusters, leaving a short stem on each grape.
2. Dip in syrup made as for Glacé Nuts, holding by stem with pincers.
3. Remove to oiled paper.
