## Ingredients

- a vanilla bean
- half a pint of milk
- a quart of cream
- a pint of milk
- a table-spoonful of flour
- six eggs
- two table-spoonfuls of milk
- half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Slice a vanilla bean and boil it slowly in half a pint of milk till all the strength is extracted and the milk highly flavoured with the vanilla.
2. Strain it and set it aside.
3. Mix a quart of cream and a pint of milk into a skillet or sauce-pan.
4. Set it on hot coals and boil it.
5. When it has come to a boil, mix a table-spoonful of flour in three table-spoonfuls of milk and stir it into the boiling liquid.
6. Beat six eggs with two table-spoonfuls of milk and pour them slowly into the mixture.
7. Stir it all the time it is boiling.
8. Five minutes after, stir in gradually half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar and then the decoction of vanilla.
9. Stir it hard a few moments and take it off the fire.
10. Set it to cool.
11. When quite cold, put it into a mould and freeze it as you would ice-cream.
