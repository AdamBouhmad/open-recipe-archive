## Ingredients

- half a pint of port wine
- half a pint of strong vinegar
- juice and grated peel of two large lemons
- a quarter of an ounce of cayenne
- a dozen blades of mace
- a quarter of an ounce of powdered cochineal

## Directions

1. Mix together half a pint of port wine, half a pint of strong vinegar, the juice and grated peel of two large lemons, a quarter of an ounce of cayenne, a dozen blades of mace, and a quarter of an ounce of powdered cochineal
2. Let it infuse a fortnight, stirring it several times a day
3. Then boil it ten minutes, strain it, and bottle it for use
