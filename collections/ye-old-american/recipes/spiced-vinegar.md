## Ingredients

- 1 quart cider vinegar
- 1/2 ounce celery seed
- 1/3 ounce dried mint
- 1/3 ounce dried parsley
- 1 garlic
- 3 small onions
- 3 whole cloves
- 1 teaspoonful whole pepper-corns
- 1 teaspoonful grated nutmeg
- salt to taste
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 1 tablespoonful good brandy

## Directions

1. Put all into a jar and cover it well
2. Let it stand for three weeks
3. Strain and bottle it well
