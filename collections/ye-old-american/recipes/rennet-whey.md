## Ingredients

- a small bit of rennet about two inches square
- cold water
- lukewarm water
- a quart pitcher of warm milk

## Directions

1. Wash a small bit of rennet about two inches square, in cold water, to get off the salt.
2. Put it into a tea-cup and pour on it sufficient lukewarm water to cover it.
3. Let it stand all night, and in the morning stir the rennet water into a quart pitcher of warm milk.
4. Cover it, and set it near the fire till a firm curd is formed.
5. Pour off the whey from it, and it will be found an excellent and cooling drink.
