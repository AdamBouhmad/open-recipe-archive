## Ingredients

- Graham flour
- white flour
- Indian meal
- salt
- molasses
- sponge (prepared from potatoes or white flour)

## Directions

1. Prepare a sponge as for white bread, using potatoes or white flour
2. On baking day, take out a certain quantity of the risen sponge and set aside for brown bread
3. Put into a tray two parts Graham flour and one-third white flour
4. To every quart of this flour mixture allow a handful of Indian meal with a teaspoonful of salt
5. Wet this up with the sponge
6. When it is mixed, add for a loaf of fair size half a teacupful of molasses
7. If there is not enough of the sponge to reduce it to the desired consistency, add a little blood-warm water
8. Knead it diligently and long
9. Make into round, comfortable loaves and set down again for the second rising after kneading again
10. Bake steadily, taking care it does not burn
11. Do not cut while hot
12. Brown flour should not be sifted
