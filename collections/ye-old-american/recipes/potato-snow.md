## Ingredients

- mealy potatoes
- salt
- pounded loaf sugar (optional, small quantity)

## Directions

1. Choose some mealy potatoes that will boil exceedingly white; pare them and cook them well, but not so as to be watery.
2. Drain them, and mash and season them well.
3. Put in the saucepan in which they were dressed, so as to keep them as hot as possible.
4. Press them through a wire sieve into the dish in which they are to be served.
5. Strew a little fine salt upon them previous to sending them to table.
6. French cooks also add a small quantity of pounded loaf sugar while they are being mashed.
