## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 3 cups boiling water
- ¾ cup lemon juice
- leaf green
- 1 teaspoon Crême de Menthe per glass
- finely chopped nut meats using almonds, filberts, pecans, and walnuts in equal proportions

## Directions

1. Dissolve two cups sugar in three cups boiling water
2. cool, add three-fourths cup lemon juice, color with leaf green, and freeze
3. Serve in champagne glasses
4. Put one teaspoon Crême de Menthe in each glass, and sprinkle with finely chopped nut meats, using almonds, filberts, pecans, and walnuts in equal proportions
