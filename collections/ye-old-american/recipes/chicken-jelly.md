## Ingredients

- 1 large chicken
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- loaf-sugar
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take a large chicken
2. Cut it up into very small pieces
3. Bruise the bones
4. Put the whole into a stone jar with a cover that will make it water tight
5. Set the jar in a large kettle of boiling water
6. Keep it boiling for three hours
7. Strain off the liquid
8. Season it slightly with salt, pepper, and mace; or with loaf-sugar and lemon
