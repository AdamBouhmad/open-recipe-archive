## Ingredients

- half a pound of the best fresh butter
- twelve large onions
- a little salt
- a little flour
- a quart of boiling water
- some of the upper crust of bread
- the yolks of two beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of the best fresh butter into a stew-pan on the fire, and let it boil till it has done making a noise.
2. Have ready twelve large onions peeled and cut small; throw them into the butter, add a little salt, and stew them a quarter of an hour.
3. Dredge in a little flour, and stir the whole very hard; and in five minutes pour in a quart of boiling water, and some of the upper crust of bread, cut small.
4. Let the soup boil ten minutes longer, stirring it often.
5. After you take it from the fire, stir in the yolks of two beaten eggs, and serve it up immediately.
