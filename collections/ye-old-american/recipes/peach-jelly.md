## Ingredients

- fine juicy free-stone peaches
- loaf-sugar
- peach-kernels
- water

## Directions

1. Pare and quarter the peaches
2. Scald them in a very little water, drain and mash them
3. Squeeze the juice through a jelly-bag
4. To every pint of juice allow a pound of loaf-sugar and a few of the peach-kernels
5. Break up the kernels and boil them by themselves for a quarter of an hour in just as much water as will cover them
6. Strain off the kernel-water and add it to the juice
7. Mix the juice with the sugar
