## Ingredients

- a pound of grated cocoa-nut
- a pint of unskimmed milk
- six ounces of white sugar
- yolks of six eggs
- white of egg

## Directions

1. To a pound of grated cocoa-nut allow a pint of unskimmed milk, and six ounces of white sugar
2. Beat very light the yolks of six eggs
3. Stir them gradually into the milk, alternately with the cocoa-nut and sugar
4. Put the mixture into a pitcher; set it in a vessel of boiling water; place it on hot coals, and simmer it till it is very smooth and thick; stirring it all the time
5. As soon as it comes to a hard boil, take it off the fire; pour it into a large bowl, and set it out to cool
6. When cold, put it into glass cups
7. Beat to a stiff froth the white of egg that was left, and pile it on the custards
