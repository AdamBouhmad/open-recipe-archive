## Ingredients

- One cup flour
- One-fourth teaspoon salt
- One-half cup cold water
- One egg well beaten
- One-half tablespoon vinegar
- One teaspoon olive oil or melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix one cup flour with one-fourth teaspoon salt
2. Add gradually one-half cup cold water
3. When perfectly smooth add one egg well beaten, one-half tablespoon vinegar, and one teaspoon olive oil or melted butter
