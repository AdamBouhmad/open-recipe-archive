## Ingredients

- 1 cup cracker crumbs
- ¼ cup melted butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- 1½ teaspoons lemon juice
- ½ tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 cup oysters
- two tablespoons oyster liquor

## Directions

1. Add seasonings and butter to cracker crumbs.
2. Clean oysters, and remove tough muscles; add soft parts to mixture, with two tablespoons oyster liquor to moisten.
