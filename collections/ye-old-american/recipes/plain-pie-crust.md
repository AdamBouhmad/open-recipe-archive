## Ingredients

- two and a half cupfuls of sifted flour
- one cupful of shortening, half butter and half lard cold
- a pinch of salt
- a heaping teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift the baking powder through the flour.
2. Rub thoroughly the shortening into the flour.
3. Mix together with half a teacupful of cold water, or enough to form a rather stiff dough.
4. Mix as little as possible, just enough to get it into shape to roll out.
5. Handle very lightly.
