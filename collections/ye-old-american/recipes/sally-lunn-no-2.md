## Ingredients

- 1 scant quart flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 teacupful milk
- 1 teacupful lard and butter mixed
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar
- ½ teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs very light, yolks and whites separately
2. Melt the shortening
3. Sift the cream-tartar into the flour
4. Add the whites the last thing
