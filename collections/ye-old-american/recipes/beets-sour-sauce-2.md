## Ingredients

- beets
- salt
- two tablespoons butter
- two tablespoons flour
- one-fourth cup vinegar
- one-fourth cup cream
- one teaspoon sugar
- one-half teaspoon salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash beets, and cook in boiling salted water until soft.
2. Drain, and reserve one-half cup water in which beets were cooked.
3. Plunge into cold water, rub off skins and cut into cubes.
4. Melt two tablespoons butter, add two tablespoons flour, and pour on the beet water.
5. Add one-fourth cup, each, vinegar and cream, one teaspoon sugar, one-half teaspoon salt, and a few grains pepper.
6. Reheat beets in sour sauce.
