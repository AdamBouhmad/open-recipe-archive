## Ingredients

- three tablespoons butter
- one-third cup flour
- one cup White Stock
- one egg yolk
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Melt three tablespoons butter, add one-third cup flour, and pour on gradually one cup White Stock.
2. Then add one egg yolk and season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
