## Ingredients

- two quarts of ripe fresh-gathered raspberries
- a quart of vinegar
- two quarts of fresh raspberries
- a pound of loaf sugar per pint of juice

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of ripe fresh-gathered raspberries into a stone or china vessel, and pour on them a quart of vinegar.
2. Let it stand twenty-four hours, and then strain it through a sieve.
3. Pour the liquid over two quarts of fresh raspberries, and let it again infuse for a day and a night.
4. Then strain it a second time.
5. Allow a pound of loaf sugar to every pint of juice.
6. Break up the sugar, and let it melt in the liquor.
7. Then put the whole into a stone jar, cover it closely, and set it in a kettle of boiling water, which must be kept on a quick boil for an hour.
8. Take off all the scum, and when cold, bottle the vinegar for use.
