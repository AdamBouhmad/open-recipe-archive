## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ¼ cup melted butter
- 1 egg, unbeaten
- 2 squares Baker's chocolate, melted
- ¾ teaspoon vanilla
- ½ cup flour
- ½ cup walnut meats, cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given.
2. Line a seven-inch square pan with paraffine paper.
3. Spread mixture evenly in pan and bake in a slow oven.
4. As soon as taken from oven turn from pan, remove paper, and cut cake in strips, using a sharp knife.
5. If these directions are not followed paper will cling to cake, and it will be impossible to cut it in shapely pieces.
