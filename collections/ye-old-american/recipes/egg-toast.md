## Ingredients

- a slice of bread
- hot water
- salt
- butter
- an egg
- a crumb of salt

## Directions

1. Brown a slice of bread nicely over the coals
2. Dip it in hot water slightly salted and butter it
3. Lay on the top an egg that has been broken into boiling water, and cooked until the white has hardened
4. Season the egg with a bit of butter and a crumb of salt
