## Ingredients

- one cup cold cooked carrot cubes
- one cup cold cooked potato cubes
- one cup cold cooked peas
- one cup cold cooked beans
- French Dressing
- lettuce leaves
- Mayonnaise Dressing
- small pieces of smoked salmon
- whites of hard boiled eggs
- yolks of hard boiled eggs
- parsley or shrimps

## Directions

1. Mix one cup each cold cooked carrot cubes and potato cubes, one cup cold cooked peas, and one cup cold cooked beans, and marinate with French Dressing
2. Arrange on lettuce leaves in four sections
3. Cover each section with Mayonnaise Dressing
4. Garnish two sections with small pieces of smoked salmon, one section with finely chopped whites of hard boiled eggs, and one section with yolks of hard boiled eggs forced through a strainer
5. Put small sprigs of parsley or shrimps in lines dividing sections
