## Ingredients

- salsify roots
- water
- milk
- butter
- flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Scrape the roots, dropping each into cold water as soon as it is cleaned.
2. Cut in pieces an inch long, put into a saucepan with hot water enough to cover them, and stew until tender.
3. Turn off nearly all the water, and add a cupful of cold milk.
4. Stew ten minutes after this begins to boil; put in a great lump of butter, cut into bits, and rolled in flour; pepper and salt to taste.
5. Boil up once, and serve.
