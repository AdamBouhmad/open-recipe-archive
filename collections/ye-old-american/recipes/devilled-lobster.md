## Ingredients

- One boiled lobster
- Mustard
- Cayenne
- Salt
- Pungent sauce
- Hot water
- Vinegar
- Butter

## Directions

1. Extract the meat from a boiled lobster and mince it finely
2. Reserve the coral
3. Season highly with mustard, cayenne, salt, and some pungent sauce
4. Toss and stir until well mixed and put into a porcelain saucepan covered with just enough hot water to keep it from burning
5. Rub the coral smooth, moistening with vinegar until thin enough to pour easily
6. Stir into the contents of the saucepan
7. Let it boil up but once before the coral and vinegar are put in
8. Stir in a heaping tablespoonful of butter
9. When it boils again, take the pan from the fire
