## Ingredients

- corn on the cob
- tomatoes
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil the corn on the cob, when it is in nice order for roasting, twenty minutes over a good fire, and cut off while hot.
2. Have your tomatoes skinned and rubbed to a smooth pulp.
3. Put in two measures of them for every one of the cut corn.
4. Salt as for the table, stirring it well in, and bring to a hard boil.
5. Can quickly, and as soon as they are cold set away in a cool, dark place.
