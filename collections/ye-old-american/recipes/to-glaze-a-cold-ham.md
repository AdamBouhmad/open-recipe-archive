## Ingredients

- ham
- beaten yolk of egg
- pounded cracker or grated crumbs of stale bread
- thick cream

## Directions

1. With a brush or quill feather go all over the ham with beaten yolk of egg
2. Then cover it thickly with pounded cracker, made as fine as flour, or with grated crumbs of stale bread
3. Lastly go over it with thick cream
4. Put it to brown in the oven of a stove, or brown it on the spit of a tin roaster, set before the fire and turned frequently
5. This glazing should be put on half an inch thick, so as to form a crust
