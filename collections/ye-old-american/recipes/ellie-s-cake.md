## Ingredients

- 1 cup of sugar
- ½ cup of butter
- 3 eggs
- ½ cup sweet milk
- 2½ cups prepared flour
- jelly or chocolate for filling

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 cup of sugar, ½ cup of butter, 3 eggs, ½ cup sweet milk, and 2½ cups prepared flour.
2. Bake in jelly-cake tins, and fill with jelly or chocolate.
