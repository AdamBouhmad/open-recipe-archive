## Ingredients

- crabs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- water
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil as lobsters or stew them
2. Take out the meat from the shell
3. Put it into a saucepan with butter, pepper, salt, a pinch of mace and a very little water
4. Dredge with flour
5. Let simmer five minutes over a slow fire
6. Serve hot
