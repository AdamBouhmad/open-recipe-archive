## Ingredients

- Ripe cherries
- Sugar
- Good crust

## Directions

1. Line the dish with a good crust, and fill with ripe cherries, regulating the quantity of sugar you scatter over them by their sweetness.
2. Cover and bake.
3. Eat cold, with white sugar sifted over the top.
