## Ingredients

- fish
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- tomatoes
- parsley
- chopped onion
- water
- pounded cloves
- wine
- mushroom catsup

## Directions

1. Dredge the fish well with flour
2. Sprinkle salt and pepper on them
3. Fry them a nice brown
4. Set them by to get cold
5. Put a quarter of a pound of butter in a frying pan
6. When it boils, fry tomatoes with the skins taken off, parsley nicely picked, and a very little chopped onion
7. When done, add as much water as will make sauce for the fish
8. Season it with pepper, salt, and pounded cloves
9. Add some wine and mushroom catsup
10. Put the fish in and when thoroughly heated, serve it up
