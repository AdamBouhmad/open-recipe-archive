## Ingredients

- salt mackerel
- boiling water
- butter
- sweet cream or rich milk
- pepper

## Directions

1. When the mackerel have soaked over night, put them in a pan and pour on boiling water enough to cover
2. Let them stand a couple of minutes, then drain them off
3. Put them in the pan with a few lumps of butter
4. Pour on a half teacupful of sweet cream, or rich milk, and a little pepper
5. Set in the oven and let it bake a little until brown
