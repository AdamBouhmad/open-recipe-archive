## Ingredients

- 1 cup mashed French chestnuts
- 2 tablespoons thick cream
- Yolks 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla
- crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given.
2. Shape in balls, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again, fry in deep fat, and drain.
