## Ingredients

- capers
- half a pint of drawn butter
- juice from the bottle in which capers are sold

## Directions

1. Chop the capers a very little, unless quite small
2. Make half a pint of drawn butter
3. Add the capers, with a large spoonful of the juice from the bottle in which they are sold
4. Let it just simmer and serve in a tureen
