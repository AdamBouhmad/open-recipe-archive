## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound of butter
- two pounds of sifted flour
- half a pound of powdered sugar
- half a pound of currants
- rich milk

## Directions

1. Rub three quarters of a pound of butter into two pounds of sifted flour, and mix in half a pound of powdered sugar, and half a pound of currants, washed and dried.
2. Wet it to a stiff paste with rich milk.
3. Roll it out, and cut it into cakes.
4. Lay them on buttered baking sheets, and put them into a moderate oven.
