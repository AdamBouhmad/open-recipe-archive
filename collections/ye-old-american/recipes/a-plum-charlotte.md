## Ingredients

- one quart of ripe plums
- bread and butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Stone a quart of ripe plums, first stew and then sweeten them
2. Cut slices of bread and butter and lay them in the bottom and round the sides of a large bowl or deep dish
3. Pour in the plums boiling hot
4. Cover the bowl and set it away to cool gradually
5. When quite cold, send it to table and eat it with cream
