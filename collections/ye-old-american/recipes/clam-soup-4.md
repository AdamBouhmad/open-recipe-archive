## Ingredients

- Twenty-five clams
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter (as large as an egg)
- One quart of milk
- One tablespoon of flour
- Cold milk
- Two crackers (optional)
- Mace (optional)
- Lemon juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop twenty-five clams fine
2. Put over the fire the liquor that was drained from them and a cup of water
3. Add the chopped clams and boil half an hour
4. Season to taste with pepper and salt and a piece of butter as large as an egg
5. Boil up again and add one quart of milk boiling hot
6. Stir in a tablespoon of flour made to a cream with a little cold milk, or two crackers rolled fine
7. Some like a little mace and lemon juice in the seasoning
