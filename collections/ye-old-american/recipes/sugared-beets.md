## Ingredients

- 4 hot boiled beets
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1½ tablespoons sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Cut beets in one-fourth inch slices
2. Add butter, sugar, and salt
3. Reheat for serving
