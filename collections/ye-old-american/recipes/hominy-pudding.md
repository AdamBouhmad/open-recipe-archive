## Ingredients

- two-thirds of a cupful of hominy
- one and a half pints of milk
- two eggs
- one tablespoonful of butter
- one teaspoonful of extract of lemon or vanilla
- one cupful of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil hominy in milk one hour
2. Pour it on the eggs, extract and sugar beaten together
3. Add butter, pour in buttered pudding-dish
4. Bake in hot oven for twenty minutes
