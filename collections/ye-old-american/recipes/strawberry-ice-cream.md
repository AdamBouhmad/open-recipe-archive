## Ingredients

- two quarts of ripe strawberries
- half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- half a pound more of powdered sugar
- two quarts of rich cream

## Directions

1. Take two quarts of ripe strawberries; hull them, and put them into a deep dish, strewing among them half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
2. Cover them, and let them stand an hour or two
3. Then mash them through a sieve till you have pressed out all the juice, and stir into it half a pound more of powdered sugar, or enough to make it very sweet, and like a thick syrup
4. Then mix it by degrees with two quarts of rich cream, beating it in very hard
5. Put it into a freezer, and proceed as in the foregoing receipt
6. In two hours, remove it to a mould, or take it out and return it again to the freezer with fresh salt and ice, that it may be frozen a second time
7. In one hour more, it should be ready to turn out
