## Ingredients

- one quart of bread crumbs
- a cup of hot milk
- one cupful of molasses
- one cupful of fruit or chopped raisins
- one teaspoonful each of spices
- one tablespoonful of butter
- a teaspoonful of salt
- one teaspoonful of soda
- about a cupful of flour sifted
- sweet sauce

## Directions

1. To one quart of bread crumbs soaked soft in a cup of hot milk, add one cupful of molasses, one cupful of fruit or chopped raisins, one teaspoonful each of spices, one tablespoonful of butter, a teaspoonful of salt, one teaspoonful of soda, about a cupful of flour sifted
2. Boil or steam three hours
3. Serve with sweet sauce
