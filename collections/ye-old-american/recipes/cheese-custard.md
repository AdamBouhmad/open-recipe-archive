## Ingredients

- two ounces of grated parmesan cheese
- whites of three eggs
- pepper
- salt
- cayenne
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of three eggs to a stiff froth
2. Mix with grated parmesan cheese, pepper, salt, cayenne, and milk or cream
3. Bake for a quarter of an hour
