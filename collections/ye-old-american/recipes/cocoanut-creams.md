## Ingredients

- uncooked cream
- fresh grated cocoanut
- confectioners' sugar
- cochineal syrup
- grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Make the uncooked cream as in the French Vanilla Cream recipe
2. Take the cream while soft, add fresh grated cocoanut to taste
3. Add sufficient confectioners' sugar to mold into balls
4. Roll the balls in the fresh grated cocoanut
5. These may be colored pink with a few drops of cochineal syrup, also brown by adding a few spoonfuls of grated chocolate; then rolling them in grated cocoanut
6. The coconut cream may be made into a flat cake and cut into squares or strips
