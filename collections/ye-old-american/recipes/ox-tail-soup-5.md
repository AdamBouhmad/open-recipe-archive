## Ingredients

- 1 small ox-tail
- 6 cups Brown Stock
- ½ cup carrot, cut in fancy shapes
- ½ cup turnip, cut in fancy shapes
- ½ cup onion, cut in small pieces
- ½ cup celery, cut in small pieces
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- ¼ cup Madeira wine
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut ox-tail in small pieces, wash, drain, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dredge with flour, and fry in butter ten minutes.
2. Add to Brown Stock, and simmer one hour.
3. Then add vegetables, which have been parboiled twenty minutes.
4. Simmer until vegetables are soft, add salt, cayenne, wine, Worcestershire Sauce, and lemon juice.
