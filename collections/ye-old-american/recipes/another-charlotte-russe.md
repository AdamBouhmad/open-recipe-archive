## Ingredients

- two tablespoonfuls of gelatine
- a little cold milk
- two coffeecupfuls of rich cream
- one teacupful of milk
- powdered sugar
- extract of vanilla
- lady-fingers or sponge cake

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in a little cold milk for two hours
2. Whip the cream stiff in a large bowl or dish and set on ice
3. Boil the milk and pour gradually over the gelatine until dissolved, then strain
4. When nearly cold, add the whipped cream, a spoonful at a time
5. Sweeten with powdered sugar and flavor with extract of vanilla
6. Line a dish with lady-fingers or sponge cake
7. Pour in cream and set in a cool place to harden
