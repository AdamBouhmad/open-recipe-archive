## Ingredients

- cold venison
- bones and trimmings
- water
- butter
- flour
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Cut the meat in nice small slices, and put the trimmings and bones into a sauce-pan with barely water enough to cover them
2. Let them stew for an hour
3. Then strain the liquid into a stew-pan; add to it some bits of butter rolled in flour, and whatever gravy was left of the venison the day before
4. Stir in some currant jelly, and give it a boil up
5. Then put in the meat, and keep it over the fire just long enough to warm it through; but do not allow it to boil, as it has been once cooked already
