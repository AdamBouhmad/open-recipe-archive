## Ingredients

- sprigs of green mint
- white sugar
- water
- pounded ice
- 2 tablespoonfuls fine brandy

## Directions

1. Slightly bruise some sprigs of green mint in a tumbler with a teaspoon.
2. Put in a generous teaspoonful of white sugar.
3. Add gradually, stirring and rubbing lightly, enough water to fill the glass three-quarters of the way to the top.
4. Fill up with pounded ice and stir hard.
5. Pour into a larger glass that you may shake up well.
6. Add 2 tablespoonfuls fine brandy.
