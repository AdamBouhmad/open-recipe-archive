## Ingredients

- one cupful of rye flour
- two cupfuls of corn meal
- one cupful of white flour
- half a teacupful of molasses or sugar
- a teaspoonful of salt
- sour milk
- a level teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a tablespoonful of water

## Directions

1. Stir all together thoroughly
2. wet up with sour milk
3. add a level teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a tablespoonful of water
4. Stir the batter as thick as can be with a spoon
5. turn into well-greased pans
