## Ingredients

- ¾ cup finely chopped popped corn
- ¾ tablespoon soft butter
- White 1 egg
- ⅓ cup sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- Blanched and chopped almonds
- Candied cherries

## Directions

1. Add butter to corn.
2. Beat egg white until stiff, and add sugar gradually, continuing the beating.
3. Combine mixtures; then add salt and vanilla.
4. Drop mixture from tip of spoon on a well buttered sheet, one inch apart.
5. Shape in circular form with case knife first dipped in cold water.
6. Sprinkle with almonds and place a piece of candied cherry on the centre of each.
7. Bake in a slow oven until delicately browned.
