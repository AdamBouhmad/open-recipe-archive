## Ingredients

- oysters
- egg
- cracker meal
- butter
- tomato sauce
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the oysters in egg batter and fine cracker meal.
2. Fry in butter over a slow fire for about ten minutes.
3. Cover the hollow of a hot platter with tomato sauce.
4. Place the oysters in it, but not covering.
5. Garnish with chopped parsley sprinkled over the oysters.
