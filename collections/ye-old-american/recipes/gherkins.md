## Ingredients

- gherkins
- salt and water
- alum
- vinegar
- mace, whole
- ginger, sliced

## Directions

1. Wipe them dry, put them into a broad stone jar
2. Scald them five or six times in the course of the day with salt and water strong enough to bear an egg, and let them set all night
3. This will make them yellow
4. Next day, having drained them from the salt and water, throw it out, wipe them dry
5. Put them into a clean vessel with a little piece of alum
6. Scald them with boiling vinegar and water, half and half of each, repeating it frequently during the day till they are green
7. Keep them as closely covered as possible
8. Put them away in stone jars, mixing among them whole mace and sliced ginger to your taste
9. Fill up with cold vinegar, and add a little alum, allowing to every hundred gherkins a piece about the size of a shelled almond
