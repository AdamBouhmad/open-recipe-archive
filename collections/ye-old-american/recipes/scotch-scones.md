## Ingredients

- One quart of sifted flour, loosely measured
- Two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- One tablespoonful of cold butter
- One teaspoonful of salt
- Sweet milk
- Flour for dusting

## Directions

1. Thoroughly mix while dry the flour with baking powder
2. Rub into it the butter and salt
3. Ensure the butter is well worked in
4. Add sweet milk enough to make a very soft paste
5. Roll out the paste about a quarter of an inch thick, using plenty of flour on the paste-board and rolling pin
6. Cut it into triangular pieces, each side about four inches long
7. Flour the sides and bottom of a biscuit tin
8. Place the pieces on it
9. Bake immediately in a quick oven from twenty to thirty minutes
10. When half done, brush over with sweet milk
