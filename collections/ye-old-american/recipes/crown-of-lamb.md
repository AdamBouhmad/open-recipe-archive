## Ingredients

- two loins of lamb containing ribs
- fat salt pork
- buttered paper
- Purée of Chestnuts

## Directions

1. Select parts from two loins containing ribs
2. Scrape flesh from bone between ribs as far as lean meat
3. Trim off backbone
4. Shape each piece in a semicircle with ribs outside
5. Sew pieces together to form a crown
6. Trim ends of bones evenly, being careful not to leave them too long
7. Wrap each bone in a thin strip of fat salt pork or insert in cubes of fat salt pork to prevent bone from burning
8. Cover with buttered paper
9. Roast one and one-fourth hours
10. Remove pork from bones before serving
11. Fill centre with Purée of Chestnuts
