## Ingredients

- one quarter of a pound rice
- a stick of cinnamon
- to a quart of milk (stirred often to keep from burning)
- half a nutmeg
- 4 spoons rose-water
- 8 eggs
- butter or puff paste a dish

## Directions

1. boil quick
2. cool and add half a nutmeg
3. 4 spoons rose-water
4. 8 eggs
5. butter or puff paste a dish
6. and pour the above composition into it
7. and bake one and half hour
