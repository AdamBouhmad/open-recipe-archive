## Ingredients

- one can chopped corn
- two eggs
- one teaspoon salt
- one-eighth teaspoon pepper
- one and one-half tablespoons melted butter
- one pint scalded milk

## Directions

1. To one can chopped corn add two eggs slightly beaten, one teaspoon salt, one-eighth teaspoon pepper, one and one-half tablespoons melted butter, and one pint scalded milk.
2. Turn into a buttered pudding-dish.
3. Bake in slow oven until firm.
