## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 1¾ cups flour
- 2½ teaspoons baking powder
- Cream Filling
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix as One Egg Cake.
2. Bake in round layer cake pans.
3. Put Cream Filling between layers and sprinkle top with powdered sugar.
