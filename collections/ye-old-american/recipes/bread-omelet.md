## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup stale bread crumbs
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs fifteen minutes in milk.
2. Add beaten yolks and seasonings.
3. Fold in whites.
4. Cook and serve as Plain Omelet.
