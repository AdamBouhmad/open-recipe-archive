## Ingredients

- one teacupful of butter
- two teacupfuls of fine white sugar
- one orange
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat to a cream one teacupful of butter and two teacupfuls of fine white sugar
2. Stir in the grated rind of one orange and the juice of two
3. Stir until all the orange juice is absorbed
4. Grate nutmeg upon the sauce and serve on a flat dish
