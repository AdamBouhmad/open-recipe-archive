## Ingredients

- slices of stale bread or stale cake
- hot lard
- boiling milk
- powdered sugar or preserves
- sweet wine sauce

## Directions

1. Cut bread in rounds or use stale cake
2. Fry them in hot lard, like crullers, to a light brown
3. Dip each slice when fried in boiling milk, to remove the grease
4. Drain quickly
5. Dust with powdered sugar or spread with preserves
6. Pile on a hot plate and serve
7. Pour sweet wine sauce over them if desired
