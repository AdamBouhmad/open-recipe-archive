## Ingredients

- equal quantity okra
- equal quantity tomatoes
- butter
- onion
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Let the okra be young and slice it
2. Skin the tomatoes
3. Put them into a pan without water
4. Add a lump of butter, an onion chopped fine, some pepper and salt
5. Stew them one hour
