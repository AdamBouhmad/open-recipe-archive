## Ingredients

- a large onion
- butter
- rich brown gravy from roast beef
- mustard
- salt
- pepper
- a tablespoonful of Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Slice a large onion and fry in butter till it is brown
2. Cover the onion with rich brown gravy, which is left from roast beef
3. Add mustard, salt and pepper, and if you choose a tablespoonful of Worcestershire sauce
4. Let this boil up, and if too thick, thin it with a little stock or gravy, or even a little hot water with butter
5. Pour this when done through a fine sieve
