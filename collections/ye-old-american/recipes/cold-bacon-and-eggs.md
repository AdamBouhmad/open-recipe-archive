## Ingredients

- bacon
- eggs
- potato
- bacon gravy
- raw egg
- cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Put leftover bacon and eggs in a wooden bowl and chop them quite fine.
2. Add a little mashed or cold chopped potato, and a little bacon gravy, if any was left.
3. Mix and mould it into little balls.
4. Roll in raw egg and cracker crumbs.
5. Fry in a spider the same as frying eggs; fry a light brown on both sides.
6. Serve hot.
