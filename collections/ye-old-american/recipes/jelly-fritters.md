## Ingredients

- 1 scant cup sponge-cake crumbs—very fine and dry
- 1 cup boiling milk
- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoonful corn-starch, wet in a little cold milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls currant or cranberry jelly

## Directions

1. Soak the cake-crumbs in the boiling milk, and stir in the corn-starch.
2. Heat all together to a boil, stirring all the time.
3. Beat the yolks light, and add to this as it cools, with the sugar.
4. Whip in the jelly, a little at a time, and put in the whites—beaten to a stiff froth—at the last.
5. Fry immediately.
