## Ingredients

- a dozen tomatoes
- a small head of cabbage
- a good-sized onion
- fine bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- a cup of sweet cream
- butter

## Directions

1. From the blossom end of a dozen tomatoes--smooth, ripe and solid--cut a thin slice and with a small spoon scoop out the pulp without breaking the rind surrounding it.
2. Chop a small head of cabbage and a good-sized onion fine and mix with them fine bread crumbs and the pulp.
3. Season with pepper, salt and sugar and add a cup of sweet cream.
4. When all is well mixed, fill the tomato shells, replace the slices and place the tomatoes in a buttered baking-dish, cut ends up.
5. Put in the pan just enough water to keep from burning.
6. Drop a small lump of butter on each tomato and bake half an hour or so, till well done.
7. Place another bit of butter on each and serve in same dish.
