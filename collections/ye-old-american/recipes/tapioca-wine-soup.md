## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup pearl tapioca
- 1 cup cold water
- 3 cups boiling water
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 3–inch piece stick cinnamon
- 1 pint claret wine
- ½ cup powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca in cold water two hours.
2. Drain, add to boiling water with salt and cinnamon; let boil three minutes, then cook in double boiler until tapioca is transparent.
3. Cool, add wine and sugar.
4. Serve very cold.
