## Ingredients

- 1½ cups flour
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup cottolene or cocoanut butter
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Mix salt with flour
2. Cut in shortening with knife
3. Moisten to dough with cold water
4. Toss on floured board
5. Pat, roll out, and roll up like a jelly roll
6. Use one-third cup of shortening if a richer paste is desired
