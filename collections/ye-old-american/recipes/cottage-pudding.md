## Ingredients

- 1 cup of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 cups flour, or enough to make a tolerably stiff batter
- ½ teaspoonful of soda
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar, sifted with the flour
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Rub the butter and sugar together, beat in the yolks, then the milk and soda, the salt, and the beaten whites, alternately with the flour.
2. Bake in a buttered mould; turn out upon a dish; cut in slices, and eat with liquid sauce.
