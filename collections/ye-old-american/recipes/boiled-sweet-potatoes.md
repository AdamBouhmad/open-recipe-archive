## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes of uniform size
- cold water

## Directions

1. Have them all as nearly the same size as possible
2. Put into cold water, without any salt
3. Boil until a fork will easily pierce the largest
4. Turn off the water, and lay them in the oven to dry for five minutes
5. Peel before sending to table
