## Ingredients

- six eggs
- one pint of rich milk
- apples or peaches
- flour
- one small cup of melted butter
- sugar
- butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs very light
2. add a pint of rich milk
3. pare some apples or peaches and slice them thin
4. make the eggs and milk into a tolerably thick batter with flour
5. add a small cup of melted butter
6. put in the fruit
7. bake it in a deep dish
8. eat with sugar, butter, and nutmeg
