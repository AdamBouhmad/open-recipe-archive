## Ingredients

- Venison steaks
- Butter
- Two tablespoons citron, cut in julienne-shaped pieces
- Two tablespoons glacéd cherries
- One tablespoon Sultana raisins
- Port wine
- One-half tumbler currant jelly
- One and one-third cups Brown Sauce
- Two tablespoons shredded almonds

## Directions

1. Cut venison steaks in circular pieces and use trimmings for the making of stock.
2. Sauté steaks in hot buttered frying-pan.
3. Soak citron, glacéd cherries, and Sultana raisins in Port wine for several hours.
4. Drain and cook fruit five minutes in one-third cup Port wine.
5. Add currant jelly, and as soon as jelly is dissolved, add Brown Sauce and shredded almonds.
6. Serve steaks with Cumberland Sauce.
