## Ingredients

- artichokes
- Chicken Force-meat I or II
- Thin White Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook as Boiled Artichokes, having them slightly underdone
2. Fill with Chicken Force-meat I or II
3. Bake thirty minutes in a moderate oven, basting twice with Thin White Sauce
4. Remove to serving dish and pour around Thin White Sauce
