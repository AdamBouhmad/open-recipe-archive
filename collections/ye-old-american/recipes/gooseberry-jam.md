## Ingredients

- gooseberries
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pick the gooseberries just as they begin to turn.
2. Stem, wash and weigh.
3. To four pounds of fruit add half a teacupful of water and boil until soft.
4. Add four pounds of sugar and boil until clear.
