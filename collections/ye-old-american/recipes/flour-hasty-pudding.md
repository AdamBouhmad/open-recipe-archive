## Ingredients

- peach-leaves
- a quart of milk
- wheat flour
- butter
- egg

## Directions

1. Tie together half a dozen peach-leaves, put them into a quart of milk, and set it on the fire to boil.
2. When it has come to a hard boil, take out the leaves, but let the pot remain boiling on the fire.
3. With a large wooden spoon in one hand, and some wheat flour in the other, thicken and stir it till it is about the consistence of a boiled custard.
4. Throw in, one at a time, a dozen small bits of butter rolled in a thick coat of flour.
5. You may enrich it by stirring in a beaten egg or two, a few minutes before you take it from the fire.
6. When done, pour it into a deep dish, and strew brown sugar thickly over the top.
7. Eat it warm.
