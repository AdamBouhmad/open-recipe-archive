## Ingredients

- nine large oysters
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take nine large oysters out of the shell
2. Wash and dry them
3. Roast over a charcoal fire on a broiler
4. Two minutes after the shells open they will be done
5. Take them off quickly, saving the juice in a small shallow tin pan
6. Keep hot until all are done
7. Butter them and sprinkle with pepper
8. Pour over a slice of toast if desired
