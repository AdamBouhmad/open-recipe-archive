## Ingredients

- milk
- cinnamon stick
- sweet almonds
- rose-water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil two quarts of milk with a stick of cinnamon and let it stand to be quite cold, first taking out the cinnamon
2. Blanch four ounces of the best sweet almonds, pound them in a marble mortar with a little rose-water
3. Mix them well with the milk, sweeten it to your taste
4. Let it boil a few minutes only, lest the almonds should be oily
5. Strain it through a very fine sieve till quite smooth, and free from the almonds
6. Serve it up either cold or lukewarm, in glasses with handles
