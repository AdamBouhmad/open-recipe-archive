## Ingredients

- 2 cups scalded milk
- ¼ cup sugar or ⅓ cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 yeast cake dissolved in ¼ cup lukewarm water
- 4⅔ cups coarse entire wheat flour

## Directions

1. Add sweetening and salt to milk; cool, and when lukewarm add dissolved yeast cake and flour
2. Beat well, cover, and let rise to double its bulk
3. Again beat, and turn into greased bread pans, having pans one-half full
4. Let rise, and bake
5. Entire Wheat Bread should not quite double its bulk during last rising
6. This mixture may be baked in gem pans
