## Ingredients

- 1 sweetbread
- ⅓ teaspoon finely chopped shallot
- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons chopped mushrooms
- 2½ tablespoons flour
- ½ cup stock
- 2 tablespoons cream
- 1 tablespoon Sherry wine
- 1 egg yolk
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Parboil a sweetbread, drain, place in a small mould, cover, and press with a weight.
2. Cut in one-half inch slices, and spread with the following mixture.
3. Fry one-third teaspoon finely chopped shallot in one and one-half tablespoons butter three minutes.
4. Add three tablespoons chopped mushrooms, and cook three minutes.
5. Add two and one-half tablespoons flour, one-half cup stock, two tablespoons cream, one tablespoon Sherry wine, one egg yolk, and salt and pepper to taste.
6. Cool, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs, fry in deep fat, and drain.
