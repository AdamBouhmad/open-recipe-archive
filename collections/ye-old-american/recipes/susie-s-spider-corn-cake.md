## Ingredients

- 1¼ cups corn meal
- 2 cups sour milk
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Mix soda, salt, and corn meal; gradually add eggs well beaten and milk.
2. Heat frying-pan, grease sides and bottom of pan with butter, turn in the mixture, place on middle grate in hot oven, and cook twenty minutes.
