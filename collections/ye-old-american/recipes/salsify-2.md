## Ingredients

- salsify roots
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Having scraped the salsify roots, and washed them in cold water, parboil them.
2. Then take them out, drain them, cut them into large pieces and fry them in butter.
3. Salsify is frequently stewed slowly till quite tender, and then served up with melted butter.
4. Or it may be first boiled, then grated, and made into cakes to be fried in butter.
