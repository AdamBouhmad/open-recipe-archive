## Ingredients

- chickens
- flour
- salt
- boiling lard
- mush
- parsley
- half a pint of rich milk
- butter
- pepper
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut them up as for the fricassee
2. Dredge them well with flour
3. Sprinkle them with salt
4. Put them into a good quantity of boiling lard and fry them a light brown
5. Fry small pieces of mush and a quantity of parsley nicely picked to be served in the dish with the chickens
6. Take half a pint of rich milk and add to it a small bit of butter, with pepper, salt, and chopped parsley
7. Stew it a little and pour it over the chickens
8. Garnish with the fried parsley
