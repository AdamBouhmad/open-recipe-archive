## Ingredients

- Damsons
- water
- sugar
- paste
- flour

## Directions

1. Stew the damsons whole in water only sufficient to prevent their burning
2. When tender and while hot, sweeten them with sugar
3. Let them stand until they become cold
4. Pour them into pie dishes lined with paste
5. Dredge flour upon them
6. Cover them with the same paste
7. Wet and pinch together the edges of the paste
8. Cut a slit in the centre of the cover through which the vapor may escape
9. Bake twenty minutes
