## Ingredients

- veal
- three or four crackers
- cold ham
- gravy
- milk
- butter
- beaten egg
- puff-paste
- parsley
- oyster liquor

## Directions

1. Mince the veal as above, and roll three or four crackers to powder.
2. Chop up some cold ham and mix with the veal in the proportion of one-third ham and two-thirds veal.
3. Add the cracker, and wet well with gravy and a little milk.
4. If you have no gravy, stir into a cup of hot milk two tablespoonfuls of butter and a beaten egg.
5. Season well to your taste, and bake in pâté pans lined with puff-paste.
6. If eaten hot, send to table in the tins.
7. If cold, slip the pâtés out and pile upon a plate, with sprigs of parsley between.
8. A little oyster liquor is a marked improvement to the gravy.
