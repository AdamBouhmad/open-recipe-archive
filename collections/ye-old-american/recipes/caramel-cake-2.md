## Ingredients

- 3 cups sugar
- 1½ cups butter
- 1 cup milk
- 4½ cups prepared flour
- 5 eggs
- 1½ cup brown sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls cold water
- half a cake Baker's chocolate (grated)
- a pinch of soda
- a large teaspoonful vanilla

## Directions

1. For the cake: Combine 3 cups sugar, 1½ cups butter, 1 cup milk, 4½ cups prepared flour, and 5 eggs and bake as for jelly-cake.
2. For the caramel filling: Boil together 1½ cup brown sugar, ½ cup milk, 1 cup molasses, 1 teaspoonful butter, 1 tablespoonful flour, and 2 tablespoonfuls cold water for five minutes.
3. Add half a cake Baker's chocolate (grated), and boil until it is the consistency of rich custard.
4. Add a pinch of soda, stir well, and remove from fire.
5. When cold, flavor with a large teaspoonful vanilla, and spread between the layers of cake.
6. Cover the top with the same, and set in an open, sunny window to dry.
