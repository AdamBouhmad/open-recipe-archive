## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 tablespoons lard
- ⅔ cup butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Wash butter
2. Mix salt with flour
3. Put in chopping tray
4. Add lard and butter
5. Chop until well mixed
6. Moisten to a dough with cold water
7. Toss on floured cloth
8. Pat and roll out
9. Fold so as to make three layers
10. Turn half-way round
11. Pat and roll out
12. Repeat
