## Ingredients

- One quart of milk
- three cupfuls of boiled and strained pumpkin
- one and one-half cupfuls of sugar
- one-half cupful of molasses
- yolks and whites of four eggs beaten separately
- a little salt
- one tablespoonful of ginger
- one tablespoonful of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat all ingredients together
2. Bake with an under crust
