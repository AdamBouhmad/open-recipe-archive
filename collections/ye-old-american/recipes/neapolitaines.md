## Ingredients

- one cup of powdered sugar
- half a cup of butter
- two tablespoonfuls of lemon juice
- three whole eggs
- three yolks
- three cups of sifted flour
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- one tablespoonful of milk
- beaten egg and milk
- chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Put all together with half a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a tablespoonful of milk
2. If it is too stiff to roll out, add just enough more milk
3. Roll it a quarter of an inch thick and cut it out with any tin cutter
4. Place the cakes in a pan slightly greased and color the tops with beaten egg and milk, with some chopped almonds over them
5. Bake in a rather quick oven
