## Ingredients

- two pints of rich milk
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- eight eggs
- half a pound of flour
- a large table-spoonful of strong fresh yeast
- butter
- powdered cinnamon
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Put two pints of rich milk into separate pans.
2. Cut up and melt in one of them a quarter of a pound of butter, warming it slightly.
3. When it is melted, stir it about, and set it away to cool.
4. Beat eight eggs till very light, and mix them gradually into the other pan of milk, alternately with half a pound of flour.
5. Mix in by degrees the milk that has the butter in it.
6. Stir in a large table-spoonful of strong fresh yeast.
7. Cover the pan, and set it near the fire to rise.
8. When the batter is quite light, heat your waffle-iron by putting it among the coals of a clear bright fire.
9. Grease the inside with butter tied in a rag, and then put in some batter.
10. Shut the iron closely, and when the waffle is done on one side, turn the iron on the other.
11. Take the cake out by slipping a knife underneath.
12. Heat and grease the iron for another waffle.
13. Send them to table quite hot, four or six on a plate, having buttered them and strewed over each a mixture of powdered cinnamon and white sugar.
