## Ingredients

- 1 apple, chopped fine
- 1 egg
- 1 lemon, chop the inside very fine and grate the rind
- 1 cup sugar
- Butter, the size of a walnut

## Directions

1. Take the thick white rind off the lemon before you chop it.
2. Take out the seeds carefully.
3. Bake in pie-crust.
