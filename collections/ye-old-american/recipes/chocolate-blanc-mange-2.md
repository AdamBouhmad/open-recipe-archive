## Ingredients

- Half a box of gelatine
- One cupful of water
- Half a cupful of grated chocolate
- A little milk
- Two cupfuls of milk
- One cupful of sugar
- Whites of three eggs
- Vanilla for flavoring
- Custard made of the yolks or sugar and cream for serving

## Directions

1. Soak half a box of gelatine in a cupful of water for an hour
2. Rub half a cupful of grated chocolate smooth in a little milk
3. Boil two cupfuls of milk
4. Add the gelatine and chocolate and one cupful of sugar
5. Boil all together eight or ten minutes
6. Remove from the fire and when nearly cold beat into this the whipped whites of three eggs, flavored with vanilla
7. Set the molds in a cold place
8. Serve cold with custard made of the yolks or sugar and cream
