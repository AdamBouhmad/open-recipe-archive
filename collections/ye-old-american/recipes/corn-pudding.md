## Ingredients

- twelve ears of tender, green, uncooked corn
- four eggs
- sugar
- flour
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. Scrape the substance out of twelve ears of tender, green, uncooked corn.
2. Add yolks and whites, beaten separately, of four eggs, a teaspoonful of sugar, the same of flour mixed in a tablespoonful of butter, a small quantity of salt and pepper, and one pint of milk.
3. Bake about half or three-quarters of an hour.
