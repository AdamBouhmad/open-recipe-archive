## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- ½ cup shortening, butter and lard mixed
- 2½ cups bread flour
- 1 tablespoon ginger
- 1 tablespoon soda
- 2 tablespoons warm milk
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Heat molasses to boiling-point, add shortening, ginger, soda dissolved in warm milk, salt, and flour.
2. Proceed as for Ginger Snaps.
