## Ingredients

- pig head
- pigs' ears
- pigs' tongue
- salted water
- salt
- pepper
- sage
- sweet marjoram
- powdered cloves
- strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil the head, ears and tongue in salted water until very tender.
2. Strip the meat from the bones and chop fine.
3. Season with salt, pepper, sage, sweet marjoram, a little powdered cloves, and half a cup of strong vinegar.
4. Mix all together thoroughly, taste to see that it is flavored sufficiently, remembering that the spice tends to keep it.
5. Pack hard in moulds or bowls, interspersing the layers with bits of the tongue cut in oblongs, squares and triangles not less than an inch in length.
6. Press down and keep the meat in shape by putting a plate on the top of each mould (first wetting the plate) and a weight upon this.
7. In two days the cheese will be ready for use.
