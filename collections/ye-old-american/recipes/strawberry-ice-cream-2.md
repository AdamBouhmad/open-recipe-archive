## Ingredients

- a cupful of sugar
- a quart of ripe strawberries
- a full cupful of sugar
- a quart of fresh thick cream

## Directions

1. Mix a cupful of sugar with a quart of ripe strawberries
2. Let them stand half a day
3. Mash and strain them through a coarse towel
4. Add to the juice a full cupful of sugar
5. When dissolved, beat in a quart of fresh thick cream
