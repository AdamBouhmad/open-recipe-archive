## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- a pound of flour sifted
- a pound of powdered sugar
- a pound of suet finely shred
- a pint of milk
- a nutmeg grated
- a gill of brandy
- a pound of currants washed, picked, and dried
- a pound of raisins stoned and floured

## Directions

1. Beat eight eggs very light
2. Add to them a pound of flour sifted, and a pound of powdered sugar
3. When it looks quite light, put in a pound of suet finely shred, a pint of milk, a nutmeg grated, and a gill of brandy
4. Mix with it a pound of currants, washed, picked, and dried, and a pound of raisins stoned and floured
5. Tie it in a thick cloth, and boil it steadily eight hours
