## Ingredients

- rhubarb stalks
- powdered loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the rhubarb stalks and cut them into small square pieces.
2. Then weigh them, and to each pound allow three quarters of a pound of powdered loaf-sugar.
3. Put the sugar and the rhubarb into a large, deep, white pan, in alternate layers, the top layer to be of sugar.
4. Cover it, and let it stand all night.
5. In the morning, put it into a preserving kettle, and boil it slowly till the whole is dissolved into a thick mass, stirring it frequently, and skimming it before every stirring.
6. Put it warm into glass jars, and tie it up with brandy paper.
