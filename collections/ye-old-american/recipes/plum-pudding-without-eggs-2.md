## Ingredients

- one cupful of finely-chopped beef suet
- two cupfuls of fine bread crumbs
- one cupful of molasses
- one cupful of chopped raisins
- one cupful of well-washed currants
- one spoonful of salt
- one teaspoonful of cloves
- one teaspoonful of cinnamon
- one teaspoonful of allspice
- one teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
- one cupful of milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Stir thoroughly together the finely-chopped beef suet, fine bread crumbs, molasses, chopped raisins, well-washed currants, salt, cloves, cinnamon, allspice and carbonate of soda
2. Add one cupful of milk and flour enough to make a stiff batter
3. Put into a well-greased pudding-mold, or a three-quart pail and cover closely
4. Set this pail into a larger kettle, close covered, and half full of boiling water, adding boiling water as it boils away
5. Steam not less than four hours
6. Serve with wine sauce or common sweet sauce
