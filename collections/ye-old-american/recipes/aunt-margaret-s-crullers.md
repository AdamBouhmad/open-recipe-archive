## Ingredients

- 1 lb. butter
- 1½ lb. powdered sugar
- 12 eggs
- Mace and nutmeg to taste
- Flour to roll out stiff

## Directions

1. Roll out in a thin sheet, cut into shapes with a jagging-iron, and fry in plenty of boiling lard
2. Test the heat first by dropping in one. It should rise almost instantly to the surface
3. Crullers and doughnuts soak in fat at the bottom of the kettle. These should be a fine yellow
4. If the fat becomes so hot that the crullers brown before they puff out to their full dimensions, take the kettle from the fire for a few minutes
5. Have enough cut out before you begin to fry them, to keep a good supply all the while on the fire
6. If you undertake the task alone, cut out all before cooking one
