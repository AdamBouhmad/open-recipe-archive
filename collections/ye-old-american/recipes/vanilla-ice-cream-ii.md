## Ingredients

- 2 cups scalded milk
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 1 quart thin cream
- 2 tablespoons vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt
2. Add egg slightly beaten and milk gradually
3. Cook over hot water twenty minutes, stirring constantly at first
4. When cool, add cream and flavoring
5. Strain and freeze
