## Ingredients

- One cup of cream
- Five eggs, whites only
- Two full cups prepared flour
- One saltspoonful of nutmeg
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Stir the whites into the cream in turn with the flour
2. Put in nutmeg and salt
3. Beat all up hard for two minutes
4. Fry in plenty of hot, sweet lard, using a spoonful of batter for each fritter
5. Drain and serve upon a hot, clean napkin
6. Eat with jelly sauce
