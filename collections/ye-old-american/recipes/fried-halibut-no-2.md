## Ingredients

- salt pork
- two pounds halibut
- salt
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Fry a few thin slices of salt pork until brown in an iron frying pan
2. Take it up on a hot platter and keep it warm until the halibut is fried
3. Wash and dry two pounds of sliced halibut
4. Sprinkle it with salt and pepper
5. Dredge it well with flour
6. Put it into the hot pork drippings and fry brown on both sides
7. Serve the pork with the fish
