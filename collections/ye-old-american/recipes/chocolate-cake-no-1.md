## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- yolks of five eggs
- one cup of milk
- whites of two eggs
- three cups and a half of sifted flour
- two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- Mixture for Filling: whites of three eggs
- two cupfuls of sugar
- one-half cake of grated chocolate
- one teaspoonful of vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Stir one cup of butter and two cups of sugar to a cream
2. Add the yolks of five eggs after they have been well beaten
3. Stir into that one cup of milk
4. Beat the whites of two of the eggs to a stiff froth and add that also
5. Put in three cups and a half of sifted flour with two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder stirred into it
6. Bake in jelly-cake tins
7. For the filling, take the remaining three whites of the eggs beaten very stiff
8. Boil two cupfuls of sugar to almost candy or until it becomes stringy or almost brittle
9. Take it hot from the fire and pour it very slowly on the beaten whites of egg, beating quite fast
10. Add one-half cake of grated chocolate and a teaspoonful of vanilla extract
11. Stir it all until cool, then spread between each cake and over the top and sides
