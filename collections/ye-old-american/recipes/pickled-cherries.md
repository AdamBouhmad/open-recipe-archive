## Ingredients

- Morella or large red tart cherries
- To every quart: 1 large cup of vinegar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 dozen whole cloves
- 1/2 dozen blades of mace

## Directions

1. Put the vinegar and sugar on to heat with the spices.
2. Boil five minutes.
3. Turn out into a covered stoneware vessel, cover, and let it get perfectly cold.
4. Strain out the spices.
5. Fill small jars three-quarters of the way to the top with fruit.
6. Pour the cold vinegar over them.
7. Cork or cover tightly.
8. Leave the stems on the cherries.
