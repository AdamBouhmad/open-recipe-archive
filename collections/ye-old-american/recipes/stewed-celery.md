## Ingredients

- 1 bunch of celery
- 1 cup milk
- 1 great spoonful of butter
- flour
- pepper and salt
- clear water

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the stalks when you have cut off the roots.
2. Cut the celery into inch lengths.
3. Stew the celery in clear water until tender.
4. Turn off the water, add the milk, and as soon as this boils, add seasoning and butter.
5. Boil up once and serve very hot.
