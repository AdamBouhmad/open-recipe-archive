## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- ½ cup sugar
- ⅔ tablespoon melted butter
- 1 cup rolled oats
- ⅓ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat egg until light, add gradually sugar, and then stir in remaining ingredients.
2. Drop mixture by teaspoonfuls on a thoroughly greased inverted dripping-pan one inch apart.
3. Spread into circular shape with a case knife first dipped in cold water.
4. Bake in a moderate oven until delicately browned.
5. To give variety use two-thirds cup rolled oats and fill cup with shredded cocoanut.
