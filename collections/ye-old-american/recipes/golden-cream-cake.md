## Ingredients

- Yolks of eight eggs
- Two cupfuls of sugar
- A pinch of salt
- Three teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- Flour

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eight eggs to the lightest possible cream
2. Add two cupfuls of sugar and a pinch of salt
3. Sift three teaspoonfuls of baking powder well with flour
4. Bake in three jelly-cake pans
5. Make an icing of the whites of three eggs and one pound of sugar
6. Spread icing between the cakes and sprinkle grated cocoanut thickly over each layer
