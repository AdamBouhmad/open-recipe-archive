## Ingredients

- one quart sour milk
- one quart hot water
- melted butter
- heavy cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat one quart sour milk to 100° F., and turn into a strainer lined with cheese-cloth
2. Pour over one quart hot water, and as soon as water has drained through, pour over another quart
3. Repeat pouring hot water
4. Gather cheese-cloth around curd to form a bag and let hang until curd is free from whey
5. Moisten with melted butter and heavy cream, and add salt to taste
6. Shape into small balls
