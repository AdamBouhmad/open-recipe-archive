## Ingredients

- tomatas
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- mustard
- cloves
- pepper
- onions

## Directions

1. Take a peck of tomatas, the small round ones being best for pickling
2. Prick every one with a fork
3. Put them into a broad stone or earthen vessel
4. Sprinkle salt between every layer of tomatas
5. Cover them, and let them remain two days in the salt
6. Put them into vinegar and water mixed in equal quantities, half and half
7. Keep them in it twenty-four hours to draw out the saltness
8. Ensure sufficient liquid to cover the tomatas well
9. To a peck of tomatas allow a bottle of mustard, half an ounce of cloves, and half an ounce of pepper, with a dozen onions sliced thin
10. Pack the tomatas in a stone jar, placing the spices and onions alternately with the layers of tomatas
11. Put them in till the jar is two-thirds full
12. Fill it up with strong cold vinegar, and stop it closely
13. The pickles will be fit to eat in a fortnight
