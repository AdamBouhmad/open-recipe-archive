## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of cold boiled rice
- one pint of flour
- one teaspoonful sugar
- one-half teaspoonful salt
- one and one-half teaspoonfuls baking powder
- one egg
- a little more than half a pint of milk

## Directions

1. Sift together flour, sugar, salt and powder
2. Add rice free from lumps, diluted with beaten egg and milk
3. Mix into smooth batter
4. Have griddle well heated, make cakes large, bake nicely brown, and serve with maple syrup
