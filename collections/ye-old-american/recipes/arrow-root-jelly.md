## Ingredients

- three table-spoonfuls of arrow root powder
- water
- yellow peel of a lemon
- white sugar
- wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix three table-spoonfuls of arrow root powder in a tea-cup of water till quite smooth
2. Cover it and let it stand a quarter of an hour
3. Put the yellow peel of a lemon into a skillet with a pint of water and let it boil till reduced to one half
4. Take out the lemon-peel and pour in the dissolved arrow root while the water is still boiling
5. Add sufficient white sugar to sweeten it well and let it boil together for five or six minutes
6. Season if thought necessary with two tea-spoonfuls of wine and some grated nutmeg
