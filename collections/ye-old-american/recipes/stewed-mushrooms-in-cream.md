## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- three-fourths cup cream
- nutmeg
- dry toast
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare mushrooms as for Stewed Mushrooms.
2. Cook with three-fourths cup cream instead of using water or stock.
3. Add a slight grating of nutmeg, pour over small finger-shaped pieces of dry toast, and garnish with toast points and parsley.
