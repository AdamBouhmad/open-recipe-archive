## Ingredients

- two fine smoked tongues
- butter
- lean of veal
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Having boiled and skinned two fine smoked tongues, cut them to pieces and pound them to a paste in a mortar, moistening them with plenty of butter as you proceed.
2. Have ready an equal quantity of the lean of veal stewed and cut into very small pieces.
3. Pound the veal also in a mortar, adding butter to it by degrees.
4. The tongue and veal must be kept separate till both have been pounded.
5. Then fill your potting cans with lumps of the veal and tongue, pressed down hard, and so placed, that when cut, the mixture will look variegated or marbled.
6. Close the cans with veal; again press it down very hard, and finish by pouring on clarified butter.
7. Cover the cans closely, and keep them in a dry place.
8. It may be eaten at tea or supper.
9. Send it to table cut in slices.
10. You may use it for sandwiches.
