## Ingredients

- 1½ cups Brioche dough
- 1-third cup raisins seeded and cut in pieces
- 1-third cup currants
- 1-third cup citron thinly sliced
- Maraschino
- sauce made same as Rum Sauce, substituting Maraschino in place of rum

## Directions

1. To Brioche dough add raisins, currants, and citron previously soaked in Maraschino for one hour.
2. Shape, let rise, and bake same as Rum Cakes.
3. Dip in sauce made same as Rum Sauce, substituting Maraschino in place of rum.
