## Ingredients

- one pint of cream
- half a cupful of cold water
- half a package of gelatine
- the rind of two oranges
- the juice of six oranges
- one cupful of sugar
- the yolks of six eggs

## Directions

1. Whip a pint of cream so long that there will be but one-half the quantity left when skimmed off
2. Soak in half a cupful of cold water a half package of gelatine
3. Grate over it the rind of two oranges
4. Strain the juice of six oranges and add to it a cupful of sugar
5. Put the half pint of unwhipped cream into a double boiler
6. Pour into it the well-beaten yolks of six eggs, stirring until it begins to thicken
7. Add the gelatine
8. Remove from the fire
9. Let it stand for two minutes and add the orange juice and sugar
10. Beat all together until about the consistency of soft custard
11. Add the whipped cream
12. Mix well and turn into molds to harden
13. Serve with sweetened cream
