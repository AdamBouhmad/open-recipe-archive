## Ingredients

- soft biscuit or toasted bread
- tomatos
- sliced cucumbers
- pepper
- salt
- chopped onion
- mustard
- oil
- water

## Directions

1. Put some soft biscuit or toasted bread in the bottom of a sallad bowl
2. Put in a layer of sliced tomatos with the skin taken off
3. Add one of sliced cucumbers, sprinkled with pepper, salt, and chopped onion
4. Repeat until the bowl is full
5. Stew some tomatos quite soft
6. Strain the juice
7. Mix in some mustard, oil, and water
8. Pour over the contents of the bowl
9. Make it two hours before it is eaten
