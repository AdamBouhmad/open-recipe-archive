## Ingredients

- ground ham
- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the bread very thin, and butter well.
2. Put in a good layer of ham, and press the two sides of the sandwiches firmly, but gently, together.
3. Then roll lengthwise, and pile in a plate or basket.
