## Ingredients

- one pint of new milk
- two eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of sifted flour or half cup of cornstarch
- one cup of sugar
- vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Put two-thirds of the milk on the stove to boil
2. Stir the sugar, flour and eggs in what is left
3. When the milk boils, put into it the whole and cook it until it is as thick as custard
4. When cool, add vanilla extract
5. Spread between the layers of cake
