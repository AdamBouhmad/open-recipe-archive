## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- two cups grated green corn
- a little salt
- one egg
- one teaspoonful of baking powder
- flour sufficient
- one tablespoonful of sugar
- one pint of huckleberries

## Directions

1. Make the same as Green Corn Griddle-Cakes, leaving out one cup of milk
2. Add one tablespoonful of sugar and a pint of huckleberries rolled in flour
