## Ingredients

- 3 cups milk
- 1½ cups sugar
- Yolks 5 eggs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 pint thin cream
- ¼ cup pineapple syrup
- 1½ cups prepared French chestnuts
- ½ cup candied fruit cut in small pieces
- ¼ cup Sultana raisins
- 8 chestnuts broken in pieces soaked several hours in Maraschino syrup
- whipped cream, sweetened and flavored with Maraschino syrup

## Directions

1. Make custard of milk, sugar, eggs, and salt
2. Strain, cool, add cream, pineapple syrup, and chestnuts
3. Freeze
4. To prepare chestnuts, shell, cook in boiling water until soft, and force through a strainer
5. Line a two-quart melon mould with part of mixture
6. To remainder add one-half cup candied fruit cut in small pieces, one-quarter cup Sultana raisins, and eight chestnuts broken in pieces, first soaked several hours in Maraschino syrup
7. Fill mould, cover, pack in salt and ice
8. Let stand two hours
9. Serve with whipped cream, sweetened and flavored with Maraschino syrup
