## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon heavy cream
- 1 egg yolk
- 1/3 cup grated cheese
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Make and fry same as Cigarettes à la Prince Henry, using cheese mixture in place of Chicken Force-meat.
2. Melt two tablespoons butter, add four tablespoons flour, and pour on gradually one-half cup milk.
3. Add one tablespoon heavy cream, one egg yolk, and one-third cup grated cheese.
4. Season highly with salt and cayenne.
5. Cool before spreading on paste.
