## Ingredients

- gooseberries
- grated bread
- eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare gooseberries in the manner described
2. Add a little grated bread
3. Use both whites and yolks of the eggs
4. Stir the mixture well
5. Bake in a deep dish
6. Eat cold with sugar grated over it
