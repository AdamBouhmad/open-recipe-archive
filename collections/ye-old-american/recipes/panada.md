## Ingredients

- bread
- water
- butter
- sugar
- nutmeg
- crackers

## Directions

1. Having pared off the crust, boil some slices of bread in a quart of water for about five minutes
2. Take out the bread and beat it smooth in a deep dish, mixing in a little of the water it has boiled in
3. Mix it with a bit of fresh butter and sugar and nutmeg to your taste
