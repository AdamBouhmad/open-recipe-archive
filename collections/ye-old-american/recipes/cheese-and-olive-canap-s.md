## Ingredients

- stale bread
- butter
- soft cheese
- salt
- finely chopped olives
- red or green pepper

## Directions

1. Cut stale bread in one-fourth inch slices.
2. Shape with a small oblong cutter with rounded corners.
3. Cream butter.
4. Add an equal quantity of soft cheese.
5. Work until smooth.
6. Season with salt.
7. Spread on bread.
8. Garnish with a one-fourth inch border of finely chopped olives and a piece of red or green pepper cut in fancy shape, in centre of each.
9. To be served in place of sandwiches on a plate covered with a doily.
