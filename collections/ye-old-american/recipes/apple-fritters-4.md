## Ingredients

- One cup sweet milk
- Two cups flour
- A heaping teaspoonful of baking powder
- Two eggs beaten separately
- One tablespoonful of sugar
- A saltspoon of salt
- Thin slices of good sour apples

## Directions

1. Heat the milk a little more than milk-warm
2. Add it slowly to the beaten yolks and sugar
3. Add flour and whites of the eggs
4. Stir all together
5. Throw in thin slices of apples, dipping the batter up over them
6. Drop into boiling hot lard in large spoonfuls with pieces of apple in each
7. Fry to a light brown
8. Serve with maple syrup or a nice syrup made with clarified sugar
