## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 1⅓ cups sugar
- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- Rind one lemon
- 1 can grated pineapple
- ¼ cup brandy
- 2 tablespoons gin

## Directions

1. Cook sugar, water, and lemon rind fifteen minutes
2. Add lemon juice and pineapple
3. Cool and strain
4. Freeze to a mush
5. Add strong liquors and continue freezing
6. Serve in frappé glasses on a plate covered with a doily
