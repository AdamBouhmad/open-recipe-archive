## Ingredients

- selected oysters
- batter
- lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean, and dry between towels, selected oysters.
2. Dip in batter, fry in deep fat, drain, and serve on a folded napkin.
3. Garnish with lemon and parsley.
4. Oysters may be parboiled, drained, and then fried.
