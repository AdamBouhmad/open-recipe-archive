## Ingredients

- Squash—stewed and strained
- Milk
- Sugar
- Eggs—beaten very light
- Ginger
- Mace
- Cinnamon
- Crust

## Directions

1. Make precisely like pumpkin pie, except that, being less rich, it requires one more egg for each pie
