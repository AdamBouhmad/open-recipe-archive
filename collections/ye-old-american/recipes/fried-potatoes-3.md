## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- lard or drippings
- salt

## Directions

1. Pare, wash, and slice some raw potatoes as thin as wafers.
2. Lay in ice-water for half an hour.
3. Wipe dry in two cloths, spreading them upon one and pressing the other upon them.
4. Have ready in the frying-pan some boiling lard or nice dripping.
5. Fry the potatoes to a light brown, sprinkle with salt, and serve in a napkin laid in a deep dish and folded over them.
6. Take from the frying-pan as soon as they are brown with a perforated skimmer, put into a cullender and shake for an instant.
7. Alternatively, chop cold boiled potatoes into bits, season with pepper and salt, and fry lightly in dripping or butter, turning them constantly until nicely browned.
