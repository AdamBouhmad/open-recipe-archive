## Ingredients

- a quart of rich boiled custard
- a quart of ripe red raspberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a quart of rich boiled custard
2. When cold, pour it on a quart of ripe red raspberries
3. Mash them in it, pass it through a sieve
4. Sweeten, and freeze it
