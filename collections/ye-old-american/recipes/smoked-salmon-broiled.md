## Ingredients

- raw smoked salmon
- water
- skillet
- buttered gridiron
- butter
- pepper
- horse-radish
- fennel-seed or parsley

## Directions

1. Take a piece of raw smoked salmon the size of your hand, or larger in proportion to the number who are to sit down to supper.
2. Wash it in two waters, rubbing off the salt.
3. Lay in a skillet with enough warm—not hot—water to cover it; let it simmer fifteen minutes, and boil five.
4. Remove it, wipe dry, and lay on a buttered gridiron to broil.
5. When it is nicely browned on both sides, transfer to a hot dish; butter liberally, and pepper to taste.
6. Garnish with hillocks of grated horse-radish interspersed with sprays of fresh or pickled fennel-seed, or with parsley.
