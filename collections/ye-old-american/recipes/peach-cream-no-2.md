## Ingredients

- A quart of fine peaches
- The whites of three eggs
- A half cupful of powdered sugar
- The yolks of three eggs
- Half a cupful of granulated sugar
- A pint of milk

## Directions

1. Pare and stone the fruit and cut in quarters
2. Beat the whites of three eggs with a half cupful of powdered sugar until it is stiff enough to cut with a knife
3. Take the yolks and mix with half a cupful of granulated sugar and a pint of milk
4. Put the peaches into the mixture
5. Place in a pudding-dish and bake until almost firm
6. Put in the whites, mixing all thoroughly again
7. Bake a light brown
8. Eat ice cold
