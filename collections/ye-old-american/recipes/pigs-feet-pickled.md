## Ingredients

- twelve pigs' feet
- hot water
- salt
- spiced vinegar
- two eggs
- cup of milk
- teaspoonful of butter
- flour
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Scrape and wash the pigs' feet clean
2. Put them into a saucepan with enough hot (not boiling) water to cover them
3. When partly done, salt them
4. Boil for four to five hours until soft
5. Pack them in a stone crock and pour over them spiced vinegar made hot
6. They will be ready to use in a day or two
7. If you wish them for breakfast, split them and make a batter of two eggs, a cup of milk, salt, a teaspoonful of butter, with flour enough to make a thick batter
8. Dip each piece in this and fry in hot lard, or dip them in beaten egg and flour and fry
