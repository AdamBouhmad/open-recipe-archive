## Ingredients

- a little mace
- half a nutmeg (grated)
- a quart cream
- 8 yolks
- 3 whites
- one spoon flour
- one quarter of a pound almonds
- one spoon rose-water
- cold cream

## Directions

1. Boil gently a little mace and half a nutmeg (grated) in a quart cream
2. when cool, beat 8 yolks and 3 whites
3. strain and mix with one spoon flour
4. one quarter of a pound almonds
5. settled, add one spoon rose-water
6. and by degrees the cold cream and beat well together
7. wet a thick cloth and flour it
8. and pour in the pudding
9. boil hard half an hour
10. take out
11. pour over it melted butter and sugar
