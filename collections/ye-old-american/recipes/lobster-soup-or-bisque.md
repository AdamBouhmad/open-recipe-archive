## Ingredients

- Three pounds of veal
- Water
- One fine middle-sized lobster
- Mace
- Cayenne
- Salt and pepper
- Egg yolk
- Butter
- Flour
- Bread

## Directions

1. Make a good broth by boiling three pounds of veal slowly in as much water as will cover it, till the meat is reduced to shreds
2. Strain the broth well
3. Boil one fine middle-sized lobster and extract all the meat from the body and claws
4. Bruise part of the coral in a mortar, and also an equal quantity of the meat
5. Mix them well together
6. Add mace, cayenne, salt and pepper
7. Make them up into force meat balls, binding the mixture with the yolk of an egg slightly beaten
8. Take three quarts of the veal broth and put it into the meat of the lobster cut into mouthfuls
9. Boil it together about twenty minutes
10. Thicken it with the remaining coral (rubbed through a sieve)
11. Add the force meat balls and a little butter rolled in flour
12. Simmer it gently for ten minutes, but do not let it come to a boil
13. Serve with small dice of bread fried brown in butter
