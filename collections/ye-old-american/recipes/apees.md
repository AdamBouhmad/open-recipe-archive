## Ingredients

- a pound of fresh butter
- two pounds of sifted flour
- a pound of powdered white sugar
- a grated nutmeg
- a table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon
- four large table-spoonfuls of carraway seeds
- a wine glass of rose water
- cold water

## Directions

1. Rub a pound of fresh butter into two pounds of sifted flour.
2. Mix in a pound of powdered white sugar, a grated nutmeg, a table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon, and four large table-spoonfuls of carraway seeds.
3. Add a wine glass of rose water.
4. Mix the whole with sufficient cold water to make it a stiff dough.
5. Roll it out into a large sheet about a third of an inch in thickness.
6. Cut it into round cakes with a tin cutter or with the edge of a tumbler.
7. Lay them in buttered pans.
8. Bake them in a quick oven, rather hotter at the bottom than at the top, till they are of a very pale brown.
