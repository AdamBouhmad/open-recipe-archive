## Ingredients

- stale bread
- three-fourths cup coffee infusion
- two tablespoons sugar
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- one egg slightly beaten
- one-fourth cup cream
- crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut stale bread in one-half inch slices, remove crusts, and cut slices in one-half inch strips.
2. Mix three-fourths cup coffee infusion, two tablespoons sugar, one-fourth teaspoon salt, one egg slightly beaten, and one-fourth cup cream.
3. Dip bread in mixture, crumbs, egg, and crumbs again.
4. Fry in deep fat and drain.
5. Serve with Coffee Cream Sauce.
