## Ingredients

- one pound apple sifted
- one pound sugar
- 9 eggs
- one quarter of a pound butter
- one quart sweet cream
- one gill rose-water
- a cinnamon
- a green lemon peal grated (if sweet apples)
- add the juice of half a lemon
- currants, raisins and citron some add, but good without them

## Directions

1. put on to paste No. 7
