## Ingredients

- well-corned flank of beef, six pounds
- one teaspoonful sage
- one teaspoonful parsley
- one teaspoonful thyme
- one teaspoonful pepper
- one teaspoonful cloves
- water
- green pickles
- curled parsley

## Directions

1. Procure a well-corned flank of beef of about six pounds
2. Wash it and remove the inner and outer skin with the gristle
3. Prepare a seasoning of one teaspoonful each of sage, parsley, thyme, pepper and cloves
4. Lay the meat upon a board and spread this mixture over the inside
5. Roll the beef up tight
6. Fasten it with small skewers
7. Put a cloth over it and bandage the cloth with tape
8. Put the beef into the stewpot and cover it with water to the depth of an inch
9. Boil gently six hours
10. Take it out of the water and place it on a board without undoing it
11. Lay a board on top of the beef
12. Put a fifty pound weight upon this board and let it remain twenty-four hours
13. Take off the bandage
14. Garnish with green pickles and curled parsley and serve
