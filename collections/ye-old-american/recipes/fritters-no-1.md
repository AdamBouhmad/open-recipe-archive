## Ingredients

- 1 pint flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 pint boiling water

## Directions

1. Stir the flour into the water by degrees, and stir until it has boiled three minutes.
2. Let it get almost cold, when beat in the yolks, then the whites of the eggs, which must be previously whipped stiff.
