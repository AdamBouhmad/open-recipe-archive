## Ingredients

- twelve large potatoes
- three heaping tablespoonfuls of flour
- one teaspoonful of baking powder
- one-half teaspoonful salt
- one or two eggs
- two teacupfuls of boiling milk

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the potatoes and grate into a little cold water
2. Strain off water and pour on boiling milk
3. Stir in eggs, salt and flour mixed with the baking powder
4. If agreeable, flavor with a little fine chopped onion
5. Bake like any other pancakes, allowing a little more lard or butter
6. Serve with stewed or preserved fruit, especially with huckleberries
