## Ingredients

- paste
- apples, peaches, or berries, pared and sliced
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare a paste and roll into one sheet
2. Lay apples, peaches, or berries in the centre, paring and slicing the fruit
3. Sprinkle with sugar
4. Close the paste over them as you would a dumpling
5. Dip a stout cloth in hot water
6. Flour the inside
7. Put in the pudding and tie tightly
8. Boil two hours and a half
9. Eat hot with sauce
