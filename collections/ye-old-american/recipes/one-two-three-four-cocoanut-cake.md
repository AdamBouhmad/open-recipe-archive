## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 cups flour
- 4 eggs (the whites only)
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar
- ½ teaspoonful soda
- ½ small cocoanut

## Directions

1. Sift cream-tartar and soda into the flour. Stir cocoanut in at the last.
