## Ingredients

- whole cooked cauliflower
- buttered crumbs
- one cup Thin White Sauce

## Directions

1. Place a whole cooked cauliflower on a dish for serving, cover with buttered crumbs, and place on oven grate to brown crumbs; remove from oven and pour one cup Thin White Sauce around cauliflower.
