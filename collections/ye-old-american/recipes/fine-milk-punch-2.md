## Ingredients

- yellow rind of four large lemons
- one quart of brandy or rum
- juice of four lemons
- a pound and a half of loaf sugar
- two grated nutmegs
- a quart of water
- a quart of rich unskimmed milk

## Directions

1. Pare off the yellow rind of four large lemons and steep it for twenty-four hours in a quart of brandy or rum
2. Mix with it the juice of the lemons, a pound and a half of loaf sugar, two grated nutmegs and a quart of water
3. Add a quart of rich unskimmed milk, made boiling hot, and strain the whole through a jelly-bag
4. Use as soon as it is cold, or make a larger quantity and bottle it
