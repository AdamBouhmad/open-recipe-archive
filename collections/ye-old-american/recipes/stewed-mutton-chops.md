## Ingredients

- loin or neck of mutton
- pepper
- salt
- water
- carrots
- turnips
- onions
- potatoes
- sweet herbs
- tomatas
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut a loin or neck of mutton into chops, and trim away the fat and bones.
2. Beat and flatten them.
3. Season them with pepper and salt, and put them into a stew-pan with barely sufficient water to cover them, and some sliced carrots, turnips, onions, potatoes, and a bunch of sweet herbs, or a few tomatas.
4. Let the whole stew slowly about three hours, or till everything is tender.
5. Keep the pan closely covered, except when you are skimming it.
6. Send it to table with sippets or three-cornered pieces of toasted bread, laid all round the dish.
