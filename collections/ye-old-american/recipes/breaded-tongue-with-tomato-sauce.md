## Ingredients

- cold boiled corned tongue
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- crumbs
- butter
- Tomato Sauce I

## Directions

1. Cut cold boiled corned tongue in slices one-third inch thick.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in egg and crumbs, and sauté in butter.
3. Serve with Tomato Sauce I.
