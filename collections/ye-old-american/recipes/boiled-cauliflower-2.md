## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- bobbinet lace or tarlatan
- drawn butter
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Pick off the leaves and cut the stalk close to the bottom of the bunch of flowers
2. Lay in cold water for half an hour
3. Unless very large, do not cut it; if you do, quarter neatly
4. Tie a close net of coarse bobbinet lace or tarlatan about it to prevent breaking or bruising
5. Put into boiling water salted and cook until tender
6. Undo and remove the net and lay the cauliflower in a hot dish
7. Have ready a large cupful of nice drawn butter and pour over it
8. A little lemon-juice makes of this a sauce tartare
9. Cut with a silver knife and fork in helping it out, and give a little of the sauce to each person
