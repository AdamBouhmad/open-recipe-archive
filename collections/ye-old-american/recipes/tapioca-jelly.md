## Ingredients

- 1 cup of tapioca
- 3 cups of cold water
- Juice of a lemon
- a pinch of the grated peel
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Soak the tapioca in the water four hours
2. Set within a saucepan of boiling water
3. Pour more lukewarm water over the tapioca if it has absorbed too much of the liquid
4. Heat, stirring frequently
5. If too thick after it begins to clear, put in a very little boiling water
6. When quite clear, put in the sugar and lemon
7. Pour into moulds
8. Eat cold with cream flavored with rose-water and sweetened
