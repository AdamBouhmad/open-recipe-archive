## Ingredients

- whites of six eggs
- one cupful of sweet milk
- two cupfuls of sugar
- four cupfuls of sifted flour
- two-thirds of a cup of butter
- flavoring
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Stir the sugar and butter to a cream.
2. Add the milk and flavoring and part of the flour.
3. Add the beaten whites of eggs, then the rest of the flour.
4. Bake carefully in tins lined with buttered white paper.
