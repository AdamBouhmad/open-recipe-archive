## Ingredients

- the seventh and eighth ribs
- fine clear coals
- lard
- mushroom catsup
- scraped horse-radish

## Directions

1. Cut the steaks half an inch thick
2. Beat them a little
3. Have fine clear coals
4. Rub the bars of the gridiron with a cloth dipped in lard before you put it over the coals
5. That none may drip to cause a bad smell
6. Put no salt on till you dish them
7. Broil them quick, turning them frequently
8. The dish must be very hot
9. Some slices of onion in it
10. Lay in the steaks
11. Sprinkle a little salt
12. And pour over them a spoonful of water and one of mushroom catsup, both made boiling hot
13. Garnish with scraped horse-radish
14. And put on a hot dish cover
15. Every thing must be in readiness
16. For the great excellence of a beef steak lies in having it immediately from the gridiron
