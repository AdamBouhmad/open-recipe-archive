## Ingredients

- raw chestnuts
- a little butter
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Peel the raw chestnuts and scald them to remove the inner skin
2. Put them in a frying pan with a little butter and toss them about a few moments
3. Add a sprinkle of salt and a suspicion of cayenne
4. Serve them after the cheese
