## Ingredients

- half a cupful of melted butter
- half a cupful of vinegar
- two tablespoonfuls of tomato catsup
- salt
- one tablespoonful of made mustard

## Directions

1. Mix half a cupful of melted butter, half a cupful of vinegar, two tablespoonfuls of tomato catsup, salt, and one tablespoonful of made mustard
2. Boil ten minutes
