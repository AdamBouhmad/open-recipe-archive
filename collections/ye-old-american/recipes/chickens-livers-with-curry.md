## Ingredients

- chickens' livers
- seasoned crumbs
- egg
- butter
- two tablespoons butter
- one-half tablespoon finely chopped onion
- two tablespoons flour
- one-half teaspoon curry powder
- one cup stock
- Rice Timbales

## Directions

1. Clean and separate livers.
2. Dip in seasoned crumbs, egg, and crumbs and sauté in butter.
3. Remove livers and to fat in pan add two tablespoons butter and one-half tablespoon finely chopped onion.
4. Cook five minutes.
5. Add two tablespoons flour mixed with one-half teaspoon curry powder and one cup stock.
6. Strain sauce over livers and serve around livers Rice Timbales.
