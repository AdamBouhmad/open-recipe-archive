## Ingredients

- six eggs
- a pound of loaf sugar powdered
- a light pound of flour
- half a grated nutmeg
- a glass of brandy
- a pint of cream
- powdered sugar for sifting

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs very light
2. Sift into them a pound of loaf sugar powdered, and a light pound of flour, with half a grated nutmeg, and a glass of brandy
3. Beat all together very well
4. Add a pint of cream
5. Pour it in a deep dish, and bake it
6. When done, sift some powdered sugar over it
