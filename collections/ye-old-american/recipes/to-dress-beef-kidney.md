## Ingredients

- fresh kidney
- flour
- butter
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- boiling water
- champagne or other wine
- mushroom catchup or walnut pickle

## Directions

1. Soak a fresh kidney in cold water and dry it in a cloth.
2. Cut it into mouthfuls, and then mince it fine.
3. Dust it with flour.
4. Put some butter into a stew-pan over a moderate fire, and when it boils put in the minced kidney.
5. When you have browned it in the butter, sprinkle on a little salt and cayenne pepper, and pour in a very little boiling water.
6. Add a glass of champagne or other wine, or a large tea-spoonful of mushroom catchup, or of walnut pickle.
7. Cover the pan closely, and let it stew till the kidney is tender.
8. Send it to table hot in a covered dish.
