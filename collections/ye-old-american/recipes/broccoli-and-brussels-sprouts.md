## Ingredients

- broccoli
- brussels sprouts
- salt
- drawn butter

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash carefully
2. Cut off the lower part of the stems and lay in cold water, slightly salted, for half an hour
3. Cook quickly in boiling water, with a little salt, until tender (twelve or fifteen minutes)
4. Cook in an uncovered saucepan
5. Drain well and lay in a neat pile lightly heaped in the centre of a dish
6. Pour drawn butter over them, or serve this in a tureen
