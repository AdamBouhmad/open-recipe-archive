## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1¾ cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1½ teaspoons ginger
- ⅔ cup thin cream

## Directions

1. Beat eggs until light, and add sugar gradually.
2. Mix and sift dry ingredients, and add alternately with cream to first mixture.
3. Turn into a buttered cake pan, and bake thirty minutes in a moderate oven.
