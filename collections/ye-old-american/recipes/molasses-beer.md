## Ingredients

- 5 quarts hops
- 5 quarts wheat bran
- 15 gallons water
- 5 quarts molasses
- 1 quart good yeast

## Directions

1. Put five quarts of hops, and five of wheat bran, into fifteen gallons of water
2. Boil it three or four hours
3. Strain it, and pour it into a cask with one head taken out
4. Put in five quarts of molasses
5. Stir it till well mixed
6. Throw a cloth over the barrel
7. When moderately warm, add a quart of good yeast, which must be stirred in
8. Stop it close with a cloth and board
9. When it has fermented and become quite clear, bottle it
