## Ingredients

- pears
- brown sugar
- water
- sweet cream or boiled custard

## Directions

1. Pare and core the pears without dividing
2. Place them in a pan and fill up the orifice with brown sugar
3. Add a little water and let them bake until perfectly tender
4. Serve nice with sweet cream or boiled custard
