## Ingredients

- a small turkey
- pepper and salt
- slices of boiled ham or tongue
- well seasoned forcemeat
- jelly

## Directions

1. Bone a small turkey
2. put pepper and salt on the inside
3. cover it with slices of boiled ham or tongue
4. fill it with well seasoned forcemeat
5. sew it up and boil it
6. cover it with jelly
