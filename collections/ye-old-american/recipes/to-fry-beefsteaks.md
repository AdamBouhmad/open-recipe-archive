## Ingredients

- beefsteak cut from the ribs or sirloin
- butter or nice beef dripping
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- cream or sweet rich milk

## Directions

1. Cut beefsteak much thinner than for broiling and remove the bone
2. Put some butter or nice beef dripping into a frying pan and set it over the fire
3. When it has boiled and become hot lay in the steaks
4. When cooked quite enough, season with salt and pepper, turn and brown on both sides
5. When done take out the steaks and lay them on a hot dish and cover the top to keep them hot
6. For gravy, add a few drops of boiling water to the pan or make gravy by putting a large tablespoonful of flour into the hot gravy left in the pan after taking up the steaks
7. Stir it smooth, then pour in a pint of cream or sweet rich milk, salt and pepper
8. Let it boil up once until it thickens
9. Pour hot into a gravy dish and send to the table with the steaks
