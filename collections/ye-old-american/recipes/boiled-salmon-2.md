## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Lemon
- Parsley
- Egg Sauce I or II, or Hollandaise Sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and boil as directed in Ways of Cooking Fish.
2. Place on a hot platter.
3. Remove skin.
4. Garnish with slices of lemon and parsley.
5. Serve with Egg Sauce I or II, or Hollandaise Sauce.
