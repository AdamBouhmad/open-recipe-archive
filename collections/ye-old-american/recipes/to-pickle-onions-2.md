## Ingredients

- small onions
- salt
- water
- cloves
- mace
- ginger
- nutmeg
- cider vinegar
- salad oil

## Directions

1. Take very small onions
2. With a sharp knife cut off the stems as close as possible, and peel off the outer skin
3. Put them into salt and water, and let them stand in the brine for six days
4. Stir them daily, and change the salt and water every two days
5. Ensure they are closely covered
6. Put the onions into jars, and give them a scald in boiling salt and water
7. Let them stand till they are cold
8. Drain them on a sieve, wipe them
9. Stick a clove in the top of each
10. Put them into wide-mouthed bottles
11. Disperse among them some blades of mace and slices of ginger or nutmeg
12. Fill up the bottles with the best cider vinegar
13. Put at the top a large spoonful of salad oil
14. Cork the bottles well
