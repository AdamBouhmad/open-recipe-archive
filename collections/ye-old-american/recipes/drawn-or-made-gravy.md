## Ingredients

- coarse pieces of the lean of beef or veal
- giblets and trimmings of poultry or game
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Beat the meat with a mallet or meat-beetle, score it, and cut it into small pieces
2. Season it with pepper and salt
3. Put it into a stew-pan with butter only
4. Heat it gradually until it becomes brown
5. Shake the pan frequently and see that it does not burn or stick to the bottom
6. It will generally be browned sufficiently in half an hour
7. Put in some boiling water, allowing one pint to each pound of meat
8. Simmer it on coals by the side of the fire for near three hours, skimming it well and keeping it closely covered
9. When done, remove it from the heat, let it stand awhile to settle, and then strain it
10. Do not thicken this gravy till you go to use it
