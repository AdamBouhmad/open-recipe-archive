## Ingredients

- rump steak
- Hollandaise Sauce I
- onion juice
- one-half tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Broil rump steak.
2. Spread with Hollandaise Sauce I to which is added a few drops onion juice and one-half tablespoon finely chopped parsley.
