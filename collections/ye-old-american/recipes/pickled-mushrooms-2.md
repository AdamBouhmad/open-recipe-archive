## Ingredients

- vinegar to cover the mushrooms
- young button mushrooms
- 2 blades pounded mace per quart of mushrooms
- 1 ounce ground pepper per quart of mushrooms
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Choose some nice young button mushrooms for pickling
2. Rub off the skin with a piece of flannel and salt
3. Cut off the stalks
4. If very large, take out the red inside and reject the black ones
5. Put them in a stewpan and sprinkle salt over them
6. Add pounded mace and pepper in the above proportion
7. Shake them well over a clear fire until the liquor flows
8. Keep them there until it is all dried up again
9. Add as much vinegar as will cover them
