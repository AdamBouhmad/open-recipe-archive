## Ingredients

- 1¼ cups steamed and strained squash
- ¼ cup sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, or ½ teaspoon lemon extract
- 1 egg
- ⅞ cup milk

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, salt, and spice or extract, add squash, egg slightly beaten, and milk gradually.
2. Bake in one crust, following directions for Custard Pie.
3. If a richer pie is desired, use one cup squash, one-half cup each of milk and cream, and an additional egg yolk.
