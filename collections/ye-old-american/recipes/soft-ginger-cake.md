## Ingredients

- one cupful of butter
- half a cupful of brown sugar
- two cupfuls of cooking molasses
- a cupful of sweet milk
- a tablespoonful of ginger
- a teaspoonful of ground cinnamon
- three eggs
- two cups of sifted flour
- a teaspoonful of soda
- a spoonful of water
- two more cupfuls of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Stir to a cream one cupful of butter and half a cupful of brown sugar.
2. Add to this two cupfuls of cooking molasses, a cupful of sweet milk, a tablespoonful of ginger, a teaspoonful of ground cinnamon.
3. Beat all thoroughly together.
4. Add three eggs, the whites and yolks beaten separately.
5. Beat into this two cups of sifted flour.
6. Add a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a spoonful of water and last, two more cupfuls of sifted flour.
7. Butter and paper two common square bread-pans.
8. Divide the mixture and pour half into each.
9. Bake in a moderate oven from forty to sixty minutes. If sour milk is used the cakes are much lighter, but either sweet or sour is most excellent.
