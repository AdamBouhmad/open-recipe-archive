## Ingredients

- stale bread
- water
- large onion
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley or sage
- two eggs
- white tape
- hot water
- two hard-boiled eggs
- salad oil
- English mustard
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Thoroughly clean the fish; cut off the head or not, as preferred
2. Cut out the backbone from the head to within two inches of the tail
3. Soak stale bread in water, squeeze dry
4. Cut in pieces a large onion, fry in butter, chop fine
5. Add the bread, two ounces of butter, salt, pepper and a little parsley or sage to the onion
6. Heat through, and when taken off the fire, add the yolks of two well-beaten eggs
7. Stuff the fish rather full, sew up with fine twine, and wrap with several coils of white tape
8. Rub the fish over slightly with butter
9. Just cover the bottom of a baking pan with hot water
10. Place the fish in it, standing back upward, and bent in the form of an S
11. Reduce the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs to a smooth paste with two tablespoonfuls good salad oil
12. Stir in half a teaspoon English mustard, and add pepper and vinegar to taste
13. Serve with this dressing
