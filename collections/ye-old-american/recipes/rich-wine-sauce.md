## Ingredients

- one cupful of butter
- two of powdered sugar
- half a cupful of wine
- a teaspoonful of grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Add the sugar gradually and when very light add the wine, which has been made hot, a little at a time
3. Add a teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
4. Place the bowl in a basin of hot water and stir for two minutes
