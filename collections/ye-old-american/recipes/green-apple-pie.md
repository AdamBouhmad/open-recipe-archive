## Ingredients

- tart apples enough for a pie
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar
- a teaspoonful of cinnamon
- a small level tablespoonful of sifted flour
- two tablespoonfuls of water
- a few bits of butter
- pie paste

## Directions

1. Peel, core and slice tart apples
2. sprinkle over about three tablespoonfuls of sugar, a teaspoonful of cinnamon, a small level tablespoonful of sifted flour, two tablespoonfuls of water, a few bits of butter
3. stir all together with a spoon
4. put it into a pie-tin lined with pie paste
5. cover with a top crust
6. bake about forty minutes
