## Ingredients

- twelve large onions
- three quarts of milk and water equally mixed
- a bit of veal or fowl
- a piece of bacon
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Chop up twelve large onions
2. boil them in three quarts of milk and water equally mixed
3. put in a bit of veal or fowl
4. and a piece of bacon with pepper and salt
5. When the onions are boiled to pulp
6. thicken it with a large spoonful of butter mixed with one of flour
7. Take out the meat
8. and serve it up with toasted bread cut in small pieces in the soup
