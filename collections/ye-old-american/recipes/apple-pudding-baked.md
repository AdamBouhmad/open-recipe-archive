## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1/2 cupful sugar
- yolks of 4 eggs
- juice and grated rind of 1 lemon
- 1/2 dozen sound green tart apples, grated
- whites of 4 eggs
- cinnamon or nutmeg

## Directions

1. Stir butter and sugar to a cream
2. Stir in well beaten egg yolks, lemon juice and grated rind, and grated apples
3. Stir in beaten egg whites and season with cinnamon or nutmeg
4. Bake and serve cold with cream
