## Ingredients

- ½ lb. powdered sugar
- ¼ lb. flour
- 4 eggs, yolks and whites separate, and beaten very stiff
- 1 lemon, all the juice, and half the grated rind

## Directions

1. Drop upon buttered paper, not too near together.
2. Try one, and if it runs, beat the mixture some minutes longer hard, adding a very little flour.
3. Your oven should be very quick, and the cakes a delicate yellow.
