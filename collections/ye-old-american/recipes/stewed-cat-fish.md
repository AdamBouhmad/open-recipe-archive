## Ingredients

- cat-fish
- salt
- shallot
- button-onion
- parsley
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Skin, clean, and cut off the heads.
2. Sprinkle with salt, to remove any muddy taste they may have contracted from the flats or holes in which they have fed, and let them lie in a cool place for an hour or so.
3. Then put them into a saucepan, cover with cold water, and stew very gently for from half to three-quarters of an hour, according to their size.
4. Add a chopped shallot or button-onion, a bunch of chopped parsley, a little pepper, a large tablespoonful of butter, a tablespoonful of flour mixed to a paste with cold water; boil up once.
5. Take out the fish carefully, and lay in a deep dish.
6. Boil up the gravy once more, and pour over the fish.
7. Send to table in a covered dish.
