## Ingredients

- Large sweet oranges
- Powdered sugar
- Wine

## Directions

1. Pare and slice large sweet oranges
2. Sprinkle powdered sugar thickly over each slice
3. Pour a couple of glasses of wine on the top
4. Sprinkle powdered sugar over all, and serve at once
