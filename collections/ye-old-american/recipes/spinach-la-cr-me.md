## Ingredients

- Boiled spinach
- Three tablespoonfuls melted butter
- Pepper
- Salt
- Three tablespoonfuls of cream
- Teaspoonful white sugar
- Boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Boil and chop very fine, or rub through a cullender.
2. Season with pepper and salt.
3. Beat in, while warm, three tablespoonfuls melted butter.
4. Put into a saucepan and heat, stirring constantly.
5. When smoking hot, add three tablespoonfuls of cream and a teaspoonful white sugar.
6. Boil up once, still stirring.
7. Press firmly into a hot bowl or other mould.
8. Turn into a hot dish and garnish with boiled eggs.
