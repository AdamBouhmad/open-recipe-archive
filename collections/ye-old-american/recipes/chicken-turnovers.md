## Ingredients

- cold roast chicken
- water
- gravy
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- sifted flour, small tablespoonful
- plain pie-crust
- hot drippings or fat

## Directions

1. Chop cold roast chicken very fine
2. Put it into a saucepan, place it over the fire, moisten it with a little water and gravy, or a piece of butter
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Add a small tablespoonful of sifted flour dissolved in a little water
5. Heat all through and remove from the fire to become cool
6. When cooled roll out some plain pie-crust quite thin
7. Cut out in rounds as large as a saucer
8. Wet the edge with cold water and put a large spoonful of the minced meat on one-half of the round
9. Fold the other half over and pinch the edges well together
10. Fry them in hot drippings or fat a nice brown, or cook in a moderate oven
