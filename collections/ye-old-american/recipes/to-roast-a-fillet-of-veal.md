## Ingredients

- fillet of veal
- bone
- fat flap
- fresh butter or suet
- grated bread-crumbs
- grated lemon-peel
- sweet marjoram
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- beaten yolk of egg
- fat ham or bacon
- water
- flour

## Directions

1. Take out the bone and secure the fat flap to the outside of the meat with skewers
2. Prepare a stuffing of fresh butter or suet minced fine and an equal quantity of grated bread-crumbs, a large table-spoonful of grated lemon-peel, a table-spoonful of sweet marjoram chopped or rubbed to powder, a nutmeg grated, and a little pepper and salt with a sprig of chopped parsley
3. Mix all these ingredients with beaten yolk of egg and stuff the place from whence the bone was taken
4. Make deep cuts or incisions all over the top of the veal and fill them with some of the stuffing
5. Stick into each hole an inch of fat ham or bacon cut very thin
6. Paper the fat and spit the veal and put it into the roaster, keeping it at first not too near the fire
7. Put a little salt and water into the dripping-pan and for awhile baste the meat with it
8. Baste with its own gravy
9. Place it nearer to the fire as it proceeds
10. A fillet of veal will require four hours roasting
11. Half an hour before it is done, remove the paper and baste the meat with butter, having first dredged it very lightly with flour
12. Skim the gravy and mix some thin melted butter with it
