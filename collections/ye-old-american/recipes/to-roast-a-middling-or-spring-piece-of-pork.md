## Ingredients

- grated bread
- minced onion
- sage
- pepper
- salt
- beaten yolk of egg
- pork
- powdered sage
- buttered twine
- butter
- flour
- glass of wine
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Make a force-meat of grated bread, and minced onion and sage, pepper, salt, and beaten yolk of egg; mix it well, and spread it all over the inside of the pork.
2. Then roll up the meat, and with a sharp knife score it round in circles, rubbing powdered sage into the cuts.
3. Tie a buttered twine round the roll of meat so as to keep it together in every direction.
4. Put a hook through one end, and roast the pork before a clear brisk fire, moistening the skin occasionally with butter.
5. Or you may bake it in a Dutch oven.
6. Thicken the gravy with a little flour, and flavour it with a glass of wine.
7. Have currant jelly to eat with it.
