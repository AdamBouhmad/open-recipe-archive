## Ingredients

- beef heart
- water
- onion
- parsley
- celery
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- browned flour

## Directions

1. After washing the heart thoroughly cut it up into squares half an inch long.
2. Put them into a saucepan with water enough to cover them.
3. If any scum rises skim it off.
4. Take out the meat, strain the liquor and put back the meat.
5. Add a sliced onion, some parsley, a head of celery chopped fine, pepper and salt and a piece of butter.
6. Stew until the meat is very tender.
7. Stir up a tablespoonful of browned flour with a small quantity of water and thicken the whole.
8. Boil up and serve.
