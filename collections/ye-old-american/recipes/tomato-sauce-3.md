## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- few grains pepper
- 1 small can condensed tomato
- ⅔ lb. lean beef
- 1 pint water

## Directions

1. Cook first four ingredients eight minutes.
2. Add tomato, 1 pint of water, and beef cut in small pieces, and cook one and one-half hours.
3. Remove meat before serving.
