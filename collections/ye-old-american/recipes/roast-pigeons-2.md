## Ingredients

- pigeons
- giblets
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare and roast or broil the same as chickens
2. They will require from twenty to thirty minutes' cooking
3. Make a gravy of the giblets or not, season it with pepper and salt, and add a little flour and butter
