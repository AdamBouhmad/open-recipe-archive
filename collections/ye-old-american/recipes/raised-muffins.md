## Ingredients

- 1 cup scalded milk
- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ¼ cup sugar
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ¼ yeast cake
- 1 egg
- 4 cups flour

## Directions

1. Add butter, sugar, and salt to milk and water; when lukewarm, add yeast cake, and when dissolved, egg well beaten, and flour
2. Beat thoroughly, cover, and let rise over night
3. In morning, fill buttered muffin rings two-thirds full; let rise until rings are full, and bake thirty minutes in hot oven
