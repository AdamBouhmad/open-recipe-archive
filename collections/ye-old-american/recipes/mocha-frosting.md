## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- 1½ cups confectioners' sugar
- 1 tablespoon breakfast cocoa
- Coffee infusion

## Directions

1. Cream butter, and add sugar gradually, continuing the beating; then add cocoa and coffee infusion, drop by drop, until of right consistency to spread or force through a pastry bag and tube.
