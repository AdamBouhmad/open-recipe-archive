## Ingredients

- fat from pan
- four tablespoons flour
- one and one-half cups boiling water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove some of the fat from pan, leaving four tablespoons
2. Place on front of range
3. Add four tablespoons flour
4. Stir until well browned
5. Add gradually one and one-half cups boiling water
6. Cook five minutes
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Strain
