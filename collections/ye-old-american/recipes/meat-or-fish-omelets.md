## Ingredients

- cold meat, fish, game or poultry
- spices
- salt
- butter
- beaten eggs
- milk, gravy, water, or white wine
- crisp herbs
- pickles
- sliced lemon

## Directions

1. Take cold meat, fish, game or poultry of any kind
2. Remove all skin, sinew, etc.
3. Either cut it small or pound it to a paste in a mortar, together with a proper proportion of spices and salt
4. Either toss it in a buttered frying pan over a clear fire till it begins to brown and pour beaten eggs upon it, or beat it up with the eggs, or spread it upon them after they have begun to set in the pan
5. Serve hot, with or without a sauce
6. Garnish with crisp herbs in branches, pickles, or sliced lemon
