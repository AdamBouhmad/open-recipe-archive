## Ingredients

- lima beans or butter beans
- water
- salt
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Shell into cold water; let them lie a while; put into a pot with plenty of boiling water and a little salt, and cook fast until tender.
2. Large ones sometimes require nearly an hour's boiling. The average time is forty minutes.
3. Drain and butter well when dished, peppering to taste.
