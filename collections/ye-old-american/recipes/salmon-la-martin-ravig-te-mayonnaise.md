## Ingredients

- one can salmon
- lettuce
- mayonnaise
- canned pimentoes
- truffles
- cooked spinach
- capers
- shallot
- anchovies
- parsley
- watercress
- Mayonnaise Dressing I

## Directions

1. Drain one can salmon, rinse, dry, and separate in flakes.
2. Moisten with Ravigôte Mayonnaise, arrange on a bed of lettuce, mask with mayonnaise, and garnish with canned pimentoes cut in triangles, and truffles cut in fancy shapes.
3. For Ravigôte Mayonnaise: Mix two tablespoons cooked spinach, one tablespoon capers, one-half shallot finely chopped, three anchovies, one-third cup parsley, and one-half cup watercress.
4. Pound in mortar until thoroughly macerated, then force through a very fine strainer.
5. Add to one-half the recipe for Mayonnaise Dressing I.
