## Ingredients

- 1 cup ground coffee
- 1 egg
- 1/3 cup cold water
- 1 pint boiling water

## Directions

1. Mix one cup ground coffee with one egg, slightly beaten, and crushed shell.
2. To one-third of this amount add one-third cup cold water.
3. Turn into a scalded coffee-pot, add one pint boiling water, and boil three minutes.
4. Let stand on back of range ten minutes.
5. Serve.
6. Keep remaining coffee and egg closely covered, in a cool place, to use two successive mornings.
