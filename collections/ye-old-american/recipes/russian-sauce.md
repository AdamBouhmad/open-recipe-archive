## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup White Stock III
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains pepper
- ½ teaspoon finely chopped chives
- ½ teaspoon made mustard
- 1 teaspoon grated horseradish
- ¼ cup cream
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add flour, and pour on gradually White Stock; then add salt, pepper, mustard, chives, and horseradish.
2. Cook two minutes, strain, add cream and lemon juice.
3. Reheat before serving.
4. Serve with Beef Tenderloins or Hamburg Steaks.
