## Ingredients

- almonds
- cream

## Directions

1. Pour hot water on the almonds, and let them stand till the skins will slip off
2. Pound them fine, and mix them with cream
3. A pound of almonds in the shells, will be sufficient for a quart of cream
4. Sweeten and freeze it
