## Ingredients

- chickens
- flour
- egg sauce or bread sauce

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and stuff as for roasting
2. Baste a floured cloth around each
3. Put into a pot with enough boiling water to cover them well
4. Stew very slowly, for the first half hour especially
5. Boil an hour or more, guiding yourself by size and toughness
6. Serve with egg or bread sauce
