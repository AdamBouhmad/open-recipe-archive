## Ingredients

- two ounces of essential oil of lavender
- two drachms of essence of ambergris
- a pint of spirits of wine

## Directions

1. Mix two ounces of essential oil of lavender, and two drachms of essence of ambergris, with a pint of spirits of wine
2. Cork the bottle, and shake it hard every day for a fortnight
