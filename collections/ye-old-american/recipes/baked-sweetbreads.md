## Ingredients

- three sweetbreads
- egg
- bread crumbs
- oiled butter
- three slices of toast
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Choose large, white sweetbreads, put them into warm water to draw out the blood, and to improve their color.
2. Let them remain for rather more than one hour.
3. Put them into boiling water, and allow them to simmer for about ten minutes which renders them firm.
4. Take them up and drain them.
5. Brush over the egg and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
6. Dip them in egg again, and then into more bread crumbs.
7. Drop on them a little oiled butter, and put the sweetbreads into a moderately heated oven.
8. Let them bake for nearly three-quarters of an hour.
9. Make three pieces of toast.
10. Place the sweetbreads on the toast, and pour round, but not over, them a good brown gravy.
