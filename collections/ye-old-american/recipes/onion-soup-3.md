## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- six large onions
- yolks of four eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of butter
- a large one of flour
- one cup full of cream
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put the butter in a frying pan.
2. Cut the onions into thin slices and drop in the butter.
3. Stir until they begin to cook; then cover tight and set back where they will simmer, but not burn, for half an hour.
4. Put the milk on to boil, and then add the dry flour to the onions and stir constantly for three minutes over the fire.
5. Turn the mixture into the milk and cook fifteen minutes.
6. Rub the soup through a strainer, return to the fire, season with salt and pepper.
7. Beat the yolks of the eggs well, add the cream to them and stir into the soup.
8. Cook three minutes, stirring constantly.
9. Pour over fried croutons in a soup tureen.
