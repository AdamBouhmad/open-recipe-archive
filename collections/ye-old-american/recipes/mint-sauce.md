## Ingredients

- young green mint
- vinegar
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Take a large bunch of young green mint
2. Wash it very clean
3. Pick all the leaves from the stalks
4. Chop the leaves very fine
5. Mix them with cold vinegar and a large proportion of powdered sugar
6. There must be merely sufficient vinegar to moisten the mint well, but by no means enough to make the sauce liquid
7. It should be very sweet
