## Ingredients

- chickens' livers
- salt and pepper
- flour
- butter
- two tablespoons butter
- two and one-half tablespoons flour
- one cup Brown Stock
- two tablespoons Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Clean and separate livers and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
2. Dredge with flour and sauté in butter.
3. Brown two tablespoons butter and add two and one-half tablespoons flour.
4. When well browned add gradually one cup Brown Stock.
5. Add two tablespoons Madeira wine and reheat livers in sauce.
