## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 2 cups of fine bread crumbs—always stale and dry
- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls melted butter
- Nutmeg to taste
- ¼ teaspoonful soda dissolved in hot water

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks very light, and having soaked the bread-crumbs well in the milk, stir these together
2. Add the butter and seasoning, with the soda
3. Add lastly the whites
4. Bake to a fine brown, and eat hot with pudding-sauce
