## Ingredients

- head of cabbage
- boiling water
- rich forcemeat
- melted butter

## Directions

1. pour boiling water on, and cover it till you can turn the leaves back
2. turn the leaves back carefully
3. take some of those in the middle of the head off
4. chop them fine
5. mix them with rich forcemeat
6. put this in
7. replace the leaves to confine the stuffing
8. tie it in a cloth
9. boil it
10. serve it up whole, with a little melted butter in the dish
