## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains pepper
- 1 cup Brown Stock
- ⅓ cup currant jelly
- 1½ tablespoons Sherry wine
- 6 slices cold cooked mutton

## Directions

1. Brown the butter, add flour, seasonings, and stock, gradually.
2. Then add jelly, and when melted, add mutton.
3. When meat is heated, add wine.
4. If mutton gravy is at hand, use instead of making a Brown Sauce.
