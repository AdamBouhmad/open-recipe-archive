## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- ⅔ cup thin cream
- 1⅔ cups flour
- 2½ teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon mace
- ¼ teaspoon ginger

## Directions

1. Put unbeaten eggs in a bowl, add sugar and cream, and beat vigorously.
2. Mix and sift remaining ingredients, then add to first mixture.
3. Bake thirty minutes in a shallow cake pan.
