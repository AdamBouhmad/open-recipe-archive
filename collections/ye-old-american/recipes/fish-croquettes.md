## Ingredients

- 1½ cups cold flaked halibut or salmon
- 1 cup thick White Sauce
- Salt and pepper
- Crumbs
- Egg
- Parsley
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. To one and one-half cups cold flaked halibut or salmon add one cup thick White Sauce
2. Season with salt and pepper, and spread on a plate to cool
3. Shape, roll in crumbs, egg, and crumbs
4. Fry in deep fat; drain
5. Arrange on hot dish for serving, and garnish with parsley
6. If salmon is used, add lemon juice and finely chopped parsley
