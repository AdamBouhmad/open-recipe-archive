## Ingredients

- pears
- lettuce leaves
- French dressing
- red pepper ribbons

## Directions

1. Wipe, pare, and cut pears in eighths lengthwise; then remove seeds.
2. Arrange on lettuce leaves, pour over French dressing, and garnish with ribbons of red pepper.
