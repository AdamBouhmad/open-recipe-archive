## Ingredients

- oranges
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Allow pound for pound
2. Pare half the oranges and cut the rind into shreds
3. Boil in three waters until tender, and set aside
4. Grate the rind of the remaining oranges; take off and throw away every bit of the thick white inner skin
5. Quarter all the oranges and take out the seeds
6. Chop, or cut them into small pieces; drain all the juice that will come away, without pressing them, over the sugar
7. Heat this, stirring until the sugar is dissolved, adding a very little water, unless the oranges are very juicy
8. Boil and skim five or six minutes; put in the boiled shreds, and cook ten minutes
9. Then add the chopped fruit and grated peel, and boil twenty minutes longer
10. When cold, put into small jars, tied up with bladder or with paper next the fruit, cloths dipped in wax over all, or put away in tumblers with self-adjusting metal tops with brandied tissue-paper pressed down closely to the fruit
