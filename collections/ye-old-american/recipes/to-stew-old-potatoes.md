## Ingredients

- old potatoes
- salt
- milk
- butter
- parsley
- flour

## Directions

1. Pare and quarter potatoes if large.
2. Soak in cold water one hour.
3. Put into a pot with enough cold salted water to cover them.
4. When almost done, turn off the water and add a like quantity of milk, and bring to a boil.
5. Before taking up, stir in a heaping tablespoonful of butter, a little salt, a handful of chopped parsley, and thicken slightly with flour previously wet in cold milk.
6. Boil one minute and pour all into a deep dish.
