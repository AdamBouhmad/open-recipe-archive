## Ingredients

- rice
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of rice, wash it clean in salt and water
2. Put it into two quarts of boiling water, and boil it briskly twenty minutes
3. Strain it through a colander and shake it into a dish, but do not touch it with your fingers nor with a spoon
