## Ingredients

- 6 potatoes, boiled and mashed fine while hot
- 6 tablespoonfuls baker's yeast
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls lard
- 1 even teaspoonful soda
- 1 quart warm water
- 3 cups flour

## Directions

1. Mash the potatoes, and work in the lard and sugar.
2. Stir to a cream, mixing in gradually a quart of the water in which the potatoes were boiled, which should have been poured out to cool down to blood warmth.
3. Beat in the flour, already wet up with a little potato-water to prevent lumping, then the yeast, lastly the soda.
4. Cover lightly if the weather is warm, more closely in winter, and set to rise over night in a warm place.
