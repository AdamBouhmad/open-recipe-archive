## Ingredients

- fresh shad
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash, wipe, and split the fish.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and lay it upon a buttered gridiron, inside downward.
3. When the lower side is browned, turn the fish.
4. One of medium size will be done in about twenty minutes.
5. Serve upon a hot dish, and lay a good piece of butter upon the fish.
