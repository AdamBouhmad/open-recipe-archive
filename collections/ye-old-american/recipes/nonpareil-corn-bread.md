## Ingredients

- 2 heaping cups of Indian meal
- 1 cup of flour
- 3 eggs
- 2½ cups milk
- 1 tablespoonful lard
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 2 tablespoonfuls cream-tartar
- 1 tablespoonful salt

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs very thoroughly—whites and yolks separately
2. Melt the lard
3. Sift the cream-tartar and soda into the meal and flour while yet dry
4. Stir this in at the last
5. Beat like mad
6. Bake quickly and steadily in a buttered mould
7. Half an hour will usually suffice
8. In cutting corn bread hold the knife perpendicularly and cut toward you
