## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 quart flour or enough for thick batter
- 1 gill baker's yeast
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful soda, dissolved in boiling water
- Nearly a quart of berries, well dredged with flour

## Directions

1. Make a batter of eggs, milk, flour, yeast, salt, and soda, and set it to rise in a warm place about four hours
2. When you are ready to boil it, stir in the dredged fruit quickly and lightly
3. Boil in a buttered mould or a floured cloth for two hours
4. Eat hot with sweet sauce
