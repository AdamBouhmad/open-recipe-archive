## Ingredients

- two green peppers
- boiling water
- one head of romaine
- one large grape fruit
- three small ripe tomatoes
- French Dressing

## Directions

1. Cook two green peppers in boiling water one minute; cool, and shred
2. Shred one head of romaine
3. Remove pulp from one large grape fruit
4. Cut three small ripe tomatoes in quarters lengthwise
5. Arrange in salad dish and serve with French Dressing
