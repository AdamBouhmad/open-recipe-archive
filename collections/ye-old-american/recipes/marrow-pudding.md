## Ingredients

- a large loaf of bread
- a pint of rich milk boiling hot
- four eggs
- a pound of beef marrow sliced thin
- a gill of brandy
- sugar
- nutmeg
- slices of citron

## Directions

1. Grate a large loaf of bread, and pour on the crumbs a pint of rich milk boiling hot
2. When cold, add four eggs, a pound of beef marrow sliced thin, a gill of brandy, with sugar and nutmeg to your taste
3. Mix all well together, and either bake or boil it
4. When done, stick slices of citron over the top
