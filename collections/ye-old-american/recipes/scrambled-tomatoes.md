## Ingredients

- a dozen tomatoes
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- five or six eggs

## Directions

1. Remove the skins from a dozen tomatoes; cut them up in a saucepan.
2. Add a little butter, pepper and salt.
3. When sufficiently boiled, beat up five or six eggs and just before you serve turn them into the saucepan with the tomatoes.
4. Stir one way for two minutes, allowing them time to be done thoroughly.
