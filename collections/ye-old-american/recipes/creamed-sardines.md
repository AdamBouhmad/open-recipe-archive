## Ingredients

- One small box sardines
- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup soft stale bread crumbs
- 1 cup cream
- Two hard-boiled eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Paprika

## Directions

1. Drain from oil one small box sardines, remove backbones from fish, then mash.
2. Melt one-fourth cup butter, add one-fourth cup soft stale bread crumbs, and one cup cream.
3. When thoroughly heated add two hard-boiled eggs finely chopped, the sardines, salt, pepper, and paprika to taste.
4. Serve on pieces of toasted bread.
