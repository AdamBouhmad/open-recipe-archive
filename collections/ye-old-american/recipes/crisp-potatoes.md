## Ingredients

- cold raw potatoes
- boiling fat
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut cold raw potatoes into shavings, cubes, or any small shape.
2. Throw them, a few at a time, into boiling fat and toss them about with a knife until they are a uniform light brown.
3. Drain and season with salt and pepper.
