## Ingredients

- rice
- cold water
- salt
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Pick the rice carefully, to clear it from husks and motes.
2. Soak it in cold water for a quarter of an hour, or more.
3. Pour off the water in which it has soaked.
4. Have ready a pot or sauce-pan of boiling water, into which you have put a little salt.
5. Allow two quarts of water to a pound of rice.
6. Sprinkle the rice gradually into the water.
7. Boil it hard for twenty minutes, then take it off the fire, and pour off all the water that remains.
8. Set the pot in the chimney corner with the lid off, while dinner is dishing, that it may have time to dry.
9. You may toss it up lightly with two forks, to separate the grains while it is drying, but do not stir it with a spoon.
