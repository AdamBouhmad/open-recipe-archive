## Ingredients

- two cups riced sweet potatoes
- three tablespoons butter
- one-half teaspoon salt
- hot milk

## Directions

1. To two cups riced sweet potatoes add three tablespoons butter, one-half teaspoon salt, and hot milk to moisten.
2. Beat until light, and pile on a vegetable dish.
