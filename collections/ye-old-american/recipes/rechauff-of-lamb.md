## Ingredients

- two tablespoons butter
- two and one-half tablespoons flour
- one-fourth teaspoon curry powder
- one-fourth teaspoon mustard
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- one-eighth teaspoon paprika
- one cup brown stock
- two tablespoons sherry wine
- cold roast lamb

## Directions

1. Brown two tablespoons butter.
2. Add two and one-half tablespoons flour and stir until well browned.
3. Add one-fourth teaspoon each of curry powder, mustard, and salt, and one-eighth teaspoon paprika.
4. Add gradually one cup brown stock and two tablespoons sherry wine.
5. Reheat cold roast lamb cut in thin slices in sauce.
