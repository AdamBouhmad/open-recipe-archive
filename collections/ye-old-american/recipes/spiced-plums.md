## Ingredients

- plums
- cider vinegar
- sugar
- cinnamon bark
- whole cloves
- broken nutmeg
- muslin bag

## Directions

1. Take seven pounds of plums, one pint of cider vinegar, four pounds of sugar, two tablespoonfuls of broken cinnamon bark, half as much of whole cloves and the same of broken nutmeg
2. Place these in a muslin bag and simmer them in a little vinegar and water for half an hour
3. Add it all to the vinegar and sugar, and bring to a boil
4. Before cooking the plums, pierce them with a darning needle several times to prevent the skins bursting while cooking
5. Add the plums and boil carefully until they are cooked tender
