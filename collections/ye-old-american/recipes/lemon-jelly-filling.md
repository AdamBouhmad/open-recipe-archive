## Ingredients

- two lemons, yellow from the rind and juice
- two cupfuls of sugar
- yolks and whites of two eggs
- one cupful of boiling water
- two tablespoonfuls of sifted flour
- half a cup of water
- one tablespoonful of melted butter

## Directions

1. Grate the yellow from the rind of two lemons and squeeze out the juice
2. Mix the sugar and yolks, then add the whites and then the lemons
3. Pour on a cupful of boiling water
4. Stir into this two tablespoonfuls of sifted flour, rubbed smooth in half a cup of water
5. Add a tablespoonful of melted butter
6. Cook until it thickens
7. When cold, spread between the layers of cake
