## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup butter
- 2 cups flour (scant)
- ¼ teaspoon soda
- Whites 6 eggs
- 1¼ cups powdered sugar
- 1½ teaspoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add flour gradually, mixed and sifted with soda, then add lemon juice.
2. Beat whites of eggs until stiff; add sugar gradually, and combine the mixtures.
3. Bake fifty minutes in a long shallow pan.
4. Cover with Opera Caramel Frosting.
