## Ingredients

- sea-bass
- flour
- salt
- butter or dripping

## Directions

1. Use smaller fish for this purpose than for boiling.
2. Clean, wipe dry, inside and out, dredge with flour and season with salt.
3. Fry in hot butter or dripping.
4. A mixture, half butter, half lard, is good for frying fish.
5. The bass should be done to a delicate brown—not to a crisp.
