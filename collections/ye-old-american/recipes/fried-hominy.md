## Ingredients

- cold boiled hominy
- butter or dripping
- lard or drippings
- milk
- melted butter
- beaten egg
- flour

## Directions

1. If large, put a good lump of butter or dripping in the frying-pan and heat.
2. Turn in some cold boiled hominy and cook until the under-side is browned.
3. Place a dish upside down on the frying-pan and upset the latter, that the brown crust may be uppermost.
4. For small hominy, cut in slices and fry in hot lard or drippings.
5. Alternatively, moisten to a soft paste with milk; beat in some melted butter, bind with a beaten egg, form into round cakes with your hands, dredge with flour and fry a light brown.
