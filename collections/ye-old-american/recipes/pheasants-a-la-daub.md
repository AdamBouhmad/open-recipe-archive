## Ingredients

- two pheasants
- savoury jelly

## Directions

1. Roast two pheasants in the nicest manner
2. Get a deep dish, the size and form of the one you intend to serve the pheasants in, it must be as deep as a tureen
3. Put in savoury jelly about an inch and a half at the bottom
4. When that is set, and the pheasants cold, lay them on the jelly with their breasts down
5. Fill the dish with jelly up to their backs
6. Take care it is not warm enough to melt the other, and that the birds are not displaced
7. Just before it is to be served, set it a moment in hot water to loosen it
8. Put the dish on the top, and turn it out carefully
