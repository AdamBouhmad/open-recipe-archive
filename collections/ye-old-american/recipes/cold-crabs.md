## Ingredients

- crabs
- sweet oil
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- mustard
- vinegar
- hard-boiled egg yolk

## Directions

1. Have taken all the meat out of the shells.
2. Make a dressing with sweet oil, salt, cayenne pepper, mustard and vinegar, as for lobster.
3. You may add to it some hard-boiled yolk of egg, mashed in the oil.
4. Put the mixture into the back-shells of the crabs, and serve it up.
5. Garnish with the small claws laid nicely round.
