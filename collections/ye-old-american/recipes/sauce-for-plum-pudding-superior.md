## Ingredients

- a cupful of sugar
- half a cupful of butter
- the yolks of four eggs
- one wine-glass of wine or one of brandy
- a pinch of salt
- one large cupful of hot cream or rich milk

## Directions

1. Cream together a cupful of sugar and half a cupful of butter
2. When light and creamy, add the well-beaten yolks of four eggs
3. Stir into this one wine-glass of wine or one of brandy, a pinch of salt and one large cupful of hot cream or rich milk
4. Beat this mixture well
5. Place it in a saucepan over the fire, stir it until it cooks sufficiently to thicken like cream
6. Be sure and not let it boil
