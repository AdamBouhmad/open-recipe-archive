## Ingredients

- four eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- half a teaspoonful of vanilla extract
- one cupful of whipped cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth and gradually beat the flavoring and sugar into them
2. When well beaten add the yolks and, lastly, the whipped cream
3. Have a dish holding about one quart slightly buttered
4. Pour the mixture into this and bake just twelve minutes
5. Serve the moment it is taken from the oven
