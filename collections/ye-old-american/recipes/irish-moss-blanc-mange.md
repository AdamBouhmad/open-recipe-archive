## Ingredients

- a small handful of moss
- one quart of milk
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar
- half a teaspoonful of vanilla flavoring
- sugar and cream

## Directions

1. Wash moss very carefully, and put it in one quart of milk on the fire
2. Let the milk simmer for about twenty minutes, or until the moss begins to dissolve
3. Remove from the fire and strain through a fine sieve
4. Add two tablespoonfuls of sugar and half a teaspoonful of vanilla flavoring
5. Put away to harden in cups or molds, and serve with sugar and cream
