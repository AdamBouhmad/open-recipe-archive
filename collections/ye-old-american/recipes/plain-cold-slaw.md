## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice cabbage very fine
2. Season with salt, pepper and a little sugar
3. Pour over vinegar and mix thoroughly
4. Serve in the centre of a platter with fried oysters around it
