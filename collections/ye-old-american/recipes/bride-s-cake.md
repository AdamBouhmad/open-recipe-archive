## Ingredients

- one scant cup of butter
- three cups of sugar
- one cup of milk
- whites of twelve eggs
- three teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- one cup of cornstarch
- three cups of sifted flour
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Cream together one scant cup of butter and three cups of sugar.
2. Add one cup of milk, then the beaten whites of twelve eggs.
3. Sift three teaspoonfuls of baking powder into one cup of cornstarch mixed with three cups of sifted flour and beat in gradually with the rest.
4. Flavor to taste.
5. Beat all thoroughly, then put in buttered tins lined with letter paper well buttered.
6. Bake slowly in a moderate oven.
7. Ice the top.
