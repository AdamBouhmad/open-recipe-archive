## Ingredients

- spare-rib
- greased paper
- flour
- butter
- fine bread-crumbs
- powdered sage
- pepper
- salt
- small onion
- brown flour
- hot water
- juice of a lemon
- tomato catsup

## Directions

1. When first put down to the fire, cover with a greased paper until it is half-done.
2. Remove it then, and dredge with flour.
3. A few minutes later, baste once with butter, and afterward, every little while, with its own gravy.
4. Just before you take it up, strew over the surface thickly with fine bread-crumbs seasoned with powdered sage, pepper, and salt, and a small onion minced into almost invisible bits.
5. Let it cook five minutes and baste once more with butter.
6. Skim the gravy, add a half cupful of hot water, thicken with brown flour, squeeze in the juice of a lemon, strain, and pour over the meat in the dish.
7. Send tomato catsup around with it, or if you prefer, put a liberal spoonful in the gravy, after it is strained.
