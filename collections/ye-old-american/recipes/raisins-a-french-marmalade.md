## Ingredients

- six fine large cooking apples
- wine-glass of Medeira wine
- half a pound of sugar
- two and a half pounds of raisins
- water

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and put them over a slow fire together with a wine-glass of Medeira wine and half a pound of sugar.
2. When well stewed, split and stone two and a half pounds of raisins, and put them to stew with the apples and enough water to prevent their burning.
3. When all appears well dissolved, beat it through a strainer bowl, and lastly through a sieve.
4. Mold, if you like, or put away in small preserve jars, to cut in thin slices for the ornamentation of pastry, or to dish up for eating with cream.
