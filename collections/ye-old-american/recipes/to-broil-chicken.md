## Ingredients

- chickens
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- hot water
- parsley
- poached eggs

## Directions

1. After dressing and washing the chickens as previously directed, split them open through the backbone
2. frog them by cutting the cords under the wings and laying the wings out flat
3. cut the sinews under the second joint of the leg and turn the leg down
4. press down the breast-bone without breaking it
5. Season the chicken with salt and pepper
6. lay it upon the gridiron with the inside first to the fire
7. put the gridiron over a slow fire
8. place a tin sheet and weight upon the chicken, to keep it flat
9. let it broil ten minutes
10. turn and proceed in the same manner with the other side
11. The chicken should be perfectly cooked, but not scorched
12. closely watch while broiling
13. arrange fire so that the heat shall be equally dispensed
14. When the fire is too hot under any one part, put a little ashes on the fire under that part
15. Dish a broiled chicken on a hot plate
16. put a large lump of butter and a tablespoonful of hot water upon the plate
17. turn the chicken two or three times that it may absorb as much of the butter as possible
18. Garnish with parsley
19. Serve with poached eggs on a separate dish
