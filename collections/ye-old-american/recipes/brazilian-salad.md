## Ingredients

- white grapes
- shredded fresh pineapple
- apples
- celery
- Brazil nuts
- lemon juice
- Cream Mayonnaise Dressing

## Directions

1. Remove skin and seeds from white grapes and cut in halves lengthwise.
2. Add an equal quantity of shredded fresh pineapple, apples pared, cored, and cut in small pieces, and celery cut in small pieces; then add one-fourth the quantity of Brazil nuts broken in pieces.
3. Mix thoroughly, and season with lemon juice.
4. Moisten with Cream Mayonnaise Dressing.
