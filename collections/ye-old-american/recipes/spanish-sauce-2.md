## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups canned tomatoes
- one-fourth onion
- sprig of parsley
- bit of bay leaf
- six cloves
- one-third teaspoon salt
- one-fourth teaspoon paprika
- cayenne
- yolks three eggs
- three tablespoons olive oil
- one tablespoon granulated gelatine
- three-fourths tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- three-fourths tablespoon cold water

## Directions

1. Cook one and one-half cups canned tomatoes fifteen minutes with one-fourth onion, sprig of parsley, bit of bay leaf, six cloves, one-third teaspoon salt, one-fourth teaspoon paprika, and a few grains cayenne.
2. Rub through a sieve.
3. Beat yolks three eggs slightly, and add, gradually, three tablespoons olive oil.
4. Combine mixtures and cook over hot water, stirring constantly.
5. Add one tablespoon granulated gelatine soaked in three-fourths tablespoon each tarragon vinegar and cold water.
6. Strain, and cool.
