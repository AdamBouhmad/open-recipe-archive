## Ingredients

- a pound of fine potatoes
- three quarters of a pound of sugar
- three quarters of a pound of butter
- a wine glass of rose water
- a glass of wine
- a glass of brandy
- a tea-spoonful of powdered mace and cinnamon
- a grated nutmeg
- the juice and grated peel of a large lemon
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Boil a pound of fine potatoes, peel them, mash them, and rub them through a cullender
2. Stir together to a cream, three quarters of a pound of sugar, and the same quantity of butter
3. Add to them gradually, a wine glass of rose water, a glass of wine, and a glass of brandy; a tea-spoonful of powdered mace and cinnamon, a grated nutmeg, and the juice and grated peel of a large lemon
4. Beat six eggs very light, and add them by degrees to the mixture, alternately with the potato
5. Bake it three quarters of an hour in a buttered dish
