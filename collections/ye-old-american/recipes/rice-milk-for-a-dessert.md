## Ingredients

- half a pint of rice
- a pint of milk
- two eggs
- butter
- sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil half a pint of rice in water till tender, pour off the water, and add a pint of milk with two eggs beaten well, stirred into it
2. Boil all together two or three minutes
3. Serve it up hot, and eat it with butter, sugar, and nutmeg
4. It may be sweetened and cooled in moulds, turned out in a deep dish, and surrounded with rich milk, with raspberry marmalade stirred into it, and strained to keep back the seeds
5. Or the milk may be seasoned with wine and sugar
