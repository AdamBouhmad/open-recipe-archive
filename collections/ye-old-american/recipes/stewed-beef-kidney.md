## Ingredients

- kidney
- pepper
- salt
- water
- flour
- parsley
- sheep's kidneys
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the kidney into slices, season highly with pepper and salt, fry it a light brown.
2. Take out the slices, then pour a little warm water into the pan.
3. Dredge in some flour, put in slices of kidney again.
4. Let them stew very gently; add some parsley if liked.
5. Sheep's kidneys may be split open, broiled over a clear fire and served with a piece of butter placed on each half.
