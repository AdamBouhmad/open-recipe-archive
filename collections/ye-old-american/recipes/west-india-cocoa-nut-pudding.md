## Ingredients

- a large ripe cocoa-nut
- a pound of grated cocoa-nut
- sixteen eggs (omitting the whites of four)
- a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- a wine glass of rose-water
- puff-paste
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Cut up and skin a large ripe cocoa-nut and grate it fine
2. Put the grated cocoa-nut into a clean cloth and squeeze and press it till all the moisture is taken out
3. Spread it on a broad tin pan and stand it up to dry, either in the sun or before the fire, stirring it up occasionally with your hands
4. When quite dry weigh a pound of it
5. Beat very light sixteen eggs omitting the whites of four and then beat into them, gradually, a pound of powdered loaf-sugar and a wine glass of rose-water
6. Give the whole a hard stirring
7. Put the mixture into deep dishes and lay puff-paste round their edges handsomely notched
8. Bake them about half an hour
9. Send them to table cold with white sugar grated over the top
