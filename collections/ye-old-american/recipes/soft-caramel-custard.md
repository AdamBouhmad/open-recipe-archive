## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- half a cupful of sugar
- six eggs
- half a teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Put the milk on to boil, reserving a cupful.
2. Beat the eggs and add the cold milk to them.
3. Stir the sugar in a small frying pan until it becomes liquid and just begins to smoke.
4. Stir it into the boiling milk.
5. Add the beaten eggs and cold milk and stir constantly until the mixture begins to thicken.
6. Set away to cool.
7. Serve in glasses.
