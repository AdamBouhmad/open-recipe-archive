## Ingredients

- 1 quart oysters
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoonful mace
- 1 teacupful cider vinegar
- 1 teacupful sherry

## Directions

1. Chop the oysters and boil in their own liquor with a teacupful of vinegar, skimming the scum as it rises
2. Boil three minutes, strain through a hair-cloth
3. Return the liquor to the fire, add the wine, pepper, salt, and mace
4. Boil fifteen minutes, and when cold bottle for use, sealing the corks
