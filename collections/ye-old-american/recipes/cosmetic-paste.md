## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of Castile soap
- water
- Indian meal
- rose-water or fragrant essence

## Directions

1. Cut the Castile soap into small pieces
2. Put it into a tin or porcelain sauce-pan, with just water enough to moisten it well
3. Set it on hot coals
4. Let it simmer till it is entirely dissolved; stir it till it becomes a smooth paste
5. Thicken it with Indian meal
6. Take it from the fire, and when cool scent it with rose-water, or with any fragrant essence you please
7. Beat and stir it hard with a silver spoon
8. When it is thoroughly mixed put it into little pots with covers
