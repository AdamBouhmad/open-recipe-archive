## Ingredients

- half a pound of good brown sugar
- two pounds of flour
- two spoonsful of yeast
- new milk
- half a pound of butter
- boiling lard

## Directions

1. Dry half a pound of good brown sugar, pound it and mix it with two pounds of flour
2. Sift it
3. Add two spoonsful of yeast, and as much new milk as will make it like bread
4. When well risen, knead in half a pound of butter
5. Make it in cakes the size of a half dollar
6. Fry them a light brown in boiling lard
