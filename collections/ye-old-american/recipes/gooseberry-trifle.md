## Ingredients

- One quart of gooseberries
- sugar
- one pint of custard
- a plateful of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Put the gooseberries into a jar, with sufficient moist sugar to sweeten them
2. Boil them until reduced to a pulp
3. Put this pulp at the bottom of a trifle dish
4. Pour over it a pint of custard
5. When cold, cover with whipped cream
6. The cream should be whipped the day before it is wanted for table
