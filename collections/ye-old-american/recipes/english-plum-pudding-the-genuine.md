## Ingredients

- one pound of stale bread
- a pint of hot milk
- one-half pound of sugar
- the yolks of eight eggs
- one pound of raisins, stoned and floured
- one pound of Zante currants, washed and floured
- a quarter of a pound of citron cut in slips and dredged with flour
- one pound of beef suet, chopped fine and salted
- one glass of wine
- one glass of brandy
- one nutmeg
- a tablespoonful of mace, cinnamon and cloves mixed
- the whites of eight eggs
- cloth
- wine or brandy sauce

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of stale bread in a pint of hot milk and let it stand and cool
2. When cold, add to it one-half pound of sugar and the yolks of eight eggs beaten to a cream
3. Add one pound of raisins, stoned and floured, and one pound of Zante currants, washed and floured
4. Add a quarter of a pound of citron cut in slips and dredged with flour
5. Add one pound of beef suet, chopped fine and salted
6. Add one glass of wine and one glass of brandy
7. Add one nutmeg and a tablespoonful of mace, cinnamon and cloves mixed
8. Beat the whole well together
9. Add the whites of the eight eggs, beaten to a stiff froth as the last thing
10. Pour into a cloth, previously scalded and dredged with flour
11. Tie it firmly, leaving room for the pudding to swell
12. Boil six hours
13. Serve with wine or brandy sauce
