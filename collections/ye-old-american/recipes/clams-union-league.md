## Ingredients

- one-half teaspoon finely chopped shallot
- one and one-half tablespoons butter
- eighteen clams
- one-half cup white wine
- two tablespoons butter
- two tablespoons flour
- one-fourth cup cream
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- fried bacon

## Directions

1. Fry one-half teaspoon finely chopped shallot in one and one-half tablespoons butter five minutes.
2. Add eighteen clams and one-half cup white wine.
3. Cook until the shells open.
4. Remove clams from shells and reduce liquor to one-third cupful.
5. Melt two tablespoons butter, add two tablespoons flour, and pour on gradually the clam liquor.
6. Add one-fourth cup cream and the clams, season with salt and pepper.
7. Refill clam-shells, sprinkle with chopped parsley, and serve on each a square piece of fried bacon.
