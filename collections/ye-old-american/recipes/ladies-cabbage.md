## Ingredients

- firm white cabbage
- eggs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- rich milk or cream

## Directions

1. Boil a firm white cabbage fifteen minutes, changing the water then for more from the boiling tea-kettle
2. When tender, drain and set aside until perfectly cold
3. Chop fine and add two beaten eggs, a tablespoonful of butter, pepper, salt, and three tablespoonfuls rich milk or cream
4. Stir all well together and bake in a buttered pudding-dish until brown
5. Eat very hot
