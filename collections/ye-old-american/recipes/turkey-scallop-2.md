## Ingredients

- cold turkey meat
- bread crumbs
- milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- hot water
- turkey gravy
- two eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of milk
- one tablespoonful of melted butter
- cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Pick the meat from the bones of cold turkey and chop it fine
2. Put a layer of bread crumbs on the bottom of a buttered dish
3. Moisten them with a little milk
4. Put in a layer of turkey with some of the filling
5. Cut small pieces of butter over the top and sprinkle with pepper and salt
6. Add another layer of bread crumbs and repeat until the dish is nearly full
7. Add a little hot water to the gravy left from the turkey and pour over it
8. Take two eggs, two tablespoonfuls of milk, one tablespoonful of melted butter, a little salt and cracker crumbs as much as will make it thick enough to spread on with a knife
9. Put bits of butter over it and cover with a plate
10. Bake three-quarters of an hour
11. Ten minutes before serving, remove the plate and let it brown
