## Ingredients

- ½ lb. butter
- 1 lb. sugar
- 6 eggs—whites and yolks separately
- Juice of one lemon
- Grated rind of two
- 1 nutmeg
- ½ glass brandy

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar, beat in the yolks, the lemon, spice, and brandy, stirring in the whites at the last.
2. Bake in pie-crust, open.
3. You may, if you wish to have these very nice, beat up the whites of but four eggs in the mixture, and whip the whites of four more into a méringue with four tablespoonfuls sugar and a little lemon-juice, to spread over the top of each pie.
4. Eat cold.
