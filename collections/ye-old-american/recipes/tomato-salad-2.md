## Ingredients

- twelve good, sound, fresh tomatoes
- Mayonnaise dressing or Cream dressing
- one head of broad-leaved lettuce

## Directions

1. Peel and slice twelve good, sound, fresh tomatoes into slices about a quarter of an inch thick
2. Set them on the ice or in a refrigerator while you make the dressing
3. Make the dressing the same as Mayonnaise, or you may use Cream dressing
4. Take one head of the broad-leaved variety of lettuce, wash, and arrange them neatly around the sides of a salad bowl
5. Place the cold, sliced tomatoes in the centre
6. Pour over the dressing and serve
