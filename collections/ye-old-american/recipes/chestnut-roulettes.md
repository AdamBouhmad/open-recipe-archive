## Ingredients

- 1 cup chestnut purée
- 2 eggs
- Few drops onion juice
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons heavy cream
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains paprika

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given, cook two minutes, and cool.
2. Shape a little larger than French chestnuts, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again.
3. Fry in deep fat, and drain on brown paper.
