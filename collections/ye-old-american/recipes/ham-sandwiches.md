## Ingredients

- thin slices of bread
- butter
- mustard
- very thin slices of cold boiled ham

## Directions

1. Cut some thin slices of bread very neatly, having slightly buttered them.
2. If you choose, spread on a very little mustard.
3. Have ready some very thin slices of cold boiled ham, and lay one between two slices of bread.
4. You may either roll them up, or lay them flat on the plates.
5. Use them at supper, or at luncheon.
