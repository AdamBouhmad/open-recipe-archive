## Ingredients

- two eggs
- three ounces of butter
- three ounces of flour
- three ounces of pounded sugar
- preserve
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Well whisk the eggs, put them into a basin
2. Stir to them the butter, which should be beaten to a cream
3. Add the flour and sifted sugar gradually
4. Mix all well together
5. Butter a baking sheet
6. Drop on it a teaspoonful of the mixture at a time, leaving a space between each
7. Bake in a cool oven
8. When half done, roll them up like wafers and put in a small wedge of bread or piece of wood to keep them in shape
9. Return them to the oven until crisp
10. Before serving, remove the bread, put a spoonful of preserve in the widest end, and fill up with whipped cream
