## Ingredients

- fresh salmon
- salted water
- vinegar
- whole black pepper
- nutmeg
- mace
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Having cleaned it, cut it into large pieces
2. Boil it in salted water as if for eating
3. Drain it, wrap it in a dry cloth, and set it in a cold place till next day
4. Make the pickle: To one quart of the water in which the salmon was boiled, allow two quarts of the best vinegar, one ounce of whole black pepper, one nutmeg grated and a dozen blades of mace
5. Boil all these together in a kettle closely covered to prevent the flavor from evaporating
6. When the vinegar thus prepared is quite cold, pour it over the salmon
7. Put on the top a tablespoonful of sweet oil, which will make it keep the longer
8. Cover it closely, put it in a dry, cool place, and it will be good for many months
