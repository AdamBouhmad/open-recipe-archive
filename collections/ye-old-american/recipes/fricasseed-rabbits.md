## Ingredients

- rabbits
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley
- powdered mace
- warm water or veal broth
- butter
- flour
- thick cream
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take a couple of fine ones, and cut them up, or disjoint them.
2. Put them into a stew-pan; season them with cayenne pepper and salt, some chopped parsley, and some powdered mace.
3. Pour in a pint of warm water (or of veal broth, if you have it) and stew it over a slow fire till the rabbits are quite tender; adding (when they are about half done) some bits of butter rolled in flour.
4. Just before you take it from the fire, enrich the gravy with a jill or more of thick cream with some nutmeg grated into it.
5. Stir the gravy well, but take care not to let it boil after the cream is in, lest it curdle.
6. Put the pieces of rabbit on a hot dish, and pour the gravy over them.
