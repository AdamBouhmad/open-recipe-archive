## Ingredients

- half a pound of shelled sweet almonds
- three ounces of shelled bitter almonds or peach-kernels
- rose water
- half a pound of fresh butter
- half a pound of powdered white sugar
- a glass of mixed wine and brandy
- twelve eggs
- puff paste
- sugar

## Directions

1. Scald and peel the almonds, throwing them, as they are peeled, into cold water.
2. Pound them one at a time in a marble mortar, adding to each a few drops of rose water, otherwise they will be heavy and oily.
3. Mix the sweet and bitter almonds together by pounding them alternately; and as you do them, take them out and lay them on a plate. Each must be beaten to a fine smooth paste, free from the smallest lumps.
4. It is best to prepare them the day before you make the pudding.
5. Stir to a cream half a pound of fresh butter and half a pound of powdered white sugar; and by degrees pour into it a glass of mixed wine and brandy.
6. Beat to a stiff froth the whites only of twelve eggs.
7. Stir alternately into the butter and sugar the pounded almonds and the beaten white of egg.
8. When the whole is well mixed, put it into a buttered dish and lay puff paste round the edge.
9. Bake it about half an hour, and when cold grate sugar over it.
