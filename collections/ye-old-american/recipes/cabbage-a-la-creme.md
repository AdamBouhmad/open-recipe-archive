## Ingredients

- two good heads of cabbage
- salt
- butter
- flour
- milk
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take two good heads of cabbage
2. Cut out the stalks
3. Boil it tender with a little salt in the water
4. Have ready one large spoonful of butter and a small one of flour rubbed into it
5. Add half a pint of milk with pepper and salt
6. Make it hot
7. Put the cabbage in after pressing out the water
8. Stew it till quite tender
