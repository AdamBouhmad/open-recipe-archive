## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- Grated rind 1 lemon
- ⅔ cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ½ cup Madeira wine
- Few grains salt
- ⅔ cup heavy cream
- macaroon dust

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs slightly
2. Add lemon juice, grated rind, wine, sugar, and salt
3. Cook until mixture thickens, stirring constantly
4. Add whites of eggs beaten stiff, and when well mixed, set in a pan of ice-water to cool, stirring occasionally
5. Beat cream until stiff, and add
6. Fill small paper cases with mixture
7. Cover with macaroon dust
8. Set in a tin mould with tight-fitting cover
9. Pack mould in salt and ice, and let stand two hours
