## Ingredients

- eighteen eggs
- sauce a-la-creme
- grated bread
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil eighteen eggs
2. Separate the yelks and whites, and cut them in dice
3. Pour over them a sauce a-la-creme
4. Add a little grated bread
5. Mix all well together
6. Let it get cold
7. Add salt and pepper
8. Make them into cakes
9. Cover them well on both sides with grated bread
10. Let them stand an hour
11. Fry them a nice brown
12. Dry them a little before the fire
13. Dish them while quite hot
