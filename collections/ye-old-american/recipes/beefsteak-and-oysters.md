## Ingredients

- beefsteak
- one quart of oysters
- three ounces of butter
- one tablespoonful of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Broil the steak the usual way
2. Put one quart of oysters with very little of the liquor into a stewpan upon the fire
3. When it comes to a boil, take off the scum that may rise
4. Stir in three ounces of butter mixed with a tablespoonful of sifted flour
5. Let it boil one minute until it thickens
6. Pour it over the steak
7. Serve hot
