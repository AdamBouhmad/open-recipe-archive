## Ingredients

- One pint of Indian meal
- A small lump of butter
- A dessertspoonful of salt
- Four eggs
- One quart of wheaten flour
- Half a pint of buttermilk, or sour cream
- Half a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
- Sweet milk

## Directions

1. Make one pint of Indian meal into mush in the usual way
2. While hot, put in a small lump of butter and a dessertspoonful of salt
3. Set the mush aside to cool
4. Beat separately till very light the whites and yolks of four eggs
5. Add the eggs to the mush, and cream in gradually one quart of wheaten flour
6. Add half a pint of buttermilk, or sour cream, in which has been dissolved half a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
7. Bring to the consistency of thin batter by the addition of sweet milk
8. Put waffle-irons on to heat an hour in advance
9. Have a brisk fire, butter the irons thoroughly, but with nicety
10. Fill the irons only half full of batter, that the waffles may have room to rise
11. Bake quickly
