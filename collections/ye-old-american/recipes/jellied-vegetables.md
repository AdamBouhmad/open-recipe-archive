## Ingredients

- one tablespoon granulated gelatine
- one-fourth cup cold water
- one cup boiling water
- one-fourth cup sugar
- one-fourth cup vinegar
- two tablespoons lemon juice
- one teaspoon salt
- one cup celery
- one-half cup finely shredded cabbage
- one and one-half canned pimentoes
- cold cooked meat
- celery tips

## Directions

1. Soak one tablespoon granulated gelatine in one-fourth cup cold water, and dissolve in one cup boiling water.
2. Add one-fourth cup, each, sugar and vinegar, two tablespoons lemon juice, and one teaspoon salt.
3. Strain, cool, and when beginning to stiffen, add one cup celery cut in small pieces, one-half cup finely shredded cabbage, and one and one-half canned pimentoes cut in small pieces.
4. Turn into a mould and chill.
5. Remove from mould and arrange around jelly thin slices of cold cooked meat overlapping one another.
6. Garnish with celery tips.
