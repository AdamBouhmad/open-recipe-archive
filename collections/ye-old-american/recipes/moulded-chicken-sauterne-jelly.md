## Ingredients

- 4-pound fowl
- 2 quarts cold water
- 4 slices carrot
- 1 onion stuck with eight cloves
- 2 stalks celery
- bit of bay leaf
- 1-half teaspoon peppercorns
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon granulated gelatine
- 1 teaspoon cold water
- salt
- pepper
- celery salt
- lemon juice
- onion juice
- Sauterne Jelly

## Directions

1. Cover fowl with cold water, and add carrot slices, onion stuck with cloves, celery stalks, bay leaf, peppercorns, and salt.
2. Bring quickly to boiling-point, and let simmer until meat is tender.
3. Remove meat from bones, and finely chop.
4. Reduce stock to three-fourths cup, cool, and remove fat.
5. Soak gelatine in cold water, and dissolve in stock which has been reheated.
6. Add to meat, and season with salt, pepper, celery salt, lemon juice, and onion juice.
7. Pack solidly into a slightly buttered one-pound baking powder tin, and chill.
8. Remove from tin, cut in thin slices, and arrange around Sauterne Jelly, beaten with a fork until light.
