## Ingredients

- pears
- water
- cup of sugar
- whole cloves
- sticks of cinnamon
- tablespoonful of lemon juice
- cream or boiled custard

## Directions

1. Peel and cut them in halves, leaving the stems on and scoop out the cores
2. Put them into a saucepan, placing them close together, with the stems uppermost
3. Pour over sufficient water, a cup of sugar, a few whole cloves and some sticks of cinnamon, a tablespoonful of lemon juice
4. Cover the stewpan closely, to stew gently till the fruit is done
5. Take out the fruit carefully and arrange it on a dish for serving
6. Boil down the syrup until quite thick
7. Strain it and allow it to cool enough to set it
8. Pour it over the fruit
9. Serve with cream or boiled custard
