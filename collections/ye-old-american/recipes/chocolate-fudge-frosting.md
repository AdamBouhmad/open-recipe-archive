## Ingredients

- 1½ tablespoons butter
- ⅓ cup unsweetened powdered cocoa
- 1¼ cups confectioners' sugar
- Few grains salt
- ¼ cup milk
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add cocoa, sugar, salt, and milk. Heat to boiling-point, and boil about eight minutes. Remove from fire and beat until creamy. Add vanilla and pour over cake.
