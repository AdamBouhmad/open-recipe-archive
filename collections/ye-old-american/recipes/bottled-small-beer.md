## Ingredients

- 1 quart bottle of the very best brisk porter
- 4 quarts of water
- 1 pint of molasses
- 1 table-spoonful of ginger

## Directions

1. Take a quart bottle of the very best brisk porter, and mix it with four quarts of water, a pint of molasses, and a table-spoonful of ginger
2. Bottle it
3. See that the corks are of the very best kind
4. It will be fit for use in three or four days
