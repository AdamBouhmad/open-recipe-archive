## Ingredients

- one pound sugar
- half an ounce allspice
- four quarts flour
- one pound butter
- melted in one pint milk
- nine eggs
- one gill emptins
- carroway seed and currants, or raisins if you please

## Directions

1. rub one pound sugar, half an ounce allspice into four quarts flour
2. into which pour one pound butter, melted in one pint milk
3. nine eggs, one gill emptins
4. make into two loaves
5. bake one and half hour
