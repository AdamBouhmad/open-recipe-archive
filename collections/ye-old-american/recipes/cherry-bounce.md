## Ingredients

- ripe morellas
- large black heart cherries
- loaf-sugar or sugar candy
- double rectified whiskey

## Directions

1. Mix together six pounds of ripe morellas and six pounds of large black heart cherries.
2. Put them into a wooden bowl or tub, and with a pestle or mallet mash them so as to crack all the stones.
3. Mix with the cherries three pounds of loaf-sugar, or of sugar candy broken up, and put them into a demijohn, or into a large stone jar.
4. Pour on two gallons of the best double rectified whiskey.
5. Stop the vessel closely, and let it stand three months, shaking it every day during the first month.
6. At the end of the three months you may strain the liquor and bottle it off.
