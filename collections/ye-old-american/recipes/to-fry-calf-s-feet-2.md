## Ingredients

- calf's feet
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- chopped parsley or sweet marjoram
- lard or butter
- parsley-sauce

## Directions

1. Having first boiled them till tender, cut them in two, and (having taken out the large bones) season the feet with pepper and salt, and dredge them well with flour.
2. Strew some chopped parsley or sweet marjoram over them, and fry them of a light brown in lard or butter.
3. Serve them up with parsley-sauce.
