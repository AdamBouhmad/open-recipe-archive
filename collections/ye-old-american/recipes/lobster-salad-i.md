## Ingredients

- lobster meat
- French Dressing
- Mayonnaise Dressing
- lettuce leaves
- lobster coral
- lobster claws
- Cream Dressing I or II

## Directions

1. Remove lobster meat from shell, cut in one-half inch cubes, and marinate with a French Dressing.
2. Mix with a small quantity of Mayonnaise Dressing and arrange in nests of lettuce leaves.
3. Put a spoonful of Mayonnaise on each, and sprinkle with lobster coral rubbed through a fine sieve.
4. Garnish with small lobster claws around outside of dish. Cream Dressing I or II may be used in place of Mayonnaise Dressing.
