## Ingredients

- 1¾ cups cold flaked fish (cod, haddock, halibut, or cusk)
- 1 cup White Sauce I
- Bit of bay leaf
- Sprig of parsley
- ½ slice onion
- Salt and pepper
- ½ cup buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Scald milk, for the making of White Sauce, with bay leaf, parsley, and onion
2. Cover the bottom of small buttered platter with one-half of the fish, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and pour over one-half the sauce
3. Repeat
4. Cover with crumbs, and bake in hot oven until crumbs are brown
