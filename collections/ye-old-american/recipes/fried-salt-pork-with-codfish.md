## Ingredients

- fat salt pork
- codfish
- Drawn Butter Sauce
- boiled potatoes
- beets

## Directions

1. Cut fat salt pork in one-fourth inch slices, cut gashes one-third inch apart in slices, nearly to rind.
2. Try out in a hot frying-pan until brown and crisp, occasionally turning off fat from pan.
3. Serve around strips of codfish which have been soaked in pan of lukewarm water and allowed to stand on back of range until soft.
4. Serve with Drawn Butter Sauce, boiled potatoes, and beets.
