## Ingredients

- Terrapin
- Boiling salted water
- Carrot
- Onion
- Celery

## Directions

1. Plunge into boiling water and boil five minutes
2. Lift out of water with skimmer, and remove skin from feet and tail by rubbing with a towel
3. Draw out head with a skewer, and rub off skin
4. Put in a kettle, cover with boiling salted water, add two slices each of carrot and onion, and a stalk of celery
5. Cook until meat is tender, which may be determined by pressing feet-meat between thumb and finger, thirty-five to forty minutes
6. Remove from water, cool, draw out nails from feet, cut under shell close to upper shell and remove
7. Empty upper shell and carefully remove and discard gall-bladder, sandbags, and thick, heavy part of intestines
