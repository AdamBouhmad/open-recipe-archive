## Ingredients

- pork leg
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- onion
- sage
- lard or drippings
- flour
- apple sauce

## Directions

1. Cut them from the leg, and remove the skin
2. Trim them and beat them
3. Sprinkle on salt and pepper
4. Prepare some beaten egg in a pan, and on a flat dish a mixture of bread crumbs, minced onion and sage
5. Put some lard or drippings into a frying pan over the fire
6. When it boils put in the cutlets, having dipped every one first in the egg, and then in the seasoning
7. Fry them twenty or thirty minutes, turning them often
8. After you have taken them out of the frying pan, skim the gravy
9. Dredge in a little flour, give it one boil, and then pour it on the dish round the cutlets
10. Have apple sauce to eat with them
