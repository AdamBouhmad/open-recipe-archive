## Ingredients

- 1 pint scalded milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2½ tablespoons bread flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 6 slices dry toast

## Directions

1. Make as Thin White Sauce.
2. Dip toast in sauce.
