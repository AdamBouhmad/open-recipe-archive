## Ingredients

- ripe barberries
- loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Take ripe barberries, and having stripped them from the stalks, mash them, and boil them in their juice for a quarter of an hour.
2. Then squeeze them through a bag; allow to each pint of juice, a pound of loaf-sugar.
3. Having melted the sugar in the juice, boil them together twenty or twenty-five minutes, skimming carefully.
4. Put it up in tumblers with tissue paper.
