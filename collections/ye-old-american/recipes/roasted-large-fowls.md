## Ingredients

- fowls
- lard
- necks and gizzards
- brown flour
- egg sauce

## Directions

1. Take the fowls when they are ready dressed
2. Put them down to a good fire
3. Dredge and baste them well with lard
4. They will be near an hour in roasting
5. Make a gravy of the necks and gizzards
6. Strain it and put in a spoonful of brown flour
7. When you dish them, pour on the gravy
8. Serve them up with egg sauce in a boat
