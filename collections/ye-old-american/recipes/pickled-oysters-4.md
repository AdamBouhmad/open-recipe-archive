## Ingredients

- one gallon of oysters
- salt
- mace
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash oysters well in their own liquor
2. Carefully clear away the particles of shell
3. Put them into a kettle and strain the liquor over them
4. Add salt to your taste
5. Let them just come to the boiling point, or until the edges curl up
6. Skim them out and lay in a dish to cool
7. Put a sprig of mace and a little cold pepper in the liquor and allow it to boil some time, skimming it now and then so long as any scum rises
8. Pour it into a pan and let it cool
9. When perfectly cool, add a half pint of strong vinegar
10. Place the oysters in a jar and pour the liquor over them
