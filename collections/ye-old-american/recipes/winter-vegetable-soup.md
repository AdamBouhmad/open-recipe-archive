## Ingredients

- three turnips
- three carrots
- three onions
- a little butter
- a bunch of celery
- three or four leeks
- one clove of garlic
- two stalks of parsley
- two cloves
- salt
- pepper
- a little grated nutmeg
- three quarts of water

## Directions

1. Scrape and slice three turnips and three carrots and peel three onions, and fry all with a little butter until a light yellow.
2. Add a bunch of celery and three or four leeks cut in pieces.
3. Stir and fry all the ingredients for six minutes.
4. When fried, add one clove of garlic, two stalks of parsley, two cloves, salt, pepper and a little grated nutmeg.
5. Cover with three quarts of water and simmer for three hours, taking off the scum carefully.
6. Strain and use.
