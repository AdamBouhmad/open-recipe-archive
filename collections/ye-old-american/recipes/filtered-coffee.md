## Ingredients

- 1 cup coffee (finely ground)
- 6 cups boiling water
- cut sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Place coffee in strainer and strainer in coffee-pot
2. Place pot on the range
3. Add gradually boiling water and allow it to filter
4. Cover between additions of water
5. Re-filter if desired stronger
6. Serve at once with cut sugar and cream
7. Put sugar and cream in cup before hot coffee for best results
