## Ingredients

- one quart of boiling water
- the peels of four large lemons (yellow outside only)
- the juice of the four lemons
- one cupful of fine white sugar
- the yolks of ten eggs

## Directions

1. Pare the peels of four large lemons into one quart of boiling water, the yellow outside only
2. Let it stand for four hours
3. Take them out and add to the water the juice of the four lemons and one cupful of fine white sugar
4. Beat the yolks of ten eggs and mix all together
5. Strain it through a piece of lawn or lace into a porcelain lined stewpan
6. Set it over a slow fire
7. Stir it one way until it is as thick as good cream, but do not let it boil
8. Take it from the fire, and when cool, serve in custard cups
