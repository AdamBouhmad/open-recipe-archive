## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 1⅔ cups flour
- 2½ teaspoons baking powder
- Orange Filling
- Orange Frosting

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, eggs well beaten, and milk.
2. Then add flour mixed and sifted with baking powder.
3. Bake in a thin sheet in a dripping-pan.
4. Cut in halves, spread one-half with Orange Filling.
5. Put over other half, and cover with Orange Frosting.
