## Ingredients

- brown crust from slices or pieces of stale bread
- tepid water
- pepper
- salt
- powdered summer savory
- sage
- melted butter
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. For an eight or ten pound turkey, cut the brown crust from slices or pieces of stale bread until you have as much as the inside of a pound loaf
2. Put it into a suitable dish and pour tepid water over it
3. Let it stand one minute, as it soaks very quickly
4. Take up a handful at a time and squeeze it hard and dry with both hands, placing it in another dish
5. When all is pressed dry, toss it all up lightly through your fingers
6. Add pepper, salt (about a teaspoonful), a teaspoonful of powdered summer savory, the same amount of sage, or the green herb minced fine
7. Add half a cup of melted butter, and a beaten egg, or not
8. Work thoroughly all together, and it is ready for dressing either fowls, fish or meats
