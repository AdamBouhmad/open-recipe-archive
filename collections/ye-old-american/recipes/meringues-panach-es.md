## Ingredients

- Meringues Glacées
- ice cream or ice cream and water ice
- whipped cream
- candied cherries

## Directions

1. Fill Meringues Glacées with ice cream, or ice cream and water ice.
2. Garnish with whipped cream forced through pastry bag and tube and candied cherries.
