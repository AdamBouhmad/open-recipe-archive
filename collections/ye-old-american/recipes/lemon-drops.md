## Ingredients

- lemon-juice
- the best loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Squeeze some lemon-juice into a pan.
2. Pound in a mortar some of the best loaf-sugar, and then sift it through a very fine sieve.
3. Mix it with the lemon-juice, making it so thick that you can scarcely stir it.
4. Put it into a porcelain saucepan, set it on hot coals, and stir it with a wooden spoon five minutes or more.
5. Take off the pan, and with the point of a knife drop the liquid on writing paper.
6. When cold, the drops will easily come off.
