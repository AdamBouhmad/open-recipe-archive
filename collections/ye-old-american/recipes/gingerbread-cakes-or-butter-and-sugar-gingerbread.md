## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- a grated nutmeg
- two ounces ginger
- one pound sugar
- three small spoons pearl ash dissolved in cream
- one pound butter
- four eggs

## Directions

1. knead it stiff
2. shape it to your fancy
3. bake 15 minutes
