## Ingredients

- sixteen yolks
- half a pound butter melted
- grate of two Seville oranges
- half pound of fine Sugar
- two spoons orange water
- two of rose-water
- one gill of wine
- half pint cream
- two naples biscuit or the crumbs of a fine loaf
- or roll soaked in cream

## Directions

1. mix all together
2. put it into rich puff-paste
3. which let be double round the edges of the dish
4. bake like a custard
