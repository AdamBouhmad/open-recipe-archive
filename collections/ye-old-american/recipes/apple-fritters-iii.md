## Ingredients

- Sour apples
- Powdered sugar
- Lemon juice
- Batter II

## Directions

1. Core, pare, and cut apples in one-third inch slices.
2. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and few drops lemon juice; cover, and let stand one-half hour.
3. Drain, dip pieces in batter, fry in deep fat, and drain.
4. Arrange on a folded napkin in form of a circle, and serve with Sabyon or Hard Sauce.
