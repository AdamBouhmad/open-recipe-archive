## Ingredients

- okra pods
- salt
- water
- butter
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the young and tender pods of long white okra in salted boiling water in granite, porcelain or a tin-lined saucepan--as contact with iron will discolor it; boil fifteen minutes.
2. Remove the stems, and serve with butter, pepper, salt and vinegar if preferred.
