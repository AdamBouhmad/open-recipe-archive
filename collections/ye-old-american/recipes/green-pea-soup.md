## Ingredients

- peas
- spinach
- butter
- flour
- salt
- cayenne
- bread for toasting

## Directions

1. Boil the peas slowly till all are entirely to pieces
2. Mash the peas well against the sides of the pot to extract their flavour
3. Strain off the liquid into a clean pot
4. Prepare spinach juice by pounding spinach in a mortar and add a tea-cup full to the soup, which will give it a fine green colour
5. Put in a quarter of a pound of the best fresh butter rolled whole in flour
6. Add a pint and a half more of shelled young peas, or a quart if you wish the soup very thick
7. Season with a very little salt and cayenne
8. Put it again over the fire and boil it till the last peas are quite soft, but not till they go to pieces
9. Have ready in a tureen two or three slices of toasted bread cut into small squares or dice
10. Pour the soup on the bread
