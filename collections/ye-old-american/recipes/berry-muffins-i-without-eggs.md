## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- ¼ cup sugar
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup milk (scant)
- 1 cup berries
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients; work in butter with tips of fingers; add milk and berries.
