## Ingredients

- two quarts of the richest cream
- one pound of pulverized sugar
- four whole eggs
- a tablespoonful of extract of vanilla
- one pound of preserved fruits (peaches, apricots, gages, cherries, pineapples)

## Directions

1. Take two quarts of the richest cream and add to it one pound of pulverized sugar and four whole eggs
2. Mix well together
3. Place on the fire, stirring constantly, and just bring to boiling point
4. Remove immediately and continue to stir until nearly cold
5. Flavor with a tablespoonful of extract of vanilla
6. Place in freezer and, when half frozen, mix thoroughly into it one pound of preserved fruits (cut up into small pieces) in equal parts of peaches, apricots, gages, cherries, pineapples
