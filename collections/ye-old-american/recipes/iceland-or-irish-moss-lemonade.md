## Ingredients

- 1 handful Irish or Iceland moss, washed in five waters
- 2 quarts boiling water
- 2 lemons, peeled and sliced
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water upon the moss, and left until cold.
2. Add peeled and sliced lemons.
3. Sweeten very well and ice.
4. Do not strain, and if it thicken too much, add cold water.
