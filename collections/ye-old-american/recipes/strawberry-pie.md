## Ingredients

- Berries
- Sugar
- Pastry crust

## Directions

1. Cap and pick over the berries, arrange in layers, besprinkle with a good coating of sugar, in a shell of pastry.
2. Fill it very full, as strawberries shrink very much in cooking.
3. Cover with crust and bake.
