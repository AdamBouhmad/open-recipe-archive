## Ingredients

- Recipe for Norwegian Prune Pudding
- whites of two eggs
- one-half cup walnut meats

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for Norwegian Prune Pudding, then add whites of two eggs beaten stiff and one-half cup walnut meats broken in pieces.
