## Ingredients

- snipes, woodcocks, or plovers
- pepper
- salt
- bread
- butter
- flour
- lard or fresh butter
- orange
- orange jelly
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Pick them immediately; wipe them, and season them slightly with pepper and salt.
2. Cut as many slices of bread as you have birds.
3. Toast them brown, butter them, and lay them in the dripping-pan.
4. Dredge the birds with flour, and put them on a small spit before a clear brisk fire.
5. Baste them with lard, or fresh butter.
6. They will be done in twenty or thirty minutes.
7. Serve them up laid on the toast, and garnished with sliced orange, or with orange jelly.
8. Have brown gravy in a boat.
