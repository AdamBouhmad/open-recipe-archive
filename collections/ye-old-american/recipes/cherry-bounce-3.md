## Ingredients

- one gallon of wild cherries
- good whisky - enough to cover the fruit
- a gill of water
- a pound of white sugar

## Directions

1. Add enough good whisky to the cherries to cover the fruit
2. Let soak two or three weeks and then drain off the liquor
3. Mash the cherries without breaking the stones and strain through a jelly-bag
4. Add this liquor to that already drained off
5. Make a syrup with a gill of water and a pound of white sugar to every two of liquor thus prepared
6. Stir in well and bottle
7. Tightly cork
