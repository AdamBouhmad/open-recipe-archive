## Ingredients

- curled parsley
- salt and water
- cider vinegar
- alum
- mace
- nutmeg
- horseradish

## Directions

1. Make a brine of salt and water strong enough to bear an egg
2. Throw into it a large quantity of curled parsley tied up in little bunches with a thread
3. After it has stood three days, stirring it frequently, take it out and drain it well
4. Lay it for three days in cold spring or pump-water, changing the water daily
5. Then scald it in hard water, and hang it, well covered, over a slow fire till it becomes green
6. Take it out, and drain and press it till quite dry
7. Boil for five minutes a quart of cider vinegar with a small bit of alum, a few blades of mace, a sliced nutmeg, and a few slips of horseradish
8. Pour it on the parsley, and put it away in a stone jar
