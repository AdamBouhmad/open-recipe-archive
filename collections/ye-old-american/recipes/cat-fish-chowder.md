## Ingredients

- cat-fish
- fat pork
- onions
- shallots
- sweet herbs
- pepper
- milk
- flour
- eggs
- Boston crackers
- butter crackers
- lemon
- cucumber pickles

## Directions

1. Skin, clean, and cut off the heads.
2. Cut the fish into pieces two inches long, and put into a pot with some fat pork cut into shreds—a pound to a dozen medium-sized fish, two chopped onions, or half a dozen shallots, a bunch of sweet herbs, and pepper.
3. The pork will salt it sufficiently. Stew slowly for three-quarters of an hour.
4. Then stir in a cup of milk, thickened with a tablespoonful of flour; take up a cupful of the hot liquor, and stir, a little at a time, into two well-beaten eggs.
5. Return this to the pot, throw in half a dozen Boston or butter crackers, split in half; let all boil up once, and turn into a tureen.
6. Pass sliced lemon or cucumber pickles, also sliced, with it.
7. Take out the backbones of the fish before serving.
