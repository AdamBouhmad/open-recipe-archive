## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup Irish moss
- 4 cups milk
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1½ teaspoons vanilla
- 1½ squares Baker's chocolate
- ¼ cup sugar
- ⅓ cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Make Irish Moss Blanc-Mange base. Melt one and one-half squares Baker's chocolate, add one-fourth cup sugar and one-third cup boiling water, stir until perfectly smooth, adding to milk just before taking from fire. Serve with sugar and cream.
