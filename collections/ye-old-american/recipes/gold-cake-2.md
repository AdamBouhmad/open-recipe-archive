## Ingredients

- one cup and a half of butter
- two cups of white sugar
- yolks of one dozen eggs
- four cupfuls of sifted flour
- one teaspoonful of baking powder
- lemon flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat to a cream one cup and a half of butter and two cups of white sugar.
2. Stir in the well-whipped yolks of one dozen eggs.
3. Add four cupfuls of sifted flour and one teaspoonful of baking powder.
4. Flavor with lemon.
5. Line the bake-pans with buttered paper and bake in a moderate oven for one hour.
