## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- yolks of twelve eggs
- one grated nutmeg
- one glass of wine
- one glass of rose-water
- one pound of sifted flour
- whites of twelve eggs

## Directions

1. Beat to a cream one pound of butter with one pound of sugar, after mixing well with the beaten yolks of twelve eggs, one grated nutmeg, one glass of wine, one glass of rose-water.
2. Stir in one pound of sifted flour and the well-beaten whites of the eggs.
3. Bake a nice light brown.
