## Ingredients

- a pint of cream
- half a cupful of sugar
- nutmeg or vanilla extract
- the whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Heat a pint of cream slowly in a double boiler
2. When nearly boiling, set it off from the fire
3. Put into it half a cupful of sugar, a little nutmeg or vanilla extract
4. Stir it thoroughly and add, when cool, the whites of two well-beaten eggs
5. Set it on the fire in a dish containing hot water to keep it warm until needed, stirring once or more
