## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Dress for broiling, following directions on page 244.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and place in a well-greased broiler.
3. Broil twenty minutes over a clear fire, watching carefully and turning broiler so that all parts may be equally browned.
4. The flesh side must be exposed to the fire the greater part of time, as the skin side will brown quickly.
5. Remove to a hot platter, spread with soft butter, and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
6. Alternatively, place chicken in dripping-pan, skin side down, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dot over with butter, and bake fifteen minutes in hot oven; then broil to finish cooking.
