## Ingredients

- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- a pint of corn meal
- two eggs
- some new milk
- a spoonful of yeast

## Directions

1. Rub a piece of butter the size of an egg, into a pint of corn meal
2. Make it a batter with two eggs, and some new milk
3. Add a spoonful of yeast
4. Set it by the fire an hour to rise
5. Butter little pans, and bake it
