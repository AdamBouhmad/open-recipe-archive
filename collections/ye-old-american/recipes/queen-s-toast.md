## Ingredients

- Slices of stale baker's bread—aërated, if you can get it
- Boiling lard
- Powdered sugar
- Sweet wine sauce

## Directions

1. Before toasting, cut the slices with a round cake-cutter, taking off all the crust.
2. Fry slices of stale baker's bread in boiling lard to a fine brown.
3. Dip each slice quickly in boiling water to remove the grease.
4. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and pile upon a hot plate.
5. Pour sweet wine sauce over them when hot, and serve at once.
