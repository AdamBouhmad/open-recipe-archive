## Ingredients

- white silver-skin onions
- salt
- melted butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel off the outside, cut off the ends
2. Put them into cold water and into a stewpan and let them scald two minutes
3. Turn off that water, pour on cold water salted a little
4. Boil slowly till tender, which will be in thirty or forty minutes, according to their size
5. When done drain them quite dry
6. Pour a little melted butter over them
7. Sprinkle them with pepper and salt and serve hot
