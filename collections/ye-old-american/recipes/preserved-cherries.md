## Ingredients

- cherries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Stone the cherries, preserving every drop of juice.
2. Weigh the fruit, allowing pound for pound of sugar.
3. Put a layer of fruit for one of sugar until all is used up; pour over the juice and boil gently until the syrup begins to thicken.
