## Ingredients

- three pounds lamb from the fore-quarter
- boiling water
- one-half cup carrot
- one-half cup turnip
- one onion
- four cups potatoes
- one-fourth cup flour
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- Dumplings

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut lamb in pieces
2. Put in kettle and cover with boiling water
3. Cook slowly two hours or until tender
4. After cooking one hour add carrot and turnip cut in one-half inch cubes and onion cut in slices
5. Fifteen minutes before serving add potatoes cut in one-fourth inch slices that have been previously parboiled five minutes in boiling water
6. Thicken with flour diluted with enough cold water to form a thin smooth paste
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Serve with Dumplings
