## Ingredients

- medium-sized grape fruits
- finely cut celery
- Mayonnaise Dressing
- celery tips
- canned pimentoes

## Directions

1. Cut medium-sized grape fruits in thirds lengthwise.
2. Remove the pulp, and add to it an equal quantity of finely cut celery.
3. Refill sections with mixture, mask with Mayonnaise Dressing, and garnish with celery tips or curled celery and canned pimentoes cut in strips.
