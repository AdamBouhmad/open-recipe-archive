## Ingredients

- wild ducks
- small peeled carrot
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- claret
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Before roasting, parboil ducks with a small peeled carrot put within each duck to absorb the unpleasant fish flavor
2. Alternatively, put into the duck a whole peeled onion, plenty of salt and pepper and a glass of claret
3. Bake in a hot oven twenty minutes
4. Serve hot with the gravy it yields in cooking and a dish of currant jelly
