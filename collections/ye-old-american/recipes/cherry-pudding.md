## Ingredients

- six eggs
- half a pint of milk
- six ounces flour
- eight ounces grated bread
- twelve ounces suet
- salt
- eighteen ounces preserved cherries or damsins
- melted butter
- sugar
- wine

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs very light
2. Add half a pint of milk, six ounces flour, eight ounces grated bread, twelve ounces suet, chopped fine, a little salt
3. When it is beat well, mix in eighteen ounces preserved cherries or damsins
4. Bake or boil it
5. Make a sauce of melted butter, sugar and wine
