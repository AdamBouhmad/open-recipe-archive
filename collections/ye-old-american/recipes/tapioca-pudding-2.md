## Ingredients

- five tablespoonfuls of tapioca
- one quart of milk
- two ounces of butter
- a cupful of sugar
- four eggs
- vanilla or bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Wash the tapioca and let it stew gently in the milk on the back part of the stove for a quarter of an hour, occasionally stirring it
2. Let it cool, mix with it the butter, sugar and eggs, which should be well-beaten, and flavor with either vanilla or bitter almonds
3. Butter a dish, put in the pudding and bake in a moderate oven for an hour
