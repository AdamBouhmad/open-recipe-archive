## Ingredients

- 1 quart of boiling water
- 2 ozs. of cocoa nibs
- 1 quart fresh milk

## Directions

1. Wet the shells or nibs up with a little cold water.
2. Add to the boiling, and cook one hour and a half.
3. Strain.
4. Put in the milk and let it heat almost to boiling.
5. Take from the fire.
