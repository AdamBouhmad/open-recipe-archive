## Ingredients

- 1 cup tapioca
- 1 quart milk
- 5 eggs—whites and yolks beaten separately
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter, melted
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the tapioca, in enough cold water to cover it, two hours; drain off the water, if it be not all absorbed
2. Soak two hours longer in the milk, which should be slightly warmed
3. When the tapioca is quite soft, beat the sugar and butter together
4. Add the yolks, the milk and tapioca, lastly the whites
5. Stir very well, and bake in a buttered dish
6. Eat warm with sweet sauce
