## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound flour
- salt
- 1 pint milk
- 3 eggs
- butter
- 3/4 pound apples
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put flour in basin and sprinkle in salt
2. Stir in milk gradually; when smooth add 3 eggs
3. Butter pie-dish and pour in batter
4. Seed and slice apples and put in batter
5. Place bits of butter over top
6. Bake three-quarters of an hour
7. Sprinkle sugar over top and serve hot
