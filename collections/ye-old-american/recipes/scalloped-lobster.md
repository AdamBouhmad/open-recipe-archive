## Ingredients

- butter
- bread crumbs
- lobster
- pepper
- salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Butter a deep dish and cover the bottom with fine bread crumbs
2. Put on this a layer of chopped lobster, with pepper and salt
3. Alternate layers of lobster and crumbs until the dish is filled, having crumbs on top
4. Put on bits of butter and moisten with milk
5. Bake about twenty minutes
