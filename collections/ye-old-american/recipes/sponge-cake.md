## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- a pound of the best loaf-sugar
- three lemons
- twelve eggs
- loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Sift half a pound of flour, and powder a pound of the best loaf-sugar
2. Grate the yellow rind and squeeze into a saucer the juice of three lemons
3. Beat twelve eggs; and when they are as light as possible, beat into them gradually and very hard the sugar, adding the lemon, and beating the whole for a long time
4. By degrees, stir in the flour slowly and lightly; for if the flour is stirred hard and fast into sponge cake, it will make it porous and tough
5. Have ready buttered, a sufficient number of little square tins, (the thinner they are the better,) half fill them with the mixture
6. Grate loaf-sugar over the top of each; put them immediately into a quick oven, and bake them about ten minutes; taking out one to try when you think they are done
7. Spread them on an inverted sieve to cool
