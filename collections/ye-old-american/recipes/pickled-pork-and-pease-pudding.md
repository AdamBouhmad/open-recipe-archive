## Ingredients

- pickled pork
- cold water
- greens or cabbage
- parsnips
- potatoes
- dried split pease
- pepper
- salt
- ounce of butter
- two beaten eggs
- cloth
- flour

## Directions

1. Soak the pork all night in cold water, and wash and scrape it clean.
2. Put it on early in the day, as it will take a long time to boil, and must boil slowly.
3. Skim it frequently.
4. Boil in a separate pot greens or cabbage to eat with it; also parsnips and potatoes.
5. To make a small pudding, have ready a quart of dried split pease, which have been soaked all night in cold water.
6. Tie them in a cloth, (leaving room for them to swell,) and boil them slowly till they are tender.
7. Drain them, and rub them through a cullender or a sieve into a deep dish.
8. Season them with pepper and salt, and mix with them an ounce of butter, and two beaten eggs.
9. Beat all well together till thoroughly mixed.
10. Dip a clean cloth in hot water, sprinkle it with flour, and put the pudding into it.
11. Tie it up very tightly, leaving a small space between the mixture and the tying, (as the pudding will still swell a little,).
12. Boil it an hour longer.
13. Send it to table and eat it with the pork.
