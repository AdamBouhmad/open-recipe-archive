## Ingredients

- lobster
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- butter
- egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Take any lobster remaining from table and pound it until the dark, light meat and coral are well mixed
2. Put with it not quite as much fine bread crumbs
3. Season with pepper, salt and a very little cayenne pepper
4. Add a little melted butter, about two tablespoonfuls if the bread is rather dry
5. Form into egg-shaped or round balls
6. Roll them in egg, then in fine crumbs
7. Fry in boiling lard
