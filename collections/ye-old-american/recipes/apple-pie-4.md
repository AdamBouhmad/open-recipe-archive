## Ingredients

- crust
- ripe apples
- powdered sugar
- tea-spoonsful of rose water
- cloves

## Directions

1. Put a crust in the bottom of a dish
2. Put on it a layer of ripe apples, pared and sliced thin
3. Then a layer of powdered sugar
4. Do this alternately till the dish is full
5. Put in a few tea-spoonsful of rose water and some cloves
6. Put on a crust and bake it
