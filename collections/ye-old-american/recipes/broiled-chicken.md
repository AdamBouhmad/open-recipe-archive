## Ingredients

- chicken
- boiling water
- butter

## Directions

1. Lay the chicken upon sticks extending from side to side of a dripping-pan full of boiling water
2. Set this in the oven, invert a tin pan over the chicken, and let it steam for half an hour
3. Split the chicken down the back and wipe perfectly dry before steaming
4. Transfer from the vapor-bath to a buttered gridiron, inside downward
5. Cover with a tin pan or common plate, and broil until tender and brown, turning several times
6. Broil for from half to three-quarters of an hour
7. Put into a hot chafing-dish, and butter very well
8. Send to table smoking hot
