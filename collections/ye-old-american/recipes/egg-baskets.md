## Ingredients

- six eggs
- cold fowl (roast chicken, duck, or turkey)
- melted butter
- pepper
- salt
- gravy
- cream or rich milk

## Directions

1. Boil six eggs hard, cut neatly in half and extract the yolks.
2. Rub these to a paste with some melted butter, pepper, and salt, and set aside.
3. Pound the minced meat of the cold fowl fine in the same manner, and mix with the egg-paste, moistening with melted butter as you proceed, or with a little gravy, if you have it to spare.
4. Cut off a slice from the bottoms of the hollowed whites of the egg, to make them stand.
5. Fill with the paste.
6. Arrange close together upon a flat dish, and pour over them the gravy left from yesterday's roast, heated boiling hot, and mellowed by a few spoonfuls of cream or rich milk.
