## Ingredients

- lobster meat, finely chopped
- hard-boiled eggs
- melted butter
- German mustard
- beef extract
- boiling water
- salt
- thin slices of buttered bread

## Directions

1. Mix an equal quantity of finely chopped lobster meat and the yolks of hard-boiled eggs forced through a sieve.
2. Moisten with melted butter, and season with German mustard, beef extract diluted with a very small quantity of boiling water, and salt.
3. Spread mixture between thin slices of buttered bread, remove crusts, and cut into fancy shapes.
