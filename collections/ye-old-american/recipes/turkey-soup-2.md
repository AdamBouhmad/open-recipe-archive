## Ingredients

- turkey carcass
- cold water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Break turkey carcass in pieces, removing all stuffing.
2. Put in kettle with any bits of meat that may have been left over.
3. Cover with cold water, bring slowly to boiling-point, and simmer two hours.
4. Strain and remove fat.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. One or two outer stalks of celery may be cooked with carcass to give additional flavor.
