## Ingredients

- Mayonnaise Dressing I or II
- ⅓ cup thick cream

## Directions

1. To Mayonnaise Dressing I or II add one-third cup thick cream, beaten until stiff.
2. This recipe should be used only when dressing is to be eaten the day it is made.
