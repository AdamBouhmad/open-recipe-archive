## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold boiled rice
- ½ cup white flour, and same of corn-meal
- 2 eggs well whipped, and milk to make soft batter
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter
- ½ teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water
- 1 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Beat the mixture smooth before baking
2. Be especially careful in greasing your irons for these waffles, as for all which contain rice
