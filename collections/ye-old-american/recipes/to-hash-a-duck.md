## Ingredients

- duck
- pepper
- mixed spices
- cold ham or bacon
- water
- large glass of red wine
- quart or more of green peas
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut up the duck and season it with pepper and mixed spices.
2. Have ready some thin slices of cold ham or bacon.
3. Place a layer of them in a stew-pan; then put in the duck and cover it with ham.
4. Add just water enough to moisten it, and pour over all a large glass of red wine.
5. Cover the pan closely and let it stew for an hour.
6. Have ready a quart or more of green peas, boiled tender, drained, and mixed with butter and pepper.
7. Lay them round the hashed duck.
