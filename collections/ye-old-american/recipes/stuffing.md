## Ingredients

- 1 cup hot riced potatoes
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- ¼ teaspoon marjoram or summer savory
- few drops onion juice
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ¼ cup soft stale bread crumbs soaked in some of the celery water and wrung in cheese-cloth
- yolk 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given.
