## Ingredients

- 2 squares Baker's chocolate
- ½ cup powdered sugar
- 1 cup cream
- ¾ tablespoon granulated gelatine
- 3 tablespoons boiling water
- ¾ cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 quart cream

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate, add powdered sugar, and gradually one cup cream
2. Stir over fire until boiling-point is reached, then add gelatine dissolved in boiling water, sugar, and vanilla
3. Strain mixture into a bowl, set in a pan of ice-water, stir constantly until mixture thickens
4. Fold in the whip from remaining cream
5. Mould, pack in salt and ice, and let stand four hours
