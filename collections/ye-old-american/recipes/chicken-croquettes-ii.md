## Ingredients

- A four-pound fowl
- Six cups boiling water
- Seven slices carrot
- Two slices turnip
- One small onion
- One stalk celery
- One bay leaf
- Three sprigs thyme
- One-fourth cup butter
- One-half cup flour
- One cup chicken stock
- One-third cup cream
- Salt
- Cayenne
- Nutmeg
- One beaten egg

## Directions

1. Clean and dress the fowl.
2. Put into a kettle with boiling water, carrot, turnip, onion, celery, bay leaf, and thyme.
3. Cook slowly until fowl is tender.
4. Remove fowl; strain liquor, cool, and skim off fat.
5. Make a thick sauce, using butter, flour, chicken stock, and cream.
6. Remove meat from chicken, chop, and moisten with sauce.
7. Season with salt, cayenne, and slight grating of nutmeg; then add beaten egg.
8. Cool, shape, crumb, and fry same as other croquettes.
9. Arrange around a mound of green peas, and serve with Cream Sauce or Wine Jelly.
