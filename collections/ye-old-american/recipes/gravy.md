## Ingredients

- a rasher or two of bacon
- veal, mutton, or beef
- sliced onions
- turnips
- carrots
- celery
- thyme
- alspice
- water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Lay bacon at the bottom of a stew pan
2. Put veal, mutton, or beef, cut in slices, over it
3. Add some sliced onions, turnips, carrots, celery, a little thyme, and alspice
4. Put in a little water and set it on the fire, stewing till it be brown at the bottom, which you will know from the pan's hissing
5. Pour boiling water over it and stew it an hour and a half
6. Season it with salt and pepper
