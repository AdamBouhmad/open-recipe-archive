## Ingredients

- A cold boiled lobster
- 4 eggs, boiled hard
- 2 tablespoonfuls salad oil
- 1 teaspoonful made mustard
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 2 teaspoonfuls white sugar
- ½ teaspoonful cayenne pepper
- Vinegar at discretion
- 1 teaspoonful of Harvey's, Worcestershire, or anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Pick out every bit of the meat from the body and claws of a cold boiled lobster
2. Lay aside the coral for the dressing, and mince the rest
3. Rub the yolks of boiled eggs to a smooth paste in a mortar or bowl, with a pestle, a silver or wooden spoon, until perfectly free from lumps
4. Add gradually, rubbing all the while, the salad oil, mustard, salt, and sugar
5. Add the coral last, which should have been worked well upon a plate with a silver knife or wooden spatula
6. Proceed slowly and carefully in the work of amalgamating the various ingredients, moistening with vinegar as they stiffen
7. Increase the quantity of vinegar as the mixture grows smooth, until it is thin enough to pour over the minced lobster
8. Toss with a silver fork and do not break the meat
9. Heap a glass dish with the inner leaves of several lettuce-heads, laying pounded ice among them, and pass with the lobster
