## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- salt and water
- fat salt pork
- frying-pan
- onion
- hot water
- pepper
- lemon juice
- brown flour
- cullender

## Directions

1. Slice the liver smoothly and lay in salt and water to draw out the blood
2. Wipe dry
3. Lard each slice with slices of fat salt pork drawn through at regular distances and projecting slightly on each side
4. Lay in a clean frying-pan and fry brown
5. When done, take out the slices and arrange neatly on a hot dish
6. Set aside to keep warm
7. Add to the gravy in the frying-pan a chopped onion, a half-cup of hot water, pepper, and the juice of a lemon
8. Thicken with brown flour
9. Boil up well
10. Run through a cullender to remove the onion and bits of crisped pork
11. Pour over the liver and serve hot
