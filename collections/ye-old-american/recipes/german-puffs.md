## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 3 cups milk
- 3 eggs—whites and yolks beaten separately and very light
- 3 teaspoonfuls melted butter
- 1 saltspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Pour in nine well-buttered cups of same size as that used for measuring, and bake to a fine brown.
2. Eat as soon as done, with sauce.
