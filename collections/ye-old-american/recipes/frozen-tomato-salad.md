## Ingredients

- one quart can tomatoes
- sugar
- salt
- cayenne
- lettuce leaves
- Mayonnaise Dressing

## Directions

1. Open one quart can tomatoes, turn from can, and let stand one hour that they may be reoxygenated
2. Add three tablespoons sugar, and season highly with salt and cayenne
3. Rub through a sieve
4. Turn into one-half pound breakfast-cocoa boxes, cover tightly, pack in salt and ice, using equal parts, and let stand three hours
5. Remove from mould, arrange on lettuce leaves, and serve with Mayonnaise Dressing
