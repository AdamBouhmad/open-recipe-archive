## Ingredients

- rich thick melted or drawn butter
- two large glasses of white wine
- two table-spoonfuls of powdered white sugar
- a powdered nutmeg

## Directions

1. Have ready some rich thick melted or drawn butter, and the moment you take it from the fire, stir in two large glasses of white wine, two table-spoonfuls of powdered white sugar, and a powdered nutmeg.
2. Serve it up with plum pudding, or any sort of boiled pudding that is made of a batter.
