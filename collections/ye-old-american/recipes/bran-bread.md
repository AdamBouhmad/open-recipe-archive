## Ingredients

- three quarts of unbolted wheat meal
- a jill of strong yeast
- a jill of molasses
- a quart of soft water
- a small tea-spoonful of pearl-ash or sal-aratus

## Directions

1. Sift the meal into a pan
2. Stir the yeast and molasses into the soft water, which must be warm but not hot
3. Add the pearl-ash or sal-aratus
4. Make a hole in the heap of flour, pour in the liquid, and proceed in the usual manner of making bread
5. This quantity may be made into two loaves
