## Ingredients

- chicken
- stuffing
- flour
- boiling water
- egg sauce or bread or oyster sauce

## Directions

1. Clean, wash and stuff as for roasting
2. Baste a floured cloth around each and put into a pot with enough boiling water to cover them well
3. The hot water cooks the skin at once and prevents the escape of the juice
4. Stew very slowly for the first half hour especially
5. Boil an hour or more, guiding yourself by size and toughness
6. Serve with egg, bread or oyster sauce
