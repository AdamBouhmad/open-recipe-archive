## Ingredients

- one pound sugar
- half pint water
- two tea spoons pearl ash dissolved in milk
- two and half pounds flour
- 4 ounces butter
- two large spoons of finely powdered coriander seed
- melted butter
- wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. One pound sugar boiled slowly in half pint water, scum well and cool, add two tea spoons pearl ash dissolved in milk, then two and half pounds flour, rub in 4 ounces butter, and two large spoons of finely powdered coriander seed, wet with above; make roles half an inch thick and cut to the shape you please; bake fifteen or twenty minutes in a slack oven—good three weeks
