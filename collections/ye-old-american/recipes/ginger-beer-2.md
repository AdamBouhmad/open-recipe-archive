## Ingredients

- 1 pound and a half of loaf-sugar
- 3 ounces of strong white ginger
- grated peel of 2 lemons
- 2 gallons of boiling water
- juice of the lemons
- 2 large table-spoonfuls of strong yeast

## Directions

1. Break up a pound and a half of loaf-sugar, and mix with it three ounces of strong white ginger, and the grated peel of two lemons
2. Put these ingredients into a large stone jar, and pour over them two gallons of boiling water
3. When it becomes milkwarm strain it, and add the juice of the lemons and two large table-spoonfuls of strong yeast
4. Make this beer in the evening and let it stand all night
5. Next morning bottle it in little half pint stone bottles, tying down the corks with twine
