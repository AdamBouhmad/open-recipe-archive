## Ingredients

- squash
- boiling water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut in pieces, remove seeds and stringy portion, and pare.
2. Place in a strainer and cook thirty minutes, or until soft, over boiling water.
3. Mash, and season with butter, salt, and pepper.
4. If lacking in sweetness, add a small quantity of sugar.
