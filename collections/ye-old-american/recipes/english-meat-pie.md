## Ingredients

- Knuckle of veal
- One slice onion
- One slice carrot
- Bit of bay leaf
- Sprig of parsley
- Twelve peppercorns
- Blade of mace
- Two teaspoons salt
- One-half pound lean raw ham
- Four tablespoons flour
- Four tablespoons butter
- Two dozen bearded oysters
- Puff paste

## Directions

1. Remove meat from bones.
2. Cover bones with cold water, add vegetables and seasonings, and heat slowly to boiling-point.
3. Add meat, boil five minutes, and let simmer until meat is tender; remove meat and reduce stock to two cups.
4. Put ham in frying-pan, cover with lukewarm water, and let stand on back of range one hour.
5. Brown butter, add flour, and when well browned add stock; then add veal and ham each cut into cubes.
6. Let simmer twenty minutes and add oysters.
7. Put in serving dish and cover with top made of puff paste.
