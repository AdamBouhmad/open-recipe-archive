## Ingredients

- bread crumbs
- cold roast mutton
- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Over the bottom of an earthen baking-dish place a layer of bread crumbs
2. Over it place alternate layers of cold roast mutton cut in thin slices, and tomatoes peeled and sliced
3. Season each with salt, pepper and bits of butter, as laid in
4. Make the top layer of tomatoes, spread over with bread crumbs
5. Bake three-quarters of an hour, and serve immediately
