## Ingredients

- ½ small loaf baker's entire wheat bread
- 3½ cups milk
- ½ cup molasses
- Butter

## Directions

1. Remove crusts from bread and cut into five slices of uniform thickness
2. Spread generously with butter
3. Arrange in baking-dish, pour over three cups of milk and molasses
4. Bake from two to three hours in a very slow oven
5. Stir three times during the first hour of baking, then add remaining milk
6. Serve with cream or vanilla ice cream
