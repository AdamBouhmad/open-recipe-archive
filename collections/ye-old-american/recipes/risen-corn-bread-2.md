## Ingredients

- corn-meal
- boiling water
- a little salt
- a tablespoonful butter

## Directions

1. Mix a tolerably stiff dough of corn-meal and boiling water, a little salt, and a tablespoonful butter
2. Let it stand four or five hours until light
3. Make into small loaves
4. Bake rather quickly
