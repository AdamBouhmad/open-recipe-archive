## Ingredients

- one third of one pound of butter
- one pound of lard
- two pound of flour
- four whites well beaten
- water q: s:

## Directions

1. Rub one third of one pound of butter, and one pound of lard into two pound of flour, wet with four whites well beaten; water q: s: to make a paste, roll in the residue of shortening in ten or twelve rollings--bake quick.
