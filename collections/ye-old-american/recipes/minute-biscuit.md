## Ingredients

- 1 pint sour, or buttermilk
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 2 teaspoonfuls melted butter
- Flour to make soft dough—just stiff enough to handle

## Directions

1. Mix, roll, and cut out rapidly, with as little handling as may be
2. Bake in a quick oven
