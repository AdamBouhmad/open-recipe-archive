## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup tea infusion
- 1 quart Apollinaris
- 2 cups strawberry syrup
- Juice 5 lemons
- Juice 5 oranges
- 1 can grated pineapple
- 1 cup Maraschino cherries

## Directions

1. Make syrup by boiling water and sugar ten minutes.
2. Add tea, strawberry syrup, lemon juice, orange juice, and pineapple; let stand thirty minutes, strain, and add ice-water to make one and one-half gallons of liquid.
3. Add cherries and Apollinaris.
4. Serve in punch-bowl, with large piece of ice.
5. This quantity will serve fifty.
