## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion (optional)
- flour
- hot milk (a quart)

## Directions

1. Peel and slice raw potatoes thin, the same as for frying.
2. Butter an earthen dish, put in a layer of potatoes, and season with salt, pepper, butter, a bit of onion chopped fine, if liked; sprinkle a little flour.
3. Put another layer of potatoes and the seasoning.
4. Continue in this way till the dish is filled.
5. Just before putting into the oven, pour a quart of hot milk over.
6. Bake three-quarters of an hour.
