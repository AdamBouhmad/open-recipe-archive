## Ingredients

- a peck of quartered cabbage
- salt
- four chopped onions
- vinegar
- one ounce of turmeric
- one gill of black pepper
- one gill of celery seed
- a few cloves
- one tablespoonful of allspice
- a few pieces of ginger
- half an ounce of mace
- two pounds of brown sugar
- four tablespoonfuls of made mustard

## Directions

1. Take a peck of quartered cabbage, put a layer of cabbage and one of salt, let it remain over night
2. in the morning squeeze them and put them on the fire, with four chopped onions covered with vinegar
3. boil for half an hour
4. then add one ounce of turmeric, one gill of black pepper, one gill of celery seed, a few cloves, one tablespoonful of allspice, a few pieces of ginger, half an ounce of mace, and two pounds of brown sugar
5. Let it boil half an hour longer
6. when cold it is fit for use
7. Four tablespoonfuls of made mustard should be added with the other ingredients
