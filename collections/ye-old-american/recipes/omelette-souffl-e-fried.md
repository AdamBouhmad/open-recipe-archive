## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls sugar (powdered)
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks separately
2. Add the sugar to the yolks, a little at a time, beating very thoroughly, until they are smooth and thick
3. The whites should stand alone
4. Put two tablespoonfuls of butter in a frying-pan, heat to boiling
5. Add the vanilla to the omelette and pour it in
6. Cook very quickly, as you would a plain one
7. Slip the knife frequently under it, to loosen from the sides and bottom
8. Turn out upon a very hot dish
9. Sift powdered sugar over the top
10. Serve instantly, or it will fall and become heavy
