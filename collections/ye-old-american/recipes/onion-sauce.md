## Ingredients

- 4 white onions
- 1 teacupful hot milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, boil tender, press the water from them, and mince fine.
2. Have ready the hot milk in a saucepan; stir in the onions, then the butter, salt, and pepper.
3. Boil up once.
