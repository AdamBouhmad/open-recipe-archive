## Ingredients

- one peck of green tomatoes
- six fresh lemons
- six pounds of sugar
- ginger root

## Directions

1. Take one peck of green tomatoes
2. Slice six fresh lemons without removing the skins, but taking out the seeds
3. Put to this quantity six pounds of sugar, common white, and boil until transparent and the syrup thick
4. Ginger root may be added, if liked
