## Ingredients

- eels
- egg yolk
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- sage
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Skin and cleanse your eels
2. Rub them with the yelk of an egg
3. Strew over them bread crumbs, chopped parsley, sage, pepper, and salt
4. Baste them well with butter, and set them in a dripping pan
5. Serve them up with parsley and butter for sauce
