## Ingredients

- peaches
- a quarter of a pound of sugar to one of fruit
- butter

## Directions

1. Stew as many peaches as you choose, allowing a quarter of a pound of sugar to one of fruit
2. Mash it up smooth as it cooks
3. When it is dry enough to spread in a thin sheet on a board greased with butter, set it out in the sun to dry
4. When dry it can be rolled up like leather, wrapped up in a cloth, and will keep perfectly from season to season
