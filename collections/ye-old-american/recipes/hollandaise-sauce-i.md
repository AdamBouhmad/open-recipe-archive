## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- Yolks 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- ⅓ cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Put butter in a bowl, cover with cold water, and wash, using a spoon
2. Divide in three pieces; put one piece in a saucepan with yolks of eggs and lemon juice
3. Place saucepan in a larger one containing boiling water, and stir constantly with a wire whisk until butter is melted
4. Add second piece of butter, and, as it thickens, third piece
5. Add water, cook one minute, and season with salt and cayenne
