## Ingredients

- fresh young spearmint leaves
- white sugar
- cider or white-wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Strip fresh young spearmint leaves from the stems
2. Wash and drain them, or dry on a cloth
3. Chop very fine and put in a gravy boat
4. To three tablespoonfuls of mint put two of white sugar and mix
5. Let it stand a few minutes, then pour over it six tablespoonfuls of good cider or white-wine vinegar
6. Make the sauce some time before it is to be used, so that the flavor of the mint may be well extracted
