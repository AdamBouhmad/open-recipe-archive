## Ingredients

- white fish
- water
- salt
- gravy
- butter
- pepper
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish as for broiling, laying it open
2. Put it into a dripping pan with the back down
3. Nearly cover with water; to one fish two tablespoonfuls of salt
4. Cover tightly and simmer (not boil) one-half hour
5. Dress with gravy, a little butter and pepper
6. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs
