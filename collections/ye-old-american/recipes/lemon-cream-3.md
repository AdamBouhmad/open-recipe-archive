## Ingredients

- four fresh lemons
- a quart of water
- sugar
- whites of six eggs
- yelks of the eggs

## Directions

1. Pare the rind very thin from four fresh lemons, squeeze the juice, and strain it
2. Put them both into a quart of water, sweeten it to your taste
3. Add the whites of six eggs, beat to a froth
4. Set it over the fire, and keep stirring until it thickens, but do not let it boil
5. Pour it in a bowl
6. When cold, strain it through a sieve
7. Put it on the fire, and add the yelks of the eggs
8. Stir it till quite thick, and serve it in glasses
