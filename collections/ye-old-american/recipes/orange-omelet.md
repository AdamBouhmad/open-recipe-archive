## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- Few grains salt
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- 2 oranges
- ½ tablespoon butter
- 2½ tablespoons orange juice

## Directions

1. Follow directions for Plain Omelet.
2. Remove skin from oranges and cut in slices, lengthwise.
3. Fold in one-third of the slices of orange, well sprinkled with powdered sugar.
4. Put remaining slices around omelet, and sprinkle with sugar.
