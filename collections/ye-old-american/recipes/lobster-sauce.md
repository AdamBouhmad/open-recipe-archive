## Ingredients

- mace
- whole pepper
- butter
- lobster

## Directions

1. Boil mace and whole pepper long enough to take out the strong taste of the spice
2. Strain it off and melt three quarters of a pound of butter in it
3. Cut the lobster in very small pieces and stew it till it is tender
