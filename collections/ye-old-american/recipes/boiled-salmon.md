## Ingredients

- middle slice of salmon
- hot salted water
- drawn butter
- parsley
- lemon
- eggs

## Directions

1. Sew up neatly in a mosquito-net bag
2. Boil a quarter of an hour to the pound in hot salted water
3. When done, unwrap with care, and lay upon a hot dish, taking care not to break it
4. Have ready a large cupful of drawn butter, very rich, in which has been stirred a tablespoonful of minced parsley and the juice of a lemon
5. Pour half upon the salmon and serve the rest in a boat
6. Garnish with parsley and sliced eggs
