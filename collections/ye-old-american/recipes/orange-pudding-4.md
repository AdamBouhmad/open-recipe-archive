## Ingredients

- two large smooth deep-coloured oranges
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of powdered white sugar
- a wine-glass of mixed wine and brandy
- six eggs
- puff-paste
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the yellow part of the rind, and squeeze the juice of the oranges.
2. Stir together to a cream half a pound of butter, and half a pound of powdered white sugar, and add a wine-glass of mixed wine and brandy.
3. Beat very light six eggs, and stir them gradually into the mixture.
4. Put it into a buttered dish with a broad edge, round which lay a border of puff-paste neatly notched.
5. Bake it half an hour, and when cool grate white sugar over it.
6. Send it to table quite cold.
