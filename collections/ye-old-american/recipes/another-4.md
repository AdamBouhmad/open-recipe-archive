## Ingredients

- 5 pound of flour
- 2 pound of butter
- 2 and a half pounds of loaf sugar
- 2 and a half pounds of raisins
- 15 eggs
- 1 pint of wine
- 1 pint of emptins
- 1 ounce of cinnamon
- 1 gill rose-water
- 1 gill of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix 5 pound of flour, 2 pound of butter, 2 and a half pounds of loaf sugar, 2 and a half pounds of raisins, 15 eggs, 1 pint of wine, 1 pint of emptins, 1 ounce of cinnamon, 1 gill rose-water, 1 gill of brandy—baked like No. 1
