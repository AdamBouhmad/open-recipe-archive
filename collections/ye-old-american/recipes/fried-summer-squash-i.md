## Ingredients

- squash
- salt
- pepper
- crumbs
- egg
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Wash, and cut in one-half inch slices.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again.
3. Fry in hot fat, and drain.
