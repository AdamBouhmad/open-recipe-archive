## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 quart California champagne
- 4 tablespoons brandy
- 2 tablespoons Medford rum
- 2 tablespoons Orange Curaçoa
- Juice 2 lemons
- 2 cups tea infusion
- Ice
- 1 quart soda water

## Directions

1. Make a syrup by boiling water and sugar ten minutes.
2. Mix champagne, brandy, rum, Curaçoa, lemon juice, and tea infusion.
3. Sweeten to taste with syrup and pour into punch-bowl over a large piece of ice.
4. Just before serving add soda water.
