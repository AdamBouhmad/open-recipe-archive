## Ingredients

- flank of beef
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon peppercorns
- bit of bay leaf
- bone

## Directions

1. Wipe, remove superfluous fat, and roll a flank of beef.
2. Put in a kettle, cover with boiling water, and add one tablespoon salt, one-half teaspoon peppercorns, a bit of bay leaf, and a bone or two which may be at hand.
3. Cook slowly until meat is in shreds; there should be but little liquor in kettle when meat is done.
4. Arrange meat in a deep pan, pour over liquor, cover, and press with a heavy weight.
5. Serve cold, thinly sliced.
