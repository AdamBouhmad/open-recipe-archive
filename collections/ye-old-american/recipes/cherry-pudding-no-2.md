## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of flour
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- a teaspoonful of salt
- milk or water
- butter
- stoned cherries
- half a cupful of sugar
- dry flour
- sweet sauce

## Directions

1. Make a crust or paste of two cupfuls of flour, two teaspoonfuls of baking powder, and a teaspoonful of salt
2. Wet up with milk or water
3. Roll out a quarter of an inch thick
4. Butter a large common bowl and line it with this paste, leaving it large enough to lap over the top
5. Fill it with stoned cherries and half a cupful of sugar
6. Gather the paste closely over the top, sprinkle a little with dry flour and cover the whole with a linen cloth, fastening it with a string
7. Put it into a pot of boiling water and cook for an hour and a half
8. Serve with sweet sauce
