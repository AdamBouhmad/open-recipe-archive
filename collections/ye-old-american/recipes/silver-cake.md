## Ingredients

- 1 lb. sugar
- ¾ lb. flour
- ½ lb. butter
- Whites of ten eggs, whipped very stiff
- 1 large teaspoonful essence bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Put next the whites of the eggs
3. Then add the flour
4. Lastly add the flavoring
5. Flavor the icing of silver cake with rose-water
