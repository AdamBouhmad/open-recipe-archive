## Ingredients

- green nasturtium seed
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- mace
- white pepper-corns
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Take the green seed after the flower has dried off.
2. Lay in salt and water two days, in cold water one day; pack in bottles and cover with scalding vinegar, seasoned with mace and white pepper-corns, and sweetened slightly with white sugar.
3. Cork, and set away four weeks before you use them.
