## Ingredients

- four ounces of white sugar
- one gallon of skimmed milk
- two ounces of baker's yeast or a cake of compressed yeast per quart bottle

## Directions

1. Dissolve four ounces of white sugar in one gallon of skimmed milk
2. Place in bottles of the capacity of one quart; add two ounces of baker's yeast or a cake of compressed yeast to each bottle
3. Cork and tie securely, set in a warm place until fermentation is well under way
4. Lay the bottles on their sides in a cool cellar
5. In three days, fermentation will have progressed sufficiently to permit the koumiss to be in good condition
