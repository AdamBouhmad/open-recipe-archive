## Ingredients

- cold boiled fowl or roast chicken
- celery
- Cream Dressing II
- capers
- small pickles

## Directions

1. Cut cold boiled fowl or remnants of roast chicken in one-half inch dice.
2. To two cups add one and one-half cups celery cut in small pieces, and moisten with Cream Dressing II.
3. Mound on a salad dish, cover with dressing, and garnish with capers, thin slices cut from small pickles, and curled celery.
