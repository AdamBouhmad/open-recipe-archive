## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- mace
- sweet marjoram
- cream
- unskimmed milk
- butter
- flour
- force-meat balls

## Directions

1. Skin them, cut them in pieces, and having soaked out the blood, season them with salt, pepper, nutmeg and mace, and strew over them some sweet marjoram shred fine.
2. Put them into a stew-pan, and pour over them half a pint of cream, or rich unskimmed milk.
3. Add some butter rolled in flour, and (if you choose) some small force-meat balls.
4. Set the stew-pan over hot coals.
5. Keep it closely covered, and stew or simmer it gently till the chicken is quite tender, but do not allow it to boil.
