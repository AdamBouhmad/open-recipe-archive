## Ingredients

- one cup of cornstarch
- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- one cup of sweet milk
- two cups of flour
- whites of seven eggs
- one teaspoonful cream of tartar
- one-half teaspoonful soda
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar to a cream.
2. Mix one teaspoonful cream of tartar with the flour and cornstarch.
3. Mix one-half teaspoonful soda with the sweet milk.
4. Add the milk and soda to the sugar and butter, then add flour, then the whites of eggs.
5. Flavor to taste.
