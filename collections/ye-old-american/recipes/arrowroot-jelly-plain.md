## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiling water
- 2 heaping teaspoonfuls of best Bermuda arrowroot
- 1 teaspoonful lemon juice
- 2 teaspoonfuls of white sugar

## Directions

1. Wet the arrowroot in a little cold water, and rub smooth.
2. Stir into the hot water, which should be on the fire and actually boiling at the time, with the sugar already melted in it.
3. Stir until clear, boiling steadily all the while, and add the lemon.
4. Wet a cup in cold water, and pour in the jelly to form.
5. Eat cold with sugar and cream flavored with rose-water.
