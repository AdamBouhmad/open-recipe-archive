## Ingredients

- Ripe, tart winter apples—Pippins, Greenings, or Baldwins
- Light-brown sugar
- Whole cloves
- Good crust
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and slice ripe, tart winter apples
2. Line your dish with a good crust
3. Put in a layer of fruit
4. Sprinkle light-brown sugar thickly over it
5. Scatter half a dozen whole cloves upon this
6. Lay on more apples, and so on, until the dish is well filled
7. Cover with crust and bake
8. Sift powdered sugar over the top before sending to table
