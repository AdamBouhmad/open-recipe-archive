## Ingredients

- twelve pippin apples
- one quart of new milk
- one quart of sifted corn meal
- a little salt
- four spoonfuls of chopped suet
- a teacupful of good molasses
- a teaspoonful of soda dissolved

## Directions

1. Pare and core twelve pippin apples; slice them very thin
2. Stir into one quart of new milk one quart of sifted corn meal
3. Add a little salt, then the apples, four spoonfuls of chopped suet and a teacupful of good molasses, adding a teaspoonful of soda dissolved
4. Mix these well together, pour into a buttered dish and bake four hours
5. Serve hot with sugar and wine sauce
