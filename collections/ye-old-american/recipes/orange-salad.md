## Ingredients

- five thin-skinned sour oranges
- one-third cup olive oil
- one and one-half tablespoons lemon juice
- one and one-half tablespoons vinegar
- one-third teaspoon salt
- one-fourth teaspoon paprika
- a few grains mustard
- watercress

## Directions

1. Cut five thin-skinned sour oranges in very thin slices, and slices in quarters.
2. Marinate with a dressing made by mixing one-third cup olive oil, one and one-half tablespoons each lemon juice and vinegar, one-third teaspoon salt, one-fourth teaspoon paprika, and a few grains mustard.
3. Serve on a bed of watercress.
