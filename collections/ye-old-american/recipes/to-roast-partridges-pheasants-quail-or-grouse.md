## Ingredients

- partridges, pheasants, quail or grouse
- soda
- stuffing
- salt pork
- butter
- water
- salt
- pepper
- browned flour
- cream

## Directions

1. Carefully cut out all the shot
2. Wash thoroughly but quickly, using soda in the water
3. Rinse again, and dry with a clean cloth
4. Stuff them and sew them up
5. Skewer the legs and wings to the body
6. Larder the breast with very thin slices of fat salt pork
7. Place them in the oven
8. Baste with butter and water before taking up
9. Season them with salt and pepper
10. Alternatively, you can leave out the pork and use only butter, or cook them without stuffing
11. Make a gravy of the drippings thickened with browned flour
12. Boil up and serve in a boat
13. These are all very fine broiled, first splitting down the back
14. Place on the gridiron the inside down, cover with a baking tin
15. Broil slowly at first
16. Serve with cream gravy
