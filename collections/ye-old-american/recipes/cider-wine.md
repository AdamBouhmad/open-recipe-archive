## Ingredients

- sweet cider
- honey

## Directions

1. Take sweet cider immediately from the press.
2. Strain it through a flannel bag into a tub, and stir into it as much honey as will make it strong enough to bear up an egg.
3. Boil and skim it, and when the scum ceases to rise, strain it again.
4. When cool, put it into a cask, and set it in a cool cellar till spring.
5. Then bottle it off; and when ripe, it will be found a very pleasant beverage.
