## Ingredients

- Preserved peaches, plums, quinces, cherries or any other sweetmeat
- Light crust
- Cloth
- Cream for serving

## Directions

1. Make a light crust, and roll a small piece of moderate thickness.
2. Fill with the fruit in quantity to make the size of a peach dumpling.
3. Tie each one in a dumpling cloth, well floured inside.
4. Drop them into hot water and boil half an hour.
5. When done, remove the cloth and send to table hot.
6. Eat with cream.
