## Ingredients

- 1½ cups fine stale bread crumbs
- 1½ cups scalded milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 4 teaspoons baking powder

## Directions

1. Add milk and butter to crumbs, and soak until crumbs are soft; add eggs well beaten, then flour, salt, and baking powder mixed and sifted.
2. Cook same as other griddle-cakes.
