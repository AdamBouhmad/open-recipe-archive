## Ingredients

- a quart of dough
- three fresh eggs
- milk-warm water

## Directions

1. Take a quart of dough from your bread at a very early hour in the morning
2. Break three fresh eggs, separating the yelks from the whites
3. Whip them both to a froth, mix them with the dough
4. Add gradually milk-warm water, till you make a batter the thickness of buckwheat cakes
5. Beat it well, and set it to rise till near breakfast time
6. Have the griddle ready, pour on the batter to look quite round
7. They do not require turning
