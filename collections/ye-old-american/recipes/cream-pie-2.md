## Ingredients

- pint of cream
- one and a half cupfuls of sugar
- whites of three eggs
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pour a pint of cream upon one and a half cupfuls of sugar
2. Let it stand until the whites of three eggs have been beaten to a stiff froth
3. Add this to the cream and beat up thoroughly
4. Grate a little nutmeg over the mixture
5. Bake without an upper crust
