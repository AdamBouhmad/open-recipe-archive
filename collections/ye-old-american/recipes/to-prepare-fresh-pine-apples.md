## Ingredients

- pine-apples
- powdered loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Cut off the top and bottom and pare off the rind.
2. Cut the pine-apples in round slices half an inch thick, and put them into a deep dish, sprinkling every slice with powdered loaf-sugar.
3. Cover them, and let them lie in the sugar for an hour or two, before they are to be eaten.
