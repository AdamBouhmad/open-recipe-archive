## Ingredients

- a broiler
- two and one-half tablespoons melted butter
- one cup stock
- one tablespoon butter
- one tablespoon flour
- one-half cup cooked potato balls
- one-third cup canned string beans, cut in small pieces
- one-third cup cooked carrot, cut in fancy shapes
- six sautéd mushroom caps

## Directions

1. Dress, clean, and truss a broiler.
2. Put in a casserole dish, brush over with two and one-half tablespoons melted butter, put on cover, and bake twenty minutes; then add one cup stock and cook until chicken is tender.
3. Thicken stock with one tablespoon, each, butter and flour cooked together, and add one-half cup cooked potato balls, one-third cup canned string beans, cut in small pieces, one-third cup cooked carrot, cut in fancy shapes, and six sautéd mushroom caps.
