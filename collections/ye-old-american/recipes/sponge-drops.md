## Ingredients

- three eggs
- one teacup of sugar
- one heaping coffeecup of flour
- one teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- half a teaspoonful of saleratus
- lemon flavoring
- washed butter

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs and sugar to a froth
2. Mix one teaspoonful of cream of tartar and half a teaspoonful of saleratus thoroughly into the flour
3. Stir the flour mixture into the eggs and sugar
4. Flavor with lemon
5. Butter tin sheets with washed butter
6. Drop in teaspoonfuls about three inches apart
7. Bake instantly in a very quick oven
8. Watch closely as they will burn easily
9. Serve with ice cream
