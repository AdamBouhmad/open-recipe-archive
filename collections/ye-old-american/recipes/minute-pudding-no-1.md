## Ingredients

- a quart of sweet milk
- a pinch of salt
- a piece of butter as large as half an egg
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. Set saucepan or deep frying pan on the stove, the bottom and sides well buttered
2. Put into it a quart of sweet milk, a pinch of salt and a piece of butter as large as half an egg
3. When it boils, stir in sifted flour, sifting it through your fingers, a handful at a time, until it becomes smooth and quite thick
4. Turn it into a dish that has been dipped in water
5. Make a sauce very sweet to serve with it
6. Add canned berries or fresh ones just before taking from the stove if desired
