## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup water
- Whites 3 eggs
- ¼ teaspoon tartaric acid

## Directions

1. Boil sugar and water until syrup when dropped from tip of spoon forms a long thread.
2. Pour syrup gradually on beaten whites of eggs, beating constantly; then add acid and continue beating.
3. When stiff enough to spread, put a thin coating over cake.
4. Beat remaining frosting until cold and stiff enough to keep in shape after being forced through a pastry tube.
5. After first coating on cake has hardened, cover with a thicker layer, and crease for cutting.
6. If frosting is too stiff to spread smoothly, thin with a few drops of water.
7. With a pastry bag and variety of tubes, cake may be ornamented as desired.
