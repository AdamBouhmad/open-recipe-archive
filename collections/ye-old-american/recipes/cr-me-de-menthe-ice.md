## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 1 cup sugar
- ⅓ cup Crême de Menthe cordial
- Burnett's Leaf Green

## Directions

1. Make a syrup as for Lemon Ice
2. add cordial and coloring
3. strain and freeze
