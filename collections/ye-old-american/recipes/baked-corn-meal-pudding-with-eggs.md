## Ingredients

- one small cupful of Indian meal
- one-half cupful of wheat flour
- cold milk
- one pint of milk
- half a cupful of sugar
- half a cupful of molasses
- two eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- a little salt
- a tablespoonful of mixed cinnamon and nutmeg
- two-thirds of a teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Stir together one small cupful of Indian meal and one-half cupful of wheat flour with cold milk
2. Scald one pint of milk and stir the mixture in it and cook until thick
3. Thin with cold milk to the consistency of batter, not very thick
4. Add half a cupful of sugar, half a cupful of molasses, two eggs, two tablespoonfuls of butter, a little salt, a tablespoonful of mixed cinnamon and nutmeg
5. Add two-thirds of a teaspoonful of soda just before putting it into the oven
6. Bake two hours
7. After baking it half an hour, stir it up thoroughly, then finish baking
8. Serve it up hot, eat it with wine sauce, or with butter and syrup
