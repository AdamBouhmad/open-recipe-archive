## Ingredients

- Two quarts of oysters
- One quart of milk
- Two tablespoonfuls of butter
- One teacupful of hot water
- Pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Strain all the liquor from the oysters
2. Add the water and heat
3. When near the boil, add the seasoning, then the oysters
4. Cook about five minutes from the time they begin to simmer, until they ruffle
5. Stir in the butter and cook one minute
6. Pour into the tureen
7. Stir in the boiling milk and send to table
