## Ingredients

- salt pork
- flour
- one or two tablespoonfuls of flour
- half a pint of milk
- pepper
- salt
- a teaspoonful of finely-chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut in thin slices, and freshen in cold water.
2. Roll in flour, and fry crisp.
3. If required quickly pour boiling water over the slices, let stand a few minutes, drain and roll in flour as before.
4. Drain off most of the grease from the frying pan.
5. Stir in while hot one or two tablespoonfuls of flour, about half a pint of milk, a little pepper, and salt if over freshened.
6. Let it boil, and pour into a gravy dish.
7. A teaspoonful of finely-chopped parsley will add pleasantly to the appearance of the gravy.
