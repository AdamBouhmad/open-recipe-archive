## Ingredients

- ¾ cup fruit pulp, peach, apricot, or quince
- Whites 3 eggs
- Sugar
- Few grains salt

## Directions

1. Rub fruit through sieve; if canned fruit is used, first drain from syrup
2. Heat, and sweeten if needed
3. Beat whites of eggs until stiff
4. Add gradually hot fruit pulp, and salt, and continue beating
5. Turn into buttered and sugared individual moulds, having them three-fourths full
6. Set moulds in pan of hot water and bake in slow oven until firm, which may be determined by pressing with finger
7. Serve with Sabyon Sauce
