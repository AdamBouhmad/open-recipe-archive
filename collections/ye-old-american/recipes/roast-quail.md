## Ingredients

- quail
- flour
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- green grape jelly
- parsley

## Directions

1. Rinse well and steam over boiling water until tender
2. Dredge with flour, and smother in butter
3. Season with salt and pepper and roast inside the stove
4. Thicken the gravy
5. Serve with green grape jelly, and garnish with parsley
