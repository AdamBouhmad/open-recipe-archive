## Ingredients

- One tablespoonful of flour
- One egg
- Cold milk
- A pinch of salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Mix one tablespoonful of flour, one egg, cold milk and a pinch of salt to a batter
2. Boil fifteen minutes in a buttered cup
3. Eat with sauce, fruit or plain sugar
