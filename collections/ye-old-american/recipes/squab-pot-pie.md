## Ingredients

- salt pork
- wild squabs
- potatoes
- dough balls
- salt
- white pepper
- mace or nutmeg
- hot water
- short pie-crust

## Directions

1. Cut into dice three ounces of salt pork
2. Divide six wild squabs into pieces at the joints and remove the skin
3. Cut up four potatoes into small squares
4. Prepare a dozen small dough balls
5. Put into a yellow, deep baking dish the pork, potatoes and squabs, and then the balls of dough
6. Season with salt, white pepper, a dash of mace or nutmeg
7. Add hot water enough to cover the ingredients
8. Cover with a short pie-crust and bake in a moderate oven three-quarters of an hour
