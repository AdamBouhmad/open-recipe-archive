## Ingredients

- veal from the leg or other lean part
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- egg
- cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut veal from the leg or other lean part into pieces the size of an oyster.
2. Season with pepper, salt and a little mace; rub some over each piece.
3. Dip in egg, then into cracker crumbs and fry.
