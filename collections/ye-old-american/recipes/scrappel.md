## Ingredients

- head, heart and any lean scraps of pork
- pepper
- salt
- corn meal

## Directions

1. Take the head, heart and any lean scraps of pork, and boil until the flesh slips easily from the bones.
2. Remove the fat, gristle and bones, then chop fine.
3. Set the liquor in which the meat was boiled aside until cold, take the cake of fat from the surface and return to the fire.
4. When it boils put in the chopped meat and season well with pepper and salt.
5. Let it boil again, then thicken with corn meal as you would in making ordinary corn meal mush, by letting it slip through the fingers slowly to prevent lumps.
6. Cook an hour, stirring constantly at first, afterwards putting back on the range in a position to boil gently.
7. When done, pour into a long, square pan, not too deep, and mould.
8. Cut into slices when cold, and fried brown, as you do mush.
