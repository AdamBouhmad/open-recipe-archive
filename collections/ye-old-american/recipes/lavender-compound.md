## Ingredients

- lavender blossoms freshly gathered
- the best brandy
- powdered cloves
- mace
- nutmeg
- cochineal

## Directions

1. Fill a quart bottle with lavender blossoms freshly gathered, and put in loosely
2. pour in as much of the best brandy as it will contain
3. Let it stand a fortnight, and then strain it
4. mix with it of powdered cloves, mace, nutmeg and cochineal, a quarter of an ounce of each; and cork it up for use in small bottles
