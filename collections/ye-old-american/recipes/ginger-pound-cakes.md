## Ingredients

- one half pound butter
- one-half pound sugar
- three-fourths pound flour
- two teaspoons baking powder
- four eggs
- one-half pound Canton ginger

## Directions

1. Cream one half pound butter and add gradually one-half pound sugar, continuing the beating.
2. Add three-fourths pound flour, mixed and sifted with two teaspoons baking powder alternately with four eggs beaten until thick and lemon-colored.
3. Then add one-half pound Canton ginger cut in small pieces.
4. Bake in small buttered and floured individual cake pans in a slow oven.
5. Cover with White Mountain Cream (see p. 528).
