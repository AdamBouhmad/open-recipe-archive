## Ingredients

- whites of half a dozen eggs
- a small teacupful of currant jelly
- cream

## Directions

1. Beat to a stiff foam the whites of half a dozen eggs
2. Add a small teacupful of currant jelly and whip all together again
3. Fill half full of cream as many saucers as you have guests
4. Drop in the centre of each saucer a tablespoonful of the beaten eggs and jelly in the shape of a pyramid
