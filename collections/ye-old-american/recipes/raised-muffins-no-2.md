## Ingredients

- Three pints of flour
- Three eggs
- A piece of butter the size of an egg
- Two heaping teaspoonfuls of white sugar
- One-half cake of compressed yeast
- One quart of milk
- Salt
- Half a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a teaspoonful of hot water

## Directions

1. Warm the milk with the butter in it
2. Cool a little and stir in the sugar
3. Add a little salt
4. Stir this gradually into the flour
5. Add the eggs well beaten
6. Dissolve the yeast in half a cup of lukewarm water and add to the other ingredients
7. When ready for baking, stir in half a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a teaspoonful of hot water
8. Butter the muffin-rings or gem-irons
9. Bake in a quick oven
