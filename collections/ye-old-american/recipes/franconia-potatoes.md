## Ingredients

- potatoes
- fat from roasting meat

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Boiled Potatoes
2. parboil ten minutes
3. drain, and place in pan in which meat is roasting
4. bake until soft, basting with fat in pan when basting meat
5. Time required for baking about forty minutes
