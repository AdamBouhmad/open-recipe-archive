## Ingredients

- small round green peppers
- vine leaves
- alum

## Directions

1. Take the small round peppers while they are green
2. With a sharp penknife extract the seeds and cores
3. Put the outsides into a kettle with vine leaves, and a little alum to give them firmness, and assist in keeping them green
4. Proceed precisely as directed for the water-melon rind
