## Ingredients

- slices of bread and butter
- a pound of currants, picked, washed and dried
- brown sugar
- four eggs
- a pint of milk
- a wine glass of rose water
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut some slices of bread and butter moderately thick, omitting the crust; stale bread is best.
2. Butter a deep dish, and cover the bottom with slices of the buttered bread.
3. Spread one third of the currants thickly over the bread and butter, and strew on some brown sugar.
4. Put another layer of bread and butter, and cover it also with currants and sugar.
5. Finish with a third layer of each.
6. Beat four eggs very light and mix them with a pint of milk, and a wine glass of rose water.
7. Pour the mixture over the whole.
8. Bake the pudding an hour, and grate nutmeg over it when done.
9. Eat it warm, but not hot.
