## Ingredients

- six slices of stale bread
- salt
- water
- butter
- eggs
- pepper
- thickened gravy or white sauce

## Directions

1. Toast six slices of stale bread, dip them in hot salted water and butter them lightly.
2. After arranging them on a platter or deep plate, break enough eggs to cover them, breaking one at a time and slip over the toast so that they do not break.
3. Sprinkle over them salt and pepper and turn over all some kind of thickened gravy--either chicken or lamb, cream or a cream sauce made the same as 'White Sauce.'
4. Turn this over the toast and eggs and bake in a hot oven until the eggs are set, or about five minutes.
5. Serve at once.
