## Ingredients

- Half a pint of buckwheat flour
- A quarter of a pint of corn meal
- A quarter of a pint of wheat flour
- A little salt
- Two eggs beaten very light
- One quart of new milk
- One tablespoonful of butter or sweet lard
- Two large tablespoonfuls of yeast

## Directions

1. Mix eggs with warm milk before adding flour
2. Mix in buckwheat flour, corn meal, wheat flour, salt, butter or lard, and yeast
3. Set to rise at night for the morning
4. If in the least sour, stir in before baking just enough soda to correct the acidity
