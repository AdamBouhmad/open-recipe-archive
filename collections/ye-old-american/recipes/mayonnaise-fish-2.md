## Ingredients

- 1 pound cold boiled fish (halibut, rock or cod)
- yolks of 4 boiled eggs
- salad oil or butter
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- 2 teaspoonfuls white sugar
- 6 tablespoonfuls vinegar
- white of 1 raw egg
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces an inch in length
2. Rub the yolks of four boiled eggs to a smooth paste with salad oil or butter
3. Add salt, pepper, mustard, two teaspoonfuls of white sugar, and six tablespoonfuls of vinegar
4. Beat the mixture until light
5. Just before pouring over the fish, stir in lightly the frothed white of a raw egg
6. Serve the fish in a glass dish with half the dressing stirred in with it
7. Spread the remainder over the top and lay lettuce leaves from the core of the head of lettuce around the edges
