## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Turnips
- 1¼ cups carrots
- ½ cup turnips
- 1¼ cups cooked peas
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup chicken stock
- ½ cup cream
- Pepper and salt
- Yolks 2 eggs
- ½ tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean carrots and turnips and cut into strips or fancy shapes; there should be one and one-fourth cups carrots and one-half cup turnips.
2. Cook separately in boiling salted water until soft.
3. Drain, and add one and one-fourth cups cooked peas.
4. Reheat in a sauce made of three tablespoons butter, three tablespoons flour, one cup chicken stock, and one-half cup cream.
5. Season to taste with pepper and salt.
6. Just before serving add yolks two eggs and one-half tablespoon lemon juice.
