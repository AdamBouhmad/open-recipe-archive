## Ingredients

- tin plate
- puff paste
- five or six select oysters
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- egg batter

## Directions

1. For each pie take a tin plate half the size of an ordinary dinner plate
2. butter it, and cover the bottom with a puff paste, as for pies
3. lay on it five or six select oysters, or enough to cover the bottom
4. butter them and season with a little salt and plenty of pepper
5. spread over this an egg batter, and cover with a crust of the paste, making small openings in it with a fork
6. Bake in a hot oven fifteen to twenty minutes, or until the top is nicely browned
