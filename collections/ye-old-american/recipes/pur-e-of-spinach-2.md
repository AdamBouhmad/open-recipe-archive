## Ingredients

- one-half peck spinach
- large quantity of boiling salted water
- one-third teaspoon soda
- one-half teaspoon sugar
- three tablespoons butter
- one tablespoon flour
- one-half cup cream
- yolk and white of hard-boiled egg
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Wash and pick over one-half peck spinach.
2. Cook in an uncovered vessel with a large quantity of boiling salted water to which is added one-third teaspoon soda and one-half teaspoon sugar.
3. Drain, chop finely, and rub through a sieve.
4. Reheat, add three tablespoons butter, one tablespoon flour, and one-half cup cream.
5. Arrange on serving dish and garnish with yolk and white of hard-boiled egg and fried bread cut in fancy shapes.
