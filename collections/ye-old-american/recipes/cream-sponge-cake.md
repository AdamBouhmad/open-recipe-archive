## Ingredients

- Yolks 4 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons cold water
- 1½ tablespoons corn-starch
- Flour
- 1¼ teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Whites 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoon lemon extract

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored, add sugar gradually, and beat two minutes
2. Add water
3. Put corn-starch in a cup and fill cup with flour. Mix and sift corn-starch and flour with baking powder and salt, and add to first mixture
4. When thoroughly mixed add whites of eggs beaten until stiff, and flavoring
5. Bake thirty minutes in a moderate oven
