## Ingredients

- a cup of rice
- butter
- cold fowl
- water
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil a cup of rice the night before; put it into a square, narrow bread-pan, set it in the ice-box.
2. Next morning cut it in half inch slices, rub over each slice a little warm butter and toast them on a broiler to a delicate brown.
3. Arrange the toast on a warm platter and turn over the whole a chicken hash made from the remains of cold fowl, the meat picked from the bones, chopped fine, put into the frying pan with butter and a little water to moisten it, adding pepper and salt.
4. Heat hot all through.
5. Serve immediately.
