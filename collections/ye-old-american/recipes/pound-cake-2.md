## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- one pound of flour sifted
- one pound of powdered sugar
- twelve eggs
- grated lemon peel
- a nutmeg
- a gill of brandy

## Directions

1. Wash the salt from a pound of butter, and rub it till it is soft as cream
2. Have ready a pound of flour sifted, one of powdered sugar, and twelve eggs well beaten
3. Put alternately into the butter, sugar, flour, and the froth from the eggs
4. Continue to beat them together till all the ingredients are in, and the cake quite light
5. Add some grated lemon peel, a nutmeg, and a gill of brandy
6. Butter the pans
7. Bake them
