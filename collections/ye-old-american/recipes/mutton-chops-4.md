## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- egg
- cracker
- lard or dripping
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Trim off the superfluous fat and skin, so as to give the chops a certain litheness and elegance of shape
2. Dip each in beaten egg, roll in pounded cracker, and fry in hot lard or dripping
3. If the fat is unsalted, sprinkle the chops with salt before rolling in the egg
4. Serve up dry and hot
