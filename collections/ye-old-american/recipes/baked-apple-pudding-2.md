## Ingredients

- 6 large firm pippins, grated
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- ½ cup sugar
- 4 eggs
- Juice of one lemon, and half the peel

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to a cream, stir in the yolks, the lemon, the grated apple, lastly the whites.
2. Grate nutmeg over the top, and bake until nicely browned.
3. Eat cold with cream.
