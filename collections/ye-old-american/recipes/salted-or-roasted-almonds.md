## Ingredients

- half a pound of almonds
- a tablespoonful of melted butter
- one tablespoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Blanch half a pound of almonds
2. Put with them a tablespoonful of melted butter and one of salt
3. Stir them till well mixed
4. Spread them over a baking-pan and bake fifteen minutes, or till crisp, stirring often
5. They must be bright yellow-brown when done
