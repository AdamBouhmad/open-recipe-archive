## Ingredients

- one pound of finest pulverized sugar
- three wine-glassfuls of clear water
- whites of four eggs
- vanilla, rose-water, or lemon juice for seasoning

## Directions

1. Add three wine-glassfuls of clear water to one pound of finest pulverized sugar
2. Let it stand until it dissolves
3. Boil it until it is perfectly clear and threads from the spoon
4. Beat well the whites of four eggs
5. Pour the sugar into the dish with the eggs, but do not mix them until the syrup is luke-warm
6. Beat all well together for one-half hour
7. Season to your taste with vanilla, rose-water, or lemon juice
8. Rub the cake with a little flour before you apply the icing
9. Put the first coating on the cake as soon as it is well mixed
10. While the first coat is drying continue to beat the remainder
11. Set the cake in a warm place near the fire
