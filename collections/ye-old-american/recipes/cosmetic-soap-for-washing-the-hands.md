## Ingredients

- one pound of castile or other nice old soap
- water
- lavender water or essence of any kind
- corn meal

## Directions

1. Scrape the soap in small pieces
2. Put it on the fire with a little water
3. Stir it till it becomes a smooth paste
4. Pour it into a bowl
5. When cold, add some lavender water or essence of any kind
6. Beat it with a silver spoon until well mixed
7. Thicken it with corn meal
8. Keep it in small pots closely covered
