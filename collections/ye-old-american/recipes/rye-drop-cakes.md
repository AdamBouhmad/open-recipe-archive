## Ingredients

- One pint of warm milk
- Half a teaspoonful of soda
- A little salt
- Four eggs well beaten
- Rye flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve half a teaspoonful of soda in one pint of warm milk
2. Add a little salt and four eggs well beaten
3. Add rye flour enough to make a thin batter
4. Bake in small cups, buttered, in a hot oven, or in small cakes upon a hot griddle
