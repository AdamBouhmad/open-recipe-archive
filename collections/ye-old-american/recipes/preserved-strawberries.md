## Ingredients

- strawberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put them in a preserving kettle over a slow fire until the sugar melts.
2. Boil twenty-five minutes, fast.
3. Take out the fruit in a perforated skimmer and fill a number of small cans three-quarters full.
4. Boil and skim the syrup five minutes longer, fill up the jars, and seal while hot.
5. Keep in a cool, dry place.
