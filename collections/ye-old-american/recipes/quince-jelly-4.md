## Ingredients

- quinces
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Select quinces that are a fine yellow
2. Rub off the down from them, core and cut them small
3. Put them in a preserving kettle with a teacupful of water for each pound
4. Let them stew gently until soft, without mashing
5. Put them in a thin muslin bag with the liquor
6. Press them very lightly
7. To each pint of the liquor put a pound of sugar
8. Stir it until it is all dissolved
9. Set it over the fire and let it boil gently until by cooling some on a plate you find it a good jelly
10. Turn it into pots or tumblers and when cold secure as directed for jellies
