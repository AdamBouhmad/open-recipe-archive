## Ingredients

- yolks of four fresh raw eggs
- two hard-boiled eggs
- oil (a tablespoonful to each yolk of raw egg)
- two heaping teaspoonfuls of dry table salt
- one teaspoonful of pepper
- one teaspoonful of made mustard
- two tablespoonfuls of vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the yolks of four fresh raw eggs, with two hard-boiled ones, into a cold bowl
2. Rub these as smooth as possible before introducing the oil
3. Introduce the oil by degrees, a few drops at a time
4. When the oil is well mixed, and assumes the appearance of jelly, put in two heaping teaspoonfuls of dry table salt, one of pepper and one of made mustard
5. Add two tablespoonfuls of vinegar gradually
6. The Mayonnaise should be the thickness of thick cream when finished
7. If it looks like curdling when mixing it, set in the ice-box or in a cold place for about forty minutes or an hour, then mix it again
8. Place it in a pan of cracked ice while mixing
