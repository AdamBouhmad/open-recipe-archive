## Ingredients

- chicken
- water
- salt
- pepper
- yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Boil the chicken slowly, putting on just enough water to cover it well, watching it closely that it does not boil down too much
2. When the chicken is tender, season with salt and a very little pepper
3. Beat the yolk of an egg light and add it, which is very nourishing
