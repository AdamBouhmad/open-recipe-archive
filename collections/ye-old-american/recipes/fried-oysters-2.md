## Ingredients

- oysters (largest and best)
- crackers
- butter

## Directions

1. Use the largest and best oysters you can find.
2. Take them carefully from the liquor.
3. Lay them in rows upon a clean cloth.
4. Press another lightly upon them to absorb the moisture.
5. Have ready some crackers crushed fine.
6. In the frying-pan heat enough nice butter to cover the oysters entirely.
7. Dip each oyster into the cracker, rolling it over that it may become completely incrusted.
8. Drop them carefully into the frying-pan.
9. Fry quickly to a light brown.
10. If the butter is hot enough they will soon be ready to take out.
11. Test it by putting in one oyster before you risk the rest.
12. Do not let them lie in the pan an instant after they are done.
13. Serve dry, and let the dish be warm.
