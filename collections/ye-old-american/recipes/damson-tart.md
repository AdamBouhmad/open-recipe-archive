## Ingredients

- Fruit
- Pastry
- Sugar
- Egg

## Directions

1. Pick over the fruit, put in a dish lined with pastry, sweeten very freely, cover and bake.
2. Brush with beaten egg when done, and return to the oven for a few minutes to glaze.
