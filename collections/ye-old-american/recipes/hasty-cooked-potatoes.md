## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Wash and peel some potatoes; cut them into slices of about a quarter of an inch in thickness.
2. Throw them into boiling salted water.
3. If of good quality, they will be done in about ten minutes.
4. Strain off the water, put the potatoes into a hot dish, chop them slightly.
5. Add pepper, salt, and a few small pieces of fresh butter.
6. Serve without loss of time.
