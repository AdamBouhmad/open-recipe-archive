## Ingredients

- sage leaves
- onions
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- lard
- water

## Directions

1. Chop a few sage leaves and two onions very fine
2. Mix them with a good lump of butter, a tea-spoonful of pepper, and two of salt
3. Put it in the goose
4. Split it and lay it down
5. Dust it with flour
6. When it is thoroughly hot, baste it with nice lard
7. Roast for an hour and a half before a good clear fire
8. When it is enough, dredge and baste it
9. Pull out the spit, and pour in a little boiling water
