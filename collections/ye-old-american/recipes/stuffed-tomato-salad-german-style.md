## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- cabbage
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- bay leaf
- peppercorns
- mustard seed
- cloves

## Directions

1. Prepare tomatoes same as Tomatoes Stuffed with Pineapple
2. Shred finely one-half a cabbage
3. Let stand two hours in salted water, allowing two tablespoons salt to one quart water
4. Cook slowly thirty minutes one-half cup each cold water and vinegar, with a bit of bay leaf, one-half teaspoon peppercorns, one-fourth teaspoon mustard seed, and six cloves
5. Strain, and pour over cabbage drained from salt water
6. Let stand two hours, again drain, and refill tomatoes
