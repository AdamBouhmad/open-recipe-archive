## Ingredients

- pieces of ribs of lean salt pork
- a slice or two of the fat of salt pork
- pepper
- half a dozen potatoes
- one pint of sour or buttermilk
- two eggs
- a teaspoonful of salt
- a level teaspoonful of soda
- flour

## Directions

1. Take pieces of ribs of lean salt pork, also a slice or two of the fat of salt pork.
2. Scald it well with hot water so as to wash out the briny taste.
3. Put it into a kettle and cover it with cold water, enough for the required want.
4. Cover it and boil an hour, season with pepper.
5. Add half a dozen potatoes cut into quarters.
6. When it all commences to boil again, drop in dumplings made from one pint of sour or buttermilk, two eggs well beaten, a teaspoonful of salt, a level teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a spoonful of water and as much flour as will make a very stiff batter.
7. Drop this into the kettle or broth by spoonfuls, and cook forty minutes, closely covered.
