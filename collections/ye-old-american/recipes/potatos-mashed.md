## Ingredients

- potatos
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. When the potatos are thoroughly boiled, drain and dry them perfectly, pick out every speck, and rub them through a colander into a clean stew-pan
2. To a pound of potatos put half an ounce of butter, and a table-spoonful of milk
3. Do not make them too moist; mix them well together
4. You may put them into shapes, touch them over with yelk of egg, and brown them very slightly before a slow fire
