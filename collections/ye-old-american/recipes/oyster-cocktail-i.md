## Ingredients

- 8 small raw oysters
- 1 tablespoon tomato catsup
- ½ tablespoon vinegar or lemon juice
- 2 drops Tabasco
- Salt
- 1 teaspoon celery, finely chopped
- ½ teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients, chill thoroughly, and serve in cocktail glasses, or cases made from green peppers placed on a bed of crushed ice.
