## Ingredients

- birds
- fine ripe orange
- grated cold ham
- bread-crumbs
- butter
- egg yolk
- fat of bacon
- meat or poultry trimmings
- water
- browned flour
- claret
- orange juice

## Directions

1. Pick and draw the birds immediately after they are brought in.
2. Before you roast them, fill the inside with pieces of a fine ripe orange, leaving out the rind and seeds. Or stuff them with grated cold ham, mixed with bread-crumbs, butter, and a little yolk of egg.
3. Lard them with small slips of the fat of bacon drawn through the flesh with a larding needle.
4. Roast them before a clear fire.
5. Make a fine rich gravy of the trimmings of meat or poultry, stewed in a little water, and thickened with a spoonful of browned flour.
6. Strain it, and set it on the fire again, having added half a pint of claret, and the juice of two large oranges.
7. Simmer it for a few minutes, pour some of it into the dish with the game, and serve the remainder in a boat.
