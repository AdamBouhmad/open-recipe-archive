## Ingredients

- ½ box gelatine or 2 tablespoons granulated gelatine
- ½ cup cold water
- ½ cup boiling water
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup orange juice
- Grated rind 1 orange
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Whip from 3½ cups cream

## Directions

1. Make same as Charlotte Russe, and mould; or make orange jelly, color with fruit red, and cover bottom of mould one-half inch deep; chill, and when firm fill with Orange Trifle mixture.
2. Cool remaining jelly in shallow pan, cut in cubes, and garnish base of mould.
