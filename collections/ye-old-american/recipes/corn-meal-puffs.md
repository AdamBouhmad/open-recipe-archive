## Ingredients

- one quart of boiling milk
- eight tablespoonfuls of Indian meal
- four tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- a teaspoonful of nutmeg
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Into one quart of boiling milk stir eight tablespoonfuls of Indian meal, four tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar and a teaspoonful of nutmeg
2. Let the whole boil five minutes, stirring constantly to prevent its adhering to the saucepan
3. Remove it from the fire, and when it has become cool stir into it six eggs, beaten as light as possible
4. Mix well, and pour the mixture into buttered teacups, nearly filling them
5. Bake in a moderate oven half an hour
6. Serve with lemon
