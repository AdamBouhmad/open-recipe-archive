## Ingredients

- one teacupful of milk
- one teacupful of bread crumbs
- six eggs
- pepper and salt
- a large spoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Let one teacupful of milk come to a boil
2. Pour it over one teacupful of bread crumbs and let it stand a few minutes
3. Break six eggs into a bowl, stir (not beat) till well mixed
4. Add the milk and bread, season with pepper and salt
5. Mix all well together and turn into a hot frying pan, containing a large spoonful of butter boiling hot
6. Fry the omelet slowly, and when brown on the bottom cut in squares and turn again
7. Fry to a delicate brown and serve hot
8. Cracker omelet may be made by substituting three or four rolled crackers in place of bread
