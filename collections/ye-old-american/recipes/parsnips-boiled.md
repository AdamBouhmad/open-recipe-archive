## Ingredients

- parsnips
- salt
- water
- melted butter or white sauce

## Directions

1. Wash, scrape and split the parsnips
2. Put them into a pot of boiling water
3. Add a little salt
4. Boil them till quite tender, which will be in from two to three hours, according to their size
5. Dry them in a cloth when done
6. Pour melted butter or white sauce over them in the dish
7. Serve them up with any sort of boiled meat or with salt cod
