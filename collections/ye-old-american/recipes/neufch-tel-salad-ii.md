## Ingredients

- one Neufchâtel cheese
- milk or cream
- parsley
- lettuce leaves
- radishes
- French Dressing

## Directions

1. Mash one Neufchâtel cheese and moisten with milk or cream
2. Shape into forms the size of robins' eggs
3. Sprinkle with finely chopped parsley, which has been dried
4. Arrange in nests of lettuce leaves, and garnish with radishes
5. Serve with French Dressing
