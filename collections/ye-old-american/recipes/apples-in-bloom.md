## Ingredients

- eight red apples
- boiling water
- 1 cup sugar
- grated rind of one-half lemon
- juice of one orange

## Directions

1. Select eight red apples, cook in boiling water until soft, turning them often; have water half surround apples.
2. Remove skins carefully, that the red color may remain, and arrange on serving dish.
3. To the water add one cup sugar, grated rind one-half lemon, and juice one orange; simmer until reduced to one cup.
4. Cool, and pour over apples.
5. Serve with Cream Sauce I or II.
