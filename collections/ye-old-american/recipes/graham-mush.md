## Ingredients

- Graham meal
- boiling salted water
- milk or cream and sugar
- butter and syrup

## Directions

1. Sift Graham meal slowly into boiling salted water, stirring briskly until thick as can be stirred with one hand
2. Serve with milk or cream and sugar, or butter and syrup
3. It will be improved by removing from the kettle to a pan, as soon as thoroughly mixed, and steaming three or four hours
4. It may also be eaten cold, or sliced and fried, like corn meal mush
