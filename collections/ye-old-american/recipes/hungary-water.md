## Ingredients

- one ounce of oil of rosemary
- two drachms of essence of ambergris
- a pint of spirits of wine

## Directions

1. Mix together one ounce of oil of rosemary and two drachms of essence of ambergris
2. Add them to a pint of spirits of wine
3. Shake it daily for a month, and then transfer it to small bottles
