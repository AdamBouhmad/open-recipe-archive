## Ingredients

- ½ lb. tapioca, soaked in a cup of cold water four hours
- 1 pint rich new milk
- ¾ cup of sugar
- 2 teaspoonfuls bitter almond or vanilla essence
- A little salt

## Directions

1. Heat the milk, and stir in the soaked tapioca.
2. When it has dissolved, add the sugar.
3. Boil slowly fifteen minutes, stirring all the time; take from the fire, and beat until nearly cold.
4. Flavor and pour into a mould dipped in cold water.
5. Turn out, and pour cold sweetened cream around it.
