## Ingredients

- celery
- milk
- salt
- butter
- white flour

## Directions

1. Wash and clean ten heads of celery, cut off the green tops and take off the outside stalks
2. Cut the heads in thin slices, boil them tender in a little milk, just enough for gravy
3. Add salt, and thicken it with a spoonful of butter and some white flour
4. Boil the shoulder and pour the sauce over it
