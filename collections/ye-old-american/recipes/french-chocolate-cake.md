## Ingredients

- whites of seven eggs
- two cups of sugar
- two-thirds of a cup of butter
- one cup of milk
- three cups of flour
- three teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- yolks of seven eggs
- one cup of grated chocolate
- Custard: two eggs
- one pint of milk
- one-half cup of sugar
- one tablespoonful of flour or cornstarch
- vanilla (two teaspoonfuls)

## Directions

1. Make the white part with the whites of seven eggs, two cups of sugar, two-thirds of a cup of butter, one cup of milk, three cups of flour and three teaspoonfuls of baking powder
2. Make the chocolate part just the same, only use the yolks of the eggs with a cup of grated chocolate stirred into it
3. Bake it in layers, the layers being light and dark
4. Make a custard with two eggs, one pint of milk, one-half cup of sugar, one tablespoonful of flour or cornstarch
5. When cool flavor with vanilla, two teaspoonfuls
6. Spread the custard between the layers
