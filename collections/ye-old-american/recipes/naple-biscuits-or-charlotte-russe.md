## Ingredients

- sponge cake
- yolks of four eggs
- a pint of boiling milk
- jelly or jam
- whites of three eggs
- three heaping tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make a double rule of sponge cake and bake it in round deep patty-pans
2. When cold cut out the inside about one-quarter of an inch from the edge and bottom, leaving the shell
3. Replace the inside with a custard made of the yolks of four eggs beaten with a pint of boiling milk, sweetened and flavored
4. Lay on the top of this some jelly or jam
5. Beat the whites of three eggs with three heaping tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar until it will stand in a heap and flavor it a little
6. Place this on the jelly
7. Set them aside in a cold place until time to serve
