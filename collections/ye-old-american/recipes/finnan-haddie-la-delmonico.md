## Ingredients

- 1 cup finnan haddie
- cold water
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- cayenne
- 1 tablespoon butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut fish in strips (there should be one cup), put in baking-pan, cover with cold water, place on back of range and allow water to heat to boiling-point; let stand on range, keeping water below boiling-point for twenty-five minutes, drain, and rinse thoroughly.
2. Separate fish into flakes, add one-half cup heavy cream and four hard-boiled eggs thinly sliced.
3. Season with cayenne, add one tablespoon butter, and sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
