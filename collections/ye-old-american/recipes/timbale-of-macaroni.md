## Ingredients

- small macaroni
- vermicelli
- spaghetti
- tagliarini
- butter
- grated cheese
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- meat
- poultry
- game

## Directions

1. Break in very short lengths small macaroni (vermicelli, spaghetti, tagliarini)
2. Let it be rather overdone
3. Dress it with butter and grated cheese
4. Work into it one or two eggs, according to quantity
5. Butter and bread crumb a plain mold
6. When the macaroni is nearly cold fill the mold with it, pressing it well down and leaving a hollow in the centre
7. Into the hollow place a well-flavored mince of meat, poultry or game
8. Fill up the mold with more macaroni, pressed well down
9. Bake in a moderately heated oven
10. Turn out and serve
