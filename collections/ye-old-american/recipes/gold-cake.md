## Ingredients

- 1 lb. sugar
- ½ lb. butter
- 1 lb. flour
- Yolks of ten eggs, well beaten
- Grated rind of one orange
- Juice of two lemons
- 1 teaspoonful soda dissolved in hot water

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. Stir in the yolks
3. Beat very hard for five minutes before putting in the flour
4. Add the soda next
5. Lastly add the lemon-juice, in which the grated orange-peel should have been steeped and strained out in a piece of thin muslin, leaving the flavoring and coloring matter in the juice
6. Flavor the icing also with lemon
