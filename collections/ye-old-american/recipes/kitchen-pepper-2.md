## Ingredients

- one ounce of ground ginger
- half an ounce of black pepper
- half an ounce of ground cinnamon
- half an ounce of nutmeg
- half an ounce of allspice
- one teaspoonful of ground cloves
- six ounces of salt

## Directions

1. Mix one ounce of ground ginger, half an ounce each of black pepper, ground cinnamon, nutmeg and allspice, one teaspoonful of ground cloves, and six ounces of salt
2. Keep in a tightly corked bottle
