## Ingredients

- Juice and grated rind of two lemons
- Two cupfuls of sugar
- Two eggs
- Crumbs of sponge cake
- Puff paste for patty-pans

## Directions

1. Mix well together the juice and grated rind of two lemons, two cupfuls of sugar, two eggs and the crumbs of sponge cake
2. Beat it all together until smooth
3. Put into twelve patty-pans lined with puff paste and bake until the crust is done
