## Ingredients

- Windsor bean
- boiling water
- bacon
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Gather them in the morning, when they are full grown, but quite young
2. Do not shell them till you are going to dress them
3. Put them into boiling water
4. Have a small bit of middling (flitch) of bacon, well boiled
5. Take the skin off, cover it with bread crumbs, and toast it
6. Lay this in the middle of the dish
7. Drain all the water from the beans
8. Put a little butter with them, and pour them round the bacon
9. When the large Windsor beans are used, put them into boiling water until the skins will slip off, and then make them into a puree as directed for turnips
