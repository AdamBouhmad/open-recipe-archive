## Ingredients

- wine or porter
- cold water
- sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix one-third wine or porter with two-thirds cold water.
2. Sweeten and grate nutmeg on the top.
3. Ice.
4. Serve dry toast with it.
