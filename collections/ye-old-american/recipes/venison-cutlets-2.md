## Ingredients

- Venison slices cut from loin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Melted butter or olive oil
- Soft stale bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean and trim slices of venison cut from loin.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Brush over with melted butter or olive oil.
4. Roll in soft stale bread crumbs.
5. Place in a broiler and broil five minutes, or sauté in butter.
6. Serve with Port Wine Sauce.
