## Ingredients

- a large handful of lavender blossoms
- a large handful of sage
- a large handful of mint
- a large handful of rue
- a large handful of wormwood
- a large handful of rosemary
- half an ounce of camphor
- alcohol
- three quarts of strong clear vinegar
- clove of garlic

## Directions

1. Chop and mix lavender blossoms, sage, mint, rue, wormwood and rosemary well
2. Put them into a jar, with half an ounce of camphor that has been dissolved in a little alcohol
3. Pour in three quarts of strong clear vinegar
4. Keep the jar for two or three weeks in the hot sun, and at night plunge it into a box of heated sand
5. Strain and bottle the liquid, putting into each bottle a clove of garlic sliced
6. After it has been bottled for a week, pour it off carefully from the sediment and filter it through blotting paper
7. Wash the bottles and return the vinegar to them
8. Keep it very tightly corked
