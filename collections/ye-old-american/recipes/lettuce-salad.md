## Ingredients

- Two or three heads white lettuce
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls salad oil
- ½ teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful white sugar
- ½ teaspoonful made mustard
- 1 teaspoonful pepper
- 4 tablespoonfuls vinegar

## Directions

1. Rub the yolks to a powder, add sugar, pepper, salt, mustard, and oil.
2. Let it stand five minutes, and beat in the vinegar.
3. Cut the lettuce up with a knife and fork,—a chopper would bruise it,—put into a bowl, add the dressing, and mix by tossing with a silver fork.
