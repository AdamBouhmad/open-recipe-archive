## Ingredients

- two dozen large, green, bell peppers
- strong brine
- water
- vinegar
- a small piece of alum
- two hard heads of white cabbage, chopped fine
- salt
- a cup of white mustard seed

## Directions

1. Take two dozen large, green, bell peppers, extract the seeds by cutting a slit in the side (so as to leave them whole)
2. Make a strong brine and pour over them
3. let them stand twenty-four hours
4. Take them out of the brine, and soak them in water for a day and a night
5. turn off this water and scald some vinegar, in which put a small piece of alum, and pour over them, letting them stand three days
6. Prepare a stuffing of two hard heads of white cabbage, chopped fine, seasoned slightly with salt and a cup of white mustard seed
7. mix it well and stuff the peppers hard and full
8. stitch up, place them in a stone jar, and pour over spiced vinegar scalding hot
9. Cover tightly
