## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one cupful of sweet milk
- sifted flour
- two large teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- a pinch of salt
- cherries
- liquid sauce

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs well
2. Mix in one cupful of sweet milk and sifted flour enough to make a stiff batter
3. Add two large teaspoonfuls of baking powder and a pinch of salt
4. Stir in as many cherries as can be stirred in
5. Boil one hour or steam and serve with liquid sauce
