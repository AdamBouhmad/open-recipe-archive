## Ingredients

- toasted bread
- boiling water
- butter
- salt
- boiling milk

## Directions

1. Toast bread as directed for dry toast.
2. Dip each slice, as it comes from the toaster, in boiling water.
3. Butter and salt slightly, and lay in a deep covered dish.
4. Have ready in a saucepan enough boiling milk to cover all well.
5. When your slices are packed, salt the milk very slightly.
6. Melt in it a bit of butter and pour over the slices.
7. Cover closely and let it stand five minutes before using.
