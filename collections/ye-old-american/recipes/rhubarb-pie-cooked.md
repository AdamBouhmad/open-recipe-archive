## Ingredients

- Rhubarb stalks
- a small piece of butter
- lemon-peel, cinnamon or nutmeg (if liked)
- thin crust
- sugar

## Directions

1. Skin the stalks
2. Cut them into small pieces
3. Wash and put them in a stewpan with no more water than what adheres to them
4. When cooked, mash them fine and put in a small piece of butter
5. When cool, sweeten to taste
6. Add a little lemon-peel, cinnamon or nutmeg if liked
7. Line your plate with thin crust
8. Put in the filling
9. Cover with crust
10. Bake in a quick oven
11. Sift sugar over it when served
