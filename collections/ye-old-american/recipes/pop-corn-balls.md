## Ingredients

- three large ears of pop-corn
- one cup of molasses
- one cup of light brown or white sugar
- one tablespoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Pop the corn
2. Shake it down in pan so the unpopped corn will settle at the bottom
3. Put the nice white popped in a greased pan
4. Take one cup of molasses, one cup of light brown or white sugar, one tablespoonful of vinegar
5. Boil until it will harden in water
6. Pour on the corn
7. Stir with a spoon until thoroughly mixed
8. Mold into balls with the hand
