## Ingredients

- Salt codfish
- Water
- Mashed potato
- Butter
- Sweet milk
- Beaten egg
- Flour
- Boiling lard or good dripping

## Directions

1. Prepare the fish precisely as for boiling whole
2. Cut in pieces when it has been duly washed and soaked, and boil twenty minutes
3. Turn off the water, and cover with fresh from the boiling tea-kettle
4. Boil twenty minutes more
5. Drain the fish very dry, and spread upon a dish to cool
6. When perfectly cold, pick to pieces with a fork, removing every vestige of skin and bone, and shredding very fine
7. Add an equal bulk of mashed potato
8. Work into a stiff batter by adding a lump of butter and sweet milk, and if desired, a beaten egg
9. Flour your hands and make the mixture into balls or cakes
10. Drop them into boiling lard or good dripping, and fry to a light brown
