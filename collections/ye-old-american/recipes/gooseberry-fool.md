## Ingredients

- two quarts of green gooseberries
- loaf sugar
- water
- rich boiled custard
- frothed cream

## Directions

1. Pick the stems and blossoms from two quarts of green gooseberries
2. Put them in a stew pan, with their weight in loaf sugar, and a very little water
3. When sufficiently stewed, pass the pulp through a sieve
4. When cold, add rich boiled custard till it is like thick cream
5. Put it in a glass bowl, and lay frothed cream on the top
