## Ingredients

- Brioche dough
- Rum Sauce

## Directions

1. Shape Brioche dough in the form of large biscuits and put into buttered individual tin moulds, having moulds two-thirds full.
2. Cover, and let rise to fill moulds.
3. Bake twenty-five minutes in a moderate oven.
4. Remove from moulds and dip in Rum Sauce.
5. Arrange on a dish and pour remaining sauce around cakes.
