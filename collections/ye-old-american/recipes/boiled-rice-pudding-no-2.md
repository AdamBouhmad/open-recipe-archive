## Ingredients

- two teacupfuls of rice
- water
- half a pound of raisins stoned and cut in halves
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Wash two teacupfuls of rice and soak it in water for half an hour
2. Turn off the water and mix the rice with half a pound of raisins stoned and cut in halves
3. Add a little salt
4. Tie the whole in a cloth, leaving room for the rice to swell to twice its natural size
5. Boil two hours in plenty of water
6. Serve with wine sauce
