## Ingredients

- Pistachio Ice Cream
- Sultana raisins soaked one hour in brandy
- Vanilla Ice Cream or whipped cream, sweetened, and flavored with vanilla

## Directions

1. Line one-pound baking-powder boxes with Pistachio Ice Cream
2. Sprinkle with Sultana raisins which have been soaked one hour in brandy
3. Fill centres with Vanilla Ice Cream or whipped cream, sweetened, and flavored with vanilla
4. Cover with Pistachio Ice Cream
5. Pack in salt and ice
6. Let stand one and one-half hours
