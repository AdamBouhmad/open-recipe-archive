## Ingredients

- yolks three eggs
- four tablespoons sugar
- one-eighth teaspoon salt
- one cup coffee infusion
- one-third cup heavy cream

## Directions

1. Beat yolks three eggs slightly, add four tablespoons sugar and one-eighth teaspoon salt, then add gradually one cup coffee infusion.
2. Cook in double boiler until mixture thickens.
3. Cool, and fold in one-third cup heavy cream beaten until stiff.
