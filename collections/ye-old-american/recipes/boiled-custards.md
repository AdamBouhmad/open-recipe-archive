## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- a quart of cold milk
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- bunch of peach leaves or a handful of broken up peach-kernels or bitter almonds
- yellow peel of a lemon
- handful of broken cinnamon
- vanilla bean
- a table-spoonful of rose-water
- maccaroon soaked in wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat eight eggs very light, omitting the whites of four
2. Mix them gradually with a quart of cold milk and a quarter of a pound of sugar
3. Put the mixture into a saucepan with a bunch of peach leaves, or a handful of broken up peach-kernels or bitter almonds; the yellow peel of a lemon, and a handful of broken cinnamon; or you may boil in it a vanilla bean
4. Set it on hot coals, and simmer it slowly, stirring it all the time
5. As soon as it comes to a boil, take it immediately off the fire, or it will curdle and be lumpy
6. Strain it: add a table-spoonful of rose-water, and put it into glass cups
7. You may lay in the bottom of each cup a maccaroon soaked in wine
8. Grate nutmeg over the top, and send it to table cold
9. Eat it with tarts or sweetmeats
