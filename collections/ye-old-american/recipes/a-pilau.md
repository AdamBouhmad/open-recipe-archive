## Ingredients

- large fine fowl
- slices of fat bacon or ham
- skewers
- two sliced onions
- white pepper
- mace
- pint of rice
- water

## Directions

1. Take a large fine fowl, and cover the breast with slices of fat bacon or ham, secured by skewers.
2. Put it into a stew-pan with two sliced onions.
3. Season it to your taste with white pepper and mace.
4. Have ready a pint of rice that has been well picked, washed, and soaked.
5. Cover the fowl with it.
6. Put in as much water as will well cover the whole.
7. Stew it about half an hour, or till the fowl and rice are thoroughly done; keeping the stew-pan closely covered.
8. Dish it all together, either with the rice covering the fowl, or laid round it in little heaps.
