## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls corn-starch, wet in a little cold water
- 3 eggs, well beaten—whites and yolks separately
- 1 cup of sugar
- Vanilla, lemon, or other essence
- 1 saltspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Heat the milk to boiling; stir in the corn-starch and salt, and boil together five minutes (in a farina-kettle), then add the yolks, beaten light, with the sugar; boil two minutes longer, stirring all the while; remove the mixture from the fire, and beat in the whipped whites while it is boiling hot.
2. Pour into a mould wet with cold water, and set in a cold place.
3. Eat with sugar and cream.
