## Ingredients

- Batter as for apple fritters
- One large pineapple

## Directions

1. Pare one large pineapple
2. Cut it in slices a quarter of an inch thick
3. Cut the slices in halves
4. Dip them into the batter and fry them
5. Serve as apple fritters
