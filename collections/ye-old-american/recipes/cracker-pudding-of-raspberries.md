## Ingredients

- one large teacupful of cracker crumbs
- one quart of milk
- one spoonful of flour
- a pinch of salt
- yolks of three eggs
- one whole egg
- half a cupful of sugar
- vanilla
- a pint of well-sugared raspberries
- whites of three eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar
- lemon extract

## Directions

1. Mix one large teacupful of cracker crumbs, one quart of milk, one spoonful of flour, a pinch of salt, the yolks of three eggs, one whole egg and half a cupful of sugar
2. Flavor with vanilla, adding a little pinch of salt
3. Bake in a moderate oven
4. When done, spread over the top, while hot, a pint of well-sugared raspberries
5. Beat the whites of the three eggs very stiff, with two tablespoonfuls of sugar, a little lemon extract, or whatever one prefers
6. Spread this over the berries and bake a light brown
7. Serve with fruit sauce made of raspberries
