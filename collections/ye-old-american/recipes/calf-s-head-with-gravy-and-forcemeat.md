## Ingredients

- pounded cloves
- mace
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- red wine
- mushroom catsup
- walnut catsup
- butter
- brown flour
- meat
- gravy
- paste
- brains
- grated bread
- egg yolk
- eggs
- brain cakes
- forcemeat balls
- parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil pounded cloves, mace, salt, and cayenne pepper together till reduced to a pint, strain it, and add two gills of red wine, one of mushroom and one of walnut catsup.
2. Thicken it with butter and brown flour.
3. The head must be cut in small pieces and stewed a few minutes in the gravy.
4. Put a paste round the edge of a deep dish, three folds, one on the other, but none on the bottom.
5. Pour in the meat and gravy, and bake it till the paste is done.
6. Pick all strings from the brains, pound them, and add grated bread, pepper and salt.
7. Make them in little cakes with the yelk of an egg, fry them a nice brown.
8. Boil six egg's hard, leave one whole and divide the others exactly in two.
9. Have some bits of paste nicely baked.
10. When the head is taken from the oven, lay the whole egg in the middle, and dispose the others, with the brain cakes and bits of paste tastily around it.
11. If it be wanted as soup, do not reduce the gravy so much, and after stewing the head, serve it in a tureen with the brain cakes and forcemeat balls fried, in place of the eggs and paste.
12. The tongue should be salted and put in brine; they are very delicate, and four of them boiled and pealed, and served with four small chickens boiled, make a handsome dish, either cold or hot, with parsley and butter poured over them.
