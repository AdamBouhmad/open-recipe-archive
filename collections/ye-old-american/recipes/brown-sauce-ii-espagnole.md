## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 1 slice carrot
- 1 slice onion
- Bit of bay leaf
- Sprig of thyme
- Sprig of parsley
- 6 peppercorns
- 5 tablespoons flour
- 2 cups Brown Stock
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook butter with carrot, onion, bay leaf, thyme, parsley, and peppercorns, until brown, stirring constantly, care being taken that butter is not allowed to burn.
2. Add flour, and when well browned, add stock gradually.
3. Bring to boiling-point, strain, and season with salt and pepper.
