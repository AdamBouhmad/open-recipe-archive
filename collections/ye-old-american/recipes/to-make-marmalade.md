## Ingredients

- two pounds of quinces
- three quarters of a pound of sugar
- a pint of springwater

## Directions

1. Put them over the fire, and boil them till they are tender
2. Take them up and bruize them
3. Put them into the liquor, let it boil three quarters of an hour
4. Then put it into your pots or saucers
