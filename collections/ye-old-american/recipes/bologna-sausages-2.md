## Ingredients

- one pound of bacon
- one pound veal
- one pound pork
- one pound suet
- grated bread or boiled rice
- skins
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take one pound of bacon--fat and lean, one ditto veal, do., pork, do., suet, chop all fine
2. Season highly
3. Fill the skins, prick and boil them an hour, and hang them to dry
4. Grated bread or boiled rice may be added
5. Clean the skins with salt and vinegar
