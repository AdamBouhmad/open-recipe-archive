## Ingredients

- one quart of thick cream
- yolks of eight eggs
- half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- rind of three large lemons
- juice of the lemons

## Directions

1. Beat well together a quart of thick cream and the yolks of eight eggs
2. Gradually beat in half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar and the grated rind of three large lemons
3. Put the mixture into a porcelain skillet and set it on hot coals till it comes to a boil
4. Take it off and stir it till nearly cold
5. Squeeze the juice of the lemons into a bowl
6. Pour the cream upon it and continue to stir it till quite cold
7. You may serve it up in a glass bowl, in glass cups, or in jelly glasses
8. Eat it with tarts or sweetmeats
