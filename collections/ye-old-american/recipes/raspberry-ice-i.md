## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 1⅔ cups sugar
- 2 cups raspberry juice
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make a syrup as for Lemon Ice
2. cool, add raspberries mashed, and squeezed through double cheese-cloth, and lemon juice
3. strain and freeze
