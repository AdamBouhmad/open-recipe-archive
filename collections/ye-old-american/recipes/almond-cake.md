## Ingredients

- four ounces of shelled sweet almonds
- two ounces of shelled bitter almonds
- rose-water
- half a pound of flour
- a pound of loaf-sugar
- thirteen eggs
- a grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Blanch, and pound in a mortar four ounces of shelled sweet almonds and two ounces of shelled bitter ones; adding, as you proceed, sufficient rose-water to make them light and white
2. Sift half a pound of flour, and powder a pound of loaf-sugar
3. Beat thirteen eggs; and when they are as light as possible, stir into them alternately the almonds, sugar, and flour; adding a grated nutmeg
4. Butter a large square pan; put in the mixture, and bake it in a brisk oven about half an hour, less or more, according to its thickness
5. When cool, ice it
