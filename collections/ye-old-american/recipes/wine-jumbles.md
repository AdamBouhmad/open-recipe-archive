## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- three eggs
- one wine-glass of wine
- one spoonful of vanilla
- flour

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar with eggs and wine
2. Add one spoonful of vanilla
3. Add flour enough to roll out
4. Roll as thin as the blade of a knife and cut with an oval cutter
5. Bake on tin-sheets in a quick oven until a dark brown
