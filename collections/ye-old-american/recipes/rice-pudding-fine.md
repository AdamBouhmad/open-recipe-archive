## Ingredients

- a teacupful of rice
- two teacupfuls of water
- three tablespoonfuls of butter
- five tablespoonful of sugar
- five eggs well beaten
- one tablespoonful of powdered nutmeg
- a little salt
- one glass of wine
- a quarter of a pound of raisins stoned and cut in halves
- a quarter of a pound of Zante currants
- a quarter of a pound of citron cut in slips
- one quart of cream

## Directions

1. Wash a teacupful of rice and boil it in two teacupfuls of water
2. Add, while the rice is hot, three tablespoonfuls of butter, five tablespoonful of sugar, five eggs well beaten, one tablespoonful of powdered nutmeg, a little salt, one glass of wine, a quarter of a pound of raisins stoned and cut in halves, a quarter of a pound of Zante currants, a quarter of a pound of citron cut in slips, and one quart of cream
3. Mix well, pour into a buttered dish and bake an hour in a moderate oven
