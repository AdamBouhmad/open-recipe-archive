## Ingredients

- porterhouse or cross-cut of the rump steak cut one and three-fourths inches thick
- butter
- Duchess Potatoes
- salt, pepper, and finely chopped parsley
- sautéd mushroom caps
- halves of small tomatoes
- cucumber slices

## Directions

1. Wipe, remove superfluous fat, and pan broil seven minutes a porterhouse or cross-cut of the rump steak cut one and three-fourths inches thick.
2. Butter a plank and arrange a border of Duchess Potatoes close to edge, using a pastry bag and rose tube.
3. Remove steak to plank, put in a hot oven, and bake until steak is cooked and potatoes are browned.
4. Spread steak with butter, sprinkle with salt, pepper, and finely chopped parsley.
5. Garnish top of steak with sautéd mushroom caps.
6. Put around steak at equal distances halves of small tomatoes sautéd in butter, and on top of each tomato a circular slice of cucumber.
