## Ingredients

- coral and spawn of a boiled lobster
- one tablespoonful of butter
- nearly a quarter of a pound of sweet butter
- one wine-glass of water or vinegar
- one teaspoonful of made mustard
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Put the coral and spawn of a boiled lobster into a mortar with a tablespoonful of butter
2. Pound it to a smooth mass, then rub it through a sieve
3. Melt nearly a quarter of a pound of sweet butter, with a wine-glass of water or vinegar
4. Add a teaspoonful of made mustard
5. Stir in the coral and spawn, and a little salt and pepper
6. Stir it until it is smooth and serve
