## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- pepper
- salt
- beaten egg
- cracker-crumbs
- hot dripping or lard
- butter or dripping
- boiling water
- browned flour

## Directions

1. Sprinkle a little pepper and salt over them; dip in beaten egg, then roll in cracker-crumbs, and fry in hot dripping or lard.
2. If you use butter or dripping, add a little boiling water to the gravy when the meat is dished; thicken with browned flour, boil up once, sending to table in a boat.
