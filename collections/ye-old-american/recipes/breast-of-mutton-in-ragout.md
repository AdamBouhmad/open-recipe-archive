## Ingredients

- brown flour
- rich gravy
- forcemeat balls fried

## Directions

1. Prepare the breast as for boiling, brown it nicely in the oven
2. Have a rich gravy well seasoned and thickened with brown flour
3. Stew the mutton in it till sufficiently done
4. Garnish with forcemeat balls fried
