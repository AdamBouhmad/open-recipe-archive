## Ingredients

- pork cutlets from the leg
- salt
- pepper
- beaten egg
- bread-crumbs
- minced onion
- sage
- lard or drippings
- flour
- apple-sauce

## Directions

1. Cut them from the leg, and remove the skin.
2. Trim them and beat them, and sprinkle on salt and pepper.
3. Prepare some beaten egg in a pan; and on a flat dish a mixture of bread-crumbs, minced onion, and sage.
4. Put some lard or drippings into a frying-pan over the fire; and when it boils, put in the cutlets; having dipped every one first in the egg, and then in the seasoning.
5. Fry them twenty or thirty minutes, turning them often.
6. After you have taken them out of the frying-pan, skim the gravy, dredge in a little flour, give it one boil, and then pour it on the dish round the cutlets.
7. Have apple-sauce to eat with them.
