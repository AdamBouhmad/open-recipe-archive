## Ingredients

- salt salmon or salt fish
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak salmon in tepid or cold water twenty-four hours, changing water several times, or let stand under faucet of running water
2. If in a hurry, or desiring a very salt relish, it may do to soak a short time, having water warm, and changing, parboiling slightly
3. At the hour wanted, broil sharply
4. Season to suit taste, covering with butter
