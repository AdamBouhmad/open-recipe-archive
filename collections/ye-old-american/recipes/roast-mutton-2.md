## Ingredients

- mutton
- water
- salt
- flour
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Wash the meat well, and dry with a clean cloth
2. Let your fire be clear and strong; put the meat on with a little water in the dripping-pan
3. Let there be a cupful of boiling water dashed over the meat when it is first put down to roast, and left to trickle into the pan
4. Allow, in roasting, about twelve minutes per pound, if the fire is good
5. Baste often—at first with salt and water, afterward with the gravy
6. If it is in danger of browning too fast, cover with a large sheet of white paper
7. Skim the gravy well, and thicken very slightly with browned flour
8. Serve with currant jelly
