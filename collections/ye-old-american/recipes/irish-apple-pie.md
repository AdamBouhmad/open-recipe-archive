## Ingredients

- apples
- brown sugar
- pounded cloves and cinnamon, or grated lemon peel
- A little quince marmalade
- A little water
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Pare and take out the cores of the apples, cutting each apple into four or eight pieces, according to their size
2. Lay them neatly in a baking dish, seasoning them with brown sugar and any spice, such as pounded cloves and cinnamon, or grated lemon peel
3. A little quince marmalade gives a fine flavor to the pie
4. Add a little water
5. cover with puff paste
6. Bake for an hour
