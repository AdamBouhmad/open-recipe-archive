## Ingredients

- Circular pieces of sponge cake
- Butter
- Canned peaches
- Powdered sugar
- Few drops lemon juice
- Slight grating nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Sauté circular pieces of sponge cake in butter until delicately browned.
2. Drain canned peaches, sprinkle with powdered sugar, few drops lemon juice, and slight grating nutmeg.
3. Melt one tablespoon butter, add peaches, and when heated, serve on cake.
