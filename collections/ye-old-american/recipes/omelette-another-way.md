## Ingredients

- six eggs
- salt
- chopped parsley
- four ounces of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Break six eggs
2. Leave out half the whites
3. Beat them with a fork
4. Add some salt and chopped parsley
5. Take four ounces of fresh butter
6. Cut half of it in small pieces and put them in the omelette
7. Put the other half in a small frying pan
8. When melted, pour in the eggs
9. Stir till it begins to set
10. Turn it up round the edges
11. When done, put a plate on and turn the pan up so that it may not break
