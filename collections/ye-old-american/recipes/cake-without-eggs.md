## Ingredients

- One teacupful of butter
- Three teacupfuls of sugar
- One pound of raisins
- One pint of sifted flour
- One teaspoonful of nutmeg
- One teaspoonful of powdered cinnamon
- One pint of thick sour cream or milk
- One teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Beat together butter and sugar until quite light
2. Stir in sifted flour
3. Add raisins seeded and chopped, mixed with sifted flour
4. Add nutmeg and powdered cinnamon
5. Dissolve soda in sour cream or milk and add lastly
6. Bake immediately in buttered tins one hour in a moderate oven
