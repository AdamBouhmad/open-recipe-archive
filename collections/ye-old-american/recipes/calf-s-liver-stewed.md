## Ingredients

- calf's liver
- salt and water
- cold water
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- sweet marjoram
- parsley
- Worcestershire sauce
- butter
- browned flour
- lemon-juice
- currant jelly
- wine

## Directions

1. Slice the liver and lay in salt and water an hour
2. Cut into dice and put over the fire with enough cold water to cover it well
3. Cover and stew steadily for an hour
4. Add salt, pepper, a little mace, sweet marjoram, parsley, and a teaspoonful Worcestershire sauce
5. Stew again steadily, not fast, for half an hour longer
6. Put in a tablespoonful of butter and two of browned flour wet with cold water
7. Add a teaspoonful of lemon-juice and one of currant jelly
8. Boil five minutes longer and dish
