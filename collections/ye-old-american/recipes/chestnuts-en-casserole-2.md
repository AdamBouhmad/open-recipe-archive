## Ingredients

- 3 cups chestnuts
- 3 cups highly seasoned chicken stock
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 1/2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Remove shells from chestnuts, put in a casserole dish
2. Pour over three cups highly seasoned chicken stock
3. Cover and cook in a slow oven three hours
4. Thicken chicken stock with two tablespoons butter and one and one-half tablespoons flour cooked together
5. Send to table in casserole dish
