## Ingredients

- lavender
- rosemary
- sage
- wormwood
- rue
- mint
- 4 quarts very strong vinegar
- garlic

## Directions

1. Take lavender, rosemary, sage, wormwood, rue, and mint, of each a large handful
2. Put them in a pot of earthen ware
3. Pour on them four quarts of very strong vinegar
4. Cover the pot closely, and put a board on the top
5. Keep it in the hottest sun two weeks
6. Strain and bottle it, putting in each bottle a clove of garlic
7. When it has settled in the bottle and become clear, pour it off gently
8. Do this until you get it all free from sediment
