## Ingredients

- Grated rind and juice ½ lemon
- ½ cup white wine or ¼ cup Sherry
- ⅓ cup sugar
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix lemon, wine, sugar, and yolks of eggs; stir vigorously over fire until it thickens, using a wire whisk; pour on to whites of eggs beaten stiff.
