## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the cabbage in cold water, removing the outside leaves, and cutting the stalk close.
2. Examine all the leaves carefully, lest insects should be lodged among them.
3. If the cabbage is large, divide it into quarters.
4. Put it into a pot of boiling water with a handful of salt, and boil it till the stalk is quite tender.
5. Half an hour will generally be sufficient for a small young cabbage; an hour for a large full-grown one.
6. Drain it well before you dish it.
7. Have ready some melted butter to eat with it.
