## Ingredients

- fair sweet apples
- two eggs well beaten per teacupful of apple
- two tablespoonfuls of fine sugar per teacupful of apple
- one of melted butter per teacupful of apple
- grated rind and half the juice of one lemon
- half a wine-glass of brandy
- one teacupful of milk

## Directions

1. Select fair sweet apples, pare and grate them
2. to every teacupful of the apple add two eggs well beaten, two tablespoonfuls of fine sugar, one of melted butter, the grated rind and half the juice of one lemon, half a wine-glass of brandy and one teacupful of milk
3. mix all well
4. pour into a deep plate lined with paste
5. put a strip of the paste around the edge of the dish
6. bake thirty minutes
