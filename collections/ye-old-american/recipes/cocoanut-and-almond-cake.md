## Ingredients

- two and one-half cups powdered sugar
- one cup butter
- four full cups prepared flour
- whites of seven eggs
- one small cup of milk
- a mere pinch of soda
- one grated cocoanut
- one-half teaspoonful nutmeg
- juice and half the grated peel of one lemon
- Filling: one pound sweet almonds
- whites of four eggs
- one heaping cup powdered sugar
- two teaspoonfuls rose-water

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Stir in lemon and nutmeg
3. Mix well
4. Add the milk and whites and flour alternately
5. Stir in the grated cocoanut swiftly and lightly
6. Bake in four jelly-cake tins
7. For filling, blanch the almonds
8. Let them get cold and dry, then pound in a Wedgewood mortar, adding rose-water as you go
9. Save about two dozen to shred for the top
10. Beat whites of four eggs stiff
11. Stir the almond paste into the icing after it is made
12. Spread between the cooled cakes
13. Make that for the top a trifle thicker and lay it on heavily
14. When it has stiffened somewhat, stick the shred almonds closely over it
15. Set in the oven to harden, but do not let it scorch
