## Ingredients

- Puff or plain paste
- One-third pound Jordan almonds blanched and finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons cracker rolled and sifted
- 3 eggs slightly beaten
- One-third cup sugar
- One-third teaspoon salt
- 2 cups milk
- One-half teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Line patty pans with puff or plain paste.
2. Blanch and finely chop one-third pound Jordan almonds.
3. Add two tablespoons cracker rolled and sifted, three eggs slightly beaten, one-third cup sugar, one-third teaspoon salt, two cups milk, and one-half teaspoon vanilla.
4. Fill patty pans with mixture and bake in a moderate oven until firm.
