## Ingredients

- leeks
- boiling salted water
- buttered toast
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and trim leeks, cook in boiling salted water until soft, and drain.
2. Arrange on pieces of buttered toast and pour over melted butter, seasoned with salt and pepper.
