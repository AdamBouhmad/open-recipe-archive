## Ingredients

- 4 teaspoons tea
- 2 cups boiling water
- cracked ice
- sugar
- lemon slice

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for making tea
2. Strain into glasses one-third full of cracked ice
3. Sweeten to taste
4. Allow one slice lemon to each glass of tea
5. Chill the infusion quickly for finer flavor
