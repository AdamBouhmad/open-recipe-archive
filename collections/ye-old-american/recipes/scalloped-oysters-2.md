## Ingredients

- cracker crumbs
- butter
- oysters
- oyster liquor
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- milk

## Directions

1. Have ready about a pint of fine cracker crumbs
2. Butter a deep earthen dish
3. Put a layer of the cracker crumbs on the bottom
4. Wet this with some of the oyster liquor
5. Next have a layer of oysters
6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and lay small bits of butter upon them
7. Then add another layer of cracker crumbs and oyster juice
8. Then oysters, pepper, salt and butter, and so on, until the dish is full, with the top layer to be cracker crumbs
9. Beat up an egg in a cup of milk and turn over all
10. Cover the dish and set it in the oven for thirty or forty-five minutes
11. When baked through, uncover the top, set on the upper grate and brown
