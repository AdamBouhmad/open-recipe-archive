## Ingredients

- strawberries
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Pick over, wash, drain, and hull strawberries; then weigh.
2. Fill glass jars with berries.
3. Make a syrup same as for Damson Preserve, cooking the syrup fifteen minutes.
4. Add syrup to overflow jars; let stand fifteen minutes, when fruit will have shrunk, and more fruit must be added to fill jars.
5. Screw on covers, put on a trivet in a kettle of cold water, heat water to boiling-point, and keep just below boiling-point one hour.
