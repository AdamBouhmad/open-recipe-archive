## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 tablespoons thick cream
- 2 tablespoons fine stale bread crumbs
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix cream, bread crumbs, and salt.
2. Put one-half tablespoon of mixture in egg-shirrer.
3. Slip in egg, and cover with remaining mixture.
4. Bake six minutes in moderate oven.
