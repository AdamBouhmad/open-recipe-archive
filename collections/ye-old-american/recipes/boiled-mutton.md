## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- salt
- water
- butter
- capers or nasturtium seed or cucumber or gherkin-pickle

## Directions

1. Wash a leg of mutton clean, and wipe dry
2. Do not leave the knuckle and shank so long as to be unshapely
3. Put into a pot with hot water (salted) enough to cover it, and boil until you ascertain, by probing with a fork, that it is tender in the thickest part
4. Skim off all the scum as it rises
5. Allow about twelve minutes to each pound
6. Take from the fire, drain perfectly dry
7. Serve with melted butter, with capers, or nasturtium seed; or, if you have neither of these, some cucumber or gherkin-pickle stirred into it
