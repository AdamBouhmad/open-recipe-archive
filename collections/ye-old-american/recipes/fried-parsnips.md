## Ingredients

- parsnips
- flour
- hot dripping or lard
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil until tender, scrape off the skin, and cut in thick lengthwise slices.
2. Dredge with flour and fry in hot dripping or lard, turning when one side is browned.
3. Drain off every drop of fat; pepper, and serve hot.
