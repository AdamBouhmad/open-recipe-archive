## Ingredients

- two cakes as for Washington pie
- one cup of sweet cream
- three tablespoonfuls of white sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Make two cakes as for Washington pie
2. Take one cup of sweet cream and three tablespoonfuls of white sugar
3. Beat with egg-beater or fork till it is stiff enough to put on without running off and flavor with vanilla
4. Put between the cakes and on top
