## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a cupful of sago
- one quart of milk
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- one cupful of white sugar
- flavoring
- yolks of four eggs
- whites of four eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of powdered white sugar
- raspberry or strawberry jam

## Directions

1. Wash the sago and put it into one quart of milk in a saucepan
2. Let it stand in boiling water on the stove or range until the sago has well swelled
3. While hot, put in two tablespoonfuls of butter with one cupful of white sugar and flavoring
4. When cool, add the well-beaten yolks of four eggs
5. Put in a buttered pudding-dish, and bake from half to three-quarters of an hour
6. Remove it from the oven and place it to cool
7. Beat the whites of the eggs with three tablespoonfuls of powdered white sugar till they are a mass of froth
8. Spread the pudding with either raspberry or strawberry jam, and then spread on the frosting
9. Put in the oven for two minutes to slightly brown
