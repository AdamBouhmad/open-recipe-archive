## Ingredients

- nice fresh white cabbage
- cider vinegar
- fresh butter
- flour
- salt
- cayenne
- eggs

## Directions

1. Take a nice fresh white cabbage, wash, and drain it, and cut off the stalk
2. Shave down the head evenly and nicely into very small shreds, with a cabbage-cutter, or a sharp knife
3. Put it into a deep china dish
4. Take a large half-pint of the best cider vinegar, and mix with it a quarter of a pound of fresh butter, divided into four bits, and rolled in flour; a small salt-spoon of salt, and the same quantity of cayenne
5. Stir all this well together, and boil it in a small saucepan
6. Have ready the yolks of four eggs well beaten
7. As soon as the mixture has come to a hard boil, take it off the fire, and stir in the beaten egg
8. Pour it boiling hot over the shred cabbage, and mix it well, all through, with a spoon
9. Set it to cool on ice or snow, or in the open air, and serve quite cold
