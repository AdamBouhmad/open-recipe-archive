## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Wash, pare, and thinly slice potatoes, using vegetable slicer
2. Let stand one-half hour in cold water, then drain, and dry between towels
3. Arrange in layers in a well buttered iron frying-pan, having pan three-fourths full
4. season each layer with salt and pepper, and brush over with melted butter
5. Cook in a moderate oven until soft and well browned
