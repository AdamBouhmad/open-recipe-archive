## Ingredients

- strawberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. After the berries are picked over, place as many as can be put carefully in the preserve kettle at once on a platter
2. To each pound of fruit add three-fourths of a pound of sugar
3. Let them stand two or three hours, till the juice is drawn from them
4. Pour the juice into the kettle and let it come to a boil
5. Remove the scum which rises
6. Put in the berries very carefully
7. As soon as they come thoroughly to a boil put them in warm jars and seal while boiling hot
