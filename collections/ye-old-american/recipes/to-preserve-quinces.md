## Ingredients

- their weight of loaf sugar
- one quart of water
- your Quinces
- oranges (if you like)

## Directions

1. Put into a bell-metal kettle or sauce pan, with one quart of water, scald and skim it till it is very clear
2. Put in your Quinces, let them boil in the sirrup for half an hour
3. Add oranges as before if you like
4. Put them in stone pots for use
