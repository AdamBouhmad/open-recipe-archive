## Ingredients

- oranges
- sugar
- water
- beaten white of egg
- brandy paper

## Directions

1. Grate the rind from the remaining oranges and put it aside
2. Quarter the oranges and take out all the pulp and juice, removing the seeds and core
3. Put the sugar into a preserving kettle with a half pint of clear water to each pound
4. Mix the sugar with some beaten white of egg, allowing one white of egg to every two pounds of sugar
5. When the sugar is all dissolved, put it on the fire and boil and skim it till it is quite clear and thick
6. Take the boiled parings and pound them to a paste in a mortar
7. Put this paste into the sugar and boil and stir it ten minutes
8. Put in the pulp and juice of the oranges and the grated rind
9. Boil all together for about half an hour till it is a transparent mass
10. When cold, put it up in glass jars, laying brandy paper on the top
