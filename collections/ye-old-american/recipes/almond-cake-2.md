## Ingredients

- 1 lb. powdered sugar
- 1 lb. flour
- ¼ lb. butter
- 8 eggs
- 1 coffee-cupful sweet almonds, blanched by putting them into hot water, stripped of their skins and beaten to a smooth paste in a Wedgewood mortar with rose-water and half a teaspoonful essence of bitter almonds
- brandy

## Directions

1. Beat whites and yolks separately
2. Stir butter and sugar to a cream
3. Add to this the yolks
4. Beat very hard before putting in the flour
5. Stir in the almond-paste alternately with the whites
6. Put in the brandy last
7. Season the icing with rose-water
