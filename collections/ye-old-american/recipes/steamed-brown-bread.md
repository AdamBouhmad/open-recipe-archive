## Ingredients

- one cup of white flour
- two of Graham flour
- two of Indian meal
- one teaspoonful of soda
- one cup of molasses
- three and a half cups of milk
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Beat well
2. steam for four hours
3. slip from the mold and set into the oven fifteen minutes
4. eat warm with butter
