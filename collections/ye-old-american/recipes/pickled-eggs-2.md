## Ingredients

- three dozen eggs
- vinegar
- white pepper
- allspice
- root ginger
- white mustard seed
- black mustard seed
- race ginger
- garlic
- horse-radish
- whole cloves
- green peppers

## Directions

1. Boil quite hard three dozen eggs
2. Drop in cold water and remove the shells
3. Pack them when entirely cold in a wide-mouthed jar, large enough to let them in or out without breaking
4. Take as much vinegar as you think will cover them entirely and boil it in white pepper, allspice, and a little root ginger
5. Pack eggs in stone or wide-mouthed glass jars, occasionally putting in a tablespoonful of white and black mustard seed mixed, a small piece of race ginger, garlic if liked, horse-radish ungrated, whole cloves, and a very little allspice
6. Slice two or three green peppers and add in very small quantities
7. They will be fit for use in eight or ten days
