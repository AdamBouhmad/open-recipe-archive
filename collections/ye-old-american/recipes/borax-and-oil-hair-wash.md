## Ingredients

- one penny's worth of borax
- half a pint of olive oil
- one pint of boiling water

## Directions

1. Pour the boiling water over the borax and oil
2. let it cool
3. then put the mixture into a bottle
4. Shake it before using
5. apply it with a flannel
