## Ingredients

- four ounces of shalots, or small button onions
- a quart of the best vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and chop fine four ounces of shalots, or small button onions.
2. Pour on them a quart of the best vinegar, and let them steep a fortnight.
3. Then strain and bottle it.
