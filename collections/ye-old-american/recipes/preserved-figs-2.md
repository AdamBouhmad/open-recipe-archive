## Ingredients

- ripe figs
- sugar
- lemon
- ginger
- water

## Directions

1. Cover the figs with cold water for twelve hours.
2. Then simmer in water enough to cover them until tender, and spread out upon a sieve to cool and harden.
3. Make a syrup of the sugar, and a cup of cold water for every pound.
4. Boil until clear of scum; put in the figs and simmer ten minutes.
5. Take them out and spread upon dishes in the sun.
6. Add the lemons and ginger; boil the syrup thick; give the figs another boil of fifteen minutes, and fill the jars three-quarters of the way to the top.
7. Fill up with boiling syrup, cover, and, when cold, seal up.
