## Ingredients

- 8 fine pippins, pared, cored, and sliced
- ½ cup very fine bread-crumbs
- 2 teaspoonfuls butter, melted
- 5 eggs
- ¾ cup sugar
- 1 oz. citron, shred finely
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg, and a dozen whole cloves
- 1 cup milk or cream

## Directions

1. Soak the bread-crumbs in the milk, cream the butter and sugar, and beat into this the yolks.
2. Next, adding the milk and soaked bread, stir until very smooth and light.
3. Put in the nutmeg and citron, and whip in the whites lightly.
4. Butter a deep dish, and put in your sliced apple, sprinkling each piece well with sugar, and scattering the cloves among them.
5. Pour the custard you have prepared over them, and bake three-quarters of an hour.
6. Sift powdered sugar over the top, and eat cold.
