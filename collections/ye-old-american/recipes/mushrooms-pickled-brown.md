## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- salt
- allegar
- ale vinegar
- cloves
- allspice
- whole pepper
- onion
- silver spoon

## Directions

1. Take a quart of large mushrooms and trim off the stalks
2. Rub them with a flannel cloth dipped in salt
3. Lay them in a pan of allegar or ale vinegar, for a quarter of an hour
4. Wash them about in it
5. Put them into a sauce-pan with a quart of allegar, a quarter of an ounce of cloves, the same of allspice and whole pepper, and a tea-spoonful of salt
6. Set the pan over coals, and let the mushrooms stew slowly for ten minutes, keeping the pan well covered
7. Take them off, let them get cold by degrees
8. Put them into small bottles with the allegar strained from the spice and poured upon them
9. Boil an onion with the mushrooms; if it turns black or blueish, infer that there is a poisonous one among them and throw them away
10. Stir them with a silver spoon
