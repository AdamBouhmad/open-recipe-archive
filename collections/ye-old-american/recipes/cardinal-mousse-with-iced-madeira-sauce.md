## Ingredients

- Pomegranate Ice
- three-fourths cup sugar
- one-third cup hot water
- whites two eggs
- one and one-half teaspoons granulated gelatine
- two tablespoons boiling water
- two cups cream
- one teaspoon vanilla
- ¼ cup orange juice
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- ½ cup Madeira wine
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- Whites 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Line a mould with Pomegranate Ice
2. Make Italian Meringue of three-fourths cup sugar, one-third cup hot water, whites two eggs, and one and one-half teaspoons granulated gelatine dissolved in two tablespoons boiling water
3. Beat until cold, and fold in whip from two cups cream
4. Flavor with one teaspoon vanilla
5. Fill the mould with mixture
6. Cover, pack in salt and ice, and let stand three hours
7. For Iced Madeira Sauce, freeze fruit juice and wine
8. Boil sugar and water, pour on slowly to beaten whites of eggs
9. Set in pan of salted ice-water, and stir until cold
10. Add to frozen mixture
