## Ingredients

- a dozen large soft clams in the shell
- three dozen opened clams
- white pepper
- minced celery
- fat bacon
- cracker dust
- table butter

## Directions

1. Purchase a dozen large soft clams in the shell and three dozen opened clams
2. Ask the dealer to open the first dozen, care being used not to injure the shells
3. Clean the shells well, and put two soft clams on each half shell
4. add to each a dash of white pepper, and half a teaspoonful of minced celery
5. Cut a slice of fat bacon into the smallest dice, add four of these to each shell
6. strew over the top a thin layer of cracker dust
7. place a piece of table butter on top, and bake in the oven until brown
