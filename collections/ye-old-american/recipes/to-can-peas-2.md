## Ingredients

- peas
- water

## Directions

1. Fill the can full of peas
2. Shake the can so they can be filled well
3. Pour into the cans enough cold water to fill to overflowing
4. Screw the cover tight as you can with your thumb and first finger
5. Proceed exactly as in canning corn
