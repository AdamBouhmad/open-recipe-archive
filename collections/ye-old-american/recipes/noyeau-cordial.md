## Ingredients

- one gallon of proof spirit
- three pounds of loaf sugar
- a tablespoonful of extract of almonds
- half a pint of apricot or peach juice

## Directions

1. To one gallon of proof spirit add three pounds of loaf sugar and a tablespoonful of extract of almonds
2. Mix well together and allow to stand forty-eight hours, covered closely
3. Strain through thick flannel and bottle
4. Add half a pint of apricot or peach juice
