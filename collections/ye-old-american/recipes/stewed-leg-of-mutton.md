## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- water
- gravy from bones, trimmings, and coarse pieces of meat
- carrots, sliced
- salt
- onions, small
- turnips, small
- tomatas or tomata catchup
- sweet marjoram
- mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. Take a leg of mutton and trim it nicely.
2. Put it into a pot with three pints of water; or with two pints of water and one quart of gravy drawn from bones, trimmings, and coarse pieces of meat.
3. Add some slices of carrots, and a little salt.
4. Stew it slowly three hours.
5. Then put in small onions, small turnips, tomatas or tomata catchup, and shred or powdered sweet marjoram to your taste, and let it stew three hours longer.
6. Serve it up with the vegetables and gravy round it, and have mashed potatoes in another dish.
