## Ingredients

- 1 quart pumpkin—stewed and strained
- 1 quart milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 7 eggs—beaten very light
- 1 teaspoonful ginger
- 1 teaspoonful mace
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat all well together and bake in crust.
