## Ingredients

- Three pounds of raw veal
- Butter the size of an egg
- Three eggs
- Three tablespoonfuls of cream or milk
- Four pounded crackers
- One teaspoonful of black pepper
- One large tablespoonful salt
- One large tablespoonful of sage

## Directions

1. Chop the veal very fine
2. Mix the eggs and cream together
3. Mix with the veal four pounded crackers, one teaspoonful of black pepper, one large tablespoonful salt, one large tablespoonful of sage
4. Mix well together and form into a loaf
5. Bake two and one-half hours, basting with butter and water while baking
6. Serve cut in thin slices
