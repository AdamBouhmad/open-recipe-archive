## Ingredients

- one orange
- a pinch of tartaric acid
- confectioners' sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the rind of one orange and squeeze the juice, taking care to reject the seeds
2. Add to this a pinch of tartaric acid
3. Stir in confectioners' sugar until it is stiff enough to form into balls the size of a small marble
