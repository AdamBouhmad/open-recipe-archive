## Ingredients

- beef, pickled
- pepper
- salt
- allspice

## Directions

1. Have your beef nicely pickled and let it stay in pickle a week
2. Take the thin, flanky pieces and put on a large potful
3. Let them boil until perfectly done
4. Pull to pieces
5. Season with pepper, salt and allspice
6. Put it in a coarse cloth and press down upon it some very heavy weight
