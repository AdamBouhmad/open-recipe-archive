## Ingredients

- three or four lumps of loaf sugar
- ice-water

## Directions

1. Dissolve three or four lumps of loaf sugar in a glass of ice-water.
2. Keep it ice-cold.
3. Take a teaspoonful every few minutes for a tickling in the throat or a hacking cough.
