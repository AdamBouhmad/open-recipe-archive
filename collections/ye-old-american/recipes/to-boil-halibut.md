## Ingredients

- halibut
- salt
- horse-radish
- parsley
- egg-sauce

## Directions

1. Score deeply the skin of the back of the halibut.
2. Put it into the kettle on the strainer with the back undermost.
3. Cover it with cold water, and throw in a handful of salt.
4. Do not let it come to a boil too fast.
5. Skim it carefully.
6. When it has boiled hard a few minutes, hang the kettle higher, or diminish the fire under it, so as to let it simmer for about thirty or thirty-five minutes.
7. Drain it, and send it to table.
8. Garnish with alternate heaps of grated horse-radish and curled parsley.
9. Accompany by a boat of egg-sauce.
