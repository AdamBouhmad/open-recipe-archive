## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold roast beef or rare steak finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- Worcestershire Sauce
- 2 tablespoons bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- yolk 1 egg slightly beaten
- flour
- Tomato Sauce

## Directions

1. Season beef with salt, pepper, onion juice, and Worcestershire Sauce
2. add remaining ingredients
3. shape after the form of small croquettes, pointed at ends
4. Roll in flour, egg, and crumbs
5. fry in deep fat
6. drain
7. serve with Tomato Sauce
