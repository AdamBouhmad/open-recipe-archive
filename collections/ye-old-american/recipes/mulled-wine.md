## Ingredients

- 1 pint of water
- 1 beaten nutmeg
- 2 sticks of cinnamon
- 1 table-spoonful of cloves
- 1 quart of wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil together in a pint of water a beaten nutmeg, two sticks of cinnamon broken up, and a table-spoonful of cloves slightly pounded
2. When reduced to one-half, strain the liquid into a quart of wine
3. Set it on hot coals
4. Take it off as soon as it comes to a boil
5. Sweeten it
6. Serve it up hot in a pitcher, surrounded by glass cups, and with it a plate of rusk
