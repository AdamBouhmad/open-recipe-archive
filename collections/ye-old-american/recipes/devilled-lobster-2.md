## Ingredients

- 2 lb. lobster
- White Sauce II
- salt
- cayenne
- lemon juice
- buttered crumbs
- mustard

## Directions

1. Prepare as Scalloped Lobster, using larger proportions of same seasonings, with the addition of mustard.
