## Ingredients

- chickens' livers
- thin slices of bacon
- small skewers

## Directions

1. Clean livers and cut each liver in six pieces.
2. Wrap a thin slice of bacon around each piece and fasten with a small skewer.
3. Put in a broiler and place over a dripping-pan.
4. Bake in a hot oven until bacon is crisp, turning once during cooking.
