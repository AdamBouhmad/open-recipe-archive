## Ingredients

- six lamb chops
- fried eggplant
- three tablespoons butter
- three and one-half tablespoons flour
- one cup rich Brown Stock
- three tablespoons lean raw ham
- one-half tablespoon butter
- two tablespoons Sherry wine
- two tablespoons finely shredded green pepper
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Broil six lamb chops, arrange on slices of fried eggplant.
2. Brown three tablespoons butter, add three and one-half tablespoons flour, and stir until well browned.
3. Add, gradually, one cup rich Brown Stock.
4. Cook three tablespoons lean raw ham cut in small cubes in one-half tablespoon butter two minutes.
5. Moisten with two tablespoons Sherry wine, and add to sauce with two tablespoons finely shredded green pepper.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Pour around the chops.
