## Ingredients

- six selected smelts
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- cream
- flour
- butter
- two tablespoons flour
- one cup White Stock
- one and one-third teaspoons Anchovy Essence
- one and one-half tablespoons butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean six selected smelts
2. Cut five diagonal gashes on each side
3. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice
4. Cover, and let stand ten minutes
5. Roll in cream
6. Dip in flour
7. Sauté in butter
8. Add to butter in pan two tablespoons flour, one cup White Stock, one and one-third teaspoons Anchovy Essence, and a few drops lemon juice
9. Just before sauce is poured around smelts, add one and one-half tablespoons butter and one teaspoon finely chopped parsley
