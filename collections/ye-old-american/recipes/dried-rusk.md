## Ingredients

- 1 pint of warm milk
- 2 eggs
- ½ teacup of butter
- Half a cup of yeast
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Set a sponge with the milk, yeast, and salt, stirring in flour until you have a thick batter
2. Early next morning add the well-beaten eggs, and flour enough to enable you to roll out the dough
3. Let this rise in the bread-bowl two hours
4. Roll into a sheet nearly an inch thick
5. Cut into round cakes
6. Arrange in your baking-pan two deep, laying one upon the other carefully
7. Let these stand for another half-hour
8. Bake
9. To dry: divide the twins, leaving one side of each cake soft, and pile them loosely in the pan, set them in the oven when the fire is declining for the night, and leave them in until morning
10. Put them in a clean muslin bag, and hang them up in the kitchen
11. They will be fit to eat upon the third day
