## Ingredients

- A grated pineapple
- sugar equal to the weight of the pineapple
- butter equal to half the weight of the pineapple
- one cupful of cream
- five eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the batter to a creamy froth
2. Add the sugar and yolks of the eggs
3. Continue beating till very light
4. Add the cream, the pineapple grated and the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth
5. Bake with an under crust
6. Eat cold
