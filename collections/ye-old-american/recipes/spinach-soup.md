## Ingredients

- 4 cups White Stock II or III
- 2 quarts spinach
- 3 cups boiling water
- 2 cups milk
- ¼ cup butter
- ⅓ cup flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- one-fourth teaspoon powdered sugar
- one-eighth teaspoon of soda

## Directions

1. Wash, pick over, and cook spinach thirty minutes in boiling water to which has been added one-fourth teaspoon powdered sugar and one-eighth teaspoon of soda
2. Drain, chop, and rub through sieve
3. Add stock, heat to boiling-point, bind, add milk, and season with salt and pepper
