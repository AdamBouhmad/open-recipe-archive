## Ingredients

- 1 lb. lean mutton or lamb, cut small
- 1 quart water, cold
- 1 tablespoonful rice, or barley, soaked in a very little warm water
- 4 tablespoonfuls milk
- salt and pepper
- a little chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Boil the meat, unsalted, in the water, keeping it closely covered, until it falls to pieces.
2. Strain it out, skim, add the soaked barley or rice.
3. Simmer half an hour, stirring often.
4. Stir in the seasoning and the milk, and simmer five minutes after it heats up well, taking care it does not burn.
5. Serve hot with cream crackers.
