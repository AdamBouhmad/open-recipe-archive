## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 quart of cider
- 1 handful of whole cloves
- sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Allow six eggs to a quart of cider
2. Put a handful of whole cloves into the cider, and boil it
3. While it is boiling, beat the eggs in a large pitcher
4. Add to them as much sugar as will make the cider very sweet
5. Pour the boiling liquor on the beaten egg
6. Continue to pour the mixture backwards and forwards from one pitcher to another, till it has a fine froth on it
7. Pour it warm into your glasses
8. Grate some nutmeg over each
