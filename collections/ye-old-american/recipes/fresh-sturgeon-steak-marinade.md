## Ingredients

- sturgeon steak
- hot water
- vinegar
- butter
- salt
- black pepper
- lemon
- egg
- bread crumbs
- fat
- French mustard

## Directions

1. Take one slice of sturgeon two inches thick
2. Let it stand in hot water five minutes
3. Drain, put it in a bowl and add a gill of vinegar, two tablespoonfuls of melted butter, half a teaspoonful of salt, a saltspoonful of black pepper and the juice of half a lemon
4. Let it stand six hours, turning it occasionally
5. Drain and dry on a napkin
6. Dip it in egg
7. Roll in bread crumbs and fry, or rather boil, in very hot fat
8. Beat up the yolks of two raw eggs, add a teaspoonful of French mustard, and by degrees, half of the marinade, to make a smooth sauce
9. Serve with the fish
