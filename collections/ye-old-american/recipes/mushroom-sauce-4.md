## Ingredients

- 1 teacupful young mushrooms
- 4 tablespoonfuls butter
- 1 teacupful cream or milk
- 1 teaspoonful flour
- Nutmeg, mace, and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Stew the mushrooms in barely enough water to cover them until tender. Drain, but do not press them, and add the cream, butter, and seasoning. Stew over a bright fire, stirring all the while until it begins to thicken. Add the flour wet in cold milk, boil up and serve in a boat, or pour over boiled chickens, rabbits, etc.
