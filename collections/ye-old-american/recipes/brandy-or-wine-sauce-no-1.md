## Ingredients

- a heaping teaspoonful of cornstarch
- cold water
- a cupful of boiling water
- one cupful of sugar
- a piece of butter as large as an egg
- half of a cupful of brandy or wine

## Directions

1. Stir a heaping teaspoonful of cornstarch in a little cold water to a smooth paste
2. Add to it a cupful of boiling water, with one cupful of sugar, a piece of butter as large as an egg
3. Boil all together ten minutes
4. Remove from the fire and when cool stir into it half of a cupful of brandy or wine
