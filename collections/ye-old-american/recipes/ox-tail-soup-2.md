## Ingredients

- 1 ox-tail
- 2 lbs. lean beef
- 4 carrots
- 3 onions
- Thyme
- butter
- pepper and salt
- two tablespoonfuls browned flour

## Directions

1. Cut the tail into several pieces and fry brown in butter
2. Slice the onions and two carrots, and when you remove the ox-tail from the frying-pan, put in these and brown them also
3. Tie them in a bag with a bunch of thyme and drop into the soup-pot
4. Lay the pieces of ox-tail in the same; then the meat cut into small slices
5. Grate over them the two whole carrots, and add four quarts of cold water, with pepper and salt
6. Boil from four to six hours, in proportion to the size of the tail
7. Strain fifteen minutes before serving it, and thicken with two tablespoonfuls of browned flour
8. Boil ten minutes longer
