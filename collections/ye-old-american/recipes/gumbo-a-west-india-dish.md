## Ingredients

- young pods of okra
- water
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Gather young pods of okra
2. Wash them clean
3. Put them in a pan with a little water, salt and pepper
4. Stew them till tender
5. Serve them with melted butter
