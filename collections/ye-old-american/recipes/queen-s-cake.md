## Ingredients

- One cupful of butter
- Three cupfuls of white sugar
- Yolks of six eggs
- One cupful of milk
- Two teaspoonfuls of vanilla or lemon extract
- Four cupfuls of flour
- Two heaping teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- One teaspoonful of soda
- One tablespoonful of warm water
- Whites of six eggs

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar together well
2. Add egg yolks and milk
3. Add vanilla or lemon extract
4. Mix thoroughly
5. Add cream of tartar to flour and sift gently over the cake while stirring
6. Dissolve soda in warm water and add to mixture, stirring well
7. Beat egg whites to stiff foam and stir in gently
8. Bake slowly immediately after adding soda and egg whites
