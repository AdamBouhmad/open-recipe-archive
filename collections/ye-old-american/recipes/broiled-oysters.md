## Ingredients

- oysters (large, fat)
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- cracker-dust or flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Choose large, fat oysters.
2. Wipe them very dry.
3. Sprinkle salt and cayenne pepper upon them.
4. You can dredge the oyster with cracker-dust or flour if you wish to have it brown.
5. Butter the gridiron well.
6. Let your fire be hot and clear.
7. If the oyster drip, withdraw the gridiron for an instant until the smoke clears away.
8. Broil quickly and dish hot.
9. Put a tiny piece of butter, not larger than a pea, upon each oyster.
