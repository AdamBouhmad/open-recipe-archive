## Ingredients

- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 cup rye flakes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ⅓ cup molasses
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- 1 yeast cake dissolved in 1 cup lukewarm water
- Flour

## Directions

1. Make same as Rolled Oats Bread.
