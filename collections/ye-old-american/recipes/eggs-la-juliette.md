## Ingredients

- Truffles
- Cold boiled tongue
- Pistachio nuts, blanched and split
- Aspic jelly
- Poached egg yolk

## Directions

1. Decorate egg-shaped individual moulds with truffles, and cold boiled tongue cut in fancy shapes, and pistachio nuts blanched and split.
2. Line mould with aspic jelly, drop in a poached egg yolk, cover with aspic jelly, let stand until firm, and turn on a thin oval slice of cold boiled tongue.
