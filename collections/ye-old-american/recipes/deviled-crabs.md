## Ingredients

- fresh crabs
- butter
- mustard powder
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- bread crumbs
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil half a dozen fresh crabs and mince them
2. Put the meat into a bowl and mix carefully with it an equal quantity of fine bread crumbs
3. Work the butter to a light cream
4. Mix the mustard well with it
5. Stir in very carefully, a handful at a time, the mixed crabs, a tablespoonful of cream and crumbs
6. Season to taste with cayenne pepper and salt
7. Fill the crab shells with the mixture
8. Sprinkle bread crumbs over the tops
9. Put three small pieces of butter upon the top of each
10. Brown them quickly in a hot oven
