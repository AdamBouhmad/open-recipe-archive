## Ingredients

- a dozen large juicy pippins
- the yellow peel of a large lemon grated very fine
- a very small portion of water
- a half cupful of sugar
- six eggs
- a quart of rich milk
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pare, core and quarter a dozen large juicy pippins
2. Stew among them the yellow peel of a large lemon grated very fine
3. Stew them till tender in a very small portion of water
4. Mash them smooth with the back of a spoon until you have a pint and a half of the stewed apple
5. Mix a half cupful of sugar with them and set them away till cold
6. Beat six eggs very light
7. Stir them gradually into a quart of rich milk alternately with the stewed apple
8. Put the mixture into cups, or into a deep dish and bake it about twenty minutes
9. Send it to table cold, with nutmeg grated over the top
