## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- 1 pint of Indian meal
- 4 eggs
- 4 spoons of flour
- little salt

## Directions

1. Beat together, baked on gridles, or fry in a dry pan, or baked in a pan which has been rub'd with suet, lard or butter
