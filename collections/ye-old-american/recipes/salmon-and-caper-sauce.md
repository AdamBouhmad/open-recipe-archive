## Ingredients

- two slices of salmon
- one-quarter pound butter
- one-half teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- one shallot
- salt
- pepper
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Lay the salmon in a baking dish
2. Place pieces of butter over it
3. Add the other ingredients, rubbing a little of the seasoning into the fish
4. Place it in the oven and baste it frequently
5. When done, take it out and drain for a minute or two
6. Lay it in a dish
7. Pour caper sauce over it and serve
