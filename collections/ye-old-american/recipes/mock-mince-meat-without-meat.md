## Ingredients

- One cupful of cold water
- Half a cupful of molasses
- Half a cupful of brown sugar
- Half a cupful of cider vinegar
- Two-thirds of a cupful of melted butter
- One cupful of raisins seeded and chopped
- One egg beaten light
- Half a cupful of rolled cracker crumbs
- One teaspoonful of cinnamon
- One teaspoonful each of cloves, allspice, nutmeg, salt and black pepper
- A wine-glassful of brandy

## Directions

1. Put the saucepan on the fire with the water and raisins; let them cook a few minutes
2. Add the sugar and molasses
3. Add the vinegar
4. Add the other ingredients
5. Lastly, add a wine-glassful of brandy
