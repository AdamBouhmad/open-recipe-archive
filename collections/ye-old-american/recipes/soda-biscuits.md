## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- a pint of warm milk
- a tea-spoonful of soda
- half a pound of sugar
- two pounds of flour

## Directions

1. Melt half a pound of butter in a pint of warm milk, adding a tea-spoonful of soda
2. Stir in by degrees half a pound of sugar
3. Sift into a pan two pounds of flour; make a hole in the middle; pour in the milk, &c., and mix it with the flour into a dough
4. Put it on your paste-board, and knead it long and hard till it becomes very light
5. Roll it out into a sheet half an inch thick
6. Cut it into little round cakes with the top of a wine glass, or with a tin cutter of that size; prick the tops
7. Lay them on tins sprinkled with flour, or in shallow iron pans
8. Bake them of a light brown in a quick oven; they will be done in a few minutes
