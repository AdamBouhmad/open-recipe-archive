## Ingredients

- 1¼ lb. sugar
- 1 lb. butter
- ½ pint cold water
- 3 eggs
- 3 lbs. flour
- 1 teaspoonful soda dissolved in hot water
- 4 tablespoonfuls caraway seed sprinkled through the flour

## Directions

1. Rub the butter, or, what is better, chop it up in the flour.
2. Dissolve the sugar in the water.
3. Mix all well with the beaten eggs.
4. Cut in square cakes, or with oval mould, and bake quickly.
