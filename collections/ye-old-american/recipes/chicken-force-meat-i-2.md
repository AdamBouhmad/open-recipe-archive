## Ingredients

- ½ cup fine stale bread crumbs
- ½ cup milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- White 1 egg
- ⅔ cup breast raw chicken
- Salt
- Few grains cayenne
- Slight grating nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cook bread and milk to a paste, add butter, white of egg beaten stiff, and seasonings.
2. Then add chicken pounded and forced through purée strainer.
