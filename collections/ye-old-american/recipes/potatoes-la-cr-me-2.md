## Ingredients

- 1 cupful milk
- 1 heaping tablespoonful butter
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- 2 cupfuls cold boiled potatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. Heat a cupful of milk.
2. Stir in a heaping tablespoonful of butter cut up in as much flour.
3. Stir until smooth and thick; pepper and salt, and add two cupfuls of cold boiled potatoes, sliced, and a little very finely chopped parsley.
4. Shake over the fire until the potatoes are hot all through, and pour into a deep dish.
