## Ingredients

- tart apples
- sugar
- butter
- cinnamon
- mace
- wine or rose-water q: s

## Directions

1. Pare, quarter and core tart apples
2. lay in paste No. 3
3. cover with the same
4. bake half an hour
5. when drawn, gently raise the top crust
6. add sugar, butter, cinnamon, mace, wine or rose-water q: s
