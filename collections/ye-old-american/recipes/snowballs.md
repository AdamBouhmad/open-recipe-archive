## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 2¼ cups flour
- 3½ teaspoons baking powder
- Whites 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, milk, and flour mixed and sifted with baking powder
2. Add the whites of eggs beaten stiff
3. Steam thirty-five minutes in buttered cups
4. Serve with preserved fruit, quince marmalade, or strawberry sauce
