## Ingredients

- bananas
- crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Remove skins from bananas, scrape, using a silver knife to remove the astringent principle which lies close to skin, and cut in halves crosswise; then remove a slice from each end.
2. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again, fry in deep fat, and drain on brown paper.
