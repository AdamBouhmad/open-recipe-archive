## Ingredients

- 6 potatoes
- 2 quarts cold water
- 4 tablespoonfuls flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and boil the potatoes until they break.
2. Leaving the water on the fire, take them out and mash fine with the flour and sugar, wetting gradually with the hot water until it is all used.
3. When lukewarm, add the remaining ingredients as directed.
