## Ingredients

- half a pound of rice
- milk
- three quarters of a pound of sugar
- three quarters of a pound of melted butter
- half a nutmeg
- six eggs
- a gill of wine
- grated lemon peel
- paste

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of rice in milk, until it is quite tender
2. Beat it well with a wooden spoon to mash the grains
3. Add three quarters of a pound of sugar, and the same of melted butter
4. Add half a nutmeg, six eggs, a gill of wine, and some grated lemon peel
5. Put a paste in the dish, and bake it
6. For change, it may be boiled, and eaten with butter, sugar, and wine
