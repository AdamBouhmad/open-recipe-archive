## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- three hard-boiled eggs
- one and one-half tablespoons finely chopped red peppers
- one-half tablespoon chopped chives
- Cream Dressing I
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Cut cold boiled potatoes in one-half inch cubes; there should be one and one-half cups.
2. Add three hard-boiled eggs finely chopped, one and one-half tablespoons finely chopped red peppers, and one-half tablespoon chopped chives.
3. Pour over Cream Dressing I and serve in nests of lettuce leaves.
