## Ingredients

- 1 teacupful sweet milk
- 1 teacupful buttermilk
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter
- Enough meal to enable you to roll it into a sheet half an inch thick

## Directions

1. Mix as above
2. Knead well
3. Bake upon a perfectly clean and sweet board, before a hot fire, with something at the back to keep it up
4. Incline at such an angle as will prevent the cake from slipping off, until it is hardened slightly by baking, then place upright
5. Baste frequently with butter until nicely crisped
