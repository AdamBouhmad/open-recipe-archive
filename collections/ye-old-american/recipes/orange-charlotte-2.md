## Ingredients

- Half a box of gelatine
- Half a cupful of water
- One and a half cupfuls of boiling water
- Two cupfuls of sugar
- One cupful of orange juice and pulp
- Juice of one lemon
- Whites of six eggs

## Directions

1. Soak half a box of gelatine in half a cupful of water for two hours
2. Add one and a half cupfuls of boiling water and strain
3. Add two cupfuls of sugar, one cupful of orange juice and pulp and the juice of one lemon
4. Stir until the mixture begins to cool, or about five minutes
5. Add the whites of six eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
6. Beat the whole until so stiff that it will only just pour into molds lined with sections of orange
7. Set away to cool
