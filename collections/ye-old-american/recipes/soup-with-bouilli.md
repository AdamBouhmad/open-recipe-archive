## Ingredients

- the nicest part of the thick brisket of beef, about eight pounds
- every thing directed for the other soup
- a Dutch oven

## Directions

1. Put it into a pot with everything directed for the other soup; make it exactly in the same way, only put it on an hour sooner, that you may have time to prepare the bouilli;
2. After it has boiled five hours, take out the beef, cover up the soup and set it near the fire that it may keep hot;
3. Take the skin off the beef, have the yelk of an egg well beaten, dip a feather in it and wash the top of your beef, sprinkle over it the crumb of stale bread finely grated, put it in a Dutch oven
