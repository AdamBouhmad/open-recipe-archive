## Ingredients

- a tea-spoonful of salt
- a large spoonful of yeast
- a quart of flour
- corn meal gruel

## Directions

1. Put a tea-spoonful of salt, and a large one of yeast, into a quart of flour
2. make it sufficiently soft, with corn meal gruel
3. when well risen, bake it in a mould
