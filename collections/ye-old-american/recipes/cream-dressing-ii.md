## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons flour
- 1½ teaspoons powdered sugar
- Few grains cayenne
- 1 teaspoon melted butter
- Yolk 1 egg
- ⅓ cup hot vinegar
- ½ cup thick cream

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients, add butter, egg, and vinegar slowly.
2. Cook over boiling water, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens.
3. Cool, and add to heavy cream, beaten until stiff.
