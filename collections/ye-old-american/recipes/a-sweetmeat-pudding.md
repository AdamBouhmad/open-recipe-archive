## Ingredients

- a quart of flour
- puff paste
- marmalade

## Directions

1. Make a quart of flour into puff paste
2. When done, divide it into three parts of unequal size
3. Roll the largest out square and moderately thin
4. Spread over it a thin layer of marmalade, leaving a margin all round about an inch broad
5. Roll the next largest in the same manner, lay it on, cover that with marmalade, leaving a margin
6. Roll the smallest, and put it on the other two, spreading marmalade
7. Fold it up, one fold over the other, the width of your hand
8. Press the ends together, tie it in a cloth securely, and place it in a kettle of boiling water, where it can lie at length without doubling
9. Boil it quickly, and when done, pour melted butter with sugar and wine in the dish
