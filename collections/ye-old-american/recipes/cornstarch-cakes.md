## Ingredients

- one cupful of butter
- one cupful of sweet milk
- half a cup of cornstarch
- two cupfuls of sugar
- two cupfuls of flour
- whites of five eggs
- two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- one teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Beat whites of five eggs to a stiff froth
2. Mix cornstarch, sugar, and flour together
3. Add butter, milk, cream of tartar, and soda to the flour mixture
4. Fold in egg whites
5. Flavor to taste
6. Bake in gem-tins or patty-pans
