## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of sugar
- half a cupful of butter
- one pint of sweet milk
- one tablespoonful of salt
- two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- one quart of flour
- one quart of huckleberries
- butter

## Directions

1. Sift two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder into a quart of flour
2. Mix with two cupfuls of sugar, half a cupful of butter, one pint of sweet milk, one tablespoonful of salt, or enough to form a thick batter
3. Add a quart of the huckleberries
4. Bake in a dripper
5. Cut into squares for the table and serve hot with butter
