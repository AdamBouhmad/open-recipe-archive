## Ingredients

- breast of veal
- ham
- pepper
- sweet herbs
- onion
- water
- green peas
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Divide the breast into pieces according to the position of the bones
2. Put them into a stew-pan with a few slices of ham, some whole pepper, a bunch of sweet herbs, and a sliced onion
3. Add sufficient water to keep it from burning and let it stew slowly till the meat is quite tender
4. Put to it a quart or more of green peas that have boiled twenty minutes in another pot and a piece of butter rolled in flour
5. Let all stew together a quarter of an hour longer
6. Serve it up with the veal in the middle, the peas round it, and the ham laid on the peas
