## Ingredients

- brandy or rum
- sugar
- milk
- nutmeg
- lemon juice or water

## Directions

1. Mix together brandy or rum, sugar, milk and nutmeg, with or without lemon juice or water
2. Take it cold with a lump of ice in each tumbler
