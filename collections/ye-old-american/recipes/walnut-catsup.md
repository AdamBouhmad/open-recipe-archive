## Ingredients

- walnuts
- salt and water
- vinegar
- pounded cloves
- mace
- garlic cloves

## Directions

1. Gather the walnuts as for pickling, and keep them in salt and water the same time
2. Pound them in a marble mortar--to every dozen walnuts, put a quart of vinegar
3. Stir them well every day for a week
4. Put them in a bag, and press all the liquor through
5. To each quart, put a tea-spoonful of pounded cloves, and one of mace, with six cloves of garlic
6. Boil it fifteen or twenty minutes, and bottle it
