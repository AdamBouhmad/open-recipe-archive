## Ingredients

- dozen large tomatos
- four ounces of butter
- salt
- pepper
- chopped onion
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Peel the skins from a dozen large tomatos
2. Put four ounces of butter in a frying pan
3. Add some salt, pepper, and a little chopped onion
4. Fry them a few minutes
5. Add the tomatos and chop them while frying
6. When nearly done, break in six eggs
7. Stir them quickly
8. Serve them up
