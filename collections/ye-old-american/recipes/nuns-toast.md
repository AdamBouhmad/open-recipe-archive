## Ingredients

- four or five hard-boiled eggs
- a piece of butter half the size of an egg
- a finely chopped onion
- a teaspoonful of flour
- a cupful of milk
- pepper
- salt
- slices of hot buttered toast

## Directions

1. Cut four or five hard-boiled eggs into slices
2. Put a piece of butter half the size of an egg into a saucepan
3. When it begins to bubble add a finely chopped onion
4. Let the onion cook a little without taking color
5. Stir in a teaspoonful of flour
6. Add a cupful of milk and stir until it becomes smooth
7. Put in the slices of eggs and let them get hot
8. Pour over neatly trimmed slices of hot buttered toast
9. Season the sauce to taste with pepper and salt
