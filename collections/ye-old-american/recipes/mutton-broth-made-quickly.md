## Ingredients

- three chops from the best part of a neck of mutton
- water
- salt
- bread crust
- parsley
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut three chops from the best part of a neck of mutton and remove the fat and skin
2. Beat the meat on both sides and slice it thin
3. Put into a small sauce-pan with a pint of water, a little salt, and some crust of bread cut into pieces
4. Add a little parsley and a small onion sliced thin
5. Cover the sauce-pan and set it over the fire
6. Boil it fast, skim it, and in half an hour it should be ready for use
