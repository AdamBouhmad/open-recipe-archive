## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of soft butter
- yolks of two eggs
- four tablespoonfuls of milk
- flavoring
- whites of eggs
- one teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- half a teaspoon of soda
- one cup of flour
- half of a pint of sweet cream

## Directions

1. Stir sugar and butter together
2. Add the yolks of two eggs well beaten
3. Add four tablespoonfuls of milk and some flavoring
4. Add the beaten whites of the eggs
5. Mix cream of tartar and soda in a cup of flour, sift it into the cake batter and stir lightly
6. Bake in a small whipping-pan
7. When the cake is cool, have ready half a pint of sweet cream sweetened and whipped to a stiff froth, also flavored
8. Spread it over the cake while fresh
