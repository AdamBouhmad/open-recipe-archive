## Ingredients

- half a pound sugar
- half a pound butter
- two pounds flour
- one glass wine
- one do. rose water
- two do. emptins
- a nutmeg
- cinnamon
- currants

## Directions

1. rub half a pound sugar, half a pound butter, into two pounds flour
2. one glass wine, one do. rose water, two do. emptins, a nutmeg, cinnamon and currants
