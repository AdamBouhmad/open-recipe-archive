## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups sugar
- two eggs
- one-half cup butter
- three-fourths cup milk
- two heaping cups flour
- one teaspoonful cream of tartar
- one-half teaspoonful of soda
- one teaspoonful molasses
- one-half cup raisins (chopped) or currants
- one-half teaspoonful cinnamon
- cloves
- allspice
- a little nutmeg
- one tablespoonful flour
- jelly for filling

## Directions

1. Put half the mixture in a small shallow tin and bake
2. To the remainder add molasses, raisins or currants, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, nutmeg and one tablespoonful flour
3. Bake this in same kind of tins
4. Put the sheets of cake together, while warm, with jelly between
