## Ingredients

- Boiled Artichokes
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- Batter I
- fat

## Directions

1. Sprinkle Boiled Artichokes cut in quarters with salt, pepper, and finely chopped parsley
2. Dip in Batter I
3. Fry in deep fat, and drain
