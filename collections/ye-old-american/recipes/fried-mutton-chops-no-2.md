## Ingredients

- mutton chops
- salt
- pepper
- beaten egg
- rolled cracker or bread-crumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops by trimming off all extra fat and skin, season them with salt and pepper
2. Dip each chop in beaten egg, then in rolled cracker or bread-crumbs
3. Dip again in the egg and crumbs, and so on until they are well coated with the crumb
4. Have ready a deep spider containing a pound or more of lard, hot enough to fry crullers
5. Drop into this hot lard the chops, frying only a few at a time, as too many cool the fat
6. Fry them brown, and serve them up hot and dry, on a warm platter
