## Ingredients

- salt codfish or fresh codfish
- mashed potatoes in equal quantity to fish
- butter, large tablespoonful
- milk
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Soak, boil, and pick the fish, if salt, as for fish-balls. If fresh, boil and pick into bits.
2. Add an equal quantity of mashed potatoes, a large tablespoonful of butter and milk, enough to make it very soft.
3. Put into a skillet, and add a little boiling water to keep it from burning.
4. Turn and toss constantly until it is smoking hot but not dry.
5. Add pepper and parsley, and dish.
