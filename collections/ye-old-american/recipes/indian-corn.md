## Ingredients

- corn
- water
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Strip it of the outside leaves and the silk, but let the inner leaves remain, as they will keep in the sweetness.
2. Put it into a large pot with plenty of water, and boil it rather fast for half an hour.
3. When done, drain off the water, and remove the leaves.
4. You may either lay the ears on a large flat dish and send them to table whole, or broken in half; or you may cut all the corn off the cob, and serve it up in a deep dish, mixed with butter, pepper and salt.
