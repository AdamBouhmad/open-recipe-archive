## Ingredients

- 1 pint raised dough
- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- ½ cup white sugar

## Directions

1. When your bread has passed its second rising, work into the dough the melted butter
2. Then work in the eggs and sugar, beaten together until very light
3. Bake in muffin-rings about twenty minutes
