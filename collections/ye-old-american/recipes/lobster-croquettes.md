## Ingredients

- One well-boiled lobster, chopped fine
- Pepper
- Salt
- Powdered mace
- Bread-crumbs
- Melted butter
- Beaten egg
- Pulverized cracker
- Butter or sweet lard for frying
- Crisped parsley

## Directions

1. To the meat of a well-boiled lobster chopped fine, add pepper, salt, and powdered mace
2. Mix with one-quarter as much bread-crumbs, well rubbed, as you have meat
3. Make into ovates or pointed balls with two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
4. Roll these in beaten egg, then in pulverized cracker
5. Fry in butter or very nice sweet lard
6. Serve dry and hot and garnish with crisped parsley
