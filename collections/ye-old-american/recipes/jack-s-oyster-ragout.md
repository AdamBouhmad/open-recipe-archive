## Ingredients

- Fresh honeycomb tripe
- Small boiled onions
- Raw oysters
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons flour
- 1½ cups thin cream
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Paprika

## Directions

1. Parboil fresh honeycomb tripe, and cut in three-fourths inch pieces; there should be one cup.
2. Add an equal quantity of small boiled onions, and twice the quantity of raw oysters which have been previously cleaned.
3. Melt three tablespoons butter, add four tablespoons flour, and pour on gradually while stirring constantly one and one-half cups thin cream.
4. Add tripe, onion, and oysters.
5. When thoroughly heated add yolks two eggs slightly beaten, and season highly with salt, pepper, and paprika.
6. Serve on pieces toasted bread.
