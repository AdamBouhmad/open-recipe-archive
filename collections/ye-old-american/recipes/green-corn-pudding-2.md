## Ingredients

- milk
- eggs
- melted butter
- white sugar
- corn

## Directions

1. Grate the corn from the cob.
2. Beat the whites and yolks of the eggs separately.
3. Put the corn and yolks together, stir hard, and add the butter.
4. Add the milk gradually, beating all the while.
5. Add the sugar and a little salt.
6. Lastly add the whites and beat together.
7. Bake slowly at first, covering the dish, for an hour.
8. Remove the cover and brown finely.
9. Warm up leftovers from dinner for breakfast by moistening with a little warm milk and stirring in a saucepan until smoking hot.
