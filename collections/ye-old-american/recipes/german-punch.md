## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 1¾ cups tomatoes
- 3 apples, cored, pared, and chopped
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- Piece ginger root
- 3 tablespoons Maraschino

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients, except cordial, and cook thirty-five minutes
2. Rub through a sieve
3. Add Maraschino and freeze to a mush
