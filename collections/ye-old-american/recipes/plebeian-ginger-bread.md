## Ingredients

- three large spoonsful of pounded ginger
- three quarts of flour
- three tea-spoonsful of pearl-ash
- a cup of water
- half a pound of butter
- a quart of molasses

## Directions

1. Mix three large spoonsful of pounded ginger, with three quarts of flour
2. Sift it
3. Dissolve three tea-spoonsful of pearl-ash in a cup of water
4. Pour it on the flour
5. Melt half a pound of butter in a quart of molasses
6. Mix it with the flour
7. Knead it well
8. Cut it in shapes
9. Bake it
