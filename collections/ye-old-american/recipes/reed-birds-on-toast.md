## Ingredients

- a dozen reed birds
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- toast

## Directions

1. Remove the feathers and legs of a dozen reed birds, split them down the back, remove the entrails, and place them on a double broiler.
2. Brush a little melted butter over them and broil the inner side thoroughly first; then lightly broil the other side.
3. Melt one quarter of a pound of butter, season it nicely with salt and pepper, dip the birds in it, and arrange them nicely on slices of toast.
