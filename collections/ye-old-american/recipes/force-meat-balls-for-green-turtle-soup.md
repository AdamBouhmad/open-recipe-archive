## Ingredients

- six tablespoonfuls of turtle meat
- the yolk of two hard-boiled eggs
- a tablespoonful of butter
- a little oyster liquor
- cayenne
- mace
- half a teaspoonful of white sugar
- a pinch of salt
- a well-beaten egg
- powdered cracker
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop six tablespoonfuls of turtle meat very fine.
2. Rub to a paste with the yolk of two hard-boiled eggs, a tablespoonful of butter, and if convenient, a little oyster liquor.
3. Season with cayenne, mace, half a teaspoonful of white sugar and a pinch of salt.
4. Bind all with a well-beaten egg.
5. Shape into small balls.
6. Dip in egg, then powdered cracker.
7. Fry in butter.
8. Drop into the soup when it is served.
