## Ingredients

- All milk
- A scant tablespoonful of sifted flour
- A little cold milk

## Directions

1. Use all milk and thicken with a scant tablespoonful of sifted flour
2. Let it boil five minutes, stirring it constantly
3. Add a little cold milk
4. Give it one boil up and it is ready for use
