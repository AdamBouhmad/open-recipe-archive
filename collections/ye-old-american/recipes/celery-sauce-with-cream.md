## Ingredients

- celery
- water
- mace
- nutmeg
- butter
- flour
- cream

## Directions

1. Prepare and boil celery, adding some mace, nutmeg, a piece of butter the size of a walnut rolled in flour
2. Add half a pint of cream
3. Boil all together
