## Ingredients

- veal kidneys with suet
- toast
- butter
- salt
- cayenne
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Order veal kidneys with the suet left on.
2. Trim, split, and broil ten minutes.
3. Arrange on pieces of toast and pour over melted butter seasoned with salt, cayenne, and lemon juice.
