## Ingredients

- drippings accumulated from different cooked meats of beef or veal
- a raw potato

## Directions

1. Put it into a basin and slicing into it a raw potato, allowing it to boil long enough for the potato to brown, which causes all impurities to disappear
2. Remove from the fire, and when cool drain it off from the sediment that settles at the bottom
3. Turn it into basins or small jars and set it in a cool place for future use
