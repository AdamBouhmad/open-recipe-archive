## Ingredients

- Twelve sardines
- Maître d'Hôtel or Lemon Butter

## Directions

1. Drain twelve sardines and cook in a chafing-dish until heated, turning frequently.
2. Place on small oblong pieces of dry toast, and serve with Maître d'Hôtel or Lemon Butter.
