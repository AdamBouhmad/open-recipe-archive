## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. lamb, from which every particle of fat has been removed
- 1 lb. veal
- A slice of corned ham
- 5 qts. water
- salt
- sweet herbs
- a chopped shallot
- 2 teaspoonfuls Worcestershire sauce
- about a third of a pound of vermicelli or macaroni

## Directions

1. Cut up the meat, cover it with a quart of water, and set it back on the range to heat very gradually, keeping it covered closely
2. At the end of an hour, add four quarts of boiling water, and cook until the meat is in shreds
3. Season with salt, sweet herbs, a chopped shallot, and two teaspoonfuls Worcestershire sauce
4. When these have boiled in the soup for ten minutes, strain and return to the fire
5. Have ready about a third of a pound of vermicelli which has been boiled tender in clear water
6. Add this; boil up once, and pour out
