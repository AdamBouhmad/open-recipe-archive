## Ingredients

- fifty round clams (quahogs)
- salt pork
- onions
- potatoes
- water
- powdered crackers
- milk
- black pepper

## Directions

1. wash the clams very thoroughly and put them in a pot with half a pint of water
2. when the shells are open they are done
3. take them from the shells and chop fine, saving all the clam water for the chowder
4. fry out the pork very gently, and when the scraps are a good brown take them out and put in the chopped onions to fry
5. fry them in a frying pan, and make the chowder kettle very clean before putting them in it
6. Add a quart of hot water to the onions
7. put in the clams, clam-water and pork scraps
8. After it boils, add the potatoes
9. when they are cooked and just before it is taken up, thicken it with a cup of powdered crackers, and add a quart of fresh milk
10. Season with good black pepper
