## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- pepper
- crumbs
- egg
- deep fat
- brown paper
- Sauce Tartare

## Directions

1. Clean fish and cut in long or short fillets
2. If cut in long fillets, roll, and fasten with small wooden skewers
3. Sprinkle fillets with salt and pepper
4. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs
5. Fry in deep fat
6. Drain on brown paper
7. Serve with Sauce Tartare
