## Ingredients

- vanilla ice cream
- marron glacé
- lady fingers
- whipped cream, sweetened
- marron glacé
- candied violets or glacé cherries

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of small paper cases with vanilla ice cream
2. Sprinkle ice cream with marron glacé broken in pieces
3. Arrange lady fingers at equal distances, and allow them to extend one inch above cases
4. Pile whipped cream, sweetened and flavored, in the centre
5. Garnish with marron glacé and candied violets or glacé cherries
