## Ingredients

- a loaf of stale bread
- one quart of milk
- half a pound of currants
- half a pound of raisins
- a quarter of a pound of citron
- one nutmeg
- one tablespoonful of mace and cinnamon mixed
- one cupful of sugar
- half a cupful of butter
- a glassful of white wine
- eight eggs
- flour
- buttered dish
- wine sauce, or wine and sugar

## Directions

1. Grate a loaf of stale bread, or enough for a pint of crumbs
2. Boil one quart of milk and turn boiling hot over the grated bread
3. Cover and let steep an hour
4. Pick, soak and dry half a pound of currants and half a pound of raisins
5. Cut a quarter of a pound of citron in large slips
6. When the bread is ready mix with it the butter, sugar, spice and citron
7. Add a glassful of white wine
8. Beat eight eggs very light
9. When the mixture is quite cold, stir them gradually in
10. Add by degrees the raisins and currants dredged with flour
11. Stir the whole very hard
12. Put it into a buttered dish
13. Bake two hours
14. Send to the table warm
15. Eat with wine sauce, or wine and sugar
