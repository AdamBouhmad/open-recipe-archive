## Ingredients

- two eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar
- a piece of butter the size of half an egg
- a teaspoonful of mustard
- a little pepper
- a teacup of vinegar
- half a cupful of thick sweet cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat up two eggs with two tablespoonfuls of sugar
2. Add a piece of butter the size of half an egg, a teaspoonful of mustard, a little pepper
3. Lastly add a teacup of vinegar
4. Put all of these ingredients into a dish over the fire and cook like a soft custard
5. Some think it improved by adding half a cupful of thick sweet cream to this dressing; in that case use less vinegar
