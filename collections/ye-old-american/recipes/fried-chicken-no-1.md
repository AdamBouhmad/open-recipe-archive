## Ingredients

- Spring chickens, a couple
- lard or dripping
- egg
- salt
- cracker-crumbs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and cut to pieces a couple of Spring chickens
2. Have ready in a frying-pan enough boiling lard or dripping to cover them well
3. Dip each piece in beaten egg when you have salted it, then in cracker-crumbs, and fry until brown
4. If the chicken is large, steam it before frying
5. When you have taken out the meat, throw into the hot fat a dozen sprigs of parsley and let them remain a minute
6. Garnish the chicken by strewing the parsley over it
