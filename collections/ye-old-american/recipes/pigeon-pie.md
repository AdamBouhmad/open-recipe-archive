## Ingredients

- four pigeons
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- pound of butter
- two pounds of sifted flour
- water

## Directions

1. Take four pigeons, and pick and clean them very nicely.
2. Season them with pepper and salt, and put inside of every one a large piece of butter and the yolk of a hard-boiled egg.
3. Have ready a good paste, allowing a pound of butter to two pounds of sifted flour.
4. Roll it out rather thick, and line with it the bottom and sides of a large deep dish.
5. Put in the pigeons, and lay on the top some bits of butter rolled in flour.
6. Pour in nearly enough of water to fill the dish.
7. Cover the pie with a lid of paste rolled out thick, and nicely notched, and ornamented with paste leaves and flowers.
