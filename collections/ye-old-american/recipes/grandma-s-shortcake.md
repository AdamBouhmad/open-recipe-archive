## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour, dried and sifted
- ¼ lb. butter, and half as much lard
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- A pinch of soda, thoroughly dissolved in just enough vinegar to cover it, and well worked in
- ice-water

## Directions

1. Add enough ice-water to enable you to roll out into paste half an inch thick.
2. Cut into squares, prick with a fork, and bake light brown.
3. Split, butter, and eat while hot.
