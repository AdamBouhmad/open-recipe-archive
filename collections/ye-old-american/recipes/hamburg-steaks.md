## Ingredients

- one pound lean raw beef
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice or one-half shallot
- nutmeg
- one egg

## Directions

1. Chop finely one pound lean raw beef
2. Season highly with salt, pepper, and a few drops onion juice or one-half shallot finely chopped
3. A few gratings of nutmeg and one egg slightly beaten may be added
4. Shape, cook, and serve as Meat Cakes
