## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- Yolks of five eggs and the whites of seven
- 6 tablespoonfuls sugar
- Vanilla flavoring—1 teaspoonful to the pint

## Directions

1. Heat the milk almost to boiling
2. Beat the yolks light and stir in the sugar
3. Add the milk in the manner described in general directions at head of this section
4. Stir in five whites whipped stiff
5. Return to the fire and stir until thick, but not until it breaks
6. Season it with vanilla
7. Pour into glass cups
8. Whip the whites of two eggs to a méringue with a heaping tablespoonful of powdered sugar
9. When the custard is cold, pile a little of this upon the top of each cup
10. You may lay a preserved strawberry or cherry, or a bit of melon sweetmeat, or a little bright jelly upon each
