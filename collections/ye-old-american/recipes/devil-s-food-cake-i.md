## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- Yolks 4 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 2⅓ cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- Whites 4 eggs
- 2 squares chocolate
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, and add gradually one-half the sugar.
2. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored, and add gradually remaining sugar.
3. Combine mixtures, and add alternately milk and flour mixed and sifted with baking powder; then add whites of eggs beaten stiff, chocolate melted, and vanilla.
4. Bake forty-five to fifty minutes in an angel cake pan.
5. Cover with White Mountain Cream (see p. 528).
