## Ingredients

- ½ box gelatine or 2 tablespoons granulated gelatine
- ½ cup cold water
- ¾ cup scalded milk
- ½ cup sugar
- ½ cup sugar, caramelized
- ¾ cup blanched and finely chopped almonds
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- Whip from 3½ cups cream
- 6 lady fingers

## Directions

1. Make same as Caramel Charlotte Russe, adding nuts before folding in cream
