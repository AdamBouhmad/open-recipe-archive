## Ingredients

- 1 pint molasses
- 1 teacup sugar
- 1 teaspoonful ginger
- 1 teaspoonful allspice
- 1 cup butter
- 5 cups flour

## Directions

1. Roll thin and cut into small cakes.
2. Bake in quick oven.
