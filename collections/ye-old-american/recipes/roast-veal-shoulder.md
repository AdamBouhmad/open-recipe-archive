## Ingredients

- veal shoulder
- bread-crumbs
- thyme
- parsley
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- melted butter or beef suet
- milk or hot water
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Stuff, making horizontal incisions near the bone to receive the dressing, and roast in like manner to the fillet.
