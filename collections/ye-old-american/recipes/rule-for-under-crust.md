## Ingredients

- three large tablespoonfuls of flour sifted
- a large tablespoonful of cold butter, or part butter and part lard
- a pinch of salt
- cold water

## Directions

1. Rub the butter or butter and lard into the flour with a pinch of salt.
2. Mix with cold water enough to form a smooth, stiff paste.
3. Roll quite thin.
