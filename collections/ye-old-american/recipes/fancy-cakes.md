## Ingredients

- rich jumble-paste
- rich, ripe peaches or canned peaches
- plums
- strawberries
- pineapples
- other available fruit
- white of an egg
- pulverized sugar

## Directions

1. Make a rich jumble-paste and roll out in any desired shape
2. Cut some paste in thick, narrow strips and lay around your cakes to form a deep, cup-like edge
3. Place on a well-buttered tin and bake
4. When done, prepare iced fruit by cutting peaches in halves, plums, strawberries, and pineapples in squares or small triangles
5. Dip fruit in the white of an egg that has been very slightly beaten and then in pulverized sugar
6. Lay the fruit in the centre of your cakes
