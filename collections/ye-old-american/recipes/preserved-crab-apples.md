## Ingredients

- red Siberian crab apples
- warm water
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pick out crab apples that are nearly perfect, leaving the stems on, and put into a preserve-kettle with enough warm water to cover them
2. Heat this to boiling, slowly, and simmer until the skins break
3. Drain, cool, and skin them; then, with a penknife, extract the cores through the blossom ends
4. Weigh them; allow a pound and a quarter of sugar and a teacupful of water to every pound of fruit
5. Boil the water and sugar together until the scum ceases to rise; put in the fruit, cover the kettle, and simmer until the apples are a clear red, and tender
6. Take out with a skimmer; spread upon dishes to cool and harden
7. Add to the syrup the juice of one lemon to three pounds of fruit, and boil until clear and rich
8. Fill your jars three-quarters full of the apples, pour the syrup in, and, when cool, tie up
