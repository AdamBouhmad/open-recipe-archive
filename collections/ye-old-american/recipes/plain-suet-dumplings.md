## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- salt
- one pound of beef suet
- cold water

## Directions

1. Sift two pounds of flour into a pan, and add a salt-spoon of salt
2. Mince very fine one pound of beef suet, and rub it into the flour
3. Make it into a stiff dough with a little cold water
4. Roll it out an inch thick or rather more
5. Cut it into dumplings with the edge of a tumbler
6. Put them into a pot of boiling water, and let them boil an hour and a half
7. Send them to table hot, to eat with boiled loin of mutton, or with molasses after the meat is removed
