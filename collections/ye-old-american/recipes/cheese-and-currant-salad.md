## Ingredients

- cream cheese
- lettuce
- lettuce leaves
- French Dressing
- Bar-le-Duc currants

## Directions

1. Mash a cream cheese and mix with finely chopped lettuce
2. Shape in balls, arrange on lettuce leaves, pour over French Dressing, and over all Bar-le-Duc currants
