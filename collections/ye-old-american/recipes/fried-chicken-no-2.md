## Ingredients

- fat salt pork, half a pound
- young chicken, broiling size
- salt
- water
- pepper
- flour
- milk, one cup
- cream
- butter, one tablespoonful
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut up half a pound of fat salt pork in a frying-pan and fry until the grease is extracted, but not until it browns
2. Wash and cut up a young chicken, soak in salt and water for half an hour
3. Wipe dry, season with pepper, and dredge with flour
4. Fry in the hot fat until each piece is a rich brown on both sides
5. Take up, drain, and set aside in a hot covered dish
6. Pour into the gravy left in the frying-pan a cup of milk—half cream is better
7. Thicken with a spoonful of flour and a tablespoonful of butter
8. Add some chopped parsley, boil up, and pour over the hot chicken
