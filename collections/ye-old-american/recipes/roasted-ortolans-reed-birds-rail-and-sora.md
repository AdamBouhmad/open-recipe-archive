## Ingredients

- oyster
- melted butter
- bread-crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- water
- toast

## Directions

1. Roll an oyster in melted butter, then in bread-crumbs seasoned with pepper and salt, and put into each bird before roasting.
2. Baste with butter and water three times, put the rounds of toast underneath, and baste freely with melted butter.
3. They will require about twenty minutes to cook.
