## Ingredients

- a nice fat shad fresh from the water
- cold water
- a wine glass of pale vinegar
- salt
- garlic
- parsley
- scraped horse-radish
- melted butter
- catsups

## Directions

1. Get a nice fat shad fresh from the water that the skin may not crack in boiling
2. put it in cold water on a slice in a kettle of proper length with a wine glass of pale vinegar, salt, a little garlic, and a bundle of parsley
3. when it is done, drain all the water from the fish
4. lay it in the dish and garnish with scraped horse-radish
5. have a sauce boat of nice melted butter to mix with the different catsups as taste shall direct
