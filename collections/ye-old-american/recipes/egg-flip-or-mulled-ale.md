## Ingredients

- one quart of good ale
- nutmeg
- six eggs
- a piece of butter or a glass of brandy
- dry toast

## Directions

1. Boil one quart of good ale with some nutmeg
2. Beat up six eggs and mix them with a little cold ale
3. Pour the hot ale to it, pour it back and forth several times to prevent its curdling
4. Warm and stir it till sufficiently thick
5. Add a piece of butter or a glass of brandy and serve it with dry toast
