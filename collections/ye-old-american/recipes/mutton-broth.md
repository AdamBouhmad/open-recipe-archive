## Ingredients

- one loin of mutton
- water
- salt
- parsley
- bread crust
- vegetables
- barley
- rice

## Directions

1. Cut off all the fat from a loin of mutton and to each pound of the lean allow a quart of water
2. Season it with a little salt and some shred parsley and put in some large pieces of the crust of bread
3. Boil it slowly for two or three hours, skimming it carefully
