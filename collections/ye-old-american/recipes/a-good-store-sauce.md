## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls horse-radish (grated)
- 1 tablespoonful allspice
- A grated nutmeg
- 3 large pickled onions (minced fine)
- 2 dozen whole black peppers
- A pinch of cayenne
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful white sugar
- 1 quart vinegar from walnut or butternut pickle

## Directions

1. Mix all the spices well together
2. Crush in a stone jar with a potato-beetle or billet of wood
3. Pour the vinegar upon these, and let it stand two weeks
4. Put on in a porcelain or clean bell-metal kettle and heat to boiling
5. Strain and set aside until next day to cool and settle
6. Bottle and cork very tightly
