## Ingredients

- Morello cherries
- for each pound of cherry juice, half a pound of sugar and six cherry kernels

## Directions

1. Seed the cherries and let them stand in a bowl over night
2. In the morning, press them through a fine cloth which has been dipped in boiling water
3. Weigh the juice, add the sugar
4. Boil fifteen minutes, removing all the scum
5. Fill small bottles that are perfectly dry with the syrup
6. When it is cold, cork the bottles tightly, seal them and keep them in a cool place, standing upright
