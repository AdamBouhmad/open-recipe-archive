## Ingredients

- Whites of four eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of currant jelly
- two tablespoonfuls of raspberry jam
- sponge cakes

## Directions

1. Beat whites of four eggs to a stiff froth
2. Add two tablespoonfuls each of sugar, currant jelly and raspberry jam
3. Eat with sponge cakes
