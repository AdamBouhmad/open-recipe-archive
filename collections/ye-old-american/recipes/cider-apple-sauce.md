## Ingredients

- four quarts of new cider
- pared and quartered apples
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil four quarts of new cider until it is reduced to two quarts
2. Put into it enough pared and quartered apples to fill the kettle
3. Let the whole stew over a moderate fire four hours
4. Add cinnamon if liked
