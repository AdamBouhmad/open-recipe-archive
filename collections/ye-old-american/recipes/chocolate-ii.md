## Ingredients

- 1½ squares Baker's chocolate
- ¼ cup sugar
- Few grains salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- one can evaporated cream or condensed milk diluted with two cups boiling water

## Directions

1. Prepare same as Chocolate I., substituting one can evaporated cream or condensed milk diluted with two cups boiling water in place of three cups milk.
2. If sweetened condensed milk is used, omit sugar.
