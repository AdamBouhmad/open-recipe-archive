## Ingredients

- lamb
- salt
- water
- flour
- butter
- young green mint
- vinegar
- sugar
- lemon

## Directions

1. Wash the meat, wipe it dry, spit it, and cover the fat with paper.
2. Place it before a clear brisk fire.
3. Baste it at first with a little salt and water, and then with its own drippings.
4. Remove the paper when the meat is nearly done, and dredge the lamb with a little flour.
5. Afterwards baste it with butter.
6. Do not take it off the spit till you see it drop white gravy.
7. Prepare some mint-sauce by stripping from the stalks the leaves of young green mint, mincing them very fine, and mixing them with vinegar and sugar.
8. Send the mint-sauce to table in a boat, and the gravy in another boat.
9. Garnish with sliced lemon.
