## Ingredients

- greens (such as dandelions, cowslips, burdock, chicory)
- salt
- boiling water
- butter
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Carefully examine the greens and throw out the tough ones
2. Thoroughly wash through several waters until entirely free from sand
3. Add a handful of salt to each pan of water used in washing the greens to free them from insects and worms
4. After the last watering allow them to stand in salted water for a half hour or longer
5. Put them into a large pot half full of boiling water, with a handful of salt
6. Boil them steadily until the stalks are tender, from five to twenty minutes according to the maturity of the greens
7. Drain them in a colander
8. Chop them a little and return them to the fire long enough to season them with salt, pepper and butter
9. Vinegar may be added if it is liked
10. Serve as soon as they are hot
