## Ingredients

- venison steaks
- salt
- pepper
- two tablespoonfuls of currant jelly
- butter

## Directions

1. Broil venison steaks over a clear fire, turning often
2. Cook more than beef
3. When sufficiently done, season with salt and pepper
4. Pour over two tablespoonfuls of currant jelly melted with a piece of butter
5. Serve hot on hot plates
