## Ingredients

- five quarts of water
- six ounces of hops
- five quarts of water
- four ounces of bruised ginger root
- four pounds of sugar
- a pint of yeast

## Directions

1. Take five quarts of water and six ounces of hops, boil it three hours
2. Strain the liquor and add to it five quarts of water and four ounces of bruised ginger root
3. Boil this again twenty minutes
4. Strain and add four pounds of sugar
5. When luke-warm put in a pint of yeast
6. Let it ferment
7. In twenty-four hours it will be ready for bottling
