## Ingredients

- perch
- salt
- flour
- lard
- melted butter
- anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the fish nicely, but do not take out the roes
2. Dry them on a cloth
3. Sprinkle some salt and dredge them with flour
4. Lay them separately on a board
5. When one side is dry, turn them
6. Sprinkle salt and dredge the other side
7. Be sure the lard boils when you put the fish in
8. Fry them with great care
9. They should be a yellowish brown when done
10. Send melted butter or anchovy sauce in a boat
