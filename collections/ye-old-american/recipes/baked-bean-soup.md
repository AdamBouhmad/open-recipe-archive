## Ingredients

- 3 cups cold baked beans
- 3 pints water
- 2 slices onion
- 2 stalks celery
- 1½ cups stewed and strained tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 tablespoon Chili sauce
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Put beans, water, onion, and celery in saucepan; bring to boiling-point and simmer thirty minutes.
2. Rub through a sieve, add tomato, and Chili sauce, season to taste with salt and pepper, and bind with the butter and flour cooked together.
3. Serve with Crisp Crackers.
