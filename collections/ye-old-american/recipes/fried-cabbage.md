## Ingredients

- cold boiled cabbage
- melted butter
- pepper
- salt
- cream
- eggs

## Directions

1. Chop cold boiled cabbage and drain very dry
2. Stir in a little melted butter, pepper, and salt, with three or four tablespoonfuls of cream
3. Heat all in a buttered frying-pan, stirring until smoking hot
4. Let the mixture stand just long enough to brown slightly on the under-side
5. Improve by the addition of a couple of beaten eggs if desired
6. Turn out by putting a flat dish above the pan, upside-down, and reversing the latter
