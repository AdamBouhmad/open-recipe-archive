## Ingredients

- ¼ cup sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup chestnut purée
- ½ cup milk
- Whites 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and flour
2. Add chestnuts and milk gradually
3. Cook five minutes, stirring constantly
4. Beat whites of eggs until stiff, and cut and fold into mixture
5. Bake same as Fruit Soufflé
6. Serve with Cream Sauce
