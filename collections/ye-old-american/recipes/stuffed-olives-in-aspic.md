## Ingredients

- olives
- green butter
- aspic jelly mixture
- liver sausage
- hard-boiled eggs
- red peppers

## Directions

1. Stone olives, using an olive stoner, and fill cavities thus made with green butter.
2. Place small Dario moulds in pan of ice-water, and pour in aspic jelly mixture one-fourth inch deep.
3. When firm put an olive in each mould (keeping olives in place by means of small wooden skewers) and add aspic by spoonfuls until moulds are filled.
4. Chill thoroughly, remove to circular slices of liver sausage, garnish with green butter forced through a pastry bag and tube, yolks of hard-boiled eggs forced through a strainer, and red peppers cut in fancy shapes.
