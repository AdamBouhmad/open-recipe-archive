## Ingredients

- oysters
- whole pepper
- blades of mace
- grated nutmeg
- butter
- flour
- bread

## Directions

1. Put the oysters into a sieve, and set it on a pan to drain the liquor from them.
2. Then cut off the hard part, and put the oysters into a stew-pan with some whole pepper, a few blades of mace, and some grated nutmeg.
3. Add a small piece of butter rolled in flour.
4. Then pour over them about half of the liquor, or a little more.
5. Set the pan on hot coals, and simmer them gently about five minutes.
6. Try one, and if it tastes raw cook them a little longer.
7. Make some thin slices of toast, having cut off all the crust.
8. Butter the toast and lay it in the bottom of a deep dish.
9. Put the oysters upon it with the liquor in which they were stewed.
