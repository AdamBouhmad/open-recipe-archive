## Ingredients

- An old chicken
- half a pound of corned ham
- an onion
- four quarts of cold water
- half a cup of rice
- salt
- pepper
- a bunch of chopped parsley
- two cups of rich milk
- a little flour
- a spoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Cut it up into quarters, put it into a soup kettle with half a pound of corned ham, and an onion
2. Add four quarts of cold water
3. Bring slowly to a gentle boil, and keep this up until the liquid has diminished one-third, and the meat drops from the bones
4. Then add half a cup of rice
5. Season with salt, pepper and a bunch of chopped parsley
6. Cook slowly until the rice is tender, then the meat should be taken out
7. Stir in two cups of rich milk thickened with a little flour
8. The chicken could be fried in a spoonful of butter and a gravy made, reserving some of the white part of the meat, chopping it and adding it to the soup
