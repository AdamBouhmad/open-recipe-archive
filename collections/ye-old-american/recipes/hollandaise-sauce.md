## Ingredients

- half a teacupful of butter
- the juice of half a lemon
- the yolk of two eggs
- a speck of cayenne pepper
- half a cupful of boiling water
- half a teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Add the yolks of eggs one by one
3. Add the lemon juice, pepper and salt, beating all thoroughly
4. Place the bowl in which is the mixture in a saucepan of boiling water
5. Beat with an egg-beater until it begins to thicken which will be in about a minute
6. Add the boiling water, beating all the time
7. Stir until it begins to thicken like soft custard
8. Stir a few minutes after taking from the fire
9. Be careful not to cook it too long
