## Ingredients

- ice cream
- grated vanilla chocolate
- flower

## Directions

1. Line two and one-half inch flower-pots with paraffine paper
2. Fill with ice cream
3. Cover cream with grated vanilla chocolate to represent earth
4. Insert a flower in each
