## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of sour cherries
- 4 lbs. of sweet cherries
- 2½ lbs. white sugar
- 1 gallon best whiskey

## Directions

1. Crush the cherries to pieces by pounding in a deep wooden vessel with a smooth billet of wood
2. Beat hard enough to crack all the stones
3. Put into a deep stone jar, mix in the sugar well, and cover with the whiskey
4. Shake around briskly and turn into a demijohn
5. Cork tightly and let it stand a month, shaking it every day, and another month without touching it
6. Then strain off and bottle
7. It is better a year than six months old
