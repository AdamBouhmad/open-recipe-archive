## Ingredients

- Fish Force-meat II
- Creamed Lobster
- Béchamel or Lobster Sauce

## Directions

1. Line a buttered tablespoon with Fish Force-meat II
2. Fill with Creamed Lobster, cover with force-meat, and garnish with force-meat, forced through a pastry bag and tube, in the form of a marguerite, having the centre colored yellow
3. Slip from spoon into boiling water, and cook eight minutes
4. Serve with Béchamel or Lobster Sauce
