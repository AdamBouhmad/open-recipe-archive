## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of chocolate
- boiling water
- eight eggs
- a quart of cream or rich milk
- three table-spoonfuls of powdered white sugar
- sweetened cream or white of egg

## Directions

1. Scrape fine a quarter of a pound of chocolate, and pour on it a pint of boiling water
2. Cover it, and let it stand by the fire till it has dissolved, stirring it twice
3. Beat eight eggs very light, omitting the whites of two
4. Stir them by degrees into a quart of cream or rich milk, alternately with the melted chocolate, and three table-spoonfuls of powdered white sugar
5. Put the mixture into cups, and bake it about ten minutes
6. Send them to table cold, with sweetened cream, or white of egg beaten to a stiff froth, and heaped on the top of each custard
