## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- water
- fresh lard or butter
- bread crumbs
- sage
- onions
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- apple sauce
- pickles

## Directions

1. Score the skin in strips about a quarter of an inch apart
2. Place it in a dripping-pan with a very little water under it
3. Cook it moderately at first, as a high heat hardens the rind before the meat is heated through
4. If it is very lean, rub with fresh lard or butter when put into the pan
5. A stuffing might be made of bread crumbs, chopped sage and onions, pepper and salt, and baked separately on a pie dish
6. A loin weighing about six pounds will roast in two hours; allow more time if it should be very fat
7. Make a gravy with flour stirred into the pork drippings
8. Serve with apple sauce and pickles
