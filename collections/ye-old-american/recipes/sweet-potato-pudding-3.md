## Ingredients

- large sweet potato, weighing two pounds
- half a pound of sugar
- half a pound of butter
- one gill of sweet cream
- one gill of strong wine or brandy
- one grated nutmeg
- little lemon peel
- four eggs
- puff paste
- preserved citron or quince marmalade
- granulated white sugar
- rich milk

## Directions

1. Boil the potato until thoroughly done
2. Mash up fine, and while hot add the sugar and butter
3. Set aside to cool while you beat the eggs light and add the seasoning last
4. Line tin plates with puff paste
5. Pour in the mixture
6. Bake in a moderate but regularly heated oven
7. When the puddings are drawn from the fire, cover the top with thinly-sliced bits of preserved citron or quince marmalade
8. Strew the top thickly with granulated white sugar
9. Serve with the addition of a glass of rich milk for each person at table
