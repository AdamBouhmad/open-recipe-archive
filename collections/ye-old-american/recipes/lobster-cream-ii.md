## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped lobster meat
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon paprika
- Few drops onion juice
- 2 egg yolks
- ⅓ cup milk
- ⅓ cup heavy cream
- White one egg, beaten stiff

## Directions

1. Cook lobster meat with butter five minutes.
2. Add flour, seasonings, egg yolks, milk, cream beaten until stiff, and white of egg.
3. Fill buttered timbale moulds three-fourths full, set in pan of hot water, cover with buttered paper, and bake until firm.
4. Serve with Lobster Sauce.
