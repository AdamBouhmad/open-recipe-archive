## Ingredients

- fresh mackerel
- salted water
- walnut catsup, tablespoonful
- anchovy paste or sauce
- lemon, half
- butter, size of an egg
- browned flour, tablespoonful
- cold water
- parsley
- nasturtium blossoms

## Directions

1. Clean the mackerel and wipe carefully with a dry, clean cloth.
2. Wash them lightly with another cloth dipped in vinegar.
3. Wrap each in a coarse linen cloth (floured) basted closely to the shape of the fish.
4. Put them into a pot with enough salted water to cover them, and boil them gently for three quarters of an hour.
5. Drain them well.
6. Take a teacupful of the water in which they were boiled, and put into a saucepan with a tablespoonful of walnut catsup, some anchovy paste or sauce, and the juice of half a lemon.
7. Let this boil up well and add a lump of butter the size of an egg, with a tablespoonful browned flour wet in cold water.
8. Boil up again and serve in the sauce-boat.
9. Garnish with parsley and nasturtium blossoms.
