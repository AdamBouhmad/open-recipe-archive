## Ingredients

- one teacupful oatmeal
- a quart of cold water
- a teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Add to one teacupful oatmeal a quart of cold water and a teaspoonful of salt
2. Put in a steamer over a kettle of cold water
3. Gradually heat and steam an hour and a half after it begins to cook
