## Ingredients

- rock fish (head and shoulders)
- cold water and salt
- scraped horse-radish
- melted butter
- chopped parsley
- anchovy butter
- roe and liver
- gill of water
- lump of butter
- lemon pickle
- pepper vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the rock nicely
2. put it into the fish kettle with cold water and salt
3. boil it gently and skim it well
4. when done, drain off the water
5. lay it in the dish and garnish with scraped horse-radish
6. have two boats of butter nicely melted with chopped parsley, or for a change, you may have anchovy butter
7. fry the roe and liver and serve in separate dishes
8. if any of the rock be left, pick it in small pieces
9. put it in a stew pan with a gill of water, a good lump of butter, some salt, a large spoonful of lemon pickle, and one of pepper vinegar
10. shake it over the fire till perfectly hot
11. serve it up
