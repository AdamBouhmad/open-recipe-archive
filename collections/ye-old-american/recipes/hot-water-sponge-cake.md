## Ingredients

- Yolks 2 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- ⅜ cup hot water or milk
- ¼ teaspoon lemon extract
- Whites two eggs
- 1 cup flour
- 1½ teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored, add one-half the sugar gradually, and continue beating
2. Then add water, remaining sugar, lemon extract, whites of eggs beaten until stiff, and flour mixed and sifted with baking powder and salt
3. Bake twenty-five minutes in a moderate oven in a buttered and floured shallow pan
