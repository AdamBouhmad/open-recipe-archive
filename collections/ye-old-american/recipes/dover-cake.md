## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 1 lb. white sugar
- ½ lb. butter
- 6 eggs
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoonful soda dissolved in vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful powdered cinnamon
- 1 tablespoonful rose-water
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with the sugar to a very light cream. Flavor the frosting with lemon-juice.
