## Ingredients

- crabs
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- cracker or bread crumbs
- eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Put the crabs into a kettle of boiling water, and throw in a handful of salt
2. Boil from twenty minutes to half an hour
3. Take them from the water when done and pick out all the meat, being careful not to break the shell
4. To a pint of meat put a little salt and pepper; taste, and if not enough add more, a little at a time, till suited
5. Grate in a very little nutmeg and add one spoonful of cracker or bread crumbs, two eggs well beaten, and two tablespoonfuls of butter
6. Stir all well together
7. Wash the shells clean, and fill each shell full of the mixture
8. Sprinkle crumbs over the top and moisten with the liquor
9. Set in the oven till of a nice brown; a few minutes will do it
10. Send to the table hot, arranged on large dishes
