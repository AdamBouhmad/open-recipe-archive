## Ingredients

- loin of veal
- salt
- water
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. See that your fire is clear and hot, and broad enough to brown both ends.
2. Cover the fat of the kidney and the back with paper to prevent it from scorching.
3. A large loin of veal will require at least four hours and a half to roast it sufficiently.
4. At first set the roaster at a tolerable distance from the fire that the meat may heat gradually in the beginning; afterwards place it nearer.
5. Put a little salt and water into the dripping-pan and baste the meat with it till the gravy begins to drop.
6. Then baste with the gravy.
7. When the meat is nearly done, move it close to the fire, dredge it with a very little flour, and baste it with butter.
8. Skim the fat from the gravy, which should be thickened by shaking in a very small quantity of flour.
