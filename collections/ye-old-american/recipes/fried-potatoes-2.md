## Ingredients

- cold potatoes
- butter
- beaten yolk of egg

## Directions

1. Take cold potatoes that have been boiled
2. Grate them, make them into flat cakes, and fry them in butter
3. You may mix some beaten yolk of egg with them
4. Cold potatoes may be fried in slices or quarters, or broiled on a gridiron
