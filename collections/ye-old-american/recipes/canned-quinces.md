## Ingredients

- quinces
- sweet apples

## Directions

1. Wipe, quarter, core, and pare quinces
2. Follow Directions for Canning
3. Quinces may be cooked with an equal weight of sweet apples wiped, quartered, cored, and pared
4. In this case use no extra sugar for apples
