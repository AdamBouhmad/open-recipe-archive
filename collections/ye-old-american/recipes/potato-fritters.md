## Ingredients

- 2 cups hot riced potatoes
- 2 tablespoons cream
- 2 tablespoons wine
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Few gratings nutmeg
- Few grains cayenne
- 3 eggs
- Yolks 2 eggs
- ½ cup flour
- deep fat

## Directions

1. Add cream, wine, and seasonings to potatoes; then add eggs well beaten, having bowl containing mixture in pan of ice-water, and beat until cold.
2. Add flour, and when well mixed, drop by spoonfuls in deep fat, fry until delicately browned, and drain on brown paper.
