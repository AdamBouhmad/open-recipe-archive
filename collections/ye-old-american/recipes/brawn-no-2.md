## Ingredients

- Pig's head, feet, and ears
- ½ teaspoonful black pepper
- ½ teaspoonful cayenne pepper
- 4 teaspoonfuls powdered sage
- 1 teaspoonful mace
- An onion minced
- Salt and saltpetre
- Two tablespoonfuls melted butter

## Directions

1. Soak the head twelve hours, and lay in a strong brine, with a tablespoonful of saltpetre
2. Let it lie three days in this; rinse; then boil it until you can draw out the bones
3. Do this very carefully from the back and under-side of the head, breaking the outline of the top as little as possible
4. Chop the meat of the feet and ears, which should have been boiled with the head, season to taste with the spices indicated, beat in the brains, or two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
5. Fill up the hollows left by the removal of the bones with this mixture
6. Tie in a flannel cloth, sewing this tightly into the shape of the head; boil an hour and a quarter, and set aside to drain and cool
7. Do not remove the cloth until next day
