## Ingredients

- three ounces of plain chocolate
- one pound of powdered sugar
- whites of three eggs
- butter
- flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put three ounces of plain chocolate in a pan and melt on a slow fire
2. Work it to a thick paste with one pound of powdered sugar and the whites of three eggs
3. Roll the mixture down to the thickness of about one-quarter of an inch
4. Cut it in small, round pieces with a paste-cutter, either plain or scalloped
5. Butter a pan slightly, and dust it with flour and sugar in equal quantities
6. Place in it the pieces of paste or mixture
7. Bake in a hot but not too quick oven
