## Ingredients

- Lemon Ice
- ¾ teaspoon Maraschino per glass
- bananas cut in one-fourth inch slices
- candied cherries cut in halves
- Malaga grapes from which skins and seeds have been removed
- angelica cut in strips

## Directions

1. Serve Lemon Ice in champagne glasses
2. Put three-fourths teaspoon Maraschino in each glass
3. garnish with bananas cut in one-fourth inch slices, and slices cut in quarters, candied cherries cut in halves, Malaga grapes from which skins and seeds have been removed, and angelica cut in strips
