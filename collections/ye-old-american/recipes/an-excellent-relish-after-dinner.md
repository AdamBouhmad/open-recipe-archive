## Ingredients

- soup or gravy from any of the dishes on the table
- pepper
- vinegar
- wine
- catsup
- salt
- the legs, liver, and gizzard of a turkey, the kidney of veal, or any thing you fancy

## Directions

1. Put some soup or gravy from any of the dishes on the table, into the stew dish
2. add a good portion of pepper, vinegar, wine, catsup and salt
3. let it be very highly seasoned
4. broil the legs, liver, and gizzard of a turkey, the kidney of veal, or any thing you fancy
5. cut it up in small pieces
6. when broiled, put it in the gravy, and stew it at table
