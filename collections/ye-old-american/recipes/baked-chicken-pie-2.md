## Ingredients

- chicken
- pork
- potatoes
- pastry
- three hard-boiled eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. If using potatoes, parboil them before putting them into the pie, as the first water in which they are boiled is rank and unwholesome.
2. Slice the potatoes and lay next the pastry squares; then another layer of pork, and so on until the chicken is used up.
3. Cover with pastry rolled out quite thick, and slit this in the middle.
4. Bake in a buttered pudding-dish.
5. Chop up the hard-boiled eggs and strew among the pieces of chicken.
6. If the chickens are tough, or even doubtful, parboil them before making the pie, adding the water in which they were boiled for gravy.
7. If they are lean, put in a few bits of butter.
8. Ornament with leaves cut out with a cake-cutter, and a star in the centre.
9. Bake an hour—more, if the pie is large.
