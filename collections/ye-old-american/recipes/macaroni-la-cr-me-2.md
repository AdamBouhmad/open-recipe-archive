## Ingredients

- macaroni
- boiling water
- cupful milk
- salt
- cup milk
- teaspoonful flour
- tablespoonful butter
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni ten minutes in boiling water.
2. Drain this off and add a cupful of milk with a little salt.
3. Stew until tender.
4. In another saucepan heat a cup of milk to boiling.
5. Thicken with a teaspoonful of flour and stir in a tablespoonful of butter.
6. Add lastly a beaten egg.
7. When this thickens, pour over the macaroni after it is dished.
