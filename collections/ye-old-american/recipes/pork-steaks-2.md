## Ingredients

- pork steaks from the loin or neck
- pepper
- salt
- sage
- minced onion
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and trim neatly.
2. Broil over a clear fire, without seasoning.
3. Add pepper, salt, a pinch of sage, another of minced onion, and a lump of butter after they are put into the hot dish.
4. Cover closely and set in the oven for five minutes, until the aroma of the condiments flavors the meat.
5. You can cook spare-rib in the same manner.
