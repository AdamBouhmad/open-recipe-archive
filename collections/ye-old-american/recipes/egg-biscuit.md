## Ingredients

- a quart of dry flour
- three heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- two well-beaten eggs
- a tablespoonful of sugar
- a teaspoonful of salt
- one cup of milk

## Directions

1. Sift together flour and baking powder
2. Rub in butter thoroughly
3. Add eggs, sugar, and salt
4. Mix all together quickly into a soft dough with milk
5. Roll out nearly half an inch thick
6. Cut into biscuits
7. Bake immediately in a quick oven from fifteen to twenty minutes
