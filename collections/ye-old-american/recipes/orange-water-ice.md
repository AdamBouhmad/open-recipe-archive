## Ingredients

- four pounds of the best double refined loaf-sugar
- a quart of water
- four dozen large ripe deep-coloured oranges
- white of one egg

## Directions

1. Roll the oranges on the table under your hand to increase the quantity of juice
2. Wash and wipe them dry
3. Take pieces of the sugar and rub them on half the oranges till you have taken off on the sugar their yellow rind or zest
4. Put that sugar with the remainder into a porcelain kettle and pour on it a quart of water into which has been beaten the white of one egg
5. When the sugar is quite melted, set the kettle on the fire and boil and skim it till the scum ceases to rise and the orange-zest is entirely dissolved
6. Stir in gradually the juice of the oranges
7. When all is in, take it directly off the fire, lest the flavour of the juice should be weakened by boiling
8. Let it cool, stirring it well
9. Put it into a freezer surrounded by pounded ice and salt and stir it hard for the first ten minutes
10. Take off the lid and repeat the stirring every five minutes till the freezing is accomplished
11. Turn it out into a glass bowl, having first washed off the ice and salt from the outside of the freezer
12. Serve it on saucers
13. Alternatively, after it has congealed in the freezer, transfer it to a pyramid or pine-apple mould and freeze it a second time for half an hour or more
14. Before turning it out, hold round the outside of the mould a cloth dipped in cold water
