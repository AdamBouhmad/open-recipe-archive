## Ingredients

- good crust
- ripe cherries
- cupful of sugar
- teaspoonful of sifted flour
- bits of butter

## Directions

1. Line your pie plate with good crust
2. Fill half full with ripe cherries
3. Sprinkle over them about a cupful of sugar and a teaspoonful of sifted flour
4. Dot a few bits of butter over that
5. Fill the crust full to the top
6. Cover with the upper crust and bake
7. The cherries should be stoned
