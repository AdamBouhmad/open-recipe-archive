## Ingredients

- one quart of warm water
- wheat flour
- half a gill of home-made yeast
- three pounds and a half of rye flour
- a dessertspoonful of salt
- half a small teaspoonful of soda
- warm water

## Directions

1. stir as much wheat flour as will make a smooth batter into warm water
2. stir into it half a gill of home-made yeast and set it in a warm place to rise
3. in the morning, put three pounds and a half of rye flour into a bowl or tray
4. make a hollow in the centre and pour in the sponge
5. add a dessertspoonful of salt and half a small teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little water
6. make the whole into a smooth dough with as much warm water as may be necessary
7. knead it well, cover it, and let it set in a warm place for three hours
8. knead it again and make it into two or three loaves
9. bake in a quick oven one hour if made in two loaves, or less if the loaves are smaller
