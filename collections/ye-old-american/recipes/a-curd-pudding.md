## Ingredients

- two quarts of milk
- half a pint of white wine
- six ounces of butter
- half a pound of loaf sugar
- half a pint of rice flour or crackers
- six eggs
- half a grated nutmeg
- slices of citron
- melted butter
- sugar
- wine

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of milk on the fire
2. When it boils, pour in half a pint of white wine
3. Strain the curd from the whey, and pound it in a mortar
4. Mix with six ounces of butter, half a pound of loaf sugar, and half a pint of rice flour, or as much crackers beaten as fine as flour
5. Add six eggs made light, and half a grated nutmeg
6. Beat all well together, and bake them in saucers in a moderate oven
7. Turn them out carefully in your dish
8. Stick thin slices of citron in them, and pour on rich melted butter, with sugar and wine
