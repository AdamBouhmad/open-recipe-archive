## Ingredients

- white beans
- 1 quart of dried or canned corn
- salt pork
- stewed tomato

## Directions

1. Prepare white beans according to the bean soup receipt.
2. If using dried corn, soak it overnight in warm water—changing this early in the morning, and pouring on more warm water, barely enough to cover the corn, and keeping it in a close vessel until ready to put on the beans.
3. Let all boil together, with pork as in the bean soup proper.
4. Strain out as usual, rubbing hard through the cullender.
5. To the beans and corn may be added a pint of stewed tomato, if desired.
