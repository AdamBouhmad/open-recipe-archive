## Ingredients

- medium-sized ham
- flour
- water
- cut paper
- bread raspings

## Directions

1. Take a medium-sized ham and place it to soak for ten or twelve hours
2. Cut away the rusty part from underneath and wipe it dry
3. Cover it rather thickly over with a paste made of flour and water
4. Put it into an earthen dish and set it in a moderately heated oven
5. When done, take off the crust carefully and peel off the skin
6. Put a frill of cut paper around the knuckle and raspings of bread over the fat of the ham, or serve it glazed and garnished with cut vegetables
7. It will take about four or five hours to bake it
