## Ingredients

- 3 cups Graham flour
- 1 cup white flour
- 1 quart of milk
- ¾ cup yeast
- 1 tablespoonful lard or butter
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar

## Directions

1. Set to rise over night
2. Bake in muffin-rings twenty minutes in a quick oven
3. Eat hot
