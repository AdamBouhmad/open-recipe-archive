## Ingredients

- sweetbread
- salt
- pepper
- crumbs
- egg
- butter
- deep fat
- Tomato Sauce

## Directions

1. Parboil a sweetbread, split, and cut in pieces shaped like a small cutlet, or cut in circular pieces.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs, sauté in butter or fry in deep fat.
3. Serve with Tomato Sauce.
