## Ingredients

- salt mackerel
- water
- butter
- cream
- pepper
- milk
- cornstarch
- parsley
- egg
- lemon
- water-cress

## Directions

1. Wash and clean off all the brine and salt
2. Put it to soak with the meat side down, in cold water over night
3. In the morning rinse it in one or two waters
4. Wrap each up in a cloth and put it into a kettle with considerable water, which should be cold
5. Cook about thirty minutes
6. Take it carefully from the cloth, take out the backbones and pour over a little melted butter and cream
7. Add a light sprinkle of pepper, or make a cream sauce
8. Heat a small cup of milk to scalding
9. Stir into it a teaspoonful of cornstarch wet up with a little water
10. When this thickens, add two tablespoonfuls of butter, pepper, salt and chopped parsley, to taste
11. Beat an egg light, pour the sauce gradually over it, put the mixture again over the fire, and stir one minute, not more
12. Pour upon the fish, and serve it with some slices of lemon, or a few sprigs of parsley or water-cress, on the dish as a garnish
