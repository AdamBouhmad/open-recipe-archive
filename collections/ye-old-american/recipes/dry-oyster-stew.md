## Ingredients

- oysters
- butter
- white pepper

## Directions

1. Take six to twelve large oysters and cook them in half a pint of their own liquor.
2. Season with butter and white pepper.
3. Cook for five minutes, stirring constantly.
4. Serve in hot soup plates or bowls.
