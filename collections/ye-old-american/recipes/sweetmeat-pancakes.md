## Ingredients

- large red beet-root that has been boiled tender
- batter
- beet juice or cochineal dissolved in brandy
- lard or fresh butter
- raspberry jam or marmalade
- powdered sugar
- sweetened cream

## Directions

1. Take a large red beet-root that has been boiled tender; cut it up and pound it in a mortar till you have sufficient juice for colouring the pancakes
2. Make a batter as in the preceding receipt, and stir into it at the last enough of the beet juice to give it a fine pink colour
3. Or instead of the beet juice, you may use a little cochineal dissolved in a very small quantity of brandy
4. Fry the pancakes in a pan greased with lard or fresh butter
5. As fast as they are done, spread thickly over them raspberry jam or any sort of marmalade
6. Roll them up nicely, and trim off the ends
7. Lay them, side by side, on a large dish, and strew powdered sugar over them
8. Send them to table hot, and eat them with sweetened cream
