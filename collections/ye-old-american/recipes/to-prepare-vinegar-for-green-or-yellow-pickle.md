## Ingredients

- one pound of ginger
- one pound of horse-radish
- one pound of mustard seed
- one ounce long pepper
- one ounce of mace
- one ounce of nutmegs
- two gallons of strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice and dry the ginger
2. Scrape and dry the horse-radish
3. Wash and dry the mustard seed
4. Pound the long pepper, mace, and nutmegs finely
5. Put all these ingredients in a pot
6. Pour two gallons of strong vinegar on them
7. Let it stand twelve months, stirring it very frequently
8. When this vinegar is used for the pickles, put two gallons more vinegar with some mace and nutmegs, and keep it for another year
9. When the prepared vinegar is poured from the ingredients, do it very carefully that it may be quite clear
