## Ingredients

- eight quinces
- three-fourths cup sugar
- one and one-half cups water

## Directions

1. Wipe, quarter, core, and pare eight quinces.
2. Put in a baking dish, sprinkle with three-fourths cup sugar, add one and one-half cups water, cover, and cook until soft in a slow oven.
3. Quinces require a long time for cooking.
