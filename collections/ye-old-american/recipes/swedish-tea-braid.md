## Ingredients

- Swedish Bread mixture
- egg yolk
- cold water
- blanched almonds, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cut off three pieces of mixture of equal size and roll, using the hands, in pieces of uniform size
2. Braid the pieces
3. Put on a buttered sheet, cover, let rise
4. Brush over with yolk of one egg, slightly beaten, and diluted with one-half tablespoon cold water
5. Sprinkle with finely chopped blanched almonds
6. Bake in a moderate oven
