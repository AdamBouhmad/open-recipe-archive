## Ingredients

- Russian Pilaf
- Chicken Force-meat II
- Buttered paper
- Brown Mushroom Sauce
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cover bottom of cutlet moulds with Russian Pilaf and cover Pilaf with Chicken Force-meat II (doubling the recipe and omitting nutmeg).
2. Set moulds in pan of hot water, cover with buttered paper, and bake in a moderate oven fifteen minutes.
3. Remove from moulds to serving dish, surround with Brown Mushroom Sauce, and garnish with parsley.
