## Ingredients

- young squashes
- cream
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. gather young squashes
2. peel, and cut them in two
3. take out the seeds
4. boil them till tender
5. put them into a colander
6. drain off the water
7. rub them with a wooden spoon through the colander
8. put them into a stew pan, with a cup full of cream, a small piece of butter, some pepper and salt
9. stew them, stirring frequently until dry
