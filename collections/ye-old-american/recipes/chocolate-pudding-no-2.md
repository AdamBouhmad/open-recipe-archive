## Ingredients

- one quart of sweet milk
- three-quarters of a cupful of grated chocolate
- yolks of five eggs
- one cupful of sugar
- vanilla
- whites of five eggs
- four tablespoonfuls of fine sugar

## Directions

1. Scald the milk and chocolate together
2. When cool, add the yolks of five eggs, one cupful of sugar; flavor with vanilla
3. Bake about twenty-five minutes
4. Beat the five whites of eggs to a stiff froth, adding four tablespoonfuls of fine sugar
5. Spread evenly over the top and brown slightly in the oven
