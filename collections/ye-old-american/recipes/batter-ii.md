## Ingredients

- 1 cup bread flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅔ cup water
- ½ tablespoon olive oil
- White 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt.
2. Add water gradually, then olive oil and white of egg beaten until stiff.
