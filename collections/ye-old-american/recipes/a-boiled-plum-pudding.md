## Ingredients

- a twelve cent loaf of bread
- a quart of rich milk
- a small bunch of peach leaves
- a pound of currants
- a pound of raisins
- three large table-spoonfuls of flour
- a pound of brown sugar
- three quarters of a pound of beef suet
- two beaten nutmegs
- a large table-spoonful of powdered mace and cinnamon
- grated peel and juice of two large lemons or oranges
- ten eggs
- a large glass of brandy
- one glass of white wine
- half a pound of citron cut in slips
- half a pound of almonds blanched and split in half lengthways

## Directions

1. Grate the crumb of a twelve cent loaf of bread
2. Boil a quart of rich milk with a small bunch of peach leaves in it
3. Strain it and set it out to cool
4. Pick, wash and dry a pound of currants
5. Stone and cut in half a pound of raisins
6. Strew over them three large table-spoonfuls of flour
7. Roll fine a pound of brown sugar
8. Mince as fine as possible three quarters of a pound of beef suet
9. Prepare two beaten nutmegs
10. Prepare a large table-spoonful of powdered mace and cinnamon
11. Prepare the grated peel and the juice of two large lemons or oranges
12. Beat ten eggs very light
13. When it is cold, stir them gradually into the milk, alternately with the suet and grated bread
14. Add, by degrees, the sugar, fruit, and spice, with a large glass of brandy, and one of white wine
15. Mix the whole very well, and stir it hard
16. Put it into a thick cloth that has been scalded and floured
17. Leave room for it to swell, and tie it very firmly, pasting the tying-place with a small lump of moistened flour
18. Put the pudding into a large pot of boiling water, and boil it steadily six hours, replenishing the pot occasionally from a boiling kettle
19. Turn the pudding frequently in the pot
20. Prepare half a pound of citron cut in slips
21. Prepare half a pound of almonds blanched and split in half lengthways
22. Stick the almonds and the citron all over the outside of the pudding as soon as you take it out of the cloth
23. Send it to table hot, and eat it with wine sauce, or with cold wine and sugar
