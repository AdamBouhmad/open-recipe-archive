## Ingredients

- strawberries
- powdered sugar
- paste
- 1 small cup of milk (half cream, if you can get it)
- whites of two eggs
- 1 tablespoonful white sugar
- ½ teaspoonful corn-starch

## Directions

1. Make in the same manner as Raspberry Cream Tart: Line a dish with paste and fill with strawberries, made very sweet with powdered sugar.
2. Cover with paste, but do not pinch it down at the edges.
3. Heat the milk to boiling.
4. Beat the whites of two eggs light and stir into the boiling milk.
5. Add 1 tablespoonful white sugar.
6. Wet ½ teaspoonful corn-starch in cold milk and add to the mixture.
7. Boil these ingredients three minutes; let them get perfectly cold before you put them into the tart.
8. Lift the top crust, which should be thicker than usual, and pour the cooled mixture upon the fruit.
9. Replace the top crust, and set the pie aside to cool.
10. Sprinkle sugar over the top before serving.
