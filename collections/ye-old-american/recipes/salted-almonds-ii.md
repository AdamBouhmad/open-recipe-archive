## Ingredients

- almonds
- one-third cup fat
- half lard and half clarified butter or all cocoanut butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Prepare almonds as for Salted Almonds I.
2. Fry in one-third cup fat, using half lard and half clarified butter or all cocoanut butter.
3. Drain, and sprinkle with salt.
