## Ingredients

- eight medium-sized potatoes
- a large onion
- some herbs
- salt
- pepper
- rich milk
- a lump of butter

## Directions

1. Peel and boil eight medium-sized potatoes with a large onion sliced, some herbs, salt and pepper.
2. Press all through a colander.
3. Thin it with rich milk and add a lump of butter, more seasoning, if necessary.
4. Let it heat well and serve hot.
