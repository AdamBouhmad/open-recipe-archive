## Ingredients

- 6 cat-fish, in average weight half a pound apiece
- ½ lb. salt pork
- 1 pint milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 head of celery, or a small bag of celery seed
- two quarts of water
- chopped sweet herbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the fish and cut them up
2. Chop the pork into small pieces
3. Put these together into the pot, with two quarts of water, chopped sweet herbs, and the celery seasoning
4. Boil for an hour, or until fish and pork are in rags, and strain, if you desire a regular soup for a first course
5. Return to the saucepan and add the milk, which should be already hot
6. Add the eggs, beaten to a froth, and a lump of butter the size of a walnut
7. Boil up once, and serve with dice of toasted bread on the top
8. Pass sliced lemon, or walnut or butternut pickles with it
