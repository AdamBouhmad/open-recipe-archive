## Ingredients

- balm of Gilead flowers
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Put loosely into a bottle as many balm of Gilead flowers as will come up to a third part of its height
2. Nearly fill up the bottle with sweet oil, which should be of the best quality
3. Let it infuse, shaking it occasionally, for several days
