## Ingredients

- one quarter of a pound butter
- one pound sugar
- sprinkled with mace
- one pound and a quarter flour
- four eggs
- one glass rose water

## Directions

1. rub one quarter of a pound butter, one pound sugar, sprinkled with mace, into one pound and a quarter flour
2. add four eggs, one glass rose water
3. bake as No. 1
