## Ingredients

- 4 pound of sugar
- 3 and a half pound of shortning (half butter and half lard)
- 9 pound of flour
- 1 dozen of eggs
- 2 ounces of cinnamon
- 1 pint of milk
- 3 spoonfuls coriander seed
- 3 gills of brandy
- 1 gill of wine
- 3 gills of emptins
- 4 pounds of raisins

## Directions

1. Rub 4 pound of sugar, 3 and a half pound of shortning (half butter and half lard) into 9 pound of flour, 1 dozen of eggs, 2 ounces of cinnamon, 1 pint of milk, 3 spoonfuls coriander seed, 3 gills of brandy, 1 gill of wine, 3 gills of emptins, 4 pounds of raisins
