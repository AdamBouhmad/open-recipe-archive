## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- one pint of bread-crumbs
- one tablespoonful of butter
- one pint of dried peaches
- two well-beaten eggs
- half a cupful of sugar
- pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Boil one pint of milk and while hot turn it over a pint of bread-crumbs
2. Stir into it a tablespoonful of butter and one pint of dried peaches stewed soft
3. When all is cool, add two well-beaten eggs, half a cupful of sugar and a pinch of salt
4. Flavor to taste
5. Put into a well-buttered pudding-dish
6. Bake half an hour
