## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. Aitchbone
- 4 cups potatoes, cut in 1/4 inch slices
- 2/3 cup turnip, cut in half-inch cubes
- 2/3 cup carrot, cut in half-inch cubes
- 1/2 small onion, cut in thin slices
- 1/4 cup flour
- salt
- pepper
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Wipe meat, remove from bone, cut in one and one-half inch cubes, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and dredge with flour.
2. Cut some of the fat in small pieces and try out in frying-pan.
3. Add meat and stir constantly, that the surface may be quickly seared; when well browned, put in kettle, and rinse frying-pan with boiling water, that none of the goodness may be lost.
4. Add to meat remaining fat, and bone sawed in pieces; cover with boiling water and boil five minutes, then cook at a lower temperature until meat is tender (time required being about three hours).
5. Add carrot, turnip, and onion, with salt and pepper the last hour of cooking.
6. Parboil potatoes five minutes, and add to stew fifteen minutes before taking from fire.
7. Remove bones, large pieces of fat, and then skim.
8. Thicken with one-fourth cup flour, diluted with enough cold water to pour easily.
9. Pour in deep hot platter, and surround with dumplings.
