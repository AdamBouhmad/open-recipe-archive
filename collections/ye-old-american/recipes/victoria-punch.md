## Ingredients

- 3½ cups water
- 2 cups sugar
- ½ cup lemon juice
- ½ cup orange juice
- Grated rind two oranges
- 1 cup angelica wine
- 1 cup cider
- 1½ tablespoons gin

## Directions

1. Prepare same as Cardinal Punch
2. Strain before freezing, to remove orange rind
