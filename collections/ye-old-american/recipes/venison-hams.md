## Ingredients

- venison
- salt
- brown sugar
- West India molasses
- fine salt
- bran
- hickory wood

## Directions

1. Mix together equal quantities of salt and brown sugar, and rub it well into the hams.
2. Put them into a tub, and let them lie seven days; turning them and rubbing them daily with the mixture of salt and sugar.
3. Next mix together equal quantities of West India molasses and fine salt.
4. Rub it over your hams, and let them lie in it a week longer.
5. Then wipe them, rub them with bran, and smoke them a fortnight over hickory wood.
6. Pack them in wood ashes; or in charcoal, if to go to sea.
