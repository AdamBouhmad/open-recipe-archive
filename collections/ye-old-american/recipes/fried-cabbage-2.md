## Ingredients

- butter
- cold boiled cabbage or steamed cabbage
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place an ounce of butter in a frying pan and heat it boiling hot
2. Take cold boiled cabbage chopped fine, or cabbage hot, cooked the same as steamed cabbage
3. Put it into the hot butter and fry a light brown
4. Add two tablespoonfuls of vinegar
5. Serve
