## Ingredients

- three tablespoonfuls of butter
- three tablespoonfuls of sifted flour
- quarter of a teaspoonful of nutmeg
- ten peppercorns
- a teaspoonful of salt
- three slices of onion
- two slices of carrot
- two sprigs of parsley
- two sprigs of thyme
- a bay leaf
- half a dozen mushrooms
- a pint of stock or water
- a cup of sweet cream

## Directions

1. Put three tablespoonfuls of butter in a saucepan
2. Add three tablespoonfuls of sifted flour, quarter of a teaspoonful of nutmeg, ten peppercorns, a teaspoonful of salt
3. Beat all well together
4. Add three slices of onion, two slices of carrot, two sprigs of parsley, two of thyme, a bay leaf and half a dozen mushrooms cut up
5. Moisten the whole with a pint of stock or water and a cup of sweet cream
6. Set it on the stove and cook slowly for half an hour, watching closely that it does not burn
7. Strain through a sieve
