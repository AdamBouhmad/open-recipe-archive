## Ingredients

- raw egg yolks
- pure olive oil
- vinegar
- made mustard
- sugar
- pepper
- salt
- onion juice
- chopped capers
- chopped cucumber pickle

## Directions

1. Take the raw yolks of two eggs, half a teacupful of pure olive oil, three tablespoonfuls of vinegar, one of made mustard, one teaspoonful of sugar, a quarter of a teaspoonful of pepper, one teaspoonful of salt, one of onion juice, one tablespoonful of chopped capers, one of chopped cucumber pickle.
2. Put together the same as mayonnaise dressing, adding the chopped ingredients the last thing.
