## Ingredients

- Dried fruit
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the fruit thoroughly
2. Soak over night in water enough to cover
3. In the morning stew slowly until nearly done in the same water
4. Sweeten to taste
5. Roll both upper and under crust thin
6. Bake
