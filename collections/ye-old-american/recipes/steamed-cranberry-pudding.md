## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 3½ cups flour
- 1¼ tablespoons baking powder
- ½ cup milk
- 1½ cups cranberries

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, and eggs well beaten
2. Mix and sift flour and baking powder
3. Add alternately with milk to first mixture
4. Stir in berries
5. Turn into buttered mould
6. Cover and steam three hours
7. Serve with thin cream, sweetened and flavored with nutmeg
