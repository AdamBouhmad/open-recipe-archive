## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cream, rich and sweet
- ½ cup sugar, powdered
- 1 glass of wine
- 1 large teaspoonful vanilla or other extract

## Directions

1. Sweeten the cream, and, when the sugar is thoroughly dissolved, churn to a strong froth
2. Lastly, stir in wine and seasoning, carefully
3. Serve at once
4. Heap in glasses, and eat with cake
