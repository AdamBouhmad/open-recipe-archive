## Ingredients

- a couple of tomatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Peel a couple of tomatoes, which split into four pieces
2. Remove the seeds and cut them into small dice
3. Fry them with a little butter until nearly done, adding salt and pepper
4. Beat the eggs and mix the tomatoes with them
5. Make the omelet as usual
6. Or stew a few tomatoes in the usual way and spread over before folding
