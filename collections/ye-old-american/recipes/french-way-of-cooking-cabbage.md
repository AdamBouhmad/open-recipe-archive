## Ingredients

- cold boiled white cabbage
- melted butter
- pepper
- salt
- cream
- eggs

## Directions

1. Chop cold boiled white cabbage and let it drain till perfectly dry
2. Stir in some melted butter to taste
3. Add pepper, salt and four tablespoonfuls of cream
4. After it is heated through add two well-beaten eggs
5. Turn the mixture into a buttered frying pan
6. Stir until it is very hot and becomes a delicate brown on the under side
7. Place a hot dish over the pan and reverse it when turned out to be served
