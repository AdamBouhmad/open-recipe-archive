## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 1 tablespoonful lard
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water
- 2 teaspoonfuls cream-tartar, sifted through the flour
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- 2 cups milk or enough to make the flour into soft dough
- 1 quart berries, chopped apples, sliced peaches, or other fruit; jam, preserves, canned fruit, or marmalade may be substituted
- sugar

## Directions

1. Roll out the crust less than half an inch thick into an oblong sheet
2. Cover thickly with the fruit and sprinkle with sugar
3. Begin at one end and roll it up closely, the fruit inside
4. Leave a narrow margin at the other end of the roll, which should be folded down closely like the flap of a pocket-book
5. Pinch the ends of the folded roll together to prevent the escape of the fruit
6. Baste up in a bag, the same size and shape as the valise
7. Flour the bag well before putting in the pudding, having previously dipped the cloth into hot water and wrung it out
8. Boil an hour and a half
9. Serve hot with sauce, and cut crosswise in slices half an inch thick
