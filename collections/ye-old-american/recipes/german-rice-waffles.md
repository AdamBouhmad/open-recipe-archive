## Ingredients

- Half a pound of rice
- Milk
- One quart of sifted flour
- Five beaten eggs
- Two spoonfuls of yeast
- Half a pound of melted butter
- A little salt
- A teacupful of warm milk

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of rice in milk until it becomes thoroughly soft
2. Remove it from the fire, stirring it constantly, and adding, a little at a time, one quart of sifted flour, five beaten eggs, two spoonfuls of yeast, a half pound of melted butter, a little salt and a teacupful of warm milk
3. Set the batter in a warm place, and, when risen, bake in the ordinary way
