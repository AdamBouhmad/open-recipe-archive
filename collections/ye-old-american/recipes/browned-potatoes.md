## Ingredients

- mashed potatoes
- egg yolk
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Mash them the same as mashed potatoes.
2. Put them into a dish that they are to be served in, smooth over the top and brush over with the yolk of an egg, or spread on a bountiful supply of butter and dust well with flour.
3. Set in the oven to brown; it will brown in fifteen minutes with a quick fire.
