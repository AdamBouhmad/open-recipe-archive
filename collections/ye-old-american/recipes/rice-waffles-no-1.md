## Ingredients

- One quart of flour
- Half a teaspoonful of salt
- One teaspoonful of sugar
- Two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- One large tablespoonful of butter
- Two eggs
- One and a half pints of milk
- One cupful of hot boiled rice

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, salt, sugar and baking powder well together
2. Rub the butter into the flour
3. Beat the eggs well, separately, and add the stiff whites last of all
