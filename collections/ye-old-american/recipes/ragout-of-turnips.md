## Ingredients

- small turnips
- butter
- sugar
- rich high seasoned gravy

## Directions

1. Peel as many small turnips as will fill a dish
2. Put them into a stew pan with some butter and a little sugar
3. Set them over a hot stove
4. Shake them about, and turn them till they are a good brown
5. Pour in half a pint of rich high seasoned gravy
6. Stew the turnips till tender, and serve them with the gravy poured over them
