## Ingredients

- two parts cold roast or boiled corned beef
- one part mashed potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- milk
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Chop the cold roast or boiled corned beef fine
2. Mix two parts beef with one part mashed potatoes, a little pepper, salt, milk, and melted butter
3. Turn all into a frying-pan, and stir until it is heated through and smoking hot, but not until it browns
4. Put into a deep dish, and if stiff enough, smooth as you would mashed potato, into a hillock
5. Alternatively, cease stirring for a few minutes, and let a brown crust form on the under side; then turn out whole into a flat dish, the brown side uppermost
6. Alternatively, mould the mixture into flat cakes; dip these in beaten egg flour, and fry in hot drippings
