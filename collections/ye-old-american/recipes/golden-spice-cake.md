## Ingredients

- yolks of seven eggs
- one whole egg
- two cupfuls of brown sugar
- one cupful of molasses
- one cupful of butter
- one large coffeecupful of sour milk
- one teaspoonful of soda
- five cupfuls of flour
- one teaspoonful of ground cloves
- two teaspoonfuls of cinnamon
- two teaspoonfuls of ginger
- one nutmeg
- a small pinch of cayenne pepper
- two cupfuls of raisins (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat eggs, sugar and butter to a light batter before putting in the molasses
2. Add the molasses, flour and milk
3. Beat it well together
4. If fruit is used, flour the raisins well and put them in last
5. Bake in a moderate oven
