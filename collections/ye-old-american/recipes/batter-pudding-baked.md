## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one pint of milk
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one teaspoonful of baking powder
- two cupfuls of sifted flour
- rich sauce

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and whites of eggs separately
2. Mix eggs, milk, salt, baking powder, and flour
3. Put the whites of the eggs in last
4. Bake in an earthen dish that can be set on the table
5. Bake forty-five minutes
6. Serve with rich sauce
