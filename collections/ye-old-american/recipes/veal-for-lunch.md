## Ingredients

- Butter
- Hard-boiled eggs in thin slices
- Veal in very thin slices
- Ham in very thin slices
- Pepper and salt
- Flour and water

## Directions

1. Butter a good-sized bowl, and line it with thin slices of hard-boiled eggs
2. Place in the bowl a layer of veal, with pepper and salt, then a layer of ham, omitting the salt, then a layer of veal, and so on, alternating with veal and ham, until the bowl is filled
3. Make a paste of flour and water as stiff as it can be rolled out
4. Cover the contents of the bowl with the paste, and over this tie a double cotton cloth
5. Put the bowl into a saucepan, or other vessel, with water just up to the rim of the bowl, and boil three hours
6. Take it from the fire, remove the cloth and paste, and let it stand until the next day
7. Turn it out and serve in very thin slices
