## Ingredients

- Recipe for Sticks
- Two teaspoons salt for each cup of dough
- Egg yolk slightly beaten and diluted with one-half tablespoon cold water (if glazed)

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for Sticks
2. Let rise and add salt to dough, allowing two teaspoons to each cup of dough
3. Shape in small sticks
4. Let rise again
5. Sprinkle with salt
6. Bake in a slow oven
7. If preferred glazed, brush over with egg yolk slightly beaten and diluted with one-half tablespoon cold water
