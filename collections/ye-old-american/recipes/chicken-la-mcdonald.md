## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold cooked chicken, cut in strips
- 3 cold boiled potatoes, cut in one-third inch slices
- 1 truffle cut in strips
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 1/2 cups scalded milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of butter, flour, and milk
2. Add chicken, potatoes, and truffle
3. As soon as heated, add seasoning
