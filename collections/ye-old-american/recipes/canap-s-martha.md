## Ingredients

- yolk one egg
- one and one-half tablespoons cream
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- one-eighth teaspoon paprika
- one-fourth teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
- cayenne
- one-fourth pound cheese
- sautéd slices of bread
- finely chopped lobster meat
- Chicken Stock or cream
- hard-boiled eggs
- lobster coral
- olives

## Directions

1. Beat yolk one egg.
2. Add one and one-half tablespoons cream, one-fourth teaspoon salt, one-eighth teaspoon paprika, one-fourth teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce, and a few grains cayenne.
3. Add one-fourth pound cheese cut in small pieces.
4. Cook until smooth, stirring constantly.
5. Spread on sautéd slices of bread, cut in fancy shapes.
6. Cover with finely chopped lobster meat held together with a thick sauce made of Chicken Stock or cream.
7. Garnish with rings of whites of hard-boiled eggs, yolks of hard-boiled eggs, and lobster coral forced through a strainer, and rings of olives.
