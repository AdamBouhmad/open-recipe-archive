## Ingredients

- best beef, lean and tender, twenty pounds
- one pint salt
- one teaspoonful saltpetre
- one quarter pound brown sugar

## Directions

1. Buy the best of beef or that part which will be the most lean and tender
2. The tender part of the round is a very good piece
3. For every twenty pounds of beef use one pint of salt, one teaspoonful of saltpetre, and a quarter of a pound of brown sugar
4. Mix them well together
5. Rub the beef well with one-third of the mixture for three successive days
6. Let it lie in the liquor it makes for six days
7. Hang up to dry
