## Ingredients

- one-half cupful of butter
- one pint of milk
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one tablespoonful of sugar
- seven cupfuls of sifted flour
- four beaten eggs
- half a cup of good lively yeast
- half a teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Warm one-half cupful of butter in a pint of milk
2. Add a teaspoonful of salt and a tablespoonful of sugar
3. Add seven cupfuls of sifted flour
4. Beat thoroughly and when the mixture is blood warm, add four beaten eggs
5. Last of all, add half a cup of good lively yeast
6. Beat hard until the batter breaks in blisters
7. Set it to rise over night
8. In the morning, dissolve half a teaspoonful of soda and stir it into the batter
9. Turn it into a well-buttered, shallow dish to rise again about fifteen or twenty minutes
10. Bake about fifteen to twenty minutes
11. The cake should be torn apart, not cut
12. Bake a light brown
