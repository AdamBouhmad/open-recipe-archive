## Ingredients

- Half a cupful of rice
- Water
- Half a teaspoonful of salt
- One cupful of milk
- A well-beaten egg
- Cream
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil half a cupful of rice in just enough water to cover it with half a teaspoonful of salt
2. When the water has boiled nearly out and the rice begins to look soft and dry, turn over it a cupful of milk
3. Let it simmer until the rice is done and nearly dry
4. Take from the fire and beat in a well-beaten egg
5. Eat it warm with cream and sugar
6. Flavor to taste
