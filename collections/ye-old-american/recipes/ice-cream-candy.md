## Ingredients

- 3 cups sugar
- ¼ teaspoon cream of tartar
- ½ cup boiling water
- ½ tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil ingredients together without stirring, until, when tried in cold water, mixture will become brittle.
2. Turn on a well buttered platter to cool.
3. As edges cool, fold towards centre.
4. As soon as it can be handled, pull until white and glossy.
5. While pulling, flavor as desired.
