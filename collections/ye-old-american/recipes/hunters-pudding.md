## Ingredients

- 1 cup finely chopped suet
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon clove
- ½ teaspoon mace
- ½ teaspoon allspice
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1½ cups raisins
- 2 tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Mix same as Suet Pudding
2. Stone, cut, and flour raisins, and add to mixture
3. Then steam
