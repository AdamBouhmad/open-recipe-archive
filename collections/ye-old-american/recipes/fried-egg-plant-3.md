## Ingredients

- fresh, purple egg-plants
- salt
- cracker or bread crumbs
- egg
- butter

## Directions

1. Take fresh, purple egg-plants of a middling size; cut them in slices a quarter of an inch thick, and soak them for half an hour in cold water, with a teaspoonful of salt in it.
2. Have ready some cracker or bread crumbs and one beaten egg; drain off the water from the slices, lay them on a napkin, dip them in the crumbs and then in the egg, put another coat of crumbs on them and fry them in butter to a light brown.
3. The frying pan must be hot before the slices are put in--they will fry in ten minutes.
