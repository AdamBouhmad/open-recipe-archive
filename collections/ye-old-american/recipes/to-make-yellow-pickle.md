## Ingredients

- articles for yellow pickle
- salt and water
- turmeric
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put all the articles intended for the yellow pickle in a pot
2. Pour boiling salt and water on them
3. Let them stand forty-eight hours
4. On a clear hot day, press the water from the articles
5. Lay them to dry in full sunshine on a table covered with a thick soft cloth with the corners pinned securely
6. Turn them frequently on a dry place until they become white and receive the colour of the turmeric more readily
7. One day of clear sunshine is enough to prepare them for the first vinegar
8. When dried, put them in a pot of plain cold vinegar with a little turmeric in it
9. Let them remain in it two weeks to draw off the water from them and to make them plump
10. Then put them in a clean pot and pour on the vinegar prepared according to directions
11. Mix the turmeric very smoothly before you add it to your pickles
