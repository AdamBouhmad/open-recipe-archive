## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- half a cup of butter
- three eggs
- half a cup of sweet milk
- two cups of flour
- two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Cream together sugar and butter
2. Add sweet milk
3. Add eggs
4. Mix baking powder into flour and add to the mixture
5. Add flavoring
6. Bake in jelly-cake tins in layers
7. Put filling between when done
