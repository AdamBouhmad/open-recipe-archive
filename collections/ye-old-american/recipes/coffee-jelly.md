## Ingredients

- ½ box gelatine or 2 tablespoons granulated gelatine
- ½ cup cold water
- 1 cup boiling water
- ⅓ cup sugar
- 2 cups boiled coffee
- Sugar
- Cream

## Directions

1. Make same as Lemon Jelly.
2. Serve with sugar and cream.
