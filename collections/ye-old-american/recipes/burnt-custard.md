## Ingredients

- a quart of milk
- eight eggs
- savoy cake
- sugar
- raspberry marmalade or preserved fruit

## Directions

1. Boil a quart of milk
2. When cold, mix with it the yelks of eight eggs
3. Stir them together over the fire a few minutes
4. Sweeten it to your taste
5. Put some slices of savoy cake in the bottom of a deep dish, and pour on the custard
6. Whip the whites of the eggs to a strong froth, lay it lightly on the top
7. Sift some sugar over it, and hold a salamander over it until it is a light brown
8. Garnish the top with raspberry marmalade, or any kind of preserved fruit
