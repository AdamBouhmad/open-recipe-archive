## Ingredients

- whites of one or more eggs
- cold water
- XXX powdered or confectioners' sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Break into a bowl the whites of one or more eggs
2. Add to it an equal quantity of cold water
3. Stir in XXX powdered or confectioners' sugar until you have it stiff enough to mold into shape with the fingers
4. Flavor with vanilla to taste
5. Form in balls, cubes or lozenge shapes
6. Lay them upon plates or waxed paper and set them aside to dry
