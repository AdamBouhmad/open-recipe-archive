## Ingredients

- one measure of jelly
- one measure of cream
- half a measure of wine
- sugar
- orange flower or rose water

## Directions

1. Boil jelly, cream, and wine together fifteen minutes over a slow fire, stirring all the time
2. Sweeten it, and add a spoonful of orange flower or rose water
3. Cool it in a mould, turn it in a dish, and pour around it cream, seasoned in any way you like
