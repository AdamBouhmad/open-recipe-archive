## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups celery
- one cup pecan nut meats
- one cup shredded cabbage
- Cream Dressing
- small white cabbage

## Directions

1. Mix one and one-half cups finely cut celery, one cup pecan nut meats broken in pieces, and one cup shredded cabbage
2. Moisten with Cream Dressing, and serve in a salad bowl made of a small white cabbage
