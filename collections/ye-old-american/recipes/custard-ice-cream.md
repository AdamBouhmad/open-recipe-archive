## Ingredients

- one quart of cream or rich milk
- half a pound of sugar
- flavoring
- a tablespoonful of cornstarch or rice flour
- milk
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Sweeten one quart of cream or rich milk with half a pound of sugar and flavor to taste
2. Put it over the fire in a farina-kettle
3. As soon as it begins to boil, stir into it a tablespoonful of cornstarch or rice flour which has been previously mixed smooth with a little milk
4. After it has boiled a few minutes, take it off the fire and stir in very gradually six eggs which have been beaten until thick
5. When quite cold, freeze it as ice-cream
