## Ingredients

- small fillets of beef
- cock's-comb shaped croûtons
- Sauce Figaro
- Brussels Sprouts or String Beans

## Directions

1. Wipe and sauté small fillets in hot omelet pan.
2. Arrange in a circle on platter with cock's-comb shaped croûtons between, and pour sauce in the centre.
3. Serve as a luncheon dish with Brussels Sprouts or String Beans.
