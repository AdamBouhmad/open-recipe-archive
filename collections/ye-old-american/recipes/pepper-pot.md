## Ingredients

- two or three pounds tripe
- knuckle of veal
- water
- pod of pepper
- spice
- sweet herbs
- salt
- dumplings
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil two or three pounds of tripe
2. Cut it in pieces
3. Put it on the fire with a knuckle of veal and a sufficient quantity of water
4. Add part of a pod of pepper, a little spice, sweet herbs according to your taste, salt, and some dumplings
5. Stew it till tender
6. Thicken the gravy with butter and flour
