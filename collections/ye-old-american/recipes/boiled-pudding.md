## Ingredients

- 1 pint grated bread crumbs
- 1/2 cupful chopped suet
- 1/2 cupful moist sugar
- salt
- 1 small tablespoonful flour
- grated rind of 1 lemon
- whites and yolks of 2 eggs
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Mix together bread crumbs, suet, sugar, salt, and flour, adding grated lemon rind
2. Moisten with well beaten egg whites and yolks and strained lemon juice
3. Stir well together and put mixture into small buttered cups
4. Tie down with cloth dipped in flour
5. Boil three-quarters of an hour
6. Turn out on a dish, strew sifted sugar over them and serve with wine sauce
