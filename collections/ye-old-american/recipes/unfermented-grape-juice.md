## Ingredients

- 10 lbs. grapes
- 1 cup water
- 3 lbs. sugar

## Directions

1. Put grapes and water in granite stewpan.
2. Heat until stones and pulp separate; then strain through jelly bag, add sugar, heat to boiling-point, and bottle.
3. This will make one gallon.
4. When served, it should be diluted one-half with water.
