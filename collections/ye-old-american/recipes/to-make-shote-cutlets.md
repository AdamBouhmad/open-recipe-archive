## Ingredients

- hind-quarter of shote
- butter
- brown flour

## Directions

1. Take the skin from the hind-quarter, and cut it in pieces
2. Prepare them in the way directed for veal cutlets
3. Make a little nice gravy with the skin and the scraps of meat left
4. Thicken it with butter and brown flour
5. Season it in any way you like
