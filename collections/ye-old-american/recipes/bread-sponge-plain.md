## Ingredients

- 1 quart warm water
- 6 tablespoonfuls baker's yeast
- 2 tablespoonfuls lard
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- Flour to make a soft batter

## Directions

1. Melt the lard in the warm water, add the sugar, then the flour by degrees, stirring in smoothly.
2. A quart and a pint of flour will usually be sufficient if the quality is good.
3. Next comes the yeast, lastly the soda.
4. Beat up hard for several minutes, and set to rise as above.
