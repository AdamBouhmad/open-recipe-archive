## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- rock-salt

## Directions

1. When gathered from the vines, wash and put in a firkin or half barrel
2. Layer cucumbers and rock-salt alternately, enough salt to make sufficient brine to cover them, no water
3. Cover with a cloth
4. Keep them under the brine with a heavy board
5. Take off the cloth and rinse it every time you put in fresh cucumbers
6. Use plenty of salt and it will keep a year
7. To prepare pickles for use, soak in hot water and keep in a warm place until they are fresh enough
8. Pour spiced vinegar over them and let them stand over night
9. Pour that off and put on fresh
