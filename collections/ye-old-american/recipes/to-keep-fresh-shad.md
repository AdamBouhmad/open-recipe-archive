## Ingredients

- fresh shad
- brown sugar
- salt
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Having cleaned the fish, split it down the back, and lay it (with the skin side downward) upon a large dish.
2. Mix together a large table-spoonful of brown sugar, a small tea-spoonful of salt, and a tea-spoonful of cayenne pepper.
3. Cover the shad with this mixture, spread on evenly, and let it rest in it till next day, (unless you want it the same evening,) keeping it in a cold place.
4. Immediately before cooking, wipe the seasoning entirely off, and dry the shad in a clean cloth.
5. Then broil it in the usual manner.
