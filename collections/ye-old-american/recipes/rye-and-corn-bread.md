## Ingredients

- one quart of rye meal or rye flour
- two quarts of Indian meal
- boiling water
- one-half cup of molasses
- two teaspoonfuls salt
- one teacup yeast
- warm water
- a level teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. place Indian meal in a pan and pour over it just enough boiling water to merely wet it, stirring constantly with a spoon
2. add rye meal or rye flour, molasses, and salt
3. make it as stiff as can be stirred with a spoon, mixing with warm water
4. let rise all night
5. in the morning add a level teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little water
6. put it in a large pan and smooth the top with the hand dipped in cold water
7. let it stand a short time
8. bake five or six hours, or if put in the oven late in the day, let it remain all night
