## Ingredients

- fresh tongue
- boiling water
- carrot
- onion
- celery
- parsley
- sauce

## Directions

1. Put tongue in kettle, cover with boiling water, and cook slowly two hours.
2. Take tongue from water and remove skin and roots.
3. Place in deep pan and surround with one-third cup each carrot, onion, and celery, cut in dice, and one sprig parsley; then pour over four cups sauce.
4. Cover closely, and bake two hours, turning after the first hour.
5. Serve on platter and strain around the sauce.
