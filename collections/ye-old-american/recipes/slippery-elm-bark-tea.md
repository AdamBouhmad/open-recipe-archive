## Ingredients

- Slippery-elm bark
- Boiling water
- Sugar
- Ice
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Break the bark into bits
2. Pour boiling water over it
3. Cover and let it infuse until cold
4. Sweeten and ice, and take for summer disorders
5. Or add lemon juice and drink for a bad cold
