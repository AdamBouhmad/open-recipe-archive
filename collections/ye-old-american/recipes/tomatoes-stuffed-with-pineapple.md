## Ingredients

- medium-sized tomatoes
- fresh pineapple
- nut meats
- Mayonnaise Dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Peel medium-sized tomatoes
2. Remove thin slice from top of each, and take out seeds and some of pulp
3. Sprinkle inside with salt, invert, and let stand one-half hour
4. Fill tomatoes with fresh pineapple cut in small cubes or shredded, and nut meats, using two-thirds pineapple and one-third nut meats
5. Mix with Mayonnaise Dressing
6. Garnish with Mayonnaise, halves of nut meats, and slices cut from tops cut square
7. Serve on a bed of lettuce leaves
