## Ingredients

- six eggs (omitting the whites of two)
- one pint of orange juice
- one pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- yellow rind of one orange

## Directions

1. Beat very light six eggs, omitting the whites of two
2. Have ready a pint of orange juice and stir it gradually into the beaten egg, alternately with a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
3. Put into a porcelain skillet the yellow rind of one orange, pared very thin
4. Pour the mixture upon it and set it over a slow fire
5. Simmer it steadily, stirring it all the time
6. When nearly ready to boil, take it off and remove the orange-peel
7. Put the mixture into glasses to get cold
