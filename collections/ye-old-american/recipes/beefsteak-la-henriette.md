## Ingredients

- one-half cup butter
- yolks of three eggs
- one tablespoon cold water
- one-half tablespoon lemon juice
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- two tablespoons tomato purée
- one tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
- one-half tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- few grains cayenne
- porterhouse steak

## Directions

1. Wash butter, and divide in three pieces.
2. Put one piece in saucepan with yolks of eggs slightly beaten and mixed with water and lemon juice.
3. Proceed same as in making Hollandaise Sauce I.
4. Add tomato, parsley, and seasonings.
5. Pour one-half sauce on a serving dish.
6. Lay a broiled porterhouse steak on sauce, and cover steak with remaining sauce.
7. Garnish with parsley.
