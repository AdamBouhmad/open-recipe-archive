## Ingredients

- 1 quart ripe red raspberries
- 1 quart strong well flavoured vinegar
- pounded loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Put a quart of ripe red raspberries in a bowl
2. Pour on them a quart of strong well flavoured vinegar
3. Let them stand twenty-four hours
4. Strain them through a bag
5. Put this liquid on another quart of fresh raspberries
6. Strain in the same manner
7. Then on a third quart
8. When this last is prepared, make it very sweet with pounded loaf sugar
9. Refine and bottle it
