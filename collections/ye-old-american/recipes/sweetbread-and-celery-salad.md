## Ingredients

- parboiled sweetbreads
- celery
- Cream Dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts of parboiled sweetbreads cut in one-half inch cubes and celery finely cut.
2. Moisten with Cream Dressing, and arrange on lettuce leaves.
