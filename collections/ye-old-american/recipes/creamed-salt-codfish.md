## Ingredients

- ¾ cup salt codfish
- 1 cup White Sauce I
- 1 beaten egg
- Hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Pick salt codfish in pieces (there should be three-fourths cup), and soak in lukewarm water, the time depending upon hardness and saltness of the fish
2. Drain, and add one cup White Sauce I
3. Add one beaten egg just before sending to table
4. Garnish with slices of hard-boiled eggs
