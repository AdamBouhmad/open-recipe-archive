## Ingredients

- Sour apples
- Sugar
- Cinnamon or nutmeg
- Lemon juice
- Lemon rind
- Boiling water
- Cream

## Directions

1. Wipe and core sour apples.
2. Put in a baking-dish, and fill cavities with sugar and spice. Allow one-half cup sugar and one-fourth teaspoon cinnamon or nutmeg to eight apples. If nutmeg is used, add a few drops lemon juice and few gratings from rind of lemon to each apple.
3. Cover bottom of dish with boiling water, and bake in a hot oven until soft, basting often with syrup in dish.
4. Serve hot or cold with cream.
