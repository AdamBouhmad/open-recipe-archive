## Ingredients

- eight or ten onions of good size
- bread crumbs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Slice the onions and boil until tender
2. Lay them in a baking-dish
3. Put in bread crumbs, butter in small bits, pepper and salt, between each layer until the dish is full, putting bread crumbs last
4. Add milk or cream until full
5. Bake twenty minutes or half an hour
