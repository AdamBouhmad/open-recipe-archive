## Ingredients

- a pint of wheat bran
- a handful of hops
- a quart of water
- four table-spoonfuls of brewer's yeast
- two of brown sugar, or four of molasses

## Directions

1. Mix a pint of wheat bran, and a handful of hops with a quart of water, and boil them together about twenty minutes.
2. Strain it through a sieve into a pan.
3. When the liquid becomes only milk-warm, stir into it four table-spoonfuls of brewer's yeast, and two of brown sugar, or four of molasses.
4. Put it into a wooden bowl, cover it, and set it near the fire for four or five hours.
5. Then bottle it, and cork it tightly next day.
