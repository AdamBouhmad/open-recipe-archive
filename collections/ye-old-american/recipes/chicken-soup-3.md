## Ingredients

- 6 cups White Stock III
- 1 tablespoon lean raw ham, finely chopped
- 6 slices carrot, cut in cubes
- 2 stalks celery
- ½ bay leaf
- ¼ teaspoon peppercorns
- 1 sliced onion
- ⅓ cup hot boiled rice

## Directions

1. Add seasonings to stock, heat gradually to boiling-point, and boil thirty minutes.
2. Strain, and add rice.
