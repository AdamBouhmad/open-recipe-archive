## Ingredients

- four-pound cod
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- Oyster Stuffing

## Directions

1. Clean the cod, sprinkle with salt and pepper, brush over with lemon juice, stuff, and sew.
2. Gash, skewer, and bake as Baked Halibut with Stuffing.
3. Serve with Oyster Sauce.
