## Ingredients

- one cup of chopped raisins seeded
- juice and grated rind of one lemon
- one cupful of cold water
- one tablespoonful of flour
- one cupful of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of chopped raisins seeded with the juice and grated rind of one lemon, one cupful of cold water, one tablespoonful of flour, one cupful of sugar and two tablespoonfuls of butter
2. Stir lightly together
3. Bake with upper and under crust
