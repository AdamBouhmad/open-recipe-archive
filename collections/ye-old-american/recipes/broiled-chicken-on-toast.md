## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- buttered toast
- cream or milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Broil the usual way and when thoroughly done take it up in a square tin or dripping-pan, butter it well, season with pepper and salt and set it in the oven for a few minutes.
2. Lay slices of moistened buttered toast on a platter; take the chicken up over it.
3. Add to the gravy in the pan part of a cupful of cream, if you have it; if not, use milk.
4. Thicken with a little flour and pour over the chicken.
