## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of chopped onion
- three tablespoonfuls of good cider vinegar
- six tablespoonfuls of water
- three tablespoonfuls of tomato catsup
- pepper and salt
- half a cup of melted butter
- one tablespoonful of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan one tablespoonful of chopped onion, three tablespoonfuls of good cider vinegar, six tablespoonfuls of water, three of tomato catsup, a little pepper and salt
2. Add half a cup of melted butter, in which stir one tablespoonful of sifted flour
3. Put all together and boil until it thickens
