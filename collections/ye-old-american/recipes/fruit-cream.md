## Ingredients

- rich, boiled custard
- wine
- vanilla
- pounded almonds
- chopped citron
- brandy
- peaches or chopped raisins

## Directions

1. Make a rich, boiled custard and flavor with wine and vanilla
2. Pour it into a freezer
3. When half frozen, add pounded almonds, chopped citron and brandy, peaches or chopped raisins
4. Have the freezer half full of custard and fill up with the fruit
5. Mix well and freeze again
