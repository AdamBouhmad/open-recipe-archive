## Ingredients

- sago
- water
- yellow peel of a lemon
- wine
- nutmeg
- milk

## Directions

1. Wash the sago through two or three waters and then let it soak for two or three hours
2. To a tea-cupful of sago allow a quart of water and some of the yellow peel of a lemon
3. Simmer it till all the grains look transparent
4. Add as much wine and nutmeg as may be proper and give it another boil altogether
