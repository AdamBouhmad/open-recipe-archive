## Ingredients

- a dozen sound, tart apples
- water
- a teacupful of sugar
- one teaspoonful of pepper
- one teaspoonful of cloves
- one teaspoonful of mustard
- two teaspoonfuls of cinnamon
- two medium-sized onions, chopped very fine
- a tablespoonful of salt
- a pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and quarter a dozen sound, tart apples; stew them until soft in as little water as possible, then pass them through a sieve.
2. To a quart of the sifted apple, add a teacupful of sugar, one teaspoonful of pepper, one of cloves, one of mustard, two of cinnamon, and two medium-sized onions, chopped very fine.
3. Stir all together, adding a tablespoonful of salt and a pint of vinegar.
4. Place over the fire and boil one hour, and bottle while hot; seal very tight.
5. It should be about as thick as tomato catsup, so that it will just run from the bottle.
