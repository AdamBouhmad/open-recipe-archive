## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- one pound of flour
- one pound of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat twelve eggs light
2. add to them one pound of flour, and one of powdered sugar
3. continue to beat all together till perfectly light
4. bake it in long pans, four inches wide, with divisions; so that each cake, when done, will be four inches long, and one and a half wide
