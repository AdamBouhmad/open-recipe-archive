## Ingredients

- beef's heart
- grated bread crumbs
- butter or minced suet
- sweet marjoram
- parsley
- grated lemon-peel
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- yolk of egg
- butter
- flour
- red wine
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Cut open the heart, and having removed the ventricles, soak it in cold water to free it from the blood.
2. Parboil it about ten minutes.
3. Prepare a force-meat of grated bread crumbs, butter or minced suet, sweet marjoram and parsley chopped fine, a little grated lemon-peel, nutmeg, pepper, and salt to your taste, and some yolk of egg to bind the ingredients.
4. Stuff the heart with the force-meat, and secure the opening by tying a string around it.
5. Put it on a spit, and roast it till it is tender throughout.
6. Add to the gravy a piece of butter rolled in flour, and a glass of red wine.
7. Serve up the heart very hot in a covered dish.
8. Eat currant jelly with it.
