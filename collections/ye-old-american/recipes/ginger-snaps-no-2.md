## Ingredients

- 1 large cup butter and lard mixed
- 1 coffee-cup sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- ½ cup water
- 1 tablespoonful ginger
- 1 tablespoonful cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful cloves
- 1 teaspoonful soda dissolved in hot water
- Flour for pretty stiff dough

## Directions

1. Roll out rather thinner than sugar cakes, and bake quickly.
2. These ginger-snaps will keep for weeks, if locked up.
