## Ingredients

- young gooseberries
- loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Select young gooseberries, make a syrup with one pound of loaf sugar to each of fruit
2. Stew them till quite clear and the syrup becomes thick, but do not let them be mashed
3. Do not cover the pan while they are stewing
