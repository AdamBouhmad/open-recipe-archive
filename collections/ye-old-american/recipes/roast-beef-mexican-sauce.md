## Ingredients

- cold roast beef
- one onion, finely chopped
- two tablespoons butter
- one red pepper
- one green pepper
- one clove of garlic
- two tomatoes peeled and cut in pieces
- one teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
- one-fourth teaspoon celery salt
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cook one onion, finely chopped, in two tablespoons butter five minutes
2. Add one red pepper, one green pepper, and one clove of garlic, each finely chopped, and two tomatoes peeled and cut in pieces
3. Cook fifteen minutes
4. add one teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce, one-fourth teaspoon celery salt, and salt to taste
5. Reheat cold roast beef cut in thin slices in this sauce
