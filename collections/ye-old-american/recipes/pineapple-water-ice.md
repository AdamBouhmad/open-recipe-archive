## Ingredients

- Pine-apple
- Sugar
- Water
- White of egg

## Directions

1. For every two pounds of sugar, make a syrup with a pint of water beaten with half the white of an egg
2. Boil and skim the syrup till quite clear
3. Get as much pine-apple juice as you can by squeezing through a sieve the bits of pine-apple after they have stood some hours in the tureen
4. Measure the juice, and to each pint of boiled syrup allow a pint of juice
5. Mix them together while the syrup is warm from the fire
6. Put it into a freezer and proceed in the usual manner
