## Ingredients

- 1½ cups orange juice
- 1½ cups sugar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- Yolks 5 eggs
- 1½ teaspoons granulated gelatine
- 3 tablespoons boiling water
- 2½ cups cream
- Candied orange peel
- Pistachio nuts

## Directions

1. Mix fruit juice, sugar, and yolks of eggs
2. Cook over boiling water until mixture thickens
3. Add gelatine dissolved in boiling water
4. Cool, freeze to a mush
5. Add whip from cream, and continue freezing
6. Mould, and serve garnished with candied orange peel and pistachio nuts
