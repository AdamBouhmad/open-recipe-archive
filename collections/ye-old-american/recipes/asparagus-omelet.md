## Ingredients

- eight or ten stalks of asparagus
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Boil with a little salt, and until about half cooked, eight or ten stalks of asparagus
2. Cut the eatable part into rather small pieces
3. Beat the egg and mix the asparagus with them
4. Make the omelet as above directed
