## Ingredients

- Whites 3 eggs
- ½ cup powdered sugar
- 1 quart cream whip
- 1½ teaspoons vanilla
- Delmonico Ice Cream

## Directions

1. Beat eggs until stiff
2. Fold in sugar, cream whip, and flavoring
3. Line a mould with Delmonico Ice Cream
4. Fill with the mixture
5. Cover, pack in salt and ice
6. Let stand two hours
