## Ingredients

- one quart milk
- six eggs
- loaf sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make a quart of milk quite hot, that it may not whey when baked
2. Let it stand to get cold, and then mix six eggs with it
3. Sweeten it with loaf sugar, and fill the custard cups
4. Put on the covers, and set them in a Dutch oven with water, but not enough to risk its boiling into the cups
5. Do not put on the top of the oven
6. When the water has boiled ten or fifteen minutes, take out a cup, and if the custard be the consistence of jelly; it is sufficiently done
7. Serve them in the cups with the covers on, and a tea-spoon on the dish between each cup
8. Grate nutmeg on the tops when cold
