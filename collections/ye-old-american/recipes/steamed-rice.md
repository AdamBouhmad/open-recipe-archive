## Ingredients

- one teacupful of rice
- one quart of milk
- a piece of butter as large as the yolk of an egg
- a pinch of salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Place rice and milk in a steamer
2. Steam from two to three hours
3. When nearly done, stir in a piece of butter as large as the yolk of an egg and a pinch of salt
4. You can use sugar if you like
