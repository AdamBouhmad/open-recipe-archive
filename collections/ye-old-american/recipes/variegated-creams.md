## Ingredients

- French Cream
- cochineal syrup
- chocolate
- alcohol

## Directions

1. Make the French Cream recipe, and divide into three parts, leaving one part white, color one pink with cochineal syrup, and the third part color brown with chocolate, which is done by just letting the cream soften and stirring in a little finely grated chocolate
2. The pink is colored by dropping on a few drops of cochineal syrup while the cream is warm and beating it in
3. Take the white cream, make a flat ball of it, and lay it upon a buttered dish, and pat it out flat until about half an inch thick
4. If it does not work easily, dip the hand in alcohol
5. Take the pink cream, work in the same way as the white and lay it upon the white
6. Then the chocolate in the same manner, and lay upon the pink, pressing all together
7. Trim the edges off smooth, leaving it in a nice, square cake, then cut into slices or small cubes, as you prefer
8. It is necessary to work it all up as rapidly as possible
