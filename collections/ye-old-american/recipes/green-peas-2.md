## Ingredients

- Peas
- Salt
- White sugar
- Mint
- Butter
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Shell and lay in cold water until you are ready to cook them.
2. Put into salted boiling water, and cook from twenty minutes to half an hour.
3. If market peas, add a small lump of white sugar and a sprig of mint.
4. Remove the mint when the peas are dished.
5. Drain well, and dish, with a great lump of butter stirred in, and a little pepper.
6. Keep hot.
