## Ingredients

- marron glacé
- rum
- vanilla ice cream
- whipped cream, sweetened
- vanilla flavoring
- candied rose leaves

## Directions

1. Break marron glacé in pieces, flavor with rum
2. Cover, and let stand one hour
3. Put in champagne glasses, allowing one and one-half marrons to each glass
4. Cover with vanilla ice cream
5. Garnish with whipped cream, sweetened and flavored with vanilla, and candied rose leaves
