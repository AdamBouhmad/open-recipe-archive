## Ingredients

- the yolks of six hard-boiled eggs
- half a tablespoonful of wheat flour
- the yolks of two raw eggs
- a teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Take the yolks of six hard-boiled eggs and half a tablespoonful of wheat flour, rub them smooth with the yolks of two raw eggs and a teaspoonful of salt.
2. Mix all well together.
3. Make it in balls, and drop them into the boiling soup a few minutes before taking it up.
