## Ingredients

- fowl
- flour
- butter
- salt
- bread crumbs
- cooked potatoes
- parsley

## Directions

1. Divide a fowl into joints and boil till the meat leaves the bone readily.
2. Take out the bones and chop the meat as small as dice.
3. Thicken the water in which the fowl was boiled with flour and season to taste with butter and salt.
4. Fill a deep dish with alternate layers of bread crumbs and chicken and slices of cooked potatoes, having crumbs on top.
5. Pour the gravy over the top and add a few bits of butter and bake till nicely browned.
6. Serve with a garnish of parsley.
