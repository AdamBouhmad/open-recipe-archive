## Ingredients

- half a pound of shelled sweet almonds
- three ounces of bitter almonds
- wine or rose-water
- eight eggs
- two-thirds of a cup of sugar
- a quart of rich milk, or part milk and part cream
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar
- a teaspoonful of lemon extract

## Directions

1. Scald and blanch half a pound of shelled sweet almonds and three ounces of bitter almonds, throwing them into a large bowl of cold water as you do them
2. Pound them one at a time into a paste, adding a few drops of wine or rose-water to them
3. Beat eight eggs very light with two-thirds of a cup of sugar
4. Mix together with a quart of rich milk, or part milk and part cream
5. Put the mixture into a saucepan and set it over the fire
6. Stir it one way until it begins to thicken, but not till it curdles
7. Remove from the fire and when it is cooled put in a glass dish
8. Beat the reserved whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
9. Season with three tablespoonfuls of sugar and a teaspoonful of lemon extract
10. Spread over the top of the custard
11. Serve cold
