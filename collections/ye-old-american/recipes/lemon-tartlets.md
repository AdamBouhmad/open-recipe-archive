## Ingredients

- Paste
- Lemon Pie II mixture
- Meringue II

## Directions

1. Bake paste as for Fruit Baskets without handles.
2. Fill with Lemon Pie II mixture, cover with Meringue II, and bake until meringue is delicately browned.
