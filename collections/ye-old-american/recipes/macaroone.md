## Ingredients

- one pound of sweet almonds
- rose water
- whites of seven eggs
- one pound of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch a pound of sweet almonds
2. Pound them in a mortar with rose water
3. Whip the whites of seven eggs to a strong froth
4. Put in one pound of powdered sugar
5. Beat it some time
6. Then put in the almonds
7. Mix them well
8. Drop them on sheets of paper buttered
9. Sift sugar over
10. Bake them quickly
11. Be careful not to let them get discoloured
