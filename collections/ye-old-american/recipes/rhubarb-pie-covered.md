## Ingredients

- rhubarb stalks
- sugar
- pie crusts
- egg
- seedless raisins (optional)

## Directions

1. Skin the stalks and cut in lengths of half an inch.
2. Strew lavishly with sugar and fill the crusts with the raw fruit.
3. Some scatter seedless raisins among the rhubarb.
4. Cover and bake nearly three-quarters of an hour.
5. Brush with egg while hot, and return to the oven to glaze.
6. Eat cold, as you do all fruit-pies.
