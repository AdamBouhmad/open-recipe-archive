## Ingredients

- pounded rosin
- boiling water

## Directions

1. As soon as the animal is killed, have the head and feet taken off, wash them clean
2. Sprinkle some pounded rosin all over the hairs
3. Then dip them in boiling water
4. Take them instantly out, the rosin will dry immediately
5. And they may be scraped clean with ease
6. The feet should be soaked in water three or four days, changing it daily
7. This will make them very white
