## Ingredients

- one cupful of sour milk
- one cupful of sweet milk
- a tablespoonful of melted butter
- yolks of four eggs
- juice and rind of one lemon
- a small cupful of white pounded sugar
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Put both kinds of milk together in a vessel, which is set in another and let it become sufficiently heated to set the curd
2. strain off the milk, rub the curd through a strainer
3. add butter to the curd, the sugar, well-beaten eggs and lemon
4. Line the little pans with the richest of puff paste and fill with the mixture
5. bake until firm in the centre, from ten to fifteen minutes
