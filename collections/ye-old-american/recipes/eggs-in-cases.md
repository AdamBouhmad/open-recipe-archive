## Ingredients

- buttered writing paper
- butter
- chopped parsley
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- eggs
- seasoned bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Make little paper cases of buttered writing paper
2. Put a small piece of butter in each, and a little chopped parsley or onion, pepper and salt
3. Place the cases upon a gridiron over a moderate fire of bright coals
4. When the butter melts, break a fresh egg into each case
5. Strew in upon them a few seasoned bread crumbs
6. When nearly done, glaze the tops with a hot shovel
7. Serve in the paper cases
