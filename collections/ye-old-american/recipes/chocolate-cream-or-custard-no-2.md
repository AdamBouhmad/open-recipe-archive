## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- two ounces of grated chocolate
- the yolks of eight eggs
- two whites of eggs
- eight tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Take one quart of milk, and when nearly boiling stir in two ounces of grated chocolate
2. Let it warm on the fire for a few moments, and then remove and cool
3. Beat the yolks of eight eggs and two whites with eight tablespoonfuls of sugar
4. Pour the milk over them
5. Flavor and bake as any custard, either in cups or a large dish
6. Make a meringue of the remaining whites
