## Ingredients

- four ounces of white sugar
- one gallon of skimmed milk
- two ounces of baker's yeast or a cake of compressed yeast

## Directions

1. Dissolve four ounces of white sugar in one gallon of skimmed milk
2. Place in bottles of the capacity of one quart
3. Add two ounces of baker's yeast or a cake of compressed yeast to each bottle
4. Cork and tie securely
5. Set in a warm place until fermentation is well under way
6. Lay the bottles on their sides in a cool cellar
7. In three days, fermentation will have progressed sufficiently to permit the koumiss to be in good condition
