## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoonfuls fresh butter
- Teacupful vinegar
- Salt and pepper to taste, with a heaping teaspoonful white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful made mustard
- Minced parsley

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream, adding gradually the vinegar, salt, and pepper.
2. Boil a bunch of parsley five minutes, chop small; beat into the butter; lastly the sugar and mustard.
3. The butter must be light as whipped egg.
