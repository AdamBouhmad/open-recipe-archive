## Ingredients

- a good squash
- 6 large apples
- 5 eggs
- 2 ounces sugar and butter each
- cinnamon and rose water to your taste
- one spoon flour
- nutmeg, salt and sugar to your taste

## Directions

1. core, boil and skin a good squash
2. and bruize it well
3. take 6 large apples
4. pared, cored, and stewed tender
5. mix together
6. add 6 or 7 spoonsful of dry bread or biscuit
7. rendered fine as meal
8. half pint milk or cream
9. 2 spoons of rose-water
10. 2 do. wine
11. 5 or 6 eggs beaten and strained
12. nutmeg, salt and sugar to your taste
13. one spoon flour
14. beat all smartly together
15. bake
