## Ingredients

- sugar grains
- coloring

## Directions

1. Stir a little coloring--as the essence of spinach, or prepared cochineal, or liquid carmine, or indigo, rouge, saffron, etc.,--into the sugar grains
2. Stir until each grain is stained
3. Spread them on a baking-sheet and dry them in a warm place
