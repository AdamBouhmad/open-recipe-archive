## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 3 tablespoons wine

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs until thick, add one-half the sugar gradually; beat whites of eggs until stiff, add gradually remaining sugar; combine mixtures, and add wine.
