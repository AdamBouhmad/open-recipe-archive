## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. apples—pared and chopped
- ¾ lb. beef suet—cleared of strings and powdered
- 1 lb. currants
- ½ lb. raisins, seeded and chopped
- ½ lb. sultana raisins
- ¼ lb. citron, cut into shreds
- 1 lemon—juice and grated rind
- 1 tablespoonful cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful cloves
- 1 teaspoonful mace
- 1 tablespoonful allspice
- 2 lbs. brown sugar
- Half-pint best brandy
- A glass of wine
- 2 teaspoonfuls salt

## Directions

1. Pack down in a stone jar, with close cover
2. Keep in a cool place
