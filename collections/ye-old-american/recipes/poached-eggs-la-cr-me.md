## Ingredients

- water
- vinegar
- salt
- eggs
- butter
- toast
- cream or milk
- corn-starch
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Nearly fill a clean frying-pan with strained water boiling hot; strain a tablespoonful of vinegar through double muslin, and add to the water with a little salt.
2. Slip your eggs from the saucer upon the top of the water (first taking the pan from the fire.)
3. Boil three minutes and a half, drain, and lay on buttered toast in a hot dish.
4. Turn the water from the pan and pour in half a cupful of cream or milk.
5. If you use the latter, thicken with a very little corn-starch.
6. Let it heat to a boil, stirring to prevent burning, and add a great spoonful of butter, some pepper and salt.
7. Boil up once, and pour over the eggs.
