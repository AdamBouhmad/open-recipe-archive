## Ingredients

- One cup rice
- White Stock
- Three-fourths cup Thick White Sauce
- Beaten yolk of one egg
- Nutmeg
- One-half teaspoon salt
- One-eighth teaspoon pepper
- Buttered paper
- Crumbs
- Egg
- Creamed fish

## Directions

1. Wash rice, and steam in White Stock.
2. Cool, and mix with Thick White Sauce to which has been added beaten yolk of egg, slight grating of nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
3. Spread mixture in buttered pan two inches thick, cover with buttered paper, and place weight on top.
4. Let stand until cold.
5. Turn from pan, cut in rounds, remove centres, leaving cases.
6. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs, and fry in deep fat.
7. Fill with creamed fish.
