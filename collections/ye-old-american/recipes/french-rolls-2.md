## Ingredients

- a quart of flour
- a little salt
- a spoonful of yeast
- two eggs
- half a pint of milk
- an ounce of butter

## Directions

1. Sift a quart of flour
2. add a little salt, a spoonful of yeast, two eggs well beaten, and half a pint of milk
3. knead it
4. set it to rise
5. next morning, work in an ounce of butter
6. make the dough into small rolls
7. bake them
