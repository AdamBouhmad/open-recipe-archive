## Ingredients

- ochra
- tomatos
- butter
- onion
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take an equal quantity of each, let the ochra be young, slice it, and skin the tomatos
2. Put them into a pan without water, add a lump of butter, an onion chopped fine, some pepper and salt
3. Stew them one hour
