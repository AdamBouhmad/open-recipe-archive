## Ingredients

- 1 quart of gooseberries, ripe
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 cup of sugar
- Yolks of four eggs
- Méringue of whites, and 3 tablespoonfuls sugar

## Directions

1. Stew the gooseberries in just water enough to cover them
2. When soft and broken, rub them through a sieve to remove the skins
3. While still hot beat in the butter, sugar, and the whipped yolks of the eggs
4. Pile in a glass dish, or in small glasses, and heap upon the top a méringue of the whipped whites and sugar
