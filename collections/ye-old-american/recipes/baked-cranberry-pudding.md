## Ingredients

- one pint of bread crumbs
- boiling water
- one tablespoonful of butter
- two eggs
- one pint of stewed cranberries

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water on one pint of bread crumbs
2. Melt one tablespoonful of butter and stir in
3. When the bread is softened, add two eggs and beat thoroughly with the bread
4. Put in one pint of the stewed fruit and sweeten to your taste
5. Serve with sweet sugar sauce
