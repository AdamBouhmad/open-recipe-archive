## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- 1 cup bread-crumbs (very fine)
- 1 onion, sliced
- A pinch of mace
- Pepper and salt to taste
- 3 tablespoonfuls butter

## Directions

1. Simmer the sliced onion in the milk until tender; strain the milk and pour over the bread-crumbs, which should be put into a saucepan.
2. Cover and soak half an hour; beat smooth with an egg-whip, add the seasoning and butter; stir in well, boil up once, and serve in a tureen.
3. If it is too thick, add boiling water and more butter.
