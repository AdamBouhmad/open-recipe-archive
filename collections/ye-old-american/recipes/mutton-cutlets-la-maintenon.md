## Ingredients

- six French Chops
- one and one-half tablespoons butter
- one tablespoon onion
- one-half cup chopped mushrooms
- two tablespoons flour
- three tablespoons stock
- one teaspoon finely chopped parsley
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- cayenne
- buttered paper cases

## Directions

1. Wipe six French Chops, cut one and one-half inches thick.
2. Split meat in halves, cutting to bone.
3. Cook two and one-half tablespoons butter and one tablespoon onion five minutes; remove onion, add one-half cup chopped mushrooms, and cook five minutes.
4. Add two tablespoons flour, three tablespoons stock, one teaspoon finely chopped parsley, one-fourth teaspoon salt, and a few grains cayenne.
5. Spread mixture between layers of chops, press together lightly, wrap in buttered paper cases, and broil ten minutes.
6. Serve with Spanish Sauce.
