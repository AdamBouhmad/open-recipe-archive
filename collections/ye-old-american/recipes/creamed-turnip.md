## Ingredients

- turnips
- salt
- one cup White Sauce I

## Directions

1. Wash turnips, and cut in one-half inch cubes.
2. Cook three cups cubes in boiling salted water twenty minutes, or until soft.
3. Drain, and add one cup White Sauce I.
