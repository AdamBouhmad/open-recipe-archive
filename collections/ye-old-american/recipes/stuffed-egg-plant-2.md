## Ingredients

- egg-plant
- minced ham
- water
- grated crumbs
- butter
- onion
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the egg-plant in two; scrape out all the inside and put it in a saucepan with a little minced ham.
2. Cover with water and boil until soft; drain off the water.
3. Add two tablespoonfuls of grated crumbs, a tablespoonful of butter, half a minced onion, salt and pepper.
4. Stuff each half of the hull with the mixture; add a small lump of butter to each and bake fifteen minutes.
