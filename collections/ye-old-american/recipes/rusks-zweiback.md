## Ingredients

- ½ cup scalded milk
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 yeast cakes
- ¼ cup sugar
- ¼ cup melted butter
- 3 eggs
- Flour

## Directions

1. Dissolve yeast cakes in milk
2. When lukewarm, add salt and one cup flour
3. Cover and let rise until very light
4. Add sugar, butter, eggs unbeaten, and flour enough to handle
5. Shape as finger rolls and place close together on a buttered sheet in parallel rows, two inches apart
6. Let rise again and bake twenty minutes
7. When cold, cut diagonally in one-half inch slices
8. Brown evenly in oven
