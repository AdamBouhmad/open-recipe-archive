## Ingredients

- half pound sugar
- half pound butter
- four pound flour
- pint milk
- pint emptins

## Directions

1. rub in half pound sugar, half pound butter, to four pound flour
2. add pint milk, pint emptins
3. when risen well, bake in pans ten minutes, fast
