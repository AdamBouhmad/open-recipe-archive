## Ingredients

- Ox-tail
- Flour
- Butter
- One sliced onion
- One-fourth cup flour
- Two cups brown stock
- Two cups water
- Two cups canned tomatoes
- One teaspoon salt
- One-fourth teaspoon pepper
- Carrot
- Turnip
- Boiled salted water
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Cut ox-tail at joints
2. Parboil five minutes
3. Wash thoroughly
4. Dredge with flour
5. Sauté in butter to which has been added a sliced onion until well browned
6. Add one-fourth cup flour, two cups each brown stock, water, and canned tomatoes, one teaspoon salt, and one-fourth teaspoon pepper
7. Turn into an earthen pudding-dish
8. Cover and cook slowly three and one-half hours
9. Remove ox-tail
10. Strain sauce and return ox-tail and sauce to oven to finish cooking
11. Add two-thirds cup each carrot and turnip shaped with a vegetable cutter in pieces one-inch long parboiled in boiled salted water five minutes
12. As soon as vegetables are soft, add Sherry wine to taste and more salt and pepper if needed
