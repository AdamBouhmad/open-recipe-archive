## Ingredients

- Grated rind 1 orange
- 1 teaspoon brandy
- ½ teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon orange juice
- Yolk 1 egg
- Confectioners' sugar

## Directions

1. Add rind to brandy and fruit juices; let stand fifteen minutes.
2. Strain, and add gradually to yolk of egg slightly beaten.
3. Stir in confectioners' sugar until of right consistency to spread.
