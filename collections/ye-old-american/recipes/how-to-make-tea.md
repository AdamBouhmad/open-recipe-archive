## Ingredients

- 3 teaspoons tea
- 2 cups boiling water

## Directions

1. Scald an earthen or china teapot.
2. Put in tea, and pour on boiling water.
3. Let stand on back of range or in a warm place five minutes.
4. Strain and serve immediately, with or without sugar and milk.
5. Avoid second steeping of leaves with addition of a few fresh ones.
