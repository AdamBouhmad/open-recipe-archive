## Ingredients

- three pounds of sifted flour
- two pounds of powdered white sugar
- four nutmegs
- a quarter of an ounce of mace powdered fine
- two pounds of stoned raisins
- two pounds of currants, picked, washed, and dried
- half a pound of citron cut large

## Directions

1. Prepare, the day before, three pounds of sifted flour, two pounds of powdered white sugar, four nutmegs, and a quarter of an ounce of mace powdered fine.
2. Prepare two pounds of stoned raisins, two pounds of currants, picked, washed, and dried (or you may substitute for the currants two additional pounds of raisins), and half a pound of citron cut large.
3. Spread the raisins, currants, and citron on a large dish, and dredge thickly over with flour, which must be mixed well among them with your hands, so as to coat them all completely.
