## Ingredients

- large ripe tomatoes
- bread
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel off the skin from large, full, ripe tomatoes
2. Put a layer in the bottom of a deep dish
3. Cover it well with bread grated fine
4. Sprinkle on pepper and salt
5. Lay some bits of butter over them
6. Put another layer of each, till the dish is full
7. Let the top be covered with crumbs and butter
8. Bake it a nice brown
