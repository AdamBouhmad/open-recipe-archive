## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 2 cups stale bread-crumbs
- 1 good handful of flour
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter
- 3 eggs, well beaten
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Work the bread and milk smooth
2. Stir in the butter and eggs, then the salt, lastly just enough flour to bind the mixture
3. If too thick, add milk
4. Take care they do not stick to the griddle
