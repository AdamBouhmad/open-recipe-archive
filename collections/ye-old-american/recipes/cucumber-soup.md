## Ingredients

- 3 large cucumbers
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 3 cups White Stock III
- 1 cup milk
- 1 slice onion
- 2 blades mace
- ½ cup cream
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Peel cucumbers, slice, and remove seeds
2. Cook in butter ten minutes; then add flour and stock
3. Scald milk with onion and mace
4. Combine mixtures and rub through a sieve
5. Reheat to boiling-point and add cream and egg yolks
6. Season with salt and pepper
