## Ingredients

- one pound of the best loaf-sugar
- whites of eight eggs
- juice of two lemons or of two large oranges
- jelly (optional)

## Directions

1. Powder a pound of the best loaf-sugar
2. Beat to a strong froth the whites of eight eggs
3. When it is stiff enough to stand alone, beat into it the powdered sugar, a tea-spoonful at a time, adding the juice of two lemons or of two large oranges
4. Beat the whole very hard
5. Drop it in oval or egg-shaped heaps upon sheets of white paper, smoothing them with a broad knife dipped in cold water
6. Place them in a moderate oven and bake them till coloured of a very pale brown
7. Take them off the papers very carefully
8. Place two bottoms together so as to unite them in an oval ball
9. Lay them on their sides to cool
10. You may scoop out a little from the under-surface of each and put in some jelly, then stick the flat sides together
