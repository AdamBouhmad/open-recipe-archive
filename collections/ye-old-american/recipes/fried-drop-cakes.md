## Ingredients

- 1⅓ cups flour
- 2½ teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon melted butter

## Directions

1. Beat egg until light
2. Add milk, dry ingredients mixed and sifted, and melted butter
3. Drop by spoonfuls in hot, new, deep fat
4. Fry until light brown and cooked through, which must at first be determined by piercing with a skewer, or breaking apart
5. Remove with a skimmer, and drain on brown paper
