## Ingredients

- oysters
- butter
- pepper
- egg
- bread crumbs
- cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Dry a quart of oysters in a cloth.
2. Dip each in melted butter well peppered; then in beaten egg, or not, then in bread or cracker crumbs also peppered.
3. Broil on a wire broiler over live coals three to five minutes.
4. Dip over each a little melted butter.
5. Serve hot.
