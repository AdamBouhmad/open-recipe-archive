## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup macaroni
- 2 quarts boiling salted water
- 1/2 onion
- 2 cloves
- 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
- 1 1/2 cups Tomato Sauce II
- 6 sliced mushrooms
- 2 slices cooked smoked beef tongue
- 1/2 cup grated cheese

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water, with butter and onion stuck with cloves
2. Drain
3. Remove onion
4. Reheat in Tomato Sauce II
5. Add six sliced mushrooms
6. Add two slices cooked smoked beef tongue cut in strips
7. Add one-half cup grated cheese
