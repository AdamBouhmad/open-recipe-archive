## Ingredients

- three-fourths cup cold cooked chicken or veal
- one-half cup heavy cream
- one-half tablespoon granulated gelatine
- three tablespoons White Stock
- another one-half cup heavy cream
- salt
- cayenne
- horseradish powder
- jelly
- lemon Sauterne or aspic
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Chop three-fourths cup cold cooked chicken or veal, and pound in a mortar.
2. Add gradually one-half cup heavy cream, and force mixture through purée strainer.
3. Add one-half tablespoon granulated gelatine dissolved in three tablespoons White Stock.
4. Add another one-half cup heavy cream and season with salt, cayenne, and horseradish powder.
5. Pour jelly into small moulds one-third inch deep, using lemon Sauterne, or aspic.
6. When firm, fill moulds with veal mixture and set aside to chill.
7. Remove from moulds and serve on lettuce leaves.
