## Ingredients

- cream cake mixture
- vanilla, coffee, or chocolate cream

## Directions

1. Shape cream cake mixture four and one-half inches long by one inch wide, by forcing through a pastry bag and tube. Bake twenty-five minutes in a moderate oven. Split, and fill with vanilla, coffee, or chocolate cream.
