## Ingredients

- three dozen hard-shell crabs
- two heads of lettuce
- Mayonnaise dressing
- crab claws
- hard-boiled eggs
- cress leaves

## Directions

1. Boil three dozen hard-shell crabs twenty-five minutes
2. Drain and let them cool gradually
3. Remove the upper shell and the tail, break the remainder apart and pick out the meat carefully
4. Remove the meat from the large claws and the creamy fat attached to the upper shell
5. Line a salad bowl with the small white leaves of two heads of lettuce
6. Add the crab meat
7. Pour over it a Mayonnaise dressing
8. Garnish with crab claws, hard-boiled eggs and little mounds of cress leaves, which may be mixed with the salad when served
