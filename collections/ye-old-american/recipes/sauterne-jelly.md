## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons granulated gelatine
- ½ cup cold water
- 1½ cups boiling water
- 1½ cups Sauterne
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 cup sugar
- Leaf green

## Directions

1. Soak two tablespoons granulated gelatine in one-half cup cold water, and dissolve in one and one-half cups boiling water.
2. Add one and one-half cups Sauterne, three tablespoons lemon juice, and one cup sugar.
3. Color with leaf green, strain into a shallow pan, chill, and cut in inch cubes.
