## Ingredients

- green peas
- water
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Into a saucepan of boiling water put two or three pints of young green peas and when nearly done and tender drain in a colander dry.
2. Melt two ounces of butter in two of flour; stir well and boil five minutes longer.
3. Should the pods be quite clean and fresh boil them first in the water, remove and put in the peas.
