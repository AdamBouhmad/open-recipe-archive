## Ingredients

- rabbits
- onion sauce

## Directions

1. Case the rabbits, skewer them with their heads straight up, the fore-legs brought down, and the hind-legs straight
2. Boil them three quarters of an hour at least
3. Smother them with onion sauce, made the same as for boiled ducks, and serve them up
