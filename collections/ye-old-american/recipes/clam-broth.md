## Ingredients

- twelve small, hard-shell clams
- half a pint of clam juice or hot water
- a pinch of cayenne
- a walnut of butter
- a gill of boiled milk

## Directions

1. Select twelve small, hard-shell clams, drain them and chop them fine
2. Add half a pint of clam juice or hot water, a pinch of cayenne, and a walnut of butter
3. Simmer thirty minutes
4. Add a gill of boiled milk
5. Strain, and serve
