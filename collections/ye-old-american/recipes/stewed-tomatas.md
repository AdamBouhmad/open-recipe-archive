## Ingredients

- Tomatas
- Cayenne
- Salt
- Grated bread
- Minced onion
- Powdered mace

## Directions

1. Peel your tomatas, cut them in half and squeeze out the seeds
2. Put them into a stew-pan without any water
3. Add cayenne and salt to your taste, some grated bread, a little minced onion, and some powdered mace
4. Stew them slowly till they are first dissolved and then dry
