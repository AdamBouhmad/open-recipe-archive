## Ingredients

- Stale bread
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut stale bread in one-third inch slices, remove crusts, spread thinly with butter.
2. Cut slices in one-third inch strips and rings.
3. Put in pan and bake until delicately browned.
4. Arrange three sticks in each ring.
