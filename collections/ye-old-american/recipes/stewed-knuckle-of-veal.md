## Ingredients

- veal knuckle
- two quarts of boiling water
- half a pound of salt pork or ham
- a carrot
- two onions
- a bunch of parsley
- a bunch of summer savory
- two dozen whole pepper-corns
- rice-flour

## Directions

1. Put the meat into a pot with two quarts of boiling water half a pound of salt pork or ham cut into strips a carrot two onions a bunch of parsley and one of summer savory all cut fine two dozen whole pepper-corns
2. Stew closely covered for three hours
3. When done take the meat from the pot and lay in the dish
4. Strain the gravy thicken with rice-flour boil up once and pour over the meat
