## Ingredients

- carrots
- water
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Wash, scrape, and lay in cold water a while.
2. Boil very tender in hot water, slightly salted.
3. Drain, and mash with a beetle or wooden spoon, working in a large spoonful of butter, with pepper and salt.
4. A little cream will improve them.
5. Mound as you would mashed potatoes, and stamp a figure upon them, or mark in squares with a knife.
