## Ingredients

- 2 large heads of celery
- 1 teacupful of broth in which the fowl is boiled
- 1 teacupful cream or milk
- Salt and nutmeg
- Heaping tablespoonful flour, and same of butter

## Directions

1. Boil the celery tender in salted water; drain, and cut into bits half an inch long.
2. Thicken the gravy from the fowl—a teacupful—with the flour; add the butter, salt, and nutmeg, then the milk.
3. Stir and beat until it is smooth; put in the celery; heat almost to boiling, stirring all the while; serve in a tureen, or, if you prefer, pour it over the boiled meat or fowls.
