## Ingredients

- shad
- good forcemeat
- bread crumbs
- salt and pepper
- thread
- butter and parsley

## Directions

1. Fill the cavity with good forcemeat
2. sew it up and tie it on a board of proper size
3. cover it with bread crumbs with some salt and pepper
4. set it before the fire to roast
5. when done on one side, turn it and tie it again
6. when sufficiently done, pull out the thread
7. serve it up with butter and parsley poured over it
