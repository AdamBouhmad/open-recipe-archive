## Ingredients

- 2 cups hot mashed potato
- 1¼ cups soft stale bread crumbs
- ¼ cup finely chopped fat salt pork
- 1 finely chopped onion
- ⅓ cup butter
- 1 egg
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon sage

## Directions

1. Add to potato, bread crumbs, butter, egg, salt, and sage; then add pork and onion.
