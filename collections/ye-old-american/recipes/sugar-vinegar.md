## Ingredients

- 1 measure sugar
- 7 measures water moderately warm
- yeast

## Directions

1. To one measure of sugar, put seven measures of water moderately warm
2. Dissolve it completely
3. Put it into a cask
4. Stir in yeast in the proportion of a pint to eight gallons
5. Stop it close, and keep it in a warm place till sufficiently sour
