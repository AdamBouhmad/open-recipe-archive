## Ingredients

- one cup cranberries cut in halves
- one-half cup raisins seeded and cut in pieces
- three-fourths cup sugar
- one tablespoon flour
- one teaspoon butter

## Directions

1. Mix one cup cranberries cut in halves, one-half cup raisins seeded and cut in pieces, three-fourths cup sugar, and one tablespoon flour.
2. Dot over with one teaspoon butter.
3. Bake between crusts.
