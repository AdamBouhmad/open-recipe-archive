## Ingredients

- one quart of sifted flour
- three large teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- one tablespoonful of cold butter
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one teaspoonful of sugar
- one well-beaten egg
- one pint of cold milk
- soft butter
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Stir into a quart of sifted flour three large teaspoonfuls of baking powder, a tablespoonful of cold butter, a teaspoonful of salt and one of sugar, and a well-beaten egg
2. Rub all well into the flour
3. Pour in a pint of cold milk and mix up quickly into a smooth dough
4. Roll it out less than half an inch thick
5. Cut with a large biscuit-cutter
6. Spread soft butter over the top of each
7. Fold one-half over the other by doubling it
8. Lay them a little apart on greased tins
9. Set them immediately in a pretty hot oven
10. Rub over the tops with sweet milk before putting in the oven to give them a glaze
