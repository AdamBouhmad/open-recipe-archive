## Ingredients

- three hard-boiled eggs
- three common crackers
- three tablespoons melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- cream
- chops
- buttered paper cases
- parsley

## Directions

1. Finely chop the whites of three hard-boiled eggs and force yolks through potato ricer, mix, and add to three common crackers, rolled and sifted.
2. Add three tablespoons melted butter, salt, pepper, and onion juice, to taste.
3. Add enough cream to make of right consistency to spread.
4. Cover chops thinly with mixture and wrap in buttered paper cases.
5. Bake twenty-five minutes in hot oven.
6. Remove from cases, place on hot platter, and garnish with parsley.
