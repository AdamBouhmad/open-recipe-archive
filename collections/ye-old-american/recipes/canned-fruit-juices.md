## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar
- white granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Select clean ripe fruit
2. Press out the juice and strain it through a flannel cloth
3. To each pint of juice add one cupful of white granulated sugar
4. Put it in a porcelain kettle
5. Bring it to the boiling point
6. Bottle while hot in small bottles
7. Seal very tight while it is hot
