## Ingredients

- cabbage
- bacon or ham
- hard-boiled egg
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil cabbage in two waters, the second being the pot liquor from the boiling meat
2. Drain thoroughly in a cullender, pressing out every drop of water that will flow, without breaking the tender leaves
3. When the meat is dished, lay the cabbage neatly about it
4. Lay upon each quarter a slice of hard-boiled egg
5. When you eat, season with pepper, salt, and vinegar
