## Ingredients

- two cups of small homony
- an equal quantity of corn meal
- a little salt
- a large spoonful of butter
- three eggs
- a sufficient quantity of milk

## Directions

1. Boil two cups of small homony very soft
2. Add an equal quantity of corn meal with a little salt, and a large spoonful of butter
3. Make it in a thin batter with three eggs, and a sufficient quantity of milk
4. Beat all together some time, and bake them on a griddle, or in waffle irons
