## Ingredients

- 1 quart boiling milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls (heaping) of ground rice
- cold milk for wetting the rice
- 1 saltspoonful of salt
- sugar and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wet the ground rice with cold milk to make a paste
2. Stir in the rice-paste into boiling milk and boil ten minutes, stirring all the while
3. Season with sugar and nutmeg
4. Eat warm with cream
