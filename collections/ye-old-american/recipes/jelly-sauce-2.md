## Ingredients

- two tablespoonfuls of sugar
- half a cupful of jelly
- a cupful of boiling water
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- a teaspoonful of cornstarch
- half a cupful of water or wine

## Directions

1. Melt the sugar and jelly over the fire in a cupful of boiling water, adding also two tablespoonfuls of butter
2. Stir into it a teaspoonful of cornstarch, dissolved in half a cupful of water or wine
3. Add it to the jelly and let it come to a boil
4. Set it in a dish of hot water to keep it warm until time to serve
5. Stir occasionally
