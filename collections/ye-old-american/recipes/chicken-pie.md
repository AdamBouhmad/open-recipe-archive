## Ingredients

- chicken
- baking powder biscuit dough
- soda biscuit dough
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- cold boiled eggs
- new potatoes
- gravy
- beaten white of egg
- celery leaves

## Directions

1. Prepare the chicken as for fricassee
2. When the chicken is stewed tender, seasoned, and the gravy thickened, take it from the fire
3. take out the largest bones
4. scrape the meat from the neck and backbone and throw the bones away
5. line the sides of a four or six quart pudding-dish with a rich baking powder or soda biscuit dough, a quarter of an inch thick
6. put in part of the chicken
7. add a few lumps of butter, pepper and salt, if needed
8. add some cold boiled eggs cut in slices
9. Add the rest of the chicken and season as before
10. a few new potatoes in their season might be added
11. Pour over the gravy, being sure to have enough to fill the dish
12. cover with a crust a quarter of an inch thick, made with a hole in the centre the size of a teacup
13. Brush over the top with beaten white of egg
14. bake for half to three-quarters of an hour
15. Garnish the top with small bright celery leaves, neatly arranged in a circle
