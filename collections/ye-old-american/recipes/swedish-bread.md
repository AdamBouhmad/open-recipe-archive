## Ingredients

- 2½ cups scalded milk
- 1 yeast cake
- flour
- ½ cup melted butter
- ⅔ cup sugar
- 1 egg, well beaten
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon almond extract

## Directions

1. Add yeast cake to one-half cup milk which has been allowed to cool until lukewarm
2. As soon as dissolved add one-half cup flour, beat thoroughly, cover, and let rise
3. When light, add remaining milk and four and one-half cups flour
4. Stir until thoroughly mixed, cover, and again let rise
5. Then add remaining ingredients and one and one-half cups flour
6. Toss on a floured cloth and knead, using one-half cup flour, cover, and again let rise
7. Shape as Swedish Tea Braid or Tea Ring I or II, and bake
