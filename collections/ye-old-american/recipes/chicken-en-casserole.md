## Ingredients

- two small, young chickens
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter
- one-third cup carrots cut in strips
- one tablespoon finely chopped onion
- four thin slices bacon cut in narrow strips
- one and one-third cups Brown Sauce
- two-thirds cup potato balls
- three tablespoons Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Cut two small, young chickens in pieces for serving.
2. Season with salt and pepper, brush over with melted butter, and bake in a casserole dish twelve minutes.
3. Parboil one-third cup carrots cut in strips five minutes, drain, and fry with one tablespoon finely chopped onion and four thin slices bacon cut in narrow strips.
4. Add one and one-third cups Brown Sauce and two-thirds cup potato balls.
5. Add to chicken, with three tablespoons Sherry wine, salt and pepper to taste.
6. Cook in a moderate oven twenty minutes, or until chicken is tender.
