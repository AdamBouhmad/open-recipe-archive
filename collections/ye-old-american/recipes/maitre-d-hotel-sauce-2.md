## Ingredients

- a teacupful of drawn butter
- the juice of a lemon
- two tablespoonfuls of minced onion
- three tablespoonfuls of chopped parsley
- a teaspoonful of powdered thyme or summer savory
- a pinch of cayenne
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a teacupful of drawn butter
2. Add to it the juice of a lemon, two tablespoonfuls of minced onion, three tablespoonfuls of chopped parsley, a teaspoonful of powdered thyme or summer savory, a pinch of cayenne and salt
3. Simmer over the fire and stir well
