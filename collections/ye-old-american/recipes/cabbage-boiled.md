## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- drawn butter or butter

## Directions

1. Clean the cabbage carefully
2. Add considerable salt to the water when boiling
3. Boil a large drumhead cabbage for an hour or a green savory cabbage for twenty minutes
4. Do not let a cabbage boil too long
5. Remove it from the water into a colander to drain
6. Serve with drawn butter, or butter poured over it
