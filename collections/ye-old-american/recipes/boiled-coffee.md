## Ingredients

- 1 cup coffee
- 1 egg
- 1 cup cold water
- 6 cups boiling water

## Directions

1. Scald granite-ware coffee-pot.
2. Wash egg, break, and beat slightly.
3. Dilute with one-half the cold water, add crushed shell, and mix with coffee.
4. Turn into coffee-pot, pour on boiling water, and stir thoroughly.
5. Place on front of range, and boil three minutes.
6. Cover spout of pot or stuff with soft paper to prevent escape of fragrant aroma.
7. Stir and pour some in a cup to be sure that spout is free from grounds.
8. Return to coffee-pot and repeat.
9. Add remaining cold water, which perfects clearing.
10. Place on back of range for ten minutes, where coffee will not boil.
11. Serve at once.
12. If any is left over, drain from grounds, and reserve for making of jelly or other dessert.
