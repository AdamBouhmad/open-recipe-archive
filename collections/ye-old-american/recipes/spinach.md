## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- poached eggs

## Directions

1. wash and pick it clean
2. drain it
3. throw it into boiling water
4. boil it a few minutes
5. press out all the water
6. put it in a stew pan with a piece of butter, some pepper and salt
7. chop it continually with a spoon till it is quite dry
8. serve it with poached eggs or without, as you please
