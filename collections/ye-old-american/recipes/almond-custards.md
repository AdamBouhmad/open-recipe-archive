## Ingredients

- 1 pint milk
- ½ pint cream
- ¼ lb. almonds, blanched and pounded to a paste
- 2 tablespoonfuls of rose-water
- Yolks of three eggs and whites of four
- 4 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 1 teaspoonful extract bitter almond in méringue

## Directions

1. Scald the milk
2. Add the beaten yolks, the sugar, the almond paste, and the whites of two eggs
3. Boil, stirring constantly until it thickens
4. Stir up well when almost cold and pour into cups
5. Make a méringue of the whites of two eggs and two tablespoonfuls powdered sugar, flavored with bitter almond, and heap upon each cup
