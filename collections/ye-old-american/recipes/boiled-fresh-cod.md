## Ingredients

- piece of fish
- thin cloth
- salted water
- warm oyster sauce

## Directions

1. Sew up the piece of fish in thin cloth, fitted to shape
2. Boil in salted water (boiling from the first), allowing about fifteen minutes to the pound
3. Carefully unwrap and pour over it warm oyster sauce
