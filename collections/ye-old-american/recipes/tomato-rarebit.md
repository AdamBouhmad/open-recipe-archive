## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ¾ cup thin cream
- ¾ cup stewed and strained tomatoes
- ⅛ teaspoon soda
- 2 cups finely cut cheese
- 2 eggs, slightly beaten
- Salt
- Mustard
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Put butter in chafing-dish; when melted, add flour.
2. Pour on, gradually, cream, and as soon as mixture thickens add tomatoes mixed with soda.
3. Then add cheese, eggs, and seasonings to taste.
4. Serve, as soon as cheese has melted, on Graham Toast.
