## Ingredients

- one cup sugar
- one cup molasses
- one cup butter
- one egg
- one tablespoonful vinegar
- one tablespoonful ginger
- one teaspoonful soda
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in boiling water
2. Mix all ingredients together like cooky dough, rather soft
