## Ingredients

- 1 cup potatoes
- 1 cup carrots
- ½ cup turnip
- ½ cup canned peas
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 slices onion
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon curry powder
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- Few grains celery salt
- 1 cup scalded milk
- Finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook one cup each potatoes and carrots, and one-half cup turnip, cut in fancy shapes, in boiling salted water until soft.
2. Drain, add one-half cup canned peas.
3. Make a sauce by cooking two tablespoons butter with two slices onion five minutes, removing onion, adding two tablespoons flour, three-fourths teaspoon salt, one-half teaspoon curry powder, one-fourth teaspoon pepper, few grains celery salt, and pouring on gradually one cup scalded milk.
4. Pour sauce over vegetables.
5. Sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
