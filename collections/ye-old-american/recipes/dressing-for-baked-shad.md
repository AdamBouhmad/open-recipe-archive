## Ingredients

- gravy from baked shad
- a large tablespoonful of catsup
- a tablespoonful of brown flour
- cold water
- the juice of a lemon
- a glass of sherry or Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Boil up the gravy in which the shad was baked
2. Put in a large tablespoonful of catsup, a tablespoonful of brown flour which has been wet with cold water, the juice of a lemon, and a glass of sherry or Madeira wine
3. Serve in a sauce boat
