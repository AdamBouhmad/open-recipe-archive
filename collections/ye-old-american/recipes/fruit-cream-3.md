## Ingredients

- four bananas
- pulp and juice of two oranges
- one tablespoon lemon juice
- one tablespoon Sherry wine
- two-thirds cup powdered sugar
- one and one-fourth tablespoons granulated gelatine
- one-fourth cup boiling water
- two cups cream

## Directions

1. Peel four bananas, mash, and rub through a sieve
2. Add pulp and juice of two oranges, one tablespoon lemon juice, one tablespoon Sherry wine, two-thirds cup powdered sugar, and one and one-fourth tablespoons granulated gelatine dissolved in one-fourth cup boiling water
3. Cool in ice-water, stirring constantly
4. Fold in whip from two cups cream
