## Ingredients

- wild ducks
- a small peeled carrot
- an onion
- salt
- pepper
- a glass of claret
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Before roasting them, parboil them with a small peeled carrot put within each duck to absorb the unpleasant fishy taste
2. An onion will have the same effect, but unless you use onions in the stuffing the carrot is preferable
3. Roast the same as tame duck
4. Or put into the duck a whole onion peeled, plenty of salt and pepper and a glass of claret
5. Bake in a hot oven twenty minutes
6. Serve hot with the gravy it yields in cooking and a dish of currant jelly
