## Ingredients

- small potatoes
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Prepare same as for Boiled Potatoes, using small potatoes, and trim egg-shaped
2. parboil ten minutes
3. drain, and place in baking-pan and bake until soft
4. basting three times with melted butter
