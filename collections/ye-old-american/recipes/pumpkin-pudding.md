## Ingredients

- one fine sweet pumpkin
- six eggs
- one quarter of a pound of butter
- one half pint of new milk
- pounded ginger
- nutmeg
- one wine glass of brandy
- sugar
- paste

## Directions

1. Stew a fine sweet pumpkin till soft and dry
2. rub it through a sieve
3. mix with the pulp six eggs quite light
4. add a quarter of a pound of butter, half a pint of new milk, some pounded ginger and nutmeg, a wine glass of brandy, and sugar to your taste
5. should it be too liquid, stew it a little drier
6. put a paste round the edges, and in the bottom of a shallow dish or plate
7. pour in the mixture
8. cut some thin bits of paste, twist them, and lay them across the top
9. bake it nicely
