## Ingredients

- young chickens
- beaten egg
- grated bread crumbs or rolled cracker
- pepper
- salt
- minced parsley
- butter
- half cupful hot water
- one cup cream or rich milk
- one cupful bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare young chickens as for fricassee by cutting them into pieces.
2. Dip each piece in beaten egg, then in grated bread crumbs or rolled cracker; season them with pepper and salt and a little minced parsley.
3. Place them in a baking pan and put on the top of each piece a lump of butter, add half of a cupful of hot water.
4. Bake slowly, basting often.
5. When sufficiently cooked take up on a warm platter.
6. Into the pan pour a cup of cream or rich milk, a cupful of bread crumbs.
7. Stir it well until cooked, then pour it over the chicken.
8. Serve while hot.
