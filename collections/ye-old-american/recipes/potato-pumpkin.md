## Ingredients

- pumpkin of a good colour, seven or eight inches in diameter
- forcemeat

## Directions

1. cut a piece off the top
2. take out all the seeds
3. wash and wipe the cavity
4. pare the rind off
5. fill the hollow with good forcemeat
6. put the top on
7. set it in a deep pan, to protect the sides
8. bake it in a moderate oven
9. put it carefully in the dish without breaking
