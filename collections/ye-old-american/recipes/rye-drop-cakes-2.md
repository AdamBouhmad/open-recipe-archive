## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup rye meal
- ⅔ cup flour
- 2½ teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- ½ cup milk
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients
2. Add milk gradually, molasses, and egg well beaten
3. Cook same as Fried Drop Cakes
