## Ingredients

- four chickens
- cider vinegar
- water
- spices

## Directions

1. Boil four chickens till tender enough for meat to fall from bones
2. put meat in a stone jar
3. pour over it three pints of cold, good cider vinegar and a pint and a half of the water in which the chickens were boiled
4. add spices if preferred
5. it will be ready for use in two days
