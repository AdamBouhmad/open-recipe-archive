## Ingredients

- halibut steaks
- eggs, two or three
- Boston or other brittle crackers
- salt
- hot fat, lard, or dripping
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe the steaks dry.
2. Beat up two or three eggs, and roll out some Boston or other brittle crackers upon the kneading-board until they are fine as dust.
3. Dip each steak into the beaten egg, then into the bread crumbs (when you have salted the fish), and fry in hot fat, lard, or nice dripping.
4. Alternatively, broil the steak upon a buttered gridiron, over a clear fire, first seasoning with salt and pepper.
5. When done, lay in a hot dish, butter well, and cover closely.
