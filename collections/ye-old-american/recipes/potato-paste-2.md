## Ingredients

- a pint and a half of flour
- fourteen large potatoes
- cold water

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes till they are thoroughly done throughout.
2. Peel, and mash them very fine.
3. Rub them through a cullender.
4. Sift the flour into a pan, and add the potatoes gradually; rubbing them well into the flour with your hands.
5. Mix in sufficient cold water to make a stiff dough.
6. Roll it out evenly.
