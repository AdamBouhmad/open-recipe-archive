## Ingredients

- barley
- water
- stoned raisins
- lemon-peel
- sugar
- liquorice root

## Directions

1. Wash clean some barley, either pearl or common
2. To two ounces of barley allow a quart of water
3. Put it into a sauce-pan, adding if you choose an equal quantity of stoned raisins; or some lemon-peel and sugar; or some liquorice root cut up
4. Let it boil slowly till the liquid is reduced one half
5. Strain it off and sweeten it
