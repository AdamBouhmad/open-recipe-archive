## Ingredients

- shad
- salt and pepper
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Separate one side from the back-bone so that it will lie open without being split in two
2. wash it clean
3. dry it with a cloth
4. sprinkle some salt and pepper on it
5. let it stand till you are ready to broil it
6. have the gridiron hot and well greased
7. broil it nicely
8. pour over it melted butter
