## Ingredients

- two ounces of gentian root
- an ounce of Virginia snake root
- an ounce of the yellow paring of orange peel
- half a drachm of cochineal
- a quart of Madeira or sherry wine, or brandy

## Directions

1. Steep these ingredients for a week or more in a quart of Madeira or sherry wine, or brandy
2. When they are thoroughly infused, strain and filter the liquor, and bottle it for use
