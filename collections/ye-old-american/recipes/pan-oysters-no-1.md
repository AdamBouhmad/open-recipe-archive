## Ingredients

- bread
- oysters
- oyster liquor
- pepper
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut some stale bread into thin slices, taking off all the crust, round the slices to fit patty-pans.
2. Toast, butter, place them in the pans and moisten with three or four teaspoonfuls of oyster liquor.
3. Place on the toast a layer of oysters, sprinkle with pepper, and put a small piece of butter on top of each pan.
4. Place all the pans in a baking-pan, and place in the oven, covering tightly.
5. They will cook in seven or eight minutes if the oven is hot; or, cook till the beards are ruffled.
6. Remove the cover, sprinkle lightly with salt, replace, and cook one minute longer.
7. Serve in patty pans.
