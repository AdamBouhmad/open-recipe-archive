## Ingredients

- four lemons
- one quart fresh cream
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pare the yellow rind very thin from four lemons
2. Put them in a quart of fresh cream, and boil it
3. Squeeze and strain the juice of one lemon, saturate it completely with powdered sugar
4. When the cream is quite cold, stir it in
5. Take care that it does not curdle
6. If not sufficiently sweet, add more sugar
