## Ingredients

- half a pound of nice brown sugar
- a quart of flour
- four ounces of butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of nice brown sugar into a quart of flour, sift it
2. Make it into a paste, with four ounces of butter melted in as much milk as will wet it
3. Knead it till light, roll it tolerably thin
4. Cut it in strips an inch wide, and just long enough to lay in a plate
5. Bake them on a griddle, put them in the plate in rows to checker each other
6. Serve them to eat with chocolate
