## Ingredients

- full-grown spring chicken
- salt
- pepper
- oysters
- butter
- cream
- rich milk
- hard-boiled eggs
- herbs
- flour

## Directions

1. Dress a full-grown spring chicken the same as for roasting
2. season it with salt and pepper inside and out
3. fill the body with oysters
4. place it in a tin pail with a close-fitting cover
5. Set the pail in a pot of fast-boiling water
6. cook until the chicken is tender
7. Dish up the chicken on a warm dish
8. pour the gravy into a saucepan
9. put into it a tablespoonful of butter
10. add half a cupful of cream or rich milk
11. add three hard-boiled eggs chopped fine
12. add some minced herbs and a tablespoonful of flour
13. let all boil up
14. pour it over the chicken
15. Serve hot
