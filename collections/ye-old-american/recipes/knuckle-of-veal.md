## Ingredients

- pearl barley
- butter
- milk
- parsley

## Directions

1. Boil a half pint of pearl barley in salt and water till quite tender
2. Drain the water from it and stir in a piece of butter
3. Put it in a deep dish
4. Have the knuckle nicely boiled in milk and water
5. And lay it on the barley
6. Pour some parsley and butter over it
