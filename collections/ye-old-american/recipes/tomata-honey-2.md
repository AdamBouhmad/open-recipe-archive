## Ingredients

- tomatas
- grated peel of a lemon
- fresh peach-leaves
- loaf-sugar
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. To each pound of tomatas, allow the grated peel of a lemon and six fresh peach-leaves.
2. Boil them slowly till they are all to pieces.
3. Squeeze and strain them through a bag.
4. To each pint of liquid allow a pound of loaf-sugar, and the juice of one lemon.
5. Boil them together half an hour, or till they become a thick jelly.
6. Put it into glasses, and lay double tissue paper closely over the top.
