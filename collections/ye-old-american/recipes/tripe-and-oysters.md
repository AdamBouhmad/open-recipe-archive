## Ingredients

- tripe
- milk
- water
- mace
- oysters
- butter
- flour
- salt
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the tripe in milk and water for four or five hours till it is quite tender.
2. Cut it up into small pieces.
3. Put it into a stew-pan with just milk enough to cover it, and a few blades of mace.
4. Let it stew about five minutes, and then put in the oysters.
5. Add a large piece of butter rolled in flour, and salt and cayenne pepper to your taste.
6. Let it stew five minutes longer, and then send it to table in a tureen.
7. First skim off whatever fat may float on the surface.
