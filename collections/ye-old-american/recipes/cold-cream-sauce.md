## Ingredients

- one cupful of sugar
- half a cupful of butter
- a cupful of sweet, thick cold cream

## Directions

1. Stir to a cream one cupful of sugar, half a cupful of butter
2. Add a cupful of sweet, thick cold cream
3. Flavor to taste
4. Stir well and set it in a cool place
