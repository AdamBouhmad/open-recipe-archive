## Ingredients

- ⅓ box gelatine or 1⅓ tablespoons granulated gelatine
- ⅓ cup cold water
- ⅓ cup boiling water
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 cup orange juice and pulp
- Whites 3 eggs
- Whip from 2 cups cream

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cold water, dissolve in boiling water, strain, and add sugar, lemon juice, orange juice, and pulp
2. Chill in pan of ice-water
3. When quite thick, beat with wire spoon or whisk until frothy
4. Add whites of eggs beaten stiff, and fold in cream
5. Line a mould with sections of oranges, turn in mixture, smooth evenly, and chill
