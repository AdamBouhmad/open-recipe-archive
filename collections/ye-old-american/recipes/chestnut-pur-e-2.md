## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- salt
- water
- scalded milk
- pepper

## Directions

1. Remove shells from chestnuts.
2. Cook until soft in boiling salted water.
3. Drain.
4. Mash.
5. Moisten with scalded milk.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Beat until light.
