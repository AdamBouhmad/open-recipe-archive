## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- eight eggs
- sugar and cinnamon to taste
- vanilla
- wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Separate the eggs and beat the yolks until thick
2. Add the milk, a little vanilla, and sweeten to taste
3. Put it into a pan or farina kettle and place it over a slow fire
4. Stir it all the time until it becomes custard
5. Pour it into a pudding-dish to get cold
6. Whisk the whites until stiff and dry
7. Have ready a pan of boiling water on the top of which place the whites
8. Cover and place them where the water will keep sufficiently hot to cause a steam to pass through and cook them
9. Place in a dish a layer of custard and white alternately
10. On each layer of custard grate a little nutmeg with a teaspoonful of wine
11. Reserve a layer of white for the cover, over which grate nutmeg
12. Send to table and eat cold
