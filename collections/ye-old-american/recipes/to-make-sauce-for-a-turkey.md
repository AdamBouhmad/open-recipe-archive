## Ingredients

- oysters
- white gravy
- lemon pickle
- flour
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. As you open the oysters, put a pint into a bowl
2. Wash them out of their own liquor, and put them in another bowl
3. When the liquor has settled, pour it off into a sauce pan with a little white gravy, and a tea-spoonful of lemon pickle
4. Thicken it with flour and a good lump of butter
5. Boil it three or four minutes
6. Put in a spoonful of good cream
7. Add the oysters
8. Keep shaking them over the fire till they are quite hot, but don't let them boil
