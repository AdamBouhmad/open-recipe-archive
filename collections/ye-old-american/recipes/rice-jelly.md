## Ingredients

- one quarter of a pound of rice
- half a pound of loaf-sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Pick and wash a quarter of a pound of rice
2. Mix it with half a pound of loaf-sugar and just sufficient water to cover it
3. Boil it till it becomes a glutinous mass
4. Strain it and season it with whatever may be thought proper
5. Let it stand to cool
