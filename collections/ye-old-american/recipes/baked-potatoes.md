## Ingredients

- large ripe potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe some large ripe potatoes.
2. Bake in a quick oven until tender, say from three-quarters of an hour to an hour, if of a good size.
3. Serve in a napkin with the skins on.
4. Tear or cut a hole in the top when you eat them, put in a bit of butter with salt and pepper.
