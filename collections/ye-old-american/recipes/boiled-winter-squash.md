## Ingredients

- winter squash
- water
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Pare it, take out the seeds, cut it in pieces, and stew it slowly till quite soft in a very little water.
2. Afterwards drain, squeeze and press it well; then mash it with a very little butter, pepper and salt.
3. They will boil in from twenty to forty minutes.
