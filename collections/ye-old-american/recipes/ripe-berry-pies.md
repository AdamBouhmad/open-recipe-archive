## Ingredients

- Berries
- crust
- flour
- sugar

## Directions

1. Line your pie-tin with crust
2. Fill half full of berries
3. Shake over a tablespoonful of sifted flour if very juicy
4. Add as much sugar as is necessary to sweeten sufficiently
5. Fill up the crust to the top, making quite full
6. Cover with crust
7. Bake about forty minutes
