## Ingredients

- a pint and a half of milk
- large half cup of sugar
- one moderately heaped teaspoonful of cornstarch
- two tablespoonfuls of grated chocolate
- one egg
- a small half cup of milk
- a pinch of salt
- stale cake
- whites of two or more eggs
- white sugar
- vanilla or lemon extract
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. To make the cream, take a pint and a half of milk and set it on the stove to boil
2. Mix together in a bowl the following named articles: large half cup of sugar, one moderately heaped teaspoonful of cornstarch, two tablespoonfuls of grated chocolate, one egg, a small half cup of milk and a pinch of salt
3. Pour into the boiling milk and remove to top of the stove and let simmer a minute or two
4. Put the cream into a large glass fruit dish, which is partly filled with stale cake (of any kind) cut up into small pieces about an inch square
5. Stir it a little
6. When the cream is cold pour over the cake just before setting it on the table
7. Beat the whites of two or more eggs stiff and sweeten with white sugar
8. Spread over the top
9. Set in a refrigerator to become cold
10. Serve in saucers
11. The cream should be flavored either with vanilla or lemon extract or nutmeg
