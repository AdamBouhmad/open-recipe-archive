## Ingredients

- oysters in shell
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Oysters for roasting should be bought in the shell.
2. Wash thoroughly, scrubbing with a brush.
3. Put in a dripping-pan, and cook in a hot oven until shells part.
4. Open, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and serve in the deep halves of the shells.
