## Ingredients

- two yelks of eggs
- grated bread
- pepper
- salt
- pounded nutmeg
- chopped parsley
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut off the flank and take the bone out, then take slices the size of the fillet and half an inch thick
2. Beat two yelks of eggs light
3. Have some grated bread mixed with pepper, salt, pounded nutmeg and chopped parsley
4. Beat the slices a little, lay them on a board and wash the upper side with the egg
5. Cover it thick with the bread crumbs, press them on with a knife
6. Let them stand to dry a little, that they may not fall off in frying
7. Then turn them gently, put egg and crumbs on in the same manner
8. Put them into a pan of boiling lard, and fry them a light brown
9. Have some good gravy ready, season it with a tea-spoonful of curry powder, a large one of wine, and one of lemon pickle
10. Thicken with butter and brown flour
11. Drain every drop of lard from the cutlets
12. Lay them in the gravy, and stew them fifteen or twenty minutes
13. Serve them up garnished with lemon cut in thin slices
