## Ingredients

- warm impressed corned beef
- cabbage
- beets
- turnips
- carrots
- potatoes

## Directions

1. After removing meat from water, skim off fat and cook vegetables (with exception of beets, which require a long time for cooking) in this water.
2. Carrots and turnips, if small, may be cooked whole; if large, cut in pieces.
3. Serve cabbage and beets in separate dishes, other vegetables on same dish with meat.
