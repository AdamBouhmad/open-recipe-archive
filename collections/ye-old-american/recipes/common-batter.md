## Ingredients

- 1 cupful milk
- pinch of salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter
- 1 cupful flour
- 1 small teaspoonful baking powder

## Directions

1. Sift flour, powder and salt together
2. Add melted butter, well beaten eggs and milk
3. Mix into very smooth batter, slightly thicker than griddle-cake batter
