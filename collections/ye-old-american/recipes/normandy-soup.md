## Ingredients

- four pounds of knuckle of veal
- twenty common-sized onions
- four quarts of water
- one third of a six-penny loaf
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- cream
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Put the knuckle of veal into a soup pot with twenty common-sized onions and about four quarts of water
2. Let it simmer slowly for two hours or more
3. Put in about one third of a six-penny loaf grated; adding a small tea-spoonful of salt, and not quite that quantity of cayenne pepper
4. Let it boil two hours longer
5. Take out the meat, and press and strain the soup through a large sieve into a broad pan
6. Measure the soup, and to every quart add a pint of cream, and about two ounces of fresh butter divided into four bits, and rolled in flour
7. Taste the soup, and if it requires additional seasoning, add a very little more salt and cayenne
8. Put the soup again over the fire, and let it just come to a boil
9. Serve it up
