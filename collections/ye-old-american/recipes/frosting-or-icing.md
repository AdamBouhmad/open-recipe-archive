## Ingredients

- whites of eggs
- powdered sugar
- lemon juice or tartaric acid (optional)
- water (if needed)

## Directions

1. Ensure eggs and platter are cold
2. Allow one small teacupful of powdered sugar for each egg white
3. Break eggs and add a small handful of sugar as soon as beating begins
4. Keep adding sugar at intervals until all is used
5. Do not beat eggs until sugar has been added in this way
6. Spread evenly over cake with a broad knife using two coats, allowing the first to dry before applying the second
7. If icing becomes too dry or stiff, thin with a little water
8. Add lemon juice or half a teaspoonful of tartaric acid while beating to make frosting white and frothy
9. For ornaments, keep about one-third of icing aside until cake icing is dried, then apply with a clean glass syringe in desired forms
10. Color reserved icing as desired with cochineal, indigo, saffron, orange rind, spinach juice, chocolate, or fruit juices
11. Set cake in a cool oven with door open to dry, or in a draught at an open window
