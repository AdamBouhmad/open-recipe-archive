## Ingredients

- calf's head
- gravy
- white wine
- nutmeg
- anchovies
- onion
- cloves
- mace
- sweetbreads
- forcemeat balls
- brains
- eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the head till the meat is almost enough for eating; then cut it in thin slices.
2. Take three quarters of a pint of good gravy, and add half a pint of white wine, half a nutmeg, two anchovies, a small onion stuck with cloves, and a little mace.
3. Boil these up in the liquor for a quarter of an hour, then strain it and boil it up again.
4. Put in the meat, with salt to your taste, let it stew a little.
5. If you choose it, you may add some sweetbreads, and make some forcemeat balls with veal.
6. Mix the brains with the yelks of eggs and fry them to lay for a garish.
7. When the head is ready to be sent in, stir in a bit of butter.
