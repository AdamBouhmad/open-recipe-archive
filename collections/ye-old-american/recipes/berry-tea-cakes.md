## Ingredients

- One cup of sugar
- Two eggs
- One and a half cups of milk
- One heaping teaspoonful of baking powder
- A piece of butter the size of an egg
- Flour sufficient to make a stiff batter
- A pint bowl of fruit or canned berries

## Directions

1. Make a stiff batter of one cup of sugar, two eggs, one and a half cups of milk, one heaping teaspoonful of baking powder, a piece of butter the size of an egg and flour
2. Stir a pint bowl of fruit or canned berries with the juice poured off into the batter
3. Bake in muffin-rings
4. Serve while warm with butter
