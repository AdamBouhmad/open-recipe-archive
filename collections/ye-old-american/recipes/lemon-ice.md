## Ingredients

- 6 lemons—juice of all, and grated peel of three
- 1 large sweet orange—juice and rind
- 1 pint of water
- 1 pint of sugar

## Directions

1. Squeeze out every drop of juice, and steep in it the rind of orange and lemons one hour
2. Strain, squeezing the bag dry
3. Mix in the sugar, and then the water
4. Stir until dissolved, and freeze by turning in a freezer—opening three times to beat all up together
