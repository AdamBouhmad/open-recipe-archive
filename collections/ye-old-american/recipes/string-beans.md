## Ingredients

- string beans
- water
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Break off the end that grew to the vine, drawing off at the same time the string upon the edge; repeat the same process from the other end.
2. Cut them with a sharp knife into pieces half an inch long, and boil them in just enough water to cover them.
3. They usually require one hour's boiling; but this depends upon their age and freshness.
4. After they have boiled until tender and the water boiled nearly out, add pepper and salt, a tablespoonful of butter and a half a cup of cream; if you have not the cream add more butter.
