## Ingredients

- walnuts
- salt and water
- vinegar
- cloves
- mace
- garlic

## Directions

1. Gather the walnuts as for pickling
2. Keep them in salt and water the same time
3. Pound them in a marble mortar
4. To every dozen walnuts put a quart of vinegar
5. Stir them well every day for a week
6. Put them in a bag and press all the liquor through
7. To each quart put a tea-spoonful of pounded cloves and one of mace with six cloves of garlic
8. Boil it fifteen or twenty minutes
9. Bottle it
