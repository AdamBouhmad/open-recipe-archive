## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 2 eggs, beaten very light
- ½ wineglass of brandy
- 1 cup powdered sugar, stirred into the eggs
- Nutmeg to taste
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla

## Directions

1. Heat the milk to boiling, and add by degrees to the beaten eggs and sugar; put in the nutmeg, and set within a saucepan of boiling water.
2. Stir until it begins to thicken.
3. Take it off and add the brandy gradually.
4. Set, until it is wanted, within a pan of boiling water.
5. Pour over the pudding when it comes from the mould.
