## Ingredients

- Rice
- Milk
- Salt
- Sugar
- Cooked pears
- Pear syrup

## Directions

1. Cook and mould rice as for Compote of Rice with Peaches.
2. Arrange on top quarters of cooked pears, and pour around pear syrup.
