## Ingredients

- button mushrooms
- salt
- water
- mace
- ginger
- cider vinegar
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Take small fresh-gathered button mushrooms
2. Peel them carefully with a penknife, and cut off the stems
3. Throw the mushrooms into salt and water as you do them
4. Put them into a porcelain skillet of fresh water
5. Cover it closely, and set it over a quick fire
6. Boil it as fast as possible for seven or eight minutes, not more
7. Take out the mushrooms, drain them
8. Spread them on a clean board, with the bottom or hollow side of each mushroom turned downwards
9. Do this as quickly as possible
10. Immediately, while they are hot, sprinkle them over with salt
11. When they are cold, put them into a glass jar with slight layers of mace and sliced ginger
12. Fill up the jar with cold cider vinegar
13. Put a spoonful of sweet oil on the top of each jar
14. Cork it closely
