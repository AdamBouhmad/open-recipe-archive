## Ingredients

- fine juicy red cherries
- loaf-sugar

## Directions

1. Take fine juicy red cherries, and stone them.
2. Save half the stones, crack them, and extract the kernels.
3. Put the cherries and the kernels into a preserving kettle over a slow fire, and let them boil gently in their juice for half an hour.
4. Then transfer them to a jelly-bag, and squeeze out the juice.
5. Measure it, and to each pint allow a pound of fine loaf-sugar.
6. Dissolve the sugar in the juice, and then boil and skim it for twenty or thirty minutes.
7. Put it up in tumblers covered with tissue paper.
