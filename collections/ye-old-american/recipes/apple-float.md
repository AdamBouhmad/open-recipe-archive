## Ingredients

- One dozen apples
- One pound and a half of sugar
- Water
- One large lemon
- An ounce of gelatine
- A pint of cold water

## Directions

1. Pare and core the apples
2. Put the apples on with water enough to cover them and let them stew until they look as if they would break
3. Take them out and put the sugar in the same water
4. Let the syrup come to a boil
5. Put in the apples and let them stew until done through and clear
6. Take them out
7. Slice into the syrup one large lemon and add an ounce of gelatine dissolved in a pint of cold water
8. Let the whole mix well and come to a boil
9. Pour upon the apples
10. The syrup will congeal
11. Eat cold with cream
