## Ingredients

- pair of fine fowls
- salt
- water
- two tablespoonfuls of powdered ginger
- one tablespoonful of fresh turmeric
- teaspoonful of ground black pepper
- mace
- cloves
- cardamom seeds
- cayenne pepper
- eight large onions
- butter
- flour
- boiled rice

## Directions

1. Take a pair of fine fowls, and having cut them in pieces lay them in salt and water till the seasoning is ready.
2. Take two tablespoonfuls of powdered ginger, one tablespoonful of fresh turmeric, a teaspoonful of ground black pepper; some mace, a few cloves, some cardamom seeds, and a little cayenne pepper with a small portion of salt.
3. Put all into a mortar, and add to them eight large onions, chopped or cut small.
4. Mix and beat all together, till the onions, spices, &c. form a paste.
5. Put the chickens into a pan with sufficient butter rolled in flour, and fry them till they are brown, but not till quite done.
6. Set over the fire a sauce-pan three parts full of water, or sufficient to cover the chickens when they are ready.
7. As soon as the water boils, throw in the curry-paste.
8. When the paste has all dissolved, and is thoroughly mixed with the water, put in the pieces of chicken to boil, or rather to simmer.
9. When the chicken is quite done, put it into a large dish, and eat it with boiled rice.
