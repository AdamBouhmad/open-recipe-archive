## Ingredients

- one quart milk
- two heaping tablespoonfuls of Indian meal
- four tablespoonfuls of sugar
- one tablespoonful of butter
- three eggs
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one-half teaspoonful of ginger
- sauce of heated syrup and butter

## Directions

1. Boil milk in double boiler
2. Sprinkle the meal into it, stirring all the while
3. Cook twelve minutes, stirring often
4. Beat together the eggs, salt, sugar and one-half teaspoonful of ginger
5. Stir the butter into the meal and milk
6. Pour this gradually over the egg mixture
7. Bake slowly one hour
8. Serve with sauce of heated syrup and butter
