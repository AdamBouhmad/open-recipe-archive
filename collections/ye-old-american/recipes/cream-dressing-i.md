## Ingredients

- ½ tablespoon salt
- ½ tablespoon mustard
- ¾ tablespoon sugar
- 1 egg slightly beaten
- 2½ tablespoons melted butter
- ¾ cup cream
- ¼ cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given, adding vinegar very slowly.
2. Cook over boiling water, stirring constantly until mixture thickens.
3. Strain and cool.
