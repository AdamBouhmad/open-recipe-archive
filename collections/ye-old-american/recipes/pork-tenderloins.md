## Ingredients

- pork tenderloins
- lard
- flour
- butter
- milk or cream
- Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Fry them in a little lard, turning them to have them cooked through
2. When done, remove, and keep hot while making a gravy by dredging a little flour into the hot fat
3. If not enough add a little butter or lard
4. Stir until browned, and add a little milk or cream
5. Stir briskly, and pour over the dish
6. A little Worcestershire sauce may be added to the gravy if desired
