## Ingredients

- pumpkin or squash
- water

## Directions

1. Cut up pumpkin or squash into small pieces, first cutting off the peel
2. Stew them until tender, add no seasoning
3. Mash them very fine with a potato masher
4. Have ready your cans, made hot
5. Fill them with the hot pumpkin or squash
6. Seal tight
7. Place in a dark, cool closet
