## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water
- Butter
- Bread for toast

## Directions

1. Pour some boiling water out of a tea-kettle through a clean cloth spread over the top of a broad stew-pan
2. Set the pan with the strained water on hot coals, and when it boils, break each egg separately into a saucer
3. Remove the pan from the fire, and slip the eggs one by one into the surface of the water
4. Let the pan stand till the white of the eggs is set
5. Place it again on the coals, and as soon as the water boils again, the eggs will be sufficiently done
6. Take them out carefully with an egg-slice, and trim off all the ragged edges from the white
7. Have ready some thin slices of buttered toast with the crust cut off
8. Lay them in the bottom of the dish, with a poached egg on each slice of toast, and send them to the breakfast table
