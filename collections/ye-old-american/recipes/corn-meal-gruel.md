## Ingredients

- two tablespoonfuls of fine Indian meal
- cold water
- saltspoonful of salt
- one quart of boiling water

## Directions

1. Mix two tablespoonfuls of fine Indian meal smooth with cold water, and a saltspoonful of salt
2. Add one quart of boiling water and cook twenty minutes
3. Stir it frequently, and if it becomes too thick use boiling water to thin it
