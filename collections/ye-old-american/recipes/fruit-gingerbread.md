## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. flour
- ¾ lb. butter
- 1 lb. sugar
- 1 lb. raisins, seeded and chopped
- 1 lb. currants, well washed
- 2 cups molasses
- ½ cup sour cream
- 6 eggs
- 1 heaping teaspoonful soda dissolved in hot water
- 2 tablespoonfuls ginger
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful cloves

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar, warm the molasses slightly, and beat these together.
2. Add the beaten yolks, next the milk and spice, the soda, the flour and whites well whipped.
3. Lastly, the fruit, which must be thickly dredged.
4. Beat well before baking.
5. Bake in two broad pans, in a moderate oven.
