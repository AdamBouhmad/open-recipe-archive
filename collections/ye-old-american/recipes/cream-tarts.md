## Ingredients

- puff paste
- one teacupful sweet and fresh cream
- egg-beater
- one heaping tablespoonful fine white sugar
- one teaspoonful lemon or vanilla

## Directions

1. Make a rich, brittle crust, with which cover your patty-pans, smoothing off the edges nicely and bake well.
2. While these shells are cooling, take one teacupful of perfectly sweet and fresh cream, skimmed free of milk; put this into a large bowl or other deep dish.
3. With your egg-beater whip it to a thick, stiff froth.
4. Add a heaping tablespoonful of fine white sugar, with a teaspoonful of lemon or vanilla.
5. Fill the cold shells with this and set in a cool place till tea is ready.
