## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- flour
- cooked carrot
- green peas
- lemon juice
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare and boil a chicken, following recipe for Boiled Fowl.
2. Reduce the liquor to two cups.
3. Make sauce with two tablespoons each butter and flour cooked together.
4. Add to sauce one-half cup each of cooked carrot (cut in fancy shapes) and green peas, one teaspoon lemon juice, yolks of two eggs, salt and pepper.
5. Place chicken on hot platter, surround with sauce, and sprinkle chicken and sauce with one-half tablespoon finely chopped parsley.
