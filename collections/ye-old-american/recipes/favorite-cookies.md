## Ingredients

- One cup of butter
- one and a half cups of sugar
- one-half cup of sour milk
- one level teaspoonful of soda
- a teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
- Flour enough to roll

## Directions

1. Make quite soft
2. Put a tablespoonful of fine sugar on a plate and dip the tops of each as you cut them out
3. Place on buttered tins and bake in a quick oven a light brown
