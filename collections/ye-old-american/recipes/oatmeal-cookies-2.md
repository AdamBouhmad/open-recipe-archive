## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- ¼ cup sugar
- ¼ cup thin cream
- ¼ cup milk
- ½ cup fine oatmeal
- 2 cups flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Beat egg until light, add sugar, cream, and milk; then add oatmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt, mixed and sifted.
2. Toss on a floured board, roll, cut in shape, and bake in a moderate oven.
