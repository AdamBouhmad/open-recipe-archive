## Ingredients

- butter
- a pint of milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Melt as much butter in a pint of milk, as will make it rich as cream
2. Make the flour into a paste with this
3. Knead it well, roll it out frequently
4. Cut it in squares, and bake on a griddle
