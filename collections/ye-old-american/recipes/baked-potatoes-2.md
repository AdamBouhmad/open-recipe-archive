## Ingredients

- potatoes
- fat of some kind

## Directions

1. Potatoes are either baked in their jackets or peeled
2. They should not be exposed to a fierce heat
3. Frequently turn them while being baked and keep from touching each other in the oven or dish
4. When done in their skins, wash and brush them before baking
5. They may be baked in wood-ashes, or in a Dutch oven in front of the fire
6. When pared they should be baked in a dish and fat of some kind added to prevent their outsides from becoming burnt
7. Cover with a folded napkin that allows the steam to escape, or absorbs the moisture
8. Serve promptly when done; require about three-quarters of an hour to one hour to bake them, if of a good size
