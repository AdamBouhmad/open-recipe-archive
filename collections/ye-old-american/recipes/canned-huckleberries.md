## Ingredients

- berries
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash berries
2. Put in a preserving kettle with a small quantity of water to prevent berries from burning
3. Cook until soft, stirring occasionally
4. Put in jars
5. No sugar is required, but a sprinkling of salt is an agreeable addition
