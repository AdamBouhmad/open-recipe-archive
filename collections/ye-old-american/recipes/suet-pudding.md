## Ingredients

- 1 cup finely chopped suet
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon ginger
- ½ teaspoon clove
- ½ teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients
2. Add molasses and milk to suet; combine mixtures
3. Turn into buttered mould, cover, and steam three hours
4. Serve with Sterling Sauce
5. Raisins and currants may be added
