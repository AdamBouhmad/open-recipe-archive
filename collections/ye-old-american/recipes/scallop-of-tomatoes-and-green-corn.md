## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- green corn
- fat pork
- shallot
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- butter
- grated bread-crumbs

## Directions

1. Make a force-meat substituting for the bread-crumbs, green corn cut from the cob, and seasoning with some fat pork chopped very fine, a minced shallot, pepper, salt, and sugar.
2. Pack tomatoes and force-meat in alternate layers in a pudding-dish.
3. Let the top layer be tomatoes, butter and season, and sift grated bread-crumbs over it to brown the scallop.
4. Bake covered half an hour.
5. Uncover and leave in the oven as much longer.
