## Ingredients

- shad
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Split and wash the shad and afterwards dry it in a cloth
2. Season it with salt and pepper
3. Have ready a bed of clear, bright coals
4. Grease your gridiron well, and as soon as it is hot, lay the shad upon it, the flesh side down
5. Cover with a dripping-pan and broil it for about a quarter of an hour, or more, according to the thickness
6. Butter it well and send it to the table
