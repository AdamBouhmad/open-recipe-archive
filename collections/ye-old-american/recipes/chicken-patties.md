## Ingredients

- cold chicken
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- onion
- chicken gravy
- cream sauce
- scalloped shells
- pastry
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Mince up fine cold chicken, either roasted or boiled
2. Season it with pepper and salt, and a little minced parsley and onion
3. Moisten it with chicken gravy or cream sauce
4. fill scalloped shells that are lined with pastry with the mixture
5. sprinkle bread crumbs over the tops
6. Put two or three tiny pieces of butter over each
7. bake brown in a hot oven
