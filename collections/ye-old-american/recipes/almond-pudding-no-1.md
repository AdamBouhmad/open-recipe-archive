## Ingredients

- two quarts of milk
- two heaping tablespoonfuls of sifted flour
- two pounds of sweet almonds
- a teaspoonful of salt
- a cupful of sugar
- flavoring
- six eggs
- butter
- a gill of brandy or wine

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of milk into a double boiler
2. Stir into it two heaping tablespoonfuls of sifted flour that has been stirred to a cream, with a little of the milk
3. When it boils, care should be taken that it does not burn
4. When cooked, take from the fire and let it cool
5. Take the skins off from two pounds of sweet almonds, pound them fine, stir them into the milk
6. Add a teaspoonful of salt, a cupful of sugar, flavoring and six well-beaten eggs, the yolks and whites beaten separately
7. Put bits of butter over the top
8. Bake one hour
