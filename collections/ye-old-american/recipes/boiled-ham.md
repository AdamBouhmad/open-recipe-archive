## Ingredients

- ham
- cold water
- black pepper
- whole cloves

## Directions

1. Remove all dust and mold by wiping with a coarse cloth
2. Soak it for an hour in cold water then wash it thoroughly
3. Cut with a sharp knife the hardened surface from the base and butt of the ham
4. Place it over the fire in cold water and let it come to a moderate boil
5. Keep it steadily at this point, allowing it to cook twenty minutes for every pound of meat
6. A ham weighing twelve pounds will require four hours to cook properly
7. Remove the skin by peeling it off and place it on a platter, the fat side up
8. Dot the surface with spots of black pepper and stick in some whole cloves
9. If the ham is to be served cold, allow it to remain in the pot until the water becomes cold
