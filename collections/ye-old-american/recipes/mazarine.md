## Ingredients

- Brioche
- rich Vanilla Ice Cream
- one can apricots
- lemon juice
- sugar
- apricot halves
- glacé cherries
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Bake Brioche in a Charlotte Russe mould or individual tins
2. Cool, cut a slice from top of cake or cakes, and remove centre or centres, leaving a wall or walls one-half inch thick
3. Fill with rich Vanilla Ice Cream
4. Invert on serving dish
5. Drain apricots and force the fruit through a strainer
6. Cook syrup until sufficiently reduced to add to fruit, and make of consistency of marmalade
7. Add a few drops lemon juice and sugar if necessary
8. Pour over ice cream
9. Decorate top with halves of apricots, glacé cherries, and whipped cream
