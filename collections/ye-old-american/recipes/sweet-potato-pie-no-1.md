## Ingredients

- firm sweet potatoes
- paste
- sugar
- whole cloves
- tablespoonful melted butter
- water
- crust

## Directions

1. Parboil, skin, and slice crosswise firm sweet potatoes.
2. Line a dish with paste, put in a layer of sliced potato, sprinkle thickly with sugar, scatter among them a few whole cloves, and cover with more slices.
3. Fill the dish in this order; put a tablespoonful of melted butter in each pie; pour in a little water; cover with crust, and bake.
4. Eat cold.
