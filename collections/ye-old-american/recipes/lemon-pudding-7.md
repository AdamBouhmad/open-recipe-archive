## Ingredients

- one small cupful of butter
- grated peel of two large lemons
- juice of one lemon
- yolks of ten eggs
- whites of five eggs
- one cupful and a half of white sugar
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Beat all together
2. Line a deep pudding-dish with puff paste
3. Bake the lemon pudding in it
4. While baking, beat the whites of the remaining five eggs to a stiff froth
5. Whip in fine white sugar to taste
6. Cover the top of the pudding when baked with the meringue
7. Return to the oven for a moment to brown
8. Eat cold; it requires no sauce
