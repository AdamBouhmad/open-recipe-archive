## Ingredients

- one pound currants
- one drachm nutmeg
- mace and cinnamon each
- a little salt
- one pound of citron
- orange peel candied
- and almonds bleach'd
- 6 pound of flour (well dry'd)
- beat 21 eggs
- add with 1 quart new ale yeast
- half pint of wine
- 3 half pints of cream
- raisins, q: s

## Directions

1. Mix one pound currants, one drachm nutmeg, mace and cinnamon each, a little salt, one pound of citron, orange peel candied, and almonds bleach'd, 6 pound of flour (well dry'd), beat 21 eggs, and add with 1 quart new ale yeast, half pint of wine, 3 half pints of cream and raisins, q: s
