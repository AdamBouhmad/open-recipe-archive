## Ingredients

- one cupful of cold boiled rice
- one cupful of sugar
- four eggs
- a pinch of soda
- a pinch of salt
- four ounces of butter
- ten drops of essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Put rice, sugar, eggs, soda and salt in a bowl and beat it up until it is very light and white
2. Beat four ounces of butter to a cream and put it into the pudding with ten drops of essence of lemon
3. Beat altogether for five minutes
4. Butter a mold and pour the pudding into it
5. Boil for two hours
6. Serve with sweet fruit sauce
