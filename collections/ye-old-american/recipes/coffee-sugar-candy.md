## Ingredients

- two pounds of coffee-sugar
- vinegar
- butter the size of a walnut
- mixed fruits and nuts

## Directions

1. Moisten coffee-sugar with vinegar
2. Put in a piece of butter as large as a walnut
3. Stew till it hardens, but take off before it gets to the brittle stage
4. Beat it with a spoon six or eight times
5. Stir in the mixed fruits and nuts
6. Pour into a wet cloth and roll it up like a pudding, twisting the ends of the cloth to mold it
7. Let it get cold and slice off pieces as it may be wanted for eating
