## Ingredients

- Brown Bread
- finely chopped peanuts
- salt
- grated cheese
- chopped English walnut meat

## Directions

1. Brown Bread to be used for sandwiches is best steamed in one-pound baking-powder boxes.
2. Spread and cut bread as for other sandwiches.
3. Put between layers finely chopped peanuts seasoned with salt; or grated cheese mixed with chopped English walnut meat seasoned with salt.
