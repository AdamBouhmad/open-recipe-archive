## Ingredients

- cold roast chicken
- cold boiled ham
- gravy or hot water
- cream
- butter
- pepper
- cold bread
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Mince some cold roast chicken, and a like quantity of cold boiled ham.
2. Put the mixture into a saucepan, with enough gravy—chicken or veal—to make a soft paste.
3. If you have no gravy, use a little hot water, a few spoonfuls of cream, and a fair lump of butter.
4. Season with pepper to your taste.
5. Stir while it heats almost to boiling, working it very smooth.
6. In about five minutes after it begins to smoke, take from the fire and spread in a dish to cool.
7. With a good-sized cake-cutter, or a plain thin-edged tumbler, cut some rounds of cold bread, and butter one side of each.
8. Sprinkle the buttered sides with grated cheese, and, when the chicken is cold, put a layer between these.
