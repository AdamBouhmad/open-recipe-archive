## Ingredients

- Yolks of four eggs, whipped very light
- 1 lemon—juice and half the grated peel
- 1 good glass of wine
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1 cup of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the sugar, add the yolks, lemon, and spice.
2. Beat ten minutes and put in the wine, still stirring hard.
3. Set within a saucepan of boiling water, and beat while it heats, but do not let it boil.
4. Pour over the pudding.
