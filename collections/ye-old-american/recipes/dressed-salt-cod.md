## Ingredients

- salt fish
- water
- vinegar
- egg sauce
- parsnips
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Steep your salt fish in water all night, with a glass of vinegar
2. This will take out the salt and make it taste like fresh fish
3. The next day boil it
4. When it is enough take off the skin
5. Pull it in fleaks into your dish
6. Pour egg sauce over it, or parsnips boiled and beat fine, with butter and cream
7. Send it to the table on a water plate, for it will soon grow cold
