## Ingredients

- four or five bunches of asparagus
- meat

## Directions

1. Cut off the green tops of asparagus, and put half of them into the soup, after the meat has been boiled to pieces and strained out
2. The asparagus must be boiled till quite dissolved, and till it has given a green colour to the soup
3. Take the remainder of the asparagus tops (which must all this time have been lying in cold water) and put them into the soup, and let them boil about twenty minutes
4. Serve it up with small squares of toast in the tureen
