## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 3/4 tablespoon butter
- 1/4 pound mild cheese
- milk
- buttered cracker crumbs
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Steam one cup rice, allowing one tablespoon salt
2. Cover bottom of buttered pudding-dish with rice
3. Dot over with three-fourths tablespoon butter
4. Sprinkle with thin shavings mild cheese and a few grains cayenne
5. Repeat until rice and one-fourth pound cheese are used
6. Add milk to half the depth of contents of dish
7. Cover with buttered cracker crumbs
8. Bake until cheese melts
