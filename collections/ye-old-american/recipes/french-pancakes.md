## Ingredients

- ¼ cup bread flour
- ⅓ cup milk
- 1 egg
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. To bread flour add milk, egg, and salt
2. Beat thoroughly
3. Heat an omelet pan, butter generously
4. Cover bottom of pan with mixture, cook until browned on one side
5. Turn, and cook on other side
