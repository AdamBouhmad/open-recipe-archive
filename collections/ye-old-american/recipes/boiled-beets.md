## Ingredients

- Beets
- pepper
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Wash the beets, but do not scrape or cut them while raw
2. Boil them from two to three hours, according to their size
3. When tender all through, take them up and scrape off all the outside
4. If young beets, split down and cut into long pieces, season with pepper, and send to table with melted butter
5. Otherwise slice thin after they are quite cold and pour vinegar over them
