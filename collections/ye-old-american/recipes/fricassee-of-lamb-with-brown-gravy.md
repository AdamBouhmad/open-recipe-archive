## Ingredients

- three pounds lamb from the fore-quarter
- boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter or mutton fat
- one and one-half cups Brown Sauce

## Directions

1. Order three pounds lamb from the fore-quarter and cut in pieces for serving
2. Wipe meat and put in kettle
3. Cover with boiling water and cook slowly until meat is tender
4. Remove from water and cool
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. Dredge with flour and sauté in butter or mutton fat
7. Arrange on platter
8. Make Brown Sauce from liquor in which meat was cooked after removing all fat
9. Pour one and one-half cups Brown Sauce around meat
10. It is better to cook meat the day before serving so fat may be more easily removed
