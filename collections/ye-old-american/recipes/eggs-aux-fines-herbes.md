## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- fresh milk
- chopped parsley
- thick cream
- five eggs
- fried bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Roll an ounce of butter in a good teaspoonful of flour
2. Season with pepper, salt and nutmeg
3. Put it into a coffeecupful of fresh milk, together with two teaspoonfuls of chopped parsley
4. Stir and simmer it for fifteen minutes
5. Add a teacupful of thick cream
6. Hard-boil five eggs and halve them
7. Arrange them in a dish with the ends upwards
8. Pour the sauce over them, and decorate with little heaps of fried bread crumbs round the margin of the dish
