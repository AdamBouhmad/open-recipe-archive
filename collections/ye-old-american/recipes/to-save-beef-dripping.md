## Ingredients

- beef fat from roasting
- salt or seasonings as needed

## Directions

1. Pour off through the spout of the roaster or tin-kitchen all the fat from the top of the gravy, after you have done basting the meat with it.
2. Hold a little sieve under the spout, and strain the dripping through it into a pan.
3. Set it away in a cool place; and next day when it is cold and congealed, turn the cake of fat, and scrape with a knife the sediment from the bottom.
4. Put the dripping into a jar; cover it tightly, and set it away in the refrigerator, or in the coldest place you have.
