## Ingredients

- clams
- pepper
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- powdered cracker
- warm milk
- clam liquor
- beaten egg
- melted butter
- clam-shells

## Directions

1. Chop the clams fine, and season with pepper and salt.
2. Mix in another dish some powdered cracker, moistened first with warm milk, then with the clam liquor, a beaten egg or two, and some melted butter.
3. Stir in with this the chopped clams.
4. Wash as many clam-shells as the mixture will fill; wipe and butter them; fill, heaping up and smoothing over with a silver knife or teaspoon.
5. Range in rows in your baking-pan, and cook until nicely browned.
6. Alternatively, if you do not care to be troubled with the shells, bake in patty-pans, sending to table hot in the tins.
