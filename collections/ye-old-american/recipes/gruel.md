## Ingredients

- three large table-spoonfuls of oatmeal or Indian meal
- one quart of water
- salt
- butter
- wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Allow three large table-spoonfuls of oatmeal or Indian meal to a quart of water
2. Put the meal into a large bowl and add the water a little at a time, mixing and bruising the meal with the back of a spoon
3. Pour off the liquid into another bowl before adding fresh water to the meal, till you have used it all up
4. Boil the mixture for twenty minutes, stirring it all the while
5. Add a little salt, then strain the gruel and sweeten it
6. A piece of butter may be stirred into it; and if thought proper, a little wine and nutmeg
7. It should be taken warm
