## Ingredients

- one tongue
- half a pound of brown sugar
- a piece of saltpetre the size of a pea
- a tablespoonful of ground cloves
- three-quarters of a pound of salt
- two quarts of water
- flour
- water
- lard

## Directions

1. Rub into each tongue a mixture made of half a pound of brown sugar, a piece of saltpetre the size of a pea and a tablespoonful of ground cloves
2. Put it in a brine made of three-quarters of a pound of salt to two quarts of water and keep covered
3. Pickle two weeks, then wash well and dry with a cloth
4. Roll out a thin paste made of flour and water
5. Smear it all over the tongue and place in a pan to bake slowly
6. Baste well with lard and hot water
7. When done scrape off the paste and skim
