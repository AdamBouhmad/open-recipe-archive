## Ingredients

- butter
- boiled milk
- flour
- cold milk
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- grated cheese
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Melt an ounce of butter and whisk into it a pint of boiled milk
2. Dissolve two tablespoonfuls of flour in a gill of cold milk, add it to the boiled milk and let it cool
3. Beat the yolks of four eggs with a heaping teaspoonful of salt, half a teaspoonful of pepper and five ounces of grated cheese
4. Whip the whites of the eggs and add them
5. Pour the mixture into a deep tin lined with buttered paper, and allow for the rising, say four inches
6. Bake twenty minutes and serve the moment it leaves the oven
