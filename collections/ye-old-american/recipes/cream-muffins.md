## Ingredients

- 1 quart sweet milk (half-cream, if you can get it)
- 1 quart flour—heaping
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful butter, and the same of lard—melted together

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs light—the yolks and whites separately
2. Add the milk, with a little salt
3. Add the shortening
4. Stir in the flour lightly
5. Bake immediately in well-greased rings half-filled with the batter
6. Your oven should be hot, and the muffins sent to table so soon as they are taken up
