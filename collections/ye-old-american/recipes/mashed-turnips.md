## Ingredients

- turnips
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. Peel and lay in cold water, slightly salted, until the water boils in the saucepan intended for them
2. Put them in and boil until very tender
3. Drain and mash in the cullender with a wooden spoon
4. Stir in at the last a tablespoonful of butter with pepper and salt to taste
5. Serve hot
6. Alternatively, if the turnips are young, rub them when tender through the cullender, add a little milk, butter, pepper, and salt, heat to boiling in a clean saucepan and serve
