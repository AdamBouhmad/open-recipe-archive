## Ingredients

- half a cake of chocolate
- one quart of milk
- four teaspoonfuls of cornstarch
- yolks of three eggs
- one cup and a half of sugar
- vanilla
- whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Break chocolate in one quart of milk and put on the range until it reaches boiling point
2. Remove the mixture from the range; add four teaspoonfuls of cornstarch mixed with the yolks of three eggs and one cup and a half of sugar
3. Stir constantly until thick
4. Remove from the fire and flavor with vanilla
5. Pour the mixture in a dish
6. Beat the whites of the three eggs to a stiff froth and add a little sugar
7. Cover the top of the pudding with a meringue and set in the oven until a light brown
8. Serve cold
