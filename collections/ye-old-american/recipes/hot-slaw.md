## Ingredients

- cabbage
- dressing for cold slaw

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage as for cold slaw
2. Put it into a stewpan and set it on the top of the stove for half an hour, or till hot all through
3. Do not let it boil
4. Make a dressing the same as for cold slaw, and while hot, pour it over the hot cabbage
5. Stir it until well mixed and the cabbage looks coddled
6. Serve immediately
