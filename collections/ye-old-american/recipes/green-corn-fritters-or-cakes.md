## Ingredients

- corn
- eggs
- milk
- cream
- melted butter
- salt
- flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Grate the corn and allow an egg and a half for every cupful, with a tablespoonful of milk or cream.
2. Beat the eggs well and add the corn by degrees, beating very hard.
3. Salt to taste and put a tablespoonful of melted butter to every pint of corn.
4. Stir in the milk and thicken with just enough flour to hold them together—say a tablespoonful for every two eggs.
5. Fry in hot lard as you would fritters, or cook upon a griddle like batter cakes.
6. Test a little first to see that it is of the right consistency.
