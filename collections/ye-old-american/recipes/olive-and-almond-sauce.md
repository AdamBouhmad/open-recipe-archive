## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup White Stock
- ½ cup cream
- ¼ cup shredded almonds
- 1 teaspoon beef extract
- 8 olives (stoned and cut in quarters)
- ½ tablespoon lemon juice
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add flour, and pour on gradually White Stock.
2. Just before serving add remaining ingredients.
3. Serve with boiled or steamed fish.
