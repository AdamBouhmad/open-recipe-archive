## Ingredients

- rock-fish
- water
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs
- celery sauce
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Having cleaned the rock-fish, put it into a fish-kettle with water enough to cover it well, having first dissolved a handful of salt in the water.
2. Set it over a moderate fire, and do not let it boil too fast.
3. Skim it well.
4. When done, drain it, and put it on a large dish.
5. Have ready a few eggs boiled hard.
6. Cut them in half, and lay them closely on the back of the fish in a straight line from the head to the tail.
7. Send with it in a boat, celery sauce flavoured with a little cayenne.
