## Ingredients

- ham
- hot water

## Directions

1. Cut the ham into very thin slices, (the thinner the better.)
2. Soak them in hot water at least half an hour, (a whole hour is better,) to draw out some of the salt; changing the water several times, and always pouring it on scalding hot.
3. After soaking, dry the slices in a cloth, and then heat your gridiron, and broil them over a clear fire.
