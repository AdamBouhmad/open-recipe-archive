## Ingredients

- One cupful of sweet milk
- The whites of two eggs
- A tablespoonful of white sugar
- A piece of butter the size of a nutmeg
- Half a cupful of cold milk
- A teaspoonful of cornstarch

## Directions

1. Heat one cupful of sweet milk until boiling
2. Beat together the whites of two eggs, a tablespoonful of white sugar and a piece of butter the size of a nutmeg
3. Add half a cupful of cold milk and a teaspoonful of cornstarch
4. Stir well together until very light and smooth
5. Add it to the boiling milk
6. Cook it until it thickens; it must not boil
7. Set it aside to cool
8. Serve in a creamer
