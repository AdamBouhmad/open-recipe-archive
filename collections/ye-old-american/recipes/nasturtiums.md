## Ingredients

- cold vinegar
- green seeds of the nasturtian

## Directions

1. Have ready a stone or glass jar of the best cold vinegar
2. Take the green seeds of the nasturtian after the flower has gone off when they are full-grown but not old
3. Pick off the stems, and put the seeds into the vinegar
4. Keep them covered with sufficient cold vinegar to cover them for a year
