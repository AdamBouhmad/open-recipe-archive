## Ingredients

- ¼ cup fine stale bread crumbs
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 egg
- ⅔ cup raw fish
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook bread and milk to a paste, add egg well beaten, and fish pounded and forced through a purée strainer.
2. Season with salt.
