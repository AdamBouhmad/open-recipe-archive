## Ingredients

- pigs' feet
- salt
- pepper
- Maître d'Hôtel Butter or Sauce Piquante

## Directions

1. Wipe, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and broil six to eight minutes.
2. Serve with Maître d'Hôtel Butter or Sauce Piquante.
