## Ingredients

- cupful cold boiled hominy
- tablespoonful melted butter
- milk
- teaspoonful white sugar
- well-beaten egg
- flour
- beaten egg
- cracker-crumbs
- hot lard

## Directions

1. To a cupful of cold boiled hominy add a tablespoonful melted butter and stir hard, moistening by degrees with a little milk, beating to a soft light paste.
2. Put in a teaspoonful of white sugar.
3. Add a well-beaten egg.
4. Roll into oval balls with floured hands.
5. Dip in beaten egg, then cracker-crumbs.
6. Fry in hot lard.
