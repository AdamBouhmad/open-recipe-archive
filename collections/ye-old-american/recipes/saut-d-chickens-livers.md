## Ingredients

- one slice bacon
- two tablespoons butter
- one finely chopped shallot
- six chickens' livers
- two tablespoons flour
- one cup Brown Stock
- one teaspoon lemon juice
- one-fourth cup sliced mushrooms
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut one slice bacon in small pieces and cook five minutes with two tablespoons butter.
2. Remove bacon and add one finely chopped shallot.
3. Fry two minutes.
4. Add six chickens' livers cleaned and separated and cook two minutes.
5. Add two tablespoons flour, one cup Brown Stock, one teaspoon lemon juice, and one-fourth cup sliced mushrooms.
6. Cook two minutes, turn into a serving dish, and sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
