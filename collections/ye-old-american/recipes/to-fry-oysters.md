## Ingredients

- oysters
- grated bread
- pepper
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take a quarter of a hundred of large oysters
2. Wash them and roll them in grated bread, with pepper and salt
3. Fry them a light brown
4. You may add a little parsley, shred fine if you choose
