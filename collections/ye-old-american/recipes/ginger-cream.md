## Ingredients

- ¼ box gelatine or 1 tablespoon granulated gelatine
- ¼ cup cold water
- 1 cup milk
- Yolks 2 eggs
- ¼ cup sugar
- Few grains salt
- 1 tablespoon wine
- ½ tablespoon brandy
- 2 tablespoons ginger syrup
- ¼ cup Canton ginger, cut in pieces
- Whip from 2½ cups cream

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine, and add to custard made of milk, eggs, sugar, and salt
2. Strain, chill in pan of ice-water
3. Add flavorings, and when it begins to thicken fold in whip from cream
