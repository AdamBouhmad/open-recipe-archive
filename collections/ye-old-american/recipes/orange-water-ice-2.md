## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of gelatine
- one gill of water
- half a pint of boiling water
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- the strained juice of six oranges
- cold water

## Directions

1. Add a tablespoonful of gelatine to one gill of water and let it stand twenty minutes
2. Add half a pint of boiling water and stir until dissolved
3. Add four ounces of powdered sugar, the strained juice of six oranges and cold water enough to make a full quart in all
4. Stir until the sugar is dissolved
5. Pour into the freezing can and freeze
