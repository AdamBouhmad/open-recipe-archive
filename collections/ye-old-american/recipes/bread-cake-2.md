## Ingredients

- 2 cups risen dough
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup butter, creamed with the sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 even teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water
- 2 tablespoonfuls sweet milk
- ½ lb. currants, well washed and dredged
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful cloves

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks very light, add the creamed butter and sugar, the spice, milk, soda, and dough.
2. Stir until all are well mixed; put in the beaten whites, lastly the fruit.
3. Beat hard five minutes, let it rise twenty minutes in two well-buttered pans, and bake half an hour or until done.
