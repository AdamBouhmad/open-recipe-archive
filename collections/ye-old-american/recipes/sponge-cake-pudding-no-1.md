## Ingredients

- common sponge cake
- butter
- four eggs
- one quart of milk

## Directions

1. Bake a common sponge cake in a flat-bottomed pudding-dish
2. When ready to use, cut in six or eight pieces
3. Split and spread with butter and return them to the dish
4. Make a custard with four eggs to a quart of milk
5. Flavor and sweeten to taste
6. Pour over the cake
7. Bake one-half hour
8. Serve with or without sauce
