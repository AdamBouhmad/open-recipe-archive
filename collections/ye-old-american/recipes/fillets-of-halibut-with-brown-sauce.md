## Ingredients

- one and one-half pound slice of halibut
- salt
- pepper
- greased pan
- one and one-half cups Brown Sauce I
- one-half cup buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut a slice of halibut weighing one and one-half pounds in eight short fillets.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, put in greased pan, and bake five minutes.
3. Drain, pour over one and one-half cups Brown Sauce I, cover with one-half cup buttered cracker crumbs, and bake.
