## Ingredients

- 2 cups of sugar
- 1 cup butter
- The yolks of five eggs and whites of two
- 1 cup of milk
- 3½ cups flour
- ⅓ teaspoonful soda
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar
- Whites of three eggs
- 1½ cup sugar
- 3 tablespoonfuls grated chocolate
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla

## Directions

1. Sift soda and cream-tartar into the flour. Bake in jelly-cake tins.
2. For mixture for filling: Beat whites of three eggs, 1½ cup sugar, 3 tablespoonfuls grated chocolate, and 1 teaspoonful vanilla well together, spread between the layers and on top of cake.
