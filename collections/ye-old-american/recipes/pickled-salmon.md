## Ingredients

- fresh salmon
- salted water
- vinegar
- whole black pepper
- nutmeg
- mace
- sweet oil
- fennel

## Directions

1. Clean the salmon and cut it into large pieces.
2. Boil it in salted water as if for eating.
3. Drain it, wrap it in a dry cloth, and set it in a cold place till next day.
4. To one quart of the water in which the salmon was boiled, allow two quarts of the best vinegar, one ounce of whole black pepper, one nutmeg grated, and a dozen blades of mace.
5. Boil all these together in a kettle closely covered to prevent the flavour from evaporating.
6. When the vinegar is quite cold, pour it over the salmon.
7. Put on the top a table-spoonful of sweet oil, which will make it keep the longer.
8. Cover it closely, put it in a dry cool place.
9. Garnish with fennel.
