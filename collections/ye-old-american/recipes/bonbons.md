## Ingredients

- fondant
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Shape fondant in small balls.
2. Allow balls to stand over night.
3. Put fondant in saucepan, and melt over hot water; color and flavor as desired.
4. During dipping, keep fondant over hot water that it may be kept of right consistency.
5. Drop centres in fondant one at a time, stir until covered, remove from fondant, put on oiled paper, and bring end of dipper over the top of bonbon, thus leaving a tail-piece which shows that bonbons have been hand dipped.
6. Stir fondant between dippings to prevent a crust from forming.
