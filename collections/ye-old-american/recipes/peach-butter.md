## Ingredients

- ripe peaches
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare ripe peaches and put them in a preserving kettle, with sufficient water to boil them soft
2. Sift through a colander, removing the stones
3. To each quart of peaches put one and one-half pounds of sugar
4. Boil very slowly one hour
5. Stir often and do not let them burn
6. Put in stone or glass jars, and keep in a cool place
