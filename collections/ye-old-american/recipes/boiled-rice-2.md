## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup whole rice
- just enough water to cover it
- 1 cup of milk
- A little salt
- 1 egg, beaten light
- cream, sugar, and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil the rice in just enough water to cover it
2. When the rice is nearly done, turn off the water
3. Add the milk and simmer, taking care it does not scorch, until the milk boils up well
4. Salt, and beat in the egg
5. Eat warm with cream, sugar, and nutmeg
