## Ingredients

- one quarter pound chocolate
- one quart milk
- six eggs
- one vanilla bean

## Directions

1. Scrape a quarter of a pound of chocolate very fine, put it in a quart of milk
2. Boil it till the chocolate is dissolved, stirring it continually
3. Thicken with six eggs
4. A Vanilla bean boiled with the milk, will improve the flavour greatly
