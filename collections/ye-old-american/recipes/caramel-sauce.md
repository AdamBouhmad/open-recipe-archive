## Ingredients

- four tablespoonfuls of white sugar
- one tablespoonful of water
- a pint of cold water
- a cup of sugar
- half a glass of brandy or a glass of wine

## Directions

1. Place over the fire a saucepan
2. When it begins to be hot, put into it four tablespoonfuls of white sugar and one tablespoonful of water
3. Stir it continually for three or four minutes, until all the water evaporates
4. Watch it carefully until it becomes a delicate brown color
5. Have ready a pint of cold water and cup of sugar mixed with some flavoring
6. Turn it into the saucepan with the browned sugar and let it simmer for ten minutes
7. Add half a glass of brandy or a glass of wine
