## Ingredients

- half a pound of veal
- half a pound of suet
- sweet herbs
- mace
- a small nutmeg
- lemon peel
- pepper
- salt
- the yelks of two eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut veal and suet fine and beat in a marble mortar or wooden bowl
2. Add a few sweet herbs shred fine, a little mace pounded fine, a small nutmeg grated, a little lemon peel, some pepper and salt, and the yelks of two eggs
3. Mix them well together
4. Make them into balls and long pieces
5. Roll them in flour and fry them brown, or if for the use of white sauce, put them in a sauce-pan of hot water and let them boil a few minutes
