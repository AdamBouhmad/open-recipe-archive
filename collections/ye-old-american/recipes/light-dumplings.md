## Ingredients

- grated bread
- butter
- beaten egg
- powdered cinnamon
- wine sauce or molasses and butter

## Directions

1. Mix together as much grated bread, butter and beaten egg (seasoned with powdered cinnamon) as will make a stiff paste
2. Stir it well
3. Make the mixture into round dumplings, with your hands well floured
4. Tie up each in a separate cloth, and boil them a short time,--about fifteen minutes
5. Eat them with wine sauce, or with molasses and butter
