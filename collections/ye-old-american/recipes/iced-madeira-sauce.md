## Ingredients

- ¼ cup orange juice
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- ½ cup Madeira wine
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- Whites 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Freeze fruit juice and wine
2. Boil sugar and water, pour on slowly to beaten whites of eggs
3. Set in pan of salted ice-water, and stir until cold
4. Add to frozen mixture
