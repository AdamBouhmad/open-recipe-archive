## Ingredients

- stale bread
- butter
- water
- sugar
- 1 quart or more huckleberries and blackberries
- thick cream

## Directions

1. Cut stale bread in thin slices and spread with butter
2. Add very little water and little sugar to berries
3. Stew a few minutes until juicy
4. Put layer of buttered bread in buttered pudding-dish, then layer of stewed berries while hot
5. Continue alternating until full, with stewed berries as final covering
6. May be improved with soft frosting over top
7. Eat cold with thick cream and sugar
