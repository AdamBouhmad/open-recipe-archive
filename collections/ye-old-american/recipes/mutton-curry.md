## Ingredients

- mutton from fore-quarter
- cold water
- one quart boiling water
- three onions
- one-half teaspoon peppercorns
- thyme
- parsley
- one-fourth cup butter
- one-fourth cup flour
- one-half teaspoon curry powder
- one-half teaspoon salt
- one-eighth teaspoon pepper
- steamed rice

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut meat from fore-quarter of mutton in one-inch pieces
2. Put in kettle and cover with cold water
3. Bring quickly to boiling-point and drain in colander
4. Pour one quart cold water over meat
5. Return meat to kettle and cover with one quart boiling water
6. Add onions cut in slices, peppercorns, thyme, and parsley
7. Simmer until meat is tender
8. Remove meat and strain liquor
9. Cook butter and flour together
10. Add curry powder, salt, and pepper to the flour mixture
11. Thicken liquor with flour mixture
12. Add meat to gravy and reheat
13. Serve with border of steamed rice
