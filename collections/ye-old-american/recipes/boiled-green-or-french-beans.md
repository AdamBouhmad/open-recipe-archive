## Ingredients

- Green or French beans
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Select young, tender, and fresh gathered beans
2. Remove the strings with a knife and take off both ends of the bean
3. Cut them in two or three pieces only
4. As you cut them, throw them into a pan of cold water and let them lay awhile
5. Boil them an hour and a half
6. Drain and press them well
7. Season them with pepper and mix into them a piece of butter
