## Ingredients

- pure muriatic acid one ounce
- water one ounce
- honey two ounces

## Directions

1. mix thoroughly
2. Take a tooth-brush
3. wet it freely with this preparation
4. briskly rub the black teeth
5. immediately wash out the mouth well with water
