## Ingredients

- two quarts of sifted flour
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one tablespoonful of sweet lard
- one egg
- half a pint of milk or plain water

## Directions

1. Make up with half a pint of milk, or if milk is not to be had, plain water will answer
2. Beat well until the dough blisters and cracks
3. Pull off a two-inch square of the dough
4. Roll it into a ball with the hand
5. Flatten and stick with a fork
6. Bake in a quick oven
