## Ingredients

- a cocoa-nut
- three quarters of a pound grated cocoa-nut
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- a glass of wine and rose water mixed
- twelve eggs
- puff paste
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Open the cocoa-nut, pare off the brown skin from the pieces, and wash them all in cold water.
2. Weigh three quarters of a pound, and grate it into a dish.
3. Cut up half a pound of butter into half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar, and stir them together to a cream; add to them a glass of wine and rose water mixed.
4. Beat the whites only of twelve eggs, till they stand alone on the rods.
5. Stir the grated cocoa-nut and the beaten white of egg alternately into the butter and sugar, giving the whole a hard stirring at the last.
6. Put the mixture into a buttered dish, lay puff paste round the flat edge, and bake it half an hour in a moderate oven.
7. When cold, grate powdered sugar over it.
