## Ingredients

- apples or peaches
- nutmeg
- whites of three eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- rose-water or vanilla

## Directions

1. Stew the apples or peaches and sweeten to taste
2. Mash smooth and season with nutmeg
3. Fill the crusts and bake until just done
4. Put on no top crust
5. Take the whites of three eggs for each pie and whip to a stiff froth, and sweeten with three tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
6. Flavor with rose-water or vanilla; beat until it will stand alone
7. Spread it on the pie one-half to one inch thick
8. Set it back into the oven until the meringue is well "set"
9. Eat cold
