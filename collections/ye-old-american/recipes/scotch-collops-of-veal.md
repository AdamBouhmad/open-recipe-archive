## Ingredients

- salt
- pepper
- gravy
- wine
- catsup
- garlic
- mace
- parsley
- bacon
- forcemeat balls
- green pickles

## Directions

1. They may be made of the nice part of the rack, or cut from the fillet
2. Rub a little salt and pepper on them, and fry them a light brown
3. Have a rich gravy seasoned with wine, and any kind of catsup you choose, with a few cloves of garlic, and some pounded mace
4. Thicken it
5. Put the collops in and stew them a short time
6. Take them out, strain the gravy over
7. And garnish with bunches of parsley fried crisp, and thin slices of middling of bacon, curled around a skewer and boiled
