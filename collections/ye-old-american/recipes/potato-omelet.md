## Ingredients

- Mashed Potatoes
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Prepare Mashed Potatoes, turn in hot omelet pan greased with one tablespoon butter, spread evenly, cook slowly until browned underneath, and fold as an omelet.
