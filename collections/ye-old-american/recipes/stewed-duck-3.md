## Ingredients

- ducks
- two or three very thin slices of salt pork
- salt
- pepper
- half a teaspoonful of powdered sage
- green sage
- one chopped onion
- a large tablespoonful of brown flour
- water
- green peas

## Directions

1. Prepare them by cutting them up the same as chicken for fricassee
2. Lay two or three very thin slices of salt pork upon the bottom of a stewpan
3. Lay the pieces of duck upon the pork
4. Let them stew slowly for an hour, closely covered
5. Season with salt and pepper, half a teaspoonful of powdered sage, or some green sage minced fine; one chopped onion
6. Stew another half hour until the duck is tender
7. Stir up a large tablespoonful of brown flour in a little water and add it to the stew
8. Let it boil up, and serve all together in one dish, accompanied with green peas
