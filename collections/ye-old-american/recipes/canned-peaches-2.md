## Ingredients

- peaches
- sugar
- peach leaves
- water

## Directions

1. To one pound of peaches allow half a pound of sugar
2. To six pounds of sugar add half a tumbler of water
3. Put in the kettle a layer of sugar and one of peaches until the whole of both are in
4. Wash about eight peach leaves, tie them up and put into the kettle
5. Remove the peach leaves when you begin to fill up the jars
6. Let the sugared fruit remain on the range, but away from the fire, until upon tipping the vessel to one side you can see some liquid
7. Fill the jars, taking them out of hot water into which they were put when cold, remaining until it was made to boil around them
8. Keep in a dark closet
