## Ingredients

- Green peas
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Gather peas as short a time as possible before cooking and lay in cold water as soon as they are shelled
2. Boil them about an hour till soft
3. When quite done, drain them and mix with them a piece of butter and a little pepper
