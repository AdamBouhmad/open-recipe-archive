## Ingredients

- one pineapple
- half a pound of fine white sugar
- half an ounce of isinglass or patent gelatine
- half a teacupful of water
- a pint of cream
- sponge cake

## Directions

1. Peel and cut a pineapple in slices
2. Put the slices into a stewpan with half a pound of fine white sugar, half an ounce of isinglass, or of patent gelatine (which is better), and half a teacupful of water
3. Stew it until it is quite tender
4. Rub it through a sieve
5. Place it upon ice, and stir it well
6. When it is upon the point of setting, add a pint of cream well whipped
7. Mix it well and pour it into a mold lined with sponge cake, or prepared in any other way you prefer
