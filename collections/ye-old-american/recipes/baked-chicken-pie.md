## Ingredients

- chickens
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- nutmeg
- butter
- flour
- cold water
- paste

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom and sides of a deep dish with a thick paste.
2. Cut up your chickens, and season them to your taste with salt, pepper, mace and nutmeg, put them in.
3. Lay on the top several pieces of butter rolled in flour.
4. Fill up the dish about two-thirds with cold water.
5. Lay on the top crust, notching it handsomely.
6. Cut a slit in the top, and stick into it an ornament of paste made in the form of a tulip.
7. Bake it in a moderate oven.
