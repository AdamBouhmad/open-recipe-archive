## Ingredients

- four cups cranberries
- one cup boiling water
- two cups sugar

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash four cups cranberries.
2. Put in a stewpan with one cup boiling water, and boil twenty minutes.
3. Rub through a sieve, add two cups sugar, and cook five minutes.
4. Turn into a mould or glasses.
