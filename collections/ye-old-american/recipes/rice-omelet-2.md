## Ingredients

- one cup of cold boiled rice
- one cupful of warm milk
- one tablespoonful of butter melted
- one level teaspoonful of salt
- pepper
- three well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Take a cup of cold boiled rice
2. Turn over it a cupful of warm milk
3. Add a tablespoonful of butter melted, a level teaspoonful of salt, a dash of pepper
4. Mix well
5. Add three well-beaten eggs
6. Put a tablespoonful of butter in a hot frying pan
7. When it begins to boil pour in the omelet and set the pan in a hot oven
8. As soon as it is cooked through, fold it double
9. Turn it out on a hot dish
