## Ingredients

- a teaspoonful of chopped onion
- minced parsley

## Directions

1. Make a plain omelet, and when ready to turn spread over it a teaspoonful each of chopped onion and minced parsley
2. Then fold, or, if preferred, mix the minces into the eggs before cooking
