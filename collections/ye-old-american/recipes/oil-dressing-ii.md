## Ingredients

- 1½ teaspoons mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons powdered sugar
- Few grains cayenne
- 2 tablespoons oil
- ⅓ cup vinegar diluted with cold water to make one-half cup
- 2 eggs, slightly beaten

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients, add egg and oil gradually, stirring constantly until thoroughly mixed.
2. Then add diluted vinegar.
3. Cook over boiling water until mixture thickens.
4. Strain and cool.
