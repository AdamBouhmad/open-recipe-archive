## Ingredients

- carrots
- boiling water
- one-half tablespoon butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash, scrape, and cut carrots in strips, cubes, or fancy shapes, cover with boiling water, let stand five minutes; drain, and cook in boiling salted water, to which is added one-half tablespoon butter, until soft.
2. Add to recipe for sauce given under Macédoine of Vegetables à la Poulette (see p. 308).
