## Ingredients

- one quart of corn meal
- three quarts of milk
- three eggs
- one gill of molasses

## Directions

1. Mix one quart of corn meal, with three quarts of milk; take care it be not lumpy
2. add three eggs and a gill of molasses
3. put it on at sun rise, to eat at three o'clock
4. tie the bag properly, as the meal swells very much
