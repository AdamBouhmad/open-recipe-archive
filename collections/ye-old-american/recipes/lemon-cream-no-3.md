## Ingredients

- three lemons
- one quart of milk
- six eggs
- one pint of water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel three lemons and squeeze out the juice into one quart of milk
2. Add the peel cut in pieces and cover the mixture for a few hours
3. Add six eggs, well beaten, and one pint of water, well sweetened
4. Strain and simmer over a gentle fire till it thickens
5. Do not let it boil
6. Serve very cold
