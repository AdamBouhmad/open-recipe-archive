## Ingredients

- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup warm boiled rice
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Whites 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- ⅞ cup flour

## Directions

1. Pour milk over rice and salt, add yolks of eggs beaten until thick and lemon color, butter, flour, and fold in whites of eggs beaten until stiff and dry.
