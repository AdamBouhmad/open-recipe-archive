## Ingredients

- good rich paste
- fowl, one fine large
- corned ham or salt pork, half a pound
- pepper
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Line the bottom and sides of a pot with a good rich paste, reserving enough for a top crust and for square bits to be scattered through the pie
2. Butter the pot very lavishly
3. Cut up a fine large fowl and half a pound of corned ham or salt pork
4. Put in a layer of pork, pepper it, and cover with pieces of the chicken and this with the paste dumplings or squares
5. If you use potatoes, parboil them before putting them into the pie
6. Slice the potatoes and lay them next the pastry squares, then another layer of pork, and so on until the chicken is used up
7. Cover with pastry rolled out quite thick, and slit this in the middle
8. Heat very slowly and boil two hours
9. Turn into a large dish, the lower crust on top, and the gravy about it
