## Ingredients

- turkey remnants
- bones
- water
- turkey gravy or white stock
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut the remnants of turkey from a previous dinner into pieces of equal size
2. Boil the bones in a quart of water until the quart is reduced to a pint
3. Take out the bones and to the liquor add turkey gravy, white stock, or a small piece of butter with salt and pepper
4. Let the liquor prepared boil up once
5. Put in the pieces of turkey, dredge in a little flour, give it one boil-up, and serve in a hot dish
