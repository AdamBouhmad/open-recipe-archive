## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup finnan haddie, parboiled, drained, and separated into small pieces
- Cayenne
- French pancakes
- Beaten egg
- Crumbs

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add flour, and pour on gradually milk
2. Add finnan haddie which has been parboiled, drained, and separated into small pieces
3. Season with cayenne, and spread on a plate to cool
4. Cut French pancakes in pieces two by four inches
5. On lower halves of pieces put one tablespoon mixture
6. Brush edges with beaten egg, fold over upper halves, press edges firmly together, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs
7. Fry in deep fat, and drain
8. Serve garnished with parsley
