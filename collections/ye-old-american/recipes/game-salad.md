## Ingredients

- one can peaches
- lettuce leaves
- two teaspoons sugar
- one teaspoon celery salt
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- one-eighth teaspoon pepper
- a few grains cayenne
- five drops Tabasco
- four tablespoons olive oil
- two tablespoons fresh lime juice

## Directions

1. Drain the syrup from one can peaches.
2. Arrange halves of fruit on lettuce leaves, and pour over all a dressing made by mixing two teaspoons sugar, one teaspoon celery salt, one-fourth teaspoon salt, one-eighth teaspoon pepper, a few grains cayenne, five drops Tabasco, and adding gradually four tablespoons olive oil and two tablespoons fresh lime juice.
