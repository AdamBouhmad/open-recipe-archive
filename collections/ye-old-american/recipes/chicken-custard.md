## Ingredients

- One-fourth cup cooked breast meat of fowl
- One-fourth cup White Stock
- 1 egg
- Salt
- Pepper
- Celery salt
- Paprika
- Slight grating nutmeg
- Few drops essence anchovy

## Directions

1. Chop cooked breast meat of fowl and rub through sieve.
2. Add White Stock and egg slightly beaten.
3. Season with salt, pepper, celery salt, paprika, slight grating nutmeg, and few drops essence anchovy.
4. Turn mixture into buttered mould, bake in a pan of hot water until firm.
5. Cool, remove from mould, and cut in small cubes.
