## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- four tablespoonfuls of melted butter, or half butter and half lard
- half a cupful of yeast
- one teaspoonful of salt
- two eggs
- two quarts of flour

## Directions

1. Beat together milk, melted butter or half butter and half lard, yeast, salt and eggs
2. Stir into this two quarts of flour
3. When this dough is risen, make into two large rolls
4. Cut across the top diagonal gashes just before putting into the oven
5. Bake as any bread
