## Ingredients

- a quart of milk
- twelve yolks of eggs
- peach leaves or peach-kernels or bitter almonds
- cinnamon or vanilla bean

## Directions

1. Make custards in the same manner as plain custards, except that to a quart of milk you must have twelve yolks of eggs, and no whites
