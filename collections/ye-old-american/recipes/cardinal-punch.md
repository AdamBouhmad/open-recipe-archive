## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 2 cups sugar
- ⅔ cup orange juice
- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- ¼ cup brandy
- ¼ cup Curaçoa
- ¼ cup tea infusion

## Directions

1. Make syrup as for Lemon Ice, add fruit juice and tea, freeze to a mush; add strong liquors and continue freezing.
2. Serve in frappé glasses.
