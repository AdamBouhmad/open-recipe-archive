## Ingredients

- full grown tomatos
- pepper
- salt
- pounded cloves
- garlic
- loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Gather full grown tomatos while quite green
2. Take out the stems, and stew them till soft
3. Rub them through a sieve
4. Put the pulp on the fire seasoned highly with pepper, salt, and pounded cloves
5. Add some garlic
6. Mix some loaf sugar with the pulp, and stew until it is a stiff jelly
