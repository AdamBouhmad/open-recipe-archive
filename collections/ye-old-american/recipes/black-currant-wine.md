## Ingredients

- four quarts of whisky
- four quarts of black currants
- four pounds of brown or white sugar
- one tablespoonful of cloves
- one tablespoonful of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Crush the currants and let them stand in the whisky with the spices for three weeks
2. Strain and add the sugar
3. Set away again for three weeks longer
4. Strain and bottle
