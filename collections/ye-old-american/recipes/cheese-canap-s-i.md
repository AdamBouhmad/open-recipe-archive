## Ingredients

- circular pieces of bread
- grated cheese
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Toast circular pieces of bread.
2. Sprinkle with a thick layer of grated cheese seasoned with salt and cayenne.
3. Place on a tin sheet and bake until cheese is melted.
4. Serve at once.
