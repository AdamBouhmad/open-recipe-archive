## Ingredients

- one cupful of cooked veal or fowl meat, minced
- a handful of fine bread crumbs
- the yolks of four hard-boiled eggs
- a tablespoon of milk
- pepper
- salt
- a half teaspoon of flour
- two beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Mix the minced meat with a handful of fine bread crumbs, the yolks of four hard-boiled eggs rubbed smooth together with a tablespoon of milk.
2. Season with pepper and salt.
3. Add a half teaspoon of flour, and bind all together with two beaten eggs.
4. Well flour the hands, and make the mixture into little balls the size of a nutmeg.
5. Drop into the soup about twenty minutes before serving.
