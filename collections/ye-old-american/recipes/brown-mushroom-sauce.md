## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons butter
- Few drops onion juice
- 3½ tablespoons flour
- 1 cup cream
- ½ lb. mushrooms
- 1 teaspoon beef extract
- Salt
- Paprika

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add onion juice, and cook until slightly browned.
2. Add flour and continue the browning.
3. Pour on, gradually, while stirring constantly, the cream.
4. Clean mushrooms, peal caps, cut in slices lengthwise, and sauté in butter five minutes.
5. Break stems in pieces, cover with cold water, and cook slowly until liquor is reduced to one-third cup.
6. Strain and dissolve beef extract in mushroom liquor.
7. Add to sauce, and season with salt and paprika.
8. Just before serving, add sautéd caps.
