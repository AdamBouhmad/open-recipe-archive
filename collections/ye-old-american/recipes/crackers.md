## Ingredients

- one pint of flour
- one heaping teaspoonful of baking powder
- four tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- half a teaspoonful salt
- white of an egg beaten
- one cup of milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Sift into a pint of flour one heaping teaspoonful of baking powder, four tablespoonfuls of melted butter, half a teaspoonful salt and the white of an egg beaten and one cup of milk
2. Mix it with more flour, enough to make a very stiff dough, as stiff as can be rolled out
3. Pound and knead a long time
4. Roll very thin like pie crust and cut out either round or square
5. Bake a light brown
