## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla or 2 tablespoons wine
- 4 tablespoons cream or milk

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, and milk and flavoring drop by drop to prevent separation.
