## Ingredients

- one cup stewed and strained tomatoes
- one cup white stock
- six canned pimentoes
- one onion
- two cloves garlic
- one-fourth cup butter
- two teaspoons salt

## Directions

1. Mix stewed and strained tomatoes with white stock
2. Rub canned pimentoes through a sieve
3. Finely chop onion and garlic
4. Combine all ingredients together
