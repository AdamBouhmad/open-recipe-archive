## Ingredients

- fish
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- chopped onion
- vinegar
- water
- oil
- pounded mace
- whole black pepper
- green fennel or parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the fish in pieces the thickness of your hand
2. Wash it and dry it in a cloth
3. Sprinkle on some pepper and salt
4. Dredge it with flour
5. Fry it a nice brown
6. When it gets cold, put it in a pot with a little chopped onion between the layers
7. Take as much vinegar and water as will cover it
8. Mix with it some oil, pounded mace, and whole black pepper
9. Pour it on and stop the pot closely
10. When served up, garnish with green fennel or parsley
