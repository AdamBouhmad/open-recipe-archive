## Ingredients

- two pineapples
- two quarts of water
- a pint of sugar
- the juice of two lemons
- the beaten whites of four eggs

## Directions

1. Grate two pineapples and mix with two quarts of water and a pint of sugar
2. Add the juice of two lemons and the beaten whites of four eggs
3. Place in a freezer and freeze
