## Ingredients

- a sweetbread
- bay leaf
- a slice of onion
- a blade of mace
- cucumber
- one-half cup thick cream
- one-fourth tablespoon granulated gelatine
- one-half tablespoon cold water
- one and one-half tablespoons boiling water
- one and one-half tablespoons vinegar
- lettuce leaves
- French Dressing

## Directions

1. Parboil a sweetbread, adding to water a bit of bay leaf, a slice of onion, and a blade of mace.
2. Cool, and cut in small cubes; there should be three-fourths cup.
3. Add an equal quantity of cucumber cubes.
4. Beat one-half cup thick cream until stiff.
5. Add one-fourth tablespoon granulated gelatine soaked in one-half tablespoon cold water and dissolved in one and one-half tablespoons boiling water, then add one and one-half tablespoons vinegar.
6. Add sweetbread and cucumber, mould, and chill.
7. Arrange on lettuce leaves, and serve with French Dressing.
