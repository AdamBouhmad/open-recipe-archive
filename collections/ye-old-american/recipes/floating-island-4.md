## Ingredients

- One quart of milk
- Five eggs
- Five tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Scald the milk
2. Add the beaten yolks and one of the whites together with the sugar
3. Stir into them a little of the scalded milk to prevent curdling
4. Add all of the milk
5. Cook it the proper thickness
6. Remove from the fire and when cool, flavor
7. Pour it into a glass dish and let it become very cold
8. Beat up the remaining four whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
9. Beat into them three tablespoonfuls of sugar and two tablespoonfuls of currant jelly
10. Dip this over the top of the custard
