## Ingredients

- rich thick crust
- juicy, rather tart peaches
- sugar
- spices and flavoring to taste
- thick crust of rich puff paste

## Directions

1. Line a deep dish with rich thick crust
2. Pare and cut into halves or quarters peaches
3. Put in sugar, spices and flavoring to taste
4. Stew it slightly and put it in the lined dish
5. Cover with thick crust of rich puff paste and bake a rich brown
6. When done, break up the top crust into small pieces and stir it into the fruit
7. Serve hot or cold with plain rich cream or cream sauce, or with a rich brandy or wine
