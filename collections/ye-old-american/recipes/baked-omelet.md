## Ingredients

- four or six eggs
- a small cup of milk
- a tablespoonful of flour or cornstarch
- a teaspoonful of baking powder
- one-half teaspoonful of salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks of four or six eggs separately
2. Add to the yolks a small cup of milk, a tablespoonful of flour or cornstarch, a teaspoonful of baking powder, one-half teaspoonful of salt
3. Add lastly, the stiff-beaten whites
4. Bake in a well-buttered pie-tin or plate about half an hour in a steady oven
5. Serve the moment it is taken from the oven, as it is liable to fall
