## Ingredients

- 2 quarts cider vinegar
- 12 anchovies, washed, soaked, and pulled to pieces
- 12 small onions, peeled and minced
- 1 tablespoonful mace
- 3 tablespoonfuls fine salt
- 3 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 tablespoonful cloves
- 3 tablespoonfuls whole black pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls ground ginger
- 1 tablespoonful cayenne
- 1 quart mushrooms, minced, or 1 quart ripe tomatoes, sliced

## Directions

1. Put into a preserving kettle and boil slowly four hours, or until the mixture is reduced to one-half the original quantity
2. Strain through a flannel bag
3. Do not bottle until next day
4. Fill the flasks to the top, and dip the corks in beeswax and rosin
