## Ingredients

- cold boiled potatoes
- good butter
- one or two eggs

## Directions

1. Slice cold boiled potatoes and fry in good butter until brown
2. Beat up one or two eggs
3. Stir eggs into potatoes just as you dish them for the table
4. Do not leave them a moment on the fire after the eggs are in, for if they harden they are not half so nice
