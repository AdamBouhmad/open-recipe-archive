## Ingredients

- two slices chicken halibut, each weighing three-fourths pound
- salt
- lemon juice
- crumbs
- egg
- deep fat
- mushroom caps
- watercress
- radishes
- three tablespoons butter
- three tablespoons flour
- one cup Fish Stock
- one-half cup cream
- one tablespoon Sauterne
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wipe two slices chicken halibut, each weighing three-fourths pound
2. Cut one piece in eight fillets
3. Sprinkle with salt and lemon juice
4. Roll and fasten with small wooden skewers
5. Cook over boiling water
6. Cut remaining slice in pieces about the size and shape of scallops
7. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs
8. Fry in deep fat
9. For Mushroom Sauce: Melt three tablespoons butter
10. Add three tablespoons flour
11. Pour on gradually, while stirring constantly, one cup Fish Stock
12. When boiling-point is reached, add one-half cup cream, three mushroom caps sliced, and one tablespoon Sauterne
13. Season with salt and pepper
14. Cook mushroom caps in sauce until soft
15. Arrange a steamed fillet in centre of each fish-plate
16. Place on top of each a cooked mushroom cap
17. Put fried fish at both right and left of fillet
18. Serve with Mushroom Sauce
19. Garnish with watercress and radishes cut in fancy shapes
