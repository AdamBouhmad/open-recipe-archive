## Ingredients

- turnip rooted cabbage root
- water
- melted butter

## Directions

1. The cabbage growing at the top is not good; cut the root in slices an inch thick
2. Peel off the rind
3. Boil the slices in a large quantity of water, till tender
4. Serve it up hot, with melted butter poured over it
