## Ingredients

- two large or four small white catfish that have been caught in deep water
- a pound of lean bacon
- a large onion cut up
- a handful of parsley chopped small
- some pepper and salt
- a sufficient quantity of water

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads
2. and skin and clean the bodies
3. cut each in three parts
4. put them in a pot
5. stew them till the fish are quite tender but not broken
6. beat the yelks of four fresh eggs
7. add to them a large spoonful of butter
8. two of flour
9. and half a pint of rich milk
10. make all these warm and thicken the soup
11. take out the bacon
12. and put some of the fish in your tureen
13. pour in the soup
14. and serve it up
