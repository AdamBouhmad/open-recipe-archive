## Ingredients

- Three cups of sifted flour
- Three teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- One cup of finely chopped suet
- One teaspoonful of salt
- Sweet milk

## Directions

1. Sift baking powder into the flour
2. Rub the finely chopped suet well into the flour with the salt
3. Wet all with sweet milk to make a dough as stiff as biscuit
4. Make into small balls as large as peaches, well floured
5. Drop into the soup three-quarters of an hour before being served
6. Require steady boiling, being closely covered, and the cover not to be removed until taken up to serve
