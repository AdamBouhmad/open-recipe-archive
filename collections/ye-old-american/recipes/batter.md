## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- 1 cup bread flour
- ¾ cup milk

## Directions

1. Beat eggs until light, add salt and pepper.
2. Add milk slowly to flour, stir until smooth and well mixed.
3. Combine mixtures.
