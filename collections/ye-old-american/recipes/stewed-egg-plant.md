## Ingredients

- purple egg plants
- water
- grated bread
- powdered sweet marjoram
- butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put them whole into a pot with plenty of water, and simmer them till quite tender.
2. Then take them out, drain them, and (having peeled off the skins) cut them up, and mash them smooth in a deep dish.
3. Mix with them some grated bread, some powdered sweet marjoram, and a large piece of butter, adding a pounded nutmeg.
4. Grate a layer of bread over the top, and put the dish into the oven and brown it.
5. You must send it to table in the same dish.
