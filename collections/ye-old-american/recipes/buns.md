## Ingredients

- 1 cup scalded milk
- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅓ cup sugar
- 1 yeast cake dissolved in ¼ cup lukewarm water
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup raisins stoned and cut in quarters
- 1 teaspoon extract lemon
- Flour

## Directions

1. Add one-half sugar and salt to milk; when lukewarm, add dissolved yeast cake and one and one-half cups flour; cover, and let rise until light
2. Add butter, remaining sugar, raisins, lemon, and flour to make a dough; let rise, shape like biscuits, let rise again, and bake
3. If wanted glazed, brush over with beaten egg before baking
