## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. mutton from fore-quarter
- 2 qts. cold water
- ½ tablespoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- 2 slices turnip
- ½ onion
- ¼ cup flour
- ¼ cup carrot, cut in small cubes
- ¼ cup turnip, cut in small cubes
- 2 tablespoons pearl barley

## Directions

1. Wipe meat, remove skin and fat, and cut meat in small pieces.
2. Add water, heat gradually to boiling-point, skim, and cook slowly two hours.
3. After cooking one hour, add salt, pepper, turnip, and onion.
4. Strain, cool, remove fat, and reheat.
5. Thicken with flour diluted with enough cold water to pour easily.
6. Cook carrot and turnip dice in boiling salted water until soft; drain, and add to soup.
7. Soak barley over night in cold water, drain, and cook in boiling salted water until soft; drain, and add to soup.
