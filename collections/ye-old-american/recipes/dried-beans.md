## Ingredients

- Dried beans
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash them and lay them in soak over night
2. Early in the morning put them into a pot with plenty of water
3. Boil them slowly till dinner time, requiring seven or eight hours to be sufficiently done
4. Take them off and put them into a sieve, straining off the liquid
5. Send the beans to table in a deep dish, seasoned with pepper, and having a piece of butter mixed with them
