## Ingredients

- sardines
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- lettuce leaves
- Mayonnaise Dressing

## Directions

1. Remove skin and bones from sardines, and mix with an equal quantity of the mashed yolks of hard-boiled eggs.
2. Arrange in nests of lettuce leaves and serve with Mayonnaise Dressing.
