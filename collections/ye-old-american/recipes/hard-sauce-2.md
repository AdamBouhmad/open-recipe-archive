## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons brandy

## Directions

1. Cream butter; add gradually brown sugar and brandy, drop by drop.
2. Force through a pastry bag with rose tube, and garnish with green leaves and candied cherries.
