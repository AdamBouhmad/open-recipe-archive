## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- salt
- two eggs
- about a pint of flour
- rich milk or cream
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well
2. Add the flour and enough milk to make it smooth
3. Butter the saucepan and put in the remainder of the milk well salted
4. When it boils, stir in the flour, eggs, etc., lightly
5. Let it cook well until the consistency of thick corn mush
6. Serve immediately with simple sauce of rich milk or cream sweetened to taste and flavored with grated nutmeg
