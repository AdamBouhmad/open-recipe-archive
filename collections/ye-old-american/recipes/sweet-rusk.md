## Ingredients

- 1 pint warm milk
- ½ cup of butter
- 1 cup of sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls yeast
- Flour

## Directions

1. Make a sponge with the milk, yeast, and enough flour for a thin batter
2. Let it rise over night
3. In the morning add the butter, eggs, and sugar, previously beaten up well together, the salt, and flour enough to make a soft dough
4. Mould with the hands into balls of uniform size
5. Set close together in a pan
6. Let them rise until very light
7. After baking, wash the tops with a clean soft cloth dipped in molasses and water
