## Ingredients

- tarragon vinegar
- sweet oil
- garlic vinegar
- mustard powder

## Directions

1. Mix tarragon vinegar with an equal portion of sweet oil
2. Add a few drops of garlic vinegar
3. Stir in mustard powder till sufficiently thick
