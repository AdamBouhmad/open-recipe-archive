## Ingredients

- one-half tablespoon finely chopped onion
- three tablespoons butter
- one-third cup chopped mushroom caps
- two tablespoons flour
- two-thirds cup cream
- one cup finnan haddie
- two tablespoons grated cheese
- yolks two eggs
- salt
- cayenne
- circular pieces of toasted bread
- buttered, soft bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Fry one-half tablespoon finely chopped onion, three tablespoons butter, and one-third cup chopped mushroom caps five minutes.
2. Add two tablespoons flour, and two-thirds cup cream.
3. Cook until mixture thickens.
4. Add one cup finnan haddie (soaked in lukewarm water to cover forty-five minutes, then separated into flakes), two tablespoons grated cheese, and yolks two eggs slightly beaten.
5. Season with salt and cayenne and pile on circular pieces of toasted bread.
6. Sprinkle with grated cheese, then with buttered, soft bread crumbs.
7. Bake until crumbs are browned.
8. Serve at once.
