## Ingredients

- Two cups of sugar
- one cup of butter
- five cupfuls of flour
- five eggs
- one small teacupful of milk
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- a little cinnamon
- nutmeg
- allspice
- ground cloves
- one-quarter of a pound of currants

## Directions

1. Cream the butter
2. add the sugar
3. cream again
4. then add yolks of eggs
5. the milk
6. beaten whites and flour
7. add cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice and ground cloves and currants rolled in flour
