## Ingredients

- half a pound of cold boiled tripe
- two ounces of butter
- a tablespoonful of chopped onion
- a teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- strong vinegar
- salt
- cayenne
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cut up half a pound of cold boiled tripe into neat squares
2. Put two ounces of butter and a tablespoonful of chopped onion in a frying pan and fry to a delicate brown
3. Add to the tripe a teaspoonful of chopped parsley and a little strong vinegar, salt and cayenne
4. Stir the pan to prevent burning
5. Cover the bottom of a platter with tomato sauce, add the contents of the pan and serve
