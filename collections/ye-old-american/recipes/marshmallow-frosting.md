## Ingredients

- one cup white fondant
- white of one egg
- one-fourth teaspoon water white vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt one cup white fondant; add the white of one egg beaten until stiff, and stir over the fire two minutes. Remove from range, and beat until of right consistency to spread. Flavor with one-fourth teaspoon water white vanilla. This is a most delicious frosting for chocolate cake, but will never spread perfectly smooth.
