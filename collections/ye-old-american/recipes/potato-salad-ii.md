## Ingredients

- two cups cold boiled riced potatoes
- one cup pecan nut meats
- French Dressing
- watercress

## Directions

1. Mix two cups cold boiled riced potatoes and one cup pecan nut meats broken in pieces.
2. Marinate with French Dressing, and arrange on a bed of watercress.
