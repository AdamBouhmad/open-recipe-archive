## Ingredients

- Warm steamed rice
- Cold boiled salmon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Egg Sauce II

## Directions

1. Line a bread pan, slightly buttered, with warm steamed rice
2. Fill the centre with cold boiled salmon, flaked, and seasoned with salt, pepper, and a slight grating of nutmeg
3. Cover with rice and steam one hour
4. Turn on a hot platter for serving, and pour around Egg Sauce II
