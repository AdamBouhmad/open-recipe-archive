## Ingredients

- Venison steak
- Salt
- Pepper
- Grease for broiler
- One-half onion
- Six slices carrot, cut in small pieces
- Two tablespoons butter
- Three tablespoons flour
- One and one-half cups Brown Stock
- A sprig of parsley
- A bit of bay leaf
- Eight peppercorns
- One teaspoon salt
- Three tablespoons Madeira wine
- One cup boiled French chestnuts
- One tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Wipe steak, sprinkle with salt and pepper, place on a greased broiler, and broil five minutes.
2. Fry onion and carrot in two tablespoons butter for five minutes.
3. Add flour and stir until well browned.
4. Add Brown Stock, parsley, bay leaf, peppercorns, and salt.
5. Let simmer twenty minutes and strain.
6. Add Madeira wine, boiled French chestnuts, and one tablespoon butter.
7. Pour sauce over steak on hot platter.
