## Ingredients

- one gallon of choice ripe tomatoes
- a small level teaspoonful of cayenne pepper
- one tablespoonful of mustard seed
- half a tablespoonful of whole cloves
- one tablespoonful of whole allspice
- one heaping tablespoonful of sugar
- one teacupful of best vinegar
- salt to suit the taste

## Directions

1. Cook one gallon of choice ripe tomatoes; strain them, and cook again until they become quite thick.
2. About fifteen minutes before taking up put into them a small level teaspoonful of cayenne pepper, one tablespoonful of mustard seed, half a tablespoonful of whole cloves, one tablespoonful of whole allspice, all tied in a thin muslin bag.
3. At the same time, add one heaping tablespoonful of sugar, and one teacupful of best vinegar and salt to suit the taste.
4. Seal up air-tight, either in bottles or jugs.
