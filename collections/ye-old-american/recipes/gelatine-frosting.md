## Ingredients

- one teaspoonful of gelatine
- one tablespoonful of cold water
- two tablespoonfuls of hot water
- one cup of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Soak one teaspoonful of gelatine in one tablespoonful of cold water for half an hour
2. Dissolve in two tablespoonfuls of hot water
3. Add one cup of powdered sugar and stir until smooth
