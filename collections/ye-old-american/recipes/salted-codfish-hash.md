## Ingredients

- fish and potatoes (prepared as for Fish Balls)
- fat salt pork

## Directions

1. Prepare as for Fish Balls, omitting egg.
2. Try out fat salt pork, remove scraps, leaving enough fat in pan to moisten fish and potatoes.
3. Put in fish and potatoes, stir until heated, then cook until well browned underneath; fold, and turn like an omelet.
