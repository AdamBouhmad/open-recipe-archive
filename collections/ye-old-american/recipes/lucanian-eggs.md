## Ingredients

- 5 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 cup cooked macaroni
- ½ cup grated cheese
- Essence Anchovy
- 1¾ cups White Sauce I
- Salt and paprika
- Onion juice
- ¾ cup buttered crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut eggs in eighths lengthwise, add macaroni, white sauce, and seasonings.
2. Arrange in buttered baking dish, cover with buttered crumbs, and bake until crumbs are brown.
