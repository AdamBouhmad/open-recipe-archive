## Ingredients

- Leg of pork
- salt
- water
- carrots
- turnips
- parsnips

## Directions

1. Choose a small, compact, well-filled leg
2. Rub it well with salt
3. Let it remain in pickle for a week or ten days, turning and rubbing it every day
4. An hour before dressing it put it into cold water for an hour, which improves the color
5. If the pork is purchased ready salted, ascertain how long the meat has been in pickle and soak it accordingly
6. Put it into a boiling-pot, with sufficient cold water to cover it
7. Let it gradually come to a boil, and remove the scum as it rises
8. Simmer it very gently until tender, and do not allow it to boil fast
9. Carrots, turnips or parsnips may be boiled with the pork, some of which should be laid around the dish as a garnish
