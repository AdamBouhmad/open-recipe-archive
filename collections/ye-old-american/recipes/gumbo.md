## Ingredients

- young tender ochras
- ripe tomatas
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- onion

## Directions

1. Take an equal quantity of young tender ochras and of ripe tomatas, for instance a quarter of a peck of each
2. Chop the ochras fine and scald and peel the tomatas
3. Put them into a stew-pan without any water
4. Add a lump of butter, a very little salt and pepper, and if you choose, an onion minced fine
5. Let it stew steadily for an hour
6. Strain it and send it to table as soup in a tureen
7. Eat dry toast with it
