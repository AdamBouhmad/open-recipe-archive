## Ingredients

- green corn
- sugar (if not entirely fresh)
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Strip off the husks, pick out all the silk and put it in boiling water.
2. If not entirely fresh, add a tablespoonful of sugar to the water, but no salt.
3. Boil twenty minutes, fast, and serve; or you may cut it from the cob, put in plenty of butter and a little salt, and serve in a covered vegetable dish.
