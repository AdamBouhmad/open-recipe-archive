## Ingredients

- one-fourth cup lobster meat per cocktail
- two tablespoons tomato catsup
- two tablespoons Sherry wine
- one tablespoon lemon juice
- six drops Tabasco Sauce
- one-eighth teaspoon finely chopped chives
- salt

## Directions

1. Allow one-fourth cup lobster meat, cut in pieces, for each cocktail.
2. Season with two tablespoons, each, tomato catsup and Sherry wine, one tablespoon lemon juice, six drops Tabasco Sauce, one-eighth teaspoon finely chopped chives, and salt to taste.
3. Chill thoroughly, and serve in cocktail glasses.
