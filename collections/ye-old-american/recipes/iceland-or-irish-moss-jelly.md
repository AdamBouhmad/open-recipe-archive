## Ingredients

- 1 handful moss, washed in five waters, and soaked an hour
- 1 quart boiling water
- 2 lemons (juice only)
- 1 glass of wine
- ¼ teaspoonful cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak the washed moss in a very little cold water.
2. Stir into the boiling water, and simmer until it is dissolved.
3. Sweeten and flavor.
4. Strain into moulds.
5. You may use two glasses of cider instead of one of wine for a fever-patient, putting in a little less water.
