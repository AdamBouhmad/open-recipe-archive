## Ingredients

- pork
- salt
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Wipe pork, sprinkle with salt and pepper, place on a rack in a dripping-pan, and dredge meat and bottom of pan with flour.
2. Bake in a moderate oven three or four hours, basting every fifteen minutes with fat in pan.
3. Make a gravy as for other roasts.
