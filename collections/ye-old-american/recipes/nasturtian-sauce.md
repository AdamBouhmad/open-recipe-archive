## Ingredients

- butter
- water
- flour
- pickled nasturtian seeds

## Directions

1. Cut about six ounces of butter into small bits and put them into a small sauce-pan
2. Mix with a wine-glass of water, sufficient flour to make a thick batter, and pour it on the butter
3. Hold the sauce-pan over hot coals, shaking it quickly round, till the butter is melted
4. Let it just boil up, then take it from the fire
5. Thicken it with the pickled nasturtians and send it to table in a boat
