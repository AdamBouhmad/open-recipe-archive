## Ingredients

- one cupful of sour cream
- one cupful of sour milk
- three eggs
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one level teaspoonful of soda
- a spoonful of water
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix one cupful of sour cream and one cupful of sour milk with three eggs, well beaten, whites and yolks separately.
2. Add one teaspoonful of salt and one level teaspoonful of soda, dissolved in a spoonful of water, and enough flour to make a very stiff batter.
3. Drop by spoonfuls into the broth and boil twenty minutes, or until no raw dough shows on the outside.
