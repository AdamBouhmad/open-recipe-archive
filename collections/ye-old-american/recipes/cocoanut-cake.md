## Ingredients

- 2 cups powdered sugar
- ½ cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls cream-tartar
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 1 grated cocoanut
- Whites of three eggs
- 1 cup of powdered sugar
- Four tablespoonfuls powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Bake as for jelly-cake.
2. To one half of the grated cocoanut add whites of three eggs, beaten to a froth, and one cup of powdered sugar.
3. Lay this between the layers.
4. Mix with the other half of the grated cocoanut four tablespoonfuls powdered sugar, and strew thickly on top of cake.
