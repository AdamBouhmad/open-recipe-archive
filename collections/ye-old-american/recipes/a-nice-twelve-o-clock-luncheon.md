## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- anchovies
- grated cheese
- chopped parsley
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut some slices of bread tolerably thick
2. Toast them slightly
3. Bone some anchovies
4. Lay half of one on each toast
5. Cover it well with grated cheese and chopped parsley mixed
6. Pour a little melted butter on
7. Brown it with a salamander
8. Serve on the dish you send it to table in
