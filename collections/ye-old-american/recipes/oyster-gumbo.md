## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- 4 cups Fish Stock
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- ½ can okra
- ⅓ can tomatoes
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean, pick over, and parboil oysters; drain, and add oyster liquor to Fish Stock
2. Cook onion five minutes in one-half the butter; add to stock
3. Then add okra, tomatoes heated and drained from some of their liquor, oysters, and remaining butter
4. Season with salt and pepper
