## Ingredients

- slices of stale baker's bread
- sugar
- a very little salt
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Pare some slices of stale baker's bread
2. Toast nicely, without burning
3. Pile in a bowl, sprinkling sugar and a very little salt between
4. Cover well with boiling water
5. Set with a tight lid upon the top in a pan of boiling water
6. Simmer gently, until the contents of the bowl are like jelly
7. Eat warm with powdered sugar and nutmeg
