## Ingredients

- fawn
- bread-crumbs
- chopped pork
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- juice of a lemon
- water
- cream
- beaten egg
- melted butter
- thin slices of fat pork
- flour
- curled parsley
- red currant jelly

## Directions

1. Clean and wash thoroughly.
2. Stuff with a good force-meat made of bread-crumbs, chopped pork, pepper and salt, a little grated nutmeg, the juice of a lemon, moistened with water and cream, bound with beaten egg and melted butter.
3. Sew up the fawn, turning the legs under, and binding close to the body.
4. Cover with thin slices of fat pork, bound on with pack-thread, crossing in every direction, and roast at a quick fire.
5. Allow twenty-two minutes to a pound.
6. Twenty minutes before it is dished, remove the pork, and set down the fawn to brown, basting with melted butter.
7. Dredge with flour, let this brown, froth with butter, and serve.
8. Garnish with abundance of curled parsley, dotted with drops of red currant jelly.
