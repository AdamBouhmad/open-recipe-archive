## Ingredients

- half a pound of sweet potatoes
- half a pound of butter
- a quarter of a pound and two ounces of powdered sugar
- a grated nutmeg
- a large tea-spoonful of powdered cinnamon
- half a tea-spoonful of beaten mace
- the juice and grated peel of a lemon
- a wine glass of rose water
- a glass of wine
- a glass of brandy
- eight eggs

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of sweet potatoes, wash them, and put them into a pot with a very little water, barely enough to keep them from burning
2. Let them simmer slowly for about half an hour; they must be only parboiled, otherwise they will be soft, and may make the pudding heavy
3. When they are half done, take them out, peel them, and when cold, grate them
4. Stir together to a cream, half a pound of butter and a quarter of a pound and two ounces of powdered sugar, add a grated nutmeg, a large tea-spoonful of powdered cinnamon, and half a tea-spoonful of beaten mace
5. Also add the juice and grated peel of a lemon, a wine glass of rose water, a glass of wine, and a glass of brandy
6. Stir these ingredients well together
7. Beat eight eggs very light, and stir them into the mixture in turn with the sweet potato, a little at a time of each
8. Having stirred the whole very hard at the last, put it into a buttered dish and bake it three quarters of an hour
9. Eat it cold
