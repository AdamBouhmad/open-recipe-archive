## Ingredients

- ½ lb. mushrooms
- 4 cups White Stock III
- ¼ cup pearl sago
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 cup heavy cream
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and chop mushrooms, and add to stock
2. Cook twenty minutes and rub through a sieve
3. Cook sago in boiling water thirty minutes, add to stock
4. As soon as boiling-point is reached, season with salt and pepper
5. Then add cream and yolks of eggs
