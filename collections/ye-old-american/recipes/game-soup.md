## Ingredients

- Two grouse or partridges, or a pair of rabbits
- half a pound of lean ham
- two medium-sized onions
- one pound of lean beef
- fried bread
- butter for frying
- pepper
- salt
- two stalks of white celery cut into inch lengths
- three quarts of water

## Directions

1. Joint your game neatly; cut the ham and onions into small pieces, fry all in butter to a light brown
2. Put into a soup-pot with the beef, cut into strips, add a little pepper
3. Pour on the water; heat slowly, and stew gently two hours
4. Take out the pieces of bird, and cover in a bowl
5. Cook the soup an hour longer; strain; cool
6. Drop in the celery and simmer ten minutes
7. Pour upon fried bread in the tureen
