## Ingredients

- two ounces of isinglass
- a pint of water
- half a pint of white wine
- grated peel and juice of two large deep-coloured oranges
- half a pound of loaf-sugar
- yolks of eight eggs

## Directions

1. Put two ounces of isinglass into a pint of water, and boil it till it has dissolved.
2. Strain it into a porcelain skillet, and add to it half a pint of white wine; the grated peel and juice of two large deep-coloured oranges; half a pound of loaf-sugar; and the yolks only of eight eggs that have been well beaten.
3. Mix the whole thoroughly; place it on hot coals and simmer it, stirring it all the time till it boils hard.
4. Take it off directly, strain it, and put it into moulds to congeal.
