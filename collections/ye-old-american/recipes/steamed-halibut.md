## Ingredients

- three-pound piece of white halibut
- cloth
- parsley
- egg sauce

## Directions

1. Select a three-pound piece of white halibut
2. Cover it with a cloth and place it in a steamer
3. Set the steamer over a pot of fast-boiling water and steam two hours
4. Place it on a hot dish surrounded with a border of parsley
5. Serve with egg sauce
