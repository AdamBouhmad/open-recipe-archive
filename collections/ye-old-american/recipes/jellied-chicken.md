## Ingredients

- one four-pound fowl
- two slices onion
- boiling water
- one-half tablespoon salt
- salt and pepper
- parsley
- hard-boiled eggs
- one teaspoon dissolved granulated gelatine

## Directions

1. Dress, clean, and cut up a four-pound fowl.
2. Put in a stewpan with two slices onion and cover with boiling water.
3. Cook slowly until meat falls from bones.
4. When half cooked, add one-half tablespoon salt.
5. Remove chicken; reduce stock to three-fourths cup, strain, and skim off fat.
6. Decorate bottom of a mould with parsley and slices of hard-boiled eggs.
7. Pack in meat freed from skin and bone and sprinkled with salt and pepper.
8. Pour on stock and place mould under heavy weight.
9. Keep in a cold place until firm.
10. In summer add one teaspoon dissolved granulated gelatine to stock.
