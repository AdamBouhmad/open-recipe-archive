## Ingredients

- plump, good-sized oysters
- oyster juice
- milk
- salt
- eggs
- flour
- butter
- lard

## Directions

1. Select plump, good-sized oysters and drain off the juice
2. To a cup of oyster juice add a cup of milk, a little salt, four well-beaten eggs, and flour enough to make batter like griddle-cakes
3. Envelope an oyster in a spoonful of this batter, or some cut them in halves or chop them fine
4. Fry in butter and lard mixed in a frying pan the same as we fry eggs, turning to fry brown on both sides
5. Send to the table very hot
