## Ingredients

- two cups of sugar
- one cup of butter
- one cup of milk
- three cups and a half of flour
- four eggs
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- large spoon cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Stir butter and sugar together
2. Add the beaten yolks of the eggs, then the milk, then flavoring and the whites
3. Put cream of tartar in flour and add last
4. Bake in buttered gem-pans, or drop the batter, a teaspoonful at a time, in rows on flat buttered tins
