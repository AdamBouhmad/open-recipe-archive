## Ingredients

- 1 heaping cup of fine dry bread-crumbs
- ½ lb. pared and chopped apples
- ½ lb. raisins, seeded and chopped
- 6 oz. currants, washed and dried
- 6 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful allspice
- 1 glass brandy
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- ½ lb. suet, chopped to powder

## Directions

1. Work the sugar into the beaten yolks; then the suet and crumbs, with the chopped apples; next the brandy and spice, then the whipped whites; lastly the fruit, well dredged with flour.
2. Boil in a buttered bowl or mould three hours.
3. Eat hot with sauce.
