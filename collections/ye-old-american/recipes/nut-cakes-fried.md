## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one ounce of sifted sugar
- two ounces of warmed butter
- two tablespoonfuls of yeast
- a teacupful of luke-warm milk
- a little salt
- one pound of flour

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs well
2. Add to them one ounce of sifted sugar, two ounces of warmed butter, two tablespoonfuls of yeast, a teacupful of luke-warm milk and a little salt
3. Whip all well together
4. Stir in by degrees one pound of flour, and, if requisite, more milk, making thin dough
5. Beat it until it falls from the spoon
6. Set it to rise
7. When it has risen make butter or lard hot in a frying pan
8. Cut from the light dough little pieces the size of a walnut
9. Without molding or kneading, fry them pale brown
10. As they are done lay them on a napkin to absorb any of the fat
