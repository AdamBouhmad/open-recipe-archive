## Ingredients

- two ounces of good cocoa
- one quart of water
- dry toast

## Directions

1. Put into a sauce-pan two ounces of good cocoa (the chocolate nut before it is ground) and one quart of water.
2. Cover it, and as soon as it has come to a boil, set it on coals by the side of the fire, to simmer for an hour or more.
3. Take it hot with dry toast.
