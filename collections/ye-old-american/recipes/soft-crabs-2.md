## Ingredients

- Soft crabs
- Hot lard or butter
- Salt
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Do not keep the crabs over night, as the shells harden in twenty-four hours
2. Pull off the spongy substance from the sides and the sand bags
3. Wash well and wipe dry
4. Have ready a pan of seething hot lard or butter
5. Fry them to a fine brown
6. Put a little salt into the lard
7. Send up hot, garnished with parsley
