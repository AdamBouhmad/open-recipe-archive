## Ingredients

- one egg thoroughly beaten
- one cup of sweet milk
- a little salt
- light bread
- butter

## Directions

1. To one egg thoroughly beaten, put one cup of sweet milk and a little salt
2. Slice light bread and dip into the mixture, allowing each slice to absorb some of the milk
3. Brown on a hot buttered griddle or thick-bottomed frying pan
4. Spread with butter and serve hot
