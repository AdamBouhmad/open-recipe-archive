## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pints of orange juice
- 1/2 pint of clear or filtered water
- 1/2 pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- yellow rind of 6 deep-coloured oranges

## Directions

1. Mix orange juice with filtered water.
2. Stir in half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar.
3. Pare very thin the yellow rind of six deep-coloured oranges, cut in pieces, and lay it at the bottom of a bowl or tureen.
4. Pour the orange juice and sugar upon it; cover it, and let it infuse an hour.
5. Strain the liquid into a freezer, and proceed as for ice cream.
6. When it is frozen, put it into a mould, (it will look best in the form of a pine-apple,) and freeze it a second time.
7. Serve it in glass cups, with any sort of very nice sweet cakes.
