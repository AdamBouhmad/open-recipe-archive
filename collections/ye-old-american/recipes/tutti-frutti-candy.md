## Ingredients

- maple fondant
- English walnut meat
- fondant colored pink
- rose flavoring
- candied cherries
- figs
- white fondant
- vanilla
- nuts
- candied pineapple

## Directions

1. Fill an oiled border-mould with three layers of melted fondant.
2. Have bottom layer maple, well mixed with English walnut meat.
3. Have second layer colored pink, flavored with rose, and mixed with candied cherries cut in quarters and figs finely chopped.
4. Have third layer white, flavored with vanilla, mixed with nuts, candied cherries cut in quarters, and candied pineapple cut in small pieces.
5. Cover mould with oiled paper, and let stand over night.
6. Remove from mould, and place on a plate covered with a lace paper napkin.
7. Fill centre with Bonbons and Glacé Nuts.
