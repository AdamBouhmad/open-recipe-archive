## Ingredients

- dates
- castanea nuts, English walnuts, or blanched almonds
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Make a cut the entire length of dates and remove stones.
2. Fill cavities with castanea nuts, English walnuts, or blanched almonds, and shape in original form.
3. Roll in granulated sugar.
4. Pile in rows on a small plate covered with a doily.
5. If castanea nuts are used, with a sharp knife cut off the brown skin which lies next to shell.
