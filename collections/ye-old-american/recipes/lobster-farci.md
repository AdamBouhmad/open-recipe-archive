## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped lobster meat
- yolks 2 hard-boiled eggs
- ½ tablespoon chopped parsley
- 1 cup White Sauce I
- slight grating nutmeg
- ⅓ cup buttered crumbs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. To lobster meat add yolks of eggs rubbed to a paste, parsley, sauce, and seasonings to taste.
2. Fill lobster shells, cover with buttered crumbs, and bake until crumbs are brown.
