## Ingredients

- 3 cups stewed apple
- Nearly a cup white sugar
- 6 eggs
- 1 quart milk
- Nutmeg
- Crust

## Directions

1. Make the stewed apple very sweet, and let it cool
2. Beat the eggs light
3. Mix the yolks well with the apple, seasoning with nutmeg only
4. Stir in gradually the milk, beating as you go on
5. Add the whites
6. Fill your crust and bake without cover
