## Ingredients

- one rabbit
- boiling water
- beaten egg
- fine bread crumbs
- salt and pepper
- butter and sweet lard
- a spoonful of flour
- a cup of milk or cream
- onion sauce
- sliced lemon

## Directions

1. Thoroughly clean and wash the rabbit
2. Put it into boiling water and let it boil ten minutes
3. Drain it and when cold, cut it into joints
4. Dip into beaten egg and then in fine bread crumbs
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Fry them in butter and sweet lard mixed over a moderate fire until brown on both sides
7. Take them out and thicken the gravy with a spoonful of flour
8. Turn in a cup of milk or cream and let all boil up
9. Turn over the rabbits and serve hot with onion sauce
10. Garnish with sliced lemon
