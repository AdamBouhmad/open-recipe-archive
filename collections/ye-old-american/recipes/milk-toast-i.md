## Ingredients

- 1 pint scalded milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2½ tablespoons bread flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- cold water
- 6 slices dry toast

## Directions

1. Add cold water gradually to flour to make a smooth, thin paste.
2. Add to milk, stirring constantly until thickened, cover, and cook twenty minutes.
3. Then add salt and butter in small pieces.
4. Dip slices of toast separately in sauce; when soft, remove to serving dish.
5. Pour remaining sauce over all.
