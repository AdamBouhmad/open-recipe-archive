## Ingredients

- One pint of hominy
- Two and one-half pints of boiling water
- One-half pint of sweet milk
- A little salt

## Directions

1. Put to soak one pint of hominy in two and one-half pints of boiling water over night in a tin vessel with a tight cover
2. In the morning add one-half pint of sweet milk and a little salt
3. Place the tin vessel containing the hominy in a kettle of boiling water on a brisk fire
4. Let boil one-half hour
