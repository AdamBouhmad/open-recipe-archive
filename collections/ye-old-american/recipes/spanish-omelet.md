## Ingredients

- French Omelet
- Tomato Sauce
- butter
- onion
- tomatoes
- mushrooms
- capers
- salt
- cayenne
- red or green pepper

## Directions

1. Mix and cook a French Omelet.
2. Serve with Tomato Sauce in the centre and around omelet.
3. For Tomato Sauce: Cook two tablespoons of butter with one tablespoon of finely chopped onion, until yellow.
4. Add one and three-fourths cups tomatoes, and cook until moisture has nearly evaporated.
5. Add one tablespoon sliced mushrooms, one tablespoon capers, one-fourth teaspoon salt, and a few grains cayenne.
6. This is improved by a small piece of red or green pepper, finely chopped, cooked with butter and onion.
