## Ingredients

- turnip tops
- salt
- water
- bacon

## Directions

1. Put them in cold water an hour before they are dressed
2. The more water they are boiled in, the better they will look
3. When the water boils, put in a small handful of salt
4. Then put in your vegetables
5. They are still better boiled with bacon in the Virginia style
6. If fresh and young, they will be done in about twenty minutes
7. Drain them on the back of a sieve
8. Put them under the bacon
