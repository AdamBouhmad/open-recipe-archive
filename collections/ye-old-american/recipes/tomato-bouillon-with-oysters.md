## Ingredients

- 1 can tomatoes
- 1½ quarts bouillon
- 1 tablespoon chopped onion
- ½ bay leaf
- 6 cloves
- ½ teaspoon celery seed
- ½ teaspoon peppercorns
- 1 pint oysters

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients except oysters, and boil twenty minutes.
2. Strain, cool, and clear.
3. Add parboiled oysters, and serve in bouillon cups with small croûtons.
