## Ingredients

- Salt codfish
- Lukewarm water
- Cold water
- Yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- Butter
- Salt
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Put the fish to soak over night in lukewarm water—as early as eight o'clock in the evening
2. Change this for more warm water at bed-time and cover closely
3. Change again in the morning and wash off the salt
4. Two hours before dinner plunge into very cold water
5. Set over the fire with enough lukewarm water to cover it, and boil for half an hour
6. Drain well and lay it on a hot dish
7. Pour over it egg-sauce prepared by rubbing the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs to a paste with butter, and prepare as the foregoing receipt
