## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- two pounds of flour
- a large spoonful of pounded coriander seeds
- three quarters of a pound of melted butter
- six eggs
- a gill of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix a pound of sugar, with two pounds of flour, and a large spoonful of pounded coriander seeds
2. sift them
3. add three quarters of a pound of melted butter, six eggs, and a gill of brandy
4. knead it well
5. roll it thin
6. cut it in shapes
7. bake without discolouring it
