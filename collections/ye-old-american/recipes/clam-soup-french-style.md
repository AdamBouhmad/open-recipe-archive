## Ingredients

- Two dozen hard shell clams
- Half a minced onion
- One ounce of butter
- One pint of hot water
- A pinch of mace
- Four cloves
- One allspice
- Six whole pepper corns
- One pint of clam-juice or hot water
- Three half-pints of milk
- One teaspoonful of flour
- Cold milk
- Fried bread (croutons)

## Directions

1. Mince two dozen hard shell clams very fine
2. Fry half a minced onion in an ounce of butter
3. Add to it a pint of hot water, a pinch of mace, four cloves, one allspice and six whole pepper corns
4. Boil fifteen minutes and strain into a saucepan
5. Add the chopped clams and a pint of clam-juice or hot water
6. Simmer slowly two hours
7. Strain and rub the pulp through a sieve into the liquid
8. Return it to the saucepan and keep it lukewarm
9. Boil three half-pints of milk in a saucepan (previously wet with cold water)
10. Whisk it into the soup
11. Dissolve a teaspoonful of flour in cold milk and add it to the soup
12. Taste for seasoning
13. Heat it gently to near the boiling point
14. Pour into a tureen previously heated with hot water
15. Serve with or without pieces of fried bread
