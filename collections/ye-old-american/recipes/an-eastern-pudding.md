## Ingredients

- a pound of flour
- half a pound of minced suet
- marmalade or cold stewed fruit (apple, peach, plum, gooseberry or cranberry)

## Directions

1. Make a paste of a pound of flour and half a pound of minced suet
2. Roll it out thin into a square or oblong sheet
3. Trim off the edges so as to make it an even shape
4. Spread thickly over it some marmalade, or cold stewed fruit, which must be made very sweet
5. Roll up the paste, with the fruit spread on it, into a scroll
6. Secure each end by putting on nicely a thin round piece rolled out from the trimmings that you cut off the edges of the sheet
7. Put the pudding into a cloth, and boil it at least three hours
8. Serve it up hot, and eat it with cream sauce, or with butter and sugar
