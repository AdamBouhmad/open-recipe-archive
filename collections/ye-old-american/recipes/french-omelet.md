## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 tablespoons milk
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Beat eggs slightly, just enough to blend yolks and whites.
2. Add the milk and seasonings.
3. Put butter in hot omelet pan; when melted, turn in the mixture.
4. As it cooks, prick and pick up with a fork until the whole is of creamy consistency.
5. Place on hotter part of range that it may brown quickly underneath.
6. Fold, and turn on hot platter.
