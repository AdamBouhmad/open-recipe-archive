## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. lean beef cut from round
- grated rind ½ lemon
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 1 egg
- ½ teaspoon onion juice
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- few gratings nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- buttered paper
- one-fourth cup butter
- one cup boiling water
- Brown Mushroom Sauce I

## Directions

1. Chop meat finely
2. Add remaining ingredients in order given
3. Shape in a roll six inches long
4. Wrap in buttered paper
5. Place on rack in dripping-pan
6. Bake thirty minutes
7. Baste every five minutes with one-fourth cup butter melted in one cup boiling water
8. Serve with Brown Mushroom Sauce I
