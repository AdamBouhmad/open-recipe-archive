## Ingredients

- a quart of milk
- arrow root
- six eggs
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of pounded sugar
- half a nutmeg
- grated lemon peel
- paste
- sugar
- slips of citron

## Directions

1. Boil a quart of milk, and make it into a thick batter, with arrow root
2. Add six eggs, half a pound of butter, the same of pounded sugar, half a nutmeg, and a little grated lemon peel
3. Put a paste in the dish, and bake it nicely
4. When done, sift sugar over it, and stick slips of citron all over the top
