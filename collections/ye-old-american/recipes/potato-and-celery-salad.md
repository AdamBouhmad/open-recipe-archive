## Ingredients

- two cups boiled potatoes
- one-half cup finely cut celery
- medium-sized apple
- French Dressing
- bright red apple

## Directions

1. To two cups boiled potatoes cut in one-half inch cubes add one-half cup finely cut celery and a medium-sized apple, pared, cut in eighths, then eighths cut in thin slices.
2. Marinate with French Dressing.
3. Arrange in a mound and garnish with celery tip and sections of bright red apple.
