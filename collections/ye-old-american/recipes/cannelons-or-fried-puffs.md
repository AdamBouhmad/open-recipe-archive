## Ingredients

- Half a pound of puff paste
- Apricot or any kind of preserve
- Hot lard

## Directions

1. Make some good puff paste and roll it out very thin
2. Cut it into pieces of an equal size, about two inches wide and eight inches long
3. Place upon each piece a spoonful of jam
4. Wet the edges with the white of egg and fold the paste over twice
5. Slightly press the edges together that the jam may not escape in frying
6. Fry them in boiling lard until of a nice brown
7. Let them remain by the side of the fire after they are colored that the paste may be thoroughly done
8. Drain them before the fire
9. Dish on a d'oyley
10. Sprinkle over them sifted sugar and serve
