## Ingredients

- 1 lb. mealy sweet potatoes
- ½ cup butter
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 1 tablespoonful cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg
- 4 eggs—whites and yolks beaten separately
- 1 cup of milk
- 1 lemon, juice and rind
- 1 glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Parboil the potatoes, and grate them when quite cold.
2. Cream the butter and sugar; add the yolk, the spice, and lemon; beat the potato in by degrees and until all is light; then the milk, then the brandy, and stir in the whites.
3. Bake in dishes lined with good paste—without cover.
4. You may make a pudding of this by baking in a deep dish—well buttered, without paste.
5. Cool before eating.
