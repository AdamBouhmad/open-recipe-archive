## Ingredients

- Calf's head
- Boiling water
- Two tablespoons butter
- Two tablespoons flour
- One cup White Stock
- One-half teaspoon salt
- One-eighth teaspoon pepper
- Few grains cayenne
- One-half cup cream
- Yolks of two eggs slightly beaten
- Two tablespoons Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Wash and clean a calf's head
2. Cook until tender in boiling water to cover
3. Cool and cut meat from cheek in small cubes
4. To two cups meat dice add one cup sauce made of two tablespoons butter, two tablespoons flour, and one cup White Stock, seasoned with one-half teaspoon salt, one-eighth teaspoon pepper, and a few grains cayenne
5. Add one-half cup cream and yolks of two eggs slightly beaten
6. Cook two minutes and add two tablespoons Madeira wine
