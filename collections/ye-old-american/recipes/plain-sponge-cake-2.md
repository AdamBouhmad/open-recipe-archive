## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- two cups of fine powdered sugar
- one cup of sifted flour
- whites of four eggs
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- one scant teacupful of boiling water
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of four eggs together with two cups of fine powdered sugar
2. Stir in gradually one cup of sifted flour and the whites of four eggs beaten to a stiff froth
3. add a cup of sifted flour in which two teaspoonfuls of baking powder have been stirred
4. add a scant teacupful of boiling water, stirred in a little at a time
5. Flavor, add salt and, however thin the mixture may seem, do not add any more flour
6. Bake in shallow tins
