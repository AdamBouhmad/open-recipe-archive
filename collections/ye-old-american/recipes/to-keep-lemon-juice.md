## Ingredients

- 1 pound of the best loaf-sugar
- 1 pint of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Powder a pound of the best loaf-sugar
2. Put it into a bowl
3. Strain over it a pint of lemon juice
4. Stir it well with a silver spoon till the sugar has entirely melted
5. Boil and skim it
6. Bottle it, sealing the corks
7. Keep it in a dry place
