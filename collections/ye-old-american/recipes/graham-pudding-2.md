## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup molasses
- ½ cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1½ cups Graham flour
- ½ teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup raisins, seeded and cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add molasses, milk, egg well beaten
2. Add dry ingredients mixed and sifted and raisins
3. Turn into buttered mould
4. Cover and steam two and one-half hours
5. Serve with Wine Sauce
