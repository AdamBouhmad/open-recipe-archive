## Ingredients

- pint of water
- lump of butter the size of an egg
- flour
- three quarters of a pound of grated cheese
- six eggs
- buttered paper

## Directions

1. Put a pint of water and a lump of butter the size of an egg into a sauce pan
2. Stir in as much flour as will make a thick batter
3. Put it on the fire and stir it continually till it will not stick to the pan
4. Put it in a bowl
5. Add three quarters of a pound of grated cheese
6. Mix it well
7. Break in two eggs and beat them well
8. Add two more eggs, then two more until you put in six
9. When it looks very light, drop it in small lumps on buttered paper
10. Bake it in a quick oven till of a delicate brown
