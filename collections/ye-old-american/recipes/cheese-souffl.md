## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- salt
- cayenne
- milk
- parmesan cheese
- eggs
- napkin

## Directions

1. Melt an ounce of butter in a saucepan
2. Mix smoothly with it one ounce of flour, a pinch of salt and cayenne and a quarter of a pint of milk
3. Simmer the mixture gently over the fire, stirring it all the time, till it is as thick as melted butter
4. Stir into it about three ounces of finely-grated parmesan, or any good cheese
5. Turn it into a basin and mix with it the yolks of two well-beaten eggs
6. Whisk three whites to a solid froth, and just before the souffle is baked put them into it
7. Pour the mixture into a small round tin; it should be only half filled, as the fondu will rise very high
8. Pin a napkin around the dish in which it is baked, and serve the moment it is baked
9. Have a metal cover strongly heated; bake for twenty minutes
10. Sufficient for six persons
