## Ingredients

- corn meal mush
- flour
- lard and butter mixed

## Directions

1. Make corn meal mush like the above recipe
2. Turn it into bread tins and when cold slice it
3. Dip each piece in flour and fry it in lard and butter mixed in the frying pan
4. Turn to brown well both sides
5. Must be served hot
