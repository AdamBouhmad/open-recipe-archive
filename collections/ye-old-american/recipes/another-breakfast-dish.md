## Ingredients

- cold roast beef
- three tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- one tablespoonful of walnut catsup
- teaspoonful of vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- spoonful of currant jelly
- teaspoonful made mustard
- warm water

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of cold roast beef
2. Lay them in a tin saucepan set in a pot of boiling water
3. Cover them with a gravy made of three tablespoonfuls of melted butter, one of walnut catsup, a teaspoonful of vinegar, a little salt and pepper, a spoonful of currant jelly, a teaspoonful made mustard, and some warm water
4. Cover tightly, and steam for half an hour, keeping the water in the outer vessel on a hard boil
