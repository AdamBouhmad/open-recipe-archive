## Ingredients

- one ounce of ginger
- one ounce of mustard
- one ounce of pepper
- three ounces of coriander seed
- three ounces of turmeric
- half an ounce of cardamoms
- one-quarter ounce of cayenne pepper
- one-quarter ounce of cinnamon
- one-quarter ounce of cumin seed

## Directions

1. Pound all these ingredients very fine in a mortar
2. Sift them and cork tight in a bottle
