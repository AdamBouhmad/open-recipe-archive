## Ingredients

- ten truffles
- a quarter of a pint of salad oil
- pepper and salt to taste
- one tablespoonful of minced parsley
- a very little finely minced garlic
- two blades of pounded mace
- one tablespoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleansing and brushing the truffles, cut them into thin slices
2. Put them in a baking-dish, on a seasoning of oil or butter, pepper, salt, parsley, garlic and mace in the above proportion
3. Bake them for nearly an hour
4. Just before serving add the lemon juice
5. Send them to the table very hot
