## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- salt
- pepper
- 2 tablespoons butter
- toast
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean one pint oysters and drain from their liquor.
2. Put in a stewpan and cook until oysters are plump and edges begin to curl.
3. Shake pan to prevent oysters from adhering to pan, or stir with a fork.
4. Season with salt, pepper, and two tablespoons butter, and pour over four small slices of toast.
5. Garnish with toast points and parsley.
