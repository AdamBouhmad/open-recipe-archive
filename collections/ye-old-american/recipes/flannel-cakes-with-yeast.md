## Ingredients

- One pint of sweet milk
- Two heaping tablespoonfuls of butter
- One pint of cold milk
- Four eggs
- One teaspoonful of salt
- Four tablespoonfuls of home-made yeast
- Flour

## Directions

1. Heat a pint of sweet milk and into it put two heaping tablespoonfuls of butter, let it melt
2. Add a pint of cold milk and the well-beaten yolks of four eggs, placing the whites in a cool place
3. Add a teaspoonful of salt, four tablespoonfuls of home-made yeast and sufficient flour to make a stiff batter
4. Set it in a warm place to rise
5. Let it stand three hours or over night
6. Before baking add the beaten whites
7. Bake like any other griddle-cakes
