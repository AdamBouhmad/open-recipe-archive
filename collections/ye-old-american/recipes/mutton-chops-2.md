## Ingredients

- two large spoonsful of boiling water
- one of mushroom catsup
- a small spoonful of butter
- some salt

## Directions

1. Cut the rack as for the harrico, broil them
2. When dished, pour over them a gravy made with two large spoonsful of boiling water, one of mushroom catsup, a small spoonful of butter and some salt
3. Stir it till the butter is melted
4. Garnish with horse-radish scraped
