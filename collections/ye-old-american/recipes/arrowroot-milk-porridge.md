## Ingredients

- One large cupful of fresh milk
- One cupful of boiling water
- One teaspoonful of arrowroot
- Cold water
- Two teaspoonfuls of white sugar
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Put the sugar into the milk and the salt into the boiling water
2. Pour the boiling water into a farina kettle
3. Mix the arrowroot with cold water to a paste
4. Add the wet arrowroot to the boiling water and boil, stirring constantly until it is clear
5. Put in the milk and cook ten minutes, stirring often
6. Give while warm, adding hot milk should it be thicker than gruel
