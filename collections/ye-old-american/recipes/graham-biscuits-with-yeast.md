## Ingredients

- one pint of water or milk
- one large tablespoonful of butter
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar
- a half cup of yeast
- a pinch of salt
- wheat flour
- Graham flour

## Directions

1. Take enough wheat flour to use up the water, making it the consistency of batter-cakes
2. Add butter, sugar, yeast, and salt
3. Add as much Graham flour as can be stirred in with a spoon
4. Set it away till morning
5. In the morning grease a pan and flour your hands
6. Take a lump of dough the size of an egg and roll it lightly between the palms of your hands
7. Let them rise twenty minutes
8. Bake in a tolerably hot oven
