## Ingredients

- squash
- boiling salted water
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash squash and cut in thick slices or quarters.
2. Cook twenty minutes in boiling salted water, or until soft.
3. Turn in a cheese-cloth placed over a colander, drain, and wring in cheese-cloth.
4. Mash, and season with butter, salt, and pepper.
