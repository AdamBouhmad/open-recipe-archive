## Ingredients

- six fillets of beef
- six mushroom caps
- one tablespoon parsley
- one tablespoon onion
- one tablespoon butter
- buttered crumbs
- Espagnole Sauce
- red pepper
- green pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook six fillets same as Sautéd Fillets of Beef à la Moelle
2. Put a sautéd stuffed mushroom cap on each
3. Sprinkle with buttered crumbs
4. Bake until crumbs are browned
5. Remove to serving dish
6. Pour around Espagnole Sauce
7. Garnish caps with strips of red and green pepper cut in fancy shapes
8. For stuffing: Clean and finely chop six mushroom caps
9. Add one tablespoon each of parsley and onion finely chopped, and one tablespoon butter
10. Moisten with Espagnole Sauce
