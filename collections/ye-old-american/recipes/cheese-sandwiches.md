## Ingredients

- one hard-boiled egg
- a quarter of a pound of common cheese grated
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- half a teaspoonful of pepper
- half a teaspoonful of mustard
- one tablespoonful of melted butter
- one tablespoonful of vinegar or cold water
- biscuits or oat-cake

## Directions

1. Take the yolk of the egg and put it into a small bowl and crumble it down
2. Put into it the butter and mix it smooth with a spoon
3. Add the salt, pepper, mustard and the cheese, mixing each well
4. Then put in the tablespoonful of vinegar, which will make it the proper thickness
5. If vinegar is not relished, then use cold water instead
6. Spread this between two biscuits or pieces of oat-cake
7. Season to taste if preferred
