## Ingredients

- four tablespoonfuls of very finely chopped citron
- four tablespoonfuls of finely chopped seeded raisins
- half a cupful of blanched almonds chopped fine
- a quarter of a pound of finely chopped figs
- whites of three eggs
- half of a cupful of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of three eggs to a stiff froth, adding half of a cupful of sugar
2. Mix thoroughly into this the whole of the chopped ingredients
3. Put it between the layers of cake when the cake is hot, so that it will cook the egg a little
