## Ingredients

- One teacupful of water
- One ounce of clarified isinglass or patent gelatine
- Juice of a lemon
- One cupful of fine white sugar
- One quart of rich cream
- Half a pint of Madeira or sherry wine
- Sugar and cream or custard sauce for serving

## Directions

1. In one teacupful of water boil until dissolved one ounce of clarified isinglass or patent gelatine, stirring continually while boiling
2. Squeeze the juice of a lemon upon a cupful of fine white sugar
3. Stir the sugar into a quart of rich cream and half a pint of Madeira or sherry wine
4. When well mixed, add the dissolved isinglass or gelatine
5. Stir all well together
6. Pour it into molds previously wet with cold water
7. Set the molds upon ice
8. Let them stand until their contents are hard and cold
9. Serve with sugar and cream or custard sauce
