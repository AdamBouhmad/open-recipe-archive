## Ingredients

- a slice of a fillet of veal, about half an inch thick
- salt pork, chopped
- bread crumbs
- one onion
- pepper
- salt
- sweet marjoram
- one egg well beaten
- veal gravy or light stock
- flour
- butter
- horse-radish

## Directions

1. Cut up a slice of a fillet of veal, about half an inch thick, into squares of three inches.
2. Mix up a little salt pork, chopped with bread crumbs, one onion, a little pepper, salt, sweet marjoram, and one egg well beaten.
3. Put this mixture upon the pieces of veal, fastening the four corners together with little bird skewers.
4. Lay them in a pan with sufficient veal gravy or light stock to cover the bottom of the pan.
5. Dredge with flour and set in a hot oven.
6. When browned on top, put a small bit of butter on each, and let them remain until quite tender, which will take twenty minutes.
7. Serve with horse-radish.
