## Ingredients

- 1 large cup fine bread-crumbs soaked in milk
- ¾ cup sugar
- 1 lemon—juice and grated rind
- 6 eggs
- ½ lb. stale sponge-cake
- ½ lb. macaroons—almond
- ½ cup jelly or jam
- one small tumbler of Sherry wine
- ½ cup milk poured upon the bread-crumbs
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter

## Directions

1. Rub the butter and sugar together; put the beaten yolks in next
2. Add the soaked bread-crumbs, the lemon juice and rind, and beat to a smooth, light paste before adding the whites
3. Butter your mould very well, and put in the bottom a light layer of dry bread-crumbs
4. Upon this put one layer of macaroons, laid evenly and closely together
5. Wet this with wine, and cover with a layer of the mixture
6. Then add slices of sponge-cake, spread thickly with jelly or jam
7. Next macaroons, wet with wine, more custard, sponge-cake, and jam, and so on until the mould is full, putting a layer of the mixture at the top
8. Cover closely, and steam in the oven three-quarters of an hour
9. Remove the cover to brown the top
10. Turn out carefully into a dish, and pour over it a sauce made of currant jelly warmed, and beaten up with two tablespoonfuls melted butter and a glass of pale Sherry
