## Ingredients

- a quart of sifted flour
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- one teaspoonful of salt
- a pint of sour milk
- one teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Rub butter and salt into flour
2. Dissolve soda and stir into milk
3. Stir milk into flour just as you add it
4. Knead quickly
5. Roll out nearly half an inch thick
6. Cut out with a biscuit-cutter
7. Bake immediately in a quick oven
