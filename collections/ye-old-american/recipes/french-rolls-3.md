## Ingredients

- a pound of flour
- two ounces of butter
- the whites only of three eggs
- a table-spoonful of strong yeast
- milk
- a salt-spoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Sift a pound of flour into a pan, and rub into it two ounces of butter
2. Mix in the whites only of three eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, and a table-spoonful of strong yeast
3. Add sufficient milk to make a stiff dough, and a salt-spoonful of salt
4. Cover it and set it before the fire to rise. It should be light in an hour
5. Put it on a paste-board, divide it into rolls, or round cakes
6. Lay them in a floured square pan, and bake them about ten minutes in a quick oven
