## Ingredients

- perch
- porgies
- flounders
- river bass
- weak-fish
- white-fish
- salt
- flour
- dripping
- lard
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and dry the fish. Lay them in a large flat dish, salt, and dredge with flour.
2. Have ready a frying-pan of hot dripping, lard, or butter; put in as many fish as the pan will hold without crowding, and fry to a light brown.
3. Send up hot in a chafing-dish.
4. In serving, lay the head of each fish to the tail of the one next him.
