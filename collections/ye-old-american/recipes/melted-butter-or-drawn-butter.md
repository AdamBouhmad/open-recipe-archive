## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of the best fresh butter
- about two tea-spoonfuls of flour
- four table-spoonfuls of cold water

## Directions

1. Have a covered sauce-pan, preferably one lined with porcelain
2. Take a quarter of a pound of the best fresh butter, cut it up, and mix with it about two tea-spoonfuls of flour
3. When it is thoroughly mixed, put it into the sauce-pan and add to it four table-spoonfuls of cold water
4. Cover the sauce-pan and set it in a large tin pan of boiling water
5. Shake it round continually, always moving it the same way, till it is entirely melted and begins to simmer
6. Then let it rest till it boils up
