## Ingredients

- One gallon of oysters
- One quart of rich milk
- Yolks of four eggs
- Two good tablespoonfuls of butter
- One tablespoonful of flour
- Pepper
- Salt
- Celery salt, marjoram, thyme, mace, or onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Scald one gallon of oysters in their own liquor
2. Add one quart of rich milk to the liquor and bring to a boil
3. Skim out the oysters and set aside
4. Mix the yolks of four eggs, two tablespoonfuls of butter, and one tablespoonful of flour well together
5. Add the milk first, then after beating the eggs, add a little of the hot liquor to them gradually
6. Stir them rapidly into the soup
7. Add the butter and seasoning of your choice (celery salt, marjoram, thyme, mace, or onion)
8. Season to taste with pepper and salt with caution
