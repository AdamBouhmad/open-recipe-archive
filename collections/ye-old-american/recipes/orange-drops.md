## Ingredients

- oranges, ripe
- loaf-sugar, best double refined, powdered

## Directions

1. Squeeze through a strainer the juice of a dozen or more ripe oranges
2. Have ready some of the best double refined loaf-sugar, powdered as fine as possible and sifted
3. Mix gradually the sugar with the juice till it is so thick you can scarcely stir it
4. Put it into a porcelain skillet
5. Set it on hot coals or over a moderate fire and stir it hard with a wooden spoon for five minutes after it begins to boil
6. Take it off the fire and with a silver spoon or the point of a broad knife, drop portions of the mixture upon a flat tin pan or a pewter dish
7. Smooth the drops and make them of good shape and regular size, which should be about that of a cent
8. When cold they will easily come off the tin
9. Never use extract or oil of orange for them
