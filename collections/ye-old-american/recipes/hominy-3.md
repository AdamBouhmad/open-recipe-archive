## Ingredients

- hominy
- cold water

## Directions

1. Wash the hominy thoroughly in one or two waters
2. Cover it with twice its depth of cold water and let it come to a boil slowly
3. If it be the large hominy, simmer six hours; if the small hominy, simmer two hours
4. When the water evaporates add hot water
5. When done it may be eaten with cream, or allowed to become cold and warmed up in the frying pan, using a little butter to prevent burning
