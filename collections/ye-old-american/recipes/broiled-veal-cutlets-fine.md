## Ingredients

- two or three pounds of veal cutlets
- egg
- bread crumbs
- two tablespoonfuls of minced savory herbs
- salt and pepper to taste
- grated nutmeg
- white letter paper
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets about three-quarters of an inch in thickness.
2. Flatten them, and brush them over with the yolk of an egg.
3. Dip them into bread crumbs and minced herbs, season with pepper and salt.
4. Fold each cutlet in a piece of white letter paper well buttered.
5. Twist the ends, and broil over a clear fire.
6. When done remove the paper.
