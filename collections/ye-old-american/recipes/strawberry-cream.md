## Ingredients

- one quart boiled custard
- ripe strawberries
- rich cream

## Directions

1. Make in the same manner as Raspberry Cream
2. The strawberries must be very ripe, and the stems picked out
3. If rich cream can be procured, it will be infinitely better
4. The custard is intended as a substitute, when cream cannot be had
