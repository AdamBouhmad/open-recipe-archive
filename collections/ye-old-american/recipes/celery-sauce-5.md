## Ingredients

- 3 cups celery, cut in thin slices
- 2 cups Thin White Sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape celery before cutting into pieces
2. Cook in boiling salted water until soft
3. Drain, rub through a sieve, and add to sauce
4. Celery sauce is often made from the stock in which fowl or turkey has been boiled, or with one-half stock and one-half milk
