## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- ⅓ cup butter
- ¾ cup milk

## Directions

1. Mix same as Strawberry Short Cake I.
2. Toss and roll on floured board.
3. Put in round buttered tin, and shape with back of hand to fit pan.
