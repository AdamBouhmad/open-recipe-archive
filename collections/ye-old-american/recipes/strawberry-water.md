## Ingredients

- one cupful of ripe hulled berries
- a quarter of a pound of pulverized sugar
- half a pint of cold water
- juice of one lemon
- one and a half pints of cold water

## Directions

1. Take one cupful of ripe hulled berries; crush with a wooden spoon, mixing with the mass a quarter of a pound of pulverized sugar and half a pint of cold water
2. Pour the mixture into a fine sieve, rub through and filter till clear
3. Add the strained juice of one lemon and one and a half pints of cold water, mix thoroughly and set in ice chest till wanted
