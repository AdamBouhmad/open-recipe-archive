## Ingredients

- rabbits
- water
- flour
- chopped parsley
- butter
- cream or beaten yolk of egg

## Directions

1. Having washed the rabbits well, put them into a pan of cold water, and let them lie in it two or three hours.
2. Then cut them into joints, dry them in a cloth, dredge them with flour, strew them with chopped parsley, and fry them in butter.
3. After you take them out of the frying-pan, stir a wine-glass of cream into the gravy, or the beaten yolk of an egg.
4. Do not let it boil, but pour it at once into the dish with the rabbits.
