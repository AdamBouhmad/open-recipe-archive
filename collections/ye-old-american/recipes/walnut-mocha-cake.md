## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup coffee infusion
- 1¾ cups flour
- 2½ teaspoons baking powder
- Whites 3 eggs
- ¾ cup walnut meats, broken in pieces

## Directions

1. Follow directions for mixing butter cake mixtures.
2. Cover with Confectioners' Frosting, using cream, and flavoring with vanilla.
