## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- Few grains pepper
- 1 tablespoon tarragon vinegar
- 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
- 6 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon chopped pickles
- 1 tablespoon chopped green pepper
- 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoon chopped chives
