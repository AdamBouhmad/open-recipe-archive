## Ingredients

- 1½ cups berries
- water
- sugar to taste
- ⅛ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Pick over and wash berries.
2. Stew until soft with enough water to prevent burning.
3. Add sugar to taste, and one-eighth teaspoon salt.
4. Line plate with paste, put on a rim, fill with berries (which have been cooled).
5. Arrange six strips pastry across the top, cut same width as rim; put on an upper rim.
6. Bake thirty minutes in moderate oven.
