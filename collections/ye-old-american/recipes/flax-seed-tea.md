## Ingredients

- One ounce of unbruised flax-seed
- A little pulverized liquorice-root
- One pint of boiling soft or rain water

## Directions

1. Pour a pint of boiling soft or rain water upon an ounce of unbruised flax-seed and a little pulverized liquorice-root
2. Place the vessel containing these ingredients near, but not on, the fire for four hours
3. Strain through a linen cloth
4. Make it fresh every day
