## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons ground coffee
- 1 cup cold water

## Directions

1. Add coffee to cold water, cover closely, and let stand over night.
2. In the morning bring to a boiling-point.
3. If carefully poured, a clear cup of coffee may be served.
