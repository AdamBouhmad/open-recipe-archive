## Ingredients

- boiled icing
- one ounce of chopped citron
- one ounce of candied cherries
- one ounce of seedless raisins
- one ounce of candied pineapple
- one ounce of blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Mix with boiled icing the chopped citron, candied cherries, seedless raisins, candied pineapple and blanched almonds
