## Ingredients

- 1 quart cold water
- 2 cups sugar
- ½ cup lemon juice
- 2 cups chopped pineapple
- 1 cup orange juice

## Directions

1. Boil water, sugar, and pineapple twenty minutes.
2. Add fruit juice, cool, strain, and dilute with ice-water.
