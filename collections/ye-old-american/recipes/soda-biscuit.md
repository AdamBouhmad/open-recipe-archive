## Ingredients

- one quart of sifted flour
- one teaspoonful of soda
- two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- one teaspoonful of salt
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- one pint of sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly
2. Rub in butter
3. Wet with sweet milk
4. Bake in a quick oven
