## Ingredients

- salted fish
- pounded cloves
- pepper
- garlic
- sweet herbs
- parsley
- tarragon
- common vinegar
- wine
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Take the quantity necessary for the dish and wash them
2. Lay them in fresh water for a night
3. Put them on the tin plate with holes and place it in the fish kettle
4. Sprinkle over it pounded cloves and pepper, with four cloves of garlic
5. Put in a bundle of sweet herbs and parsley, a large spoonful of tarragon, and two of common vinegar, with a pint of wine
6. Roll one quarter of a pound of butter in two spoonsful of flour
7. Cut it in small pieces and put it over the fish
8. Cover it closely and simmer it over a slow fire half an hour
9. Take the fish out carefully and lay it in the dish
10. Set it over hot water and cover it till the gravy has boiled a little longer
11. Take out the garlic and herbs
12. Pour it over the fish and serve it up
