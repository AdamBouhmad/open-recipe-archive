## Ingredients

- two cups of butter
- one pint of powdered sugar
- one quart of flour
- a teaspoonful of salt
- eight eggs
- a wine-glass of brandy
- a quarter of a pound of citron

## Directions

1. Stir two cups of butter to a cream.
2. Beat in the following ingredients each one in succession: one pint of powdered sugar, one quart of flour, a teaspoonful of salt.
3. Beat the yolks and whites of eight eggs separately and add them.
4. Add a wine-glass of brandy.
5. Then last of all add a quarter of a pound of citron cut into thin slices and floured.
6. Line two cake pans with buttered paper and turn the cake batter in.
7. Bake in a moderate oven about three-quarters of an hour.
