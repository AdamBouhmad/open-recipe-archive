## Ingredients

- potatoes
- lard or clarified drippings

## Directions

1. Pare and lay in ice-water for an hour.
2. Choose the largest and soundest potatoes you can get for this dish.
3. At the end of the hour, pare with a small knife round and round in one continuous curling strip.
4. Fry a few at a time, for fear of entanglement, in lard or clarified drippings.
5. Drain and arrange neatly upon a hot flat dish.
