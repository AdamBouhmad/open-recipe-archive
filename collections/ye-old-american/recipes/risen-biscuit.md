## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- ¾ cup lard or butter (half-and-half is a good rule)
- ¾ cup of yeast
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- flour to make a soft dough

## Directions

1. Mix over night, warming the milk slightly and melting the lard or butter
2. In the morning, roll out into a sheet three-quarters of an inch in thickness
3. Cut into round cakes and set these closely together in a pan
4. Let them rise for twenty minutes and bake twenty minutes
5. Alternatively, set the ingredients with half as much flour in the form of a thin sponge and work in the rest of the flour five hours later
6. Let this rise five hours more and proceed as already directed
