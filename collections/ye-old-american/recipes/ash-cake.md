## Ingredients

- corn-meal
- boiling water
- a little salt
- a tablespoonful butter

## Directions

1. Mix as for corn-meal pone
2. Sweep a clean spot upon the hot hearth
3. Put the bread down and cover with hot wood ashes
4. Wash and wipe dry before it is eaten
5. Alternatively lay a cabbage-leaf above and below the pone
6. The bread is thus steamed before it is baked, and is made ready for eating by stripping off the leaves
