## Ingredients

- sturgeon with skin on
- salt
- cloves of garlic
- flour
- melted butter
- chopped parsley
- mushroom catsup
- lemon pickle
- pepper vinegar

## Directions

1. Leave the skin on, which must be nicely scraped
2. take out the gristle
3. rub it with salt and let it lie an hour
4. put it on in cold water with some salt and a few cloves of garlic
5. dredge it with flour before it is put into the water
6. skim it carefully
7. when dished, pour over it melted butter with chopped parsley, a large spoonful of mushroom catsup, one of lemon pickle, and one of pepper vinegar
8. send some of it to table in a sauce boat
