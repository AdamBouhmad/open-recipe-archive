## Ingredients

- 5 or 6 slices fat pork
- 4 pounds fresh cod or sea-bass
- chopped onions
- parsley
- summer savory
- pepper (black or cayenne)
- Boston or butter or whole cream crackers
- butter
- cold water
- flour
- sliced lemon
- pickles
- stewed tomatoes

## Directions

1. Fry five or six slices of fat pork crisp in the bottom of the pot
2. Take them out and chop them into small pieces, put them back into the bottom of the pot with their own gravy
3. Cut four pounds of fresh cod or sea-bass into pieces two inches square
4. Lay enough of the fish on the pork to cover it
5. Follow with a layer of chopped onions, a little parsley, summer savory and pepper
6. Then a layer of split Boston, or butter, or whole cream crackers which have been soaked in warm water until moistened through but not ready to break
7. Above this put a layer of pork and repeat the order--onions, seasoning, crackers and pork, until materials are exhausted
8. Let the topmost layer be buttered crackers well soaked
9. Pour in enough cold water to barely cover all
10. Cover the pot and stew gently for an hour, watching that the water does not sink too low
11. Should it leave the upper layer exposed, replenish cautiously from the boiling tea-kettle
12. When thoroughly done, take out with a perforated skimmer and put into a tureen
13. Thicken the gravy with a tablespoonful of flour and about the same quantity of butter
14. Boil up and pour over the chowder
15. Serve with sliced lemon, pickles and stewed tomatoes
