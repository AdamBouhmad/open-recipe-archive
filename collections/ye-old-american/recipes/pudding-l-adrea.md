## Ingredients

- 2 cups thin cream
- 1½ tablespoons granulated gelatine
- 2 tablespoons cold water
- ¾ cup sugar
- Whites 4 eggs
- 3 tablespoons Sherry
- 1½ tablespoons Sauterne
- Sauterne jelly mixture
- pistachio nuts
- glacéd cherries

## Directions

1. Make one-half recipe for Sauterne Jelly, allowing one and one-third tablespoons granulated gelatine
2. Color one-half green and one-half red
3. Fill sections of a fancy mould alternately with green and red jelly
4. In the green jelly mould pistachio nuts cut in quarters; in red jelly glacéd cherries cut in quarters
5. Scald cream, add gelatine soaked in cold water, then add whites of eggs beaten until stiff; add sugar
6. Remove from range, set in pan of ice-water, and stir occasionally until mixture thickens
7. Add flavoring and turn into mould
8. Chill thoroughly and remove from mould
