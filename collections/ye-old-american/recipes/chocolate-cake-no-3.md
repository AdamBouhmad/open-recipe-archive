## Ingredients

- two cups sugar
- one cup butter
- yolks of five eggs
- whites of two eggs
- one cup milk
- two teaspoonfuls baking powder
- three and one-half cups flour
- Mixture for Filling: whites of three eggs
- one and one-half cups of sugar
- three tablespoonfuls of grated chocolate
- one teaspoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Thoroughly mix two teaspoonfuls baking powder with three and one-half cups flour while dry
2. Mix two cups sugar, one cup butter, yolks of five eggs, whites of two eggs and one cup milk together
3. Mix in the flour and baking powder
4. Bake in jelly tins
5. For the filling, beat together whites of three eggs, one and one-half cups of sugar, three tablespoonfuls of grated chocolate, one teaspoonful of vanilla
6. Spread between the layers and on top of the cake
