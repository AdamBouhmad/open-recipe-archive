## Ingredients

- 3 cups milk
- 1 cup sugar
- Yolks 5 eggs
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1½ cups heavy cream
- Whites 5 eggs
- ⅓ cup pistachio meats
- ⅓ cup filbert meats
- ⅓ cup English walnut meats
- ⅓ cup almond meats
- 1 teaspoon almond extract
- 1 tablespoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Make a custard of first four ingredients, strain, and cool
2. Add heavy cream beaten until stiff, whites of eggs beaten until stiff, nut meats finely chopped, and flavoring
3. Freeze
