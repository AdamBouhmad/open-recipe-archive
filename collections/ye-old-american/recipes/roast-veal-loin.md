## Ingredients

- veal loin
- salt and water
- gravy
- flour
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Allow at least a quarter of an hour to each pound.
2. Heat gradually, baste frequently—at first with salt and water, afterward with gravy.
3. When the meat is nearly done, dredge lightly with flour, and baste once with melted butter.
4. Should the meat brown too fast, cover with white paper.
5. Skim the gravy; thicken with a tablespoonful of flour, boil up, and put into the gravy-boat.
