## Ingredients

- Four tongues
- Boiling water
- Six slices carrot
- Two stalks celery
- One onion stuck with eight cloves
- One teaspoon peppercorns
- One-half tablespoon salt
- One-fourth cup butter
- Six tablespoons flour
- Two cups Brown Stock
- Two-thirds teaspoon salt
- One-half teaspoon paprika
- Few grains cayenne
- One tablespoon vinegar
- One-half tablespoon capers
- One cucumber pickle thinly sliced
- Cold cooked beets

## Directions

1. Cook four tongues until tender in boiling water to cover, with six slices carrot, two stalks celery, one onion stuck with eight cloves, one teaspoon peppercorns, and one-half tablespoon salt
2. Take tongues from water and remove skin and roots
3. Cut in halves lengthwise and reheat in Sauce Piquante
4. For Sauce Piquante: Brown one-fourth cup butter
5. Add six tablespoons flour and stir until well browned
6. Add two cups Brown Stock and cook three minutes
7. Season with two-thirds teaspoon salt, one-half teaspoon paprika, few grains of cayenne, one tablespoon vinegar, one-half tablespoon capers, and one cucumber pickle thinly sliced
8. Serve garnished with cucumber pickles and cold cooked beets cut in fancy shapes
