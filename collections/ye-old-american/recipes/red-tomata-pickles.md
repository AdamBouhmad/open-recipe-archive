## Ingredients

- small, round, button tomatas
- vinegar
- mace
- cloves
- whole black pepper
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Fill three quarters of a jar with small, round, button tomatas when quite ripe
2. Put them in whole
3. Pour over them sufficient cold vinegar highly flavoured with mace, cloves, and whole black pepper to raise them to the top
4. Add a table-spoonful of sweet oil
5. Cover the jar closely
