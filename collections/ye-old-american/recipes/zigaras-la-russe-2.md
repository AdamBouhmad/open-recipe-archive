## Ingredients

- Puff paste
- Cheese mixture
- Two tablespoons butter
- Four tablespoons flour
- One-half cup milk
- One tablespoon heavy cream
- One egg yolk
- One-third cup grated cheese
- Salt
- Cayenne
- Egg
- Crumbs

## Directions

1. Make and fry same as Cigarettes à la Prince Henry, using cheese mixture in place of Chicken Force-meat.
2. Melt butter, add flour, and pour on gradually milk.
3. Then add heavy cream, egg yolk, and grated cheese.
4. Season highly with salt and cayenne.
5. Cool before spreading on paste.
