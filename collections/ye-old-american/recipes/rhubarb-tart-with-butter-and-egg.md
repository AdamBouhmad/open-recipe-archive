## Ingredients

- rhubarb stalks
- water
- sugar
- butter (a lump the size of a hickory-nut for each pie)
- egg (one well-beaten egg for each pie)
- pastry

## Directions

1. Skin the stalks and cut into small pieces.
2. Put into a saucepan with very little water, and stew slowly until soft.
3. Sweeten while hot.
4. After sweetening the stewed rhubarb, stir in a lump of butter the size of a hickory-nut for each pie.
5. Stir in a well-beaten egg for each pie.
6. Bake in pastry.
7. Lay cross-bars of pastry over the top.
