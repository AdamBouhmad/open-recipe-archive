## Ingredients

- fresh or dry chervil leaves
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. Half fill a bottle with fresh or dry chervil leaves
2. Fill the bottle with good vinegar and heat it gently by placing it in warm water, which bring to boiling point
3. Remove from the fire
4. When cool cork
5. In two weeks it will be ready for use
