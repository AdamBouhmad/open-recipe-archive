## Ingredients

- flour, one quart
- cream tartar, two teaspoonfuls
- soda, one teaspoonful
- milk, a teacupful
- salt, a teaspoonful
- minced chicken, veal or mutton
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- gravy

## Directions

1. Mix two teaspoonfuls of cream tartar with one quart of flour
2. Dissolve one teaspoonful of soda in a teacupful of milk
3. Add a teaspoonful of salt
4. Do not use shortening of any kind, but roll out the mixture half an inch thick
5. On it lay minced chicken, veal or mutton seasoned with pepper and salt and free from gristle
6. Roll the crust over and over, and put it on a buttered plate and place in a steamer for half an hour
7. Serve for breakfast or lunch, giving a slice to each person with gravy served with it
