## Ingredients

- Rolled Wafers
- almonds blanched and chopped
- other nut meats or shredded cocoanut

## Directions

1. Before baking Rolled Wafers, sprinkle with almonds blanched and chopped.
2. Other nut meats or shredded cocoanut may be used in place of almonds.
