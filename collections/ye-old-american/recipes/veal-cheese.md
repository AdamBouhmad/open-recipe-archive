## Ingredients

- equal quantities of boiled sliced veal
- smoked tongue
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare equal quantities of boiled sliced veal and smoked tongue.
2. Pound the slices separately in a mortar, moistening with butter as you proceed.
3. Pack it in a jar or pail, mixing it in alternate layers; first the tongue and then the veal, so that when cut it will look variegated.
4. Press it down hard and pour melted butter over the top.
5. Keep it well covered and in a dry place.
