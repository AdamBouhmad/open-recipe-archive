## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup washed rice
- 3/4 cup tomatoes, stewed and strained
- 1 cup Brown Stock, highly seasoned
- 3 tablespoons butter

## Directions

1. Add tomato to stock
2. Heat to boiling-point
3. Add rice
4. Steam until rice is soft
5. Stir in butter with a fork
6. Keep uncovered that steam may escape
7. Serve in place of a vegetable, or as border for curried or fricasseed meat
