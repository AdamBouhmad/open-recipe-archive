## Ingredients

- Green nasturtium seed when they are full-grown, but not yellow
- Boiling vinegar, slightly spiced

## Directions

1. Gather green nasturtium seed when they are full-grown, but not yellow
2. Dry for a day in the sun
3. Put into small jars or wide-mouthed bottles
4. Cover with boiling vinegar, slightly spiced, and when cool, cork closely
5. In six weeks they will be fit for use
