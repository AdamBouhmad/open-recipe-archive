## Ingredients

- stale sponge cake
- a pint of good wine
- half a pound of sweet almonds
- a pint of good boiled custard

## Directions

1. Take a stale sponge cake and cut the bottom and sides of it, so as to make it stand even in a glass fruit dish
2. Make a few deep gashes through it with a sharp knife
3. Pour over it a pint of good wine and let it stand and soak into the cake
4. In the meantime, blanch, peel and slice lengthwise half a pound of sweet almonds
5. Stick them all over the top of the cake
6. Have ready a pint of good boiled custard, well flavored, and pour over the whole
7. To be dished with a spoon
