## Ingredients

- cold boiled chicken
- Mayonnaise or Cream Salad Dressing
- salt
- pepper
- rich chicken stock

## Directions

1. Chop cold boiled chicken, and moisten with Mayonnaise or Cream Salad Dressing; or season with salt and pepper, and moisten with rich chicken stock.
2. Prepare as other sandwiches.
