## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- half a box of gelatine
- the yolks of three eggs
- one cupful of sugar
- vanilla or lemon for flavoring

## Directions

1. Soak half a box of gelatine in one quart of milk for an hour
2. Place it on the fire and stir often
3. Beat the yolks of three eggs very light with a cupful of sugar
4. Stir into the scalding milk and heat until it begins to thicken (it should not boil, or it will curdle)
5. Remove from the fire and strain through thin muslin or tarlatan
6. When nearly cold flavor with vanilla or lemon
7. Wet a dish or mold in cold water and set aside to stiffen
