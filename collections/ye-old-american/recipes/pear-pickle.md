## Ingredients

- small pears
- cider vinegar
- sugar
- allspice
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Select small, sound pears and remove the blossom end
2. Stick them with a fork
3. Allow to each quart of pears one pint of cider vinegar and one cup of sugar
4. Put in a teaspoonful allspice, cinnamon and cloves to boil with the vinegar
5. Add the pears and boil, and seal in jars
