## Ingredients

- selected oysters
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- egg
- cracker or stale bread crumbs
- 2 cups cabbage, finely shredded
- 2 green peppers, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon celery seed
- ¼ teaspoon mustard seed
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- ¼ cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Follow directions for Fried Oysters.
2. For Philadelphia Relish, mix ingredients in order given: cabbage, green peppers, celery seed, mustard seed, salt, brown sugar, and vinegar.
3. Serve Fried Oysters with Philadelphia Relish.
