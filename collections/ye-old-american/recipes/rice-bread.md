## Ingredients

- six ounces of rice
- a quart of water
- two pounds of flour
- two tea-spoonsful of salt
- two large spoonsful of yeast

## Directions

1. Boil six ounces of rice in a quart of water, till it is dry and soft
2. put it into two pounds of flour
3. mix it in well
4. add two tea-spoonsful of salt, two large spoonsful of yeast, and as much water as will make it the consistence of bread
5. when well risen, bake it in moulds
