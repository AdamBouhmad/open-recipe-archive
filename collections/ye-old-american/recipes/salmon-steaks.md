## Ingredients

- salmon
- flour
- chalk
- steak tongs
- napkin
- anchovy sauce or prawn sauce or lobster sauce
- white paper
- butter
- string or pins
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Split the salmon and take out the bone as nicely as possible, without mangling the flesh.
2. Cut it into fillets or steaks about an inch thick.
3. Dry them lightly in a cloth, and dredge them with flour.
4. Have ready some clear bright coals, such as are fit for beef-steaks.
5. Let the gridiron be clean and bright, and rub the bars with chalk to prevent the fish from sticking.
6. Broil the slices thoroughly, turning them with steak tongs.
7. Send them to table hot, wrapped in the folds of a napkin that has been heated.
8. Serve up with them anchovy, or prawn, or lobster sauce.
