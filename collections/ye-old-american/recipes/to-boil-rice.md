## Ingredients

- rice
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. put two cups full of rice in a bowl of water
2. rub it well with the hand
3. pour off the water
4. repeat until the water ceases to be discoloured
5. put the rice into two and a half cups of cold water
6. add a tea-spoonful of salt
7. cover the pot close
8. set it on a brisk fire
9. let it boil ten minutes
10. pour off the greater part of the water
11. remove the pot to a bed of coals
12. let it remain a quarter of an hour to soak and dry
