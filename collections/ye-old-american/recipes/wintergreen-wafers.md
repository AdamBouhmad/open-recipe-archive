## Ingredients

- 1 oz. gum tragacanth
- 1 cup cold water
- Confectioners' sugar
- Oil of wintergreen

## Directions

1. Soak gum tragacanth in water twenty-four hours and rub through a fine wire sieve; add enough confectioners' sugar to knead.
2. Flavor with a few drops of oil of wintergreen.
3. If liked pink, color with fruit red.
4. Roll until very thin on a board or marble dredged with sugar.
5. Shape with a small round cutter or cut in three-fourths inch squares.
6. Spread wafers, cover, and let stand until dry and brittle.
