## Ingredients

- the rind of one orange
- the juice of two large oranges
- a large cupful of sugar
- a heaping tablespoonful of flour
- the yolks of three eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- pie paste
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar
- the juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Grate the rind of one and use the juice of two large oranges
2. Stir together a large cupful of sugar and a heaping tablespoonful of flour
3. Add to this the well-beaten yolks of three eggs, two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
4. Reserve the whites for frosting
5. Turn this into a pie-pan lined with pie paste
6. Bake in a quick oven
7. When done so as to resemble a finely baked custard, spread on the top of it the beaten whites, which must be sweetened with two tablespoonfuls of sugar
8. Spread evenly and return to the oven and brown slightly
