## Ingredients

- currants
- powdered white sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and squeeze the currants in the usual way
2. Place the juice in a stone or earthen vessel and set away in a cool place in the cellar
3. In about twenty-four hours remove the considerable amount of froth from the surface produced by fermentation
4. Strain the whole again through the jelly bag
5. Weigh the juice and add an equal weight of powdered white sugar
6. Stir constantly until entirely dissolved
7. Put into jars and tie up tightly
8. Set away and at the end of another twenty-four hours a perfectly transparent jelly will be formed
