## Ingredients

- 3 coffee cups of lemonade (strong and sweet)
- 1 glass champagne
- 1 glass rum
- 2 oranges—juice only
- 2 eggs—whites only—well whipped
- ½ lb. powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the powdered sugar into the stiffened egg whites
2. Mix lemonade, champagne, and rum together
3. Combine with the sugar and egg white mixture
4. Ice abundantly or, if you prefer, freeze
