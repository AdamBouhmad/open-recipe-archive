## Ingredients

- eggs
- boiling salted water
- salt
- butter
- muffin rings
- buttered toast
- parsley

## Directions

1. Have ready a shallow pan two-thirds full of boiling salted water, allowing one-half tablespoon salt to one quart of water.
2. Put two or three buttered muffin rings in the water.
3. Break each egg separately into a cup, and carefully slip into a muffin ring.
4. The water should cover the eggs.
5. When there is a film over the top, and the white is firm, carefully remove with a buttered skimmer to circular pieces of buttered toast, and let each person season his own egg with butter, salt, and pepper.
6. If cooked for an invalid, garnish with four toast points and a bit of parsley.
