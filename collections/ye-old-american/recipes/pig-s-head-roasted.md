## Ingredients

- head of a half-grown pig
- salted water
- beaten egg
- bread-crumbs
- pepper
- sage
- onion
- butter
- water
- brains
- fine bread-crumbs
- salt
- flour
- hot fat
- brown flour
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take the head of a half-grown pig; clean and split it, taking out the brains and setting these aside in a cool place.
2. Parboil the head in salted water, drain off this, wipe the head dry, and wash all over with beaten egg.
3. Dredge thickly with bread-crumbs, seasoned with pepper, sage, and onion.
4. Roast, basting twice with butter and water; then with the liquor in which the head was boiled; at last with the gravy that runs from the meat.
5. Wash the brains in several waters until they are white; beat to a smooth paste, add one-quarter part fine bread-crumbs, pepper, and salt.
6. Make into balls, binding with a beaten egg; roll in flour and fry in hot fat to a light brown.
7. Arrange about the head when it is dished.
8. Skim the gravy left in the dripping-pan, thicken with brown flour, add the juice of a lemon, and boil up once.
9. Pour it over the head.
