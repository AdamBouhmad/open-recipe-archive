## Ingredients

- Honeycomb tripe
- One tablespoon finely chopped onion
- Two tablespoons butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut honeycomb tripe in pieces two inches long by one-half inch wide, having three cupfuls
2. Put in a pan and place in oven that water may be drawn out
3. Cook one tablespoon finely chopped onion in two tablespoons butter until slightly browned
4. Add tripe drained from water and cook five minutes
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and finely chopped parsley
