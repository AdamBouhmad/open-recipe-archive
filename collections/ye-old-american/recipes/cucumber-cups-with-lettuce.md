## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- lettuce leaves
- Sauce Tartare

## Directions

1. Pare cucumbers, cut in quarters crosswise, remove centres from pieces.
2. Arrange on lettuce leaves, and fill cups with Sauce Tartare.
