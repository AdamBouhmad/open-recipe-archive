## Ingredients

- large potatoes
- Thin White Sauce
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Select large potatoes, wash, pare, and soak
2. Shape in balls with a French vegetable cutter
3. Cook in boiling salted water until soft
4. drain, and to one pint potatoes add one cup Thin White Sauce
5. Turn into hot dish, and sprinkle with finely chopped parsley
