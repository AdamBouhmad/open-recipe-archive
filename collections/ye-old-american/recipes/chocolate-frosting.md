## Ingredients

- whites of four eggs
- three cups of powdered sugar
- nearly a cup of grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of four eggs a very little; they must not become white
2. Stir in the chocolate
3. Put in the sugar gradually, beating to mix it well
