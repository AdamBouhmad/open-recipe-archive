## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- ¾ cup sugar
- ⅔ tumbler currant jelly
- Ice
- 1 cup orange juice
- ½ cup lemon juice
- 2 bottles ginger ale
- ⅓ cup brandy

## Directions

1. Make a syrup by boiling sugar and water fifteen minutes
2. Add jelly, and, as soon as dissolved, add a piece of ice to cool mixture
3. Add fruit juices, ale, and brandy
4. Color red
5. Freeze to a mush
6. Serve in glasses and insert in each glass a small sprig of holly with berries
