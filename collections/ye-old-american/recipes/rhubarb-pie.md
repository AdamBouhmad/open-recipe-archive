## Ingredients

- Rhubarb stalks
- paste
- a large teacupful of sugar to a quart bowl of cut rhubarb
- a saltspoonful of salt
- nutmeg
- flour
- rich pie crust

## Directions

1. Cut the large stalks off where the leaves commence
2. Strip off the outside skin
3. Cut the stalks in pieces half an inch long
4. Line a pie dish with paste rolled rather thicker than a dollar piece
5. Put a layer of the rhubarb nearly an inch deep
6. Strew over with a saltspoonful of salt and a little nutmeg grated
7. Shake over a little flour
8. Cover with a rich pie crust
9. Cut a slit in the centre
10. Trim off the edge with a sharp knife
11. Bake in a quick oven until the pie loosens from the dish
