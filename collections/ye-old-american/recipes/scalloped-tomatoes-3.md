## Ingredients

- one can tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- sugar
- buttered cracker crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Remove contents from one can tomatoes and drain tomatoes from some of their liquor.
2. Season with salt, pepper, a few drops of onion juice, and sugar if preferred sweet.
3. Cover the bottom of a buttered baking-dish with buttered cracker crumbs, cover with tomatoes.
4. Sprinkle top thickly with buttered crumbs.
5. Bake in a hot oven until crumbs are brown.
