## Ingredients

- beef tenderloin
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- pork

## Directions

1. Wipe, remove fat, veins, and any tendinous portions; skewer in shape, and lard upper side with grain of meat, following directions for larding on page 23.
2. Place on a rack in small pan, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dredge with flour, and put in bottom of pan small pieces of pork.
3. Bake twenty to thirty minutes in hot oven, basting three times.
4. Take out skewer, remove meat to hot platter, and garnish with watercress.
5. Serve with Mushroom, Figaro, or Horseradish Sauce I.
