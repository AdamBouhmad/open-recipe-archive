## Ingredients

- One quart of rich milk or cream
- A cupful of wine
- Half a cupful of sugar
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put the sugar and wine into a bowl and the milk lukewarm in a separate vessel
2. When the sugar is dissolved in the wine, pour the milk in, holding it high
3. Pour it back and forth until it is frothy
4. Grate nutmeg over it
