## Ingredients

- 1 peck sound green tomatoes
- salt
- 2 dozen medium-sized onions
- 6 red peppers
- 1 quart cider vinegar
- whole spices
- black pepper
- cold, pure cider vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash and slice, without peeling, one peck of sound green tomatoes
2. Put them into a jar in layers with a slight sprinkling of salt between
3. This may be done over night; in the morning drain off the liquor
4. Peel and slice two dozen medium-sized onions
5. Chop six red peppers fine
6. Make some spiced vinegar by boiling for half an hour a quart of cider vinegar with whole spices in it
7. Take a porcelain kettle and place in it some of the sliced tomatoes, then some of the sliced onions
8. Shake in some black pepper and some of the chopped red peppers
9. Pour over some of the spiced vinegar
10. Repeat with the tomatoes, onions, etc., until the kettle is full
11. Cover with cold, pure cider vinegar and cook until tender, but not too soft
12. Turn into a jar well covered and set in a cool place
