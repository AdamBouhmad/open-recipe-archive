## Ingredients

- 10 ozs. almond paste
- 5 ozs. confectioners' sugar
- White 1 egg
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix same as Macaroons.
2. Dredge a board with sugar, knead mixture slightly, and shape in a long roll.
3. Pat, and roll one-fourth inch thick, using a rolling-pin.
4. After rolling the piece should be four inches wide.
5. Spread with frosting made of white of one egg and two-thirds cup confectioners' sugar beaten together until stiff enough to spread.
6. Cut in strips four inches long by three-fourths inch wide.
7. This must be quickly done, as a crust soon forms over frosting.
8. To accomplish this, use two knives, one placed through mixture where dividing line is to be made, and the other used to make a clean sharp cut on both sides of first knife.
9. Knives should be kept clean by wiping on a damp cloth.
10. Remove strips as soon as cut, to a tin sheet, greased with lard and then floured.
11. Bake twenty minutes on centre grate in a slow oven.
