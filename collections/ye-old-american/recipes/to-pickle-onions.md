## Ingredients

- white onions
- salt and water
- turmeric
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Get white onions that are not too large
2. Cut the stem close to the root with a sharp knife
3. Put them in a pot
4. Pour on boiling salt and water to cover them
5. Stop the pot closely
6. Let them stand a fortnight, changing the salt and water every three days
7. Stir them daily so those that float will not become soft
8. At the end of this time, take off the skin and outer shell
9. Put them in plain cold vinegar with a little turmeric
