## Ingredients

- confectioners' sugar
- raspberry jam
- French Cream
- cochineal coloring
- raspberry juice

## Directions

1. Stir enough confectioners' sugar into a teaspoonful of raspberry jam to form a thick paste
2. Roll it into balls between the palms of your hands
3. Put a lump of French Cream into a teacup and set it into a basin of boiling water, stirring it until it has melted
4. Then drop a few drops of cochineal coloring to make it a pale pink, or a few drops of raspberry juice, being careful not to add enough to prevent its hardening
5. Dip these little balls into the sugar cream, giving them two coats
6. Lay aside to harden
7. Remember to keep stirring the melted cream, or if not it will turn back to clear syrup
