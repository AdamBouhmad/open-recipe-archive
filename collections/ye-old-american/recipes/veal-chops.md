## Ingredients

- best end of a rack of veal
- white paper
- butter
- forcemeat
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Take the best end of a rack of veal, cut it in chops, with one bone in each
2. Leave the small end of the bone bare two inches
3. Beat them flat, and prepare them with eggs and crumbs, as the cutlets
4. Butter some half-sheets of white paper
5. Wrap one round each chop, skewer it well, leaving the bare bone out
6. Broil them till done, and take care the paper does not burn
7. Have nice white sauce in a boat
