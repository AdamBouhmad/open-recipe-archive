## Ingredients

- Chapon
- One head lettuce
- One cucumber

## Directions

1. Place a chapon in bottom of salad bowl.
2. Wash, drain, and dry one head lettuce, arrange in bowl.
3. Place between leaves one cucumber cut in thin slices.
4. Serve with French Dressing.
