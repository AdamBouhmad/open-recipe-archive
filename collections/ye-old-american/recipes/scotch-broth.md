## Ingredients

- three pounds mutton from fore-quarter
- three pints cold water
- one-half cup barley
- two tablespoons butter
- one-fourth cup carrot
- one-fourth cup turnip
- one-fourth cup onion

## Directions

1. Wipe mutton cut from fore-quarter
2. Cut lean meat in one-inch cubes
3. Put in kettle and cover with three pints cold water
4. Bring quickly to boiling-point and skim
5. Add barley which has been soaked in cold water over night
6. Simmer one and one-half hours or until meat is tender
7. Put bones in a second kettle, cover with cold water, heat slowly to boiling-point, skim, and boil one and one-half hours
8. Strain water from bones and add to meat
9. Fry five minutes in butter
10. Add carrot, turnip, and onion
