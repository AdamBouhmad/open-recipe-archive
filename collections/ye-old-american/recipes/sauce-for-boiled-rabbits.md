## Ingredients

- livers
- two eggs
- large spoonful of grated white bread
- broth
- sweet herbs
- two spoonsful of white wine
- one spoonsful of vinegar
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the livers and shred them very small
2. Chop two eggs not boiled very hard
3. Mix with a large spoonful of grated white bread, some broth, sweet herbs, two spoonsful of white wine, one of vinegar, and a little salt
4. Add some butter
5. Stir all together
6. Take care the butter does not oil
