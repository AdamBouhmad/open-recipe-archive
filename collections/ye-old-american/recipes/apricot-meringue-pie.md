## Ingredients

- canned apricots
- half a cup of sugar
- beaten yolk of an egg
- crust
- beaten white of an egg
- one tablespoonful of sugar
- pastry strips

## Directions

1. Cut the apricots fine and mix them with half a cup of sugar and the beaten yolk of an egg
2. Fill the crust and bake
3. Let it stand for two or three minutes
4. Cover with a meringue made of the beaten white of an egg and one tablespoonful of sugar
5. Set back in a slow oven until it turns a golden brown
