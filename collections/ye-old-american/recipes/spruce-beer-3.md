## Ingredients

- one ounce of hops per gallon of water
- a spoonful of ginger per gallon of water
- a pint of molasses or a pound of brown sugar
- half an ounce or less of the essence of spruce
- a teacupful of yeast

## Directions

1. Allow an ounce of hops and a spoonful of ginger to a gallon of water
2. When well boiled, strain it and put in a pint of molasses or a pound of brown sugar, and half an ounce or less of the essence of spruce
3. When cool, add a teacupful of yeast
4. Put into a clean tight cask and let it ferment for a day or two
5. Bottle it for use
