## Ingredients

- one ounce of the best tea
- a table spoonful of water
- a quart of boiling cream
- four tea-spoonsful of a strong infusion of rennet in water
- preserved fruit

## Directions

1. Put one ounce of the best tea in a pitcher, pour on it a table spoonful of water, and let it stand an hour to soften the leaves
2. Then put to it a quart of boiling cream, cover it close
3. In half an hour strain it
4. Add four tea-spoonsful of a strong infusion of rennet in water, stir it
5. Set it on some hot ashes, and cover it
6. When you find by cooling a little of it, that it will jelly, pour it into glasses, and garnish with thin bits of preserved fruit
