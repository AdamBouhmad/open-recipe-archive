## Ingredients

- cold fresh fish or salt codfish
- 1 pint milk
- sliced onion
- parsley
- butter (size of an egg)
- salt
- white pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls cornstarch or flour
- cold milk
- cracker or bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Pick any cold fresh fish or salt codfish left from dinner into fine bits, carefully removing all the bones
2. Take a pint of milk in a suitable dish and place it in a saucepan of boiling water
3. Put into it a few slices of onion cut very fine and a sprig of parsley minced fine
4. Add a piece of butter as large as an egg, a pinch of salt, a sprinkle of white pepper
5. Stir in two tablespoonfuls of cornstarch or flour rubbed in a little cold milk
6. Let all boil up and remove from the fire
7. Butter the sides and bottom of a dish you wish to serve it in
8. Put first a layer of the minced fish, then a layer of the cream
9. Sprinkle over that some cracker or bread crumbs
10. Put a layer of fish again and so on until the dish is full
11. Spread cracker or bread crumbs last on the top to prevent the milk from scorching
