## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit
- Pepper
- Salt
- Flour
- Butter or dripping
- Broth made of bits of veal or lamb
- 1 minced onion
- Great spoonful of walnut catsup
- Bunch of sweet herbs
- Pinch of cloves
- Pinch of allspice
- Half a teaspoonful of cayenne
- Browned flour
- Tablespoonful of butter
- Juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Cut off the head, joint, and lay in soak for an hour.
2. Season the pieces with pepper and salt, dredge with flour, and fry in butter or nice dripping until brown.
3. Take from the fat, lay in a saucepan, and cover with broth made of bits of veal or lamb.
4. Add a minced onion, a great spoonful of walnut catsup, a bunch of sweet herbs, a pinch of cloves and one of allspice, half a teaspoonful of cayenne.
5. Cover closely, and simmer for half an hour.
6. Lay the pieces of hare in order upon a hot dish and cover to keep warm.
7. Strain the gravy, return to the saucepan, thicken with browned flour, put in a tablespoonful of butter, squeeze in the juice of a lemon, pour over the rabbits, and send to table.
