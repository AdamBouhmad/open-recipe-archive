## Ingredients

- light dough
- peeled and sliced oranges
- white sugar
- grated orange peel

## Directions

1. Make a light dough the same as for apple dumplings
2. Roll it out into a long narrow sheet, about quarter of an inch thick
3. Spread thickly over it peeled and sliced oranges
4. Sprinkle it plentifully with white sugar
5. Scatter over all a teaspoonful or two of grated orange peel
6. Roll it up
7. Fold the edges well together to keep the juices from running out
8. Boil it in a floured cloth one hour and a half
9. Serve it with lemon sauce
