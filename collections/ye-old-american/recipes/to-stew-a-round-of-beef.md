## Ingredients

- a round of fresh beef
- cold water
- an onion
- a carrot
- a turnip
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- kitchen pepper (mixture of black pepper, cayenne, cinnamon, cloves, ginger and nutmeg)

## Directions

1. Trim off some pieces from a round of fresh beef and take out the bone and break it.
2. Put the bone and the trimmings into a pan with some cold water, and add an onion, a carrot, and a turnip all cut in pieces, and a bunch of sweet herbs.
3. Simmer them for an hour, and having skimmed it well, strain off the liquid.
4. Season the meat highly with what is called kitchen pepper, that is, a mixture, in equal quantities, of black pepper, or of cayenne, cinnamon, cloves, ginger and nutmeg, all finely powdered.
5. Fasten it with skewers, and tie it firmly round with tape.
6. Lay skewers in the bottom of the stew-pan; place the beef upon them, and then pour over it the gravy you have prepared from the bone and trimmings.
7. Simmer it about an hour and a half, and then turn the meat.
