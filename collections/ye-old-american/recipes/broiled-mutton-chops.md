## Ingredients

- loin of mutton
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the chops from a tenderloin of mutton, remove a portion of the fat, and trim them into a nice shape; slightly beat and level them.
2. Place the gridiron over a bright clear fire, rub the bars with a little fat, and lay on the chops.
3. While broiling frequently turn them, and in about eight minutes they will be done.
4. Season with pepper and salt, dish them on a very hot dish, rub a small piece of butter on each chop, and serve very hot and expeditiously.
