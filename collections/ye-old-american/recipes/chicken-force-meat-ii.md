## Ingredients

- ½ breast raw chicken
- White of 1 egg
- Salt
- Pepper
- Slight grating nutmeg
- Heavy cream

## Directions

1. Chop chicken finely, or force through a meat chopper
2. Pound in mortar, add gradually white of egg, and work until smooth
3. Add heavy cream slowly until of right consistency, which can only be determined by cooking a small ball in boiling salted water
4. Add seasonings, and rub through sieve
