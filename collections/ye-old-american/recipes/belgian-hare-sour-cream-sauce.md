## Ingredients

- A hare
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eight slices carrot, cut in small pieces
- One-half small onion
- Two tablespoons bacon fat
- One cup Brown Stock
- One cup heavy cream
- Juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Clean and split hare.
2. Lard back and hind legs, and season with salt and pepper.
3. Cook carrot and onion in bacon fat for five minutes.
4. Add Brown Stock and pour around hare in pan.
5. Bake forty-five minutes, basting often.
6. Add heavy cream and juice of lemon.
7. Cook fifteen minutes longer, and baste every five minutes.
8. Remove to serving dish, strain sauce, thicken, season with salt and pepper, and pour around hare.
