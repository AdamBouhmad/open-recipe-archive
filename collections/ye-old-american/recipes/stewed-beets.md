## Ingredients

- Beets
- butter
- flour
- boiled onion
- parsley
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil beets first
2. Scrape and slice them
3. Put them into a stew-pan with a piece of butter rolled in flour, some boiled onion and parsley chopped fine, and a little vinegar, salt and pepper
4. Set the pan on hot coals
5. Let the beets stew for a quarter of an hour
