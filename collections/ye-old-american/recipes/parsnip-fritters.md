## Ingredients

- three large parsnips
- two eggs
- one cup rich milk
- one tablespoonful butter
- one teaspoonful salt
- three tablespoonfuls flour

## Directions

1. Boil tender, mash smooth and fine, picking out the woody bits.
2. Beat the eggs light, stir in the mashed parsnips, beating hard; then the butter and salt, next the milk, lastly the salt.
3. Fry as fritters, or as griddle-cakes.
