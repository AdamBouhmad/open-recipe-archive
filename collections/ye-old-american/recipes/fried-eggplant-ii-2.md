## Ingredients

- an eggplant
- salt
- water
- pepper
- batter or flour, egg, and crumbs
- deep fat

## Directions

1. Pare an eggplant, cut in one-fourth inch slices, and soak over night in cold salted water.
2. Drain, let stand in cold water one-half hour, and drain again.
3. Dry between towels.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Dip in batter, or dip in flour, egg, and crumbs.
6. Fry in deep fat.
