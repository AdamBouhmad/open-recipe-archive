## Ingredients

- 4 quarts ripe berries
- 1 quart best cider vinegar
- 1 lb. white sugar
- 1 pint fine brandy

## Directions

1. Put the berries in a stone jar, pour the vinegar over them
2. Add the sugar, and pound the berries to a paste with a wooden pestle, or mash with a spoon
3. Let them stand in the sun four hours
4. Strain and squeeze out all the juice, and put in the brandy
5. Seal up in bottles; lay them on their sides in the cellar, and cover with sawdust
6. Stir two tablespoonfuls into a tumbler of ice-water when you wish to use it
