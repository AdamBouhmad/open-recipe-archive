## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup rice
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 cup canned tomatoes
- 1/2 cup cold cooked chicken cut in dice
- White Stock highly seasoned
- salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. Cook rice in boiling salted water
2. Drain, and pour over hot water to thoroughly rinse
3. Heat omelet pan
4. Add butter
5. As soon as butter is melted add rice
6. Cook three minutes
7. Add tomatoes, chicken, and enough stock to moisten
8. Cook five minutes
9. Season highly with salt and cayenne
10. If not rich enough, add more butter
