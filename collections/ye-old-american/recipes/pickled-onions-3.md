## Ingredients

- small onions
- salt and water
- hot spiced vinegar
- a tablespoonful of sweet oil

## Directions

1. Peel small onions until they are white
2. Scald them in salt and water until tender, then take them up, put them into wide-mouthed bottles, and pour over them hot spiced vinegar
3. when cold cork them close
4. Keep in a dry, dark place
5. A tablespoonful of sweet oil may be put in the bottles before the cork
