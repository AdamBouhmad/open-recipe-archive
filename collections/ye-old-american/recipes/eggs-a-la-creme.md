## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- crusts of bread
- stale bread, grated
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil twelve eggs just hard enough to allow you to cut them in slices
2. Cut some crusts of bread very thin
3. Put them in the bottom and round the sides of a moderately deep dish
4. Place the eggs in
5. Strew each layer with the stale bread grated, and some pepper and salt
