## Ingredients

- a large spoonful of yeast
- a little salt
- a quart of buckwheat meal
- cold water

## Directions

1. Put a large spoonful of yeast and a little salt, into a quart of buckwheat meal
2. Make it into a batter with cold water
3. Let it rise well, and bake it on a griddle
