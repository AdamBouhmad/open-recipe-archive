## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- ¾ cup macaroni broken in 1 inch pieces
- Salt and pepper
- Flour
- ½ cup buttered crumbs
- ¼ cup butter

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water until soft
2. Drain, and rinse with cold water
3. Put a layer in bottom of a buttered pudding-dish, cover with oysters, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dredge with flour, and dot over with one-half of the butter
4. Repeat, and cover with buttered crumbs
5. Bake twenty minutes in hot oven
