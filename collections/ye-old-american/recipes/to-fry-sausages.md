## Ingredients

- small piece of lard or butter
- sausages
- cold water

## Directions

1. Put a small piece of lard or butter into the frying pan
2. Prick the sausages with a fork and lay them in the melted grease
3. Keep moving them about, turning them frequently to prevent bursting
4. In ten or twelve minutes they will be sufficiently browned and cooked
5. Alternatively, cover them with cold water and let it come to the boiling point
6. Turn off the water and fry them
7. Or put them in a baking-pan in the oven, turning them once or twice
8. A pound will cook brown in ten minutes in a hot oven
