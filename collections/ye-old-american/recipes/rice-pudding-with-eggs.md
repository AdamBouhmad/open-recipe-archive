## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 4 eggs
- ½ cup rice
- ¾ cup sugar
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- Handful of raisins, seeded and cut in two

## Directions

1. Soak the rice in a pint of the milk an hour, then set the saucepan containing it where it will slowly heat to a boil.
2. Boil five minutes; remove and let it cool.
3. Beat the yolks, add the sugar and butter, the rice and the milk in which it was cooked, with the pint of unboiled; the beaten whites, and finally the raisins.
4. Grate nutmeg on the top, and bake three-quarters of an hour, or until the custard is well set and of a light brown.
5. Eat cold.
