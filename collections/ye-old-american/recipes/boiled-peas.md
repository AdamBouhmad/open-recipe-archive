## Ingredients

- Peas
- Cold water
- Salt
- Butter
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Remove peas from pods, cover with cold water, and let stand one-half hour.
2. Skim off undeveloped peas which rise to top of water, and drain remaining peas.
3. Cook until soft in a small quantity of boiling water, adding salt the last fifteen minutes of cooking.
4. Season with butter, salt, and pepper.
