## Ingredients

- Fine even bunches of currants
- White of egg
- Cold water
- Pulverized sugar

## Directions

1. Pick fine even bunches
2. Dip them, one at a time, into a mixture of frothed white of egg, and a very little cold water
3. Drain them until nearly dry, and roll in pulverized sugar
4. Repeat the dip in the sugar once or twice, and lay them upon white paper to dry
