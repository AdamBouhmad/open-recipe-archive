## Ingredients

- a pound of the best brown sugar
- two quarts of West India molasses
- the grated rind and the juice of three lemons
- the juice and rind of three large oranges
- blanched almonds
- ground-nuts

## Directions

1. Mix a pound of the best brown sugar with two quarts of West India molasses, which must be perfectly sweet
2. Boil it in a preserving kettle over a moderate fire for three hours
3. Skim it well, and stir it frequently after the scum has ceased to rise
4. Take care that it does not burn
5. Have ready the grated rind and the juice of three lemons
6. Stir them into the molasses after it has boiled about two hours and a half, or you may substitute the juice and rind of three large oranges
7. The mixture should boil at least three hours, that it may be crisp and brittle when cold
8. Take it off the fire when it ceases boiling
9. Have ready a square tin pan that should be buttered
10. Put the mixture into it, and set it away to cool
11. You may make molasses candy with almonds blanched and slit into pieces, stirring them in by degrees after the mixture has boiled two hours and a half
12. Or you may blanch a quart of ground-nuts and put them in instead of the almonds
