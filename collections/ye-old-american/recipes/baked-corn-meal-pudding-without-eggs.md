## Ingredients

- a large cupful of yellow meal
- a teacupful of cooking molasses
- a quart of boiling milk
- some salt
- a large tablespoonful of powdered ginger
- a cupful of finely-chopped suet or a piece of butter the size of an egg
- a pint of cold milk

## Directions

1. Take a large cupful of yellow meal and a teacupful of cooking molasses and beat them well together
2. Add to them a quart of boiling milk, some salt and a large tablespoonful of powdered ginger
3. Add a cupful of finely-chopped suet or a piece of butter the size of an egg
4. Butter a brown earthen pan and turn the pudding in, let it stand until it thickens
5. As you put it into the oven, turn over it a pint of cold milk, but do not stir it, as this makes the jelly
6. Bake three hours
7. Serve warm with hard sauce
