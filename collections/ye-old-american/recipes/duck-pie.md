## Ingredients

- cold roast ducks
- water
- pastry

## Directions

1. Cut all the meat from cold roast ducks
2. Put the bones and stuffing into cold water; cover them and let boil
3. Put the meat into a deep dish
4. Pour on enough of the stock made from the bones to moisten
5. Cover with pastry slit in the centre with a knife
6. Bake a light brown
