## Ingredients

- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 tablespoons grated cheese
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1/4 teaspoon mustard
- salt
- cayenne
- melted butter
- 1 cup White Sauce

## Directions

1. Cut four hard-boiled eggs in halves crosswise; remove yolks, mash, and add two tablespoons grated cheese, one teaspoon vinegar, one-fourth teaspoon mustard, and salt and cayenne to taste.
2. Add enough melted butter to make mixture of the right consistency to shape.
3. Make in balls size of original yolks, and refill whites.
4. Arrange on a serving dish, pour around one cup White Sauce, cover, and reheat.
