## Ingredients

- four or five parsnips
- a teaspoonful of wheat flour
- a beaten egg
- a tablespoonful of lard or beef drippings
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil four or five parsnips; when tender take off the skin and mash them fine.
2. Add to them a teaspoonful of wheat flour and a beaten egg.
3. Put a tablespoonful of lard or beef drippings in a frying pan over the fire, add to it a saltspoonful of salt.
4. When boiling hot put in the parsnips; make it in small cakes with a spoon.
5. When one side is a delicate brown turn the other.
6. When both are done take them on a dish, put a very little of the fat in which they were fried over and serve hot.
