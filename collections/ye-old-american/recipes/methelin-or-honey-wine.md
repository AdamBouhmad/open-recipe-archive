## Ingredients

- new honey, strained
- spring water
- whole egg
- white Jamaica ginger, bruised - two ounces per fifteen gallons
- cloves and mace - one ounce per fifteen gallons
- cinnamon, bruised - one and one-half ounces per fifteen gallons
- yeast

## Directions

1. Add spring water to strained honey
2. Put a whole egg into it
3. Boil this liquor till the egg swims above the liquor
4. Strain and pour it in a cask
5. To every fifteen gallons add two ounces of white Jamaica ginger bruised, one ounce of cloves and mace, and one and one-half ounces of cinnamon, all bruised together and tied up in a muslin bag
6. Accelerate the fermentation with yeast
7. When worked sufficiently, bung up
8. In six weeks draw off into bottles
