## Ingredients

- 1 lb. powdered sugar
- 1 lb. butter
- 1 lb. flour
- 12 eggs
- 1 lb. currants well washed and dredged
- 1 lb. raisins seeded and chopped
- ½ lb. citron cut into slips
- 1 tablespoonful cinnamon
- 2 teaspoonfuls nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful cloves
- 1 wineglass brandy

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. Add the beaten yolks of the eggs
3. Stir all well together before putting in half of the flour
4. Add the spice next
5. Stir in the whipped whites alternately with the rest of the flour
6. Lastly add the brandy
7. Bake at least two hours in deep tins lined with well-buttered paper
8. Lay the icing on stiff and thickly
9. Test the cakes well and be sure they are quite done before taking them from the oven
