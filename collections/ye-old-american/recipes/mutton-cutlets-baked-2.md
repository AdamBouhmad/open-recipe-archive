## Ingredients

- mutton cutlets
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare them the same as for frying
2. Lay them in a dripping-pan with a very little water at the bottom
3. Bake quickly, and baste often with butter and water
4. Make a little brown gravy and turn over them when they are served
