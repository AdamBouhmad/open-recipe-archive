## Ingredients

- pair of fine fowls
- pepper
- light biscuit dough
- butter
- cold ham
- potatoes
- water, a quart
- flour

## Directions

1. Take a pair of fine fowls, cut them up, wash the pieces, and season with pepper only
2. Make a light biscuit dough, and plenty of it
3. Roll out the dough not very thin, and cut most of it into long squares
4. Butter the sides of a pot, and line them with dough nearly to the top
5. Lay slices of cold ham at the bottom of the pot, and then the pieces of fowl, interspersed all through with squares of dough and potatoes, pared and quartered
6. Pour in a quart of water
7. Cover the whole with a lid of dough, having a slit in the centre, through which the gravy will bubble up
8. Boil it steadily for two hours
9. Half an hour before you take it up, put in through the hole in the centre of the crust some bits of butter rolled in flour, to thicken the gravy
10. When done, put the pie on a large dish, and pour the gravy over it
