## Ingredients

- crackers
- raisins
- citron
- milk and water
- rich sauce

## Directions

1. Soak and split some crackers
2. Lay the surface over with raisins and citron
3. Put the halves together
4. Tie them in a bag
5. Boil fifteen minutes in milk and water
6. Serve with rich sauce
