## Ingredients

- cold underdone mutton or lamb
- gravy or broth
- butter
- warm water
- catsup
- pepper
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Cut some slices of cold underdone mutton or lamb; put them in a frying-pan with enough gravy or broth to cover them.
2. Or, if you have neither of them, make a gravy of butter, warm water, and catsup.
3. Heat to boiling, and stir in pepper and a great spoonful of currant jelly.
4. Send to table in a chafing-dish, with the gravy poured about the meat.
