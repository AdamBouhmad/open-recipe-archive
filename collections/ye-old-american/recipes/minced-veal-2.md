## Ingredients

- cold roast veal fillet, shoulder, or breast
- cold water
- sweet herbs
- pepper
- salt
- bacon or ham-bone
- flour
- butter
- cream or milk
- lemon juice
- Sherry or Madeira wine
- buttered toast
- poached egg

## Directions

1. Cut all meat from the bones of cold roast veal
2. Put the bones, outside slices, and gristly pieces into a saucepan with a cup of cold water, sweet herbs, pepper, and salt
3. If using bacon or ham-bone, add this and omit the salt
4. Stew all together for an hour
5. Strain and thicken with flour
6. Return to the fire and boil five minutes longer, stirring in a tablespoonful of butter
7. Meanwhile, mince the cold veal
8. When the gravy is ready, put the mince in a little at a time
9. Let it almost boil, then add two tablespoonfuls of cream or three of milk, stirring all the while
10. Squeeze in the juice of a lemon
11. Add half a glass of Sherry or Madeira wine a moment later
12. Heap the mince-meat into a shape in a flat dish or chafing-dish
13. Lay triangles of buttered toast about the base of the mound
14. Top with a poached egg
