## Ingredients

- three heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- three cups of Graham flour
- one cup of white flour
- a large teaspoonful of salt
- half a cup of sugar
- milk or water
- butter

## Directions

1. Stir together three heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder, three cups of Graham flour and one cup of white flour
2. add a large teaspoonful of salt and half a cup of sugar
3. Mix all thoroughly with milk or water into as stiff a batter as can be stirred with a spoon
4. If water is used, a lump of butter as large as a walnut may be melted and stirred into it
5. Bake immediately in well-greased pans
