## Ingredients

- two drachms oil of lemon
- two drachms oil of bergamot
- two drachms oil of lavender
- one drachm oil of cedrat
- three drachms tincture of benzoin
- ten drops neroli
- ten drops ambergris
- two drops attar of roses
- a pint of spirits of wine

## Directions

1. Mix together in a new phial the oils and essences
2. Pour the mixture into a pint of spirits of wine
3. Cork and shake the bottle and set it away for use
