## Ingredients

- Three cups milk
- Two cups sugar
- One cup yeast
- Two cups sugar
- Two cups butter
- Three eggs
- Half a nutmeg
- One tablespoonful cinnamon
- One pound raisins
- A gill of brandy

## Directions

1. Stir three cups milk, two cups sugar, and one cup yeast to a batter and let stand over night
2. In the morning add two cups sugar, two cups butter, three eggs, half a nutmeg, one tablespoonful cinnamon, one pound raisins, and a gill of brandy
3. Dissolve a half-teaspoonful of soda in a tablespoonful of milk in the morning
4. Pour into greased pans and let rise until quite light before baking
5. Bake until done
