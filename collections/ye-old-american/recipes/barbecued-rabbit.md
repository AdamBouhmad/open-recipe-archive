## Ingredients

- 1 plump young rabbit
- Salted water
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of made mustard
- Crisped parsley

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the rabbit, which must be plump and young, and having opened it all the way on the under-side, lay it flat, with a small plate or saucer to keep it down, in salted water for half an hour.
2. Wipe dry and broil whole, with the exception of the head, when you have gashed across the back-bone in eight or ten places that the heat may penetrate this, the thickest part.
3. Your fire should be hot and clear, the rabbit turned often.
4. When browned and tender, lay upon a very hot dish, pepper and salt and butter profusely, turning the rabbit over and over to soak up the melted butter.
5. Cover and set in the oven for five minutes.
6. Heat in a tin cup two tablespoonfuls of vinegar seasoned with one of made mustard.
7. Anoint the hot rabbit well with this, cover and send to table garnished with crisped parsley.
