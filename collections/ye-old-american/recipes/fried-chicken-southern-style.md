## Ingredients

- two young chickens
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- one pound fat salt pork
- White Sauce
- half milk
- half cream

## Directions

1. Clean, singe, and cut in pieces for serving, two young chickens
2. Plunge in cold water, drain but do not wipe
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and coat thickly with flour, having as much flour adhere to chicken as possible
4. Try out one pound fat salt pork cut in pieces, and cook chicken slowly in fat until tender and well browned
5. Serve with White Sauce made of half milk and half cream
