## Ingredients

- 2½ cups powdered sugar
- ¾ cup of butter
- 1 cup sweet milk
- 3 cups flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 lemon, juice and rind
- 1 small teaspoonful soda
- whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Bake in a square or oblong tin, and frost with whites of two eggs beaten stiff with powdered sugar.
