## Ingredients

- two pounds beef
- one pound mutton
- chicken or half a pullet
- small piece of pork
- water
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- two or three apples or pears
- tomatoes
- cimblins
- mint
- lima beans
- snaps
- celery tops

## Directions

1. Take two pounds beef, one pound mutton, a chicken, or half a pullet, and a small piece of pork
2. Put them into a pot with very little water
3. Set it on the fire at ten o'clock to stew gently
4. Sprinkle over it an onion chopped small, some pepper and salt, before you pour in the water
5. At half after twelve, put into the pot two or three apples or pears, peeled and cut in two
6. Add tomatoes with the skin taken off
7. Add cimblins cut in pieces
8. Add a handful of mint chopped
9. Add lima beans and snaps and any kind of vegetable you like
10. Let them all stew together till three o'clock
11. Add some cellery tops cut small at half after two
