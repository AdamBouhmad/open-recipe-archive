## Ingredients

- potatoes
- Maître d'Hôtel Butter

## Directions

1. Wash, pare, and shape potatoes in balls, using a French vegetable cutter, or cut potatoes in one-half inch cubes
2. There should be two cups
3. Soak fifteen minutes in cold water
4. cook in boiling salted water to cover until soft
5. Drain, and add Maître d'Hôtel Butter
