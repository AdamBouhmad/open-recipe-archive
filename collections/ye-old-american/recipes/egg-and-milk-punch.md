## Ingredients

- 1 tumbler of milk, well sweetened
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoonfuls best brandy

## Directions

1. Beat an egg very light with the sugar.
2. Stir the egg mixture in before the brandy is added.
3. Give very cold with ice.
