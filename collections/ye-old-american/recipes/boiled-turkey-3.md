## Ingredients

- oyster stuffing
- salt
- salt pork or bacon or ham

## Directions

1. Prepare as you would for baking or roasting
2. Fill with an oyster stuffing
3. Tie the legs and wings close to the body
4. Place in salted boiling water with the breast downward
5. Skim it often and boil about two hours, but not till the skin breaks
6. Serve with oyster or celery sauce
7. Boil a nicely pickled piece of salt pork and serve a thin slice to each plate
