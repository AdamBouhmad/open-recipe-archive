## Ingredients

- six bananas
- two tablespoons melted butter
- one-third cup sugar
- two tablespoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove skins from six bananas and cut in halves lengthwise.
2. Put in a shallow granite pan or on an old platter.
3. Mix two tablespoons melted butter, one-third cup sugar, and two tablespoons lemon juice.
4. Baste bananas with one-half the mixture.
5. Bake twenty minutes in a slow oven, basting during baking with remaining mixture.
