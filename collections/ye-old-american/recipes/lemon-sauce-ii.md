## Ingredients

- ½ cup sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 tablespoon corn-starch or 1½ tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1½ tablespoons lemon juice
- few gratings nutmeg
- few grains salt

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and corn-starch, add water gradually, stirring constantly; boil five minutes, remove from fire, add butter, lemon juice, and nutmeg.
