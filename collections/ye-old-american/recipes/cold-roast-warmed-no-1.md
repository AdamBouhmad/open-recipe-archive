## Ingredients

- remains of a cold roast
- butter or drippings
- onion
- meat gravy
- flour
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut from the remains of a cold roast the lean meat from the bones into small, thin slices.
2. Put over the fire a frying pan containing a spoonful of butter or drippings.
3. Cut up a quarter of an onion and fry it brown, then remove the onion.
4. Add the meat gravy left from the day before, and if not thick enough add a little flour; salt and pepper.
5. Turn the pieces of meat into this and let them simmer a few minutes.
6. Serve hot.
