## Ingredients

- one cupful of mashed ripe currants
- one cupful of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of water
- one tablespoonful of flour
- yolks of two eggs
- beaten whites of two eggs
- two tablespoonfuls powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one cupful of mashed ripe currants with one cupful of sugar, two tablespoonfuls of water, one tablespoonful of flour beaten with the yolks of two eggs
2. Bake
3. Frost the top with the beaten whites of the eggs and two tablespoonfuls powdered sugar
4. Brown in oven
