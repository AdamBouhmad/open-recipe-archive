## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 sliced onion
- 1½ cups mushroom caps finely chopped
- 1 small parboiled sweetbread, finely chopped
- 1 cup White Sauce II
- ¼ cup stale bread crumbs
- 1 red pepper chopped
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Yolks 2 eggs, well beaten
- Whites 2 eggs, beaten until stiff

## Directions

1. Cook butter with sliced onion five minutes.
2. Add mushroom caps finely chopped, and small parboiled sweetbread, finely chopped.
3. Add White Sauce II, stale bread crumbs, red pepper chopped, salt, yolks of eggs, well beaten, and whites of eggs, beaten until stiff.
4. Fill buttered timbale moulds, set in pan of hot water, cover with buttered paper, and bake fifteen minutes.
5. Remove to serving dish and pour around Mushroom Sauce.
