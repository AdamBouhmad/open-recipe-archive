## Ingredients

- freshened codfish
- onion
- butter
- water
- tomatoes
- pepper
- dipped toast

## Directions

1. Take a teacupful of freshened codfish picked up fine.
2. Fry a sliced onion in a tablespoonful of butter; when it has turned a light brown, put in the fish with water enough to cover it.
3. Add half a can of tomatoes, or half a dozen of fresh ones.
4. Cook all nearly an hour, seasoning with a little pepper.
5. Serve on slices of dipped toast, hot.
