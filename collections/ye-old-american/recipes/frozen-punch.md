## Ingredients

- twelve fine lemons
- a gallon of water
- two pounds of loaf-sugar
- half a quart of rum or whiskey

## Directions

1. Make as for punch, omitting one half of the rum or whiskey
2. Put it into an ice-cream freezer, shaking or stirring it all the time
3. When it is frozen, send it round immediately, in small glasses with a tea-spoon for each
