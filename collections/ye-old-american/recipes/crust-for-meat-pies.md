## Ingredients

- 1 quart of flour
- 3 tablespoonfuls of lard
- 2½ cups milk
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- hot water
- 2 teaspoonfuls of cream-tartar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Wet the soda with hot water, and stir into the milk
2. Sift the cream-tartar into the dry flour
3. Work up very lightly and quickly, and do not get too stiff
4. If you can get prepared flour, omit the soda and cream-tartar
