## Ingredients

- 1 gallon of water
- 1 pound of the best white sugar
- 1 jill or more of strong yeast

## Directions

1. To every gallon of water allow a pound of the best white sugar, and a jill or more of strong yeast
2. Mix the sugar and water together
3. Boil and skim it till the scum ceases to rise
4. Pour it into a tub
5. When it cools to lukewarm heat, put into it the yeast spread on pieces of toast
6. Let it work two days
7. Put it into an iron-hooped cask
8. Set it in a sunny place for five months, leaving the bung loose, but keeping the bung-hole covered
9. In five months it will be good clear vinegar
10. Bottle it for use
