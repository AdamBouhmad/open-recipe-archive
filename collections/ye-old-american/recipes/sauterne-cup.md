## Ingredients

- 1 quart soda water
- 2 cups Sauterne wine
- Rind ½ orange
- Rind ½ lemon
- 2 tablespoons Orange Curaçoa
- ½ cup sugar (scant)
- Mint leaves
- Few slices orange
- 12 strawberries

## Directions

1. Add Curaçoa to rind of fruit and sugar; cover, and let stand two hours.
2. Add Sauterne, strain, and stand on ice to chill.
3. Add chilled soda water, mint leaves, slices of orange, and strawberries.
