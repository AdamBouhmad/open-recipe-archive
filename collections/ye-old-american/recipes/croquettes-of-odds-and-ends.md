## Ingredients

- scraps or bits of good food
- one raw egg
- flour
- butter
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Chop well any scraps or bits of good food such as frizzled beef and cream, lean meat of mutton chops, minced beef, cold hard-boiled eggs, cold chopped potato, mashed potato, poultry, and crumbs of bread
2. Season the chopped mixture
3. Mix with one raw egg, a little flour and butter, and boiling water
4. Make into round cakes, thick like fish-balls
5. Brown well with butter in a frying pan or on a griddle
6. Serve on small squares of buttered toast with cold celery if in season
