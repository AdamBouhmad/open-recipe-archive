## Ingredients

- Plain paste
- Sliced peaches
- Sugar
- Whipped cream
- Peach leaves

## Directions

1. Bake plain paste over inverted patty pans.
2. Roll paste one-eighth inch thick, and cut in strips one-fourth inch wide.
3. Twist strips in pairs and bake over a one-fourth pound baking-powder box, thus making handles.
4. Fill cases with sliced peaches sprinkled generously with sugar, insert handles, garnish with whipped cream and peach leaves.
