## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cream
- 4 tablespoonfuls powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix all well together, stirring until the sugar is dissolved.
2. Eat with jam puddings, queen of all puddings, Alice's pudding, and peach roley-poley.
