## Ingredients

- quarter of a pound of butter
- large table-spoonful of flour
- chopped parsley
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- gill of cream
- grated bread

## Directions

1. Put a quarter of a pound of butter with a large table-spoonful of flour rubbed well into it in a sauce pan
2. Add some chopped parsley, a little onion, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
3. Add a gill of cream
4. Stir it over the fire until it begins to boil
5. Pour it over the eggs
6. Cover the top with grated bread
7. Set it in a Dutch oven with a heated top
8. When a light brown, send it to table
