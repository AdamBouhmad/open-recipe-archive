## Ingredients

- fresh honeycomb tripe
- fine cracker dust
- olive oil or melted butter
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wipe tripe as dry as possible, dip in fine cracker dust and olive oil or melted butter, draining off all fat that is possible, and again dip in cracker dust.
2. Place in a greased broiler and broil five minutes, cooking smooth side of tripe the first three minutes.
3. Place on a hot platter, honeycomb side up, spread with butter, and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Broiled tripe is at its best when cooked over a charcoal fire.
