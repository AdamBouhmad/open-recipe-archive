## Ingredients

- grated cocoanut
- French candy
- confectioners' sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Take two tablespoonfuls of grated cocoanut and half as much French candy
2. Work them both together with your hand till the cocoanut is all well mixed in it
3. If you choose, you can add a drop of vanilla
4. If too soft to work into balls, add confectioners' sugar to stiffen
5. Make into balls the size of hazelnuts and dip twice, as in the foregoing recipes, flavoring the melted French Cream with vanilla
