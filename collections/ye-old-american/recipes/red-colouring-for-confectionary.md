## Ingredients

- twenty grains of cochineal
- fifteen grains of cream of tartar
- a piece of alum the size of a cherry stone
- a jill of soft water

## Directions

1. Take twenty grains of cochineal, and fifteen grains of cream of tartar finely powdered; add to them a piece of alum the size of a cherry stone.
2. Boil them with a jill of soft water, in an earthen vessel, slowly, for half an hour.
3. Strain it through muslin, and keep it tightly corked in a phial.
