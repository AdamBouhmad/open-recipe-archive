## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- ½ cup flour
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- ¾ teaspoon mustard
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- 1½ cups scalded milk
- 1 can Kornlet
- 1 egg
- 3 teaspoons Worcestershire Sauce
- 1 cup buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add flour mixed with dry seasonings, and pour on gradually the milk.
2. Add Kornlet, egg slightly beaten, and Worcestershire Sauce.
3. Pour into a buttered baking-dish, cover with crumbs, and bake until crumbs are brown.
