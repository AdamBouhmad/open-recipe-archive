## Ingredients

- one pint of oysters
- one teacup of milk or cream
- two tablespoonfuls of cold butter
- a pinch of cayenne and salt
- one tablespoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Heat oysters in their own liquor long enough to come to a boil, or until they begin to ruffle
2. Skim out the oysters into a warm dish
3. Put into the liquor a teacup of milk or cream, two tablespoonfuls of cold butter, a pinch of cayenne and salt
4. Thicken with a tablespoonful of flour stirred to a paste
5. Boil up and then add the oysters
