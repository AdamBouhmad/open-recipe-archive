## Ingredients

- three pints of cold water
- a quart of sifted Indian meal
- half a pint of wheat flour
- a small tea-spoonful of salt
- butter
- molasses

## Directions

1. Pour into a pan three pints of cold water, and stir gradually into it a quart of sifted Indian meal which has been mixed with half a pint of wheat flour, and a small tea-spoonful of salt.
2. Give it a hard stirring at the last.
3. Have ready a hot griddle, and bake the batter immediately, in cakes about the size of a saucer.
4. Send them to table piled evenly, but not cut.
5. Eat them with butter or molasses.
