## Ingredients

- one can corn
- one cup flour
- one teaspoon baking powder
- two teaspoons salt
- one-fourth teaspoon paprika
- two eggs
- fresh hot lard

## Directions

1. Chop corn.
2. Mix and sift dry ingredients.
3. Add yolks of eggs beaten until thick.
4. Fold in whites of eggs beaten stiff.
5. Cook in a frying-pan in fresh hot lard.
6. Drain on paper.
