## Ingredients

- a quart of ripe raspberries, mashed
- a quart of good cider vinegar
- one pound of white sugar
- a pint of good brandy

## Directions

1. Turn over a quart of ripe raspberries, mashed, a quart of good cider vinegar
2. Add one pound of white sugar
3. Mix well
4. Let stand in the sun four hours
5. Strain it and squeeze out the juice
6. Put in a pint of good brandy
7. Seal it up in bottles, air-tight
8. Lay them on their sides in the cellar
9. Cover them with sawdust
