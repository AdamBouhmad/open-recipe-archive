## Ingredients

- beef
- salt
- saltpetre
- 5 gallons of water
- 1 gallon of salt
- 4 ounces saltpetre
- 1½ lb. brown sugar

## Directions

1. Rub each piece of beef well with salt mixed with one-tenth part of saltpetre, until the salt lies dry upon the surface
2. Put aside in a cold place for twenty-four hours
3. Repeat the process, rubbing in the mixture very thoroughly
4. Put away again until the next day
5. Boil the brine of 5 gallons of water, 1 gallon of salt, 4 ounces saltpetre, and 1½ lb. brown sugar for ten minutes
6. Let it get perfectly cold
7. Pour over the beef, having wiped the latter entirely dry
8. Examine the pickle from time to time to see if it keeps well
9. If not, take out the meat without delay, wipe it, and rub in dry salt, covering it well until you can prepare new and stronger brine
