## Ingredients

- onions
- equal quantities of butter and lard or nice drippings
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and slice them
2. Fry them brown in equal quantities of butter and lard or nice drippings
3. Cover until partly soft
4. Remove the cover and brown them
5. Salt and pepper
