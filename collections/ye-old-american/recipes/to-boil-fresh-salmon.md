## Ingredients

- fresh salmon
- salt
- cold water

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, handling it as little as possible, and cutting it open no more than is absolutely necessary
2. Place it on the strainer of a large fish-kettle and fill it up with cold water
3. Throw in a handful of salt
4. Let it boil slowly
5. Allow a quarter of an hour to each pound
6. Skim it well or the colour will be bad
7. Try it with a fork to determine when it is completely boiled
8. Lift up the strainer when it is completely boiled
