## Ingredients

- tongue
- brown aspic
- cooked carrot
- turnip
- beet
- parsley
- aspic jelly mixture

## Directions

1. Cook a tongue according to directions on page 210.
2. After removing skin and roots, run a skewer through tip of tongue and fleshy part, thus keeping tongue in shape.
3. When cool, remove skewer.
4. Put a round pan in ice-water, cover bottom with brown aspic, and when firm decorate with cooked carrot, turnip, beet cut in fancy shapes, and parsley.
5. Cover with aspic jelly mixture, adding it by spoonfuls so as not to disarrange vegetables.
6. When this layer of mixture is firm, put in tongue, adding gradually remaining mixture as in Tomatoes in Aspic.
