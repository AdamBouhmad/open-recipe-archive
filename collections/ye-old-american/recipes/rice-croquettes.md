## Ingredients

- half a cup rice
- 1 pint milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 3 eggs
- grated lemon-peel
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter
- saltspoonful salt
- warm water
- flour
- beaten egg
- fine cracker-crumbs
- nice lard

## Directions

1. Soak the rice three hours in warm water enough to cover it.
2. Drain almost dry and pour in the milk.
3. Stew in a farina-kettle, or one saucepan set in another of hot water, until the rice is very tender.
4. Add the sugar, butter and salt, and simmer ten minutes.
5. Whisk the eggs to a froth and add cautiously, taking the saucepan from the fire while you whip them into the mixture.
6. Return to the range or stove and stir while they thicken, not allowing them to boil.
7. Remove the saucepan and add the grated lemon-peel.
8. Turn out upon a well-greased dish to cool.
9. When cold and stiff, flour your hands and roll into oval or pear-shaped balls.
10. Dip in beaten egg, then in fine cracker-crumbs.
11. Fry in nice lard.
