## Ingredients

- pickerel
- hot water
- butter
- sweet cream or rich milk
- parsley
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Carefully clean and wipe the fish, and lay in a dripping pan with enough hot water to prevent scorching
2. Use a perforated sheet of tin, fitting loosely, or several muffin rings to keep it off the bottom
3. Lay it in a circle on its belly, head and tail touching, and tied
4. Bake slowly, basting often with butter and water
5. Have ready a cup of sweet cream or rich milk to which a few spoons of hot water has been added
6. Stir in two large spoons of melted butter and a little chopped parsley
7. Heat all by setting the cup in boiling water
8. Add the gravy from the dripping-pan, and let it boil up once
9. Place the fish in a hot dish and pour over it the sauce
10. Alternatively, an egg sauce may be made with drawn butter; stir in the yolk of an egg quickly, and then a teaspoon of chopped parsley
