## Ingredients

- salmon steaks
- cloth
- flour
- butter
- gridiron
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Dry well with a cloth, and dredge with flour, and lay them upon a well-buttered gridiron, over clear hot coals.
2. Turn with a broad-bladed knife slipped beneath, and a flat wire egg-beater above, lest the steak should break.
3. When done to a light brown, lay in a hot dish, butter each steak, seasoning with salt and pepper, cover closely, and serve.
