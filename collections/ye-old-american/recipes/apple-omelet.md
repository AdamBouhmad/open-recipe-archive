## Ingredients

- nine large, tart apples
- four eggs
- one cup of sugar
- one tablespoonful of butter
- cinnamon or other spices

## Directions

1. Take nine large, tart apples, four eggs, one cup of sugar, one tablespoonful of butter
2. Add cinnamon or other spices to suit your taste
3. Stew the apples till they are very soft
4. Mash them so that there will be no lumps
5. Add the butter and sugar while they are still warm
6. Let them cool before putting in the beaten eggs
7. Bake this till it is brown
8. You may put it all in a shallow pudding-dish or in two tin plates to bake
