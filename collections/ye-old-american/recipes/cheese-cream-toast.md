## Ingredients

- stale bread
- grated cheese
- a pint of milk
- two tablespoonfuls of plain flour
- salt and butter

## Directions

1. Toast the slices and cover them slightly with grated cheese.
2. Make a cream for ten slices out of a pint of milk and two tablespoonfuls of plain flour.
3. The milk should be boiling, and the flour mixed in a little cold water before stirring in.
4. When the cream is nicely cooked, season with salt and butter.
5. Set the toast and cheese in the oven for three or four minutes and then pour the cream over them.
