## Ingredients

- one can apricots
- Custard Soufflé mixture

## Directions

1. Drain and reserve syrup from one can apricots and cut fruit into quarters
2. Put closely together on bottom of a buttered baking-dish
3. Pour over Custard Soufflé mixture
4. Bake from thirty-five to forty minutes in a slow oven
5. Serve with apricot syrup and whipped cream sweetened and flavored with vanilla or vanilla ice cream
