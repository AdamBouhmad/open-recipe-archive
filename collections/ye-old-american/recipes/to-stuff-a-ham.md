## Ingredients

- well smoked ham
- parsley
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take a well smoked ham, wash it very clean
2. Make incisions all over the top two inches deep
3. Stuff them quite full with parsley chopped small and some pepper
4. Boil the ham sufficiently
5. Do not take off the skin
6. It must be eaten cold
