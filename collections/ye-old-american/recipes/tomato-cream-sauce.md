## Ingredients

- ½ can tomatoes
- Sprig of thyme
- 1 stalk celery
- 1 slice onion
- Bit of bay leaf
- 1 cup White Sauce I
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- ¼ teaspoon soda

## Directions

1. Cook tomatoes twenty minutes with seasonings; rub through a strainer, add soda, then White Sauce.
2. Serve with Baked Fish or Lobster Cutlets.
