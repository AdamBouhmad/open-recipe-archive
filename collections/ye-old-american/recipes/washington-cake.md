## Ingredients

- 1 pound butter
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 pound flour
- 6 eggs
- 1 pint rich milk or cream
- 1 glass wine
- 1 glass brandy
- 1 powdered nutmeg
- 1 table-spoonful powdered cinnamon
- 1 small tea-spoonful soda or sal-aratus

## Directions

1. Stir together a pound of butter and a pound of sugar
2. Sift into another pan a pound of flour
3. Beat six eggs very light, and stir them into the butter and sugar, alternately with the flour and a pint of rich milk or cream
4. Add a glass of wine, a glass of brandy, a powdered nutmeg, and a table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon
5. Stir in a small tea-spoonful of soda, or sal-aratus, that has been melted in tepid water
6. Stir the whole very hard; put it into a buttered tin pan
7. Bake it in a brisk oven
