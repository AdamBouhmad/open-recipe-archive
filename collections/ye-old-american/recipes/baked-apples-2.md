## Ingredients

- sweet apples (Campfields or Pound Sweets)
- water
- powdered sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Cut out the blossom end of sweet apples with a sharp penknife; wash, but do not pare them
2. Pack them in a large pudding-dish
3. Pour a cupful of water in the bottom, cover closely with another dish or pan
4. Set in a moderate oven, and steam until tender all through
5. Pour the liquor over them while hot, and repeat this as they cool
6. Set on the ice several hours before tea
7. Transfer them to a glass dish, pouring the juice over them again
8. Eat with powdered sugar and cream
