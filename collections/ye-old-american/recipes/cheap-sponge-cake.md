## Ingredients

- Yolks 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon hot water
- 1 cup flour
- 1½ teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Whites 3 eggs
- 2 teaspoons vinegar

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored, add sugar gradually, and continue beating
2. Add water, flour mixed and sifted with baking powder and salt, whites of eggs beaten until stiff, and vinegar
3. Bake thirty-five minutes in a moderate oven, in a buttered and floured cake pan
