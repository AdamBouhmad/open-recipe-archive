## Ingredients

- ripe red raspberries
- best French brandy
- water
- powdered loaf sugar

## Directions

1. To each quart of ripe red raspberries, put one quart of best French brandy
2. Let it remain about a week, then strain it through a sieve or bag, pressing out all the liquid
3. When you have got as much as you want, reduce the strength to your taste with water, and put a pound of powdered loaf sugar to each gallon
4. Let it stand till refined
