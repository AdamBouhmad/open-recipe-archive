## Ingredients

- a quart of rich milk
- eight eggs
- half a pound of butter
- four large table-spoonfuls of powdered white sugar
- a grated nutmeg
- half a pound of currants
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Boil a quart of rich milk.
2. Beat eight eggs, put them to the milk, and let the milk and eggs boil together till they become a curd.
3. Drain it through a very clean sieve, till all the whey is out.
4. Put the curd into a deep dish, and mix with it half a pound of butter, working them well together.
5. When it is cold, add to it the beaten yolks of four eggs, and four large table-spoonfuls of powdered white sugar; also a grated nutmeg.
6. Stir in, by degrees, half a pound of currants that have been previously picked, washed, dried, and dredged with flour.
7. Lay puff paste round the rim of the dish, and bake the cheese cake half an hour.
8. Send it to table cold, dredged with sugar.
