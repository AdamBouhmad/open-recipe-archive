## Ingredients

- ½ lb. almond paste
- 2 ozs. confectioners' sugar
- White 1 small egg
- Almonds, blanched and finely chopped

## Directions

1. Mix same as Macaroons.
2. Shape mixture, which is quite soft, in a long roll.
3. Cut pieces from roll three-fourths inch long.
4. Roll each separately in chopped nuts, at the same time shaping to form a crescent.
5. Bake twenty minutes on a buttered tin sheet in a slow oven.
6. Cool, and frost with Confectioners' Frosting, made thin enough to apply with a brush, and flavored with lemon juice until quite acid.
7. Other nuts may be used in place of almonds.
