## Ingredients

- 12 large, fresh lemons
- 4 tablespoonfuls white mustard-seed
- 1 tablespoonful turmeric
- 1 tablespoonful white pepper
- 1 teaspoonful cloves
- 1 teaspoonful mace
- 1 saltspoonful cayenne
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls grated horse-radish
- 1 shallot, minced fine
- Juice of the lemons
- 2 tablespoonfuls table-salt

## Directions

1. Grate the rind of the lemons
2. Pound or grind the spices, and put all together, including the horse-radish
3. Strew the salt over all, add the lemon-juice, and let it stand three hours in a cool place
4. Boil in a porcelain kettle half an hour
5. Pour into a covered vessel—china or stone—and let it stand a fortnight, stirring well every day
6. Then strain, bottle, and seal
