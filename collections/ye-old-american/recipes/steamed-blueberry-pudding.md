## Ingredients

- dry ingredients
- butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup blueberries rolled in flour

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients and work in butter same as for Steamed Apple Pudding
2. Add one cup each of milk and blueberries rolled in flour
3. Turn into buttered mould
4. Steam one and one-half hours
5. Serve with Creamy Sauce
