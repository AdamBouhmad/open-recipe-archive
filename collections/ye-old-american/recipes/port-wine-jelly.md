## Ingredients

- one ounce of isinglass
- warm water
- one pint of port wine
- two ounces of sugar candy
- one ounce of gum arabic
- half a nutmeg

## Directions

1. Melt in a little warm water an ounce of isinglass
2. Stir it into a pint of port wine, adding two ounces of sugar candy, an ounce of gum arabic, and half a nutmeg grated
3. Mix all well and boil it ten minutes or till everything is thoroughly dissolved
4. Strain it through muslin and set it away to get cold
