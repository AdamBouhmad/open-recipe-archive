## Ingredients

- One cup bread crumbs
- One cup walnut meats cut in pieces
- One cup cold cooked chicken cut in cubes
- One and one-half tablespoons butter
- One and one-half tablespoons flour
- One-half cup chicken stock
- Salt
- Celery salt
- Paprika
- Nutmeg
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Mix bread crumbs, walnut meats, and chicken.
2. Moisten with a sauce made by melting butter, adding flour, and pouring on gradually, while stirring constantly, chicken stock.
3. Season with salt, celery salt, paprika, nutmeg, and Sherry wine.
4. Shape in balls, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs, fry in deep fat, and drain on brown paper.
5. Serve with a sauce made of one-half chicken stock and one-half cream and flavored with Sherry wine.
