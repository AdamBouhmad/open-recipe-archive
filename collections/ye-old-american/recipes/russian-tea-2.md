## Ingredients

- 3 teaspoons tea
- 2 cups boiling water
- thin slice of lemon or a few drops of lemon juice
- sugar
- preserved strawberry or candied cherry

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for making tea.
2. Russian Tea may be served hot or cold, but always without milk.
3. A thin slice of lemon, from which seeds have been removed, or a few drops of lemon juice, is allowed for each cup.
4. Sugar is added according to taste.
5. In Russia a preserved strawberry to each cup is considered an improvement.
6. Garnish with a candied cherry.
