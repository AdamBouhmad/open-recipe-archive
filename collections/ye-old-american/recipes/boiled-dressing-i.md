## Ingredients

- ½ tablespoon salt
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1½ tablespoons sugar
- Few grains cayenne
- ½ tablespoon flour
- Yolks 2 eggs
- 1½ tablespoons melted butter
- ¾ cup milk
- ¼ cup vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients, add yolks of eggs slightly beaten, butter, milk, and vinegar very slowly.
2. Cook over boiling water until mixture thickens.
3. Strain and cool.
