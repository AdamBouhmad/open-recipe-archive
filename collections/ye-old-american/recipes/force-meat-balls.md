## Ingredients

- a pound of the lean of a leg of veal
- a pound of beef suet
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- nutmeg
- chopped sage or sweet marjoram
- a half-pint of oysters
- six eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. Mince the veal and beef suet together very fine
2. Season to taste with pepper, salt, mace, nutmeg, and chopped sage or sweet marjoram
3. Chop a half-pint of oysters and beat six eggs very well
4. Mix the whole together and pound it to a paste in a marble mortar
5. If not using immediately, put it away in a stone pot, strew a little flour on the top, and cover it closely
6. When ready to use, divide into equal parts and flour your hands
7. Roll it into round balls, all of the same size
8. Either fry them in butter or boil them
