## Ingredients

- one heaping pint of flour
- half a cupful of sugar
- one cupful of milk
- one teaspoonful of soda dissolved in the milk
- one tablespoonful of butter
- two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- nutmeg
- wine or brandy sauce, or sweet sugar sauce

## Directions

1. Rub two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar dry in the flour
2. Flavor with nutmeg
3. Bake in a moderate oven
4. Cut in slices and serve warm with wine or brandy sauce, or sweet sugar sauce
