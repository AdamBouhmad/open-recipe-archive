## Ingredients

- two tablespoons butter
- one-fourth cup grated Young America Cheese
- one teaspoon vinegar
- salt
- paprika
- mustard
- anchovy essence
- thin slices of bread

## Directions

1. Cream two tablespoons butter, and add one-fourth cup grated Young America Cheese and one teaspoon vinegar.
2. Season with salt, paprika, mustard, and anchovy essence.
3. Spread mixture between thin slices of bread.
