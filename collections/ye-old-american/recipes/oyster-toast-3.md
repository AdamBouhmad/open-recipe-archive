## Ingredients

- large oysters
- beaten egg
- cracker or bread crumbs
- rich cream sauce
- celery

## Directions

1. Select the large ones, used for frying, and first dip them in beaten egg, then in either cracker or bread crumbs and cook upon a fine wire gridiron, over a quick fire.
2. Toast should be made ready in advance, and a rich cream sauce poured over the whole.
3. After pouring on the sauce, finely cut celery strewn over the top adds to their delicacy.
