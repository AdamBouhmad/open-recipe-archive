## Ingredients

- Game birds
- Stuffing
- Butter
- Water
- Salt
- Flour
- Browned flour

## Directions

1. Clean, truss, and stuff as you do chickens.
2. Roast at a hot fire, and baste with butter and water until brown.
3. Sprinkle with salt, dredge lightly at the last with flour to froth the birds, and serve hot.
4. Thicken the gravy with browned flour, boil up, and serve in a boat.
5. Wash the inside of all game—prairie-fowls in particular—with soda and water, rinsing out carefully afterward with fair water.
