## Ingredients

- beets
- butter
- flour
- onion
- parsley
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil them first and then scrape and slice them.
2. Put them into a stewpan with a piece of butter rolled in flour, some boiled onion and parsley chopped fine, and a little vinegar, salt and pepper.
3. Set the pan on the fire, and let the beets stew for a quarter of an hour.
