## Ingredients

- the juice of four lemons
- four eggs
- a cupful of sugar
- half a package of gelatine
- one pint of water

## Directions

1. Strain lemon juice on the sugar
2. Beat the yolks of the eggs and mix with the remainder of the water, having used a half cupful of the pint in which to soak the gelatine
3. Add the sugar and lemon to this and cook until it begins to thicken
4. Add the gelatine
5. Strain this into a basin, which place in a pan of water to cool
6. Beat with a whisk until it has cooled but not hardened
7. Add the whites of the eggs until it begins to thicken
8. Turn in a mold and set to harden
9. Serve with powdered sugar and cream
