## Ingredients

- aspic jelly mixture
- hard-boiled eggs
- truffles
- pistachio nuts
- cold cooked fowl
- Mayonnaise
- dissolved granulated gelatine
- lettuce leaves
- Mayonnaise Dressing

## Directions

1. Cover bottom of individual moulds set in ice-water with aspic jelly mixture.
2. When jelly is firm decorate with yolks and whites of hard-boiled eggs cooked as for Harlequin Slices and truffles cut in fancy shapes, or pistachio nuts blanched and cut in halves.
3. Cover decorations with aspic mixture, being careful not to disarrange the designs.
4. Finely chop cold cooked fowl (preferably breast meat), moisten with Mayonnaise to which is added a small quantity of dissolved granulated gelatine, and shape in balls.
5. Put a ball in each mould, and add gradually aspic mixture to fill moulds.
6. Chill thoroughly, remove to lettuce leaves, and arrange around a dish of Mayonnaise Dressing.
