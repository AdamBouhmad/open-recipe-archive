## Ingredients

- 1 quart cold water
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ lb. Canton ginger
- ½ cup orange juice
- ½ cup lemon juice

## Directions

1. Chop ginger, add to water and sugar, boil fifteen minutes; add fruit juice, cool, strain, and dilute with crushed ice.
