## Ingredients

- parsnips
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the parsnips
2. Slice them about half of an inch thick
3. Put them in a saucepan of boiling water containing just enough to barely cook them
4. Add a tablespoonful of butter
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Cover closely
7. Stew them until the water has cooked away, watching carefully and stirring often to prevent burning, until they are soft
