## Ingredients

- two quarts of green gooseberries
- water
- a table-spoonful of butter
- sugar
- six eggs
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Top and tail two quarts of green gooseberries
2. Stew them in a very little water; stirring and mashing them frequently
3. When they have stewed till entirely to pieces, take them out, and with a wooden spoon press the pulp through a cullender
4. Stir in (while the pulp is hot) a table-spoonful of butter, and sufficient sugar to make it very sweet
5. Beat six eggs very light
6. Simmer the gooseberry pulp over a gentle fire, and gradually stir the beaten eggs into it
7. When it comes to a boil, take it off immediately, stir it very hard, and set it out to cool
8. Serve it up cold in glasses or custard cups, grating some nutmeg over each
