## Ingredients

- one quart of fine white flour
- three tablespoonfuls of cold butter
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one tablespoonful of white sugar
- one beaten egg
- one cup of sour cream
- one teaspoonful of soda
- water

## Directions

1. Sift one quart of fine white flour
2. Rub into it three tablespoonfuls of cold butter, a teaspoonful of salt, a tablespoonful of white sugar
3. Add a beaten egg to a cup of sour cream
4. Turn it into the other ingredients
5. Dissolve a teaspoonful of soda in a spoonful of water
6. Mix all together, handling as little as possible
7. Roll lightly into two round sheets
8. Place on pie-tins and bake from twenty to twenty-five minutes in a quick oven
