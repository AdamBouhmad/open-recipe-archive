## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of veal knuckle or scrag, with the bones broken and meat cut up
- 3 qts. water
- ¼ lb. Italian macaroni
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the meat alone in the water for nearly three hours until it is reduced to shreds
2. Boil the macaroni until tender, in enough water to cover it, in a vessel by itself. The pieces should not be more than an inch in length
3. Add a little butter to the macaroni when nearly done
4. Strain the meat out of the soup, season to your taste, put in the macaroni, and the water in which it was boiled
5. Let it boil up, and serve
