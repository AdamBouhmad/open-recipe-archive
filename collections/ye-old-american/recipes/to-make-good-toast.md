## Ingredients

- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the bread in even slices, and moderately thick.
2. It is much nicer when the crust is pared off before toasting.
3. Use a long-handled toasting-fork.
4. Toast the bread, sitting at a comfortable distance from the fire, so the bread will be equally browned all over.
5. When one side is done, take it off from the fork, and turn the other.
6. Send it to table hot, in a heated plate, or in a toast-rack.
7. Butter it to your taste.
8. Toast should neither be burnt nor blackened in any way.
9. You may lay it in even piles, and butter it before it goes to table; cutting each slice in half.
