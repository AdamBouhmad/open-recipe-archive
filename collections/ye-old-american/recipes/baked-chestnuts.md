## Ingredients

- one pint chestnuts
- Chicken Stock
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Remove shells from one pint chestnuts.
2. Put in a baking-dish.
3. Cover with Chicken Stock highly seasoned with salt and cayenne.
4. Bake until soft, keeping covered until nearly done.
5. Keep a small quantity of stock in pan to serve with chestnuts.
