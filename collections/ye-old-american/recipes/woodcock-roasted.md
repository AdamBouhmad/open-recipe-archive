## Ingredients

- woodcock
- bread
- butter
- flour
- gravy
- lemon

## Directions

1. Skin the head and neck of the bird and pluck the feathers
2. Truss it by bringing the beak of the bird under the wing, and fastening the pinion to the thigh
3. Twist the legs at the knuckles and press the feet upon the thigh
4. Put a piece of bread under each bird to catch the drippings
5. Baste with butter, dredge with flour
6. Roast fifteen or twenty minutes with a sharp fire
7. When done, cut the bread in diamond shape, each piece large enough to stand one bird upon
8. Place them aslant on your dish
9. Serve with gravy enough to moisten the bread, serve some in the dish and some in the tureen
10. Garnish with slices of lemon
