## Ingredients

- tail piece of sturgeon
- pepper and salt
- flour
- a pint of good gravy
- catsup
- wine
- pounded cloves
- brown flour and butter
- forcemeat balls
- parsley

## Directions

1. Skin the tail piece and cut off the gristle
2. cut it into slices about half an inch thick
3. sprinkle over them pepper and salt
4. dredge them with flour
5. fry them a nice light brown
6. have ready a pint of good gravy seasoned with catsup, wine, and a little pounded cloves
7. thicken it with brown flour and butter
8. when the cutlets are cold, put them into the gravy and stew them a few minutes
9. garnish the dish with nice forcemeat balls and parsley fried crisp
