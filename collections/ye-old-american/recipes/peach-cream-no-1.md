## Ingredients

- Two cupfuls of canned peaches
- One cupful of sugar
- One-half packet of gelatine
- The whites of six eggs

## Directions

1. Mash very smooth two cupfuls of canned peaches and run them through a sieve
2. Cook for three minutes in a syrup made by boiling together one cupful of sugar and stirring all the time
3. Place the pan containing the syrup and peaches into another of boiling water
4. Add one-half packet of gelatine prepared the same as in previous recipes
5. Stir for five minutes to thoroughly dissolve the gelatine
6. Take it from the fire and place in a pan of ice-water
7. Beat until nearly cool and then add the well-frothed whites of six eggs
8. Beat this whole mixture until it commences to harden
9. Pour into a mold and set away to cool
10. Place on the ice to cool for two or three hours before serving with cream and sugar
