## Ingredients

- quinces
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the quinces into thin slices like apples for pies
2. To one quart jarful of quince, take a coffeesaucer and a half of sugar and a coffeecupful of water
3. Put the sugar and water on the fire
4. When boiling put in the quinces
5. Have ready the jars with their fastenings
6. Stand the jars in a pan of boiling water on the stove
7. When the quince is clear and tender put rapidly into the jars, fruit and syrup together
8. Fill the jars so that the syrup overflows, and fasten up tight as quickly as possible
