## Ingredients

- two ten cent cream cheeses
- milk
- cream
- one-half cup grated Young America cheese
- one cup whipped cream
- three-fourths tablespoon granulated gelatine
- one tablespoon cold water
- one tablespoon boiling water
- salt
- paprika
- lettuce leaves
- Curry Dressing

## Directions

1. Work two ten cent cream cheeses until smooth
2. Moisten with milk and cream, using equal parts
3. Add one-half cup grated Young America cheese, one cup whipped cream, and three-fourths tablespoon granulated gelatine soaked in one tablespoon cold water and dissolved in one tablespoon boiling water
4. Season highly with salt and paprika, and turn into a border mould
5. Chill, remove from mould, arrange on lettuce leaves, fill centre with lettuce leaves, and serve with Curry Dressing
