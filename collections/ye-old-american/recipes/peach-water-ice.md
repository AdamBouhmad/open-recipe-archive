## Ingredients

- Soft, ripe, juicy, freestone peaches
- Loaf-sugar
- Water
- Peach kernels

## Directions

1. Pare the peaches, stone them, and cut them in pieces
2. Put the pieces into a linen bag and squeeze the juice into a deep pan
3. Crack the stones, scald and blanch the kernels, and break them in half
4. Make a syrup allowing half a pint of water to each pound of loaf-sugar
5. Boil the kernels in the syrup, taking them out when the syrup is done
6. Measure the peach-juice, allowing a pint of it to each pint of syrup
7. Mix them together while the syrup is hot
8. Freeze it
