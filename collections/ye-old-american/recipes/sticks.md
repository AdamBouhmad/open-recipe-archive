## Ingredients

- 1 cup scalded milk
- ¼ cup butter
- 1½ tablespoons sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 yeast cake dissolved in ¼ cup lukewarm water
- White 1 egg
- 3¾ cups flour

## Directions

1. Add butter, sugar, and salt to milk
2. When lukewarm, add dissolved yeast cake, white of egg well beaten, and flour
3. Knead and let rise
4. Shape as small biscuits
5. Roll on board where there is no flour with hands until eight inches in length
6. Keep of uniform size and rounded ends by bringing fingers close to, but not over, ends of sticks
7. Let rise again
8. Start baking in a hot oven, reducing heat, that sticks may be crisp and dry
