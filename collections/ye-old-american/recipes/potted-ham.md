## Ingredients

- two pounds of lean ham
- one pound of fat
- two teaspoonfuls of powdered mace
- half a nutmeg, grated
- more than half a teaspoonful of cayenne
- clarified lard

## Directions

1. Mince the ham, fat and lean together in the above proportion
2. Pound it well in a mortar, seasoning it with cayenne pepper, pounded mace and nutmeg
3. Put the mixture into a deep baking-dish and bake for half an hour
4. Press it well into a stone jar and fill up the jar with clarified lard
5. Cover it closely and paste over it a piece of thick paper
6. If well seasoned, it will keep a long time in winter
