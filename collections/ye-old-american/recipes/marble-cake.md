## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup milk
- Whites of three eggs
- 2 cups prepared flour
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup molasses
- ¼ cup milk
- ½ cup nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful cinnamon
- ½ teaspoonful allspice
- ½ teaspoonful soda
- 2 cups flour
- Yolks of three eggs

## Directions

1. Prepare light batter: Combine 1 cup white sugar, ½ cup butter, ½ cup milk, whites of three eggs, and 2 cups prepared flour.
2. Prepare dark batter: Combine ½ cup brown sugar, ¼ cup butter, ½ cup molasses, ¼ cup milk, ½ cup nutmeg, 1 teaspoonful cinnamon, ½ teaspoonful allspice, ½ teaspoonful soda, 2 cups flour, and yolks of three eggs.
3. Butter your mould, and put in the dark and light batter in alternate tablespoonfuls.
