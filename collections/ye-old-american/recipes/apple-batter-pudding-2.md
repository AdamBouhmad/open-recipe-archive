## Ingredients

- half a pound of flour
- salt
- a pint of milk
- three eggs
- butter
- three-quarters of a pound of apples
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of flour into a basin, sprinkle in a little salt, stir in gradually a pint of milk; when quite smooth add three eggs
2. Butter a pie-dish, pour in the batter
3. Take three-quarters of a pound of apples, seed and cut in slices, and put in the batter
4. Place bits of butter over the top
5. Bake three-quarters of an hour
6. When done, sprinkle sugar over the top and serve hot
