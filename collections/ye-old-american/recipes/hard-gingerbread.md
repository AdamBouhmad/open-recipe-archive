## Ingredients

- two cups of brown sugar
- two cupfuls of cooking molasses
- one cup of shortening
- one large tablespoonful of ginger
- one tablespoonful of ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make the same as Soft Gingerbread, omitting the eggs and mixing hard enough to roll out like biscuit.
2. Roll nearly half an inch thick and cut out like small biscuits, or it can be baked in a sheet or on a biscuit-tin.
3. Cut slits a quarter of an inch deep across the top of the tin from side to side.
4. When baked and while hot, rub over the top with molasses and let it dry on.
