## Ingredients

- 3 cups warm milk
- ½ cup yeast
- 2 tablespoonfuls melted butter
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- 1 saltspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water
- Flour to make good batter

## Directions

1. Set the milk, yeast, salt, and flour as a sponge, leaving out the butter and soda
2. When very light, beat in the melted butter, with a very little flour, to prevent the butter from thinning the batter too much
3. Stir in the soda hard
4. Fill pattypans or muffin-rings with the mixture
5. Let them stand fifteen minutes before baking
