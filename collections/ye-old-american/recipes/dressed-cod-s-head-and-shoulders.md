## Ingredients

- cod's head and shoulders
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- flour
- butter
- white bread crumbs
- lemon
- scraped horse-radish
- barberries
- small fish
- fried oysters
- roe and liver
- lobster

## Directions

1. Take out the gills and the blood from the bone
2. Wash the head very clean
3. Rub over it a little salt
4. Lay it on your fish plate
5. Throw in the water a good handful of salt, with a glass of vinegar
6. Put in the fish and let it boil gently half an hour, or three quarters if it is a large one
7. Take it up very carefully
8. Strip the skin nicely off
9. Set it before a brisk fire
10. Dredge it all over with flour and baste it well with butter
11. When the froth begins to rise, throw over it some very fine white bread crumbs
12. Keep basting it all the time to make it froth well
13. When it is a fine light brown, dish it up
14. Garnish it with a lemon cut in slices, scraped horse-radish, barberries, a few small fish fried and laid around it, or fried oysters
15. Cut the roe and liver in slices and lay over it a little of the lobster out of the sauce in lumps
16. Serve it up
