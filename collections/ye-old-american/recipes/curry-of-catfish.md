## Ingredients

- white channel catfish
- quart of water
- two onions
- chopped parsley
- butter
- flour
- curry powder

## Directions

1. Take the white channel catfish
2. Cut off their heads, skin and clean them
3. Cut them in pieces four inches long
4. Put as many as will be sufficient for a dish into a stew pan with a quart of water, two onions, and chopped parsley
5. Let them stew gently till the water is reduced to half a pint
6. Take the fish out and lay them on a dish
7. Cover them to keep them hot
8. Rub a spoonful of butter into one of flour
9. Add a large tea-spoonful of curry powder
10. Thicken the gravy with it
11. Shake it over the fire a few minutes
12. Pour it over the fish
13. Be careful to have the gravy smooth
