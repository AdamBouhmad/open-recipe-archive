## Ingredients

- white onions
- brine
- whole mace
- white pepper-corns
- cloves
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, which should be fine white ones—not too large.
2. Let them stand in strong brine for four days, changing it twice.
3. Heat more brine to a boil, throw in the onions, and boil three minutes.
4. Throw them at once into cold water, and leave them there four hours.
5. Pack in jars, interspersing with whole mace, white pepper-corns, and cloves.
6. Fill up with scalding vinegar in which you have put a cupful of sugar for every gallon.
7. Cork while hot.
8. They will be ready for use in a month, but will be better at the end of three months.
