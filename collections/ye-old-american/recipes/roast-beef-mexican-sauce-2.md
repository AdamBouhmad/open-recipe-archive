## Ingredients

- Cold roast beef
- One onion finely chopped
- Two tablespoons butter
- One red pepper
- One green pepper
- One clove of garlic
- Two tomatoes
- One teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce

## Directions

1. Reheat cold roast beef cut in thin slices in Mexican Sauce
2. For Mexican Sauce: Cook one onion, finely chopped, in two tablespoons butter five minutes
3. Add one red pepper, one green pepper, and one clove of garlic, each finely chopped, and two tomatoes peeled and cut in pieces
4. Cook fifteen minutes
5. Add one teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
