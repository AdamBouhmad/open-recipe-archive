## Ingredients

- a pint of molasses
- a pint of milk
- four eggs
- a cupful of beef suet chopped fine or half a cupful of butter
- corn meal
- a teaspoonful of pulverized cinnamon
- a teaspoonful of nutmeg
- a teaspoonful of soda
- one teaspoonful of salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Warm a pint of molasses and a pint of milk, stir well together
2. Beat four eggs and stir gradually into molasses and milk
3. Add a cupful of beef suet chopped fine, or half a cupful of butter, and corn meal sufficient to make a thick batter
4. Add a teaspoonful of pulverized cinnamon, the same of nutmeg, a teaspoonful of soda, one of salt, and stir all together thoroughly
5. Dip a cloth into boiling water, shake, flour a little
6. Turn in the mixture, tie up, leaving room for the pudding to swell
7. Boil three hours
8. Serve hot with sauce made of drawn butter, wine and nutmeg
