## Ingredients

- two cups of Graham flour
- one cup wheat flour
- two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- one teaspoonful of salt
- three eggs well beaten
- one tablespoonful of lard or butter melted
- three cups of sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix together dry two cups of Graham flour, one cup wheat flour, two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder and one teaspoonful of salt
2. Add three eggs well beaten, one tablespoonful of lard or butter melted and three cups of sweet milk
3. Cook immediately on a hot griddle
