## Ingredients

- red beet roots
- salt

## Directions

1. Dress in the same way as parsnips, only neither scraped nor cut till after they are boiled
2. They will take from an hour and a half to three hours in boiling, according to their size
3. Send to the table with salt fish, boiled beef, etc.
