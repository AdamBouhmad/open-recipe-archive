## Ingredients

- fine ripe lemons
- loaf-sugar
- cold water

## Directions

1. Roll lemons under your hand on the table to increase the quantity of juice
2. Cut and squeeze them into a pitcher
3. Mix the juice with loaf-sugar and cold water
4. To half a pint of lemon juice allow a pint and a half of water, and ten or twelve moderate sized lumps of sugar
5. Send it round in little glasses with handles
6. For very good lemonade, allow the juice of one lemon and four or five lumps of sugar, filling up the glass with water
7. In summer use ice water
