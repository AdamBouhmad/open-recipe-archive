## Ingredients

- sugar beans
- butter

## Directions

1. Like all other spring and summer vegetables, they must be young and freshly gathered
2. Boil them till tender
3. Drain them
4. Add a little butter, and serve them up
5. To preserve them for winter use: gather them on a dry day, when full grown, but quite young
6. Have a clean and dry keg
7. Sprinkle some salt in the bottom
8. Put in a layer of pods containing the beans, then a little salt
9. Do this till the keg is full
10. Lay a board on with a weight, to press them down
11. Cover the keg very close, and keep it in a dry, cool place
12. When used, the pods must be washed, and laid in fresh water all night
13. Shell them next day, and keep them in water till you are going to boil them
14. When tender, serve them up with melted butter in a boat
