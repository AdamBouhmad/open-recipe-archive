## Ingredients

- mutton cutlets from the neck
- melted butter
- beaten egg
- cracker-crumbs
- water
- butter and water for basting
- bones and meat trimmings
- cold water
- sweet herbs
- pepper
- salt
- tomato catsup
- browned flour

## Directions

1. Cut cutlets from the neck, and trim neatly.
2. Lay aside the bits of bone and meat you cut off, to make gravy.
3. Pour a little melted butter over the cutlets, and let them lie in it for fifteen minutes, keeping them just warm enough to prevent the butter from hardening.
4. Dip each in beaten egg, roll in cracker-crumbs, and lay them in your dripping-pan with a very little water at the bottom.
5. Bake quickly, and baste often with butter and water.
6. Put on the bones, etc., in enough cold water to cover them; stew, and season with sweet herbs, pepper, and salt, with a spoonful of tomato catsup.
7. Strain when all the substance is extracted from the meat and bones; thicken with browned flour, and pour over the cutlets when they are served.
