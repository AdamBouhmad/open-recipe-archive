## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons cocoa
- ¼ cup sugar
- A few grains salt
- 4 cups milk
- ¾ cup boiling water

## Directions

1. Scald milk.
2. Mix cocoa, sugar, and salt, adding enough boiling water to make a smooth paste; add remaining water and boil one minute.
3. Pour into scalded milk.
4. Beat two minutes, using Dover egg-beater.
