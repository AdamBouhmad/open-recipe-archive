## Ingredients

- beets
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash, but do not touch with a knife before they are boiled.
2. If cut while raw, they bleed themselves pale in the hot water.
3. Boil until tender—if full-grown at least two hours.
4. When done, rub off the skins, slice round if large, split if young, and butter well in the dish.
5. Salt and pepper to taste.
6. A nice way is to slice them upon a hot dish, mix a great spoonful of melted butter with four or five of vinegar, pepper and salt, heat to boiling, and pour over the beets.
