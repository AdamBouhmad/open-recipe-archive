## Ingredients

- currants or any small fruits, either fresh or dried
- sugar to taste
- thin slices of bread with the crust cut off

## Directions

1. Stew currants or any small fruits, either fresh or dried, and sweeten with sugar to taste
2. Pour hot over thin slices of bread with the crust cut off, placed in a suitable dish
3. Make layers first a layer of bread, then the hot stewed fruit, then bread and fruit, then bread, leaving the fruit last
4. Put a plate over the top and when cool, set it on ice
5. Serve with sugar and cream
