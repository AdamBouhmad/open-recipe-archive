## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 tablespoon corn-starch
- few grains salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons brandy
- fruit red for coloring

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, corn-starch, and salt.
2. Add gradually, while stirring constantly, boiling water, and boil five minutes.
3. Remove from fire, add lemon juice and brandy; then color with fruit red.
