## Ingredients

- thin cream
- vanilla, brandy, or wine
- preserve

## Directions

1. Sweeten thin cream, flavor with vanilla, brandy, or wine, then whip.
2. Half fill frappé glasses with any preserve, pile on lightly the whip.
