## Ingredients

- Vanilla Ice Cream
- lady fingers
- preserved strawberries or large fresh fruit
- two and one-half cups milk
- yolks four eggs
- one cup sugar
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- one cup heavy cream
- one tablespoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Line a brick mould with Vanilla Ice Cream
2. Put in layer of lady fingers
3. Fill the centre with preserved strawberries or large fresh fruit cut in halves
4. Make custard of two and one-half cups milk, yolks four eggs, one cup sugar, and one-fourth teaspoon salt
5. Strain, cool, add one cup heavy cream and one tablespoon vanilla
6. Freeze the custard
7. Cover with ice cream, pack in salt and ice, and let stand one hour
