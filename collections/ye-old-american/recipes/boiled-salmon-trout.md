## Ingredients

- salmon-trout
- salt
- water
- cream
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and dry the trout; envelop in a thin cloth fitted neatly to the shape of the fish, lay within a fish-kettle, cover with salted water (cold), and boil gently half an hour or longer, according to the size.
2. When done, unwrap and lay in a hot dish.
3. Pour around it cream-sauce made as for baked salmon-trout, with the omission of the fish-gravy, and serve.
