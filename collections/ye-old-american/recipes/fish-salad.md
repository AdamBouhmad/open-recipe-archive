## Ingredients

- a fresh white fish or trout
- chopped cabbage, celery or lettuce
- salt and pepper
- tender leaves of the heart of lettuce

## Directions

1. Take a fresh white fish or trout, boil and chop it, but not too fine
2. Put with the same quantity of chopped cabbage, celery or lettuce
3. Season the same as chicken salad
4. Garnish with the tender leaves of the heart of lettuce
