## Ingredients

- one coconut nut
- one quart cream

## Directions

1. Take the nut from its shell, pare it, and grate it very fine
2. Mix it with a quart of cream
3. Sweeten, and freeze it
4. If the nut be a small one, it will require one and a half to flavour a quart of cream
