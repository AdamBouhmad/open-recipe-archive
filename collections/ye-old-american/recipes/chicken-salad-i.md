## Ingredients

- cold boiled fowl or roast chicken
- French Dressing
- celery
- cold or ice-water
- Cream, Oil, or Mayonnaise Dressing
- hard-boiled eggs
- capers

## Directions

1. Cut cold boiled fowl or remnants of roast chicken in one-half inch cubes, and marinate with French Dressing.
2. Add an equal quantity of celery, washed, scraped, cut in small pieces, chilled in cold or ice-water, drained, and dried in a towel.
3. Just before serving moisten with Cream, Oil, or Mayonnaise Dressing.
4. Mound on a salad dish, and garnish with yolks of hard-boiled eggs forced through a potato ricer, capers, and celery tips.
