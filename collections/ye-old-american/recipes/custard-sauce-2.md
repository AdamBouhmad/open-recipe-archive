## Ingredients

- one cupful of sugar
- two beaten eggs
- one pint of milk
- flavoring to taste
- brandy or wine, if preferred

## Directions

1. Heat the milk to boiling
2. Add by degrees the beaten eggs and sugar
3. Put in the flavoring and set within a pan of boiling water
4. Stir until it begins to thicken
5. Take it off and stir in the brandy or wine gradually
6. Set, until wanted, within a pan of boiling water
