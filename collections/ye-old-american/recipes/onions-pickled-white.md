## Ingredients

- small white onions
- salt
- water
- milk
- mace
- cider vinegar
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Peel some very small white onions
2. Lay them for three days in salt and water, changing the water every day
3. Wipe them, and put them into a porcelain kettle with equal quantities of milk and water, sufficient to cover them well
4. Simmer them over a slow fire, but when just ready to boil take them off
5. Drain and dry them
6. Put them into wide-mouthed glass bottles
7. Intersperse them with blades of mace
8. Boil a sufficient quantity of the best cider vinegar to cover them and fill up the bottles
9. Add to it a little salt
10. When it is cold, pour it into the bottles of onions
11. Put at the top of each bottle a spoonful of sweet oil
12. Set them away closely corked
