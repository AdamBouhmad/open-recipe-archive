## Ingredients

- a loaf of bread
- marrow or butter
- a handful of currants or stoned raisins
- four eggs
- a quart of milk boiled and cooled
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- a grated nutmeg
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Cut a loaf of bread as thin as possible
2. Put a layer of it in the bottom of a deep dish
3. Strew on some slices of marrow or butter, with a handful of currants or stoned raisins
4. Do this till the dish is full, let the currants or raisins be at the top
5. Beat four eggs, mix with them a quart of milk that has been boiled a little and become cold, a quarter of a pound of sugar, and a grated nutmeg
6. Pour it in, and bake it in a moderate oven
7. Eat it with wine sauce
