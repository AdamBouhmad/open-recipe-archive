## Ingredients

- rhubarb
- sugar
- one cupful of sour milk
- two eggs
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- flour

## Directions

1. Chop rhubarb pretty fine, put in a pudding dish and sprinkle sugar over it
2. Make a batter of one cupful of sour milk, two eggs, a piece of butter the size of an egg, half a teaspoonful of soda and enough flour to make batter about as thick as for cake
3. Spread it over the rhubarb and bake till done
4. Turn out on a platter upside down, so that the rhubarb will be on top
5. Serve with sugar and cream
