## Ingredients

- one cup of stale bread crumbs
- one onion
- two ounces of butter
- pepper and salt
- a little mace
- milk

## Directions

1. Cut the onion fine, and boil it in milk till quite soft
2. Strain the milk on to the stale bread crumbs, and let it stand an hour
3. Put it in a saucepan with the boiled onion, pepper, salt and mace
4. Give it a boil, and serve in sauce tureen
