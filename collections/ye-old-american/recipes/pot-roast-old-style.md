## Ingredients

- a piece of fresh beef weighing about five or six pounds
- water
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Take a piece of fresh beef weighing about five or six pounds that is not too fat
2. Wash it and put it into a pot with barely sufficient water to cover it
3. Set it over a slow fire
4. After it has stewed an hour salt and pepper it
5. Stew it slowly until tender, adding a little onion if liked
6. Do not replenish the water at the last, but let all nearly boil away
7. When tender all through take the meat from the pot and pour the gravy in a bowl
8. Put a large lump of butter in the bottom of the pot
9. Dredge the piece of meat with flour and return it to the pot to brown, turning it often to prevent its burning
10. Take the gravy that you have poured from the meat into the bowl and skim off all the fat
11. Pour this gravy in with the meat and stir in a large spoonful of flour wet with a little water
12. Let it boil up ten or fifteen minutes and pour into a gravy dish
13. Serve both hot, the meat on a platter
