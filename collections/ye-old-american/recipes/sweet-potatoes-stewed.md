## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- slices of boiled ham
- one or two chickens
- pepper
- salt
- herbs
- water

## Directions

1. wash and wipe them, and if they be large, cut them in two lengths
2. put them at the bottom of a stew pan
3. lay over some slices of boiled ham
4. on that, put one or two chickens cut up with pepper, salt, and a bundle of herbs
5. pour in some water
6. stew them till done
7. take out the herbs
8. serve the stew in a deep dish
9. thicken the gravy, and pour over it
