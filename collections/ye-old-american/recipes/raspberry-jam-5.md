## Ingredients

- raspberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pick over raspberries.
2. Mash a few in the bottom of a preserving kettle, using a wooden potato masher, and so continue until the fruit is used.
3. Heat slowly to boiling-point, and add gradually an equal quantity of heated sugar.
4. Cook slowly forty-five minutes.
5. Put in a stone jar or tumblers.
