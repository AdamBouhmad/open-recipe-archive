## Ingredients

- 2¾ cups scalded milk
- ¼ cup cold milk
- ⅓ cup corn-starch
- ¼ cup sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ can grated pineapple
- Whites 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Follow directions for Rebecca Pudding, and add pineapple just before moulding. Fill individual moulds, previously dipped in cold water. Serve with cream.
