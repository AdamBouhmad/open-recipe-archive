## Ingredients

- one cup brown sugar
- two cups molasses
- one large cup butter
- two teaspoonfuls soda
- two teaspoonfuls ginger
- three pints flour to commence with

## Directions

1. Rub shortening and sugar together into the flour
2. Add enough more flour to roll very smooth and very thin
3. Roll out to wafer-like thinness
4. Bake in a quick oven without burning
5. The dough can be kept for days by putting it in the flour barrel under the flour, and bake a few at a time
6. They should become perfectly cold before putting aside
