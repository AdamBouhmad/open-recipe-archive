## Ingredients

- One quart of properly stewed pumpkin pressed through a colander
- good, rich milk
- a teaspoonful of salt
- half a cupful of molasses or brown sugar
- a tablespoonful of ginger

## Directions

1. Add enough good, rich milk to the pumpkin, sufficient to moisten it enough to fill two good-sized earthen pie-plates
2. Add a teaspoonful of salt, half a cupful of molasses or brown sugar, and a tablespoonful of ginger
3. Bake
