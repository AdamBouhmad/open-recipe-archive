## Ingredients

- Some stale sponge, or plain cup cake, cut into rounds with a cake-cutter
- Hot lard
- Strawberry or other jam, or jelly
- A little boiling milk
- Cream

## Directions

1. Cut the cake carefully and fry a nice brown.
2. Dip each slice for a second in a bowl of boiling milk, draining this off on the side of the vessel; lay on a hot dish and spread thickly with strawberry jam, peach jelly, or other delicate conserve.
3. Pile them neatly and send around hot, with cream to pour over them.
