## Ingredients

- proper mushrooms
- salt
- garlic
- quarter of an ounce of cloves pounded

## Directions

1. Take the flaps of the proper mushrooms from the stems
2. Wash them, add some salt, and crush them
3. Boil them some time, strain them through a cloth
4. Put them on the fire again with salt to your taste, a few cloves of garlic, and a quarter of an ounce of cloves pounded, to a peck of mushrooms
5. Boil it till reduced to less than half the original quantity
6. Bottle and cork it well
