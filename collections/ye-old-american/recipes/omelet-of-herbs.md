## Ingredients

- parsley
- thyme
- sweet marjoram
- two tablespoonfuls of mixed parsley, onion, chives, shallots
- sorrel
- chevril
- butter
- eggs

## Directions

1. Mix parsley, thyme and sweet marjoram
2. For an omelet jardiniere, mince fine two tablespoonfuls of mixed parsley, onion, chives, shallots and a few leaves each of sorrel and chevril
3. Stir into the beaten eggs before cooking
4. Use a little more butter to fry it than a plain one
