## Ingredients

- a clear good fire
- butter for basting
- flour
- dripping
- more butter
- a little salt
- parsley shred fine
- a nice sallad
- green peas
- fresh beans
- colliflower
- asparagus

## Directions

1. Lay down to a clear good fire that will not want stirring or altering.
2. Baste with butter.
3. Dust on flour.
4. Baste with the dripping.
5. Before you take it up, add more butter and sprinkle on a little salt and parsley shred fine.
6. Send to table with a nice sallad, green peas, fresh beans, or a colliflower, or asparagus.
