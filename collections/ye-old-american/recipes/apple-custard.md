## Ingredients

- a dozen large juicy pippins
- yellow peel of a large lemon
- water
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- six eggs
- a quart of rich milk
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and quarter a dozen large juicy pippins
2. Strew among them the yellow peel of a large lemon grated very fine
3. Stew them till tender, in a very small portion of water
4. When done, mash them smooth with the back of a spoon
5. Mix a quarter of a pound of sugar with them, and set them away till cold
6. Beat six eggs very light, and stir them gradually into a quart of rich milk, alternately with the stewed apple
7. Put the mixture into cups, or into a deep dish, and bake it about twenty minutes
8. Send it to table cold, with nutmeg grated over the top
