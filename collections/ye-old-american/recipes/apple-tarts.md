## Ingredients

- apples
- cinnamon
- rose-water
- wine
- sugar to your taste

## Directions

1. Stew and strain the apples, add cinnamon, rose-water, wine and sugar to your taste, lay in paste, royal, squeeze thereon orange juice---bake gently.
