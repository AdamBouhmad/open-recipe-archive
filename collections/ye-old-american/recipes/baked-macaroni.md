## Ingredients

- half a pound pipe macaroni
- boiling water
- salt
- mild rich cheese
- butter
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Break half a pound of pipe macaroni in pieces an inch long and put into a saucepan of boiling water slightly salted.
2. Stew gently twenty minutes. It should be soft, but not broken or split.
3. Drain well and put a layer in the bottom of a buttered pie or pudding-dish.
4. Upon this grate some mild, rich cheese and scatter over it some bits of butter.
5. Spread upon the cheese more macaroni and fill the dish in this order, having macaroni at the top, buttered well, without the cheese.
6. Add a few spoonfuls of cream or milk and a very little salt.
7. Bake covered half an hour, then brown nicely and serve in the bake-dish.
