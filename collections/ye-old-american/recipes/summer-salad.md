## Ingredients

- 3 heads of lettuce
- 2 teaspoonfuls green mustard leaves
- A handful of water-cresses
- Four or five very tender radishes
- 1 cucumber
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful pepper
- 1 teaspoonful made mustard
- 1 teacupful vinegar
- 2 tablespoonfuls salad oil
- fennel-heads and nasturtium-blossoms for garnish

## Directions

1. Mix the dressing as for lettuce salad.
2. Cut up the hearts of the lettuce, the radishes and cucumber, into very small pieces; chop the mustard and cress.
3. Pour over these the dressing, tossing very lightly, not to bruise the young leaves.
4. Heap in a salad-bowl upon a lump of ice, and garnish with fennel-heads and nasturtium-blossoms.
