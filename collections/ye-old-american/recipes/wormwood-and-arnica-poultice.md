## Ingredients

- Wormwood and Arnica
- half a pint of cold water
- bran or rye meal

## Directions

1. Steep the herbs in half a pint of cold water and when all their virtue is extracted stir in a little bran or rye meal to thicken the liquid; the herbs must not be removed from the liquid.
