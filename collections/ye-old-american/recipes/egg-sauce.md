## Ingredients

- four eggs
- quarter of a pound of good melted butter

## Directions

1. Boil four eggs for ten minutes
2. Chop half the whites, put them with the yelks, and chop them both together, but not very fine
3. Put them into a quarter of a pound of good melted butter
4. Put it in a boat
