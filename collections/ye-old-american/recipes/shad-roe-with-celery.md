## Ingredients

- Shad roe
- Salt
- Acidulated water
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ½ cup chopped celery
- Onion juice
- Lemon juice
- Salt and pepper
- Toasted bread

## Directions

1. Clean shad roe, cook in boiling, salted, acidulated water twenty minutes, and drain
2. Plunge into cold water, drain, remove membrane, and separate roe into pieces
3. Melt three tablespoons butter, add roe, and cook ten minutes
4. Add one tablespoon butter, chopped celery, few drops each onion and lemon juice, and salt and pepper
5. Serve on pieces of toasted bread
