## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sugar
- ¾ cup sweet milk
- 1 teaspoonful saleratus
- 2 teaspoonfuls ginger
- Flour for tolerably stiff dough

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients and bake.
