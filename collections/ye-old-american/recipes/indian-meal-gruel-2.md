## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of boiling water
- 1 cup of Indian meal
- 1 tablespoonful flour, wet up with cold water
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Wet the meal and flour to a smooth paste, and stir into the water while it is actually boiling.
2. Boil slowly one hour, stirring up well from the bottom.
3. Season with salt to taste.
