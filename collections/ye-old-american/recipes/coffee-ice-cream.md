## Ingredients

- 3 pints of cream
- 1 cup of black coffee—very strong and clear
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls arrowroot, wet up with cold water

## Directions

1. Heat half the cream nearly to boiling, stir in the sugar, and, when this is melted, the coffee
2. Then the arrowroot
3. Boil all together five minutes, stirring constantly
4. When cold, beat up very light, whipping in the rest of the cream by degrees
5. Then freeze
