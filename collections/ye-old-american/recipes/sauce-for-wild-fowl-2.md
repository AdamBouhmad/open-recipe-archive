## Ingredients

- a gill of claret
- as much water
- grated bread
- three heads of shallots
- whole pepper
- mace
- grated nutmeg
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Let claret, water, grated bread, shallots, whole pepper, mace, grated nutmeg, and salt stew over the fire
2. Beat it up with butter
3. Put it under the wild fowl, which being a little roasted, will afford gravy to mix with this sauce
