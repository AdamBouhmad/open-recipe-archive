## Ingredients

- Two cupfuls of Graham flour
- One cupful of wheat flour
- Two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- One tablespoonful of sugar
- One tablespoonful of salt
- One well-beaten egg
- Sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix flour, baking powder, sugar, salt and egg
2. Mix with sweet milk to make a thin batter
3. Beat it well
4. Have gem-irons well greased
5. Fill two-thirds full
6. Bake in a hot oven from fifteen to twenty minutes
