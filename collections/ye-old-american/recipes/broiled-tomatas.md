## Ingredients

- large ripe tomatas
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take large ripe tomatas, wipe them, and split them in half
2. Broil them on a gridiron till brown, turning them when half done
3. Have ready in a dish some butter seasoned with a little pepper
4. When the tomatas are well broiled, put them into the dish and press each a little with the back of a spoon, so that the juice may run into the butter and mix with it
5. Send them to table hot
