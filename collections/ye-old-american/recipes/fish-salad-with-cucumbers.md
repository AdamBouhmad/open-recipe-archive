## Ingredients

- one and one-half cups cold cooked flaked halibut, haddock, or cod
- salt
- cayenne
- lemon juice
- Cream Dressing II
- one-third tablespoon granulated gelatine
- one and one-half tablespoons cold water
- one-half cup heavy cream
- lettuce leaves
- cucumber

## Directions

1. Season one and one-half cups cold cooked flaked halibut, haddock, or cod, with salt, cayenne, and lemon juice.
2. Cover, and let stand one hour.
3. To Cream Dressing II add one-third tablespoon granulated gelatine soaked in one and one-half tablespoons cold water.
4. As soon as dressing begins to thicken, add one-half cup heavy cream beaten until stiff, then fold in the fish.
5. Turn into individual moulds, chill, remove from moulds, arrange on lettuce leaves, garnish each with a thin slice of cucumber.
