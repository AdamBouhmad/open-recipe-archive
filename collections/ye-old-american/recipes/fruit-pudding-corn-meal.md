## Ingredients

- a pint of hot milk
- sifted Indian meal
- a teaspoonful of salt
- half a cup of molasses
- a teaspoonful of soda dissolved
- a pint of whortleberries or chopped sweet apple

## Directions

1. Take a pint of hot milk and stir in sifted Indian meal till the batter is stiff
2. Add a teaspoonful of salt and half a cup of molasses, adding a teaspoonful of soda dissolved
3. Stir in a pint of whortleberries or chopped sweet apple
4. Tie in a cloth that has been wet, and leave room for it to swell, or put in a pudding-pan and tie a cloth over
5. Boil three hours; the water must boil when it is put in
6. You can use cranberries and sweet sauce
