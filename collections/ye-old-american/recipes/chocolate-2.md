## Ingredients

- Half a cupful of grated chocolate
- One pint of water
- One pint of milk

## Directions

1. Rub the chocolate smooth in a little cold water
2. Stir into the boiling water
3. Boil twenty minutes
4. Add the milk and boil ten minutes more, stirring it often
5. Sweeten to your taste
