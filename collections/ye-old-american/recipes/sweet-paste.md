## Ingredients

- one pound and a quarter of the finest flour
- three ounces of powdered loaf-sugar
- one pound of the best fresh butter
- yolks of two beaten eggs
- half a pint of rich milk

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and powdered loaf-sugar into a deep dish
2. Cut up the butter in it and rub it fine with your hands
3. Make a hole in the middle and pour in the yolks of two beaten eggs
4. Mix them with the flour
5. Wet the whole to a stiff paste with half a pint of rich milk
6. Knead it well and roll it out
