## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- 2 eggs
- 3 cups flour
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar
- ½ teaspoonful soda
- 1 saltspoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter

## Directions

1. Sift the cream-tartar into the flour with the salt
2. Dissolve the soda in a little hot water
3. Beat the eggs very well
4. Add the flour the last thing
5. If the batter is too stiff, put in more milk
