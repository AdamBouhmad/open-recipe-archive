## Ingredients

- five sweet oranges
- one coffeecupful of white sugar
- one pint of milk
- yolks of three eggs
- one tablespoonful of cornstarch
- butter
- two tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the oranges into thin slices, taking out the seeds
2. Pour the sugar over them and let them stand
3. Set the milk in a suitable dish into another of boiling water
4. Let the milk get boiling hot
5. Add a piece of butter as large as a nutmeg, the cornstarch made smooth with a little cold milk, and the well-beaten yolks of the eggs and a little flavoring
6. Stir it all well together until it is smooth and cooked
7. Set it off and pour it over the oranges
8. Beat the whites to a stiff froth, adding two tablespoonfuls of sugar
9. Spread over the top for frosting
10. Set into the oven a few minutes to brown
11. Eat cold
