## Ingredients

- lobster
- salt
- water
- good melted butter
- meat spoonful of lemon pickle
- walnut catsup
- slice of lemon
- horse-radish
- beaten mace
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Take a lobster, if it be alive, stick a skewer in the rent of the tail to keep the water out
2. Throw a handful of salt in the water
3. When it boils, put in the lobster and boil it half an hour
4. If it has spawn on it, pick them off and pound them exceedingly fine in a marble mortar
5. Put them into half a pound of good melted butter
6. Take the meat out of the lobster and pull it in bits
7. Put it in your butter, with a meat spoonful of lemon pickle, and the same of walnut catsup
8. Add a slice of lemon, one or two slices of horse-radish, a little beaten mace, salt and cayenne to your taste
9. Boil them one minute
10. Take out the horse-radish and lemon and serve it up in your sauce boat
