## Ingredients

- kippered herrings in can
- pepper
- lemon juice
- melted butter
- parsley
- lemon

## Directions

1. Remove fish from can, and arrange on a platter that may be put in the oven; sprinkle with pepper, brush over with lemon juice and melted butter, and pour over the liquor left in can.
2. Heat thoroughly, and garnish with parsley and slices of lemon.
