## Ingredients

- large, ripe potatoes
- roast beef
- flour
- gravy

## Directions

1. Boil and peel some large, ripe potatoes
2. Three-quarters of an hour before a piece of roast beef is removed from the fire, skim the fat from the gravy
3. Put the potatoes in the dripping-pan, having dredged them well with flour
4. Baste them, to prevent scorching, with the gravy
5. When quite brown, drain on a sieve
6. Lay them about the meat in the dish
