## Ingredients

- one-half cup butter
- two cups sugar
- three-quarters of a cup sweet milk
- two and one-half cups flour
- whites of eight eggs
- one teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- one-half teaspoonful soda
- For the Frosting: whites of three eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar
- one tablespoonful of grated chocolate

## Directions

1. Combine one-half cup butter, two cups sugar, three-quarters of a cup sweet milk, two and one-half cups flour, whites of eight eggs, one teaspoonful of cream of tartar, one-half teaspoonful soda
2. Bake in shallow pans
3. For the frosting, take the whites of three eggs, three tablespoonfuls of sugar and one tablespoonful of grated chocolate per egg
4. Put the cake together with the frosting
5. Frost the top of the cake with the same
