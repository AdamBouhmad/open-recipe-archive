## Ingredients

- four large lemons
- a pint of boiling water
- one tea-spoonful of raw green tea of the best sort
- a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- a bottle of champagne or any liquor suitable for punch

## Directions

1. Roll lemons on the table to make them more juicy
2. Pare them as thin as possible
3. Cut out all the pulp, and throw away the seeds and the white part of the rind
4. Put the yellow rind and the pulp into a pint of boiling water with one tea-spoonful of raw green tea
5. Let all boil together about ten minutes
6. Strain it through linen
7. Stir in a pound of powdered loaf-sugar and a bottle of champagne or of any liquor suitable for punch
8. Set it again over the fire
9. When just ready to boil, remove it, and pour it into a china bowl or pitcher, to be sent round in glasses
