## Ingredients

- currants, very ripe
- ¾ lb. white sugar for 1 quart of juice
- ½ pint of water for 1 quart of juice

## Directions

1. Pick, stem, mash, and strain the currants
2. Stir all together long and well
3. Put into a clean cask, leaving out the bung, and covering the whole with a bit of lace or mosquito net
4. Let it ferment about four weeks
5. Rack off when it is quite still, and bottle
