## Ingredients

- round loaves
- oysters
- water
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Take little round loaves, cut off the tops
2. Scrape out all the crumbs
3. Put the oysters into a stew pan with the crumbs that came out of the loaves, a little water, and a good lump of butter
4. Stew them together ten or fifteen minutes
5. Put in a spoonful of good cream
6. Fill your loaves
7. Lay the bit of crust carefully on again
8. Set them in the oven to crisp
