## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 can grated pineapple or 1 pineapple shredded
- 1⅓ cups orange juice
- ½ cup lemon juice
- 1 quart Apollinaris

## Directions

1. Prepare and freeze same as Pineapple Frappé.
