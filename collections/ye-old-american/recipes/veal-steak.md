## Ingredients

- veal steak
- four or five young onions
- a great teaspoonful of tomato catsup or twice the quantity of stewed tomato
- a lump of butter the size of an egg
- a little thyme or parsley
- a small teacupful of hot water
- browned flour
- a half-glass of wine

## Directions

1. Broil upon a well-greased gridiron over a clear fire
2. Turn frequently while the steaks are cooking
3. Put into a saucepan four or five young onions minced fine, a great teaspoonful of tomato catsup, or twice the quantity of stewed tomato, a lump of butter the size of an egg, and a little thyme or parsley, with a small teacupful of hot water
4. Let them stew together while the steaks are broiling
5. Thicken before turning the gravy out with a spoonful of browned flour
6. Add a half-glass of wine if you please
7. Boil up once hard
8. When the steaks are dished with a small bit of butter upon each, pour the mixture over and around them
