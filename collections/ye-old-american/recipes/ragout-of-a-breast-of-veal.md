## Ingredients

- brisket
- gravy
- wine
- walnut catsup
- mushroom catsup
- curry powder
- garlic
- sweatbreads

## Directions

1. Separate the joints of the brisket, and saw off the sharp ends of the ribs
2. Trim it neatly, and half roast it
3. Put it in a stew pan with a quart of good gravy seasoned with wine, walnut and mushroom catsup, a tea-spoonful of curry powder, and a few cloves of garlic
4. Stew it till tender
5. Thicken the gravy
6. And garnish with sweatbreads nicely broiled
