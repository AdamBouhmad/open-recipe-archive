## Ingredients

- celery
- one-half cup bread flour
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- pepper
- one-third cup milk
- one egg
- deep fat
- Tomato Sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape celery, cut in three-inch pieces.
2. Mix one-half cup bread flour, one-fourth teaspoon salt, a few grains pepper, one-third cup milk, and one egg well beaten to make batter.
3. Dip celery pieces in batter.
4. Fry in deep fat.
5. Drain on brown paper.
6. Serve with Tomato Sauce.
