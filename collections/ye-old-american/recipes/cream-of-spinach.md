## Ingredients

- spinach
- four ounces of fresh butter
- a little grated nutmeg
- a teaspoonful of salt
- two quarts of strong stock
- a tablespoonful of butter
- a teaspoonful of granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Pick, wash and boil enough spinach to measure a pint, when cooked, chopped and pounded into a soft paste
2. Put it into a stewpan with four ounces of fresh butter, a little grated nutmeg, a teaspoonful of salt
3. Cook and stir it about ten minutes
4. Add to this two quarts of strong stock; let boil up, then rub it through a strainer
5. Set it over the fire again, and, when on the point of boiling, mix with it a tablespoonful of butter, and a teaspoonful of granulated sugar
