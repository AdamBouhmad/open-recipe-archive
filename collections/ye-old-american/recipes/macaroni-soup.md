## Ingredients

- a rich beef or other soup
- pepper or salt
- half a pound of small pipe macaroni

## Directions

1. To a rich beef or other soup, in which there is no seasoning other than pepper or salt, take half a pound of small pipe macaroni.
2. Boil it in clear water until it is tender.
3. Drain it and cut it in pieces of an inch length.
4. Boil it for fifteen minutes in the soup and serve.
