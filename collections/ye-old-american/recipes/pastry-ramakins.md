## Ingredients

- odd pieces of light puff paste
- grated cheese
- yolk of egg

## Directions

1. Take the remains or odd pieces of any light puff paste left from pies or tarts.
2. Gather up the pieces of paste, roll it out evenly, and sprinkle it with grated cheese of a nice flavor.
3. Fold the paste in three, roll it out again, and sprinkle more cheese over.
4. Fold the paste, roll it out, and with a paste-cutter shape it in any way that may be desired.
5. Brush the ramakins over with yolk of egg before they are placed in the oven.
6. Bake the ramakins in a brisk oven from ten to fifteen minutes.
7. Dish them on a hot napkin and serve quickly.
