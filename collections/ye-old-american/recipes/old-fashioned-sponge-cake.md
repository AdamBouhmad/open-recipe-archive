## Ingredients

- Two cups of sifted white sugar
- two cups of flour measured before sifting
- ten eggs
- a pinch of salt
- lemon
- two large teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Stir the yolks and sugar together until perfectly light
2. add a pinch of salt
3. beat the whites of the eggs to a very stiff froth and add them with the flour, after beating together lightly
4. flavor with lemon
5. Bake in a moderate oven about forty-five minutes
