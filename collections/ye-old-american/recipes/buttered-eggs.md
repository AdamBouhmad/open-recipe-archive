## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 egg
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Heat omelet pan.
2. Put in one tablespoon butter; when melted, slip in an egg, and cook until the white is firm.
3. Turn it over once while cooking.
4. Add more butter as needed, using just enough to keep egg from sticking.
