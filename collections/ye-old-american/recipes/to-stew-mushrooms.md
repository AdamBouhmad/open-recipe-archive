## Ingredients

- Small button mushrooms
- Water
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter
- Flour
- Cream or rich milk
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Wash the mushrooms clean, peel off the skin, and cut off the stalks
2. Put the trimmings into a small sauce-pan with just enough water to keep them from burning, covering them closely, and let them stew a quarter of an hour
3. Strain the liquor and put the mushrooms into a clean sauce-pan
4. Add the liquid to them with a little nutmeg, pepper and salt, and a piece of butter rolled in flour
5. Stew them fifteen minutes
6. Just before taking them up, stir in a very little cream or rich milk and some beaten yolk of egg
7. Serve them hot, keeping the pan closely covered and shaking it round frequently
