## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1½ cups sugar
- Whites six eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 2½ cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon cream of tartar
- ½ teaspoon almond extract

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for mixing butter cakes. Bake forty-five to fifty minutes in deep, narrow pans. Cover with white frosting.
