## Ingredients

- a chicken
- half a pound of macaroni
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- chicken liquor
- a cup of cream

## Directions

1. Boil a chicken until very tender, take out all the bones, and pick up the meat quite fine
2. Boil half a pound of macaroni until tender, first breaking it up to pieces an inch long
3. Butter a deep pudding dish
4. Put on the bottom a layer of the cooked macaroni, then a layer of the minced chicken, bits of butter, pepper and salt, then some of the chicken liquor
5. Put another layer of macaroni, and so on, until the dish is filled
6. Pour a cup of cream over the whole
7. Bake half an hour
8. Serve on a platter
