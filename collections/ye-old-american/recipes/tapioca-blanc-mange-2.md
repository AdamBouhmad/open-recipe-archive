## Ingredients

- 1 cup of tapioca
- 2 cups cold water
- 3 cups boiling milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- Rose-water or vanilla

## Directions

1. Wash tapioca well before soaking
2. Soak the tapioca four hours
3. Stir, with the water in which it was soaked, into the boiling milk
4. Sweeten and boil slowly, stirring all the while, fifteen minutes
5. Take off, flavor and pour into moulds
6. Eat cold with cream
