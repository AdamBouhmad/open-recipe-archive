## Ingredients

- one cup of dark cooking molasses
- one cup of sour cream
- one teaspoonful of soda
- a little warm water
- a teaspoonful of salt
- one heaping teaspoonful of ginger

## Directions

1. Dissolve soda in a little warm water.
2. Mix ingredients together about as thick as cup cake.
3. To be eaten warm.
