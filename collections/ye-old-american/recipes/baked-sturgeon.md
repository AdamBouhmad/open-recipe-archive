## Ingredients

- sturgeon (five or six pounds)
- salt
- water
- bread-crumbs
- fat salt pork
- sweet herbs
- butter
- drawn butter
- caper sauce
- catsup

## Directions

1. A piece of sturgeon weighing five or six pounds is enough for a handsome dish.
2. Skin it and let it stand in salt and water for half an hour.
3. Parboil it to remove the oil.
4. Make a dressing of bread-crumbs, minute bits of fat salt pork, sweet herbs, and butter.
5. Gash the upper part of the fish quite deeply, and rub this force-meat well in.
6. Put in a baking-pan with a little water to keep it from burning, and bake for an hour.
7. Serve with a sauce of drawn butter, in which has been stirred a spoonful of caper sauce and another of catsup.
