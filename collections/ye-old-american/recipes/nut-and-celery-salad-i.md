## Ingredients

- English walnut or pecan nut meat
- celery
- French Dressing
- shredded lettuce

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts of English walnut or pecan nut meat cut in pieces, and celery cut in small pieces
2. Marinate with French Dressing
3. Serve with a border of shredded lettuce
