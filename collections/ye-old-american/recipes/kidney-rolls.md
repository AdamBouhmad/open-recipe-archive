## Ingredients

- one-half cup stale bread crumbs
- one-half small onion, finely chopped
- one-half tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- salt and pepper
- beaten egg
- thin slices of bacon
- lambs' kidney

## Directions

1. Mix one-half cup stale bread crumbs, one-half small onion finely chopped, and one-half tablespoon finely chopped parsley.
2. Season with salt and pepper and moisten with beaten egg.
3. Spread mixture on thin slices of bacon.
4. Fasten around pieces of lambs' kidney, using skewers.
5. Bake in a hot oven twenty minutes.
