## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 5 ozs. flour
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla
- Shredded almonds

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, eggs slightly beaten, flour, and flavoring.
2. Drop by spoonfuls on an inverted buttered dripping-pan.
3. Spread very thinly, using a knife, in circular shapes about three inches in diameter.
4. Sprinkle with almonds, and bake in a slow oven.
5. Remove from pan, and shape at once over the handle of a wooden spoon.
