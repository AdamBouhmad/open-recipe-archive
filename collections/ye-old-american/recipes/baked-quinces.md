## Ingredients

- quinces
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and quarter; extract the seeds and stew the fruit in clear water until a straw will pierce them
2. Put into a baking-dish with a half cupful of sugar to every eight quinces
3. Pour over them the liquor in which they were boiled
4. Cover closely, and steam in the oven one hour
5. Take out the quinces, lay them in a covered bowl to keep warm
6. Return the syrup to the saucepan, and boil twenty minutes
7. Pour over the quinces, and set away covered, to cool
8. Eat cold
