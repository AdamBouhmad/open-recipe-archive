## Ingredients

- 3 lemons to a quart of water
- 6 tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Pare the yellow peel from the lemons, and, unless you mean to use the Sherbet immediately, leave it out
2. Slice and squeeze the lemons upon the sugar
3. Add a very little water, and let them stand fifteen minutes
4. Fill up with water; ice well, stir, and pour out
