## Ingredients

- cold roast lamb
- two tablespoons butter
- three-fourths tablespoon vinegar
- one-fourth cup currant jelly
- one-fourth teaspoon French mustard
- salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. Cut cold roast lamb in thin slices.
2. Reheat in sauce made by melting two tablespoons butter.
3. Add three-fourths tablespoon vinegar, one-fourth cup currant jelly, one-fourth teaspoon French mustard, and salt and cayenne to taste.
