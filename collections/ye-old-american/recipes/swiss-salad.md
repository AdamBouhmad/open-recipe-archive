## Ingredients

- one cup cold cooked chicken
- one cucumber
- one cup chopped English walnut meats
- one cup French peas
- French Dressing
- Mayonnaise Dressing

## Directions

1. Mix one cup cold cooked chicken cut in cubes, one cucumber pared and cut in cubes, one cup chopped English walnut meats, and one cup French peas.
2. Marinate with French Dressing, arrange on serving dish, and garnish with Mayonnaise Dressing.
