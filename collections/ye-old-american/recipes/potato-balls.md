## Ingredients

- mashed potatos
- egg
- flour
- bread crumbs
- clean dripping

## Directions

1. Mix mashed potatos with the yelk of an egg, roll them into balls, flour them, or cover them with egg and bread crumbs
2. Fry them in clean dripping, or brown them in a Dutch oven
