## Ingredients

- 1 cup fine Entire Wheat Flour
- ½ cup pastry flour
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons lard
- ½ cup butter
- Cold water
- slightly beaten white of one egg
- 1 teaspoon cold water
- chopped nut meat
- salt

## Directions

1. Make same as Plain Paste
2. Roll to one-fourth inch in thickness
3. Cut in finger-shaped pieces
4. Bake and cool
5. Brush over with slightly beaten white of one egg diluted with one teaspoon cold water
6. Sprinkle with chopped nut meat seasoned with salt
7. Return to oven to slightly brown nut meats
8. Serve with salad course
