## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- three quarters of a pint of water
- two pounds of sifted flour
- white of egg
- venison, game, or poultry

## Directions

1. Cut up the butter and put it into a sauce-pan with water
2. Cover it and set it on hot coals
3. Have ready two pounds of sifted flour in a pan and make a hole in the middle
4. Pour in the melted butter as soon as it boils
5. Gradually mix in the flour with a spoon
6. When well mixed, knead it with your hands into a stiff dough
7. Sprinkle your paste-board with flour and lay the dough upon it
8. Continue to knead it with your hands till it no longer sticks and is quite light
9. Let it stand an hour to cool
10. Cut off pieces for the bottom and top and roll them out thick
11. Roll out a long piece for the sides or walls and fix it on the bottom to stand up all round
12. Cement them together with white of egg, pinching and closing firmly
13. Put in the ingredients of your pie
14. Lay on the lid or top crust, pinching the edges closely together
15. Ornament the sides and top with leaves or flowers of paste shaped with a tin cutter
16. Notch or scollop the edges handsomely
17. Glaze it all over with white of egg
18. Bake it four hours
