## Ingredients

- ¾ cup apricot pulp
- ¾ cup heavy cream
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Drain canned apricots from their syrup, and rub through a sieve. Beat cream until stiff, add to apricot pulp, and sweeten to taste. Serve with German toast.
