## Ingredients

- butter
- thin slices of bread
- sugar
- cinnamon
- chopped apple or fruit
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful sifted flour
- 3 cupfuls milk
- salt
- wine and lemon sauce

## Directions

1. Butter sides and bottom of deep pudding-dish
2. Butter thin slices of bread and sprinkle with sugar, cinnamon, chopped apple or fruit between each slice until dish is full
3. Beat up 2 eggs and add flour
4. Stir with milk and salt
5. Pour over bread and let stand one hour
6. Bake slowly covered three-quarters of an hour, then remove cover and brown
7. Serve with wine and lemon sauce
