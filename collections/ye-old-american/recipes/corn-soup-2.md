## Ingredients

- corn
- one dozen ears of corn
- a gallon of water
- a pint of new milk
- two well-beaten eggs
- salt
- pepper
- a tablespoonful of good butter
- two tablespoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Cut the corn from the cob, and boil the cobs in water for at least an hour
2. Then add the grains, and boil until they are thoroughly done
3. Put one dozen ears of corn to a gallon of water, which will be reduced to three quarts by the time the soup is done
4. Pour on a pint of new milk, two well-beaten eggs, salt and pepper to your taste
5. Continue the boiling a while longer, and stir in a tablespoonful of good butter rubbed up with two tablespoonfuls of flour to season and thicken it a little
