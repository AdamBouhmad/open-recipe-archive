## Ingredients

- Gelatine
- Eggs
- Powdered sugar
- Hot cream
- Remaining sugar
- 4 bananas, mashed pulp
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Whip from 3½ cups cream
- 12 lady fingers

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cold water, beat whites of eggs slightly, add powdered sugar, and gradually hot cream, cook over hot water until it thickens
2. Add soaked gelatine and remaining sugar, strain into a pan set in ice-water
3. Add bananas and lemon juice, stir until it begins to thicken
4. Fold in whip from cream
5. Line a melon mould with lady fingers trimmed to just fit sections of mould
6. Turn in the mixture, spread evenly, and chill
