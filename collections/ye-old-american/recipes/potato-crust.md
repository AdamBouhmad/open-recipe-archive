## Ingredients

- a dozen medium-sized potatoes
- one good teaspoonful of salt
- two tablespoonfuls of cold butter
- half a cupful of milk or cream
- flour sufficient to roll out

## Directions

1. Boil and mash the potatoes
2. add salt, butter and milk or cream
3. Stiffen with flour sufficient to roll out
