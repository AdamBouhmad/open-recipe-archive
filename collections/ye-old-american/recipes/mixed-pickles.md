## Ingredients

- cauliflower heads
- small onions
- peppers
- cucumbers
- nasturtiums
- green beans
- cider vinegar
- sugar
- salt
- mustard

## Directions

1. Scald in salt water until tender cauliflower heads, small onions, peppers, cucumbers cut in dice, nasturtiums and green beans
2. Drain until dry and pack into wide-mouthed bottles
3. Boil in each pint of cider vinegar one tablespoonful of sugar, half a teaspoonful of salt and two tablespoonfuls of mustard
4. Pour over the pickle and seal carefully
5. Other spices may be added if liked
