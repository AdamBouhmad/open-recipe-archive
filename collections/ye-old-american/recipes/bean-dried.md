## Ingredients

- 1 quart kidney beans, rice beans, field beans, or French mock-turtle soup beans
- 1 gallon of cold water
- 2 pounds of salt pork
- 1 head of celery
- pepper
- cayenne pepper
- lemon slices

## Directions

1. Soak a quart of beans over night in soft lukewarm water.
2. Put them over the fire next morning, with one gallon of cold water and about two pounds of salt pork.
3. Boil slowly for three hours, keeping the pot well covered.
4. Shred into it a head of celery, add pepper—cayenne, if preferred.
5. Simmer half an hour longer, strain through a cullender, and serve, with slices of lemon passed to each guest.
