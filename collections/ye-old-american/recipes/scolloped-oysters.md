## Ingredients

- stale bread
- butter
- oysters
- cayenne
- nutmeg
- oyster liquor

## Directions

1. Having grated a sufficiency of stale bread, butter a deep dish, and line the sides and bottom thickly with bread crumbs.
2. Then put in a layer of seasoned oysters, with a few very small bits of butter on them.
3. Cover them thickly with crumbs, and put in another layer of oysters and butter, till the dish is filled up, having a thick layer of crumbs on the top.
4. Put the dish into an oven, and bake them a very short time, or they will shrivel.
5. Serve them up hot.
