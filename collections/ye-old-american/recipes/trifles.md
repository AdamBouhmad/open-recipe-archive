## Ingredients

- one egg
- a tablespoonful of sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Work one egg and a tablespoonful of sugar to as much flour as will make a stiff paste
2. Roll it as thin as a dollar piece
3. Cut it into small round or square cakes
4. Drop two or three at a time into the boiling lard
5. When they rise to the surface and turn over they are done
6. Take them out with a skimmer and lay them on an inverted sieve to drain
7. When served for dessert or supper put a spoonful of jelly on each
