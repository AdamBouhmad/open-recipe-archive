## Ingredients

- 6 pound of flour
- 3 of sugar
- 2 and a half pound of shortning (half butter, half lard)
- 6 eggs
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 ounce of cinnamon
- 1 ounce of coriander seed
- 1 pint of emptins
- 2 gills brandy
- 1 pint of milk
- 3 pound of raisins

## Directions

1. Mix 6 pound of flour, 3 of sugar, 2 and a half pound of shortning (half butter, half lard), 6 eggs, 1 nutmeg, 1 ounce of cinnamon and 1 ounce of coriander seed, 1 pint of emptins, 2 gills brandy, 1 pint of milk and 3 pound of raisins
