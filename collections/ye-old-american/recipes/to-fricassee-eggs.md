## Ingredients

- six eggs
- flour
- one egg
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- boiling lard
- rich well seasoned gravy
- crisped parsley

## Directions

1. Boil six eggs for five minutes
2. Lay them in cold water
3. Peel them carefully
4. Dredge them lightly with flour
5. Beat one egg light
6. Dip the hard eggs in
7. Roll them in bread crumbs seasoned with pepper, salt, and grated nutmeg
8. Cover them well with this
9. Let them stand some time to dry
10. Fry them in boiling lard
11. Serve them up with any kind of rich, well seasoned gravy
12. Garnish with crisped parsley
