## Ingredients

- half a cupful of hot water
- teaspoonful of lemon-juice
- three tablespoonfuls of veal or chicken broth
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- tablespoonful of butter
- minced parsley
- well-whipped egg
- poached eggs

## Directions

1. Make the sauce by putting half a cupful of hot water in a saucepan, with a teaspoonful of lemon-juice, three tablespoonfuls of veal or chicken broth (strained), pepper, salt, mace, and a tablespoonful of butter, with a little minced parsley.
2. Boil slowly ten minutes, and stir in a well-whipped egg carefully, lest it should curdle.
3. Have ready some poached eggs in a deep dish, and pour the sauce over them.
