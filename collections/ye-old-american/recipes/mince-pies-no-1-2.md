## Ingredients

- Four pounds of lean boiled beef chopped fine
- Twice as much of chopped green tart apples
- One pound of chopped suet
- Three pounds of raisins, seeded
- Two pounds of currants picked over, washed and dried
- Half a pound of citron, cut up fine
- One pound of brown sugar
- One quart of cooking molasses
- Two quarts of sweet cider
- One pint of boiled cider
- One tablespoonful of salt
- One tablespoonful of pepper
- One tablespoonful of mace
- One tablespoonful of allspice
- Four tablespoonfuls of cinnamon
- Two grated nutmegs
- One tablespoonful of cloves
- One pint of good brandy
- One pint of Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly and warm it on the range until heated through
2. Remove from the fire and when nearly cool, stir in a pint of good brandy and one pint of Madeira wine
3. Put into a crock, cover it tightly and set it in a cold place where it will not freeze, but keep perfectly cold
