## Ingredients

- cold broiled steak or roast beef
- water
- one-half onion
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- potatoes
- buttered pudding-dish
- baking-powder biscuit mixture or pie-crust

## Directions

1. Cut remnants of cold broiled steak or roast beef in one-inch cubes
2. Cover with boiling water
3. add one-half onion
4. cook slowly one hour
5. Remove onion
6. thicken gravy with flour diluted with cold water
7. season with salt and pepper
8. Add potatoes cut in one-fourth inch slices, which have been parboiled eight minutes in boiling salted water
9. Put in a buttered pudding-dish
10. cool
11. cover with baking-powder biscuit mixture or pie-crust
12. Bake in a hot oven
13. If covered with pie crust, make several incisions in crust that gases may escape
