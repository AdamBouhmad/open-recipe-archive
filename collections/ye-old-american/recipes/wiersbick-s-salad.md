## Ingredients

- small tomatoes
- lettuce leaves
- cucumber slices
- truffle
- green pepper
- three tablespoons Louit Frères mustard
- one-fourth teaspoon salt
- one-eighth teaspoon paprika
- one tablespoon vinegar
- one-half teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
- one-half cup olive oil

## Directions

1. Peel small tomatoes of uniform size and scoop out a portion of centres
2. Arrange in nests of lettuce leaves
3. Garnish top of each with a slice of cucumber, slice of truffle cut in fancy shape, and ring of green pepper
4. Mix three tablespoons Louit Frères mustard, one-fourth teaspoon salt, one-eighth teaspoon paprika, one tablespoon vinegar, and one-half teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
5. Add slowly, while stirring constantly, one-half cup olive oil
6. Serve with this dressing
