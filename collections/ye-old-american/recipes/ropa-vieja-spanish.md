## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- cold meat or fowl

## Directions

1. Peel the skin from ripe tomatoes
2. Put them in a pan with a spoonful of melted butter, some pepper and salt
3. Shred cold meat or fowl
4. Put it in
5. Fry it sufficiently
