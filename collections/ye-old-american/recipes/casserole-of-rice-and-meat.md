## Ingredients

- steamed rice
- two cups cold, finely chopped, cooked mutton
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- celery salt
- onion juice
- lemon juice
- one-fourth cup cracker crumbs
- one egg slightly beaten
- hot stock or water
- Tomato Sauce
- veal

## Directions

1. Line a mould, slightly greased, with steamed rice.
2. Fill the centre with two cups cold, finely chopped, cooked mutton, highly seasoned with salt, pepper, cayenne, celery salt, onion juice and lemon juice.
3. Add one-fourth cup cracker crumbs, one egg slightly beaten, and enough hot stock or water to moisten.
4. Cover meat with rice.
5. Cover rice with buttered paper to keep out moisture while steaming.
6. Steam forty-five minutes.
7. Serve on a platter surrounded with Tomato Sauce. Veal may be used in place of mutton.
