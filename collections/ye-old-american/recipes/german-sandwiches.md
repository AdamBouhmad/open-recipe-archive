## Ingredients

- Zweiback
- jelly or marmalade
- finely cut English walnut meats

## Directions

1. Use Zweiback.
2. Spread slices, thinly cut, with jelly or marmalade.
3. Sprinkle with finely cut English walnut meats.
4. Cover with thinly cut slices and remove crusts.
