## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup macaroni broken in inch pieces
- 2 quarts boiling water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups White Sauce
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups scalded milk

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water twenty minutes or until soft
2. Drain in strainer
3. Pour over it cold water to prevent pieces from adhering
4. Reheat in White Sauce
5. For White Sauce: Melt two tablespoons butter
6. Add two tablespoons flour with one-half teaspoon salt
7. Pour on slowly one and one-half cups scalded milk
