## Ingredients

- paste
- jam or preserve
- stars or leaves of paste
- strips of paste
- Dried fruit or cranberries

## Directions

1. Use larger pans required for tarts proper, the size of small, shallow pie-tins
2. bake the paste and cool
3. fill with the jam or preserve
4. place a few stars or leaves on the top, or strips of paste, criss-crossed on the top, all of which have been previously baked on a tin by themselves
5. Dried fruit, stewed until thick, makes fine tart pies, also cranberries stewed and well sweetened
