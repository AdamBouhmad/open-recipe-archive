## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1½ tablespoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup sugar
- 2 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients; beat egg, add milk, and pour slowly on first mixture.
2. Beat thoroughly, and add butter.
3. Cook same as Sour Milk Griddle-cakes.
4. Begin cooking cakes at once or more baking powder will be required.
