## Ingredients

- crab-apples
- water

## Directions

1. Select apples that should be juicy and ripe
2. Quarter the fruit and remove the black spots in the cores
3. Put into a preserving kettle over the fire with a teacupful of water in the bottom to prevent burning
4. Add more water as it evaporates while cooking
5. When boiled to a pulp, strain the apples through a coarse flannel
6. Proceed as for currant jelly
