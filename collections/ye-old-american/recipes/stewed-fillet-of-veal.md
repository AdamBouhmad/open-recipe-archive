## Ingredients

- veal fillet
- sliced ham which has been already boiled
- two large cups of boiling water
- flour

## Directions

1. Stuff and bind with twine as for roasting
2. Cover the top and sides with sliced ham which has been already boiled securing with skewers or twine crossing the meat in all directions
3. Lay in a pot and put in two large cups of boiling water
4. Cover immediately and closely and stew gently for four or five hours
5. Remove the cover as seldom as possible and only to ascertain whether the water has boiled away
6. If it is too low replenish from the boiling kettle
7. Take off the strings when the meat is done
8. Arrange the ham about the fillet in the dish and serve a bit with each slice of veal
9. Strain the gravy thicken with flour boil up once and send in a boat
