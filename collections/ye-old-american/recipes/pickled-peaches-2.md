## Ingredients

- 10 lbs. fruit—pared
- 4½ lbs. sugar
- 1 quart vinegar
- mace
- cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Lay the peaches in the sugar for an hour; drain off every drop of syrup, and put over the fire with about a cup of water.
2. Boil until the scum ceases to rise.
3. Skim; put in the fruit and boil five minutes.
4. Take out the peaches with a perforated skimmer, and spread upon dishes to cool.
5. Add the vinegar and spices to the syrup.
6. Boil fifteen minutes longer, and pour over the fruit in glass jars.
