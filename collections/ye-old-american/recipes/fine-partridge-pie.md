## Ingredients

- partridges
- fresh mushrooms
- grated bread crumbs
- cream
- beaten yolk of egg
- rich paste
- cold boiled ham
- whole button mushrooms
- hard boiled yolks of eggs
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Truss your partridges and loosen all the joints with a knife, but do not cut them apart
2. Scald, peel, and chop some fresh mushrooms
3. Mix the mushrooms with grated bread crumbs moistened with cream and beaten yolk of egg
4. Stuff the partridges with this mixture
5. Cover the sides and bottom of a deep dish with a rich paste
6. Add a layer of cold boiled ham sliced very thin
7. Add some whole button mushrooms and some hard boiled yolks of eggs
8. Season with pepper only
9. Put in the partridges, laying on each a bit of butter rolled in flour
10. Cover the whole with a thick lid of paste handsomely notched and ornamented with paste leaves
11. Before putting on the cover, pour a little water into the pie
