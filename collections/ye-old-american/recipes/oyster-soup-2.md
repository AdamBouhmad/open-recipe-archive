## Ingredients

- 1 quart oysters
- 4 cups milk
- 1 slice onion
- 2 stalks celery
- 2 blades mace
- Sprig of parsley
- Bit of bay leaf
- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅓ cup flour
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and pick over oysters as for Oyster Stew; reserve liquor, add oysters slightly chopped, heat slowly to boiling-point, and let simmer twenty minutes
2. Strain through cheese-cloth, reheat liquor, and thicken with butter and flour cooked together
3. Scald milk with onion, celery, mace, parsley, and bay leaf; remove seasonings, and add to oyster liquor
4. Season with salt and pepper
