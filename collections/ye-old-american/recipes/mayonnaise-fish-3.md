## Ingredients

- cold boiled fish (halibut, rock or cod)
- eggs
- salad oil or butter
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- white sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a pound or so of cold boiled fish (halibut, rock or cod), not chop, but cut, into pieces an inch in length
2. Mix in a bowl a dressing as follows: The yolks of four boiled eggs rubbed to a smooth paste with salad oil or butter
3. Add to these salt, pepper, mustard, two teaspoonfuls of white sugar, and, lastly, six tablespoonfuls of vinegar
4. Beat the mixture until light, and just before pouring it over the fish, stir in lightly the frothed white of a raw egg
