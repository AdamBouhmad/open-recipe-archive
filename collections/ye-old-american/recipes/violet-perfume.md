## Ingredients

- twelve drops of genuine oil of rhodium
- a lump of loaf-sugar
- two ounces of orris root powder

## Directions

1. Drop twelve drops of genuine oil of rhodium on a lump of loaf-sugar
2. Pound the sugar in a marble mortar with two ounces of orris root powder
3. Sew up the powder in little silk bags, or keep it in a tight box
