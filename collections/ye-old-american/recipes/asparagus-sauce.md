## Ingredients

- A dozen heads of asparagus
- 2 teacupfuls drawn butter
- 2 eggs
- The juice of half a lemon
- Salt and white pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the tender heads in a very little salted water. Drain and chop them. Have ready a pint of drawn butter, with two raw eggs beaten into it; add the asparagus, and season, squeezing in the lemon-juice last. The butter must be hot, but do not cook after putting in the asparagus heads.
