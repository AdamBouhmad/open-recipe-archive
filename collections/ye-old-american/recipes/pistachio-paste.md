## Ingredients

- Marshmallow Paste
- Few drops extract of almond
- One-third cup pistachio nuts blanched and chopped
- Leaf green to color

## Directions

1. To Marshmallow Paste add a few drops extract of almond, one-third cup pistachio nuts blanched and chopped, and leaf green to color.
2. Use same as Marshmallow Paste.
