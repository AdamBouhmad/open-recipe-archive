## Ingredients

- one pound of grated cocoanut
- a quart of milk
- eight eggs
- four tablespoonfuls of sifted white sugar
- a glass of wine
- a small cracker
- two spoonfuls of melted butter
- half a nutmeg
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Cut off the brown part of the cocoanut, grate the white part
2. Mix it with milk and set it on the fire and let it boil slowly eight or ten minutes
3. The eggs and sugar should be beaten together to a froth
4. Stir in the wine
5. Put them into the milk and cocoanut, which should be first allowed to get quite cool
6. Add the cracker and nutmeg
7. Turn the whole into deep pie plates, with a lining and rim of puff paste
8. Bake them as soon as turned into the plates
