## Ingredients

- 3 cups stale bread crumbs
- 1/2 cup melted butter
- salt and pepper
- few drops onion juice
- 1 pint oysters

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in the order given.
2. Add oysters, cleaned and drained from their liquor.
