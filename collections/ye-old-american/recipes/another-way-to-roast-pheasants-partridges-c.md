## Ingredients

- fine raw oysters
- salt
- nutmeg
- egg yolk
- cold ham or bacon
- white paper
- butter
- oyster-sauce

## Directions

1. Chop some fine raw oysters, omitting the hard part; mix them with salt, and nutmeg, and add some beaten yolk of egg to bind the other ingredients.
2. Cut some very thin slices of cold ham or bacon, and cover the birds with them.
3. Wrap them closely in sheets of white paper well buttered, put them on the spit, and roast them before a clear fire.
4. Send them to table with oyster-sauce in a boat.
