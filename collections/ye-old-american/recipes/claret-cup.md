## Ingredients

- 1 quart claret wine
- ½ cup Curaçoa
- 1 quart Apollinaris
- ⅓ cup orange juice
- 2 tablespoons brandy
- Sugar
- Mint leaves
- Cucumber rind
- 12 strawberries

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients, except Apollinaris, using enough sugar to sweeten to taste.
2. Stand on ice to chill, and add chilled Apollinaris just before serving.
