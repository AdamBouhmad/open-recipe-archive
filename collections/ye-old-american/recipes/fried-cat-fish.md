## Ingredients

- cat-fish
- salt
- eggs
- powdered cracker
- lard
- dripping

## Directions

1. Skin, clean, and remove the heads.
2. Sprinkle with salt, and lay aside for an hour or more.
3. Have ready two or three eggs beaten to a froth, and, in a flat dish, a quantity of powdered cracker.
4. Dip the fish first in the egg, then in the cracker, and fry quickly in hot lard or dripping.
5. Take up as quick as done.
