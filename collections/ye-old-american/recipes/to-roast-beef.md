## Ingredients

- a brisk hot fire
- salt and water for basting
- one quarter of an hour to every pound of beef
- pricking with a fork to determine doneness

## Directions

1. Have a brisk hot fire.
2. Hang down rather than to spit.
3. Baste with salt and water.
4. One quarter of an hour to every pound of beef, though tender beef will require less, while old tough beef will require more roasting.
5. Pricking with a fork will determine you whether done or not.
6. Rare done is the healthiest and the taste of this age.
