## Ingredients

- one package of gelatine
- one cupful of cold water
- three cupfuls of sugar
- juice of three lemons
- grated rind of one lemon
- one quart of boiling water
- one pint of sherry wine

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine and cold water together two hours
2. Add three cupfuls of sugar, juice of three lemons and grated rind of one
3. Pour over this a quart of boiling water and stir until dissolved
4. Add one pint of sherry wine
5. Strain through a napkin
6. Turn into molds dipped in cold water
7. Place in the ice box for several hours
