## Ingredients

- 2 cups of milk, boiling
- 2 tablespoonfuls rice-flour
- cold milk for wetting the rice-flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar

## Directions

1. Wet up the rice-flour with cold milk
2. Boil ten minutes, stirring all the while
3. Flavor to taste
4. Eat warm with cream
