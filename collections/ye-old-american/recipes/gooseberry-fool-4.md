## Ingredients

- quart of ripe gooseberries
- water
- tablespoonful of melted butter
- cupful of sugar
- yolks of three eggs
- beaten whites of eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Stew a quart of ripe gooseberries in just enough water to cover them
2. When soft, rub them through a colander to remove the skins and seeds
3. While hot stir into them a tablespoonful of melted butter and a cupful of sugar
4. Beat the yolks of three eggs and add that
5. Whip all together until light
6. Fill a large glass fruit dish and spread on the top the beaten whites mixed with three tablespoonfuls of sugar
