## Ingredients

- one cupful of sugar
- four eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of cornstarch
- one pint of milk
- one tablespoonful of butter
- juice and rind of two lemons

## Directions

1. Wet the cornstarch in some of the milk, then stir it into the remainder of the milk, which should be boiling on the stove, stirring constantly and briskly for five minutes
2. Take it from the stove, stir in the butter and let it cool
3. Beat the yolks and sugar together, then stir them thoroughly into the milk and cornstarch
4. Stir in the lemon juice and grated rind, doing it very gradually, making it very smooth
5. Bake in a well-buttered dish
6. To be eaten cold
