## Ingredients

- sufficient vinegar to cover the mushrooms
- to each quart of mushrooms two blades pounded mace
- one ounce ground pepper
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Choose some nice young button mushrooms for pickling and rub off the skin with a piece of flannel and salt, and cut off the stalks
2. if very large, take out the red inside, and reject the black ones, as they are too old
3. Put them in a stewpan, sprinkle salt over them, with pounded mace and pepper in the above proportion
4. shake them well over a clear fire until the liquor flows and keep them there until it is all dried up again
5. add as much vinegar as will cover them
6. just let it simmer for one minute and store it away in stone jars for use
7. When cold tie down with bladder and keep in a dry place
