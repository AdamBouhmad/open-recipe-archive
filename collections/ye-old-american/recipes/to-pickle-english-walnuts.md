## Ingredients

- young walnuts
- salt and water
- whole pepper
- cloves
- garlic
- mustard seed
- horse-radish
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Gather the walnuts when the nut is so young that you can run a pin into it easily
2. Pour boiling salt and water on them
3. Let them be covered with it nine days, changing it every third day
4. Take them out and put them on dishes in the air for a few minutes
5. Turn them over to make them black much sooner
6. Put them in a pot
7. Strew over some whole pepper, cloves, a little garlic, mustard seed, and horse-radish scraped and dried
8. Cover them with strong cold vinegar
