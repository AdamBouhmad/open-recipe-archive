## Ingredients

- slices of cold boiled ham
- beaten egg
- cracker-crumbs
- fat from salt pork
- salt pork bits
- crisped parsley

## Directions

1. Dip some slices of cold boiled ham—cut rather thick—in beaten egg, then in cracker-crumbs.
2. Fry them in fat extracted from some bits of salt pork.
3. Take the dry fried pork from the pan before putting in the ham.
4. Garnish with crisped parsley.
