## Ingredients

- About three tablespoonfuls of some good preserve
- Cream to fill a quart mold
- Three-quarters of an ounce of isinglass or gelatine
- Half a pint of water

## Directions

1. Rub the preserve through a sieve with as much cream as will fill a quart mold
2. Dissolve three-quarters of an ounce of isinglass or gelatine in half a pint of water
3. When almost cold, mix it well with the cream
4. Put it into a mold
5. Set in a cool place and turn out next day
