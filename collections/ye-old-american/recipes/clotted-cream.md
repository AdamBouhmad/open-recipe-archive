## Ingredients

- one jill of rich milk
- one large wine glass of rose water
- four ounces of white sugar
- yolks of two eggs
- one quart of the best cream

## Directions

1. Mix together a jill of rich milk, a large wine glass of rose water, and four ounces of white sugar
2. Add to it the beaten yolks of two eggs
3. Stir the mixture into a quart of the best cream
4. Set it over hot coals and let it just come to a boil, stirring it all the time
5. Take it off and pour it into a glass bowl and set it away to get cold
6. Eat it with fresh strawberries, raspberries, or with any sort of sweetmeats
