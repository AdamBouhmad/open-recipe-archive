## Ingredients

- Puff or plain paste
- Jam
- Meringue II
- Almonds blanched and shredded
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Roll puff or plain paste one-eighth inch thick; cut a piece ten inches long by seven inches wide.
2. Place on a sheet, wet edges, and put on a half-inch rim.
3. Prick with fork six times, and bake in hot oven.
4. Cool, and spread with jam, cover with Meringue II, and almonds blanched and shredded.
5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and bake.
