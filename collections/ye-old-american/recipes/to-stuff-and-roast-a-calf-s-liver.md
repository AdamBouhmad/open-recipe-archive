## Ingredients

- fresh calf's liver
- forced meat, or stuffing made of part of the liver parboiled
- fat of bacon minced very fine
- sweet herbs powdered
- grated bread
- spice finely powdered
- pepper
- salt
- fat bacon

## Directions

1. Take a fresh calf's liver, and having made a hole in it with a large knife run in lengthways, but not quite through
2. Have ready a forced meat, or stuffing made of part of the liver parboiled, fat of bacon minced very fine, and sweet herbs powdered
3. Add to these some grated bread and spice finely powdered, with pepper and salt
4. With this stuffing fill the hole in the liver, which must be larded with fat bacon
5. And then roasted, flouring it well, and basting with butter till it is enough
6. This is to be served up hot, with gravy sauce having a little wine in it
