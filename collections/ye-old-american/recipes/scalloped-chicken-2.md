## Ingredients

- Butter for baking-dish
- Cold cooked sliced chicken
- Boiled macaroni or rice
- White, Brown, or Tomato Sauce
- Buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Butter a baking-dish.
2. Arrange alternate layers of cold cooked sliced chicken and boiled macaroni or rice.
3. Pour over sauce.
4. Cover with buttered cracker crumbs and bake in a hot oven until crumbs are brown.
