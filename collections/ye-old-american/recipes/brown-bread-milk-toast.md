## Ingredients

- brown bread slices
- 1 pint scalded milk
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2½ tablespoons bread flour
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Make same as Milk Toast, using slices of toasted brown bread in place of white bread.
2. Brown bread is better toasted by first drying slices in oven.
