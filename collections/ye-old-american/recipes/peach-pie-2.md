## Ingredients

- Peaches
- crust
- sugar
- three peach kernels chopped fine to each pie
- a very little water

## Directions

1. Peel, stone and slice the peaches
2. Line a pie plate with crust
3. Lay in your fruit
4. Sprinkle sugar liberally over them in proportion to their sweetness
5. Allow three peach kernels chopped fine to each pie
6. Pour in a very little water
7. Bake with an upper crust, or with cross-bars of paste across the top
