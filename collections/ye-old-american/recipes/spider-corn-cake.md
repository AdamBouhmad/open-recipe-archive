## Ingredients

- two eggs
- one-fourth cup sugar
- one cup sweet milk
- one cup of sour milk
- one teaspoonful soda
- a teaspoonful of salt
- one and two-thirds cups of granulated corn meal
- one-third cup flour
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- one more cup of sweet milk

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs and one-fourth cup sugar together
2. add one cup sweet milk and one cup of sour milk in which you have dissolved one teaspoonful soda
3. Add a teaspoonful of salt
4. mix one and two-thirds cups of granulated corn meal and one-third cup flour with this
5. Put a spider or skillet on the range and when it is hot melt in two tablespoonfuls of butter
6. Turn the spider so that the butter can run up on the sides of the pan
7. Pour in the corn-cake mixture and add one more cup of sweet milk, but do not stir afterwards
8. Put this in the oven and bake from twenty to thirty-five minutes
