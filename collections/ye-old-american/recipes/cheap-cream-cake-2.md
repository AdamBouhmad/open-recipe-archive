## Ingredients

- one cup of sugar
- one egg
- one cup sweet milk
- two cups flour
- one tablespoonful butter
- two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Flavor to taste.
2. Divide into three parts and bake in round shallow pans.
3. For cream: Beat one egg and one-half cup sugar together, then add one-quarter cup flour, wet with a very little milk and stir this mixture into one-half pint of boiling milk, until thick; flavor to taste.
4. Spread the cream when cool between the cakes.
