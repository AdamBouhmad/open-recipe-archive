## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- the yolks of six eggs
- six tablespoonfuls of sugar
- one tablespoonful of sifted flour
- the whites of six eggs
- six tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- vanilla for flavoring
- lemon for flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil a quart of milk
2. When boiling stir into it the well-beaten yolks of six eggs
3. Add six tablespoonfuls of sugar and one tablespoonful of sifted flour, which have been well beaten together
4. When boiled, turn it into a dish
5. Pour over it the whites beaten to a stiff froth, mixing with them six tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
6. Set all in the oven and brown slightly
7. Flavor the top with vanilla and the bottom with lemon
8. Serve cold
