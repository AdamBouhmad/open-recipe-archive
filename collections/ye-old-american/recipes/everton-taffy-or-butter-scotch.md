## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of sugar
- two cupfuls of dark molasses
- one cupful of cold butter
- grated rind of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Boil over a slow fire until it hardens when dropped in cold water
2. Pour thinly into tins well buttered
3. Mark into inch squares before it cools
