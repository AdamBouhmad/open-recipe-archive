## Ingredients

- twelve pippins
- a pound of loaf sugar
- a pint of water
- twelve cloves
- a quart of rich boiled custard

## Directions

1. Pare and core twelve pippins, slice them tolerably thick
2. Put a pound of loaf sugar in a stew pan, with a pint of water and twelve cloves
3. Boil and skim it, then put in the apples, and stew them till clear, and but little of the syrup remains
4. Lay them in a deep dish, and take out the cloves
5. When the apples are cold, pour in a quart of rich boiled custard
6. Set it in water, and make it boil till the custard is set
7. Take care the water does not get into it
