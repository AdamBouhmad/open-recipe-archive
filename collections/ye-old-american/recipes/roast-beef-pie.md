## Ingredients

- roast beef or beefsteak
- rich biscuit dough
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- cold potatoes
- cold gravy
- paste

## Directions

1. Cut up roast beef or beefsteak into thin slices
2. Line a deep dish on the sides with rich biscuit dough rolled very thin, about a quarter of an inch thick
3. Lay some beef slices into the dish
4. Sprinkle with pepper and salt
5. Put in a small bit of butter, a few slices of cold potatoes, and a little cold gravy
6. Make another layer of beef, another layer of seasoning, and continue until the dish is filled
7. Cover the whole with paste leaving a slit in the centre
8. Bake half an hour
