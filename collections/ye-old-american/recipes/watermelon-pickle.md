## Ingredients

- watermelon rind
- water
- white sugar
- vinegar
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil ten pounds of watermelon rind in pure water until tender
2. Drain the water off
3. Make a syrup of two pounds of white sugar, one quart of vinegar, half an ounce of cloves, one ounce of cinnamon
4. Pour the syrup over the rind boiling hot three days in succession
