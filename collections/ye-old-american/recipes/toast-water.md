## Ingredients

- Slices of toast, nicely browned
- boiling water to cover them
- sugar to taste
- ice
- lemon-juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Brown the toast without a symptom of burning
2. Cover closely with boiling water
3. Let them steep until cold
4. Strain the water
5. Sweeten to taste
6. Put a piece of ice in each glassful
7. If the physician thinks it safe, add a little lemon-juice
