## Ingredients

- ham
- flour
- hot water

## Directions

1. Soak for twelve hours
2. Trim away the rusty part from the under side and edges, wipe very dry
3. Cover the bottom with a paste made of flour and hot water, and lay it upside down in the dripping-pan, with water enough to keep it from burning
4. Bake five hours, or allow fully twenty-five minutes to a pound
5. Baste now and then, to prevent the crust from cracking and scaling off
6. When done, peel off the crust and the skin, and glaze as you would a cold ham
7. Put cut paper about the knuckle, and garnish with parsley and sliced red beet—pickled
