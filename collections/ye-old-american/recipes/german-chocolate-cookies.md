## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 bars German chocolate
- ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Grated rind ½ lemon
- 1⅓ cups almonds, blanched and chopped
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder

## Directions

1. Beat eggs until light, add sugar gradually, and continue the beating.
2. Add chocolate, grated, and remaining ingredients.
3. Drop from tip of spoon on a buttered sheet, and bake in a moderate oven.
