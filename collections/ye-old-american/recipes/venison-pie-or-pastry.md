## Ingredients

- neck, breast and shoulder of venison
- fat and lean meat together
- bones and trimmings
- pepper
- salt
- water or veal broth
- good rich paste
- nutmeg
- mace
- bits of butter
- flour
- glass of port wine
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces with fat and lean together
2. Put the bones and trimmings into the stewpan with pepper and salt and water or veal broth enough to cover it
3. Simmer until you have drawn out a good gravy
4. Strain the gravy
5. Make a good rich paste and roll it rather thick
6. Cover the bottom and sides of a deep dish with one sheet of paste
7. Put in your meat, having seasoned it with pepper, salt, nutmeg and mace
8. Pour in the gravy prepared from the trimmings and a glass of port wine
9. Lay on the top some bits of butter rolled in flour
10. Cover the pie with a thick lid of paste and ornament it handsomely with leaves and flowers formed with a tin cutter
11. Bake two or more hours according to the size
12. Just before it is done, pull it forward in the oven and brush it over with beaten egg
13. Push it back and let it slightly brown
