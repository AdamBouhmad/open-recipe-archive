## Ingredients

- ducks
- onions
- gravy
- cream
- flour

## Directions

1. Stuff the ducks as before
2. Cut the roots off small onions, blanch them in scalding water, then pick and put them into a stew pan with a little gravy
3. Set them over a gentle fire, and let them simmer
4. When they are done, thicken them with cream and flour
5. When the ducks are roasted, dish them, pour the ragout of onions over, and serve them up hot
