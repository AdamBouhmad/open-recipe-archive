## Ingredients

- currants, perfectly ripe
- a pound and a quarter of refined sugar per pint of juice

## Directions

1. Currants for jelly should be perfectly ripe and gathered the first week of the season
2. Strip them from the stalks, put them into a stone jar, and set in a vessel of hot water over the fire
3. Keep the water around it boiling until the currants are all broken, stirring them up occasionally
4. Squeeze them through a coarse cloth or towel
5. To each pint of juice allow a pound and a quarter of refined sugar
6. Put the sugar into a porcelain kettle, pour the juice over it, stirring frequently
7. Skim it before it boils; boil about twenty minutes, or until it congeals in the spoon when held in the air
8. Pour it into hot jelly glasses and seal when cool
