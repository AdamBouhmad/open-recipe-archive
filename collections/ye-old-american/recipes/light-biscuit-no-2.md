## Ingredients

- one pint of sponge
- one tablespoonful of melted butter
- one tablespoonful of sugar
- the white of one egg

## Directions

1. Beat egg white to a foam
2. Combine with sponge, butter, and sugar
3. Let rise until light
4. Mold into biscuits
5. When light bake
