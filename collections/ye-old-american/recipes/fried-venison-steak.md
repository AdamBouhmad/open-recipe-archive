## Ingredients

- breast of venison
- quarter of a pound of butter
- salt
- pepper
- wheat flour or rolled crackers
- heaping teaspoonful of flour
- small teacupful of boiling water
- tablespoonful of currant jelly
- glass of wine
- tablespoonful of white sugar

## Directions

1. Cut a breast of venison into steaks
2. Make a quarter of a pound of butter hot in a pan
3. Rub the steaks over with a mixture of a little salt and pepper
4. Dip them in wheat flour or rolled crackers
5. Fry a rich brown until both sides are done
6. Take them up on a dish and put a tin cover over
7. Dredge a heaping teaspoonful of flour into the butter in the pan
8. Stir it with a spoon until it is brown, without burning
9. Put to it a small teacupful of boiling water with a tablespoonful of currant jelly dissolved into it
10. Stir it for a few minutes then strain it over the meat and serve
11. A glass of wine with a tablespoonful of white sugar dissolved in it may be used for the gravy instead of the jelly and water
