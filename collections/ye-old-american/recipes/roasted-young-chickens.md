## Ingredients

- young chickens
- lard
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. When you kill young chickens, pluck them very carefully
2. Truss and put them down to a good fire
3. Dredge and baste them with lard
4. They will take a quarter of an hour in roasting
5. Froth them up
6. Lay them on the dish
7. Pour butter and parsley on
8. Serve them up hot
