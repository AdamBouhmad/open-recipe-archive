## Ingredients

- four pound of flour
- one and half pound of butter
- split pigeons
- turkey pies
- veal
- mutton or birds
- slices of pork
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Four pound of flour, one and half pound of butter rolled into paste, wet with cold water, line the pot therewith, lay in split pigeons, turkey pies, veal, mutton or birds, with slices of pork, salt, pepper, and dust on flour, doing thus till the pot is full or your ingredients expended, add three pints water, cover tight with paste, and stew moderately two and half hours.
