## Ingredients

- dandelions
- boiling salted water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash thoroughly, remove roots, drain, and cook one hour or until tender in boiling salted water.
2. Allow two quarts water to one peck dandelions.
3. Season with butter, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve with vinegar.
