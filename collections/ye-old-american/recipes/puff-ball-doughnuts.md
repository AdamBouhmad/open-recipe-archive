## Ingredients

- Three eggs
- one cupful of sugar
- a pint of sweet milk
- salt
- nutmeg
- flour
- two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Add two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder to the flour
2. Beat all until very light
3. Drop by the dessertspoonful into boiling lard
