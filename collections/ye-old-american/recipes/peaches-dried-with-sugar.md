## Ingredients

- yellow peaches
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Peel yellow peaches, cut them from the stone in one piece
2. Allow two pounds of sugar to six pounds of fruit
3. Make a syrup of three-quarters of a pound of sugar and a little water
4. Put in the peaches, a few at a time, and let them cook gently until quite clear
5. Take them up carefully on a dish and set them in the sun to dry
6. Strew powdered sugar over them on all sides, a little at a time
7. If any syrup is left, remove to fresh dishes
8. When they are quite dry, lay them lightly in a jar with a little sugar sifted between the layers
