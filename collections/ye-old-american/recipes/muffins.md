## Ingredients

- a quart of flour
- a little salt
- a large spoonful of yeast
- the white of a fresh egg
- cold water

## Directions

1. Sift a quart of flour
2. put to it a little salt, and a large spoonful of yeast
3. beat the white of a fresh egg to a strong froth
4. add it
5. make the flour up with cold water, as soft as you can to allow it to be handled
6. set it in a moderately warm place
7. next morning, beat it well with a spoon
8. put it on the griddle in a round form
9. bake it nicely, turning them frequently till done
