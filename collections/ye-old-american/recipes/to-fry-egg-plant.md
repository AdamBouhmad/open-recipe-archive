## Ingredients

- egg plants
- salt
- water
- pepper
- yolk of egg
- bread-crumbs
- lard
- butter

## Directions

1. Do not pare your egg plants if they are to be fried, but slice them about half an inch thick and lay them an hour or two in salt and water to remove their strong taste.
2. Then take them out, wipe them, and season them with pepper only.
3. Beat some yolk of egg; and in another dish grate a sufficiency of bread-crumbs.
4. Have ready in a frying-pan some lard and butter mixed, and make it boil.
5. Then dip each slice of egg plant first in the egg, and then in the crumbs, till both sides are well covered.
6. Fry them brown, taking care to have them done all through, as the least rawness renders them very unpalatable.
