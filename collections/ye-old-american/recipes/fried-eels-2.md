## Ingredients

- eels
- wheat flour or rolled cracker
- hot lard or beef dripping
- salt
- parsley

## Directions

1. After cleaning the eels well, cut them in pieces two inches long
2. Wash them and wipe them dry
3. Roll them in wheat flour or rolled cracker, and fry in hot lard or beef dripping, salted
4. They should be browned all over and thoroughly done
5. Serve with crisped parsley
