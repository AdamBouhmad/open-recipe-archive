## Ingredients

- salt pork
- onion
- fresh cod or haddock
- pepper
- biscuit or crackers
- milk or water
- potatoes
- cold water
- butter
- flour
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of salt pork, and having half boiled it, cut it into slips
2. Cover the bottom of a pot with some of them
3. Then strew on some sliced onion
4. Have ready a large fresh cod, or an equal quantity of haddock, tutaug, or any other firm fish
5. Cut the fish into large pieces, and lay part of it on the pork and onions
6. Season it with pepper
7. Cover it with a layer of biscuit, or crackers that have been previously soaked in milk or water
8. You may add also a layer of sliced potatoes
9. Next proceed with a second layer of pork, onions, fish, &c. and continue as before till the pot is nearly full; finishing with soaked crackers
10. Pour in about a pint and a half of cold water
11. Cover it close, set it on hot coals, and let it simmer about an hour
12. Then skim it, and turn it out into a deep dish
13. Leave the gravy in the pot till you have thickened it with a bit of butter rolled in flour, and some chopped parsley
14. Then give it one boil up, and pour it hot into the dish
