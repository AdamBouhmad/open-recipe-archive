## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Beat egg slightly, add salt, and flour enough to make very stiff dough.
2. Knead, toss on slightly floured board, and roll thinly as possible, which may be as thin as paper.
3. Cover with towel, and set aside for twenty minutes.
4. Cut in fancy shapes, using sharp knife or French vegetable cutter; or the thin sheet may be rolled like jelly roll, cut in slices as thinly as possible, and pieces unrolled.
5. Dry, and when needed cook twenty minutes in boiling salted water.
6. Drain, and add to soup.
