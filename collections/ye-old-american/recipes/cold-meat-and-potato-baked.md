## Ingredients

- cold butter
- chopped onion
- flour
- cold milk or water
- salt
- pepper
- cold meat
- mashed potatoes
- cold hominy or rice

## Directions

1. Put in a frying pan a round tablespoonful of cold butter; when it becomes hot, stir into it a teaspoonful of chopped onion and a tablespoonful of flour, stirring it constantly until it is smooth and frothy.
2. Add two-thirds of a cupful of cold milk or water.
3. Season this with salt and pepper and allow it to come to a boil.
4. Add a cupful of cold meat finely chopped and cleared from bone and skin; let this all heat thoroughly.
5. Turn it into a shallow dish well buttered.
6. Spread hot or cold mashed potatoes over the top, and cook for fifteen or twenty minutes in a moderate hot oven.
7. Cold hominy, or rice may be used in place of mashed potatoes.
