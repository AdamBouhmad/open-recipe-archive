## Ingredients

- Berries
- Water
- Sugar
- Pastry

## Directions

1. Wash and pick over the berries.
2. Put into a porcelain saucepan with a very little water, and simmer until they burst open and become soft.
3. Run through a cullender to remove the skins, and sweeten to taste.
4. Bake in pastry shells, with a cross-bar of pastry over the top.
