## Ingredients

- half a pound of plain, white soap
- alcohol
- a tablespoonful of pulverized borax
- oil of bergamot

## Directions

1. dissolve in a small quantity of alcohol, as little as can be used
2. add a tablespoonful of pulverized borax
3. Shave the soap and put it in a small tin basin or cup
4. place it on the fire in a dish of boiling water
5. when melted, add the alcohol
6. remove from the fire
7. stir in oil of bergamot sufficient to perfume it
