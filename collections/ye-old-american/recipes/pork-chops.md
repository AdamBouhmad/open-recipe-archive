## Ingredients

- pork chops
- beaten egg
- cracker-crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- minced onion
- sage
- hot lard or drippings
- apple-sauce
- tomato catsup

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and trim them.
2. Dip first in beaten egg, then in cracker-crumbs seasoned with salt, pepper, minced onion, and a little sage.
3. Fry in hot lard or drippings twenty or thirty minutes, turning often.
4. Send apple-sauce to the table with them, and season with tomato catsup.
