## Ingredients

- 2 young rabbits
- Salt and water
- Pint of cold water
- Bunch of sweet herbs
- 1 onion
- Pinch of mace
- Pinch of nutmeg
- Pepper
- Half a pound of fat salt pork
- Cup of cream or milk
- 2 eggs
- Tablespoonful of butter
- Flour
- Cold milk
- Juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Clean two young rabbits, cut into joints, and soak in salt and water an hour.
2. Put into a saucepan with a pint of cold water, a bunch of sweet herbs, an onion finely minced, a pinch of mace, one of nutmeg, pepper, and half a pound of fat salt pork, cut into slips.
3. Cover, and stew until tender.
4. Take out the rabbits and set in a dish where they will keep warm.
5. Add to the gravy a cup of cream (or milk), two well-beaten eggs stirred in a little at a time, and a tablespoonful of butter.
6. Thicken with flour wet in cold milk and boil up once.
7. Take the saucepan from the fire, squeeze in the juice of a lemon, stirring all the while, and pour over the rabbits.
8. Do not cook the head or neck.
