## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- half the whites
- half a nutmeg
- one pint cream
- a little salt
- 4 spoons fine flour
- oil or butter pans, cups, or bowls

## Directions

1. Whip 6 eggs
2. half the whites
3. take half a nutmeg
4. one pint cream and a little salt
5. 4 spoons fine flour
6. oil or butter pans, cups, or bowls
7. bake in a quick oven one hour
8. Eat with sweet sauce
