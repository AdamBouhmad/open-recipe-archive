## Ingredients

- large juicy pippins
- lemon juice
- grated lemon-peel
- minced apple
- hot lard
- nutmeg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and parboil (in a very little water) some large juicy pippins
2. When half done, take them out, drain them, and mince them very fine
3. Make a batter according to the preceding receipt; adding some lemon juice and grated lemon-peel
4. Stir into the batter a sufficient quantity of the minced apple to make it very thick
5. Fry the fritters in hot lard as before directed
6. Eat them with nutmeg and sugar
