## Ingredients

- one gallon of water
- four pounds of ordinary washing soda
- a quarter of a pound of soda
- two pounds of unslaked lime

## Directions

1. Heat the water to boiling hot, put in the soda, boil about five minutes
2. pour it over two pounds of unslaked lime, let it bubble and foam until it settles
3. turn it off and bottle it for use
4. a tablespoonful put into a suds of three gallons, and a little, say a quarter of a cupful, in the boiler when boiling the clothes, makes them very white and clear
5. Must be well rinsed afterwards
6. the boiling, sudsing, rinsing and bluing, should be done in quick succession, until the clothes are ready to hang on the line
