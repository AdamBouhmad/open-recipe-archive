## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- Butter, the size of an egg
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar, sifted in the flour
- ½ teaspoonful soda, dissolved in a tablespoonful milk

## Directions

1. Bake in jelly-cake tins, and spread, when cold, with fruit-jelly.
2. If prepared flour be used leave out soda and cream-tartar.
