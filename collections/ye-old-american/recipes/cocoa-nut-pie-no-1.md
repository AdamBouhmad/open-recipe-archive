## Ingredients

- ½ lb. grated cocoa-nut
- ¾ lb. white sugar (powdered.)
- 6 oz. butter
- 5 eggs—the whites only
- 1 glass white wine
- 2 tablespoonfuls rose-water
- 1 tablespoonful nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar, and when well mixed, beat very light, with the wine and rose-water.
2. Add the cocoanut with as little and as light beating as possible; finally, whip in the stiffened whites of the eggs with a few skillful strokes, and bake at once in open shells.
3. Eat cold, with powdered sugar sifted over them.
