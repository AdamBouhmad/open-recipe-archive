## Ingredients

- several dozen of the largest and finest clam shells
- clams
- powdered mace and nutmeg
- butter
- grated bread-crumbs

## Directions

1. Save several dozen of the largest and finest shells, which must afterwards be washed clean, and wiped dry
2. Chop the clams fine, and mix with them some powdered mace and nutmeg
3. Butter the sides and bottom of a large, deep dish, and cover the bottom with a layer of grated bread-crumbs
4. Over this scatter some very small bits of the best fresh butter
5. Put in a thick layer of the chopped clams
6. Add another layer of grated bread-crumbs, and little bits of butter
7. Add a layer of chopped clams, and proceed in this manner till the dish is full, finishing at the top with a layer of crumbs
8. Set the dish in the oven, and bake it about a quarter of an hour
9. Have ready the clam-shells and fill them with the baked mixture, either leaving them open, or covering each with another clam-shell
10. Place them on large dishes, and send them to table hot
