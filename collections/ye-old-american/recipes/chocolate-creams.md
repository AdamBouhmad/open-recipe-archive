## Ingredients

- French Cream
- Baker's chocolate

## Directions

1. Use French Cream, and form it into small cone-shaped balls with the fingers
2. Lay them upon paper to harden until all are formed
3. Melt one cake of Baker's chocolate in an earthen dish or small basin; by setting it in the oven it will soon melt; do not let it cook, but it must be kept hot
4. Take the balls of cream, one at a time, on the tines of a fork, pour the melted chocolate over them with a teaspoon and when well covered, slip them from the fork upon oiled paper
