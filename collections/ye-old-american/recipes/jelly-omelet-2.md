## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- sugar
- jam, jelly, or marmalade

## Directions

1. Mix and cook Plain Omelet, omitting pepper and one-half the salt, and adding one tablespoon sugar.
2. Spread before folding with jam, jelly, or marmalade.
3. Fold, turn, and sprinkle with sugar.
