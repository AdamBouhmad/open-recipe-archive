## Ingredients

- Four cups of sifted flour
- one teacupful of butter
- two cups of sugar
- the juice of one lemon
- the grated peel from the outside
- three eggs whipped very light
- a half teaspoonful of soda
- a tablespoonful of milk

## Directions

1. Beat thoroughly each ingredient, adding, after all is in, a half teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a tablespoonful of milk
2. Roll out as any cookies and bake a light brown
3. Use no other wetting
