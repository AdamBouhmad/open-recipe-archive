## Ingredients

- 4 cups milk
- ⅓ cup rice
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup sugar
- Grated rind ½ lemon

## Directions

1. Wash rice, mix ingredients, and pour into buttered pudding-dish; bake three hours in very slow oven, stirring three times during first hour of baking to prevent rice from settling.
