## Ingredients

- four chickens
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- water
- two table spoonsful of flour
- four ounces of butter
- half a pint of new milk
- gill of white wine

## Directions

1. Take off the legs and wings of four chickens
2. Separate the breasts from the backs
3. Cut off the necks and divide the backs across
4. Clean the gizzards nicely
5. Put them with the livers and other parts of the chicken after being washed clean into a sauce pan
6. Add pepper, salt, and a little mace
7. Cover them with water and stew them till tender
8. Take them out
9. Thicken half a pint of the water with two table spoonsful of flour rubbed into four ounces of butter
10. Add half a pint of new milk
11. Boil all together a few minutes
12. Add a gill of white wine, stirring it in carefully that it may not curdle
13. Put the chickens in and continue to shake the pan until they are sufficiently hot
14. Serve them up
