## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 2 cups bread-crumbs
- 4 eggs
- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 large lemon—juice and half the rind, grated

## Directions

1. Soak the bread in the milk; add the beaten yolks, with the butter and sugar rubbed to a cream, also the lemon.
2. Bake in a buttered dish until firm and slightly brown.
3. Draw to the door of the oven and cover with a méringue of the whites whipped to a froth with three tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar, and a little lemon-juice.
4. Brown very slightly; sift powdered sugar over it, and eat cold.
