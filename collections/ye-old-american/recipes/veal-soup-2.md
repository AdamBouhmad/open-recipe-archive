## Ingredients

- A knuckle of veal
- Three quarts of cold water
- A small quantity of salt
- One small tablespoonful of uncooked rice
- The yolk of one egg
- A teacupful of cream, or in hot weather, new milk
- A piece of butter the size of a hickory nut

## Directions

1. Put a knuckle of veal into three quarts of cold water, with a small quantity of salt, and one small tablespoonful of uncooked rice
2. Boil slowly, hardly above simmering, four hours, when the liquor should be reduced to half the usual quantity
3. Remove from the fire
4. Into the tureen put the yolk of one egg, and stir well into it a teacupful of cream, or, in hot weather, new milk
5. Add a piece of butter the size of a hickory nut
6. Strain the soup, boiling hot, into the tureen, stirring all the time
7. Just at the last, beat it well for a minute
