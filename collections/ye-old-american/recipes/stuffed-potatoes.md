## Ingredients

- large potatoes
- butter
- cream
- salt
- pepper
- grated cheese
- milk
- egg

## Directions

1. Bake large, fair potatoes until soft
2. Cut a round piece off the top of each
3. Scrape out the inside carefully, so as not to break the skin, and set aside the empty cases with the covers
4. Mash the inside very smoothly, working into it while hot some butter and cream—about half a teaspoonful of each for every potato
5. Season with salt and pepper, with a good pinch of grated cheese for each
6. Work it very soft with milk, and put into a saucepan to heat, stirring, to prevent burning
7. When scalding hot, stir in one well-beaten egg for six large potatoes
8. Boil up once, fill the skins with the mixture, replacing the caps
9. Return them to the oven for three minutes
10. Arrange upon a napkin in a deep dish, the caps uppermost
11. Cover with a fold of the napkin, and eat hot
