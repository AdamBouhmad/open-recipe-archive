## Ingredients

- ripe peaches
- sugar
- cream or rich milk

## Directions

1. Get fine soft peaches perfectly ripe, peel them, take out the stones, and put them in a China bowl
2. Sprinkle some sugar on, and chop them very small with a silver spoon
3. If the peaches be sufficiently ripe, they will become a smooth pulp
4. Add as much cream or rich milk as you have peaches
5. Put more sugar, and freeze it
