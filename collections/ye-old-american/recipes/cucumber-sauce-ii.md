## Ingredients

- One-half cup heavy cream
- One-fourth teaspoon salt
- Few grains pepper
- Two tablespoons vinegar
- One cucumber

## Directions

1. Beat one-half cup heavy cream until stiff, and add one-fourth teaspoon salt, few grains pepper, and gradually two tablespoons vinegar; then add one cucumber, pared, chopped, and drained.
