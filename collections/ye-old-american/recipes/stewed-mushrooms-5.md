## Ingredients

- one-half pound mushrooms
- three tablespoons butter
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- one-half cup hot water or stock

## Directions

1. Wash one-half pound mushrooms.
2. Remove stems, scrape, and cut in pieces.
3. Peel caps, and break in pieces.
4. Melt three tablespoons of butter, add mushrooms, cook two minutes.
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dredge with flour, and add one-half cup hot water or stock.
6. Cook slowly five minutes.
