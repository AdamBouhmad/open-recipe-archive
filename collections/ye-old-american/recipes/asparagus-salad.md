## Ingredients

- stalks of canned asparagus
- bright red pepper
- lettuce leaves
- French Dressing
- one-half tablespoon tomato catsup

## Directions

1. Drain and rinse stalks of canned asparagus.
2. Cut rings from a bright red pepper one-third inch wide.
3. Place three or four stalks in each ring.
4. Arrange on lettuce leaves and serve with French Dressing, to which has been added one-half tablespoon tomato catsup.
