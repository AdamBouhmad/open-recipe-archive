## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one quart cream
- sweetened to your taste
- half a nutmeg
- a little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Four eggs beat and put to one quart cream, sweetened to your taste, half a nutmeg, and a little cinnamon--baked.
