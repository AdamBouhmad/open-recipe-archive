## Ingredients

- 1 box Coxe's gelatine, soaked in 1 pint of cold water one hour
- 1 quart of boiling water poured over this
- 1½ lb. white sugar
- 2 lemons—juice and peel
- 1 pint of wine

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine in the cold water one hour
2. Pour 1 quart of boiling water over this, and stir until the gelatine is dissolved
3. Put all over the fire, boil up once well, and strain through a double flannel bag into moulds
