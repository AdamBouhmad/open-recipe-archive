## Ingredients

- 1½ squares Baker's chocolate
- ¼ cup sugar
- Few grains salt
- 1 cup boiling water
- 3 cups milk

## Directions

1. Scald milk.
2. Melt chocolate in small saucepan placed over hot water, add sugar, salt, and gradually boiling water.
3. When smooth, place on range and boil one minute.
4. Add to scalded milk, mill, and serve in chocolate cups with whipped cream.
5. One and one-half ounces vanilla chocolate may be substitute for Baker's chocolate; being sweetened, less sugar is required.
