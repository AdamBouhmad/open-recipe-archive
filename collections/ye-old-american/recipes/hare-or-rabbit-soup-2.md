## Ingredients

- rabbit or hare
- three small onions
- bunch of sweet herbs

## Directions

1. Dissect the rabbit and crack the bones
2. Prepare precisely as you would the venison soup, only putting in three small onions instead of one, and a bunch of sweet herbs
3. Hares which are too tough to be cooked in any other way make excellent game soup
4. Also, the large gray squirrel of the Middle and Southern States may be used
