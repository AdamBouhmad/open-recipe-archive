## Ingredients

- Three coffeecupfuls of good thick sweet cream
- half a cup of powdered sugar
- three teaspoonfuls of vanilla
- three-fourths of an ounce of best gelatine
- one teacup of hot water

## Directions

1. Whip the cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla to a stiff froth
2. Dissolve three-fourths of an ounce of best gelatine in a teacup of hot water
3. When cool pour it in the cream and stir it gently from the bottom upward, cutting the cream into it, until it thickens
4. Set the dish containing the cream in another dish containing ice-water, or cracked ice
5. When finished pour in molds and set on ice or in any very cold place
