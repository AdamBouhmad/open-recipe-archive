## Ingredients

- 1 cup dried beans
- salt
- water
- 3/4 cup cream
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak one cup dried beans over night
2. Drain, and cook in boiling salted water until soft
3. Drain, add three-fourths cup cream, and season with butter and salt
4. Reheat before serving
