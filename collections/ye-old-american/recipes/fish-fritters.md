## Ingredients

- piece of salt codfish picked up very fine
- cold water
- 1 pint milk
- 4 eggs
- pinch of salt
- 1 large teaspoonful baking powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Pick up the salt codfish very fine and put it into a saucepan with plenty of cold water
2. Bring it to a boil and turn off the water
3. Add another of cold water
4. Let this boil with the fish about fifteen minutes, very slowly
5. Strain off this water, making the fish quite dry, and set aside to cool
6. Stir up a batter of a pint of milk, four eggs, a pinch of salt, one large teaspoonful of baking powder in flour, enough to make thicker than batter cakes
7. Stir in the fish and fry like any fritters
