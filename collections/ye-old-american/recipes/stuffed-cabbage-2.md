## Ingredients

- cabbage
- cold boiled pork or ham, or cooked sausage-meat
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- thyme
- bread-crumbs
- muslin strip
- pot-liquor from boiled beef or ham
- butter
- flour
- rich milk or cream

## Directions

1. Chop the bits taken out very small and mix with some cold boiled pork or ham, or cooked sausage-meat, a very little onion, pepper, salt, a pinch of thyme, and some bread-crumbs
2. Fill the cavity of the cabbage with this mixture
3. Bind a wide strip of muslin over the hole in the top
4. Lay the cabbage in a large saucepan with a pint of pot-liquor from boiled beef or ham
5. Stew gently until very tender
6. Take out the cabbage and unbind carefully, and lay in a dish
7. Keep hot while you add to the strained gravy pepper, a piece of butter rolled in flour, and two or three tablespoonfuls rich milk or cream
8. Boil up and pour over the cabbage
