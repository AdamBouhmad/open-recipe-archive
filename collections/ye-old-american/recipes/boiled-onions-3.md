## Ingredients

- onions
- cold water
- boiling salted water
- milk
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put onions in cold water and remove skins while under water.
2. Drain, put in a saucepan, and cover with boiling salted water; boil five minutes, drain, and again cover with boiling salted water.
3. Cook one hour or until soft, but not broken.
4. Drain, add a small quantity of milk, cook five minutes, and season with butter, salt, and pepper.
