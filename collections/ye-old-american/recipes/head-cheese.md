## Ingredients

- forehead of a fresh pig
- ears of a fresh pig
- feet of a fresh pig
- nice scraps trimmed from the hams of a fresh pig
- pepper
- salt
- sage
- summer savory
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil the forehead, ears and feet, and nice scraps trimmed from the hams of a fresh pig, until the meat will almost drop from the bones.
2. Separate the meat from the bones, put in a large chopping-bowl, and season with pepper, salt, sage and summer savory.
3. Chop it rather coarsely; put it back in the same kettle it was boiled in, with just enough of the liquor in which it was boiled to prevent its burning.
4. Warm it through thoroughly, mixing it well together.
5. Pour it into a strong muslin bag, press the bag between two flat surfaces, with a heavy weight on top.
6. When cold and solid it can be cut in slices.
7. Serve good cold, or warmed up in vinegar.
