## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup flour
- 1 cup milk or Chicken Stock
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oyster liquor

## Directions

1. Wash oysters, reserve liquor, heat, strain, add oysters, and cook until plump.
2. Remove oysters, and make a sauce of butter, flour, oyster liquor, and milk.
3. Add oysters, and season with salt and pepper.
