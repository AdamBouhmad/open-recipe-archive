## Ingredients

- two even tablespoonfuls of cornstarch
- one quart of milk
- three eggs
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- a small piece of butter
- four tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- lemon, vanilla, or raspberry for flavoring

## Directions

1. Heat the milk to nearly boiling and add the starch, previously dissolved in a little cold milk
2. Add the eggs well beaten with four tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
3. Let it boil up once or twice, stirring it briskly, and it is done
4. Flavor with lemon, or vanilla, or raspberry, or to suit your taste
5. Serve very cold as a good substitute for ice cream
