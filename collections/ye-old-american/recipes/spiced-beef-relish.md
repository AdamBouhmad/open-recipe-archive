## Ingredients

- two pounds of raw, tender beefsteak
- salt
- pepper
- a little sage
- two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- two rolled crackers made very fine
- two well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the beefsteak very fine
2. Put into it salt, pepper and a little sage
3. Add two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
4. Add two rolled crackers made very fine and two well-beaten eggs
5. Make it up into the shape of a roll
6. Bake it
7. Baste with butter and water before baking
8. Cut in slices when cold
