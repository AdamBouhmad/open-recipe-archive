## Ingredients

- some good tart apples
- a strip of orange peel
- a strip of quince peel
- sufficient water
- sugar in the proportion of half a pound to one pound of fruit
- one pint of whipped cream
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Peel, core and quarter some good tart apples of nice flavor
2. Stew them with a strip of orange and a strip of quince peel, sufficient water to cover the bottom of the stewpan, and sugar in the proportion of half a pound to one pound of fruit
3. When cooked, press the pulp through a sieve
4. When cold, dish and cover with one pint of whipped cream flavored with lemon peel
