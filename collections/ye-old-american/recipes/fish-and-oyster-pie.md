## Ingredients

- cold fish such as cod or haddock
- 2 dozen oysters
- pepper and salt
- bread crumbs
- 1/2 teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful of finely chopped parsley
- made melted butter or thin white sauce
- oyster-liquor

## Directions

1. Clear the fish from the bones, and put a layer of it in a pie-dish, which sprinkle with pepper and salt
2. Add a layer of bread crumbs, oysters, nutmeg and chopped parsley
3. Repeat this till the dish is quite full
4. Form a covering either of bread crumbs, which should be browned, or puff-paste, which should be cut off into long strips, and laid in cross-bars over the fish, with a line of the paste first laid round the edge
5. Before putting on the top, pour in some made melted butter, or a little thin white sauce, and the oyster-liquor
6. Bake for 1/4 hour if of cooked fish, or 3/4 hour if made of fresh fish and puff-paste
