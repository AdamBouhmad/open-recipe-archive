## Ingredients

- four Bartlett pears
- butter
- two ounces sweet chocolate
- one tablespoon sugar
- one and one-fourth cups milk
- one teaspoon arrowroot
- one-fourth cup cream
- few grains salt
- one and one-half tablespoons butter
- one-fourth cup powdered sugar
- one-half teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Pare four Bartlett pears, cut in fourths lengthwise, and sauté in butter until browned.
2. Arrange in serving dish.
3. Cook two ounces sweet chocolate, one tablespoon sugar, and one and one-fourth cups milk in double boiler five minutes.
4. Add one teaspoon arrowroot mixed with one-fourth cup cream and a few grains salt, and cook ten minutes.
5. Melt one and one-half tablespoons butter, add one-fourth cup powdered sugar, and cook until well caramelized, stirring constantly.
6. Add to first mixture, and flavor with one-half teaspoon vanilla.
7. Chill thoroughly.
