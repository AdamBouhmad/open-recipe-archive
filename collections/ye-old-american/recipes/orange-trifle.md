## Ingredients

- the thin parings from the outside of a dozen oranges
- good cognac
- sugar
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take the thin parings from the outside of a dozen oranges and put to steep in a wide-mouthed bottle
2. Cover it with good cognac and let it stand twenty-four hours
3. Skin and seed the oranges and reduce to a pulp
4. Press this through a sieve, sugar to taste
5. Arrange in a dish and heap with whipped cream flavored with the orange brandy
6. Ice two hours before serving
