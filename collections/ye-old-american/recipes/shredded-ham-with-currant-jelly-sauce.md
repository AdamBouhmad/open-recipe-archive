## Ingredients

- ½ tablespoon butter
- ⅓ cup currant jelly
- Few grains cayenne
- ¼ cup Sherry wine
- 1 cup cold cooked ham, cut in small strips

## Directions

1. Put butter and currant jelly into the chafing-dish.
2. As soon as melted, add cayenne, wine, and ham.
3. Simmer five minutes.
