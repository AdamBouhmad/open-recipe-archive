## Ingredients

- one pound of extra refined sugar
- one ounce of fine white starch
- whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Add one ounce of fine white starch to one pound of extra refined sugar
2. Pound finely together and sift through gauze
3. Beat the whites of three eggs to a froth
4. Add the powdered sugar by degrees or it will spoil the froth of the eggs
5. When all the sugar is stirred in continue the whipping for half an hour longer, adding more sugar if the ice is too thin
6. Take a little of the icing and lay it aside for ornamenting afterward
7. When the cake comes out of the oven, spread the sugar icing smoothly over it with a knife and dry it at once in a cool oven
8. For ornamenting, tinge the icing with cochineal for pink, saffron for yellow, spinach juice for green
9. Mix coloring first with a little colorless spirit then stir into the white icing until the tint is deep enough
10. To ornament, make a cone of stiff writing paper and squeeze the colored icing through it to form leaves, beading or letters as desired
