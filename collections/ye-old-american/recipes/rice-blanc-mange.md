## Ingredients

- tea-cup full rice
- half pint milk
- sugar
- wine
- nutmeg
- boiled custard

## Directions

1. Boil a tea-cup full of rice in a very small of water, till it is near bursting
2. Add half a pint of milk, boil it to a mush, stirring all the time
3. Season it with sugar, wine, and nutmeg
4. Dip the mould in water, and fill it
5. When cold, turn it in a dish, and surround it with boiled custard seasoned, or syllabub
6. Garnish it with marmalade
