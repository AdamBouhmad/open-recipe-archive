## Ingredients

- 2 lb. lobster
- White Sauce
- curry powder
- flour
- salt
- cayenne
- lemon juice
- buttered crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare as Scalloped Lobster, adding to flour one-half teaspoon curry powder when making White Sauce.
