## Ingredients

- boiling water
- eggs
- butter
- toast
- parsley
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Strain some boiling water into a frying-pan, which must also be perfectly clean.
2. When the water boils, break the eggs separately into a saucer.
3. Take the frying-pan off, and slip the eggs, one by one, carefully upon the surface.
4. When all are in, put back over the fire and boil gently three minutes.
5. Take out with a perforated skimmer, drain, and lay upon slices of buttered toast in a hot dish.
6. Garnish with parsley, and dust with pepper and salt.
