## Ingredients

- raw oysters
- ice
- parsley
- lemon
- ferns

## Directions

1. Raw oysters are served on oyster plates, or in a block of ice.
2. Place block of ice on a folded napkin on platter, and garnish the base with parsley and quarters of lemon, or ferns and lemon.
