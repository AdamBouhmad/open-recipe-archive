## Ingredients

- six eggs
- one pint sifted flour
- one ounce of butter
- one ounce of sugar
- one cup of milk
- jelly
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat together until smooth six eggs and a pint sifted flour
2. Melt one ounce of butter and add to the batter, with one ounce of sugar and a cup of milk
3. Beat until smooth
4. Put a tablespoonful at a time into a frying pan slightly greased, spreading the batter evenly over the surface by tipping the pan about
5. Fry to a light brown
6. Spread with jelly, roll up, dust with powdered sugar and serve hot
