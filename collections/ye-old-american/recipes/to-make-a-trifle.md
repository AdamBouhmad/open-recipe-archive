## Ingredients

- Savoy cake or Naples biscuit
- white wine
- rich boiled custard
- half a pint cream
- sugar
- preserves

## Directions

1. Put slices of Savoy cake or Naples biscuit at the bottom of a deep dish
2. Wet it with white wine, and fill the dish nearly to the top with rich boiled custard
3. Season half a pint of cream with white wine and sugar
4. Whip it to a froth
5. As it rises, take it lightly off, and lay it on the custard
6. Pile it up high and tastily
7. Decorate it with preserves of any kind, cut so thin as not to bear the froth down by its weight
