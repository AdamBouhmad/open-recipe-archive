## Ingredients

- six large anchovies
- their own pickle
- six small onions
- a little black pepper
- a little cayenne
- half a glass of soy
- four glasses of mushroom catchup
- two glasses of claret
- two glasses of black walnut pickle

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar six large anchovies, moistening them with their own pickle.
2. Then chop and pound six small onions.
3. Mix them with a little black pepper and a little cayenne, half a glass of soy, four glasses of mushroom catchup, two glasses of claret, and two of black walnut pickle.
4. Put the mixture into a small sauce-pan or earthen pipkin, and let it simmer slowly till all the bones of the anchovies are dissolved.
5. Strain it, and when cold, bottle it for use; dipping the cork in melted rosin, and tying leather over it.
6. Fill the bottles quite full.
