## Ingredients

- Whites 4 eggs
- ⅞ cup powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put whites of eggs and sugar in bowl.
2. Beat mixture until stiff enough to hold its shape.
3. Add lemon juice drop by drop, continuing the beating.
