## Ingredients

- some fine-flavored sour apples
- sugar
- the white of an egg

## Directions

1. Stew some fine-flavored sour apples tender, sweeten to taste
2. Strain them through a fine wire sieve
3. Break into one pint of strained apples the white of an egg
4. Whisk the apple and egg very briskly till quite stiff
5. Serve eaten with a nice boiled custard
