## Ingredients

- yeast
- corn meal

## Directions

1. Make a pan of yeast the same as Home-Made Yeast
2. mix in with it corn meal that has been sifted and dried
3. knead it well until it is thick enough to roll out
4. cut into cakes or crumble up
5. Spread out and dry thoroughly in the shade
6. keep in a dry place
