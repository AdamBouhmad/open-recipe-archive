## Ingredients

- Feet, neck, pinions, and giblets of three chickens, or of two ducks or two geese
- 1½ lb. veal
- ½ lb. ham
- 3 qts. water
- bunch of sweet herbs
- pinch of allspice
- large tablespoonful of flour
- cold water
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- glass of brown sherry
- tablespoonful of tomato or walnut catsup
- Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Crack the bones into small pieces, and cut the meat into strips.
2. Put all together with the giblets over the fire, with a bunch of sweet herbs and a pinch of allspice.
3. Stew slowly for two hours.
4. Take out the giblets and set them aside in a pan where they will keep warm.
5. Take up a teacupful of the hot soup and stir into this a large tablespoonful of flour which has been wet with cold water and rubbed to a smooth paste; then, two tablespoonfuls of butter.
6. Return to the pot and boil for fifteen minutes.
7. Season at the last with a glass of brown sherry and a tablespoonful of tomato or walnut catsup.
8. Add a little Worcestershire sauce if desired.
9. Chop and add the giblets, and boil up once.
