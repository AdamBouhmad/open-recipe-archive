## Ingredients

- ½ tablespoon finely chopped shallot
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ¼ lb. sausage meat
- 12 canned mushrooms, finely chopped
- 1 cup chestnut purée
- ⅓ cup stale bread crumbs
- ½ tablespoon finely chopped parsley
- 24 French chestnuts cooked and left whole
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook shallot with butter five minutes, add sausage meat, and cook two minutes, then add mushrooms, chestnut purée, parsley, and salt and pepper.
2. Heat to boiling-point, add bread crumbs and whole chestnuts.
3. Cool mixture before stuffing goose.
