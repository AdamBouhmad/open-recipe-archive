## Ingredients

- One lively lobster
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Choose a lively lobster, not too large
2. Put a handful of salt into a pot of boiling water
3. Tie the claws together if not already skewered
4. Plunge the lobster into the prepared bath
5. Boil from half an hour to an hour, as size demands
6. Draw out and lay face downward in a sieve to dry
7. When cold, split open the body and tail, and crack the claws to extract the meat
8. Throw away the lady fingers and the head
