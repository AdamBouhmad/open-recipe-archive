## Ingredients

- a round of beef
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- thyme
- parsley
- cellery tops
- seed pounded
- carrots cut small
- water

## Directions

1. Lard it well
2. Put it in a Dutch oven
3. Cut the meat from a shin of beef, or any coarse piece in thin slices
4. Put round the sides and over the top some slices of bacon
5. Salt, pepper, onion, thyme, parsley, cellery tops, or seed pounded
6. And some carrots cut small
7. Strew the pieces of beef over
8. Cover it with water
9. Let it stew very gently till perfectly done
10. Take out the round
11. Strain the gravy
12. Let it stand to be cold
13. Take off the grease carefully
14. Beat the whites of four eggs
15. Mix a little water with them
16. Put them to the gravy
17. Let it boil till it looks clear
18. Strain it
19. And when cold, put it over the beef
