## Ingredients

- bass
- water
- salt
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. After thoroughly cleaning it place in a saucepan with enough water to cover it
2. Add two tablespoonfuls of salt
3. Set the saucepan over the fire, and when it has boiled about five minutes try to pull out one of the fins
4. If it loosens easily from the body carefully take the fish out of the water, lay it on a platter
5. Surround it with half a dozen hard-boiled eggs, and serve it with a sauce
