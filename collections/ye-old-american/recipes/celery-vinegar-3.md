## Ingredients

- A bunch of fresh celery, or a quarter of a pound of celery seed
- 1 quart best vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful white sugar

## Directions

1. Cut up the celery into small bits, or pour the seed into a jar; scald the salt and vinegar, and pour over the celery stalks or seed; let it cool, and put away in one large jar tightly corked
2. In a fortnight strain and bottle in small flasks, corking tightly
