## Ingredients

- the juice of six lemons
- the grated rind of three lemons
- a large sweet orange (juice and rind)
- a pint of water
- two cupfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Squeeze out all the juice and steep it in the rind of orange and lemons a couple of hours
2. Squeeze and strain through a towel
3. Add a pint of water and two cupfuls of sugar
4. Stir until dissolved
5. Turn into a freezer and proceed as for ice-cream, letting it stand longer, two or three hours
