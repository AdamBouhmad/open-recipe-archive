## Ingredients

- salt pork middling
- dried beans
- lukewarm water
- warm water

## Directions

1. Parboil a piece of the middling of salt pork, and score the skin.
2. Allow a pound to a quart of dried beans, which must be soaked over night in lukewarm water.
3. Change this twice for more and warmer water, and in the morning put them on to boil in cold.
4. When they are soft, drain off the liquor, put the beans in a deep dish, and half-bury the pork in the middle, adding a very little warm water.
5. Bake a nice brown.
