## Ingredients

- good-sized potatoes
- ice-water
- lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel good-sized potatoes, and slice them as evenly as possible.
2. Drop them into ice-water.
3. Have a kettle of very hot lard, as for cakes.
4. Put a few at a time into a towel and shake, to dry the moisture out of them, and then drop them into the boiling lard.
5. Stir them occasionally, and when of a light brown take them out with a skimmer, and they will be crisp and not greasy.
6. Sprinkle salt over them while hot.
