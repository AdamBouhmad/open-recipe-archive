## Ingredients

- butter
- small sponge cakes or slices of stale plain cake
- one-half pint of jelly
- one pint of hot water

## Directions

1. Butter pudding-mold
2. Dissolve one-half pint of jelly in a pint of hot water
3. Soak small sponge cakes or slices of stale plain cake in this liquid
4. Fill the mold with the soaked cake
5. Make a sufficient quantity of custard to fill the mold and leave as much more to be boiled in a dish by itself
6. Set the mold, after being tightly covered, into a kettle
7. Boil one hour
8. Turn out of the mold and serve with some of the other custard poured over it
