## Ingredients

- berries (huckleberries, grapes, blackberries, currants, raspberries, cherries, or strawberries)
- sugar (one tablespoonful per quart of fruit)

## Directions

1. Heat slowly to boiling in a large kettle.
2. When they begin to boil, if there is much juice in the kettle, dip out the surplus with a dipper or cup.
3. Leave the berries almost dry before putting in the sugar.
4. Add sugar in the proportion of one tablespoonful to each quart of fruit.
5. Boil all together fifteen minutes, and can.
