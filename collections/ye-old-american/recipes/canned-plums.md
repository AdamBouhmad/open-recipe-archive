## Ingredients

- plums
- water (one gill per three quarts of fruit)
- sugar (one quarter of a pound per three quarts of fruit)

## Directions

1. Prick with a needle to prevent bursting.
2. Prepare a syrup allowing a gill of pure water and a quarter of a pound of sugar to every three quarts of fruit.
3. When the sugar is dissolved and the water blood-warm, put in the plums.
4. Heat slowly to a boil.
5. Let them boil five minutes—not fast or they will break badly.
6. Fill up the jars with plums, pour in the scalding syrup until it runs down the sides, and seal.
