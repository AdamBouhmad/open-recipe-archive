## Ingredients

- Bluefish
- Butter, one-fourth cup
- Egg yolks, 2
- Onion, 2 tablespoons finely chopped
- Capers, 2 tablespoons finely chopped
- Pickles, 2 tablespoons finely chopped
- Parsley, 2 tablespoons finely chopped
- Lemon juice, 2 tablespoons
- Vinegar, 1 tablespoon
- Salt, one-half teaspoon
- Paprika, one-third teaspoon
- Potato balls
- Cucumber ribbons
- Lemon
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Split and bone the bluefish, place on a well-buttered sheet, and cook twenty minutes in a hot oven.
2. Cream one-fourth cup butter, add yolks of two eggs, and when well mixed add two tablespoons each onion, capers, pickles, and parsley finely chopped, two tablespoons lemon juice, one tablespoon vinegar, one-half teaspoon salt, and one-third teaspoon paprika.
3. Sprinkle fish with salt, spread with mixture, and continue the baking until fish is done.
4. Remove to serving dish and garnish with potato balls, cucumber ribbons, lemon cut in fancy shapes, and parsley.
