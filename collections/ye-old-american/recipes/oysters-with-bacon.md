## Ingredients

- oysters
- thin slice of bacon for each
- small wooden skewers

## Directions

1. Clean oysters, wrap a thin slice of bacon around each, and fasten with small wooden skewers
2. Put in a broiler, place broiler over dripping-pan, and bake in a hot oven until bacon is crisp and brown, turning broiler once during the cooking
3. Drain on brown paper
