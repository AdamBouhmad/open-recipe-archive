## Ingredients

- 2 cups powdered sugar
- ⅔ cupful butter
- 4 eggs
- ½ cupful milk
- ½ teaspoonful soda
- 1 teaspoonful cream-tartar
- 3 cups flour
- ½ pint of milk
- 2 small teaspoonfuls corn-starch
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla
- ½ cup sugar

## Directions

1. Bake in thin layers as for jelly-cake.
2. Heat the milk to boiling, and stir in the corn-starch, wet with a little cold milk.
3. Take out a little and mix gradually with the beaten egg and sugar.
4. Return to the rest of the custard, and boil, stirring constantly until quite thick.
5. Let it cool before you season, and spread on cake.
6. Season the icing also with vanilla.
7. Spread between the layers when cold.
