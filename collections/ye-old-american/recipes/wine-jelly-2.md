## Ingredients

- 3 cups of sugar
- 1 pint of wine—pale Sherry or White
- 1 cup of cold water
- 1 package Coxe's gelatine
- Juice of two lemons and grated peel of one
- 1 quart of boiling water
- 1 good pinch of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine in the cold water one hour
2. Add to this the sugar, lemons, and cinnamon
3. Pour over all a quart of boiling water, and stir until the gelatine is thoroughly dissolved
4. Put in the wine, strain through a double flannel bag, without squeezing
5. Wet your moulds with cold water, and set the jelly away in them to cool
