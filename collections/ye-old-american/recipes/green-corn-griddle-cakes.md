## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- two cups grated green corn
- a little salt
- two eggs
- one teaspoonful of baking powder
- flour sufficient

## Directions

1. Combine milk, grated green corn, salt, eggs, and baking powder
2. Add flour sufficient to make a batter to fry on the griddle
3. Butter them hot and serve
