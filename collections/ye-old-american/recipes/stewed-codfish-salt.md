## Ingredients

- thick white piece of salt codfish
- cold water
- 1 pint milk
- butter
- pepper
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 2 well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Lay the salt codfish in cold water for a few minutes to soften it a little
2. Shred it in very small bits
3. Put it over the fire in a stew pan with cold water
4. Let it come to a boil and turn off this water carefully
5. Add a pint of milk to the fish or more according to quantity
6. Set it over the fire again and let it boil slowly about three minutes
7. Add a good-sized piece of butter and a shake of pepper
8. Add a thickening of a tablespoonful of flour in enough cold milk to make a cream
9. Stew five minutes longer
10. Just before serving stir in two well-beaten eggs
