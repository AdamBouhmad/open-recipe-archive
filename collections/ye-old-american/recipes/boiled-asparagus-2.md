## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- water
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut off lower parts of stalks as far down as they will snap
2. Untie bunches, wash, remove scales, and retie
3. Cook in boiling salted water fifteen minutes or until soft, leaving tips out of water first ten minutes
4. Drain, remove string, and spread with soft butter, allowing one and one-half tablespoons butter to each bunch asparagus
