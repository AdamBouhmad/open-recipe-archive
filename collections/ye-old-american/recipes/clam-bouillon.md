## Ingredients

- ½ peck clams
- 3 cups cold water

## Directions

1. Wash and scrub with a brush clams, changing the water several times
2. Put in kettle with three cups cold water, cover tightly, and steam until shells are well opened
3. Strain liquor, cool, and clear
