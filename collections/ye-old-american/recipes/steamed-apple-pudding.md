## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons butter
- ¾ cup milk
- 4 apples cut in eighths
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients
2. Work in butter with tips of fingers
3. Add milk gradually, mixing with a knife
4. Toss on floured board, pat and roll out
5. Place apples on middle of dough
6. Sprinkle with one tablespoon sugar mixed with one-fourth teaspoon each of salt and nutmeg
7. Bring dough around apples and carefully lift into buttered mould or five-pound lard pail
8. Cover closely and steam one hour and twenty minutes
9. Serve with Vanilla or Cold Sauce
