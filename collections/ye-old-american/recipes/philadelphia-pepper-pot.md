## Ingredients

- tripe
- calves' feet
- water
- red pepper
- sweet marjoram
- sweet basil
- thyme
- onions
- potatoes
- salt
- butter
- flour
- eggs

## Directions

1. Put two pounds of tripe and four calves' feet into the soup-pot and cover them with cold water
2. Add a red pepper, and boil closely until the calves' feet are boiled very tender
3. Take out the meat, skim the liquid, stir it, cut the tripe into small pieces, and put it back into the liquid
4. If there is not enough liquid, add boiling water
5. Add half a teaspoonful of sweet marjoram, sweet basil, and thyme, two sliced onions, sliced potatoes, salt
6. When the vegetables have boiled until almost tender, add a piece of butter rolled in flour
7. Drop in some egg balls, and boil fifteen minutes more
8. Take up and serve hot
