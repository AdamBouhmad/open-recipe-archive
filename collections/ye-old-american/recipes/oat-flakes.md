## Ingredients

- a quart of boiling water
- a saltspoonful of salt
- three ounces of flakes
- cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put into a double saucepan or porcelain-lined pan a quart of boiling water
2. Add a saltspoonful of salt
3. When it is boiling add, or rather stir in gradually, three ounces of flakes
4. Keep stirring to prevent burning
5. Let it boil from fifteen to twenty minutes
6. Serve with cream and sugar
