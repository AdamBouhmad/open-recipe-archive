## Ingredients

- brisket of beef with hard fat
- a small quantity of water
- carrots
- turnips
- onions
- celery
- pepper-corns
- half a pint of the soup
- a spoonful of catsup
- a teaspoonful of made mustard
- a little flour
- a bit of butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Put the part that has the hard fat into a stewpot with a small quantity of water
2. Let it boil up and skim it thoroughly
3. Add carrots, turnips, onions, celery and a few pepper-corns
4. Stew till extremely tender
5. Take out all the flat bones and remove all the fat from the soup
6. Either serve that and the meat in tureen, or the soup alone, and the meat on a dish garnished with some vegetables
7. For the sauce: Take half a pint of the soup and mix it with a spoonful of catsup, a teaspoonful of made mustard, a little flour, a bit of butter and salt
8. Boil all together a few minutes
9. Pour it round the meat
