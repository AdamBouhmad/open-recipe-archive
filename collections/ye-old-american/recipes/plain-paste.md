## Ingredients

- 1½ cups flour
- ¼ cup lard
- ¼ cup butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Wash butter, pat, and form in circular piece
2. Add salt to flour
3. Work in lard with tips of fingers or case knife
4. Moisten to dough with cold water
5. Toss on board dredged sparingly with flour
6. Pat and roll out
7. Fold in butter as for puff paste
8. Pat and roll out
9. Fold so as to make three layers
10. Turn half-way round
11. Pat and roll out
12. Repeat
13. Use at once or fold in cheese-cloth, put in covered tin, and keep in cold place, but never in direct contact with ice
