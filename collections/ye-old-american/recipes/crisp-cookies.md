## Ingredients

- One cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- three eggs well beaten
- a teaspoonful of soda
- two of cream of tartar
- spoonful of milk
- one teaspoonful of nutmeg
- one of cinnamon
- Flour enough to make a soft dough

## Directions

1. Try a pint of sifted flour to begin with, working it in gradually
2. Spread a little sweet milk over each and sprinkle with sugar
3. Bake in a quick oven a light brown
