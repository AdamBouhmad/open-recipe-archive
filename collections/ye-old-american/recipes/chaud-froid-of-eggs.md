## Ingredients

- six hard-boiled eggs
- one-third cup cold cooked chicken
- two tablespoons cold cooked ham
- two tablespoons chopped raw mushroom caps
- one-half tablespoon chopped truffles
- yolks of four eggs
- Spanish Sauce
- aspic
- cress

## Directions

1. Cut six hard-boiled eggs in halves lengthwise and remove yolks.
2. Mix one-third cup cold cooked chicken finely chopped, two tablespoons cold cooked ham finely chopped, two tablespoons chopped raw mushroom caps, one-half tablespoon chopped truffles, and yolks of four of the eggs rubbed through a sieve.
3. Moisten with Spanish Sauce and refill whites with mixture.
4. Mask eggs with Spanish Sauce, garnish with truffles, cut in fancy shapes, and brush over with aspic.
5. Arrange on serving dish and garnish with cress.
