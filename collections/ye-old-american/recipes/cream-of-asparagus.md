## Ingredients

- fresh asparagus
- salted water
- stock or water
- onion
- butter
- flour
- milk
- cream

## Directions

1. Use two bundles of fresh asparagus for making two quarts of soup
2. Cut the tops from one of the bunches and cook them twenty minutes in salted water, enough to cover them
3. Cook the remainder of the asparagus about twenty minutes in a quart of stock or water
4. Cut an onion into thin slices and fry in three tablespoonfuls of butter ten minutes, being careful not to scorch it
5. Add the asparagus that has been boiled in the stock and cook this five minutes, stirring constantly
6. Add three tablespoonfuls of dissolved flour and cook five minutes longer
7. Turn this mixture into the boiling stock and boil twenty minutes
8. Rub through a sieve
9. Add the milk and cream and the asparagus heads
10. If water is used in place of stock, use all cream
