## Ingredients

- 1 square Baker's chocolate
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ⅓ cup water
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate
2. Add butter, sugar, and water
3. Let boil fifteen minutes
4. Cool slightly and add vanilla
