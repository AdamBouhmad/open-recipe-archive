## Ingredients

- 2½ cups berries
- Flour
- ½ cup sugar
- ⅛ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Line a deep plate with Plain Paste.
2. Fill with berries slightly dredged with flour.
3. Sprinkle with sugar and salt.
4. Cover and bake forty-five to fifty minutes in a moderate oven.
