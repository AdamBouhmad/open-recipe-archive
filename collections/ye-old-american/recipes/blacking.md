## Ingredients

- one quarter of a pound of ivory black
- two ounces of sugar candy
- one quarter of an ounce of gum tragacanth
- one bottle of porter

## Directions

1. Pound them all very fine
2. Boil a bottle of porter
3. Stir the ingredients in while boiling hot
