## Ingredients

- three pounds of beef from centre of fillet
- salt
- olive oil
- red pepper
- parsley
- one and one-half cups rich brown sauce
- two-thirds teaspoon meat extract
- one tablespoon lemon juice
- one and one-half tablespoons parsley
- one tablespoon butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Trim off fat and skin from three pounds of beef cut from centre of fillet
2. Flatten with a broad-bladed cleaver
3. Sprinkle with salt
4. Brush over with olive oil
5. Broil over a clear fire twenty minutes
6. Remove to serving dish
7. Garnish with red pepper cut in fancy shapes and parsley
8. For Espagnole Sauce: To one and one-half cups rich brown sauce add two-thirds teaspoon meat extract
9. Add one tablespoon lemon juice
10. Add one and one-half tablespoons finely chopped parsley
11. Just before serving add one tablespoon butter and salt and pepper to taste
