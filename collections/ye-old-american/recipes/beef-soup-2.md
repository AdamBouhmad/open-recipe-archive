## Ingredients

- A small shin of beef of moderate size
- Five or six quarts of cold water
- A tablespoonful of salt
- A teaspoonful of pepper
- One carrot
- Two turnips
- Two tablespoonfuls of rice or pearl barley
- One head of celery
- A teaspoonful of summer savory powdered fine
- Two potatoes
- Made mustard and catsup
- A glass of brown sherry

## Directions

1. Select a small shin of beef of moderate size, crack the bone in small pieces, wash and place it in a kettle to boil, with five or six quarts of cold water
2. Let it boil about two hours, or until it begins to get tender
3. Season it with a tablespoonful of salt, and a teaspoonful of pepper
4. Boil it one hour longer
5. Add one carrot, two turnips, two tablespoonfuls of rice or pearl barley, one head of celery, and a teaspoonful of summer savory powdered fine, with the vegetables minced up in small pieces like dice
6. After these ingredients have boiled a quarter of an hour, put in two potatoes cut up in small pieces
7. Let it boil half an hour longer
8. Take the meat from the soup, and if intended to be served with it, take out the bones and lay it closely and neatly on a dish, and garnish with sprigs of parsley
9. Serve made mustard and catsup with it
10. Serve very hot
