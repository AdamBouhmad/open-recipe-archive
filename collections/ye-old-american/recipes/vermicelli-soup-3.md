## Ingredients

- quarter of a pound of vermicelli
- one quart of warm water
- a good beef, veal, lamb, or chicken soup or broth
- quarter of a pound of sweet butter

## Directions

1. Swell quarter of a pound of vermicelli in a quart of warm water.
2. Add it to a good beef, veal, lamb, or chicken soup or broth, with quarter of a pound of sweet butter.
3. Let the soup boil for fifteen minutes after it is added.
