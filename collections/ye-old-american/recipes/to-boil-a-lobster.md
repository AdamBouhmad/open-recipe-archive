## Ingredients

- salt
- lobster
- twine
- parsley

## Directions

1. Put a handful of salt into a large kettle or pot of boiling water.
2. When the water boils very hard put in the lobster, having first brushed it, and tied the claws together with a bit of twine.
3. Keep it boiling from half an hour to an hour in proportion to its size.
4. When it is done, take it out, lay it on its claws to drain, and then wipe it dry.
5. Send it to table cold, with the body and tail split open, and the claws taken off.
6. Lay the large claws next to the body, and the small ones outside.
7. Garnish with double parsley.
