## Ingredients

- ½ cup lentils
- ¼ cup dried lima beans
- 3 pints water
- ½ small onion
- 1 stalk celery
- 3 slices carrot
- 1 sprig parsley
- ½ cup stale bread crumbs
- 1 egg slightly beaten
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 tablespoon flour
- ⅓ cup hot cream

## Directions

1. Soak lentils and dried lima beans overnight in cold water to cover; drain, add three pints water, one-half small onion, one stalk celery, three slices carrot, and a sprig of parsley.
2. Cook until lentils are soft, remove seasonings, drain, and rub through a sieve.
3. To pulp add one-half cup stale bread crumbs, one egg slightly beaten, and salt and pepper to taste.
4. Melt one tablespoon butter, add one tablespoon flour, and pour on gradually one-third cup hot cream; combine mixtures, and cool.
5. Shape, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again, fry in deep fat, and drain on brown paper.
6. Serve with Tomato Sauce I.
