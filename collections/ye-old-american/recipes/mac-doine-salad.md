## Ingredients

- cold cooked cauliflower
- peas
- carrots
- outer stalks of celery
- small gherkin
- tomato

## Directions

1. Marinate separately cold cooked cauliflower, peas, and carrots cut in small cubes, and outer stalks of celery finely cut.
2. Arrange peas and carrots in alternate piles in centre of a salad dish.
3. Pile cauliflower on top.
4. Arrange celery in four piles at equal distances.
5. At top of each pile place a small gherkin cut lengthwise in very thin slices, beginning at blossom end and cutting nearly to stem end.
6. Open slices to represent a fan.
7. Place between piles of celery a slice of tomato.
