## Ingredients

- one quart stewed and strained
- 3 pints cream
- 9 beaten eggs
- sugar
- mace
- nutmeg and ginger
- paste No. 7 or 3

## Directions

1. laid into paste No. 7 or 3
2. and with a dough spur, cross and chequer it
3. and baked in dishes three quarters of an hour
