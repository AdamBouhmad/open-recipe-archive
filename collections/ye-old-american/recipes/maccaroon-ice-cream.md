## Ingredients

- a quart of cream
- two ounces of bitter almonds or peach kernels blanched and broken up
- six eggs
- half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
- the best almond maccaroons

## Directions

1. From a quart of cream take half a pint and boil in it slowly two ounces of bitter almonds or peach kernels previously blanched and broken up
2. When it is highly flavoured with the almonds, strain the half pint and mix it with the remaining pint and a half of cream
3. Add to the cream by degrees six eggs previously beaten till very light and half a pound of powdered loaf-sugar
4. Crumble a sufficient quantity of the best almond maccaroons to make a thick batter when stirred gradually into the mixture of cream, sugar, and eggs
5. Beat the mixture till perfectly smooth
6. Give it a boil, stirring it well while boiling
7. Put it into a freezer and freeze it as usual
8. Transfer it to a pyramid mould and freeze it again
