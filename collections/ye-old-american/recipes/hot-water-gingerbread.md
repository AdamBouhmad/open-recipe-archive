## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- ½ cup boiling water
- 2¼ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1½ teaspoons ginger
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 4 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Add water to molasses.
2. Mix and sift dry ingredients.
3. Combine mixtures and add butter.
4. Beat vigorously.
5. Pour into a buttered shallow pan.
6. Bake twenty-five minutes in a moderate oven.
