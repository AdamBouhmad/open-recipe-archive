## Ingredients

- one pint of rye flour
- one quart of corn meal
- one teacupful of Graham flour
- half a teacupful of molasses or brown sugar
- a teaspoonful of salt
- two-thirds of a teacupful of home-made yeast
- warm water
- one teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Mix into as stiff a dough as can be stirred with a spoon, using warm water for wetting
2. Let it rise several hours, or over night
3. in the morning, or when light, add a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a spoonful of warm water
4. beat it well and turn it into well-greased, deep bread-pans, and let it rise again
5. Bake in a moderate oven from three to four hours
