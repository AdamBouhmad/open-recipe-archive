## Ingredients

- four large lemons
- a quart of brandy or rum
- a pound and a half of loaf-sugar
- two nutmegs
- a quart of water
- a quart of rich unskimmed milk

## Directions

1. Pare off the yellow rind of four large lemons
2. Steep it for twenty-four hours in a quart of brandy or rum
3. Mix with it the juice of the lemons, a pound and a half of loaf-sugar, and two grated nutmegs
4. Add a quart of water
5. Add a quart of rich unskimmed milk, made boiling hot
6. Strain the whole through a jelly-bag
7. Use it as soon as it is cold, or make a larger quantity and bottle it
8. It will keep several months
