## Ingredients

- a large tablespoonful of salt codfish
- cold water
- half a cupful of milk or thin cream
- butter
- pepper
- one teaspoonful of flour or cornstarch
- a slice of dipped toast

## Directions

1. Pick up a large tablespoonful of salt codfish very fine
2. Freshen it considerably by placing it over the fire in a basin, covering it with cold water as it comes to a boil
3. Turn off the water and freshen again if very salt, then turn off the water until dry
4. Pour over half a cupful of milk or thin cream, add a bit of butter, a sprinkle of pepper, and a thickening made of one teaspoonful of flour or cornstarch, wet up with a little milk
5. When this boils up, turn over a slice of dipped toast
