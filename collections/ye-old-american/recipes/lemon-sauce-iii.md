## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- yolks of 3 eggs
- ⅓ cup boiling water
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- few gratings lemon rind

## Directions

1. Cream butter, add sugar gradually, and yolks of eggs, slightly beaten; then add water, and cook over boiling water until mixture thickens.
2. Remove from range, add lemon juice and rind.
3. Serve with Apple Pudding or Pop-overs.
