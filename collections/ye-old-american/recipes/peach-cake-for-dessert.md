## Ingredients

- three sheets of sponge cake
- nice ripe peaches
- cream
- sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Bake three sheets of sponge cake, as for jelly cake
2. Cut nice ripe peaches in thin slices, or chop them
3. Prepare cream by whipping, sweetening and adding flavor of vanilla, if desired
4. Put layers of peaches between the sheets of cake
5. Pour cream over each layer and over the top
6. Eat soon after it is prepared
