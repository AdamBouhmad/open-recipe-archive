## Ingredients

- 1¼ cups rye meal
- 1¼ cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup molasses
- 1¼ cups milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix and bake as Graham Muffins II, adding molasses with milk.
