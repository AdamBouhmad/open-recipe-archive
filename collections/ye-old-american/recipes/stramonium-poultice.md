## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of Indian meal
- one gill of boiling water
- one tablespoonful of bruised stramonium seeds

## Directions

1. Stir one tablespoonful of Indian meal into a gill of boiling water and add one tablespoonful of bruised stramonium seeds.
