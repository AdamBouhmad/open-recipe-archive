## Ingredients

- hard-boiled eggs
- pepper
- salt
- beaten raw egg
- fine bread-crumbs or powdered cracker
- dripping or butter

## Directions

1. Boil hard, and cut in round thick slices.
2. Pepper and salt; dip each in beaten raw egg, then in fine bread-crumbs or powdered cracker, and fry in nice dripping or butter, hissing hot.
3. Drain off every drop of grease, and serve on a hot dish for breakfast, with sauce, like that for fricasseed eggs, poured over them.
