## Ingredients

- fresh mackerel
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- cream sauce
- egg sauce

## Directions

1. Cook fresh mackerel in water salted, and a little vinegar added
2. They can be served in the same way as the salt mackerel, with cream sauce or egg sauce
