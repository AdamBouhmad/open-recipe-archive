## Ingredients

- 1¼ cups Graham flour
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup sour milk
- ⅓ cup molasses
- ¾ teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients; add milk to molasses, and combine mixtures.
