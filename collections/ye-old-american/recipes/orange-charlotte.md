## Ingredients

- one-third of a box of gelatine
- one-third of a cupful of cold water
- one-third of a cupful of boiling water
- one cup of sugar
- the juice of one lemon
- one cupful of orange juice and pulp
- a little grated orange peel
- the whites of four eggs
- pieces of sponge cake

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine in the cold water one hour
2. Pour the boiling water over the lemon and orange juice and cover it and let stand half an hour
3. Add the sugar and let it come to a boil on the fire
4. Stir in the gelatine and when it is thoroughly dissolved, take from the fire
5. When cool enough, beat into it the four beaten whites of eggs
6. Turn into the mold with pieces of sponge cake placed all around the mold and set in a cold place to stiffen
