## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 tablespoons butter
- more milk
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Use recipe for Baking Powder Biscuit I or II, with the addition of more milk, that mixture may be dropped from spoon without spreading.
2. Drop by spoonfuls on a buttered pan, one-half inch apart.
3. Brush over with milk, and bake in hot oven eight minutes.
