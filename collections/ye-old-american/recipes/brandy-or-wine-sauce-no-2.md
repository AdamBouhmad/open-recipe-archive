## Ingredients

- one cupful of butter
- two of powdered sugar
- the whites of two eggs
- five tablespoonfuls of sherry wine or brandy
- a quarter of a cupful of boiling water

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add the whites of the eggs, one at a time, unbeaten
3. Add the wine or brandy
4. Place the bowl in hot water and stir till smooth and frothy
