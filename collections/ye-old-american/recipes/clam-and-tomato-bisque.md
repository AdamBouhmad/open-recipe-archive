## Ingredients

- 1 quart clams
- 1½ cups cold water
- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅓ cup flour
- ½ onion
- 2 cups cream
- 1 cup stewed and strained tomatoes
- ⅛ teaspoon soda
- Salt
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Pour water over clams, then drain.
2. To water add hard part of clams finely chopped.
3. Heat slowly to boiling-point, cook twenty minutes, then strain.
4. Cook butter with onion five minutes; remove onion, add flour and gradually clam water.
5. Add cream, soft part of clams, and as soon as boiling-point is reached, tomatoes to which soda has been added.
6. Season with salt and cayenne, and serve at once.
