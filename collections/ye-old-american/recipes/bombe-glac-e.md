## Ingredients

- sherbet or water ice
- ice cream or thin Charlotte Russe mixture

## Directions

1. Line a mould with sherbet or water ice
2. Fill with ice cream or thin Charlotte Russe mixture
3. Cover, pack in salt and ice
4. Let stand two hours
