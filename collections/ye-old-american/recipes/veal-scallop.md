## Ingredients

- cold roast or stewed veal
- pepper
- salt
- finely powdered crackers
- butter
- milk
- gravy or broth
- warm water
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Chop some cold roast or stewed veal very fine
2. Put a layer in the bottom of a buttered pudding dish and season with pepper and salt
3. Next have a layer of finely powdered crackers
4. Strew some bits of butter upon it and wet with a little milk
5. Then more veal seasoned as before and another round of cracker-crumbs with butter and milk
6. When the dish is full wet well with gravy or broth diluted with warm water
7. Spread over all a thick layer of cracker seasoned with salt wet into a paste with milk and bound with a beaten egg or two if the dish be large
8. Stick butter-bits thickly over it
9. Invert a tin pan so as to cover all and keep in the steam
10. Bake if small half an hour three-quarters will suffice for a large dish
11. Remove the cover ten minutes before it is served and brown
