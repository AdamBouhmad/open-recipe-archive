## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 cup loppered milk or cream
- 4 eggs
- 6 cups flour, or just enough to stiffen into a rollable paste
- 2 tablespoonfuls coriander seed, ground or beaten
- 1 tablespoonful soda, dissolved in boiling water
- Two teaspoonfuls cream-tartar, if using sweet milk

## Directions

1. If you use sweet milk, add two teaspoonfuls cream-tartar.
2. You may substitute caraway for the coriander-seed.
