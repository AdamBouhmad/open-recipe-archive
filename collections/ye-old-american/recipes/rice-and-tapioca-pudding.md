## Ingredients

- ½ cup rice
- ½ cup tapioca
- ¾ cup sugar
- 3 pints milk
- Cinnamon to taste

## Directions

1. Soak the tapioca in a cup of the milk three hours; wash the rice in several waters, and soak in another cup of milk as long as you do the tapioca.
2. Sweeten the remaining quart of milk; put all the ingredients together, and bake two hours in a slow oven.
3. Eat cold.
