## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of currant or grape jelly
- the white of one egg
- a teaspoonful of sugar
- a teacupful of boiling water
- a slice of dry toast or two crackers

## Directions

1. Take one tablespoonful of currant or grape jelly, beat with it the white of one egg and a teaspoonful of sugar
2. Pour on it a teacupful of boiling water, and break in a slice of dry toast or two crackers
