## Ingredients

- raw ham
- butter
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Cut raw ham into dice
2. Fry with butter and when cooked enough, turn the beaten egg over it and cook as a plain omelet
3. If boiled ham is used, mince it and mix with the egg after they are beaten
4. Bacon may be used instead of raw ham
