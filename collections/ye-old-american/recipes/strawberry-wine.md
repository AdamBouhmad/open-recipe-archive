## Ingredients

- 3 quarts of strawberries, mashed and strained
- 1 quart of water
- 1 lb. of sugar

## Directions

1. Mash and strain strawberries
2. Add water and sugar to the juice
3. Stir up well and ferment in a clean, sweet cask, leaving the bung out
4. When the working subsides close tightly, or rack off into bottles
