## Ingredients

- twelve selected smelts
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- butter
- buttered paper
- buttered crumbs
- Sauce Bearnaise
- one tablespoon finely chopped onion
- one tablespoon butter
- one-fourth cup finely chopped mushrooms
- one-fourth cup soft part of oysters

## Directions

1. Clean and wipe as dry as possible the smelts.
2. Stuff, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and brush over with lemon juice.
3. Place in buttered shallow plate, cover with buttered paper, and bake five minutes in hot oven.
4. Remove from oven, sprinkle with buttered crumbs, and bake until crumbs are brown.
5. Serve with Sauce Bearnaise.
