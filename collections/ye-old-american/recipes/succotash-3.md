## Ingredients

- hot boiled corn
- hot boiled shelled beans
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut hot boiled corn from cob.
2. Add equal quantity of hot boiled shelled beans.
3. Season with butter and salt.
4. Reheat before serving.
