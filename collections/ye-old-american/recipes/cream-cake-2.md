## Ingredients

- Four eggs
- Two teacups of sugar
- One cup of sweet cream
- Two heaping cupfuls of flour
- One teaspoonful of soda
- Two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Beat egg whites and yolks separately
2. Mix sugar, sweet cream, and egg yolks together
3. Mix cream of tartar with flour before sifting
4. Stir flour into the mixture
5. Add beaten egg whites last thing before flour and stir gently without beating
6. Bake until done
