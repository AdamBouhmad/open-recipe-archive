## Ingredients

- pears (about three-fourths of a pound)
- cloves
- lemon or orange peel
- two cupfuls of water
- one pint of cream or milk
- four eggs
- sugar to taste
- a pinch of salt
- a tablespoonful of flour
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pare some nice ripe pears
2. Put them in a saucepan with a few cloves, some lemon or orange peel, and stew about a quarter of an hour in two cupfuls of water
3. Put them in your pudding-dish
4. Beat eggs and sugar well, add the flour, grate some nutmeg, add the cream by degrees, stirring all the time
5. Pour this over the pears and bake in a quick oven
6. Serve cold with sweetened cream
7. Apples or peaches may be substituted
