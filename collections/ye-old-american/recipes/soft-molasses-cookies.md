## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1¾ teaspoons soda
- 1 cup sour milk
- ½ cup shortening, melted
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Add soda to molasses and beat thoroughly; add milk, shortening, ginger, salt, and flour.
2. Enough flour must be used to make mixture of right consistency to drop easily from spoon.
3. Let stand several hours in a cold place to thoroughly chill.
4. Toss one-half mixture at a time on slightly floured board and roll lightly to one-fourth inch thickness.
5. Shape with a round cutter, first dipped in flour.
6. Bake on a buttered sheet.
