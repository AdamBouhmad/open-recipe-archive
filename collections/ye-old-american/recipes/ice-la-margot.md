## Ingredients

- vanilla ice cream
- whipped cream, sweetened
- pistachio flavoring
- pistachio nuts or Malaga grapes

## Directions

1. Serve vanilla ice cream in champagne glasses
2. Cover ice cream with whipped cream, sweetened, flavored with pistachio, and tinted very light green
3. Garnish with pistachio nuts or Malaga grapes cut in halves
