## Ingredients

- a pint of milk
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a cup of rich cream
- salt
- a beaten egg
- toast lightly buttered

## Directions

1. Heat a pint of milk to boiling
2. Add a piece of butter the size of an egg
3. Stir a tablespoonful of flour smoothly into a cup of rich cream
4. Add some of the boiling milk to this
5. Heat it gradually and prevent the flour from lumping
6. Stir into the boiling milk and let it cook a few moments
7. Salt to taste
8. After taking from the fire stir in a beaten egg
9. Strain the mixture on to toast lightly buttered
