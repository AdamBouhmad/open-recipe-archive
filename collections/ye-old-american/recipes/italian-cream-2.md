## Ingredients

- 2 pints of cream
- 2 cups of sugar
- 2 lemons—juice and grated peel
- 2 tablespoonfuls of brandy

## Directions

1. Sweeten the cream and beat in the lemons gradually, not to curdle it
2. Add the brandy and freeze in a patent freezer, or by turning quickly
3. In turning the freezer, open twice during the operation, to stir and beat the contents smooth
