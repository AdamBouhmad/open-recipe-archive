## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons rice
- 2 cups milk
- ½ onion
- 3 cloves
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash rice, add to milk, and cook in double boiler until soft.
2. Rub through a fine strainer, return to double boiler, add onion stuck with cloves, and cook fifteen minutes.
3. Remove onion, add butter, salt, and pepper.
