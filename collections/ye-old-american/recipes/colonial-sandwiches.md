## Ingredients

- one-half recipe Milk and Water Bread made with entire wheat flour
- one and one-half tablespoons molasses
- one-half cup candied orange peel finely cut
- one-half cup pecan nut meats broken in pieces
- butter

## Directions

1. Make one-half the recipe for Milk and Water Bread, using entire wheat flour.
2. Add one and one-half tablespoons molasses.
3. After the first rising, add while kneading one-half cup candied orange peel finely cut and one-half cup pecan nut meats broken in pieces.
4. Put into buttered one-pound baking-powder tins until one-third full.
5. Let rise and bake.
6. Cool and make into sandwiches.
