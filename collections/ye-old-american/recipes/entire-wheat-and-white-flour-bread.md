## Ingredients

- 2 cups scalded milk
- ¼ cup sugar or ⅓ cup molasses
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 yeast cake dissolved in ¼ cup lukewarm water
- 3¼ cups entire wheat flour
- 2¾ cups white flour

## Directions

1. Use same ingredients as for Entire Wheat Bread, with exception of flour
2. Use three and one-fourth cups entire wheat and two and three-fourths cups white flour
3. The dough should be slightly kneaded, and if handled quickly will not stick to board
4. Loaves and biscuits should be shaped with hands instead of pouring into pans, as in Entire Wheat Bread
