## Ingredients

- 5 potatoes, riced
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Few grains pepper
- ⅓ cup hot milk

## Directions

1. To five riced potatoes add three tablespoons butter, one teaspoon salt, few grains pepper, and one-third cup hot milk.
2. Beat with fork until creamy, reheat, and pile lightly in hot dish.
