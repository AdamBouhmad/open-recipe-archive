## Ingredients

- forty grains of carbonate of soda
- thirty grains of tartaric acid in small crystals
- spring water

## Directions

1. Fill a soda bottle with spring water
2. put in the mixture of carbonate of soda and tartaric acid
3. cork it instantly with a well-fitting cork
