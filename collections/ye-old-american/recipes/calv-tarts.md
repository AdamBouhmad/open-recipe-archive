## Ingredients

- Puff or plain paste
- Canned peaches
- Blanched Jordan almonds

## Directions

1. Roll puff or plain paste one-eighth inch thick, and cut in rounds of correct size to cover inverted circular tins.
2. Cover tins with paste, prick several times, and bake until delicately browned.
3. Place one-half a canned peach in each case and fill each cavity with one-half a blanched Jordan almond.
