## Ingredients

- 1 lb. sugar
- ¾ lb. sifted flour
- 6 oz. butter
- The whipped whites of ten eggs
- bitter almond flavoring

## Directions

1. Bake in square, not very deep tins. Flavor the frosting with vanilla.
