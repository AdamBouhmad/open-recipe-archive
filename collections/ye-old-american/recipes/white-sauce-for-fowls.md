## Ingredients

- scrag of veal
- necks of fowls
- bits of mutton or veal
- blade or two of mace
- black pepper corns
- one anchovy
- head of celery
- bunch of sweet herbs
- slice of the end of a lemon
- quart of water
- quarter of a pound of butter
- flour
- two spoonsful of pickled mushrooms
- yelks of two eggs
- tea cup full of good cream
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put the scrag of veal, necks of fowls, or any bits of mutton or veal in a sauce pan with a blade or two of mace, a few black pepper corns, one anchovy, a head of celery, a bunch of sweet herbs, and a slice of the end of a lemon
2. Put in a quart of water
3. Cover it close and let it boil till it is reduced to half a pint
4. Strain it
5. Thicken it with a quarter of a pound of butter mixed with flour
6. Boil it five or six minutes
7. Put in two spoonsful of pickled mushrooms
8. Mix the yelks of two eggs with a tea cup full of good cream and a little nutmeg
9. Put it in the sauce
10. Keep shaking it over the fire, but don't let it boil
