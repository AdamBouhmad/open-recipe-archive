## Ingredients

- 5 large mushroom caps
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ⅓ cup cream
- 1 cup chicken stock
- Salt
- Paprika
- 1 chopped truffle

## Directions

1. Clean five large mushroom caps, cut in halves crosswise, then in slices.
2. Sauté in three tablespoons butter five minutes.
3. Dredge with two tablespoons flour, add one-third cup cream and one cup chicken stock, and cook two minutes.
4. Season with salt and paprika, and add one chopped truffle.
