## Ingredients

- an eggplant
- salt
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Pare an eggplant and cut in very thin slices.
2. Sprinkle slices with salt and pile on a plate.
3. Cover with a weight to express the juice, and let stand one and one-half hours.
4. Dredge with flour and sauté slowly in butter until crisp and brown.
