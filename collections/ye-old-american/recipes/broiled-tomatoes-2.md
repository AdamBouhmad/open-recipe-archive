## Ingredients

- large firm tomatoes
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- sugar
- made mustard

## Directions

1. Select large, firm tomatoes and do not peel.
2. Slice half an inch thick.
3. Broil upon an oyster gridiron for a few minutes.
4. Have ready in a cup some hot butter, seasoned with pepper, salt, a little sugar, and a half a teaspoonful of made mustard.
5. As soon as the tomatoes are done, dip each piece in this mixture and lay upon a hot chafing-dish.
6. When all are dished, heat what remains of the seasoning to a boil.
7. Pour upon them and serve at once.
