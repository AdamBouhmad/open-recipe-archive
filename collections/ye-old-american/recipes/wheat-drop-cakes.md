## Ingredients

- One pint of cream
- Six eggs well beaten
- A little salt
- Wheat flour

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs well
2. Mix with one pint of cream and a little salt
3. Add wheat flour enough to make a thin batter
4. Bake in little cups buttered in a hot oven fifteen minutes
