## Ingredients

- 1 lb. halibut
- ⅔ cup milk
- Yolk 1 egg
- 1¼ teaspoons salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- Few grains cayenne
- ⅔ teaspoon corn-starch
- ⅓ cup thick cream

## Directions

1. Force fish through a meat chopper, then rub through a sieve or finely chop.
2. Add yolk of egg, seasonings, corn-starch, and cream beaten until stiff.
3. Cook same as Halibut Timbales I and serve with Cream or Lobster Sauce.
