## Ingredients

- Yolks 4 eggs
- Grated rind and juice 1 lemon
- 1 cup sugar
- Whites 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat yolks until thick and lemon-colored
2. Add sugar gradually and continue beating
3. Add lemon rind and juice
4. Cut and fold in whites of eggs beaten until dry
5. Turn into buttered pudding-dish
6. Set in pan of hot water, and bake thirty-five to forty minutes
7. Serve with or without sauce
