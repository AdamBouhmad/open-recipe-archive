## Ingredients

- Three cups of sugar
- Two scant cups of butter
- One cup of sour milk
- Five eggs
- One teaspoonful of soda
- Three tablespoonfuls of cinnamon
- Half a nutmeg
- Two cups of raisins
- One cup of currants
- Four cups of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients as usual
2. Stir fruit in at the last, dredged in flour
3. Line cake-pans with paper well buttered
4. Bake in oven at even temperature, longer than plain cake
