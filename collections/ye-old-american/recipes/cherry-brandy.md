## Ingredients

- morello cherries
- common black cherries
- boiling water
- brandy
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Get equal quantities of morello and common black cherries
2. Fill your cask, and pour on (to a ten gallon cask) one gallon of boiling water
3. In two or three hours, fill it up with brandy
4. Let it stand a week, then draw off all, and put another gallon of boiling water, and fill it again with brandy
5. At the end of the week, draw the whole off, empty the cask of the cherries, and pour in your brandy with water, to reduce the strength
6. First dissolving one pound of brown sugar in each gallon of your mixture
