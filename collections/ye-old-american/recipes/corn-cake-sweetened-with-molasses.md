## Ingredients

- 1 cup corn meal
- ¾ cup flour
- 3½ teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup molasses
- ¾ cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoon melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix and bake as Golden Corn Cake, adding molasses to milk.
