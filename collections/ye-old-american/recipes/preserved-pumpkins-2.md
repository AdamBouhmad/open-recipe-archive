## Ingredients

- pumpkin (one pound)
- loaf sugar, roughly pounded (one pound)
- lemon juice (one gill)
- water (half a pint to every three pounds of sugar)

## Directions

1. Obtain a good, sweet pumpkin
2. Halve it, take out the seeds and pare off the rind
3. Cut it into neat slices
4. Weigh the pumpkin
5. Put the slices in a pan or deep dish in layers, with the sugar sprinkled between them
6. Pour the lemon juice over the top, and let the whole remain for two or three days
7. Boil all together, adding half a pint of water to every three pounds of sugar used until the pumpkin becomes tender
8. Turn the whole into a pan, where let it remain for a week
9. Drain off the syrup, boil it until it is quite thick, skim
