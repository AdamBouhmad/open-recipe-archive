## Ingredients

- carrots
- water
- broth
- pepper
- salt
- cream or milk
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Scrape, and lay in cold water half an hour or more.
2. Boil whole three-quarters of an hour, drain, and cut into round slices a quarter of an inch thick.
3. Put on in a saucepan with a teacupful of broth—veal, or beef, or mutton; pepper and salt to taste, and stew gently half an hour.
4. Just before they are done, add four tablespoonfuls cream or milk, and a good lump of butter cut into bits, and rolled in flour.
5. Boil up and serve.
6. If you have not the broth, use water, and put in a tablespoonful of butter when the saucepan is set on the fire, in addition to the quantity specified.
