## Ingredients

- Three cupfuls of sugar
- One cupful of butter
- One cupful of milk
- Whites of eight eggs
- Four cups of sifted flour
- One tablespoonful of baking powder
- Flavoring
- Whites of four eggs
- Two cups of fine white sugar
- Juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Cream sugar and butter together, making it very light
2. Add milk
3. Beat whites of eight eggs very stiff
4. Add half of the beaten egg whites to the other ingredients
5. Mix baking powder well into sifted flour
6. Stir flour mixture into the cake
7. Add flavoring
8. Add remaining beaten egg whites
9. Bake in layers like jelly cake
10. Make icing using whites of four eggs beaten to stiff froth with two cups sugar and juice of half a lemon
11. Spread icing thickly on each layer
12. Stack layers and ice all over the top and sides
