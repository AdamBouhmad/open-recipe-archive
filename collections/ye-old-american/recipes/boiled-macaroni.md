## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup macaroni broken in inch pieces
- 2 quarts boiling water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/2 cup cream

## Directions

1. Cook macaroni in boiling salted water twenty minutes or until soft
2. Drain in strainer
3. Pour over it cold water to prevent pieces from adhering
4. Add cream
5. Reheat
6. Season with salt
