## Ingredients

- 1 teacupful salt codfish picked very fine
- 2 cups mashed potatoes
- 1 pint cream or milk
- 2 well-beaten eggs
- 1 half cup butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Freshen the salt codfish (desiccated is nice to use)
2. Mix the codfish, mashed potatoes, cream or milk, well-beaten eggs, butter, salt and pepper
3. Bake in an earthen baking dish from twenty to twenty-five minutes
4. Serve in the same dish, placed on a small platter, covered with a fine napkin
