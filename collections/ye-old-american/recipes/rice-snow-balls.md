## Ingredients

- two teacupfuls of rice
- one teacupful of water
- one of milk
- a little salt
- vanilla
- a rich soft custard

## Directions

1. Wash two teacupfuls of rice and boil it in one teacupful of water and one of milk, with a little salt
2. If the rice is not tender when the milk and water are absorbed, add a little more milk and water
3. When the rice is tender, flavor with vanilla
4. Form it into balls, or mold it into a compact form with little cups
5. Place these rice balls around the inside of a deep dish
6. Fill the dish with a rich soft custard flavored the same way
7. Serve either hot or cold
