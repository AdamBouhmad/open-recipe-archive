## Ingredients

- one-quarter of a pound of macaroni
- hot water
- salt
- one half pint of milk
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- one tablespoonful of flour
- two tablespoonfuls of cream
- white pepper
- cayenne pepper
- one-quarter to one-half a pound of grated cheese

## Directions

1. Boil one-quarter of a pound of macaroni in plenty of hot water, salted, until tender
2. Put half a pint of milk in a double boiler
3. When it boils stir into it a mixture of two tablespoonfuls of butter and one of flour
4. Add two tablespoonfuls of cream, a little white and cayenne pepper; salt to taste, and from one-quarter to one-half a pound of grated cheese, according to taste
5. Drain and dish the macaroni
6. Pour the boiling sauce over it and serve immediately
