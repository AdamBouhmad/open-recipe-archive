## Ingredients

- chickens
- soda or baking-powder biscuits

## Directions

1. Take chickens, and make a fricassee
2. Just before you are ready to dish it up, have ready two baking-tins of rich soda or baking-powder biscuits
3. Take them from the oven hot, split them apart by breaking them with your hands
4. Lay them on a large meat platter, covering it
5. Pour the hot chicken stew over all
6. Send to the table hot
