## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound butter
- one pound of good sugar
- ten whites to a foam
- yolks and beat together
- one spoon rose water
- 2 of brandy
- one and a quarter of a pound flour

## Directions

1. work three quarters of a pound butter, one pound of good sugar, 'till very white
2. whip ten whites to a foam
3. add the yolks and beat together
4. add one spoon rose water, 2 of brandy
5. and put the whole to one and a quarter of a pound flour
6. if yet too soft add flour and bake slowly
