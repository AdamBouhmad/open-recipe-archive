## Ingredients

- turkey bones
- water
- a little summer savory and celery
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- a small piece of butter

## Directions

1. Take the turkey bones and boil three-quarters of an hour in water enough to cover them.
2. Add a little summer savory and celery chopped fine.
3. Just before serving, thicken with a little flour (browned).
4. Season with pepper, salt and a small piece of butter.
