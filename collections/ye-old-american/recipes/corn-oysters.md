## Ingredients

- raw corn
- one well-beaten egg
- one-fourth cup flour
- salt
- pepper
- deep fat

## Directions

1. Grate raw corn from cobs.
2. To one cup pulp add one well-beaten egg, one-fourth cup flour, and season highly with salt and pepper.
3. Drop by spoonfuls and fry in deep fat, or cook on a hot, well-greased griddle.
4. Make about the size of large oysters.
