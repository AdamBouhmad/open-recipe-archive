## Ingredients

- one quart can of oysters
- oyster liquor
- hot water
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- cold milk
- light biscuit dough

## Directions

1. Scald a quart can of oysters in their own liquor
2. When it boils, skim out the oysters and set them aside in a warm place
3. To the liquor add a pint of hot water
4. Season well with salt and pepper and a generous piece of butter
5. Thicken with flour and cold milk
6. Have ready nice light biscuit dough, rolled twice as thick as pie crust
7. Cut out into inch squares
8. Drop them into the boiling stew
9. Cover closely and cook forty minutes
10. When taken up, stir the oysters into the juice and serve all together in one dish
