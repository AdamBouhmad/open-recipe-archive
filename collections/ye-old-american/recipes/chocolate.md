## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoonfuls grated chocolate to each pint of water
- As much milk as you have water
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Put on the water boiling hot.
2. Rub the chocolate smooth in a little cold water.
3. Stir into the boiling water.
4. Boil twenty minutes.
5. Add the milk and boil ten minutes more, stirring frequently.
6. You can sweeten upon the fire or in the cups.
