## Ingredients

- oysters
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- toast

## Directions

1. Put one quart of oysters in a basin with their own liquor and let them boil three or four minutes.
2. Season with a little salt, pepper and a heaping spoonful of butter.
3. Serve on buttered toast.
