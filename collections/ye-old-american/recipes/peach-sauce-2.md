## Ingredients

- dried peaches
- water
- brown sugar

## Directions

1. Take a quart of dried peaches and soak them in cold water till they are tender
2. Drain them and put them into a covered pan with a very little water
3. Set them on coals and simmer them till they are entirely dissolved
4. Mash them with brown sugar
5. Send them to table cold to eat with roast meat, game or poultry
