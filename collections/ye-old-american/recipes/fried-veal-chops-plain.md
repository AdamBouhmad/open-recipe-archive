## Ingredients

- veal chops
- salt
- pepper
- beaten egg
- cracker crumbs
- drippings or hot lard and butter mixed
- flour
- cream or milk
- fresh tomatoes

## Directions

1. Sprinkle over them salt and pepper, then dip them in beaten egg and cracker crumbs.
2. Fry in drippings, or hot lard and butter mixed.
3. If you wish a gravy with them, add a tablespoonful of flour to the gravy they were fried in and turn in cream or milk.
4. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
5. Boil up and serve hot with the gravy in separate dish.
6. Accompany with a few sound fresh tomatoes, sliced and fried in the same grease the cutlets were, and all dished on the same platter.
