## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar
- cider vinegar
- ground cinnamon
- cloves

## Directions

1. Take seven pounds of fruit, four pounds of sugar, one pint of good cider vinegar, one tablespoonful of ground cinnamon, one teaspoonful of cloves
2. Put into a kettle and boil until the fruit is soft
3. Skim out the fruit, putting it on dishes until the syrup is boiled down thick
4. Turn the fruit back into the syrup again, so as to heat it all through
5. Seal it hot in glass jars, and set it in a cool, dark place
