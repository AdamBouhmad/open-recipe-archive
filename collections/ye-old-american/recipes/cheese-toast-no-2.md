## Ingredients

- half an ounce of butter
- four ounces of mild American cheese
- half a pint of cream
- two eggs
- a little salt
- crisp toast

## Directions

1. Put half an ounce of butter in a frying pan
2. When hot add gradually four ounces of mild American cheese
3. Whisk it thoroughly until melted
4. Beat together half a pint of cream and two eggs
5. Whisk into the cheese
6. Add a little salt
7. Pour over the crisp toast and serve
