## Ingredients

- ½ tablespoon granulated gelatine
- 1½ tablespoons cold water
- ¾ cup chicken stock
- 1 cup chopped cooked chicken
- 1 cup cream beaten until thick
- 1 tablespoon Sherry wine
- Few grains cayenne

## Directions

1. Soak one-half tablespoon granulated gelatine in one and one-half tablespoons cold water, and dissolve in three-fourths cup chicken stock.
2. Add one cup chopped cooked chicken, and stir until the mixture begins to thicken, then add one cup cream beaten until thick.
3. Add one tablespoon Sherry wine and a few grains cayenne.
4. Mould, chill, and serve on lettuce leaves.
