## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- Whites 4 eggs
- Vanilla

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for mixing butter cakes. Bake in layers, and put between layers and on top Ice Cream Frosting.
