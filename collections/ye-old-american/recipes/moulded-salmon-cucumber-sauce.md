## Ingredients

- 1 can salmon
- ½ tablespoon salt
- 1½ tablespoons sugar
- ½ tablespoon flour
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- Few grains cayenne
- Yolks 2 eggs
- 1½ tablespoons melted butter
- ¾ cup milk
- ¼ cup vinegar
- ¾ tablespoon granulated gelatine
- 2 tablespoons cold water

## Directions

1. Remove salmon from can, rinse thoroughly with hot water, and separate in flakes.
2. Mix dry ingredients, add egg yolks, butter, milk, and vinegar.
3. Cook over boiling water, stirring constantly until mixture thickens.
4. Add gelatine soaked in cold water.
5. Strain, and add to salmon.
6. Fill individual mould, chill, and serve with Cucumber Sauce.
