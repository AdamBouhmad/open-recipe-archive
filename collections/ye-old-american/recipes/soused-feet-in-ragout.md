## Ingredients

- feet
- flour
- well seasoned gravy
- brown flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Split the feet in two
2. Dredge them with flour and fry them a nice brown
3. Have some well seasoned gravy thickened with brown flour and butter
4. Stew the feet in it a few minutes
