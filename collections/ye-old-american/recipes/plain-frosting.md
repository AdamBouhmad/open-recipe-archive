## Ingredients

- White 1 egg
- 2 teaspoons cold water
- ¾ cup confectioners' sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla or ½ tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Beat white of egg until stiff.
2. Add water and sugar.
3. Beat thoroughly, then add flavoring.
4. Use more sugar if needed.
5. Spread with a broad-bladed knife.
