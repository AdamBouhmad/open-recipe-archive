## Ingredients

- one quart of water
- the juice and pulp of two lemons
- one coffeecupful of sugar
- four tablespoonfuls of cornstarch
- four or five oranges
- the beaten whites of three eggs
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Take one quart of water, the juice and pulp of two lemons, one coffeecupful of sugar
2. When boiling hot, add four tablespoonfuls of cornstarch
3. Let it boil fifteen minutes, stirring all the time
4. When cold, pour it over four or five oranges that have been sliced into a glass dish
5. Over the top spread the beaten whites of three eggs, sweetened and flavored with vanilla
