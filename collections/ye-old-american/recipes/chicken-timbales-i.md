## Ingredients

- Slightly buttered Dario moulds
- Chopped truffles or slices of truffles
- Chicken Force-meat I
- Creamed Chicken and Mushrooms
- Chopped truffles

## Directions

1. Garnish slightly buttered Dario moulds with chopped truffles or slices of truffles cut in fancy shapes.
2. Line with Chicken Force-meat I, fill centres with Creamed Chicken and Mushrooms, to which has been added a few chopped truffles.
3. Cover with Force-meat, and bake same as Lobster Timbales.
4. Serve with Béchamel or Yellow Béchamel Sauce.
