## Ingredients

- six fillets of beef
- salt and pepper
- butter
- one-fourth cup glacéd cherries
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook six fillets same as Sautéd Fillets of Beef à la Moelle.
2. Arrange on serving dish, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and spread with butter.
3. Soak one-fourth cup glacéd cherries fifteen minutes in boiling water.
4. Drain, cut in halves, cover with Sherry wine, and let stand three hours.
5. Pour Cherry Sauce over fillets.
