## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups orange juice
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- Grated rind of two oranges

## Directions

1. Make syrup as for Lemon Ice
2. add fruit juice and grated rind
3. cool, strain, and freeze
