## Ingredients

- goose
- three pints of bread crumbs
- six ounces of butter or part butter and part salt pork
- one teaspoonful each of sage, black pepper and salt
- one chopped onion
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- giblets
- flour

## Directions

1. Stuff the goose with the following mixture: three pints of bread crumbs, six ounces of butter or part butter and part salt pork, one teaspoonful each of sage, black pepper and salt, one chopped onion
2. Do not stuff very full and stitch openings firmly together to keep flavor in and fat out
3. Place in a baking pan with a little water
4. Baste frequently with salt and water, some add vinegar
5. Turn often so that the sides and back may be nicely browned
6. Bake two hours or more
7. When done take from the pan and pour off the fat
8. To the brown gravy left add the chopped giblets which have previously been stewed until tender, together with the water they were boiled in
9. Thicken with a little flour and butter rubbed together
10. Bring to a boil and serve
