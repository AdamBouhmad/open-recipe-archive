## Ingredients

- Boston or friable crackers
- butter
- oyster liquor
- milk
- oysters
- salt
- pepper
- egg

## Directions

1. Crush and roll several handfuls of Boston or other friable crackers.
2. Put a layer in the bottom of a buttered pudding-dish.
3. Wet this with a mixture of the oyster liquor and milk, slightly warmed.
4. Next, have a layer of oysters.
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and lay small bits of butter upon them.
6. Then another layer of moistened crumbs, and so on until the dish is full.
7. Let the top layer be of crumbs, thicker than the rest.
8. Beat an egg into the milk you pour over them.
9. Stick bits of butter thickly over it.
10. Cover the dish and set it in the oven.
11. Bake half an hour.
12. If the dish be large, remove the cover, and brown by setting it upon the upper grating of oven, or by holding a hot shovel over it.
