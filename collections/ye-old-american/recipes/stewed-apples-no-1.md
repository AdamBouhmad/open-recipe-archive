## Ingredients

- a dozen green tart apples
- water
- a cupful of loaf sugar for every twelve apples
- a pinch of mace
- a dozen whole cloves
- cream or boiled custard

## Directions

1. Take a dozen green tart apples, core and slice them
2. Put into a saucepan with just enough water to cover them
3. Cover the saucepan closely, and stew the apples until they are tender and clear
4. Take them out, put them into a deep dish and cover them
5. Add to the juice in the saucepan a cupful of loaf sugar for every twelve apples, and boil it half an hour
6. Add to the syrup a pinch of mace and a dozen whole cloves just ten minutes before taking from the fire
7. Pour scalding hot over the apples and set them in a cold place
8. Eat ice cold with cream or boiled custard
