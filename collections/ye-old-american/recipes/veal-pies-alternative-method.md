## Ingredients

- butter
- sliced hard-boiled eggs
- veal cut in thin slices
- pepper
- sliced ham
- lemon-juice
- flour
- hot water

## Directions

1. Butter a large bowl very thickly and line with sliced hard-boiled eggs
2. Put in perpendicular layers a lining of veal cut in thin slices and seasoned with pepper
3. Next one of sliced ham each slice peppered and sprinkled with lemon-juice
4. Alternate more veal and more ham until the dish is packed to the brim
5. Cover with a thick paste made of flour and hot water just stiff enough to handle with ease
6. Press this closely to the outside of the bowl which should not be at all greasy
7. Let it overlap the rim about half an inch
8. Set the bowl in a pot of hot water not so deep that it will bubble over the top
9. Boil steadily not hard for at least three hours
10. Remove the paste the next day when bowl and contents are perfectly cold
11. Turn out the pie into a large plate or flat dish
12. Cut in circular slices thin as a wafer beginning at the top keeping your carver horizontal
