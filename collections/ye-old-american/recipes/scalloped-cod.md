## Ingredients

- Cold flaked cod
- Salt and pepper
- Oysters
- Melted butter
- Onion juice
- Lemon juice
- Cayenne
- Cracker crumbs
- 3 tablespoons oyster liquor
- Egg or Hollandaise Sauce I

## Directions

1. Line a buttered baking-dish with cold flaked cod, sprinkle with salt and pepper
2. Cover with a layer of oysters (first dipped in melted butter, seasoned with onion juice, lemon juice, and a few grains of cayenne, and then in cracker crumbs)
3. Add three tablespoons oyster liquor
4. Repeat, and cover with buttered cracker crumbs
5. Bake twenty minutes in hot oven
6. Serve with Egg or Hollandaise Sauce I
