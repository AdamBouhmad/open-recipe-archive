## Ingredients

- Halibut, 2 pounds
- Vinegar, 1½ tablespoons
- Powdered tarragon, ⅛ teaspoon
- Peppercorns, 3
- Bay leaf, bit of
- Parsley, sprig of
- Shallot, finely chopped, ½ teaspoon
- Salt and cayenne
- Egg yolks, 3
- Brown Stock, ⅔ cup
- Butter, ¼ cup
- Flour, 1 tablespoon
- Capers, ½ tablespoon
- Parsley, ½ tablespoon

## Directions

1. Cook first six ingredients until reduced one-half.
2. Strain.
3. Add yolks of eggs well beaten, one-half each brown stock and butter, and cook over hot water, stirring constantly until thickened.
4. Add gradually remaining butter mixed with flour and stock.
5. As soon as mixture thickens, add capers, parsley finely chopped, and salt and cayenne.
6. Steam the halibut by cooking over boiling water.
7. Serve with the Silesian Sauce.
