## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Salt
- Butter
- Pepper
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Toast points

## Directions

1. Remove roots, carefully pick over (discarding wilted leaves), and wash in several waters to be sure that it is free from all sand.
2. When young and tender put in a stewpan, allow to heat gradually, and boil twenty-five minutes, or until tender, in its own juices.
3. Drain thoroughly, chop finely, reheat, and season with butter, salt, and pepper.
4. Mound on a serving dish and garnish with slices of hard-boiled eggs and toast points.
