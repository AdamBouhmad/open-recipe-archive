## Ingredients

- selected clams
- one cup bread flour
- one-half teaspoon salt
- cayenne
- two-thirds cup milk
- two eggs
- cream toast
- salt
- celery salt
- pepper
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Clean and dry selected clams.
2. Mix and sift one cup bread flour, one-half teaspoon salt, and a few grains cayenne.
3. Add gradually two-thirds cup milk, and two eggs well beaten.
4. Dip clams in batter, fry in deep fat, and drain on brown paper.
5. Serve on small slices of cream toast, seasoned with salt, celery salt, pepper, and cayenne.
