## Ingredients

- two pounds of cherries
- one pound and a half of sugar
- half a pint of fair water

## Directions

1. Melt some sugar in it
2. When it is melted, put in your other sugar and your cherries
3. Then boil them softly, till all the sugar be melted
4. Then boil them fast, and skim them
5. Take them off two or three times and shake them, and put them on again
6. And let them boil fast
7. And when they are of a good colour, and the sirrup will stand, they are boiled enough
