## Ingredients

- 3 cups Arlington meal
- 1 cup flour
- 3½ teaspoons soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup molasses (scant)
- 2½ cups sour milk

## Directions

1. Mix same as Boston Brown Bread and steam four hours.
2. This bread may often be eaten when bread containing corn meal could not be digested.
