## Ingredients

- one quart of rose-water
- an ounce and a half of gum tragacanth
- a drachm of oil of roses

## Directions

1. To one quart of rose-water add an ounce and a half of gum tragacanth
2. let it stand forty-eight hours, frequently straining it
3. then strain through a coarse linen cloth
4. let it stand two days
5. again strain
6. add to it a drachm of oil of roses
