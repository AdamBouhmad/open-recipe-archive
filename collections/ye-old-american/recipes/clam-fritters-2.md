## Ingredients

- fifty small or twenty-five large sand clams
- wheat flour
- three eggs
- sweet milk
- clam liquor
- lard or beef fat

## Directions

1. Take fifty small or twenty-five large sand clams from their shells
2. if large, cut each in two, lay them on a thickly-folded napkin
3. put a pint bowl of wheat flour into a basin, add to it three well-beaten eggs, half a pint of sweet milk and nearly as much of their own liquor
4. beat the batter until it is smooth and perfectly free from lumps, then stir in the clams
5. Put plenty of lard or beef fat into a thick-bottomed frying pan, let it become boiling hot
6. put in the batter by the spoonful
7. let them fry gently
8. when one side is a delicate brown turn the other
