## Ingredients

- three cups of white sugar
- one cup of butter
- one cup of sweet milk
- six eggs
- one teaspoonful of vanilla or lemon extract
- two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- four cups and a half of flour
- one cup and a half of citron

## Directions

1. Cream together three cups of white sugar and one cup of butter.
2. Add one cup of sweet milk and six eggs with whites and yolks beaten separately.
3. Add one teaspoonful of vanilla or lemon extract.
4. Sift two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder with four cups and a half of flour and add.
5. Add one cup and a half of citron, sliced thin and dredged with flour.
6. Divide into two cakes and bake in tins lined with buttered letter paper.
