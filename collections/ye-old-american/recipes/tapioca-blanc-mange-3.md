## Ingredients

- Half a pound of tapioca
- One pint of milk
- A pinch of salt
- Sugar to taste
- Strawberry or raspberry jam
- A little cream
- Lemon or vanilla for flavoring

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca an hour in one pint of milk
2. Boil till tender
3. Add a pinch of salt
4. Sweeten to taste
5. Put into a mold
6. When cold turn it out
7. Serve with strawberry or raspberry jam around it and a little cream
8. Flavor with lemon or vanilla
