## Ingredients

- 3 cups sugar
- 1½ cups butter
- 1 cup milk
- 4½ cups prepared flour
- 5 eggs
- 1½ cup brown sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 teaspoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls cold water

## Directions

1. Make caramel filling with 1½ cup brown sugar, ½ cup milk, 1 cup molasses, 1 teaspoonful butter, 1 tablespoonful flour, and 2 tablespoonfuls cold water.
