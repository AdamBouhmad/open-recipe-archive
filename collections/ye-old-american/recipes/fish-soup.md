## Ingredients

- A large, fine fish
- Water (one quart for each pound of fish)
- An onion
- A bunch of sweet herbs
- Butter
- Salt and pepper
- Worcestershire sauce (optional)
- Fried bread
- Thin slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the fish thoroughly
2. Put it over the fire with a sufficient quantity of water, allowing one quart of water for each pound of fish
3. Add an onion cut fine and a bunch of sweet herbs
4. When the fish is cooked and is quite tasteless, strain all through a colander
5. Return to the fire and add some butter, salt and pepper to taste
6. Add a small tablespoonful of Worcestershire sauce if liked
7. Serve with small squares of fried bread and thin slices of lemon
