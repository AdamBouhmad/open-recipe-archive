## Ingredients

- three cupfuls of soft sugar
- two cupfuls of flour
- half a cupful of butter
- one teacupful of loppered milk
- five eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of rose-water
- three-quarters of a pound of almonds, blanched and chopped
- one teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Stir in the beaten yolks the milk, flour, rose-water, almonds
3. Add the beaten whites very lightly and quickly
4. Stir in one teaspoonful of soda dissolved in boiling water
5. Drop in rings on buttered paper and bake at once
