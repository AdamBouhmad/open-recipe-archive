## Ingredients

- 1 and one-third cups nut meat (castaneas, English walnuts, or almonds) cut in quarters
- 1 pound sugar

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a buttered shallow pan with nut meat (castaneas, English walnuts, or almonds) cut in quarters.
2. Pour over sugar, melted as for Peanut Nougat.
3. Mark in bars.
