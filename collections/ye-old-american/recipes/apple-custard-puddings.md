## Ingredients

- a quart of pared and quartered apples
- half a cupful of water
- half a cupful of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- grated rind and juice of a lemon
- two cupfuls of grated bread crumbs
- two tablespoonfuls of flour
- two well-beaten eggs
- hard sweet sauce

## Directions

1. Put a quart of pared and quartered apples into a stewpan, with half a cupful of water and cook them until they are soft
2. Remove from the fire and add half a cupful of sugar, two tablespoonfuls of butter and the grated rind and the juice of a lemon
3. Have ready mixed two cupfuls of grated bread crumbs and two tablespoonfuls of flour; add this also to the apple mixture
4. Stir in two well-beaten eggs
5. Turn all into a well-buttered pudding-dish and bake forty-five minutes in a moderate oven
6. Serve with sugar and cream or hard sweet sauce
