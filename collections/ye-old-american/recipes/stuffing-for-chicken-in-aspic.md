## Ingredients

- breast and meat from second joints of an uncooked chicken or one pound of uncooked lean veal
- one-half cup cracker crumbs
- hot stock
- salt
- pepper
- celery salt
- cayenne
- lemon juice
- one egg

## Directions

1. Chop finely breast and meat from second joints of an uncooked chicken, or one pound of uncooked lean veal.
2. Add one-half cup cracker crumbs, hot stock to moisten, salt, pepper, celery salt, cayenne, lemon juice, and one egg slightly beaten.
3. In stuffing boned chicken, stuff body, legs, and wings, being careful that too much stuffing is not used, as an allowance must be made for the swelling of cracker crumbs.
