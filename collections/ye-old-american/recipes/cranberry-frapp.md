## Ingredients

- 1 quart cranberries
- 2 cups water
- 2 cups sugar
- Juice 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Cook cranberries and water eight minutes; then force through a sieve.
2. Add sugar and lemon juice, and freeze to a mush, using equal parts of ice and salt.
