## Ingredients

- toasted bread
- fresh mushrooms
- butter
- cream
- poached eggs
- white sauce
- grated Parmesan cheese
- canned pimentoes

## Directions

1. Cover circular pieces of toasted bread with sliced fresh mushrooms sautéd in butter and moistened with cream.
2. Poach eggs and arrange on mushrooms.
3. Pour over all white sauce to which grated Parmesan cheese has been added.
4. Sprinkle with grated cheese and put in oven to brown.
5. Garnish with canned pimentoes cut in fancy shapes.
