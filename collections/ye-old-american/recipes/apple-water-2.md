## Ingredients

- 1 large juicy pippin
- 3 cups of cold water (1 quart if the apple is very large)
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Pare and quarter the apple, but do not core it
2. Put it on the fire in a tin or porcelain saucepan with the water
3. Boil, closely covered, until the apple stews to pieces
4. Strain the liquor at once, pressing the apple hard in the cloth
5. Strain this again through a finer bag
6. Set away to cool
7. Sweeten with white sugar
8. Ice for drinking
