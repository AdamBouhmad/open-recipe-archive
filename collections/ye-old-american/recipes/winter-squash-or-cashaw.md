## Ingredients

- winter squash
- water
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Pare it, take out the seeds, cut it in pieces.
2. Stew it slowly till quite soft, in a very little water.
3. Afterwards drain, squeeze, and press it well.
4. Mash it with a very little butter, pepper and salt.
