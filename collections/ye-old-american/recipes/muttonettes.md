## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- bread crumbs
- beaten egg
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- sage
- summer savory
- water
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut from a leg of mutton slices about half an inch thick
2. On each slice lay a spoonful of stuffing made with bread crumbs, beaten egg, butter, salt, pepper, sage and summer savory
3. Roll up the slices, pinning with little skewers or small wooden toothpicks to keep the dressing in
4. Put a little butter and water in a baking-pan with the muttonettes
5. Cook in hot oven three-quarters of an hour
6. Baste often, and when done thicken the gravy
7. Pour over the meat, garnish with parsley, and serve on hot platter
