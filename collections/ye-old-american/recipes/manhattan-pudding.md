## Ingredients

- 1½ cups orange juice
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- Sugar
- 1 pint heavy cream
- ½ cup powdered sugar
- ½ tablespoon vanilla
- ⅔ cup chopped walnut meats

## Directions

1. Mix fruit juices and sweeten to taste
2. Turn mixture in brick mould
3. Whip cream, and add sugar, vanilla, and nut meats
4. Pour over the first mixture to overflow mould
5. Cover with buttered paper, fit on cover
6. Pack in salt and ice
7. Let stand three hours
