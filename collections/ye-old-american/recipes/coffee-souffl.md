## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups coffee infusion
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2/3 cup sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 tablespoon granulated gelatine

## Directions

1. Mix coffee infusion, milk, one-half of the sugar and gelatine
2. Heat in double boiler
3. Add remaining sugar, salt, and yolks of eggs slightly beaten
4. Cook until mixture thickens
5. Remove from range
6. Add whites of eggs beaten until stiff and vanilla
7. Mould, chill, and serve with cream
