## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Toast some slices of bread very nicely, without allowing them to burn or blacken.
2. Put them into a pitcher, and fill it up with boiling water.
3. Let it stand till it is quite cold; then strain it, and put it into a decanter.
4. Alternatively, put the toasted bread into a mug and pour cold water on it.
5. Cover it closely, and let it infuse for at least an hour.
6. Drink it cold.
