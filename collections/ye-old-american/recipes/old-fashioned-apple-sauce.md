## Ingredients

- a dozen medium-sized apples
- a heaping coffeecupful of sugar
- one coffeecupful of water

## Directions

1. Pare and chop a dozen medium-sized apples
2. Put them in a deep pudding-dish
3. Sprinkle over them a heaping coffeecupful of sugar and one of water
4. Place them in the oven and bake slowly two hours or more, or until they are a deep red brown
