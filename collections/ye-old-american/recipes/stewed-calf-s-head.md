## Ingredients

- calf's-head
- flour
- salt
- parsley
- sage
- pepper
- powdered cloves
- butter
- beaten egg
- cracker-crumbs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Wash the head in several waters, and taking out the brains, set them by in a cool place.
2. Tie the head in a floured cloth and boil it two hours in hot water slightly salted.
3. Wash the brains carefully, picking out all the bits of skin and membrane, cleansing them over and over until they are perfectly white.
4. Stew in just enough water to cover them.
5. Take them out, mash smooth with the back of a wooden spoon, and add gradually a small teacupful of the water in which the head is boiled.
6. Season with chopped parsley, a pinch of sage, pepper, salt, and powdered cloves, with a great spoonful of butter.
7. Set it over the fire to simmer in a saucepan until you are ready.
8. When the head is tender, take it up and drain very dry.
9. Score the top, and rub it well over with melted butter; dredge with flour and set in the oven to brown.
10. Or, you can use beaten egg and cracker-crumbs in place of the butter and flour.
11. When you serve the head, pour the gravy over it.
