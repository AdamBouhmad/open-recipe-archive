## Ingredients

- 2½ cups hot liquid (water, or milk and water)
- ⅓ cup molasses
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- ¼ yeast cake dissolved in ¼ cup lukewarm water
- 3 cups flour
- 3 cups Graham flour

## Directions

1. Prepare and bake as Entire Wheat Bread
2. The bran remaining in sieve after sifting Graham flour should be discarded
