## Ingredients

- gill of claret
- water
- grated bread
- three heads of shallots
- whole pepper
- mace
- grated nutmeg
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a gill of claret with as much water
2. Add some grated bread, three heads of shallots, a little whole pepper, mace, grated nutmeg, and salt
3. Let them stew over the fire
4. Beat it up with butter
5. Put it under the wild fowl
