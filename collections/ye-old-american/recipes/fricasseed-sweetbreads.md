## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- rich gravy
- egg
- cream
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the uncooked sweetbreads into thin slices.
2. Let them simmer in a rich gravy for three-quarters of an hour.
3. Add a well-beaten egg, two tablespoonfuls of cream and a tablespoonful of chopped parsley.
4. Stir all together for a few minutes and serve immediately.
