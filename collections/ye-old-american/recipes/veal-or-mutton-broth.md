## Ingredients

- two pounds scrag-end of mutton
- two quarts of cold water
- one ounce of pearl barley or rice
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- one turnip
- one carrot
- one onion

## Directions

1. Put the scrag-end of mutton in a saucepan with two quarts of cold water and an ounce of pearl barley or rice
2. When it is coming to a boil, skim it well, then add half a teaspoonful of salt
3. Let it boil until half reduced, then strain it and take off all the fat and it is ready for use
4. If vegetables are liked in this broth, take one turnip, one carrot and one onion, cut them in shreds and boil them in the broth half an hour
