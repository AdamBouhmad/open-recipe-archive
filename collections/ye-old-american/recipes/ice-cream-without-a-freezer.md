## Ingredients

- the yolks of eight eggs
- four cupfuls of sugar
- one quart of rich milk
- the whites of eight eggs
- three pints of rich sweet cream
- five teaspoonfuls of vanilla or other flavoring
- pounded ice and rock salt

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of eight eggs very light, and add thereto four cupfuls of sugar, and stir well
2. Add to this, little by little, one quart of rich milk that has been heated almost to boiling, beating all the while
3. Put in the whites of eight eggs beaten to a stiff froth
4. Boil the mixture in a pail set inside another containing hot water for about fifteen minutes or until it is as thick as a boiled custard, stirring steadily meanwhile
5. Pour into a bowl to cool
6. When quite cold, beat into it three pints of rich sweet cream and five teaspoonfuls of vanilla, or such other flavoring as you prefer
7. Put it into a pail having a close-fitting cover and pack in pounded ice and rock salt, about three-fourths ice and one-forth salt
8. Before putting the ice on top of the cover, beat the custard for five minutes steady
9. Put on the cover and put the ice and salt over it, and cover the whole with a thick mat, blanket or carpet and let it stand for an hour
10. Carefully uncover and scrape from the bottom and sides of the pail the thick coating of frozen custard
11. Beat again very hard, until the custard is a smooth, half-congealed paste
12. Put on the cover, ice, salt and blanket, and leave it for five or six hours, replenishing the ice and salt if necessary
