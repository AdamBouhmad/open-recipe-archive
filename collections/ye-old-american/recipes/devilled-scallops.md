## Ingredients

- 1 quart scallops
- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅓ teaspoon made mustard
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- ⅔ cup buttered cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Clean scallops, drain, and heat to the boiling-point
2. Drain again, and reserve liquor
3. Cream the butter, add mustard, salt, cayenne, two-thirds cup reserved liquor, and scallops chopped
4. Let stand one-half hour
5. Put in a baking-dish, cover with crumbs, and bake twenty minutes
