## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice a quarter of an inch thick.
2. Pack in a pudding-dish, seasoning each layer with salt, pepper, butter, and a very little white sugar.
3. Bake covered half an hour.
4. Remove the lid and brown for fifteen minutes.
