## Ingredients

- 1 cup corn meal
- 1 cup white flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- ¼ cup sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅞ cup milk
- 2 eggs
- ¼ cup melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients.
2. Add milk, gradually, eggs well beaten, and butter.
3. Bake in a buttered, shallow pan, in a hot oven.
