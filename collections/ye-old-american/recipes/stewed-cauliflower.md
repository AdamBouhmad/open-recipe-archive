## Ingredients

- cauliflowers
- salt
- milk
- rice or wheat flour
- melted butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Use for this dish the smaller and more indifferent cauliflowers
2. Cut them into small clusters and lay in cold salt and water half an hour
3. Stew fifteen minutes in boiling water
4. Turn most of this water off, leaving but half a teacupful in the saucepan
5. Add to this a half-cupful of milk thickened with a very little rice or wheat flour, and two tablespoonfuls of melted butter, pepper, and salt
6. Shake the saucepan over the fire gently until it boils
7. Take out the cauliflowers with a perforated skimmer, lay in order upon a dish, and pour the sauce over them
