## Ingredients

- three or four hard crackers that are baked quite brown
- one quart of water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Break in pieces three or four hard crackers that are baked quite brown
2. Let them boil fifteen minutes in one quart of water
3. Remove from the fire, let them stand three or four minutes
4. Strain off the liquor through a fine wire sieve, and season it with sugar
