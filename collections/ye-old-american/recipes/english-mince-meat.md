## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. raisins, seeded
- 5 lbs. suet
- 5 lbs. apples
- 4 lbs. citron
- 1½ lbs. blanched almonds
- 5 lbs. currants
- 5 lbs. light brown sugar
- ½ teaspoon mace
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- 2½ cups brandy

## Directions

1. Cook raisins, suet, apples, citron, currants, and sugar slowly for one and one-half hours; then add almonds, spices, and brandy.
