## Ingredients

- two large flounders
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- one-fourth cup white wine
- parsley
- one tablespoon finely chopped shallot
- one tablespoon butter
- two tablespoons flour
- White Stock
- two tablespoons butter
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Skin and bone two large flounders, and cut into eight fillets
2. Put into a buttered pan
3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and lemon juice
4. Add one-fourth cup white wine
5. Cover and cook fifteen minutes
6. Remove to serving dish
7. For Bercy Sauce: Fry one tablespoon finely chopped shallot in one tablespoon butter five minutes
8. Add two tablespoons flour
9. Pour on gradually the liquor left in pan with enough White Stock to make one cup
10. Add two tablespoons butter, and salt and cayenne to taste
11. Pour sauce over fish
12. Sprinkle with finely chopped parsley
