## Ingredients

- macaroni
- half a pound beef
- cold water
- minced onion
- pint tomatoes
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into inch lengths and stew twenty minutes, or until tender.
2. Cut half a pound of beef into strips and stew half an hour. The water should be cold when the meat is put in.
3. At the end of that time, add a minced onion and a pint of tomatoes peeled and sliced.
4. Boil for an hour and strain through a cullender when you have taken out the meat.
5. The sauce should be well boiled down. Return the liquid to the saucepan, add a good piece of butter, with pepper and salt, and stew until you are ready to dish the macaroni.
6. Drain the macaroni well, sprinkle lightly with salt, and heap upon a chafing-dish or in a root-dish.
7. Pour the tomato-sauce over it; cover and let it stand in a warm place ten minutes before sending to table.
8. Send around grated cheese with it.
