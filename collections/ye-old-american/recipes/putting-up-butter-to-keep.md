## Ingredients

- pure common salt
- white sugar
- saltpetre
- butter
- brine

## Directions

1. Take of the best pure common salt two quarts, one ounce of white sugar and one of saltpetre; pulverize them together completely
2. Work the butter well, then thoroughly work in an ounce of this mixture to every pound of butter
3. Make the butter into half-pound rolls
4. Put into the following brine--to three gallons of brine strong enough to bear an egg, add a quarter of a pound of white sugar
