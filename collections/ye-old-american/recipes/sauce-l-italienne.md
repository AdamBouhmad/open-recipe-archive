## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons onion, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons carrot, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons lean raw ham, finely chopped
- 12 peppercorns
- 2 cloves
- Sprig marjoram
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2½ tablespoons flour
- 1 cup Brown Stock
- 1¼ cups white wine
- ½ tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cook first six ingredients with butter five minutes, add flour, and stir until well browned.
2. Add gradually stock and wine.
3. Strain, reheat, and after pouring around fish sprinkle with parsley.
