## Ingredients

- water-melon rind
- loaf sugar
- green vine leaves
- alum
- water
- white of egg
- ginger tea
- spring or pump water
- lemon-juice
- brandy

## Directions

1. Having pared off the green skin, cut the rind of a water-melon into pieces of any shape you please (stars, diamonds, circles, crescents or leaves)
2. Weigh the pieces, and allow to each pound a pound and a half of loaf sugar
3. Set the sugar aside, and put the pieces of melon-rind into a preserving kettle, the bottom and sides of which you have lined with green vine leaves
4. Put a layer of vine leaves between each layer of melon-rind, and cover the top with leaves
5. Disperse among the pieces some very small bits of alum, each about the bigness of a grain of corn, allowing one bit to every pound of the melon-rind
6. Pour in just water enough to cover the whole, and place a thick double cloth over the top of the kettle
7. Let it simmer (but not boil) for two hours
8. Take out the pieces of melon-rind and spread them on dishes to cool
9. If you find that they taste of the alum, simmer them in very weak ginger tea for about three hours
10. Melt the sugar in clear spring or pump water, allowing a pint of water to a pound and a half of sugar
11. Mix in with it some white of egg beaten to a stiff froth (the white of one egg will be enough for two pounds of sugar)
12. Boil and skim it; and when the scum ceases to rise, put in the melon-rind, and let it simmer an hour
13. Take it out and spread it to cool on dishes, return it to the syrup, and simmer it another hour
14. Put it into a tureen
15. Boil up the syrup again, and pour it over the melon-rind
16. Cover it, and let it stand all night
17. Next morning give the syrup another boil; adding to it some lemon-juice, allowing the juice of one lemon to a quart of the syrup
18. When you find it so thick as to hang in a drop on the point of the spoon, it is sufficiently done
19. Put the rind into glass jars, pour in the syrup, and secure the sweetmeats closely from the air with paper dipped in brandy, and a leather outer cover
