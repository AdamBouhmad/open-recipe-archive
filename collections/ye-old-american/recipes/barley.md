## Ingredients

- 2 cups boiling water
- 2 tablespoonfuls pearl barley, picked over and washed
- A pinch of salt
- 2 teaspoonfuls white sugar, not heaping

## Directions

1. Soak the barley half an hour in a very little lukewarm water
2. Stir, without draining, into the boiling water, salted very slightly
3. Simmer one hour, stirring often
4. Strain before sweetening
