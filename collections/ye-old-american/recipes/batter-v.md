## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅔ cup milk or water
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Whites 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon melted butter or olive oil

## Directions

1. Mix salt and flour, add milk gradually, yolks of eggs beaten until thick, butter, and whites of eggs beaten until stiff.
