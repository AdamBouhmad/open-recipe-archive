## Ingredients

- potatoes
- deep fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and pare potatoes
2. Slice, using a vegetable slicer which comes for this purpose, and let stand in a bowl of cold water two hours
3. Drain, and dry between towels
4. Fry in deep fat, drain on brown paper, and sprinkle with salt
