## Ingredients

- ½ lb. Jordan almonds
- ¼ cup boiling water
- ½ cup sugar
- Whites 4 eggs
- 1¼ cups powdered sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Blanch almonds.
2. Finely shred one-half of them and dry slowly in oven.
3. Put water and sugar in a saucepan and as soon as boiling-point is reached, add remaining almonds.
4. Cook until the syrup is of a golden brown color.
5. Turn into a pan and cool.
6. Finely pound in mortar.
7. Beat whites of eggs until stiff.
8. Add gradually sugar, then vanilla, almonds, and salt.
9. Shape and sprinkle with shredded almonds.
10. Sift sugar over them.
11. Bake in a slow oven twenty-five minutes.
