## Ingredients

- Goosberries, Damsons, or Plumbs
- clean and dry glass bottles
- new corks
- water

## Directions

1. Gather them when dry, full grown, and not ripe
2. Pick them one by one, put them into glass bottles that are very clean and dry
3. Cork them close with new corks
4. Put a kettle of water on the fire, and put in the bottles with care
5. Wet not the corks, but let the water come up to the necks
6. Make a gentle fire till they are a little codled and turn white
7. Do not take them up till cold
8. Then pitch the corks all over, or wax them close and thick
9. Then set them in a cool dry cellar
