## Ingredients

- Cucumber
- Lettuce leaves
- Parisian French Dressing

## Directions

1. Remove thick slices from both ends of a cucumber, cut off a thick paring, and with a sharp-pointed knife cut five parallel grooves lengthwise of cucumber at equal distances.
2. Then cut in thin parallel slices crosswise, keeping cucumber in its original shape.
3. Arrange on lettuce leaves.
4. Pour over Parisian French Dressing.
5. Serve with fish course.
