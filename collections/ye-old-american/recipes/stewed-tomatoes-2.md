## Ingredients

- a dozen sound ripe tomatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over a dozen sound ripe tomatoes; let them remain for a few moments.
2. Peel off the skins, slice them and put them over the fire in a well-lined tin or granite-ware saucepan.
3. Stew them about twenty minutes, then add a tablespoonful of butter, salt and pepper to taste.
4. Let them stew fifteen minutes longer and serve hot.
