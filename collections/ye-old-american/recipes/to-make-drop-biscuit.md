## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- twelve ounces of flour
- one pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Beat eight eggs very light
2. Add to them twelve ounces of flour, and one pound of sugar
3. When perfectly light, drop them on tin sheets
4. Bake them in a quick oven
