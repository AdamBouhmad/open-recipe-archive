## Ingredients

- one quart of blueberries
- soda crackers
- powdered sugar
- cold sweet cream

## Directions

1. Pick over carefully one quart of blueberries and keep them on ice until wanted
2. Put into each bowl for each guest two soda crackers, broken in not too small pieces
3. Add a few tablespoonfuls of berries, a teaspoonful of powdered sugar
4. Fill the bowl with the richest of cold sweet cream
