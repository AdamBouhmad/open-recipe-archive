## Ingredients

- sweet-breads
- linen cloth
- fat salt pork
- butter
- grease

## Directions

1. Wash very carefully, and dry with a linen cloth.
2. Lard with narrow strips of fat salt pork, set closely together, using a larding-needle.
3. Lay the sweet-breads in a clean, hot frying-pan, which has been well buttered or greased.
4. Cook to a fine brown, turning frequently until the pork is crisp.
