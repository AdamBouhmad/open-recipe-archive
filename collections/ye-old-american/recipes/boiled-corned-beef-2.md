## Ingredients

- corned beef
- water
- stout tape or twine
- drawn butter
- mashed turnips

## Directions

1. If your piece is a round, skewer it well into shape, and tie it up with stout tape or twine when you have washed it in three or four waters and removed all the salt from the outside
2. Put into a pot, and cover with cold water
3. Allow, in boiling, about twenty minutes to a pound
4. Turn the meat three times while cooking
5. When done, drain very dry
6. Serve with drawn butter in a sauce-boat
7. Send around mashed turnips with the meat, boiled in a separate pot
