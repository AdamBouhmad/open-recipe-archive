## Ingredients

- 1 lb. butter
- 1 lb. of suet, freed from strings and chopped fine
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 2½ lbs. of flour
- 2 lbs. of raisins, seeded, chopped, and dredged with flour
- 2 lbs. of currants, picked over carefully after they are washed
- ¼ lb. of citron, shred fine
- 12 eggs, whites and yolks beaten separately
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 cup of brandy
- ½ oz. of cloves
- ½ oz. of mace
- 2 grated nutmegs

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar; beat in the yolks when you have whipped them smooth and light; next put in the milk; then the flour, alternately with the beaten whites; then the brandy and spice; lastly the fruit, well dredged with flour.
2. Mix all thoroughly; wring out your pudding-cloth in hot water; flour well inside, pour in the mixture, and boil five hours.
