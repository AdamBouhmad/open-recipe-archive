## Ingredients

- one large teacupful of rice
- a little water
- pared, cored and quartered apples, or any fruit

## Directions

1. Cook rice partially with a little water
2. Line an earthen basin with part of it
3. Fill nearly full with pared, cored and quartered apples, or any fruit you choose
4. Cover with the balance of your rice
5. Tie a cloth tightly over the top and steam one hour
6. Serve with sweet sauce
