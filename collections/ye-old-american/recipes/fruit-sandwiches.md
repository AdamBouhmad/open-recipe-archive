## Ingredients

- figs
- water
- lemon juice
- thin slices of buttered bread
- finely chopped peanuts
- pieces of buttered bread

## Directions

1. Remove stems and finely chop figs.
2. Add a small quantity of water, cook in double boiler until a paste is formed, then add a few drops of lemon juice.
3. Cool mixture, and spread on thin slices of buttered bread.
4. Sprinkle with finely chopped peanuts and cover with pieces of buttered bread.
