## Ingredients

- quails
- puff-paste
- salt pork or ham
- hard-boiled eggs
- pepper
- parsley
- lemon juice
- butter
- flour
- gravy from parboiling

## Directions

1. Clean, truss, and stuff the birds.
2. Loosen the joints with a penknife, but do not separate them.
3. Parboil them for ten minutes, while you prepare a puff-paste.
4. Line a deep dish with this; put in the bottom some shreds of salt pork or ham; next, a layer of hard-boiled eggs, buttered and peppered; then the birds, sprinkled with pepper and minced parsley.
5. Squeeze some lemon-juice upon them, and lay upon the breasts pieces of butter rolled in flour.
6. Cover with slices of egg, then with shred ham; pour in some of the gravy in which the quails were parboiled, and put on the lid, leaving a hole in the middle.
7. Bake over an hour.
