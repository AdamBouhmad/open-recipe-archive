## Ingredients

- six anchovies
- a pint of strong vinegar
- three table-spoonfuls of India soy
- three table-spoonfuls of mushroom catchup
- two heads of garlic bruised small
- a quarter of an ounce of cayenne
- cochineal powder

## Directions

1. Dissolve six anchovies in a pint of strong vinegar and then add to them three table-spoonfuls of India soy, and three table-spoonfuls of mushroom catchup, two heads of garlic bruised small, and a quarter of an ounce of cayenne.
2. Add sufficient cochineal powder to colour the mixture red.
3. Let all these ingredients infuse in the vinegar for a fortnight, shaking it every day, and then strain and bottle it for use.
4. Let the bottles be small, and cover the corks with leather.
