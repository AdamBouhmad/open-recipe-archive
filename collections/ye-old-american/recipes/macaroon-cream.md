## Ingredients

- 1/4 box gelatine or 1 tablespoon granulated gelatine
- 1/4 cup cold water
- 2 cups scalded milk
- Yolks of 3 eggs
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 2/3 cup pounded macaroons
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- Whites of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cold water
2. Make custard of milk, yolks of eggs, sugar, and salt
3. Add gelatine and strain into pan set in ice-water
4. Add macaroons and flavoring, stirring until it begins to thicken
5. Add whites of eggs beaten stiff
6. Mould, chill, and serve garnished with macaroons
