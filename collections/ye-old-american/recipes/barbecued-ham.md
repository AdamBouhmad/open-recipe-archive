## Ingredients

- ham slices
- scalding water
- pepper
- made mustard
- vinegar
- wine
- white sugar

## Directions

1. If your ham is raw, soak as directed for broiled ham.
2. Lay the slices flat in a frying-pan; pepper each and lay upon it a quarter of a teaspoonful of made mustard.
3. Pour about them some vinegar, allowing half a teaspoonful to each slice.
4. Fry quickly and turn often.
5. When done to a fine brown, transfer to a hot dish.
6. Add to the gravy in the pan half a glass of wine and a very small teaspoonful of white sugar.
7. Boil up and pour over the meat.
