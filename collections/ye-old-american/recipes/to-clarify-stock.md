## Ingredients

- Stock
- White of one egg per quart of stock
- Water

## Directions

1. Place the stock in a clean saucepan, set it over a brisk fire
2. When boiling, beat the whites of the eggs up well in a little water
3. Add a little hot stock and beat to a froth
4. Pour gradually into the pot
5. Beat the whole hard and long
6. Allow it to boil up once, and immediately remove and strain through a thin flannel cloth
