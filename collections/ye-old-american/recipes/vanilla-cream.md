## Ingredients

- a Vanilla bean
- a quart of rich milk
- eight eggs

## Directions

1. Boil a Vanilla bean in a quart of rich milk, until it has imparted the flavour sufficiently
2. Take it out, and mix with the milk, eight eggs, yelks and whites beaten well
3. Let it boil a little longer
4. Make it very sweet, for much of the sugar is lost in the operation of freezing
