## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- bread-crumbs
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in slices a quarter of an inch thick.
2. Pack in a pudding-dish in alternate layers, with a force-meat made of bread-crumbs, butter, salt, pepper, and a little white sugar.
3. Spread thickly upon each stratum of tomatoes.
4. When the dish is nearly full, put tomatoes uppermost, a good bit of butter upon each slice.
5. Dust with pepper and a little sugar.
6. Strew with dry bread-crumbs.
7. Bake covered half an hour.
8. Remove the lid and bake brown.
