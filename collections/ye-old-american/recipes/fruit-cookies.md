## Ingredients

- One cupful and a half of sugar
- one cupful of butter
- one-half cup of sweet milk
- one egg
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- a teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
- three tablespoonfuls of English currants or chopped raisins

## Directions

1. Mix soft and roll out, using just enough flour to stiffen sufficiently
2. Cut out with a large cutter
3. wet the tops with milk and sprinkle sugar over them
4. Bake on buttered tins in a quick oven
