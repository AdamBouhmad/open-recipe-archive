## Ingredients

- raspberries
- sugar
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Let the raspberries be freshly gathered and quite ripe
2. Pick from the stalks
3. Put them into a large jar after breaking the fruit a little with a wooden spoon
4. Place this jar, covered, in a saucepan of boiling water
5. When the juice is well drawn, which will be in from three-quarters to one hour, strain the fruit through a fine hair-sieve or cloth
6. Measure the juice, and to every pint allow one pound of white sugar
7. Put the juice and sugar into a preserving pan
8. Place it over the fire and boil gently until the jelly thickens, when a little is poured on a plate
9. Carefully remove all the scum as it rises
10. Pour the jelly into small pots
11. Cover down and keep in a dry place
