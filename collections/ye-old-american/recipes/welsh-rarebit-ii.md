## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- ½ lb. soft mild cheese, cut in small pieces
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon mustard
- Few grains cayenne
- ⅓ to ¼ cup ale or lager beer
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Put butter in chafing-dish, and when melted, add cheese and seasonings.
2. As cheese melts, add ale gradually, while stirring constantly.
3. Add egg slightly beaten.
4. Serve same as Welsh Rarebit I.
