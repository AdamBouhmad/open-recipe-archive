## Ingredients

- about a pint of stale bread
- a quart of boiling milk
- a piece of butter the size of a small egg
- a teaspoonful of cinnamon
- one teaspoonful of nutmeg grated
- half a cupful of sugar
- one-quarter of a teaspoonful of soda
- four eggs
- hard sauce

## Directions

1. Break up about a pint of stale bread after cutting off the crust, pour over it a quart of boiling milk
2. Add to this a piece of butter the size of a small egg
3. Cover the dish tight and let it stand until cool
4. With a spoon mash it until fine, adding a teaspoonful of cinnamon and one of nutmeg grated, half a cupful of sugar and one-quarter of a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little hot water
5. Beat up four eggs very light and add last
6. Turn all into a well-buttered pudding-dish and bake three-quarters of an hour
7. Serve it warm with hard sauce
