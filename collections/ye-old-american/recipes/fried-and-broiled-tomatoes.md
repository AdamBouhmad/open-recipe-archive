## Ingredients

- firm, large, ripe tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut firm, large, ripe tomatoes into thick slices, rather more than a quarter of an inch thick.
2. Season with salt and pepper, dredge well with flour, or roll in egg and crumbs.
3. Fry them brown on both sides evenly, in hot butter and lard mixed.
4. Alternatively, prepare them the same as for frying, broiling on a well-greased gridiron, seasoning afterward the same as beefsteak.
