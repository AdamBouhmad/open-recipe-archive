## Ingredients

- fine large sweet potatoes
- fresh butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Select fine large sweet potatoes, all nearly the same size.
2. Boil them well and then peel off the skins.
3. Then lay the potatoes in a large baking-dish; put some pieces of fresh butter among them, and sprinkle them very freely with powdered sugar.
4. Bake them slowly, till the butter and sugar form a crust.
5. They should be eaten after the meat.
