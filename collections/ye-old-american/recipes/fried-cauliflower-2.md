## Ingredients

- cauliflower branches
- flour
- eggs
- water
- salt
- hot fat

## Directions

1. Mix flour with two yolks of eggs
2. Add water enough to make a rather thin paste
3. Add salt to taste
4. Beat the two whites until stiff
5. Mix the whites with the yolks, flour and water
6. Dip each branch of the cauliflower into the mixture
7. Fry them in hot fat
8. When done, take them off with a skimmer and turn into a colander
9. Dust salt all over and serve warm
