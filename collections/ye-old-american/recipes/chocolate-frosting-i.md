## Ingredients

- 1½ squares chocolate
- ⅓ cup scalded cream
- Few grains salt
- Yolk 1 egg
- ½ teaspoon melted butter
- Confectioners' sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate over hot water, add cream gradually, salt, yolk of egg, and butter.
2. Stir in confectioners' sugar until of right consistency to spread.
3. Add flavoring.
