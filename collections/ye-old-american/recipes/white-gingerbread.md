## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- three quarters of a pound of butter
- a pound of common white sugar powdered
- three table-spoonfuls of the best white ginger
- four eggs
- a small tea-spoonful of pearl-ash
- a wine glass of sour milk

## Directions

1. Sift two pounds of flour into a deep pan, and rub into it three quarters of a pound of butter
2. Mix in a pound of common white sugar powdered and three table-spoonfuls of the best white ginger
3. Beat four eggs very light, and mix them gradually with the other ingredients in the pan
4. Add a small tea-spoonful of pearl-ash melted in a wine glass of sour milk
5. Stir the whole as hard as possible
6. Flour your paste-board and lay the lump of dough upon it
7. Roll it out into a sheet an inch thick, adding more flour if necessary
8. Butter a large shallow square pan and lay the dough into it
9. Bake it in a moderate oven
10. When cold, cut it into squares, or cut it out into separate cakes with a jagging iron previous to baking
11. Do not lay them too close together in the pan, lest they run into each other
