## Ingredients

- one pint each milk and emptins
- laid into flour, in sponge
- one pound butter melted
- not hot
- milk

## Directions

1. next morning add one pound butter melted, not hot
2. and knead into as much flower as will with another pint of warmed milk
3. be of a sufficient consistence to make soft
4. some melt the butter in the milk
