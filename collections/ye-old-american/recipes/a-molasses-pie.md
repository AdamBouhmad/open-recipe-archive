## Ingredients

- good paste
- molasses
- a table-spoonful, or more, of ground ginger
- a sliced orange or lemon
- a tea-spoonful of powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a good paste, and having rolled it out thick, line a pie-dish with a portion of it
2. Fill up the dish with molasses, into which you have previously stirred a table-spoonful, or more, of ground ginger
3. Lay a sliced orange or lemon in the bottom before you put in the molasses
4. To the ginger you may add a tea-spoonful of powdered cinnamon
5. Cover it with an upper crust of the paste
6. Notch the edges neatly
7. Bake it brown
