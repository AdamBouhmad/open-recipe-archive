## Ingredients

- half a pound of sweet almonds
- coffeecupful of white sugar
- whites of two eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and pound them to a paste
2. Add to them the sugar and the beaten whites of eggs
3. Work the whole together with the back of a spoon
4. Roll the mixture in your hands in balls about the size of a nutmeg
5. Dust sugar over the top
6. Lay them on a sheet of paper at least an inch apart
7. Bake in a cool oven a light brown
