## Ingredients

- carded cotton or split wadding
- pure Florence oil
- fresh rose leaves or leaves of sweet pink, jasmine, wall-flower, tuberose, magnolia blossoms, or other odoriferous flowers

## Directions

1. Steep carded cotton or split wadding in some pure Florence oil
2. Place a layer of this cotton in the bottom of a deep china dish or earthen pipkin
3. Cover it with a thick layer of fresh rose leaves or other odoriferous flowers
4. Spread over the flower-leaves another layer of cotton that has been steeped in oil
5. Alternate layers of flowers and oil-steeped cotton till the vessel is quite full
6. Cover it closely, and let it stand in the sun for a week
7. Throw away the flower-leaves, carefully press out the oil from the cotton, and put it into a small bottle for use
