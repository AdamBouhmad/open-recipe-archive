## Ingredients

- A batter as for Pancakes
- Nice jam or jelly
- Powdered sugar
- Wine sauce or sweetened cream

## Directions

1. Fry the pancakes.
2. Lay upon a hot plate, spread quickly with nice jam or jelly, and roll up neatly upon the preserves.
3. Sprinkle lightly with powdered sugar, and send around with wine sauce or sweetened cream.
