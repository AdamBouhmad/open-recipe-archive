## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. knuckle of veal
- 2 quarts cold water
- 1 tablespoon salt
- ½ teaspoon peppercorns
- 1 onion
- 2 stalks celery
- Blade of mace

## Directions

1. Wipe meat, remove from bone, and cut in small pieces.
2. Put meat, bone, water, and seasonings in kettle.
3. Heat gradually to boiling-point, skimming frequently.
4. Simmer four or five hours, and strain.
5. If scum has been carefully removed, and soup is strained through double thickness of cheese-cloth, stock will be quite clear.
