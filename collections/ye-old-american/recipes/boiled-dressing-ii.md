## Ingredients

- Yolks 4 eggs
- ½ cup olive oil
- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1½ teaspoons salt
- 3 teaspoons powdered sugar
- 1 pint whipped cream

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs slightly, add gradually one-half of the oil and lemon juice.
2. Cook in double boiler until mixture thickens.
3. Chill, and add gradually remaining oil, salt, and sugar.
4. Just before serving add cream.
