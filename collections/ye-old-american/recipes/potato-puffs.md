## Ingredients

- mashed potatoes
- butter
- egg
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the potatoes as directed for mashed potato.
2. While hot, shape in balls about the size of an egg.
3. Have a tin sheet well buttered, and place the balls on it.
4. As soon as all are done, brush over with beaten egg.
5. Brown in the oven.
6. When done, slip a knife under them and slide them upon a hot platter.
7. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
