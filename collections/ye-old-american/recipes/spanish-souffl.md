## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup stale bread crumbs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 3 eggs
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add crumbs, cook until slightly browned, stirring often
2. Add milk and sugar, cook twenty minutes in double boiler
3. Remove from fire, add unbeaten yolks of eggs
4. Cut and fold in whites of eggs beaten until stiff, and flavor
5. Bake same as Fruit Soufflé
