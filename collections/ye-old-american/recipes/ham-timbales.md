## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- ¼ cup stale bread crumbs
- ⅔ cup milk
- 1 cup chopped cooked ham
- ½ tablespoon chopped parsley
- 2 eggs
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Make and bake same as Chicken Timbales II, using chopped cooked ham in place of chicken.
2. Serve with Béchamel Sauce.
