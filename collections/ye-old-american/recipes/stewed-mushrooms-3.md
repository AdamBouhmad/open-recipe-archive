## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- water
- warm milk
- veal or chicken gravy or drawn butter
- flour
- egg
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Rub them white, stew in water ten minutes; strain partially, and cover with as much warm milk as you have poured off water.
2. Stew five minutes in this; salt and pepper, and add some veal or chicken gravy, or drawn butter.
3. Thicken with a little flour wet in cold milk, and a beaten egg.
