## Ingredients

- 3 cups Graham flour
- 1 cup white flour
- 3 cups milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls lard
- 1 heaping tablespoonful white sugar
- 1 saltspoonful of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 2 teaspoonfuls cream-tartar

## Directions

1. Mix and bake as you do the white soda-biscuit (Mrs. E——'s)
2. They are good cold as well as hot
