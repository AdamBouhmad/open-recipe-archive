## Ingredients

- one-half a package Cox's gelatine
- a cupful of cold water
- one and a half cupfuls of sugar
- one cupful of boiling water
- juice of one lemon
- whites of four eggs
- one pint of milk
- yolks of four eggs
- four tablespoonfuls of sugar
- grated rind of a lemon

## Directions

1. Pour over gelatine a cupful of cold water and add one and a half cupfuls of sugar
2. When soft, add one cupful of boiling water and the juice of one lemon
3. Add the whites of four well-beaten eggs
4. Beat all together until it is light and frothy, or until the gelatine will not settle clear in the bottom of the dish after standing a few minutes
5. Put it on a glass dish
6. Serve with a custard made of one pint of milk, the yolks of four eggs, four tablespoonfuls of sugar and the grated rind of a lemon; boil
