## Ingredients

- beef heart
- water
- dressing
- gravy
- wine
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Wash it carefully and open it sufficiently to remove the ventricles.
2. Soak it in cold water until the blood is discharged.
3. Wipe it dry and stuff it nicely with dressing, as for turkey.
4. Roast it about an hour and a half.
5. Serve it with the gravy, which should be thickened with some of the stuffing and a glass of wine.
6. Serve with currant jelly.
