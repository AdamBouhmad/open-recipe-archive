## Ingredients

- one sound cabbage
- salt
- cold vinegar
- equal quantities of mace, allspice, cinnamon and black pepper
- a cup of sugar to every gallon of vinegar
- a teaspoonful of celery seed to every pint
- a few slices of beetroot

## Directions

1. Cut a sound cabbage into quarters, spread it on a large flat platter or dish and sprinkle thickly with salt
2. set it in a cool place for twenty-four hours
3. drain off the brine, wipe it dry and lay it in the sun two hours
4. cover with cold vinegar for twelve hours
5. Prepare a pickle by seasoning enough vinegar to cover the cabbage with equal quantities of mace, allspice, cinnamon and black pepper, a cup of sugar to every gallon of vinegar, and a teaspoonful of celery seed to every pint
6. Pack the cabbage in a stone jar
7. boil the vinegar and spices five minutes and pour on hot
8. Cover and set away in a cool, dry place
