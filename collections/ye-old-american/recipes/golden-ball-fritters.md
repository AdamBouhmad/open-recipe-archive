## Ingredients

- One pint of water
- A piece of butter as large as an egg
- One tablespoonful of sugar
- One pint of sifted flour
- Six eggs beaten separately

## Directions

1. Put water, butter, and sugar into a stewpan
2. When it boils stir in the flour, stirring briskly and thoroughly
3. Remove from the fire and when nearly cooled beat into it six eggs, each one beaten separately and added one at a time, beating the batter between each
4. Drop the stiff dough into boiling lard by teaspoonfuls
5. Stir the boiling lard around and around so that it whirls when dropping in the fritters, causing them to assume a round shape like balls
6. Eat with syrup or melted sugar and butter flavored
