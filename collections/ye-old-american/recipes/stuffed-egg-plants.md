## Ingredients

- egg plants
- grated bread-crumbs
- butter
- minced sweet herbs
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- beaten yolk of egg
- made gravy

## Directions

1. Parboil them to take off their bitterness.
2. Then slit each one down the side, and extract the seeds.
3. Have ready a stuffing made of grated bread-crumbs, butter, minced sweet herbs, salt, pepper, nutmeg, and beaten yolk of egg.
4. Fill with it the cavity from whence you took the seeds.
5. Bake the egg plants in a Dutch oven.
6. Serve them up with a made gravy poured into the dish.
