## Ingredients

- two porringers of cream
- one of white wine
- the skin of a lemon
- whites of three eggs
- sweetening to your taste

## Directions

1. Take two porringers of cream and one of white wine, grate in the skin of a lemon, take the whites of three eggs, sweeten it to your taste, then whip it with a whisk, take off the froth as it rises and put it into your syllabub glasses or pots, and they are fit for use.
