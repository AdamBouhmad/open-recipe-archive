## Ingredients

- two small slices halibut
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- melted butter
- greased fish-sheet
- Welsh Rarebit

## Directions

1. Sprinkle two small slices halibut with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
2. Then brush over with melted butter, place in dripping-pan on greased fish-sheet, and bake twelve minutes.
3. Remove to hot platter for serving, and pour over it a Welsh Rarebit.
