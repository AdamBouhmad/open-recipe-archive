## Ingredients

- six pounds lean young fresh pork
- three pounds fat
- three tea-spoonfuls dried sage per pound of meat
- six tea-spoonfuls pepper
- six tea-spoonfuls salt

## Directions

1. Clear the pork from the skin, sinews, and gristle
2. Mince it all as fine as possible
3. Take some dried sage, pick off the leaves and rub them to powder
4. Mix the fat and lean well together, and season it with six tea-spoonfuls of pepper, and the same quantity of salt
5. Strew on the powdered sage, and mix the whole very well with your hands
6. Put it away in a stone jar, packing it down hard; and keep it closely covered
7. Set the jar in a cool dry place
8. When you wish to use the sausage-meat, make it into flat cakes about an inch thick and the size of a dollar
9. Dredge them with flour, and fry them in nothing, over rather a slow fire, till they are well browned on both sides, and thoroughly done
