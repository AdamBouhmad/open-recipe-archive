## Ingredients

- ripe red raspberries
- strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Put a quart of ripe red raspberries in a bowl
2. Pour on them a quart of strong well flavoured vinegar
3. Let them stand twenty-four hours, strain them through a bag
4. Put this liquid on another quart of fresh raspberries, which strain in the same manner
5. Then put on a third quart
