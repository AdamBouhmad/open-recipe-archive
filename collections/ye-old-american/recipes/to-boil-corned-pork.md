## Ingredients

- fresh pork
- salt
- water
- cabbage
- cayenne pepper
- mustard

## Directions

1. Take a nice piece of fresh pork, (the leg is the best,) rub it with salt, and let it lie in the salt two days.
2. Boil it slowly in plenty of water, skimming it well.
3. When the meat is about half done, you may put into the same pot a fine cabbage, washed clean and quartered.
4. The pork and the cabbage should be thoroughly done, and tender throughout.
5. Send them to table in separate dishes, having drained and squeezed all the water out of the cabbage.
6. Take off the skin of the pork, and touch the outside at intervals with spots of cayenne pepper.
7. Eat mustard with it.
