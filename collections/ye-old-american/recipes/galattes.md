## Ingredients

- Puff or plain paste
- White of egg
- Cinnamon
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Roll puff or plain paste one-eighth inch thick.
2. Shape with an oblong cutter three and one-half inches long by one and three-fourths inches wide.
3. Brush over with white of egg and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
4. Bake in a hot oven.
