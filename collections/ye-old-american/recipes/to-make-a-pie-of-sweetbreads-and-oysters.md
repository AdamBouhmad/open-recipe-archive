## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- oysters
- pepper
- salt
- cream
- butter
- yelks of eggs
- flour
- puff paste
- forcemeat balls
- green pickles

## Directions

1. Boil the sweetbreads tender
2. Stew the oysters
3. Season them with pepper and salt
4. Thicken with cream, butter, the yelks of eggs and flour
5. Put a puff paste at the bottom and around the sides of a deep dish
6. Take the oysters up with an egg spoon, lay them in the bottom
7. And cover them with the sweetbreads
8. Fill the dish with gravy
9. Put a paste on the top
10. And bake it
11. This is the most delicate pie that can be made
12. The sweetbread of veal is the most delicious part, and may be broiled, fried, or dressed in any way, and is always good
