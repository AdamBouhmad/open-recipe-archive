## Ingredients

- cooked lamb
- two cups sauce
- one-fourth cup butter
- one-fourth cup flour
- one cup White Stock
- one cup milk
- two blades of mace
- salt and pepper
- one tablespoon Mushroom Catsup
- large croûtons
- green peas
- potato border
- finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Cut remnants of cooked lamb in cubes or strips.
2. Make sauce of one-fourth cup each of butter and flour, one cup White Stock, and one cup of milk which has been scalded with two blades of mace.
3. Season with salt and pepper and add one tablespoon Mushroom Catsup, or any other suitable table sauce.
4. Reheat two cups meat in two cups sauce.
5. Garnish with large croûtons.
6. Serve around green peas, or in a potato border.
7. Sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
