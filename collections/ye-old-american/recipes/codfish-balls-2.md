## Ingredients

- 1 pint bowl codfish picked very fine
- 2 pint bowls whole raw peeled potatoes sliced thickly
- butter (size of an egg)
- 1 well-beaten egg
- 3 spoonfuls cream or rich milk
- flour
- 1 ounce butter
- 1 ounce lard

## Directions

1. Put the codfish and potatoes together in plenty of cold water
2. Boil until the potatoes are thoroughly cooked
3. Remove from the fire and drain off all the water
4. Mash them with the potato masher
5. Add a piece of butter the size of an egg, one well-beaten egg, and three spoonfuls of cream or rich milk
6. Flour your hands and make into balls or cakes
7. Put an ounce each of butter and lard into a frying pan
8. When hot, put in the balls and fry a nice brown
