## Ingredients

- one cup chopped apple
- one-half cup raisins seeded and chopped
- one-half cup currants
- one-fourth cup butter
- one tablespoon molasses
- one tablespoon boiled cider
- one cup sugar
- one teaspoon cinnamon
- one-half teaspoon cloves
- one-half nutmeg grated
- one salt-spoon of mace
- one teaspoon salt
- stock in which meat was cooked
- one cup chopped meat
- two tablespoons Barberry Jelly

## Directions

1. Mix together chopped apple, raisins, currants, butter, molasses, boiled cider, sugar, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, mace, and salt.
2. Add enough stock in which meat was cooked to moisten; heat gradually to boiling-point, and simmer one hour; then add chopped meat and Barberry Jelly.
3. Cook fifteen minutes.
