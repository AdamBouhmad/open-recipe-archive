## Ingredients

- split peas or green peas
- water
- salt pork
- beef
- celery
- sweet herbs
- fried bread
- 1 quart of tomatoes, stewed soft

## Directions

1. Make according to either of the green pea receipts, in summer with green—in winter with dried and split peas.
2. Just before straining the soup, add a quart of tomatoes, which have already been stewed soft.
3. Let the whole come to a good boil, and strain.
4. If the stewed tomato be watery, strain off the superfluous liquid before pouring into the pea soup, or it will be too thin.
