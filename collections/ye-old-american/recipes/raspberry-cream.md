## Ingredients

- a quart of thick sweet cream
- raspberry juices to your taste
- double refined sugar to your taste

## Directions

1. boil it two or three wallops
2. take it off the fire and strain some juices of raspberries into it to your taste
3. stir it a good while before you put your juice in, that it may be almost cold
4. when you put it to it, and afterwards stir it one way for almost a quarter of an hour
5. then sweeten it to your taste and when it is cold you may send it up
