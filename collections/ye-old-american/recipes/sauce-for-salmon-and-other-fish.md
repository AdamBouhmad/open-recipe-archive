## Ingredients

- one cupful of milk
- one tablespoonful of cornstarch
- liquor from the salmon
- one great spoonful of butter
- one raw egg
- juice of half a lemon
- mace and cayenne pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Heat one cupful of milk to a boil
2. Thicken with a tablespoonful of cornstarch previously wet up with cold water
3. Add the liquor from the salmon and one great spoonful of butter
4. Add the raw egg beaten light to thickened milk when you have stirred in the butter and liquor
5. Take from the fire and season with mace and cayenne pepper to taste
6. Let it stand in hot water three minutes, covered
7. Add lemon juice and turn out immediately
8. Pour it all over and around the salmon
