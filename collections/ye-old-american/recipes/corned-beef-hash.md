## Ingredients

- cooked corned beef
- cold boiled potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- milk or cream
- parsley

## Directions

1. Remove skin and gristle from cooked corned beef, then chop the meat
2. When meat is very fat, discard most of the fat
3. To chopped meat add an equal quantity of cold boiled chopped potatoes
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. put into a hot buttered frying-pan
6. moisten with milk or cream
7. stir until well mixed
8. spread evenly
9. then place on a part of the range where it may slowly brown underneath
10. Turn, and fold on a hot platter
11. Garnish with sprig of parsley in the middle
