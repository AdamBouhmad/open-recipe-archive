## Ingredients

- two pounds of ripe grapes
- sugar
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Pulp through a sieve two pounds of ripe grapes, enough to keep back the stones
2. Add sugar to taste
3. Put into a trifle dish and cover with whipped cream, nicely flavored
4. Serve very cold
