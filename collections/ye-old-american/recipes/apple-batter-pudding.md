## Ingredients

- 1 pint rich milk
- 2 cups flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- ¼ teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water
- eight apples
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and core eight apples carefully, and range them closely together in a deep dish.
2. Beat the batter very light and pour over them.
3. Unless the apples are very ripe and sweet, fill the centre of each with white sugar.
4. Bake an hour, and eat hot with sweet sauce.
