## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup rice
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 cup canned tomatoes
- 1/2 cup cold cooked lamb cut in dice
- White Stock highly seasoned
- 1 chicken's liver
- salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for Turkish Pilaf III, substituting cold cooked lamb in place of chicken
2. Add a chicken's liver sautéd in butter, then separated into small pieces
