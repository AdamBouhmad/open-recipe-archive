## Ingredients

- Lyonnaise Tripe
- One tablespoon white wine
- One-third cup Tomato Sauce
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Add to Lyonnaise Tripe one tablespoon white wine
2. Cook until quite dry
3. Add one-third cup Tomato Sauce
4. Cook two minutes
5. Season with salt and pepper and serve
