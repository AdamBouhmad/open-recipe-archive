## Ingredients

- half an ounce of carbonate of ammonia
- one ounce of borax
- one quart of water
- two ounces of glycerine

## Directions

1. Dissolve half an ounce of carbonate of ammonia and one ounce of borax in one quart of water
2. then add two ounces of glycerine
