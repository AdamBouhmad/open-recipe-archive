## Ingredients

- oysters
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Open the oysters and put them in a bowl
2. Wash them out of their own liquor
3. Put some in the scollop shells
4. Strew over them a few bread crumbs, and lay a slice of butter on them
5. Then more oysters, bread crumbs, and a slice of butter on the top
6. Put them into a Dutch oven to brown
7. Serve them up in the shells
