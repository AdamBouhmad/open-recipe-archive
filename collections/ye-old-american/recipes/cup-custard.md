## Ingredients

- an egg
- two teaspoonfuls of sugar
- a pinch of salt
- a pinch of grated nutmeg
- good sweet milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Break into a coffeecup an egg, put in two teaspoonfuls of sugar, beat it up thoroughly, a pinch of salt and a pinch of grated nutmeg
2. Fill up the cup with good sweet milk
3. Turn it into another cup, well buttered, and set it in a pan of boiling water, reaching nearly to the top of the cup
4. Set in the oven, and when the custard is set, it is done
5. Eat cold
