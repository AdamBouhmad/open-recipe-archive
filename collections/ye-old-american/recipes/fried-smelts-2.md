## Ingredients

- smelts
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- egg
- crumbs
- deep fat
- parsley
- lemon
- fried gelatine
- Sauce Tartare

## Directions

1. Clean smelts, leaving on heads and tails
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Dip in flour, egg, and crumbs
4. Fry three to four minutes in deep fat
5. As soon as smelts are put into fat, remove fat to back of range so that they may not become too brown before cooked through
6. Arrange on hot platter
7. Garnish with parsley, lemon, and fried gelatine
8. Serve with Sauce Tartare
