## Ingredients

- twelve fine lemons
- a gallon of water
- two pounds of loaf-sugar
- a quart of rum or of the best whiskey

## Directions

1. Roll lemons under your hand on the table
2. Pare off the yellow rind very thin
3. Boil the rind in a gallon of water till all the flavour is drawn out
4. Break up into a large bowl two pounds of loaf-sugar, and squeeze the lemons over it
5. When the water has boiled sufficiently, strain it from the lemon-peel
6. Mix it with the lemon juice and sugar
7. Stir in a quart of rum or of the best whiskey
