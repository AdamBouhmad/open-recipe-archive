## Ingredients

- three or four pounds of beef
- a few sliced carrots
- one or two sliced onions
- a cupful of hot water
- pepper
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Grease the bottom of an iron pot
2. Place in it three or four pounds of beef
3. Be very careful that it does not burn and turn it until it is nicely browned
4. Set a muffin ring under the beef to prevent its sticking
5. Add a few sliced carrots, one or two sliced onions, and a cupful of hot water
6. Keep covered and stew slowly until the vegetables are done
7. Add pepper and salt
8. If you wish more gravy, add hot water and thicken with flour
9. Serve on a dish with the vegetables
