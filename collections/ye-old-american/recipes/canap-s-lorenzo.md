## Ingredients

- slices of bread
- two tablespoons butter
- one teaspoon white of egg
- Crab Mixture
- creamed butter
- cheese
- parsley

## Directions

1. Toast slices of bread cut in shape of horseshoes.
2. Cream two tablespoons butter, and add one teaspoon white of egg.
3. Spread slices of bread, rounding with Crab Mixture.
4. Cover with creamed butter.
5. Sprinkle with cheese.
6. Brown in the oven.
7. Serve on a napkin, ends towards centre of dish.
8. Garnish with parsley.
