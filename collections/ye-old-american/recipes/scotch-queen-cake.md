## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- two quarts of oatmeal
- one pound of powdered sugar
- a table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon and mace
- rose water (optional)
- half a pound of currants (optional)

## Directions

1. Melt a pound of butter by putting it into a skillet on hot coals
2. Set it away to cool
3. Sift two quarts of oatmeal into a deep pan
4. Mix with it a pound of powdered sugar and a table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon and mace
5. Make a hole in the middle and put in the melted butter
6. Mix it with a knife till you have formed of the whole a lump of dough
7. If it is too stiff, moisten it with a little rose water
8. Knead it well and roll it out into a large oval sheet, an inch thick
9. Cut it down the middle and then across, so as to divide it into four cakes
10. Prick them with a fork and crimp or scollop the edges neatly
11. Lay them in shallow pans
12. Set them in a quick oven and bake them of a light brown
