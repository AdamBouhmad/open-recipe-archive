## Ingredients

- six medium-sized sweet potatoes
- two tablespoons butter
- cream
- salt
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Bake six medium-sized sweet potatoes, remove from oven, cut in halves lengthwise, and scoop out inside.
2. Mash, add two tablespoons butter, and cream to moisten.
3. Season with salt and Sherry wine.
4. Refill skins and bake five minutes in a hot oven.
