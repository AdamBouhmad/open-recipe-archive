## Ingredients

- grapes
- sugar
- vinegar
- ground cloves
- allspice
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take the pulp from the grapes, preserving the skins
2. Boil the pulp and rub through a colander to get out the seeds
3. Add the skins to the strained pulp and boil with the sugar, vinegar and spices
4. To every seven pounds of grapes use four and one-half pounds of sugar, one pint of good vinegar
5. Spice quite highly with ground cloves and allspice, with a little cinnamon
