## Ingredients

- a pound of sugar
- two large tea-cups full of milk
- a pound of butter
- two pounds of flour
- a table-spoonful of cinnamon
- a handful of carraway seeds
- a tea-spoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Wet a pound of sugar with two large tea-cups full of milk.
2. Rub a pound of butter into two pounds of flour.
3. Add a table-spoonful of cinnamon, or a handful of carraway seeds.
4. Mix in the sugar and add a tea-spoonful of soda dissolved, and make the whole into a stiff dough.
5. Knead it, and then roll it out into a sheet about half an inch thick.
6. Beat it on both sides with the rolling-pin.
7. Cut it out with the edge of a tumbler into round cakes.
8. Prick them with a fork, lay them in buttered pans, and bake them light brown in a quick oven.
