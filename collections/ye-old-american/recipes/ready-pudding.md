## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- about one pound of flour
- six eggs with yolks and whites separately beaten
- one teaspoonful of salt
- four tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Make a batter of milk and flour
2. Add eggs with yolks and whites separately beaten, salt and sugar
3. It should be as stiff as can possibly be stirred with a spoon
4. Dip a spoonful at a time into quick boiling water
5. Boil from five to ten minutes
6. Take out and serve hot with sauce or syrup
