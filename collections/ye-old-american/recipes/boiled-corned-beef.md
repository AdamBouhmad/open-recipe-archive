## Ingredients

- corned beef
- water
- carrots
- turnips
- salt

## Directions

1. Cover the beef well with water and lay it on a fish drainer.
2. Hang it over a moderate fire that it may heat gradually all through.
3. Carefully take off the scum as it rises, and when no more appears, keep the pot closely covered, and let it boil slowly and regularly, with the fire at an equal temperature.
4. Allow at least four hours to a piece weighing about twelve pounds, and from that to five or six hours in proportion to the size.
5. Turn the meat twice in the pot while it is boiling.
6. Put in some carrots and turnips about two hours after the meat.
7. In carving a round of beef, slice it horizontally and very thin.
8. Do not help any one to the outside pieces, as they are generally too hard and salt.
9. Serve with French mustard.
