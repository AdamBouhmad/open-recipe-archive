## Ingredients

- 1 cupful boiling milk
- 2 dessertspoonfuls best arrowroot, rubbed smooth in cold water
- 2 teaspoonfuls white sugar
- vanilla or other essence

## Directions

1. Boil until it thickens well, stirring all the while.
2. Eat cold with cream, flavored with rose-water, and sweetened to taste.
