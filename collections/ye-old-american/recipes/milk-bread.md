## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- ½ teacupful of yeast
- ¼ lb. butter
- one tablespoonful white sugar
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Make the milk blood-warm
2. Stir into the milk a pint of flour, the sugar, lastly the yeast
3. Beat all together well and let them rise five or six hours
4. Melt the butter and add with a little salt
5. Work in flour enough to make a stiff dough
6. Let this rise four hours and make into small loaves
7. Set near the fire for half an hour and bake
8. In warm weather, add a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in warm water to the risen sponge
