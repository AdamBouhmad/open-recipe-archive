## Ingredients

- canned shrimps
- cold or ice water
- Cream Dressing II
- lettuce leaves
- capers
- olive

## Directions

1. Remove shrimps from can, cover with cold or ice water, and let stand twenty minutes.
2. Drain, dry between towels, remove intestinal veins, and break in pieces, reserving six of the finest.
3. Moisten with Cream Dressing II, and arrange on nests of lettuce leaves.
4. Put a spoonful of dressing on each, and garnish with a whole shrimp, capers, and an olive cut in quarters.
