## Ingredients

- ½ lb. ground rice
- 1 lb. rice-flour, dried and sifted
- 1 lb. powdered sugar
- ½ lb. butter
- 4 eggs
- Juice and half the grated rind of a lemon
- 1 tablespoonful orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Beat yolks and whites very light.
2. Put the sugar with the yolks.
3. Beat ten minutes, add the orange-flower water and lemon.
4. Add the flour and whites alternately, lastly.
5. Beat the mixture half an hour.
6. Bake immediately in patty-pans.
7. Eat while fresh.
