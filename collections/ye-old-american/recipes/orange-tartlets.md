## Ingredients

- 2 fine Havana oranges, juice of both, and grated peel of one
- ¾ cup of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- ½ lemon—juice only, to wet 1 teaspoonful corn-starch

## Directions

1. Beat all well together, and bake in tartlet shells without cover.
