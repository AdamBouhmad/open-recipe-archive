## Ingredients

- ½ cup pecan nut meat
- ½ cup English walnut meat
- ½ cup figs
- ½ cup dates
- Grated rind 1 orange
- 1 tablespoon orange juice
- 1 square chocolate, melted
- powdered sugar
- melted unsweetened chocolate
- small pieces blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Mix nut meats, figs, and dates, and force through a meat chopper, or chop finely.
2. Add remaining ingredients, toss on a board sprinkled with powdered sugar, and roll to one-third inch in thickness.
3. Cut in domino shapes, spread thinly with melted unsweetened chocolate, and decorate with small pieces blanched almonds to imitate dominoes.
