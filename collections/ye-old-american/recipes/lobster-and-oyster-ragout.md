## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup flour
- ¾ cup oyster liquor
- ¾ cup cream
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- Few grains cayenne
- Few drops onion juice
- 1 pint oysters parboiled
- ¾ cup lobster dice
- 1½ tablespoons Sauterne
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Make a sauce of first eight ingredients.
2. Add oysters, lobster dice, wine, and parsley.
