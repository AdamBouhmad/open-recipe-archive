## Ingredients

- ½ cup molasses
- ½ cup milk
- 1⅔ cups flour
- ½ teaspoon soda
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon clove
- ¼ teaspoon allspice
- ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
- ½ lb. dates, stoned and cut in pieces

## Directions

1. Mix and steam same as Graham Pudding
2. Serve with Wine Sauce
