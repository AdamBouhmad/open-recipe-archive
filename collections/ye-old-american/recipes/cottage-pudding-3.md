## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ⅔ cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 2¼ cups flour
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add sugar gradually, and egg well beaten
2. Mix and sift flour, baking powder, and salt
3. Add alternately with milk to first mixture
4. Turn into buttered cake pan
5. Bake thirty-five minutes
6. Serve with Vanilla or Hard Sauce
