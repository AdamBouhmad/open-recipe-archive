## Ingredients

- a small, delicate fillet of veal
- oyster force meat
- water
- rich white sauce
- oyster sauce
- stewed celery
- slices of bacon
- a boiled tongue

## Directions

1. Choose a small, delicate fillet and prepare as for roasting, or stuff it with an oyster force meat
2. After having washed it thoroughly, cover it with water and let it boil very gently three and a half or four hours, keeping it well skimmed
3. Send it to the table with a rich white sauce, or, if stuffed with oysters, a tureen of oyster sauce
4. Garnish with stewed celery and slices of bacon
5. A boiled tongue should be served with it
