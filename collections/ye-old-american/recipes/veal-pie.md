## Ingredients

- two pounds of veal from the loin, fillet, or best end of the neck
- water
- pound of butter
- two pounds of flour
- black pepper
- cold ham, sliced thin
- yolks of eggs, boiled hard
- button mushrooms

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of veal cut from the loin, fillet, or the best end of the neck.
2. Remove the bone, fat, and skin, and put them into a sauce-pan with half a pint of water to stew for the gravy.
3. Make a good paste, allowing a pound of butter to two pounds of flour.
4. Divide it into two pieces, roll it out rather thick, and cover with one piece the sides and bottom of a deep dish.
5. Put in a layer of veal, seasoned with black pepper, then a layer of cold ham sliced thin, then more veal, more ham, and so on till the dish is full; interspersing the meat with yolks of eggs boiled hard.
6. If you can procure some small button mushrooms they will be found an improvement.
7. Pour in, at the last, the gravy you have drawn from the trimmings, and put on the lid of the pie, notching the edge handsomely, and ornamenting the centre with a flower made of paste.
8. Bake the pie at least two hours and a half.
