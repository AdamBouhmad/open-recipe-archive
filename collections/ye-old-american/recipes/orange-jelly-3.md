## Ingredients

- six oranges
- two lemons
- two-ounce package of gelatine
- one pint of water
- two cupfuls of sugar
- three pints of boiling water

## Directions

1. Put the gelatine to soak in a pint of water
2. Squeeze the orange juice into a bowl, also the lemon juice, and grate one of the lemon skins in with it
3. Put about two cupfuls of sugar with the gelatine
4. Stir in the orange juice and pour over all three pints of boiling water, stirring constantly
5. When the gelatine is entirely dissolved, strain through a napkin into molds or bowls wet with cold water
6. Set aside to harden
