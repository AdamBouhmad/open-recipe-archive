## Ingredients

- currants, ripe
- a half pint of water
- less than a pound of sugar to a quart of the mashed fruit
- a small quantity of brandy or whisky

## Directions

1. Stem, mash and strain the currants
2. Add a half pint of water and less than a pound of sugar to a quart of the mashed fruit
3. Stir well up together and pour into a clean cask, leaving the bung-hole open, or covered with a piece of lace
4. Let it stand for a month to ferment
5. Just before bottling you may add a small quantity of brandy or whisky
