## Ingredients

- Gooseberries
- Water
- Sugar
- Puff-paste
- Egg

## Directions

1. Top and tail the gooseberries.
2. Put into a porcelain kettle with enough water to prevent burning, and stew slowly until they break.
3. Take them off, sweeten well, and set aside to cool.
4. When cold pour into pastry shells, and bake with a top crust of puff-paste.
5. Brush all over with beaten egg while hot, set back in the oven to glaze for three minutes.
6. Eat cold.
