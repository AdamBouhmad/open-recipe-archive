## Ingredients

- 1½ tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped onion
- 2 tablespoons corn-starch
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ⅓ cup finely chopped celery
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- Few grains paprika
- 1 cup cold cooked chicken, cut in small cubes
- Yolk 1 egg

## Directions

1. Cook butter and onion five minutes
2. Add corn-starch and stock gradually
3. Add lemon juice, celery, salt, paprika, and chicken
4. When well heated, add yolk of egg slightly beaten, and cook one minute
5. Serve with buttered Graham toast
