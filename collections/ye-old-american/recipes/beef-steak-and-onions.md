## Ingredients

- steak
- three or four onions, chopped
- beef-dripping or butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the steak as directed for Beef-Steak.
2. While it is broiling put three or four chopped onions in a frying-pan with a little beef-dripping or butter.
3. Stir and shake them briskly until they are done, and begin to brown.
4. Dish your steak and lay the onions thickly on top.
5. Cover and let all stand five or six minutes, that the hot onions may impart the required flavor to the hot meat.
6. In helping your guests, inquire if they will take onions with the slices of steak put upon their plates.
