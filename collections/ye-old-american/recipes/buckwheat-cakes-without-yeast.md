## Ingredients

- Two cups of buckwheat flour
- One cup of wheat flour
- A little salt
- Three teaspoonfuls baking powder
- About equal parts of milk and water
- A little molasses

## Directions

1. Mix flour, wheat flour, salt, and baking powder thoroughly
2. Add about equal parts of milk and water until the batter is of the right consistency
3. Stir until free from lumps
4. If they do not brown well, add a little molasses
