## Ingredients

- a pound of shelled bitter almonds
- an ounce of nutmegs
- a pound of fine loaf-sugar
- one grain of ambergris
- a gallon of white brandy or proof spirit

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar, and mix together a pound of shelled bitter almonds, an ounce of nutmegs, a pound of fine loaf-sugar, and one grain (apothecaries' weight) of ambergris.
2. Infuse these ingredients for a week in a gallon of white brandy or proof spirit.
3. Then filter it, and bottle it for use.
