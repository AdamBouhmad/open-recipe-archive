## Ingredients

- eight pounds of the best double-refined loaf-sugar
- whites of four eggs
- two quarts of very clear spring or pump water
- cold water

## Directions

1. Break up or powder the eight pounds of sugar
2. Beat the whites of four eggs to a strong froth
3. Stir the white of egg gradually into two quarts of very clear spring or pump water
4. Put the sugar into a porcelain kettle and mix with it the water and white of egg
5. While the sugar is melting, stir it frequently
6. When it is entirely dissolved, put the kettle over a moderate fire and let it boil
7. Carefully take off the scum as it comes to the top
8. Pour in a little cold water when you find the syrup rising so as to run over the edge of the kettle
9. When it first boils hard, pour in half a pint of cold water to keep down the bubbles so that the scum may appear and be easily removed
