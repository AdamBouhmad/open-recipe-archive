## Ingredients

- 1 teacup powdered sugar
- 3 eggs
- ½ teaspoonful cream-tartar
- ¼ teaspoonful soda
- 1 teacupful flour
- lemon juice and rind (half of one lemon)

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 teacup powdered sugar, 3 eggs, ½ teaspoonful cream-tartar, ¼ teaspoonful soda, and 1 teacupful flour.
2. Flavor with lemon—half the juice and half the rind of one.
3. Bake twenty minutes in shallow tins.
