## Ingredients

- cod fish
- Irish potatoes or parsnips
- onions
- melted butter
- grated nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- brandy or wine
- cream or thickened milk
- paste

## Directions

1. Boil the fish tender
2. Pick it from the bones
3. Take an equal quantity of Irish potatoes or parsnips boiled and chopped
4. Take the same quantity of onions well boiled
5. Add a sufficiency of melted butter, some grated nutmeg, pepper, and salt, with a little brandy or wine
6. Rub them in a mortar till well mixed
7. If too stiff, liquify it with cream or thickened milk
8. Put paste in the bottom of a dish
9. Pour in the fish and bake it
