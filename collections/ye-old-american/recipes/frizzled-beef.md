## Ingredients

- smoked or dried beef
- cold water
- sweet butter
- eggs
- white pepper
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Shave off very thin slices of smoked or dried beef
2. Put them in a frying pan and cover with cold water
3. Set on the back of the range or stove and let come to a very slow heat, allowing time to swell to natural size but not to boil
4. Stir it up and drain off the water
5. Melt one ounce of sweet butter in the frying pan and add the wafers of beef
6. When they begin to frizzle or turn up, break over them three eggs
7. Stir until the eggs are cooked
8. Add a little white pepper
9. Serve on slices of buttered toast
