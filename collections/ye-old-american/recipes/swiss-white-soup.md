## Ingredients

- a sufficient quantity of broth for six people
- three eggs
- two spoonfuls of flour
- one cup milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the broth.
2. Beat up three eggs well with two spoonfuls of flour and one cup milk.
3. Pour these gradually through a sieve into the boiling soup.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
