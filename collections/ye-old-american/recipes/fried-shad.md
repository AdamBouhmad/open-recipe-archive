## Ingredients

- roe-shad
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- lard or drippings
- water-cresses
- pickled cauliflower
- catsup

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and wipe a fine roe-shad.
2. Split and cut each side into four pieces, leaving out the head, and removing fins and tail.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and dredge with flour.
4. Have ready a frying pan of boiling hot lard or drippings; put in the fish and fry brown, turning at the end of five minutes to cook the other side.
5. Fry the roe in the same way.
6. Lay the fish in the middle of the dish, and the roe outside of it.
7. Garnish with water-cresses and sprigs of pickled cauliflower, and eat with catsup.
