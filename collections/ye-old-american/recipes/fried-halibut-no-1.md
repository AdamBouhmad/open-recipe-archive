## Ingredients

- halibut slices
- eggs
- brittle crackers
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- butter

## Directions

1. Select choice, firm slices from the fish
2. Carefully wash and dry with a soft towel
3. With a sharp knife take off the skin
4. Beat up two eggs
5. Roll out brittle crackers upon the kneading board until they are as fine as dust
6. Dip each slice into the beaten egg, then into the cracker crumbs after you have salted and peppered the fish
7. Place them in a hot frying pan half full of boiling lard, in which a little butter has been added to make the fish brown nicely
8. Turn and brown both sides
9. Remove from frying pan and drain
10. Serve hot
