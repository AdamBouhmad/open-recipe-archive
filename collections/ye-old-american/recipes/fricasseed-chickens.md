## Ingredients

- cold water
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- lard
- butter
- cream
- nutmeg
- mace
- cayenne
- parsley

## Directions

1. Having cut up your chickens, lay them in cold water till all the blood is drawn out.
2. Then wipe the pieces, season them with pepper and salt, and dredge them with flour.
3. Fry them in lard or butter; they should be of a fine brown on both sides.
4. When they are quite done, take them out of the frying-pan, cover them up, and set them by the fire to keep warm.
5. Skim the gravy in the frying-pan and pour into it half a pint of cream; season it with nutmeg, mace, and cayenne, and thicken it with a small bit of butter rolled in flour.
6. Give it a boil, and then pour it round the chickens, which must be kept hot.
7. Put some lard into the pan, and fry some parsley in it to lay on the pieces of chicken; it must be done green and crisp.
