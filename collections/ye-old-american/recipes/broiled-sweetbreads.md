## Ingredients

- sweetbreads
- pepper
- salt
- powdered mace
- melted butter
- white wine or mushroom catchup
- toast

## Directions

1. Split open and skewer the sweetbreads; season them with pepper and salt, and with powdered mace.
2. Broil them on a gridiron till thoroughly done.
3. While they are broiling, prepare some melted butter seasoned with mace and a little white wine, or mushroom catchup; and have ready some toast with the crust cut off.
4. Lay the toast in the bottom of a dish; place the sweetbreads upon it, and pour over them the drawn butter.
