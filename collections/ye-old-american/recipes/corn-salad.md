## Ingredients

- one can corn
- mustard
- onion juice
- French Dressing
- lettuce or chiccory

## Directions

1. Drain one can corn and season with mustard and onion juice.
2. Marinate with French Dressing, let stand one hour, then drain.
3. Arrange on a bed of lettuce or chiccory.
