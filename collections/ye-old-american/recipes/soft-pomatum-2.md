## Ingredients

- half a pound of fresh lard
- a quarter of a pound of beef marrow
- water
- two spoonfuls of brandy
- scented essence

## Directions

1. Soak half a pound of fresh lard and a quarter of a pound of beef marrow in water for two or three days
2. Squeeze and press it every day, and change the water
3. Drain off the water, and put the lard and marrow into a sieve to dry
4. Transfer it to a jar, and set the jar into a pot of boiling water
5. When the mixture is melted, put it into a basin, and beat it with two spoonfuls of brandy
6. Drain off the brandy, perfume the pomatum by mixing with it any scented essence that you please
7. Tie it up in gallipots
