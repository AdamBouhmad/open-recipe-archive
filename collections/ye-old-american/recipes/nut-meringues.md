## Ingredients

- Meringue Glacée mixture
- chopped nut meat (almonds, English walnuts, or hickory nuts)

## Directions

1. Add chopped nut meat to Meringue Glacée mixture.
2. Shape by dropping mixture from tip of spoon in small piles one-half inch apart, or by using pastry bag and tube.
3. Sprinkle with nut meat.
4. Bake.
