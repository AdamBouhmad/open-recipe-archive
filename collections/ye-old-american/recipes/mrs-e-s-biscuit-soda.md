## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 2 heaping tablespoonfuls of lard
- 2 cups sweet (if you can get it) new milk
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 2 teaspoonful cream-tartar
- 1 saltspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Rub the soda and cream-tartar into the flour and sift all together before they are wet
2. Put in the salt
3. Add the lard, rubbed into the prepared flour quickly and lightly
4. Pour in the milk
5. Work out the dough rapidly, kneading with as few strokes as possible
6. If the flour stiffen it too much, add more milk
7. Roll out lightly
8. Cut into cakes at least half an inch thick
9. Bake in a quick oven
