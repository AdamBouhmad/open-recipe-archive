## Ingredients

- one egg
- a pinch of salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat up one egg light, add a pinch of salt, and flour enough to make a very stiff dough.
2. Roll out very thin, like thin pie crust, dredge with flour to keep from sticking.
3. Let it remain on the bread board to dry for an hour or more.
4. Roll it up into a tight scroll, like a sheet of music.
5. Begin at the end and slice it into slips as thin as straws.
6. After all are cut, mix them lightly together, and keep them floured a little until you are ready to drop them into your soup.
7. Drop them into your soup shortly before dinner.
