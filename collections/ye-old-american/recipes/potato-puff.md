## Ingredients

- cold mashed potato
- butter
- eggs
- cream or milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Take two cupfuls of cold mashed potato and stir into it two tablespoonfuls of melted butter, beating to a white cream before adding anything else.
2. Put with this two eggs whipped very light and a teacupful of cream or milk, salting to taste.
3. Beat all well, pour into a deep dish, and bake in a quick oven until it is nicely browned.
