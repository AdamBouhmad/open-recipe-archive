## Ingredients

- a fine ripe pumpkin
- a large handful of hops
- water
- half a pint of good strong yeast

## Directions

1. Pare a fine ripe pumpkin, and cut it into pieces.
2. Put them into a kettle with a large handful of hops, and as much water as will cover them.
3. Boil them till the pumpkin is soft enough to pass through a cullender.
4. Put the pulp into a stone jar, adding half a pint of good strong yeast to set it into a fermentation.
5. The yeast must be well stirred into the pumpkin.
6. Leave the jar uncovered till next day; then secure it tightly with a cork.
