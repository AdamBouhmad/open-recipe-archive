## Ingredients

- a quart of cream
- the whites of 8 eggs
- half a pint of wine
- double refined sugar
- musk or Amber gum tied in a rag
- a bit of lemon peel tied in the middle of the whisk

## Directions

1. mix it together
2. sweeten it to your taste with double refined sugar
3. whip it up with a whisk
4. take off the froth with a spoon
5. and put into glasses
