## Ingredients

- maccaroni
- water
- milk
- a large lump of butter
- cream
- sugar
- nutmeg or powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. First put on the maccaroni in a very little water.
2. Let it come to a hard boil, and then drain off the water.
3. Put it on again with milk instead of water, and a large lump of butter.
4. Boil it till quite tender all through.
5. Then, while hot, mix in a little cream, and add some sugar and nutmeg, or powdered cinnamon.
