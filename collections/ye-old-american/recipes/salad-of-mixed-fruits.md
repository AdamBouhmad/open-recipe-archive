## Ingredients

- pineapple
- four sweet oranges
- four fine bananas
- grapes of mixed colors
- half a pint of clear sugar syrup
- half an ounce of good brandy

## Directions

1. Put in the centre of a dish a pineapple properly pared, cored and sliced, yet retaining as near as practicable its original shape
2. Peel, quarter and remove the seeds from four sweet oranges and arrange them in a border around the pineapple
3. Select four fine bananas, peel and cut into slices lengthwise and arrange these zigzag-fence fashion around the border of the dish
4. In the V-shaped spaces around the dish put tiny mounds of grapes of mixed colors
5. To half a pint of clear sugar syrup add half an ounce of good brandy, pour over the fruit and serve
