## Ingredients

- one cup of granulated sugar
- one-fourth of a cup of milk or five tablespoonfuls of milk
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Take one cup of granulated sugar and dampen it with one-fourth of a cup of milk
2. Place it on the fire in a suitable dish and stir it until it boils
3. Let it boil for five minutes without stirring
4. Remove it from the fire and set the dish in another of cold water
5. Add flavoring
6. While it is cooling, stir or beat it constantly until it becomes a thick, creamy frosting
