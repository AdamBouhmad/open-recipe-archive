## Ingredients

- a quart of the best vinegar
- four ounces of scraped horseradish

## Directions

1. Make a quart of the best vinegar boiling hot, and pour it on four ounces of scraped horseradish.
2. Let it stand a week, then strain it off, renew the horseradish, adding the same vinegar cold, and let it infuse a week longer, straining it again at the last.
