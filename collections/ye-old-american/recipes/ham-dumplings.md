## Ingredients

- cold ham
- pepper
- minced sage
- half a pound of chopped suet or half a pound of butter
- a pound of flour
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Chop some cold ham, the fat and lean in equal proportions
2. Season it with pepper and minced sage
3. Make a crust, allowing half a pound of chopped suet, or half a pound of butter to a pound of flour
4. Roll it out thick, and divide it into equal portions
5. Put some minced ham into each, and close up the crust
6. Have ready a pot of boiling water, and put in the dumplings
7. Boil them about three quarters of an hour
