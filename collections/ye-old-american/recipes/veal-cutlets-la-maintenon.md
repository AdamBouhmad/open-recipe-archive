## Ingredients

- veal cutlets
- beaten egg
- pounded cracker
- powdered sweet herbs
- pepper
- salt
- buttered note or letter paper
- buttered gridiron

## Directions

1. Cut cutlets nearly three-quarters of an inch thick and trim in shape
2. Dip each in beaten egg, then into pounded cracker seasoned with powdered sweet herbs, pepper, and salt
3. Wrap each cutlet in a half-sheet of note or letter paper, well buttered
4. Lay them upon a buttered gridiron
5. Broil over a clear fire, turning often and dexterously
6. Secure the papers by fringing the ends and twisting these after the cutlets are put in
7. If the envelopes become soiled or darkened during broiling, transfer to clean warm papers when done
8. Twist the ends and serve
9. Pour any gravy from the soiled papers into the clean ones over the cutlets
