## Ingredients

- ½ lb. pickled shrimps
- ¼ lb. good old cheese
- 1 tablespoonful salad oil
- ½ teaspoonful cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 teaspoonful white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful made mustard
- 4 tablespoonfuls celery or onion vinegar

## Directions

1. Mince the shrimps and grate the cheese.
2. Work into the latter, a little at a time, the various condiments enumerated above, the vinegar last.
3. Let all stand together ten minutes before adding the shrimps.
4. When this is done, stir well for a minute and a half and serve in a glass dish, garnished with lemon, or (if you can get one) in a clean crab-shell.
