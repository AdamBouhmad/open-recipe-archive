## Ingredients

- eels
- cold water
- salt
- parsley
- butter
- flour
- pepper

## Directions

1. Skin and clean, carefully extracting all the fat from the inside.
2. Cut into lengths of an inch and a half; put into a saucepan, with enough cold water to cover them; throw in a little salt and chopped parsley, and stew slowly, closely covered, for at least an hour.
3. Add, at the last, a great spoonful of butter, and a little flour wet with cold water, also pepper.
4. Serve in a deep dish.
