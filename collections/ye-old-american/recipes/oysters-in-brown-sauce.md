## Ingredients

- 1 pint oysters
- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup flour
- 1 cup oyster liquor
- ½ cup milk
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon Anchovy essence
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper

## Directions

1. Parboil and drain oysters, reserve liquor, heat, strain, and set aside for sauce
2. Brown butter, add flour, and stir until well browned
3. Add oyster liquor, milk, seasonings, and oysters
4. Use for filling patty cases or vol-au-vents
