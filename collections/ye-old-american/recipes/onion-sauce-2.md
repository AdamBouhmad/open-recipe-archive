## Ingredients

- one heaping tablespoonful of flour
- half a cupful of butter
- two cups of boiling milk
- four tender boiled onions
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Work together until light one heaping tablespoonful of flour and half a cupful of butter
2. Gradually add two cups of boiling milk and stir constantly until it comes to a boil
3. Stir into that four tender boiled onions that have been chopped fine
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste
