## Ingredients

- onions
- water
- butter
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil eight or ten large onions
2. Change the water two or three times while they are boiling
3. When enough, chop them on a board to keep them a good colour
4. Put them in a sauce pan with a quarter of a pound of butter and two spoonsful of thick cream
5. Boil it a little, and pour it over the ducks
