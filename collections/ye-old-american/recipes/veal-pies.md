## Ingredients

- veal
- cold water
- good paste
- hard-boiled eggs
- butter
- pepper
- sliced ham or thin strips of salt pork
- lemon-juice
- aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. Take out all the bone and put with the fat and refuse bits such as skin or gristle in a saucepan with a large teacupful of cold water to make gravy
2. Cut veal in thin even slices instead of chopping
3. Line a pudding-dish with a good paste and put a layer of veal in the bottom
4. Put a layer of hard-boiled eggs sliced with each piece buttered and peppered before it is laid upon the veal
5. Cover these with sliced ham or thin strips of salt pork
6. Squeeze a few drops of lemon-juice upon the ham
7. Alternate another layer of veal and so on until ready for the gravy
8. Stew the gravy for half an hour or so with the addition of pepper and a bunch of aromatic herbs
9. Strain through a thin cloth and pour over the pie
10. Cover with crust and bake two hours
