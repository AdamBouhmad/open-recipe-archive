## Ingredients

- Tripe
- Butter
- Onion
- One-eighth green pepper finely chopped
- One tablespoon flour
- One-half cup stock
- One-fourth cup drained tomatoes
- One fresh mushroom
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut, bake, and drain tripe as for Lyonnaise Tripe
2. Cook same quantity of butter and onion
3. Add one-eighth green pepper finely chopped, one tablespoon flour, one-half cup stock, one-fourth cup drained tomatoes, and one fresh mushroom cut in slices
4. Add tripe and cook five minutes
5. Season with salt and pepper
