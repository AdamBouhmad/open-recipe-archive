## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls grits (cracked wheat) soaked in a little cold water one hour
- 1 quart boiling water
- 1 cup milk
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Boil the soaked grits in the quart of water one hour, stirring up often
2. Add the milk and boil half an hour longer
3. Sweeten to taste
