## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of flour
- two ounces butter
- two ounces grated parmesan cheese
- a pinch of salt
- a few grains of cayenne pepper
- yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Mix into a paste with the yolk of an egg.
2. Roll out to the thickness of a silver quarter, about four or five inches long.
3. Cut into strips about a third of an inch wide.
4. Twist them as you would a paper spill and lay them on a baking-sheet slightly floured.
5. Bake in a moderate oven until crisp, but they must not be the least brown.
6. If put away in a tin these straws will keep a long time.
7. Serve cold, piled tastefully on a glass dish.
