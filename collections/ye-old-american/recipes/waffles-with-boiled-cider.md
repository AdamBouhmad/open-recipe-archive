## Ingredients

- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Whites 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- Twice as much cider as sugar

## Directions

1. Follow directions for making Waffles
2. For boiled cider: allow twice as much cider as sugar, and let boil until of a syrup consistency
3. Serve waffles with boiled cider
