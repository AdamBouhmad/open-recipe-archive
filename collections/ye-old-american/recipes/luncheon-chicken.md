## Ingredients

- 1½ cups cold cooked chicken, cut in small dice
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 slice carrot, cut in small cubes
- 1 slice onion
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup Chicken Stock
- Salt
- Pepper
- ⅔ cup buttered cracker crumbs
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Cook butter five minutes with vegetables.
2. Add flour and gradually the stock.
3. Strain and add chicken dice.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Turn on a slightly buttered platter and sprinkle with cracker crumbs.
6. Make four nests, and in each nest slip an egg.
7. Cover eggs with crumbs and bake in a moderate oven until whites of eggs are firm.
