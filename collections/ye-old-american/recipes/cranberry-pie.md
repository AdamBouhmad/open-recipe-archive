## Ingredients

- Fine, sound, ripe cranberries
- one cupful of white sugar
- half a cup of water
- a tablespoon full of sifted flour
- crust

## Directions

1. Take fine, sound, ripe cranberries and with a sharp knife split each one until you have a heaping coffeecupful
2. Put them in a vegetable dish or basin
3. Put over them one cupful of white sugar, half a cup of water, a tablespoon full of sifted flour
4. Stir it all together and put into your crust
5. Cover with an upper crust
6. Bake slowly in a moderate oven
