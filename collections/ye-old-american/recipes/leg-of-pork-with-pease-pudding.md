## Ingredients

- small leg of pork
- small delicate pease
- cloth

## Directions

1. Boil a small leg of pork that has been sufficiently salted
2. Score the top and serve it up
3. Get small delicate pease, wash them well, and tie them in a cloth, allowing a little room for swelling
4. Boil them with the pork
5. Then mash and season them, tie them up again and finish boiling it
6. Take care not to break the pudding in turning it out
