## Ingredients

- Calf's heart
- Fish Stuffing I
- Sage
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Hot fat
- Boiling water

## Directions

1. Wash a calf's heart and remove veins, arteries, and clotted blood
2. Stuff using half quantity of Fish Stuffing I seasoned highly with sage
3. Sew
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
5. Roll in flour and brown in hot fat
6. Place in small, deep baking-pan
7. Half cover it with boiling water
8. Cover closely and bake slowly two hours, basting every fifteen minutes
9. Add more water if necessary
10. Remove heart from pan and thicken the liquor with flour diluted with a small quantity of cold water
11. Season with salt and pepper and pour around the heart before serving
