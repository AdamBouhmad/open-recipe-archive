## Ingredients

- mutton
- salt
- pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Wipe meat
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Place on rack in dripping-pan
4. Dredge meat and bottom of pan with flour
5. Bake in hot oven one and one-fourth hours, basting every fifteen minutes
6. Serve with Currant Jelly Sauce
