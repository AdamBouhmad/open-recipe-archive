## Ingredients

- loaf-sugar
- lemons
- water

## Directions

1. Break up into large pieces six pounds of fine loaf-sugar.
2. Take twelve large ripe lemons, and without cutting them grate the yellow rind upon the sugar.
3. Put the sugar, with the lemon gratings and two quarts of water, into a preserving kettle, and let it dissolve.
4. When it is all melted, boil it till quite thick, skimming it till no more scum rises; it will then be done.
5. Have ready the juice of all the lemons, stir it in, and boil it ten minutes more.
6. Bottle it, and keep it in a cold place.
