## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- ⅛ tablespoon salt
- Few grains cayenne
- 1 tablespoon grated cheese
- Graham bread sandwiches

## Directions

1. Beat eggs slightly, add one-half teaspoon melted butter, salt, cayenne, and cheese.
2. Melt remaining butter, add mixture, and cook until firm, without stirring.
3. Roll, and sprinkle with grated cheese.
4. Serve with Graham bread sandwiches.
