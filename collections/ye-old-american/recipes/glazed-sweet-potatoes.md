## Ingredients

- six medium-sized potatoes
- salt
- water
- one-half cup sugar
- four tablespoons water
- one tablespoon butter

## Directions

1. Wash and pare six medium-sized potatoes.
2. Cook ten minutes in boiling salted water.
3. Drain, cut in halves lengthwise, and put in a buttered pan.
4. Make a syrup by boiling three minutes one-half cup sugar and four tablespoons water; add one tablespoon butter.
5. Brush potatoes with syrup and bake fifteen minutes, basting twice with remaining syrup.
