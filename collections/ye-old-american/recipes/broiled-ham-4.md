## Ingredients

- ham slices
- cold water
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut your ham into thin slices, which should be a little less than one quarter of an inch thick
2. Trim very closely the skin from the upper side of each slice and trim off the outer edge where the smoke has hardened the meat
3. If the ham is very salt lay it in cold water for one hour before cooking then wipe with a dry cloth
4. Broil over a brisk fire, turning the slices constantly
5. It will require about five minutes and should be served the last thing directly from the gridiron
6. Place on a warm platter with a little butter and a sprinkle of pepper on the top of each slice
