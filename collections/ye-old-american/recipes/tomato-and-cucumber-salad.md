## Ingredients

- tomato slices
- cucumber slices
- lettuce leaves
- red and green peppers
- French Dressing
- Mayonnaise Dressing

## Directions

1. Arrange alternate slices of tomato and cucumber until six slices have been piled one on top of another
2. Place on lettuce leaves
3. Garnish with strips of red and green peppers
4. Remove seeds from peppers and parboil two minutes before using
5. Serve with French and Mayonnaise Dressing
