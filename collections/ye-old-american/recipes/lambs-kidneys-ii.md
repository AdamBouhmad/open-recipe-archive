## Ingredients

- six kidneys
- salt and pepper
- butter
- one-half tablespoon finely chopped onion
- two tablespoons butter
- three tablespoons flour
- one and one-half cups hot stock
- one tablespoon Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Soak, pare, trim, and slice six kidneys.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Sauté in butter and remove to a hot dish.
4. Cook one-half tablespoon finely chopped onion in two tablespoons butter until brown.
5. Add three tablespoons flour and pour on slowly one and one-half cups hot stock.
6. Season with salt and pepper and strain.
7. Add kidneys and one tablespoon Madeira wine.
