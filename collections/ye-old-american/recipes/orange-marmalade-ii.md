## Ingredients

- nine oranges
- six lemons
- four quarts water
- eight pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Slice oranges and lemons crosswise with a sharp knife as thinly as possible
2. Remove seeds and put in a preserving kettle with four quarts water
3. Cover and let stand thirty-six hours
4. Boil for two hours
5. Add eight pounds sugar and boil one hour longer
