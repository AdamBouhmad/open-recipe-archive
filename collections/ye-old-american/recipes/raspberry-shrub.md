## Ingredients

- one quart of raspberry juice
- half a pound of loaf sugar
- a pint of Jamaica rum, or part rum and brandy

## Directions

1. Dissolve half a pound of loaf sugar
2. Mix thoroughly with one quart of raspberry juice and a pint of Jamaica rum or part rum and brandy
3. Bottle for use
