## Ingredients

- ½ cup molasses
- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup flour (scant)
- ⅔ cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon ginger

## Directions

1. Heat molasses to boiling-point, add butter, then slowly, stirring constantly, flour mixed and sifted with ginger and sugar.
2. Drop small portions from tip of spoon on a buttered inverted dripping-pan two inches apart.
3. Bake in a slow oven, cool slightly, remove from pan, and roll over handle of wooden spoon.
