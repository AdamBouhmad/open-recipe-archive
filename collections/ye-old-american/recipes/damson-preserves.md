## Ingredients

- damsons
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Wipe damsons with a piece of cheese-cloth wrung out of cold water, and prick each fruit five or six times, using a large needle; then weigh.
2. Make a syrup by boiling three-fourths their weight in sugar with water, allowing one cup to each pound of sugar.
3. As soon as syrup reaches boiling-point, skim, and add plums, a few at a time, that fruit may better keep in shape during cooking.
4. Cook until soft.
5. Put into glass or stone jars.
