## Ingredients

- yelk of an egg
- salt
- bread crumbs
- caper sauce
- current jelly cut in slices

## Directions

1. Prepare it as before, score the top
2. Wash it over with the yelk of an egg, sprinkle some salt, and cover it with bread crumbs
3. Bake it, and pour caper sauce in the dish
4. It may also be roasted, the skin taken off and frothed nicely
5. Serve it up with good gravy, and garnish with current jelly cut in slices
