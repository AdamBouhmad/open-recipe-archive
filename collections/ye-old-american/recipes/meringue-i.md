## Ingredients

- Whites 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
- ½ tablespoon lemon juice or ¼ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat whites until stiff.
2. Add sugar gradually and continue beating.
3. Add flavoring.
