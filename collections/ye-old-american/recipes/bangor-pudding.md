## Ingredients

- 1⅓ cups cracker crumbs
- Boiling water
- 2 cups milk
- ⅓ cup molasses
- 1 egg
- 1 cup raisins

## Directions

1. Moisten cracker crumbs with boiling water, and let stand until cool
2. Add milk, molasses, egg slightly beaten, and raisins seeded and cut in pieces
3. Turn into a buttered pudding mould, and steam eight hours
4. Let stand in mould to cool
5. Serve cold with Cream Sauce II
