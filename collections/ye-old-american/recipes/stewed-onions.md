## Ingredients

- young onions
- cold water
- hot water
- milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Top, tail, and skin the onions.
2. Lay them in cold water half an hour or more.
3. Put into a saucepan with hot water enough to cover them.
4. When half done, throw off all the water, except a small teacupful.
5. Add a like quantity of milk, a great spoonful of butter, with pepper and salt to taste.
6. Stew gently until tender.
7. Turn into a deep dish.
8. If the onions are strong and large, boil in three waters, throwing away all of the first and second, and reserving a very little of the third to mix with the milk.
