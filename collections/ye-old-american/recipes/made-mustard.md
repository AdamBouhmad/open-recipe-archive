## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls best English mustard
- 2 teaspoonfuls salt
- 2 teaspoonfuls white sugar
- 1 teaspoonful white pepper
- 2 teaspoonfuls salad oil
- Vinegar to mix to a smooth paste—celery or Tarragon vinegar if you have it
- 1 small garlic, minced very small

## Directions

1. Put the mustard in a bowl and wet with the oil, rubbing it in with a silver or wooden spoon until it is absorbed. Wet with vinegar to a stiff paste; add salt, pepper, sugar, and garlic, and work all together thoroughly, wetting little by little with the vinegar until you can beat it as you do cake-batter. Beat five minutes very hard; put into wide-mouthed bottles—empty French mustard bottles, if you have them—pour a little oil on top, cork tightly, and set away in a cool place.
