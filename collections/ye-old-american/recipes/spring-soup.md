## Ingredients

- 1 quart White Stock I or II
- 1 large onion thinly sliced
- 3 tablespoons butter
- ½ cup stale baker's bread
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup cream
- 2 tablespoons flour
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook onion fifteen minutes in one tablespoon butter; add to stock, with bread broken in pieces.
2. Simmer one hour; rub through sieve.
3. Add milk, and bind with remaining butter and flour cooked together; add cream, and season.
