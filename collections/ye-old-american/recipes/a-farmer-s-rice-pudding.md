## Ingredients

- a common-sized tea-cup of rice
- a quart of rich milk or cream and milk mixed
- a quarter of a pound of brown sugar
- a table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash a common-sized tea-cup of rice through cold water.
2. Stir it raw into a quart of rich milk, or of cream and milk mixed; adding a quarter of a pound of brown sugar, and a table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon.
3. Put it into a deep pan, and bake it two hours or more.
4. When done, the rice will be perfectly soft, which you may ascertain by dipping a tea-spoon into the edge of the pudding and taking out a little to try.
5. Eat it cold.
