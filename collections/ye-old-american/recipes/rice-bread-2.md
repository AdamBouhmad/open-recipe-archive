## Ingredients

- 1 quart warm water
- 1 teacupful yeast
- 1 tablespoonful white sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls lard
- 1 quart wheat flour
- three pints of warm milk
- three teacupfuls rice-flour wet to a thin paste with cold milk, and boiled four minutes

## Directions

1. Make a sponge with warm water, yeast, white sugar, lard, and wheat flour
2. Beat well together and when it has risen (about five hours), add three pints of warm milk and three teacupfuls rice-flour wet to a thin paste with cold milk and boiled four minutes
3. Stir into the batter when it should be a little more than blood-warm
4. If not thick enough to make out into dough, add a little wheat-flour
5. Knead thoroughly and treat as you would wheat bread in the matter of the two risings and baking
