## Ingredients

- potatoes
- chopped onion
- pepper
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Peel, cook and mash the required quantity
2. Add while hot a little chopped onion, pepper and salt
3. Form it into small oval balls and dredge them with flour
4. Place around the meat about twenty minutes before it is taken from the oven
5. When nicely browned, drain dry and serve hot with the meat
