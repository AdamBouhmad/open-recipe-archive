## Ingredients

- a cocoa-nut
- powdered loaf-sugar
- the milk of the nut
- red sugar-sand

## Directions

1. Break up a cocoa-nut.
2. Peel and wash the pieces in cold water, and grate them.
3. Mix in the milk of the nut and some powdered loaf-sugar.
4. Form the grated cocoa-nut into little balls upon sheets of white paper.
5. Make them all of a regular and handsome form.
6. Touch the top of each with a spot of red sugar-sand.
7. Place them to dry for twenty-four hours in a warm room where nothing is likely to disturb them.
