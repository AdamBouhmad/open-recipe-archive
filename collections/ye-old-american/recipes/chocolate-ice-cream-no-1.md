## Ingredients

- four ounces of grated chocolate
- a cupful of sweet milk
- a quart of thick sweet cream
- vanilla
- a cupful of sugar

## Directions

1. Add four ounces of grated chocolate to a cupful of sweet milk
2. Mix it thoroughly to a quart of thick sweet cream
3. Sweeten with a cupful of sugar
4. Beat again and freeze
