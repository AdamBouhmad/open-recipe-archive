## Ingredients

- five fresh eggs
- half a cup of sugar
- one quart of sweet rich milk
- extract of lemon or vanilla
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat five fresh eggs, the whites and yolks separately, the yolks with half a cup of sugar, the whites to a stiff froth.
2. Stir them gradually into a quart of sweet rich milk previously boiled and cooled.
3. Flavor with extract of lemon or vanilla and half a teaspoonful of salt.
4. Rub butter over the bottom and sides of a baking-dish or tin basin.
5. Pour in the custard, grate a little nutmeg over and bake in a quick oven.
6. Set the dish in a shallow pan of hot water reaching nearly to the top, the water to be kept boiling until the custard is baked; three-quarters of an hour is generally enough.
7. Run a teaspoon handle into the middle of it; if it comes out clean it is baked sufficiently.
