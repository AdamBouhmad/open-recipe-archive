## Ingredients

- one coffeecupful of granulated sugar
- half of a cupful of water
- a piece of butter the size of a walnut
- lemon or vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Boil all together until it becomes the consistency of syrup
2. Flavor with lemon or vanilla extract
3. A tablespoonful of lemon juice is an improvement
