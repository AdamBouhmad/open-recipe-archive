## Ingredients

- two cups of butter
- three cups of sugar
- two small cups of milk
- seven cups of sifted flour
- four eggs, the whites and yolks separately beaten
- one teacupful of seeded raisins
- one teacupful of well-washed and dried currants
- one teacupful of sliced citron
- one tablespoonful of powdered cinnamon
- one teaspoonful of mace
- one teaspoonful of soda
- one teacupful of home-made yeast
- a wine-glass of wine or brandy

## Directions

1. Take part of the butter and warm it with the milk
2. Stir in part of the flour and the yeast and let it rise
3. Then add the other ingredients with a wine-glass of wine or brandy
4. Turn all into well-buttered cake-tins and let rise again
5. Bake slowly in a moderate oven for two hours
