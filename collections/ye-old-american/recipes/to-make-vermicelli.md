## Ingredients

- two or three fresh eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat two or three fresh eggs quite light
2. Make them into a stiff paste with flour
3. Knead it well and roll it out very thin
4. Cut it in narrow strips
5. Give them a twist
6. Dry them quickly on tin sheets
