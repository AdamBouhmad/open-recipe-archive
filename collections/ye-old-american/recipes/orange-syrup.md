## Ingredients

- oranges
- loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Pare the oranges and squeeze and strain the juice from the pulp
2. To one pint of juice allow one pound and three-quarters of loaf sugar
3. Put the juice and sugar together
4. Boil and skim it until it is cream
5. Strain it through a flannel bag and let it stand until it becomes cool
6. Put in bottles and cork tight
