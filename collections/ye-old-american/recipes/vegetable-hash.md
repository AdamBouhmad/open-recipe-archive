## Ingredients

- cabbage
- parsnips
- potatoes
- pepper
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Chop rather coarsely the remains of vegetables left from a boiled dinner, such as cabbage, parsnips, potatoes, etc.; sprinkle over them a little pepper, place in a saucepan or frying pan over the fire.
2. Put in a piece of butter the size of a hickory nut; when it begins to melt, tip the dish so as to oil the bottom and around the sides; then put in the chopped vegetables.
3. Pour in a spoonful or two of hot water from the tea-kettle, cover quickly so as to keep in the steam.
4. When heated thoroughly take off the cover and stir occasionally until well cooked.
5. Serve hot.
