## Ingredients

- eight sour apples
- one cup sugar
- eight cloves
- water

## Directions

1. Wipe, quarter, core, and pare eight sour apples.
2. Put in a saucepan, sprinkle with one cup sugar, add eight cloves, and enough water to prevent apples from burning.
3. Cook to a mush, stirring occasionally.
