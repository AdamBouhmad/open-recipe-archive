## Ingredients

- puff paste trimmings
- strawberry or other preserves

## Directions

1. Roll paste a little thicker than ordinary pies
2. cut out with a round cutter, first dipped in hot water, to make the edges smooth
3. place in small tart-pans, first pricking a few holes at the bottom with a fork before placing them in the oven
4. Bake from ten to fifteen minutes
5. Let the paste cool a little
6. fill it with preserve
