## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon granulated gelatine
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 cup sugar
- Whites of 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
- Macaroons

## Directions

1. Dissolve gelatine in boiling water
2. Add sugar
3. As soon as dissolved set bowl containing mixture in pan of ice-water
4. Add whites of eggs and vanilla
5. Beat until mixture thickens
6. Turn into a shallow pan, first dipped in cold water
7. Let stand until thoroughly chilled
8. Remove from pan and cut in pieces the size and shape of marshmallows
9. Roll in macaroons which have been dried and rolled
10. Serve with sugar and cream
