## Ingredients

- one pint oysters
- cold boiled fowl
- salad dressing
- lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. Clean, parboil, and drain one pint oysters.
2. Remove tough muscles, and mix soft parts with an equal quantity of cold boiled fowl cut in one-half inch dice.
3. Moisten with any salad dressing, and serve on a bed of lettuce leaves.
