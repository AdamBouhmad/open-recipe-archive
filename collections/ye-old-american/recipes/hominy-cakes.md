## Ingredients

- 2 cups fine hominy, boiled and cold
- 1 cup white flour
- 1 quart milk
- 3 eggs, very well beaten
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Beat smooth the hominy
2. Work in the milk and salt, then the flour, lastly the eggs
3. Bake at once, and keep the mixture well stirred
