## Ingredients

- chine
- beaten egg
- cracker-crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- water
- cucumber pickles

## Directions

1. Wash the chine over with beaten egg, dredge with cracker-crumbs, seasoned with salt and pepper, and roast, basting with butter and water once when the meat is heated through, afterward with its own gravy.
2. This is a palatable supper-dish when cold.
3. Garnish with cucumber pickles cut in round slices.
