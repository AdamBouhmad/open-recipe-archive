## Ingredients

- peaches
- peach kernels
- lemon juice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Crack one-third of the kernels and put them in the jar with the peaches, which should be pared, stoned, and sliced
2. Heat in a pot of boiling water, stirring from time to time until the fruit is well broken
3. Strain, and to every pint of peach juice add the juice of a lemon
4. Measure again, allowing a pound of sugar to a pint of liquid
5. Heat the sugar very hot, and add when the juice has boiled twenty minutes
6. Let it come to a boil, and take instantly from the fire
