## Ingredients

- quart of picked huckleberries
- buttered pie dish
- pie paste
- half of a teacupful of brown sugar
- teaspoonful of flour
- saltspoonful of salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put a quart of picked huckleberries into a basin of water and take off whatever floats
2. Take up the berries by the handful, pick out all the stems and unripe berries and put them into a dish
3. Line a buttered pie dish with a pie paste
4. Put in the berries half an inch deep
5. To a quart of berries, put half of a teacupful of brown sugar
6. Dredge a teaspoonful of flour over
7. Strew a saltspoonful of salt and a little nutmeg grated over
8. Cover the pie, cut a slit in the centre, or make several small incisions on either side of it
9. Press the two crusts together around the edge
10. Trim it off neatly with a sharp knife
11. Bake in a quick oven for three-quarters of an hour
