## Ingredients

- one quart of Graham flour
- half a pint of Indian meal
- one gill of yeast
- one teaspoonful of salt
- warm water

## Directions

1. Mix the flour and meal
2. Pour on enough warm water to make batter rather thicker than that for buckwheat cakes
3. Add the yeast
4. When light bake on griddle not too hot
