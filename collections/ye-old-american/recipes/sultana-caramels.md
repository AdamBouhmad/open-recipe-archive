## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- ½ cup milk
- ¼ cup molasses
- ¼ cup butter
- 2 squares chocolate
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- ½ cup English walnut or hickory nut meat, cut in pieces
- 2 tablespoons Sultana raisins

## Directions

1. Put butter into a saucepan; when melted, add sugar, milk, and molasses.
2. Heat to boiling-point, and boil seven minutes.
3. Add chocolate, and stir until chocolate is melted; then boil seven minutes longer.
4. Remove from fire, beat until creamy, add nuts, raisins, and vanilla, and pour at once into a buttered tin.
5. Cool slightly, and mark in squares.
6. The nut meats and raisins may be omitted.
