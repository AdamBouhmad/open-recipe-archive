## Ingredients

- one quart of ripe fresh huckleberries or blueberries
- half a teaspoonful of mace or nutmeg
- three eggs, well beaten separately
- two cupfuls of sugar
- one tablespoonful of cold butter
- one cupful of sweet milk
- one pint of flour
- two teaspoonfuls of baking powder

## Directions

1. Roll the berries well in the flour
2. Combine sugar, butter, milk, flour and baking powder
3. Add the floured berries last of all
4. Bake half an hour and serve with sauce
