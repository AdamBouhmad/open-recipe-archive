## Ingredients

- tea
- lemon slice or lemon juice
- sugar
- preserved strawberry or candied cherry

## Directions

1. Follow recipe for making tea
2. Serve hot or cold without milk
3. Add a thin slice of lemon with seeds removed, or a few drops of lemon juice to each cup
4. Add sugar according to taste
5. Garnish with a preserved strawberry or candied cherry to each cup
