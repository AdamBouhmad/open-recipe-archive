## Ingredients

- 2 eggs, beaten stiff
- 1 large cup of sugar
- 5 tablespoonfuls boiling milk
- ½ teaspoonful arrow-root or corn-starch, wet with cold milk
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg, or mace
- 1 tablespoonful butter

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the sugar, add the beaten eggs, and work all to a creamy froth.
2. Wet the corn-starch and put in next with the spice—finally, pour in by the spoonful the boiling milk, beating well all the time.
3. Set within a saucepan of boiling water five minutes, stirring all the while, but do not let the sauce boil.
