## Ingredients

- roe shad
- salt
- drawn butter
- chopped eggs
- parsley
- egg-sauce
- capers
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Clean, wash, and wipe the fish.
2. Cleanse the roes thoroughly, and having sprinkled both shad and eggs with salt, wrap in separate cloths and put into a fish-kettle, side by side.
3. Cover with salted water, and boil from half an hour to three-quarters, in proportion to the size.
4. Serve the shad upon a hot dish, with a boat of drawn butter mingled with chopped eggs and parsley, or egg-sauce.
5. Lay the roes about the body of the fish.
6. Garnish with capers and slices of hard boiled eggs.
