## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 2½ tablespoons flour
- Grated rind 2 lemons
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon butter

## Directions

1. Mix sugar and flour, add grated rind, lemon juice, and egg slightly beaten.
2. Put butter in saucepan; when melted, add mixture, and stir constantly until boiling-point is reached.
3. Care must be taken that mixture does not adhere to bottom of saucepan.
4. Cool before spreading.
