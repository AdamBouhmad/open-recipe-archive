## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- egg
- deep fat

## Directions

1. Pare cucumbers and cut lengthwise in one-third inch slices.
2. Dry between towels.
3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again.
5. Fry in deep fat.
6. Drain.
