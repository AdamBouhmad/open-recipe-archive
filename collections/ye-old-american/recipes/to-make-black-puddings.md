## Ingredients

- blood
- boiled rice or oatmeal
- leaf fat
- pepper
- salt
- herbs
- skins

## Directions

1. Catch the blood as it runs from the hog, stir it continually till cold to prevent its coagulating
2. When cold thicken it with boiled rice or oatmeal
3. Add leaf fat chopped small, pepper, salt, and any herbs that are liked
4. Fill the skins and smoke them two or three days
5. They must be boiled before they are hung up
6. Prick them with a fork to keep them from bursting
