## Ingredients

- three cucumbers
- force-meat using Chicken Force-meat I or II with veal substituted for chicken
- White Stock
- one and one-half cups Béchamel Sauce
- dry toast

## Directions

1. Pare three cucumbers, cut in halves crosswise, and remove seeds.
2. Let stand in cold water thirty minutes.
3. Drain and wipe.
4. Fill with force-meat, using recipe for Chicken Force-meat I or II, substituting veal for chicken.
5. Place upright on a trivet in a saucepan.
6. Half surround with White Stock and cover.
7. Cook forty minutes.
8. Place on thin slices of dry toast, cut in circular shapes.
9. Pour around one and one-half cups Béchamel Sauce.
10. Serve as a vegetable course or an entrée.
