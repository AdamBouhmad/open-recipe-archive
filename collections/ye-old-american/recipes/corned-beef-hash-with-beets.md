## Ingredients

- Corned Beef hash
- cooked beets
- potatoes
- cold roast beef or roast beef and corned beef

## Directions

1. When preparing Corned Beef hash, add one-half as much finely chopped cooked beets as potatoes
2. Cold roast beef or one-half roast beef and one-half corned beef may be used
