## Ingredients

- six smooth, medium-sized tomatoes
- cracker crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- onion juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Wipe, and remove a thin slice from stem end of six smooth, medium-sized tomatoes.
2. Take out seeds and pulp, and drain off most of the liquid.
3. Add an equal quantity of cracker crumbs, season with salt, pepper, and a few drops onion juice.
4. Refill tomatoes with mixture.
5. Place in a buttered pan, sprinkle with buttered crumbs.
6. Bake twenty minutes in a hot oven.
