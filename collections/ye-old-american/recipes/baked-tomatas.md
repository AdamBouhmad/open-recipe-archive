## Ingredients

- Large fine tomatas
- Grated bread-crumbs
- Butter
- Salt
- Cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Peel some large fine tomatas, cut them up, and take out the seeds
2. Put them into a deep dish in alternate layers with grated bread-crumbs, and a very little butter in small bits
3. Season the whole with a little salt and cayenne pepper
4. Set it in an oven and bake it for at least three hours
