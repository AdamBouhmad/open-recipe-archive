## Ingredients

- 1 bottle Jamaica Ginger Extract
- 1 oz. cream-tartar
- 6 quarts water
- 1 lb. sugar
- grated peel of a lemon
- 1 tablespoonful brewers' yeast

## Directions

1. Stir until the sugar is melted
2. Put in the grated peel of a lemon, and heat until blood-warm
3. Add a tablespoonful of brewers' yeast
4. Stir well and bottle, wiring down the corks
5. It will be fit for use in four days
