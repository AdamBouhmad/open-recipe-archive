## Ingredients

- green corn
- milk
- flour
- egg
- salt
- butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the corn from the cob, and put into a bowl with a cup of milk to every cup of corn, a half cup of flour, one egg, a pinch of salt, and a little butter.
2. Mix well into a thick batter, and fry in small cakes in very hot butter.
3. Serve with plenty of butter and powdered sugar.
