## Ingredients

- dried beans
- water
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash and soak over night in lukewarm water, changing it several times for warmer.
2. If this is done they will require but two hours' boiling.
3. Drain very thoroughly, pressing them firmly, but lightly, in the cullender with a wooden spoon; salt, pepper and mix in a great lump of butter when they are dished.
