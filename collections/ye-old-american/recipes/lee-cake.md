## Ingredients

- 10 eggs
- 1 lb. sugar
- ½ lb. flour
- 2 lemons
- 1 orange
- 1 lb. and a quarter powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat whites and yolks separately; add to all the yolks and the whites of seven eggs the sugar, the rind of two lemons, and juice of one. Bake as for jelly-cake.
2. To the whites of three eggs allow a pound and a quarter of powdered sugar; beat stiff as for icing, take out enough to cover the top of the cake and set aside.
3. Add to the rest the juice and half the grated rind of a large orange. When the cake is nearly cold, spread this between the layers.
4. Beat into the icing reserved for the top a little lemon-juice, and, if needed, more sugar. It should be thicker than that spread between the cakes.
