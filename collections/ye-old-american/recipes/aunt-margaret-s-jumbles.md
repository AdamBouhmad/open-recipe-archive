## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 teacup milk
- 5 eggs
- ½ teaspoonful soda dissolved in boiling water
- 1 teaspoonful nutmeg
- Sufficient flour to make soft dough

## Directions

1. Roll out, cut into shapes and sift sugar over them before they go into the oven.
