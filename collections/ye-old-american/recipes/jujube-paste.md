## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of sugar
- one-quarter of a pound of gum arabic
- one pint of water
- essence of lemon
- grain of cochineal

## Directions

1. Let the mixture of sugar, gum arabic, and water stand until the gum is dissolved in a warm place on the back of the stove
2. Draw forward and cook until thick
3. Try in cold water; it should be limber and bend when cold
4. Pour in buttered pans, an eighth of an inch thick
5. When cool, roll up in a scroll
