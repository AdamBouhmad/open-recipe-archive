## Ingredients

- fruit
- cinnamon or other seasonings
- slices of bread
- butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Stew any kind of fruit, and season it in any you like best
2. soak some slices of bread in butter
3. lay them while hot, in the bottom and round the sides of a dish, which has been rubbed with butter
4. put in your fruit
5. lay slices of bread prepared in the same manner on the top
6. bake it a few minutes
7. turn it carefully into another dish
8. sprinkle on some powdered sugar
9. glaze it with a salamander
