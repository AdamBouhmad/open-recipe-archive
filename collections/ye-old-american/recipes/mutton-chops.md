## Ingredients

- rack
- mushroom catsup
- butter
- salt
- horse-radish

## Directions

1. Cut the rack as for the harrico, broil them
2. And when dished, pour over them a gravy made with two large spoonsful of boiling water
3. One of mushroom catsup, a small spoonful of butter and some salt
4. Stir it till the butter is melted
5. And garnish with horse-radish scraped
