## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup rice
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup boiling water
- 1 and 3/4 cups hot stewed tomatoes
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and drain one-half cup rice
2. Cook in one tablespoon butter until brown
3. Add one cup boiling water
4. Steam until water is absorbed
5. Add one and three-fourths cups hot stewed tomatoes
6. Cook until rice is soft
7. Season with salt and pepper
