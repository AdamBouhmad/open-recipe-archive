## Ingredients

- ⅔ cup cracker crumbs
- 1 quart scalded milk
- ⅓ cup sugar
- ¼ cup melted butter
- 2 eggs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla or ¼ teaspoon spice
- Whites 2 eggs
- ¼ cup powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make same as Bread Pudding, using two-thirds cup cracker crumbs in place of bread crumbs
2. After baking, cover with meringue made of whites two eggs, one-fourth cup powdered sugar, and one tablespoon lemon juice
3. Return to oven to cook meringue
