## Ingredients

- rose leaves
- best fourth proof French brandy

## Directions

1. Gather the leaves of roses while the dew is on them, and as soon as they open put them into a wide-mouthed bottle
2. When the bottle is full pour in the best fourth proof French brandy
3. It will be fit for use in three or four weeks and may be frequently replenished
