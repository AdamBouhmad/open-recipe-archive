## Ingredients

- pieces of broken loaf-sugar
- yellow rind of four lemons or oranges
- half a pound of loaf-sugar powdered
- juice of the lemons
- six eggs
- a quart of cream
- stale Naples biscuit or square sponge cakes

## Directions

1. Rub off the yellow rind of four lemons or oranges on pieces of broken loaf-sugar
2. Pulverize the sugar and mix it with half a pound of loaf-sugar already powdered and moistened with the juice of the lemons
3. Beat six eggs very light and stir them gradually into a quart of cream, in turn with the sugar and lemon
4. Grate stale Naples biscuit or square sponge cakes very fine and stir them gradually into the mixture in sufficient quantity to make a thick batter
5. Beat the mixture till perfectly smooth and free from lumps
6. Put it into a porcelain stew-pan and give it one boil up, stirring it nearly all the time
7. Put it into a freezer and freeze it in the usual manner
8. Transfer it to a pyramid mould and freeze it a second time for half an hour or more
9. When quite frozen, take it out of the mould upon a glass or china dish
