## Ingredients

- round clams
- hot water
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash clean as many round clams as required
2. pile them in a large iron pot, with half a cupful of hot water in the bottom, and put over the fire
3. as soon as the shells open take out the clams, cut off the hard, uneatable "fringe" from each with strong, clean scissors
4. put them into a stewpan with the broth from the pot, and boil slowly till they are quite tender
5. pepper well and thicken the gravy with flour stirred into melted butter
