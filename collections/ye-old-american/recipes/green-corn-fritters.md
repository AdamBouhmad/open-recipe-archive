## Ingredients

- one pint of grated, young and tender, green corn
- three eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of milk or cream
- one tablespoonful of melted butter
- one teaspoonful of salt
- flour
- one teaspoonful of baking powder
- hot lard
- butter and lard mixed

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well
2. Add the corn by degrees, also the milk and butter
3. Thicken with just enough flour to hold them together, adding a teaspoonful of baking powder to the flour
4. Have ready a kettle of hot lard
5. Drop the corn from the spoon into the fat and fry a light brown
6. They are also nice fried in butter and lard mixed, the same as fried eggs
