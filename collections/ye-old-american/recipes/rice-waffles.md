## Ingredients

- two gills of rice
- three gills of flour
- a little salt
- two ounces melted butter
- two eggs beaten well
- as much milk as will make it a thick batter

## Directions

1. Boil two gills of rice quite soft
2. Mix with it three gills of flour, a little salt, two ounces melted butter, two eggs beaten well, and as much milk as will make it a thick batter
3. Beat it till very light, and bake it in waffle irons
