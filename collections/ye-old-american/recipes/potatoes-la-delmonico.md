## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the potatoes with a vegetable cutter into small balls about the size of a marble.
2. Put them into a stewpan with plenty of butter and a good sprinkling of salt.
3. Keep the saucepan covered, and shake occasionally until they are quite done, which will be in about an hour.
