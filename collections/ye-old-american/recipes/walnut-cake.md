## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- Yolks 3 eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 1¾ cups flour
- 2½ teaspoons baking powder
- Whites 2 eggs
- ¾ cup walnut meat, broken in pieces

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients in order given.
2. Bake forty-five minutes in a moderate oven.
3. Cover with White Mountain Cream, crease in squares, and put one-half walnut on each square.
