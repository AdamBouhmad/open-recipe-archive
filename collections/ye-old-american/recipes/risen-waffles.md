## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 1 heaping quart flour
- 5 tablespoonfuls yeast
- 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful melted butter
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Set the mixture—minus the eggs and butter—over night as a sponge
2. Add these in the morning, and bake in waffle-irons
