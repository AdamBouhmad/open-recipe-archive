## Ingredients

- 1¾ cups milk
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ¼ yeast cake
- ¼ cup lukewarm water
- 2 cups flour
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Whites 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Scald milk; add salt and butter, and when lukewarm, add yeast cake dissolved in water, and flour
2. Beat well; let rise over night
3. Add yolks of eggs well beaten, and whites of eggs beaten stiff
4. Cook same as Waffles
5. By using a whole yeast cake, the mixture will rise in one and one-half hours
