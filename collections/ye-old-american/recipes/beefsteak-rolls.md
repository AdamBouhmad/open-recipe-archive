## Ingredients

- dressing for turkey or duck
- round steak
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- egg
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare a good dressing such as you like for turkey or duck
2. Take a round steak and pound it, but not very hard
3. Spread the dressing over it
4. Sprinkle in a little salt, pepper, and a few bits of butter
5. Lap over the ends and roll the steak up tightly
6. Tie closely
7. Spread two great spoonfuls of butter over the steak after rolling it up
8. Wash with a well-beaten egg
9. Put water in the bake-pan
10. Lay in the steak so as not to touch the water
11. Bake as you would a duck, basting often
12. A half-hour in a brisk oven will bake
13. Make a brown gravy and send to the table hot
