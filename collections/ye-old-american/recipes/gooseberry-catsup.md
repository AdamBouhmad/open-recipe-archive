## Ingredients

- ten pounds of fruit gathered just before ripening
- five pounds of sugar
- one quart of vinegar
- two tablespoonfuls of ground black pepper
- two tablespoonfuls of allspice
- two tablespoonfuls of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Boil the fruit in vinegar until reduced to a pulp, then add sugar and the other seasoning.
2. Seal it hot.
