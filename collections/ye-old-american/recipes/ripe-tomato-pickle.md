## Ingredients

- 2 gallons tomatoes, peeled, but not sliced
- 1 pint vinegar
- 2 lbs. sugar
- mace
- nutmeg
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put all on together, heat slowly to a boil, and simmer one hour.
2. Put up in glass jars.
