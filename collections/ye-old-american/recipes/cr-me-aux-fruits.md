## Ingredients

- ¼ box gelatine or 1 tablespoon granulated gelatine
- ¼ cup cold water
- ¼ cup scalded milk
- ½ cup sugar
- Whites of 2 eggs
- ½ pint thick cream
- ⅓ cup milk
- ⅓ cup cooked prunes, cut in pieces
- ⅓ cup chopped figs

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in cold water, dissolve in scalded milk, and add sugar.
2. Strain in pan set in ice-water, stir constantly, and when it begins to thicken add whites of eggs beaten stiff, cream (diluted with milk and beaten), prunes, and figs.
3. Mould and chill.
