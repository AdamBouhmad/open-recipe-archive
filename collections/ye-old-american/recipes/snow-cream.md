## Ingredients

- A quart of thick, sweet cream
- Three tablespoonfuls of cornstarch flour
- Cold cream
- The whites of six eggs

## Directions

1. Heat a quart of thick, sweet cream
2. When ready to boil, stir into it quickly three tablespoonfuls of cornstarch flour, blended with some cold cream
3. Sweeten to taste and allow it to boil gently, stirring for two or three minutes
4. Add quickly the whites of six eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
5. Do not allow it to boil up more than once after adding the eggs
6. Flavor with lemon, vanilla, bitter almond or grated lemon peel
7. Lay the snow thus formed quickly in rocky heaps on silver or glass dishes, or in shapes
8. Keep cold and serve iced
