## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of boiling water
- 1 cup of Indian meal
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- Salt to taste
- sugar and nutmeg (optional)

## Directions

1. Wet the meal and flour to a smooth paste
2. Stir into the water while it is actually boiling
3. Boil slowly one hour, stirring up well from the bottom
4. Season with salt to taste
5. Add sugar if desired, or pepper added to the salt if preferred
