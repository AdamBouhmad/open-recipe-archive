## Ingredients

- 1 cup cracker crumbs
- ⅓ cup butter
- ⅓ cup boiling water
- salt
- pepper
- powdered sage, summer savory, or marjoram

## Directions

1. Melt butter in water, and pour over crackers, to which seasonings have been added
