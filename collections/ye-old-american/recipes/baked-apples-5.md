## Ingredients

- Nice fruit
- White sugar

## Directions

1. Get nice fruit, a little tart and juicy, but not sour
2. Clean them nicely
3. Bake in a moderate oven for about an hour
4. When the skin cracks and the pulp breaks through in every direction they are done
5. Take out and serve with white sugar sprinkled over them
