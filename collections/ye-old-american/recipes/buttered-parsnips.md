## Ingredients

- parsnips
- three tablespoonfuls melted butter
- pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil tender and scrape.
2. Slice a quarter of an inch thick lengthwise.
3. Put into a saucepan with three tablespoonfuls melted butter, pepper and salt, and a little chopped parsley.
4. Shake over the fire until the mixture boils.
5. Lay the parsnips in order upon a dish, pour the sauce over them, and garnish with parsley.
6. It is a pleasant addition to this dish to stir a few spoonfuls of cream into the sauce after the parsnips are taken out; boil up, and pour upon them.
