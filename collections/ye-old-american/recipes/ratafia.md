## Ingredients

- a pound of shelled bitter almonds
- an ounce of nutmegs
- a pound of fine loaf-sugar
- one grain (apothecaries' weight) of ambergris
- a gallon of white brandy or proof spirit

## Directions

1. Pound in a mortar, and mix together the bitter almonds, nutmegs, loaf-sugar, and ambergris
2. Infuse these ingredients for a week in a gallon of white brandy or proof spirit
3. Filter it, and bottle it for use
