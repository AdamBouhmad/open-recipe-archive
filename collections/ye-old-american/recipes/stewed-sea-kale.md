## Ingredients

- sea-kale
- salt
- water
- rich gravy
- butter
- milk
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clip off the stems, wash well, tie in neat bunches, and when it has lain in cold water an hour or so, put into a saucepan of boiling water, slightly salted.
2. Boil fifteen minutes, drain well, clip the threads, and return to the saucepan, with a little rich gravy if you have it.
3. If not, pour in three or four tablespoonfuls of butter drawn in milk, pepper and salt, and simmer eight or ten minutes.
