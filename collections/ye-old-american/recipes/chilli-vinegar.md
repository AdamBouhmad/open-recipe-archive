## Ingredients

- a hundred red chillies or capsicums
- a quart of the best vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a hundred red chillies or capsicums, fresh gathered; cut them into small pieces and infuse them for a fortnight in a quart of the best vinegar shaking the bottle every day.
2. Then strain it.
