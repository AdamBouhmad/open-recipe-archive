## Ingredients

- one pound sugar
- one pound butter
- one pound flour
- one pound or ten eggs
- rose water one gill
- spices to your taste

## Directions

1. one pound sugar, one pound butter, one pound flour, one pound or ten eggs, rose water one gill, spices to your taste
2. watch it well
3. it will bake in a slow oven in 15 minutes
