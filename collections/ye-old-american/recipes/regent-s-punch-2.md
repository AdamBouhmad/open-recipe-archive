## Ingredients

- 1 lb. loaf-sugar or rock candy
- 1 large cup strong black tea—(made)
- 3 wineglasses of brandy
- 3 wineglasses of rum
- 1 bottle champagne
- 2 oranges—juice only
- 3 lemons—juice only
- 1 large lump of ice

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients with the large lump of ice
