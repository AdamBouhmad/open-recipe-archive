## Ingredients

- bread
- oysters
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- cream
- eggs
- flour
- water

## Directions

1. Grate a loaf of bread
2. Chop a score or more of oysters fine
3. Add nutmeg, pepper and salt to your taste
4. Mix it up into a light forcemeat with a quarter of a pound of butter, a spoonful or two of cream, and three eggs
5. Stuff the craw with it, and make the rest into balls and boil them
6. Sew up the turkey
7. Dredge it well with flour
8. Put it in a kettle of cold water
9. Cover it, and set it over the fire
10. As the scum begins to rise, take it off
11. Let it boil very slowly for half an hour
12. Take off your kettle and keep it closely covered
13. If it be of a middle size, let it stand in the hot water half an hour, with the steam being kept in, to stew it
14. Pour on a little oyster sauce when you dish it
15. Lay the balls round, and serve it up with the rest of the sauce in a boat
