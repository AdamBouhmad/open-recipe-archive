## Ingredients

- One quart of rich cream
- One scant pint of milk
- Six eggs
- Sugar
- Vanilla for flavoring
- One full ounce of Cox's gelatine
- A very little water
- Buttered paper
- Sponge cake or lady-fingers

## Directions

1. Whip one quart of rich cream to a stiff froth and drain well on a nice sieve
2. To one scant pint of milk add six eggs beaten very light
3. Make very sweet and flavor high with vanilla
4. Cook over hot water till it is a thick custard
5. Soak one full ounce of Cox's gelatine in a very little water and warm over hot water
6. When the custard is very cold beat in lightly the gelatine and the whipped cream
7. Line the bottom of mold with buttered paper
8. Line the side with sponge cake or lady-fingers fastened together with the white of an egg
9. Fill with the cream
10. Put in a cold place or on ice in summer
11. To turn out, dip the mold for a moment in hot water
