## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ¾ cup butter
- 1 egg yolk
- ¾ teaspoon water

## Directions

1. Mix and sift two cups flour and one-half cup brown sugar.
2. Wash three-fourths cup butter and work into first mixture, using tips of fingers.
3. Roll to one-third inch in thickness, shape with a fluted round cutter five inches in diameter.
4. Cut each piece in quarters and crease with the dull edge of a case knife to represent folds of a fan.
5. Brush over with yolk of egg diluted with three-fourths teaspoon water.
6. Bake in a slow oven.
