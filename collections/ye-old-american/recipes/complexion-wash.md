## Ingredients

- one drachm of benzoin gum in powder
- one drachm nutmeg oil
- six drops of orange-blossom tea
- half pint of rain-water
- one pint of sherry wine

## Directions

1. Put in a vial one drachm of benzoin gum in powder, one drachm nutmeg oil, six drops of orange-blossom tea, or apple blossoms put in half pint of rain-water and boiled down to one teaspoonful and strained, one pint of sherry wine
2. Bathe the face morning and night
