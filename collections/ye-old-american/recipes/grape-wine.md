## Ingredients

- grapes
- water
- three pounds of sugar to one gallon of the mixture

## Directions

1. Mash the grapes and strain them through a cloth
2. Put the skins in a tub, after squeezing them, with barely enough water to cover them
3. Strain the juice thus obtained into the first portion
4. Put three pounds of sugar to one gallon of the mixture
5. Let it stand in an open tub to ferment, covered with a cloth, for a period of from three to seven days
6. Skim off what rises every morning
7. Put the juice in a cask and leave it open for twenty-four hours
8. Bung it up and put clay over the bung to keep the air out
9. Let your wine remain in the cask until March, when it should be drawn off and bottled
