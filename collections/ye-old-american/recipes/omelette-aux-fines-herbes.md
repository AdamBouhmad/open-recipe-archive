## Ingredients

- eggs
- milk
- butter
- 2 or 3 tablespoonfuls chopped parsley
- green thyme
- sweet marjoram
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. After the yolks and whites are mixed together with the milk, stir in, with two or three strokes of the spoon or whisk, two tablespoonfuls of chopped parsley, green thyme, and sweet marjoram, with pepper and salt
2. Fry instantly
