## Ingredients

- two quarts of rye
- two quarts of Indian meal
- three pints of milk
- two tea-spoonfuls of salt
- half a pint of good fresh yeast

## Directions

1. Sift the rye and Indian meal
2. Mix them well together
3. Boil the milk
4. Pour it boiling hot upon the meal
5. Add two tea-spoonfuls of salt
6. Stir the whole very hard
7. Let it stand till it becomes of only a lukewarm heat
8. Stir in half a pint of good fresh yeast
9. Knead the mixture into a stiff dough
10. Set it to rise in a pan
11. Cover it with a thick cloth that has been previously warmed, and set it near the fire
