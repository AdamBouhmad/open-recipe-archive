## Ingredients

- Puff or plain paste
- Canned peaches
- Powdered sugar
- Brandy

## Directions

1. Roll puff or plain paste one-eighth inch thick, cut in two and one-half inch squares, and bake in hot oven.
2. Cool, press down the centres, and arrange on each one-half a canned peach drained from syrup and heated in oven.
3. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and put brandy in each cavity.
4. Light just before sending to table.
