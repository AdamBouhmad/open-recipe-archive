## Ingredients

- 2 cups stale bread crumbs
- 1 quart scalded milk
- ⅓ cup sugar
- ¼ cup melted butter
- 2 eggs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla or ¼ teaspoon spice

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs in milk, set aside until cool
2. Add sugar, butter, eggs slightly beaten, salt, and flavoring
3. Bake one hour in buttered pudding-dish in slow oven
4. Serve with Vanilla Sauce
5. In preparing bread crumbs for puddings avoid using outside crusts
6. With a coarse grater there need be but little waste
