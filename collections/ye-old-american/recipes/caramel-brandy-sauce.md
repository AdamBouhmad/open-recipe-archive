## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoon brandy
- Yolks 2 eggs
- Whites 2 eggs
- ½ cup milk or cream

## Directions

1. Make same as Brandy Sauce, substituting brown sugar in place of powdered sugar.
