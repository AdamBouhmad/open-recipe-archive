## Ingredients

- 3 cups white Indian meal
- 1 cup white flour
- 1 tablespoonful butter, melted and added in the morning
- 1 quart milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls of yeast
- 1 teaspoonful soda, dissolved in hot water, and added in the morning
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Mix over night
