## Ingredients

- Haddock, 4 pounds
- Salt and pepper
- Fish Stuffing
- Fat salt pork, narrow strips
- Fat salt pork, small pieces
- Butter, melted
- Flour
- Drawn Butter, Egg or Hollandaise Sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the haddock, sprinkle with salt inside and out, stuff, and sew.
2. Cut five diagonal gashes on each side of backbone and insert narrow strips of fat salt pork, having gashes on one side come between gashes on other side.
3. Shape with skewers in form of letter S, and fasten skewers with small twine.
4. Place on greased fish-sheet in a dripping-pan.
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
6. Brush over with melted butter.
7. Dredge with flour.
8. Place around fish small pieces of fat salt pork.
9. Bake one hour in hot oven, basting as soon as fat is tried out, and continue basting every ten minutes.
10. Serve with Drawn Butter, Egg or Hollandaise Sauce.
