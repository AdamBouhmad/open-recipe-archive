## Ingredients

- Celery
- Salt

## Directions

1. Scrape and wash it well and let it lie in cold water till shortly before it goes to table
2. Dry it in a cloth, trim it, and split down the stalks almost to the bottom, leaving on a few green leaves
3. Send it to table in a celery glass and eat it with salt only, or chop it fine and make a salad dressing for it
