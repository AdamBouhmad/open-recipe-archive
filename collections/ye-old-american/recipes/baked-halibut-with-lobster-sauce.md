## Ingredients

- three-pound halibut
- fat salt pork
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- water
- sprig of parsley
- slice of onion
- two slices carrot
- bit of bay leaf
- one-fourth cup butter
- Lobster Sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the halibut.
2. Cut gashes in top, and insert a narrow strip of fat salt pork in each gash.
3. Place in dripping pan on fish-sheet, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and dredge with flour.
4. Cover bottom of pan with water, add sprig of parsley, slice of onion, two slices carrot cut in pieces, and bit of bay leaf.
5. Bake one hour, basting with one-fourth cup butter and the liquor in pan.
6. Serve with Lobster Sauce.
