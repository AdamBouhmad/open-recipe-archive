## Ingredients

- ¾ cup corn meal
- 1¼ cups flour
- ¼ cup sugar
- 5 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 or 2 tablespoons melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix and sift dry ingredients.
2. Add milk, egg well beaten, and butter; bake in shallowed buttered pan in hot oven twenty minutes.
