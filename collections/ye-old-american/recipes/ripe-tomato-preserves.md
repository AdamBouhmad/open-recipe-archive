## Ingredients

- round yellow or egg tomatoes
- sugar
- lemons

## Directions

1. Let them stand together over night.
2. Drain off the syrup and boil it, skimming well.
3. Put in the tomatoes and boil gently twenty minutes.
4. Take out the fruit with a perforated skimmer, and spread upon dishes.
5. Boil the syrup down until it thickens, adding, just before you take it up, the juice of three lemons.
6. Put the fruit into the jars and fill up with hot syrup.
7. When cold, seal or tie up.
