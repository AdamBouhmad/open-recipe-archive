## Ingredients

- two and one-half pounds sour apples
- one and one-half pounds light brown sugar
- juice and rind of one and one-half lemons
- one-half ounce ginger root
- a few grains salt
- water

## Directions

1. Wipe, quarter, core, pare, and chop sour apples; there should be two and one-half pounds.
2. Put in a stewpan and add one and one-half pounds light brown sugar, juice and rind of one and one-half lemons, one-half ounce ginger root, a few grains salt, and enough water to prevent apples from burning.
3. Cover, and cook slowly four hours, adding water as necessary.
