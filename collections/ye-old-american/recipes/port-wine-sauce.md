## Ingredients

- 1 cup Brown Sauce, from which onion has been omitted
- one-eighth tumbler currant jelly
- 2 tablespoons Port wine
- Few grains cayenne

## Directions

1. To one cup Brown Sauce, from which onion has been omitted, add one-eighth tumbler currant jelly, two tablespoons Port wine, and a few grains cayenne.
