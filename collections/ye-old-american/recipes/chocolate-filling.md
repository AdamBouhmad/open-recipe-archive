## Ingredients

- 2½ squares chocolate
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 3 tablespoons milk
- Yolk 1 egg
- ½ teaspoon vanilla

## Directions

1. Melt chocolate over hot water, add one-half the sugar, and milk; add remaining sugar, and yolk of egg; then cook in double boiler until it thickens, stirring constantly at first, that mixture may be perfectly smooth.
2. Cool slightly, flavor, and spread.
