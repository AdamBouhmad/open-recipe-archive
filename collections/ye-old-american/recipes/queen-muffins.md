## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- ¾ cup of yeast
- 2 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of lard or butter
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- Flour to make a good batter
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Set the batter—leaving out the eggs—to rise over night
2. In the morning beat the eggs very light
3. Stir into the batter
4. Bake in muffin-rings twenty minutes in a quick oven
