## Ingredients

- winter squash
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut open the squash, take out the seeds and without paring cut it up into large pieces.
2. Put the pieces on tins or in a dripping-pan, place in a moderately hot oven and bake about an hour.
3. When done, peel and mash like mashed potatoes, or serve the pieces hot on a dish, to be eaten warm with butter like sweet potatoes.
