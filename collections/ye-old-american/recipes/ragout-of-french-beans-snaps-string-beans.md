## Ingredients

- young fresh French beans
- boiling water
- well seasoned brown gravy
- forcemeat balls

## Directions

1. Let them be young and fresh gathered
2. String them, and cut them in long thin slices
3. Throw them in boiling water for fifteen minutes
4. Have ready some well seasoned brown gravy
5. Drain the water from the beans
6. Put them in the gravy
7. Stew them a few minutes, and serve them garnished with forcemeat balls
8. There must not be gravy enough to float the beans
