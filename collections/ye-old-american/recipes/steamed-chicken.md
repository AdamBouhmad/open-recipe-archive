## Ingredients

- chicken
- pepper
- salt
- gravy
- cayenne pepper
- flour
- cream
- cornstarch
- nutmeg
- celery salt

## Directions

1. Rub the chicken on the inside with pepper and half a teaspoonful of salt
2. place in a steamer in a kettle that will keep it as near the water as possible
3. cover and steam an hour and a half
4. when done, keep hot while dressing is prepared
5. cut up and arrange on the platter
6. For the dressing: Boil one pint of gravy from the kettle without the fat
7. add cayenne pepper and half a teaspoonful of salt
8. stir a tablespoonful of flour into a quarter of a pint of cream until smooth
9. add to the gravy
10. Cornstarch may be used instead of the flour, and some cooks add nutmeg or celery salt
11. serve with the dressing over it
