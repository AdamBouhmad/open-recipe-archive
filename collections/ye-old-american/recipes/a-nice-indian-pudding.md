## Ingredients

- 3 pints scalded milk
- 7 spoons fine Indian meal
- 7 eggs
- half pound raisins
- 4 ounces butter
- spice and sugar

## Directions

1. stir well together while hot
2. let stand till cooled
3. add 7 eggs
4. half pound raisins
5. 4 ounces butter
6. spice and sugar
7. bake one and half hour
