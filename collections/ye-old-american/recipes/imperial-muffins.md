## Ingredients

- 1 cup scalded milk
- ¼ cup sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1¾ cups flour
- 1 cup corn meal
- ¼ cup butter
- ⅓ yeast cake dissolved in ¼ cup lukewarm water

## Directions

1. Add sugar and salt to milk; when lukewarm add dissolved yeast cake, and one and one-fourth cups flour
2. Cover, and let rise until light, then add corn meal, remaining flour, and butter
3. Let rise over night; in the morning fill buttered muffin rings two-thirds full; let rise until rings are full and bake thirty minutes in hot oven
