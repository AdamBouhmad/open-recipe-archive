## Ingredients

- wild pigeons
- bacon
- boiled eggs
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut the pigeons into four pieces each, and do not stuff them.
2. Parboil and lay in the dish in alternate layers with the bacon and boiled eggs.
3. Make the gravy richer than for the quails, by the addition of a good lump of butter, rolled in flour, stirred in and boiled up to thicken before you put it on the fire.
