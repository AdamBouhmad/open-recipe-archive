## Ingredients

- two pounds of lean pork
- two pounds of lean veal
- two pounds of fresh lean beef
- two pounds of fat salt pork
- one pound of beef suet
- ten tablespoonfuls of powdered sage
- one ounce each of parsley, savory, marjoram and thyme mixed
- two teaspoonfuls of cayenne pepper
- two teaspoonfuls of black pepper
- one grated nutmeg
- one teaspoonful of cloves
- one minced onion
- salt to taste
- beef skins
- hot water
- oil or melted butter
- ginger or pepper
- sliced lemons

## Directions

1. Chop or grind the meat and suet
2. Season the mixture
3. Stuff into beef skins and tie these up
4. Prick each in several places to allow the escape of steam
5. Put into hot, not boiling, water and heat gradually to the boiling point
6. Cook slowly for one hour
7. Take out the skins and lay them to dry in the sun, upon clean sweet straw or hay
8. Rub the outside of the skins with oil or melted butter
9. Place in a cool, dry cellar
10. If you wish to keep them more than a week, rub ginger or pepper on the outside then wash it off before using
11. Cut in round slices and lay sliced lemons around the edge of the dish
