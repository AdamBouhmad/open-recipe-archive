## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound of butter
- one pound of sifted flour
- one pound of powdered sugar
- a large table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon
- three well beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Rub three quarters of a pound of butter into a pound of sifted flour
2. Mix in a pound of powdered sugar and a large table-spoonful of powdered cinnamon
3. Mix it into a dough with three well beaten eggs
4. Roll it out into a sheet
5. Cut it into round cakes
6. Bake them in a quick oven for a few minutes
