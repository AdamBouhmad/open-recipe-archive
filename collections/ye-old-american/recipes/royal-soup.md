## Ingredients

- 1 cup stale bread crumbs
- ½ cup milk
- Yolks 3 hard-boiled eggs
- Breast meat from a boiled chicken
- Salt and pepper
- 1½ cups scalded milk
- 3½ cups White Stock III
- 2½ tablespoons butter
- 2½ tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Soak bread crumbs in milk, add yolks of eggs rubbed through a sieve and chicken meat also rubbed through a sieve.
2. Add gradually milk, and chicken stock highly seasoned.
3. Bind with butter and flour cooked together, and season with salt and pepper.
