## Ingredients

- veal, fat and lean
- slices of boiled ham
- salt
- pepper
- grated nutmeg
- parsley, minced fine
- thyme, minced fine
- gravy

## Directions

1. Chop veal and ham very fine
2. Season with salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, and a small quantity of parsley and thyme minced very fine
3. Make a paste with a little gravy
4. Cover the bottoms of small moulds with paste
5. Fill them with the meat
6. Put thin lids on
7. Bake them crisp
