## Ingredients

- one pound of cocoanut, grated
- half a pound of powdered white sugar
- the milk of the cocoanut, or a pint of cream
- two tablespoonfuls of rose-water
- a pint of rich milk
- whites of eight eggs
- a teaspoonful of powdered nutmeg and cinnamon
- loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Grate as much cocoanut as will weigh a pound
2. Mix half a pound of powdered white sugar with the milk of the cocoanut, or with a pint of cream, adding two tablespoonfuls of rose-water
3. Stir in gradually a pint of rich milk
4. Beat to a stiff froth the whites of eight eggs
5. Stir them into the milk and sugar, a little at a time, alternately with the grated cocoanut
6. Add a teaspoonful of powdered nutmeg and cinnamon
7. Put the mixture into cups and bake them twenty minutes in a moderate oven, set in a pan half filled with boiling water
8. When cold, grate loaf sugar over them
