## Ingredients

- One full coffeecupful of ground coffee
- One egg and part of the shell
- Half cupful of cold water
- One quart of boiling water
- Good cream
- Lump sugar
- Three-quarters of a pound of Java coffee
- One quarter of a pound of Mocha coffee

## Directions

1. Stir the ground coffee with one egg and part of the shell, adding a half cupful of cold water
2. Put it into the coffee boiler, and pour on to it a quart of boiling water
3. As it rises and begins to boil, stir it down with a silver spoon or fork
4. Boil hard for ten or twelve minutes
5. Remove from the fire and pour out a cupful of coffee, then pour back into the coffeepot
6. Place it on the back of the stove or range where it will keep hot and not boil
7. It will settle in about five minutes
8. Send to the table hot
9. Serve with good cream and lump sugar
