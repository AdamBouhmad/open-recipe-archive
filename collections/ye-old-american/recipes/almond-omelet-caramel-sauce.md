## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoons caramel sauce
- Few grains salt
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- ¾ tablespoon butter
- shredded almonds

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of eggs until thick and lemon-colored.
2. Add caramel, salt, and vanilla.
3. Cut and fold in whites of eggs beaten until stiff and dry.
4. Put three-fourths tablespoon butter in a hot omelet pan.
5. Cover bottom of pan with shredded almonds.
6. Turn in mixture, and cook and fold same as Plain Omelet.
7. For Caramel Sauce: Pour one cup sugar in omelet pan, and stir constantly, over hot part of range, until melted to a light brown syrup.
8. Add three-fourths cup hot water, and let simmer ten minutes.
