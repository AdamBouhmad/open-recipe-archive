## Ingredients

- egg plant
- yelk of an egg
- grated bread
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. parboil them to take off the bitter taste
2. cut them in slices an inch thick, but do not peel them
3. dip them in the yelk of an egg
4. cover them with grated bread, a little salt and pepper
5. when this has dried, cover the other side the same way
6. fry them a nice brown
