## Ingredients

- 12 medium-sized tomatoes, peeled and sliced
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 raw egg, well beaten
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- ¼ spoonful cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoonful white sugar
- 1 tablespoonful salad oil
- 2 teaspoonfuls made mustard
- 1 teacupful vinegar

## Directions

1. Rub the yolks to a smooth paste, adding by degrees the salt, pepper, sugar, mustard, and oil.
2. Beat the raw egg to a froth and stir in—lastly the vinegar.
3. Peel the tomatoes, slice them a quarter of an inch thick, and set the dish on ice, while you are making ready the dressing.
4. Stir a great lump of ice rapidly in this—the dressing—until it is cold; take it out, cover the tomatoes with the mixture, and set back on the ice until you send to table.
