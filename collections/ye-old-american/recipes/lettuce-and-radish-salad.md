## Ingredients

- Lettuce
- Six radishes
- Round radishes for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare and arrange as for Dressed Lettuce.
2. Place between leaves six radishes which have been washed, scraped, and cut in thin slices.
3. Garnish with round radishes cut to represent tulips. See page 299.
4. Serve with French Dressing.
