## Ingredients

- well flavoured apples
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of powdered sugar
- rinds of two lemons
- six eggs
- paste
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take well flavoured apples, bake, but do not burn them
2. Rub them through a sieve
3. Take one pound of the apples so prepared
4. Mix with it, while hot, half a pound of butter, and half a pound of powdered sugar
5. Add the rinds of two lemons grated
6. When cold, add six eggs well beaten
7. Put a paste in the bottom of a dish, and pour in the apples
8. Half an hour will bake it
9. Sift a little sugar on the apples when baked
