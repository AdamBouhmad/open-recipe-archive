## Ingredients

- 1 lb. butter
- 1 lb. sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 lb. flour, or enough to make out a soft dough
- Wineglass (small) rose-water
- Buttered paper

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar, add the beaten yolks, then the rose-water.
2. Add next half the flour.
3. Add lastly the whites, stirred in very lightly, alternately with the remaining flour.
4. Have ready a pan, broad and shallow, lined on the bottom with buttered paper.
5. With a tablespoon form regular rings of the dough upon this, leaving a hole in the centre of each.
6. Bake quickly, and sift fine sugar over them as soon as they are done.
7. You may substitute lemon or vanilla for the rose-water.
