## Ingredients

- 1 quart of flour
- 3 eggs—the whites and yolks beaten separately and until stiff
- 3 cups of milk. If sour, no disadvantage, if soda be added
- A little salt

## Directions

1. The excellence of these depends upon thorough beating and quick baking
