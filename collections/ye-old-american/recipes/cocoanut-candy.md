## Ingredients

- one cocoanut
- one and one-half pounds of granulated sugar
- milk of cocoanut
- finely grated cocoanut

## Directions

1. Put sugar and milk of cocoanut together
2. Beat slowly until the sugar is melted
3. Boil five minutes
4. Add cocoanut (finely grated), boil ten minutes longer
5. Stir constantly to keep from burning
6. Pour on buttered plates
7. Cut in squares
8. Will take about two days to harden
