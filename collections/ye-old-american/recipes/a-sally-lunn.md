## Ingredients

- a pound and a half of flour
- two ounces of butter
- a pint of milk
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- three well-beaten eggs
- two table-spoonfuls of the best fresh yeast

## Directions

1. Sift into a pan a pound and a half of flour
2. Make a hole in the middle, and put in two ounces of butter warmed in a pint of milk, a salt-spoonful of salt, three well-beaten eggs, and two table-spoonfuls of the best fresh yeast
3. Mix the flour well into the other ingredients, and put the whole into a square tin pan that has been greased with butter
4. Cover it, set it in a warm place, and when it is quite light, bake it in a moderate oven
5. Send it to table hot, and eat it with butter
