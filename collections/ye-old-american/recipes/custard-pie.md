## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 quart of milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls white sugar
- Flavor with vanilla or other essence

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and sugar light, and mix with the milk; flavor, whip in the whites, which should be already a stiff froth, mix well, and pour into shells.
2. Grate nutmeg upon the top.
3. Bake this as cup-custard, or a custard pudding, in cups or a deep dish set in a pan of boiling water.
