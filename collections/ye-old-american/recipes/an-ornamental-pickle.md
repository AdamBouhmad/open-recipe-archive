## Ingredients

- fresh eggs
- cold water
- red beets
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil fresh eggs half an hour
2. Put them in cold water
3. Boil red beets until tender, peel and cut in dice form
4. Cover beets with vinegar, spiced
5. Shell the eggs and drop into the pickle jar
