## Ingredients

- fillet
- bacon
- gravy
- sorrel
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut slices from the fillet an inch thick and six inches long
2. Lard them with slips of lean middling of bacon
3. Bake them a light brown
4. Stew them in well seasoned gravy, made as thick as rich cream
5. Serve them up hot
6. And lay round the dish sorrel stewed with butter, pepper and salt, till quite dry
