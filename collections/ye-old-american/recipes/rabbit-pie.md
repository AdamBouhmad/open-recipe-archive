## Ingredients

- A pair of rabbits
- Salted water
- A quarter of a pound of fat pork
- Four eggs
- Pepper
- Butter
- Powdered mace
- Lemon-juice
- Puff-paste
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cut a pair of rabbits into eight pieces each, soak in salted water half an hour, and stew until half done in enough water to cover them.
2. Cut a quarter of a pound of fat pork into slips, and boil four eggs hard.
3. Lay some bits of pork in the bottom of a deep dish and upon these a layer of the rabbit.
4. Upon this spread slices of boiled egg, peppered and buttered.
5. Sprinkle with a little powdered mace, and squeeze a few drops of lemon-juice upon each piece of meat.
6. Proceed in this order until the dish is full, the top layer being pork.
7. Pour in the water in which the rabbit was boiled, when you have salted it and added some lumps of butter rolled in flour.
8. Cover with puff-paste, cut a slit in the middle, and bake one hour, laying paper over the top should it brown too fast.
