## Ingredients

- one and one-half pounds of salmon
- half a cupful of water
- a little salt
- a little white pepper
- one clove
- one blade of mace
- three pieces of sugar
- one shallot
- a heaping teaspoonful of mustard
- half a teacupful of vinegar
- six tomatoes
- a few sprigs of parsley
- one wine-glassful of sherry

## Directions

1. Cut one and one-half pounds of salmon into pieces one inch square
2. Put the pieces in a stewpan with half a cupful of water, a little salt, a little white pepper, one clove, one blade of mace, three pieces of sugar, one shallot and a heaping teaspoonful of mustard mixed smoothly with half a teacupful of vinegar
3. Let this boil up once and add six tomatoes peeled and cut into tiny pieces, a few sprigs of parsley finely minced, and one wine-glassful of sherry
4. Let all simmer gently for three-quarters of an hour
5. Serve very hot, and garnish with dry toast cut in triangular pieces
