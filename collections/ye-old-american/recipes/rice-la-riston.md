## Ingredients

- 2 thin slices bacon
- 1/2 raw medium-sized cabbage
- 1/4 cup rice, boiled
- 1/2 teaspoon chopped parsley
- salt and pepper
- 1/2 cup White Stock

## Directions

1. Finely chop two thin slices bacon
2. Add to one-half raw medium-sized cabbage, finely chopped
3. Cover, and cook slowly thirty minutes
4. Add one-fourth cup rice, boiled
5. Add one-half teaspoon chopped parsley
6. Add salt and pepper to taste
7. Moisten with one-half cup White Stock
8. Cook fifteen minutes
