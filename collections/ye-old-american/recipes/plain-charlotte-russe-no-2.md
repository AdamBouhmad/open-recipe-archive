## Ingredients

- thin slices of sponge cake
- wine
- whites of three eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of finely powdered sugar
- a glass of sweet wine
- one pint of thick sweet cream

## Directions

1. Put some thin slices of sponge cake in the bottom of a glass sauce dish
2. Pour in wine enough to soak it
3. Beat up the whites of three eggs until very light
4. Add to it three tablespoonfuls of finely powdered sugar, a glass of sweet wine and one pint of thick sweet cream
5. Beat it well and pour over the cake
6. Set it in a cold place until served
