## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- three pounds of flour
- half a pint of good yeast
- six eggs
- half a pint of milk
- six ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Rub half a pound of sugar into three pounds of flour
2. Sift it
3. Pour on half a pint of good yeast
4. Beat six eggs, add half a pint of milk
5. Mix all together
6. Knead it well; if not soft enough, add more milk—it should be softer than bread
7. Make it at night
8. In the morning, if well risen, work in six ounces of butter
9. Bake it in small rolls
10. When cold, slice it, lay it on tin sheets
11. Dry it in the oven
