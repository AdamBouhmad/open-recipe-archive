## Ingredients

- entrails of fat full grown fowls
- liver
- gizzard
- other giblets
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- dripping or butter
- six eggs

## Directions

1. Take the entrails of fat full grown fowls
2. Empty them of their contents
3. Open them with a sharp knife
4. Scrape off the inner coat
5. Wash them clean
6. Put them on to boil with the liver, gizzard, and other giblets
7. Add salt, pepper, and chopped onion
8. When quite tender, set them by to cool
9. Put some nice dripping or butter in a pan
10. When it boils put the giblets
11. Add salt and fry them a nice brown
12. When nearly done, break six eggs in a bowl
13. Beat them a little
14. Pour them over the giblets
15. Stir them for a few minutes
16. Serve them up
