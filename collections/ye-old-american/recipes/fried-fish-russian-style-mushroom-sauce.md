## Ingredients

- two slices chicken halibut
- salt
- pepper
- one-third cup white wine
- heavy cream
- flour
- deep fat
- five slices carrot
- two slices onion
- sprig parsley
- bit of bay leaf
- one-fourth teaspoon peppercorns
- two cups cold water
- two tablespoons butter
- three tablespoons flour
- one-third cup heavy cream
- yolks two eggs
- cayenne
- one-half pound mushroom caps
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut two slices chicken halibut in fillets
2. Sprinkle fillets with salt and pepper
3. Pour over one-third cup white wine
4. Cover, and let stand thirty minutes
5. Drain
6. Dip each piece separately in heavy cream, then in flour
7. Fry in deep fat
8. Cook skin and bones removed from fish with five slices carrot, two slices onion, sprig parsley, bit of bay leaf, one-fourth teaspoon peppercorns, and two cups cold water until reduced to one cup liquid
9. Make sauce of two tablespoons butter, three tablespoons flour, the fish stock, and one-third cup heavy cream
10. Add yolks two eggs, salt, pepper, cayenne, and white wine to taste
11. Arrange fish on serving dish
12. Cover with one-half pound mushroom caps cleaned, then sautéd in butter
13. Pour over sauce
