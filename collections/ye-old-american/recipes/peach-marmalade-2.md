## Ingredients

- ripe yellow free-stone peaches
- powdered loaf sugar
- bitter almonds or peach-kernels
- water

## Directions

1. Pare, stone, and quarter the peaches
2. To each pound of peaches allow three quarters of a pound of powdered loaf sugar and half an ounce of bitter almonds or peach-kernels
3. Blanch the almonds or kernels in scalding water and pound them smooth in a mortar
4. Scald the peaches in a very little water and mash them to a pulp
5. Mix them with the sugar and pounded almonds
6. Put the whole into a preserving kettle
7. Let it boil to a smooth thick jam, skimming and stirring it well and keeping the pan covered as much as possible
8. Fifteen minutes will generally suffice for boiling it
9. When cold, put it up in glass jars
