## Ingredients

- peaches
- water
- 7 lbs. of fruit per measure
- 3 lbs. of sugar per 7 lbs. of fruit
- 3 pints of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful each of allspice, mace, and cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful celery-seed
- 1 teaspoonful cloves

## Directions

1. Rub the fur off with a coarse cloth, and prick each peach with a fork.
2. Heat in just enough water to cover them until they almost boil; take them out.
3. Add to the water sugar in the following proportions: for every 7 lbs. of fruit, 3 lbs. of sugar.
4. Boil fifteen minutes; skim.
5. Add 3 pints of vinegar and 1 tablespoonful (each) of allspice, mace, and cinnamon, 1 teaspoonful celery-seed, and 1 teaspoonful cloves.
6. Put the spices in thin muslin bags.
7. Boil all together ten minutes, then put in the fruit, and boil until they can be pierced with a straw.
