## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- ¾ cup sugar
- Yolks 7 eggs
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 2½ cups thin cream
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon lemon
- Angel Food

## Directions

1. Make custard of milk, sugar, eggs, and salt
2. Cool, strain, and flavor
3. Whip cream, remove whip; there should be two quarts
4. Add to custard
5. Freeze
6. Serve plain or with Angel Food
