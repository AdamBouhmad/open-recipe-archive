## Ingredients

- 1½ cups flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup lard
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Mix salt with flour
2. Reserve one and one-fourth tablespoons lard
3. Work in remainder to flour, using tips of fingers or a case knife
4. Moisten to a dough with water
5. Toss on a floured board
6. Pat and roll out
7. Spread with one tablespoon reserved lard
8. Dredge with flour
9. Roll up like a jelly roll
10. Pat and roll out
11. Again roll up
12. Cut from the end of roll a piece large enough to line a pie plate
13. Pat and roll out, keeping the paste as circular in form as possible
14. Use remaining one-fourth tablespoon lard to dot over upper crust of pie just before sending to oven
