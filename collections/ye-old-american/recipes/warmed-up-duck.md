## Ingredients

- roast duck
- butter
- water
- cold gravy
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the bones, pick out all the little tidbits in the recesses
2. Lay them in a frying pan, and cover with water and the cold gravy left from the roast
3. Add a piece of butter
4. Let all boil up once and if not quite thick enough, stir in a little dissolved flour
5. Serve hot
