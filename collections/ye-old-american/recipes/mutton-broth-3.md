## Ingredients

- three pounds mutton from the neck
- two quarts cold water
- one teaspoon salt
- pepper
- three tablespoons rice or three tablespoons barley

## Directions

1. Wipe meat and remove skin and fat
2. Cut in small pieces
3. Put into kettle with bones and cover with cold water
4. Heat gradually to boiling-point and skim
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Cook slowly until meat is tender
7. Strain and remove fat
8. Reheat to boiling-point
9. Add rice or barley and cook until tender
10. If barley is used, soak over night in cold water
11. Some of the meat may be served with the broth
