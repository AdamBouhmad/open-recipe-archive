## Ingredients

- 1 quart cream
- 2 lemons—the juice of one and the grated peel of one and a half
- 2 cups of sugar

## Directions

1. Sweeten the cream, beat the lemon gradually into it, and put at once into the freezer
2. Freeze rapidly in a patent freezer, or the acid is apt to turn the milk
3. You may make orange ice-cream in the same way
