## Ingredients

- a stale loaf
- a pint of boiling milk
- six eggs
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of powdered sugar
- half a nutmeg
- a glass of brandy
- grated lemon peel
- paste

## Directions

1. Grate the crumb of a stale loaf, and pour on it a pint of boiling milk
2. Let it stand an hour, then beat it to a pulp
3. Add six eggs, well beaten, half a pound of butter, the same of powdered sugar, half a nutmeg, a glass of brandy, and some grated lemon peel
4. Put a paste in the dish, and bake it
