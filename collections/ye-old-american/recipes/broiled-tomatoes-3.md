## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- crumbs
- egg
- butter

## Directions

1. Wipe and cut in halves crosswise, cut off a thin slice from rounding part of each half.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, dip in crumbs, egg, and crumbs again.
3. Place in a well-buttered broiler, and broil six to eight minutes.
