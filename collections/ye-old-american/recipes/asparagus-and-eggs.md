## Ingredients

- Twenty-five or thirty heads of asparagus
- Cupful of rich drawn butter
- Pepper
- Salt
- Five or six eggs
- Yolks and whites of eggs
- Three tablespoonfuls of milk or cream

## Directions

1. Cut twenty-five or thirty heads of asparagus into bits half an inch long, and boil fifteen minutes.
2. Have a cupful of rich drawn butter in a saucepan, and put in the asparagus when you have drained it dry.
3. Heat together to a boil, seasoning with pepper and salt, and pour into a buttered bake dish.
4. Beat the eggs—yolks and whites separately—to a froth.
5. Season with butter, pepper, and salt.
6. Stir them together, with the addition of three tablespoonfuls of milk or cream, and pour evenly over the asparagus mixture in the dish.
7. Put in the oven until the eggs are set.
