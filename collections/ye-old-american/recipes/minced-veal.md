## Ingredients

- cold veal
- pepper
- salt
- sweet marjoram
- lemon-peel, grated
- nutmeg
- bones and trimmings
- water
- butter rolled in flour
- milk or cream
- bread, three-cornered pieces, toasted and buttered

## Directions

1. Take some cold veal, cut it into slices, and mince it very finely with a chopping-knife.
2. Season it to your taste with pepper, salt, sweet marjoram rubbed fine, grated lemon-peel and nutmeg.
3. Put the bones and trimmings into a sauce-pan with a little water, and simmer them over hot coals to extract the gravy from them.
4. Put the minced veal into a stew-pan, strain the gravy over it, add a piece of butter rolled in flour, and a little milk or cream.
5. Let it all simmer together till thoroughly warmed, but do not allow it to boil lest the meat having been once cooked already, should become tasteless.
6. When you serve it up, have ready some three-cornered pieces of bread toasted and buttered; place them all round the inside of the dish.
