## Ingredients

- 1 cup molasses
- 1 cup sour milk
- 2⅓ cups flour
- 1¾ teaspoons soda
- 2 teaspoons ginger
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup melted butter

## Directions

1. Mix soda with sour milk and add to molasses.
2. Sift together remaining dry ingredients.
3. Combine mixtures and add butter.
4. Beat vigorously.
5. Pour into a buttered shallow pan.
6. Bake twenty-five minutes in a moderate oven.
