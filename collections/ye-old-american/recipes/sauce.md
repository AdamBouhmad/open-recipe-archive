## Ingredients

- One and one-half tablespoons butter
- Three tablespoons flour
- One-third cup oyster liquor
- Two tablespoons cream
- Salt
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Melt butter, add flour, and gradually one-third cup oyster liquor and cream.
2. Season with salt and cayenne.
