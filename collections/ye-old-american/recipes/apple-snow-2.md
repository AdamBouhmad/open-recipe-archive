## Ingredients

- Whites 3 eggs
- ¾ cup apple pulp
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pare, quarter, and core four sour apples, steam until soft, and rub through sieve; there should be three-fourths cup apple pulp.
2. Beat on a platter whites of eggs until stiff (using wire whisk), add gradually apple sweetened to taste, and continue beating.
3. Pile lightly on glass dish, chill, and serve with Boiled Custard.
