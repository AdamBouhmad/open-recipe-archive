## Ingredients

- two slices of lamb
- melted butter
- salt
- pepper
- three-fourths cup carrot
- one tablespoon bacon fat
- one tablespoon finely chopped onion
- one cup potato balls
- two cups thin Brown Sauce
- three tablespoons Sherry wine
- twelve small onions
- one tablespoon sugar

## Directions

1. Wipe two slices of lamb cut one and one-fourth inches thick from centre of leg
2. Put in hot frying-pan and turn frequently until seared and browned on both sides
3. Brush over with melted butter
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Bake in casserole dish twenty minutes or until tender
6. Parboil carrot cut in strips fifteen minutes
7. Drain and sauté in bacon fat to which has been added finely chopped onion
8. Add to lamb with potato balls, Brown Sauce, and Sherry wine
9. Season with pepper to taste
10. Cook until potatoes are soft
11. Add onions that have been cooked until soft, then drained and sautéd in butter to which sugar has been added
12. Serve from casserole dish
