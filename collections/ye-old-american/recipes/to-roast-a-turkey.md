## Ingredients

- bread
- beef suet
- sausage meat or veal
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- eggs
- lard
- lemon
- pickles

## Directions

1. Make the forcemeat: take the crumb of a loaf of bread, a quarter of a pound of beef suet shred fine, a little sausage meat or veal scraped and pounded very fine, nutmeg, pepper, and salt to your taste
2. Mix it lightly with three eggs
3. Stuff the craw with it
4. Spit it, and lay it down a good distance from the fire, which should be clear and brisk
5. Dust and baste it several times with cold lard
6. When it is enough, froth it up, dish it, and pour on the same gravy as for the boiled turkey, or bread sauce
7. Garnish with lemon and pickles, and serve it up
