## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- Brown Stock
- bacon
- fresh mushroom caps
- toast
- salt
- cayenne
- Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Trim kidneys, cook in Brown Stock ten minutes, drain, and cut in slices.
2. Arrange alternate slices of kidney and thinly sliced bacon on skewers with a fresh mushroom cap at either end of each skewer.
3. Broil until bacon is crisp and arrange on pieces of toast.
4. Pour over sauce made from stock in which kidneys were cooked, seasoned with salt, cayenne, and Madeira wine.
