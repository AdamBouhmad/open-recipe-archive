## Ingredients

- five or six pounds of fine red raspberries
- white sugar
- one quart of currant juice

## Directions

1. To five or six pounds of fine red raspberries (not too ripe) add an equal quantity of the finest quality of white sugar.
2. Mash the whole well in a preserving kettle.
3. Add about one quart of currant juice (a little less will do).
4. Boil gently till it jellies upon a cold plate.
5. Put into small jars and cover with brandied paper and tie a thick white paper over them.
6. Keep in a dark, dry and cool place.
