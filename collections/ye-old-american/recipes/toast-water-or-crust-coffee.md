## Ingredients

- stale pieces of crusts of bread
- boiling water
- sugar
- ice
- cream

## Directions

1. Take stale pieces of crusts of bread, the end pieces of the loaf, toast them a nice, dark brown, care to be taken that they do not burn in the least, as that affects the flavor
2. Put the browned crusts into a large milk pitcher, and pour enough boiling water over to cover them
3. Cover the pitcher closely, and let steep until cold
4. Strain, and sweeten to taste
5. Put a piece of ice in each glass
