## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- one-fourth cup butter
- one slice onion
- one slice carrot
- one slice turnip
- one-half bay leaf
- one sprig thyme
- one sprig parsley
- three cups hot water
- one and one-half teaspoons salt
- twelve peppercorns
- three tablespoons butter
- four tablespoons flour

## Directions

1. Order a leg of mutton boned.
2. Wipe, stuff, sew, and place in deep pan.
3. Cook five minutes in one-fourth cup butter, a slice each of onion, carrot, and turnip cut in dice, one-half bay leaf, and a sprig each of thyme and parsley.
4. Add three cups hot water, one and one-half teaspoons salt, and twelve peppercorns; pour over mutton.
5. Cover closely, and cook slowly three hours, uncovering for the last half-hour.
6. Remove from pan to hot platter.
7. Brown three tablespoons butter, add four tablespoons flour, and stir until well browned; then pour on slowly the strained liquor; there should be one and three-fourths cups.
