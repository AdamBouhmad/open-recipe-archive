## Ingredients

- sugar
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Put one cupful of sugar and two teaspoonfuls of water in a saucepan on the fire
2. Stir constantly until it is quite a dark color
3. Add a half cupful of water and a pinch of salt
4. Let it boil a few minutes
5. When cold, bottle
