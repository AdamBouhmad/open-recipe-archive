## Ingredients

- two pounds of rice
- half a pound of butter
- salt
- peppercorns
- cloves
- mace
- a pound and a half of bacon
- two fowls
- hard boiled eggs
- fried whole onions

## Directions

1. Wash two pounds of rice, boil it in a little water with half a pound of butter, some salt, peppercorns, cloves, and mace
2. Keep the saucepan closely covered until the rice is sufficiently cooked
3. Have ready a pound and a half of bacon, and two fowls nicely boiled
4. Place the bacon in the middle of a dish, and the fowls on each side
5. Cover over with the boiled rice, and garnish with hard boiled eggs and fried whole onions
