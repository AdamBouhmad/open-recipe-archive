## Ingredients

- half an ounce of Cayenne pepper
- a pint of white wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Put half an ounce of Cayenne pepper into a bottle with a pint of white wine vinegar, cork it tightly, and shake it well for a few days
2. it will be soon ready for use
