## Ingredients

- half a fowl
- about a quart of water
- the other half fowl
- half a teacupful of rice
- a blade or two of mace
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put half a fowl into a saucepan with about a quart of water, let it boil to rags
2. strain off the meat, and to the liquor add the other half fowl cut up into joints, half a teacupful of rice, a blade or two of mace, and pepper and salt to taste
3. Let this stew until the fowl is very tender and nearly all the gravy is absorbed
4. send to table
