## Ingredients

- one fowl
- butter
- two tablespoonfuls of currie powder
- one lemon
- half a cocoa nut
- one teaspoonful of salt
- fried onion
- one clove of garlic
- butter and flour

## Directions

1. Skin a fowl and cut it up into small joints
2. Fry it in butter a light brown
3. Put it into a saucepan with two tablespoonfuls of currie powder, the juice of a lemon, half a cocoa nut finely grated, a teaspoonful of salt, a little fried onion, and a clove of garlic
4. Let it simmer slowly for three hours, adding a thickening of butter and flour, just before it is sufficiently cooked
