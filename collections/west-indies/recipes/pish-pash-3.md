## Ingredients

- half a fowl
- water
- the other half fowl
- half a teacupful of rice
- a blade or two of mace
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Put half a fowl into a saucepan with about a quart of water, let it boil to rags, then strain off the meat
2. To the liquor add the other half fowl cut up into joints, half a teacupful of rice, a blade or two of mace, and pepper and salt to taste
3. Let this stew until the fowl is very tender and nearly all the gravy is absorbed, then send to table
