## Ingredients

- cayenne
- ground ginger
- black pepper
- cinnamon
- mace
- white pepper
- grated nutmegs
- ground cloves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take of cayenne, ground ginger, black pepper, cinnamon and mace each one quarter of an ounce
2. of white pepper and grated nutmegs one ounce, and half a teaspoonful of ground cloves
3. mix well and put into a bottle
4. it will be found a great addition to soups and gravies, and mixed with a little vinegar to form a paste will make a delicious sauce for cold meat, steaks, &c.
