## Ingredients

- butter
- one rather large onion
- one fowl
- two or three apples
- water or gravy
- one tablespoonful of currie powder
- one dessert-spoonful of dry mustard
- milk
- half a tea-cupful of vinegar
- one lemon
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a piece of butter twice as big as a walnut, fry it with a rather large onion cut into thin slices
2. Cut up a fowl into joints, and put it into a stewpan with a fried onion
3. Simmer it for a quarter of an hour
4. Add two or three chopped apples, and as much water or gravy as will cover it
5. Let it stew slowly for an hour
6. Take a tablespoonful of currie powder, and a dessert-spoonful of dry mustard, mix them together with milk into a liquid, and pour it into the stewpan
7. Add half a tea-cupful of vinegar, the juice of a lemon, and a little salt
8. Simmer it for half an hour, skim it well, and then serve it up
