## Ingredients

- a piece of cod
- half a fried onion
- good stock
- teaspoonful of currie powder
- two ounces of butter
- salt
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Take a piece of cod, pull it into large flakes, and fry it till brown
2. Put this into a stewpan, with half a fried onion
3. Pour over it sufficient good stock to cover it
4. Add a teaspoonful of currie powder, and two ounces of butter, with salt to taste; also the juice of half a lemon
5. Simmer for a quarter of an hour, or until the fish is cooked, thicken the gravy, and serve
