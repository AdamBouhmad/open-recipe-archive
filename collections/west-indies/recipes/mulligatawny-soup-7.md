## Ingredients

- one rabbit or fowl
- five fried onions
- a small piece of garlic
- three pints of stock
- two tablespoonfuls of currie powder
- one ounce of pounded almonds
- a little lemon or mango juice
- a good lump of butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Skin and cut up a rabbit or fowl into small joints
2. Brown them in a frying-pan
3. Put them into a stewpan with five fried onions, a small piece of garlic, and three pints of stock into which you have previously mixed two tablespoonfuls of currie powder
4. Simmer gently for two hours
5. Add one ounce of pounded almonds and mix well
6. Add a little lemon or mango juice, with a good lump of butter and salt to taste
7. Serve very hot and send up a large dish of boiled rice with it, or the rice may be put into the soup, if preferred
