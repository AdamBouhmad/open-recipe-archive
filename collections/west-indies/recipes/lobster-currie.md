## Ingredients

- two onions
- half an ounce of butter
- one tablespoonful and a half of currie powder
- half a pint of good gravy
- meat from a large lobster
- tablespoonful of vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry two onions in half an ounce of butter, until they are nicely browned
2. Mix one tablespoonful and a half of currie powder, with half a pint of good gravy, and put this with the fried onions into a stewpan
3. Take the meat from a large lobster, cut it into rather small pieces, and add it to the gravy and onions with a tablespoonful of vinegar or lemon juice
4. Simmer slowly for about half an hour, and serve
