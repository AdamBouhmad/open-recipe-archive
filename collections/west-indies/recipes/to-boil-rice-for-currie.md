## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the rice in several waters, then leave it in a basin of cold water to soak for two or three hours
2. Have ready a saucepan full of water, with a little salt in it
3. When the water boils, drain the rice and put it into the saucepan
4. Let it boil very quickly for about seven minutes, then pour it into a colander, and place the colander on the top of the saucepan, that the water may quite drain off
5. The rice ought to be stirred with a fork that the grains may be separated
6. It ought to be boiled in a large quantity of water, and it will be sufficiently cooked when the grains become a little soft, and over done if they at all stick together
7. Rice should always be served in a separate dish from the currie
