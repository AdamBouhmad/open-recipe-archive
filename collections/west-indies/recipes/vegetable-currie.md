## Ingredients

- four large potatoes
- butter
- pint of brown gravy
- one raw onion
- one previously fried onion
- half a small vegetable marrow
- a handful of green peas
- a handful of French beans
- a few slices of cucumber
- one tablespoonful and a half of currie powder
- tablespoonful of vinegar
- salt
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- teaspoonful of flour
- a small piece of mint

## Directions

1. Peel and cut up in square pieces, four large potatoes, and fry them in butter until they are a light brown colour
2. Put them into a stewpan with a pint of brown gravy, one raw onion, and one previously fried, half a small vegetable marrow cut into pieces, a handful of green peas, the same of French beans, and a few slices of cucumber
3. Add one tablespoonful and a half of currie powder, a tablespoonful of vinegar, and salt to taste
4. Simmer very slowly, stirring carefully from time to time until the vegetables are nearly cooked
5. Then add a quarter of a pound of butter mixed with a teaspoonful of flour to thicken the gravy, and simmer again until the vegetables are sufficiently cooked but not broken
6. A small piece of mint is by some considered an improvement
