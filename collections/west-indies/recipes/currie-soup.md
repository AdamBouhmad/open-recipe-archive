## Ingredients

- three or four onions
- two ounces of butter
- flour
- a large tablespoonful of currie powder
- three tablespoonfuls of gravy soup
- a fowl
- a large tablespoonful of tamarinds
- rice

## Directions

1. Mince small three or four onions according to their size
2. Put them into a saucepan with two ounces of butter
3. Dredge in some flour and fry them of a light brown, taking care not to burn them
4. Rub in by degrees a large tablespoonful of currie powder till it is quite a paste
5. Gradually stir in three tablespoonfuls of gravy soup, mixing it well together
6. Boil it gently until it is well flavoured with the currie powder
7. Strain it into another saucepan and add a fowl skinned and cut into small pieces
8. Stew it slowly for an hour
9. Take out half a pint of the soup and stew in it a large tablespoonful of tamarinds until you can easily take away the stones
10. Strain and stir into the soup
11. Boil all together for a quarter of an hour
12. Serve rice with it
