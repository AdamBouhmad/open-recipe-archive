## Ingredients

- three quarts of the best vinegar
- eight ounces of salt
- two ounces of ginger
- half an ounce of mace
- a quarter of a pound of eschalots
- one ounce of white pepper
- one ounce of mustard seed
- half a tablespoonful of good cayenne pepper
- fruits or vegetables

## Directions

1. To three quarts of the best vinegar, put eight ounces of salt, two ounces of ginger, half an ounce of mace, a quarter of a pound of eschalots, one ounce of white pepper, one ounce of mustard seed, and half a tablespoonful of good cayenne pepper
2. boil it all together and when cold put it into a jar
3. gather any fruits or vegetables you like, wipe them clean and put them into your pickle
4. secure the jar closely by tying leather and a bladder over it
