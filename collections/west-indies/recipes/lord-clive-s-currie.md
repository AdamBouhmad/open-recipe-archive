## Ingredients

- six sliced onions
- one green apple
- a clove of garlic
- a little good stock
- one teaspoonful of currie powder
- a few tablespoonfuls of stock
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- a salt-spoonful of Cayenne pepper
- uncooked meat
- a piece of butter

## Directions

1. Take six sliced onions, one green apple, and a clove of garlic
2. Stew them to a pulp in a little good stock
3. Add one teaspoonful of currie powder, a few tablespoonfuls of stock, a salt-spoonful of salt, and the same quantity of Cayenne pepper
4. Add to this gravy any kind of uncooked meat cut into small pieces
5. Add a piece of butter
