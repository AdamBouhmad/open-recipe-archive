## Ingredients

- two ounces of almonds
- butter
- one onion
- the rind of half a lemon
- one dessert-spoonful of currie powder
- half a pint of water
- one chicken
- one teacup-ful of cream
- one lemon

## Directions

1. Blanch two ounces of almonds
2. Fry them in a little butter until they are brown, but do not let them burn
3. Pound them to a cream with an onion and the rind of half a lemon
4. Mix a dessert-spoonful of currie powder with half a pint of water, and put this with the almonds into a stewpan, with a chicken cut up into joints
5. Let it simmer gently for nearly an hour
6. Add a teacup-ful of cream and let it nearly boil
7. Squeeze into it the juice of a lemon, and serve up
