## Ingredients

- the juice of four lemons
- a few pieces of lemon peel
- a small quantity of salt
- a little tamarind juice
- half a teaspoonful of Cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan the juice of four lemons, a few pieces of lemon peel, a small quantity of salt, a little tamarind juice, and half a teaspoonful of Cayenne pepper
2. simmer this for a few minutes, and turn it into a basin
