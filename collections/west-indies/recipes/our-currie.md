## Ingredients

- one moderate sized fowl
- one sliced onion
- one onion fried brown
- one clove of garlic
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one tablespoonful and a half of currie powder
- one lemon or one tablespoonful of vinegar
- a small piece of mint
- water or gravy
- a quarter of a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Skin a moderate sized fowl, and cut it up
2. Put it into a stewpan with one sliced onion, and another previously fried brown, one clove of garlic, one teaspoonful of salt, one tablespoonful and a half of currie powder, and the juice of one lemon, or one tablespoonful of vinegar
3. Add a small piece of mint
4. Cover the meat with water or gravy, and let it stew very slowly for an hour and a half
5. Add a quarter of a pound of butter, and let it stew an hour and a half more
