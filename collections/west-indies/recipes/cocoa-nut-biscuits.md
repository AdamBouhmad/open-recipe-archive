## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound grated cocoa nut
- three quarters of a pound pounded loaf sugar
- one teaspoonful arrow root
- one egg

## Directions

1. Mix three quarters of a pound of grated cocoa nut, the same quantity of pounded loaf sugar, and one teaspoonful of arrow root with one egg
2. Bake on buttered papers
