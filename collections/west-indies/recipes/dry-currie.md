## Ingredients

- two ounces of butter
- one tablespoonful of currie powder
- stock
- fowl or uncooked meat
- onion
- three cloves
- a small piece of stick cinnamon
- three cardamom seeds
- two bay leaves

## Directions

1. Melt two ounces of butter in a frying pan until it is a little burnt
2. Mix with it a tablespoonful of currie powder, and let it fry till brown
3. Put it with very little stock into a saucepan
4. Cut up your fowl, or any uncooked meat, into pieces
5. Add a little onion cut very small, three cloves, a small piece of stick cinnamon, three cardamom seeds, and two bay leaves
6. Let all simmer together for two hours or longer, using very little stock so there should be no gravy when served up
