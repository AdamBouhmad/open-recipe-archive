## Ingredients

- four oranges
- quarter of a pound butter
- grated orange peel
- bread crumbs
- yolks of four eggs

## Directions

1. Peel and quarter four oranges, take out the pips
2. Stew them until quite soft
3. Beat them in a basin with a quarter of a pound of butter, a little grated orange peel, a few bread crumbs, and the well beaten yolks of four eggs
4. Sweeten to taste
5. Pour it into a pie dish
6. Bake in a gentle oven
