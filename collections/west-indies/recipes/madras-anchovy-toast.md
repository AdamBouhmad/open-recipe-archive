## Ingredients

- a piece of butter the size of an apple
- well-beaten yolks of two eggs
- cayenne pepper to taste
- half a teaspoonful of white wine or a teaspoonful of beer
- a tablespoonful of anchovy paste
- hot toast

## Directions

1. Put a piece of butter the size of an apple upon a very hot plate
2. add the well-beaten yolks of two eggs, cayenne pepper to taste, half a teaspoonful of white wine, or a teaspoonful of beer, and a tablespoonful of anchovy paste
3. Mix well
4. soak hot toast in it, and serve very hot
