## Ingredients

- six sliced onions
- one green apple
- one clove of garlic
- good stock
- one teaspoonful of currie powder
- a few tablespoonfuls of stock
- a salt-spoonful of salt
- Cayenne pepper
- uncooked meat
- a piece of butter the size of a walnut
- flour

## Directions

1. Stew the onions, apple, and garlic to a pulp in a little good stock
2. Add one teaspoonful of currie powder, a few tablespoonfuls of stock, a salt-spoonful of salt, and the same quantity of Cayenne pepper
3. Add to this gravy any kind of uncooked meat cut into small pieces, adding a piece of butter the size of a walnut, rolled in flour
4. Stew slowly for two hours and a half
