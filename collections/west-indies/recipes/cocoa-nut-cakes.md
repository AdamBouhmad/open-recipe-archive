## Ingredients

- fresh cocoa nut
- sweet almonds
- bitter almond
- two or three ounces sifted white sugar
- white of egg

## Directions

1. Grate a fresh cocoa nut, and leave it to dry
2. Add to it a few sweet almonds and one bitter almond, also grated, two or three ounces of sifted white sugar, and sufficient well-beaten white of egg to make the whole into a stiff paste
3. Roll it into round balls
4. Bake on a greased tin until they are quite dry, and the top of them slightly browned
5. Put them off on to a cold dish
