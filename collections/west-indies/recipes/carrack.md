## Ingredients

- eight pickled walnuts
- one head of garlic
- walnut vinegar
- soy
- mushroom catsup
- a tablespoonful of Harvey sauce
- one quart of vinegar
- mango pickle

## Directions

1. Chop eight pickled walnuts and one head of garlic, put these into a large jar
2. add walnut vinegar, soy, and mushroom catsup, of each half a tumblerful, a tablespoonful of Harvey sauce, and one quart of vinegar
3. put the jar in a dry place, and shake it every day for a month
4. a few spoonfuls of mango pickle is a great improvement
