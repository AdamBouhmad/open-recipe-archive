## Ingredients

- one fowl
- two ounces of butter
- three sliced onions
- one pint of boiling stock
- one tablespoonful of currie powder
- flour
- cold water
- two sour apples
- half a lemon

## Directions

1. Cut up a fowl into small joints and put it into a stewpan with two ounces of butter, and three sliced onions
2. Let it stew until the meat has acquired a nice brown colour, but do not allow it to get black
3. Add one pint of boiling stock
4. Mix one tablespoonful of currie powder and the same quantity of flour, with a little cold water into a smooth paste, and put this into the stewpan
5. Peel and slice two sour apples (taking out the core), which add to the fowl, and other ingredients
6. Let all stew gently, for at least an hour
7. Squeeze into it the juice of half a lemon and serve
