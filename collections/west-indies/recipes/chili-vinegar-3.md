## Ingredients

- four dozen fresh chilies
- a pint of strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop, and pound in a mortar four dozen fresh chilies, and put them into a bottle with a pint of strong vinegar
2. shake the bottle every day, and in three weeks it will be ready for use
