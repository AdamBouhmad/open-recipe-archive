## Ingredients

- a quart bottle nearly full of vinegar
- five ounces of eschalots
- half a teaspoonful of Cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Put into a quart bottle nearly full of vinegar, five ounces of eschalots which have been well bruised, and add half a teaspoonful of Cayenne pepper
2. cork the bottle well, shake it up, and then leave it for a fortnight
3. at the end of this time, strain it through fine muslin, and bottle it again
