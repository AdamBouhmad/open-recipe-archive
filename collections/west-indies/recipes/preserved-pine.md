## Ingredients

- one pound white sugar
- quarter of a pint water
- pine apple

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with one pound of white sugar to a quarter of a pint of water
2. Boil it until it becomes clear, skimming it carefully
3. Have ready a pine apple peeled and cut into slices
4. When the syrup is ready and boiling, put in the slices of pine, and simmer for about three quarters of an hour
5. Put into glasses or jars, and when cold tie down
