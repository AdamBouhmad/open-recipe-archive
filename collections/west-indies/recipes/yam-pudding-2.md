## Ingredients

- moderate sized yam
- half a pound butter
- half a pound sugar
- cinnamon
- eight eggs
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Take a moderate sized yam, and boil it until it is soft
2. Peel it and beat it fine with half a pound of butter, half a pound of sugar, and a little cinnamon
3. Beat up eight eggs, leaving out four whites, with a little orange flower water
4. Put the mixture into a buttered dish
5. Bake it
