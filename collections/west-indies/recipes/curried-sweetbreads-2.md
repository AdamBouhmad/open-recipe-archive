## Ingredients

- veal gravy
- fried onion
- tablespoonful of vinegar or lemon juice
- tablespoonful of currie powder
- salt
- two ounces of butter
- flour
- two or three sweetbreads

## Directions

1. Have ready some good veal gravy, add to it a very small quantity of fried onion, a tablespoonful of vinegar or the juice of a lemon, the same quantity of currie powder, and salt to taste
2. Rub two ounces of butter into enough flour to make the gravy (which ought to be about three-quarters of a pint) a proper thickness
3. Cut up two or three sweetbreads into pieces about two inches square
4. Stew them gently in the gravy until sufficiently cooked, and serve
