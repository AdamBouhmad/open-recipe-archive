## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of currie powder
- one teaspoonful of flour
- water
- half a pint of good stock or gravy soup
- a large onion
- two dozen large prawns
- two ounces of butter
- tablespoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix one tablespoonful of currie powder and one teaspoonful of flour, with a little water, into a smooth paste
2. Stir it into half a pint of good stock or gravy soup
3. Add a large onion, sliced and fried, and simmer it in a stewpan until it thickens
4. Have ready two dozen large prawns, taken whole from their shells, and put them into the stewpan
5. Simmer for a quarter of an hour, stirring occasionally, care being taken not to break the prawns
6. Then add two ounces of butter, and a tablespoonful of vinegar, and simmer for a quarter of an hour longer
