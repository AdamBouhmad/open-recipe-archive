## Ingredients

- a filleted sole
- vinegar
- gravy
- fried onions
- currie powder
- a large lump of butter, about two ounces

## Directions

1. Take a filleted sole, a large thick one, cut it into pieces, not too small, lay them in vinegar for an hour
2. Have ready some gravy, prepared with fried onions and currie powder, as for prawn currie
3. Add to it the pieces of sole, and a large lump of butter, about two ounces
4. Simmer gently for half an hour, or rather longer if the sole is thick
