## Ingredients

- apples
- onions
- uncooked veal
- currie powder
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut up some apples and onions into slices, and some uncooked veal into round slices the same size
2. Have ready some small skewers and put upon each skewer twelve slices of meat, apples, and onions, alternately
3. Sprinkle well over them some currie powder
4. Fry them in a stewpan, with sufficient butter to cover them
5. Send to table without removing the skewers
