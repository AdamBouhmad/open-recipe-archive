## Ingredients

- strong syrup
- fine peaches
- good brandy

## Directions

1. Make a strong syrup, which boil and skim well
2. Have ready some fine peaches, gathered before they are over ripe
3. Rub the lint off them carefully with a cloth
4. Take a needle and run it down the seam of each peach to the depth of the skin, and place them in a jar, covering them with good brandy, and let them remain two hours
5. Take them out and put them into a preserving pan, so that each may lie singly
6. Pour the syrup over them, and boil them until they look clear, but be careful not to break them
7. Remove the peaches with a spoon into glasses, and when the syrup is cold, mix it with the brandy and pour it on the peaches
8. The glasses must be well tied down with a bladder, and the air carefully excluded
