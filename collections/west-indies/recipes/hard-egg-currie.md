## Ingredients

- two onions
- butter
- tablespoonful of currie powder
- pint of good stock
- cup of cream
- arrowroot or rice flour
- six or eight hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Slice two onions, and fry them in butter
2. Boil them with a tablespoonful of currie powder in a pint of good stock until quite cooked
3. Then add a cup of cream, and thicken with arrowroot or rice flour
4. Simmer it slowly for a few minutes, adding six or eight hard boiled eggs cut into slices
5. Heat the eggs thoroughly, but do not let them boil
