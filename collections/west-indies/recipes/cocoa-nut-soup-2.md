## Ingredients

- three ounces of finely-grated cocoa nut
- three pints of veal stock
- corn or rice flour
- salt
- half a pint of cream
- nutmeg or mace

## Directions

1. Put three ounces of finely-grated cocoa nut (omitting the dark rind) to three pints of veal stock
2. Boil it gently for an hour
3. Mix with a little cold stock enough corn or rice flour to make the soup sufficiently thick
4. Season to taste
5. Add half a pint of cream
6. Simmer all together for about a quarter of an hour
7. Nutmeg or mace may be added if liked
