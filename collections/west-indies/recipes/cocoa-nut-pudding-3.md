## Ingredients

- fresh cocoa nut
- one ounce candied citron
- half a lemon
- quarter of a pound sifted white sugar
- two ounces fresh butter
- yolks of three or four eggs
- paste

## Directions

1. Remove the dark rind from the cocoa nut and grate it very finely
2. Chop up an ounce of candied citron very small, and grate the rind of half a lemon
3. Put these ingredients into a basin, adding to them a quarter of a pound of sifted white sugar
4. Oil over the fire two ounces of fresh butter, and pour it over the other ingredients
5. Mix well, and put to them the yolks of three or four eggs
6. Add a squeeze of lemon, and pour into a tart dish lined with paste
7. Bake in a slow oven
