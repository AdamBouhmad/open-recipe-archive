## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of pudding raisins
- a quarter of a pound of apples
- the same quantity of moist sugar
- chopped tomatos from which you have taken the seeds
- two ounces of tamarinds
- a tablespoonful of salt
- a tablespoonful of cayenne
- a teaspoonful of ground ginger
- two large onions
- very little chopped lemon peel
- a pint of lemon juice
- one quart of vinegar

## Directions

1. Stone and cut up into rather large pieces, a quarter of a pound of pudding raisins
2. add to these, a quarter of a pound of apples chopped in small pieces, the same quantity of moist sugar, and chopped tomatos from which you have taken the seeds, two ounces of tamarinds, a tablespoonful of salt, ditto of cayenne, a teaspoonful of ground ginger, two large onions chopped fine, very little chopped lemon peel, a pint of lemon juice, and one quart of vinegar
3. mix all these ingredients well together
4. then put them into a jar, well covered
5. Stir the mixture every day for four or five weeks
6. if at the end of that time it is too thin, pour enough of the liquid away to make it of a proper thickness, but not dry
7. bottle the chutney in bottles or jars for use
