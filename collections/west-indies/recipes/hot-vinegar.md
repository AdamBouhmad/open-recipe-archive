## Ingredients

- two cloves of garlic
- a pint of vinegar
- a tablespoonful of Worcester sauce
- a teaspoonful of salt
- an ounce of ground white pepper
- half an ounce of cayenne

## Directions

1. Chop fine two cloves of garlic, put this into a bottle, with a pint of vinegar, a tablespoonful of Worcester sauce, a teaspoonful of salt, an ounce of ground white pepper, and half an ounce of cayenne
2. Shake it up well, then let it remain for a month
3. Strain it through fine muslin and bottle it in small bottles, which should be well corked down
