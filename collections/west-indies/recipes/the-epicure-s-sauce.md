## Ingredients

- one capsicum
- two eschalots
- one or two bird's-eye chilies
- two tablespoonfuls of port wine
- six tablespoonfuls of mushroom catsup
- two tablespoonfuls of soy
- two tablespoonfuls of walnut catsup
- half a teaspoonful of cayenne
- the same quantity of white pepper
- half a pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop fine, one capsicum, two eschalots, and one or two bird's-eye chilies
2. add two tablespoonfuls of port wine, six tablespoonfuls of mushroom catsup, two of soy, and two of walnut catsup, half a teaspoonful of cayenne, and the same quantity of white pepper
3. put all into a bottle, which place in a warm place for two or three weeks
4. then strain, and add half a pint of vinegar
