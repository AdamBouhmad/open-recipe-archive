## Ingredients

- 6 oz. of sweet almonds
- 1/4 lb. of sifted sugar
- rind of 1/2 lemon
- white of 1 egg
- puff-paste

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds, and chop them fine
2. Rub the sugar on the lemon-rind, and pound it in a mortar
3. Mix this with the almonds and the white of the egg
4. Roll some puff-paste out; cut it in any shape that may be preferred, such as diamonds, rings, ovals, &c., and spread the above mixture over the paste
5. Bake the bouchées in an oven, not too hot, and serve cold
