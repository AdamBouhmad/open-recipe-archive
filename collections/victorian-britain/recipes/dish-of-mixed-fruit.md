## Ingredients

- pine
- moss
- apples
- pears
- plums
- peaches
- grapes

## Directions

1. Place a tumbler in the centre of the dish, and in this tumbler, the pine, crown uppermost.
2. Round the tumbler put a thick layer of moss, and over this, apples, pears, plums, peaches, and such fruit as is simultaneously in season.
3. By putting a layer of moss underneath, so much fruit is not required, besides giving a better shape to the dish.
4. Place grapes on the top of the fruit, a portion of some of the bunches hanging over the sides of the dish in a négligé kind of manner, which takes off the formal look of the dish.
5. In arranging the plums, apples, &c., let the colours contrast well.
