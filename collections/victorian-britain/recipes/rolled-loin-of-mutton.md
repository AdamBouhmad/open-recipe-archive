## Ingredients

- About 6 lbs. of a loin of mutton
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1/4 teaspoonful of pounded allspice
- 1/4 teaspoonful of mace
- 1/4 teaspoonful of nutmeg
- 6 cloves
- forcemeat
- 1 glass of port wine
- 2 tablespoonfuls of mushroom ketchup
- red-currant jelly
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Hang the mutton till tender and bone it
2. Sprinkle over it pepper, mace, cloves, allspice, and nutmeg, all of which must be pounded very fine
3. Let it remain for a day
4. Make a forcemeat by recipe No. 417 and cover the meat with it
5. Roll and bind it up firmly
6. Half bake it in a slow oven and let it grow cold
7. Take off the fat, and put the gravy into a stewpan
8. Flour the meat, put it in the gravy, and stew it till perfectly tender
9. Take out the meat and unbind it
10. Add to the gravy wine and ketchup, give one boil, and pour over the meat
11. Serve with red-currant jelly and, if obtainable, a few mushrooms stewed for a few minutes in the gravy
