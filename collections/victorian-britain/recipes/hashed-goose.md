## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast goose
- 2 onions
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 pint of boiling water
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of port wine
- 2 tablespoonfuls of mushroom ketchup
- sippets of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut up the goose into pieces of the size required.
2. Put the inferior joints, trimmings, &c., into a stewpan to make the gravy.
3. Slice and fry the onions in the butter of a very pale brown.
4. Add these to the trimmings, and pour over about a pint of boiling water.
5. Stew these gently for 3/4 hour, then skim and strain the liquor.
6. Thicken it with flour, and flavour with port wine and ketchup, in the above proportion.
7. Add a seasoning of pepper and salt, and put in the pieces of goose.
8. Let these get thoroughly hot through, but do not allow them to boil, and serve with sippets of toasted bread.
