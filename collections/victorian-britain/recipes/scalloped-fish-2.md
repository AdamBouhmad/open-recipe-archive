## Ingredients

- Any cold fish
- 1 egg
- milk
- 1 large blade of pounded mace
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of anchovy sauce
- pepper and salt to taste
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Pick the fish carefully from the bones, and moisten with milk and the egg
2. Add the other ingredients, and place in a deep dish or scallop shells
3. Cover with bread crumbs, butter the top, and brown before the fire
4. When quite hot, serve
