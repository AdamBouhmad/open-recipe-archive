## Ingredients

- 4 anchovies
- 1 oz. of butter
- 1/2 pint of melted butter
- cayenne to taste
- lemon-juice (optional)

## Directions

1. Bone the anchovies, and pound them in a mortar to a paste, with 1 oz. of butter.
2. Make the melted butter hot, stir in the pounded anchovies and cayenne; simmer for 3 or 4 minutes; and if liked, add a squeeze of lemon-juice.
3. Alternatively, stir in 1-1/2 tablespoonfuls of anchovy essence to 1/2 pint of melted butter, and add seasoning to taste.
4. Boil the whole up for 1 minute, and serve hot.
