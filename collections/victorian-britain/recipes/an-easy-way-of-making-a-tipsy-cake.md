## Ingredients

- 12 stale small sponge-cakes
- raisin wine
- 1/2 lb. of jam
- 1 pint of custard No. 1423

## Directions

1. Soak the sponge-cakes, which should be stale (on this account they should be cheaper), in a little raisin wine.
2. Arrange them on a deep glass dish in four layers, putting a layer of jam between each, and pour round them a pint of custard, made by recipe No. 1423, decorating the top with cut preserved fruit.
