## Ingredients

- 6 lemons
- 2 quarts of boiling water
- 2 quarts of vinegar
- 1 oz. of cloves per quart of vinegar
- 1/2 oz. of white pepper per quart of vinegar
- 1 oz. of bruised ginger per quart of vinegar
- 1/4 oz. of mace and chilies per quart of vinegar
- 1 oz. of mustard-seed per quart of vinegar
- 1/2 stick of sliced horseradish per quart of vinegar
- a few cloves of garlic per quart of vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the lemons into a brine that will bear an egg
2. Let them remain in it 6 days, stirring them every day
3. Have ready 2 quarts of boiling water, put in the lemons, and allow them to boil for 1/4 hour
4. Take them out, and let them lie in a cloth until perfectly dry and cold
5. Boil up sufficient vinegar to cover the lemons, with all the above ingredients, allowing the same proportion as stated to each quart of vinegar
6. Pack the lemons in a jar, pour over the vinegar boiling hot, and tie down with a bladder
7. They will be fit for use in about 12 months, or rather sooner
