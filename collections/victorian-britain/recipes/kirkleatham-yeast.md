## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of hops
- 4 quarts of water
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/2 pint of yeast

## Directions

1. Boil the hops and water for 20 minutes
2. Strain, and mix with the liquid 1/2 lb. of flour and not quite 1/2 pint of yeast
3. Bottle it up, and tie the corks down
4. When wanted for use, boil potatoes according to the quantity of bread to be made (about 3 lbs. are sufficient for about a peck of flour)
5. Mash them, add to them 1/2 lb. of flour, and mix about 1/2 pint of the yeast with them
6. Let this mixture stand all day, and lay the bread to rise the night before it is wanted
