## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of fleshy beef
- 1-1/2 pint of water
- 1/4 saltspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into small square pieces, after trimming off all the fat, and put it into a baking-jar, with the above proportion of water and salt
2. Cover the jar well, place it in a warm, but not hot oven, and bake for 3 or 4 hours
3. When the oven is very fierce in the daytime, it is a good plan to put the jar in at night, and let it remain till the next morning, when the tea will be done
4. Strain it, and put by in a cool place until wanted
5. It may also be flavoured with an onion, a clove, and a few sweet herbs, &c., when the stomach is sufficiently strong to take those
