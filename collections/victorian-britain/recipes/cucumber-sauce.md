## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 cucumbers
- 2 oz. of butter
- 6 tablespoonfuls of brown gravy

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers
2. Quarter them and take out the seeds
3. Cut them into small pieces
4. Put them in a cloth and rub them well, to take out the water which hangs about them
5. Put the butter in a saucepan
6. Add the cucumbers
7. Shake them over a sharp fire until they are of a good colour
8. Pour over it the gravy
9. Mix this with the cucumbers
10. Simmer gently for 10 minutes
11. It will then be ready to serve
