## Ingredients

- almonds
- raisins

## Directions

1. These are usually served on glass dishes, the fruit piled high in the centre, and the almonds blanched, and strewn over.
2. To blanch the almonds, put them into a small mug or teacup, pour over them boiling water, let them remain for 2 or 3 minutes, and the skins may then be easily removed.
