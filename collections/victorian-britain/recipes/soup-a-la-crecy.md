## Ingredients

- 4 carrots
- 2 sliced onions
- 1 cut lettuce
- chervil
- 2 oz. butter
- 1 pint of lentils
- the crumbs of 2 French rolls
- half a teacupful of rice
- 2 quarts of medium stock No. 105

## Directions

1. Put the vegetables with the butter in the stewpan, and let them simmer 5 minutes
2. Add the lentils and 1 pint of the stock, and stew gently for half an hour
3. Fill it up with the remainder of the stock, let it boil another hour, and put in the crumb of the rolls
4. When well soaked, rub all through a tammy
5. Have ready the rice boiled; pour the soup over this, and serve
