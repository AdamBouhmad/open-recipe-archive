## Ingredients

- Mustard
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Mix mustard with water that has been boiled and allowed to cool.
2. Put the mustard in a cup, with a small pinch of salt, and mix with it very gradually sufficient boiled water to make it drop from the spoon without being watery.
3. Stir and mix well, and rub the lumps well down with the back of a spoon, as well-mixed mustard should be perfectly free from these.
