## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/4 lb. of butter or lard
- 1 egg
- a piece of German yeast the size of a walnut
- warm milk

## Directions

1. Put the flour (which should be perfectly dry) into a basin
2. Mix with it the salt, and rub in the butter or lard
3. Beat the egg well, stir to it the yeast, and add these to the flour with as much warm milk as will make the whole into a smooth paste
4. Knead it well
5. Let it rise near the fire, and when well risen, form it into cakes
6. Place them on tins, let them rise again for a few minutes before putting them into the oven
7. Bake from 1/4 to 1/2 hour in a moderate oven
8. Butter the cakes, and eat them hot as soon as baked
