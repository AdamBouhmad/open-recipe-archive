## Ingredients

- syrup made by recipe No. 1512 (1 pint)
- greengages (1 quart)

## Directions

1. Make a syrup by recipe No. 1512, skim it well
2. Put in the greengages when the syrup is boiling, having previously removed the stalks and stones from the fruit
3. Boil gently for 1/4 hour, or until the fruit is tender; but take care not to let it break
4. Take the greengages carefully out, place them on a glass dish
5. Boil the syrup for another 5 minutes, let it cool a little
6. Pour over the fruit, and when cold, it will be ready for use
