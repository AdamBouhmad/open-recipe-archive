## Ingredients

- 9 lbs. of salt
- 8 oz. of sugar
- 9 oz. of powdered saltpetre

## Directions

1. Rub the above ingredients well into the tongues, and keep them in this curing mixture for 2 months, turning them every day
2. Drain them from the pickle, cover with brown paper, and have them smoked for about 3 weeks
