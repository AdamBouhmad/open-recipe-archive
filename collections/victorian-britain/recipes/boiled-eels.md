## Ingredients

- 4 small eels
- sufficient water to cover them
- a large bunch of parsley

## Directions

1. Choose small eels for boiling; put them in a stewpan with the parsley, and just sufficient water to cover them; simmer till tender.
2. Take them out, pour a little parsley and butter over them, and serve some in a tureen.
