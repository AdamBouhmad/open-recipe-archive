## Ingredients

- To each 1 gallon of water allow 1 oz. of salt
- 15 or 16 artichokes
- 1 oz. butter
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the artichokes until tender.
2. Drain and press the water from them, and beat them up with a fork.
3. When thoroughly mashed and free from lumps, put them into a saucepan with the butter and a seasoning of white pepper and salt.
4. Keep stirring over the fire until the artichokes are quite hot, and serve.
