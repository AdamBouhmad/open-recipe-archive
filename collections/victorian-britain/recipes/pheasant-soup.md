## Ingredients

- 2 pheasants
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 2 slices of ham
- 2 large onions sliced
- 1/2 head of celery
- crumb of two French rolls
- yolks of 2 eggs boiled hard
- salt and cayenne to taste
- pounded mace
- 3 quarts of stock No. 105

## Directions

1. Cut up the pheasants, flour and braise them in the butter and ham till they are of a nice brown, but not burnt
2. Put them in a stewpan, with the onions, celery, and seasoning, and simmer for 2 hours
3. Strain the soup
4. Pound the breasts with the crumb of the roll previously soaked, and the yolks of the eggs
5. Put it to the soup, give one boil, and serve
