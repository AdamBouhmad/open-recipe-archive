## Ingredients

- roes of 2 carp
- piece of fresh tunny about the size of a hen's egg
- small shalot, chopped
- very good butter

## Directions

1. Bleach the carp roes by putting them for 5 minutes in boiling water slightly salted
2. Hash up together the roe and the tunny, so as to mix them well
3. Throw the whole into a saucepan with a sufficient quantity of very good butter
4. Whip it up until the butter is melted
