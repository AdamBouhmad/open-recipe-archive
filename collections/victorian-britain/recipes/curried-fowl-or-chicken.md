## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast fowls
- 2 large onions
- 1 apple
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 dessertspoonful of curry-powder
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- 1/2 pint of gravy
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Slice the onions, peel, core, and chop the apple, and cut the fowl into neat joints
2. Fry these in the butter of a nice brown
3. Add the curry-powder, flour, and gravy, and stew for about 20 minutes
4. Put in the lemon-juice, and serve with boiled rice, either placed in a ridge round the dish or separately
5. Two or three shallots or a little garlic may be added, if approved
