## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of short crust No. 1212
- from 9 to 12 good-sized apricots
- 3/4 pint of water
- 1/2 lb. of sugar

## Directions

1. Make a short crust by recipe No. 1212, and line a mould with it as directed in recipe No. 1391
2. Boil the sugar and water together for 10 minutes
3. Halve the apricots, take out the stones, and simmer them in the syrup until tender
4. Watch them carefully, and take them up the moment they are done, for fear they break
5. Arrange them neatly in the flanc or case
6. Boil the syrup until reduced to a jelly
7. Pour it over the fruit, and serve either hot or cold
