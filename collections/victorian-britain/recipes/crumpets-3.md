## Ingredients

- mixture more like batter than dough
- iron rings
- good butter

## Directions

1. Make the mixture in the same manner as muffins, but let it be more like batter than dough
2. Let it rise for about 1/2 hour
3. Pour it into iron rings, which should be ready on a hot-plate
4. Bake them, and when one side appears done, turn them quickly on the other
5. Have ready a very bright clear fire
6. Put the crumpet on a toasting-fork, and hold it before the fire, not too close, until it is nicely brown on one side, but do not allow it to blacken
7. Turn it, and brown the other side
8. Spread it with good butter
9. Cut it in half
10. When all are done, pile them on a hot dish, and send them quickly to table
