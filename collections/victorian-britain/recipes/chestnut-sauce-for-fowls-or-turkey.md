## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of chestnuts
- 1/2 pint of white stock
- 2 strips of lemon-peel
- Cayenne to taste
- 1/4 pint of cream or milk

## Directions

1. Peel off the outside skin of the chestnuts, and put them into boiling water for a few minutes.
2. Take off the thin inside peel, and put them into a saucepan, with the white stock and lemon-peel, and let them simmer for 1-1/2 hour, or until the chestnuts are quite tender.
3. Rub the whole through a hair-sieve with a wooden spoon.
4. Add seasoning and the cream.
5. Let it just simmer, but not boil, and keep stirring all the time.
6. Serve very hot and quickly.
