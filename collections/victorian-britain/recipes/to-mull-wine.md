## Ingredients

- 1 pint of wine
- 1 large cupful of water
- sugar and spice to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the spice in the water until the flavour is extracted
2. Add the wine and sugar
3. Bring the whole to the boiling-point
4. Serve with strips of crisp dry toast, or with biscuits
