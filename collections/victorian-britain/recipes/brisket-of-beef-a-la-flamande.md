## Ingredients

- About 6 or 8 lbs. of the brisket of beef
- 4 or 5 slices of bacon
- 2 carrots
- 1 onion
- a bunch of savoury herbs
- salt and pepper to taste
- 4 cloves
- 4 whole allspice
- 2 blades of mace
- weak stock or water

## Directions

1. Choose that portion of the brisket which contains the gristle, trim it, and put it into a stewpan with the slices of bacon, which should be put under and over the meat
2. Add the vegetables, herbs, spices, and seasoning, and cover with a little weak stock or water
3. Close the stewpan as hermetically as possible, and simmer very gently for 4 hours
4. Strain the liquor, reserve a portion of it for sauce, and the remainder boil quickly over a sharp fire until reduced to a glaze, with which glaze the meat
5. Garnish the dish with scooped carrots and turnips, and when liked, a little cabbage; all of which must be cooked separately
6. Thicken and flavour the liquor that was saved for sauce, pour it round the meat, and serve
