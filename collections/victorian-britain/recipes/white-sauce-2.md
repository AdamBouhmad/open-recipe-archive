## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of white stock
- 1/2 pint of cream
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Have ready a delicately-clean saucepan, into which put the stock, which should be well flavoured with vegetables, and rather savoury
2. Mix the flour smoothly with the cream, add it to the stock
3. Season with a little salt, and boil all these ingredients very gently for about 10 minutes, keeping them well stirred the whole time, as this sauce is very liable to burn
