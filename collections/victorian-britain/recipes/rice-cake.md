## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of ground rice
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar
- 9 eggs
- 20 drops of essence of lemon, or the rind of 1 lemon
- 1/4 lb. of butter

## Directions

1. Separate the whites from the yolks of the eggs
2. Whisk them both well, and add to the latter the butter beaten to a cream
3. Stir in the flour, rice, and lemon (if the rind is used, it must be very finely minced), and beat the mixture well
4. Add the whites of the eggs
5. Beat the cake again for some time
6. Put it into a buttered mould or tin
7. Bake it for nearly 1-1/2 hour
