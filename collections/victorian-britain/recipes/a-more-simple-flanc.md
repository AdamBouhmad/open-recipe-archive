## Ingredients

- paste
- white of egg
- raw fruit
- sugar
- apricot jam (optional)

## Directions

1. Roll out the paste and cut the bottom of a round or oval shape
2. Cut a narrow strip for the sides
3. Stick the sides to the bottom piece with white of egg
4. Fill the flanc with raw fruit and sufficient sugar to sweeten it nicely
5. Bake until the crust is of equal thickness throughout and perfectly joined so juice does not escape
6. Serve hot or cold, garnished with candied citron or melted apricot jam poured over
