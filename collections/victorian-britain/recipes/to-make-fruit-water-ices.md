## Ingredients

- 1 pint of fruit-juice
- 1 pint of syrup made by recipe No. 1513
- pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Select nice ripe fruit; pick off the stalks, and put it into a large earthen pan, with a little pounded sugar strewed over.
2. Stir it about with a wooden spoon until it is well broken, then rub it through a hair sieve.
3. Make the syrup by recipe No. 1513, omitting the white of the egg.
4. Let it cool, add the fruit-juice, mix well together, and put the mixture into the freezing-pot.
5. Proceed as directed for Ice Puddings, No. 1290, and when the mixture is equally frozen, put it into small glasses.
