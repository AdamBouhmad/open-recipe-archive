## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of shin of beef
- 1/2 onion
- 1/4 carrot
- 2 or 3 sprigs of parsley and savoury herbs
- a piece of butter about the size of a walnut
- cayenne and mace to taste
- 3/4 pint of water

## Directions

1. Cut up the meat into very small pieces, slice the onion and carrot, and put them into a small saucepan with the butter.
2. Keep stirring over a sharp fire until they have taken a little colour, when add the water and the remaining ingredients.
3. Simmer for 1/2 hour, skim well, strain, and flavour, when it will be ready for use.
