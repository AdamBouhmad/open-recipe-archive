## Ingredients

- syrup made by recipe No. 1512
- nearly a quart of gooseberries
- boiling water
- cold water
- tablespoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Top and tail the gooseberries, which should not be very ripe
2. Pour over them some boiling water; then take them out, and plunge them into cold water, with which has been mixed a tablespoonful of vinegar
3. Make a pint of syrup by recipe No. 1512
4. When it boils, drain the gooseberries and put them in
5. Simmer them gently until the fruit is nicely pulped and tender, without being broken
6. Dish the gooseberries on a glass dish, boil the syrup for 2 or 3 minutes
7. Pour over the gooseberries, and serve cold
