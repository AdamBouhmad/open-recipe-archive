## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Cold butter

## Directions

1. Choose large potatoes, as much of a size as possible
2. Wash them in lukewarm water, and scrub them well
3. Put them into a moderate oven, and bake them for about 2 hours, turning them three or four times whilst they are cooking
4. Serve them in a napkin immediately they are done, as, if kept a long time in the oven, they have a shrivelled appearance
5. Send to table with them a piece of cold butter
