## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 oz. of cayenne
- 2 tablespoonfuls of walnut ketchup
- 2 tablespoonfuls of soy
- a few shreds of garlic and shalot
- 1 quart of vinegar

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients into a large bottle, and shake well every day for a fortnight
2. Keep it in small bottles well sealed, and in a few days it will be fit for use
