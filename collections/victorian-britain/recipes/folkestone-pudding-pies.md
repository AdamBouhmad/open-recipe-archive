## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 3 oz. of ground rice
- 3 oz. of butter
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- flavouring of lemon-peel or bay-leaf
- 6 eggs
- puff-paste
- currants

## Directions

1. Infuse 2 laurel or bay leaves, or the rind of 1/2 lemon, in the milk, and when it is well flavoured, strain it, and add the rice
2. boil these for 1/4 hour, stirring all the time
3. then take them off the fire, stir in the butter, sugar, and eggs, and let these latter be well beaten before they are added to the other ingredients
4. when nearly cold, line some patty-pans with puff-paste, fill with the custard, strew over each a few currants, and bake from 20 to 25 minutes in a moderate oven
