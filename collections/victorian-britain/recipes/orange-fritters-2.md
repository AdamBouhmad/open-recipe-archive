## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/2 oz. of butter
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt
- 2 eggs
- milk
- oranges
- hot lard or clarified dripping
- pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Make a nice light batter with the flour, butter, salt, eggs, and sufficient milk to make it the proper consistency
2. Peel the oranges and remove as much of the white skin as possible
3. Divide each orange into eight pieces, without breaking the thin skin, unless it be to remove the pips
4. Dip each piece of orange in the batter
5. Have ready a pan of boiling lard or clarified dripping
6. Drop in the oranges and fry them a delicate brown from 8 to 10 minutes
7. Lay them on a piece of blotting-paper before the fire to drain away the greasy moisture
8. Dish them on a white d'oyley
9. Sprinkle over them plenty of pounded sugar and serve quickly
