## Ingredients

- 6 dozen of oysters
- 2 quarts of white stock
- 1/2 pint of cream
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1-1/2 oz. of flour
- salt
- cayenne
- mace to taste

## Directions

1. Scald the oysters in their own liquor; take them out, beard them, and put them in a tureen.
2. Take a pint of the stock, put in the beards and the liquor, which must be carefully strained, and simmer for 1/2 an hour.
3. Take it off the fire, strain it again, and add the remainder of the stock with the seasoning and mace.
4. Bring it to a boil, add the thickening of butter and flour, simmer for 5 minutes, stir in the boiling cream, pour it over the oysters, and serve.
