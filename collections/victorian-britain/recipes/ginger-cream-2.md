## Ingredients

- The yolks of 4 eggs
- 1 pint of cream
- 3 oz. of preserved ginger
- 2 dessertspoonfuls of syrup
- sifted sugar to taste
- 1 oz. of isinglass

## Directions

1. Slice the ginger finely; put it into a basin with the syrup, the well-beaten yolks of eggs, and the cream
2. Mix these ingredients well together, and stir them over the fire for about 10 minutes, or until the mixture thickens
3. Take it off the fire, whisk till nearly cold, sweeten to taste, add the isinglass, which should be melted and strained, and serve the cream in a glass dish
4. It may be garnished with slices of preserved ginger or candied citron
