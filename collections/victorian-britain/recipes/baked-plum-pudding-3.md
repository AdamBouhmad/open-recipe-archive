## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of flour
- 1 lb. of currants
- 1 lb. of raisins
- 1 lb. of suet
- 2 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- a few slices of candied peel

## Directions

1. Chop the suet finely; mix with it the flour, currants, stoned raisins, and candied peel
2. moisten with the well-beaten eggs, and add sufficient milk to make the pudding of the consistency of very thick batter
3. Put it into a buttered dish, and bake in a good oven from 2-1/4 to 2-1/2 hours
4. turn it out, strew sifted sugar over, and serve
