## Ingredients

- Teal
- butter
- flour
- brown or orange gravy
- water-cresses
- lemon

## Directions

1. Choose fat plump birds, after the frost has set in.
2. Truss them in the same manner as wild duck, No. 1022.
3. Roast them before a brisk fire and keep them well basted.
4. Serve with brown or orange gravy, water-cresses, and a cut lemon.
