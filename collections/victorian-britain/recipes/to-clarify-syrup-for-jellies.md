## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of loaf sugar to every quart of water
- the white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Put the sugar and water into a stewpan
2. Set it on the fire, and when the sugar is dissolved, add the white of the egg, whipped up with a little water
3. Whisk the whole well together, and simmer very gently until it has thrown up all the scum
4. Take this off as it rises
5. Strain the syrup through a fine sieve or cloth into a basin, and keep it for use
