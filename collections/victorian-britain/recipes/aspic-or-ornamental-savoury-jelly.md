## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of knuckle of veal
- 1 cow-heel
- 3 or 4 slices of ham
- any poultry trimmings
- 2 carrots
- 1 onion
- 1 faggot of savoury herbs
- 1 glass of sherry
- 3 quarts of water
- salt
- whole white pepper
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Lay the ham on the bottom of a stewpan, cut up the veal and cow-heel into small pieces, and lay them on the ham; add the poultry trimmings, vegetables, herbs, sherry, and water, and let the whole simmer very gently for 4 hours, carefully taking away all scum that may rise to the surface.
2. Strain through a fine sieve, and pour into an earthen pan to get cold.
3. Have ready a clean stewpan, put in the jelly, and be particular to leave the sediment behind, or it will not be clear.
4. Add the whites of 3 eggs, with salt and pepper, to clarify; keep stirring over the fire, till the whole becomes very white; then draw it to the side, and let it stand till clear.
5. When this is the case, strain it through a cloth or jelly-bag, and use it for moulding poultry, etc. Tarragon vinegar may be added to give an additional flavour.
