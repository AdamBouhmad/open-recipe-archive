## Ingredients

- 1 crab
- 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of oil
- salt, white pepper, and cayenne, to taste

## Directions

1. Empty the shells, and thoroughly mix the meat with the above ingredients, and put it in the large shell.
2. Garnish with slices of cut lemon and parsley.
3. The quantity of oil may be increased when it is much liked.
