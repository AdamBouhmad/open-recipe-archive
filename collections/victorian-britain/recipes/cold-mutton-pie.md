## Ingredients

- the remains of a cold leg, loin, or neck of mutton
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 blades of pounded mace
- 1 dessertspoonful of chopped parsley
- 1 teaspoonful of minced savoury herbs
- a little minced onion or shalot
- 3 or 4 potatoes
- 1 teacupful of gravy
- crust

## Directions

1. Cold mutton may be made into very good pies if well seasoned and mixed with a few herbs; if the leg is used, cut it into very thin slices; if the loin or neck, into thin cutlets.
2. Place some at the bottom of the dish; season well with pepper, salt, mace, parsley, and herbs.
3. Then put a layer of potatoes sliced, then more mutton, and so on till the dish is full.
4. Add the gravy, cover with a crust, and bake for 1 hour.
