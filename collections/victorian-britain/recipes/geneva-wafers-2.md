## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 3 oz. of butter
- 3 oz. of flour
- 3 oz. of pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Well whisk the eggs; put them into a basin, and stir to them the butter, which should be beaten to a cream
2. Add the flour and sifted sugar gradually, and then mix all well together
3. Butter a baking-sheet, and drop on it a teaspoonful of the mixture at a time, leaving a space between each
4. Bake in a cool oven; watch the pieces of paste, and, when half done, roll them up like wafers, and put in a small wedge of bread or piece of wood, to keep them in shape
5. Return them to the oven until crisp
6. Before serving, remove the bread, put a spoonful of preserve in the widest end, and fill up with whipped cream
