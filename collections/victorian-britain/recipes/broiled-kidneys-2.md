## Ingredients

- Sheep kidneys
- pepper and salt to taste
- butter

## Directions

1. Ascertain that the kidneys are fresh, and cut them open very evenly, lengthwise, down to the root, but do not separate them.
2. Skin them, and pass a skewer under the white part of each half to keep them flat.
3. Broil over a nice clear fire, placing the inside downwards.
4. Turn them when done enough on one side, and cook them on the other.
5. Remove the skewers, place the kidneys on a very hot dish, season with pepper and salt, and put a tiny piece of butter in the middle of each.
6. Serve very hot and quickly, and send very hot plates to table.
