## Ingredients

- 2 dozen oysters
- 3 cloves
- 1 blade of mace
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of flour
- Cayenne and salt to taste
- Mashed potatoes
- A few slices of cold roast beef

## Directions

1. Put the oysters in a stewpan, with their liquor strained
2. Add the cloves, mace, butter, flour, and seasoning
3. Let them simmer gently for 5 minutes
4. Have ready in the centre of a dish round walls of mashed potatoes, browned
5. Into the middle pour the oyster sauce, quite hot
6. Round the potatoes place, in layers, slices of the beef, which should be previously broiled over a nice clear fire
