## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of common asphaltum
- 1/2 pint of linseed oil
- 1 quart of oil of turpentine

## Directions

1. Melt the asphaltum, and add gradually to it the other two ingredients.
2. Apply this with a small painter's brush, and leave it to become perfectly dry.
3. The grate will need no other cleaning, but will merely require dusting every day, and occasionally brushing with a dry black-lead brush.
4. When fires are required, the bars, cheeks, and back of the grate will need black-leading in the usual manner.
