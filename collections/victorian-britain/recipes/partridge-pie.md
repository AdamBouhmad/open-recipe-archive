## Ingredients

- 3 partridges
- pepper
- salt
- 1 teaspoonful of minced parsley
- mushrooms
- 3/4 lb. of veal cutlet
- a slice of ham
- 1/2 pint of stock
- puff paste
- egg yolk
- butter

## Directions

1. Line a pie-dish with a veal cutlet; over that place a slice of ham and a seasoning of pepper and salt.
2. Pluck, draw, and wipe the partridges; cut off the legs at the first joint, and season them inside with pepper, salt, minced parsley, and a small piece of butter.
3. Place them in the dish, and pour over the stock.
4. Line the edges of the dish with puff paste, cover with the same, brush it over with the yolk of an egg, and bake for 3/4 to 1 hour.
