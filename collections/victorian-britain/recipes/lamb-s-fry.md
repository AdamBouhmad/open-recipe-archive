## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of lamb's fry
- 3 pints of water
- egg and bread crumbs
- 1 teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the fry for 1/4 hour in the above proportion of water
2. Take it out and dry it in a cloth
3. Grate some bread down finely, mix with it a teaspoonful of chopped parsley and a high seasoning of pepper and salt
4. Brush the fry lightly over with the yolk of an egg
5. Sprinkle over the bread crumbs, and fry for 5 minutes
6. Serve very hot on a napkin in a dish, and garnish with plenty of crisped parsley
