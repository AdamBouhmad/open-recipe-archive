## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of fresh butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- salt to taste
- 1/2 gill of water
- 1/2 spoonful of white vinegar
- a very little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix the flour and water to a smooth batter, carefully rubbing down with the back of a spoon any lumps that may appear.
2. Put it in a saucepan with all the other ingredients, and let it thicken on the fire, but do not allow it to boil, lest it should taste of the flour.
