## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 ribs of beef
- wooden skewers
- tape

## Directions

1. Choose a fine rib of beef, and have it cut according to the weight you require, either wide or narrow
2. Bone and roll the meat round, secure it with wooden skewers, and, if necessary, bind it round with a piece of tape
3. Spit the beef firmly, or, if a bottle-jack is used, put the joint on the hook, and place it near a nice clear fire
4. Let it remain so till the outside of the meat is set, when draw it to a distance, and keep continually basting until the meat is done, which can be ascertained by the steam from it drawing towards the fire
5. Remove the skewers, put in a plated or silver one, and send the joint to table with gravy in the dish, and garnish with tufts of horseradish
