## Ingredients

- 1 quart of young green peas
- a small bunch of parsley
- 2 young onions
- 2 quarts of medium stock No. 105

## Directions

1. Boil the peas till quite tender, with the parsley and onions; then rub them through a sieve, and pour the stock to them.
2. Do not let it boil after the peas are added, or you will spoil the colour.
3. Serve very hot.
