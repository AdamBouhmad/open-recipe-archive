## Ingredients

- 12 fine black truffles
- a few slices of fat bacon
- 1 carrot
- 1 turnip
- 2 onions
- a bunch of savoury herbs, including parsley
- 1 bay-leaf
- 2 cloves
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- 2 glasses of champagne
- 1/2 pint of stock

## Directions

1. Carefully select the truffles, reject those that have a musty smell, and wash them well with a brush, in cold water only, until perfectly clean
2. Put the bacon into a stewpan, with the truffles and the remaining ingredients
3. Simmer these gently for an hour, and let the whole cool in the stewpan
4. When to be served, rewarm them, and drain them on a clean cloth
5. Arrange them on a delicately white napkin, that it may contrast as strongly as possible with the truffles, and serve
