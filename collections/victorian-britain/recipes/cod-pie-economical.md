## Ingredients

- Any remains of cold cod
- 12 oysters
- Sufficient melted butter to moisten it
- Mashed potatoes enough to fill up the dish

## Directions

1. Flake the fish from the bone, and carefully take away all the skin.
2. Lay it in a pie-dish, pour over the melted butter and oysters (or oyster sauce, if there is any left), and cover with mashed potatoes.
3. Bake for 1/2 an hour, and send to table of a nice brown colour.
