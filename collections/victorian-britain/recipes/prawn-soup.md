## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of fish stock or water
- 2 pints of prawns
- the crumbs of a French roll
- anchovy sauce or mushroom ketchup to taste
- 1 blade of mace
- 1 pint of vinegar
- a little lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Pick out the tails of the prawns, put the bodies in a stewpan with 1 blade of mace, 1/2 pint of vinegar, and the same quantity of water; stew them for 1/4 hour, and strain off the liquor.
2. Put the fish stock or water into a stewpan; add the strained liquor, pound the prawns with the crumb of a roll moistened with a little of the soup, rub them through a tammy, and mix them by degrees with the soup; add ketchup or anchovy sauce to taste, with a little lemon-juice.
3. When it is well cooked, put in a few picked prawns; let them get thoroughly hot, and serve.
4. If not thick enough, put in a little butter and flour.
