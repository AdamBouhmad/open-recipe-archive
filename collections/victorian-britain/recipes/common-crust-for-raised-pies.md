## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 1/2 pint of water
- 1-1/2 oz. of butter
- 1-1/2 oz. of lard
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan the water; when it boils, add the butter and lard
2. When these are melted, make a hole in the middle of the flour
3. Pour in the water gradually
4. Beat it well with a wooden spoon, and be particular in not making the paste too soft
5. When it is well mixed, knead it with the hands until quite stiff, dredging a little flour over the paste and board, to prevent them from sticking
6. When it is well kneaded, place it before the fire, with a cloth covered over it, for a few minutes; it will then be more easily worked into shape
