## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of mutton suet
- salt and white pepper to taste
- 4 lbs. of the neck of pork
- 1 dessertspoonful of powdered sage

## Directions

1. Well dry the flour, mince the suet, and put these with the butter into a saucepan, to be made hot, and add a little salt.
2. When melted, mix it up into a stiff paste, and put it before the fire with a cloth over it until ready to make up.
3. Chop the pork into small pieces, season it with white pepper, salt, and powdered sage.
4. Divide the paste into rather small pieces, raise it in a round or oval form, fill with the meat, and bake in a brick oven.
5. These pies will require a fiercer oven than those in the preceding recipe, as they are made so much smaller.
