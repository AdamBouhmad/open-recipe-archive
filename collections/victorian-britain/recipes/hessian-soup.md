## Ingredients

- 1/2 ox's head
- 1 pint of split peas
- 3 carrots
- 6 turnips
- 6 potatoes
- 6 onions
- 1 head of celery
- 1 bunch of savoury herbs
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 blades of mace
- a little allspice
- 4 cloves
- the crumb of a French roll
- 6 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Clean the head, rub it with salt and water, and soak it for 5 hours in warm water
2. Simmer it in the water till tender, put it into a pan and let it cool
3. Skim off all the fat
4. Take out the head, and add the vegetables cut up small, and the peas which have been previously soaked
5. Simmer them without the meat, till they are done enough to pulp through a sieve
6. Add the seasoning, with pieces of the meat cut up
7. Give one boil, and serve
