## Ingredients

- 1 lobster
- 2 oz. of butter
- grated nutmeg
- salt, pepper, and pounded mace, to taste
- bread crumbs
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Pound the meat of the lobster to a smooth paste with the butter and seasoning, and add a few bread crumbs.
2. Beat the eggs, and make the whole mixture into the form of a lobster; pound the spawn, and sprinkle over it.
3. Bake 1/4 hour, and just before serving, lay over it the tail and body shell, with the small claws underneath, to resemble a lobster.
