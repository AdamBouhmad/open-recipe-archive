## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of rice
- 4 lbs. of wheat flour
- nearly 3 tablespoonfuls of yeast
- 1/4 oz. of salt
- warm water

## Directions

1. Boil the rice in water until it is quite tender
2. Pour off the water, and put the rice, before it is cold, to the flour
3. Mix these well together with the yeast, salt, and sufficient warm water to make the whole into a smooth dough
4. Let it rise by the side of the fire
5. Form it into loaves, and bake them from 1-1/2 to 2 hours, according to their size
