## Ingredients

- 1/4 pint of water
- whites of 2 eggs
- currants
- pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Select very fine bunches of red or white currants, and well beat the whites of the eggs.
2. Mix these with the water.
3. Take the currants, a bunch at a time, and dip them in.
4. Let them drain for a minute or two, and roll them in very fine pounded sugar.
5. Lay them to dry on paper, when the sugar will crystallize round each currant.
