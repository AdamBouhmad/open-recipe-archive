## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of suet crust
- 1-1/2 pint of green gooseberries
- 1/4 lb. of moist sugar

## Directions

1. Line a pudding-basin with suet crust, rolled out to about 1/2 inch in thickness
2. With a pair of scissors, cut off the tops and tails of the gooseberries
3. Fill the basin with the fruit
4. Put in the sugar
5. Cover with crust
6. Pinch the edges of the pudding together
7. Tie over it a floured cloth
8. Put it into boiling water
9. Boil from 2-1/2 to 3 hours
10. Turn it out of the basin, and serve with a jug of cream
