## Ingredients

- 1 pint of water
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 4 teaspoonfuls of vinegar
- 4 fresh eggs
- 1/2 gill of cream
- salt, pepper, and pounded sugar to taste
- 1 oz. of butter

## Directions

1. Put the water, vinegar, and salt into a frying-pan, and break each egg into a separate cup
2. bring the water to boil, and slip the eggs gently into it without breaking the yolks
3. Simmer them from 3 to 4 minutes, but not longer
4. with a slice, lift them out on to a hot dish, and trim the edges
5. Empty the pan of its contents, put in the cream, add a seasoning to taste of pepper, salt, and pounded sugar
6. bring the whole to the boiling-point; then add the butter, broken into small pieces
7. toss the pan round and round till the butter is melted
8. pour it over the eggs, and serve
