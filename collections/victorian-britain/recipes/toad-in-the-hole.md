## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 lb. of rump-steak
- 1 sheep's kidney
- pepper and salt to taste
- 3 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Cut up the steak and kidney into convenient-sized pieces, and put them into a pie-dish, with a good seasoning of salt and pepper
2. Mix the flour with a small quantity of milk at first, to prevent its being lumpy
3. Add the remainder of the milk and the 3 eggs, which should be well beaten
4. Put in the salt, stir the batter for about 5 minutes, and pour it over the steak
5. Place it in a tolerably brisk oven immediately, and bake for 1-1/2 hour
