## Ingredients

- 2 tench
- 2 eels
- 2 onions
- a faggot of herbs
- 4 blades of mace
- 3 anchovies
- 1 pint of water
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1 teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- the yolks of 6 hard-boiled eggs
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Clean and bone the tench, skin and bone the eels, and cut them into pieces 2 inches long, and leave the sides of the tench whole.
2. Put the bones into a stewpan with the onions, herbs, mace, anchovies, water, and seasoning, and let them simmer gently for 1 hour.
3. Strain it off, put it to cool, and skim off all the fat.
4. Lay the tench and eels in a pie-dish, and between each layer put seasoning, chopped parsley, and hard-boiled eggs.
5. Pour in part of the strained liquor, cover in with puff paste, and bake for 1/2 hour or rather more.
6. When done, heat the remainder of the liquor, which pour into the pie.
