## Ingredients

- pheasant
- two snipes
- beef
- lard
- truffles
- pepper and salt to taste
- bread
- liver
- anchovy
- fresh butter
- Florida oranges

## Directions

1. Pluck the pheasant
2. Draw the two snipes, putting the bodies on one plate, and the livers, &c., on another
3. Take off the flesh, and mince it finely with a little beef, lard, a few truffles, pepper and salt to taste
4. Stuff the pheasant carefully with this mixture
5. Cut a slice of bread, larger considerably than the bird
6. Cover it with the liver, &c., and a few truffles
7. Add an anchovy and a little fresh butter to these
8. Put the bread, &c., into the dripping-pan
9. Roast the bird
10. Place it on the preparation
11. Surround it with Florida oranges
