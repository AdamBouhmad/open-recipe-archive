## Ingredients

- 3 dozen tomatoes
- To every pound of tomato-pulp: 1 pint of Chili vinegar
- 1 oz. of garlic
- 1 oz. of shalot
- 2 oz. of salt
- 1 large green capsicum
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cayenne
- 2 pickled gherkins
- 6 pickled onions
- 1 pint of common vinegar
- the juice of 6 lemons

## Directions

1. Choose the tomatoes when quite ripe and red
2. Put them in a jar with a cover to it and bake them till tender
3. The better way is to put them in the oven overnight when it will not be too hot
4. Examine them in the morning to see if they are tender
5. Do not allow them to remain in the oven long enough to break them
6. They should be sufficiently soft to skin nicely and rub through the sieve
7. Measure the pulp
8. To each pound of pulp add the above proportion of vinegar and other ingredients
9. Chop very fine the garlic, shalot, capsicum, onion, and gherkins
10. Boil the whole together till everything is tender
11. Rub it through a sieve again and add the lemon-juice
12. Boil the whole again till it becomes as thick as cream, keeping it continually stirred
13. Bottle it when quite cold, cork well, and seal the corks
