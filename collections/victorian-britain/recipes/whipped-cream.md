## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cream
- 3 oz. of pounded sugar
- 1 glass of sherry or sweet white wine
- rind of 1/2 lemon
- white of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Rub the sugar on the lemon-rind and pound it in a mortar until quite fine
2. Beat up the white of the egg until quite stiff
3. Put the cream into a large bowl with the sugar, wine, and beaten egg
4. Whip it to a froth
5. As fast as the froth rises, take it off with a skimmer and put it on a sieve to drain in a cool place
6. Whip the cream in a cool place, and in summer, over ice if obtainable
7. Make this the day before it is wanted
8. Serve plain whipped cream on a glass dish and garnish with strips of angelica, pastry leaves, or pieces of bright-coloured jelly
