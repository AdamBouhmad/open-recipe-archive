## Ingredients

- 8 or 10 tomatoes
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 oz. of butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Take off the stalks from the tomatoes
2. cut them into thick slices, and put them into a deep baking-dish
3. add a plentiful seasoning of pepper and salt, and butter in the above proportion
4. cover the whole with bread crumbs
5. drop over these a little clarified butter
6. bake in a moderate oven from 20 minutes to 1/2 hour, and serve very hot
