## Ingredients

- 3 oz. of grated chocolate
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1-1/2 pint of cream
- 1/2 oz. of clarified isinglass
- the yolks of 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs well; put them into a basin with the grated chocolate, the sugar, and 1 pint of the cream
2. Stir these ingredients well together, pour them into a jug, and set this jug in a saucepan of boiling water
3. Stir it one way until the mixture thickens, but do not allow it to boil, or it will curdle
4. Strain the cream through a sieve into a basin
5. Stir in the isinglass and the other 1/2 pint of cream, which should be well whipped
6. Mix all well together, and pour it into a mould which has been previously oiled with the purest salad-oil, and, if at hand, set it in ice until wanted for table
