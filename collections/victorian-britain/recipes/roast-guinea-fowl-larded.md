## Ingredients

- A Guinea-fowl
- lardoons
- flour
- salt
- gravy
- bread-sauce

## Directions

1. When this bird is larded, it should be trussed the same as a pheasant; if plainly roasted, truss it like a turkey.
2. After larding and trussing it, put it down to roast at a brisk fire.
3. Keep it well basted, and a short time before serving, dredge it with a little flour, and let it froth nicely.
4. The breast, if larded, should be covered with a piece of paper, and removed about 10 minutes before serving.
5. Serve with a little gravy in the dish, and a tureen of the same, and one of well-made bread-sauce.
