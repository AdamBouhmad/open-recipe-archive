## Ingredients

- 3 large cucumbers
- flour
- butter
- rather more than 1/2 pint of good brown gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the cucumbers lengthwise the size of the dish they are intended to be served in
2. Empty them of the seeds, and put them into boiling water with a little salt, and let them simmer for 5 minutes
3. Take them out, place them in another stewpan, with the gravy, and let them boil over a brisk fire until the cucumbers are tender
4. Should these be bitter, add a lump of sugar
5. Carefully dish them, skim the sauce, pour over the cucumbers, and serve
