## Ingredients

- Rib of beef bones
- 1 onion chopped fine
- a few slices of carrot and turnip
- 1/4 pint of gravy
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. The bones for this dish should have left on them a slight covering of meat; saw them into pieces 3 inches long
2. Season them with pepper and salt, and put them into a stewpan with the remaining ingredients
3. Stew gently, until the vegetables are tender, and serve
