## Ingredients

- 1 quart bottle of champagne
- 2 bottles of soda-water
- 1 liqueur-glass of brandy or Curaçoa
- 2 tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- 1 lb. of pounded ice
- a sprig of green borage

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients into a silver cup; stir them together, and serve the same as claret-cup.
2. Should the above proportion of sugar not be found sufficient to suit some tastes, increase the quantity.
3. When borage is not easily obtainable, substitute for it a few slices of cucumber-rind.
