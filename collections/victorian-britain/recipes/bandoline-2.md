## Ingredients

- 1 oz. of gum-tragacanth
- 1/4 pint of cold water
- 3 pennyworth of essence of almonds
- 2 teaspoonfuls of old rum

## Directions

1. Put the gum-tragacanth into a wide-mouthed bottle with the cold water
2. Let it stand till dissolved, then stir into it the essence of almonds
3. Let it remain for an hour or two, when pour the rum on the top
4. When any is required for use, dilute it with a little cold water until the desired consistency is obtained, and keep it in a small bottle, well corked, for use
