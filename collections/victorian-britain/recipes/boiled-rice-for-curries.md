## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of rice
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Pick, wash, and soak the rice in plenty of cold water
2. Have ready a saucepan of boiling water, drop the rice into it, and keep it boiling quickly, with the lid uncovered, until it is tender, but not soft
3. Take it up, drain it, and put it on a dish before the fire to dry
4. Do not handle it much with a spoon, but shake it about a little with two forks, that it may all be equally dried, and strew over a little salt
5. It may be heaped lightly on a dish by itself, or be laid round the dish as a border, with a curry or fricassee in the centre
