## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of Normandy pippins
- 1 quart of water
- 1/2 teaspoonful of powdered cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoonful of ground ginger
- 1 lb. of moist sugar
- 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Well wash the pippins, and put them into 1 quart of water with the above proportion of cinnamon and ginger, and let them stand 12 hours
2. Put these all together into a stewpan, with the lemon sliced thinly, and half the moist sugar
3. Let them boil slowly until the pippins are half done
4. Add the remainder of the sugar, and simmer until they are quite tender
5. Serve on glass dishes for dessert
