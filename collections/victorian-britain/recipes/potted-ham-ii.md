## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of lean ham
- 1/2 lb. of fat
- 1 teaspoonful of pounded mace
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pounded allspice
- 1/2 nutmeg
- pepper to taste
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Cut some slices from the remains of a cold ham and mince them small.
2. Pound the ham in a mortar to a fine paste, with the fat.
3. Gradually add the seasoning and spices, and be very particular that all the ingredients are well mixed and the spices well pounded.
4. Press the mixture into potting-pots, pour over clarified butter, and keep it in a cool place.
