## Ingredients

- Puff-paste
- white of an egg
- sifted sugar
- jelly or preserve

## Directions

1. Make some good puff-paste; roll it out to the thickness of 1/4 inch, and, with a round fluted paste-cutter, stamp out as many round pieces as may be required
2. Brush over the upper side with the white of an egg
3. Roll up the pieces, pressing the paste lightly together where it joins
4. Place the rolls on a baking-sheet, and bake for about 1/4 hour
5. A few minutes before they are done, brush them over with the white of an egg; strew over sifted sugar, put them back in the oven
6. When the icing is firm and of a pale brown colour, they are done
7. Place a strip of jelly or preserve across each roll, dish them high on a napkin, and serve cold
