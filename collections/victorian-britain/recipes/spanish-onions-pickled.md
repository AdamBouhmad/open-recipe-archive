## Ingredients

- Onions
- vinegar
- salt
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Cut the onions in thin slices
2. Put a layer of them in the bottom of a jar
3. Sprinkle with salt and cayenne
4. Add another layer of onions and season as before
5. Proceed in this manner till the jar is full
6. Pour in sufficient vinegar to cover the whole
7. The pickle will be fit for use in a month
