## Ingredients

- 1 glass of port wine
- 1 tablespoonful of Leamington sauce (No. 459)
- 1 tablespoonful of mushroom ketchup
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- 1 slice of lemon-peel
- 1 large shalot cut in slices
- 1 blade of mace
- cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients into a stewpan, set it over the fire, and let it simmer for about 5 minutes; then strain and serve the sauce in a tureen.
