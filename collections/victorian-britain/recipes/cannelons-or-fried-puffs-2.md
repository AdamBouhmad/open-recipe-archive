## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of puff-paste
- apricot or any kind of preserve
- hot lard
- white of egg
- sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Make some good puff-paste; roll it out very thin, and cut it into pieces of an equal size, about 2 inches wide and 8 inches long.
2. Place upon each piece a spoonful of jam, wet the edges with the white of egg, and fold the paste over twice.
3. Slightly press the edges together, that the jam may not escape in the frying.
4. When all are prepared, fry them in boiling lard until of a nice brown, letting them remain by the side of the fire after they are coloured, that the paste may be thoroughly done.
5. Drain them before the fire, dish on a d'oyley, sprinkle over them sifted sugar, and serve.
