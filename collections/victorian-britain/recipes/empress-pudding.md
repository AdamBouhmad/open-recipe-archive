## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of rice
- 2 oz. of butter
- 3 eggs
- jam
- sufficient milk

## Directions

1. Boil the rice in the milk until very soft
2. Add the butter
3. Boil it for a few minutes after the latter ingredient is put in
4. Set it by to cool
5. Well beat the eggs
6. Stir these in
7. Line a dish with puff-paste
8. Put over this a layer of rice, then a thin layer of any kind of jam, then another layer of rice, and proceed in this manner until the dish is full
9. Bake in a moderate oven for 3/4 hour
