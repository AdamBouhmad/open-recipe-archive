## Ingredients

- 3 sweetbreads
- Egg and bread crumbs
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Rather more than 1/2 pint of Maître d'hôtel sauce No. 466

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbreads in warm water for an hour
2. Boil them for 10 minutes
3. Cut them in slices
4. Egg and bread crumb them
5. Season with pepper and salt
6. Put them into a frying-pan with the above proportion of butter
7. Keep turning them until done, which will be in about 10 minutes
8. Dish them
9. Pour over them a Maître d'hôtel sauce, made by recipe No. 466
10. The dish may be garnished with slices of cut lemon
