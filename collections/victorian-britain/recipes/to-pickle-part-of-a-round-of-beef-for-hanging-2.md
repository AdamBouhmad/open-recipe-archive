## Ingredients

- 14 lbs. of a round of beef
- 1-1/2 lb. of salt
- 1/2 oz. of powdered saltpetre
- or 1 lb. of salt
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 4 oz. of powdered saltpetre

## Directions

1. Rub in, and sprinkle either of the above mixtures on 14 lbs. of meat
2. Keep it in an earthenware pan, or a deep wooden tray, and turn twice a week during 3 weeks
3. Bind up the beef tightly with coarse linen tape, and hang it in a kitchen in which a fire is constantly kept, for 3 weeks
