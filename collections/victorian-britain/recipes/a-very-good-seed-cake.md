## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 6 eggs
- 3/4 lb. of sifted sugar
- pounded mace and grated nutmeg to taste
- 1 lb. of flour
- 3/4 oz. of caraway seeds
- 1 wineglassful of brandy

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Dredge in the flour
3. Add the sugar, mace, nutmeg, and caraway seeds, and mix these ingredients well together
4. Whisk the eggs, stir to them the brandy, and beat the cake again for 10 minutes
5. Put it into a tin lined with buttered paper
6. Bake it from 1-1/2 to 2 hours
