## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 oz. of whole ginger
- 1/4 pint of whiskey
- 3 lbs. of apples
- 2 lbs. of white sugar
- the juice of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Bruise the ginger, put it into a small jar, pour over sufficient whiskey to cover it, and let it remain for 3 days
2. Cut the apples into thin slices, after paring and coring them
3. Add the sugar and the lemon-juice, which should be strained
4. Simmer all together very gently until the apples are transparent, but not broken
5. Serve cold, and garnish the dish with slices of candied lemon-peel or preserved ginger
