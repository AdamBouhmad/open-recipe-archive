## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of suet
- 1/2 lb. of bread crumbs
- 1/2 lb. of moist sugar
- the rind and juice of 1 large lemon

## Directions

1. Chop the suet finely, mix it with the bread crumbs and sugar
2. mince the lemon-rind and strain the juice
3. stir these into the other ingredients
4. mix well, and put the mixture into small buttered cups, and bake for rather more than 1/2 hour
5. turn them out on the dish, and serve with lemon-sauce
6. The above ingredients may be made into small balls, and boiled for about 1/2 hour; they should then be served with the same sauce as when baked
