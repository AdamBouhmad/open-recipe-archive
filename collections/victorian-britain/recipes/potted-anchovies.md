## Ingredients

- anchovies
- pounded mace
- cayenne
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pound the anchovies in a mortar to a paste
2. Mix the butter gradually with them
3. Rub the whole through a sieve
4. Put it by in small pots for use
5. Carefully exclude the air with a bladder
