## Ingredients

- 1/2 breakfast-cupful of sugar
- 1 breakfast-cupful of rich sour cream
- 2 breakfast-cupfuls of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
- honey to taste

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and cream together
2. Dredge in the flour, with as much honey as will flavour the mixture nicely
3. Stir it well, that all the ingredients may be thoroughly mixed
4. Add the carbonate of soda
5. Beat the cake well for another 5 minutes
6. Put it into a buttered tin
7. Bake it from 1/2 to 3/4 hour
8. Let it be eaten warm
