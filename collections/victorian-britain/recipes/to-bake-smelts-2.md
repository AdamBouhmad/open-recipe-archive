## Ingredients

- 12 smelts
- bread crumbs
- 1/4 lb. of fresh butter
- 2 blades of pounded mace
- salt and cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Wash, and dry the fish thoroughly in a cloth
2. Arrange them nicely in a flat baking-dish
3. Cover them with fine bread crumbs
4. Place little pieces of butter all over them
5. Season and bake for 15 minutes
6. Just before serving, add a squeeze of lemon-juice, and garnish with fried parsley and cut lemon
