## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of ripe white currants
- the rind of 2 lemons
- 1/4 oz. of grated ginger
- 1 quart of whiskey
- 1 lb. of lump sugar

## Directions

1. Strip the currants from the stalks
2. Put them into a large jug
3. Add the lemon-rind, ginger, and whiskey
4. Cover the jug closely
5. Let it remain covered for 24 hours
6. Strain through a hair sieve
7. Add the lump sugar
8. Let it stand 12 hours longer
9. Bottle and cork well
