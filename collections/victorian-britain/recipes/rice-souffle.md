## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of ground rice
- 1 pint of milk
- 5 eggs
- pounded sugar to taste
- flavouring of lemon-rind, vanilla, coffee, chocolate, or anything that may be preferred
- a piece of butter the size of a walnut

## Directions

1. Mix the ground rice with 6 tablespoonfuls of the milk quite smoothly
2. Put it into a saucepan with the remainder of the milk and butter
3. Keep stirring it over the fire for about 1/4 hour, or until the mixture thickens
4. Separate the yolks from the whites of the eggs
5. Beat the former in a basin
6. Stir to them the rice and sufficient pounded sugar to sweeten the soufflé
7. Add the sugar as sparingly as possible, as the less sugar there is used, the lighter will be the soufflé
8. Whisk the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth or snow
9. Mix them with the other preparation
10. Pour the whole into a soufflé-dish
11. Put it instantly into the oven
12. Bake it about 1/2 hour in a moderate oven
13. Take it out, hold a salamander or hot shovel over the top, sprinkle sifted sugar over it
14. Send the soufflé to table in the dish it was baked in, either with a napkin pinned round, or inclosed in a more ornamental dish
