## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoonful of mixed mustard
- 1 teaspoonful of pounded sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of salad oil
- 4 tablespoonfuls of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- cayenne and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put the mixed mustard into a salad-bowl with the sugar, and add the oil drop by drop, carefully stirring and mixing all these ingredients well together.
2. Proceed in this manner with the milk and vinegar, which must be added very gradually, or the sauce will curdle.
3. Put in the seasoning, when the mixture will be ready for use.
