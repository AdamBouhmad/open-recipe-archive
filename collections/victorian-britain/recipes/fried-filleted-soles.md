## Ingredients

- large soles
- egg
- bread crumbs
- fried parsley
- cut lemon

## Directions

1. Skin and wash the fish, and raise the meat carefully from the bones, and divide it into nice handsome pieces.
2. Roll the fillets, after dividing each one in two pieces, and either bind them round with twine, or run a small skewer through them.
3. Brush over with egg, and cover with bread crumbs.
4. Fry them as directed in the foregoing recipe, and garnish with fried parsley and cut lemon.
