## Ingredients

- 7 good-sized apples
- the rind of 1/2 lemon or 4 cloves
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 3/4 pint of water
- 1/2 pint of custard No. 1423

## Directions

1. Pare and take out the cores of the apples, without dividing them, and, if possible, leave the stalks on
2. Boil the sugar and water together for 10 minutes
3. Put in the apples with the lemon-rind or cloves, whichever flavour may be preferred, and simmer gently until they are tender, taking care not to let them break
4. Dish them neatly on a glass dish
5. Reduce the syrup by boiling it quickly for a few minutes
6. Let it cool a little
7. Pour it over the apples
8. Have ready quite 1/2 pint of custard made by recipe No. 1423
9. Pour it round, but not over, the apples when they are quite cold
10. A few almonds blanched and cut into strips, and stuck in the apples, would improve their appearance
