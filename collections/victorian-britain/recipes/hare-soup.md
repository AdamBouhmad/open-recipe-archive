## Ingredients

- 1 hare fresh-killed
- 1 lb. of lean gravy-beef
- 1 slice of ham
- 1 carrot
- 2 onions
- 1 faggot of savoury herbs
- 1/4 oz. of whole black pepper
- browned flour
- 1/4 pint of port wine
- the crumb of two French rolls
- salt and cayenne to taste
- 3 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Skin and paunch the hare, saving the liver and as much blood as possible
2. Cut it in pieces, and put it in a stewpan with all the ingredients, and simmer gently for 8 hours
3. Strain through a sieve, put the best parts of the hare in the soup, and serve
