## Ingredients

- 1 calf's foot
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 pint of water
- 1 blade of mace
- the rind of 1/4 lemon
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Well clean the foot, and either stew or bake it in the milk-and-water with the other ingredients from 3 to 4 hours
2. To enhance the flavour, an onion and a small quantity of celery may be added, if approved
3. 1/2 a teacupful of cream, stirred in just before serving, is also a great improvement to this dish
