## Ingredients

- 1 lemon
- 1 tumbler-ful of cold water
- pounded sugar to taste
- 4 small teaspoonful of carbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from the lemon and strain it
2. Add it to the water, with sufficient pounded sugar to sweeten the whole nicely
3. When well mixed, put in the soda
4. Stir well
5. Drink while the mixture is in an effervescing state
