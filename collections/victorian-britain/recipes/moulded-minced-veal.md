## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of cold roast veal
- a small slice of bacon
- 1/4 teaspoonful of minced lemon-peel
- 1/2 onion chopped fine
- salt, pepper, and pounded mace to taste
- a slice of toast soaked in milk
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mince the meat very fine, after removing from it all skin and outside pieces, and chop the bacon; mix these well together, adding the lemon-peel, onion, seasoning, mace, and toast.
2. When all the ingredients are thoroughly incorporated, heat up an egg, with which bind the mixture.
3. Butter a shape, put in the meat, and bake for 3/4 hour; turn it out of the mould carefully, and pour round it a good brown gravy.
