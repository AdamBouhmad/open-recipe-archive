## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- a little flour
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Boil 6 eggs for 20 minutes, strip off the shells, take the yolks and pound them in a mortar.
2. Beat the yolks of the other 2 eggs; add them, with a little flour and salt, to those pounded; mix all well together, and roll into balls.
3. Boil them before they are put into the soup or other dish they may be intended for.
