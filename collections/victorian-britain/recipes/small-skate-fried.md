## Ingredients

- Skate
- sufficient vinegar to cover them
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 sliced onion
- a small bunch of parsley
- the juice of 1/2 lemon
- hot dripping

## Directions

1. Cleanse the skate, lay them in a dish, with sufficient vinegar to cover them
2. Add the salt, pepper, onion, parsley, and lemon-juice
3. Let the fish remain in this pickle for 1-1/2 hour
4. Drain them well, flour them, and fry of a nice brown, in hot dripping
