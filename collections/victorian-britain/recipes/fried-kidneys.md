## Ingredients

- Kidneys
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys open without quite dividing them and remove the skin
2. Put a small piece of butter in the frying-pan
3. When the butter is melted, lay in the kidneys the flat side downwards
4. Fry them for 7 or 8 minutes, turning them when they are half-done
5. Serve on a piece of dry toast
6. Season with pepper and salt, and put a small piece of butter in each kidney
7. Pour the gravy from the pan over them, and serve very hot
