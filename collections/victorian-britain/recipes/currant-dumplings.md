## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 6 oz. of suet
- 1/2 lb. of currants
- rather more than 1/2 pint of water

## Directions

1. Chop the suet finely, mix it with the flour, and add the currants, which should be nicely washed, picked, and dried
2. Mix the whole to a limp paste with the water
3. Divide it into 7 or 8 dumplings; tie them in cloths, and boil for 1-1/4 hour
4. Alternatively, they may be boiled without a cloth: make them into round balls, and drop into boiling water, and move them about at first, to prevent them from sticking to the bottom of the saucepan
5. Serve with a cut lemon, cold butter, and sifted sugar
