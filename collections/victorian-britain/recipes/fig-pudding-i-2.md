## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of figs
- 1 lb. of suet
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of bread crumbs
- 2 eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Cut the figs into small pieces, grate the bread finely, and chop the suet very small; mix these well together, add the flour, the eggs, which should be well beaten, and sufficient milk to form the whole into a stiff paste
2. butter a mould or basin, press the pudding into it very closely, tie it down with a cloth, and boil for 3 hours, or rather longer
3. turn it out of the mould, and serve with melted butter, wine-sauce, or cream
