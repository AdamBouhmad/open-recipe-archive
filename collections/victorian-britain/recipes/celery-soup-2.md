## Ingredients

- 9 heads of celery
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- nutmeg to taste
- 1 lump of sugar
- 1/2 pint of strong stock
- a pint of cream
- 2 quarts of boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut the celery into small pieces; throw it into the water, seasoned with the nutmeg, salt, and sugar.
2. Boil it till sufficiently tender; pass it through a sieve, add the stock, and simmer it for half an hour.
3. Now put in the cream, bring it to the boiling point, and serve immediately.
