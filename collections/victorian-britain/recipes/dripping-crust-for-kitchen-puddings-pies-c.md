## Ingredients

- 1 lb flour
- 6 oz clarified beef dripping
- 1/2 pint water

## Directions

1. Clarify the dripping by recipes No. 621 or 622
2. Weigh it, and to every lb of flour allow the above proportion of dripping
3. With a knife, work the flour into a smooth paste with the water
4. Roll it out 3 times, each time placing on the crust 2 oz of the dripping, broken into small pieces
