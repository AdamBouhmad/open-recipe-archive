## Ingredients

- Flounders
- egg
- bread crumbs
- boiling lard
- crisped parsley

## Directions

1. Cleanse the fish, and, two hours before they are wanted, rub them inside and out with salt, to render them firm
2. Wash and wipe them very dry, dip them into egg, and sprinkle over with bread crumbs
3. Fry them in boiling lard, dish on a hot napkin, and garnish with crisped parsley
