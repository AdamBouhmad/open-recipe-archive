## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 lbs. of moist sugar
- 3/4 lb. of salt
- 1/4 lb. of garlic
- 1/4 lb. of onions
- 3/4 lb. of powdered ginger
- 1/4 lb. of dried chilies
- 3/4 lb. of mustard-seed
- 3/4 lb. of stoned raisins
- 2 bottles of best vinegar
- 30 large unripe sour apples

## Directions

1. Make the sugar into syrup
2. Pound the garlic, onions, and ginger finely in a mortar
3. Wash the mustard-seed in cold vinegar, and dry it in the sun
4. Peel, core, and slice the apples, and boil them in a bottle and a half of the vinegar
5. When all this is done and the apples are quite cold, put them into a large pan
6. Gradually mix the whole of the rest of the ingredients, including the remaining half-bottle of vinegar
7. Stir well until the whole is thoroughly blended
8. Put into bottles for use
9. Tie a piece of wet bladder over the mouths of the bottles, after they are well corked
