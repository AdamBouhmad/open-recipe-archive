## Ingredients

- 6 cucumbers
- 3 moderate-sized onions
- not quite 1 pint of white stock
- cayenne and salt to taste
- the yolks of 2 eggs
- a very little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pare and slice the cucumbers, take out the seeds, and cut the onions into thin slices
2. Put these both into a stewpan, with the stock, and let them boil for 1/4 hour or longer, should the cucumbers be very large
3. Beat up the yolks of 2 eggs
4. Stir these into the sauce
5. Add the cayenne, salt, and grated nutmeg
6. Bring it to the point of boiling, and serve
7. Do not allow the sauce to boil, or it will curdle
