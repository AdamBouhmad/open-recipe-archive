## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 pint of damsons
- 1/4 lb. of moist sugar
- 3/4 lb. of suet or butter crust
- 3/4 lb. of flour

## Directions

1. Make a suet crust with 3/4 lb. of flour by recipe No. 1215
2. Line a buttered pudding-basin with a portion of it
3. Fill the basin with the damsons
4. Sweeten them, and put on the lid
5. Pinch the edges of the crust together, that the juice does not escape
6. Tie over a floured cloth
7. Put the pudding into boiling water
8. Boil from 2-1/2 to 3 hours
