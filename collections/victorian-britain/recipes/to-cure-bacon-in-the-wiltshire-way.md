## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 lb. of coarse sugar
- 1-1/2 lb. of bay-salt
- 6 oz. of saltpetre
- 1 lb. of common salt

## Directions

1. Sprinkle each flitch with salt, and let the blood drain off for 24 hours; then pound and mix the above ingredients well together and rub it well into the meat, which should be turned every day for a month; then hang it to dry, and afterwards smoke it for 10 days.
