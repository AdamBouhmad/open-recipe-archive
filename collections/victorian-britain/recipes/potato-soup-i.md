## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of mealy potatoes, boiled or steamed very dry
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 quarts of stock No. 105

## Directions

1. When the potatoes are boiled, mash them smoothly, that no lumps remain
2. Gradually put them to the boiling stock
3. Pass it through a sieve, season, and simmer for 5 minutes
4. Skim well, and serve with fried bread
