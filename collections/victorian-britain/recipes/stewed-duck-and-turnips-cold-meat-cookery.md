## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast duck
- 1/2 pint of good gravy
- 4 shalots
- a few slices of carrot
- a small bunch of savoury herbs
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- 1 lb. of turnips, weighed after being peeled
- 2 oz. of butter
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cut up the duck into joints, fry the shalots, carrots, and herbs, and put them, with the duck, into the gravy; add the pounded mace, and stew gently for 20 minutes or 1/2 hour.
