## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of rice
- 1 quart of stock or broth
- 3 oz. of butter
- minced fowl
- egg
- bread crumbs
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Put the rice into the proportion of cold stock or broth, and let it boil very gently for 1/2 hour
2. Add the butter, and simmer it till quite dry and soft
3. When cold, make it into balls, hollow out the inside, and fill with minced fowl made by recipe No. 956
4. Cover over with rice, dip the balls into egg, sprinkle them with bread crumbs, and fry a nice brown
5. Dish them, and garnish with fried parsley
6. Oysters, white sauce, or a little cream, may be stirred into the rice before it cools
