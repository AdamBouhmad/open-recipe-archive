## Ingredients

- The remains of a cold shoulder of lamb
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 oz. of butter
- about 1/2 pint of stock or gravy
- 1 tablespoonful of shalot vinegar
- 3 or 4 pickled gherkins

## Directions

1. Take the blade-bone from the shoulder, and cut the meat into collops as neatly as possible
2. Season the bone with pepper and salt, pour a little oiled butter over it, and place it in the oven to warm through
3. Put the stock into a stewpan, add the ketchup and shalot vinegar, and lay in the pieces of lamb
4. Let these heat gradually through, but do not allow them to boil
5. Take the blade-bone out of the oven, and place it on a gridiron over a sharp fire to brown
6. Slice the gherkins, put them into the hash, and dish it with the blade-bone in the centre
7. It may be garnished with croutons or sippets of toasted bread
