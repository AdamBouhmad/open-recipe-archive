## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 6 anchovies
- 1 small bunch of parsley

## Directions

1. Wash, bone, and pound the anchovies well in a mortar
2. Scald the parsley, chop it, and rub through a sieve
3. Pound all the ingredients together
4. Mix well, and make the butter into pats immediately
5. Garnish with parsley
