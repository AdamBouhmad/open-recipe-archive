## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of the best mustard
- 1/4 lb. of flour
- 1/2 oz. of salt
- 4 shalots
- 4 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of ketchup
- 1/4 bottle of anchovy sauce
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Put the mustard, flour, and salt into a basin, and make them into a stiff paste with boiling water
2. Boil the shalots with the vinegar, ketchup, and anchovy sauce, for 10 minutes
3. Pour the whole, boiling, over the mixture in the basin
4. Stir well, and reduce it to a proper thickness
5. Put it into a bottle, with a bruised shalot at the bottom, and store away for use
