## Ingredients

- remains of cold turkey
- ham or bacon
- shalots
- butter
- flour
- yolks of eggs
- egg and bread crumbs
- parsley-stems
- rolled bacon

## Directions

1. Mince the meat finely with ham or bacon
2. Make a gravy of the bones and trimmings, well seasoning it
3. Mince the shalots, put them into a stewpan with the butter
4. Add the flour and mix well
5. Put in the mince and about 1/2 pint of the gravy made from the bones
6. When just boiled, add the yolks of 2 eggs
7. Put the mixture out to cool and then shape it in a wineglass
8. Cover the croquettes with egg and bread crumbs
9. Fry them a delicate brown
10. Put small pieces of parsley-stems for stalks
11. Serve with rolled bacon cut very thin
