## Ingredients

- 1 lb flour
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt
- 2 eggs
- 1/3 pint water
- 6 oz butter

## Directions

1. Spread the flour, which should be sifted and thoroughly dry, on the paste-board
2. Make a hole in the centre, into which put the butter
3. Work it lightly into the flour
4. When quite fine, add the salt
5. Work the whole into a smooth paste with the eggs (yolks and whites) and water
6. Make it very firm
7. Knead the paste well, and let it be rather stiff, that the sides of the pie may be easily raised, and that they do not afterwards tumble or shrink
