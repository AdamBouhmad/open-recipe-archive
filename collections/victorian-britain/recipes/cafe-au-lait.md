## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoonfuls of strong coffee
- a breakfast-cupful of good hot milk
- a little cream

## Directions

1. Add 6 tablespoonfuls of strong coffee to a large proportion of good hot milk
2. If cream is available and the milk cannot be depended on for richness, mix a little cream with the milk to improve the taste
