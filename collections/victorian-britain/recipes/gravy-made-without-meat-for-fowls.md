## Ingredients

- The necks, feet, livers, and gizzards of the fowls
- 1 slice of toasted bread
- 1/2 onion
- 1 faggot of savoury herbs
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1/2 pint of water
- thickening of butter and flour
- 1 dessertspoonful of ketchup

## Directions

1. Wash the feet of the fowls thoroughly clean, and cut them and the neck into small pieces
2. Put these into a stewpan with the bread, onion, herbs, seasoning, livers, and gizzards
3. Pour the water over them and simmer gently for 1 hour
4. Take out the liver, pound it, and strain the liquor to it
5. Add a thickening of butter and flour, and a flavouring of mushroom ketchup
6. Boil it up and serve
