## Ingredients

- white or green gooseberries
- sugar (equal weight to fruit)
- water (1/2 pint per lb. of sugar)
- oiled paper
- tissue-paper
- unbeaten white of an egg

## Directions

1. Select the gooseberries not very ripe, either white or green, and top and tail them
2. Boil the sugar with water (allowing 1/2 pint to every lb.) for about 1/4 hour, carefully removing the scum as it rises
3. Put in the gooseberries, and simmer gently till clear and firm
4. Try a little of the jam on a plate; if it jellies when cold, it is done
5. Pour into pots. When cold, cover with oiled paper, and tissue-paper brushed over on both sides with the unbeaten white of an egg
6. Store away in a dry place
