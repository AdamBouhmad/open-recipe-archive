## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/2 oz. of butter
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt
- 2 eggs
- milk
- peaches
- hot lard or clarified dripping

## Directions

1. Make a nice smooth batter in the same manner as directed in recipe No. 1393, and skin, halve, and stone the peaches, which should be quite ripe.
2. Dip them in the batter, and fry the pieces in hot lard or clarified dripping, which should be brought to the boiling-point before the peaches are put in.
3. From 8 to 10 minutes will be required to fry them, and, when done, drain them before the fire, and dish them on a white d'oyley.
4. Strew over plenty of pounded sugar, and serve.
