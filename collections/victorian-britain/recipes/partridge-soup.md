## Ingredients

- partridges
- butter
- ham
- onions
- celery
- cold game remains
- stock
- sugar
- carrot
- turnip
- salt and seasoning

## Directions

1. Cut the partridges into pieces, and braise them in the butter and ham until quite tender
2. Take out the legs, wings, and breast, and set them by
3. Keep the backs and other trimmings in the braise, and add the onions and celery
4. Add any remains of cold game and 3 pints of stock
5. Simmer slowly for 1 hour, strain it, and skim the fat off as clean as possible
6. Put in the pieces that were taken out, give it one boil, and skim again to have it quite clear
7. Add the sugar and seasoning
8. Simmer the cut carrot and turnip in 1 pint of stock
9. When quite tender, put them to the partridges, and serve
