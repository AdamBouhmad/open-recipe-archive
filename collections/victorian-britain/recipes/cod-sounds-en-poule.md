## Ingredients

- 12 chopped oysters
- 3 chopped anchovies
- 1/4 lb. of bread crumbs
- 1 oz. of butter
- 2 eggs
- Salt, pepper, nutmeg, and mace to taste
- 4 cod sounds
- Milk and water
- Lard
- Flour

## Directions

1. Make the forcemeat by mixing the chopped oysters, anchovies, bread crumbs, butter, eggs, and seasoning well together.
2. Wash the sounds, and boil them in milk and water for 1/2 an hour; take them out and let them cool.
3. Cover each with a layer of forcemeat, roll them up in a nice form, and skewer them.
4. Rub over with lard, dredge with flour, and cook them gently before the fire in a Dutch oven.
