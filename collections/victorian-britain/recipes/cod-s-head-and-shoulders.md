## Ingredients

- cod's head and shoulders
- sufficient water to cover the fish
- 5 oz. of salt to each gallon of water
- cut lemon
- horseradish
- roe and liver
- oyster sauce
- plain melted butter

## Directions

1. Cleanse the fish thoroughly
2. Rub a little salt over the thick part and inside of the fish 1 or 2 hours before dressing it
3. Lay it in the fish-kettle with sufficient cold water to cover it
4. Be very particular not to pour the water on the fish as it is liable to break it
5. Keep it just simmering
6. If the water should boil away, add a little by pouring it in at the side of the kettle, not on the fish
7. Add salt in the proportion of 5 oz. to each gallon and bring it gradually to a boil
8. Skim very carefully
9. Draw it to the side of the fire and let it gently simmer till done
10. Take it out and drain it
11. Serve on a hot napkin and garnish with cut lemon, horseradish, the roe and liver
12. Serve oyster sauce and plain melted butter with this
