## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of port wine
- a saltspoonful of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 2 sliced onions
- a faggot of sweet herbs
- nutmeg and mace to taste
- the juice of a lemon
- 2 anchovies
- 1 or 2 barbels according to size

## Directions

1. Boil the barbels in salt and water till done
2. Pour off some of the water
3. To the remainder, put the port wine, salt, vinegar, sliced onions, sweet herbs, nutmeg, mace, lemon juice, and anchovies
4. Simmer gently for 1/2 hour or rather more
5. Strain
6. Put in the fish
7. Heat it gradually but do not let it boil or it will be broken
