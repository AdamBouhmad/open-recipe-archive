## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of bread crumbs
- 1/2 lb. of suet
- 1/2 lb. of currants
- 1/2 lb. of apples
- 1/2 lb. of moist sugar
- 6 eggs
- 12 sweet almonds
- 1/2 saltspoonful of grated nutmeg
- 1 wineglassful of brandy

## Directions

1. Chop the suet very fine
2. Wash the currants, dry them, and pick away the stalks and pieces of grit
3. Pare, core, and chop the apple, and grate the bread into fine crumbs, and mince the almonds
4. Mix all these ingredients together, adding the sugar and nutmeg
5. Beat up the eggs, omitting the whites of three
6. Stir these to the pudding, and when all is well mixed, add the brandy
7. Put the pudding into a buttered mould
8. Tie down with a cloth, put it into boiling water, and let it boil for 3 hours
