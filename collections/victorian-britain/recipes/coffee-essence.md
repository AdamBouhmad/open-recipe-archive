## Ingredients

- ground coffee
- chicory
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Roast the coffee and put it into a percolator or filter with the chicory
2. Pour slowly over it the boiling water
3. When it has all filtered through, warm the coffee sufficiently to bring it to the simmering-point, but do not allow it to boil
4. Filter it a second time
5. Put it into a clean and dry bottle and cork it well
6. Two tablespoonfuls of this essence are quite sufficient for a breakfast-cupful of hot milk
7. When the essence is bottled, pour another 3 tea-cupfuls of boiling water slowly on the grounds
8. When filtered through, this will be a very weak coffee
9. The next time essence is to be prepared, make this weak coffee boiling and pour it on the ground coffee instead of plain water
10. Always cork the bottle well that contains this preparation until the day it is wanted for making fresh essence
