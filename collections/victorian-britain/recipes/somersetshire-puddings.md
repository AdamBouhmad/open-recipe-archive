## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- flour equal in weight to the eggs
- pounded sugar equal in weight to the eggs
- butter equal in weight to the eggs
- flavouring of grated lemon-rind, bitter almonds, or essence of vanilla

## Directions

1. Carefully weigh the various ingredients, by placing on one side of the scales the eggs, and on the other the flour, then the sugar, and then the butter
2. Warm the butter, and with the hands beat it to a cream
3. Gradually dredge in the flour and pounded sugar, and keep stirring and beating the mixture without ceasing until it is perfectly smooth
4. Add the eggs, which should be well whisked, and either of the above flavourings that may be preferred
5. Butter some small cups, rather more than half-fill them
6. Bake in a brisk oven for about 1/2 hour
7. Turn them out, dish them on a napkin, and serve custard or wine-sauce with them
