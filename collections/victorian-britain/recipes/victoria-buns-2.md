## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of pounded loaf sugar
- 1 egg
- 1-1/2 oz. of ground rice
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1-1/2 oz. of currants
- a few thin slices of candied peel
- flour

## Directions

1. Whisk the egg, stir in the sugar, and beat these ingredients well together
2. Beat the butter to a cream
3. Stir in the ground rice, currants, and candied peel, and as much flour as will make it of such a consistency that it may be rolled into 7 or 8 balls
4. Put these on to a buttered tin
5. Bake them from 1/2 to 3/4 hour
6. Put them into the oven immediately, or they will become heavy
