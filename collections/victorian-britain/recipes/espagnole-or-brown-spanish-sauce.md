## Ingredients

- 2 slices of lean ham
- 1 lb. of veal
- 1-1/2 pint of white stock
- 2 or 3 sprigs of parsley
- 1/2 a bay-leaf
- 2 or 3 sprigs of savoury herbs
- 6 green onions
- 3 shalots
- 2 cloves
- 1 blade of mace
- 2 glasses of sherry or Madeira
- thickening of butter and flour

## Directions

1. Cut up the ham and veal into small square pieces, and put them into a stewpan.
2. Moisten these with 1/2 pint of the stock, and simmer till the bottom of the stewpan is covered with a nicely-coloured glaze, when put in a few more spoonfuls to detach it.
3. Add the remainder of the stock, with the spices, herbs, shalots, and onions, and simmer very gently for 1 hour.
4. Strain and skim off every particle of fat, and when required for use, thicken with butter and flour, or with a little roux.
5. Add the wine, and, if necessary, a seasoning of cayenne; when it will be ready to serve.
