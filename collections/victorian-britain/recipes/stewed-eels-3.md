## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of eels
- 1 pint of rich strong stock
- 1 onion
- 3 cloves
- a piece of lemon-peel
- 1 glass of port or Madeira
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cream
- flour for thickening
- cayenne to taste
- lemon-juice to taste
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Wash and skin the eels, and cut them into pieces about 3 inches long
2. pepper and salt them, and lay them in a stewpan
3. pour over the stock, add the onion stuck with cloves, the lemon-peel, and the wine
4. Stew gently for 1/2 hour, or rather more, and lift them carefully on a dish, which keep hot
5. Strain the gravy, stir to the cream sufficient flour to thicken
6. mix altogether, boil for 2 minutes, and add the cayenne and lemon-juice
7. pour over the eels and serve
