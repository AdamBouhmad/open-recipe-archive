## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of powdered sugar
- 1/2 a pint of water

## Directions

1. Place the sugar in a stewpan over a slow fire until it begins to melt, keeping it stirred with a wooden spoon until it becomes black
2. Then add the water, and let it dissolve
3. Cork closely, and use a few drops when required
