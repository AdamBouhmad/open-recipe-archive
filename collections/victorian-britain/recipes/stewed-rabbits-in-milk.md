## Ingredients

- 2 very young rabbits, not nearly half grown
- 1-1/2 pint of milk
- 1 blade of mace
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- a little salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. Mix the flour very smoothly with 4 tablespoonfuls of the milk
2. When this is well mixed, add the remainder
3. Cut up the rabbits into joints
4. Put them into a stewpan, with the milk and other ingredients
5. Simmer them very gently until quite tender
6. Stir the contents from time to time, to keep the milk smooth and prevent it from burning
7. Cook for 1/2 hour
