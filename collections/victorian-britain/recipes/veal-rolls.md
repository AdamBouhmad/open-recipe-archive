## Ingredients

- The remains of a cold fillet of veal
- egg and bread crumbs
- a few slices of fat bacon
- forcemeat No. 417

## Directions

1. Cut a few slices from a cold fillet of veal 1/2 inch thick
2. rub them over with egg
3. lay a thin slice of fat bacon over each piece of veal
4. brush these with the egg
5. spread the forcemeat thinly over this
6. roll up each piece tightly
7. egg and bread crumb them
8. fry them a rich brown
9. Serve with mushroom sauce or brown gravy
