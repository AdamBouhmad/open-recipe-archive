## Ingredients

- 1-3/4 lb. treacle
- 1 lb. of moist sugar
- 1 lb. of butter
- 2-3/4 lbs. of flour
- 1-1/2 oz. of ground ginger
- 1-1/2 oz. of allspice
- 1-1/2 oz. of coriander seeds

## Directions

1. Let the allspice, coriander seeds, and ginger be freshly ground
2. Put them into a basin, with the flour and sugar
3. Mix these ingredients well together
4. Warm the treacle and butter together
5. With a spoon work it into the flour, &c., until the whole forms a nice smooth paste
6. Drop the mixture from the spoon on to a piece of buttered paper
7. Bake in rather a slow oven from 20 minutes to 1/2 hour
