## Ingredients

- 1 lb flour
- 2 oz sifted sugar
- 3 oz butter
- 1/2 pint boiling milk

## Directions

1. Crumble the butter into the flour as finely as possible
2. Add the sugar
3. Work the whole up to a smooth paste with the boiling milk
4. Roll it out thin
5. Bake in a moderate oven
