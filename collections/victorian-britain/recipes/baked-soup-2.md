## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of any kind of meat, any trimmings or odd pieces
- 2 onions
- 2 carrots
- 2 oz. of rice
- 1 pint of split peas
- pepper and salt to taste
- 4 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat and vegetables in slices
2. Add to them the rice and peas
3. Season with pepper and salt
4. Put the whole in a jar
5. Fill up with the water
6. Cover very closely
7. Bake for 4 hours
