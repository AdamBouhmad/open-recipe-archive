## Ingredients

- Remains of cold turbot
- Béchamel
- Bread crumbs
- Butter
- 4 or 5 tablespoonfuls of béchamel

## Directions

1. Cut the flesh of the turbot into small dice, carefully freeing it from all skin and bone
2. Put them into a stewpan, and moisten with 4 or 5 tablespoonfuls of béchamel
3. Let it get thoroughly hot, but do not allow it to boil
4. Spread the mixture on a dish, cover with finely-grated bread crumbs, and place small pieces of butter over the top
5. Brown it in the oven, or with a salamander
