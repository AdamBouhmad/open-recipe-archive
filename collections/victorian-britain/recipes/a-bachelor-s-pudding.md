## Ingredients

- 4 oz. of grated bread
- 4 oz. of currants
- 4 oz. of apples
- 2 oz. of sugar
- 3 eggs
- a few drops of essence of lemon
- a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and mince the apples very finely, sufficient, when minced, to make 4 oz.
2. Add to these the currants, which should be well washed, the grated bread, and sugar
3. Whisk the eggs, beat these up with the remaining ingredients
4. When all is thoroughly mixed, put the pudding into a buttered basin
5. Tie it down with a cloth and boil for 3 hours
