## Ingredients

- 1 pint of water
- 1-1/2 to 2 oz. of isinglass
- 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1 Seville orange
- 1 lemon
- about 9 China oranges

## Directions

1. Put the water into a saucepan, with the isinglass, sugar, and the rind of 1 orange, and the same of 1/2 lemon
2. Stir these over the fire until the isinglass is dissolved, and remove the scum
3. Add the juice of the Seville orange, the juice of the lemon, and sufficient juice of China oranges to make in all 1 pint
4. Stir all together over the fire until it is just on the point of boiling
5. Skim well
6. Strain the jelly through a very fine sieve or jelly-bag
7. When nearly cold, put it into a mould previously wetted
8. When quite set, turn it out on a dish, and garnish it to taste
