## Ingredients

- A few slices of cold mutton
- tomato sauce (No. 529)
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Cut some nice slices from a cold leg or shoulder of mutton
2. Season them with pepper and salt
3. Broil over a clear fire
4. Make some tomato sauce by recipe No. 529
5. Pour it over the mutton
6. Serve very hot
