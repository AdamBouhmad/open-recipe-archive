## Ingredients

- brill
- 1/4 lb. of salt to each gallon of water
- a little vinegar
- lemon-juice
- cut lemon
- parsley
- horseradish
- lobster coral
- lobster or shrimp sauce
- plain melted butter

## Directions

1. Clean the brill and cut off the fins
2. Rub it over with a little lemon-juice to preserve its whiteness
3. Set the fish in sufficient cold water to cover it
4. Throw in salt in the proportion of 1/4 lb. to each gallon and a little vinegar
5. Bring it gradually to boil
6. Simmer very gently till the fish is done, about 10 minutes depending on size
7. Serve it on a hot napkin
8. Garnish with cut lemon, parsley, horseradish, and a little lobster coral sprinkled over the fish
9. Send lobster or shrimp sauce and plain melted butter to table with it
