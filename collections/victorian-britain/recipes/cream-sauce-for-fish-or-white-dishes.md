## Ingredients

- 1/3 pint of cream
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- salt to taste
- cayenne to taste
- a small quantity of pounded mace or lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Put the butter in a very clean saucepan
2. Dredge in the flour
3. Keep shaking round till the butter is melted
4. Add the seasoning and cream
5. Stir the whole till it boils
6. Let it just simmer for 5 minutes
7. Add either pounded mace or lemon-juice to taste to give it a flavour
