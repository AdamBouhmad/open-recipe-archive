## Ingredients

- To each 1 gallon of water allow 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- artichokes
- melted butter or cream sauce

## Directions

1. Wash, peel, and shape the artichokes in a round or oval form, and put them into a saucepan with sufficient cold water to cover them, salted in the above proportion.
2. Let them boil gently until tender.
3. Take them up, drain them, and serve them in a napkin, or plain, whichever mode is preferred.
4. Send to table with them a tureen of melted butter or cream sauce, a little of which may be poured over the artichokes when they are not served in a napkin.
