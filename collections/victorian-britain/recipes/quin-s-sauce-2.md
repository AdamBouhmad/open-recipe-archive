## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of walnut pickle
- 1/2 pint of port wine
- 1 pint of mushroom ketchup
- 1 dozen anchovies
- 1 dozen shalots
- 1/4 pint of soy
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cayenne

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients into a saucepan, having previously chopped the shalots and anchovies very small.
2. Simmer for 15 minutes, strain, and, when cold, bottle off for use.
3. The corks should be well sealed to exclude the air.
