## Ingredients

- bread
- lard or clarified dripping

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into thin slices
2. Place them in a cool oven overnight
3. When thoroughly dry and crisp, roll them down into fine crumbs
4. Put some lard or clarified dripping into a frying-pan
5. Bring it to the boiling-point
6. Throw in the crumbs, and fry them very quickly
7. Lift them out with a slice as soon as done
8. Drain them before the fire from all greasy moisture
9. When quite crisp, they are ready for use
