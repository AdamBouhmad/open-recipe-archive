## Ingredients

- 1/2 gallon of water
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- a very small piece of soda
- cabbages

## Directions

1. Pick off all the dead outside leaves
2. cut off as much of the stalk as possible
3. cut the cabbages across twice, at the stalk end; if they should be very large, quarter them
4. Wash them well in cold water
5. place them in a colander, and drain
6. put them into plenty of fast-boiling water, to which have been added salt and soda in the above proportions
7. Stir them down once or twice in the water
8. keep the pan uncovered
9. let them boil quickly until tender
10. The instant they are done, take them up into a colander
11. place a plate over them
12. let them thoroughly drain
13. dish, and serve
