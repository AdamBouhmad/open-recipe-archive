## Ingredients

- 3 oz. of semolina
- 1-1/2 pint of milk
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 12 bitter almonds
- 3 oz. of butter
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Flavour the milk with the bitter almonds, by infusing them in it by the side of the fire for about 1/2 hour
2. Strain it, and mix with it the semolina, sugar, and butter
3. Stir these ingredients over the fire for a few minutes
4. Take them off, and gradually mix in the eggs, which should be well beaten
5. Butter a pie-dish
6. Line the edges with puff-paste
7. Put in the pudding
8. Bake in rather a slow oven from 40 to 50 minutes
9. Serve with custard sauce or stewed fruit, a little of which may be poured over the pudding
