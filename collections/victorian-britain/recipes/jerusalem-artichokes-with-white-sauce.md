## Ingredients

- 12 to 15 artichokes
- 12 to 15 Brussels sprouts
- 1/2 pint of white sauce, No. 538

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the artichokes in the shape of a pear
2. Cut a piece off the bottom of each, that they may stand upright in the dish
3. Boil them in salt and water until tender
4. Have ready 1/2 pint of white sauce, made by recipe No. 538
5. Dish the artichokes
6. Pour over them the sauce
7. Place between each a fine Brussels sprout: these should be boiled separately, and not with the artichokes
