## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 6 oz. of currants
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of dried flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking-powder
- 3 eggs
- 1 teacupful of milk
- 2 oz. of sweet almonds
- 1 oz. of candied peel

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Wash, pick, and dry the currants
3. Whisk the eggs
4. Blanch and chop the almonds
5. Cut the peel into neat slices
6. Mix the dry ingredients together
7. Add the butter, milk, and eggs
8. Beat the mixture well for a few minutes
9. Put the cake into a buttered mould or tin
10. Bake it for rather more than 1-1/2 hour
