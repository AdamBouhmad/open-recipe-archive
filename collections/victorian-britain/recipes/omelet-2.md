## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 eggs
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- 1/2 teacupful of milk
- salt
- cayenne
- chopped herbs
- shreds of onion

## Directions

1. Make a thin cream of the flour and milk
2. Beat up the eggs and mix all together
3. Add a pinch of salt and a few grains of cayenne
4. Melt the butter in a small frying-pan
5. When very hot, pour in the batter
6. Let the pan remain for a few minutes over a clear fire
7. Sprinkle upon the omelet some chopped herbs and a few shreds of onion
8. Double the omelet dexterously and shake it out of the pan onto a hot dish
