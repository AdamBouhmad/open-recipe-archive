## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 2 eggs
- 3 oz. of pounded sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of brandy

## Directions

1. Put the milk in a very clean saucepan, and let it boil
2. Beat the eggs, stir to them the milk and pounded sugar, and put the mixture into a jug
3. Place the jug in a saucepan of boiling water
4. Keep stirring well until it thickens, but do not allow it to boil, or it will curdle
5. Serve the sauce in a tureen, stir in the brandy, and grate a little nutmeg over the top
