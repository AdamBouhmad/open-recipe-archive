## Ingredients

- 1 kidney
- 1 dessertspoonful of minced parsley
- 1 teaspoonful of minced shalot
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 pint of gravy
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sherry
- flour
- croûtons

## Directions

1. Take off a little of the kidney fat, mince it very fine, and put it in a frying-pan.
2. Slice the kidney, sprinkle over it parsley and shalots in the above proportion, add a seasoning of pepper and salt, and fry it of a nice brown.
3. When it is done enough, dredge over a little flour, and pour in the gravy and sherry.
4. Let it just simmer, but not boil any more, or the kidney would harden.
5. Serve very hot, and garnish with croûtons.
