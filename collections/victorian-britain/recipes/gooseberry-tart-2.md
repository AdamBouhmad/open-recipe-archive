## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 pint of gooseberries
- 1/2 lb. of short crust
- 1/4 lb. of moist sugar

## Directions

1. With a pair of scissors cut off the tops and tails of the gooseberries
2. Put them into a deep pie-dish
3. Pile the fruit high in the centre
4. Put in the sugar
5. Line the edge of the dish with short crust
6. Put on the cover
7. Ornament the edges of the tart
8. Bake in a good oven for about 3/4 hour
9. Before being sent to table, strew over it some fine-sifted sugar
10. Serve with a jug of cream, or a dish of boiled or baked custards
