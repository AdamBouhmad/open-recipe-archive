## Ingredients

- The remains of cold neck or loin of mutton
- 2 oz. of butter
- 3 onions
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- 1/2 pint of good gravy
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 tablespoonfuls of port wine
- 1 tablespoonful of mushroom ketchup
- 2 carrots
- 2 turnips
- 1 head of celery

## Directions

1. Cut the cold mutton into moderate-sized chops, and take off the fat.
2. Slice the onions, and fry them with the chops, in a little butter, of a nice brown colour.
3. Stir in the flour, add the gravy, and let it stew gently nearly an hour.
4. Boil the vegetables until nearly tender, slice them, and add them to the mutton about 1/4 hour before it is to be served.
5. Season with pepper and salt, add the ketchup and port wine, give one boil, and serve.
