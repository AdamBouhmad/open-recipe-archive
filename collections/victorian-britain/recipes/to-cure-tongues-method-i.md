## Ingredients

- 1 oz. of saltpetre
- 1/2 oz. of black pepper
- 4 oz. of sugar
- 3 oz. of juniper berries
- 6 oz. of salt

## Directions

1. Rub the above ingredients well into the tongue, and let it remain in the pickle for 10 days or a fortnight
2. Drain it, tie it up in brown paper, and have it smoked for about 20 days over a wood fire; or it may be boiled out of this pickle
