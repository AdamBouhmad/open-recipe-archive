## Ingredients

- Puff-paste
- white of an egg
- pounded sugar
- apricot jam or marmalade or red-currant jelly

## Directions

1. Roll some good puff-paste out thin, and cut it into 2-1/2-inch squares
2. Brush each square over with the white of an egg, then fold down the corners, so that they all meet in the middle of each piece of paste
3. Slightly press the two pieces together, brush them over with the egg, sift over sugar, and bake in a nice quick oven for about 1/4 hour
4. When they are done, make a little hole in the middle of the paste, and fill it up with apricot jam, marmalade, or red-currant jelly
5. Pile them high in the centre of a dish, on a napkin, and garnish with the same preserve the tartlets are filled with
