## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- flour
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper to taste
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Wipe the sprats, and dip them in a batter made of the eggs, flour, and bread crumbs seasoned with salt and pepper.
2. Fry of a nice brown, serve very hot, and garnish with fried parsley.
