## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of lean beef
- 1 tablespoonful of water
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- salt to taste
- cayenne to taste
- pounded mace to taste
- black pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Procure a nice piece of lean beef, as free as possible from gristle, skin, &c., and put it into a jar (if at hand, one with a lid) with 1 tablespoonful of water
2. Cover it closely, and put the jar into a saucepan of boiling water, letting the water come within 2 inches of the top of the jar
3. Boil gently for 3-1/2 hours
4. Take the beef, chop it very small with a chopping-knife, and pound it thoroughly in a mortar
5. Mix with it by degrees all, or a portion, of the gravy that will have run from it, and a little clarified butter
6. Add the seasoning, put it in small pots for use, and cover with a little butter just warmed and poured over
