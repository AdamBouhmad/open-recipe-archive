## Ingredients

- A cold roast fowl
- Mayonnaise sauce No. 468
- 4 or 5 young lettuces
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
- a few water-cresses
- endive

## Directions

1. Cut the fowl into neat joints, lay them in a deep dish, piling them high in the centre
2. Sauce the fowl with Mayonnaise made by recipe No. 468
3. Garnish the dish with young lettuces cut in halves, water-cresses, endive, and hard-boiled eggs; these may be sliced in rings, or laid on the dish whole, cutting off at the bottom a piece of the white, to make the egg stand
4. The sauce should not be poured over the fowls until the moment of serving
