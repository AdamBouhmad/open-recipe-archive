## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Cold butter

## Directions

1. Scrub potatoes well in lukewarm water
2. Put them into a moderate oven and bake them for about 2 hours, turning them three or four times whilst they are cooking
3. Serve them in a napkin immediately they are done
4. Send to table with them a piece of cold butter
