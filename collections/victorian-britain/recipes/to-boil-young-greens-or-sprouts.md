## Ingredients

- 1/2 gallon of water
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- a very small piece of soda
- greens or sprouts

## Directions

1. Pick away all the dead leaves and wash the greens well in cold water
2. Drain them in a colander
3. Put them into fast-boiling water, with salt and soda in the above proportion
4. Keep them boiling quickly, with the lid uncovered, until tender
5. The moment they are done, take them up to preserve their colour
6. When well drained, serve
