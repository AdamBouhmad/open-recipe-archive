## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 oz. of butter
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Butter a dish rather thickly with good fresh butter; melt it
2. break the eggs into it the same as for poaching, sprinkle them with white pepper and fine salt
3. put the remainder of the butter, cut into very small pieces, on the top of them
4. Put the dish on a hot plate, or in the oven, or before the fire, and let it remain until the whites become set, but not hard
5. serve immediately, placing the dish they were cooked in on another
6. To hasten the cooking of the eggs, a salamander may be held over them for a minute; but great care must be taken that they are not too much done
