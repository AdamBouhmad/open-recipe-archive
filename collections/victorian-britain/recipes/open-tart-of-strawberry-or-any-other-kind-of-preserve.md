## Ingredients

- Trimmings of puff-paste
- any kind of jam

## Directions

1. Butter a tart-pan of the shape shown in the engraving
2. Roll out the paste to the thickness of 1/2 an inch, and line the pan with it
3. Prick a few holes at the bottom with a fork
4. Bake the tart in a brisk oven from 10 to 15 minutes
5. Let the paste cool a little
6. Fill it with preserve
7. Place a few stars or leaves on it, which have been previously cut out of the paste and baked
8. The tart is ready for table
