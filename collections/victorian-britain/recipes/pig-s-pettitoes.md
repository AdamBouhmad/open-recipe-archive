## Ingredients

- A thin slice of bacon
- 1 onion
- 1 blade of mace
- 6 peppercorns
- 3 or 4 sprigs of thyme
- 1 pint of gravy
- Pepper and salt to taste
- Thickening of butter and flour

## Directions

1. Put the liver, heart, and pettitoes into a stewpan with the bacon, mace, peppercorns, thyme, onion, and gravy
2. Simmer these gently for 1/4 hour
3. Take out the heart and liver, and mince them very fine
4. Keep stewing the feet until quite tender, which will be in from 20 minutes to 1/2 hour, reckoning from the time that they boiled up first
5. Put back the minced liver
6. Thicken the gravy with a little butter and flour
7. Season with pepper and salt
8. Simmer over a gentle fire for 5 minutes, occasionally stirring the contents
9. Dish the mince, split the feet, and arrange them round alternately with sippets of toasted bread
10. Pour the gravy in the middle
