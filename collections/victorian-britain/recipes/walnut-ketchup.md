## Ingredients

- 100 walnuts
- 1 handful of salt
- 1 quart of vinegar
- 1/4 oz. of mace
- 1/4 oz. of nutmeg
- 1/4 oz. of cloves
- 1/4 oz. of ginger
- 1/4 oz. of whole black pepper
- a small piece of horseradish
- 20 shalots
- 1/4 lb. of anchovies
- 1 pint of port wine

## Directions

1. Procure the walnuts at the time you can run a pin through them, slightly bruise, and put them into a jar with the salt and vinegar
2. Let them stand 8 days, stirring every day
3. Then drain the liquor from them, and boil it, with the above ingredients, for about 1/2 hour
4. It may be strained or not, as preferred, and, if required, a little more vinegar or wine can be added, according to taste
5. When bottled well, seal the corks
