## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/2 tablespoonful of made mustard
- salt and cayenne to taste
- 3 tablespoonfuls of olive-oil
- 1 tablespoonful of tarragon or plain vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil 3 eggs quite hard for about 1/4 hour, put them into cold water, and let them remain in it for a few minutes.
2. Strip off the shells, put the yolks in a mortar, and pound them very smoothly.
3. Add to them, very gradually, the mustard, seasoning, and vinegar, keeping all well stirred and rubbed down with the back of a wooden spoon.
4. Put in the oil drop by drop, and when this is thoroughly mixed with the other ingredients, add the yolk of a raw egg, and stir well, when it will be ready for use.
5. The quantities of oil and vinegar may be increased or diminished according to taste.
