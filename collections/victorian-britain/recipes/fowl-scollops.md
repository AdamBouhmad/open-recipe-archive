## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast or boiled fowl
- 1/2 pint of Béchamel or white sauce

## Directions

1. Strip off the skin from the fowl
2. Cut the meat into thin slices, and warm them in about 1/2 pint, or rather more, of Béchamel or white sauce
3. When quite hot, serve, and garnish the dish with rolled ham or bacon toasted
