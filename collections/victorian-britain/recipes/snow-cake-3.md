## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of tous-les-mois
- 1/4 lb. of white pounded sugar
- 1/4 lb. of fresh or washed salt butter
- 1 egg
- juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Add the egg, previously well beaten, and then the other ingredients
3. If the mixture is not light, add another egg, and beat for 1/4 hour, until it turns white and light
4. Line a flat tin with raised edges with a sheet of buttered paper
5. Pour in the cake and put it into the oven
6. Bake in a rather slow oven, 1 to 1-1/4 hour, and do not allow the cake to brown at all
7. Let it cool a few minutes
8. With a clean sharp knife cut it into small square pieces
9. Gently remove the pieces to a large flat dish to cool before putting away
