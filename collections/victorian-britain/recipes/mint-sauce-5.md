## Ingredients

- 4 dessertspoonfuls of chopped mint
- 2 dessertspoonfuls of pounded white sugar
- 1/4 pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash the mint, which should be young and fresh-gathered, free from grit.
2. Pick the leaves from the stalks, mince them very fine, and put them into a tureen.
3. Add the sugar and vinegar, and stir till the former is dissolved.
