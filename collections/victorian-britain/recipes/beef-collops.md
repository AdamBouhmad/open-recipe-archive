## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of rump-steak
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1 pint of gravy (water may be substituted for this)
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 shalot finely minced
- 1/2 pickled walnut
- 1 teaspoonful of capers

## Directions

1. Have the steak cut thin, and divide it in pieces about 3 inches long.
2. Beat these with the blade of a knife, and dredge with flour.
3. Put them in a frying-pan with the butter, and let them fry for about 3 minutes.
4. Then lay them in a small stewpan, and pour over them the gravy.
5. Add a piece of butter, kneaded with a little flour, put in the seasoning and all the other ingredients, and let the whole simmer, but not boil, for 10 minutes.
6. Serve in a hot covered dish.
