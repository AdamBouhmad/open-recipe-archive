## Ingredients

- bread
- lard or clarified dripping

## Directions

1. Cut the bread into thin slices
2. Stamp them out in whatever shape you like—rings, crosses, diamonds, &c.
3. Fry them in clear boiling lard or clarified dripping in the same manner as bread crumbs
4. Drain them until thoroughly crisp before the fire
5. Fry some of a pale colour, and others of a darker hue for variety
