## Ingredients

- Vinegar and water
- 2 heads of celery
- 2 turnips
- 3 onions
- Large bunch of savoury herbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the ham as in the preceding recipe
2. Let it soak for a few hours in vinegar and water
3. Put it on in cold water
4. When it boils, add the vegetables and herbs
5. Simmer very gently until tender
6. Take it out and strip off the skin
7. Cover with bread-raspings
8. Put a paper ruche or frill round the knuckle
