## Ingredients

- About 4 lbs. of the inside fillet of the sirloin
- 1 onion
- a small bunch of parsley
- salt and pepper to taste
- sufficient vinegar to cover the meat
- glaze
- Spanish sauce, No. 411

## Directions

1. Lard the beef with bacon, and put it into a pan with sufficient vinegar to cover it, with an onion sliced, parsley, and seasoning.
2. Let it remain in this pickle for 12 hours.
3. Roast it before a nice clear fire for about 1-1/4 hour.
4. When done, glaze it.
5. Pour some Spanish sauce round the beef, and the remainder serve in a tureen.
6. It may be garnished with Spanish onions boiled and glazed.
