## Ingredients

- 6 tomatoes
- 2 shalots
- 1 clove
- 1 blade of mace
- salt and cayenne to taste
- 1/4 pint of gravy or stock

## Directions

1. Cut the tomatoes in two and squeeze the juice and seeds out
2. Put them in a stewpan with all the ingredients
3. Let them simmer gently until the tomatoes are tender enough to pulp
4. Rub the whole through a sieve
5. Boil it for a few minutes and serve
