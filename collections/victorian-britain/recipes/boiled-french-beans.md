## Ingredients

- 1/2 gallon of water
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- a very small piece of soda
- French beans

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads and tails, and a thin strip on each side of the beans, to remove the strings
2. Divide each bean into 4 or 6 pieces, according to size, cutting them lengthways in a slanting direction
3. Put them into cold water with a small quantity of salt dissolved in it
4. Have ready a saucepan of boiling water, with salt and soda in the above proportion
5. Put in the beans and keep them boiling quickly with the lid uncovered
6. Be careful that they do not get smoked
7. When tender, which may be ascertained by their sinking to the bottom of the saucepan, take them up and throw them into a colander
8. When drained, dish and serve with plain melted butter
