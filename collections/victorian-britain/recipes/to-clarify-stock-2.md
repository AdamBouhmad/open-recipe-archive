## Ingredients

- The whites of 2 eggs
- 1/2 pint of water
- 2 quarts of stock

## Directions

1. Take the whites of 2 eggs, carefully separated from their yolks, whisk them well together with the water, and add gradually the 2 quarts of boiling stock, still whisking
2. Place the soup on the fire, and when boiling and well skimmed, whisk the eggs with it till nearly boiling again
3. Draw it from the fire, and let it settle, until the whites of the eggs become separated
4. Pass through a fine cloth, and the soup should be clear
