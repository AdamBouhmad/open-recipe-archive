## Ingredients

- 3 cauliflowers
- 1/2 pint of sauce blanche or French melted butter
- 3 oz. of butter
- salt and water

## Directions

1. Cleanse the cauliflowers and cut the stalks off flat at the bottom
2. Boil them until tender in salt and water, to which the butter has been added
3. Be careful to take them up the moment they are done, or they will break
4. Drain them well, and dish them in the shape of a large cauliflower
5. Have ready 1/2 pint of sauce made by recipe No. 378
6. Pour it over the flowers, and serve hot and quickly
