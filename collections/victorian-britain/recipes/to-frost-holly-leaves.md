## Ingredients

- Sprigs of holly
- Oiled butter
- Coarsely-powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Procure some nice sprigs of holly
2. Pick the leaves from the stalks, and wipe them with a clean cloth free from all moisture
3. Place them on a dish near the fire, to get thoroughly dry, but not too near to shrivel the leaves
4. Dip them into oiled butter, sprinkle over them some coarsely-powdered sugar, and dry them before the fire
5. Keep them in a dry place, as the least damp would spoil their appearance
