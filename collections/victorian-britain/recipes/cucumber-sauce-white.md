## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 cucumbers
- 1/2 pint of white stock, No. 107
- cayenne to taste
- salt to taste
- the yolks of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the cucumbers into small pieces, after peeling them and taking out the seeds
2. Put them in a stewpan with the white stock and seasoning
3. Simmer gently till the cucumbers are tender, which will be in about 1/4 hour
4. Add the yolks of the eggs well beaten
5. Stir them to the sauce
6. Do not allow it to boil
7. Serve very hot
