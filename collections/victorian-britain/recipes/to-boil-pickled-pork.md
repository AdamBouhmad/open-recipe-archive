## Ingredients

- Pork
- Water

## Directions

1. Should the pork be very salt, let it remain in water about 2 hours before it is dressed
2. Put it into a saucepan with sufficient cold water to cover it
3. Let it gradually come to a boil, then gently simmer until quite tender
4. Allow ample time for it to cook, as nothing is more disagreeable than underdone pork, and when boiled fast, the meat becomes hard
