## Ingredients

- gravy saved from the meat
- 1 teaspoonful of tomato sauce
- 1 teaspoonful of Harvey's sauce
- 1 teaspoonful of good mushroom ketchup
- 1/2 glass of port wine or strong ale
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- 1 onion finely minced
- slices of cold roast beef
- sippets of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients but the beef into a stewpan with whatever gravy may have been saved from the meat the day it was roasted.
2. Let these simmer gently for 10 minutes.
3. Take the stewpan off the fire; let the gravy cool, and skim off the fat.
4. Cut the beef into thin slices, dredge them with flour, and lay them in the gravy.
5. Let the whole simmer gently for 5 minutes, but not boil, or the meat will be tough and hard.
6. Serve very hot, and garnish with sippets of toasted bread.
