## Ingredients

- Larks
- egg
- bread crumbs
- fresh butter
- clarified butter
- slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Pick, gut, and clean them; when they are trussed, brush them over with the yolk of an egg.
2. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, and roast them before a quick fire.
3. Baste them continually with fresh butter, and keep sprinkling with the bread crumbs until the birds are well covered.
4. Dish them on bread crumbs fried in clarified butter, and garnish the dish with slices of lemon.
