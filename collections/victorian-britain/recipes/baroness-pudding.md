## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of suet
- 3/4 lb. of raisins weighed after being stoned
- 3/4 lb. of flour
- 1/2 pint of milk
- 1/4 saltspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the suet, by carefully freeing it from skin, and chop it finely
2. Stone the raisins, and cut them in halves
3. Mix both these ingredients with the salt and flour
4. Moisten the whole with the above proportion of milk
5. Stir the mixture well
6. Tie the pudding in a floured cloth, which has been previously wrung out in boiling water
7. Put the pudding into a saucepan of boiling water, and let it boil, without ceasing, 4-1/2 hours
8. Serve merely with plain sifted sugar, a little of which may be sprinkled over the pudding
