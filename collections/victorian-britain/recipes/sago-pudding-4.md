## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 pint of milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sago
- rind of 1/2 lemon
- 3 oz. of sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1-1/2 oz. of butter
- grated nutmeg
- puff-paste

## Directions

1. Put the milk and lemon-rind into a stewpan, place it by the side of the fire, and let it remain until the milk is well flavoured with the lemon
2. Strain it, mix with it the sago and sugar
3. Simmer gently for about 15 minutes
4. Let the mixture cool a little
5. Stir to it the eggs, which should be well beaten, and the butter
6. Line the edges of a pie-dish with puff-paste
7. Pour in the pudding
8. Grate a little nutmeg over the top
9. Bake from 3/4 to 1 hour
