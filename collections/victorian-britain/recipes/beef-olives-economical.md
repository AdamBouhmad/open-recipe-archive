## Ingredients

- remains of underdone cold roast beef
- bread crumbs
- 1 shalot finely minced
- pepper and salt to taste
- gravy made from the beef bones
- butter and flour for thickening
- 1 tablespoonful of mushroom ketchup

## Directions

1. Cut some slices of underdone roast beef about half an inch thick
2. Sprinkle over them some bread crumbs, minced shalot, and a little of the fat and seasoning
3. Roll them, and fasten with a small skewer
4. Have ready some gravy made from the beef bones; put in the pieces of meat
5. Stew them till tender, which will be in about 1-1/4 hour, or rather longer
6. Arrange the meat in a dish, thicken and flavour the gravy, and pour it over the meat, when it is ready to serve
