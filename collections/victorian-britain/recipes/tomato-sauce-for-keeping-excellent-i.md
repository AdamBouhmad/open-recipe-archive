## Ingredients

- To every quart of tomato-pulp: 1 pint of cayenne vinegar
- 3/4 oz. of shalots
- 3/4 oz. of garlic, peeled and cut in slices
- salt to taste
- To every six quarts of liquor: 1 pint of soy
- 1 pint of anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Gather the tomatoes quite ripe
2. Bake them in a slow oven till tender
3. Rub them through a sieve
4. To every quart of pulp add cayenne vinegar, shalots, garlic, and salt in the above proportion
5. Boil the whole together till the garlic and shalots are quite soft
6. Rub it through a sieve
7. Put it again into a saucepan
8. To every six quarts of the liquor, add 1 pint of soy and the same quantity of anchovy sauce
9. Boil altogether for about 20 minutes
10. Bottle off for use and carefully seal or rosin the corks
