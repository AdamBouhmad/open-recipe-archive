## Ingredients

- 1 gurnet
- salt
- water
- parsley
- butter
- anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Cleanse the fish thoroughly
2. cut off the fins
3. have ready some boiling water, with salt in the proportion of 6 oz. of salt to each gallon of water
4. put the fish in
5. simmer very gently for 1/2 hour
6. serve with parsley and butter, or anchovy sauce
