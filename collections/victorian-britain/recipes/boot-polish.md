## Ingredients

- ivory-black 4 oz.
- treacle 4 oz.
- sulphuric acid 1 oz.
- best olive-oil 2 spoonfuls
- best white-wine vinegar 3 half-pints

## Directions

1. Mix the ivory-black and treacle well in an earthen jar
2. Add the sulphuric acid, continuing to stir the mixture
3. Pour in the oil
4. Add the vinegar, stirring it in by degrees, until thoroughly incorporated
