## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 lbs. of the loin or neck of veal
- 15 young carrots
- A few green onions
- 1 pint of green peas
- 12 new potatoes
- A bunch of savoury herbs
- Pepper and salt to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- 2 tablespoonfuls of tomato sauce
- 2 tablespoonfuls of mushroom ketchup
- Flour

## Directions

1. Dredge the meat with flour
2. Roast or bake it for about 3/4 hour until it acquires a nice brown colour
3. Put the meat into a stewpan with the carrots, onions, potatoes, herbs, pepper, and salt
4. Pour over it sufficient boiling water to cover it
5. Stew gently for 2 hours
6. Take out the meat and herbs
7. Put it in a deep dish
8. Skim off all the fat from the gravy
9. Flavour it with lemon-juice, tomato sauce, and mushroom ketchup in the above proportion
10. Have ready a pint of green peas boiled
11. Put these with the meat
12. Pour over it the gravy
13. Serve
14. The dish may be garnished with a few forcemeat balls
