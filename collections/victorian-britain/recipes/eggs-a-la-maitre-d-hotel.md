## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of fresh butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1/2 pint of milk
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of minced parsley
- the juice of 1/2 lemon
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Put the flour and half the butter into a stewpan; stir them over the fire until the mixture thickens
2. pour in the milk, which should be boiling
3. add a seasoning of pepper and salt, and simmer the whole for 5 minutes
4. Put the remainder of the butter into the sauce, and add the minced parsley
5. boil the eggs hard, strip off the shells, cut the eggs into quarters, and put them on a dish
6. Bring the sauce to the boiling-point, add the lemon-juice, pour over the eggs, and serve
