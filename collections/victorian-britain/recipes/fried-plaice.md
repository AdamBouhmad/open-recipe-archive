## Ingredients

- Hot lard, or clarified dripping
- egg and bread crumbs
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Wash and wipe the fish thoroughly dry, and let them remain in a cloth until it is time to dress them.
2. Brush them over with egg, and cover with bread crumbs mixed with a little flour.
3. Fry of a nice brown in hot dripping or lard, and garnish with fried parsley and cut lemon.
4. Send them to table with shrimp-sauce and plain melted butter.
