## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of Spanish chestnuts
- 1/4 pint of cream
- salt to taste
- cayenne to taste
- mace to taste
- 1 quart of stock No. 105

## Directions

1. Take the outer rind from the chestnuts, and put them into a large pan of warm water.
2. As soon as this becomes too hot for the fingers to remain in it, take out the chestnuts, peel them quickly, and immerse them in cold water, and wipe and weigh them.
3. Now cover them with good stock, and stew them gently for rather more than 3/4 of an hour, or until they break when touched with a fork; then drain, pound, and rub them through a fine sieve reversed.
4. Add sufficient stock, mace, cayenne, and salt, and stir it often until it boils, and put in the cream.
