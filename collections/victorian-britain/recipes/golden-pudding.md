## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of bread crumbs
- 1/4 lb. of suet
- 1/4 lb. of marmalade
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Put the bread crumbs into a basin; mix with them the suet, which should be finely minced, the marmalade, and the sugar; stir all these ingredients well together
2. beat the eggs to a froth, moisten the pudding with these, and when well mixed, put it into a mould or buttered basin
3. tie down with a floured cloth, and boil for 2 hours
4. When turned out, strew a little fine-sifted sugar over the top, and serve
