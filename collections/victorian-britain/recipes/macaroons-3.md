## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of sweet almonds
- 1/2 lb. of sifted loaf sugar
- the whites of 3 eggs
- wafer-paper
- orange-flower water or plain water
- almond strips
- sugar for strewing

## Directions

1. Blanch, skin, and dry the almonds, and pound them well with a little orange-flower water or plain water
2. add to them the sifted sugar and the whites of the eggs, which should be beaten to a stiff froth
3. mix all the ingredients well together
4. When the paste looks soft, drop it at equal distances from a biscuit-syringe on to sheets of wafer-paper
5. put a strip of almond on the top of each
6. strew some sugar over
7. bake the macaroons in rather a slow oven, of a light brown colour; when hard and set, they are done, and must not be allowed to get very brown
