## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Hot butter or clarified dripping
- Salt

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin slices, as nearly the same size as possible.
2. Make some butter or dripping quite hot in a frying-pan.
3. Put in the potatoes, and fry them on both sides of a nice brown.
4. When they are crisp and done, take them up, place them on a cloth before the fire to drain the grease from them.
5. Serve very hot, after sprinkling them with salt.
6. The remains of cold potatoes may also be sliced and fried by the above recipe, but the slices must be cut a little thicker.
