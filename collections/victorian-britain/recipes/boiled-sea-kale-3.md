## Ingredients

- Sea-kale
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Well wash the kale
2. Cut away any wormeaten pieces
3. Tie it into small bunches
4. Put it into boiling water, salted in the above proportion
5. Let it boil quickly until tender
6. Take it out and drain
7. Untie the bunches
8. Serve with plain melted butter or white sauce, a little of which may be poured over the kale
