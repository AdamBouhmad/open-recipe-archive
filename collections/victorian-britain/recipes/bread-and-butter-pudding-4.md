## Ingredients

- butter
- raisins, stoned
- bread
- eggs
- milk
- sugar
- grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Butter a pudding-basin, and line the inside with a layer of raisins that have been previously stoned
2. Nearly fill the basin with slices of bread and butter with the crust cut off
3. Beat the eggs; add to them the milk, sugar, and grated nutmeg
4. Mix all well together, and pour the whole on to the bread and butter
5. Let it stand 1/2 hour
6. Tie a floured cloth over it
7. Boil for 1 hour, and serve with sweet sauce
8. Care must be taken that the basin is quite full before the cloth is tied over
