## Ingredients

- remains of roast chickens
- 1/2 teacupful of rice
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 quart of stock

## Directions

1. Take all the white meat and pound it with the rice, which has been slightly cooked, but not much
2. When it is all well pounded, dilute with the stock, and pass through a sieve
