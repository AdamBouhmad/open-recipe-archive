## Ingredients

- 4 middling-sized mackerel
- a nice delicate forcemeat
- 3 oz. of butter
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, take out the roes, and fill up with forcemeat, and sew up the slit.
2. Flour, and put them in a dish, heads and tails alternately, with the roes; and, between each layer, put some little pieces of butter, and pepper and salt.
3. Bake for 1/2 an hour, and either serve with plain melted butter or a maître d'hôtel sauce.
