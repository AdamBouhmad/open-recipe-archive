## Ingredients

- 1 large thick haddock
- 2 bay-leaves
- savoury herbs
- parsley
- butter
- pepper
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut up the haddock into square pieces
2. make a basin hot by means of hot water, which pour out
3. lay in the fish, with the bay-leaves and herbs
4. cover with boiling water
5. put a plate over to keep in the steam
6. let it remain for 10 minutes
7. take out the slices
8. put them in a hot dish
9. rub over with butter and pepper
10. serve
