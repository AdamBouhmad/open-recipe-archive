## Ingredients

- 2 dozen apples
- 1-1/2 pint of spring-water
- to every pint of juice 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1/2 oz. of isinglass
- the rind of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and cut the apples into quarters
2. Boil them with the lemon-peel until tender
3. Strain off the apples and run the juice through a jelly-bag
4. Boil the isinglass in 1/2 pint of water
5. Put the strained juice, sugar, and boiled isinglass into a lined saucepan or preserving-pan
6. Boil all together for about 1/4 hour
7. Put the jelly into moulds
8. Serve as a supper-dish with custard or whipped cream round it, optionally adding lemon-juice to improve the flavour
