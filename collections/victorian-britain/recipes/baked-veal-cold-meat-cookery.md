## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of cold roast veal
- a few slices of bacon
- 1 pint of bread crumbs
- 1/2 pint of good veal gravy
- 1/2 teaspoonful of minced lemon-peel
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- cayenne and salt to taste
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Mince finely the veal and bacon; add the bread crumbs, gravy, and seasoning, and stir these ingredients well together.
2. Beat up the eggs thoroughly; add these, mix the whole well together, put into a dish, and bake from 3/4 to 1 hour.
3. When liked, a little good gravy may be served in a tureen as an accompaniment.
