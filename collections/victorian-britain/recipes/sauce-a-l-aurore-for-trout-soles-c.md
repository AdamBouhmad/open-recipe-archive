## Ingredients

- The spawn of 1 lobster
- 1 oz. of butter
- 1/2 pint of Béchamel
- the juice of 1/2 lemon
- salt and cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Take the spawn and pound it in a mortar with the butter, until quite smooth, and work it through a hair sieve.
2. Put the Béchamel into a stewpan, add the pounded spawn, the lemon-juice, which must be strained, and a plentiful seasoning of cayenne and salt.
3. Let it just simmer, but do not allow it to boil, or the beautiful red colour of the sauce will be spoiled.
4. A small spoonful of anchovy essence may be added at pleasure.
