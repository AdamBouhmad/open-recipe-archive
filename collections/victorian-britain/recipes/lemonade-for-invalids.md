## Ingredients

- 1/2 lemon
- lump sugar to taste
- 1 pint of boiling water

## Directions

1. Pare off the rind of the lemon thinly
2. cut the lemon into 2 or 3 thick slices, and remove as much as possible of the white outside pith, and all the pips
3. Put the slices of lemon, the peel, and lump sugar into a jug
4. pour over the boiling water
5. cover it closely, and in 2 hours it will be fit to drink
6. It should either be strained or poured off from the sediment
