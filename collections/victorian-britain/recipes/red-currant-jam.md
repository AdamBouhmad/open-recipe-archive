## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of fruit
- 3/4 lb. of loaf sugar per lb. of fruit

## Directions

1. Let the fruit be gathered on a fine day
2. Weigh it, and then strip the currants from the stalks
3. Put them into a preserving-pan with sugar in the above proportion
4. Stir them, and boil them for about 3/4 hour
5. Carefully remove the scum as it rises
6. Put the jam into pots
7. When cold, cover with oiled papers
8. Over these put a piece of tissue-paper brushed over on both sides with the white of an egg
9. Press the paper round the top of the pot, and, when dry, the covering will be quite hard and air-tight
