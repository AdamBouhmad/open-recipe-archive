## Ingredients

- 3 oz. of bread crumbs
- 1 teaspoonful of minced savoury herbs
- 8 oysters
- 2 anchovies
- 2 oz. of suet
- salt
- pepper
- pounded mace to taste
- 6 tablespoonfuls of cream or milk
- yolks of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Beard and mince the oysters
2. Prepare and mix the other ingredients by recipe No. 416, and blend the whole thoroughly together
3. Moisten with the cream and eggs
4. Put all into a stewpan, and stir it over the fire till it thickens
5. Put it into the fish, which should have previously been cut open, and sew it up
