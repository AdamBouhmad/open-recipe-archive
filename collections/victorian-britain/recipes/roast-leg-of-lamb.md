## Ingredients

- Lamb
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Place the joint at a good distance from the fire at first, and baste well the whole time it is cooking
2. When nearly done, draw it nearer the fire to acquire a nice brown colour
3. Sprinkle a little fine salt over the meat, empty the dripping-pan of its contents
4. Pour in a little boiling water, and strain this over the meat
5. Serve with mint sauce and a fresh salad, and for vegetables send peas, spinach, or cauliflowers to table with it
