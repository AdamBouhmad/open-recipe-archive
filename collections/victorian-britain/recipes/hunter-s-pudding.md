## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of raisins
- 1 lb. of currants
- 1 lb. of suet
- 1 lb. of bread crumbs
- 3 lb. of moist sugar
- 8 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 3 lb. of mixed candied peel
- 1 glass of brandy
- 10 drops of essence of lemon
- 10 drops of essence of almonds
- 1/2 nutmeg
- 2 blades of mace
- 6 cloves

## Directions

1. Stone and shred the raisins rather small
2. Chop the suet finely
3. Rub the bread until all lumps are well broken
4. Pound the spice to powder
5. Cut the candied peel into thin shreds
6. Mix all these ingredients well together, adding the sugar
7. Beat the eggs to a strong froth
8. As they are beaten, drop into them the essence of lemon and essence of almonds
9. Stir these to the dry ingredients
10. Mix well
11. Add the brandy
12. Tie the pudding firmly in a cloth
13. Boil it for 6 hours at the least: 7 or 8 hours would be still better for it
14. Serve with boiled custard, or red-currant jelly, or brandy sauce
