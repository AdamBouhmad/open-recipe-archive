## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of treacle
- 1/2 lb. of suet
- the rind and juice of 1 lemon
- a few strips of candied lemon-peel
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the suet finely
2. Mix with it the flour, treacle, lemon-peel minced, and candied lemon-peel
3. Add the cream, lemon-juice, and 2 well-beaten eggs
4. Beat the pudding well, put it into a buttered basin, tie it down with a cloth, and boil from 3-1/2 to 4 hours
