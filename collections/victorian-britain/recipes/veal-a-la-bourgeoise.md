## Ingredients

- 2 to 3 lbs. of the loin or neck of veal
- 10 or 12 young carrots
- a bunch of green onions
- 2 slices of lean bacon
- 2 blades of pounded mace
- 1 bunch of savoury herbs
- pepper and salt to taste
- a few new potatoes
- 1 pint of green peas
- butter
- tomato sauce and ketchup

## Directions

1. Cut the veal into cutlets and trim them
2. Put the trimmings into a stewpan with a little butter
3. Lay in the cutlets and fry them a nice brown colour on both sides
4. Add the bacon, carrots, onions, spice, herbs, and seasoning
5. Pour in about a pint of boiling water
6. Stew gently for 2 hours on a very slow fire
7. When done, skim off the fat
8. Take out the herbs
9. Flavour the gravy with a little tomato sauce and ketchup
10. Have ready the peas and potatoes, boiled separately
11. Put them with the veal and serve
