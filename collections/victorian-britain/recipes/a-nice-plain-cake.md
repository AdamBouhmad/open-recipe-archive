## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of Borwick's baking-powder
- 1/4 lb. of good dripping
- 1 teacupful of moist sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 breakfast-cupful of milk
- 1 oz. of caraway seeds
- 1/2 lb. of currants

## Directions

1. Put the flour and baking-powder into a basin; stir those together; then rub in the dripping, add the sugar, caraway seeds, and currants; whisk the eggs with the milk, and beat all together very thoroughly until the ingredients are well mixed.
2. Butter a tin, put in the cake, and bake it from 11/2 to 2 hours.
