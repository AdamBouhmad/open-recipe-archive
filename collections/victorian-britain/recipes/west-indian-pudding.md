## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cream
- 1/2 lb. of loaf-sugar
- 1/2 lb. of Savoy or sponge-cakes
- 8 eggs
- 3 oz. of preserved green ginger

## Directions

1. Crumble down the cakes, put them into a basin, and pour over them the cream, which should be previously sweetened and brought to the boiling-point
2. Cover the basin, well beat the eggs, and when the cream is soaked up, stir them in
3. Butter a mould, arrange the ginger round it, pour in the pudding carefully, and tie it down with a cloth
4. Steam or boil it slowly for 1-1/2 hour, and serve with the syrup from the ginger, which should be warmed, and poured over the pudding
