## Ingredients

- A large fowl
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 handfuls of button mushrooms
- 1 slice of lean ham
- 3/4 pint of thickened gravy
- 1 teaspoonful of lemon-juice
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pounded sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut the fowl into quarters, roast it until three-parts done, and keep it well basted whilst at the fire
2. Take the fowl up, broil it for a few minutes over a clear fire, and season it with pepper and salt
3. Put the mushrooms into a stewpan with a small piece of butter, the ham, a seasoning of pepper and salt, and the gravy; simmer these gently for 1/2 hour
4. Add the lemon-juice and sugar
5. Dish the fowl, and pour the sauce round them
