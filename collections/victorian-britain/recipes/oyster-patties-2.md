## Ingredients

- 2 dozen oysters
- 2 oz. butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cream
- a little lemon-juice
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- cayenne to taste
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald the oysters in their own liquor, beard them, and cut each one into 3 pieces.
2. Put the butter into a stewpan, dredge in sufficient flour to dry it up.
3. Add the strained oyster-liquor with the other ingredients.
4. Put in the oysters, and let them heat gradually, but not boil fast.
5. Make the patty-cases as directed for lobster patties, No. 277.
6. Fill with the oyster mixture, and replace the covers.
