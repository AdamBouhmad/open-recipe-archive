## Ingredients

- 1 pint of new milk
- 1-1/4 oz. of isinglass
- rind of 1/2 lemon
- 1/4 lb. of loaf sugar
- 10 bitter almonds
- 1/2 oz. of sweet almonds
- 1 pint of cream

## Directions

1. Put the milk into a saucepan, with the isinglass, lemon-rind, and sugar, and let these ingredients stand by the side of the fire until the milk is well flavoured
2. Add the almonds, which should be blanched and pounded in a mortar to a paste, and let the milk just boil up
3. Strain it through a fine sieve or muslin into a jug, add the cream, and stir the mixture occasionally until nearly cold
4. Let it stand for a few minutes, then pour it into the mould, which should be previously oiled with the purest salad-oil, or dipped in cold water
5. Omit the sediment at the bottom of the jug when pouring into the mould
6. Turn it out carefully on a dish by loosening the edges from the mould, placing a dish on it, and turning it quickly over
7. Garnish as fancy dictates
