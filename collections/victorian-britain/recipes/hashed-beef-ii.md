## Ingredients

- remains of ribs or sirloin of beef
- 2 onions
- 1 carrot
- 1 bunch of savoury herbs
- pepper
- salt
- 1/2 blade of pounded mace
- flour
- rather more than 1 pint of water
- sippets of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Take off all the meat from the bones of ribs or sirloin of beef.
2. Remove the outside brown and gristle; place the meat on one side.
3. Well stew the bones and pieces with the onions, carrot, herbs, pepper, salt, and mace for about 2 hours, till it becomes a strong gravy, and is reduced to rather more than 1/2 pint.
4. Strain this, thicken with a teaspoonful of flour, and let the gravy cool.
5. Skim off all the fat.
6. Lay in the meat, let it get hot through, but do not allow it to boil.
7. Garnish with sippets of toasted bread.
