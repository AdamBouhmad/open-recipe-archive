## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of arrowroot
- 1/2 lb. of pounded white sugar
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- whites of 6 eggs
- flavouring to taste, of essence of almonds, or vanilla, or lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Stir in the sugar and arrowroot gradually, at the same time beating the mixture
3. Whisk the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
4. Add them to the other ingredients and beat well for 20 minutes
5. Put in whichever of the flavourings may be preferred
6. Pour the cake into a buttered mould or tin
7. Bake it in a moderate oven from 1 to 1-1/2 hour
