## Ingredients

- oysters to fill a pint measure
- 1 pint of sherry
- 3 oz. of salt
- 1 drachm of cayenne
- 2 drachms of pounded mace

## Directions

1. Procure the oysters very fresh, and open sufficient to fill a pint measure; save the liquor, and scald the oysters in it with the sherry
2. Strain the oysters, and put them in a mortar with the salt, cayenne, and mace; pound the whole until reduced to a pulp, then add it to the liquor in which they were scalded
3. Boil it again five minutes, and skim well
4. Rub the whole through a sieve, and, when cold, bottle and cork closely
5. The corks should be sealed
