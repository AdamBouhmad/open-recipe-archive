## Ingredients

- 1 pennyworth of borax
- 1/2 pint of olive-oil
- 1 pint of boiling water

## Directions

1. Pour the boiling water over the borax and oil
2. Let it cool
3. Put the mixture into a bottle
4. Shake it before using, and apply it with a flannel
