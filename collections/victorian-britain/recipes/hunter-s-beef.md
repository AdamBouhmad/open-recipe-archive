## Ingredients

- 25 lbs. round of beef
- 3 oz. saltpetre
- 3 oz. coarse sugar
- 1 oz. cloves
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1/2 oz. allspice
- 1 lb. salt
- 1/2 lb. bay-salt
- 1/2 pint water
- suet
- flour and water for crust

## Directions

1. Let the beef hang for 2 or 3 days, and remove the bone.
2. Pound spices, salt, &c. in the above proportion, and let them be reduced to the finest powder.
3. Put the beef into a pan, rub all the ingredients well into it, and turn and rub it every day for rather more than a fortnight.
4. When it has been sufficiently long in pickle, wash the meat, bind it up securely with tape, and put it into a pan with 1/2 pint of water at the bottom.
5. Mince some suet, cover the top of the meat with it, and over the pan put a common crust of flour and water.
6. Bake for 6 hours, and, when cold, remove the paste.
7. Save the gravy that flows from it. The beef may be glazed and garnished with meat jelly.
