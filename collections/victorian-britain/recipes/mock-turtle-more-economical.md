## Ingredients

- A knuckle of veal weighing 5 or 6 lbs.
- 2 cow-heels
- 2 large onions stuck with cloves
- 1 bunch of sweet herbs
- 3 blades of mace
- salt to taste
- 12 peppercorns
- 1 glass of sherry
- 24 force-meat balls
- a little lemon-juice
- 4 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients, except the force-meat balls and lemon-juice, in an earthen jar, and stew for 6 hours.
2. Do not open it till cold.
3. When wanted for use, skim off all the fat, and strain carefully; place it on the fire, cut up the meat into inch-and-a-half squares, put it, with the force-meat balls and lemon-juice, into the soup, and serve.
4. It can be flavoured with a tablespoonful of anchovy, or Harvey's sauce.
