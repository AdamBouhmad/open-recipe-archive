## Ingredients

- 2 slices of large cod, or the remains of any cold fish
- 3 oz. of butter
- 1 onion sliced
- A teacupful of white stock
- Thickening of butter and flour
- 1 small teaspoonful of curry-powder
- 1/4 pint of cream
- Salt and cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Flake the fish, and fry it of a nice brown colour with the butter and onions; put this in a stewpan, add the stock and thickening, and simmer for 10 minutes.
2. Stir the curry-powder into the cream; put it, with the seasoning, to the other ingredients; give one boil, and serve.
