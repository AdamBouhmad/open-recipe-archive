## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of coarse sugar
- 3/4 lb. of salt
- 1 oz. of saltpetre
- 1/2 a teacupful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Rub the hams well with common salt, and leave them for a day or two to drain.
2. Then rub well in, the above proportion of sugar, salt, saltpetre, and vinegar, and turn them every other day.
3. Keep them in the pickle 1 month, drain them, and send them to be smoked over a wood fire for 3 weeks or a month.
