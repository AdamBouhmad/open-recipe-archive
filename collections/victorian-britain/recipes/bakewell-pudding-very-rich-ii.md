## Ingredients

- 3/4 pint of bread crumbs
- 1 pint of milk
- 4 eggs
- 2 oz. of sugar
- 3 oz. of butter
- 1 oz. of pounded almonds
- jam

## Directions

1. Put the bread crumbs at the bottom of a pie-dish
2. Over them put a layer of jam of any kind that may be preferred
3. Mix the milk and eggs together
4. Add the sugar, butter, and pounded almonds
5. Beat all well together
6. Pour it into the dish and bake in a moderate oven for 1 hour
