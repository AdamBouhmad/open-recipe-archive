## Ingredients

- 4 oz. of Patna rice
- salt
- cayenne
- mace
- 2 quarts of white stock

## Directions

1. Throw the rice into boiling water, and let it remain 5 minutes
2. Pour it into a sieve, and allow it to drain well
3. Add it to the stock boiling, and allow it to stew till it is quite tender
4. Season to taste
5. Serve quickly
