## Ingredients

- Wheatears
- Fresh butter

## Directions

1. After the birds are picked, gutted, and cleaned, truss them like larks
2. Put them down to a quick fire
3. Baste them well with fresh butter
4. When done, which will be in about 20 minutes, dish them on fried bread crumbs
5. Garnish the dish with slices of lemon
