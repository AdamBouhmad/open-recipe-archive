## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast beef
- 1/4 lb. of bacon or ham per pound of cold meat
- Pepper and salt to taste
- 1 small bunch of minced savoury herbs
- 1 or 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mince the beef very finely (if underdone it will be better)
2. Add to it the bacon, which must also be chopped very small, and mix well together
3. Season and stir in the herbs
4. Bind with an egg, or 2 should 1 not be sufficient
5. Make it into small square cakes, about 1/2 inch thick
6. Fry them in hot dripping
7. Serve in a dish with good gravy poured round them
