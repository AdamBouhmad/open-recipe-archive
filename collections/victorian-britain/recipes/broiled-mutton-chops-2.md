## Ingredients

- Loin of mutton
- pepper and salt
- a small piece of butter

## Directions

1. Cut the chops from a well-hung tender loin of mutton
2. Remove a portion of the fat
3. Trim them into a nice shape
4. Slightly beat and level them
5. Place the gridiron over a bright clear fire
6. Rub the bars with a little fat
7. Lay on the chops
8. Whilst broiling, frequently turn them
9. In about 8 minutes they will be done
10. Season with pepper and salt
11. Dish them on a very hot dish
12. Rub a small piece of butter on each chop
13. Serve very hot and expeditiously
