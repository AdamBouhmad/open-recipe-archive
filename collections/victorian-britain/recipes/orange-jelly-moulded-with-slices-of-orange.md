## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 pint of orange jelly
- 4 oranges
- 1 pint of clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Boil 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar with 1/2 pint of water until there is no scum left, carefully removing scum as it rises
2. Carefully peel the oranges
3. Divide them into thin slices, without breaking the thin skin
4. Put these pieces of orange into the syrup, where let them remain for about 5 minutes
5. Take them out, and use the syrup for the jelly, which should be made by recipe No. 1454
6. When the oranges are well drained, and the jelly is nearly cold, pour a little of the jelly into the bottom of the mould
7. Lay in a few pieces of orange
8. Pour a little jelly over these, and when this is set, place another layer of oranges, proceeding in this manner until the mould is full
9. Put it in ice, or in a cool place
10. Before turning it out, wrap a cloth round the mould for a minute or two, which has been wrung out in boiling water
