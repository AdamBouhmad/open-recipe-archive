## Ingredients

- A slice of bread
- 1 quart of boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut a slice from a stale loaf (a piece of hard crust is better than anything else for the purpose)
2. Toast it of a nice brown on every side, but do not allow it to burn or blacken
3. Put it into a jug
4. Pour the boiling water over it
5. Cover it closely
6. Let it remain until cold
7. When strained, it will be ready for use
