## Ingredients

- 1/2 oz. of chocolate per person
- 1/2 pint of water per oz. of chocolate
- 1/2 pint of milk per oz. of chocolate
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Make the milk-and-water hot
2. Scrape the chocolate into it
3. Stir the mixture constantly and quickly until the chocolate is dissolved
4. Bring it to the boiling-point
5. Stir it well
6. Serve directly with white sugar
7. Alternatively, using a mill: put in the scraped chocolate, pour over it the boiling milk-and-water, and mill it over the fire until hot and frothy
