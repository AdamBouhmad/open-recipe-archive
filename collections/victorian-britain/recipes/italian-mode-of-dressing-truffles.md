## Ingredients

- 10 truffles
- 1/4 pint of salad-oil
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of minced parsley
- a very little finely-minced garlic
- 2 blades of pounded mace
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice

## Directions

1. After cleansing and brushing the truffles, cut them into thin slices, and put them in a baking-dish, on a seasoning of oil, pepper, salt, parsley, garlic, and mace in the above proportion.
2. Bake them for nearly an hour, and, just before serving, add the lemon-juice, and send them to table very hot.
