## Ingredients

- 1/2 gallon of water
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- broccoli

## Directions

1. Strip off the dead outside leaves, and the inside ones cut off level with the flower
2. Cut off the stalk close at the bottom
3. Put the broccoli into cold salt and water, with the heads downwards
4. Let them remain in this for about 3/4 hour until perfectly free from insects
5. Put them into a saucepan of boiling water, salted in the above proportion
6. Keep them boiling quickly over a brisk fire, with the saucepan uncovered
7. Take them up with a slice the moment they are done
8. Drain them well
9. Serve with a tureen of melted butter, a little of which should be poured over the broccoli
