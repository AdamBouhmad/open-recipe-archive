## Ingredients

- red, rough, ripe gooseberries (8 lbs.)
- red-currant juice (1 quart)
- loaf sugar (5 lbs.)
- oiled paper
- tissue-paper
- white of an egg

## Directions

1. Have the fruit gathered in dry weather, and cut off the tops and tails
2. Prepare 1 quart of red-currant juice, the same as for red-currant jelly No. 1533
3. Put it into a preserving-pan with the sugar, and keep stirring until the latter is dissolved
4. Keep it boiling for about 5 minutes; skim well
5. Put in the gooseberries, and let them boil from 1/2 to 3/4 hour
6. Turn the whole into an earthen pan, and let it remain for 2 days
7. Boil the jam up again until it looks clear
8. Put it into pots, and when cold, cover with oiled paper
9. Over the jars put tissue-paper brushed over on both sides with the white of an egg, and store away in a dry place
10. Keep the jam well stirred and well skimmed, to prevent it burning at the bottom of the pan
