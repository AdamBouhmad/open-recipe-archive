## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of sago
- 1/3 pint of water
- 1/4 pint of port or sherry
- rind and juice of 1 small lemon
- sugar to taste
- a little pounded cinnamon when the flavour is liked

## Directions

1. Wash the sago in two or three waters
2. Put it into a saucepan, with the water and lemon-peel
3. Let it simmer gently by the side of the fire for 10 minutes
4. Take out the lemon-peel
5. Add the remaining ingredients
6. Give one boil, and serve
7. Be particular to strain the lemon-juice before adding it to the sauce
