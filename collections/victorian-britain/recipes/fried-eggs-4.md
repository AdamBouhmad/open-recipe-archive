## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/4 lb. of lard, butter or clarified dripping

## Directions

1. Place a delicately-clean frying-pan over a gentle fire.
2. Put in the fat, and allow it to come to the boiling-point.
3. Break the eggs into cups, slip them into the boiling fat, and let them remain until the whites are delicately set.
4. Whilst they are frying, ladle a little of the fat over them.
5. Take them up with a slice, drain them for a minute from their greasy moisture, trim them neatly.
6. Serve on slices of fried bacon or ham, or place the eggs in the middle of the dish, with the bacon put round as a garnish.
