## Ingredients

- tea leaves
- boiling water
- a few grains of carbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Warm the teapot with boiling water and let it remain for two or three minutes for the vessel to become thoroughly hot, then pour it away
2. Put in the tea, allowing a teaspoonful to each person and one over
3. Pour in from 1/2 to 3/4 pint of boiling water
4. Close the lid and let it stand for the tea to draw from 5 to 10 minutes
5. Fill up the pot with water
6. For very hard water, a few grains of carbonate of soda may be added before the boiling water is poured on the tea, but use sparingly to avoid a soapy taste
7. When more tea is required, empty the pot of the old leaves, scald it, and make fresh tea in the usual manner
