## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- 3 oz. of butter
- pepper and salt to taste
- mushroom-sauce or good gravy

## Directions

1. Take care that the pigeons are quite fresh, and carefully pluck, draw, and wash them.
2. Split the backs, rub the birds over with butter, season them with pepper and salt.
3. Broil them over a moderate fire for 1/4 hour or 20 minutes.
4. Serve very hot, with either mushroom-sauce or a good gravy.
