## Ingredients

- Remains of cold game
- 1 onion stuck with 3 cloves
- a few whole peppers
- a strip of lemon-peel
- salt to taste
- thickening of butter and flour
- 1 glass of port wine
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- 1 tablespoonful of ketchup
- 1 pint of water or weak stock

## Directions

1. Cut the remains of cold game into joints, reserving the best pieces.
2. Put the inferior pieces and trimmings into a stewpan with the onion, pepper, lemon-peel, salt, and water or weak stock.
3. Stew these for about an hour and strain the gravy.
4. Thicken the gravy with butter and flour.
5. Add the wine, lemon-juice, and ketchup.
6. Lay in the pieces of game and let them gradually warm through by the side of the fire.
7. Do not allow it to boil, or the game will be hard.
8. When on the point of simmering, serve and garnish the dish with sippets of toasted bread.
