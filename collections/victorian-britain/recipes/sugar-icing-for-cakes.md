## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- the whites of 4 eggs
- 1 oz. of fine starch

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs to a strong froth, and gradually sift in the sugar, which should be reduced to the finest possible powder, and gradually add the starch, also finely powdered.
2. Beat the mixture well until the sugar is smooth.
3. With a spoon or broad knife lay the icing equally over the cakes.
4. Place the cakes in a very cool oven, and allow the icing to dry and harden, but not to colour.
5. All iced cakes should be kept in a very dry place.
