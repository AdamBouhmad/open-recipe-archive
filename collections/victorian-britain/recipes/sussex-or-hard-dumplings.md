## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/2 pint of water
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Mix the flour and water together to a smooth paste, previously adding a small quantity of salt.
2. Form this into small round dumplings.
3. Drop them into boiling water, and boil from 1/2 to 3/4 hour.
4. They may be served with roast or boiled meat; in the latter case they may be cooked with the meat, but should be dropped into the water when it is quite boiling.
