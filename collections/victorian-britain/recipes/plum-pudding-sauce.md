## Ingredients

- 1 wineglassful of brandy
- 2 oz. of very fresh butter
- 1 glass of Madeira
- pounded sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Put the pounded sugar in a basin, with part of the brandy and the butter.
2. Let it stand by the side of the fire until it is warm and the sugar and butter are dissolved.
3. Add the rest of the brandy, with the Madeira.
4. Either pour it over the pudding, or serve in a tureen.
