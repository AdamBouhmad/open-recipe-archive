## Ingredients

- walnuts
- 3 quarts of vinegar per quart of walnut-juice
- 1 pint of Indian soy per quart of walnut-juice
- 1 oz. of cayenne per quart of walnut-juice
- 2 oz. of shalots per quart of walnut-juice
- 3/4 oz. of garlic per quart of walnut-juice
- 1/2 pint of port wine per quart of walnut-juice

## Directions

1. Be very particular in choosing the walnuts as soon as they appear in the market
2. Pound them in a mortar to a pulp
3. Strew some salt over them, and let them remain thus for two or three days, occasionally stirring and moving them about
4. Press out the juice
5. To each quart of walnut-liquor allow the above proportion of vinegar, soy, cayenne, shalots, garlic, and port wine
6. Pound each ingredient separately in a mortar, then mix them well together, and store away for use in small bottles
7. The corks should be well sealed
