## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of suet
- 1/4 lb. of moist sugar
- 2 large teaspoonfuls of grated ginger

## Directions

1. Shred the suet very fine, mix it with the flour, sugar, and ginger; stir all well together
2. butter a basin, and put the mixture in dry; tie a cloth over, and boil for 3 hours
