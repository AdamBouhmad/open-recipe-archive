## Ingredients

- A few slices of cold roast veal
- a few slices of cold ham
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of minced parsley
- a little pepper
- good gravy

## Directions

1. Cut off all the brown outside from the veal, and cut the eggs into slices.
2. Procure a pretty mould; lay veal, ham, eggs, and parsley in layers, with a little pepper between each.
3. When the mould is full, get some strong stock, and fill up the shape.
4. Bake for 1/2 hour, and when cold, turn it out.
