## Ingredients

- Perch, tench, soles, eels, or flounders
- water
- bunch of chopped parsley
- roots
- salt
- finely-chopped parsley
- roots cut into slices
- brown bread and butter

## Directions

1. Put some water into a stewpan with a bunch of chopped parsley, some roots, and sufficient salt to make it brackish
2. Let these simmer for 1 hour
3. Stew the fish in this water
4. When they are done, take them out to drain
5. Have ready some finely-chopped parsley, and a few roots cut into slices of about one inch thick and an inch in length
6. Put the fish in a tureen or deep dish, strain the liquor over them, and add the minced parsley and roots
7. Serve with brown bread and butter
