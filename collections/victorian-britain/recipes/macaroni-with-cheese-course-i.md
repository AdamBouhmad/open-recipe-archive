## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of pipe macaroni
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 6 oz. of Parmesan or Cheshire cheese
- Pepper and salt to taste
- 1 pint of milk
- 2 pints of water
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Put the milk and water into a saucepan with sufficient salt to flavour it; place it on the fire, and, when it boils quickly, drop in the macaroni.
2. Keep the water boiling until it is quite tender; drain the macaroni, and put it into a deep dish.
3. Have ready the grated cheese, either Parmesan or Cheshire; sprinkle it amongst the macaroni and some of the butter cut into small pieces, reserving some of the cheese for the top layer.
4. Season with a little pepper, and cover the top layer of cheese with some very fine bread crumbs.
5. Warm, without oiling, the remainder of the butter, and pour it gently over the bread crumbs.
6. Place the dish before a bright fire to brown the crumbs; turn it once or twice, that it may be equally coloured, and serve very hot.
