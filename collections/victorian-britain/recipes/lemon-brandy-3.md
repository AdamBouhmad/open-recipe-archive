## Ingredients

- 1 pint of brandy
- the rind of two small lemons
- 2 oz. of loaf-sugar
- 1/4 pint of water

## Directions

1. Peel the lemons rather thin, taking care to have none of the white pith.
2. Put the rinds into a bottle with the brandy, and let them infuse for 24 hours, when they should be strained.
3. Boil the sugar with the water for a few minutes, skim it, and, when cold, add it to the brandy.
4. A dessertspoonful of this will be found an excellent flavouring for boiled custards.
