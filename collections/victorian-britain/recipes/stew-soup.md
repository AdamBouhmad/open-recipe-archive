## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of beef
- 5 onions
- 5 turnips
- 3/4 lb. of rice
- a large bunch of parsley
- a few sweet herbs
- pepper and salt
- 2 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Cut the beef up in small pieces, add the other ingredients, and boil gently for 2 1/2 hours
