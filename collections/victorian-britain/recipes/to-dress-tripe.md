## Ingredients

- Tripe
- onion sauce
- milk and water

## Directions

1. Ascertain that the tripe is quite fresh, and have it cleaned and dressed
2. Cut away the coarsest fat, and boil it in equal proportions of milk and water for 3/4 hour
3. Have ready some onion sauce made by recipe No. 484, dish the tripe, smother it with the sauce, and the remainder send to table in a tureen
