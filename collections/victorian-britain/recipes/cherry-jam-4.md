## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of fruit, weighed before stoning
- 1/2 lb. of sugar per lb. of fruit
- 1 pint of red-currant juice per 6 lbs. of fruit
- 1 lb. of sugar per pint of juice

## Directions

1. Weigh the fruit before stoning, and allow half the weight of sugar
2. Stone the cherries, and boil them in a preserving-pan until nearly all the juice is dried up
3. Add the sugar, which should be crushed to powder, and the currant-juice, allowing 1 pint to every 6 lbs. of cherries (original weight), and 1 lb. of sugar to every pint of juice
4. Boil all together until it jellies, which will be in from 20 minutes to 1/2 hour
5. Skim the jam well, keep it well stirred
6. A few minutes before it is done, crack some of the stones, and add the kernels
