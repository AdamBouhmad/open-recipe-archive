## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of minced parsley
- 1/2 pint of melted butter
- water, slightly salted

## Directions

1. Put into a saucepan a small quantity of water, slightly salted, and when it boils, throw in a good bunch of parsley which has been previously washed and tied together in a bunch
2. Let it boil for 5 minutes, drain it, mince the leaves very fine, and put the above quantity in a tureen
3. Pour over it 1/2 pint of smoothly-made melted butter
4. Stir once, that the ingredients may be thoroughly mixed, and serve
