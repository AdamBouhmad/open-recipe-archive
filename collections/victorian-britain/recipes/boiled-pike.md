## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of salt to each gallon of water
- a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the pike, and fasten the tail in its mouth by means of a skewer.
2. Lay it in cold water, and when it boils, throw in the salt and vinegar.
3. The time for boiling depends on the size of the fish; a middling-sized pike will take about 1/2 an hour.
4. Serve with Dutch or anchovy sauce, and plain melted butter.
