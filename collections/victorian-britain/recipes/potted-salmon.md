## Ingredients

- Salmon
- pounded mace, cloves, and pepper to taste
- 3 bay-leaves
- 1/4 lb. butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Skin the salmon, and clean it thoroughly by wiping with a cloth (water would spoil it)
2. cut it into square pieces, which rub with salt
3. let them remain till thoroughly drained
4. lay them in a dish with the other ingredients, and bake
5. When quite done, drain them from the gravy, press into pots for use
6. when cold, pour over it clarified butter
