## Ingredients

- A few slices of tolerably lean cold roast or boiled beef
- 3 oz. of butter
- 2 onions
- 1 wineglassful of beer
- 1 dessertspoonful of curry powder

## Directions

1. Cut up the beef into pieces about 1 inch square.
2. Put the butter into a stewpan with the onions sliced, and fry them of a lightly-brown colour.
3. Add all the other ingredients, and stir gently over a brisk fire for about 10 minutes.
4. Should this be thought too dry, more beer, or a spoonful or two of gravy or water, may be added; but a good curry should not be very thin.
5. Place it in a deep dish, with an edging of dry boiled rice, in the same manner as for other curries.
