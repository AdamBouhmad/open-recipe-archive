## Ingredients

- About 3 dozen good boiling apples
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 pint of water
- the rind of 1/2 lemon minced very fine
- the whites of 2 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of pounded sugar
- a few sweet almonds

## Directions

1. Peel and core a dozen of the apples without dividing them
2. Stew them very gently in a lined saucepan with 1/2 lb. of sugar and 1/2 pint of water until tender, then lift them carefully on to a dish
3. Pare, core, and cut the remainder of the apples into thin slices
4. Put them into the same syrup with the lemon-peel and boil gently until reduced to a marmalade, keeping them stirred to prevent burning
5. Cover the bottom of a dish with some of the marmalade
6. Layer the stewed apples over the marmalade, placing marmalade in the insides and between each apple
7. Continue layering, forming the whole into a raised oval shape
8. Whip the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth and mix with the pounded sugar
9. Cover the apples very smoothly all over with the icing
10. Blanch and cut each almond into 4 or 5 strips
11. Place these strips at equal distances over the icing sticking up
12. Strew over a little rough pounded sugar
13. Place the dish in a very slow oven to colour the almonds and for the apples to get warm through
