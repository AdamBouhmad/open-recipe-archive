## Ingredients

- egg
- bread crumbs
- hot lard
- plain melted butter or anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, brush it over with egg, and cover with bread crumbs.
2. Have ready some boiling lard.
3. Put the fish in, and fry a nice brown.
4. Serve with plain melted butter or anchovy sauce.
