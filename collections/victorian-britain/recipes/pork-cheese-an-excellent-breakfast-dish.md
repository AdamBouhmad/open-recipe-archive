## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of cold roast pork
- pepper
- salt
- 1 dessertspoonful of minced parsley
- 4 leaves of sage
- a very small bunch of savoury herbs
- 2 blades of pounded mace
- a little nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoonful of minced lemon-peel
- good strong gravy

## Directions

1. Cut, but do not chop, the pork into fine pieces, and allow 1/4 lb. of fat to each pound of lean.
2. Season with pepper and salt.
3. Pound well the spices, and chop finely the parsley, sage, herbs, and lemon-peel, and mix the whole nicely together.
4. Put it into a mould, fill up with good strong well-flavoured gravy, and bake rather more than one hour.
5. When cold, turn it out of the mould.
