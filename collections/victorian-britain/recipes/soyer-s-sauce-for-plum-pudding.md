## Ingredients

- The yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of powdered sugar
- 1 gill of milk
- a very little grated lemon-rind
- 2 small wineglassfuls of brandy

## Directions

1. Separate the yolks from the whites of 3 eggs, and put the former into a stewpan; add the sugar, milk, and grated lemon-rind, and stir over the fire until the mixture thickens; but do not allow it to boil.
2. Put in the brandy; let the sauce stand by the side of the fire, to get quite hot; keep stirring it, and serve in a boat or tureen separately, or pour it over the pudding.
