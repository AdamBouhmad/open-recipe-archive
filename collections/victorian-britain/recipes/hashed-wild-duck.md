## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast wild duck
- 1 pint of good brown gravy
- 2 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs
- 1 glass of claret
- salt
- cayenne
- mixed spices to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon or Seville orange-juice

## Directions

1. Cut the remains of the duck into neat joints, put them into a stewpan, with all the above ingredients.
2. Let them get gradually hot by the side of the fire, and occasionally stir the contents.
3. When on the point of boiling, serve, and garnish the dish with sippets of toasted bread.
