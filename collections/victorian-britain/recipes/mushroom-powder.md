## Ingredients

- 1/2 peck of large mushrooms
- 2 onions
- 12 cloves
- 1/4 oz. of pounded mace
- 2 teaspoonfuls of white pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the mushrooms, wipe them perfectly free from grit and dirt, remove the black fur, and reject all those that are at all worm-eaten.
2. Put them into a stewpan with the above ingredients, but without water.
3. Shake them over a clear fire, till all the liquor is dried up, and be careful not to let them burn.
4. Arrange them on tins, and dry them in a slow oven.
5. Pound them to a fine powder, which put into small dry bottles.
6. Cork well, seal the corks, and keep it in a dry place.
7. In using this powder, add it to the gravy just before serving, when it will merely require one boil-up.
