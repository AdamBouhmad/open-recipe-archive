## Ingredients

- 3/4 pint of milk
- 3/4 pint of cream
- 1-1/2 oz. of isinglass
- raspberry jelly
- sugar to taste
- 2 tablespoonfuls of brandy

## Directions

1. Boil the milk, cream, and isinglass together for 1/4 hour, or until the latter is melted, and strain it through a hair sieve into a basin
2. Let it cool a little
3. then add to it sufficient raspberry jelly, which, when melted, would make 1/3 pint, and stir well till the ingredients are thoroughly mixed
4. If not sufficiently sweet, add a little pounded sugar with the brandy
5. whisk the mixture well until nearly cold
6. put it into a well-oiled mould, and set it in a cool place till perfectly set
