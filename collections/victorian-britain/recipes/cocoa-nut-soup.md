## Ingredients

- 6 oz. of grated cocoa-nut
- 6 oz. of rice flour
- 1/2 a teaspoonful of mace
- cayenne to taste
- salt to taste
- 1/4 of a pint of boiling cream
- 3 quarts of medium stock No. 105

## Directions

1. Take the dark rind from the cocoa-nut, and grate it down small on a clean grater; weigh it, and allow, for each quart of stock, 2 oz. of the cocoa-nut.
2. Simmer it gently for 1 hour in the stock, which should then be strained closely from it, and thickened for table.
