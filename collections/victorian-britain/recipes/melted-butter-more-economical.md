## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- salt to taste
- 1/2 pint of water

## Directions

1. Mix the flour and water to a smooth batter, which put into a saucepan.
2. Add the butter and a seasoning of salt, keep stirring one way till all the ingredients are melted and perfectly smooth.
3. Let the whole boil for a minute or two, and serve.
