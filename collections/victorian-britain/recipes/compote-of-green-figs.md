## Ingredients

- 1 pint of syrup No. 1512
- 1-1/2 pint of green figs
- The rind of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Make a syrup by recipe No. 1512, boiling with it the lemon-rind, and carefully remove all the scum as it rises
2. Put in the figs, and simmer them very slowly until tender
3. Dish them on a glass dish
4. Reduce the syrup by boiling it quickly for 5 minutes
5. Take out the lemon-peel, pour the syrup over the figs
6. When cold, the compote will be ready for table
7. A little port wine, or lemon-juice, added just before the figs are done, will be found an improvement
