## Ingredients

- 2 soles
- salt, cayenne, and pounded mace to taste
- the juice of 1/2 lemon
- salt and water
- 1/2 pint of cream

## Directions

1. Skin, wash, and fillet the soles, and divide each fillet in 2 pieces.
2. Lay them in cold salt and water, which bring gradually to a boil.
3. When the water boils, take out the fish, lay it in a delicately clean stewpan, and cover with the cream.
4. Add the seasoning, simmer very gently for ten minutes, and, just before serving, put in the lemon-juice.
