## Ingredients

- Black currants
- 1/4 pint of water per pint of juice
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar per pint of juice

## Directions

1. Strip the currants from the stalks by holding the bunch in one hand, and passing a small silver fork down the currants
2. Put them into a jar, place this jar in a saucepan of boiling water, and simmer them until their juice is extracted
3. Strain them, and to every pint of juice allow the above proportion of sugar and water
4. Stir these ingredients together cold until the sugar is dissolved
5. Place the preserving-pan on the fire, and boil the jelly for about 1/2 hour, reckoning from the time it commences to boil all over
6. Carefully remove the scum as it rises
7. When the jelly becomes firm when a little is put on a plate, it is done
8. Put it into small pots, and cover
