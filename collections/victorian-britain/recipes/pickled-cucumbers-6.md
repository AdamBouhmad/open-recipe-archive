## Ingredients

- 1 oz. of whole pepper
- 1 oz. of bruised ginger
- sufficient vinegar to cover the cucumbers
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the cucumbers in thick slices
2. Sprinkle salt over them
3. Let them remain for 24 hours
4. The next day, drain them well for 6 hours
5. Put them into a jar
6. Pour boiling vinegar over them
7. Keep them in a warm place
8. In a short time, boil up the vinegar again
9. Add pepper and ginger in the above proportion
10. Instantly cover them up
11. Tie them down with bladder
12. In a few days they will be fit for use
