## Ingredients

- horseradish vinegar
- cayenne
- 1/2 a teacupful of mustard

## Directions

1. Have ready sufficient horseradish vinegar to mix with the above proportion of mustard.
2. Put the mustard in a cup, with a slight seasoning of cayenne.
3. Mix it perfectly smooth with the vinegar, adding this a little at a time.
4. Rub down with the back of a spoon any lumps that may appear, and do not let it be too thin.
