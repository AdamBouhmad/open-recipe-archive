## Ingredients

- The remains of roast venison
- its own or mutton gravy
- butter and flour for thickening

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from the bones in neat slices
2. If there is sufficient of its own gravy left, put the meat into this, as it is preferable to any other
3. Should there not be enough, put the bones and trimmings into a stewpan, with about a pint of mutton gravy; let them stew gently for an hour, and strain the gravy
4. Put a little flour and butter into the stewpan, keep stirring until brown, then add the strained gravy, and give it a boil up; skim and strain again, and, when a little cool, put in the slices of venison
5. Place the stewpan by the side of the fire, and, when on the point of simmering, serve: do not allow it to boil, or the meat will be hard
6. Send red-currant jelly to table with it
