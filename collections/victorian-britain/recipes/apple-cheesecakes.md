## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of apple pulp
- 1/4 lb. of sifted sugar
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 4 eggs
- the rind and juice of 1 lemon
- puff-paste

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and boil sufficient apples to make 1/2 lb. when cooked
2. Add to these the sugar, the butter, which should be melted
3. Add the eggs, leaving out 2 of the whites, and take grated rind and juice of 1 lemon
4. Stir the mixture well
5. Line some patty-pans with puff-paste
6. Put in the mixture
7. Bake about 20 minutes
