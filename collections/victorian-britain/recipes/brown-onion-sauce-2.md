## Ingredients

- 6 large onions
- rather more than 1/2 pint of good gravy
- 2 oz. of butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Slice and fry the onions of a pale brown in a stewpan, with the above quantity of butter, keeping them well stirred, that they do not get black
2. When a nice colour, pour over the gravy, and let them simmer gently until tender
3. Now skim off every particle of fat, add the seasoning, and rub the whole through a tammy or sieve
4. Put it back in the saucepan to warm, and when it boils, serve
