## Ingredients

- Turnips
- to each 1/2 gallon of water allow 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Pare the turnips, and, should they be very large, divide them into quarters; but, unless this is the case, let them be cooked whole.
2. Put them into a saucepan of boiling water, salted in the above proportion, and let them boil gently until tender.
3. Try them with a fork, and, when done, take them up in a colander; let them thoroughly drain, and serve.
