## Ingredients

- Snipes
- butter
- flour
- buttered toast
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Skin the snipe and the neck.
2. Twist the legs at the first joint, press the feet upon the thighs, and pass a skewer through these and the body.
3. Place four on a skewer, tie them on to the jack or spit.
4. Roast before a clear fire for about 1/4 hour.
5. Put some pieces of buttered toast into the dripping-pan to catch the trails.
6. Flour and froth the birds nicely.
7. Dish the pieces of toast with the snipes on them, and pour round, but not over them, a little good brown gravy.
8. Send to table very hot and expeditiously.
