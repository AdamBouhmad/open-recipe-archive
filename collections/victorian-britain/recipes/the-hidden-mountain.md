## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- a few slices of citron
- sugar to taste
- 1/4 pint of cream
- a layer of any kind of jam

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks of the eggs separately
2. Mix them and beat well again, adding a few thin slices of citron, the cream, and sufficient pounded sugar to sweeten it nicely
3. When the mixture is well beaten, put it into a buttered pan, and fry the same as a pancake; but it should be three times the thickness of an ordinary pancake
4. Cover it with jam, and garnish with slices of citron and holly-leaves
5. Serve this dish cold
