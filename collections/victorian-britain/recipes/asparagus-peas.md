## Ingredients

- 100 heads of asparagus
- 2 oz. of butter
- a small bunch of parsley
- 2 or 3 green onions
- flour
- 1 lump of sugar
- the yolks of 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- salt

## Directions

1. Carefully scrape the asparagus
2. Cut it into pieces of an equal size, avoiding that which is in the least hard or tough
3. Throw them into cold water
4. Boil the asparagus in salt and water until three-parts done
5. Take it out, drain, and place it on a cloth to dry the moisture away from it
6. Put it into a stewpan with the butter, parsley, and onions
7. Shake over a brisk fire for 10 minutes
8. Dredge in a little flour
9. Add the sugar
10. Moisten with boiling water
11. When boiled a short time and reduced, take out the parsley and onions
12. Thicken with the yolks of 2 eggs beaten with the cream
13. Add a seasoning of salt
14. When the whole is on the point of simmering, serve
15. Make the sauce sufficiently thick to adhere to the vegetable
