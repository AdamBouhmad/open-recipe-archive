## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of bitter almonds
- 1 oz. of sweet almonds
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- the rinds of 3 lemons
- 1 quart of Irish whiskey or gin
- 1 tablespoonful of clarified honey
- 4 pint of new milk

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound the almonds, and mix with them the sugar, which should also be pounded
2. Boil the milk; let it stand till quite cold
3. Mix all the ingredients together, and let them remain for 10 days, shaking them every day
4. Filter the mixture through blotting-paper
5. Bottle off for use in small bottles, and seal the corks down
