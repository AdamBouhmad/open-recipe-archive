## Ingredients

- Salsify
- water
- salt
- butter
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Scrape the roots gently, so as to strip them only of their outside peel
2. Cut them into pieces about 4 inches long
3. As they are peeled, throw them into water with which has been mixed a little lemon-juice, to prevent their discolouring
4. Put them into boiling water, with salt, butter, and lemon-juice in the above proportion
5. Let them boil rapidly until tender
6. Try them with a fork; and, when it penetrates easily, they are done
7. Drain the salsify
8. Serve with a good white sauce or French melted butter
