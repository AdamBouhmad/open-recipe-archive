## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of suet crust
- 1 lb. of treacle
- 1/2 teaspoonful of grated ginger

## Directions

1. Make a suet crust with 1 lb. of flour by rolling it out to the thickness of 1/2 inch
2. Spread the treacle equally over it, leaving a small margin where the paste joins
3. Close the ends securely
4. Tie the pudding in a floured cloth
5. Plunge it into boiling water, and boil for 2 hours
