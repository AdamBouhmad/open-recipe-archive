## Ingredients

- 10 or 12 large turnips
- to each 1/2 gallon of water allow 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- 2 oz. of butter
- cayenne or white pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Pare the turnips, quarter them, and put them into boiling water, salted in the above proportion; boil them until tender; then drain them in a colander, and squeeze them as dry as possible by pressing them with the back of a large plate.
2. When quite free from water, rub the turnips with a wooden spoon through the colander.
