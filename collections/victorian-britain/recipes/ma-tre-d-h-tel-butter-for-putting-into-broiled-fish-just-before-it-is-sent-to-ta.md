## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 2 dessertspoonfuls of minced parsley
- salt and pepper to taste
- the juice of 1 large lemon

## Directions

1. Work the above ingredients well together, and let them be thoroughly mixed with a wooden spoon.
2. If this is used as a sauce, it may be poured either under or over the meat or fish it is intended to be served with.
