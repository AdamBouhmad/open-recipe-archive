## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar
- the rind of 1 lemon
- 1 oz. of ground ginger
- 1 nutmeg grated
- 1/2 teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
- 1 gill of milk

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the flour
2. Add the sugar, which should be finely pounded and sifted, and the minced lemon-rind, ginger, and nutmeg
3. Mix these well together
4. Make the milk just warm
5. Stir in the soda
6. Work the whole into a nice smooth paste
7. Roll it out
8. Cut it into cakes
9. Bake in a moderate oven from 15 to 20 minutes
