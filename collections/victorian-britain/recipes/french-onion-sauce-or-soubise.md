## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of Béchamel
- 1 bay-leaf
- pounded mace
- cayenne
- 6 onions
- a small piece of ham

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and cut them in halves
2. Put them in a stewpan, with just sufficient water to cover them, and add the bay-leaf, ham, cayenne, and mace
3. Be careful to keep the lid closely shut, and simmer them until tender
4. Take them out and drain thoroughly
5. Rub them through a tammy or sieve with a wooden spoon, and put them to 1/2 pint of Béchamel
6. Keep stirring over the fire until it boils, when serve
7. If it should require any more seasoning, add it to taste
