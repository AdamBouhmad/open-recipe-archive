## Ingredients

- 1/2 oz. of cayenne pepper
- 1/2 pint of strong spirit, or 1 pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the vinegar, or spirit, into a bottle, with the above proportion of cayenne, and let it steep for a month, when strain off and bottle for use.
