## Ingredients

- 1-1/4 lb. flour
- 1/2 lb. butter
- rather more than 1/2 pint water

## Directions

1. Rub the butter lightly into the flour
2. Mix it to a smooth paste with the water
3. Roll it out 2 or 3 times, and it will be ready for use
