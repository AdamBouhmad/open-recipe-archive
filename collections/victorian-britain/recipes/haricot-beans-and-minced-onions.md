## Ingredients

- 1 quart of white haricot beans
- 4 middling-sized onions
- 1/4 pint of good brown gravy
- pepper and salt to taste
- a little flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Peel and mince the onions not too finely, and fry them in butter of a light brown colour
2. Dredge over them a little flour, and add the gravy and a seasoning of pepper and salt
3. Have ready a pint of haricot beans well boiled and drained; put them with the onions and gravy
4. Mix all well together, and serve very hot
