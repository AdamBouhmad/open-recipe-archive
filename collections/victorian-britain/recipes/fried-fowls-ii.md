## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast fowl
- vinegar
- salt and cayenne to taste
- 4 minced shalots
- yolk of egg
- bread crumbs
- 1 blade of pounded mace per teacupful of bread crumbs
- 5 teaspoonful of minced lemon-peel per teacupful of bread crumbs
- 1 saltspoonful of salt per teacupful of bread crumbs
- a few grains of cayenne per teacupful of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Steep the pieces of fowl as in the preceding recipe
2. Dip them into the yolk of an egg or clarified butter
3. Sprinkle over bread crumbs with which have been mixed salt, mace, cayenne, and lemon-peel in the above proportion
4. Fry a light brown, and serve with or without gravy, as may be preferred
