## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of good mutton broth
- 6 dozen oysters
- 2 oz. butter
- 1 oz. of flour

## Directions

1. Beard the oysters, and scald them in their own liquor; then add it, well strained, to the broth; thicken with the butter and flour, and simmer for 1/4 of an hour.
2. Put in the oysters, stir well, but do not let it boil, and serve very hot.
