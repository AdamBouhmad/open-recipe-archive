## Ingredients

- 2 large lemons
- 6 large apples
- 1/2 lb. of suet
- 1 lb. of currants
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 2 oz. of candied lemon-peel
- 1 oz. of citron
- mixed spice to taste

## Directions

1. Pare the lemons, squeeze them, and boil the peel until tender enough to mash
2. Add to the mashed lemon-peel the apples, which should be pared, cored, and minced
3. Add the chopped suet, currants, sugar, sliced peel, and spice
4. Strain the lemon-juice to these ingredients, stir the mixture well, and put it in a jar with a closely-fitting lid
5. Stir occasionally, and in a week or 10 days the mincemeat will be ready for use
