## Ingredients

- 6 oz. of rice
- the yolks of 4 eggs
- 1/2 a pint of cream
- rather more than 2 quarts of stock No. 105

## Directions

1. Boil the rice in the stock, and rub half of it through a tammy
2. Put the stock in the stewpan, add all the rice, and simmer gently for 5 minutes
3. Beat the yolks of the eggs, mix them with the cream (previously boiled), and strain through a hair sieve
4. Take the soup off the fire, add the eggs and cream, stirring frequently
5. Heat it gradually, stirring all the time; but do not let it boil, or the eggs will curdle
