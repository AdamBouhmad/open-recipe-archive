## Ingredients

- 3 roots of celery
- 1 pint of gravy
- 2 onions sliced
- 2 lbs. of cold roast or boiled beef

## Directions

1. Cut the celery into 2-inch pieces, put them in a stew-pan, with the gravy and onions, simmer gently until the celery is tender.
2. When the celery is tender, add the beef cut into rather thick pieces.
3. Stew gently for 10 minutes, and serve with fried potatoes.
