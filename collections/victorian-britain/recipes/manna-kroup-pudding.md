## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of manna kroup
- 12 bitter almonds
- 1 pint of milk
- sugar to taste
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound the almonds in a mortar; mix them with the manna kroup; pour over these a pint of boiling milk, and let them steep for about 1/4 hour.
2. When nearly cold, add sugar and the well-beaten eggs; mix all well together; put the pudding into a buttered dish, and bake for 1/2 hour.
