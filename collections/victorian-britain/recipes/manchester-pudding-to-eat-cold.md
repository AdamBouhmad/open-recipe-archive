## Ingredients

- 3 oz. of grated bread
- 1/2 pint of milk
- a strip of lemon-peel
- 4 eggs
- 2 oz. of butter
- sugar to taste
- puff-paste
- jam
- 3 tablespoonfuls of brandy

## Directions

1. Flavour the milk with lemon-peel, by infusing it in the milk for 1/2 hour; then strain it on to the bread crumbs, and boil it for 2 or 3 minutes; add the eggs, leaving out the whites of 2, the butter, sugar, and brandy; stir all these ingredients well together.
2. Cover a pie-dish with puff-paste, and at the bottom put a thick layer of any kind of jam; pour the above mixture, cold, on the jam, and bake the pudding for an hour.
3. Serve cold, with a little sifted sugar sprinkled over.
