## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of stock No. 105
- 1/2 pint of Madeira or sherry
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 bay-leaf
- thickening of butter and flour

## Directions

1. Clean and crimp the tench
2. Carefully lay it in a stewpan with the stock, wine, salt and pepper, and bay-leaf
3. Let it stew gently for 1/2 hour
4. Take it out, put it on a dish, and keep hot
5. Strain the liquor, and thicken it with butter and flour kneaded together, and stew for 5 minutes
6. If not perfectly smooth, squeeze it through a tammy
7. Add a very little cayenne, and pour over the fish
8. Garnish with balls of veal forcemeat
