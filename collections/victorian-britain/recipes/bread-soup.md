## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of bread crusts
- 2 oz. butter
- 1 quart of common stock
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil the bread crusts in the stock with the butter
2. Beat the whole with a spoon
3. Keep it boiling till the bread and stock are well mixed
4. Season with a little salt
