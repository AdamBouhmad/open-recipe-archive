## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast loin of pork
- 1 oz. of butter
- 2 onions
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- 1/2 pint of gravy
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1 teaspoonful of vinegar and mustard

## Directions

1. Cut the pork into nice-sized cutlets, trim off most of the fat, and chop the onions.
2. Put the butter into a stewpan, lay in the cutlets and chopped onions, and fry a light brown.
3. Add the remaining ingredients, simmer gently for 5 or 7 minutes, and serve.
