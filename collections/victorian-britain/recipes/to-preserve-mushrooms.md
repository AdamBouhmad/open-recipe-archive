## Ingredients

- 3 oz. of butter per quart of mushrooms
- pepper and salt to taste
- juice of 1 lemon
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Peel the mushrooms, put them into cold water, with a little lemon-juice
2. Take them out and dry them very carefully in a cloth
3. Put the butter into a stewpan capable of holding the mushrooms
4. When it is melted, add the mushrooms, lemon-juice, and a seasoning of pepper and salt
5. Draw them down over a slow fire, and let them remain until their liquor is boiled away, and they have become quite dry, but be careful in not allowing them to stick to the bottom of the stewpan
6. When done, put them into pots, and pour over the top clarified butter
7. To re-warm them, put the mushrooms into a stewpan, strain the butter from them, and they will be ready for use
