## Ingredients

- Loin of pork
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets from a delicate loin of pork, bone and trim them neatly, and cut away the greater portion of the fat.
2. Season them with pepper.
3. Place the gridiron on the fire; when quite hot, lay on the chops and broil them for about 1/4 hour, turning them 3 or 4 times.
4. Be particular that they are thoroughly done, but not dry.
5. Dish them, sprinkle over a little fine salt, and serve plain, or with tomato sauce, sauce piquante, or pickled gherkins, a few of which should be laid round the dish as a garnish.
