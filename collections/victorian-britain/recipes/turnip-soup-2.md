## Ingredients

- 3 oz. of butter
- 9 good-sized turnips
- 4 onions
- 2 quarts of stock No. 106
- seasoning to taste

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in the stewpan, but do not let it boil
2. Wash, drain, and slice the turnips and onions very thin
3. Put them in the butter, with a teacupful of stock
4. Stew very gently for an hour
5. Add the remainder of the stock
6. Simmer another hour
7. Rub it through a tammy
8. Put it back into the stewpan, but do not let it boil
9. Serve very hot
