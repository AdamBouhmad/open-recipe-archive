## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 lb. of leg, loin, or neck of mutton
- 2 onions
- 2 lettuces
- 1 pint of green peas
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1/4 pint of water
- 1/4 lb. of clarified butter
- a little cayenne (when liked)

## Directions

1. Mince the above quantity of undressed leg, loin, or neck of mutton, adding a little of the fat, also minced
2. Put it into a stewpan with the remaining ingredients
3. Shred the lettuce and onion rather fine
4. Closely cover the stewpan after the ingredients have been well stirred
5. Simmer gently for rather more than 2 hours
6. Serve in a dish with a border of rice round, the same as for curry
