## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1/4 lb. of pounded sugar
- a wineglassful of brandy or rum

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream, until no lumps remain
2. add the pounded sugar, and brandy or rum
3. stir once or twice until the whole is thoroughly mixed, and serve
