## Ingredients

- 1/4 oz. of celery-seed
- 1 pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Crush the seed by pounding it in a mortar.
2. Boil the vinegar, and when cold, pour it to the seed.
3. Let it infuse for a fortnight, when strain and bottle off for use.
