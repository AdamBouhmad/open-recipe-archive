## Ingredients

- Haunch of mutton
- salt
- flour
- boiling water
- red-currant jelly

## Directions

1. Let this joint hang as long as possible without becoming tainted, and while hanging dust flour over it
2. Wash the outside well, lest it should have a bad flavour from keeping
3. Flour it and put it down to a nice brisk fire, at some distance, so that it may gradually warm through
4. Keep continually basting
5. About 1/2 hour before it is served, draw it nearer to the fire to get nicely brown
6. Sprinkle a little fine salt over the meat, pour off the dripping, add a little boiling water slightly salted, and strain this over the joint
7. Place a paper ruche on the bone, and send red-currant jelly and gravy in a tureen to table with it
