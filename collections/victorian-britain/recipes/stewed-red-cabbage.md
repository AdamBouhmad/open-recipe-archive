## Ingredients

- 1 red cabbage
- a small slice of ham
- 1/2 oz. of fresh butter
- 1 pint of weak stock or broth
- 1 gill of vinegar
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the cabbage into very thin slices
2. put it into a stewpan, with the ham cut in dice, the butter, 1/2 pint of stock, and the vinegar
3. cover the pan closely
4. let it stew for 1 hour
5. When it is very tender, add the remainder of the stock, a seasoning of salt and pepper, and the pounded sugar
6. mix all well together
7. stir over the fire until nearly all the liquor is dried away
8. serve
