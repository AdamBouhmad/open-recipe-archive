## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 2 oz. of butter
- 2 oz. of pounded sugar
- 2 oz. of sweet almonds
- 4 bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound the almonds in a mortar to a smooth paste
2. Melt the butter, dredge in the flour, and add the sugar and pounded almonds
3. Beat the mixture well, and put it into cups or very tiny jelly-pots, which should be well buttered
4. Bake in a moderate oven for about 20 minutes, or longer should the puffs be large
5. Turn them out on a dish, the bottom of the puff upper-most, and serve
