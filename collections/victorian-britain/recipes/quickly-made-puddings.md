## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of sifted sugar
- 1/4 lb. of flour
- 1 pint of milk
- 5 eggs
- a little grated lemon-rind

## Directions

1. Make the milk hot
2. Stir in the butter, and let it cool before the other ingredients are added to it
3. Stir in the sugar, flour, and eggs, which should be well whisked, and omit the whites of 2
4. Flavour with a little grated lemon-rind
5. Beat the mixture well
6. Butter some small cups, rather more than half fill them
7. Bake from 20 minutes to 1/2 hour, according to the size of the puddings
8. Serve with fruit, custard, or wine sauce, a little of which may be poured over them
