## Ingredients

- The remains of a cold calf's head
- rather more than 1/2 pint of Maitre d'hôtel sauce

## Directions

1. Make the sauce by recipe No. 466, and have it sufficiently thick that it may nicely cover the meat; remove the bones from the head, and cut the meat into neat slices.
2. When the sauce is ready, lay in the meat; let it gradually warm through, and, after it boils up, let it simmer very gently for 5 minutes, and serve.
