## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 3/4 pint of Béchamel sauce No. 368
- dessertspoonful of finely-minced parsley
- leaves of puff-paste or fried croûtons

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs hard
2. Put them into cold water and peel them
3. Take out the yolks whole and shred the whites
4. Make 3/4 pint of Béchamel sauce by recipe No. 368
5. Add the parsley to the sauce
6. When the sauce is quite hot, put the yolks of the eggs into the middle of the dish and the shred whites round them
7. Pour over the sauce
8. Garnish with leaves of puff-paste or fried croûtons
