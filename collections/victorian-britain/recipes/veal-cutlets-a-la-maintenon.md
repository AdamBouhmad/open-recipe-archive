## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 lbs. of veal cutlets
- egg and bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of minced savoury herbs
- salt and pepper to taste
- a little grated nutmeg
- buttered paper
- melted butter or a good gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the cutlets about 3/4 inch in thickness
2. Flatten them
3. Brush them over with the yolk of an egg
4. Dip them into bread crumbs and minced herbs
5. Season with pepper and salt and grated nutmeg
6. Fold each cutlet in a piece of buttered paper
7. Broil them
8. Send them to table with melted butter or a good gravy
