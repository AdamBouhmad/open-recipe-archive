## Ingredients

- Puff-paste No. 1205 or 1206
- apples
- 2 oz. of moist sugar per lb. of unpared apples
- 1/2 teaspoonful of finely-minced lemon-peel
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- 2 or 3 tablespoonfuls of water

## Directions

1. Make 1/2 lb. of puff-paste by either of the above-named recipes
2. Place a border of it round the edge of a pie-dish
3. Fill it with apples pared, cored, and cut into slices
4. Sweeten with moist sugar
5. Add the lemon-peel and juice, and 2 or 3 tablespoonfuls of water
6. Cover with crust
7. Cut it evenly round close to the edge of the pie-dish
8. Bake in a hot oven from 1/2 to 3/4 hour, or rather longer, should the pie be very large
9. When it is three-parts done, take it out of the oven
10. Put the white of an egg on a plate, and with the blade of a knife, whisk it to a froth
11. Brush the pie over with this
12. Sprinkle upon it some sifted sugar, and then a few drops of water
13. Put the pie back into the oven, and finish baking
14. Be particularly careful that it does not catch or burn
