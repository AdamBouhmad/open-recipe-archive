## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of mashed potatoes
- 1/4 pint of good broth or stock
- 2 oz. of butter

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes, well drain them, and pound them smoothly in a mortar, or beat them up with a fork
2. Add the stock or broth, and rub the potatoes through a sieve
3. Put the puree into a very clean saucepan with the butter
4. Stir it well over the fire until thoroughly hot, and it will then be ready to serve
