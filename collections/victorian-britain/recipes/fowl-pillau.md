## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of rice
- 2 oz. of butter
- a fowl
- 2 quarts of stock or good broth
- 40 cardamum-seeds
- 1/2 oz. of coriander-seed
- 1/4 oz. of cloves
- 1/4 oz. of allspice
- 1/4 oz. of mace
- 1/4 oz. of cinnamon
- 1/2 oz. of peppercorns
- 4 onions
- 6 thin slices of bacon
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- flour

## Directions

1. Well wash 1 lb. of the best Patna rice, put it into a frying-pan with the butter, which keep moving over a slow fire until the rice is lightly browned
2. Truss the fowl as for boiling, put it into a stewpan with the stock or broth
3. Pound the spices and seeds thoroughly in a mortar, tie them in a piece of muslin, and put them in with the fowl
4. Let it boil slowly until it is nearly done; then add the rice, which should stew until quite tender and almost dry
5. Cut the onions into slices, sprinkle them with flour, and fry, without breaking them, of a nice brown colour
6. Have ready the slices of bacon curled and grilled, and the eggs boiled hard
7. Lay the fowl in the form of a pyramid upon a dish, smother with the rice, garnish with the bacon, fried onions, and the hard-boiled eggs cut into quarters, and serve very hot
8. Before taking the rice out, remove the spices
