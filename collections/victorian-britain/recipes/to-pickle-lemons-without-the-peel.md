## Ingredients

- 6 lemons
- 1 lb. of fine salt
- vinegar
- 1 oz. of cloves per quart of vinegar
- 1/2 oz. of white pepper per quart of vinegar
- 1 oz. of bruised ginger per quart of vinegar
- 1/4 oz. of mace and chilies per quart of vinegar
- 1 oz. of mustard-seed per quart of vinegar
- 1/2 stick of sliced horseradish per quart of vinegar
- a few cloves of garlic per quart of vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel the lemons, slit each one down 3 times, so as not to divide them
2. Rub the salt well into the divisions
3. Place them in a pan, where they must remain for a week, turning them every other day
4. Put them in a Dutch oven before a clear fire until the salt has become perfectly dry
5. Arrange them in a jar
6. Pour over sufficient boiling vinegar to cover them, to which have been added the ingredients mentioned in the foregoing recipe
7. Tie down closely, and in about 9 months they will be fit for use
