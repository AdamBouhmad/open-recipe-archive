## Ingredients

- 3 sweetbreads
- Egg and bread crumbs
- Oiled butter
- 3 slices of toast
- Brown gravy

## Directions

1. Choose large white sweetbreads
2. Put them into warm water to draw out the blood and to improve their colour
3. Let them remain for rather more than 1 hour
4. Put them into boiling water and allow them to simmer for about 10 minutes
5. Take them up and drain them
6. Brush over with egg
7. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
8. Dip them in egg again and then into more bread crumbs
9. Drop on them a little oiled butter
10. Put the sweetbreads into a moderately-heated oven and let them bake for nearly 3/4 hour
11. Make 3 pieces of toast
12. Place the sweetbreads on the toast
13. Pour round, but not over them, a good brown gravy
