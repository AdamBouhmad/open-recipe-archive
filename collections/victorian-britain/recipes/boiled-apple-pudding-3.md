## Ingredients

- Crust No. 1215
- apples
- sugar to taste
- 1 small teaspoonful of finely-minced lemon-peel
- 2 tablespoonfuls of lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Make a butter-crust by recipe No. 1213, or a suet one by recipe No. 1215, using for a moderate-sized pudding from 3/4 to 1 lb. of flour, with the other ingredients in proportion
2. Butter a basin
3. Line it with some of the paste
4. Pare, core, and cut the apples into slices
5. Fill the basin with these apples
6. Add the sugar, the lemon-peel and juice
7. Cover with crust
8. Pinch the edges together
9. Flour the cloth, place it over the pudding, and tie it securely
10. Put it into plenty of fast-boiling water
11. Let it boil from 1-1/2 to 2-1/2 hours, according to the size
12. Turn it out of the basin and send to table quickly
