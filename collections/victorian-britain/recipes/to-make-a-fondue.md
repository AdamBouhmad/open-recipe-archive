## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- cheese (the weight of 2 eggs in Parmesan or good Cheshire cheese)
- butter (the weight of 2 eggs)
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Separate the yolks from the whites of the eggs
2. Beat the yolks in a basin
3. Grate the cheese, or cut it into very thin flakes
4. Break the butter into small pieces
5. Add the butter to the other ingredients, with sufficient pepper and salt to season nicely
6. Beat the mixture thoroughly
7. Well whisk the whites of the eggs
8. Stir them lightly in
9. Either bake the fondue in a soufflé-dish or small round cake-tin
10. Fill the dish only half full, as the fondue should rise very much
11. Pin a napkin round the tin or dish
12. Serve very hot and very quickly
