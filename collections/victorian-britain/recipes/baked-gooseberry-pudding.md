## Ingredients

- Gooseberries
- 3 eggs
- 1-1/2 oz. of butter
- 1/2 pint of bread crumbs
- sugar to taste
- puff-paste

## Directions

1. Put the gooseberries into a jar, previously cutting off the tops and tails
2. Place this jar in boiling water, and let it boil until the gooseberries are soft enough to pulp
3. Beat them through a coarse sieve
4. To every pint of pulp add 3 well-whisked eggs, 1-1/2 oz. of butter, 1/2 pint of bread crumbs, and sugar to taste
5. Beat the mixture well
6. Put a border of puff-paste round the edge of a pie-dish
7. Put in the pudding
8. Bake for about 40 minutes
9. Strew sifted sugar over, and serve
