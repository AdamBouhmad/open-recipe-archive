## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 small carrot
- 6 shalots
- 1 small bunch of savoury herbs, including parsley
- 1/2 a bay-leaf
- 2 slices of lean ham
- 2 cloves
- 6 peppercorns
- 1 blade of mace
- 3 whole allspice
- 4 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 1/2 pint of stock
- 1 small lump of sugar
- 1/4 saltspoonful of cayenne
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put into a stewpan the butter, with the carrot and shalots, both of which must be cut into small slices
2. add the herbs, bay-leaf, spices, and ham (which must be minced rather finely), and let these ingredients simmer over a slow fire, until the bottom of the stewpan is covered with a brown glaze
3. Keep stirring with a wooden spoon, and put in the remaining ingredients
4. Simmer very gently for 1/4 hour
5. skim off every particle of fat
6. strain the sauce through a sieve, and serve very hot
