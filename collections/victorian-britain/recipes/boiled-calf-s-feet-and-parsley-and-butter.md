## Ingredients

- 2 calf's feet
- 2 slices of bacon
- 2 oz. of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of lemon-juice
- salt and whole pepper to taste
- 1 onion
- a bunch of savoury herbs
- 4 cloves
- 1 blade of mace
- water
- parsley and butter No. 493

## Directions

1. Procure 2 white calf's feet; bone them as far as the first joint, and put them into warm water to soak for 2 hours.
2. Put the bacon, butter, lemon-juice, onion, herbs, spices, and seasoning into a stewpan; lay in the feet, and pour in just sufficient water to cover the whole.
3. Stew gently for about 3 hours.
4. Take out the feet, dish them, and cover with parsley and butter, made by recipe No. 493.
5. Strain the liquor they were boiled in and put by in a clean basin for use.
