## Ingredients

- 1 new-laid egg
- 1 large breakfast-cupful of tea or coffee

## Directions

1. Beat up the whole of the egg in a basin
2. Put it into a cup (or a portion of it, if the cup be small)
3. Pour over it the tea or coffee very hot, added very gradually
4. Stir all the time to prevent the egg from curdling
