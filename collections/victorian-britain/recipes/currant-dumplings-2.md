## Ingredients

- suet
- flour
- currants
- water or milk
- lemon
- cold butter
- sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Chop the suet finely, mix it with the flour, and add the currants, which should be nicely washed, picked, and dried
2. Mix the whole to a limp paste with the water (if wanted very nice, use milk)
3. Divide it into 7 or 8 dumplings; tie them in cloths, and boil for 1-1/4 hour
4. Alternatively, they may be boiled without a cloth: make them into round balls, and drop into boiling water, and move about at first, to prevent them from sticking to the bottom of the saucepan
5. Serve with a cut lemon, cold butter, and sifted sugar
