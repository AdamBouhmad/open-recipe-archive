## Ingredients

- Widgeons
- a little flour
- butter
- brown gravy or orange gravy
- a cut lemon

## Directions

1. Truss in the same manner as wild duck, No. 1022, but must not be kept so long before they are dressed.
2. Put them down to a brisk fire; flour, and baste them continually with butter.
3. When browned and nicely frothed, send them to table hot and quickly.
4. Serve with brown gravy, or orange gravy, No. 488, and a cut lemon.
