## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of eels
- 1 pint of rich strong stock
- 1 onion
- 3 cloves
- a piece of lemon-peel
- 1 glass of port or Madeira
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cream
- thickening of flour
- cayenne and lemon-juice to taste

## Directions

1. Wash and skin the eels, and cut them into pieces about 3 inches long; pepper and salt them, and lay them in a stewpan.
2. Pour over the stock, add the onion stuck with cloves, the lemon-peel, and the wine.
3. Stew gently for 1/2 hour, or rather more, and lift them carefully on a dish, which keep hot.
4. Strain the gravy, stir to the cream sufficient flour to thicken; mix altogether, boil for 2 minutes, and add the seasoning.
