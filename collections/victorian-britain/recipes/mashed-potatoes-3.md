## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- 1 oz. of butter to every lb. of mashed potatoes
- 2 tablespoonfuls of milk
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes in their skins
2. When done, drain them, and let them get thoroughly dry by the side of the fire
3. Peel them, and, as they are peeled, put them into a clean saucepan
4. With a large fork beat them to a light paste
5. Add butter, milk, and salt in the above proportion, and stir all the ingredients well over the fire
6. When thoroughly hot, dish them lightly, and draw the fork backwards over the potatoes to make the surface rough, and serve
