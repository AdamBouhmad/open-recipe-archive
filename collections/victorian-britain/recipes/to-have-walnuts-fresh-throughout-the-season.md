## Ingredients

- walnuts
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. To every pint of water allow 1 teaspoonful of salt.
2. Place the walnuts in the salt and water for 24 hours at least.
3. Take them out, and rub them dry.
4. Old nuts may be freshened in this manner; or walnuts, when first picked, may be put into an earthen pan with salt sprinkled amongst them, and with damped hay placed on the top of them, and then covered down with a lid.
5. They must be well wiped before they are put on table.
