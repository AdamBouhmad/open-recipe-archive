## Ingredients

- 4 large apples
- a little grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful of minced lemon-peel
- 2 large tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 6 oz. of currants
- 3/4 lb. of suet crust

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and cut the apples into slices
2. Put them into a saucepan, with the nutmeg, lemon-peel, and sugar
3. Stir them over the fire until soft
4. Have ready the above proportion of crust, roll it out thin
5. Spread the apples over the paste
6. Sprinkle over the currants
7. Roll the pudding up, closing the ends properly
8. Tie it in a floured cloth
9. Boil for 2 hours
