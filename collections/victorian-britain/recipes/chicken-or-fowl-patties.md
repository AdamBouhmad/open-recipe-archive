## Ingredients

- remains of cold roast chicken or fowl
- 2 oz. of ham per 1/4 lb. of meat
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of veal gravy
- 1/2 teaspoonful of minced lemon-peel
- cayenne, salt, and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- 1 oz. of butter rolled in flour
- puff paste
- yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Mince very small the white meat from a cold roast fowl, after removing all the skin
2. Weigh it, and to every 1/4 lb. of meat allow the proportion of minced ham
3. Put these into a stewpan with the remaining ingredients
4. Stir over the fire for 10 minutes or 1/4 hour, taking care that the mixture does not burn
5. Roll out some puff paste about 1/4 inch in thickness
6. Line the patty-pans with this
7. Put upon each a small piece of bread, and cover with another layer of paste
8. Brush over with the yolk of an egg
9. Bake in a brisk oven for about 1/4 hour
10. When done, cut a round piece out of the top, and with a small spoon, take out the bread
11. Be particular in not breaking the outside border of the crust
12. Fill the patties with the mixture
