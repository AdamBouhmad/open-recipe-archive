## Ingredients

- Mashed potatoes
- salt and pepper to taste
- a very little minced parsley (when liked)
- egg
- bread crumbs
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Boil and mash the potatoes by recipe No. 1145
2. Add a seasoning of pepper and salt, and, when liked, a little minced parsley
3. Roll the potatoes into small balls
4. Cover them with egg and bread crumbs
5. Fry in hot lard for about 10 minutes
6. Let them drain before the fire
7. Dish them on a napkin
8. Serve
