## Ingredients

- Grouse
- cayenne, salt, and pepper to taste
- 1 lb. of rump-steak
- 1/2 pint of well-seasoned broth
- puff paste
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Line the bottom of a pie-dish with the rump-steak cut into neat pieces.
2. If the grouse are large, cut them into joints; if small, they may be laid in the pie whole.
3. Season highly with salt, cayenne, and black pepper.
4. Pour in the broth and cover with puff paste.
5. Brush the crust over with the yolk of an egg.
6. Bake from 3/4 to 1 hour.
7. If the grouse are cut into joints, stew the backbones and trimmings with an onion, a little sherry, a bunch of herbs, and a blade of mace to make gravy, and pour this in after the pie is baked.
