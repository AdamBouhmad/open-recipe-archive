## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of loaf sugar
- 2 pints of water
- 1 oz. of citric acid
- 12 drachm of essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water together for 1/4 hour, and put it into a basin, where let it remain till cold
2. Beat the citric acid to a powder, mix the essence of lemon with it
3. Add these two ingredients to the syrup
4. Mix well, and bottle for use
5. Two tablespoonfuls of the syrup are sufficient for a tumbler of cold water
