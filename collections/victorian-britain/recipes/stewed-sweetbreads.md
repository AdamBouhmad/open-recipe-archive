## Ingredients

- 3 sweetbreads
- 1 pint of white stock No. 107
- Thickening of butter and flour
- 6 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- White pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbreads in warm water for 1 hour
2. Boil them for 10 minutes
3. Take them out and put them into cold water for a few minutes
4. Lay them in a stewpan with the stock
5. Simmer them gently for rather more than 1/2 hour
6. Dish them
7. Thicken the gravy with a little butter and flour
8. Let it boil up
9. Add the remaining ingredients
10. Allow the sauce to get quite hot, but not boil
11. Pour it over the sweetbreads
