## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of raisins
- 3 lbs. of currants
- 1-1/2 lb. of lean beef
- 3 lbs. of beef suet
- 2 lbs. of moist sugar
- 2 oz. of citron
- 2 oz. of candied lemon-peel
- 2 oz. of candied orange-peel
- 1 small nutmeg
- 1 pottle of apples
- the rind of 2 lemons
- the juice of 1
- 1/2 pint of brandy

## Directions

1. Stone and cut the raisins once or twice across, but do not chop them
2. wash, dry, and pick the currants free from stalks and grit, and mince the beef and suet, taking care that the latter is chopped very fine
3. slice the citron and candied peel
4. grate the nutmeg, and pare, core, and mince the apples
5. mince the lemon-peel, strain the juice
6. when all the ingredients are thus prepared, mix them well together, adding the brandy when the other things are well blended
7. press the whole into a jar, carefully exclude the air, and the mincemeat will be ready for use in a fortnight
