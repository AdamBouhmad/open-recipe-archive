## Ingredients

- calf's udder
- water

## Directions

1. Put the udder into a stewpan with sufficient water to cover it
2. Let it stew gently till quite done
3. Take it out to cool
4. Trim all the upper parts
5. Cut it into small pieces
6. Pound well in a mortar, till it can be rubbed through a sieve
