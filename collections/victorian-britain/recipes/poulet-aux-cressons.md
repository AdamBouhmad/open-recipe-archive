## Ingredients

- A fowl
- a large bunch of water-cresses
- 3 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- 1/4 pint of gravy
- salt

## Directions

1. Truss and roast a fowl by recipe No. 952, taking care that it is nicely frothed and brown.
2. Wash and dry the water-cresses, pick them nicely, and arrange them in a flat layer on a dish.
3. Sprinkle over a little salt and the above proportion of vinegar.
4. Place the fowl over these, and pour over it the gravy.
5. Serve with a little gravy in a tureen.
6. When not liked, the vinegar may be omitted.
