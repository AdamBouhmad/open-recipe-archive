## Ingredients

- 6 good-sized apples
- sifted sugar to taste
- a piece of butter the size of a walnut
- water

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and quarter the apples, and throw them into cold water to preserve their whiteness.
2. Put them in a saucepan, with sufficient water to moisten them, and boil till soft enough to pulp.
3. Beat them up, adding sugar to taste, and a small piece of butter.
