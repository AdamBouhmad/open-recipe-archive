## Ingredients

- sugar and butter, the weight of 3 eggs
- flour, the weight of 2 eggs
- rind of 1 small lemon
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Melt the butter to a liquid state, but do not allow it to oil
2. Stir to this the sugar and finely-minced lemon-peel
3. Gradually dredge in the flour, keeping the mixture well stirred
4. Whisk the eggs; add these to the pudding
5. Beat all the ingredients until thoroughly blended
6. Put them into a buttered mould or basin
7. Boil for 2 hours, and serve with sweet sauce
