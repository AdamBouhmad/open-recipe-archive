## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of oysters
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 pint of milk
- sufficient flour to make the batter
- pepper and salt to taste
- a little nutmeg
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Scald the oysters in their own liquor, beard them, and lay them on a cloth, to drain thoroughly.
2. Break the eggs into a basin, mix the flour with them, add the milk gradually, with nutmeg and seasoning.
3. Put the oysters in the batter.
4. Make some lard hot in a deep frying-pan, put in the oysters, one at a time.
5. When done, take them up with a sharp-pointed skewer, and dish them on a napkin.
