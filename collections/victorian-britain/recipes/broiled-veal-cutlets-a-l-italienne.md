## Ingredients

- Neck of veal
- salt and pepper to taste
- the yolk of 1 egg
- bread crumbs
- 1/2 pint of Italian sauce No. 453
- clarified butter
- rashers of broiled ham

## Directions

1. Cut the veal into cutlets, flatten and trim them nicely
2. Powder over them a little salt and pepper
3. Brush them over with the yolk of an egg
4. Dip them into bread crumbs, then into clarified butter, and afterwards in the bread crumbs again
5. Broil or fry them over a clear fire, that they may acquire a good brown colour
6. Arrange them in the dish alternately with rashers of broiled ham
7. Pour the sauce, made by recipe No. 453, in the middle
