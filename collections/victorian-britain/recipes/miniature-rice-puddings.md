## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of rice
- 1-1/2 pint of milk
- 2 oz. of fresh butter
- 4 eggs
- sugar to taste
- flavouring of lemon-peel, bitter almonds, or vanilla
- a few strips of candied peel

## Directions

1. Let the rice swell in 1 pint of the milk over a slow fire, putting with it a strip of lemon-peel
2. Stir to it the butter and the other 1/2 pint of milk, and let the mixture cool
3. Add the well-beaten eggs, and a few drops of essence of almonds or essence of vanilla, whichever may be preferred
4. Butter well some small cups or moulds
5. Line them with a few pieces of candied peel sliced very thin
6. Fill them three parts full, and bake for about 40 minutes
7. Turn them out of the cups on to a white d'oyley, and serve with sweet sauce
