## Ingredients

- Grouse
- butter
- a thick slice of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Let the birds hang as long as possible.
2. Pluck and draw them.
3. Wipe, but do not wash them, inside and out.
4. Truss them without the head, the same as for a roast fowl.
5. Put them down to a sharp clear fire.
6. Keep them well basted the whole of the time they are cooking.
7. Serve them on a buttered toast, soaked in the dripping-pan, with a little melted butter poured over them, or with bread-sauce and gravy.
