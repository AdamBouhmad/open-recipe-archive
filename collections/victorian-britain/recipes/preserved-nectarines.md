## Ingredients

- sugar
- water
- nectarines

## Directions

1. Divide the nectarines in two, take out the stones, and make a strong syrup with sugar and water
2. Put in the nectarines, and boil them until they have thoroughly imbibed the sugar
3. Keep the fruit as whole as possible, and turn it carefully into a pan
4. The next day boil it again for a few minutes, take out the nectarines, put them into jars
5. Boil the syrup quickly for 5 minutes, pour it over the fruit, and, when cold, cover the preserve down
