## Ingredients

- 1 lb flour
- 5 or 6 oz beef suet
- 1/2 pint water

## Directions

1. Free the suet from skin and shreds
2. Chop it extremely fine
3. Rub it well into the flour
4. Work the whole to a smooth paste with the above proportion of water
5. Roll it out, and it is ready for use
