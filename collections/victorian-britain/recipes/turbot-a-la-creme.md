## Ingredients

- Remains of cold turbot
- 2 oz. of butter
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- Salt
- Cayenne
- Pounded mace

## Directions

1. Clear away all skin and bone from the flesh of the turbot
2. Cut the flesh into nice square pieces, as equally as possible
3. Put into a stewpan the butter, let it melt, and add the cream and seasoning
4. Let it just simmer for one minute, but not boil
5. Lay in the fish to warm, and serve it garnished with croutons or a paste border
