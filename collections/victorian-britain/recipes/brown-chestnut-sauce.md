## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of chestnuts
- 1/2 pint of stock
- 2 lumps of sugar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the chestnuts by scalding and peeling them.
2. Put them in a stewpan with the stock and sugar, and simmer them till tender.
3. When done, add Spanish sauce in the above proportion, and rub the whole through a tammy.
4. Keep this sauce rather liquid, as it is liable to thicken.
