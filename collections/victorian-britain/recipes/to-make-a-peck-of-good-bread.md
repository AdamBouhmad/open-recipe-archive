## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of potatoes
- 6 pints of cold water
- 1/2 pint of good yeast
- a peck of flour
- 2 oz. of salt

## Directions

1. Peel and boil the potatoes
2. Beat them to a cream while warm
3. Add 1 pint of cold water, strain through a colander, and add to it 1/2 pint of good yeast, which should have been put in water over-night, to take off its bitterness
4. Stir all well together with a wooden spoon, and pour the mixture into the centre of the flour
5. Mix it to the substance of cream, cover it over closely, and let it remain near the fire for an hour
6. Add the 5 pints of water, milk-warm, with 2 oz. of salt; pour this in, and mix the whole to a nice light dough
7. Let it remain for about 2 hours
8. Make it into 7 loaves, and bake for about 1-1/2 hour in a good oven
