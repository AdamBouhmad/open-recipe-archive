## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of oil
- 1/2 a glass of white wine
- sufficient flour to thicken
- 12 anchovies

## Directions

1. Mix the oil and wine together, with sufficient flour to make them into a thickish paste
2. Cleanse the anchovies, wipe them, dip them in the paste, and fry of a nice brown colour
