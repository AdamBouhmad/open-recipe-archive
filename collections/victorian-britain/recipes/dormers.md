## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of cold mutton
- 2 oz. of beef suet
- pepper and salt to taste
- 3 oz. of boiled rice
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs
- made gravy

## Directions

1. Chop the meat, suet, and rice finely
2. Mix well together
3. Add a high seasoning of pepper and salt
4. Roll into sausages
5. Cover them with egg and bread crumbs
6. Fry in hot dripping of a nice brown
7. Serve in a dish with made gravy poured round them and a little in a tureen
