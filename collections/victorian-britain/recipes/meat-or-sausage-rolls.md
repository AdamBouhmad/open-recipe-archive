## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of puff-paste
- sausage-meat
- the yolk of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Make 1 lb. of puff-paste and roll it out to the thickness of about 1/2 inch, or rather less
2. Divide it into 8, 10, or 12 squares, according to the size the rolls are intended to be
3. Place some sausage-meat on one-half of each square
4. Wet the edges of the paste, and fold it over the meat
5. Slightly press the edges together, and trim them neatly with a knife
6. Brush the rolls over with the yolk of an egg
7. Bake them in a well-heated oven for about 1/2 hour, or longer should they be very large
