## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of fruit, weighed before being stripped from the stalks
- 3/4 lb. of loaf sugar per lb. of fruit
- 1 gill of water per lb. of fruit

## Directions

1. Let the fruit be very ripe, and gathered on a dry day
2. Strip it from the stalks, and put it into a preserving-pan, with a gill of water to each lb. of fruit
3. Boil these together for 10 minutes
4. Add the sugar, and boil the jam again for 30 minutes, reckoning from the time when the jam simmers equally all over, or longer, should it not appear to set nicely when a little is poured on to a plate
5. Keep stirring it to prevent it from burning
6. Carefully remove all the scum
7. When done, pour it into pots
8. Let it cool, cover the top of the jam with oiled paper, and the top of the jars with a piece of tissue-paper brushed over on both sides with the white of an egg
