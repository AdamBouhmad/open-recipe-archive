## Ingredients

- 6 oz. of butter
- 9 oz. of flour

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a stewpan over a slow fire
2. Dredge in very gradually the flour
3. Stir it till of a light-brown colour, doing it very slowly so the flour will not burn and impart a bitter taste to the sauce it is mixed with
4. Pour it in a jar and keep it for use
