## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- the rind of 1/2 large lemon
- sugar to taste
- 5 heaped tablespoonfuls of rice-flour
- 1 oz. of sweet almonds
- 1/2 pint of custard

## Directions

1. Boil the milk and lemon-rind together until the former is well flavoured
2. Take out the lemon-rind and stir in the rice-flour, which should first be moistened with cold milk, and add sufficient loaf sugar to sweeten it nicely
3. Boil gently for about 5 minutes, and keep the mixture stirred
4. Take it off the fire, let it cool a little, and pour it into a glass dish
5. When cold, cut the rice out in the form of a star, or any other shape that may be preferred
6. Take out the spare rice, and fill the space with boiled custard
7. Blanch and cut the almonds into strips
8. Stick them over the trifle, and garnish it with pieces of brightly-coloured jelly, or preserved fruits, or candied citron
