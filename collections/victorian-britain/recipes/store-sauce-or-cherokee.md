## Ingredients

- 1/2 oz. of cayenne pepper
- 5 cloves of garlic
- 2 tablespoonfuls of soy
- 1 tablespoonful of walnut ketchup
- 1 pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil all the ingredients gently for about 1/2 hour
2. Strain the liquor and bottle off for use
