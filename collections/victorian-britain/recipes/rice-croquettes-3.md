## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of rice
- 1 quart of milk
- 6 oz. of pounded sugar
- flavouring of vanilla, lemon-peel, or bitter almonds
- egg
- bread crumbs
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Put the rice, milk, and sugar into a saucepan, and let the former gradually swell over a gentle fire until all the milk is dried up
2. Just before the rice is done, stir in a few drops of essence of any of the above flavourings
3. Let the rice get cold
4. Form it into small round balls
5. Dip them into yolk of egg
6. Sprinkle them with bread crumbs
7. Fry them in boiling lard for about 10 minutes, turning them about, that they may get equally browned
8. Drain the greasy moisture from them, by placing them on a cloth in front of the fire for a minute or two
9. Pile them on a white d'oyley, and send them quickly to table
10. A small piece of jam is sometimes introduced into the middle of each croquette
