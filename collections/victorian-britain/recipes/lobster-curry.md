## Ingredients

- 1 lobster
- 2 onions
- 1 oz. butter
- 1 tablespoonful of curry-powder
- 1/2 pint of medium stock
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- boiled rice

## Directions

1. Pick the meat from the shell, and cut it into nice square pieces
2. Fry the onions of a pale brown in the butter
3. Stir in the curry-powder and stock, and simmer till it thickens
4. Put in the lobster and stew the whole slowly for 1/2 hour, stirring occasionally
5. Just before sending to table, put in the lemon-juice
6. Serve boiled rice with it, the same as for other curries
