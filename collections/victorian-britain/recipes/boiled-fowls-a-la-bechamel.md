## Ingredients

- A pair of fowls
- 1 pint of Béchamel
- a few bunches of boiled brocoli or cauliflower

## Directions

1. Truss and boil the fowls by recipe No. 938
2. Make a pint of Béchamel sauce by recipe No. 367
3. Pour some of this over the fowls, and the remainder send to table in a tureen
4. Garnish the dish with bunches of boiled cauliflowers or brocoli, and serve very hot
5. The sauce should be made sufficiently thick to adhere to the fowls; that for the tureen should be thinned by adding a spoonful or two of stock
