## Ingredients

- crayfish
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Throw crayfish into boiling water, to which has been added a good seasoning of salt and a little vinegar.
2. When done, which will be in 1/4 hour, take them out and drain them.
3. Let them cool, arrange them on a napkin, and garnish with plenty of double parsley.
