## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of lean pork
- 3/4 lb. of fat bacon
- 1/4 oz. of salt
- 1 saltspoonful of pepper
- 1/4 teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful of minced parsley

## Directions

1. Remove from the pork all skin, gristle, and bone, and chop it finely with the bacon.
2. Add the remaining ingredients, and carefully mix altogether.
3. Pound it well in a mortar, make it into convenient-sized cakes, flour these, and fry them a nice brown for about 10 minutes.
