## Ingredients

- 1/2 quartern of dough
- boiling water
- milk

## Directions

1. Make a very light dough as for bread, using to mix it, milk, instead of water
2. Divide it into 7 or 8 dumplings
3. Plunge them into boiling water, and boil them for 20 minutes
4. Serve the instant they are taken up, as they spoil directly, by falling and becoming heavy
5. In eating them do not touch them with a knife, but tear them apart with two forks
6. They may be eaten with meat gravy, or cold butter and sugar
