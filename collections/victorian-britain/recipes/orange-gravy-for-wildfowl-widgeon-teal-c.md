## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of white stock
- 1 small onion
- 3 or 4 strips of lemon or orange peel
- a few leaves of basil
- juice of a Seville orange or lemon
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 glass of port wine

## Directions

1. Put the onion, cut in slices, into a stewpan with the stock, orange-peel, and basil, and let them simmer very gently for 1/4 hour or rather longer, should the gravy not taste sufficiently of the peel
2. Strain it off, and add to the gravy the remaining ingredients
3. Let the whole heat through, and, when on the point of boiling, serve very hot in a tureen which should have a cover to it
