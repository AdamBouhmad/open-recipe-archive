## Ingredients

- 4 apples, peeled and cored
- 4 onions
- 4 leaves of sage
- 4 leaves of lemon thyme
- sufficient water
- sufficient pulp of mealy potatoes
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Boil the apples, onions, sage, and lemon thyme in a stewpan with sufficient water to cover them.
2. When done, pulp them through a sieve, removing the sage and thyme.
3. Add sufficient pulp of mealy potatoes to cause it to be sufficiently dry without sticking to the hand.
4. Add pepper and salt, and stuff the bird.
