## Ingredients

- apples
- evergreen leaves

## Directions

1. The apples should be nicely wiped with a dry cloth, and arranged on a dish, piled high in the centre, with evergreen leaves between each layer.
2. The inferior apples should form the bottom layer, with the bright-coloured large ones at the top.
3. The leaves of the laurel, bay, holly, or any shrub green in winter, are suitable for garnishing dessert dishes.
