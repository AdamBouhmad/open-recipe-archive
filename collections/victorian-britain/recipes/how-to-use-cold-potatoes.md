## Ingredients

- Cold potatoes, 1 lb
- Flour, 2 tablespoonfuls per lb of potatoes
- Minced onions, 2 tablespoonfuls per lb of potatoes
- Butter, 1 oz per lb of potatoes
- Milk

## Directions

1. Mash the potatoes with a fork until perfectly free from lumps.
2. Stir in the flour, minced onions, and butter.
3. Add sufficient milk to moisten them well.
4. Press the potatoes into a mould, and bake in a moderate oven until nicely brown, which will be in from 20 minutes to 1/2 hour.
5. Turn them out of the mould, and serve.
