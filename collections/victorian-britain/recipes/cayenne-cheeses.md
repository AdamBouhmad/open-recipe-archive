## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of grated cheese
- 1/3 teaspoonful of cayenne
- 1/3 teaspoonful of salt
- water

## Directions

1. Rub the butter in the flour
2. Add the grated cheese, cayenne, and salt
3. Mix these ingredients well together
4. Moisten with sufficient water to make the whole into a paste
5. Roll out, and cut into fingers about 4 inches in length
6. Bake them in a moderate oven a very light colour
7. Serve very hot
