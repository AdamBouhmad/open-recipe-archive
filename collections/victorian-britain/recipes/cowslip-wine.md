## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of lump sugar per gallon of water
- rind of 2 lemons
- juice of 1 lemon
- rind of 1 Seville orange
- juice of 1 Seville orange
- 1 gallon of cowslip pips
- 1 bottle of brandy to every 4-1/2 gallons of wine
- 3 tablespoonfuls of good fresh brewers' yeast to every 9 gallons of wine

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water together for 1/2 hour
2. Carefully remove all the scum as it rises
3. Pour this boiling liquor on the orange and lemon-rinds and the strained juice
4. When milk-warm, add the cowslip pips or flowers, picked from the stalks and seeds
5. Add the yeast
6. Let it ferment 3 or 4 days
7. Put all together in a cask with the brandy
8. Let it remain for 2 months
9. Bottle it off for use
