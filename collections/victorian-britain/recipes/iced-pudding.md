## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of sweet almonds
- 2 oz. of bitter ones
- 3/4 lb. of sugar
- 8 eggs
- 1-1/2 pint of milk

## Directions

1. Blanch and dry the almonds thoroughly in a cloth
2. Pound them in a mortar until reduced to a smooth paste
3. Add to these the well-beaten eggs, the sugar, and milk
4. Stir these ingredients over the fire until they thicken, but do not allow them to boil
5. Strain and put the mixture into the freezing-pot
6. Surround it with ice
7. Freeze it as directed in recipe 1290
8. When quite frozen, fill an iced-pudding mould
9. Put on the lid
10. Keep the pudding in ice until required for table
11. Turn it out on the dish
12. Garnish it with a compôte of any fruit that may be preferred
13. Pour a little over the top of the pudding
14. This pudding may be flavoured with vanilla, Curaçoa, or Maraschino
