## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of treacle
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1/4 lb. of coarse brown sugar
- 1-1/2 lb. of flour
- 1 oz. of ginger
- 1/2 oz. of ground allspice
- 1 teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
- 1/4 pint of warm milk
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Put the flour into a basin, with the sugar, ginger, and allspice
2. Mix these together
3. Warm the butter, and add it, with the treacle, to the other ingredients
4. Stir well
5. Make the milk just warm
6. Dissolve the carbonate of soda in it
7. Mix the whole into a nice smooth dough with the eggs, which should be previously well whisked
8. Pour the mixture into a buttered tin
9. Bake it from 3/4 to 1 hour, or longer, should the gingerbread be very thick
10. Just before it is done, brush the top over with the yolk of an egg beaten up with a little milk
11. Put it back in the oven to finish baking
