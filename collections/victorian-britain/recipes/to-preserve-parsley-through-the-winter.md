## Ingredients

- freshly-gathered parsley
- boiling water, slightly salted

## Directions

1. Use freshly-gathered parsley for keeping, and wash it perfectly free from grit and dirt
2. Put it into boiling water which has been slightly salted and well skimmed, and then let it boil for 2 or 3 minutes
3. Take it out, let it drain, and lay it on a sieve in front of the fire, when it should be dried as expeditiously as possible
4. Store it away in a very dry place in bottles, and when wanted for use, pour over it a little warm water, and let it stand for about 5 minutes
