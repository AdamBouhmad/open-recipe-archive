## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of melted butter
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- salt and pepper to taste
- juice of 1/2 large lemon
- 2 minced shalots

## Directions

1. Make 1/2 pint of melted butter by recipe No. 376.
2. Stir in the above ingredients, and let them just boil when it is ready to serve.
