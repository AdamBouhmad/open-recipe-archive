## Ingredients

- Wild duck
- flour
- butter
- lemon

## Directions

1. Carefully pluck and draw the duck.
2. Cut off the heads close to the necks, leaving sufficient skin to turn over, and do not cut off the feet.
3. Truss the birds as desired.
4. Roast the birds before a quick fire.
5. When first put down, let them remain for 5 minutes without basting to keep the gravy in.
6. Afterwards baste plentifully with butter.
7. A few minutes before serving dredge them lightly with flour and baste well.
8. Send them to table nicely frothed and full of gravy.
9. If the birds have a fishy taste, baste them for a few minutes with hot water to which have been added an onion and a little salt, then take away the pan and baste with butter.
10. Serve with a good gravy in the dish, or orange gravy, and send to table with them a cut lemon.
