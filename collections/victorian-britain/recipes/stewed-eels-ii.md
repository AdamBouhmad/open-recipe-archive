## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of middling-sized eels
- 1 pint of medium stock
- 1/4 pint of port wine
- salt to taste
- cayenne to taste
- mace to taste
- 1 teaspoonful of essence of anchovy
- the juice of 1/2 a lemon

## Directions

1. Skin, wash, and clean the eels thoroughly
2. cut them into pieces 3 inches long, and put them into strong salt and water for 1 hour
3. dry them well with a cloth, and fry them brown
4. Put the stock on with the heads and tails of the eels, and simmer for 1/2 hour
5. strain it, and add all the other ingredients
6. Put in the eels, and stew gently for 1/2 hour, then serve
