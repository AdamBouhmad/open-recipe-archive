## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast chicken
- 1/4 lb. of fresh butter per lb. of meat
- salt and cayenne to taste
- 1 teaspoonful of pounded mace
- 1/4 small nutmeg per lb. of meat
- clarified butter for covering
- 2 or 3 slices of ham, minced (optional improvement)

## Directions

1. Strip the meat from the bones of cold roast fowl
2. Free the meat from gristle and skin, and weigh it
3. To every lb. of meat, allow 1/4 lb. of fresh butter, salt and cayenne to taste, 1 teaspoonful of pounded mace, and 1/4 small nutmeg
4. Cut the meat into small pieces
5. Pound it well with the fresh butter
6. Sprinkle in the spices gradually, and keep pounding until reduced to a perfectly smooth paste
7. Put it into potting-pots for use
8. Cover it with clarified butter, about 1/4 inch in thickness
9. If to be kept for some time, tie over a bladder
10. Keep it in a dry place
