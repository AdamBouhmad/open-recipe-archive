## Ingredients

- 6 oz. of flour
- 2 eggs
- not quite 1 pint of milk
- salt to taste
- 1-1/2 lb. of rump-steaks
- 1 kidney
- pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Cut the steaks into nice square pieces, with a small quantity of fat, and the kidney divide into small pieces.
2. Make a batter of flour, eggs, and milk in the above proportion; lay a little of it at the bottom of a pie-dish.
3. Put in the steaks and kidney, which should be well seasoned with pepper and salt, and pour over the remainder of the batter.
4. Bake for 1-1/2 hour in a brisk but not fierce oven.
