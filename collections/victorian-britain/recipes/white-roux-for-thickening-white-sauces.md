## Ingredients

- 6 oz. of butter
- 9 oz. of flour

## Directions

1. Allow the same proportions of butter and flour as in the brown roux recipe
2. Proceed in the same manner as for brown roux, but do not keep it on the fire too long
3. Take care not to let it colour
4. Pour it into a jar to use when wanted
