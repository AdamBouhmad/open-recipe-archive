## Ingredients

- sprats
- flour
- suet

## Directions

1. Wipe them dry; fasten them in rows by a skewer run through the eyes.
2. Dredge with flour, and broil them on a gridiron over a nice clear fire.
3. The gridiron should be rubbed with suet.
4. Serve very hot.
