## Ingredients

- remains of any joint of cold roast mutton
- 1 or 2 onions
- 1 bunch of savoury herbs
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 blades of pounded mace or nutmeg
- 2 tablespoonfuls of gravy
- mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. Mince an onion rather fine, and fry it a light-brown colour
2. Add the herbs and mutton, both of which should be also finely minced and well mixed
3. Season with pepper and salt, and a little pounded mace or nutmeg, and moisten with the gravy
4. Put a layer of mashed potatoes at the bottom of a dish
5. Add the mutton layer
6. Add another layer of potatoes
7. Bake for about 1/2 hour
