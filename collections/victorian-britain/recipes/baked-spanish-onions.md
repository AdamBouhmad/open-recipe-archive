## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 Spanish onions
- salt
- water
- cold butter
- pepper and salt
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Put the onions, with their skins on, into a saucepan of boiling water slightly salted, and let them boil quickly for an hour
2. Take them out, wipe them thoroughly, wrap each one in a piece of paper separately, and bake them in a moderate oven for 2 hours, or longer, should the onions be very large
3. They may be served in their skins, and eaten with a piece of cold butter and a seasoning of pepper and salt; or they may be peeled, and a good brown gravy poured over them
