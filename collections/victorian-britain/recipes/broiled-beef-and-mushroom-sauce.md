## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 dozen small button mushrooms
- 1 oz. of butter
- Salt and cayenne to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of mushroom ketchup
- Mashed potatoes
- Slices of cold roast beef

## Directions

1. Wipe the mushrooms free from grit with a piece of flannel, and salt
2. Put them in a stewpan with the butter, seasoning, and ketchup
3. Stir over the fire until the mushrooms are quite done
4. Pour it in the middle of mashed potatoes, browned
5. Place round the potatoes slices of cold roast beef, nicely broiled over a clear fire
