## Ingredients

- Batter
- 8 slices of bread and butter
- 3 or 4 tablespoonfuls of jam

## Directions

1. Make a batter, the same as for apple fritters No. 1393
2. Cut some slices of bread and butter, not very thick
3. Spread half of them with any jam that may be preferred, and cover with the other slices
4. Slightly press them together, and cut them out in square, long, or round pieces
5. Dip them in the batter, and fry in boiling lard for about 10 minutes
6. Drain them before the fire on a piece of blotting-paper or cloth
7. Dish them, sprinkle over sifted sugar, and serve
