## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast or boiled chicken
- 2 lettuces
- a little endive
- 1 cucumber
- a few slices of boiled beetroot
- salad-dressing No. 506
- hard-boiled eggs for garnish

## Directions

1. Trim neatly the remains of the chicken
2. Wash, dry, and slice the lettuces
3. Place the lettuce in the middle of a dish
4. Put the pieces of fowl on the top
5. Pour the salad-dressing over them
6. Garnish the edge of the salad with hard-boiled eggs cut in rings, sliced cucumber, and boiled beetroot cut in slices
7. Alternatively, rub the yolks through a hair sieve, chop the whites very finely, and arrange on the salad in small bunches, yellow and white alternately
8. Do not make long before it is wanted for table
