## Ingredients

- 4 quarts of liquor in which a leg of mutton or beef has been boiled
- a few beef-bones
- 6 large carrots
- 2 large onions
- 1 turnip
- salt and pepper to taste
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Put the liquor, bones, onions, turnip, pepper, and salt, into a stewpan, and simmer for 3 hours.
2. Scrape and cut the carrots thin, strain the soup on them, and stew them till soft enough to pulp through a hair sieve or coarse cloth.
3. Then boil the pulp with the soup, which should be of the consistency of pea-soup.
4. Add cayenne.
5. Pulp only the red part of the carrot, and make this soup the day before it is wanted.
