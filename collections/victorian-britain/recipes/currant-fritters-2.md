## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 4 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of boiled rice
- 3 tablespoonfuls of currants
- sugar to taste
- a very little grated nutmeg
- hot lard or clarified dripping

## Directions

1. Put the milk into a basin with the flour, which should previously be rubbed to a smooth batter with a little cold milk
2. Stir these ingredients together
3. Add the well-whisked eggs, the rice, currants, sugar, and nutmeg
4. Beat the mixture for a few minutes, and, if not sufficiently thick, add a little more boiled rice
5. Drop it, in small quantities, into a pan of boiling lard or clarified dripping
6. Fry the fritters a nice brown, and, when done, drain them on a piece of blotting-paper, before the fire
7. Pile them on a white d'oyley, strew over sifted sugar, and serve them very hot
8. Send a cut lemon to table with them
