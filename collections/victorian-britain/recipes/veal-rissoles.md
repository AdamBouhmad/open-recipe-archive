## Ingredients

- A few slices of cold roast veal
- a few slices of ham or bacon
- 1 tablespoonful of minced parsley
- 1 tablespoonful of minced savoury herbs
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- a very little grated nutmeg
- cayenne and salt to taste
- 2 eggs well beaten
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Mince the veal very finely with a little ham or bacon
2. add the parsley, herbs, spices, and seasoning
3. mix into a paste with an egg
4. form into balls or cones
5. brush these over with egg
6. sprinkle with bread crumbs
7. fry a rich brown
8. Serve with brown gravy, and garnish the dish with fried parsley
