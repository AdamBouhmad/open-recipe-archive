## Ingredients

- Lamb
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Have ready a clear brisk fire
2. Put down the joint at a little distance, to prevent the fat from scorching, and keep it well basted all the time it is cooking
3. Serve with mint sauce and a fresh salad, and send to table with it, either peas, cauliflowers, or spinach
