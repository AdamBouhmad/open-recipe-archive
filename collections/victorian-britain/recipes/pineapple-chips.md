## Ingredients

- Pineapples
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Pare and slice the fruit thinly, put it on dishes, and strew over it plenty of pounded sugar.
2. Keep it in a hot closet, or very slow oven, 8 or 10 days, and turn the fruit every day until dry; then put the pieces of pine on tins, and place them in a quick oven for 10 minutes.
3. Let them cool, and store them away in dry boxes, with paper between each layer.
