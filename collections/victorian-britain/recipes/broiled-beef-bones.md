## Ingredients

- The bones of ribs or sirloin
- Salt, pepper, and cayenne

## Directions

1. Separate the bones, taking care that the meat on them is not too thick in any part
2. Sprinkle them well with salt, pepper, and cayenne
3. Broil over a very clear fire
4. When nicely browned they are done; but do not allow them to blacken
