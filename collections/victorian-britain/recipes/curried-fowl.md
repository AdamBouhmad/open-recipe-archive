## Ingredients

- 1 fowl
- 2 oz. of butter
- 3 onions sliced
- 1 pint of white veal gravy
- 1 tablespoonful of curry-powder
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 apple
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a stewpan, with the onions sliced, the fowl cut into small joints, and the apple peeled, cored, and minced
2. Fry of a pale brown, add the stock, and stew gently for 20 minutes
3. Rub down the curry-powder and flour with a little of the gravy, quite smoothly, and stir this to the other ingredients
4. Simmer for rather more than 1/2 hour
5. Just before serving, add the above proportion of hot cream and lemon-juice
6. Serve with boiled rice, which may either be heaped lightly on a dish by itself, or put round the curry as a border
