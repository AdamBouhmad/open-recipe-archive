## Ingredients

- A few slices of hot buttered toast
- 1 anchovy to each slice
- 1/4 pint of cream
- yolks of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Separate the yolks from the whites of the eggs; beat the former, stir to them the cream, and bring the sauce to the boiling-point, but do not allow it to boil, or it will curdle.
2. Have ready some hot buttered toast, spread with anchovies pounded to a paste; pour a little of the hot sauce on the top, and serve very hot and very quickly.
