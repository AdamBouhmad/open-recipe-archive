## Ingredients

- Remains of cold roast beef (any amount)
- Bacon or ham (1/4 lb. per pound of cold meat)
- Pepper and salt to taste
- 1 small bunch of minced savoury herbs
- 1 or 2 eggs
- Hot dripping for frying
- Good gravy for serving

## Directions

1. Mince the beef very finely (if underdone it will be better).
2. Add to it the bacon, which must also be chopped very small, and mix well together.
3. Season, stir in the herbs, and bind with an egg, or 2 should 1 not be sufficient.
4. Make it into small square cakes, about 1/2 inch thick.
5. Fry them in hot dripping, and serve in a dish with good gravy poured round them.
6. Fry for 10 minutes.
