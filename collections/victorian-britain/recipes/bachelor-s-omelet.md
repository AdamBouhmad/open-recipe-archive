## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 eggs
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- 1/2 teacupful of milk
- a pinch of salt
- a few grains of cayenne
- chopped herbs
- a few shreds of onion

## Directions

1. Make a thin cream of the flour and milk
2. Beat up the eggs, mix all together, and add a pinch of salt and a few grains of cayenne
3. Melt the butter in a small frying-pan, and, when very hot, pour in the batter
4. Let the pan remain for a few minutes over a clear fire
5. Sprinkle upon the omelet some chopped herbs and a few shreds of onion
6. Double the omelet dexterously, and shake it out of the pan on to a hot dish
