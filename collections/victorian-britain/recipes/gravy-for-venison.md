## Ingredients

- Trimmings of venison
- 3 or 4 mutton shank-bones
- salt to taste
- 1 pint of water
- 2 teaspoonfuls of walnut ketchup

## Directions

1. Brown the trimmings over a nice clear fire, and put them in a stewpan with the shank-bones and water
2. Simmer gently for 2 hours
3. Strain and skim, and add the walnut ketchup and a seasoning of salt
4. Let it just boil, when it is ready to serve
