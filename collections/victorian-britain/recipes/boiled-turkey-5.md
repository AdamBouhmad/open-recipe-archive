## Ingredients

- Turkey
- forcemeat No. 417

## Directions

1. Pluck the bird, carefully draw, and singe it with a piece of white paper
2. Wash it inside and out, and wipe it thoroughly dry with a cloth
3. Cut off the head and neck, draw the strings or sinews of the thighs, and cut off the legs at the first joint
4. Draw the legs into the body, fill the breast with forcemeat made by recipe No. 417
5. Run a skewer through the wing and the middle joint of the leg, quite into the leg and wing on the opposite side
6. Break the breastbone, and make the bird look as round and as compact as possible
7. Put the turkey into sufficient hot water to cover it
8. Let it come to a boil, then carefully remove all the scum
9. Let it simmer very gently for about 1-1/2 hour to 1-3/4 hour, according to the size
10. Serve with either white, celery, oyster, or mushroom sauce, or parsley-and-butter, a little of which should be poured over the turkey
11. Accompany with boiled ham, bacon, tongue, or pickled pork
12. If oyster sauce is served, the turkey should be stuffed with oyster forcemeat
