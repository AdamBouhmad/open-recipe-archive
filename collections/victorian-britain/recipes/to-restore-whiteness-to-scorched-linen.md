## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of vinegar
- 2 oz. of fuller's-earth
- 1 oz. of dried fowls' dung
- 1/2 oz. of soap
- the juice of 2 large onions

## Directions

1. Boil all these ingredients together to the consistency of paste
2. Spread the composition thickly over the damaged part
3. Allow it to dry on
4. Wash the place once or twice, and every trace of scorching will disappear
