## Ingredients

- 1 heart
- Stuffing of veal forcemeat, No. 417

## Directions

1. Put the heart into warm water to soak for 2 hours
2. Wipe it well with a cloth
3. Cut off the lobes
4. Stuff the inside with a highly-seasoned forcemeat (No. 417)
5. Fasten it in, by means of a needle and coarse thread
6. Tie the heart up in paper
7. Set it before a good fire, being very particular to keep it well basted, or it will eat dry, there being very little of its own fat
8. Two or three minutes before serving, remove the paper and baste well
9. Serve with good gravy and red-currant jelly or melted butter
