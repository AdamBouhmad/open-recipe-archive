## Ingredients

- 3 oz. of flour
- 3 eggs
- 1/3 pint of milk

## Directions

1. Mix the flour to a smooth batter with a small quantity of the milk
2. stir in the eggs, which should be well whisked, and then the remainder of the milk
3. beat the whole to a perfectly smooth batter, and should it be found not quite thin enough, add two or three tablespoonfuls more milk
4. Have ready a frying-pan, with plenty of boiling lard in it
5. drop in rather more than a tablespoonful at a time of the batter, and fry the fritters a nice brown, turning them when sufficiently cooked on one side
6. Drain them well from the greasy moisture by placing them upon a piece of blotting-paper before the fire
7. dish them on a white d'oyley
8. sprinkle over them sifted sugar
9. send to table with them a cut lemon and plenty of pounded sugar
