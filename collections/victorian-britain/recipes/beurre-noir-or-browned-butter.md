## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of minced parsley
- 3 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a fryingpan over a nice clear fire, and when it smokes, throw in the parsley, and add the vinegar and seasoning.
2. Let the whole simmer for a minute or two, when it is ready to serve.
