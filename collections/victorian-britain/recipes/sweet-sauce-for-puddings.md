## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of melted butter made with milk
- 4 heaped teaspoonfuls of pounded sugar
- flavouring of grated lemon-rind, or nutmeg, or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make 1/2 pint of melted butter by recipe No. 380, omitting the salt; stir in the sugar, add a little grated lemon-rind, nutmeg, or powdered cinnamon, and serve.
2. Previously to making the melted butter, the milk can be flavoured with bitter almonds, by infusing about half a dozen of them in it for about 1/2 hour; the milk should then be strained before it is added to the other ingredients.
