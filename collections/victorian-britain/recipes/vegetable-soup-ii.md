## Ingredients

- Equal quantities of onions, carrots, turnips
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- a crust of toasted bread
- 1 head of celery
- a faggot of herbs
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoonful of powdered sugar
- 2 quarts of common stock or boiling water
- Allow 3/4 lb. of vegetables to 2 quarts of stock, No. 105

## Directions

1. Cut up the onions, carrots, and turnips
2. Wash and drain them well
3. Put them in the stewpan with the butter and powdered sugar
4. Toss the whole over a sharp fire for 10 minutes, but do not let them brown
5. Pour the stock or boiling water on them
6. Add the bread, celery, herbs, and seasoning
7. Stew for 3 hours
8. Skim well and strain it off
9. When ready to serve, add a little sliced carrot, celery, and turnip
10. Flavour with a spoonful of Harvey's sauce, or a little ketchup
