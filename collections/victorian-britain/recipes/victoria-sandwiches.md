## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- pounded sugar equal in weight to the eggs
- butter equal in weight to the eggs
- flour equal in weight to the eggs
- 1/4 saltspoonful of salt
- layer of jam or marmalade

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Dredge in the flour and pounded sugar
3. Stir these ingredients well together
4. Add the eggs, which should be previously thoroughly whisked
5. Beat the mixture well for about 10 minutes
6. Butter a Yorkshire-pudding tin and pour in the batter
7. Bake it in a moderate oven for 20 minutes
8. Let it cool
9. Spread one half of the cake with a layer of nice preserve
10. Place the other half of the cake over it
11. Press the pieces slightly together
12. Cut it into long finger-pieces
13. Pile them in crossbars on a glass dish and serve
