## Ingredients

- 5 or 6 artichokes
- To each 1/2 gallon of water allow 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1 bunch of savoury herbs
- 2 oz. of butter
- a large piece of cinder in a muslin bag
- plain melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut the ends of the leaves, as also the stems.
2. Put the artichokes into boiling water, with the above proportion of salt, pepper, herbs, and butter.
3. Let them boil quickly until tender, keeping the lid of the saucepan off.
4. When the leaves come out easily, they are cooked enough.
5. To keep them a beautiful green, put a large piece of cinder into a muslin bag, and let it boil with them.
6. Serve with plain melted butter.
