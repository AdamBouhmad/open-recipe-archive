## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. of lard
- 14 lbs. of flour
- milk and water
- 3 lbs. of meat
- 1 oz. of salt
- 2-1/4 oz. of pepper
- a small quantity of cayenne
- 1 pint of water

## Directions

1. Rub into the flour a portion of the lard; the remainder put with sufficient milk and water to mix the crust, and boil this gently for 1/4 hour.
2. Pour it boiling on the flour, and knead and beat it till perfectly smooth.
3. Raise the crust in either a round or oval form.
4. Cut up the pork into pieces the size of a nut, season it in the above proportion, and press it compactly into the pie, in alternate layers of fat and lean, and pour in a small quantity of water.
5. Lay on the lid, cut the edges smoothly round, and pinch them together.
6. Bake in a brick oven, which should be slow, as the meat is very solid.
7. Keep the crust warm during the operation to prevent it from falling; a glass bottle or small jar may be placed in the middle of the paste, and the crust moulded on this.
