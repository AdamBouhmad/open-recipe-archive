## Ingredients

- haddock
- forcemeat
- butter
- egg
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- lemon
- brown gravy
- anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish, without cutting it open much
2. put in a nice delicate forcemeat
3. sew up the slit
4. brush it over with egg
5. sprinkle over bread crumbs
6. baste frequently with butter
7. garnish with parsley and cut lemon
8. serve with a nice brown gravy, plain melted butter, or anchovy sauce
