## Ingredients

- Any remains of cold cod
- 4 tablespoonfuls of béchamel
- 2 oz butter
- pepper and salt to taste
- fried bread
- a few bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Flake the cod carefully, leaving out all skin and bone
2. put the béchamel in a stewpan with the butter, and stir it over the fire till the latter is melted
3. add seasoning, put in the fish, and mix it well with the sauce
4. Make a border of fried bread round the dish, lay in the fish, sprinkle over with bread crumbs, and baste with butter
5. Brown either before the fire or with a salamander, and garnish with toasted bread cut in fanciful shapes
