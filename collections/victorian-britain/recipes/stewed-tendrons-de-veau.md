## Ingredients

- The gristles from 2 breasts of veal
- Stock No. 107
- 1 faggot of savoury herbs
- 2 blades of pounded mace
- 4 cloves
- 2 carrots
- 2 onions
- A strip of lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Detach the gristles from the bone
2. Cut them neatly out, so as not to spoil the joint for roasting or stewing
3. Put them into a stewpan with sufficient stock, No. 107, to cover them
4. Add the herbs, mace, cloves, carrots, onions, and lemon
5. Simmer these for nearly, or quite, 4 hours
6. They should be stewed until a fork will enter the meat easily
7. Take them up and drain them
8. Strain the gravy
9. Boil it down to a glaze
10. Glaze the meat with this glaze
11. Dish the tendrons in a circle
12. Place croûtons fried of a nice colour between each
13. Put mushroom sauce, or a purée of green peas or tomatoes, in the middle
