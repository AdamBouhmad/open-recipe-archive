## Ingredients

- haddock
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Scrape the fish
2. take out the inside
3. wash it thoroughly
4. lay it in a kettle, with enough water to cover it and salt in the proportion of 1/4 lb. of salt to each gallon of water
5. simmer gently from 15 to 20 minutes, or rather more, should the fish be very large
6. For small haddocks, fasten the tails in their mouths, and put them into boiling water for 10 to 15 minutes
7. serve with plain melted butter, or anchovy sauce
