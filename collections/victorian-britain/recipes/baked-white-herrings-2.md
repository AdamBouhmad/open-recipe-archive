## Ingredients

- 12 herrings
- 4 bay-leaves
- 12 cloves
- 12 allspice
- 2 small blades of mace
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take the herrings
2. cut off the heads
3. gut them
4. put them in a pie-dish, heads and tails alternately
5. between each layer, sprinkle over the bay-leaves, cloves, allspice, mace, cayenne pepper and salt
6. cover the fish with the vinegar
7. bake for 1/2 hour
8. do not use it till quite cold
