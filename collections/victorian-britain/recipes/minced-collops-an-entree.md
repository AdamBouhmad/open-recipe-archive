## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of rump-steak
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 onion minced
- 1/4 pint of water
- 1 tablespoonful of Harvey's sauce, or lemon-juice, or mushroom ketchup
- 1 small bunch of savoury herbs

## Directions

1. Mince the beef and onion very small, and fry the latter in butter until of a pale brown.
2. Put all the ingredients together in a stewpan, and boil gently for about 10 minutes.
3. Garnish with sippets of toasted bread, and serve very hot.
