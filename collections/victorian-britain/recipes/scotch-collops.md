## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast veal
- a little butter
- flour
- 1/2 pint of water
- 1 onion
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- 1/2 teaspoonful of finely-minced lemon-peel
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sherry
- 1 tablespoonful of mushroom ketchup

## Directions

1. Cut the veal the same thickness as for cutlets, rather larger than a crown-piece
2. Flour the meat well
3. Fry a light brown in butter
4. Dredge again with flour
5. Add 1/2 pint of water, pouring it in by degrees
6. Set it on the fire
7. When it boils, add the onion and mace
8. Let it simmer very gently about 3/4 hour
9. Flavour the gravy with lemon-juice, peel, wine, and ketchup
10. Give one boil and serve
