## Ingredients

- 6 potatoes
- 4 turnips, or 2 if very large
- 2 carrots
- 2 onions
- 2 mushrooms, if obtainable
- 1 head of celery
- 1 large slice of bread
- 1 small saltspoonful of salt
- 1/4 saltspoonful of ground black pepper
- 2 teaspoonfuls of Harvey's sauce
- 6 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Peel the vegetables, and cut them up into small pieces
2. Toast the bread rather brown
3. Put all into a stewpan with the water and seasoning
4. Simmer gently for 3 hours, or until all is reduced to a pulp
5. Pass it through a sieve in the same way as pea-soup
