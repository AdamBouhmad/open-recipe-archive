## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Boiling water

## Directions

1. Pare the potatoes, throw them into cold water as they are peeled.
2. Put them into a steamer.
3. Place the steamer over a saucepan of boiling water, and steam the potatoes from 20 to 40 minutes, according to the size and sort.
4. When a fork goes easily through them, they are done.
5. Take them up, dish, and serve very quickly.
