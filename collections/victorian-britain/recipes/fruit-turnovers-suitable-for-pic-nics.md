## Ingredients

- Puff-paste
- any kind of fruit
- sugar to taste
- white of an egg
- sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Make some puff-paste; roll it out to the thickness of about 1/4 inch, and cut it out in pieces of a circular form
2. pile the fruit on half of the paste, sprinkle over some sugar, wet the edges and turn the paste over
3. Press the edges together, ornament them, and brush the turnovers over with the white of an egg; sprinkle over sifted sugar, and bake on tins, in a brisk oven, for about 20 minutes
