## Ingredients

- 9 slices of bread and butter
- about 6 good-sized apples
- 1 tablespoonful of minced lemon-peel
- 2 tablespoonfuls of juice
- moist sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Butter a pie-dish.
2. Place a layer of bread and butter, without the crust, at the bottom; then a layer of apples, pared, cored, and cut into thin slices.
3. Sprinkle over these a portion of the lemon-peel and juice, and sweeten with moist sugar.
4. Place another layer of bread and butter, and then one of apples, proceeding in this manner until the dish is full.
5. Cover it up with the peel of the apples, to preserve the top from browning or burning.
6. Bake in a brisk oven for rather more than 3/4 hour.
7. Turn the charlotte on a dish, sprinkle sifted sugar over, and serve.
