## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of scraped horseradish
- 1 teaspoonful of made mustard
- 1 teaspoonful of pounded sugar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of vinegar

## Directions

1. Grate or scrape the horseradish very fine, and mix it with the other ingredients, which must be all well blended together; serve in a tureen.
