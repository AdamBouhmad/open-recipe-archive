## Ingredients

- 8 good boiling apples
- 1/2 pint of water
- 6 oz. of sugar
- a layer of apple marmalade
- 8 preserved cherries
- garnishing of apricot jam

## Directions

1. Peel the apples, and, with a vegetable-cutter, push out the cores
2. Boil them in the above proportion of sugar and water, without being too much done, and take care they do not break
3. Have ready a white apple marmalade
4. Cover the bottom of the dish with this, level it, and lay the apples in a sieve to drain
5. Pile them neatly on the marmalade, making them high in the centre, and place a preserved cherry in the middle of each
6. Garnish with strips of candied citron or apricot jam, and the dish is ready for table
