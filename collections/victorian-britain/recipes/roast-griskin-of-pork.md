## Ingredients

- Pork
- powdered sage

## Directions

1. Put it down to a bright fire, and flour it.
2. About 10 minutes before taking it up, sprinkle over some powdered sage.
3. Make a little gravy in the dripping-pan, strain it over the meat, and serve with a tureen of apple sauce.
4. Take particular care that it is well basted to prevent it from becoming hard and dry.
