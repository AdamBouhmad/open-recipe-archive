## Ingredients

- very strong coffee
- a little brandy or liqueurs

## Directions

1. Make the coffee very strong
2. Serve in very small cups, well sweetened, with the addition of a little brandy or liqueurs, which may be added or not at pleasure
3. Never mix with milk or cream
4. Alternatively, pour a tablespoonful of coffee essence into each cup and fill it up with boiling water
