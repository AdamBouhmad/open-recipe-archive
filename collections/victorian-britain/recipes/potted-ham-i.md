## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of lean ham
- 1 lb. of fat
- 2 teaspoonfuls of pounded mace
- 1/2 nutmeg grated
- rather more than 1/2 teaspoonful of cayenne
- clarified lard

## Directions

1. Mince the ham, fat and lean together in the above proportion.
2. Pound it well in a mortar, seasoning it with cayenne pepper, pounded mace, and nutmeg.
3. Put the mixture into a deep baking-dish, and bake for 1/2 hour.
4. Then press it well into a stone jar, fill up the jar with clarified lard, cover it closely, and paste over it a piece of thick paper.
