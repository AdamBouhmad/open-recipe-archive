## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of lard
- 2 pennyworth of castor-oil
- scent

## Directions

1. Let the lard be unsalted
2. Beat it up well
3. Add the castor-oil, and mix thoroughly together with a knife, adding a few drops of any scent that may be preferred
4. Put the pomatum into pots, which keep well covered to prevent it turning rancid
