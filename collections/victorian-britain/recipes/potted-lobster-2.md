## Ingredients

- 2 lobsters
- nutmeg, pounded mace, white pepper, and salt to taste
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 3 or 4 bay-leaves
- clarified butter

## Directions

1. Take out the meat carefully from the shell, but do not cut it up
2. Put some butter at the bottom of a dish, lay in the lobster as evenly as possible, with the bay-leaves and seasoning between
3. Cover with butter, and bake for 3/4 hour in a gentle oven
4. When done, drain the whole on a sieve, and lay the pieces in potting-jars, with the seasoning about them
5. When cold, pour over it clarified butter
