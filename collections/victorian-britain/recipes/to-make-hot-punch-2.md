## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of rum
- 1/2 pint of brandy
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1 large lemon
- 1/2 teaspoonful of nutmeg
- 1 pint of boiling water

## Directions

1. Rub the sugar over the lemon until it has absorbed all the yellow part of the skin
2. Put the sugar into a punchbowl
3. Add the lemon-juice (free from pips)
4. Mix these two ingredients well together
5. Pour over them the boiling water
6. Stir well together
7. Add the rum, brandy, and nutmeg
8. Mix thoroughly
