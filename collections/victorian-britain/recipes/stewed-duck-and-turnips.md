## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast duck
- 1/2 pint of good gravy
- 4 shalots
- a few slices of carrot
- a small bunch of savoury herbs
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- 1 lb. of turnips, weighed after being peeled
- 2 oz. of butter
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cut up the duck into joints, fry the shalots, carrots, and herbs, and put them, with the duck, into the gravy
2. Add the pounded mace, and stew gently for 20 minutes or 1/2 hour
3. Cut about 1 lb. of turnips, weighed after being peeled, into 1/2-inch squares
4. Put the butter into a stewpan, and stew them till quite tender, which will be in about 1/2 hour, or rather more
5. Season with pepper and salt, and serve in the centre of the dish, with the duck, &c. laid round
