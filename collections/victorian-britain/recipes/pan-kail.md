## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of cabbage, or Savoy greens
- 1/4 lb. of butter or dripping
- salt and pepper to taste
- oatmeal for thickening
- 2 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage very fine, thicken the water with oatmeal, put in the cabbage and butter, or dripping; season and simmer for 1-1/2 hour.
2. It can be made sooner by blanching and mashing the greens, adding any good liquor that a joint has been boiled in, and then further thicken with bread or pounded biscuit.
