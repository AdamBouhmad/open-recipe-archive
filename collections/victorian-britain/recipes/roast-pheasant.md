## Ingredients

- Pheasant
- flour
- butter
- brown gravy
- bread sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck and draw the bird
2. Wipe the inside with a damp cloth
3. Truss it in the same manner as partridge, No. 1039
4. If the head is left on, bring it round under the wing, and fix it on to the point of the skewer
5. Roast it before a brisk fire
6. Keep it well basted
7. Flour and froth it nicely
8. Serve with brown gravy, a little of which should be poured round the bird, and a tureen of bread sauce
