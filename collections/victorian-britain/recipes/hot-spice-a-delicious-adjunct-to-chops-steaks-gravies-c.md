## Ingredients

- 3 drachms of ginger
- 3 drachms of black pepper
- 3 drachms of cinnamon
- 7 cloves
- 1/2 oz. mace
- 1/4 oz. of cayenne
- 1 oz. grated nutmeg
- 1-1/2 oz. white pepper

## Directions

1. Pound the ingredients, and mix them thoroughly together, taking care that everything is well blended.
2. Put the spice in a very dry glass bottle for use.
3. The quantity of cayenne may be increased, should the above not be enough to suit the palate.
