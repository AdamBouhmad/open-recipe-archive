## Ingredients

- sweet-oil
- vinegar
- treacle
- lamp-black 1 oz.

## Directions

1. Mix equal proportions of sweet-oil, vinegar, and treacle, with 1 oz. of lamp-black
2. When all the ingredients are thoroughly incorporated, rub the mixture on the boots with the palm of the hand
3. Put them in a cool place to dry
