## Ingredients

- A few slices of cold roast beef
- 3 oz. of butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- 3 onions
- 1/2 pint of gravy

## Directions

1. Slice the onions and put them into a frying-pan with the cold beef and butter
2. Place it over the fire, and keep turning and stirring the ingredients to prevent them burning
3. When of a pale brown, add the gravy and seasoning
4. Let it simmer for a few minutes, and serve very hot
