## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- 2 oz. butter
- 1/3 pint of milk
- a few grains of salt

## Directions

1. Mix the butter and flour smoothly together on a plate, put it into a lined saucepan, and pour in the milk.
2. Keep stirring it one way over a sharp fire.
3. Let it boil quickly for a minute or two, and it is ready to serve.
