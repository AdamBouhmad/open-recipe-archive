## Ingredients

- Apples
- to every lb. of pulp 3/4 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of minced lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Peel, core, and boil the apples with only sufficient water to prevent them from burning
2. Beat them to a pulp
3. To every lb. of pulp add the sugar in lumps
4. Dip the lumps into water and put them into a saucepan
5. Boil till the syrup is thick and can be well skimmed
6. Add this syrup to the apple pulp with the minced lemon-peel
7. Stir it over a quick fire for about 20 minutes, or until the apples cease to stick to the bottom of the pan
8. Pour the jelly into moulds which have been previously dipped in water
9. Turn out for dessert or a side-dish, with custard poured round and garnished with strips of citron or blanched almonds
