## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of powdered loaf sugar
- 1 teacupful of water
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 6 drops of essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Put the water and sugar into a brass pan, and beat the butter to a cream.
2. When the sugar is dissolved, add the butter, and keep stirring the mixture over the fire until it sets, when a little is poured on to a buttered dish.
3. Just before the toffee is done, add the essence of lemon.
4. Butter a dish or tin, pour on it the mixture, and when cool, it will easily separate from the dish.
