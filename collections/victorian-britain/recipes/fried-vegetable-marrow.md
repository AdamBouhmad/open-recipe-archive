## Ingredients

- 3 medium-sized vegetable marrows
- egg and bread crumbs
- hot lard
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Peel, and boil the marrows until tender in salt and water
2. Drain them and cut them in quarters, and take out the seeds
3. When thoroughly drained, brush the marrows over with egg, and sprinkle with bread crumbs
4. Have ready some hot lard, fry the marrow in this, and, when of a nice brown, dish
5. Sprinkle over a little salt and pepper, and serve
