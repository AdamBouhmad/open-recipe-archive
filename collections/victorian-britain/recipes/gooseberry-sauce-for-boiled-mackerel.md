## Ingredients

- 1 pint of green gooseberries
- 3 tablespoonfuls of Béchamel, No. 367 (veal gravy may be substituted)
- 2 oz. of fresh butter
- salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the gooseberries in water until quite tender
2. Strain them and rub them through a sieve
3. Put into a saucepan the Béchamel or gravy with the butter and seasoning
4. Add the pulp from the gooseberries
5. Mix all well together
6. Heat gradually through
7. A little pounded sugar may be added by those who consider it an improvement
