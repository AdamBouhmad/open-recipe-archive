## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 1/2 lb. of lard or flead
- 1/2 pint of water
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Clear the flead free from skin, and slice it into thin flakes
2. Rub it into the flour, add the salt, and work the whole into a smooth paste, with the above proportion of water
3. Fold the paste over two or three times
4. Beat it well with the rolling-pin
5. Roll it out, and it will be ready for use
