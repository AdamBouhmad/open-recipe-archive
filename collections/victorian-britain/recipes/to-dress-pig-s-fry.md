## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 lb. of pig's fry
- 2 onions
- a few sage-leaves
- 3 lbs. of potatoes
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put the lean fry at the bottom of a pie-dish, sprinkle over it some minced sage and onion, and a seasoning of pepper and salt.
2. Slice the potatoes; put a layer of these on the seasoning, then the fat fry, then more seasoning, and a layer of potatoes at the top.
3. Fill the dish with boiling water, and bake for 2 hours, or rather longer.
