## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoonfuls of prepared cocoa
- 1 breakfast-cup
- boiling milk
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Put the cocoa into a breakfast-cup
2. Pour over it sufficient cold milk to make it into a smooth paste
3. Add equal quantities of boiling milk and boiling water
4. Stir all well together
5. Take care not to allow the milk to get burnt
