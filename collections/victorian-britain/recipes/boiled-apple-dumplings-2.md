## Ingredients

- 6 apples
- 3/4 lb. of suet-crust
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Pare and take out the cores of the apples without dividing them
2. Sweeten
3. Roll each apple in a piece of crust
4. Be particular that the paste is nicely joined
5. Put the dumplings into floured cloths
6. Tie them securely
7. Put them into boiling water
8. Keep them boiling from 1/2 to 3/4 hour
9. Remove the cloths
10. Send them hot and quickly to table
