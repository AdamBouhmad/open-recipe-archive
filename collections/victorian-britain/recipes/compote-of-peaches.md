## Ingredients

- 1 pint of syrup No. 1512
- about 15 small peaches

## Directions

1. Divide the peaches, take out the stones, and pare the fruit
2. Make a syrup by recipe No. 1512, put in the peaches, and stew them gently for about 10 minutes
3. Take them out without breaking, arrange them on a glass dish, boil the syrup for 2 or 3 minutes, let it cool, pour it over the fruit, and, when cold, it will be ready for table
