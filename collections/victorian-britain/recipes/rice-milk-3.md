## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of rice
- 1 quart of milk
- sugar to taste
- when liked, a little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Well wash the rice
2. Put it into a saucepan with the milk
3. Simmer gently until the rice is tender
4. Stir it from time to time to prevent the milk from burning
5. Sweeten it
6. Add a little grated nutmeg
7. Serve
