## Ingredients

- A few thin slices of cold boiled beef
- Butter
- Cabbage
- 1 sliced onion
- Pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Fry the slices of beef gently in a little butter, taking care not to dry them up
2. Lay them on a flat dish
3. Cover with fried greens
4. The greens may be prepared from cabbage sprouts or green savoys
5. Boil them till tender
6. Well drain them
7. Mince them and place over the beef
