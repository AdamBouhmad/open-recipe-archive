## Ingredients

- Large white potatoes
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Choose large white potatoes, as free from spots as possible
2. Boil them in their skins in salt and water until perfectly tender
3. Drain and dry them thoroughly by the side of the fire
4. Peel them
5. Put a hot dish before the fire
6. Rub the potatoes through a coarse sieve on to this dish
7. Do not touch them afterwards, or the flakes will fall
8. Serve as hot as possible
