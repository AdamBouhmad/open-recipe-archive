## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of almonds
- 4 bitter almonds
- 1 glass of sherry
- 4 eggs
- the rind and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 3 oz. of butter
- 1 pint of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- puff-paste for lining pie-dish

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound the almonds to a smooth paste with the water
2. Mix these with the butter, which should be melted
3. Beat up the eggs, grate the lemon-rind, and strain the juice
4. Add these, with the cream, sugar, and wine, to the other ingredients, and stir them well together
5. When well mixed, put it into a pie-dish lined with puff-paste, and bake for 1/2 hour
