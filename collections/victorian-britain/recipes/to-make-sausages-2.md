## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of pork, fat and lean, without skin or gristle
- 1 lb. of lean veal
- 1 lb. of beef suet
- 1/2 lb. of bread crumbs
- the rind of 1/2 lemon
- 1 small nutmeg
- 6 sage-leaves
- 1 teaspoonful of pepper
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of savory
- 1/2 teaspoonful of marjoram

## Directions

1. Chop the pork, veal, and suet finely together.
2. Add the bread crumbs, lemon-peel (which should be well minced), and a small nutmeg grated.
3. Wash and chop the sage-leaves very finely.
4. Add these with the remaining ingredients to the sausage-meat, and when thoroughly mixed, either put the meat into skins, or, when wanted for table, form it into little cakes, which should be floured and fried.
