## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- skimmed milk

## Directions

1. Warm the butter in the milk until the former is dissolved, and then mix it with the flour into a very stiff paste
2. Beat it with a rolling-pin until the dough looks perfectly smooth
3. Roll it out thin
4. Cut it with the top of a glass into round biscuits
5. Prick them well, and bake them from 6 to 10 minutes
