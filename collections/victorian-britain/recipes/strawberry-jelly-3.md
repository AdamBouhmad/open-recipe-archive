## Ingredients

- Strawberries
- pounded sugar
- 1-1/4 oz. of isinglass per pint of juice
- water
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Pick the strawberries, put them into a pan, squeeze them well with a wooden spoon.
2. Add sufficient pounded sugar to sweeten them nicely, and let them remain for 1 hour, that the juice may be extracted.
3. Add 1/2 pint of water to every pint of juice.
4. Strain the strawberry-juice and water through a bag.
5. Measure it, and to every pint allow 1-1/4 oz. of isinglass, melted and clarified in 1/4 pint of water.
6. Mix this with the juice.
7. Add a little lemon-juice to improve the flavour if the fruit is very ripe, making sure it is well strained before adding to the other ingredients.
8. Put the jelly into a mould, and set the mould in ice.
