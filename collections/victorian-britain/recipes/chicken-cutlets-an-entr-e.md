## Ingredients

- 2 chickens
- salt to taste
- white pepper to taste
- cayenne to taste
- 2 blades of pounded mace
- egg
- bread crumbs
- clarified butter
- 1 strip of lemon-rind
- 2 carrots
- 1 onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls of mushroom ketchup
- butter and flour for thickening
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Remove the breast and leg bones of the chickens; cut the meat into neat pieces after having skinned it, and season the cutlets with pepper, salt, pounded mace, and cayenne.
2. Put the bones, trimmings, &c., into a stewpan with 1 pint of water, adding carrots, onions, and lemon-peel in the above proportion; stew gently for 1-1/2 hour, and strain the gravy.
3. Thicken it with butter and flour, add the ketchup and 1 egg well beaten; stir it over the fire, and bring it to the simmering-point, but do not allow it to boil.
4. In the mean time, egg and bread-crumb the cutlets, and give them a few drops of clarified butter; fry them a delicate brown, occasionally turning them.
5. Arrange them pyramidically on the dish, and pour over them the sauce.
