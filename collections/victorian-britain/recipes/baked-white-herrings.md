## Ingredients

- 12 herrings
- 4 bay-leaves
- 12 cloves
- 12 allspice
- 2 small blades of mace
- cayenne pepper and salt to taste
- sufficient vinegar to fill up the dish

## Directions

1. Take the herrings, cut off the heads, and gut them.
2. Put them in a pie-dish, heads and tails alternately, and between each layer, sprinkle over the above ingredients.
3. Cover the fish with the vinegar, and bake for 1/2 hour, but do not use it till quite cold.
4. The herrings may be cut down the front, the backbone taken out, and closed again.
