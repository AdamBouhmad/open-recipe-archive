## Ingredients

- 2 pecks of crystal gooseberries
- 6 gallons of water
- 12 lbs. of foots sugar of the coarsest brown quality

## Directions

1. Mash the gooseberries (which should be quite ripe) in a tub with a mallet
2. Put to them the water nearly milk-warm
3. Let this stand 24 hours
4. Strain it through a sieve and put the sugar to it
5. Mix it well and tun it
6. Stir the mixture from the bottom of the cask two or three times daily for three or four days to assist the melting of the sugar
7. Paste a piece of linen cloth over the bunghole
8. Set the cask in a warm place, but not in the sun
9. The following spring draw it off into stone bottles
10. The vinegar will be fit for use twelve months after it is made
