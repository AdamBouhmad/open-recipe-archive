## Ingredients

- 6 ripe apples
- 1 lemon
- 1/2 lb. of lump sugar
- 1/2 pint of water
- green angelica or candied citron

## Directions

1. Select the apples of a moderate size, peel them, cut them in halves, remove the cores, and rub each piece over with a little lemon
2. Put the sugar and water together into a lined saucepan, and let them boil until forming a thickish syrup
3. Lay in the apples with the rind of the lemon cut thin, and the juice of the same
4. Let the apples simmer till tender
5. Take them out very carefully, drain them on a sieve, and reduce the syrup by boiling it quickly for a few minutes
6. When both are cold, arrange the apples neatly on a glass dish, pour over the syrup, and garnish with strips of green angelica or candied citron
