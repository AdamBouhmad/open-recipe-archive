## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- the yolks of 8 eggs
- 6 oz. of sugar
- 1 oz. of isinglass
- essence of vanilla to taste

## Directions

1. Put the milk and sugar into a saucepan, and let it get hot over a slow fire.
2. Beat up the yolks of the eggs, to which add gradually the sweetened milk.
3. Flavour the whole with essence of vanilla, put the mixture into a jug, and place this jug in a saucepan of boiling water.
4. Stir the contents with a wooden spoon one way until the mixture thickens, but do not allow it to boil, or it will be full of lumps.
5. Take it off the fire; stir in the isinglass, which should be previously dissolved in about 1/4 pint of water, and boiled for 2 or 3 minutes.
6. Pour the cream into an oiled mould, put it in a cool place to set, and turn it out carefully on a dish.
7. Instead of using the essence of vanilla, a pod may be boiled in the milk instead, until the flavour is well extracted. A pod, or a pod and a half, will be found sufficient for the above proportion of ingredients.
