## Ingredients

- 6 heads of endive
- 1 pint of broth
- 3 oz. of fresh butter
- salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Wash and boil the endive as in the preceding recipe; chop it rather fine, and put into a stewpan with the broth.
2. Boil over a brisk fire until the sauce is all reduced.
3. Put in the butter, pepper, salt, and grated nutmeg (the latter must be very sparingly used).
4. Mix all well together, bring it to the boiling point, and serve very hot.
