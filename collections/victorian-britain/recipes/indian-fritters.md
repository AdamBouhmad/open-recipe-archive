## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- boiling water
- the yolks of 4 eggs
- the whites of 2
- hot lard or clarified dripping
- jam

## Directions

1. Put the flour into a basin, and pour over it sufficient boiling water to make it into a stiff paste, taking care to stir and beat it well, to prevent it getting lumpy
2. Leave it a little time to cool, and then break into it (without beating them at first) the yolks of 4 eggs and the whites of 2, and stir and beat all well together
3. Have ready some boiling lard or butter; drop a dessertspoonful of batter in at a time, and fry the fritters of a light brown
4. Serve on a dish, with a spoonful of preserve or marmalade dropped in between each fritter
