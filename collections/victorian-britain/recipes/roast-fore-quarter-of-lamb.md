## Ingredients

- Lamb
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Make up the fire in good time, that it may be clear and brisk when the joint is put down
2. Place it at a sufficient distance to prevent the fat from burning, and baste it constantly till the moment of serving
3. Lamb should be very thoroughly done without being dried up, and not the slightest appearance of red gravy should be visible
4. Serve with a little gravy made in the dripping-pan, the same as for other roasts, and send to table with it a tureen of mint sauce, No. 469, and a fresh salad
5. A cut lemon, a small piece of fresh butter, and a little cayenne, should also be placed on the table
