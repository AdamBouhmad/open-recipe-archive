## Ingredients

- 4 young vegetable marrows, or more, if very small
- 1/2 pint of cream
- salt and white pepper to taste
- 2 quarts of white stock, No. 107

## Directions

1. Pare and slice the marrows
2. Put them in the stock boiling
3. When done almost to a mash, press them through a sieve
4. At the moment of serving, add the boiling cream and seasoning
