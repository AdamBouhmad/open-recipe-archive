## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1/4 lb. of powdered lump sugar
- 4 eggs
- puff-paste
- a layer of any kind of jam

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. stir in the powdered sugar
3. whisk the eggs, and add these to the other ingredients
4. When these are well mixed, line a dish with puff-paste
5. spread over a layer of any kind of jam that may be preferred
6. pour in the mixture, and bake the pudding for rather more than 1/2 hour
