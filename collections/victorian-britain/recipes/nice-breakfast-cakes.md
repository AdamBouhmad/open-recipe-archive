## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of tartaric acid
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
- 1-1/2 breakfast-cupful of milk
- 1 oz. of sifted loaf sugar
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, tartaric acid, and salt well together, taking care that the two latter ingredients are reduced to the finest powder, and stir in the sifted sugar, which should also be very fine.
2. Dissolve the soda in the milk, add the eggs, which should be well whisked.
3. With this liquid work the flour, &c. into a light dough.
4. Divide it into small cakes, put them into the oven immediately, and bake for about 20 minutes.
