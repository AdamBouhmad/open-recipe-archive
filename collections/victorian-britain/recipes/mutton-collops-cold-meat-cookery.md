## Ingredients

- a few slices of a cold leg or loin of mutton
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- 1 small bunch of savoury herbs minced very fine
- 2 or 3 shalots
- 2 or 3 oz. of butter
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- 1/2 pint of gravy
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Cut some very thin slices from a leg or the chump end of a loin of mutton; sprinkle them with pepper, salt, pounded mace, minced savoury herbs, and minced shalot.
2. Fry them in butter, stir in a dessertspoonful of flour, add the gravy and lemon-juice, simmer very gently about 5 or 7 minutes, and serve immediately.
