## Ingredients

- 1/2 gallon of water
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- a very small piece of soda
- brussels sprouts
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Clean the sprouts from insects and nicely wash them
2. Pick off any dead or discoloured leaves from the outsides
3. Put them into a saucepan of boiling water, with salt and soda in the above proportion
4. Keep the pan uncovered and let them boil quickly over a brisk fire until tender
5. Drain and dish
6. Serve with a tureen of melted butter, or with a maître d'hôtel sauce poured over them
7. Alternatively, when dished, stir in about 1-1/2 oz. of butter and a seasoning of pepper and salt
