## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 6 oz. of pounded sugar
- the rind of 1 lemon
- 6 oz. of flour
- thick cartridge paper

## Directions

1. Break the eggs into a basin, separating the whites from the yolks
2. beat the yolks well, mix with them the pounded sugar and grated lemon-rind
3. beat these ingredients together for 1/4 hour
4. dredge in the flour gradually
5. when the whites of the eggs have been whisked to a solid froth, stir them to the flour, &c.
6. beat the mixture well for another 5 minutes
7. draw it along in strips upon thick cartridge paper to the proper size of the biscuit
8. bake them in rather a hot oven; but let them be carefully watched, as they are soon done, and a few seconds over the proper time will scorch and spoil them
