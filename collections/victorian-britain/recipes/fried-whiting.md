## Ingredients

- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- A little flour
- Hot lard or clarified dripping
- Fried parsley
- Shrimp sauce
- Plain melted butter

## Directions

1. Take off the skin, clean, and thoroughly wipe the fish free from all moisture
2. Fasten the tail in the mouth by means of a small skewer
3. Brush the fish over with egg, dredge with a little flour, and cover with bread crumbs
4. Fry them in hot lard or clarified dripping of a nice colour, and serve them on a napkin, garnished with fried parsley
5. Send them to table with shrimp sauce and plain melted butter
