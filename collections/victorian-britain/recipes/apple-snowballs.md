## Ingredients

- 2 teacupfuls of rice
- apples
- moist sugar
- cloves

## Directions

1. Boil the rice in milk until three-parts done; then strain it off.
2. Pare and core the apples without dividing them.
3. Put a small quantity of sugar and a clove into each apple.
4. Put the rice round them, and tie each ball separately in a cloth.
5. Boil until the apples are tender; then take them up, remove the cloths, and serve.
