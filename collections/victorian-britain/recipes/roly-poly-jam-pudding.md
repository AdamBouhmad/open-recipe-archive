## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb of suet-crust
- 3/4 lb. of any kind of jam

## Directions

1. Make a nice light suet-crust and roll it out to the thickness of about 1/2 inch
2. Spread the jam equally over it, leaving a small margin of paste without any, where the pudding joins
3. Roll it up, fasten the ends securely, and tie it in a floured cloth
4. Put the pudding into boiling water, and boil for 2 hours
