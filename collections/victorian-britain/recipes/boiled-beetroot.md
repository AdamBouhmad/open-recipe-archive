## Ingredients

- Beetroot
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Wash the beets thoroughly
2. Do not prick or break the skin before they are cooked, or they would lose their beautiful colour in boiling
3. Put them into boiling water and let them boil until tender, keeping them well covered
4. If to be served hot, remove the peel quickly, cut the beetroot into thick slices, and send to table with melted butter
5. For salads, pickle, etc., let the root cool, then peel and cut it into slices
