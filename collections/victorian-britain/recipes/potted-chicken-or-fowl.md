## Ingredients

- remains of cold roast chicken
- 1/4 lb. of fresh butter per lb. of meat
- salt and cayenne to taste
- 1 teaspoonful of pounded mace
- 1/4 small nutmeg

## Directions

1. Strip the meat from the bones of cold roast fowl
2. When it is freed from gristle and skin, weigh it
3. To every lb. of meat, allow the proportion of butter, seasoning, and spices
4. Cut the meat
