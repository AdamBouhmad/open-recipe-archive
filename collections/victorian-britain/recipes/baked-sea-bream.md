## Ingredients

- 1 bream
- salt, pepper, and cayenne to taste
- 1/4 lb. of butter

## Directions

1. Well wash the bream, but do not remove the scales, and wipe away all moisture with a nice dry cloth
2. Season it inside and out with salt, pepper, and cayenne
3. lay it in a baking-dish
4. Place the butter, in small pieces, upon the fish
5. bake for rather more than 1/2 an hour
