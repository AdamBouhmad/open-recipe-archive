## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 birds
- butter
- fried bread crumbs
- brown gravy
- bread-sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck and draw the birds, wipe them inside and out with damp cloths, and truss them in the following manner: Bring the head round under the wing, and the thighs close to the sides; pass a skewer through them and the body, and keep the legs straight.
2. Roast them before a clear fire, keep them well basted, and serve on fried bread crumbs, with a tureen of brown gravy.
3. When liked, bread-sauce may also be sent to table with them.
