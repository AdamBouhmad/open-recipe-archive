## Ingredients

- 9 gallons of water
- 27 lbs. of loaf sugar
- 9 lemons
- 12 oz. of bruised ginger
- 3 tablespoonfuls of yeast
- 2 lbs. of raisins stoned and chopped
- 1 pint of brandy

## Directions

1. Boil together for 1 hour in a copper the water, sugar, lemon-rinds, and bruised ginger
2. Remove every particle of scum as it rises
3. Put it into a large tub or pan when sufficiently boiled
4. When nearly cold, add the yeast, which must be thick and very fresh
5. The next day, put all in a dry cask with the strained lemon-juice and chopped raisins
6. Stir the wine every day for a fortnight
7. Add the brandy
8. Stop the cask down by degrees
9. In a few weeks it will be fit to bottle
