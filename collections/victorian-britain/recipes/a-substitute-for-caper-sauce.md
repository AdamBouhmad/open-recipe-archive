## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of melted butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cut parsley
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 tablespoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil the parsley slowly to let it become a bad colour; cut, but do not chop it fine.
2. Add it to 1/2 pint of smoothly-made melted butter, with salt and vinegar in the above proportions.
3. Boil up and serve.
