## Ingredients

- 16 to 20 mushroom-flaps
- butter
- pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cut off a portion of the stalk, peel the top, and wipe the mushrooms carefully with a piece of flannel and a little fine salt
2. Put them into a tin baking-dish, with a very small piece of butter placed on each mushroom
3. Sprinkle over a little pepper, and let them bake for about 20 minutes, or longer should the mushrooms be very large
4. Have ready a very hot dish, pile the mushrooms high in the centre, pour the gravy round, and send them to table quickly, with very hot plates
