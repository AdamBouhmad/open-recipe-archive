## Ingredients

- A few slices of rich cheese
- toast
- mustard
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut some nice rich sound cheese into rather thin slices; melt it in a cheese-toaster on a hot plate, or over steam.
2. When melted, add a small quantity of mixed mustard and a seasoning of pepper; stir the cheese until it is completely dissolved.
3. Brown it before the fire, or with a salamander.
4. Fill the bottom of the cheese-toaster with hot water, and serve with dry or buttered toasts, whichever may be preferred.
5. If the cheese be not very rich, a few pieces of butter may be mixed with it to great advantage.
6. Serve the mixture very hot, and very quickly send to table.
