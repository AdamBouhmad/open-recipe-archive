## Ingredients

- 1 small carrot
- a small faggot of sweet herbs, including parsley
- 1 onion
- 5 or 6 mushrooms (when obtainable)
- 1 bay-leaf
- 6 cloves
- 1 blade of mace
- 2 oz. of butter
- 1 glass of sherry
- 1-1/2 pint of white stock, No. 107
- thickening of butter and flour
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Cut up the onion and carrot into small rings
2. Put them into a stewpan with the herbs, mushrooms, bay-leaf, cloves, and mace
3. Add the butter
4. Simmer the whole very gently over a slow fire until the onion is quite tender
5. Pour in the stock and sherry
6. Stew slowly for 1 hour
7. Strain it off into a clean saucepan
8. Make a thickening of butter and flour and put it to the sauce
9. Stir it over the fire until perfectly smooth and mellow
10. Add the lemon-juice
11. Give one boil and it will be ready for table
