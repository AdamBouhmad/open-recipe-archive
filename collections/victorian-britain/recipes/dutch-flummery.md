## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 oz. of isinglass
- the rind and juice of 1 lemon
- 1 pint of water
- 4 eggs
- 1 pint of sherry, Madeira, or raisin-wine
- sifted sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Put the water, isinglass, and lemon-rind into a lined saucepan, and simmer gently until the isinglass is dissolved
2. Strain this into a basin
3. Stir in the eggs, which should be well beaten, the lemon-juice, which should be strained, and the wine
4. Sweeten to taste with pounded sugar
5. Mix all well together
6. Pour it into a jug, set this jug in a saucepan of boiling water over the fire, and keep stirring it one way until it thickens, but take care that it does not boil
7. Strain it into a mould that has been oiled or laid in water for a short time, and put it in a cool place to set
8. A tablespoonful of brandy stirred in just before it is poured into the mould improves the flavour of this dish
