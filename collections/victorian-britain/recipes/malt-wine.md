## Ingredients

- 5 gallons of water
- 28 lbs. of sugar
- 6 quarts of sweet-wort
- 6 quarts of tun
- 3 lbs. of raisins
- 1/2 lb. of candy
- 1 pint of brandy

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water together for 10 minutes
2. Skim it well, and put the liquor into a convenient-sized pan or tub
3. Allow it to cool
4. Mix it with the sweet-wort and tun
5. Let it stand for 3 days
6. Put it into a barrel; here it will work or ferment for another three days or more
7. Bung up the cask, and keep it undisturbed for 2 or 3 months
8. After this, add the raisins (whole), the candy, and brandy
9. In 6 months' time, bottle the wine off
