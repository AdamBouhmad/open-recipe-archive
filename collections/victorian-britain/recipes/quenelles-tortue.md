## Ingredients

- veal leg
- bread crumbs
- milk
- calf's udder
- butter
- egg yolks
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- spices
- eggs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take out the fleshy part of a leg of veal about 1 lb.
2. Scrape off all the meat without leaving any sinews or fat
3. Soak in milk about the same quantity of crumbs of bread
4. When the bread is well soaked squeeze it and put it into a mortar with the veal
5. Add a small quantity of calf's udder a little butter the yolks of 4 eggs boiled hard a little cayenne pepper salt and spices
6. Pound the whole very fine
7. Thicken the mixture with 2 whole eggs and the yolk of another
8. Try this farce or stuffing in boiling-hot water to ascertain its consistency
9. If it is too thin add the yolk of an egg
10. Take half of it and put into it some chopped parsley
11. Let the whole cool in order to roll it of the size of the yolk of an egg
12. Poach it in salt and boiling water
13. When very hard drain on a sieve and put it into the turtle
