## Ingredients

- 2 large mackerel
- 1 oz. butter
- 1 small bunch of chopped herbs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of medium stock
- 3 tablespoonfuls of béchamel
- salt, cayenne, and lemon-juice to taste

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, and fillet it; scald the herbs, chop them fine, and put them with the butter and stock into a stewpan.
2. Lay in the mackerel, and simmer very gently for 10 minutes; take them out, and put them on a hot dish.
3. Dredge in a little flour, add the other ingredients, give one boil, and pour it over the mackerel.
