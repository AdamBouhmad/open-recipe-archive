## Ingredients

- 100 crayfish
- pounded mace
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 oz. butter

## Directions

1. Boil the fish in salt and water; pick out all the meat and pound it in a mortar to a paste.
2. Whilst pounding, add the butter gradually, and mix in the spice and seasoning.
3. Put it in small pots, and pour over it clarified butter, carefully excluding the air.
