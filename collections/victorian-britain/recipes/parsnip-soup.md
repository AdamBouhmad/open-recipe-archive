## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of sliced parsnips
- 2 oz. of butter
- salt and cayenne to taste
- 1 quart of stock No. 106

## Directions

1. Put the parsnips into the stewpan with the butter, which has been previously melted, and simmer them till quite tender.
2. Then add nearly a pint of stock, and boil together for half an hour.
3. Pass all through a fine strainer, and put to it the remainder of the stock.
4. Season, boil, and serve immediately.
