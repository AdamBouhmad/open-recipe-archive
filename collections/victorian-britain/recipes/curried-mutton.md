## Ingredients

- The remains of any joint of cold mutton
- 2 onions
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1 dessertspoonful of curry powder
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- salt to taste
- 1/4 pint of stock or water

## Directions

1. Slice the onions in thin rings
2. Put them into a stewpan with the butter
3. Fry of a light brown
4. Stir in the curry powder, flour, and salt
5. Mix all well together
6. Cut the meat into nice thin slices (or mince if insufficient)
7. Add it to the other ingredients
8. When well browned, add the stock or gravy
9. Stew gently for about 1/2 hour
10. Serve in a dish with a border of boiled rice, the same as for other curries
