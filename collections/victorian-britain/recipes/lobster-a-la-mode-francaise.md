## Ingredients

- 1 lobster
- 4 tablespoonfuls of white stock
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cream
- pounded mace
- cayenne to taste
- bread crumbs
- butter
- puff-paste

## Directions

1. Pick the meat from the shell, and cut it up into small square pieces
2. Put the stock, cream, and seasoning into a stewpan, add the lobster, and let it simmer gently for 6 minutes
3. Serve it in the shell, which must be nicely cleaned, and have a border of puff-paste
4. Cover it with bread crumbs, place small pieces of butter over, and brown before the fire, or with a salamander
