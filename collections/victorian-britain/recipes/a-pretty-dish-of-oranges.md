## Ingredients

- 6 large oranges
- 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1/4 pint of water
- 1/2 pint of cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls of any kind of liqueur
- sugar to taste
- salad-oil

## Directions

1. Put the sugar and water into a saucepan and boil them until the sugar becomes brittle
2. Ascertain the sugar is sufficiently boiled by taking up a small quantity in a spoon and dipping it in cold water
3. Peel the oranges and remove as much of the white pith as possible
4. Divide them into nice-sized slices, without breaking the thin white skin which surrounds the juicy pulp
5. Place the pieces of orange on small skewers
6. Dip them into the hot sugar
7. Arrange them in layers round a plain mould, which should be well oiled with the purest salad-oil
8. Line the sides of the mould only with the oranges and leave the centre open for the cream
9. Let the sugar become firm by cooling
10. Turn the oranges carefully out on a dish
11. Fill the centre with whipped cream flavoured with any kind of liqueur and sweetened with pounded sugar
