## Ingredients

- The gristles from 2 breasts of veal
- stock No. 107
- 1 faggot of savoury herbs
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- 4 cloves
- 2 carrots
- 2 onions
- a strip of lemon-peel
- egg and bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of chopped mushrooms
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sherry
- the yolk of 1 egg
- 3 tablespoonfuls of cream

## Directions

1. After removing the gristles from a breast of veal, stew them for 4 hours, as in the preceding recipe, with stock, herbs, mace, cloves, carrots, onions, and lemon-peel.
2. When perfectly tender, lift them out and remove any bones or hard parts remaining.
3. Put them between two dishes, with a weight on the top, and when cold, cut them into slices.
4. Brush these over with egg, sprinkle with bread crumbs, and fry a pale brown.
5. Take 1/2 pint of the gravy they were boiled in, add 2 tablespoonfuls of chopped mushrooms, a seasoning of salt and pepper, the sherry, and the yolk of an egg beaten with 3 tablespoonfuls of cream.
6. Stir the sauce over the fire until it thickens; when it is on the point of boiling, dish the tendrons in a circle, and pour the sauce in the middle.
