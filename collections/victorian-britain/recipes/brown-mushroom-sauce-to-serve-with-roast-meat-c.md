## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of button mushrooms
- 1/2 pint of good beef gravy
- 1 tablespoonful of mushroom ketchup
- thickening of butter and flour

## Directions

1. Put the gravy into a saucepan, thicken it, and stir over the fire until it boils
2. Prepare the mushrooms by cutting off the stalks and wiping them free from grit and dirt
3. Put them into the gravy, and let them simmer very gently for about 10 minutes
4. Add the ketchup, and serve
