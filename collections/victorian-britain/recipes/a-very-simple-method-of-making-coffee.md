## Ingredients

- 1/2 oz. or 1 tablespoonful of coffee per person
- 1 pint of water per oz. of coffee

## Directions

1. Have a small iron ring made to fit the top of the coffee-pot inside
2. To this ring sew a small muslin bag
3. Fit the bag into the pot and pour some boiling water in it
4. When the pot is well warmed, put the ground coffee into the bag
5. Pour over as much boiling water as is required and close the lid
6. When all the water has filtered through, remove the bag and send the coffee to table
7. Pour the water on the coffee gradually so that the infusion may be stronger
8. The bag must be well made so that none of the grounds escape through the seams
