## Ingredients

- Any remains of cold fish, such as cod or haddock
- 2 dozen oysters
- pepper and salt to taste
- bread crumbs sufficient for the quantity of fish
- 1/2 teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful of finely-chopped parsley
- made melted butter or a little thin white sauce
- oyster-liquor

## Directions

1. Clear the fish from the bones, and put a layer of it in a pie-dish, which sprinkle with pepper and salt
2. Then a layer of bread crumbs, oysters, nutmeg, and chopped parsley
3. Repeat this till the dish is quite full
4. You may form a covering either of bread crumbs, which should be browned, or puff-paste, which should be cut into long strips, and laid in cross-bars over the fish, with a line of the paste first laid round the edge
5. Before putting on the top, pour in some made melted butter, or a little thin white sauce, and the oyster-liquor, and bake
