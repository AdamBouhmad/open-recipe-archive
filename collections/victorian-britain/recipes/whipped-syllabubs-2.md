## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of cream
- 1/4 pint of sherry
- 1/8 pint of brandy
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- grated nutmeg
- 3 oz. of pounded sugar
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients together
2. Put the syllabub into glasses
3. Heap a little whipped cream made in the same manner on the top of them
