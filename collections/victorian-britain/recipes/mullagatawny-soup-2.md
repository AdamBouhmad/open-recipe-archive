## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of curry powder
- 6 onions
- 1 clove of garlic
- 1 oz. of pounded almonds
- a little lemon-pickle, or mango-juice, to taste
- 1 fowl or rabbit
- 4 slices of lean bacon
- 2 quarts of medium stock, or, if wanted very good, best stock

## Directions

1. Slice and fry the onions of a nice colour; line the stewpan with the bacon.
2. Cut up the rabbit or fowl into small joints, and slightly brown them.
3. Put in the fried onions, the garlic, and stock, and simmer gently till the meat is tender; skim very carefully.
4. When the meat is done, rub the curry powder to a smooth batter; add it to the soup with the almonds, which must be first pounded with a little of the stock.
5. Put in seasoning and lemon-pickle or mango-juice to taste, and serve boiled rice with it.
