## Ingredients

- 2 pints of broad beans
- 1/2 pint of stock or broth
- a small bunch of savoury herbs, including parsley
- a small lump of sugar
- yolk of 1 egg
- 1/4 pint of cream
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Procure some young and freshly-gathered beans and shell sufficient to make 2 pints
2. Boil them until nearly done
3. Drain them and put them into a stewpan with the stock, finely-minced herbs, and sugar
4. Stew the beans until perfectly tender and the liquor has dried away a little
5. Beat up the yolk of an egg with the cream and add this to the beans
6. Let the whole get thoroughly hot and when on the point of simmering, serve
