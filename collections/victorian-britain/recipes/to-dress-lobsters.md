## Ingredients

- boiled lobster
- salad-oil
- parsley

## Directions

1. Rub the lobster over with a little salad-oil, which wipe off again
2. Separate the body from the tail
3. Break off the great claws, and crack them at the joints, without injuring the meat
4. Split the tail in halves, and arrange all neatly in a dish, with the body upright in the middle
5. Garnish with parsley
