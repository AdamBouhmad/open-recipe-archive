## Ingredients

- bones
- a small piece of common paste
- a floured cloth
- dry toast

## Directions

1. Have the bones neatly sawed into convenient sizes, and cover the ends with a small piece of common crust, made with flour and water.
2. Over this tie a floured cloth, and place them upright in a saucepan of boiling water, taking care there is sufficient to cover the bones.
3. Boil them for 2 hours, remove the cloth and paste, and serve them upright on a napkin with dry toast.
