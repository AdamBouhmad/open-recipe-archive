## Ingredients

- A large fowl
- forcemeat No. 417
- a little flour
- brown gravy
- bread sauce

## Directions

1. Select a large plump fowl and fill the breast with forcemeat, made by recipe No. 417.
2. Truss it firmly, the same as for a plain roast fowl.
3. Dredge it with flour, and put it down to a bright fire.
4. Roast it for nearly or quite an hour, should it be very large.
5. Remove the skewers, and serve with a good brown gravy and a tureen of bread sauce.
