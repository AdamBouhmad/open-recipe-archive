## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of the pulp of apples
- 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar
- 6 oz. of butter
- the rind of 1 lemon
- 6 eggs
- puff-paste

## Directions

1. Peel, core, and cut the apples, as for sauce
2. Put them into a stewpan, with only just sufficient water to prevent them from burning
3. Let them stew until reduced to a pulp
4. Weigh the pulp
5. To every 1/2 lb. add sifted sugar, grated lemon-rind, and 6 well-beaten eggs
6. Beat these ingredients well together
7. Melt the butter
8. Stir it to the other things
9. Put a border of puff-paste round the dish
10. Bake for rather more than 1/2 hour
