## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1/4 lb. of butter or lard
- 1/2 lb. of currants
- 1 teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
- the whites of 4 eggs
- 1/2 pint of milk

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Well whisk the whites of the eggs
3. Stir all the ingredients together but the soda
4. Add the soda when all is well mixed and the cake is ready to be put into the oven
5. After the mixture has been well beaten, stir in the soda
6. Put the cake into a buttered mould
7. Bake it in a moderate oven for 1-1/2 hour
