## Ingredients

- 1/2 oz. of ground coffee
- 1 pint of milk

## Directions

1. Let the coffee be freshly ground; put it into a saucepan, with the milk, which should be made nearly boiling before the coffee is put in, and boil both together for 3 minutes
2. Clear it by pouring some of it into a cup, and then back again, and leave it on the hob for a few minutes to settle thoroughly
3. This coffee may be made still more nutritious by the addition of an egg well beaten, and put into the coffee-cup
