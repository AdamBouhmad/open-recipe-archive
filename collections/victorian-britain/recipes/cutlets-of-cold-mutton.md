## Ingredients

- The remains of cold loin or neck of mutton
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs
- brown gravy (No. 436) or tomato sauce (No. 529)

## Directions

1. Cut the remains of cold loin or neck of mutton into cutlets
2. Trim them and take away a portion of the fat, should there be too much
3. Dip them in beaten egg
4. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
5. Fry them a nice brown in hot dripping
6. Arrange them on a dish
7. Pour round them either a good gravy or hot tomato sauce
