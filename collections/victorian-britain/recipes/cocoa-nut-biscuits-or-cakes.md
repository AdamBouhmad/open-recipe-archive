## Ingredients

- 10 oz. of sifted sugar
- 3 eggs
- 6 oz. of grated cocoa-nut

## Directions

1. Whisk the eggs until they are very light.
2. Add the sugar gradually.
3. Stir in the cocoa-nut.
4. Roll a tablespoonful of the paste at a time in your hands in the form of a pyramid.
5. Place the pyramids on paper, put the paper on tins.
6. Bake the biscuits in rather a cool oven until they are just coloured a light brown.
