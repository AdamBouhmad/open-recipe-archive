## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of chopped suet
- 1/4 lb. of currants
- 1/4 lb. of raisins
- 1 tablespoonful of moist sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of ground ginger
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients into a basin, having previously stoned the raisins, and washed, picked, and dried the currants.
2. Mix well with a clean knife.
3. Dip the pudding-cloth into boiling water, wring it out, and put in the mixture.
4. Have ready a saucepan of boiling water, plunge in the pudding, and boil for 3 hours.
5. Turn it out on the dish, and serve with sifted sugar.
