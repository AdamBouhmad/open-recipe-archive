## Ingredients

- 6 heads of celery
- to each 1/2 gallon of water allow 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- 1/3 pint of cream

## Directions

1. Wash the celery thoroughly; trim, and boil it in salt and water until tender.
2. Put the cream and pounded mace into a stewpan; shake it over the fire until the cream thickens.
3. Dish the celery, pour over the sauce, and serve.
