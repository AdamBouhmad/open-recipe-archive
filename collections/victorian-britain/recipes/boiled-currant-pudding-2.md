## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of suet
- 1/2 lb. of currants
- milk
- lemon
- cold butter
- sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the currants, dry them thoroughly, and pick away any stalks or grit
2. Chop the suet finely
3. Mix all the ingredients together, and moisten with sufficient milk to make the pudding into a stiff batter
4. Tie it up in a floured cloth, put it into boiling water, and boil for 3-1/2 hours
5. Serve with a cut lemon, cold butter, and sifted sugar
