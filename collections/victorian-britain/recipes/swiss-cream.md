## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of macaroons or 6 small sponge-cakes
- sherry
- 1 pint of cream
- 5 oz. of lump sugar
- 2 large tablespoonfuls of arrowroot
- the rind of 1 lemon
- the juice of 1/2 lemon
- 3 tablespoonfuls of milk

## Directions

1. Lay the macaroons or sponge-cakes in a glass dish, and pour over them as much sherry as will cover them, or sufficient to soak them well.
2. Put the cream into a lined saucepan, with the sugar and lemon-rind, and let it remain by the side of the fire until the cream is well flavoured, when take out the lemon-rind.
3. Mix the arrowroot smoothly with the cold milk.
4. Add this to the cream, and let it boil gently for about 3 minutes, keeping it well stirred.
5. Take it off the fire, stir till nearly cold, when add the lemon-juice, and pour the whole over the cakes.
6. Garnish the cream with strips of angelica, or candied citron cut thin, or bright-coloured jelly or preserve.
