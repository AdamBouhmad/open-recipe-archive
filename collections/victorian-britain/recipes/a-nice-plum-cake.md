## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 lb. of currants
- 2 oz. of candied lemon-peel
- 1/2 pint of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of ammonia or carbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Put the flour into a basin with the sugar, currants, and sliced candied peel
2. Beat the butter to a cream, and mix all these ingredients together with the milk
3. Stir the ammonia into 2 tablespoonfuls of milk and add it to the dough
4. Beat the whole well, until everything is thoroughly mixed
5. Put the dough into a buttered tin, and bake the cake from 1-1/2 to 2 hours
