## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of coffee
- a piece of butter the size of a nut
- a dessert-spoonful of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Before roasting, add to every 3 lbs. of coffee a piece of butter the size of a nut and a dessert-spoonful of powdered sugar
2. Roast in the usual manner
3. The addition of the butter and sugar develops the flavour and aroma of the berry; the quality of the butter must be of the very best description
