## Ingredients

- Endive
- mustard-and-cress
- boiled beetroot
- 3 or 4 hard-boiled eggs
- celery

## Directions

1. Shred the celery into thin pieces, after having carefully washed and cut away all wormeaten pieces.
2. Cleanse the endive and mustard-and-cress free from grit, and arrange these high in the centre of a salad-bowl or dish.
3. Garnish with the hard-boiled eggs and beetroot, both of which should be cut in slices.
4. Pour into the dish, but not over the salad, either of the sauces No. 506, 507, or 508.
5. Never dress a salad long before it is required for table, as, by standing, it loses its freshness and pretty crisp and light appearance; the sauce, however, may always be prepared a few hours beforehand, and when required for use, the herbs laid lightly over it.
