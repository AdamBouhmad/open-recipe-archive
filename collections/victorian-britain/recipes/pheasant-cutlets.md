## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 pheasants
- egg and bread crumbs
- cayenne and salt to taste
- brown gravy
- stock
- herbs
- vegetables
- seasoning

## Directions

1. Procure 3 young pheasants that have been hung a few days
2. Pluck, draw, and wipe them inside
3. Cut them into joints
4. Remove the bones from the best of these
5. Put the backbones, trimmings, &c., into a stewpan, with a little stock, herbs, vegetables, seasoning, &c., to make the gravy
6. Flatten and trim the cutlets of a good shape
7. Egg and bread crumb them
8. Broil them over a clear fire
9. Pile them high in the dish
10. Pour under them the gravy made from the bones, which should be strained, flavoured, and thickened
11. Stick one of the small bones on the point of each cutlet
