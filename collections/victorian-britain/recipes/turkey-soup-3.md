## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of medium stock
- remains of a cold roast turkey
- 2 oz. of rice-flour or arrowroot
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of Harvey's sauce or mushroom ketchup

## Directions

1. Cut up the turkey in small pieces, and put it in the stock
2. let it simmer slowly until the bones are quite clean
3. Take the bones out, and work the soup through a sieve
4. when cool, skim well
5. Mix the rice-flour or arrowroot to a batter with a little of the soup
6. add it with the seasoning and sauce, or ketchup
7. Give one boil, and serve
