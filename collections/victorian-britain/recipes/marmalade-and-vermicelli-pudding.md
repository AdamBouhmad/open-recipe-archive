## Ingredients

- 1 breakfastcupful of vermicelli
- 2 tablespoonfuls of marmalade
- 1/4 lb. of raisins
- sugar to taste
- 3 eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Pour some boiling milk on the vermicelli, and let it remain covered for 10 minutes
2. then mix with it the marmalade, stoned raisins, sugar, and beaten eggs
3. Stir all well together
4. put the mixture into a buttered mould
5. boil for 1-1/2 hour, and serve with custard sauce
