## Ingredients

- 1 oz. of whole black pepper
- 1/2 oz. of allspice
- 1 oz. of salt
- 1/2 oz. grated horseradish
- 1/2 oz. of pickled shalots
- 1 pint of mushroom ketchup or walnut pickle

## Directions

1. Pound all the ingredients finely in a mortar, and put them into the ketchup or walnut-liquor
2. Let them stand for a fortnight, when strain off the liquor and bottle for use
3. Either pour a little of the sauce over the steaks or mix it in the gravy
