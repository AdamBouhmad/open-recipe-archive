## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of scraped horseradish
- 1 oz. of minced shalot
- 1 drachm of cayenne
- 1 quart of vinegar

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients into a bottle, which shake well every day for a fortnight
2. When it is thoroughly steeped, strain and bottle
3. It will be fit for use immediately
