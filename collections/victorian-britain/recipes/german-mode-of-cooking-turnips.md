## Ingredients

- 8 large turnips
- 3 oz. of butter
- pepper and salt to taste
- rather more than 1/2 pint of weak stock or broth
- 1 tablespoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Make the butter hot in a stewpan, lay in the turnips, after having pared and cut them into dice, and season them with pepper and salt
2. Toss them over the fire for a few minutes, then add the broth
3. Simmer the whole gently till the turnips are tender
4. Brown the above proportion of flour with a little butter
5. Add this to the turnips, let them simmer another 5 minutes, and serve
