## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 cauliflowers
- rather more than 1/2 pint of white sauce
- 2 tablespoonfuls of grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 oz. of fresh butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cleanse and boil the cauliflowers by recipe No. 1104, and drain them
2. Dish them with the flowers standing upright
3. Have ready the white sauce
4. Pour sufficient of it over the cauliflowers just to cover the top
5. Sprinkle over this some rasped Parmesan cheese and bread crumbs
6. Drop on these the melted butter
7. Brown with a salamander, or before the fire
8. Pour round, but not over, the flowers the remainder of the sauce
