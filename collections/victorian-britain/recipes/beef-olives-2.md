## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of rump-steak
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoonful of minced savoury herbs
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1 pint of stock
- 2 or 3 slices of bacon
- 2 tablespoonfuls of any store sauce
- butter and flour for thickening

## Directions

1. Have the steaks cut rather thin, slightly beat them to make them level, cut them into 6 or 7 pieces
2. Brush over with egg, and sprinkle with herbs, which should be very finely minced
3. Season with pepper and salt, and roll up the pieces tightly, and fasten with a small skewer
4. Put the stock in a stewpan that will exactly hold them
5. Lay in the rolls of meat, cover them with the bacon, cut in thin slices, and over that put a piece of paper
6. Stew them very gently for full 2 hours
7. Take them out, remove the skewers, thicken the gravy with butter and flour, and flavour with any store sauce that may be preferred
8. Give one boil, pour over the meat, and serve
