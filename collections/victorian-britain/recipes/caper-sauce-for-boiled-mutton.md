## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of melted butter
- 3 tablespoonfuls of capers or nasturtiums
- 1 tablespoonful of their liquor

## Directions

1. Chop the capers twice or thrice, and add them, with their liquor, to 1/2 pint of melted butter, made very smoothly.
2. Keep stirring well.
3. Let the sauce just simmer, and serve in a tureen.
