## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 6 oz. of finely-chopped suet
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt
- 1/2 saltspoonful of pepper
- 1/2 pint of milk or water

## Directions

1. Chop the suet very finely, after freeing it from skin, and mix it well with the flour
2. Add the salt and pepper (this latter ingredient may be omitted if the flavour is not liked)
3. Make the whole into a smooth paste with the above proportion of milk or water
4. Tie the pudding in a floured cloth, or put it into a buttered basin
5. Boil from 2-1/2 to 3 hours
