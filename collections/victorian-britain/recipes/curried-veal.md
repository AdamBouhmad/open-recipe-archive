## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast veal
- 4 onions
- 2 apples sliced
- 1 tablespoonful of curry-powder
- 1 dessertspoonful of flour
- 1/2 pint of broth or water
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- butter
- boiled rice
- pickles, capsicums, and gherkins

## Directions

1. Slice the onions and apples, and fry them in a little butter; then take them out, cut the meat into neat cutlets, and fry these of a pale brown.
2. Add the curry-powder and flour, put in the onion, apples, and a little broth or water, and stew gently till quite tender; add the lemon-juice, and serve with an edging of boiled rice.
3. The curry may be ornamented with pickles, capsicums, and gherkins arranged prettily on the top.
