## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of turpentine
- 1 tablespoonful of sweet oil
- emery powder

## Directions

1. Mix the turpentine and sweet oil together, stirring in sufficient emery powder to make the mixture of the thickness of cream.
2. Put it on the article with a piece of soft flannel, rub off quickly with another piece, then polish with a little dry emery powder and clean leather.
