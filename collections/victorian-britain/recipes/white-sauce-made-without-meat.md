## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of butter
- 2 small onions
- 1 carrot
- 1/2 a small teacupful of flour
- 1 pint of new milk
- salt and cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Cut up the onions and carrot very small, and put them into a stewpan with the butter
2. Simmer them till the butter is nearly dried up
3. Then stir in the flour, and add the milk
4. Boil the whole gently until it thickens
5. Strain it, season with salt and cayenne, and it will be ready to serve
