## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- salt and water
- 1 tablespoonful of minced parsley to every 6 potatoes
- 2 oz. of butter
- pepper and salt to taste
- 4 tablespoonfuls of gravy
- 2 tablespoonfuls of lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes clean, and boil them in salt and water by recipe No. 1138
2. When they are done, drain them, let them cool
3. Peel and cut the potatoes into thick slices
4. Put the butter into a stewpan with the pepper, salt, gravy, and parsley; mix these ingredients well together
5. Put in the potatoes, shake them two or three times, that they may be well covered with the sauce
6. When quite hot through, squeeze in the lemon-juice, and serve
