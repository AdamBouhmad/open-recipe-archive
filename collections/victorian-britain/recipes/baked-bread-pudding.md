## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of grated bread
- 1 pint of milk
- 4 eggs
- 4 oz. of butter
- 4 oz. of moist sugar
- 2 oz. of candied peel
- 6 bitter almonds
- 1 tablespoonful of brandy

## Directions

1. Put the milk into a stewpan, with the bitter almonds
2. let it infuse for 1/4 hour
3. bring it to the boiling point
4. strain it on to the bread crumbs, and let these remain till cold
5. then add the eggs, which should be well whisked, the butter, sugar, and brandy, and beat the pudding well until all the ingredients are thoroughly mixed
6. line the bottom of a pie-dish with the candied peel sliced thin, put in the mixture, and bake for nearly 3/4 hour
