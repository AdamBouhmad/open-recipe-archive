## Ingredients

- ears of young and green Indian wheat
- 1/2 gallon of water
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- melted butter
- toast

## Directions

1. Take off the outside sheath and remove the waving fibres from the ears
2. Place the ears in boiling water, where they should remain for about 25 minutes (a longer time may be necessary for larger ears than ordinary)
3. When sufficiently boiled and well drained, send them to table whole, and with a piece of toast underneath them
4. Serve melted butter with them
