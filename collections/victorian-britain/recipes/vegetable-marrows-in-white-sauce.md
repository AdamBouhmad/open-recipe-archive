## Ingredients

- 4 or 5 moderate-sized marrows
- 1/2 pint of white sauce

## Directions

1. Pare the marrows
2. Cut them in halves, and shape each half at the top in a point, leaving the bottom end flat for it to stand upright in the dish
3. Boil the marrows in salt and water until tender
4. Take them up very carefully, and arrange them on a hot dish
5. Have ready 1/2 pint of white sauce, made by recipe No. 539
6. Pour this over the marrows, and serve
