## Ingredients

- 1 oz. of isinglass
- 6 large oranges
- 1 lemon
- sugar to taste
- water
- 1/2 pint of good cream

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from the oranges and lemon and strain it
2. Put it into a saucepan with the isinglass and sufficient water to make in all 1-1/2 pint
3. Rub the sugar on the orange and lemon-rind and add it to the other ingredients
4. Boil all together for about 10 minutes
5. Strain through a muslin bag
6. When cold, beat up with it 1/2 pint of thick cream
7. Wet a mould or soak it in cold water
8. Pour in the cream and put it in a cool place to set
