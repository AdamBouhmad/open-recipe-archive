## Ingredients

- 1 oz. of fresh butter
- 1 oz. of suet
- 1 oz. of fat bacon
- 1 small teaspoonful of minced savoury herbs, including parsley
- a little onion, when liked, shredded very fine
- salt, nutmeg, and cayenne to taste
- 4 oz. of bread crumbs
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients well together, carefully mincing them very finely
2. beat up the egg, moisten with it, and work the whole very smoothly together
3. Oysters or anchovies may be added to this forcemeat, and will be found a great improvement
