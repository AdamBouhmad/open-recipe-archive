## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of the scrag end of the neck of mutton
- 1 onion
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- 4 turnip
- 1/2 pints of water
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put the mutton into a stewpan
2. pour over the water cold and add the other ingredients
3. When it boils, skim it very carefully, cover the pan closely, and let it simmer very gently for an hour
4. strain it, let it cool, take off all the fat from the surface, and warm up as much as may be required, adding, if the patient be allowed to take it, a teaspoonful of minced parsley which has been previously scalded
