## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of raisins
- a few thin slices of bread and butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- sugar to taste
- 1/4 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Butter a pudding-basin, and line the inside with a layer of raisins that have been previously stoned
2. Nearly fill the basin with slices of bread and butter with the crust cut off
3. In another basin, beat the eggs; add to them the milk, sugar, and grated nutmeg; mix all well together
4. Pour the whole on to the bread and butter; let it stand 1/2 hour
5. Tie a floured cloth over it; boil for 1 hour, and serve with sweet sauce
6. Care must be taken that the basin is quite full before the cloth is tied over
