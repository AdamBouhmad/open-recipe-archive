## Ingredients

- 1 pint of shelled shrimps
- 1/4 lb. of fresh butter
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- cayenne to taste
- nutmeg (optional, when liked)

## Directions

1. Have ready a pint of picked shrimps
2. put them, with the other ingredients, into a stewpan
3. let them heat gradually in the butter, but do not let it boil
4. Pour into small pots
5. when cold, cover
