## Ingredients

- 1 crab
- salt to taste
- pounded mace to taste
- cayenne to taste
- 1/2 pint of melted butter made with milk

## Directions

1. Choose a nice fresh crab
2. Pick all the meat away from the shell
3. Cut it into small square pieces
4. Make 1/2 pint of melted butter by recipe No. 380
5. Put in the fish and seasoning
6. Let it gradually warm through
7. Simmer for 2 minutes
8. It should not boil
