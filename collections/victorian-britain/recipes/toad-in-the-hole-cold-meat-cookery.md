## Ingredients

- 6 oz. of flour
- 1 pint of milk
- 3 eggs
- butter
- a few slices of cold mutton
- pepper and salt to taste
- 2 kidneys

## Directions

1. Make a smooth batter of flour, milk, and eggs in the above proportion
2. butter a baking-dish, and pour in the batter
3. Into this place a few slices of cold mutton, previously well seasoned, and the kidneys, which should be cut into rather small pieces
4. bake about 1 hour, or rather longer, and send it to table in the dish it was baked in
5. Oysters or mushrooms may be substituted for the kidneys, and will be found exceedingly good
