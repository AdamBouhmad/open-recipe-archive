## Ingredients

- 2 large onions
- 1 large carrot
- 2 oz. of butter
- 3 pints of boiling water
- 1 bunch of savoury herbs
- a wineglassful of good beer
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Slice, flour, and fry the onions and carrots in the butter until of a nice light-brown colour
2. Add the boiling water and the remaining ingredients
3. Let the whole stew gently for about an hour
4. Strain, and when cold, skim off all the fat
5. Thicken it in the same manner as recipe No. 436, and, if thought necessary, add a few drops of colouring No. 108
