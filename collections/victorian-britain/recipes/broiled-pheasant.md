## Ingredients

- 1 pheasant
- a little lard
- egg and bread crumbs
- salt and cayenne to taste
- mushroom-sauce, sauce piquante, or brown gravy
- game-bones and trimmings

## Directions

1. Cut the legs off at the first joint
2. Cut the remainder of the bird into neat pieces
3. Put them into a frying pan with a little lard
4. When browned on both sides, and about half done, take them out and drain them
5. Brush the pieces over with egg
6. Sprinkle with bread crumbs with which has been mixed a good seasoning of cayenne and salt
7. Broil them over a moderate fire for about 10 minutes, or rather longer
8. Serve with mushroom-sauce, sauce piquante, or brown gravy, in which a few game-bones and trimmings have been stewed
