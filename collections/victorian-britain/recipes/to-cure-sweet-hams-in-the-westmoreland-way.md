## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of common salt
- 3 lbs. of coarse sugar
- 1 lb. of bay-salt
- 3 quarts of strong beer

## Directions

1. Before the hams are put into pickle, rub them the preceding day well with salt, and drain the brine well from them.
2. Put the above ingredients into a saucepan, and boil for 1/4 hour.
3. Pour over the hams, and let them remain a month in the pickle.
4. Rub and turn them every day, but do not take them out of the pickling-pan.
5. Have them smoked for a month.
