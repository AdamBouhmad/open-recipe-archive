## Ingredients

- The remains of cold boiled sole or cod
- pepper, salt, and pounded mace to taste
- 1 dozen oysters to each lb. of fish
- 3 tablespoonfuls of white stock
- 1 teacupful of cream thickened with flour
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Clear the fish from the bones, lay it in a pie-dish, and between each layer put a few oysters and a little seasoning.
2. Add the stock, and, when liked, a small quantity of butter.
3. Cover with puff paste, and bake for 1/2 hour.
4. Boil the cream with sufficient flour to thicken it; pour in the pie, and serve.
