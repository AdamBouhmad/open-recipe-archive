## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of gin
- 1/2 lb. of honey
- 1/2 lb. of soft soap
- 1/2 pint of water

## Directions

1. Mix the above ingredients together
2. Lay each breadth of silk upon a clean kitchen table or dresser, and scrub it well on the soiled side with the mixture
3. Have ready three vessels of cold water
4. Take each piece of silk at two corners, and dip it up and down in each vessel, but do not wring it
5. Take care that each breadth has one vessel of quite clean water for the last dip
6. Hang it up dripping for a minute or two
7. Dab it in a cloth
8. Iron it quickly with a very hot iron
