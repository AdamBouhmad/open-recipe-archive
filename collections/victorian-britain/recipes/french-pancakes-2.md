## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 oz. of butter
- 2 oz. of sifted sugar
- 2 oz. of flour
- 1/2 pint of new milk

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs thoroughly, and put them into a basin with the butter, which should be beaten to a cream
2. Stir in the sugar and flour
3. When these ingredients are well mixed, add the milk
4. Keep stirring and beating the mixture for a few minutes
5. Put it on buttered plates, and bake in a quick oven for 20 minutes
6. Serve with a cut lemon and sifted sugar, or pile the pancakes high on a dish, with a layer of preserve or marmalade between each
