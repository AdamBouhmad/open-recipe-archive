## Ingredients

- cauliflowers
- salt
- water
- plain melted butter

## Directions

1. Choose cauliflowers that are close and white
2. Trim off the decayed outside leaves, and cut the stalk off flat at the bottom
3. Open the flower a little in places to remove the insects
4. Let the cauliflowers lie in salt and water for an hour previous to dressing them, with their heads downwards
5. Put them into fast-boiling water, with salt in proportion of 1 heaped tablespoonful to each 1/2 gallon of water
6. Let them boil briskly over a good fire, keeping the saucepan uncovered
7. Skim the water well
8. When the cauliflowers are tender, take them up with a slice and let them drain
9. If large enough, place them upright in the dish
10. Serve with plain melted butter, a little of which may be poured over the flower
