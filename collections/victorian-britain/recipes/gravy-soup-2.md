## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. of shin of beef
- 1 knuckle of veal weighing 5 lbs.
- a few pieces or trimmings of veal
- 2 slices of nicely-flavoured lean ham
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 2 onions
- 2 carrots
- 1 turnip
- nearly a head of celery
- 1 blade of mace
- 6 cloves
- a bunch of savoury herb with endive
- salt and pepper to taste
- 3 lumps of sugar
- 5 quarts of boiling soft water

## Directions

1. Slightly brown the meat and ham in the butter, but do not let them burn
2. Pour to it the water, and as the scum rises, take it off
3. When no more scum appears, add all the other ingredients, and let the soup simmer slowly by the fire for 6 hours without stirring it any more from the bottom
4. Take it off, and let it settle
5. Skim off all the fat you can, and pass it through a tammy
