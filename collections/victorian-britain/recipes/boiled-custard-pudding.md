## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 4 eggs
- lemon-rind or cinnamon for flavouring
- butter

## Directions

1. Flavour the milk by infusing in it a little lemon-rind or cinnamon
2. Whisk the eggs
3. Stir the flour gradually to these
4. Pour over them the milk, and stir the mixture well
5. Butter a basin that will exactly hold it
6. Put in the custard, and tie a floured cloth over
7. Plunge it into boiling water, and turn it about for a few minutes, to prevent the flour from settling in one part
8. Boil it slowly for 1/2 hour
9. Turn it out of the basin, and serve
