## Ingredients

- Knuckle of veal
- pepper and salt to taste
- flour
- 1 onion
- 1 head of celery, or a little celery-seed
- a faggot of savoury herbs
- 2 blades of pounded mace
- butter and flour for thickening
- a few young carrots
- 1 tablespoonful of ketchup
- 1 tablespoonful of tomato sauce
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sherry
- the juice of 1/4 lemon
- hot water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat from a knuckle of veal into neat slices, season with pepper and salt, and dredge them with flour
2. Fry them in a little butter of a pale brown, and put them into a stewpan with the bone (which should be chopped in several places)
3. Add the celery, herbs, mace, and carrots
4. Pour over all about 1 pint of hot water, and let it simmer very gently for 2 hours, over a slow but clear fire
5. Take out the slices of meat and carrots, strain and thicken the gravy with a little butter rolled in flour
6. Add the remaining ingredients, give one boil, put back the meat and carrots, let these get hot through, and serve
