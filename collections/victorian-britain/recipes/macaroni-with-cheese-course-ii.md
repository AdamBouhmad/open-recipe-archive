## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of pipe or riband macaroni
- 1/2 pint of milk
- 1/2 pint of veal or beef gravy
- The yolks of 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 3 oz. of grated Parmesan or Cheshire cheese
- 1 oz. of butter

## Directions

1. Wash the macaroni, and boil it in the gravy and milk until quite tender, without being broken.
2. Drain it, and put it into rather a deep dish.
3. Beat the yolks of the eggs with the cream and 2 tablespoonfuls of the liquor the macaroni was boiled in; make this sufficiently hot to thicken, but do not allow it to boil; pour it over the macaroni.
4. Over the macaroni, sprinkle the grated cheese and the butter broken into small pieces; brown with a salamander, or before the fire, and serve.
