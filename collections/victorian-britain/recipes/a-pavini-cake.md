## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of ground rice
- 1/2 lb. of raisins stoned and cut into small pieces
- 1/4 lb. of currants
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 2 oz. of sweet almonds
- 1/4 lb. of sifted loaf sugar
- 1/2 nutmeg grated
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of carbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Stone and cut the raisins into small pieces
2. Wash, pick, and dry the currants
3. Melt the butter to a cream, but without oiling it
4. Blanch and chop the almonds, and grate the nutmeg
5. Mix all these ingredients well together
6. Make the milk warm, stir in the soda, and with this liquid make the whole into a paste
7. Butter a mould, rather more than half fill it with the dough
8. Bake the cake in a moderate oven from 1-1/2 to 2 hours, or less time should it be made into 2 cakes
