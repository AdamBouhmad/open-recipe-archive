## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 slices of bread
- clarified butter
- anchovy paste (No. 227)
- made mustard or a few grains of cayenne

## Directions

1. Toast 2 or 3 slices of bread, or, if wanted very savoury, fry them in clarified butter
2. Spread on them the paste, No. 227
3. Made mustard, or a few grains of cayenne, may be added to the paste before laying it on the toast
