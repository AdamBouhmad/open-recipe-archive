## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of good boiling apples
- 3/4 teaspoonful of white pepper
- 6 cloves
- cayenne or ginger to taste
- 3 quarts of medium stock

## Directions

1. Peel and quarter the apples, taking out their cores
2. Put them into the stock, stew them gently till tender
3. Rub the whole through a strainer, add the seasoning, give it one boil up, and serve
