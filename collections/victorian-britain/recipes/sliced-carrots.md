## Ingredients

- 5 or 6 large carrots
- a large lump of sugar
- 1 pint of weak stock
- 3 oz. of fresh butter
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Scrape and wash the carrots, cut them into slices of an equal size
2. Boil them in salt and water, until half done
3. Drain them well
4. Put them into a stewpan with the sugar and stock
5. Let them boil over a brisk fire
6. When reduced to a glaze, add the fresh butter and a seasoning of salt
7. Shake the stewpan about well, and when the butter is well mixed with the carrots, serve
