## Ingredients

- Snipes
- butter
- flour
- toast

## Directions

1. Dress without being drawn.
2. Pluck and wipe them outside.
3. Truss them with the head under the wing, having previously skinned that and the neck.
4. Twist the legs at the first joint.
5. Press the feet upon the thighs and pass a skewer through these and the body.
6. Place four on a skewer and tie them on to the jack or spit.
7. Roast before a clear fire for about 1/4 hour.
8. Put some pieces of buttered toast into the dripping-pan to catch the trails.
9. Flour and froth the birds nicely.
10. Dish the pieces of toast with the snipes on them.
11. Pour round, but not over them, a little good brown gravy.
12. Send to table very hot and expeditiously.
