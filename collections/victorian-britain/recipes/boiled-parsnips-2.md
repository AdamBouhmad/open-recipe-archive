## Ingredients

- Parsnips
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt to each gallon of water

## Directions

1. Wash the parsnips, scrape them thoroughly, and, with the point of the knife, remove any black specks about them, and, should they be very large, cut the thick part into quarters
2. Put them into a saucepan of boiling water salted in the above proportion
3. Boil them rapidly until tender, which may be ascertained by thrusting a fork in them
4. Take them up, drain them, and serve in a vegetable-dish
