## Ingredients

- The liquor in which a joint of meat has been boiled, say 4 quarts
- trimmings of fresh meat or poultry
- shank-bones
- roast-beef bones
- any pieces the larder may furnish
- vegetables, spices, and the same seasoning as in the foregoing recipe

## Directions

1. Let all the ingredients simmer gently for 6 hours, taking care to skim carefully at first
2. Strain it off, and put by for use
