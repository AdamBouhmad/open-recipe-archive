## Ingredients

- 6 oz. of rice
- 1 quart of milk
- 3 oz. of sugar
- 1 oz. of fresh butter
- 6 oz. of orange marmalade
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Swell the rice in the milk, with the sugar and butter, over a slow fire until it is perfectly tender, which will be in about 3/4 hour
2. When the rice is done, strain away the milk, should there be any left
3. Mix with it the marmalade and well-beaten eggs
4. Stir the whole over the fire until the eggs are set
5. Spread the mixture on a dish to the thickness of about 1/2 inch, or rather thicker
6. When it is perfectly cold, cut it into long strips
7. Dip them in a batter the same as for apple fritters
8. Fry them a nice brown
9. Dish them on a white d'oyley, strew sifted sugar over, and serve quickly
