## Ingredients

- 5 or 6 artichokes
- salt and water
- 1/4 lb. of flour
- a little salt
- the yolk of 1 egg
- milk
- lemon-juice
- hot lard or dripping
- crisped parsley
- plain melted butter

## Directions

1. Trim and boil the artichokes by recipe No. 1080, and rub them over with lemon-juice, to keep them white.
2. When they are quite tender, take them up, remove the chokes, and divide the bottoms.
3. Dip each piece into batter, fry them in hot lard or dripping, and garnish the dish with crisped parsley.
4. Serve with plain melted butter.
