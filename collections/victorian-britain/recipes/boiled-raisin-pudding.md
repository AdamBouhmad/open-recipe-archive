## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of stoned raisins
- 1/2 lb. of chopped suet
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt
- milk

## Directions

1. Stone the raisins and chop the suet finely
2. Mix them with the flour, add the salt, and when these dry ingredients are thoroughly mixed, moisten the pudding with sufficient milk to make it into rather a stiff paste
3. Tie it up in a floured cloth, put it into boiling water, and boil for 4 hours
4. Serve with sifted sugar
