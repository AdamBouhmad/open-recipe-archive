## Ingredients

- remains of cold roast fowl
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- salt
- cayenne
- pounded mace
- 1 onion
- 1 faggot of savoury herbs
- 6 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 1 oz. of butter
- two teaspoonfuls of flour
- 1/2 teaspoonful of finely-minced lemon-peel
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- sippets of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut out from the fowl all the white meat, and mince it finely without any skin or bone
2. Put the bones, skin, and trimmings into a stewpan with an onion, a bunch of savoury herbs, a blade of mace, and nearly a pint of water
3. Let this stew for an hour, then strain the liquor
4. Chop the eggs small and mix them with the fowl
5. Add salt, cayenne, and pounded mace, put in the gravy and remaining ingredients
6. Let the whole just boil, and serve with sippets of toasted bread
