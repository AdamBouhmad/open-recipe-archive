## Ingredients

- 3 plovers
- butter
- flour
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Choose plovers that feel hard at the vent, as that shows their fatness.
2. Pluck off the feathers and wipe the outside of the birds with a damp cloth.
3. Do not draw them.
4. Truss with the head under the wing.
5. Put them down to a clear fire and lay slices of moistened toast in the dripping-pan to catch the trail.
6. Keep them well basted.
7. Dredge them lightly with flour a few minutes before they are done.
8. Let them be nicely frothed.
9. Dish them on the toasts, over which the trail should be equally spread.
10. Pour round the toast a little good gravy and send some to table in a tureen.
