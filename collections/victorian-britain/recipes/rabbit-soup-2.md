## Ingredients

- 2 large rabbits, or 3 small ones
- a faggot of savoury herbs
- 1/2 head of celery
- 2 carrots
- 1 onion
- 1 blade of mace
- salt and white pepper to taste
- pounded mace
- 1/2 pint of cream
- yolks of 2 eggs boiled hard
- crumb of a French roll
- nearly 3 quarts of water
- veal stock or water
- arrowroot or rice-flour
- vermicelli or pearl barley

## Directions

1. Make the soup with the legs and shoulders of the rabbit, and keep the nice pieces for a dish or entrée
2. Put them into warm water, and draw the blood
3. When quite clean, put them in a stewpan, with a faggot of herbs, and a teacupful, or rather more, of veal stock or water
4. Simmer slowly till done through, and add the 3 quarts of water, and boil for an hour
5. Take out the rabbit, pick the meat from the bones, covering it up to keep it white
6. Put the bones back in the liquor, add the vegetables, and simmer for 2 hours
7. Skim and strain, and let it cool
8. Pound the meat in a mortar, with the yolks of the eggs, and the crumb of the roll previously soaked
9. Rub it through a tammy, and gradually add it to the strained liquor, and simmer for 15 minutes
10. Mix arrowroot or rice-flour with the cream (say 2 dessert-spoonfuls), and stir in the soup
11. Bring it to a boil, and serve
