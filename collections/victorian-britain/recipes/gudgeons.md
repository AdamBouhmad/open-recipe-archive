## Ingredients

- egg
- bread crumbs
- hot lard
- flour

## Directions

1. Do not scrape off the scales, but take out the gills and inside
2. cleanse thoroughly
3. wipe them dry
4. flour and dip them into egg
5. sprinkle over with bread crumbs
6. fry of a nice brown
