## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of mashed potatoes
- 2 oz. of butter
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 pint of milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sherry
- 1/4 saltspoonful of salt
- the juice and rind of 1 small lemon
- 2 oz. of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil sufficient potatoes to make 1/2 lb. when mashed
2. Add to these the butter, eggs, milk, sherry, lemon-juice, and sugar
3. Mince the lemon-peel very finely, and beat all the ingredients well together
4. Put the pudding into a buttered pie-dish, and bake for rather more than 1/2 hour
