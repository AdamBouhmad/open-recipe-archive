## Ingredients

- Equal quantities of fat bacon and lean veal
- to every lb. of meat, allow 1 teaspoonful of minced sage
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Chop the meat and bacon finely
2. to every lb. allow the above proportion of very finely-minced sage
3. add a seasoning of pepper and salt
4. mix the whole well together
5. make it into flat cakes
6. fry a nice brown
