## Ingredients

- 12 peppercorns
- 2 bay-leaves
- 1/2 pint of vinegar
- 4 mackerel

## Directions

1. Boil the mackerel as in the recipe No. 282, and lay them in a dish; take half the liquor they were boiled in; add as much vinegar, peppercorns, and bay-leaves; boil for 10 minutes, and when cold, pour over the fish.
