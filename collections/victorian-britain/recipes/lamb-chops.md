## Ingredients

- Loin of lamb
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Trim off the flap from a fine loin of lamb, and cut it into chops about 3/4 inch in thickness
2. Have ready a bright clear fire; lay the chops on a gridiron, and broil them of a nice pale brown, turning them when required
3. Season them with pepper and salt
4. serve very hot and quickly, and garnish with crisped parsley, or place them on mashed potatoes
5. Asparagus, spinach, or peas are the favourite accompaniments to lamb chops
