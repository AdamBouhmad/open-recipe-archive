## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 apples
- 1/4 lb. of bread crumbs
- 3 oz. of sugar
- 3 oz. of currants
- salt and grated nutmeg to taste
- the rind of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 wineglassful of brandy

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and mince the apples into small pieces, and mix them with the other dry ingredients
2. beat up the eggs, moisten the mixture with these, and beat it well
3. stir in the brandy, and put the pudding into a buttered mould
4. tie it down with a cloth, boil for 1-1/2 hour, and serve with sweet sauce
