## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast duck
- 2 oz. of butter
- 3 or 4 slices of lean ham or bacon
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 2 pints of thin gravy
- 1 or a small bunch of green onions
- 3 sprigs of parsley
- 3 cloves
- 1 pint of young green peas
- cayenne and salt to taste
- 1 teaspoonful of pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a stewpan
2. Cut up the duck into joints, lay them in with the slices of lean ham or bacon
3. Make it brown, then dredge in a tablespoonful of flour, and stir this well in before adding the gravy
4. Put in the onion, parsley, cloves, and gravy
5. When it has simmered for 1/4 hour, add a pint of young green peas, and stew gently for about 1/2 hour
6. Season with cayenne, salt, and sugar
7. Take out the duck, place it round the dish, and the peas in the middle
