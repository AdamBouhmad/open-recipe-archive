## Ingredients

- To every lb. of raisins allow a lb. of loaf sugar
- pounded cinnamon and cloves to taste

## Directions

1. Stone the raisins
2. Put them into a stewpan with the sugar, cinnamon, and cloves, and let them boil for 1-1/2 hour, stirring all the time
3. Let the preparation cool a little, pour it into a glass dish, and garnish with strips of candied lemon-peel and citron
