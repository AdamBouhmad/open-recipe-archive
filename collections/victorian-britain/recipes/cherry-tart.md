## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 lb. of cherries
- 2 small tablespoonfuls of moist sugar
- 1/2 lb. of short crust

## Directions

1. Pick the stalks from the cherries, put them, with the sugar, into a deep pie-dish just capable of holding them, with a small cup placed upside down in the midst of them
2. Make a short crust with 1/2 lb. of flour
3. Lay a border round the edge of the dish; put on the cover, and ornament the edges
4. Bake in a brisk oven from 1/2 hour to 40 minutes
5. Strew finely-sifted sugar over, and serve hot or cold
