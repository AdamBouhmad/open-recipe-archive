## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of milk
- 1/2 pint of cream
- 2 oz. of flour
- 3 oz. of pounded sugar
- 6 eggs
- 2 oz. of butter
- puff-paste
- essence of vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix the flour to a smooth batter, with the milk
2. Stir in the cream, sugar, the eggs, which should be well whisked, and the butter, which should be beaten to a cream
3. Put in some essence of vanilla, drop by drop, until the mixture is well flavoured
4. Line some dariole-moulds with puff-paste, three-parts fill them with the batter, and bake in a good oven from 25 to 35 minutes
5. Turn them out of the moulds on a dish, without breaking them
6. Strew over sifted sugar, and serve
