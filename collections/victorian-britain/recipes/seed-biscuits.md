## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of sifted sugar
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 1/2 oz. of caraway seeds
- 3 eggs
- milk or the white of an egg

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. stir in the flour, sugar, and caraway seeds
3. when these ingredients are well mixed, add the eggs, which should be well whisked
4. Roll out the paste, with a round cutter shape out the biscuits
5. bake them in a moderate oven from 10 to 15 minutes
6. The tops of the biscuits may be brushed over with a little milk or the white of an egg, and then a little sugar strewn over
