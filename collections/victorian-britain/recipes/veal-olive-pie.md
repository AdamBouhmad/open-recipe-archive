## Ingredients

- A few thin slices of cold fillet of veal
- A few thin slices of bacon
- forcemeat No. 417
- a cupful of gravy
- 4 tablespoonfuls of cream
- puff-crust
- shalot
- cayenne

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices from a fillet of veal, place on them thin slices of bacon, and over them a layer of forcemeat, made by recipe No. 417, with an additional seasoning of shalot and cayenne
2. Roll them tightly, and fill up a pie-dish with them
3. Add the gravy and cream
4. Cover with a puff-crust, and bake for 1 to 1-1/2 hour: should the pie be very large, allow 2 hours
5. The pieces of rolled veal should be about 3 inches in length, and about 3 inches round
