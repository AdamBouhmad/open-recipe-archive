## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of macaroni
- 1-1/2 pint of milk
- the rind of 1/2 lemon
- 3 oz. of lump sugar
- 3/4 pint of custard

## Directions

1. Put the milk into a saucepan, with the lemon-peel and sugar.
2. Bring it to the boiling-point, drop in the macaroni, and let it gradually swell over a gentle fire, but do not allow the pipes to break.
3. The form should be entirely preserved; and, though tender, should be firm, and not soft, with no part beginning to melt.
4. Should the milk dry away before the macaroni is sufficiently swelled, add a little more.
5. Make a custard by recipe No. 1423.
6. Place the macaroni on a dish, and pour the custard over the hot macaroni.
7. Grate over it a little nutmeg, and, when cold, garnish the dish with slices of candied citron.
