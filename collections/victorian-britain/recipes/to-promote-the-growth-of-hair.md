## Ingredients

- Equal quantities of olive-oil and spirit of rosemary
- a few drops of oil of nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix the ingredients together
2. Rub the roots of the hair every night with a little of this liniment, and the growth of it will very soon sensibly increase
