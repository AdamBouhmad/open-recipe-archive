## Ingredients

- remains of cold roast fowl
- 1 pint of water
- 1 onion
- 2 or three small carrots
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- pepper and salt
- 1 small bunch of savoury herbs
- butter and flour
- 1-1/2 tablespoonful of mushroom ketchup
- sippets of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut off the best joints from the fowl
2. Make the remainder into gravy by adding to the bones and trimmings a pint of water, an onion sliced and fried of a nice brown, the carrots, mace, seasoning, and herbs
3. Let these stew gently for 1-1/2 hour
4. Strain the liquor, and thicken with a little flour and butter
5. Lay in the fowl, thoroughly warm it through, add the ketchup
6. Garnish with sippets of toasted bread
