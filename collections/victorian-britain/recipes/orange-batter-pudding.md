## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- 1-1/4 oz. of loaf sugar
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Make the batter with the above ingredients, put it into a well-buttered basin, tie it down with a cloth, and boil for 1 hour
2. As soon as it is turned out of the basin, put a small jar of orange marmalade all over the top, and send the pudding very quickly to table
