## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1 quart of milk
- 1-1/2 oz. of isinglass
- rind of 1/2 lemon
- 4 laurel-leaves

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients into a lined saucepan, and boil gently until the isinglass is dissolved
2. Taste it occasionally, to ascertain when it is sufficiently flavoured with the laurel-leaves
3. Take out the laurel-leaves, and keep stirring the mixture over the fire for about 10 minutes
4. Strain it through a fine sieve into a jug
5. When nearly cold, pour it into a well-oiled mould, omitting the sediment at the bottom
6. Turn it out carefully on a dish, and garnish with preserves, bright jelly, or a compote of fruit
