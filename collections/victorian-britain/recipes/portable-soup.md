## Ingredients

- 2 knuckles of veal
- 3 shins of beef
- 1 large faggot of herbs
- 2 bay-leaves
- 2 heads of celery
- 3 onions
- 3 carrots
- 2 blades of mace
- 6 cloves
- a teaspoonful of salt
- sufficient water to cover all the ingredients

## Directions

1. Take the marrow from the bones
2. Put all the ingredients in a stock-pot, and simmer slowly for 12 hours, or more, if the meat be not done to rags
3. Strain it off, and put it in a very cool place
4. Take off all the fat, reduce the liquor in a shallow pan, by setting it over a sharp fire, but be particular that it does not burn
5. Boil it fast and uncovered for 8 hours, and keep it stirred
6. Put it into a deep dish, and set it by for a day
7. Have ready a stewpan of boiling water, place the dish in it, and keep it boiling
8. Stir occasionally, and when the soup is thick and ropy, it is done
9. Form it into little cakes by pouring a small quantity on to the bottom of cups or basins
10. When cold, turn them out on a flannel to dry
11. Keep them from the air in tin canisters
