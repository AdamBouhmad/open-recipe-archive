## Ingredients

- Salmon
- 1/2 oz. of whole pepper
- 1/2 oz. of whole allspice
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 bay-leaves
- equal quantities of vinegar and the liquor in which the fish was boiled

## Directions

1. After the fish comes from table, lay it in a nice dish with a cover to it, as it should be excluded from the air
2. take away the bone
3. boil the liquor and vinegar with the other ingredients for 10 minutes
4. let it stand to get cold
5. pour it over the salmon
6. in 12 hours this will be fit for the table
