## Ingredients

- 1/4 pint of walnut ketchup
- 1/4 pint of mushroom ketchup
- 2 tablespoonfuls of Indian soy
- 2 tablespoonfuls of port wine
- 1/4 oz. of white pepper
- 2 oz. of shalots
- 1/4 oz. of cayenne
- 1/4 oz. of cloves
- 3/4 pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the whole of the ingredients into a bottle, and let it remain for a fortnight in a warm place, occasionally shaking up the contents.
2. Strain, and bottle off for use.
3. This sauce will be found an agreeable addition to gravies, hashes, stews, &c.
