## Ingredients

- 4 heads of celery
- 1/2 pint of melted butter, made with milk
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- Salt and white pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Wash the celery, boil it in salt and water till tender, and cut it into pieces 2 inches long.
2. Make 1/2 pint melted butter by recipe No. 380.
3. Put in the celery, pounded mace, and seasoning.
4. Simmer for three minutes, when the sauce will be ready to serve.
