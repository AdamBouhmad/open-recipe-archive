## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of solid beef
- 1 oz. of butter
- 1 clove
- 2 button onions or 1/2 a large one
- 1 saltspoonful of salt
- 1 quart of water

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into very small dice
2. Put it into a stewpan with the butter, clove, onion, and salt
3. Stir the meat round over the fire for a few minutes, until it produces a thin gravy
4. Add the water, and let it simmer gently from 1/2 to 3/4 hour, skimming off every particle of fat
5. When done, strain it through a sieve, and put it by in a cool place until required
