## Ingredients

- 1/2 gallon of water
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- vegetable marrows
- melted butter or salt butter
- toast

## Directions

1. Have ready a saucepan of boiling water, salted in the above proportion
2. Put in the marrows after peeling them, and boil them until quite tender
3. Take them up with a slice, halve, and, should they be very large, quarter them
4. Dish them on toast, and send to table with them a tureen of melted butter, or, in lieu of this, a small pat of salt butter
