## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- yolk of 1 egg
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt
- cold water
- 1 lb. of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Put the flour on to the paste-board and make a hole in the centre
2. Into this hole put the yolk of the egg, the lemon-juice, and salt
3. Mix the whole with cold water into a soft flexible paste, with the right hand, and handle it as little as possible
4. Squeeze all the buttermilk from the butter, wring it in a cloth, and roll out the paste
5. Place the butter on this, and fold the edges of the paste over, so as to hide it
6. Roll it out again to the thickness of 1/4 inch
7. Fold over one third, over which again pass the rolling-pin; then fold over the other third, thus forming a square
8. Place it with the ends, top, and bottom before you, shaking a little flour both under and over, and repeat the rolls and turns twice again
9. Flour a baking-sheet, put the paste on this, and let it remain on ice or in some cool place for 1/2 hour
10. Then roll twice more, turning it as before
11. Place it again upon the ice for 1/4 hour, give it 2 more rolls, making 7 in all, and it is ready for use when required
