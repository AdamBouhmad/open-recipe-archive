## Ingredients

- 1/2 gallon of water
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- broad beans
- parsley and butter

## Directions

1. Shell the beans
2. Put them into boiling water, salted in the above proportion
3. Let them boil rapidly until tender
4. Drain them well in a colander
5. Dish and serve with them separately a tureen of parsley and butter
