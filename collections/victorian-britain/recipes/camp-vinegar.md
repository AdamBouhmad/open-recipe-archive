## Ingredients

- 1 head of garlic
- 1/2 oz. cayenne
- 2 teaspoonfuls of soy
- 2 teaspoonfuls of walnut ketchup
- 1 pint of vinegar
- cochineal to colour

## Directions

1. Slice the garlic, and put it, with all the above ingredients, into a clean bottle.
2. Let it stand to infuse for a month, when strain it off quite clear, and it will be fit for use.
3. Keep it in small bottles well sealed, to exclude the air.
