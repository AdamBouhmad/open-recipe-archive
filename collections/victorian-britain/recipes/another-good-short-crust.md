## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 8 oz. of butter
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 2 oz. of sifted sugar
- about 1/4 pint of milk

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the flour
2. Add the sugar, and mix the whole as lightly as possible to a smooth paste, with the yolks of eggs well beaten, and the milk
