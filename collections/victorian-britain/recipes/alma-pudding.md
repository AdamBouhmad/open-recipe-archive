## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of fresh butter
- 1/2 lb. of powdered sugar
- 1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of currants
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a thick cream, strew in, by degrees, the sugar, and mix both these well together.
2. Dredge the flour in gradually, add the currants, and moisten with the eggs, which should be well beaten.
3. Butter a mould that will hold the mixture exactly.
4. Tie it down with a cloth, put the pudding into boiling water, and boil for 5 hours.
5. When turned out, strew some powdered sugar over it, and serve.
