## Ingredients

- A few chopped mushrooms and shalots
- 1/2 pint of stock, No. 105
- 1/2 glass of Madeira
- the juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pounded sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Put the stock into a stewpan with the mushrooms, shalots, and Madeira, and stew gently for 1/4 hour
2. Then add the remaining ingredients, and let them just boil
3. When the sauce is done enough, put it in another stewpan, and warm it in a bain marie
