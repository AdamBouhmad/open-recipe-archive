## Ingredients

- The liver of a fowl
- one tablespoonful of minced parsley
- 1/2 pint of melted butter

## Directions

1. Wash and score the liver, boil it for a few minutes, and mince it very fine.
2. Blanch or scald a small bunch of parsley, of which there should be sufficient when chopped to fill a tablespoon.
3. Add this, with the minced liver, to 1/2 pint of smoothly-made melted butter.
4. Let it just boil when serve.
