## Ingredients

- pounded galls 1 oz.
- logwood-chips 1 oz.
- red French wine (ordinaire) 3 lbs.
- pounded gum-arabic 1/2 lb.
- lump-sugar 1/2 lb.
- green copperas 1 oz.
- brandy 3 lbs.

## Directions

1. Boil together the galls, logwood-chips, and wine till the liquid is reduced to half the quantity
2. Pour it off through a strainer
3. Dissolve the gum-arabic in the preceding decoction
4. Add the sugar and copperas
5. When all is dissolved and mixed together, stir in the brandy, mixing it smoothly
