## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast fowl
- 3 or 4 sliced onions
- 1 tablespoonful of curry-powder
- salt to taste
- butter

## Directions

1. Divide the fowl into joints
2. Slice and fry the onions in a little butter, taking care not to burn them
3. Sprinkle over the fowl a little curry-powder and salt
4. Fry these nicely, pile them high in the centre of the dish, cover with the onion, and serve with a cut lemon on a plate
5. Care must be taken that the onions are not greasy: they should be quite dry, but not burnt
