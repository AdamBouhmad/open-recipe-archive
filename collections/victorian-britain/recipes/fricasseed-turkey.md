## Ingredients

- remains of cold roast or boiled turkey
- strip of lemon-peel
- bunch of savoury herbs
- onion
- pepper and salt
- water
- cream
- yolk of an egg
- sippets of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut some nice slices from the remains of a cold turkey
2. Put the bones and trimmings into a stewpan with the lemon-peel, herbs, onion, pepper, salt, and water
3. Stew for an hour
4. Strain the gravy and lay in the pieces of turkey
5. When warm through, add the cream and the yolk of an egg
6. Stir it well round
7. When getting thick, take out the pieces, lay them on a hot dish, and pour the sauce over
8. Garnish the fricassée with sippets of toasted bread
9. Celery or cucumbers, cut into small pieces, may be put into the sauce; if the former, it must be boiled first
