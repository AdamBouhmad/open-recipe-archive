## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 pint of jelly
- 1/2 pint of cream
- 1 glass of sherry
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Make the above proportion of calf's-feet or isinglass jelly, colouring and flavouring it in any way that may be preferred
2. Soak a mould, open in the centre, for about 1/2 hour in cold water
3. Fill it with the jelly, and let it remain in a cool place until perfectly set
4. Turn it out on a dish
5. Fill the centre with whipped cream, flavoured with sherry and sweetened with pounded sugar
6. Pile this cream high in the centre, and serve
