## Ingredients

- Mushroom-flaps
- pepper and salt to taste
- butter
- lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Cleanse the mushrooms by wiping them with a piece of flannel and a little salt
2. Cut off a portion of the stalk, and peel the tops
3. Broil them over a clear fire, turning them once, and arrange them on a very hot dish
4. Put a small piece of butter on each mushroom, season with pepper and salt, and squeeze over them a few drops of lemon-juice
5. Place the dish before the fire, and when the butter is melted, serve very hot and quickly
