## Ingredients

- a quart of French beans
- 3 oz. of fresh butter
- pepper and salt to taste
- juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Cut and boil the beans by the preceding recipe
2. When tender, put them into a stewpan and shake over the fire to dry away the moisture from the beans
3. When quite dry and hot, add the butter, pepper, salt, and lemon-juice
4. Keep moving the stewpan, without using a spoon, as that would break the beans
5. When the butter is melted and all is thoroughly hot, serve
6. If the butter should not mix well, add a tablespoonful of gravy and serve very quickly
