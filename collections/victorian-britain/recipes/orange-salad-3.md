## Ingredients

- 6 oranges
- 1/4 lb. of muscatel raisins
- 2 oz. of pounded sugar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of brandy

## Directions

1. Peel 5 of the oranges; divide them into slices without breaking the pulp, and arrange them on a glass dish
2. Stone the raisins, mix them with the sugar and brandy, and mingle them with the oranges
3. Squeeze the juice of the other orange over the whole
4. A little pounded spice may be put in when the flavour is liked; but this ingredient must be added very sparingly
