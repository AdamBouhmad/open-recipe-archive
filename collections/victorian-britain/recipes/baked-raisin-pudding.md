## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 3/4 lb. of stoned raisins
- 1/2 lb. of suet
- a pinch of salt
- 1 oz. of sugar
- a little grated nutmeg
- milk

## Directions

1. Chop the suet finely
2. Stone the raisins and cut them in halves
3. Mix these with the suet, add the salt, sugar, and grated nutmeg, and moisten the whole with sufficient milk to make it of the consistency of thick batter
4. Put the pudding into a buttered pie-dish, and bake for 1-1/2 hour, or rather longer
5. Turn it out of the dish, strew sifted sugar over, and serve
