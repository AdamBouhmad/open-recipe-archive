## Ingredients

- Damsons
- 3/4 lb. of loaf sugar per lb. of fruit

## Directions

1. Have the fruit gathered in dry weather; pick it over, and reject any that is at all blemished
2. Stone the damsons, weigh them, and to every lb. allow 3/4 lb. of loaf sugar
3. Put the fruit and sugar into a preserving-pan; keep stirring them gently until the sugar is dissolved, and carefully remove the scum as it rises
4. Boil the jam for about an hour, reckoning from the time it commences to simmer all over alike
5. It must be well stirred all the time, or it will be liable to burn and stick to the pan
6. When the jam looks firm, and the juice appears to set, it is done
7. Take it off the fire, put into pots, cover it down, when quite cold, with oiled and egged papers, and store it away in a dry place
