## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of rice-flour
- 1/4 lb. of pounded lump sugar
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. stir in the rice-flour and pounded sugar
3. moisten the whole with the eggs, which should be previously well beaten
4. Roll out the paste, shape it with a round paste-cutter into small cakes
5. bake them from 12 to 18 minutes in a very slow oven
