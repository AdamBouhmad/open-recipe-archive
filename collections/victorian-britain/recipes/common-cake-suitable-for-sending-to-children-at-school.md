## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of flour
- 4 oz. of butter or clarified dripping
- 1/2 oz. of caraway seeds
- 1/4 oz. of allspice
- 1/2 lb. of pounded sugar
- 1 lb. of currants
- 1 pint of milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls of fresh yeast

## Directions

1. Rub the butter lightly into the flour
2. Add all the dry ingredients, and mix these well together
3. Make the milk warm, but not hot
4. Stir in the yeast, and with this liquid make the whole into a light dough
5. Knead it well, and line the cake-tins with strips of buttered paper
6. The paper should be about 6 inches higher than the top of the tin
7. Put in the dough
8. Stand it in a warm place to rise for more than an hour
9. Bake the cakes in a well-heated oven
10. If this quantity be divided in two, they will take from 1-1/2 to 2 hours' baking
