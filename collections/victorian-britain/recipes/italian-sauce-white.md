## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of white stock, No. 107
- 2 tablespoonfuls of chopped mushrooms
- 1 dessertspoonful of chopped shalots
- 1 slice of ham, minced very fine
- 1/4 pint of Béchamel, No. 367
- salt to taste
- a few drops of garlic vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pounded sugar
- a squeeze of lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Put the shalots and mushrooms into a stewpan with the stock and ham, and simmer very gently for 1/2 hour
2. When add the Béchamel
3. Let it just boil up, and then strain it through a tammy
4. Season with the above ingredients, and serve very hot
5. If this sauce should not have retained a nice white colour, a little cream may be added
