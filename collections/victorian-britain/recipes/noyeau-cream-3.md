## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 oz. of isinglass
- juice of 2 lemons
- noyeau to taste
- pounded sugar to taste
- 1-1/2 pint of cream

## Directions

1. Dissolve the isinglass in a little boiling water
2. Add the lemon-juice, and strain this to the cream
3. Put in sufficient noyeau and sugar to flavour and sweeten the mixture nicely
4. Whisk the cream well
5. Put it into an oiled mould, and set the mould in ice or in a cool place
6. Turn it out, and garnish the dish to taste
