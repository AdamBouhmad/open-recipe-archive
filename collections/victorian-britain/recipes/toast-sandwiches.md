## Ingredients

- Thin cold toast
- Thin slices of bread-and-butter
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Place a very thin piece of cold toast between 2 slices of thin bread-and-butter in the form of a sandwich
2. Add a seasoning of pepper and salt
3. This sandwich may be varied by adding a little pulled meat, or very fine slices of cold meat, to the toast
