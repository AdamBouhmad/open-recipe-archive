## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoonfuls of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of arrowroot
- 1 pint of milk
- 2 oz. of butter
- sugar to taste
- the rind of 1/2 lemon
- 4 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of brandy

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with the lemon-rind until well flavoured; then strain it, and mix with it the flour, arrowroot, butter, and sugar
2. Boil these ingredients for a few minutes, keeping them well stirred
3. then take them off the fire and mix with them the eggs, yolks and whites, beaten separately and added separately
4. Boil some sugar to candy; line a mould with this, put in the brandy, then the mixture; tie down with a cloth, and boil for rather more than 1 hour
