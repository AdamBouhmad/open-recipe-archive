## Ingredients

- Equal weight of fine loaf sugar and Seville oranges
- 1 pint of water to every 12 oranges

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges carefully, remove a little of the white pith, and boil the rinds in water 2 hours, changing the water three times to take off a little of the bitter taste
2. Break the pulp into small pieces, take out all the pips, and cut the boiled rind into chips
3. Make a syrup with the sugar and water; boil this well, skim it, and, when clear, put in the pulp and chips
4. Boil all together from 20 minutes to 1/2 hour; pour it into pots, and, when cold, cover down with bladders or tissue-paper brushed over on both sides with the white of an egg
