## Ingredients

- 1-1/4 pint of milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 3 eggs
- 2 oz. of finely-shredded suet
- 1/4 lb. of currants
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Mix the milk, flour, and eggs to a smooth batter
2. add a little salt, the suet, and the currants, which should be well washed, picked, and dried
3. put the mixture into a buttered pie-dish, and bake in a moderate oven for 1-1/4 hour
4. When fresh fruits are in season, the pudding may be made with damsons, plums, red currants, gooseberries, or apples
5. when made with these, the pudding must be thickly sprinkled over with sifted sugar
