## Ingredients

- 1 shad
- oil
- pepper and salt
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Scale, empty and wash the fish carefully
2. make two or three incisions across the back
3. Season it with pepper and salt
4. let it remain in oil for 1/2 hour
5. Broil it on both sides over a clear fire
6. serve with caper sauce
