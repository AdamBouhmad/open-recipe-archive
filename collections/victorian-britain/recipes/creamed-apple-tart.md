## Ingredients

- Puff-crust No. 1205 or 1206
- apples
- 2 oz. of moist sugar per lb. of pared and cored apples
- 1/2 teaspoonful of minced lemon-peel per lb. of apples
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice per lb. of apples
- 1/2 pint of boiled custard
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make an apple tart by the preceding recipe, with the exception of omitting the icing.
2. When the tart is baked, cut out the middle of the lid or crust, leaving a border all round the dish.
3. Fill up with a nicely-made boiled custard.
4. Grate a little nutmeg over the top, and the pie is ready for table.
5. This tart is usually eaten cold.
