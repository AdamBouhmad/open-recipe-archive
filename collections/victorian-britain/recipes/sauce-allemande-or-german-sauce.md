## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of sauce tournée
- yolks of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Put the sauce into a stewpan, heat it, and stir to it the beaten yolks of 2 eggs, which have been previously strained.
2. Let it just simmer, but not boil, or the eggs will curdle.
3. After the eggs are added to the sauce, it must be stirred without ceasing.
