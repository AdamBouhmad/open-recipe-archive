## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 birds
- butter
- flour
- fried bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Pluck, draw, and truss them in the same manner as grouse.
2. Roast them before a brisk fire.
3. Flour and froth them nicely.
4. Serve on buttered toast with a tureen of brown gravy.
5. Bread sauce, when liked, may be sent to table with them, and fried bread crumbs substituted for the toasted bread.
