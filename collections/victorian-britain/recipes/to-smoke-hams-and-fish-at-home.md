## Ingredients

- old hogshead
- cross-stick
- iron pan
- sawdust
- small pieces of green wood
- red-hot iron

## Directions

1. Take an old hogshead, stop up all the crevices, and fix a place to put a cross-stick near the bottom, to hang the articles to be smoked on.
2. In the side, cut a hole near the top, to introduce an iron pan filled with sawdust and small pieces of green wood.
3. Turn the tub upside down, hang the articles upon the cross-stick, introduce the iron pan in the opening, and place a piece of red-hot iron in the pan.
4. Cover it with sawdust.
5. Let a large ham remain 40 hours, and keep up a good smoke.
