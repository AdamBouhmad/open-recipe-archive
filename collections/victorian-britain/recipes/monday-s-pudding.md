## Ingredients

- The remains of cold plum-pudding
- brandy
- 5 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- nutmeg or lemon-rind for flavouring

## Directions

1. Cut the remains of a good cold plum-pudding into finger-pieces, soak them in a little brandy, and lay them cross-barred in a mould until full.
2. Make a custard with the proportion of milk and eggs, flavouring it with nutmeg or lemon-rind.
3. Fill up the mould with it; tie it down with a cloth, and boil or steam it for an hour.
4. Serve with a little of the custard poured over, to which has been added a tablespoonful of brandy.
