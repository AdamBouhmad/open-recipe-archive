## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of salad-oil
- 4 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- salt and pepper to taste
- cucumber

## Directions

1. Pare the cucumber, cut it equally into very thin slices, and commence cutting from the thick end; if commenced at the stalk, the cucumber will most likely have an exceedingly bitter taste, far from agreeable.
2. Put the slices into a dish, sprinkle over salt and pepper, and pour over oil and vinegar in the above proportion.
3. Turn the cucumber about, and it is ready to serve.
