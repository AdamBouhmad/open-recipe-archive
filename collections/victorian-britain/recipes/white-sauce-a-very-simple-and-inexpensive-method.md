## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 pint of milk
- 1-1/2 oz. of rice
- 1 strip of lemon-peel
- 1 small blade of pounded mace
- salt and cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with the lemon-peel and rice until the latter is perfectly tender
2. Take out the lemon-peel and pound the milk and rice together
3. Put it back into the stewpan to warm
4. Add the mace and seasoning
5. Give it one boil and serve
6. This sauce should be of the consistency of thick cream
