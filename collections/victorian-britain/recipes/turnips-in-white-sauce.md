## Ingredients

- 7 or 8 turnips
- 1 oz. of butter
- 1/2 pint of white sauce
- a little butter
- a small lump of sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the turnips in the shape of pears or marbles
2. Boil them in salt and water, to which has been added a little butter, until tender
3. Take them out, drain, arrange them on a dish
4. Pour over the white sauce made by recipe No. 538 or 539, and to which has been added a small lump of sugar
5. When approved, a little mustard may be added to the sauce
