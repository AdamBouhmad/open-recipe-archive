## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of leaf, or the inside fat of a pig
- 1-1/2 lb. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of moist sugar
- 1/2 lb. of currants
- 1 oz. of candied lemon-peel
- ground allspice to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the leaf into small pieces
2. Put it into a large dish and place in a quick oven
3. Be careful that it does not burn; in a short time it will be reduced to oil with the small pieces of leaf floating on the surface
4. Gather all the scraps together and put them into a basin with the flour
5. Rub them well together
6. Add the currants, sugar, candied peel cut into thin slices, and the ground allspice
7. When all ingredients are well mixed, moisten with sufficient cold water to make the whole into a nice paste
8. Roll it out thin and cut it into shapes
9. Bake the cakes in a quick oven from 15 to 20 minutes
