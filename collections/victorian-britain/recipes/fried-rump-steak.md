## Ingredients

- steaks
- butter or clarified dripping
- pepper
- salt
- ketchup

## Directions

1. Put some butter or clarified dripping into a frying-pan; let it get quite hot, then lay in the steaks.
2. Turn them frequently until done, which will be in about 8 minutes, or rather more, should the steaks be very thick.
3. Serve on a very hot dish, in which put a small piece of butter and a tablespoonful of ketchup, and season with pepper and salt.
4. They should be sent to table quickly, as when cold, the steaks are entirely spoiled.
5. Optional gravy: As soon as the steaks are done, dish them, pour a little boiling water into the frying-pan, add a seasoning of pepper and salt, a small piece of butter, and a tablespoonful of Harvey's sauce or mushroom ketchup. Hold the pan over the fire for a minute or two, just let the gravy simmer, then pour on the steak, and serve.
