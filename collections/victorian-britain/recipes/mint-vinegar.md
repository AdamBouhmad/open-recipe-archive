## Ingredients

- vinegar
- mint

## Directions

1. Procure some nice fresh mint, pick the leaves from the stalks, and fill a bottle or jar with them.
2. Add vinegar to them until the bottle is full.
3. Cover closely to exclude the air, and let it infuse for a fortnight.
4. Strain the liquor, and put it into small bottles for use, of which the corks should be sealed.
