## Ingredients

- Beef (12 to 16 lbs. from the silver side)
- Water
- Carrots
- Turnips
- Parsnips
- Suet dumplings (optional)

## Directions

1. Take from 12 to 16 lbs. of silver side of the round after it has been in salt about 10 days.
2. Just wash off the salt, skewer it up in a nice round-looking form, and bind it with tape to keep the skewers in their places.
3. Put it in a saucepan of boiling water, set it upon a good fire, and when it begins to boil, carefully remove all scum from the surface.
4. When it is well skimmed, draw the pot to the corner of the fire, and let it simmer very gently until done.
5. Remove the tape and skewers, which should be replaced by a silver one.
6. Pour over a little of the pot-liquor, and garnish with carrots.
7. Boil for about 3 hours after the water boils.
