## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of bread crumbs
- 1/2 lb. of good figs
- 6 oz. of suet
- 6 oz. of moist sugar
- 1/2 saltspoonful of salt
- 3 eggs
- nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Mince the suet and figs very finely
2. Add the remaining ingredients, taking care that the eggs are well whisked
3. Beat the mixture for a few minutes
4. Put it into a buttered mould
5. Tie it down with a floured cloth
6. Boil the pudding for 5 hours
7. Serve with wine sauce
