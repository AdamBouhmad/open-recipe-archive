## Ingredients

- 1 quart of red or black currants, measured with the stalks
- 1/4 lb. of moist sugar
- 3/4 lb. of flour
- suet crust or butter crust
- butter
- raspberries (optional, about 1/2 pint)

## Directions

1. Make, with 3/4 lb. of flour, either a suet crust or butter crust
2. Butter a basin, and line it with part of the crust
3. Put in the currants, which should be stripped from the stalks, and sprinkle the sugar over them
4. Put the cover of the pudding on; make the edges very secure, that the juice does not escape
5. Tie it down with a floured cloth, put it into boiling water, and boil from 2-1/2 to 3 hours
6. If boiled without a basin, allow 1/2 hour less
7. If desired, brown the crust with a salamander, or put into a very hot oven for a few minutes to colour it
