## Ingredients

- 1 quartern of dough
- 1/4 lb. of moist sugar
- 1/4 lb. of butter or good beef dripping
- 1/4 pint of warm milk
- 1/2 grated nutmeg or 1/2 oz. of caraway seeds

## Directions

1. If you are not in the habit of making bread at home, procure the dough from the baker's, and, as soon as it comes in, put it into a basin near the fire; cover the basin with a thick cloth, and let the dough remain a little while to rise.
2. In the mean time, beat the butter to a cream, and make the milk warm; and when the dough has risen, mix with it thoroughly all the above ingredients, and knead the cake well for a few minutes.
3. Butter some cake-tins, half fill them, and stand them in a warm place, to allow the dough to rise again.
4. When the tins are three parts full, put the cakes into a good oven, and bake them from 13/4 to 2 hours.
