## Ingredients

- 1 oz. of dried lemon-thyme
- 1 oz. of dried winter savory
- 1 oz. of dried sweet marjoram and basil
- 2 oz. of dried parsley
- 1 oz. of dried lemon-peel

## Directions

1. Prepare and dry the herbs by recipe No. 445
2. Pick the leaves from the stalks, pound them, and sift them through a hair-sieve
3. Mix in the above proportions, and keep in glass bottles, carefully excluding the air
4. This provides them ready for use at a moment's notice
