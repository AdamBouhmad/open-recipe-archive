## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- sugar to taste
- a little grated lemon-rind
- 2 oz. of raisins
- 4 tablespoonfuls of marmalade
- a few slices of sponge cake

## Directions

1. Sweeten the milk with lump sugar, add a little grated lemon-rind
2. Stir to this the eggs, which should be well whisked
3. Line a buttered mould with the raisins, stoned and cut in half
4. Spread the slices of cake with the marmalade, and place them in the mould
5. Pour in the custard
6. Tie the pudding down with paper and a cloth
7. Boil gently for 1 hour: when cold, turn it out, and serve
