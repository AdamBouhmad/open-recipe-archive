## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of melted butter
- 3 dessertspoonfuls of capers
- 1 dessertspoonful of their liquor
- a small piece of glaze
- 1/4 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/4 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1 tablespoonful of anchovy essence

## Directions

1. Cut the capers across once or twice, but do not chop them fine.
2. Put them in a saucepan with 1/2 pint of good melted butter, and add all the other ingredients.
3. Keep stirring the whole until it just simmers, when it is ready to serve.
