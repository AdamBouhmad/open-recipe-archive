## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of shin of beef
- 1/4 lb. of pearl barley
- a large bunch of parsley
- 4 onions
- 6 potatoes
- salt and pepper
- 4 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Put in all the ingredients
2. Simmer gently for 3 hours
