## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast or boiled beef
- seasoning to taste of salt, pepper, and minced herbs
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Mince the beef tolerably fine with a small amount of its own fat
2. Add a seasoning of pepper, salt, and chopped herbs
3. Put the whole into a roll of puff paste, and bake for 1/2 hour, or rather longer, should the roll be very large
4. Beef patties may be made of cold meat, by mincing and seasoning beef as directed above, and baking in a rich puff paste in patty-tins
