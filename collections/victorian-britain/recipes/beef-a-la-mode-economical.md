## Ingredients

- About 3 lbs. of clod or sticking of beef
- 2 oz. of clarified dripping
- 1 large onion
- flour
- 2 quarts of water
- 12 berries of allspice
- 2 bay-leaves
- 1/2 teaspoonful of whole black pepper
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into small pieces, and roll them in flour.
2. Put the dripping into a stewpan with the onion, which should be sliced thin.
3. Let it get quite hot; lay in the pieces of beef, and stir them well about.
4. When nicely browned all over, add by degrees boiling water in the above proportion, and, as the water is added, keep the whole well stirred.
5. Put in the spice, bay-leaves, and seasoning, cover the stewpan closely, and set it by the side of the fire to stew very gently, till the meat becomes quite tender, which will be in about 3 hours.
6. Remove the bay-leaves before it is sent to table.
