## Ingredients

- Breast of mutton
- bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of minced savoury herbs (put a large proportion of parsley)
- pepper and salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cut off the superfluous fat
2. Bone it
3. Sprinkle over a layer of bread crumbs, minced herbs, and seasoning
4. Roll, and bind it up firmly
5. Boil gently for 2 hours
6. Remove the tape
7. Serve with caper sauce No. 382, a little of which should be poured over the meat
