## Ingredients

- 2 oz. of butter
- 3 onions
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- 4 tablespoonfuls of gravy, or stock
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoonful of made mustard
- 1 teaspoonful of vinegar
- the juice of 1/2 lemon

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a stewpan, set it on the fire, and, when browning, throw in the onions, which must be cut into small slices
2. Fry them brown, but do not burn them
3. add the flour, shake the onions in it, and give the whole another fry
4. Put in the gravy and seasoning, and boil it gently for 10 minutes
5. skim off the fat
6. add the mustard, vinegar, and lemon-juice
7. give it one boil, and pour round the steaks, or whatever dish the sauce has been prepared for
