## Ingredients

- 1 dozen large apples
- moist sugar to taste
- 1 small teacupful of cold water
- the grated rind of one lemon
- 1 pint of milk
- 4 eggs
- 2 oz. of loaf sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel, cut, and core the apples
2. Put them into a lined saucepan with the cold water
3. As they heat, bruise them to a pulp
4. Sweeten with moist sugar and add the grated lemon-rind
5. When cold, put the fruit at the bottom of a pie-dish
6. Pour over it a custard made with the above proportion of milk, eggs, and sugar
7. Grate a little nutmeg over the top
8. Place the dish in a moderate oven and bake from 25 to 35 minutes
