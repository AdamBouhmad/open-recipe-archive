## Ingredients

- pigeons
- pepper
- salt
- 3/4 oz. butter per bird
- fried parsley
- parsley and butter

## Directions

1. Wipe the birds very dry
2. Season them inside with pepper and salt
3. Put about 3/4 oz. of butter into the body of each
4. Put them down to a bright fire
5. Baste them well the whole of the time they are cooking
6. Cook from 20 to 30 minutes until done
7. Garnish with fried parsley
8. Serve with a tureen of parsley and butter
9. Serve with bread-sauce and gravy the same as for roast fowl, or egg-sauce
