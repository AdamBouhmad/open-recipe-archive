## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 shalots
- 1 oz. of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- white sauce
- pepper
- salt
- pounded mace
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pounded sugar
- remains of cold roast fowls
- yolks of 2 eggs
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Mince the fowl, carefully removing all skin and bone
2. Fry the shalots in the butter
3. Add the minced fowl, dredge in the flour, put in the pepper, salt, mace, and pounded sugar
4. Add sufficient white sauce to moisten it
5. Stir to it the yolks of 2 well-beaten eggs, and set it by to cool
6. Make the mixture up into balls, egg and bread-crumb them, and fry a nice brown
7. Serve on a border of mashed potatoes, with gravy or sauce in the centre
