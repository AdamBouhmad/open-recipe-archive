## Ingredients

- 1 lb flour
- 6 oz butter
- 1/2 pint water

## Directions

1. With a knife, work the flour to a smooth paste with 1/2 pint of water
2. Roll the crust out rather thin
3. Place the butter over it in small pieces
4. Dredge lightly over it some flour
5. Fold the paste over
6. Repeat the rolling once more, and the crust will be ready for use
