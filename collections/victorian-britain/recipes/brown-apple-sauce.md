## Ingredients

- 6 good-sized apples
- 1/2 pint of brown gravy
- cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Put the gravy in a stewpan, and add the apples, after having pared, cored, and quartered them.
2. Let them simmer gently till tender; beat them to a pulp, and season with cayenne.
