## Ingredients

- 1 pint of picked strawberries
- 1/3 pint of cream
- 2 oz. of finely-pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Pick the stalks from the fruit, place it on a glass dish, and sprinkle over it pounded sugar
2. Slightly stir the strawberries, that they may all be equally sweetened
3. Pour the cream over the top, and serve
