## Ingredients

- Pigeons
- 3 oz. of butter
- pepper and salt to taste
- fried parsley
- parsley and butter
- Bread-sauce
- gravy
- egg-sauce

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads and necks, truss the wings over the backs, and cut off the toes at the first joint.
2. Pigeons should be carefully cleaned, as no bird requires so much washing.
3. Wipe the birds very dry, season them inside with pepper and salt, and put about 3/4 oz. of butter into the body of each.
4. Put them down to a bright fire, and baste them well the whole of the time they are cooking (they will be done enough in from 20 to 30 minutes).
5. Garnish with fried parsley, and serve with a tureen of parsley and butter. Bread-sauce and gravy, the same as for roast fowl, are exceedingly nice accompaniments to roast pigeons, as also egg-sauce.
