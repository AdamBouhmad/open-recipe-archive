## Ingredients

- Slices of cold roast beef
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 sliced onion
- 1 teaspoonful of minced savoury herbs
- 5 or 6 tablespoonfuls of gravy or sauce of any kind
- mashed potatoes

## Directions

1. Butter the sides of a deep dish, and spread mashed potatoes over the bottom of it
2. on this place layers of beef in thin slices (this may be minced if there is not sufficient beef to cut into slices), well seasoned with pepper and salt, and a very little onion and herbs, which should be previously fried of a nice brown
3. then put another layer of mashed potatoes, and beef, and other ingredients, as before
4. pour in the gravy or sauce, cover the whole with another layer of potatoes, and bake for 1/2 hour
5. This may be served in the dish, or turned out
