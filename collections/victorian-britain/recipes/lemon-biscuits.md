## Ingredients

- 1-1/4 lb. of flour
- 3/4 lb. of loaf sugar
- 6 oz. of fresh butter
- 4 eggs
- 1 oz. of lemon-peel
- 2 dessertspoonfuls of lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Rub the flour into the butter
2. stir in the pounded sugar and very finely-minced lemon-peel
3. when these ingredients are thoroughly mixed, add the eggs, which should be previously well whisked, and the lemon-juice
4. Beat the mixture well for a minute or two
5. drop it from a spoon on to a buttered tin, about 2 inches apart, as the cakes will spread when they get warm
6. place the tin in the oven, and bake the cakes of a pale brown from 15 to 20 minutes
