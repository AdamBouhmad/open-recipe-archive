## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 8 oz. butter
- 4 oz. lard
- not quite 1/2 pint water

## Directions

1. Mix the flour to a smooth paste with not quite 1/2 pint of water
2. Roll it out 3 times, the first time covering the paste with butter, the second with lard, and the third with butter
3. Keep the rolling-pin and paste slightly dredged with flour, to prevent them from sticking
