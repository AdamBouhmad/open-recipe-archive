## Ingredients

- The remains of cold roast pork
- 2 onions
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- 2 blades of pounded mace
- 2 cloves
- 1 tablespoonful of vinegar
- 1/2 pint of gravy
- pepper and salt to taste
- sippets of toasted bread

## Directions

1. Chop the onions and fry them of a nice brown.
2. Cut the pork into thin slices, season them with pepper and salt, and add these to the remaining ingredients.
3. Stew gently for about 1/2 hour, and serve garnished with sippets of toasted bread.
