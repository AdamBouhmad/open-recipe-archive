## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of sherry
- 1/4 pint of water
- yolks of 6 eggs
- 2 oz. of pounded sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of minced lemon-peel
- a few pieces of candied citron cut thin

## Directions

1. Separate the yolks from the whites of 5 eggs; beat them, and put them into a very clean saucepan (if at hand, a lined one is best)
2. Add all the other ingredients
3. Place them over a sharp fire, and keep stirring until the sauce begins to thicken
4. Take it off and serve
5. Note: If it is allowed to boil, it will be spoiled, as it will immediately curdle
