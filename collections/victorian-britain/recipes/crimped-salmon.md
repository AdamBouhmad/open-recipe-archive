## Ingredients

- Salmon
- salt and water
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Cut salmon into slices 2 or 3 inches thick
2. Lay these in cold salt and water for 1 hour
3. Have ready some boiling water, salted, as in recipe No. 301, and well skimmed
4. Put in the fish, and simmer gently for 1/4 hour, or rather more
5. Garnish the same as boiled salmon, and serve with the same sauces
