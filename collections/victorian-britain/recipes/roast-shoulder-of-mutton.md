## Ingredients

- Shoulder of mutton
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Put the joint down to a bright, clear fire; flour it well, and keep continually basting.
2. About 1/4 hour before serving, draw it near the fire, that the outside may acquire a nice brown colour, but not sufficiently near to blacken the fat.
3. Sprinkle a little fine salt over the meat, empty the dripping-pan of its contents, pour in a little boiling water slightly salted, and strain this over the joint.
4. Serve with onion sauce, or stewed Spanish onions, and sometimes baked potatoes.
