## Ingredients

- Loin of mutton
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut and trim off the superfluous fat
2. See that the butcher joints the meat properly
3. Have ready a nice clear fire and put down the meat
4. Dredge with flour, and baste well until it is done
5. Make the gravy as for roast leg of mutton, and serve very hot
