## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 lb. of rice
- 3 pints of milk
- sugar to taste
- flavouring of bitter almonds
- 3 oz. of butter
- the yolks of 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Put the milk into a stewpan, with sufficient essence of bitter almonds to flavour it well
2. Add the rice, which should be washed, picked, and drained
3. Let it swell gradually in the milk over a slow fire
4. When it is tender, stir in the sugar, butter, and yolks of eggs
5. Butter a mould, press in the rice, and proceed in exactly the same manner as in recipe No. 1350
6. When the casserole is ready, fill it with a compôte of any fruit that may be preferred, or with melted apricot-jam, and serve
