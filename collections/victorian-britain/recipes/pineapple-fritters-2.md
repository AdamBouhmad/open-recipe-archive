## Ingredients

- A small pineapple
- a small wineglassful of brandy or liqueur
- 2 oz. of sifted sugar
- batter as for apple fritters

## Directions

1. Pare the pine with as little waste as possible
2. cut it into rather thin slices
3. soak these slices in the brandy or liqueur and pounded sugar for 4 hours
4. make a batter the same as for apple fritters, substituting cream for the milk, and using a smaller quantity of flour
5. dip in the pieces of pine, and fry them in boiling lard from 5 to 8 minutes
6. turn them when sufficiently brown on one side
7. drain them from the lard before the fire
8. dish them on a white d'oyley
9. strew over them sifted sugar
10. serve quickly
