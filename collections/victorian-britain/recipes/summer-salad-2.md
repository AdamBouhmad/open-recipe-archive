## Ingredients

- 3 lettuces
- 2 handfuls of mustard-and-cress
- 10 young radishes
- a few slices of cucumber
- hard-boiled eggs
- nasturtiums
- cut vegetable-flowers

## Directions

1. Let the herbs be as fresh as possible for a salad, and, if at all stale or dead-looking, let them lie in water for an hour or two, which will very much refresh them.
2. Wash and carefully pick them over, remove any decayed or wormeaten leaves, and drain them thoroughly by swinging them gently in a clean cloth.
3. With a silver knife, cut the lettuces into small pieces, and the radishes and cucumbers into thin slices.
4. Arrange all these ingredients lightly on a dish, with the mustard-and-cress, and pour under, but not over the salad, either of the sauces No. 506, 507, or 508.
5. Do not stir it up until it is to be eaten.
6. It may be garnished with hard-boiled eggs, cut in slices, sliced cucumbers, nasturtiums, cut vegetable-flowers, and many other things that taste will always suggest to make a pretty and elegant dish.
