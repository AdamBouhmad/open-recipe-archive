## Ingredients

- 5 or 6 Spanish onions
- 1 pint of good broth or gravy

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, taking care not to cut away too much of the tops or tails, or they would then fall to pieces
2. Put them into a stewpan capable of holding them at the bottom without piling them one on the top of another
3. Add the broth or gravy, and simmer very gently until the onions are perfectly tender
4. Dish them, pour the gravy round, and serve. Instead of using broth, Spanish onions may be stewed with a large piece of butter: they must be done very gradually over a slow fire or hot-plate, and will produce plenty of gravy
