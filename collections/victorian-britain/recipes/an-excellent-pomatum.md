## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 lb. of lard
- 1/2 pint of olive-oil
- 1/2 pint of castor-oil
- 4 oz. of spermaceti
- bergamot, or any other scent
- elder-flower water

## Directions

1. Wash the lard well in the elder-flower water
2. Drain, and beat it to a cream
3. Mix the two oils together, and heat them sufficiently to dissolve the spermaceti, which should be beaten fine in a mortar
4. Mix all these ingredients together with the brandy and whatever kind of scent may be preferred
5. Whilst warm pour into glass bottles for use, keeping them well corked
6. To liquefy the pomatum, set the bottle in a saucepan of warm water
