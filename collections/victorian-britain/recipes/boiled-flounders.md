## Ingredients

- Sufficient water to cover the flounders
- salt in the proportion of 6 oz. to each gallon
- a little vinegar
- plain melted butter or parsley and butter

## Directions

1. Pat on a kettle with enough water to cover the flounders, lay in the fish, add salt and vinegar in the above proportions, and when it boils, simmer very gently for 5 minutes
2. They must not boil fast, or they will break
3. Serve with plain melted butter, or parsley and butter
