## Ingredients

- 1 rabbit
- a few strips of bacon
- rather more than 1 pint of good broth or stock
- a bunch of savoury herbs
- salt and pepper to taste
- thickening of butter and flour
- 1 glass of sherry

## Directions

1. Well wash the rabbit, cut it into quarters, lard them with strips of bacon, and fry them
2. Put them into a stewpan with the broth, herbs, and a seasoning of pepper and salt
3. Simmer gently until the rabbit is tender
4. Strain the gravy, thicken it with butter and flour
5. Add the sherry, give one boil, pour it over the rabbit, and serve
6. Garnish with slices of cut lemon
