## Ingredients

- Puff-paste No. 1205
- the white of an egg
- sifted loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Make some good puff-paste by recipe No. 1205; roll it out to the thickness of about 1/4 inch, and, with a round fluted paste-cutter, stamp out as many pieces as may be required
2. work the paste up again, and roll it out to the same thickness, and with a smaller cutter, stamp out sufficient pieces to correspond with the larger ones
3. Again stamp out the centre of these smaller rings
4. brush over the others with the white of an egg, place a small ring on the top of every large circular piece of paste, egg over the tops, and bake from 15 to 20 minutes
5. Sift over sugar, put them back in the oven to colour them
6. fill the rings with preserve of any bright colour
7. Dish them high on a napkin, and serve
