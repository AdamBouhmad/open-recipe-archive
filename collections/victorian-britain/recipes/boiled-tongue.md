## Ingredients

- 1 tongue
- a bunch of savoury herbs
- water

## Directions

1. In choosing a tongue, ascertain how long it has been dried or pickled, and select one with a smooth skin
2. If a dried one and rather hard, soak it at least for 12 hours previous to cooking it; if fresh from the pickle, 2 or 3 hours will be sufficient
3. Put the tongue in a stewpan with plenty of cold water and a bunch of savoury herbs
4. Let it gradually come to a boil, skim well and simmer very gently until tender
5. Peel off the skin, garnish with tufts of cauliflowers or Brussels sprouts, and serve
