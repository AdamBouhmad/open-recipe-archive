## Ingredients

- The yolks of 4 eggs
- 4 oz. of pounded sugar
- 1 lemon
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- puff-crust

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs to a froth
2. Mix with them the sugar and warmed butter
3. Stir these ingredients well together, putting in the grated rind and strained juice of the lemon-peel
4. Line a shallow dish with puff-paste
5. Put in the mixture, and bake in a moderate oven for 40 minutes
6. Turn the pudding out of the dish, strew over it sifted sugar, and serve
