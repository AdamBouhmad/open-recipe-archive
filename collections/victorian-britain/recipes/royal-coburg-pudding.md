## Ingredients

- 1 pint of new milk
- 6 oz. of flour
- 6 oz. of sugar
- 6 oz. of butter
- 6 oz. of currants
- 6 eggs
- brandy and grated nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Mix the flour to a smooth batter with the milk
2. Add the remaining ingredients gradually
3. When well mixed, put it into four basins or moulds half full
4. Bake for 3/4 hour
5. Turn the puddings out on a dish, and serve with wine sauce
