## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of bread crumbs
- 1-1/2 pint of milk
- 6 oz. of marrow
- 4 eggs
- 1/4 lb. of raisins or currants, or 2 oz. of each
- sugar and grated nutmeg to taste

## Directions

1. Make the milk boiling, pour it hot on to the bread crumbs, and let these remain covered for about 1/2 hour
2. shred the marrow, beat up the eggs, and mix these with the bread crumbs
3. add the remaining ingredients
4. beat the mixture well, and either put it into a buttered mould and boil it for 2-1/2 hours, or put it into a pie-dish edged with puff-paste, and bake for rather more than 3/4 hour
5. Before sending it to table, sift a little pounded sugar over, after being turned out of the mould or basin
