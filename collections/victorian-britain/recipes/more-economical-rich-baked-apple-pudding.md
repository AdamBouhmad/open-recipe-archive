## Ingredients

- 12 large apples
- 6 oz. of moist sugar
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 4 eggs
- 1 pint of bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and cut the apples, as for sauce
2. Boil them until reduced to a pulp
3. Add the butter, melted, and the eggs, which should be well whisked
4. Beat up the pudding for 2 or 3 minutes
5. Butter a pie-dish
6. Put in a layer of bread crumbs
7. Then the apple
8. Then another layer of bread crumbs
9. Flake over these a few tiny pieces of butter
10. Bake for about 1/2 hour
