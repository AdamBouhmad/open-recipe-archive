## Ingredients

- Kidneys
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Skin the kidneys and pass a skewer under the white part of each half to keep them flat
2. Broil over a nice clear fire, placing the inside downwards
3. Turn them when done enough on one side, and cook them on the other
4. Remove the skewers and place the kidneys on a very hot dish
5. Season with pepper and salt, and put a tiny piece of butter in the middle of each
6. Serve very hot and quickly, and send very hot plates to table
