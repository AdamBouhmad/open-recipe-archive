## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls of grated horseradish
- 1 teaspoonful of pounded sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of pepper
- 2 teaspoonfuls of made mustard
- vinegar
- 3 or 4 tablespoonfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Grate the horseradish, and mix it well with the sugar, salt, pepper, and mustard
2. Moisten it with sufficient vinegar to give it the consistency of cream
3. Serve in a tureen
4. To heat it to serve with hot roast beef, put it in a bain marie or a jar, which place in a saucepan of boiling water
5. Make it hot, but do not allow it to boil, or it will curdle
