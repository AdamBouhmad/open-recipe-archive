## Ingredients

- 3 partridges
- salt
- cayenne
- a small piece of butter
- brown gravy or mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Pluck, draw, and cut the partridges in half, and wipe the inside thoroughly with a damp cloth.
2. Season them with salt and cayenne, broil them over a very clear fire, and dish them on a hot dish.
3. Rub a small piece of butter over each half, and send them to table with brown gravy or mushroom sauce.
