## Ingredients

- Veal stuffing
- buttered paper
- the tail-end of a sturgeon

## Directions

1. Cleanse the fish, bone and skin it
2. Make a nice veal stuffing (see Forcemeats), and fill it with the part where the bones came from
3. Roll it in buttered paper
4. Bind it up firmly with tape, like a fillet of veal
5. Roast it in a Dutch oven before a clear fire
6. Serve with good brown gravy, or plain melted butter
