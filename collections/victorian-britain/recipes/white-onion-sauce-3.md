## Ingredients

- 9 large onions, or 12 middling-sized ones
- 1 pint of melted butter made with milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt, or rather more
- salt (for soaking water)

## Directions

1. Peel the onions and put them into water to which a little salt has been added, to preserve their whiteness, and let them remain for 1/4 hour
2. Then put them in a stewpan, cover them with water, and let them boil until tender; if the onions should be very strong, change the water after they have been boiling for 1/4 hour
3. Drain them thoroughly, chop them, and rub them through a tammy or sieve
4. Make 1 pint of melted butter, and when that boils, put in the onions, with a seasoning of salt
5. Stir it till it simmers, when it will be ready to serve
