## Ingredients

- 1/2 a pint of green peas, if in season
- a little chervil
- 2 shredded lettuces
- 2 onions
- a very small bunch of parsley
- 2 oz. of butter
- the yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 pint of water
- seasoning to taste
- 2 quarts of stock No. 105

## Directions

1. Put in a very clean stewpan the chervil, lettuces, onions, parsley, and butter, to 1 pint of water, and let them simmer till tender
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. When done, strain off the vegetables, and put two-thirds of the liquor they were boiled in to the stock
4. Beat up the yolks of the eggs with the other third, give it a toss over the fire
5. At the moment of serving, add this, with the vegetables which you strained off, to the soup
