## Ingredients

- 1 pint of port wine
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1 lemon
- grated nutmeg
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Rub lumps of sugar on the lemon-rind until all the yellow part of the skin is absorbed
2. Squeeze the juice and strain it
3. Add the sugar and lemon-juice to the port wine, with the grated nutmeg
4. Pour over it the boiling water
5. Cover the jug
6. When the beverage has cooled a little, it will be fit for use
