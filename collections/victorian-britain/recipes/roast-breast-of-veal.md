## Ingredients

- Veal
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Wash the veal, well wipe it, and dredge it with flour; put it down to a bright fire, not too near, as it should not be scorched.
2. Baste it plentifully until done; dish it, pour over the meat some good melted butter, and send to table with it a piece of boiled bacon and a cut lemon.
