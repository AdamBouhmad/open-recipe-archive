## Ingredients

- Ham
- Common crust

## Directions

1. Soak the ham in water for at least 12 hours
2. Wipe it dry and trim away any rusty places underneath
3. Cover it with a common crust, taking care that it is of sufficient thickness all over to keep the gravy in
4. Place it in a moderately-heated oven
5. Bake for nearly 4 hours
6. Take off the crust and skin
7. Cover with raspings, the same as for boiled ham
8. Garnish the knuckle with a paper frill
