## Ingredients

- 7 or 8 large carrots
- 1 teacupful of broth
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1/2 teacupful of cream
- butter and flour for thickening

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots nicely; half-boil, and slice them into a stewpan
2. Add the broth, pepper and salt, and cream
3. Simmer till tender, being careful the carrots are not broken
4. A few minutes before serving, mix a little flour with about 1 oz. of butter
5. Thicken the gravy with this mixture
6. Let it just boil up, and serve
