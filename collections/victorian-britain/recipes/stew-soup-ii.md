## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of beef, mutton, or pork
- 1/2 pint of split peas
- 4 turnips
- 8 potatoes
- 2 onions
- 2 oz. of oatmeal or 3 oz. of rice
- 2 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat in small pieces, as also the vegetables, and add them, with the peas, to the water
2. Boil gently for 3 hours
3. thicken with the oatmeal, boil for another 1/4 hour, stirring all the time, and season with pepper and salt
