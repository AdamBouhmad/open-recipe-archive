## Ingredients

- 1 sheep's head
- sufficient water to cover it
- 3 carrots
- 3 turnips
- 2 or 3 parsnips
- 3 onions
- a small bunch of parsley
- 1 teaspoonful of pepper
- 3 teaspoonfuls of salt
- 1/4 lb. of Scotch oatmeal

## Directions

1. Clean the head well, and let it soak in warm water for 2 hours, to get rid of the blood.
2. Put it into a saucepan, with sufficient cold water to cover it, and when it boils, add the vegetables, peeled and sliced, and the remaining ingredients.
3. Before adding the oatmeal, mix it to a smooth batter with a little of the liquor.
4. Keep stirring till it boils up; then shut the saucepan closely, and let it stew gently for 1-1/2 or 2 hours.
5. It may be thickened with rice or barley, but oatmeal is preferable.
