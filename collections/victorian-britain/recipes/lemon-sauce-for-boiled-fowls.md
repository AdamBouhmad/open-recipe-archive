## Ingredients

- 1 small lemon
- 3/4 pint of melted butter

## Directions

1. Cut the lemon into very thin slices, and these again into very small dice
2. Have ready 3/4 pint of melted butter
3. Put in the lemon
4. Let it just simmer, but not boil, and pour it over the fowls
