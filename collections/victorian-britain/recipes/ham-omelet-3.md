## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 4 oz. of butter
- 1/2 saltspoonful of pepper
- 2 tablespoonfuls of minced ham

## Directions

1. Mince the ham very finely, without any fat, and fry it for 2 minutes in a little butter
2. Make the batter for the omelet, stir in the ham, and proceed as directed in recipe No. 1456
3. Do not add any salt to the batter, as the ham is usually sufficiently salt to impart a flavour to the omelet
4. Serve very hot and quickly, without gravy
