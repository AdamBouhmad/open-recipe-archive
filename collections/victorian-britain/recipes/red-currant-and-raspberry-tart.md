## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 pint of picked currants
- 1/2 pint of raspberries
- 3 heaped tablespoonfuls of moist sugar
- 1/2 lb. of short crust
- sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Strip the currants from the stalks, and put them into a deep pie-dish, with a small cup placed in the midst, bottom upwards
2. Add the raspberries and sugar
3. Place a border of paste round the edge of the dish, cover with crust, and ornament the edges
4. Bake from 1/2 to 3/4 hour
5. Strew some sifted sugar over before being sent to table
