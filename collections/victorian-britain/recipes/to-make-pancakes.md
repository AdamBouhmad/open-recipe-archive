## Ingredients

- eggs
- flour
- milk
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Ascertain that the eggs are fresh and break each one separately in a cup
2. Whisk them well and put them into a basin with the flour, salt, and a few drops of milk
3. Beat the whole to a perfectly smooth batter
4. Add by degrees the remainder of the milk
5. The batter, when ready for frying, should be of the consistency of thick cream
6. Place a small frying-pan on the fire to get hot
7. Let it be delicately clean so the pancakes will not stick
8. When quite hot, put into it a small piece of butter, allowing about 1/2 oz. to each pancake
9. When it is melted, pour in the batter, about 1/2 teacupful to a pan 5 inches in diameter
10. Fry it for about 4 minutes, or until it is nicely brown on one side
11. By pouring in a small quantity of batter and so making the pancakes thin, the necessity of turning them is obviated
