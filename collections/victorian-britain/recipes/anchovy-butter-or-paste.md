## Ingredients

- 2 dozen anchovies
- 1/2 lb. of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Wash the anchovies thoroughly
2. Bone and dry them, and pound them in a mortar to a paste
3. Mix the butter gradually with them, and rub the whole through a sieve
4. Put it by in small pots for use, and carefully exclude the air with a bladder, as it soon changes the colour of anchovies, besides spoiling them
