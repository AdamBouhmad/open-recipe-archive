## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of the middle, or best end of the neck of mutton
- salt

## Directions

1. Trim off a portion of the fat, should there be too much
2. If it is to look particularly nice, the chine-bone should be sawn down, the ribs stripped halfway down, and the ends of the bones chopped off
3. Put the meat into sufficient boiling water to cover it
4. When it boils, add a little salt and remove all the scum
5. Draw the saucepan to the side of the fire, and let the water get so cool that the finger may be borne in it
6. Simmer very slowly and gently
