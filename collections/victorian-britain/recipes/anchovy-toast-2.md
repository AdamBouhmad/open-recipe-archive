## Ingredients

- toast (2 or 3 slices of bread)
- clarified butter
- anchovy paste
- made mustard or cayenne

## Directions

1. Toast 2 or 3 slices of bread, or if wanted very savoury, fry them in clarified butter
2. Spread the paste on them
3. May add made mustard or a few grains of cayenne to the paste before laying it on the toast
