## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 1 pint of water
- 1 oz. butter
- 1 oz. salt
- a little lemon-juice
- 2 middling-sized soles
- good mushroom sauce

## Directions

1. Cleanse the soles, but do not skin them, and lay them in a fish-kettle, with the milk, water, butter, salt, and lemon-juice.
2. Bring them gradually to boil, and let them simmer very gently till done, which will be in about 7 minutes.
3. Take them up, drain them well on a cloth, put them on a hot dish, and pour over them a good mushroom sauce.
