## Ingredients

- Leg of lamb
- Béchamel sauce, No. 367

## Directions

1. Do not choose a very large joint, but one weighing about 5 lbs.
2. Have ready a saucepan of boiling water, into which plunge the lamb
3. When it boils up again, draw it to the side of the fire, and let the water cool a little
4. Then stew very gently for about 1-1/4 hour, reckoning from the time that the water begins to simmer
5. Make some Béchamel by recipe No. 367, dish the lamb, pour the sauce over it, and garnish with tufts of boiled cauliflower or carrots
6. When liked, melted butter may be substituted for the Béchamel
7. Send to table with it some of the sauce in a tureen, and boiled cauliflowers or spinach
