## Ingredients

- To every lb. of raspberries allow 1 lb. of sugar
- 1/4 pint of red-currant juice

## Directions

1. Let the fruit for this preserve be gathered in fine weather, and used as soon after it is picked as possible
2. Take off the stalks, put the raspberries into a preserving-pan, break them well with a wooden spoon, and let them boil for 1/4 hour, keeping them well stirred
3. Add the currant-juice and sugar, and boil again for 1/2 hour
4. Skim the jam well after the sugar is added
