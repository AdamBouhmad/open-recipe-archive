## Ingredients

- cherries
- raspberries
- currants
- strawberries
- leaves

## Directions

1. Pile cherries, raspberries, currants, and strawberries in different layers, with plenty of leaves between each layer; so that each fruit is well separated.
2. The fruit should be arranged with a due regard to colour, so that they contrast nicely one with the other.
3. Arrange a layer of white cherries at the bottom, then one of red raspberries; over that a layer of white currants, and at the top some fine scarlet strawberries.
