## Ingredients

- 16 eggs
- 1 quart of vinegar
- 1/2 oz. of Black pepper
- 1/2 oz. of Jamaica pepper
- 1/2 oz. of ginger

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for 12 minutes, then dip them into cold water, and take off the shells.
2. Put the vinegar, with the pepper and ginger, into a stewpan, and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
3. Place the eggs in a jar, pour over them the vinegar, &c., boiling hot, and, when cold, tie them down with bladder to exclude the air.
4. This pickle will be ready for use in a month.
