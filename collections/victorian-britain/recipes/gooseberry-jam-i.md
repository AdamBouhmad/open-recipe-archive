## Ingredients

- red hairy gooseberries
- loaf sugar (3/4 lb. per lb. of fruit)
- red-currant juice (1/2 pint per 6 lbs. of fruit)
- oiled and egged papers

## Directions

1. Select red hairy gooseberries; have them gathered in dry weather, when quite ripe, without being too soft
2. Weigh them; with a pair of scissors, cut off the tops and tails
3. Prepare 1/2 pint of red-currant juice per 6 lbs. of fruit, drawn as for jelly
4. Put the gooseberries and currant-juice into a preserving-pan
5. Let them boil tolerably quickly, keeping them well stirred
6. When they begin to break, add to them the sugar, and keep simmering until the jam becomes firm
7. Carefully skim and stir it, that it does not burn at the bottom
8. It should be boiled rather a long time, or it will not keep
9. Put it into pots (not too large); let it get perfectly cold
10. Cover the pots down with oiled and egged papers
