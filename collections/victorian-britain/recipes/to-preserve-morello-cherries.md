## Ingredients

- cherries
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Select ripe cherries; pick off the stalks, and reject all that have any blemishes
2. Boil the sugar and water together for 5 minutes
3. Put in the cherries, and boil them for 10 minutes, removing the scum as it rises
4. Turn the fruit into a pan, and let it remain until the next day
5. Boil it all again for another 10 minutes, and, if necessary, skim well
6. Put the cherries into small pots; pour over them the syrup, and, when cold, cover down with oiled papers, and the tops of the jars with tissue-paper brushed over on both sides with the white of an egg, and keep in a dry place
