## Ingredients

- 6 oz. butter
- 6 onions sliced
- 4 heads of celery
- 2 lettuces
- a small bunch of parsley
- 2 handfuls of spinach
- 3 pieces of bread-crust
- 2 blades of mace
- salt and pepper to taste
- the yolks of 2 eggs
- 3 teaspoonfuls of vinegar
- 2 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a stewpan, and put in the onions to stew gently for 3 or 4 minutes.
2. Then add the celery, spinach, lettuces, and parsley, cut small.
3. Stir the ingredients well for 10 minutes.
4. Now put in the water, bread, seasoning, and mace.
5. Boil gently for 1-1/2 hour, and, at the moment of serving, beat in the yolks of the eggs and the vinegar, but do not let it boil, or the eggs will curdle.
