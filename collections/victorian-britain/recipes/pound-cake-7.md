## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 1-1/4 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of pounded loaf sugar
- 1 lb. of currants
- 9 eggs
- 2 oz. of candied peel
- 1/2 oz. of citron
- 1/2 oz. of sweet almonds
- a little pounded mace when liked

## Directions

1. Work the butter to a cream
2. Dredge in the flour
3. Add the sugar, currants, candied peel which should be cut into neat slices, and the almonds which should be blanched and chopped, and mix all these well together
4. Whisk the eggs, and let them be thoroughly blended with the dry ingredients
5. Beat the cake well for 20 minutes
6. Put it into a round tin, lined at the bottom and sides with a strip of white buttered paper
7. Bake it from 1-1/2 to 2 hours in a well heated oven
