## Ingredients

- 1/2 gallon of water
- 1 heaped tablespoonful of salt
- carrots

## Directions

1. Cut off the green tops
2. wash and scrape the carrots
3. should there be any black specks, remove them
4. If very large, cut them in halves, divide them lengthwise into four pieces
5. put them into boiling water, salted in the above proportion
6. let them boil until tender, which may be ascertained by thrusting a fork into them
7. dish, and serve very hot
