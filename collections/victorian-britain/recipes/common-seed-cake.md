## Ingredients

- 1/2 quartern of dough
- 1/4 lb. of good dripping
- 6 oz. of moist sugar
- 1/2 oz. of caraway seeds
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. If the dough is sent in from the baker's, put it in a basin covered with a cloth, and set it in a warm place to rise
2. With a wooden spoon beat the dripping to a liquid
3. Add it, with the other ingredients, to the dough, and beat it until everything is very thoroughly mixed
4. Put it into a buttered tin
5. Bake the cake for rather more than 2 hours
