## Ingredients

- 3 oz. of isinglass or gelatine
- 2 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Put the isinglass or gelatine into a saucepan with the above proportion of cold water
2. Bring it quickly to boil, and let it boil very fast, until the liquor is reduced one-half
3. Carefully remove the scum as it rises
4. Strain it through a jelly-bag, and it will be ready for use
5. If not required very clear, it may be merely strained through a fine sieve, instead of being run through a bag
