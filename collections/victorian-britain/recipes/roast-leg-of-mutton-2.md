## Ingredients

- Leg of mutton
- salt
- flour
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Hang the mutton almost as long as it will keep
2. Flour it, and put it in a cool airy place for a few days, if the weather will permit
3. Wash off the flour and wipe it very dry
4. Cut off the shank-bone and put it down to a brisk clear fire
5. Dredge with flour, and keep continually basting the whole time it is cooking
6. About 20 minutes before serving, draw it near the fire to get nicely brown
7. Sprinkle over it a little salt, dish the meat, pour off the dripping
8. Add some boiling water slightly salted, strain it over the joint, and serve
