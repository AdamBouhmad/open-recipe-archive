## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 oz. of butter
- 1/4 pint of milk
- 1 large teaspoonful of yeast
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Warm the butter in the milk, add to it the yeast and salt, and mix these ingredients well together.
2. Put the flour into a pan, stir in the above ingredients, and let the dough rise, covered in a warm place.
3. Knead it well, make it into rolls, let them rise again for a few minutes, and bake in a quick oven.
4. Richer rolls may be made by adding 1 or 2 eggs and a larger proportion of butter, and their appearance improved by brushing the tops over with yolk of egg or a little milk.
