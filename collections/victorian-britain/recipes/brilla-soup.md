## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of shin of beef
- 3 carrots
- 2 turnips
- a large sprig of thyme
- 2 onions
- 1 head of celery
- salt and pepper to taste
- 4 quarts water

## Directions

1. Take the beef, cut off all the meat from the bone, in nice square pieces, and boil the bone for 4 hours
2. Strain the liquor, let it cool, and take off the fat
3. Put the pieces of meat in the cold liquor
4. Cut small the carrots, turnips, and celery; chop the onions, add them with the thyme and seasoning, and simmer till the meat is tender
5. If not brown enough, colour it with browning
