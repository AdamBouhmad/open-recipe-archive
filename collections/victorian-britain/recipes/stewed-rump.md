## Ingredients

- A rump of beef
- bunch of savoury herbs
- 2 onions
- 12 cloves
- pepper and salt to taste
- butter and flour for thickening
- 1 glass of port wine
- vinegar
- ketchup

## Directions

1. Cut out the bone, sprinkle the meat with a little cayenne, and bind and tie it firmly up with tape
2. Put it into a stewpan with sufficient stock to cover it, and add vinegar, ketchup, herbs, onions, cloves, and seasoning
3. Simmer very gently for 4 or 5 hours, or until the meat is perfectly tender, which may be ascertained by piercing it with a thin skewer
4. When done, remove the tape, lay it into a deep dish, which keep hot
5. Strain and skim the gravy, thicken it with butter and flour, add a glass of port wine and any flavouring to make the gravy rich and palatable
6. Let it boil up, pour over the meat, and serve
