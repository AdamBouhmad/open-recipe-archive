## Ingredients

- 1 Seville orange
- 1 tablespoonful of brandy
- 1/4 lb. of loaf sugar
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 1 pint of cream
- candied orange-peel or preserved chips

## Directions

1. Boil the rind of the Seville orange until tender and beat it in a mortar to a pulp
2. Add to it the brandy, the strained juice of the orange, and the sugar
3. Beat all together for about 10 minutes, adding the well-beaten yolks of eggs
4. Bring the cream to the boiling-point and pour it very gradually to the other ingredients
5. Beat the mixture till nearly cold
6. Put it into custard-cups and place the cups in a deep dish of boiling water
7. Let them remain till quite cold
8. Take the cups out of the water and wipe them
9. Garnish the tops of the creams with candied orange-peel or preserved chips
