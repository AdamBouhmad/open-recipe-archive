## Ingredients

- slices of salmon, 1 inch thick
- pepper and salt
- butter
- white paper
- anchovy or caper sauce
- chopped herbs (optional)
- spice (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut the slices 1 inch thick, and season them with pepper and salt
2. butter a sheet of white paper, lay each slice on a separate piece, with their ends twisted
3. broil gently over a clear fire
4. serve with anchovy or caper sauce
5. When higher seasoning is required, add a few chopped herbs and a little spice
