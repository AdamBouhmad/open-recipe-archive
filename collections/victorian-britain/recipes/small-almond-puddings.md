## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sifted sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 1 tablespoonful of brandy
- a spoonful of water

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound the almonds to a smooth paste with a spoonful of water
2. Warm the butter, mix the almonds with this, and add the other ingredients, leaving out the whites of 2 eggs
3. Be particular that these are well beaten
4. Mix well, butter some cups, half fill them, and bake the puddings from 20 minutes to 1/2 hour
5. Turn them out on a dish, and serve with sweet sauce
