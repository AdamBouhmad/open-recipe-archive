## Ingredients

- strawberries, raspberries, currants, or any of these fruits mixed, or peaches
- finely-pounded sugar
- wine or brandy, or liqueur
- pounded cinnamon

## Directions

1. Strip the fruit from the stalks, pile it on a dish, and sprinkle over it finely-pounded sugar
2. Pour about 6 large tablespoonfuls of wine or brandy, or 3 tablespoonfuls of liqueur, in the middle of the fruit
3. When the flavour is liked, a little pounded cinnamon may be added
4. When helping the fruit, lightly stir it, that the wine and sugar may be equally distributed
