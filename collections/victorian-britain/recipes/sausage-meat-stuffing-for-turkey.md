## Ingredients

- 6 oz. of lean pork
- 6 oz. of fat pork
- 2 oz. of bread crumbs
- 1 small tablespoonful of minced sage
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Chop the meat and fat very finely, mix with them the other ingredients, taking care that the whole is thoroughly incorporated.
2. Moisten with the egg, and the stuffing will be ready for use.
