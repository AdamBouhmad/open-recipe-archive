## Ingredients

- A calf's liver
- flour
- a bunch of savoury herbs, including parsley
- 2 minced shalots
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- 1 tablespoonful of vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon-juice
- pepper and salt to taste
- 1/4 pint water
- butter

## Directions

1. Procure a calf's liver as white as possible, and cut it into slices of a good and equal shape
2. Dip them in flour, and fry them of a good colour in a little butter
3. When they are done, put them on a dish, which keep hot before the fire
4. Mince the herbs very fine, put them in the frying-pan with a little more butter
5. Add the remaining ingredients, simmer gently until the herbs are done, and pour over the liver
