## Ingredients

- Equal quantities of fat bacon and lean veal
- 1 teaspoonful of minced sage per lb. of meat
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Chop the meat and bacon finely
2. To every lb. allow minced sage
3. Add a seasoning of pepper and salt
4. Mix the whole well together
5. Make it into flat cakes
6. Fry a nice brown
