## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of grated bread
- 1/4 lb. of chopped suet
- 1/4 lb. of moist sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 large lemon

## Directions

1. Mix the bread, suet, and moist sugar well together, adding the lemon-peel, which should be very finely minced
2. Moisten with the eggs and strained lemon-juice
3. Stir well, and put the mixture into small buttered cups
4. Tie them down and boil for 3/4 hour
5. Turn them out on a dish, strew sifted sugar over them, and serve with wine sauce
