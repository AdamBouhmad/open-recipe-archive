## Ingredients

- 4 oz. or 1 tablespoonful of ground coffee per person
- 1/3 pint of water per oz. of coffee

## Directions

1. Warm the urn with boiling water
2. Remove the lid and movable filter and place the ground coffee at the bottom of the urn
3. Put the movable filter over this and screw the lid, inverted, tightly on the end of the centre pipe
4. Pour into the inverted lid the proportion of boiling water
5. When all the water has disappeared from the funnel and made its way down the centre pipe and up again through the ground coffee by hydrostatic pressure, unscrew the lid and cover the urn
6. Pour back direct into the urn, not through the funnel, one, two, or three cups according to the size of the percolator, in order to make the infusion of uniform strength
7. The contents will then be ready for use and should run from the tap strong, hot, and clear
8. Coffee should be served very hot in the same vessel in which it is made
