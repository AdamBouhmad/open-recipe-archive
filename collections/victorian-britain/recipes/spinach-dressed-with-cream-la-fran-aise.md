## Ingredients

- 2 pailfuls of spinach
- 2 tablespoonfuls of salt
- 2 oz. of butter
- 8 tablespoonfuls of cream
- 1 small teaspoonful of pounded sugar
- a very little grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil and drain the spinach as in recipe No. 1155
2. chop it finely, and put it into a stewpan with the butter
3. stir over a gentle fire, and, when the butter has dried away, add the remaining ingredients, and simmer for about 5 minutes
4. Previously to adding the cream, boil it first, in case it should curdle
5. Serve on a hot dish, and garnish either with sippets of toasted bread or leaves of puff-paste
