## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of suet
- 1/4 lb. of currants
- 1/4 lb. of raisins
- 1/4 lb. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of treacle
- 1/2 pint of milk

## Directions

1. Chop the suet finely
2. Mix with it the currants, which should be nicely washed and dried
3. Add the raisins, which should be stoned
4. Add the flour, bread crumbs, and treacle
5. Moisten with the milk
6. Beat up the ingredients until all are thoroughly mixed
7. Put them into a buttered basin
8. Boil the pudding for 3-1/2 hours
