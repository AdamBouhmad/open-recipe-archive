## Ingredients

- salt and water
- 1 oz. of bruised ginger
- 1/2 oz. of whole black pepper
- 1/4 oz. of whole allspice
- 4 cloves
- 2 blades of mace
- a little horseradish
- 1 quart of vinegar

## Directions

1. Let the gherkins remain in salt and water for 3 or 4 days
2. Take them out and wipe perfectly dry
3. Put them into a stone jar
4. Boil sufficient vinegar to cover them with spices and pepper in the stated proportions for 10 minutes
5. Pour it, quite boiling, over the gherkins
6. Cover the jar with vine-leaves and put over them a plate
7. Set them near the fire where they must remain all night
8. Next day drain off the vinegar
9. Boil it up again and pour it hot over the gherkins
10. Cover up with fresh leaves and let the whole remain till quite cold
11. Tie down closely with bladder to exclude the air
12. In a month or two, they will be fit for use
