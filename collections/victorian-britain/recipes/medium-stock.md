## Ingredients

- 4 lbs. of shin of beef, or 4 lbs. of knuckle of veal, or 2 lbs. of each
- any bones, trimmings of poultry, or fresh meat
- 1/2 a lb. of lean bacon or ham
- 2 oz. of butter
- 2 large onions
- 3 cloves
- 1 turnip
- 3 carrots
- 1/2 a leek
- 1 head of celery
- 2 oz. of salt
- 1/2 a teaspoonful of whole pepper
- 1 large blade of mace
- 1 small bunch of savoury herbs
- 4 quarts and 1/2 pint of cold water

## Directions

1. Cut up the meat and bacon or ham into pieces about 3 inches square
2. Rub the butter on the bottom of the stewpan
3. Put in 1/2 a pint of water, the meat, and all the other ingredients
4. Cover the stewpan, and place it on a sharp fire, occasionally stirring its contents
5. When the bottom of the pan becomes covered with a pale, jelly-like substance, add 4 quarts of cold water, and simmer very gently for 5 hours
6. Do not let it boil quickly
7. Skim off every particle of grease whilst it is doing, and strain it through a fine hair sieve
