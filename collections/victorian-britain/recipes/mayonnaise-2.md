## Ingredients

- yolks of 2 eggs
- 6 tablespoonfuls of salad-oil
- 4 tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- salt and white pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of white stock
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Put the yolks of the eggs into a basin, with a seasoning of pepper and salt.
2. Have ready the above quantities of oil and vinegar, in separate vessels.
3. Add them very gradually to the eggs.
4. Continue stirring and rubbing the mixture with a wooden spoon.
5. It should be made in a very cool place, or, if ice is at hand, it should be mixed over it.
6. When the vinegar and oil are well incorporated with the eggs, add the stock and cream, stirring all the time, and it will then be ready for use.
