## Ingredients

- minced lobster
- 4 tablespoonfuls of béchamel
- 6 drops of anchovy sauce
- lemon-juice
- cayenne to taste
- puff-paste
- bread
- egg

## Directions

1. Line the patty-pans with puff-paste, and put into each a small piece of bread
2. Cover with paste, brush over with egg, and bake of a light colour
3. Take as much lobster as is required, mince the meat very fine, and add the béchamel, anchovy sauce, lemon-juice, and cayenne
4. Stir it over the fire for 6 minutes
5. Remove the lids of the patty-cases, take out the bread, fill with the mixture, and replace the covers
