## Ingredients

- sprigs of holly
- oiled butter
- coarsely-powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Procure some nice sprigs of holly; pick the leaves from the stalks, and wipe them with a clean cloth free from all moisture
2. Place them on a dish near the fire, to get thoroughly dry, but not too near to shrivel the leaves
3. Dip them into oiled butter, sprinkle over them some coarsely-powdered sugar
4. Dry them before the fire
5. Keep in a dry place, as the least damp would spoil their appearance
