## Ingredients

- 1 pint of mushroom-buttons
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- 1 blade of pounded mace
- 1 pint of cream
- 2 oz. of butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Rub the buttons with a piece of flannel and salt, to take off the skin.
2. Cut off the stalks, and put them in a stewpan with the above ingredients, previously kneading together the butter and flour.
3. Boil the whole for about ten minutes, stirring all the time.
4. Pour some of the sauce over the fowls, and the remainder serve in a tureen.
