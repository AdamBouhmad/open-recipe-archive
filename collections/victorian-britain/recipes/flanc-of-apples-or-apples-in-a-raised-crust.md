## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of short crust No. 1211 or 1212
- 9 moderate-sized apples
- the rind and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 lb. of white sugar
- 3/4 pint of water
- a few strips of candied citron
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a short crust by either of the above recipes
2. Roll it out to the thickness of 1/2 inch and butter an oval mould
3. Line it with the crust and press it carefully all round the sides to obtain the form of the mould, but be particular not to break the paste
4. Pinch the part that just rises above the mould with the paste-pincers
5. Fill the case with flour and bake it for about 3/4 hour
6. Take it out of the oven and remove the flour
7. Put the case back in the oven for another 1/4 hour and do not allow it to get scorched
8. Peel and take out the cores of the apples with a small knife or a cutter without dividing the apples
9. Put them into a small lined saucepan with sugar, water, lemon juice and rind in the above proportion
10. Let them simmer very gently until tender
11. Take out the apples and let them cool
12. Arrange them in the flanc or case
13. Boil down the syrup until reduced to a thick jelly
14. Pour it over the apples and garnish them with a few slices of candied citron
