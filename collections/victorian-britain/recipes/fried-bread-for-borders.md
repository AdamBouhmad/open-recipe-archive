## Ingredients

- bread
- lard or clarified dripping
- beaten white of an egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut slices of bread in any fanciful shape
2. Fry them in clear boiling lard or clarified dripping until quite crisp
3. Dip one side of the sippet into the beaten white of an egg mixed with a little flour
4. Place it on the edge of the dish
5. Continue in this manner till the border is completed
6. Arrange the sippets alternately, a pale and a dark one
