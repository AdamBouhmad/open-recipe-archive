## Ingredients

- 1 large teacupful of linseed
- 1/4 lb. of sun raisins
- 2 oz. of stick liquorice
- 2 quarts of soft water
- 1/4 lb. of pounded sugar-candy
- 1 tablespoonful of old rum
- 1 tablespoonful of best white-wine vinegar or lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Put linseed, sun raisins, and stick liquorice into soft water
2. Let simmer over a slow fire till reduced to one quart
3. Add pounded sugar-candy
4. Add rum and vinegar as the decoction is taken (not at first, as this makes it flat and less efficacious)
5. The dose is half a pint, made warm, on going to bed
6. A little may be taken whenever the cough is troublesome
