## Ingredients

- 1 loin of lamb
- a few slices of bacon
- 1 bunch of green onions
- 5 or 6 young carrots
- a bunch of savoury herbs
- 2 blades of pounded mace
- 1 pint of stock
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Bone a loin of lamb, and line the bottom of a stewpan just capable of holding it, with a few thin slices of fat bacon
2. Add the remaining ingredients, cover the meat with a few more slices of bacon, pour in the stock, and simmer very gently for 2 hours
3. Take it up, dry it, strain and reduce the gravy to a glaze, with which glaze the meat, and serve it either on stewed peas, spinach, or stewed cucumbers
