## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 pint of milk
- 3/4 pint of bread crumbs
- sugar to taste
- 4 eggs
- 1 oz. of butter
- 3 oz. of currants
- 1/4 teaspoonful of grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make the milk boiling, and pour it on the bread crumbs
2. Let these remain till cold
3. Add the other ingredients, taking care that the eggs are well beaten and the currants well washed, picked, and dried
4. Beat the pudding well, and put it into a buttered basin
5. Tie it down tightly with a cloth
6. Plunge it into boiling water, and boil for 1-1/4 hour
7. Turn it out of the basin, and serve with sifted sugar
8. Any odd pieces or scraps of bread answer for this pudding; but they should be soaked overnight, and, when wanted for use, should have the water well squeezed from them
