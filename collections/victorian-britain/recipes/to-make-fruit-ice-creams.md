## Ingredients

- 1 pint of fruit-juice
- 1 pint of cream
- sugar to taste
- melted isinglass (optional, for moulding)

## Directions

1. Let the fruit be well ripened; pick it off the stalks, and put it into a large earthen pan.
2. Stir it about with a wooden spoon, breaking it until it is well mashed.
3. With the back of the spoon, rub it through a hair sieve.
4. Sweeten it nicely with pounded sugar.
5. Whip the cream for a few minutes, add it to the fruit, and whisk the whole again for another 5 minutes.
6. Put the mixture into the freezing-pot, and freeze in the same manner as directed for Ice Pudding, No. 1290, taking care to stir the cream, &c., two or three times, and to remove it from the sides of the vessel, that the mixture may be equally frozen and smooth.
7. If moulded, add a small quantity of melted isinglass to enable them to keep their shape.
