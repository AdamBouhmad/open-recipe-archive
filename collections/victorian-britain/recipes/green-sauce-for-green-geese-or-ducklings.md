## Ingredients

- 1/4 pint of sorrel-juice
- 1 glass of sherry
- 1/2 pint of green gooseberries
- 1 teaspoonful of pounded sugar
- 1 oz. of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Boil the gooseberries in water until they are quite tender
2. Mash them and press them through a sieve
3. Put the pulp into a saucepan with the above ingredients
4. Simmer for 3 or 4 minutes
5. Serve very hot
