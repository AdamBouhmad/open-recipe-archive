## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 pike
- a nice delicate stuffing
- 1 egg
- bread crumbs
- 1/4 lb. butter

## Directions

1. Scale the fish, take out the gills, wash, and wipe it thoroughly dry.
2. Stuff it with forcemeat, sew it up, and fasten the tail in the mouth by means of a skewer.
3. Brush it over with egg, sprinkle with bread crumbs, and baste with butter.
4. Put it in a well-heated oven.
5. When the pike is of a nice brown colour, cover it with buttered paper, as the outside would become too dry.
6. If 2 are dressed, a little variety may be made by making one of them green with a little chopped parsley mixed with the bread crumbs.
7. Serve anchovy or Dutch sauce, and plain melted butter with it.
