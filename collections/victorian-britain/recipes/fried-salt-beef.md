## Ingredients

- slices of cold salt beef
- pepper
- 1/4 lb. of butter
- mashed potatoes
- pickled gherkins
- liquor from mixed pickle

## Directions

1. Cut any part of cold salt beef into thin slices.
2. Fry them gently in butter, and season with a little pepper.
3. Have ready some very hot mashed potatoes, lay the slices of beef on them.
4. Garnish with 3 or 4 pickled gherkins.
5. Alternatively, cold salt beef may be warmed in a little liquor from mixed pickle, drained, and served as above.
