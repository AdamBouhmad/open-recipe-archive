## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of coriander-seed
- 1/4 lb. of turmeric
- 2 oz. of cinnamon-seed
- 1/2 oz. of cayenne
- 1 oz. of mustard
- 1 oz. of ground ginger
- 1/2 ounce of allspice
- 2 oz. of fenugreek-seed

## Directions

1. Put all the ingredients in a cool oven, where they should remain one night
2. Pound them in a mortar
3. Rub them through a sieve
4. Mix thoroughly together
5. Keep the powder in a bottle, from which the air should be completely excluded
