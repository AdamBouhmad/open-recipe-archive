## Ingredients

- veal
- parsley
- two or three shallots
- refined oil
- salt
- pepper
- beef or pork fat
- flour
- broth or veal cooking juices
- vinegar
- watercress
- salt water

## Directions

1. Prepare a marinade with lots of parsley and two or three shallots, chopped fine and dissolved in a couple spoonfuls of refined oil, to which salt and pepper are added
2. Rub the piece of veal successively on all sides with this marinade so that by the time it goes to the roaster it has absorbed all the oil from the marinade
3. Spread the chopped fine herbs well soaked in oil over the entire surface of the veal
4. Wrap the meat tightly in greased paper, fastened securely with string to prevent the herbs from shifting or falling
5. When the roast is nearly cooked and only needs to take on color, carefully remove the paper
6. Using a metal spatula, collect the fine herbs adhered to the paper and meat
7. Place these herbs in a pan with 125 grams of beef or pork fat and a spoonful of flour to make a blonde sauce, moistened and extended with a little broth or the degreased juices from the roast and a thread of vinegar
8. Serve this sauce separately in a sauce boat, and the veal on a platter, garnished with watercress dipped in salted water
