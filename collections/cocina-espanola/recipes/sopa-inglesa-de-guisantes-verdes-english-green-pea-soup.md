## Ingredients

- water, three liters
- fresh green peas, one liter, freshly shelled
- butter
- cucumber pulp, three cucumbers, peeled, seeded, and sliced
- lettuce hearts, three tender ones
- parsley, chopped
- fine fresh peas, half liter
- bouquet of fine herbs
- small onions, two or three
- salt and pepper to taste
- mint leaves, finely chopped, one handful

## Directions

1. Cook one liter of fresh, freshly shelled peas well in three liters of water
2. Drain and crush the peas in a mortar
3. Dissolve them in their own broth and pass through a fine hair sieve
4. In butter, sauté the sliced pulp of three peeled and seeded cucumbers with three tender lettuce hearts and chopped parsley for about one hour over low heat, taking care not to let the butter darken
5. Remove the vegetables with a skimmer and add to the prepared and strained broth
6. Carefully remove all the butter that will rise to the surface
7. Put the broth back on the fire and season to taste with salt and pepper
8. Separately, cook half a liter of fine, fresh peas with a bouquet of fine herbs and two or three small onions
9. When the soup is boiling, incorporate the whole peas, which should be very well cooked
10. Pour the soup into the tureen, already cut and prepared
11. English cooks add a handful of finely chopped mint leaves to this soup
