## Ingredients

- Oil
- Garlic
- Paprika
- Salt
- Water
- Eggs (optional)

## Directions

1. Heat oil to fry with garlic
2. When the garlic is fried, remove from heat, add paprika and salt all together
3. To prevent the paprika from burning, turn it once, add water and cook
4. Season and the soup can be strained
5. Eggs can also be poached in the broth
