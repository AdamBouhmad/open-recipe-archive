## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- pork lard
- tomato sauce
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and trim the kidneys
2. Heat pork lard in a pan over very high heat
3. Fry the kidneys, being careful to distinguish between sautéing and frying, and remove them from the pan as soon as they begin to burst
4. Mix the fried kidneys with a very thick tomato sauce
5. Serve
