## Ingredients

- clams
- water
- peppercorns
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the clams and place them in a casserole with the corresponding water
2. Season with peppercorns
3. Let cook over low heat, stirring them a little
4. Sprinkle with lemon juice
