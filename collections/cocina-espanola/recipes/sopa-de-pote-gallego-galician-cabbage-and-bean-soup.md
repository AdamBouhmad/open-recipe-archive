## Ingredients

- Four pounds of cabbage
- One pound of dried white beans
- Four pounds of potatoes
- One quarter pound of lard (pork fat)
- Two or three pounds of beef
- Two pounds of ham
- One pound total of blood sausage and longaniza or chorizo
- Bread crusts for toasting

## Directions

1. Cook everything except the cabbage and beans together in a pot over low heat for six hours
2. Cook the cabbage separately by blanching it
3. Cook the beans separately so they do not fall apart before time
4. Add the blanched cabbage to the pot, then add the beans
5. Make the soup with the broth and toasted bread crusts, soaking them well
6. Serve the vegetables and meat separately in different serving dishes
