## Ingredients

- eight quail for four persons
- butter from cows
- water
- four to six types of legumes
- large croutons
- broth or concentrated meat juice

## Directions

1. Prepare, truss, and bridle the quail.
2. Brown them over strong fire in butter from cows.
3. Moisten with a quarter of a measure of water and let them cook at low fire for half an hour.
4. Separately, cook four to six different types of legumes.
5. In a baking dish, arrange the quail on large croutons, forming a star with heads grouped in the center.
6. Fill the spaces between them with cooked legumes, combining types and colors.
7. When the dish is well decorated, pour broth from the pot or concentrated meat juice.
8. Bake in the oven at gentle fire for a quarter of an hour.
