## Ingredients

- beef
- bacon pieces
- lard
- sorrel
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak the meat and remove the skin
2. Insert small pieces of bacon at intervals throughout the meat
3. Heat lard in a cazuela pot
4. Place the meat in the pot without turning it
5. Cover with an iron lid and place fire on top with coals underneath
6. Cook slowly
7. Prepare a sauce from cooked sorrel
8. Pour the sorrel sauce around the sides of the meat, not on top
9. Serve, being careful not to let the juices reduce completely
