## Ingredients

- 60 grams beef butter
- half tablespoon flour
- 1 bowl hot milk
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. To 60 grams of beef butter, melted over gentle heat, add half a tablespoon of flour
2. Stir much so the flour does not brown, and without stopping to stir, gradually add a bowl of very hot milk, and salt and pepper according to taste
3. It is better to thin this sauce rather than make it thick, because as it generally serves to reheat certain dishes, it reduces easily and thickens quickly at the slightest simmer
