## Ingredients

- thin slices of bacon
- thin slices of Trevélez ham
- twelve onions the size of apples
- a heaping spoonful of pork lard
- roasted meat juice from the previous evening
- beef broth
- one egg yolk
- a thread of vinegar

## Directions

1. Fill the bottom of a casserole with thin slices of bacon, and on top, as a second layer, place very thin slices of Trevélez ham.
2. Trim twelve onions the size of apples into slices, and use them to cover the ham and bacon.
3. When the onions have cooked almost over brisk heat, remove and set aside the ham and bacon.
4. Add a heaping spoonful of pork lard, stir the onion in the casserole with a spatula, lower the heat and take care that it does not brown.
5. Season well and add roasted meat juice from the previous evening to a small pot of broth, then bind with a dissolved egg yolk and a thread of vinegar.
6. This onion garnish serves for all kinds of dishes, and can be presented as a platter at family tables with the ham and bacon, though these should be saved for other uses.
