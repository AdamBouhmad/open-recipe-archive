## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Pork lard
- Milk
- Flour
- Butter from cow's milk
- Eggs and breadcrumbs for breading
- Oil for frying

## Directions

1. Fry the eggs in pork lard that is not boiling and let them cool on a board.
2. Separately, make béchamel (milk, flour, and butter from cow's milk).
3. Once prepared, place it with a spoon over the eggs, waiting for this paste to cool.
4. Then coat everything with eggs and breadcrumbs, frying it in very hot oil.
