## Ingredients

- tender chickens, carefully quartered
- one pound of fresh tomatoes per chicken
- pork lard
- salt and pepper to taste
- two tablespoons of flour
- four tablespoons of fresh water

## Directions

1. Scald tender chickens, carefully quartered, in boiling water and give them a strong boil for fifteen minutes.
2. Fry the tomatoes in pork lard until they thicken and the water they contain disappears.
3. Season and pass through a fine food mill to obtain a broth of light consistency.
4. Cook the chicken quarters in this tomato broth over low heat for one hour, allowing them to become saturated with all the flavor of the tomato.
5. A few minutes before serving, make a slurry with two tablespoons of flour and four tablespoons of fresh water and pour it into the saucepan, letting it cook for a very short time.
