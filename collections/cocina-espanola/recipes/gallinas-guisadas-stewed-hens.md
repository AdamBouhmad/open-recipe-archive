## Ingredients

- chicken, cleaned and prepared
- pork lard
- chopped onion
- parsley
- broth
- wine or vinegar
- fresh lard
- spices
- chopped vegetables, particularly sorrel

## Directions

1. Cut the cleaned and prepared chicken into quarters
2. Brown the quarters in pork lard with chopped onion and parsley
3. When browned, add the necessary broth to cover everything and a little wine or vinegar and fresh lard
4. Season with spices and if desired, add chopped vegetables, particularly sorrel, which make a good stew
