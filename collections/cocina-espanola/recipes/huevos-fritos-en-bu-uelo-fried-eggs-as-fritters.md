## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Lard or oil

## Directions

1. When the lard or oil is ready for frying, add the egg that has been previously emptied into a cup.
2. The amount of fat, as in all frying, must be considerable.
3. With a dry skimmer, gradually fold up the white until you can flip the egg, which will look like a fritter if the operation is carried out quickly and over high heat.
4. Serve the eggs thus fried with puffed potatoes and shoestring potatoes, serving everything together in elegant disorder on a napkin, on a silver or Ruolz plate.
