## Ingredients

- quail
- a slice of veal
- a slice of ham
- carrots
- onions
- aromatic herbs
- bacon strips
- oiled paper
- cooked peas with bacon

## Directions

1. Cook the quail in a pot with a slice of veal and another of ham, carrots, onions, and a bunch of aromatic herbs.
2. Cover immediately with strips of bacon and oiled paper.
3. Place fire above and below.
4. After removing the broth, add cooked peas with bacon.
