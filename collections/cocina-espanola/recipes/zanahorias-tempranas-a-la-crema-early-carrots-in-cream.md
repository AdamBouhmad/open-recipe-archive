## Ingredients

- Early carrots
- Water, a few spoonfuls
- A touch of cow's butter
- Salt and pepper
- Finely chopped fine herbs
- Half a cup of cream, or double the amount of milk if cream is not available
- Two egg yolks
- One tablespoon of milk
- A pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Prepare carrots as in the previous recipe and cut into thin slices the width of a peseta coin
2. Cook in a few spoonfuls of water with a touch of cow's butter, salt, pepper, and trimmed fine herbs
3. When they lack little to be entirely cooked, pour over them half a cup of cream, or if not available, double that amount of milk
4. Finish cooking over slow heat
5. At the moment of serving these carrots in a covered legume dish, bind the sauce with two egg yolks dissolved in one tablespoon of milk with a pinch of salt
