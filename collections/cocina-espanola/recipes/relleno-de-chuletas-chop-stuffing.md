## Ingredients

- a dozen shallots
- equal volume of mushrooms
- a good amount of parsley
- 250 grams of aged bacon, cut into small pieces
- 150 grams of beef or pork butter
- four tablespoons of oil
- a crumb of bread, carefully crumbled
- spices
- salt

## Directions

1. Mince very finely a dozen shallots and an equal volume of mushrooms with a good amount of parsley.
2. In a saucepan, cook 250 grams of aged bacon cut into small pieces in 150 grams of beef or pork butter.
3. Incorporate the shallots and mushrooms, then the parsley.
4. When everything is cooked, add four tablespoons of oil and a carefully crumbled bread crumb.
5. Season generously with spices and salt.
6. Spread this mixture on both sides of each chop.
7. Wrap the chops in white paper cut as shown in the figure and stained with oil.
8. Roast on the grill or in the oven.
