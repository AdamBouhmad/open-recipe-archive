## Ingredients

- One hare
- Fine strips of bacon lard
- Cow or pig lard for sautéing

## Directions

1. A hare can be roasted without larding, but it tastes better when the meatier parts are larded with very fine strips of bacon
2. Since hare meat does not have enough consistency to lard raw, once the hare is ready and prepared to go into the roaster, sauté it in cow or pig lard in a pan and only briefly, just enough for the meat to firm up slightly and acquire tenderness
