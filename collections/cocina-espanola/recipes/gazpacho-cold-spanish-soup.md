## Ingredients

- salt
- one raw bell pepper
- two tomatoes of good color and size
- crumb of half a loaf of bread, soaked in water
- one small cup of raw oil
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Place salt, raw bell pepper, and two tomatoes of good color and size in a mortar, crushing everything perfectly
2. Add the crumb of half a loaf of bread that has been prepared soaked in water
3. Mash this mixture together, adding one small cup of raw oil in the same manner as is done with mayonnaise, so that it binds well after working it for at least a quarter hour
4. Add vinegar and water to taste
5. Pass through a strainer
6. Place small pieces of bread in the resulting thick broth
7. Serve in summer during hot hours
