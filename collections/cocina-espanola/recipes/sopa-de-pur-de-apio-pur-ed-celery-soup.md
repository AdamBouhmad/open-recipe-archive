## Ingredients

- 12 celery stalks
- butter
- sugar
- broth
- half a quart of Spanish sauce
- cow butter
- poultry broth
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take 12 celery stalks; peel and wash well to remove dirt
2. Then blanch for ten minutes; cool and cut into four parts
3. Brown with butter and a little sugar
4. Cover with broth and let cook well
5. Add half a quart of Spanish sauce
6. Pass as all the others
7. While hot, add a little cow butter and poultry broth with nutmeg, and serve
