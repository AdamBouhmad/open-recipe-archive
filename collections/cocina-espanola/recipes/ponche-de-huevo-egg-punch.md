## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- 250 grams piloncillo sugar, ground
- 1 quartillo lemon water
- 1 quartillo white sugar syrup, well cooked
- 2 quartillos 92-degree alcohol without anise
- 1 quartillo strong brewed tea

## Directions

1. Mix the four egg yolks with 250 grams of ground piloncillo sugar for a quarter hour
2. Add little by little the lemon water, then the sugar syrup, then the alcohol, then the tea
3. Place everything in a large pot or pan, filling only halfway to prevent inflammation
4. Over gentle heat, constantly stir with a bundle of willow branches, heating well without allowing it to boil
