## Ingredients

- tomatoes with tender skin
- salt
- pepper
- oil

## Directions

1. Choose tomatoes of tender skin, without wrinkles, the size of eggs. Wash and dry them, and remove the stem.
2. Cut them in half crosswise and place them on a plate or dish with the cut side up.
3. Sprinkle generously with ground salt and a pinch of pepper, and drop by drop, pour oil over each half so that they soak and are well moistened.
4. To eat them, place them on radish dishes or in a soup plate, but inverted, with the rounded surface on top, sprinkled again with salt.
5. Eat them alone as a side dish between courses, or as a complement to the salad served to each diner.
