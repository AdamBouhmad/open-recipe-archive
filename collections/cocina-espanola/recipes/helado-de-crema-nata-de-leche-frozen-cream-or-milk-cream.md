## Ingredients

- One and a half quarter-liters of milk
- Half a quarter-liter of cream
- Lemon rinds
- Twelve ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Put everything in a saucepan to cook until it begins to thicken, stirring always with a spoon
2. Remove then from the heat, pass through a cloth, let it cool and then pour it into the sorbetera to freeze it
