## Ingredients

- sorrel leaves
- one lettuce heart
- a good handful of parsley, finely chopped
- butter
- two egg yolks
- water

## Directions

1. Cut a good amount of sorrel leaves and add one lettuce heart and a good handful of finely chopped parsley.
2. Place everything in a pot and cook slowly for a quarter hour with a little butter, stirring constantly.
3. When the herbs are completely broken down and well cooked, add the necessary amount of water.
4. As soon as it has boiled, strain the soup.
5. Only when the soup is in the tureen, bind it with a pair of egg yolks dissolved in water using the known procedure.
