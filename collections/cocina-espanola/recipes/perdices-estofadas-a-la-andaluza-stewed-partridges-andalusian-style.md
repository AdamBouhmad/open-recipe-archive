## Ingredients

- partridges
- bacon
- onion
- garlic
- spices
- lard
- white wine
- water

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the partridges, then brown them and place in a suitable pot with diced fried bacon, coarsely chopped onion in abundance, crushed garlic, spices of all kinds, lard and white wine
2. Cook at low heat, placing a small pan with water on a piece of brown paper on top of the pot
3. Some serve without further preparation; others pour beaten egg yolks on top; some add chocolate, either beaten on top or one ounce whole inside the partridge when browning it
