## Ingredients

- Twelve eggs
- Very fine slices of raw ham
- Pork lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Dried parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites separately and keep the yolks, two by two, in small bowls.
2. In a round baking dish or pan at least twenty centimeters in diameter, line the bottom completely with very fine slices of raw ham.
3. Add pork lard on top and place the pan on strong heat over a griddle, preferably not over a small stove.
4. When the fat begins to sizzle, pour all the beaten egg whites over it, and as soon as they set, add the yolks one by one.
5. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and finely chopped dried parsley.
6. Serve as an appetizer course.
