## Ingredients

- Sole fish
- Egg
- Grated bread
- Oil for frying
- One hundred grams of majordomo sauce
- Parsley
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare a sole and make an incision from the gills to four centimeters from the tail to remove the spine.
2. Break the spine with the back of the knife, from top to bottom, so it can be removed easily.
3. Coat the sole with egg and grated bread, and fry.
4. When fried, remove the spine and place one hundred grams of majordomo sauce in the opening.
5. Serve with parsley and a lemon cut in quarters.
