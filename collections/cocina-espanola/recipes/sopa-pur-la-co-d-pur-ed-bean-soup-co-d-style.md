## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of red beans
- cold water
- small pieces of bacon
- 1 onion
- carrot
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley
- 2 cloves

## Directions

1. Clean and wash two pounds of red beans
2. Place them to cook in cold water with small pieces of bacon, one onion, carrot, and a bundle of bay leaf, thyme, parsley, and two cloves
3. Once cooked, remove the bacon, vegetables, and water
4. Pass through a fine sieve with broth
5. Season, heat, and serve with fried bread croutons
