## Ingredients

- Meat slices, cut in square form
- Ground meat filling
- String

## Directions

1. Cut meat slices into square form and wrap a ground meat filling around them as if they were wafers.
2. Proceed in the same manner as explained for simple carbonadas, but tie each portion well with string so the filling does not escape.
3. Remove the string when serving.
