## Ingredients

- 2 fat chickens
- butter
- parsley sprigs
- saffron
- small onions
- mushrooms
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the chickens into pieces
2. Blanch for 3 minutes in boiling water and drain
3. Place in a pan with butter, parsley sprigs, saffron, small onions and mushrooms; stew everything together
4. Add a spoonful of flour and moisten with broth
5. Season with salt and pepper and cook
6. Remove the chicken pieces and reduce the sauce
7. Strain the sauce, dissolve egg yolks in it without letting them cook, and pour over the chicken pieces with lemon juice
