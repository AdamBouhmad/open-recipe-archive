## Ingredients

- oil
- dried peppers
- garlic cloves
- tomato
- artichokes or peas
- rice
- saffron
- red pepper
- water
- fresh fish

## Directions

1. Fry three or four small round dried peppers (called flores in Murcia) in oil with speed and minimal frying as they burn easily
2. Set aside the peppers on cloth and in the same oil add two or three garlic cloves, tomato, artichokes or peas depending on season
3. When everything is well stewed, add the rice, stir for a few moments, adding saffron and red pepper
4. Cover with water and when it boils add fresh, clean fish cut into pieces
5. Turn once and cook over good heat
6. Pound the peppers well in a mortar and when done dissolve them in a little water
7. Add this mixture to the rice and let it cook like the previous recipe
