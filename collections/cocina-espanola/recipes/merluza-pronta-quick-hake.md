## Ingredients

- hake
- good oil
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- dried parsley
- cooking water from the fish
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook hake in good salted water
2. Place in a shallow metal plate or bowl
3. Using a wooden spatula and fork, remove all bones and skin, leaving the flesh clean and intact
4. Heat good oil (two tablespoons per pound of hake)
5. Away from the fire, add two minced garlic cloves to the hot oil in the pan
6. Sprinkle the hake with ground salt, a pinch of pepper, and one teaspoon of dried parsley powder
7. Pour the hot oil over the hake and cover the plate
8. When bringing to the table, add two tablespoons of the water in which the fish cooked and a touch of vinegar to the sauce
