## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- dried beans
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare the leg of mutton as previously described.
2. Cook the dried beans beforehand in water.
3. Half an hour before serving, incorporate the well-drained beans into the meat and its juices.
4. Vaporize the water they contain by reheating slowly for about fifteen minutes, during which time they will absorb the meat juices.
