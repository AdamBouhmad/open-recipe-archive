## Ingredients

- six liters of water
- two kilos of beef from the leg or breast
- one ring of blood sausage
- three chorizos
- five ounces of pork
- five ounces of chickpeas from Castile or Lanzarote
- three doses of salt
- pumpkin, over one kilo more or less
- chayote
- cabbage
- green beans
- corn
- tender bubangos
- yams
- pears
- five pounds of potatoes
- three pounds of sweet potatoes
- saffron
- garlic
- cloves

## Directions

1. After the sun has traveled a short space, light the black stove with hazel or heather coals
2. Pour six liters of water into the cooking pot
3. Add the beef from the leg or breast, blood sausage ring, chorizos, and pork
4. Add the chickpeas and salt
5. Cook until these parts are cooked, then set them aside
6. As the pot boils vigorously with steam rising inside, add the pumpkin, chayote, cabbage, green beans, corn, and tender bubangos, yams, and pears
7. When the pepper and all these ingredients are seasoned, remove the vegetables
8. Replace them later with potatoes and sweet potatoes
9. Grind saffron, garlic, and cloves together in the mortar with an iron pestle
10. Pour one spoonful of broth into the mortar and dilute the spices
11. Throw the spices directly into the pot
12. As the sun declines, return the vegetables to the fire
13. Unite all the components and cook for a brief time, receiving full heat
14. Place the resulting dish on a wide platter, white as an almond flower
