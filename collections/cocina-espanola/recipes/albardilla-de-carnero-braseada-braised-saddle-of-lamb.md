## Ingredients

- 1 saddle of lamb (albardilla), deboned
- Salt and pepper
- Fine herbs
- Strips of bacon
- Four to six onions
- A bouquet of fine herbs
- Two ladles of broth
- Equal quantity of white wine

## Directions

1. Bone the saddle of lamb, salt and pepper it strongly, and season with fine herbs on its inner side.
2. Fold it in a bit toward the inside to give it the shape of a cake, and tie it with string so it does not lose its form.
3. Put strips of bacon, four or six onions, and a bouquet of fine herbs in a braising pan.
4. Place the saddle of lamb over this garnish, adding two ladles of broth and an equal quantity of white wine.
5. Let it cook with little heat from below and somewhat more from above, with a dome-shaped lid, for about three hours.
6. After three hours, without removing the meat from the pan, carefully remove the string and then the skin, which will come off easily.
7. Place the saddle of lamb on a platter and sprinkle with dried parsley made into powder, covering it completely as if it were painted green, and serve it with its own juice.
