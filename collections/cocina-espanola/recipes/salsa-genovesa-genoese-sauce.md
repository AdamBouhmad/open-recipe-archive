## Ingredients

- White sauce base
- one tablespoon of dried parsley in powder or fresh parsley in greater quantity
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. When the mixture is cooking slowly, incorporate one tablespoon of dried parsley in powder or fresh parsley in greater quantity and the juice of half a lemon
2. Continue the operation as previously to obtain classical Genoese sauce
