## Ingredients

- five eggs
- two ounces of starch, well sifted
- four ounces of ground white sugar
- fried oil for greasing the mold

## Directions

1. Beat five eggs and mix with two ounces of starch, well sifted, and four ounces of ground white sugar
2. Beat again
3. Grease the mold with fried oil
4. Pour in the batter
5. Cover well and place over gentle heat with heat on the lid
6. Test for doneness with a straw or knitting needle; when it comes out clean, it is cooked
7. It cooks much better in the oven
