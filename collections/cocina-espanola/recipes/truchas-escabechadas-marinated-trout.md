## Ingredients

- Trout, fried
- Strong vinegar
- Lemon slices
- Bay leaves
- Pepper
- Clove
- Onion, sliced in rings
- Bundle of fine herbs
- Garlic clove
- Oil

## Directions

1. After frying the trout, place them undrained in a baking dish and, while still very hot, pour over them strong vinegar, some lemon slices, two bay leaves, a pinch of pepper, clove, onion rings, a bundle of fine herbs and a clove of garlic.
2. Put the dish on the heat, and as soon as it begins to boil, cover and set aside until the dish cools.
3. Then remove the trout and broth and place in a deep platter so that they are well bathed, and add enough oil so that a thin layer remains on top, which is what preserves the escabeche in all its best flavor.
4. When you want to eat these trout, serve them without the broth.
