## Ingredients

- macaroni
- salt
- water
- butter
- 30 grams grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 tablespoon toasted breadcrumbs
- 15 grams butter

## Directions

1. Cook the macaroni as in previous recipes.
2. Line a small gratin dish with a thin layer of butter.
3. Fill it with the macaroni, forming a mound approximately six centimeters high.
4. Sprinkle with 30 grams of grated Parmesan cheese and half a tablespoon of toasted breadcrumbs.
5. Melt 15 grams of butter and pour it over the macaroni.
6. Place over low heat and cover with an iron lid with quite hot coals on top.
7. If the burner flame is lit properly, the macaroni should be cooked in ten minutes.
8. For fast days, macaroni gratin can also be made by substituting the broth with milk.
