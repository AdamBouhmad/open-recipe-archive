## Ingredients

- onions
- lard
- flour
- broth from the puchero
- salt
- pepper
- thin slices of beef
- vinegar
- tomato

## Directions

1. Cut onions and cook in lard until tender
2. Add a bit of flour and stir until it takes color
3. Add broth from the puchero, salt and pepper
4. When the sauce is almost reduced, add thin slices of leftover beef
5. Sprinkle with a little vinegar and remove from heat when well combined
6. Add tomato if available
