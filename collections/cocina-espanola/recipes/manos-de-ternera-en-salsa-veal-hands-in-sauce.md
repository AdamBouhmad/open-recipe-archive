## Ingredients

- veal hands
- onion
- parsley
- roasted hazelnuts, finely ground
- nutmeg
- pepper
- two bay leaves
- white wine from Rueda

## Directions

1. Soak the hands in very hot water for a considerable time.
2. Clean and peel carefully to remove all skin residue.
3. Cook at low heat for five hours.
4. Transfer to another pot with the prepared sauce.
5. The sauce should contain: onion, parsley, finely ground roasted hazelnuts, a little nutmeg, a pinch of pepper, and two bay leaves.
6. Cook the hands in this sauce for another hour at low heat.
7. A few minutes before removing from heat, sprinkle with several spoonfuls of white wine from Rueda.
8. Serve hot.
