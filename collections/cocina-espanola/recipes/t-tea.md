## Ingredients

- Tea leaves
- Water

## Directions

1. Heat the teapot by pouring boiling water into it, then empty it
2. Put in as many heaping teaspoons of tea as there are cups desired
3. Pour a small quantity of boiling water over the leaves
4. Let it rest for three or four minutes, covering the teapot well
5. Pour boiling water in the necessary amount
6. Serve the tea
7. More tea can be obtained by adding boiling water again, but never reuse leaves that have already released their aroma this way
