## Ingredients

- 250 grams lean ham
- cooked carrot from pot broth
- six mushrooms called champignons
- two truffles
- 125 grams beef butter
- one tablespoon flour
- one cup concentrated broth
- one glass Champagne wine
- a few tablespoons of Spanish sauce
- juice of one lemon
- a good piece of separately roasted meat

## Directions

1. Reheat and partially cook the ham in its own fat in a pot
2. Remove from pot and cut into small pieces
3. At the same time, cut a carrot cooked in pot broth, six champignon mushrooms and two truffas into equal sized pieces
4. Put everything in the pot with beef butter and dust the stew with one tablespoon of flour
5. Wet with one cup of concentrated broth, one glass of Champagne wine and a few tablespoons of Spanish sauce
6. When everything has cooked well and very little sauce remains, season this stew with the juice of one lemon
7. Serve with a good piece of separately roasted meat
