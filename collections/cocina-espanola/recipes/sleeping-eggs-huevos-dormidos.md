## Ingredients

- Water
- Vinegar (half the amount of water)
- Salt
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Boil water in a pan with half its amount in vinegar and sufficient salt.
2. Remove from the heat and carefully break the desired eggs on top without breaking them or stirring them.
3. When the whites are set, remove them, starting with the first ones, and place in cold water.
4. Remove from the cold water and place on a platter over a dish of sorrel, spinach, chicory, etc., or over any purée, or with a sauce made from shallots, vinegar, and mustard or another sauce of this type.
