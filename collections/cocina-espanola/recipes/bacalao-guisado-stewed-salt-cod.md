## Ingredients

- salt cod pieces
- flour
- parsley
- water
- fried onion
- spices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook some pieces of salt cod, wrap them in flour, fry them and place them in a casserole, and over them parsley and water that covers them, fried onion, spices and lemon juice
2. Cook thus until the greater part of the sauce is consumed
