## Ingredients

- whiting
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- milk
- toasted breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Clean the whole whiting and coat thoroughly inside and outside with flour
2. Sprinkle with salt and a pinch of pepper
3. Dip in a mixture of beaten egg and one tablespoon of milk
4. Roll in toasted breadcrumbs
5. Dip in the egg mixture again
6. Roll in breadcrumbs again
7. Let dry briefly to form a crust
8. Fry for longer than you would for plain fried whiting
