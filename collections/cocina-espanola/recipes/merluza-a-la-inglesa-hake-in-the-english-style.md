## Ingredients

- One large piece of hake
- Salt
- Lemon juice
- Melted cow butter
- Egg yolks
- Breadcrumbs
- Cow butter
- Cooked potatoes
- Fried bread slices
- Butter sauce

## Directions

1. Take a large piece of hake and after cleaning and removing the central bone, split in two from top to bottom
2. Clean both pieces well of small bones and skin, leaving only the two rounded loins
3. Cut into thin slices across, about half a finger thick, and flatten them carefully
4. Season with salt and lemon juice, place on a strainer to drain for a time. Dry with a cloth
5. Pass through melted cow butter mixed with egg yolks, then coat with breadcrumbs, pressing with the knife blade
6. Place the well-prepared pieces in a pot or pan with melted cow butter. With all remaining meat, make a forcemeat to fill a smooth mold, which will be cooked in a water bath one hour before
7. At serving time, reheat the pieces, being careful they brown on both sides. Empty the mold onto the platter, placing on top cooked potatoes mashed into the shape of purée and around the pieces alternated with slices of fried bread, and separately a butter sauce if desired
