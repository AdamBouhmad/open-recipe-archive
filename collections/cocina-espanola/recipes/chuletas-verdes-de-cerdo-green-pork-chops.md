## Ingredients

- lemon juice
- parsley, chopped
- two tarragon leaves, finely chopped
- half a dozen shallots
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- nutmeg
- pork chops
- toasted and grated bread

## Directions

1. In a bowl or tureen, macerate in lemon juice for six hours a good amount of chopped parsley, two tarragon leaves finely chopped, and half a dozen shallots, with salt, pepper, cloves and nutmeg.
2. After that time, grill the chops on a grill grate over high heat.
3. Little by little incorporate the fat that the chops release into the bowl and mix it with the marinade, which is thickened and bound with toasted and grated bread.
4. At the moment of serving, place the chops on a very hot platter and coat them on both sides so that the meat is not visible with this paste garnish, using a spatula for this.
5. Despite the precaution of heating the platter, the time spent in the operation and the garnish that is cold will cool the chops; to serve them hot after being prepared, put them in the oven with the door open or cover them with a cloche cover with fire on top.
