## Ingredients

- 1 saddle of lamb (albardilla), braised
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Braise a saddle of lamb.
2. After braising, coat it with breadcrumbs and place it in the oven at very strong heat for half an hour, turning it a couple of times.
3. The oven must have capacity to contain the entire saddle of lamb, which when prepared this way is a dish of considerable dimensions and luxury.
