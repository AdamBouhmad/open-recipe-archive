## Ingredients

- whole whiting
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- milk
- toasted bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Flour the whole whiting on the outside, and excessively on the inside
2. Sprinkle with salt and a pinch of pepper, and soak in a beaten mixture of egg and one spoonful of milk
3. Wrap in toasted bread crumbs and soak again in the egg, then wrap again in bread crumbs
4. Let dry a little so the crust takes shape, then fry, allowing more time for cooking than for naturally fried whiting
5. The whiting should result very firm and rolled up, without the shape of the fish being very marked
6. Serve as is, or with any of the infinite known sauces, but thick tomato sauce is what best suits the dish
