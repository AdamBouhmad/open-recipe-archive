## Ingredients

- twelve egg yolks
- nine ounces of white sugar
- one pound of superior flour
- twelve egg whites
- one and a half pounds of sugar for clarified syrup
- one quarter liter of Málaga wine
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat in a pot twelve egg yolks with nine ounces of white sugar
2. When well beaten, add one pound of superior flour
3. Add twelve egg whites beaten as for meringue
4. Transfer this mixture to a box made beforehand with a sheet of paper
5. Cut into squares
6. Dip the squares in a clarified sugar syrup made with one and a half pounds of sugar brought to the point of caramel, into which one quarter liter of Málaga wine is mixed
7. Upon removing from the bath, sprinkle with ground cinnamon and let them air dry on esparto mats
