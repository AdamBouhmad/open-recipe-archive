## Ingredients

- 125 grams of salted bacon
- about twenty white sausages
- quenelles or small meatballs
- mushrooms
- truffles or truffles of the earth
- veal or lamb sweetbreads, in equal volume portions to the sausages
- white wine

## Directions

1. Give color in a saucepan with 125 grams of salted bacon to about twenty white sausages.
2. When the bacon has also colored, incorporate quenelles or small meatballs, mushrooms, and even truffles or truffles of the earth, cut in quantity equal in volume of each substance to that of the sausages.
3. Moisten everything so it is covered with white wine.
