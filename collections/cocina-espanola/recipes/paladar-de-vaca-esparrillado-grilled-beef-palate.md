## Ingredients

- beef palate
- refined oil
- salt
- pepper
- garlic cloves or shallots
- parsley
- spicy sauce or tomato sauce or pepitoria sauce

## Directions

1. After cooking, drain the palate and cool it in its own broth.
2. Soak it in a marinade of refined oil with salt, pepper, two cloves of garlic, or better yet, some finely trimmed shallots with a little parsley.
3. Let the palate soak for a couple of hours, turning it frequently so the oil penetrates well on all sides.
4. Place it on the grill like a beefsteak and serve with a spicy sauce, or with tomato, or with pepitoria sauce prepared as explained in the formula for beef tongue in pepitoria.
