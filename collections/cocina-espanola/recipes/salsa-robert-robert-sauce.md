## Ingredients

- 50 grams of butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- two large onions, the size of apples
- half a pint of water
- strong aromatic vinegar
- one full tablespoon of mustard

## Directions

1. Begin by making a blonde sauce with good color using 50 grams of butter and one tablespoon of flour.
2. Incorporate two large onions, the size of apples, finely chopped, and let them brown well, but without burning the little pieces at the edges.
3. Moisten with half a pint of water and let it cook slowly so the sauce reduces.
4. At the moment of serving, remove the sauce from the fire and add a thread of strong aromatic vinegar and one full tablespoon of mustard.
5. Note: the sauce is better when broth from the pot, well cleaned of fat, is used instead of water.
