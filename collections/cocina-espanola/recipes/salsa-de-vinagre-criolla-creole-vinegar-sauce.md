## Ingredients

- broth and vinegar in equal parts
- salt
- three or four finely chopped garlic cloves
- pepper
- sufficient parsley

## Directions

1. Take a pan and add equal parts of broth and vinegar
2. Add salt, three or four finely chopped garlic cloves, and pepper
3. Add sufficient parsley and mash everything
4. Put everything on the fire for about one hour
5. This sauce can be applied to all roasted meats
