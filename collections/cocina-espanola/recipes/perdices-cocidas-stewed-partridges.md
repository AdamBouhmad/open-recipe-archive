## Ingredients

- partridges
- bacon
- thin strips of veal
- bacon slices
- carrots
- onions
- parsley
- meat scraps
- salt and pepper
- broth
- white wine, equal part to the broth
- juice of 1 sour orange
- zest of 1 lemon, grated
- tomato sauce, either fresh or preserved, optional

## Directions

1. Lard the partridges and wrap them with bacon and thin strips of veal
2. Prepare a pot with slices of bacon
3. Place the partridges in it with carrots, onions, parsley and meat scraps
4. Season and moisten with broth and an equal part of white wine
5. Cook everything over low heat
6. Strain the cooking liquid after removing the fat
7. Add the juice of 1 sour orange and a little grated lemon zest
8. Pour the sauce over the partridges
9. Alternatively, in place of broth for moistening, another sauce may be used, and tomato sauce, whether fresh or preserved, is in my opinion what best suits stewed partridge
