## Ingredients

- Sardines
- Oil
- Fresh butter
- Parsley
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the sardines.
2. Anoint them with oil and open them lengthwise.
3. With them thus extended, roast them over live coals on the grill.
4. Place them on a plate or serving dish where you will have previously put very fresh butter, kneaded with chopped parsley and a pinch of pepper.
