## Ingredients

- fish broth
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make the necessary quantity of fish broth as previously indicated, and with it prepare a soup that is rather clear than thick.
2. With part of the broth and over low heat, stirring always, thicken it by allowing some egg yolks to set.
3. Incorporate it into the soup at the moment of serving.
