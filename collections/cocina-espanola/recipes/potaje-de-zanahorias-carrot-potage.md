## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Water
- Salt
- Oil
- Onion
- Spices
- Hot water
- Honey or sugar
- Vinegar
- Flour
- Eggs

## Directions

1. On Fridays, cook the carrots with water and salt
2. Cut them into small pieces and sauté with oil and onion
3. Place them in a pot or casserole and season with all spices and salt, adding hot water to cover them
4. Add honey or sugar and vinegar so they are well sweetened with a hint of vinegar acidity
5. Finally, fry some flour until toasted and dissolve it with the same carrot broth
6. If desired, poach eggs and add them one by one to the soup bowl to cook slightly, and serve the soup in this state
