## Ingredients

- Chickpeas, cooked in seasoned water
- Spinach, a handful
- Fried oil
- Flour, one tablespoon
- Egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cook the chickpeas in seasoned water.
2. Cook apart or with them a handful of spinach, cut roughly.
3. Incorporate fried oil with one tablespoon of flour browned in it.
4. At the moment of serving, bind with an egg yolk dissolved in water.
