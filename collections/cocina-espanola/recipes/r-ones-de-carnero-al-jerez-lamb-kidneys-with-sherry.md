## Ingredients

- Lamb kidneys
- Beef butter
- Salt and pepper
- Bread for croutons
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Sauté the kidneys as for the previous recipe
2. Fry the bread croutons in the same sherry wine instead of plain oil
3. Moisten the sautéed kidneys with good sherry wine
4. Serve on the wine-soaked croutons
