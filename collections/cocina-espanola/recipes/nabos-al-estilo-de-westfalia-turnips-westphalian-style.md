## Ingredients

- 2 ounces of pork lard
- 1/4 pound of Aviles ham, cut into tiny pieces
- 1 teaspoon of flour
- 1 quart of cold water
- 2 pounds of tender turnips, scraped and washed
- spices to taste

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, blanch the ham pieces in the pork lard
2. Add 1 teaspoon of flour and mix without letting it brown
3. Wet everything with 1 quart of cold water
4. When it comes to a boil, add the 2 pounds of tender turnips, scraped and washed beforehand
5. Season with spices to taste
6. Let cook for an hour and a half over very low heat
