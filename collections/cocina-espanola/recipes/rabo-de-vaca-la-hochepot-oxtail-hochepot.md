## Ingredients

- Oxtail
- Six carrots
- Two onions
- Cloves
- Half a cabbage
- Salt
- Pepper
- Meat stock

## Directions

1. Cut the oxtail into as many pieces as it has vertebrae, the same as if it were meat for a boiled dinner
2. At mid-cooking, add six carrots, two onions with their corresponding cloves, and half a cabbage cut into strips, with good seasoning of salt and pepper
3. When the oxtail is well cooked, after about six hours, remove it from the pot along with the vegetables, and place them on a platter with the pieces of oxtail in the center and the vegetables around it
4. Degrease the broth carefully, add meat stock, reduce over high heat, and pour this broth over the dish to serve it
