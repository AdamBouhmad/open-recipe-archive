## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- fresh bacon
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs, finely chopped
- half liter of Sherry wine
- small onions
- sausages in strings

## Directions

1. Lard the meat with fresh bacon and prepare it as for cooking in a pot
2. Soak it in oil for twelve hours with plenty of salt, pepper and finely chopped fine herbs
3. Place the leg in a pot with half a liter of Sherry wine
4. Add a garnish of small onions and sausages in strings
5. Cook slowly for five hours with the pot well covered
6. Serve as a lunch dish, which can be completed with mushrooms Bordelaise style and lettuce salad seasoned with small onions
