## Ingredients

- hake pieces from the middle body downward, including the tail
- beef butter
- white wine, about half a quarter-liter for a quarter-pound of butter and three pounds of hake
- breadcrumbs
- parsley, chopped
- salt

## Directions

1. Take the piece or pieces of hake desired from the middle body downward, including the tail.
2. Soak the pieces and remove the scales, then dry them with a napkin.
3. Place them on wire grills to roast over low heat until they are golden on all sides.
4. Melt beef butter in a pan and remove the foam it releases.
5. Add about half a quarter-liter of white wine for the quantity of a quarter-pound of butter and three pounds of hake with breadcrumbs and chopped parsley.
6. Let the sauce boil only once, pouring it over the pieces and serving them promptly.
7. Proportion the quantity of white wine proportionally according to the amount of butter and hake used.
