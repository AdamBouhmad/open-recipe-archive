## Ingredients

- Fish meat from carp, tench, barbel, eel or shad, cleaned of bones and skin
- Fine herbs
- Mushrooms
- One or two truffles for half a pound of fish mixture
- Bread crumbs from half a roll soaked in hot milk
- Salt
- Pepper
- Grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop together the clean fish meat from several fish
2. Chop separately fine herbs, mushrooms and one or two truffles for half a pound of fish mixture
3. Combine the fish with the herbs, mushrooms and truffas, add bread crumbs soaked in hot milk, seasoning with salt, pepper and grated nutmeg
4. Use to fill large and small fish of sea and fresh water
5. Alternatively, form into croquettes, coat in frying batter, fry in oil for an excellent dish for fasting days
