## Ingredients

- 4 partridges
- Spanish sauce
- Sherry wine
- juice or essence from bones

## Directions

1. Roast 4 partridges
2. Remove all the meat and pound it in a mortar
3. Add Spanish sauce reduced with Sherry wine
4. Pass through a sieve and strainer
5. When serving add the juice, essence, or broth obtained from the bones
6. Serve
