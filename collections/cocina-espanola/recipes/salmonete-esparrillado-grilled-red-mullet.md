## Ingredients

- red mullet
- salt
- pepper
- oil

## Directions

1. Make symmetrical transverse cuts in the skin on both sides so it does not burst with heat
2. Sprinkle the cuts with salt and a pinch of pepper, and sprinkle with oil
3. Leave for an hour apart on a plate
4. Afterwards clean very well with a cloth and roast on the grill over live fire
5. Serve with steward's sauce, but the best sauce for red mullets is the one from the recipe of Miss Sicsú
