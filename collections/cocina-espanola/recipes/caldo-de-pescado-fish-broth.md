## Ingredients

- Fish from the sea, very fresh
- Water
- Salt
- Carrot
- Celery rib
- Chervil
- Parsley
- Onion
- Clove of spice
- Bay leaf
- Butter from cows or olive oil

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of fish per liter of water with salt, a carrot, a rib of celery, chervil, parsley, an onion with its clove of spice, a bay leaf, and a little cow butter or one tablespoon of oil
2. When the fish has cooked well, pass the broth and drain it, but without pressing it
3. Use as a base for all white sauces for fish
4. Can be preserved in a cool place for several days
