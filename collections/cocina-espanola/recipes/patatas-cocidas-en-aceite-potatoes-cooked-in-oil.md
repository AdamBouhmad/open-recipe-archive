## Ingredients

- Oil in large quantity
- Potatoes, sliced into wheels

## Directions

1. Put much oil in a pan and at the same time add potatoes cut into wheels.
2. Place the pan over low heat and let the potatoes cook; they cannot take on color or be firm this way, but they taste very good and are impregnated with fat.
