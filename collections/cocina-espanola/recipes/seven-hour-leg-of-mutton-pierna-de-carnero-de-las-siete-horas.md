## Ingredients

- leg of mutton, not deboned
- good ham, lean part

## Directions

1. Place a large piece of good ham lean at the bottom of the pot
2. Place the leg of mutton on top
3. Cook for seven hours
4. Place another equal piece of ham on top of the leg
