## Ingredients

- leftover turkey
- salt
- pepper
- white roux
- milk
- clean broth from stew
- fried croutons

## Directions

1. Make a very fine mincing with all the meat, seasoning well with salt and pepper.
2. Separately, prepare a white roux that is moistened with half a quartillo of milk and half clean broth from the stew.
3. Cook the meat mince in this sauce until it thickens well.
4. Serve in a dish, rounding the surface of the paste and studding it with fried croutons.
