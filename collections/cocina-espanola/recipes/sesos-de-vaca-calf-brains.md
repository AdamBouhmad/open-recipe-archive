## Ingredients

- calf brains
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- thyme
- garlic
- parsley

## Directions

1. Place brains in boiling water to clean them of blood and membrane
2. Soak in cold water for two hours
3. Place in clean water and bring to a boil
4. Add vinegar, salt, pepper, bay leaf, thyme, garlic and parsley
5. Simmer for half an hour until cooked
