## Ingredients

- salmon steaks
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves
- broth
- white wine
- mushrooms (champignons)
- truffles
- chicken livers
- rooster crests
- ham
- flour
- butter
- nutmeg
- eggs

## Directions

1. Take a good salmon steak, coat it with oil, salt and pepper, and place two or three bay leaves on each side, then roast it on a grill.
2. In a mixture of broth and white wine, cook a medium jar of mushrooms and a smaller jar of truffles if you do not have fresh ones.
3. Gather chicken livers, rooster crests, and ham pieces, and sauté all together.
4. In a very light flour sauce, shred the aforementioned salmon, adding a good piece of butter.
5. Finally, mix everything that has been mentioned, sprinkle it with nutmeg and beat the eggs, folding the omelet in the French manner so that it does not become compacted and hard, but rather juicy and loose.
