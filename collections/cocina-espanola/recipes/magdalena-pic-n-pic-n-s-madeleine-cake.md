## Ingredients

- two ounces of butter for melting
- butter for greasing the mold
- a quarter pound of ground sugar
- a quarter pound of flour
- lemon zest equal to about one teaspoon of coffee
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Melt two ounces of butter in a pan
2. Grease a mold or cake pan with melted butter on the inside and let it cool
3. In the pan, add a quarter pound of ground sugar and another quarter pound of flour, with lemon zest equal to about one teaspoon of coffee
4. Beat and mix all this with the melted butter until forming a homogeneous and crumbly mixture, like sand
5. Add two eggs, whites and yolks
6. Stir and beat with a spatula to bind the batter, like thick pap
7. Fill the greased mold and bake this in twenty minutes at a strong oven heat
8. Let cool and remove from the mold
