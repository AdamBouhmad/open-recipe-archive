## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of cow butter
- three tablespoons of flour
- one and a half pints of good concentrated broth

## Directions

1. For eight persons, melt a quarter pound of cow butter in a saucepan
2. Incorporate three tablespoons of flour and season without letting it take color
3. Add one and a half pints of good concentrated broth
4. Cook everything for a quarter of an hour
5. Strain the soup
