## Ingredients

- Lamb breast
- Three or four slices of ham
- Bacon slices
- Onions
- Clove spice
- Herbs

## Directions

1. Remove the large bones attached to the cartilage of a lamb breast and divide it into eight equal pieces, oval at one end and pointed at the other.
2. Place three or four slices of ham in a pot and on top place the lamb pieces, covered with slices of bacon.
3. Add two onions, clove spice, and herbs.
