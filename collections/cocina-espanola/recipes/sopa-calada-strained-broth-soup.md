## Ingredients

- Very fine bread cuts
- Boiling broth
- Mint leaf (optional)

## Directions

1. Place very fine cuts of bread in the soup tureen.
2. Pour boiling broth over it in quantity according to whether you want the soup clear or thick.
3. Let it soak for a few minutes, being careful to cover the tureen or pot.
4. Serve. Some add a mint leaf. French bread is better than Spanish bread for this and for all soups, and bread that is two or three days old is preferable to fresh bread.
