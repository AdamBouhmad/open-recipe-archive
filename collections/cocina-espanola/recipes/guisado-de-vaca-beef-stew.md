## Ingredients

- piece of beef leg
- salt
- bay leaves
- bacon, part fat and part lean
- garlic
- pepper
- oil
- sufficient broth
- spices
- parsley
- tomato

## Directions

1. Cut a piece of beef leg into small pieces and add to the pot with salt and bay leaves
2. Chop bacon that is part fat and part lean, fry it and also add to the pot
3. Mash garlic with pepper, dissolving it in a little water, and add to the stew
4. Add oil to reheat it at low heat
5. When appropriate add sufficient broth and season with salt, spices, parsley and tomato
