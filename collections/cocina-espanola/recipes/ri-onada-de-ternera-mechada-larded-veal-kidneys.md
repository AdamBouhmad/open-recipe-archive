## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- bacon
- beef fat
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Split the kidneys lengthwise and open the two halves like the pages of a book
2. Lard them with bacon using a fine larding needle
3. Sauté in a pan with beef fat and good seasoning
4. Complete the cooking over low heat, with less heat below and considerably more above the pot lid
5. The points of the bacon lards should be toasted so the dish looks good, and be naturally placed with art and symmetry
6. Serve with the veal juices added at the moment of serving, and arrange the sauce in the central hollow formed by the two open halves of the kidneys without allowing much sauce to appear on the platter
