## Ingredients

- endive, lettuce or other greens, well washed and dried
- three tablespoons fine oil
- one tablespoon strong vinegar
- one dozen salted anchovies in brine from glass jars

## Directions

1. One hour before eating, toss the greens with three tablespoons of fine oil
2. At the moment of serving, incorporate one tablespoon of strong vinegar and one dozen salted anchovies in brine
3. Mix everything well and serve
