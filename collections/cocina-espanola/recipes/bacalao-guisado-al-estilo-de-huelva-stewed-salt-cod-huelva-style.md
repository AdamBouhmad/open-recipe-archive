## Ingredients

- salt cod pieces, prepared and cooked
- onion
- garlic
- pepper
- saffron

## Directions

1. Prepared and cooked pieces of salt cod, chop onion abundantly, fry it, crush garlic, pepper, and saffron, mix with the onion and add to the serving dish when serving the cod
