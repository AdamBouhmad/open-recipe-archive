## Ingredients

- 60 grams of anchovy fillets, well washed and cleaned
- 60 grams of beef butter

## Directions

1. Pound the anchovy fillets well in a mortar without adding any liquid or seasoning
2. Incorporate 60 grams of beef butter into the mortar
3. Continue pounding until this butter mixture is well manipulated and uniform
4. Present at the table as a spread or reserve for seasoning and garnishing other dishes
