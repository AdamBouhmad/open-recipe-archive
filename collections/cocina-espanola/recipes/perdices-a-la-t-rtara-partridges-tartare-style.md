## Ingredients

- partridges
- melted beef butter
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- aromatic herbs
- mustard sauce

## Directions

1. After plucking, cleaning and singeing, split the partridges in half
2. Soak them in melted beef butter, cover with bread crumbs and sprinkle with pepper, salt and ground aromatic herbs
3. Roast on a grill over low heat and serve with piquant sauce containing plenty of mustard
