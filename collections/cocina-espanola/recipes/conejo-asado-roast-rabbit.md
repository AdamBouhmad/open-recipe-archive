## Ingredients

- one whole rabbit
- butter from cows or pigs
- bacon strips
- oil or fat for basting
- salt

## Directions

1. First thing to do is sauté the whole rabbit in cow or pork butter and let it cool
2. Then lard it easily with thin strips of bacon, the same way as done with hare
3. If the rabbit is very tender, it will be good to wrap it in a sheet of greased paper so it cooks without drying out
4. Remove the paper halfway through cooking so it browns, moistening it frequently with its own juices
5. Serve the roast rabbit alone on a platter, and apart in a sauce boat, its own juice, reinforced with a spicy sauce
