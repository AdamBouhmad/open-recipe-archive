## Ingredients

- sea bream
- salt
- garlic
- oil
- vinegar
- broth
- lemon or bitter orange juice

## Directions

1. After scaling and cleaning well, dry with a cloth and add a little salt
2. Roast on the grill over low heat
3. When roasted, and at the time of serving, pour fried garlic in oil and a little vinegar over it
4. Also add, if desired, a little broth from the pot with a bit of lemon or bitter orange juice
