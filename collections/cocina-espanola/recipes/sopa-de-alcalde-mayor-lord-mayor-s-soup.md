## Ingredients

- Four pig ears
- Four pig hands
- Four liters of water
- Parsley
- Celery
- Thyme
- Onions
- Cloves
- 250 grams of butter
- Flour of the finest quality
- Fine herbs including parsley, chervil, bay leaf and mint

## Directions

1. Cook four pig ears and four pig hands slowly over low heat in four liters of water for four hours
2. Add a bouquet made with parsley, celery and thyme and some onions with cloves stuck in them
3. After four hours, remove the ears from the broth, which will be very cooked, and leave the hands to cook for two more hours
4. Strain the broth through a fine sieve, let cool, and when cold remove all the solidified fat from the surface
5. Debone the ears and hands and cut the meat into very small pieces, place in a covered dish with a napkin until ready to prepare the soup
6. In a pan over gentle heat, melt 250 grams of butter, to which add, stirring constantly with a spatula, all the flour it can absorb
7. Keep this mixture over the fire for ten minutes while stirring, taking care that the flour does not brown
8. Gradually incorporate the prepared broth, letting it cook for a while after each portion is added
9. When all the broth is in the pan, season and dress the meat from the ears and hands with two tablespoons of fine herbs - parsley, chervil, bay leaf, mint, all chopped like tobacco
