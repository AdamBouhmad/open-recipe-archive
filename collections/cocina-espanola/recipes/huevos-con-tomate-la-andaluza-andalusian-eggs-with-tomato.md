## Ingredients

- good Andalusian oil
- tomatoes, well peeled
- 2 eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry the peeled tomatoes well in good Andalusian oil.
2. Season and reduce the sauce by half.
3. In the same oil, fry 2 eggs Spanish-style, soft inside with crispy edges.
4. Serve on a plate with the tomato sauce on the side.
