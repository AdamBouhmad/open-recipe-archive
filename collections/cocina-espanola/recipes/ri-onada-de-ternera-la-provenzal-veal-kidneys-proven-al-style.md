## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- beef tongue
- beef fat
- onions
- refined oil
- broth

## Directions

1. Cool a cooked beef tongue that has been prepared as indicated in the spicy beef tongue recipe
2. Cut the thickest part into thin slices and trim these into strips or lards like bacon for larding
3. Lard the veal kidneys, prepared as in the previous recipe, with these strips
4. Sauté until it takes on good color in beef fat
5. In another pan, sauté and brown two or three finely cut onions in refined oil
6. Moisten with two cups of broth and strain through a sieve
