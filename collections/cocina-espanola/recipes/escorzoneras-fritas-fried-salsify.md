## Ingredients

- salsify
- flour
- water
- oil
- lemon juice
- pork lard
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Clean the salsify by scraping and washing well in boiling water, cutting the stems in half
2. Cook at a rolling boil for 15 minutes in boiling water
3. One hour before frying, dissolve 1 tablespoon of flour in 2 tablespoons of water
4. Season and add another tablespoon of oil and a few drops of lemon juice
5. Let the batter rest
6. Coat the salsify in the batter
7. Fry in pork lard over very high heat and serve immediately
