## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/4 cuartillo milk
- 2 ounces fresh beef butter
- salt and pepper
- flour
- lard
- broth from the pot

## Directions

1. In a bowl, place four eggs, a quarter part of a cuartillo of milk, two ounces of fresh beef butter, a bit of salt and pepper; beat everything mixing it with a bit of flour until a consistent paste is made.
2. Form the meatballs the size of an olive, dusting them with flour, and fry them in lard.
3. Place them in the tureen and pour the broth from the pot over them.
