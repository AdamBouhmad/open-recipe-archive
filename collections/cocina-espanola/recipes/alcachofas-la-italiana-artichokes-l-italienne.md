## Ingredients

- artichokes
- lemon juice
- water
- salt
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Divide the artichokes into four equal parts and remove the heart. Rub them with lemon juice.
2. Cook them with water, salt and more lemon juice. Remove and let drain.
3. Serve them with Italian sauce.
