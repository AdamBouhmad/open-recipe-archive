## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- water
- bacon strips
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- parsley
- onions
- veal strips
- beef strips
- carrots
- fine herbs
- broth
- gherkins

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue well and boil in salted water for about half an hour
2. Pass to cold water and remove the skin when cold
3. Season bacon strips with salt, pepper, spices, minced parsley and onions
4. Lard the tongue with the seasoned bacon strips
5. Place in a casserole with well-seasoned fine herbs, bacon strips, veal strips, beef strips, carrots, onions and spices
6. Moisten with puchero broth
7. Cook the tongue on very low heat for four hours
8. When serving, remove the outer skin
9. Have strained broth with gherkin pieces to pour over as sauce
10. Place on serving platter and pour the gherkin sauce over all
