## Ingredients

- Milk, one and a half quartillo
- Cream, half a quartillo
- Lemon peel
- Sugar, twelve ounces

## Directions

1. Put everything in a pan to cook until it begins to thicken, stirring constantly with a spoon
2. Remove from heat, pass through a cloth, let cool
3. Put in the freezer to freeze
