## Ingredients

- Pajeles
- Short court bouillon

## Directions

1. Cook the pajeles in short court bouillon
2. Place them without overlapping in a baking dish or pan that can go to the fire
3. Sprinkle with oil and a little salt
4. Give them ten minutes in the oven at high heat to make a very delicate dish
