## Ingredients

- veal chops
- melted butter or refined oil
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. After they are slowly grilled on the grill, taking care of the fire so they do not burn, and after having soaked them thoroughly, better than merely spread, in melted butter or refined oil.
2. When the chops are on the grill and with each turn given to them, season with salt and pepper without excess and serve from the grill to the table.
