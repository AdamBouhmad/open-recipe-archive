## Ingredients

- beef
- onion
- clove
- garlic head
- bay leaf
- oregano
- wine
- parsley
- oil

## Directions

1. Put in a pot the meat, cut into regular pieces, with two or three onions and a clove of spice, a head of garlic unpeeled, a bay leaf and oregano, a glass of wine, parsley and a jicara of oil per pound of meat
2. Brown everything over low heat and stir often
3. Place brown paper in the mouth of the pot and a cup of water on top
