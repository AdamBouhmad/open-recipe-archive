## Ingredients

- 3 wine glasses Dutch gin
- 0.5 wine glass curaçao
- 0.5 wine glass bitter bitters, preferably Angostura
- scrapings of nutmeg
- zest of 0.5 lemon
- crushed ice, equal volume to the spirits combined
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. In a punch bowl, combine the gin, curaçao, and bitters
2. Add the nutmeg scrapings and lemon zest
3. Add crushed ice in equal volume to the combined spirits
4. Beat the egg well and mix both white and yolk with everything, stirring for a considerable time little by little
5. Sweeten according to the consumer's taste
6. Serve as an aperitif
