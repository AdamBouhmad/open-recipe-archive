## Ingredients

- two deciliters of water
- 50 grams of butter
- 15 grams of sugar
- 116 grams of flour
- three or more eggs

## Directions

1. In a two-liter saucepan, put two deciliters of water, 50 grams of butter, and 15 grams of sugar
2. Bring to a boil and remove from heat at the first boil
3. Add 116 grams of flour and mix well with a wooden spoon
4. Return to heat for four minutes, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon to prevent the dough from sticking
5. Remove from heat
6. Break one egg into the pan and stir with the spoon
7. When well mixed, add two more eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition
8. If the dough is too stiff, add half an egg or a whole egg as needed
9. To test the consistency, take a spoonful of dough; it should neither spread nor stick to the spoon, but rather fall by its own weight
10. When the dough is finished, sprinkle flour on the work surface
