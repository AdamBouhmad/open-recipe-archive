## Ingredients

- 750 grams of meat
- 125 grams of bone
- 4 liters of water
- 30 grams of salt
- 150 grams of carrots
- 150 grams of onion
- 200 grams of leeks
- 10 grams of celery
- 1 clove
- 150 grams of turnips

## Directions

1. Fill the stove well with charcoal and light it properly from the start
2. The pot can cook for three hours without needing to touch the stove once the fire is well lit
3. Whenever it is necessary to increase the fire, avoid very rapid boiling; vegetables that cook at a boil are not suitable for the stew, which needs to simmer over low heat
4. Be careful when placing the lid on the pot to leave an opening of two fingers
5. Pierce the clove into an onion and add to the pot along with all other ingredients
