## Ingredients

- Leftover cooked or fried cod
- Two tablespoons of oil
- Two tablespoons of flour
- Half a quarter-liter of broth, milk, or water
- Salt
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Remove skin and bones from the leftover cod and chop it very fine.
2. In a saucepan, heat two tablespoons of oil until it is hot.
3. Add two tablespoons of flour.
4. Stir well, and before the flour takes color, add half a quarter-liter of broth, milk, or water.
5. Season and add chopped parsley.
6. Add the chopped cod.
