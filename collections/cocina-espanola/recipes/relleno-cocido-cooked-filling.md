## Ingredients

- Half a pound of raw chicken breast
- Butter from cows or pork
- Bread crumbs
- Several spoonfuls of pot broth
- Half a pound of veal breast
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Two or three carrots
- Fine herbs
- Concentrated broth

## Directions

1. Reheat in butter half a pound of raw chicken breast over moderate heat without allowing it to color; when well cooked, remove from pan and let cool and drain to retain as little fat as possible
2. In the same pan, cook bread crumbs slowly over low heat with several spoonfuls of pot broth to reduce to thick paste
3. Separately, cook half a pound of veal breast in much water with salt, pepper and two or three carrots; when cooked, let cool
4. In a marble mortar, successively crush the chicken breast, bread, and veal in that order, then pass each through a fine sieve
5. Combine the three purées and mix exactly in the mortar in equal parts, adding a couple of spoonfuls of finely chopped fine herbs
6. Moisten this filling with concentrated broth and let cook until it acquires good consistency
