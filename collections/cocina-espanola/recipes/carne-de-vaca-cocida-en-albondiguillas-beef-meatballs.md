## Ingredients

- beef
- potatoes
- butter or fat
- eggs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mince very fine the piece of beef with potatoes cooked in the ashes
2. Add a little butter or fat from cattle, some whole eggs, salt, pepper, etc.
3. Mix it all well together, form balls that can be browned in butter
4. Serve with a piquant sauce
