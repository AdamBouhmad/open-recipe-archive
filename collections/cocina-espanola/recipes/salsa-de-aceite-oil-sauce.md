## Ingredients

- 4 egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- 125 grams clarified oil

## Directions

1. In a pan placed over embers or inside a bowl with boiling water, dissolve 4 egg yolks with salt and pepper
2. Once these are warm, pour 125 grams of good clarified oil over them and shake the mixture vigorously
3. Prevent the sauce from cooking and ensure it does not get too hot, as the eggs would separate from the oil, the binding would fail and the sauce would not work
4. Make it at the precise moment of serving as an accompaniment to vegetables cooked in water
5. Alternatively, submerge a bowl for a few minutes in boiling water, remove and dry quickly, and while it retains heat, prepare the mixture of eggs with oil and its corresponding seasoning
