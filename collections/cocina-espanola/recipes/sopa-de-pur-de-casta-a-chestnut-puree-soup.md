## Ingredients

- one pound of dried but fresh chestnuts
- one and a half ounces of beef butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- broth or pap to form the soup
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Cook the chestnuts well in water
2. Pass them through a food mill
3. In a pan, melt one and a half ounces of beef butter
4. When it begins to be shiny, add one tablespoon of flour
5. Before it takes color, moisten everything with the broth or pap that will form the soup
6. At the first boil, add the chestnut paste
7. Season everything and pass through a finer strainer after a quarter hour of cooking
8. Keep this soup near the fire until the moment of serving, when it is thickened away from the fire with egg yolk as already explained
9. Pour it into the tureen, where there will be toasted or fried bread in small pieces of the same shape
