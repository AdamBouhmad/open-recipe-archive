## Ingredients

- butter
- small onions
- flour
- red wine
- broth
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- thyme
- beef slices

## Directions

1. Fry small onions in butter, add a spoonful of flour and brown it
2. Add a small glass of red wine and half a glass of broth, some mushrooms, salt, pepper, bay leaf and thyme
3. When the sauce is made, pour it over the slices of meat arranged on a fire-resistant plate
4. Leave this over coals for half an hour and serve
