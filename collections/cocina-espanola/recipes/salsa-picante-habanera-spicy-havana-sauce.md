## Ingredients

- juice of two lemons
- half a glass of vinegar
- finely chopped dates
- a pinch of pepper
- sufficient parsley
- four or five garlic cloves
- one bay leaf
- a small quantity of cumin
- two small bowls of substantial broth
- four tablespoons of Havana sauce
- flour, if needed

## Directions

1. Squeeze all the juice from two lemons
2. Mix with half a glass of vinegar and finely chopped dates
3. Add a pinch of pepper, sufficient parsley, four or five garlic cloves, one bay leaf, and a small quantity of cumin
4. Put all this in a pan and bring to a boil for as long as necessary until it reduces to half
5. Add about two small bowls of substantial broth and four tablespoons of Havana sauce
6. Let it cook a little to thicken, and if you want it to thicken faster, add a little toasted flour
