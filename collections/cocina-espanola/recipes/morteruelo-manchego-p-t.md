## Ingredients

- Pork liver
- Loin
- Poultry
- Oil
- Garlic, fried
- Bread
- Spices

## Directions

1. Take pork liver, loin, and poultry, and sauté them with oil and fried garlic
2. Cook everything thoroughly so that the poultry bones can be removed and the loin and liver fall apart
3. Place all the cooked meat inside a clean mortar and pound it well
4. Pass it through a strainer
5. Place it in the broth where all these meats have cooked and mix well
6. If you like spices, season with spices
7. Grate bread and add it to the broth, incorporating it with the aforementioned meats until everything forms a paste
8. Put it in earthenware jars and store it for some time, keeping it free of flies
9. Eat it as desired
