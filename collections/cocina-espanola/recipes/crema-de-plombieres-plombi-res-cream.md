## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- one tablespoon of rice flour cream
- three glasses of very hot milk
- necessary sugar
- one grain of salt
- two glasses of Kümmel, or vanilla, lemon, etc. for flavoring

## Directions

1. Mix well in a saucepan eight egg yolks and one tablespoon of rice flour cream, three glasses of very hot milk, and place over the fire, stirring well with a wooden spoon.
2. As soon as it begins to thicken, remove from the fire without ceasing to stir, and heat again until it cooks for a few minutes.
3. Add the necessary sugar and one grain of salt, and when it is cold, pour it into another saucepan and place over ice, stirring from time to time with the spoon.
4. As soon as it takes consistency with the cold, add two glasses of Kümmel, or flavor beforehand with vanilla, lemon, etc.
5. Place in a serving dish, or in small cups, or over sponge cakes.
