## Ingredients

- two quartillos of water
- two pounds of piloncillo sugar
- three dozen egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of piloncillo sugar in two quartillos of water.
2. Strain through a felt cloth.
3. Beat three dozen egg yolks until they bind together in cordoncillo.
4. Mix well.
5. Continue beating.
6. Place in a flan mold or small molds.
7. Cook the dish in a water bath.
8. Do not remove from the mold until completely cold.
