## Ingredients

- Fine oil
- Four or five trout of half a pound each
- Salt
- Pepper
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. In a metal baking dish that is to go to the table, pour fine oil to coat the bottom and place four or five trout of half a pound each.
2. Season with salt and pepper, and over high heat brown them, moving the dish so they do not stick.
3. When they are browned on one side, turn them to the other, and cover them then with breadcrumbs mixed together.
