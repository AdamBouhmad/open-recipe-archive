## Ingredients

- meat from the legs of roasted lamb
- bacon pieces
- butifarra
- kid goat
- one head of garlic
- white wine
- brandy
- bay leaf
- oregano
- tarragon
- salt

## Directions

1. Make a chopped mixture with the meat from the legs of roasted lamb
2. Form a paste or dough and manipulate and spread it well
3. Expose it to brisk charcoal fire on grills and once it takes good color, divide into pieces
4. Place the pieces in a pot, seasoning with the appropriate salt
5. Add some pieces of bacon and butifarra, kid goat, one whole head of garlic, some white wine and brandy in proportion, bay leaf, oregano and tarragon
6. Cook everything in a well-covered pot with brown paper and a bowl of water on top as a seal to refresh the stew if necessary
