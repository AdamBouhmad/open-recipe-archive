## Ingredients

- Small fresh broad beans
- Oil
- Garlic
- Toasted hazelnuts
- Toasted bread
- Water
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Remove only the ends of the beans
2. Cook first and then drain
3. Place in a pan and add oil with fried garlic
4. Make a sauce with toasted hazelnuts, a little toasted bread soaked in water and vinegar, pepper, and eggs proportionate to the sauce
5. Dilute the sauce with seasoned water
6. Pour over the beans
