## Ingredients

- Multiple eggs, quantity depending on desired size
- A clean bladder

## Directions

1. Break as many eggs as desired, more or less depending on the size desired for the final egg.
2. Separate the yolks from the whites when breaking them.
3. Take a very clean bladder whose capacity can hold all the yolks and fill it with them after they have been well beaten.
4. Tie the bladder very well to prevent air from entering and suspend it in boiling water until the yolks have taken on a suitable consistency.
5. Untie the string of the bladder and remove a giant yolk.
6. Then take a larger bladder, place the beaten egg whites in it, place the yolk in the middle, and tie it again.
7. Expose everything to the heat of boiling water until the enormous white has hardened enough that the final bladder can be broken and release the enormously large egg.
