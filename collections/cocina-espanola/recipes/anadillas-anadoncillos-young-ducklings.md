## Ingredients

- Young duckling, cleaned and prepared
- Bacon leaves, one underneath and one on top
- 125 grams of lean veal
- 125 grams of ham, cut into small cubes
- Carrots
- Onions
- Salt and pepper
- Bouquet of fresh herbs

## Directions

1. Cook the duckling in a pot between two bacon leaves, one underneath and one on top, with 125 grams of lean veal and an equal amount of ham cut into small cubes, carrots, onions, good seasoning of salt and pepper, and a bouquet of fresh herbs.
