## Ingredients

- One egg
- Two tablespoons of water
- Two tablespoons of flour
- A pinch of salt
- A small amount of butter
- A few drops of lemon or cognac

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl
2. Work together with a wooden spatula without handling by hand until a good dough forms
3. The dough will soon separate from the bowl on its own
4. Place on a table or cutting board
5. Dust strongly with flour and continue dusting while working slightly with a rolling pin
6. Spread into a thin sheet the thickness of a coin
7. Carefully and precisely, draw lines on the dough four centimeters apart, first lengthwise and then crosswise at the same distance to create squares
8. Mold and close the squares on the same board
