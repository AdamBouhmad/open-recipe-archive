## Ingredients

- partridges
- oil
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- garlic
- spices
- brine

## Directions

1. Pluck, clean and cut the partridges into pieces
2. Fry them in oil; when they are half-fried, transfer them to vinegar with bay leaves, crushed garlic and spices
3. Place them in a vessel, add brine, and cover with oil on top
4. Seal hermetically with parchment and place the vessel in a cool location
