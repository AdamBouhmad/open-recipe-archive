## Ingredients

- 60 grams of flour
- 1 whole egg
- 6 egg yolks
- 200 grams of powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon of orange blossom water
- zest of 1 lemon
- 1 quarter liter of milk
- puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Dissolve 60 grams of flour with 1 whole egg in a saucepan
2. Add afterwards 6 egg yolks, 200 grams of powdered sugar, 1 tablespoon of orange blossom water, the zest of 1 lemon, and 1 quarter liter of milk
3. Line the inside of a smooth, not tall mold, though it may be very wide, with a good layer of puff pastry dough
4. Pour in the flan mixture
5. Bake in a bell oven with heat from above and below
6. When cold, remove from the mold to serve, dusting it with sugar
