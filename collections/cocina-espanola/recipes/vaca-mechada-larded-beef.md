## Ingredients

- beef
- bacon
- butter
- sorrel

## Directions

1. Soak the meat, and after removing the skins well, place inside many small pieces of bacon at intervals
2. Put butter in a casserole, and when hot, add the meat, which should not be stirred
3. Cover with an iron lid and with fire on top and coals below, cook it slowly
4. Afterwards prepare a sauce of cooked sorrel, which should be poured not on top of the meat but at its sides
5. Serve, being careful not to let the juice reduce too much
