## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- cumin, ground
- hot pepper, a little
- saffron
- nutmeg
- broth from the pot

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous sauce, adding to it a little hot pepper, saffron and nutmeg
2. Follow the same rules and make it a little before serving
