## Ingredients

- fish broth
- butter
- sorrel
- chervil
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Make a broth with sea fish
2. In a pot, fry butter with cut sorrel, chervil and onions
3. Moisten with the fish broth, salt and add pepper
4. Bring to a boil for one hour
5. Blanch the broth and thicken with egg yolks beaten in water
6. Serve over slices of bread
