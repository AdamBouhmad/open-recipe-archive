## Ingredients

- eggs
- peach jam
- apricot jam
- stewed peach slices
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Make the omelet as you would with potatoes, mushrooms, or pea purée à la française, and with butter, but being careful not to add salt.
2. At the moment of folding it in the pan, add peach jam, apricot jam, stewed peach slices, currant jelly, etc.
3. Use a large, wide, and flat pan so that the surface is exaggerated in relation to its thickness, which should be slight.
