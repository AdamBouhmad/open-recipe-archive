## Ingredients

- pork jowl (papada de cerdo)
- oil
- paprika (pimentón)
- clove (very little)
- caraway (alcaravea)
- pepper (pimienta)
- flour made from bitter vetch (harina de almortas)
- water

## Directions

1. In a little hot oil, add pork jowl cut into small pieces.
2. Remove these pieces when fried, and in the resulting fat add paprika, clove (very little), caraway and pepper.
3. Gradually add a certain quantity of bitter vetch flour to brown in the fat.
4. Add fairly hot water little by little until the flour is dissolved and cook, constantly stirring with a wooden paddle until the porridge no longer tastes raw.
5. When the porridge is ready, place the pieces of pork jowl on top.
