## Ingredients

- potatoes
- eggs
- parsley
- small onion
- garlic clove
- mustard
- vinegar
- fried oil
- salt
- lettuce
- celery or watercress
- asparagus tips
- artichoke hearts
- mushrooms
- truffles
- beetroot

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes unpeeled and cook eggs in the same pot
2. In a bowl, mash six hard boiled eggs, incorporating finely chopped parsley, a little small onion, a garlic clove, seasoning to taste, a touch of mustard and all the vinegar the salad dressing will need
3. Once cooked and peeled, slice the potatoes and place them at the bottom of a salad bowl
4. Pour fried oil that has been prepared in advance and cooled, sprinkling with salt, making sure the potatoes are well soaked in the liquid
5. On top, place well-cleaned, well-washed and well-dried lettuce leaves, mixed with celery or watercress, and cover everything with desired garnish
6. The salad may include a variety of vegetables to enhance its appearance: asparagus tips, artichoke hearts, mushrooms, truffles, beetroot, and whatever else is desired
7. At the time of serving, pour the dressing as explained and mix, toss or work the salad for a quarter hour
8. María Tubau salad should have no sauce residue in the dish in which it is prepared; this is its true point
