## Ingredients

- one hundred twenty-five grams of beef or pork butter
- twelve large fresh mushrooms, finely chopped
- one carrot
- one turnip
- one onion
- some small onions
- finely chopped parsley
- one cup of broth
- one cup of white wine
- one handful of crumb of tender bread
- two cups of milk

## Directions

1. In one hundred twenty-five grams of beef or pork butter, sauté twelve large fresh mushrooms, finely chopped, a carrot and turnip in slices, one onion, some small onions, and finely chopped parsley
2. When the vegetables are half cooked in the fat, gradually add one cup of broth and then another of white wine
3. Let cook for half an hour over low heat
4. Season according to each person's taste and pass through a hair sieve
5. Separately, cook one handful of crumb of tender bread in two cups of milk until the liquid is consumed
6. Strain through a fine sieve and add this breadcrumb to the preceding sauce
