## Ingredients

- 1 large bottle Roéderer Champagne
- 1 small siphon of seltzer water
- 1 small glass of Sherry
- 1 larger glass of Curaçao
- 1 equal glass of Martel Cognac
- 1 whole fine lemon zest
- 2 large spoonfuls of ground sugar
- 1 quarter pound of strawberries, lightly crushed
- 1 pound of ice

## Directions

1. Mix everything well one hour before serving
2. Incorporate the ice at the last moment
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Pour into white Bohemian crystal pitchers to serve
