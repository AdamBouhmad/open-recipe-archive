## Ingredients

- long strips of bacon
- carrots, split
- three or four onions
- bouquet of fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- leg of mutton, deboned or well tenderized

## Directions

1. Place long strips of bacon, split carrots, three or four onions, a bouquet of fine herbs with salt and pepper at the bottom of a braising pot
2. Place the deboned leg of mutton on top, or if not deboned, well tenderized with a mallet and with the protruding bone cut but not completely removed from the piece of meat to which it is attached, so as to be able to reconstruct the handle for good presentation at the moment of serving
3. Fill the pot with water and skim as soon as it begins to boil
4. Let it boil until the liquid reduces to three quarters
5. Remove the leg of mutton from the vessel, strain or pass the broth through a sieve, and return both meat and liquid to the pot
6. Cook from this moment with great slowness until it reaches its point
7. If the broth is clear or thin, reduce it separately
8. At the moment of serving, add to its broth a little Spanish sauce or the juices from roasted meat with a glass of white wine
