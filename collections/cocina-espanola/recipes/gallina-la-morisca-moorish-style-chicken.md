## Ingredients

- 2 hens or 4 young chickens
- onion
- bacon in dice
- broth
- spices (excluding cloves)
- vinegar
- fresh butter
- flour
- vegetables, chopped (optional)

## Directions

1. Roast the hens or chickens
2. Cut them into quarters
3. Fry a little onion with a little diced bacon; stew it very well
4. Add broth and season with all spices except cloves, and cook slowly
5. Add a little vinegar so they are well soured
6. If you have some fresh butter, add it inside and you can fry a little flour in this butter as described previously, because this dish should not carry eggs
7. If you wish to add a little chopped vegetable, you can
8. This dish should come out a little yellow
