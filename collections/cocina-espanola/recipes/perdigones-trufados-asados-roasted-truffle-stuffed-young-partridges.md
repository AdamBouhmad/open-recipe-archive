## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Truffles
- Truffles, cooked beforehand in white wine, in quantity equal to half the weight of the bird
- White wine

## Directions

1. Stuff the young partridges with a cooked filling mixed with truffles that have been cooked beforehand in white wine, using as many truffles as half the weight of the bird
2. Roast them carefully on the spit so the meat does not dry out
3. Serve with a sauce made with truffles soaked in the juice from the roast
