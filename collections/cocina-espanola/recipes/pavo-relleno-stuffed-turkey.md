## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Minced poultry or sausage meat
- Cooked chestnuts, cut into four pieces

## Directions

1. Fill the turkey generally with minced poultry or sausage meat mixed with cooked chestnuts cut into four pieces
2. Roast as for regular turkey, but requiring an additional half hour to one hour
3. Follow the same rules as for roasted turkey
