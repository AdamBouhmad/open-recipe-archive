## Ingredients

- two chickens
- two pounds of apples, well cleaned and peeled
- one liter of water
- two ounces of sugar
- a little clove
- a touch of vanilla

## Directions

1. Roast two chickens either on a spit, in a saucepan, or in the oven.
2. For the apple compote, cook two pounds of apples that are well cleaned and peeled in one liter of water with two ounces of sugar, a little clove, and a touch of vanilla.
3. Allow three quarters of the water to reduce and pass through a food mill into a purée.
4. Pour into a saucepan and, stirring with a spatula, hasten the complete evaporation of the remaining water.
5. Serve the whole roasted chickens over this compote.
