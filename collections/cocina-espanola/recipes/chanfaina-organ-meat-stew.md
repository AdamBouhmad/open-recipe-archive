## Ingredients

- liver
- lungs
- cooked blood
- all from mutton
- oil
- onions
- garlic
- spices
- chopped parsley
- paprika
- water
- flour
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut pieces of liver, lungs and cooked blood from mutton
2. Sauté in oil, adding onions, garlic, spices, chopped parsley and paprika
3. After everything is well sautéed, add water and bring to a boil
4. Fry flour until well browned, dissolve it with water, and add to the stew to thicken the broth
5. If desired, an egg mixture can be added
