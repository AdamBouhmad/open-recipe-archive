## Ingredients

- Two or three chickens
- Good consummate broth
- Skins and broken bones from the roasted birds
- Half a dozen sweet almonds, well blanched
- A crumb of bread the size of an egg
- Broth from the stew pot

## Directions

1. Roast two or three chickens on a spit according to the number of diners
2. Prepare good consummate broth separately and incorporate into it the skins and broken bones of the roasted birds for good flavor
3. Clean the chicken meat very well, removing any sinews and all hard parts or those that have browned
4. Pound the meat in a marble mortar with half a dozen well-blanched sweet almonds
5. Soak a crumb of bread the size of an egg in broth from the stew pot
6. Incorporate the bread crumb into the bird puree, moistening it with broth so it is more thin than thick and can pass easily through a fine strainer
7. Heat this puree at the moment of serving
