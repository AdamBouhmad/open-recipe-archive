## Ingredients

- smoked or cured herring
- good oil

## Directions

1. Choose smoked or cured herring, making sure they are not too old as they could be too salty
2. Remove the head and skin completely, dividing them lengthwise into two equal parts
3. Extract the middle spine and all other small bones
4. From each regular-sized herring form two fillets of about ten centimeters long by three wide
5. Cut these fillets into strips of one centimeter wide throughout
6. Place on a pickled plate with good oil and serve
