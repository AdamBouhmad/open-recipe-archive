## Ingredients

- chicken, killed and plucked
- one pound of pork loin, minced very fine
- three pounds of veal from the leg, minced very fine
- salt
- pepper
- ham slices
- onions
- carrots
- garlic
- thyme
- parsley

## Directions

1. When the chicken is hot after being killed and plucked, open it with a pocketknife from the back of the neck to the tail, cutting carefully until the bones are reached
2. Cut carefully the meat and skin adhered to the bones and remove them so that all the inside comes out and only the meat and skin remain
3. Mince one pound of pork loin and three pounds of veal from the leg very fine, seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Fill the chicken with this mixture, adding some ham slices and filling all the hollow spaces of the chicken so that it acquires the same form as when it had bones
5. Sew the open part with needle and thread and wrap it in a new well-sewn cloth
6. Cook in water with onions, carrots, garlic, thyme and parsley until done
7. Remove when it is seen that it is cooked
