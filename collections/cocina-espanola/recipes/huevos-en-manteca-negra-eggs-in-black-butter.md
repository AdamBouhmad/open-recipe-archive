## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Fresh unsalted butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Place a good piece of fresh unsalted butter in a pan and let it take on a deep caramel color.
2. Add the eggs with great care and season with salt and a pinch of pepper.
3. When the white is well set and before the yolk sets, slide them gently together from the pan to a dish.
4. Pour the butter over them and sprinkle the surface with a little parsley.
5. In the same pan, heat a spoonful of vinegar, which is also added to finish the operation.
