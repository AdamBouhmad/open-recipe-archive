## Ingredients

- two chops together, each with two ribs
- meat for meatballs filling

## Directions

1. Cut two chops together, each comprising two ribs, and removing the bone from one of them, flatten them well and perfect their shape.
2. Open the meat across, beating the two parts with the handle of the knife, but without separating from the bone.
3. Fill the hollow in the middle with a finely chopped meat filling from meatballs, and join the two parts of the meat of the chops together again, and roast on the grill until they are well done.
