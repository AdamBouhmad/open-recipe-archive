## Ingredients

- skate, cleaned and cut
- water with salt
- half a quartillo of vinegar
- onions
- bouquet of fine herbs
- white sauce
- capers

## Directions

1. After cleaning and cutting the skate regularly across the fibers, soak it for one hour in very salted water
2. Cook it in a short broth made with part of the salted water from the soaking, half a quartillo of vinegar, some onions, and a bouquet of fine herbs
3. When cooked, remove the skin completely and place the skate on a deep platter
4. Pour white sauce over it and add a good quantity of capers
