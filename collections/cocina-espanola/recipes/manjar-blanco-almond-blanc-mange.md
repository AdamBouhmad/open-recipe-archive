## Ingredients

- one pound of sweet almonds
- twenty bitter almond kernels, all peeled
- cold water
- three glasses of simple jelly, somewhat warmed

## Directions

1. Prepare by putting one pound of sweet almonds and twenty bitter almond kernels in cold water infusion for twenty-four hours, all of them peeled.
2. Pound them immediately, sprinkling during this operation with some spoonfuls of very cold water; and when no fragment is distinguishable from them and the paste has enough homogeneity, add four or five glasses of water, strain this mass first through a cloth, pressing it with force, and then through a napkin.
3. Mix three glasses of simple jelly, somewhat more than lukewarm, and empty the resulting liquid into the mold.
