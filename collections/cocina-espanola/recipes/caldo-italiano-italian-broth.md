## Ingredients

- One piece of bacon
- Veal four times larger than the bacon
- Carrots
- Onions
- A small piece of beef
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, place a piece of bacon and another piece of veal four times larger.
2. Sauté and brown the meat well with the fat from the bacon over very high heat.
3. When this operation is finished, add the meat and bacon to a pot filled with plenty of boiling water.
4. Add carrots, onions, and a small piece of beef.
5. Cook for three hours over gentle heat.
6. If the broth is for the sick, use beef fat instead of bacon.
