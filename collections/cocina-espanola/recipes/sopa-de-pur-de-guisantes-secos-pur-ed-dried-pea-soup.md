## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of dried peas
- cow butter
- onions
- carrots
- broth
- herbs

## Directions

1. Brown in a saucepan with cow butter, onions and carrots cut
2. When they are tender, add 2 pounds of dried peas, browning them with the vegetables for some time
3. Moisten with broth
4. Well covered, add a small bundle of herbs and cook beside the fire for about two hours
5. Pass through a fine sieve
6. Then season, heat, and serve
