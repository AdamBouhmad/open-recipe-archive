## Ingredients

- Water
- Coffee, measured according to taste

## Directions

1. In an earthenware pot—it must be a pot and earthenware—heat the necessary water.
2. When it boils vigorously, add the coffee measured according to the taste of the one who will drink it.
3. Cover the mouth of the pot with a napkin well soaked in cold water and formed into a ball.
4. After three or four minutes, uncover the pot and pour in two or three spoonfuls of cold water.
5. With the help of a very fine cloth strainer, transfer the liquid from the pot to a cooking coffeepot.
6. Serve immediately.
