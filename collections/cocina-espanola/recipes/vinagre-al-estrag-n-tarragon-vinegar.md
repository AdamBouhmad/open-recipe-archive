## Ingredients

- One quart of strong vinegar
- Fresh tarragon branches

## Directions

1. Heat a quart of strong vinegar without allowing it to boil.
2. Submerge two or three freshly cut sprigs of tarragon in the hot vinegar.
3. When the vinegar has cooled, pour it into a bottle and add new sprigs of fresh tarragon to infuse.
4. Leave the tarragon branches in the bottle, replenishing the vinegar as it is used so that it remains saturated with the aroma of the plant.
