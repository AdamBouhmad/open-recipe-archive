## Ingredients

- oxtail
- salted water
- cabbage
- carrots
- turnips
- parsnips
- onions
- bacon pieces
- sausage
- good broth
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the oxtail into three parts
2. Blanch it with salted water
3. Cook in a marmita or casserole with cabbage, carrots, turnips, parsnips, onions, pieces of bacon and sausage
4. Moisten with good broth
5. Simmer gently for four to five hours
6. Drain everything and place in a serving dish
7. Pour the cooking liquid over it after reducing it and adding a bit of Spanish sauce
