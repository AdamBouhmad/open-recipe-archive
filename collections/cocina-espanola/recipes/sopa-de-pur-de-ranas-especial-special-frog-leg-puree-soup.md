## Ingredients

- 4 dozen frog legs
- cow butter
- onion
- carrots
- white wine
- pepper
- sauce
- poultry broth
- white broth
- egg yolks
- milk

## Directions

1. Take 4 dozen frog legs, wash and trim the nails
2. Blanch in salted water for 4 minutes
3. Refresh and dry them
4. Sauté with cow butter, finely cut onion and carrots
5. Moisten with half a quartillo of white wine and a little pepper
6. After 10 minutes remove and pound in a mortar
7. Add a quartillo of sauce, pass this through a sieve and strainer
8. Thin with poultry broth, a little white broth, and beaten egg yolks with milk and cow butter
9. Serve
