## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Oil or butter
- Butter or oil for basting

## Directions

1. In a metal plate or pan, heat a little fat—oil or pork butter, but properly butter from cows.
2. Carefully add the eggs, letting them set as desired over moderate heat.
3. Serve in the utensil in which they are made.
4. To give them shine on the surface and make the yolks appear clear, brush them with melted butter or oil with a spoon, cover with a lid and some coals on top for a couple of minutes.
5. Eggs on a plate accept all manner of garnishes—croutons, blanched ham, peas, asparagus tips, mushrooms, truffles, anchovy strips, sausages, etc.
