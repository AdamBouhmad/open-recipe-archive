## Ingredients

- one good hen
- one fine partridge
- one pound of well-cured ham, cut into very thin slices
- one pound of veal shank
- six bird feet, burned beforehand on the grill
- fine herbs bouquet
- two carrots
- two onions
- a few spoonfuls

## Directions

1. Place the hen, partridge, ham, veal shank, burned bird feet, herbs bouquet, carrots, onions, and spoonfuls in the pot
