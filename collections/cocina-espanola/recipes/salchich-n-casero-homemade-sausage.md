## Ingredients

- Two pounds of pork loin
- Half pound of very fat bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg, grated
- A few grains of coriander
- Beef intestines
- Paper for wrapping
- Retama (broom plant) for smoking

## Directions

1. Mince together the pork loin and bacon; the mixture need not be very fine
2. Season with plenty of salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, and a few grains of coriander
3. Fill beef intestines of variable length with this mixture
4. Hang the sausage inside the kitchen chimney to cure in smoke for ten days, being careful it does not burn, and wrap it first in three or four sheets of brown paper
5. During this time, burn a little retama in the chimney three or four times so the sausage comes out exquisite
6. After smoking, cook the sausage in water with salt, pepper, carrots, onions studded with cloves, and a few bay leaves
7. Two hours of slow cooking are sufficient to finish the operation
8. Let the sausage cool in the same water in which it cooked
9. Remove, let drain, and eat sliced into thin slices, the thinner the more flavorful
