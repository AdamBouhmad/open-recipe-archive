## Ingredients

- lean meat from beef leg
- chopped bacon
- onion
- garlic cloves
- spices
- white wine
- parsley
- salt
- paper

## Directions

1. Take the needed lean meat from beef leg and put to cook in the morning in the meat pot
2. When cooked, remove and let cool, cut into pieces
3. Fry chopped bacon in small pieces, and in its fat fry the beef
4. After frying, put in the pot where it is to be stewed
5. The bacon that is removed from the pan is put back in and fried again with chopped onion
6. Put everything in the pot with all spices, some mashed garlic cloves, white wine and parsley
7. Cook everything at low heat and cover the pot with double paper, putting a pot of water on top
8. Half an hour before serving season with salt
9. If the beef is good it has better taste than veal
