## Ingredients

- One cleaned chicken
- Salt
- Butter
- Garlic
- Half an onion
- Parsley
- Water
- Potatoes in pieces
- Calabash or zucchini as a substitute according to taste

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chicken, divide it into pieces by cutting at the joints and season lightly with salt
2. In a skillet or pot, heat butter until very hot, then add a little garlic, half an onion and parsley, being careful to fry all this without burning
3. Once this is well fried, add the chicken, turning it so it takes color
4. Immediately add water until it covers the chicken, and when the chicken is half cooked, add potatoes in pieces
5. When the potatoes are well cooked the tínola is ready
6. This dish should be broth-like
7. The potatoes can be replaced with calabash or zucchini according to taste
