## Ingredients

- Breasts of fattened capon
- Unsweetened crackers, not very large
- Hard-boiled egg slices
- Pitted olives
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Water
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Make a salad with the capon breasts, crackers, hard-boiled egg slices, and pitted olives
2. Season everything beforehand with olive oil, salt, and pepper
3. Incorporate water and vinegar at the moment of serving
4. The dish should be soaked in its dressing, not soupy
5. In summer, use ice water, and it becomes the Italian version of gazpacho
