## Ingredients

- large white clams
- butter
- garlic
- parsley
- scallions
- water
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Select the largest and whitest clams, taking care they have no parasites
2. Clean thoroughly in fresh water
3. Cook and open the clams
4. Make a sauté of garlic, parsley and scallions with butter
5. Add the clams and let them brown a little
6. Add water in proportion
7. Season with pepper and salt
8. Let boil for a while
9. Serve
