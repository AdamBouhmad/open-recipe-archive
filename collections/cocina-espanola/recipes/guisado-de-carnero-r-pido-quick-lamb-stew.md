## Ingredients

- Very small and thin pieces of lamb breast
- Salt
- Pepper
- A pinch of flour
- Broth
- Fine cuts of potatoes

## Directions

1. Over high heat in a skillet, brown very small and thin pieces of lamb breast.
2. Brown them well and even toast them a little.
3. Season with salt and pepper, dust with a pinch of flour, and wet with a little broth.
4. Add fine cuts of potatoes.
5. In one hour the stew is ready.
