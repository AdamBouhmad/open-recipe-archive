## Ingredients

- capon breast
- unsweetened crackers
- hard-boiled eggs
- pitted olives
- olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Make a salad with capon breasts, unsweetened crackers not too large, slices of hard-boiled eggs, and pitted olives
2. Season everything beforehand with olive oil, salt, and pepper
3. At the moment of serving, add water and vinegar and mix together
4. The dish should be soaked in its dressing, not brothy
5. In summer, use ice-cold water
