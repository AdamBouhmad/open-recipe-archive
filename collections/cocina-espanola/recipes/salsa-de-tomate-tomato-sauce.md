## Ingredients

- 125 grams of fat (oil, butter, or lard)
- eight large tomatoes the size of eggs
- two onions of equal size
- broth or water
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the tomatoes and onions in any manner.
2. Fry them well in a pan in the hot fat.
3. Moisten everything with a little broth or water, finishing the cooking over low heat.
4. Season during the frying.
5. Pass the sauce through a strainer, forcing it to pass with a wooden spoon.
6. If the sauce is thin, stir it over the fire so it boils a little and reduces.
7. If it is thick, thin it with broth.
