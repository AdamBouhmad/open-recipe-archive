## Ingredients

- peppers
- picadillo (any ground meat mixture)
- bread, crumbled
- oil
- clean broth

## Directions

1. Hollow out the peppers and clean them inside without breaking them.
2. Fill them with a picadillo kneaded with crumbled bread.
3. Fry them in oil and place them in a baking dish.
4. Moisten them with clean broth and cook them in a camp oven with gentle heat above and below.
