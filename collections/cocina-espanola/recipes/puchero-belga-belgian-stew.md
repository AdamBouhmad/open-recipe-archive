## Ingredients

- from two to six pounds beef
- three medium carrots
- two turnips
- four leeks
- one parsnip or gallego turnip
- a bundle of parsley
- one roasted onion
- two or three cloves
- sufficient salt
- hot water

## Directions

1. Take from two to six pounds of beef and put in a pot, adding half an azumbre of water per pound, and put on moderate fire, which should be increased little by little in order to extract the foam
2. Remove the foam as it rises to the surface until none appears
3. Leave the pot for eight consecutive hours on an equal and moderate fire, and after the first four hours, add three medium carrots, two turnips, four leeks, one parsnip or gallego turnip, all cut in half
4. Add a bundle of parsley, larger or smaller as proportionate, one roasted onion with two or three cloves stuck in it, sufficient salt, and take care to add hot water as the first evaporates
5. If to this mixture is added a whole bird, or at least half a chicken, the scraps of a turkey or ribs of roasted lamb, the result is a tasty stew
