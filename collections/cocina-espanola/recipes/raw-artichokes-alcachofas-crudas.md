## Ingredients

- Tender, early artichokes
- Egg yolk
- English mustard
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Salt
- White pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the artichokes well of stem and outer leaves
2. Make a pepper sauce with the yolk of a hard-boiled egg, a little English mustard, two spoonfuls of oil, one of vinegar, salt and white pepper
3. Serve the artichokes with this cold sauce
