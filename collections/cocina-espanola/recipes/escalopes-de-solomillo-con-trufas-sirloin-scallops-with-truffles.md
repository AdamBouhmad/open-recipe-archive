## Ingredients

- sirloin
- beef butter
- truffles
- broth
- Sherry wine
- Spanish sauce or roasting juice
- mushrooms as alternative to truffles
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the sirloin, whether raw or cooked, into thin small pieces, then trim them like five-peseta coins
2. Sauté the raw or cooked meat in beef butter
3. Serve with sautéed and cooked truffles in broth with Sherry wine, pouring over all a Spanish sauce or roasting juice
4. As an alternative to truffles, mushrooms prepared the same way can be used, and in many cases the scallops are served only with very thick tomato sauce
