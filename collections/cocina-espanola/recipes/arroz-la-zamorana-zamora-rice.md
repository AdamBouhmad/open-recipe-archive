## Ingredients

- four ounces of excellent lard
- one pound of onions
- half pound of turnips
- parsley
- oregano
- thyme
- half dozen garlic cloves
- sweet pepper
- water
- pork paw
- pork ear
- pork snout
- one pound of good ham

## Directions

1. Melt four ounces of excellent lard in a clay pot or bowl
2. Stew in it one pound of onions and half pound of turnips
3. Add parsley, oregano, thyme, half a dozen garlic cloves and a little sweet pepper
4. Fry and cook everything for four hours before the onion takes color
5. After incorporating water, add pork paw, ear and snout, all cut into small pieces and properly deboned
6. Add one pound of good ham and complete the seasoning
