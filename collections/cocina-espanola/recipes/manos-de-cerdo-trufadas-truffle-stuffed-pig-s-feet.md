## Ingredients

- pig's hands, already cooked à la Sainte-Menehould
- fine forcemeat for birds
- truffle trimmings, cooked separately in good white wine or clean broth
- caul fat from pork or veal
- melted beef lard
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Debone the pig's hands that have already been cooked à la Sainte-Menehould
2. Prepare a fine forcemeat as for stuffing birds and mix it with a good quantity of truffle trimmings that have been cooked separately in good white wine or clean broth
3. Stuff the deboned hands with this mixture and wrap them in caul fat from pork or veal, which is the transparent membrane that envelops the fat
4. Without delay, soak the truffle-stuffed hands in melted beef lard
5. Coat them generously with breadcrumbs and brown them on the grill over moderate heat, taking time to color them well
6. Serve the truffle-stuffed pig's feet alone, accompanied by a few sips of Champagne
