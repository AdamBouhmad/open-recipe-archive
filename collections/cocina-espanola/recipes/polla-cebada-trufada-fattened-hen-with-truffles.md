## Ingredients

- fattened hen
- 500 grams truffles
- 250 grams bacon pieces
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and empty the freshly killed bird, carefully removing the main bone of the breastbone without tearing the skin
2. In a separate pan over high heat, sauté 500 grams of truffles cut into quarters in 250 grams of chopped bacon
3. Stir the truffles with a spatula and let them cook slowly for half an hour in the bacon fat
4. Remove from heat and let cool
5. Fill the interior of the bird with the cooled truffle mixture
6. Place the stuffed bird in a cold place in a basting pan for 34 hours in summer or 48 hours in winter, without touching it, so the meat becomes well infused with the truffle aroma
7. Roast in the rotisserie covered with a thin sheet of bacon and wrapped in thick well-greased paper
8. One hour is sufficient to roast the bird over medium heat
9. Remove the paper and bacon so it takes on color
10. Serve very hot, plain or in its own juices, sprinkled with some truffle shavings that were set aside earlier
