## Ingredients

- noodles (fideo), broken by hand
- broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Add broken noodles by the handful to boiling broth.
2. Let them cook without becoming too soft, stirring occasionally with a wooden spoon.
3. Remove the saucepan from the heat when the noodles begin to dissolve, as this would cloud the soup and diminish its pleasant flavor.
4. For clear soup, use thirty grams of noodles per liter of broth. For thicker soup, double the amount of noodles.
5. Serve immediately as this soup is only good when made at the moment of serving.
