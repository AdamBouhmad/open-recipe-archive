## Ingredients

- Salmon, cooked in blue court-bouillon
- Truffles, cut into wedges
- Mushrooms, cut into wedges
- Jerez wine
- Veal lardoons, sliced thin and glazed
- Squab breast trimmings
- Squab breast meatballs
- Crayfish
- Fresh oysters, shucked
- Fine oil

## Directions

1. Cook the salmon in blue court-bouillon and drain it.
2. Insert wedges of truffles alternating with wedges of mushrooms into the loins.
3. Place the prepared salmon in an oval serving dish and sprinkle with Jerez wine.
4. Cover entirely with thin slices of larded and glazed veal, squab breast trimmings, and squab breast meatballs, which shall adorn the platter with crayfish placed among them, and a garnish of fresh shucked oysters.
5. With the dish thus arranged and carefully sprinkled with fine oil, so that the garnish does not become disarranged or decomposed, place in the oven at high heat for five minutes, and leave it there for half an hour more, but with the oven door open.
