## Ingredients

- 1 kilo of choucroute
- 1 pound of smoked bacon, finely sliced
- 1 sobreasada or chorizo weighing half a pound
- 125 grams of pork lard
- half a cuartillo of white wine
- 2 ladles of good clean broth
- salt and pepper
- pinch of grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Desalt the choucroute by soaking in water for three hours. Drain well and press it with two casserole lids.
2. Place the choucroute in a casserole over gentle heat with 1 pound of smoked bacon cut into very fine slices, 1 sobreasada or chorizo weighing half a pound, and 125 grams of pork lard.
3. Moisten with half a cuartillo of white wine and 2 ladles of good clean broth. Season carefully with salt and pepper and add a pinch of grated nutmeg.
4. Cook the cabbage with the bacon and chorizo for five consecutive hours over very low heat.
5. When serving, cut the sausage into slices and arrange them on the platter with symmetry. If the broth is abundant, reduce it over a lively fire while arranging the dish, but without adding anything else.
