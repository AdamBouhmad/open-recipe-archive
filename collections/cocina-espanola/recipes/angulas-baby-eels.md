## Ingredients

- Baby eels
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Garlic

## Directions

1. Clean the baby eels with a cloth and scald them with boiling water, dry them again and place in a covered serving dish.
2. Sprinkle with salt and a touch of pepper, and keep the dish in a bain-marie so the plate is very hot.
3. At the moment of serving, pour over them, straining, oil fried with garlic, very hot and only in quantity enough to coat the baby eels without leaving excess broth.
