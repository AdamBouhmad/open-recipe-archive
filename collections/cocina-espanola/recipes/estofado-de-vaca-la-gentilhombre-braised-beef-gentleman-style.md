## Ingredients

- A solid piece of beef, preferably from the top muscle
- Fresh bacon strips, quite thick
- Salt and spices

## Directions

1. The piece of beef for this preparation should be solid, if possible from the top muscle.
2. Lard it with strips of fresh bacon that are quite thick.
3. Season and spice it raw.
4. This dish, which is none other than the best Beef à la Mode in the Parisian style, is corrected and titled by me, as one of the best preparations for beef after roasting, and very suitable for large households with good management.
