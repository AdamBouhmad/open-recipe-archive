## Ingredients

- bogas (fish)
- butter
- flour
- fresh butter
- fine herbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Roast the bogas on grills, wrapped in paper spread with butter.
2. Make a white sauce with a handful of flour and fresh butter, adding fine herbs and the juice of a lemon.
3. Serve the bogas dry on a separate plate and the sauce in a sauce boat at the same time.
