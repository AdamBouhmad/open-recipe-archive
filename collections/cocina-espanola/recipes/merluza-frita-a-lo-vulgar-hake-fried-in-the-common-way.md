## Ingredients

- Hake pieces from the middle or loin
- Flour
- Beaten eggs
- Pork lard or oil

## Directions

1. After washing and scaling the hake pieces, remove the bones and skin while raw
2. Cut into thin slices and flatten them with a spatula
3. Coat in flour, then in beaten eggs
4. Fry immediately in pork lard or oil until dry
5. Serve. Some season at the table with a little vinegar or juice of sour orange or lemon
