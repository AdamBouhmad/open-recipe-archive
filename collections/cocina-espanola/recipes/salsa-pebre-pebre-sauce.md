## Ingredients

- vinegar
- shallot
- thyme
- bay leaf
- parsley
- small onion
- pepper
- brown sauce
- broth

## Directions

1. Fill a small saucepan about two-thirds with vinegar.
2. Add shallot, thyme, bay leaf, parsley, small onion, and a good amount of pepper.
3. Make a brown sauce which is diluted in broth.
4. Let it boil for a quarter hour.
5. Before serving, strain through a sieve.
