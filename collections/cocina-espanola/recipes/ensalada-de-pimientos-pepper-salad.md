## Ingredients

- green tender peppers
- tomato
- oil
- salt
- vinegar
- garlic
- cumin
- mint

## Directions

1. Choose the most tender peppers; peel and cut into thin round slices
2. Mash well-peeled tomato with the seeds removed, add oil, vinegar, salt, cumin and a pinch of dried mint or chopped green mint if available, mix everything well, add the necessary water, and they can be served
3. This formula is from La Rioja
