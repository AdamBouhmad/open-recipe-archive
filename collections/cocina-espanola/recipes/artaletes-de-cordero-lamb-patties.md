## Ingredients

- legs of roasted lamb
- fat bacon
- beef marrow
- half a dozen egg yolks
- some vegetables
- spearmint
- marjoram
- all spices
- salt
- four hard-boiled egg yolks
- lemon juice
- thin caul fat from mutton
- pork lard

## Directions

1. Cook the legs of roasted lamb
2. When cooked, chop very finely and mix with fat bacon and beef marrow
3. Add half a dozen egg yolks, some vegetables, spearmint and marjoram, seasoning with all spices and plenty of salt
4. Crush four hard-boiled egg yolks with a little lemon juice and add to the mixture
5. Take thin caul fat from mutton and form the artaletes (patties) with it
6. Place them in a well-greased baking dish with pork lard
7. Cook on low heat with heat applied to the lid
