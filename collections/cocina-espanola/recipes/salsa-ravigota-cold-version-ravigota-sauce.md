## Ingredients

- fine herbs (parsley, chervil, chives, watercress, pimpernel, and celery), trimmed in advance
- one tablespoon of capers
- one pair of anchovies
- one egg yolk
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon of oil
- one tablespoon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Pound the fine herbs in a mortar for a considerable time.
2. When all of this forms a uniform paste, add one egg yolk, salt and pepper, one tablespoon of oil and another of vinegar.
3. Beat the mixture for the space of ten minutes.
