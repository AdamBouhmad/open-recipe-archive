## Ingredients

- Thin slices of raw ham
- Bread slices without crust, passed through lard
- Fine herbs
- Mushrooms
- Truffles or earth nuts (criadillas de tierra)
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. In a dish or casserole, place thin slices of raw ham and bread slices passed through lard.
2. Layer alternately with fine herbs, mushrooms, and truffles or earth nuts.
3. Sprinkle everything with breadcrumbs.
4. Cook in the oven for two or three hours to serve hot, or better yet, place on hot coals with fire on top in a casserole appropriate for this purpose, common in Austrian kitchens.
5. Serve hot.
