## Ingredients

- Fat slices of veal breast
- Oil
- Garlic cloves
- Parsley
- Mint
- Salt
- Pine nuts
- Onion, chopped
- Water
- A handful of rice
- Beets, cut into rough pieces

## Directions

1. Fry and brown in oil fat slices of veal breast and also brown some garlic cloves.
2. Incorporate parsley, mint, much salt, pine nuts, and some chopped onion.
3. Cook this in a pot over low heat with plenty of water.
4. Half an hour before eating, add a handful of rice and beets cut into rough pieces to fill the vessel.
