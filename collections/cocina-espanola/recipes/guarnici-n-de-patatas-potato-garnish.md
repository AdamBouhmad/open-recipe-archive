## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- salt
- beef butter or pork fat
- shallots
- parsley
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the potatoes and cook them in salted water until well drained
2. In a saucepan with a wide base, place the potatoes in a single layer with a good amount of beef or pork fat to soak them in
3. Complete the cooking on the stove, stirring continually with a spatula until the potatoes are cooked and well saturated with fat
4. Add finely chopped shallots and parsley, a touch of salt and pepper
5. Serve in a dish around the cold meat from the stew
