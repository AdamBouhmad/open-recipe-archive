## Ingredients

- squabs
- butter
- bacon scraps
- small white sausages
- one carrot
- one head of cabbage, blanched and drained
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Sauté the squabs in butter with bacon scraps
2. Add small white sausages, one carrot, and one head of white cabbage that has been blanched and drained
3. When the squabs are cooked, drain the cabbage again
4. Place the squabs on a serving dish with the cabbage shredded on top and the sausages and bacon scraps arranged around
5. Reduce the sauce and pour over everything
