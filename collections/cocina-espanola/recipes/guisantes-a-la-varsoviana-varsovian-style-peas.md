## Ingredients

- Peas
- Fresh butter from cows
- Small tender onions
- Lettuce hearts
- Sugar
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Wrap raw peas in fine fresh butter from cows
2. Place on gentle heat with small tender onions and two or three lettuce hearts
3. Seal the pot hermetically, placing a greased white paper before the lid
4. Let cook for half an hour
5. Lightly dust with ground sugar
6. Do not bind with egg yolk
7. Do not salt or season until the last moment, and only sparingly
