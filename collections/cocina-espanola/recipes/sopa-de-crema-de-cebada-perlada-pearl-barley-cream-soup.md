## Ingredients

- butter from cows
- flour
- water
- pearl barley, cooked to saturation
- egg yolk
- fried bread cubes

## Directions

1. Make a roux with butter and flour in sufficient quantity
2. Add the water from cooked pearl barley to create a purée
3. Remove from heat and bind with an egg yolk beaten in half a cup of water
4. Serve with small cubes of fried bread in the soup
