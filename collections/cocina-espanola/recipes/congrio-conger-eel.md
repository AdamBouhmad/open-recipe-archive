## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram piece of conger eel
- 200 grams onion cut into thin slices or strips of half a centimeter
- 20 grams parsley sprigs
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 deciliters vinegar
- 1 garlic clove
- 30 grams salt
- 20 grams peppercorns
- water

## Directions

1. Purchase a piece of conger eel of one kilogram; if the piece contains intestines, be careful to remove them
2. Wash and tie it as you would meat for a stew, then blanch for a quarter hour in boiling water
3. Remove from water, drain and place in a pot with a capacity of four liters, which will be filled with water in such a way that it well covers the fish
4. Add the onion cut into thin slices or strips of half a centimeter, parsley sprigs, 2 bay leaves, 2 deciliters of vinegar, 1 garlic clove, 30 grams of salt, 20 grams of peppercorns
5. Cook very gently for half an hour, drain and serve on a platter over a napkin, garnishing it with parsley
6. Serve separately in a sauce boat either white sauce or hollandaise sauce, or better yet melted butter
