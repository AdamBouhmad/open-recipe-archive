## Ingredients

- Cooked cod
- Egg
- Bread crumbs
- Oil
- Sugar (optional)

## Directions

1. Once cooked, cut the cod into small pieces.
2. Coat them in beaten egg, then roll in bread crumbs.
3. Moisten again with egg and coat a second time in bread crumbs.
4. Fry in oil.
5. If desired, sprinkle sugar on top before serving.
