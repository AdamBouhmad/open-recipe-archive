## Ingredients

- Two pounds of sweet potato
- One pound of almonds, untoasted
- Six egg yolks
- One pound of superior sugar
- Half a quarter-liter of water
- Zest of one lemon

## Directions

1. Put half a quarter-liter of water, the sugar, and the sweet potato on to cook until half-done
2. Add the almonds and let the syrup cool
3. Dissolve the six egg yolks and combine everything, stirring constantly until it becomes a thick cream
4. Before putting the water, sugar, and sweet potato on the fire, add the zest of one lemon with the sweet potato, sugar, or water (it makes no difference)
