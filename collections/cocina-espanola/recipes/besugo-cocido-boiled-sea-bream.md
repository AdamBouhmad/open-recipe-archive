## Ingredients

- sea bream
- water
- oil
- garlic
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put sufficient water in a bream pan, and when it is boiling place the sea bream
2. After one boiling turn it over, and having cooked sufficiently, reduce the broth
3. Fry oil with some garlic and pour it over the fish
4. At the time of serving add vinegar
