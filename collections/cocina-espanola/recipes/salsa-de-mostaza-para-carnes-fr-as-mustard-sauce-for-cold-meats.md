## Ingredients

- pieces of meat
- mustard
- vinegar
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut the pieces of meat
2. Add mustard, vinegar, and oil in proportion to the amount of sauce you want to make
3. Add salt and pepper and lemon juice
4. Stir for fifteen or twenty minutes and serve this way
