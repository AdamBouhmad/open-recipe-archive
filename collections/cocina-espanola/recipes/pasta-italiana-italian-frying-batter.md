## Ingredients

- 250 grams flour
- warm water as needed
- 2 tablespoons refined and clarified oil

## Directions

1. Make this paste exactly the same as the French pasta
2. Use refined and clarified oil instead of beef butter, in the quantity of only 2 tablespoons
