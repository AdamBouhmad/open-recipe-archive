## Ingredients

- Thin fillets cut from the lamb loin
- Bread crumbs
- Fresh vegetables in equal form and size
- Cow butter

## Directions

1. Cut thin fillets from the high loin of the lamb.
2. Bread them and grill them on the grill.
3. Serve with a garnish of all the fresh vegetables on hand, prepared in equal form and size, and previously reheated and cooked in cow butter to produce the true jardinière garnish.
