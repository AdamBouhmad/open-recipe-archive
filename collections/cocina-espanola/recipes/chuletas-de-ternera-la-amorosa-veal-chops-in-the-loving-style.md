## Ingredients

- veal chops
- lard
- good oil
- bacon scrapings
- ham bits
- sweet wine (Malaga, Marsala, or Alicante)
- broth or meat juice
- salt and pepper
- bread croutons

## Directions

1. Beat the veal chops slightly and flour them, one by one, evenly.
2. In a pan, for twelve chops, melt one ounce of lard with three tablespoons of very good oil, some bacon scrapings and ham bits.
3. Sauté the chops over high heat, and when they have taken on a good color and are well browned, moisten them with two glasses of Malaga, Marsala, or Alicante wine, or any sweet wine.
4. Let it cook over low heat until the wine is consumed, and then season and add broth or a little juice to bathe the meat.
5. At the moment of serving, remove the chops, which are placed on a plate over croutons of equal shape, and pour over them the sauce that is in the pan, straining and sifting it through fine linen.
