## Ingredients

- Leftover roasted hare or rabbit meat
- Flour
- Eggs
- Spicy sauce or tomato sauce
- Ground meat
- Streaky bacon
- Cow's butter
- Milk

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover roasted hare or rabbit into small pieces and coat them with flour and egg.
2. Fry the coated pieces.
3. Dress them in a thick spicy or tomato sauce.
4. Mix the ground meat with streaky bacon and make a paste with flour, cow's butter, and milk, creating a type of béchamel.
5. Cook this hash over low heat for half an hour until it thickens with the milk.
6. Let it cool, and hours later make croquettes, either coat them in flour or wrap them in breadcrumbs to fry.
