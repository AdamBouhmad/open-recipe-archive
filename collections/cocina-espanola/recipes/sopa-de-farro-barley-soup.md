## Ingredients

- barley - four ounces for four bowls
- almonds, blanched - four ounces
- sugar - four ounces
- meat broth or water

## Directions

1. Shake the barley, which comes half-milled, to remove the chaff
2. Wash the barley three times with warm water
3. Cook it with meat broth until somewhat thick
4. Pound blanched almonds in a mortar (use few, only to whiten the barley)
5. Pound everything together
6. Pass through a cloth, pressing well, so only the sugar remains
7. Ensure it is very sweet
8. Do not cook more than a couple of boils, as it would turn brown
9. Alternatively, make it with almond milk, which is better because with broth it turns brown, and with milk it is very white though less substantial
10. To make it with almond milk, cook first with water or broth
11. Serve the soup
