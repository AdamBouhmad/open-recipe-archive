## Ingredients

- hake
- oil
- laurel leaves
- salt
- lemon slices
- garlic
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and cut into regular slices
2. Fry in oil
3. Once cold, place in a flat iron fish platter
4. Layer with laurel leaves, salt, lemon slices, and mashed garlic
5. Cover with equal parts water and vinegar
6. Can be served the next day or kept for several days in the same platter
