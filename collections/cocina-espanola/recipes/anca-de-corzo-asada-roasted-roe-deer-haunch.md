## Ingredients

- Roe deer haunch or hind leg
- Lard for larding

## Directions

1. Lard the haunch immediately after hunting
2. Keep it in the air for three or four days according to temperature
3. Roast it in the oven as one would roast a lamb leg, but for less time since the roe deer leg has less meat
4. Serve alone in its natural state, well moistened with its own juices, with a handle attached to the bone
