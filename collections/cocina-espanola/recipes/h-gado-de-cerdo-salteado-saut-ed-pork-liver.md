## Ingredients

- pork liver
- beef butter
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- shallots, finely chopped
- half a tablespoon of flour
- one ladle of broth
- a thin stream of vinegar
- one tablespoon of rum or cognac

## Directions

1. Trim the pork liver into small equal pieces and sauté in a pan with beef butter, turning frequently so that the cooking is uniform.
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and remove the liver pieces from the pan when they are slightly overcooked.
3. In the fat from the sauté, add finely chopped fine herbs and shallots and half a tablespoon of flour, with one ladle of broth and a thin stream of vinegar.
4. Once the sauce is bound, return the liver pieces to the pan so that they are soaked and cooked in the sauce, just for two minutes, adding one tablespoon of rum or cognac to aromatize the dish at the moment of serving.
