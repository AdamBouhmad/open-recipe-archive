## Ingredients

- Potatoes, sliced thin in wheels
- Oil or lard in large quantity

## Directions

1. Fry a few potatoes cut into thin wheels in a large quantity of oil or lard.
2. Remove the fried potatoes with a large skimming spoon and let them air until the liquid returns to boiling.
3. Return them to the hot oil, and they will come out puffed like fritters.
