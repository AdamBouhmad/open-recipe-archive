## Ingredients

- four squabs
- 125 grams of lard
- 60 grams of bacon scraps
- two dozen small mushrooms
- one good tablespoon of flour
- salt and pepper
- one or two ladles of clear broth
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Cut four squabs into four pieces each
2. Sauté the pieces in lard with bacon scraps and small mushrooms the size of walnuts
3. Turn the squab pieces frequently; they will cook in only a quarter hour over high heat
4. Once ready, remove the squabs from the pan
5. In the same fat, brown a good tablespoon of flour until it takes on a dark toasted color, and season with salt and pepper
6. Moisten with one or two ladles of clear broth
7. Return the squabs to the sauce to soak well and heat through
8. Arrange the squabs in a mound on a serving dish, pour the sauce over them, and add the juice of one lemon
