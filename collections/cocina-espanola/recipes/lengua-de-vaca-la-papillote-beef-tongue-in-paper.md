## Ingredients

- beef tongue, cooked and cooled
- filling for cutlets
- fresh bacon, thinly sliced
- white greased paper

## Directions

1. Cut the cooled cooked tongue into slices half an inch thick, cutting them diagonally so they have more surface area.
2. Prepare a filling like that made for cutlets.
3. Coat both sides of each piece of tongue with the filling.
4. Place thin slices of fresh bacon on top of the filling.
5. Wrap the prepared pieces of tongue in white greased paper and roast them on the grill.
