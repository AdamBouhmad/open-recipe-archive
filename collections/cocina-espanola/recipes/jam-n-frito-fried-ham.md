## Ingredients

- Ham slices
- Flour
- Pork lard
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Scald the ham slices in a skillet with boiling water and give them three boils.
2. Remove the ham and immerse it successively in two cold waters.
3. Make it drain and dry with a napkin.
4. Dust the slices lightly with flour, just enough to coat them.
5. Fry them in three minutes in plenty of hot pork lard.
6. Serve with a pinch of pepper.
