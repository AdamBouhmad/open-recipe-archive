## Ingredients

- ham slices with some fat
- pork lard
- powdered sugar
- half a tablespoon of water

## Directions

1. Cut slices of ham with some fat and keep in water for three or four minutes
2. Make a small box from half a sheet of paper and coat its bottom well with pork lard
3. Place the ham there, adding powdered sugar and half a tablespoon of water
4. Place on the fire over the grill, turning the slices
5. After three minutes the dish is ready
