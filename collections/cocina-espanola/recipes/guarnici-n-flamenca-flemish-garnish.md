## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- cabbage
- lettuce hearts
- beef or pork lard
- onions
- Spanish sauce or meat juice from previous meals

## Directions

1. Cook carrots and turnips in salted water in proportion to the size of the dish to be garnished.
2. Before cooking, cut the vegetables into pieces of equal shape and size.
3. Separately, cook cabbage and lettuce hearts, and sauté them in beef or pork lard with several onions, taking care that they do not burn.
4. Place the carrot pieces, turnips, lettuce stalks, and cut cabbage symmetrically in a circle around the dish, and the onions around the vegetables in the form of a cord, pouring Spanish sauce or meat juice from previous meals over everything on the platter.
