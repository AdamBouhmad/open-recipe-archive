## Ingredients

- Six egg yolks
- One quarter pound of sugar
- Three glasses of rum
- Double the amount of egg whites beaten to meringue point

## Directions

1. Beat the six egg yolks with the sugar and rum
2. Mix with the egg whites beaten to meringue point
3. Thicken slightly over heat
4. Remove from heat and beat again
5. Pour into a punch bowl to serve cold in cups with fine or coarse crackers
