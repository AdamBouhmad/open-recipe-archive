## Ingredients

- Whole lamb or half lamb or quarter lamb
- Thin sheets of bacon
- Salt
- Pepper
- White oiled paper

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and debone the neck up to the shoulder blades.
2. Keep the two halves of the front quarters open and spread apart by means of strong small reeds cut to measure and used as levers.
3. Break in half the bones of the two hind legs in order to cross the legs, mounting one on top of the other.
4. Secure the lamb to the roasting spit so that it cannot move or deform.
5. Cover or line it entirely with thin sheets of bacon and wrap it afterwards in white oiled paper, which is tied.
6. Carry it to a very strong fire and roast it for two hours.
7. Remove the paper and remove also the bacon sheets that have not melted or been consumed.
8. Rekindle the fire to give color to the meat on all sides.
9. Season with salt and pepper, sprinkling well.
10. Serve when the skin is very crispy.
