## Ingredients

- two or three dozen small oysters
- fish broth
- butter
- croutons

## Directions

1. In a mortar, crush and pound two or three dozen small oysters.
2. Place them in fish broth and let cook for half an hour.
3. Fry some croutons in butter and place them in the tureen.
4. At the moment of serving, pour over them the oyster broth, strained and made into a thick purée.
