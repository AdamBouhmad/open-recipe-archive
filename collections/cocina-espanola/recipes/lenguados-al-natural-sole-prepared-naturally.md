## Ingredients

- Sole fish
- Sea water or very salted water
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cook the sole in sea water or very salted water.
2. Remove from the fish kettle and drain.
3. Serve on a napkin on a platter with melted butter in a sauce boat.
4. Note: The sole should be added to the water only when it is boiling vigorously.
5. Ten minutes without interruption are sufficient for the operation.
