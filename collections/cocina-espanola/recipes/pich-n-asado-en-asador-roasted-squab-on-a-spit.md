## Ingredients

- one squab, plucked and cleaned
- one grape leaf
- one thin slice of bacon

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the bird
2. When the season permits, wrap it in a grape leaf
3. Cover with a thin slice of bacon
4. Roast over gentle heat for half an hour, prepared as shown in the illustration
5. The roasted squab should be well-done but not dry
