## Ingredients

- 125 grams salted bacon
- two dozen white sausages
- quenelles or meatballs
- mushrooms
- truffles or earthnuts
- white wine or dry sherry
- Spanish sauce or good pot broth
- sweetbreads of veal or lamb
- rooster combs

## Directions

1. Brown the bacon and white sausages in a pot
2. When the bacon is also browned, add quenelles, meatballs, mushrooms, and truffles or earthnuts, cut into equal volume to the sausages
3. Wet everything with white wine or dry sherry
4. Add Spanish sauce or good pot broth, and once the sauce has reduced by half, give a couple of boils with the seasoned meat already incorporated
5. Serve the stew
6. Alternatively, this stew can be made by cooking at the same time as the sausages, veal or lamb sweetbreads and rooster combs
