## Ingredients

- one kilogram of pork fat
- 600 grams of chopped onion
- one kilogram of bread, crumbled and moistened 24 hours before
- 250 grams of raw oil
- 125 grams of paprika
- salt
- a pinch of anise seeds

## Directions

1. Place one kilogram of pork fat in a cauldron over the fire, stirring constantly so it does not stick
2. When half melted, add 600 grams of chopped onion and continue stirring with a wooden spoon
3. After a quarter hour, add one kilogram of bread that has been crumbled and moistened 24 hours before, continuing to stir
4. Pour 250 grams of raw oil and 125 grams of paprika over the mixture, add the corresponding salt and a pinch of anise seeds
5. Keep over the fire, stirring constantly until the ingredients form a harmonious whole, or a lightly compact mass
6. While hot, stuff into casings
