## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts
- salt
- cow butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the Brussels sprouts. Submerge them in plenty of boiling water at a rolling boil, with an excess of salt.
2. After five minutes, remove them and pass them through two or three cold waters.
3. Drain and dry them over a lively fire in a casserole with cow butter, salt and pepper.
