## Ingredients

- small fresh fava beans
- oil
- garlic, fried
- toasted hazelnuts
- toasted bread
- water
- vinegar
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Remove only the ends of the fava beans.
2. Cook the beans first, then drain them.
3. Place them in an earthenware dish and add oil with fried garlic.
4. Make a sauce with toasted hazelnuts, a little toasted bread soaked in water and vinegar, pepper, and eggs proportional to the sauce.
5. Dilute the sauce with seasoned water and pour it over the beans.
6. Let them boil over gentle heat.
