## Ingredients

- Coffee
- Water

## Directions

1. Use ordinary filters such as those employed in the kitchens of private homes in Paris and all of France.
2. These filters are based on the principle of the Dutch method.
3. Use a tin coffeepot with two bodies.
4. When the coffee is made in such a vessel, transfer it immediately to another vessel of porcelain or glazed earthenware if you wish to drink it in good conditions, as coffee blackens much in tin and takes on a disagreeable acrid flavor.
