## Ingredients

- Cooked cod
- Oil
- Garlic
- Sweet or hot paprika
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Once cooked and drained, place the cod on plates.
2. Fry oil and garlic, add sweet or hot paprika, and as much vinegar as oil has been used.
3. Add this sauce to the cod when serving.
