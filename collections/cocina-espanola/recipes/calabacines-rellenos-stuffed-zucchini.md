## Ingredients

- Zucchini
- Picadillo (minced meat mixture)
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Cut about a finger's length from each end of the zucchini
2. Pierce lengthwise with a reed, extracting the interior part
3. Fill the hollowed interior with picadillo, the same mixture generally used for stuffed onions
4. Cook the zucchini
5. Incorporate a small sauce made with almonds
