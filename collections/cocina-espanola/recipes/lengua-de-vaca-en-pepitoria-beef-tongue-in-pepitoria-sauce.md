## Ingredients

- cooked beef tongue
- beef broth, one cup
- white wine, one quarter liter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, finely chopped
- two egg yolks
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked tongue into strips after peeling it.
2. Simmer very gently in a saucepan with one cup of broth and one quarter liter of white wine.
3. Season moderately with salt and pepper.
4. When the broth has reduced by half, arrange the slices symmetrically on a serving dish.
5. Away from heat, incorporate into the sauce finely chopped parsley and two egg yolks diluted in a little broth with a dash of vinegar.
6. Pour the thickened sauce over the tongue at the moment of serving.
