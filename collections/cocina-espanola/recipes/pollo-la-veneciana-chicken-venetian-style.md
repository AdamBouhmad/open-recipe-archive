## Ingredients

- One chicken, cleaned and singed
- Butter
- White wine
- Broth
- A bunch of parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter mixed with flour
- Grated cheese

## Directions

1. Open the chicken down the back from the neck to the tail and flatten it with a mallet
2. Cook it in butter with white wine and broth, adding a bunch of parsley, salt and pepper over low heat
3. When cooked, strain and reduce the broth, adding butter mixed with flour
4. Place the chicken on a plate that can withstand fire, and pour the sauce over it
5. Cover the chicken and sauce with grated cheese and place over moderate heat in a small oven
6. Serve when it has taken color
