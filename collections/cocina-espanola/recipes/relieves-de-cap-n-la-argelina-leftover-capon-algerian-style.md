## Ingredients

- leftover pieces of capon, hen, or chicken, well cut
- clean broth
- roasting juices
- parsley
- verjuice or strong vinegar with tarragon

## Directions

1. Cook the well-cut leftover poultry pieces for a quarter of an hour in clean broth reinforced with roasting juices.
2. When the sauce has reduced by half, incorporate parsley that has been trimmed and blanched in boiling water.
3. Give everything a couple of boils.
4. At the moment of serving, add a tablespoon of verjuice if available, or strong tarragon vinegar if not.
