## Ingredients

- six well-prepared veal chops
- 125 grams butter or lard
- salt and pepper
- bread croutons
- meat juice
- broth

## Directions

1. Sauté six well-prepared chops in a pan, without breading them, in 125 grams of butter or lard.
2. Let them take color over high heat, turning them continually, and finish cooking with only the fat and the proper seasoning.
3. Serve these chops over bread croutons fried in lard, and when placing them in the dish as a base for the chops, pour over them the meat juice, extended with a little broth.
