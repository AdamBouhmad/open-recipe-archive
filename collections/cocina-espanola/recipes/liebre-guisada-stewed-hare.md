## Ingredients

- hare
- white wine
- butter or bacon
- spices
- two cloves garlic, crushed
- salt
- onion, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the hare into pieces and soak in white wine.
2. Fry with butter or bacon.
3. Transfer to a pot with spices, two crushed cloves of garlic, and salt.
4. In the remaining fat, fry finely chopped onion and add to the pot with the wine in which the hare soaked.
5. Cook for at least two hours.
