## Ingredients

- snails
- water
- salt
- ash
- beef butter
- parsley
- garlic head
- cloves
- thyme
- laurel
- flour
- egg yolks
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Wash and prepare the snails as previously described
2. Place them in a pot of boiling water with a little salt and a bundle of ash tied well
3. After a quarter of an hour remove them and their shells come off easily
4. Wash in cold water and cook again
5. Drain and place in a saucepan with beef butter, parsley, one head of garlic, two cloves, thyme, laurel, and one tablespoon of flour
6. Mix everything and thicken, without cooking, with egg yolks
7. When serving, add a little lemon juice
