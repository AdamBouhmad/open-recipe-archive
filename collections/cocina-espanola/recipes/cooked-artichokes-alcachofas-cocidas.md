## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut off the tips and trim the leaves of the artichokes
2. Boil in plenty of water with plenty of salt, as with all green vegetables
3. When the liquid boils vigorously, cook until pulling a leaf from an artichoke causes it to separate immediately
4. Place in a colander on a plate
5. When they can be squeezed slightly with the fingers without burning, drain them well so they release the water
6. If they are to be eaten only cooked, place them in the oven with the door open or on the edge of the stove to dry slightly and not cool
