## Ingredients

- Hake
- Cold water
- Onion
- Garlic
- Parsley
- All spices
- Good tarragon vinegar
- Six hard-boiled egg yolks
- Hard-boiled egg whites
- Fried hot oil

## Directions

1. Cook the hake whole in cold water over high heat. At the first boil, remove the pot from the fire as the fish is cooked
2. Work together for a quarter hour a mixture of chopped onion, garlic, parsley, and all spices with good tarragon vinegar
3. Add six hard-boiled egg yolks and mash and mix with the previous mixture
4. Add the hard-boiled egg whites cut into small pieces
5. At the moment of serving, place the hake in a deep platter. Pour over it the hot fried oil in quantity to complete the prepared seasoning, which is poured over all
