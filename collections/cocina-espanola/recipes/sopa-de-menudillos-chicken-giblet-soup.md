## Ingredients

- chicken giblets
- lard
- rice or toasted bread soup

## Directions

1. Mince very finely and clean perfectly the giblets of chicken.
2. Sauté them in a pan with lard and incorporate them into rice soup or toasted bread soup when it is already in the tureen.
