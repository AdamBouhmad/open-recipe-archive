## Ingredients

- usable pieces of conger eel
- good vinegar
- complete seasoning of all herbs
- onions
- garlic
- oil

## Directions

1. Place the usable pieces of conger eel in a saucepan and cover well with good vinegar.
2. Season with a complete seasoning of all herbs, onions, garlic, and other seasonings.
3. Cook this for ten minutes.
4. When the dish is cold, place it with its broth in a narrow-necked pot or vessel.
5. Cover the surface of the liquid with oil.
6. Prepared this way, the conger eel will last many months.
7. Serve the pickled conger eel as an appetizer on a platter with a garnish of chopped parsley.
8. Eat it with very hot fried oil presented in a sauce boat.
