## Ingredients

- veal hands
- quenelles
- salt and pepper to taste
- eggs
- breadcrumbs
- oil for frying
- spicy sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal hands with quenelle filling as in the previous recipe.
2. Do not tie with twine.
3. Coat the hands in beaten egg, then breadcrumbs, then egg again.
4. Fry well until golden.
5. Serve with spicy sauce or tomato sauce poured over top.
