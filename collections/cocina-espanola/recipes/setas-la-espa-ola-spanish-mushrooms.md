## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter or oil
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- pine nut or almond sauce
- fried flour

## Directions

1. Remove the stems, wash thoroughly in plenty of water, and dry well.
2. Sauté in butter or oil.
3. Add salt, pepper, and a little broth.
4. Once well cooked, add pine nut or almond sauce, thickened with fried flour.
