## Ingredients

- lobster
- butter from Isigny
- four large onions the size of eggs
- dry white wine
- Spanish sauce
- tomato puree
- Cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the lobster in the natural way, that is, in cold water in which it bathes well, remove it at the first boil and let it cool to completely remove the shell.
2. Cut the tail into large pieces and place them in a deep serving dish that can go to the fire forming a ring.
3. With the meat from the legs and everything found in the interior of the head make a very fine chopped mixture, and with it fill the hollow of the ring.
4. Sauté well in a saucepan with butter from Isigny four large onions the size of eggs, chop them very finely, and after three minutes, the sautéed mixture, moisten with dry white wine and let cook half an hour.
5. Then add Spanish sauce and tomato puree in equal parts, and a hint of Cayenne pepper.
6. Reduce the sauce by two-thirds over vivid fire, and strain through fine sieve, pouring it into the serving dish over the lobster.
7. Ten minutes before serving this dish, prepared as has been said, cook in the oven, over gentle fire for a quarter of an hour.
