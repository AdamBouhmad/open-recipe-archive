## Ingredients

- one pound meat
- half onion
- parsley
- two garlic cloves
- pepper
- half teaspoon paprika
- one tomato or tomato preserve
- two bay leaves
- one cup oil
- salt
- potatoes
- turnips
- carrots
- artichokes
- peas
- squash
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. For each pound of meat put in a pot half onion chopped, a little parsley, two mashed garlic cloves, a dust of pepper, half teaspoon of paprika, one tomato or tomato preserve, two bay leaves, one cup of oil and corresponding salt
2. Put everything together on low heat, stirring often because it can stick easily
3. When browned add enough water to cook
4. When half cooked potatoes, turnips, carrots can be added, and in their time artichokes, peas, squash and mushrooms
5. When well cooked the dish can be served
