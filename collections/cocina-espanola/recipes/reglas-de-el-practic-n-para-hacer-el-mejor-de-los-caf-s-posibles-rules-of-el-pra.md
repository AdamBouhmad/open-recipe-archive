## Ingredients

- Coffee beans
- Felt bag
- Blanket

## Directions

1. Toast it uniformly and remove it from the roaster a little before, when you see that it still lacks a little to be at its point.
2. Put it in a little felt bag and wrap this, covering it, with a blanket so that it sweats and thus completes its true roasting.
3. Grind the precise amount at the moment of making it.
4. Use a vessel of copper or iron tin-plated.
