## Ingredients

- fried bread
- garlic
- cumin
- one clove
- some strands of saffron
- broth
- breadcrumb for thickening

## Directions

1. Grind in the mortar fried bread, garlic and cumin, one clove, and some strands of saffron
2. Dissolve in broth, thicken with breadcrumb and give it a few boils
