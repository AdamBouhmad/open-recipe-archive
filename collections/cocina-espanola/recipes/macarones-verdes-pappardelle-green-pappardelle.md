## Ingredients

- 1 bunch spinach
- salt
- water
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 pound flour
- 2 ounces beef fat
- broth
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Cook a bunch of spinach with water and salt, and once fully cooked, pound it.
2. Mix this paste with a dough composed of four eggs, half a pound of flour, and two ounces of beef fat.
3. Mix everything well with a rolling pin, extending it on a board.
4. Cut into strips similar to noodles, leaving them to dry in the sun. This appears to be indispensable, as oven heat is not suitable.
5. Once well dried, cook them in meat broth.
6. Serve sprinkled with Parmesan cheese.
