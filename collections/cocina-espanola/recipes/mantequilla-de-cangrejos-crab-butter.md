## Ingredients

- Two dozen crabs
- One pound beef lard

## Directions

1. Dry the crabs in the oven
2. Pound them in a mortar until they are converted to powder
3. Knead the powder with the beef lard and place everything over low heat so that the lard liquefies
4. Pass and squeeze by compression through a metal sieve placed over a vessel filled with cold water
5. As the crab butter passes through the strainer and falls into the water, it will solidify
6. Remove the solidified butter from the water and shape it, while cleaning and drying it with a white cloth, repeating as many times as necessary to remove all moisture
