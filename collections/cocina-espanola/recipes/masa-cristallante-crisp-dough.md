## Ingredients

- Flour
- Cow butter, three-quarters pound per pound of flour
- Water
- Two egg whites, beaten until snowy
- Powdered sugar, one quarter-pound
- A pinch of gum arabic, sifted

## Directions

1. Place the flour on the table and make a hole in the center
2. Put the butter in small pieces into the hole at a ratio of three-quarters pound per pound of flour
3. Add some water and two egg whites beaten until they reach the consistency of snow with powdered sugar and a pinch of sifted gum arabic
4. Mix everything together
5. Knead for half an hour
