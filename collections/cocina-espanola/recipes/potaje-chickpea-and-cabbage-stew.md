## Ingredients

- well-burned oil
- onion scraps
- modest piece of ham, cut into small pieces
- one tablespoon of flour
- 350 grams of chickpeas
- one cabbage
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take a cooking pot and add the well-burned oil, onion scraps, and ham pieces cut small
2. Put all this on the fire to fry, adding immediately one tablespoon of flour
3. Have previously boiled and cooked 350 grams of chickpeas and one cabbage
4. Combine the chickpeas and cabbage with their cooking water to the pot ingredients
5. Season with pepper and salt
6. Cook for half an hour over low heat
