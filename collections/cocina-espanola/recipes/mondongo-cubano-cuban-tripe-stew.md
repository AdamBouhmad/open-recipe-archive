## Ingredients

- mondongo (offal)
- hot water
- lemon juice
- salt
- pork or veal foot
- dried corn
- sweet potato
- fresh corn
- lemon juice
- garlic heads
- ripe plantains
- annatto (bija)
- bread soaked in saffron
- saffron
- cumin
- cilantro

## Directions

1. Clean the mondongo well in hot water and lemon juice, then cut into small pieces.
2. Place in a pot with water and salt, bring to a boil, and add a pork or veal foot with some dried corn.
3. When the meat is tender, add more water, sweet potato, fresh corn, juice of half a lemon, two whole garlic heads, and ripe plantains cut in half.
4. Place a small amount of annatto in a cloth bag and introduce into the pot; remove the bag when it first boils, and continue cooking everything until it looks all white.
5. In a mortar, mix bread soaked in saffron, three garlic cloves, cumin, and cilantro with some of the mondongo broth.
6. Add this mixture to the stew and let it thicken slightly.
