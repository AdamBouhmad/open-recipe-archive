## Ingredients

- green beans, finest variety
- salt
- fresh water
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the green beans of the finest variety, cut them lengthwise and place them to cook for ten minutes with plenty of salt in boiling water at a rolling boil.
2. Remove them in a colander and place them in a bowl with fresh water, from where they pass to another bowl, also with water, and there they lose the excess salt.
3. Cold and drained, at the moment of eating them season with oil, vinegar and a pinch of pepper, and it results in a luxury salad if one knows how to garnish it.
