## Ingredients

- cleaned sole fillets
- fine herbs
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- fine spices

## Directions

1. Cut sole fillets along the back from already emptied and cleaned fish
2. Cover with fine herbs chopped and reheated in butter
3. Place the dark side down on a plate in which fresh butter has been spread
4. Pour melted butter over them as well
5. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs, salt and fine spices
6. Place over high heat for two minutes to set and serve
