## Ingredients

- a handful of pine nuts, well washed and shelled
- some cloves of garlic
- cumin
- one cooked egg yolk
- white broth or water

## Directions

1. Take a handful of pine nuts well washed and shelled, put them in the mortar with some cloves of garlic, cumin and one cooked egg yolk
2. Grind, and diluted in white broth or water, give it a boil and it is ready to serve
