## Ingredients

- 250 grams flour
- one quarter liter of warm water
- 60 grams very fresh butter

## Directions

1. Place 250 grams of flour in a deep plate and gradually add one quarter liter of warm water, enough so that sixty grams of very fresh butter soften and melt.
2. The key to this batter is in how you work it so that it is uniformly bound together and has a consistency like that of well-made chocolate.
3. If not all of the warm water is necessary to reach this state, part of the butter may remain on the surface of the unused water, and in this case it should be collected with a spoon.
