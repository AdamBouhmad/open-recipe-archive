## Ingredients

- bread crumb
- oil
- four to six garlic cloves
- paprika
- broth from the pot or water
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. In an earthenware pot, sauté bread crumb in oil.
2. Add four to six garlic cloves and plenty of paprika.
3. Before it blackens, moisten everything with broth from the pot or with water.
4. Crush a small handful of pine nuts and add them to the sauce at the same time as the paprika.
5. Serve when ready.
