## Ingredients

- Duck, cleaned and prepared
- Stuffing of choice (same as for fattened pullets or turkey)

## Directions

1. Stuff the duck the same way as a fattened pullet or turkey, using the same stuffing or whichever stuffing is most agreeable to the palate or easiest to prepare.
2. Spit the duck on the roasting spit and place it in a shallow pan to which two supports have been added.
3. Roast in a strong oven without needing to touch it during cooking.
4. Use more time than if roasting a duck naturally.
