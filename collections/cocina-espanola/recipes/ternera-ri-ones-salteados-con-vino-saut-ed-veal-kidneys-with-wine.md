## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- flour
- lard or butter
- broth
- wine, such as white wine, red wine, Sherry, Bordeaux, or Champagne
- parsley
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare as for the previous recipe but use only half the broth
2. Add two glasses of white wine, red wine, Sherry, Bordeaux, Champagne, or similar wine
3. Cook and serve as directed in the previous recipe
