## Ingredients

- Young carrots
- Pork lard
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare and cut carrots as in the previous formula
2. Sauté and fry the young carrots in a spacious pan with pork lard, sprinkling with salt, but there should be only enough fat in the pan to season the dish, accounting for what it absorbs
