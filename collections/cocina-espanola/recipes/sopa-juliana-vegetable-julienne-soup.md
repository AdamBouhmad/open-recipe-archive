## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Turnips
- Leeks
- Potatoes
- Onions
- Celery
- Butter from cows
- Lettuce leaves
- Parsley
- Chervil
- Peas, fresh (if season permits)
- Tender beans (if season permits)
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut carrots, turnips, leeks, potatoes, onions, and celery into thin threads
2. Sauté them in cow butter so they take on color
3. Add finely chopped lettuce leaves, parsley, and chervil
4. If the season permits, augment with a handful of fresh peas and another of tender beans
5. When the vegetables are slightly cooked, add the necessary water and season
6. Off the heat, incorporate a piece of fresh cow butter
