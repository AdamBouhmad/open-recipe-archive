## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter
- lemon juice
- salt
- spices
- water
- bread, for making crusts
- garlic, optional

## Directions

1. Sauté in butter with lemon juice, salt, spices, and one spoonful of water.
2. After sautéing and cooking, place over fried bread crusts that have been already arranged on the serving dish.
3. If desired, add one garlic clove while cooking, which should be removed before placing the mushrooms on the bread crusts.
