## Ingredients

- water
- oil
- vegetables for concentrated broth
- fish

## Directions

1. Put water and oil in a pot and add the indicated vegetables for concentrated broth
2. Cut the fish into pieces and place in the pot
3. Salt and moisten with vigil broth if available, or otherwise with water
4. Sprinkle again with very good oil
5. Cook for a quarter hour
6. Remove the fish and serve each type separately with one of the most appropriate condiments
7. Strain the broth and soak and soak the fried croutons or slices of bread
8. Serve
9. A little white wine or brandy may be added if desired
