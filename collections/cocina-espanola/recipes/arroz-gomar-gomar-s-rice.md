## Ingredients

- One kilogram of the best rice
- One pound of salmon
- One pound of sole
- Half pound of eels
- Meat from two lobsters
- Breasts of six capons
- Twenty wings of quails
- One pound of ham
- Asparagus
- Artichokes
- Mushrooms
- Truffles
- Peas
- Tomatoes
- Peppers
- Cumin
- Pine nuts
- Three dozen crabs
- Small fillets of anchovies
- Oil
- Small onion
- Parsley
- Chervil
- Oregano
- Fennel
- Garlic
- Young garlic
- Saffron

## Directions

1. Prepare all the ingredients as listed
2. Cook together to create the Gomar rice dish
