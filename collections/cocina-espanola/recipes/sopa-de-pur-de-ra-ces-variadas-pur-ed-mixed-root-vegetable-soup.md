## Ingredients

- 2 onions
- 8 carrots
- 2 turnips
- 2 parsnips
- boiling water
- cow butter
- broth

## Directions

1. Peel, wash, and cut 2 onions, 8 carrots, 2 turnips, and 2 parsnips
2. Place in boiling water for a quarter hour over strong fire
3. Cool and dry, placing them in a sieve
4. Brown with cow butter, stirring from time to time so they do not stick to the bottom of the saucepan
5. When ready, moisten with broth, letting them boil slowly beside the fire
6. After cooking, pass as the previous ones
7. Heat, season, and serve
