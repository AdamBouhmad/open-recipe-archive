## Ingredients

- butter from beef
- white sauce
- brown sauce
- beef broth
- burned sugar or caramel
- egg yolk
- water

## Directions

1. For meatless days, prepare with beef butter, a white sauce reinforced with a pronounced brown sauce, which is bound at the moment of serving with egg yolk diluted in water.
2. For meat meals, to the white sauce add reduced beef broth, which is colored with burned sugar or caramel, as one colors broth.
