## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- fine Valencia oil
- parsley, finely chopped
- garlic, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- silver spoon

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the mushrooms.
2. Fry in fine Valencia oil.
3. Once fried, sauté with finely chopped parsley and garlic, all very fine.
4. Season with salt and some excess pepper, clove, and nutmeg.
5. During the operation, stir and handle the mushrooms with a silver spoon, real silver.
6. If the spoon darkens or becomes covered with a dark veil that tarnishes it, discard the mushrooms.
7. If the spoon keeps its color, serve the mushrooms immediately from the pan to the table. This is a dish that should be eaten very hot.
