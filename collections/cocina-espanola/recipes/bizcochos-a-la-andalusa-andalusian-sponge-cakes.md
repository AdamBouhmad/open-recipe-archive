## Ingredients

- two egg whites
- two ounces of pulverized flour
- one and a half ounces of starch in powder

## Directions

1. Beat two egg whites until white as snow
2. Set aside the yolks
3. To the yolks add two ounces of pulverized flour and one and a half ounces of starch in powder
4. Mix everything after beating very well
5. Place in a paper box and put in the oven
