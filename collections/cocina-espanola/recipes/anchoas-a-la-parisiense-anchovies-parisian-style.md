## Ingredients

- Anchovies without spines
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Mustard
- Parsley
- Garlic

## Directions

1. Arrange the anchovies without spines on a plate.
2. Arrange them into decorative figures with hard-boiled egg yolks and whites, cut and combined by color.
3. Make a sauce with two hard-boiled eggs, oil, vinegar, mustard, finely chopped parsley, and finely chopped garlic.
4. Cover the contents of the serving dish with this sauce and serve at the table as a breakfast dish.
