## Ingredients

- veal sweetbreads
- white roux-based sauce
- fine herbs
- small onions
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- mushrooms
- artichoke hearts
- truffles
- clean broth
- two or three egg yolks
- verjuice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Blanch the veal sweetbreads in boiling water
2. Immediately submerge them in cold water (better if frozen)
3. Prepare a white roux-based sauce and add chopped fine herbs, small onions, salt, pepper and a clove
4. Cook mushrooms, artichoke hearts cut in quarters, and truffles in the sauce
5. Moisten with a ladle of clean broth
6. Twenty minutes before the vegetables finish cooking, add the sweetbreads
7. Thicken the sauce with two or three egg yolks dissolved in cold water and a spoonful of verjuice or vinegar
8. Serve immediately
