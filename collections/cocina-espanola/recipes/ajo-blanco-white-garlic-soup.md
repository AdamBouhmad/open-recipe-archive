## Ingredients

- blanched almonds
- one clove of garlic
- oil
- vinegar
- water
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Mash blanched almonds with one clove of garlic, a little oil and the corresponding vinegar
2. Crush this mixture very well until it becomes like a somewhat watery ointment
3. Dissolve it well in a tureen or serving dish where the bread has been crumbled with sufficient water and bread
