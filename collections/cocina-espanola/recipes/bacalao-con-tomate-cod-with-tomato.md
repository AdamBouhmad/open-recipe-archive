## Ingredients

- Cod
- Onion
- Tomatoes
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Cut into pieces and soak in water to desalt, changing the water from time to time
2. Cook and then place on a board to drain
3. Meanwhile fry onion and tomatoes in abundance
4. Arrange the cod pieces in a suitable pot, putting a layer of tomato and onion on top of the pieces
5. Add a little parsley and some crushed garlic
6. When the pot is full, add water until everything is covered
7. Bring to a boil a couple of times and season with salt if necessary
8. Serve
