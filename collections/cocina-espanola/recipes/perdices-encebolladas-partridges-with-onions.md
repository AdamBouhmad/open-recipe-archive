## Ingredients

- partridges
- pork lard
- one pound onions
- bay leaves
- thyme
- parsley
- chervil
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the birds
2. Put pork lard in a pan and sauté one pound of finely chopped onions well with good seasoning, two bay leaves, thyme, parsley and chervil if available
3. Once the onion is softened, add broth or water until it forms a paste
4. Scald the partridges over high heat with a little lard in a pan, then add them to the onion mixture
5. Cook at low heat for about one hour if the partridges are not very tough
6. Serve them on a platter, covering them with the onion paste that results from this stew
