## Ingredients

- pickled cucumbers
- flour
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Chop the pickled cucumbers finely and put in a saucepan with a pinch of flour, butter, salt and pepper.
2. Add the broth, keep over heat until it thickens, and serve.
