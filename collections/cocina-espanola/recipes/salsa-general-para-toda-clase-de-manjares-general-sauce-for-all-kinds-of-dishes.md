## Ingredients

- broth
- vinegar
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- orange peel
- shallots
- bay leaf
- thyme
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. In a saucepan place one measure of broth, another of vinegar, and two of white wine.
2. Add salt and pepper, one clove, a piece of orange peel, six crushed shallots, bay leaf, thyme, lemon juice.
3. Cover well and place in infusion for twelve hours over coals.
4. Strain through cheesecloth, sieve or fine strainer.
5. Bottle and use as needed.
6. It keeps for a long time and is used for all kinds of meats, poultry, fish and vegetables.
