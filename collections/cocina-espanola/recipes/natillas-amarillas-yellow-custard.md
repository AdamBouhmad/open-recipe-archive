## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- half pound of powdered sugar
- one quarter liter of milk
- lady fingers
- powdered sugar for dusting
- cinnamon
- confectionery or similar toppings

## Directions

1. Beat eight egg yolks in a saucepan; add half a pound of powdered sugar and beat again; then pour in a quarter liter of milk slowly, without ceasing to beat it so that it binds well.
2. Put everything over low heat, without ceasing to beat it slowly, and when it comes to a boil remove it, pouring it into the platter in which it is to be served, placing some lady fingers on top, and dusted with very fine powdered sugar and a little cinnamon, confectionery or similar items.
