## Ingredients

- Lean pork meat
- lard
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- ground pimiento

## Directions

1. Cut the lean pork meat into solid pieces about the size of chocolate ounces
2. In a deep frying pan with very little pork lard, brown and sear the pieces over a lively fire
3. Season with salt, abundant pepper, and four cloves of garlic, finely minced
4. Continue sautéing the meat so it cooks evenly
5. Dust with ground pimiento, and to prevent it from blackening, moisten the meat first with a small ladleful of broth and a few drops of lemon or vinegar
6. Continue sautéing for about half an hour
7. Then cover the pan and set it aside near the fire so the meat steams and puffs up
