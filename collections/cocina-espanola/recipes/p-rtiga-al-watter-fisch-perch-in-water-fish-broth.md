## Ingredients

- parsley roots with stalks and leaves
- parsnips
- onions
- red pepper
- salt
- perch, cleaned inside and out and well-scaled

## Directions

1. Boil in very salted water over high heat three or four fat parsley roots with their stalks and leaves, along with parsnips cut in pieces, sliced onions, and a red pepper for ten minutes.
2. Remove the parsnips, onions, and pepper from the broth; leave the parsley.
3. Without stopping the boiling, add the cleaned and well-scaled perch.
4. When cooked, remove from the pot and let drain and cool.
5. Serve cold without sauce, accompanied by thin, equal slices of rye bread spread with butter on alternating sides, stacked together in pairs.
