## Ingredients

- rabbit
- beef lard
- mushrooms
- parsley
- bay leaf
- 1 tablespoon flour
- wine and broth in equal parts
- salt
- pepper
- rabbit liver

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit meat into pieces and brown in beef lard with mushrooms, parsley, and chopped bay leaf.
2. Add a tablespoon of flour, wine and broth in equal measure.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Cook the liver separately and when serving, add it to the sauce and mix together.
