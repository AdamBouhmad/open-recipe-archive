## Ingredients

- Lard
- One onion
- Salt and pepper
- Broth or water
- Flour
- Potatoes cut into angular chunks the size of walnuts

## Directions

1. In lard, sauté a trimmed onion until it takes on color.
2. Season with salt and pepper and moisten with broth or water after sprinkling with a little flour.
3. Add the potatoes cut into angular chunks the size of walnuts and cook over very low heat.
