## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- twine
- flour
- white linen cloth
- turnips
- salted boiling water
- cow butter
- capers
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take a good leg and without skinning it, break the shank to fold it over the meat and secure it with many turns of twine.
2. Dust the leg completely with flour.
3. Wrap it in white linen cloth as if covering it, leaving no part uncovered.
4. Place in a pot and braise with twenty turnips cut into slices and enough hot salted water to cover the meat.
5. Cook slowly for two hours.
6. Remove the turnips and the leg with its juice.
7. Finish cooking the turnips separately in water and pass them through a fine strainer.
8. When the turnip purée has good consistency, add a couple of spoonfuls of thick cream, form into a pyramid, and season with salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg.
9. Remove the wrapping from the leg.
10. Melt cow butter and mix with capers.
11. Serve the meat with its garnish of turnip purée, over which pour the juice.
