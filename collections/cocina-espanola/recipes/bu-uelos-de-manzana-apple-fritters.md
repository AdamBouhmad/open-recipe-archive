## Ingredients

- four large, healthy apples
- brandy or cognac
- 30 grams of powdered sugar
- flour
- egg whites
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. Select four large healthy apples that should yield two dozen fritters
2. Core the apples using an apple corer about two centimeters wide to remove the core and seeds
3. Peel the apples and cut them crosswise into slices about half a finger thick
4. Place the slices in a dish with brandy or cognac and 30 grams of powdered sugar, mixing carefully to avoid breaking the slices
5. Half an hour before serving, drain the slices on a clean kitchen cloth and dry them well, as this prevents the batter from adhering properly to the apple
6. Prepare a special batter made with flour, brandy or cognac, and water
7. Beat egg whites until they resemble meringue and mix with the batter
8. Take each apple slice with a spoon, wrap it in the batter being careful not to break it, and fry in very hot oil
9. When removed from oil, sprinkle with sugar
10. Whole apples may also be used, but they must be briefly boiled in water before soaking in brandy
