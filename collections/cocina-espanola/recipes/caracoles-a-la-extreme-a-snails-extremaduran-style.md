## Ingredients

- snails
- onion
- oil
- salt
- water
- vegetables
- bread
- spices
- fennel
- thyme
- garlic
- flour
- sour juice

## Directions

1. Wash the snails in four to six waters
2. Place them in a wide vessel with water and heat over fire until they emerge from their shells and die with the body completely outside
3. Remove and wash them very well with salt and more waters
4. Cook them to remove the verdín
5. Fry onion with oil and mix with the snails, sautéing them well and adding salt and water
6. Cook for three to four hours
7. Mince vegetables and pound them with a little bread, seasoning with spices, fennel, and thyme
8. Dilute the mixture with the broth from the snails; if the broth is clear, add fried flour
9. Add garlic and sour juice at the time of serving
