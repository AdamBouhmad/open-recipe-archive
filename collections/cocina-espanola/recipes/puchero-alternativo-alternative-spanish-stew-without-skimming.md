## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- water
- beef
- salt

## Directions

1. Place chickpeas alone in boiling water with salt and let them boil for half an hour without removing the white foam that forms, which will gradually disappear on its own.
2. After half an hour, add the meat.
3. This method eliminates the need for skimming and produces a clean-looking stew.
