## Ingredients

- partridges
- sardines
- tomatoes
- lemon
- pepper
- parsley

## Directions

1. Pluck, clean and brush the partridges
2. Place one well-cleaned, beheaded sardine inside each partridge
3. Roast them in a casserole with tomatoes or lemon juice, a little pepper and parsley
4. When done, remove the sardines
