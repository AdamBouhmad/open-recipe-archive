## Ingredients

- 1 Lombard or white headed cabbage
- ground sausage meat
- roasted chestnuts, chopped
- fine herbs bouquet
- salt and pepper
- kitchen twine
- bacon slice
- carrots
- onions
- whole cloves
- broth from the pot
- 115 grams fresh cow butter
- 1 tablespoon potato starch

## Directions

1. Scald a Lombard or white headed cabbage in boiling water. Open the leaves slightly without deforming the vegetable and carefully remove the heart.
2. Prepare a filling of ground sausage meat with an equal quantity of roasted and chopped chestnuts, a bouquet of fine herbs, and good seasoning of salt and pepper. Place this filling in the hollow left by the heart.
3. Tie the cabbage with kitchen twine and cover the filling with a slice of bacon.
4. Place the cabbage in a braising pot or casserole in which there is a good wide slice of bacon. Around it place carrots and onions studded with whole cloves.
5. Cover completely with broth from the pot and cook over gentle heat. Place the cabbage on a serving platter.
6. Strain the sauce and reduce it by half, adding 115 grams of fresh cow butter and 1 tablespoon of potato starch.
7. Once the sauce is bound, pour it over the cabbage.
