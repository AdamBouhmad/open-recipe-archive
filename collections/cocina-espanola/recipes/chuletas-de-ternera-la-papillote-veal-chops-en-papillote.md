## Ingredients

- veal chops
- filling for chops
- white paper, greased and cut

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops as for the previous formula, or better yet, spread both sides with a chop filling that has already been explained how to make.
2. Then wrap them in white greased and cut paper, as shown in the drawing, and grill them on the grill.
3. They can also be roasted on a gentle fire in a braising pan or on a tray in the oven.
4. Serve these chops with their paper wrapping, which can be well browned but not burned.
