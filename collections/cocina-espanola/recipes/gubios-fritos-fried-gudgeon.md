## Ingredients

- gudgeon fish
- flour
- oil

## Directions

1. Scale the gudgeons and gut them
2. Clean and dry with a fine cloth without washing
3. Place in a napkin and sprinkle flour over them
4. Gather the four corners of the napkin as if making a bundle and shake the fish to coat thoroughly with flour
5. Let rest in the napkin for half an hour until the fish's moisture has made the flour adhere well
6. Flour again with fresh flour
7. In a large pan holding four pounds of oil, fry about thirty gudgeons the size and thickness of a finger over high heat to perfection
8. Do not fry more quantity at once in the same oil, nor fry thirty gudgeons in less fat, as this will spoil the operation
9. When well fried, the gudgeons are eaten in their entirety including head, bones, and flesh
