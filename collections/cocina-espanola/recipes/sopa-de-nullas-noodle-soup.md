## Ingredients

- 250 grams flour de flor (fine flour)
- salt
- a pinch of pepper
- six egg yolks
- water, as needed
- broth from puchero

## Directions

1. Combine the fine flour with salt and a pinch of pepper. Incorporate the necessary water to give the dough good consistency, adjusting so that the flour can be absorbed by the yolks without adding water.
2. Work and knead the dough, then extend it with a rolling pin into thin sheets.
3. Cut the sheets into long strips about half a centimeter wide or even thinner, or shape the scraps as desired.
4. Let the nullas rest for a couple of hours exposed to air before using.
5. Add them to the broth as described for noodles, and they must cook for at least half an hour.
6. This is a very nutritious soup.
