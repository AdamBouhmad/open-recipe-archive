## Ingredients

- mondongo (offal)
- hot water
- lemon juice
- salt
- dried corn
- veal or pork foot
- pumpkin
- yuca
- malanga
- sweet potato
- plantains
- saffron threads
- raisins
- toasted almonds
- capers with vinegar
- herbs

## Directions

1. Clean the mondongo with hot water and lemon juice, cut into small pieces, and place in a casserole with water, salt, and dried corn.
2. Add a veal or pork foot for substance and flavor, and bring to a boil.
3. When the meat is tender, add finely chopped pumpkin, yuca, malanga, sweet potato, and plantains cut in half.
4. When everything is well cooked, add saffron threads, raisins, toasted almonds, two spoonfuls of capers with their vinegar, and herbs.
5. Let cook for about an hour, remove from heat, let rest a bit, and serve hot.
