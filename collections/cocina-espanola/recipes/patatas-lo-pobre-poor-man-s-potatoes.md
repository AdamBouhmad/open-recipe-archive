## Ingredients

- Potatoes, cut into small angular chunks
- Parsley powder
- Garlic cloves, finely minced
- Salt and pepper
- Water

## Directions

1. Fry the potatoes cut into small angular chunks in a pan, and remove them when cooked but before they take on color.
2. Remove all the fat from the pan and return the potatoes to it.
3. Season with salt and pepper and mash them with a fork in the same pan over heat.
4. Add powdered parsley and finely minced garlic cloves; stir and manipulate the dish with the fork.
5. Add a few drops of water so it softens at the moment of serving.
