## Ingredients

- 1 entrecote (ribeye steak)
- refined oil
- parsley, chopped
- shallots, chopped
- salt and pepper
- beef butter

## Directions

1. Heat the grill or pan lightly greased, with the meat rubbed with fat on all sides and very high heat.
2. Cook the entrecote on the grill or in the pan, rubbing the meat with refined oil.
3. On the second turn of the entrecote, sprinkle over it a spoonful of chopped parsley and shallots, dusted with salt and pepper.
4. Carefully transfer the meat with a spatula to a serving dish with about a walnut of beef butter.
5. Serve as the juices from the meat mingle with the melted butter.
