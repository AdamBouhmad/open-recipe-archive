## Ingredients

- lamb testicles
- egg
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- pork lard
- lemon
- croutons

## Directions

1. Clean the testicles well and remove filaments and bloody threads
2. Cut in half, blanch in boiling water until white, then immediately submerge in very cold water
3. Remove and cut into pieces like orange sections
4. Dip in beaten egg seasoned with salt and pepper, coat with breadcrumbs, dip again in egg
5. Fry in pork lard
6. Serve in a pyramid on a well-pressed napkin on a platter with croutons and lemon quarters for guests to squeeze juice over them as they eat
