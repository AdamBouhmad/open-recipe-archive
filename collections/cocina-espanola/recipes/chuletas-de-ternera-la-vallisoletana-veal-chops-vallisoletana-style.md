## Ingredients

- two veal chops together, each comprising two ribs
- meat from small meatballs for stuffing
- bone from one of the chops

## Directions

1. Cut two chops together, each comprising two ribs, and remove the bone from one of them; flatten them well and perfect their shape.
2. Open the meat across, pounding both parts with the back of a knife, but without separating it from the bone.
3. Fill the hollow in the middle with a finely chopped mixture of meatball meat, and join the two parts of the chop meat together again.
4. Roast on the grill until well done.
5. Cover the bottom of a serving dish with the same chopped mixture.
6. Place the chops on top, sauce them with their own juice, and keep them in a hot oven for four minutes, then serve.
