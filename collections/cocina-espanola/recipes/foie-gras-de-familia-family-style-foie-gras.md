## Ingredients

- one hand of veal
- one pound pork liver
- one quarter pound bacon
- one pound onions
- four garlic cloves
- one veal marrow bone, well filled with marrow
- one liter water
- half liter white wine
- all spices: parsley, laurel, mint, thyme and rosemary
- leftover roasted veal
- one quarter pound lard
- two tablespoons flour
- broth from cooking

## Directions

1. Cook for six consecutive hours over low heat in one liter of water and half liter of white wine: one hand of veal, one pound of pork liver, one quarter pound of bacon, one pound of onions, four garlic cloves and one veal marrow bone, well filled with marrow.
2. Season with all spices: parsley, laurel, mint, thyme and rosemary.
3. After six hours, having incorporated the leftover roasted veal one hour before, shred everything, and aided by its own broth, pass through a sieve to form a purée.
4. In a saucepan, melt one quarter pound of lard, and in it brown two tablespoons of flour.
5. Thin and bind with the broth from cooking.
