## Ingredients

- tripe or offal
- hot water
- vinegar or lemon juice
- salt
- veal hand or bone
- ham, cut into pieces
- Extremaduran chorizo
- Castilian chickpeas
- lemon juice
- garlic heads
- paprika
- bread soaked in saffron
- saffron
- cumin
- cilantro

## Directions

1. Wash the tripe or offal in hot water, then wash again with good vinegar or lemon juice.
2. Cut into small pieces and place in a pot with water, salt, veal hand or bone, ham pieces, Extremaduran chorizo, Castilian chickpeas, juice of half a lemon, and whole garlic heads with paprika.
3. In a mortar, mix bread soaked in saffron, three garlic cloves, cumin, and cilantro with some of the offal broth.
4. Add this mixture to the stew and let it thicken slightly.
5. Serve always very hot.
6. If using tripe, turn inside out before cooking.
