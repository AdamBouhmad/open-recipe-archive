## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Butter or oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cut, peel and wash the spinach
2. Sauté it with butter or oil, salt and pepper
3. Add a piece of butter for the sauce
4. Serve with fried bread crusts in the soup bowl
