## Ingredients

- Empanada dough
- Flour
- Financière sauce
- Five rooster crests
- Five crayfish
- Five mushrooms
- One beautiful truffle
- Clear glaze

## Directions

1. Make a mold of empanada dough
2. Fill it with flour, cover it and flute the edge
3. When the dough is cooked, remove the lid, leaving a two-centimeter border
4. Remove the flour from the interior, brush the inside and outside with a pastry brush and dry in the oven for four minutes
5. Prepare a financière sauce
6. Make the garnish with five rooster crests, five crayfish, five mushrooms and a beautiful truffle
7. Fill the pastry with the sauce and arrange the garnish in pyramid form
8. Glaze the dough with clear glaze and serve
