## Ingredients

- Cod, prepared and desalted
- Large tomatoes
- Onions, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Sweet peppers, chopped
- Parsley
- Oil
- Spices
- Sweet paprika
- Salt

## Directions

1. After preparing and desalting the cod, take some large tomatoes, cut them in half and roast them on grills.
2. Remove the skin and place in a dish to release all their juice.
3. Chop very finely onions, garlic, sweet peppers and a little parsley.
4. Fry these in oil, and after a short time, add the tomatoes and fry everything well.
5. Place the cod in a casserole and pour this mixture over it, adding spices, a little sweet paprika and salt.
6. Boil it for a while so it takes on the sauce, and once cooked, serve.
