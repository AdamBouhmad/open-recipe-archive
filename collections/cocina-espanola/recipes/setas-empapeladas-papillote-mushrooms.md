## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter
- paper
- parsley, chopped
- onion, chopped
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the mushrooms into pieces.
2. Coat with butter and wrap in paper.
3. Mix with chopped parsley and onion, ground salt, and pepper.
4. Roast on the grill over gentle heat and serve in the paper.
