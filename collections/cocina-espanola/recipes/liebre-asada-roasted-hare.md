## Ingredients

- hare
- bacon strips, finely sliced
- butter or lard
- flour
- meat broth
- white wine
- hare liver
- shallots
- fine herbs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Lard the meatiest parts of the hare with very fine strips of bacon.
2. Before larding, briefly reheat the prepared hare in butter or lard in a pan just enough to firm the meat slightly and give it tenderness.
3. Allow to cool, then lard easily, following the model that also works for rabbit.
4. Roast the hare for one hour.
5. For the sauce: brown flour in butter, moisten with a ladle of broth and a glass of white wine.
6. Add the hare liver, finely minced, with two or three shallots and fine herbs, all finely chopped, with appropriate seasoning.
7. Reduce the sauce by half so it becomes thick and tasty.
8. Incorporate the juice from the roasted hare.
9. Serve the hare without sauce on the platter, with the sauce presented separately in a sauceboat.
