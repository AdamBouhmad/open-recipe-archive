## Ingredients

- calf's brains, blanched and chilled
- salsa rubia-blanca (blonde-white sauce), well seasoned with salt and pepper
- 125 grams beef butter
- 2 heaping tablespoons finely chopped fine herbs, well seasoned with salt and pepper
- vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Blanch and chill the brains. Cut each brain into three pieces and cook for half an hour in a well-seasoned blonde-white sauce.
2. Place the brains in a serving dish and keep warm near the fire.
3. In a separate hot pan away from the fire, melt 125 grams of beef butter mixed with 2 heaping tablespoons of finely chopped fine herbs, well seasoned with salt and pepper.
4. Add a few drops of vinegar or lemon juice to the butter mixture.
5. Pour this sauce over the brains and serve.
