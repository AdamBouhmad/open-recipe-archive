## Ingredients

- half a pound of bread crumb
- hot milk
- salt
- black pepper
- nutmeg powder
- 60 grams of beef butter

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumb in hot milk
2. Cook slowly over very low heat until it becomes a thick pap
3. Season with salt, plenty of pepper, and powdered nutmeg
4. At the moment of serving, incorporate 60 grams of melted beef butter, stirring much and quickly with a spatula into the pap
