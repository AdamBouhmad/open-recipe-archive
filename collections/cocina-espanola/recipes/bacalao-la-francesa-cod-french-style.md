## Ingredients

- Cod
- Shallots or small onions
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Onion
- Lemon
- Black pepper
- Oil
- Butter
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. After prepared as indicated and cooked, take the platter or pan for serving
2. Place in it shallots or small onions, crushed garlic, parsley, onion, lemon sliced without skin or seeds, peppercorns, two tablespoons of oil and a piece of butter the size of an egg
3. Place the cod on top, cover with a layer of the same mixture and with bread crumbs
4. Place the platter over gentle heat so it simmers little by little
5. Add lemon juice, a little pepper, and brown in a covered oven
