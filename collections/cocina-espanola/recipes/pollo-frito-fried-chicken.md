## Ingredients

- Pieces of a good-eating chicken
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Chopped shallots
- Onions, divided
- Flour
- Onions, cut in wheels

## Directions

1. Soak the pieces of chicken in oil with salt, pepper, chopped shallots, and divided onions for five hours
2. Remove, drain, and coat heavily with flour, frying them in very good oil
3. Fry onions cut in wheels, also floured in the same oil until well browned
4. Place the browned onions in the center of a platter and arrange the fried chicken pieces around them
