## Ingredients

- chopped onions
- butter
- a little broth
- meat cut into pieces
- a little vinegar or mustard

## Directions

1. Reheat chopped onions in rounds with butter, adding a little broth
2. Cook the meat cut into pieces in this
3. Once it has cooked a little, add a little vinegar or mustard
4. Serve
