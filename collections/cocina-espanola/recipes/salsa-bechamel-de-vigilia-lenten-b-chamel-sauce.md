## Ingredients

- 60 grams beef butter or fine Valencia oil
- a handful of mushrooms
- half tablespoon flour
- half liter milk
- salt and pepper
- 2 or 3 cloves of garlic (optional)

## Directions

1. Sauté in 60 grams of beef butter or fine Valencia oil a small handful of carefully trimmed mushrooms
2. Add half a tablespoon of flour and stir very quickly with the spatula so the flour does not brown
3. Moisten little by little with half a liter of milk, seasoned with salt and pepper
4. Let cook until the sauce has taken on consistency
5. 2 or 3 cloves of garlic can be added if desired, which should be removed before serving
