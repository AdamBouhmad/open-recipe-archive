## Ingredients

- parsley, finely chopped
- spearmint, finely chopped
- tarragon, finely chopped
- one tablespoon of Parmesan cheese
- clean broth in calculated quantity

## Directions

1. Combine finely chopped parsley, spearmint, and tarragon with one tablespoon of Parmesan cheese
2. Add clean broth in calculated quantity
