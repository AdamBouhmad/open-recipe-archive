## Ingredients

- 5, 7, or 9 beautiful winter pears of equal size
- 2 liters syrup at 16 degrees
- 1 teaspoon vegetable carmine
- 1/4 vanilla stick

## Directions

1. Select 5, 7, or 9 beautiful winter pears of equal size, as much as possible, and peel them, taking care that they remain very smooth and avoiding letting the knife leave marks.
2. Place them in a saucepan with a capacity of 2 liters and cover them with syrup at 16 degrees, to which add 1 teaspoon of vegetable carmine and 1/4 of a vanilla stick.
3. Boil very gently until they are completely cooked, which is tested by seeing if the pears yield to finger pressure, and let the pears cool in the syrup.
4. Drain them and reduce the syrup over high heat until it reaches 32 degrees.
5. Place one pear in the middle of the compote dish and the other 4, 6, or 8 around it, and pour the syrup, which should be cold, over them.
