## Ingredients

- cooked beef palate
- beef broth, half a cup
- vinegar, half a cup
- salt
- pepper
- garlic cloves, about six
- beef butter, sixty grams
- flour, half a tablespoon
- flour for coating
- pork lard for frying
- parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Cut a cooked and prepared palate into long pieces as thick as fingers.
2. Soak them in a marinade of half a cup of broth and half a cup of vinegar with plenty of salt, pepper, and about six finely chopped cloves of garlic.
3. Warm this marinade slightly so that sixty grams of beef butter kneaded with half a tablespoon of flour can dissolve in it.
4. Let the dish soak for three hours.
5. Remove the pieces, drain them, and coat them with flour for frying very crisp in pork lard.
6. Serve this dish with fried parsley sprigs.
