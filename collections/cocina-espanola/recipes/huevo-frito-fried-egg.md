## Ingredients

- egg
- oil or lard

## Directions

1. Break the egg, putting the white in one cup and the yolk in another
2. Lightly agitate the white without beating it
3. Place the pan over high heat
4. Heat the liquid (oil or lard) until it produces a thin vapor with a brownish color, turning into the characteristic blue smoke of fats when boiling begins
5. At this moment, remove the pan from the heat
6. Pour the white in a thin stream from a hand's breadth of height, as if decanting
7. Immediately and without losing time, with great speed, throw the yolk into the middle of the white
8. Cover the pan and after three minutes remove the lid and take out the fried egg, placing it on the plate in which it is to be eaten
9. Do not add salt; the consumer should add it to their taste
