## Ingredients

- 250 grams flour
- warm water as needed
- 60 grams beef butter
- 1 cup white wine or 1 heaping tablespoon of brandy or cognac
- peeled apples, cleaned of seeds and sliced into rounds
- oranges cut into wheels
- torrijas (French toast)
- other sweets as desired

## Directions

1. Use the same formula as French pasta with the sole difference that when mixing add either 1 cup of white wine or 1 heaping tablespoon of brandy or cognac
2. Dip peeled apples cleaned of seeds and cut into slices, oranges cut into wheels, torrijas, and some other sweets, and fry them in this batter
