## Ingredients

- onions
- oil
- pepper
- sugar
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut both ends off the onions and remove the outer thick leaves.
2. Place them in a casserole with oil, pepper, sugar, and ground cinnamon.
3. Roast over gentle heat, covering with an iron lid and with coals on top until they are well browned.
