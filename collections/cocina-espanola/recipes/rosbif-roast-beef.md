## Ingredients

- A piece of beef, never as large as the English volume
- Good refined oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley, chopped
- Two bay leaves
- Some chopped shallots
- The juices from the meat

## Directions

1. Twelve hours before putting it on the spit, place the piece of rosbif in good refined oil seasoned with salt, pepper, chopped parsley, two bay leaves, and some chopped shallots
2. Turn the rosbif frequently so that it soaks evenly
3. Roast it for two or three hours, depending on size
4. Do not remove the meat from the spit until its interior is cooked but maintains a rosy color without releasing blood
5. Serve at natural temperature with its own juices in a sauce boat, and around the meat may be placed some potatoes in the Dutch style shaped like zucchini, peeled after being cooked in steam
