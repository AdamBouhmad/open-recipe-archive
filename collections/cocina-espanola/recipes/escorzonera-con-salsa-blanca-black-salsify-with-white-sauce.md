## Ingredients

- 2 kilos of salsify
- 2 liters of water
- 2 tablespoons of vinegar
- 100 grams of finely chopped beef kidney fat
- 2 tablespoons of salt
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Place the salsify in a pot with 2 liters of water and 2 tablespoons of vinegar
2. Cut the heads off the salsify and scrape them completely to remove the black skin, placing them in the water as they are scraped
3. Put 100 grams of finely chopped beef kidney fat in a 3-liter pot and melt it without browning
4. Add 2 liters of water, salt, and 2 tablespoons of vinegar
5. Stir over high heat until the first boil
6. Put the salsify in the pot and cover, leaving a 4-centimeter opening
7. Cook very gently for half an hour at the side of the stove
8. Drain and cut into 7-centimeter pieces
9. Place in a 2-liter pot, cover with white sauce, and serve
