## Ingredients

- camuesa apples
- 8 deciliters syrup at 16 degrees
- juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Cut camuesas in half, removing the cores and seeds.
2. Peel them, taking care that they remain of equal size, and place 8 deciliters of syrup at 16 degrees in a saucepan with a capacity of 2 liters, adding the juice of half a lemon to cook all this slowly.
3. When the camuesas are cooked, drain them in a strainer, and then strain the syrup, which is reduced to 32 degrees.
4. Once the camuesas are completely cold, arrange them in the compote dish, one in the middle and the rest around it, and sprinkle with the syrup.
