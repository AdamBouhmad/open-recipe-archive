## Ingredients

- oil or pork lard, one-fifth the volume of the tomatoes
- tomatoes, halved and without seeds
- a good tablespoon of flour
- one tablespoon of oil or lard
- water
- one egg yolk

## Directions

1. In oil or pork lard, one-fifth the volume of the tomatoes used for the sauce, fry halved and unseeded tomatoes
2. When they are fried and refried, pass through a strainer and the residue that remains continues to be recooked in the same pan with a little water
3. Pass this broth again, collecting it separately in a bowl
4. In the same pan, toast a good tablespoon of flour in another tablespoon of oil or lard and lengthen with the water that is in the bowl
5. Pour this over the first passed tomato, give it a boil, and away from the fire bind it with the yolk of an egg
