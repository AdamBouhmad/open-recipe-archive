## Ingredients

- Parsley, finely chopped
- Onions, finely chopped
- Sixty grams of beef or pork lard, or oil
- A good tablespoon of flour
- Salt and spices
- A couple of ladles of broth
- Two egg yolks
- Water
- Cooked beef

## Directions

1. Finely chop parsley and onions.
2. Sauté them in sixty grams of beef or pork lard, or oil if preferred, being careful that the fat does not brown.
3. Add a good tablespoon of flour, season and spice.
4. Moisten with a couple of ladles of broth and let the mixture cook for ten minutes.
5. Cut the cooked beef into thin slices, removing cartilage and fatty parts.
6. Give it a couple of boils in the sauce, which will be thickened when serving with two egg yolks dissolved in water.
