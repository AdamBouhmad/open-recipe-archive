## Ingredients

- one beef chop, prepared and larded
- one liter of broth
- 125 grams of fresh bacon
- a bouquet of parsley and scallions
- six carrots
- one bay leaf
- two onions with cloves
- one head of red cabbage, cut into slices

## Directions

1. Prepare and lard the chop as previously described
2. Cook over low heat for one hour in one liter of broth with 125 grams of fresh bacon
3. Add a bouquet of parsley and scallions, six carrots, one bay leaf, two onions with cloves, and one head of red cabbage cut into slices
4. Let cook two hours more
5. Remove the meat, take off the twine, and place on a large serving platter
6. Arrange the cabbage and carrots around it
7. If the broth is thin, reduce it over high heat before pouring it over the dish
