## Ingredients

- desalted and cleaned salt cod
- pepper
- nutmeg
- onions in slices
- parsley sprigs
- oil
- vinegar
- bay leaf
- tarragon
- flour
- beaten egg
- breadcrumbs
- oil for frying
- fried parsley for garnish

## Directions

1. From a good desalted and cleaned salt cod, cut strips or tiras the length of a finger, but without removing the skin
2. When all are cut, place them in adobo in a casserole with pepper, nutmeg, onions in slices, parsley sprigs, oil, vinegar, bay leaf, and tarragon, stirring from time to time
3. At the moment of serving this cod, dry it, pass it through flour, then through beaten egg and breadcrumbs, frying it well in good oil and placing it after draining on the plate with a garnish of fried parsley
