## Ingredients

- sole
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- oil for frying
- 100 grams of mayordoma sauce
- parsley
- lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare a sole and make an incision from the gills to four centimeters from the tail to remove the spine
2. Break the spine from top to bottom with the back of the knife so it can be removed easily
3. Coat the sole with egg and breadcrumbs, and fry it
4. Once fried, remove the spine and place 100 grams of mayordoma sauce in the opening
5. Serve with parsley and a lemon cut into quarters
