## Ingredients

- Frog legs
- Pork lard or good Marseille oil
- Cooked potato slices
- Thin strips of red peppers from La Rioja

## Directions

1. Clean the frog legs and soak them, then dry them
2. Fry them in pork lard or good Marseille oil
3. In a serving dish, arrange slices of cooked potatoes
4. Place the fried frog legs on this bed
5. Season by sprinkling, covering everything with thin strips of red peppers from La Rioja
6. Place the dish in the oven at medium heat and serve very hot
