## Ingredients

- 10 to 15 carrots
- sufficient quantity of butter
- good broth

## Directions

1. Place in a saucepan with sufficient quantity of butter 10 to 15 carrots cut into slices
2. Moisten with good broth when they are well browned
3. Cook them
4. Strain, return to cook for four hours
5. Place over any kind of soup, bound as a paste
