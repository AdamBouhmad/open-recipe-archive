## Ingredients

- 500 grams turtle
- 1 kilogram beef
- 1 kilogram veal
- 1 kilogram mutton
- carrots
- onions
- seasoning and spices
- 1 bottle Madeira or Port wine
- quenelles

## Directions

1. Prepare a finished broth with 1 kilogram beef, equal weight of veal and equal weight of mutton, allowing to cook for six hours with carrots, onions and corresponding seasoning and spices.
2. Strain the broth, cool it and remove the fat.
3. In this prepared broth, cook the pieces of turtle on low heat for the space of four hours.
4. At the moment of serving, add a bottle of Madeira or Port wine with quenelles cut into slices, but these are only placed in the soup when everything is in the tureen.
