## Ingredients

- rabbit, cleaned and cut into pieces
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- bay leaves
- nutmeg powder
- mushrooms
- parsley
- butter
- flour
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleaning, cut the rabbit into pieces and place them in a pot with oil, salt, pepper, garlic, some bay leaves, and powdered nutmeg.
2. Cook with heat below and above for a quarter of an hour.
3. Remove half of the oil and add mushrooms and chopped parsley, removing the bay leaves and garlic.
4. Thicken the sauce with fried butter and flour, add the juice of a lemon, and serve.
