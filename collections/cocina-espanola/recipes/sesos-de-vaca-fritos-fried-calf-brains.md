## Ingredients

- Calf brains
- Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Batter for frying
- Pork lard

## Directions

1. Boil the calf brains for half an hour in water with salt, pepper and a bouquet of fine herbs, after scalding and cleaning them
2. Remove the brains from the water, drain and let them cool
3. Dip the small or large pieces in a frying batter
4. Fry them in pork lard, making them take on a good golden color
