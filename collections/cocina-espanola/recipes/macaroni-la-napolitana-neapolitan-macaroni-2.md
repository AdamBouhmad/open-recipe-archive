## Ingredients

- macaroni
- salt
- water
- beef stock
- beef fat or lard
- tomato sauce from a good stew
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare a good stew, ensuring it does not burn and the sauce takes a sufficiently dark color. When necessary to add broth, use that from the pot.
2. Blanch or cook the macaroni with salt and water until they reach the desired doneness, as some prefer them very soft and others less so.
3. Drain the macaroni in a sieve until they release all their water.
4. Pass them to a saucepan containing beef fat, which should be kept near the fire so it does not cool and the macaroni become well coated.
5. Pass the sauce or gravy from the stew through a sieve.
6. Arrange the macaroni in a serving dish in layers, alternating with grated Parmesan cheese and sauce until all is used.
7. Serve hot. If good beef fat is not available, pork fat may be used.
