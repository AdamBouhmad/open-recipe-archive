## Ingredients

- 8 to 10 eggs for 4 servings
- truffles
- beef butter
- 2 tablespoons of cooked, cold cow's milk
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- croutons fried in pork butter

## Directions

1. Brush the truffles clean and cut them into small pieces.
2. Sauté the truffles in a pan with beef butter over high heat.
3. Beat the egg whites until foamy in one bowl.
4. In 2 tablespoons of cooked, cold milk, beat the yolks, incorporating parsley, salt, pepper, clove, and nutmeg.
5. Combine the yolks with the whites and continue beating, then add half an ounce of beef butter in a piece.
6. Pour the mixture into the pan with the truffles.
7. Stir and turn the eggs with a wooden spatula until the mixture is uniform and gelatinous in consistency.
8. This operation takes only a few seconds.
9. Present with croutons fried in pork butter, cut into squares, rounds, or triangles.
