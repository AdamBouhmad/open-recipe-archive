## Ingredients

- Meat leftovers
- Flour
- Butter
- Egg
- Four-centimeter square sheets of pasta dough
- Salt
- Lemon or cognac

## Directions

1. Make a minced mixture from the meat leftovers
2. Bind the mixture with a roux made from flour browned in butter and egg, as if making alboidigrilles
3. Take portions the size of hazelnuts and place them in the center of a four-centimeter square sheet of dough
4. Fold the dough over itself to enclose the meat
5. Join the edges of the dough by moistening them slightly and making marks with a knife as if crenellating
6. Fry or bake in a strong oven
