## Ingredients

- woodcock offal
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Grind the offal of the woodcock in a mortar
2. Add white wine, salt, pepper, thyme, and bay leaf
3. Place over fire
4. When reduced to half its volume, pass through a sieve
5. Serve the woodcocks with this sauce
