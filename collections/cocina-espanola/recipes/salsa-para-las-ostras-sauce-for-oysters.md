## Ingredients

- butter or oil
- flour
- broth or water
- pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley
- one slice or juice of lemon
- oysters
- breadcrumbs, if desired
- mushrooms, optional

## Directions

1. Mix butter or oil with flour
2. Moisten with broth, pepper, and a little salt
3. Add chopped parsley and a slice or juice of lemon
4. Stir everything over the fire
5. Make the sauce a little thick
6. When it begins to boil, add the oysters with the water they have released, which should have been carefully collected in a separate glass
7. Let cook for fifteen or twenty minutes
8. Pour into a plate and serve, if desired with breadcrumbs
9. When adding the parsley, mushrooms can be added if desired
10. If there is no broth, you can moisten with water, but thicken it with egg yolks at the moment of serving
