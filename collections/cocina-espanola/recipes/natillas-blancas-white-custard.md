## Ingredients

- twelve egg whites
- two egg yolks
- half quarter liter of milk
- sugar
- ginger biscuits

## Directions

1. Beat twelve egg whites well to stiff peaks, and add two yolks mixed beforehand with half a quarter liter of very sweetened milk, to achieve a papilla that, placed in a deep platter or terrine in a water bath with heat on top, produces white custard.
2. The surface is completely covered with black ginger biscuits.
