## Ingredients

- one kilogram of clams
- best quality oil
- two large onions, trimmed
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- one clove of garlic, chopped
- two spoonfuls of flour
- water
- white wine
- two slices of lemon
- two bay leaves
- a bundle of fine herbs

## Directions

1. Wash the clams well in warm water and keep them in cold water for a good while
2. In a casserole with the best oil, fry two large trimmed onions for one kilogram of clams
3. Do not let them take color and season with a pinch of salt, pepper, clove, nutmeg and chopped garlic
4. Sprinkle with two spoonfuls of flour and let cook until half-toasted
5. Then add water to form a thick paste, and after three boils add white wine, two slices of lemon, two bay leaves and a bundle of fine herbs
6. When it boils vigorously, add the clams, taking care that the casserole is very large
7. Cook the clams this way for three-quarters of an hour over very low heat, with the casserole covered and a pair of hot coals on the lid or two or three pieces of coke in ignition
