## Ingredients

- skate
- water with salt
- half a quartillo of vinegar
- onions
- bouquet of fine herbs
- black butter sauce
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cook the skate as described in the previous recipe: soak in salted water for one hour, then cook in a short broth made with the salted water, half a quartillo of vinegar, onions, and a bouquet of fine herbs
2. Place the cooked skate on a platter
3. Pour the black butter sauce over the skate
4. Serve with boiled potatoes prepared plain and served separately
