## Ingredients

- smelts
- milk
- flour
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. Thread the smelts spaced out by half dozens on needles or lancets as shown
2. Dip the threaded smelts in milk, coat with flour
3. Fry them in very hot oil very quickly
4. Serve still threaded, with each string being generally one diner's portion
