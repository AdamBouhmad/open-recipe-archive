## Ingredients

- Good vinegar
- Water
- Salt and pepper
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Shallots, finely chopped
- Calf's hands, deboned, cut into pieces, and cooked
- Frying batter
- Pork lard for frying
- Parsley sprigs for garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare a marinade with good vinegar diluted half with salted and peppered water, finely chopped parsley, and finely chopped shallots.
2. Soak the deboned, cut, and naturally cooked calf's hands in this marinade for three hours while cold.
3. Remove the hands from the marinade and coat them in frying batter.
4. Fry them in pork lard until they are a good color, crispy, and very hot.
5. Serve adorned with parsley sprigs on a well-folded napkin placed on the platter, arranged in a pyramid or dome shape.
