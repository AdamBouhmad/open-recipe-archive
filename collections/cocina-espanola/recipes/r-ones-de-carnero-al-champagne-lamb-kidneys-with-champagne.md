## Ingredients

- Two pounds of lamb kidneys
- Half pound of beef butter
- Half ounce salt for seasoning
- Half ounce flour
- One quarter bottle of Champagne
- A good cook

## Directions

1. Combine the kidneys, butter, salt, flour and Champagne according to the formula of Mr. Cirodde
2. Cook with skill and attention of a good cook
