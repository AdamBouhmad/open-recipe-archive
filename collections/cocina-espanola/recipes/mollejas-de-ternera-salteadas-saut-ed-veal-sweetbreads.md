## Ingredients

- veal sweetbreads
- beef butter
- tomato sauce
- financière garnish

## Directions

1. Blanch and blanch the sweetbreads, then cool in fresh water
2. Drain and cut into small pieces
3. Sauté in beef butter over high heat
4. Allow them to take on good color
5. Place on a serving platter
6. Top with tomato sauce or better yet, a financière garnish
