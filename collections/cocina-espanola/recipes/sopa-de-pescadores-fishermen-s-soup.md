## Ingredients

- large fish
- small fish or trash fish
- water
- oil
- fennel
- bay leaves
- lemon zest
- garlic head
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- bread

## Directions

1. Separate the large and beautiful fish from the small fish or trash fish
2. Boil the latter in water and when cooked, drain by pressing in a strainer
3. Put in a pot as many ladles of the drippings and broth in which the fish has cooked as there are diners
4. Sprinkle with very little good quality oil
5. Add a bundle of herbs composed of fennel, one or two bay leaves, lemon zest, a head of garlic and an onion
6. Add salt and pepper and boil very vigorously over ardent heat until the oil is well mixed, does not float on top and forms with the juice a whitish pearly sauce
7. Cut the large fish, which should be very fresh and alive if possible, principally red or golden fish
8. Boil with much heat and when cooked, stop the heat and pour the broth over bread cut into slices
