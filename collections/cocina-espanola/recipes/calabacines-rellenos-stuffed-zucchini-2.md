## Ingredients

- zucchini
- picadillo (ground meat mixture)
- almonds

## Directions

1. Cut about one finger's length from each end of the zucchini.
2. Drill through the length with a reed, extracting the interior part.
3. Place a picadillo in the hollowed interior (the same one used generally for stuffed onions).
4. Cook them and incorporate a small sauce made with almonds.
