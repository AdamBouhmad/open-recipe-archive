## Ingredients

- 125 grams of truffles, well cleaned
- 60 grams of fresh beef butter
- one cup of good consumed broth
- two spoonfuls of glaze (if on hand)
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the truffles, brushing them with a suitable brush rather than washing them
2. Sauté them in the fresh beef butter without allowing the butter to brown
3. Moisten with one cup of good consumed broth, to which add, if on hand, two spoonfuls of glaze
4. After twenty minutes of cooking over gentle heat, remove the sauce from the heat
5. Skim off the fat and serve
6. This sauce may also be made very well with oil and should be seasoned somewhat sharply with salt and pepper
