## Ingredients

- Lamb kidneys
- Beef butter
- Salt and pepper
- Bread pieces for croutons

## Directions

1. Prepare the kidneys as if for skewers, but this time completely separate the two halves
2. Sauté them in a pan over high heat in beef butter, seasoning with salt and pepper as they cook
3. Separately, fry bread cubes the same size as the kidney pieces and in equal number
4. Place the kidney pieces on a very hot platter, each piece on its crouton
5. Pour meat juice or Spanish sauce over the top
