## Ingredients

- Cucumbers
- Salt
- Fresh water

## Directions

1. Peel the cucumbers and cut into wheels the thickness of a peseta coin
2. Place in a shallow soup plate
3. Add plenty of salt as each wheel is arranged
4. Cover the plate with another plate
5. Every two hours, hold both plates with both hands, keeping them together, and stand them upright so the water the cucumber releases drains
6. After eight hours, remove and wash in very fresh water
7. Let drain and dry
8. Eat with the dressing desired
