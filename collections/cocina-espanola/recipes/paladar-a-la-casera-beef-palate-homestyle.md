## Ingredients

- palate
- water
- butter
- onion
- broth
- parsley bundle
- salt
- pepper
- mustard

## Directions

1. Clean carefully and cook in water
2. Cut into strips
3. Brown onion in butter
4. When half fried, add the palate with it
5. Moisten with broth, add a bundle of parsley, salt and pepper
6. Let the sauce reduce and serve with a little mustard
