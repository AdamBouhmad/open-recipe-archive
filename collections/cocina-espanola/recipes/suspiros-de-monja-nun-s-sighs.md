## Ingredients

- two ounces of butter
- four ounces of sugar
- one glass of water
- grated lemon zest
- flour
- three eggs
- powdered sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Put two ounces of butter, four ounces of sugar, one glass of water, and grated lemon zest in a saucepan
2. Add flour and bring to a boil until a paste forms that detaches easily from the pan
3. Mix in three eggs and stir everything well
4. Spread the mixture on a plate using the handle of a kitchen spoon and cut into small pieces
5. Drop the pieces into hot oil (not too hot) and fry them
6. Drain on a napkin and serve sprinkled with finely ground sugar
7. This is a traditional dish for All Saints' Day
