## Ingredients

- 1 chicken, well cleaned
- salt
- pepper
- onion
- spices
- bacon slices
- egg yolks, beaten
- sugar
- cinnamon
- celery
- watercress
- endive

## Directions

1. Boil the cleaned chicken a little in a pot or broth with salt, pepper, onion and spices
2. Wrap it in bacon slices and place on a spit
3. Roast over low heat
4. Remove the bacon slices and continue roasting the bird until it takes color
5. Anoint it with beaten egg yolks
6. To serve, you can add a little sugar and cinnamon and present it on a bed of celery, watercress or endive
