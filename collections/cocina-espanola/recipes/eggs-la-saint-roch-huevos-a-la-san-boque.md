## Ingredients

- Two eggs per person
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Breadcrumbs
- Butter from cow's milk, about the size of a walnut

## Directions

1. In small specially-made bowls or dishes of metal or porcelain, like those used for eggs en cocotte, place two eggs in each.
2. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, parsley, and breadcrumbs to cover the surface.
3. Add a piece of butter from cow's milk about the size of a walnut.
4. Cover the bowls and cook the eggs with heat on top to the desired degree of doneness.
5. Serve and eat the eggs in the same bowls.
