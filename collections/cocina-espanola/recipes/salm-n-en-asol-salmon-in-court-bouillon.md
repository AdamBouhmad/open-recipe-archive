## Ingredients

- one whole salmon
- 250 grams fresh butter
- five or six onions
- cloves
- carrots, cut into rounds
- three or four bay leaves
- salt
- pepper
- parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the salmon through the gills without splitting the belly.
2. Cook the salmon in a blue court-bouillon as indicated at the beginning of the fish section.
3. Add to the court-bouillon 250 grams of very fresh butter, five or six onions studded with cloves, a good quantity of carrots cut into rounds, three or four bay leaves, and season well with salt and pepper.
4. Serve the salmon cooked whole and adorned with sprigs of parsley on a flat, oval wooden platter without paint, or on a well-arranged wooden board, wrapped carefully in a pressed napkin.
