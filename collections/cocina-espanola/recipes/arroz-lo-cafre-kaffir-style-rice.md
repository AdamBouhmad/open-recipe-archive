## Ingredients

- one watermelon
- pre-fried oil
- small pieces of zucchini
- chicken, fish, or meat
- peppers
- tomatoes
- water
- rice

## Directions

1. Choose a watermelon according to the number of diners.
2. Cut off the top as a lid and carefully hollow it out, removing the flesh in presentable pieces to serve as an appetizer at the beginning of the meal.
3. Continue cleaning the inside of all its flesh until only the rind remains, with the interior well smoothed, resulting in a cavity shaped like a bowl.
4. Inside, place pre-fried oil, small pieces of zucchini, chicken, fish or meat that have been previously seasoned.
5. Season well; add very finely cut peppers and tomatoes.
6. Fill the watermelon with water to two or three fingers below its edges.
7. Add rice in an amount equal to one third of the volume of what is contained in it.
8. Cover with the lid placed upside down, and with a piece of strong canvas, tie and secure the watermelon well so that the lid does not shift.
9. Dig a hole in the ground, bury the watermelon, and cover it with some sand.
10. Then spread quicklime on top to form a mound, and extinguish it with water.
11. After three quarters of an hour, remove the watermelon and serve this perfectly cooked and flavorful rice, made without fire and without a vessel.
