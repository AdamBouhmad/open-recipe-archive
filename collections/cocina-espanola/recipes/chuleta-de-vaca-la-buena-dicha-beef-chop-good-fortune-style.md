## Ingredients

- 125 grams beef butter
- 1 beef chop, prepared and seasoned

## Directions

1. Brown the beef chop in the butter, turning occasionally so it colors on both sides.
2. Place the pan over low heat or on a cooktop away from direct flame.
3. Cover with a lid and place fire on top, renewing it every half hour.
4. After two hours of cooking, remove the meat from the pan and remove the string.
5. Place on a serving dish and pour the degreased cooking juice over the top.
