## Ingredients

- Fresh tuna pieces
- Oil
- Pepper
- Remoulade or tartar sauce

## Directions

1. Brush the pieces of fresh tuna with oil using a kitchen brush and sprinkle with pepper.
2. Roast them over a gentle fire on grills until both sides of each piece crackle.
3. Serve with a remoulade or tartar sauce.
