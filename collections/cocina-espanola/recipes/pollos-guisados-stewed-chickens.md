## Ingredients

- chickens, well cleaned
- butter
- onion
- spices
- salt
- white wine
- water
- chicken livers and garlic

## Directions

1. Heat butter in a frying pan and sear the chickens, then place them in the pot where they are to cook.
2. In the remaining fat, fry some onion and add it to the chickens with all the spices.
3. Add a little salt and a glass of white wine, and place over a gentle fire with a small pot of water on top and a cloth or paper to prevent evaporation of the substances, cooking for about one hour.
4. Roast the chicken livers, crush them with a garlic clove, mix them with the broth, and season well.
5. Cover again and allow to boil a couple of times, which produces an excellent sauce.
