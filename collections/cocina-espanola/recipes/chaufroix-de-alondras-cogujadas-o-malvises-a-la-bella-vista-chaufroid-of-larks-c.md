## Ingredients

- Larks, crested larks, or warblers
- white wine
- breasts of other birds of the same species
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- paper boxes
- white wine for moistening
- meat jelly from consummate broth
- truffle shavings

## Directions

1. Clean the birds and rinse their interior thoroughly with white wine.
2. Fill them with a minced filling made only from breasts of other birds of the same species, converted into paste and kneaded with butter, well seasoned with salt and pepper.
3. Roast them in the oven prepared in this way, over gentle heat without allowing them to take color.
4. Once roasted, let them cool.
5. Place each bird in loosely fitting paper boxes with the breast facing upward.
6. With a brush and good white wine, moisten the bird well and fill the box.
7. Cover the bird with meat jelly from consummate broth, sprinkled and scattered with truffle shavings.
