## Ingredients

- 1 cooked lobster
- Fine herbs, finely chopped
- 2 shallots, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons refined oil
- 1 small glass of aniseed brandy
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Open the cooked crustacean and extract from the body the substance that fills it
2. If the lobster is female, add the thousands of roe that are attached in clusters below the first articulation of the tail
3. Crush and beat and mix all of this with a wooden spatula
4. Add the finely chopped fine herbs and shallots, two tablespoons of refined oil and one small glass of aniseed brandy
5. Once the mixture is well made, clarify or lengthen it with lemon juice in sufficient quantity to give the sauce the consistency desired
