## Ingredients

- two good chickens
- three pounds of calf kidney fat
- four carrots
- two large onions with cloves inserted
- one ladle of concentrated broth
- one bouquet of fine herbs
- consumed broth
- white roux made with flour
- one good handful of mushrooms
- salt
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place the two chickens, calf kidney fat, carrots, onions with cloves, concentrated broth, and bouquet of fine herbs in a pot.
2. The important point in preparing the glaze is that the meats do not stick to the pot and do not take on color.
3. When the juice begins to thicken, moisten with a little consumed broth and add gradually, little by little, until it well bathes the meats.
4. Prick the meat at once with a knife so that it releases its juice, and let it cook over low heat.
5. Prepare separately a white roux with flour, in which cook a good handful of well-cleaned mushrooms, cut up and seasoned beforehand in the cold with salt and lemon juice.
6. Mix the white roux with the broth in which the meats are cooking.
7. Continue cooking everything for two hours.
8. Skim and degrease and strain through a metal mesh strainer.
9. If the preparation is done well, the glaze will result white.
