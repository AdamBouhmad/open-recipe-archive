## Ingredients

- Cod, cut into regular pieces
- Water
- Garlic cloves, peeled
- Bread crumbs
- Sweet paprika, ground
- Parsley, chopped
- Oil
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut the cod into regular pieces and place in a casserole with water for about eight hours.
2. When the salt has been released, place in a serving dish, drain the water well, remove the bones, and arrange in a frying pan, well spread out.
3. Add peeled garlic cloves cut in half, bread crumbs, a little sweet ground paprika, and chopped parsley.
4. Place another layer of cod, also spread out; add four ounces of oil, water and salt until it is covered.
5. Cover well and cook over low heat.
6. When no broth remains, it can be served.
