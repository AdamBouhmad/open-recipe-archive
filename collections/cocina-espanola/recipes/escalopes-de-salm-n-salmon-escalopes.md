## Ingredients

- Salmon
- Cow butter, in sufficient quantity
- Salt and pepper
- Tomato sauce, or genovese sauce or Italian sauce
- Breadcrumb croutons

## Directions

1. Cut the salmon into thin slices, and from the large ones make two or more to make them all equal.
2. Place in a pan with cow butter in sufficient quantity and season, but do not overdo it.
3. Sauté over high heat until completely cooked and place in a crown on a round platter.
4. Pour a sauce in the center, preferably tomato sauce.
5. If this is not to the guests' liking, or if there is no fresh tomato, genovese or Italian sauce are suitable to substitute for it.
6. When placing the salmon escalopes in a crown, it is a good effect to intersperse them with breadcrumb croutons of the same size.
