## Ingredients

- beefsteaks, cut thicker than those prepared for the grill
- beef butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- sufficient quantity of broth to bathe the beefsteaks
- a bouquet of fine herbs
- one onion
- one carrot
- half a turnip
- salt
- pepper
- three tablespoons of rice flour
- a pinch of Cayenne pepper
- four or five shallots

## Directions

1. Brown in beef butter by turning them, some beefsteaks cut thicker than those prepared for the grill.
2. Remove them from the pot, and in the butter that remains add one tablespoon of flour and stir quickly to make a golden roux that is moistened with a sufficient quantity of broth to bathe the beefsteaks, which are incorporated into the sauce immediately.
3. As soon as the sauce begins to cook, add a bouquet of fine herbs, an onion, a carrot, and half a turnip, all of it in very thin slices; season with salt and pepper, and let cook over very low heat with the pot well covered for three hours.
4. Half an hour before serving, add to the sauce three tablespoons of rice flour with a pinch of Cayenne pepper, and four or five shallots placed and sautéed separately in beef butter.
