## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- legumes

## Directions

1. Prepare the leg as previously described.
2. Cook the legumes beforehand in water and have them ready.
3. Half an hour before serving, incorporate the well-drained legumes into the meat and its juices after vaporizing the water they contain.
4. Allow them to stew slowly, cooking gently for about fifteen minutes, during which time they will absorb the meat juices.
5. Instead of beans, by the same procedure, the leg can be served with cauliflower, pea purée, sorrel paste or spinach paste, lentils, etc.
