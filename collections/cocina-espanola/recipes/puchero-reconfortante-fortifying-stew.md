## Ingredients

- common puchero
- one old chicken
- a piece of lean beef
- a piece of veal
- two carrots
- onions
- leeks
- a bouquet of aromatic herbs
- cloves

## Directions

1. Add to the common puchero one old chicken, a piece of lean beef and another of veal, two carrots, onions, leeks, a bouquet of aromatic herbs, and cloves
2. Cook until reduced to one third
3. Then remove the fat
