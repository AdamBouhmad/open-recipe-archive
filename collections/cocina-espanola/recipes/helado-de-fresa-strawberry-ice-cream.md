## Ingredients

- Strawberries, two and a half pounds
- Red currants, half a pound
- Water, one quartillo
- Sugar, one pound

## Directions

1. Crush the strawberries and currants in a sieve so the pulp passes through and the seed grains remain on top
2. Pour water over the dregs, press and stir well, and combine the liquid that passes with the previous liquid
3. Dissolve the sugar over heat in a little water, combine everything, mix well, and once it is cold, freeze it
