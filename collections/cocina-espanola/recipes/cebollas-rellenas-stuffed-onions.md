## Ingredients

- lean meat, chopped
- fat bacon, chopped
- parsley
- garlic
- breadcrumbs
- fried onion
- salt
- spices
- oil
- toasted flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of chopped lean meat and fat bacon, parsley, garlic, breadcrumbs, fried onion, salt, and spices.
2. Remove the inner layers of the onions with a knife.
3. Fill the onions with the mixture and place them in a casserole with the filling facing upward.
4. Cook them.
5. Make a sauce with fried oil, toasted flour, and a few drops of vinegar.
