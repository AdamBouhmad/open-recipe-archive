## Ingredients

- veal liver
- olive oil
- finely chopped fine herbs
- mushrooms, finely chopped
- salt and pepper
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cut the veal liver into pieces.
2. In a pot, add several spoonfuls of oil, finely chopped fine herbs with some finely chopped mushrooms, and place a layer of liver pieces on top.
3. Moisten with more oil and sprinkle with fine herbs and mushrooms as before, but season strongly with salt and pepper this time.
4. Add another layer of liver and another layer of seasoning, continuing in this manner until all liver is used, ending with a layer of mushrooms and fine herbs.
5. Cook all of this at low heat with one quarter bottle of white wine and with the pot well covered.
6. Serve hot.
