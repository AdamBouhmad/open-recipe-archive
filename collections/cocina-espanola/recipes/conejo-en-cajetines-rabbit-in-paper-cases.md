## Ingredients

- young rabbit, cut into pieces
- butter
- fine herbs
- liver of the rabbit
- egg yolks
- bacon slices
- butter for spreading
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the young rabbit into pieces and sauté them in butter.
2. Place them in paper cases with a filling composed of fine herbs, the liver of the same rabbit, and two egg yolks to give it consistency.
3. Cover the intervals of the case with this filling and cover everything with slices of bacon and paper spread with butter.
4. Place in the oven in this manner, and before serving remove the fat and add an Italian sauce.
