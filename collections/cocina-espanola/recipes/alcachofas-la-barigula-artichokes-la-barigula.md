## Ingredients

- large artichokes
- water
- salt
- sausage meat
- bread
- good quality butter
- fine herbs
- flour
- small mushrooms (if available)
- refined oil
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean the artichokes and cook them in boiling water with a little salt, but less cooked than for other dishes. Drain them well of all the water they contain.
2. Make a picadillo with sausage meat and crumbled bread, and mix well with very good quality butter. Sauté this, adding seasoning, fine herbs and a tablespoon of flour.
3. If you have small mushrooms on hand, incorporate them, cut very finely.
4. Fill the artichokes with this picadillo after cutting and removing internally, above the heart, some of the center leaves to form a cavity.
5. Place the artichokes thus prepared in a saucepan whose bottom is lightly bathed with a little refined oil. With the same, brush them on top and cook them for five minutes, covering the saucepan with a lid and fire on top.
6. If you see that they are drying out, moisten them with a few spoonfuls of broth from the pot.
