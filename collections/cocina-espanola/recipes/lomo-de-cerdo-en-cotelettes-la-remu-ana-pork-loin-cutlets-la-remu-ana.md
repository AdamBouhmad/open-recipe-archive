## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- lard
- salt
- nutmeg powder
- four glasses of good sherry

## Directions

1. Cut the pork loin into slices one centimeter thick, cut on the bias
2. Coat each slice lightly with lard and season with salt and a dusting of nutmeg
3. Place them in a frying pan with four glasses of good sherry and cover well
4. After ten minutes they will give off a very rich aroma
5. In less than another ten minutes the pan will be as clean as a plate
