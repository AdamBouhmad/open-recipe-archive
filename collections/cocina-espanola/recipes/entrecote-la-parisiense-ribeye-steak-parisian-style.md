## Ingredients

- 1 entrecote (ribey steak), 15 centimeters long and 6-7 centimeters wide
- melted beef butter
- parsley, finely chopped
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Flatten the meat by striking it on its edge (thickness) and refine and shape the edges with a knife.
2. Soak the raw meat in melted beef butter.
3. Over high heat, sear and cook directly on the cooktop, turning to cook the other side when the first side is done.
4. Place on a serving dish with a touch of beef butter kneaded with finely chopped parsley, salt and a touch of pepper.
5. Before serving, mark the meat with three or four lines using a heated poker from the stove to simulate the toasted marks of grill grates.
6. Serve with fried potatoes or other elegant potato preparations.
