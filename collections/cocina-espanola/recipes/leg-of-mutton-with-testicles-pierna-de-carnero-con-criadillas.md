## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- mutton testicles
- thick bacon
- white wine

## Directions

1. After removing the bone from the leg of mutton and taking out half a pound of meat from the center, mince it with testicles to replace the removed part.
2. Lard with thick bacon and tie.
3. Lard again with testicles cut into thin strips.
4. Keep this way for twenty-four hours.
5. After that time, cook over low fire, surrounded by strips of bacon and moistened with white wine.
6. Strain the sauce, reduce it and serve.
