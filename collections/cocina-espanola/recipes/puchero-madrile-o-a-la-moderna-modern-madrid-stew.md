## Ingredients

- Two liters of water
- Half a kilogram of beef from the part called morcillo (chuck)
- One hand of veal
- One bone of canilla (shin bone)
- One piece of codillo (elbow) the size of an ounce of chocolate
- Chickpeas
- Salt

## Directions

1. Six hours before beginning, soak the chickpeas in cold water and salt
2. In a pot of three liters capacity, pour two liters of water
3. Place on the fire, and when it boils vigorously, add half a kilogram of beef from the part called morcillo
4. Add one hand of veal, one shin bone, and one piece of codillo the size of an ounce of chocolate
5. These items should be added in the order indicated, gradually and successively, so that the boiling does not interrupt
6. While the water continues to boil vigorously, incorporate the chickpeas and add salt
