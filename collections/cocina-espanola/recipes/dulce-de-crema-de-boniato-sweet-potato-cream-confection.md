## Ingredients

- two pounds of sweet potato
- one pound of untoasted almonds
- six egg yolks
- one pound of superior sugar
- half a cuartillo of water
- zest of one lemon

## Directions

1. Place half a cuartillo of water, the sugar, and the sweet potato at half-cooked; add the almonds, let the syrup cool, dissolve the six egg yolks and combine everything, stirring it without ceasing until it becomes a thick cream
2. Before putting the water, sugar, and sweet potato on the fire, add with the sweet potato, or with the sugar, or with the water (it makes no difference), the zest of one lemon
