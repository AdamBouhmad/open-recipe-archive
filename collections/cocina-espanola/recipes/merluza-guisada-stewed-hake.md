## Ingredients

- Oil
- Onions
- Shallots
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Flour
- Water

## Directions

1. In a pot, heat oil and blanch well-trimmed onions, shallots, garlic, and parsley
2. Before the onion takes color, dust with flour and season
3. Add water in sufficient amount to cover the hake in a single piece and place it in the pot
4. Bring to a boil over high heat, then let cook slowly for half an hour
5. Strain the excess broth, which is reserved for soup the next day; the remainder with all the seasoning constitutes the sauce for the stewed hake
