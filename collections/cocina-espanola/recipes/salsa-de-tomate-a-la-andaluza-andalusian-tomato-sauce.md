## Ingredients

- tomatoes, cleaned and unwashed
- olive oil
- two cloves of garlic, finely chopped
- salt

## Directions

1. Without peeling, washed and drained tomatoes, fry them well in oil with two cloves of garlic finely chopped.
2. Let them pass and even burn a little.
3. Season with salt.
4. Without straining the sauce, which results very thick, serve the tomato with its skin.
