## Ingredients

- three roasted woodcocks
- juice of four lemons
- one lemon cut into thin slices
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- grated nutmeg
- two tablespoons of mustard
- one glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Take three roasted woodcocks and carve them in a serving dish according to proper technique
2. Crush the livers and remaining offal
3. Squeeze the juice of four lemons and add one lemon cut into thin slices
4. Add salt, pepper, spices, and grated nutmeg, two tablespoons of mustard, and a glass of white wine
5. Prepare this mixture in a saucepan and place it over hot coals
6. Beat it so it is well mixed and do not let it cook
7. Drizzle with a few drops of oil
8. Place the breasts of the bird on top and serve very hot
