## Ingredients

- bread slices
- raw oil
- butter of cow's milk
- 125 grams of butter
- skate liver
- parsley
- small onions
- shallots
- capers
- anchovy fillets
- soft bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut slices of bread that are twice as long as they are wide and soak them in raw oil
2. Fry the bread slices in cow's milk butter to make canapés
3. Melt 125 grams of cow's milk butter in a pot and stew and cook the skate livers in it with plenty of chopped parsley, small onions, shallots, and capers, all chopped very finely
4. When the liver is cooked, remove it from the pot and set aside
5. Place the fried bread slices on an oval metal platter
6. Spread each piece of bread first with a layer of the finely chopped vegetables cooked in their liquid
7. Place a piece of skate liver on top of each bread slice with a couple of anchovy fillets, then finish with a second spreading of the fine herbs mixture
8. Once all the canapés are garnished equally, sprinkle them with very finely crumbled soft bread
9. Cover the platter with a bell cover loaded with a small amount of coals just to brown the surface of the dish
