## Ingredients

- two snipe
- salt

## Directions

1. Roast two snipe on a spit at medium temperature after cleaning them very well on the outside, knowing that inside, like shells and snails, these animals are not gutted
2. When the snipe are half-roasted, divide them according to all the good rules of carving art
3. Then cut the wings, legs, breast, and tail into two pieces each
4. Place the pieces in a serving dish
5. Prepare the sauce in the plate where the carving was done
