## Ingredients

- 6 hard-boiled eggs
- broth from the cooking pot
- parsley, dried and pulverized

## Directions

1. Use 6 hard-boiled eggs for 6 diners
2. In 4 spoonfuls of broth from the cooking pot, mash and thin the 6 cooked yolks from the hard-boiled eggs and form a paste
3. Once well worked, thin over the heat in a pan by adding all the broth little by little without ceasing to stir while it cooks
4. As soon as the mixture has homogeneous consistency, pour the contents of the pan into the soup tureen where the whites from the hard-boiled eggs are prepared beforehand, cut into equal small pieces like small dice, and mixed with dried parsley pulverized
5. Serve
