## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter
- Onion
- Flour
- Milk
- Salt

## Directions

1. Brown the butter and in it fry for every two eggs a well-sliced onion about the size of an apple.
2. Dissolve a little flour in a cup of milk and add it along with the hard-boiled eggs cut into rounds.
3. Season and let it cook very slowly for about a quarter hour.
