## Ingredients

- salt cod
- onion
- parsley
- nutmeg
- flour
- 2 ounces of lard
- 2 tablespoons of flour
- 1 quartillo of milk

## Directions

1. Let the salt cod soak in water for one hour
2. Chop some onion and parsley, grate nutmeg, and prepare flour
3. Cut the salt cod into very thin pieces and place in a pot of water
4. Bring the pot of water to a boil, then remove from heat, cover carefully, and let the cod rest
5. In a second pot, put 2 ounces of lard and 2 tablespoons of flour
6. Add the chopped onion, parsley, and nutmeg to the second pot
7. Slowly pour in the quartillo of milk while stirring constantly with a wooden spoon, always in the same direction
8. Remove the salt cod from the first pot, dry it thoroughly, and transfer it to the second pot with the sauce
9. Place the pot over heat and let it boil once
10. The dish is ready to serve
