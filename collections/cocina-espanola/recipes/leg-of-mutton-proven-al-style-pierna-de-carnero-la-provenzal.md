## Ingredients

- leg of mutton, good size
- twelve garlic cloves
- anchovy fillets, well washed, double the amount of garlic cloves

## Directions

1. Embed symmetrically in the meaty part of the leg of mutton twelve garlic cloves and twice as many fillets of well-washed anchovies, as if they were larding strips of bacon
2. Roast the meat in the same manner and with the care recommended for roasted leg of mutton
3. While the meat is roasting, peel one liter of garlic cloves
4. Blanch the garlic by scalding it in boiling water, submerging it repeatedly in different waters, then let it cool in fresh water
5. Cook the garlic in a bowl of broth
6. When the leg of mutton is roasted to its point, well degrease the juice it has released
7. Serve the meat with the garnish of garlic
