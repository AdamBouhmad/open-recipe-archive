## Ingredients

- two large spoonfuls of oil
- mushrooms, finely chopped
- one onion, sliced into rounds
- garlic
- scallions
- one bay leaf
- one clove
- some parsley
- a pinch of flour
- salt and pepper
- one ladleful of broth
- one ladleful of white wine
- lemon juice (optional)
- some truffles, sliced (optional)

## Directions

1. Place the oil in the pan with the mushrooms, onion, garlic, scallions, bay leaf, clove, and parsley
2. Sauté everything together
3. Add a pinch of flour, salt and pepper
4. Moisten with one ladleful of broth and another of white wine
5. Simmer over gentle heat for thirty to forty minutes
6. Strain and serve, if desired, with lemon juice
7. Some sliced truffles may also be added
