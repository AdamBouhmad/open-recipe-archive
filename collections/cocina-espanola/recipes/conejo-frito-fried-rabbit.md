## Ingredients

- rabbit
- white wine
- lemon juice
- thyme
- bay leaf
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- picante sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit into pieces and place in a marinade of white wine.
2. Add to the marinade the juice of a lemon, thyme, bay leaf, garlic cut into small pieces, salt, and pepper.
3. After two hours, drain and coat in flour mixed with water.
4. Fry the rabbit.
5. Serve with a picante sauce.
