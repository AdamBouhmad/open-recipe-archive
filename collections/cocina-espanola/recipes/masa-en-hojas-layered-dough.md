## Ingredients

- One pound of cow butter per pound of flour
- Water, cold

## Directions

1. Mix the dough well, adding cold water
2. Flatten with a rolling pin until it reaches the thickness of a coin
3. Brush the surface well with cow butter
4. Fold in half
5. Pass the rolling pin over it again and brush again with butter and flour
6. Repeat this process as many times as desired to create layers, at least three times
