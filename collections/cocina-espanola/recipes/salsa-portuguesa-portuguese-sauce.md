## Ingredients

- 125 grams of beef butter
- 2 raw egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place together in a pan the beef butter, egg yolks, and lemon juice with salt and pepper
2. Heat the pan over low heat enough to melt the butter while continuously stirring
3. Be patient and careful so that the eggs bind well with the butter and lemon without curdling
