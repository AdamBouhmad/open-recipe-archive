## Ingredients

- 1 cauliflower
- salt
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Boil salted water in a large pot, and when it boils vigorously, add the cauliflower well washed in several waters and divided into florets that form the head.
2. Cook for a quarter hour. Remove and let drain. To prevent it from cooling, place it in a casserole without covering it, near the fire.
3. Prepare the white sauce and bind it in a small casserole. Take the cooked cauliflower pieces one by one and arrange them in an enameled earthenware soup tureen with the florets facing down and tightly packed.
4. Take a silver or white metal platter of equal size but not as deep, which is to be used for serving the dish, place it as a cover over the tureen and turn it over so that the cauliflower appears whole on the serving platter.
5. Pour the sauce over the top.
