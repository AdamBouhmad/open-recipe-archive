## Ingredients

- 24 apples, peeled and cleaned of seeds and seed cells
- half a glass of water
- lemon zest
- cinnamon
- half a pound of sugar
- eight eggs, well beaten as for an omelet
- butter for greasing the mold

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and clean them of seeds and seed cells
2. Cook them in a pot with half a glass of water, lemon zest, cinnamon, and half a pound of sugar
3. When the apples are well cooked, pass them through a fine sieve to make a purée
4. Let them cool and then add, stirring gradually, eight eggs well beaten as for an omelet
5. When the mixture is well combined, grease a mold with butter
6. Fill it with the mixture and cook it in a water bath
