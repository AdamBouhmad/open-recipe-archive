## Ingredients

- Sirloin, cleaned of fat, skin, and sinew
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the sirloin into slices of half an inch thick
2. Flatten them slightly
3. Cook or roast on a cast iron griddle placed over the heat
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Each whole sirloin can yield about twelve regular beefsteaks
