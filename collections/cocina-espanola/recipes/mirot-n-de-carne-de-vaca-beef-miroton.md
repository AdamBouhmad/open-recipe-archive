## Ingredients

- good broth
- parsley
- scallions
- tarragon
- chervil
- capers
- pickled gherkins
- salt
- pepper
- beef

## Directions

1. Pour good broth into a pan, add parsley, scallions, tarragon, chervil, capers and pickled gherkins, salt and pepper
2. Chop everything and place the beef, cut into thin slices, on top
3. Make a second layer similar to the one below
4. Cover and simmer very gently for half an hour
