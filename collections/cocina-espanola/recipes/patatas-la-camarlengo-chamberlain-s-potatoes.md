## Ingredients

- Two pounds of potatoes
- One quarter pound of hard cheese, grated (Gruyere, Parma, Chester, or Flemish)
- Flour
- Beaten egg
- Lard for frying
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook two pounds of potatoes, and when cooked, mash them in a mortar, adding a quarter pound of grated hard cheese.
2. Mix everything very well and mold the paste into croquettes.
3. Coat them in flour, dip them in beaten egg, and pass them through flour again.
4. When completely cold, fry them in lard and dust with salt at the moment of serving.
