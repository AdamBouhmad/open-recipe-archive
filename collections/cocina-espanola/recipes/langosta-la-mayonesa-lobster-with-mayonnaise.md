## Ingredients

- 1 cooked lobster
- Mayonnaise sauce
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Lettuce hearts
- Various garnishes

## Directions

1. Cook the lobster
2. Serve the lobster whole and open, split in half lengthwise on a napkin
3. Serve the mayonnaise sauce separately in a sauce boat
4. Garnish the platter with hard-boiled egg yolks, lettuce hearts and other decorative items
