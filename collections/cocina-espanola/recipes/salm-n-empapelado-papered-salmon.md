## Ingredients

- three tablespoons Marseille oil
- finely chopped parsley
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- garlic
- nutmeg
- juice of half a lemon
- two pound piece of salmon
- breadcrumbs
- good quality paper

## Directions

1. In a soup plate, put three tablespoons of Marseille oil and beat and mix well with finely chopped parsley, onion, salt, pepper, clove, garlic, nutmeg, and the juice of half a lemon.
2. From a two pound piece of salmon, cut thin slices in the longitudinal direction of the fish, like slices of cured ham.
3. One by one, soak each slice in the mixture and wrap it in breadcrumbs.
4. Place each slice in a good quality paper envelope.
