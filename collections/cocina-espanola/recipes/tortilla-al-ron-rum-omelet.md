## Ingredients

- eight very fresh eggs
- 125 grams of ground sugar
- zest of one large lemon, grated
- three or four tablespoons of thick cream
- a pinch of salt
- six tablespoons of good rum

## Directions

1. Prepare the omelet exactly as the Sweet Omelet.
2. Once made, place it on a silver or metal serving dish.
3. Sprinkle with sugar.
4. Pour six tablespoons of good rum over it.
5. Set the rum on fire to serve.
6. Alternatively, you can make the omelet with Kirsch or any other high-proof spirit.
