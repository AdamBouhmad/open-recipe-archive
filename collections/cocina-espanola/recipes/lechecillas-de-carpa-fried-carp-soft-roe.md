## Ingredients

- Carp soft roe
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Oil for frying
- Frying batter
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Soak the carp soft roe in fresh water, renewing it many times over an hour.
2. In a pot over high heat, put a quarter of a liter of water with salt and a little vinegar.
3. When it begins to boil, add the soft roe to give two or three boils.
4. Remove and let drain and cool.
5. When cold, wrap in frying batter and fry in very hot oil until golden.
6. Serve on a platter over a napkin, as with all fried foods, with a garnish of fried parsley.
