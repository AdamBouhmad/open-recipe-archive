## Ingredients

- veal kidney
- pork lard
- salt and pepper
- two bay leaves
- two ladles of broth or cold water
- parsley sprig

## Directions

1. In pork lard and in a braising pan or earthenware casserole, brown the veal kidney and make it take color
2. The fire must be lively for this operation, which will last ten to twelve minutes
3. Then cover the pan, after seasoning the meat with salt and pepper and adding two bay leaves
4. Cook on very low heat for three hours, taking care to turn the meat every half hour, and at the midpoint of cooking moisten the meat with two ladles of broth or cold water, incorporating at the same time a sprig of parsley
5. Serve as roasted veal Spanish style
