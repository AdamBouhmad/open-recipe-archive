## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 150 grams of beef butter
- breadcrumbs
- parsley, finely chopped
- chives, finely chopped
- salt and pepper
- tomato sauce or béchamel
- pieces of fried ham

## Directions

1. Hard boil the eggs and cut them in half lengthwise or crosswise.
2. Remove the yolks and mash them together with 150 grams of beef butter, breadcrumbs, finely chopped parsley and chives, salt and pepper.
3. Fill the hollows of the egg white halves with this mixture, forming a rounded mound.
4. Place the filled eggs symmetrically in a baking dish with a little beef butter.
5. Bake in the oven at low heat for 15 minutes.
6. Serve separately with tomato sauce or béchamel sprinkled with pieces of fried ham.
