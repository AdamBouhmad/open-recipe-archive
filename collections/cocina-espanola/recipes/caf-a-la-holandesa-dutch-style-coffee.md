## Ingredients

- 125 grams of freshly roasted coffee
- Half a cuartillo (approximately 250 ml) of boiling water

## Directions

1. Use a tall copper coffeepot, tin-plated inside and out, with a ring at the mouth that holds the cloth filter.
2. Recently roasted coffee should be ground in a hand mill.
3. Place 125 grams of ground coffee in the cloth filter for half a cuartillo of boiling water.
4. Pour the boiling water over the coffee in the filter and cover the coffeepot.
5. Let it strain naturally.
6. Place the coffeepot on the kitchen plate in a location where it cannot boil, as it cools naturally while filtering.
