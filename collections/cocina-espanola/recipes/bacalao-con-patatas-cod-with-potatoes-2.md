## Ingredients

- Soaked cod
- Flour
- Water
- Peeled potatoes
- Garlic, fried
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Bread crumb

## Directions

1. After soaking the cod and coating it in flour, fry it.
2. When golden, add water and peeled potatoes and let cook.
3. When most of the water has been consumed, stew with fried garlic, parsley, pepper and a bread crumb, bringing it to a boil.
