## Ingredients

- one and a half liters of water
- one piece of bread crust
- four turnips
- four potatoes of regular size
- parsley
- one ounce of beef butter

## Directions

1. Cook the bread crust, turnips, and potatoes in one and a half liters of water
2. Season, crumble everything and pass through a food mill
3. Add enough water to form a thick pap and let it cook very slowly for twenty minutes, stirring constantly with a spatula
4. Add a couple of tablespoons of very finely chopped parsley and one ounce of beef butter
5. As soon as this melts, and without allowing the soup to cook, remove it from the fire and serve
