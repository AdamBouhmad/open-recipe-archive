## Ingredients

- flour
- lard or fat

## Directions

1. Proceed as for the previous sauce, but taking care that the flour is not toasted, but rather binds while conserving its white color
2. This is obtained by adding more flour before the fat is smoking hot
3. This sauce serves in white stews and is used for binding in glazes
