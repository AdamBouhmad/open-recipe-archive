## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- broth
- red or white wine
- small onions
- lard
- flour
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue
2. Remove the skin and cut into wheels
3. Strain the broth in which it cooked and remove the fat
4. Place in a casserole with two glasses of red or white wine
5. Add small onions fried in lard with a bit of flour
6. Reduce the sauce to the right consistency
7. Place the tongue on pieces of fried bread
8. Pour the sauce over the tongue
