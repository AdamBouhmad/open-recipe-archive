## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of beef butter
- three tablespoons of flour
- one and a half quartillos of good concentrated broth

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a pan
2. Incorporate three tablespoons of flour and season without allowing it to take color
3. Add one and a half quartillos of good concentrated broth
4. Cook everything for a quarter hour
5. Sieve the soup as indicated for others, thicken it, and pour it into the tureen, where the corresponding garnish will already be placed
