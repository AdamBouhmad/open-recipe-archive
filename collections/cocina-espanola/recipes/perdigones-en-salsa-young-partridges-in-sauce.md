## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Testicles or sweetbreads
- Mushrooms
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Roast the young partridges
2. Remove the breastbone and chest bone
3. Cut the meat into pieces the size of dice, along with some testicles or sweetbreads and some mushrooms
4. Cook everything in a Spanish sauce, adding the pieces of young partridge only when the testicles and mushrooms are cooked
5. Keep them hot while preparing
6. At the moment of serving, place the mixture in the cavity of the young partridges with sauce
