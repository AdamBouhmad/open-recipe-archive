## Ingredients

- partridges
- small onions
- carrots
- turnips
- Brussels sprouts
- truffles
- spices
- parsley
- bay leaf
- mint
- thyme
- rosemary

## Directions

1. Bone the partridges after stewing and cut their meat into strips
2. Arrange in a pyramid on a platter with small onions, carrots, turnips, Brussels sprouts, truffles and an abundance of spices, parsley, bay leaf, mint, thyme and rosemary
