## Ingredients

- one quarter litre of water
- two ounces of coffee
- equal quantity of milk
- sugar
- a little flour powder
- five egg yolks

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, bring one quarter litre of water to a boil; when it boils vigorously, add two ounces of coffee and stir well with a spoon, putting it back over the fire until it increases in volume four or five times over the vessel.
2. Let it rest to settle clearly, without straining and mixing it with an equal quantity of milk and sugar, and cook everything again so that it reduces a little.
3. In a pan, dissolve very well with a little flour powder five egg yolks, which are stretched with the milk and coffee, until the mixture is perfect.
4. Then pass this through a sieve.
5. In a deep serving dish or in caramelized molds on the inside, set the cream to curdle in a water bath.
