## Ingredients

- Fresh green broad beans
- Salted water
- Pork lard
- Good chorizo
- Bacon
- Sweet pepper

## Directions

1. Shell fresh green broad beans until you have one liter
2. Remove the little black skin or nail at one end
3. Wash and cook in boiling salted water with rolling boil
4. When cooked, which is proven by seeing if they crush with finger pressure, place with part of their water in a pot with pork lard and appropriate seasoning
5. Separately, fry slices of good chorizo and strips of bacon
6. Incorporate into the beans with a little sweet pepper
7. Remove from heat at the first boil
8. Serve this succulent dish
