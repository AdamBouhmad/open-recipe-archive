## Ingredients

- chicken
- beef
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- hard boiled eggs
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Shred a chicken to convert all its meat into threads and scraps
2. With a quarter part of cooked beef make a minced mixture and prepare a thick sauce with it, seasoned extensively, oil and vinegar
3. Incorporate the chicken scraps gradually, stirring little by little each time they are added, while drizzling oil over them
4. When everything is finished, stir more and complete by adding more vinegar
5. Serve in the same salad bowl, garnishing with hard boiled eggs and lettuce leaves and hearts
