## Ingredients

- Hake
- Water
- Salt in grain

## Directions

1. Clean the hake and place it whole in a fish kettle filled with cold water
2. Put on the fire with a pinch of salt in grain, not to salt but to precipitate the gelatinous part of the fish
3. The moment the water begins to boil, remove the kettle from the fire and keep the hake there until serving or preparing it as prescribed
