## Ingredients

- hake
- salt
- lemon juice
- flour
- egg

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well and cut into slices
2. Sprinkle with salt and a few drops of lemon
3. Coat with flour
4. Fry in plenty of hot oil
5. Dip in well-beaten egg
6. Fry again
7. Remove and drain well
