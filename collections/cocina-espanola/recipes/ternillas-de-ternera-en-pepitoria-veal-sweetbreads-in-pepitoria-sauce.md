## Ingredients

- veal sweetbreads
- butter from cows or pork
- flour
- clean broth
- bundle of fine herbs
- onions the size of walnuts
- mushrooms
- salt and pepper
- parsley
- egg yolks
- water

## Directions

1. Brown the sweetbreads in cow or pork butter, being careful that it does not take on color.
2. Add a heaping tablespoon of flour and brown it without letting it toast.
3. Moisten with clean broth and add a bundle of fine herbs, a good handful of onions the size of walnuts, and an equal quantity of trimmed mushrooms.
4. Season with restraint and cook over low heat until the sweetbreads are very tender.
5. Sprinkle generously with finely chopped parsley.
6. Serve by binding the sauce away from the heat with two or three egg yolks dissolved in half a glass of water.
