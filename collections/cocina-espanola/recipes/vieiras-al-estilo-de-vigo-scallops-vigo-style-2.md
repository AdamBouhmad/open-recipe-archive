## Ingredients

- Large scallops, size of palm of hand
- parsley, minced
- garlic
- scallions
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- refined oil
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the scallop shell
2. Cut the meat and mix it with minced parsley, garlic, scallions, pepper, clove, and nutmeg
3. Fill the shell with this mixture
4. Pour one tablespoon of refined oil over the top
5. Cover everything with breadcrumbs
6. Roast the scallops in the oven at high heat for a quarter of an hour
