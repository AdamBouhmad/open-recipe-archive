## Ingredients

- oil
- parsley
- garlic
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- water
- hake
- lemon or orange juice

## Directions

1. Heat fried oil in a pot with parsley and minced garlic
2. Add salt and breadcrumbs, sauté briefly
3. Add water and the hake to cook
4. Once done, serve with a few drops of lemon or orange juice
