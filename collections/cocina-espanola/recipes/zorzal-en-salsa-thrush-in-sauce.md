## Ingredients

- Thrush, well fattened
- Piece of bacon
- Peppercorn
- Butter for frying
- Thrush livers and kidneys
- Broth
- Flour

## Directions

1. Clean the thrush well and place a piece of bacon and a peppercorn inside
2. Fry in good butter
3. Remove the thrush's livers and kidneys, smoke and salt them, then grind in a mortar
4. Dilute the resulting paste in a small amount of broth
5. Add a little flour
6. Pour the sauce over the cooked thrush
7. Cook over high heat for half an hour until the dish is delicate, flavorful, and nourishing
