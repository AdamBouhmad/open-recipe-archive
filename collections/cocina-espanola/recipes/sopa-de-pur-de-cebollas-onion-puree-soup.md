## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of onions
- quarter pound of cow butter
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- laurel
- 1 liter of cold water
- broth from the cooking pot
- pearl crackers

## Directions

1. Cut 2 pounds of onions well and sauté over high heat in a pan with a quarter pound of cow butter
2. Season during the sautéing with salt, a hint of pepper, thyme, and laurel
3. Before they take color, moisten with 1 liter of cold water
4. Let cook over low heat with the pan covered for five quarters of an hour
5. Pass through a vegetable mill and obtain a puree white as snow
6. Bind off the heat and serve by thinning with broth from the cooking pot and adding some pearl crackers
