## Ingredients

- chicken or mutton
- chickpeas (optional)
- ham (optional)
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the stew with chicken or mutton
2. If it is to be substantial, add some chickpeas and ham
3. The broth should be cooked before serving
4. If greater substance is desired, break up the chicken mixing it with bread crumbs and dilute the broth
5. Then strain it, season it, put it on the fire, but without letting it cook
