## Ingredients

- one beef chop, prepared and larded
- one quarter liter of broth
- one quarter liter of white wine or Sherry
- three hours cooking time over low heat
- macaroni
- broth from the stew pot
- grated Parmesan cheese, or equal parts Gruyère and Manchego cheese

## Directions

1. Cook one beef chop, prepared and larded but slightly more seasoned, in one quarter liter of broth mixed with one quarter liter of white wine or Sherry
2. After three hours of cooking over low heat, remove the pan from the heat
3. Pass the juice through a sieve and reduce by half
4. Separately, cook macaroni in broth from the stew pot and sprinkle generously with grated Parmesan cheese, or if not available, equal parts Gruyère and Manchego cheese
5. Place the macaroni on a serving platter and place the chop on top
6. Pour the reduced cooking juice over everything
