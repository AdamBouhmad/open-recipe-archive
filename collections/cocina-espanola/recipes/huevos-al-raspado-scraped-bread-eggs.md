## Ingredients

- breadcrumbs
- beef butter
- 1 anchovy
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- 3 egg yolks
- salt and pepper
- fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. In a cooking pot, combine scraped breadcrumbs with beef butter, finely chopped anchovy, parsley, onion, and garlic.
2. Mix everything together with 3 egg yolks over low heat.
3. Break the remaining eggs on top.
4. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and season with salt and pepper.
5. Surround with fried bread crusts.
