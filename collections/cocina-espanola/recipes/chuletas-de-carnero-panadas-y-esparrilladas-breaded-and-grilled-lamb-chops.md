## Ingredients

- Lamb chops
- Melted cow butter
- Bread crumbs
- Salt and pepper
- Potatoes
- Tomato sauce, thick

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops as described.
2. Oil them or coat both sides with melted cow butter.
3. Cover with bread crumbs and roast on the grill over not-too-strong heat, turning two or three times so they cook through.
4. Season with salt and pepper on one of the turns.
5. Serve in a crown with potatoes and a thick tomato sauce, well strained but without binding.
