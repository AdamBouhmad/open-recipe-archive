## Ingredients

- Three tablespoons of Marseille oil
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Onion
- Salt
- Pepper
- Clove
- Garlic
- Nutmeg
- Juice of half a lemon
- Two pounds of salmon
- Breadcrumbs
- White paper

## Directions

1. In a soup plate, put three tablespoons of Marseille oil and beat and mix well with finely chopped parsley, onion, salt, pepper, clove, garlic, nutmeg and the juice of half a lemon.
2. From a piece of two pounds of salmon, cut thin slices lengthwise like ham slices.
3. One by one, soak each slice in the mixture and wrap in breadcrumbs.
4. Place each slice in a sheet of good white paper, closed like a letter, and roast slowly over a grill, to serve on a napkin placed on a platter.
