## Ingredients

- woodcocks, plucked and cleaned
- pork belly
- parsley
- garlic
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Open the woodcocks underneath the tail sufficiently to extract the intestines and offal
2. Chop all the extracted parts with a piece of pork belly about half the amount of the woodcock offal, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper
3. Fill the woodcocks with this mixture
4. Cover them with thin slices of pork belly
5. Roast over low heat
