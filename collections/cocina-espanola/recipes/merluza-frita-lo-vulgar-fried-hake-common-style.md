## Ingredients

- hake pieces from the middle or loin
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. After washing and scaling the hake pieces from the middle or loin, remove the bones and skin while raw.
2. Cut into thin slices and flatten them with a spatula.
3. Fry the slices.
