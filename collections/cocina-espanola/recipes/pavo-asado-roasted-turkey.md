## Ingredients

- Young, tender, well-fattened turkey
- Bacon leaves
- Greased paper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place the turkey on the spit, larded with bacon leaves and wrapped in greased paper
2. Roast very slowly for a considerable time so that it cooks through completely
3. At halfway through cooking, remove the bacon and paper so the turkey takes on color
4. Sprinkle with salt only, continuously basting with its own fat
5. Serve very hot, natural, pouring the thoroughly defatted juices over it
