## Ingredients

- veal brains
- white roux-based sauce
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Blanch the brains in boiling water
2. Immediately submerge in very cold water
3. Cut each brain into three pieces
4. Cook for half an hour in a well-seasoned white roux-based sauce
5. Place the brains on a serving platter and keep warm near the fire
