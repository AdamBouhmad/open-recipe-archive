## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- water
- pepper
- salt
- thyme
- bay leaf
- spices
- parsley
- chervil
- onions
- carrots
- white sauce
- pickled cucumbers

## Directions

1. In a large pot where the leg can fit comfortably, boil a large quantity of water with pepper, salt, thyme, bay leaf, all manner of spices, parsley, chervil, onions and carrots.
2. When the water boils vigorously, place the leg in the pot, cover tightly and continue cooking vigorously without stopping for an instant.
3. Cook for as many quarter hours as the leg weighs in pounds. For example, if it weighs four pounds, cook for one hour.
4. Remove immediately from the pot and serve with white sauce and trimmed pickled cucumbers.
