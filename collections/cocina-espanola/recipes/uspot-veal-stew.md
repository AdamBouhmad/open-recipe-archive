## Ingredients

- Veal shanks
- Water
- Salt
- Bacon
- Onion
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Ginger
- Broth
- Six egg yolks
- White bread crumb

## Directions

1. Take veal shanks cut into pieces and boil them with water and salt
2. When they are half cooked, remove them and place them in a pan
3. Take bacon cut into cubes and fry it, and have a quantity of onion sliced lengthwise and fry it in the bacon, and add it to the pan over the meat, and season with pepper, nutmeg and ginger
4. Then add broth until it covers and cook slowly
5. For each plate, have six hard-boiled egg yolks and a crumb of white bread soaked in vinegar
