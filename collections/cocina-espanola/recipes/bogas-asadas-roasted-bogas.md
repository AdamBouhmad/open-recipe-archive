## Ingredients

- bogas (fish)
- butter
- rice flour
- fine herbs
- pickles
- olives
- white wine
- lemons

## Directions

1. Roast the fish on grills wrapped individually in paper, with the interior side spread with butter.
2. Serve with lemon halves on the platter for those who wish to squeeze the juice over them.
3. Prepare separately in a sauce boat a white sauce made with rice flour, butter, mixed and well-blended fine herbs, chopped pickles and olives, and sufficient white wine to make the sauce quite thick.
