## Ingredients

- some sorrel leaves
- a small piece of fried bread
- parsley
- garlic
- cumin
- broth from the pot
- bread or toasted flour for thickening
- pepper and clove (optional)

## Directions

1. Take some sorrel leaves, a small piece of fried bread, parsley, garlic and cumin
2. Pound everything in the mortar until well ground and made into a paste
3. Dissolve in broth from the pot and thicken with bread or toasted flour
4. Some also add pepper and clove to this sauce, and give the whole a few boils
