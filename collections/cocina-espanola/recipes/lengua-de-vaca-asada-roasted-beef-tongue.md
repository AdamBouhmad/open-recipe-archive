## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- lard or bacon for larding
- salt
- pepper
- sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue for at least four hours as previously described.
2. Remove the skin and lard it like a piece of sirloin.
3. Place it on the roasting spit until it is well done.
4. Serve this dish with a sauce seasoned with salt and pepper, mixed with the juice from the tongue, or else over a purée of potatoes or dried peas.
