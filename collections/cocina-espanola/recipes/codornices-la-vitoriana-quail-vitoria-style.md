## Ingredients

- quail
- small sticks or canes
- butter
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Clean the quail well and remove the legs, head, and wings.
2. With small sticks or canes, pierce through the thighs from side to side.
3. Place them in a cooking pot with butter and add broth or water.
4. Remove and split them lengthwise down the middle.
5. Remove the gizzards and fry them again.
