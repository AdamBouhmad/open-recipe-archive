## Ingredients

- green legumes of choice
- small slices of carrots
- onions
- butter
- fried bread pieces

## Directions

1. Cook any of these green legumes in broth, adding some small slices of carrots and onions and some butter
2. Once cooked, pound in a mortar and pass through a sieve
3. Pour this juice over pieces of fried bread, and once they are well soaked it can be served
4. If you wish the puree to keep its green color, you can add the juice of some mashed spinach
5. If these purees are to be used on fasting days, use fish or amphibian broth and oil
6. These purees can also be made from dried legumes by the same method, as well as from potatoes, sorrel, celery, or any other herbs or vegetables
