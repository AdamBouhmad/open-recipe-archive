## Ingredients

- veal liver
- olive oil
- fine herbs
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- white wine, one quartillo
- meat juice

## Directions

1. Cut the veal liver into pieces
2. Pour a few spoonfuls of olive oil into a saucepan
3. Add chopped fine herbs and finely chopped mushrooms
4. Place a layer of liver pieces on top
5. Moisten with more olive oil and sprinkle with fine herbs and mushrooms as before, seasoning heavily with salt and pepper
6. Continue alternating layers of liver and seasoning until all liver is used, finishing with a layer of mushrooms and fine herbs
7. Cook everything slowly over low heat with one quartillo of white wine in a tightly covered saucepan
8. When the liver is cooked, remove it from the saucepan and place the pieces on a serving platter
9. Pour the cooking liquid over it, reinforced with meat juice
