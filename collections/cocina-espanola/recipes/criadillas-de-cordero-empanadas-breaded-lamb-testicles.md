## Ingredients

- Lamb testicles
- Egg, beaten
- Salt and pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Pork lard for frying
- Lemon quarters
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Blanch the testicles with their membranes in boiling water until they whiten
2. Immediately submerge them in very cold water
3. Remove and cut into pieces like orange segments
4. Wrap them in beaten egg seasoned with salt and pepper
5. Coat with breadcrumbs, then dip again in egg and coat once more with breadcrumbs
6. Fry in pork lard
7. Serve in a pyramid on a well-pressed napkin on a platter with croutons and lemon quarters for each guest to squeeze juice as they eat
