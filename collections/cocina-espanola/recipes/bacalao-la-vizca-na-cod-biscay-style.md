## Ingredients

- Cod
- Tomatoes
- Onion
- Oil
- Pork lard
- Toasted bread crust
- Broth
- Choricero peppers
- Flour
- Salt

## Directions

1. After soaked and cut into pieces, give it a boil while roasting a few tomatoes on the grill
2. Remove the skin from the tomatoes and mash well with a wooden spoon on a plate
3. Chop much onion finely and put in oil to sauté
4. When still without taking color, add the tomato and finish sautéing
5. Place the cod in a pot with method and order, pour the tomato, onion and oil in which they were fried on top
6. Leave on low heat until the cod is well cooked
7. Take care to stir the pot frequently so the cod takes the sauce, and always do it on low heat
8. For the sauce, use half oil and half pork lard with sufficient chopped onion, and fry carefully; when the onion is golden, add a toasted bread crust and a little broth
9. Have about six choricero peppers cooked, with veins and dust removed beforehand; their flesh grated with a knife will be added to the fried onion
10. Add a tablespoon of flour and strain everything well through a sieve so that it remains a fairly thick sauce
11. Introduce the cod without large bones into it, and bringing it to a gentle boil, serve
