## Ingredients

- a handful of watercress
- equal quantity of purslane, chopped
- water
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Take a handful of watercress and equal quantity of chopped purslane
2. Squeeze out the juice from both plants well
3. Once extracted, place in a pan with water and bring to a boil
4. When you believe they are cooked, remove, drain, and mash them
5. Dilute with the desired sauce, adding a little spicy sauce
6. Strain through a fine sieve and serve
