## Ingredients

- Hen or beef
- Lean bacon
- Poultry as desired
- Pork loin
- Sausages
- Hare
- Blood sausages
- Beef tongue
- Pork feet
- Pork ears
- Cured beef
- Vegetables
- Parsley
- Mint
- Garlic
- Onions
- Rye flour for dark dough
- All spices

## Directions

1. Cook the hen or beef and a piece of lean bacon with all the poultry desired, pork loin, sausages, hare and blood sausages; this must be roasted before being put to cook
2. In another vessel, cook cured beef, beef tongues, pork feet and ears
3. From the broth of both pots, pour into a vessel and cook the vegetables, parsley, mint, garlic and onions there, which should be roasted beforehand
4. Remove all this in pieces, so that it is well divided, and let it cool
5. Make a thick bowl or mold of dark rye flour dough, and fill it with said meats, seasoning it with all spices
6. Once filled, put it in the oven, and when the dough is half cooked, make a hole in it and pour in the broth, letting it cook for one hour
