## Ingredients

- veal sweetbreads
- butter from cows or pork
- defatted broth
- bundle of fine herbs
- one liter of freshly shelled peas
- flour
- water or broth

## Directions

1. Cut the sweetbreads into small pieces and brown them in cow or pork butter.
2. Moisten with defatted broth, only as much as necessary so the sweetbreads can cook without sticking to the pan.
3. When almost cooked, add a bundle of fine herbs and one liter of freshly shelled peas.
4. Once the peas are cooked and consequently the meat as well, remove everything and let it drain in the strainer.
5. Reduce the sauce, which generally becomes quite thin with the juice of the peas, skim off the fat and add one tablespoon of flour well dissolved in water or broth from the pot.
6. When the sauce has taken on a certain consistency, pour it into a covered serving dish or vegetable bowl, in which the sweetbreads and peas should already be placed.
