## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. In small earthenware or porcelain ramekins with handles, place about a walnut of butter or half a tablespoon of oil in each.
2. When the fat is hot, add the egg and complete its cooking in the oven at moderate heat.
