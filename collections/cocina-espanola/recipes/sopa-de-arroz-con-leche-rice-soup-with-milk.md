## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt
- lemon zest
- milk
- orange flower water, one tablespoon

## Directions

1. Give the rice a couple of boils in water with a pinch of salt
2. Drain the rice well until no water remains
3. Finish cooking the rice in milk, which should have had the lemon zest infused in it
4. When serving, add one tablespoon of orange flower water
