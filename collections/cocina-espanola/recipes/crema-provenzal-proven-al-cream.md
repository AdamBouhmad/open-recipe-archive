## Ingredients

- White sauce base
- garlic
- small fragments of mushrooms
- fine recooked oil

## Directions

1. Rub the interior of a sauce boat with garlic
2. In the sauce, place very small fragments of mushrooms instead of capers and fine recooked oil instead of lard
3. This will yield the famous Provençal cream, very tasty for eating boiled cod after a good soaking
