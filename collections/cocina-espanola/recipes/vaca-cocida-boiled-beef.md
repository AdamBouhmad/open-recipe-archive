## Ingredients

- Beef from the hindquarters
- Bones
- Frameworks, feet and necks of birds and game
- Salt
- Legumes
- Garlic
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Choose preferably beef from the hindquarters or part of it, remove the bones, roll it and tie it with twine
2. Place it in a pot with the frameworks, feet and necks of birds and game used for appetizers
3. Skim well over high heat; once it has cooked a little, add salt, all varieties of legumes and garlic, to taste
4. Remove the beef when it is cooked through, being careful to withdraw it when properly cooked, although the broth may have less substance, because if you attend to the latter, the meat will come out shredded and overcooked
5. Serve it either surrounded by fresh parsley, chopped in sauce garnished with onions and legumes, or with pastries
