## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Oil
- Salt and pepper or other sauce

## Directions

1. After plucking, gutting, singeing, and turning the young partridges by the feet, open them from the tail to the crop
2. Flatten them with a mallet
3. Bathe them in an oil marinade
4. Place them on the grill over high heat
5. Serve with a pepper sauce or any other sauce
