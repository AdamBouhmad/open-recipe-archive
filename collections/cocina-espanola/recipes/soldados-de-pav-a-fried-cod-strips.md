## Ingredients

- Good and long strips of soaked cod
- Frying batter
- A pinch of saffron
- Plenty of oil

## Directions

1. Soak the cod strips
2. Clean them of skin and bones
3. Soak them in frying batter colored with a pinch of saffron
4. Fry in plenty of oil until very toasted and crispy
