## Ingredients

- 125 grams of truffles
- 125 grams of beef butter, divided into two equal parts
- 2 tablespoons of Spanish sauce
- 1 cup of concentrated broth
- 1 glass of Jerez wine

## Directions

1. Cut truffles into thin slices
2. With half of the butter, partially cook the truffles over low heat
3. Add the two tablespoons of Spanish sauce, the cup of concentrated broth, and the glass of Jerez
4. Let the sauce reduce over low heat
5. Remove grease from the sauce
6. At the moment of serving, incorporate the other half of the butter, which should melt away from the fire with the heat of the sauce while stirring gently
