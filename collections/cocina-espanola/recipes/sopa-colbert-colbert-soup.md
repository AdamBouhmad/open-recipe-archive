## Ingredients

- fresh vegetables
- thin slices instead of julienne cuts
- eggs, as many as there are diners

## Directions

1. Prepare a julienne soup but with fresh vegetables cut into thin slices instead of thin strips
2. Heat the soup until it is thick
3. When serving, add poached eggs to the soup tureen, one egg per diner
