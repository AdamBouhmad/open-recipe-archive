## Ingredients

- Duck, cleaned and prepared
- Butter or lard

## Directions

1. Spit the duck on a roasting spit after cleaning and preparing it.
2. Remove excess fat beforehand.
3. Roast slowly over the fire so that the remaining fat melts gently.
4. When cooking is nearly complete, increase the heat so the duck takes on color.
5. Serve on a layer of croutons soaked in its fat, with the roasting juice in a gravy boat, completely free of grease.
