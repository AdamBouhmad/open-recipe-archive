## Ingredients

- beef
- butter or oil
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- water
- spices

## Directions

1. Brown the meat in a pan or casserole with butter or oil, parsley, chopped onion and garlic
2. When the meat is half cooked, transfer it to a pot
3. Add a little water and let it cook
4. Sprinkle with spices dissolved in water
