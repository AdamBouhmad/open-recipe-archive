## Ingredients

- carp fillets
- tench fillets
- eel fillets
- salmon fillets
- carp roe
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- beef butter
- fine herbs
- carp skin
- fine new linen cloth
- water
- white wine

## Directions

1. With fillets of carp, tench, eel and salmon, make a hash that is mixed with carp roe, salt, pepper, clove, nutmeg, beef butter and fine herbs
2. Knead everything and give it the shape of ham, covering the surface with carp skin
3. Wrap it in fine, new linen cloth and sew it very tightly
4. Cook this newly made ham for one hour in water and white wine in equal parts, well seasoned and spiced in the liquid
5. When the dish is cold, unsew the wrapping and prepare it on a platter decorated with watercress and purslane
6. Cut it into slices like ham, and neither the appearance nor the taste of this Bourbon dish is disagreeable
