## Ingredients

- Lamb kidneys
- Beef butter
- Salt and pepper
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the kidneys the same way as for skewers, threading them on larding needles or small reeds
2. Remove them from the skewers for serving and place them on a very hot platter
3. On the platter, have a mixture of beef butter with salt, pepper and parsley
4. Pass and repass the kidneys over and through this mixture to coat them
5. Present at the table
