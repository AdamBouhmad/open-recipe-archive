## Ingredients

- macaroni
- salt
- water
- beef stock from the pot
- butter
- Parmesan cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Prepare a good stew, making sure it does not burn and that the sauce takes on a rather dark color
2. When necessary to add stock, use stock from the pot
3. Put the macaroni to blanch or cook with salt and water until they reach the degree of cooking most suitable, since some prefer them very soft and others not as much
4. After cooking, drain them in a sieve so they drain well
5. Then transfer them to a saucepan that has beef butter, which should be kept near the fire so it does not cool and the macaroni will be well coated
