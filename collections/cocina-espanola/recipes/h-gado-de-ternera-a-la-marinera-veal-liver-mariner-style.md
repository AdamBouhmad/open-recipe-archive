## Ingredients

- veal liver
- quarter kilogram of beef or pork butter
- flour
- finely chopped fine herbs
- red wine
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. In a quarter kilogram of beef or pork butter, sauté and brown the veal liver cut into thin, equal pieces like domino tiles.
2. Turn with a wooden spatula and dust with flour and two good spoonfuls of finely chopped fine herbs.
3. Moisten with a glass of red wine.
4. When the sauce has come to a boil two or three times, remove the liver pieces and place on a preheated serving platter.
5. Add to the remaining sauce a good handful of mushrooms that have been trimmed and cooked beforehand.
6. After this has simmered for three or four minutes, pour the entire sauce over the liver on the platter.
7. Serve hot.
