## Ingredients

- two quarts of very good concentrated broth
- about three tablespoons of very fine minced mixture made with rooster crests and kidneys, tender parts of chicken offal and well-blanched mushrooms
- truffles or earth truffles if available
- small pieces of artichoke hearts
- a little ham
- pitted olives
- one glass of good Jerez wine
- salt

## Directions

1. In a saucepan add two quarts of very good concentrated broth and about three tablespoons of very fine minced mixture made with rooster crests and kidneys, tender parts of chicken offal and well-blanched mushrooms
2. If there are truffles or earth truffles, add a bit of each if available
3. Add small pieces of artichoke hearts, a little ham, pitted olives, one glass of good Jerez wine and a touch of salt
4. Let it cook together
