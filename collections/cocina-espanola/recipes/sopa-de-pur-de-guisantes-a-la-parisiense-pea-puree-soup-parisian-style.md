## Ingredients

- one liter of dried peas
- four liters of pot broth
- beef butter
- three carrots
- three turnips
- three onions
- white part of one celery stalk

## Directions

1. Soak one liter of dried peas in water for twenty-four hours
2. After that time, drain them well and cook them in four liters of pot broth
3. In a separate pot, sauté three carrots, three turnips, and three onions, all cut into slices, in beef butter
4. When the vegetables have taken on color and the peas have cooked for half an hour, add the sautéed vegetables to the broth
5. Cook at medium heat for three hours
6. Remove the peas and reduce them to a puree
7. Mix the puree with the broth, adjusting the consistency with more or less liquid, or letting it reduce if too thin
8. Twenty minutes before finishing, cut the white part of a celery stalk into strips, sauté it in beef butter, and cook it in the pea soup for that time
9. Season according to taste after the soup is finished
10. Serve hot
