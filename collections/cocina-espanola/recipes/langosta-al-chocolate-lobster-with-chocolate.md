## Ingredients

- 1 lobster
- Fine clarified oil
- Onions, finely chopped
- Parsley
- Chervil
- Spices
- ½ ounce powdered chocolate
- Pot liquor broth
- 1 glass of Jerez wine

## Directions

1. In fine clarified oil, sauté or braise finely chopped onions, parsley, chervil and a dash of spices
2. Bind with half an ounce of powdered chocolate and lengthen the sauce with a little pot liquor broth, sprinkled with a glass of Jerez wine
3. Blanch and divide the lobster beforehand into small pieces, preserving each one its part of shell
4. Finish cooking over low heat, incorporating the lobster into the already prepared sauce, with fire on top of the pot lid
