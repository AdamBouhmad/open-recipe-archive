## Ingredients

- veal hand
- paste for frying made three hours in advance
- fried eggs
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the veal hand in the pot with the stew.
2. Debone and set aside.
3. The next day at lunch, cut into small pieces the size of walnuts.
4. Coat the pieces in the frying paste.
5. Fry them.
6. Serve accompanying fried eggs, or alone, with tomato sauce.
