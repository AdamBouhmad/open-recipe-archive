## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- minced poultry, ham or meat
- tomato pulp
- toasted breadcrumbs
- oil
- dried parsley
- seasoning

## Directions

1. Cut the tomatoes as in the previous recipe and hollow out a little.
2. Complete a filling with the pulp removed from them, made of minced poultry, ham or meat.
3. Fill the tomatoes making a small pile on top, and sprinkle generously with toasted breadcrumbs kneaded with oil, dried parsley powder, and good seasoning.
4. Do this already placing the tomatoes on the metal dish in which they are to be served, and place it in the oven or cover it with a dome cover with fire on top.
