## Ingredients

- 1.5 pounds syrup at small thread stage
- zest of 2 lemons
- juice of 8 lemons
- 0.5 quartillo water
- 3 egg whites
- three-quarters of a glass of rum

## Directions

1. Mix the syrup, lemon zest, lemon juice, and water
2. Strain and pour into the freezer to freeze
3. Beat the three egg whites until they form stiff peaks like snow
4. When serving, incorporate the egg whites together with three-quarters of a glass of rum
