## Ingredients

- pieces of cooked veal from the previous day
- poultry broth
- broth from the pot
- four egg yolks
- a little parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Take pieces of cooked veal from the previous day and cut into smaller pieces
2. Add poultry broth with broth from the pot
3. Put in a pan to boil
4. Add four egg yolks, a little parsley, and butter
5. Cook over low heat until the sauce is bound
