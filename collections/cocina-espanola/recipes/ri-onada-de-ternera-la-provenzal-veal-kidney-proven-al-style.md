## Ingredients

- cooked beef tongue, cooled
- veal kidney
- beef lard
- refined oil
- two or three onions
- two cups of broth
- salt and pepper
- two or three glasses of white wine
- mushroom trimmings

## Directions

1. Cool a cooked beef tongue prepared as in the spicy sauce formula and cut the thickest part into thin slices
2. Cut these slices into strips or shreds like bacon for larding
3. Lard the veal kidney prepared as in the previous formula with these strips and brown it well in beef lard
4. Separately, brown and color two or three finely cut onions very well in refined oil
5. Moisten with two cups of broth and strain through a fine sieve to moisten the veal with this sauce
6. Add good seasoning of salt and pepper, two or three glasses of white wine according to the volume of the kidney, and let everything cook very slowly for two hours
7. Pass the onions remaining in the sieve through a fine sieve to convert them into a purée
8. Cook mushroom trimmings in this purée very slowly so the sauce thickens gradually
9. Place this garnish on the meat and in the serving dish to complete the plate
