## Ingredients

- 12 celery stalks
- butter
- sugar
- broth
- half a quartillo of Spanish sauce
- cow butter
- poultry broth
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take 12 celery stalks, peel and wash them well to remove dirt
2. Blanch for 10 minutes
3. Refresh and cut into four parts
4. Sauté with butter and a little sugar
5. Cover with broth and cook well
6. Add half a quartillo of Spanish sauce
7. Pass through a sieve like all such soups, and when hot add a little cow butter and poultry broth with nutmeg
8. Serve
