## Ingredients

- Lamb chops
- Tarragon, chopped fine like snuff
- Salt and pepper
- Potato purée

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops as in the previous formula.
2. When turning them on the grill, sprinkle with tarragon chopped fine like snuff at the same time as salt and pepper.
3. Arrange the chops on their sides in a crown on a round platter with potato purée in the center.
