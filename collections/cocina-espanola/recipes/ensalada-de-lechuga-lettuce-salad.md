## Ingredients

- lettuce
- small onion
- mint
- oil
- salt
- vinegar
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Well washed and the white part chopped more or less finely to each one's taste, chop small onion and green mint; mix everything and dress with oil, salt, vinegar, garnishing with hard boiled eggs in slices
2. Whole hearts can also be presented at the table, and each person can chop them on their plate and dress them to their taste
