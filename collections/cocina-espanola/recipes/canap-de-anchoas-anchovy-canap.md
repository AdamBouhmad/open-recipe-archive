## Ingredients

- Sandwich bread slice
- Oil
- Parmesan or Manchego cheese, grated
- Twenty to thirty anchovies
- Milk

## Directions

1. Cut a slice of sandwich bread.
2. Fry it in oil and place it in a baking pan on a bed of grated Parmesan or Manchego cheese.
3. Place twenty to thirty anchovies soaked in milk solidly on the bread.
4. Pour fine oil over it.
5. Sprinkle with the same grated cheese.
6. Bake in the oven at medium heat for fifteen minutes.
