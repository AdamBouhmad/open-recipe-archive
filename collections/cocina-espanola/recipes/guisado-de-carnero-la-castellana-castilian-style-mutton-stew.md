## Ingredients

- mutton shoulder
- oil
- turnips
- butter
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onions
- cloves
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton shoulder into pieces and place in a pot with oil
2. Give it several turns over good heat until it takes on a good golden color, then remove and drain
3. Cut turnips into strips, pass them through the previous sauce and let them take color; remove and drain them as well
4. Make a roux with butter and flour, pass the mutton through it, thinning it with broth
5. Add salt, pepper, parsley, onions, cloves, bay leaf, and add the turnips
6. When the meat is almost cooked, remove the fat and finish cooking, letting everything boil slowly
7. If the sauce is too thin, let it thicken over the fire
8. Place in a serving dish, garnishing with the turnips, and serve
