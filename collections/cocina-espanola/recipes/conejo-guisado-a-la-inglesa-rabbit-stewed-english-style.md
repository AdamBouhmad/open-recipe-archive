## Ingredients

- rabbit
- bread crumbs moistened in milk
- parsley
- sage
- pepper
- chopped bacon
- beef marrow, salted
- bacon leaves
- white wine
- onion or lentil sauce or puree

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbit well.
2. Stuff it with bread crumbs moistened in milk, parsley, sage, pepper, chopped bacon, and salted beef marrow.
3. Sew and close the rabbit.
4. Cook in a fairly large pot on a bed of bacon leaves and white wine.
5. To serve, add an onion or lentil sauce or puree.
