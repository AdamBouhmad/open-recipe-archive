## Ingredients

- one small ham
- thyme
- basil
- veal slices
- generous white wine
- pot broth
- two lemons, peeled and cut into thin rounds
- fine herbs bouquet
- four onions
- two heads of garlic
- six or eight cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil

## Directions

1. Soak a small ham in plenty of water for at least twenty-four hours to desalt it
2. Cook it in water with a good handful of thyme and basil
3. Remove the ham and place it in a pot prepared with veal slices
4. Add generous white wine, pot broth, two peeled lemons cut into thin rounds, a bouquet of fine herbs, four onions, two heads of garlic, six or eight cloves, thyme, bay leaf, and basil
5. Let it cook well
6. Allow it to cool and serve with the aspic that has formed
