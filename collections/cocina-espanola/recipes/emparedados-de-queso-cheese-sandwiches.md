## Ingredients

- bread
- fresh Burgos cheese
- dry sherry wine
- eggs
- milk
- sugar
- lemon zest
- fine clarified oil

## Directions

1. Prepare sandwiches by spreading the bread with a thin layer of good fresh Burgos cheese
2. Lightly moisten with dry sherry
3. Coat in egg beaten with milk, sugar, and grated lemon zest
4. Fry until golden in fine clarified oil
5. Dust while hot with powdered sugar
6. Serve these sandwiches cold
