## Ingredients

- eggs
- ground almonds in equal quantity to eggs by weight
- sugar
- a pinch of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix well all ingredients
2. Work the mixture for a long time
3. Place in strong paper boxes
4. Bake in the oven with a white coating of sugar on top
