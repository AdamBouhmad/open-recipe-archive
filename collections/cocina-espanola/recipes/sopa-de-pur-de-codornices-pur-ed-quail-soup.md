## Ingredients

- 12 quails
- 8 nuts of cow butter
- good white wine, about half a quart
- 4 quail breasts
- bread crumb
- egg
- good broth

## Directions

1. Remove the breasts from a dozen quails
2. Fry them in cow butter for eight nuts worth of time
3. Then add good white wine, about half a quart, and let it reduce
4. With the other four breasts make a very fine forcemeat to form with bread crumb and egg some very small balls
5. Add good broth, and crush the breasts in a mortar
6. Mix everything together and serve
