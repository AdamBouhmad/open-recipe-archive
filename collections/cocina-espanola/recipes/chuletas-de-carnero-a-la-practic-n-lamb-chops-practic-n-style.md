## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- pork lard
- red peppers
- meat broth
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Brown and sauté the lamb chops for two minutes in a pan with pork lard, adding them one by one
2. Insert each browned chop into a whole red pepper that has been previously cooked in meat broth and trimmed at the stem
3. Drain each pepper well and with its chop inside, well seasoned, roast over strong heat on a grill or in the oven
4. Serve the stuffed peppers on a platter with tomato sauce
