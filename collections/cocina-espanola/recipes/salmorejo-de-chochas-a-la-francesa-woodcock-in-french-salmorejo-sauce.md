## Ingredients

- Three woodcocks
- Two shallots
- Two cloves
- Mixed herb bouquet
- Half bottle of red Bordeaux wine
- One liter of Spanish broth
- Fried bread pieces
- Stuffing

## Directions

1. Clean and roast three woodcocks.
2. Once cold, cut them into pieces and remove the skins, placing the pieces in a flat sauté pan.
3. Pound the frames and remaining meat and place in a saucepan with two shallots, two cloves, and a mixed herb bouquet.
4. Add half a bottle of red Bordeaux wine.
5. Reduce to half and add one liter of Spanish broth.
6. Cook very gently for half an hour beside the fire, taking care to skim and decrease.
7. Strain through a sieve and reduce the sauce until it coats the spoon.
8. Sauce the woodcocks in the sauté pan.
9. Arrange in the platter in pyramid form, sauce them, and garnish with pieces of fried bread spread with a filling.
