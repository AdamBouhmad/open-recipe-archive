## Ingredients

- roasted chicken, turkey or hen breasts, or good meat from rabbits, hares, partridges, or woodcocks, roasted or braised
- Spanish sauce
- two egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cut into very small and equal pieces the breasts of roasted chickens, turkeys or hens, or the good meat from roasted or braised rabbits, hares, partridges, or woodcocks.
2. Cook them in a Spanish sauce, which is thickened when serving with two dissolved egg yolks.
