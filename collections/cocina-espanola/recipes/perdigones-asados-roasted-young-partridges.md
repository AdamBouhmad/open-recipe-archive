## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Bacon, thin slice

## Directions

1. Truss the young partridges as you would chickens, wrapping them with a thin slice of bacon
2. Stretch the legs and secure them on the spit or roasting rod so they do not bend, following the figure shown for such birds on page 440
3. For red partridges, wrap the head and neck very well in thick wet paper so the feathers do not burn
4. Arrange the bird on a platter with invisible small canes that keep the head upright as if the bird were alive and the body hidden in undergrowth, which is simulated with watercress that completely envelops the roasted meat, well sprinkled with its own juice
