## Ingredients

- fine, firm, and slightly tart apples
- lemon juice
- water

## Directions

1. Choose fine, firm, and slightly tart apples.
2. Peel and cut them into quarters, remove the seeds and their compartments, and soak the pieces in very cold water acidulated with all the juice of one lemon, which is pressed perfectly.
3. Remove the apple pieces one by one, drain them and cut them across into very thin slices the thickness of coins, which greatly facilitates cooking.
4. Put them in a saucepan without water over medium fire, until they begin to liquefy.
