## Ingredients

- half a pound of ground sugar
- eight egg yolks
- a little anise
- fried oil or melted butter

## Directions

1. To half a pound of ground sugar, add eight egg yolks and a little anise
2. Beat until very white
3. Form the cakes
4. Cut into cubes and let them air dry
5. When the mass is a bit dry, moisten or sprinkle with fried oil or melted butter
6. Place in the oven until completely cooked
