## Ingredients

- beef
- poultry substance or other
- broth
- white wine

## Directions

1. Mince the meat very fine
2. Put it on the fire and add a little poultry substance or other, a little broth and white wine to moisten it
3. Leave it on the fire until it is done and serve it without further seasoning
