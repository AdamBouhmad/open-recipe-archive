## Ingredients

- Chocolate
- Amber at the proportion of 60 to 72 grams per pound of chocolate

## Directions

1. Prepare chocolate with amber in the specified proportion
2. Serve a quarter portion (cuartilla) of the mixture to provide restorative properties
