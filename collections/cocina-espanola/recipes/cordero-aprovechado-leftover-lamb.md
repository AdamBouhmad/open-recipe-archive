## Ingredients

- 125 grams beef butter
- pieces of roasted lamb
- 1 liter water or broth from the pot
- lamb head, well cooked
- lamb heart
- lamb liver
- lamb offal
- lamb feet and hands
- salt
- pepper
- bundle of fine herbs
- two onions
- one carrot

## Directions

1. Brown the 125 grams of beef butter to a deep color
2. Add pieces of roasted lamb, water or broth from the pot, the well-cooked lamb head, heart, liver, offal, feet and hands
3. Season with salt, pepper, a bundle of fine herbs, two onions and a carrot cut into strips
4. Cook for two hours
5. Serve with the head in the center, surrounded by all the offal
