## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- lard for larding
- strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut thin, large fillets from the pork loin, about the size of the palm of the hand
2. Lard or mechanically prepare them as you would fricandeau of veal
3. Soak them in strong vinegar for 24 hours
4. Drain them and thread them one beside the other, with their faces in contact, onto a spit
5. Secure the spit properly on the roasting rod so they roast well, presenting them to the fire on their edge
6. Baste them with their own juices as you turn them, and salt and pepper during the operation
7. Unthread the fillets and serve generally with potato purée or in their own juices, with an abundant finely chopped mixture of pickled gherkins
