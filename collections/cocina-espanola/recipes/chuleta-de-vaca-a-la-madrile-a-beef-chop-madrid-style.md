## Ingredients

- one beef chop, prepared and larded
- rice cooked plain
- cooking broth and juices from the chop

## Directions

1. Prepare and lard the chop as described for previous preparations
2. Cook it in the same manner as the Milanese style chop
3. Instead of macaroni, place plain cooked rice on the serving platter
4. Place the meat on top of the rice and pour its juice over everything
