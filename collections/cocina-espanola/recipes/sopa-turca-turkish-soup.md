## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of the best and freshest cow butter
- two tablespoons of corn starch
- half a pint of milk
- one liter of pot broth
- three hard-boiled eggs
- equal volume of beets
- double the volume of truffles in shavings or slivers
- one egg yolk

## Directions

1. For eight persons, melt a quarter pound of cow butter in a saucepan
2. Partially fry two tablespoons of corn starch in it
3. Moisten with half a pint of milk
4. Season and add spices, then extend with one liter of pot broth
5. Add three hard-boiled eggs cut into dice shape
6. Add an equal volume of beets, cut the same way
7. Add double the volume of truffles in shavings or slivers
8. At the moment of serving and off the heat, bind the soup with the egg yolk dissolved according to the ritual
