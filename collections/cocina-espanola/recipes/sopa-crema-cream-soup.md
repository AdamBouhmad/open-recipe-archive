## Ingredients

- half a liter of broth
- twelve egg yolks
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and whites very well separately
2. Then mix them and beat more in a pan
3. Away from the fire, pour the hot strained broth from the pot little by little over the mixture, with its natural seasoning
4. Cook in a bain-marie so it curdles like custard
5. Cook the eight remaining egg whites in a bain-marie in any vessel, and when they are hard, cut them so they result in very thin strips, imitating noodles
