## Ingredients

- six eggs
- one kilogram potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- mustard
- clove
- nutmeg
- parsley, finely chopped
- tarragon, finely chopped
- onion, finely chopped
- one tablespoon good vinegar
- six tablespoons oil
- six lettuce heads for the leaves

## Directions

1. Hard boil six eggs and steam one kilogram of potatoes
2. Two hours before eating, mash the eggs in a large salad bowl with salt, pepper, mustard, clove, nutmeg with parsley, tarragon and onion in sufficient quantity, mixing with one tablespoon of good vinegar
3. Add six tablespoons of oil, and in this thick sauce add the potatoes and mix, stirring for a long time
4. Cover everything with the leaves of six lettuce heads, well washed and dried
5. At the moment of eating, add vinegar that should be strong and in quantity half the amount of oil
6. Shake and mix well, stir vigorously and eat
