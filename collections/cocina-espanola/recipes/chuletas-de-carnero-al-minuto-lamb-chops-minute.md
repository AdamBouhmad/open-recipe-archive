## Ingredients

- Lamb chops
- Salt and pepper
- Potatoes, fried or golden in oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the chops by removing the skin and anything that cannot be eaten, except the main bone.
2. Beat them on both sides with a mallet or wooden spatula and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
3. Quickly sear over high heat on the grill, turning only once so the juices are not lost.
4. Serve with fried or golden potatoes, boiled in steam.
