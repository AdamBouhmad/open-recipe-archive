## Ingredients

- frog legs
- butter
- frying batter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Before placing the frog legs in the pot, they may be passed through hot butter; forty legs are then sufficient for a soup for six people
2. Alternatively, soak the frog legs in a frying batter, fry them and serve with fried parsley
