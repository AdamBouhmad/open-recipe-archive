## Ingredients

- four quartillos of good fresh cream
- one spoonful of gum tragacanth powder
- powdered sugar
- two small glasses of maraschino, rum, or another good liqueur

## Directions

1. Pour four quartillos of good fresh cream into a porcelain bowl with one spoonful of gum tragacanth powder.
2. Place the bowl over crushed ice and beat the cream well with a wire whisk.
3. As thick, compact foam forms, remove it with a skimmer and place it in a sieve to drain, continuing this same operation until no more foam remains.
4. After it has drained well, place it in another clean bowl.
5. Add the powdered sugar as needed.
6. Before serving at the table, add two small glasses of maraschino, rum, or any other good liqueur.
7. This cream is placed on a serving dish, over a fine pastry tart or in a timbal formed with sponge cakes.
8. It can also be flavored with vanilla by grinding it with piloncillo sugar; with chocolate by dissolving one ounce in half a glass of boiling water; with orange or lemon by scraping the zests.
