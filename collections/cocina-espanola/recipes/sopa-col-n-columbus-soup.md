## Ingredients

- 1 pound of lamb
- pork butter
- onion, finely chopped
- 8 or 9 potatoes
- half a pound of rice
- spearmint, chopped
- oregano
- salt
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Cut 1 pound of lamb into small pieces
2. Sauté in pork butter with a little finely chopped onion
3. In a large pot bring water to boil and incorporate the fried meat
4. Add 8 or 9 potatoes cut in pieces, half a pound of rice, a little chopped spearmint, a pinch of oregano, and salt
5. Skim off foam
6. When about to serve, beat 2 eggs well with their whites and introduce them into the pot
7. The result should be brothy and served as soup in soup bowls
