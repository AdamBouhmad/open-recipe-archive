## Ingredients

- endive
- tomato
- oil
- cumin
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- garlic

## Directions

1. Chop the tender and white part, mash tomato on a plate with oil, cumin, salt, vinegar and water or garlic if desired; mix everything with the endive, and it can also be dressed only with oil and vinegar and a hint of garlic
