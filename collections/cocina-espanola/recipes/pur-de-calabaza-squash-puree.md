## Ingredients

- squash pulp, in pieces
- goat's milk
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the squash pulp in pieces in good goat's milk with a bit of salt.
2. Pass it through a sieve.
3. Serve it seasoned outside the heat with butter.
