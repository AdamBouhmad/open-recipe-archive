## Ingredients

- leftover cooked or fried salt cod
- two tablespoons of oil
- two tablespoons of flour
- half a quartillo of broth, milk, or water
- salt and seasoning
- parsley
- one egg white

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and bones from the leftover salt cod and chop it very finely.
2. Heat two tablespoons of oil in a saucepan until golden, then add two tablespoons of flour.
3. Stir well, and before the flour takes color, add half a quartillo of broth, milk, or water.
4. Season and add chopped parsley.
5. Add the finely chopped salt cod to the saucepan and remove from heat.
6. Knead the mixture and incorporate a beaten egg white whipped into meringue.
7. Let cool and form into small balls or croquettes.
8. Bread them well and fry in oil at the moment of serving.
