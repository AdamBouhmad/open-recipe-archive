## Ingredients

- large scallops
- parsley, chopped
- garlic
- scallions
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- refined oil
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Select large scallops about the size of the palm of a hand
2. Remove the meat from the shell and cut it
3. Mix with a hash of parsley, garlic, scallions, pepper, clove and nutmeg
4. Fill the shell with this mixture
5. Pour a spoonful of refined oil over the top
6. Cover everything with bread crumbs
7. Roast the scallops in the oven at high heat for a quarter of an hour
