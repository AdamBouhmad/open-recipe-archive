## Ingredients

- Fresh sardines
- Sea water
- Oil

## Directions

1. Remove sardines from the nets and wash them in sea water.
2. Soak the sardines in oil in a pot so they absorb the oil.
3. Thread them by batches onto long sticks or reeds.
4. Hold the sticks by one end in your hand while toasting them over the flame of torches burning on a grill placed outside the boat and fixed at the bow.
5. Alternatively, when buying recently caught sardines, roast them at home in the same manner with sticks in the hearth, but first wash them in sea water and soak them in oil.
