## Ingredients

- ham
- plenty of water
- bottle of Jerez wine
- rice
- carrots
- lettuce
- onions
- green beans
- paper for decoration

## Directions

1. Perfect and desalt the ham for two days
2. Wrap it in white linen and place it in a brasier with plenty of water
3. Let it boil with very gentle heat until completely cooked
4. When the ham is cooked, drain and trim it
5. Place it in another brasier with a bottle of Jerez wine
6. Let it boil very gently for half an hour with the brasier well covered
7. Remove the ham and glaze it
8. Form a base of rice in the bottom of the serving platter, lower than the thickness of the ham, about four centimeters; place the ham on the base and arrange small bunches of carrots, lettuce, onions, and green beans around it
9. Decorate the ham bone with a bow of curled paper
