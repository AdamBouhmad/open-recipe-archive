## Ingredients

- veal kidney
- beef lard
- one liter of broth
- parsley bouquet
- celery sprig
- salt and pepper
- one hundred twenty-five grams of rice
- one liter of freshly shelled peas

## Directions

1. Brown and color a veal kidney in beef lard and moisten with one liter of broth, adding a bouquet of parsley, a sprig of celery and seasoning generously with salt and pepper
2. After one hour of cooking, set aside half the broth and half-cook one hundred twenty-five grams of rice in it
3. Incorporate the rice with the meat along with one liter of freshly shelled peas, and let everything cook until the rice and peas are done
4. Place the meat on a serving dish that can go to the fire, on top of the rice and peas well mixed together, and put it in the oven with the door open, or leave it near the heat so it does not cool while the decanted and reduced sauce takes on enough consistency by cooking a while
5. Pour the sauce over the dish when serving
