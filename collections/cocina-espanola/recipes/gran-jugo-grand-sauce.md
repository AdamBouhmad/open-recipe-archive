## Ingredients

- a good layer of butter from cows
- half a pound of bacon
- one pound of ham
- two pounds of beef
- concentrated broth

## Directions

1. On the bottom of a saucepan of considerable capacity, place a good layer of butter from cows, and on top half a pound of bacon and one pound of ham, trimmed into very thin slices, with two pounds of beef, cut into slices half an inch thick.
2. Place the saucepan over high heat, let the meats stick a little but without burning, and wet with a small ladle of concentrated broth.
3. Prick the slices of beef so they retain none of their juice.
