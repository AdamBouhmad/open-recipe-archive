## Ingredients

- 125 grams of bacon scraps
- butter
- four squabs
- mushrooms
- bouquet of fresh herbs
- salt
- pepper
- two ladles of broth
- blond-white sauce
- cooking juice from the squabs

## Directions

1. Sauté the bacon scraps in butter, then quickly brown and sear the four squabs in the same pan, turning frequently to color evenly
2. Add sliced mushrooms, a bouquet of fresh herbs, salt, pepper, and two ladles of broth
3. Cook over low heat until the squabs are well done
4. Remove the squabs from the pan and place on a preheated serving dish
5. Arrange the mushrooms and bacon scraps around them
6. Prepare a blond-white sauce and thin it with the cooking juices from the squabs
7. Pour the sauce over the birds and serve immediately
