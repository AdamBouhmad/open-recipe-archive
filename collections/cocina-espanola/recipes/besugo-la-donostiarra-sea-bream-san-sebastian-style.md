## Ingredients

- Sea bream
- Salt
- Raw oil
- Capon feather
- Very hot oil with garlic
- Lemon drops

## Directions

1. Clean the sea bream very carefully, sprinkle it with salt and leave it hanging in a fresh and suitable place
2. One hour before presenting it to the table, place the fish on grills under which live oak charcoal fire burns
3. Take the feather of a capon, wet it well in raw oil and gently coat the sea bream with it, turning it several times until the skin is well toasted
4. When the moment comes to serve it, open the fish and sprinkle it with very hot oil with garlic and a few drops of lemon
