## Ingredients

- Fresh cream
- Sugar molded, one quarter of the weight of the cream

## Directions

1. Mix fresh cream with the fourth part of its weight of molded sugar
2. Serve as is without further preparation in a porcelain compote dish, or better and more elegantly, in a shallow bowl that fits well in a silver cup or other metal vessel
