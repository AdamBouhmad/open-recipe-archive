## Ingredients

- bread cut into equal small squares
- sugar
- milk
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Toast equal squares of bread sprinkled with sugar on both sides.
2. Place them in the tureen and pour over them very hot, sweetened milk.
3. Dissolve egg yolks in a little milk in the proportion of two yolks per liter of milk used.
4. Add this binding at the moment of serving, but the soup should not be very hot then, as it would curdle.
