## Ingredients

- twelve fresh egg yolks
- four glasses of Marsala or Sherry wine
- six ounces of sugar
- one handful of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take twelve fresh egg yolks and four glasses of Marsala or Sherry wine, six ounces of sugar and one handful of cinnamon; place everything in a saucepan over a lively fire, stir quickly with a chocolate whisk until the foam has filled the saucepan; serve immediately in cream cups.
