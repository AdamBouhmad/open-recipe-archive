## Ingredients

- bread, finely sliced
- salt
- sugar to taste
- milk, two liters
- sugar, 300 grams
- lemon zest, whole
- bay leaves, three to four
- cinnamon stick, one small piece
- egg yolks, six

## Directions

1. In one pot, cook two liters of milk with 300 grams of sugar, the whole zest of a lemon, three or four bay leaves, and a small stick of cinnamon
2. In another pot, away from heat, dissolve six egg yolks in the third part of the cooked milk
3. Place this mixture over low heat, stirring constantly until it thickens slightly
4. Pour the rest of the milk into a soup tureen with the finely sliced bread
5. At the moment of serving, incorporate the thickened milk and egg mixture
