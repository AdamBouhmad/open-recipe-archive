## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Oil
- Ham
- Poultry breast
- Breadcrumb paste
- Dried parsley
- Garlic
- Broth

## Directions

1. Soak the artichokes in oil before cooking
2. Cook the artichokes
3. Soak them again in the same oil in which they were first soaked
4. After a while, remove them and with a wooden spoon and fingers, hollow out each artichoke by opening the leaves
5. Fill with a chopped mixture, generally of ham and poultry breast
6. Close the artichoke and tie it with twine to keep the filling inside during cooking, as the artichoke tends to open naturally
7. Pour a little oil over the filling and sprinkle breadcrumb paste mixed with dried parsley and small pieces of garlic on top
8. Cook in the oven if you prefer them dried out, but it is better to simmer them for a quarter hour over low heat with a few spoonfuls of broth in a braising pan with strong heat on top of the lid
