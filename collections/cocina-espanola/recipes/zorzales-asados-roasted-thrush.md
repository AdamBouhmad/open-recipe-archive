## Ingredients

- thrush
- thin sheets of bacon, very thin

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the thrush very well.
2. Open them not to remove the innards, but only the crop or gizzard.
3. Make a hole below one leg, press the bird, and the crop will come out on its own.
4. Wrap with thin sheets of bacon, but very thin.
5. Thread them by half-dozens, inserting a thin spit rod between the wing and the leg, in such a way that the bacon sheets do not come loose.
6. Fix the rod to the spit so it does not move, and roast at high heat for twenty minutes.
