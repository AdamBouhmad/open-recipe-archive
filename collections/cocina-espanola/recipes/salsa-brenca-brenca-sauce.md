## Ingredients

- poultry of any kind such as chickens, squabs, partridges
- onions
- beef butter
- white wine
- spices
- salt
- lemon juice
- fried bread slices

## Directions

1. Roast poultry of any kind, such as chickens, squabs, partridges, etc., and cut into quarters.
2. Cut onions into strips and fry in beef butter.
3. Generally combine with the roast, reheating everything very well.
4. Add a little white wine, spices, salt and lemon juice.
5. Serve this sauce with slices of fried bread.
