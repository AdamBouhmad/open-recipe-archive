## Ingredients

- one beefsteak prepared as the first English-style
- anchovy butter
- potatoes
- beef butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the beefsteak as the first English-style beefsteak, and instead of placing in the center of the platter of beefsteaks the beef butter seasoned à la mayordoma, place the anchovy butter whose formula has already been given in the sauces section.
2. The beefsteak thus seasoned must have as an essential garnish potatoes sautéed and browned in beef butter.
