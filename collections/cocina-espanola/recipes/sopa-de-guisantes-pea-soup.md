## Ingredients

- half a liter of fine, fresh peas
- a bouquet of fine herbs
- two or three small onions
- salt
- pepper
- optional: a handful of finely chopped mint leaves

## Directions

1. Cook half a liter of fine, fresh peas with a bouquet of fine herbs and two or three small onions.
2. When the soup is cooking, incorporate the whole peas, which should be very cooked.
3. Strain the prepared and filtered broth, carefully removing all the fat that rises to the surface.
4. Return the broth to the fire and season to taste with salt and pepper.
5. Pour the strained soup into the tureen where the peas have already been prepared.
