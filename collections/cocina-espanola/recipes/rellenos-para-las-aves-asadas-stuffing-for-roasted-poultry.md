## Ingredients

- sausage meat
- poultry liver
- two egg yolks
- thirty to forty roasted chestnuts
- crumb of half a roll
- broth from the pot
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare by chopping and mixing the sausage meat with the liver of the bird to be stuffed, in quantity proportioned to the volume of the bird, two egg yolks, thirty or forty roasted chestnuts so they do not fall apart when the whole cooks, the crumb of half a roll soaked in broth from the pot, and the appropriate seasoning of salt and pepper.
2. When the bird is a large turkey or medium-sized duck, the raw liver can be chopped and mixed with the stuffing, but if the bird is small, the liver should be half-cooked beforehand, because the roasting is not taking place long enough for the liver to cook completely as it should.
3. This stuffing can be rectified and improved by the following method: half-cook the liver and chop it very finely, then mix it with scrapings of truffles well seasoned with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg.
