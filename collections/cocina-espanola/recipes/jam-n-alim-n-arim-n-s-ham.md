## Ingredients

- oil, well burned
- onion scraps
- modest piece of ham cut into small pieces
- one tablespoon of flour
- 350 grams of chickpeas
- one cabbage
- water
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. In a casserole, pour some well-burned oil, some onion scraps and a modest piece of ham cut into small pieces
2. Put everything on the fire to fry, adding one tablespoon of flour right away
3. Before or after, as you wish, have boiled and cooked 350 grams of chickpeas and one cabbage
4. Unite the cooked chickpeas and cabbage with their water to the ingredients in the casserole
5. Add pepper and salt
6. Cook for half an hour over low heat
