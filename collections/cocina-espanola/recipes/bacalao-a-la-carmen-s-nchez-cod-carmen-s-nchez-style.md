## Ingredients

- Best Scottish cod
- Fine oil, fried
- Toasted bread crumbs
- Parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Soak the finest Scottish cod available.
2. After six hours, clean of skin and bones and separate into leaves.
3. In thin strips of fish, grease with fine fried oil the entire interior of a terrine or silver serving dish, sprinkling it with toasted bread crumbs, and place the pieces in layers to cover the surface with one row.
4. Cover this lightly with oil and bread crumbs mixed with a little finely chopped parsley.
5. Place another row of cod and more oil and bread crumbs and parsley, and so on successively, until this kind of cake rises two or three inches according to the number of diners.
6. Cover the surface of the pastry well with bread crumbs alone, soaked slightly in oil, and place in the oven at medium heat.
