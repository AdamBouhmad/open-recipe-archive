## Ingredients

- Peas
- Salted water
- Fresh butter from cows
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Submerge the peas in boiling salted water
2. Remove and drain them to place on a serving platter
3. Make a hollow in the center and place a piece of fresh butter from cows, kneaded with chopped parsley
4. Cover and wrap the butter with the spatula, as the heat of the peas will melt it
5. Serve immediately
