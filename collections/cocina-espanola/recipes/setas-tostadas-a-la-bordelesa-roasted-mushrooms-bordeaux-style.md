## Ingredients

- large, open mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- fresh herbs

## Directions

1. Select large and open mushrooms.
2. Clean them and remove the stem.
3. Place them on a grill with the concave part facing up.
4. Fill the hollows with butter, salt, pepper, minced garlic, and finely chopped fresh herbs.
5. Let them roast well and toast on the convex side.
6. Serve on a platter in which butter has been melted.
