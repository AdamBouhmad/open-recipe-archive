## Ingredients

- Long tender squash
- Butter or oil
- Onion
- Chickpea broth or water
- Spices
- Milk
- Flour
- Saffron
- Celery
- Sugar and cinnamon (optional)
- Bread slices
- Torrijas (fried batter)

## Directions

1. Take long tender squash, scrape the rind, cut into small pieces and blanch in water and salt
2. Put some butter or oil with onion in a casserole and sauté the squash slightly
3. Add chickpea broth or hot water to cover it, or chopped greens
4. Season with all spices and a little milk
5. Take some butter and heat it in a pan; once hot, add a little flour, frying it so it does not turn black or thick in the pan
6. Sprinkle the squash dish with saffron and celery to avoid setting it with eggs
7. If desired, sugar and cinnamon can be added
8. Serve over bread slices and garnish with torrijas
