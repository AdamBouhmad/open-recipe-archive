## Ingredients

- 2 pounds macaroni, thin as a pencil
- salt
- 2 pounds tomatoes
- clarified oil
- raw tomato halves

## Directions

1. Purchase macaroni as thin as a pencil.
2. Half-cook two pounds of macaroni by dropping them into a large quantity of boiling water, seasoning with salt.
3. Remove from the pot or saucepan and place in a colander to drain the water.
4. In a very clarified oil and in a skillet, fry two pounds of tomatoes; season and pass through a fine sieve to obtain a thick broth.
5. Place the macaroni in a deep, round serving dish.
6. Pour the tomato sauce over and cover the entire surface with halves of raw tomatoes, upon which drop some raw oil.
7. Place the dish in the oven under high heat for fifteen minutes and serve.
