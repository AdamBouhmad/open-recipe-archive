## Ingredients

- veal chops with more meat
- thin strips of ham
- butter
- broth
- fried bread cubes

## Directions

1. Cut leaving more meat, and after larding them finely with very thin strips of ham using larding needles, pass them through butter to brown them a little.
2. Let them drain of the butter, and when cold arrange the ends and place in a pan with a little broth.
3. Give them a couple of boilings and dress with fried bread cubes, which is how they are served.
