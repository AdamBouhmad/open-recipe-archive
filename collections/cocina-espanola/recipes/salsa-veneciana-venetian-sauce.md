## Ingredients

- six quartillos of white poultry broth
- two ounces of beef butter
- flour
- nutmeg, grated
- three egg yolks
- beef butter the size of a walnut
- lemon juice
- eight or ten tarragon leaves

## Directions

1. Reduce six quartillos of white poultry broth to half over good heat
2. Prepare a roux made with two ounces of beef butter and the same amount of flour, stirring it for five minutes so it does not take color, and mix the said broth with it
3. Move it to the side of the stove so it cooks slowly for the space of one hour, seasoning it with a little grated nutmeg
4. After this time, degrease it and bind with three egg yolks beaten with a piece of beef butter the size of a walnut and lemon juice
5. Strain through cheesecloth, putting it in a bain-marie to heat it
6. At the moment of serving, blanch eight or ten tarragon leaves in boiling water, trim them and add them to the sauce
