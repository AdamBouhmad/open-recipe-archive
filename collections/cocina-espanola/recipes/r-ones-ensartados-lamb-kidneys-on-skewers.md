## Ingredients

- Six lamb kidneys
- Beef butter, melted
- Breadcrumbs
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Remove the membrane from the kidneys
2. Cut each kidney lengthwise along the outer curved line without completely separating the two halves so they form a single piece when opened
3. Thread three kidneys onto each skewer, piercing through both halves of each kidney
4. Soak the prepared kidneys in melted beef butter that has not boiled
5. Coat with breadcrumbs mixed with salt and pepper
6. Place the kidneys on live coals on a grill and turn twice to cook through
7. If using reed skewers or larding needles, remove the kidneys before serving
8. If using presentable skewers, serve the kidneys still threaded on the skewers, each one being one guest's portion, accompanied by a piece of lemon to eat the kidneys plain
