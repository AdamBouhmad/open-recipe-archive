## Ingredients

- veal chops
- butter or oil

## Directions

1. Allow the piece of meat from which the chops are to be cut to age, then remove the large bone from the end, the skin, the tendons, and the fat if desired.
2. Flatten with a mallet or iron or wooden plank, and round them with a knife, cleaning the bone or rib of the muscular meat, leaving only the end bare for easy gripping.
3. Roast on the grills a little, then stew in a pan with butter or oil, as preferred and convenient, or leave them on the grills so they reach their proper point of cooking and can be presented at the table.
