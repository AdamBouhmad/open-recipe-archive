## Ingredients

- thin slices of raw ham
- bread slices without crust, passed through butter
- fine herbs
- mushrooms
- truffles or earth truffles
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. In a casserole or pot, place thin slices of raw ham and bread slices (without crust) that have been passed through butter, along with fine herbs, mushrooms, and truffles or earth truffles, arranged alternately with the ham
2. Sprinkle everything with breadcrumbs
3. Bake in the oven for two or three hours to serve hot, or better yet, place it over hot coals with fire on top in a suitable pot, very common in Austrian kitchens
