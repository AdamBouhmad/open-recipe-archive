## Ingredients

- one piece of beef butter the size of an egg
- one teaspoon of curry
- nutmeg
- two spoonfuls of flour
- two ladfuls of water or broth
- another piece of beef butter

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a pan and melt it over gentle heat, stirring constantly
2. Add the curry and nutmeg
3. Add the flour and stir
4. Without stopping stirring, add two ladfuls of water or broth
5. Let cook for a quarter hour
6. At the moment of serving, bind with another piece of beef butter, which should melt with the heat of the sauce and away from the fire in the pan
