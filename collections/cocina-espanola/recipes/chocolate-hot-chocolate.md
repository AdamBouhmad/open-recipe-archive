## Ingredients

- One and a half ounces of chocolate
- Water or milk

## Directions

1. Break the chocolate into small pieces
2. Dissolve it in boiled water or milk in a saucepan
3. Heat gradually while stirring constantly with a wooden spatula
4. Let it cook over low heat for a quarter hour so that the mixture acquires consistency
5. Serve very hot
