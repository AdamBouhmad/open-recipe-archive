## Ingredients

- eggs
- one cup of milk per pair of eggs
- a small amount of flour

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs, and when they are well beaten, pour into them one cup of milk for each pair of eggs.
2. With a little flour beat again, and immediately pour everything into the pan, making a French omelet, which results in the smoothest and most exquisite of omelets.
