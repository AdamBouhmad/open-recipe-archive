## Ingredients

- One woodcock
- Bacon leaves, thin
- Twine or string
- Woodcock giblets
- 60 grams of beef lard
- A pair of truffles
- Croutons
- Watercress

## Directions

1. Pluck and singe the woodcock.
2. Clean and truss it, passing the beak through the body.
3. Remove the giblets by opening along the back and extracting the intestines.
4. Mince the giblets and mix with 60 grams of beef lard and trimmed truffles.
5. Stuff the woodcock body with this mixture.
6. Cover the woodcock with thin bacon leaves, securing them with twine according to the illustration.
7. Roast over gentle, even, and prolonged heat so that the abundant meat of the bird cooks through well.
8. Remove the bacon and twine at the moment of serving.
9. Serve with fried croutons soaked in the juices and plenty of watercress on the platter.
