## Ingredients

- veal liver
- bacon strips
- refined olive oil
- fine herbs
- onion slices
- salt
- pepper
- greased paper

## Directions

1. Lard a good veal liver generously with strips of bacon
2. With a sharp knife, cut the liver into slices one finger thick
3. Soak the slices for two hours in refined olive oil beaten with fine herbs, onion slices, salt and pepper
4. Wrap each liver slice individually in sheets of greased paper
5. Place on a grill over gentle heat for at least half an hour, turning the papillotes from time to time
