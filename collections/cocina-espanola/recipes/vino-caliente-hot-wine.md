## Ingredients

- 2 liters red wine
- 5 grams cinnamon stick
- 2 cloves
- zest of 2 sour oranges
- 250 grams piloncillo sugar

## Directions

1. For twenty-four hours, steep the cinnamon stick, cloves, and orange zest in the red wine
2. Strain the wine through a coffee filter
3. Put over gentle heat in a pan with the piloncillo sugar
4. When the wine is about to boil, pour it into a punch bowl
5. Drink it boiling hot, as it becomes unpleasant and cloying when lukewarm or cold
