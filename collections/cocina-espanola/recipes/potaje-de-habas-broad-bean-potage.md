## Ingredients

- Broad beans
- Oil
- Lettuce
- Spices
- Herbs, especially cilantro
- Salt
- Hot water
- Vinegar
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Shell the broad beans, ensuring they are tender
2. Put oil in a casserole or earthenware pot and place it over coals
3. When hot, add the broad beans, having prepared ahead washed and shredded lettuce as finely as possible without using a knife
4. Squeeze out the water and mix with the beans so both vegetables sauté together
5. The beans will release enough water to serve as broth
6. Apply all spices and herbs, especially green cilantro
7. Season with salt, add hot water and vinegar
8. Cook until everything is softened, finally adding raw eggs so they set curled with the beans
