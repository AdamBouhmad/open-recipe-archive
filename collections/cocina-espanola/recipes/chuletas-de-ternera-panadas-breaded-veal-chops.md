## Ingredients

- grated bread
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs, finely chopped
- veal chops
- melted butter or lard or refined oil

## Directions

1. Grate the bread and mix well with salt, pepper, and finely chopped fine herbs.
2. Prepare the chops and soak them in melted butter, lard, or oil as if they were to be prepared plain, so that the grated bread adheres perfectly to the meat.
3. Grill on the grill and serve alone or with the garnish that best suits.
