## Ingredients

- beef butter
- saffron
- chili pepper
- broth
- sauce

## Directions

1. Put beef butter in a saucepan with a little saffron and well-crushed chili pepper, and place over fire to fry a little.
2. Add a little broth and the same amount of sauce, and let reduce until it reaches a regular consistency.
3. Strain through cheesecloth and place by the fire.
4. When about to serve, add a little more beef butter.
