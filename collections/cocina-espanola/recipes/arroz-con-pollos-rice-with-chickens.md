## Ingredients

- chickens
- lard
- garlic cloves
- tomato
- parsley
- artichokes
- peas
- red pepper
- pepper
- clove
- saffron
- rice
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean and cut three or four chickens into pieces
2. Fry them well in a pan or clay pot with lard
3. Add one or two garlic cloves, tomato, parsley, artichokes and peas, stewing everything well
4. Incorporate a little red pepper, pepper and clove and a hint of saffron
5. Add the rice and after two turns cover well with broth and cook over high heat following the previous method
6. This can be made with hens, partridges, etc., but when using tough birds or meats, add the rice only after they are very well cooked
