## Ingredients

- one young duck
- two slices of bacon
- 125 grams of lean veal
- 125 grams of ham, cut into small cubes
- carrots
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- bouquet of fresh herbs
- one bowl of clear broth

## Directions

1. Cook the young duck in a casserole between two slices of bacon, one underneath and one on top
2. Add the lean veal, ham cut into small cubes, carrots, onions, good seasoning of salt and pepper, bouquet of fresh herbs, and a bowl of clear broth
3. Cook for no more than thirty minutes
4. Serve the young duck accompanied as desired
5. Garnish with fresh peas sautéed separately with bacon scraps if desired
