## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Parsley
- Batter or egg for coating

## Directions

1. Prepare the cauliflower first in an adobo with salt, vinegar and parsley
2. Cook it as is customary
3. Let it drain to remove excess moisture
4. Wrap it in a batter or coat it with egg
5. Fry it in oil, dividing it into florets
