## Ingredients

- 250 grams flour
- 1 cuartillo (approximately 1 liter) of warm water
- 60 grams fresh beef butter
- 1 and a half egg whites, beaten to stiff peaks

## Directions

1. Place 250 grams of flour in a deep plate
2. Gradually add warm water little by little, just enough to soften and melt 60 grams of very fresh beef butter
3. Work the paste so that it is uniformly bound and has a consistency like well-made chocolate
4. If not all the warm water is needed, any remaining beef butter on the surface of the unused water should be collected with a wooden spatula and incorporated into the paste
5. When the oil is ready for frying, beat 1 and a half egg whites to stiff peaks and incorporate them quickly into the paste at the same moment the foods to be fried are dipped into it
