## Ingredients

- Veal kidney
- Beef marrow
- Fresh white bacon
- Mushrooms
- Chives
- Bread crumb soaked in meat juice
- Thyme
- Two raw eggs
- Quail, roasted
- Rum

## Directions

1. Make a hash with veal kidney, beef marrow, very fresh white bacon, mushrooms, chives, bread crumb soaked in meat juice, thyme and two raw eggs.
2. Mix well, make a mass and fill a mold whose bottom and sides will be lined with thin bacon leaves.
3. Cover the mold and with moderate heat below and strong heat above the lid, let it cook for one hour.
4. Turn out the poupeton onto a platter as if it were a flan, and around it place as garnish the quail, roasted beforehand and lightly sprinkled at the moment of serving with a few drops of rum.
