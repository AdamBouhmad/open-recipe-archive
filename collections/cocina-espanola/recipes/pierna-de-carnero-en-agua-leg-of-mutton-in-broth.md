## Ingredients

- One leg of mutton, deboned or well tenderized
- A pair of long strips of bacon
- Some carrots, cut up
- Three or four onions
- A bouquet of fine herbs
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. In a braising pot, which is a large thin metal cooking pot with a lid, place at the bottom a pair of long strips of bacon, some cut-up carrots, three or four onions, a bouquet of fine herbs, and the appropriate seasoning of salt and pepper
2. Place the deboned leg of mutton on top, or if not deboned, well tenderized with a mallet and with the protruding bone part cut but not completely removed from the piece of meat to which it is attached
