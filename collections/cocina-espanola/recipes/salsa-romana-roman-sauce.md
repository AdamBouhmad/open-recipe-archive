## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter from cows
- the same amount of lean ham
- one pound of veal
- two feet from a well-fattened hen
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- laurel
- two or three onions
- carrots
- a dozen yolks of hard-boiled eggs, well crushed
- two quarts of milk

## Directions

1. Cut the butter, ham, veal, and hen feet into small pieces.
2. Season with salt, pepper, clove, laurel, onions, and carrots.
3. When all is browned, add a dozen yolks of hard-boiled eggs, well crushed, and mix well for a perfect mixture.
4. Wet with two quarts of milk, stirring constantly with a spatula, and cook for one hour over low heat, never stopping the stirring.
5. Pass through a sieve.
6. This sauce should not be prepared except at the moment of serving, as it keeps the least amount of time.
