## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 2 ounces of beef butter
- 1 tablespoon of milk or water
- a small piece of beef butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Heat 2 ounces of beef butter in a pan.
2. Separate the egg whites from the yolks.
3. Beat the yolks separately in a bowl, seasoning them and adding 1 tablespoon of milk or water.
4. Beat the whites in a meringue in another bowl.
5. Combine the yolks and whites together, mixing very well, and add a small piece of beef butter.
6. When the butter in the pan is boiling, pour in the mixture.
7. Hold the pan by the handle with your left hand and beat the eggs with a wooden spatula in your right hand during cooking.
8. Cook until the desired consistency is reached, either firm or soft according to taste.
