## Ingredients

- fat white clams
- oil or butter
- parsley, chopped
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Select the fattest and whitest clams
2. Clean them beforehand
3. Cook and open them
4. Set aside their water
5. Add oil or butter and chopped parsley
6. Serve with their own water and lemon juice
