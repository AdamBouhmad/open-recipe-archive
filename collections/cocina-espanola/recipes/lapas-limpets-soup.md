## Ingredients

- many limpets
- sea water
- oil
- large quantity of garlic
- flour
- pimentón
- galletas (ship's biscuits)

## Directions

1. Cook many limpets in sea water
2. Remove the limpets from their shells and sauté them separately in a little oil with a large quantity of garlic
3. Sprinkle with flour and a little pimentón, then add the water in which they cooked, passed through a strainer, and crumble in galletas to serve the dish as a soup
