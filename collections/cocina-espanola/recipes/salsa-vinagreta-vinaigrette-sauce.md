## Ingredients

- cooked egg yolks
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- oil
- onion
- egg whites

## Directions

1. It is made hot and cold, depending on whether the oil is fried and hot with well-sautéed onion, or raw, with raw onion also.
2. Crush two cooked egg yolks in a bowl and dilute in vinegar, seasoning with salt and pepper.
3. Add trimmed parsley and work everything well until the moment of adding the onion and oil.
4. When serving the sauce, whether cold or hot, cut the two hard egg whites into small, square pieces and mix into the sauce.
5. Some add a hint of garlic, but shallot very finely chopped is preferable, and better still early small onions.
6. This sauce has endless applications.
