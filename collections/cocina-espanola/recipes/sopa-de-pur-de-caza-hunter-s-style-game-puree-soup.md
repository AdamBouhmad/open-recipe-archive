## Ingredients

- one hare or partridge
- game essence
- butter
- onion slices
- carrots
- half a quartillo of Jerez wine
- thyme
- bay leaf
- two quartillos of broth
- bread croutons
- small game meat balls

## Directions

1. Debone a hare or partridge, and with the bones prepare a substance or essence called game essence
2. Coat the bottom of a pan with this essence and butter, placing some onion slices and some carrots
3. On top of all this place the bones, add half a quartillo of Jerez wine, thyme and bay leaf
4. Move to low heat to cook
5. Add two quartillos of broth, letting it simmer at the side of the stove for two hours
6. Sauté the meats, pound in a mortar and pass with broth through a strainer
7. Heat in a bain-marie, adding the said essence, and serve with bread croutons, or small meat balls or pellets of game meat
