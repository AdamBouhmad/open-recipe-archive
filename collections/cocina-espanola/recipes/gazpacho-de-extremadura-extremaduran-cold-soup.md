## Ingredients

- oil
- salt
- one or two cloves of garlic
- bread crumbs soaked
- water
- bell pepper (optional)
- onion
- tomato
- green bell pepper
- cucumber

## Directions

1. In a mortar place a little oil, salt and one or two cloves of garlic; mash well
2. Add a little crumb of soaked bread and mash again with the oil, garlic and salt until they form like an ointment
3. Add a few drops of water and stir with the pestle so it begins to dissolve, gradually adding more water while stirring until the mortar is full
4. Empty into a serving dish and finish adding the desired amount of water
5. Crumble bread by hand into pieces, not with a knife, leaving it to soften in this broth until ready to eat
6. About a quarter hour before eating, add a little diced onion for flavor
7. Add vinegar only at the moment of serving, as if added earlier the bread absorbs it all and becomes sour
8. Diced tomato, green bell pepper and cucumber are very good additions; tomato and bell pepper are almost always added
