## Ingredients

- calf's brains
- salt, a good handful
- strong vinegar, one glass
- water, very fresh and acidulated excessively with vinegar or lemon
- 125 grams melted butter
- finely chopped fine herbs
- half a glass vinegar
- water acidulated with vinegar or lemon
- black butter sauce

## Directions

1. Add a good handful of salt and one glass of strong vinegar to boiling water. Blanch the brains in this water.
2. Chill the brains immediately in very fresh water acidulated excessively with vinegar or lemon.
3. Prepare a mixture with 125 grams melted butter, finely chopped fine herbs, and half a glass of vinegar.
4. Cook the brains in this mixture for half an hour, adding some of the acidulated water in which they were chilled.
5. When the brains are cooked, no sauce should remain.
6. Cut the brains into thin slices and place in a metal serving dish.
7. Pour black butter sauce over the brains and serve.
