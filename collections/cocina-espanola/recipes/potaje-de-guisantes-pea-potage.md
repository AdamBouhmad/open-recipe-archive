## Ingredients

- Peas
- Lettuce hearts
- Sorrel
- Spices and seasonings

## Directions

1. Add the peas to the boiling pot along with lettuce hearts and sorrel
2. Season with the same spices as other potages
3. If the peas are dried, cook them the same way as chickpeas
