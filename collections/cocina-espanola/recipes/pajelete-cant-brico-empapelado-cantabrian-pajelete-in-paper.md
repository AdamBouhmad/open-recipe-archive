## Ingredients

- Pajelete
- Oil
- Breadcrumbs
- Chopped parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine oil
- One clove of garlic
- White paper
- Oil for coating paper

## Directions

1. Clean the pajelete and grease it with oil, fill the belly with crumbled bread kneaded with chopped parsley, salt and pepper, fine oil and one clove of garlic
2. Wrap it in a sheet of white paper coated with oil and roast it on grills
3. Serve without removing it from the paper
