## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- refined oil
- fresh butter from cows
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- clove spice
- nutmeg
- good rum

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys and soak them in refined oil for two hours
2. Remove the kidneys from the oil and sauté them over high heat in a pan with very fresh butter for five minutes at most
3. Set the kidneys aside on a plate
4. Prepare finely chopped onion in an amount equal to the volume of the kidneys
5. In the butter remaining in the pan, adding more if necessary, sauté the onion, stirring well with a spatula so that it glazes and gelatinizes without taking on color
6. Season with salt, pepper, a pinch of clove spice, and a hint of nutmeg
7. Over high heat, incorporate the kidneys and pour over them a glass of good rum
8. Shake the pan vigorously for two minutes, allowing the contents to remain in constant movement
9. Serve on a platter, preferably within a basket of fried bread croutons made from a loaf
