## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of green peas
- boiling water
- salt
- sugar
- broth
- bread croutons

## Directions

1. Cook 2 pounds of green peas in boiling salted water over strong fire so it does not lose its color
2. Pass to cold water and then through a fine sieve
3. Place in a clean saucepan with a little sugar
4. Add sufficient broth so it is neither too clear nor too thick
5. Serve well hot with bread croutons
