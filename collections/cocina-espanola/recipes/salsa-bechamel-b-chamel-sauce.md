## Ingredients

- half liter glazed broth
- good meat broth
- half liter cream

## Directions

1. Reduce over high heat half a liter of glaze to which a couple of ladles of good meat broth from the pot has been incorporated
2. Let it cook, stirring the liquid constantly until it decreases by one third of its original volume
3. Separately also reduce by one third half a liter of cream
4. Mix the cream with the reduced sauce, stirring always until it has the consistency of a clear paste
5. Then strain by pressing firmly through a clear strainer
6. If the dish it is destined for is not yet ready, it is necessary to maintain the temperature of the sauce in a water bath so it does not cool
