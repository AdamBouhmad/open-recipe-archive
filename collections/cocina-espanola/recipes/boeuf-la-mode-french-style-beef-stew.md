## Ingredients

- beef from the rump
- bacon strips
- carrots
- onions
- cloves of spice
- parsley
- scallions
- bay leaves
- thyme branch
- salt
- pepper
- veal knuckle
- water

## Directions

1. Choose one of the best pieces of rump, lard it with strips of bacon, and put it to simmer slowly for about four hours with carrots, onions, cloves of spice, parsley and scallions, two bay leaves, a branch of thyme, salt, pepper, a veal knuckle and four glasses of water
2. When sufficiently cooked, serve it surrounded by these vegetables
