## Ingredients

- 125 grams of the best and freshest beef butter
- salt
- pepper
- 1 small teaspoon of finely chopped parsley
- several shallots, chopped finely
- a few drops of vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Knead together the beef butter with salt, pepper, parsley, chopped shallots, and vinegar or lemon juice
2. Incorporate this butter mixture away from the fire into cooked fresh or dried vegetables, drained and very hot, so that the butter melts and the mixture combines well
3. Place this butter in a very hot serving dish
4. Serve it on top of grilled fish, chops, steaks, beefsteaks and other dishes, tossing them on all sides until the butter melts
5. Present at the table with the distinctive name 'à la mayordoma'
