## Ingredients

- at least 500 grams of turtle meat
- 1 kilogram of beef
- 1 kilogram of veal
- 1 kilogram of mutton
- carrots
- onions
- seasoning and spices as appropriate
- 1 bottle of Madeira or Port wine
- quenelles cut into slices

## Directions

1. Cut the meat of a turtle into pieces as large as walnuts, which can be compared to that of a calf's kidney.
2. Make the meat release its slime in successive changes of water.
3. At least 500 grams of turtle are needed for a good complete soup.
4. Prepare a rich broth with 1 kilogram of beef, equal weight of veal and the same of mutton, letting it cook for six hours with carrots, onions and the appropriate seasoning and spices.
5. In this broth, strained, cooled and defatted, cook the pieces of turtle over low heat for four hours.
6. At the moment of serving, add a bottle of Madeira or Port wine with quenelles cut into slices; but these are not added to the soup until everything is ready.
