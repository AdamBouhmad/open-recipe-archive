## Ingredients

- water
- bread
- salt
- paprika
- pure oil
- Spanish garlic

## Directions

1. Place a wide and deep earthenware bowl on the fire
2. Fill it with water
3. Break bread into small fragments and add to the water
4. Sprinkle with salt and paprika
5. Drizzle with pure oil
6. Peel two cloves of Spanish garlic and hide them in the spongy bread mixture
7. Heat everything together over the fire until it condenses
8. When a brown crust forms around the edges, place the bowl on a serving dish
9. Serve hot
