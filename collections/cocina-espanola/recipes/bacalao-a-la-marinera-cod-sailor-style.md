## Ingredients

- Cooked cod
- Cod broth
- Cooked egg yolk
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Raw oil
- Vinegar
- Small cooked onions

## Directions

1. After cooking the cod, place it in a serving dish with a little of its own broth in which a cooked egg yolk, crushed garlic and a little pepper are dissolved.
2. Add raw oil and vinegar and some small onions cooked with the same cod.
