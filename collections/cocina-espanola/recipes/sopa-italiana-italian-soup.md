## Ingredients

- Partridge, pheasant, woodcock or other game bird breasts
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Bread crumb
- Broth from the pot
- Flour
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make meatballs with game bird breasts, without any mixture of poultry
2. Proceed in the same manner as for Spanish soup but the result will be more coarse
