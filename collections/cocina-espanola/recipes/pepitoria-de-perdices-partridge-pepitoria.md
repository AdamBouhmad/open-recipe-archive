## Ingredients

- partridges
- pork lard
- flour
- water
- white wine
- small onions
- mushrooms
- garlic
- celery
- bacon

## Directions

1. Cut the partridges into four pieces
2. Brown them over high heat in pork lard, and when very golden, sprinkle with flour
3. Stir well and season
4. Moisten with half a quart of water and a glass of the best white wine available, and add very small onions, mushrooms, one clove of garlic and pieces of celery stalks
5. Cook everything for one hour at low heat
6. Place the dish on a platter and cover with a large sheet of bacon like a veil
7. Before serving, keep it in the oven for thirty minutes at medium heat
