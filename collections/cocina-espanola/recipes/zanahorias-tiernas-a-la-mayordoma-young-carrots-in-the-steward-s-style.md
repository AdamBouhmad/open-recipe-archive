## Ingredients

- Young carrots
- Fresh butter
- One tablespoon of flour
- A good pinch of finely chopped fine herbs
- Water or broth, a few spoonfuls
- Salt and pepper
- Two or three egg yolks
- Two tablespoons of fresh water

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots and cut them into four pieces lengthwise
2. Sauté over high heat in fresh butter with one tablespoon of flour and a good pinch of finely chopped fine herbs
3. Temper the heat so the butter does not color
4. Moisten with a few spoonfuls of water or broth, seasoning strongly with salt and pepper
5. Once the carrots are cooked, remove the pan from heat and place them on a serving dish
6. Pour over them a binding of two or three egg yolks, dissolved in two tablespoons of fresh water
