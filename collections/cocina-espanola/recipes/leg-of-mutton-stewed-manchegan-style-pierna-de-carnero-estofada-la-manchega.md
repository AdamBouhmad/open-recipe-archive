## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- aged bacon
- spices
- red wine
- vinegar
- onions
- turnips
- carrots
- aromatic herbs
- garlic
- pepper

## Directions

1. Bone the leg of mutton well and lard it with aged bacon, seasoning with some excess of spices.
2. Place in a pot where the meat fits comfortably.
3. Cook in a quarter liter of red wine and two spoonfuls of vinegar, with six onions the size of a hen's egg, turnips, carrots, aromatic herbs, a couple of garlic cloves and a hint of pepper.
4. Cook this very slowly for four hours with the pot well covered.
5. Serve with a little of its degreased broth.
