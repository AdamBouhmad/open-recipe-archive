## Ingredients

- a piece of beef butter the size of an egg
- one heaping spoonful of flour
- water or clear broth
- the same ingredients as the dark financière (poultry quenelles, mushrooms, truffles, artichoke hearts, veal sweetbreads, tongue à l'écarlate, olives, wine or sherry, salt and pepper)
- two egg yolks
- milk

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, place a piece of beef butter the size of an egg and one heaping spoonful of flour
2. Mix well over high heat and then moisten with water or clear broth
3. Incorporate the same ingredients as in the dark financière garnish and cook over low heat for half an hour
4. Remove from heat and bind with two egg yolks mixed with milk
5. Pour into a serving dish to garnish the dish to be served
