## Ingredients

- potatoes
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- refined oil
- aromatic vinegar from Orleans
- two glasses of Bordeaux white wine, Iquem brand
- parsley
- chervil
- aromatic herbs
- sea dates (dátiles de mar)
- celery stalk
- truffles
- Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in broth and slice them
2. While still warm, season with salt and pepper, refined oil and aromatic vinegar from Orleans
3. Add two glasses of Bordeaux white wine, Iquem brand, and finely chopped parsley, chervil and aromatic herbs
4. Cook sea dates in broth with a celery stalk, cleaned well of filaments
5. Mix everything together, first removing the shellfish from their shells, combining with the seasoned potatoes, stirring slowly with two spatulas
6. The proportion of sea dates to potatoes should be one third less, so that the shellfish flavor is barely perceptible
7. Once the salad is well mixed and tossed, cover the domed surface with slices of truffles cooked in Champagne wine
8. Make this salad two hours before serving so it is completely cold
