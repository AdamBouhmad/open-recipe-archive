## Ingredients

- Concentrated beef broth made from beef shank, veal knuckles, chicken, and bacon
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Chicken breast cutlets
- Langoustines
- Anchovy fillets
- Shredded salt cod
- Raw oysters
- Parsley
- Shallots
- Gelatin from the broth

## Directions

1. In a bowl, cup, or mold, place slices of hard-boiled egg at the bottom
2. On top, add a layer of cut chicken breast pieces, langoustines, anchovy fillets, shredded salt cod, raw oysters, or any similar items
3. Sprinkle with a fine mixture of chopped parsley and shallots
4. Add another layer of hard-boiled egg slices
5. Add another layer of one of the aforementioned foods
6. Add another layer of parsley and shallots, and continue thus successively until the mold or cup is completely filled and level with the edges
7. Pour the concentrated, well-degreased and clarified broth carefully into the bowl
8. Place the aspic in a very cool, airy location to set
9. To serve, loosen with a spatula the edges of the dish that adhere to the bowl
10. Submerge the bowl, dipping it in and out a couple of times, in boiling water
11. Turn it over onto a plate on which you have first placed a well-ironed napkin
12. When desired, give the broth a blood color by adding a concentrated infusion of finely chopped roasted beet without losing its transparency
