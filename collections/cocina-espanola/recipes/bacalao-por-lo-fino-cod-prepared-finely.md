## Ingredients

- Cod soaked for twenty-four hours
- Plenty of boiling water
- Fresh butter from cows
- Steamed potatoes

## Directions

1. Soak the cod for twenty-four hours
2. Clean it of skin and bones and shred it into long strips
3. Submerge the strips in plenty of boiling water and boil for five minutes
4. Cover the pot and leave in the water for half an hour, maintaining it without boiling at high temperature
5. Serve the drained strips between the folds of a napkin on a hot dish
6. Eat with fresh butter from cows, spreading it on the fish on the same plate, accompanied by steamed potatoes as bread
7. Alternatively, serve with oil and salt, or with any sauce served separately, best with a well-bound mayonnaise
