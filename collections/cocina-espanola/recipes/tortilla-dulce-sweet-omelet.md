## Ingredients

- eight very fresh eggs
- 125 grams of ground sugar
- zest of one large lemon, grated
- three or four tablespoons of thick cream
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Separate the egg yolks from the whites of eight very fresh eggs.
2. Incorporate 125 grams of ground sugar into the yolks, beating them vigorously.
3. Add the grated zest of one large lemon, three or four tablespoons of thick cream, and a pinch of salt.
4. Combine the prepared yolks with the whites.
5. Beat everything together.
6. Make the omelet in the Spanish or French style as explained in pages 664-668.
7. Once the omelet is made and placed on the serving dish, sprinkle with sugar.
8. Pass a heated stoker from the stove-cooker over it, drawing lines well to make the caramelization pleasing to the sight.
