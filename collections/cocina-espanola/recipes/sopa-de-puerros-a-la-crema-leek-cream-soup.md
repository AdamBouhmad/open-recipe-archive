## Ingredients

- two ounces of cow butter or pork lard
- one pound of leeks, cut into hazelnut-sized pieces
- two heaping tablespoons of flour
- pot broth or water
- salt to taste
- small fried croutons

## Directions

1. For six persons, put two ounces of cow butter or pork lard in a saucepan
2. When the fat is rusty, sauté in it one pound of leeks, cut into pieces the size of hazelnuts, using heads, stalks and leaves
3. Before they take color, add two heaping tablespoons of flour
4. When the flour begins to toast, moisten everything with pot broth or water and season to taste
5. Let cook for half an hour
6. At the moment of serving, bind the soup as indicated for others
7. In the tureen, if desired, have small fried croutons beforehand
