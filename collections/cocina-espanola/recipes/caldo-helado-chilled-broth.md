## Ingredients

- Cooked meat broth
- Salt
- Egg yolks
- Sugar
- Cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. After the meat is well cooked and seasoned with salt without spices, remove the broth that has no fat, and let it cool
2. For each cup of broth, add two well-beaten and loosened egg yolks and one ounce of sugar
3. Dissolve them with the cooling broth and sugar, stirring everything together
4. If the quantity of broth is small, put it in cups, and for each one a pot of boiling water is needed
5. Place the cup over the mouth of the pot and let it boil until the contents of the cup set like custard
6. Serve with powdered sugar and cinnamon
7. If you have many cups to prepare, use a pot for each one; but if you prefer to make it in a large earthenware dish putting heat above and below, you can, and thus cut it like a tart. It is good and many desire to know how to make it
