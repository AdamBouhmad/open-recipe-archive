## Ingredients

- 4 pounds of white potatoes
- water
- salt
- cow butter
- poultry broth
- 4 egg yolks
- water

## Directions

1. Cook 4 pounds of white peeled potatoes, cut in quarters, in water and salt
2. Before total cooking, remove all the water well, and in the same covered saucepan place in the oven so they finish cooking and become floury
3. Then stir with a spoon, adding a little cow butter
4. Pass through a fine sieve
5. Dissolve with poultry broth and pass through a tamis
6. At the time of serving, add 4 egg yolks beaten in water
