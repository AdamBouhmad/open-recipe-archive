## Ingredients

- spinach paste
- fried ham slices
- croutons
- fat or butter from frying the ham

## Directions

1. Prepare spinach paste as described in the vegetables section
2. Arrange it in a pyramid or dome shape on a round platter
3. On its surface, place with art and symmetry fried ham slices alternating with croutons sautéed in the fat or butter in which the ham was fried
4. The ham slices and croutons should have, if possible, the same form and size, generally triangular for the ham
