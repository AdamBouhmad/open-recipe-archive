## Ingredients

- half a pound of Malaga raisins
- half a pound of Corinth raisins
- half a pound of beef kidney fat
- a quarter pound of flour
- a quarter pound of very crumbled bread crumb
- two ounces of ground sugar
- three ounces total of citron, orange peel, and angelica in syrup
- a glass of milk
- a glass of rum
- one lemon
- nutmeg, ginger, and cinnamon mixed together to fill half a coffee spoon
- an equal amount of ground salt
- three eggs

## Directions

1. Skin and shred the kidney fat with great care and mince it very finely, as if it were bread crumbs
2. Mix it with the bread crumb and flour, and dissolve with milk
3. Beat the three eggs well with the nutmeg, ginger, and cinnamon
4. Mix with the previous mixture and incorporate the citron, orange peel, angelica, Malaga and Corinth raisins, and the glass of rum with the lemon juice, salt, and sugar
5. Mix everything well with a spatula until the spatula can stand upright planted in the mixture
6. Grease an appropriate English mold with butter
7. Cover it with a thick, coarse cloth soaked in water
