## Ingredients

- small lean ham slices
- beef butter
- half teaspoon of flour
- broth
- drops of pure wine brandy
- powdered sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the small lean ham slices and fry them in beef butter
2. Remove them from the pan and set them aside on a plate
3. In the same butter, add half a teaspoon of flour and moisten with broth and a few drops of pure wine brandy
4. Dust with powdered sugar according to the diners' preference for sweetness
5. Incorporate the ham and cook for ten minutes away from the heat source, with the pan covered and its lid topped with hot coals
