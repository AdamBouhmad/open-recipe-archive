## Ingredients

- Zucchini
- Onion
- Pepper
- Salt
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Chop zucchini into very small pieces
2. Fry in a pan where onion has already been fried, also chopped fine
3. Let fry until the liquid released is well consumed
4. Add a little pepper and salt
5. Thicken with well-beaten eggs
