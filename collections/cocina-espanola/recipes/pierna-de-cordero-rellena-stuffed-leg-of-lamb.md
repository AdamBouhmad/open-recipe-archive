## Ingredients

- roasted lamb, cold
- lamb meat trimmed and macerated
- hard egg yolks
- bread crumbled and toasted
- fine herbs finely chopped
- spices
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Debone the cold roasted lamb neck.
2. Break the breast and apply the shoulder blades forming a well-wrapped bundle tied with twine, with the legs and broken bone extremities.
3. Fill the whole package with a filling composed of trimmed and macerated lamb meat, hard egg yolks, toasted breadcrumbs and finely chopped fine herbs seasoned with spices.
4. Tie the lamb meat and place in the oven for half an hour at gentle heat.
5. Serve whole as an appetizer course, with a green sauce, or with a ragout of truffles with ham juice.
