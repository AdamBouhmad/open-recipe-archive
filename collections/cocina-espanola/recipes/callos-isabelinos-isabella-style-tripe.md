## Ingredients

- tripe, well cleaned
- water
- salt
- pork fat
- roasted garlic head
- ham
- chorizo
- longaniza
- morcilla (blood sausage)
- paprika
- saffron
- parsley
- garlic
- bread soaked
- pepper
- clove
- cilantro
- caraway
- cumin
- cinnamon (optional)
- pine nuts or hazelnuts (optional)

## Directions

1. After cleaning well, place tripe in a pot with water and salt, a piece of pork fat, and a roasted garlic head, proportioned to quantity.
2. Optionally, add ham, chorizo, longaniza, morcilla, or similar items.
3. When everything is cooked, remove the pork fat, mash it, and dissolve it with a little paprika, saffron, parsley, garlic, and soaked bread, along with small amounts of spices: pepper, clove, cilantro, caraway, and cumin.
4. Add cinnamon, pine nuts, or hazelnuts if desired, ensuring all flavors are balanced and no single taste predominates.
5. Let cook a while and serve.
