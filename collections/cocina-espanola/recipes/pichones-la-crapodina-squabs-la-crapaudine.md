## Ingredients

- squabs
- breadcrumbs
- one ladle of broth
- three tablespoons of vinegar
- one tablespoon of minced shallots
- one tablespoon of finely chopped parsley
- salt
- pepper
- mustard

## Directions

1. Prepare the squabs with legs and wings extended
2. Bread the squabs with breadcrumbs
3. Grill them over low heat on a grill
4. Prepare the sauce by heating a ladle of broth with three tablespoons of vinegar, one tablespoon of minced shallots, one tablespoon of finely chopped parsley, salt, pepper, and mustard
5. Serve the splayed squabs on the platter with the sauce on the side
