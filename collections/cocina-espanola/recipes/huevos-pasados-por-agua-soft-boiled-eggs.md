## Ingredients

- eggs
- water

## Directions

1. Place the eggs to be soft-boiled in cold water, covering them well
2. Over high heat, bring the water to a boil
3. At the first bubble of boiling, remove the vessel from the fire
4. Serve the eggs to the table in a bowl or covered dish, filled with the water in which they cooked, cooled slightly with cold water to stop all cooking while preserving the heat
5. The eggs will be uniformly soft-boiled with slightly set yolks and milky, loose, and uniform whites
6. Use only very fresh eggs for this preparation
