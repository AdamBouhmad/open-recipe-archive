## Ingredients

- Peas prepared as in the natural recipe
- Two or three lumps of sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the peas exactly as in the natural formula.
2. Add two or three lumps of sugar at the moment of serving.
3. The sugar dissolves and sweetens the entire dish equally, without need of stirring.
