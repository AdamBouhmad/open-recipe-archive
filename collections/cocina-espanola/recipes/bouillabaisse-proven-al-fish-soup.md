## Ingredients

- Fish of all types
- Bread
- Water
- Olive oil
- Salt and seasonings
- Flour (a small amount)

## Directions

1. In a cauldron with olive oil, fry fish of all types
2. Remove the fried fish and fry bread slices in the same oil
3. Prepare the broth by adding water to the oil in a proportion of ten parts water to one part oil and season
4. In home kitchens, use a frying pan instead of a cauldron and limit the variety of fish to two or three types at most, two white fish and one blue fish
5. Fry the bread and then strain the oil into a cooking pot
6. Add a small amount of flour just before seasoning and before adding the water
7. Clean the fried fish carefully, shred it, and combine with a well-prepared sauce
8. Form the mixture into a pyramid shape to serve
