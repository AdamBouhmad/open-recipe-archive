## Ingredients

- brick-shaped sandwich bread
- eggs
- lard or olive oil

## Directions

1. Cut slices from brick-shaped sandwich bread two centimeters thick, in the direction of the width
2. Using a glass whose mouth diameter is slightly smaller than the square of the slice, press it into the bread as if using a cutter, to about half the depth of the piece
3. Empty the marked part of the breadcrumb and it will remain like a hollow, which should be refined and profiled carefully, moistening the bread with water
4. In a large frying pan capable of holding at least four slices, put a sufficient quantity of lard or olive oil according to the taste of the diners
5. Over high heat, when the liquid in the pan is ready to fry, quickly throw the bread slices with the hollowed part facing up
6. In each hollow, drop an egg without loss of time, as soon as the crumb has taken on a toasted color
7. Remove and serve on a well-pressed napkin placed over a round dish of English metal or Ruolz silver
