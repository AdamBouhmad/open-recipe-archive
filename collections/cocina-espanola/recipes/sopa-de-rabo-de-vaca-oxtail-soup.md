## Ingredients

- 1 oxtail
- fine beef butter
- flour
- cayenne pepper
- broth from the pot

## Directions

1. In the pot that is customarily used in a house, incorporate a whole oxtail.
2. When cooked, remove it and cut it into small pieces, preserving the bone in those that allow it.
3. Sauté these pieces in fine beef butter and toast them well.
4. Dust them with flour and a pinch of Cayenne pepper, and moisten with the broth from the pot.
5. Serve this soup very clean and clarified broth, with two or three small pieces of oxtail in each diner's plate.
