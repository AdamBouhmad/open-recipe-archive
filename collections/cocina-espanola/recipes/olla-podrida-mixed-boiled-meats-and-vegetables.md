## Ingredients

- Chickpeas
- Meat
- Hen
- Bacon
- Ham
- Pork feet
- Pork ear
- Stuffings
- Poultry offal

## Directions

1. Once the chickpeas and meat have been prepared as described, skim it
2. Add a hen, bacon, ham, feet, pork ear, stuffings, poultry offal and everything that Nature created to be eaten, cooked together
