## Ingredients

- broth
- fat
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- shallots
- parsley

## Directions

1. In the saucepan add broth and fat, a spoonful of vinegar, salt, pepper, and five or six shallots and parsley.
2. Let boil until the shallots are well cooked.
3. This is a sauce that serves to reheat the meat from stew that remained from the previous meal.
