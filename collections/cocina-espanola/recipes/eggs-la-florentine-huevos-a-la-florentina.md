## Ingredients

- A small amount of pork lard
- Water
- Salt and seasoning
- Plenty of finely cut parsley
- Eggs
- Tender croutons

## Directions

1. In a dish, platter, or pan that can go to the fire, melt a small amount of pork lard.
2. Before it turns russet, add water, seasoning, and plenty of finely cut parsley.
3. Off the heat, carefully add the eggs one by one.
4. Over low heat, poach them.
5. Serve immediately with very tender croutons.
