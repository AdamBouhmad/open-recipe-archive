## Ingredients

- three pounds good fresh healthy meat
- eight quartillos cold water
- salt
- carrots
- turnips
- small onions
- parsley
- cloves
- one garlic clove
- one roasted onion or a little burned sugar for color

## Directions

1. For three pounds of good fresh healthy meat put eight quartillos of cold water and salt, letting it cook little by little and skimming it well
2. Then add carrots, turnips, small onions, parsley, cloves, one garlic clove and one roasted onion, or a little burned sugar to give color to the broth
3. After cooking six hours on low fire, the puchero is done, and the broth, after straining, is poured over the soup
