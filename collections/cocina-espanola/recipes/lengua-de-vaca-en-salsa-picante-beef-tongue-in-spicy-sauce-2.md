## Ingredients

- One beef tongue
- Carrots
- Onions
- One celery stalk

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue as you would meat for a stew, with carrots, onions, and a celery stalk.
2. Six hours of cooking is better than five.
3. Remove the skin from the tongue after taking it from the pot, make a lengthwise incision to open it flat on the plate, and pour spicy sauce or whichever sauce you wish over it—this is an appreciative dish and goes with everything.
