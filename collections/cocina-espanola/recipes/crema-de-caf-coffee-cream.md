## Ingredients

- Coffee, infused for three or four hours
- One liter of good cream
- Two egg whites
- 60 grams of molded pilón sugar
- 15 grams of pulverized gum arabic

## Directions

1. Place coffee in infusion for three or four hours in one liter of good cream after having roasted it without allowing it to become black
2. Pass the cream through a strainer to separate the coffee beans
3. Add two egg whites, 60 grams of molded pilón sugar, and 15 grams of pulverized gum arabic
4. Beat everything a great deal to the point of meringue
5. Remove the foam as it forms and continue until the liquid part has concentrated
6. Serve like the whipped cream in a round nickel dish
