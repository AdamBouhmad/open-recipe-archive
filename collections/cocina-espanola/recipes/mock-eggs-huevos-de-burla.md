## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Cream of choice (vanilla, lemon, chocolate, etc.)
- Or: almond paste or any aspic that sets when cooled

## Directions

1. Make a small hole in an egg with the tip of a knife.
2. Loosen the yolk with a thick needle so it can be emptied.
3. Once all the yolk has been removed, fill this shell with the desired cream using a funnel.
4. Place the egg or eggs in egg cups in a pan where they can be bathed in water.
5. Heat them in a double boiler (bain-marie).
6. Wash them and serve on a napkin as soft-boiled eggs.
7. They can also be filled with almond paste or any aspic that sets when cooled.
