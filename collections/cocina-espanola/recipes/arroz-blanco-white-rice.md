## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- rice, well cleaned and washed
- a small amount of pork lard

## Directions

1. First, bring water with a bit of salt to a boil, if possible in an earthenware vessel.
2. As soon as the water bubbles, add about half of well-cleaned and thoroughly washed rice.
3. Leave it on the fire until the moment the liquid begins to run out, and before it dries completely, remove it.
4. Keep it at a certain warmth, but away from the heat.
5. Coat it with a small amount of pork lard, just enough to moisten the top layer, and by the temperature it will spread throughout all the rice.
6. Do not let it toast or form lumps and clots.
