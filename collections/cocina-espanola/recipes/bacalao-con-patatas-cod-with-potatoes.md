## Ingredients

- Cod
- Flour
- Potatoes
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Bread crumb

## Directions

1. After soaked and wrapped in flour, fry it
2. When golden, add water and peeled potatoes
3. Cook, and when it has consumed most of the water, stew with fried garlic, parsley, pepper and a bread crumb
4. Bring to a boil
