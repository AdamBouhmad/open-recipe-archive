## Ingredients

- Pastry dough
- Eight quails
- Clarified butter
- Salt and pepper
- Three hundred grams of calf liver
- Two hundred fifty grams of bacon
- Bread plug
- Truffles, crumbled
- Chicken sweetbreads
- Pastry, for the lid

## Directions

1. Make a pastry mold as described in the previous article
2. Prepare eight quails by cutting them lengthwise in two parts
3. Place them in a sauté pan with clarified butter
4. Season with salt and pepper and sauté over the fire for ten minutes
5. Drain and let cool
6. Make a filling by placing diced bacon in a sauté pan and adding salt and pepper
7. Sauté for four minutes, then add the calf liver also cut into small dice
8. Sauté for another four minutes, let cool, mash everything and strain through a horsehair sieve
9. Put a ring of filling at the bottom of the pastry mold, two centimeters thick, leaving the rest of the mold uncovered
10. Place eight selected half quails all turned the same side in the pastry mold, putting a bed of filling between them to hold them up
11. Form a second layer of filling and quails in the same manner
12. In the hollow left in the center of the rings, place a bread plug wrapped in a slice of bacon
13. Put a layer of filling over the quails and cover with the pastry lid, fluted and glazed, and bake in the oven
14. When the pastry is cooked, remove the lid and the bread plug from the center
15. Place the pastry on the serving dish and pour crumbled truffles inside
16. On top arrange a garnish of chicken sweetbreads and cover with crumbled truffles that project above the sweetbreads
