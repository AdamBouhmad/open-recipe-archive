## Ingredients

- rice
- broth from previous day's puchero
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Wash the rice thoroughly and place it in a saucepan with cold water.
2. As the rice absorbs the water, add more until it is cooked about halfway, then gradually incorporate broth little by little and complete the cooking.
3. Add a pinch of salt to the broth when it is put to cook with the rice.
4. For a more delicate preparation, cook slowly until the rice is slightly overcooked and almost dissolved in the broth, which will naturally change from caramelized to whitish color.
5. Alternatively, for the traditional Spanish method, boil the rice in broth at a rolling boil for twenty to thirty minutes, stirring occasionally.
