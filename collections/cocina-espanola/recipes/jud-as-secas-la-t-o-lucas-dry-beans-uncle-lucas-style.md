## Ingredients

- dry beans
- salt
- water
- onion, chopped in volume equal to that of the beans
- one tablespoon of fried and re-fried oil
- one ounce of well-trimmed bacon for each quarter pound of beans

## Directions

1. Wash the beans that are to be prepared very well and soak them in slightly salted water for two or three hours.
2. Then cook them as stated, according to their drying time, in plenty of water, either boiling or cold, and until their skin begins to wrinkle.
3. Remove them then and drain them.
4. Place them in a pot with chopped onion equal in volume to that of the beans, one tablespoon of fried and re-fried oil and one ounce of well-trimmed bacon for each quarter pound of beans.
