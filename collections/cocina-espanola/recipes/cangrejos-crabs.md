## Ingredients

- good butter from cows
- refined oil
- one large onion, about the size of a lemon
- parsley
- tarragon
- thyme
- bay leaf
- clove
- nutmeg
- salt
- pepper
- two cloves of garlic
- flour
- half a quartillo of water
- crabs, well washed and freshly castrated
- half a quartillo of white wine

## Directions

1. Heat the butter cut with a spoonful of refined oil, add the onion, finely chopped, parsley also chopped, tarragon, thyme, bay leaf, clove, nutmeg, salt, pepper and two cloves of garlic
2. When the onion takes color, add a heaping spoonful of flour, and when it has toasted well, incorporate half a quartillo of water at the same time as the crabs, well washed and freshly castrated
3. Over very high heat, with the pan covered, sauté the contents for three minutes and let cook over low heat for ten minutes, after having added half a quartillo of white wine
