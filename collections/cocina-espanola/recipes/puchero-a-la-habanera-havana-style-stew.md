## Ingredients

- two pounds veal loin
- a piece of salt pork
- a handful of chickpeas soaked the night before
- one pound fresh pork
- a quarter chicken
- one plantain
- one chorizo
- a handful of tender beans
- half a cabbage
- a piece of squash
- an eggplant
- a little malanga
- a piece of Castilian squash
- two turnips
- two sweet potatoes
- four tomatoes
- two shallots
- one onion
- a piece of island squash
- two or three garlic cloves
- a few grains of cumin
- a few grains of cilantro
- two grains of pepper
- saffron

## Directions

1. Prepare a medium pot with water filled halfway
2. Add a little salt, two pounds veal from the loin area, a piece of salt pork, a handful of chickpeas soaked from the night before and one pound fresh pork
3. Add a quarter chicken and put to cook, taking care to remove the foam
4. After it has boiled about one hour, add, well peeled and cleaned, a ripe plantain with its skin, a chorizo, a handful of tender beans, half a cabbage, a piece of squash, an eggplant, a little malanga, a piece of Castilian squash, two turnips, two sweet potatoes, four tomatoes, two shallots, one onion and a piece of island squash
5. Let everything cook about another hour and take care to remove any foam that rises again
6. To season it, pound in a mortar with a little salt, two or three garlic cloves, a few grains of cumin and cilantro and two of pepper, and when all is made into a paste, dilute it with broth from the pot and add it to the pot with two threads of crumbled saffron
7. Let it boil a while and serve
