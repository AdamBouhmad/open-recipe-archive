## Ingredients

- thirty frogs
- carrots
- turnips
- celery
- leeks
- water

## Directions

1. Clean thirty frogs, keeping only the legs
2. Cook the legs with carrots, turnips, celery and leeks
3. Add water in the proportion of one liter for 150 grams of frogs
4. Cook and treat and serve as an ordinary stew
