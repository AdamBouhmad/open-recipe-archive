## Ingredients

- mutton from any part of the animal, cut into small pieces
- bacon, cut into pieces
- oil
- onion, finely chopped
- meat broth
- parsley, chopped fine like snuff
- mint
- young garlic shoots
- lettuce hearts
- pine nuts, soaked and chopped
- spices
- lemon or orange juice

## Directions

1. Sauté the mutton pieces over low heat in an earthenware pot with the bacon pieces, oil and finely chopped onion
2. After two hours, stirring constantly so the dish does not stick, add meat broth
3. When everything is very well cooked, add the chopped parsley, mint, young garlic shoots, lettuce hearts, chopped pine nuts, spices, and lemon or orange juice
