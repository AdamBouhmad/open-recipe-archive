## Ingredients

- grouper
- blue court-bouillon

## Directions

1. Cook the fish in blue court-bouillon and let it cool in the broth
2. Remove and place on a platter, which is placed in the pantry, very tilted, so the fish drains well
3. When about to eat, place on a napkin in a serving dish, and garnish with watercress or lamb's lettuce
4. Serve with cold sauces: mayonnaise, remoulade, tartar, vinaigrette, etc., together with the vegetable that garnishes it
