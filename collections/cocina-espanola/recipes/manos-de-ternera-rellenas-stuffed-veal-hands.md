## Ingredients

- veal hands
- quenelles
- salt and pepper to taste
- twine
- Spanish sauce
- one glass of Jerez wine

## Directions

1. Carefully debone the veal hands, splitting them only along the inside edge without damaging them.
2. Cook the hands in their natural state, then drain and let cool.
3. Fill the hollow with a well-seasoned quenelle stuffing.
4. Tie with twine to prevent the filling from escaping.
5. Cook for another half hour in Spanish sauce with one glass of Jerez wine.
6. Remove the twine as you place each hand on the serving platter.
7. Pour the cooking sauce over top and serve.
