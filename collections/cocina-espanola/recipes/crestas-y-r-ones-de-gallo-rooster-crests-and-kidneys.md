## Ingredients

- rooster crests
- rooster kidneys (double the number of crests)
- salt
- pepper
- herb bouquet
- flour
- white-blonde sauce
- broth
- gravy from roasted bird
- 60 grams beef butter
- flour
- fine herbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak the rooster crests and kidneys in water and wash in several cold waters
2. Cook them with salt, pepper and an herb bouquet in water blanched with a spoonful of flour
3. Prepare a white-blonde sauce separately, lengthen it with broth, adding a ladle of gravy from roasted bird
4. Incorporate the crests and kidneys and let them cook in the sauce very slowly for about 15 minutes
5. At the moment of serving, melt 60 grams of beef butter kneaded with a little flour and finely chopped herbs into the sauce
6. Add all the juice of one lemon
