## Ingredients

- Lamb breast, bones removed
- Three or four slices of ham
- Bacon slices
- Two onions
- Clove
- Sprig of thyme
- Bay leaf
- Carrots, sliced into wheels
- Salt and pepper
- Two ladles of clean broth from the pot

## Directions

1. Remove the large bones attached to the cartilage from a lamb breast and divide it into eight equal pieces, oval at one end and pointed at the other.
2. In a casserole, place three or four slices of ham, and on top the lamb pieces, covered with bacon slices.
3. Add two onions, clove, a sprig of thyme, a bay leaf, and carrots cut into wheels, seasoning with salt and pepper and moistening with two ladles of clean broth.
4. Cook over low heat, below and above, for three hours.
5. Remove the lamb pieces, degrease the juices, reduce them, and pour over the meat on a serving platter.
6. Serve the lamb pieces with a minced mushroom garnish, which is its obligatory garnish.
