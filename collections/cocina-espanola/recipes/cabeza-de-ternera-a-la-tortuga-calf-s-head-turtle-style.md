## Ingredients

- Calf's head, cooked
- Financiera garnish
- White wine
- Cayenne pepper or sweet Murcia pepper
- Grated nutmeg
- Calf's tongue
- Six hard-boiled eggs
- Cooked crayfish
- Bread cubes (croutons)
- White wine for soaking croutons

## Directions

1. Cook the calf's head until tender.
2. Cut the cooked calf's head into pieces of appropriate size for individual servings.
3. Prepare a financiera garnish separately.
4. Add a couple of glasses of white wine to the sauce, ensuring it is not too thick.
5. Season well with Cayenne pepper or sweet Murcia pepper and grated nutmeg.
6. Cut the calf's tongue into small cubes the size of dice and add to the sauce along with the hard-boiled eggs, also cut into small cubes.
7. Arrange the pieces of calf's head in a pyramid shape on a round, large platter.
8. Pour the financiera sauce over the meat, covering the entire surface.
9. Arrange cooked crayfish over the surface, in number greater than the number of diners, alternating with triangular croutons soaked in white wine and fried, cut to the size of half a calling card.
