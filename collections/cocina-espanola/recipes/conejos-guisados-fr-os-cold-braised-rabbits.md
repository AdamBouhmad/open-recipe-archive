## Ingredients

- roasted rabbits
- salt
- pepper
- salad greens
- lemon slices
- oil
- vinegar
- candied fruit strips
- pomegranate seeds
- confits
- lettuce hearts
- olives (pitted)

## Directions

1. Roast the rabbits
2. Mince all the meat from the loins and legs, leaving only the heads
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Make a salad of all herbs
5. Compose the dish of your minced rabbit and salad, making a ring on the plate, placing small piles of meat alternating with salad
6. In the middle place some well-prepared salad with all the things usually added, with lemon slices, and season with oil and vinegar
7. Decorate the outside with some strips of candied fruit, pomegranate seeds, confits and all the things that are usually put on salads
8. In the middle place some whole lettuce hearts standing upright, and over the minced rabbit or salad place pitted olives
9. This dish is good for afternoon meals, because it should be eaten cold
