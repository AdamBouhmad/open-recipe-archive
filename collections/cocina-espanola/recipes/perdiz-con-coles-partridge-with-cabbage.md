## Ingredients

- 1 partridge, cleaned, emptied, singed and trussed
- thin strips of bacon, salted two hours prior in brine reinforced with pepper and a pinch of nutmeg
- 2 sheets of bacon, one for underneath and one on top
- half a pound of veal loin, sliced
- 2 carrots
- 2 onions with cloves embedded in them
- salt and pepper
- bundle of fine herbs
- 1 glass of fine white wine from San Sadurni de Noya
- 1 bowl of clean broth
- cabbage heads
- half a pound of mixed bacon, cut into slivers
- 1 good piece of Catalan butifarra or a half pound of ham

## Directions

1. Clean, empty, singe and truss the partridge, and lard its entire breast with many very thin strips of bacon that have been placed in brine two hours prior, reinforced with pepper and a pinch of nutmeg
2. Place the partridge in a pot with a perforated removable base, between two broad sheets of bacon, one underneath and one on top, after settling the perforated movable base at the bottom of the vessel
3. Add half a pound of veal loin in slices, 2 carrots, 2 onions with cloves of spice embedded in their flesh, salt and pepper, a bundle of fine herbs, 1 glass of the finest white wine from San Sadurni de Noya and 1 bowl of clean broth
4. Separately, blanch cabbage heads by boiling them 2 or 3 minutes in salted boiling water
5. Drain them well and turn them to air them so they do not retain a drop of water
6. Place them on top of the partridge in the pot, with half a pound of mixed bacon cut into slivers and a good piece of Catalan butifarra or a half pound of ham
7. Moisten everything with sufficient clean broth and cook over very low heat for two hours, with the pot well covered with its lid
8. After this time, take the movable piece of the vessel and remove very slowly and carefully all the contents
9. Place the partridge on a serving platter and set aside the carrots, pieces of veal, butifarra, ham, etc. on another platter, leaving only the cabbages on the movable piece on a plate to squeeze out their juice well, compressing them with a spatula
10. Meanwhile, reduce the sauce from the pot by half, and place the cabbages on top of the partridge, wrapping the bird completely, and on top of the cabbages place the butifarra or ham in slices or small even pieces
11. Make a hash from the veal, bacon, onion if any remains and carrots, on which the partridge will be placed on the platter before garnishing it with the cabbages and butifarra
