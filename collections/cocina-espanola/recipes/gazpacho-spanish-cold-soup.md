## Ingredients

- salt
- one raw bell pepper
- two tomatoes of good color and size
- the crumb of half a loaf of bread
- water for soaking bread
- one small cup of raw olive oil
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. In a mortar, place salt, a raw bell pepper, and two tomatoes of good color and size, crushing everything perfectly.
2. Add the crumb of half a loaf of bread that has been prepared soaked in water, and mash this mixture together.
3. Mix in a small cup of raw olive oil in the same manner as is done with mayonnaise, so it binds well after working it for at least a quarter hour.
4. Add vinegar and water to taste.
5. Pass through a strainer, and to that thick broth that results, add small pieces of bread.
6. Eat in summer, during hours of heat.
