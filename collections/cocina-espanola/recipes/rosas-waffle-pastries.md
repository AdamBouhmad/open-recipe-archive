## Ingredients

- pasta more liquid than for fritters
- sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. The molds should be well greased internally when the liquid is poured into them with a spoon
2. Close the mold, and with a knife clean the dough that oozes out
3. Place the mold on a small stove or brazier like those for ironing
4. Alternatively, dip the small mold in the liquid batter up to half its height
5. Let it drain a little, and quickly submerge it in hot oil
6. Immediately release the dough from the mold
7. Turn it over and remove it to dust with sugar
