## Ingredients

- Leftover partridge meat
- Gelatin
- Truffles
- Egg white
- Crushed ice
- Glazed game broth
- Rice
- Ravigote sauce

## Directions

1. Make balls with the leftover partridge.
2. Prepare molds for galantine and coat the inside with gelatin.
3. Decorate with truffles and egg white, then plunge into crushed ice.
4. Pour gelatin into the molds to a height of about one inch.
5. Coat the partridge balls with glazed game broth.
6. When the gelatin begins to set, place the balls on it, one in each mold.
7. Fill the molds with gelatin, cover with a lid, and place ice on top.
8. When the galantines are firm, remove from the mold and place on a rice base covered with ravigote sauce.
