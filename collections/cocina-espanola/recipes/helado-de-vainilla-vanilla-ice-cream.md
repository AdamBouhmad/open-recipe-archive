## Ingredients

- Milk, one and a half quartillo
- Cream, half a quartillo
- Vanilla, half an ounce
- Sugar, twelve ounces

## Directions

1. Split the vanilla and cut into small pieces
2. Pound with a little sugar in a stone mortar until it pulverizes
3. Put in a pan with the milk, cream, and sugar, and cook until it thickens a little
4. Pass through a cloth, and once it is cold, freeze it
