## Ingredients

- butter or oil for frying
- bread
- one ounce of fried bacon per pound of meat, finely chopped
- one tomato weighing about two ounces
- meat
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- water
- parsley
- garlic

## Directions

1. Heat butter or oil to fry, add a little bread
2. When well fried, remove it, keeping only the oil or butter
3. Coat the meat with flour, a little pepper, and corresponding salt
4. Let it brown (rehogue) until golden
5. Add necessary water and let it cook until tender
6. Make a sauce with parsley, garlic, and everything that was removed when adding the meat
7. After one more boil, it can be served
