## Ingredients

- Beefsteaks, thinner and smaller than for grilling
- Salt and pepper
- Flour
- Pork lard
- Blonde sauce enlarged with broth
- Mushrooms
- Cow butter

## Directions

1. Cut the beefsteaks thinner and smaller than for grilling
2. Season them with salt and pepper on each side, flour them, and fry them in pork lard for about two and a half minutes
3. Turn them constantly so they cook evenly, and when removing them from the pan, drain them very well so they release the fat they contain
4. Prepare a blonde sauce enlarged with broth and incorporate mushrooms that have been previously sautéed in cow butter without allowing it to brown
5. Combine everything and let it cook for half an hour, seasoning the dish when serving according to the taste of the diner
