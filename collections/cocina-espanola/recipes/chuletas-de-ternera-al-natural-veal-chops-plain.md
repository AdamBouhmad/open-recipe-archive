## Ingredients

- veal chops
- beef lard, melted, or refined oil
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the veal chops by cleaning them of skin and greasy parts
2. Flatten them by beating them firmly on both sides with a wooden spatula kept for this purpose
3. Slowly roast them on the grill, taking care with the fire so they do not burn
4. After soaking them well, better than merely coating them, in melted beef lard or refined oil
5. When the chops are on the grill and with each turn given to them, season with salt and pepper without excess
6. Serve them from the grill to the table
