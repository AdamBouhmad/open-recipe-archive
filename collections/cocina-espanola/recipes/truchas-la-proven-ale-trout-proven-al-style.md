## Ingredients

- Fine oil
- Four or five trout of half a pound each
- Salt
- Pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Parsley
- Chives
- Garlic

## Directions

1. In a metal baking dish that will go to the table, pour fine oil to coat the bottom and arrange four or five trout of half a pound each.
2. Season with salt and pepper, and over high heat brown them, moving the baking dish so they do not stick.
3. When browned on one side, turn them over and cover with breadcrumbs mixed with salt, pepper, parsley, chives, and garlic, all chopped very finely.
4. With very low heat below and heat above on the pan lid, cook for about a quarter hour, taking care to frequently moisten the surface with the little oil at the bottom, and serve when it takes on good color.
