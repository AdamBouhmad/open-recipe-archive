## Ingredients

- Bread slices
- Sugar
- Vigil broth
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Take toasted bread slices with which to assemble the soup
2. Place the dish over a little heat, adding ground sugar
3. When the dish is very hot, add enough vigil broth to cover the soup, putting more sugar on top
4. Cover with another plate and let it braise well
5. When the broth is well absorbed into the bread, beat eight egg yolks, adding a little broth, the juice of half a lemon or a few drops of vinegar
6. Pour it all on top, adding more sugar
7. Finish cooking with heat from above inside a bell-shaped cover
