## Ingredients

- potatoes
- beef butter or pork fat

## Directions

1. Peel, wash and well drain the potatoes
2. Place them in a saucepan with a wide base with plenty of beef or pork fat to soak them in, arranging them in a single layer on the bottom
3. Place the saucepan over high heat and also place heat under the lid
4. Turn the potatoes carefully to cook them evenly
