## Ingredients

- Leg of beef
- Chickpeas
- Bacon

## Directions

1. Cook the beef in water with chickpeas, bacon and other ingredients as desired.
2. Serve with the broth.
3. Cold leftovers from good quality pieces make an excellent cold dish for breakfast, prepared in various ways.
