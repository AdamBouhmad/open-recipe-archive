## Ingredients

- Two kilos of salmon
- Three pounds of fish forcemeat
- Pastry dough
- Salt and spices
- Puff pastry, for the second covering

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and bones from two kilos of salmon
2. Prepare three pounds of fish forcemeat
3. Mold a pastry that can contain the salmon and the forcemeat
4. Spread a layer of forcemeat on the bottom
5. Place slices of salmon four centimeters thick on top
6. Sprinkle with salt and spices
7. Alternate layers of forcemeat and salmon until the pastry is full
8. Cover with a pastry lid and then add a second puff pastry covering, and so on successively
