## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 cauliflowers
- salt
- 2 ladles of sauce
- sugar
- butter
- egg yolks
- milk
- poultry broth

## Directions

1. Divide 2 or 3 cauliflowers into florets, trimming the stalks well
2. Cook in salted water for about 12 minutes to blanch
3. Place in cold water, drain well
4. Put with about 2 ladles of boiling sauce
5. Cook for about 20 minutes over very low heat so it reduces without burning
6. Pass through a sieve with poultry broth, then through a strainer
7. Add a little sugar, butter, and egg yolks beaten with milk
8. Serve
