## Ingredients

- pieces of hens, chickens, and fattened capons
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- spicy sauce or Spanish sauce
- flour
- pork or beef fat
- clean broth from stew
- sherry

## Directions

1. Soak the pieces of poultry in oil for a couple of hours, seasoning heavily with salt and pepper.
2. Remove and let drain.
3. Brown and toast the pieces over live coals on grills.
4. Serve as a fine table dish with a spicy sauce or Spanish sauce.
5. Alternatively, prepare a dark flour roux in pork or beef fat, moisten with clean broth from the stew and a tablespoon of sherry.
