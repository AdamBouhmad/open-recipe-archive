## Ingredients

- Orange blossoms, eight ounces
- Sugar, twelve ounces
- Water, two azumbres

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar with the water
2. Pour it boiling over the orange blossoms placed in a vessel, cover well and leave in infusion for six hours
3. Pass through a sieve and freeze
