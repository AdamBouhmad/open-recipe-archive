## Ingredients

- veal liver
- bacon strips
- salt and pepper
- caul fat or well-greased white paper
- meat juice
- shallots
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Lard the veal liver generously with thick strips of bacon.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Wrap well in caul fat or well-greased white paper.
4. Roast on a spit at low heat, as the liver must be well-cooked.
5. Remove the wrapping and place on a serving platter.
6. Pour over it meat juice mixed with a minced shallot mixture and the juice of one lemon.
7. Serve hot.
