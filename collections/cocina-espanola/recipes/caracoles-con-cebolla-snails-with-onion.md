## Ingredients

- snails
- water
- salt
- thyme
- laurel
- parsley
- oregano
- onions
- oil

## Directions

1. Prepare and wash the snails as previously described
2. Place them in cold water and heat progressively until it reaches boiling, skimming well
3. Add salt, thyme, laurel, parsley, and oregano
4. When cooked, drain them well
5. Fry onions in oil and add them to the pan so they sauté together, turning frequently
