## Ingredients

- 3 beef chops in one piece
- 500 grams veal shoulder
- 1 veal hand, deboned and cut into four pieces
- 1 liter concentrated broth
- 12 carrots
- small onions
- beef butter

## Directions

1. Have the butcher remove the bones from the end chops, keeping only the bone from the middle chop with its attached piece of meat.
2. Prepare, lard, season and tie the meat as described in previous formulas.
3. Place in a braising pan with the veal shoulder, veal hand cut into four pieces, and concentrated broth.
4. After two hours of cooking, add the carrots cut lengthwise into equal pieces.
5. Continue cooking for two to three more hours until the meat is very well done.
6. Remove from the pan and remove the string.
7. Place on a serving dish, arranging a row of small onions browned and colored in beef butter around it, and another row of carrots cooked at the same time.
8. Pass the strained, degreased and reduced juice through a sieve and pour over the meat and vegetables at serving time.
