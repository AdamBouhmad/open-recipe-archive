## Ingredients

- sea bream
- salt
- oil
- water
- vinegar
- lemon slices
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the fish well, add a little finely ground salt and leave for one day hanging in the shade in a ventilated place
2. Then cut into pieces and fry in oil until golden
3. This same oil, with one quarter part water and three parts vinegar, some slices of lemon and bay leaves, is poured into the same pot in which the pieces of sea bream have been arranged and covered very well
4. After ten or twelve days it can be served
