## Ingredients

- One cleaned and singed turkey, completely boned raw
- Half pound of veal, minced
- Half pound of raw ham, minced
- One quarter pound of very fresh bacon, minced
- Salt
- Pepper
- All spices
- Cognac
- Truffles, cut like half-pesetas
- Scarlet tongue, in small cubes
- Peeled pistachios

## Directions

1. Bone the turkey completely while raw and separate all its trimmings
2. Shred the meat from the breasts and legs and place in a bowl, seasoning with salt, pepper, and a few drops of oil
3. Let the turkey season for a couple of hours
4. Mince half a pound of veal, half a pound of raw ham, and a quarter pound of very fresh bacon, seasoning well with all spices and moistening with a glass of cognac
5. Knead this mixture with the shredded turkey meat and incorporate truffles cut like half-pesetas, small cubes of scarlet tongue, and some peeled pistachios
6. Shape the mixture into a cylindrical form, wrap it tightly in strong linen cloth with ten or twelve turns, and bind securely with string
7. Place the galantine in a braising pot over coals with a dome-shaped cover on top of the pot
8. Cook for three hours in concentrated broth that covers it
