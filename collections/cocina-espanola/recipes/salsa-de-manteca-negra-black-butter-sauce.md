## Ingredients

- 125 grams of beef butter
- salt
- pepper
- 1 ladle of good vinegar
- 1 small teaspoon of capers

## Directions

1. Melt the beef butter in a pan over fire until it reaches a very dark color
2. Season well and add the ladle of good vinegar
3. Stir vigorously and remove the pan from the fire
4. At the moment of serving, add the small teaspoon of capers to the butter
