## Ingredients

- Sausage meat
- Bread crumbs soaked in water
- Salt
- Sweet pepper
- Pine nuts or almonds
- Oil

## Directions

1. Macerate all ingredients together with oil
2. Use to stuff birds, make empanadas, meat cones, and when egg is added, meatballs to be cooked half an hour before serving in the pot
