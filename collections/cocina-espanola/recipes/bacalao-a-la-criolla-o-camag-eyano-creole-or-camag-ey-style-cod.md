## Ingredients

- Prepared cod
- Six peppercorns
- Parsley
- Six garlic cloves
- Eight toasted hazelnuts
- Bread crumbs
- Vinegar
- Grated nutmeg
- Cold water
- Fried oil
- Garlic
- Lemon juice
- Salt

## Directions

1. After preparing as indicated, dry and cook the cod.
2. When cooked, drain well and place in a casserole.
3. Crush in a mortar six peppercorns, a little parsley, six garlic cloves, eight toasted hazelnuts, bread crumbs, a little vinegar, and grated nutmeg.
4. Dissolve everything with a little cold water and pour into the casserole with the cod.
5. Add fried oil with garlic, lemon juice, and necessary salt.
6. Place on the fire to cook a little and can be served.
