## Ingredients

- veal
- beef fat or pork fat for wrapping
- salt

## Directions

1. Wrap the piece of veal meat destined for roasting in paper greased with beef fat or pork fat
2. Roast over fire, being careful to cook thoroughly
3. A quarter hour before removing the meat from the roaster, remove the paper wrapping so it takes on the golden color it needs
4. Season with salt only at the moment the paper is removed
5. Serve the roasted veal on a platter with all its juices, well degreased
