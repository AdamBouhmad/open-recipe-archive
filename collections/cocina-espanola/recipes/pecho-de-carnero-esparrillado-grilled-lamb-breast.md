## Ingredients

- A piece of lamb breast
- Lard
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Shallots, finely chopped
- Lentil puree, dried pea puree, or bean puree, or sorrel or spinach paste soaked in meat juice

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of lamb breast in the pot of cocido before it is overdone.
2. Remove it, drain it, and coat it with lard to bread with bread crumbs.
3. Grill it on the grill, turning it, and each time dust it with salt, pepper, parsley, and finely chopped shallots.
4. Serve this dish on a puree of lentils, dried peas, or beans, or with sorrel or spinach paste soaked in meat juice.
