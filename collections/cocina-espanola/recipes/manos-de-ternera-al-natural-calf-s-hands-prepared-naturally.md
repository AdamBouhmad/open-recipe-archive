## Ingredients

- Calf's hands, deboned and cut into medium pieces
- Broth
- Fine herbs, finely chopped
- Shallots, finely chopped
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the deboned calf's hands, cut into medium pieces, in clean broth for three hours with the same seasoning and same ingredients as for calf's head prepared naturally.
2. When cooked, serve on a platter in slices or thin pieces.
3. Separately, provide a dry garnish of finely chopped fine herbs and shallots.
4. Each diner seasons their portion on their plate with oil and vinegar.
