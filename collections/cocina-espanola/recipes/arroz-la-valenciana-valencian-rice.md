## Ingredients

- oil or lard
- peppers
- chickens
- ducks
- pork loin
- sausages
- garlic cloves
- tomato
- parsley
- red pepper
- salt
- saffron
- pepper
- clove
- artichokes
- peas
- green beans
- broth or water
- rice
- eel

## Directions

1. Prepare a pan whose bottom equals the size of the burner where it will be placed, with well-lit charcoal or wood fire
2. Add oil or lard in proportion and when well heated, fry several peppers, then remove them to clean when needed
3. Fry pieces of chickens, ducks, pork loin and sausages until golden
4. Add two or three peeled and chopped garlic cloves, tomato, parsley, red pepper, salt, saffron, a little pepper and clove, stirring everything until well fried
5. Add artichokes, peas or green beans cut into pieces, turn twice to stew
6. Add hot broth or water and let boil until everything is cooked
7. Increase the fire, add necessary broth, and when it boils add sufficient rice until it stands straight when stirred with a spoon
8. Cook over high heat, adding the peppers and some eel pieces
9. When half cooked, reduce the fire and let it cook slowly without stirring or moving it
10. When ready, remove it and after resting briefly, serve it
