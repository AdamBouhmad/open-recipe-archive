## Ingredients

- one quarter litre of good white wine
- sugar
- lemon zest
- cinnamon
- seven or eight eggs, using the yolks

## Directions

1. Pour one quarter litre of good white wine, sugar, lemon zest and cinnamon into a saucepan, and bring to a boil.
2. Break seven or eight eggs to use the yolks, which are beaten with a spatula until well combined, also binding them with the wine, which is poured in little by little.
3. Then pass through a sieve and pour into the cups so that it curdles in a water bath.
