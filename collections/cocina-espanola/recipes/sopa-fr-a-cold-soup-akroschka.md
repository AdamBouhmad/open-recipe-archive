## Ingredients

- beer
- ham loin
- slices of cold meats
- raw onion
- shallots
- wheat grains macerated in brine
- slices of pickled gherkins
- ice chips
- crackers or toasted bread

## Directions

1. Soak in beer: ham loins, slices of cold meats, cut raw onion, a strong dose of finely chopped shallots, grains of wheat macerated in brine, slices of pickled gherkins, and very fine ice chips like pine nuts.
2. This forms the foundation of the broth with which crackers or toasted bread are strained.
