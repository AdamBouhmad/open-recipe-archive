## Ingredients

- Sea bream
- Two tablespoons of fried oil
- Water
- Breadcrumbs
- Shallots
- One clove of garlic
- Parsley
- Two tablespoons of fried oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- A pinch of clove spice
- One small glass of white wine
- Orange slices

## Directions

1. In an oval baking dish, place the sea bream well positioned
2. Pour two tablespoons of fried oil and place the sea bream on top, adding water to bathe it at the bottom
3. In a bowl, make a mixture with breadcrumbs, shallots, one clove of garlic and finely chopped parsley; add two tablespoons of fried oil, salt, pepper and a pinch of clove spice, and thin the paste with a small glass of white wine
4. Place the fish in the oven at high heat
5. Leave it for five minutes and remove to coat it well with the paste, which should be somewhat crumbly
6. Make three or four transverse cuts to insert half-slices of orange, and place it back in the oven
7. Serve after twenty minutes
