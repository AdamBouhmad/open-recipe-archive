## Ingredients

- Lamb breast, cut into pieces
- A little lard
- Flour
- Three ladles of hot water
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bay leaf
- A pinch of cumin
- One tablespoon of sweet paprika
- Onions
- Potatoes, equal size or cut equal
- Three green peppers, fried in good oil beforehand

## Directions

1. Brown the lamb pieces in very little lard and dust with flour until well browned, without burning.
2. Immediately wet with three ladles of hot water and season with salt, pepper, bay leaf, a pinch of cumin, and one tablespoon of sweet paprika.
3. Add onions and potatoes of equal size or cut equally.
4. Cook everything over very low heat for three hours.
5. One hour before serving, incorporate the strips of the three green peppers that have been fried beforehand in good oil and drained before adding them to the pot.
