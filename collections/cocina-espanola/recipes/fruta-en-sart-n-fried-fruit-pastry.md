## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of flour
- 6 eggs, yolks and whites
- a little ground salt
- 2 tablespoons of brandy
- a little orange blossom water or zest of 1 lemon
- water and milk in equal proportions

## Directions

1. In a pot or saucepan, place 2 pounds of flour, 6 eggs (yolks and whites), a little ground salt, and 2 tablespoons of brandy, as well as a little orange blossom water or zest of 1 lemon
2. Dissolve all this with a proportionate quantity of water and an equal quantity of milk to obtain a kind of porridge, more thin than thick
3. Leave in this state for 2 or 3 hours
4. When ready to fry, place the pan on the fire with a little lard or a couple of tablespoons of oil
5. When hot, pour a spoonful of batter that spreads in the pan to form a very thin layer
6. When fried on one side, turn it over by striking the pan handle
7. Remove, dust with powdered sugar, and continue the operation until all the batter is prepared
