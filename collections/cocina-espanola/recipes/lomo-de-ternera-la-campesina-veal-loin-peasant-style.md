## Ingredients

- a good veal loin cut lengthwise
- thin strips of bacon
- lemon slices
- raw onion slices
- oil
- salt and pepper
- butcher's twine
- white greased paper
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Lard the fattiest part of a good veal loin cut lengthwise with thin strips of bacon.
2. Place in a long serving dish, and place on top about six lemon slices and as many raw onion slices.
3. Pour in enough oil so the meat becomes well soaked and the oil overflows around the veal in the dish.
4. Season with salt and pepper in moderation.
5. After a quarter hour, turn the meat over, seasoning again.
6. After twenty minutes, wrap the veal loin, tying it well with butcher's twine, in white greased paper, and cook in a pan with the rest of the marinade.
7. When the meat is almost cooked, remove the paper and finish cooking over very low heat.
8. Serve with a little of its own juice and potatoes arranged around it, cooked beforehand and browned in the frying.
