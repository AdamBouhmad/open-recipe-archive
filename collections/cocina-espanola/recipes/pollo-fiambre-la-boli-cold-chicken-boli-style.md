## Ingredients

- One good chicken, boned
- Very thin slices of bacon
- Very small chorizos, more or less spicy according to taste
- A few lightly salted anchovies

## Directions

1. Bone a good chicken and fill it with the following: first, very thin slices of bacon; second, very small chorizos, more or less spicy according to your taste; third, a few lightly salted anchovies
2. After filling the bird, roast it on a spit, not in an oven, skillet, or pot
3. Let it cool for at least twelve hours
4. Finally, place it between two large pieces of crumb bread, which by pressing the chicken without destroying it, will become soaked with the juice it releases
