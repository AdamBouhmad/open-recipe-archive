## Ingredients

- milk, one liter
- noodles, thirty grams
- salt
- sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Add thirty grams of noodles per liter of milk to boiling milk
2. Be careful that the soup does not become too thick
3. Season only with a pinch of salt and sugar to the consumer's taste
