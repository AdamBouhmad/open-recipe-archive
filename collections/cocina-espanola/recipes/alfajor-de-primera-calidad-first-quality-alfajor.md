## Ingredients

- One azumbre of white honey
- Three medios of hazelnuts and one pound of almonds, all toasted and chopped
- One and a half ounces of cinnamon powder
- Two ounces of aniseed, four drachms of clove and four drachms of cilantro, all toasted and ground
- One pound of toasted sesame
- Eight pounds of flour powder, extracted from hard biscuits without salt or yeast, very well baked in the oven
- Half a pound of sugar

## Directions

1. With half a pound of sugar make a syrup
2. Then add the honey, and when it reaches the proper point, add the prepared ingredients, three handfuls of sifted flour and the flour powder
3. Stir it so that everything is well mixed
4. Make the balls while hot
5. Bathe them in syrup
6. Cover them with fine sugar with some cinnamon, and wrap them in paper
7. In each pound of balls there should be from eight to twelve, so that they turn out well
8. Note: The difficulty and secret of the alfajor lies in the proper point of the honey; practice is the teacher here, as in everything
