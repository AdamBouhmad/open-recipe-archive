## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Butter
- Salsa a la pebre

## Directions

1. Take the young partridges as is customary
2. Split them into two pieces along the kidneys
3. Place them in a pan
4. Sauté them in butter, stirring well
5. Place them on the grill as they fit
6. Serve with salsa a la pebre
