## Ingredients

- sea bream
- oil
- water
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. In an oval serving dish, in which the sea bream is well placed, pour a couple of spoonfuls of already fried oil, and on top place the sea bream, adding water to bathe it at the base
2. In a bowl make a mixture with bread crumbs
