## Ingredients

- cooked beef tongue
- white wine
- melted beef butter
- toasted bread crumbs
- beef butter, melted
- parsley
- shallots
- cumin
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place the cooked tongue in very well-cut slices in a baking dish or round serving dish that can go in the oven.
2. Moisten with white wine and pour a little melted beef butter over it.
3. Cover the entire dish with toasted bread crumbs kneaded with melted beef butter, parsley, shallots, and a little cumin, all very finely chopped.
4. Cook in the oven over strong heat for twenty minutes.
5. If there is no oven in the kitchen, place the pan over low heat so the dish does not brown underneath, and cover the pan or dish with a camp oven lid, well loaded with hot coals on top, for half an hour of cooking.
