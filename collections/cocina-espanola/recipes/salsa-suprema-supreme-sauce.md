## Ingredients

- glaze
- reduced beef broth
- 50 grams fresh beef butter
- verjuice or juice of one sour lemon

## Directions

1. In a saucepan over high heat, add at once a measure of glaze and another of reduced beef broth.
2. Let reduce by half, and add 50 grams of fresh beef butter.
3. At the moment of serving, the classic way is to flavor the sauce with a spoonful of verjuice, and when this is lacking, replace it with all the juice of one sour lemon.
