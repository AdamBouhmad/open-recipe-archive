## Ingredients

- Water
- Chickpeas
- Beef or mutton, well washed and skinned
- Ham
- Bacon
- Small onion
- Half hen
- Turkey offal
- Salt
- Warm water as needed

## Directions

1. In a pot proportioned to the food to be cooked, add water and once hot, add the chickpeas and meat well washed and skinned; for each pound of beef or mutton, add half a pound of chickpeas
2. When it begins to boil, skim it, being careful not to overdo it so as not to deprive the stew of its substance
3. Two hours later, add some ham, bacon and a small onion
4. Let it all boil at low heat, seasoning with salt and adding warm water from time to time
5. If half a hen and turkey offal are added to this mixture, one will achieve with such simple and easy method the best of ordinary stew
6. The broth serves to soak all stews in which a liquid substance is needed without resorting to water
7. The vegetables are cooked separately with aged bacon, chorizo or blood sausage
