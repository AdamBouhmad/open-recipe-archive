## Ingredients

- salt cod soaked at least twenty-four hours
- onion cut into thick slices or rings
- tomato
- garlic cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- oil
- cloves
- pepper
- sweet pimiento

## Directions

1. After having soaked in water for at least twenty-four hours, divide into pieces and arrange as follows: place in a casserole a good layer of onion cut into thick slices or rings, tomato, some garlic cloves well divided, and a little pepper and cinnamon
2. On top of this place another layer of cod pieces, on top of that another layer of onion, tomato, and garlic, then another layer of cod, and so continue until the casserole is full
3. Pour oil abundantly over everything, cloves, pepper, and sweet pimiento, and put on low heat, without adding broth until the juice of the onion and tomato has been consumed
