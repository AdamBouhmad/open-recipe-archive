## Ingredients

- rabbit
- 125 grams beef or pork lard
- 125 grams bacon, cut into small squares
- 1 heaping tablespoon flour
- 2 ladles of broth
- 2 ladles of red wine
- 1 good river eel
- bouquet of fine herbs
- 2 dozen small onions
- pork lard for the onions
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the rabbit as in a gibelota, but using red wine instead of white wine.
2. When seasoning, add a good river eel cut into pieces the same size as the rabbit pieces.
3. Cook everything together and serve.
