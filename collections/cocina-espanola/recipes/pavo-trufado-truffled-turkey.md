## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Truffles

## Directions

1. Fill the turkey with truffles, increasing the quantity in proportion to the volume of the turkey
2. Account for the cooking time, as a turkey requires more time than a fattened hen or capon
3. The turkey may rest with the truffles inside for five or six days in a cool place with no humidity, as dampness would spoil the dish and make the flavor repugnant
