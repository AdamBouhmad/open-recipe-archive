## Ingredients

- crusts of hard bread
- turnips
- potatoes
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook in water with salt and a piece of butter, crusts of hard bread, turnips and potatoes cut into slices and pieces of equal size.
2. When everything has cooked, mash it and pass through a fine sieve.
3. Return the pot to the fire and clarify the broth if it is thick.
4. Add a handful of finely chopped parsley and another piece of butter.
5. For meat days, meat broth makes this soup more excellent.
