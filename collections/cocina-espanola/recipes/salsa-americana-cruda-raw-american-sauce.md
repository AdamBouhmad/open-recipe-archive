## Ingredients

- cooked yellow squash
- three or four garlic cloves
- three roasted tomatoes, peeled
- sufficient salt
- a few grains of pepper
- half a small cup of vinegar
- double quantity of broth
- one small onion
- a little finely chopped parsley
- sweet pepper, all raw and cold

## Directions

1. Mash all the squash, garlic, roasted tomatoes, salt, and pepper in a mortar
2. Add half a small cup of vinegar with double the quantity of broth
3. Add one small onion, a little finely chopped parsley, and sweet pepper, all raw and cold
4. This sauce is very good for vegetables
