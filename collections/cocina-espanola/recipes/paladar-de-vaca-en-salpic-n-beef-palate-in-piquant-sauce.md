## Ingredients

- palate
- salt
- vinegar
- pepper
- herbs
- garlic
- parsley
- oil

## Directions

1. Place in a plate salt, a spoonful of vinegar, and pepper
2. Beat it all together
3. Add fine herbs, garlic, parsley, all chopped, and three spoonfuls of oil
4. Cook the pieces of palate cut into thin slices in this mixture
