## Ingredients

- meat
- butter or oil
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- water
- spice

## Directions

1. Reheat the meat in a pan or cooking vessel with butter or oil, parsley, onion and chopped garlic
2. When the meat is half cooked, transfer to a pot or cooking vessel
3. Add a little water and let cook, seasoning with a grain of spice
