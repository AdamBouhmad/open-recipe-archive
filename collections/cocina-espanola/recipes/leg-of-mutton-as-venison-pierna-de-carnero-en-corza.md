## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- white wine
- strong vinegar
- salt
- peppercorns
- thyme
- rosemary
- tarragon
- juniper berries
- mint stems
- bacon strips

## Directions

1. Make a marinade with good white wine and very strong vinegar, plenty of salt, peppercorns, thyme, rosemary, tarragon, some juniper berries, and one or two mint stems.
2. Prepare the leg of mutton as a venison quarter would be prepared, that is, skinned and larded with thin strips of bacon.
3. Soak the leg, well bathed in the marinade, for three days.
4. Remove the meat from the marinade and roast it carefully on a spit.
