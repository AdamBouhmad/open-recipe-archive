## Ingredients

- bread, grated
- cheese, grated - 4 ounces for forty cups
- chard
- lettuce
- liver
- parsley
- sorrel
- pepper
- saffron
- clove
- cinnamon
- salt
- meat
- broth
- fifty eggs for one hundred cups

## Directions

1. Cook the chard, lettuce, and liver
2. Chop all together with a handful of parsley and sorrel
3. Place the chopped mixture in the pot where the soup is to be made, with the cheese
4. Add pepper, saffron, clove, and cinnamon
5. When the meat is cooked, season it with salt, saffron, and pepper so it takes on color
6. Pour the broth into the pot with the herbs and bring to a boil
7. Gradually add bread, stirring with a ladle until it thickens
8. Remove from heat and place in a straw basket to rest
9. Prepare a small pot with a bit of warm broth
10. Beat fifty eggs in another pot for one hundred cups and mix with the warm broth
11. Gradually pour the egg mixture into the bureto pot, stirring constantly so it does not become lumpy
