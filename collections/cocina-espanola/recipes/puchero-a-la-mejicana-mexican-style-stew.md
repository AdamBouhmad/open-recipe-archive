## Ingredients

- two pounds veal
- two pounds beef
- a piece of Veracruz or Chicago salt pork
- half a cabbage
- one lettuce
- two or three turnips
- carrots
- a handful of soaked chickpeas
- turkey scraps
- half a chicken
- some roasted lamb bones
- a little parsley
- three cloves
- one roasted onion

## Directions

1. Take a pot containing about two jars of cold water
2. Add two pounds veal, two pounds beef and a piece of salt pork
3. Put on low fire
4. Add two more jars of water when the first ones have lost their coldness
5. Increase the fire to produce foam
6. Remove the foam as it rises until no more appears
7. After boiling about eight hours on equal fire, and when it has cooked four hours, add half a cabbage, one lettuce, two or three turnips, equal quantity of carrots, a handful of soaked chickpeas, scraps of a turkey, half a chicken and some roasted lamb bones, a little parsley, three cloves and one roasted onion
8. Add hot water if needed until the eight hours are completed
