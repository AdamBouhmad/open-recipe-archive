## Ingredients

- one pound of meat cut into small pieces like hazelnuts
- half pound of chopped onions
- one head of garlic
- two or three tablespoons of oil
- paprika
- salt
- four pounds of potatoes cut into slices
- water

## Directions

1. In an earthenware pot in the evening, taking advantage of the day's fire, reheat or recook one pound of meat cut into small pieces, half pound of chopped onions, one head of garlic, and two or three tablespoons of oil, reinforced with a little paprika and corresponding salt
2. Let this cook during the night, burning well
3. One hour before the next day's lunch, pour the contents of the pot into a pot, fill with water, add four pounds of potatoes cut into slices, and serve at the table for twelve people to eat
