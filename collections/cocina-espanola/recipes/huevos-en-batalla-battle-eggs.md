## Ingredients

- 12 eggs for 6 people
- very fine slices of raw ham
- pork butter
- salt
- pepper
- dried parsley

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites separately and keep the yolks, two by two, in small bowls.
2. In a round baking dish at least 20 centimeters in diameter, place very fine slices of raw ham on the bottom, covering it completely.
3. Pour pork butter over it and place the dish over strong heat on a griddle rather than a burner.
4. When the fat begins to sizzle, pour all the beaten egg whites over it.
5. As soon as the whites set, begin adding the yolks.
6. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and dried parsley.
