## Ingredients

- quail
- fresh bacon
- water or broth
- onion
- parsley
- garlic
- spices

## Directions

1. After plucking and cleaning well, melt fresh bacon that is not rancid and fry the quail whole.
2. When browned, remove them and place in a pot with water or broth from the boiled meat.
3. In the same grease, fry onion, parsley, chopped garlic, and spices.
4. Add everything to the pot and cover it with brown paper.
5. Cook at low fire.
