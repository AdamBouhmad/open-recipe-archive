## Ingredients

- meat from leg
- salt
- butter
- bacon
- garlic
- parsley
- toasted garlic
- pine nuts or almonds

## Directions

1. Take meat from leg, cut into small pieces and put in a pot with salt, butter, bacon, garlic and chopped parsley
2. Before cooking make a sauce with toasted garlic, parsley, pine nuts or almonds
3. Let cook for a while
