## Ingredients

- One azumbre of white honey
- Three half libras of hazelnuts, toasted and chopped
- One libra of almonds, toasted and chopped
- One and a half onza of cinnamon powder
- Two onzas of aniseed, toasted and ground
- Four drachmes of clove, toasted and ground
- Four drachmes of cilantro, toasted and ground
- One libra of sesame, toasted

## Directions

1. Prepare the white honey
2. Toast and chop the hazelnuts and almonds
3. Powder the cinnamon
4. Toast the aniseed and grind it
5. Toast and grind the cloves and cilantro
6. Toast the sesame
