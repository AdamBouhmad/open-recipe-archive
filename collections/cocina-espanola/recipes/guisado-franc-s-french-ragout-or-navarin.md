## Ingredients

- one kilogram of mutton with bone, cut into large pieces like eggs
- one quarter pound of butter
- one pound of chopped onions
- flour
- water in volume four times greater than the pot contents
- salt
- pepper
- carrots
- small onions
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Heat the butter and brown the cut mutton pieces until they achieve a blonde color in a wide pot
2. Add one pound of chopped onions
3. When they have taken color, dust lightly with flour and add water in a volume four times greater than the pot contents
4. Let boil over low heat for three hours
5. At half cooking time, season and incorporate carrots, small onions, and potatoes, which must always cook over low heat
6. The onion will disappear and the sauce will reduce to just enough liquid so that all the meats and vegetables are well impregnated, resulting in a thick consistency
7. Serve when the meat and vegetables are well cooked
