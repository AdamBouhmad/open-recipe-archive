## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- salt
- fine oil
- garlic
- parsley
- bread

## Directions

1. Clean the mushrooms well in water with plenty of salt.
2. Dry them.
3. Fry them in fine oil with a good quantity of minced garlic and parsley.
4. Serve very hot with slices of toasted bread on the side.
5. Serve with white wine to drink.
