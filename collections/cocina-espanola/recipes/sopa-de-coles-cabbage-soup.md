## Ingredients

- cabbage or kale (curly kale and white head varieties are best)
- 250 to 500 grams fresh pork knuckle (codillo)
- broth from previous day's puchero
- water for blanching

## Directions

1. Trim and wash the cabbages thoroughly in several changes of fresh water.
2. Blanch them by scalding with boiling water for ten minutes.
3. After blanching, drain the cabbages thoroughly.
4. Place them in the bottom of a wide, flat-bottomed pot with the fresh pork knuckle.
5. Pour broth from the previous day's puchero over them.
6. Cook very slowly until the pork knuckle and cabbages are well cooked.
7. Strain the broth to clarify the soup, placing a portion of the kale on top and reserving the remainder.
