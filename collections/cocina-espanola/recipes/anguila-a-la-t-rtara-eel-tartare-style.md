## Ingredients

- one eel
- broth
- reduced sauce
- breadcrumbs
- eggs
- tartare sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the eel into pieces and cook in broth sauce
2. When cooked, remove and drain
3. Pass the cooking liquid through a strainer and decrease it by reducing
4. Incorporate several spoonfuls of well-reduced thick sauce
5. Place the sauce in a pan to cool
6. Pass the eel pieces through the cooled sauce and breadcrumbs
7. Then pass through beaten eggs and breadcrumbs again
8. Twenty minutes before serving, place on the grill with low heat over a sheet of paper
9. Arrange on a platter in pyramid form
10. Serve with tartare sauce on the side in a sauceboat
