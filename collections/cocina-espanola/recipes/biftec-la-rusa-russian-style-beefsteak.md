## Ingredients

- one slice of sirloin four centimeters thick and the full length
- salt
- pepper
- filling of meat or poultry
- kitchen twine
- white paper
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Cut from a sirloin a slice four centimeters thick and of the full length.
2. Extend it on the cutting board and beat both sides, sprinkling it with salt and pepper.
3. Prepare a filling of meat or poultry, and mold it into a roll or sausage of one inch thick and shorter than the width of the meat blade.
4. Wrap the filling in the meat, rolling it well, and tie it with kitchen twine so that the filling does not escape.
5. Thus prepared, the beefsteak is roasted on the spit after having wrapped it in white paper greased with fresh butter.
6. According to the number and according to the appetite that the diners are presumed to have, prepare one or more beefsteaks, which will be roasted at the same time, skewered together on the roasting spit.
7. They must cook before good fire for at least an hour and a half, and twenty minutes before serving remove the paper and baste continuously with its own juices.
8. Serve these beefsteaks with Spanish sauce apart, or with any other, and if you do not want to spend much, prepare a spicy sauce or take advantage of the juice from the meat of the roast from the previous day.
