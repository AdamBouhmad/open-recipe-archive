## Ingredients

- tripe, cleaned and prepared
- onions
- parsley
- carrots
- bay leaf
- basil
- cloves
- salt
- paprika
- water
- melted butter
- scallions
- garlic, finely chopped
- pepper
- hot sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the cleaned tripe with onions, parsley, carrots, bay leaf, basil, cloves, salt, paprika, and sufficient water.
2. Remove and drain the tripe, then cut into thick pieces about four fingers in size.
3. Coat the pieces in melted butter mixed with parsley, scallions, finely chopped garlic, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve with a very spicy sauce.
