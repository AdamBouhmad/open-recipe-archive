## Ingredients

- sole
- flour
- oil for frying
- mushrooms
- parsley
- scallions
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- oil
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the sole as if for frying, and make an opening along the spine where the dark skin is
2. Wrap it in flour and fry it
3. After letting it drain, carefully remove the spine
4. Prepare a filling composed of fried mushrooms, finely chopped with parsley, scallions, salt, pepper and nutmeg, seasoned with oil and lemon juice
5. After kneading the filling, reheat it well and introduce it in place of the spine to form a kind of savory sausage with the fish
