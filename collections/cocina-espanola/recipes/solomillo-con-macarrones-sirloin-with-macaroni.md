## Ingredients

- pieces of sirloin
- beef or pork butter
- Spanish sauce or broth with meat juice
- Italian macaroni
- lean ham
- truffles
- concentrated broth
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Sauté the sirloin pieces in beef or pork butter and season them in Spanish sauce or broth with meat juice
2. Arrange the sirloin on a platter as in the previous recipe
3. Cook good Italian macaroni in broth without fat
4. Separately, cook 60 grams of lean ham and 60 grams of trimmed truffles
5. Cook the truffles and ham slowly for twenty minutes in concentrated broth with a glass of Sherry
6. Incorporate the truffles and ham into the cooked macaroni
7. Fill the hollow formed by the meat slices on the platter with this macaroni garnish
