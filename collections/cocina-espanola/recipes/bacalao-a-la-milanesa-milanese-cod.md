## Ingredients

- cod
- sauce made with broth
- tomato
- cebolletas (small onions)
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. After desalting, cooking and cleaning as described for the previous recipe, place it in flakes in a pot between two fires, in a roasting pan, with half a quartillo of sauce made with broth, tomato, a little chopped cebolletas and two good tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese
2. Place the cod in this sauce, stirring well
