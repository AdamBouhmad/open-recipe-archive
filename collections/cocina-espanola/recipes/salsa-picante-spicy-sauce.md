## Ingredients

- two or three finely chopped shallots
- one cacillo of good vinegar
- two cacillos of broth
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- thirty to fifty grams of pickled gherkins

## Directions

1. For meat days, cook two or three very finely chopped shallots with one cacillo of good vinegar until the liquid is consumed
2. Then moisten with two cacillos of broth and, according to each person's taste, add salt, pepper, nutmeg, etc.
3. Let cook over low heat for twenty minutes
4. At the moment of using this sauce in a dish, incorporate thirty to fifty grams of finely chopped pickled gherkins
