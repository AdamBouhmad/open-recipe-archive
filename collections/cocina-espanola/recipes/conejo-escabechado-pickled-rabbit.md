## Ingredients

- rabbit
- bacon and raw ham for larding
- olive oil
- thyme
- bay leaf
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Remove the bones and lard the rabbit with bacon and raw ham.
2. Close and tie with twine.
3. Put in a pot with olive oil, thyme, and bay leaf, and brown until it no longer releases liquid.
4. Remove, drain, and cut into pieces that fit into jars with wide mouths.
5. When no more pieces fit in a jar, fill with olive oil and cover with moistened parchment.
6. This way it can be preserved for a long time.
7. To serve, slice the pieces into very thin rounds and arrange in a serving dish decorated with chopped parsley and oil.
