## Ingredients

- oil
- onion
- assorted fine herbs
- equal parts broth and white wine
- spinach greens
- parsley
- chervil

## Directions

1. Sauté onion and all manner of fine herbs with oil
2. Moisten with equal parts broth and white wine
3. Cook as the previous sauce
4. At the moment of serving, strain
5. Add spinach greens, parsley, and chervil
