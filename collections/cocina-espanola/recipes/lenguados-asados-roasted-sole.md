## Ingredients

- Sole fish
- Butter
- Fine herbs
- Chopped garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Breadcrumbs
- Mustard
- White wine
- Mushrooms
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. In a baking dish or fish kettle, place a sauce of butter and fine herbs.
2. Add chopped garlic, salt and pepper.
3. Place the sole on top, covered with a light layer of grated bread crust and melted mustard.
4. Add a little white wine and some mushrooms.
5. Place in the oven at low heat.
6. When done, remove and add lemon juice.
7. Alternatively, it can be placed over coals on the baking dish cover.
