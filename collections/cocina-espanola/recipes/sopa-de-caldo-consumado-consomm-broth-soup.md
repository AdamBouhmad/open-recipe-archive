## Ingredients

- Two liters of water
- One kilogram of beef, carefully cleaned of all fat
- Vegetables and seasoning as used in a good puchero
- One hen

## Directions

1. In two liters of water with the same vegetables and seasoning as used in a good puchero, cook one kilogram of beef, with the piece carefully cleaned of all fat.
2. At the same time, over very high heat, half-roast a hen, that is, color and brown it well, without it being cooked inside.
3. Place the hen in the pot and let it cook until the beef is perfectly cooked.
4. This operation cannot last less than eight hours. The boiling shall be very slow, but without any interruption, and the broth should reduce by half.
5. This consommé broth, which is taken plain or which serves as the best broth for all broth-based soups, is easy to make and is not relatively expensive for a household that habitually prepares it. The hen meat, minced, has many applications: for fillings, croquettes, etc.
