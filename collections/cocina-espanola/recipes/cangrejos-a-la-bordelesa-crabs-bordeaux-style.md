## Ingredients

- four dozen crabs
- two ounces of very good butter from cows
- one tablespoon of refined oil
- one large onion the size of a lemon
- parsley
- tarragon
- thyme
- bay leaf
- clove
- nutmeg
- salt
- pepper
- two cloves of garlic
- one heaping tablespoon of flour
- half a quarter-liter of water

## Directions

1. Fry in two ounces of very good butter from cows, mixed with one tablespoon of refined oil, one large onion the size of a lemon, chopped very finely, parsley also chopped, tarragon, thyme, bay leaf, clove, nutmeg, salt, pepper and two cloves of garlic.
2. When the onion takes on color add one heaping tablespoon of flour, and when it has toasted well incorporate half a quarter-liter of water at the same time as the crabs, well washed and freshly cleaned.
3. Over very vivid fire, and with the saucepan covered, sauté the contents for three minutes and let cook over gentle fire for ten.
