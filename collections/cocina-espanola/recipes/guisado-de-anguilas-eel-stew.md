## Ingredients

- eels
- garlic
- parsley
- pepper
- saffron
- clove
- cinnamon
- salt
- water
- pine nuts or hazelnuts
- bread crumb
- egg

## Directions

1. Choose river eels that are best, preferring those with dark back and white belly
2. Cut eels into pieces, wash well and put in a cooking vessel
3. Add garlic, chopped parsley, pepper, saffron, clove and cinnamon
4. Cook with water and salt
5. As they cook, make a sauce of pine nuts or hazelnuts with a piece of moistened bread and garlic
6. If the hazelnuts are toasted, the bread should also be toasted
7. Add the sauce and let give a couple of boils, moving the vessel so it does not scorch and adding salt in proportion
