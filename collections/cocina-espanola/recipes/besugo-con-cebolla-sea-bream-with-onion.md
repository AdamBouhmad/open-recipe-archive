## Ingredients

- sea bream
- onion
- bread
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. After cleaning, put whole or cut into slices, to fry in a casserole with chopped onion
2. When the onion is already fried, toast a little bread, crush it with a grain of pepper, dissolve it in broth from the pot and pour it over the sea bream
