## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon flour
- salt
- white pepper in powder
- a pinch of nutmeg
- 30 grams beef butter
- 2 cloves

## Directions

1. Mix together in a pan 1 tablespoon of flour with ground salt, white pepper in powder and a pinch of nutmeg
2. Add 30 grams of beef butter and 2 cloves of spices
3. Place over low heat with enough water to form a clear paste; stir constantly and gradually add 250 grams of beef butter in parts
4. Keep this sauce on the fire for a quarter hour without stopping to stir, being careful that it does not boil, as it would spoil
5. Before serving, strain through a metal mesh strainer
