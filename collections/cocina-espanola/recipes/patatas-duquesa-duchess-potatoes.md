## Ingredients

- Potato puree
- Cow butter
- Beaten eggs
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Lard or cow butter for frying

## Directions

1. Make croquettes or small meatballs of potato puree, manipulated with cow butter, beaten eggs, and plenty of finely chopped parsley.
2. Fry them in lard or sauté them in cow butter.
