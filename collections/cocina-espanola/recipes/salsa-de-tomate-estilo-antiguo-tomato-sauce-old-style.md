## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- cumin, ground
- broth from the pot

## Directions

1. Peel the tomatoes, which is achieved by putting them in water and boiling for a moment so they release the skin easily
2. Make them into a paste, dissolving this paste in broth from the pot and adding some ground cumin
3. If the tomato was preserved in dried form, it will be necessary to put it in water with the vessel over a pot that is on the fire, so that it heats with the steam, taking care to stir it from time to time so that it dissolves perfectly, and in this state it can be served
