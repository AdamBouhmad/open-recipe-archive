## Ingredients

- half pound of onions, finely chopped
- two ounces of beef butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- one liter of cold water
- egg yolk
- bread slices, fried or toasted
- quarter pound of grated Gruyere cheese

## Directions

1. Fry the finely chopped onions in a pan or pot with beef butter
2. Season and spice to taste
3. When the onion has taken on much color and before it begins to toast at the edges, add one tablespoon of flour
4. When it is golden, add one liter of cold water, pouring it little by little and letting it boil each time
5. Let it cook slowly for a few minutes
6. Remove from heat and bind with egg yolk as explained for other soups
7. Place fried or toasted bread slices in the soup tureen, sprinkled with grated Gruyere cheese
8. Stir everything with a spatula as you pour the broth into the tureen to prevent the cheese from clumping
9. Serve hot
