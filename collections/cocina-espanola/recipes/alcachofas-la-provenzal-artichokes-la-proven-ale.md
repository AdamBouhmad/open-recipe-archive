## Ingredients

- artichokes
- oil
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare and clean the artichokes and soak them in oil.
2. Place them in a casserole with oil, garlic, salt and pepper.
3. Let them cook with very low heat, and place on top a lid with well-lit embers.
4. Once they are cooked, remove the garlic, place them on a serving dish to sprinkle them afterwards with lemon juice before serving.
