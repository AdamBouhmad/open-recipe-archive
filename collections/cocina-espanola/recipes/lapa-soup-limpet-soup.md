## Ingredients

- many limpets
- seawater
- a little oil
- a large quantity of garlic
- flour
- a little paprika
- crackers

## Directions

1. Cook many limpets in seawater
2. Remove the shellfish from their shells
3. Sauté them separately in a little oil with a large quantity of garlic
4. Sprinkle with flour and a little paprika
5. Add the water in which they cooked, passed through a strainer
6. Crumble crackers to serve the dish as a soup
