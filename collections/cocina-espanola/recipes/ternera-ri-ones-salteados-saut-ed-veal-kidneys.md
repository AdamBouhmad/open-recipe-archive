## Ingredients

- veal kidneys
- flour
- lard or butter
- broth or water
- parsley
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Heat fat in a pan over high heat and cook the kidneys until they take on color
2. Remove the kidneys from the pan and set aside on a plate
3. In the same fat, brown a good spoonful of flour, and before it takes on much color, deglaze with broth or water
4. Incorporate finely chopped parsley and bring the sauce to a boil
5. Add the kidneys back to the pan and cook for three minutes
6. Serve hot and the kidneys will be tender
