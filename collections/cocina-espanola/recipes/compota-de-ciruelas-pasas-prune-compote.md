## Ingredients

- 500 grams dried plums
- water
- 5 grams cinnamon stick

## Directions

1. Weigh 500 grams of dried plums; wash them and place them in a saucepan with a capacity of 1 liter, which is then filled with water.
2. Cook over low heat and add 5 grams of cinnamon stick.
