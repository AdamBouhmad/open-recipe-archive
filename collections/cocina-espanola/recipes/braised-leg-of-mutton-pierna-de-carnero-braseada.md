## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- strips of good bacon
- carrots
- onions
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- good degreased broth

## Directions

1. Debone the meat and lard it with strips of good bacon, securing it then with kitchen twine so it does not deform
2. Place the meat in a braising pot with many strips of bacon, carrots, onions, fine herbs and an exaggerated seasoning of salt, pepper, clove and nutmeg
3. Pour good degreased broth over it in sufficient quantity to bathe the meat
4. Break the leg bone into three pieces
5. Cook over high heat until the boiling becomes vigorous, and from then on, over low heat, cover the pot with coals on top and leave until properly cooked, testing doneness by introducing a larding needle into the meat from time to time
6. The true way to prepare this dish consists in allowing all the broth to be consumed by the slow cooking, so that when serving the meat, the juice accompanying it in the serving dish is scarcely abundant
