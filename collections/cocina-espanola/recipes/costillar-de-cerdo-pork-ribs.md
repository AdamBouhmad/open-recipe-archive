## Ingredients

- ribs of roasted pork
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. When many ribs of roasted pork are cooked in one piece and some are left over, cut them when cold
2. Soak them in oil
3. Dust with salt and pepper
4. Bread them with breadcrumbs
5. Turn them on the grill over very gentle heat
