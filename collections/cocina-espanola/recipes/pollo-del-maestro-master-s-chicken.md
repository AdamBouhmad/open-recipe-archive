## Ingredients

- one plump chicken with full breasts
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- two small glasses of good cognac
- butter from Isigny

## Directions

1. Fill the chicken with oil as if it were a vessel and rinse it, then empty it and let it drain.
2. Incorporate salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg into the interior.
3. Finish this interior preparation with a splash of two small glasses of good cognac, of the kind drunk by Barbieri, from Cognac in the Lower Charente, a French department.
4. Once this is done and the chicken is on the spit, coat it with butter from Isigny and roast it over a brisk fire.
