## Ingredients

- Lamb chops
- Cow butter
- Vinegar
- Meat juices
- Potatoes
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare and beat the lamb chops.
2. Sauté them in cow butter, turning repeatedly, completing this in five or six minutes.
3. With the same butter used for sautéing the meat, make a light brown sauce; add a thread of vinegar and a couple of spoonfuls of meat juice if available.
4. Pour this sauce over the chops, which will be waiting on a platter, with some garnish in the center or around.
5. Serve with potatoes, also sautéed separately in cow butter.
