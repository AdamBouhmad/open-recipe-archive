## Ingredients

- Sole fish
- Milk
- Flour
- Abundant oil for frying
- Parsley for frying
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Make an incision on the darker side of the fish down the middle, from head to tail.
2. Soak in milk for half an hour.
3. Drain well and flour heavily.
4. Submerge in abundant hot oil for frying.
5. Fry for only a few minutes until done.
6. Serve adorned with fried parsley, which is eaten with the fish, well sprinkled by the diner with lemon juice.
