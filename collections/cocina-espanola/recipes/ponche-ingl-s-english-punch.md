## Ingredients

- 60 grams green tea
- zest of 2 lemons
- 1 quartillo boiling water
- 250 grams ground sugar
- juice of 2 lemons
- 1 bottle rum

## Directions

1. In a large teapot, steep the green tea and lemon zest in boiling water for half an hour
2. In a pan, combine the ground sugar and lemon juice, stirring with a spatula until the sugar melts
3. Add the bottle of rum and the tea from the teapot
4. Heat everything in a bain-marie without allowing it to boil
5. When serving in the punch bowl, ignite the punch
