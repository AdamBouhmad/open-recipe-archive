## Ingredients

- six good veal chops
- melted butter
- grated bread
- salt
- pepper
- six eggs
- Parmesan cheese
- 250 grams fresh butter

## Directions

1. Prepare six good veal chops and soak them in melted butter so that they take the grated bread well mixed with salt and pepper.
2. Beat six eggs, and in the beaten egg dip the already breaded chops, and immediately bread them again with grated bread mixed with Parmesan cheese.
3. It is necessary to insist on this second breading so that the chops are covered with the greatest amount of grating.
4. Melt 250 grams of very fresh butter and fry the chops in it, which should take on a very good color.
