## Ingredients

- desalted, cooked, and cleaned salt cod
- media cuartillo of sauce made with broth
- tomato
- chopped scallions
- two good tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese
- melted butter

## Directions

1. After desalting, cooking, and cleaning as described previously, place the cod in sheets in a casserole between two fires, in a roasting pan, with half a quartillo of sauce made with broth, tomato, a little chopped scallions, and two good tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese
2. Place the cod in this sauce, stirring well so that it binds together
3. Then empty it into a serving dish that can go to the fire and place on top a layer of the same cheese, watering it well with melted butter
4. Finally, cook it in the oven at medium heat
