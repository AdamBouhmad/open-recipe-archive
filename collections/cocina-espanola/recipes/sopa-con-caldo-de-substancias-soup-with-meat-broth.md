## Ingredients

- veal hands
- pieces of beef
- game
- old fowl
- vegetables
- garlic
- clove

## Directions

1. Place veal hands, pieces of beef, game and old fowl in the broth from the pot; stir and add everything to the same broth from the pot as the gelatin forms; skim.
2. Incorporate vegetables, garlic and clove; allow to reduce on low heat for five hours, and finally strain to make bread soup or prepare rice, noodles, etc.
