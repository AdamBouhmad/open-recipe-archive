## Ingredients

- broth
- game and poultry substance
- white wine
- parsley
- small onion
- bay leaf
- garlic head
- cloves
- oil
- cilantro
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- testicles
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Boil and skim the foam in a casserole a certain quantity of broth, to which the substance of small game and poultry will be added, and if desired juice, defatting it and straining through a sieve.
2. Also prepare with equal parts of substance and broth, a glass of white wine, a bundle of parsley, a small onion, a bay leaf, a head of garlic, two cloves, two or three spoonfuls of oil, a bundle of cilantro, an onion sliced in rings, all of which should boil for two hours, then defatted and salt and pepper added.
3. With testicles, mushrooms and sufficient quantity of substance or defatted broth, the same sauce as before is achieved.
4. The meatless sauce is made by lining the bottom of a casserole with oil, and placing in it carrots, onions cut in rings, and slices of fish of all kinds; immediately moisten with meatless broth and bring to boil. Add garlic, mushrooms and white wine until everything reduces to a regular consistency.
