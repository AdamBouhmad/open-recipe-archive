## Ingredients

- onion
- parsley
- oil
- cornstarch
- 24 crayfish per liter of water

## Directions

1. Sauté a little onion and minced parsley, all in oil; add a small spoonful of cornstarch and lengthen while seasoning with twice the water that will make the broth.
2. When it boils vigorously, add twenty-four crayfish per liter of water, cook until the water is reduced by half, and with the broth soak toasted bread and serve.
