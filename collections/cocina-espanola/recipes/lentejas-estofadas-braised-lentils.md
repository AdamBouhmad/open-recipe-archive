## Ingredients

- dried lentils
- good piece of streaky bacon
- onions
- carrots
- salt and seasoning

## Directions

1. Season the lentils with a good piece of streaky bacon, onions and carrots, with good seasoning.
2. Cook slowly so the lentil becomes tender and delicate to the palate.
3. Avoid rapid cooking, which will make the dish hard and inedible.
