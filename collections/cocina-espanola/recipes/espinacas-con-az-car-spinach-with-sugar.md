## Ingredients

- Spinach paste made with beef butter
- Sugar lumps
- Milk, two tablespoons
- One egg yolk

## Directions

1. Incorporate sugar lumps to the paste when it has been made with beef butter, and stir much while the sweet incorporates into the dish.
2. The egg binding, instead of making it with water, is made with milk.
