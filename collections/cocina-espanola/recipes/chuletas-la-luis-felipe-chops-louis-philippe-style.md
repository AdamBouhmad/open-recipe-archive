## Ingredients

- veal chops
- kitchen twine

## Directions

1. Beat and cut the veal chops well and season them raw, tie three together, well stacked with kitchen twine, as if they were a single thick chop, and thus grill them on the grill, very well done.
2. Serve them plain, without the twine, of course, but in the form and position they had on the grill, and they are eaten as the curious reader will see.
