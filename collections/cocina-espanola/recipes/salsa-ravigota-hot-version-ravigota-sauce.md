## Ingredients

- fine herbs (parsley, chervil, chives, watercress, pimpernel, and celery), carefully chopped
- broth
- 30 grams of butter
- half a tablespoon of flour
- salt
- pepper
- a small stream of vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the same fine herbs as for cold ravigota in broth for a quarter of an hour, but only carefully chopped.
2. Incorporate 30 grams of butter, kneaded with half a tablespoon of flour.
3. Season well with salt, pepper, and a small stream of vinegar.
4. As soon as the butter has melted, remove the sauce from the fire to serve it.
