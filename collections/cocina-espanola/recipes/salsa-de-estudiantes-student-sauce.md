## Ingredients

- six shallots or garlic cloves
- parsley
- one cup of water
- one ladle of broth
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Without sautéing in any fat, cook six shallots or garlic cloves with a little parsley in one cup of water.
2. Add one ladle of broth and one tablespoon of vinegar with the corresponding seasoning of salt and pepper.
3. When this has cooked for twenty minutes, the sauce or broth can be served.
