## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters water
- 1 chicken

## Directions

1. In a liter and a half of water, cook a chicken after cleaning it well and scalding and singeing its feet.
2. Allow to cook on low heat until the liquid is reduced by half, which serves for the sick, as well as the soup made with it is for convalescents.
