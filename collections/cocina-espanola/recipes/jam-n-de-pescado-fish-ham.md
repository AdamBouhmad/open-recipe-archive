## Ingredients

- carp fillets
- tench fillets
- eel fillets
- salmon fillets
- carp roe
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- nutmeg
- beef butter
- fine herbs
- carp skin

## Directions

1. Make a hash with fillets of carp, tench, eel and salmon, mixed with carp roe, salt, pepper, clove, nutmeg, beef butter and fine herbs
2. Knead everything and shape it like a ham, covering the surface with carp skin
3. Wrap it in fine, new linen cloth and sew it very tightly
4. Cook this newly made ham for one hour in equal parts water and white wine, well seasoned and spiced
5. When the dish is cold, unsew the wrapping and serve it on a platter decorated with watercress and purslane
6. Cut it into slices like regular ham
