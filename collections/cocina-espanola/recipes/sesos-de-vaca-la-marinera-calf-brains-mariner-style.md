## Ingredients

- Calf brains
- Beef lard
- Twelve small onions
- Twelve mushrooms
- Salted water
- Beef lard
- One tablespoon of flour
- Two glasses of red wine

## Directions

1. With the brains already cooked, cut and cooled, cook separately in beef lard, sautéing well, twelve small onions until they take color
2. In a separate pan, in salted water mixed with thirty grams of beef lard, cook twelve mushrooms trimmed very neatly
3. Dust the onions with one tablespoon of flour, moistening them first in the water in which the mushrooms have cooked, and also with two glasses of red wine
4. Then combine the mushrooms with the onions and cook everything over low heat for half an hour in this marinera sauce, incorporating the prepared brains broken up beforehand
5. To serve this dish, do not pour all the contents of the pan onto the platter, but first place the brains very well arranged with the help of a skimmer, and pour the sauce over them
