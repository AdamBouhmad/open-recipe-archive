## Ingredients

- chicken
- lard
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- water
- potatoes
- or zucchini

## Directions

1. Heat lard in a pan or pot until very hot
2. Add a little garlic, half an onion, and parsley, taking care to fry all of this without burning it
3. Once well fried, add the chicken, turning it so it takes on color
4. Immediately after, add water until the chicken is covered
5. When the chicken is half-cooked, add potatoes cut into pieces
6. When they are well cooked, the tinola is finished
7. The dish should be brothy
8. Note: the chicken should be cut at the joints, as this prevents small bones from remaining in the broth
