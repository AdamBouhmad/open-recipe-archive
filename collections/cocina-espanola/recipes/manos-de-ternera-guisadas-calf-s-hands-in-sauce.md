## Ingredients

- Calf's hands, cooked
- Broth from cooking
- Flour
- Fine herbs
- Two egg yolks
- Tarragon vinegar
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the calf's hands as described previously.
2. Remove the hands from the broth and reduce the broth by adding a pinch of flour and fine herbs with appropriate seasoning.
3. Return the calf's hands to the sauce for a few minutes.
4. At the moment of serving, bind the sauce with two egg yolks dissolved in water and a small stream of tarragon vinegar.
