## Ingredients

- quail
- butter from cows
- salt
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. After cleaning, turn the quail on the roasting spit.
2. Have a pot on the fire with butter from cows, in which you will add salt, some bread crumbs, a pinch of pepper, and one egg yolk for every two quail.
3. With some feathers, brush the quail repeatedly with this mixture until they are well browned.
4. Alternatively, roast them slowly and wrapped in grape leaves, and serve with whatever sauce is most pleasing to the diner.
