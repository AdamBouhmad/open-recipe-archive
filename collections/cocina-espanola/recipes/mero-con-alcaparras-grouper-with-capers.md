## Ingredients

- cleaned grouper
- salt
- oil
- water
- capers
- almonds
- roasted garlic
- parsley

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned grouper in a casserole with salt and oil that is only warm
2. When putting it in, turn it once, then add water
3. When the fish is cooked, make a sauce with crushed capers, almonds, roasted garlic and parsley, pass it through a strainer
4. Shortly after, the grouper can be served with its sauce on top
