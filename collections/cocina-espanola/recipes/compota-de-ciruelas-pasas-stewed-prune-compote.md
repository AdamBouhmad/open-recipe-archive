## Ingredients

- 500 grams of prunes
- water to fill a one-liter pot
- 5 grams of cinnamon stick
- 60 grams of sugar

## Directions

1. Weigh 500 grams of prunes; wash them and place them in a pot with capacity of one liter, which is then filled with water
2. Cook over low heat and add 5 grams of cinnamon stick
3. When the prunes are cooked, that is, when they are soft and spongy but without opening, drain them in a sieve; strain the broth through a chinois strainer and remove the cinnamon; then add 60 grams of sugar and reduce the broth to 32 degrees
4. Place the prunes in the compote dish
5. Pour the reduced broth over them when it is completely cold
