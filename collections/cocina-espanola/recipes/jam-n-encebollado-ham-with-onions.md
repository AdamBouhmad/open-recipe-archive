## Ingredients

- fine, thin ham slices
- pork butter
- two bowls of cold water
- vinegar
- twelve medium-sized onions
- one tablespoon of flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry and brown fine, thin ham slices in pork butter
2. Successively soak them in two bowls of cold water acidulated with vinegar
3. In the frying fat, soften twelve medium onions cut into dice
4. When the onion is done, add one tablespoon of flour and make a light brown sauce
5. Moisten and bind with the vinegared water
6. Incorporate the ham slices and cook everything over low heat in a covered pot for half an hour
