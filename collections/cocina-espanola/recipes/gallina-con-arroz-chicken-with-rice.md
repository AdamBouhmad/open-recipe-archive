## Ingredients

- 125 grams of bacon
- pork lard
- chicken
- rice in quantity proportioned to the volume of the chicken
- clean broth
- salt
- two or three spoonfuls of roasting juice

## Directions

1. Brown 125 grams of bacon in pork lard in a pot
2. Remove the bacon from the pot and place the chicken, turning it so it browns well and tempering the fire so the fat does not become black
3. In a separate pot, bring rice to boil in clean broth in quantity proportioned to the volume of the chicken
4. When the rice is half cooked, remove the chicken from the pot for a moment and add the rice, stirring well so it mixes and soaks in the juices
5. Place the chicken back in the pot with the bacon well browned and cut into pieces
6. Moisten with broth and finish cooking on very low heat until the chicken and bacon are done
7. At the moment of serving, add two or three spoonfuls of roasting juice
