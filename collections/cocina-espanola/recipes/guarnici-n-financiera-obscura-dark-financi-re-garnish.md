## Ingredients

- one liter of broth
- a handful of rooster crests and kidneys and other rooster offal, cooked and prepared beforehand
- another handful of poultry quenelles
- an equal quantity of mushrooms, chopped and blanched in boiling water
- truffles, cut into pieces
- a couple of artichoke hearts, also cut into pieces
- veal sweetbreads
- two slices of tongue à l'écarlate, finely chopped
- twenty or thirty pitted olives
- one glass of white wine or sherry
- salt
- pepper
- three or four spoonfuls of meat juice
- starch or flour for binding

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, place one liter of broth with a handful of rooster crests and kidneys and other rooster offal, all cooked and prepared beforehand
2. Add another handful of poultry quenelles, an equal quantity of mushrooms that have been chopped and blanched in boiling water, truffles cut into pieces, a couple of artichoke hearts also cut into pieces, veal sweetbreads, and two slices of finely chopped tongue à l'écarlate
3. Add twenty or thirty pitted olives, one glass of white wine or sherry, salt and a touch of pepper
4. Cook everything for twenty minutes over very low heat
5. Add three or four spoonfuls of meat juice and bind with starch or flour
6. Pour this garnish into a serving dish and place the prepared dishes on top to serve à la financière
