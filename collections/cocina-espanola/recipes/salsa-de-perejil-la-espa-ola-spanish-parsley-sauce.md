## Ingredients

- a small bunch of very green parsley
- vinegar in small quantity
- one clove of garlic, crushed
- some cumin

## Directions

1. Take a small bunch of very green parsley and chop it well with a knife or crescent-shaped blade on a board, or pound it in a mortar until it becomes a paste
2. Dissolve this paste in a little water, and better yet in broth from the pot
3. Add vinegar in small quantity, one crushed clove of garlic and some cumin
