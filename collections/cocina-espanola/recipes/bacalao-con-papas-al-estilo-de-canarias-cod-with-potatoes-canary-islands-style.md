## Ingredients

- Cod
- Oil
- Peeled potatoes, cut
- Eight garlic cloves
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Saffron threads
- Bread crumb, soaked
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean and desalt the cod; fry it in oil until it browns on both sides, then remove it.
2. In the same oil, fry the peeled and cut potatoes and eight garlic cloves.
3. Once fried, remove them from the pan, then place the cod back with the potatoes and a pitcher of water, letting it cook.
4. When everything is cooked, crush the fried garlic, some parsley, pepper, a few saffron threads and a soaked bread crumb.
5. Dissolve all this in the cod's broth and add it to the stew.
6. Season, bring to a boil once more, and serve.
