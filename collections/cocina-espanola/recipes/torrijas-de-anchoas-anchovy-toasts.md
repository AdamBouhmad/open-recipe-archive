## Ingredients

- Long, thin slices of bread
- Oil
- Virgin oil
- Lemon juice
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Cebolleta (small onion)
- Shallots
- Anchovies
- White wine

## Directions

1. Fry the long, thin slices of bread in oil.
2. Separate them on a plate.
3. Pour over them a sauce made with virgin oil, lemon juice, pepper, parsley, cebolleta, and shallots, all finely chopped.
4. Place two or three small fillets of anchovies rinsed in white wine on each toast.
