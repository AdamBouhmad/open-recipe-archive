## Ingredients

- ham
- lean meat
- onion
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of ham and two parts more of lean meat
2. Cut it fine and mince a little raw onion, which is sprinkled on top
3. Sprinkle with raw oil, pepper, salt and a little vinegar
4. This will be a cold dish to serve preferably in the countryside
