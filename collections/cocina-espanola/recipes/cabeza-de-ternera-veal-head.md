## Ingredients

- veal head
- lemons
- white linen cloth
- water
- lemon pieces
- carrots
- parsnip
- onions
- cloves
- bundle of parsley and fine herbs
- salt and pepper in grains
- flour of fine wheat (60 grams, optional)
- cow butter (25-30 grams, optional)

## Directions

1. Scald the veal head and remove the major bones.
2. Rub or scrub the head all over with the two halves of a lemon to blanch it and make it appetizing.
3. Wrap the veal head very tightly in white linen cloth so it does not lose its shape.
4. Place it in boiling water seasoned with the lemon pieces that were used to rub it, plus two or three carrots, one parsnip, two onions with their corresponding cloves, a bundle of parsley and fine herbs, and good seasoning of salt and pepper in grains.
5. Cook for at least four hours over very low heat.
6. For kitchens that do not spare expenses, blanch 60 grams of fine wheat flour in the cooking water and add 25 to 30 grams of cow butter.
7. When cooking is complete, unsew the cloth wrapping and let the head drain.
8. Serve hot with a piquant sauce or cold ravigota.
9. In the same dish where the veal is placed, put four shells filled respectively with finely chopped fine herbs, finely trimmed gherkins, finely minced shallots and scallions, and chopped hard-boiled egg.
