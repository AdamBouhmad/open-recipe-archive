## Ingredients

- Potatoes, unpeeled
- Water
- Salt (implied)

## Directions

1. Place an iron pot on the heat with just enough water to moisten the bottom
2. Place two or three rounded stones the size of eggs in the pot
3. Fill the vessel with unpeeled potatoes so that water does not touch them
4. Fill the space above the potatoes with a strong cloth dampened in water and wrung out, as if it were a plug
5. Cover the pot with its lid and place a weight on top
6. Maintain a medium fire to keep water boiling; potatoes will cook in half an hour from the steam
7. Serve the potatoes in the basket or between the folds of a napkin in an elegant metal basket
