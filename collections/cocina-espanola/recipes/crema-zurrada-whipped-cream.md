## Ingredients

- One liter of cream
- 125 grams of impalpable powdered sugar
- Five grams of gum of tragacanth, also in powder
- Thirty grams of orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Put all these ingredients in an earthenware bowl
2. Beat everything strongly and for a long time with a small bundle of wicker rods until the mixture becomes entirely the same foam
3. Let it rest afterward for five or six minutes without touching it
4. Place in a pyramid on a special shallow dish, somewhat fluted and brilliant, so that the delicacy reflects on the edges like in a mirror
