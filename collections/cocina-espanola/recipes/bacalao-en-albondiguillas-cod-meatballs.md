## Ingredients

- Cooked cod, minced
- Bread crumb
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Beaten eggs
- Fish stock
- Oil
- Bread crust
- Roasted garlic
- Saffron

## Directions

1. Make a mixture of minced cooked cod, bread crumb, salt, pepper, parsley and garlic all ground together, and beaten eggs in proportion to achieve a regular consistency.
2. Once well mixed, form balls, roll in bread crumb and fry.
3. Cook the meatballs in fish stock and then stew them in fried oil with garlic and a bread crust.
4. Crush the bread crust with roasted garlic, parsley, pepper and a bit of saffron, and mix this with the meatballs.
