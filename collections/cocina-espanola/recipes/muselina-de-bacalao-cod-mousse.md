## Ingredients

- Cod soaked and cleaned of skin and bones
- Three tablespoons of flour
- One tablespoon of water
- Two tablespoons of oil
- One egg yolk
- Seasoning
- A few drops of cognac

## Directions

1. Soak and clean the cod, shred it
2. Soak the shredded cod for two hours before serving in a paste made from flour, water, oil, egg yolk, seasoning, and a few drops of cognac
3. With a fork, take the shredded cod pieces and sauté them in plenty of good oil over very high heat
