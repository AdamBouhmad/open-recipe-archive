## Ingredients

- 125 grams of pork butter
- 125 grams of ham loin
- 12 large onions the size of apples
- salt
- pepper
- a little flour
- 1 ladle of concentrated broth
- 2 egg yolks

## Directions

1. In the pork butter, sauté and cook well the ham loin cut into pieces, and the onions cut into slices and then crosswise into dice-like pieces
2. Season with salt, pepper, and a little flour
3. Moisten with the ladle of concentrated broth
4. When the ham and onions are well cooked and the sauce has reduced by half, remove from fire and pass through a strainer, pressing well
5. Before serving, bind the sauce with the two egg yolks diluted in cold water
6. The ham can be kept for other uses or cut into small pieces to enhance the appearance and taste of the sauce
