## Ingredients

- Beefsteak
- Anchovy butter
- Potatoes
- Butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the beefsteak as described in the English-style recipe
2. In the center of the platter of beefsteaks, place anchovy butter instead of cow butter prepared in the butler's style, using the anchovy butter formula already given in the sauces section
3. Serve as an essential garnish sautéed potatoes that are browned in cow butter
