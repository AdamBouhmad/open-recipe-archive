## Ingredients

- two ounces of chocolate
- two ounces of ground sugar
- one quarter litre of milk
- a little cream
- five egg yolks

## Directions

1. Pound two ounces of chocolate into powder and place in a saucepan with two ounces of ground sugar, one quarter litre of milk and a little cream.
2. Let it cook until reduced by a third, and when it is already cold or almost cold, incorporate, as in the previous cream, the beaten mixture of five egg yolks, and finish the operation in the same way.
