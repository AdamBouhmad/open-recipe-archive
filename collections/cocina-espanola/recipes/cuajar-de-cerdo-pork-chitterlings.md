## Ingredients

- pork chitterlings
- pork butter

## Directions

1. Wash and rewash the chitterlings
2. Let them sit overnight in brine and in the open air
3. Drain and cut into strips like macaroni
4. Sauté and fry in good pork butter until well browned and crispy
5. Serve with a side dish of chard sautéed in oil
