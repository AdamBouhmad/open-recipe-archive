## Ingredients

- one pound of veal
- half pound of lean ham
- two chicken legs
- half pound of butter
- onion
- carrot
- one or two cloves of spice
- bay leaf
- several cooked and well-crushed egg yolks
- three cups of milk

## Directions

1. Cut one pound of veal, half of lean ham and two chicken legs into small pieces
2. Put them in a saucepan with half a pound of butter over good heat, adding onion, carrot, one or two cloves of spice and bay leaf, which will be reheated until the onion is golden, then remove from heat
3. Add several cooked and well-crushed egg yolks, stirring everything well so they incorporate
4. Then add three cups of milk little by little, stirring constantly without ceasing so the yolks bind with it
5. Bring to heat, stirring everything until it comes to a boil, then move it to the side of the stove so its cooking is slow for about two hours
6. After this time, when its consistency is not too thick, degrease it and pass it through cheesecloth
