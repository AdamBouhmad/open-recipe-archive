## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- White sauce
- Parmesan or Manchego cheese, grated
- Breadcrumbs
- Beef lard, fried

## Directions

1. Prepare the cauliflower as in previous recipes
2. Cover it with white sauce in which grated Parmesan or Manchego cheese has been added
3. Cover with a layer of breadcrumbs and grated cheese
4. Brown the edges in fried beef lard
5. Add another layer of breadcrumbs and cheese
6. A quarter hour before serving, place in a serving dish in a convection oven to form the crust or gratin and take on color
