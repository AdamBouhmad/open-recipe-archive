## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Butter or lard
- Flour
- White wine
- Broth
- Salt and pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the young partridges without cutting them
2. Cook them over high heat in butter or lard, turning them frequently so they color evenly
3. Once cooked, remove them and set aside on a platter, keeping it well covered near the fire
4. In the same cooking fat, lightly toast a tablespoon of flour
5. Moisten it with a glass of white wine and a ladle of clean broth
6. Season with salt and pepper and reduce by half
7. Add the juice of one whole lemon
8. Pour this sauce over the young partridges at the moment of serving at the table
