## Ingredients

- turnips of pear shape
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- white wine
- cinnamon stick

## Directions

1. Scrape and wash the turnips of pear shape
2. Cut them in half, trimming the tip and head
3. Blanch them like carrots and give them a boil in salted water
4. Drain and place them in a single layer in a baking dish without overlapping
5. Cover them with a little butter
6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and extend with broth, allowing it to reduce over high heat
7. Remove the turnips and place them in the same arrangement on a serving plate
8. Pour over the remaining cooking juices, reinforced with a glass of white wine and a cinnamon stick
