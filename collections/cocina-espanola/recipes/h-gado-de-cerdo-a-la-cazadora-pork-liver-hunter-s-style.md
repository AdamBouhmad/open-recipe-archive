## Ingredients

- refined oil
- one pork liver
- bacon, minced
- parsley, minced
- shallots, minced
- salt
- pepper
- bacon, in thin leaves
- drippings juice from roasting
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place in a serving dish with refined oil a pork liver, which is split lengthwise down the middle without the two pieces separating, so that it looks like an open lamb kidney.
2. Then make incisions lengthwise and crosswise, as if it were gridded like a bar of chocolate, and the grooves or marks of the cuts are filled with a hash of bacon, parsley and shallots minced.
3. After two hours, moisten the entire surface of the liver with the oil that is in the dish, and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Then close the liver, which was open like a book, lard it with thin leaves of bacon and roast it in the oven or on the plate, in a roasting pan or baking sheet.
5. When the liver is well cooked, place it on the serving platter, skim the fat from the roasting juice and strain it to pour over the dish, strengthened with lemon juice.
