## Ingredients

- fine, thin ham slices
- oil
- pepper
- parchment paper

## Directions

1. Place fine, thin ham slices on a sheet of parchment paper, oiled and sprinkled with a little pepper
2. Wrap in the paper making a large papillote
3. Roast in the oven at medium heat or on a grill, turning a couple of times
4. Serve in the same paper on a platter
