## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- one pound of sugar
- flour
- wafers

## Directions

1. Beat twelve eggs with one pound of sugar
2. When the batter is white and thick, add flour that is well incorporated
3. Place wafers on the bottoms of the cake pans
4. Pour batter into the pans to a thickness of about one inch
5. Cover the pans and apply gentle heat above and below
6. When well set, remove the pans and take out one cake from each
7. Cut these into very thin slices
8. Place them in the oven on pastries to biscotti until they are lightly toasted
