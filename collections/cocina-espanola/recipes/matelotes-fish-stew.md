## Ingredients

- pieces of lamprey
- pieces of eel
- pieces of tench
- pieces of carp
- butter
- onion, finely chopped
- small onions the size of walnuts
- flour
- red wine
- salt and seasoning

## Directions

1. Sauté the pieces of lamprey, eel, tench, and carp in butter with the finely chopped onion and small onions.
2. Season well and sprinkle with flour.
3. Add red wine and let it cook for half an hour over high heat.
