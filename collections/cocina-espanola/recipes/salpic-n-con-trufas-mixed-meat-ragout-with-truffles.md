## Ingredients

- 125 grams of beef butter
- same weight of lean ham, cut into small squares
- half of a large onion, finely chopped
- a handful of mushrooms
- four or five truffles in very thin slices
- veal or lamb sweetbreads
- lamb or veal testicles
- salt
- spices
- concentrated broth
- a couple of tablespoons of meat juice or roasting drippings
- three egg yolks dissolved in water
- a teaspoon of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Sauté in 125 grams of beef butter the same weight of lean ham cut into very small squares, and half of a large onion also finely chopped.
2. When the onion has taken a good golden color, add a handful of mushrooms cut up and four or five truffles in very thin slices, veal or lamb sweetbreads, lamb or veal testicles, and good seasoning of salt and spices.
3. Moisten with concentrated broth, to which a couple of tablespoons of meat juice or roasting drippings are added.
4. Cook everything over a slow fire for the space of half an hour.
5. At the moment of serving, away from the fire, add three egg yolks dissolved in water and a teaspoon of lemon juice.
