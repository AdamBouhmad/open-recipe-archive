## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of chickpeas
- cold water
- salt
- hot meat broth
- onion
- bacon
- carrot
- herbs

## Directions

1. Place the chickpeas the day before in cold water with a little salt to soak
2. Cook in a saucepan with hot meat broth, with one onion, bacon, carrot, and a small bundle of herbs
3. Ensure the boiling is slow
4. When cooked, remove the onion, carrot, and bacon
5. Pass through a fine sieve
6. Well degrease the broth and season
7. Heat and serve
