## Ingredients

- 1 raw egg
- small pieces of truffles
- salt
- a small piece of beef butter
- eggshell
- lime and egg white paste

## Directions

1. Drill a hole in the raw egg with a diameter of a coin.
2. Introduce small pieces of truffles and a little salt with a small piece of beef butter.
3. Stir everything inside with a stick, breaking the yolk.
4. Seal the hole with a patch made from another eggshell and a mixture of lime and egg white.
5. Let it dry.
6. Boil the egg in water and serve in an egg cup with the patched end facing down.
