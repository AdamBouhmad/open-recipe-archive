## Ingredients

- one beef chop, prepared as in previous recipes and properly seasoned
- 125 grams of beef fat

## Directions

1. Sauté the prepared and seasoned chop in 125 grams of beef fat
2. Turn occasionally so it browns on both sides
3. Then place the pan over low heat or on a stovetop in a location where it does not receive direct heat action
4. Cover the pan
