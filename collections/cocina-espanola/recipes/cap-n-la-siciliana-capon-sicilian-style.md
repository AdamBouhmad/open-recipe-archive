## Ingredients

- One well-fattened capon
- Melted beef butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Truffles
- Champagne wine
- Beef butter for basting
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare a good fattened capon and rinse its interior with melted beef butter, seasoning well with salt and pepper
2. Fill a pot halfway with truffles and Champagne wine and bring to a boil for about ten minutes over high heat
3. Remove the pot from the fire and place the capon inside to marinate in the aroma for three hours
4. Remove the capon and briefly brown it in beef butter, then let it cool
5. Insert a piece of beef butter inside the capon and squeeze the juice of a lemon over it
6. Braise over low heat for one hour with the butter from the browning
7. Ten minutes before serving, pour the Champagne and truffles over the capon
