## Ingredients

- 1 piece of cod
- onion
- carrot
- 3 or 4 small onions
- butter
- lemon juice
- nutmeg
- pepper

## Directions

1. Soak a piece of cod in plenty of cold water for about thirty hours before using it, changing the water two or three times
2. After this time, clean it well of its scales, wash and drain it, placing it to cook in three times its volume of water with onion and carrot
3. The moment it begins to cook, remove the pot or saucepan, covering it with a lid
4. Shortly after, drain it using a cloth or canvas
5. Remove bones and skin, doing this as little as possible, removing it in flakes which will be placed in a pot
6. Place this over hot ashes or mild coals, covered with its lid and little fire on top, with the only object being to dry it of the humidity it may have
7. Meanwhile, cut 3 or 4 small onions into small cubes, which will be fried in butter until they brown
8. Add the cod inside, sauté everything together a little and season with lemon juice, nutmeg and pepper
