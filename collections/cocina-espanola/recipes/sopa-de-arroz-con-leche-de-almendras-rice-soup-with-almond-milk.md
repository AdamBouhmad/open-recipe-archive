## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt
- lemon zest
- bay leaves, two
- almonds, 250 grams, shelled and crushed
- water for crushing, one spoonful
- water, one quartillo, slightly warm
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the rice slowly in water with little salt, the lemon zest, and two bay leaves
2. Prepare almond milk by crushing 250 grams of shelled almonds in a marble mortar, adding one spoonful of water during the crushing to prevent the oil from separating
3. Place the paste in a white napkin to drain well
4. Dissolve the paste in one quartillo of slightly warm water, passing it through the napkin by compression five or six times, continuously wetting the napkin with the milk
5. Sugar the rice and give it the right cooking point by adding the almond milk very slowly while it cooks gently
6. Remove the lemon zest, bay leaves, and cinnamon if used
7. Serve the rice flavored with orange flower water
