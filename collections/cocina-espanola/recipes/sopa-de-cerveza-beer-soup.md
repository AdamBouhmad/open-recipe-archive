## Ingredients

- one liter of very strong beer
- 30 grams of sugar
- five grams of cilantro
- rye bread or very dark bread
- one egg yolk
- water

## Directions

1. Heat one liter of very strong beer with 30 grams of sugar and five grams of cilantro.
2. Strain this liquid over rye or very dark bread cut into thin slices.
3. At the moment of serving, bind with one egg yolk dissolved in water.
