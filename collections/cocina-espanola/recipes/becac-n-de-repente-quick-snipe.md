## Ingredients

- half a dozen snipe
- fresh butter
- six chopped shallots
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- powdered nutmeg
- one glass of good sherry
- juice of two lemons
- one tablespoon of toasted and grated bread

## Directions

1. In ten minutes sauté half a dozen snipe over high heat in a saucepan with fresh butter, six chopped shallots, a bay leaf, salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg
2. Remove the birds from the saucepan
3. Deglaze the juices with a glass of good sherry
4. Add the juice of two lemons and one tablespoon of toasted and grated bread
5. Stir vigorously with a wooden spatula
6. Return the snipe to the saucepan to give them a couple of boils
7. Serve in a dish very hot with the sauce over the top
