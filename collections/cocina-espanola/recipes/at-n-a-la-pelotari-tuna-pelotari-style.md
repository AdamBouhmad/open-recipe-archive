## Ingredients

- Fresh tuna slices, two fingers thick
- Refined oil
- Two cloves of garlic, cut into slivers
- Some onions, sliced into rings
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Clove spice
- Salt and pepper
- A good handful of parsley in sprigs
- Beef lard
- White wine from Valdepeñas
- Shallots
- Mushrooms
- Chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare slices of fresh tuna two fingers thick and soak them for four hours in refined oil with two cloves of garlic cut into slivers, some onions sliced into rings, thyme, bay leaf, clove spice, and a good seasoning of salt and pepper, with a good handful of parsley in sprigs.
2. Turn the tuna continuously in the marinade so that it soaks evenly and remove the slices, drain them, and dry both sides of each slice well with a fine white linen cloth.
3. Grease them with beef lard and roast them over a gentle fire on the grill.
4. Separately, prepare a brown-white sauce, well seasoned with salt and pepper, which is thinned with white wine from Valdepeñas, the stronger the better.
5. Chop some shallots and mushrooms and mix them with chopped parsley; incorporate this into the pan and cook until the sauce reduces by half, to moisten with it at the moment of serving the roasted tuna slices, placed on a platter as they come from the grill.
6. Serve this dish very hot.
