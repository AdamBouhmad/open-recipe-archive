## Ingredients

- tender and white part of endive
- tomato
- oil
- cumin
- salt
- vinegar
- water or garlic to taste

## Directions

1. Chop the tender and white part of the endive
2. Mash tomato in a plate with oil, cumin, salt, vinegar and water or garlic if desired
3. Mix everything with the endive
4. Alternatively, dress the endive with only oil, vinegar and a hint of garlic
