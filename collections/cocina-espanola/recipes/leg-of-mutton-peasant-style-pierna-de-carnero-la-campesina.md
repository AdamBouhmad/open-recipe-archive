## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- beef butter
- salt
- pepper
- two or three onions
- two or three carrots
- one cup of degreased broth
- flour or potato starch

## Directions

1. Debone the leg, tie it and secure it well with twine
2. Sauté it in beef butter until it is well browned on all sides
3. Add salt, pepper, two or three onions and as many carrots and one cup of degreased broth
4. Cook slowly over low heat for five hours in a cooking pot
5. When the leg is almost cooked, strain or pass the sauce and thicken it with half a spoon of flour or potato starch, but only after it has been well degreased
6. Finish cooking without changing the intensity of the heat
7. Serve
