## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- cold water
- bacon strips
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- dark espeso sauce
- blond sauce
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the tongue for twenty-four hours in cold water, changing it several times
2. Blanch in boiling water and scrape to remove the skin
3. Lard with strips of bacon seasoned with pepper, salt and minced parsley
4. Cook slowly for four to five hours
5. Remove from heat and open the tongue lengthwise without separating it
6. Place on serving platter
7. Remove fat from the cooking liquid
8. Coat with a thick dark sauce
9. Can also be served with a blond sauce or tomato sauce instead
