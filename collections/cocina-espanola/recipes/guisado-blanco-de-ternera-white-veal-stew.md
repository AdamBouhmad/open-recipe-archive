## Ingredients

- veal scraps
- two tablespoons flour
- butter or lard
- one ladle of clear broth
- one ladle of cream or two of milk
- mushrooms (champignons)
- small onions
- salt and pepper
- three egg yolks
- water

## Directions

1. In a saucepan, brown two tablespoons of flour in butter or lard, and moisten with one ladle of clear broth and another of cream, or in its absence, two of milk.
2. In this sauce, seasoned slightly, place the pieces of veal to warm, but without allowing them to cook.
3. Separately, cook mushrooms and very small onions in clear broth; season with salt and pepper and incorporate into the meat.
4. When serving this white stew, bind it with three egg yolks dissolved in water, so that the stew becomes creamy and slightly yellowish.
5. Make sure the sauce is not too thin, resulting in a dish fit for a good table, and more than leftovers, a variant of any of the white veal stews.
