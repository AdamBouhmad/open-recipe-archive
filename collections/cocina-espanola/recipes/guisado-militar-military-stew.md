## Ingredients

- meat cut into small pieces
- oil or butter
- chopped onion
- a little sweet pepper
- garlic
- pepper
- tomatoes, peeled and chopped
- salt
- hot water or pot broth
- bay leaf
- potatoes cut into thick pieces

## Directions

1. Chop the meat into small pieces and put to reheat in a pot, pan or cooking vessel with oil or butter, chopped onion, a little sweet pepper, garlic, pepper, tomatoes that have been peeled and chopped, and corresponding salt
2. Cook at low heat with its own broth, stirring from time to time
3. Add hot water or pot broth, adding at the same time a little bay leaf and some potatoes cut into thick pieces
4. Let it cook for the necessary time
