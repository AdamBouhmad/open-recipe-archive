## Ingredients

- 60 grams anchovy fillets, well washed and cleaned
- 60 grams beef lard

## Directions

1. Pound the anchovy fillets thoroughly and well in a mortar, without adding any liquid or seasoning
2. Continue pounding while incorporating the beef lard into the mortar
3. Continue pounding until the butter is well worked and uniform
4. Present at the table as a spread, or reserve for seasoning and garnishing other dishes
