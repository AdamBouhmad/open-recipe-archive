## Ingredients

- roasted fat or pork lard
- parsley
- mushrooms
- breadcrumbs
- good broth
- boiled beef
- pork lard
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Place at the bottom of a metal or fire-resistant plate a little roasted fat or pork lard
2. Make a bed of very finely chopped parsley and mushrooms
3. Cover this bed with breadcrumbs
4. Place the slices of boiled meat on top, arranging them side by side, continuing until all meat is used
5. Moisten with good broth and dampen from time to time
6. Simmer gently for three quarters of an hour
7. Serve with potatoes browned in pork lard, arranged around this stew
