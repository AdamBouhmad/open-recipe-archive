## Ingredients

- Two veal feet
- Six pounds of beef or cow meat
- One and a half pounds of veal top
- Five pounds of mutton
- Water
- Salt

## Directions

1. Place all ingredients in an earthenware pot and cook with low heat in sufficient water.
2. Skim carefully and strain the broth, pressing it well.
3. Boil a second time with fresh water, strain again, and let both broths cool to remove the fat.
4. Combine the two broths.
5. Strain and evaporate in a double boiler until it has the consistency of paste.
6. Remove from the vessel and spread on marble stone.
7. Cut into small tablets and finish drying in a double boiler or oven until brittle.
8. Store in a glass bottle sealed with cork, sealed with wax, and kept in a dry place.
9. To use: dissolve half an ounce or 15 grams of these tablets in water, add a little salt, heat over gentle heat, and serve or use in making soups.
