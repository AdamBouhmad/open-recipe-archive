## Ingredients

- leftover meat from white meats such as veal and poultry
- béchamel sauce made with broth, water, or milk
- toasted breadcrumbs
- beaten egg
- pork fat for frying

## Directions

1. Mince the meat very finely but without making it pasty.
2. Mix with béchamel sauce made with broth, water, or milk so that the mixture is thick and well bound.
3. Empty the contents of the pan onto a cutting board and let cool in a cool place for two or three hours, cooling and drying.
4. Form the mixture into a square cake about an inch and a half high on the cutting board.
5. With a large knife, score it equally into a grid pattern.
6. Each small square is the measure of one croquette.
7. In a bowl almost full of toasted breadcrumb grating, take a portion of cake with a spoon and toss it until you can pick it up with your hand and mold it into a cylinder.
8. Soak the croquette in beaten egg and in another bowl coat it with new breadcrumb grating.
9. When all croquettes are made and lined up on the cutting board, fry them in hot pork fat at the moment of serving.
