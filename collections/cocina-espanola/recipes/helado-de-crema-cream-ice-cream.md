## Ingredients

- Egg yolks, ten
- Milk, two cuartillos
- Refined sugar, half a pound
- Chocolate, half a pound (optional)

## Directions

1. Mix the egg yolks, milk, and sugar perfectly and put over low heat, stirring without stopping until it reaches a regular thickness
2. Remove at once, and after a short time freeze it
3. If adding chocolate, cook it with common water first and then mix it in
