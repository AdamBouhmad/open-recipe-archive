## Ingredients

- two chicken or poultry breasts
- white poultry broth
- fried bread
- flour
- beef butter
- mushrooms
- nutmeg
- lemon juice
- beef butter the size of a walnut

## Directions

1. Cook two chicken or poultry breasts in white poultry broth, and when cold remove them and pound in the mortar
2. Prepare a roux as before and add the broth in which the breasts have cooked, increasing it with some mushrooms and nutmeg
3. Incorporate the pounded meats, and after degreasing this sauce, strain it through cheesecloth and put it in a bain-marie
4. When serving, add the lemon juice and about the size of a walnut of beef butter
