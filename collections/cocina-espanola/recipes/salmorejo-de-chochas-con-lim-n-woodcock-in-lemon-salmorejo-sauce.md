## Ingredients

- Three roasted woodcocks
- Four lemons
- One additional lemon, sliced thin
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Nutmeg, grated
- Two tablespoons of mustard
- One glass of wine
- Woodcock livers and giblets

## Directions

1. Carve three roasted woodcocks on a platter according to proper technique.
2. Pound the livers and remaining giblets.
3. Squeeze the juice from four lemons and add one lemon sliced into thin rounds.
4. Add salt, pepper, spices, and grated nutmeg.
5. Add two tablespoons of mustard and one glass of wine.
6. Combine with the pounded giblets and serve with the carved woodcock.
