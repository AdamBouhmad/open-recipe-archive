## Ingredients

- veal liver
- 125 grams of beef butter
- bacon strips
- salt and pepper
- one onion
- two carrots
- one quarter bottle of white wine

## Directions

1. In a pot with 125 grams of beef butter, sauté the veal liver which has been larded with thick strips of bacon.
2. Turn to brown on all sides and season moderately with salt and pepper.
3. Add one onion, two carrots, and one quarter bottle of white wine.
4. Cook for about three hours with the pot well covered, turning the meat frequently to prevent sticking.
5. Serve hot.
