## Ingredients

- Quail
- Pork lard
- Bay leaf
- Red bell peppers
- Broth
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Clean the quail and tie their legs and wings.
2. Season and grease them with pork lard inside, adding a bay leaf.
3. Sauté and brown them in pork lard.
4. Place each quail inside a fat, clean red bell pepper.
5. Give the prepared peppers a turn in butter and, continuously basting them with a little broth and Sherry wine, roast them in the oven on high heat for about a quarter hour.
