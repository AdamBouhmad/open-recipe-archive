## Ingredients

- One liter of shelled peas
- 30 grams of beef butter
- Two or three onions
- A pair of lettuce hearts
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 125 grams of beef butter
- One tablespoon of flour
- Two egg yolks
- Half a glass of water

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan one liter of shelled peas with 30 grams of beef butter, two or three onions, a pair of lettuce hearts, and good seasoning of salt and pepper.
2. Cook this over gentle heat and sauté in the saucepan frequently, or better yet, stir with one of the boxwood spatulas that should be available in a kitchen, so that the peas do not stick, before they begin to release the vegetable water they contain.
3. When cooking is finished, remove from the saucepan the parsley bundle and the lettuce, leaving only the onions.
4. Add 125 grams of beef butter kneaded with one tablespoon of flour; stir vigorously everything for two minutes.
5. Remove the saucepan from heat and pour over the peas, at the moment of serving, two egg yolks dissolved in half a glass of water.
