## Ingredients

- leftover turkey
- pork fat
- onion
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- clean broth
- turnips
- dried parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the leftover turkey into pieces the size of walnuts and sauté and brown them in pork fat with a chopped onion.
2. When the onion has taken color, dust with flour, season with salt, pepper, and a bay leaf, and moisten with clean broth.
3. Cook slowly over low heat with turnips cut into thin slices.
4. At the moment of serving, dust the stew with dried parsley powder.
