## Ingredients

- clams, well cleaned
- butter
- parsley, chopped
- pepper
- salt
- broth, water, or white wine
- egg yolks, beaten
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place well-cleaned clams in a casserole with butter
2. As they open from the heat, remove their top shell
3. When all are open, check them and remove any parasites
4. Pass the water that has come out of them through a sieve
5. Mix a piece of butter with chopped parsley, pepper and salt, moistening it with broth, water or white wine
6. Let boil for a few minutes
7. At the time of serving, add a beaten egg mixture and lemon juice
