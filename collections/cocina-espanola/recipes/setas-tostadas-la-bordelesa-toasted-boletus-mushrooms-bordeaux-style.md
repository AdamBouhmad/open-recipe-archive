## Ingredients

- large open mushrooms
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- garlic, finely chopped
- fine herbs, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Choose large, open mushrooms.
2. Clean and remove the stem.
3. Place on the grill with the concave part up and fill the hollows with butter, salt, pepper, finely chopped garlic, and fine herbs.
4. Allow to roast well and toast.
