## Ingredients

- veal liver
- four eggs
- two spoonfuls of olive oil
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- olive oil for frying
- thick tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the veal liver into thin slices in the shape of ladyfinger biscuits
2. Make a batter with four eggs, two spoonfuls of olive oil, salt and pepper
3. Coat the liver slices one by one in the batter
4. Dust with flour
5. Fry in olive oil
6. Arrange on a serving platter in a crown shape
7. Fill the hollow center with a thick tomato sauce
