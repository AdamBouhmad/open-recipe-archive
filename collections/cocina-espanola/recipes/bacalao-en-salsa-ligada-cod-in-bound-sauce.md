## Ingredients

- Cod
- Milk
- Water
- Bound sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak in equal parts water and milk
2. Scald to remove skin more easily
3. After well prepared, cook in cold water
4. Turn when it begins to boil and remove quickly
5. Drain well and place on serving platter
6. Cover with a bound sauce with a few drops of lemon
7. Serve immediately
