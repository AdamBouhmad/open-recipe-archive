## Ingredients

- Calf's hands, deboned carefully
- Quenelles filling, well seasoned
- Spanish sauce
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Debone the calf's hands carefully and with precision, taking care to split them only along the length of the inner side without damaging them.
2. Cook the hands naturally until done, then drain and let them cool.
3. Fill the hollow of each hand generously with a well-seasoned quenelles filling.
4. Tie with kitchen twine so that the filling does not escape.
5. Recook for half an hour in Spanish sauce with a glass of Sherry wine.
6. When serving, remove the twine as the hands are placed on the platter.
