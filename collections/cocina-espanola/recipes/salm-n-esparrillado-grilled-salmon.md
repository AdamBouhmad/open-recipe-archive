## Ingredients

- Salmon, thin slices
- Oil
- Shallots, minced
- Parsley, minced
- Salt and pepper
- Vinegar
- Cow butter

## Directions

1. Very simple to make.
2. Thin slices, well coated with oil, are grilled on grills and are served to eat with a sauce made on the spot, with very fried oil that is poured into a metal sauce boat, and into which a mincing of shallots and parsley is incorporated, everything very finely cut, salt and pepper.
3. If this little sauce is not liked, it is substituted with oil and vinegar, or cow butter, in which the piece of food is dipped in the same plate before bringing it to the mouth.
4. Salmon should always be eaten with potatoes cooked in steam as if they were bread.
