## Ingredients

- cat's tongue biscuits (lenguas de gato)
- apple marmalade
- guava jelly or well-set coffee cream

## Directions

1. Line the inside of a mold evenly with cat's tongue biscuits
2. The biscuits must fit well together so that there is no space between them
3. Fill the mold with apple marmalade and even out the surface
4. Close the surface with the same biscuits in a double row
5. Turn out onto a platter
6. Fill the hollow that results in the dish with guava jelly or well-set coffee cream
