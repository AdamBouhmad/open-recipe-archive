## Ingredients

- Thrush
- Pitted olive
- Salt

## Directions

1. Clean and empty the thrush
2. Place a pitted olive and a pinch of salt inside
3. Skewer with other thrushes on an iron rod
4. Roast, being careful not to expose them to direct flame
