## Ingredients

- capon
- vegetables for broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook a well-prepared, clean capon in a pot with the same vegetables as for a good stew, but not as much water
2. When cooked, remove from the pot and place on a serving platter near the heat
3. Decrease the small amount of broth that should remain
4. Reduce it considerably
5. Serve the capon in this broth, sprinkling it with coarse salt or what is called cooking salt
