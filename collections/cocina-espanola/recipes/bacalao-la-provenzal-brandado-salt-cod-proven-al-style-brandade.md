## Ingredients

- about two pounds of well-desalted salt cod from the thickest part
- cold water
- fine oil
- onion
- bay leaf
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take about two pounds of well-desalted salt cod, but making sure it is from the thickest part; cut it into square pieces and put it in a sufficient quantity of cold water so that it cooks on low heat, and when it is about to come to a boil, set it aside and cover it with its lid, leaving it in this condition for about ten minutes
2. After which drain it through a sieve, and then through cloth or canvas so that no moisture remains; carefully remove the bones, leaving the skins
3. Fry in a casserole in very fine oil a chopped onion with a bay leaf, then putting the cod inside and stirring it strongly with the spatula until it becomes a paste
4. During this work put in some tablespoons of very fine oil, letting it fall very slowly, and at the same time some drops of lemon juice
5. By force of work and with the help of the oil and lemon, the cod acquires a whiteness and surprising elasticity, thus transforming itself into a healthy and appetizing food
6. One must be careful not to drop too much oil at once, because it would be very easy for it to break apart, as happens with mayonnaise and garlic-oil or alioli; but this is easily avoided by adding it very little by little
