## Ingredients

- desalted and cooked salt cod without skin or bones
- milk
- ham cut very fine
- onion
- carrot
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley
- lard or butter
- three ounces of flour
- nutmeg
- Parmesan cheese, grated
- butter

## Directions

1. Desalted and cooked, clean, and without skin or bones, separate into sheets and incorporate into a sauce prepared as follows: cook milk, stirring it without ceasing with a wooden spatula, until by evaporation it reduces to a little, less than half
2. Meanwhile, sauté in a casserole ham cut very fine, onion, carrot, a bundle of bay leaf, thyme, and parsley, with pork or cow butter; put in about three ounces of flour, and well mixed, season with a little nutmeg
3. Let everything boil for about half an hour, without ceasing to stir, then pass it through a sieve
4. In this sauce, thus prepared, put the cod and give it a few boils
5. Then place it in a serving dish that resists fire; sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and sprinkle with cow butter; cover with the hood oven or put the dish in the kitchen oven, well heated, until the dish takes color, and serve
