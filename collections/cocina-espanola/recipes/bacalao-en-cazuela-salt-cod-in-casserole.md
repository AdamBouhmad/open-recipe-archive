## Ingredients

- salt cod cut into pieces
- breadcrumbs
- garlic
- parsley
- raw oil
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the cod into pieces and put it in a casserole or cooking pot, so that it covers the bottom well
2. On top of it put a thick layer of breadcrumbs, garlic and parsley in abundance, then another layer of cod, and so successively until the casserole is full
3. Pour raw oil, garlic, pepper and water on top, which covers everything
4. Cover the casserole and put it on low heat, until it is almost dry, and thus it is served
