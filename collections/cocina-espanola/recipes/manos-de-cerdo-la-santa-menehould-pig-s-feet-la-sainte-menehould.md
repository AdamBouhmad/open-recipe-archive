## Ingredients

- pig's hands, singed, scalded and thoroughly washed
- bacon slices
- pieces of lean veal
- carrots
- onions
- clove buds
- bouquet of fine herbs
- one and a half quartillo of good red wine
- one and a half quartillo of clean broth
- melted beef lard
- breadcrumbs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Split the pig's hands lengthwise along the inner curved side
2. Tie them around with kitchen twine, wrapping many times so they do not lose their shape during cooking
3. In a braising pan, place bacon slices, pieces of lean veal, carrots, onions with clove buds stuck in them, a bouquet of fine herbs, one and a half quartillo of good red wine and one and a half quartillo of clean broth
4. The amount of liquid should be proportioned to the quantity of hands being prepared, as cooking must last twenty-four hours and proceed very slowly
5. Allow the hands to cool completely after cooking
6. Cut the strings that hold them
7. Soak them well in melted beef lard and bread them with breadcrumbs seasoned with salt and pepper
8. Grill them over high heat to serve very hot and dry, omitting the sauce in which they cooked
