## Ingredients

- ham scraps, finely chopped
- fine herbs, finely chopped in equal amount to ham scraps
- ham fat fried with any lean leftover ham in equal amount to the chopped mixture
- thin slices and rounds of bread crumb in sufficient quantity

## Directions

1. Finely chop ham scraps, adding an equal amount of finely chopped fine herbs, and mix everything together
2. Add an equal amount of fried ham fat mixed with any leftover lean ham from a previous meal
3. Cut thin slices and rounds of bread crumb in sufficient quantity
4. In a round casserole, layer the bread slices one on top of another, forming layers, and between them place a bed of bread covered with the chopped mixture, and another layer of ham slices, alternating until the casserole is full, forming a mass in the shape of a loaf, ending with a crust
5. Turn it over onto its reverse side and place it in a camp oven, where it will be kept until it toasts heavily
