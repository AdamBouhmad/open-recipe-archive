## Ingredients

- bread
- water
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- cumin
- garlic
- onions (optional)
- cucumbers (optional)

## Directions

1. Crumble the bread
2. Add sufficient water, oil, vinegar, pepper, salt, crushed cumin and garlic
3. If desired, add onions and cucumbers sliced into rounds
4. Mix together
