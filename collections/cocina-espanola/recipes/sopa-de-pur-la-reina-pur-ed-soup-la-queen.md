## Ingredients

- 4 chickens
- 18 blanched almonds
- 6 cooked egg yolks
- 1/2 pound of rice, washed and cooked in broth
- 2 quarts of white broth
- bread croutons
- small meatballs or forcemeat balls

## Directions

1. Roast four chickens on a spit
2. Once cold, remove the meat and pound in a mortar
3. Add 18 blanched almonds and six cooked egg yolks
4. When all is well reduced to paste, add 1/2 pound of rice well washed and cooked in broth
5. Put all this in a saucepan, adding about two quarts of white broth
6. Heat without boiling and pass through a sieve then through a clean piece of fine cloth
7. Return to a clean saucepan
8. At the time of serving, heat in a bain-marie
9. Serve with croutons, small meatballs, or small forcemeat balls
