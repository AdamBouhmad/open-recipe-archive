## Ingredients

- young birds such as onceyos, swifts, thrushes, turtle doves and other tender small birds
- thin roasting spit or iron or cane skewers accommodating six birds each
- slices of fat bacon
- grated bread
- salt
- lemon wheels
- sugar
- lemon juice
- cinnamon
- pepper
- broth

## Directions

1. Thread the birds on a thin roasting spit, pierced through, or place them on iron or cane skewers, six birds to each skewer.
2. Tie the skewers to the spit.
3. Place a slice of fat bacon between each bird.
4. Put to roast.
5. When roasted, sprinkle grated bread and salt over them so they are well covered.
6. Serve with lemon wheels.
7. Prepare a sweet-and-sour sauce with a little sugar, lemon juice, a little cinnamon, a pinch of pepper, salt, and a little broth.
8. This sauce is very good for all roasted meats, for roasted or fried chicken livers, for veal and bird cutlets, and particularly for the sick, as most of them enjoy this sauce because everything sweet-and-sour is of good taste.
