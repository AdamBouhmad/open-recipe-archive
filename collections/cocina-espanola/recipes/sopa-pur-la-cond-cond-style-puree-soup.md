## Ingredients

- two pounds of red beans
- cold water
- pieces of bacon
- one onion
- carrots
- a bunch of bay leaf, thyme, parsley and two cloves

## Directions

1. Clean and wash two pounds of red beans, putting them to cook in cold water with some pieces of bacon, an onion, carrots and a bunch of bay leaf, thyme, parsley and two cloves
2. Once cooked, remove the bacon, legumes and water
3. Pass with broth through a linen strainer and season
