## Ingredients

- 1 cooked lobster
- Hard-boiled egg yolks
- Capers
- Small pickled cucumbers
- Anchovy fillets
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Pepper powder
- English or French mustard, optional

## Directions

1. Cook the lobster
2. Break it apart and extract the meat
3. Make a salad composed of hard-boiled egg yolks, capers, small pickled cucumbers cut into thin rounds and anchovy fillets
4. Season everything with oil, vinegar and pepper powder, and if desired, English or French mustard
