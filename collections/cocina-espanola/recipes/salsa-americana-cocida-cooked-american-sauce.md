## Ingredients

- meat broth
- Madeira wine
- butter
- corn flour, well toasted
- orange juice, sour

## Directions

1. With broth made with meat, etc., but with the addition of Madeira wine, make a sauce with butter and corn flour, well toasted beforehand in a pan in the oven, so that when mixed with the butter it does not have to stay long in it
2. Add half of the broth and let it cook for two hours, and with the other half put in a pan over strong heat make a thick sauce, stirring continuously in all directions so it does not stick
3. When done, mix it with the sauce and pass it through cheesecloth, then add the juice of a sour orange
