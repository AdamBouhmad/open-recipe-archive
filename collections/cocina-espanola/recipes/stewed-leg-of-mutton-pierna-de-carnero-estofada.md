## Ingredients

- leg of mutton, deboned and prepared
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- carrots
- broth

## Directions

1. Debone and prepare the leg of mutton as described for the previous preparation
2. Cook the leg of mutton always over low heat and with the pot covered, with salt, pepper, onions, carrots, etc., and only as much broth as necessary so the meat does not stick to the vessel
3. Turn the leg from time to time so it cooks evenly
4. Keep the pot covered at all times with parchment paper and its lid on top
5. Serve with the little juice that remains after the operation
