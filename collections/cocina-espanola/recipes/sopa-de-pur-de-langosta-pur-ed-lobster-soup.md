## Ingredients

- 2 lobsters
- boiling water
- salt
- carrots
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- herbs
- reduced sauce, half a quart
- glass of Sherry or Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Place 2 lobsters to cook in boiling salted water with carrots, onion, thyme, bay leaf, and a small bundle of herbs for twenty minutes
2. Remove from fire and when cold, remove the meat from the tails, bodies, and legs, as well as the roe under the tail
3. Pound all these meats and roe in a mortar, reserving the meat from one of the tails
4. When all is paste, incorporate with half a quart of reduced sauce and a glass of Sherry or Madeira
5. Operate in all ways as is known, and when served place in the soup tureen the reserved tail, cut into small cubes
