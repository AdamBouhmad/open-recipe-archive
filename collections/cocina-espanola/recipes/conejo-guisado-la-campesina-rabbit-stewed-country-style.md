## Ingredients

- one rabbit, skinned and cleaned
- raw oil
- parsley
- garlic
- pepper
- hot water
- bay leaves
- cloves
- cinnamon
- capers or lime slices (optional)

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the rabbit without wetting it
2. Cut into pieces and place in a pot or casserole with raw oil, parsley, garlic and pepper to sauté over low heat
3. Add hot water and when half-cooked, add bay leaves, cloves and a pinch of cinnamon
4. It will be very good with some capers or lime slices
