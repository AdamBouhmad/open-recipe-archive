## Ingredients

- whiting
- flour
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Clean the whiting
2. Coat with flour
3. For whole whiting: form it into a ring by putting the tail in the mouth and pressing with wet fingers to seal
4. Heat oil until it begins to smoke with a blue hue over high heat
5. Fry in plenty of hot oil with enough space for the fish to move freely
6. Season with salt when plating to serve
7. Use three to four pounds of oil for half a dozen medium-sized whiting in a large pan
