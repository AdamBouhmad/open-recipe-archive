## Ingredients

- Potatoes, cooked and sliced into wheels
- Fresh cow butter
- Parsley, finely chopped
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Place the hot cooked potatoes sliced into wheels in a tureen or vegetable dish with a lid.
2. Season with salt and pepper and add pieces of fresh cow butter kneaded with finely chopped parsley.
3. Put the lid on the dish and shake and turn the potatoes in the same dish while holding the lid so the butter melts and the mayordoma sauce distributes evenly among the potatoes.
