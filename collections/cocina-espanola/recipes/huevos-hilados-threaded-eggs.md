## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one liter of water
- two dozen egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar in one liter of water, taking care that the point is weak, as soon as it begins to thread.
2. Sift two dozen yolks through a clear sieve with the aid of a spatula, and add them gradually to the clarified sugar, stirring very quickly during cooking, which will last two minutes.
3. This mass or paste is poured into a funnel of cylindrical form, whose bottom is like that of a metal strainer, with holes the size the egg threads should have, and these are collected in very cold water, aiding their passage through the funnel with a wooden pestle or handle that weighs and presses the mass.
