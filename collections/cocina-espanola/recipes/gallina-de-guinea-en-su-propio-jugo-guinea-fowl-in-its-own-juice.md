## Ingredients

- 1 guinea fowl, cleaned and without entrails
- melted lard
- lemon juice
- salt
- garlic, crushed
- parsley, chopped
- pepper
- 2 or 3 bay leaves
- half a bowl of raw oil
- water
- 8 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned guinea fowl on a grill, anointing it frequently with melted lard, lemon juice, salt and crushed garlic
2. When half roasted, place it in a casserole with lard, chopped parsley, pepper, salt, 2 or 3 bay leaves, the remaining lard from roasting, half a bowl of raw oil and enough water to cover it
3. Let it boil until tender
4. Remove the guinea fowl and strain the broth into the casserole
5. Dissolve 8 egg yolks in the broth, beating them well so the broth thickens
6. Let it boil a little and pour over the guinea fowl, which should be in a serving dish, and serve
