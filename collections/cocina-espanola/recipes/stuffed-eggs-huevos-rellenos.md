## Ingredients

- Hard-boiled eggs
- Breadcrumbs soaked in milk
- Fresh butter
- Onions
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine spices

## Directions

1. Cut hard-boiled eggs in half lengthwise.
2. Mash the yolks with an equal amount of breadcrumbs soaked in milk and squeezed dry.
3. Add the same quantity of fresh butter.
4. Add finely chopped onions and parsley, salt, pepper, and fine spices.
5. Mash this mixture and soften it with raw egg yolks.
6. Fill the hard whites with this mixture.
7. Place the remaining mixture on a fireproof plate forming a layer about the thickness of a finger.
8. Arrange the eggs on top.
9. Place the plate in a camp oven with light heat above and below so they take on color.
