## Ingredients

- Sole fish for four persons
- Thirty grams of butter
- Two deciliters of white wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Italian sauce
- Grated and toasted breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Choose fish for four persons; remove the gills, intestines and black skin; scale the white skin, wash and dry.
2. Make an incision on the skinless side of half a centimeter deep on each side of the spine, without touching it, tilting the knife from left to right.
3. Clean, then place in an oval dish thirty grams of butter, two deciliters of white wine, two pinches of salt, and two pinches of pepper.
4. Place the sole also and let it cook for five minutes, covering it with two spoonfuls of Italian sauce and sprinkling with a layer of grated and toasted breadcrumbs of half a centimeter.
5. Cover the gratin dish with the baking dish cover, with moderate fire on top, as there will also be on the stove, ensuring that cooking is not interrupted.
6. Serve at quarter of an hour.
