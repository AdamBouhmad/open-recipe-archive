## Ingredients

- egg-plants
- parsley
- celery
- one or two dozen cloves of garlic
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Procure as many egg-plants as are required
2. Take the stalks off
3. Split each one in three or four lengthways, without separating from the bottom
4. Scald them
5. Place them in a wicker-basket one over the other
6. Put a piece of board or a plate over them
7. Place an iron weight over
8. Let them remain for eight or ten hours, so as to draw the bitter water from them
9. Chop up some parsley and celery
10. Mix with one or two dozen of cloves of garlic, each cut in two
11. Stuff between each slice of the egg-plants with the ingredients
12. Tie up each of the egg-plants with the stalks of celery, to prevent the stuffing from falling out
13. Arrange them in a stone jar one over the other
14. Cover them with wine vinegar
15. Place a piece of clean board over
16. Put an iron weight on the top to press it
17. Let it remain for about three weeks
