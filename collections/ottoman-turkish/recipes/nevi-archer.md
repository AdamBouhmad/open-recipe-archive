## Ingredients

- a pound or so of flour
- half or three-quarters of a pound of scalded fresh butter
- two or three eggs
- three or four tablespoonfuls of cream
- a little salt
- two or three tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Work lightly in a basin a pound or so of flour, with half or three-quarters of a pound of scalded fresh butter
2. Add two or three eggs, three or four tablespoonfuls of cream, a little salt, two or three tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
3. Work all together well to form a nice stiff paste
4. Make it into cakes, or in any shape you please
5. Arrange them in a buttered baking-tin
6. Egg over, and bake a nice brown in the oven
7. Sprinkle some powdered sugar over, and serve
