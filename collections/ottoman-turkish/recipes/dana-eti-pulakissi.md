## Ingredients

- neck or loin of veal
- salt
- water
- parsley
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Cut as much as is wanted of a neck or loin of veal in pieces the size of kidneys
2. Wash and place them in a stewpan, with a little salt, and cover them with water
3. Set it on the fire
4. When boiling, skim and simmer until the meat is nearly cooked
5. Add a handful or two of picked parsley, and let it simmer for a few minutes longer
6. Two minutes before taking it off the fire, add the juice of one lemon
7. Dish it up, and serve
