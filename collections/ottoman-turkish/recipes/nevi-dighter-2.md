## Ingredients

- three pounds of quinces
- four quarts of water
- three pounds of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut three pounds of quinces.
2. Put them in a stewpan with four quarts of water.
3. Set the pan on the fire and let it boil until the fruit becomes like a jelly.
4. Take it off and pass it through a cloth or a sieve into another pan.
5. Add three pounds of powdered sugar.
6. Set it to boil again till it becomes a thick syrup.
7. Take it off and strain it again.
8. When nearly cold, fill your preserve jars and cover them when cold.
9. Try a little on a plate before potting to be certain.
10. If it sets, it is done.
