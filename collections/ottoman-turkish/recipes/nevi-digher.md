## Ingredients

- any fish
- three or four onions
- a plate of picked parsley
- salt
- pepper
- one or two tablespoonfuls of tomato sauce
- half a teacup of wine vinegar
- half a teacup of olive oil
- one teacup of water

## Directions

1. Get any fish you fancy and clean it well
2. Cut three or four onions in thin slices and mix them with a plate of picked parsley
3. Lay half on the bottom of a stewpan
4. Place the fish over and the other half of the onions and parsley on the top
5. Add sufficient salt and pepper, one or two tablespoonfuls of tomato sauce, half a teacup of wine vinegar, the same quantity of olive oil, and one of water
6. Set the pan on a charcoal fire and let it simmer gently until the fish is cooked
7. Dish it up and serve
