## Ingredients

- two or three dozens of smelts
- salt
- flour
- egg
- hot olive oil
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take out the insides of two or three dozens of smelts, wash and sprinkle them with a little salt.
2. Dip them in flour, and then in egg.
3. Fry them a nice colour in hot olive oil.
4. When done, dish up tastefully, squeeze a lemon over, and serve hot.
