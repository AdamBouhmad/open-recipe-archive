## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- jam
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Proceed as No. 194
2. When the omelette is nearly cooked put in the centre some jam of any sort
3. Turn the edges over, let it set a moment, then turn it over on to a dish
4. Shake some white sugar over, and serve
