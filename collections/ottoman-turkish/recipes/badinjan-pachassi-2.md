## Ingredients

- egg-plants
- salt water
- flour
- fresh butter
- three or four cloves of garlic
- a little salt
- a small teacup of wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut as many egg-plants as are wanted in slices a quarter of an inch thick, and put them in salt water for about ten minutes
2. Dip them in flour, fry them a nice brown in fresh butter, and arrange them in a baking-dish
3. Well pound three or four cloves of garlic in a mortar, add a little salt, and a small teacup of wine vinegar
4. Stir it well with a wooden spoon, and pour it over the egg-plants
5. Place the dish on a moderate charcoal fire until the liquor is absorbed, then take it off, and serve
