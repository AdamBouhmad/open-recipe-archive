## Ingredients

- three pounds of crushed sugar
- two pints of cold water
- white of an egg
- one pound of morella cherries

## Directions

1. Put about three pounds of crushed sugar in a stewpan, with two pints of cold water, and the white of an egg, well beaten up
2. Stir it well, and put it on the fire
3. When boiling, skim it well, and let it simmer until a little thickish
4. Add a pound of morella cherries with the stalks taken off, and well washed
5. Let it boil gently for a few minutes longer, then take it off
6. When nearly cold, take the cherries out with a strainer, and pass the syrup through a clean cloth
7. Bottle and cork it up
