## Ingredients

- one pound of strawberries or raspberries
- three or four ounces of powdered sugar
- two or three tablespoonfuls of water
- an ounce and a half of melted isinglass
- half a pint of the fruit

## Directions

1. Take off the stalks of a pound of strawberries or raspberries, wash and put them in a stewpan.
2. Add three or four ounces of powdered sugar, and two or three tablespoonfuls of water.
3. Put the pan on a moderate charcoal fire, and boil till rather thick, stirring it at the same time with a wooden spoon.
4. When cold, pass it through a sieve into a basin.
5. Add an ounce and a half of melted isinglass to half a pint of the fruit, and stir it well.
