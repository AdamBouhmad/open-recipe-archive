## Ingredients

- vermicelli
- butter
- water
- syrup

## Directions

1. Make three or four pounds of vermicelli as No. 149
2. Lay it in a rather deep baking-tin, about two inches thick
3. Nearly cover it with scalded fresh butter
4. Bake it a nice colour on both sides in the oven
5. When done, strain the butter
6. Pour hot water over to wash out all the fat
7. Pour hot syrup over, as No. 149
8. Put it again in the oven for five minutes or so
9. Cut it in four crossways, or in any fanciful shape you please
10. Dish up and serve hot or cold
