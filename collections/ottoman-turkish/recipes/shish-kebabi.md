## Ingredients

- five pounds of loin or leg of mutton
- four middle-sized onions
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- half a dozen tomatos or four or five tablespoonfuls of preserved tomato sauce
- water

## Directions

1. Cut five pounds of loin or leg of mutton in pieces the size of an egg
2. Chop four middle-sized onions, and sprinkle them over the meat with sufficient salt, pepper, and cinnamon
3. Rub them in well with the hands, and let it remain covered for two or three hours
4. If in season, squeeze half a dozen tomatos, and pass the juice through a sieve into a basin; if not, mix four or five tablespoonfuls of preserved tomato sauce in three quarters of a pint of water
5. Pass the pieces of meat one by one on the skewers, place them in front of the fire, and gradually turn them
6. As soon as the meat begins to brown, baste it now and then with the tomato juice
7. When nicely cooked, dish it up, and serve hot
