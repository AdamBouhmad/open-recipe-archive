## Ingredients

- vine leaves
- raw mutton
- one or two onions
- three or four tablespoonfuls of rice well washed
- salt
- pepper
- water
- broth

## Directions

1. Prepare some vine leaves
2. Chop up fine a sufficient quantity of raw mutton with one or two onions
3. Add three or four tablespoonfuls of rice well washed, a little salt and pepper, and mix them well together with a little water, to make it like a paste
4. Lay the vine leaves open, put in each one a teaspoonful of this stuffing, and wrap them up about the length and the thickness of your thumb
5. Squeeze them gently with your hand one by one, that they may retain the stuffing while cooking
6. Place them in a saucepan without any water, and set it on the fire with the cover over
7. When their own moisture is exhausted, add some of the broth, and let it simmer until it forms a nice gravy at the bottom
8. Take one out and try if the meat and the rice are done; if not, add some more broth
9. When the rice and the meat are quite tender, dish up, and serve
