## Ingredients

- three or four French rolls
- eggs
- scalded fresh butter
- white sugar
- water
- rose-water

## Directions

1. Cut the French rolls in slices lengthways, about a quarter of an inch thick
2. Beat up a few eggs in a basin
3. Dip the slices of bread in the beaten eggs
4. Fry them a nice delicate colour in scalded fresh butter on both sides
5. Take them out with a slice and let them drain
6. Make a syrup of white sugar and water as in No. 159
7. Put the fried bread in hot syrup and let them soak for ten or fifteen minutes
8. Take them out and place them tastefully on a dish
9. Sprinkle a little rose-water over and serve
10. If rolls are not handy, slices of bread with the crusts taken off can be used instead
