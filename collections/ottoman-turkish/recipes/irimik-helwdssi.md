## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter
- a pound and six ounces of sago
- four ounces of skinned almonds
- a pint and a half of boiling milk
- a pound and a half of loaf sugar
- a pint and a half of water
- rose-water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Set on the fire a stewpan, with three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter
2. When hot, add gradually a pound and six ounces of sago, with four ounces of skinned almonds, and keep stirring it until the almonds are a nice brown
3. Pour in all at once a pint and a half of boiling milk, with boiling syrup, which you have previously made of a pound and a half of loaf sugar and a pint and a half of water
4. Stir it well, put the cover over, and let it remain for fifteen minutes
5. Sprinkle a little rose-water over, dish up, shake some sugar over, and serve
