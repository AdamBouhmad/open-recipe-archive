## Ingredients

- half a dozen egg-plants
- raw mutton
- one or two onions
- mint
- small quantity of saffron
- two or three tablespoonfuls of the inside of egg-plants
- handful of well-washed rice
- salt
- pepper
- rib-bones of mutton
- broth
- juice of two or three lemons

## Directions

1. Cut off the tops and take out the insides of half a dozen egg-plants, leaving only sufficient to bear the stuffing
2. Put them in salt water for ten minutes or so
3. Mince fine a sufficient quantity of raw mutton
4. Add one or two onions, a little mint, a small quantity of saffron, two or three tablespoonfuls of the inside of egg-plants, all chopped fine
5. Add a handful of well-washed rice, a little salt and pepper
6. Mix them well all together with your hands
7. Stuff the egg-plants with it, and cover them with the tops
8. Lay some rib-bones of mutton on the bottom of a stewpan, and place the egg-plants over carefully
9. Cover them with broth, add the juice of two or three lemons
10. Set the pan on a charcoal fire with the cover over, and let it simmer until the egg-plants are soft, and a nice gravy is formed at the bottom
11. Dish them up carefully, and serve
