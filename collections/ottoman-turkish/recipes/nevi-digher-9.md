## Ingredients

- flour
- salt
- two or three ounces of fresh butter
- water
- tablespoonful of fried minced meat
- finely-chopped onions
- hot butter

## Directions

1. Put sufficient flour upon a pastry slab, make a hole in the centre, in which put a little salt, two or three ounces of fresh butter melted, mix all together, then add sufficient water, and make it a softish paste.
2. While working it, dip your hands in butter now and then, and knead it till a nice paste is formed.
3. Throw some flour lightly over and under, roll it out with a rolling-pin to the thickness of half-a-crown.
4. Put a tablespoonful of fried minced meat on it, mixed with finely-chopped onions stewed brown in fresh butter, or grated cheese, about an inch and a half distance from each other.
5. Wet lightly round them with a paste-brush, and place a similar piece of paste over all.
6. Press round the part where the minced meat or cheese is, to make the paste stick.
7. Cut them out like half an egg, and fry them in hot butter.
8. When nicely browned, take them out, and let them drain, dish up.
