## Ingredients

- six ounces of the best rice
- two pints and three-quarters of nice milk
- powdered sugar
- rose-water (optional)

## Directions

1. Wash and scald six ounces of the best rice
2. Drain it
3. Pour into a stewpan two pints and three-quarters of nice milk
4. Set it on the fire
5. As soon as it commences to boil, add the rice
6. Let it simmer until quite soft
7. Take it off and pour it into a pie-dish
8. Put it on a very slow charcoal fire, covered with ashes, or on the hob
9. Let it remain for about fifteen minutes, during which time the top of the rice will be covered with cream
10. When cold, sprinkle sufficient powdered sugar over
11. Serve (You may sweeten the rice while boiling, and add one or two tablespoonfuls of rose-water)
