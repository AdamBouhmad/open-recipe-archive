## Ingredients

- egg-plants
- salt
- flour
- water
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Procure as many egg-plants as are wanted, cut them in slices about half an inch thick
2. Sprinkle them with salt, and let them remain about an hour
3. When the bitter juice is drawn from them, put two or three ounces of flour into a basin, which mix with water to form a smooth batter, not too thick
4. Dip the egg-plants in it, and fry them in fresh butter till a nice colour, turning them over
5. When done, take them out with a slice, let them drain, dish up, and serve
