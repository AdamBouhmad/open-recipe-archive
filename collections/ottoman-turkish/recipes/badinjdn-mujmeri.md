## Ingredients

- six or seven egg-plants
- water
- salt
- three or four onions
- fresh butter
- four or five tablespoonfuls of fried minced meat of raw mutton
- parsley chopped fine
- pepper
- two or three eggs

## Directions

1. Procure six or seven egg-plants, cut them in two lengthways, take out the insides
2. Boil them a little in water, moderately salt
3. Take them out, and put them in a basin
4. Add three or four finely chopped onions, previously fried a nice brown in fresh butter, four or five tablespoonfuls of fried minced meat (prepared of raw mutton), a handful of parsley chopped fine, sufficient salt and pepper, and two or three eggs well beaten up
5. Mix them well together
6. Butter a baking-pan, in which arrange half of the egg-plants, and stuff them with the above mixture
7. Cover them with the remaining half, and bake them in a rather hot oven
8. When the egg-plants are shrunk and soft, they are cooked
9. Dish up, and serve
