## Ingredients

- one pound of fresh gathered violets
- two pints and a gill of boiling water
- three pounds of crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Procure a pound of fresh gathered violets, cut the stalks off, and put the leaves in a basin
2. Pour two pints and a gill of boiling water over, immediately cover it, and let it remain for twelve or fifteen hours
3. Pass the liquor through a clean cloth into a stewpan
4. Add about three pounds of crushed sugar
5. Stir with a wooden spoon till the sugar is dissolved, and set it on a charcoal fire
6. Just as it commences to boil up, instantly remove it, and let it remain covered till nearly cold
7. Pass it again through a cloth, bottle and cork it up till wanted
