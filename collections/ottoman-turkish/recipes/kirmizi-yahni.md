## Ingredients

- leg of lamb or mutton
- fresh butter
- three or four onions
- half or three-quarters of a pint of chick-peas

## Directions

1. Cut as much as is required of a leg of lamb or mutton in pieces, and boil as above
2. Add three or four finely-sliced onions, fried in fresh butter
3. Add half or three-quarters of a pint of chick-peas previously scalded in water
4. Cook and serve as No. 36
