## Ingredients

- morella cherries
- two round penny loaves
- water
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare some morella cherries as above.
2. Cut two round penny loaves in slices, and toast them.
3. Lay them in a pie-dish or saucepan, put the cherries over with the syrup, and nearly cover them with water.
4. Put the pan on the fire, and let it simmer till the slices of toasted bread are soft.
5. Dish up one over the other, laying between them the cherries, and sprinkling at the same time powdered sugar over.
6. Serve hot or cold.
