## Ingredients

- 2 pounds morella cherries
- 4 pounds white sugar
- 1 pint of water

## Directions

1. Take off the stalks and extract the stones of the cherries
2. Put 4 pounds of white sugar in a stewpan with a pint of water
3. Put it on the fire until it begins to thicken
4. Add the cherries and stir with a wooden spoon
5. Boil till rather thick
6. Try a little on a plate to see if it sets
7. When cold, fill the reserve jars or glass bowls with covers
8. Let them remain in a cool place
