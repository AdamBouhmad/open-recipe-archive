## Ingredients

- egg-plants
- parsley
- celery
- one or two dozen cloves of garlic
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Procure as many egg-plants as are required and take the stalks off
2. Split each one in three or four lengthways without separating from the bottom
3. Scald them
4. Place them in a wicker-basket one over the other
5. Put a piece of board or a plate over them with an iron weight on top
6. Let them remain for eight or ten hours to draw the bitter water from them
7. Chop up some parsley and celery
8. Mix with one or two dozen cloves of garlic, each cut in two
9. Stuff between each slice of the egg-plants with the ingredients
10. Tie up each of the egg-plants with the stalks of celery to prevent the stuffing from falling out
11. Arrange them in a stone jar one over the other
12. Cover them with wine vinegar
13. Place a piece of clean board over and an iron weight on the top to press it
14. Let it remain for about three weeks before using
