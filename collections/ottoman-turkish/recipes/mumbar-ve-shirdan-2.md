## Ingredients

- ventricle or intestine of sheep
- raw mutton
- one or two teacupfuls of rice
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- water

## Directions

1. Get some ventricle or intestine of sheep well cleaned and ready for use
2. Mince a sufficient quantity of raw mutton
3. Add one or two teacupfuls of rice, well washed, sufficient salt and pepper, a little cinnamon, and mix them well together with a little water, to form it like a paste
4. Tie one end of the ventricle and stuff it with the minced meat through a funnel
5. Tie the other end and prick it all over with a needle
