## Ingredients

- one pint of the best white honey, previously boiled and skimmed
- one pound of the best fresh butter, previously melted
- one pint of hot or cold water
- one pound of ground rice
- one teaspoonful of carbonate of soda dissolved in three tablespoonfuls of water
- powdered sugar
- one pound of loaf-sugar (alternative to honey)

## Directions

1. Put in a clean stewpan a pint of the best white honey, which you have previously boiled and skimmed, a pound of the best fresh butter, previously melted, a pint of hot or cold water, a pound of ground rice, and a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda dissolved in three tablespoonfuls of water
2. Put the pan on a charcoal fire, and stir it until reduced to a thick pulp
3. Pour it in a lightly-buttered baking-tin, spread it all over with a spoon, and bake it a nice brown in a hot oven
4. When done, cut it up in diamonds, sprinkle some powdered sugar over, dish up in pyramid, and serve
5. If honey is not fancied, use a pound of loaf-sugar, previously dissolved in water, and boiled to the consistency of a syrup
