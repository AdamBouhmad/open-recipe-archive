## Ingredients

- a pound of wheat-starch
- water
- a pound of the best white honey
- half a pound of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Put in a clean stewpan a pound of wheat-starch, a pint of water, a pound of the best white honey, and half a pound of fresh butter melted
2. Put it on the fire and commence stirring until the butter begins to appear on the surface of the mixture
3. Keep stirring from the bottom of the pan and pressing it with the back of the spoon for a few minutes longer
4. Dish up like a dome and serve
