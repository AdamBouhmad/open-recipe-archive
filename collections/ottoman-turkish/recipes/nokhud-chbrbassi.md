## Ingredients

- chick peas
- broth
- cinnamon
- toasted or fried bread

## Directions

1. Scald a sufficient quantity of chick peas, and remove the pods, or skins
2. Put them again into the same water, and boil them until quite soft
3. Drain and mash them with the back of a wooden spoon, and pass them through a sieve into a stewpan
4. Moisten it with as much broth as the quantity of soup wanted
5. Set it on the fire, and let it simmer until reduced to a nice consistency
6. Pour it in a tureen, sprinkle a little cinnamon over, and serve with toasted or fried bread in small dice
