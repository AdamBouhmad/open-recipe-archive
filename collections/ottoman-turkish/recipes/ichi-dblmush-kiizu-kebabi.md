## Ingredients

- small lamb
- liver
- heart
- fresh butter
- onions
- skinned pistachios
- currants
- rice
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- onion-juice

## Directions

1. Get the whole of a small lamb with the liver and the heart
2. Place it in a large saucepan or iron pot without the liver and the heart
3. Cover it with water, set it on the fire, and let it boil for ten or fifteen minutes
4. Chop up the liver very fine together with the heart
5. Put three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter in a stewpan with three or four chopped onions
6. Put it on the fire, and let them stew till nearly brown
7. Add the liver and the heart, with a handful of skinned pistachios, and the same of currants well cleaned
8. Stir till the liver begins to cook, then add two or three pounds of the best rice, well washed, sufficient salt, pepper, and cinnamon
9. Keep stirring, and add now and then a little of the liquor in which the lamb is boiled, until the rice is rather soft
10. Stuff the lamb with this preparation, and sew it up
11. Rub the lamb all over with some onion-juice
12. Place it in a baking tin, and bake it a nice brown on both sides in a very hot oven
13. When done, dish up, and serve
