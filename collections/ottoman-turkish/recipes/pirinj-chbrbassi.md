## Ingredients

- three or four ounces of the best rice
- salt
- broth
- yolk of one or two eggs
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash three or four ounces of the best rice
2. Put it into a stewpan, with a little salt, and three or four pints of broth
3. Set the pan on the fire, and let it boil gently till the rice is soft
4. Add the yolk of one or two eggs prepared as above
5. Pour the soup into a tureen, sprinkle a little cinnamon over, and serve
