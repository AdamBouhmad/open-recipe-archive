## Ingredients

- one pound of loaf sugar
- two pints of water
- cake (No. 186)
- rose-water
- skinned almonds or pistachios

## Directions

1. Put a pound of loaf sugar in a stewpan with two pints of water
2. Set it to boil until it becomes a syrup
3. Take it off and pour it into a rather deep baking-tin
4. Put it on a moderate charcoal fire
5. When boiling, put in a cake (No. 186)
6. Bruise it with the back of a wooden spoon
7. Continue in this manner as many cakes as it will take
8. When thick, take it off
9. Sprinkle some rose-water over
10. Let it remain until cold
11. Dish it up
12. Ornament the top with some skinned almonds or pistachios
13. Serve
