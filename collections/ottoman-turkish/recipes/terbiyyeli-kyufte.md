## Ingredients

- two or three pounds of raw mutton
- two or three onions
- salt
- pepper
- water
- yolks of three or four eggs
- juice of a lemon or two

## Directions

1. Mince very fine two or three pounds of the best part of raw mutton, with two or three onions peeled, and a little salt and pepper
2. Make it in balls the size of nuts
3. Put them in a saucepan half full of water, set it on the fire until done
4. Beat up the yolks of three or four eggs, with the juice of a lemon or two, and a little salt
5. Pour it in a saucepan, put it on a slow fire, and keep stirring until it begins to thicken
6. Add three or four tablespoonfuls (one at a time) of the liquor in which the balls of meat have been boiling
7. Stir it for two or three minutes longer, then dish the meat up, pour this sauce over
