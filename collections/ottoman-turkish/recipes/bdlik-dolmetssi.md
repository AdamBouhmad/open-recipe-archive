## Ingredients

- three or four middle-sized mackerel, whitings, or other fish
- half a teacup of olive oil
- three or four finely-chopped onions
- two or three ounces of pistachios, skinned
- one or two tablespoonfuls of currants
- pepper
- salt
- mixed spice
- flour
- egg
- hot olive oil

## Directions

1. Get three or four middle-sized mackerel, or whitings, or any other fish you may fancy, well clean them
2. Take out the gills
3. Break the bones of the tails, and skin them, without tearing the skin, in one piece, and the head must be attached to the skin
4. Remove the flesh from the bones, and chop it up
5. Put half a teacup of olive oil in a frying pan, place it on the fire
6. When hot, put in three or four finely-chopped onions, and stir them with a wooden spoon until nicely browned
7. Add the fish, with two or three ounces of pistachios, skinned, one or two tablespoonfuls of currants, a little pepper and salt, and a little mixed spice
8. Keep stirring until the fish is nearly done
9. Stuff the fish skins with it
10. Dip them in flour, and then in egg, and fry them in hot olive oil till a nice colour, turning them over
11. When done, take them out with a slice carefully, let them drain
12. Dish up on a white napkin, and serve
