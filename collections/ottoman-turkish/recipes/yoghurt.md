## Ingredients

- four pints of pure milk
- two tablespoonfuls of old curd

## Directions

1. Put four pints of pure milk into a particularly clean stewpan
2. Put it on a charcoal fire and let it bubble once
3. Take it off and pour it into a basin
4. When cold enough to bear your finger in it, add two tablespoonfuls of old curd
5. Stir it well with the handle of a wooden spoon
6. Put the basin on some straw in a warm corner of the pantry
7. Cover the basin all over with a piece of flannel three or four thicknesses
8. Let it remain for ten or twelve hours
9. Serve as it is when nicely set with the top covered with cream
