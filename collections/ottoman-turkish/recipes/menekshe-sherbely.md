## Ingredients

- 1 pound very fresh violet-leaves
- 5 pounds white sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Procure a pound of very fresh violet-leaves
2. Put them on a convenient dish or a clean board with 5 pounds of white sifted sugar
3. Rub well together until the sugar becomes the same colour as the violet leaves

5. Put them by
