## Ingredients

- one or two pounds of clotted cream
- paste as No. 135
- scalded fresh butter
- white of four or five eggs

## Directions

1. Procure one or two pounds of clotted cream
2. Prepare forty or fifty pieces of paste as No. 135, and lay half of them in a baking-tin, sprinkling a little scalded fresh butter between each piece
3. Put in a basin the white of four or five eggs, divide the cream in pieces, rub them with the white of eggs both under and over with a quill or a paste-brush, and lay them all over the paste
4. Cover them with the remaining pieces of paste, sprinkling butter between each as before
5. Cut it in diamonds, or any other shape you fancy
6. Bake it a delicate colour, then pour some syrup over as above, and serve
