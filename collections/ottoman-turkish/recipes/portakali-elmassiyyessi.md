## Ingredients

- six nice oranges
- five or six ounces of powdered sugar
- half a pint of water
- a small teacup of water
- an ounce of isinglass
- a few drops of prepared cochineal

## Directions

1. Get six nice oranges, take off the rind of one of them, and remove the pith, place them in a basin
2. Squeeze the juice of the oranges in it
3. Put five or six ounces of powdered sugar in a saucepan with half a pint of water, and boil it until it becomes a syrup
4. Take it off, and add the juice and the rind of the oranges
5. Put the saucepan again on the fire with the cover over
6. As soon as it begins to boil, skim it well, and add gradually a small teacup of water
7. Let it boil one or two minutes longer, then add an ounce of isinglass, dissolved as directed
8. Stir well, and pass it through a jelly-bag till clear
9. Add a few drops of prepared cochineal, and pour it in a mould
10. Place it on ice until firm, then turn it out and serve
