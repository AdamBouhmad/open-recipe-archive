## Ingredients

- prawns
- parsley
- salt
- lemon juice
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Take off the skins and the heads of three or four dozen prawns
2. Arrange them in a dish with a handful or two of picked parsley
3. Sprinkle a pinch or two of salt over, with the juice of one or two lemons
4. Add two or three tablespoonfuls of the best oil, and serve
