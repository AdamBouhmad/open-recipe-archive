## Ingredients

- cabbage-lettuces
- beet-root
- eggs, hard-boiled
- chervil, chopped
- tarragon
- chives or young onions
- salt
- pepper
- olive oil
- wine vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Procure two or three cabbage-lettuces
2. Remove the outer leaves and throw them away
3. Pull the others one by one
4. Wash and well drain them in a cloth
5. Put them in the bowl, with a few slices of beet-root, one or two hard-boiled eggs cut in quarters
6. Add two teaspoonfuls of chopped chervil, one of tarragon, and one of chives or young onions
7. Sprinkle over with your fingers half a teaspoonful of salt, one or two pinches of pepper
8. Add two or three tablespoonfuls of the best olive oil, and one and-a-half of wine vinegar, or the juice of a lemon
9. Turn the salad in the bowl with a spoon and fork until well mixed, and serve
