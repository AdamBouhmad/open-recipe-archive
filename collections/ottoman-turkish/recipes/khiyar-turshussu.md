## Ingredients

- one or two dozen of small cucumbers
- fennel
- salt water

## Directions

1. Get one or two dozen of small cucumbers
2. Put some fennel on the bottom of a stone jar
3. Place the cucumbers over
4. Lay some fennel on the top
5. Cover them with salt water
6. Place a piece of board over them
7. Put an iron weight or a heavy stone on the top to press it
8. Put the jar for a few days in a place where the sun is powerful, or in a warm place
9. Remove it to a cool place
10. Let it remain eight or ten days before using
