## Ingredients

- six or seven egg-plants
- salt
- a teacupful of olive oil
- half a pound of onions
- a pound of well-washed rice
- pepper
- a tablespoonful of finely-chopped mint
- a teacup of water

## Directions

1. Prepare six or seven egg-plants as No. 107
2. Sprinkle some salt over the insides of the egg-plants
3. After a few minutes squeeze them well with your hands so as to be free from bitter water
4. Chop them up
5. Put a teacupful of olive oil in a saucepan and place it on the fire
6. When hot, put in half a pound of onions chopped fine and stir till they begin to brown
7. Add the insides of the egg-plants and stir for three minutes or so
8. Add a pound of well-washed rice, sufficient salt and pepper, a tablespoonful of finely-chopped mint, and a teacup of water
9. Keep stirring until the moisture is absorbed
10. Stuff the egg-plants moderately with it and put the lids over
11. Prick them here and there with a fork
12. Place them in a saucepan and nearly cover them with water
13. Set it on the fire with the cover over and let it boil gently until the liquor is absorbed
14. Dish up and serve hot or cold
