## Ingredients

- white sugar
- water
- muffins
- pistachios or almonds
- clotted cream
- syrup

## Directions

1. Put a pound of white sugar in a clean saucepan, with two pints of water
2. Set it to boil until it becomes a syrup
3. Cut open four or five muffins
4. Put them in the syrup for two or three minutes
5. Take them out carefully with a slice
6. Lay half of them in a baking-tin
7. Sprinkle some pounded pistachios or almonds over
8. Lay a layer of clotted cream over, a quarter of an inch thick
9. Sprinkle again pistachios or almonds
10. Cover them with the other half of the muffins
11. Pour three-quarters of a pint of the syrup over
12. Put it in the oven, or on a moderate charcoal fire, until the syrup is nearly absorbed
13. Dish up and serve
