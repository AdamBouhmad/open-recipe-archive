## Ingredients

- leg or any other part of mutton
- salt
- pepper
- water
- chick-peas
- onions
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut as much as is wanted of a leg or any other part of mutton in pieces the size of walnuts or eggs
2. Wash and place them in a stewpan with a little salt and pepper
3. Cover them with water and put it on a charcoal fire
4. Let it boil for about twenty minutes and skim it well
5. Add half a pint of chick-peas previously scalded in water, and two or three onions sliced
6. Let it simmer until it forms a nice thick gravy at the bottom of the pan and the meat and peas are tender
7. Dish it up with two or three slices of toasted bread on the bottom of the dish
8. Serve whilst hot
