## Ingredients

- three or four ounces of raw mutton
- a small onion
- a little parsley
- a little fresh butter
- five or six eggs
- half a teaspoonful of salt
- a pinch or two of pepper
- three or four ounces of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Mince three or four ounces of raw mutton, with a small onion and a little parsley, and partly fry them in a little fresh butter
2. Break into a basin five or six eggs, and beat them up well with a fork
3. Add the minced meat with half a teaspoonful of salt and a pinch or two of pepper, and beat them up well
4. Put three or four ounces of fresh butter into the frying-pan, and put it on the fire
5. When hot, pour in the mixture, and fry a delicate colour on both sides
6. When done, turn it onto a dish carefully, and serve
