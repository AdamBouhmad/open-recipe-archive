## Ingredients

- three or four pomegranates
- four ounces of loaf sugar
- water equal to the quantity of juice

## Directions

1. Cut open three or four pomegranates and take the grains out
2. Pick up the large ones and put the small ones in a cloth
3. Squeeze all the juice from them into a saucepan
4. Add four ounces of loaf sugar with the same quantity of water as the juice
5. Stir on a slow fire until it becomes thickish
6. Remove it and let it cool
7. Proceed as above by pouring into a glass bowl and serving with ice and essence
