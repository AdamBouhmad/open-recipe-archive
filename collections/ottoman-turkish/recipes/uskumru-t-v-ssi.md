## Ingredients

- mackerel
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- mixed spice
- lemons
- eggs
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Take out the insides and the gills of half a dozen middle-sized mackerel, wash well, and sprinkle them with a little salt, and let them remain twenty minutes or so
2. Peel two or three onions, and well pound them in a mortar, to which add sufficient salt and pepper, mixed spice, the juice of two or three lemons, and the yolks of three or four eggs, and well beat them up
3. Pour it into a pie-dish, roll the mackerel in it, and let them remain for about two hours
4. Fry them a nice brown in hot olive oil, turning them over
5. When done, take them out with a slice and let them drain well, dish them up tastefully, squeeze a lemon over, if approved of, and serve hot
