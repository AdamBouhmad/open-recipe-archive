## Ingredients

- an egg-plant
- six eggs
- a tablespoonful of currants
- two tablespoonfuls of skinned pistachios
- pepper
- powdered cinnamon
- four or five ounces of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Peel an egg-plant, take out the inside, if seeded, and grate it or chop it up fine
2. Scald it in moderately-salt water, strain and squeeze from it all the water in a cloth
3. Break in a basin six eggs, beat them well up, and add the egg-plants, with a tablespoonful of currants, two of skinned pistachios, a little pepper and powdered cinnamon, mix well all together
4. Put in the frying-pan four or five ounces of fresh butter, put it on the fire till hot, then pour in the mixture and fry it a nice brown on both sides
5. When done, turn it over carefully onto a dish, and serve
