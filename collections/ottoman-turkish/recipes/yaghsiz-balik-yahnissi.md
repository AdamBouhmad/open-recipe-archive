## Ingredients

- six or seven red mullets
- salt
- three pints of water
- three or four onions
- three or four heads of garlic
- two or three onions
- two or three handfuls of parsley
- pepper
- half a lemon

## Directions

1. Get six or seven red mullets, take out the insides, well clean them, and sprinkle some salt over
2. Put three pints of water in a saucepan with three or four onions cut in four crossways and three or four heads of garlic with only the outer skins peeled
3. Put it on the fire and let boil until the water is reduced to about one pint
4. Take the onions and the garlic out, throw the onions away, but squeeze the garlic and add the pulp to the liquor
5. Peel two or three onions and chop them very fine with two or three handfuls of parsley
6. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper over and well rub them
7. Stuff the fish with it and lay it carefully in a stewpan
8. Pour the liquor over, set it on a charcoal fire and let it simmer till the fish is nicely cooked
9. Dish it up, squeeze half a lemon over, and serve hot
