## Ingredients

- rump steak or veal
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- wine vinegar
- cinnamon
- cummin
- broth
- butter
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut as much as is wanted of rump steak or veal in slices the size of a hand, and about half an inch thick
2. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper over, place them on a gridiron, set it on a moderate charcoal fire, and frequently turn them
3. When nicely browned, place them in a stewpan with a few cloves of garlic peeled, and a half or a tea-cup of wine vinegar, a little cinnamon and cummin
4. Cover them with broth, put the pan with the cover over on a moderate charcoal fire, and let it simmer till tender
5. Serve hot
