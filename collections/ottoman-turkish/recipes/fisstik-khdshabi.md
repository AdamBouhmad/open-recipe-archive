## Ingredients

- half or a pound of pistachios
- a teacup or two of rose-water
- powdered sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Procure half or a pound of pistachios and skin them
2. Pound the broken and part of the small ones very fine in a stone mortar
3. Add a teacup or two of rose-water and stir it well
4. Pass it through a clean cloth into a glass bowl
5. Mix it with sufficient powdered sugar and water to make a nice sherbet
6. Add the remaining pistachios and serve
