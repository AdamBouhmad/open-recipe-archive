## Ingredients

- two pounds of white sugar
- two quarts of water
- about five ounces of wheat starch
- essence of rose or orange-flower
- almond-oil
- skinned pistachios or almonds (optional)

## Directions

1. Dissolve two pounds of white sugar in a stewpan, with two quarts of water
2. Add about five ounces of wheat starch and stir it well
3. When as smooth as milk, put it on a moderate charcoal fire
4. Continually stir it for about three hours, or until it forms a smooth substance, but not too thick
5. Add a few drops of the essence of rose or orange-flower
6. Stir it for a few minutes longer
7. Pour it into a mould previously oiled with almond-oil
8. Put it on ice, or in a cool place, till firm
9. When ready, dip the mould in warm water and turn it out
10. Serve (you can ornament the top with some skinned pistachios or almonds)
