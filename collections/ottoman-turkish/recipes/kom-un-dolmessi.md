## Ingredients

- a musk-melon or any other melon
- raw minced mutton
- fresh butter
- two onions
- half a teacup of rice
- salt
- pepper
- a tablespoonful of currants
- two or three tablespoonfuls of almonds, skinned
- two or three tablespoonfuls of pistachios

## Directions

1. Procure a musk-melon, or any other one
2. Cut out the part where the stalk is, twice the size of a crown piece, so as to form a lid
3. Take out the seeds, and part of the inside
4. Fry a nice brown a sufficient quantity of raw minced mutton in fresh butter, with two onions chopped fine
5. Add half a teacup of rice well cleaned, sufficient salt and pepper, a tablespoonful of currants, two or three tablespoonfuls of almonds, skinned, and the same of pistachios
6. Stir three or four minutes longer, and take it off
7. Stuff the melon with this mixture
8. Put the piece over that you have previously cut out for a lid, and pin it round with a few wooden pins
9. Put it in a baking-dish, and bake it nicely in the oven
10. Dish it up, and serve
