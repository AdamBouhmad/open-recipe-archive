## Ingredients

- two or three heads of endive
- small teaspoonful of salt
- one or two pinches of pepper
- juice of a small lemon
- three tablespoonfuls of olive oil
- tablespoonful of chopped chervil
- one tablespoonful of chives

## Directions

1. Remove the dark green leaves from endive
2. Pick off the others one by one
3. Cut the large ones in two
4. Wash and drain them in a cloth, by shaking it to and fro violently, and extract all the water
5. Put in the salad-bowl a small teaspoonful of salt, one or two pinches of pepper, the juice of a small lemon, three tablespoonfuls of olive oil
6. Beat up until it becomes like cream
7. Put in the salad
8. Turn it with spoon and fork till well mixed
9. Serve
