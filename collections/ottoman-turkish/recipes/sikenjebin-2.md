## Ingredients

- white of an egg
- three pounds of powdered sugar
- three-quarters of a pint of the best white-wine vinegar
- sweet-marjoram

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan the white of an egg, well beaten up, three pounds of powdered sugar
2. Pour in all at once three-quarters of a pint of the best white-wine vinegar
3. Stir it once round with a wooden spoon
4. Set it on a charcoal fire, and boil it for half a minute, then skim and take it off
5. Instantly put in a small bunch of sweet-marjoram tied up in a piece of muslin
6. Put the cover over the pan, and leave it until cold
7. Pass it through a clean cloth, bottle and cork it up until wanted
