## Ingredients

- four or five beetroots
- water
- one or two cloves of garlic
- salt
- ounce of powdered sugar
- half a pint of nice wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the stalks off and well wash four or five beetroots
2. Boil them till tender
3. Peel and cut them into rather thin slices
4. Arrange them in a jar or glass bowl
5. Add half a pint of the water in which they are boiled
6. Pound one or two cloves of garlic in a stone mortar with a little salt
7. Add an ounce of powdered sugar, and half a pint of nice wine vinegar
8. Stir well
9. Pour it over the beetroots
10. Ready for use
