## Ingredients

- loin of mutton or lamb
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut sufficient quantity of a loin of mutton or lamb in pieces the size of walnuts, and rinse them with water
2. Sprinkle one or two chopped onions over, with sufficient salt, pepper, and cinnamon
3. Mix well together, put them in a stewpan without any water, and cover it air tight
4. Set it on a moderate charcoal fire for nearly four hours, and stir it now and then
5. Be careful, for if the fire is too slow the meat will not brown nicely
6. When the pieces of meat are a nice colour and tender, dish them up, and serve hot
