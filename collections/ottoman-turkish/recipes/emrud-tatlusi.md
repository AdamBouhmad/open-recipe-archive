## Ingredients

- stewing pears
- syrup
- cloves
- prepared cochineal

## Directions

1. Peel and take off the stalks of as many stewing pears as are wanted
2. If too large, cut them in two and remove the cores
3. Place them in a stewpan with some syrup, a few cloves, and a few drops of prepared cochineal
4. Let it simmer gently on a slow fire until the pears are tender
5. Take it off and serve cold
