## Ingredients

- two pounds of morella cherries
- four pounds of white sugar
- one pint of water

## Directions

1. Take off the stalks and extract the stones of two pounds of morella cherries.
2. Put four pounds of white sugar in a stewpan with a pint of water.
3. Put it on the fire until it begins to thicken.
4. Add the cherries and stir with a wooden spoon.
5. Boil till rather thick.
6. Try a little on a plate.
7. If it sets, take it off.
8. When cold, fill the reserve jars or glass bowls with covers.
9. Let them remain in a cool place.
