## Ingredients

- one or two dozen of either ripe or unripe tomatoes
- stuffing

## Directions

1. Procure one or two dozen of either ripe or unripe tomatoes
2. Cut off the tops about the size of a shilling, but without separating them, so as to form lids
3. Take out the seeds and part of the inside
4. Prepare some stuffing
5. Stuff them, and close the lids
6. Place them carefully in a stewpan, and nearly cover them with broth
7. Put the pan on the fire, and let it simmer till a sufficient gravy is left
8. Dish up tastefully, and serve
