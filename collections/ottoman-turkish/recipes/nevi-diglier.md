## Ingredients

- four or five pounds of loin of mutton
- six or seven onions
- salt
- pepper
- four or five vegetable marrows
- water
- juice of two or three lemons
- three or four teaspoonfuls of powdered sugar
- four or five cloves of garlic
- two or three tablespoonfuls of chopped mint

## Directions

1. Get four or five pounds of loin of mutton, remove the bones, and cut the meat in pieces the size of walnuts
2. Add six or seven onions sliced fine
3. Sprinkle sufficient salt and pepper over, mix them all together, and rub them well with your hands
4. Peel four or five vegetable marrows, and cut them in slices; take out the insides, if seeded
5. Lay the bones of the mutton on the bottom of a convenient stewpan; then some slices of vegetable marrow over, then some pieces of mutton, then vegetable marrow, and so forth, till you have laid the whole
6. Cover them with water, add the juice of two or three lemons and a little salt
7. Set it on the fire, and let it simmer gently until the meat and the vegetable marrows are tender
8. Add three or four teaspoonfuls of powdered sugar, four or five cloves of garlic, skinned and well pounded, and two or three tablespoonfuls of chopped mint
9. Let it simmer for five or six minutes longer, then dish it up, and serve
