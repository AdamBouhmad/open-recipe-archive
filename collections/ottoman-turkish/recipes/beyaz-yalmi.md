## Ingredients

- mutton from leg or other part
- salt
- pepper
- water
- half a pint of chick-peas
- two or three onions
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut as much as is wanted of a leg or any other part of mutton in pieces the size of walnuts or eggs
2. Wash and place them in a stewpan with a little salt and pepper
3. Cover them with water
4. Put it on a charcoal fire, and let it boil for about twenty minutes
5. Skim it well
6. Add half a pint of chick-peas previously scalded in water, and two or three onions sliced
7. Let it simmer until it forms a nice thick gravy at the bottom of the pan and the meat and peas are tender
8. Dish it up with two or three slices of toasted bread on the bottom of the dish
9. Serve whilst hot
