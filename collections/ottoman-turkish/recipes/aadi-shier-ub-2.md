## Ingredients

- three pounds of loaf sugar
- two pints of cold water
- white of an egg

## Directions

1. Put three pounds of loaf sugar in a clean stewpan, with two pints of cold water
2. Whip up the white of an egg, and add it to the sugar
3. Stir well with a wooden spoon, and set it to boil until it becomes a little thickish
4. Skim it well
5. Just before you take it off, add a few drops of the essence of orange-flower, or any other essence you choose
6. Pass it through a clean cloth
7. When cold, bottle and cork it up until wanted
