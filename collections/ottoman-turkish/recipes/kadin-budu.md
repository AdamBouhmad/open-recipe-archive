## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- two or three chopped onions
- handful or two of rice
- salt
- pepper
- four or five eggs
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Mince very fine as much as is required of a leg of mutton, add two or three chopped onions, with a handful or two of rice, well washed, sufficient salt and pepper
2. knead well, and make it into shapes, as the fleshy part of a leg of capon
3. throw them in boiling water
4. When nearly cooked, take them out, and let them remain till cold
5. beat up four or five eggs, dip the pieces of meat in it, and fry them in hot fresh butter till a nice colour
6. When done, take them out, and let them drain
7. Dish up tastefully and serve hot
