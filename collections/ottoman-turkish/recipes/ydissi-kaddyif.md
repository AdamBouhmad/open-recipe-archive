## Ingredients

- crumpets
- loaf sugar
- water
- eggs
- butter
- pistachios or almonds
- essence of rose or orange-flower

## Directions

1. Get six or seven crumpets
2. Put a pound of loaf sugar into a saucepan, with two pints of water
3. Set it to boil until it becomes a syrup
4. Keep it boiling gently by the side of the fire
5. Beat up five or six eggs in a basin
6. Dip the crumpets one after the other in the eggs
7. Fry them a nice brown on both sides in scalded fresh butter
8. Throw them in the syrup
9. Take them out instantly and dish them up
10. Sprinkle between each one some pounded pistachios or almonds
11. Put one or two drops of the essence of rose or orange-flower in a tea-cup of water
12. Pour it over the crumpets
13. Put it in the oven or on a slow charcoal fire for five minutes or so
14. Then serve
