## Ingredients

- botargo (fish-eggs)
- olive oil
- lemon juice
- pistachios or almonds

## Directions

1. Procure a pound of botargo and pound it well in a stone mortar
2. Add a teaspoonful of the best olive oil and the same of lemon-juice and pound it again
3. Add another teaspoonful of oil and lemon-juice and beat it up well
4. Continue in this way until you bring it to the consistency of a very thick cream
5. Pour it in a dish or plate and ornament the top with some skinned pistachios or almonds
6. Serve
