## Ingredients

- nine ounces of wheat-starch
- six pints of water
- two pounds and three quarters of loaf sugar
- half a pint of water
- three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter
- a quarter of a pound of skinned almonds

## Directions

1. Put nine ounces of wheat-starch in a basin with about six pints of water
2. Stir it until smooth and pass it through a fine sieve
3. Put in a stewpan two pounds and three quarters of loaf sugar with half a pint of water and stir until the sugar is dissolved
4. Add by degrees the starch and water and set it on the fire
5. Stir it gently until it gets very thick
6. Add one tablespoonful at a time, three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter, previously melted, and a quarter of a pound of skinned almonds
7. Keep stirring and taste it now and then
8. When it does not stick to the teeth, pour it into a convenient dish
9. When cold, cut it up in diamonds, dish up and serve
