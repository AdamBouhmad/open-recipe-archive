## Ingredients

- a pound of sultana raisins
- a pint of water
- loaf sugar
- a few drops of the essence of orange-flower or rose
- a few small pieces of ice

## Directions

1. Get a pound of sultana raisins, take the stalks off and well wash them
2. Put them in a stewpan with a pint of water, and set it to boil until they are swollen up
3. Take it off, and pass the liquor through a sieve into a saucepan
4. Add sufficient loaf sugar, and boil until it becomes thickish, then remove it
5. When cold, pour it in a glass bowl, and moderate the sweetness with cold water
6. Put the boiled raisins in, with a few drops of the essence of orange-flower or rose, and a few small pieces of ice, and serve
