## Ingredients

- three or four dozen of mussels
- half a pint of olive oil
- two or three onions chopped fine
- a pound of well-washed rice
- two or three ounces of pistachios, skinned
- an ounce of currants
- a little salt and pepper
- mixed spice
- a teacup of water

## Directions

1. Procure three or four dozen of mussels, well scrape the shells in water, wash them several times, and open them with a knife without separating one from the other
2. Put half a pint of olive oil in a saucepan, with two or three onions chopped fine, and set it on the fire
3. When hot, begin to stir with a wooden spoon until the onions are nicely browned
4. Add a pound of well-washed rice, two or three ounces of pistachios, skinned, and an ounce of currants, a little salt and pepper, and mixed spice, and stir till the rice begins to brown
5. Add a teacup of water, and keep stirring till the moisture is dispersed
6. Take it off and let it nearly cool
7. Stuff the mussels, and close the shells
8. Arrange them in a stewpan, nearly cover them with water, place a plate over, inside downwards, set the pan on the fire with the cover over, and let it boil until the whole of the liquid is absorbed
9. Dish up carefully, and serve hot or cold
