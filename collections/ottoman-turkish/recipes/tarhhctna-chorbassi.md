## Ingredients

- two pounds and a half of crumbs of French rolls
- three-quarters of a pound of curd
- broth
- bread
- fresh butter
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Put two pounds and a half of crumbs of French rolls in a stone mortar, with three-quarters of a pound of curd
2. Pound them well together, and let it remain for one night
3. Put it in a stewpan, and moisten it by degrees with as much broth as is required by bruising and beating it up smooth with a wooden spoon
4. Set it on a moderate fire, and let it simmer for about an hour
5. Take it off, and pour it into a tureen, and serve it with bread cut into small dice and fried in fresh butter
6. The addition of a small quantity of cream or milk will render it still more pleasant
