## Ingredients

- one pound of very fresh violet-leaves
- five pounds of white sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Procure a pound of very fresh violet-leaves.
2. Put them on a convenient dish or a clean board with five pounds of white sifted sugar.
3. Rub well together until the sugar becomes the same colour as the violet leaves.
4. Fill your preserve jars and cover them air tight.
5. Put them by until wanted.
