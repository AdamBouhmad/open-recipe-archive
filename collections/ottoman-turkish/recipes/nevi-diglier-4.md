## Ingredients

- nine ounces of wheat-starch
- water
- two pounds and three quarters of loaf sugar
- three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter
- a quarter of a pound of skinned almonds

## Directions

1. Put nine ounces of wheat-starch in a basin with about six pints of water
2. Stir it until smooth and pass it through a fine sieve
3. Put in a stewpan two pounds and three quarters of loaf sugar with half a pint of water and stir until the sugar is dissolved
4. Add by degrees the starch and water
5. Set it on the fire and stir it gently until it gets very thick
6. Add one tablespoonful at a time three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter previously melted
7. Add a quarter of a pound of skinned almonds and keep stirring
8. Taste it now and then
9. When it does not stick to the teeth pour it into a convenient dish
10. When cold, cut it up in diamonds
11. Dish up and serve
