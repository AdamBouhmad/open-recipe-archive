## Ingredients

- vermicelli
- butter
- clotted cream
- syrup

## Directions

1. Prepare four pounds of vermicelli as No. 149
2. Lay half of it in a buttered baking-tin, and the other half in another buttered tin
3. Pour in each about half a pound of scalded fresh butter
4. Bake them a delicate colour on both sides
5. When cooked, strain and wash out all the butter from both
6. Lay some clotted cream over one, half an inch thick
7. Cover it with the other one
8. Pour hot syrup all over as No. 149
9. Put it again in the oven until the syrup is nearly absorbed
10. Cut it in any shape you fancy, or turn it on to a dish as it is, and serve
