## Ingredients

- six or seven dozen young capsicums
- parsley
- mint
- strong wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Procure six or seven dozen young capsicums
2. Cut the stalks off and scald them
3. Place them in a wicker basket
4. Place a piece of clean board or a plate over them
5. Put an iron weight on the top
6. Let it remain for one night so the bitter water may drain from them
7. Place them in a stone jar with some parsley and mint between them
8. Cover them with strong wine vinegar
9. Cover the jar and let it remain in the pantry for ten or fifteen days before using
10. If you wish them to be kept for any length of time, change the vinegar occasionally
