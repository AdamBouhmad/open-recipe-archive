## Ingredients

- three pounds of fresh morella cherries
- a pound and a quarter of crushed loaf sugar
- two or three pints of water
- a few drops of the essence of orange-flower or rose
- three or four pieces of ice

## Directions

1. Pick out the large cherries and squeeze the juice from the small ones through a cloth into a saucepan
2. Add a pound and a quarter of crushed loaf sugar and boil it for one minute
3. Put the large cherries into a stewpan with two or three pints of water and set it to boil until the cherries are tender
4. Take it off and let it partly cool
5. Take the cherries out carefully, without bruising them
6. Put about two pints of the water in which the cherries are boiled in a glass bowl
7. Sweeten it with the syrup which you have prepared, according to palate
8. Add the cherries, with a few drops of the essence of orange-flower or rose, and three or four pieces of ice
9. Serve
