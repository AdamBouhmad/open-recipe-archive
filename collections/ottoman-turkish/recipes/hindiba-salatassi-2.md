## Ingredients

- two or three heads of endive
- salt
- pepper
- juice of a small lemon
- three tablespoonsful of olive oil

## Directions

1. Remove the dark green leaves from the endive heads
2. Pick off the other leaves one by one
3. Cut the large ones in two
4. Wash and drain them in a cloth by shaking it to and fro violently
5. Extract all the water
6. Put in the salad-bowl a small teaspoonful of salt, one or two pinches of pepper, the juice of a small lemon, three tablespoonsful of olive oil
7. Beat up until it becomes like cream
8. Put in the salad and turn it with spoon and fork till well mixed
9. Serve
