## Ingredients

- 1 pound fresh rose-leaves
- 2 pounds and a half white sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Procure a pound of fresh rose-leaves and cut the white ends off with a pair of scissors
2. Throw the white ends away
3. Put the other parts in a basin with 2 pounds and a half of white sifted sugar
4. Work it together with the hands until the rose-leaves are reduced to a pulp or a paste
5. Fill the preserve jars or glass bowls with cover
6. Arrange them on a tray and put it where the sun is powerful
7. When near sun-set, put the cover over and take them in
8. The next day uncover the jars and expose the preserve to the sun as before
9. Continue the same for a month or until the top of the preserve is nicely crystallized
