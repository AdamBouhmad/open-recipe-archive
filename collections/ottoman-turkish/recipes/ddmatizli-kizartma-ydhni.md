## Ingredients

- mutton
- fresh butter
- onions
- green tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- ripe tomatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Cut five or six pounds of the best part of mutton into pieces about the size of kidneys, wash and dry them in a cloth
2. Fry them a nice colour in fresh butter
3. Place them in a stewpan with three or four sliced onions fried in fresh butter, eight or ten green tomatoes each cut in four, and sufficient salt and pepper
4. Squeeze two or three pounds of ripe tomatoes in a basin, pass the juice through a cloth or sieve, and then pour it in the stewpan with sufficient water to cover the meat
5. Set it on a charcoal fire with the cover over, and let it simmer until the liquor is reduced to a nice thick gravy
6. Dish it up tastefully, and serve
