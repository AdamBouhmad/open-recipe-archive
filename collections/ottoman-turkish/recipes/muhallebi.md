## Ingredients

- about three pints of nice milk
- six or seven ounces of rice-flour
- milk or water
- three or four ounces of powdered sugar
- a few drops of the essence of orange-flower
- almond oil
- pieces of clotted cream

## Directions

1. Pour into a stewpan about three pints of nice milk, set it on the fire
2. As soon as it commences to boil, put six or seven ounces of rice-flour in a basin, which mix with milk or water, to form a smooth batter
3. Add it gradually to the milk, with three or four ounces of powdered sugar, and a few drops of the essence of orange-flower, and keep stirring it until it forms a smooth substance, but not too thick
4. Pour it into a mould previously oiled with almond oil; put it on ice, or in cold water
5. When firm, dip the mould in warm water, turn it out on to a dish, ornament the top with pieces of clotted cream, the size of nuts, and serve
