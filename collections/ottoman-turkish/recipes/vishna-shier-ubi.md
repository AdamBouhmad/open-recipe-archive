## Ingredients

- morella cherries
- sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Squeeze as many pounds of morella cherries as are wanted, and pass the juice through a cloth into a basin, and leave it for twenty-four hours
2. Strain it gently into another basin, and throw the residue away
3. Put the juice in a very clean stewpan, with double the quantity of sifted sugar
4. Stir till the sugar is dissolved, and set it to boil
5. As soon as it bubbles up take it off, and pass it through a sieve or cloth
6. When cold, bottle and cork it up until wanted
