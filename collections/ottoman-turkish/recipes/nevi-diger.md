## Ingredients

- raw beef or mutton
- two or three onions
- equal quantity of Pilaw
- three or four eggs
- salt
- pepper
- parsley chopped fine
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Mince a sufficient quantity of raw beef or mutton together with two or three onions
2. Pound it well in a mortar with an equal quantity of Pilaw
3. Break over it three or four eggs
4. Sprinkle sufficient salt and pepper, with a handful of parsley chopped fine
5. Knead well all together, and let it remain covered for half an hour
6. Divide it in pieces the size of an egg
7. Fry them in boiling fresh butter
8. When all are nicely browned, dish up tastefully, and serve hot
