## Ingredients

- four or five egg-plants
- two or three pounds of mutton
- one or two onions, chopped
- salt
- mutton or beef broth
- juice of two or three small lemons
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel four or five egg-plants, cut them in slices about three-quarters of an inch thick, and partly scald them in salt water
2. Cut two or three pounds of mutton in pieces the size of walnuts, sprinkle one or two chopped onions over with a little salt, and rub them well together with your hands
3. Place into a stewpan the pieces of meat and the egg-plants, in layers, cover them with mutton or beef broth, add the juice of two or three small lemons
4. Put the pan on the fire with the lid on, and let it simmer until it forms a nice gravy at the bottom
5. Dish it up, sprinkle a little cinnamon over, and serve
