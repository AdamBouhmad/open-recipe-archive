## Ingredients

- five or six chopped onions
- a little salt
- some water
- fresh butter
- three or four eggs
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan five or six chopped onions, a little salt, and some water, set it on the fire, and boil it for a few minutes
2. Drain the onions, and put them in a fryingpan with some fresh butter
3. Set the pan on the fire, and stew the onions until the moisture is dispersed
4. Beat up three or four eggs, and add it by degrees to the onions
5. Put it on the fire again, and stir it for three minutes or so with a wooden spoon
6. Take it off, and lay it between pieces of paste as No. 86, and bake it a nice colour
7. Dish up and serve
