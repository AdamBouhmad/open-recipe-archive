## Ingredients

- half a pound of fresh butter
- half a pound of wheat-starch
- a pint of water
- a pound of white sugar
- water
- rose-water
- powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put on the fire a stewpan with half a pound of fresh butter
2. When it is melted, take it off
3. Add half a pound of wheat-starch, which you have previously dissolved in a pint of water
4. Add a pound of white sugar, previously melted in a little water
5. Set it on the fire and keep stirring it
6. When it becomes crummy, stir it a few minutes longer
7. Take it off, dish up
8. Sprinkle a little rose-water over with a little powdered cinnamon and serve
