## Ingredients

- lettuces
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolks, hard boiled
- wine vinegar or lemon-juice
- olive oil
- chervil, chopped
- tarragon
- young onions
- beet-root

## Directions

1. Get two or three lettuces
2. Remove the faded and all the coarse green leaves, and cut the green top off
3. Pull the leaves off one by one, and cut them in three or four pieces, according to the size of each leaf
4. Well wash and drain them in a cloth
5. Put in a salad-bowl half a teaspoonful of salt, one or two pinches of pepper
6. Add the yolk of an egg or two, hard boiled, then bruise them to a pulp with the back of a wooden spoon
7. Add half a tablespoonful of the best wine vinegar or lemon-juice and stir it round
8. Pour a tablespoonful of olive oil over by degrees, and keep stirring
9. Add a spoonful more of vinegar, and two of oil, and beat them well up
10. When as smooth as cream, add the lettuce, with a tablespoonful of chopped chervil, and a teaspoonful of tarragon, one of young onions, and half-a-dozen slices or so of beet-root
11. Turn the salad gently till well mixed, and serve
