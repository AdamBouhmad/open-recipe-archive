## Ingredients

- raw mutton or beef
- two or three onions peeled
- salt
- pepper
- cummin
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Mince very fine as much as is wanted of either raw mutton or beef, with two or three onions peeled
2. sprinkle sufficient salt, pepper, and cummin
3. knead well and make it up in the shape of small buns
4. fry them in fresh butter, or broil them on a gridiron a nice brown
5. When done, arrange them in a dish, and serve hot, with chopped parsley mixed with thinly sliced onions round the dish
