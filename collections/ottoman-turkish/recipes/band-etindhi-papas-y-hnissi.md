## Ingredients

- five or six pounds of loin of veal
- onions
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- cummin
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut five or six pounds of a loin of veal in pieces the size of eggs
2. Place them in rather deep earthen stewpan, with three or four sliced onions, five or six heads of garlic, salt and pepper, a little cummin, and a teacup of wine vinegar, without water
3. Put the cover over the pan air tight, or paste it round with paper, so that no steam shall escape
4. Put it in an oven, or simmer it on a moderate fire for about three or four hours
5. When done, dish it up tastefully, and serve
