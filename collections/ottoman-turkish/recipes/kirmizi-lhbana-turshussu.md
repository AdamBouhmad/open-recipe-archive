## Ingredients

- two or three red cabbages
- salt
- peppercorns
- parsley
- mint
- thyme
- strong wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut two or three red cabbages into thin slices
2. Remove the hard stalks
3. Place them on a dish or board
4. Cover them with salt for ten or twelve hours
5. Turn them now and then
6. Clean off all the salt
7. Lay them in a stone jar with some peppercorns and a bunch of parsley mixed with some mint and thyme
8. Cover them with strong wine vinegar
9. Put the lid on the jar
10. Let it remain in a place for three or four weeks before using
