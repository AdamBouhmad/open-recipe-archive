## Ingredients

- nasturtium flowers
- chervil
- salt
- olive oil
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put a plate of flowers of the nasturtium in a salad-bowl with a tablespoonful of chopped chervil
2. Sprinkle over with your fingers half a teaspoonful of salt and two or three tablespoonfuls of olive oil and the juice of a lemon
3. Turn the salad in the bowl with a spoon and fork till well mixed and serve
