## Ingredients

- mutton or lamb
- raw onion-juice
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- cinnamon
- cummin

## Directions

1. Cut as much as is wanted of mutton or lamb in pieces the size of walnuts
2. Pour a small teacup of raw onion-juice over, mixed with sufficient salt and pepper
3. Rub it well in, and let them remain for about two hours
4. Pass them on the skewers one by one, place them in front of a moderate fire, and turn them continually
5. As soon as the meat begins to get brown, and the gravy drops, sprinkle a little flour over
6. When the meat is thoroughly done, sprinkle a little cinnamon and cummin over, and serve hot
