## Ingredients

- a pound and six ounces of fresh butter
- two pounds of the best flour
- sifted sugar
- a finely-grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put in a saucepan a pound and six ounces of fresh butter, and set it on the fire
2. When melted, mix in gradually two pounds of the best flour, and stir until the flour is done
3. Take it off, and while it is getting cold, beat it up till it becomes a smooth substance
4. Add by degrees as much sifted sugar as it will take, and a finely-grated nutmeg
5. Work it well with your hands, and make it into small cakes, any fanciful shape you please
6. Place them in a buttered tin, and bake a delicate colour in the oven
