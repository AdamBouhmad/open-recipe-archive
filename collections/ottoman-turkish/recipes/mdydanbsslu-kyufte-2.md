## Ingredients

- raw mutton
- salt
- pepper
- fresh butter
- two or three onions sliced thin
- one or two handfuls of chopped parsley
- two or three teacups of broth
- three or four tablespoonfuls of wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Mince very fine a sufficient quantity of raw mutton with a little salt and pepper
2. Make it into balls the size of nectarines, and fry them a nice brown in fresh butter
3. Partly fry in the same butter two or three onions, sliced thin
4. Add one or two handfuls of chopped parsley, and keep stirring for two or three minutes
5. Lay them on the bottom of a saucepan, and place the balls of meat over
6. Add two or three teacups of broth and three or four tablespoonfuls of wine vinegar
7. Set it on a slow charcoal fire, and let it simmer for twenty minutes or so, and serve
