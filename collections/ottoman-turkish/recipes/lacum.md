## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter
- one pound and six ounces of the best flour
- three-quarters of a pint of water
- about ten eggs
- jam or any other jam, clotted cream, or minced meat

## Directions

1. Put three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter in a saucepan, and set it on the fire
2. When hot, add one pound and six ounces of the best flour, and keep stirring till it becomes a light brown
3. Pour three-quarters of a pint of water over, and continue stirring until it becomes like a paste
4. Take it off and let it remain till cold
5. Add about ten eggs, and work it with your hands to form a softish paste
6. Divide it in round pieces the size of small peaches, hollow the centre of each with the point of your finger
7. Lay them in a buttered baking-tin, and bake them a nice delicate colour
8. When done, fill them with the jam, or any other jam, clotted cream, or minced meat, previously stewed brown in fresh butter
9. Dish them up tastefully on a white napkin, and serve
