## Ingredients

- one or two pounds of dried pears
- two or three pints of boiling water
- powdered sugar
- a few cloves
- a few small pieces of ice

## Directions

1. Wash one or two pounds of dried pears
2. Cut each one in four crossways and remove the cores
3. Put them in an earthen pan and pour two or three pints of boiling water over
4. Cover the pan and leave it for one night
5. Place them in a stewpan with the liquid
6. Add some powdered sugar with a few cloves according to palate
7. Set it to boil for a few minutes and take it off
8. Let it cool and pass the liquid into a glass bowl through a sieve
9. Put in the pears with a few small pieces of ice
10. Serve
