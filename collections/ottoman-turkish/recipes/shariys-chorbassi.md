## Ingredients

- three or four pints of broth
- hearts and livers of several chickens, half cooked
- a little salt
- five or six ounces of mixed vermicelli
- white pepper
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put three or four pints of broth in a stewpan with the hearts and the livers of several chickens, half cooked and cut into small dice, and a little salt
2. Put it on the fire
3. When boiling, add five or six ounces of mixed vermicelli, and let it boil till tender
4. Remove the pan, and pour the contents in a tureen
5. Sprinkle a little white pepper and cinnamon over, and serve
