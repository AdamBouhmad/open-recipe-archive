## Ingredients

- raw leg of mutton
- three or four onions
- rice flour
- salt
- pepper
- parsley chopped very fine
- juice of one or two lemons
- teacup of broth

## Directions

1. Mince very fine as much as is wanted of a raw leg of mutton
2. Pound three or four onions in a mortar, and squeeze the juice over the meat
3. Knead it well with a handful of rice flour, sufficient salt and pepper
4. Make it into balls the size of small peaches
5. Roll them in parsley chopped very fine, till they are well covered
6. Arrange them in a saucepan, add the juice of one or two lemons, with a teacup of broth
7. Set it on a moderate charcoal fire, with the cover over
8. When the liquor is nearly absorbed, add some more broth, and let it simmer till nicely cooked
9. Dish it up tastefully, and serve hot
