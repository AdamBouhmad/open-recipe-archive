## Ingredients

- a pound of fresh butter
- a pound and a half of flour
- two pints of boiling syrup
- honey or loaf sugar
- water
- a pint of boiling milk
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Put a pound of fresh butter in a very clean stewpan
2. Put it on a charcoal fire and scald it
3. Take it off and when it gets a little cold, add by degrees a pound and a half of the best flour
4. Set it again on the fire and keep stirring until the flour becomes a gold colour
5. Take it off and immediately pour two pints of boiling syrup over, which you have previously prepared of honey or loaf sugar and water
6. Pour a pint of boiling milk over
7. Put the cover over and let it remain for five minutes
8. Stir it well with a wooden fork
9. Dish up in tablespoonfuls and let it bear the impression of the spoon
10. Shake some white sugar over and serve
