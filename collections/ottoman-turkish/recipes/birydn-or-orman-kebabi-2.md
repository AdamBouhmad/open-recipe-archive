## Ingredients

- a whole sheep or a lamb, cleaned
- onion-juice
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Rub the sheep or lamb all over with onion-juice, sufficient salt and pepper
2. Pass it on a pole sufficiently long
3. Wrap the sheep up in sheets of paper
4. Make a large wood or charcoal fire
5. Thrust in the ground at each end of the fire, half a yard distant, a stake with fangs sufficiently large and strong for the pole to rest on
6. Place the pole on them and keep turning it gently
7. Put under the sheep a dripping-pan to catch the gravy
8. Baste the sheep with a bunch of feathers or a piece of clean linen tied at the top of a stick
9. When half done, remove the papers and turn it quickly
10. When nicely browned all over, take out the pole
11. Sprinkle some cinnamon over and serve
