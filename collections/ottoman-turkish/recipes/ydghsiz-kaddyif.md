## Ingredients

- crumpets
- milk
- powdered sugar
- clotted cream

## Directions

1. Lay four or five crumpets in a rather deep baking-tin
2. Pour over them half a pint of milk
3. Sprinkle powdered sugar over
4. Lay a layer of clotted cream
5. Lay an equal number of crumpets over
6. Pour half a pint more milk, then sugar
7. Put it on a charcoal fire and let it boil for a little while
8. Take it off just before the cream is melted
9. When rather cold, dish them up carefully
10. Shake some powdered sugar over and serve
