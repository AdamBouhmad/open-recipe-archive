## Ingredients

- two or three chickens
- salt
- chicken livers, hearts, and gizzards
- fresh butter
- three-quarters of a pound of well-washed rice
- two or three tablespoonfuls of pistachios
- a tablespoonful of currants
- pepper
- cinnamon
- a small teacup of hot water
- broth

## Directions

1. Get two or three chickens, well clean them
2. Sprinkle a little salt over, and let them remain for ten minutes or so
3. Chop up the livers, hearts, and the gizzards, very fine
4. Place them in a frying-pan with sufficient fresh butter
5. Add three-quarters of a pound of well-washed rice, two or three tablespoonfuls of pistachios, a tablespoonful of currants, sufficient salt and pepper, and a little cinnamon
6. Stir round for a few minutes
7. Add a small teacup of hot water, and keep stirring till the water is absorbed and the rice partly soft
8. Stuff the chickens with it, sew them up, and fry them a nice brown in fresh butter
9. Place them in a stewpan, nearly cover them with broth
10. Put the pan on a charcoal fire with the cover over, and let them simmer till the chickens are quite tender
11. Dish up, and serve
