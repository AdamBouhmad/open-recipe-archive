## Ingredients

- leg or rump of beef
- vinegar
- pepper-corns
- orange-peel
- garlic
- fresh butter
- tomatoes
- water
- salt
- macaroni
- broth
- cheese

## Directions

1. Get in one piece five or six pounds of the leg or rump of beef
2. Cover it with vinegar, and let it remain four and twenty hours
3. Wash it, and make deep holes all over
4. Fill the holes with pepper-corns, orange-peel, and cloves of garlic peeled
5. Place it in a stewpan with three-quarters of a pound of fresh butter
6. Set it on a moderate charcoal fire for three or four hours, turning it now and then
7. When nicely browned, add the juice of two or three pounds of tomatoes with two or three pints of water, and sufficient salt and pepper
8. Let it simmer till the meat is nicely cooked
9. Take it out, and keep it hot
10. Put in the stewpan about two pounds and a half of the best macaroni
11. If the liquor is not enough, add some of the broth, and boil till the macaroni is tender
12. Let it be rather firm to the touch
13. Dish it up, sprinkle some finely-grated cheese over, then place the beef in the centre of the macaroni, and serve
