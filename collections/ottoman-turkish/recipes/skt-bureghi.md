## Ingredients

- three pints of milk
- five or six eggs
- six ounces of flour
- salt
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Pour three pints of milk in a basin
2. Add five or six eggs, six ounces of flour, and a small teaspoonful of salt
3. Beat up well together
4. Well butter a convenient baking-tin, pour in the preparation, and bake it a nice brown
5. When done, cut it in diamonds, and serve
