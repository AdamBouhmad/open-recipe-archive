## Ingredients

- three or four pounds of mutton
- salt
- pepper
- three or four ounces of fresh butter
- three or four egg-plants

## Directions

1. Cut three or four pounds of mutton into small pieces, wash and dry them in a cloth, then place them in a stewpan with sufficient salt and pepper
2. Set it on the fire, and stir with a wooden spoon until it begins to hiss, which indicates that all the moisture is dispersed
3. Add three or four ounces of fresh butter, and keep stirring till the meat is nicely browned
4. When done, take them out with a hand-strainer, and place them in a dish; keep it hot
5. Cut three or four egg-plants in thin slices, sprinkle them with salt, and let them remain for a few minutes
6. When the bitter juice is drawn from them, fry them a nice colour on both sides in the remaining fat from the meat, then arrange them tastefully over the meat, or round the dish, sprinkle a little pepper over, and serve hot
