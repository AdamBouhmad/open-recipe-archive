## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of sweet almonds
- three drachms of bitter almonds
- three-quarters of a pint of water
- another three-quarters of a pint of water
- two pounds and three-quarters of powdered loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Scald three-quarters of a pound of sweet almonds, and three drachms of bitter almonds
2. Skin and pound them in a stone mortar until in pulp
3. Mix with three-quarters of a pint of water, and pass it through a close muslin into a basin
4. Put the residue in the mortar and pound it again, which mix with another three-quarters of a pint of water, pass it again through the muslin into the basin
5. Put in a stewpan two pounds and three-quarters of powdered loaf sugar, add the liquor
6. Stir well with a wooden spoon, set it on a charcoal fire, and let it boil for one minute
7. Take it off, and pass it through the muslin again, bottle, and cork it up till wanted
