## Ingredients

- a pound of fresh butter
- a pound of loaf sugar
- water
- half a pound of wheat-starch

## Directions

1. Put a pound of fresh butter in a stewpan with a pound of loaf sugar previously dissolved in a small quantity of water
2. Set it on a charcoal fire
3. When boiling, add by degrees half a pound of wheat-starch which you have previously made a batter with a pint of water
4. Keep stirring and tasting it now and then
5. When it does not stick to the teeth, it is done
6. Dish up and serve
