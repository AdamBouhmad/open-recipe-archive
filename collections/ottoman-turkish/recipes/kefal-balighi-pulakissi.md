## Ingredients

- half a dozen very fresh grey mullets
- salt
- an ounce of fresh butter
- half a teacup of olive oil
- three-quarters of a pound of sliced onions
- water
- a handful of chopped parsley
- cayenne pepper
- the juice of one or two lemons

## Directions

1. Take out the gills and the insides of half a dozen very fresh grey mullets
2. Cut them in two crossways
3. Wash and sprinkle them with a little salt and let them remain for an hour
4. Put in a stewpan an ounce of fresh butter and half a teacup of olive oil
5. Add a little salt and three-quarters of a pound of sliced onions
6. Put it on the fire and let the onions stew but not brown
7. Lay the fish over and cover it with water
8. Let simmer for twenty or thirty minutes
9. When nearly done, add a handful of chopped parsley and a pinch or two of cayenne pepper
10. Add the juice of one or two lemons and let it simmer for four or five minutes longer
11. Dish it up and serve hot
