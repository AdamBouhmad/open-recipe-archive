## Ingredients

- pound of flour
- salt
- eggs
- water
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Put on the dresser a pound of flour, make a hole in the centre, in which put a teaspoonful of salt
2. Break in two eggs, mix all together
3. Add sufficient water, and form it a softish flexible paste
4. Divide it in two, and roll them out to the size of the baking-tin you intend to use
5. Sprinkle some melted fresh butter over, and fold over the edges of the paste so as to hide the butter
6. Throw a little flour both under and over, and roll them out again as before
7. Repeat the same five or six times more
8. Butter the baking-tin, in which lay one of the two pieces of paste
9. Lay any preparation you choose all over it, half an inch thick, and cover it with the other paste
10. Make about fifteen deep holes in the paste here and there with the point of a knife
11. Bake it a nice brown on both sides
12. When done, cut it in diamonds, dish up tastefully, and serve hot
