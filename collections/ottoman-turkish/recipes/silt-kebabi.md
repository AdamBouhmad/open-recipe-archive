## Ingredients

- leg or loin of mutton or lamb
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut a sufficient quantity of a leg or loin of mutton or lamb in pieces the size of a small egg
2. Sprinkle with your fingers sufficient salt, pepper, and cinnamon over, and let them remain for an hour or two
3. Place them in a stewpan, and cover them with milk
4. Set it on the fire until the milk begins to boil, then take it off the fire, skim it well, and take the pieces of meat out
5. When cold, pass them on skewers one by one, put them before a moderate fire, and turn them gently
6. Get ready some fresh milk, and keep it hot by the side of the fire
7. As soon as the meat commences to brown, baste it now and then with the hot milk, with a quill
8. When the gravy drops freely, sprinkle a small quantity of flour over the meat
9. Continue the same till it is nicely cooked
10. Dish it up tastefully, sprinkle a little cinnamon over, and serve hot
