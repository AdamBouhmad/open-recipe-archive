## Ingredients

- paste
- any of the ingredients used in preceding pastry
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Place on a paste-board three or four layers of paste, one over the other
2. Put over in small lots any of the ingredients used in the preceding pastry, about an inch and a half distance from each other
3. Wet slightly round them with a paste-brush, and cover them with the same number of pastes as before, but a little larger
4. Press over each lot with a Turkish coffee-cup, or a mould the size of an egg-cup, and cut them out, which will then be in the shape of the above cup
5. When all done, either fry them in hot butter, or arrange them in a baking-tin, previously buttered
6. Sprinkle a little butter over them with a paste-brush, and bake them a nice colour in a moderate oven
7. When done, dish them up on a white napkin, and serve hot
