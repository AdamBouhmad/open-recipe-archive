## Ingredients

- leg of lamb or mutton
- fresh butter
- onions
- chick-peas

## Directions

1. Cut as much as is required of a leg of lamb or mutton in pieces
2. Boil as in Beyaz Ydhmi
3. Add three or four finely-sliced onions, fried in fresh butter
4. Add half or three-quarters of a pint of chick-peas previously scalded in water
5. Cook and serve as Beyaz Ydhmi
