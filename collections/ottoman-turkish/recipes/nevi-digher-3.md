## Ingredients

- vermicelli
- clotted cream
- ground rice
- butter
- syrup

## Directions

1. Make three or four pounds of vermicelli as No. 149
2. Lay half of it in a buttered baking-tin
3. Put in a basin three-quarters of a pound of clotted cream, and work it with six or seven ounces of ground rice
4. Lay it all over the vermicelli
5. Cover it with the other half of vermicelli
6. Pour in a little scalded butter
7. Bake it a nice brown on both sides
8. Pour some syrup over as above and serve
