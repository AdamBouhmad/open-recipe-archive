## Ingredients

- four or five pounds of swordfish
- salt
- flour
- egg
- olive oil
- one or two handfuls of picked parsley
- five or six cloves of garlic
- Cayenne pepper
- the juice of two or three lemons
- two teacups of water

## Directions

1. Procure four or five pounds of swordfish in one piece
2. Cut it in slices, wash them, and sprinkle a little salt over
3. Dip them in flour and then in egg
4. Partly fry them in olive oil
5. Spread one or two handfuls of picked parsley on the bottom of a stewpan with five or six cloves of garlic
6. Lay the slices of fish over
7. Spread the same quantity of parsley and garlic on the top
8. Add sufficient salt and two or three pinches of Cayenne pepper
9. Add the juice of two or three lemons and two teacups of water
10. Put the pan over a charcoal fire with the lid on and let it simmer gently for twenty minutes or until the liquor is nearly absorbed
11. Dish it up and serve
