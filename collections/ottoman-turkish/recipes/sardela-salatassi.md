## Ingredients

- sardines
- vinegar
- fresh vinegar
- parsley, chopped
- onions, chopped
- olives
- salt
- wine vinegar or lemon juice
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Put as many sardines as are wanted in a basin, with some vinegar, and let them remain in it for half an hour
2. Scrape off the scales gently and wash them in fresh vinegar
3. Arrange them round the edge of a dish
4. Put in the centre two or three handfuls of chopped parsley, two or three tablespoonfuls of chopped onions, and one or two dozens of olives, all mixed together
5. Put half a teaspoonful of salt in a basin, with two or three tablespoonfuls of the best wine vinegar, or the juice of two lemons, and about half a teacup of the best olive oil
6. Beat up well with a wooden fork or spoon until it becomes like cream
7. Pour it over the sardines and the parsley, and serve
