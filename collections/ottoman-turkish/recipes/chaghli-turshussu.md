## Ingredients

- unripe fruit such as peaches, nectarines, almonds, apricots, cherries, apples, plums, pears
- very strong wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Gather any unripe fruit that may have fallen from the trees, such as peaches, nectarines, almonds, apricots, cherries, apples, plums, pears
2. Mix all together
3. Place them in bottles or in a stone jar
4. Cover them with very strong wine vinegar
5. Tie up the mouths of the bottles with a piece of leather
6. Let them remain for thirty or forty days before using
