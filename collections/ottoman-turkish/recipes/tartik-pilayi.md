## Ingredients

- two or three dozen of small scallops
- six or seven ounces of fresh butter
- two or three finely-chopped onions
- a pound of the best rice, well washed
- a little salt and pepper
- half a teacup of the best olive-oil
- water

## Directions

1. Procure two or three dozen of small scallops, scrape and well wash them, and open the shells without separating one from the other
2. Put six or seven ounces of fresh butter in a pan, with two or three finely-chopped onions
3. Set it on the fire, and fry them partly brown
4. Add a pound of the best rice, well washed, a little salt and pepper, and stir it with a wooden spoon for ten minutes or so
5. Stuff the scallops with the rice, and place them carefully in a convenient saucepan, with half a teacup of the best olive-oil
6. Cover them with water, put a plate over upside downwards, put the lid on, and let it simmer on a moderate fire until there is no liquid remaining
7. Arrange them in a dish, and serve, hot or cold
