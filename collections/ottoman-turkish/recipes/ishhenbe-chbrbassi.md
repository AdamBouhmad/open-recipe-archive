## Ingredients

- three pounds of well-cleaned and uncooked bullock's tripe
- two pints of water
- salt
- broth
- yolks of two or three eggs

## Directions

1. Get three pounds of well-cleaned and uncooked bullock's tripe
2. Put it into a stewpan with two pints of water, sufficient salt
3. Put it on the fire
4. When boiling, skim it well, and let it simmer until tender
5. Take it off and skim off the fat
6. Take out the tripe, and chop it up very fine
7. Put it again in the pan with what liquor is left, which you have previously passed through a sieve
8. Add one or two pints of broth, set it again on the fire, and let it simmer for ten minutes or so
9. Add the yolks of two or three eggs prepared as in the Terbiyeli Jigher Chbrbassi recipe, and serve
