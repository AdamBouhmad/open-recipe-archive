## Ingredients

- salmon in one piece
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- bay-leaf
- lemon

## Directions

1. Remove the bones and cut the salmon in pieces the size of an egg
2. Sprinkle sufficient salt and pepper over, with a handful or two of parsley chopped fine
3. Let them remain for about an hour
4. Pass them on skewers with a bay-leaf between each piece
5. Put them before a moderate fire and turn them until the fish is nicely browned
6. Dish it up tastefully, with some parsley and one or two lemons cut in quarters round it
7. Serve hot
