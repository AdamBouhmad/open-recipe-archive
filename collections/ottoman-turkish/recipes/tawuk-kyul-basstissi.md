## Ingredients

- fowls
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- broth

## Directions

1. Split one or two nice fowls lengthways, and remove the whole of the bones with a sharp knife
2. Cut the flesh in four or five pieces as you fancy, and beat the thicker parts even with a rolling pin
3. Sprinkle them with sufficient salt and pepper, and a little cinnamon, and let them remain for one or two hours
4. Place them on a gridiron, set it on a moderate charcoal fire, and constantly turn them with a pair of tongs
5. When nicely browned, lay them in a stewpan, and cover them with broth
6. Put the pan on a moderate charcoal fire, and let simmer until tender
7. Dish up, and serve hot
