## Ingredients

- two or three chickens
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- raw onion-juice
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Clean two or three chickens
2. Split each one with a sharp knife, and remove the whole of the bones
3. Cut the flesh in small pieces, but without separating one piece from another
4. Sprinkle them with sufficient salt, pepper, and cinnamon
5. Rub them in with half a teacup of raw onion-juice, and let them remain for an hour
6. Pass them on skewers, and place them before a moderate fire
7. Turn them continually, and when the chickens begin to brown, baste them with fresh butter with a quill, until they are nicely cooked
8. Dish up, and serve hot
