## Ingredients

- two pounds of very fresh cream-cheese
- ounce of flour
- sifted sugar or honey

## Directions

1. Get two pounds of very fresh cream-cheese, break it in small pieces into a stewpan, put it on the fire, and stir until it is melted
2. Add an ounce of flour, and keep stirring until the butter begins to run from the cheese
3. Take it off, dish up, and serve with some sifted sugar or honey over
