## Ingredients

- any fish
- salt
- a teacupful of olive oil
- four or five onions
- a handful or two of chopped parsley
- half a teacupful of wine vinegar
- one teacupful of water
- three or four tomatoes
- preserved tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Get any fish you fancy, scale and clean it well
2. If a large one, cut it in three or four pieces
3. Sprinkle some salt over and let them remain for a little while
4. Put a teacupful of olive oil in a frying-pan with four or five sliced onions and a handful or two of chopped parsley
5. Put it on the fire and stew it rather brown
6. Lay half of it on the bottom of a rather deep baking-dish and place the fish over
7. Put the other half of the onions and parsley on the top with the oil that is left in the frying-pan
8. Add half a teacupful of wine vinegar and one of water, three or four tomatoes cut in four crossways, or one or two tablespoonfuls of preserved tomato sauce if the tomato is not in season
9. Put it in the oven for twenty minutes or so
10. When nicely baked, dish it up and serve
