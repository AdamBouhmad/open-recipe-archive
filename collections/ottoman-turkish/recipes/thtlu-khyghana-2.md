## Ingredients

- five eggs
- a tablespoonful of milk
- a dessertspoonful of powdered sugar
- a little salt
- three or four ounces of fresh butter
- powdered sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Break five eggs in a basin, and beat them up
2. Add a tablespoonful of milk, a dessertspoonful of powdered sugar, a little salt, and beat them well up
3. Put three or four ounces of fresh butter in the frying-pan, pour in the eggs, and fry a nice colour
4. Serve with powdered sugar over
