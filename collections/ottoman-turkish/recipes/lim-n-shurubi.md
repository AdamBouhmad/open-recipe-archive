## Ingredients

- three pounds of powdered sugar
- white of an egg
- two pints of water
- three-quarters of a pint of lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Put three pounds of powdered sugar in a stewpan, with the white of an egg well beaten up, and two pints of water
2. Stir well and set it on the fire
3. When boiling, skim it well, and as soon as it begins to thicken add three-quarters of a pint of lemon-juice, and let it boil for half a minute longer
4. Take it off
5. When nearly cold, pass it through a clean cloth, bottle, and cork it up till wanted
