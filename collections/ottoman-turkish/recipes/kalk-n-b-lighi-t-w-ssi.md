## Ingredients

- turbot
- salt
- five or six lemons
- three-quarters of a pint of salt water
- half an ounce of saffron
- oil or fresh butter

## Directions

1. Get a turbot as large as is required, cut the fins off, wash and sprinkle it with sufficient salt.
2. Cut in slices five or six lemons, lay half of them on the bottom of a convenient dish, place the fish over, and cover it with the other half of the lemons.
3. Pour on it three-quarters of a pint of salt water coloured with half an ounce of saffron.
4. Place a plate or dish over, so as to press the fish slightly, and let it remain for one night.
5. The next day fry it a nice brown either in oil or fresh butter, and serve.
