## Ingredients

- a teacup and a half of nice olive-oil
- two pints of water
- a little salt
- a pound of well-washed rice
- any fish
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put a teacup and a half of nice olive-oil in a stewpan, and set it on the fire
2. As soon as it begins to bubble, pour into it two pints of water, with a little salt
3. When boiling, add a pound of well-washed rice
4. Lay over any fish you choose, previously well cleaned and seasoned with salt
5. Put the cover over the pan, and let it simmer until the liquor is absorbed
6. Take it off, and place it on the hob for fifteen minutes or so
7. Dish up the rice, and place the fish over tastefully, sprinkle a little cinnamon over, and serve hot
