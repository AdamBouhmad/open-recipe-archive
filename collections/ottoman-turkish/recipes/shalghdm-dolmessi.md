## Ingredients

- half a dozen middle-sized turnips
- raw mutton
- two onions
- chopped parsley
- a handful of well-cleaned rice
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- two or three tablespoonfuls of wine vinegar
- one tablespoonful of honey or powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Peel half a dozen middle-sized turnips, and hollow them out
2. Mince sufficient raw mutton, add two finely chopped onions, a little chopped parsley, a handful of well-cleaned rice, and a little salt and pepper
3. Mix well all together with the hands
4. Stuff the turnips with it, and place the lids over
5. Place them in a stewpan, and nearly cover them with broth
6. Add a little salt
7. Put the pan on the fire with the cover over, and let it simmer until the turnips are nearly soft
8. Add two or three tablespoonfuls of wine vinegar, and one of honey or powdered sugar
9. When sufficient gravy is left, and the turnips are quite done, dish up tastefully, and serve
