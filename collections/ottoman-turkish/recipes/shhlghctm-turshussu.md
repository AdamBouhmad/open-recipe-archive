## Ingredients

- turnips
- powdered mustard-seeds
- sugar
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut turnips in four crosswise
2. Boil them in plenty of water till nearly tender
3. Take out the turnips into a colander
4. Put a moderate quantity of powdered mustard-seeds in the water and let it remain for two or three hours
5. Pass it through a cloth or a sieve into a basin
6. Sweeten it just sufficient to taste the sugar
7. Place the turnips in a stone jar
8. Half cover them with the sweetened water and half with nice wine vinegar
9. Put the cover over the jar and let it remain a few days before using
