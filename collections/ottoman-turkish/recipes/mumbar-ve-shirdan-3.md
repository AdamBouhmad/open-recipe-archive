## Ingredients

- ventricle or intestine of sheep
- raw mutton
- one or two teacupfuls of rice, well washed
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- water
- fresh butter
- egg

## Directions

1. Get some ventricle or intestine of sheep well cleaned and ready for use
2. Mince a sufficient quantity of raw mutton
3. Add one or two teacupfuls of rice, well washed, sufficient salt and pepper, a little cinnamon, and mix them well together with a little water, to form it like a paste
4. Tie one end of the ventricle and stuff it with the minced meat through a funnel
5. Tie the other end and prick it all over with a needle
6. Nearly fill a saucepan with water and sufficient salt, and place it on the fire
7. When boiling, throw in the stuffed ventricle
8. When partly cooked, take it out and place it on a trencher
9. Twist it round and round to form it like a twisted bun
10. When cold, fry it a nice colour in hot fresh butter, and serve
11. If approved of, cut it in thin slices, dip them in egg, and then fry them in butter, and serve
