## Ingredients

- ten or twelve pounds of black or white grapes
- mustard-seeds

## Directions

1. Pick out the best bunches of grapes
2. Squeeze the juice from the bruised and small ones
3. Pass it through a sieve into a stewpan free from smell
4. Set it to boil for a few minutes
5. Wash the bunches you have selected
6. Lay two or three of them on the bottom of a stone jar
7. Sprinkle some mustard-seeds over, then grapes, again mustard-seeds over, then grapes, continuing so till the whole of the grapes are laid
8. Pour the boiled grape-juice over
9. Cover the jar air-tight and let it remain for thirty or forty days before using
