## Ingredients

- twenty or thirty cakes
- rose-water
- pounded almonds or pistachios
- egg
- hot fresh butter
- boiling syrup

## Directions

1. Prepare twenty or thirty cakes as No. 186, and moisten them by sprinkling rose-water over
2. Fold them in two separately
3. Put on each one some pounded almonds or pistachios
4. Fold them in square or triangular shape
5. Dip them in egg, and fry them a delicate colour on both sides in hot fresh butter
6. Drain them, and then throw them in boiling syrup
7. When sufficiently soaked, take them out
