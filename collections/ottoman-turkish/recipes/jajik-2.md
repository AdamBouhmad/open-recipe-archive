## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- garlic
- curd
- ice water

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in thin small dice one or two small cucumbers
2. Sprinkle them with sufficient salt and toss them up
3. Pound two or three cloves of garlic and put them in a bowl
4. Add one or two pints of the best curd and beat it up with water until you bring it to the thickness of thin cream
5. Put the cucumbers in with a few pieces of ice
6. Stir it and serve
