## Ingredients

- six or seven egg plants
- water
- salt
- three or four onions
- fresh butter
- four or five tablespoonfuls of fried minced meat
- raw mutton
- parsley
- pepper
- two or three eggs

## Directions

1. Procure six or seven egg plants, cut them in two lengthways, take out the insides, and boil them a little in water, moderately salt
2. take them out, and put them in a basin, add three or four finely chopped onions, previously fried a nice brown in fresh butter, four or five tablespoonfuls of fried minced meat (prepared of raw mutton), a handful of parsley, chopped fine, sufficient salt and pepper, and two or three eggs well beaten up, and mix them well together
3. butter a baking-pan, in which arrange half of the egg-plants, and stuff them with the above mixture
4. cover them with the remaining half, and bake them in a rather hot oven
5. When the egg-plants are shrunk and soft, they are cooked
6. Dish up, and serve
