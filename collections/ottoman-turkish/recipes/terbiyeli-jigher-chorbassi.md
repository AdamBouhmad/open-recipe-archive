## Ingredients

- lamb's or sheep's lights
- salt
- water
- broth
- yolks of two or three eggs
- juice of one or two lemons or wine vinegar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Get a lamb's or sheep's lights and cut it into small dice
2. Put them in a stewpan with a little salt, and a small quantity of water
3. Put it on the fire until the lights are nearly cooked
4. Add three or four pints of broth, and let it simmer till the pieces of lights are quite done
5. Beat up the yolks of two or three eggs in a small saucepan, with the juice of one or two lemons, or with a little wine vinegar
6. Stir it on a slow fire with a wooden spoon till a little thick
7. Take it off and pour it gradually into the soup, stirring it quickly at the same time, to prevent the eggs from curdling
8. Pour the soup into a tureen, sprinkle a little cinnamon over, and serve
