## Ingredients

- whole sheep or lamb
- onion-juice
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Get a whole sheep or a lamb, already cleaned, rub it all over with onion-juice, sufficient salt and pepper
2. Pass it on a pole sufficiently long, and wrap the sheep up in sheets of paper
3. Make a large wood or charcoal fire, thrust in the ground at each end of the fire, half a yard distant, a stake, with fangs sufficiently large and strong for the pole to rest on with the sheep
4. Place the pole on them, and keep turning it gently
5. Put under the sheep a dripping-pan to catch the gravy, and baste the sheep with a bunch of feathers, or a piece of clean linen tied at the top of a stick
6. When half done, remove the papers and turn it quickly
7. When nicely browned all over, take out the pole, dish it up, sprinkle some cinnamon over, and serve
