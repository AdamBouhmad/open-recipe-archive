## Ingredients

- grey mullet or other fish
- olive-oil
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- wine vinegar
- broth

## Directions

1. Get some grey mullet, or any other fish you fancy, clean it well, then scald it
2. Rub a gridiron with a little olive-oil, and place the fish over
3. Set it on a moderate charcoal fire, and turn it now and then until both sides are nearly browned
4. Take it off; then lay one or two handfuls of chopped parsley on the bottom of a baking dish or tin, and lay the fish over with a little salt and pepper
5. Add half a teacup of wine vinegar, and two of broth
6. Put it on a charcoal fire until the broth is absorbed, then dish it up, and serve hot
