## Ingredients

- one pound and a quarter of the best fresh butter
- twelve eggs
- one pound and a quarter of powdered sugar
- two pounds and two ounces of sago
- three quarters of a pound of loaf sugar
- seven pints of water

## Directions

1. Put in a basin a pound and a quarter of the best fresh butter, and work it well with clean hands until it is like a cream
2. Break in twelve eggs, and beat up well together
3. Mix in by degrees one pound and a quarter of powdered sugar
4. While beating up, add gradually two pounds and two ounces of sago, and keep beating it up for a few minutes longer
5. Butter a rather deep baking-tin, in which pour the preparation
6. Bake it a nice colour in a hot oven
7. Dissolve three quarters of a pound of loaf sugar in seven pints of water, and keep it lukewarm
8. When the pudding is nicely browned, pour gradually some of the sherbet or sugar and water all over with a ladle
9. Put it again in the oven until the sherbet is absorbed, and continue in this manner until you have used the whole of the sherbet
10. Cut it up in diamonds, dish up tastefully, shake some powdered sugar over, and serve
