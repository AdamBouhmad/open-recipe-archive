## Ingredients

- French beans
- salt
- flour
- four or five eggs
- four or five tablespoonfuls of fried minced meat
- pepper
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Scald sufficient quantity of French beans with a little salt
2. when partly tender, put them on a board, and chop them up
3. place them in a basin, sprinkle some flour over, and rub them smooth with your hands
4. add four or five eggs, four or five tablespoonfuls of fried minced meat, a little salt and pepper, and beat them up well with a fork
5. put some fresh butter into the fryingpan, set it on the fire until hot
6. fry the mixture a tablespoonful at a time
7. When all are fried a nice colour, dish them up tastefully, and serve hot
