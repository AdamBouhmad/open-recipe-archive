## Ingredients

- six or seven dozen young capsicums
- parsley
- mint
- strong wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Procure six or seven dozen young capsicums
2. Cut the stalks off
3. Scald them
4. Place them in a wicker basket
5. Place a piece of clean board or a plate over them
6. Put an iron weight on the top
7. Let it remain for one night, so as the bitter water may drain from them
8. Place them in a stone jar
9. Put some parsley and mint between them
10. Cover them with strong wine vinegar
11. Cover the jar
12. Let it remain in the pantry for ten or fifteen days before using
13. If you wish them to be kept for any length of time, change the vinegar occasionally
