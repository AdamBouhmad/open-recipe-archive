## Ingredients

- two or three well-cleaned fowls
- sufficient salt
- one or two onions
- half a stick of celery
- five or six pints of water

## Directions

1. Put the fowls in a stewpan with sufficient salt, one or two onions cut cross-wise, half a stick of celery, and five or six pints of water
2. Put the pan on the fire
3. As soon as boiling commences, skim it well
4. Let it simmer till the fowls are quite tender
5. Pass the broth through a sieve
