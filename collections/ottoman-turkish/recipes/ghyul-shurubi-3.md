## Ingredients

- one pound and six ounces of fresh-gathered rose-leaves
- one pint and a half of water
- two pounds and three-quarters of sifted sugar
- the white of an egg well beaten up

## Directions

1. Pour a pint and a half of water in a very clean stewpan and put it on the fire.
2. When boiling, put the rose-leaves in, and as soon as it boils up again take it off.
3. Put the cover over and leave it till cold.
4. Pass it through a sieve into the stewpan and cast the residue away.
5. Add two pounds and three-quarters of sifted sugar and the white of an egg well beaten up.
6. Put the pan on the fire.
7. When it boils up, skim and take it off.
8. Pass it through a clean cloth, bottle, and cork it up till required.
