## Ingredients

- seven or eight red mullets
- two or three onions
- mixed spice
- salt
- pepper
- juice of one or two lemons
- yolks of two or three eggs
- water
- a quarter of a pint of olive oil
- chopped parsley
- half a pint of water
- half a teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Take out the gills and the insides of seven or eight red mullets, and wash them
2. Peel two or three onions, and well pound them in a mortar
3. Add a little mixed spice, salt, and pepper, the juice of one or two lemons, the yolks of two or three eggs, and a little water, and beat them up well together with a wooden spoon
4. Pour it over the mullets, and let them remain for about two hours
5. Put a quarter of a pint of olive oil in a flat stewpan, with a plate of chopped parsley
6. Put it on the fire, and just scald it
7. Add half a pint of water, half a teaspoonful of salt
8. When it boils, lay the mullets in with the ingredients, and let it simmer for fifteen or twenty minutes
9. Dish up tastefully, and serve hot
