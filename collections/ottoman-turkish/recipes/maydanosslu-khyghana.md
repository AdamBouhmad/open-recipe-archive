## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- teaspoonful of finely chopped onions or spring onions
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Proceed exactly as No. 194
2. Add a tablespoonful of chopped parsley, a teaspoonful of finely chopped onions, or spring onions, sufficient salt and pepper
3. Fry as above, and serve
