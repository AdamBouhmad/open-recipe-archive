## Ingredients

- half a calf's head or four of the feet
- sufficient water
- salt
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Get half a calf's head, or four of the feet, wash, and put them in a stewpan with sufficient water and salt
2. Set it on the fire, and let it boil until tender
3. Remove the meat from the bones, and cut it in small dice
4. Prepare ten or twelve pieces of paste as No. 78, but instead of scalding them in water, scald them in boiling fresh butter
5. When all done, take out and place them in a colander, and when free from fat, lay half of them in a rather deep and circular baking-tin one over the other, sprinkling between each piece some of the liquor in which the calf's head is boiled
6. Lay the pieces of calf's head all over, cover them with the remaining pieces of paste the same way as before, and bake it a nice colour
7. Dish up, and serve
