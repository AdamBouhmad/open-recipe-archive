## Ingredients

- pieces of paste (No. 135)
- ground rice
- milk
- white of one or two eggs
- cream cheese or powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare some pieces of paste as No. 135, and lay half of them in a baking-tin
2. Boil a sufficient quantity of ground rice with milk till reduced to a thick pulp
3. When cold, put it in a basin with the white of one or two eggs, and work it with your hands for five or ten minutes, or until you bring it to the thickness of clotted cream
4. Lay it all over the paste, and cover it with the remaining pieces of paste
5. Cut it up in diamonds, and bake it as above
6. Dish up tastefully, sprinkle sugar over, and serve
