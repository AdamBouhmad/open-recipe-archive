## Ingredients

- cabbage-lettuces
- beet-root
- eggs
- chervil
- tarragon
- chives or young onions
- salt
- pepper
- olive oil
- wine vinegar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Procure two or three cabbage-lettuces and remove the outer leaves and throw them away
2. Pull the others one by one then wash and well drain them in a cloth
3. Put them in the bowl with a few slices of beet-root and one or two hard-boiled eggs cut in quarters
4. Add two teaspoonfuls of chopped chervil, one of tarragon, and one of chives or young onions
5. Sprinkle over with your fingers half a teaspoonful of salt and one or two pinches of pepper
6. Sprinkle two or three tablespoonfuls of the best olive oil and one and-a-half of wine vinegar or the juice of a lemon
7. Turn the salad in the bowl with a spoon and fork until well mixed and serve
