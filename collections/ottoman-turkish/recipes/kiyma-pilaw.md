## Ingredients

- about a pound of nice mutton
- a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
- a pound of the best rice, well washed
- two pints of hot water
- salt
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Chop up about a pound of nice mutton
2. Put it in a saucepan with a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
3. Set it on the fire, and let the meat stew a nice brown
4. Add a pound of the best rice, well washed, two pints of hot water, sufficient salt, and a pinch or two of cinnamon
5. Let it boil slowly till there is no liquid remaining
6. Take it off and place it on the hob with the cover over for twenty or thirty minutes
7. Dish it up in a dome shape, and serve hot
