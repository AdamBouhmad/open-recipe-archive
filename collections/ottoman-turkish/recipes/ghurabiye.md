## Ingredients

- two pounds and a half of the best fresh butter, melted
- two pounds and a half of powdered sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Put in a basin or pan two pounds and a half of the best fresh butter, melted, with two pounds and a half of powdered sugar
2. Stir it well
3. Add gradually as much flour as it will take
4. Knead it well, and make it into small cakes
5. Place them in a buttered tin, and bake a nice colour in the oven
