## Ingredients

- hare
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- water
- wine vinegar
- garlic
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Cut a hare in pieces about the size of kidneys, wash them well, and let them remain for one or two hours, or until thoroughly cleansed from blood
2. Place them in a stewpan with five or six onions sliced thin, and sufficient salt and pepper
3. Cover them with water, set it on a charcoal fire till the hare is half cooked
4. Strain the liquor into a basin, and take out the onions
5. Put the liquor in a stewpan with a teacup and a half of wine vinegar, and eight or ten cloves of garlic peeled
6. Put it on the fire, and let it boil until the liquor is reduced to half
7. Fry the onions in fresh butter, and put them over the hare with the liquor
8. Set it on the fire, and let it simmer until a nice thick gravy is formed at the bottom
9. Dish it up, and serve
