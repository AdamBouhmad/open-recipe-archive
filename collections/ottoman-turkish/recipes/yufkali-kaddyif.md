## Ingredients

- puff-paste
- vermicelli
- butter
- syrup

## Directions

1. Roll out very thin six pieces of puff-paste
2. Lay three of them on the bottom of a well-buttered baking-tin, one over the other
3. Lay some vermicelli (No. 149) over, about half or three-quarters of an inch thick
4. Pour five or six tablespoonfuls of scalded fresh butter all over
5. Cover it with the other three pieces of paste
6. Prick it here and there with a carving-fork
7. Bake it a nice brown on both sides
8. When done pour some syrup over as No. 149
9. Let it remain covered for ten minutes or so
10. Cut it in diamonds, or any other fanciful shape you please
11. Dish up tastefully and serve
