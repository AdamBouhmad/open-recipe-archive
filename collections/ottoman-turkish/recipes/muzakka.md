## Ingredients

- one or two pounds of raw mutton or lamb
- two or three onions
- two or three ounces of fresh butter
- salt
- two or three tablespoonfuls of chopped parsley
- three or four egg-plants
- salt water
- a pint of broth

## Directions

1. Mince very fine one or two pounds of raw mutton or lamb
2. Put it in a frying-pan with two or three finely chopped onions, two or three ounces of fresh butter, and a little salt
3. Set it on the fire and let the meat and the onions stew a nice brown, stirring it now and then
4. Two minutes before taking it off, add two or three tablespoonfuls of chopped parsley and stir it well
5. Cut three or four egg-plants in slices lengthways and place them in salt water for ten or fifteen minutes
6. Place the minced meat and the egg-plants in layers into a pan
7. Add a pint of broth or a little more
8. Put the pan on a moderate charcoal fire with the cover over and let it simmer until the liquor is nearly absorbed and the egg-plants are nicely cooked
9. Turn it on to a dish without spoiling the shape and serve
