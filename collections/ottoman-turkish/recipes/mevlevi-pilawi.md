## Ingredients

- two quarts of water
- sufficient salt
- a pound of rice, well washed
- half a pound of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of water in a saucepan with sufficient salt, put it on the fire.
2. When boiling, throw into it a pound of rice, well washed, and boil until each grain is soft and separate.
3. Drain it through a clean cloth or in a colander, put it back in the pan, and set it on a slow fire without the cover until the moisture is dispersed.
4. Put a saucepan on the fire with half a pound of fresh butter.
5. When it boils, pour it over the rice and stir it once round with a fork, put the pan on the hob with the cover over, and let it remain for five or ten minutes.
6. Dish up, and serve hot.
