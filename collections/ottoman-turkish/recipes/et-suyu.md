## Ingredients

- five or six pounds of shin of beef or knuckle of veal
- sufficient salt
- three or four onions
- a small stick of celery
- three or four quarts of water

## Directions

1. Wash the meat well
2. Put it in a stewpan with sufficient salt, three or four onions cut in four cross-wise, a small stick of celery, and three or four quarts of water
3. Set the pan on the fire
4. When boiling, skim it well
5. Let it simmer until the meat separates from the bones
6. Pass the broth through a sieve
