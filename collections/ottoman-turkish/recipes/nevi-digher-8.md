## Ingredients

- a hare
- loin of mutton
- three chopped onions
- a handful of parsley chopped fine
- salt
- pepper
- thin caul-fat

## Directions

1. Cut a hare into pieces
2. Mix with as many pieces of loin of mutton as there are of hare, all of equal size
3. Sprinkle three chopped onions over, with a handful of parsley chopped fine, sufficient salt and pepper
4. Rub the hare and mutton with these, and let them remain covered with a cloth for one or two hours
5. Pass them on skewers, first mutton, then hare, and so on until the whole of them are on skewers
6. If handy, wrap them up with some thin caul-fat
7. Put them before the fire, and turn them gently
8. As soon as the gravy begins to drop, sprinkle a little flour over
9. When the meat is nicely cooked, dish it up, pour the gravy over, and serve
