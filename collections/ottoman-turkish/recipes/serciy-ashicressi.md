## Ingredients

- skinned wheat
- water
- two or three ounces of washed currants
- a quarter or half a pound of loaf sugar
- a little musk
- two or three tablespoonfuls of rose-water
- skinned almonds
- pistachios

## Directions

1. Put in a stewpan as much skinned wheat as is required, with plenty of water.
2. Set it on the fire, and boil until the grains begin to crack.
3. Take out with a wooden spoon what pulp there is on the top, and put it in a basin.
4. Add some more water, and continue so until you have extracted all the goodness from the grains.
5. Strain the remaining grains through a sieve, and put all the pulp you have collected into a saucepan.
6. Add two or three ounces of washed currants, and a quarter or half a pound of loaf sugar, previously made into syrup.
7. Put it on a charcoal fire and begin stirring it.
8. When boiling, add a little musk mixed with two or three tablespoonfuls of rose-water, and stir it one or two minutes longer.
9. Take it off and pour it into a dish.
10. Arrange on the top tastefully some skinned almonds and pistachios, and serve when cold.
