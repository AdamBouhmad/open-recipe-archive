## Ingredients

- a dozen gherkins
- a dozen vegetable marrows the size of your finger
- a dozen melons the size of walnuts
- a dozen water-melons
- a dozen pumpkins or gourds
- a dozen and a half button onions
- best wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Procure a dozen gherkins, the same of vegetable marrows the size of your finger, ditto of melons the size of walnuts, ditto of water-melons, ditto of pumpkins or gourds, and a dozen and a half of button onions
2. Scald and drain them
3. Prick each one here and there with a wooden needle
4. Arrange them in a stone jar
5. Cover them with the best wine vinegar
6. Let it remain covered for ten or fifteen days before using
7. If it is to be kept for any length of time, change the vinegar every five days
