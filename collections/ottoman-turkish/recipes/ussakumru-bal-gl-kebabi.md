## Ingredients

- fresh mackerel
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- fennel
- bay-leaf

## Directions

1. Get as many fresh mackerel as are required, cut off the point of the head
2. If large, divide each one in three pieces; if small, in two pieces
3. Wash and dry them in a cloth
4. Sprinkle them with sufficient salt and pepper, and a handful or two of chopped parsley mixed with fennel, and let them remain for an hour
5. Put them on skewers, with a bay-leaf between each piece, and cook them before a moderate fire, turning them until nicely browned
