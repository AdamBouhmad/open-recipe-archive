## Ingredients

- botargo (fish-eggs)
- olive oil
- lemon-juice
- pistachios or almonds, skinned

## Directions

1. Procure a pound of botargo
2. Pound it well in a stone mortar
3. Add a teaspoonful of the best olive oil and the same of lemon-juice, and pound it again
4. Add another teaspoonful of oil and lemon-juice, and beat it up well
5. Continue in this way until you bring it to the consistency of a very thick cream
6. Pour it in a dish or plate
7. Ornament the top with some skinned pistachios or almonds, and serve
