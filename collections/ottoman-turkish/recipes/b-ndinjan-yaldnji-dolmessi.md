## Ingredients

- six or seven egg-plants
- salt
- a teacupful of olive oil
- half a pound of onions
- a pound of well-washed rice
- pepper
- a tablespoonful of finely-chopped mint
- a teacup of water

## Directions

1. Prepare six or seven egg-plants
2. Sprinkle some salt over the insides of the egg-plants
3. After a few minutes squeeze them well with your hands, so as to be free from bitter water, and chop them up
4. Put a teacupful of olive oil in a saucepan, place it on the fire
5. When hot, put in half a pound of onions chopped fine, and stir till they begin to brown
6. Add the insides of the egg-plants, and stir for three minutes or so
7. Add a pound of well-washed rice, sufficient salt and pepper, a tablespoonful of finely-chopped mint, and a teacup of water
8. Keep stirring until the moisture is absorbed
9. Stuff the egg-plants moderately with it, put the lids over
10. Prick them here and there with a fork
11. Place them in a saucepan, and nearly cover them with water
12. Set it on the fire with the cover over, and let it boil gently until the liquor is absorbed
13. Dish up, and serve hot or cold
