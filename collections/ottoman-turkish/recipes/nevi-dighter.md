## Ingredients

- 3 pounds quinces
- 4 quarts of water
- 3 pounds powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cut the quinces as above
2. Put them in a stewpan with 4 quarts of water
3. Set the pan on the fire and let it boil until the fruit becomes like a jelly
4. Take it off and pass it through a cloth or a sieve into another pan
5. Add 3 pounds of powdered sugar
6. Set it to boil again till it becomes a thick syrup
7. Take it off and strain it again
8. Try a little on a plate before potting to see if it sets
9. When nearly cold, fill your preserve jars and cover them when cold
