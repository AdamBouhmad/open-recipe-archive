## Ingredients

- crumpets
- butter
- powdered sugar
- pounded pistachios or almonds
- rose-water

## Directions

1. Get as many crumpets as are wanted
2. Fry them a nice colour in scalded fresh butter
3. Whilst hot, arrange them in a dish
4. Sprinkle between them powdered sugar, with pounded pistachios or almonds, and rose-water
5. Let them remain covered for a few minutes and serve
