## Ingredients

- a sufficient quantity of spinach
- fresh butter
- four or five very thin round pieces of puff-paste
- three or four eggs
- half a pound of nice cheese, grated

## Directions

1. Well clean a sufficient quantity of spinach, pick off the stalks, boil it for one or two minutes, let it drain, and chop it up very fine
2. Well butter a rather deep and circular baking-tin, in which lay four or five very thin round pieces of puff-paste, one over the other
3. Sprinkle a little fresh butter over with a paste-brush, and lay the spinach all over
4. Break into a basin three or four eggs, to which add half a pound of nice cheese, grated; beat up well together, and lay it over the spinach
5. Put it in a rather hot oven, and bake it a nice colour
6. When done, cut it in four crossways, and serve hot
