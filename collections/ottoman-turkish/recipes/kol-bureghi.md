## Ingredients

- paste
- eggs
- broth
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Lay on the paste-board separately as many pieces of paste as are required
2. Beat up two or three eggs in a basin, with three or four tablespoonfuls of broth
3. Dip a paste brush in it, rub it all over each piece of the paste, and fold them separately in two
4. Lay on each one lengthways any preparation you fancy, fold the two side edges over, and then the ends, thus making it an oblong shape
5. Make the others in the same shape, and fry them a nice brown on both sides in hot fresh butter
6. When all done, let them drain, then dish up on a white napkin, and serve hot
