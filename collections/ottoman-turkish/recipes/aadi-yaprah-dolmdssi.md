## Ingredients

- vine leaves
- raw mutton
- one or two onions
- three or four tablespoonfuls of rice
- salt
- pepper
- water
- broth

## Directions

1. Prepare some vine leaves as No. 111
2. Chop up fine a sufficient quantity of raw mutton with one or two onions
3. Add three or four tablespoonfuls of rice well washed, a little salt and pepper
4. Mix them well together with a little water to make it like a paste
5. Lay the vine leaves open and put in each one a teaspoonful of this stuffing
6. Wrap them up about the length and the thickness of your thumb
7. Squeeze them gently with your hand one by one so that they may retain the stuffing while cooking
8. Place them in a saucepan without any water and set it on the fire with the cover over
9. When their own moisture is exhausted, add some of the broth
10. Let it simmer until it forms a nice gravy at the bottom
11. Take one out and try if the meat and the rice are done
12. If not, add some more broth
13. When the rice and the meat are quite tender, dish up and serve
