## Ingredients

- Seven or eight rather unripe peaches
- Half a pound of loaf sugar
- A pint of water

## Directions

1. Peel seven or eight rather unripe peaches
2. Divide each one in two and remove the stones and the red parts where the stones have been
3. Arrange them in a stewpan
4. Put in a saucepan half a pound of loaf sugar with a pint of water
5. Set it to boil until it becomes a syrup
6. Pour it over the peaches
7. Put the pan on the fire till they are tender
8. Place them on a dish tastefully
9. Pour the syrup over and serve
