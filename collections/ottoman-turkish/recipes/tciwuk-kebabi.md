## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- flour
- paper

## Directions

1. Clean a chicken, or as many as are required
2. Sprinkle sufficient salt, pepper, and cinnamon over, and let it remain for one or two hours
3. Put it on the spit, and place it before the fire
4. When the gravy begins to drop, sprinkle a little flour over, and continue the same till the chicken is just upon being done
5. Wrap it up with some thin paper, and place it again before the fire for twenty minutes or so
6. Take the paper off, and serve hot
