## Ingredients

- three or four mackerel
- salt
- olive oil
- parsley
- onions
- lemon

## Directions

1. Clean three or four mackerel, cut off the points of the heads
2. Sprinkle them with sufficient salt, and let them remain for about an hour
3. Rub a gridiron with some olive oil, lay the mackerel over
4. Broil gently on a charcoal fire
5. When done, dish it up
6. Place some chopped parsley mixed with finely-sliced onions over
7. Serve hot, with a lemon squeezed over the fish
