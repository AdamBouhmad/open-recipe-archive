## Ingredients

- a pound and six ounces of fresh-gathered rose-leaves
- a pint and a half of water
- two pounds and three-quarters of sifted sugar
- white of an egg

## Directions

1. Procure a pound and six ounces of fresh-gathered rose-leaves
2. Pour in a very clean stewpan a pint and a half of water, put it on the fire
3. When boiling, put the rose-leaves in, and as soon as it boils up again take it off
4. Put the cover over, and leave it till cold
5. Pass it through a sieve into the saucepan, and cast the residue away
6. Add two pounds and three-quarters of sifted sugar and the white of an egg well beaten up
7. Put the pan on the fire
8. When it boils up, skim and take it off
9. Then pass it through a clean cloth, bottle, and cork it up till required
