## Ingredients

- three or four pomegranates
- four ounces of loaf sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Cut open three or four pomegranates, take the grains out
2. Pick up the large ones, put the small ones in a cloth, and squeeze all the juice from them into a saucepan
3. Add four ounces of loaf sugar, with the same quantity of water as the juice, and stir on a slow fire until it becomes thickish
4. Remove it and let it cool
5. Proceed as with the orange sherbet
