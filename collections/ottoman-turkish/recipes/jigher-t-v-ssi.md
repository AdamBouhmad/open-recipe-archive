## Ingredients

- sheep's or lamb's liver
- salt
- flour
- hot butter
- parsley
- onions

## Directions

1. Get a sheep's or lamb's liver, wash, and cut it into large dice
2. Sprinkle a little salt over
3. Dip them in flour, and fry them a nice brown in hot butter
4. When done, take them out and let them drain
5. Dish up, sprinkle a handful or two of chopped parsley mixed with finely-sliced onions over, and serve very hot
