## Ingredients

- three or four onions
- fresh butter
- water
- salt
- tablespoonful of vinegar
- six or seven eggs
- pepper
- powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel and slice fine three or four onions, and fry them a nice brown in fresh butter
2. Lay them on the bottom of a dish, and keep it hot
3. Pour in a pan a pint of water, add a little salt, and a tablespoonful of vinegar, put it on the fire
4. When boiling, break carefully in the pan six or seven eggs, simmer till the white is firm
5. Take them out with a slice, and arrange them over the onions
6. Pour a little hot fresh butter over them, sprinkle a little pepper and powdered cinnamon over, and serve whilst hot
