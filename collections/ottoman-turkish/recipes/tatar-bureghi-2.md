## Ingredients

- paste
- fried minced mutton or beef
- water
- scalded butter
- nice curd
- a pinch of pepper

## Directions

1. Make some paste as No. 83
2. Roll out as many pieces as are wanted to the thickness of a sixpence
3. Cut them in checks or square pieces, about two inches long and two broad
4. Lay half a teaspoonful of fried minced mutton or beef on one corner of each piece, and fold the other half over, thus forming it of a triangular shape
5. Press the two edges with your fingers to make the paste stick
6. When the whole is ready, put on the fire a saucepan nearly full of water
7. When boiling, throw in the pieces of paste, and let them boil until nicely cooked
8. Drain them, and dish them up
9. Sprinkle a little scalded butter over, with some nice curd, and a pinch of pepper, and serve hot
