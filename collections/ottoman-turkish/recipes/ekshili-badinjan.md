## Ingredients

- four or five egg-plants
- salt
- olive oil or fresh butter
- four or five onions
- half a teacup of wine vinegar
- one teacup of water
- two or three teaspoonfuls of honey or sugar

## Directions

1. Cut four or five egg-plants in rather thin slices lengthways
2. Sprinkle them with salt and let them remain until the bitter juice is drawn from them
3. Fry them a nice colour in olive oil or fresh butter and lay them in a pan
4. Fry a nice brown four or five sliced onions in fresh butter or olive oil with a little salt and lay them over the egg-plants
5. Add half a teacup of wine vinegar, one of water, and two or three teaspoonfuls of honey or sugar
6. Set the pan on a moderate fire until the liquor is nearly absorbed
7. Dish them up carefully and serve
