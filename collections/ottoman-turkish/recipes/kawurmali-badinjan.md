## Ingredients

- mutton
- egg-plants
- salt
- pepper
- half a pint of broth
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut mutton in pieces and fry them
2. Bake three or four good-sized egg-plants in an oven
3. When soft, peel them
4. Lay them in a pan, over which place the pieces of meat with the fat that is left from it in the stewpan
5. Add half a pint of broth
6. Put the pan on a slow charcoal fire with the cover over and let it simmer for twenty minutes
7. Dish up carefully, sprinkle a little cinnamon and pepper over, and serve while hot
