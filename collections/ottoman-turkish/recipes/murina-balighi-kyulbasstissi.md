## Ingredients

- small cod-fish slices
- salt
- flour
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Cut from a small cod-fish as many slices as are required
2. Rub them with a little salt, and dip them in flour
3. Rub a gridiron with olive oil, lay the fish over
4. Set it on a moderate charcoal fire, and turn the fish often
5. When nicely browned on both sides, take it off
6. Cover the bottom of a dish with some finely-chopped parsley and onions
7. Place the fish over, with one or two lemons cut in quarters round the dish
8. Serve hot
