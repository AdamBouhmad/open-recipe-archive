## Ingredients

- two pints of water
- a pound of the best rice, well washed
- sufficient salt
- half a pound of fresh butter
- a wet cloth

## Directions

1. Pour two pints of water in a saucepan, and put it on the fire
2. When boiling, throw in a pound of the best rice, well washed, and sufficient salt, and boil it until the whole of the liquid is absorbed
3. Put in a saucepan half a pound of fresh butter, and set it on the fire until it boils
4. Pour it all at once over the rice, put the cover over the pan, wrap a wet cloth round it, to prevent the steam from escaping, and keep it on the hob for half an hour
5. Take the lid off and stir it once round with a fork
6. Dish up in the shape of a dome, and serve hot
