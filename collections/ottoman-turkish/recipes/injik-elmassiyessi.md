## Ingredients

- three or four calf's feet
- six or seven pints of water
- a pound and a quarter of powdered sugar
- a pint and three-quarters of water
- the juice of three or four lemons
- the rind of one lemon, with the pith taken off
- the whites and shells of five or six eggs
- a few drops of the essence of rose or orange-flower
- a few white and black grapes
- strawberries and cherries

## Directions

1. Procure three or four calf's feet, wash and place them in a stew-pan, with six or seven pints of water, and put it on the fire
2. When boiling, skim and simmer until the flesh comes off the bones; then pass it through a clean cloth or a sieve into a basin, and let it remain until quite hard
3. Remove all the fat, &c., from the top
4. Put in a saucepan a pound and a quarter of powdered sugar, a pint and three-quarters of water, the juice of three or four lemons, the rind of one, with the pith taken off, the whites and shells of five or six eggs, and stir with a wooden spoon until the sugar is dissolved
5. Add the jelly, set it on the fire, and stir well until it boils; then pass it through a jelly-bag till quite clear
6. Add a few drops of the essence of rose or orange-flower, and then put it in a mould, with a few white and black grapes, the same of strawberries and cherries, &c.
7. Place it on ice till quite firm, turn it out, and serve
