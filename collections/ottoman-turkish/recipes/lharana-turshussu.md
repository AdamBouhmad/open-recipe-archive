## Ingredients

- three or four nice white-heart cabbages
- yeast
- half a dozen of ripe capsicums
- brine

## Directions

1. Cut the stalks off
2. Remove the outer leaves of three or four nice white-heart cabbages
3. Cut them in four crosswise
4. Put a little yeast on the bottom of a convenient stone jar
5. Place the cabbages over
6. Add half a dozen of ripe capsicums, chopped fine
7. Cover them with brine
8. Put the lid over the jar
9. Place it in the pantry
10. Pour the brine in the jar to and fro every five days
11. When the cabbage is turned acid, it is then fit for use
