## Ingredients

- vermicelli
- half a pound of scalded fresh butter
- hot syrup

## Directions

1. Prepare as much vermicelli as is required
2. Twist it like a rope, twice the thickness of your thumb, and make it into cakes the size of a Bath bun
3. Lay them in a buttered baking-tin, pour in about half a pound of scalded fresh butter, and bake it on both sides a nice brown
4. When done, pour some hot syrup all over, and let it remain for ten minutes or so
5. Dish up, and serve hot or cold
