## Ingredients

- nice mutton
- sufficient salt
- water
- sufficient flour
- yolks of a few eggs
- sufficient wine vinegar or lemon-juice

## Directions

1. Cut some nice mutton into pieces the size of small walnuts
2. Wash and put them in a stewpan with sufficient salt and as much water as the quantity of soup required
3. Put the pan on the fire
4. When boiling, skim it well
5. Let it simmer until the pieces of meat are tender and skim off the fat
6. Put in a basin sufficient flour, which mix with water to form a smooth batter, but not too thick
7. Add it to the broth by degrees, stirring at the same time, so as to prevent it from getting lumpy
8. Let it simmer till the smell of flour is dispersed
9. Take it off the fire
10. Beat up the yolks of a few eggs in a small saucepan with sufficient wine vinegar or lemon-juice
11. Set it on a slow charcoal
