## Ingredients

- mutton or lamb neck
- salt
- pepper
- sweet marjoram
- parsley
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut from the neck of mutton or lamb as many cutlets as are required
2. Remove the bone at the thick end and beat them out with a rolling pin or a small chopper
3. Sprinkle with your fingers sufficient salt and pepper, and a small quantity of sweet marjoram
4. Rub them well in the chops
5. Place them on a gridiron, set it on a moderate charcoal fire, turn them frequently with a pair of tongs
6. When nicely cooked, serve whilst hot
7. If desired, place a handful or two of parsley together with an onion, chopped fine, over the cutlets
