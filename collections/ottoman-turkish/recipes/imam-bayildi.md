## Ingredients

- half a dozen egg-plants
- salt
- five or six onions
- olive oil or fresh butter
- half a dozen peeled cloves of garlic
- a pint of water

## Directions

1. Procure half a dozen egg-plants and cut each one in four lengthways without separating from the stalk
2. Sprinkle them with salt and let them remain for five or ten minutes
3. Peel and cut five or six onions in rather thick slices
4. Fry them partly brown in olive oil or fresh butter
5. Lay half of them on the bottom of a stewpan, over which place the egg-plants and lay the other half of the onions over them
6. Add half a dozen peeled cloves of garlic and a pint of water or little more
7. Put the pan on the fire and let it simmer till the egg-plants are soft
8. Dish them up carefully and serve hot or cold
