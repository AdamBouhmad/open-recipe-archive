## Ingredients

- raw mutton
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- onion juice
- a teacup of water
- a quarter of a pound of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Mince very fine as much as is required of the best part of raw mutton
2. Sprinkle a little salt, pepper, and cinnamon over
3. Knead well with a little onion juice, and make it into balls the size of walnuts
4. Put in a bowl or basin a teacup of water, and a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
5. Arrange the balls of meat in the bowl, and place it into an iron pot
6. Put the cover over and paste it round, or wrap a wet cloth round it, so as to prevent the steam from escaping
7. Set it on a moderate fire, and let it simmer for about two hours
8. Dish it up tastefully, and serve hot
