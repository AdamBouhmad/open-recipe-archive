## Ingredients

- two quarts of water
- sufficient salt
- a pound of rice, well washed

## Directions

1. Put two quarts of water in a saucepan with sufficient salt, put it on the fire
2. When boiling, throw into it a pound of rice, well washed, and boil until each grain is soft and separate
3. Drain it through a clean cloth or in a colander, put it back in the pan, and set it on a slow fire without the cover
