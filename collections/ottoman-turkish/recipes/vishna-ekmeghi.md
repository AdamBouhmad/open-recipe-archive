## Ingredients

- a pound of loaf sugar
- a pint of water
- two pounds of morella cherries
- two French penny rolls
- yolks of two eggs
- eight tablespoonfuls of milk
- hot fresh butter

## Directions

1. Put a pound of loaf sugar into a stewpan with a pint of water, put it on the fire
2. When boiling, skim it, then add two pounds of morella cherries with the stalks taken off and the stones taken out
3. Stir with a wooden spoon, and boil them until getting tender
4. Cut two French penny rolls lengthwise in eight slices
5. Put the yolks of two eggs in a basin, with eight tablespoonfuls of milk, and beat it up well
6. Dip quickly each slice in it, and fry it a nice colour on both sides in hot fresh butter
7. When all done, lay them in a convenient pie-dish, then put the cherries over with the syrup
