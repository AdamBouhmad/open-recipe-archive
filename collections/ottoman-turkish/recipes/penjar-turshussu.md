## Ingredients

- four or five beetroots
- garlic
- salt
- powdered sugar
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the stalks off and well wash four or five beetroots
2. Boil them till tender
3. Peel and cut them into rather thin slices
4. Arrange them in a jar or glass bowl with half a pint of the water in which they are boiled
5. Pound one or two cloves of garlic in a stone mortar with a little salt
6. Add an ounce of powdered sugar and half a pint of nice wine vinegar
7. Stir well and pour it over the beetroots
8. Ready for use
