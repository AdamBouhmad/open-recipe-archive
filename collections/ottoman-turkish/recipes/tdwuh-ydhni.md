## Ingredients

- fowl
- mutton
- water
- salt
- fresh butter
- onions
- chick-peas
- wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut a well-cleaned fowl in two lengthways
2. Place it in a stewpan with two or three pounds of the middle of a neck of mutton, cover them with water, put in a little salt, set it on the fire, and let it boil till the fowl is half cooked, and skim it well
3. Take the fowl out and fry it a nice colour in fresh butter
4. Place it in a stewpan with six or eight sliced onions fried in butter, a pint and a quarter of chick-peas, previously boiled a little soft and skinned, two or three tablespoonfuls of wine vinegar, and the liquor in which the fowl is boiled, add sufficient salt
