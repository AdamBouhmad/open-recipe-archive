## Ingredients

- a pound of sultana raisins
- a pint of water
- sufficient loaf sugar
- a few drops of the essence of orange-flower or rose
- a few small pieces of ice

## Directions

1. Get a pound of sultana raisins and take the stalks off
2. Well wash them and put them in a stewpan with a pint of water
3. Set it to boil until they are swollen up
4. Take it off and pass the liquor through a sieve into a saucepan
5. Add sufficient loaf sugar and boil until it becomes thickish
6. Remove it and let it cool
7. Pour it in a glass bowl and moderate the sweetness with cold water
8. Put the boiled raisins in with a few drops of the essence of orange-flower or rose and a few small pieces of ice
9. Serve
