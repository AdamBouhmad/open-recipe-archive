## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- onions
- fresh butter
- skinned pistachios
- currants
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- caul-fat

## Directions

1. Mince three or four pounds of a leg of mutton, or any other part of raw mutton
2. Put three chopped onions in a stewpan with some fresh butter, and partly fry them
3. Add the minced mutton with half a handful of skinned pistachios, the same of currants, a little salt, pepper, and cinnamon
4. Stir round with a spoon until the minced meat is also partly fried
5. Cut a caul-fat in pieces about the size of vine leaves
6. Divide the minced meat in each piece, and wrap it up to the shape of a small cup, or any shape you may fancy
7. Lay them on a baking tin
8. Place it in a hot oven until nicely browned
9. Dish up tastefully, and serve
