## Ingredients

- half a pound of rice
- three pints of water
- one wine-glass of rose-water
- powdered sugar
- powdered almonds or walnuts

## Directions

1. Pound, rather coarse, half a pound of rice in a mortar
2. Put three pints of water in a stewpan with a wine-glass of rose-water
3. Set it on the fire
4. When boiling, add the rice
5. Let it simmer till nearly thick, stirring it now and then
6. Sweeten it with powdered sugar according to taste
7. Let it simmer for a few minutes longer
8. Pour it in a dish
9. When cold, sprinkle some powdered almonds or walnuts over
10. Serve
