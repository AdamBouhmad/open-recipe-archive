## Ingredients

- paste prepared with fresh butter and eggs
- fresh butter
- finely-minced raw lamb or mutton
- onions
- salt
- almonds
- pistachios
- pepper
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Roll out as thin as a wafer twenty pieces of paste prepared with fresh butter and eggs
2. Well butter the inside of a shallow and dome-like mould
3. Lay carefully ten of the pastes one over the other, which must reach up the brim of the mould
4. Place in a rather deep fryingpan some finely-minced raw lamb or mutton, two or three onions chopped fine, a little salt and a little fresh butter
5. Set it on the fire, and stew them partly brown
6. Add one or two ounces of almonds skinned, the same of pistachios, a little pepper and cinnamon
7. Mix them well together with a wooden spoon
8. Fill up the mould with this mixture, cover the top with the remaining ten pieces of paste
9. Butter the bottom of a baking-tin, in which place the mould upside down
10. Put it in a hot oven, and bake it a nice brown
11. When done, turn it out of the mould on to a dish, and serve
