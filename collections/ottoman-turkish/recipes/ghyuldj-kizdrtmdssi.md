## Ingredients

- twenty or thirty cakes (No. 186)
- rose-water
- pounded almonds or pistachios
- egg
- fresh butter
- boiling syrup

## Directions

1. Prepare twenty or thirty cakes as No. 186
2. Moisten them by sprinkling rose-water over
3. Fold them in two separately
4. Put on each one some pounded almonds or pistachios
5. Fold them in square or triangular shape
6. Dip them in egg
7. Fry them a delicate colour on both sides in hot fresh butter
8. Drain them
9. Throw them in boiling syrup, prepared as above
10. When sufficiently soaked, take them out with a spoon
11. Arrange them tastefully on a dish
12. Serve hot or cold
