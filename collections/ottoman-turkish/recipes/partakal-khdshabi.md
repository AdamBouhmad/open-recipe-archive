## Ingredients

- five or six oranges
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- the juice of two oranges
- water
- a few drops of the essence of orange-flower
- a few small pieces of ice

## Directions

1. Peel five or six oranges, and divide them in pieces
2. Cut each clove or piece of orange in two, and remove the pips and the thin membranes from them
3. Put four ounces of powdered sugar in a stewpan, with the juice of two oranges
4. If they are not sufficiently sweet, add more sugar
5. Put it on a slow fire, and stir with a wooden spoon till it begins to boil
6. Take it off and let it cool
7. Pour in a glass bowl, and mix it with water to form a nice sherbet
8. Add the pieces of orange, with a few drops of the essence of orange-flower, and a few small pieces of ice, and serve
