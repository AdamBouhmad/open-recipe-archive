## Ingredients

- mackerel, fresh
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and boil three or four very fresh mackerel
2. When cold, remove the heads and extract all the bones
3. Arrange the fish in a dish
4. Dress it as No. 203
5. Sprinkle a little cayenne pepper over, and serve
