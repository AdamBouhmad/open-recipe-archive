## Ingredients

- best part of mutton
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- thin caul-fat

## Directions

1. Cut as much as is required of the best part of mutton in pieces the size of an egg
2. Sprinkle one or two chopped onions over, with sufficient salt, pepper, and cinnamon
3. Mix well together, and let them remain for one or two hours
4. Pass the pieces of meat on the skewers, and wrap them up with some thin caul-fat
5. Place them before the fire, and turn them gently
6. As soon as the meat begins to brown, baste it with the gravy that drops from it
7. When done, dish it up tastefully, and serve hot
