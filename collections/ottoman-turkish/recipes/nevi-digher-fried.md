## Ingredients

- flour
- three or four ounces of fresh butter
- two or three eggs
- a little milk
- two or three pinches of salt
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Work lightly in a basin a sufficient quantity of flour with three or four ounces of fresh butter
2. Add two or three eggs, a little milk, and two or three pinches of salt
3. Work all well to form a nice paste
4. Make it into small diamond-shaped cakes
5. Fry them a delicate colour in hot fresh butter
6. Take them out and place them in a strainer
7. When free from grease, dish them up in crown or pyramid
8. Shake some powdered sugar over, and serve
