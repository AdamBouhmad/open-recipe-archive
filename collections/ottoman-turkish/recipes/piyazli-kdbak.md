## Ingredients

- seven or eight small-sized vegetable-marrows
- cheese, grated
- one or two tablespoonfuls of chopped parsley
- half a tablespoonful of mint chopped
- three or four eggs
- hot fresh butter
- broth

## Directions

1. Peel very thin seven or eight small-sized vegetable-marrows and take out the insides
2. Stuff them with some nice cheese, grated and mixed with one or two tablespoonfuls of chopped parsley, and half a tablespoonful of mint chopped
3. Beat up three or four eggs, in which dip the vegetable-marrows, and fry them a nice brown in hot fresh butter
4. Take them out and place them in a stewpan, cover them with broth
5. Set the pan on a charcoal fire, and let it boil gently, until the liquor is nearly absorbed
6. Dish up, and serve
