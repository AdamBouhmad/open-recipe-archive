## Ingredients

- grey mullet or other fish
- olive-oil
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- wine vinegar
- broth

## Directions

1. Get some grey mullet or any other fish you fancy
2. Clean it well, then scald it
3. Rub a gridiron with a little olive-oil and place the fish over
4. Set it on a moderate charcoal fire and turn it now and then until both sides are nearly browned
5. Take it off
6. Lay one or two handfuls of chopped parsley on the bottom of a baking dish or tin
7. Lay the fish over with a little salt and pepper
8. Add half a teacup of wine vinegar and two of broth
9. Put it on a charcoal fire until the broth is absorbed
10. Dish it up and serve hot
