## Ingredients

- one or two pounds of figs
- two or three pints of hot or cold water
- a few drops of orange-flower
- a few pieces of ice

## Directions

1. Cut off the stalks of one or two pounds of figs
2. Prick each one here and there with a wooden needle
3. Place them in a basin with two or three pints of hot or cold water
4. Let them remain for ten or twelve hours
5. Pass the liquid through a sieve into a glass bowl
6. Add the figs with a few drops of orange-flower and a few pieces of ice
7. Serve
