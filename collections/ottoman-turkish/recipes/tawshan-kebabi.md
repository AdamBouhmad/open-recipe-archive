## Ingredients

- hare
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- wine vinegar
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Procure a nice hare, clean it well, cut it into pieces, each about the size of a kidney
2. Put them in cold water, and let them remain for two or three hours
3. Take them out, and dry them in a cloth, season with sufficient salt and pepper, and let them remain for twenty minutes or so
4. Pass them on the skewers one by one, put them before a moderate fire
5. While they are turning, peel five or six cloves of garlic, and well pound them in a mortar with a little salt
6. Add a small teacup of wine vinegar, stir it well, pour it in a small saucepan, add a quarter of a pound of fresh butter
7. Place the saucepan on a moderate charcoal fire, and stir well with a wooden spoon until the butter is melted
8. Take off, and keep it hot
