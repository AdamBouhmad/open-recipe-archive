## Ingredients

- mussels
- flour
- eggs
- hot olive oil or fresh butter
- salt
- pepper
- lemon

## Directions

1. Open as many mussels as are required, and remove them from the shells.
2. Dip them in flour, and then in eggs.
3. Fry them a nice colour in hot olive oil or fresh butter.
4. Dish up, sprinkle a little salt and pepper over, and serve hot with lemon.
