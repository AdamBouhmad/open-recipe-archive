## Ingredients

- mutton
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- cummin
- onions
- fresh butter
- wine vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Cut some mutton into pieces the size of walnuts
2. Peel a sufficient quantity of garlic
3. Mix two teaspoonfuls of salt, one of pepper, and one of cummin
4. Lay seven or eight cloves of garlic on the bottom of a stewpan
5. Sprinkle a pinch of the seasoning, then add some pieces of mutton
6. Season again, then garlic, then mutton, and so forth, until the whole is laid
7. Add two or three sliced onions, fried in fresh butter
8. Add a teacup and a half of wine vinegar, and three of water
9. Set it on a charcoal fire, and let it simmer until the meat is nicely browned and tender
10. Dish up, and serve
