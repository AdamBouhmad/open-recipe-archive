## Ingredients

- mutton or lamb
- salt
- pepper
- sweet marjoram
- parsley
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut from the neck of mutton or lamb as many cutlets as are required, remove the bone at the thick end, and beat them out with a rolling pin or a small chopper
2. Sprinkle with your fingers sufficient salt and pepper, and a small quantity of sweet marjoram; rub them well in the chops
3. Place them on a gridiron, set it on a moderate charcoal fire, turn them frequently with a pair of tongs
4. When nicely cooked, serve whilst hot
5. If desired, place a handful or two of parsley together with an onion, chopped fine, over the cutlets
