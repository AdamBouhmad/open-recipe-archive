## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- garlic
- wine vinegar
- water
- olive oil
- parsley

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in four lengthwise one or two cucumbers
2. Cut them in thin slices and sprinkle a little salt over
3. Shake them up
4. Pound two or three cloves of garlic in a stone mortar
5. Add two or three tablespoonfuls of the best wine vinegar and one of water and two or three of the best olive oil
6. Stir it well
7. Arrange the slices of cucumber in a plate with a handful of chopped parsley and pour the vinegar and oil over
8. Serve
