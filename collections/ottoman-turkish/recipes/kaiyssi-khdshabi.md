## Ingredients

- three pounds of apricots
- half a pint of water
- a pound and a quarter of powdered sugar
- two or three pints of water
- a few drops of the essence of almonds or other essence
- a few pieces of ice

## Directions

1. Pick out the large apricots and put the small ones in a saucepan, with half a pint of water or a little more
2. Set it to boil until the stones come out
3. Pass them through a cloth into a basin, and well squeeze the juice from them
4. Put the juice in the pan with a pound and a quarter of powdered sugar
5. Set it to boil for a minute, and pass it again through the cloth into the basin
6. Put the large ones in a saucepan, with two or three pints of water, and boil them until they begin to crack
7. Take it off and let it cool
8. Pass the liquor through a sieve into a glass bowl
9. According to taste, sweeten it with the syrup which you have prepared
10. Add the apricots, with a few drops of the essence of almonds or any other essence you fancy, and a few pieces of ice, and serve
