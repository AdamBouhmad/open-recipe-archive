## Ingredients

- six ounces of tamarinds
- two pints and a gill of water
- three pounds of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Put six ounces of tamarinds in an earthen pan with two pints and a gill of water
2. Set it to boil until it is reduced to a pint and a half
3. Take it off and pass the liquor through a sieve into a stewpan, add three pounds of powdered sugar, and boil it for half a minute
4. Take it off
5. When nearly cold, pass it through a clean cloth, bottle, and cork it up till wanted
