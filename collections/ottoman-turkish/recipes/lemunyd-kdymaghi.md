## Ingredients

- a pound of strawberries or raspberries
- three or four ounces of powdered sugar
- two or three tablespoonfuls of water
- an ounce and a half of melted isinglass
- half a pint of the fruit
- a pint of whipped cream
- a few drops of the essence of rose or orange-flower

## Directions

1. Take off the stalks of a pound of strawberries or raspberries, wash and put them in a stewpan, with three or four ounces of powdered sugar, and two or three tablespoonfuls of water
2. Put the pan on a moderate charcoal fire, and boil till rather thick, stirring it at the same time with a wooden spoon
3. When cold, pass it through a sieve into a basin, add an ounce and a half of melted isinglass to half a pint of the fruit, and stir it well
4. Add a pint of whipped cream, with a few drops of the essence of rose or orange-flower; mix well all together
5. Pour it into a mould, and place it on ice, or in a cool place, until firm
6. When ready, turn it out and serve
