## Ingredients

- sheep's or lamb's liver
- thin caul-fat
- two or three skinned cloves of garlic
- half a teacup of wine vinegar
- two teacups of broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut as much as is wanted, sheep's or lamb's liver in dice, an inch and a half square
2. Pass them on skewers, and wrap each one with a thin piece of caul-fat
3. Place them before a moderate fire, and turn them slowly until the liver is nicely roasted
4. When done, dish it up
5. Well pound two or three skinned cloves of garlic in a mortar, add half a teacup of wine vinegar, two of broth, and a little salt and pepper
6. Stir it well with a wooden spoon, and pour it over the liver
7. Put the cover on the dish, and place it in the oven, or on a charcoal fire covered with ash for five minutes or so, and then serve
