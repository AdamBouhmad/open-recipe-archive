## Ingredients

- oysters
- salt
- pepper
- olive oil
- lemon

## Directions

1. Open as many oysters as are wanted, lay them on a gridiron
2. Sprinkle with your fingers a little salt and pepper over each one, with a few drops of olive oil
3. Set the gridiron on a charcoal fire
4. When the oysters are nicely broiled and begin to stick to the shells, dish them up in their shells
5. Serve with lemon
