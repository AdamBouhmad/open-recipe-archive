## Ingredients

- sheep's or lamb's liver
- salt
- flour
- hot butter
- handful or two of chopped parsley
- finely-sliced onions

## Directions

1. Get a sheep's or lamb's liver, wash, and cut it into large dice, sprinkle a little salt over.
2. Dip them in flour, and fry them a nice brown in hot butter.
3. When done, take them out and let them drain.
4. Dish up, sprinkle a handful or two of chopped parsley mixed with finely-sliced onions over, and serve very hot.
