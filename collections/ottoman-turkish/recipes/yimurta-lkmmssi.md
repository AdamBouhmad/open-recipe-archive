## Ingredients

- a pound of flour
- salt
- water
- eggs
- cream

## Directions

1. Put a pound of flour in a stewpan with a small teaspoonful of salt
2. Mix with water to form a smooth batter
3. Set it on a charcoal fire and stir it till it becomes thick
4. Take it off and let it cool
5. When cold, beat it up with eggs until it becomes a smooth thick batter
6. Proceed exactly as No. 161
7. Dish up and serve with cream over, if approved of
