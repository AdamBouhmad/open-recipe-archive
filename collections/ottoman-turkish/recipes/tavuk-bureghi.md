## Ingredients

- fowl
- water
- salt
- onions
- fresh butter
- pepper
- paste

## Directions

1. Put a nice and well-cleaned fowl in a saucepan with some water and a little salt
2. Set it on the fire, and boil it till sufficiently tender
3. Take the fowl out and when cold, remove the meat from the bones, and chop it up fine
4. Stew one or two finely-chopped onions in fresh butter until quite brown, but not burnt
5. Mix them well with the fowl and add a little salt and pepper
6. Butter a baking-tin, in which place ten very fine layers of paste one over the other, sprinkling a little butter between each one
7. Lay the minced fowl all over
8. Cover it with the same number of pastes as before
9. Cut it in four crossways, pour a little hot fresh butter over
10. Put it in the oven, bake it a nice brown, and serve hot
