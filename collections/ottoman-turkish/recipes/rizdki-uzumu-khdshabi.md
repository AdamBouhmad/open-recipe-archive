## Ingredients

- a pound of raisins
- two or three pints of water
- powdered sugar
- a few drops of any essence of flowers
- some pieces of ice

## Directions

1. Take off the stalks of a pound of raisins and wash them
2. Place them in a stewpan with two or three pints of water
3. Set it to boil until they are quite swollen up
4. Add some powdered sugar according to palate and boil them for one or two minutes longer
5. Take it off and let it cool
6. Pour all together into a glass bowl
7. Add a few drops of any essence of flowers with some pieces of ice
8. Serve
