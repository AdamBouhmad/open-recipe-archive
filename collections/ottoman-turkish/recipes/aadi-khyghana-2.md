## Ingredients

- four or five eggs
- salt
- pepper
- about two ounces of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Break four or five eggs in a basin
2. Add a little salt and pepper
3. Beat them well up with a fork
4. Put into a very clean frying-pan about two ounces of fresh butter
5. Put it on the fire
6. When hot, pour in the eggs
7. Keep stirring till all is nicely set
8. Let it slip to the edge of the pan, laying hold by the handle and raising it slantwise, which will give an elongated shape to the omelette
9. Turn in the edges and let it set a moment
10. Turn it over on to a dish
11. Serve
