## Ingredients

- 1 round steak
- 1 tomato
- 1 large onion
- salt
- pepper
- Cayenne
- tablespoonful of lard
- one clove of garlic
- tablespoonful of flour
- two tomatoes, sliced
- half tablespoonful of vinegar
- cup of water

## Directions

1. Select a nice round steak and beat well.
2. Cut into grillades of about four inches square and season highly with salt and pepper and Cayenne.
3. Put a tablespoonful of lard into the frying pan, and when it heats, add a chopped onion and one clove of garlic.
4. As these brown, add one tablespoonful of flour, making a Brown Roux.
5. Then add two tomatoes, sliced, with their juices.
6. As this browns lay the grillades upon it.
7. Cover closely, and as it browns on one side, turn on the other.
8. Then add a half tablespoonful of vinegar and a cup of water.
9. Stir well and set back on the stove and let it simmer slowly for about a half hour.
10. This is very nice served with hominy at breakfast, or with red or white beans and boiled rice at dinner.
