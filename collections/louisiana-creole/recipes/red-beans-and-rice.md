## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Dried Red Beans
- 1 Carrot
- 1 Onion
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Pound of Ham or Salt Meat
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Wash the beans and soak them over night, or, at least five or six hours, in fresh, cold water.
2. When ready to cook, drain off this water and put the beans in a pot of cold water, covering with at least two quarts.
3. Let the water heat slowly.
4. Then add the ham or salt pork, and the herbs and onion and carrot, minced fine.
5. Boil the beans at least two hours, or until tender enough to mash easily under pressure.
6. When tender, remove from the pot, put the salt meat or ham on top of the dish, and serve hot as a vegetable, with boiled rice.
