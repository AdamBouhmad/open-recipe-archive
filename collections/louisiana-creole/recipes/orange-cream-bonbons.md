## Ingredients

- 1 Ounce of Orange Sugar
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 3/4 Gill of Orange Flower Water

## Directions

1. Work the orange sugar, using one ounce, with the whites of the eggs and make a Royal Icing
2. Work the gum, Orange Flower Water, using half a gill, and the icing sugar into a stiff paste
3. Proceed as in Chocolate Creams, finally covering with the Royal Icing with the white cream forming the interior of the Bonbons and the orange or yellow the outside
