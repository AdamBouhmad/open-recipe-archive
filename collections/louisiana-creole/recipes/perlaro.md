## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Flour
- 1 Pound of Butter, or Half Butter and Lard
- 12 Eggs
- 1½ Ounce of Yeast
- 1 Gill of Brandy
- 1 Tablespoonful of Orange Flower Water
- Milk or Tepid Water to Form a Dough
- 1 Pound of Figs or Stoned Cherries

## Directions

1. Make the same dough as for the Brioche.
2. At the moment of adding the leavened flour and the reserved flour, add the stoned cherries or peeled figs, the latter cut in halves.
3. Then proceed as for Brioches, and at the last kneading add the orange flower water and the brandy.
4. Bake as you would the Brioche.
