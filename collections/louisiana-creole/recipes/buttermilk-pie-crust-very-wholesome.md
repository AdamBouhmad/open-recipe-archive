## Ingredients

- a pint of buttermilk
- one large teacupful of lard
- one teaspoonful of salt
- a teaspoonful of soda
- flour enough to form a soft dough

## Directions

1. Mix the lard and flour by rubbing them together
2. then add the other ingredients
