## Ingredients

- 1 Small Young Chicken
- 12 Rounds of Puff Paste
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1/2 Pint of Milk
- 1/2 Can of Mushrooms
- A Pinch of Grated Nutmeg
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1 Egg

## Directions

1. Roast or broil the chicken nicely.
2. Make a Puff Paste.
3. Cut a dozen rounds with a biscuit cutter; mark a smaller round or top for a cover.
4. Brush with a beaten egg, and mark on the surface of each with the cutter, dipping it each time in hot water, so that the marked outline may remain perfect.
5. Set in a brisk oven and let them brown nicely for twelve minutes.
6. Then remove the covers gently with a knife and fill with the following garnishing.
7. Remove all the chicken meat from the bone and chop very fine.
8. Put a tablespoonful of butter in a sautoire or stewpan and add a tablespoonful of sifted flour.
9. Stir till smooth.
10. Pour in gradually a half pint of hot milk till the same reaches the consistency of a thick cream.
11. Season to taste with salt and pepper and a little nutmeg, and add one-half can of mushrooms finely chopped and the chicken.
12. Stir constantly and let it come to a boil.
13. Then remove from the fire and fill the patties.
14. Set the covers on, serve on a hot dish.
