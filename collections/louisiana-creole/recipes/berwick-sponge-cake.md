## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- ⅛ cups of sugar
- 2 cups of flour
- 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
- 1 cup of cold water
- 1 teaspoon of soda
- salt
- 3 more cups of flour

## Directions

1. Beat 6 eggs, yolks and whites together, 2 minutes
2. Add ⅛ cups of sugar and beat 6 minutes
3. Add 2 cups of flour with 2 teaspoons cream of tartar and beat 2 minutes
4. Add 1 cup of cold water with 1 teaspoon of soda dissolved in it, and beat 1 minute
5. Flavor, add a little salt
6. Add 3 more cups of flour (without cream of tartar) and beat 1 minute
