## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Lime Juice
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup
- 3/4 Ounce of Soluble Essence of Limes

## Directions

1. Proceed in exactly the same manner as for Lemon Syrup, and add the essence when about to bottle.
