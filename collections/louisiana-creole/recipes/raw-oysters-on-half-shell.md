## Ingredients

- 6 Oysters to Each Plate
- Cracked Ice
- Maunsell White or Black Pepper, Cayenne and Vinegar
- Lemon, Sliced or Cut in Quarters

## Directions

1. Allow six oysters to each person where the bivalve is used to begin the dinner or breakfast.
2. Have the oysters opened in their shell and remove one half of the shell.
3. Drain the water from the oyster shell, without disturbing the oyster, and place in plates, with cracked ice.
4. Sprinkle with a quarter of a sliced lemon in the center of the plate.
5. Serve with black pepper and Cayenne, if desired, or the famous Maunsell White, sold in all New Orleans oyster saloons.
