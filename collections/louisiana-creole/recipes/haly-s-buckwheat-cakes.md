## Ingredients

- one quart of fresh buckwheat flour
- half a cup of yeast
- one tablespoonful of salt
- one and a half quarts of milk and water warmed
- a teaspoonful of soda or saleratus
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Beat all well with a large spoon
2. Pour the mixture in a tall jar as in that it rises better than in a flaring or open crock
3. In the morning add a teaspoonful of soda or saleratus just before frying the cakes
4. Grease the griddle and fry them brown
5. Eat with syrup or honey
