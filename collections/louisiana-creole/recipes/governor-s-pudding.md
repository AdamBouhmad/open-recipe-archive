## Ingredients

- 1 large cocoanut
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- whites of 6 eggs
- nutmeg
- sugar to the taste

## Directions

1. To 1 large cocoanut add 1/2 lb. of butter, the whites of 6 eggs, nutmeg and sugar to the taste
2. to be baked in a rich paste
