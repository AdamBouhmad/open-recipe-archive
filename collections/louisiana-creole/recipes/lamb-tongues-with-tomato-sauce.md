## Ingredients

- 6 Tongues
- 1 Onion
- 1 Pint of Broth or Water
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 2 Sprigs of Thyme
- A Tomato Sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the tongues as in the recipe given above, omitting the vegetables
2. When done, place the tongues on a hot dish
3. Pour over a rich Tomato Sauce and serve
