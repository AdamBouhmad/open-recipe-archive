## Ingredients

- 4 Layers of Cake
- 1 Cup of Pineapple, Peach, Raspberry or any Jelly

## Directions

1. Make a layer cake, and spread, while hot, with any kind of jelly, peach, lemon, raspberry, apple, strawberry, or the like
