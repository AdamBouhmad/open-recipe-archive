## Ingredients

- four ounces of almonds
- one spoonful of water
- one pint of cream
- two spoonfuls of rose water
- yolks of four eggs
- sugar to make it pretty sweet

## Directions

1. Blanch and beat four ounces of almonds fine with a spoonful of water
2. Beat a pint of cream with two spoonfuls of rose water
3. Add them to the yolks of four eggs and as much sugar as will make it pretty sweet
4. Stir it over a slow fire till it is of a proper thickness, but do not boil
5. Pour it into custard glasses
