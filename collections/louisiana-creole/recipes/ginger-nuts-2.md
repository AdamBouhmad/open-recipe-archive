## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of sifted flour
- 2 quarts of molasses
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of candied orange peel, cut small
- 1 ounce of strong ginger, ground
- 1 ounce of allspice
- 1 lb. of butter oiled

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well together and set it by for some time
2. Roll it out in pieces the size of a walnut
3. Put them in rows on a baking plate
4. Press them flat with the hand
5. Bake them in a slow oven for 10 minutes
