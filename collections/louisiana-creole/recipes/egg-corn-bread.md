## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cornmeal
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter or Lard
- 1 Pint of Milk (Buttermilk if Possible)
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Scald the meal with boiling water sufficient to melt.
2. Beat the yolks of the eggs very, very light.
3. Add the corn meal and melted butter, and the salt, and beat till very light, moistening with the milk.
4. Then add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
5. Beat all well together.
6. Pour into a buttered or greased pan, or into shallow tins, and bake quickly.
