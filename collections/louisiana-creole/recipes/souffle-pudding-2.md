## Ingredients

- 3 ounces of sifted flour
- 2 1/2 ounces of granulated sugar
- 1 pint of sweet milk
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Put on your milk to boil, reserving about a gill to wet the flour
2. When the milk comes to a boil, add the flour which has been wet with the milk, also the butter and sugar
3. Let this thicken well
4. Put it in a bowl to get cold, at least for 1 or 2 hours before you are ready to use it
5. About 1/2 hour before you are ready to use, have the yolks well beaten, which you add by degrees to the cold paste
6. Last of all, add the whites, which have been whipped until very light
7. Have a yellow bowl buttered, put the paste in it, and bake in a hot oven a good 1/2 hour
8. Serve with a hard sauce of butter and sugar, flavored as you wish
9. Serve immediately, as it will fall by standing
