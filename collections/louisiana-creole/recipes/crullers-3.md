## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- three eggs
- half a pint of sour milk
- one teaspoonful of soda
- half a nutmeg
- flour
- sugar for topping
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Combine butter, sugar, eggs, sour milk, soda, and half a nutmeg
2. Add flour to roll thin
3. Cut in fancy shapes
4. Put two pounds of lard in a deep skillet and heat until very hot
5. Fry the crullers, replenishing lard once or twice as it becomes brown and scorched
6. Crullers should be a light brown of uniform color
7. Sprinkle sugar over them when done
