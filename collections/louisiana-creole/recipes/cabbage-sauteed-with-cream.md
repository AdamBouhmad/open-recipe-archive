## Ingredients

- A fine head of white cabbage
- 1 cupful of cream or milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- A pinch of black pepper
- Salt to taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the cabbage according to recipe
2. Blanch in hot water for ten minutes
3. Drain and throw into cold water
4. Drain again and chop up the cabbage
5. Put into a saucepan with two tablespoonfuls of butter
6. Add salt to taste and the black pepper
7. Take a tablespoonful of flour and blend well with a little milk
8. Mix well with a cup of cream or milk
9. Add this to the cabbage and mix well
10. Let all cook for three-quarters of an hour or an hour till done
11. Arrange neatly on a hot dish and serve
