## Ingredients

- 1 lb. flour
- 1 lb. sugar
- 12 whites of eggs
- 10 yolks of eggs
- 2 glasses of Madeira wine
- 1 teaspoonful of mace

## Directions

1. Drop on pans
2. Let them harden gradually in the stove until quite crisp, turning frequently
