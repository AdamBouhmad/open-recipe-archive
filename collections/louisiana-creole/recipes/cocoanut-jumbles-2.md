## Ingredients

- 1 large cocoanut, grated
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of flour
- 8 eggs
- cocoanut and sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Rub 1 lb. of butter into 1 lb. of flour
2. Add 8 eggs and the grated cocoanut to form a stiff dough
3. Make the jumbles with the hands
4. Grate cocoanut and sugar over them
5. Bake them
