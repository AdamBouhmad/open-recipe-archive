## Ingredients

- 12 Lamb's Feet
- 1 Pint of Broth
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 2 Sprigs of Parsley
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the feet well and boil
2. When the water is reduced, take out the feet, cut in pieces, taking out all the bones
3. Put back in the saucepan, add a tablespoonful of butter blended well with a tablespoonful of flour
4. Stir well and add two sprigs of parsley, minced very fine, and the juice of one lemon
5. Let this simmer for ten minutes longer and serve hot
