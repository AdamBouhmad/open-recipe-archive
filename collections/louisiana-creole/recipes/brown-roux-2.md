## Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful Flour

## Directions

1. Melt the tablespoonful of butter slowly
2. Add gradually the flour, sprinkling it in and stirring constantly, till every portion is a nice, delicate brown
3. Never make it too brown, because it must continue browning as the other ingredients are added
