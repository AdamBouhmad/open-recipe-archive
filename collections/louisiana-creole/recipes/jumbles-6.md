## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Butter
- 0.75 Pound of Flour
- 4 Eggs
- 0.75 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Rose Water
- 0.5 Grated Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add the eggs, thoroughly beaten, and then add the Rose Water and the nutmeg
3. Gradually add the flour, and beat well
4. Beat till very light before adding the flour
5. Dust a paper on a baking sheet with sugar, instead of flour, and roll out the paste to about one-eighth of an inch in thickness
6. Then use a small cutter with a round center, and take the centers out, thus forming pretty rings
7. Bake in a moderate oven till a light brown
8. Take them out and sift powdered sugar over them
