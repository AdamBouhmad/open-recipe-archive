## Ingredients

- goose
- sixty large chestnuts
- two ounces of butter
- one onion
- sprig of parsley
- one pint of good broth
- juice of two oranges
- half a pound of currant jelly
- lemon peel

## Directions

1. Get the goose ready as usual
2. Peel sixty large chestnuts by scalding
3. Put them in a stew pan with two ounces of butter, one onion chopped fine, and a sprig of parsley
4. Chop and mix all together and stuff the goose with it
5. Mix with the chestnuts one pint of good broth, and stew them down in it before stuffing the goose
6. Boil down the gravy very much
7. When the goose is served, add the juice of two oranges, half a pound of currant jelly, and a lemon peel in the gravy
8. Pour this over the goose when it goes to the table
