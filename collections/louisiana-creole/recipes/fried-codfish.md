## Ingredients

- Codfish
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Egg
- 1 Cup of Grated Bread Crumbs
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Parsley and Lemon to Garnish

## Directions

1. Soak the Codfish overnight, and boil twenty minutes, or until very tender.
2. Take out and cut into slices of one-inch thickness, and dry with a clean towel.
3. Have ready a pint of milk, and season well with pepper only; season the Codfish, rubbing a little black pepper to taste and a dash of Cayenne over it.
4. Soak the fish in the milk.
5. Have ready some crushed bread crumbs and a well-beaten egg.
6. Dip each slice first into the egg, and then roll in bread crumbs, patting lightly.
7. Drop into boiling lard; the fish must swim in the lard.
8. When fried a golden brown, take out with a skimmer, drain off all fat, and serve hot on a bed of fried parsley, garnished with sliced lemon.
