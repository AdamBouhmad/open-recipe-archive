## Ingredients

- six heads of celery
- half a pint of water
- three teaspoonfuls of flour
- milk
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the green tops of celery, slice the remainder into small bits and boil in half a pint of water until it is tender
2. Mix three teaspoonfuls of flour smoothly, with a little milk
3. Add six spoonfuls more of milk, stir it in
4. Add a little salt and a small piece of butter
5. On boiling take off
