## Ingredients

- One half package of Coxe's gelatine
- 1 cup of cold water
- 1 J cups of sugar
- 1 cup of boiling water
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Whites of 4 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- Yolks of 4 eggs
- Grated rind of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Pour over gelatine 1 cup of cold water, and add 1 J cups of sugar
2. When soft, add 1 cup of boiling water, juice of 1 lemon and the whites of 4 well beaten eggs
3. Beat all together until very light
4. Put in glass dish
5. Pour over it custard made as follows: heat 1 pint of milk with yolks of 4 eggs and grated rind of 1 lemon and boil
