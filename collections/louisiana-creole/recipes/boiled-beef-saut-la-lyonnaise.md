## Ingredients

- The Bouilli
- 3 Onions
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard
- 1 Tablespoonful of Olive Oil
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chili Vinegar
- The Peel of One Lemon

## Directions

1. Slice the onions and brown them in lard, using about one tablespoonful.
2. Skim the lard off the onions and put the beef in the pan.
3. Stir up and smother.
4. Add the oil, the peel of a lemon, cut fine, and the Chili vinegar.
5. Serve hot.
