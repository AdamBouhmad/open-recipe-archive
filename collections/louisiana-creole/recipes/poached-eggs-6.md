## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar, optional

## Directions

1. Have the frying pan filled with boiling water.
2. Add salt.
3. Some add also a tablespoonful of vinegar, but this is a matter of taste.
4. The eggs must be absolutely fresh.
5. Break the eggs into the boiling water.
