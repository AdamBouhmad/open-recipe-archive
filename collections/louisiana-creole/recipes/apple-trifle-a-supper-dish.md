## Ingredients

- tart apples
- sugar
- lemon
- two eggs
- half a pint of milk
- half a cup of sugar
- one pint of rich cream
- strips of citron
- apple jelly

## Directions

1. Make a marmalade by stewing tart apples in sugar, seasoned with lemon
2. Lay it when cold in a deep glass dish
3. Pour over it a boiled custard made of two eggs, half a pint of milk, sweetened with half a cup of sugar
4. Whip a pint of rich cream to a froth
5. Pile it high on the custard
6. Ornament with strips of citron and apple jelly laid on the whipped cream
