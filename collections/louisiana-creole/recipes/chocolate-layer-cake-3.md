## Ingredients

- A Layer Cake
- 1 Cup of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Cup of Milk or Water
- 4 Ounces of Chocolate
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of White Sugar

## Directions

1. Make a layer cake
2. Boil four ounces of chocolate in one cup of milk or water
3. Add one cup of sugar, and let it boil till thick
4. Spread between the layers of cake
5. Or take four ounces of chocolate, melt with a little boiling water, just sufficient to make a paste, and work till smooth
6. Beat the whites of two eggs to a stiff froth, add four tablespoonfuls of white sugar, and then proceed as in Almond Layer Cake, having a layer of chocolate on top
