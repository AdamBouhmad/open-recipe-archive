## Ingredients

- venison steak
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- wine
- flour

## Directions

1. Season with pepper, salt, and butter
2. Cook quickly on a hot gridiron if to be broiled
3. If the meat is not fat, make a gravy for it of wine, flour, and butter
4. Serve hot
