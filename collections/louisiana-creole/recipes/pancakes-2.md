## Ingredients

- 5 yolks of eggs
- 1 lb. of flour well sifted
- cream
- whites of 3 eggs
- fresh butter
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Break 5 yolks of eggs in 1 lb. of flour well sifted
2. Mix it well with cream until the batter is very thin
3. Beat up the whites of 3 eggs very light and mix it with the batter
4. Have ready fresh butter creamed, and to each pancake put 2 tablespoonfuls in the pan, which must be made hot
5. Fry them a light brown
6. Turn a plate up in the dish, and lay them on one another, powdering each with white sugar
