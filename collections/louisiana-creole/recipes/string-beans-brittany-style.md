## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Fresh String Beans
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Pint of Chicken Consomme or Water
- 1 Medium-Sized Onion
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- Chopped Parsley to Garnish
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Take a medium-sized onion, peel and cut into small dice-shaped pieces.
2. Put the onion in a saucepan with the butter and let it saute to a golden brown.
3. Add the flour gradually, blending well, and moisten with the consomme or water.
4. Let the mixture come to a boil and skim the broth.
5. Then add the string beans, which have already been boiled and drained.
6. Let them simmer for ten minutes, adding in the meantime one finely-minced clove of garlic.
7. After ten minutes, place the beans in a hot dish, sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve hot.
