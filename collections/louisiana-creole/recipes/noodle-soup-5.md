## Ingredients

- 3 egg yolks
- 2 egg whites
- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- cold water
- 1 quart bouillon or consomé
- boiling salt water

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of three eggs and the white of two together until very light
2. Add one cup of flour and one half teaspoon of salt
3. Mix with cold water to make a stiff paste
4. Roll very thin
5. Roll each strip to form a tube and cut in strips
6. Grease and simmer a few at a time in boiling salt water for about twenty minutes
7. Simmer the noodles in the soup about fifteen minutes
