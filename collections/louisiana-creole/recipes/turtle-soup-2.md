## Ingredients

- leg of beef
- turtle
- water
- mace
- allspice
- cloves
- salt
- black pepper
- cayenne pepper
- parsley
- thyme
- onions
- flour
- brown sugar
- ham
- sweet herbs
- eggs
- Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Cut up the turtle in small pieces and throw them into a pot
2. Before doing this, take a leg of beef and boil it to a gravy
3. Put in the pieces of turtle, adding as much water as will cover the whole, about 2 inches
4. Let it boil slowly for 3 hours
5. Put in the seasoning and citron when the soup is half done
6. To 2 quarts and a half of soup add 1/2 an ounce of mace, a dessertspoonful of allspice, 1/2 teaspoonful of cloves, and salt, black and cayenne pepper to your taste
7. Use parsley, thyme, and onions in the soup while boiling
8. When nearly done thicken with 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
9. Take a tablespoonful of brown sugar and burn it to give it good color
10. When sufficiently burnt add a wineglass of water
11. Use 2 tablespoonfuls of this coloring in the soup
12. Make forcemeat balls with a bit of the turtle, a small piece of ham, and seasoned highly with sweet herbs
13. Add the yolk of one egg, roll it into balls, fry them and put them into the soup
14. Boil 1/2 a dozen eggs hard, slice them and put them in
15. Add 1/2 a pint of Madeira wine
16. After the wine cooks a little, the soup will be ready for table
