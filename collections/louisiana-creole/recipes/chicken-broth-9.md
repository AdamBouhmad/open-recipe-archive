## Ingredients

- 1 good large chicken
- cold water
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the chicken carefully and cut it up, being careful to mash all the bones with an ax.
2. Place in a saucepan of cold water, and let it simmer gently for four or five hours, until it is boiled down to about two cups of broth.
3. Season with a little salt and pepper, omitting all vegetables.
