## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sweet Almonds
- 1 Ounce of Butter
- 2 Pounds of Sifted Flour
- The Whites of 8 Eggs
- Royal Icing to Garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare the paste as above directed, keeping it somewhat stiffier and firmer, however.
2. Add two whites of eggs of Royal Icing (see recipe), and work both together till thoroughly incorporated.
3. Fill the biscuit forcer, and push the macaroons through upon buttered wafer paper, as directed.
4. Bake in a very moderate oven. Otherwise the excessive heat will cause the macaroons to run into one another, and produce a useless mass.
5. Put the Petits Fours in pretty plaited paper cases.
