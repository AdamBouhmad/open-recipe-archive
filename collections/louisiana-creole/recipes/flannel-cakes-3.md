## Ingredients

- 1 quart new milk
- flour
- 2 eggs well beaten
- 1 large spoonful yeast
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Thicken 1 quart of new milk with flour to the consistency of fritter batter
2. Add 2 eggs well beaten, 1 large spoonful of yeast, and a little salt
3. Mix all well together
4. Set it to rise at night for breakfast
5. Do not stir in the morning
6. Bake on the griddle as buckwheat cakes
