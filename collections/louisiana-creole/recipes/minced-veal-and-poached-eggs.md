## Ingredients

- one pound of cold veal
- half a pint of sauce
- a cup of cream
- pepper
- salt
- six poached eggs
- slices of red tongue or ham

## Directions

1. Chop one pound of cold veal very fine.
2. Boil half a pint of sauce till it begins to thicken or glaze.
3. Add a cup of cream and the minced veal.
4. Season with pepper and salt.
5. When dished put six poached eggs around it, alternately with slices of red tongue or ham.
