## Ingredients

- The Zest of 3 Lemons
- 1 Pound of Loaf Sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the zest or outer rinds of the lemons on pieces of loaf sugar, and then set to dry
2. When dry, pound very fine, sift, and put in a jar and cork tight
