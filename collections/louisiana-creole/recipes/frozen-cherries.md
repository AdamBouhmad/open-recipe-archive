## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Cherries
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Quart of Water

## Directions

1. Stone the cherries, mix them with the sugar and add the water.
2. Mix well and turn into the freezer and proceed to freeze as in directions for Ice Cream.
3. Serve with Whipped Cream or rich milk.
