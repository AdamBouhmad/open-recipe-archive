## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Orange Sherbet
- ¾ Pint of Cherries, Seeded
- ¾ Pint of Stoned Apricots
- ¾ Pint of Stoned Peaches
- ¾ Pint of Figs
- ¼ Pint of Pineapple
- ¾ Pint of Watermelon

## Directions

1. Cut the watermelon and the pineapple into very small square lozenges, first seeding the watermelon.
2. Cut the rest of the fruits very fine.
3. Put a layer of the watermelon into the freezer, and sprinkle generously with white powdered sugar.
4. Put a layer of the mixed fruits, sugar abundantly, pour in a little of the sherbet, and proceed in this way until the whole form is nearly filled with the mixture in alternate layers.
5. Set in a double boiler just long enough for the sugar to melt.
6. Take out, pour over the orange sherbet, close tightly, and set to freeze.
