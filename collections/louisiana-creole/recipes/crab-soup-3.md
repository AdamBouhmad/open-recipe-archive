## Ingredients

- one dozen large crabs
- one tablespoon of lard
- two tablespoons of butter
- one large onion
- one clove of garlic
- one whole can of tomatoes, or 18 or 20 fresh ones
- salt
- black pepper
- red pepper
- the rind of a lemon
- one tablespoon of lemon juice
- about one-half dozen grated soda crackers
- boiling water
- fat from the crabs
- a little thyme
- parsley
- sweet marjoram

## Directions

1. Have ready one dozen large crabs; separate the fat from the back shell and bodies; cleanse them from the dead men's fingers
2. Boil the claws and pick the meat from them
3. Have ready a large stew-pan, into which put one tablespoonful of lard, two of butter, one large onion cut up into not very small pieces; one clove of garlic
4. Allow this to brown a little, then add one whole can of tomatoes, or 18 or 20 fresh ones
5. After they have stewed 10 minutes, add the meat from the crab claws, then the crabs
6. Season with salt and black and red pepper, the rind of a lemon, and one tablespoonful of the juice
7. Sift over this about one-half dozen grated soda crackers
8. Add boiling water to make the required quantity of soup, and allow it to boil moderately for two hours
9. A little while before serving, add the fat from the crabs and a little thyme, parsley and sweet marjoram
