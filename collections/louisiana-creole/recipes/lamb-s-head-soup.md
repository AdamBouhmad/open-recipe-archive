## Ingredients

- lamb's head
- 2 quarts of water
- pepper
- salt
- onion
- herbs
- turnip
- carrot
- celery
- heart and liver

## Directions

1. Prepare the head, and put to it 2 quarts of water
2. Boil it until the head becomes so tender that the bones may be taken out
3. Cut it into small pieces and put them back into the pot
4. Cut up into small pieces half of the heart and liver, and add them
5. Season with pepper, salt, a little onion (chopped), a few herbs, turnip, carrot and celery
6. Boil all together over a slow fire for several hours, skimming well
7. If the water boils away too much add more, which must be boiling
