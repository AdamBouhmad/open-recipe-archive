## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Boiled Rice
- 1 Cup of Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Milk or Water
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 1/4 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of Sugar

## Directions

1. Mash the rice through a sieve.
2. Sift the flour, baking powder and salt together, and add the sugar to the milk.
3. Add this to the flour and mix in the rice till free from any lumps.
4. Then add the eggs, the whites and yolks beaten separately, and mix into a very smooth batter.
5. Have the griddle well-heated.
6. Bake the cakes to a nice brown, and serve with Louisiana Syrup.
