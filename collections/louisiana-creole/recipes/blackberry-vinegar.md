## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Blackberries
- 1 Quart of Good French Vinegar
- 1 Pound of white sugar per pint of liquor

## Directions

1. Put one quart of blackberries into a jar or deep vessel, and pour over the vinegar
2. Let them stand for twenty-four hours
3. Strain them, and again add the fruit
4. Repeat this operation and let stand twenty-four hours three times total
5. Strain through a muslin bag
6. Add one pound of white sugar to every pint of the liquor
7. Boil the whole in a porcelain kettle for half an hour
8. When cold, bottle it and keep it in a cool place
