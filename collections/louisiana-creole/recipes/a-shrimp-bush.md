## Ingredients

- 100 Lake Shrimp
- Celery Tips
- Asparagus Tips

## Directions

1. Boil the shrimp according to recipe.
2. Take a glass fruit or cake stand.
3. Pile the shrimp first into a small deep dish, and put a close cover on to press them down.
4. Turn them over and they will cling together in one solid mass.
5. If a cake stand is used, set a glass bowl or goblet on it.
6. Place the shrimp on top of this glass bowl or goblet.
7. Take dainty bits of celery tips and asparagus tips, and heap around as a border.
8. Form another row a little lower, intermingled with asparagus tips and celery tops, between which the pink shrimp are gracefully placed.
9. Serve the shrimp from the bush as an hors d'oeuvre.
