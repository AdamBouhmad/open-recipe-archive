## Ingredients

- 1 cup of water
- 1/2 cup of butter
- 1 cup of sifted flour
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup of milk
- 2 egg yolks
- 4 tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cornstarch or fine flour
- cold milk
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Put 1 cup of water on the fire
2. When hot, melt in 1/2 cup of butter
3. When it boils, beat in 1 cup of sifted flour
4. If lumpy, stir till perfectly smooth
5. Set it aside to cool
6. When cold, stir in, one at a time, three eggs without first beating them
7. Drop the batter on tins in small spoonfuls
8. Bake 1/2 hour in a quick oven
9. For the cream: put 1 cup of milk to boil
10. Beat the yolks of 2 eggs very stiff
11. Add 4 tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
12. When the milk boils, pour it on the eggs, stirring all the time
13. Wet 2 tablespoonfuls of cornstarch or fine flour with a little cold milk
14. Add it to the custard and return it to the fire
15. Stir until quite thick
16. When cold, flavor with vanilla
17. The cream is put in the puff by lifting the top with a knife and dropping in the cream with a spoon
