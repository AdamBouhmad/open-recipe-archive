## Ingredients

- pounded sugar in the weight of 8 eggs
- sifted Indian meal in the weight of 6 eggs
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 grated nutmeg or 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake
