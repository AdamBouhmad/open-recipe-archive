## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of oysters with the liquor
- 3 quarts of water
- 8 slices of lean ham
- 2 onions
- 6 egg yolks
- 4 tablepoonfuls of wheat flour
- 1 pint of rich milk
- 1 quart of raw oysters
- 1 teaspoonful of white pepper
- half teaspoonful of grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put 3 quarts of water, 8 slices of lean ham, and 2 onions into a pot with the oysters and liquor
2. Boil this down to half the quantity
3. Run it through a cullender and put the liquor alone back into the pot and let it come just to a boil
4. Beat up the yolks of 6 eggs with 4 tablepoonfuls of wheat flour and 1 pint of rich milk
5. Stir this into the oyster liquor after it has been strained and made boiling hot
6. Add a quart of raw oysters and let the whole thicken a little on the fire
7. Add 1 teaspoonful of white pepper and a half of grated nutmeg
