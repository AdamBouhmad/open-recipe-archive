## Ingredients

- Sliced Tomatoes
- 2 Dozen Pickled Onions
- 1/2 Pint of Mayonnaise Dressing

## Directions

1. Make of sliced tomatoes and pickled onions, prettily arranged around a small bed of Mayonnaise heaped in the center.
