## Ingredients

- 1 cocoanut
- whites of 7 eggs
- 3 teacups of sifted flour
- 2 teacups of sifted white sugar
- ½ cup of butter
- ½ cup of milk or water
- 2 teaspoons yeast powder
- For icing: 3 whites of eggs
- 1 lb. of sugar

## Directions

1. Grate 1 cocoanut and set aside
2. Beat the whites of 7 eggs very light
3. Mix 3 teacups of sifted flour, 2 teacups of sifted white sugar, ½ cup of butter, and ½ cup of milk or water
4. Beat well together, and add 2 teaspoons yeast powder
5. Bake in 4 jelly cake pans
6. Make an icing of 3 whites of eggs and 1 lb. of sugar beaten together until stiff enough to stand as you drop it from the beater
7. Flavor icing to taste
8. Spread the icing on one of the cakes, then sprinkle cocoanut over, and lightly press with palm of the hand
9. Place another cake upon the first, and continue the process until all are used
10. Use icing only on the top of the last cake, or spread grated cocoanut over the others
