## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of ginger sliced and dried
- 1 lb. of horse radish scraped
- 1 lb. of white mustard seed washed and dried
- 1 lb. of garlic salted, sliced and dried
- 1 lb. of nutmegs
- 1 ounce of long peppers
- 1 ounce of turmeric
- 1.5 gallons of vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the vegetables intended for the pickle into a kettle
2. Pour the vinegar and spices over them
3. Set them over a clear fire
4. Let them boil a few moments
5. Put them in jars and tie them up tightly
