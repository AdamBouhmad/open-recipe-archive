## Ingredients

- dough
- cream or new milk
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Take dough early in the morning and make it into a thin batter with cream or new milk
2. Set it to rise
3. Put it thin on a hoe and bake it quickly
4. Have ready melted butter to put over it
