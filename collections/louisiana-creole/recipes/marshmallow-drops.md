## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pound of White Gum Arabic
- 1 Pint of Water
- The Whites of 4 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Extract of Vanilla
- 1/2 Pound of the Finest White Sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the Gum Arabic in one pint of water and strain, and add one-half pound of the finest white sugar, and place over the fire, stirring constantly all the time until the syrup is dissolved
2. Then gradually add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
3. Stir in the mixture until it becomes thin and does not adhere to the fingers
4. Then flavor with the Vanilla Extract, and drop in tablets of about the size of a five-cent piece upon a sheet dusted with powdered starch
5. Make the shape of the tablets slightly convex, and in two hours remove from the papers, dry for a few minutes in the stove and put away for use
