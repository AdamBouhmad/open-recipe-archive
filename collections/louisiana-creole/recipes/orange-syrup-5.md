## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Orange Juice
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup
- 1/4 Ounce of Lemon Juice

## Directions

1. Proceed in exactly the same manner as for Lemon Syrup, and bottle when almost cold.
