## Ingredients

- warm water
- good buckwheat
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 2 handfuls cornmeal
- 1 wineglassful good yeast
- warm milk

## Directions

1. Take as much warm water as you will require for the size of the family
2. Thicken this with good buckwheat to a fritter batter
3. Add 1 teaspoonful of salt if 2 quarts is made, 2 handfuls of cornmeal, and 1 wineglassful of good yeast
4. Warm milk in the place of warm water is a great improvement
5. Be particular not to scald the yeast or the cakes will be heavy
