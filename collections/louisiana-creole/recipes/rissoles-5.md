## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Cold Roast Beef or Veal, or Left-Over Meat of any Kind
- 2 Ounces of Stale Bread, Wet, and Squeezed Thoroughly
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Minced Parsley
- 1 1/2 Ounce of Flour
- 1 1/4 Tablespoonfuls of Milk or Water
- 1/2 Teaspoonful Each of Salt and Black Pepper
- A Dash of Cayenne
- 1 Bay Leaf, Chopped Fine, With Sprigs of Parsley and Thyme

## Directions

1. Mince the meat finely and season well.
2. Mix the ingredients thoroughly with it, adding, if you have it, minced Chaurice or sausage meat, or a little cold ham minced.
3. Form it into balls, using two tablespoonfuls for each ball.
4. Brush lightly with milk, toss in a little flour, pat to get off all superfluous flour, and brown nicely in boiling lard.
5. Drain off all lard and serve on a platter, garnished with parsley sprigs.
