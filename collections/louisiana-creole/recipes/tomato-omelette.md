## Ingredients

- 2 onions
- tomatoes to make 3 pints when cooked
- 1½ teaspoonfuls crumbs of bread
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 1 heaping teaspoonful black pepper
- 4 tablespoonfuls butter
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Parboil 2 onions
2. While this is doing, peel a sufficient quantity of tomatoes to make 3 pints when cooked
3. Cut them up, and add the onions, also 1½ teaspoonfuls of crumbs of bread, 1 tablespoonful of salt, 1 heaping teaspoonful of black pepper, and 4 tablespoonfuls of butter
4. Beat these thoroughly together and set them over a slow fire, gradually to stew
5. They should cook never less than 3 hours, but the longer the better
6. About 15 minutes before they are to be served up, beat 6 eggs, and stir them in
7. Put them on a hot fire, and let them boil, stirring them all the time
