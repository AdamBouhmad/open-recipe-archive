## Ingredients

- A Small Bunch of Fresh Sorrel
- 3 Quarts of Boiling Water
- 1 Cup of Cream or Rich Milk
- 1 Cup of Mashed Potatoes
- 4 Eggs
- 3 Sprigs of Parsley
- 3 Leaves of Lettuce
- 1 Onion
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- A Pinch of Nutmeg
- Pepper and Salt to Taste

## Directions

1. Wash the leaves and stem them, the entire length of the leaf.
2. Chop them fine until you have a quantity equal to a pint or two teacupfuls.
3. Chop the other vegetables and put these and the sorrel into a saucepan with the butter.
4. Cover and let them stew gently for ten minutes.
5. Add the flour, which has been well mixed with a little water.
6. Pour gradually, stirring always, into the three quarts of boiling water.
7. Beat the yolks of the eggs and mix with a little cream or milk in a tureen.
8. Rub the rest of the cream or milk smooth with the mashed potato and put into the soup.
9. Add the seasonings.
10. Prepare toast in the form of dice, rubbing them first with the raw onion.
11. Pour some of the boiling soup over the eggs in the tureen and mix very carefully.
12. Put in the pieces of toast, and then add the remainder of the soup.
13. Cover and stand five minutes in a warm oven, and serve hot.
