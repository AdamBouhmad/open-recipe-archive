## Ingredients

- 6 Fresh Creole Eggs
- 1 Ounce of Powdered French Chocolate
- 4 Ounces of Flour
- 10 Ounces of Pulverized Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Extract

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the sugar to a cream.
2. Add the chocolate, and mix well.
3. Then add the flour, alternating with the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
4. Add one tablespoonful of Vanilla Extract.
5. Bake upon wafer sheets in small cakes to a light color.
