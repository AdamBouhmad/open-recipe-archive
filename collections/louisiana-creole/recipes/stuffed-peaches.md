## Ingredients

- fresh white mustard seed
- vinegar
- scraped horseradish
- powdered ginger
- red pepper pods
- small onions or garlic clove
- chopped peach
- packthread
- salad oil
- leather

## Directions

1. Cut a piece from the stem end of peaches just sufficient to allow the seed to come out
2. Throw them into cold vinegar until you make the stuffing
3. Wet fresh white mustard seed with vinegar and allow to swell a few hours
4. Mix mustard seed, scraped horseradish, powdered ginger, red pepper pods, small onions or garlic clove with vinegar
5. Add half as much chopped peach to the mixture
6. Stuff the peaches hard with this mixture
7. Replace the piece cut out and tie it up tight with packthread
8. Boil a quart of vinegar for each dozen peaches and season it with the same spices as the stuffing
9. Boil the spices in a small bag, then put in the peaches and let them scald ten or fifteen minutes
10. Place the peaches in jars and pour scalding vinegar well spiced over them until they are covered
11. Add at the top a tablespoonful of salad oil
12. Cover the jar tight by tying leather over it
