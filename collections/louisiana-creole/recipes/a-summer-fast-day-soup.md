## Ingredients

- The Hearts of 6 Lettuce Cut in Pieces
- 2 Large Onions
- 2 Cucumbers, Pared and Sliced
- 4 Pints of Young Green Peas
- Chopped Parsley
- 3 Lumps of Crushed Sugar
- 1/4 Pound of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- A Sprig of Mint

## Directions

1. Chop the vegetables fine and stew all together, except the young peas
2. After one hour add the young peas
3. Press them through a sieve and put all into the water in which they have been boiled
4. Add to this the vegetables that have been stewed in the butter and simmer about an hour and a half
5. A sprig of mint is added just before the soup is taken off the fire
