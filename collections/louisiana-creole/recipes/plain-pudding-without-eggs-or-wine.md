## Ingredients

- One pound of chopped and stoned raisins
- Half a pound of suet
- One pound of flour
- A cup of bread crumbs
- Two tablespoonfuls of molasses
- A pint of milk
- Nutmeg grated
- A lemon peel chopped

## Directions

1. Cut the suet very fine and mix it with the flour
2. Add the bread crumbs, lemon and nutmeg, with the stoned raisins, to a pint of milk
3. Mix all together and put in the molasses
4. Keep it closely covered in a cool place
5. When it is wanted, pour it in a floured cloth and boil it five hours
6. Serve with rich sauce
