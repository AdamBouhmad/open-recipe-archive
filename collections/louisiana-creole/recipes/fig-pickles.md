## Ingredients

- figs
- salt
- water
- pure, strong vinegar
- light brown sugar
- cinnamon
- cloves
- horse radish

## Directions

1. Gather the figs when fully swollen, but not quite ripe, leaving the stems on
2. Soak them 10 or 12 hours in moderately strong brine (salt and water)
3. Take them out of this brine
4. Rinse with clear water and place in glass or stone jars
5. Have a kettle of pure, strong vinegar
6. Add a gallon of light brown sugar to each gallon of vinegar, with such spices as you prefer for flavoring, cinnamon and a few cloves
7. Place this kettle of vinegar over the fire, stirring and dissolving the sugar
8. When the vinegar comes to a simmer, pour it over the figs, covering them about an inch
9. Repeat this process of scalding the vinegar and pouring over figs about 3 times
10. A small piece of horse radish is an improvement
