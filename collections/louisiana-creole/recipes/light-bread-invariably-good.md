## Ingredients

- nine pint cups of flour
- one pint cup of good yeast made from hops
- two pint cups of warm water
- a pint cupful of warm milk

## Directions

1. Take nine pint cups of flour, one pint cup of good yeast made from hops, two pint cups of warm water and a pint cupful of warm milk
2. Make into a sponge, let this rise
3. when risen, knead it with all your strength, work more flour into it, and let it rise again
4. When it is light, you must bake it in loaves
