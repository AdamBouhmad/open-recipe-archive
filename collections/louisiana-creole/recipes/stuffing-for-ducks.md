## Ingredients

- 2 Dozen Oysters
- 1 Cup of Bread, Wet and Squeezed
- 1 Onion Chopped Very Fine
- 1/8 Square Inch of Ham
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1/8 Teaspoonful of Sage
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme, Parsley and Bay Leaf, Minced Very Fine
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. The Creoles generally stuff the domestic duck when roasted, using an oyster stuffing
2. The wild duck stuffed with oysters is a most delectable dish
3. The domestic duck is always roasted and stuffed
4. Serve with Currant Jelly
