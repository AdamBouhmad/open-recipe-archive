## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Cream
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Gill of Cognac

## Directions

1. Proceed in exactly the same manner as in the directions for Whipped Cream with Rum, substituting instead a gill of good old Cognac
2. Serve in the same manner directed
