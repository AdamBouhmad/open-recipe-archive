## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Cream
- 2 Ounces of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Gill of Curacao

## Directions

1. Proceed in exactly the same manner as in the directions for Whipped Cream with Rum, adding, instead of the Rum, one gill of Curacao.
