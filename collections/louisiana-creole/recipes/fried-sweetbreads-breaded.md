## Ingredients

- 3 Pairs of Sweetbreads
- 1 Egg
- Grated Bread Crumbs
- Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and parboil the sweetbreads and then trim off all tendons and nerves.
2. Cut into pieces of about two inches long and roll first in a well-beaten egg and then in bread crumbs.
3. Drop into boiling fat and fry till a golden brown.
4. Serve with a Cream Sauce.
