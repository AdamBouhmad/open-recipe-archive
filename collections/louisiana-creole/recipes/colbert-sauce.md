## Ingredients

- 3/4 pint of Madeira Sauce
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of consommé
- 1 teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- The juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Put a pint of very thick Madeira Sauce in a saucepan
2. Add gradually the butter and consommé
3. Mix well without allowing the mixture to boil
4. When ready to serve add the juice of half a lemon and a teaspoonful of chopped parsley
