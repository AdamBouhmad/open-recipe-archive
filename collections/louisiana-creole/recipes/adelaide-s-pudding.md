## Ingredients

- 6 apples
- sugar
- cinnamon
- 1 egg
- flour
- 1 teaspoonfal of salt
- wine and sugar sauce

## Directions

1. Pare and core 6 apples
2. Fill up the cores with sugar and cinnamon
3. Make a batter of 1 egg, a little flour, 1 teaspoonfal of salt
4. Pour around it and bake
5. Serve hot with wine and sugar sauce
