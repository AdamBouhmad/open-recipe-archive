## Ingredients

- 1 Pint Bottle of Boned Anchovies
- 1 Hard-Boiled Egg
- Chopped Parsley to Garnish

## Directions

1. Take the contents of one pint bottle of boned Anchovies; drain them of all oil on a cloth and then arrange nicely in a flat glass or china celery or radish dish.
2. Take a hard-boiled egg, hash or slice daintily and decorate the dish with this.
3. Sprinkle over some chopped parsley and serve.
