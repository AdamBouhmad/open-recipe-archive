## Ingredients

- boiling water
- salted water
- sherry wine
- hot buttered toast
- salt
- pepper
- cayenne
- mace
- butter

## Directions

1. Like crabs and lobsters, terrapins are thrown alive into boiling water and let boil till the outer shell and toe-nails can be removed.
2. Then wash and boil them in salted water till the fleshy part of the leg is tender.
3. Put them in a bowl or deep dish, take off the second shell, remove the sand bag and gall bladder, and cut off the spongy part.
4. Cut up the meat, season it with salt, pepper, cay, and mace, thicken with butter and flour, and cook.
5. Just before serving put in a gill of sherry wine for every terrapin, and pour all over hot buttered toast.
