## Ingredients

- 1 Cake of Grated French or German Chocolate
- 1 Quart of New Milk
- Sugar to taste or two tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Set the milk to boil
2. In the meantime mix the grated chocolate with a little hot water, to blend thoroughly
3. Stir this in the milk as it begins to simmer, and let it boil up once
4. Serve hot, each one sweetening to taste, or allow two tablespoonfuls of sugar to the entire mixture
5. Serve hot, with or without Whipped Cream
