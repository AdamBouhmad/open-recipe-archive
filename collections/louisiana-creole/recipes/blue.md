## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water
- 1 Ounce of Indigo
- 1 Tablespoonful of Alcohol

## Directions

1. Take one pound of sugar, one pint of water and boil to a syrup
2. Add one ounce of Indigo, powdered very fine
3. Let all cool
4. add a little alcohol, and strain and bottle
