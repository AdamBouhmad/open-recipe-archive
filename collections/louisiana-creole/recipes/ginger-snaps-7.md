## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Molasses
- 1 Cup of Lard or Butter
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 0.75 Cup of Sour Cream
- 1 Tablespoonful of Ground Ginger
- 2 Eggs
- 2.5 Teaspoonfuls of Soda
- Flour Sufficient to Roll Thick

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients, making the batter slightly stiffer
2. Cut into fancy shapes and bake in a quick oven
