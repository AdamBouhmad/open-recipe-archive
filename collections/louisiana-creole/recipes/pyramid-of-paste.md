## Ingredients

- puff paste
- preserve or jam
- yolk of an egg

## Directions

1. Roll out some nicely prepared puff paste, an inch in thickness
2. cut the paste into any shape you may fancy
3. make each smaller until the top one is not larger than a cent
4. between each piece of paste spread some nice preserve or jam
5. turn up the edges of the paste, and brush the sides and top with the yolk of an egg well beaten
6. lay the pyramid on a baking tin, and bake a nice brown
7. serve hot
