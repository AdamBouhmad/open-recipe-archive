## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Sugar
- ¼ Grated Nutmeg
- ½ Cup of Butter
- ½ Cup of Boiling Water
- 2 Wineglasses of Madeira

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and the sugar to a cream, using powdered sugar, and then add one cup of boiling water
2. Stir in gradually two wineglasses of good Madeira wine or Sherry, and add one teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
3. Put into a double boiler and stir until the sauce is hot, but do not let it boil
4. Serve, either hot or cold, with any pudding
