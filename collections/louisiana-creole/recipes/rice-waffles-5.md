## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Cups of Softly Boiled Rice
- 2 Ounces of Butter
- 1 Pint of Scalded Milk
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Tablespoonful of Wheat Flour

## Directions

1. The rice must be cold and well mashed.
2. Melt the two tablespoonfuls of butter into the milk, which has been allowed to cool.
3. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the whites separately, making the latter come to a stiff froth.
4. Mix the rice and milk.
5. Beat thoroughly, and then add a half teaspoonful of salt and one of baking powder, and the flour.
6. Put the yolks into the batter, first blending well, and lastly add the whites, and beat well again.
7. The waffle iron should be very hot, and well greased in every part.
8. Always have a little brush with which to grease the waffle irons.
9. Pour the batter into a pitcher, so that you may more easily fill the irons.
10. Open the irons, pour the batter from the pitcher and fill the iron quickly.
11. Then close quickly and set on the fire.
12. As soon as the edges are set, turn the iron and bake on the other side.
13. Two minutes should be all the time required to bake a waffle nicely.
14. The waffle must be baked evenly.
15. Always select the simple waffle baker with four compartments in preference to the more complicated one.
