## Ingredients

- ducks
- bread
- butter
- onions
- lard
- slips of bacon
- giblets
- water
- pepper
- salt
- mace or cloves
- lemon juice or lemon pickle
- flour

## Directions

1. Truss the ducks and stuff them with bread, butter and onions
2. Place them with lard in a frying pan
3. Have prepared in an iron pot slips of bacon, giblets, water, pepper, salt, a little mace or cloves
4. Put in the ducks and let them stew gently but constantly for 2 hours
5. Flour the ducks each time that you turn them in the pot
6. Add lemon juice or lemon pickle
7. Thicken with butter rolled in flour
