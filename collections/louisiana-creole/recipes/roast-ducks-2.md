## Ingredients

- ducks
- bread
- butter
- onion
- sage
- pepper
- salt
- brown gravy

## Directions

1. Well pluck the ducks without tearing the skin, removing all the plugs
2. Clean the inside thoroughly with a little warm water
3. Stuff them with bread and butter, onion, sage, pepper and salt
4. Roast them before a brisk fire, but not too close
5. Baste very frequently
6. They will take from 1/2 hour to 1 hour
7. When the breast plumps, they will be done
8. Serve them with a rich brown gravy
