## Ingredients

- equal weight of fruit and double refined sugar
- 1 pint of red currant juice to every 3 lbs. of strawberries
- largest scarlets, not dead ripe

## Directions

1. Lay fruit in a large dish and sprinkle 1/2 the sugar in fine powder
2. Give a gentle shake to the dish that the sugar may reach the under side of the fruit
3. The next day make a thin syrup with the remainder of the sugar
4. Allow 1 pint of red currant juice to every 3 lbs. of strawberries
5. Simmer them in this till sufficiently jellied
