## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk or Cream
- 1/4 Pound of the Best French Chocolate
- 1/4 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Extract

## Directions

1. Boil the milk, and add to it a half pound of the best French chocolate, grated
2. Let it boil and thicken, beating till smooth
3. Add a quarter of a pound of white pulverized sugar
4. When cool add half a quart of cream and the Vanilla Extract
5. Pour into the freezer, after mixing well, and proceed as in directions for making and freezing cream
