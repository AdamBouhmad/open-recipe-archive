## Ingredients

- 12 Large Louisiana Oranges
- 1 Quart of Water
- The Juice of 2 Lemons
- The Zest of 3 Oranges
- 1 Pint of Sugar

## Directions

1. Put the sugar and water to boil.
2. Grate the zest or yellow rind of three oranges, and add to the syrup, and let it boil for five minutes.
3. Then set away to cool.
4. Extract the juice from the oranges and lemons, and grate the fruit.
5. Mix this with the syrup, and then strain all through a thin muslin cloth.
6. Turn into the freezer, and proceed as in Ice Cream.
