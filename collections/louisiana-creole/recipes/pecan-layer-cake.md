## Ingredients

- 4 Layers of Cup or Sponge Cake
- The Whites of 3 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Minced Louisiana Pecans
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon Extract

## Directions

1. Make a good Layer Cake or Cup Cake
2. Beat the whites of three eggs to a stiff froth
3. Add three tablespoonfuls of sugar, and one cup of minced and pounded pecans
4. Add one teaspoonful of extract of lemon, and fill the cake, leaving a layer of the filling on top
