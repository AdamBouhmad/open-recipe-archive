## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- dessertspoonful of flour
- crushed strawberries

## Directions

1. Boil 1 pint of milk, well sweetened
2. Moisten a dessertspoonful of flour with a little of the hot milk
3. Put the milk on the fire and stir with a wooden spoon
4. Pour in the juice of crushed strawberries till the cream becomes a pretty color
5. Strain the whole through a sieve
