## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of New Orleans Molasses
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 3/4 Cup of Melted Butter
- 3/4 Cup of Sherry Wine
- 3 Cups Flour
- 3/4 Teaspoonful Baking Powder
- 1 1/2 Teaspoonful Each of the Four Spices

## Directions

1. Dissolve the baking powder in the flour, mixing thoroughly
2. Melt the butter and add it to the molasses, and then add the milk, mixing thoroughly
3. Add the flour, gradually mixing well, and then add the wine, and lastly the spices
4. Blend all well together
5. Pour into a well-buttered pan, and let it bake for about two hours
6. Serve with Foamy Sauce
