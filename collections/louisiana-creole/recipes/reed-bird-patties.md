## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Reed Birds
- 1 Salpicon Royal
- 1/2 Pound of Foundation Paste
- Bacon
- Madeira Sauce

## Directions

1. Pick and clean the birds and season well; then bind each with a thin strip of bacon
2. Make a Foundation Paste (Pâté-à-Foncer) and line six patty molds with this
3. Put in a moderate oven and bake for fifteen minutes; then empty them and let them dry well in the open oven for five minutes
4. Let them cool
5. Fill the bottom of each mold with a tablespoonful of Salpicon Royal, and place in each mold two of the nicely prepared birds
6. Lay the patties on a baking dish or roasting pan and set in the oven, whose heat must be moderate, and let them roast for fifteen or twenty minutes
7. Remove from the oven, moisten each patty with a tablespoonful of hot Madeira Sauce, and send to the table hot
