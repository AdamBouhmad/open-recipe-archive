## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Flour, Finely Sifted
- 2 Cupfuls of Sweet Milk
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 3 Eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and the yolks of the eggs separately, the former to a stiff froth.
2. Add the flour to the yolks, and beat till very light.
3. Add the yeast powder and beat again.
4. Then add the sweet milk and beat well.
5. Now add the whites of the eggs and mix all to a very smooth, light batter, as thick as a batter that will run in a stream from the mouth of a spoon.
6. Have ready the hot griddles, which you will grease with lard lightly, or better still, with a piece of fat bacon.
7. Drop the cakes on the griddle from a large spoon, and bake about two minutes to a nice brown, turning first on one side and then quickly on the other.
8. Pile the cakes on a plate in the mouth of the oven, buttering each generously as soon as baked, and send to the table almost as fast as you bake.
