## Ingredients

- one ounce or one ounce and a half of gelatine
- one pint of cold water
- three-quarters of a pint of boiling water
- one and a half pounds of white sugar
- juice and grated rind of one lemon
- a spoonful of any essence
- whites of two eggs
- one pint of wine
- two tablespoonfuls of extract of lemon or vanilla

## Directions

1. Pour gelatine upon one pint of cold water, and let it remain to soften for an hour or so
2. Pour three-quarters of a pint of boiling water on to the gelatine, and stir until the gelatine is dissolved
3. Add to it one and a half pounds of white sugar, the juice and grated rind of one lemon, and a spoonful of any essence
4. Beat the whites of two eggs well, and stir briskly into the mixture
5. Put it on a gentle fire, let it simmer slowly, take it off as soon as it boils up
6. Add a pint of wine and two tablespoonfuls of extract of lemon or vanilla
7. Strain it through a jelly bag until it runs clear
8. After it is clear and still warm, pour in a pint of wine and set the jelly on ice in summer, or to cool in the winter
