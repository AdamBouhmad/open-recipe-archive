## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 1/4 pound butter
- 2 eggs, well beaten
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- A pinch of soda, or 1/4 teaspoonful yeast powder
- Sour milk or buttermilk if using soda, or sweet milk if using yeast powder
- Strawberries, raspberries, or other fruits for filling

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and yeast powder together, if you desire it instead of soda
2. Otherwise sift the flour, add the salt, and mix the butter thoroughly together, rubbing between your hands till there is not a lump left
3. Add the well-beaten eggs and milk to make a soft dough
4. Divide dough in two, one for the upper and the other for the lower crust
5. Sprinkle thickly between with fruits such as strawberries, raspberries, etc.
6. Bake in the oven
7. The dough should be soft and crisp
