## Ingredients

- rounds of beef
- saltpetre
- common salt

## Directions

1. Rub the rounds first with saltpetre and then with common salt
2. They will make their own brine, in which they should be turned every day
3. A round of small beef only requires ten days in winter; it grows hard if kept longer; in warm weather less time suffices
