## Ingredients

- 2 large lemons
- lemon peel
- 6 large apples
- 1 lb. of suet
- 1 lb. of currants
- ½ lb. of sugar
- candied fruit

## Directions

1. Squeeze 2 large lemons
2. Boil the peel till tender enough to beat to a mush
3. Combine with apples, suet, currants, sugar, and candied fruit
