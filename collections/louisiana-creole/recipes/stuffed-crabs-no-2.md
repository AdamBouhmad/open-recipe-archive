## Ingredients

- 2 Dozen Large Fine Crabs
- Grated Bread Crumbs
- Salt and Pepper
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- Thyme
- Parsley and Onion
- Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Boil the crabs according to recipe.
2. Clean and cut and pick out all the meat.
3. Chop an onion fine; chop the thyme, bay leaf, and parsley and hard-boiled eggs, and mix well with the crab meat.
4. Season highly with hot pepper and salt to taste.
5. Put one tablespoonful heaping over with butter into the frying pan.
6. As it melts, add the onion and fry, being careful not to burn.
7. Then add the crab meat, and, if desired, the very small clove of a garlic, chopped very fine.
8. Let this fry about five minutes, stirring constantly.
9. Mix thoroughly, fry three minutes longer, and then take off the stove.
10. Stuff the crab shells, forming a rolling lump in the middle.
11. Sprinkle lightly with grated bread crumbs, and put a dot of butter on top.
12. Place in a quick oven and let them bake about five minutes, or until a nice brown.
