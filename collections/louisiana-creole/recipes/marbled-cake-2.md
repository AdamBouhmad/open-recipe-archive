## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 cochineals
- a wineglass of water
- a small pinch of soda
- sponge cake or white cake

## Directions

1. Soak cochineals in a wineglass of water, adding a small pinch of soda
2. Pour the cake into the pan for baking
3. At the same time pour with it this water, mixing it well with the cake
