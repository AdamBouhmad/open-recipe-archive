## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- The Yolks of 6 Eggs
- 1/2 Pound of Pulverized Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla or Lemon Extract
- 1 Pint of Cream

## Directions

1. Set one pint of milk to boil
2. Beat the yolks of six eggs with a half pound of white pulverized sugar until light
3. Stir in the milk
4. Take from the fire, and let cool
5. Flavor with vanilla and freeze
6. Whip one pint of cream to a stiff froth, and stir in
7. Fill molds or fancy paper cases with the mixture
8. Pack in salt and ice for two hours, and serve
