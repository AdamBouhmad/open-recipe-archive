## Ingredients

- four dozen oysters
- pepper
- mace
- salt
- wine vinegar
- crackers
- fresh vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash four dozen oysters
2. Let them be fine and large, with plenty of their own liquor
3. Pick them carefully
4. Strain their liquor and to it add a dessertspoonful of pepper
5. Two blades of mace, a tablespoonful of salt, and a cup of strong wine vinegar
6. Simmer the oysters in this five minutes
7. Then put them in small jars
8. Boil the pickle again, and when cold add a cup of fresh vinegar
9. And fill up the jars, cork them, and set away for use
