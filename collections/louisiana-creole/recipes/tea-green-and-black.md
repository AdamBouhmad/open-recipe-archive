## Ingredients

- tea
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Scald your tea-pot always before putting in the tea
2. Throw out the scalding water and allow a teaspoonful of tea to each person expected to drink it
3. Turn on half a pint of boiling water at first and let it steep—green tea requires about five minutes, black tea ten minutes
4. After this, pour on more boiling water according to the number of persons
