## Ingredients

- 1 cup of powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 8 eggs
- 1 heaping cup of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of sweet cream
- 2 teaspoonfuls of yeast powder
- nearly 1 quart of strawberries

## Directions

1. Rub butter into the sugar
2. Add eggs and flour
3. Add sweet cream and yeast powder
4. Bake in three jelly cake pans
5. When cold, lay between them nearly a quart of strawberries
6. Sprinkle sugar over each layer and over the top cake
