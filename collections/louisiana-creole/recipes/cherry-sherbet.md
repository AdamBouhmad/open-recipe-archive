## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Cherries
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Quart of Water

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water together for five minutes
2. Stone the cherries and add them to the syrup
3. Set to cool
4. Press through a sieve till all the juice is extracted from the cherries
5. Set to freeze and serve as directed
