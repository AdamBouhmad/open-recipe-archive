## Ingredients

- 3 Pounds of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Dip the forefinger into iced water, and then into the boiling sugar, and instantly again into the iced water, to prevent burning the finger
2. Pinch between the index finger and thumb
3. If a small thread forms, which breaks when the attempt is made to pull apart through the fingers, the proper degree is reached
