## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs. of flour
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1 very large spoonful of butter
- 1/2 lb. of almonds
- 1 nutmeg
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Form and bake
