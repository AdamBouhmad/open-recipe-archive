## Ingredients

- a pint of bread crumbs
- a quart of milk
- four eggs
- a cup of sugar
- a lump of butter (the size of an egg)
- peach or nutmeg for flavoring
- acid preserve or jelly, such as plums, apples, grapes, or currants
- a full cup of white sugar
- lemon extract

## Directions

1. Make one pint of milk boiling hot, pour it over the bread crumbs, and beat it smooth.
2. When cool, add a cup of sugar, and the yolks of the four eggs; also a lump of butter (the size of an egg).
3. Beat all well together, thin it by adding the rest of the milk, flavor it with peach or nutmeg, and set it in the oven to bake.
4. Bake only long enough to cook the eggs.
5. When slightly brown on top, take the pudding out of the stove, and set it to cool.
6. When cool, spread over it a layer of acid preserve or jelly.
7. Make an icing or meringue of the whites of the eggs, beaten up with a full cup of white sugar; flavor this with lemon extract.
8. Put the pudding again in the stove, and brown.
