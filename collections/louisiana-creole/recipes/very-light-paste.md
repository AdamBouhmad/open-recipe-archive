## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of flour
- 2½ lbs. of butter
- ½ lb. of lard
- 2 well beaten eggs
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. add 2 well beaten eggs to the flour
2. wash the butter nicely, and then salt the crust
3. Wet it with water enough to make it roll easily
4. roll it out once, divide the paste and put rather more butter in 1 part for the top
5. get it all in 3 times rolling it
6. bake with moderate heat
