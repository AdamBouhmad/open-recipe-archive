## Ingredients

- Calf's Liver
- A Slice of Pork Fat
- 1 Onion
- 2 Sprigs of Thyme
- 2 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Take calf's liver and pork fat, in the proportions of two-thirds liver and one-third fat
2. Grind both together very, very fine
3. Mince an onion, and two sprigs each of thyme and parsley, and one bay leaf, and mix with the ground meat
4. Add a half teaspoonful of grated nutmeg and salt and pepper to taste
5. Mix well
6. Put one tablespoonful of hot butter in a frying pan and throw in the chopped meat
7. Let all blend well together without cooking for about two minutes, stirring all the time
8. Take the mixture off, and, when it cools, form into little balls about the size and shape of a pecan
9. Roll these in flour, and then parboil in boiling water that has been well seasoned with pepper and salt
10. Place around the meat and pour the sauce over and serve hot
