## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 1/2 Pints of Apple Juice
- 1 Quart of Water
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- about a dozen apples

## Directions

1. Pare, core and quarter the apples, and cook till very tender
2. Put the sugar and water to boil
3. Add the apples, and when very tender strain all through a sieve
4. Add the juice of the lemon and set to freeze as in Ice Cream
5. Serve as needed
