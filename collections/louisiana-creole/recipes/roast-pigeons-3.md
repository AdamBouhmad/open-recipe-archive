## Ingredients

- thin slices of fat bacon
- their giblets
- the livers
- the stock the liver was boiled in

## Directions

1. Truss them when plucked and drawn
2. Lay thin slices of fat bacon on their breasts
3. Bake them three-quarters of an hour
4. Make a gravy with their giblets, which should have been boiling for the purpose
5. Chop up the livers, etc., brown them and serve with the pigeons
6. Thin the gravy with the stock the liver was boiled in
