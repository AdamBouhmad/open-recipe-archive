## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Louisiana Rice
- 3 Quarts of Cold Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Salt
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1/4 Cup of Cream
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1 Egg
- Mushroom Sauce

## Directions

1. Make a Fricassee of Chicken with White Gravy.
2. Boil the rice according to recipe and then mash the rice thoroughly and add the butter and season with salt and pepper.
3. Take a raised pie pan or casserole and press the rice into this, and set away to cool.
4. When cool, cut out the center of the rice and fill the wall and bottom with the white fricassee of chicken.
5. Cover the top with the rice which you have cut out, laying on lightly, so as not to press the chicken sauce through.
6. Beat an egg well and brush over this.
7. Set in the oven and bake.
8. Serve with Mushroom Sauce.
