## Ingredients

- Birds
- Salt and Pepper
- Melted Butter
- Truffle
- Lump of Butter
- Pinch of Chopped Thyme
- Chopped Parsley
- Bay Leaf
- Pinch of Four Spices
- Strips of Bacon
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Water
- Buttered French Toast
- Sprigs of Parsley
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare the birds, cleaning out the entrails of the larger birds, and leaving the small ones untouched.
2. Rub with salt and pepper and melted butter.
3. In the larger birds, if you can afford it put a truffle or two, for stuffing, and in all put a little lump of butter and a little salt and pepper, a pinch of chopped thyme, parsley and bay leaf, and a small pinch of the four spices.
4. Bind with strips of bacon, and place in a baking pan with a tablespoonful of butter.
5. Let them bake or roast thirty minutes or less, according to size.
6. Prepare the gravy by simply adding a tablespoonful of water, letting it cook for two minutes.
7. Strain; let it cook for two minutes more, and pour upon the breast of the bird.
8. Serve always on buttered French toast.
9. Garnish nicely with sprigs of parsley and lemon, and serve hot.
