## Ingredients

- one pint bowl of bakers' chocolate grated
- two bowls of yellow sugar
- one bowl of New Orleans molasses
- one half a cup of milk
- a piece of butter the size of a small egg
- vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil about twenty-five minutes
2. Pour in buttered tins
3. Mark deeply with a knife
