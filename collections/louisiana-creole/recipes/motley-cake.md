## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sugar
- % Pound of Butter
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 12 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of Peach Extract
- 1 Teaspoonful of Fruit Coloring

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream and then add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs.
2. Beat until very light.
3. Then add gradually the flour, into which you will have sifted, in the second sifting, the baking powder.
4. Mix thoroughly and beat vigorously.
5. Then add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
6. Now divide the batter into two parts, and put one spoonful of Peach Extract into one, and one spoonful of fruit coloring into the other.
7. Mix well, and drop by spoonfuls into the buttered cake pan, first a spoonful of the white, and then one of the pink, until all the batter is used up.
8. Bake from forty-five minutes to one hour in a moderate oven.
