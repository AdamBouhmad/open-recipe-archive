## Ingredients

- 12 Pounds of Fine, Ripe Cherries
- 3 Pounds of Sour Cherries
- 1 Pound of Currants
- 3 Pounds of Raspberries
- 1 Pint of the Best French Brandy
- 4 Ounces of Sugar to Every Pint of Fruit Liquor

## Directions

1. Stone the fruit and stem the berries
2. Put all together in a bowl, and mash well
3. Let the fruit stand for two hours
4. Press out the juice through a cloth or sieve
5. Add one pint of the best French brandy and four ounces of sugar to every pint of liquor obtained
6. Put in a stone jar, cover tight, and let it stand for a month
7. At the end of the time strain and filter
8. Bottle and keep in a cool place
