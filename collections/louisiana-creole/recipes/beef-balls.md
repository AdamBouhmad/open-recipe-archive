## Ingredients

- tender beef (fat and lean)
- onion
- boiled parsley
- grated crumbs of bread
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- beaten egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Mince very fine a piece of tender beef, fat and lean
2. Mince also an onion, with some boiled parsley
3. Add grated crumbs of bread, and season with pepper, salt, and nutmeg
4. Mix the whole together
5. Moisten with a beaten egg, roll it into balls, flour, and fry them
