## Ingredients

- French artichokes in cans
- chicken
- egg yolks
- butter
- flour
- onion
- chicken broth
- mushrooms
- truffles
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- lemon juice
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Get the very best French artichokes in cans
2. Boil a chicken in very little water
3. When done, chop it as fine as possible
4. Beat the yolks of 3 eggs and stir them into the chicken water
5. Put 1 spoonful of butter in a frying pan
6. Thicken it with a little flour
7. Fry some onion minced very fine in it
8. Pour in some of the chicken broth
9. Stir it until it is like a rich cream
10. Stir in the chicken
11. Chop some mushrooms very fine, also 2 or 3 truffles, and add to it
12. Season with salt, pepper, parsley, and a little lemon juice
13. Let it get cold
14. Put it in the artichokes in pyramid shape
15. Cover each one with very finely sifted bread crumbs
16. Put them in a pan
17. Pour over them the balance of the chicken broth
18. Let them simmer slowly, taking great care to preserve their shape
19. Put a few mushrooms or truffles sliced into the gravy
20. Serve them upon a dish dressed with sprigs of parsley or sliced lemons, and the sauce in a boat
