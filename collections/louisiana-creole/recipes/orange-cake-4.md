## Ingredients

- 4 ripe oranges
- 1 lb. of the best loaf sugar
- 1 lb. of good butter
- 14 ounces of flour
- 10 eggs

## Directions

1. Roll the oranges under your hand on the table till quite soft
2. Break up the loaf sugar, and on some of the pieces rub off the yellow rind of the oranges
3. Cut the oranges and squeeze the juice through a strainer
4. Powder the sugar and mix the orange juice with it
5. Wash and squeeze the butter in a pan of cold water till you have extracted all the salt and milk
6. Cut up the butter in the pan of sugar and orange, and stir it hard till perfectly light
7. Sift the flour into a pan
8. Beat the eggs very light and thick
9. Stir them by degrees into the butter and sugar, alternately with the flour, a little of each at a time
10. Beat the whole very hard for some time after all the ingredients are in
11. Put the mixture into a large, shallow, square pan, well buttered
12. Set immediately into a brisk oven until properly baked
