## Ingredients

- a hind quarter of lamb or mutton
- brown sugar
- half a pint of wine
- half a pint of vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a hind quarter of lamb or mutton; rub it well all over with brown sugar, half a pint of wine, and same of vinegar
2. Let it stay in this pickle for a day or two, if the weather is cold
3. When it is wanted, wash it, dry it, and roast it, or it may be cut into steaks, or made into a pie like venison
