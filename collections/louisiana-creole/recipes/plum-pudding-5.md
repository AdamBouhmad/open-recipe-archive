## Ingredients

- One and a half lbs. of raisins
- J lb. of currants
- i lb. of bread crumbs
- J lb. of flour
- J lb. of beef suet
- J lb. of citron and orange peel
- i nutmeg
- 9 eggs
- 1 wineglass of brandy
- A little ground ginger

## Directions

1. Chop the suet as fine as possible, and mix it with the bread crumbs and flour
2. Add the currants, washed and dried
3. Add the citron and orange peel cut in thin slices, and the raisins stoned and divided
4. Mix all together with the grated nutmeg and ginger
5. Stir in the 9 eggs well beaten, and the brandy, and again mix, so that all the ingredients may be moistened
6. Put it into a buttered mould or cloth
7. Tie it tightly, and boil it for six hours
