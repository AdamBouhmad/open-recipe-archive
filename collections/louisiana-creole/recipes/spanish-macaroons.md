## Ingredients

- 8 Ounces of Sweet Almonds
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- Yolks of 12 Eggs
- Grated Zest of 2 Oranges
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar almost to a syrup
2. Add the pounded almonds, and let them simmer gently for five minutes
3. Add the flavoring and let them simmer five minutes longer over a slow fire of smothered charcoal
4. Stir occasionally
5. At the end of ten minutes add the yolks of the eggs, beaten very light
6. Stir the paste over the kitchen fire with a quickened heat until it becomes firm and compact
7. Remove from the fire, and when it is cool roll it in your hands, which should be slightly greased with oil of sweet almonds
8. Lay them out in small cakes on wafer paper, spread on a baking sheet
9. Bake at a quick heat
