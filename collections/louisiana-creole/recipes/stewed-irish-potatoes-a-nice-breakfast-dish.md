## Ingredients

- six potatoes
- cold, salted water
- cold water
- half a cup of sweet milk
- a little salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- a spoonful of butter, rolled in flour or a teaspoonful of flour

## Directions

1. Wash, peel, and slice six potatoes
2. Throw them for a few moments into cold, salted water
3. Take them out in five minutes and place them in a stew pan on the fire
4. Cover them with cold water
5. When tender, throw off all the water
6. Pour over them half a cup of sweet milk, a little salt, pepper, and chopped parsley
7. Thicken them with a spoonful of butter, rolled in flour, or a teaspoonful of flour, beaten in carefully to prevent it from lumping
8. Stew a few moments and serve in a covered dish
