## Ingredients

- white clings
- one pound of sugar per pound of fruit
- half a pint of water per pound of fruit
- an egg

## Directions

1. Select white clings if you desire to preserve them whole
2. peel and weigh them
3. to each pound of fruit put one pound of sugar and half a pint of water
4. Put the syrup to boil, clarify it with an egg
5. as it boils remove the scum
6. Keep the peaches in cold water all the time the syrup is boiling
7. When the syrup has boiled clear, put in the peaches
8. let them boil gently for half an hour
9. then take them out on a dish for two hours
10. put them back in the syrup and boil again until they are clear
11. put them in jars and pour the syrup over them
12. cork and seal up for future use
