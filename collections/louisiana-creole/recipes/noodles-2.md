## Ingredients

- two eggs
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Make a stiff dough with two eggs, a little salt, and sufficient flour
2. Roll this out very thin
3. Shake on a little flour and rub it in
4. Fold the dough over and roll it up
5. Cut it fine with a knife
6. Have ready a pot almost full of boiling water into which you have put a little salt
7. Drop the noodles in and boil them for five or six hours
8. Pour the water off
9. Fry the noodles in plenty of butter
