## Ingredients

- truffles
- fat bacon
- livers of veal or fowl
- sweet herbs
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- chestnuts (sixty)
- green onions or chives
- sweet basil
- parsley
- thyme
- nutmeg
- eggs (three)

## Directions

1. Many persons like truffles for stuffing for a roast pig; they should be mixed with fat bacon, livers of veal or fowl, sweet herbs, pepper, salt and butter.
2. Chestnut stuffing is prepared by roasting sixty chestnuts.
3. Remove their hulls while hot, and pound them fine, add four ounces of butter, run this through a sieve, and add to it a few green onions, or chives, sweet basil, parsley and thyme;
4. grate in a nutmeg, put in pepper and salt, and bind it with three eggs.
5. Stuff the pig with it and serve with tomato sauce.
