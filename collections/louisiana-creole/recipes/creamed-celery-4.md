## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cut celery
- 1 cupful of milk or cream
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cut fresh, crisp celery into pieces of an inch, until you have a pint.
2. Wash thoroughly, and put in boiling water and cook until tender, which will be in about twenty-five minutes or a half hour.
3. Put a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan, and add the flour, blending without allowing to brown.
4. When perfectly smooth, add the cream or milk, and let it come to a boil.
5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
6. Drain out the celery, and add it to the sauce, and stir gently, letting it cook twenty minutes longer.
7. Serve hot. The dish should be kept covered while cooking.
