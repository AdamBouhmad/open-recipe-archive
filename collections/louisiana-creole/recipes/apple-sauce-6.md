## Ingredients

- rich, tart apples
- a very little water

## Directions

1. Peel, quarter, and core some rich, tart apples
2. Add a very little water, cover and set them over the fire
3. When tender, mash them smooth, and serve with roasted pork, goose, or any other gross meat
