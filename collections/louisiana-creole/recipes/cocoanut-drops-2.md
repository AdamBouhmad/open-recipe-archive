## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 5 cocoanuts
- 1 cup of sugar to 2 of the cocoanuts

## Directions

1. Grate the nuts fine, and dry it in a moderate oven
2. Beat up the whites of the eggs
3. Add the sugar, allowing 1 cup to 2 of the cocoanuts, and beat to a paste
4. Stir in the cocoanut, and drop in a pan or on a sheet of paper
5. Bake for 10 minutes
