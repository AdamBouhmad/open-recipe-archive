## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Pounds of Stoned Raisins
- 1 1/2 Pints of Sugar
- 1 1/2 Pints of Flour
- 3/4 Pound of Butter
- 3/4 Pint of Milk
- 6 Eggs
- 3/4 Gill of White or Sherry Wine
- 1/2 Gill of Brandy
- 2 1/2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and baking powder together.
2. Then beat the butter and sugar to a cream, add the yolks of the eggs, and beat till very light.
3. Beat the whites to a stiff froth, and then add alternately the whites and flour to the mixture, beating vigorously till light.
4. Now add the wine and brandy.
5. Flour the raisins, and shake lightly, till each becomes slightly coated on each side, and add to the cake batter, and then begin to stir lightly, stirring just sufficient to mix.
6. Have ready a deep baking pan or two, lined with buttered paper, and bake in a slightly quick oven for from forty-five minutes to an hour.
7. Have a thick layer of paper over the cake for the first half hour of baking.
