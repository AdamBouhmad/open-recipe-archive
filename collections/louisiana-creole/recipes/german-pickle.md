## Ingredients

- sound, ripe cucumbers
- 3 quarts of cucumber pieces
- 3 cups of vinegar
- 4 cups of water
- 1 quart of vinegar
- 1 pint of sugar
- cinnamon bark
- 1 teaspoonful of pimento

## Directions

1. Peel cucumbers and remove the seeds
2. Cut lengthways into strips an inch wide
3. To 3 quarts of the pieces add 3 cups of vinegar, and 4 of water
4. Soak 24 hours, stirring once or twice
5. Put 1 quart of vinegar on the fire
6. Add 1 pint of sugar, a little cinnamon bark, 1 teaspoonful of pimento, tied in a piece of cloth
7. Scald all together
8. Add the cucumbers and boil till soft
9. Put in glass jars
