## Ingredients

- a half pint of warm water
- a half pint of yeast
- 6 moderate sized potatoes
- 1 dessertspoonful of salt
- 3 pints of flour

## Directions

1. Combine the warm water and yeast
2. Add the potatoes and salt
3. Stir in 3 pints of flour lightly
