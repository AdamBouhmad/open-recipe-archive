## Ingredients

- ⅓ a Pineapple
- ⅓ Cup of Sugar
- 1 Gill of White Wine
- Cream Puff Paste

## Directions

1. Make a Cream Paste.
2. Prepare the pineapple as in the above recipe.
3. Dip in the Cream Puff Paste and proceed as in Apple Fritters.
