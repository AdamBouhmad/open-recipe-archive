## Ingredients

- corn
- eggs
- 1 tablespoonful wheat flour
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Grate the corn while green and tender with a coarse grater into a deep dish
2. To 2 ears of corn allow 1 egg
3. Beat the whites and yolks separately
4. Add them to the corn with 1 tablespoonful of wheat flour and 1 tablespoonful of butter
5. Add salt and pepper to the taste
6. Lay them in hot butter with a spoon and fry them on both sides
