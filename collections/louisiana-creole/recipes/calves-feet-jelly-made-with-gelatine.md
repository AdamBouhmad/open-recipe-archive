## Ingredients

- three quarts of water
- one pint of white wine
- six teaspoonfuls of brandy
- six lemons, juice and peel
- six eggs, the whites slightly beaten, the shells crushed
- three pounds of white sugar
- four ounces of gelatine

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine in one quart of the measured water; let it remain for one-half an hour
2. Mix the ingredients named with the other two quarts, and let all boil twenty minutes
3. Strain it through a flannel bag without squeezing
4. Wet the jelly mould in cold water
5. Pour the jelly in, and leave it to cool, or put it on ice until wanted
