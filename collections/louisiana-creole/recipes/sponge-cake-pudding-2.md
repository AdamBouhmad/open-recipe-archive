## Ingredients

- 3 stale sponge cakes
- the peel of a lemon
- the juice of a lemon
- cold milk
- 3 eggs
- a teacupful of orange juice
- a little sugar
- a thick slice of butter
- paste

## Directions

1. Grate 3 stale sponge cakes and with them the peel of a lemon, and all the juice
2. Mix them in cold milk until they are quite soft
3. Beat 3 eggs together, and stir them by degrees into the biscuits
4. Add a teacupful of orange juice, and a little sugar, a thick slice of butter well melted
5. Mix all well together
6. Put it in a dish with paste round the edges
7. Bake it slowly
