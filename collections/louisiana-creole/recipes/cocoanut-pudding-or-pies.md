## Ingredients

- a cocoanut
- the milk of the cocoanut
- white sugar
- butter
- five eggs well beaten
- a cup of milk
- the grated rind of a lemon

## Directions

1. Break a cocoanut and save the milk.
2. Peel off the brown skin, then throw each piece into cold water, and let it stay a few minutes to cool.
3. Take the pieces out, wipe dry and grate.
4. Add their own weight of white sugar and half the weight of butter.
5. Rub the butter and sugar to a cream, add five well beaten eggs, and a cup of milk.
6. Throw into the mixture the milk of the cocoanut and the grated rind of a lemon.
7. Bake in a pudding-dish, or make it into pies with a bottom crust.
8. Ornament the top of the pies with fancy twists of paste.
