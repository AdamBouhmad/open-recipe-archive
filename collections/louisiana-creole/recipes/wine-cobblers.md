## Ingredients

- 1 Wineglassful of Sherry or Any Other Wine
- A Small Piece of Lemon Peel
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sugar or Syrup

## Directions

1. Take a glass, put in a tablespoonful of sugar or syrup, a small piece of cut lemon peel, one wineglass of Sherry or any other of the above wines, and then fill the glass with crushed ice
2. Stir well
3. Decorate the mixture with sprigs of fresh mint or strawberries or sliced pineapple, and serve
