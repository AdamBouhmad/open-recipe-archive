## Ingredients

- A Bunch of Salsify
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Cup of Water
- 2 Eggs
- 1/2 Pound of Flour
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Teaspoonful of Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and scrape the salsify and boil as directed in recipe Boiled Parsnips till very tender.
2. Then cut into long, narrow strips.
3. Make a batter by mixing the flour with the yolks of the eggs, beaten well.
4. Then add the salt and pepper, and gradually one cup of water, till it is absorbed.
5. Then add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
6. Mix thoroughly.
7. Add the salsify to the batter.
8. Dip out one spoon at a time of the batter, and fry in boiling lard.
9. Serve as an entree at dinner.
