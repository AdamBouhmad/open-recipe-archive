## Ingredients

- Several bunches of fresh parsley
- Slightly salted water
- Ice

## Directions

1. Take several bunches of fresh parsley, trim off all the coarse stems.
2. Wash in slightly salted water, drain and place in a colander.
3. Set over a watertight vessel.
4. Shave some ice, cover the parsley with it and let it keep fresh and cool for table garnishes.
