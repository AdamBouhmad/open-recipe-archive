## Ingredients

- 3 Ounces of Saffron
- 1 Ounce of Alum
- 20 Ounces of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water
- alcohol

## Directions

1. Take three ounces of Saffron, one ounce of powdered Alum, twenty ounces of sugar, and one pint of water and boil all together
2. Strain and add a little alcohol, and bottle for use
