## Ingredients

- fine, large, ripe strawberries
- powdered sugar
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take fine, large, ripe strawberries; hull, or stem them, and set them on ice till just before they are wanted
2. Put a large quantity of powdered sugar in a bowl with a layer of strawberries and sugar, until the bowl is full
3. Have ready a whipped cream, which must be placed on the top of the fruit
