## Ingredients

- six eggs
- a cup of butter
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- lemon
- walnut catsup

## Directions

1. Boil six eggs hard
2. When cold, peel them and put them into a cup of butter, melted
3. Mix with a little flour, make it hot
4. Stir in pepper and salt
5. Add lemon and walnut catsup as preferred
