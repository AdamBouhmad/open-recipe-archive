## Ingredients

- One hundred oranges
- Ten gallons of boiling water
- Twenty-five pounds of lump sugar
- A few tablespoonfuls of good yeast

## Directions

1. Take the outer rind of one hundred oranges pared, so that no white appears
2. Pour upon them ten gallons of boiling water, let it stand ten hours and keep slightly warm
3. While still warm, add the juice of the oranges, mixed with twenty-five pounds of lump sugar, and a few tablespoonfuls of good yeast
4. Let it ferment five days, or until the fermentation has ceased, and the wine is clear
5. Bottle it
