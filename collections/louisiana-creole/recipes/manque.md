## Ingredients

- The Yolks of 8 Eggs
- The Whites of 3 Eggs, Beaten to a Froth
- % Pound of White Pulverized Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Cup of Flour
- The Grated Zest of 1 Lemon
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and butter and yolks of the eggs to a very light cream, and then add the grated zest of the lemon.
2. Gradually add the flour, which you will have mingled and sifted with the baking powder.
3. Then add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
4. Beat lightly and quickly, and place in a buttered cake pan, and bake for thirty minutes in a moderately quick oven.
