## Ingredients

- 6 Ears of Corn
- 3/4 Pint of Milk
- 1 Egg
- 1/2 Cup of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the batter in exactly the same manner as the Corn Fritters recipe.
2. Bake on a griddle like batter cakes, and serve hot, with generous layers of butter between.
