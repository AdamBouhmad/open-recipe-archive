## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 pint sour cream
- flour
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- 1 cup new milk
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Beat very lightly 2 eggs
2. Stir them into 1 pint of sour cream
3. Sift in as much flour as will make a stiff batter
4. Dissolve 1 teaspoonful of soda in a cup of new milk
5. Add this and a little salt
6. Mix them well and pour into rather a shallow pan
7. Bake quickly a nice brown
8. Serve hot
