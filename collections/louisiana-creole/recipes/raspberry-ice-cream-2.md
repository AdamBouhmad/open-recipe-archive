## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream
- 1 Quart of Raspberries or 2 Tablespoonfuls of Raspberry Extract
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Yolks of 6 Eggs
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Set the milk to boil, add the sugar, and when well-dissolved take from the fire, add the eggs, well beaten to a cream, and set aside to cool.
2. Then, when cool, add the remaining half of the sugar, which you will have blended with the berries, which have been mashed well, and allowed to stand for several hours, and then press through a sieve.
3. Mix well with the milk, add the lemon juice, turn into the freezer, and freeze as above directed.
4. If the berries are too pale to impart a sufficient rosy tinge to the cream, add a few drops of Raspberry Extract.
5. If not in season use two tablespoonfuls of Raspberry Extract, and proceed as in recipe for Vanilla Cream.
