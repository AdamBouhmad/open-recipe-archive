## Ingredients

- 6 Ripe Oranges
- 1 Pound Pulverized Sugar
- 1 Pound of Butter
- 14 Ounces of Flour
- 10 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Wash the oranges. Then dry and roll them under your hand on a board till very soft
2. Grate the zest of the oranges, and then cut up the fruit fine, and squeeze through a strainer till all the juice is extracted
3. Wash the butter till you extract all the salt, and then beat it to a cream
4. Add the sugar, and beat thoroughly, and then add the orange zest and juice, and beat till very light
5. Add the yolks of the eggs, well-beaten, and again beat the mixture till light
6. Then, by degrees, add the flour, adding alternately the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
7. Beat the whole for a half hour, and then bake in a moderate oven for forty-five minutes or an hour, using the broom-wisp test
8. If you ice the cake, add orange juice to the icing
