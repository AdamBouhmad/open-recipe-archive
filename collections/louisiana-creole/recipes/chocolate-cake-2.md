## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- one pound of flour
- four eggs
- one half pint of milk
- one teaspoonful of soda
- two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- three whites of eggs
- three cups of sifted sugar
- chocolate to taste

## Directions

1. Sift the cream of tartar and soda into the flour
2. Mix all ingredients well together, and bake in two cakes
3. Beat three whites of eggs with three cups of sifted sugar, and add chocolate to taste
4. Spread a layer of this icing between the cakes and on the top and sides
