## Ingredients

- kidneys
- flour
- butter
- plain beef stock, or beef gravy
- one chopped onion
- one cupful of mushrooms
- mushroom catsup

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys into slices, wash and dry them carefully
2. Pepper and salt them, roll them in flour, and fry in butter till of a delicate brown color
3. Pour some plain beef stock, or beef gravy, in the pan
4. Add a chopped onion, and stew for half an hour
5. Put in a cupful of mushrooms, and cook for fifteen minutes
6. Mushroom catsup will serve as a substitute. Use one-half the quantity of catsup
