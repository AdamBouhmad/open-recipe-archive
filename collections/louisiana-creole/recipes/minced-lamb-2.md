## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Cold Minced Lamb
- 1 Can of Mushrooms
- 4 Ounces of Butter
- 1/2 Pint of Veal Stock
- 1 1/2 Gills of Cream
- Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 1 chopped onion
- 1 tablespoonful of blended flour
- Croûtons (buttered)

## Directions

1. Place the butter in a frying pan
2. Add one chopped onion and brown slightly
3. Add the mushrooms, and season to taste
4. Add the half pint of veal broth, if you have it; if not, boiling water or milk, and let it simmer a few minutes
5. Thicken with a tablespoonful of blended flour
6. Add the minced lamb and a gill and a half of cream
7. Let all simmer, stirring constantly
8. When done, which will be in about ten minutes, take off the fire
9. Add the yolks of two eggs, beaten, and stir constantly
10. Place in a hot dish, garnish with Croûtons (buttered) and serve
