## Ingredients

- A pound of flour
- Six ounces of finely chopped suet
- Four eggs
- Milk to make a firm batter

## Directions

1. Add by degrees six ounces of finely chopped suet to a pound of flour
2. Add four eggs, together with as much milk as will make a firm batter
3. Beat all together hard, until the last moment before placing it in the oven
4. Pour it into a buttered dish
5. Bake
6. Serve as soon as done, with plain syrup, or butter and sugar sauce
