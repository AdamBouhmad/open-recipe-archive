## Ingredients

- 1.5 ounces isinglass
- 2 lemons
- noyeau
- cream

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1.5 ounces of isinglass
2. Add 2 lemons and as much noyeau as pleasant to the taste
3. Add enough cream to fill the mould, and whisk it well
