## Ingredients

- 3 Pounds of Veal Brisket
- 2 Onions
- 1 Carrot
- 3/4 Can of Mushrooms
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- Yolks of 2 Eggs
- Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1/2 Gallon of Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Take a brisket of veal and cut into pieces of two square inches
2. Put in a stew pot and cover with a half gallon of water, and add salt and pepper and two onions and one carrot, chopped fine
3. Let it boil till very tender
4. Take the meat out of the saucepan, and keep the water in which it was boiled
5. Take another saucepan and put a tablespoonful of butter in it, and as it melts add a tablespoonful of flour
6. Mix well, continuing to dissolve till it becomes a smooth cream; do not let it brown
7. Add one pint of the water in which the veal was boiled
8. Stir well, making it very light, and not thick
9. Add one-half can of mushrooms, and let the whole boil about fifteen minutes, so as to be very light
10. Put in the veal, which is already cooked
11. Let it simmer for about fifteen minutes longer, and take off the fire
12. Add the yolks of two eggs, well beaten, with two tablespoonfuls of the gravy and the juice of one lemon
13. Serve hot
