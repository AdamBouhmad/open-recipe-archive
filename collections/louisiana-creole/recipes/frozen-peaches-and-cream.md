## Ingredients

- nice soft, ripe peaches
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. Peel and stone nice soft, ripe peaches
2. Sprinkle enough sugar on them to make them very sweet
3. Chop them up fine until they are a pulp
4. Add to them as much cream as you have peaches
5. Put them into the freezer and turn it briskly until the cream is well frozen
