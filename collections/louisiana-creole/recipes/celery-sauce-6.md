## Ingredients

- bunch of celery
- water
- pint of milk
- spoonful of butter
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Mash a bunch of celery
2. Boil it soft in water
3. Cut the sticks into pieces 2 inches long
4. Make a sauce with a pint of milk, a spoonful of butter, flour, and salt to taste
5. Put the celery in and let it boil up once
6. Serve
