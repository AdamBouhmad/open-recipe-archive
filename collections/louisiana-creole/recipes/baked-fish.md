## Ingredients

- 1 large fish weighing 5 pounds or more
- sweet lard
- 24 or 30 cloves
- mace
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1 small teaspoonful of cayenne
- 2 teaspoonfuls of black pepper
- 12 balls of butter about the size of a walnut
- 2 handfuls of chopped onion
- 1 handful of parsley
- 1 handful of bread crumbs
- egg
- pork
- 1 pint of water
- 1/2 pint red wine

## Directions

1. Take a large fish, weighing 5 pounds, or more
2. Scue the sides, and brown in a frying pan with a little sweet lard
3. Pound fine 24 or 30 cloves, a little mace, a tablespoonful of salt, a small teaspoonful of cayenne, 2 teaspoonfuls of black pepper, 12 balls of butter about the size of a walnut, each rolled in flour, 2 handfuls of chopped onion, 1 handful of parsley, 1 handful of bread crumbs
4. Fill the fish and sew up
5. Rub over the outside with egg, and cover with remainder of dressing
6. Put some slices of pork in the pan
7. Put the fish on, and set in the oven till brown
8. Add 1 pint of water, and bake 2 hours, basting it
9. 15 minutes before taking up, add 1/2 pint red wine
