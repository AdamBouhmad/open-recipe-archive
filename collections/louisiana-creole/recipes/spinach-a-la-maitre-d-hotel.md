## Ingredients

- ½ Peck of Spinach
- 1 Cupful of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- A Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel

## Directions

1. Prepare the spinach as directed in recipe Plain Boiled Spinach.
2. Pour over a Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel, cook a few minutes, and serve hot.
