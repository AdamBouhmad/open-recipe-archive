## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 6 eggs
- 6 spoonfuls of flour
- 1/2 spoonful of butter
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. add 6 eggs, well beaten, 6 spoonfuls of flour, 1/2 spoonful of butter, 1 nutmeg, and 1 teaspoonful of salt to 1 pint of milk
2. pour it into a well greased pan, and bake for 1 hour
3. to be eaten with wine, sugar and butter sauce
