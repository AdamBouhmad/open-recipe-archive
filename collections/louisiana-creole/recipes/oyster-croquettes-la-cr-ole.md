## Ingredients

- 3 Dozen Fine Oysters
- 1 Cup of the White Meat of a Chicken Minced
- 6 Finely Chopped Mushrooms
- 1 Teaspoonful of Onion Juice
- 3/4 Cup of Cream
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Tablespoonful of Parsley
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- Salt and Cayenne Pepper to Taste
- Butter

## Directions

1. Boil the oysters about three minutes in their own liquor. Drain and chop the oysters fine.
2. Take a half cup of the liquor in which the oysters have been boiled, set it on the fire and add the chopped oysters.
3. Then add the half cup of cream, the chopped mushrooms and the minced chicken.
4. Stir thoroughly into this boiling mixture the butter and flour which have been rubbed smoothly.
5. Add the chopped parsley, onion Juice, salt and Cayenne, and mix well.
6. Then add the yolks of the eggs, well beaten.
7. Let it cook about two minutes and turn it out into a dish to cool.
8. When cold, roll Into cylinders about two inches in length and one inch In diameter.
9. Pass through bread crumbs and fry in boiling lard.
10. Serve immediately on a bed of fried parsley.
