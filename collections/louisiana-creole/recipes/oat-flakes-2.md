## Ingredients

- 1 Teacupful of Oat Flakes
- 1 Quart of Boiling Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- sugar and cream, or simply cream or milk

## Directions

1. Put the quart of water into a porcelain-lined saucepan, and let it come to a boil
2. Add the salt, and when it is boiling stir in gradually the oat flakes
3. Keep stirring to prevent burning
4. Let the preparation boil for about fifteen or twenty minutes
5. Serve with sugar and cream, or simply cream or milk
