## Ingredients

- the cup cake recipe above
- 1 pint of fresh milk, boiling hot
- 2 tablespoons of cornstarch dissolved in a little cold milk
- grated cocoanut

## Directions

1. bake the cake in jelly pans, or whole and sliced
2. dissolve cornstarch in cold milk
3. add to boiling milk
4. stir continually until as thick as blanc mange
5. put on a layer of this and grated cocoanut
