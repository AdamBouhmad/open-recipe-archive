## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- cream
- 1 large spoonful finely powdered sugar
- 1 quart thick cream
- bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Break 5 eggs; beat the yolks with some cream and a large spoonful of finely powdered sugar
2. Put into a saucepan 1 quart of thick cream, with a few bitter almonds, and set it on the fire
3. When it has once boiled, take out the almonds, and add the beaten eggs
4. Stir all together, and keep it hot for some time, without allowing it to boil, observing to stir always the same way
5. When thick enough, pour it into cups for the table
