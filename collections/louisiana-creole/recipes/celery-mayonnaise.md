## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Crisp White Celery
- A Mayonnaise Sauce

## Directions

1. Chop the celery, or rather cut fine, as indicated in the above recipe
2. Mix the Mayonnaise with it
3. Garnish nicely with celery tips and serve
