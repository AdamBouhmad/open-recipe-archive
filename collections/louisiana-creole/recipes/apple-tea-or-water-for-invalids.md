## Ingredients

- ripe apples
- peel of a fresh lemon
- boiling water
- loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Cut some ripe apples into thin pieces
2. add the peel of a fresh lemon
3. pour boiling water over them
4. let it stand till cold
5. then sweeten with loaf sugar
