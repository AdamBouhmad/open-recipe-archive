## Ingredients

- 10 fine, fresh Creole eggs
- 1 quart of milk
- 1 pound of white granulated sugar
- 1 gill of fine French Cognac
- 1 grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks to a cream, add the sugar, and beat to a cream.
2. Blend all thoroughly, beating till very, very light.
3. Pour over the boiling milk, stirring well.
4. When thoroughly blended add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, and the liquor, and serve hot.
