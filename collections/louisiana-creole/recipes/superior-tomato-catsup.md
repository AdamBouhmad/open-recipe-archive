## Ingredients

- a bushel of ripe tomatoes
- a pint and a half of salt
- four tablespoonfuls of ground cloves
- four tablespoonfuls of cayenne pepper
- a quarter of a pound of allspice
- a tablespoonful of black pepper
- a head of garlic skinned and separated
- a half gallon of vinegar

## Directions

1. Get a bushel of ripe tomatoes, scald them until they are soft enough to squeeze through a sieve
2. When strained, add to the pulp a pint and a half of salt, four tablespoonfuls of ground cloves, same of cayenne pepper, a quarter of a pound of allspice and a tablespoonful of black pepper, a head of garlic skinned and separated, and a half gallon of vinegar
3. Boil until it is reduced one-half, then bottle
