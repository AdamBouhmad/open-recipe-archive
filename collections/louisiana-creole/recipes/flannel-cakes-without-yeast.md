## Ingredients

- 1 quart buttermilk
- wheat flour
- 1 large tablespoonful butter
- a little salt
- 2 teaspoonfuls carbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Thicken 1 quart of buttermilk with wheat flour
2. Add 1 large tablespoonful of butter, a little salt, and 2 teaspoonfuls of carbonate of soda
3. Pour on a griddle iron or in rings
