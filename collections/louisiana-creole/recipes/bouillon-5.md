## Ingredients

- 6 pounds of beef, without bone or fat
- 6 quarts of cold water
- 4 cloves
- allspice
- a small cup of fresh or canned tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- 1 spoon of celery seed
- 1 bay leaf
- a piece of red pepper pod, without seeds
- 1 clove of garlic
- a sprig of thyme
- 1 onion
- 1 small bunch of celery
- 1 medium-sized carrot, chopped fine

## Directions

1. Put the beef and water into the soup kettle and bring to a boil
2. Add cloves, allspice, tomatoes, salt, celery seed, bay leaf, red pepper pod, and garlic
3. Set aside and let it simmer gently, never allowing the soup to crack
4. After two and a half hours add a sprig of thyme, onion cut in pieces, small bunch of celery, and medium-sized carrot chopped fine
5. Replace the cover and let the bouillon boil gently for two and one-half hours more, making five hours of actual boiling when not intended for invalid use
6. If you decide not to clarify the soup, set it aside and let it settle, then carefully pour off the upper portion without disturbing the sediment
7. Add about a tablespoonful of celery and a little cayenne
