## Ingredients

- a pound of the best white sugar
- a pound of blanched almonds
- a few drops of rose-water
- the well-beaten whites of six eggs
- a little flour
- white sugar

## Directions

1. To a pound of the best white sugar, sifted, add a pound of blanched almonds
2. put in a few drops of rose-water as you beat them together in a mortar
3. Add to them the well-beaten whites of six eggs
4. form the paste into shapes in the palm of the hand by using a little flour
5. butter some sheets of white paper, and drop the macaroons on it, leaving a space between them
6. Strew a little white sugar on them, and put in the oven to bake a light brown
