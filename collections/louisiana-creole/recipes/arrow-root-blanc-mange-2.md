## Ingredients

- two tablespoonfuls of arrow-root
- cold water
- a pint of milk
- white sugar
- colored jelly

## Directions

1. Mix in a little cold water, two tablespoonfuls of arrow-root
2. sweeten a pint of milk with white sugar and put the arrow-root in the milk
3. Let it boil a few minutes, stirring it constantly
4. take it off, and if desired, you can let it cool and mould it in a bowl or jelly form
5. or it is nice to be eaten warm
6. Colored jelly over it is an improvement when moulded
