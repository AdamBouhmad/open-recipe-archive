## Ingredients

- 4 fresh lemons
- 1 quart of water
- sugar
- whites of 6 eggs
- yolks of 6 eggs
- macaroons

## Directions

1. Pare the rind very thin from 4 fresh lemons
2. Squeeze the juice and strain it
3. Pour them both into 1 quart of water
4. Sweeten to your taste
5. Add the whites of 6 eggs beaten to a froth
6. Put it on the fire and keep stirring it until it thickens, but do not let it boil
7. Pour it into a bowl
8. When cold, strain it through a sieve
9. Put it on the fire again and add the yolks of the eggs
10. Stir until quite thick
11. When cold, serve up in small glasses
12. On each glass lay a nice macaroon
