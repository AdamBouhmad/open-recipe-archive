## Ingredients

- 1 Onion
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Pickle a Finger Long
- 1 Teaspoonful of Mustard
- 2 Gills of Consomme
- Salt and Cayenne
- 1 Glass of White Wine
- Juice of a Lemon

## Directions

1. Brown the onion in butter, and add the two cloves of garlic, minced very fine.
2. When brown, add one pickle, minced very fine, and add a teaspoonful of mustard prepared.
3. Then add two gills of consomme and one glass of White Wine, and the juice of a lemon, and allow it to cook slowly.
4. Season with salt and hot pepper (piment fort), and serve with shell fish, chicken, sweetbreads, etc.
5. This is a hot sauce.
