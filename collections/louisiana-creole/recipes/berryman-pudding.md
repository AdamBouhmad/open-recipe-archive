## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of best white sugar
- 8 eggs
- 8 ounces of butter
- A soup plate of cornmeal mush
- Juice and rind of 2 lemons

## Directions

1. Mix all well together till very light
2. Bake in a moderate oven
