## Ingredients

- ½ Pound of Brains
- 1 Onion
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Brown Butter Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the brains in exactly the same manner as indicated in the above recipe.
2. Serve with Brown Butter Sauce.
