## Ingredients

- Cress
- Vinegar
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare in exactly the same manner as lettuce, washing and bringing to the table firm and crisp
2. In this salad use for dressing only Tarragon vinegar, salt and pepper to taste
