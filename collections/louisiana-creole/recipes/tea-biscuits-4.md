## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 1/2 pint milk
- 3 eggs
- 2 ounces butter or lard
- 1 teaspoonful sugar
- 1/4 cake of compressed yeast

## Directions

1. Scald the milk and add the butter
2. Set to cool, and then add the salt and the sugar and yeast
3. Add one-half of the flour
4. Mix well, and then beat rapidly for about five or ten minutes
5. Cover up the pan with a cloth, and set in a warm place for about two hours
6. Take up, add the well-beaten eggs and the remainder of the flour
7. Make a soft dough, and knead lightly but constantly for about twenty minutes
8. If the dough is then elastic, form into loaves and set to rise in a warm place
9. When it has increased to twice its bulk, which will be in about two hours, pinch off into small pieces about half the size of a hen's egg, and mold into a small ball
10. Place all in a pan about two inches apart, and cover again for half an hour
11. Bake in a moderate oven for about a quarter of an hour to a nice brown
