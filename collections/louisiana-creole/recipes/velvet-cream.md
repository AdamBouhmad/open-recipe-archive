## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- yolks of 6 eggs
- 6 tablespoonfuls sugar
- 1 spoonful flour
- whites of 6 eggs
- 6 tablespoonfuls powdered sugar
- vanilla
- lemon

## Directions

1. Boil a quart of milk
2. When boiling, stir into it the yolks of 6 eggs, 6 tablespoonfuls of sugar, 1 spoonful of flour, which has been well beaten together
3. When boiled, turn into a dish, and pour over it the whites beaten to a stiff froth, and mixed with 6 tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar, and brown slightly
4. Flavor the top with vanilla and the bottom with lemon
