## Ingredients

- ¾ Cup of Rice
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 2 Large Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- ½ Cup of Currants
- ½ Cup of Raisins
- ¾ Cup of Citron
- ¾ Teaspoonful of Vanilla Essence
- The Yolks of Two Eggs

## Directions

1. Put the milk into a farina boiler, and add the rice which you will have washed well.
2. Boil until very thick.
3. Seed the raisins and prepare the other fruits.
4. Beat the eggs well and add them to the rice, and then stir in the sugar.
5. Beat until very smooth.
6. Then take from the fire, and add the essence of vanilla, the raisins and currants, and the citron.
7. Turn out all into a dish to cool.
8. Then form into pretty pyramids, and dip first in a well-beaten egg, and then in bread crumbs.
9. Fry in boiling lard.
10. Drain well.
11. When about to serve, put a small piece of Currant Jelly on the top of each croquette.
12. Then dust the whole with powdered sugar, and serve with Sauce a la Vanille.
