## Ingredients

- one quart of flour
- one half pint of milk
- one quarter of a pound of butter
- two eggs
- mace
- nutmeg
- a cup of yeast

## Directions

1. Add mace, nutmeg and a cup of yeast to the flour, milk, butter and eggs
2. Set it to rise
3. Make up into rusks
4. Bake on buttered tins when light
5. Serve hot
