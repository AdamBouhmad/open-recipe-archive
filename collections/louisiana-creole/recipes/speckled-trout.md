## Ingredients

- Filets of Speckled Trout
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Parsley
- Sliced Lemon
- Pepper and Salt
- Sweet oil

## Directions

1. Skin and cut the fish, after cleaning, into filets or square pieces, slicing across the back
2. Remove all the bones and rub well with pepper and salt, and a little sweet oil mixed with the pepper and salt
3. Place on the gridiron or double broiler and broil carefully, turning the broiler frequently to prevent the fish being charred before it is done
4. When nicely browned, place on a bed of parsley and garnish with sliced lemon
5. Serve with Sauce a la Tartare
