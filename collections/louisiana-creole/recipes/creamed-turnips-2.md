## Ingredients

- 6 Turnips
- Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Cut large turnips in quarters for this dish.
2. Small ones may be boiled whole.
3. When tender, drain and put into a colander and press out all water.
4. Make a rich Cream Sauce and pour over the turnips.
5. Serve with boiled leg of mutton, either by placing the turnips around the leg of mutton on the dish and pouring the sauce over, or separate, as a vegetable.
