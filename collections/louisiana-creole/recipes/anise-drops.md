## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Sugar
- 6 Eggs
- 1 Quart of Flour
- 0.25 Teaspoonful of Essence of Anise

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and eggs for about half an hour
2. Add the flour gradually, and the essence
3. Beat well
4. Have ready buttered sheets of tin, and drop the mixture from the spoon, and bake to a delicate brown
