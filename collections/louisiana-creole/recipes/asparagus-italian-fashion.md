## Ingredients

- asparagus
- sweet oil
- water
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Take some asparagus, break them in pieces
2. Boil them soft and drain the water off
3. Take a little sweet oil, water and vinegar
4. Let it boil
5. Season it with pepper and salt
6. Pour it over the asparagus
