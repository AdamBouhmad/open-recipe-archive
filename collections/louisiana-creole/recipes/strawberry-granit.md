## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Strawberries
- 1 Tablespoonful of Strawberry Extract
- 1.5 Pounds of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Mash the berries and cover with the sugar, and set aside to form a syrup.
2. Then drain and press through a sieve, extracting all the juice.
3. Add the extract.
4. Mix all with the water, and turn into a freezer and freeze till a light, snowy frosting appears.
5. Then serve in long, thin glasses.
