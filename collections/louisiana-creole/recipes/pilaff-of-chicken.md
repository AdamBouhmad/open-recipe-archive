## Ingredients

- 1 Chicken About Four Pounds
- 0.5 Cup of Rice
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the chicken as you would for a fricassee.
2. Put in a stewpan and cover well with water.
3. Add salt and pepper to taste, having previously rubbed the fowl with salt and pepper.
4. Let the chicken simmer gently for about an hour.
5. Take a half cup of rice and wash it thoroughly.
6. Add it to the chicken.
7. Salt again to taste.
8. Cover and let all simmer for about twenty minutes longer.
9. Make a Tomato Sauce.
10. Dish the chicken and rice together, setting the chicken in the center of the dish and the rice around for a border.
11. Serve hot.
