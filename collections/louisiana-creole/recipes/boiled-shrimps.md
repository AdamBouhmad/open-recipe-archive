## Ingredients

- 100 Fine River Shrimp
- A Large Bunch of Celery and Celery Tops
- 2 Dozen Allspice
- 2 Blades of Mace
- 1 Dozen Cloves
- 4 Sprigs each of Thyme, Parsley and Bay Leaf
- 1 Red Pepper Pod
- Cayenne
- Black Pepper
- Salt
- Parsley Sprigs to Garnish

## Directions

1. Select fine large River shrimp for this purpose
2. Into a pot of water put a great quantity of salt, almost enough to make a brine
3. Pepper a great bunch of celery and celery tops, chopped fine
4. Add two dozen allspice, two blades of mace, one dozen cloves, mashed fine
5. Add thyme, parsley, bay leaf, chopped fine
6. Add Cayenne and a red pepper pod
7. When this has boiled so that all the flavor of the herbs have been thoroughly extracted, throw in the shrimp
8. Let them boil ten minutes and then set the pot aside and let the shrimp cool in their own water
9. Serve in a platter on a bed of cracked ice, and garnish with parsley sprigs
