## Ingredients

- 1 Fine Squirrel or Rabbit
- 2 Slices or 1/2 Pound of Lean Ham
- 3 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Large Onion
- 3 Dozen Oysters
- 2 Quarts of Oyster Water
- 1/2 Pod of Red Pepper, Without the Seeds
- A Dash of Cayenne
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Skin, clean and cut up the squirrel or rabbit, as for a fricassee
2. Dredge well with salt and black pepper
3. Cut the ham into dice shapes, and chop the onion, parsley and thyme very fine
4. Put the lard or butter into a deep stew pot and, when hot, put in the squirrel or rabbit
5. Cover closely and fry for about eight or ten minutes
6. Then proceed in exactly the same manner as for Chicken Gumbo
7. Add the file at the time indicated
8. Serve with boiled Louisiana rice
