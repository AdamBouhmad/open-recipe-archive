## Ingredients

- a dozen fine crisp cucumbers
- four large onions
- salt
- pepper
- boiling vinegar
- mace
- nutmeg
- ginger

## Directions

1. Take a dozen fine crisp cucumbers and four large onions
2. Cut both in thick slices
3. Sprinkle salt and pepper on them and let them stand
4. Next day drain them well and scald them in boiling vinegar; cover close after scalding
5. Next day scald again with a bag of mace, nutmeg and ginger in the vinegar
6. Place them in jars and cork close
7. If the vinegar seems to have lost its strength, replace with fresh, and put the bag of spices in again to keep the flavor
