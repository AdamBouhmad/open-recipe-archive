## Ingredients

- Six eggs
- 8 tablespoonfuls of sifted flour
- 1 quart of milk
- 1 saltspoon of salt
- Pippins, pared, cored and sweetened
- Yolk of 1 egg
- Butter
- Sugar
- A little wine

## Directions

1. Stir the flour gradually into the milk, carefully dissolving all the lumps
2. Beat the eggs very light and add them by degrees to the milk and flour
3. Pour the batter over a dish of pippins, pared, cored and sweetened, mixing all well together
4. Bake it
5. Eat it with a rich sauce, made of the yolk of 1 egg, butter, sugar and a little wine
