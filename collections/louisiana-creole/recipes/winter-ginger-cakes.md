## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 pint of sweet cream
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- 2 1/2 ounces of ginger, powdered
- 2 lbs. of flour

## Directions

1. Break 3 eggs in a bowl and beat them well
2. Add 1 pint of sweet cream, which must be beaten with them, and the whole put into a saucepan over the fire
3. Stir till it gets warm
4. Add 1 lb. of butter and 1 lb. of loaf sugar and 2 1/2 ounces of ginger, both powdered
5. Stir the whole carefully over the fire, just to melt the butter
6. Pour it on to 2 lbs. of flour and form it all into a paste
7. Roll it or break it into pieces as you think proper and bake it
