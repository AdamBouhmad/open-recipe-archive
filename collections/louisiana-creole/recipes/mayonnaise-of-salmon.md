## Ingredients

- 1 can of Salmon
- 2 good sized heads of young lettuce
- 1 cupful of boiling milk
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonful of flour
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- pepper and salt
- Sauce a la Mayonnaise
- sliced hard-boiled eggs
- celery tips

## Directions

1. Make a bed of the crisp hearts of the lettuce leaves and tear the other leaves into small pieces with a fork, making very delicate shreds
2. Drain the oil from the can of Salmon and separate the fish into flakes
3. Take a cupful of boiling milk and a tablespoonful of butter and two of flour and stir over the fire until quite smooth
4. Add to this a tablespoonful of chopped parsley and the Salmon
5. Season with pepper and salt and let it cook about ten minutes
6. Set away to cool and then place on the bed of lettuce leaves
7. Cover with a Sauce a la Mayonnaise and garnish nicely with sliced hard-boiled eggs and celery tips
