## Ingredients

- two dozen crabs
- yolks of two hard boiled eggs
- thyme
- parsley
- onion
- butter
- black pepper
- cayenne
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil two dozen crabs
2. Pick out all the meat
3. Chop the yolks of two hard boiled eggs, some thyme, parsley and onion, slightly fried in butter
4. Add a lump of butter size of a hen's egg, and mix well
5. Add a little black pepper and cayenne before putting back in shell
6. Sprinkle over with bread crumbs
7. Put a bit of butter on top
8. Bake them brown
