## Ingredients

- a dozen fresh, tender ears of corn
- cold water
- butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Cut corn off the cob, and put it in a stew pan with a quart of cold water
2. No salt at first
3. Let it cook half an hour and then stir in a lump of fresh butter, a spoonful of flour, and salt and pepper to taste
4. If too dry, add a cup of sweet milk, or water, if the milk is not convenient
