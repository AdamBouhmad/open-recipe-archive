## Ingredients

- 8 Nice Potatoes
- A Pot of Boiling Water
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Chopped Parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes well, scrubbing thoroughly, to take off every particle of earth that adheres.
2. Then put them in a potato steamer, and set over a pot of boiling water. Cover tight and steam till you can pierce with a fork.
3. When done, remove the jackets, or skin, and sprinkle with salt and pepper; add a tablespoonful of butter in which you have mingled chopped parsley; and serve immediately.
