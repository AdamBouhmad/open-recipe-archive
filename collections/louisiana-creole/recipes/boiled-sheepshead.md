## Ingredients

- A 3-Pound Sheepshead
- 1 Sliced Onion
- 3 Bay Leaves
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Sprig of Parsley
- 1 Sprig of Sweet Marjoram
- 1 Tablespoonful French Vinegar
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- A Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and thoroughly clean the fish, and then lay on a pan and open the flesh on either side by making the letter "S" with the knife
2. Tie the fish well with cord or twine, wrapping it around the body
3. Put the fish in a deep saucepan and add boiling water sufficient for the fish to swim in it
4. Throw in a tablespoonful of spice (well mashed), a sliced onion, three bay leaves, a sprig of parsley (all chopped very fine), a tablespoonful of French vinegar, and salt and pepper to taste
5. Cover well and let it boil for ten minutes
6. Prepare a Cream Sauce and serve immediately
