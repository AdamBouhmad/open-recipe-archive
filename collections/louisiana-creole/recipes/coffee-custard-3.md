## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- five spoonfuls of white sugar
- four eggs
- half a cup of corn starch
- cold milk
- a cup of hot strong coffee
- sponge cake

## Directions

1. Boil one quart of milk with five spoonfuls of white sugar
2. Beat four eggs separately
3. Throw the whites into the boiling milk for two minutes and dip them out with a skimmer as soon as they are cooked
4. Beat the four yolks of the eggs with half a cup of corn starch wet with a little cold milk
5. Set it aside until you can put into the hot milk a cup of hot strong coffee
6. Pour in the mixed corn starch and eggs, give it a little boil and take it off
7. Place the pure white boiled eggs on the rich brown custard
8. Serve with sponge cake
