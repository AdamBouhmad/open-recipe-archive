## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Beef without fat or bone
- 2 Cups of Okra, chopped fine
- 1/2 Pound of Butter
- 4 Quarts of Cold Water
- 1 Onion, sliced and chopped
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the beef into small pieces
2. Season well with butter, pepper and salt
3. Fry it in the soup kettle with the onion and butter, until very brown
4. Add the cold water and let it simmer for an hour and a half
5. Add the okra and let it simmer gently for three or four hours longer
