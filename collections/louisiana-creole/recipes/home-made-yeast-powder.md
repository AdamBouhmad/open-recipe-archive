## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. cream of tartar
- 1 lb. soda

## Directions

1. Mix thoroughly 2 lbs. of cream of tartar and 1 lb. of soda
2. Use 1 teaspoonful of the mixture to 1 pint of flour, sifting it into the dry flour
