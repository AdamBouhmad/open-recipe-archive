## Ingredients

- 2 ounces isinglass
- 3 quarts water
- 1.5 lbs. powdered sugar
- 4 lemons
- 1 pint good wine
- 3 eggs
- mace

## Directions

1. Combine 2 ounces of isinglass, 3 quarts of water, 1.5 lbs. of powdered sugar, 4 lemons, 1 pint of good wine, 3 eggs, mace to the taste
