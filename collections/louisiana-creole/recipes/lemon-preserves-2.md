## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sugar for Every Pound of Lemons
- The Juice of 1 Lemon for Every Pound of Fruit
- A Tablespoonful of Alum for Every Quart of Water
- Salt
- Ginger Root

## Directions

1. Cut the lemon in halves, after paring, and remove the seeds.
2. Then cut it up into a number of thin slices, and put them into a vessel with cold water, adding a tablespoonful of alum to every quart of water, or a little salt.
3. After several hours put the lemon into cool, fresh water, and let it remain an hour, and drain.
4. Then put it into a kettle and cover with two quarts of boiling water.
5. Add the rinds of the lemon or citron, and a few pieces of ginger root.
6. Let all boil till perfectly clear.
7. Then take out the fruit and drain dry.
8. Then prepare a syrup, allowing one pound of sugar for every pound of fruit, and add the juice of a lemon for each pound of fruit.
9. Put the citron or lemon in without the ginger, and allow it to boil for three-quarters of an hour.
10. It will become a beautiful, clear amber color.
11. Proceed to bottle or seal in jars immediately.
