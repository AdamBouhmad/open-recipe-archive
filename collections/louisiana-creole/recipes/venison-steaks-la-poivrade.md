## Ingredients

- 6 Filets or as Many Filets as Desired
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Sauce Poivrade
- Butter
- Chopped Parsley

## Directions

1. Take the filets or Venison steaks from any part of the Venison. The best are from the haunch or leg, and cut three-quarters of an inch in thickness.
2. Rub them well with salt and pepper.
3. Fry in butter, allowing about five minutes to the steak.
4. Place in a heated dish, and garnish with melted butter and chopped parsley.
5. Serve with a Sauce Poivrade for Venison, pouring the sauce over the steaks.
