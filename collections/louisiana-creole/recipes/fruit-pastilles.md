## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 Ounces of Sugar
- 1/2 Ounce of Any Fruit Juice

## Directions

1. Make in exactly the same manner as Orange Pastilles
2. Use one-half an ounce of juice and three and a half ounces of sugar, and add a little more sugar if it appears liquid after taking from the fire
