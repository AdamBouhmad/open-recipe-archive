## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of powdered sugar
- whites of 9 eggs
- grated cocoanut

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of eggs to a froth
2. The sugar should be worked in slowly with a wooden spoon
3. Have ready a pan well greased and floured
4. Drop a tablespoonful and sprinkle well with grated cocoanut
5. Bake in a slow oven till a light brown
