## Ingredients

- 3 Oranges
- The Zest of 3 Oranges
- 1 Pint of Brandy
- 1 Pound of Sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the zest of three fine Louisiana oranges, very, very fine.
2. Place this oil or zest which you will have rubbed on one pound of powdered sugar in a jar.
3. Add one pint of good brandy or alcohol, and the juice of three fine oranges, from which you will have taken out the seeds.
4. Let the infusion stand for three days.
5. Filter and bottle for use.
