## Ingredients

- one pound of fine loaf sugar
- half a pint of cold water
- six ounces of grated chocolate
- vanilla

## Directions

1. add one pound of fine loaf sugar to half a pint of cold water
2. boil over a brisk fire until the sugar, when pressed with the fingers, presents the appearance of strong glue
3. add six ounces of grated chocolate
4. flavor with vanilla
