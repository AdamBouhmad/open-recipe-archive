## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Sifted Flour
- 1 Large Heaping Kitchenspoonful of Butter or Lard
- ¾ Cup of Tepid Water
- ½ Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Blend the salt and the flour, and then gradually mix in the butter, if for apple dumplings or rolls.
2. Use lard if intended for dumplings for chicken stews, veal stews, etc.
3. Add the water by degrees, using good judgment always, and lifting the dough out as you wet it, never wetting twice in the same place.
4. Mix and knead lightly, and then sift a light coating of flour on the board, and roll out with the rolling pin, and use for all purposes in this book where Plain Paste is indicated.
5. If necessary to keep the paste awhile, place in a napkin in the ice box.
