## Ingredients

- 6 Grains of Saffron
- 6 Grains of Musk
- 6 Grains of Ambergris
- 1/2 Drachm of Basil Seed
- 1/2 Drachm of Orange Peel
- 18 Grains of Mace
- 18 Grains of Nutmeg
- 1/2 Drachm of Storax Calamite
- 1/2 Pound of Sugar
- Gum Tragacanth

## Directions

1. Pound the Musk, Storax and Ambergris to a sugar, so as to extract all the resinous properties
2. Make the whole into a paste with a half pound of sugar and just enough Gum Tragacanth to give it sufficient consistency
3. Mix the paste thoroughly and then cut into small tablets that will weigh about a drachm each
