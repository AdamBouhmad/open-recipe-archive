## Ingredients

- 6 slices of ham
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of lard
- parsley

## Directions

1. Soak the ham in hot water before frying.
2. Cut slices of about half an inch in thickness, of sufficient size to lay an egg upon them.
3. Lay the ham in the hot frying pan, and let it fry until the fat becomes transparent.
4. Take the slices out and put them on a hot dish.
5. Break the eggs, one by one, into a saucer, and slip them into the frying pan.
6. Fry in the same lard in which you have fried the ham.
7. When the yolks are quite set, take them out, and lay one egg on each slice of ham.
8. Garnish nicely with parsley, and serve hot.
