## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Chocolate
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 1/4 Cup of Milk
- 1 Tablespoonfull of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Set the sugar to boil
2. Scrape the chocolate in the milk, and add it to the boiling sugar, and stir in the butter
3. Then boil till it forms a syrup
4. Take from the fire and turn upon a greased slab or upon a buttered dish or pan that is quite square
5. When it begins to cool sufficient to receive an impression from the finger, cut into small squares with the case-knife and set in a cool, dry place to harden
6. A good test as to whether the mixture is done is to drop a little from the end of a spoon into a cup of cold water. If it begins to harden instantly, it is ready to take from the stove
