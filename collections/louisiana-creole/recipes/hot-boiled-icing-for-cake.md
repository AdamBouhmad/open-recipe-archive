## Ingredients

- one pint of powdered sugar
- two large tablespoonfuls of water
- whites of four eggs
- lemon or vanilla

## Directions

1. Dissolve one pint of powdered sugar in two large tablespoonfuls of water
2. set it on the fire to boil
3. While this syrup is heating on the stove, beat the whites of four eggs to a strong froth
4. take off the boiling syrup, and beat it to the white of eggs, holding it high over the pans, and pouring it in a stream on the eggs
5. flavor with lemon, or vanilla
6. spread it on the cake, while the icing is warm
7. set the cake for a few moments in the oven to harden the icing
