## Ingredients

- 1 Calf's Liver
- 1 Carrot
- 1 Onion
- 1 Turnip
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Pint of Broth or Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- White Wine or Sherry

## Directions

1. Wash the liver and lard it well with needles
2. Put a tablespoonful of lard or butter into the frying pan
3. When hot, add immediately the onion, carrot and turnip, all sliced very fine
4. Add the fine herbs, nicely minced
5. Let these brown and add the liver
6. Pour over this about two spoons of White Wine or one of Sherry
7. Add about a pint of consomme or boiling water
8. Season highly
9. Cover the saucepan well and set back on the fire
10. Let it simmer for about half an hour and serve
