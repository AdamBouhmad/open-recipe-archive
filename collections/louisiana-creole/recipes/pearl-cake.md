## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pound of Butter
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Soda
- 1 Pound of Flour
- 1/2 Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Extract

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and the sugar to a cream
2. Beat the eggs, whites and yolks separately, and add the yolks
3. Then beat till very light
4. Add the soda, dissolved in a tablespoonful of boiling water, and then add the milk and beat carefully
5. Add the flour gradually, and beat vigorously
6. Then add the spices and essence, and lastly the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
7. Bake for an hour in a moderate oven
