## Ingredients

- ½ pint of milk
- ½ pint of water
- 1 egg
- 1 teacupful of yeast
- 2 large lumps of loaf sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix the milk, water, egg, yeast and sugar
2. Make the milk lukewarm
3. Mix with flour to the consistency of pound cake
4. Rise over night
