## Ingredients

- 1½ lbs. of white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- ½ pint of milk
- ½ lb. of butter
- some mace
- a little wine
- flour

## Directions

1. beat all ingredients with a sufficient quantity of flour to roll out
2. cut into pretty shapes
