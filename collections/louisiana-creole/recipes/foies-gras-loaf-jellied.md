## Ingredients

- Foies Gras
- Slice of Fat Fresh Pork
- Slice of Lean Pork, Grated
- Quarter Can of Mushrooms
- Quarter Can of Truffles
- Calf's Foot
- Bay Leaf
- Grated Bread Crumbs
- Sprig Each of Thyme and Parsley
- Quarter Teaspoonful of Ground Allspice, Cinnamon, Cloves and Mace
- Three-Quarter Cup of the Soft of Bread
- Juice of One Lemon
- Bits of Lemon Peel
- Tablespoonful of French Brandy
- Egg, well beaten

## Directions

1. Prepare in exactly the same manner as the Loaf of Foies Gras, only the sweetbreads are omitted
2. In their place add an egg, well beaten, and a piece of grated lean and fat fresh pork meat
3. Add a half can of mushrooms and a quarter of a can of truffles, and a piece of calf's foot
4. Cook into a mixture of the consistency of gravy, mash well and strain in a sieve after seasoning very highly
5. Add the juice and let this simmer for about five minutes
