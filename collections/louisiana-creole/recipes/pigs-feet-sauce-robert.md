## Ingredients

- 3 Pigs' Feet
- 1 Tablespoonful of butter or olive oil
- 1 Tablespoonful of salt
- 3/8 Tablespoonful of pepper
- Grated bread crumbs
- 1/2 Pint of Sauce Robert

## Directions

1. Boil three good-sized Pigs' Feet in salted water.
2. When tender, take out of the water and drain thoroughly.
3. Split the feet in two and place in a dish.
4. Season well with salt and pepper.
5. Rub them with the olive oil or butter.
6. Roll the feet in grated bread crumbs.
7. Put them to broil, allowing four minutes to each side of the feet.
8. Prepare a hot Sauce a la Robert.
9. Pour this sauce in a warm dish.
10. Lay the feet nicely over it and send to the table hot.
