## Ingredients

- 1 Grated Cocoanut
- 1 Quart of Milk
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Cornstarch

## Directions

1. Put the milk in a boiler and add the cornstarch as it commences to boil, first moistening the cornstarch with a little water.
2. Stir until very smooth.
3. Then add the yolks of the eggs, well beaten, and then the sugar.
4. Lastly, add the whites, beaten to a stiff froth.
5. Then add the grated cocoanut, turn into the baking pan, and bake for half an hour.
6. Serve cold, with Vanilla Sauce.
