## Ingredients

- 1 quart of thick sour cream
- 1 spoonful of fresh butter
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- hot water
- 1 tablespoonful of sifted flour
- white cornmeal

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 teaspoonful of soda in hot water
2. Combine the sour cream, fresh butter, soda solution, and sifted flour
3. Add white cornmeal to form a soft dough
4. Work well with the hands
5. Bake in a buttered pan
6. Serve very hot
