## Ingredients

- the whites of ten eggs
- one tumbler of sifted flour
- one and a half tumblers of sifted white sugar
- half a teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of ten eggs to a froth
2. Stir the cream of tartar and salt well into the flour
3. Add the sugar to the whites first
4. Stir in the flour very lightly last of all
5. Flavor with any delicate extract
6. Bake immediately
7. No soda required
