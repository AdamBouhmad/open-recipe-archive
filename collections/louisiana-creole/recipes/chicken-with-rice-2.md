## Ingredients

- 1 Young Chicken
- 0.75 Cup of Rice

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the chicken as in Poulet au Riz, only do not cut up the chicken, but stew whole.
2. When three-fourths done, add the rice.
3. In serving place the chicken in the center of the dish and heap the rice around.
