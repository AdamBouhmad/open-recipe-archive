## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- one pound of flour
- six eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of lemon juice or white wine
- one pound of seeded raisins or citron
- one and a half pounds of blanched almonds cut fine
- one grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make a batter with butter, sugar, flour, and eggs
2. add lemon juice or white wine
3. add raisins or citron dredged with flour
4. add blanched almonds cut fine and grated nutmeg
