## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of small cherry or chili peppers
- 3/4 pint of best French vinegar

## Directions

1. Fill a pint bottle about half full of small cherry peppers or chili peppers, red and green.
2. Fill up with the best French vinegar or cider.
3. Bottle well, and in about a week it will be ready for use.
