## Ingredients

- 3 Quarts of Good Bouillon or Consomme
- The Yolks of 3 Eggs
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Flour
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare a good Bouillon or Consomme
2. Beat the yolks of three eggs and the white of two together until very light
3. Add one cup of flour, one half teaspoonful of salt, and mix with cold water, making a stiff paste
4. Roll very thin
5. Roll each strip to form a tube
6. Cut in strips and grease
7. Simmer a few at a time in boiling salt water for about twenty minutes
8. Simmer the noodles in the soup about fifteen minutes
