## Ingredients

- 3 Pounds of Steak for Broiling Purposes
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- Chopped Parsley and Lettuce Leaves to Garnish
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Dredge the meat well with salt and pepper and then brush lightly with butter.
2. Place it on the hot gridiron and let it broil quickly for four minutes; then turn on the other side for four minutes longer.
3. When done take off, place in a hot dish, butter nicely.
