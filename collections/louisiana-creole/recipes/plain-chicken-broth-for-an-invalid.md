## Ingredients

- A young fowl cut into four parts
- One quart of cold water
- A little salt
- The white heart of a head of lettuce
- A handful of chervil
- Two tablespoonfuls of pearl barley

## Directions

1. Cut a young fowl into four parts, wash well in cold water, put the pieces in a stewpan with one quart of cold water and a little salt;
2. Let it boil gently, skim it well;
3. Add the white heart of a head of lettuce and a handful of chervil.
4. Boil the broth for an hour, then strain it into a bowl.
5. Two tablespoonfuls of pearl barley added to the broth when first put on makes it quite nourishing for an invalid.
