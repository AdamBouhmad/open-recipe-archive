## Ingredients

- sweet corn
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- egg

## Directions

1. Cut sweet corn from the ear
2. Add plenty of milk
3. Season with salt, pepper and butter
4. Turn into a baking dish
5. Beat an egg and spread over the top
6. Bake 20 minutes
