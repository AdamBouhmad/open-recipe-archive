## Ingredients

- 1 Peck of Elderberries
- 2.5 Gallons of Water
- 0.5 Pound of Sugar
- 0.5 Pint of Hop Yeast

## Directions

1. Bruise the Elderberries
2. Dilute with the water and boil for ten minutes
3. Strain well and press out the juice from the berries
4. Measure the whole, and add the sugar
5. While half warm add the yeast
6. Let the whole ferment for ten days
7. Cork well
8. After three months draw off the wine and bottle for use
