## Ingredients

- juice of 8 oranges
- 6 lemons
- peel of the fruit
- 1 pint of cold water
- 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar
- 1 1/2 ounces of isinglass
- beaten whites of 7 eggs

## Directions

1. Grate the peel of the fruit and steep it in a pint of cold water
2. When the flavor is extracted, mix the water with the juice of 8 oranges and 6 lemons
3. Add 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar, 1 1/2 ounces of isinglass, and the beaten whites of 7 eggs
4. Put all into a saucepan and stir till it boils
5. Let it boil a few minutes
6. Strain it till clear through a jelly bag
7. Put it into a mould or glasses
