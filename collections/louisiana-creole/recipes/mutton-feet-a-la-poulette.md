## Ingredients

- 32 Mutton Feet
- Boiling Water
- 1/2 Lemon
- Salt
- Cream Sauce
- Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1/2 Tablespoonful of French Vinegar
- 1 Gill of Water
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 1/2 Can of Mushrooms (if desired)

## Directions

1. Scald the mutton feet in boiling water and remove every vestige of wool that may adhere, cleaning and scraping the feet.
2. Then place them in a pot, cover well with boiling water, add half of a lemon (including peel and meat) to the water, and salt well.
3. In the meantime, prepare a Sauce a la Poulette as follows: Make a Cream Sauce and add the juice of one lemon, or half a tablespoonful of good vinegar.
4. Take the mutton feet out of the water in which they have been boiled, take out the big bones from the feet.
5. Put the mutton feet into the Sauce a la Poulette, add a gill of water, let all simmer about five minutes, and then take off the fire and add the yolks of two eggs, beaten well, stirring well into the sauce.
6. Serve hot.
