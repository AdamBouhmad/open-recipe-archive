## Ingredients

- strong brine
- cucumbers
- fig leaves
- grape leaves
- vinegar
- water
- alum
- horse radish
- garlic
- 2 lbs. of brown sugar
- turmeric
- cloves
- mace
- nutmeg
- allspice
- celery seed
- cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Make a strong brine and let it boil in a bell metal skillet
2. Place cucumbers in a jar with layers of fig and grape leaves, alternately, until filled
3. Pour the boiling brine over the cucumbers
4. Cover up closely
5. Repeat the next day with the same brines, but fresh leaves
6. If sufficiently green, scald with vinegar and water on the day following
7. Let them remain 24 hours
8. Boil strong vinegar with a small piece of alum, horse radish and garlic, with 2 lbs. of brown sugar and a small quantity of turmeric, cloves, mace, and nutmeg, allspice, celery seed and cayenne pepper, say ½ ounce of each
