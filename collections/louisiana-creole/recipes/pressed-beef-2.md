## Ingredients

- brisket of beef
- salt
- saltpetre

## Directions

1. Salt a brisket of beef with salt and saltpetre for 5 days
2. Boil it gently till extremely tender
3. Put it under a large weight or in a cheese press till perfectly cold
4. Serve cold for sandwiches
