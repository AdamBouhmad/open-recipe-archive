## Ingredients

- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 6 Sausages (Chaurice)
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Cup Bread Crumbs
- 4 Sprigs of Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Take the left-over bouilli cut in slices and parboil slightly in butter.
2. Make a forcemeat or quenelle of pork sausage, garlic, parsley and moistened bread crumbs, add two eggs, salt and pepper.
3. Put a layer of this farci between each layer of sliced beef, and then add the bread crumbs, mixed with chopped parsley.
4. Put the beef in oiled paper, folded as tightly as possible, cook a quarter of an hour in the oven and serve in the papillottes (paper).
