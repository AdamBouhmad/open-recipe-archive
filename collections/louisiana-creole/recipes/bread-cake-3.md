## Ingredients

- 2 teacups of risen dough
- a teacup of sugar
- a cup of butter
- 2 eggs
- a few raisins (stoned and cut)
- a mite of soda
- cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Mix together the risen dough, sugar, butter, eggs, and raisins
2. Add a mite of soda and cream of tartar
3. Mix the cream of tartar with the dough
4. Dissolve the soda in a little milk and add lastly
