## Ingredients

- 2 Onions
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme, Parsley and Bay Leaf
- 2 Pickles, 2 Inches In Length
- 1 Teaspoonful of Strong French Vinegar
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Cayenne or Hot Pepper
- Sauce Piquante
- A Teaspoonful More of Prepared Mustard
- Two More Tablespoonfuls of Minced Parsley
- The Juice of a Lemon

## Directions

1. Make a Sauce Piquante
2. Add a teaspoonful more of prepared mustard, and two more of minced parsley, the juice of a lemon
3. Let it boil up once
4. Serve with steak, pork chops, liver sauté, turkey or goose
