## Ingredients

- 1 quart oysters
- liquor
- slice of stale bread
- tablespoonful butter
- pepper
- salt
- celery

## Directions

1. Put 1 quart of oysters with their liquor into a chopping dish or stew pan
2. Crumble in a slice of stale bread, tablespoonful of butter, pepper and salt to your taste
3. Stew from ten to fifteen minutes
4. The addition of a little celery cut up is a great improvement
