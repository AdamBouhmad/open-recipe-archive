## Ingredients

- warm hominy
- salt
- butter
- cream
- 1 or 2 eggs
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Season warm hominy with salt, butter, and cream
2. Add 1 or 2 eggs
3. Add a little flour to stiffen it
4. Fry on a griddle
5. Serve hot
