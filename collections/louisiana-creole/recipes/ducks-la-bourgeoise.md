## Ingredients

- 2 Fine Canvas-Back Ducks
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 3 Tomatoes (fresh or canned)
- 12 Onions
- 2 Carrots
- 1/2 Glass of Madeira Wine
- The Zest of 1 Lemon
- 1/2 Pint of Sauce Espagnole
- 1/2 Pint of Consomme or Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1/2 Ounce of Salt Pork

## Directions

1. Prepare the Ducks, and cook according to the first recipe provided.
2. After placing finally in the saucepan, add twelve small onions which have been nicely glazed.
3. Add the two carrots cut into small dice and which have been cooked in salted water for two minutes, before adding to the ducks.
4. Also add a half ounce of salt pork cut into half-inch pieces.
5. Let these cook for fifteen minutes with the ducks and serve on a hot dish with Croutons.
