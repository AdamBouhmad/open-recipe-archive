## Ingredients

- 3 pints rich milk
- vanilla
- yolks of 14 eggs
- whites of 4 eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 3 pints of rich milk with vanilla, and let it cool
2. Beat the yolks of 14 eggs and the whites of 4
3. Sweeten the milk and eggs
4. Fill the custard cups and set them in a Dutch oven, with as much water as will reach nearly to the tops of the cups
5. Put over the fire and make the water boil, until you perceive a scum forming on the top of the custards
6. Put them in the stove, brown them quickly, and take them out of the water
