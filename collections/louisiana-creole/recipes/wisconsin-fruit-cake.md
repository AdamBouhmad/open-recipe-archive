## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound of raw salt, fat pork
- a pint of boiling water
- one cup of sugar
- two cups of molasses
- two teaspoonfuls of cloves
- one teaspoonful of cinnamon
- one nutmeg
- two teaspoonfuls of saleratus
- one pound and a half of raisins
- one pound of citron and currants
- flour

## Directions

1. Take three quarters of a pound of raw salt, fat pork, chopped very fine
2. pour on a pint of boiling water, one cup of sugar, two cups of molasses, two teaspoonfuls of cloves, one of cinnamon, one nutmeg, two teaspoonfuls of saleratus, one pound and a half of raisins, also a pound of citron and currants if liked
3. add flour as stiff as can be stirred
4. bake very slowly an hour, or longer if necessary, as it will burn without great care
5. This will make three loaves, and will keep well
