## Ingredients

- fresh tongue
- salt
- saltpetre
- 1 tablespoonful each of allspice, cloves and nutmeg
- 1 lemon peel
- 1 teaspoonful ginger
- 1 teaspoonful thyme
- 1 teaspoonful summer savory
- 1 piece of lemon
- 1 ounce of suet
- pepper

## Directions

1. Corn a fresh tongue 2 days, with salt and a little saltpetre mixed
2. Lay it on one side and bore holes with a steel used for knives
3. Staff these holes with a mixture of 1 tablespoonful each of allspice, cloves and nutmeg, 1 lemon peel grated, 1 teaspoonful ginger, 1 teaspoonful thyme, 1 teaspoonful summer savory, 1 piece of lemon, 1 ounce of suet chopped fine, salt and pepper to taste
4. Place in a dish and under a heavy press for 24 hours
5. Add water to its own brine and boil until it will skin
6. Cut off its roots even or nicely
7. Begin at the small end to roll
8. Bandage very tightly
9. Stuff the paste into the middle
10. Boil 3 hours
11. When done put under heavy press again
