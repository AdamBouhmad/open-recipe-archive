## Ingredients

- a knuckle of veal or a piece of lamb
- a quart of water and a teaspoonful of salt to each pound
- half a pound of rolled crackers
- a quart of nice oysters
- a large tablespoonful of butter
- pepper and salt to taste
- macaroni or vermicelli (if preferred)

## Directions

1. Allow a quart of water and a teaspoonful of salt to each pound
2. Set it over the fire, let it come to a boil, skim it well and then set it back on the stove
3. Let it simmer for two hours
4. This will form a fine, strong, nourishing stock for the soup
5. Take out the meat, and skim the stock clear
6. Put in half a pound of rolled crackers and a quart of nice oysters
7. Let it boil up, and finish by putting in a large tablespoonful of butter, and pepper and salt to taste
8. Macaroni or vermicelli can be substituted for the crackers, if preferred
