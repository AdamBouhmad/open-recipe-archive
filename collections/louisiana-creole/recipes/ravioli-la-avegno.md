## Ingredients

- Four lbs. round of beef
- a tablespoonful of lard
- a little flour
- onions
- salt
- pepper
- water
- 1 pound of dry mushrooms

## Directions

1. Lard the beef
2. Put in a pot with a tablespoonful of lard
3. Smother on a slow fire
4. When ready to fry, sprinkle with a little flour
5. Fry brown
6. Add onions, salt, and pepper to taste, and water enough to cook thoroughly
7. About 1/2 an hour before done, put in 1 pound of dry mushrooms
8. This daube is made for its gravy to the ravioles
