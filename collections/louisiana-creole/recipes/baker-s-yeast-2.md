## Ingredients

- 2 ounces or more of hops
- 3 pints of water
- 2 lbs. of malt
- 1/2 pint of good yeast

## Directions

1. Boil hops into a vessel with water
2. As it decreases, carefully stir all the time
3. Strain the liquor and mix it well with malt
4. Cover and let it stand for 8 hours, or until it is milkwarm
5. Stir in 1/2 pint of good yeast
6. When mixed well together, let it work for 10 hours
7. Strain it through a sieve
