## Ingredients

- One-half lb. of gelatine
- cold water
- boiling water
- 1½ pints of milk
- yolks of 6 eggs
- vanilla
- whites of 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Soak gelatine in a little cold water
2. When soaked, dissolve in ½ pint of boiling water
3. Make sweet custard with 1½ pints of milk and the yolks of 6 eggs
4. Flavor with vanilla
5. Add gelatine to the custard and set in a cool place to congeal
6. When it begins to harden, add the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth
7. Beat well through the custard
8. Pour into a dish lined with sponge cake
