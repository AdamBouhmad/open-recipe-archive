## Ingredients

- beef or veal (chopped fine)
- pepper
- salt
- ginger
- 4 eggs
- stale bread (a large plateful)
- flour
- tomato or walnut catsup

## Directions

1. Chop the beef or veal very fine and remove all the stringy parts
2. Season it with pepper, salt and a little ginger
3. Beat up 4 eggs very light
4. Take a large plateful of stale bread and make them in balls
5. Roll them in flour and fry them
6. Take 2 tablespoonfuls of flour, mix with cold water
7. Put some seasoning and hot water into the frying pan
8. After it has boiled throw it over the balls and let it stew about 15 minutes before dishing them in tomato or walnut catsup
