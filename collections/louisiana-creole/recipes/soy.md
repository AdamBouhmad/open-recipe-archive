## Ingredients

- a common size jar of anchovies
- 1 quart of mushroom catsup
- 1 quart of walnut pickle
- 1 gallon of Maderia wine
- black mustard seed

## Directions

1. Bruise and strain the anchovies
2. Add mushroom catsup, walnut pickle, Maderia wine and a little black mustard seed
3. Boil ½ hour
4. Bottle and cork tight
5. Seal with wax and in 10 days the soy will be fit for use
