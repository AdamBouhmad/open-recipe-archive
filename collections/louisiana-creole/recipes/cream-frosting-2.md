## Ingredients

- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 2 Cups of Pulverized Sugar
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Rich Cream
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Extract

## Directions

1. Proceed exactly as in Frosting, only omit the cornstarch, and add an equal measure of rich cream
2. Flavor with Vanilla Extract
3. Boil the cream
