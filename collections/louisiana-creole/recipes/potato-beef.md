## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- 1 or more beaten eggs
- butter or drippings of cold beef
- thin slices of beef without the fat

## Directions

1. Boil some potatoes till well done all through
2. Peel them and put them into a large pan
3. Mash them smoothly, adding as you proceed some milk and 1 or more beaten eggs, well mixed, to the potatoes
4. Rub the bottom of a white pudding dish with nice butter or some drippings of cold beef
5. Cover it with a thick layer of mashed potatoes
6. Put in thin slices of beef (without the fat), enough to cover the potatoes
7. Then a layer of mashed potatoes, then beef, then potatoes, till the dish is full, heaping them up in the centre
8. Bake it in an oven
