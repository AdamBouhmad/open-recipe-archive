## Ingredients

- A Fine Young Stingaree
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- A Handful of Chopped Parsley
- The Juice of 1 Lemon, or 1 Spoonful of French Vinegar
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Take a fine young Stingaree and cut off the tail.
2. Cut the wings from the body and throw away the rest of the fish.
3. Throw the wings into boiling water and parboil them.
4. When boiled ten minutes, take them off the water and then take all the skin off.
5. Put one tablespoonful of butter in a frying pan and let it brown without burning.
6. When a nice coffee color, add a handful of chopped parsley and let it brown for a half minute.
7. Then add the juice of one lemon or a spoonful of French vinegar.
8. Pour this over the fish, salt and pepper to taste, and serve hot.
