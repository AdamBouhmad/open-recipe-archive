## Ingredients

- paste
- three ounces of jam (strawberry or raspberry)
- three ounces of bread crumbs
- three ounces butter
- three ounces sugar
- the rind and juice of a large lemon
- a tablespoonful of water

## Directions

1. Line a pie dish with paste
2. Spread on this three ounces of jam
3. Beat well in a basin three ounces of bread crumbs, three ounces butter, and the same of sugar, and the rind and juice of a large lemon
4. Add this to the pastry and jam
5. If the lemon is not very juicy, add a tablespoonful of water to it
6. Bake half an hour
