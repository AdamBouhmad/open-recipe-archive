## Ingredients

- 1 lb. powdered sugar
- ½ lb. butter
- ½ lb. of flour
- ¼ lb. corn starch
- whites of 16 eggs

## Directions

1. Wash the butter free of salt
2. Cream it well, and add the sugar, using a porcelain pie masher instead of a spoon
3. Add gradually the flour and cornstarch
4. Add lastly the well beaten whites
5. Flavor with almond to taste
6. Bake as pound cake
