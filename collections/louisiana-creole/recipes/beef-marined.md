## Ingredients

- Four pounds of beef, from the round or shoulder
- Four tablespoonfuls of olive oil
- Three bay leaves
- One onion
- One lemon
- Tablespoonful of vinegar
- One-eighth teaspoonful each of ground cloves, mace and allspice
- Salt and cayenne

## Directions

1. Mix the spices, salt and pepper thoroughly and rub well into both sides of the beef
2. Chop the onions fine, and cover the meat with them
3. Mix the oil and vinegar and the juice of one lemon, and pour this over the meat
4. Set it in the ice box, or a cool place, and let it stand overnight
5. Put it into the stewpan, and be careful to retain all the juices of the spices
6. Set on the fire and let it simmer ten minutes, adding the bay leaves, chopped very fine
7. Add a tablespoonful of flour, rolled smoothly in a half teaspoonful of butter or lard, melted
8. Let this brown, and then half cover the meat with boiling water
9. Cover closely and set on the oven, letting the beef cook two hours, and turning once, so that both sides may be well penetrated by the heat
10. Serve on a hot dish, pouring the gravy over
