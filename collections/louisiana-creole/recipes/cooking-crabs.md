## Ingredients

- 2 dozen crabs
- 1 pint of water
- 1 pint of vinegar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of salt

## Directions

1. Add 1 pint of water, 1 pint of vinegar, and 2 tablespoonfuls of salt to an iron pot
2. After the mixture boils, put the crabs in with a cloth over them, excluding the air
3. Let them boil from 12 to 15 minutes
