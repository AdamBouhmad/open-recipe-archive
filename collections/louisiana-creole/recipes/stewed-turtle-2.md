## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Turtle Meat
- 1 Onion
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter or Lard
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Cup of Water
- A Wineglassful of Sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Cut the meat of the turtle about an inch in size.
2. Chop an onion and put all into a saucepan, with a tablespoonful of lard to brown.
3. As it begins to brown, add a tablespoonful of flour, one bay leaf, one clove of garlic, and a sprig of thyme, chopped very fine.
4. Mix this thoroughly with the turtle meat, then add a wineglassful of Sherry or Madeira, and a cup of water.
5. Cook for half an hour.
