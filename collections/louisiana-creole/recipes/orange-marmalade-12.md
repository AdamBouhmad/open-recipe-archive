## Ingredients

- 6 Oranges
- 2 Lemons
- ¾ of a Pound of Sugar for every Pound of Fruit

## Directions

1. Select fine, ripe Louisiana oranges.
2. Remove the rind and grate it.
3. Grate the pulp, preserving carefully every particle of juice.
4. Extract the seeds.
5. Allow the grated rind and juice of two lemons to every six oranges.
6. Weigh the whole and allow for every pound of fruit three-fourths of a pound of sugar.
7. Cook the whole, adding the grated rind of the oranges.
8. When done turn into jelly tumblers.
9. Test by turning a portion on a plate.
10. When it begins to jelly, which will be in about twenty-five minutes, turn into the tumblers and seal.
