## Ingredients

- 1 Teacupful of Honey
- 1 Teacupful of Sugar
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Melted Butter
- 1 Pint of Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of Carraway Seeds

## Directions

1. Take one teacupful each of honey and sugar, and mix well
2. Add a half teaspoonful of melted butter and the yolks of two well-beaten eggs
3. Sift one teaspoonful of baking powder into a pint of flour and add
4. Beat all together till very light, and add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
5. Finally, add a teaspoonful of carraway seed, if desired, and bake in a moderate oven from half an hour to three-quarters of an hour
