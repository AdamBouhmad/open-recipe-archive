## Ingredients

- Watermelon
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Take the bright red heart of the watermelon
2. Cut into dainty and fanciful forms, and place in a dish in the sun to dry
3. Make a syrup as directed for Crystallized Fruits
4. With the point of a long hatpin or hairpin immerse the watermelon in it
5. Hang to dry from these points
