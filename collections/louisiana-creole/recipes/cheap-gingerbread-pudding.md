## Ingredients

- a cup of butter
- three and a half cups of flour
- one cup of milk
- one cup of molasses
- one teaspoonful of saleratus
- rich sauce

## Directions

1. Take a cup of butter, rub it up with three and a half cups of flour, one cup of milk, one cup of molasses, and one teaspoonful of saleratus
2. Steam three hours
3. Serve with a rich sauce
