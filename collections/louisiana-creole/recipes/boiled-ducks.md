## Ingredients

- ducks
- salt
- flour
- milk
- onion

## Directions

1. Clean and pluck them, preserving the skin from rents
2. Salt them for 30 hours previous to cooking
3. Flour a clean white cloth and boil them in it
4. A moderate sized duck will take about 1 hour's boiling
5. Make a rich onion sauce with milk
6. Send it to table with the duck
