## Ingredients

- potatoes
- very hot lard
- fine salt

## Directions

1. Wash and pare a sufficient quantity for the meal
2. Slice them in the machine, taking care to bear down lightly, so as to have the slices very thin
3. Have ready a vessel of very hot lard, and drop the sliced potatoes into it
4. Let them remain till they begin to brown
5. Take them out with a wire ladle
6. Scatter a little fine salt over them, and serve while hot
