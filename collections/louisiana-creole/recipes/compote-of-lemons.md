## Ingredients

- 1/2 Dozen Lemons
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Water

## Directions

1. A Compote of whole lemons is prepared in exactly the same manner as the above, using twice the amount of sugar, or sugar to taste.
2. When squeezed thoroughly and boiled they make an excellent Creole remedy for colds and coughs.
