## Ingredients

- good, fresh, ground unbolted flour
- three quarts of wheat meal
- one half a cup of good yeast
- three spoonfuls of molasses
- water
- flour enough to mould it
- a little soda

## Directions

1. Get good, fresh, ground unbolted flour, and sift it through a common hair sieve
2. Take three quarts of this wheat meal, one half a cup of good yeast, and three spoonfuls of molasses
3. Mix to a sponge with water
4. Work in flour enough to mould it
5. Put a little soda in the batter before moulding, as it is more disposed to ferment than fine or bolted flour
6. Proceed as you do with common wheat bread
