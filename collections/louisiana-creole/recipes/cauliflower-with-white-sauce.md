## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- pepper
- butter
- salt
- drawn butter
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the green stalks
2. If the heads are large, divide them into quarters
3. Wash and boil them with a little pepper, butter and salt
4. Serve with drawn butter or white sauce, when they become soft and tender
