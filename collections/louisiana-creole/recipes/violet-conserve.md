## Ingredients

- 2 Ounces of Freshly-Gathered Violet Petals
- 1 1/2 Pounds of Sugar
- Distilled Water

## Directions

1. Pound two ounces of freshly-gathered violet petals, with great care, into a paste, after steeping in distilled water sufficient to make a paste.
2. Then add the sugar, in proportion of one and a half pounds and boil to a flake.
