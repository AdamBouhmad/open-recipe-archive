## Ingredients

- 12 Oranges
- 1.5 Pints of Orange Juice
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Quart of Water

## Directions

1. Peel six oranges, removing every particle of pulp.
2. Slice nicely and place in a dish, and sprinkle over them the granulated sugar.
3. Set aside for several hours to make a syrup.
4. Squeeze the juice from the remaining six oranges and add to the syrup formed from the other oranges, which must be carefully drained of all syrup.
5. Add the water; mix well and strain.
6. Then turn into a freezer and freeze till a light frosting appears.
7. Serve in small glasses.
