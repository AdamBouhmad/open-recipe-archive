## Ingredients

- figs
- brine
- alum water
- vinegar
- sugar
- cinnamon
- mace
- cloves

## Directions

1. Put the figs in brine at night
2. In the morning, or after being in brine about twelve hours, take them out
3. Wash off the salt
4. Put them in alum water for three hours
5. Take them out and scald them in hot water until heated through
6. Make a syrup of a quart of vinegar, a pint of sugar with a tablespoonful of cinnamon, mace, and cloves each
7. Boil half an hour
8. Pour on the figs boiling hot
9. Repeat the boiling next day
10. Bottle up and seal for future use
