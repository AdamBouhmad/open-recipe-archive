## Ingredients

- one ounce of isinglass
- one pint of water
- one-half pint of milk
- yolks of four eggs
- one-fourth of a pound of sugar
- vanilla or lemon for flavoring
- one quart of cream
- lady fingers or sponge cake

## Directions

1. Boil one ounce of isinglass in a pint of water until reduced one-half
2. While it is boiling, make a custard of one-half pint of milk, yolks of four eggs, and one-fourth of a pound of sugar
3. Flavor the custard with vanilla or lemon
4. Take a quart of cream and whip it up to a fine froth
5. When the isinglass is nearly cold, so that it will not curdle the cream, stir it and the cream into the custard
6. Beat all thoroughly and set it on ice
7. Line a glass dish with lady fingers and pour in the cream, or slice any light sponge cake and lay it on the bottom and sides of the glass bowl
8. Lay fine fancy sugar-drops on top if desired
