## Ingredients

- Apples
- Sugar
- White of an egg

## Directions

1. Pare and core the fruit, which should be juicy and tart
2. Lay the apples in a vessel to cook without putting in any water
3. Cover them closely, and cook until properly soft
4. Strain the juice, and add three-fourths of a pound of sugar to a pint of apple juice
5. Beat in the white of an egg to clarify the jelly, and skim it as it boils
6. Try it and, as soon as it jellies, take it from the fire and put it in glasses
