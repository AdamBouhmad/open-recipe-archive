## Ingredients

- 1 Box of Potted Ham
- 2 Ounces of Grated Boiled Ham
- Thin Slices of Green Gherkins
- 6 Slices of Toast

## Directions

1. Prepare delicate strips of Toast, spread with Potted Ham to a quarter of an inch in thickness.
2. Sprinkle lightly with grated ham and decorate the edges, and cover if desired, with thinly-sliced Green Gherkins.
