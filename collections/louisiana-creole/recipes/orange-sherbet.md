## Ingredients

- juice from a dozen oranges
- boiling water
- peel from oranges
- boiling water and sugar (a pint to a pound)

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from a dozen oranges
2. Pour boiling water on the peel and cover it closely
3. Boil water and sugar (a pint to a pound) to a syrup and skim it clear
4. When all are cold, mix the syrup, juice and peel with as much water as may be necessary to make a rich orangeade
5. Strain it and set the vessel containing it on ice
