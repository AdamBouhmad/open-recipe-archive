## Ingredients

- a glass of thick cream
- a cup of white sugar
- 3 macaroons pounded
- a few almonds
- a little grated lemon
- 8 egg yolks and 3 egg whites
- slices of fried bread and butter

## Directions

1. Let the cream, sugar, pounded macaroons, almonds, and grated lemon boil
2. Add the yolks of 8 eggs and whites of 3 eggs
3. Beat the whole up over a bath
4. Serve on slices of fried bread and butter for tea
