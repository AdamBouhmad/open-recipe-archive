## Ingredients

- a pint of cornmeal
- a pint of milk
- a pint of molasses
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of powdered ginger
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs light
2. Add the cornmeal, milk, molasses, butter, and powdered ginger—the meal last
3. Bake in a pan
