## Ingredients

- lean ham
- yolk of an egg
- pepper
- butter
- square bits of toast

## Directions

1. Grate some lean ham
2. Mix with it the yolk of an egg, pepper it, and fry it in butter
3. Pour on square bits of toast, and brown it with a salamander
