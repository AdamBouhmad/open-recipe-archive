## Ingredients

- 12 tomatoes
- 3 pounded crackers
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel and slice 12 tomatoes, picking out the seed
2. add 3 pounded crackers, and pepper and salt to your taste
3. stew twenty minutes
