## Ingredients

- two quarts of light hop or potato yeast sponge
- a pint of new warm milk
- three beaten eggs
- a cup of butter or lard
- one large cup of sugar
- a large spoonful of cinnamon
- salt
- flour
- boiling lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mix the yeast sponge with warm milk, beaten eggs, butter or lard, sugar, cinnamon and salt
2. Beat this well and sift in flour to make a soft dough
3. Set this in a warm place to rise
4. When it is light roll it out on the board a little thicker than pie crust
5. Cut with a knife in squares of about three inches
6. Let them stand a little
7. Fry them in plenty of boiling lard in a pot half full of boiling lard to ensure their being light and nicely browned
