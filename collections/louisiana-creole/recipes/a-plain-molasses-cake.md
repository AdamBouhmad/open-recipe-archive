## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Louisiana Molasses
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 1 Cup of Boiling Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Soda
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful Ginger

## Directions

1. Dissolve the soda in boiling water, and add it to the molasses.
2. Then melt the butter, and add, and pour in gradually the boiling water, mixing well.
3. Now add gradually the flour, beating well, and add the ginger.
4. Beat until all is very smooth, and then bake for a half hour in a moderate oven.
