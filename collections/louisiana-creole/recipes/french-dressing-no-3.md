## Ingredients

- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Oil
- 1 Tablespoonful of Tarragon Vinegar
- A Saltspoon Each of Black Pepper and Salt
- Chopped Onion and Parsley
- The Juice of Half an Onion

## Directions

1. Mix these in the order given above, adding the onion juice and parsley, well chopped, last.
2. Serve with salads.
