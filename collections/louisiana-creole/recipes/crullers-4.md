## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 3 cups brown sugar
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoonful yeast powder
- flour to roll

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Roll out
3. Cut out with a biscuit cutter
4. Cut from the centre with an apple corer
5. Twist into fanciful shapes
6. Fry in a potato fryer in plenty of boiling lard
