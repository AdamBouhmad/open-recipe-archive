## Ingredients

- small rich pears
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Take small rich pears, and boil them gently in water until they will yield to the pressure of the finger.
2. They must not be soft, or they will not preserve well.
3. Take them out when a little boiled; let them cool, and pare them neatly, leaving a little of the stem on, as well as the blossom end.
4. Make a syrup of a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit, and when it is boiling hot, pour it on the pears.
5. Next day boil them in the syrup till clear, and bottle them for use.
