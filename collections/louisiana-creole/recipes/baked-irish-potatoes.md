## Ingredients

- 8 good sized Irish potatoes
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 pint of milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil soft 8 good sized Irish potatoes
2. Mash them and add 2 tablespoonfuls of butter while hot
3. Mix with it 1 pint of milk, add salt
4. Put in a dish and bake 1 hour
