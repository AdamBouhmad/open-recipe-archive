## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- pint of juice
- spoonful of butter
- salt
- pepper
- 2 eggs
- small handful of bread crumbs
- teacup of milk
- 1 onion

## Directions

1. Scald the tomatoes and rub them through the sieve
2. To 1 pint of juice, add a spoonful of butter, a little salt and pepper, 2 eggs well beaten
3. Add a small handful of bread crumbs, soaked in a teacup of milk, and 1 onion thinly sliced
4. Stew over a slow fire for 1 or 2 hours
