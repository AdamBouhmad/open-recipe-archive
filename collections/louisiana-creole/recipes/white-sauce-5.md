## Ingredients

- A Tablespoonful and a Half of Butter
- A Tablespoonful and a Half of Flour
- Half a Cup of Water or White Broth
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Blend the flour and butter in the saucepan without browning in the least
2. Add by degrees the boiling water or White Consommé of veal or chicken, stirring until smooth
3. Boil three minutes
4. Salt and pepper to taste
5. Add the juice of half a lemon
