## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pound of Flour
- 4 Eggs
- 1/2 Pint of Cold Milk
- 4 Ounces of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Glass of Jelly

## Directions

1. Prepare the batter exactly as in the above recipe
2. When the pancakes are nicely cooked, arrange them neatly on a dish, and spread over each a half tablespoonful of apple, strawberry, raspberry, currant, peach or any kind of jelly desired
3. Roll them up nicely and dress on a dessert dish and sprinkle powdered white sugar over them
4. Send to the table hot
