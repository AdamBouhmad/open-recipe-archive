## Ingredients

- cherries with stems
- 1 pint of vinegar per quart
- 2 large teaspoonfuls of sugar per quart
- a dozen cloves per quart
- allspice
- 5 or 6 blades of mace per quart

## Directions

1. Let the vinegar and spices boil together for about five minutes, and then turn into an earthen jar and let them cool.
2. Then strain and fill the pickle jars with the cherries about three-quarters full, and pour the cold vinegar over them.
3. Bottle tight, and keep in a cool place.
