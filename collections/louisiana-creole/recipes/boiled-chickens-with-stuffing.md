## Ingredients

- chicken
- stuffing
- wheat flour
- boiling water
- hard-boiled egg sauce, parsley sauce, or oyster sauce

## Directions

1. Truss and stuff the chicken as for roasting, dredge it all over with wheat flour, and put it in a pot of boiling water
2. Take the pot off the fire for five minutes after the chicken is put in, or the skin will crack
3. Let it boil gently according to its age and weight, an old fowl requiring twice as long to boil as a young one; allow fifteen minutes to the pound
4. Take off all the scum as it rises, and when done serve with hard-boiled egg sauce, or parsley, or oyster sauce
