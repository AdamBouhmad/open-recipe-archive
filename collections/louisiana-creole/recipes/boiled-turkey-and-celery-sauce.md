## Ingredients

- turkey hen
- two pounds of fat veal dressing
- veal bones
- turkey giblets
- carrot
- onions
- head of celery
- sweet herbs
- puree of celery or oyster sauce

## Directions

1. Draw a fine, young turkey hen, and remove the angular part of the breast bone.
2. Take two pounds of fat veal dressing and stuff the turkey with it.
3. Put over the fire to cook the veal, bones, and turkey giblets, to make some white soup stock; season this and let it boil until you want to put the turkey on to cook.
4. Now truss your turkey and put it in a boiling pot with a carrot, two onions, a head of celery, and a bunch of sweet herbs.
5. Pour over the turkey the stock from the veal and giblets; cover with it, if enough; if not, put in water to cover it and set it to boil.
6. When it has boiled one hour, put it on the back of the stove, and let it simmer and braise, until dinner.
7. Take off any strings that may look badly; dish it up.
8. Pour over it a well-made puree of celery, or oyster sauce, and send to table.
