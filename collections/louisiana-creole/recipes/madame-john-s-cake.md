## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 7 Ounces Butter
- The Whites of 16 Eggs, Whipped to a Stiff Froth
- 1 Pound Sifted Flour
- Juice of 1 Orange
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Cream the sugar and butter together till very light.
2. Then add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff snow, after which stir in gradually the flour and one teaspoonful of baking powder.
3. Flavor with the juice of one orange, or with a tablespoonful of Orange Extract, and bake for from thirty to forty-five minutes in a quick oven.
4. Use the broom-wisp test.
