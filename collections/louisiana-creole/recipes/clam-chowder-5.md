## Ingredients

- 50 to 100 small clams
- salt pork
- biscuits
- milk or warm water
- pepper
- mace
- onions
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Put clams into boiling water and when all shells have opened, take them out and extract all the hard part and throw it away
2. Slice thin as much salt pork as will produce half a pint of liquid or gravy
3. Take out all the pork, leaving the liquid in the pot
4. Add to it a layer of clams, then a layer of biscuits soaked in milk or warm water
5. Add another layer of clams, then another layer of soaked biscuits
6. Add more clams, seasoned with pepper and mace
7. If desired, add three or more onions, boiled and sliced
8. Add some nice potatoes, boiled, peeled and cut very fine
9. Cover the whole with a nice paste, and bake it in an iron oven
