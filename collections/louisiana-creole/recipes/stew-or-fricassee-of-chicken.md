## Ingredients

- chicken
- water
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- parsley
- toasted bread
- eggs

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the chicken, cut it up as for frying
2. Lay it in a stew-pan with water to cover it
3. Add a teaspoonful of salt and half as much pepper
4. Set it to boil very gently, take off all scum as it rises
5. When the chicken is tender, which will be in an hour, take a tea-cup of butter, a tablespoonful of flour worked up in it, and a bunch of parsley, put them in the stew-pan with the chicken
6. Let all stew twenty minutes
7. Serve on toasted bread
8. Egg-balls around the toast add much to the beauty of this dish
