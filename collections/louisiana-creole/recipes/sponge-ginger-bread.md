## Ingredients

- one cup of sour milk
- one cup of molasses
- one-half cup of butter
- two eggs
- one and a half teaspoons of saleratus
- one tablespoonful of ginger
- flour

## Directions

1. Warm the butter, molasses and ginger
2. Add the milk, flour and saleratus
3. Make batter as thick as pound cake
4. Bake as quickly as you can
