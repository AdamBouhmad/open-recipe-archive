## Ingredients

- spawn of a lobster
- fresh butter
- white sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Pound the spawn of a lobster very smooth, with a small piece of fresh butter, and press it through a sieve in the white sauce and a large spoonful of lemon juice
2. Set it over a clear fire to simmer for a minute or two, taking care it does not boil
