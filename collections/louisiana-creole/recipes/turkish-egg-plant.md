## Ingredients

- egg plant
- 2 lbs. cold beef, mutton or veal
- 1 onion
- 6 whole peppers
- 1 teaspoon cloves
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- celery seed
- white pepper
- salt
- cold gravy or water

## Directions

1. Slice the egg plant and just brown it in a frying pan
2. Chop the cold beef, mutton or veal very fine
3. Season with one fine chopped onion, 6 whole peppers, 1 teaspoon cloves, 1 teaspoon allspice, celery seed, white pepper and salt
4. Put in baking dish a layer of egg plant, then of beef, and so on until it is filled
5. Have layer of egg plant on top
6. Pour cold gravy or water on the whole
7. Cover with another dish when set in oven
8. Remove it in time to let the top brown a little before done
