## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Endives
- French Dressing
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chervil, Chopped Very Fine

## Directions

1. Prepare the endives in the same manner as the lettuce.
2. When ready to serve, add the chervil and the French dressing.
3. Serve immediately.
