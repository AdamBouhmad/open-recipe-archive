## Ingredients

- a pint of new milk
- an ounce of Bermuda arrow-root
- cold milk
- a little salt
- fine white sugar
- lemon or orange water
- vanilla bean

## Directions

1. Put a pint of new milk to boil
2. make a smooth batter with an ounce of Bermuda arrow-root and cold milk
3. add a little salt
4. when the milk is boiling stir in the batter
5. let the fire be gentle or it will scorch
6. sweeten this with fine white sugar
7. let it boil a few minutes
8. flavor with lemon, or orange water, or if lemon is objected to, boil a vanilla bean in the milk before the arrow-root is put in
9. Take it off the fire, pour it in a mould and set it on ice
10. serve jelly or jam with the blanc mange, or eat it with cream
