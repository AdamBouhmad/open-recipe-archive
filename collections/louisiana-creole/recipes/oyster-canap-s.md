## Ingredients

- 3 Dozen Oysters
- 6 Slices of Toast
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Hollandaise Sauce
- Parsley Sprigs

## Directions

1. Blanch the oysters and then mince very fine.
2. Mix with two tablespoonfuls of Hollandaise Sauce, and then spread over thin strips of Buttered toast.
3. Sprinkle lightly with Parsley, which has been grated so fine as to be almost a dust.
4. Put a bit of butter on top of each Canapé, set in the oven for a few minutes, and send to the table hot.
