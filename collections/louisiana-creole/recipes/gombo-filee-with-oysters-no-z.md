## Ingredients

- a tablespoonful of flour
- a tablespoonful of lard
- two quarts of oysters
- a cupful of cut leeks or onions
- a large slice of ham
- some parsley

## Directions

1. Fry a tablespoonful of flour in a tablespoonful of lard.
2. Let it brown slowly so as not to scorch.
3. Boil the liquor of two quarts of oysters, and when it is boiling throw in a cupful of cut leeks or onions, a large slice of ham, some parsley, and stir in the browned flour.
4. Let this cook fifteen minutes;
5. then pour in two quarts of oysters.
6. Let them boil a few minutes, season with salt and pepper;
7. take out the parsley and sift in half a cup of dried and pounded fresh filee;
8. if not fresh more will be required.
