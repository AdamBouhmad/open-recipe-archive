## Ingredients

- The Hind Quarter of a Lamb
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Parsley to Garnish
- Mint Sauce

## Directions

1. Select a fine, fresh, white hind-quarter of lamb.
2. Roast in exactly the same manner as indicated in 'Roast Leg of Mutton' (see recipe), only allow about twenty minutes to the pound in cooking.
3. Serve with garnish of parsley and a Mint Sauce. (See recipe.)
4. Roast Lamb is always served with fresh, young green peas and asparagus tips.
