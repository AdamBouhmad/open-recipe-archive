## Ingredients

- 0.5 Pint of Root Beer Extract
- 10 Pounds of Sugar
- 10 Gallons of Lukewarm, Filtered Mississippi River Water
- 1 Pint of Yeast, or 1.5 Cakes of Compressed Yeast

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar thoroughly in the water
2. Add the Root Beer Extract and the yeast
3. If using cake yeast, dissolve it first in a little water
4. Stir until thoroughly mixed
5. Bottle in strong bottles or in jugs, corking and tying the corks very securely
6. Set the beer away in a place of even temperature
7. It will be ready for use in twenty-four hours after bottling, or even ten, but will be far more effervescing if allowed to stand three or four days
