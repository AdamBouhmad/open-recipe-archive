## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Indian Meal
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- A well-beaten egg
- Milk
- Fat bacon skin for greasing

## Directions

1. Make the dough as above, adding a well-beaten egg and milk instead of water.
2. Beat till very light.
3. Drop the stiff batter in large spoonfuls on a frying pan that you will have greased well with fat bacon skin.
4. Fry to a golden brown.
5. After each cake is taken from the skillet, wipe the latter off and regrease with the fat bacon before putting the next cake upon it.
6. Serve hot at breakfast or dinner.
