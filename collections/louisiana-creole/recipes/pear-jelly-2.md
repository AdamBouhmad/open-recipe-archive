## Ingredients

- Pears, just ripe
- Water sufficient to cover
- 1 pound of sugar to every pint of liquid

## Directions

1. Choose pears that are just ripe, and no more.
2. Pare and cut in two, and take out the stones.
3. Boil them in water sufficient to cover well.
4. Strain through a hair sieve, and pour the decoction into earthen dishes.
5. To every pint of liquid add a pound of sugar.
6. Set on the fire, let it boil twenty minutes, skimming while on the fire.
7. Pour immediately into cups, glasses or preserve jars.
