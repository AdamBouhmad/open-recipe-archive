## Ingredients

- 6 Ears of Corn
- 2 Quarts of Boiling Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Only young and tender corn should be boiled
2. Have ready a pot of boiling water
3. Remove the green outside husks and the silk, as far as possible
4. Put the corn into the kettle of boiling water
5. Let it boil rapidly twenty or thirty minutes, if the ears are large
6. Serve immediately after removing again all the silk
7. Heap the corn on a platter and serve to each person an ear with a small butter plate of butter, pepper and salt
