## Ingredients

- 3 Ceps
- 3/4 Pint of Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel
- Grated Bread Crumbs
- Sliced Lemon
- Chopped Parsley

## Directions

1. Drain the Ceps from their oil
2. Season well with salt and pepper
3. Roll in fresh bread crumbs finely grated
4. Broil nicely on double broiler
5. Arrange nicely on toast
6. Pour over each slice some of the Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel
7. Garnish nicely with sliced lemon and serve hot
