## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- one cup of clabber
- a teaspoonful of soda
- grated orange or lemon peel
- a good quart of sifted flour
- sugar for sprinkling
- strips of citron

## Directions

1. Stir soda into the clabber
2. roll dough and cut in rounds with a hole in the middle
3. bake in a quick oven
4. sprinkle sugar over them and stick strips of citron in each cake when placing in baking pan
