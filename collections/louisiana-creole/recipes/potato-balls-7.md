## Ingredients

- 8 potatoes
- 1 kitchenspoonful of butter
- three-quarter pint of milk
- 2 eggs
- cold left-over meat or pork, minced very fine
- salt and pepper
- minced parsley and shallots or onions
- boiling lard

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes as above, or use cold left-over potatoes for this dish.
2. Mash the potatoes well, and add an equal quantity of cold left-over meat or pork, minced very fine.
3. Season well with salt and pepper, minced parsley and shallots or onions.
4. Mix well with the yolks of two eggs, and form into balls of medium size.
5. Brush with the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, and fry in boiling lard.
6. Serve with garnish of chopped parsley or with meat gravy.
