## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Finest White Icing Sugar
- 2 Ounces of Gum Arabic
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 4 Ounces of French Chocolate

## Directions

1. Soak two ounces of the finest Gum Arabic in a gill of hot water
2. Add two pounds of the finest icing sugar and the whites of two eggs
3. Strain the soaked gum through a piece of muslin bag into a bowl
4. Add the essence to the strained gum and fill in with as much icing as it will hold, till it forms a stiff, elastic body
5. Dissolve four ounces of French Chocolate with about a tablespoonful of water and incorporate this with the whites of two eggs of Royal Icing
6. Fill a biscuit forcer with the vanilla preparation and push it through upon a sheet of paper well-dredged with sugar
7. With a small knife cut off the cream as it is pressed out into pieces of about the size of a filbert
8. Place them in the baking pan in the oven for a few minutes to dry their surfaces
9. Dip these white balls into the Chocolate Icing, holding one at a time on the tip of the fork, so as to be able to turn it all around and ice each part
10. Put on a wire tray and set to dry in the oven for about ten minutes
