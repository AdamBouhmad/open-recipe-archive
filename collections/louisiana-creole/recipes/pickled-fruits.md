## Ingredients

- 6 pounds of fruit
- 3 pounds of white sugar
- 1 pint of best cider vinegar
- 1 ounce each of cloves, allspice, mace and cinnamon
- 1 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put the fruit into a kettle with alternate layers of sugar and spices, and add the vinegar.
2. Let all boil for about five minutes, and then skim out the fruit and pack in glass jars as it begins to cool.
3. Let the syrup boil till thick, and then pour the boiling syrup over.
4. Bottle tight, and examine from time to time.
5. If they show signs of fermenting, open the jars or set them uncovered in a kettle of boiling water, and heat the contents till they begin to bubble and cork again.
