## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of ground Mocha coffee
- 1 glass of boiling water
- 1 lb. sugar
- yolks of 8 eggs
- 4 glasses of boiling milk
- 6 ounces of sugar
- 1 ounce of clarified isinglass

## Directions

1. Infuse 1 lb. of ground Mocha coffee in a glass of boiling water
2. When the coffee is precipitated, pour it off clear
3. Boil 1 lb. sugar to a rich syrup
4. Pour the coffee to it
5. Set it on a slow fire, that the sugar may dissolve gently
6. When it is perfectly melted, stir in gently the yolks of 8 eggs, 4 glasses of boiling milk, and 6 ounces of sugar
7. Put it on a moderate fire, stirring with a wooden spoon
8. When it begins to simmer, pass it through a fine sieve
9. Let it stand till lukewarm
10. Mix into it 1 ounce of clarified isinglass, and finish it by dissolving the isinglass
11. Pour into moulds to cool
