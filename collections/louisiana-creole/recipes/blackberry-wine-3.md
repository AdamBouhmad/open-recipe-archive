## Ingredients

- 4 Quarts of Currant Juice
- 1.33 Quarts of Cold Water
- 3 Pounds of Granulated Sugar

## Directions

1. Mix all the above ingredients well together
2. Let them stand for twenty-four hours
3. Let it stand for two days
4. Skim well, and set in a cool place to ferment slowly for at least six days
5. At the end of this time remove any froth that has collected, and add a half teacupful of the best French Brandy
6. Close tightly
7. In two days, when it is well-settled, draw off, without disturbing the sediment
8. Bottle tightly and seal
