## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 glass of Jamaica rum
- 1 teaspoonful of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks well
2. Add the milk and then add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
3. Beat all together
4. The longer the eggs are beaten, the lighter will be the omelet
5. Make a plain omelet
6. Fold and turn quickly into a hot dish
7. Place three spoonfuls of sugar on top of the omelet
8. Bring to the table hot
9. Pour the rum over the omelet and around
10. Set the rum on fire with a match
11. With a tablespoon dash the burning rum over the omelet till all the sugar has melted over and all the rum has evaporated
12. When it ceases burning serve immediately
