## Ingredients

- 2 Ounces of Coffee
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1/2 Pint of Cream
- 4 Ounces of Powdered Sugar
- The Yolks of 3 Eggs

## Directions

1. Parch two ounces of coffee and throw the grains, while hot, into the boiling milk.
2. Proceed exactly as in Creamed Coffee.
