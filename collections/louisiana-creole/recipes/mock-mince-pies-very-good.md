## Ingredients

- six crackers
- one and a half cups of warm water
- one cup of good brown sugar
- one cup of raisins
- one cup of molasses
- one-half cup of cider or strong vinegar
- half a cup of butter
- a lemon and its rind
- a nutmeg
- one teaspoonful of cloves
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak six crackers in one and a half cups of warm water
2. add to them one cup of good brown sugar, one cup of raisins, one cup of molasses, and one-half cup of cider or strong vinegar
3. Beat in half a cup of butter, season with a lemon and its rind, a nutmeg, one teaspoonful of cloves, and ground cinnamon
