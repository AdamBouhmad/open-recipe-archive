## Ingredients

- 1 pound of liver of the beef
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 large onions
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice the onions nicely.
2. Put one tablespoonful of butter into the frying pan and add the onions.
3. When brown, take the liver which you have cut into slices of about three inches in length and one half inch in thickness, and season well with salt and pepper, and lay it over the onions.
4. Stir well.
5. Cover and let it fry for about three minutes, and then turn over and let it cook three minutes more.
6. Pour a teaspoonful of vinegar on top and again season to taste.
7. Let it simmer three or four minutes longer and serve hot.
