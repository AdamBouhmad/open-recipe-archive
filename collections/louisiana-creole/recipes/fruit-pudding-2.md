## Ingredients

- 1 pint of hot milk
- sifted Indian meal
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- a little molasses
- 1 pint of whortleberries or chopped sweet apple
- cranberries

## Directions

1. Take a pint of hot milk, and stir in sifted Indian meal till the batter is stiff
2. Add a teaspoonful of salt and a little molasses
3. Stir in a pint of whortleberries or chopped sweet apple
4. Tie in a cloth that has been wet, and leave room for it to swell, or put it in a pudding pan, and tie a cloth over
5. Boil 3 hours
6. The water must boil when it is put in
7. You can use cranberries, and use sweet sauce
