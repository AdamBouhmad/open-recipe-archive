## Ingredients

- partridges
- force meat
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- powdered mace
- fine flour
- gravy
- 2 spoonfuls of lemon pickle or anchovy
- 1 quarter of a lemon
- sweet marjoram

## Directions

1. Truss the partridges and stuff them with force meat
2. Rub a lump of butter in pepper, salt, and powdered mace, and put it into the bodies
3. Sew them up and after dredging them with fine flour, fry them in butter to a light brown
4. Put them in a stewpan with gravy sufficient to cover them
5. Add 2 spoonfuls of lemon pickle or anchovy, a quarter of a lemon sliced, and a sprig of sweet marjoram
6. Cover them closely and stew them for about ½ hour
7. Thicken the gravy if necessary
