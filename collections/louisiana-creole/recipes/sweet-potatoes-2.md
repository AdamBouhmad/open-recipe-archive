## Ingredients

- sweet potatoes
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Bake plain in their skins
2. Or boil, peel and slice, serve with butter
3. Or boil, and then slice in a pan, butter and sugar thrown over them, and bake in the stove
4. Or boil and mash with butter, and brown in the oven like Irish potatoes
