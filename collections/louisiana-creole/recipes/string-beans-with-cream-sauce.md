## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Fresh String Beans
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1/2 Cup of Fresh Milk or Cream
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. String and wash the beans according to the first recipe.
2. Place in a saucepan and cover with boiling water.
3. Let them boil for an hour.
4. Then drain off the water.
5. Take a tablespoonful of butter and one of flour, and blend well.
6. Add a half cup of fresh milk or cream, or one-half pint of the water in which the beans have been boiled.
7. Season nicely with salt and pepper.
8. Stir in the beans; set them back on the stove for several minutes; let them simmer gently.
9. Season again with salt and pepper to taste, and serve hot.
