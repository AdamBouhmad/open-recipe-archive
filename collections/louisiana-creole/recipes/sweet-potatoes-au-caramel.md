## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Boiling Lard or Butter
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes and then cut them lengthwise in halves
2. Fry in boiling lard or butter, and while frying, sprinkle generously with sugar
