## Ingredients

- a fine, fat leg of mutton
- fat bacon
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Select a fine, fat leg, cut holes in it, and lard it with fat bacon
2. Season with parsley, pepper, and salt and put it to bake in a slow oven
3. Roast it for two hours, and serve with tomato sauce
