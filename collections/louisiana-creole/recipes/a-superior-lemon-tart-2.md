## Ingredients

- six lemons
- one pound of sugar
- one-fourth of a pound of butter
- five eggs
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from six lemons, wash the rinds and boil them; if too strong of the lemon oil, it is better to change the water.
2. Grate or pound the rinds, and when tender and cold, add to them one pound of sugar, one-fourth of a pound of butter, and the yolks and whites of five eggs.
3. Stir in the juice of the lemons, and cook the batter gently until it is thick as honey.
4. Bake it in puff paste without tops.
5. Ornament with fancy strips of paste.
