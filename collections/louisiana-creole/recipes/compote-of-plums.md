## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Plums
- 1/4 Pound of Sugar
- % Cup of Water

## Directions

1. Pick off the stems of the plums and prick them all over with a pin
2. Let them simmer in sugar, which you will have boiled to a syrup for about fifteen minutes
3. Proceed as in a Compote of Cherries
