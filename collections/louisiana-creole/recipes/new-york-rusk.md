## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of flour
- 8 ounces of sugar
- 6 ounces of butter
- 5 spoonfuls of yeast
- 1 pint of milk at tepid heat

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the flour
2. Then add the sugar
3. Then add the yeast
4. If the milk is not sufficient, add a little warm water
5. Make the dough stiff
6. Make them after breakfast
7. Bake for tea on tin sheets
