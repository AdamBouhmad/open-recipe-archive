## Ingredients

- 1 gallon of skinned tomatoes
- 4 spoonfuls of salt
- 4 spoonfuls of black pepper
- 3 spoonfuls of mustard
- ½ a spoonful of allspice
- 8 pods of red pepper
- sharp vinegar
- 2 spoonfuls of the juice of garlic

## Directions

1. To one gallon of skinned tomatoes, put 4 spoonfuls of salt, 4 of black pepper, 3 of mustard, ½ a spoonful of allspice, 8 pods of red pepper
2. Make all the ingredients fine
3. Simmer slowly in sufficient sharp vinegar to have 2 quarts of catsup
4. After simmering 3 or 4 hours, strain it through a wire sieve
5. Those who like it may add 2 spoonfuls of the juice of garlic, after the simmering is somewhat over, and the ingredients are cool
