## Ingredients

- 6 pounds of lean beef
- 2 large-sized onions
- 2 carrots
- 2 stalks celery
- 1 piece of cabbage
- salt and cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Select six pounds of lean beef, rump of beef and some bones, and cut the meat into small pieces, the bones also being mashed
2. Put this on in about six quarts of cold water
3. When it comes to a boil, skim well
4. Add a teaspoonful of salt to help the scum rise more thoroughly, and skim as it rises
5. Take two large sized onions, two carrots, a piece of cabbage and two pieces of celery; chop fine and add to the soup
6. Let it boil six hours, or until the broth is reduced about one-half the quantity
7. Pass all through a colander and then strain through a coarse flannel cloth
8. Season highly with cayenne pepper and salt to the taste
9. If cloudy or muddy before straining, crush the shells of two eggs and put them into the soup and let it come to a good boil, set it back about ten minutes and then strain
10. Add vermicelli, macaroni, or patés according to taste
