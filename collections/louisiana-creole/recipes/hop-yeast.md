## Ingredients

- a handful of hops
- 3 pints of water
- 3 or 4 potatoes
- flour
- yeast
- 1 teacup of warm water
- 1 teacup of brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon of ginger
- 1 tablespoon of salt

## Directions

1. Boil hops in water strong
2. Put potatoes in a crock and mash very fine
3. Put the flour on top
4. Pour the hop water on boiling hot, mixing well with the potatoes and flour
5. Let it stand until milkwarm
6. Add some yeast to make it rise
7. When it gets light, add 1 teacup of warm water, 1 of brown sugar, 1 teaspoon of ginger, 1 tablespoon of salt
8. Stir it down well when you put in the ingredients
9. Let it rise and fall several times
10. Put away for use
