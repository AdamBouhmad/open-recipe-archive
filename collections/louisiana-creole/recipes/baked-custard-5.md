## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 8 eggs
- 5 or 6 spoonfuls of beaten sugar
- salt
- small piece of gelatine
- nutmeg or lemon rind

## Directions

1. Mix a quart of milk with 8 well beaten eggs
2. Strain the mixture
3. Add to it 5 or 6 spoonfuls of beaten sugar, a pinch of salt, with a small piece of gelatine
4. Pour the custard into a deep dish
5. Grate nutmeg or lemon rind over the top
6. Bake in a moderate oven
7. Serve it cold
