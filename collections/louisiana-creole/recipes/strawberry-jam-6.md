## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Strawberries
- 1 Pound of Sugar

## Directions

1. Select fine, ripe strawberries and weigh.
2. Allow equal weights of fruit and sugar.
3. Proceed to boil as directed.
4. When they begin to jelly, they are ready to be turned into the jelly glasses.
