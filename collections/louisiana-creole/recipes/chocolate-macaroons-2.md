## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of the best prepared cocoa
- whites of 4 eggs
- 1 lb. of powdered sugar
- a little sifted flour
- sweet oil or good butter

## Directions

1. Scrape down the cocoa very fine
2. Beat the whites of 4 eggs to a stiff froth
3. Beat into the white of egg 1 lb. of powdered sugar in turn with the chocolate
4. Add a little sifted flour if the mixture appears too thin
5. Grease the bottom of some oblong tin pans very slightly with sweet oil or good butter
6. Form the mixture into small, thick cakes and lay them in the pan not close together
7. Bake them a few minutes
8. Sift sugar over them while warm
