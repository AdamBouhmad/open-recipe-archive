## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Oatmeal
- 1 Quart of Boiling Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- cream

## Directions

1. Soak the oatmeal, when the coarse Scotch Oats are used, over night
2. Cook for a half hour in the morning, boiling constantly, and salt to taste
3. It is always best to set in a double boiler
4. Serve with cream
