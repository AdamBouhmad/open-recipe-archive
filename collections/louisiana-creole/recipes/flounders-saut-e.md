## Ingredients

- flounders
- eggs
- olive oil

## Directions

1. Clean and trim the fish
2. dip them in a couple of eggs well beaten
3. put 6 tablespoonfuls of olive oil in a frying pan
4. place over the fire
5. when quite hot put in the fish
6. let it remain 5 minutes
7. turn over and fry the other side
