## Ingredients

- large red cabbage
- water
- cider vinegar
- butter
- flour
- salt
- cayenne
- eggs

## Directions

1. Take a large red cabbage
2. Wash, drain, and shred it finely
3. Put it in a deep dish
4. Cover it closely and set it on the top of a stove or in a bake oven until it is warm all through
5. Make a dressing as for cold slaw
6. Pour it boiling over the cabbage
7. Cover the dish and send it to the table as warm as possible
