## Ingredients

- A Pint of Broth to Each Person
- 1 Ounce of Vermicelli to Each Person

## Directions

1. Prepare a good Bouillon, or Pot-au-Feu, or Consomme, according to the taste of the household
2. A half hour before serving add the vermicelli to the broth
3. Serve hot
