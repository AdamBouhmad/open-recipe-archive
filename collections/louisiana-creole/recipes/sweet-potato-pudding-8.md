## Ingredients

- 6 Sweet Potatoes
- 3/4 Pound of Fine Sugar
- 3/4 Pound of Butter
- The Zest of a Lemon, Grated Fine
- 1 Wineglass of Good Brandy
- 1/2 Grated Nutmeg
- 1/4 Ground Allspice
- 1 Piece of Ground Cinnamon
- 1 Pint of Milk or Cream
- Yolks of 6 eggs
- Whites of 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil or bake the potatoes, peel and press them through a very fine sieve
2. Add to them the yolks of six eggs, and beat well
3. Add the butter, beating well and thoroughly, and add the sugar and milk
4. Beat all very light, and add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
5. Mix thoroughly, and add the grated nutmeg, ground spices, and blend well with the potatoes
6. Add finally, if desired, a wineglassful of good old brandy or whisky
7. Set the pan with the potatoes in an oven, and let it bake for an hour
8. Place a brown paper on top for the first three-quarters of an hour, and take it off at the last quarter for the top to brown nicely
9. Sprinkle with powdered sugar, and serve hot or cold
