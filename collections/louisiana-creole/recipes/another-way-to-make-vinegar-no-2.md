## Ingredients

- one gallon of water
- two pounds of coarse brown sugar
- one slice of raised wheat-bread
- yeast

## Directions

1. To a gallon of water put two pounds of coarse brown sugar
2. Boil and skim it for half an hour
3. Put it in a tub or jar to ferment
4. Add to it in the tub a slice of raised wheat-bread soaked in yeast
5. It can be bottled off or put in a cask in a week or two, but must be left unstopped, and the bung covered with muslin to keep out insects
