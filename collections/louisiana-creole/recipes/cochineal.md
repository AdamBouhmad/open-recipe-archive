## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Powdered Cochineal
- 1 Ounce of Burnt Alum
- 2 Ounces of Salts of Tartar
- 2 Ounces of Cream of Tartar
- 12 Ounces of Sugar
- 2 Quarts of Water

## Directions

1. Boil the water in a porcelain-lined vessel, and add the other ingredients
2. Remove from the fire, and then strain at once through a sieve or cloth, and bottle for use
