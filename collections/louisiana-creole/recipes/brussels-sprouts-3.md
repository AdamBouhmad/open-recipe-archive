## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Brussels Sprouts
- ¾ Gallon of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Pick the sprouts carefully, rejecting all loose, dead leaves
2. Throw the sprouts into cold fresh water so that any lurking insects may be drawn out
3. Wash and pick carefully after the sprouts have remained about twenty minutes in the water
4. Put them into half a gallon of boiling water and add immediately a tablespoonful of salt and a quarter of a spoon of bicarbonate of soda
5. Let the sprouts boil uncovered for twenty minutes or just long enough to make them tender all through
6. Drain in a colander, season well with pepper and salt
7. Serve in a heated dish with a Drawn Butter Sauce poured over
