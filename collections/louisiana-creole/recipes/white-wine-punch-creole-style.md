## Ingredients

- 2½ Quarts of Sauterne
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Lemon Juice
- ¾ Pint of Brandy
- 1 Quart of Seltzer Water
- ¾ of a Grated Pineapple
- ¾ of a Sliced Pineapple
- 2 Dozen Cherries
- Sprigs of Fresh Young Mint
- 1 Large Piece of Ice

## Directions

1. Take a large punch bowl
2. Put one pound of sugar into it, with one pint of lemon juice, two and a half quarts of Sauterne (White Wine), half a pint of brandy, two quarts of Apollinaris or Seltzer, one-half of a grated pineapple
3. Decorate with sliced pineapple and whole cherries and sprigs of fresh young mint
4. Put in a large piece of ice, and let it cool, and serve in small glass cups
