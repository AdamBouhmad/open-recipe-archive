## Ingredients

- 1 Fine Pineapple
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1/2 Pound of Flour
- 3 Eggs
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 3/4 Cup of Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the milk in a saucepan and add the butter
2. Sift the flour and add gradually to the mixture, stirring briskly for three minutes
3. Take off the fire, and add the yolks of the eggs, beaten very light
4. Stir well, and add the powdered sugar
5. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth, and add gradually to the milk
6. Take a pudding mold and butter well; sprinkle with sugar and put a layer of the milk-and-egg preparation about half an inch thick at the bottom
7. Put over this a layer of finely-sliced pineapple, then add another layer of the milk and eggs and a layer of the pineapple, and continue till all the pineapple and the mixture are used up
8. Set the mold in a tin pan of warm water, and place in the oven and cover with buttered paper
9. Let the pudding steam for one hour; then remove from the oven, turn into a pudding dish, and serve with a Sauce au Kirsch
