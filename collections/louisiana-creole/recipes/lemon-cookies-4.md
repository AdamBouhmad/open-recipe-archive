## Ingredients

- 6 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Butter
- 3 Cups of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon Extract
- Sufficient flour to make a nice dough

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and butter to a cream
2. Add the sugar, and beat well
3. Add the extract of lemon, and then the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
4. Add sufficient flour to make a nice dough, just stiff enough to mold, and then roll thin, and bake in a quick oven to a very light brown
