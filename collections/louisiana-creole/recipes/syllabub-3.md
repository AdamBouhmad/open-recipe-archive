## Ingredients

- the juice of a large lemon
- the yellow rind of a lemon
- one glass of brandy
- two glasses of white wine
- a quarter of a pound of powdered sugar
- a pint of thick cream
- the whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Take the juice of a large lemon, and the yellow rind pared thin
2. Put these ingredients into a pan with one glass of brandy and two glasses of white wine, and let them remain one night
3. the next day add a pint of thick cream, and the whites of two eggs beaten together
4. beat them all together to a fine froth
5. serve in jelly glasses
