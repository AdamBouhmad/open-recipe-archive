## Ingredients

- 3 Pigeons
- 2 Dozen Crawfish
- 1 Slice of Fat Bacon
- 1 Square Inch of Ham
- 1 Onion
- 1 Carrot
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Juice of a Lemon
- 1 Cup of Bouillon
- Buttered Croftons

## Directions

1. Clean the pigeons nicely, and rub inside and out with the juice of a lemon.
2. Rub with salt and pepper, and brush with melted butter.
3. Place thin strips of fat bacon in the bottom of a saucepan, lay the pigeons on this, and cover with another thin layer of strips of bacon.
4. Cover with butter and set in a slow oven, and let them simmer gently.
5. Take a small square inch of ham, chop or mince very fine, and fry in a tablespoonful of butter.
6. Add an onion and a carrot, chopped fine.
7. Let these brown, and then add an herb bouquet, minced very fine.
8. When brown, add a cup of bouillon, and let it boil for ten minutes.
9. Pour this sauce over the baking pigeons, and let them cook slowly for about an hour, or until done.
10. In the meantime boil about two dozen nice crawfish according to recipe, and when the pigeons are done, place them on buttered Croftons and place between each a garnish of crawfish.
11. Add about a half cup of the broth in which the crawfish have been boiled to the gravy in the baking dish.
12. Let it simmer for five minutes till reduced slightly, and pour over the pigeons.
