## Ingredients

- fish slices
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- white paper
- drawn butter or anchovy sauce

## Directions

1. Cut some slices of fish about 1 inch thick
2. Season with pepper and salt
3. Wrap each piece in half a sheet of white paper, well buttered
4. Twist the ends of the paper and broil them over a fire of bright coals for 10 minutes
5. Serve in the butter, with drawn butter or anchovy sauce
