## Ingredients

- the whole of a turtle
- a leg of beef
- water
- 2 quarts and a half of soup
- an ounce of mace
- a dessertspoonful of allspice
- a teaspoonful of cloves
- salt
- black pepper
- cayenne pepper
- parsley
- thyme
- onions
- two tablespoons of flour

## Directions

1. Take the whole of the turtle out of the shell; cut it in pieces, that it may be more easily scalded
2. Throw these pieces, with the fins, into the pot, and when scalded, take off the coarse skin of the fins and lay them aside to make another dish
3. Take off the thick skin of the stomach; under it lies the fat, or what is termed the citron
4. Cut up the turtle in small pieces, throw them into a pot
5. Before doing this, take a leg of beef and boil it to a gravy
6. Put in the pieces of turtle, adding as much water as will cover the whole, about 2 inches
7. Let it boil slowly for 3 hours
8. The seasoning and the citron should be put in when the soup is half done
9. To 2 quarts and a half of soup add an ounce of mace, a dessertspoonful of allspice, a teaspoonful of cloves, and salt, black and cayenne pepper to your taste
10. Use parsley, thyme, and onions in the soup while boiling
11. When nearly done thicken with 2 tablespoons of flour
