## Ingredients

- a piece of butter the size of a hen's egg
- 1 pint of cornmeal
- 2 eggs
- some new milk
- a spoonful of yeast

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the cornmeal
2. Make it a batter with 2 eggs and some new milk
3. Add a spoonful of yeast
4. Set it by the fire 1 hour to rise
5. Butter little pans and bake it
