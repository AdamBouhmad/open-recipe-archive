## Ingredients

- 5 Pounds Rump of Beef
- 5 Pounds of Beef Bones
- 2 Calfs Feet
- 1 Large Herb Bouquet
- 1 Stalk of Celery
- 3 Large Carrots
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Roast five pounds of the rump of beef well and brown, but rare
2. Chop it in small pieces and put in a pot with two gallons of water
3. Add the bones and calf's feet, all raw
4. Add a large herb bouquet, one stalk of celery and three large carrots
5. Let the whole come to a boil
6. As the scum rises skim
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste
8. Let all boil till reduced to one quart
9. Strain this, and it will make a jelly or glace when cold
