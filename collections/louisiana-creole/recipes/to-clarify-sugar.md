## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of sugar
- white of 1 egg
- 1 1/2 pints of water

## Directions

1. To 3 lbs. of sugar allow the white of 1 egg, and 1 1/2 pints of water
2. Break the sugar into small lumps
3. Put it into a saucepan, and pour the water over it
4. Let it stand some time before it is put on the fire
5. Add the beaten white of the egg
6. Stir it until the sugar is entirely dissolved
7. When it boils up, pour in a pint of cold water
8. Let it boil up a second time
9. Take it off the fire
10. Let it settle for 15 minutes
11. Remove the scum
12. Put it on the fire again, and boil it till thick enough
