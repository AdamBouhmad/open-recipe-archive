## Ingredients

- 2 fine oranges
- 1/4 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of butter, free of salt
- yolks of 16 eggs
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Pare the yellow rinds of 2 fine oranges very thin
2. Beat it extremely fine in a marble mortar
3. Add to it 1/4 lb. of sugar
4. Beat the yolks of 16 eggs very light, and add to them the other ingredients
5. Mix and beat until they are all of a color
6. Lay a puff paste with a double rim in a dish
7. Pour in the pudding and bake it
