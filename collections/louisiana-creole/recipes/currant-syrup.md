## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Currant Juice
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup

## Directions

1. Pick, seed, wash and dry the currants carefully.
2. Then pound them well and set aside in a cool place for thirty-six hours.
3. Make a Plain Syrup and proceed as in the General Directions for Making Fruit Syrups.
