## Ingredients

- a pint of young tender okra
- half as much skinned, ripe tomatoes
- an onion
- a tablespoonful of butter
- salt
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Take a pint of young tender okra, chop it up fine
2. Add to it half as much skinned, ripe tomatoes
3. Add an onion cut up in slices
4. Add a tablespoonful of butter, a little salt and pepper, and a spoonful of water
5. Stew all together till tender
6. Serve with meat or poultry
