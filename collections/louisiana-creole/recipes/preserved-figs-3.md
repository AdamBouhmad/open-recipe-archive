## Ingredients

- white figs, nearly ripe
- white sugar, equal weight with the figs

## Directions

1. Score figs across, as deep as the outside skin
2. Make a syrup of white sugar, equal weight with the figs, which you may clarify if you see proper
3. Put in the figs and boil them gently until they look clear
4. Take them off the fire and again boil the syrup till very thick and rich
5. Pour it over the figs hot
