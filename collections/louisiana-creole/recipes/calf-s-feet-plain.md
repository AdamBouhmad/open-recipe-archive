## Ingredients

- 3 Calfs Feet
- 3 Quarts of Cold Water
- 1 Gill of Vinegar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Onion
- 1 Carrot
- 12 Whole Pepper Corns
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Salt
- 1 Herb Bouquet

## Directions

1. Split each of the calf's feet in two
2. Carefully remove the larger bones and cut the meat into pieces of about one inch square
3. Soak well in fresh water for one hour
4. Wash and drain thoroughly
5. Put two tablespoonfuls of flour and three quarts of cold water into a saucepan
6. Stir well, mixing thoroughly
7. Place the feet in the mixture and add one onion chopped fine, twelve whole peppers, one carrot cut into fine shreds, the herb bouquet and two tablespoonfuls of salt
8. Let the feet boil briskly for about one hour
9. Take from the fire and drain well
10. They are now ready to serve with any sauce that may be desired
