## Ingredients

- 8 Large, Fine, Rosy Apples
- A Plain Paste
- Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare a Plain Paste and cut into squares.
2. Put in the center of each a fine, juicy apple, pared and cored, or a half apple, pared and cored.
3. Wrap the fruit up in the dough and place these in small dumpling cloths.
4. Put them into boiling water and let them boil for at least an hour.
5. On taking out the apple, if so desired, the inner core may be filled with some delicate jelly or marmalade.
6. Serve with Hard, Cream or Brandy Sauce.
