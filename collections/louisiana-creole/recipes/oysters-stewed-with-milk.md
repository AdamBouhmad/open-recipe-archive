## Ingredients

- a pint of fine oysters
- one-half pint of their own liquor
- a half a pint of milk
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- pepper and salt to taste
- crackers
- dressed celery

## Directions

1. Take a pint of fine oysters, one-half pint of their own liquor and a half a pint of milk
2. Boil the liquor, take off the scum
3. Put in a quarter of a pound of butter
4. Pepper and salt to taste
5. And serve crackers and dressed celery with them
