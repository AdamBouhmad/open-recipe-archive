## Ingredients

- A Spring Chicken
- Melted Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Select spring chickens for broiling
2. Clean the chickens nicely and singe
3. Split down the middle of the back, laying the chicken open
4. Break the breastbone with a mallet and flatten out the chicken
5. Season well with salt and pepper and brush with melted butter
6. Have the broiler ready over a moderate fire
7. Place the chicken between the double broiler
8. Let the fowl broil slowly for about a half hour if the chicken is very tender, otherwise three-quarters of an hour
9. Keep a plate over it all the time to retain its flavor better
10. Turn the chicken as needed
