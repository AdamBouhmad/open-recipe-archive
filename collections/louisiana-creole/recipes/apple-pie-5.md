## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of apples, well boiled and pounded
- ½ lb. of butter beaten to a cream
- 6 eggs with whites well beaten
- ½ lb. of powdered sugar
- peel of 2 lemons, well boiled and pounded
- thin crust

## Directions

1. To boiled and pounded apples add butter beaten to a cream while warm
2. Add eggs with whites well beaten
3. Add powdered sugar and lemon peel
4. Put thin crust at the bottom and round the sides of the dish
5. Bake 1 hour
