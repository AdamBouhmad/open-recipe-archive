## Ingredients

- 6 large lemons
- 1 lb. of salt
- 6 large cloves of garlic
- 2 ounces of horse radish sliced thin
- 1 ounce of cloves, mace, nutmeg, and cayenne
- 2 ounces of flour of mustard
- 2 quarts of vinegar

## Directions

1. Wipe the lemons
2. Put on them salt, garlic, horse radish, cloves, mace, nutmeg, cayenne, and flour of mustard
3. Add vinegar
4. Boil 1 hour
5. Set the jar by and stir it daily for 6 weeks
6. Keep it closed
