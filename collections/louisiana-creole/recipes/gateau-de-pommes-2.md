## Ingredients

- half lb. of loaf sugar
- 1 pint of spring water
- 2 lbs. of apples
- 1 large lemon
- isinglass

## Directions

1. Boil half lb. of loaf sugar in a pint of spring water till it becomes sugar again
2. Pound and core 2 lbs. of apples
3. Put all together in a stewpan
4. Grate in the pulp of a large lemon and stew it till it becomes a stiff jelly
5. Put it into a mould
6. It will turn out when cold
7. If the apples are flat, you may add a little lemon juice
8. A custard put in the dish is an improvement
9. If not stiff enough, add a little isinglass
