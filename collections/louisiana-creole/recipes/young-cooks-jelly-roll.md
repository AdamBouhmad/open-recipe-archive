## Ingredients

- a cup of white sugar
- one cup of flour
- three eggs
- baking powder
- jelly

## Directions

1. Make the sponge by taking a cup of white sugar, one cup of flour, and three eggs
2. Mix and add baking powder with the flour
3. Bake in a stewpan with a quick fire
4. turn the cake out on a towel when done
5. spread the jelly while it is still warm and soft
6. roll it carefully
7. Cut it in slices when cold
