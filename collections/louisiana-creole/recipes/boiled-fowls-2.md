## Ingredients

- fowls
- flour
- cold water
- parsley
- butter
- oysters or celery sauce

## Directions

1. Flour a white cloth and put the fowls in cold water
2. Let them simmer for 1 of an hour
3. Serve with parsley and butter, or oysters or celery sauce
