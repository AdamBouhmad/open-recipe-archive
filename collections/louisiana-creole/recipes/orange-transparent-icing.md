## Ingredients

- ¾ Pound of Fine White Sugar
- Half the White of 1 Egg
- The Zest of 1 Orange
- 1 Tablespoonful of Orange Essence or Orange Juice

## Directions

1. Make Orange Transparent Icing by flavoring with a little orange sugar, rubbing against the zest of the orange, or tinging with Saffron and flavoring with Curaçao
