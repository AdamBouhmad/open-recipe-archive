## Ingredients

- onions (six white)
- milk (a cup of hot)
- butter (a large spoonful)
- pepper
- salt
- flour (to thicken)

## Directions

1. Peel and boil six white onions, and when tender pour off the water;
2. chop the onions small, and add to them a cup of hot milk, a large spoonful of butter, and pepper and salt to taste;
3. thicken with a little flour if preferred.
