## Ingredients

- 3 spoonfuls lemon juice
- lemon peel grated
- apricot jam
- 1 pint cream
- 1/2 ounce isinglass
- sugar
- macaroons

## Directions

1. Put in the dish you intend sending to table, 3 spoonfuls of the lemon juice with a little of the peel grated, to apricot jam
2. Boil together 1 pint of cream, 1/2 ounce of isinglass, and some sugar
3. When nearly cold, pour it on the sweetmeat
4. Put a few macaroons at the bottom of the dish
5. Make a few hours before using
