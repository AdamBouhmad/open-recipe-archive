## Ingredients

- 100 Fine Lake Shrimp
- 1 Cup of Milk
- Grated Bread Crumbs
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- A Dash of Cayenne
- Fried Parsley and Parsley Tips and Olives to Garnish

## Directions

1. Use Fine Lake shrimp for this recipe
2. Boil first according to the recipe given for Boiled Shrimps
3. Take off the fire, pick off shells and season well
4. Take a pan of milk, season well with salt and pepper
5. Roll the shrimp well in this, then roll them in grated bread crumbs or yellow cornmeal (the latter being preferable)
6. Fry in boiling lard
7. The shrimp must swim in the lard
8. When they are a nice golden brown, skim out with a skimmer and drain on heated brown paper
9. Serve on a hot dish on a bed of fried parsley and garnish with parsley tips and olives
