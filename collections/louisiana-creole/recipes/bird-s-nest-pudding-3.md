## Ingredients

- 8 or 10 apples
- 2 eggs
- 1 J cups of white sugar
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- 4 cups of milk
- 2 cups of flour
- 1 teaspoon yeast powder
- I cup of sugar for scalding

## Directions

1. Pare and core 8 or 10 apples
2. Place in deep flat dish
3. Mix 2 eggs, 1 J cups of white sugar, 1 tablespoon of butter, 4 cups of milk, 2 cups of flour well
4. Add 1 teaspoon yeast powder last
5. Scald apples with I cup of sugar
6. Pour over the apples the mixture and bake
