## Ingredients

- one-quarter of garlic
- 1 lb. of scraped horse radish
- 1 lb. of white mustard seed
- 1 lb. of black mustard seed
- 1 ounce of long pepper
- 2 ounces of black pepper
- 2 ounces of turmeric
- 1 ounce of mace
- 1 ounce of cloves
- 1 lb. of race ginger
- 4 ounces of olive oil
- salt
- vinegar
- allspice
- mustard

## Directions

1. Fill the mangoes with salt, let them stand 24 hours
2. Wipe them clear from salt
3. Mix the garlic, scraped horse radish, white mustard seed, black mustard seed, long pepper, black pepper, turmeric, mace, cloves, race ginger cut up fine, and olive oil together
4. Put them in the mangoes and sew them up
5. Have some vinegar spiced well with common spices, such as black pepper, allspice, horse radish and mustard
6. Boil it well and pour it over the mangoes
7. Tie the jar closely
