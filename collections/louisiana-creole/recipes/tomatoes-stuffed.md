## Ingredients

- five large tomatoes
- parsley
- fat bacon
- ham
- bread crumbs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- thyme
- two eggs
- brown sauce or gravy

## Directions

1. Take five large tomatoes, slice off that part which joins the stalk
2. Cut out a little of their pulp, take out the seeds, and strain them
3. Chop up the pulp with a handful of parsley, a slice of fat bacon, a slice of ham, and a cup of bread crumbs
4. Fry all these, and season with butter, pepper, salt, thyme, and the yolks of two eggs
5. Take it off the fire as soon as the eggs are beaten in
6. Stuff the tomatoes
7. Bake them for half an hour
8. Pour some brown sauce or gravy over them and serve
