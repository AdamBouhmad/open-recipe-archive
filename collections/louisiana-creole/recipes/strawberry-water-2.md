## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of strawberry juice
- 1 quart of water
- 8 ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Choose fine ripe strawberries, stem and bruise them in a little water, and let them stand for an hour or so
2. Squeeze them in a cloth and pour the juice off clear
3. To half a pint of the juice add one quart of water and eight ounces of sugar
4. Stir well, strain, and put it on ice, and serve as needed during the sultry summer days
