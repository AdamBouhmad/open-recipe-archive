## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Cream
- 1 Pint of New Milk
- 6 Eggs
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 3/4 Cup of Strong Cafe Noir (Black Coffee)

## Directions

1. Boil the milk and take from the fire
2. Whip the cream to a stiff froth
3. Add the sugar and yolks of eggs, thoroughly beaten, to the milk and coffee, sweetening to taste
4. Set aside to cool, and add the whipped cream
5. Proceed to freeze as directed
