## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Clarified Sugar
- Sufficient Water to Melt

## Directions

1. Take one pound of clarified sugar and add just sufficient water to melt
2. Boil it till it is very brittle and has the slightest odor of beginning to burn
3. Pour it on a buttered slab or plate
4. As soon as it is cool enough to receive an impression from the finger, cut it out with a common case knife into small squares of about an inch in size
5. Glace with another coating of sugar
6. Let them dry and keep them tightly closed
