## Ingredients

- 2 ounces of almonds, blanched and pounded
- a large spoonful of orange flower water or rose water
- 1 pint of new milk
- yolks of 6 eggs
- 1 lb. of crushed sugar
- 1 quart of cream
- 1 lb. of fine sugar
- slices of sponge cake
- apple or quince jelly

## Directions

1. Pound 2 ounces of almonds, blanched, to a paste with a large spoonful of orange flower water or rose water, to prevent oiling
2. Put on a pint of new milk to boil
3. Beat the yolks of 6 eggs with 1 lb. of crushed sugar
4. When the milk boils, stir in the eggs and sugar, and then add the almonds
5. Mix all together, and set it aside to cool
6. Whip 1 quart of cream to a stiff froth, with 1 lb. of fine sugar
7. Drain the froth
8. Lay some slices of sponge cake in a dish
9. Spread apple or quince jelly on it
10. Pour the cold custard over it
11. Then add the whipped cream
