## Ingredients

- 10 Medium-Sized Peaches
- 1 Pint of Cold Water
- 1/2 Pound of Granulated Sugar
- 1 Gill of Kirsch or Brandy, if desired

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches and cut them into quarters, carefully removing the stones.
2. Put them into a saucepan, with a pint of cold water and a half pound of granulated sugar.
3. Set on the stove, and, when they begin to boil, skim well.
4. Let them cook for six minutes longer, stirring slowly; avoid mashing the peaches.
5. Remove from the fire and add, if desired, a gill of Kirsch or Brandy.
6. Mix well and pour the peaches into a dessert dish to cool.
7. Serve cold, either plain or with cream.
