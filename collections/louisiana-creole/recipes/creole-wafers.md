## Ingredients

- 9 Ounces of Flour
- 8 Ounces of Sifted Sugar
- 8 Eggs
- 1 Wineglassful of Noyau
- 1 Pint Whipped Cream
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla
- A Pinch of Salt

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and yolks of the eggs to a cream.
2. Add the Noyau, salt, Vanilla and flour, and incorporate thoroughly till light.
3. Add the whites of the eggs and the cream.
4. Mix all lightly together, taking great care that all are well-mixed.
5. Heat the wafer irons over a clear charcoal fire.
6. When hot brush inside with a little clarified butter.
7. Pour a large spoonful of butter into the under-sheet of the wafer irons, then close in.
8. Bake to a golden brown color.
9. Sprinkle lightly with sugar.
