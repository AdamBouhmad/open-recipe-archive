## Ingredients

- One pound and a half of grated bread
- One pound and a half of raisins
- One pound and a half of currants
- One pound of beef-suet
- Peel of one large lemon
- Three ounces of almonds
- A little nutmeg or mixed spice
- Sugar to taste
- Three quarters of a pound of candied orange, lemon and citron
- Eight or nine eggs
- Half a pint of milk
- Two wineglassfuls of brandy

## Directions

1. Stone the raisins
2. Wash and pick the currants
3. Chop the suet very fine
4. Mix with them a pound and a half of grated bread
5. Add the candied peel cut into shreds
6. Add the almonds blanched and minced
7. Add the mixed spice and sugar to taste
8. When all are thoroughly blended, stir it well together with eight or nine well-beaten eggs, two glassfuls of brandy, and half a pint of milk
9. Tie it in a cloth
10. Boil it for five hours or five hours and a half, or divide it into equal parts, and boil it in moulds or basins for half the time
