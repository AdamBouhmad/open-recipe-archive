## Ingredients

- Hearts of 3 heads of celery
- ½ cupful of grated ham
- ½ cupful of cream
- ¾ cup of fine bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the celery hearts till tender, then drain and pound to a paste.
2. Mix with a cupful each of grated ham, cream and fine bread crumbs; season to taste with salt and pepper and add a tablespoonful of butter.
3. Steam the mixture till it thickens, then fill small patty cases with it and serve hot.
