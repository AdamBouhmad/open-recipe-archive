## Ingredients

- 1 large cut glass filled with water
- 3 lumps of sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of good brandy or whisky
- The juice of half a lemon
- A bit of lemon or orange peel
- 6 sprigs of fresh young mint
- A few ripe strawberries
- Crushed ice
- Crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Take one large cut glass, half fill with water, add six sprigs of fresh mint, three lumps of sugar.
2. Stir well, till the sugar is absorbed.
3. Add a tablespoonful of good brandy or whisky, and stir well.
4. Add a little lemon and orange peel, and the juice of half a lemon, if desired.
5. Fill the glass with crushed ice, and decorate on top with sprigs of mint.
6. Place a few ripe strawberries on top of the mint, sprinkle lightly with crushed sugar, and serve.
