## Ingredients

- 1 teacup of lump tapioca
- milk
- 1 pint of new milk
- 1 large spoonful of butter
- salt
- 2 large spoonfuls of sugar
- 1 wineglass of rose water
- nutmeg
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Wash over night 1 teacup of the best lump tapioca, and let it remain in milk to soak all night
2. the next morning boil 1 pint of new milk and stir in the soaked tapioca
3. add 1 large spoonful of butter and a little salt, 2 large spoonfuls of sugar, 1 wineglass of rose water, a little grated nutmeg
4. beat 4 eggs very light and stir in
5. mix all well together, and bake in a dish half an hour
