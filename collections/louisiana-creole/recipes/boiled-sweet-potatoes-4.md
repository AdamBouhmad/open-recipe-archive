## Ingredients

- 6 Potatoes
- Drawn Butter Sauce

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes well, using a cloth or brush, and removing every particle of earth
2. Clip the edges of the roots that adhere
3. Put them to boil in a kettle of boiling water, and let them cook till they may be easily pierced with a fork
4. Drain off the water, and set the kettle on the back of the stove
5. Cover and let the potatoes steam a few minutes
6. Take them out after five minutes or so, and peel and serve them with Drawn Butter Sauce, or cut in slices and spread butter over them, and set in the oven for a few seconds, and serve hot with daubes, fish, grillades, etc.
