## Ingredients

- ripe, sound, cling-stone peaches
- vinegar
- brown sugar
- cloves

## Directions

1. Remove the down with a brush like a clothes brush from ripe, sound, cling-stone peaches
2. Make a gallon of good vinegar hot
3. Add to it four pounds of brown sugar
4. Boil and skim it clear
5. Stick five or six cloves into each of the peaches, then pour the hot vinegar over them
6. Cover the vessel and set it in a cold place for eight or ten days
7. Drain off the vinegar, make it hot, skim it, and again turn it over the peaches
8. Let them become cold, then put them into glass jars and secure as directed for preserves
