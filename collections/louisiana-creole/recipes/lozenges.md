## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Pounds of Finest White Icing Sugar
- 2 Ounces of Any Essence Preferred
- 1 Ounce of Gum Tragacanth
- 2 Ounces of Tepid Water

## Directions

1. Take one ounce of Gum Tragacanth and soak it in two ounces of tepid water for about six hours
2. Squeeze and wring out through a cloth
3. Add about a pound and a half of fine icing sugar and the desired essence in the proportion of about two ounces of essence to the quantities given
4. Work this prepared gum on a marble slab as you would knead dough until it is firm and white, yet elastic
5. Gradually work in the sugar and add the essence and work till smooth and very elastic
6. Roll the paste out with fine sugar dredged on the slab and cut into ten-cent size pieces and into diamond shapes
7. Place them in rows upon sugar-powdered baking sheets and dry in the oven
8. Keep them in a well-stopped jar in a cool place
