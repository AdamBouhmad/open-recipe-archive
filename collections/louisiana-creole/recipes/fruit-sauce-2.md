## Ingredients

- 4 Peaches, Apples, Apricots or Pears
- 1 Cup Sugar
- ¼ Cup Water
- 1 Pint of Cream
- Whites of 2 Eggs
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Brandy

## Directions

1. Pare the fruit; take out the stones or core; slice and put into a saucepan with the water and sugar, and let it stew gently until very tender
2. Press through a colander or sieve
3. Set the cream to boil, and then beat it into the fruit thoroughly
4. Take off the fire, add the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth, set to cool, and serve with fruit puddings made of the same fruits
