## Ingredients

- 1 cup of boiled rice
- 3/4 cup of sugar
- 1 ounce of pineapple
- 2 ounces of raisins
- 2 ounces of currants
- 1 ounce of grated orange peel
- 1 quart of milk
- the yolks of 6 eggs
- 3/4 of a grated nutmeg
- the whites of two eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of powdered white sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the rice well, and then drain through a colander and set to boil with the milk.
2. When it has cooked for twenty minutes, add all the fruits, being careful to have the pineapple cut into dice, and the raisins seeded, and the currants picked, washed and dried.
3. Add the orange peel and grated nutmeg, and finally the eggs, which have been well beaten in the sugar till very light.
4. Let all simmer for just one minute.
5. Take off the stove, place in a baking pan, and set to brown nicely in a quick oven for about twenty-five minutes.
6. When well browned, have ready a meringue, which you will have made by beating the whites of two eggs to a froth, and add to this two tablespoonfuls of powdered white sugar.
7. Spread all this over the pudding.
8. Let it brown slightly in a hot oven.
9. Serve either hot or very cold.
