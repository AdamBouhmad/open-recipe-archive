## Ingredients

- mustard
- salt
- garlic or tarragon leaves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put on a plate an ounce of the best mustard, add to it salt, a clove of garlic or a few tarragon leaves.
2. Mince the garlic, stir it in, and pour on vinegar till it is of the proper thickness for use.
