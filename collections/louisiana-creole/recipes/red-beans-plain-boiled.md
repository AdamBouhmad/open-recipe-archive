## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Dried Red Beans
- 1/2 spoonful of Lard or Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Soak the beans in cold water over night, or at least five or six hours, and drain off the water, and place them in a pot of cold water, using at least a quart of water to a pint of beans.
2. Let them boil for at least an hour and a half or two hours, and then season nicely with salt and pepper.
3. Add a half tablespoonful of lard or butter, let them cook for fifteen minutes more, and serve in their own juice.
