## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 4 heaping teaspoonfuls of Gruyere cheese, grated
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Break the eggs into a saucepan.
2. Add the butter, the grated cheese, a little salt and pepper.
3. Place the saucepan on a hot fire.
4. Stir the mixture around and around till the edges begin to thicken.
5. When of the consistency of a thick starch take off the fire.
6. Serve immediately on buttered toast.
