## Ingredients

- veal
- ham
- salt
- mushrooms
- parsley
- eschalots
- soup stock
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- paste

## Directions

1. Cut a pound of veal and a pound of ham into slices, salt them slightly.
2. Chop a cupful of mushrooms, a bunch of parsley, some eschalots, and fry them lightly.
3. Add to them a pint of soup stock, boil it together for five minutes and pour it into the piepan where you have placed your ham and veal.
4. Put a dozen hard-boiled yolks of eggs in among the contents of the pie, cover it with a nice paste and bake it one hour and a half.
