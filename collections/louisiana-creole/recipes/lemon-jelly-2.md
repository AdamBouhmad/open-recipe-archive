## Ingredients

- 3 ounces isinglass
- 1 pint water
- juice of 12 large oranges
- juice of 3 lemons
- 3 lbs. sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve isinglass in 1 pint of water
2. Press the juice of 12 large oranges and 3 lemons
3. Boil 3 lbs. of sugar in 1 pint of water to a syrup
4. Strain the juice and isinglass into the boiling syrup, and stir it a little while
5. When nearly cold, pour into moulds
