## Ingredients

- Equal parts of currants and raspberries
- 1 pound of sugar to every pint of berry juice

## Directions

1. Take equal parts of currants and raspberries and strain the juice.
2. Allow one pound of sugar to every pint of berry juice.
3. Proceed as for Currant Jelly.
