## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 12 Mushrooms
- 2 Truffles
- 1 Pint of Sauce Allemande

## Directions

1. Clean, pare and blanch the sweetbreads.
2. Then cut into very small pieces and put them into a saucepan with two tablespoonfuls of butter.
3. Let them sauté a moment and then add the mushrooms and truffles all cut very fine.
4. Add a pint of Sauce Allemande, and let the mixture cook on a slow fire for six or eight minutes, tossing gently all the while.
5. Add a teaspoonful of butter, stir well and use for any garnishing desired.
