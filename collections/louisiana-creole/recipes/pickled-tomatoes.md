## Ingredients

- fair, ripe tomatoes
- pounded mace
- cloves
- cinnamon
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Prick the skins of fair, ripe tomatoes
2. Spread them in layers
3. On each layer put pounded mace, cloves and cinnamon
4. Pour cold vinegar over the whole
