## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Hominy
- 1 Cup of Flour
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 3 Eggs
- 3 Cups of Sour Milk or Warm Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Soda

## Directions

1. Boil and mash the hominy, or utilize left-over hominy.
2. Add the flour, into which you will have mixed the salt.
3. Beat the eggs very light and add, and when all has been beaten very light add a teaspoonful of soda, dissolved in a little warm water.
4. Bake on a griddle to a nice brown.
5. If sweet milk is used, be careful to use, instead of the soda, two teaspoonfuls of baking powder.
6. Serve with Louisiana Molasses or Syrup.
