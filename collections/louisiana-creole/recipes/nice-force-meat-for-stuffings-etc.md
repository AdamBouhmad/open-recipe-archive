## Ingredients

- cold chicken
- veal
- beef
- pepper
- salt
- sweet herbs
- nutmeg
- minced ham
- garlic
- raw eggs (two)
- butter
- marrow or drippings

## Directions

1. Take equal quantities of cold chicken, veal and beef; shred small and mix together;
2. season with pepper, salt, sweet herbs, and a little nutmeg, 7. e., if intended for white meat or anything delicately flavored, but if meant for a savory dish add a little minced ham, and garlic;
3. pound or chop this very fine (it is well, and saves trouble, to run it through a sausage chopper);
4. and make it in a paste with two raw eggs, some butter, marrow or drippings;
5. stuff your joint, or poultry, and if there is some not used, roll it round the balls, flour them and fry in boiling lard.
6. This is a nice garnish for a side dish.
