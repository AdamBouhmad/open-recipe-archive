## Ingredients

- 2 large spoonfuls of brown sugar
- 2 large spoonfuls of good butter
- yolks of 3 eggs
- a nutmeg (grated), equal proportion of cornmeal and flour

## Directions

1. Stir together the brown sugar and butter
2. Beat the yolks of 3 eggs and add to the sugar and butter
3. Grate a nutmeg
4. Add an equal proportion of cornmeal and flour to knead it
5. Spread it on a board and glaze it with the white of an egg
6. Bake before the fire as other journey cake
