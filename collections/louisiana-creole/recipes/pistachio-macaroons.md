## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Bitter Almonds
- 6 Ounces of Shredded Pistachio Kernels or Peanuts
- 12 Ounces of Sifted Sugar
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds and then pound them well
2. Mix with the whites of the eggs till thoroughly pulverized
3. Mix with the shredded pistachio kernels or peanuts, and stir well
4. Fill a biscuit forcer with the preparation, and drop the macaroons in round balls about the size of a hickory nut upon a wafer sheet of paper
5. Spread upon a baking sheet
6. Bake in a moderate oven till very slightly colored
