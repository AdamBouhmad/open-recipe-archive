## Ingredients

- nice, fresh new potatoes
- lard
- two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- a teaspoonful of prepared mustard
- a dash of Cayenne
- two teaspoonfuls of vinegar
- the yolks of two eggs

## Directions

1. Take nice, fresh new potatoes and boil them.
2. When done, take off and let evaporate, being careful not to have cooked them too much.
3. Take a deep frying pan, and heat the lard to the boiling point.
4. When it begins to boil, drop in the potatoes, and let them fry for about five minutes.
5. Lift out with a skimmer, drain quickly, and put them into a saucepan with two tablespoonfuls of melted butter into which you have rubbed well a teaspoonful of prepared mustard.
6. Add a dash of Cayenne and two teaspoonfuls of vinegar.
7. Let it cook for three or four minutes, shaking constantly, and then take off.
8. Add the yolks of two eggs, well beaten, and serve immediately with Grillades à la Sauce or Grillades Panees.
