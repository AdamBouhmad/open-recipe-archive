## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Flour
- 1 Pint of Milk
- ½ Cake of Compressed Yeast
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Gill of Warm Water

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and salt together.
2. Scald the milk, and when tepid add the yeast, dissolved in a little warm water, to one-half of the milk.
3. Add one-half of the flour, and make a thin batter, stirring very smooth.
4. Set it to rise in a warm place, and at the end of three hours make it into a dough with the remainder of the milk and flour, and knead well till the dough no longer adheres to the board or to your hands.
5. Then shape it with your hands into small balls, set to rise for a half hour longer, or an hour, and bake in a moderate oven for a half hour.
