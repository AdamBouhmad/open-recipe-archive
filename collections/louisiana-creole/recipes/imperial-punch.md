## Ingredients

- 1 Ripe Pineapple
- 4 Oranges
- 2 Pounds of Sugar
- The Finely-Grated Zest of 2 Lemons
- 1 Pint Equal Parts of Maraschino, Noyeau, Kirsch and Curacao
- 1 Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Pint of Champagne
- The Whites of 8 Eggs
- 3 Pints of Water

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of the water and the sugar
2. Peel and grate the pineapple very fine
3. Grate the zest of two lemons very fine, and mix with the pineapple
4. Add the juices of the oranges and lemons
5. Mix thoroughly, and pour over all the boiling syrup
6. Let it stand till cold
7. Strain through a fine sieve into the ice cream freezer, pressing out all the juice of the pineapple as you strain
8. Whip the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth, and add, mixing well
9. Add the liqueurs, stir well, and then proceed to freeze as in Creole Punch
10. When you have detached the frozen punch from the sides and bottom of the freezer for the third time, add the champagne
11. Let it continue freezing for several turns more, and serve immediately in punch glasses
