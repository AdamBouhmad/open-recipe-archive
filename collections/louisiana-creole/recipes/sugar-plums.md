## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Best Confectionery Sugar
- Sufficient Water to Dissolve
- 1 Pound of Any Fruit

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of the best sugar, and add sufficient water to dissolve it or to start it boiling
2. Let it boil to a syrup
3. Place the fruit to be crystallized in a pan, and pour over the syrup
4. Turn the fruit lightly by shaking the pan till every side is coated, and set to cool
5. When cool, drain off the syrup and set the pans on their sides, so that remaining particles may be drained off, and every side be coated with the crystallized mixture
6. It is always best to pour the syrup over the fruit while it is warm, as it takes a firmer hold and makes a brighter crystal
