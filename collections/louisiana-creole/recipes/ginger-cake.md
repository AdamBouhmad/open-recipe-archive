## Ingredients

- 1.5 teacups flour
- 0.5 cup molasses
- 0.5 cup brown sugar
- 0.5 cup hot water
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 heaped tablespoon butter
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- 0.5 teaspoon cloves

## Directions

1. Mix molasses and sugar, into which put the butter, adding also hot water
2. Beat yolks and add
3. Add 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, 1 of ginger, and 0.5 of cloves
4. Add whites well beaten, and add flour last of all
5. Dissolve the soda in a little water
6. Place immediately in a moderate oven
7. Bake about 0.75 of an hour
