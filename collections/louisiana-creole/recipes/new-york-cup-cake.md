## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 4 cups of sifted flour
- 3 cups of powdered white sugar
- 1 cup of butter
- 1 cup of rich milk
- 1 glass of wine
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- a small teaspoonful of pearl ash
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Warm the milk and cut up the butter in it
2. Beat the eggs very light
3. Stir them into the milk, in turn with the flour
4. Add the spice and wine
5. Dissolve pearl ash in a little vinegar and add lastly
6. Stir all very hard
