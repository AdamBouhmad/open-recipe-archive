## Ingredients

- five cups of sugar
- three cups of butter
- two cups of milk
- ten cups of sifted flour
- six eggs
- three small nutmegs
- one teaspoonful of saleratus
- one pound of raisins
- one-third of a pound of citron

## Directions

1. Stir the butter and sugar to a cream
2. then add part of the flour, the milk and the beaten yolks of the eggs
3. then add the rest of the flour and the whites of the eggs
4. add the fruit as you get the cake ready for the oven
5. season to taste
6. Bake one hour
