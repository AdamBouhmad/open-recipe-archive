## Ingredients

- 2 large, ripe pineapples
- powdered loaf sugar
- rich cream
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut into 4 pieces 2 large, ripe pineapples
2. Stand them up successively in a deep dish, and grate them from the rind
3. When all is grated, transfer it to a large glass bowl, and make it very sweet by mixing it with powdered loaf sugar
4. Whip to a stiff froth a sufficiency of rich cream, adding to it some sugar, and heap it high upon the grated pineapple
