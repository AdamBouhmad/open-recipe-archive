## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls of marmalade
- 2 or 3 grated biscuits
- crumbled stale bread
- 3 even tablespoonfuls of butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- lemon chips

## Directions

1. To a dish that will hold 2 1/2 pints, put 4 tablespoonfuls of marmalade
2. add 2 or 3 grated biscuits, or the same quantity of crumbled stale bread, 3 even tablespoonfuls of butter, 3 eggs beat very light, 1 pint of milk
3. add some lemon chips and bake it
