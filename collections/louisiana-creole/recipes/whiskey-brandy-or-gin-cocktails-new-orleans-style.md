## Ingredients

- Two dashes of Boker's, Angostura or Peychaud bitters
- One lump of sugar
- one piece of lemon peel
- one tablespoonful of water
- one wineglassful of liquor
- plenty of ice

## Directions

1. Stir well
2. strain into a cocktail glass
