## Ingredients

- yolks of 6 eggs
- dessertspoonful potato flour
- 1 dessertspoonful orange flower water
- salt
- whites of 6 eggs
- 2 ounces butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Put the yolks of 6 eggs in a large basin
2. Add a dessertspoonful of potato flour and 1 dessertspoonful of orange flower water
3. Add a very little salt
4. Stir these together with a wooden spoon for about 10 minutes
5. Whip the 6 whites and mix them in lightly with the batter
6. Put 2 ounces of butter into a saucepan and set it on the fire
7. As soon as the butter begins to sputter, pour the batter into it
8. Set the pan over the fire and as the batter becomes partially set round the sides and at the bottom, toss it over gently
9. Turn the omelette out neatly in the form of a dome on a silver dish greased with butter
10. Put it in the oven and bake it for about 12 minutes
11. Shake some sugar over it and serve immediately
