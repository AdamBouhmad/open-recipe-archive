## Ingredients

- 8 Ounces of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 1/2 Pint of Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sugar
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- The White of 1 Egg
- 1 Spoonful of Whipped Cream
- 1 Vanilla Bean
- Powdered White Sugar

## Directions

1. Put the milk into a saucepan and infuse the vanilla bean, and let the milk reduce to one-half.
2. Then remove the bean, and add a tablespoonful of butter.
3. When it comes to a good boil, add the flour, well sifted.
4. Stir briskly till a stiff paste is formed that will not adhere to the saucepan.
5. Then take off the fire and add an ounce of powdered white sugar that has been beaten well with the yolks of two eggs.
6. Have ready the white of one egg beaten to a stiff froth, and mix a spoonful of whipped cream with this, and add to the paste.
7. Mix well, and then roll the paste out on a flour board that has been slightly sprinkled with flour, and sprinkle lightly on top with the flour.
8. Cut into small cakes, drop into very hot lard, and let them cook to a golden brown.
9. Then arrange nicely on a dish, sift powdered sugar over them, and send to the table hot.
