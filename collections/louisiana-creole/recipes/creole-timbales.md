## Ingredients

- 2 Raw Chicken Breasts
- 1 Cup of Bread, Soaked in Milk
- 1/2 Tablespoonful of Butter
- The Yolks of Four Eggs
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Veloute Sauce
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 6 Plain Pancakes
- 1 Gill Madeira Sauce

## Directions

1. Cut up into medium-sized pieces two raw chicken breasts; pound them in a mortar with the same quantity of bread soaked in milk, a half tablespoonful of fresh butter and the yolks of four eggs.
2. Season with salt and pepper and a dash of Cayenne; mix all well together.
3. Mix in a bowl with three tablespoonfuls of Veloute Sauce.
4. Butter the half dozen small timbale molds and line them with plain pancake.
5. Fill the molds with the chicken mixture and cover with small round pieces of pancake.
6. Steam them in a moderate oven for about ten minutes.
7. Take out of the oven and remove the timbales from the mold; garnish a hot dish nicely with sprigs of parsley, place the timbales upon it.
8. Pour over a gill of hot Madeira Sauce and send to the table hot.
