## Ingredients

- jelly or blanc mange
- 3 lemons
- 4 eggs
- blanc mange

## Directions

1. Make the foundation of the nest of jelly or blanc mange
2. Rasp the skin of 3 lemons and preserve it
3. Lay it around and on the jelly like straw
4. Take out the contents of 4 eggs through a small hole
5. Fill the shells with blanc mange
6. When cold, break off the shells and lay the mange eggs in the nest
