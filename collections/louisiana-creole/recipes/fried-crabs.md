## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Fine Large Crabs
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 6 Tablespoonfuls of Butter or Lard
- Stale Bread Crumbs, Grated
- Thyme
- Parsley
- Bay Leaf
- Onions
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Boil the crabs according to recipe.
2. Cut off the claws and crack and cut the crabs into quarters as for stewing.
3. Season well with pepper and salt.
4. Have ready a pint of milk.
5. Mix pepper and salt in proportions of about two teaspoonfuls each.
6. Have ready a pan of boiling lard and a plate of grated bread crumbs.
7. Dip the crabs into the milk and then roll in the bread crumbs and drop into the boiling lard, frying about ten minutes or until a golden brown.
8. Serve on a platter with the claws piled in the center, the bodies of the crabs grouped nicely around, and garnish with sprigs of parsley.
