## Ingredients

- milk
- wheat flour
- 4 eggs
- a gill of yeast
- a spoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Make a thick batter of milk and wheat flour
2. Add eggs and beat light
3. Add yeast and butter
4. Let it rise some hours
5. As you take them out of the iron, butter and sprinkle them
