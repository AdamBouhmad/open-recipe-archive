## Ingredients

- 6 ounces of rice
- 6 ounces of flour
- the yolks and whites of 9 eggs
- 1 lb. of lump sugar pounded and sifted
- 1 ounce of carraway seeds

## Directions

1. Beat this 1 hour
2. Bake it for the same length of time in a quick oven
