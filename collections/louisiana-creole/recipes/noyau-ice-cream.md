## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 8 Eggs
- A Gill of Noyau

## Directions

1. Boil the milk and take from the fire, and then add the sugar and the yolks of the eggs, beaten to a thick cream
2. Add a gill of Noyau Extract and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
3. Set to cool, and then turn into a mold and freeze as above directed
