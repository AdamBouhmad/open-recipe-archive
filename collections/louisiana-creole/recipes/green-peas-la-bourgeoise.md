## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Peas
- 1½ Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Ounce of Chopped Onion
- 1 Herb Bouquet, Minced Fine
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- ½ Pint of Cream or Milk or Water

## Directions

1. Shell and boil the peas until done, about forty minutes if the peas are large.
2. Add sprigs of parsley, thyme and bay leaf, finely minced.
3. Drain off all water and add one tablespoonful and a half of butter.
4. Blend the flour, and add the milk, preferably, mixing together and stirring well, to prevent browning or burning.
5. Serve hot.
