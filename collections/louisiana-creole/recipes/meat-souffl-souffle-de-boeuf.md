## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Cold Meat
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Cup of Cold Milk
- 2 Eggs
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Put two level tablespoonfuls of butter into a frying pan, and when it is hot add two tablespoonfuls of flour, rubbing smoothly and letting it brown.
2. Then add gradually one cup of cold milk.
3. Stir this until it boils.
4. Add one-half teaspoonful of salt; a little pepper and one cupful of chopped meat, or fowl, that has been left over.
5. When this comes to a boil, add the yolk of two beaten eggs.
6. Let it cook a moment longer and set to cool.
7. Then beat the whites of the eggs and when the meat mixture is cold, fold them in carefully.
8. Turn this into a buttered dish and bake in a moderate oven twenty minutes.
9. Serve as soon as removed from the fire.
