## Ingredients

- one cocoanut
- six oranges or one pineapple
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the cocoanut, and slice the oranges or pineapple
2. In a glass dish lay a layer of fruit, and a layer of the grated cocoanut, until your bowl is full
3. Strew powdered sugar over each layer of fruit, and on the top
