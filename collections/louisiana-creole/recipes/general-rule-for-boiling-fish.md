## Ingredients

- fish
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- cloves

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the fish thoroughly
2. Make a small letter "S" with knife on the back
3. Pass twine around the body of the fish so as to secure it
4. Never wrap or tie in a cloth
5. Have ready a kettle of boiling water
6. Throw in a sprig of onion, thyme and bay leaf, eight or ten cloves
