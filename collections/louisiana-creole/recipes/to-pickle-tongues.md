## Ingredients

- a beefs tongue
- 1 pound of white salt
- 1 ounce of saltpetre
- 1 pound of coarse salt

## Directions

1. Take a beefs tongue, rub it with 1 pound of white salt, 1 ounce of saltpetre, 1 pound of coarse salt
2. Rub it well and turn it every day in the pickle for 2 weeks
3. This pickle will do several tongues, only adding a little more white salt
