## Ingredients

- 1½ Cups of Milk or Water
- 1½ Cups of White Sugar
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour or Cornstarch
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered White Sugar
- The Juice and Grated Zest of 2 Lemons

## Directions

1. Have ready the pie pans, with the baked crust
2. Beat the sugar and the yolks well together, and add the juice and grated zest of the lemons
3. Blend the flour and milk gradually, and pour it through a sieve into the eggs
4. Mix thoroughly
5. Fill the lined pans with this, and set in the oven to bake for twenty or thirty minutes
6. Add gradually three tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar to the whites of the eggs, beating steadily all the time, and when it is all absorbed beat it to a stiff froth
7. Place this over the top of the pie by spoonfuls, and then smooth lightly with the spoon
8. Place in the oven, and let it brown
