## Ingredients

- eggs
- 1 tumbler of brown sugar
- 1 lb. of melted butter
- 1 quart of flour
- 1 tumbler of milk with 1 teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Pour eggs well beaten with a tumbler of brown sugar
2. Add 1 lb. of melted butter
3. Add 1 quart of flour
4. Add 1 tumbler of milk with 1 teaspoonful of soda
5. Mix all well together
6. Pour into a pan, and bake in a quick oven
7. When done, serve it with wine and sugar sauce
