## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Powdered Sugar
- 1/2 Cup of Butter
- 1 Wineglassful of Brandy
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Ground Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream.
2. Add the brandy and the powdered cinnamon and serve cold.
