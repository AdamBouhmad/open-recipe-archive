## Ingredients

- The Bouilli
- ¾ Dozen Tomatoes
- 2 Cups of Bouillon
- ¼ Teaspoonful of Flour
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme and Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Butter
- Salt

## Directions

1. Take a half dozen fine, ripe tomatoes, and parboil them in butter, being careful not to let them burn.
2. Add a pinch of flour and two good cups of bouillon, a little salt
