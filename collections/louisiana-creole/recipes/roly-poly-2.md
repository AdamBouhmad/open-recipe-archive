## Ingredients

- 1 lb. veal
- 1 lb. lean beef
- 1/4 lb. pork
- 1/4 loaf bread, chopped fine
- 1 chopped onion
- spice to taste
- 3 eggs
- beef stock, shin of veal, or calves' feet for basting

## Directions

1. Chop bread fine, wet it and squeeze the water out
2. Mix chopped onion and spice to taste
3. Beat 3 eggs well into the mixture
4. Form into a roll
5. Bake 1.5 hours, basting all the while with beef stock, shin of veal, or calves' feet
6. Set it away to cool
7. Cut in slices and serve
