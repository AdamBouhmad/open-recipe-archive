## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoonful of mace
- 1 teaspoonful of allspice
- 1/2 spoonful of cloves
- 1 onion
- a handful of parsley
- 1 teacup of wine
- water
- 1 large spoonful of butter
- 3 spoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Have the scales well taken from the fish, well washed and wiped dry
2. put the fish into the pan and half fry it
3. chop mace, allspice, cloves, onion, and parsley very fine together
4. rub the butter and flour well together, then add warm water to thin it
5. add all the ingredients
6. put the fish on the strainer, and then in the kettle
7. pour the gravy over it
8. stew it half an hour
9. dish it for the table
