## Ingredients

- 1 Young Hen
- 1/4 Pound of Nice Bacon
- 1 Lemon
- 2 Carrots
- 2 Onions
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- 1 1/2 Cups Broth
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Thyme
- Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf

## Directions

1. Clean and singe the chicken nicely, and, after taking out the entrails, truss it as in roasting turkey
2. Place in the frying pan small pieces of fat bacon, cut in very slender strips of about the size of your finger
3. Place over this slices of lemon, very fine, and cover again with slender bits of bacon
4. Moisten this with a half cup of water and lay over two carrots, cut in thin slices, and two onions, cut likewise, and a teaspoonful each of thyme, parsley and one bay leaf, minced fine
5. Place on top of this the chicken and cover closely
6. Let it cook on a good fire for three-quarters of an hour, or a half hour, if the chicken is exceedingly tender
7. When done, take out the chicken, add one-half cup of broth to the liquor in which it has been boiling
8. Stir well and season highly, and pour over the chicken and serve
