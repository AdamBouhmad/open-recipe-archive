## Ingredients

- 4 Left-Over Potatoes
- Butter
- Sugar
- Milk
- Flour
- Egg

## Directions

1. To every two tablespoonfuls of mashed potatoes add one of butter, one of sugar, one pint of milk, and four tablespoonfuls of flour
2. Beat all well together, and add one egg, well beaten
3. Bake in waffle iron
