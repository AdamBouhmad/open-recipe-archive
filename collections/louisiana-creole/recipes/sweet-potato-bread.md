## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Mashed Sweet Potatoes
- 1½ Pints of Flour
- 1 Pint of Milk or Warm Water
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- ½ Teacup of Fresh Yeast, or Half a Cake of Compressed Yeast

## Directions

1. The potatoes must be baked, peeled and mashed.
2. Put the milk, or water, and the yeast and salt into a bowl or pan; add the butter, which must be melted.
3. Beat thoroughly, and set over night in a warm place.
4. In the morning add the potatoes, mashed and pressed through a sieve, and gradually add the flour, which must be sifted three times.
5. Beat to a light sponge; then cover, and set to rise in a warm place till it doubles its size.
6. Then turn out on a bread board and mold into loaves; put in a slightly greased pan and let rise till light.
7. Then bake in a moderate oven 45 minutes.
