## Ingredients

- cold cabbage
- pot liquor
- water
- milk
- butter
- clarified drippings
- cream
- flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Drain cold cabbage left from dinner from the pot liquor in which it was boiled
2. Simmer for half an hour in water, or milk and water
3. Pour off all the water when it is tender
4. Stir in the pot a lump of butter or clarified drippings
5. Let it cook gently
6. Throw in a cup of milk or cream
7. Thicken it with flour
8. Season with pepper and salt
9. Serve with the cream gravy poured over the cabbage
