## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Flour
- 1 Cup of Water
- 4 Eggs
- 1.5 Cup of Water
- 2 Ounces of Chocolate
- 4 Ounces of Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Make a Cream Puff Paste as directed in Cream Puffs recipe
2. Put into a tube or a pastry bag and press out upon well-buttered tins in the shape of Lady Fingers
3. This will give the eclairs the right shape, making them about five inches long
4. Set them to bake in a Quick oven twenty or thirty minutes
5. Fill in with a mixture made by melting two ounces of chocolate and four ounces of powdered sugar, and set away to cool
6. You may ice by dipping one end into the icing, and then setting away to dry
7. The eclairs may be filled with preserved fruits, Orange Icing, Whipped Cream, or any marmalade
