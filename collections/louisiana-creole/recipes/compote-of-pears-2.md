## Ingredients

- 7 Large, Fine Pears
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Juice of a Lemon
- 1/4 Cup of Water

## Directions

1. Scrape the pears spirally from head to stalk with a tapering tin cutter, to take out the cores
2. Put the sugar and one-half cup of water on the fire, and add to it four or five pears, which you will have cut into pieces
3. Place the whole pears in it, and let them simmer, adding the lemon juice to keep them white
4. When done, place the pears in order in a dish
5. Let the syrup stew down to a nice jelly, and then pour over and serve
