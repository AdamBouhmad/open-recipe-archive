## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 12 whites and 10 yolks of eggs
- 2 glasses of wine

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. These should gradually harden in the oven till quite crisp
3. Frequently turn in the tins
