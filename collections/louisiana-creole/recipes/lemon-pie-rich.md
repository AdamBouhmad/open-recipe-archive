## Ingredients

- five eggs
- two lemons
- one cup and a half of sugar
- puff paste or plain family crust

## Directions

1. Beat all together except the whites of three eggs
2. Beat the whites of three eggs stiff with sugar
3. When the pies are cold spread this icing on top and brown lightly
4. Make the crust of puff paste, or in any way that is liked
