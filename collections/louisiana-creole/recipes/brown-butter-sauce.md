## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pound of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Cut Parsley (not chopped)
- 3 Tablespoonfuls Juice of Lemon or Vinegar
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in a saucepan, and when it begins to smoke it is browning.
2. Then add two tablespoonfuls of cut parsley, and let it brown half a minute longer.
3. Then add three tablespoonfuls of the juice of a lemon or Tarragon Vinegar, and let it simmer two minutes longer, and serve with Stingaree or Rai aux Beurre Noir (see recipe), calves' brains or crawfish boiled.
