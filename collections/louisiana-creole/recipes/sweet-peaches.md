## Ingredients

- 1 peck of cling-stone peaches
- salt
- water
- 1 gallon of vinegar
- 4 lbs. of brown sugar
- allspice
- cloves
- nutmeg
- mace

## Directions

1. Cover peaches with salt and water
2. Let them remain all night
3. In the morning, take them out
4. To 1 gallon of vinegar add 4 lbs. of brown sugar, with allspice, cloves, nutmeg and mace to the taste
5. Boil it, and pour it over the peaches hot
6. Do the same 4 days in succession
