## Ingredients

- 1½ Cups of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Corn Starch or 2 of Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- The Juice and Inner Fruit and Zest of Two Grated Lemons

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and butter to a cream
2. Add the eggs, the yolks and the white beaten separately, and then add the hot water and the juice and meat of the lemon and the grated zest
3. Moisten the flour or cornstarch with a little cold water, and stir into the mixture
4. Line the pie pans with plain paste or pie crust
5. Bake the under crust a few minutes
6. Fill the pans with the mixture, cover with an upper crust, and bake in a quick oven for half an hour
