## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 4 potatoes
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- a little salt
- flour
- 1 small teacup of home made yeast

## Directions

1. Pass the potatoes through a cullender with the milk
2. Mix enough flour to make it the thickness of muffins
3. Add yeast and let it stand 4 hours
4. Add sufficient flour to make it as stiff as bread dough
5. Mould it off and put it in pans
6. Let it remain for 3 hours
7. Bake in a quick oven 20 minutes
