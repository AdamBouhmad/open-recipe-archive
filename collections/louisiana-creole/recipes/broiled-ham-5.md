## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 Thin Slices of Boiled Ham
- 6 or 8 Slices of Buttered Toast

## Directions

1. Always use boiled ham for broiling.
2. Slice it about a half inch thick, according to the number to be served, and trim off the rough edges.
3. Have the broiler very hot, lay the slices of ham upon it and brown well.
4. Serve with buttered toast.
