## Ingredients

- 1 quart of thick gruel
- 1 large tablespoonful of butter
- 9 eggs
- a little salt
- 2 spoonfuls of flour and cornmeal
- 1 small teaspoonful of soda
- warm water

## Directions

1. Cook the butter in the gruel
2. Remove from the fire and pour into a wooden bowl
3. Stir until almost cool
4. Add the eggs and meal
5. Dissolve 1 small teaspoonful of soda in warm water and add lastly
6. The cornmeal should be white and fresh
