## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 2 lbs. of flour
- 1 lb. of good brown sugar
- 4 well beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Rub the butter with the flour till quite fine and smooth
2. Add the brown sugar
3. Roll fine
4. Mix all together with the beaten eggs
5. Break the paste into small bits
6. Bake upon floured tins
