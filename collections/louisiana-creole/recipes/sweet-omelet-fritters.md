## Ingredients

- A Plain Omelet
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Brandy
- Creole Fritter Batter
- Powdered White Sugar

## Directions

1. Make an omelet, and add two tablespoonfuls of sugar and a tablespoonful of good brandy.
2. Cook it nicely and very soft, and then cut it into small slices.
3. Plunge these into the fritter batter, let them fry to a golden brown, and then drain in the mouth of the oven on brown paper.
4. Place in a heated dish, sprinkle well with fine white sugar, and serve hot.
