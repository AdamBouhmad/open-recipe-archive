## Ingredients

- a quart of cream sweet or sour
- five eggs
- a cup of sugar
- soda optional
- two tablespoonfuls of yeast powder or baking powder optional
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix cream, eggs and sugar
2. If the cream be sour, add soda to sweeten it; if sweet, put in two tablespoonfuls of yeast powder or any good baking powder
3. Add flour to mix
4. Roll out
5. Fry in boiling lard
