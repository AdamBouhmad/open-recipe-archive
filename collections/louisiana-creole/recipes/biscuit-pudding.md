## Ingredients

- 1 pint of boiling milk
- 3 Naples biscuits
- 4 eggs
- nutmeg
- a little brandy
- a spoonful of sugar
- flour

## Directions

1. Pour a pint of boiling milk over 3 Naples biscuits grated
2. Cover it close
3. When cold, add the yolks of 4 eggs and the whites of 2, nutmeg, a little brandy, a spoonful of sugar and flour
4. Boil it 1 hour in a bowl
