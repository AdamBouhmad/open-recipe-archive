## Ingredients

- 2 ounces of blanched almonds or peach kernels
- 1 white of an egg, beaten to a stiff froth
- refined sugar

## Directions

1. Throw peach kernels in cold water to take off the bitter taste if using those
2. Drain and pound in a mortar until they become a stiff paste
3. Take the white of an egg and beat it to a stiff froth
4. Mix it with the almonds
5. Sift in as much refined sugar as will turn it to a stiff dough
6. Form it into small cakes
7. Put them on sheets of tin which must be greased
8. Bake them in a very moderate oven
