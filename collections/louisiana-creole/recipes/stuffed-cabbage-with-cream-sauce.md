## Ingredients

- 1 Fine Head Cabbage
- 1 Dozen Fine Chaurice
- 1 Onion
- 3 Sprigs each of Thyme, Parsley and Bay Leaf
- 1 Square inch of Ham
- 1 Inch of a Red Pepper Pod
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Select a fine tender head of cabbage. Pick off the outer leaves, and let it soak in cold fresh water for an hour.
2. Then throw the whole head into a pot of boiling water for about three-quarters of an hour.
3. In the meantime make a stuffing by taking one dozen fine Chaurice, or sausage-meat, one onion, and three sprigs each of thyme and parsley, and one bay leaf.
4. Mince the herbs and onion very fine, and add to them one square inch of finely minced ham.
5. Chop the sausage meat over, and mingle this with the ham and herbs, and then add the finely minced onion and one clove of finely minced garlic.
6. Mix these well together. Season to taste.
7. Take the cabbage out of the water, and open carefully to the very heart, and put in a teaspoonful of the dressing.
8. Fold over this two or three leaves, and then insert the mixture in between another layer of leaves, and so continue until each layer of leaves has been nicely stuffed.
9. Press all firmly together, and then tie in the large leaves, which you will have boiled with the cabbage head.
10. Put it into the kettle of boiling water, and add a little salt and two inches of a red pepper pod, thyme, parsley and bay leaf.
11. Let it boil for two hours.
12. When done carefully untie the larger leaves in which you have cooked the cabbage, and lay the head in a well-heated dish.
13. Pour over all a Cream Sauce, and serve hot.
