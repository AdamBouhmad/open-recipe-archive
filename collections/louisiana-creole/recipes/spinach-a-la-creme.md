## Ingredients

- ½ Peck of Spinach
- 1 Cupful of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Powdered Sugar
- 6 Croutons
- A Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the spinach as for Plain Boiled Spinach.
2. When ready to take from the saucepan, have ready slices of buttered toast, or Croutons fried in butter.
3. Roll the Croutons in sugar before frying.
4. Heap the spinach in little mounds upon them, sprinkle lightly with sugar, and pour over a Cream Sauce, and serve hot.
