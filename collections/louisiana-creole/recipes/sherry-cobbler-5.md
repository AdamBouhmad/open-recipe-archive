## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Lemonade
- 1/2 Bottle of Sherry or any Other Wine
- Small Bits of Lemon Peel

## Directions

1. To one quart of lemonade add half a bottle of Sherry and proceed to freeze as directed above, or hand around in glasses half full of crushed ice
2. Add a little lemon peel
