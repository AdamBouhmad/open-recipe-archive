## Ingredients

- two eggs
- a cup of sweet milk
- one cup of water
- three cups of flour
- two tablespoonfuls of yeast powder
- a tablespoonful of melted lard or butter
- a teaspoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix two tablespoonfuls of yeast powder with flour before sifting
2. Combine eggs, milk, water, sifted flour, melted lard or butter, and sugar
3. Mix all well
4. Bake in waffle irons
