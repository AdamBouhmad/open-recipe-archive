## Ingredients

- 1 Filet of Beef
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard
- 6 Onions, sliced fine
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 2 Sprigs each of Thyme and Bay Leaf
- 3 Sprigs of Parsley
- ½ Can of Tomatoes or 6 Large Fresh Ones
- 1 Pint of Hot Water
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Smother the filet in exactly the same manner as already described
2. When cooked for about a half hour, add one-half can of tomatoes and their juice, or six large fresh tomatoes sliced in their juice
3. Let the mixture simmer for an hour and a half longer
4. Season well and serve, pouring the gravy over the filet
