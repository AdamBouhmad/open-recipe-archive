## Ingredients

- 4 Layers of Cake
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Powdered Sugar
- 2 Cups of Grated Cocoanut
- White Sugar to Sprinkle

## Directions

1. Make a good layer cake
2. Beat the whites of two eggs till very frothy
3. Add one cup of powdered sugar, and beat till very stiff
4. Grate the cocoanut, and then proceed as in filling the Almond Cake, having a layer of the cocoanut on top, sprinkled with white sugar
