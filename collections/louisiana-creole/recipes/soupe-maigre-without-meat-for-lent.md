## Ingredients

- Half a pound of butter
- Six onions sliced
- Two heads of celery cut small
- One-half a head of white cabbage
- A bunch of chopped parsley
- Three rolled crackers
- Two quarts of boiling milk, or milk and water
- Two well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Melt half a pound of butter in a stewpan, put in six onions sliced;
2. Add two heads of celery cut small, one-half a head of white cabbage, and a bunch of chopped parsley;
3. Let them boil twenty minutes, then stir in three rolled crackers;
4. Pour in two quarts of boiling milk, or milk and water;
5. Let this boil up gently for half an hour, and just before serving stir in two well-beaten eggs.
