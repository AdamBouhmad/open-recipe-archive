## Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Grated Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Set the milk to boil, and beat the yolks of the eggs and sugar very light: add them to the boiling milk
2. Stir for two minutes over the fire
3. Take off and add the Vanilla and the whites of the eggs beaten to a froth; mix thoroughly, and serve either hot or cold.
