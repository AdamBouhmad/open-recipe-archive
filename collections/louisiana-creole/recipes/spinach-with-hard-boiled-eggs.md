## Ingredients

- ½ Peck of Spinach
- 1 Cupful of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 3 Hard-Boiled Eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the spinach and prepare to the point indicated in recipe for Spinach Plain Boiled.
2. Then take the spinach from the saucepan, and arrange in a mound on a dish, and garnish with slices of hard-boiled eggs.
