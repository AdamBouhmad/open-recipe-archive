## Ingredients

- half a pound of almonds
- a little rose-water
- half a pound of sifted sugar
- the whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound with a little rose-water half a pound of almonds
2. add half a pound of sifted sugar
3. add the whites of two eggs (not beaten), form into a paste
4. Dip your hand in water, and roll the preparation into balls the size of a nutmeg
5. lay them an inch apart, on buttered paper, in a baking tin
6. Bake in a slow oven until a light brown
