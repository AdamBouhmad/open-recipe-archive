## Ingredients

- ham
- onions
- cloves
- parsley
- sweet herbs
- sugar
- bread crumbs
- vegetables
- savory jelly

## Directions

1. Soak and clean your ham, boil it with onions, cloves, parsley and sweet herbs until it is nearly done, then let it cool in its own liquor.
2. When cold, pull off the skin and place the ham in the oven gate, with a little sugar and bread crumbs over it till it is brown.
3. If it is to be eaten hot, serve with vegetables and some acid or piquant sauce; if cold, send up savory jelly, No. 21.
