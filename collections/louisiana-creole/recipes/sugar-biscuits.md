## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of sifted flour
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 1/2 lbs. of powdered sugar
- 1/2 pint of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of brandy
- 1 small teaspoonful of soda dissolved in water
- 4 tablespoonfuls of carraway seed

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake
