## Ingredients

- five ounces of sweet almonds
- three ounces of bitter almonds or peach kernels
- a little white of egg
- half a pound of sifted flour
- a pound of sugar
- four yolks of eggs
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound five ounces of sweet, and three ounces of bitter almonds (or peach kernels), with a little white of egg
2. Put half a pound of sifted flour on your dough board, make a hole in the middle of the flour
3. in which put the almonds, with a pound of sugar, four yolks of eggs, and a little salt
4. Make into a paste
5. Cut in pieces the size of a nut
6. lay them half an inch apart, on sheets of paper, in a baking-pan
7. bake in a moderate oven for fifteen or twenty minutes
