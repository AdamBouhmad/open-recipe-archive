## Ingredients

- 1 claret glass filled with crushed ice
- 2½ tablespoonfuls of crème de noyau
- Seltzer

## Directions

1. Put two tablespoonfuls of crème de noyau in a glass of finely-crushed ice and seltzer and serve at almost freezing point.
