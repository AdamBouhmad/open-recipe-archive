## Ingredients

- 3 Pounds of Brisket of Lamb
- 1 Onion
- 2 Carrots
- 1/2 Dozen Cloves
- 1 Leek
- 1 Bouquet of Thyme, Parsley and Bay Leaf
- 3/4 Pound of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls Flour
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 1/2 Can of Mushrooms or Green Peas
- Salt and Pepper
- 1 Pint of water
- The juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the brisket of the lamb into pieces of two square inches
2. Put in a stew pot and cover with a half gallon of water, and add salt and pepper and two onions and one carrot, chopped fine
3. Let it boil till very tender
4. Take the meat out of the saucepan and keep the water in which it was boiled
5. Take another saucepan and put a tablespoonful of butter in it, and as it melts add a tablespoonful of flour
6. Let it brown lightly, and add one pint of the water in which the meat was boiled
7. Stir well, making it very light, and not thick
8. Add one-half can of mushrooms, and let the whole boil about fifteen minutes, so as to be very light
9. Put in the meat, which is already cooked
10. Let it simmer for about fifteen minutes longer, and take off the fire and add the yolks of two eggs, well beaten, two tablespoonfuls of the gravy, and the juice of one lemon
11. Serve hot
