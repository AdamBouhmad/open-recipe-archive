## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Flour
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Heaping Teaspoonful of Lard
- 1 Pint of Milk or Water, or Half and Half

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and add the salt, mixing thoroughly.
2. Then add the lard, and blend by rubbing through the hands till not a lump remains in the flour.
3. Now add gradually the water or milk, or the milk and water combined, using half and half of each, and knead all the whole till the dough, which must not be too soft, but rather stiff, is formed.
4. Then lay the dough on a biscuit board on a block, and beat for a half hour with a rolling pin.
5. Knead lightly, and beat again for a full ten minutes, till from every portion of the surface and sides the air bubbles or blisters form.
6. Then roll out and cut into round biscuits with the cutter, or square ones with a knife, and stick here and there with a fork.
7. Bake in a moderate oven for about ten or fifteen minutes, till a delicate brown above and below.
