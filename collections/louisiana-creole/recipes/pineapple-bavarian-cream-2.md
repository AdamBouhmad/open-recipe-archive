## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Grated Pineapple
- 1 Pint of Cream
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 3/4 Pint of Gelatine
- 3/4 Cup of Cold Water

## Directions

1. Cover the gelatine with the water, and let it soak for half an hour
2. Stand it over boiling water, and let it melt
3. Grate the pineapple, and add the sugar, and let it simmer gently till reduced to a liquid
4. Stir the gelatine into the pineapple, and dissolve thoroughly
5. Place it in a tin vessel, and stand over cracked ice, stirring constantly
6. When it begins to thicken, add the whipped cream, and set away to harden, after turning into a mold
7. Serve with whipped cream
