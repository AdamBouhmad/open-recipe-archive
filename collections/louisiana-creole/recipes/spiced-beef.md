## Ingredients

- 10 pounds round of beef
- 1 teaspoonful of saltpetre
- 1 tablespoonful of brown sugar
- salt
- allspice
- salt pork
- water

## Directions

1. Take a round of beef of 10 pounds with 1 teaspoonful of saltpetre and 1 tablespoonful of brown sugar to stand in it about 2 days
2. Rub in a slop-bowl full of salt and let stand 10 days, turning it every day
3. Sprinkle over about a spoonful of allspice
4. Cut salt pork in strips size of your finger and have them well dipped in ground allspice
5. Gash the beef with a knife and stuff in thick as you please on both sides
6. Put in a large kettle and cover well with water and the juices from the meat
7. Boil slowly about 6 hours
8. When cold turn and trim
