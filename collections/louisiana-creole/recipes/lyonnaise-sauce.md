## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Tomatoes
- Equal Parts of Onions
- 1/2 Spoon of Butter
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme and Bay Leaf, Minced Fine
- Sherry to Taste
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- Salt and Pepper and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Make a good Tomato Sauce
2. Add to this the equal parts of onion browned in butter
3. Stir well, add a little lemon juice, and serve with any meats
