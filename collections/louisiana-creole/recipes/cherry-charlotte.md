## Ingredients

- 1 Pound Cherries
- 1 Pound White Sugar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- The Soft of Bread

## Directions

1. Seed the cherries, and butter the bottom of a pudding dish.
2. Cover the bottom with a layer of the bread crumbs which must be soft.
3. Add a layer of the cherries, which have been cooked like the apples, into a marmalade.
4. Sprinkle with sugar, and add another layer of cherries, and then a layer of bread crumbs, alternating till the dish is filled, letting the last layer be of crumbs.
5. Dot the last layer with bits of butter.
6. Put in an oven and bake for an hour.
7. Serve cold with cream.
