## Ingredients

- pair of young chickens
- water
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Cut up a pair of young chickens
2. Lay them in a pan of cold water to extract the blood
3. Wipe them dry
4. Season with pepper and salt
5. Dredge them with flour
6. Fry them in lard
7. Both sides should be of a rich brown
8. Take them out of the pan and keep them near the fire
