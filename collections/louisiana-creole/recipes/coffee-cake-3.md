## Ingredients

- 1 cup of sugar
- 1 cup of butter
- 1 cup of molasses
- 1 egg
- 1 cup of warm coffee
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 4 cups of flour
- 1 lb. of raisins or currants
- 1 tablespoonful of cloves
- 1 tablespoonful of cinnamon
- 1 tablespoonful of nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, butter, molasses, and egg well together
2. Add 1 cup of warm coffee with 1 teaspoonful of soda
3. Add 4 cups of flour, 1 lb. of raisins or currants, 1 tablespoonful each of cloves, cinnamon and nutmeg
