## Ingredients

- 6 quinces
- little water
- lemon peel
- sugar
- 4 eggs
- ½ pint of cream
- mace and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pare quinces, cut them in quarters
2. Stew them with a little water and lemon peel
3. Let them cook gently till soft
4. Rub them through a sieve
5. Mix with sugar till very sweet
6. Add 4 eggs, ½ pint of cream, mace and nutmeg
7. Bake it in paste
