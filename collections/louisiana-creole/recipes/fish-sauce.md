## Ingredients

- 1 pound of anchovies
- 1 pint of port wine
- 1/2 pint of strong vinegar
- 1 onion
- a few cloves
- a little allspice
- whole pepper
- a few blades of mace
- a handful of thyme, green or dried
- 1 large lemon, sliced with the skin

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients into a saucepan
2. cover close
3. stew gently until the anchovies are dissolved
4. strain and bottle for use
