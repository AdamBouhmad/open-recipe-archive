## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 Parsnips
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Chopped Parsley to Garnish

## Directions

1. Boil the parsnips as directed, and then cut into strips of the length of the parsnip, and half an inch in width.
2. Put a big cookingspoonful of butter into the saucepan, and add the parsnips.
3. Sprinkle well with salt and pepper.
4. Cover and let them fry, but only slightly brown, on either side.
5. Serve with chopped parsley as a garnish.
