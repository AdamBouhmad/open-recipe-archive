## Ingredients

- 1½ Pints of Fresh Milk
- 2 Quarts of White Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 2 Ounces of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Teaspoonful of Sugar (which may be omitted, according to taste)
- ¾ Cake of Compressed Yeast, Dissolved in Warm Water

## Directions

1. Boil the milk, take off the stove, and add the butter, sugar and salt.
2. Then add the well-beaten eggs.
3. Stir in about one and a half pounds of flour, more or less, to make a stiff batter, and beat till air bubbles accumulate all over the surface.
4. Then cover with a close cloth and set to rise over night in a warm place.
5. Then take up the dough and knead lightly with the remaining flour to make a nice, soft dough, and shape with your hands into nice, small rolls or balls.
6. Set them together in the baking pan, which you will have lightly sprinkled with flour.
7. Then set to rise for an hour longer.
8. Set in the oven, and bake about a half hour.
