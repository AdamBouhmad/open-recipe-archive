## Ingredients

- 4 loaves of bread
- 3 quarts of wheat flour
- 3 quarts of boiling water
- 12 spoonfuls of family yeast
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix the bread, flour and boiling water thoroughly
2. Let the mixture remain until lukewarm
3. Add the yeast
4. Put it in a warm place to rise
5. When light, knead in flour to make it sufficiently stiff
6. Mould off and let it stand for a minute rising
7. Bake it
