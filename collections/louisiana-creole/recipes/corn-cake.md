## Ingredients

- 1 quart of sour milk
- sifted cornmeal
- a teaspoonful of salt
- a teaspoonful of soda
- a cup of milk
- 1 teacup of good molasses
- 1 large spoonful of lard or butter

## Directions

1. Thicken the sour milk with sifted cornmeal
2. Dissolve a teaspoonful of soda in a cup of milk
3. Add the salt, molasses, and lard or butter
4. Beat these ingredients well together
5. Pour it into a well greased iron baking pan with an iron cover
6. Put it in the fire-place when the fire is raked for the night
7. Put fire and hot ashes over and around it
8. In the morning you will have a nice brown loaf for breakfast
