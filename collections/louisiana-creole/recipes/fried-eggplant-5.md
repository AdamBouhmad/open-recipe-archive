## Ingredients

- 2 Young Eggplants
- 2 Eggs
- Flour to Make a Light Batter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Lard
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Slice the eggplants very thin, paring them if large, and leaving the skin on if very young and tender.
2. Make a light batter with the eggs and flour.
3. Season the eggplant well with salt and pepper.
4. Soak the slices in the batter.
5. Lift out and fry in the boiling lard.
6. When done on one side, turn on the other with a cake turner.
7. Remove the eggplants.
8. Drain them on brown paper in the mouth of the oven, and serve hot on a flat and open dish or platter.
