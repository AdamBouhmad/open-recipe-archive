## Ingredients

- 1 pint of large roasted chestnuts
- 1 pint of boiling stock
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Roast the chestnuts, and peel and mash them very fine
2. Make a Brown Roux with the flour and butter
3. Add the boiling stock
4. Let it boil for about five minutes
5. Add the mashed chestnuts, stirring constantly, and seasoning to taste
6. Let it boil for two minutes
7. Take off and serve hot with Broiled Dindonneau (turkey chicks) or Roast Turkey
