## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of chaurice (about 6 to a pound)
- 1 clove of minced garlic
- 1/2 can of tomatoes
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of black pepper
- 1 large onion
- 1/2 spoon of lard
- About 1/2 cup of boiling water

## Directions

1. Place a half teaspoonful of lard in the frying pan or stewpan, and when it heats, add the chopped onion.
2. Let this brown slightly and then add the minced garlic.
3. Add the half can of tomatoes.
4. As this browns, put in the sausage which you have pricked gently.
5. Cover and let them simmer for about five minutes, then add the seasonings to taste.
6. Add about a half cup of boiling water.
7. Cover well and let all simmer for twenty minutes longer.
