## Ingredients

- onions
- fresh butter
- eggs
- milk
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel and slice 10 or 12 mild onions, and fry them in fresh butter
2. Drain them well when you take them up
3. Mince them as fine as possible
4. Beat 4 eggs until very thick and light
5. Stir them gradually into 1 pint of milk in turn with the minced onions
6. Season the whole with plenty of grated nutmeg, and stir it very hard
7. Put into a deep white dish, and bake it about 1 hour
8. Send it to table as a side dish, to be eaten with poultry
