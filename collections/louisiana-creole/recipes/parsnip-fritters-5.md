## Ingredients

- parsnips
- salted water
- egg
- spoonful of milk
- flour
- pepper
- salt
- hot lard

## Directions

1. Boil the parsnips in salted water until they are done
2. Make a batter of an egg, a spoonful of milk and flour, pepper and salt
3. When the parsnips are cool enough to handle cut them in rounds
4. Dip them in the batter and drop them into hot lard
5. Fry a light brown, turn them and fry the other side
6. Drain them from the grease when brown on both sides
