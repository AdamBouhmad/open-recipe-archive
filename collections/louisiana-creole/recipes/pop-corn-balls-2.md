## Ingredients

- six quarts of pop corn
- one pint of molasses

## Directions

1. Boil one pint of molasses about fifteen minutes
2. Put the corn into a large pan
3. Pour the boiled molasses over it
4. Stir it briskly until thoroughly mixed
5. With clean hands make into balls of the desired size
