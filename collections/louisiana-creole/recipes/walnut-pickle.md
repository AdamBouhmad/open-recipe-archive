## Ingredients

- walnuts
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- ground pepper
- ginger
- mace
- cloves
- nutmegs
- mustard seed

## Directions

1. Pick the walnuts about the Fourth of July when they should be so soft that a pin can be run through them
2. Lay them in salt and water ten days, change the water two or three times during the ten days
3. Rub off the outside with a coarse cloth
4. For one hundred nuts, make a pickle of two quarts of vinegar, one ounce of ground pepper, same of ginger, half an ounce of mace, cloves, nutmegs and mustard seed
5. Put these spices in a bag, lay it in the vinegar and boil all together a few minutes
6. Set the pickle away for use
7. If the vinegar is not very strong, add fresh vinegar to the last scalding of the pickles
