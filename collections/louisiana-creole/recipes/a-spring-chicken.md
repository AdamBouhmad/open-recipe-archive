## Ingredients

- A Spring Chicken
- Melted Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Select spring chickens for broiling. For a family of six several will be required.
2. Clean the chickens, nicely, singe, and then split down the middle of the back, laying the chicken open.
3. Break the breastbone with a mallet and flatten out the chicken.
4. Season well with salt and pepper, and brush with melted butter.
5. Have the broiler ready over a moderate fire, and place the chicken between the double broiler and let the fowl broil slowly for about a half hour, if the chicken is very tender, otherwise three-quarters of an hour.
6. Keep a plate over it all the time, as it will retain its flavor better.
7. Turn the chicken frequently, so that it may be broiled through and through.
8. It should be slightly browned on the skin side.
9. When done, place in a heated dish, pour over melted butter and garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.
10. A garnish of cresses is very pretty. The dish is then called 'Poulet Grille aux Cressons.'
