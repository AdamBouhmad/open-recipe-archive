## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- 1 Quart of Water
- ¾ Ounce of Yeast
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- ¼ Pound of Lard
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sugar (if desired)

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar (if desired), the yeast and salt, with the milk and the water.
2. Then add flour sufficient to make a nice dough of medium firmness.
3. Add the lard, and work the dough until smooth.
4. Then form into a round mass and set to rise.
5. When the dough has doubled its size, work anew and mold into small round balls and set into greased baking pans at a little distance apart from one another.
6. Set to rise again and when they have doubled their size bake in a brisk oven.
