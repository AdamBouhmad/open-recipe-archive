## Ingredients

- 1 quart of flour
- 1 tablespoonful of yeast
- new milk
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Add as much milk as will make a slack dough
2. Add salt and set it to rise
3. In the morning work it up with very little flour
4. Bake in a brisk oven
