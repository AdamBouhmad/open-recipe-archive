## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- one pound of powdered sugar
- six eggs
- one pound of corn starch

## Directions

1. Rub half a pound of butter to a cream with one pound of powdered sugar
2. Beat six eggs lightly
3. Add one pound of corn starch
4. Flavor and bake in small patty pans
