## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Claret
- 1½ Pounds of Sugar
- 6 Oranges
- 3 Lemons
- ¾ Ounce of Cinnamon (Whole Stick)

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from the oranges and lemons
2. Take the peeling of the oranges and lemons and boil with one quart of water and the sugar and cinnamon
3. Strain, and then add the claret and lemon juice and orange juice
4. Let all come to a boil again and serve hot
