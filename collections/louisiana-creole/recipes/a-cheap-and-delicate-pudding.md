## Ingredients

- a tablespoonful of butter
- a cup of sugar
- a cup of milk
- two eggs
- a pint of sifted flour
- a small teaspoonful of soda
- two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- two tablespoonfuls of water
- lemon sauce or rich wine sauce

## Directions

1. Put into the flour a small teaspoonful of soda and two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar; sift this in carefully, and set the flour aside
2. Beat the eggs, yolks and whites together, briskly until they foam
3. Add to the eggs two tablespoonfuls of water; beat them sharply again until the tissues of the eggs thoroughly blend with the water
4. Mix the sugar and butter together; add the eggs, beat again
5. Pour in the flour which will make a stiff batter
6. Thin this with the small cup of milk (sweet milk is the best)
7. Bake in shallow pans and serve with lemon sauce, or a rich wine sauce if that is preferred
