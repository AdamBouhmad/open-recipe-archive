## Ingredients

- Lamb Sweetbreads
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Glass of Madeira Wine
- 1 Small Onion
- 1 Shallot
- 3 Mushrooms
- 1/4 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley
- 1 Gill of Sauce Espagnole

## Directions

1. Clean and pare and blanch the sweetbreads.
2. Then set aside to cool.
3. Lard nicely with very fine larding needles.
4. Put two tablespoonfuls of butter in a saucepan and add the sweetbreads.
5. Pour over one glass of Madeira Wine, cover well with the onion, shallot, mushrooms, clove of garlic and parsley, all minced very fine.
6. Cover with a piece of buttered paper and set in the oven for ten minutes or until they are a nice golden brown color.
7. Then take out of the oven and lay the sweetbreads on a dish.
8. Set the saucepan back on the stove and add a gill of Sauce Espagnole.
9. Let all cook for five minutes.
10. Have ready six small boxes of buttered paper; pour a little of the gravy in the bottom of each case, then place a sweetbread in each and set in a baking dish.
11. Set the dish in the oven for five minutes.
12. Then arrange a napkin nicely in a dish, set the cases of sweetbreads upon it and send to the table hot.
