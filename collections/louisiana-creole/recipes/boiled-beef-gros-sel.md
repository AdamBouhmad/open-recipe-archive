## Ingredients

- The Bouilli
- 1 Bunch of Parsley
- 1 Head of Lettuce
- A French Dressing or salt

## Directions

1. Take the bouilli from the bouillon, and serve on a platter, laying the whole on a bed of parsley and lettuce
2. Serve with salt or French dressing
