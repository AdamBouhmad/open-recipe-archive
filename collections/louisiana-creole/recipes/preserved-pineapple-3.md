## Ingredients

- pineapples
- sugar (1 lb. per lb. of fruit)
- water (very small quantity)

## Directions

1. Pare the pineapples and cut them in thick slices
2. Weigh the slices, and to each lb. of fruit put 1 lb. of sugar
3. Dissolve the sugar in a very small quantity of water
4. Stir it and set it over the fire in a kettle
5. Boil it 10 minutes
6. Put in the fruit, letting it remain 1/2 hour
7. Put the preserve in a pan or dish to cool
8. Have the jars ready to put away for use
9. Cover it well with the jelly
10. Exclude it well from the air, and it will keep some time
