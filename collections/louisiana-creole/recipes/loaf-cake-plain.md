## Ingredients

- three cups of sweet milk
- two cups of sugar
- one cup of yeast
- flour
- two eggs well beaten
- fruit and spice to taste

## Directions

1. stir in flour to make it quite thick, and let it rise over night
2. In the morning add two eggs well beaten, fruit and spice to taste
3. let it rise till hen
4. Bake in a slow oven
