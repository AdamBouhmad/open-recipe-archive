## Ingredients

- 1 lb. loaf sugar
- 1 pint spring water
- 2 lbs. apples
- 1 large lemon
- isinglass (optional)

## Directions

1. Boil 1 lb. of loaf sugar in a pint of spring water till it becomes sugar again
2. Pound and core 2 lbs. of apples
3. Put all together in a stewpan, grate in the pulp of a large lemon, and stew it till it becomes a stiff jelly
4. Put it into a mould; it will turn out when cold
5. If the apples are flat, you may add a little lemon juice
6. A custard put in the dish is an improvement; if not stiff enough, add a little isinglass
7. Some think it better to leave out 1 lb. of sugar and reduce the water
