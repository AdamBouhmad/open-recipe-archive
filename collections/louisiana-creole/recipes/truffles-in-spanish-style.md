## Ingredients

- 10 Truffles
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Salad Oil or Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sliced Parsley
- 3 Finely Minced Shallots
- 3 Sprigs of Minced Parsley
- 1/2 Glass of White Wine
- Sauce Espagnole
- Salt and Pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Slice the Truffles very fine, and place in a saucepan on a slow fire, with a tablespoonful of butter or two of oil, salt and pepper to taste and the minced parsley and shallots.
2. After letting them smother for a few minutes, moisten well with the White Wine or Champagne and a Sauce Espagnole, using equal proportions of the wine and sauce.
3. Quicken the fire slightly, and let them cook slowly till done.
4. Skim off all grease, and serve hot with ragouts, etc.
