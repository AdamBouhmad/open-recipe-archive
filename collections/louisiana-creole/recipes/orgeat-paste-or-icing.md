## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Peeled Almonds
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Orange Flower Water

## Directions

1. To one pound of skinned almonds add one pound of sugar
2. Pound as directed to a pulp in a mortar, occasionally adding a few drops of water to prevent the almonds from running to oil
3. Use Orange Flower Water
4. Add fine white sifted sugar till you have sufficient to make a paste
5. Work till well-amalgamated and elastic
