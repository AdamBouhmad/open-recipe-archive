## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- sugar or salt

## Directions

1. Take the tomatoes, when perfectly ripe, and scald them in hot water, in order to take off the skin easily
2. When skinned, boil them well in a little sugar or salt, but no water
3. Spread them in cakes about an inch thick, and place the cakes in the sun
4. They will be ready in 3 or 4 days to pack away in bags, which should be hung in a dry place
