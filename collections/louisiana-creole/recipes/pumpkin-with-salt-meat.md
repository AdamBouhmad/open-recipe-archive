## Ingredients

- pumpkin
- salt meat
- brown sugar
- molasses
- smoked meat

## Directions

1. Slice the pumpkin and put it in the oven with brown sugar, or good molasses
2. Slice some smoked meat and lay it in among the pumpkin
3. Cook it tender
