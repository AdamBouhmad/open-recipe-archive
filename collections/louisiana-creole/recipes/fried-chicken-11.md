## Ingredients

- 1 Spring Chicken
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Lard
- 2 Eggs
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the chicken into joints
2. Dredge well with salt and pepper
3. Make a nice batter with the eggs and flour and roll the chicken in this, patting lightly
4. Place in the hot lard in the frying pan and let it cook for about three-quarters of an hour, watching carefully that it may not burn
5. Serve on a platter garnished with chopped parsley and cresses
