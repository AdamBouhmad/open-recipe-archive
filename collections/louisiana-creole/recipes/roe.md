## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Roe
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- The Juice of a Lemon
- Lemon Sliced in Quarters
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. First wash the eggs well and cut out fibres.
2. Butter a silver dish or, if you have not one, a nice agate.
3. Lay the roe in the dish.
4. Split them open, if large, and put a dot of butter on top of each.
5. Sprinkle nicely with salt and pepper.
6. Place in the oven, bake a few minutes, and serve hot with lemon juice.
