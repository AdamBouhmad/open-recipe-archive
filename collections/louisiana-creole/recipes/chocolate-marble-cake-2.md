## Ingredients

- The Whites of 6 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Butter
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 3 Cups of Flour
- % Cup of Sweet Milk
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- % Cake of Grated Chocolate
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla Extract

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream, and then add the milk.
2. Sift the flour and baking powder together, and stir into the cake gradually.
3. Then add the vanilla essence and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
4. Divide the cake batter in two, and mix the grated chocolate into one half.
5. Then put a dark layer of cake in the pan, then a light, and continue so alternating until all the batter is used.
6. Bake in a moderate oven for three-quarters of an hour.
