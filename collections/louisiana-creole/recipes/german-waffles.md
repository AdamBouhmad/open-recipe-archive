## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 1 lb. fresh butter
- 8 eggs
- ½ lb. sifted flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls strong fresh yeast
- 1 wineglass rose water
- fresh butter for greasing
- sugar flavored with powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Warm a quart of milk and cut up in it 1 lb. of the best fresh butter
2. Stir it about to soften in the warm milk
3. Beat 8 eggs till very thick and smooth
4. Stir them gradually into the milk and butter, in turn with ½ lb. of sifted flour
5. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of strong, fresh yeast
6. Cover the pan with a clean cloth and set it in a warm place to rise
7. When the batter has risen nearly to the top and is covered with bubbles, it is time to bake
8. First stir in a wineglass of rose water
9. Heat your waffle iron in a good fire
10. Grease its inside with the fresh butter used for the waffle mixture
11. Fill it and shut the iron closely
12. Turn it on the fire so that both sides of the cakes may be equally well done
13. Take them out of the iron by slipping a knife underneath
14. Grease and prepare the iron for another waffle
15. Butter them and send them to the tea table very hot
16. Serve with a glass dish of sugar flavored with powdered cinnamon
