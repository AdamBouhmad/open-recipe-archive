## Ingredients

- One teacup of pearl tapioca
- cold water
- hot water
- whites of 3 eggs
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1 quart of cream

## Directions

1. Soak tapioca over night in cold water
2. Pour hot water on the soaked tapioca and boil until a clear jelly
3. When cold, add the whites of 3 eggs, 1 lb. of sugar, 1 quart of cream
4. Whip well, strain and flavor
5. Freeze
