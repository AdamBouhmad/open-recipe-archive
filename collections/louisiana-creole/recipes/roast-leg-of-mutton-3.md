## Ingredients

- 1 leg of mutton
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Select a fine, tender leg of mutton
2. Wipe thoroughly with a damp towel and dredge with salt and pepper, thoroughly rubbing, so that the meat may be penetrated by the seasoning
3. Place the mutton in a baking pan, set in a quick oven and bake, basting every ten minutes or so, allowing twelve minutes to every pound
4. Press with the fingers or stick with a fork; the juice will spurt out, and it is then ready to serve
5. Decorate the bone with a quilling of white paper, and serve in its own sauce
6. Place the dish on which mutton is placed on a very hot plate
