## Ingredients

- 6 stalks of celery
- 1 egg
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 1 cup of milk or bouillon
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the celery into pieces and blanch and boil for about thirty minutes.
2. Drain.
3. Put a tablespoonful of butter into a saucepan, and add the flour, blending without browning.
4. Add the milk and salt and pepper to taste.
5. When it begins to boil add the chopped celery.
6. Let all simmer for twenty minutes longer, and then take off the fire and add the well-beaten yolk of an egg.
7. Season again to taste, and serve.
