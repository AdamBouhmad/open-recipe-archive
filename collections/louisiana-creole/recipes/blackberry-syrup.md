## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pint of Blackberry Juice
- 1 Gallon of Syrup

## Directions

1. Pick, stem and wash the blackberries, then pound and mash them well set aside in a cool place for thirty-six hours.
2. Then strain through a bag, pressing out all the juice, and proceed in exactly the same manner as for Apricot Syrup.
