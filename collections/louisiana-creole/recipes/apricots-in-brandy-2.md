## Ingredients

- Fresh apricots
- Half the weight of the fruit in sugar
- Water
- Brandy
- Bladder skin

## Directions

1. Gather apricots as fresh as possible, taken from the tree as soon as ripe
2. Rub each one with a coarse towel, but do not peel it
3. Make a syrup of half the weight of the fruit in sugar, and just water enough to dissolve it
4. When the syrup is prepared and hot, put in the apricots, let them simmer until tender
5. Take the fruit out, and place it on dishes, then expose them to the sun, or in a warm oven to dry and harden
6. Boil the syrup again, after the fruit is out, until it is quite rich and thick
7. Skim it carefully
8. When the apricots are cold and firm, put them in white earthen preserve-jars and fill up with syrup and brandy, half and half
9. Tie up with bladder skin
