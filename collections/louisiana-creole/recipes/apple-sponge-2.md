## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Apples
- 1 Pound Sugar
- 3 Eggs
- 1/4 Box of Gelatine
- The Grated Zest of a Lemon
- 1/2 Pint of Boiling Water

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water, and when very clear skim the surface.
2. Have the apples pared and cored and sliced nicely, and place them in the syrup.
3. Let them stew until very tender.
4. Then add the gelatine, which has been soaked in cold water, to the mixture.
5. When well done press all through a sieve.
6. Then add the rind and juice of the lemons, using only the zest of the rind in grating the lemon.
7. Stir until it begins to cool and thicken.
8. Then add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, stirring and beating until cool and thick.
9. Turn the Apple Sponge in a dish and serve with Vanilla Sauce poured over it.
