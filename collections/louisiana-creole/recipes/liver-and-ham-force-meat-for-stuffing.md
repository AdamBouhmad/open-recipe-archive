## Ingredients

- calf’s liver
- fat ham or bacon
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- chopped mushrooms
- parsley
- shallots

## Directions

1. Take a calf’s liver, or the livers of three or four turkeys, or geese; lay them in cold water, till ready to use them;
2. cut with them the same quantity of fat ham or bacon; throw them into a saucepan, and let them fry a good brown;
3. season with salt, pepper, spices, chopped mushrooms, parsley and three shallots.
4. When soft, chop them fine, or else pass them through a sausage grinder.
5. This recipe can be used for raised pies, or as an addition to turkey stuffing.
