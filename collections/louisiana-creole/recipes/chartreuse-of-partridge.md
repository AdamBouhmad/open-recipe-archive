## Ingredients

- 3 Fine Partridges
- A Fine Tender Head of Cabbage
- 12 Chaurice (Sausage)
- ¾ Pound of Salt Pork
- 3 Small Onions
- 2 Turnips
- 2 Carrots
- 4 Cloves
- 1½ Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- ¾ Cup of Green Peas
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- 1 Pint of Veal or Chicken Broth
- 1 Pint of Consomme
- ¾ Pint of Demi Glace, or Madeira Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the partridges and cabbage exactly as for the recipe Partridges and Cabbage.
2. Butter a three-pint mold lightly; cut the turnips, carrots and onion into small even pieces, using a vegetable tube; put a layer of the cut vegetables in the bottom of the mold.
3. Lay on top a layer of the cooked cabbage, cut the partridges into pieces and place a layer of them on the cabbage, filling in the hollow spaces with cabbage chopped fine and the chopped vegetables.
4. Fill in further with the sliced sausage and lay on top six slices of the salt pork; then place another layer of the partridges, fill in the hollow places with the sliced turnips and carrots and onions and the sausage.
5. Place on top another layer of cabbage, covering the top well with the cabbage and pressing down very carefully; decorate the mold prettily around the edges with the sliced carrots and turnips and place in a tin baking pan and set in a moderate oven for fifteen minutes.
6. Have at hand a hot dish turn the mold upside down and carefully draw it off the preparation. Send to the table hot and serve with Demi Glace, or Madeira Sauce.
