## Ingredients

- six nice fat crabs
- onions
- butter
- lard
- flour
- chopped parsley
- green onion
- boiled rice

## Directions

1. Take six nice fat crabs, wash them, and while alive chop off the claws
2. Then clean the rest of the crabs carefully and lay them in a dish
3. Chop up two onions fine
4. Fry them in a tablespoonful of butter and lard mixed
5. When brown and soft stir in a large spoonful of flour
6. Which must also brown nicely
7. Throw in some chopped parsley and a little green onion
8. And when they are cooked pour on a quart of boiling water—this is the gravy
9. Now put in the crabs without parboiling
10. Let them simmer in the gravy for half an hour
11. And serve with boiled rice
12. Parboiling crabs destroys their flavor
13. They should be alive to the last moment
