## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 1 pint of powdered sugar
- 1 pint of Indian meal sifted
- 1 pint of wheat flour
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 nutmeg grated
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1/2 glass of wine and brandy mixed

## Directions

1. Stir the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Beat the eggs very light
3. Stir the meal and eggs alternately into the butter and sugar
4. Add the spice and liquor
5. Stir all well
6. Butter a tin pan and put in the mixture
7. Bake it in a moderate oven
