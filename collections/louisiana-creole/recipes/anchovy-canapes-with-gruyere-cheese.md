## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of French Toast
- 2 Ounces of Gruyere Cheese
- 16 Anchovies
- 6 Minced Gherkins
- 1 Ounce of Anchovy Butter
- Pie Paste

## Directions

1. Prepare the French toast
2. Pound the Anchovies to a paste with the Gruyere cheese
3. Line very shallow gem pans with pie paste
4. Bake and set to cool
5. Fill in with the Anchovy preparation
6. Invert this on a circle of nicely buttered Anchovy toast
7. Garnish with the minced gherkins and serve
