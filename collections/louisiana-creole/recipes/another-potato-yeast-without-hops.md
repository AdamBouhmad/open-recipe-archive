## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water
- flour
- yeast cake or good yeast

## Directions

1. Boil and mash sufficient potatoes to fill a pint cup
2. Add to them a pint of water and boil them together
3. Stir in flour enough to form a thick batter
4. When cool, add a yeast cake or a cup of good yeast
5. Bottle and put away in a cool place
