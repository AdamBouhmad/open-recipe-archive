## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Fruit, Peaches, or Cherries, or Bananas, or Pears, etc.
- 1 Pint of Whipped Cream
- 1 Cup of Sugar

## Directions

1. Stone the peaches, and slice or cut them nicely, adding sugar to taste
2. In like manner stone, but do not cut, the cherries if this fruit is used
3. If bananas or pears are preferred peel and slice nicely
4. Seed the pears
5. Sprinkle the fruit with sugar, and set it aside in a cool place for two hours
6. Serve with the whipped cream, heaping it in generous measure at the moment of serving
