## Ingredients

- 6 Green Peppers
- 3 Tomatoes
- A Plain French Dressing

## Directions

1. Parboil the peppers so that they will peel easily, and scald the tomatoes.
2. Peel them, removing the seeds of the peppers.
3. Cut the peppers into one-inch pieces, slice the tomatoes, and serve with plain French dressing as a Salad.
