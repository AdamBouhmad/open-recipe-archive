## Ingredients

- half a glass of water
- a tablespoonful of white powdered sugar
- a gill of Madeira or Port
- nutmeg
- pounded ice
- lady-cake or pound-cake

## Directions

1. Take half a glass of water, sweeten it with a tablespoonful of white powdered sugar, and stir well until dissolved
2. add a gill of Madeira or Port
3. add some nutmeg and pounded ice
4. Serve with lady-cake or pound-cake, cut small
