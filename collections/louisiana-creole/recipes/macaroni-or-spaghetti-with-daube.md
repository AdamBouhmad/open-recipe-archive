## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pound of Macaroni
- A Veal or Beef Daube
- 1 Pint of Hot Water or Broth

## Directions

1. Prepare a daube.
2. After it has cooked about an hour and a half, and is about two-thirds done, add the macaroni according to the number to be served, using between a quarter and a half pound for six, and cutting the macaroni into lengths of about five inches, to facilitate serving.
3. Let it boil for about three-quarters of an hour in the daube.
4. If there is not sufficient gravy for it to cook well, add a half pint or a pint more of hot water or hot broth, according as the macaroni appears to absorb after it has been in the pot four or five minutes.
5. Cook until very tender, and on serving place the daube in the center of the dish and heap the macaroni around.
