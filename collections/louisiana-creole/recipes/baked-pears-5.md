## Ingredients

- large pound pears
- lemon peel
- sugar
- cloves or pounded cinnamon
- port wine

## Directions

1. The best for baking are the large pound pears; remove the skins; cut them in half, and take out the cores; lay them in a deep dish, with a thin slip of lemon peel in the place from which each core was taken
2. Sprinkle them with sugar, and strew some cloves or pounded cinnamon among them; pour into the dish some port wine; to 1 dozen large pears allow 1 lb. of sugar, and 1 pint of wine
3. Cover the dish with a large sheet of brown paper, tied on; set it in a moderate oven and let them bake till tender all through, which you may ascertain by sticking a straw through them; you must not let them remain long enough in the oven to break to pieces
