## Ingredients

- Cochineal
- Brazil wood
- nitric acid
- muriatic acid
- bismuth

## Directions

1. Pound the Cochineal in a mortar
2. add two gallons of water, and let it boil for an hour and a quarter
3. Add about three drachms of nitro-muriate of tin, made by taking two drachms of nitric acid, two drachms of muriatic acid and one drachm of bismuth, and dissolving under a gentle heat
4. Continue boiling the Cochineal ten minutes longer
5. strain the mixture through a fine cloth, and let it stand for eighteen hours
6. Pour off the water, and add the solution of tin, in three pints of water, and let it stand for five days longer, changing the water every twenty-four hours
7. When free from any taste or smell the Carmine has been sufficiently washed, and is ready for use
8. Put into china saucers and dry in the stove
