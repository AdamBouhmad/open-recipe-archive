## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Boiling Water
- 1 Cup of Fresh Milk
- 1 Large tablespoonful of Farina
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of White Sugar
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Take a cup of boiling water, one cup of milk, and a pinch of salt
2. Slightly salt the water
3. Set one boiler within another, the latter boiler being filled with boiling water
4. Stir the Farina into the cup of boiling water, and let it boil, stirring constantly, till it thickens
5. Add the milk, stirring it gradually, and let it boil about fifteen minutes longer
6. Sweeten, and when it is cool give to the child
7. Warm when it is needed, with a little boiling water
