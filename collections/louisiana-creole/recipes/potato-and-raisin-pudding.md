## Ingredients

- 2 ounces of butter
- 4 eggs
- 1 ounce of sugar
- lemon peel
- 6 ounces of grated potatoes
- 1 lb. of raisins

## Directions

1. Mix 2 ounces of butter, 4 eggs, 1 ounce of sugar and lemon peel well together
2. add 6 ounces of grated potatoes
3. add 1 lb. of raisins, and bake it in a form or mould by 2 degrees of heat for half an hour
4. serve it with or without snow
