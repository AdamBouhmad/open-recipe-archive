## Ingredients

- two or three young squirrels or rabbits
- two ounces of butter
- handful of chopped mushrooms
- bunch of parsley
- two shallots
- pepper
- salt
- thyme or sweet herbs
- glass of white wine
- half cup of brown gravy from veal or chicken
- juice of half a lemon
- paste

## Directions

1. Cut up two or three young squirrels or rabbits
2. Put them in a saucepan to cook with two ounces of butter, a handful of chopped mushrooms, a bunch of parsley and two shallots chopped
3. Season with pepper and salt, and a little thyme or sweet herbs
4. Cook them a light brown
5. Throw in a glass of white wine, a half cup of brown gravy from veal or chicken, and the juice of half a lemon
6. Toss all up on the fire fifteen or twenty minutes
7. Make a nice paste, line the sides of the pan, pour in the stewed rabbit, and cover with paste
8. Bake until a light brown, and eat cold or hot
