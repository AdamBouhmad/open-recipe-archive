## Ingredients

- % Box of Gelatine
- 1 Pint of Boiling Water
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- The Whites of 3 Eggs
- The Juice of 3 Lemons
- The Yolks of the Eggs
- 1/2 Cup of Sugar
- Milk
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatine in the boiling water
2. When nearly cool, add one cup of powdered sugar and the juice of one lemon
3. Beat well and lightly, and strain
4. When cold, beat as white as snow, and add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
5. Beat all thoroughly, and pour into a mold
6. Stand in a cold place for four hours to harden
7. Beat the yolks of the eggs together with a half cup of sugar till very light
8. Stir them into the milk, which should be boiling
9. Let them cook, stirring constantly, for two or three minutes
10. Add a teaspoonful of Vanilla, and set to cool
11. Serve with the pudding, pouring the sauce upon it
