## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream
- 1/2 Pint of Gelatine, or 1/2 Box
- 1 Gill of Cold Water
- 1/4 Cup of Sugar
- The Yolks of 4 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla or Rum

## Directions

1. Follow the general directions given for making Cream Desserts, viz: Soaking the gelatine, and, before whipping the cream, cool it for at least an hour on the ice
2. Put one pint of fresh cream or milk in a farina boiler, and add the sugar, and bring it to a boil
3. As it boils, pour in the gelatine, stir constantly till the latter is well dissolved and blended, and set aside to cool
4. Beat the yolks of the eggs to a cream, and stir them in the cold cream, blending and beating vigorously
5. Add the Vanilla or Rum, and set it away on the ice
6. When the boiled cream is quite cold, and has begun to thicken, stir it till perfectly smooth, and then stir in the whipped cream with a few light strokes
7. Have ready the mold or freezer, well rinsed, and fill with the cream
8. Set it away to cool and when quite firm turn it out, and serve with plain cream or whipped cream
