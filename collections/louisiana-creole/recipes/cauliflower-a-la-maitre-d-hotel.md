## Ingredients

- 1 Large or 2 Medium-Sized Cauliflowers
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- A Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel

## Directions

1. Boil the cauliflower whole, as in the first recipe.
2. Bring them to the table whole, like blooming flowerlets, in a dish nicely garnished, and serve with a Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel and lemon cut in quarters.
