## Ingredients

- a bushel of ripe tomatoes
- a pint and a half of salt
- four tablespoonfuls of ground cloves
- four tablespoonfuls of cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Get a bushel of ripe tomatoes
2. scald them until they are soft enough to squeeze through a sieve
3. When strained, add to the pulp a pint and a half of salt, four tablespoonfuls of ground cloves, same of cayenne pepper
