## Ingredients

- peel of four oranges
- half a pound of sugar
- one-quarter of a pound of butter
- yolks of six eggs
- juice of the oranges
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Boil the peel of four oranges in two waters, to take out the bitter taste
2. When tender, pound up with half a pound of sugar, one-quarter of a pound of butter, and the yolks of six eggs
3. Beat the mixture well and add the juice of the oranges; if the oranges are large the juice of two will be sufficient to make two pies
4. Put puff paste in your pans, fill them half full of the confection, and bake lightly
