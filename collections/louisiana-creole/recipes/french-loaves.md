## Ingredients

- 1 Ounce of Compressed Yeast
- 2 Quarts of Water
- 1½ Ounces of Salt
- Flour Sufficient to Make a Smooth Dough

## Directions

1. Make the dough as in Bakers' Bread, and after it has risen and begins to drop, scale the dough off, and cut and mold them into little loaves of about the length of a man's hand.
2. Mold them round and set them to rise again.
3. When they are very light, which will be in a few minutes, take them up and work them well separately.
