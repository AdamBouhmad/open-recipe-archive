## Ingredients

- 3 cups of flour
- 2 cups of sugar
- 1 cup of butter
- 1 cup of cream
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of soda and cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Mix all well together
2. Beat light
3. Bake in a moderate oven
