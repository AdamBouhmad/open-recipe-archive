## Ingredients

- 0.5 Pound of Grated Cheese
- 1 Gill of Fresh Cream or Milk
- 3 Creole Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Flour
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Pinch of Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Pinch Cayenne
- Salt to Taste

## Directions

1. Grate the cheese very fine and then add to it the boiling milk.
2. Add gradually the pepper, Cayenne and salt.
3. Add the butter and flour, rubbed well together.
4. When the cheese is well dissolved, take the mixture off.
5. Add nutmeg and the beaten yolks of three eggs and the whites of two eggs beaten to a froth.
6. Stir the whole well.
7. Place all in a shallow earthen dish, add a little butter that has been well melted and put in the stove for a few minutes till it begins to solidify well.
8. Take out and spread on top the white of one egg, beaten to a stiff froth.
9. Set one minute in the stove, let it brown slightly and serve immediately.
