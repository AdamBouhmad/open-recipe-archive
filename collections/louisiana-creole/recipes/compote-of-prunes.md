## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Prunes
- 1/4 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Glass of Claret

## Directions

1. Soak the prunes over night in cold water, and drain
2. Put them into a saucepan with water, cover the saucepan, and let them boil over a moderate fire
3. When they begin to soften, add a glass of red wine and a quarter of a pound of sugar
4. Let it boil, stirring frequently, till the prunes are cooked well
5. Take them from the fire, rub a piece of sugar over an orange or a lemon, and when it is completely saturated with the zest, put into a dish
6. Pour the prunes in with their juice, and serve
