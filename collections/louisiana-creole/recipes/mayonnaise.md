## Ingredients

- yolk of 1 raw egg
- 1 teaspoonful of made mustard
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- sweet oil
- 1 tablespoonful of vinegar
- a little red pepper (optional)

## Directions

1. The mustard and salt to be well rubbed together
2. add the egg
3. pour on very slowly the sweet oil, rubbing hard all the time, till as much is made as is wanted
4. add a tablespoonful of vinegar
5. when these ingredients are mixed, they should look perfectly smooth
6. if it curdles, add a little more mustard or a little vinegar
7. With shrimps or oysters, a little red pepper rubbed in is an improvement
