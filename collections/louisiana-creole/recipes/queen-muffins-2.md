## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Sweet Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Melted Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder or 1/2 Cake of Compressed Yeast

## Directions

1. If yeast is used, set the batter to rise over night, as in Egg Muffins, and then proceed in exactly the same manner, adding the eggs in the morning.
2. Beat the eggs separately till very light, add to the batter, and pour into muffin rings or gem pans and let the mixture rise a half hour longer.
3. Then bake for twenty minutes in a quick oven.
4. If yeast powder is used, beat the yolks of the eggs to a cream, add the scalded milk and butter, and add the flour.
5. Beat till very light, and add the yeast powder, and finally the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
6. Turn into gem pans or muffin rings, and bake for twenty minutes in a hot oven.
