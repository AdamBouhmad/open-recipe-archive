## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of raw veal
- 1 lb. of marrow or beef suet
- pepper
- salt
- the yolk of an egg
- butter
- crumbs of bread

## Directions

1. Take 1 lb. of raw veal, and shred it very fine
2. Add 1 lb. of marrow or beef suet, and season it with pepper and salt
3. Beat it very fine in a marble mortar, and make it the shape of sweet breads
4. Beat well the yolk of an egg and dip these into it
5. Fry them in a good deal of butter, and lay crumbs of bread between them
6. Pour a little melted butter on them
7. They must not be fried of a dark color
