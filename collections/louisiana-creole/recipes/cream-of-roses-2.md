## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 pounds of rose leaves
- 2 quarts of brandy
- 1 pound of sugar
- 1 quart of water
- a few drops of cochineal

## Directions

1. Pound the rose leaves in a stone vessel, and then pour over the brandy, and infuse for six days
2. Distill the liquor by boiling to one-half in a bain-marie
3. Mix it thoroughly with one pound of sugar, dissolved in a quart of water or in rose water
4. Color with a few drops of cochineal, and filter and bottle for use
