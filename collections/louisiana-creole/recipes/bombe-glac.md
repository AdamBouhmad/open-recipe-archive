## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- The Yolks of 6 Eggs
- 1/2 Pound of White Pulverized Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Extract
- 1/2 Pint of Strawberry Sherbet

## Directions

1. Line Bombe Glacé molds about three-fourths of an inch thick with Strawberry Water Ice
2. Fill the center with the Biscuit Glacés prepared as above
3. Ensure the biscuit is freezing cold when put into the mold
4. Pack in salt and ice, and set to freeze several hours
