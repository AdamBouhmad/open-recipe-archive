## Ingredients

- 2½ Pints of Milk
- ¾ Pound of Sugar
- The Yolks of 3 Eggs
- 4 Ounces of Boiled Rice
- 1 Gill of Curaçoa
- ¾ Pint of Milk of Almonds
- A Compote of 8 Oranges

## Directions

1. Boil the rice in the milk till very soft. Do not mash.
2. Set the pint and a half extra milk to boil, and add the rice and the milk of almonds and the sugar and eggs, beaten to a cream.
3. Make a custard. Take from the fire, and add the Curaçoa, and mix well.
4. Set to freeze as above directed, and when cold turn out of the mold, place in a dish and garnish around with the oranges, pour the syrup over all, and serve.
