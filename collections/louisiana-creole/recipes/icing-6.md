## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of sugar
- 2 wineglasses of water
- 6 eggs

## Directions

1. To 1 lb. of sugar, put 2 wineglasses of water
2. Set it on the fire and let it simmer
3. Have 6 eggs beaten to a froth
4. Add the syrup to the eggs after it has cooled a little
5. Beat it 1 hour, and then use it
