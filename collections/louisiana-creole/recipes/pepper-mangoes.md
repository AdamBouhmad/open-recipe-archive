## Ingredients

- large, green peppers
- salt
- water
- nice white cabbages
- celery seed
- mustard seed
- turmeric
- spices
- pure sweet oil
- boiling vinegar

## Directions

1. Take large, green peppers and put them in salt and water
2. Let them stand 9 days
3. The day before you are to take them out, get nice white cabbages and chop them very fine
4. Sprinkle them well with salt and let them stand all night
5. In the morning drain off the water, and mix with them celery seed, mustard seed, and a little turmeric, and all kinds of spice
6. Moisten the whole with pure sweet oil
7. Take the seed out of the peppers and stuff the pods with the above mixture
8. Cover them with boiling vinegar and tie them up closely
