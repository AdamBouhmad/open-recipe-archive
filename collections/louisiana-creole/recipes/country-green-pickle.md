## Ingredients

- one peck of tomatoes
- eight green peppers
- cabbage
- brine
- vinegar
- horseradish
- spices

## Directions

1. Chop eight green peppers fine
2. Soak the tomatoes and pepper twenty-four hours in weak brine
3. Drain off the brine, and add to the green tomatoes a head of finely chopped cabbage
4. Scald all in boiling vinegar twenty minutes
5. Skim it out from the vinegar, and place in a large jar
6. Add three pints of grated horseradish and such other spices as you please
7. Fill the jars with strong cold vinegar and tie up for use
