## Ingredients

- 6 ounces of rice
- 1 quart of water
- 2 lbs. of flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- 2 of yeast
- milk or water

## Directions

1. Boil rice in a quart of water till dry and soft
2. Put it into 2 lbs. of flour
3. Mix it well, add 2 teaspoonfuls of salt, 2 of yeast, and enough milk or water to make it a proper consistency
4. Bake it in moulds, when well risen
