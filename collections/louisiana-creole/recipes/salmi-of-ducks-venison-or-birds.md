## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 cup of soup broth
- 2 whole leeks
- carrots
- thyme
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- pieces of game
- 1 cup of claret wine
- juice of ½ a lemon
- bread cut into diamonds
- slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Put 1 tablespoonful of butter into the saucepan, stirring in 2 tablespoonfuls of flour, letting it melt without browning
2. Add 1 cup of soup broth, 2 whole leeks, carrots, a little thyme and parsley, pepper and salt
3. Let it boil for half an hour
4. Withdraw the vegetables, put in the pieces of game, and let simmer until done
5. Add 1 cup of claret wine and juice of ½ a lemon
6. Have ready some fried pieces of bread cut into diamonds, lay them around the dish, put in the game in the middle, pour the sauce over the whole
7. Garnish the dish with slices of lemon
