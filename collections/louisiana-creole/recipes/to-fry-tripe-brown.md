## Ingredients

- tripe
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- bacon grease
- flour
- vinegar

## Directions

1. It must be thoroughly boiled and tender, or no frying will make it good.
2. Let it be perfectly cold, cut it in pieces, roll each piece in salt, pepper and flour, and fry brown in bacon grease.
3. When nicely brown take it up, dredge a little flour in the gravy, and put in a half cup of vinegar.
4. Serve in a sauceboat, or pour over the tripe as preferred.
