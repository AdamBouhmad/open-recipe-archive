## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Tripe
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Sprig of Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 8 small white onions
- Sauce a la Poulette

## Directions

1. Prepare the tripe according to the directions given under the heading of "Tripe."
2. Make a rich Sauce a la Poulette, always omitting the eggs till later.
3. Take eight small white onions that have already been boiled in plain water until they are perfectly tender, and add to the sauce.
4. Let them stew for about five minutes.
5. Then add the tripe, which has been cut into pieces of three inches in length, and one-half inch in width, and stew the whole gently for about a half hour.
6. Take off the fire and add the beaten yolks of two eggs, stirring constantly and serve hot.
