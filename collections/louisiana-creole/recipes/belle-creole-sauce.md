## Ingredients

- The Yolks of 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 1/2 Cup Bread Crumbs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 3/4 Cup of Cream
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1/2 cup of consommé or broth
- onion juice
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put the butter into the saucepan, and add the flour, letting it blend well, without burning or browning, for this is a white sauce
2. When it becomes a delicate yellow, add the bread crumbs, stir for one minute, and add the half cup of consommé or broth
3. Stir well, and add a half cup of cream, and salt and pepper to taste
4. Add the chopped parsley as a garnish, and a little onion juice
5. Take off the fire and add the well-chopped yolks of two eggs, and a tablespoonful of lemon juice
6. Serve with boiled fish or boiled meats of any kind
