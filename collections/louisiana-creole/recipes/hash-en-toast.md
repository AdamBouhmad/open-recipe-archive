## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cold Meat
- 1 Pint of Boiling Water or Milk
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Slices of Bread

## Directions

1. Cut the left-over roast, bouilli or steak into small squares
2. To each Pint of these little squares allow one tablespoonful of butter, one tablespoonful of flour and a half pint of boiling water
3. Put the butter into a frying pan, and as it melts add the flour, being careful not to let it burn
4. When browned add the water, or preferably milk, and stir until it begins to boil
5. Then add the hashed and seasoned meat, and season again to taste
6. Set the hash on a moderate fire and let it simmer for fifteen minutes
7. In the meantime, toast slices of bread and butter them
8. Set them in a hot dish, spread each slice with the hash very thickly and pour the sauce over and serve
