## Ingredients

- 2 cups of picked codfish
- 2 cups of mashed potatoes
- 1/4 cup of cream
- Bread crumbs
- 2 beaten eggs
- Pepper and Cayenne to taste
- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of black pepper

## Directions

1. Soak the codfish overnight and boil until tender.
2. Pour off this water, and scald again with hot water; pick fine, scald again and then drain thoroughly, pressing out all the water.
3. Mash the potatoes, and melt about three tablespoonfuls of butter and mix well in the potatoes.
4. Add the codfish and mix thoroughly, seasoning with about a teaspoonful of black pepper, and a dash of Cayenne to taste.
5. Add this to the cream, and again mix.
6. Mold the codfish into round or oval balls.
7. Roll in the egg, which has been well beaten, and pass through the bread crumbs, patting gently.
8. Lay them in a frying basket, if you have one, and sink into the boiling lard, or otherwise drop into the boiling lard, having tested the heat with a bit of bread.
9. When a golden brown, lift out the basket, or skim out the balls with a skimmer; drain well of all the fat by laying on a heated brown paper, and then serve hot on a dish garnished with sprigs of parsley.
