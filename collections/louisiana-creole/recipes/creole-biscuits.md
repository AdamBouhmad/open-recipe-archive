## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Grated Lemon
- 4 Ounces of Orange Flower Marmalade
- 4 Ounces of Apricot or Peach Marmalade
- The Whites of 4 Eggs
- 3 Ounces of white pulverized sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the fruits and lemon thoroughly together.
2. Add three ounces of white pulverized sugar and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
3. Place the mixture in small cakes on white paper.
4. Bake lightly.
5. Cover with sugar beaten with the white of an egg, in meringue form.
6. Bake to a light brown.
