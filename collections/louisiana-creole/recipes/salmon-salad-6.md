## Ingredients

- 1 Can of Salmon
- 1-3 the Quantity of Chopped Celery
- 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- Sliced Lemon
- Olives
- 1 Pickled Beet
- A Plain French Dressing
- crisp bed of lettuce

## Directions

1. Flake the salmon and heap into a salad bowl
2. Mix with chopped celery using one-third of the latter in proportion to the quantity of salmon
3. Add a Plain French Dressing
4. When ready to serve turn into a salad dish on which you have arranged a crisp bed of lettuce
5. Garnish with sliced lemon, olives, hard-boiled eggs, and one daintily sliced pickled beet
