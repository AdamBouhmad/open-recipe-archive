## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Currant Juice
- 1 Quart of Water
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- one quart of currants

## Directions

1. Mash one quart of currants
2. Boil the sugar and water for five minutes
3. Add the currants and strain through a napkin or sieve, being careful to press very hard to extract the juice of the currants
4. Set to freeze as directed
