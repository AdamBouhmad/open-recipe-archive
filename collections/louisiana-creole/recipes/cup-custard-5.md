## Ingredients

- 4 Eggs
- 1 Quart of Milk
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Cornstarch (If desired)
- 3/4 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla Extract
- Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs together until light, and then add the sugar, and beat till very light.
2. Now add the milk and the nutmeg, and put a pan into the oven.
3. Fill the pan with boiling water.
4. Pour the custard into cups or into a smaller pan, and set in the boiling water, and let it bake till firm in the center.
5. Set to cool, and serve in the cups if they have been used.
6. Otherwise serve in dessert plates.
