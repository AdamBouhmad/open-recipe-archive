## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Butter
- 1 Pound of Sifted Flour
- 1 Pound of Good Brown Sugar
- 6 Well-Beaten Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream.
2. Add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs, and then alternately the flour, with which the baking powder has been sifted, and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
3. Bake in a buttered cake pan for about an hour in a moderate oven.
