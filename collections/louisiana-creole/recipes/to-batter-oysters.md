## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 1 dessertspoonful of butter
- wheat flour
- pepper and salt
- 1 quart of oysters
- boiling lard

## Directions

1. Make a light batter of 8 eggs, a dessertspoonful of butter, a little wheat flour, pepper and salt to the taste
2. Drain the oysters from the liquor
3. Stir them into the batter
4. Drop the mixture from a ladle into boiling lard
5. Let the fritters cook until they are of a rich brown
