## Ingredients

- 3 quarts good consomé
- 10 pieces dried toast
- 1 carrot, cut fine
- 1 head of celery, cut fine
- 1 turnip, cut fine
- salted boiling water

## Directions

1. Prepare a good consomé
2. Parboil the vegetables in salted boiling water
3. When tender, drain off the water and add to them about two and one-half quarts of the boiling consomé
4. Let them simmer until they are very tender
5. Prepare the toasts and put them into a saucepan with enough consomé to cover them
6. Simmer gently until the toasts have absorbed all the consomé and show signs of drying up
7. Add a little hot consomé and detach them from the saucepan
8. Lay them in the tureen and pour the soup with the vegetables very gently over them
9. Serve immediately
