## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Apples
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Orange or Apricot Marmalade
- 1/2 Cup of Water
- 1/4 Teaspoon of Ground Cinnamon
- 1/4 of a Grated Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pare fine, large apples, take out the core, and be careful to leave the apple entire
2. Fill the opening with orange or apricot marmalade
3. Put them together in a pan with a pound of clarified sugar
4. Add a half cup of water
5. Let them cook soft in the oven, sprinkling first with grated cinnamon
6. Add grated nutmeg when done, and serve either hot or cold
