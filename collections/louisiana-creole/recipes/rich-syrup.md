## Ingredients

- cold water (1 pint)
- white sugar (2 lbs.)
- white of egg (1/2 of one)
- cold water (1 teaspoonful)

## Directions

1. One pint of cold water to 2 lbs. of white sugar
2. When it is dissolved, set it over a moderate fire
3. Beat 1/2 the white of an egg, and put it to the sugar before it gets warm
4. Watch it, and when it is ready to run over, put in 1 teaspoonful of cold water
5. After the boiling ceases, put it on the fire again
6. Do this 3 times, and the last time take it off and let it settle
7. Take off the scum, and strain the syrup through a jelly bag
