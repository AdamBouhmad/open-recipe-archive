## Ingredients

- 1 lb. veal
- 1 lb. lean beef
- 1/2 lb. pork
- 1/2 loaf bread
- 1 chopped onion
- spices
- 3 eggs
- beef stock, the shin of veal, or calves' feet

## Directions

1. Chop veal, beef, and pork fine
2. Chop 1/2 loaf bread fine, wet it and squeeze the water out
3. Add 1 chopped onion
4. Add spices to taste
5. Beat 3 eggs well into the mixture
6. Form into a roll
7. Bake 1.5 hours, basting all the while with beef stock, the shin of veal, or calves' feet
8. Set it away to cool
9. Cut in slices and serve
