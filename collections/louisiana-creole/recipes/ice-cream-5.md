## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 3 quarts of milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs separately and very light
2. Sweeten to the taste, stirring the sugar into the yolks
3. Stir in the milk
4. Stir in the whites last
5. Flavor to the taste
6. Freeze
