## Ingredients

- parsnips
- beaten eggs
- milk
- flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Boil and peel a dozen parsnips
2. Split and cut them in pieces
3. Make batter, allowing 4 beaten eggs to 1 pint of milk and 4 tablespoonfuls of flour
4. Have ready over the fire a frying pan with boiling lard
5. Put in a large spoonful of batter upon every piece of parsnip, and cover with another spoonful of batter
6. Proceed thus till you have used up the parsnips
7. When done, drain them from the lard, and serve them hot at breakfast or dinner
