## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Perch
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1 Sliced Lemon
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Proceed in the same manner indicated under the heading General Rule for Broiling Fish
2. If large, split the fish down the middle of the back, else broil whole
3. Serve with garnishes of parsley and sliced lemon
