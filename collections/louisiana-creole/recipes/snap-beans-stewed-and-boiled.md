## Ingredients

- snap beans
- boiling water
- half cup of broth
- cup of milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Pick and snap them when green and tender
2. Cut them small
3. Throw into boiling water
4. Let them cook gently for two hours
5. Stir in a half cup of broth, and a cup of milk
6. Let them stew in this for half an hour longer
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste
