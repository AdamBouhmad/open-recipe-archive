## Ingredients

- 6 Turnips
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Boil the turnips according to directions, and, when very tender, drain in a colander.
2. Put two tablespoonfuls of butter into a frying pan, and when it is hot, add the turnips.
3. Season well with salt and pepper, and, when nicely browned, dish on a hot platter and serve with roast ducks.
