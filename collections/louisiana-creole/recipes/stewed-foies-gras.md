## Ingredients

- Terrine of Foies Gras
- Thin Strips of Bacon
- Carrot
- Onion
- Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley
- Wineglassful of White Wine
- Two Spoonfuls of French Brandy
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Juice of a Lemon

## Directions

1. Get the prepared Foies Gras and cut them into slices or filets
2. Lard them with a larding needle
3. Place at the bottom of the saucepan small strips of bacon, cut very thin and fine
4. Add one carrot, nicely sliced and one onion, nicely sliced, and a tablespoonful of chopped parsley
5. Cover this with narrow strips of bacon, and moisten with sufficient white wine to cover well, and two spoonfuls of brandy
6. Add the juice of a lemon and let it simmer well for a few minutes
7. Add the livers, and let them simmer for ten minutes longer
8. Season to taste, cook five minutes more and serve hot
