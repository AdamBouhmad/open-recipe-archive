## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Mashed Sweet Potatoes
- % Pound of Sugar
- % Pound of Butter
- 8 Eggs
- The Juice and Rind of an Orange and Lemon
- 1/2 Spoon of Ground Mace
- The Same of Cinnamon and Allspice
- % Grated Nutmeg
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Brandy
- 1/4 Cup of Sherry

## Directions

1. Grate the potatoes and mash them through a sieve, if you use them raw
2. If you use the cold boiled potatoes, simply mash them
3. Beat the butter and the sugar to a rich cream
4. Add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs
5. Add the grated potatoes, and the spices
6. Add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
7. Mix all well
8. Add the liquors
9. Turn into a buttered baking pan
10. Let the pudding bake in a moderate oven for three-quarters of an hour
11. Serve hot or cold, with Cream Sauce
