## Ingredients

- 3 Fine Partridges
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 3 Large Tomatoes
- 2 Sprigs Each of Thyme and Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- ⅛ Glass of Sherry or Madeira Wine
- 1 Cup of Water or Consomme
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Croutons to Garnish

## Directions

1. Clean the Partridges; singe, draw and wipe well
2. Cut up the birds as for Fricasseed Chicken
3. Rub well with salt and pepper and place in a stewpan, and let them brown well on either side
4. Add the finely-chopped onion and the herbs, minced very fine
5. Let these brown, and add the tablespoonful of flour
6. Let brown nicely, and add the chopped tomatoes and their juice
7. Cover and let simmer about five minutes
8. Add the wine and a cup of water or consommé
9. Cover closely and let all cook for fifteen minutes
10. Serve hot, using toasted Croutons for a garnish
