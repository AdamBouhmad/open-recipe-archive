## Ingredients

- 1 round of veal
- 1 tablespoonful of lard
- 1 beaten egg
- 1/2 cup of bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. The round of the veal is always used for this.
2. Cut the veal into squares of about four inches.
3. Season well with salt, pepper and Cayenne.
4. Beat an egg well and take each grillade and soak it well in the egg.
5. Roll in bread crumbs grated.
6. Have ready a pan of boiling grease, sufficient for the grillades to swim in it.
7. Fry to a nice brown and serve very hot.
