## Ingredients

- ducks
- onion
- sage
- mint
- sweet herbs
- 1 pint good beef gravy
- pepper
- salt
- green peas
- flour
- butter
- port wine

## Directions

1. Roast the ducks for 20 minutes
2. Place them in a stewpan with an onion cut in slices, a little sage and mint and sweet herbs chopped fine
3. Add about a pint of good beef gravy, seasoned with pepper and salt
4. Let it stew gently for 20 minutes
5. Take out the duck carefully and keep it warm
6. Strain the gravy
7. Pour it into a clean stewpan, and when well heated, add the duck and a quart of green peas
8. Let it simmer for a half hour
9. Add a little flour and butter, a glass of port wine
10. Send to table with the peas in the same dish as the duck
