## Ingredients

- one pound of grated bread crumbs
- a pint of boiling milk
- a pound of chopped suet
- half a pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- half a pound of sifted flour
- one dozen eggs
- one pound of raisins
- one pound of currants
- half a pound of citron
- one tablespoonful of ground cinnamon
- one tablespoonful of cloves
- one tablespoonful of allspice
- one grated nutmeg
- a glass of brandy
- the rind and juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Prepare all the ingredients except the beating of the eggs, the day before making the pudding
2. Take one pound of grated bread crumbs, pour over them a pint of boiling milk
3. Add a pound of chopped suet, half a pound of butter, one pound of sugar, half a pound of sifted flour, one dozen eggs, one pound of raisins, one pound of currants, half a pound of citron
4. Add one tablespoonful of ground cinnamon, one of cloves and allspice, also one grated nutmeg, a glass of brandy, the rind and juice of two lemons
5. Tie it in a piece of thick, unbleached cotton, allowing room for the pudding to swell
6. Boil five hours
7. Serve with butter and sugar sauce
