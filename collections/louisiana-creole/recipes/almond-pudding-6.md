## Ingredients

- 4 ounces of crumbs of bread, sliced
- 1 1/2 pints of cream
- 1 lb. of blanched almonds
- 2 teaspoonfuls of orange flower water
- yolks of 8 eggs and whites of 4
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar
- 3 or 4 ounces of melted butter

## Directions

1. Steep 4 ounces of crumbs of bread, sliced, in 1 1/2 pints of cream, or grate the bread
2. Beat 1 lb. of blanched almonds very fine, till they become a paste, with 2 teaspoonfuls of orange flower water
3. Beat up the yolks of 8 eggs and the whites of 4
4. Mix all well together
5. Put in 1 lb. of loaf sugar, and stir in 3 or 4 ounces of melted butter
6. Put it over the fire, stirring it until it is thick
7. Lay a sheet of paper at the bottom of a dish, and pour in the ingredients
8. Bake 1/2 hour
