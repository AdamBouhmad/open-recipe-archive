## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Sweet Milk
- 1/2 Pound of Flour
- 2 Ounces of Butter
- Salt
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and the yolks separately till very, very light.
2. Warm the milk and add the melted butter.
3. When cold, mix with the yolks of the eggs, and add the flour and salt, beating till very light.
4. Now add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
5. Stir lightly, turn into buttered tins, filling each about half full, and bake to a light brown.
