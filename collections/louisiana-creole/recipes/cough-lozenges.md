## Ingredients

- 1 Ounce of Gum Dragon
- 2 Ounces of Orange Flower Water
- 2 Pounds of Fine Sugar Icing
- 50 Drops of Paregoric
- 20 Drops of Ipecacuanha
- 1 1/2 Ounces Syrup of Squills

## Directions

1. Work the gum on the slab with about one-third of the sugar
2. Work in gradually the syrup of squills
3. After working well add the rest of the sugar and again work
4. Add the Paregoric and the Ipecacuanha
5. Proceed to finish these old-fashioned lozenges in the manner directed in the general recipe
