## Ingredients

- 1 cup of good brown sugar
- 2 1/2 large spoonfuls of good butter
- 1 well whipped egg
- a little grated nutmeg
- a large spoonful of orange flower water
- 1 pint of flour
- 2 teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- 1 cup of new milk
- 1 teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Beat to a cream 1 cup of good brown sugar and 2 1/2 large spoonfuls of good butter
2. Stir in 1 well whipped egg, a little grated nutmeg, and a large spoonful of orange flower water
3. Sift into a pan 1 pint of flour, and stir in 2 teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
4. Dissolve in a cup of new milk 1 teaspoonful of soda
5. Beat these well together
6. Add the milk and soda with the flour, eggs and spice
7. Butter a baking dish, and pour in the pudding
8. Bake 1/2 an hour
9. Serve with wine sauce
