## Ingredients

- one quart of cranberries
- one teacupful of water
- one teacupful of brown sugar

## Directions

1. Wash and pick a quart of cranberries
2. Put them into a stew-pan, with a teacupful of water, and the same of brown sugar
3. Cover the pan and let them stew gently for one hour
4. Mash them smooth with a silver spoon
5. Dip a quart bowl in cold water, pour in the stewed cranberries, and leave till cold
6. Serve with roast pork, ham, turkey or goose
