## Ingredients

- The Whites of Four Eggs
- 1 Pint of Cream
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla
- 1/4 Cup of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sherry

## Directions

1. Whip the cream and set on ice.
2. Beat the whites of the eggs to a foam, and then add the sifted white sugar, and beat steadily till the mixture becomes very stiff and glossy.
3. Add the Sherry and Vanilla.
4. Stir the whipped cream carefully into the meringue, and set it in small custard glasses to get cold.
5. Serve very cold, as a summer dessert.
