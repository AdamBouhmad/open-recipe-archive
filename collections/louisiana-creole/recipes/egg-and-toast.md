## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- milk
- butter
- cornstarch
- salt
- pepper
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for five minutes till hard
2. Take out of the water and cool
3. Take off the shells and separate the whites from the yolks
4. Rub the yolks through a sieve and chop the whites very fine
5. Have the milk ready to boil and rub the butter and cornstarch together
6. Add to the boiling milk
7. Then add the whites of the eggs and salt and pepper to taste
8. Prepare buttered toast and cover it with a layer of white sauce
9. Add a layer of the yolks of the eggs
10. Add another layer of the sauce and another layer of the yolks
11. Add the remainder of the sauce
12. Sprinkle the top with a little salt and pepper
13. Set in the oven and let it stand two minutes
14. Serve hot
