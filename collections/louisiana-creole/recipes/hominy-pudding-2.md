## Ingredients

- 1 pint of hominy liquor
- a lump of fresh butter
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 lemon
- powdered loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Take from the hominy, when nearly done, 1 pint of the liquor
2. Put into it, while hot, a lump of fresh butter, 4 eggs, the juice of half a lemon with the rind grated
3. Sweeten to the taste with powdered loaf sugar, and bake it in a rich paste
