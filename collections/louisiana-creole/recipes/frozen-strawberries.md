## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Strawberries
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Mash the berries with the sugar and set aside for an hour.
2. Add the water and mix well with the strawberries.
3. Turn the mixture into the freezer and proceed to freeze as in Ice Cream.
4. Serve with rich milk or Whipped Cream.
