## Ingredients

- large, fat, thick terrapin bodies
- salt
- eggs
- fresh butter
- beaten yolks of eggs
- sherry or madeira
- powdered nutmeg
- mace
- lumps of butter
- liquorice

## Directions

1. Select large, fat, thick bodies (females are best)
2. Put them whole into water that is boiling hard
3. Add a little salt
4. Let them boil until done
5. Take off the shells
6. Extract the meat
7. Carefully remove the sand-bags, gall and entrails
8. Cut the meat into pieces
9. Put in stew pan with eggs, and sufficient fresh butter to stew well
10. Let it stew until quite hot, keeping the pan carefully covered and shaking it over the fire
11. Make a sauce of beaten yolks of eggs highly flavored with sherry or madeira, powdered nutmeg and mace, and a few lumps of butter
12. Stir this well over the fire until it comes almost to a boil
13. Serve separately if you prefer or add a small lump of liquorice about the size of a 5 cent piece
14. Serve very hot
