## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Small, White Onions
- 1 Tablespoonfal Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful Flour
- ½ Pint of Milk or Cream
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Boil the onions as directed in the Boiled Onions recipe.
2. When very tender, take off the fire and drain.
3. Put one tablespoonful of flour into a saucepan, and add a tablespoonful of butter.
4. Set on the fire, and let all blend well together, rubbing very smooth, without browning.
5. Then add half a pint of milk.
6. Stir continually till it boils.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and pour over the onions, and serve hot.
