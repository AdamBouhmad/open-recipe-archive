## Ingredients

- whites of two eggs
- quarter of a pound of powdered sugar
- quarter of a pound of blanched and cut almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of two eggs with a quarter of a pound of powdered sugar and a quarter of a pound of blanched and cut almonds
2. Form them into rings on letter paper
3. Put the paper on tin and place them in the stove oven to harden and brown lightly
