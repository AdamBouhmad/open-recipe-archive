## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Louisiana Oranges
- 3/4 Pound of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Glass of Rum or Brandy

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges whole, removing the peel entirely down to the tips of the stem end.
2. Cut in slices, and cut out the seeds.
3. Pile the oranges in a neat heap in a dish.
4. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
5. Boil some sugar to a syrup, using about half a pound to half a pound of oranges.
6. Add a glass of rum or brandy, and, when cold, pour over the oranges and serve.
