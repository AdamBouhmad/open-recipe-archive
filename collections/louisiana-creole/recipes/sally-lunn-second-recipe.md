## Ingredients

- a cup of risen yeast
- a cup of warm sweet milk
- one-half a cup of white sugar
- a large spoonful each of lard and butter mixed and warmed
- four eggs well beaten
- three and one-half cups of sifted flour
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Pour a cup of risen yeast into a bowl
2. add a cup of warm sweet milk, one-half a cup of white sugar, and a large spoonful each of lard and butter mixed and warmed
3. also add four eggs well beaten, three and one-half cups of sifted flour, and a little salt
4. Beat all this well
5. pour into a warm and well greased cake pan
6. set it to rise in a warm place in winter, and a cool one in summer
7. If made up at night, add a little more flour, or make the dough a little stiffer, and allow it to rise in a tureen or crock, and pour it in the pan and let it rise a little before baking
8. bake like a cake
