## Ingredients

- venison steak
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash the venison steak nicely and drain the water from it
2. Butter it very thoroughly
3. Sprinkle with pepper and salt
4. Cook it in a hurry
5. Serve it very hot
