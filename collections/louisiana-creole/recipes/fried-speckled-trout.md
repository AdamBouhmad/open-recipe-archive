## Ingredients

- 6 Filets of Speckled Trout
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 1 Onion
- 4 Sprigs of Parsley
- 2 Gills of Mustard
- 1/4 Pint of Mayonnaise Dressing
- Parsley
- Sliced Lemon
- Salt and Pepper
- Cracker or bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Select fine Spotted Trout and skin the fish and clean thoroughly
2. Cut the Trout into filets or steaks
3. Take the yolks of two eggs and a little milk and beat together
4. Add salt and pepper and soak the Trout well in this, rolling over and over
5. Take it out of the pan and roll in cracker or bread crumbs
6. Pat the fish a little all over with your hands and then fry in the same manner as Croakers
7. Serve on a bed of fried parsley, with garnish of sliced lemon
8. For the Sauce a la Tartare: Take one large onion and four or five sprigs of parsley and chop fine
9. Squeeze the juice out well and parboil, chopping fine and squeezing out the water
10. Prepare about one-half pint of Mayonnaise Dressing and add about two gills of mustard
11. Mix well and add to the parsley and onion and juices
12. Serve the sauce cold
