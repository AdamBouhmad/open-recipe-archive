## Ingredients

- milk
- yeast
- 5 eggs
- 1 lb. sugar
- ½ lb. butter
- 1 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make up a loaf of bread with milk and yeast
2. Next morning add to it 5 eggs, 1 lb. of sugar, ½ lb. of butter, and 1 nutmeg
3. Beat it well
4. Set it in a mould to rise until evening
