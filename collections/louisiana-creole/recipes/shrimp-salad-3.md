## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Cold Boiled Shrimp
- 1 Head of Crisp Lettuce
- Mayonnaise Sauce
- Salt and pepper
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Sliced beets
- Celery

## Directions

1. Take Lake Shrimp and River Shrimp combined, if you have them.
2. Cut the larger Lake Shrimp into two.
3. Season well with salt and pepper.
4. Chop some lettuce. Season well.
5. Place first a layer of shrimp and then of lettuce, and spread over all a Mayonnaise Sauce.
6. Garnish nicely with sliced hard-boiled eggs, sliced beets and celery and lettuce tips, and serve very cold.
