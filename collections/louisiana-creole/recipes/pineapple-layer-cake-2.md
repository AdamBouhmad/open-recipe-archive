## Ingredients

- Four layers of sponge cake
- Pineapple
- Powdered sugar
- Cream Sauce (made to the consistency of Cream Puff Filling)

## Directions

1. Pare the pineapple, being careful to cut out all the eyes and the core.
2. Grate the fruit very fine.
3. Place a layer of the fruit over the bottom layer of the cake.
4. Sprinkle with a little powdered sugar.
5. Spread over this a little Cream Sauce, which has been made to the consistency of Cream Puff Filling.
6. Put a second layer of the cake over this.
7. Cover nicely with the grated pineapple, sugar and Cream Filling.
8. Proceed thus till the top layer is placed over the pineapple.
9. Ice or sprinkle with white sugar.
