## Ingredients

- 3 Kidneys
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Lemon Juice
- Chopped Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Wash the kidneys well and slice them; cut into thin pieces of about three inches long and two inches wide
2. Run a wooden or silver skewer through to hold them together, and season well with salt and pepper
3. Brush with a little butter, and put on a double broiler and broil for about five minutes, turning over the broiler to allow each side to cook
4. Place on a platter and pour over melted butter and chopped parsley and lemon juice, and serve hot, as you would broiled steak
