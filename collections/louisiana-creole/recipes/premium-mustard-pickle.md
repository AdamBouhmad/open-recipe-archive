## Ingredients

- three quarts of small cucumbers, gherkins, or green tomatoes
- salt water
- vinegar
- one ounce of white mustard seed
- two ounces of turmeric
- three ounces of sliced ginger
- two ounces of shredded horseradish
- one-half pound of mustard
- three pounds of brown sugar
- one-half pint of sweet oil
- one ounce each of celery seed, black pepper, cloves, mace
- one teaspoonful of cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Soak three quarts of small cucumbers, gherkins, or green tomatoes, in strong salt water for three days
2. Put them into fresh cold water for a day or two
3. Scald them in plain vinegar and set them by in a place to cool
4. Take a gallon of vinegar, add to it one ounce of white mustard seed, two ounces of turmeric, three of sliced ginger, two of shredded horseradish, one-half pound of mustard, three pounds of brown sugar, one-half pint of sweet oil, one ounce each of celery seed, black pepper, cloves, mace, and one teaspoonful of cayenne pepper
5. Boil all these ingredients for fifteen minutes and pour it on the cucumbers, gherkins, or other scalded vegetable
