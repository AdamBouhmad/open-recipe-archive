## Ingredients

- 8 Potatoes
- 1 Spoonful of Flour
- Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Vinegar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Salt and Pepper
- Water

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes
2. Take out of the water, drain and set to the side of the stove for five minutes, to evaporate
3. Blend one tablespoonful of flour with two of butter, and, as it melts, add about three large tablespoonfuls of water
4. Let it come to a boil, and take off the stove and add the yolk of an egg, which has been beaten very light
5. Add two teaspoonfuls of vinegar
6. Beat well, and pour over the potatoes, and serve either hot or cold à la salade
