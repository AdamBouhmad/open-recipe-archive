## Ingredients

- three pounds of lean beefsteak
- salt
- pepper
- beef broth
- mushroom catsup
- Harvey’s or Worcestershire sauce
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Cut three pounds of lean beefsteak
2. Salt, pepper and fry quickly so as to brown without cooking through
3. Then place in a deep dish
4. Get four dozen oysters, beard them, and lay them in the pan over the beef
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Take the gravy in which the steaks were fried
7. Pour out some of the grease
8. Dredge in a tablespoonful of flour
9. Let it brown and add to it a pint of good beef broth
10. Then put in a wine-glassful of mushroom catsup
11. Some of Harvey’s or Worcestershire sauce
12. Heat it, and let it boil up a few times
13. Then pour it over the oysters and steak
14. When the gravy has become cool
15. Cover the pie with a good puff paste
16. And bake it for an hour and a half
