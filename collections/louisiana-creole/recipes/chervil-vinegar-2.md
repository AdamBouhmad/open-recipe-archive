## Ingredients

- 1 pint of dried or fresh chervil leaves
- 1 quart of best French vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a wide-mouthed bottle, and half fill it with either fresh or dry chervil leaves.
2. Fill the bottle with the best vinegar, and set it in hot water till it reaches the boiling point.
3. Then remove from the fire.
4. When cool cork and in about two weeks it will be ready for use.
