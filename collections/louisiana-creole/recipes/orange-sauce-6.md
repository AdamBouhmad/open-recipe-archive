## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1/4 Cup of Water
- The Juice and Grated Zest of 2 Oranges
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful Grated Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar, butter and yolks of the eggs to a cream
2. Then add the water and the juice and grated zest of two oranges
3. Set to boil in a double boiler; add, if desired, when it begins to simmer well, one tablespoonful of wine
4. Take off the fire and add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a froth
5. Serve either hot or cold
6. If served cold, add the whites of the eggs at the moment of serving, as also the grated nutmeg
7. Serve with Orange Pudding, Orange Roly-Poly, etc.
