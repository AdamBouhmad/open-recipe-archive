## Ingredients

- 2 Raw Chicken Breasts
- The Yolks of 4 Eggs
- Bread Soaked In Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Butter
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Teaspoonful Each of Thyme and Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- A Pinch of Grated Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cut up the chicken and pound in a mortar
2. Add an equal quantity of bread soaked in milk or water and well squeezed
3. Add the butter and the yolks of the eggs
4. Blend well and season highly with salt and pepper and the minced herbs, and add a pinch of grated nutmeg
5. Mix all together and roll into balls, and use as desired
