## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Fresh Figs
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 1 Cup of Flour
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Grated Nutmeg
- 1/2 Cup of Butter

## Directions

1. Peel the figs and cut fine.
2. Then make a batter of the eggs, flour and milk, according to recipe.
3. When smooth, add the fruit.
4. Turn into a baking pan, and bake for an hour.
5. Serve with Wine Sauce.
