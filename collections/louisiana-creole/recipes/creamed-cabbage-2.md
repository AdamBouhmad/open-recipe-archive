## Ingredients

- 1 head of tender white cabbage
- A cream sauce

## Directions

1. Take a fine delicate head of white cabbage
2. Cut it in quarters and soak in cold water for an hour
3. Remove all the hard parts and cut the remainder into fine shreds
4. Put it into the stewing pan and pour over boiling water enough to cover
5. Season with salt and pepper
6. Let it boil at least thirty minutes and then drain in a colander
7. Have ready a heated dish
8. Turn the cabbage into this and pour over a cream sauce
