## Ingredients

- A Filet of Lamb
- A Sauce à la Béchamel

## Directions

1. Roast the lamb as in the manner given, and prepare a 'Sauce à la Béchamel.' (See recipe.)
2. Slice the lamb and pour over the sauce and serve.
