## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 3/4 pound of sugar
- 1 cup of milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and whites of the eggs together.
2. Add the flour, and beat very light.
3. Add the milk, pouring gradually, and having the batter no thicker than cream.
4. Add the salt, and mix well.
5. Have a wide pancake pan, and let it be very hot.
6. Grease it with butter, or better still, with a piece of fat bacon.
7. Pour in batter sufficient to just cover the bottom of the pan.
8. In a minute, or perhaps less time, the cake must be ready to turn.
9. Toss the cake and catch it in the pan, and bring the brown side up without failure.
10. Continue baking and tossing the pancakes, rolling them up with sugar and cinnamon and butter mixed, or buttered and rolled in molasses.
11. Serve hot.
