## Ingredients

- 1 lb. Baker's prepared cocoa
- 1 quart rich milk
- 2 or 3 tablespoonfuls loaf sugar
- 8 eggs
- chocolate macaroon

## Directions

1. Make some strong chocolate, allowing 1 lb. of the best, which is Baker's prepared cocoa, to a quart of rich milk, first fixing the milk, and scrape the chocolate to a smooth paste
2. Boil them together 1 hour
3. While warm, stir in 2 or 3 tablespoonfuls of loaf sugar
4. Set it away to cool
5. Have ready 8 wellbeaten eggs, and stir them gradually into the chocolate
6. Bake the mixture in cups, and serve them up with a chocolate macaroon laid on the top of each
