## Ingredients

- 1 pound of sugar
- 3 quarts of water
- juice of 2 dozen lemons
- juice of 1 pineapple
- 2 bottles of seltzer water
- 3 dozen fine, large strawberries
- slices of pineapple
- slices of lemon
- a large piece of ice
- crushed ice

## Directions

1. Take a large punch bowl
2. Put into it one pound of sugar, three quarts of water, the juice of two dozen lemons, the juice of one pineapple, two bottles of seltzer water
3. Mix all well
4. Add three dozen fine, large strawberries, and decorate with slices of pineapple and lemon sliced very thin
5. In case there is not sufficient sugar, sweeten again to taste
6. Put a large piece of ice in the bowl
7. When ready to serve, fill glasses one-quarter full of crushed ice, and fill up with the lemonade, being careful to have several strawberries and a slice or two of lemon and pineapple in each glass
