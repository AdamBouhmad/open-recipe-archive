## Ingredients

- 2 Fine Canvas-Back Ducks
- 3/4 Pint of Veal Broth or Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Dozen Mushrooms, Sliced
- 3 Fresh or Canned Tomatoes
- 1 Onion
- 3/4 Glass of Madeira Wine or Lemon Juice
- 3/4 Pint of Sauce Espagnole
- The Zest of 1 Lemon
- Croutons
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Pick the ducks; singe, draw, and, after rinsing clean within, wipe neatly within and without.
2. Cut off the wings, legs and breasts.
3. Take the two carcasses and sprinkle rightly with salt and place in the oven to bake about six minutes.
4. Remove the carcasses and hash them up.
5. Put them into the saucepan; add a pint of veal broth, consommé or water in lieu of either of these.
6. Add a herb bouquet tied together and let the preparation simmer for about a quarter of an hour over a moderate fire.
7. Put a tablespoonful of butter into a saucepan, and lay in the wings, breasts and legs of the Ducks.
8. Season lightly with salt and pepper, and set on a very brisk fire and let cook for a few minutes, on either side.
9. Add a half glassful of Madeira wine and a half pint of Sauce Espagnole and the grated zest of a lemon.
10. Take the gravy from the carcasses and strain over the Ducks, and allow all to cook about a quarter of an hour.
11. Place on a hot dish and decorate nicely.
