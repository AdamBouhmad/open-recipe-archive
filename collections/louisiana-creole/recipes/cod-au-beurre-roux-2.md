## Ingredients

- a piece of cod
- some butter
- a little flour
- a little sugar in powder
- slices of onion
- vinegar
- crisp parsley

## Directions

1. Boil a piece of cod and separate it into flakes
2. Brown some butter
3. Dredge in a little flour, and a little sugar in powder
4. In this fry some slices of onion a fine brown
5. Throw in the vinegar
6. Boil it up
7. Pour over the fish
8. Serve it with crisp parsley
