## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of Toast
- 1 Cup of Forcemeat of Game (White Meat)
- ¾ Cup of Forcemeat of Game (Dark Meat)

## Directions

1. Prepare triangular-shaped pieces of toast
2. Butter nicely
3. Spread over with a game forcemeat (Woodcock, Snipe, Reed Birds, etc.)
4. Decorate the edges with a forcemeat of game of different color, for effect
5. Serve
