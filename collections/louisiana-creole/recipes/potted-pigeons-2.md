## Ingredients

- pigeons
- bread crumbs
- sweet marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- egg
- chopped pork or butter
- pork
- powdered cloves
- flour
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Wash the pigeons
2. Prepare dressing with bread crumbs, plenty of sweet marjoram, a little salt, pepper, 1 egg, and either chopped pork or piece of butter
3. Stuff the pigeons and lay on their backs in a pan
4. Put pork about an inch in size on breast of each
5. Throw over them a very little salt and powdered cloves
6. Cover all thick with flour from dredging box
7. Pour boiling water over the breasts
8. Cook about 2 hours, basting frequently
