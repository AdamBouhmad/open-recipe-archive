## Ingredients

- half a teacup of melted butter
- a teaspoonful of salt
- a cup of good yeast
- a pint of lukewarm water
- flour

## Directions

1. Stir half a teacup of melted butter, a teaspoonful of salt and a cup of good yeast, into a pint of lukewarm water
2. Add flour to make a stiff batter
3. Set this to rise
4. When light drop this mixture onto flat buttered tins, several inches apart
5. Let them stay in a warm place fifteen minutes, before being put in the oven to bake
6. Bake them quickly to a light brown color
