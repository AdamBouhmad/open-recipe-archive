## Ingredients

- 4 Carrots
- 2 Onions
- 1 Gill of Cream
- 2 Sticks of Celery
- 3 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Make a good puree of fine, red carrots and then strain in butter.
2. Add a gill of rich cream and salt and pepper to the taste.
3. Put the bouilli in the platter and pour the sauce around it, serving hot just after the soup.
