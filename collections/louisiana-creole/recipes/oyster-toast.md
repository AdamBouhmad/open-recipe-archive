## Ingredients

- a quart of oysters
- a little cream or fresh butter
- some pepper and salt
- thin slices of toast
- fresh butter
- a thin slice of fresh lemon
- parsley

## Directions

1. Scald a quart of oysters in their own liquor
2. Take them out and pound or chop them to a paste
3. Add a little cream or fresh butter, and some pepper and salt
4. Get ready some thin slices of toast moistened with boiling water
5. And spread with fresh butter
6. Then, spread over the butter the oyster paste
7. Put a thin slice of fresh lemon on each piece
8. And lay parsley on the platter
9. Serve this very hot or it will not be good
