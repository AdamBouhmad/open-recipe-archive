## Ingredients

- Three cups of sugar
- half a cup of vinegar
- one-third of a cup of water
- butter
- flavoring essence

## Directions

1. Boil sugar, vinegar, and water together until it is thick and will harden when dropped into a cup of water
2. Butter some dishes
3. Just before filling them, add to the candy some flavoring essence
4. Pour the candy on the buttered dishes
5. When a little cool prepare to pull it until it is white and light
