## Ingredients

- head
- liver
- eggs
- meat from under-shell
- shell
- bacon
- onions
- water

## Directions

1. Cut off the head and let it bleed well
2. Separate the bottom shell from the top with care
3. For fear of breaking the gall bag
4. Throw the liver and eggs, if any, into a bowl of water
5. Slice off all the meat from the under-shell and put in water also
6. Break the shell in pieces, wash carefully and place it in a pot
7. Cover it with water, and add one pound of middling or flitch of bacon with four chopped onions
8. Set this on the fire to boil
