## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of race ginger
- 1 lb. of horse radish
- 1 lb. of mustard seed
- ½ lb. of garlic
- 2 ounces black pepper
- 2 ounces of cloves
- ½ ounce of mace
- mangoes
- salt
- water
- 1 pint of made mustard
- sweet oil
- boiling vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak and dry the race ginger well
2. Do the horse radish the same way
3. Wash the mustard seed well
4. Soak and bleach the garlic
5. Lay the mangoes in salt and water till they are yellow
6. Scald the mangoes until they are green
7. Soak the ingredients well and beat them together in a mortar
8. Mix them with a pint of made mustard and as much sweet oil as will make the ingredients into a paste
9. Fill the mangoes, and put what is left between the layers
10. Pour over them as much boiling vinegar as will cover them
