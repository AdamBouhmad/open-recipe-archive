## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 1 egg
- 1 spoonful good yeast
- milk
- 2 ounces butter

## Directions

1. Make 1 quart of flour, 1 egg, and 1 spoonful of good yeast into a thin batter with milk
2. Put in 2 ounces of butter
3. In summer this should be done early in the morning
4. In winter, the night before
