## Ingredients

- 100 oysters with their juice
- one large onion
- a tablespoonful of flour
- red pepper
- a large spoonful of butter
- one or two tablespoonfuls of filee

## Directions

1. Take 100 oysters with their juice, and one large onion;
2. slice the onion into hot lard and fry it brown, adding when brown a tablespoonful of flour and red pepper.
3. When thick enough pour in the oysters.
4. Boil together twenty minutes.
5. Stir in a large spoonful of butter and one or two tablespoonfuls of filee;
6. then take the soup from the fire and serve with rice.
