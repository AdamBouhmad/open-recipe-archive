## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- six eggs
- one cup of white sugar
- half cup of sugar
- rose, lemon, or peach-leaf flavoring

## Directions

1. Set the milk to boil
2. Beat the whites of the eggs with a half cup of sugar, and drop into the boiling milk for two minutes
3. With a skimmer remove the boiled whites, and put on a dish to cool
4. When the whites are taken off, stir into the milk the yolks and sugar, previously well beaten up together
5. Add rose, lemon, or peach-leaf flavoring
6. Run this through a sieve into the bowl you expect to serve it in
7. Pile up the whites on the custard
