## Ingredients

- beets
- butter
- sugar
- salt
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the beets clean, but do not trim the roots
2. If the beets are young and tender, cook them whole
3. Stew them in a little butter, with sugar, salt and vinegar added
4. Let them simmer in this batter for twenty minutes
5. Serve
