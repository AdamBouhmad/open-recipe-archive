## Ingredients

- 1 fresh beef tongue, boiled and chopped very fine
- 2 lbs. of suet, picked and chopped fine
- 4 lbs. of raisins
- 2 lbs. of Sultana plums
- 3 lbs. of currants, washed and dried
- 2 lbs. of white sugar
- 1 pint of brandy
- 1 bottle of wine
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- mace
- allspice
- apples

## Directions

1. Boil and chop beef tongue very fine
2. Combine with suet, raisins, plums, currants, sugar, brandy, wine, nutmeg, cinnamon, mace, and allspice
3. Mix ingredients well together and keep in a closely covered jar
4. The day you bake the pie, add a few apples cut up very fine
5. Line the dish with light paste, covering the top with the same and bake in a moderate oven
