## Ingredients

- large, ripe, full-blown cauliflowers
- boiling water
- one ounce of mace
- one quart of good cider vinegar
- white pepper
- white mustard seed
- ginger

## Directions

1. Take large, ripe, full-blown cauliflowers
2. Divide the pieces equally
3. Throw them into a kettle of boiling water
4. Boil them until a little soft, but not as much as if for the table
5. Take the pieces out and let them cool
6. Scald an ounce of mace, to each quart of good cider vinegar
7. Pour it hot on the cauliflower
8. Spices such as are usually used in pickling, improve this recipe, and should be tied in a bag and thrown in with the pickle at the last
9. Use white pepper, white mustard seed and ginger as the spices suitable for this pickle
