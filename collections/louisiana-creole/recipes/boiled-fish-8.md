## Ingredients

- fish
- sprig of onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- eight or ten cloves
- about two dozen allspice
- bit of lemon peel
- red pepper pod
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the fish thoroughly.
2. Make a small letter 'S' with knife on the back.
3. Pass twine around the body of the fish so as to secure it.
4. Have ready a kettle of boiling water and throw in a sprig of onion, thyme and bay leaf, eight or ten cloves, about two dozen allspice, all mashed fine; a bit of lemon peel and a red pepper pod.
5. When the water has boiled long enough to have extracted the flavor of these ingredients, drop the fish in carefully, so as to avoid breaking.
6. Let it boil about ten minutes and then take out carefully.
7. Put into a strainer and drain quickly.
8. Place on a bed of parsley with garnishes of lemon and serve either a Mayonnaise or Genoise Sauce or Sauce Hollandaise.
