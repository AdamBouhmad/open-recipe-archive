## Ingredients

- 1 Chicken
- 3/4 Tablespoonful Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Proceed in exactly the same manner as for roasting a turkey
2. The chicken must bake in a quick oven, allowing fifteen minutes to every pound
3. A roasted chicken may be stuffed or not, according to taste, with a stuffing of oysters, eggs or truffles in exactly the same manner as turkey
