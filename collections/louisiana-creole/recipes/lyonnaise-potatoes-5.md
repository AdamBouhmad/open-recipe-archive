## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Cold Boiled Potatoes, Cut in Dice
- 2 Onions
- 2 Large Tablespoonfuls Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Take cold boiled potatoes, and cut them into slices.
2. Slice the onions fine.
3. Put the butter into the frying pan and as it melts add the onions.
4. Let them fry until half done.
