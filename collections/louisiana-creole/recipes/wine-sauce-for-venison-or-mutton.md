## Ingredients

- a pint of soup from the stock pot
- a dozen cloves
- a teaspoon of salt
- pepper
- a cup of wine or currant jelly

## Directions

1. Take from the stock pot a pint of the soup
2. Let it boil down to half a pint
3. Season with a dozen cloves, a teaspoon of salt, and a little pepper
4. Stir in a cup of wine, or of currant jelly
