## Ingredients

- 6 Plantains
- 1/4 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Rum
- Kirsch or Maraschino Sauce

## Directions

1. Have ready a pile of hot ashes in the oven
2. Put the plantains (unpeeled) into the ashes and cover well with ashes
3. Let them roast thus for half an hour
4. Take out, peel, cut into quarters and sprinkle with sugar
5. Set in the oven a few minutes and serve hot, with a Rum, Kirsch or Maraschino Sauce
