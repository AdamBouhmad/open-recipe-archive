## Ingredients

- 1 pint flour
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- 1/2 pint sweet milk

## Directions

1. Sift the flour very, very fine, and then add the salt, and mix well
2. Add the milk and make into a nice, rather stiff dough
3. Place on a biscuit board and beat for fully a half hour
4. Turn the dough over and beat for fifteen minutes more
5. Roll them out into cakes that are almost transparent in thinness, but which can also be handled without losing their shape
6. Lay them gently in the pan, or roll them out on a baking sheet which has been sprinkled with flour
7. Bake quickly in a hot oven
