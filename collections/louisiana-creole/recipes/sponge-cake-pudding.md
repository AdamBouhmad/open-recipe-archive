## Ingredients

- stale sponge or other plain cake
- a little more than a pint of milk
- two or three beaten eggs
- sugar and butter

## Directions

1. Crumble stale sponge or other plain cake into a little more than a pint of milk and two or three beaten eggs
2. Bake it
3. Serve with a sauce of sugar and butter beaten together
