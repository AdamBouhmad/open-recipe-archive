## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- one cup of sour cream or thick milk
- a teaspoonful of saleratus dissolved in hot water
- a gill of brandy
- half a grated nutmeg
- a teaspoonful of essence of lemon or the yellow rind of a grated lemon
- flour

## Directions

1. Stir in flour until the batter is as thick as pound cake
2. bake an inch deep in a buttered basin
