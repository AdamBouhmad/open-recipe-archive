## Ingredients

- oysters
- 3 or 4 eggs
- grated bread
- grated nutmeg
- mace
- salt
- lard

## Directions

1. Choose the largest and finest oysters
2. Beat the yolks of 3 or 4 eggs
3. Mix with them grated bread and a small quantity of grated nutmeg, mace, and a little salt
4. Stir this batter well
5. Dip the oysters into it
6. Fry them in lard, till they are of a light brown color
7. Take care not to do them too much
8. Serve them up hot
