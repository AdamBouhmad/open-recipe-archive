## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Finest White Icing Sugar
- 2 Pounds of Gum Arabic
- 2 Ounces of Essence of Mocha Coffee
- 1/2 Gill of Fine Old Cognac

## Directions

1. Work gum, brandy and icing sugar into a paste
2. Cover with the Royal Icing flavored with Essence of Coffee
3. Proceed in the same manner as Chocolate Creams
