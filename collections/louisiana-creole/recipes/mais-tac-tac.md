## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Louisiana Molasses
- 3/4 Pound of Indian Corn (Parched)

## Directions

1. Boil one pint of Louisiana molasses
2. As it comes to the boiling point, throw in about three-quarters of a pound of parched Indian corn, parched to a blossom
3. Stir well
4. Pour into little paper cases, about five or six inches in length, three in width and one and a half in depth
5. Let these cool before touching
