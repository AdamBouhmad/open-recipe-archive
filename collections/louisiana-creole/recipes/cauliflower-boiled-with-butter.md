## Ingredients

- 2 Medium-Sized Cauliflowers
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- A Tablespoonful of Salt
- A Pinch of Pepper
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare the cauliflower as in recipe Boiled Cauliflower, Cream Sauce.
2. After picking and washing thoroughly put in a saucepan and cover with cold water.
3. Add the salt and pepper and a tablespoonful of butter.
4. Let it cook for a half hour and then take the cauliflower from the pan and drain through a colander.
5. Place them on a dish and add a sauce made of one tablespoonful of butter, one of vinegar and a dash of salt and pepper, all mixed thoroughly, and serve hot.
