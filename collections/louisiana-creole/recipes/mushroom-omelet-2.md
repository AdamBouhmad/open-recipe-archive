## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1/2 can of mushrooms
- 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Stew the mushrooms a few minutes
2. Chop them fine
3. Make a plain omelet
4. When it is ready to fold, place the mushrooms across the center
5. Fold twice over
6. Let it cook two minutes longer and serve hot
