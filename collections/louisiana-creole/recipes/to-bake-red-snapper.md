## Ingredients

- large fish
- stale bread crumbs
- onions
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- water
- flour

## Directions

1. Take a large fish, clean it, cut off the head, and draw the entrails through that part, as it must not be cut open
2. Take the crumbs of stale bread, some onions and parsley (chopped), pepper and salt for seasoning
3. Stuff the fish with this seasoning
4. Put small lumps of butter all over the dish
5. Pour in water to the depth of 2 inches
6. Sprinkle over it a little flour
7. Put the pan in a well heated oven and bake an hour and a half
