## Ingredients

- a calf's head
- salt
- water
- butter
- flour
- lemon juice
- tomatoes
- pepper
- sweet herbs

## Directions

1. Have a head nicely cleaned, and soak it in salt and water to make it look white.
2. Remove the eyes.
3. Take out the tongue and salt it.
4. Of the brains make a separate dish.
5. To boil the head put it in a pot of lukewarm water and boil till very tender.
6. Serve with sauce made of butter, flour and water, some lemon juice and tomatoes.
7. If to bake, dredge flour over it, put on bits of butter, season with pepper, salt, and sweet herbs, set in a hot oven and baste with the water in which it was boiled.
