## Ingredients

- white parts of four or five heads of celery
- green celery leaves
- a fine baked chicken
- yolks of six hard-boiled eggs
- two spoonfuls of mixed mustard
- a teaspoonful of white sugar
- oil
- half a cup of vinegar
- white heads of lettuce
- four hard-boiled eggs sliced

## Directions

1. Cut up the white parts of four or five heads of celery, reserving the green leaves
2. Pick all the meat from a fine baked chicken, chop this up, and mix it with the cut-up celery
3. Lay it in a glass or china dish, where it will be cool
4. To make the dressing, rub the yolks of six hard-boiled eggs to a paste, with two spoonfuls of mixed mustard, a teaspoonful of white sugar, and enough oil to make it perfectly smooth
5. Put this in slowly, a little at a time
6. Finish the dressing by pouring in half a cup of vinegar
7. Pour this over the celery and chicken, and garnish with white heads of split lettuce, also the reserved celery leaves, and four sliced hard-boiled eggs
