## Ingredients

- 1 pound of pulverized sugar
- whites of 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of rose or orange flower water or vanilla essence
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Have the eggs set in the refrigerator for about an hour before using.
2. Break the whites into a cold, shallow dish, and throw in a handful of sugar at a time, beating meanwhile, and adding at short intervals, till you have used up all the sugar.
3. Beat the eggs and the sugar and a teaspoonful of rose or orange flower water or vanilla essence together till the eggs are very light.
4. When the mixture is stiff and smooth, begin to lay a thin coating of the ice over the cake, using a knife to spread it, and occasionally dipping the knife into the lemon juice, which will enable you to smooth it nicely.
5. When it is covered with this first coat, set in a warm place to harden — or in a moderate oven, where you may let it remain about four minutes, or in the sunshine where there is no dust around.
6. When it is hardened it will be ready for the next coat.
7. This coat will be much smoother and whiter than the first.
