## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 10 eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls of mace
- 2 teaspoonfuls of nutmeg
- 1 large wineglass of brandy
- 1 large wineglass of wine
- 1 lb. of citron
- 1 lb. of almonds
- rose water

## Directions

1. Blanch almonds and beat fine with rose water
2. Separate the whites of the eggs from the yolks
3. Mix the yolks and sugar together
4. Beat the whites to a very light froth, then add to the yolks and sugar
5. Cream the butter light and mix in by degrees
6. Beat all together very light
7. Mix in the flour, beat it till quite smooth
8. Add the mace and nutmeg, then the wine and brandy, and lastly the raisins, citron and almonds
9. Stir all well together
10. Butter the cups, fill them nearly full, and bake in a quick oven
