## Ingredients

- one tablespoonful of butter
- one cup of sugar
- one cup of milk
- two eggs
- one teaspoonful of soda
- one pint of sifted flour
- two spoonfuls of cream of tartar
- powdered sugar
- one tablespoonful of butter for sauce
- one cup of powdered sugar for sauce
- lemon extract or lemon juice for sauce
- half a pint of boiling water for sauce

## Directions

1. Mix butter, sugar, milk, eggs, soda, sifted flour, and cream of tartar like cake.
2. Bake quickly in shallow tin pans.
3. Dredge the top with powdered sugar, which gives a nice crust.
4. For the sauce: beat together one tablespoonful of butter, one cup of powdered sugar, lemon extract or lemon juice for seasoning, with half a pint of boiling water until it foams.
