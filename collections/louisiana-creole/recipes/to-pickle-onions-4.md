## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- whole pepper
- mustard seed

## Directions

1. Peel the onions, boil some strong salt and water and put it over them, cover, and let them stand twenty-four hours
2. Take them up with a skimmer
3. Make some vinegar boiling hot, put to it whole pepper and mustard seed, and pour it over the onions to cover them
4. When cold cover close
