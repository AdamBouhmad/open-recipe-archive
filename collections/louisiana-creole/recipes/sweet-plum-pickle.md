## Ingredients

- eight pounds of fruit
- four pounds of sugar
- two quarts of vinegar
- one ounce of cinnamon
- one ounce of cloves

## Directions

1. Boil the vinegar, sugar and spices together
2. Skim it carefully
3. Pour it boiling on the fruit
4. Pour it off, and skim and scald each day for three days
5. If for putting away, scald it the fourth time and cork up tightly
