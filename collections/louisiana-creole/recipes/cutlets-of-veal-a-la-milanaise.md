## Ingredients

- 6 Veal Cutlets
- 1 Egg
- 1 Cup of Bread Crumbs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Melted Butter
- A Bed of Macaroni or Spaghetti
- Tomato Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare as above.
2. Serve the cutlets on a bed of boiled macaroni or spaghetti.
3. Pour over the whole a Tomato Sauce.
