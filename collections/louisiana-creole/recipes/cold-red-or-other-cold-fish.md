## Ingredients

- cold fish
- bones
- onions
- parsley
- milk or cream
- flour
- yolks of 1 or 2 eggs
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Set the cold fish away with some of the bones
2. put these bones on to stew, with onions and parsley cut up
3. make a thickening of milk, or cream, flour, the yolks of 1 or 2 eggs, pepper and salt
4. when the onions are done, take out the bones, and cut up the fish, as picked crabs
5. put it into the above gravy, and stew till quite hot
6. then add the above thickening
