## Ingredients

- 1 quart of water
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 cup of rice

## Directions

1. Put a quart of water on the fire, and let it boil well, with a teaspoonful of salt
2. Wash a cup of rice well in cold water
3. When the water commences to boil well add the rice
4. Stir occasionally, and gently with a wooden spoon
5. As soon as the grains commence to soften, do not stir or touch the rice again
6. Let it continue to boil rapidly for about twenty minutes, or until the grains begin to swell out
7. Take the cover off and pour off the water, and set the pot in the oven, so that the rice may swell up
8. Let it stand in the oven about ten minutes
9. Do not let it brown, but simply dry
10. Take it off, and let it stand a few minutes
11. Pour out into a dish
