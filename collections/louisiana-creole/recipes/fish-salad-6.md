## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Cold Boiled Fish
- 1 Head of Lettuce
- Mayonnaise Sauce

## Directions

1. Use cold boiled left-over fish, picking nicely into bits of about an inch and a half square.
2. Follow the same directions as in the above recipes, only do not mix lettuce and fish in layers.
