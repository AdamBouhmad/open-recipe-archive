## Ingredients

- 8 Tomatoes
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Cup of Grated Bread Crumbs
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Scald and skin the tomatoes.
2. Then place a layer of them at the bottom of a baking dish, cover with a layer of bread crumbs, spread very thick, and season with salt and pepper and dots of butter at intervals.
3. Continue in this way till the last layer is reached, finishing with a layer of bread crumbs, sprinkle with salt and pepper, dot with butter, and put in an oven to bake for an hour and a quarter.
4. Canned tomatoes will require about half this time.
