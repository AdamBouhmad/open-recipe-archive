## Ingredients

- one cupful of chopped suet
- half a cup of molasses
- one cup of raisins chopped
- a teaspoonful of powdered cloves and cinnamon
- one-half cup of sugar
- two eggs well beaten
- half a cup of sweet milk
- a little salt
- two teaspoonfuls of yeastpowder
- flour

## Directions

1. Stir in flour until it is a thick batter.
2. Flour a cloth, and pour in the mixture, leaving room to swell.
3. Boil two hours.
