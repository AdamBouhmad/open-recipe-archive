## Ingredients

- mealy potatoes
- salt
- butter
- flour
- yeast
- lukewarm water or milk

## Directions

1. Boil thoroughly and mash fine, mealy potatoes
2. Add salt and a very little butter
3. Rub them with twice their quantity of flour
4. Stir in the yeast, moisten with lukewarm water or milk, till stiff enough to mould
5. It will rise quicker than common wheat bread, and should be baked as soon as risen
