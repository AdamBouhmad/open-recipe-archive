## Ingredients

- 8 Fine Young Partridges
- Slice of Toast Bread
- 1½ Ounces of Fresh Bread Crumbs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- ½ Cup of Cold Water
- ½ Cup of Cream or Milk
- ⅜ Teaspoonful of Salt
- 6 White Peppers
- Parsley sprigs or watercress

## Directions

1. Prepare the Partridges and roast according to recipe for Roast Partridge
2. Make a bread sauce as follows: Crumble one and a half ounces of fresh bread crumbs and place in a saucepan with a half cup of cold water
3. When the water heats, add a tablespoonful of butter, six whole white peppers and a half teaspoonful of salt
4. Cook for five minutes
5. Add a half cup of rich milk or cream
6. Let the whole cook five minutes more
7. Remove the white peppers
8. Place the Partridges on the toasted bread, and garnish the dish nicely with parsley sprigs or watercress
9. Send to the table hot with the sauce in a separate dish
10. Pour the sauce over the Partridges when serving
