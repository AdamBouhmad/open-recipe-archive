## Ingredients

- 3 Small Brioches
- 1 Tablespoonful of Essence of Vanilla
- Lemon or Orange Flower Water
- Creole Fritter Batter
- Powdered White Sugar
- Sweetened milk

## Directions

1. Cut the Brioche into slices, more or less thin, and soak them in sweetened milk, to which you have added the essence of orange flower water, vanilla or lemon.
2. Drop them into a light fritter batter, or simply drop them into boiling lard.
3. Fry to a golden yellow.
4. Sprinkle with white sugar, and serve.
