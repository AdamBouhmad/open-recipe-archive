## Ingredients

- shrimps
- catsup
- butter
- salt
- red pepper
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Boil your shrimps and pick them
2. Put them into an a la braise dish
3. Add two tablespoonfuls of catsup and one of butter to every half pint of shrimps
4. Season with salt, red and black pepper to your taste
