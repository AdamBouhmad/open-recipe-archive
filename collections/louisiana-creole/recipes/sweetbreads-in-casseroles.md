## Ingredients

- 3 Pairs of Sweetbreads
- 1/4 Can of Mushrooms
- 2 Dozen Oysters
- 1 Glass of Sherry Wine
- Sauce a la Poulette

## Directions

1. Parboil the Sweetbreads in exactly the same manner as in the recipe for Sweetbreads a la Poulette.
2. Cut them into dice pieces after parboiling.
3. Add a quarter of a can of finely-chopped mushrooms to the sauce, and a glass of Sherry wine.
4. Take two dozen oysters and cut in pieces, taking off all the hard portions.
5. Add the chopped sweetbreads to the sauce, and after ten minutes, add the oysters.
6. Let them cook for five minutes.
7. Have ready a pan filled with a rich vol-au-vent crust, pour the mixture in and serve.
8. Alternatively, make the vol-au-vent crust into small shells; bake and fill with the sweetbreads.
