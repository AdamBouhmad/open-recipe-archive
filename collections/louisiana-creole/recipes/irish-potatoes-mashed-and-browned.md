## Ingredients

- potatoes
- sweet butter
- a little milk
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil them without peeling
2. Peel them while hot
3. Mash them up with sweet butter, a little milk, pepper and salt
4. Smooth over with a knife blade, and slightly brown in the oven
