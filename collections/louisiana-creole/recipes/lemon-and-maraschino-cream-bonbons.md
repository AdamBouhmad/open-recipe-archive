## Ingredients

- 2 Ounces of Soaked Gum Arabic
- 1 Ounce of Lemon Sugar
- 1/2 Gill of Maraschino
- The Whites of 2 Eggs

## Directions

1. Use the same amount of soaked Gum Arabic, icing sugar, whites of eggs and one ounce of lemon sugar, made by rasping the lemon on the sugar, and half a gill of Maraschino
2. Follow the same directions as for Orange Bonbons, with the Maraschino forming the cream for the inside and the lemon sugar for the outside
