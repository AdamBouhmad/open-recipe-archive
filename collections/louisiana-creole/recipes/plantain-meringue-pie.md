## Ingredients

- 2 Plantains
- 3/4 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Sweet Spices
- Whites of 3 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Essence

## Directions

1. Peel, slice and stew the plantains, mash through a sieve, and proceed as in recipe for Apple Meringue Pie.
