## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered White Sugar
- The Yolks of 3 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonfuls of Vanilla Essence
- ¾ Gill of Maraschino or Good Cognac

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the sugar well together, and add the vanilla essence
2. Beat thoroughly
3. Boil the milk and add the beaten egg to the milk as it comes to a boil
4. Stir briskly till it comes to a boil once more
5. Take from the fire and add the maraschino or brandy according to taste
