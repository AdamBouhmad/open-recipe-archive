## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Hog or Beef Blood (1 pint)
- 1 1/2 Pound of Hog Fat
- 2 Onions
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Season Highly
- 1/4 Clove of Garlic
- 1/2 teaspoonful each of Allspice, Mace, Clove and Nutmeg, finely ground
- 1/2 teaspoonful each of Fine Herbs

## Directions

1. Mince the onions fine and fry them slightly in a small piece of the hog fat
2. Add the minced garlic
3. Hash and mince the remaining fat very fine
4. Mix the fat thoroughly with the beef blood
5. Mix in the onions and season highly with the spices and fine herbs
6. Take the prepared casings or entrails and fill with the mixture
7. Tie the sausage casing at the further end before attempting to fill
8. Tie the other end, making the sausage into strings of about two feet
9. Wash them thoroughly on the outside after filling
10. Tie again in spaces of three inches or less in length
11. Place them to cook in a pot of tepid water, never letting them boil
12. Let them remain on the slow fire till you can prick the sausage with a needle and no blood will exude
13. Take them out, let them dry and cool
14. Fry in boiling lard or broil to serve
