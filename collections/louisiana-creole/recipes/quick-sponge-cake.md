## Ingredients

- 4 Eggs
- 1½ Cups of Sugar
- 2 Cups of Flour
- ¾ Cup of Cold Water
- 1 Teaspoonful Lemon Extract
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar to a cream
2. Add the water, and mix well
3. Then gradually add the flour, alternating with the whites of the eggs, beaten to a thick froth
4. Bake from thirty to forty-five minutes in a quick oven
