## Ingredients

- 4 yolks of eggs beaten
- juice of 1/2 a lemon
- a little grated nutmeg
- 1 tablespoonful of vinegar
- a good sized piece of butter

## Directions

1. Put 4 yolks of eggs beaten, juice of 1/2 a lemon, a little grated nutmeg, a tablespoonful of vinegar, and a good sized piece of butter into a saucepan
2. set it on a gentle fire
3. stir well, but do not let it boil
