## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Peach Juice
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup
- 1/2 Ounce of Peach Essence

## Directions

1. Pare and stone the peaches, then pound and mash well and set aside in a cool place for thirty-six hours
2. Strain through a bag, pressing out all the juice
3. Proceed in exactly the same manner as for Apricot Syrup
4. Add the essence as the syrup cools and you are about to bottle it
