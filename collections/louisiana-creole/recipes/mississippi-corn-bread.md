## Ingredients

- one quart of buttermilk
- two eggs
- three spoonfuls of butter
- a teaspoonful of saleratus
- meal

## Directions

1. Stir meal into the buttermilk until it is as thick as buckwheat batter
2. Add two eggs, three spoonfuls of butter, and a teaspoonful of saleratus
3. Bake in squares about one inch thick
4. It will require half an hour in a hot oven
