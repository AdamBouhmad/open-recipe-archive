## Ingredients

- Whiskey, brandy, gin, white wine, claret or catawba
- fine ice
- one tablespoonful of white sugar
- a little raspberry syrup
- strawberries and pineapple
- seltzer water

## Directions

1. Fill the glass half full of fine ice
2. put in one tablespoonful of white sugar
3. add a little raspberry syrup, strawberries and pineapple
4. pour in your liquor
5. fill up with seltzer water
6. Stir all rapidly
