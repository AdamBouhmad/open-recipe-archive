## Ingredients

- a quarter of a peck of green tomatoes
- a quarter of a peck of pickling-beans
- a quarter of a peck of white onions
- one dozen cucumbers
- green peppers
- one head of cabbage
- ground mustard
- celery seed
- salt
- cider vinegar
- two tablespoonfuls of sweet oil
- two tablespoonfuls of white sugar

## Directions

1. Take a quarter of a peck each, of green tomatoes, pickling-beans, and white onions, scald the onions separately
2. Add one dozen cucumbers, green peppers, and a head of cabbage chopped
3. Season with ground mustard, celery seed, and salt to taste
4. Pour over these the best cider vinegar to cover them
5. Let all boil two hours
6. While hot add two tablespoonfuls of sweet oil and the same of white sugar
7. Bottle and seal up carefully in wide-mouthed glass jars
