## Ingredients

- Calves Brains
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Small Onion
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Sprig of Parsley
- Sauce aux Beurre Noir

## Directions

1. Prepare the brains as mentioned above by boiling
2. Place in a saucepan with a tablespoonful of butter
3. Cut up a tiny onion and add also a sprig of thyme, bay leaf and parsley, all minced very fine
4. Add to the butter
5. Add the brains cut in slices a half inch thick
6. Season again to taste
7. Fry for five minutes and serve with a Sauce aux Beurre Noir
