## Ingredients

- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Barley, or a Half Cup
- 1 Pint of Cream or Milk
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 3 Quarts of Water (boiling)
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Scald the barley and then put into a kettle with three quarts of boiling water.
2. Let it boil about three hours.
3. Take it off and mash thoroughly, and strain through a sieve.
4. Add the hot milk to the stock of the barley, season with salt and pepper, and let it come to a boil.
5. Take off and add the yolks of two eggs.
