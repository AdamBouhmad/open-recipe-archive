## Ingredients

- a pint of cut okra
- a cupful of hot lard or fat of side meat
- a pint and a half of corn
- flour
- a half cup of milk
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Put a pint of cut okra in a frying pan in which there is a cupful of hot lard, or the fat of side meat
2. Let it fry a little, then cut into it a pint and a half of corn
3. Fry it until it is thoroughly cooked
4. Pour off some of the grease, and dredge in a little flour, and a half cup of milk
5. Pepper and salt must be added just before dishing it up
