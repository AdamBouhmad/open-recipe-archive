## Ingredients

- okra
- fresh butter
- pepper
- milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Use small and tender okra
2. Wash them, and cut off a small piece from each end
3. Boil them till very tender throughout
4. Drain well, and transfer them to a deep dish
5. Lay among them some bits of fresh butter, and season with pepper
6. Cover the dish, that the butter may melt the sooner
7. Or make a sauce of 1 pint of milk boiled, then add 1 lb. of good fresh butter, divided into 4 pieces, each piece rolled in a little flour
8. Stir the butter in gradually and smoothly as the milk is taken off the fire
9. Pour this sauce over the dish of okra, and keep it covered till it has gone to table
