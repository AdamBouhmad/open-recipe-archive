## Ingredients

- 1/2 ounce of isinglass
- 1 cup of sweet milk
- 1 pint of rich cream
- powdered loaf sugar
- vanilla or rose essence
- whites of 7 eggs
- sponge cake

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1/2 ounce of isinglass in a cup of sweet milk
2. Have ready a pint of rich cream
3. Stir into the cream powdered loaf sugar until it is very sweet
4. Flavor with vanilla, rose or any other essence
5. Beat up the whites of 7 eggs very light
6. Stir the dissolved isinglass into the bowl of cream after it is sweetened and flavored
7. Set the bowl into a tub of ice and stir until it thickens
8. Add last the whites of eggs
9. Put the mixture into a mould lined with sponge cake
10. Place the mould on ice
11. The cake can be joined with a little isinglass to make it stick
12. The charlotte may be made in the morning for a late dinner, or in winter over night
13. Turn the mould after cutting it round and the charlotte will come out
