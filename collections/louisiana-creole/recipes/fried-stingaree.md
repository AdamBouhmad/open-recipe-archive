## Ingredients

- A Fine Young Stingaree
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- A Handful of Chopped Parsley
- The Juice of 1 Lemon, or 1 Spoon of French Vinegar
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Egg and Bread Crumbs
- Boiling Lard
- Fried Parsley
- Sauce a la Poivrade

## Directions

1. Prepare the Stingaree wings as directed in the above recipe.
2. Cut it into square pieces of about three inches.
3. Let these soak in the vinegar which has been charged with the salt and minced parsley.
4. Roll in egg and bread crumbs and fry in boiling lard.
5. Drain and serve on a bed of fried parsley, with a Sauce a la Poivrade.
