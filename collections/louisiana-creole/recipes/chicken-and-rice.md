## Ingredients

- 1 Fine Chicken
- 1/8 Square Inch of Ham
- 2 Small Turnips
- 2 Carrots
- 2 Onions
- 1 Small Piece Lemon Peel
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Small Piece of Red Pepper Pod
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 2 Whole Cloves, Without the Seed
- 3 Sliced Tomatoes
- 3 Sprigs Each of Thyme and Parsley
- 2 Quarts of Water
- 2 Tablespoonfuls Butter
- 1 Cup of Well-washed Rice
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Clean and cut and season the chicken well with salt and pepper.
2. Put the butter into the saucepan and let it melt, and add the seasoned chicken.
3. Let it brown well, and add the vegetables, all chopped very fine.
4. Add the minced herbs and garlic, and after this the spices.
5. Let all simmer gently for ten minutes, and pour over two quarts of boiling water.
6. Stir and season again to taste and set back on the stove and let it simmer steadily and slowly for three-quarters of an hour.
7. When two-thirds cooked, add one cup of well-washed rice, stir well, seasoning again to taste.
8. Do not let the rice become mushy. Let the grains stand out.
9. Let all cook for twenty minutes longer and serve, taking out first the pieces of chicken and ranging the rice around as a garnish.
10. Serve with the sauce poured over.
