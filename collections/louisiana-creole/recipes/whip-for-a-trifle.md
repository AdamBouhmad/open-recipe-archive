## Ingredients

- 1 pint of cream
- 1 quart of water
- half lb. of powdered sugar
- juice of 4 lemons

## Directions

1. Combine 1 pint of cream, 1 quart of water, half lb. of powdered sugar, and the juice of 4 lemons
2. Whisk it to a strong froth
3. Take the froth off as it rises with a skimmer and lay it on a sieve to drain
4. Continue doing so as long as you can obtain any
5. It must be made and kept in a cool place 4 or 6 hours before it is wanted
