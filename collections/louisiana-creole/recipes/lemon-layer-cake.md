## Ingredients

- The Grated Rind and Juice of 1 Lemon
- 4 Layers of Cake
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Egg
- 1 Teaspoonful of Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Flour

## Directions

1. Make a good layer sponge or cup cake
2. Prepare a good layer filling of creamed lemon by taking one cup of sugar, one egg, the grated rind and juice of one lemon, one teaspoonful of water and one of flour
3. Blend the sugar, water and flour, and add the well-beaten egg
4. Add the rind and juice of the lemon
5. Boil in a bain-marie (a kettle set in a kettle of hot water), and let it thicken
6. Spread between the layers, and ice, or sprinkle with sugar
