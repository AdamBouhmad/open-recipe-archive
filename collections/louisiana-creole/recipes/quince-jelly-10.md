## Ingredients

- Quinces
- 1 pound of white sugar to every pint of fruit liquid

## Directions

1. Pare and core the quinces, and cut into slices.
2. Boil until well done, which may be determined by running a fork through them.
3. Strain the liquor through a flannel bag, and measure it, allowing one pound of white sugar to every pint of juice.
4. Place the whole on a fire in the preserving kettle, and watch closely.
5. Do not let it boil over.
6. Try occasionally on a saucer.
7. When it begins to grow solid it is done.
8. Bottle as directed.
