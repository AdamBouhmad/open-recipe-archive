## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of beef
- 1 cup of water
- carrot
- turnip
- greens
- salt

## Directions

1. To each 1 lb. of beef, add a cup of water
2. Put in at the same time a carrot, a turnip and other greens
3. Let simmer slowly
4. Add salt to suit taste
