## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Nice, Small White Onions
- 1 Tablespoonfal Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Throw the onions in their skins into cold water, and peel them.
2. Then put them into a saucepan of boiling water.
3. Add a teaspoonful of salt, and let them boil about forty minutes, or until you can pierce them easily with a fork.
4. Then put into a dish, and drain off all water.
5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste and pour over a Drawn Butter Sauce (see Beurre a la Maitre d'Hotel), and serve hot.
6. The large Spanish onions will require about an hour longer to boil tender.
