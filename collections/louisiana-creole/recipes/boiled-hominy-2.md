## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Hominy
- 2 Quarts of Water
- Salt to Taste

## Directions

1. Hominy should always be soaked over night in cold water.
2. Wash the hominy, and put into two quarts of water to soak.
3. In the morning turn both hominy and water into a saucepan, and let it boil slowly for three or four hours.
4. Serve with sugar and cream.
5. It may also be eaten with butter and salt and pepper.
