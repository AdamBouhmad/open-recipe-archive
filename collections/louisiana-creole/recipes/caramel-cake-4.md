## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Butter
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 1½ Cups of Flour, Sifted
- 1 Cup of Cornstarch
- Whites of 7 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add the sugar, and beat till very light
2. Add the milk
3. Mix well
4. Sift the flour, cornstarch and baking powder together, and add gradually to the mixture, beating vigorously all the while
5. Add the vanilla and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
6. Mix all quickly and lightly, turn into a long family pan, and bake for about half an hour in a moderate oven
7. Apply the broom wisp test
8. When done, take out of the oven and set to cool
9. When cool, boil together 2 Cups of Brown Sugar, 1 Cup Sweet Cream, 2 Teaspoonfuls Vanilla, and 1 Tablespoonful of Butter until it sugars, and spread over the top and sides
10. Alternatively, make a mixture of ¾ Pound Brown Sugar, ¾ Pound Chocolate, ¼ Cup Milk, 1 Tablespoonful Butter, and 2 Tablespoonfuls of Vanilla
11. Grate the chocolate, and set all to boiling together until thick enough to spread over the top and sides of the cake
