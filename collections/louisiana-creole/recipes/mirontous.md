## Ingredients

- The Left-Over Bouilli
- 3 Large Onions
- 6 Shallots
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 2 Pickles
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Slice the onions fine; brown in one tablespoonful of butter.
2. Chop the shallots and add to the onions, then add the garlic, thyme and bay leaf, all chopped very fine, and season with salt, Cayenne and black pepper to taste.
3. When the whole is browning nicely, add a tablespoonful of flour and water, or left-over broth, sufficient to cover.
4. Season this to taste and then take two pickles, about one finger long, slice very fine, and add.
5. Let all boil about fifteen minutes and then lay the cold bouilli, which has been thickly sliced, in the sauce.
6. Set it to bake in the oven about twenty minutes.
7. Garnish with buttered toast and serve hot.
