## Ingredients

- 1 lb. and 4 ounces flour
- 1 lb. and 2 ounces butter
- 1 lb. sugar
- whites of 24 eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoonful soda
- almond essence

## Directions

1. Stir the butter and flour to a cream
2. Beat the whites very lightly, then add the sugar, and again beat well
3. Mix all the ingredients together, and at the last add 1 teaspoonful of soda mixed with a little white of egg
4. Season with almond essence
5. Pour into a mould and bake
6. Ice the cake when cold
