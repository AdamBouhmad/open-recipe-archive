## Ingredients

- fowl
- butter
- black pepper
- bread for bread sauce or parsley and butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fowl thoroughly
2. Roast it 20 minutes, unless a very fine one; a fine fowl will take 3 quarters of an hour
3. Place a small lump of butter, well covered with black pepper, within the fowl previous to roasting
4. Serve with bread sauce, or parsley and butter; egg sauce is sometimes sent to table with it
