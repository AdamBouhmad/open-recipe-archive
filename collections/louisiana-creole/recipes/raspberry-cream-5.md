## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 tablespoonfuls of stiff raspberry jelly
- cream
- sugar
- wine

## Directions

1. Put 3 or 4 tablespoonfuls of stiff raspberry jelly into a large bowl
2. In another bowl froth the cream, having seasoned it with sugar and wine
3. Skim the froth as it rises and add it to the jelly
4. Beat and stir the jelly until you add frothed cream enough to make it of a handsome color and not too sweet to the taste
