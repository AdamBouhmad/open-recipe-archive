## Ingredients

- fowls
- sweet herbs
- butter
- small champignons
- flour
- broth
- pistachoes
- eggs
- cream
- lemon

## Directions

1. Spit your fowls and let them roast
2. When done, take them off and cut off the wings and the white of the breasts
3. Keep a small sauce ready in a stewpan, made with sweet herbs, a little good butter, small champignons cut in slices
4. Put it over the fire, with a dust of flour in it
5. Stir and moisten it with a ladleful of good broth
6. See it be of a good taste
7. Scald the pistachoes and cut into slices, put them in and make a thick sauce with 4 or 5 yolks of eggs
8. Beat the same up with cream
9. Put in the white and legs of fowls, with juice of a lemon
10. Cut your wings only in two
11. Place the slices of your fowls in the bottom of the dish with your sauce over it, and serve up hot for an entry
