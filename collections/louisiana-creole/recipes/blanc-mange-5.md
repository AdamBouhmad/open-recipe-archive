## Ingredients

- 2 ounces of isinglass
- 1 pint boiling water
- bitter and sweet almonds blanched
- rose water
- sugar
- 2| quarts of cream, or a mixture of cream and milk

## Directions

1. Whip 2 ounces of isinglass
2. Pour a pint of boiling water over it to dissolve it
3. Next day, break it up
4. Add to it some bitter and sweet almonds blanched
5. Beat them with rose water and sugar
6. Put it on the fire and let it boil up 5 or 6 times
7. Take it off and strain it through a muslin bag
8. Put 2| quarts of cream, or a mixture of cream and milk, on the fire to simmer
9. Stir in the isinglass which has been prepared
10. Let them simmer together for a short time
11. Take it off and pour into a pitcher
12. When sufficiently cool, fill the moulds with it
