## Ingredients

- fresh beef
- pepper
- salt
- sage
- carrots
- celery
- turnips

## Directions

1. Wash a piece of fresh beef and put in on the night before it is wanted
2. Boil it several hours until perfectly tender
3. Take it off and strain it
4. In the morning separate every particle of fat from it and put it over the fire
5. Season it with pepper and salt
6. Put in a spoonful of sage to color it
7. Prepare the vegetables nicely: carrots, celery, and turnips, cut small and boiled in the soup till tender
