## Ingredients

- green walnuts
- salt
- spring water
- blades of mace
- nutmeg
- strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Pare green walnuts very thin till the white appears
2. Throw them into spring water with a handful of salt
3. Keep them under water 6 hours
4. Put them in a stewpan to simmer 5 minutes, but do not let them boil
5. Take them out and put them in cold water and salt, keeping them quite under the water with a board
6. Lay them on a cloth and cover them with another to dry
7. Carefully rub them with a soft cloth
8. Put them into the jar with blades of mace and nutmeg cut in slices
9. Pour strong vinegar over them and put away for use
