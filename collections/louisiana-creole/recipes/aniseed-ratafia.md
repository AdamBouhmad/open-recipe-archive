## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Aniseed
- 2 Pints of Good French Cognac
- 1 Pint of Water
- 1½ Pounds of Sugar

## Directions

1. Bruise the aniseed and steep them for one month in the brandy.
2. Pass them through a sieve.
3. Add a syrup made of one pint of water and one pound and a half of sugar.
4. Filter and bottle.
