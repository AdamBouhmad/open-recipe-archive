## Ingredients

- A Fine Bluefish, or Fish in Quantity to Equal 3 Pounds
- 1 Tablespoonful of Olive Oil
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Sliced Lemon
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- Parsley to Garnish
- Sauce à la Maître d'Hôtel
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Split the fish in the middle of the back if the fish is large; if small, broil whole
2. Season well by rubbing with salt and pepper, mixed with a little sweet oil
3. Put the fish on the broiler and see that it is browned well on both sides, using always the double wire broiler
4. When done, place in a heated dish (heat by placing in a bain-marie or hot-water bath and dry thoroughly)
5. Butter the fish nicely and squeeze the juice of a lemon over it
6. Garnish with parsley and sliced lemon, and serve with a Sauce à la Maître d'Hôtel
