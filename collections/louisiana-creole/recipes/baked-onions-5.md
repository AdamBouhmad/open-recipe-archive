## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Large Spanish Onions
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Drawn Butter Sauce

## Directions

1. Trim the onions nicely, but do not peel them.
2. Put them into a kettle of boiling water, and let them boil rapidly for about an hour.
3. Drain in a colander.
4. Put them in a baking pan, and let them bake slowly for about an hour.
5. Take out and remove the skins, and place in a vegetable dish.
6. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, and serve with Drawn Butter Sauce.
