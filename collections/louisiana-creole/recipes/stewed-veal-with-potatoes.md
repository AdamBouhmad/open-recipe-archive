## Ingredients

- 1 Brisket or Knuckle of Veal
- 2 Small Potatoes
- 2 Large Onions
- 2 Carrots
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme, Parsley and Sweet Marjoram
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Proceed in the same manner as for Ragout de Veau a la Bourgeoise
2. Peel the potatoes and cut into quarters or halves, according to size
3. Just before adding the water, add the potatoes
4. Add the boiling water after they are in the stew about ten minutes, and set back, allowing it to simmer for an hour and a half, or until very tender
