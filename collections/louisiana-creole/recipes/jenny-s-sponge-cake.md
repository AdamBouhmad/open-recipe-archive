## Ingredients

- ten eggs
- one pound of sifted fine sugar
- half a pound of flour
- lemon or orange-flower water flavoring

## Directions

1. Beat ten eggs separately
2. Put the yolks with a pound of sifted fine sugar in a bowl
3. Beat it again very light
4. Put in the whites
5. Stir in half a pound of flour last of all
6. Do not beat the batter after the flour is stirred in
7. Flavor with lemon or orange-flower water
8. Pour the mixture into pans lined with buttered paper
9. Do not place more than one and a half inches of batter in each pan
10. Bake twenty minutes
11. If the oven is too hot, cover the top of the pans with a paper or pasteboard to prevent scorching
