## Ingredients

- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 3 Tomatoes
- 2 Onions
- 6 Mushrooms
- 1 Pint of Consommé
- salt
- pepper
- herb bouquet
- juice of a lemon or six drops of vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the flour and butter into a saucepan and blend well
2. Moisten with one pint of consommé or water
3. Add the chopped tomatoes, onions and mushrooms and season with a pinch of salt and pepper
4. Add an herb bouquet and let it boil for an hour
5. Before serving add the juice of a lemon or six drops of vinegar
6. If you have fresh game two tablespoonfuls of blood may be added but do not let it boil after this
