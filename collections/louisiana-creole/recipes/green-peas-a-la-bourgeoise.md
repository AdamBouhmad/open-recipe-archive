## Ingredients

- 1.5 pints of green peas
- fresh butter
- parsley
- lettuce cut in fours
- a little sugar
- yolk of 2 eggs
- a little cream

## Directions

1. Wash 1.5 pints of green peas
2. Put them into a stewpan with fresh butter, parsley, lettuce cut in fours, and a little sugar
3. Let these boil in their own juice over a slow fire
4. When all the liquid is boiled away, add a thickening made of the yolk of 2 eggs and a little cream
5. Let the whole remain a few minutes on the fire
