## Ingredients

- 6 Louisiana oranges
- 1/2 box of Gelatine
- 1/2 gill of Curacao
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Extract the juice of the oranges.
2. Soak the gelatine, if prepared, and set the bowl in boiling water till the gelatine melts.
3. Add the juice of the orange, and add sugar to taste.
4. Add a wineglassful of the best Curacao, and strain the jelly through a coarse piece of swig.
5. Set in a mold to harden.
