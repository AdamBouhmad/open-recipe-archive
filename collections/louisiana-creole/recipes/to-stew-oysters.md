## Ingredients

- 1 peck oysters
- quarter pound butter
- wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put quarter pound of butter to 1 peck of oysters
2. Let the oysters boil until they look plump
3. Then add wine and thickening to your taste
4. When you put them in the tureen grate a little nutmeg on the top
