## Ingredients

- 3 cups dried apples
- 3 cups molasses
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon soda
- spice
- salt

## Directions

1. Soak 3 cups of dried apples over night in cold water
2. Chop them in the morning, and put them on the fire with 3 cups of molasses
3. Stew until soft, but not pulpy
4. When cold, mix with 3 cups of flour, 1 cup of butter, 3 eggs and a teaspoon of soda
5. Season with plenty of spice, not forgetting a little salt
6. Bake in a steady oven
7. This will make 2 good sized loaves
