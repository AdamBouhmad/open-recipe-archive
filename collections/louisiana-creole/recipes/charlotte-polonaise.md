## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint sweet milk
- 2 ounces grated chocolate
- 2 ounces white sugar
- 1 lb. macaroons
- 1/2 pint milk
- 4 ounces blanched almonds
- 1 dozen blanched bitter almonds
- 4 ounces pounded citron
- 4 ounces white sugar
- sponge cake
- wine
- rich custard
- white of egg
- fine sugar
- raspberry or other jelly
- icing

## Directions

1. Boil in 1/2 pint of sweet milk, until perfectly dissolved, 2 ounces of grated chocolate, 2 ounces of white sugar, 1 lb. of macaroons, which must be first broken into small pieces
2. In another 1/2 pint of milk, mix 4 ounces of blanched almonds finely pounded and very sweet, 1 dozen blanched bitter almonds, 4 ounces of pounded citron, and 4 ounces of white sugar
3. Let this mixture also boil a few minutes
4. Set them both away to cool
5. Cut a sponge cake into slices 1/2 an inch thick
6. Wet them well with wine and spread them alternately with the chocolate and almond mixtures
7. Put them one upon another, until the dish is filled
8. Then pour a rich custard over them, and when they are well soaked, cover the whole with a floating island, made with white of egg, whipped to a froth, and sweetened and flavored with fine sugar and raspberry or other jelly
9. Instead of the custard and floating island, you may cover the cake thickly with icing, such as is used for pound cake
10. The polonaise must not be put into the oven that the icing may be dried, but should be prepared an hour or two before it is wanted that the icing may become glossy
