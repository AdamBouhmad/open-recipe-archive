## Ingredients

- a large spoonful of sweet butter
- two onions
- two carrots
- thyme
- two cloves
- two eschalots
- a bunch of parsley
- garlic
- flour
- a cup of beef or veal stock
- half a cup of strong vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put a large spoonful of sweet butter in a stewpan
2. Slice into it two onions, two carrots, a little thyme, two cloves, two eschalots and a bunch of parsley
3. Add a clove of garlic if liked
4. Let them cook until the carrot is soft
5. Shake in a little flour
6. Let it cook five minutes more
7. Add a cup of beef or veal stock, and half a cup of strong vinegar
8. Skim and strain through a sieve
9. Add salt and pepper when boiling
