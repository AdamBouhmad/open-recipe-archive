## Ingredients

- 12 pippin apples
- 1 quart of new milk
- 1 quart of sifted cornmeal
- a little salt
- 4 spoonfuls of chopped suet
- a teacup of good molasses

## Directions

1. Pare and core 12 pippin apples
2. Slice them very thin
3. Stir into 1 quart of new milk, 1 quart of sifted cornmeal
4. Add a little salt, then the apples, 4 spoonfuls of chopped suet and a teacup of good molasses
5. Mix these well together
6. Pour into a buttered dish, and bake 4 hours
7. Serve hot, with sugar and wine sauce
