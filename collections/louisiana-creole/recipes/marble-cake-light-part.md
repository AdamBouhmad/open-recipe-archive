## Ingredients

- 2 cups of white sugar
- 1 cup of butter
- 1 cup of cream, either sweet or sour
- 2 teaspoons of cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoon of soda
- 3 1/2 cups of flour
- whites of 7 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients for light part
2. Alternate layers of light and dark
3. Draw a knife through 2 or 3 times
4. Bake
