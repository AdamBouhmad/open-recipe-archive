## Ingredients

- 1 head of cauliflower
- 1/2 head of cabbage, cut as for slaw
- 1 quart of tiny cucumbers
- 1 quart of small button onions
- 2 or 3 pods of green and red peppers, chopped fine
- 3/4 pound of French mustard
- 1/2 teacupful of celery seed
- 1 teacupful of grated horseradish
- 1 whole garlic, minced very fine
- 1 pint of string beans
- 1 gill of salad oil
- 1 gallon of vinegar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of mustard seed
- 1 cupful of brown sugar
- 1/2 ounce of turmeric
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Take young cauliflower heads, small onions, peppers, cucumbers cut in dice, nasturtiums and string beans in the proportions given above, and boil till tender
2. Drain very dry and pack in wide-mouthed bottles
3. Boil in one pint of cider vinegar, one tablespoonful of sugar, half a teaspoonful of salt and two tablespoonfuls of mustard seed and spices
4. Pour over the pickles, mix well, and seal carefully
