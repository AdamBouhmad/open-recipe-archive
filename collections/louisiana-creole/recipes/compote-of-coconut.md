## Ingredients

- 1 Coconut
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Pound of Fine White Loaf Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Extract

## Directions

1. Divide the coconut into quarters and peel off all the brown skin.
2. Soak the clean, white pieces in cold water, with a little lemon juice and a little salt.
3. Cut them in thin slices first, and then in thick threadlike shreds.
4. Drain the shreds in a clean sieve, and wash them in another water, to free them entirely from oil.
5. Drain well.
6. Boil one pound of the finest white loaf sugar.
7. Remove it from the fire, and throw in the prepared coconut, and set back on the fire.
8. Let it come to a good boil, stirring lightly with a silver fork.
9. Lift them out with the fork, after they have boiled up once, and place the coconut shreds in a sieve.
10. Let the sugar boil up well again.
11. Add the juice of a lemon to the syrup, boil it very thin, and then throw in the coconut shreds, mixing them very lightly in the syrup.
12. Separate the shreds carefully with two silver forks, holding one fork in each hand.
13. Dish up in a raised pile in a dish.
14. When cold, pour over the clear syrup, flavoring with vanilla extract, and serve.
