## Ingredients

- crabs
- rolled cracker
- hot lard
- salt
- favorite sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the crabs properly
2. Dip them into rolled cracker
3. And fry them in hot lard salted
4. They must be dried carefully before frying
5. Or they will not brown well
6. Serve with any favorite sauce
