## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream
- 1/4 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Quart of Apricots or 1 Pint Can

## Directions

1. Prepare the cream as in recipe Ice Cream and let cool for half an hour
2. Pare and mash the apricots, being careful to remove the stones
3. Mash them into the cream, mixing thoroughly for five minutes till well dissolved in the cream
4. Strain through a fine sieve into a freezer, pressing the fruit down with a wooden spoon
5. Proceed to freeze as in the directions for Plain Ice Cream
