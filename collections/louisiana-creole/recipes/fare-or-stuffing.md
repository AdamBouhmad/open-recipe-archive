## Ingredients

- 2 bunches of spinach
- white meat of a roast or boiled chicken
- 1 calf's brain
- 6 raw eggs
- 1 bottle of sweet oil
- salt
- pepper
- a pint of flour
- 2 eggs
- a little water
- grated parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Boil 2 bunches of spinach, chop fine with the white meat of a roast or boiled chicken
2. Add 1 calf's brain, previously boiled and cleaned
3. Stir the whole well in a bowl with 6 raw eggs, 1 bottle of sweet oil, and season with salt and pepper to taste
4. Take a pint of flour, add 2 eggs, with a little water, making the paste as stiff as possible
5. Roll out flat and thin, put in a layer of the stuffing all the length of the paste
6. Double the paste over and cut lengthways with a paste wheel, and also cut crossways, making small squares
7. Put squares of paste in a pot of boiling water, and boil steadily for 1/2 hour
8. Strain and put 1 layer of squares in bottom of deep dish
9. Sprinkle grated parmesan cheese
10. Pour some of the daube gravy with mushrooms, previously made thin
11. Add another layer of squares, cheese and gravy, and so on
12. Serve hot
