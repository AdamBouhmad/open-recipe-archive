## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoonful of French vermouth
- 1 dash of orange bitters
- A dash of syrup
- A bit of lemon peel
- Finely crushed ice

## Directions

1. Mix all the above ingredients well together, stir well and strain into a small punch glass, and serve very cold.
