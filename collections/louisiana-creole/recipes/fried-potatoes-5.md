## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salted water
- boiling fat
- salt

## Directions

1. Pare and cut the potatoes in thin slices
2. Throw them as you cut them into salted water to cool, and make them crisp
3. Put them piece by piece on a dry towel and wipe dry
4. Drop them into boiling fat, enough to float them
5. As they brown dip them out with a skimmer, and salt them a little
