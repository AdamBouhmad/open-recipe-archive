## Ingredients

- 4 pecks of ripe tomatoes
- 2 quarts of vinegar
- 1/2 pound of salt
- 1/2 pound of sugar
- 3/8 ounce of black pepper
- 3/8 ounce of allspice
- 1 ounce of mustard
- 1 ounce of ginger, ground
- 1/3 ounce of cloves, ground
- 1/8 ounce of garlic, minced very fine
- a good dash of cayenne
- 3/8 pint of alcohol

## Directions

1. Set the tomatoes to boil in a porcelain-lined pot, and, after half an hour, press them through a sieve, to remove all the seeds and skin.
2. Then return the liquid obtained to the pot, and let it reduce to three-quarters.
3. Now add the vinegar, and let all reduce to about one and a quarter quarts.
4. Mix thoroughly, and add the spices, garlic, ground mustard, pepper, etc., and let all come to a boil, stirring continually.
5. Then take from the fire, and add the alcohol.
6. Bottle while the mixture is very hot, and seal well.
