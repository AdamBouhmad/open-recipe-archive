## Ingredients

- 1 fine head of cabbage
- 1 pound of corned beef
- 1 red pepper pod
- 1 teaspoonful of chili pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the meat in cold water and put it in a large kettle
2. Cover with cold water
3. Let it simmer gently for two hours
4. Prepare the cabbage according to directions in the above recipe
5. Add the cabbage and let all boil for two hours longer
6. When done, put the cabbage in a dish with the meat in the center
7. Serve with tomato catsup or horseradish or mustard sauce
