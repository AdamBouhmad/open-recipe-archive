## Ingredients

- a pound of beef
- half a pound of veal brisket
- three dozen okra pods
- one onion
- a pod of red pepper

## Directions

1. Chop a pound of beef and half a pound of veal brisket into squares an inch thick;
2. slice three dozen okra pods, one onion, a pod of red pepper, and fry all together.
3. When brown pour in half a gallon of water;
4. add more as it boils away.
5. Serve with rice as usual.
