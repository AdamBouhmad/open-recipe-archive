## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of fresh butter
- 1 medium sized onion
- 1 whole tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of curry powder
- 1 chicken jointed and cut up into small pieces, or 2 lbs. of mutton, lamb or veal without fat
- 1 teacup of water
- pineapple
- cocoanut
- potato
- green peas
- rice

## Directions

1. Put 1 tablespoonful of fresh butter into a saucepan and let it boil
2. Add a medium sized onion, cut into thin slices
3. Fry the onion a deep brown
4. To the fried onion add a whole tablespoonful of butter and 1 of curry powder
5. Stir well and cook for a few moments
6. Add the meat and stir the whole over the fire
7. Add a teacup of water
8. Let the whole simmer over a gentle fire for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally
9. Remove the saucepan to the top of the stove and let the contents simmer for 1½ to 2 hours, must not let it boil
10. Add cocoanut, potato, green peas to taste, only just long enough before the curry is done to insure their being thoroughly cooked
11. Serve rice separately, having been well drained and put into cold water to boil
