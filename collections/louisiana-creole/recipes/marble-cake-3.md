## Ingredients

- whites of four eggs
- one cup of white sugar
- half a cup of butter
- half a cup of sweet milk
- one teaspoonful of cream tartar
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- yolks of four eggs
- one cup of brown sugar
- half a cup of molasses
- half a cup of sour milk
- one teaspoonful of soda
- spices to suit the taste

## Directions

1. Make white part with egg whites, white sugar, butter, sweet milk, cream tartar and soda
2. make black part with yolks, brown sugar, molasses, butter, sour milk, soda and spices
3. put first black, then white dough, until all is in
4. bake
