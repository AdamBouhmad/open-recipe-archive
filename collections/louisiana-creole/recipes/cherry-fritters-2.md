## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Fine Cherries
- 1 Egg
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Madeira or Malaga Wine
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sugar
- The Zest of a Lemon
- Bread slices
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the cherries by taking out the stones.
2. Make them into a thick marmalade, adding a tablespoonful of Madeira or Malaga Wine.
3. Soak nice slices of bread in milk and egg, seasoned well with a tablespoonful of sugar and the grated zest of a lemon.
4. Take the slices, when well-soaked, and spread the marmalade well over them, making it adhere very thickly and closely into the meshes of the bread.
5. Then fry in boiling lard.
6. Sprinkle nicely with powdered sugar, and serve hot.
7. Alternatively, mix the cherries in Creole Fritter Batter, drop by spoonfuls into the boiling lard, fry to a golden brown, drain in the oven, and sprinkle with powdered white sugar and serve hot.
