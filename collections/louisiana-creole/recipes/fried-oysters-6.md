## Ingredients

- 100 oysters
- yolk of 4 eggs
- bread crumbs
- powdered biscuit
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- butter
- corn meal

## Directions

1. Take 100 oysters and dry them in a coarse towel
2. Beat very light the yolk of 4 eggs
3. Add some bread crumbs or powdered biscuit, pepper, salt and a little mace
4. Stir these well together, and dip your oysters into the mixture
5. Fry them in boiling butter till they become a light brown
6. Corn meal may be substituted for the bread or biscuit
