## Ingredients

- 2 dozen large oysters
- their liquor
- a little water
- 1 or 2 spoonfuls of flour
- 2 ounces of fresh butter

## Directions

1. Put into a stewpan, with their liquor, 2 dozen large oysters and a little water
2. when it boils, take out the oysters with a silver spoon, and drain them on a hair sieve
3. let the liquor settle, and pour it off from the sediment
4. put it into a stewpan with 1 or 2 spoonfuls of flour, and 2 ounces of fresh butter
5. let it stand until the flour is a little fried
6. add the liquor, which must be made quite hot
