## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1/2 pint of clotted cream
- a little salt
- 3/4 lb. of butter
- the yolk of egg for gilding

## Directions

1. Put the flour upon a pie-board
2. Make a hole in the middle, put in the clotted cream and a little salt
3. Mix the paste lightly
4. Let it stand for 1 hour
5. Add the butter
6. Roll it out 6 times, the same as puff paste
7. Form it into small cakes
8. Gild them with the yolk of egg
9. Bake in an oven
