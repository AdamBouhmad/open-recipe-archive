## Ingredients

- 1 dozen fine, large crabs
- 1.5 cups of rice
- 3 quarts of broth
- 3 tomatoes
- 2 onions
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 2 sprigs of thyme
- 2 bay leaves
- 1/2 teaspoonful of chili pepper
- salt and black pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Boil the crabs
2. Cut in pieces, cutting the bodies into quarters
3. Proceed in exactly the same manner as in making shrimp jambalaya
