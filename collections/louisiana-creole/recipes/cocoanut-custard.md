## Ingredients

- 1 coconut grated
- 1 pint milk
- 3 eggs
- sugar
- butter the size of a walnut
- rind of 1 lemon
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Grate 1 nut into 1 pint of milk and 3 eggs
2. Add sugar to the taste, butter the size of a walnut, the rind of 1 lemon, and a little nutmeg
