## Ingredients

- Morella cherries
- mace
- cinnamon
- brown sugar (2 large tablespoonfuls to 1 quart of vinegar)

## Directions

1. Remove the stems
2. Prepare the vinegar with mace and cinnamon, with sufficient quantity of brown sugar (say 2 large tablespoonfuls to 1 quart of vinegar)
3. Boil all together for a few minutes
4. Put it over the cherries when cold
5. Put them away in jars, carefully excluding the air
