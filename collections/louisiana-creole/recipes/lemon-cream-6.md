## Ingredients

- 5 large lemons
- 20 spoonfuls spring water
- 6 eggs
- 10 ounces loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Take 5 large lemons; pare them as thin as possible, and steep the parings with the juice of the lemons in 20 spoonfuls of spring water
2. Strain into a silver saucepan through a jelly bag
3. Add the whites well beaten of 6 eggs, and 10 ounces of loaf sugar
4. Set the saucepan over a very slow fire, stirring all the time one way
5. Skim it, and when scalding hot, pour it into glasses
