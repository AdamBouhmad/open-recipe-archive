## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of String Beans
- 1 Pound of Lean Ham or Salt Pork
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Proceed to prepare the beans in exactly the same manner as mentioned above.
2. Throw them into cold water, and let them stand for about ten minutes.
3. Have ready a large saucepan of boiling water in which you will have placed a pound of salt pork or ham.
