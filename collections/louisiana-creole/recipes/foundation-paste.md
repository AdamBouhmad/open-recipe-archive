## Ingredients

- ¾ Pound of Flour
- 1 Ounce of Butter
- ¾ Pint of Water
- ¾ Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Sift the flour well; mix with the salt; then rub the flour and butter lightly together, being careful not to let it harden under the touch.
2. When the butter and flour are well kneaded, add the water gradually, kneading little by little, till well kneaded.
3. Do not work the dough; simply mix lightly and thoroughly.
4. Flour the table lightly, turn the paste out upon it, and press in the center and around the edges, repeating this operation three or four times.
5. Then flatten the paste, lay on a dish and cover with a towel and set in a cool place till ready to use, which should be in about twenty-five minutes, at least.
