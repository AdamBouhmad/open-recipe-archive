## Ingredients

- eight pounds of beef or veal
- one pound of ham or bacon
- eight onions
- pepper, salt, and sweet herbs to taste
- the liquor of a boiled turtle
- wine
- spice
- cayenne
- catsup
- the flesh of the turtle, prepared as in recipe No. 1
- sliced lemon
- whole pepper
- a bunch of parsley
- two leeks sliced
- a quarter of a pound of good sweet butter
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a lemon sliced thin

## Directions

1. Cut the head off the turtle the day before you dress it, and drain the blood thoroughly from the body
2. Then cut it up in the following manner: Divide the back, belly, head and fins from the intestines and lean parts
3. Be careful not to cut the gall bag
4. Scald in boiling water to remove the skin and shell
5. Cut up in neat pieces and throw into cold water
6. Boil the back and belly in a little water long enough to extract the bones easily
7. If for a large company a leg of veal will also be required, and a slice of ham, which must be stewed with the lean parts till well browned
8. Then add boiling water, and the liquor and bones of the boiled turtle
9. Season with sliced lemon, whole pepper, a bunch of parsley, two leeks sliced, and salt to taste
10. Let this all boil slowly for four hours then strain
11. Add the pieces of back, belly, head and fins (take the bones from the fins)
12. Pour in half a pint of Madeira wine and a quarter of a pound of good sweet butter, with a tablespoonful of flour worked in it
13. Also, a lemon sliced thin
14. Let it boil gently for two hours, then serve
