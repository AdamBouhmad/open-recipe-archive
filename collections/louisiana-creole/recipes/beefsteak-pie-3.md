## Ingredients

- butter
- thin paste
- beef
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg
- allspice
- catsup
- onion
- boiled Irish potatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Butter a deep dish, spread a thin paste over the bottom, sides and edges
2. Cut away from the beef all the bone, fat and gristle
3. Cut the beef in thin pieces the size of the palm of your hand
4. Beat it well with a rolling pin
5. Put a layer of the beef, seasoned with pepper, salt, nutmeg, allspice, a little catsup and onion
6. Slice boiled Irish potatoes, and put a layer on the meat until the dish is filled
7. Pour in a little water and butter
8. Bake 1 hour
