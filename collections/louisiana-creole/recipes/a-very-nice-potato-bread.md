## Ingredients

- two pounds or pints of flour
- one pound or pint of warm, mashed mealy Irish potatoes
- milk and water
- a cup of yeast
- a little salt
- a little lard or butter

## Directions

1. To two pounds or pints of flour, add one pound or pint of warm, mashed mealy Irish potatoes
2. Add to this milk and water, a cup of yeast and a little salt
3. Make it after kneading it very well, into loaves, and place them in a pan to rise
4. If you desire a rich, short bread you may add a little lard or butter, but it is nice without
