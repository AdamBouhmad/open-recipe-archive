## Ingredients

- split peas
- water
- parsley
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- celery seed
- butter

## Directions

1. To 2 quarts of split peas, put 2 gallons of water, a handful of parsley, a little cayenne pepper, salt and celery seed, to suit taste
2. Put the peas in soak the night before
3. After boiling pass through a sieve
4. Add a large lump of good butter
