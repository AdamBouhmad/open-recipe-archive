## Ingredients

- 1 dozen large green peppers
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 chopped shallots
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- A dash of sweet paprica
- Salt to taste
- 1 pint of consomme
- Chopped yolks of hard-boiled eggs
- Fancy slices of toast

## Directions

1. Plunge the peppers into hot boiling fat and rub off the skin.
2. Cut them into halves and cut off the stem and take out the seeds.
3. Carefully cut away the ribs.
4. Let the peppers saute for ten minutes in a tablespoonful of butter.
5. Moisten with a pint of consomme; season with the chopped shallots, parsley and sweet paprica.
6. Let all cook slowly for a half hour, or until done.
7. Prepare fancy slices of toast, and serve the peppers on these with a delicate garnish of chopped yolks of hard-boiled eggs.
