## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of Bread
- 1 Pint of Hot Cream or Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter

## Directions

1. Toast the bread nicely, and butter well on both sides.
2. Lay in a dish, and pour over hot milk.
3. Serve hot.
