## Ingredients

- ½ lb. of flour
- ½ lb. of loaf sugar
- yolks of eggs

## Directions

1. Sift ½ lb. of flour and ½ lb. of loaf sugar together
2. mix them well with a knife
3. stir in as many yolks of eggs as will make it into a stiff paste
4. this must be well stirred
5. roll it on a flour board, and cut into shape of leaves with a cutter
6. bake a light brown, as other paste
