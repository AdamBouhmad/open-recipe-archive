## Ingredients

- 1 pint of molasses
- piece of butter the size of an egg
- 1 tablespoonful of white ginger
- 1 teaspoonful of soda dissolved in milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Warm 1 pint of molasses
2. Stir in while warm a piece of butter the size of an egg, 1 tablespoonful of white ginger, 1 teaspoonful of soda dissolved in milk
3. Strain this into the mixture
4. When cool, sift in as much flour as will make it stiff
5. Roll it out in cake and bake on tins
