## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Buttermilk
- 3 Cups of Sugar
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 1 Cup of Butter
- 4 Eggs
- ¾ Teaspoonful of Soda

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream, and then add the sugar, which has been sifted fine.
2. Beat to a cream.
3. Add the yolks of the eggs, and continue beating till very light.
4. Mash the soda in the buttermilk, dissolving thoroughly, first in a half cupful, and then mixing well with the remainder of the milk.
5. Add this to the eggs and butter.
6. Then gradually add the sifted flour, and beat till very smooth.
7. Lastly add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
8. Bake for forty-five minutes in a moderate oven.
