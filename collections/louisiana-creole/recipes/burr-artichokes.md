## Ingredients

- burr artichokes
- salted water
- salt
- drawn-butter sauce
- vinegar
- mustard
- oil

## Directions

1. Get them young
2. Wash them in salted water
3. Put them to boil
4. Boil until you can pull off a leaf easily
5. Salt them and serve with drawn-butter sauce, with vinegar in it, or mustard and oil, as preferred
