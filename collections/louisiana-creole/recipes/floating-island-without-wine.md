## Ingredients

- whites of five eggs
- a little currant jelly
- one pint of cream
- a teaspoonful of extract
- one pint of sweet milk
- yolks of two eggs
- half a cup of white sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of five eggs with a little currant jelly until they are quite thick
2. Sweeten a pint of cream, add a teaspoonful of extract, pour it in the bowl
3. Drop your whites of eggs and jelly by spoonfuls on the cream
4. If you can not procure cream, you may make a substitute of a custard, made of a pint of sweet milk, yolks of two eggs, and half a cup of white sugar
