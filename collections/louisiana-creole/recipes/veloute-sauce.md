## Ingredients

- 5 Ounces Butter, or 1 Tablespoonful and a Half
- 1 Tablespoonful and a Half of Flour
- 2 Gills of Water
- The Well-Beaten Yolks of 2 Eggs
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Juice of a Lemon
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley

## Directions

1. Blend the flour and butter as in White Sauce, only letting it become slightly yellow
2. Add by degrees the boiling water, and season to taste
3. Add a tablespoonful of white wine if desired
4. Add the juice of half a lemon, and a tablespoonful of chopped parsley
5. Let it simmer for about ten minutes
6. Take from the fire and add the well-beaten yolks of two eggs
7. Serve immediately with any boiled fish or meats
