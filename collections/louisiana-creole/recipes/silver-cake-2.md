## Ingredients

- two coffee-cups of butter
- two pints of fine white sugar
- beaten whites of eighteen eggs
- four pints of flour
- one pint maizena or corn starch flour
- two coffee-cups of water
- almond flavoring
- two teaspoonfuls of yeast powder

## Directions

1. Cream two coffee-cups of butter with two pints of fine white sugar
2. add the beaten whites of eighteen eggs, and four pints of flour
3. thin this mixture gradually as you beat in the flour and eggs, by pouring in two coffee-cups of water
4. Flavor with almond
5. sift the flour and add two teaspoonfuls of yeast powder
6. bake in a large pan
