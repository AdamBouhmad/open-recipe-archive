## Ingredients

- 1 quart of flour
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 lb. of suet chopped fine
- 1 lb. of brown sugar
- 8 eggs
- 1 lb. of stoned raisins
- ½ lb. of currants
- ¼ lb. of citron
- sugar
- butter
- wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Combine 1 quart of flour, 1 pint of milk, 1 lb. of suet chopped fine, 1 lb. of brown sugar, 8 eggs, 1 lb. of stoned raisins, ½ lb. of currants, ¼ lb. of citron
2. Make a sauce of sugar and butter creamed together, with wine and nutmeg
3. Boil it 6 hours
