## Ingredients

- 4 Carrots of Good Size
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful Flour
- 3/4 Pint of Milk
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots and throw them into cold water for about a quarter of an hour.
2. Then put them in a saucepan, and cover with boiling water.
3. Add the salt, and let them cook for an hour and a half, boiling steadily.
4. After this time expires, take them out and drain off all water.
5. Then cut neatly into one-half inch dice pieces, or slice thin.
6. Put a tablespoonful of butter in the frying pan, and add the flour, blending well, but without browning.
7. Then add the milk, and let it simmer to a rich cream sauce.
8. Add the carrots to this, and let them simmer gently for about twenty minutes.
9. Then add a little chopped parsley and sugar, and serve hot.
