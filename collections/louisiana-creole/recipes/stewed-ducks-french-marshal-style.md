## Ingredients

- 1 Pair of Fine Ducks, French or Canvas-Back
- 12 Godiveau Quenelles
- 12 Mushrooms
- 2 Onions Chopped Fine
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 3 Sprigs Each of Thyme and Parsley
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Square Inch of Ham
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1 Glass of Madeira or Sherry Wine
- Croutons to Garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare the Ducks exactly as in the recipe for Stewed Ducks With Turnips, omitting the turnips.
2. Add ten minutes before serving, twelve small Godiveau Quenelles and the wine.
3. Garnish the dish with Croutons and twelve nicely cooked mushrooms, cut in two.
4. Send to the table hot.
