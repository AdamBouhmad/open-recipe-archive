## Ingredients

- 1 Two-Pound Diamond-Back Terrapin
- 2 Fine Large Onions
- 6 Cloves
- 1 Square Inch of Ham
- 6 Allspice
- 1/2 of a Small Lemon
- 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 1 Glass of Sherry Wine
- Parsley
- Thyme
- Bay Leaf
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Taste
- a grated nutmeg
- a glass of Sherry or Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Clean the terrapin as you would a turtle
2. Place in a kettle and boil till tender
3. Take out and cut into small pieces, saving the water
4. Proceed as for Turtle Soup
5. When it boils up take from the fire, add a grated nutmeg, a glass of Sherry or Madeira wine and serve
6. Make farci or forcemeat balls in exactly the same manner as for Turtle Soup
7. Serve with green pickle and delicate slices of fried toast
