## Ingredients

- One lump sugar
- two dashes of Boker's bitters or Angostura bitters
- two tablespoonfuls of Belfast ginger ale
- one wineglassful of whiskey or brandy
- one lemon peel
- plenty of ice

## Directions

1. Shake well
2. strain in fancy glass
