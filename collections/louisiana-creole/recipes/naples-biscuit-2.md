## Ingredients

- four eggs
- half a pound of fine white sugar
- half a pound of sifted flour
- essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs light
2. add half a pound of fine white sugar, and half a pound of sifted flour
3. Flavor with essence of lemon
