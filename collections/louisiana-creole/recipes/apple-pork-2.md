## Ingredients

- fillet of pork
- salt
- pepper
- stewed apples
- nutmeg
- sugar
- rind of a lemon
- 2 dozen apples
- lemon juice
- water

## Directions

1. Rub the fillet of pork with a little salt and pepper
2. Score the outside skin
3. Take out the bone and fill it with nice stewed apples, free of paring and cores, nicely seasoned with nutmeg and sugar, with bits of the rind of a lemon
4. Have ready 2 dozen apples, pared, cored and cut in slices or quarters, sweetened well with sugar, and flavored with lemon
5. Add a little of the lemon juice for improvement
6. Put the pork into a large pot or iron bake oven
7. Fill up with the cut apples the space all around, adding just sufficient water to keep it from burning
8. Stew or bake it for 3 hours
9. When done, serve it on a clean dish and send it very hot to the table
