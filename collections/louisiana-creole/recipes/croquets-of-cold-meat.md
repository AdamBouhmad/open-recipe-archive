## Ingredients

- any kind of boiled or roasted meat
- a piquante sauce
- bread
- capers
- mushrooms
- salt
- spices
- fine herbs
- egg
- grated bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Take any kind of boiled or roasted meat and mix them together, so that it is clear of bones
2. Make a hash of it, and mix it with a piquante sauce
3. The mixture must be very thick; if it be too thin, put a little bread with it
4. Add capers, mushrooms, salt, spices and fine herbs, boiled together
5. Let it get cold on a chopping board or dish
6. Make balls of it and press them flat
7. Roll them in egg and grated bread, and fry them in butter, brown on both sides
