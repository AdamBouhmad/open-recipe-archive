## Ingredients

- chicken
- egg
- yolks of 2 eggs
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- cupful of Parmesan cheese

## Directions

1. Boil a chicken until the meat separates from the bones
2. Pound the meat
3. Add 1 egg whole, and the yolks of 2 others, a little cinnamon, pepper, salt, and a cupful of Parmesan cheese, finely grated
4. Mix the ingredients well
5. Stuff the macaroni
6. Put it to boil in the broth or water in which the chicken was boiled
