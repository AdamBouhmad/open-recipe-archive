## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Young Partridges
- 6 Thin Slices of Bacon
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Sprigs of Thyme
- 2 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Toasted Bread
- Parsley or Watercress to Garnish

## Directions

1. Clean, singe and draw and wipe the birds neatly.
2. Rub each bird well with salt and pepper, and then with melted butter.
3. If it can be afforded, stuff the bird with truffles, placing one or two in each bird.
4. Place within the bird a pinch of salt and pepper, a lump of butter about the size of a peanut, and a pinch of chopped thyme, parsley and bay leaf, all minced very fine.
5. Bind the birds with thin strips of bacon, and fasten each strip with a skewer.
6. Put the Partridges in a baking pan in a brisk oven, and add two tablespoonfuls of butter and one of water.
7. Let them bake from twenty-five to thirty minutes, according to size.
