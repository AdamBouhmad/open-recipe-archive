## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Butter
- 1 Teacup of Milk
- 2½ Teacups of Flour
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Pound of Raisins
- 1 Pound of Figs
- 1 Pound of Powdered Almonds
- The Whites of 7 Eggs

## Directions

1. Take one teacupful of sugar and one of butter and beat to a cream
2. Add a teacupful of milk and two and a half teaspoonfuls of flour sifted, with two heaping teaspoonfuls of baking powder
3. Add one pound of raisins, well-seeded, and one pound each of shredded figs and pounded almonds
4. After mixing all these well, add the whites of seven eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
5. Bake in a slow oven
6. When done, make a light syrup and spread over the top, and garnish with pieces of finely-cut preserved citron, oranges, preserved, currants and other candies
