## Ingredients

- 3 Pounds of Corned Beef
- 2 Carrots
- 2 Turnips
- 1 Stalk of Celery
- 2 Onions
- 1 Clove of Garlic

## Directions

1. Put the corn beef on to boil in a large pot of cold water
2. The pot should be well covered
3. When it begins boiling well, set it back to cook gently, and allow twenty-five minutes to each pound of beef
4. When within two hours of being cooked, add two carrots, two turnips, a stalk of celery, two onions, one clove of garlic, chopped fine, and let these boil with the beef
5. Serve with the vegetables ranged whole around the dish
