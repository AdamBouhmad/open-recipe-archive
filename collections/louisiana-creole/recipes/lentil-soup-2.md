## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Lentils
- 2 Quarts of Water
- 1 Onion
- 1 Small Bunch of Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter or Lard
- 1 Pod of Red Pepper, Without the Seeds
- 1 Stalk of Celery, Chopped Fine
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Wash the lentils, and, if dried, soak them over night
2. Drain off the water and put them in a saucepan with the cold water
3. Allow them to come gradually to a boil
4. Set them back on the stove and let them simmer gently for about two hours
5. Melt the butter in the saucepan and fry in it the minced onion, celery, parsley, thyme and bay leaf, and let these brown
6. Add them to the lentils
7. Boil about an hour longer, and, if particularly tender, press all through a colander
8. Return to the fire and add the seasonings
9. Let them boil up once and serve with Croutons
