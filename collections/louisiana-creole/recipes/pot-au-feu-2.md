## Ingredients

- 1 lb. beef
- 1 cup of water
- a carrot
- a turnip
- other greens
- salt

## Directions

1. Put beef, water, carrot, turnip and other greens in at the same time
2. Let simmer slowly
3. Add salt to suit taste
