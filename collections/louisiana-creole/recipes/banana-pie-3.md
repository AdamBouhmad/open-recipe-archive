## Ingredients

- 4 Large Bananas
- ¾ Cup of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Sweet Spices, Blended Equally
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Water

## Directions

1. Peel, slice and stew the bananas and mash through a sieve
2. Prepare a Pie Paste and proceed as in Apple Pie
