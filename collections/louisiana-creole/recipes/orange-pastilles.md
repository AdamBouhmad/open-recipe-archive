## Ingredients

- The Strained Juice of 2 Oranges
- 1/4 Ounce of Orange Sugar
- 3 1/4 Ounces of Coarse Sugar

## Directions

1. To make the orange sugar, rub the rind of the oranges on the sugar and dry
2. Then proceed to add the water and three and a quarter ounces of sugar and flavoring
3. As soon as it begins to dissolve in the pan, take a small wooden spoon and stir it for a few minutes
4. Then remove it from the fire, stirring continuously, and drop the pastilles upon a sheet of paper, cutting them off in drops about the size of a pea with a curved piece of wire or a hairpin
5. When they are quite dry, turn the paper upside down, brush the reverse side with a paste brush slightly moistened with water, and shake off the pastilles with the point of a knife upon a dry sieve
6. Then move the sieve to and fro over a very slow fire, so that all the moisture may be removed from the drops, and keep them in well-bottled jars
