## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of almonds
- a small tablespoonful of fresh butter
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 3 gills of water

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/2 lb. of almonds, and fry them in a small tablespoonful of fresh butter, until they are of a light brown
2. Wipe with a towel and put them into a bowl or pan
3. Make a syrup with 1 lb. of sugar and 3 gills of water; boil it to a thread
4. Care must be taken to boil it to the exact candying point
5. Pour it boiling upon the almonds, and stir them until the sugar hardens around them
