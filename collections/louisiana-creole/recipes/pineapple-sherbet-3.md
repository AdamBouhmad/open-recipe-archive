## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Grated Pineapple
- 1 Quart of Water
- 1 Pint of Sugar
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Peel and slice and pound the pineapple to a pulp.
2. Then rub through a strainer.
3. Put the sugar and water to boil for five minutes.
4. Then set away to cool.
5. Extract the juice from the lemon and mix this with the syrup.
6. Add the pineapple and juice, and then strain all through a thin muslin cloth.
7. Turn into the freezer and proceed as in ice cream.
