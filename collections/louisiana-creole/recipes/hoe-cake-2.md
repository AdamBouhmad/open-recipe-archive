## Ingredients

- 3 spoonfuls of hominy
- 2 spoonfuls of rice flour
- a little butter
- milk sufficient to make it soft

## Directions

1. Combine the hominy, rice flour, butter, and milk to make a soft dough
2. Bake on a griddle or on a hoe
