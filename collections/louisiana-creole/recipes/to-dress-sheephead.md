## Ingredients

- sheephead 18 or 20 inches long
- 1/2 pint of tomato catsup
- a large spoonful of butter
- 1 pint of water
- salt
- black pepper
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Take a sheephead 18 or 20 inches long and put it into a pan
2. Place that in a dutch oven
3. Add 1/2 pint of tomato catsup, a large spoonful of butter, 1 pint of water, salt, black and red pepper to suit the taste
4. Cook it over the fire and serve it up with the dressing
