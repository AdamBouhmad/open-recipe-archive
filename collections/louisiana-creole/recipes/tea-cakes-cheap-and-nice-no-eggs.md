## Ingredients

- one cup of butter or a large spoonful of lard
- two cups of sugar
- one cup of sour milk
- one teaspoonful of soda
- grated orange peel or nutmeg
- flour enough to roll out
- a pinch of salt (if using lard)

## Directions

1. Roll very thin
2. cut with fancy cutters
3. bake in a quick oven
