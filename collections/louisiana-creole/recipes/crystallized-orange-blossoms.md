## Ingredients

- Orange Blossoms
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Pick the petals one by one from the flowers, and put them into water
2. Drain this off, and then squeeze the flowers with your hands to bruise them, and drain on a sieve
3. Make a fine syrup, equal in bulk to the flowers, clarify it and add the flowers
4. Let the syrup boil up about seven times
5. Remove it from the fire and let it stand till the sugar forms a coating all around the petals
6. Drain them, separate them on a paper for an hour or so, till perfectly dry
7. Put in boxes lined with white paper, and keep in a cool, dry place
