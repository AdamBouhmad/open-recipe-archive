## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Lemon Jelly
- 1 Cup of Stoned Cherries
- 1 Cup of Malaga Grapes
- 2 Oranges
- 2 Bananas
- 1/2 a Pineapple
- 1 Pint of Snow Cream

## Directions

1. Make a Lemon Jelly, add stoned cherries, a few Malaga grapes, and the meat of two oranges, two bananas and half a pineapple, sliced.
2. Pour a little jelly into each custard glass.
3. Put a layer first of one fruit, then of another, then a layer of snow cream, and continue alternating till the glass is filled with the snow on top.
4. Serve ice cold.
