## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- half a box of gelatine
- one cup of sugar
- six ounces of grated chocolate
- cream or wine sauce for serving

## Directions

1. Make in the same manner as gelatine blanc mange
2. Stir six ounces of grated chocolate in the boiling milk for seasoning
3. Eat with cream or wine sauce
