## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Bananas
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Quart of Water
- The Juice of 2 Oranges

## Directions

1. Peel and mash the bananas
2. Boil the sugar and water together for five minutes
3. Take from the fire, and let cool
4. Add the bananas and the orange juice, and press all through a sieve
5. Set to freeze as directed
