## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 3 tablespoonfuls rennet wine

## Directions

1. Make 1 quart of milk lukewarm
2. Add 3 tablespoonfuls of rennet wine
3. After having seasoned the milk to the taste, set it by a short time, when it will be fit for use
