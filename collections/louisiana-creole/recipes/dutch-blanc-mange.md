## Ingredients

- 1 ounce of isinglass
- 1 quart of water
- yolks of 8 eggs
- wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 ounce of isinglass in a quart of water
2. Let it boil down to 1 pint
3. Add to this the yolks of 8 eggs, well beaten
4. Season highly with wine and sugar to the taste
5. Put it in a shape
6. Turn it out when cold
