## Ingredients

- 6 Large Potatoes
- A Handful of Hops
- 3 Pints of Water
- 1/2 Cup of Sugar
- 1/2 Cup of Yeast
- Flour Sufficient to Make a Batter
- Cornmeal to Blend

## Directions

1. Take six large potatoes, and pare them, and then boil in about three pints of water.
2. Take a handful of hops, and tie them in a muslin bag, and boil with the potatoes.
3. When these are thoroughly cooked, drain the water on sufficient flour to make a good batter, and set the mixture on the stove for a few minutes, till the flour is well-scalded.
4. Do not let it boil or simmer under any consideration.
5. Take it from the fire, and let it cool.
6. Then mash the potatoes and add them to the flour, and add a half cup of sugar and a half cup of yeast.
7. Let it stand in a warm place till it has thoroughly risen.
8. Then add cornmeal that has been sifted and dried, and knead well, until you have a dough thick enough to roll out, and that will crumble when dry.
9. Cut this dough into cakes, and spread on a board and place in the shade to dry.
10. Then keep in a box in a dry place.
