## Ingredients

- 1½ pints of rich cream
- 4 tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- powdered nutmeg
- a dozen bitter almonds (slightly broken up) or a dozen peach leaves

## Directions

1. Boil 1½ pints of rich cream with 4 tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar, some powdered nutmeg, and a dozen bitter almonds slightly broken up, or a dozen peach leaves
2. When boiled, take it off the fire and strain it
3. If it is to be eaten with boiled pudding or with dumplings, send it to table hot
4. Let it get cold if you intend it as an accompaniment to fruit pies or tarts
