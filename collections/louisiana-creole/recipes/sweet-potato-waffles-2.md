## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Mashed Sweet Potatoes
- 1 Cup of Melted Butter
- ¼ Cup of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 2 Eggs
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Flour

## Directions

1. Mash the potatoes through a sieve, and add the eggs, beaten separately.
2. Mix well, and add the sugar, and beat till very light.
3. Then add the butter and beat again, and add the milk, mixing thoroughly.
4. Now add the flour, using sufficient to make a thin batter, that will bind the potatoes.
5. Grease the waffle irons well, and proceed to bake as above indicated.
