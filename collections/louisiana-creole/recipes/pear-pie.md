## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Pears
- 1/4 Cup of Sugar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Water
- A Plain Paste

## Directions

1. Peel and core and slice six fine pears.
2. Put them in a vessel with the sugar and water, and stew.
3. Prepare a plain paste, and proceed in exactly the same manner as for Apple Pie.
