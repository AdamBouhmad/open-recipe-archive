## Ingredients

- perch
- 1.5 ounces of butter
- flour
- 2 or 3 small onions
- small bunch of sweet herbs
- 1/2 pint of wine

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the fish
2. Place them in a stewpan with the butter rolled in flour, the sweet herbs minced fine, and the small onions
3. Pour over them the white wine and let them simmer until cooked enough
4. Garnish sides of dish with thin slices of lemon
