## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 8 ounces of granulated sugar
- 1 pint of sweet milk
- 3 pints of double sweet cream
- vanilla bean or 1 tablespoonful of Burnett's extract

## Directions

1. Break in a bowl the yolks of the eggs
2. Stir in them the 8 ounces of sugar until they bubble, do not beat
3. Put the milk to boil in a bain Marie
4. When it comes to a boil, stir in the eggs and sugar
5. Do not let it boil after you put the eggs in, only simmer to let it thicken well
6. Strain this custard through a piece of Swiss muslin and set aside to get cold
7. Have 3 pints of sweet cream packed in ice and salt to get very cold
8. Whip it well until it is quite thick
9. Gradually whip the cream into the cold custard
10. If vanilla bean is used, boil in the milk; if the extract, stir in the custard when cold
11. Freeze this until it is quite stiff and then put in moulds and pack in ice
12. If desired, add crystallized fruit, cut into quarters, and after the cream is partly frozen, stir the fruit in and pack in mould
