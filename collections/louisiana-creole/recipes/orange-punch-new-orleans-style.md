## Ingredients

- 6 Louisiana Oranges
- 1 Glass of Rum
- ¾ Glass of Best Brandy
- 2 Quarts of Boiling Water
- 1 Pound of Loaf Sugar
- 1 Small Glass of Cherry Bounce
- 1 Large Piece of Ice

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and water, and the rum and brandy
2. Add the juice of six oranges, and the grated peel of three
3. Let all infuse for one hour
4. Then set to cool
5. When ready to serve add one small glassful of Cherry Bounce
6. Serve in small glass cups
