## Ingredients

- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 6 Stoned Olives
- 12 Mushrooms
- 1 Glass of Sherry Wine
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- A Dash of Cayenne
- 1 pint of consommé

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, then remove from the fire and add the flour
2. Blend with a wooden spoon till smooth
3. Moisten with one pint of consommé till it reaches the consistency of cream
4. Then add the chopped mushrooms, stoned olives, pepper, salt and Cayenne
5. Before serving add the wine
6. Serve hot
