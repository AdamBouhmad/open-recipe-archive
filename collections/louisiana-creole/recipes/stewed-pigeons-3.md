## Ingredients

- pigeons
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- mace
- sweet herbs
- butter
- 1 quart of good gravy
- white wine
- picked mushrooms
- pepper corns
- 1 slice of onion
- pickled oysters
- yolk of 1 egg
- lemons

## Directions

1. Season the pigeons with salt, pepper, cloves, mace and sweet herbs
2. Wrap this seasoning up in a piece of butter and put in and stuff them
3. Tie up the neck and vent
4. Half roast them
5. Put them in the saucepan with 1 quart of good gravy, a little white wine, some picked mushrooms, pepper corns, a slice of onion, and some pickled oysters
6. Stew all together till sufficiently done
7. Thicken it with butter and the yolk of 1 egg
8. Garnish with lemons
