## Ingredients

- white or brown figs (a little more than half ripe)
- sugar (3/4 lbs. per lb. of fruit)
- lemon peel
- ginger

## Directions

1. Pick the white or brown figs when a little more than half ripe
2. Peel them very thin
3. To 1 lb. of fruit put 3/4 lbs. of sugar
4. Make a syrup, and put the figs into it with lemon peel and ginger
5. Let them boil till clear, stirring frequently
