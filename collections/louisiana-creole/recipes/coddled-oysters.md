## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 oysters to each person
- 6 slices of bread
- 1 large tablespoonful of butter
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of black pepper
- A dash of Cayenne
- 2 sprigs of parsley chopped very fine
- 1 bay leaf minced fine
- 3 cloves
- 1 blade of mace
- 1 pint of oyster liquor

## Directions

1. Toast five or six slices of bread to a nice brown and butter them on both sides.
2. Drain the liquor from the oysters and put it in a saucepan.
3. When hot, add a large lump of butter.
4. Have ready a baking dish and place the toast within.
5. Lay the oysters on the toast, having seasoned well with salt, Cayenne pepper, chopped parsley, bay leaf, mace and cloves.
6. Put the liquor of the oysters over the toast until it is well absorbed.
7. Set in an oven and bake for five or six minutes with a quick fire.
