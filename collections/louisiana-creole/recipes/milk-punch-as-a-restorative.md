## Ingredients

- chopped ice
- a large tablespoonful of white sugar
- a wineglass of gin, rum or brandy
- fresh milk

## Directions

1. half fill a large tumbler (it should hold a pint) with chopped ice
2. add to it a large tablespoonful of white sugar
3. beat it a little with the ice
4. pour on it a wineglass of gin, rum or brandy
5. fill up with fresh milk
