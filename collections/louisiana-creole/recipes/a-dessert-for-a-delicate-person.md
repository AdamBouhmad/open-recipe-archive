## Ingredients

- one cup of rice
- a teacup of rich sweet cream
- half a teacup of any acid jelly—currant is the best but plum, strawberry or lemon will do
- sweetened cream

## Directions

1. Boil one cup of rice until perfectly soft, then add a teacup of rich sweet cream, and half a teacup of any acid jelly.
2. Put it over the fire a few minutes, turn it into a mould.
3. Eat with sweetened cream.
