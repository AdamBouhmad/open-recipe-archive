## Ingredients

- 2 Ounces of Bruised Ginger Root
- 2 Ounces of Cream of Tartar
- 4 Quarts of Boiling Water
- The Juice and Rind of 1 Lemon
- 1½ Pounds of Granulated Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Yeast, or ½ of a Cake

## Directions

1. Bruise and pound the ginger well, and put it into a bowl with the boiling water
2. Add the juice of a lemon and the well-grated zest
3. Add the Cream of Tartar and the sugar, mixing and stirring constantly
4. When it is lukewarm add the yeast, dissolved in a little water
5. Mix well and cover tightly, and let it stand for six hours
6. Then strain it and put it into bottles with patent tops and keep in a cool place
