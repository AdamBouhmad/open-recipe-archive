## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Fine Louisiana Oranges
- % Pound of Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges and cut in slices
2. Sprinkle with sugar, to form a syrup
3. Let them stand for several hours in a cool place
4. Serve either as a preliminary to breakfast or as a dessert
