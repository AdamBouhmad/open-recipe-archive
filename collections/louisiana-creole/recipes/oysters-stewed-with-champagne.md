## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of butter
- a quart of oysters
- grated bread which has been toasted, beaten and sifted
- some cut parsley
- a little pepper and salt
- bits of butter cut thin
- a pint of champagne

## Directions

1. Put into a silver chafing dish a quarter of a pound of butter
2. Lay in a quart of oysters
3. Strew over them grated bread which has been toasted, beaten and sifted
4. Some cut parsley and a little pepper and salt
5. Cover the top with bits of butter cut thin
6. Pour on a pint of champagne
7. Cover and cook
8. This may be done in a pan or oven
