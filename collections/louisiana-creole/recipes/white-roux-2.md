## Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful Flour

## Directions

1. Put the butter and flour simultaneously into the saucepan
2. Do not allow to brown
3. Moisten with a little broth or boiling water
4. Allow to boil a few minutes till thick
