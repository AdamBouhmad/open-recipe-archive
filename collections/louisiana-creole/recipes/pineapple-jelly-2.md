## Ingredients

- Pineapple, pared
- 3/4 of a pound of white sugar to every pound of grated pineapple

## Directions

1. Pare the pineapple, take out the eyes and grate carefully, or pick into fine pieces.
2. To every pound of pineapple allow three-quarters of a pound of white sugar.
3. Put the pineapple and the sugar to boil in a porcelain-lined kettle and cook over a moderate fire for ten or fifteen minutes.
4. Pour into jelly glasses or jars, cover tightly, and set in a cool place.
