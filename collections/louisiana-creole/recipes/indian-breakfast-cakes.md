## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- corn-meal
- salt
- saleratus
- two well-beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Take a quart of milk scalding hot
2. stir into it as much corn-meal as will make a thick batter
3. add of salt and saleratus in fine powder, each a teaspoonful
4. when a little cooled, add two well-beaten eggs
5. bake in buttered pans, in a quick oven
