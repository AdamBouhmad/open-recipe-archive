## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of tripe
- 3 onions
- 3 carrots
- 1 dozen whole bay leaves
- 1 dozen whole cloves
- 1 dozen whole allspice
- 3 cloves of garlic (whole)
- 1 ounce of thyme (whole)
- 2 dozen pieces of bacon 2 inches square
- 1/2 bottle of white wine
- 1 cup of broth or water
- butter
- salt, Cayenne and Chili pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Take three pounds of tripe and cut the tripe into pieces of about two inches square
2. Slice three onions and three large carrots very fine
3. Take one dozen whole bay leaves, one ounce of thyme whole, one dozen whole cloves, and the same number of allspice, three whole cloves of garlic, two dozen pieces of very thin bacon cut into pieces of two inches square
4. Have ready a two-gallon earthen jar that can stand baking in an oven
5. Put in the bottom of the jar a thin layer of butter
6. Place on top a thin layer of bacon, then a thin layer of onions, carrots, bay leaves, thyme, garlic, spices, dividing into two equal portions the whole amount
7. Sprinkle over the whole salt, Cayenne and Chili pepper
8. On top of this lay one-half of the tripe
9. Over the tripe place a layer of bacon; then vegetables, seasonings, etc
10. Over this place another layer of tripe, and remnants of thyme, bay leaf, vegetables, bacon, etc., as below, this being the last layer
11. Pour over all a half bottle of White Wine and one cup of broth or water
12. Cover the jar closely with a layer of Pie Paste
13. Set in a very moderate oven, and let it cook slowly for at least five hours of constant, steady cooking
