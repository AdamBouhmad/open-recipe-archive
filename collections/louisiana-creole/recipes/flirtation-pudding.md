## Ingredients

- custard ingredients for a rich boiled custard
- whites of 2 eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a rich boiled custard, and when cold, beat to a froth the whites of 2 eggs
2. Drop it in spoonfuls on the top of the custard
3. Set it before the fire to brown the whites slightly
