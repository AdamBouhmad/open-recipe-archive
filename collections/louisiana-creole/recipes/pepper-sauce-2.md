## Ingredients

- 1 carrot, minced fine
- 2 sprigs each of thyme and parsley
- 1 onion, minced fine
- 1 pint of consommé
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 wineglass of sherry or Madeira
- 1/2 grated lemon
- 1 small piece of celery
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- A dash of cayenne

## Directions

1. Put the butter in the saucepan, and as it melts, add the flour
2. Let it brown slowly, and then add one pint of consommé
3. Let it boil, and add the minced herbs and vegetables and the zest or outer skin of half a grated lemon
4. Let all boil slowly for an hour and a half
5. Add a wineglassful of sherry or Madeira and season with salt and black pepper (hot) and a dash of cayenne
6. Let it boil for ten minutes longer, take off the stove and strain, and serve with any game
