## Ingredients

- Cod
- A little browned butter
- A little flour
- Sugar
- One onion
- Tablespoonful of vinegar

## Directions

1. For cod au beurre roux, boil a piece of cod and separate it into flakes; brown some butter, dredge in a little flour, and a little sugar in powder, and in this fry some slices of onion a fine brown;
2. Throw in the vinegar, boil it up, pour over the fish, and serve it with crisp parsley.
