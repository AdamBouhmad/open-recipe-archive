## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pound of Macaroni or Spaghetti
- 1 Head of Edam's Cheese
- 3/4 Pound of Grated Parmesan Cheese
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Boil a little over one-quarter of a pound of macaroni, or sufficient to fill the head, according to the directions for Boiled Macaroni.
2. When boiled, take out of the water and set in a dish.
3. Take about half a pint of the liquor in which the macaroni was boiled, strain it and set it back on the stove.
4. Add the flour and butter blended, and three-quarters of the grated cheese, and let it boil five minutes.
5. Pour this sauce over the macaroni in the dish and mix well, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Fill the head with this mixture, and sprinkle on top the rest of the grated cheese.
7. Set in the stove to bake, and when nicely browned, serve hot from the shell of the cheese.
