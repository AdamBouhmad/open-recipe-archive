## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- lard
- beefsteak gravy

## Directions

1. Trim and peel them, and boil in water until quite tender, then dish them
2. Season with salt, pepper and butter
3. Or cut in slices and fry a light brown
4. Wash in two or three waters after being sliced, then put into hot lard and some of the beefsteak gravy, fry gently until a light color, and serve around the steak
