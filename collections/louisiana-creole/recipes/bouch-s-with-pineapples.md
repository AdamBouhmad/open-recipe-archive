## Ingredients

- 9 Eggs
- 1 Pound of Pulverized Sugar
- 3/4 Pound of Flour
- 1 Cup of Preserved Pineapples
- Transparent Icing to Garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare the drop cakes one inch and a half in diameter
2. Place smaller slices of thin, preserved pineapple upon each
3. Gloss or glacer over with Transparent Icing, flavoring with pineapple syrup
