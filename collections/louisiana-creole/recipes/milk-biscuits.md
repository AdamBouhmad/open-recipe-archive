## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of sifted flour
- 1 lb. of good butter
- 1 pint of new milk, warmed
- salt
- 1 wineglass of good yeast
- 3 eggs beaten
- grated nutmeg
- 1 lb. of flour

## Directions

1. Stir this into the flour, mix well, and add a wineglass of good yeast, 3 eggs beaten, and grated nutmeg
2. Set it to rise
3. When risen, sift on the board 1 lb. of flour
4. Mix all together, make it into cakes, and bake quickly
