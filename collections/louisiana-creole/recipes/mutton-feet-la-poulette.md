## Ingredients

- Mutton feet
- Half of a lemon (including peel and meat)
- Salt
- Cream Sauce
- Juice of one lemon or half a tablespoonful of good vinegar
- A gill of water
- Yolks of two eggs
- Half can of mushrooms (optional)

## Directions

1. Cover mutton feet well with boiling water, add half of a lemon (including peel and meat) to the water, and salt well.
2. Prepare a Cream Sauce and add the juice of one lemon, or half a tablespoonful of good vinegar.
3. Take the mutton feet out of the water in which they have been boiled and take out the big bones from the feet.
4. Put the mutton feet into the Sauce à la Poulette, add a gill of water, and let all simmer about five minutes.
5. Take off the fire and add the yolks of two eggs, beaten well, stirring well into the sauce.
6. Serve hot. Many of the Creoles add a half can of mushrooms to the mutton feet before putting in the sauce.
