## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 2 cups of butter
- 2 cups of sugar
- 1 pint of milk
- 1/2 pint of yeast
- juice and rind of 1 lemon
- flour

## Directions

1. Make it early in the morning in winter, and about 12 o'clock in the summer
2. Work flour into the mixture to make it as stiff as pound cake
3. Bake it for tea
4. Eat it with fresh butter
