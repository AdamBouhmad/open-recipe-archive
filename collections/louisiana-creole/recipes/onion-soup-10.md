## Ingredients

- 3 Large Onions, Sliced Very Thin or Chopped
- 1/4 Cup of Flour
- 1 Large Spoon of Butter
- 1 Quart of Milk
- 2 Large Potatoes, Mashed Fine
- Dice of Bread or Toast
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the onions in the butter, until reddish brown.
2. Then add the flour and stir until browned, gently; do not burn.
3. Put the boiling water in gradually, stirring perfectly smooth, and adding the salt and pepper; mix well and boil one minute.
4. Then pour it into the kettle and set back.
5. Before serving, add the milk warmed, and rubbed with mashed potatoes until they are a smooth paste.
6. Simmer a few moments.
7. Have the pieces of toast ready in the tureen and pour in the hot soup.
