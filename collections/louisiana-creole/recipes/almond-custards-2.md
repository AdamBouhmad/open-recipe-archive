## Ingredients

- 1 pint cream
- 1/2 lb. almonds blanched and pounded fine
- 2 tablespoonfuls rose water
- yolks of 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound almonds fine with 2 tablespoonfuls of rose water
2. Sweeten to the taste
3. Beat up the yolks of 4 eggs
4. Strain all the ingredients together over the fire until the mixture is thick
5. Pour it into cups
