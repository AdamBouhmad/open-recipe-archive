## Ingredients

- yolks of 8 eggs
- whites of 4 eggs
- 1 lb. of brown sugar
- 2 lbs. of flour
- 6 ounces of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- milk
- spice

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of 8 eggs and whites of 4 eggs very light
2. Add 1 lb. of brown sugar
3. Rub 2 lbs. of flour and 6 ounces of butter well together
4. Add a teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little milk
5. Add any kind of spice you like
6. Pour the beaten eggs and sugar on the flour and butter
7. Knead well
8. Roll and bake in a quick oven
