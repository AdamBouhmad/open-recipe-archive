## Ingredients

- whites of 2 eggs
- 2 teacups granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoonful citric acid
- extract of lemon

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar until clear, with just enough water to moisten it
2. Beat the eggs to a stiff froth, and pour boiling sugar very slowly into the eggs
3. Dissolve 1 teaspoonful citric acid and a small tablespoonful of water, putting enough in to flavor delicately, and add a little extract of lemon
