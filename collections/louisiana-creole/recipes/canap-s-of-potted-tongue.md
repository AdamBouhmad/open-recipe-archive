## Ingredients

- 1 Box of Potted Tongue
- Strips of Cooked Pork Tongue
- 6 Slices of Toast

## Directions

1. Prepare the toast and cut into delicate circles.
2. Spread with a layer of Potted Tongue one-quarter of an inch in thickness.
3. Decorate with strips of cooked Red Tongue in lattice forms, that is with strips laid one over the other, like a lattice work.
