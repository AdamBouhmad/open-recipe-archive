## Ingredients

- 12 Shallots
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Vinegar
- 5 Tablespoonfuls of Oil
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Chopped Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Mix all together as in a Sauce Ravigote, cold
2. Add the oil and vinegar
3. Serve cold with cold boiled meat, cold boiled fish, etc.
