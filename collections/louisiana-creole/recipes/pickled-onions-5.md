## Ingredients

- onions
- salt
- water
- strong brine
- good vinegar
- mace
- horse radish
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Put the onions in salt and water strong enough to bear an egg
2. Change the water every day
3. Make a strong brine and pour it on boiling hot for 3 days
4. Lay them on a sieve to drain
5. Put them in the jar
6. Fill it with good vinegar
7. Add mace and horse radish
8. If you wish them white, put salt and oil over the top
9. If you prefer them yellow, add from 2 to 3 tablespoonfuls of turmeric with the vinegar
10. Tie them up very close and they will keep any length of time for table use
