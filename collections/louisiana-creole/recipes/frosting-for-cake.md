## Ingredients

- egg
- ten teaspoonfuls of finest powdered sugar
- a teaspoonful of lemon extract

## Directions

1. To each egg used take ten teaspoonfuls of finest powdered sugar, and a teaspoonful of lemon extract
2. Beat quickly
3. allow at least five minutes for each spoonful of sugar
