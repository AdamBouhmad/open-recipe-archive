## Ingredients

- one pint of chopped onions
- three gills of white mustard seed
- three tablespoonfuls of ground mustard
- three tablespoonfuls of celery seed
- one pound of brown sugar
- three quarts of good vinegar
- two gallons of chopped cabbage

## Directions

1. Put together one pint of chopped onions, three gills of white mustard seed, three tablespoonfuls of ground mustard and the same of celery seed
2. Add a pound of brown sugar and three quarts of good vinegar
3. Cook this compound slowly until it begins to thicken
4. Pour it hot upon two gallons of chopped cabbage, which should be shaved or chopped very thin
5. This pickle is ready to bottle for use when it has boiled fifteen minutes
