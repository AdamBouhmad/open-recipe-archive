## Ingredients

- a quart of flour
- two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- a tablespoonful of lard or butter
- a tablespoonful of soda
- a cup of water
- milk, or milk and water
- a little salt

## Directions

1. To each quart of flour add two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar sifted through it
2. Put in a tablespoonful of lard or butter
3. Dissolve a tablespoonful of soda in a cup of water and pour it on the flour
4. Mix with milk, or milk and water, to a soft dough
5. Add a little salt
6. Roll out on the floured biscuit board
7. Cut with the biscuit cutter and bake quickly
