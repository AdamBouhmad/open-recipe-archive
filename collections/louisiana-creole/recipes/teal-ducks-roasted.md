## Ingredients

- 3 Pairs of Teal Ducks
- 6 Thin Strips of Bacon
- 6 Truffles
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Water
- 6 Slices of Toast
- Currant Jelly
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the duck nicely and put one truffle and a lump of butter about the size of a peanut, with salt and pepper, on the inside.
2. Rub well with salt and pepper and a little butter melted.
3. Take a thin strip of bacon and bind it around the body of the duck, fastening with a skewer.
4. Place in the oven for roasting (directions continue on next page).
