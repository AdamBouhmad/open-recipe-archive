## Ingredients

- large fine plums
- white sugar powdered
- best cider vinegar
- nutmeg
- mace
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take large fine plums, perfect and quite ripe
2. To every quart of plums allow ½ lb. of the best white sugar powdered, and a large pint of the best cider vinegar
3. Melt the sugar in the vinegar
4. Put it, with the fruit, in a porcelain kettle
5. Previously prick all the plums to the stone with a large needle
6. Lay among them some small muslin bags filled with broken nutmeg, mace, and cinnamon
7. Give them one boil up, skimming them well
8. Put them warm into stone jars, with the bags interspersed
9. Cork them immediately
