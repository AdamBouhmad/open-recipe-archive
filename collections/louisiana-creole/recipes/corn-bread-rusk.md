## Ingredients

- six cupfuls of cornmeal
- 4 cupfuls of wheat flour
- 2 cupfuls of molasses
- 2 teaspoonfuls of soda
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Mix the cornmeal, wheat flour, molasses, soda, and salt well together
2. Knead it into dough
3. Make 2 cakes of it
4. Pour into tin or iron pans
5. Bake 1 hour
