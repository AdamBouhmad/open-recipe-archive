## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sugar
- Water to Dilute
- Flavoring Extract to Taste

## Directions

1. Pound and sift a pound of sugar that has been doubly refined, first through a coarse and then through a fine sieve
2. Put in an earthen vessel, and add the flavoring extract, whether of lemon, orange, cinnamon, cloves, rose or violet or vanilla or carmine
3. Dilute the sugar with a little water and the liquid
4. Set the saucepan on the fire, being careful to select a saucepan that has a spout
5. As soon as the mixture begins to bubble up about the sides of the saucepan, stir it once well and take from the fire, and drop it in small lumps through the spout of the saucepan upon sheets of tin, and let it stand two hours to dry
6. Then put in the Stove a few seconds to finish drying
7. As they become hard and brilliant, take them out, else they will lose their flavor
