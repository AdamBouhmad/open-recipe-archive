## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Cornstarch
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- The Whites of 3 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon Extract

## Directions

1. Dissolve the cornstarch lightly in one pint of milk, and then add the sugar and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
2. Put this mixture into a pint of boiling milk.
3. Let it come to a boil.
4. Flavor with the lemon; pour into cups to cool.
5. When very cold, turn out, and serve with Jelly and cream.
6. This will serve six persons. Serve, if you wish, with a Cream Sauce.
