## Ingredients

- eggs
- boiling water
- salt
- vinegar (optional)
- black pepper
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Have the frying pan filled with boiling water.
2. Add salt and optionally a tablespoonful of vinegar.
3. Break the eggs into a saucer, one by one, and gently slip off into the water, without breaking the yolk.
4. Break another and another, until you have four in the pan, and allow the eggs to stand apart.
5. Let them boil thus on the water, till the white forms a thin veil over the yolks.
6. Take them up gently and neatly round off the ragged edges.
7. Sprinkle the top with a little black pepper.
8. Place on buttered toast, and serve immediately.
