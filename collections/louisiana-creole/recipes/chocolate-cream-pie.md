## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Milk
- ½ of a Cake of Vanilla or French Chocolate
- ¾ Cup of White Sugar
- 6 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Dissolve the chocolate in a little milk, and then stir into the boiling milk
2. Let it boil about four minutes
3. Set to cool
4. When almost cool, add the yolks of all the eggs and the whites of three, saving the remainder for the meringue
5. Stir these eggs well into the chocolate, and add the essence of vanilla
6. Put into pie pans, in which you will already have baked the under crust
7. Set in the oven and let the custard grow to a set state
8. When half done, spread over the whites of the eggs, which have been whipped to a stiff froth, with two tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
9. Let the pies brown lightly, and serve cold
