## Ingredients

- cabbage leaves
- beet leaves
- turnip leaves
- mustard leaves
- spinach
- cresses
- parsley
- green onions
- cooking soda
- veal brisket
- ham
- large onion
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- hot water
- boiled rice

## Directions

1. Take equal parts of cabbage leaves, beet leaves, turnip leaves, mustard leaves, spinach, cresses, parsley and green onions
2. Soak them and wash them thoroughly
3. Trim them by taking off the coarse mid rib of the leaf
4. Boil them for at least two hours
5. Add to the water a teaspoonful of cooking soda
6. Strain them and chop up as you would spinach
7. Take a piece of veal brisket and a slice of ham and cut into small pieces
8. Add a large onion, chopped fine, salt and pepper
9. Fry all in a heaping spoonful of lard to a rich brown
10. Add the greens, stirring with a wooden spoon
11. Add as much hot water as will make sufficient gumbo for the family
12. Serve with boiled rice
