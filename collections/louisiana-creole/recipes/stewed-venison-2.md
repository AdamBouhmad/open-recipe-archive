## Ingredients

- venison slices
- butter
- pepper
- mustard
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- claret wine
- currant jelly

## Directions

1. Have the chafing dish ready
2. Have the slices of venison nicely cut to proper thickness
3. Between the slices, as you place them in the dish, put bits of good butter, pepper, mustard, salt, a little cayenne pepper and claret wine
4. Let it stew 10 or 15 minutes
5. Put sufficient currant jelly to make it palatably sweet
