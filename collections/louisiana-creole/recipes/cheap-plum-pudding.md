## Ingredients

- A cup of chopped suet
- A cup of raisins
- A cup of currants and citron mixed
- A cup of sweet milk
- Two eggs
- A cup of molasses
- A teaspoonful of soda
- Three and a half cups of sifted flour or bread crumbs
- A little salt

## Directions

1. Combine the chopped suet, raisins, currants and citron mixed, sweet milk, eggs, molasses, and soda
2. Add three and a half cups of sifted flour or bread crumbs, and a little salt
3. Boil three or four hours
4. Serve with hard sauce of beaten butter, sugar and nutmeg; or with butter, sugar and wine sauce
