## Ingredients

- six mellow apples
- pastry
- long narrow strips of citron
- one pint of powdered sugar
- half a pint of butter
- yolks of eight eggs
- whites of eight eggs
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel and core six mellow apples.
2. Line a pudding dish with pastry.
3. Lay the apples in the bottom of the dish, and stick long narrow strips of citron around them.
4. Stir to a cream a pint of powdered sugar, and half a pint of butter.
5. Beat separately the yolks and whites of eight eggs.
6. Mix them with the butter and sugar, season with nutmeg.
7. Place it on the fire, and stir until it is hot.
8. Pour it over the apples, and bake immediately.
9. It can be eaten warm or cold.
10. It should be covered with a pan when first put into the oven, to prevent the top from browning too soon.
