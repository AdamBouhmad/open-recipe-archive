## Ingredients

- two large heads of cabbage
- 8 large onions
- 2 teacups of salt
- 1 gallon of vinegar
- 1 teacup of white mustard seed
- 1 teacup of celery seed
- 1 teacup of grated horse radish
- 1 whole garlic
- 2 or 3 pods of green or red peppers
- 2 teacups of brown sugar
- 1 teacup of the best sweet oil
- 2 ounces of turmeric
- 1 teacup of mustard

## Directions

1. Cut cabbage as for slaw, but not so fine
2. Slice 8 large onions
3. Mix the onions with the cut cabbage, and sprinkle about 2 teacups of salt over them
4. Mix the salt thoroughly in the cabbage, and let them stand not less than 12 hours
5. Drain all the water from the cabbage through a muslin cloth
6. Put the cabbage in a porcelain kettle and cover it with a gallon of vinegar, 1 teacup of white mustard seed, 1 teacup of celery seed, 1 teacup of grated horse radish, 1 whole garlic minced fine, 2 or 3 pods of green or red peppers cut fine
7. Let this come to a boil, and add 2 teacups of brown sugar
8. Set this aside until the next day to get cold
9. Have a paste made of 1 teacup of the best sweet oil, 2 ounces of turmeric and 1 teacup of mustard, made quite thick and stirred into the oil
10. Mix this paste in well with the hand into the vinegar and cabbage
11. It will be fit for use in 10 days
