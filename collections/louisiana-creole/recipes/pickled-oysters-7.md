## Ingredients

- oysters
- mace
- pepper
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil the oysters till quite done, in their own liquor, with as much mace and pepper as will season them
2. Pour off and strain the liquor and vinegar sufficient to cover the oysters
3. Stir in salt to your taste
