## Ingredients

- one ounce of isinglass or gelatine
- one and a half pints of milk
- half a pound of white sugar
- yolks of six eggs
- vanilla
- one pint of cream
- strips of sponge cake

## Directions

1. Boil one ounce of isinglass or gelatine, in one and a half pints of milk
2. sweeten it with half a pound of white sugar
3. beat in the yolks of six eggs
4. flavor it with vanilla
5. When this mixture begins to stiffen as it grows cold, stir into it one pint of cream whipped to a froth
6. Ornament the glass dish it is to be served in, with strips of sponge cake
7. pour the Charlotte Russe in
8. Set it in a cool place until wanted
