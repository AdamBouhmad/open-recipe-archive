## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1½ Pints of Flour
- ¾ Cupful of Butter
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- ¾ Cup of Sugar
- ¾ Cake of Compressed Yeast

## Directions

1. Warm the butter in a pint of milk till the milk reaches the boiling point but do not let it boil
2. Add the salt and a tablespoonful of sugar and let it cool
3. When tepid add the flour, well sifted, and beat thoroughly into the mixture
4. Add the yeast, dissolved in a little hot milk or water
5. Beat continuously for at least five minutes
6. When the batter begins to break into blisters, cover it and set to rise for the night
7. In the morning add the yolks of the eggs, beaten till very light, and the whites, beaten to a stiff froth
8. Mix carefully and dissolve a half teaspoonful of soda in the mixture if it seems any way sour
9. Turn the whole into a shallow buttered dish and set to rise for fifteen minutes longer
10. Bake about twenty or twenty-five minutes in a moderately quick oven till it is a light brown
