## Ingredients

- brisket
- salt
- pepper
- saltpetre
- mace
- cloves
- allspice

## Directions

1. Cut brisket from the bones and wash
2. Lay in a large dish
3. Cover well with salt, pepper and a little saltpetre
4. Cover to stand several days, as the weather will permit
5. Wipe off the dressing and place on a clean dish
6. Powder mace and cloves and allspice together and spread thickly over the beef
7. Roll it over and over and tie up well
8. Sew up tightly in a cloth and boil slowly all day
9. Serve cold
