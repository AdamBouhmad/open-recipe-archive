## Ingredients

- 1 Pound Sugar
- 1½ Pounds Butter
- 1 Pound of Flour
- The Whites of 10 Eggs
- ⅛ Teaspoonful of Almond Extract

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add the sugar, and beat till very, very light
2. Add the flour alternately with the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, and then the essence extract
3. Bake in a moderate oven one hour
