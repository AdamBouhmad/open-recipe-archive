## Ingredients

- 2 pints of cream
- 6 ounces of powdered loaf sugar
- juice of two large lemons
- 2 glasses of white wine
- 2 ounces of isinglass
- four small teacupfuls of water

## Directions

1. Put 2 pints of cream into 2 bowls
2. With 1 bowl mix 6 ounces of powdered loaf sugar, the juice of two large lemons, and 2 glasses of white wine
3. Add the other pint of cream and stir the whole very hard
4. Boil 2 ounces of isinglass with four small teacupfuls of water until it is reduced to half
5. Stir the isinglass, lukewarm, into the other ingredients
6. Put them into a glass dish to congeal
