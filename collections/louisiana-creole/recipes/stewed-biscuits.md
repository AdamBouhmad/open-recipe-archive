## Ingredients

- 6 Left-Over Biscuits
- ¾ Pound of Butter
- 1½ Pints of Milk

## Directions

1. Take a porcelain-lined saucepan, cut the biscuit in halves, lay in the bottom of the saucepan, place a layer of butter over this, and then a layer of biscuit, and so on till all are used up.
2. Pour milk over the whole, let it come to a boil, and serve hot.
