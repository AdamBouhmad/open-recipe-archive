## Ingredients

- bread
- butter
- oysters
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- cloves
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Toast several slices of bread quite brown, and butter them on both sides
2. Take a baking dish and put the toast around the sides
3. Pour the oysters into the dish and season to your taste, with butter, pepper and salt
4. Add mace or cloves
5. Crumb bread on the top of the oysters, and bake with quick heat for 15 minutes
