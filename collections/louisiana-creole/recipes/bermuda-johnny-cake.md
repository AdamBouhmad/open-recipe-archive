## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- a little salt
- milk

## Directions

1. To each pint of flour allow 2 ounces of butter
2. Work in a small part of the butter with flour
3. Mix it up with a little salt and milk to consistency of paste
4. Roll it out and spread on the butter as for paste, twice dividing the butter
5. Put the paste into as many pieces as you wish cakes and roll them out round
6. 1 pint of flour will make 2 cakes the size of large plate
7. Bake them on a griddle
8. Let each cake remain on the griddle a short time off the fire after being done to make them crisp
9. Tear open each and butter quickly
