## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of lean beef or veal
- 1/2 lb. of salt pork
- 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon of black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon of cloves
- allspice
- mace
- teacupful of bread crumbs
- tablespoonful of butter
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt

## Directions

1. Chop the meat very fine
2. Mix all of the ingredients thoroughly
3. Steam in a mould for 3 hours
