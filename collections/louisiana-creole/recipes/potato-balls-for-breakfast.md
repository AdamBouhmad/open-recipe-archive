## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- salt
- egg
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Pare and boil dry the potatoes
2. Put them into a hot pan and mash with a lump of butter and a little salt
3. Beat this well and make it into little cakes or roll it into balls
4. Dip them into egg and sprinkle with bread crumbs
5. Fry a nice brown
