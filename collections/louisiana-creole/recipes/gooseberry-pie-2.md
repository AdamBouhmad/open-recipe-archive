## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Gooseberries
- 1½ Cups of Sugar
- A Plain Pie Paste

## Directions

1. Top and tail the gooseberries
2. Line two deep pie dishes with a plain Pie Paste
3. Fill with berries
4. Add nearly one cup of sugar to each pie, and proceed as in recipe for Apple Pie
5. Set in the oven and let the pies bake for three-quarters of an hour
