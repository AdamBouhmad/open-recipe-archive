## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Freshly-Gathered Mint
- The Zests of 5 Lemons
- 7 Pints of Fine Old French Cognac
- 2 Drachms of Essence of Peppermint
- 4 Pounds of Sugar
- 2 Quarts of Water

## Directions

1. Gather the mint very fresh, and wash lightly in fresh water.
2. Cut it very fine, and add the grated zests of the lemons.
3. Pound well in a stone jar, and macerate or steep for eight days in seven pints of good brandy.
4. Then distill it, or boil to one-half, covering the spout of the boiler with a piece of kid in which you have cut small holes.
5. Then add a half drachm of essence of peppermint.
6. Dissolve four pounds of sugar in three pints of water, and let it cool.
7. Then mix it thoroughly with the mint infusion and let it stand for a half hour.
8. Then filter and bottle it, and keep in a cool, shady place.
