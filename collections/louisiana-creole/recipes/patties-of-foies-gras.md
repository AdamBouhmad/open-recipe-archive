## Ingredients

- Terrine of Foies Gras
- Pound of Fat Pork
- Pound of Goose Fat
- Half Can of Truffles
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Glass of Sherry Wine
- Puff Paste

## Directions

1. Procure the fat livers of geese
2. Chop the livers, fat pork and goose fat very fine, allowing pound for pound of the fat meat and goose fat to the same quantity of livers
3. Season well with salt and pepper, and moisten well with Sherry wine
4. Chop a half can of truffles and mix
5. Put all in a quart or pint measure baking pan which you will have lined with Puff Paste
6. The pan must be about two and a half or three inches deep
7. Bake this paste, then fill in with the foies gras
8. Cover with a light cover of the dough, and decorate around the edges with the clippings of dough that remain
9. Place the pie in the oven, and let it bake for about an hour to a nice brown, covering for the first three-quarters of an hour with a piece of paper, to prevent burning
10. When done, serve in the dish in which it was baked
