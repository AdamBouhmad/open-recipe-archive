## Ingredients

- wild geese
- bread
- butter
- onion
- cayenne
- mustard

## Directions

1. Stuff wild geese with a dressing of bread, butter, and a small quantity of pungent seasoning, such as onion, cayenne, or mustard
2. Cook rare
