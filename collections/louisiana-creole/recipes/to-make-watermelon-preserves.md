## Ingredients

- watermelon rind
- alum
- grape leaves
- sugar
- water
- ginger
- lemons
- lemon skins
- saffron

## Directions

1. Take the firm outside rind of the watermelon; scrape off the green and cut out the soft inside.
2. Cut the rind into any shapes you choose, stars, crescents, diamonds, etc.
3. After they have been boiled in alum and leaves to green and harden, weigh them and make a syrup of a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit, with a cup of water to each pound.
4. Boil the syrup clear, and put in the cut rinds, and boil them until transparent.
5. Flavor with ginger for green color, and lemons for the yellow.
6. If the rind is wanted yellow you must boil it with fresh lemon skins and a little saffron before preserving it.
