## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound of butter
- one pound of flour
- nine eggs, yolks only
- one pound of sifted sugar
- twelve eggs, whites only
- brandy or wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Cream three quarters of a pound of butter
2. Sift a pound of flour gradually into the butter and cream them together
3. Beat the yolks of nine eggs light in another pan
4. Stir a pound of sifted sugar into the eggs
5. Mix well
6. Beat the whites of twelve eggs to a froth
7. Add the whites to the yolks and sugar
8. Pour this into the pan containing the butter and flour
9. Beat all well together
10. Add a little brandy or wine and nutmeg
11. Bake carefully in a large pan
12. Ensure the oven is not hotter on the top than at the bottom
13. Allow the cake to rise and bake from the bottom
