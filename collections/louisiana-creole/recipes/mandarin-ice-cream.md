## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream or Milk
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Yolks of 6 Eggs
- The Grated Zest of 2 Mandarins
- The Juice of 1 Dozen Mandarins, With the Grated Pulp

## Directions

1. Peel the mandarins, grate the rind or zest of two very fine, extract the juice of twelve, and after taking out the seeds, mash the inner fruit or pulp through a sieve
2. Put the milk or cream on to boil
3. Beat the yolks of eggs and sugar till very light, and beat the whites to a stiff froth
4. Take the milk from the fire and pour it over the sugar and yolks, stirring all the while
5. Set to cool, then add the whites of the eggs, and finally the grated mandarin pulp, zest and juice, and mix well
6. Turn all into a freezer and proceed as in the directions given for making Ice Cream
