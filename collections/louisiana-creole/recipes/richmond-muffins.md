## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of flour
- 1 1/2 pints of new milk
- 2 large spoonfuls of butter
- 3 eggs
- salt
- 3 spoonfuls of good yeast

## Directions

1. Sift 2 lbs. of flour
2. Warm 1 1/2 pints of new milk and 2 large spoonfuls of butter
3. Stir into the flour
4. Beat 3 eggs very lightly, a little salt
5. Beat all well together till thoroughly mixed
6. Then add 3 spoonfuls of good yeast
7. Set it to rise
8. When risen, bake in muffin rings in an oven
