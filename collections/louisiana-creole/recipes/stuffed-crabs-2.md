## Ingredients

- one dozen crabs
- grated crackers
- onion
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- four tablespoons butter
- one egg
- juice of one lemon
- tomato
- toasted bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil for a few moments one dozen crabs
2. Crack and pick out all of the meat
3. To this meat, add one-third as much grated crackers, one onion chopped fine, a little parsley, pepper and salt, four tablespoons of butter
4. Mix well and add one egg
5. Mix again, then add the juice of one lemon, with a good sized tomato
6. Fill the shells with the mixture
7. Bake about half hour before serving
8. Grate over them some toasted bread crumbs
