## Ingredients

- About a half pint of brown sugar
- Two pints of water

## Directions

1. Put the brown sugar in a pan on a slow fire
2. Let it burn or parch, slowly stirring all the time
3. When it turns a dark brown, add two pints of water and stir well
4. Bottle it and put it away
5. Use a few drops at a time to color and thicken gravies and soup broths
