## Ingredients

- artichokes
- water
- milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the artichokes
2. Parboil them in the water
3. Then boil in milk, salt to the taste
4. Boil 15 minutes in the water, and in the milk, until they are so soft that a straw may be passed through them
