## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sweet Almonds
- 1 Ounce of Butter
- 2 Pounds of Sifted Sugar
- The Whites of 6 Eggs

## Directions

1. Blanch or scald the almonds by throwing them into a pan of boiling water, and letting them remain on the fire until you can take one of the almonds between your fingers and easily push off the skin
2. Drain and cool the almonds in cold water, and drain again
3. Rub the almond skins or hulls off with your hands in a cloth, throw the almonds in cold water with a little salt, wash clean, drain and dry them in another cloth
4. Pound them to a pulp that will be as smooth as flour
5. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
6. Add a little of the sugar and a little of the eggs as you pound, to prevent the almonds from becoming oily
7. Keep the paste firm as you add the remainder of the sugar and the eggs
8. Have ready a baking sheet on which you will have placed a buttered piece of wafer paper
9. Lay the macaroons out on this, by dropping them from the spoon, in the form of a guinea fowl egg
10. Place the macaroons about an inch apart, so that the balls will not touch one another
11. Pass a wet paste brush gently over their surface
12. Put them in the oven, and bake at a very moderate heat till they are a light, delicate brown or fawn color
13. Take out and set to cool, and take away any excess of wafer paper that may cling to the edges
14. Keep in a dry place
