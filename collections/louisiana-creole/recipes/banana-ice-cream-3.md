## Ingredients

- 6 Large Bananas
- 1 Quart of Cream
- The Yolks of 3 Eggs
- 1/4 Pound of Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the bananas and slice and mash them
2. Boil the milk, and add the sugar, stirring till well dissolved
3. Add the yolks of the eggs, beaten very light, and let cool for an hour
4. Stir in the bananas, which you will have previously beaten to a smooth paste
5. Proceed as directed above
