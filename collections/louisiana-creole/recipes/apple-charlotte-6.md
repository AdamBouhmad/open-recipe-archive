## Ingredients

- 6 Large Apples
- 6 Apricots
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Cup of Sugar, or Sugar to Taste
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- ground cinnamon
- allspice to taste
- a quarter of a grated nutmeg
- 6 slices of bread

## Directions

1. Pare the apples and then cut them into slices, taking out the seeds and core.
2. Put them in a saucepan with three large tablespoonfuls of butter.
3. Add a little water to stew gently, and add the apricots.
4. Use four tablespoonfuls of butter and ground cinnamon, and allspice to taste.
5. Let all cook to a nice marmalade.
6. Take off and add a quarter of a grated nutmeg.
7. Cut six slices of bread, and butter well.
8. Butter a pan and garnish it around the bottom and around with the slices of bread, which must be very thin.
9. Fill with the apples, and cover on top with the bread.
10. Dot with butter and sprinkle with sugar, and put in an oven and bake to a golden brown.
11. When done, carefully loosen the edges and turn into a dish.
12. Serve it hot, with sugar and cream, or with a Custard Sauce, flavored with good brandy.
