## Ingredients

- Two eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- 2 teacups of sugar
- 1 scant cup of flour
- 1 quart of cream

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and 1 cup of sugar together
2. Add the flour
3. Have the milk boiling and put into the mixture
4. Stir over the fire until it is as thick as mush
5. Put the other cup of sugar dry into a pan and stir over the fire until it is melted
6. Add this to the mush and set it away to cool
7. When cold, strain into the cream and freeze
