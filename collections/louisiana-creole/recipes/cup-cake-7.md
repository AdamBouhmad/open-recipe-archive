## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Butter
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 4 Cups of Flour
- 1 Cup of Sour Cream
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Brandy
- 1 Tablespoonful of Rose or Vanilla Extract
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Soda
- Sweet Milk

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs, and then add the cream
3. Mix thoroughly, and sift in gradually the flour, beating thoroughly
4. Add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, and the brandy and rose water
5. Dissolve a half teaspoonful of soda in a little sweet milk and add, being very careful that there are no lumps in the soda
6. Beat well for a few minutes and bake at once, in small tins or cups, which you will grease well with butter and only half fill with the cake batter
