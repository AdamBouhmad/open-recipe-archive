## Ingredients

- 1 Can of Asparagus or 2 Bunches of Fresh Asparagus
- 1 Can of Green Peas or 1 Pint of Fresh Green Peas
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 6 Tablespoonfuls of Cream Sauce
- 6 or 8 Buttered Croutons
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the tender parts of the asparagus into pieces of about one inch in length.
2. Take the asparagus water in which they were put up, and set on the stove to heat.
3. Add the canned green peas to the fresh peas that have already been boiled.
4. Throw in the asparagus tips, and add water sufficient to cover.
5. Boil rapidly for ten minutes; then drain very thoroughly and return to the fire.
6. Add one tablespoonful of butter, salt, pepper and six tablespoonfuls of Cream Sauce.
7. Stir carefully, so that you may not break the tips.
8. Serve on neat Croutons of buttered toast, or place in a dish and bring piping hot to the table as an entree.
