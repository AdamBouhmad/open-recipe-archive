## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Fine White Sugar (Granulated)
- 1 Freely-Grated Cocoanut (Small Size)
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Cochineal

## Directions

1. Proceed in exactly the same manner as with White Pralines, only add about a tablespoonful of Cochineal to the pralines, just before taking off the fire.
2. Proceed to drop on a marble slab, as above.
