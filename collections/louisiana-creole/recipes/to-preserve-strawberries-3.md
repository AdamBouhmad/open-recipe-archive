## Ingredients

- 1 lb. strawberries
- 1 lb. sugar

## Directions

1. To 1 lb. of strawberries add 1 lb. of sugar, strew it over them, and let them remain until they have made a syrup
2. Put them in a skillet, and let them simmer over a fire until they are half or two-thirds done; take them off and spread them in dishes; they must remain in the sun until they are sufficiently cooled, to keep the summer; be careful not to bruise the strawberries in stirring them
