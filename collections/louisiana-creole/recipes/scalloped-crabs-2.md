## Ingredients

- The Meat of 1 Dozen Picked Crabs
- A Dash of Grated Nutmeg
- Bread Crumbs
- 1 Egg
- Pepper and Salt to Taste
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Boil and pick the crabs according to recipe given above
2. Beat an egg thoroughly and add to the meat which has been seasoned highly with Cayenne and salt to taste
3. Take one clove of garlic, chop fine and add
4. Sift into this mixture fine grated bread crumbs or cracker crumbs, and mix thoroughly
5. Beat an egg, roll the crabs into boulets or graceful meat balls, and then bind by rolling lightly in the egg
6. Roll in the bread crumbs, grated nicely, and then drop into boiling lard, and fry until a pale golden brown, which will generally require about three minutes
7. Wash and clean the shells thoroughly and wipe dry
8. Set a boulet in the center of each and garnish prettily with sprigs of parsley and sliced lemon
9. Serve very hot
