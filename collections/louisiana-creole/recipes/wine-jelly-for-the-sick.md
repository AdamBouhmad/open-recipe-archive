## Ingredients

- one pint of Madeira wine
- one pint of water
- one ounce of isinglass
- a teacupful of water
- sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take one pint of Madeira wine, one pint of water, and one ounce of isinglass dissolved in a teacupful of water
2. Let the wine and water be boiling hot, then stir into it the dissolved isinglass, and sugar to taste
3. make it quite sweet
4. let it come to a boil
5. try it by taking a little in a saucer, and if not a good jelly when cold, boil it until it is so
6. if lemon is allowed, use the juice of two to flavor this jelly
