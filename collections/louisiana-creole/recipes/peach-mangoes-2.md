## Ingredients

- large free-stone peaches
- brine
- vinegar
- white mustard seed
- horseradish
- powdered ginger
- red pepper
- onions
- garlic

## Directions

1. Steep some large free-stone peaches in brine for two days
2. Wipe each peach carefully, and cut a hole in it just sufficient to allow the seed to come out
3. Throw them into cold vinegar until you make the stuffing
4. Take fresh white mustard seed which has been wet with vinegar, and allowed to swell a few hours, scraped horseradish, powdered ginger, a few pods of red pepper, a few small onions, or a clove of garlic
5. Mix all with vinegar, and add half as much chopped peach
6. Stuff the peaches hard with this mixture
7. Replace the piece cut out, and tie it up tight with packthread
8. Boil a quart of vinegar for each dozen peaches
9. Season it with the same spices as the stuffing
10. Boil the spices in a small bag, and then put in the peaches
