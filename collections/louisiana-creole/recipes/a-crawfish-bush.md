## Ingredients

- 100 Fine Crawfish
- Celery Tips
- Asparagus Tips
- Parsley Tips

## Directions

1. Boil the Crawfish according to recipe
2. Take a glass fruit or cake stand and place in the center of the table
3. Set a goblet upon it
4. Fit the goblet with celery tips and parsley tips, and hang a number of Crawfish gracefully around the goblet from the rim or outer edges
5. Continue hanging the dish with celery, asparagus and parsley tips, and hanging the Crawfish around the edges of the fruit stand, and in and out amid the greenery
6. Serve the Crawfish from the bush
