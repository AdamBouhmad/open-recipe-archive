## Ingredients

- white veal
- fat bacon
- mushrooms
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- sweet herbs
- egg yolks
- truffles
- boiled smoked tongue
- calf's udder or veal
- turkey or capons or fowls
- cloth
- bones
- calf's feet or gelatine
- onions
- cloves
- green onions
- sweet basil
- thyme
- mace
- pepper corns
- wine or brandy

## Directions

1. Chop up one pound of white veal, with a pound of fat bacon
2. Season high with chopped mushrooms, parsley, pepper, salt, and a bunch of sweet herbs
3. When chopped fine, pound them in a mortar or pass them through a sausage grinder
4. Add to this the yolks of three eggs, and place it by in a basin for use
5. Peel a pound of truffles, and cut up a boiled smoked tongue, a pound of fat bacon, or a pound of calf's udder or veal
6. Bone a turkey, or two fine capons, or fowls, and draw the skin from the legs and pinions inside
7. Take the turkey on a napkin
8. Cut slices from the thick breast and place it on the skin where it seems to be thin
9. Distribute the flesh of the fowl as evenly as you can on the skin
10. Season it slightly with pepper and salt
11. Spread a layer of the prepared force-meat in the basin, let it be an inch thick
12. Place the cut-up tongue, bacon and veal
13. Lay a row of chopped truffles and a layer of the force-meat until the skin is covered, or as full as it will hold
14. Sew it up the back, the ends tied, like a cushion, or roly-poly
15. Butter a cloth and put it tightly over the turkey skin
16. Tie it up tightly
17. Place it in a round stew-pan with the bones and any trimmings of veal or poultry at hand
18. Add to it two boiled calf's feet, or an ounce of gelatine
19. Add two onions stuck with four cloves, a bunch of parsley, six green onions, a bunch of sweet basil, and a bunch of thyme, two blades of mace, and a dozen pepper corns, or whole peppers
20. Moisten all with half a pint of wine or brandy
