## Ingredients

- a pint of cold potatoes
- six eschalots
- pepper
- salt
- two tablespoonfuls of oil
- one of made mustard
- half a cup of vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice a pint of cold potatoes, put them in a dish
2. Chop over them six eschalots, pepper and salt them
3. Pour over them a dressing of two tablespoonfuls of oil, one of made mustard, and half a cup of vinegar
