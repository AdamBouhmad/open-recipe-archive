## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- ¾ Cake of Compressed Yeast
- 1 Tablespoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Scald the milk and set to cool
2. Add the salt and gradually beat in the flour
3. Beat till very smooth and add the yeast
4. Beat continuously for ten minutes and then set in a warm place to rise
5. After several hours beat well again and add the melted butter
6. Mix thoroughly and then turn into greased muffin rings
7. Bake till a beautiful brown, turning first on one side and then on the other
8. Send to the table very hot
9. Break open with your fingers and butter nicely
