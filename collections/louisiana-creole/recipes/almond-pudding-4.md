## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of almonds
- 1 spoonful of rose water
- 1 spoonful of cream or milk
- 1 large spoonful of pounded biscuit
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 7 eggs
- 1 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/2 lb. of almonds, beat them smooth in a mortar
2. add 1 spoonful of rose water, 1 spoonful of cream or milk thickened with 1 large spoonful of pounded biscuit, 1/2 lb. of sugar, 7 eggs, and 1 nutmeg
3. bake it carefully in a moderate oven
