## Ingredients

- handsome pippin apples
- water
- jelly
- whites of eggs
- sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Select handsome pippin apples if you can get them, pare and core them whole
2. Put them in the oven with a little water in a deep dish, and let them cook a little but not enough to break
3. When plumped, take them out and let them get cold
4. Fill the centre of each apple with jelly
5. Make an icing of the whites of eggs, beaten with sifted sugar
6. Carefully cover each apple with it, wetting the knife while smoothing the icing
7. Sift a little sugar over them and put them in the oven to harden, but not to brown
