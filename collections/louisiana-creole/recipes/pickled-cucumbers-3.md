## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- spices

## Directions

1. Wipe and put them in salt and water, strong enough to bear an egg
2. Let them remain 6 or 7 days
3. Scald in vinegar to green them
4. Put them in cold spiced vinegar
