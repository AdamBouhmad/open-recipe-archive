## Ingredients

- 1 pint of broth (white) or boiling water
- Lemon juice
- 1 can of mushrooms
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Make a brown roux, melting the butter in the saucepan, and adding the flour, and stirring till well browned
2. Stir in the boiling stock, or water, if you have not the stock
3. Add the mushrooms, and salt and pepper to taste
4. Add the juice of half a lemon and let it cook for about fifteen minutes longer
5. Pour the sauce over the filet, and serve hot
