## Ingredients

- Boiled grits or hominy
- Salt and pepper
- Flour
- Beaten egg
- Lard

## Directions

1. After the grain is boiled, let it cool.
2. Season with salt and pepper, and spread on a biscuit board.
3. When perfectly cold, cut into slices and dust each slice with a little flour.
4. Brush again with a beaten egg, and fry in lard till a light brown.
