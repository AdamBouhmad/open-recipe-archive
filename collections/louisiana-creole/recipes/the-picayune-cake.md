## Ingredients

- 1 cup of finely sifted flour
- 1 cup of fine white sugar
- whites of 8 eggs
- 2 teaspoonfuls of baking powder
- 1 teaspoonful of vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoonful lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth, after which very little beating will be required.
2. Add slowly a heaping cupful of fine white sugar, and one cup of flour, both of which have been sifted four times to reach this measurement.
3. Then add one teaspoonful each of vanilla and lemon extract, and two teaspoonfuls of baking powder, just before putting into the oven.
4. Bake in a moderate oven for twenty-five minutes.
