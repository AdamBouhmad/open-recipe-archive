## Ingredients

- 1/2 gallon of new milk
- 1 tablespoonful of wine with prepared rennet steeped for a week
- sugar
- 1 pint of cream
- wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Warm 1/2 gallon of new milk
2. Before the milk is heated, sweeten to the taste
3. Stir into it a tablespoonful of wine in which prepared rennet has been steeped for a week
4. While warm, stir the wine round several times in the bowl in which it is to be served
5. Let it stand for 3 hours on ice if the weather is very warm
6. Season a pint of cream with wine, sugar and nutmeg to make a rich syllabub
7. Serve the syllabub with the slip
