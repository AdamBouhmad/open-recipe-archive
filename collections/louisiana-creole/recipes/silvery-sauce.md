## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla or Lemon
- 2 Cups of Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Brandy

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and the butter to a cream
2. Set the milk to boil, and as it begins to simmer, add the sugar and butter
3. Let it simmer gently for a few minutes, and then take from the fire and add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
4. Add the essence, stirring till thoroughly mixed
5. Add the brandy and serve, either hot or cold, with any berry pudding.
