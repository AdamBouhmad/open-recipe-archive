## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- Whites of Four Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful Flour
- ¾ Cup Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla Essence or Lemon Juice

## Directions

1. Blend the flour and butter well together
2. When light add the milk gradually
3. Add the essence of vanilla or the juice of a lemon
4. Add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
5. Stir them well into the boiling mixture
6. Fill the pie pans, which you have filled with crust, baked according to directions
7. Place in the oven and bake until a nice brown
8. Serve very cold
