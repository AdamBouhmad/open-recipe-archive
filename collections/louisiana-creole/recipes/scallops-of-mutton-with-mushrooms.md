## Ingredients

- mutton scallops
- wine
- button mushrooms
- truffles
- broth or soup stock
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Saute, or fry the scallops brown, then pour off the fat.
2. Add a glass of wine, a dozen button mushrooms, three ounces of truffles cut in pieces, and a cup of broth, or the stock of plain soup without vegetables.
3. Simmer gently, and finish by adding the juice of a lemon.
