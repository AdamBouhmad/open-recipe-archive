## Ingredients

- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful Chopped Parsley
- Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. This sauce is made simply by melting butter and adding a little chopped parsley.
2. Add the juice of a lemon, if desired.
3. It is used as a garnish for broiled meat, fish, chicken, etc.
