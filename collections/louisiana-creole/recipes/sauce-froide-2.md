## Ingredients

- parsley
- chervil
- tarragon
- chives
- burnet
- five or six tablespoonfuls of oil or three yolks of hard-boiled eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- made mustard
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Mince quite fine some parsley, chervil, tarragon, chives and burnet
2. Mix them in five or six tablespoonfuls of oil, or three yolks of hard-boiled eggs rubbed down smooth
3. Add two tablespoonfuls of vinegar, some made mustard, salt and pepper
4. Beat all together until it is smooth and thick
5. Serve in a sauce-boat
