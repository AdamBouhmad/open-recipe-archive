## Ingredients

- 3-4 Cup Rice
- 1.5 Pints Milk
- 1 Quart Cream
- 1.5 Cups Sugar
- Yolks of 8 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla
- The Grated Peel of an Orange
- 12 Sweet Louisiana Oranges
- 1 Pound Sugar
- 1 Gill Water
- 1/4 of a Grated Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash the rice clean, and boil according to recipe in about a pint and a quarter of water
2. In half an hour take off and drain all water, and press through a sieve
3. Add it to the milk, and let it boil slowly a half hour longer without burning
4. Whip the cream to a stiff froth, and add the drain to the rice or milk, and set the whipped cream to cool until it is needed
5. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the sugar until they are very light
6. Add them to the boiling rice, stirring constantly and well, and let it cook for two minutes, adding in the meantime the grated peel of the orange
7. Take the mixture from the fire
8. Add the tablespoonful of vanilla, and the grated nutmeg
9. Mix well and set out to cool
10. Remove the dasher from the ice cream freezer, and when the mixture has become very cool, turn it into the freezer and let it set packed in rock salt and ice for three hours
11. Take a dozen sweet Louisiana oranges, and peel and cut them crosswise into halves
12. Take out the cores with the sharp point of a penknife, and set them in a dish ready for use
13. Put a pound of sugar to boil with one gill of water, and after ten minutes add the juice of half a lemon
14. Put a few pieces at a time of the oranges into this boiling liquid, and lay them out side by side in a flat dish
15. Pour over them the syrup that remains from the boiling, and set the dish in the ice box to cool
16. When ready to serve, wipe thoroughly the outside of the can that contains the pudding, and all around the edges, so as to remove any traces of salt
17. Wet a towel in boiling water and stand the can upon it
18. Open the can
19. Put a round dish on top, and then turn quickly upside down, and remove the can
20. If the pudding adheres, repeat the applications of the hot towel at the bottom and around
21. Place the oranges on top and all around the pudding, and pour over them the syrup, which has become cool, but not frozen
22. Serve immediately
