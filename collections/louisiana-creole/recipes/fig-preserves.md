## Ingredients

- figs
- sugar
- water
- alum

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water syrup as directed in previous recipe.
2. Let the figs be firm, not dead ripe or they will boil to a mass.
3. They should be laid in alum the day before they are to be preserved, then taken out, washed, and put into the boiling syrup.
4. Boil for three hours, or until transparent.
5. Bottle as usual and seal up with wax.
