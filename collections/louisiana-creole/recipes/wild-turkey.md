## Ingredients

- turkey
- oysters
- bread
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- button mushrooms
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. If the turkey is old, or tough, boil it one hour before being stuffed for baking.
2. Stuff it with oysters, bread and butter, and season with pepper and salt.
3. Baste with butter, and the juice of the turkey.
4. Make the gravy by putting in the pan a pint of oysters, or button mushrooms, throw in a cup of cream, or milk, salt and pepper.
5. Send to table hot, with the turkey.
