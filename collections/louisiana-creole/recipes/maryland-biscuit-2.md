## Ingredients

- flour
- salt
- lump of butter or lard
- new milk

## Directions

1. Take any quantity of flour the family may require, put in salt and lump of butter or lard, rub it well in the flour
2. Then moisten it with new milk, work it well, and beat it with a rolling pin until perfectly light
3. Bake rather slowly a light brown
