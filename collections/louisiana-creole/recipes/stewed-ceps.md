## Ingredients

- 3 Ceps
- 1 Onion, Minced Fine
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley
- 1/4 Cup of Milk

## Directions

1. Drain the Ceps from their oil
2. Slice nicely
3. Put a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan
4. Add the minced onion and the clove of garlic minced very fine
5. Moisten with a half cup of milk
6. Let the mixture simmer gently for twenty minutes
7. Serve the Ceps on slices of toast with the sauce poured around
