## Ingredients

- Six cups of sugar
- one cup of vinegar
- one cup of water
- one spoonful of butter
- one teaspoonful of soda dissolved in a little hot water
- lemon or vanilla for flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil all together without stirring, for half an hour
2. Flavor with lemon or vanilla
3. Pull like the old-fashioned molasses candy, or cool on a buttered plate
