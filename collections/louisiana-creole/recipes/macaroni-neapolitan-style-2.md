## Ingredients

- 1/4 Pound of Macaroni
- 1/2 Pint of Sauce Espagnole
- 1/2 Pint of Tomato Sauce
- 3/4 Pound of Grated Parmesan Cheese
- 6 Mushrooms
- 2 Truffles
- 1 Ounce of Smoked Beef Tongue
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1/2 Bottle of Claret
- 1 Carrot
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- 2 Sprigs of Celery

## Directions

1. Boil the macaroni, and cut into pieces of about two inches long, after draining thoroughly.
2. Place it in a saucepan with a half pint of Tomato Sauce and Sauce Espagnole, and add the cheese.
3. Add the herb bouquet, tied together.
4. Cut the truffles and mushrooms and carrot into dice-shaped pieces; mince the celery and add.
5. Add the beef tongue cut into small dice-shaped pieces.
6. Add the Claret.
7. Let all cook for about fifteen minutes, tossing frequently in the meantime.
8. When ready to serve, remove the herb bouquet and send to the table hot.
