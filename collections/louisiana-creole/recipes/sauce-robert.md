## Ingredients

- four large onions
- three ounces of butter
- one spoonful of flour
- half a pint of veal, or beef gravy, or soup
- salt
- pepper
- one dessertspoonful of made mustard

## Directions

1. Cut into small pieces four large onions
2. Brown them with three ounces of butter and a spoonful of flour
3. When yellow-brown, pour on them half a pint of veal, or beef gravy, or soup
4. Let all simmer for half an hour
5. Season with salt and pepper, and at the moment of serving, add a dessertspoonful of made mustard
