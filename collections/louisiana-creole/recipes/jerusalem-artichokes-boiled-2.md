## Ingredients

- 6 Jerusalem Artichokes
- Sauce a la Maltre d'Hotel or a la Creme

## Directions

1. Wash the artichokes in cold water, and scrape them.
2. Then throw them into cold water, and let them soak for an hour or so.
3. Take out and drain.
4. Put them in a saucepan; cover with boiling water, and let them boil slowly until tender; watch carefully, as they will easily harden again.
5. Serve with a Sauce a la Maltre d'Hotel or a Cream Sauce.
