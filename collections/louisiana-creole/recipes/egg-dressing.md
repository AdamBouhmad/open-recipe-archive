## Ingredients

- 4 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 1 Cup of Bread, Wet and Squeezed Thoroughly
- 1 Chopped Onion
- 1/8 Square Inch of Ham
- 1 Teaspoonful of Butter
- 1/8 Teaspoonful of Sage
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme, Parsley and Bay Leaf
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Wet the bread and squeeze thoroughly
2. Chop the eggs fine and mix with the bread
3. Mince the herbs and add
4. Season well with salt and pepper
5. Chop the onion and fry it in one tablespoonful of butter
6. As it browns add the bread, into which you have mixed the sifted sage, if desired
7. Add, as it fries, the half square inch of ham, minced very fine
8. Season again to taste, and let all fry about ten minutes
9. Take off the stove and stuff the fowl or fish and proceed with the arrangement for baking
