## Ingredients

- a pint of milk
- one-half a cup of flour
- one cup of sugar
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the milk and flour together
2. add the eggs and sugar
3. flavor the custard with lemon
4. take the first or outside cakes, and split each one gently
5. place in it the cream or custard, which must be cold before you introduce it
6. Put into each cake about a teaspoonful of the cream
