## Ingredients

- 6 Beets
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- A Pinch of Black Pepper
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the beet roots as in recipe for Boiled Beets
2. Peel neatly and cut up into dice-shaped pieces
3. Put them in a saucepan with a tablespoonful of butter
4. Season with a little pinch of black pepper and salt to taste
5. Sprinkle the powdered sugar over them
6. Let them cook for five minutes, tossing them lightly and almost constantly
7. Send to the table hot as a vegetable
