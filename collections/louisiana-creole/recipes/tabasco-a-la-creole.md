## Ingredients

- 3 Dozen Large Red Peppers
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1/2 Pint of Hot Spiced Vinegar

## Directions

1. Take three dozen large red Tabasco peppers or Chili peppers and one clove of garlic, chopped very fine
2. Scald them until very tender
3. Mash the peppers and garlic together
4. Press them through a sieve
5. Dilute the paste with spiced vinegar till it reaches the consistency of rich cream
6. Bottle and seal for use
