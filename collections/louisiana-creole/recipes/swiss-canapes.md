## Ingredients

- 1 Ounce of Anchovy Butter
- 8 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 6 Stuffed Olives
- 3 Minced Green Gherkins
- 6 Slices of Toast

## Directions

1. Prepare the French toast, and cut six delicate pieces into the shape of a triangle
2. Spread these with Anchovy butter
3. Decorate along one side with the whites of the eggs, finely minced
4. Decorate along the second triangular edge with the minced yolks of the eggs
5. Decorate on the third with the minced green gherkins
6. Place a stuffed olive in the center
7. Arrange nicely on a folded napkin on a dish and serve
