## Ingredients

- 5 Parsnips
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Drippings of Roast Beef
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Chopped Parsley to Garnish

## Directions

1. Brush and scrape or peel the parsnips.
2. Boil as directed in salted water till tender.
3. When done, drain off the water and cut the parsnips into slices lengthwise of about half an inch in thickness.
4. Put two tablespoonfuls of the drippings of the roast beef in the saucepan, and add a teaspoonful of butter.
5. When hot add the sliced parsnips.
6. When they are brown on one side, turn on the other and let this brown also.
7. Place on a hot platter, sprinkle with chopped parsley and salt and pepper, and serve with roast meats.
