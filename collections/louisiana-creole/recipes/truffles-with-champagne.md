## Ingredients

- 6 Truffles
- 1 Glass of Champagne
- Spices and vinegar for Marinade

## Directions

1. Make a Marinade, that is, a liquor with spices and vinegar; set to cook, and add a glass of champagne.
2. Add the Truffles, and season again to taste.
3. Let all cook for upwards of half an hour.
4. Take the Truffles from the sauce.
5. Let them cool, and serve on a folded napkin as an hors d'oeuvre.
