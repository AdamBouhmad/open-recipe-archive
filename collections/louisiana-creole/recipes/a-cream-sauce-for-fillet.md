## Ingredients

- 1 can of mushrooms
- 1 small box of truffles
- 1/2 small lemon
- 2 large tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 large tablespoon of flour
- 1 small teaspoon of eschallot or onion, minced extremely fine
- 3/5 of a teacup of rich sweet milk
- cayenne pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Pour a can of mushrooms through a cullender, to drain them well
2. put them in a clean wooden bowl, and with a vegetable mincer, mince them as fine as you can
3. add a small box of truffles, well drained and minced
4. drain the juice of 1/2 of a small lemon over them
5. put 2 large tablespoonfuls of butter in a saucepan
6. when quite hot, stir in 1 large tablespoon of flour
7. have a small teaspoon of eschallot or onion, minced extremely fine, which stir in the butter and flour
8. stir it all the time to keep from burning, and let it remain long enough on the fire to take a golden color
9. then pour slowly in 3/5 of a teacup of rich sweet milk
10. let this mix thoroughly, stirring all the time, for about 5 minutes, to prevent lumping
11. add to the sauce the minced mushrooms and truffles, and let all simmer together for a few minutes
12. season this highly with cayenne pepper and salt
13. the sauce should be quite pasty before the mushrooms are added
14. This sauce should be made quite thick, and can be made early in the day
15. when wanted, place the vessel in one of boiling water, to get well heated
16. Last of all, add the juice of the other 1/2 of the lemon
