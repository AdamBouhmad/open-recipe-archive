## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Angelica Stalk or Leaves
- 1 Ounce of Angelica Seed
- 1 Drachm of Nutmeg
- ⅜ Drachm of Ceylon Cinnamon
- 1 Drachm of Coriander Seed
- ½ Drachm of Cloves
- 4 and a quarter Pounds of sugar
- 2 and a half Pints of water

## Directions

1. Bruise the seeds and leaves well by pounding, and steep for two months in an earthen pitcher, well-covered, having added all the spices.
2. Take out and strain and add a syrup made of four and a quarter pounds of sugar and two and a half pints of water.
3. Filter and bottle, and keep in a cool place.
