## Ingredients

- uncooked corn ears
- 1 cup flour
- 1 egg
- milk
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Grate corn from uncooked ears
2. For batter, mix 1 cup flour, 1 egg, and milk enough to soften the whole sufficiently
3. Add salt and pepper
4. Fry brown in thin cakes
