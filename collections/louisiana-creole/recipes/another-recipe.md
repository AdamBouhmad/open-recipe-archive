## Ingredients

- two cupfuls of butter
- two cupfuls of molasses
- one cupful of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of ginger
- four tablespoonfuls of cream
- one teaspoonful of soda
- one-half an ounce of cinnamon
- about one and a half pounds of flour

## Directions

1. Roll
2. cut
3. bake in a moderate oven
