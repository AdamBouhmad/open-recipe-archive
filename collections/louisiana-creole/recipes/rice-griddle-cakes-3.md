## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1⅔ Cups of Cold Boiled Rice
- 1½ Cups of Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 2 Heaping Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Large Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Scald the milk and set it to cool.
2. Press the rice through a sieve, and then add the well-beaten yolks of two eggs, then the salt, yeast powder and flour, blended, and beat well.
3. Then add the milk, blending thoroughly, and finally the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
4. Mix thoroughly and bake on a hot griddle.
