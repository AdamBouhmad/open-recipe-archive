## Ingredients

- damsons
- brown sugar
- vinegar
- 1 ounce of cinnamon
- 2 ounces of mace
- 2 ounces of allspice
- 2 ounces of cloves

## Directions

1. Stick the damsons with a fork to prevent splitting them
2. Weigh 1 lb. of brown sugar to every 3 lbs. of damsons
3. Make a syrup by letting them boil a very short time with the spices
4. Pour into a jar when cold
5. Pour the syrup from the damsons for 3 mornings and make it boiling hot, then pour over the fruit, but not until nearly cold
6. On the 4th morning put the fruit into the jars also, and remain till quite done (but not too much)
