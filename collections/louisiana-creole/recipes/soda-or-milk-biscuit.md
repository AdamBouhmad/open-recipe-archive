## Ingredients

- a pound of sifted flour
- the yolk of an egg
- a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda
- a little milk
- a teaspoonful of salt
- milk

## Directions

1. To a pound of sifted flour, put the yolk of an egg
2. Dissolve a teaspoonful of carbonate of soda in a little milk
3. Put it and a teaspoonful of salt to the flour, with as much milk as will make a stiff paste
4. Work it well together
5. Beat it for some minutes with a rolling-pin
6. Roll it very thin
7. Cut it in round or square biscuits
8. Bake in a moderate oven until they are crisp
