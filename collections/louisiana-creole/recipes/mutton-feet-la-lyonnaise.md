## Ingredients

- 12 Mutton Feet
- 2 Onions
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Pint of Broth
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Clean and boil the mutton feet in the same way as indicated in the above recipe.
2. When done take out the large bones and cut the feet into two-inch pieces.
3. Cut two onions very fine, mincing them, and brown in a saucepan with a tablespoonful of butter.
4. When slightly brown, add a tablespoonful of flour and mix well, making a nice Brown Roux.
5. Add about a pint of the broth in which the mutton feet have been boiled.
6. As it boils, skim off the grease and let it simmer for about ten minutes.
7. Add the mutton feet and let them simmer ten minutes longer and serve hot, with Croutons of bread fried in butter.
