## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of ripe apples, pared and cut thin
- lemon peel
- 1/4 lb. of white sugar

## Directions

1. Stew apples until quite soft with lemon peel
2. Add 1/4 lb. of white sugar
3. Stew it 1/4 hour
