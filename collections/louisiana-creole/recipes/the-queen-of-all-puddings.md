## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Bread Crumbs
- 1 Quart of Milk
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 4 Eggs
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- The Juice of a Lemon
- % Pint of Seeded Currants
- % Pint of Raisins
- Fruit Jelly
- Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the bread crumbs in water for about an hour
2. Squeeze thoroughly
3. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the sugar very light
4. Add the milk and the bread crumbs, beating all light
5. Add the melted butter and the seeded raisins and the currants
6. Add the juice of one lemon and the grated zest of half a lemon
7. Beat all thoroughly together
8. Set in a moderate oven for a half hour
9. Beat the whites to a very stiff froth
10. Add four tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar, and beat thoroughly till very stiff
11. After half an hour take the pudding out of the oven, if it is nicely browned
12. Put over the top a layer of some fruit jelly, and then a layer of the whites, and then another layer of the jelly, and a last layer of the whites of the eggs
13. Set it back in the oven to brown for several minutes
14. Serve cold with Cream or Wine Sauce
