## Ingredients

- two cups of butter
- four cups of sugar
- sixteen eggs
- six cups of sifted flour
- two teaspoonfuls of yeast powder
- vanilla or lemon for flavoring

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar together
2. add the beaten yolks
3. alternately add flour and beaten whites
4. put yeast powder in the flour
5. flavor with vanilla or lemon
6. bake in a four-quart cake mould with oiled bottom and sides and paper on bottom
7. finish with chocolate icing
