## Ingredients

- 1 pint of oysters
- 1/4 pound of veal
- 1/4 pound of suet
- bread crumbs
- 1 egg
- flour
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Chop oysters with veal and suet, and some bread crumbs
2. Add one egg and a little flour
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Pound them in a mortar
5. Fry in small cakes
