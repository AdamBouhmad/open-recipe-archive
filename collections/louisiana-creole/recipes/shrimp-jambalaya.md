## Ingredients

- 1.5 Cups of Rice
- 80 Lake Shrimp
- 3 Tomatoes
- 2 Onions
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful Flour
- 0.5 Teaspoonful of Chili Pepper
- Salt and Pepper
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 2 Sprigs Each of Thyme and Bay Leaf
- Parsley
- Cayenne to Taste
- 3 Quarts of Broth or Water, or Oyster Water

## Directions

1. Chop two onions very fine, and put them in a saucepan to brown with a tablespoonful of butter.
2. After a few minutes add a tablespoonful of flour and stir well.
3. Then add chopped thyme, bay leaf and parsley, and two cloves of garlic, minced very fine.
4. Let all of this fry five minutes longer, and be careful not to let it burn or brown too much.
5. Add a half teaspoonful of Chili pepper, and three large tomatoes, chopped fine, and also add the juice.
6. Let all brown or simmer for ten minutes longer.
7. When cooked, add three quarts of broth or water, or oyster water which has been heated to the boiling point.
8. Let all boil well, and then add the lake shrimp, which you will already have boiled according to recipe.
9. Let the mixture boil again for five minutes, and add one cup and a half of rice, which has been well washed.
10. Mix all well, and let boil for a half or three-quarters of an hour longer, stirring every once in a while.
11. Serve hot.
