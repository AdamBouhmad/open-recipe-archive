## Ingredients

- ten or a dozen oranges
- one pound of loaf sugar
- half a glass of water
- whipped cream
- one glass of maraschino

## Directions

1. Take ten or a dozen oranges, remove the peel, all the white part and the seeds
2. Do this carefully by quartering them, retaining the transparent pulp and juice
3. Do not break the skins of the sections
4. Boil a pound of loaf sugar in half a glass of water until the syrup strings when lifted on a fork
5. Take it from the fire and dip each section of orange in this candy while it is hot
6. You can do this by placing each one on a little stick cut for the purpose
7. As the pieces are dipped, arrange them in some pretty form on a dish or bowl
8. Fill up the hollow with whipped cream, sweetened and seasoned with a glass of maraschino
