## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Currants
- ¾ Cup of Sugar
- Plain Pie Paste

## Directions

1. Clean and pick and wash the currants well
2. Drain nicely, and then put into a saucepan with one-half cup of sugar
3. Stew nicely, and then proceed as in the directions given for making Apple Pie
