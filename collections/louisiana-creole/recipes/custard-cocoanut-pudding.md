## Ingredients

- one cocoanut grated
- a quart of milk
- four eggs
- a cup of sugar
- paste

## Directions

1. Grate one cocoanut.
2. Beat sugar and eggs light, then stir in the milk, and last the cocoanut and such flavoring as you may prefer.
3. Pour this into a deep pan lined with paste.
4. Put fancy strips of paste across it, and bake lightly.
