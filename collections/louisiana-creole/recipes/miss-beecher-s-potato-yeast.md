## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- flour
- salt
- hot water
- brewers' yeast or home-brewed yeast

## Directions

1. Mash six boiled potatoes
2. Mix in half a coffeecup of flour and two teaspoonfuls of salt
3. Add hot water until it is a batter
4. Beat all well together
5. When it is blood-warm add to it one-half cup of brewers' yeast or a whole cup of home-brewed yeast
6. When this is light, put it in a bottle and cork it tight for use
7. Keep it as cool as possible
