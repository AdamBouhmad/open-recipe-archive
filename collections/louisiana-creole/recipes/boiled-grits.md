## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Grits
- 2 Quarts of Water
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Salt

## Directions

1. Wash the grits in fresh cold water, and throw off the refuse
2. Wash again and drain
3. Into two quarts of cold water put the grits
4. Add the salt, and stir frequently while they are coming to the boiling point
5. Set back on the stove and let them cook slowly for about an hour
6. Serve hot with any meat, with gravy, or serve with milk, as oatmeal, as a preliminary to breakfast, or simply eat with butter
