## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk or Cream
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 6 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Essence of Strawberry
- 1 Teaspoonful of Essence of Vanilla
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chocolate
- 1 Teaspoonful of Cream of Violets
- 1 Teaspoonful of Spinach Greenery

## Directions

1. Prepare a Plain Ice Cream
2. Divide it into portions
3. Color one with Essence of Strawberry, another with Spinach Greenery, another with Vanilla to obtain yellow, another with Creme de Violettes, another with Chocolate, etc.
4. Freeze all these in separate molds that come specially prepared for this purpose
5. When frozen, take from the individual molds and pack in a large ice cream freezer in a beautiful order of arrangement, so that the colors may blend well
6. Freeze again, and then remove from the mold according to directions given for unmolding
7. Cut into slices and serve
