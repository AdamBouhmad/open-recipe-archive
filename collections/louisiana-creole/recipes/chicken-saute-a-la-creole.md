## Ingredients

- 2 Fine Spring Chickens
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 6 Large Fresh Tomatoes, or 1/2 Can
- 6 Fresh, Sweet Green Peppers
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 3 Large Onions
- 3 Sprigs Each of Thyme and Parsley
- 2 Bay Leaves
- 1 Pint of Consomme or Boiling Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Take two spring chickens and clean nicely and cut into pieces at the joints.
2. Season well with salt and pepper.
3. Put two tablespoonfuls of butter into a stewpan, and, when it melts, add the chicken.
4. Let this brown slowly for a good five minutes.
5. Have ready three large onions sliced.
6. Add these to the chickens and let them brown.
