## Ingredients

- 50 or more shrimp
- salt
- big black pepper
- a little nutmeg (grated)
- a bit of butter
- rice
- bouillon
- cream
- shrimp shells
- crust of bread
- Madeira

## Directions

1. Wash shrimp in 5 or 6 waters
2. Put them in a saucepan, adding salt and big black pepper, a little nutmeg (grated) and a bit of butter, on a brisk fire
3. Stir with a spoon for ½ hour
4. When cooked let the shrimp drain
5. Remove the meats and mash in mortar
6. Boil rice in bouillon ½ hour
7. Strain and put it in mortar with the shrimp
8. When well pounded, put in saucepan and thin with bouillon and pass through a sieve
9. Thin this purée (cream) so that it be neither too thick nor too thin
10. Mash the shrimp shells and add juice or butter in which the shrimps have been cooked
11. Strain the purée in a cullender, it should then be of reddish color
12. Put this in a saucepan on a moderate fire
13. Do not let come to a boil, but should be quite hot
14. Put crust of bread in soup dish and pour on a little broth, hot
15. When ready to serve add a little Madeira
