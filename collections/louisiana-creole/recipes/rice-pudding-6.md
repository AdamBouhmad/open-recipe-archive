## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls of soft boiled rice
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 quart of milk
- 8 eggs
- few sticks of cinnamon
- sugar to the taste

## Directions

1. scald the milk
2. add a few sticks of cinnamon, and while warm, stir into it the rice, butter, and eggs, which must be first beaten
3. sweeten to the taste, and bake in a dish
