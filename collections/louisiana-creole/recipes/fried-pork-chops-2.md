## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 Pork Chops
- Grated bread crumbs
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Wash the pork chops and season well with salt and pepper.
2. Roll in grated bread crumbs and fry in boiling lard twenty-five minutes.
3. Continue until they have reached a rich brown.
4. Take out, place on a platter and serve with pickles or a Sauce aux Cornichons.
