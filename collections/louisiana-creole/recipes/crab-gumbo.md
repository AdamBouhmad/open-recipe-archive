## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Hard-Shell or Soft-Shell Crabs
- 1 Onion
- 6 Large Fresh Tomatoes
- 2 Pints of Okra, or Fifty Counted
- 1/2 Pod of Red Pepper, Without the Seeds
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig of Thyme or Parsley
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard or Two Level Spoons of Butter
- Salt and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Scald the hard-shell crabs and clean according to recipe already given, taking off the dead man's fingers and the spongy substances
2. Remove the sandbags on the under part
3. Cut off the claws, crack and cut the body of the crab in quarters
4. Season nicely with salt and pepper
5. Put the lard into the pot, and when hot throw in the bodies and claws
6. Cover closely, and after five or ten minutes add the skinned tomatoes, chopped onions, thyme and parsley, stirring occasionally to prevent scorching
7. After five minutes add the okras, sliced fine
8. When well-browned without the semblance of scorching, add the bay leaf, chopped fine, and the juice of the tomatoes
9. Pour over about two quarts and a half of boiling water
10. Set back on the stove and let it simmer well for about an hour, having thrown in the pepper pod
11. When nearly ready to serve, season according to taste with Cayenne and added salt
12. Pour into a tureen and serve with boiled rice
