## Ingredients

- ripe peaches
- powdered loaf sugar, same weight as pulp
- 1/2 ounce of bitter almonds, blanched and pounded

## Directions

1. Gather the peaches when ripe
2. Peel and stone them
3. Put them into a preserving pan and put them over the fire till hot
4. Rub them through a sieve
5. Add to 1 lb. of pulp the same weight of powdered loaf sugar and 1/2 ounce of bitter almonds, blanched and pounded
6. Let it boil 10 or 15 minutes
7. Stir and skim well
