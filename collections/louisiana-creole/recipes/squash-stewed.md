## Ingredients

- squashes
- sweet milk or cream
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- onion
- ham

## Directions

1. If not very young, peel the squashes
2. Steam or boil them until tender
3. Season them with sweet milk or cream, and a little butter, pepper and salt
4. Let them stew down in this until they are thick, and of the consistence of mashed potatoes
