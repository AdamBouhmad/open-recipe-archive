## Ingredients

- six eggs
- a cup of melted butter
- pepper
- salt
- lemon juice or vinegar or catsup

## Directions

1. Boil six eggs
2. When cold, take off the shells, and slice them into a cup of melted butter
3. Add pepper and salt
4. Stir constantly while heating
5. Add the juice of a lemon, or vinegar, or catsup as preferred
