## Ingredients

- 2 Dozen Oysters
- 1 Cup of Bread, Wet and Squeezed
- 1 Onion, Chopped Very Fine
- 1/4 Square Inch Ham
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 1/8 Teaspoonful of Sage
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme, Parsley and Bay Leaf, Minced Very Fine
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Wet the soft of the bread and squeeze thoroughly till you have one cup, judging the quantity of stuffing always by the size of the fowl to be stuffed, and adding more in proportion, if needed.
2. Season the bread well with salt and pepper, and add the minced herbs, mixing well.
3. Take a tablespoonful of butter and put in the frying pan.
4. As it melts, add the onion, which must be chopped very fine.
5. Let this brown for about five minutes, and while frying add the bread and stir well.
6. Then add the square inch of ham, minced very fine.
7. Mix well and let all fry well.
8. Season again to taste.
9. Then add the two dozen oysters, cut in two, with all the hard portions taken off.
10. Mix all well, and fry for a few minutes longer.
