## Ingredients

- underdone roast beef
- cayenne
- powdered nutmeg or mace or chopped sweet herbs
- mushroom gravy
- paste

## Directions

1. Mince all the lean and a very little of the fat of underdone roast beef
2. Season it with cayenne and powdered nutmeg, or mace, or else chopped sweet herbs
3. If you have a fancy for mushroom gravy, moisten the meat with that
4. Make a nice paste and cut it into small circular sheets, rolled out not very thin
5. Cover 1/2 of each sheet of paste with the minced beef (not too near the edge)
6. Fold over the other 1/2 so as to form a half-moon
7. Crimp them with a sharp knife
8. Lay the patties in square baking pans
9. Prick them with a fork and bake them brown
