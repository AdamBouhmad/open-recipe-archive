## Ingredients

- apple compote
- whites of 8 eggs
- 1 teacup sugar
- white of eggs

## Directions

1. Fill a dish with apple compote
2. Whip the whites of 8 eggs to a stiff froth
3. Sprinkle in the eggs 1 teacup of sugar, working them well together
4. Spread this over the apple
5. Beat some white of eggs without sugar, and pile it high in pyramidical form, and brown it of a light fawn color
