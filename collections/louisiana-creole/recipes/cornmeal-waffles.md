## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Cornmeal
- 1 Cup of Flour
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Melted Butter
- ¾ Teaspoonful of Soda, dissolved in hot water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Scald the milk and then add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs
2. Add the milk and the soda, dissolved in a little hot water
3. Add the melted butter and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
4. Add the flour gradually making a nice batter, not too thick nor yet too thin
5. Beat till all is very smooth
6. Proceed to bake as for Plain Waffles
