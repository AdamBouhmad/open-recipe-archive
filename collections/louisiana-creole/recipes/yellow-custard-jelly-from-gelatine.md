## Ingredients

- one ounce of gelatine
- one pint of water
- one quart of milk
- yolks of eight eggs
- vanilla or lemon for flavoring

## Directions

1. Soak one ounce of gelatine in one pint of water
2. Add a quart of milk
3. Set the milk and gelatine on to get hot, let it give one boil up
4. Sweeten it, and when a little cooled stir in the beaten yolks of eight eggs
5. Do not let the eggs boil up or you might curdle them
6. Flavor with vanilla or lemon, pour into moulds, and set in a cool place, or on ice to harden
