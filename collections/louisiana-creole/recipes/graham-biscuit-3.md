## Ingredients

- 3 cups Graham flour
- 1 cup white flour
- 1 teaspoonful Baking Powder
- 1 heaping tablespoonful sugar or molasses
- 2 cups lukewarm water

## Directions

1. Mix the batter until it reaches the consistency of oatmeal porridge
2. Pour into buttered tins
3. Let it rise till light
4. Bake in a moderate oven
5. Milk may be used instead of water, if you have it
