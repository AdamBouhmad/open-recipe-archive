## Ingredients

- boiled cauliflower
- well-beaten egg
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Take the white part of a boiled cauliflower after it is cold
2. Chop it very small and mix with it a sufficient quantity of well-beaten egg, to make a very thick batter
3. Fry it in fresh butter in a small pan
4. Send it hot to table
