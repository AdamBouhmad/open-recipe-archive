## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Whipped Cream
- 1 Gill of Rum
- 2 Ounces of Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Whip a pint of fresh sweet cream, adding a gill of rum and whipping till it reaches a stiff froth
2. Transfer to a china bowl and set on ice till ready to use
3. Have a dessert dish ice cold when ready to serve and drop the cream into this spoon by spoon, with greatest care so that it will not fall
4. Pile up high in a pretty pyramid shape and serve immediately
