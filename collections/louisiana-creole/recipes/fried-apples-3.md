## Ingredients

- 3 Fine Apples
- Boiling Lard
- 3/4 Cup of White Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Pare the apples and cut into round slices.
2. Have ready a pan of boiling lard, and fry to a delicate golden brown.
3. Place in a colander, heated, and in which you have placed a piece of brown paper.
4. Drain in the mouth of the oven.
5. Place in a dish, and sprinkle with sugar, and serve hot with roast meat, roast pork, etc.
