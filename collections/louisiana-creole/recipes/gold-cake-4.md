## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup of butter
- 2 cups of sugar
- yolks of 6 eggs, beaten very stiff
- 1/2 cup of milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- flour
- vanilla, bitter almond, rose water, cinnamon, or nutmeg for flavoring
- currants and raisins

## Directions

1. Stir the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add the beaten egg yolks
3. Add the milk with soda dissolved in it
4. Mix the cream of tartar with flour enough to make it as thick as cup cake
5. Add to the mixture
6. Flavor as desired
7. Bake
