## Ingredients

- 4 large sweet potatoes
- 1 teacupful of brown sugar
- 1 teacupful of New Orleans molasses
- 1 teacupful of milk
- 1 teacupful of butter
- 4 eggs
- The zest of one lemon, grated
- A small bit of orange peel grated very fine
- 1/2 teaspoonful of nutmeg
- 3/4 teaspoonful of ground cloves
- 1/4 teaspoonful of ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grate the potatoes and the zest of the lemon and orange.
2. Beat the eggs well; beat the butter and sugar till creamy, add the eggs, beat well, then add the grated potato and spices and milk; beat all well together.
3. Add the grated zest of the orange and lemon, put the mixture in a well-buttered pan and bake slowly for about an hour.
4. Serve hot or cold cut in slices.
