## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Lemon Sherbet
- 1/4 Pint of Champagne
- 1/2 Pint of Jamaica Rum
- 1 Gill of Maraschino
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Make a Lemon Sherbet, and freeze it very hard.
2. Then add the liquors slowly, and beat well.
3. Turn into a freezer, pack and cover it well, and let it stand for four or five hours.
4. The punch will not freeze perfectly on account of the alcohol.
5. Serve in a rather liquid state, in glasses.
