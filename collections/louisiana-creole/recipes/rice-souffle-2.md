## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of rice flour
- 1/4 pound of sugar
- 1/2 pint cream
- 6 fresh Creole eggs
- 1 teaspoonful vanilla
- 1/2 teaspoontul salt
- the grated peel of half an orange
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Boil the rice well, and when very soft add the half pint of cream, and let it come to a boil.
2. Beat the butter and sugar and the yolks of the eggs together until very light.
3. Add the rice, which has been boiled in the milk.
4. Set on the fire, and add the grated peel of a half orange, and stir continually till it thickens.
5. Add the salt and stir well.
6. Take from the fire, and add the vanilla.
7. Have the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff thick froth.
8. Pour this over the rice.
9. Set in the oven a few minutes to brown, and serve immediately while very hot.
