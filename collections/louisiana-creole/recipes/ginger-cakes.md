## Ingredients

- 6 cups of flour
- 3 cups of molasses
- 1 cup of sour milk
- 1 cup of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of soda dissolved in water
- 1 tablespoonful of ginger
- salt
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake
