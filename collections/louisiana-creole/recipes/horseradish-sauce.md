## Ingredients

- a spoonful of mustard
- three tablespoonfuls of vinegar
- salt
- two spoonfuls of cream
- horseradish
- a clove of garlic

## Directions

1. To a spoonful of mustard add three tablespoonfuls of vinegar and a little salt
2. If you have it, put in two spoonfuls of cream
3. Grate into this as much horseradish as will thicken it
4. Mash a clove of garlic into the mixture
