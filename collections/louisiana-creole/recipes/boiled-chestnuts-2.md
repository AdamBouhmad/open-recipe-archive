## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Chestnuts
- 1 Pint of Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel

## Directions

1. Prepare and boil the chestnuts according to above directions
2. When done, drain and press through a colander
3. The chestnuts must be cooked very soft for this dish
4. Add a Drawn Butter Sauce and sprinkle with salt and pepper or a Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel
5. Serve as an entrée, like mashed potatoes
