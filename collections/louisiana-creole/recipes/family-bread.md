## Ingredients

- 2½ quarts of flour
- 2 eggs
- 2 or 3 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- 1 pint of water
- 1 gill of yeast
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar, and 4 tablespoonfuls of the flour
2. Stir the yeast and water with the salt well together, and add gradually to the egg
3. Sprinkle some of the flour in the bottom of the breadpan
4. Pour in the batter
5. Sprinkle some of the flour on the top, and set it in a cool place to rise
6. When risen enough, work in the remainder of the flour
7. Make the batter at 12 o'clock in winter, and 4 in summer
