## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Fresh young Green Peas
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Shell the peas, and when you have a pint, put the peas into cold water, drain and put into a saucepan of boiling water.
2. Add a teaspoonful of salt to prevent the peas from cracking, and let them boil rapidly for at least twenty minutes.
3. To ascertain if they are done, take one out and press with a fork.
4. As soon as done, drain off all water; put a large tablespoonful of butter into the saucepan with the peas, season with pepper to taste, pour into a vegetable dish and serve hot.
