## Ingredients

- 8 Ounces of Shelled Almonds
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered Sugar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Melted Butter
- 2 Eggs
- ¼ Spoonful of Ground Cinnamon
- ¼ Teaspoonful of Orange Flower Water
- ¼ Gill of Rum

## Directions

1. Prepare a pie paste, and peel three ounces of shelled almonds
2. Put them into a mortar and pound to a paste, with the powdered sugar and a raw egg
3. When reduced to a very fine paste, add the melted butter, ground cinnamon and the orange flower water
4. Beat the yolk of an egg well, and then beat the white to a stiff froth and mix thoroughly
5. Add the rum and mix again
6. Line the pie pans with the pie paste, and fill with the preparation of almonds
7. Set in a moderate oven for thirty-five or forty minutes
8. Set to cool for a half hour
9. Prepare a Glace a l'Eau by putting one ounce of white granulated sugar into a saucepan, with one tablespoonful of cold water, and let it come to a boil
10. Take off the fire and add immediately a tablespoonful of curacoa
11. Mix thoroughly, and then glaze the surface of the cake with this
12. Let it cool and send to the table
