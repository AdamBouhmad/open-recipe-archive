## Ingredients

- A 3-Pound Sheepshead
- 2 Onions
- 1 Bunch of Parsley
- 2 Sprigs of Thyme
- 4 Bay Leaves
- 1 Sprig of Sweet Marjoram
- 1 Quart of Boiled Cream
- Yolks of 4 Eggs
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Bread Crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the Sheepshead as for boiling
2. When quite done, take out of the water and flake off all the flesh from the bones
3. Have ready a quarter of boiled cream or milk and beat the yolks of four eggs and mix with the cream
4. Chop one large onion, a bunch of parsley, a sprig each of thyme and bay leaf, and add to the cream and eggs
5. Let it boil up once, and while boiling, throw in three tablespoonfuls of flour, rubbed perfectly smooth, in a little cream, and about two tablespoonfuls of butter
6. Remove from the fire
7. Have ready a deep dish, well buttered, and put in a layer of fish and then a layer of the sauce, until the dish is full
8. Sprinkle over with bread crumbs
9. Place in the oven and bake about a half hour, or until brown
