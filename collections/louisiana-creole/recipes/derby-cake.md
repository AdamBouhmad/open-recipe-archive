## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of sifted flour
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of raisins
- 1 lb. of good brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 pint of new milk

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well together
2. Roll it out thin
3. Cut it into round cakes
4. Lay them on baking tins
5. Bake in a moderately heated oven for 10 minutes
