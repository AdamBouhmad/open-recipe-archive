## Ingredients

- 3 quarts sweet milk
- 1 quart cream
- yolks of 6 eggs
- 1 teacup starch
- 2 teacups powdered sugar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Put the milk in a porcelain or granite vessel to boil
2. Beat the eggs and sugar till very light and free of lumps
3. Dissolve the starch in a little cold milk and add to the sugar and eggs
4. When the milk boils, pour it slowly into the sugar, eggs and starch, beating all the time
5. Wash the vessel in hot water, slightly butter with the fingers, and return to the fire to thicken
6. Stir constantly till thick, and set away to cool
7. When ready to freeze, add the cream whipped, and flavor to taste with vanilla
