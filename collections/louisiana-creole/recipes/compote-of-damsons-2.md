## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Damsons
- 3/4 Pound of Sugar
- 3/4 Cup of Water

## Directions

1. Pick off the stems of the damsons and prick them all over with a pin.
2. Let them simmer in sugar, which you will have boiled to a syrup for about fifteen minutes.
3. Then proceed as in a Compote of Cherries.
