## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Fresh Pork, Very Lean
- 1 Pound of Fat
- 2 Pounds of Filet of Beef
- 1 Large Onion
- 1 Teaspoonful of Cayenne Pepper
- 1 Teaspoonful of Black Pepper
- 3 Teaspoonfuls of Salt
- 1 Bay Leaf, Chopped Fine
- 1/4 Teaspoonful Each of Ground Allspice, Cloves, and Nutmeg
- 1/4 Teaspoonful Each of Fine Herbs
- 1 Clove of Garlic

## Directions

1. Mince and hash the meat very fine, mixing the beef and pork and fat
2. Season highly with the Cayenne, salt and pepper, mixing thoroughly
3. Season next with the minced onion and garlic and mix well
4. Season with the minced herbs and spices, mixing all thoroughly
5. Fill the casings, which are never very large for Saucissons
6. Tie them in sausages of about a finger in length, or three inches
7. Fry in boiling lard and serve whole, placing several on each plate
