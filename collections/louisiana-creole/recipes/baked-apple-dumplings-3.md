## Ingredients

- pie crust
- yeast
- six good-sized tart apples
- sugar
- butter
- cinnamon
- spices
- nutmegs
- mace
- water
- wine or sugar and butter sauce

## Directions

1. Make a nice pie crust, raised with yeast, or not, as you desire
2. Divide it into six parts, and roll each part thin
3. Have ready six good-sized tart apples, pared and cored
4. Fill up the cores with sugar and butter
5. Close the dough neatly around the apples, and turn that side down in a deep dish
6. If they are made with raised dough they should stand one hour
7. If with unleavened paste, sprinkle some sugar over them, also a little grounded cinnamon or other spice
8. Spread a little batter over each of the dumplings as they go to the oven
9. Put plenty of spices, nutmegs, cinnamon and mace
10. Throw a little water in the dish, and bake three-quarters of an hour
