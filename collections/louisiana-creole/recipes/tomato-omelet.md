## Ingredients

- 1 quart fine, ripe tomatoes
- 2 onions
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- large spoonful of bread crumbs
- 5 eggs
- butter

## Directions

1. Select 1 quart of fine, ripe tomatoes, pour over them boiling water to remove the skin, then chop them finely
2. Put them into a saucepan without any water
3. Chop 2 onions very finely, cover closely, and let them simmer slowly, an hour
4. Then add a little salt and cayenne pepper, a large spoonful of bread crumbs, and cover tightly
5. Beat up 5 eggs to a stiff froth
6. Have ready a heated pan, and a small piece of butter and grease it
7. Stir the eggs into the tomatoes
8. Mix all together, and pour it into the pan
9. Brown it on one side, fold it over, and serve up on a hot dish
