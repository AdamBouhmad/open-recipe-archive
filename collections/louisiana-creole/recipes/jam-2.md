## Ingredients

- Ripe fruit
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. You must weigh your fruit (say blackberries), and allow three quarters of a pound of good sugar to each pound of fruit
2. Crush the fruit and sugar, with a biscuit beater, until they are well mashed
3. Add a gill of water to each pound of fruit
4. Boil gently (not rapidly like jelly) until it becomes a jelly-like mass
5. When done, put it into glasses, or small earthenware pots and when cold, cover up like jelly
