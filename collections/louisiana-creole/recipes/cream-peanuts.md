## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Fine White Sugar
- 1 Teacupful of Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Extract of Vanilla
- 3/4 Pound of Peanuts, Shelled

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water till it comes to a thread
2. Flavor highly with extract of vanilla
3. Take from the fire and stir until it is a creamy white
4. Throw in the peanuts
5. Drop in a little white granulated sugar
6. Put them on a table on a piece of white paper and shake well till each stands apart
7. Roll again lightly in white powdered or granulated sugar and let them dry
