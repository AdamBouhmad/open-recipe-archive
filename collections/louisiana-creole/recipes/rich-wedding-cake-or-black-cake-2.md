## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- nine eggs, the whites and yolks beaten separately
- one pound of butter
- one pound of brown sugar
- one teacupful of molasses
- one ounce of grated nutmeg or ground mace
- one teaspoonful of ground allspice
- one teaspoonful of cinnamon
- a gill of brandy
- three pounds of currants
- three pounds of raisins
- half a pound of flour
- a pound of citron

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks of eggs separately
2. Beat butter to a cream
3. Beat the mixture of butter, sugar, molasses, and spices well
4. Pick, wash, and dry three pounds of currants
5. Stone and cut three pounds of raisins
6. Strew half a pound of flour over currants and raisins and mix well
7. Stir the currants and raisins with citron cut in slips into the cake mixture
8. Line tin pans with buttered paper
9. Put the mixture in an inch and a half or two inches deep
10. Bake in a moderate oven an hour and a half or two hours
11. Ice according to directions
