## Ingredients

- 15 Ripe Pomegranates
- 4 Quarts of Good French Brandy
- 3 Pounds of Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the pomegranates in slices and steep them for fifteen days in the brandy.
2. Squeeze through a cloth, add a syrup made with three pounds of sugar, and filter and bottle for use.
