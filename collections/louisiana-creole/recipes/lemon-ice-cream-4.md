## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream or Milk
- 6 Eggs
- 1/4 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lemon Extract

## Directions

1. Put the milk or cream on to boil
2. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the sugar very light, and then beat the whites to a stiff froth
3. Take the milk off the fire, and pour it over the sugar and yolks, stirring all the while
4. Add the whites, mixing thoroughly, and two tablespoonfuls of extract of lemon
5. Set to cool, and then turn into the freezer, pack, and stir around in the ice for about ten minutes
6. Finish as in recipe for making and freezing Cream
