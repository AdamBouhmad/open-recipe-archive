## Ingredients

- 1 Spring Chicken
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Lard
- Salt and Pepper
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1/2 Pint of Milk

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the chicken at the joints
2. Dredge well with salt and pepper, and a little flour
3. Put the lard into the frying pan, and, when hot, add the chicken, letting it fry slowly for three-quarters of an hour until done
4. Be careful not to burn
5. When done, arrange the pieces on a hot dish
6. Pour off all the fat that remains in the frying pan but one tablespoonful
7. Add to this a tablespoonful of sifted flour
8. Mix thoroughly and then pour in a half pint of rich cream or milk
9. Season well with salt and pepper, let it come to a slight boil and pour over the chicken and serve
