## Ingredients

- 6 Eggs
- 3/4 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Pint Flour
- 1 Pint Milk
- 1 Grated Lemon
- 1 Pint of Rich Cream
- A Pinch of Salt

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and the grated lemon.
2. Beat the yolks of the eggs well, and add to the mixture.
3. Then add the flour, well-sifted; beat light, and add one pint of milk and one pint of rich cream.
4. Beat the whites to a stiff froth and add.
5. Bake a half hour in a buttered dish.
6. Serve with Lemon Sauce.
