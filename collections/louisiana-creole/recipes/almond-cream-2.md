## Ingredients

- 1 quart new cream
- 1/2 nutmeg grated
- 1 blade of mace
- a bit of lemon peel
- 1 lb. almonds
- a spoonful of rose or orange flower water
- whites of 9 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil the cream with nutmeg grated, a blade of mace, a bit of lemon peel, and sweeten to the taste
2. Blanch 1 lb. of almonds
3. Beat them very fine with a spoonful of rose or orange flower water
4. Take the whites of 9 eggs well beaten and strain them to the almonds
5. Beat them together
6. Rub them very well through a coarse hair sieve
7. Mix all together with the cream and set on the fire
8. Stir it one way all the time till it boils
9. Pour it into cups or dishes, and when cold serve it up
