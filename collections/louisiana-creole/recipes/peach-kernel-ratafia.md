## Ingredients

- ½ Pound Each of Peach or Apricot Kernels
- 4 Pints of Brandy
- 2½ Pounds of Sugar
- 2 Pints of Water

## Directions

1. Pound the peach or apricot kernels — some also pound the peach stones — steep them for one whole month in four pints of brandy in an earthen jar.
2. At the end of that time add a syrup made of two and a half pounds of sugar and two pints of water.
3. Mix all well together, and then filter as directed above, and bottle and seal, and keep in a cool, shady place.
