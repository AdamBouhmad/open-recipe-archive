## Ingredients

- butter
- sponge cake
- citron
- raisins
- currants
- spice
- 1 quart of milk
- 8 eggs
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Butter a mould, and place a layer of sponge cake all around it, then citron, raisins, currants, and any spice you like
2. then add more cake and more fruit until the mould is filled
3. boil 1 quart of milk, and pour it hot on 8 well beaten eggs
4. then pour all into the mould
5. tie it in a cloth, boil it 2 hours, and serve it with cold sauce of butter and sugar, creamed well together
