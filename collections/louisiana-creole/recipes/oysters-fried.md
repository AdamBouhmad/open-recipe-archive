## Ingredients

- large oysters
- a cup of lard
- wheat flour
- rolled cracker
- beaten egg
- mushroom catsup
- Harvey’s or Worcestershire sauce

## Directions

1. Take large oysters from their own liquor
2. Dry and lay them in a towel till you heat
3. Very hot, a cup of lard in a thick-bottomed pan
4. Dip each oyster in wheat flour, or rolled cracker, until it will hold no more
5. Then lay it in the pan
6. The fire must be moderate, or the oysters will scorch before cooking through
7. They will brown on one side in five minutes, then turn them
8. Oysters may be dipped in beaten egg and rolled cracker, and then fried
