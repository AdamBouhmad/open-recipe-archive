## Ingredients

- red cabbage
- salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of whole black pepper
- allspice
- cloves
- cinnamon
- 2 quarts of vinegar

## Directions

1. Lay slices of red cabbage into a jar, and strew salt over each layer
2. Put 2 tablespoonfuls of whole black pepper, allspice, cloves and cinnamon in a bag, and scald them in 2 quarts of vinegar
3. Pour the vinegar over the cabbage, and cover it tight
4. It will be fit for use in 3 days
