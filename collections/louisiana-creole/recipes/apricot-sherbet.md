## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pint of Apricot Juice
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Quart of Water
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- about one dozen apricots

## Directions

1. Put the sugar and water to boil
2. Pare the apricots, press them through a sieve, and add to the boiling syrup
3. Let all boil for five or ten minutes
4. Press through a sieve again
5. Add the lemon juice, mix well and set to freeze as in general directions for freezing Ice Cream
