## Ingredients

- coffee, ground
- boiling water
- cream, fresh and sweet
- milk

## Directions

1. Proceed in the same manner as in the making of Cafe Noir, allowing the usual quantity of boiling water to the amount of coffee used.
2. When made, pour the coffee into delicate china cups, allowing a half cup of coffee to each cup.
3. Serve, at the same time, a small pitcher of very sweet and fresh cream, allowing a half cup of cream to a half cup of coffee.
4. The milk should always be boiled, and the cream very hot.
