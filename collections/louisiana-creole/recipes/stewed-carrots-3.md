## Ingredients

- carrots
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- fresh butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Half boil the carrots
2. Scrape them nicely, and cut them into thick slices
3. Put them into a stewpan with as much milk as will barely cover them, a very little salt and pepper, and a sprig or two of chopped parsley
4. Simmer them till perfectly tender
5. When nearly done, add a piece of fresh butter rolled in flour
6. Send them to table hot
