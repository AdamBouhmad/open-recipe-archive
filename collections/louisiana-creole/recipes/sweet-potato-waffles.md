## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls mashed sweet potato
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- 1 pint milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls wheat flour

## Directions

1. Mix all well together
2. Bake in a waffle iron
