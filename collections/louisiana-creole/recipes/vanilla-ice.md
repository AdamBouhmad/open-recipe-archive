## Ingredients

- Two pints of milk
- 8 ounces of cream
- 12 ounces of sugar
- 4 grains of vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat vanilla with a little sugar in a marble mortar till it becomes powdered
2. Put it in a stewpan or skillet with the milk, cream and sugar
3. Let them boil until the whole is sufficiently thick
4. Strain it through a cloth and pour it into a bowl to cool
