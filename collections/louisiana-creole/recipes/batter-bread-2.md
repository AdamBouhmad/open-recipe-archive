## Ingredients

- 6 spoonfuls of flour
- 3 spoonfuls of cornmeal
- a little salt
- eggs
- a sufficient quantity of rich milk

## Directions

1. Sift together the flour, cornmeal, and salt
2. Make a thin batter with flour, eggs, and a sufficient quantity of rich milk
3. Beat the yolks and whites of the eggs separately
4. Bake it in little tin moulds in a quick oven
