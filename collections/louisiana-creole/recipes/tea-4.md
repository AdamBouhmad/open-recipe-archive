## Ingredients

- Tea
- Fresh boiling water
- Cream or milk
- Sugar

## Directions

1. First see that the teapot is perfectly clean
2. Pour boiling water into it, and let it stand for five minutes, so that the metal or earthen urn may become thoroughly heated
3. Throw out this water, and drain the urn well
4. Allow a teaspoonful of tea for each cup of boiling water, or, if you desire stronger tea, allow two teaspoonfuls
5. Put this tea into the hot pot, and pour over one pint of boiling water, if you wish to make a quart, or, according to the quantity desired, one-half a cup of water to each cup
6. Let this solution draw for five minutes in front, not on top, of the stove or range
7. After five minutes add the other pint of water, or water in proportion to the number of cups desired, allowing a half cup more for each person
8. Serve at once, very hot
9. Put the cream or milk or sugar into the cups
10. Pour a little hot water into each cup to warm it, and then empty before putting in the milk and tea
11. Pour the tea through a strainer, so as to avoid the possibility of leaves or dregs passing into the cup
