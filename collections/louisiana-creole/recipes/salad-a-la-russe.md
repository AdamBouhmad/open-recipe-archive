## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- snap beans
- water
- salt
- butter
- black pepper
- vinegar
- green peas
- artichoke hearts
- asparagus
- mayonnaise

## Directions

1. Cut up all kinds of vegetables, such as carrots, turnips, snap beans, etc.
2. Boil them in water with salt and butter, then drain and season lightly with salt, black pepper and vinegar.
3. Add a few cooked green peas, mashed and well drained.
4. Put all in a salad dish in the form of a pyramid, and lightly cover it over with mayonnaise.
5. If you have the hearts of artichokes put them around the dish, as a wreath, with a little asparagus mixed in.
6. Keep as cool as possible until served.
