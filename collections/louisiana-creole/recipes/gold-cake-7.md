## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 Cups of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 1/4 Cup of Butter
- 3 Cups of Flour
- The Yolks of 6 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Add the sugar and beat till very light
3. Add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs and beat vigorously till light
4. Gradually add the flour, into which you will have sifted the baking powder
5. Bake for forty-five minutes in a moderate oven
