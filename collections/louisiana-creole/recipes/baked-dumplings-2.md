## Ingredients

- 8 Large, Fine, Rosy Apples
- A Plain Paste
- Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare a Plain Paste and cut into squares.
2. Proceed to prepare the dumplings in exactly the same manner as in the recipe for Boiled Dumplings.
3. Instead of boiling the dumplings, set in the oven in a baking dish and bake to a delicate brown.
4. Serve with a Hard, Cream or Brandy Sauce.
