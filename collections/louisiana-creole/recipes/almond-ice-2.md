## Ingredients

- 2 pints of milk
- 8 ounces of cream
- 2 ounces of orange flower water
- 8 ounces of sweet almonds
- 4 ounces of bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Pound all in a marble mortar, pouring in from time to time a few drops of water
2. When thoroughly pounded, add the orange flower water and half the milk
3. Pass this, tightly squeezed, through a cloth
4. Boil the rest of the milk with the cream and keep stirring it with a wooden spoon
5. As soon as it is thick enough, pour in the almond milk
6. Give it 1 boiling, take it off and let it cool in a bowl or pitcher
7. Pour it into the mould for freezing
