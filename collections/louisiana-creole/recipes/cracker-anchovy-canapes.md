## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Soda Crackers or Butter Crackers
- 2 Dozen Anchovies
- 1 Ounce of Anchovy Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the Anchovies into halves
2. Butter one side of six crackers with the Anchovy butter
3. Lay on this four Anchovies sliced in half
4. Cover each with a cracker
5. Place on a folded napkin on a dish and serve
6. A very pretty conceit at luncheons is to tie the crackers across and around with narrow green ribbon, making a delicate knot in the center
