## Ingredients

- 3 heads of celery
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 ounces of Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Batter

## Directions

1. Cut the celery into stalks three inches long.
2. Tie the stalks in bundles allowing three to a bundle.
3. Boil till tender in salted water, then take out.
4. Remove the strings and drain.
5. Season with salt and pepper, and the grated Parmesan cheese.
6. Dip in batter and fry and serve as a vegetable.
