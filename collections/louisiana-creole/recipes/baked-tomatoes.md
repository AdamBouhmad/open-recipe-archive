## Ingredients

- dozen or more fine, ripe tomatoes
- butter
- small pieces of bread
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Scald and peel 1 dozen or more fine, ripe tomatoes
2. Butter a shallow baking dish, and put in the finest without breaking them
3. Fill up the space between with small pieces of bread well buttered
4. Mash the rest of the tomatoes, and strain out all the hard parts
5. Mix with a spoonful of butter, pepper and salt
6. Pour it over the dish, and strew bread crumbs on the top
7. Bake about ¼ an hour
