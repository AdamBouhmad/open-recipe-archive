## Ingredients

- a box of Cox's gelatine
- one quart of boiling water
- a teaspoonful of tartaric acid
- four cups of sugar
- the beaten whites of three eggs
- a tablespoonful of good extract of lemon

## Directions

1. Take a box of Cox's gelatine, pour over it one quart of boiling water, and stir until it is dissolved
2. Add a teaspoonful of tartaric acid, and four cups of sugar
3. let it dissolve and bring it to a boil
4. while boiling, stir in the beaten whites of three eggs
5. let this boil up once again and take it off the fire
6. when nearly cool, add to it a tablespoonful of good extract of lemon
7. Strain the mixture into moulds or cups, and set it in a cool place, or on ice, to become firm
