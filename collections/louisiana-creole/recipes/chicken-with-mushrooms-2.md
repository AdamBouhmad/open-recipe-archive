## Ingredients

- 1 Fine Spring Chicken
- 1/2 Can of Mushrooms
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Chopped Onions
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Glass of Madeira or Sherry Wine
- 1/8 Square Inch of Ham to Season
- Thyme, Parsley, Bay Leaf

## Directions

1. Cut into joints and season a nicely cleaned chicken.
2. Put it in a saucepan with two tablespoonfuls of butter, and let it simmer for about ten minutes, browning slightly.
3. Add two nicely-chopped onions, and let these brown slightly.
4. Add one-half of a square inch of ham, chopped very fine indeed.
5. Add thyme, parsley and bay leaf, following carefully the order given in adding the ingredients.
6. A minute later add the garlic, which has been minced very fine.
7. Let all brown together for ten minutes.
8. Cut the mushrooms into halves, put them with their water into the pot, stirring well.
9. Let them simmer five minutes.
10. Add a wineglass of Sherry or Madeira, stir and cover the pot closely, so that it can smother well.
11. If the sauce appears too thick add about a half cup of broth or boiling water.
12. Season to taste, and let all cook very slowly for an hour longer over a steady fire.
