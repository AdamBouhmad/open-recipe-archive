## Ingredients

- four cabbage heads
- strong brine
- clear water
- strong vinegar
- powdered turmeric
- five pounds of sugar
- three ounces of turmeric
- two ounces of ginger
- four ounces of horseradish
- two ounces of white mustard seed
- one-half ounce of celery seed
- quarter of an ounce of mace
- two ounces of whole pepper
- four ounces of garlic

## Directions

1. Cut four cabbage heads into eighths, if large, or quarters, if small
2. Soak it in strong brine for three days
3. Scald it in clear water until you can pierce it with a straw
4. Take it out and dry it on large dishes for twenty-four hours
5. Put it into strong vinegar, with powdered turmeric, sufficient to color the cabbage yellow
6. Let it remain in this vinegar ten days
7. Take it out and drain on a sieve for several hours
8. Pack in a jar alternately one layer of cabbage and one of spices
9. For each gallon of vinegar allow five pounds of sugar, three ounces of turmeric, two of ginger, four of horseradish, two of white mustard seed, one-half ounce of celery seed, quarter of an ounce of mace, two ounces of whole pepper, white if you can get it, and four ounces of garlic
10. Scald the vinegar and sugar together
11. Pour hot on the cabbage and the spices
12. Cover tight
