## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Flour
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1/2 Cake of Compressed Yeast, or 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Large Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the Muffin batter as above and set to rise over night.
2. In the morning prepare the eggs as above indicated.
3. Blend and beat till light.
4. Then turn the muffin batter into greased muffin rings, and bake about twenty minutes in a hot oven.
5. The Muffins must be eaten hot.
