## Ingredients

- 6 Artichokes
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter

## Directions

1. Pluck off the coarse scales of the artichokes, and then throw the vegetable into cold water.
2. Let it stand for an hour.
3. Then drain.
4. Cut the meat into delicate slices, and fry in butter, just as you would potato chips.
5. Serve with Filet of Beef, Veal Saute, Smothered Chicken, etc.
