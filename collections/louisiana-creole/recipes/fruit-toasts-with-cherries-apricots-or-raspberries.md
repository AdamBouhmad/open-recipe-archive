## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of French Toast
- 1 Cup of Fruit Marmalade
- Cream to Serve

## Directions

1. Prepare thin slices of very dry French toast, and butter generously.
2. Take a dish, and lay on each slice of toast generous allotments of the fruits you wish to serve.
3. Pour all remains of syrup over the toast, and serve with cream.
