## Ingredients

- The Juice of 1 Large Louisiana Orange
- The Juice of 4 Lemons
- ¾ Pound of Pulverized Sugar
- ½ Pint of Water
- 1 Gill of Kirsch
- 1 Gill of Fine Rum
- 1 Gill of Champagne

## Directions

1. Put the sugar into a bowl and squeeze over the juices of the orange and lemons
2. Mix well till all the sugar is impregnated with the juices
3. Add a half-pint of cold water, and then add the Kirsch, and stir for five or six minutes
4. Strain all through a fine sieve into the ice cream freezer
5. Remove the sieve and pour into the freezer the rum and champagne
6. Cover well, and let the mixture freeze for about five minutes
7. Remove the cover and detach the mixture from the sides and bottom, and cover again
