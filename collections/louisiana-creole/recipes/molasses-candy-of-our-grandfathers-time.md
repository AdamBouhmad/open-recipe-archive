## Ingredients

- One quart of molasses
- butter the size of an egg
- one teaspoonful of essence of wintergreen

## Directions

1. Stew over a brisk fire till it will harden on being dropped into cold water
2. Add essence of wintergreen when it is almost done
3. Pull it while warm, with buttered hands
4. Cut in sticks
