## Ingredients

- sliced red fish of from 2 to 3 or 4 pounds
- butter
- flour
- boiling vinegar
- wine
- salt
- pepper
- laurel leaf
- 2 slices of lemon

## Directions

1. Rub the fish well with butter and roll in flour
2. Pour over it a large wine-glass of boiling vinegar
3. Place the fish in a stewpan with wine enough to cover it
4. Add salt, pepper, a laurel leaf or two, and 2 slices of lemon
5. Let all simmer gently
6. Remove the fish and strain the liquor
7. Pour it over the fish and serve with dish garnished with slices of lemon
