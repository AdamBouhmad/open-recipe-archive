## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Chestnuts
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Shell the chestnuts
2. Throw them into a saucepan of boiling water for twenty minutes
3. Take them out and remove the dark outer skin
4. Remove the boiling water in the saucepan and add the chestnuts
5. Let them cook for twenty minutes more, or until they may be easily pierced with a fork
6. Take them out and drain
7. Put a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan
8. Add the flour and blend gradually, without browning
9. Add the pint of milk and then add the chestnuts
10. Let all cook for fifteen minutes longer
11. Season to taste
