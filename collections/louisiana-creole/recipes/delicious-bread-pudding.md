## Ingredients

- bread slices
- butter
- currants
- citron
- a quart of milk
- four eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Butter some slices of bread, cut thin, and lay them in a dish, with currants and citron between
2. Pour over it a quart of milk, with four well-beaten eggs, and sugar sufficient to sweeten to taste
3. Bake
4. Serve with sauce
