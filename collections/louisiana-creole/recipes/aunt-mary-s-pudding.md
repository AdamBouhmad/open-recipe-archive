## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of stoned raisins
- currants nicely washed
- suet finely minced
- bread and apples grated
- brown sugar, 1/4 lb. of each
- 4 well beaten eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of pounded ginger
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/4 nutmeg grated
- 1 wineglass of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix 1 lb. of stoned raisins, currants nicely washed, suet finely minced, bread and apples grated, and brown sugar, 1/4 lb. of each
2. Add 4 well beaten eggs
3. Add 1 teaspoonful of pounded ginger, 1/2 teaspoonful of salt, 1/4 nutmeg grated, and 1 wineglass of brandy
4. Mix all the ingredients well, and boil it in a cloth for 2 hours
5. Serve with a sauce of melted butter, a glass of wine, and sugar to the taste
