## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Plain Tripe
- 2 Potatoes
- 1 Pound of Honeycomb Tripe
- Sprig of Parsley
- 1 Knuckle of Veal
- 3 Quarts of Cold Water
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- 1 Onion
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Flour
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Cayenne to Suit the Taste
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. The knuckle of the veal is best for this. Wash and put into the soup kettle, covering with water and bring it to a slow boil.
2. Carefully skim off the scum.
3. Let it simmer gently for three hours.
4. The tripe should be prepared the way before. Wash it thoroughly in cold water and boil for about seven hours.
5. Put away in the ice-box till needed.
6. Chop the parsley and herbs fine and one-half of the dried pepper pod, and add to the boiling knuckle of veal, and also the potatoes, which have been cut into dice.
7. Cut up the tripe into pieces of about one inch square.
8. Take out the knuckle of veal and cut any meat into small pieces, and add all with the tripe to the soup.
9. At the boiling point, season with salt and pepper.
10. Throw croutons into the soup, let it simmer for about ten minutes more and serve.
