## Ingredients

- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Pound of White Sugar
- 1 Gill of Sherry or Madeira Wine

## Directions

1. Proceed exactly as in Lemon or Orange Icing, only adding instead one gill of good Sherry or Madeira wine
2. Then proceed as in Plain Icing
