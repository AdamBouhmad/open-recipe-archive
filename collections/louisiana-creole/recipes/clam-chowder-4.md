## Ingredients

- 50 to 100 small clams
- water
- salt pork
- biscuits
- milk or warm water

## Directions

1. Put into boiling water from 50 to 100 small clams
2. When all their shells have opened, take them out and extract all the hard part and throw it away
3. Slice thin as much salt pork as will produce half a pint of liquid or gravy
4. Take out all the pork, leaving the liquid in the pot
5. Add to it a layer of clams, then a layer of biscuits soaked in milk or warm water
6. Next another layer of clams
