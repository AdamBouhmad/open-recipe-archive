## Ingredients

- corn from the cob
- eggs
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Cut as much corn from the cob as you require; a dozen ears make a large pudding
2. To every three ears allow an egg, a spoonful of butter, a little pepper and salt, to suit your taste
3. Fill and cover it with sweet milk
4. Let this bake an hour
