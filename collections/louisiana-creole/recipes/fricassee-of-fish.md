## Ingredients

- large fish
- fried onions
- parsley
- tomatoes
- a little garlic

## Directions

1. All large fish make nice fricassee.
2. Cut the fish into slices and lay it in a gravy made of fried onions, parsley, tomatoes and a little garlic;
3. fry it in butter and serve.
4. Add sauce if liked.
