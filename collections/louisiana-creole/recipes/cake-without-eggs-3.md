## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Sifted Flour
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 1 1/4 Cups of Sugar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful Baking Powder
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon Extract

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and the butter to a cream, and then add the milk, stirring carefully.
2. Add the flour, and beat well and hard.
3. Add the salt, flavoring extract and baking powder.
4. Mix all thoroughly, and bake in a moderate oven for a half hour.
