## Ingredients

- 6 Turnips
- Drawn Butter Sauce
- Pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Boil the turnips whole, selecting very tender ones.
2. They will require about an hour of boiling.
3. Always use hot water in putting on the turnips.
4. Drain off water, when tender, and serve with a Drawn Butter Sauce, pepper and salt.
5. Serve with roast beef, mutton or roast duck.
