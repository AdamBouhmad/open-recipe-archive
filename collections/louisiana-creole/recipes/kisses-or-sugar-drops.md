## Ingredients

- half a cup of butter
- one cup of sugar
- three well-beaten eggs
- half a pound of sifted flour
- half a grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Rub to a cream half a cup of butter, with one cup of sugar
2. Add three well-beaten eggs, half a pound of sifted flour, and half a grated nutmeg
3. Drop this mixture on buttered tins, by the spoonful; let them be two or three inches apart
4. Sprinkle sugar over them
5. Bake quickly
