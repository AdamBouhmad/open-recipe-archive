## Ingredients

- 4 stalks of celery
- 2 hard-boiled eggs
- 1 onion
- Salt and pepper
- 1 tablespoonful vinegar
- Parsley
- Tarragon vinegar and oil

## Directions

1. Wash and scrape the celery well, and then chop it fine.
2. Chop an onion very fine, and also several sprigs of parsley.
3. Take a hard-boiled egg and cut fine.
4. Mix all these together, pour over a little tarragon vinegar and oil, if desired, and serve as a salad.
