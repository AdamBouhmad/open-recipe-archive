## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- 1 lb. of brown sugar to 2 lbs. of tomatoes
- 1 pint of vinegar to 1 lb. of tomatoes
- cinnamon
- allspice
- mace
- cloves
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the tomatoes
2. Put 1 lb. of brown sugar to 2 lbs. of tomatoes
3. Sprinkle it on, and let it stand 2 hours
4. Boil them till the scum ceases to rise, skimming it off as fast as it rises
5. Add 1 pint of vinegar to 1 lb. of tomatoes
6. Add cinnamon, allspice, mace, cloves and black pepper
7. Boil them slowly until transparent
8. Be very careful to take off the scum
