## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful and a half of butter
- 1 tablespoonful and a half of flour
- Half a cup of water or white broth
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoonful and a half of finely minced parsley
- 1 tablespoonful of cream

## Directions

1. To the recipe for white sauce add one tablespoonful and a half of finely minced parsley
2. You may also add a tablespoonful of cream
3. This is nice with boiled fish or boiled chicken
