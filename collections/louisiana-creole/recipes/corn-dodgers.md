## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Indian Meal
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Scald the meal with boiling water.
2. Add the melted lard and the salt.
3. Use sufficient boiling water to make a very stiff batter or soft dough.
4. Then take up a handful of the mixture, and mold it with your hands into oval mounds, tossing the cake of dough lightly between your hands in a dexterous manner, and leaving the impression of your fingers across.
5. Bake the pones thus formed in a quick oven.
