## Ingredients

- macaroons
- dried cherries
- seedless raisins
- Madeira or sherry wine
- a pint of milk
- two eggs
- flavoring
- half a pound of white sugar
- butter and sugar sauce

## Directions

1. Line a mould with macaroons, as described for Iced Cabinet Pudding.
2. Fill the mould with dried cherries, seedless raisins and macaroons, in layers.
3. Pour a little Madeira or sherry wine over them.
4. Pour over all a custard made of a pint of milk, two eggs and flavoring, sweetened with half a pound of white sugar.
5. In summer cover the mould up in ice and salt until wanted.
6. In winter steam it and serve with butter and sugar sauce.
