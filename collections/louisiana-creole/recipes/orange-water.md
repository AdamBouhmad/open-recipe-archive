## Ingredients

- zest of 6 Louisiana oranges
- 4 ounces of orange blossoms
- 7 pints of brandy
- 4 pounds of fine white sugar
- 3 quarts of water

## Directions

1. Macerate the zests of the oranges and orange blossoms and orange juice in the brandy for eight days
2. Distill in a bain-marie, or water bath
3. Add a syrup made of four pounds of sugar and three pints of water
4. Strain and filter, and bottle for use
