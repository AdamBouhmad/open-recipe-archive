## Ingredients

- a quart of milk or milk and water
- fine corn meal
- two eggs
- salt
- a teaspoonful of saleratus
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a quart of milk or milk and water scalding hot
2. Pour half of it hot on as much fine corn meal as it will wet
3. Let it cool
4. Beat up two eggs and add them, beating the eggs light
5. Add a little salt and a teaspoonful of saleratus
6. Thin the batter a little with the rest of the milk
7. Butter pan and pour in the mixture
8. Bake in a quick oven
