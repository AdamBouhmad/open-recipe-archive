## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 Oysters to Each Person
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 6 or 8 Pieces of Buttered Toast or Milk Toast
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Melted Butter
- Chopped Parsley to Garnish

## Directions

1. Broil the oysters according to the recipe given above.
2. Have ready a heated dish; sprinkle the oysters with salt and pepper and pour over melted butter.
3. Serve on small pieces of buttered toast, or milk toast.
4. Sprinkle with finely-chopped parsley.
