## Ingredients

- shrimps
- salt water
- strong vinegar
- water
- allspice
- sweet oil
- brandy

## Directions

1. Pick the shrimps and rinse them well in salt and water
2. Take two parts of strong vinegar and one of water, add a few allspice, and boil this pickle
3. Pour it hot over the shrimps
4. If the shrimps are to be sent to a distance, boil the pickle again, adding a little more vinegar with a few tablespoonfuls of sweet oil
5. Ensure they are entirely covered with the pickle
6. Place a paper wet with brandy over the top
