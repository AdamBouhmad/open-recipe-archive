## Ingredients

- crust
- nice fat salt pork
- apples
- allspice
- pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make the crust in the usual manner
2. Spread it over a deep plate
3. Cut nice fat salt pork very thin, and slice some apples
4. Place a layer of apples, then a layer of pork
5. Sprinkle with allspice, pepper, and sugar between each layer
6. Have three or four layers, and let the last one be apples
7. Sprinkle in sugar and spice
8. Cover with a top crust, and bake an hour
