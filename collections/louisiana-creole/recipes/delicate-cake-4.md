## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 Cups of Flour
- 1 3/4 Cups of Sugar
- 3/4 Cup of Cornstarch, Sifted With the Flour
- 1/2 Cup of Butter
- 3/4 Cup of Milk
- Whites of 6 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls Baking Powder
- 3/4 Teaspoonful of Extract of Lemon
- 1/4 Teaspoonful Salt

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and beat it and the sugar to a cream.
2. Add the milk, and stir carefully.
3. Add gradually the flour, into which has been sifted the salt and the cornstarch, and mix thoroughly.
4. Add the flavoring extract, and, lastly, the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
5. Sift the baking powder over the whites.
6. Mix quickly and lightly with the batter, and bake forty-five minutes in a moderate oven.
