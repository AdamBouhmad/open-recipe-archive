## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of flour
- 3/4 lb. of sugar
- 3 eggs, leaving out the yolk of one
- 1 egg yolk
- rose water
- mace
- pounded almonds
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Mix butter, flour, sugar, and eggs together in a stiff paste
2. Roll it out and cut it into strips
3. Mix the yolk of 1 egg with rose water
4. Spread it on the top of the cakes
5. Strew mace with pounded sugar and almonds on the top of the cakes
6. Bake them on tin sheets
