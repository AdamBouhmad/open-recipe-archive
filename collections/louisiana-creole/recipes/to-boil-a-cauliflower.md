## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- water
- drawn butter

## Directions

1. Let the cauliflower lie a short time in salt and water
2. Put it into boiling water with a handful of salt
3. Keep the pot uncovered and skim the water well
4. A small cauliflower will require 15 minutes to boil
5. A large one will require 20 minutes to boil
6. Dress with drawn butter
