## Ingredients

- The Filet or Part of Lower Shoulder Blade of Veal
- 6 Peppercorns
- 2 Calves' Feet
- 1 Blade of Mace
- 2 Large Onions
- 2 Carrots
- 1 Gill of French Vinegar
- 1 Dozen Cloves Mashed Fine
- 1 Allspice Mashed Fine
- 3 Quarts of Beef Broth
- Pepper, Cayenne and Salt to Taste

## Directions

1. Cut the veal into fine pieces, season well, and put it in a kettle with the calves' feet
2. Season highly with pepper, Cayenne and salt to taste
3. Add three quarts of beef broth, or pot-au-feu
4. Add the minced vegetables, herbs and the peppercorns
5. Let it boil gently until it forms a jelly, which will be in about two and a half hours
6. Take out the veal and calves' feet, and carefully remove all the bones, if any, and place in a mold
7. Let the liquor in which it was boiled boil until it is reduced to about a quart, adding, in the meantime, the good vinegar
8. When reduced, pour over the meat and set it in a cold place over night
9. When cold, turn out of the mold and garnish nicely with sliced lemon and parsley sprigs and serve in slices
