## Ingredients

- 3 Eggs
- 1 Pound of White Pulverized Sugar
- 3/4 Pound of Flour
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Extract
- 1 Cup of Currant or Other Jelly for Filling
- Transparent Icing to Garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare a sponge or cup cake batter.
2. Have ready a baking sheet, which you will cover with white writing paper.
3. Lay the batter out on the paper by forcing it through a biscuit cutter in rounds that will measure one inch and a half in diameter.
4. Dredge the surface with white sugar very lightly.
5. Bake them for about fifteen minutes or less, until they are a light color.
6. Trim nicely and place them upon a wire drainer.
7. Place circular pieces of currant or other preserves on top.
8. Garnish with a meringue or Transparent Icing.
9. Set in the stove or the sun to dry, and in ten minutes they will be ready to serve.
