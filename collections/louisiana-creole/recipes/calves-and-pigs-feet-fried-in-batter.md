## Ingredients

- calves' or pigs' feet
- flour
- eggs
- milk
- salt
- pepper
- hot lard
- toast

## Directions

1. Wash and cook the feet tender, the day before using.
2. When wanted, wash and roll them in a little flour to dry.
3. Make a batter of flour, eggs, milk, and a little salt and pepper (one egg is sufficient to two feet).
4. Take out the largest bones and roll the feet in batter, or lay them in a pan with hot lard, and pour the batter over them.
5. Fry a delicate brown and serve on toast.
