## Ingredients

- two pints of sugar
- two cups of butter
- two cups of cold water
- whites of eighteen eggs
- four pints of flour
- two teaspoonfuls of yeast powder
- almond flavoring

## Directions

1. cream the butter and sugar together
2. Add two cups of cold water
3. beat to a froth the whites of eighteen eggs, mix them with the butter and sugar
4. mix flour and yeast powder together, and stir gently into the batter
5. Flavor with almond
