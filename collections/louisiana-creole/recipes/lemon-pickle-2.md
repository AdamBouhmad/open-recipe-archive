## Ingredients

- 12 lemons
- 2 lbs. of salt
- 8 or 9 cloves of garlic
- mace
- nutmeg
- cayenne
- allspice
- ½ ounce of each spice
- ½ lb. of flour of mustard
- 1½ gallon of good vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the lemons into 6 pieces each
2. Put on them salt, garlic, mace, nutmeg, cayenne, allspice and flour of mustard
3. Add vinegar
4. Boil the whole for ½ hour
5. Put it in a jar, and set it by for 8 weeks, stirring it every day
6. After which, pour it into small bottles, and close them very tight
