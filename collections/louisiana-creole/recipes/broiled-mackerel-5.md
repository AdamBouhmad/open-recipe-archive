## Ingredients

- 2 salt mackerel
- 1 cup of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- Black pepper
- Sliced lemon
- Olives
- Parsley sprigs
- Cream sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the mackerel and wash thoroughly as directed above, only using boiling water.
2. Have ready some milk, seasoned well with black pepper and soak the mackerel in the milk until thoroughly impregnated.
3. Take out and wipe dry with a towel.
4. Dredge the mackerel with butter, and place between a double broiler, over a slow fire, broiling about fifteen or twenty minutes, the under side being allowed to broil first.
5. When done, take off and pour over melted butter and chopped parsley.
6. Garnish the dish with sprigs of parsley, sliced lemons and olives, and serve hot.
