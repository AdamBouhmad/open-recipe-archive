## Ingredients

- onions (six large)
- butter (two large spoonfuls)
- pepper
- salt
- mushroom catsup
- vinegar (a cup)
- wine (a cup)

## Directions

1. Boil six large onions; change the water two or three times, while it is boiling, which takes away the strong taste.
2. When soft, chop and put them in a saucepan with two large spoonfuls of butter, a little pepper and salt;
3. now add either mushroom catsup, a cup of vinegar or a cup of wine, whichever is preferred.
