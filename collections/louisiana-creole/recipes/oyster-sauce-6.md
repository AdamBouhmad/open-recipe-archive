## Ingredients

- oysters
- oyster liquor
- 3 or 4 blades of mace
- melted butter
- thick cream or rich milk

## Directions

1. When the oysters are opened, take care of all the liquor, and boil them in it
2. take the oysters out and put to the liquor 3 or 4 blades of mace
3. add to it some melted butter, with thick cream, or rich milk
4. put in the oysters and give them a boil
5. when that is done take them off the fire
