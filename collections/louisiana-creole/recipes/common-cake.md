## Ingredients

- 1 lb. and a half of flour
- 1 lb. of good brown sugar
- 8 well beaten eggs
- 1 ounce of carraway seeds
- 1 tablespoonful of fresh yeast
- 1 tablespoonful of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of water

## Directions

1. Mix flour, sugar, eggs, and carraway seeds together
2. Add the fresh yeast, milk, and water
3. Let it stand a short time
4. Bake it in a floured tin
