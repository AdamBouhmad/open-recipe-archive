## Ingredients

- 3 Dozen Fine, Large, Fat Oysters
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Buttered Toast

## Directions

1. Clean the oyster shells thoroughly, set them on the top of the stove or place in a baking pan until the shell is easily removed
2. Remove the flat outer shell
3. Butter the oyster in the deep shell and serve very hot with salt and pepper
4. As the shells open put in a little butter
