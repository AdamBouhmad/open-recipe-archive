## Ingredients

- 1 Filet of Beef
- 1 Dozen Uniform-Sized Tomatoes
- 1 Cup of Mushrooms
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- ¾ Cup of Stale Bread Crumbs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Grated Onion
- Sprigs of Chopped Parsley
- Salt and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Smother the filet according to the directions given above, adding two chopped tomatoes to the sauce
2. Take the tomatoes and cut off the stem end, scoop out the soft inside, being careful to retain the skins in proper shape
3. Take a half cup of mushrooms, one-half cup of stale bread crumbs which have been wet and squeezed, one clove of garlic chopped very fine, one grated onion, and a sprig of chopped parsley
4. Chop the mushrooms fine
5. Place a tablespoonful of butter into a frying pan, and when melted, add the bread crumbs which have been seasoned with salt and pepper and Cayenne, and mixed thoroughly with the chopped onion or garlic and the parsley
6. When these begin to fry, add the chopped mushrooms, stirring constantly for about five or eight minutes
7. Serve with the Filet and Stuffed Tomatoes
