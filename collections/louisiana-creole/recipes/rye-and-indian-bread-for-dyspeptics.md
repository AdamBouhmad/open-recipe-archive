## Ingredients

- a pint of rye flour
- a pint of Indian meal
- a cup of boiling water
- a cup of yeast
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Scald the Indian meal with a cup of boiling water
2. When lukewarm, mix in the rye flour and a cup of yeast
3. Add a little salt
4. Knead it as for other bread
5. Bake for two hours
