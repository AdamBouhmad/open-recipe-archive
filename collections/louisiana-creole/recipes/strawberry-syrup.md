## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Strawberry Juice
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup
- 1 Ounce of Raspberry Essence

## Directions

1. Pick, stem and wash the strawberries, then pound and mash them well
2. Let them stand for thirty-six hours in a cool place
3. Strain through a bag
4. Cook the sugar and water till it forms the syrup called Le Petit Cassé, or crack, that is, till it instantly snaps asunder when placed between the fingers, after dipping them in cold water
5. Add the fruit syrups, and mix thoroughly
6. Let it boil for about five minutes
7. Take off the fire, let it get almost cold, and then put up in bottles
