## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream
- 1 Quart of Strawberries or 2 Tablespoonfuls of Strawberry Extract
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 6 Eggs
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Set the milk to boil; take from the fire, add the yolks of the eggs, well beaten to a cream, with half the sugar, and set aside to cool.
2. Then, when cool, add the remaining half of the sugar, which you will have blended with the strawberries, mashed well, and allowed to stand for several hours, and then press through a sieve.
3. Mix well with the milk, add the lemon juice and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth; turn into the freezer, and freeze as above directed.
4. If the strawberries are too pale to impart a sufficiently rosy tinge to the cream add a few drops of Cochineal.
5. When not in season flavor with the Extract.
