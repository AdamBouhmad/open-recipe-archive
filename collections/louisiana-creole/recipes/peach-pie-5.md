## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Large Peaches
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- A Plain Paste

## Directions

1. Pare and cut the peaches into slices
2. Stew the peaches and proceed in exactly the same manner as for Apple Pie
3. Sprinkle the tops of the covers with fine white powdered sugar when ready to serve
