## Ingredients

- bread
- eggs
- butter
- salt
- anchovy paste

## Directions

1. Toast six pieces of bread, shape them round, before browning
2. keep them where they will be hot until you poach the eggs
3. Take a tin dipper, half fill it with boiling water, and drop it gently into the pot again, holding it so that none of the water from the pot can get into the dipper
4. keep it firm by holding it yourself or getting it held for you
5. break a nice fresh egg into the dipper
6. let it stand until the white is firm
7. Lay each egg on one of the slices of toast
8. use butter and salt on the toast for both egg and toast
9. break each egg in this way until your six eggs and six pieces of toast are used
10. butter very freely
11. serve hot
12. Anchovy paste may be spread on the toast before the eggs are put on
