## Ingredients

- 6 Venison Cutlets
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Melted Butter
- Chopped Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Watercress to Garnish

## Directions

1. Trim the cutlets nicely, rub well with salt and pepper, and brush with a little butter.
2. Broil over a quick clear fire, allowing about eight minutes, or less, according to size, to each cutlet.
3. Place in a very hot dish, pour over a little melted butter and chopped parsley.
4. Garnish with watercress, and serve with Currant Jelly.
