## Ingredients

- 1 cup of minced ham
- 3 eggs, beaten with the whites and yolks separate
- 1 teaspoonful of finely chopped parsley
- Pepper to taste
- Cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Mix together the chopped parsley, ham and yolks of eggs and a dash of cayenne pepper.
2. Beat all very hard till it becomes light.
3. Add the whites of the eggs, which have been beaten to a froth.
4. Beat together sufficiently to mix well.
5. Fill a dish and bake in an oven for eight or ten minutes and serve with a cream sauce.
