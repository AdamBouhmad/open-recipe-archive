## Ingredients

- ½ pint rice
- 1½ pints flour
- 1 lb. butter
- 1 salt spoonful salt
- 5 eggs
- 1 quart milk

## Directions

1. Pick and wash ½ pint of rice
2. Boil it very soft
3. Drain it and let it get cold
4. Sift 1½ pints of flour over the pan of rice
5. Mix in 1 lb. of butter that has been warmed by the fire and 1 salt spoonful of salt
6. Add 5 eggs beat very light
7. Stir them gradually into a quart of milk
8. Beat all together very light
9. Bake it in muffin rings or waffle irons
10. Send them to the table very hot
11. Eat them with butter, honey or molasses
