## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Banana Juice
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup
- 1/4 Ounce of Banana Essence
- The Juice of a Lemon

## Directions

1. Peel the bananas, then cut into small pieces and pound and mash well and set aside in a cool place for thirty-six hours.
2. Then strain through a bag and proceed in exactly the same manner as for Apricot Syrup, and add the essence and lemon juice when about to bottle.
