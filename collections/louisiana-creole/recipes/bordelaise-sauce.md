## Ingredients

- 2 Shallots
- 1/2 Glass Claret
- 3/4 Pint of Sauce Espagnole
- A Dash of Red Pepper

## Directions

1. Cut two shallots very fine; put in a saucepan with a half glassful of Claret; reduce one-half.
2. Add three-quarters of a pint of good Sauce Espagnole (see recipe) and a dash of red pepper.
3. Cook for twenty minutes and serve hot.
4. In serving this sauce, the flavor may be increased by adding a dozen round slices of blanched Marrons.
