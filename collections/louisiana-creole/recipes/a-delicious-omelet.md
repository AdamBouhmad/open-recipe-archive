## Ingredients

- six eggs
- a tablespoonful of chopped green onion
- a tablespoonful of parsley, chopped fine
- two tablespoonfuls of water
- salt
- a teaspoonful of nice sweet butter

## Directions

1. Beat separately, and lightly, six eggs
2. add to them a tablespoonful of chopped green onion, and the same of parsley, chopped fine
3. beat them into the eggs with two tablespoonfuls of water, and at the last moment a little salt
4. Have a thick-bottomed skillet or pan on the fire, put in a teaspoonful of nice sweet butter
5. when this is hot put in the eggs
6. Take a broad-bladed knife and keep rolling the omelet as it sets
7. do not let it get too brown, but roll it in an oblong shape
8. never turn an omelet over, but push and roll it as described
9. slide it on a hot dish
10. pour a spoonful of melted butter over it
11. send it to table hot
