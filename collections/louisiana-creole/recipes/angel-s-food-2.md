## Ingredients

- 1 box gelatine
- 1 quart of milk
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 cup of white sugar
- 1 cup sherry wine
- 1 teaspoon of vanilla
- whites of 3 eggs
- 2 1/2 cups of sugar

## Directions

1. Soak 1 box gelatine in 1 quart of milk 1 hour
2. Let it come to a boil
3. Beat together yolks of 3 eggs, 1 cup of white sugar, 1 cup sherry wine, 1 teaspoon of vanilla
4. Add to milk and gelatine
5. Let it boil
6. Add whites of the eggs beaten with 2 1/2 cups of sugar
7. Let it simmer
