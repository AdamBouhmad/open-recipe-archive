## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Good Cream
- 3/4 Cup of Powdered White Sugar
- 1/4 Box of Gelatine
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Sherry Wine
- 1/2 Pound of Lady Fingers

## Directions

1. Line a glass dish with the lady fingers, or with small slices of cake.
2. Soak the gelatine in cold water, just covered, for half an hour.
3. Whip the cream, and then dissolve the soaked gelatine with the Sherry Wine.
4. Add the Vanilla Extract.
5. Add the sugar to the cream carefully, and then strain in the gelatine very carefully.
6. Stir immediately, and then pour the cream over the lady fingers, and set away to harden.
