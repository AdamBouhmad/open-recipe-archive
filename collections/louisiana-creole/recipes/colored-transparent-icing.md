## Ingredients

- ¾ Pound of Fine White Sugar
- Half the White of 1 Egg
- Flavoring and Coloring According to Taste

## Directions

1. For Green Icing, use the Extract of Spinach and flavor with Cedrat
2. For Rose Pink, flavor with the Essential Oil of Roses and add a few drops of Cochineal
3. For Violet Icing, flavor with Syrup of Violet
4. Proceed as in Plain Icing
