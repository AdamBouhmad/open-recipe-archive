## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Prepared Tripe
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Sprig of Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1/2 Clove of Garlic
- 1/2 Pint of Milk
- Chopped Parsley to Garnish
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Take the prepared tripe and cut into strips of about one finger length and a half inch in width.
2. Put a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan; add a finely-sliced onion, a sprig of parsley and a bay leaf, minced.
3. Stir in the melted butter without letting it brown, then add a tablespoonful of flour.
4. Stir well and add a pint of milk.
5. Stir constantly till it comes to a boil, seasoning to the taste with salt and pepper.
6. Then add the well-seasoned tripe and let it cook over a moderate fire for about five minutes.
