## Ingredients

- marrowfat peas
- water
- milk
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil the marrowfat peas in water
2. When soft, pour off the water
3. Fill up with milk, or milk and water
4. Boil a little longer
5. Season with butter, pepper and salt
6. Thicken with a teaspoon of flour stirred in among the peas
