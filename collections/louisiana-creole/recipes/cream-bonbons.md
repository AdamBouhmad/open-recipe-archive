## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of the Finest White Icing Sugar
- 2 Ounces of Gum Arabic
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Gill of Any Essence Desired

## Directions

1. Soak two ounces of the finest Gum Arabic in a gill of hot water
2. Use two pounds of the finest icing sugar, the whites of 2 eggs, and a few drops of essence
3. Strain the soaked gum through a piece of muslin bag into a bowl, and add the essence to it
4. Fill in with as much icing as it will hold, till it forms a stiff, yet elastic body
5. Fill a biscuit forcer with the preparation and push it through upon a sheet of paper, well-dredged with sugar
6. With a small knife cut off the cream, as it is pressed out, into pieces of about the size of a filbert
7. Place them in the baking pan in the oven for a few minutes, merely to dry their surfaces
8. Dip these white balls into the chocolate icing, holding one at a time on the tip of the fork, so as to be able to turn it all around and ice each part
9. Put on a wire tray, set to dry in the oven for about ten minutes
