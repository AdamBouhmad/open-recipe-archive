## Ingredients

- three dozen oysters
- half a pound of butter
- flour
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- a cup of milk or cream
- a cup of oyster juice

## Directions

1. Put three dozen oysters in a stewpan, without their juice, which save
2. Mix with the oysters, half a pound of butter, thickened with flour (work it well with a spoon)
3. Season with cayenne pepper and salt
4. Thin with a cup of milk or cream, and a cup of oyster juice
5. Boil altogether for ten minutes
