## Ingredients

- onions
- garlic
- olive oil
- fish
- tomatoes
- salt
- black pepper
- red pepper
- thyme
- sweet-bay
- parsley
- white wine
- water
- bread

## Directions

1. Chop some onions and garlic very fine, fry them in olive oil.
2. When slightly colored add some fish cut up in slices.
3. Add a few tomatoes scalded, peeled and sliced, some salt, black and red pepper, thyme, sweet-bay, parsley, and half a bottle of white wine, and enough water to cover the fish.
4. Put it over a brisk fire and boil a quarter of an hour.
5. Put slices of toasted bread in a deep dish, place the fish on a shallow dish with some broth, and pour the balance on the bread and serve hot.
