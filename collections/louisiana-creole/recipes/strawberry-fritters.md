## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Fine Large Strawberries
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of White Wine
- Grated Zest of ½ Lemon
- Fritter Batter a la Creole

## Directions

1. For this purpose have fine, large strawberries.
2. Make a Fritter Batter a la Creole, only just before adding the whites of the eggs add the grated zest of half a lemon and two tablespoonfuls of White Wine.
3. The batter must be of the consistency of thick cream.
4. Stem the strawberries, and drop them into the batter.
5. Proceed to fry as in recipe given for Apple Fritters, allowing two or three strawberries to each fritter.
6. Or make a marmalade of the strawberries, keeping them whole and proceed as in Orange Fritters.
