## Ingredients

- Sponge cake
- A tablespoonful of any favorite extract
- Currants
- Seedless raisins
- Sliced citron
- Chopped almonds
- One pint of milk
- The yolks of two eggs
- A quarter of a pound of sugar
- Half a teaspoonful of extract of lemon, rose, or almonds

## Directions

1. Cut some sponge cake about half an inch thick
2. Shape it nearly to the mould
3. Dilute a tablespoonful of any favorite extract, and pour it on to the cake
4. Commence to fill up the mould in layers of currants, seedless raisins, sliced citron, and chopped almonds
5. Add a layer of cake
6. Continue until it is full
7. Make a custard of one pint of milk, the yolks of two eggs, a quarter of a pound of sugar, and half a teaspoonful of extract of lemon, rose, or almonds
8. Let it simmer a little, but not enough to curdle
9. When it simmers, take it off, and let it cool a little
10. When only lukewarm pour it over the cake mixture
