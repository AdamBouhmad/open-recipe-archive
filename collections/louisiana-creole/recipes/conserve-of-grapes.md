## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Fruit Juice
- ½ Pound of Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the grapes for one hour.
2. Press them through a sieve.
3. Add a half pound of sugar to every pint of liquid.
4. Proceed as directed above.
