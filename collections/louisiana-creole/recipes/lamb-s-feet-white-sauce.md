## Ingredients

- 12 Lamb's Feet
- 1 Pint of Broth
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- The Yolk of 1 Egg
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter

## Directions

1. Clean the feet well, and, after boiling, take out all the bones, cut in little pieces of about two inches or less
2. Season nicely and cook in a pint of their own water over a slow fire
3. Add the juice of a lemon
4. Throw in the beaten yolk of an egg to bind nicely, and serve hot
