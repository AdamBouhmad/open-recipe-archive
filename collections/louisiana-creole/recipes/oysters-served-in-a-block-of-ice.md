## Ingredients

- 6 Oysters to Each Person
- A Square Block of Ice
- Black Pepper and Cayenne
- Sprigs of Parsley and Radishes to Garnish
- Lemon Cut in Quarters

## Directions

1. Have your dealer send a square block of Ice of the size desired and make a hollow in the center of the block by placing a flat-iron on the top, scooping out with the iron the shape desired.
2. Place a folded napkin on a platter and stand the block of ice upon it.
3. Pepper the oysters nicely with Cayenne and black pepper, and place in the ice.
4. Take sprigs of parsley and decorate the platter, placing between decorated radishes, and alternate slices of lemon.
5. Serve the oysters with lemon cut in quarters.
6. The cavity should be square and deep, leaving walls of ice about two inches in thickness.
7. Smilax may be substituted for the parsley or mixed with it.
