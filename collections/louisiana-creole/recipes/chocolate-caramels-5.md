## Ingredients

- half a pound of chocolate
- three pounds of dark brown sugar
- one-eighth pound of butter
- one small teacup of milk
- vanilla, or grated lemon or orange-peel

## Directions

1. Boil half a pound of chocolate, three pounds of dark brown sugar, one-eighth pound of butter, and one small teacup of milk very quickly over a hot fire
2. Stir constantly
3. Season with vanilla, or grated lemon or orange-peel
4. When it becomes hard on being dropped into water, take it off the fire
5. Stir for a few moments before pouring into buttered dishes
6. Before it is quite cool, cut into little squares
