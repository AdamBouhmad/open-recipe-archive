## Ingredients

- butter
- a dessert-spoonful of flour
- salt
- pepper
- parsley, chopped
- 1/2 pint of cream
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Put into a stewpan a good sized piece of butter, a dessert-spoonful of flour, some salt, pepper and parsley, chopped
2. Add 1/2 pint of cream
3. Put this sauce on the fire and stir it till it boils
4. Slice the potatoes and throw into the sauce
