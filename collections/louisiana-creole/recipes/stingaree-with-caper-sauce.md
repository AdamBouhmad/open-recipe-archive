## Ingredients

- A Fine Young Stingaree
- A Pint of French Vinegar
- A Handful of Parsley Minced Very Fine
- A Teaspoonful of Salt
- Parsley to Garnish
- Caper Sauce

## Directions

1. Cut off the tail of the Stingaree.
2. Cut off the wings, and throw the rest of the fish away.
3. Throw the wings into boiling water charged with salt.
4. Parboil them for fifteen or twenty minutes.
5. Take them out of the water and skin thoroughly.
6. Place in a heated dish and pour over a Caper Sauce.
