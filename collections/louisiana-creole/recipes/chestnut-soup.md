## Ingredients

- lean fresh beef, mutton or venison
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- water
- 1 quart or more of chestnuts
- fresh butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Make a soup of the lean of fresh beef, mutton or venison in the best manner
2. Season with cayenne pepper and a little salt, allowing rather less than a quart of water to each pound of meat
3. Skim and boil it till the meat drops from the bone
4. Strain it and put it in a clean pot
5. Have ready a quart or more of nice chestnuts, boiled and peeled
6. Put them into the soup with some small bits of fresh butter rolled in flour
7. Boil the soup 10 minutes longer before it goes to the table
