## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of sugar
- ¾ lb. of butter
- 6 eggs
- 1 small spoonful of cinnamon
- 1 small spoonful of cloves
- 1 wineglass of brandy
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 cup of sour cream

## Directions

1. beat the butter and sugar together until smooth
2. add the flour and eggs by degrees
3. add the brandy and spice alternately
4. dissolve soda in sour cream
5. stir it all well
6. put in the mould to bake
