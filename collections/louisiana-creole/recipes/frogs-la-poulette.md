## Ingredients

- 6 Frog Legs
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1/2 Can of Mushrooms
- 1 Tablespoon Flour
- 1 Lemon
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 2 Eggs

## Directions

1. Clean and skin the Frogs' legs and cut them in small pieces of about an inch in size
2. Have ready a stew pot and put in the Frogs' legs, with salt and pepper to taste, and half a lemon cut fine
3. Cover well with water and let it cook till the meat is tender
4. When cooked, drain and put the meat aside
5. Make a sauce: Take one tablespoonful of butter and two spoonfuls of flour; rub the flour smoothly and put in a sauce pan with the butter to melt
6. Add two cups of water and stir well
7. When it begins to boil well, add a half can of mushrooms
8. Season with salt and Cayenne
9. Add the frogs and season again to taste, and let this boil ten minutes
10. Beat the yolks of two raw eggs well, take the frogs off the fire and stir in the yolks thoroughly
11. Add the juice of one-half lemon and serve
