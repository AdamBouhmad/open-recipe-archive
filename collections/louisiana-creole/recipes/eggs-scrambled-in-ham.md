## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of finely minced boiled ham
- 1 tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Mince the ham very fine.
2. Break the eggs, one by one, into a saucer, and add to the bowl in which you have minced the ham.
3. Mix all together.
4. Place a tablespoonful of butter in the frying pan.
5. Add the eggs and ham, stir briskly.
6. When it comes to the consistency of starch take off the fire.
7. Serve hot on buttered toast.
