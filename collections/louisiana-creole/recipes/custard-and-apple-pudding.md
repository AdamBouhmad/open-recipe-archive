## Ingredients

- 4 apples
- Paste
- Sugar
- Boiled custard

## Directions

1. Peel and cut in slices 4 apples
2. Put a paste in the bottom of the dish
3. Lay a covering of apples and sugar
4. Put a boiled custard over the top, and bake it
