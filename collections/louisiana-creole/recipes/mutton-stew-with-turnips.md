## Ingredients

- 4 Pounds of the Brisket of Mutton
- 6 Turnips
- 3 Large Onions
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1/2 Clove of Garlic
- 1/4 Tablespoonful of Lard
- 2 Quarts of Water
- 1 Square Inch of Ham, Chopped Very Fine
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Cut the mutton into pieces of about an inch in length and thickness, and season well.
2. Proceed to make the stew as mentioned above, only instead of adding the potatoes, add turnips parboiled, and cut into halves or quarters.
