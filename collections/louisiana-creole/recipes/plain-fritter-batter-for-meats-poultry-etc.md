## Ingredients

- 1 Cup Flour
- 1 Cup of Water
- 2 Eggs
- 1/8 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Tablespoonful of Melted Butter

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the flour together, and add the melted butter and the salt.
2. Then add the water, and beat well, and finally add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
3. Some add a half teaspoonful of baking powder according to taste.
