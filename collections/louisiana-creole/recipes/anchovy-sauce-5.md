## Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoonful of Anchovy Butter
- 1.5 Tablespoonfuls Flour
- 1.5 Tablespoonfuls Butter

## Directions

1. Make a White Sauce
2. Add to this a tablespoonful of Anchovy butter
3. Let it melt and season to taste in the sauce
4. Serve with boiled fish
