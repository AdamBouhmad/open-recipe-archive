## Ingredients

- egg plant
- fried onions
- parsley
- egg
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- bread
- bread crumbs
- fresh tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cut in half and take out the centre
2. Boil the inside
3. When soft, chop fine
4. Season with fried onions, parsley, egg, salt, butter, pepper and bread
5. Stuff the outsides
6. Cover with bread crumbs
7. Add the insides of fresh tomatoes, if you choose
