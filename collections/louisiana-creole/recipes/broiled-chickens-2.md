## Ingredients

- pair of chickens
- pepper
- salt
- egg yolks
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Split a pair of chickens down the back
2. Wipe the inside and season with pepper and salt
3. Prepare some beaten yolks of eggs and bread crumbs
4. Dip the outside of the chickens in the batter
5. Put them on a gridiron on a bed of bright coals
6. Lay the chickens on the gridiron with the inside down
7. Broil them 20 minutes
8. Just before taking them from the fire, add bits of butter
