## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of white sugar
- 12 eggs
- 2 lbs. of raisins
- 2 lbs. of currants
- 1 lb. of citron
- 2 tablespoonfuls of mixed spice (mace and cinnamon)
- 2 nutmegs, powdered
- 1 large glass of wine
- 1 glass of brandy
- 1/2 glass of rose water

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake for 4 or 5 hours
3. Ice it next day
