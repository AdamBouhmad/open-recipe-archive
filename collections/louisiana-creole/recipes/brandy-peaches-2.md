## Ingredients

- fine freestone peaches
- boiling water
- brandy
- fruit water
- granulated sugar

## Directions

1. Select fine freestone peaches, yellow or white, not overripe
2. Scald them with boiling water, cover well, and let them boil till tender
3. Take them out, and drain on a platter, and put in the jars and cover with brandy, and let them stand one week
4. Make a syrup, allowing a half pint of the water in which the peaches were boiled to one pound of granulated sugar
5. Take out the peaches, drain them, and put into glass jars
6. Let the syrup cool, and mix equal parts of this with equal parts of the brandy, in which the peaches were put up
7. Pour over the peaches, and seal
