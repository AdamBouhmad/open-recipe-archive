## Ingredients

- ½ Gill of Kirsch
- 1 Pint of Cold Water
- ¾ Pound of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Cornstarch

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and water in a saucepan and set on the stove on a brisk fire
2. Let it come to a boil
3. Add a tablespoonful of cornstarch which has been diluted in a tablespoonful of water
4. Stir well for three minutes, and then take from the fire and add immediately a half gill of Kirsch
5. Mix well, strain through a sieve and serve with puddings
