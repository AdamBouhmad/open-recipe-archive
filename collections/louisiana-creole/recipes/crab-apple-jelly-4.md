## Ingredients

- Crab apples
- Water
- Loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the apples, cut them up, remove all defects, remove the seeds and the blossom end; but do not pare them
2. Lay them in your preserving-kettle, and cover them with water
3. Boil them until they are soft, but do not let them mash up from too much boiling
4. Drain off all the water, and mash the apples with the back of a silver spoon
5. Put this in a jelly bag, and place a deep dish under it to collect the juice
6. To every pint of the juice allow a pint of loaf sugar
7. Boil it and skim it
8. It will be ready to dip out into tumblers in half an hour, if you have complied with these directions
9. Always dip jelly out with a silver spoon, as any other kind darkens fruit
