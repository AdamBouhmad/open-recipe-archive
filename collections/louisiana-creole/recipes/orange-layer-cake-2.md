## Ingredients

- 4 Layers of Pound or Cup Cake
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 3 Cups of Sugar
- The Grated Rind and Juice of 1 Orange
- Juice of Half a Lemon
- 1 Teaspoonful of Orange Extract

## Directions

1. Make a good layer, pound or cup cake
2. Then make a filling of the whites of two eggs, three cups of sugar, the grated rind and juice of one orange, half a lemon's juice, two teaspoonfuls of orange extract
3. Mix all thoroughly, and spread between the layers of the cake
