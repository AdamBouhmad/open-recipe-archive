## Ingredients

- The Yolks of 4 Eggs
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered White Sugar
- 2 Gills of Madeira Wine
- The Zest of Half a Lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the sugar well together, and when they come to a rich cream set on the stove and whip well for two minutes
2. Add the zest of a lemon or one tablespoonful of a lemon, or one tablespoonful of lemon essence, and drop in gradually the Madeira wine
3. Stir without ceasing for two minutes more, and then serve very hot with Fruit, Bread Puddings, etc.
