## Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoonful of Melted Butter
- The Juice of Half a Lemon
- Yolk of 1 Egg
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chopped Parsley

## Directions

1. Melt the butter; add the juice of half a lemon; mix well and take off the stove and add the yolk of one egg, well beaten.
2. Add a teaspoonful of chopped parsley, beating steadily.
3. This sauce is very light, and as soon as removed from the fire is served hot with the fish.
