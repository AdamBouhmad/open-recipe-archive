## Ingredients

- a handful of chopped mushrooms
- a bunch of parsley
- two shallots chopped
- pepper
- salt
- thyme or sweet herbs
- a glass of white wine
- a half cup of brown gravy from veal or chicken
- the juice of half a lemon
- a cup of sweet milk
- a piece of butter, as large as a hen's egg
- paste

## Directions

1. Season mushrooms, parsley, and shallots with pepper, salt, and thyme or sweet herbs
2. Cook them a light brown
3. Throw in a glass of white wine, a half cup of brown gravy from veal or chicken, and the juice of half a lemon
4. If you have no gravy on hand, add to the rabbits a cup of sweet milk, and a piece of butter, as large as a hen's egg
5. Toss all up on the fire fifteen or twenty minutes
6. Make a nice paste, line the sides of the pan
7. Pour in the stewed rabbit, and cover with paste
8. Bake until a light brown
9. Eat cold or hot
