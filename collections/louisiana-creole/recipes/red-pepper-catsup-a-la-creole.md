## Ingredients

- 4 Dozen Pepper Pods
- 2 Quarts of Best French Vinegar
- 1 Quart of Water
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Grated Horseradish
- 5 Onions
- 1 Garlic

## Directions

1. Take four dozen pods of ripe green peppers, two quarts of vinegar, one of water, three tablespoonfuls of grated horseradish, five onions sliced, and one garlic
2. Boil all together till the onions are very soft
3. Mash all together to a paste and strain, rubbing through the sieve till nothing remains but the seeds and skins
4. When cold, bottle and cork tightly for use
