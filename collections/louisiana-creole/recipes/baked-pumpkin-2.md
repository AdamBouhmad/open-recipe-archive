## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Pumpkin or Cashaw
- Butter or Gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the pumpkin in halves and then into quarters. Two quarters are enough for a family of six, if the pumpkin is large.
2. Remove the seeds, but do not peel the rind.
3. Place in a baking pan with the rind downwards, and bake until so tender that it may be pierced easily with a fork.
4. Serve in the rind at the table, helping it by spoonfuls.
5. It is eaten with butter or gravy.
