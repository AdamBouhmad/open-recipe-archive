## Ingredients

- 5 cups of flour
- 2 cups of molasses
- 1 cup of sugar
- 1 cup of butter
- 8 eggs
- 1 teacup of allspice and ginger mixed

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake in a pan or small cups
