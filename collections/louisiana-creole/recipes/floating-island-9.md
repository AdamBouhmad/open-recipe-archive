## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- 4 Eggs
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla
- 3/4 Cup of Currant Jelly

## Directions

1. Put the milk to boil in a farina boiler.
2. Beat the yolks of the eggs with the sugar till very light, and stir them into the milk as it begins to boil.
3. Let it thicken one minute.
4. Take from the fire, and add the vanilla and set aside to cool.
5. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth.
6. When the mixture is cold pour it into a glass dish, heap on the whites of the eggs, and dot here and there with bits of Currant Jelly.
7. Dust these with powdered sugar, and serve very cold.
