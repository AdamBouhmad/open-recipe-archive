## Ingredients

- yolks of four hard-boiled eggs
- white of two raw ones
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Make a paste of the yolks of four hard-boiled eggs and the white of two raw ones; season with salt and cayenne pepper.
2. Take bits of the paste the size of small marbles, run them in flour and roll into balls; fry carefully in butter and drop into the soup.
