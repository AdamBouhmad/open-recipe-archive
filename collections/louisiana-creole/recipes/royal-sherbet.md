## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Fine Louisiana Oranges
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Brandy
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Orange Syrup

## Directions

1. Take fine, fresh Louisiana oranges, and press out all juice, and mash the pulp and strain through a sieve.
2. To each pint of juice allow two tablespoonfuls of brandy and two of orange syrup.
3. Sweeten to taste and freeze and serve according to the directions given for Creole Punch.
