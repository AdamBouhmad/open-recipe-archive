## Ingredients

- The Filets of 2 Chickens
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Small Onion, sliced
- 1 Minced Bay Leaf
- 1 Blade of Mace
- 4 Cloves
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Form the white meat neatly into filets by patting and flattening
2. Season well with salt and pepper
3. Put the butter into the stewpan and add the sliced onion, the bay leaf (whole) and the spices
4. Let all simmer without browning
5. Lay in the filets of chicken, being careful not to let them brown
6. Let them simmer gently and add one cup of the water in which you have broiled the dark meat of the chicken
7. Let all simmer gently for an hour
8. When done, arrange the filets tastefully on a dish, garnish with parsley sprigs and Croutons of bread nicely shaped in diamond form and fried in butter
