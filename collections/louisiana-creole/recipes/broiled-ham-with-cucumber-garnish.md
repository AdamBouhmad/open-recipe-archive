## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 slices of boiled ham
- Pepper
- Cucumbers
- Butter
- Salted vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut thin as many slices of ham as desired and broil evenly over hot coals.
2. When well brown, butter and add pepper, sprinkling.
3. Serve with slices of cucumber that have been steeped in salted vinegar several hours ranged around it.
