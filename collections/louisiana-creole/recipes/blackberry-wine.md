## Ingredients

- berries
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mash the berries without boiling them
2. Strain the juice
3. To six pints of juice, add two pints of water and three pounds of sugar
4. Mix thoroughly and put it in a wide-mouthed stone jar to ferment
5. Cover it carefully with a cloth, to keep out all insects
6. Open it and skim it every morning; then cover it up again carefully
7. When it ceases to ferment, strain it and put it in a demijohn
8. Do not cork it tightly, as it must have a little air, but cover the loose stopper with a piece of muslin or tarlatan, to keep out the insects
9. It will be ready to bottle in two months
