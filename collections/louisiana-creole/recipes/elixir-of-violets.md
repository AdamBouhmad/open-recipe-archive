## Ingredients

- 10 Ounces of Syrup of Violets
- 4 Ounces of Filtered Raspberry Juice
- 4 Pounds of Sugar
- 2 Quarts of the Best Brandy or Alcohol
- 2 Pints of Water

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with the sugar and water, and let it cool.
2. Mix the violet syrup and the Raspberry Juice, and add it to the alcohol.
3. Add the sugar syrup, when cool, mix all thoroughly, strain and bottle tightly.
