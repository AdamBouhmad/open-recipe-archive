## Ingredients

- whites of 11 eggs
- 1.5 tumblers sifted powdered sugar
- 1 tumbler sifted flour
- 1 teaspoonful cream of tartar
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Sift the flour four times, leaving the tumbler even full
2. Then add the cream of tartar, and sift again
3. Beat the eggs to a stiff froth on a dish, and on same dish add sugar lightly, then flour gently, then vanilla
4. Do not stop beating until you put it in the oven to bake
5. Bake 40 minutes in a moderately heated oven, and if too soft let it remain a few minutes longer
6. Do not open the oven until it has been in 15 minutes
7. Turn the pan upside down to cool, and when cold take out by loosening cake with a knife
8. Then ice
