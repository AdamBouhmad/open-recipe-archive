## Ingredients

- Pippins or other tart apples
- Water
- Grated rind of a lemon
- 1 pound of sugar to every pint of juice

## Directions

1. Pare apples and cut into quarters
2. Put into a preserving kettle and cover with water
3. Add the grated rind of a lemon
4. Let them boil to a marmalade
5. Strain the juice without squeezing through a clean jelly bag made of flannel
6. For every pint of juice add one pound of fine sugar
7. If the apples are not very tart, add the juice also
