## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 oysters to each person
- Salt and pepper
- Finely grated bread crumbs
- Parsley
- Boiling lard

## Directions

1. Drain the oysters, allowing about six or eight to each person to be served.
2. Salt and pepper and then roll oysters in bread crumbs, grated very fine.
3. Drop in the frying pan of boiling lard, having sufficient lard to allow the oysters to swim in the grease.
4. Remove when a golden brown and place on brown paper and drain.
5. Serve on a platter garnished with parsley or on a bed of fried parsley.
