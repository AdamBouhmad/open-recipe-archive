## Ingredients

- 100 Lake Shrimp
- 1 Dozen Tomatoes
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Cup of Grated Bread Crumbs or Crackers

## Directions

1. Boil the shrimps according to recipe
2. Butter a deep dish well and place within a layer of grated bread crumbs or powdered crackers
3. Pick and clean the shrimp and season well
4. Stew about a dozen tomatoes in a little butter and season with pepper and salt
5. Place a layer of the tomatoes in the dish and then a thin layer of crackers or grated bread and over this a layer of shrimp
6. Continue till you have four or five layers, the last being of the grated bread crumbs
7. Put little dots of butter here and there
8. Place in the oven and bake till quite brown
