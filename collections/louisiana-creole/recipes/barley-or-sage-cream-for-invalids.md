## Ingredients

- sage or barley
- water
- a stick of cinnamon
- rich boiled custard
- sugar
- a glass of wine

## Directions

1. Wash the sage or barley clean; take a cup of either
2. Put it on the fire with water to cover it
3. Boil it gently until it is soft
4. While boiling, put in a stick of cinnamon, or any seasoning that is agreeable
5. When the barley has boiled soft and thick, take it off and strain it
6. Add to it a rich boiled custard, sweeten it to taste
7. Add a glass of wine, if liked
8. Serve it frozen, or not, as is liked best by the sick
