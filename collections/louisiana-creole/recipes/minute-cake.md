## Ingredients

- 6 Eggs
- 5 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- 3 Tablespoonfuls Flour
- 2 Tablespoonfuls Milk
- The Juice and Rind of a Lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs to a cream, and then add the sugar, beating thoroughly till very, very light.
2. Then blend the flour and milk until smooth, and add.
3. Add the juice of the lemon and the grated zest, and beat well again.
4. Then add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a thick froth, turn into a buttered dish and bake fifteen minutes in a quick oven.
