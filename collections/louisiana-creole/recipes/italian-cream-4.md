## Ingredients

- 3 quarts of cream
- 2 lemons
- 3 glasses of white wine
- 1| ounces of isinglass
- 4 small teacupfuls of hot water

## Directions

1. Put 3 quarts of cream into 2 bowls
2. Sweeten one sweet enough for the two
3. Add the juice of 2 lemons and 3 glasses of white wine
4. Add the other quart of cream
5. Stir the whole very hard
6. Boil 1| ounces of isinglass with 4 small teacupfuls of hot water until reduced to 1/2
7. Strain the isinglass, lukewarm, into the other ingredients
8. Put it into moulds
