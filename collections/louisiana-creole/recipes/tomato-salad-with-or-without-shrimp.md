## Ingredients

- a dozen large tomatoes
- three or four sweet peppers
- pepper
- salt
- oil
- mustard
- vinegar
- a pint of shelled shrimp

## Directions

1. Slice a dozen large tomatoes, slice with them three or four sweet peppers
2. Pepper and salt the tomatoes
3. Lay slices of tomato and a little sweet pepper until the dish is full
4. Pour over all a dressing of oil, mustard and vinegar
5. A pint of shelled shrimp is a great improvement to this salad, but it is good without
