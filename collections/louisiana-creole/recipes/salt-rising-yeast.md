## Ingredients

- a pint of new milk
- a teaspoonful of salt
- flour

## Directions

1. Take a pint of new milk, warm from the cow if possible
2. Put in a teaspoonful of salt
3. thicken it with flour to the consistency of batter cakes
4. Set this in a warm place to rise
5. make your biscuit or bread up with it, and some new milk, or milk and water warmed together
