## Ingredients

- 6 Tenderloin Trout Cutlets
- 1 Cup of Milk
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Flour
- Parsley
- Sliced Lemon
- Mayonnaise Sauce or Sauce a la Tartare

## Directions

1. Cut the fish (Tenderloin Trout) into filets or square pieces
2. Remove all the bones, dip in milk which has been freely salted and peppered, and roll in flour
3. Drop into the well-heated lard so that the filets will swim and fry to a golden brown
4. When done, drain on a brown paper and serve on a bed of fried parsley, with garnishes of parsley and sliced lemon
5. Serve with either a Mayonnaise Sauce, a Sauce a la Tartare or a Tomato Sauce
