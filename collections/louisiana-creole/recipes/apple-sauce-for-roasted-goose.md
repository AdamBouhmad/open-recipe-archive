## Ingredients

- fine apples
- water
- butter
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and slice some fine apples
2. Put them into a saucepan with enough water to keep them from burning
3. Stew them till quite soft and tender
4. Mash them to a paste
5. Make them very sweet, adding butter and nutmeg
6. Be careful not to have the sauce thin and watery
