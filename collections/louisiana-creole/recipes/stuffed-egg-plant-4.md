## Ingredients

- egg plant
- fried onions
- parsley
- egg
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- bread
- fresh tomatoes

## Directions

1. Cut in half, take out the centre
2. Boil the inside
3. When soft, chop fine, and season with fried onions, parsley, egg, salt, butter, pepper and bread
4. Stuff the outsides
5. Cover with bread crumbs, and add the insides of fresh tomatoes, if you choose
