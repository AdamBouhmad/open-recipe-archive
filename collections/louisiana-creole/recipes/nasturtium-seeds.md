## Ingredients

- cold cider vinegar
- green seeds of nasturtiums

## Directions

1. Keep a large glass jar of cold cider vinegar
2. Put in the green seeds of nasturtiums after the flowers are off, and the seeds full grown, but not hard
3. Remove the stalks
4. To flavor them with spice, boil the vinegar with a bag of spice in it, and pour it on hot, leaving the spice in the jar
