## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Stale Cake
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Cup Raisins
- 1 1/2 Pints Milk
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Utilize left-over cake in this pudding.
2. Beat the eggs well, and mix and then, when very light, add the milk.
3. If you use stale cake, add two tablespoonfuls of sugar; if you use stale bread, add half a cup of sugar.
4. Mix thoroughly.
5. Spread the bottom of the pan with the raisins, and then put a layer of cake over them, sliced very thin.
6. Sprinkle this with raisins, and continue adding the cake and raisins alternately till all are used.
7. Add a little essence of lemon or vanilla to the egg mixture, and pour all this over the cake.
8. Set in the oven and bake for an hour.
9. Serve with Hard or Lemon Sauce.
