## Ingredients

- 9 eggs
- 1 pound of sugar
- 14 ozs. of flour

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks with a whisk till quite thick
2. Mix the sugar with them, and beat till light
3. Beat the whites well, and add them by degrees
4. Put the flour in gradually
5. Bake in a quick oven
