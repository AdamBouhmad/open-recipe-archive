## Ingredients

- wild ducks
- small onion
- butter
- bunch of celery
- pepper
- salt
- lemon

## Directions

1. Truss wild ducks and lay them in a pan to bake with a small onion in the body
2. Put butter over them, with a bunch of celery, a little pepper and salt
3. Cook slowly and garnish with lemon
4. Wild ducks should be wiped dry after they are drawn, and rubbed on the inside with pepper and salt, except the canvas-back, which should be left to its own delicious flavor
