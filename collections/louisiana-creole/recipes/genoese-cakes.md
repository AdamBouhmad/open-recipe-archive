## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of sugar
- four eggs
- half a pound of flour
- a small glass of brandy or wine

## Directions

1. Bake in a square sheet
2. ice it and cut into diamonds
3. ornament with dots or stripes of any kind of bright jelly or preserves
