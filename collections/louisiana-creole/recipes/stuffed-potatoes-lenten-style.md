## Ingredients

- 6 large potatoes
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- the yolk of 1 egg
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- three-quarter cup of milk or cream
- three-quarter teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Bake the potatoes in the oven, and, when done, cut one end and scoop out the meat without breaking the skin.
2. Add the yolk of one egg, the butter, chopped parsley, salt and pepper to taste, and the grated nutmeg, and moisten all with the cream or milk.
3. Mix well and beat very light.
4. Then refill the skins and return to the oven for a few minutes, till very hot and slightly browned, and serve.
