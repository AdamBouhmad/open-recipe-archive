## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of Lean Ham
- 6 Slices of Toast
- 1 Tablespoonful of French Mustard
- 1 Gill of Cold White Sauce
- 1 Boiled Onion
- 1 Cooked Garlic
- 2 Ounces of Grated Parmesan Cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare the toast nicely, cutting the slices the full width of the bread, paring the edges nicely and toasting and buttering well.
2. Lay on each piece of toast a thin slice of very lean ham, which has been lightly spread with French mustard.
3. Spread lightly over this a cold White Sauce to which has been added while cooking a finely-minced onion and clove of garlic, and a dash of Parmesan cheese.
4. Dredge the top of the Canapé with Parmesan cheese, then sprinkle lightly with finely-grated bread crumbs.
5. Set in the oven for five minutes and bake and send to the table hot.
