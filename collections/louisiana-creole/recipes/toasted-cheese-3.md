## Ingredients

- 0.5 Pound of Cheese
- 6 Slices of Bread
- Salt and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Toast the bread nicely and butter it.
2. Cut the cheese into very thin slices and hold to the fire, letting it toast nicely, first on one side and then on the other.
3. Lay this upon the buttered toast and serve hot.
