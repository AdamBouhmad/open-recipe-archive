## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Apples
- 6 Spoonfuls of Sugar
- 1 Cupful of Water

## Directions

1. Cut the blossom end of the apple, and wash, but do not peel.
2. Set them in a baking pan, heap a spoonful of sugar over each in the little place scooped out at the blossom end, and pour a cupful of water in the bottom of the pan.
3. Set in the oven, and bake till very tender.
4. Place in a dish, pour over the syrup, and serve either hot or cold.
