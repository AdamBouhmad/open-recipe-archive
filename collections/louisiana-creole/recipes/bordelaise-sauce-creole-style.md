## Ingredients

- 1 Onion or 2 Shallots
- 1 Tablespoonful of Olive Oil
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Peel the onion or shallots and chop fine.
2. Put in a saucepan with one tablespoonful of olive oil; let the onion sauté well.
3. Pour the sauce over tenderloin filets or sirloin steaks when it is desired to serve these a la Bordelaise.
4. A tablespoonful of Red Wine may be added to the sauce.
