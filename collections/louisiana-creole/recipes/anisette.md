## Ingredients

- 10 drops of oil of aniseed
- 3 pints of alcohol
- 2 pounds of the finest white clarified loaf sugar
- 1½ pints of water

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with the water and sugar, and clarify.
2. When cold, add the aniseed oil, which has been well-dissolved in the alcohol.
3. Mix all well, filter and bottle tightly.
