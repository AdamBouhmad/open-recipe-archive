## Ingredients

- 1/2 peck of very ripe peaches
- 2 lbs. of the best white sugar
- 1 quart of cream
- 1 quart of milk

## Directions

1. Pare and cut finely 1/2 peck of very ripe peaches
2. Stir in well 2 lbs. of the best white sugar
3. Let it stand for 2 or 3 hours
4. Stir them well together
5. Put into a preserving kettle to simmer for 20 minutes, stirring all the time to prevent scorching
6. Pour them into a pan
7. When quite cold, add 1 quart of cream and 1 quart of milk
8. Stir them together
9. Put instantly in the freezer to prevent curdling
