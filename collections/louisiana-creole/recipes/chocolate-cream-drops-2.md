## Ingredients

- one-half a cup of cream
- two cups of white sugar
- chocolate
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix cream with white sugar
2. Boil and stir fully five minutes
3. Set the dish into another of cold water
4. Stir until it becomes hard
5. Make into small balls about the size of marbles
6. With a fork roll each one separately in the chocolate, which has in the meantime been put in a bowl over the boiling teakettle and melted
7. Put on brown paper to cool
8. Flavor with vanilla, if desired
