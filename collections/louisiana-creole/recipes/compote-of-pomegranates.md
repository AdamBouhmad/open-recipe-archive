## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Pomegranates
- % Pound of Sugar
- % Cup of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Orange Juice or Maraschino

## Directions

1. Cut a circle about the size of a five-cent piece out of the peel of the pomegranate, using a sharp-pointed knife
2. Split down the sides of their skins, and carefully remove all the bright ruby pips, without bruising them
3. Throw these into a dish
4. Make a syrup, flavored with orange juice or Maraschino, and pour over and serve
