## Ingredients

- yolks of five eggs
- half a pound of sugar
- half a pound of blanched almonds
- yellow part of one lemon grated
- half a pound of flour
- whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Beat for one hour the yolks of five eggs with half a pound of sugar
2. Add half a pound of blanched almonds pounded fine and the yellow part of one lemon grated
3. Mix well and add half a pound of flour very gradually
4. Roll out the paste and cut it into strips the length and size of the forefinger
5. Beat lightly the whites of two eggs and wet the fingers
