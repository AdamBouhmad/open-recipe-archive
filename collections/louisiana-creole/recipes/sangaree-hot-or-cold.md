## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Claret
- 1 Cup of Boiling Water
- 6 Whole Cloves
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- 12 Whole Allspice
- 2 Inches of Stick Cinnamon
- Grated Nutmeg to Taste

## Directions

1. Boil the water and spices for fifteen minutes, till the water is thoroughly flavored with the spices
2. Then remove from the fire and dissolve the sugar in it
3. Put the Claret into a pitcher
4. Strain the water and Sugar into it
5. Flavor with the nutmeg, and add more sugar, if necessary, to taste
6. Serve hot immediately
