## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of sifted cornmeal
- 1 pint of boiling hot new milk
- 8 eggs (whites and yolks separated)
- a pinch of salt
- 1 spoonful of lard

## Directions

1. Pour boiling hot new milk on the sifted cornmeal
2. Stir well together
3. Beat the whites and yolks of eggs separately
4. Reserve the whites for the last ingredient added
5. Add salt and lard
6. Beat the whole quite light
7. Grease the pan and pour in the mixture
8. Bake 1 hour
