## Ingredients

- Sprigs of spruce
- One ounce of hops
- A spoonful of ginger
- A gallon of water
- A pint of Louisiana Molasses or a pound of brown sugar
- A teacupful of yeast (home-made)

## Directions

1. Take sprigs of spruce and boil until you have an ounce of strong essence
2. Take one ounce of hops and a spoonful of ginger, and put them into a gallon of water
3. When well-boiled, strain the mixture, and add a pint of Louisiana Molasses or a pound of brown sugar, and then add the extract of Spruce
4. When cool add a teacupful of yeast (home-made), and put all into a clean, tight cask, and let it ferment for twenty-four hours or two days
5. Then bottle for use
