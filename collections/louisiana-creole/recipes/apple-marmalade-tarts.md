## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Pie Paste
- 3 Ounces of Apple Marmalade
- 3 Apples
- 2 Ounces of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Prepare the Pie Paste and line six tart molds with it
2. Peel and core the apples and cut into quarters, and put into a saucepan with a pint of cold water
3. Let them cook on a hot stove for ten minutes
4. Remove and drain, and let cool for half an hour
5. Cut the quarters into three slices each
6. Line the bottom of the molds with the marmalade divided evenly
7. Arrange the sliced apples nicely over the marmalade, and dredge nicely with two ounces of powdered sugar
8. Set in a baking dish and bake for twenty minutes, or a half hour if necessary, in a moderate oven
9. Take out and spread nicely with apple marmalade and serve
