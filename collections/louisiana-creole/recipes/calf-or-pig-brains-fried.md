## Ingredients

- brains
- salt water
- wheat flour or beaten egg
- bread crumbs
- butter or lard
- pepper
- salt
- lemon

## Directions

1. Wash the brains in salt water, and wipe dry.
2. Dip in wheat flour or in beaten egg and then in bread crumbs.
3. Fry in butter or lard.
4. Season with pepper, salt and lemon sliced.
