## Ingredients

- 6 Veal Cutlets
- 1 Egg
- 1 Cup of Bread Crumbs
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Lemon and Parsley to Garnish
- Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel

## Directions

1. Have the cutlets cut thin.
2. Season well with salt and pepper.
3. Beat an egg well and roll the cutlets in the egg, then roll in bread crumbs.
4. Drop in boiling lard and fry to a nice golden brown.
5. Take out, drain off the lard, place on a heated dish and serve with garnish of parsley and sliced lemon and a Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel.
6. The addition of a little lemon juice adds to the flavor when eating the cutlets.
