## Ingredients

- 2½ Quarts of Claret
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- ½ Quarts of Seltzer or Appolinaris Water
- 1 Pint of Lemon Juice
- 1 Large Piece of Ice
- 2 Lemons, Sliced Very Thin

## Directions

1. Slice the lemons very thin
2. Mix all the above ingredients together
3. Add the sliced lemons as a garnish
4. Stir well, set to cool, and serve in small glass cups or punch glasses
