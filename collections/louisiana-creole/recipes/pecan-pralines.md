## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Brown Sugar
- ½ Pound of Freshly-Peeled and Cut Louisiana Pecans
- 1 Spoon of Butter
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Water

## Directions

1. Set the sugar to boil, and as it begins to boil add the pecans, which you will have divested of their shells and cut some into fine pieces, others into halves, and others again into demi-halves.
2. Let all boil till the mixture begins to bubble, and then take off the stove and proceed to lay on a marble slab, as above, to dry.
3. Be careful to stir the mixture constantly till the syrup begins to thicken and turn to sugar.
4. Then take from the stove and proceed to turn on the marble slab.
