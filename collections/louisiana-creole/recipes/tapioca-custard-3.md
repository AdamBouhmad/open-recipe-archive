## Ingredients

- 3/4 Cup of Tapioca
- 3/4 Cup of Sugar
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Water
- 1 Tumbler of Currant or Strawberry Jelly

## Directions

1. Wash the tapioca well several times, and then soak it well in the pint of water for two hours.
2. Add it to the Jelly, mixing thoroughly, and then add the sugar, and mix again until the tapioca is very clear.
3. Beat the whites of the eggs to a very stiff froth, and add them to the tapioca, and turn it into a mold and set away to harden.
4. Make a Vanilla Cream Sauce with the yolks of the eggs, and serve cold with the custard.
