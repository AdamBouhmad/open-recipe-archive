## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- 4 tablespoonfuls butter or 2 of lard
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- A pinch of soda, or 1/4 teaspoonful yeast powder
- Tepid water

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and yeast powder together, if you desire it instead of soda
2. Otherwise sift the flour, add the salt, and mix the lard or butter thoroughly together, rubbing between your hands till there is not a lump left
3. Add sufficient tepid water to make a nice, rather stiff dough
4. Roll out into a thin sheet, about half an inch thick, and criss-cross with a knife lightly in diamond shapes
5. Set in the oven and bake for about fifteen minutes to a light brown
6. Bring to the table split open and buttered, and eaten while hot
