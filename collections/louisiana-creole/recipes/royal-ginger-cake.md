## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of flour
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 pint of molasses
- 1 gill of cream
- 1 cup of ginger

## Directions

1. Mix flour, sugar, and butter well together with molasses, cream, and ginger
2. Roll out and bake in a slack oven
