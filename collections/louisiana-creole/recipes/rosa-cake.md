## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of flour
- 2 lbs. of butter
- 2 lbs. of sugar
- yolks of 4 eggs
- whites of 8 eggs
- 1 glass of wine
- 1 glass of rose water

## Directions

1. Mix all well together
2. Lay them on tin sheets in small lumps
3. Bake in a quick oven
