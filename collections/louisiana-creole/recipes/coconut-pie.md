## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- ¾ Cup of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Grated Coconut
- 2 Eggs
- ⅜ of a Grated Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs very light
2. Add the sugar and beat with the eggs until very light
3. Add the grated nutmeg, and finally the coconut
4. Make a pie crust, using half the quantity, as a coconut pie is always an uncovered pie
5. Fill with the mixture, and bake in the oven for about half an hour
