## Ingredients

- eggs
- 1/2 teaspoonful of flour
- 2 tablespoonfuls of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of chopped ham
- 1/2 grated onion
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks to a cream and add the other ingredients
2. Rub all these smoothly together
3. Add the whites of eggs, beaten to a froth
4. Beat all thoroughly together
5. Put a tablespoonful of butter in the frying pan
6. When it melts add the omelet
7. Let it stand, shaking occasionally to prevent from sticking to the pan, till the eggs are quite set
8. Fold as in a plain omelet
9. Turn into a hot dish and serve
