## Ingredients

- ½ Pound of Brains
- 1 Onion
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Grated Breadcrumbs
- Parsley to Garnish

## Directions

1. Plunge the brains into cold water to disgorge them of all blood and remove the fine skin and blood that surround them.
2. Blanch with scalding water.
3. In five minutes take them out of the hot water and put them into a saucepan and cover with cold water.
4. Add a tiny onion, sliced fine, parsley and a whole bay leaf.
5. Let them simmer gently for five minutes.
6. Take from the fire and drain.
7. When cold cut into pieces of a square inch and dip in a batter or tomato sauce, and then in grated bread crumbs, patting gently.
8. Drop into boiling lard and fry to a golden brown.
9. Take out and drain off grease, and serve on a bed of fried parsley.
10. A garnish of boiled green peas is also very pretty and palatable.
