## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Almonds
- 1 Ounce of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Take about a pint of almonds and shell them
2. Blanch or scald them in boiling water till the skin comes off easily
3. Dry well and place in a flat tin dish
4. Add about an ounce of butter and set the plate in a moderate oven until the almonds are a golden brown
5. Take them out of the oven and add a heaping tablespoonful of salt
6. Stir them round and round, dredging quickly with the salt, and turn them into a dish to cool
7. Pick out the almonds and throw aside the extra salt
8. When cool, place in a box for use, covering tightly
