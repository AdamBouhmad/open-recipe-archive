## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- nine eggs
- one pound of butter
- one pound of brown sugar
- one teacupful of molasses
- one ounce of grated nutmeg or ground mace
- one teaspoonful of ground allspice
- one teaspoonful of cinnamon
- a gill of brandy
- three pounds of currants
- three pounds of raisins
- half a pound of flour
- one pound of citron

## Directions

1. Beat one pound of flour with nine eggs, the whites and yolks beaten separately, one pound of butter beaten to a cream, one pound of brown sugar, one teacupful of molasses, one ounce of grated nutmeg or ground mace, one teaspoonful of ground allspice, one teaspoonful of cinnamon, and a gill of brandy
2. Pick, wash, and dry three pounds of currants
3. stone and cut three pounds of raisins
4. strew half a pound of flour over them, mix it well through
5. stir them with one pound of citron, cut in slips, into the cake
