## Ingredients

- 2 ounces of French isinglass
- 3 pints of water
- 1 pint of good cream
- 1 glass of pure peach water

## Directions

1. Boil 2 ounces of French isinglass in 3 pints of water, 20 minutes
2. Strain this into 1 pint of good cream
3. Let it simmer 5 minutes
4. Add 1 glass of pure peach water
5. Sweeten, and strain through a sieve into a mould
