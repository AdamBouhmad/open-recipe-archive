## Ingredients

- a cup of butter
- a half pint of water
- two cups of sifted flour
- five eggs well beaten
- a quarter of a spoonful of soda dry

## Directions

1. Boil a cup of butter with a half pint of water
2. while it is boiling, stir in two cups of sifted flour
3. let it cool
4. when cool, add five eggs well beaten, and a quarter of a spoonful of soda dry
5. Drop this mixture with a teaspoon on tins
6. bake in a quick oven
