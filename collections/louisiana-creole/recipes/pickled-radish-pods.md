## Ingredients

- radish pods
- salt
- water
- white vinegar
- mace
- black pepper
- horse radish

## Directions

1. Put the pods, which must be gathered when very young, into salt and water all night
2. Boil the salt and water they were laid in
3. Pour it upon the pods, and cover the jar close, to keep in the steam
4. When it is nearly cold, pour it on again, and continue to do so till the pods are green
5. Drain them on a sieve and make a pickle for them of white vinegar, mace, black pepper and horse radish
6. Pour it boiling hot upon the pods
7. Tie them down, and put them away for use
