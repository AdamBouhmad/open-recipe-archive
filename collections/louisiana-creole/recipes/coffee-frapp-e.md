## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of coffee (black and very strong)
- A dash of old French Cognac
- 2 teaspoonfuls of sugar
- Finely-crushed ice

## Directions

1. Take a large glass, mix the above ingredients together, fill with finely-crushed ice, and pour into a small Burgundy glass, and serve very cold.
