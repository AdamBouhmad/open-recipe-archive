## Ingredients

- 8 Thin Slices of Ham
- Pepper to Taste
- Parsley to Garnish

## Directions

1. Slice the ham thin.
2. Heat the frying pan very hot.
3. Lay in the ham in its own fat and fry over a quick fire.
4. After taking the ham out of the pan, drop in the eggs.
5. If you do not like eggs fried on both sides, baste the eggs with the hot grease, and be sure to cook the yolks whole.
6. When they are well set, without being hard, take the eggs out and lay one on each slice of ham.
7. Garnish with parsley.
8. Sprinkle the eggs with salt and pepper very lightly and serve.
