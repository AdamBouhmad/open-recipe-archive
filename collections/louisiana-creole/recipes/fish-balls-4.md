## Ingredients

- Remains of any fish
- 1 large onion
- 1 sprig of thyme
- 3 sprigs of parsley
- 1 clove of garlic
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 egg
- Bread crumbs
- A dash of Cayenne

## Directions

1. Take the remains of any fish, season well with chopped parsley, onions, thyme and Cayenne, and a touch of garlic if the flavor is liked.
2. Then mince all well, adding a tablespoonful of butter and mix with one-third bread crumbs, mashed well.
3. Beat the yolk and white of an egg and roll the balls, shaped in the form of a cake, in this.
4. Pat with bread crumbs and fry in butter to a pale brown.
