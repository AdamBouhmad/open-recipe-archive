## Ingredients

- shrimps
- butter
- salt
- black pepper
- red pepper

## Directions

1. To every half pint of shrimps, put a tablespoonful of butter
2. Add a little salt, black and red pepper
3. Stew over a slow fire till perfectly done
4. When the gravy becomes of a pinkish hue, it is sufficiently cooked
