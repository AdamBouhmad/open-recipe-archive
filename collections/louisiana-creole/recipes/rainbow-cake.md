## Ingredients

- 6 Layers of Sponge or Pound Cake
- 3 Ounces of Pink Icing
- 3 Ounces of Violet Icing
- 3 Ounces of Chocolate Icing
- 3 Ounces of Vanilla Icing
- 3 Ounces of White Icing

## Directions

1. Make a good layer cake, either Pound or Sponge.
2. Prepare the Cream Icings.
3. Place these between the alternate layers in the order given.
4. Ice the cake nicely.
