## Ingredients

- 3 Cupfuls of Orange Juice
- 1 Cupful of Water
- 1 Cupful of Fine Old Port Wine
- % Cupful of Brandy
- 2 Small Cupfuls of Sugar
- 2 Ripe, Delicate-Skinned Lemons

## Directions

1. Wash the lemons and dry, and then slice them very thin, without grating or squeezing into a bowl
2. Take the sugar and water and make a boiling syrup, and pour over the lemons, and let the whole mixture cool
3. Add the orange juice
4. Take out the slices of lemon, and save them for later use
5. Strain the punch into an ice cream freezer, add the slices of lemon and liqueurs, and freeze as hard as possible for three or four minutes
6. Take a wooden spoon and detach the frozen punch from the sides of the freezer and from the bottom, mix in with the inner liquid mixture, and cover and freeze again for three or four minutes
7. Repeat this four or five times
8. Serve in punch glasses
