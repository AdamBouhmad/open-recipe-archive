## Ingredients

- fish
- vinegar
- butter
- flour
- 1 pound good butter
- 2 blades of mace
- 10 cloves
- cinnamon
- red pepper
- salt
- water
- 1 pint port wine
- lemon or horse radish

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well by putting it in a dish and pouring vinegar over it to take off the slime and scales
2. Put some butter into a frying pan and make it hot
3. Put the fish into the pan and let it remain over the fire for 6 minutes
4. Take it out and put it into the kettle in which it is to be stewed
5. Sprinkle some flour into the pan, put it over the fire for 3 minutes and pour it over the fish
6. Take 1 pound of good butter, roll it well in flour, and put it to the fish
7. Add 2 blades of mace, 10 cloves, a little cinnamon, red pepper, and salt, with water sufficient to keep it from burning
8. Put it over a slow fire to stew
9. When half done, add a pint of port wine
10. When done, put in a dish, pour the gravy over it, and garnish with lemon or horse radish
