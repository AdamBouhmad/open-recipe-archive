## Ingredients

- water
- sweet corn meal or oatmeal
- milk
- salt
- butter
- wine or brandy
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put a quart of water on to boil in a stew-pan
2. Take a tablespoonful of sweet corn meal, or oatmeal, make it into a batter with milk and salt
3. Stir it in the boiling water and let it boil gently for half an hour
4. When served it may be sweetened and nutmeg grated over it
5. If wanted for a strengthening nourishment, a bit of butter and a glass of wine or brandy may be added
6. Use very little salt
