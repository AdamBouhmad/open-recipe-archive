## Ingredients

- souse
- butter
- flour
- boiling water
- beaten cloves
- allspice
- 1 glass of Madeira wine
- oysters

## Directions

1. Fry the souse of a light brown
2. In another vessel make a gravy of a large lump of butter rolled in flour with a little boiling water
3. Add to it beaten cloves and allspice, with 1 glass of Madeira wine
4. A dozen or two of oysters dressed with the souse adds much to its excellence
