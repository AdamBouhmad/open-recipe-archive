## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of white sugar
- 1 lb. of flour
- 12 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 2 lbs. of citron
- 2 lbs. almonds
- 2 cocoanuts
- almond paste or icing

## Directions

1. Beat eggs separately
2. Cream butter and stir with the sugar
3. Cut citron small
4. Blanch almonds and cut into strips
5. Grate cocoanuts
6. Beat all a long time
7. Pour into a mould
8. Bake
9. Ice either with almond paste or icing into which you have stirred a grated cocoanut
