## Ingredients

- Spray of parsley
- Sprig of thyme
- Celery
- Parsley
- Bay leaf

## Directions

1. Tie together the spray of parsley, sprig of thyme, celery, parsley and bay leaf
2. This bouquet will flavor a gallon of soup if cooked for an hour
