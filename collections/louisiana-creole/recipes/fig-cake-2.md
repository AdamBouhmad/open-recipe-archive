## Ingredients

- 1 Cup Butter
- 2 Cups Sugar
- 1 Cup Milk
- 3 Cups of Seeded Raisins
- 1 Pound of Figs, Chopped Very Fine
- 1 Egg
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1/4 Grated Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream and add the sugar.
2. Beat very light, and add the egg.
3. Beat well and add the milk; carefully and gradually add the flour, into which you have sifted the baking powder.
4. Add the raisins and the figs, and beat thoroughly; add the grated nutmeg, and bake for an hour in a moderate oven.
5. Bake slowly, and use the broom wisp test.
6. If not done, bake longer, till the straw comes out clean and dry.
