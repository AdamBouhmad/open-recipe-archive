## Ingredients

- 6 tomatoes
- 4 green peppers
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1 1/2 cups of vinegar
- Cayenne and chili pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the vinegar
2. Add the chopped tomatoes and green peppers and the minced onion, adding a tablespoonful of sugar
3. Let all boil one hour
4. Season to taste
5. Strain and serve with any fish or meats
