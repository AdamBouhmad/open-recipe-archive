## Ingredients

- 4 Cups of Flour
- 1 Cup of Butter
- 1 Cup of Sweet Cream
- 3 Cups of Sugar
- The Whites of 10 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of White Rose Extract

## Directions

1. Proceed in exactly the same manner as for White Mountain Cake, only adding the cream just before adding the flour and the whites of the eggs
