## Ingredients

- Celery
- Salt and water

## Directions

1. Scrape and wash the celery nicely.
2. Cut off the long outer leaves, leaving the tips nice and crisp.
3. Set in a celery glass or bowl, with about one inch of salt and water, and serve as an appetizer, or hors d'oeuvre, at the beginning of the meal.
