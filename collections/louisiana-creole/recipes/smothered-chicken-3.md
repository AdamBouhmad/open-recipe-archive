## Ingredients

- 1 Chicken
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard

## Directions

1. After cleaning the young chicken, split down the back and dredge with salt and pepper
2. Put a tablespoonful of lard into the frying pan, and, when it is hot, add the chicken
3. Let it simmer gently for about fifteen minutes, then add a half cup of water, and set back on the stove, and let it simmer gently and steadily for about an hour
4. Serve with a garnish of chopped parsley
