## Ingredients

- 3 Fine Partridges
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- The Juice of a Sour Orange
- Toasted Croutons
- Watercress or Parsley to Garnish
- Drawn Butter Sauce

## Directions

1. Broil the Partridges according to recipe for Broiled Partridge
2. Prepare a Drawn Butter Sauce and add the juice and zest of a sour orange
3. Pour over the birds and let it soak down into the toast
4. Serve hot, with garnishes of parsley or watercress
