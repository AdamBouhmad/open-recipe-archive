## Ingredients

- eggs
- cold water
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- sage
- cloves
- thyme
- lard
- butter
- goose
- fat bacon
- water

## Directions

1. Beat 2 eggs until very light
2. Add a pint of cold water and stir in flour until you have a stiff batter
3. When quite smooth add salt and pepper to taste
4. Add 2 good sized onions, sage, cloves, and 2 tablespoons of thyme, chopped fine
5. Put a large spoonful of lard and one of butter into pan
6. When boiling stir in the batter and continue to stir until it becomes dry and light
7. After stuffing, put the goose in oven with a slice of fat bacon and pint of water
8. Baste with flour as it cooks, adding some boiling water when necessary
9. If not a mere gosling, parboil it first, more or less as may be necessary
