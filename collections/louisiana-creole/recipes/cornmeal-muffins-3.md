## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Cornmeal
- 1/4 Cup of Sifted Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 1 1/2 Cups of Buttermilk or Sweet Milk
- 2 Spoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Scald the meal.
2. Scald the milk and add the butter.
3. When lukewarm add to the cornmeal and beat well.
4. Add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs, and beat well.
5. Add the flour, into which the yeast powder must previously be sifted.
6. Mix well and add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
7. Beat steadily for about ten minutes, and then pour into greased muffin rings or gem pans, and bake for twenty minutes, in a quick oven.
8. If buttermilk is used, add a teaspoonful of soda, dissolved in the milk.
