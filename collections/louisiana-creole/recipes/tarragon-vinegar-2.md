## Ingredients

- 3/8 pint of tarragon leaves
- 1 quart of best cider or white wine

## Directions

1. Gather the tarragon leaves and expose them to the heat of the sun for several days.
2. Throw them into the best cider or white wine and steep for about two weeks.
3. Carefully strain the liquor, using a flannel bag.
