## Ingredients

- 4 cups of sifted flour
- 1 cup of butter
- 1 cup of sugar
- 1 cup of molasses
- ½ cup of sour milk
- 1 teaspoon of soda
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon of ginger
- 1 teaspoon of spice
- 1 teaspoon of cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon of cloves
- 1 teaspoon of mace

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together and bake
