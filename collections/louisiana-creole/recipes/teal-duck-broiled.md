## Ingredients

- 3 Pairs of Teal Ducks
- 6 Strips of Bacon
- Melted Butter
- Chopped Parsley
- Olives and Slices of Lemon to Garnish
- 6 Slices of Toast
- Currant Jelly

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the duck in exactly the same manner as for roasting, binding with the strip of bacon.
2. Place on a broiler, turning frequently, and let it broil for about thirty minutes, very slowly.
3. Serve with melted butter and chopped parsley spread over, and the juice of a lemon squeezed in.
4. Garnish the dish nicely with sprigs of parsley, slices of lemon and olives.
5. Serve with Currant Jelly.
