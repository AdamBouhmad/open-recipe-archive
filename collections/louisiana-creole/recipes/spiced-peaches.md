## Ingredients

- 9 lbs. of nice peaches
- 4 lbs. of sugar
- 1 pint of good vinegar
- whole allspice

## Directions

1. Pare, stone and halve the peaches first
2. Boil the peaches in water till tender
3. Put in vinegar and sugar, with a little whole allspice till done
