## Ingredients

- half a pound of grated apples
- half a pound of fine white sugar
- half a pound of butter
- six eggs
- the peel of one lemon grated
- the strained juice of two lemons
- pie paste

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of grated apples, half a pound of fine white sugar, half a pound of butter, six eggs well beaten, the peel of one lemon grated, and the strained juice of two
2. Line the dish with pie paste, put the pudding in, and bake in a quick oven
