## Ingredients

- 12 eggs, yolks and whites beaten well and separately
- 1 lb. of pounded and sifted loaf sugar
- grated peel of a lemon
- 1/2 lb. of sifted flour
- 1/2 lb. of sifted, dried-ground rice
- 4 ounces of sweet almonds, pounded
- 1 ounce of bitter almonds, pounded
- 1 tablespoonful of orange flower water

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks and whites well and separately
2. Mix all ingredients gradually
3. Beat them well
4. Paper the pan
5. Bake the cake for one hour
