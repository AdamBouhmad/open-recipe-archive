## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Irish Potatoes
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Flour
- 1½ Pints of Milk
- ½ Cake of Compressed Yeast

## Directions

1. Mash the boiled, left-over potatoes through a sieve, and then add the milk and the sifted flour.
2. Add the yeast, which you will have dissolved in a little warm water, and set the whole to rise for two hours.
3. At the end of that time add the eggs, beaten separately, and mix well.
4. Set to rise again for a half hour, till light, and then proceed to bake as in Plain Waffles.
