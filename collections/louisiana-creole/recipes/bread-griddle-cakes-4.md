## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Boiling Milk
- 2 Cups of Fine Bread Crumbs
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Melted Butter
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1/2 Cup of Sifted Flour
- Soda

## Directions

1. Soak the bread in the boiling milk, and as it cools beat it to a very smooth paste.
2. Then add the yolks of the eggs, beaten very light, and the soda, dissolved in a little warm water.
3. Finally add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, and a half cupful of sifted flour to bind well.
4. Bake on a hot griddle, butter, and serve with Louisiana Molasses or Syrup.
