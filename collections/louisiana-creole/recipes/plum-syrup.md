## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Plum Juice
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup

## Directions

1. Stone the plums, pound and mash well and set aside in a cool place for thirty-six hours
2. Proceed in exactly the same manner as in the recipe for Apricot Syrup
