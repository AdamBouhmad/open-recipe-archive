## Ingredients

- two pounds or pint cups full of light risen dough
- half a pound of butter
- one half pound of sugar
- one half pint of milk
- three eggs
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- flour
- boiling lard for frying

## Directions

1. Add butter, sugar, milk, eggs, cinnamon and nutmeg to the risen dough
2. Cover it and set it to rise
3. When light, add a little flour to stiffen the dough
4. Cut it into shapes
5. Fry in boiling lard
