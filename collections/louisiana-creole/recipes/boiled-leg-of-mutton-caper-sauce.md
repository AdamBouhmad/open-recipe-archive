## Ingredients

- 1 leg of mutton
- 1 herb bouquet
- salt and pepper to taste
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Rub the leg of mutton well with salt and pepper
2. Have ready a pot of boiling water, into which you have thrown the herbs, bay leaf, salt and pepper, allowing a teaspoonful each of the two latter ingredients
3. Put the leg of mutton into the water, being very careful to have it well covered with water, else the meat will blacken
4. Let it boil gently but steadily, allowing fifteen minutes to every pound of meat
5. When done, place on a dish and serve with a Caper Sauce
6. In serving slice nicely and put a few drops of lemon on each slice, and pour over the Caper sauce
