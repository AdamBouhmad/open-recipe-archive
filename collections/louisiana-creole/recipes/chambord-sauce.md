## Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Large Onion Minced
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 3 Large Tomatoes
- 1 Truffle, If Desired
- 6 Thinly Sliced Mushrooms
- 1 Pint Oyster Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 2 Sprigs of Parsley
- 2 Cloves, Mashed
- 4 Allspice, Ground

## Directions

1. Brown the onion in the butter, but do not let it burn.
2. Add three large tomatoes, chopped fine, with their juice and the finely-minced herbs, the thinly-sliced truffles and mushrooms.
3. Let these brown well for about ten minutes.
4. Then add the pint of oyster water, and season to taste.
5. Add, if you have them, three or four crawfish, chopped fine, and one dozen oysters.
6. Let all boil twenty minutes longer, and season to taste.
7. Serve with Baked Red Snapper and other baked fish.
