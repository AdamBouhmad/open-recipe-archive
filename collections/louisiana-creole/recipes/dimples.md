## Ingredients

- whites of three eggs
- three quarters of a pound of sugar
- three quarters of a pound of blanched almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of three eggs very stiff
2. Add gradually three quarters of a pound of sugar and beat till it is well mixed
3. Blanch almonds and cut them into pieces as small as peas
4. Stir them into the egg and sugar
5. Drop the mixture in spots as large as a half penny on white paper upon a tin
6. Bake in a cool oven
