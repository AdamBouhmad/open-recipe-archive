## Ingredients

- 1/2 Cup of Butter
- 1 1/2 Cups of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- The Whites of 6 Eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Add the well-beaten sugar and beat well
3. Add the yolks of the eggs and beat till very light
4. Add the milk and mix carefully
5. Sift the flour and baking powder together, and add gradually to the mixture
6. Turn into a buttered pan and bake in a moderate oven for forty-five minutes
