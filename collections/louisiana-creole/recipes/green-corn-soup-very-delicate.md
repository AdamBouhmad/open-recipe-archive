## Ingredients

- at least a pint of corn
- a quart of sweet milk
- a teaspoonful of salt
- a piece of butter the size of a hen’s-egg
- pepper to suit your taste

## Directions

1. Cut corn from the cob until you have at least a pint
2. Cover it with a quart of sweet milk
3. Let it boil half an hour
4. Add a teaspoonful of salt
5. Skim it carefully
6. Then throw into it a piece of butter the size of a hen’s-egg and pepper to suit your taste
7. Serve with rolls or toasted bread
