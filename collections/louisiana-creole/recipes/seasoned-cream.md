## Ingredients

- 1 gill of rich milk
- a wineglass of rose water
- 4 ounces of powdered white sugar
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 quart of good sweet cream
- preserves

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 gill of rich milk, a wineglass of rose water, and 4 ounces of powdered white sugar
2. Add the yolks of 2 eggs well beaten
3. Stir all into 1 quart of good sweet cream
4. Set it over hot coals
5. Let it boil, stirring it all the time
6. Take it off and when cool enough, pour it into a glass bowl
7. When cool, eat with it any sort of preserves
