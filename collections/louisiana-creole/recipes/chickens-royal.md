## Ingredients

- chickens
- good gravy and broth
- mushrooms
- morels
- truffles
- cocks-combs
- petty-patties
- sweet-breads

## Directions

1. Lard the chickens, and force the bellies and pass them off
2. Stew them in good gravy and broth, gold color
3. Make a ragoo of mushrooms, morels, truffles and cocks-combs
4. When your chickens are done enough dish them up, lay your ragoo over and garnish with petty-patties and fried sweet-breads
