## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Best Sauternes
- 1 Pint of Old French Cognac
- 1 Pint of Lemon Juice
- 1 Pint of Pineapple Juice
- 1 Quart of Seltzer Water
- ½ Grated Pineapple
- ¾ of a Sliced Pineapple
- 2 Dozen Cherries
- 1 Large Piece of Ice

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and water
2. Add the liquors and the fruit juices and the grated and sliced fruit
3. Stir well, set to cool and serve in small glass cups
