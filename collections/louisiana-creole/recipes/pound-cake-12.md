## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pound of Butter
- 1 Pound of Flour
- 10 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful Baking Powder
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lemon or Vanilla Extract
- a gill of brandy

## Directions

1. Wash the salt from one pound of butter. Then take a tablespoon and take out one heaping tablespoonful of butter from the pound
2. After washing the butter, beat it with the white pulverized sugar to a thick cream
3. Have the baking powder and the flour sifted together
4. Beat the yolks of the eggs well, and then add them to the sugar and butter, beating till very light
5. Then add a part of the flour, and beat light
6. Add a part of the whites of the eggs and mix thoroughly
7. Then alternate with the flour and the whites of the eggs until all are used up
8. Add the essence of lemon or vanilla and a gill of brandy, and continue beating vigorously for a half hour
9. Put the batter in a cake pan (a Turk's head), lined with a buttered paper, and bake for one hour in a moderate oven
10. Use the broom-straw test. If the cake is not quite done, bake a quarter of an hour longer
11. Never touch or move a pound cake in the oven till the center is set
