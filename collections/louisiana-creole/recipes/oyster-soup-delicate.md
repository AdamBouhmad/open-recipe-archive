## Ingredients

- the liquor of oysters
- a pint of water or milk (milk is preferable)
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- toasted bread-crumbs that have been toasted and pounded
- a quart of oysters to two quarts of liquor

## Directions

1. Take the oysters from their liquor
2. To every quart of the liquor add a pint of water or milk (milk is preferable)
3. Season with salt, pepper, butter, and toasted bread-crumbs that have been toasted and pounded
4. When this has boiled, put in a quart of oysters to two quarts of liquor
5. Let all boil a few minutes, and serve
