## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Brown Sugar
- 1 Pound of Butter
- 1 Pound of Flour
- 2 Pounds Currants
- 2 Pounds of Seeded Raisins
- ¾ Pound of Citron, Cut very Thin
- ¾ Pound of Chipped Figs
- 1 Glass of Jelly
- 1 Wineglass of Brandy
- 1 Pound Almonds, Blanched and Chopped
- 12 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Extract of Cinnamon
- 1 Teaspoonful Cloves
- 2 Teaspoonfuls Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Wash and cleanse the currants thoroughly.
2. Seed the raisins.
3. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream.
4. Then add the yolks of the eggs, well beaten.
5. Add one-half of the flour and the spices.
6. Mix all the fruit in the remaining flour, having first sliced the citron very thin, and add to the mixture.
7. Then add the jelly, which should be homemade and of the best quality.
8. Otherwise, add one cup of the best Louisiana molasses.
9. Mix thoroughly.
10. Add the whites of the eggs and the brandy last.
11. Divide into two tins, which must be lined with well-greased paper.
12. Bake for four hours in a slow oven.
13. Test with the broom wisp.
14. If not done through cook longer, watching carefully, and using the test till the straw is dry.
