## Ingredients

- One dozen large green apples
- water
- whites of 2 eggs
- sugar
- nutmeg
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil one dozen large green apples in as little water as possible
2. Pass them through a fine hair sieve
3. When cold, sweeten to the taste
4. Add the whites of 2 eggs well beaten
5. Beat the whole with a spoon until it is quite stiff
6. When ready for table, grate nutmeg over it
7. Serve with cream
