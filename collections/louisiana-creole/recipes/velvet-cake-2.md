## Ingredients

- one cup of yeast
- three eggs well beaten
- one quart of warm milk
- one quart of sifted flour
- salt
- a large spoonful of butter well beaten

## Directions

1. Combine yeast, eggs, milk, flour, salt, and butter
2. let it rise
3. Pour into greased muffin rings
4. bake
