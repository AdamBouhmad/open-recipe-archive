## Ingredients

- a dozen tart apples
- sugar
- melted butter
- juice and grated rind of two lemons
- a cup of milk
- four eggs
- half a nutmeg
- pastry
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Quarter, and stew a dozen tart apples
2. To each teacup of this pulp, rubbed through a sieve, add a teacup of sugar, half a cup of melted butter, the juice and grated rind of two lemons, a cup of milk, four eggs and half a nutmeg
3. Beat all together and bake in pans lined with pastry, with a rim of puff paste around the edge
