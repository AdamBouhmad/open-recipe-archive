## Ingredients

- 1 large or 4 small terrapins
- 1/2 pound of butter
- 6 eggs
- allspice
- mace
- pepper
- salt
- small quantity of eschalot
- 2 or 3 slices of bread

## Directions

1. Take 1 large or 4 small terrapins
2. After they have been opened, have the meat well chopped
3. Add to it half a pound of butter, 6 eggs, allspice, mace, pepper, salt, and a small quantity of eschalot
4. Add 2 or 3 slices of bread which have been soaked and mashed smooth
5. Stir these well together
6. Have the shells washed
7. Put the mixture into them
8. Bake a light brown
