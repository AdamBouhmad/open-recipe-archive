## Ingredients

- chickens
- bread and butter stuffing
- butter
- fat bacon
- sweet herbs
- flour
- hot water
- parsley
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Draw them and stuff with rich bread and butter stuffing
2. Baste them with butter and a little fat bacon, seasoned with sweet herbs
3. Brown nicely
4. Make gravy by sifting in a tablespoonful of flour and a cup of hot water
5. Add a little chopped parsley
6. Serve with hard-boiled eggs on the dish with the chickens
