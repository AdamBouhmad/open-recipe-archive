## Ingredients

- 9 Eggs
- 1 Pound of White Pulverized Sugar
- ½ Pound Flour
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 3 Bananas
- 1 Cup of Cream Sauce
- Whites of 3 Eggs
- three tablespoonfuls of sugar

## Directions

1. Make a Sponge Cake; slice in two across the cake.
2. Slice bananas lengthwise very nicely and lay on the bottom layer of cake.
3. Sprinkle with a little powdered sugar, and then spread over a little Cream Sauce, which you will have made to the consistency of Cream Puff filling.
4. Put over this the second layer of cake, and then repeat the process of spreading the bananas and cream on the layer.
5. Make a meringue with the whites of three eggs and three tablespoonfuls of sugar, and spread this over the whole.
6. Set in a quick oven and bake to a delicate brown.
7. Serve, either hot or cold, with a Wine Sauce, preferably Sauce au Vin de Madere.
