## Ingredients

- steak
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Get up a quick blaze in the stove with some kindlings.
2. Put in a pan, over the blaze, a little butter; when it is hot lay in your steak; let it fry quickly; while frying cover the pan.
3. Work some butter, salt and pepper together in a tin pan, and when the steak is done to taste, let it lie in this mixture a few minutes, and then serve.
4. Do not salt a steak until it is cooked as salt will toughen it and draw out its juices.
