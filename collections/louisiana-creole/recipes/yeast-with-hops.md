## Ingredients

- Irish potatoes
- hops
- water
- flour
- good yeast or yeast cake

## Directions

1. Peel and boil eight large Irish potatoes
2. Boil a handful of hops in a little water or in the water the potatoes were boiled in
3. Mash the potatoes fine and strain the water from the hops over them
4. Put in a cup of flour to the potatoes before the water is poured on
5. Mix all together and beat it
6. Put in half a cup of good yeast or a yeast cake
