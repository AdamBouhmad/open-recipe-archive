## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- one pound of sugar
- five and one-half ounces of butter
- whites of seventeen eggs
- two or three drops of oil of bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Cream the well-washed butter
2. add the sugar and cream again
3. alternate the whites with flour
4. flavor last of all
5. bake in a square or long pan
