## Ingredients

- fresh eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Break the whites and yolks separately
2. Beat the whites till they come to a light froth
3. Beat the yolks till they are quite light
4. Beat the whites and yolks together
5. Season well
6. Melt the butter in a frying pan, letting it grow hot but not brown
7. Pour in the mixture of egg
8. Let it stand about two minutes, shaking occasionally to prevent it from sticking to the pan
9. Continue shaking over a quick fire until the eggs are set
10. Roll the omelet, folding it in two or three rolls and making it long and narrow
11. Take a hot dish and turn the omelet into it
12. Garnish with parsley and serve hot immediately
