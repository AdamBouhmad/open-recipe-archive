## Ingredients

- A Pair of Rabbits
- 2 Onions
- 1 Square Inch of Ham
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Clove of Garlic, Chopped Very Fine
- 1 Herb Bouquet, Chopped Fine
- 1 Glass of Claret
- 1 Cup of Water
- 1 Can of Mushrooms
- Salt and Pepper
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the rabbit.
2. Wash well and cut into pieces at the joints, and rub well with salt and pepper.
3. Chop two onions very fine, and put them in the stewpan with a tablespoonful of melted butter.
4. Let them brown slightly; then add the rabbit.
5. Let it brown slightly, and then add one tablespoonful of flour, and let this brown a little.
6. Chop the square inch of ham very fine, mincing it, and add.
7. Then add the clove of garlic, and two sprigs each of thyme and parsley and a bay leaf, minced fine.
8. Let this brown nicely, and pour over one glass of good Claret.
9. Let this cook for ten minutes, stirring it constantly, so that it will not burn, and then add one cup of boiling water.
10. Stir well, season again to taste, and let it boil for thirty minutes, and serve hot.
