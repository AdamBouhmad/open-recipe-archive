## Ingredients

- a coffee-cup of tapioca
- a pint and a half of milk
- four eggs
- lemon and peach

## Directions

1. Put a coffee-cup of tapioca (soak it well first) into a pint and a half of milk
2. set it where it will get hot slowly
3. take it off when it boils
4. when cool add four well-beaten eggs
5. flavor with lemon and peach
6. sweeten it to taste
7. bake for an hour in a hot oven
8. If this is wanted for one person, take half the quantity of tapioca and milk
