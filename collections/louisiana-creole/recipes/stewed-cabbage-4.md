## Ingredients

- 1 head of cabbage
- 1 pound ham or salt pork
- Salt to taste
- Pinch of red pepper pod
- 1 tablespoonful of lard
- 2 chaurice or sausage

## Directions

1. Parboil the cabbage after cutting into quarters
2. Let it boil well about half an hour
3. Take it out of the water and drain nicely, separating the leaves down to the heart as it cools
4. Cut the ham into pieces of about two inches long
5. Take a tablespoonful of lard and put in a stewing pan
6. Put into this the ham or salt meat and let it fry well
7. Add two chaurice or sausage
8. As these brown well, moisten with half a cup of boiling water and let simmer gently for fifteen minutes
9. When well browned, add little by little the cabbage, stirring it well
10. Let it simmer gently for an hour and a half or longer, covering well and stirring frequently to prevent burning
11. Add an inch of red pepper pod cut fine and salt to taste
12. Serve hot
