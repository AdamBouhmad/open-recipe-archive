## Ingredients

- plantains
- flour
- lard
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Use plantains that are perfectly ripe and yellow all over
2. Peel them
3. Split them in long slips, and dredge slightly with flour
4. Have ready a frying pan filled with boiling lard
5. Put in the plantains and fry them well
6. When done, take them up on a perforated skimmer and drain back the lard into the frying pan
7. Dish, and send them to table with powdered sugar to eat with them
