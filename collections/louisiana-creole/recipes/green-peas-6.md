## Ingredients

- green peas
- salt
- a teaspoonful of pounded loaf sugar
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Use young and freshly shelled green peas
2. Wash them clean
3. Put them in a bag and the bag into plenty of boiling water with a little salt and a teaspoonful of pounded loaf sugar
4. Boil them till tender, taking from 3/4 to 1 hour
5. Never let them stand in the water after they are done
6. Season them with butter, salt and pepper
