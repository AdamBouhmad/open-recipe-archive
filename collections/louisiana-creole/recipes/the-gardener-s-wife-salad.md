## Ingredients

- 1 Carrot
- 3 Beets
- 3/4 Cup of Green Peas
- 1 Cup String Beans
- A Plain French Dressing

## Directions

1. Take fine strips of vegetables of different colors, cooked and cold, with green peas and string beans, and dress nicely with oil and vinegar and serve.
