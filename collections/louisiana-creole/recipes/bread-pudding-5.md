## Ingredients

- 1 loaf of bread
- 1 pint of boiled milk
- yolks of 6 eggs
- whites of 3 eggs well beaten
- 2 tablespoons of flour
- nutmeg
- 1 lb. of raisins

## Directions

1. Pour boiled milk on a loaf of bread
2. Mix into it when soft the yolks of 6 eggs and the whites of 3 eggs well beaten, flour, nutmeg, and raisins
3. Boil 2 hours and serve with sauce
