## Ingredients

- a quart of cranberries
- a pint of water
- a pound of clean brown sugar
- corn starch

## Directions

1. Pick a quart of cranberries free from imperfections
2. Put a pint of water to them, and put them in a stew-pan over a moderate fire
3. Add a pound of clean brown sugar, and stew them gently until they are soft
4. Mash them with a silver spoon and turn them into a dish to become cold
5. Make them in pies or tarts
6. If they are strained and are too thin to jelly, add a spoonful of corn starch to thicken
