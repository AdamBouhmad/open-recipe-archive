## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk or Cream
- 6 Eggs
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful Each of Coloring Extracts: Spinach Green, Essence of Vanilla, Orange Juice or Saffron, and Creme de Violettes

## Directions

1. Make a Plain Ice Cream
2. Divide into three distinct and equal portions
3. Color one with Spinach Green to obtain the green effect
4. Flavor another with Essence of Vanilla, and tinge with orange juice or a bit of saffron, to obtain the golden yellow
5. Color the third with a deep Creme de Violettes, to obtain the royal purple
6. Freeze in separate molds
7. Pack into one freezer, and when frozen remove according to directions for taking ice cream whole from a mold
8. Cut in slices and serve
