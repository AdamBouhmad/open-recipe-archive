## Ingredients

- nuts
- salt
- water
- common vinegar
- good vinegar
- allspice
- cloves
- horse radish
- made mustard
- red pepper
- garlic

## Directions

1. Put the nuts in strong salt and water for 12 days, changing the water several times
2. After which put them in fresh water a short time
3. Wipe them dry and put them in the sun until they become black
4. Put them in common vinegar for several weeks
5. Spice the good vinegar with allspice and cloves, horse radish, made mustard, red pepper, garlic, and a little salt
6. Boil the vinegar and pour it over the nuts while hot
