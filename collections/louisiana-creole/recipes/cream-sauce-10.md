## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 2 gills of fresh milk or cream
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Melt the butter in the saucepan
2. Add the flour gradually, letting it blend without browning in the least
3. Add the boiling milk or cream and stir without ceasing
4. Add salt and white pepper to taste
5. Serve immediately with boiled fish
