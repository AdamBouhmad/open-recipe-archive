## Ingredients

- 5 eggs per quart of milk
- sugar
- essence

## Directions

1. Make your custard, allowing 5 eggs to a quart of milk
2. Use much more sugar than for ordinary custard
3. Flavor with whatever essence may be preferred
4. Freeze
