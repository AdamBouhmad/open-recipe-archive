## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Mushrooms
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chopped Parsley
- 6 Slices of Toast
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Drain the mushrooms of their liquor, and place in a stewpan with the butter; season to taste with salt and pepper; cover and let them cook for ten minutes, tossing almost constantly.
2. Add the juice of a lemon and the chopped parsley.
3. Place six slices of toast on a dish, garnish these nicely with the mushrooms and serve.
