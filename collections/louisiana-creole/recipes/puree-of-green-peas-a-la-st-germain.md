## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Green Peas
- 1 Pint of Chicken Broth
- 1 Pint of Sweet Cream
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- 2 Sprigs of Mint
- A Pinch Each of Salt and White Pepper
- 1 Teaspoonful of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 8 Chicken Quenelles to Garnish

## Directions

1. Shell and clean the peas and put them in a saucepan or sautoire, with one pint of chicken broth and one pint of sweet cream.
2. Add an herb bouquet, in which you will have tied two sprigs of mint.
3. Let the peas cook for twenty minutes, or until very tender, and then remove the herb bouquet and mint; take from the fire, and run the peas through a sieve.
4. Season with salt and pepper and a little powdered sugar; add a tablespoonful of butter; set on the fire five minutes longer, and then serve on a hot dish with Chicken Quenelles to garnish.
