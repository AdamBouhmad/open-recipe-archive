## Ingredients

- herbs (spinach, lettuce and mustard tops predominate; green cabbage, raddish, and turnip tops in small quantities)
- 1 tablespoonful of lard
- 1 lb. of brisket of veal
- a pinch of flour
- onions
- ½ lb. of raw ham
- red or green pepper
- rice

## Directions

1. Thoroughly boil herbs together
2. Chop herbs fine
3. Put in a pot 1 tablespoonful of lard
4. When hot, put in 1 lb. of brisket of veal
5. Smother slowly for ½ hour
6. Add a pinch of flour
7. Let veal fry brown
8. Add onions to taste
9. Add ½ lb. of raw ham cut in small pieces
10. Put in herbs for a little while
11. Add boiling water, say about 1½ pints
12. Let stew together for about ¾ hour
13. Add red or green pepper and serve with rice in separate dish
