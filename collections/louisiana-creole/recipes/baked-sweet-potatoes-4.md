## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 Potatoes
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes, and scrub the skins, but do not peel them
2. Rub the skins of the potatoes lightly all over with a little lard, butter, or fat bacon to render the skins soft and pliable
3. Put the potatoes in a baking pan in the oven, and let them cook until their centers are mellow to the touch
4. Serve in their jackets immediately
