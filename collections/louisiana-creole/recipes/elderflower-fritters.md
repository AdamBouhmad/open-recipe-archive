## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Tender White Elder Flowers
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- White of 2 Eggs
- Creole Fritter Batter

## Directions

1. Select a sufficient quantity of beautiful, tender white flowers, and soak them in the whites of two eggs, beaten to a snowy froth, and well blended with white sugar.
2. Dip them by spoonfuls into the fritter batter, and fry according to directions.
