## Ingredients

- 6 Potatoes
- Cream Sauce
- Juice of a Lemon

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes whole.
2. Pour over them a Cream Sauce, remembering always to keep them whole.
3. Add the juice of a lemon.
4. Serve hot with fried chicken.
