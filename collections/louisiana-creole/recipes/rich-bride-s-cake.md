## Ingredients

- four pounds of sifted flour
- four pounds of sweet fresh butter
- two pounds of white powdered sugar
- six eggs for every pound of flour
- an ounce of ground mace or nutmeg
- a tablespoonful of lemon extract or orange-flower water
- four pounds of currants
- four pounds of raisins
- two pounds of citron
- one pound of blanched almonds
- half a pint of brandy

## Directions

1. Wash currants through several waters and pick clean from grit
2. Spread currants on a folded cloth to dry
3. Stone and cut raisins in two
4. Cut citron in slips
5. Chop or slice one pound of blanched almonds
6. Beat the yolks of the eggs with the sugar to a smooth paste
7. Beat the butter and flour together
8. Add the yolks and sugar to the butter and flour
9. Add the spice and half a pint of brandy
10. Beat the whites of the eggs to a froth and add them
11. Stir all together for some time
12. Strew half a pound of flour over the fruit and mix it through
13. By degrees stir the fruit into the cake
14. Butter large tin basins and line them with white paper
15. Put in the mixture two inches deep
16. Bake in a moderate oven two hours
17. Prepare the fruit the day before making the cake
