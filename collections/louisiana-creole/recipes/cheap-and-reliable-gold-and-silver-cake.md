## Ingredients

- two cups of butter
- four cups of sugar
- two cups of sweet milk or water
- eight cups of sifted flour
- four teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- two teaspoonfuls of soda
- yolks and whites of eight eggs

## Directions

1. cream butter and sugar together
2. beat separately the yolks and whites of eight eggs
3. Take half the batter; use the yolks for the gold cake, and the whites for the silver cake
4. Flavor differently, as with rose and lemon
