## Ingredients

- two gills of celery seed
- sharp vinegar

## Directions

1. Take two gills of celery seed
2. pound them and put them in a bottle
3. fill the bottle with sharp vinegar
4. shake it every day for two weeks
5. then strain and bottle it for use
