## Ingredients

- sugar equal in weight to the eggs used
- flour equal to half the weight of eggs used
- eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and sugar together
2. Add the flour
3. Beat the whites to a high froth
4. Add the whites last
5. Stir them thoroughly together
6. Put into a paper-lined basin or pan
7. Bake in a quick oven
8. Optional: flavor with lemon, rose, or vanilla extracts, nutmeg, or brandy
9. Optional: add pounded almonds or grated cocoanut
