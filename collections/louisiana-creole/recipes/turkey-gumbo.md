## Ingredients

- The Remains of a Turkey
- 1/2 Pound of Lean Ham
- 2 Tablespoons of Butter or 1 of Lard
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 3 Sprigs of Parsley
- 3 Dozen Oysters
- 1 Large Onion
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 2 Quarts of Oyster Water
- 1/2 Pod of Red Pepper, Without the Seeds
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Strip the turkey meat, black and white, from the bones and carcass
2. Chop fine and add to the hot lard
3. Add the ham, cut fine into dice shapes
4. Proceed exactly as in the Chicken Gumbo recipe, adding the boiling water and throwing in the bones and carcass of the turkey
5. At the proper time remove the carcass and bones
6. Add the oysters
7. Remove the pot and File the gumbo
8. Serve with boiled rice
