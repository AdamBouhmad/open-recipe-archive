## Ingredients

- turtle steaks from the thick part of the turtle fins
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- flour
- butter
- lard
- water
- lemon

## Directions

1. Season the steaks with pepper, salt and mace
2. Flour them
3. Fry in butter and lard mixed together
4. When fried pour a little water over them
5. Let them simmer for a quarter of an hour
6. Just before serving squeeze a lemon over the steaks
