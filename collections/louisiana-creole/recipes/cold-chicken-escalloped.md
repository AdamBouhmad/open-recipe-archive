## Ingredients

- cold chicken
- gravy or hot water
- salt
- pepper
- sweet fresh butter
- powdered cracker
- celery or pickle

## Directions

1. Mince cold chicken without the skin
2. Wet it with gravy or hot water
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. To the minced meat of one chicken, put two ounces of sweet, fresh butter, cut small
5. Rub tin or silver scallop pans with butter
6. Strew over the bottom powdered cracker
7. Lay the minced chicken in
8. Strew cracker over the top
9. Bake in a hot oven long enough to brown the top
10. Serve with celery or pickle
