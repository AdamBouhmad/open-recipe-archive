## Ingredients

- one quart of milk or milk and water
- one pint of corn meal
- four tablespoonfuls of flour
- one tablespoonful of salt
- a teaspoon of butter

## Directions

1. Beat up all ingredients
2. Bake on a griddle or in shallow pans
