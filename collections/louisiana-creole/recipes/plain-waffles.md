## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 2 Cups of Milk
- 1⅛ Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Large Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and salt and baking powder together
2. Beat the yolk of the egg till very light
3. Add the melted butter to the milk, which should be scalded and grown lukewarm
4. Mix in the whites of the eggs
5. Add the flour gradually, making a nice, light batter, adding more milk if it appears stiff
6. Have your waffle irons ready, thoroughly heated
7. Grease the interior of the irons with melted lard using a brush or cloth
8. Pour the batter into a pitcher and fill the irons until the elevations are lightly covered
9. Close the irons quickly and turn it over
10. Bake the waffle about two minutes, or till a nice, delicate brown
11. Carefully remove from the irons and place in a hot plate or waffle dish in the mouth of the oven
12. Send to the table very hot
