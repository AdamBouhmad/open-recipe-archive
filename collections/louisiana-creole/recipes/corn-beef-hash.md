## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Corn Beef, Cooked
- 1 Pint of Left-Over Potatoes
- 1 Grated Onion
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Cup of Broth or Water
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig of Parsley
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Taste
- Slices of Buttered Toast
- Sliced Lemon

## Directions

1. Mix the meat thoroughly after mincing fine together with the chopped potatoes
2. Grate the onion and a half clove of garlic if desired, and chop the herbs fine and mix thoroughly with the beef, seasoning highly
3. Put the butter into the frying pan, add the meat and the broth or water, and stir constantly till it boils
4. Spread, after it has cooked for about twenty minutes on slices of buttered toast
5. Pour over the gravy and garnish with sprigs of parsley and sliced lemon
