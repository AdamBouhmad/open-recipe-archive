## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of White Honey
- 2 Ounces of Coriander
- 6 Ounces of Fine White Pulverized Sugar
- 3 Quarts of Water
- 1 Ounce of the Fresh Zest of Lemon
- 1 Ounce of Cloves
- ½ Ounce of Benzoine
- 1 Ounce of Storax Calamite
- ½ Drachm of Tincture of Vanilla
- 4 Ounces of Orange Flower Water
- 5 Quarts of Highly Rectified Spirits

## Directions

1. Pound to a powder all the ingredients which require it, mix with the honey, benzoin, etc., pour over the alcohol, and steep for fifteen days.
2. Distill the liquor till it is reduced to four quarts, using a water bath, or bain-marie, for the distillation.
3. Make a syrup of six pounds of fine white sugar, add the Vanilla, color a deep red with Cochineal, and add to the liquor.
4. Blend and shake well, bottle and keep in a cool place.
