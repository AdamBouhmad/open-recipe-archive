## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of flour
- 1 quart of sugar
- 8 spoonfuls of milk
- butter sufficient to knead them
- the rind of 3 lemons and the juice of 1

## Directions

1. Mix the ingredients together
2. Knead and bake
