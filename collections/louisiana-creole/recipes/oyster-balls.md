## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Chopped Oysters
- 2 Pints of Chopped Sausage Meat
- 1 Egg
- Grated Bread Crumbs

## Directions

1. To every pint of chopped oysters add one pint of chopped sausage meat.
2. Roll in bread crumbs; season highly.
3. Add one egg and roll in bread crumbs.
4. Make into small cakes and fry In boiling lard.
5. Serve hot.
