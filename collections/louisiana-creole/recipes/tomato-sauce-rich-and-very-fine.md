## Ingredients

- a dozen large ripe tomatoes
- four ounces of raw ham
- a dozen small eschalots
- a bunch of thyme or parsley
- butter
- a cupful of good soup-stock
- salt

## Directions

1. Pick off the stalks from the tomatoes
2. Extract the seeds and watery juice by squeezing them in the hand
3. Place the pulp in a stewpan with four ounces of raw ham, cut into cubes
4. Add a dozen small eschalots and a bunch of thyme or parsley
5. Throw in a little butter, and fry all gently until the tomatoes soften sufficiently to be passed through a strainer
6. Mix this puree with a cupful of good soup-stock
7. Add the strained juice of the tomatoes
8. Let boil fifteen minutes, then set it by to clarify
9. Serve it hot
