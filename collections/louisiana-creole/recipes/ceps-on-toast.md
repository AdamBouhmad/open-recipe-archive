## Ingredients

- 3 ceps
- The juice of 1 lemon
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- 6 slices of toast

## Directions

1. Drain the ceps from their oil, slice nicely and fry lightly in a frying pan.
2. When thoroughly heated take from the pan, sprinkle lightly with chopped parsley and lemon juice.
3. Arrange daintily on slices of toast and send to the table hot.
