## Ingredients

- 2 pints of pumpkin or cashaw
- A dressing of butter or gravy

## Directions

1. Cut the pumpkin in halves and then into quarters.
2. Remove the seeds, but do not peel the rind.
3. Place in a baking pan with the rind downwards.
4. Bake until so tender that it may be pierced easily with a fork.
5. Serve in the rind at the table.
