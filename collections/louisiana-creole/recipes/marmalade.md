## Ingredients

- Ripe fruit (peaches, quinces, apples, oranges or cranberries)
- Three quarters of a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit
- A little water (half a cup to a pound)

## Directions

1. Boil fruit to a pulp with a little water
2. Crush the fruit
3. Put in three quarters of a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit, add a little water (half a cup to a pound)
4. Boil until it is a jellied mass
5. When done, put it in glass or white earthenware
