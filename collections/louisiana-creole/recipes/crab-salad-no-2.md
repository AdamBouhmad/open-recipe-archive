## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Large Crabs
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Olive Oil
- 1 Tablespoonful of French Vinegar
- 1 Saltspoonful Salt
- 1 Head Lettuce
- Pepper and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Pick the boiled crabs carefully, keeping the pieces as large as possible
2. Lay in a salad bowl
3. Mix a dressing of two tablespoonfuls of sweet oil, one of French vinegar, one saltspoon of salt, black pepper and Cayenne, according to taste, and pour over the crabs which have been cooled in the ice box
4. Clean the lettuce well and put a row of crisp leaves around the edge of the salad dish
5. Garnish nicely with sliced lemon and hard-boiled eggs sliced
