## Ingredients

- 3½ Pounds of Pears
- 2 Pounds of Sugar
- Grated rinds of several lemons

## Directions

1. Peel the pears and cut them into quarters, extracting all the seeds.
2. Then throw them into fresh water, sufficient to half cover, and set them on the fire.
3. When they begin to grow soft, mash them with a wooden spoon.
4. Then add the sugar and the grated rinds of several lemons; mix well.
5. Set all back upon the fire.
6. Stir the mixture while boiling till it is of the proper consistency.
7. Pour it into jars or glasses.
