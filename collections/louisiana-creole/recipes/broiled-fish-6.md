## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- pepper
- sweet oil or butter

## Directions

1. Clean the fish, without cutting off the head and tail.
2. When the fish is large, split down the back; else broil whole.
3. Have a clear moderate fire.
4. Before placing on the broiler, rub the fish well with salt and pepper, mixed in a little sweet oil or a little butter.
5. If the fish is small, broil on a quick, clear fire; if large, the fire must be moderate.
6. Expose first the flesh side to the fire, and then the skin.
7. When the fish is done through and through, remove the gridiron from the fire, and loosen the fins from the broiler with a knife.
8. Place the hot dish over the fish and turn the two back again, thus separating the gridiron from the fish and placing the latter in the dish.
9. Butter well, season with a little pepper and salt, if deemed necessary.
10. Pour over a tablespoonful of chopped parsley and lemon juice.
11. Serve with garnishes of sliced lemon and parsley, or garnishes of delicate green lettuce leaves.
