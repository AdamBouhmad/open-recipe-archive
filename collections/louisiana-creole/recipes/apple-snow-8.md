## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Apples
- 1 Cup Fine White Sugar
- The Whites of Six Fresh Creole Eggs
- The Juice of One Lemon
- grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Pare and core and cut up the apples, and steam them in two tablespoonfuls of water, with a little grated lemon peel, till quite soft.
2. Then press them through a sieve, and add the sugar and the lemon juice.
3. Beat the whites of the eggs to a very stiff froth, and add two-thirds to the apples, stirring all the time.
4. When the eggs and apples are very light beat this with a little powdered white sugar to form a meringue.
5. Place the apples in custard glasses, heap on the meringue, and serve immediately.
