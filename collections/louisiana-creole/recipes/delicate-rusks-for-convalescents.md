## Ingredients

- half a pint of new milk
- one cup of hop yeast
- flour
- half a pint of milk
- one cup of sugar
- one cup of butter
- one egg
- one nutmeg

## Directions

1. Add flour to half a pint of new milk and one cup of hop yeast to make a batter
2. Set the sponge at night
3. In the morning add half a pint of milk, one cup of sugar, one cup of butter, one egg, one nutmeg, and flour to make it sufficiently stiff
4. Let it rise, then roll it and cut it out
5. Let it rise again and then bake
