## Ingredients

- peel of 3 sweet oranges
- 3 pints of cold water
- 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Put the peel of 3 sweet oranges in 3 pints of cold water
2. Boil down to 1 quart
3. Add 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar
4. Boil together
