## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoonfuls of mashed potatoes
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of sugar
- 1 pint of milk
- 4 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 egg

## Directions

1. Beat all well together, and add one egg, well beaten.
2. Bake in waffle iron, and serve with butter spread over.
