## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Dough from Roly Poly recipe
- White Sugar
- Lemon or Orange Sauce

## Directions

1. Seed the oranges and lay them thinly sliced all over the sheet of dough.
2. Sprinkle lightly with white sugar, and roll up and fasten closely in the pudding cloth.
3. Boil for one hour, and eat with Lemon or Orange Sauce.
