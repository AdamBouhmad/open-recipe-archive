## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. of flour
- 3 lbs. of sugar
- 3 lbs. of butter
- 6 eggs
- 1 quart of milk
- 1 pint of wine
- 1 pint of yeast
- 4 lbs. of fruit
- 1 ounce of citron
- 1 ounce of nutmeg
- mace to the taste

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Divide the quantity and bake in moulds, which hold about 2 lbs.
