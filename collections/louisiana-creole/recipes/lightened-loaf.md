## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of flour
- 1 pint of sugar
- 1 pint of raisins
- 1 lb. of butter
- 5 eggs
- ½ pint of yeast
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix a part of the flour, milk and yeast at night to rise
2. Work in the other materials in the morning
