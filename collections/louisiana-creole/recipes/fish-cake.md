## Ingredients

- fish
- water
- herbs
- pepper
- salt
- bread
- parsley
- white of an egg
- melted butter
- bread raspings

## Directions

1. Take any kind of fish, and cut off the flesh
2. put the heads, bones, fins, etc., on the fire, with a little water
3. season with herbs, a little pepper and salt, and stew for gravy
4. mince the flesh of the fish fine, and mix it with a third of bread, parsley, pepper and salt
5. add the white of an egg, and a small quantity of melted butter
6. Form it into the shape of a cake
7. cover it with raspings of bread
8. fry it a pale brown
