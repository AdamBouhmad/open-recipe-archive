## Ingredients

- 4 Fresh Fine Tomatoes
- French Dressing

## Directions

1. Slice the tomatoes nicely and place on a salad dish.
2. Never peel or scald tomatoes intended for salad.
3. Serve nicely with a plain French dressing or any of the above dressings.
