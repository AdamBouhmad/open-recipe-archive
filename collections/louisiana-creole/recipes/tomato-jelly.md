## Ingredients

- ripest and best tomatoes (slices)
- brown sugar (1 lb. per pint of juice, or 1/2 lb. for meat)
- eggs (whites of 8 per gallon of juice)
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fill a large jar with slices of the ripest and best tomatoes
2. Lay a cloth over the jar, and over that put a piece of dough, to keep in the heat
3. Place the jar in a large pot of water, and boil 4 or 5 hours constantly
4. Strain the juice through a coarse hair sieve
5. To every pint of juice add 1 lb. of the best brown sugar, if you wish the jelly very sweet, or half that quantity, if to eat with meat
6. Add the whites of 8 eggs to every gallon of juice
7. Skim it and boil till nearly half evaporated
8. Put it into glasses, and keep them in the sun till sufficiently thick
9. A very good jelly may be made to eat with meat, by putting salt, pepper, mace and nutmeg, instead of sugar
