## Ingredients

- beef
- water

## Directions

1. To have it very tender it should be boiled slowly, and the pot well skimmed
2. The meat should be well covered with water, so that the skim may be removed easily
3. When beef is very salt it should boil 1/2 of an hour; then take it up, throw away the water it has boiled in
4. Fill up the pot with fresh water, replace the beef and let it boil gently for 3 hours
5. Observe to take off all the scum as it rises
6. The round is the most choice piece to boil, next, the H bone
