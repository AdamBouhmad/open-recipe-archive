## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 2 cups of brown sugar
- 4 cups of flour
- 2 cups of butter
- 1 cup of cream
- 2 cups of molasses
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 cup of ginger

## Directions

1. Add brown sugar to eggs
2. Add flour, butter, cream, molasses, soda, and ginger
3. Beat very light and bake in cups or a pan
