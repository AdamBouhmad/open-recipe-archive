## Ingredients

- 5 pounds of Lean Beef
- 5 Quarts of Water
- 2 Turnips
- 2 Carrots
- 2 Onions
- 2 Leeks
- A Small Stalk of Celery
- 3 Tomatoes
- A Small half-Head of White Cabbage
- 1 Gill of green Peas
- A tablespoonful of lard

## Directions

1. Set the beef and water in a close vessel where they will heat gradually
2. After boiling five or six hours add the following vegetables, which have been carefully prepared
3. Cut the vegetables into long, thin shreds
4. Take a tablespoonful of lard, heat and add the vegetables, letting them fry or smother until a golden brown
5. Add to the boiling broth
6. If fresh peas are used they must be boiled apart
7. If canned peas, simply add to the broth after throwing in the vegetables
8. Let them cook in the broth one hour longer and serve hot with the vegetables
