## Ingredients

- 100 River or Lake Shrimp
- 1 Small Onion
- 1 Bunch of Celery
- 3 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- Salt and Pepper
- Sliced Lemons
- Beets
- Celery Tips
- Mayonnaise Sauce

## Directions

1. Boil and pick the shrimp.
2. If Lake shrimp are used, serve whole; if River shrimp, slice in two.
3. Season well with salt and pepper.
4. Chop celery fine and add a little onion.
5. Place the shrimp in the salad dish and pour over all fine Mayonnaise Sauce.
6. Garnish with sliced hard-boiled eggs, sliced lemon, beets and celery tops.
