## Ingredients

- 6 fine, ripe tomatoes
- 2 onions
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of minced ham
- 1/2 clove of garlic
- bread crumbs
- salt, pepper and Cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Scald and skin six fine, ripe tomatoes and chop them fine
2. Chop two onions and mince the garlic very fine
3. Add a large spoonful of bread crumbs
4. Fry them with a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan till quite brown
5. Add the tomatoes and salt, pepper and Cayenne to taste
6. Let all stew for at least an hour
7. Prepare the eggs as for Ham Omelet
8. When the tomatoes are quite done have ready a heated frying pan and a half tablespoonful of butter
9. Pour this into the pan
10. As they become set pour in the center the tomatoes
11. Fold the omelet over and cook for two minutes longer
12. Roll gently into a dish and serve hot
