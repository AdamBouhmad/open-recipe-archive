## Ingredients

- one cup of butter and lard mixed
- one cup of sugar
- one cup of molasses
- half a cup of water
- one tablespoonful of ginger
- one teaspoonful of soda in hot water
- flour

## Directions

1. Mix butter and lard
2. Combine with sugar, molasses, water, and ginger
3. Dissolve soda in hot water and add
4. Add flour enough to roll the dough soft
