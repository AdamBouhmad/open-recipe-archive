## Ingredients

- The Bouilli
- 1 Dozen Shallots
- 1 Glass of White Wine
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Sauce Espagnole
- Beef Marrow from the Bone
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Slice the left-over beef.
2. Hash the shallots into very fine pieces; add a glass of white wine, pepper and salt to taste, and boil to half the quantity over a brisk fire.
3. Add the mashed beef marrow from the bone and two teaspoonfuls of Sauce Espagnole, first melting the marrow in a little bouillon.
4. Stir rapidly over the fire, and as soon as it begins to bubble, withdraw it and set it back on the stove, letting it simmer gently for a quarter of an hour.
5. Add the sliced beef for about ten minutes and then serve with Croûtons or fried crusts.
