## Ingredients

- 6 Head of Lettuce
- 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 12 Sausages (Chaurice)
- 1½ Cups Bread Crumbs
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Garlic
- Lard

## Directions

1. Take six fine, firm heads of lettuce, strip off all the green leaves, wash thoroughly and soak and blanch in boiling water.
2. Then throw them into cold water.
3. When very cold squeeze in a towel till they are thoroughly dry and cut off the stalks from below without injuring the heart.
4. Fill this open place with forcemeat balls, made from the bouilli, fried in lard with fresh bread crumbs soaked in bouillon and worked into the meat, chopped up with pepper, salt and garlic, and add one or two hard-boiled eggs.
5. Tie the balls up and cook without adding water and fill the heart of the lettuce.
6. This may be served around the body of the bouilli and makes a beautiful garnish.
