## Ingredients

- 2 cups of molasses
- 1 cup of grated chocolate
- 1 cup of milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls of vanilla
- 1 tablespoon of butter

## Directions

1. Boil 25 minutes
2. Set to cool in buttered pans
3. Mark in squares
