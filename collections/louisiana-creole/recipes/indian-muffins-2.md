## Ingredients

- 1 quart of boiling water
- cornmeal
- flour
- a teaspoonful of salt
- 1 wine glass of yeast

## Directions

1. Stir into the boiling water as much cornmeal as will make a nice batter
2. When warm, put in as much flour as will make a stiff batter
3. Add salt and yeast
4. Set it to rise
5. When risen, pour into the muffin rings, and bake slowly in an oven
6. Serve them with nice fresh butter
