## Ingredients

- two wineglasses of best French brandy
- one half wineglass of kirsh
- one half wineglass of maraschino
- a small quantity of cinnamon and allspice
- about ten cubes of white sugar

## Directions

1. Pour into a large silver bowl two wineglasses of best French brandy, one half wineglass of kirsh, the same of maraschino, and a small quantity of cinnamon and allspice
2. Put in about ten cubes of white sugar; do not crush them, but let them become saturated with the liquor
3. Remove the lumps of sugar, place in a ladle and cover with brandy
4. Ignite it, then lift it with the contents from the bowl, but do not mix
5. After it has burned about fifteen minutes serve in wine glasses
