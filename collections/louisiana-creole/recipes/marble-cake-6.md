## Ingredients

- % Cup of Butter
- % Cup of Milk
- 1% Cups of White Pulverized Sugar
- 2% Cups of Flour
- The Whites of 4 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of Extract of Lemon
- % Cup of Butter
- 1 Cup of Brown Sugar
- The yolks of 4 Eggs
- % Cup of Milk
- 1% Cups of Flour
- 2 Ounces of Melted Chocolate
- 1 Teaspoonful Vanilla
- 1 Teaspoonful of Cloves (if desired)
- 1 Teaspoonful Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream, and gradually add the sugar, beaten thoroughly till very light.
2. Then add the milk, and stir carefully.
3. Add one-half of the flour, evenly, divided.
4. Beat vigorously, and add the Lemon Extract.
5. Mix well, and add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a thick froth, and the remainder of the flour.
6. Then stand this mixture to one side while you make the dark part of the cake.
7. Beat the butter and the sugar to a cream, till very light, and then add the beaten yolks of the eggs, and beat till very light.
8. Add the milk, and stir carefully.
9. Then add the flour, and blend and beat till smooth.
10. Dissolve the chocolate in a little of the milk, and add, mixing well, and then add the vanilla essence and the baking powder to the mixture.
11. Add the baking powder to the first mixture, and mix thoroughly and lightly.
12. Then grease a piece of brown paper, and line the cake pan, and put in first a spoonful of the white mixture and then one of the dark, and continue alternating thus till all is used.
13. Set in a moderate oven, and bake for three-quarters of an hour.
14. Try the broom-straw test, and if it comes out clean and dry, the cake is done.
15. If not, bake a quarter of an hour longer.
16. The cake must cool in the pan in which it was baked.
