## Ingredients

- 1 cup of butter
- 2 cups of white sugar
- whites of 6 eggs, beaten stiff
- 1 cup of sweet milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 4 cups of flour
- vanilla, bitter almond or rose water for flavoring

## Directions

1. Stir the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add the beaten egg whites
3. Mix the soda into the sweet milk and add to the mixture
4. Stir the cream of tartar into the flour and add to the cake
5. Flavor with vanilla, bitter almond or rose water
6. Bake
