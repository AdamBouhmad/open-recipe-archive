## Ingredients

- two cups of flour
- a teaspoonful of yeast powder per cup of flour
- the yolks of three eggs
- one cup of fine white sugar
- half a cup of water mixed with extract or wine
- the whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Sift the flour and to each cup put a teaspoonful of yeast powder
2. Beat the yolks of three eggs and one cup of fine white sugar together with half a cup of water mixed with extract or wine
3. beat this well in the yolks and sugar (only half a cup)
4. froth up the whites of the eggs, add them
5. last of all, beat in the flour with the powder in it
6. Bake quickly in square or jelly cake pans
