## Ingredients

- 2 salt mackerel
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- Chopped parsley
- Sliced lemon

## Directions

1. Soak the mackerel overnight, and in the morning take out of the water, wash thoroughly, taking off every portion of salt, and wash again.
2. Have ready a deep pan of boiling water; place the mackerel within and let it boil ten or fifteen minutes until done, which can be known by the flesh beginning to part from the bones.
3. Serve whole on a platter garnished with parsley.
4. Pour over the mackerel melted butter and chopped parsley, and bring to the table very hot.
