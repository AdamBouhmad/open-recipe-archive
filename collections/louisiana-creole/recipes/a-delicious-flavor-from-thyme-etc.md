## Ingredients

- thyme
- mint
- sweet marjoram
- rosemary
- strong vinegar or brandy

## Directions

1. pick thyme, mint, sweet marjoram and rosemary from the stalks when gathered in full perfection
2. put into a large jar
3. pour strong vinegar or brandy over them
4. let them stay in this twenty-four hours
5. take the herbs out, and throw in fresh bunches
6. do this three times
7. strain the liquor or vinegar
8. Cork and seal the bottles tight
9. Do not let the herbs stay more than twenty or twenty-four hours in the liquid before straining
