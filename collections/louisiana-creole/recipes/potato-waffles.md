## Ingredients

- 1 lb. potatoes
- 1 lb. flour
- 4 eggs
- milk
- 1 cup yeast

## Directions

1. Mix 1 lb. of potatoes, 1 lb. of flour, 4 eggs, and as much milk as will make it the consistency of fritters
2. Add 1 cup of yeast
