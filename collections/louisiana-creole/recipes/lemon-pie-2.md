## Ingredients

- three lemons
- a cup and a half of powdered sugar
- three tablespoonfuls of butter
- yolks of four eggs
- rich puff paste
- whites of four eggs
- powdered loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Grate the rind and express the juice of three lemons
2. Rub together a cup and a half of powdered sugar and three tablespoonfuls of butter
3. Beat up the yolks of four eggs, and add to the butter and sugar, lastly the lemon
4. Bake on a rich puff paste without an upper crust
5. While the pie is baking beat up the whites of the four eggs with powdered loaf sugar
6. Spread it over the top of the pie when done
7. Set back in the oven a few moments to brown lightly
