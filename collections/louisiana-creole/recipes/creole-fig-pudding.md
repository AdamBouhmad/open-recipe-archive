## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pound of Figs
- 3/4 Pound of Grated Bread Crumbs
- 1 Teacupful of Milk
- 2 1/2 Ounces of Sugar
- 3 Ounces of Butter
- 2 Eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the figs fine, and beat the sugar and the yolks of the eggs to a thick cream.
2. Add the melted butter and the bread crumbs, which have been wet and squeezed thoroughly.
3. Add the milk and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a thick froth.
4. Butter a mold and sprinkle with flour.
5. Steam lightly for three hours.
6. Serve with a Hard, Cream or Brandy Sauce.
