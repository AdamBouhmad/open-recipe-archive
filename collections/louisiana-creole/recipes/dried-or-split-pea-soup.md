## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Dried or Split Peas
- 1 Quart of Boiling Water
- 1 Small Bunch of Celery
- 1 Bunch of Parsley
- 1 Quart of Good Milk or Cream
- 2 Onions
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Soak the peas overnight after washing them in cold water and rejecting all that float.
2. In the morning drain off the water and cover the peas again with one quart of boiling water, setting them back on the stove and letting them cook slowly until tender.
3. Cut up the onion and parsley and celery into fine pieces and add to the boiling peas.
4. When perfectly tender remove from the stove and press through a sieve or colander and add the salt and pepper.
5. Return the soup to the fire and let it boil up once.
6. Just before serving add the rich cream or milk, stirring well.
7. Serve with Croqutons or Oyster Crackers.
