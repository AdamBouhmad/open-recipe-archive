## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Rice
- 1 Pint of Cowpeas
- 1 Large Onion
- 1/4 Pound of Salt Meat
- 1 Square Inch of Ham
- Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Chop the small meat, after washing, into dice, and mince the ham.
2. Boil the cowpeas and the salt meat and ham together.
3. Add the onion, minced very fine.
4. Boil the rice according to recipe for boiled rice.
5. Chop the meat well.
6. After the peas and the rice are cooked, pour the rice into the pot of peas, which must not be dry, but very moist.
7. Mix well.
8. Let all simmer for five minutes, and then serve hot.
