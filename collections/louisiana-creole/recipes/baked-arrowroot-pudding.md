## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 2 large tablespoonfuls of butter
- 2 large tablespoonfuls of rose water
- 8 egg yolks
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1 stick of cinnamon
- arrowroot

## Directions

1. Boil the milk and cinnamon together
2. Take the saucepan from the fire, and stir in quickly the arrowroot, which must be previously rubbed smooth in a little water or cold milk
3. Pour it into the dish in which it is to be baked, and while warm stir in the butter
4. The stick of cinnamon must be taken out of the pap as soon as it is turned into the dish, before the butter is mixed with it
5. Beat the eggs and sugar together until very light, and add them to the other ingredients
6. Bake for 15 or 20 minutes in rather a quick oven
