## Ingredients

- yolk of 1 hard boiled egg
- yolks of 2 raw eggs
- salt
- pepper
- 1 pint of oil
- tarragon vinegar
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped gherkins
- 1 ditto of chopped capers
- 1 ditto of eschallots and parsley
- 2 of French mustard
- a very little cayenne pepper
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mash the yolk of a hard boiled egg
2. add the yolks of 2 raw ones, with salt and pepper
3. mix with a wooden spoon, dropping slowly a pint of oil, and moisten with tarragon vinegar to taste, but keep it thick
4. add a tablespoonful of chopped gherkins, 1 ditto of chopped capers, ditto of eschallots and parsley, 2 of French mustard, a very little cayenne pepper, and sugar, and more salt, if required
