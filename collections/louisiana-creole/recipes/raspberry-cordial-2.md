## Ingredients

- fruit
- loaf sugar
- brandy

## Directions

1. Squeeze the fruit through a flannel bag
2. To every quart of juice add a pound of loaf sugar
3. Put it in a stone jar and stir it constantly for half an hour
4. Allow it to stand for three days, then strain it again
5. Add to each quart of juice a quart of fine brandy
