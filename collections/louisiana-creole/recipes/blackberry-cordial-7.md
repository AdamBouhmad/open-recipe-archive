## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Blackberry Juice
- 2 Pounds of Sugar
- ½ Ounce of Ground Cloves
- ¼ Ounce of Allspice
- 1 Pint of the Best French Brandy

## Directions

1. Boil eight quarts of blackberries in a porcelain-lined pot till the juice is all extracted.
2. Take off the fire and press out all the juice through a flannel bag.
3. Add the sugar, allowing one glass of sugar always to two glasses of blackberries, if you wish to make larger quantities than the above measurements.
4. Add the cloves and the allspice, and set on the fire, and let it come to a good boil.
5. Let it boil up several times.
6. Take off, strain and add the brandy.
7. Bottle while hot.
8. Cork well and seal, and set away in a cool place, to be used after two months.
