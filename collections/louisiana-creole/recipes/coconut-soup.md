## Ingredients

- calves' feet
- mace
- lemon
- water

## Directions

1. Take eight calves' feet that have been scalded and scraped but not skinned
2. Put them into a soup kettle with 6 or 7 blades of mace and the yellow rind of a lemon grated
3. Pour on a gallon of water
4. Cover the kettle and let it boil very slowly, skimming it well, till the flesh is reduced to rags and has dropped entirely from the bones
5. Strain it into a broad white-ware pan and set it away to get cool
6. When it has congealed, scrape off the fat and sediment
7. Cut up the cake of jelly, or stock, and put it into a clean porcelain vessel
