## Ingredients

- six or eight large, ripe tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- rolled cracker or toasted bread
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over six or eight large, ripe tomatoes
2. Let them remain in it a few minutes to scald the skins
3. Take them out and skin them
4. Chop them up and put them to stew with a little salt, pepper, and a small piece of butter
5. Add a spoonful of rolled cracker or toasted bread, and a tablespoonful of sugar, if liked; if not, omit the sugar
6. Let them stew gently for half an hour longer
