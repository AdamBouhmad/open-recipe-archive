## Ingredients

- whites of 10 eggs
- 10 tablespoonfuls of sifted crushed sugar, very finely powdered
- glaizing sugar
- delicate preserve
- flavoring as desired

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites to a stiff froth, adding the sugar slowly
2. When well beaten and quite stiff, put it in the form of a large egg on paper
3. Ornament with the glaizing sugar
4. Lay the paper on a tin in a moderate oven
5. When of a light brown, take them out
6. Remove from beneath all that which is not cooked with a spoon
7. Place them in the oven when cold to dry
8. Put any kind of delicate preserve in each and flavor as fancied
