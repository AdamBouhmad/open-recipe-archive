## Ingredients

- The Yolks of 3 Eggs
- 2 Chopped Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 1 Bay Leaf, Minced Fine
- 1 Onion
- 6 Peppers
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Pint of Veal or Chicken Broth
- Juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Chop the onions and put in the saucepan with the butter and bay leaf.
2. Stir in the flour to thicken and moisten with the broth.
3. Mix well, and add the nutmeg, and salt and pepper to taste.
4. Beat the yolks of the eggs separately with the juice of half a lemon.
5. Pour gradually into the sauce, but do not let it boil after they are added.
6. Press through a sieve, and when ready to serve, sprinkle with two chopped hard-boiled eggs and a teaspoonful of minced parsley.
