## Ingredients

- 3 Heads of Chicory
- 4 Tablespoonfuls Butter
- 2 Glasses of Cream or Milk
- A Pinch of Nutmeg
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Pick nicely three heads of Chicorfie casting away all the outer green leaves.
2. Then wash the heads carefully in fresh cold water; drain and wash again, and blanch for ten minutes in boiling salted water; remove and throw into cold water to cool.
3. Then drain of all the water, and chop the Chicorfie very fine.
4. Put in a saucepan with four tablespoonfuls of butter and let cook for a quarter of an hour.
5. Pour two glassfuls of milk or rich cream over it; add a pinch of grated nutmeg, and salt and pepper to taste.
6. Mix all thoroughly together for five minutes on the stove; then remove, put in a dish, garnish nicely with Croutons fried in butter, and serve hot.
