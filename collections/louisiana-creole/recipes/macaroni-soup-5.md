## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Broth
- 1/4 Pound of Macaroni

## Directions

1. Prepare a good Pot-au-Feu, or Bouillon, according to directions given
2. Break the macaroni into two-inch length pieces
3. Add to the boiling broth about a half hour or so before serving
4. Alternatively cook the macaroni separately in salted boiling water about ten or fifteen minutes, drain thoroughly, and drop into the boiling broth about fifteen minutes before serving
5. Serve, optionally with Parmesan cheese, grated
