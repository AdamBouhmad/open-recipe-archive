## Ingredients

- 6 Triangular Slices of Toast
- 1 Box of Sardines
- 3 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 6 Anchovies
- A Dash of Red Pepper

## Directions

1. Pound the sardines and the hard-boiled eggs to a paste, season with a dash of Red Pepper and the juice of a lemon and spread the buttered toast with the mixture.
2. Decorate in the center with a coiled Anchovy.
3. Or, simply pound the sardines to a paste, season with a dash of red pepper and the juice of a lemon, and spread on the slices of buttered toast.
