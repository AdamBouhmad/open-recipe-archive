## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint rich milk
- handful bitter almonds
- 1 quart milk
- 8 eggs
- 1 pint powdered sugar
- macaroon

## Directions

1. Boil in 1/2 pint of rich milk a handful of bitter almonds, blanched and broken up
2. When highly flavored, strain that milk and set aside
3. Boil 1 quart of milk by itself, and when cold, stir in slowly 8 well beaten eggs, adding the flavored milk, and 1 pint of powdered sugar
4. Stir the whole very hard at the last
5. Bake it in cups, and when done and quite cold, put on the top of each a macaroon with 4 others placed around it
