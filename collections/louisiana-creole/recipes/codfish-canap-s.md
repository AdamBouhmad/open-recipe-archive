## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Boiled Salt Codfish
- 1 Green Pepper
- 3 Young Onions
- 1 Teaspoonful of Tarragon Vinegar
- Capers to Garnish

## Directions

1. Boil the codfish, or utilize left-over fish; mince finely and mix thoroughly with the minced green peppers and young onions.
2. Season with one teaspoonful of Tarragon vinegar.
3. Spread on triangular-shaped pieces of toast placed on a dish on a folded napkin.
4. Decorate nicely with capers and serve.
