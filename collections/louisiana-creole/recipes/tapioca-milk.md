## Ingredients

- a large tablespoonful of tapioca
- a quart of sweet milk
- a little salt
- sugar
- nutmeg or cinnamon

## Directions

1. Wash and soak a large tablespoonful of tapioca
2. put it to a quart of sweet milk
3. add a little salt
4. cover it, and set it over a gentle fire for an hour
5. Take it up, add sugar and nutmeg, or cinnamon to taste
