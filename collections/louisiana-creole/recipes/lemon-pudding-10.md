## Ingredients

- Three lemons
- juice of 1 lemon
- 3 cups of sugar
- 7 soda crackers
- 3 cups of cold water

## Directions

1. Grate three lemons and the juice of 1
2. Soak 7 soda crackers 1 hour in 3 cups of cold water
3. Mix with 3 cups of sugar
4. Bake in a nice paste
