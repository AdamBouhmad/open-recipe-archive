## Ingredients

- 1 Gill of Good Cognac or Rum
- 5 Ounces of Powdered Sugar
- The Juice of 3 Oranges
- The Zest of Half a Lemon
- The Grated Rind of Half an Orange
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla Essence

## Directions

1. Put the rum or brandy, the sugar, orange rind, lemon zest and vanilla essence into a saucepan and set on the stove
2. As soon as the ingredients light into a flame, cover tightly with a lid and let all infuse together for two minutes
3. Then take from the fire and add the juice of three Louisiana oranges
4. Serve very hot as a pudding sauce.
