## Ingredients

- 4 Tomatoes
- 2 Green Peppers
- 1 Large Onion
- French Dressing, Plain

## Directions

1. Slice the tomatoes, onions and green peppers nicely and thin.
2. Arrange on a dish, placing a layer of tomatoes, an alternate layer of onion and green pepper, and tomatoes mixed.
3. Dress either before bringing to the table, or at the table, with French dressing.
