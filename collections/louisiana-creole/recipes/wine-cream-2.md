## Ingredients

- 1 quart of cream
- 6 tablespoonfuls of white wine
- nutmeg
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Set 1 quart of cream on the fire
2. When it boils, stir in very slowly 6 tablespoonfuls of white wine, taking care that the cream does not curdle
3. Season with nutmeg and orange flower water
4. Sweeten to the taste
5. Let it stand until it is cold
