## Ingredients

- 12 Ounces of Sweet Almonds
- 1 Ounce of Bitter Almonds
- 1½ Pounds of Sifted Sugar
- 1 Ounce of Vanilla Sugar
- The Whites of 3 Eggs
- 1 White of Egg Royal Icing

## Directions

1. Prepare the paste exactly as above, and when you take it up in the bowl add the Royal Icing, keeping the paste firm.
2. Spread the massepains upon wafer sheets, using only the ring shape, and bake in a slack oven till a very light color.
