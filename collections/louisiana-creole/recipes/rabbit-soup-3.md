## Ingredients

- 2 Young Rabbits
- 2 Quarts of Cold Water
- 1 Onion
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Blade of Mace
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1/2 Cup of Rice
- Cayenne Pepper, 1/2 Pod
- Salt to the Taste

## Directions

1. The rabbits should be well skinned and singed.
2. Wash thoroughly in warm water; this is very important.
3. Then cut the meat into small pieces and put into the soup pot, with the quantity of water given.
4. Chop the onion, mace and bay leaf and add.
5. Place on a very moderate fire, and let it simmer gently until the meat has grown very tender. This will require about two hours or less.
6. Add the salt, pepper and rice, and simmer for an hour longer.
7. Pour into the tureen over croutons and serve.
8. The Creoles add two tablespoonfuls of sherry or port wine, thus increasing the delicacy of the flavor.
