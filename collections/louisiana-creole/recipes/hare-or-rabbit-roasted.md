## Ingredients

- grated bread crumbs
- suet or butter
- a chopped onion
- the liver of the rabbit chopped
- a lemon peel grated
- eggs
- a little claret
- butter
- the drippings in the pan
- a little cream or milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbits and wash them through several waters
2. Stuff with grated bread crumbs, suet or butter, a chopped onion, the liver of the rabbit chopped, and a lemon peel grated
3. Moisten with eggs and a little claret
4. Put this in the rabbit and sew it up
5. Baste with butter, and cook for two hours
6. Make the gravy with the drippings in the pan, a little cream or milk, and flour
