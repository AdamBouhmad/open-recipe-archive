## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Sugar
- 1 Pound of Almonds, blanched and Pounded to a Paste
- The Whites of 3 Eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs
2. Stir in the almonds and sugar
3. Proceed to ice according to general directions
