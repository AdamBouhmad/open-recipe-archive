## Ingredients

- 2¼ lbs. apples
- 1½ lbs. coarse white sugar
- whites of 3 eggs
- water
- orange peel
- citron

## Directions

1. Put more than a quart of water to the beaten sugar; clarify the sugar with the whites of 3 eggs
2. As the apples are peeled, they must be dropped in water; when the syrup is sufficiently clear, put them in; when half done, add orange peel and cut citron
