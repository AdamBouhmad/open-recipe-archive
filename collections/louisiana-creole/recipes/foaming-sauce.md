## Ingredients

- The Whites of 3 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Gill of Boiling Water
- 1 Glass of Sherry Wine or a Tablespoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
2. Melt the sugar in the boiling water, and then add a wine-glass of Sherry
3. Stir in the frothy whites, and set to cool
