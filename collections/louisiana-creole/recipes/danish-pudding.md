## Ingredients

- 1 quart of rich milk
- 4 eggs
- 1½ spoonfuls of flour
- sugar
- ½ lb. of butter
- 1 cup of sugar
- 1 wineglass of wine
- yolk of 1 egg
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil 1 quart of rich milk and let stand till almost cold
2. Beat 4 eggs for ½ hour with 1½ spoonfuls of flour
3. Mix the milk and sugar with it
4. Tie it up in a close cloth, well floured
5. Boil it 1 hour
6. Make a sauce of ½ lb. of butter, 1 cup of sugar, with 1 wineglass of wine, the yolk of 1 egg, and a little nutmeg, and serve with the pudding
