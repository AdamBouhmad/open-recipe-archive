## Ingredients

- Fine Cucumbers
- Salt
- A French Salad Dressing

## Directions

1. Wash the cucumbers, cut off the bitter end, and pare the skin to a sufficient depth to remove the green portion.
2. Score them lengthwise with a table fork.
3. Put them into a salad earthen bowl and sprinkle with salt.
4. Set in an ice box for three hours.
5. Cut into delicate slices and serve with a French Salad Dressing, the dressing being served in a separate dish.
