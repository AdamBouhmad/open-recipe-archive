## Ingredients

- 6 Artichokes
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- a pint of water
- Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Wash and skin the artichokes, and boil until tender in a pint of water.
2. Press them through a colander, and return to the fire in a saucepan in which you have placed a tablespoonful of butter, salt and pepper.
3. Stir well, and let them simmer for five minutes longer, and serve with a Cream Sauce.
