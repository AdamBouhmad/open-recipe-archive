## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Indian Meal
- 1/2 Cup Butter
- 1 Cup Sugar
- 2 Cups Milk
- 3/4 Cup of Molasses
- 1 Cup of Seeded Raisins
- salt
- 1 teaspoonful of extract of cinnamon
- 1/4 of a grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. Pour sufficient boiling water on the meal to wet it thoroughly, and then beat in the butter
2. Add the sugar, beating thoroughly, and the salt
3. Then add the milk, stirring well, also the molasses and the raisins
4. Add one teaspoonful of extract of cinnamon, and one-quarter of a grated nutmeg
5. Mix thoroughly
6. Bake for two and a half or three hours, and serve cold with cream
