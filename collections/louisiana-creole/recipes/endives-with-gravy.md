## Ingredients

- 4 Heads of Chicory
- 1 Onion
- 1 Carrot
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- 1/2 Pound of Bacon
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1 Pint of Veal or Chicken Consomme

## Directions

1. Clean and pick the Chicorfie well, pare off all the outer leaves, and wash the heads well in several waters.
2. Then drain and put to blanch in salted water for ten minutes; at the end of this time, throw them in the cold water to cool; drain and cut into quarters.
3. Put the piece of bacon in the bottom of a sautoire or stewpan, and add an onion and carrot and herb bouquet, minced very fine.
4. Lay the Chicorfie on top of this, season with a teaspoonful of salt and a half teaspoonful of black pepper, and cover with a buttered paper.
5. Then set the sautoire in the oven, and let the Chicorfie cook for ten minutes, when it will be a golden brown.
6. Moisten with half a pint of veal or chicken consomme, cover and again set in the oven for thirty minutes.
7. Arrange the Chicorfie on a hot dish; strain the sauce, pour over and serve.
