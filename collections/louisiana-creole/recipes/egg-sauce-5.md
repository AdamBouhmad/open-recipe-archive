## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of chicken water
- 1 dessertspoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- a little salt
- 4 or 6 hard boiled eggs, chopped fine

## Directions

1. Just before serving the chickens, take 1/2 pint of the chicken water
2. stew into it a dessertspoonful of butter, a tablespoonful of flour, and a little salt
3. set it on the fire and let it thicken, stirring all the time
4. add 4 or 6 hard boiled eggs, chopped fine
