## Ingredients

- macaroni
- meat
- sweet herbs
- butter
- pepper
- eschalot
- eggs
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Boil macaroni till it is tender
2. Line a mould with it, fitting it in closely
3. Make a mince of any kind of meat, raw or cooked
4. Season with sweet herbs, butter, pepper chopped eschalot, and a couple of eggs
5. Fill the mould with this and boil for twenty minutes
6. Serve with white sauce No. 10 put around the macaroni
