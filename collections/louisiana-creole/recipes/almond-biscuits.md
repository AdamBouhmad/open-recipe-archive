## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of almonds, blanched and pounded very fine
- rose or orange flower water
- yolks of 8 eggs
- whites of 4 eggs
- 3/4 lb. of loaf sugar
- 3 ounces of flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound almonds very fine with as much rose or orange flower water as will prevent their oiling
2. Beat yolks of 8 and whites of 4 eggs until very light
3. Add to the almonds
4. Beat in 3/4 lb. of loaf sugar
5. Continue to beat while the oven is heating
6. When ready, add 3 ounces of flour, which must be stirred in and not beaten
7. Butter the pan and fill 3/4 full
8. Dust a little sugar over as you put them in the oven
9. Bake
