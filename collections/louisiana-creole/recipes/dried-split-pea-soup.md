## Ingredients

- a good beef marrow-bone of one or two pounds weight
- the remains of roast beef-bones and gravy
- a slice of ham
- a gallon of cold water
- two cups of split peas or small white beans
- two carrots
- two turnips
- two large onions or three small ones
- a stalk of celery cut in pieces
- a bunch of thyme
- a teaspoonful of mixed black and red pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Put these in a pot with a gallon of cold water
2. Throw in the pot two cups of split peas or small white beans, two carrots, two turnips, two large onions or three small ones, a stalk of celery cut in pieces, a bunch of thyme, and a teaspoonful of mixed black and red pepper
3. When the vegetables are quite soft, which will be in about two hours, take the soup from the fire
4. Strain it through a sieve or coarse cloth
5. Add salt, and put on the fire again and boil for a few moments
6. Then pour it over toasted bread
