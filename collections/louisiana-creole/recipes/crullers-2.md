## Ingredients

- two cups of sugar
- one cup of butter
- three eggs
- one cup of sour milk
- one teaspoonful of soda
- flavoring to taste
- boiling lard for frying

## Directions

1. Combine sugar, butter, eggs, sour milk, and soda
2. Add flavoring to taste
3. Fry in boiling lard or bake lightly in the stove
