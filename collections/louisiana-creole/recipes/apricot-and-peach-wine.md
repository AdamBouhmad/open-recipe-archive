## Ingredients

- Apricots or peaches
- Water
- Loaf sugar
- Peach kernels (optional)

## Directions

1. Mash the apricots or peaches in a mortar, remove the stones
2. To eight pounds of the pulp, add one quart of water
3. Let this stand twenty-four hours
4. Strain, and to each gallon of the juice, add two pounds of loaf sugar
5. Let it ferment, and when perfectly clear, bottle it
6. A few of the kernels may be added for flavoring, if wished
