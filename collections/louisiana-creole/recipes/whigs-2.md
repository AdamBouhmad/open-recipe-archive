## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 2 lbs. of flour
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 4 tablespoonfuls of white sugar
- 6 eggs
- 1 teacup of yeast
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- mace or nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients in the morning
2. Season with mace or nutmeg
3. Set it to rise
4. If it does not rise sufficiently, add a small teaspoonful of yeast powder dissolved in a little milk just before it is baked
5. Bake it in a pan for tea
6. Eat it hot with fresh butter and pounded sugar sifted over it
