## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- One pound of bread crumbs
- Three quarters of a pound of stoned raisins
- Three quarters of a pound of currants
- Three quarters of a pound of suet
- Four eggs
- Milk to moisten, say about one pint

## Directions

1. Let the suet be finely chopped
2. Stone the raisins
3. Wash, pick and dry the currants well
4. Mix them with the other dry ingredients
5. Stir all well together
6. Beat and strain in the eggs
7. Add just enough of the milk to make it mix properly
8. Tie it up in a well floured cloth
9. Put it into boiling water
10. Boil for five hours
11. Serve with butter and sugar sauce, or wine sauce
