## Ingredients

- clams
- 1 ounce of powdered mace and nutmeg
- butter
- grated bread crumbs
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop the clams fine, and mix with them 1 ounce of powdered mace and nutmeg
2. Butter the sides and bottom of a large, deep dish
3. Cover the bottom with a layer of grated bread crumbs
4. Scatter some very small bits of the best fresh butter over this
5. Add a thick layer of the chopped clams
6. Add another layer of grated bread crumbs and small bits of butter
7. Add another layer of clams, and so on till the dish is full
8. Finish at the top with a layer of crumbs, butter and salt
