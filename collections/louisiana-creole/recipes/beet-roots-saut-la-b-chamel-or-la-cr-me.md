## Ingredients

- 6 Beet Roots
- 1 Ounce of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- ½ Pint of Béchamel or Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the beets exactly as in the preceding recipe
2. About five minutes before serving add half a pint of hot Béchamel or Cream Sauce
3. Serve as a vegetable
