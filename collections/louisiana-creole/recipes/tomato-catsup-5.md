## Ingredients

- 1 peck of tomatoes
- salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of scraped horse radish
- 1 large onion
- cloves
- ground allspice
- red pepper
- 1 pint of strong vinegar
- 1 teacup of made mustard

## Directions

1. Slice and wash the tomatoes
2. Boil or stew them with a little salt, until perfectly soft
3. Strain them through a hair sieve, and press as much of the pulp through as possible
4. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of scraped horse radish, 1 large onion stuck full of cloves, ground allspice, red pepper and salt, to the taste
5. Put all on together, boiling, until the catsup looks rich and thick
6. When done, add 1 pint of strong vinegar, and 1 teacup of made mustard
7. Let the catsup be cold, before you bottle it
8. Let it be free of the onion and horse radish
9. Rosin the bottles tightly, and put away for use
