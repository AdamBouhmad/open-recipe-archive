## Ingredients

- two quarts of green peas
- a double-handful of parsley
- four stalks of green mint
- a good handful of green onions
- two quarts of veal or beef stock
- a half pound of butter
- a spoonful of sugar

## Directions

1. Place it on the fire, throw in the above peas, mint and onions
2. Let them all boil; when they are thoroughly done take them out, drain them and pound them well together
3. Put them in the stewpan again with the liquor
4. Warm it and run it through a sieve
5. Add at the last moment a half pound of butter and a spoonful of sugar
6. Serve with fried bread
