## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- one pound of butter
- one quart of molasses
- four tablespoonfuls of allspice
- four tablespoonfuls of cinnamon
- eight tablespoonfuls of ground ginger

## Directions

1. Roll thin
2. cut out in the shape of the small ginger-nuts sold at the confectioners'
3. bake in a rather quick oven
