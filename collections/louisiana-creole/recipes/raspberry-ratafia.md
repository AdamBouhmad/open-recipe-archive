## Ingredients

- 8 Pounds of Raspberries
- 2 Gallons of Brandy
- 7 Pounds of Sugar
- 3 Quarts of Water

## Directions

1. Steep the berries, which you will have mashed well, for one month, or at least fifteen days, in the brandy.
2. Add a syrup made of the sugar and water.
3. Filter and proceed to bottle as directed.
