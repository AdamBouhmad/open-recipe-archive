## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 4 of the small measures of yeast powder
- 1 lb. of white sugar
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- whites of 20 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful essence of lemon
- 1 1/2 teaspoonfuls vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix flour, yeast powder, sugar, butter, egg whites, lemon essence, and vanilla
2. Cover the pan
3. Keep the stove hot enough to make the cake rise nicely
4. Add a little more fuel if necessary
5. Bake about 2 hours
