## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 pints of flour
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of beaten cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Roll them in flour and mould them thin
3. Bake in a moderate oven
