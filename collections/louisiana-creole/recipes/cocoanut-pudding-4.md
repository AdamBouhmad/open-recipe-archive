## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of sugar
- yolks of 10 eggs
- ½ pint of cream
- ½ gill of brandy
- juice of 1 lemon
- peel of 1 lemon grated
- ½ lb. of cocoanut

## Directions

1. Combine 1 lb. of sugar, the yolks of 10 eggs, ½ pint of cream, ½ gill of brandy, the juice of 1 lemon, with the peel grated, ½ lb. of the cocoanut
2. Bake it in a nice paste
