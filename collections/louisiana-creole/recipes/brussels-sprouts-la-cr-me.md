## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Brussels Sprouts
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chopped Parsley
- ½ Cupful of Cream or Milk
- A Pinch of Nutmeg
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Prepare and boil the Brussels Sprouts as in the preceding recipe
2. Drain thoroughly and put in a saucepan with two tablespoonfuls of butter
3. Season with salt and pepper to taste and a pinch of nutmeg
4. Add a half cup of cream or milk
5. Toss lightly for five or ten minutes but do not let them boil
6. Place on a hot dish, garnish nicely and serve hot
