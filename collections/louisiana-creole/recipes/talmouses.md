## Ingredients

- 1/4 Pound of Flour
- 1 Glass of Water
- A Pinch of Salt
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 6 Eggs
- 2 Cream Cheeses
- A Brioche or Plain Paste

## Directions

1. Put the butter in a saucepan, and add the water and the salt.
2. Let all come to a boil, stirring constantly.
3. Then take from the fire and add the eggs, beaten well, and the cream cheese.
4. Mix all well.
5. Then take small molds or tins and line with the Plain Paste, or Brioche Paste, rolled very thin.
6. Place within the preparation of cream cheese in quantity equal to an egg in size.
7. Then cover the cakes with a piece of rolled dough, and bake in a slow oven.
