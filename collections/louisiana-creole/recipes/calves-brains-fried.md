## Ingredients

- Calves Brains
- 1 Onion
- 2 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Grated Bread Crumbs
- Lard for frying
- Fried parsley for bed
- Boiled green peas for garnish

## Directions

1. Plunge the brains into cold water to disgorge them of all blood and remove the fine skin and blood that surrounds them
2. Blanch with scalding water
3. In five minutes take them out of the hot water and put them into a saucepan and cover with cold water
4. Add a tiny onion sliced fine, parsley and bay leaf whole
5. Let them simmer gently for five minutes
6. Take from the fire and drain
7. When cold, cut into pieces of a square inch and dip in a batter or tomato sauce, then in grated bread crumbs, patting gently
8. Drop into boiling lard and fry to a golden brown
9. Take out and drain of grease and serve on a bed of fried parsley
10. Garnish with boiled green peas
