## Ingredients

- a filet of mutton
- salt and pepper to taste
- garnish of green peas

## Directions

1. The filet of mutton is a square cut from the loin
2. Proceed to dredge with salt and pepper, and roast in exactly the same manner as leg of mutton
3. Serve with a garnish of green peas piled around
