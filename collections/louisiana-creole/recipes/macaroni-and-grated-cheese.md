## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of macaroni
- grated cheese
- salt
- butter
- new milk

## Directions

1. break macaroni into lengths, and throw it into cold water to soak, an hour or so after breakfast
2. Boil it an hour
3. take it out of the pot and put in the bottom of the pan a layer of the boiled macaroni and then a layer of grated cheese
4. strew over the top a teaspoonful of salt and some lumps of butter as big as a nutmeg
5. fill up the pan with new milk and bake until browned on top, but never let it get dry; it is better to put water in, if your milk has given out, than to let it get the least dry
