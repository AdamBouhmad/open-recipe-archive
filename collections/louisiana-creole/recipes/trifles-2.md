## Ingredients

- two or three fresh eggs
- a saltspoonful of salt
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. Beat two or three fresh eggs a few minutes
2. add a saltspoonful of salt, and enough of sifted flour to make into a stiff paste
3. roll very thin
4. cut into small round cakes
5. fry in boiling lard, and sprinkle sugar over them
