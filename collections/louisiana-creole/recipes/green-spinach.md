## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Young Spinach
- A Few Grains of Powdered Alum
- sugar
- alcohol

## Directions

1. Pound one pound of young Spinach to a pulp
2. carefully squeeze all the juice out of it
3. Set to boil, and the moment it begins to curdle remove from the fire
4. strain all the juice from it
5. add to the curd the same weight in sugar, a few grains of powdered alum, and a little alcohol, and bottle for use
6. Keep in a cool place
