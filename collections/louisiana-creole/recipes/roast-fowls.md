## Ingredients

- fowls
- butter
- pepper
- bread crumbs
- flour
- salt
- sausage meat, truffles, or chestnuts (optional)

## Directions

1. Truss the fowls nicely
2. Make a stuffing of butter and some pepper
3. Dry up the butter with a few bread crumbs
4. Baste it well
5. Add flour and salt before you take it from the fire
6. Optionally stuff the fowl with good sausage meat, truffles, or chestnuts
