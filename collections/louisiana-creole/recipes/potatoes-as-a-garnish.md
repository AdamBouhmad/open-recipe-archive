## Ingredients

- 6 Potatoes
- Beef, Veal or Mutton Drippings

## Directions

1. Select nice, round potatoes
2. Wash them clean, boil, or better, steam them
3. Peel and lay them in the pan with the beef or mutton or veal
4. Let them brown awhile before the meat is done, basting them when you baste the meat with the drippings from the roast
5. Place as a garnish around the roast, and bring to the table hot
