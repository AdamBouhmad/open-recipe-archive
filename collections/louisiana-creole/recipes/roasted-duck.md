## Ingredients

- duck or ducks
- salt
- pepper
- sage and onion stuffing
- mashed potatoes
- bread
- butter
- onions
- egg

## Directions

1. Clean, draw and truss the duck, or ducks, wash them nicely, salt and pepper them
2. Get ready a sage and onion stuffing or stuff with mashed potatoes, or bread, butter, onions, pepper and salt mixed, and bound together with an egg
