## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pound of Sifted Flour
- 3/4 Pound of Sugar
- 2 Quarts of Strawberries
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 6 Eggs
- 1 Quart of Cream
- 3/4 Pound of Powdered Sugar
- Salt
- Grated zest of half a lemon
- Butter

## Directions

1. Stem the strawberries.
2. Sweeten to taste, and slightly mash.
3. Then set them aside.
4. Blend the sugar and butter together until very smooth, and add the baking powder, and blend well.
5. Add the salt.
6. Then add the eggs, breaking them in whole, one at a time, and beat all well for ten minutes.
7. Add the grated zest of half a lemon; by degrees add the flour, and mix all together very gently.
8. Divide into two parts; put one into well-buttered baking tins and bake in a moderate oven thirty minutes.
9. When done, remove the cakes and allow them to cool.
10. Detach the cakes from the tins by passing a knife gently around.
11. Have a dessert dish ready and lay one of the cakes upon it.
12. Spread two tablespoonfuls of whipped cream over, and then cover liberally with strawberries.
13. Cover with another cake and spread as before with cream and then with strawberries.
14. Lay the other cake on top and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
15. Serve with Whipped Cream a la Vanille or with rich cream or milk.
