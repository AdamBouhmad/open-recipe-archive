## Ingredients

- 1 pint of new milk
- 3 large spoonfuls of rice flour
- 1 cup of milk
- salt
- 1 large spoonful of butter
- rind of 1 lemon
- nutmeg
- 1 wineglass of brandy
- 3 large spoonfuls of good brown sugar
- 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Put on a pint of new milk to scald
2. While this is heating, stir 3 large spoonfuls of rice flour into a cup of milk
3. When quite smooth, stir it into the boiling milk
4. Add a little salt and a large spoonful of butter, the rind of 1 lemon, a little nutmeg, and 1 wineglass of brandy
5. Beat all well together
6. Add 3 large spoonfuls of good brown sugar
7. Beat 5 eggs very light and stir in
8. Butter a dish and pour it in
9. Bake it as long as custard
