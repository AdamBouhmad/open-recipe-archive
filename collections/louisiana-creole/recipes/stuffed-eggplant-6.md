## Ingredients

- 6 Eggplants of Good Size
- 1½ Cups of Bread, Wet and Squeezed
- 1 Tomato
- 1 Onion
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- Thyme, Parsley, Bay Leaf
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter

## Directions

1. Cut the eggplant in the middle, and put to boil in cold water for about half an hour, or until tender.
2. Take out of the water and set to cool.
3. When quite cool, take out the seeds and throw away.
4. Scoop out carefully the soft meat of the eggplant, and leave the skins unbroken.
5. Set these skins carefully aside.
6. Chop the soft eggplant fine, and then wet and squeeze one and a half cups of bread.
7. Chop the onion and tomato fine, and mince the herbs and garlic very fine.
8. Season the eggplant well with salt and pepper.
9. Put the butter in the frying pan and brown the onion in it slightly.
10. Add the chopped tomato and its juice, and let this fry for four or five minutes.
11. Add the minced herbs and the clove of garlic, and almost immediately the chopped eggplant.
12. Add the bread at once, and mix all well.
13. Season again to taste, and let all fry for about five minutes.
14. Fill the shells with the stuffing, sprinkle the top lightly with bread crumbs, dot with butter, and set in the oven to bake to a nice brown.
