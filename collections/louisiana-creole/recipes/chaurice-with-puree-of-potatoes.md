## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of chaurice
- 4 Irish potatoes
- 1 egg, well beaten
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prick the sausages and lay them in the bottom of a pan.
2. Make a soft puree of potatoes and pour this over the sausage.
3. Spread a beaten egg very evenly on top, sprinkle with bread crumbs, and place in the oven and let it bake a half hour.
