## Ingredients

- 4 Bananas
- Boiling Lard
- White Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the bananas, and cut them in two, lengthwise.
2. Slice lengthwise about a quarter of an inch in thickness.
3. Have ready a pan of boiling lard.
4. Lay the bananas in it, and fry brown, first on one side and then on the other, sifting with a little powdered sugar.
5. When done, skim out of the pan, place neatly in a dish, sprinkle with powdered sugar again, and serve hot, as an entrée.
