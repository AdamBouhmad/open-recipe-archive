## Ingredients

- a fowl
- a cup of raw rice
- a slice of ham minced
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut up and stew a fowl
2. When half done, add a cup of raw rice, a slice of ham minced, and pepper and salt
3. Let all cook together until the rice swells and absorbs all the gravy of the stewed chicken, but it must not be allowed to get hard or dry
4. Serve in a deep dish
