## Ingredients

- eggs
- cream
- grated cheese
- pepper
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Break six eggs into a bowl
2. add a gill of cream, four ounces of grated cheese, some pepper and a little salt
3. beat the whole together
4. pour into a pan
5. roll up and bake as directed
6. Butter it well before sending to table
