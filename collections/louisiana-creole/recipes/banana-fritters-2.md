## Ingredients

- 3 Bananas
- Fritter Batter a la Creole

## Directions

1. Make a Fritter Batter a la Creole.
2. Peel the bananas and then cut them in halves.
3. Slice them nicely, according to length.
4. Dip in the fritter batter, and proceed to cook and serve as in the recipe for Apple Fritters.
