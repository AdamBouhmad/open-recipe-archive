## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Flour
- 3/4 Pound of Butter
- 1/2 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Louisiana Molasses
- 1/4 Pound of Raisins
- 3/4 Pound of Currants, Washed and Dried
- 3/4 Cup of Sour Cream
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Soda or Baking Powder (Level)
- 1 Teaspoonful of Ground Ginger
- 3/4 Teaspoonful Each of Ground Cinnamon and Cloves

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream, and warm the molasses and beat with the sugar and butter
2. Add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs, and the milk and spices and ginger, mixing thoroughly
3. Add the soda, dissolved in a tablespoonful of boiling water and mix well
4. Mix the seeded raisins and currants well and then add the flour by degrees, beating vigorously
5. Finally add the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth, and beat thoroughly
6. Put in two shallow pans in the oven for about two hours
7. Use the broom wisp test before taking the cake out of the oven
