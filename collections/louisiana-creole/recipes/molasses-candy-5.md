## Ingredients

- two quarts of molasses
- one pound of brown sugar
- juice of two lemons
- extract
- a pint of parched pinders, or pecan meats, or almonds cut up
- butter

## Directions

1. Let the molasses and sugar boil moderately, without stirring it, for two hours
2. If not thick enough to pull then, let it boil a little longer
3. Put in your extract
4. When the candy is cool enough to handle, put into the pot parched pinders, pecan meats, or almonds cut up
5. Butter two large dishes and pour out the candy
