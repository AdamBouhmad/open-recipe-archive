## Ingredients

- One-half tablespoonful of powdered sugar
- one wine-glass of water
- one of whiskey, brandy or gin
- one-half dozen sprigs of mint
- plenty of fine ice
- strawberries and pineapples or any fruit in season

## Directions

1. Use plenty of fine ice
2. decorate with strawberries and pineapples, or any fruit in season
