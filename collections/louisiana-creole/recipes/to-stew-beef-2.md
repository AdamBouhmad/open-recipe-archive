## Ingredients

- beef
- salt
- water
- potatoes
- parsnips

## Directions

1. Wash the beef, rub salt over it, and put it in a pot, and just cover it with water
2. Set it over a slow fire, and after it has stewed an hour, put in some potatoes, pared and cut in half, and some parsnips, scraped and split
3. Let them stew with the beef till quite tender
4. Turn the meat several times in the pot
5. When all is done, serve up the meat and vegetables together, and the gravy in a boat, having first skimmed it
