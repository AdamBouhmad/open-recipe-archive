## Ingredients

- 4 large lemons
- 1 pint of wine
- 1 lb. loaf sugar
- 1 quart of cream
- preserved fruit

## Directions

1. Pare off the rinds of 4 large lemons and put it in the bottom of a deep dish
2. Press the juice into a bowl containing 1 pint of wine and 1 lb. loaf sugar
3. By degrees, mix in 1 quart of cream
4. Pour the whole into the dish with the lemon peel
5. Let the mixture remain untouched 3 hours
6. Beat it with rods to a stiff froth, first taking out the peel
7. Put in each of the glasses a tablespoonful of preserved fruit
8. Heap upon it the syllabub so as to stand high on the top of the glass
