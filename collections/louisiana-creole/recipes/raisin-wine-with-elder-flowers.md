## Ingredients

- Six pounds of raisins
- Six gallons of water
- Twelve pounds of white sugar
- Half-pint of yeast
- Half a pound of elder flowers

## Directions

1. Boil six pounds of raisins in six gallons of water
2. When soft, rub them to a pulp, and pass through a colander to get rid of the stones
3. Add this pulp to the water it was boiled in
4. Put to it twelve pounds of white sugar and a half-pint of yeast
5. When clear, suspend half a pound of elder flowers in it to flavor the wine
6. Withdraw the flowers and bottle off the wine
