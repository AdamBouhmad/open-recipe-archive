## Ingredients

- 6 Potatoes
- Boiling Lard
- Chopped Parsley to Garnish

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and then cut into three-inch dice or sections like an orange.
2. Have ready a frying pan of very hot, boiling lard, and let them fry to a golden yellow or brown.
3. Do not burn.
4. Take them out with a skimmer, and drain in a heated colander.
5. Stand the colander in the mouth of the oven while you fry the remainder of the potato.
6. When done, sprinkle with salt, garnish with chopped parsley, and serve hot.
