## Ingredients

- rice
- cold water

## Directions

1. Pick the rice clean
2. Wash it well in cold water before attempting to cook, rubbing the rice well with the hands, to get all the dust off
3. Pour off the first water, and add fresh
4. Pour off this, and add fresh again
5. The rice will then be ready to cook
