## Ingredients

- two soup plates of okra
- 5 quarts of water
- salt
- meat
- 1 soup plate and a half of tomatoes
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut up in fine slices two soup plates of okra
2. Put into a soup pot with 5 quarts of water and a little salt at 10 o'clock
3. At 11 o'clock put your meat into the soup pot
4. At 12 o'clock peel a soup plate and a half of tomatoes, and after straining them through a cullender throw them into the soup pot
5. Season with pepper and salt
6. Allow all the ingredients to boil till 3 o'clock, when it is fit to be served up
