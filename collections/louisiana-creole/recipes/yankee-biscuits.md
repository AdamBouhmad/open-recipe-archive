## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- 1 quart of flour
- lard or butter
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- water or milk

## Directions

1. Mix 2 teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar into 1 quart of flour
2. Dissolve 1 teaspoonful of soda in water or milk
3. Mix all together, roll it out, cut it into cakes, and bake it as soon as you please
