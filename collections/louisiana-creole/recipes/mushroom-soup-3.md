## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound good macaroni
- 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms or a half can of mushrooms
- 1 carrot and onion
- 2 tablespoonfuls butter
- 2 pints consomé or bouillon
- 1 pint cream
- 2 tablespoonfuls flour
- salt and pepper to taste
- three teaspoonfuls salt
- two quarts boiling water

## Directions

1. Break the macaroni into pieces of about three inches
2. Wash and put into a stewpan with two quarts of boiling water and three teaspoonfuls of salt
3. Let the macaroni boil half an hour
4. Put the butter and flour in a small stewpan and beat to a cream
5. Add the chopped onion, carrot and pepper with remaining salt and broth
6. Heat slowly
7. When the sauce begins to boil, set it back where it will only simmer for about twenty minutes
8. Add the cream and strain the sauce
9. Pour the water from the macaroni and in its place put the sauce and mushrooms
10. Cook for five minutes and serve hot
