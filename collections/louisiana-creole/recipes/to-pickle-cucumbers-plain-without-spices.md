## Ingredients

- one hundred small cucumbers
- salt
- boiling vinegar
- vine leaves

## Directions

1. Take one hundred small cucumbers, or more if you wish
2. Salt them freely and let them remain eight or ten hours
3. Drain them
4. Put them into boiling vinegar enough to cover them
5. Place vine leaves among and over them to green them
6. Let them scald a few minutes in the vinegar
7. Take them from the fire but place them near it to keep warm and become green
8. If the leaves turn yellow, put fresh ones among them
9. When green pack them away in jars
10. Season them at any time you may desire
11. Scald them thoroughly with boiling vinegar to keep well
