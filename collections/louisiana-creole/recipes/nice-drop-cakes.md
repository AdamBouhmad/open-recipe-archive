## Ingredients

- one-half pound sugar
- one-quarter pound of butter
- four well beaten eggs
- one-half pound of currants
- a spoonful of brandy
- grated nutmeg or lemon peel
- flour sufficient for a stiff batter

## Directions

1. Cream together sugar and butter
2. add four well beaten eggs
3. add one-half pound of currants, a spoonful of brandy, grated nutmeg or lemon peel
4. add flour sufficient for a stiff batter
5. Beat well
6. Drop by spoonfuls on buttered tins
7. bake in a quick oven
