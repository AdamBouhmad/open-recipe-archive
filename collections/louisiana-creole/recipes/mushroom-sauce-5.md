## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- water
- butter
- flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the mushrooms, cut them in small pieces, and put them in a saucepan;
2. cover them with water, and let them boil soft;
3. then stir in butter, mixed in flour, until it is thick enough to form a nice sauce;
4. add pepper and salt.
