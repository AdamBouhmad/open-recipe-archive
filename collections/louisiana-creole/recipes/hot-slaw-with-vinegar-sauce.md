## Ingredients

- cabbage
- two cups of strong vinegar
- one teaspoonful of mustard
- salt
- one tablespoonful of butter
- three teaspoons of white sugar
- yolks of two or three eggs

## Directions

1. Slice the cabbage with a patent slicer
2. Lay it in very cold water for an hour
3. Take it out, drain it thoroughly
4. Place it in the dish it is to be served in
5. Take two cups of strong vinegar to a quart of cut cabbage, stir in one teaspoonful of mustard and salt, one tablespoonful of butter, and three teaspoons of white sugar
6. Make this all hot
7. At the last moment stir in the yolks of two or three eggs; stir rapidly
8. Pour the sauce on to the chopped cabbage in the dish
9. Serve instantly or the sauce will harden
