## Ingredients

- 7 ounces of good grated cheese
- 2 ounces of flour
- Yolks of 3 eggs
- A little salt and pepper
- J pint of milk
- A little more milk
- A large tablespoonful of butter
- Whites of 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Add to 7 ounces of good grated cheese, 2 ounces of flour, the yolks of 3 eggs, well beaten, and a little salt and pepper
2. Mix all together in a pan
3. Add J pint of milk
4. Place this mixture on the fire, taking care not to let it burn
5. When it has been cooking for a short time, add a little more milk and a large tablespoonful of butter
6. Take it from the fire and allow it to cool
7. Beat up the whites of 6 eggs, very stiff, and add it gradually to the rest
8. When the whole is well mixed together, put it in a dish or deep plate, and place it in a cooking stove moderately heated
9. As soon as the mixture has risen well, and is of a good color, serve it
