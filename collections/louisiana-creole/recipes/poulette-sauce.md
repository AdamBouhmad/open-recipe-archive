## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- The yolks of 2 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- 1 pint of consommé or water
- Juice of an onion
- Salt and pepper to taste
- The juice of half a lemon

## Directions

1. Melt the butter and flour, blending well without browning
2. Add a half pint of water, or consommé, the juice of one lemon, and let it simmer twenty minutes
3. Season to taste
4. Take from the fire, add the yolks of two well-beaten eggs and the juice of a lemon and serve immediately
5. If necessary to keep warm, set in a bowl of boiling water (a bain-marie) till ready to serve
