## Ingredients

- ½ Peck of Spinach
- 2 Hard-Boiled or Poached Eggs
- 1 Cup of Gravy or Consomme

## Directions

1. Boil the spinach according to the Plain Boiled Spinach recipe.
2. When ready to take from the saucepan, after adding the butter and seasonings, put it in a saucepan and pour over a cup of roast beef gravy or consomme.
3. Let it cook for twenty minutes, and serve hot.
