## Ingredients

- the weight of 3 eggs in sugar
- the weight of 2 eggs in flour
- 5 eggs well beaten
- a little orange flower water

## Directions

1. When the 5 eggs are well beaten, gradually add the sugar
2. Add the flour and orange flower water
3. Beat all well together
4. Bake it in a floured tin
