## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Butterbeans
- 2 Pints of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful (heaping) of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Soak the butterbeans for about a half hour in water.
2. Pour off this cold fresh water, and then put them in a porcelain lined saucepan, or one of agate, and cover with two pints of water.
3. Let them boil well for about an hour, or less, if they are very tender.
4. As soon as they crush easily under pressure, take off the fire, drain off water, season well with salt and pepper.
5. Butter well with a heaping tablespoonful of butter, add a teaspoonful of parsley, minced very fine, and serve hot.
