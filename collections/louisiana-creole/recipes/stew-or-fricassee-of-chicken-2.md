## Ingredients

- chicken
- water
- salt
- pepper
- a teacup of butter
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a bunch of parsley

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the chicken, cut it up as for frying, lay it in a stew-pan with water to cover it
2. Add a teaspoonful of salt and half as much pepper; set it to boil very gently, take off all scum as it rises
3. When the chicken is tender, which will be in an hour, take a teacup of butter, a tablespoonful of flour worked into it, and a bunch of parsley, put them in the stew-pan with the chicken
4. Let all stew twenty minutes, and serve
