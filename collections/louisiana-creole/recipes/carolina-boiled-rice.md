## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Pick your rice clean and wash it in two cold waters
2. Do not drain off the last water until you are ready to put the rice on the fire
3. Prepare a saucepan with water and a little salt in it
4. When it boils, sprinkle in the rice
5. Boil it hard twenty minutes, keeping it covered
6. Take it from the fire and pour off the water
7. Set the saucepan on the back of the stove with the lid off to allow the rice to dry and the grains to separate
