## Ingredients

- ¾ Cup of Farina
- 1 Quart of Water or Milk
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Add the salt to the milk or water
2. Sift in slowly sufficient Farina to make a thick gruel
3. Set in a double boiler
4. Let it cook for about a half hour, stirring frequently while it boils
