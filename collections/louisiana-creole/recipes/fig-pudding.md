## Ingredients

- Six ounces of suet
- 6 ounces of bread crumbs
- 6 ounces of sugar
- J lb. of figs chopped
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1 nutmeg
- J glass brandy
- 1 teaspoonful of yeast powder

## Directions

1. Steam 3 hours
2. Serve with a wine sauce
