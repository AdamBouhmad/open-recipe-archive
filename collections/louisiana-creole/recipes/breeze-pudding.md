## Ingredients

- i box of gelatine
- 1 pint of boiling water
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- Juice of 2 lemons
- Whites of 3 eggs
- 3 eggs
- Yolks of the other eggs
- A quart of milk

## Directions

1. Dissolve i box of gelatine in 1 pint of boiling water
2. Add 2 cupfuls of sugar and the juice of 2 lemons
3. After this has become cool (not cold), break into it the whites of 3 eggs
4. Beat all to a stiff froth
5. Pour into moulds
6. When ready for use, turn out, pouring over the following custard: make a soft custard with 3 eggs added to the yolks of the other eggs and a quart of milk
