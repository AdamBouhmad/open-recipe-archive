## Ingredients

- a firm white head of cabbage
- two cups of strong vinegar
- a quart of cut cabbage
- one teaspoonful of mustard
- salt
- a tablespoonful of butter
- three teaspoons of white sugar
- yolks of two or three eggs

## Directions

1. Chop fine a firm white head of cabbage, or better than that, slice it with a patent slicer
2. Lay it in very cold water for an hour
3. Take it out, drain it, and when drained thoroughly, place it in the dish it is to be served in
4. Take two cups of strong vinegar to a quart of cut cabbage, stir in it one teaspoonful of mustard and salt, a tablespoonful of butter, and three teaspoons of white sugar
5. Make this all hot, and at the last moment stir in the yolks of two or three eggs
6. Stir rapidly and pour on to the chopped cabbage in the dish
7. It should be served instantly or the sauce will harden
