## Ingredients

- A Fine Temperance Beverage mixture
- a bottle of champagne
- a bottle of white wine

## Directions

1. Add to the Fine Temperance Beverage mixture a bottle of champagne, and a bottle of white wine
2. You have a very delicious punch for festive occasions
