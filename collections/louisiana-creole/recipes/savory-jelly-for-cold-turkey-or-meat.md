## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef
- veal or beef bones
- half a pound of sweet ham or bacon
- thyme
- basil
- parsley
- marjoram
- salt
- pepper
- whites and shells of three eggs
- three blades of mace
- a cup of wine or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put in the pot two pounds of beef
2. If you have veal or beef bones, break them and throw them in also
3. Add half a pound of sweet ham or bacon
4. Add all the sweet herbs, such as thyme, basil, parsley and marjoram
5. Last of all, add salt and pepper to taste
6. Boil for three or four hours
7. When it is sufficiently boiled, take off, strain, and put away to cool
8. Take off all the fat and sediment
9. Clarify by throwing into it the whites and shells of three eggs
10. Add three blades of mace and a cup of wine or lemon juice
11. Place it again on the fire, let it boil a few times, and strain it through a jelly-bag
