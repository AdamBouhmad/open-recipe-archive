## Ingredients

- 4 lemons
- 1 pound of refined sugar
- 1/2 can of Gelatine
- 1 quart of water

## Directions

1. Grate three or four lemons into a pound of refined sugar, and press out the juice.
2. Boil one-half a can of Gelatine in one quart of water.
3. When reduced to one-half the quantity, add the juice and sugar.
4. Set all to boil on a very hot coal fire.
5. Pour immediately into a glass or porcelain dish.
6. Fill the glasses and set in a cool place.
