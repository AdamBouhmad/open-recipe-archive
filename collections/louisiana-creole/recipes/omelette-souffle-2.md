## Ingredients

- six eggs
- four dessertspoonfuls of white sugar powdered
- yellow rind of a lemon
- quarter of a pound of butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs, the whites and yolks separately
2. put to the yolks four dessertspoonfuls of white sugar powdered, and the yellow rind of a lemon chopped very fine
3. mix them thoroughly
4. whip the whites to a high froth and add them to the yolks
5. Put quarter of a pound of butter into the pan, over a brisk fire
6. as soon as it is completely melted pour in the mixture
7. stir it that the butter may be completely incorporated with the eggs
8. When it is so, put it in a buttered dish and set it over hot embers or ashes
9. strew powdered sugar over the top and color it with a hot shovel
10. Serve as soon as possible, as it soon falls
