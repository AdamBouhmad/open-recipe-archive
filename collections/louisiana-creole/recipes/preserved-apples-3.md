## Ingredients

- equal weights of white sugar and apples
- 1 pint of water to every 3 lbs. of sugar
- grated peel of 1 or 2 lemons
- 2 pieces of white ginger

## Directions

1. Peel, core and chop the apples fine
2. Allow 1 pint of water to every 3 lbs. of sugar
3. Boil the sugar quite thick, skimming it well
4. Add the apples to the grated peel of 1 or 2 lemons and 2 pieces of white ginger
5. Boil until the apples look clear and yellow
6. This will keep for years
