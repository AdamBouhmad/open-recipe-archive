## Ingredients

- watermelon rind
- fresh water
- alum
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the watermelon rind into thin slices
2. Throw into a bucket of fresh water, into which you have dissolved a teaspoonful of alum for every quart of water
3. Let it remain for several hours, until they grow crisp
4. Proceed in exactly the same manner as in the recipe for Citron preserves
