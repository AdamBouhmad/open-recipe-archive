## Ingredients

- 1 pint of water
- 1 pint of white wine
- 2 ounces of butter
- large bunch of parsley
- 3 leeks
- 1 clove of garlic
- bunch of thyme
- 2 bay leaves
- basil
- slices of onion
- carrots
- salt
- pepper
- fish

## Directions

1. Tie together a large bunch of parsley, 3 leeks, 1 clove of garlic, a bunch of thyme, 2 bay leaves, and a little basil
2. Boil the fish, or rather let it simmer in the courtbouillon with 1 pint of water, 1 pint of white wine, 2 ounces of butter, some slices of onion and carrots, salt and pepper until done
3. Remove the fish and strain the liquor
4. Place fish in the dish and pour liquor over it
5. Garnish the sides of dish with slices of lemon
