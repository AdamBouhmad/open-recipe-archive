## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of flour sifted
- 4 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of yeast
- 1 pint of milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix a teaspoonful of salt with the flour, and set the pan before the fire to rise
2. Warm the milk and stir it into the flour, so as to make a stiff batter
3. Beat the eggs very light and stir them into the yeast
4. Add the eggs and yeast to the batter, and beat all well together
5. If it is too stiff, add a little more warm milk
6. Cover the pan closely and set it to rise near the fire
7. Bake it when quite light
8. Have the baking irons hot, grease them, pour on a ladleful of batter, let it slowly bake, and when done on one side, turn it to the other
9. Butter them and send them to the table hot
