## Ingredients

- onions (three)
- butter (a spoonful)
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- sage
- parsley
- thyme
- liquor in which the fowl was cooked
- catsup (a tablespoonful)

## Directions

1. Slice three onions after peeling them; fry them a bright brown in a spoonful of butter;
2. sprinkle a little flour in, and let it brown also;
3. add salt, pepper, and also sage, if for goose or duck, and parsley and thyme if for chickens or roast meat;
4. add a cup of the liquor in which the fowl was cooked, let it boil up and add a tablespoonful of catsup.
