## Ingredients

- 1 pint of scalding milk
- 1 quart of sifted cornmeal
- a spoonful of good lard
- 3 well beaten eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 tablespoonfuls of yeast

## Directions

1. Pour the scalding milk on the sifted cornmeal
2. When cool, stir in the lard, eggs, and salt
3. Stir this with 2 tablespoonfuls of yeast
4. Beat all together and set it to rise
5. When risen, bake in tins
