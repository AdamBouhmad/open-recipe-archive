## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 lemons
- 1 ounce isinglass
- 1 quart cream

## Directions

1. Grate the peel of 2 or 3 lemons into a dish
2. Squeeze the juice of 3 upon it
3. Sweeten it well
4. Dissolve 1 ounce of isinglass in a very little water
5. Strain it in a quart of cream, which you will boil
6. Put it into a jug, and pour it as slowly as possible, that the mixture may froth
7. Do not move the dish until the contents are quite cold
8. The cream should be poured in as hot as the safety of the dish will permit
