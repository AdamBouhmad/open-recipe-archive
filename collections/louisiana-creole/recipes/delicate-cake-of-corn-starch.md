## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- one-fourth of a pound of butter
- whites of eight eggs
- quarter of a pound of corn starch
- quarter of a pound of common flour
- one teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- half spoon of fine soda

## Directions

1. Beat all very light
2. add to the flour a teaspoonful of cream of tartar, and a half-spoon of fine soda
3. Flavor with lemon or rose
