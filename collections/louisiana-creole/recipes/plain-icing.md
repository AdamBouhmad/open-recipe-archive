## Ingredients

- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lemon Juice
- 1 Pound of White Pulverized Sugar
- Essence of Rose, Orange or Lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of the eggs, and sift in gradually the sugar, beating all the while
2. Add in the beginning a tablespoonful of Rose or Lemon Essence or Orange Flower Water
3. Beat very, very light, till sufficient almost to stand alone
4. Have ready in another bowl a little lemon juice
5. Begin to lay on the icing in a thin coat over the cake with a knife, occasionally dipping the knife into the lemon juice, to make a nice, smooth icing
6. When it is covered with this coat, set in the oven or the sun to harden
7. Add another coating as before
