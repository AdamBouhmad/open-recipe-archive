## Ingredients

- 1½ Cups of Sifted, Powdered White Sugar
- 1 Cup Sifted Flour
- Whites of 11 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful Vanilla
- ½ Teaspoonful Salt

## Directions

1. Put the baking powder and the salt (or use cream of tartar instead of baking powder) into the flour, and sift it seven times.
2. If you use cream of tartar, add it in the last sifting.
3. Sift the sugar seven times.
4. Beat the whites of the eggs to a very stiff froth, and add gradually the sugar, mixing very carefully.
5. Then add the flour, gradually beating all the while, and lastly add the flavoring extract.
6. Pour the mixture into a Turk's head baking pan (ungreased).
7. Bake for forty-five minutes in a moderate oven.
8. Test with the broom wisp.
9. When done, turn the cake upside down, resting the pan on the tube till the cake falls off.
