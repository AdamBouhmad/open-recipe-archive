## Ingredients

- a quart of milk
- a tablespoonful of flour
- cold milk
- a little salt
- loaf sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make a quart of milk boiling hot
2. make a tablespoonful of flour into a batter with cold milk
3. add a little salt and stir it in the boiling milk
4. stirring it constantly for five minutes while it boils
5. flavor with anything agreeable
6. Sweetened with loaf sugar, and nutmeg grated plentifully over it
