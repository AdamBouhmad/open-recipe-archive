## Ingredients

- fruit
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Weigh the fruit, and to each pound of fruit use a pound of sugar
2. make a syrup of the sugar with a half pint of water to each pound of fruit
3. Boil it clear
4. put in the fruit and cook it well
5. boil gently till the fruit is clear
