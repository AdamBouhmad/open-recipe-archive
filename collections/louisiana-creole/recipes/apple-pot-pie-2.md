## Ingredients

- a pint of flour
- a heaping spoonful of lard
- a little salt
- cold water
- one dozen apples
- one large cup of sugar
- one tablespoonful of spices
- two of molasses
- one of butter
- water
- a little flour

## Directions

1. Rub into a pint of flour a heaping spoonful of lard
2. Strew in a little salt, and work it until the mass becomes numberless little globules and balls
3. Moisten with cold water, and press them together until they adhere
4. Pare, core and quarter one dozen apples
5. Put them in a baking pan, with one large cup of sugar, one tablespoonful of spices, two of molasses and one of butter
6. add water until the fruit is nearly covered
7. put it in the oven to bake and stew, and brown
8. When the apples begin to soften, dredge in a little flour, for the juice, though plentiful, must not be watery
9. Roll out the pastry
10. Cut the cover to suit the pan, and make the trimmings into dumplings, which must be dropped at intervals among the fruit
11. Fold the pie cover in half, make several oblique incisions for openings, lay it on and brown it lightly
12. Serve on a dish like peach cobbler
