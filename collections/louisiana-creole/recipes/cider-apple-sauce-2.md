## Ingredients

- new sweet cider
- apples

## Directions

1. Boil down new sweet cider, till about as thick as good molasses
2. When cold, strain it through a sieve
3. As soon as it boils put in the apples, pared, quartered and cored
4. Stew over a slow fire till the fruit is perfectly tender
