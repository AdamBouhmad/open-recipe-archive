## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- % Pound of Sago
- 1/4 Cup of Powdered Sugar
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla Essence

## Directions

1. Boil the milk and add the sago, and stir briskly for ten or fifteen minutes
2. Take off the stove and set to cool
3. Add the sugar and mix thoroughly
4. Add the eggs and a tablespoonful of Vanilla Essence and mix thoroughly
5. Butter a pudding mold or pan and fill with the mixture
6. Set in a tin pan and fill the latter with hot water
7. Place in the oven and let it steam for half an hour
8. Remove from the fire, turn into a dessert dish and send to the table hot
9. Serve with a Cream Sauce
