## Ingredients

- yolks of ten eggs
- ten tablespoonfuls of pulverized sugar
- three pints of new milk
- one pint of the best brandy
- whites of ten eggs

## Directions

1. Take the yolks of ten eggs; add to them ten tablespoonfuls of pulverized sugar, three pints of new milk, and one pint of the best brandy
2. Beat up the whites the last thing, and stir in, after the liquor is poured in
