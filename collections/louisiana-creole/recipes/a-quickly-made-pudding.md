## Ingredients

- crackers
- raisins
- butter
- wine
- sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Split a few crackers, lay the surface over with raisins, and place the halves together again
2. Tie them closely in a cloth
3. Boil them fifteen minutes
4. Serve with a rich sauce of butter, wine, sugar and nutmeg
