## Ingredients

- ingredients for queen cake (omitting wine, brandy and rose water)
- grated yellow rind of 4 large, ripe oranges
- juice of 4 large, ripe oranges

## Directions

1. Make a mixture precisely as for queen cake, only omit the wine, brandy and rose water
2. Substitute the grated yellow rind and the juice of 4 large, ripe oranges
3. Stir into the batter in turn with the eggs and flour
4. Flavor the icing with orange juice
