## Ingredients

- 1 Half Cup of Rice
- 3 Pints of Broth or Consomme

## Directions

1. Prepare the clear Pot-au-Feu or Consomme
2. When nearly done add one half cupful of rice, which has been thoroughly washed and dried
3. Cook for about twenty-five minutes longer, or until done
4. Serve
