## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Dried Red Beans
- 1 Pound Bacon
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Soak the beans over night.
2. Drain off all water.
3. Place in a pot and cover well with cold water.
4. Add the bacon, leaving it in a single square piece.
5. When both have boiled about two hours, season well with pepper and a little salt, if necessary.
6. Place the bacon in the center of a baking dish.
7. Drain the beans and put them around the bacon.
8. Fill the pan to the top with liquor in which the beans have been boiled, and bake one hour and a half, or until the liquor is nearly all absorbed.
9. Serve hot.
