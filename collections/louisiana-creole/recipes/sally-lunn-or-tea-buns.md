## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 1/2 quart of good yeast
- flour
- 1/2 lb. of butter melted in sweet milk
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Add yeast to milk
2. Put in as much flour as will make a stiff batter
3. Let it stand 6 or 8 hours
4. Add melted butter and eggs to sugar
5. Work in sufficient flour to make it the consistency of dough
6. Let it stand 1 hour
7. Work it off, forming it into buns or in a large mould
8. Let it stand 1/2 hour
9. Bake it in a quick oven
