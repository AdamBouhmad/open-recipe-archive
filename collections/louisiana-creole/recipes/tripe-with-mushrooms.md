## Ingredients

- tripe
- pepper
- salt
- flour or rolled cracker
- bacon fat
- vinegar
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Clean and parboil tripe before cooking.
2. When it is white and tender, cut it into pieces suitable to fry; pepper and salt it, and dip it in flour or rolled cracker, then drop it into hot bacon fat.
3. When browned on both sides, take up and make a gravy of some of the fat in which it was fried, a little flour, and a wineglass of good vinegar.
4. Pour this around the tripe and serve with mushrooms.
