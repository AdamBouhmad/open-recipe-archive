## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoonful of brown sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of wine vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak all night in water enough to cover the ham
2. Put the ham into a large vessel full of cold water and simmer 20 minutes to each lb. of meat
3. When done, take off the skin
4. Grate bread crumbs over the top
5. Sprinkle 1 tablespoonful of brown sugar over the bread crumbs
6. Pour over them 1 tablespoonful of wine vinegar
7. Bake for 1 hour
8. Serve cold
