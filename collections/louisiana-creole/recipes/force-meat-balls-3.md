## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound of beef or veal and chopped chicken about an inch in thickness
- a little of the liver and tongue of the calf
- 1/2 dozen small onions
- 1 tablespoonful of sweet marjoram
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful powdered black pepper
- 1 teaspoonful mace
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cloves (powdered)
- 3 eggs
- 3 grated crackers (sifted)
- 1/2 gill of good sherry wine
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 teaspoonfuls of salt
- lard or butter for frying

## Directions

1. Chop the beef or veal and chopped chicken about an inch in thickness.
2. Add a little of the liver and tongue of the calf, a half dozen small onions, one tablespoonful of sweet marjoram, one grated nutmeg, a teaspoonful each of powdered black pepper and mace, and a half teaspoonful of cloves (powdered), three eggs, three grated crackers (sifted), half a gill of good sherry wine, a tablespoonful of butter and two teaspoonfuls of salt.
3. Chop up and mix thoroughly together.
4. Roll in balls and fry slowly in lard or butter.
5. Serve with the soup.
