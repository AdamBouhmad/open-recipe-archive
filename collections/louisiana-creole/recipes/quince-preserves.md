## Ingredients

- ripe quinces
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pare the ripe quinces, and then cut them into slices or rings
2. Weigh them and put them into the preserving kettle and cover with water
3. When they are quite tender, pour off the superfluous water, leaving about half a pint to every pound of fruit
4. Add sugar, pound for pound
5. Let the whole boil till the fruit is tender
6. Carefully drain out the fruit and lay it in the jars
7. Return the kettle to the fire, and let the syrup boil to a jelly
8. Fill the jars with it
