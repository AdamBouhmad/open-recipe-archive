## Ingredients

- a chicken
- butter
- pepper
- salt
- liquid the chicken was boiled in

## Directions

1. Boil a chicken until it is tender
2. Take out all the bones
3. Chop the meat fine
4. Season with butter, pepper and salt
5. Pour in enough of the liquid the chicken was boiled in to make it moist
6. Simmer for a few moments
7. Put it in a mould to cool
8. Slice nicely when serving
