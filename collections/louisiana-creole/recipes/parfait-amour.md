## Ingredients

- 2 Ounces of Zest of Lemons
- 4 Ounces of Zest of Limes
- 2 Drachms of Cloves
- 3 Sprigs of Rosemary Leaves
- A Handful of Orange Blossoms
- 2½ Gallons of Alcohol
- 10 Pounds of Sugar
- 3 Quarts of Water

## Directions

1. Grate the zests of the lemons and limes carefully, without touching the white inner pulp.
2. Bruise the rosemary leaves and orange blossoms and steep in the alcohol fifteen days.
3. Make a syrup with the sugar and water, add the liqueur, distill and filter, color lightly with Cochineal, and bottle for use.
