## Ingredients

- whites of eight eggs
- one pound of white sugar finely ground
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- a tea-cupful of sweet milk
- a small teaspoonful of powdered volatile salts or saleratus
- sifted flour
- a teaspoonful of orange-flower water or lemon extract
- a quarter of a pound of shelled almonds blanched
- white of egg

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of eight eggs to a high froth
2. add gradually the sugar
3. beat butter to a cream
4. add milk with dissolved salts to butter
5. put eggs to the butter and milk
6. add flour to make it as thick as pound-cake mixture
7. add flavoring
8. add almonds beaten to a paste with white of egg
9. beat whole together until light and white
10. line square tin pan with buttered paper
11. put in mixture an inch deep
12. bake half an hour in a quick oven
13. turn upside down when cold and ice the exposed side
14. mark in slices the width of a finger and two inches and a half long
