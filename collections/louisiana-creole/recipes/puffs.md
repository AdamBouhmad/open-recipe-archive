## Ingredients

- seven or eight potatoes
- two well-beaten eggs
- two tablespoonfuls of melted butter
- a cup of milk

## Directions

1. Mash seven or eight potatoes smoothly
2. Mix in with them two well-beaten eggs, two tablespoonfuls of melted butter, also well-beaten, and a cup of milk
3. Pour it into a pan and bake in a hot stove
