## Ingredients

- one cup of molasses
- one and a half cups of sugar
- one cup of butter
- four eggs
- a cup of sour milk
- one heaping-spoonful of soda
- flour

## Directions

1. grease the pan carefully as molasses cake is liable to stick
2. Make the batter stiff with flour
3. bake in a pan
