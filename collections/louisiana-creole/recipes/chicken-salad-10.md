## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Cold Boiled Chicken
- 3/4 Pint of Mayonnaise Sauce
- 1 Head of Crisp Fresh Lettuce
- Hard-Boiled Eggs
- Sliced Beets
- Celery Tips

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into small dice
2. Chop half of the lettuce very fine, and season well with salt and pepper
3. Make a bed of the remainder of the lettuce leaves, and place first a layer of the chicken and then of the lettuce, until you have used all
4. Spread the Mayonnaise Sauce over the top nicely, and garnish prettily with slices of cold hard-boiled eggs, sliced beets, celery tips, etc.
