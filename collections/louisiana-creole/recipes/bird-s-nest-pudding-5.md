## Ingredients

- 6 Apples
- 1 Pint of Rich Cream
- 2 Cups of Flour
- 1 Teaspoonful of Soda

## Directions

1. Pare and core the apples, and then put them in the bottom of a buttered dish.
2. Mix the cream with the flour until smooth, and add a teaspoonful of baking powder, which you will have dissolved well in a tablespoonful of boiling water.
3. Mix again thoroughly, and pour over the apples.
4. Set in a moderate oven, and bake for one hour.
5. Serve with Hard Cream or Brandy Sauce.
