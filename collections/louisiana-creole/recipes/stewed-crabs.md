## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 crabs
- white wine
- pepper
- salt
- grated nutmeg
- bread crumbs
- yolks of 2 eggs
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take 3 or 4 crabs and pick the meat out of the body and claws
2. Take care that no spongy part be left among it, or any of the shell
3. Put this meat into a stew pan with a little white wine, some pepper and salt, and a little grated nutmeg
4. Heat all this together and put in some crumbs of bread, the yolks of 2 eggs beat up, and 1 spoonful of vinegar
5. Stir all well together
6. Make some toasted sippets, lay them on a plate and pour in the crabs
7. Serve it up hot
