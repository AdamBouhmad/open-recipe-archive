## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Sweet, Fresh Cream
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Nutmeg, Grated
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla, or Sherry or Madeira

## Directions

1. Add the sugar essence or wine to the cream
2. Stir constantly till thoroughly dissolved
3. Beat vigorously, and then add the nutmeg and set to cool
4. Serve with stewed apples and other stewed fruits, or fruit puddings
