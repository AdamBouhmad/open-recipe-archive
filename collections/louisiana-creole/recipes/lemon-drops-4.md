## Ingredients

- half a pound of finely powdered sugar
- lemon juice
- grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Pour just enough lemon juice on the sugar to dissolve it
2. Boil to the consistency of thick syrup
3. Drop this in plates
4. Put in a warm place to harden
5. Add grated lemon peel and proceed
