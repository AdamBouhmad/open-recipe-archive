## Ingredients

- ripe currants
- boiled cream
- cinnamon finely beaten

## Directions

1. Bruise currants that are quite ripe in boiled cream
2. Put in some cinnamon, finely beaten
3. Sweeten to the taste
4. Strain it through a sieve
