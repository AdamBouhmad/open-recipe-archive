## Ingredients

- a quart of Graham flour
- one half cup of brown sugar
- one teaspoon of salt
- two tablespoonfuls of yeast
- warm water or milk
- a little soda

## Directions

1. Take a quart of Graham flour, one half cup of brown sugar, one teaspoon of salt, two tablespoonfuls of yeast, warm water or milk enough to soften it sufficiently to stir readily with a spoon
2. When it is light, stir up again and drop in rings and bake
3. If made over night, add a little soda in the morning
4. Bake soft
