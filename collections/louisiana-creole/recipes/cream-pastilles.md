## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Fine White Confectionery Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonfull of Vinegar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon Extract
- 1 Teaspoonful of Cream of Tartar
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Water

## Directions

1. Moisten the sugar with the water and set to boil
2. Add vinegar and Cream of Tartar
3. Let it boil till brittle
4. Then take from the fire and add the Lemon Extract
5. Turn quickly on a wet slab or buttered plates
6. When it begins to cool, pull till very, very white, and then cut into small squares of about a half inch
