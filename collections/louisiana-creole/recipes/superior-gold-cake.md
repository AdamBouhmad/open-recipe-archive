## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- one pound of flour
- yolks of ten eggs
- one teaspoonful of soda mixed with a little hot water

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar together
2. beat the eggs light, and add them to the butter and sugar
3. stir in the flour and soda
