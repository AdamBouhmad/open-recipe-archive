## Ingredients

- 5 Eggs
- 0.75 Pound of Powdered Sugar
- 0.75 Pound of Sifted Flour
- 0.75 Pound of Butter
- 0.25 Teaspoonful of Vanilla
- Grated Rind of a Lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream and add the yolks of the eggs, the lemon rind and the vanilla, and beat till very, very light
2. Then add the flour, beating constantly, and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
3. Turn into buttered tins, and bake in a quick oven till a delicate brown
4. Serve cold
