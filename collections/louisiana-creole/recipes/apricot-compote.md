## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Apricots
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1/4 Pound of Apple Jelly

## Directions

1. Cut the apricots in two, and peel
2. Rinse them in cold water
3. Cook them in syrup made of sugar and a little water
4. Add a little Apple Jelly to the sugar after you have taken out the apricots
5. Mix the jelly well with the syrup, pour over the apricots and serve
