## Ingredients

- butter
- pounded biscuits
- shrimps
- stewed tomatoes
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Butter well a deep dish
2. Place a thick layer of pounded biscuits in the dish, having picked and boiled your shrimps
3. Put a layer of stewed tomatoes, with a little butter, pepper and salt
4. Add a thinner layer of beat biscuits and another of shrimps
5. Continue with layers until 3 or 4 layers of both are put in the dish
6. The last layer must be of biscuits
7. Bake quite brown
