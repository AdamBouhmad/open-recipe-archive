## Ingredients

- One tablespoonful of good yeast
- one egg
- one large spoonful of butter
- one pound or pint of flour
- a little salt
- enough milk

## Directions

1. Set it to rise in a warmed pan until it is light
2. sift a cup of flour into the bread-tray, and pour the light batter in
3. work it well and keep the dough very soft
4. set the dough to rise
5. when light, make into rolls, and lay them on a warmed and buttered pan
6. set them by the fire to rise again
7. baste the top over with butter
8. bake in a quick oven as soon as they are light
