## Ingredients

- 1 Teaspoonful Each of Ground Cloves and Cinnamon
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon Extract
- 2 Cups of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Cup of Sweet Milk
- The Whites of 4 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- Flour Sufficient to Make a Soft Batter

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add the milk and the baking powder, and gradually add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
3. Beat the batter hard for ten minutes, after adding sufficient flour to make a nice soft batter
4. Pour into baking tins, and bake to a delicate brown
5. When cold ice with Plain Icing
