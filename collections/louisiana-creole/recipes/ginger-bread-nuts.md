## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of white powdered sugar
- 1 ounce of grated ginger
- peel of 1 lemon

## Directions

1. Rub butter, sugar, ginger, and lemon peel into flour
2. Bake in a slow oven
