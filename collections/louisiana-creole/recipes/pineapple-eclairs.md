## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Flour
- 1 Cup of Water
- 4 Eggs
- 3/4 Cup of Water
- 2 Ounces of Preserved Pineapple or Pineapple Mousse

## Directions

1. Make Pineapple Eclairs in the same manner as Chocolate Eclairs
2. Fill them in with preserved pineapple or Pineapple Mousse
