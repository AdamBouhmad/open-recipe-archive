## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of crushed sugar
- 12 eggs whipped to a froth
- 1 lb. of flour
- 3 lbs. of stoned raisins
- 3 lbs. of cleaned currants
- 5 grated nutmegs
- 1 ounce of pounded cinnamon
- 1 teaspoonful of ground cloves sifted
- 1 lb. of citron cut into thin slices

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Stir in the whipped eggs
3. Sift in the flour
4. Add the raisins, currants, nutmegs, cinnamon, cloves, and citron
5. Mix all well together
6. Bake in a buttered pan in rather a moderate oven
