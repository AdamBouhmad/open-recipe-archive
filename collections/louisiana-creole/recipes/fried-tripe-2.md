## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Tripe
- 1 Egg
- Grated Bread Crumbs
- Parsley and Lemon to Garnish
- Sauce Piquante or Sauce Poivrade

## Directions

1. Prepare the tripe, boil well, and cut into pieces of three inches in length and one in width.
2. Roll it in a beaten egg and then roll in grated bread crumbs.
3. Drop in boiling lard and fry to a golden brown.
4. Take off the fire and place on a bed of fried parsley and garnish with sliced lemon.
5. Serve with a Sauce Piquante or a Sauce Poivrade.
