## Ingredients

- 1 Large, Ripe Pineapple
- 1 Quart of Cream or Milk
- 6 Eggs
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Peel the pineapple, and then grate and pound and mash the pulp with one-half of the sugar
2. Press through a sieve
3. Put the milk or cream on to boil
4. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the remaining half of the sugar very light, and then beat the whites to a stiff froth
5. Take the milk off the fire, and pour it over the sugar and yolks, stirring all the while
6. Add the whites, mixing thoroughly
7. Add the pineapple and juice and mix well
8. Set to cool and then turn into the freezer, pack and stir around in the ice box for about ten minutes, and proceed as in the recipe for making and freezing Cream
