## Ingredients

- duck
- butter
- buttered toast
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Split the duck like a partridge down the back
2. Broil on clear coals, butter freely
3. Pepper and salt when broiled, just before putting on the butter
4. Serve on buttered toast
