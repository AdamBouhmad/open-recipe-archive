## Ingredients

- 1 pint of potatoes
- 1 pint of water
- flour
- 1 pint of cold water
- 1/2 pint hop tea

## Directions

1. Put potatoes in water and boil it until reduced to 1 teaspoonful of water
2. Peel the potatoes and mash them into a light teacupful of flour with the boiling water in which they were boiled
3. After mixing well, add 1 pint of cold water in small quantity at a time
4. Add 1/2 pint hop tea
5. Set it to rise
