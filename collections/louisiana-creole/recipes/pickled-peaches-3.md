## Ingredients

- peaches
- weak lye
- strong salt and water
- strong vinegar
- mace
- cloves
- horse radish
- white mustard seed
- turmeric

## Directions

1. Wash your peaches in weak lye
2. Rub them with a coarse cloth to remove the fur
3. Put them in strong salt and water for 7 or 8 days
4. Wipe them dry
5. Put them carefully into the jars
6. Cover them with strong vinegar
7. Add mace, cloves, horse radish, white mustard seed and turmeric
