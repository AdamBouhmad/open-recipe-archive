## Ingredients

- 1 gallon of water
- 4 handfuls of cut okra
- 1 handful of lima beans
- 3 cymblins
- small piece of fresh meat or fowl
- 5 tomatoes
- butter
- flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Let water boil
2. Put into it 4 handfuls of cut okra
3. Half an hour after, put in a handful of lima beans, 3 cymblins and a small piece of fresh meat or fowl
4. Before you take up the soup, put in 5 tomatoes cut in slices
5. When all are well boiled and mixed together, take butter rolled in flour, but not to make the soup too thick
6. Season with pepper and salt
7. Make it in a stone vessel and stir it with a wooden spoon
8. Put your soup on early, that it may simmer on the fire
