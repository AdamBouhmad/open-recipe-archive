## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Pints of Raspberry Juice or Pulp
- 1 1/2 Pints of Pickled Raspberries
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 3 Drops of Cochineal
- The Juice of 1/2 Lemon

## Directions

1. Stem the berries, and add the sugar and lemon, and stand aside in their juice for an hour and a half.
2. Then strain through a fine sieve, and add the reserved juice.
3. Turn into the freezer, and proceed to freeze according to directions given under heading Ice Cream.
