## Ingredients

- 1 Large or 2 Small Cauliflowers
- A Teaspoonful of Salt
- A White Sauce or Sauce Allemande

## Directions

1. Separate the cauliflower, piece by piece, having taken off the outer leaves and cut off the rough stalk.
2. Place them in a pot of boiling water, with a teaspoonful of salt, and let them boil rapidly for about half an hour, till the stalks are tender.
3. When done, take them out gently with a skimmer, that you may not break them.
4. Place in a bowl and pour over a Sauce Blanche or Sauce Allemande.
