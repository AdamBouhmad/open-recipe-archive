## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- vanilla
- yolks of 8 eggs
- 1 small tablespoonful of flour
- 3 ounces of white sugar

## Directions

1. Put on 1 quart of milk, with as much vanilla as will highly flavor it
2. Add the yolks of 8 eggs, beaten with a small tablespoonful of flour
3. Mix these together as for boiled custard
4. Put 3 ounces of white sugar into a clean frying pan
5. Let it boil up clear
6. As soon as it is brown, add a little of the custard to prevent the sugar from getting in lumps
7. Pour the whole into the custard, and sweeten it to the taste
