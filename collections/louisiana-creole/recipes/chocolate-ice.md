## Ingredients

- 6 yolks of eggs
- ½ lb. of sugar
- ½ lb. of chocolate
- boiling water
- 1 quart of cream and milk

## Directions

1. Have 6 yolks of eggs and ½ lb. of sugar well strained together as for cake
2. Roll out ½ lb. of chocolate
3. Pour a teacupful of boiling water on it, a little at a time, until it is well mixed
4. Boil 1 quart of cream and milk together
5. When it boils, mix it with the chocolate, a little at a time
6. Put it on the fire and when it boils, pour it on the eggs, mixing it all the time
7. Put it again on the fire and stir it until it becomes thick
8. It must not boil
9. When cold, freeze it
