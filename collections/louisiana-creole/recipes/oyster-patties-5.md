## Ingredients

- 4 Dozen Oysters
- 3/4 Can of Mushrooms
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Small Onion, Grated
- Pepper, Salt, Chopped Parsley and Lemon Juice
- Flour
- Puff paste

## Directions

1. Prepare a puff paste (see recipe) and lay on the ice to cool.
2. Boil the oysters in their own liquor. Drain, put the butter in the saucepan, and when it is heated, add the grated onion and rub in the flour until smooth.
3. Add a gill of mushroom Juice and pepper, salt and Cayenne to taste, and the mushrooms chopped in quarters.
4. Then add the oysters and let all stew about five minutes, adding the lemon Juice.
5. A tablespoonful of cream will improve the oysters. If this is used, omit the lemon Juice.
6. Line the small tins with the puff paste and put In each three or four oysters according to the size of the pate.
7. Cover with the paste and bake in a quick oven about fifteen minutes.
