## Ingredients

- one quart of rye meal
- two quarts of Indian meal
- two tablespoonfuls of molasses
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix one quart of rye meal, two quarts of Indian meal, and two tablespoonfuls of molasses thoroughly with sweet milk
2. Let it stand two hours
3. Bake in a slow oven
