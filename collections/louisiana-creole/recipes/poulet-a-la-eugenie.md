## Ingredients

- 1 or 2 chickens
- water
- small piece of pork
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- mace
- celery seed
- small delicate onion

## Directions

1. Prepare 1 or 2 chickens for boiling
2. Cover well with water and put in a small piece of pork
3. Boil until the chicken falls from the bone
4. Select the white meat and mince or mash in a mortar until it becomes paste
5. Put in a skillet
6. Place this where it will keep warm and add nearly as much butter as you have chicken
7. Add a little salt, pepper, mace and celery seed if you choose
8. Boil a small, delicate onion, chopped fine and mix through it when simmered
9. Let it become quite firm and cold before pressing into jelly jars
