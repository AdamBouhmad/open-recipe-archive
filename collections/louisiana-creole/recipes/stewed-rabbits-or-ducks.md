## Ingredients

- 2 rabbits
- 2 or 3 onions
- 1 bunch of parsley
- 6 spoonfuls of claret wine
- 3 spoonfuls of vinegar
- 2 or 3 anchovies
- 1 lb. of fresh butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Put 2 or 3 onions and 1 bunch of parsley with the rabbits
2. Half boil them and cut them in pieces as for a fricassee
3. Take the onions, parsley and the liver and cut it small
4. Take 6 spoonfuls of claret wine and 3 of vinegar
5. Dissolve 2 or 3 anchovies into it
6. Put all this in the stewpan with 1 lb. of fresh butter worked up with flour
7. Thicken it and serve it up
