## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Flour
- 1/2 Cup of Butter
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Water
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla
- 1 Tablespoonful of Cornstarch

## Directions

1. Set the water to boil, and while boiling, stir in the butter
2. Add the flour, and stir continually till the paste leaves the sides of the saucepan
3. Set the mixture to cool
4. When cool, stir one after another, the three eggs in, without beating them
5. After adding the last egg, beat very vigorously for about four minutes
6. Drop by tablespoonfuls on buttered tins, and set in the oven to bake from twenty to thirty minutes
7. Watch carefully so that they will not burn
8. Rub the cornstarch in a little water and add it to the boiling milk
9. Let it boil three minutes, stirring constantly
10. Beat the eggs, without separating, and the sugar till light, and add to the boiling milk
11. Add the vanilla, stirring all well
12. When cold make an opening inside, through the side of the paste, with a sharp knife, and fill it with the custard
13. Fill the cakes and set away to cool
