## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. of flour
- 5 teaspoonfuls of dry soda
- 6 teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- 6 teaspoonfuls of salt
- milk
- flour
- a little shortening

## Directions

1. Pulverize 6 lbs. of flour
2. mix 5 teaspoonfuls of dry soda all through it, then 6 teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar, and 6 of salt
3. incorporate all these
4. add milk and flour, a little shortening
5. make into a soft dough
6. bake in tins
