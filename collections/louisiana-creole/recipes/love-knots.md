## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 5 spoonfuls of white sugar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda dissolved in 2 teaspoonfuls of cold water
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- flour enough to roll

## Directions

1. Mix the eggs, sugar, soda mixture, and butter together
2. Add flour to make a dough to roll
3. Cut the sheet in slips
4. Tie them in love knots
5. Fry in pure white lard
