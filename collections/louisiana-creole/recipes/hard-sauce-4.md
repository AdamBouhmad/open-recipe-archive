## Ingredients

- one quarter of a pound of butter
- half a pound of pulverized white sugar
- one wineglass of wine or brandy
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat a quarter of a pound of butter to a cream
2. Stir into it half a pound of pulverized white sugar
3. Beat it until it is light
4. A wineglass of wine or brandy may be added
5. Grate nutmeg over it
6. Put it on ice if the weather is warm
