## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- 1 tablespoonful of black pepper
- 1 tablespoonful of allspice
- fish
- butter
- walnut pickle vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 tablespoonful of salt, 1 of black pepper, and 1 of allspice
2. Rub the fish after being well cleaned with this mixture
3. Put them in a deep dish of a size to suit the fish, as the bottom should be covered
4. Butter the dish before putting the fish in, with a bit of butter on the top of each fish
5. Mix walnut pickle vinegar, and if it is very sharp, add a little water after the dish is filled
6. Bake it in a slow oven
