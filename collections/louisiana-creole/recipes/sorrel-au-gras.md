## Ingredients

- Sorrel
- Croutons
- Butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the sorrel in exactly the same manner as spinach, or Epinards au Jus.
2. Reject all fibrous portions.
3. After you have scalded it, drain thoroughly.
4. Then chop and press through a colander.
5. Garnish nicely with Croutons fried in butter.
