## Ingredients

- green ham of 18 lbs.
- 1 dessertspoonful of saltpetre
- 1 pound of brown sugar
- fine salt
- ground black pepper

## Directions

1. Apply 1 dessertspoonful of saltpetre and 1 pound of brown sugar to the fleshy side of the ham and about the hock
2. Cover the fleshy side with fine salt 1 inch thick
3. Pack away in tubs, to remain from 3 to 6 weeks, according to size
4. Before smoking, rub off all the salt from the ham
5. Cover well with ground black pepper, particularly about the bone and hock
6. Hang up and drain for 2 weeks
7. Smoke with green wood 8 weeks, till the rind is brown
