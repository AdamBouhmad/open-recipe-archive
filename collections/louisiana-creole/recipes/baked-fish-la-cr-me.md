## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- water
- 1 quart of cream or milk
- yolks of 4 eggs
- bunch of parsley
- 1 onion
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1/2 pound of butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil the fish with salt in the water
2. flake off from the bones
3. boil 1 quart of cream (or milk), and pour hot on the yolks of 4 well beaten eggs
4. add a bunch of parsley, 1 onion, and salt
5. let it boil up and then strain
6. put it on the fire again, and when boiling, throw in 3 tablespoonfuls of flour, perfectly smooth
7. add 1/2 pound of butter
8. butter a deep dish, and put a layer of fish, then a layer of sauce
9. put in fish and sauce, until the dish is full
10. finish off with bread crumbs
11. Bake 1/2 hour
