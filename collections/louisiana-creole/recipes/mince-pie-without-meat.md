## Ingredients

- one pound of currants
- one pound of peeled and chopped apples
- one pound of suet chopped fine
- one pound of moist brown sugar
- quarter of a pound of chopped and stoned raisins
- the juice of four oranges and two lemons
- the peel of one lemon chopped
- a wine-glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix all carefully and put in a cool place
2. Eat this pie hot, and when it is baked, put in a tablespoonful of butter, but put none in the mixture
