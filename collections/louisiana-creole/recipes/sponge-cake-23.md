## Ingredients

- 9 Eggs
- 1 Pound of White Pulverized Sugar
- 3/4 Pound Flour
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs very light, and then add the sugar, and beat till very light.
2. Then add the juice of the lemon, and add gradually one-half of the flour.
3. Have the whites of the eggs beaten to a very stiff froth, so clear that they may be cut with a knife.
4. Add one-half to the cake, and then add the remaining half of the flour.
5. Beat well.
6. Now add the remaining half of the whites, and beat lightly.
7. Pour into a cake pan, lined at the bottom and around with buttered paper, and bake in a quick oven from forty-five minutes to one hour, using the broom-straw test.
8. Cover the cake with a layer of thick brown paper when you first put it in the oven.
9. Keep the heat of the oven steady.
