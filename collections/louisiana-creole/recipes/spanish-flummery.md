## Ingredients

- 1 ounce isinglass
- 1 pint water
- yolks of 8 eggs
- juice of 2 large lemons
- rind of 1 lemon
- 1/2 lb. sugar
- 1 quart good Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Boil isinglass in water till dissolved
2. Beat up the yolks of 8 eggs, the juice of 2 large lemons, and the rind of 1, 1/2 lb. of sugar
3. Add all these to the isinglass, stirring it over the fire till it boils
4. Add to it 1 quart of good Madeira wine
5. Strain it through a sieve
6. Keep stirring it till nearly cold, and then pour it into moulds
