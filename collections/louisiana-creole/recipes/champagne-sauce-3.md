## Ingredients

- 1 glass of Champagne
- 2 cloves
- 6 whole peppers
- 1 bay leaf
- 3/4 of a pint of Sauce Espagnole
- 1/8 teaspoonful of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Put the Champagne, cloves, peppers, bay leaf and sugar in a saucepan
2. Set on the fire and reduce for five minutes
3. Moisten the mixture with three quarters of a pint of Sauce Espagnole
4. Let it cook for fifteen minutes longer
5. Strain well and serve
