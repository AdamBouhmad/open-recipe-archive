## Ingredients

- one cup of molasses
- one and a half cups of brown sugar
- one tablespoonful of vinegar
- a lump of butter the size of an egg
- two cups of popped corn

## Directions

1. Boil molasses, brown sugar, vinegar, and butter until thick
2. Chop two cups of popped corn rather fine
3. Put it into the boiling candy
4. Pour it all on the buttered plates
5. Cut in squares to be eaten without pulling
