## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. of New Orleans sugar
- water
- 1 lb. of butter

## Directions

1. Boil until it cracks
2. When almost done, stir constantly to prevent burning
3. When done pour on a marble slab, or in a flat dish
4. When cool, pull till light brown
