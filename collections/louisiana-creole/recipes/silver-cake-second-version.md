## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs. of flour
- yeast powder
- whites of 28 eggs
- 2 lbs. of sugar
- 12 ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Mix flour, yeast powder, egg whites, sugar, and butter
2. Flavor to taste
3. Bake
