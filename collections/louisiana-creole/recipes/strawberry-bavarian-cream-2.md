## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Fresh, Stemmed Strawberries
- 1/2 Box of Gelatine, or 1/2 Pint
- 1 Pint of Cream
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Water

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine, and prepare the whipped cream
2. Mash the strawberries and press them through a sieve
3. Add the white powdered sugar
4. Melt the gelatine by standing over boiling water, and then add it to the strawberry juice
5. Mix thoroughly
6. Turn it into a tin pan or mold, set the basin on a pan of cracked ice, stir constantly till it begins to thicken, and then add the whipped cream, stirring it gradually till all is thoroughly mixed
7. Pour into a mold and stand in an ice-cold place to harden
