## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Mashed Potatoes
- 4 Apples
- 4 Onions
- 1/2 Teaspoonful Powdered Sage
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Thyme
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Water

## Directions

1. Place the apples and onions and herbs in a saucepan and add water sufficient to cover nicely
2. Let all cook together till soft
3. Mash well and rub through a sieve
4. Add the cup of mashed potatoes and mix well, seasoning with salt and pepper
5. Stuff the body and craw, sew up and truss the goose, and bake according to recipe
