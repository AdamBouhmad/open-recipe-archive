## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Dandelion Greens
- A Ham Shank
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Cut off the coarse roots; wash the leaves thoroughly.
2. Steep in salt and water for five hours to remove the bitterness.
3. Boil a ham shank for two hours, throw in the dandelions, and cook gently for forty-five minutes.
4. Drain and chop fine; season with butter, pepper and salt.
5. Mince the ham very fine and sprinkle over the greens.
6. Spread over sliced hard-boiled eggs and serve hot.
