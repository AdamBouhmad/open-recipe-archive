## Ingredients

- 3 Pounds of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Continue boiling till the sugar reaches a light brown color
2. Take the vessel off immediately, or it will burn
3. On taking the vessel from the fire, insert the bottom in cold water to prevent the sugar from burning
