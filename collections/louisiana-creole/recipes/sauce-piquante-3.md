## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint of vinegar
- pepper
- thyme
- 5 tablespoonfuls of broth or clear gravy
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix in a stewpan 1/2 pint of vinegar, a very little pepper and thyme
2. Boil away to half the quantity
3. Add 5 tablespoonfuls of broth or clear gravy
4. Let that again boil away to half and add a little salt
