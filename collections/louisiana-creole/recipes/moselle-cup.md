## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Moselle Wine
- 1 Sliced Lemon
- 1 Sliced Orange
- A Dash of Good Brandy
- A Dash of Maraschino
- 1/2 Pint of Seltzer Water
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1/2 of a Grated Pineapple
- 1 Large Piece of Ice
- 3/4 Tablespoonful of Sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and water, add the wine and the liqueurs, and the juice of the lemon and sliced orange and lemon, and the grated pineapple
2. Stir well
3. Let cool, and serve in small thin tumblers
4. Decorate the top with sprigs of fresh green mint
