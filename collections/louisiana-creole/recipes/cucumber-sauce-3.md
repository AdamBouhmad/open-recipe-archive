## Ingredients

- 1 Nice, Tender Cucumber
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Prepared Mustard
- The Yolk of One Egg
- Seasoning to Taste
- Juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Peel and grate the cucumber, and add the mustard, mixing thoroughly.
2. Add the juice of one lemon and the yolk of one egg, beaten thoroughly.
3. This is a delicious salad dressing.
