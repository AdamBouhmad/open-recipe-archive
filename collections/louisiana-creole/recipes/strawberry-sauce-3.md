## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Cream
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1/4 Cup of Butter
- 1 Cup of fresh Mashed Strawberries

## Directions

1. Mash the strawberries, and press them through a sieve
2. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream; whip the cream and beat in the strawberries
3. Then add the berry juice and mix thoroughly
4. Add two tablespoonfuls of sherry, if desired, or brandy, and serve with strawberry pudding, strawberry shortcake, or strawberry pie
5. It may also be served cold with a plain bread pudding
