## Ingredients

- 6 Parsnips
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Cup of Water
- 2 Eggs
- % Pound of Flour
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Teaspoonful of Pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the parsnips until very tender.
2. Cut into long, narrow strips.
3. Make a batter by mixing the flour with the yolks of the eggs, beaten well.
4. Add the salt and pepper, and gradually one cup of water, till it is absorbed.
5. Add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
6. Mix thoroughly.
7. Add the parsnips to the batter.
8. Dip out one at a time, in a spoon of butter, and fry in boiling lard.
9. Serve as an entree at dinner.
