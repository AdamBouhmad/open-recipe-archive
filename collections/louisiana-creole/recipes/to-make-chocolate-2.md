## Ingredients

- best chocolate
- half a pint of milk or milk and water for each square or large spoonful of ground chocolate

## Directions

1. Scrape the best chocolate
2. Allow for each square or large spoonful of ground chocolate half a pint of milk or milk and water
3. Let it boil a few moments
4. Put it on the back part of the stove until wanted
