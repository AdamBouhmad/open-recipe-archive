## Ingredients

- fish
- butter
- onions
- white wine
- cullis
- bread crumbs
- stock
- lemon
- mushrooms
- parsley
- Worcestershire or Sultana sauce
- sugar
- cayenne pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut off the fins, and make an incision in the back
2. butter a saucepan, and put into it a teaspoonful of finely chopped onions and a wineglassful of white wine
3. place the fish in the pan, pouring over it 6 tablespoonfuls of cullis
4. sprinkle fine bread crumbs over it, and stick a few small pieces of butter about it
5. put it in a moderate oven for 20 minutes or 1/2 hour
6. carefully lift it into a silver or plated dish
7. Put into the saucepan 4 tablespoonfuls of stock
8. let it boil 5 minutes, stirring all the time
9. add the juice of a lemon, a teaspoonful of chopped mushrooms, 1 of minced parsley, and 1 of Worcestershire or Sultana sauce, a little sugar, cayenne pepper, and salt
10. beat all together, and pour over the fish
11. around which put some whole mushrooms and sliced lemon
12. Put it into the oven for 1/2 of an hour
13. pass the salamander over it and serve very hot
