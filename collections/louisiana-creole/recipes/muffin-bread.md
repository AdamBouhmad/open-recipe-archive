## Ingredients

- 3 pints of flour
- a half pound of butter
- 5 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of good yeast

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks of eggs separately
2. Work the butter well in the flour
3. Put in the eggs
4. Beat all very light
5. Add 3 tablespoonfuls of good yeast
6. Bake it in a pan with oven rather quick at the bottom and clear at the top until it begins to bake
7. Then quicken the fire by degrees
