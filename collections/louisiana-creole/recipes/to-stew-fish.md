## Ingredients

- fish
- vinegar
- butter
- flour
- 1 pound of good butter
- 2 blades of mace
- 10 cloves
- cinnamon
- red pepper
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Clean the fish well
2. put it in a dish and pour vinegar over it; this will take off the slime, and the scales come off easily
3. put some butter into a frying pan and make it hot
4. put the fish into the pan and let it remain over the fire for 6 minutes more
5. take it out and put it into the kettle in which it is to be stewed
6. sprinkle some flour into the pan
7. put it over the fire for 3 minutes and pour it over the fish
8. take 1 pound of good butter, and roll it well in flour, and put it to the fish
9. add 2 blades of mace, 10 cloves, a little cinnamon, red pepper, and salt, with water sufficient to keep it from burning
10. put it over a slow fire to stew
11. when half done, add a little red wine
