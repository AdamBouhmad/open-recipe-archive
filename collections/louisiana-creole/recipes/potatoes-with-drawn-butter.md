## Ingredients

- 6 Potatoes
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Chopped Parsley
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes according to the boiled potatoes recipe.
2. Peel and pour over them a tablespoonful of melted butter, in which you have mingled chopped parsley.
3. Salt and pepper to taste.
