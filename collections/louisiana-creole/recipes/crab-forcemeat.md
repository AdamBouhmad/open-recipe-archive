## Ingredients

- The Meat of 12 Crabs
- 1 Onion
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Teaspoonful of White Pepper
- A Dash of Cayenne
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 12 Mushrooms, if desired
- The Yolks of 3 Eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the onion very fine and fry in one tablespoonful of butter until a golden brown
2. Add a tablespoonful of flour and moisten with a quarter of a pint of water, or oyster juice, till the sauce begins to thicken well
3. Season with the salt and pepper and a dash of Cayenne
4. Add the clove of garlic, finely minced, and the herbs
5. Add the crab meat, finely minced, and the mushrooms, if desired
6. Cook for a half hour in the saucepan, and then take off the fire and add the yolks of the eggs
7. Stir again for a moment, cool and roll into balls and use as desired
