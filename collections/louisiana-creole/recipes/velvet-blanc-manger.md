## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Cream
- 3/4 Cup of Powdered Sugar
- 3/4 Cup of Gelatine, Dissolved in Water (hot)
- 1 Glass of White Wine
- 1 Teaspoonful of Almond Extract

## Directions

1. Boil the cream, sugar and gelatine together, stirring until smooth.
2. Add a teaspoonful of Almond Extract.
3. Mix well, and pour into a mold, and set to cool and harden.
