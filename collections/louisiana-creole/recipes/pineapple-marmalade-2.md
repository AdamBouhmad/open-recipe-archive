## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Pineapple
- ¾ of a Pound of Sugar

## Directions

1. Pare the pineapple and remove the eyes.
2. Grate carefully, preserving the juice.
3. Allow three-fourths of a pound of sugar to every pound of the grated fruit.
4. Proceed as directed above.
