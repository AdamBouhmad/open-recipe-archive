## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- green onion, chopped
- parsley, fine-cut
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks of six eggs separately
2. put in a tablespoonful of butter, a spoonful of chopped green onion and one of fine-cut parsley
3. mix with the eggs
4. put it into a thick-bottomed pan, in which you have placed a half cup of butter
5. Roll it up as it cooks
6. tilt the pan on one side, that the omelet may cook on the other side
7. roll up again as it cooks
8. Do not let it get hard and brown, but keep it soft
9. Keep on rolling as well as you can
10. When the eggs cook, butter, pepper and salt them
11. turn on a dish
