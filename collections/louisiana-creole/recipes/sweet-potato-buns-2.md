## Ingredients

- 2 nice potatoes
- flour
- nutmeg
- sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of good yeast
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Boil and mash 2 nice potatoes
2. Rub in as much flour as will make it like bread
3. Add nutmeg and sugar to your taste, with 1 tablespoonful of good yeast
4. When it has risen, work in 2 tablespoonfuls of butter, cut finely
5. Form it into small rolls, and bake on tins a nice brown
6. Serve hot; break open and butter
