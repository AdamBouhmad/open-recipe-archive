## Ingredients

- 8 or 9 good sized apples
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 1 quart of milk
- 4 whites of eggs
- 3 large spoonfuls of pulverized sugar
- colored nonpareils

## Directions

1. Select 8 or 9 good sized apples
2. Core them
3. Put them into a pan and cover them with cold water
4. Whilst they are cooking slowly, make a nice boiled custard of the yolks of 4 eggs and 1 quart of milk
5. Set it aside to cool
6. When the apples are quite soft, lay them carefully on a sieve and remove the skin
7. Put the pulp into a bowl and whip to a stiff froth 4 whites of eggs and 3 large spoonfuls of pulverized sugar
8. Beat the apple pulp to a stiff froth and add to it the whipped eggs
9. Beat them until they resemble snow
10. Pour the cold custard into a glass bowl and heap on the whipped snow
11. Finish by sprinkling on colored nonpareils
