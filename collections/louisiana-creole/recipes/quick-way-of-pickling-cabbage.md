## Ingredients

- 2 nice white cabbages
- salt
- water
- weak vinegar
- turmeric
- good cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Quarter the cabbages; if very large more than quarter them
2. Put them in salt and water to remain 24 hours
3. Boil them till tender in weak vinegar with a little turmeric in it
4. Put them in jars and pour over them good cold vinegar
5. Tie them up
