## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Sugar
- A Pinch of Cream of Tartar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla, Peach or Strawberry Extract

## Directions

1. Take two cups of sugar and a small pinch of Cream of Tartar
2. Stir with just enough water to make a thick paste
3. Flavor with Peach or Strawberry Extract
4. If Strawberry is used, add a teaspoonful of Cochineal
5. Beat well, and if not stiff enough, add more sugar
6. Proceed as in Plain Icing
