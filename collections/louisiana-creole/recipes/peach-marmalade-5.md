## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Peaches
- ¾ of a Pound of Sugar

## Directions

1. Allow three-fourths of a pound of sugar for every pound of fruit.
2. Take ripe fruit; pare and cut into small pieces.
3. Place in the preserving kettle, with a layer of fruit and a layer of sugar, and so alternate till all is in.
4. Cook over the fire as directed.
5. Watch carefully that they do not burn.
6. When they begin to form a jelly take off the fire and put away in stone jars or glasses.
