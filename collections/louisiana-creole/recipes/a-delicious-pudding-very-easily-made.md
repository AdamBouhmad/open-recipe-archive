## Ingredients

- butter
- thin slices of rolls
- currants
- citron cut up fine
- a quart of milk
- four eggs
- half a pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Butter some thin slices of rolls.
2. Lay them in a pudding-dish with currants and citron cut up fine, and strewed between the slices.
3. Pour over the rolls a custard made of a quart of milk, four eggs and half a pound of sugar.
4. Flavor this and bake lightly.
