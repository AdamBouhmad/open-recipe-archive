## Ingredients

- 1 Pineapple
- 3 Teaspoonfuls of Vanilla
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Jamaica Rum
- 1 Quart of Cream
- 1 Pound of Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the pineapple, and cut one-half into slices
2. Lay them in a bowl and sprinkle with powdered sugar and one-third of the rum
3. Cover and set aside till needed
4. Grate the remainder of the pineapple into an earthen vessel, and add sugar sufficient to sweeten to taste, and pour the vanilla and the rest of the rum over it
5. Mix carefully with the cream, which should be at least three times the weight of the pineapple
6. Turn all into an ice cream mold and proceed to freeze as directed
7. Serve with slices of pineapple and its syrup around the frozen composition, which should be turned out of the mold according to directions given for making and freezing cream
