## Ingredients

- 6 Pigs Feet
- 2 Bay Leaves
- 3 Blades of Mace
- 1 Dozen whole cloves
- 1 Whole red pepper pod
- 1 Pint of good cider vinegar
- Salt, pepper and cayenne to taste

## Directions

1. Select young and tender pigs' feet.
2. Clean and scrape well and soak in cold water several hours.
3. Split and crack the feet in several places.
4. Put them in a stewpot and cover with cold water.
5. Let them simmer until tender.
6. When done, lay in a crock.
7. Boil the vinegar, mace, cloves, bay leaves and pepper pod together a few minutes.
8. Season the feet with salt and pepper, and pour the spiced vinegar over while boiling hot.
9. Cover the crock and set to cool.
10. The feet will be ready for use in twenty-four hours.
