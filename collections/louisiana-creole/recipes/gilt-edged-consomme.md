## Ingredients

- 1 fine chicken
- 1 good soup bone
- 1 slice of fine ham
- 1 gallon of water
- 2 eggs, whites and shells
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- 1/2 small parsnip
- 1/2 carrot
- 1/2 head of celery
- 1 onion
- 3 cloves
- salt and pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Have the fowl thoroughly cleaned, and put the chicken, beef and ham into a kettle of cold water of the quantity mentioned, and boil slowly for five hours, being careful to keep the pot well covered.
2. Chop the onion and vegetables and fry them in a little butter, and add all the seasonings to the soup.
3. Boil two hours longer, and set away overnight in an ice box.
4. The next day remove all the fat; from the top take out the jelly, leaving the thickest part of the sediment.
5. Mix in the shells and the whites of eggs and boil quickly for about ten minutes.
6. Set it on the hearth to settle.
7. Pour the soup through a thin bag without squeezing; if it does not come out perfectly clear, pass it through again.
8. It should be a beautiful golden-brown color.
