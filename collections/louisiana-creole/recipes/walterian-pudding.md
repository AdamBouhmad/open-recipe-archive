## Ingredients

- ½ lb. of pippins
- ½ lb. of sugar
- ½ lb. of butter
- rind of a fresh lemon
- 5 eggs

## Directions

1. Grate ½ lb. of pippins
2. Stir to a cream ½ lb. of sugar and ½ lb. of butter, and add the apples
3. Grate the rind of a fresh lemon
4. Whip very light 5 eggs, and beat all well together
5. Bake it in puff paste ½ hour
