## Ingredients

- 1 pint of stewed gooseberries
- 1 lb. of white sugar
- 2 lbs. of fresh butter
- 2 lbs. of grated bread
- 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Stew gooseberries with all the juice made very sweet
2. Add 1 lb. of white sugar, 2 lbs. of fresh butter, 2 lbs. of grated bread, 3 eggs
