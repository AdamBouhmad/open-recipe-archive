## Ingredients

- 3 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 1 Raw Yolk of Egg
- 1 Tablespoonful of Tarragon Vinegar
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Olive Oil
- 1/8 Clove of Garlic, Minced Very Fine
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Prepared Mustard
- Salt and Cayenne to Taste
- The Juice of Half a Lemon

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs till hard
2. Remove the shells and set aside the whites, which you will have crumbled fine for a garnish
3. Put the yolks into a bowl and mash very fine, till perfectly smooth
4. Add the mustard and mix well, and the seasonings of vinegar and salt and Cayenne to taste
5. Add the olive oil, drop by drop, working in the egg all the time, and then add the yolk of the raw egg
6. Work in thoroughly, till light
7. Add the juice of half a lemon
8. Mix well, increasing the quantities of oil or vinegar, according to taste, very slightly
9. Serve with cold meats, fish or salads
