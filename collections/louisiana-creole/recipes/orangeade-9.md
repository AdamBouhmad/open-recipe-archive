## Ingredients

- 3 oranges
- juice of 1 lemon
- 4 ounces of sugar
- 1 1/2 pints of water

## Directions

1. Use ripe, juicy Louisiana oranges
2. Peel one orange and cut it in thin slices lengthwise, and then put it into a vessel with four ounces of sugar and a pint and a half of water
3. Squeeze the juice of the lemon and two more oranges into another vessel, and stir well
4. Pour it on the sliced orange
5. Let it stand for a few minutes, and it is ready to use
6. Strain if you desire
