## Ingredients

- three-fourths of a pound of butter
- one pound of white sugar
- beaten whites of seventeen eggs
- one pound of sifted flour
- lemon or rose flavoring

## Directions

1. Wash and cream three-fourths of a pound of butter
2. Add one pound of white sugar
3. Beat them well together
4. Add the beaten whites of seventeen eggs alternately with a pound of sifted flour
5. Flavor with lemon or rose
6. Bake
