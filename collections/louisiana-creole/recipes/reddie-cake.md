## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of sifted flour
- 1 lb. of powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of powdered cinnamon
- 3 well beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the flour
2. Mix in the sugar and cinnamon
3. Add the eggs and mix all into a dough
4. Roll it out
5. Cut it into round cakes
6. Bake in a quick oven
