## Ingredients

- sponge cake or lady fingers
- whipped cream, custard or blancmange
- the whites of three eggs
- a quarter of a pound of sugar
- half a pint of cream

## Directions

1. beat the whites of three eggs to a high froth, with a quarter of a pound of sugar, and half a pint of cream, until it is quite thick and high
2. flavor this to your taste with lemon or vanilla
3. pour it into a cake-lined mould
4. place some of the sliced cake or lady fingers on top of the mould and over the cream
5. set it on ice
6. when wanted turn it on a dish and serve
