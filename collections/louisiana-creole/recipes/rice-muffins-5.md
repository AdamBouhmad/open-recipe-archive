## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Boiled Rice
- 1 Cup of Flour
- 3 Eggs
- 1 1/2 Pints of Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard or Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Mash the rice through a close sieve and beat the yolks very light, and add.
2. Add the milk and butter, and then sift in the flour and half a teaspoonful of baking powder.
3. Add the sugar and the whites of the eggs.
