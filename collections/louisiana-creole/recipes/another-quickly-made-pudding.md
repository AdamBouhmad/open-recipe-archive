## Ingredients

- a light, square loaf of bread
- raisins or currants
- rich sauce

## Directions

1. Get a light, square loaf of bread, split it in three or four horizontal slices
2. Strew in between the slices cut-up raisins or currants
3. Tie it up again
4. Boil half an hour
5. Serve it with a rich sauce
