## Ingredients

- six eggs
- a cup of chopped ham
- two small onions
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Beat up six eggs until quite light
2. add to them a cup of chopped ham and two small onions minced very fine
3. The onions should be cooked a little before being put into the eggs, or they will not be cooked enough
4. When mixed together put it into a thick-bottomed pan and commence rolling
5. When it is a light brown, give it the last roll
6. let it lie a moment in the pan, then dish it
7. Put fresh butter as it goes to table
