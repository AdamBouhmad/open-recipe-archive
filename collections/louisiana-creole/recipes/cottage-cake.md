## Ingredients

- 3 eggs, separately beaten
- 1 common sized teacup of brown sugar
- 2 teacups of molasses
- 1 teacup of beaten ginger
- 2 teacups of butter
- 1 teacup of cream or milk with a small piece of soda dissolved in it
- 3 cups of flour
- orange or lemon peel for flavoring

## Directions

1. Make it the consistency of pound cake
2. Flavor with orange or lemon peel
3. Bake it either in a large size or small tins
