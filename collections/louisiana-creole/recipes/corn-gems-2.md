## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Cornmeal
- 1 Cup of Flour
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Sweet Milk
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Baking Powder
- Salt

## Directions

1. Boil one-half of the milk and pour it over the cornmeal
2. Add the butter instantly and stir well
3. When thoroughly mixed add the cold milk and the well-beaten yolks of the eggs
4. Add the salt and then the flour, into which you will have sifted the baking powder
5. Mix thoroughly and then add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
6. Pour into greased or buttered gem pans, filling only half full
7. Bake about a half hour in a hot oven
