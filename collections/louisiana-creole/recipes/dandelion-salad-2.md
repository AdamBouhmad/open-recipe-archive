## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Fresh White Dandelion
- A Plain French Dressing

## Directions

1. Cut off the roots and green portion of the leaves; wash and steep in salt and water.
2. When they become crisp, drain and press dry.
3. Rub the salad bowl with a clove of garlic and season the dandelions with French dressing.
4. This salad may also be served with two hard-boiled eggs cut in quarters or sliced and laid over; or with two medium-sized beets, sliced, and seasoned with a plain French dressing.
