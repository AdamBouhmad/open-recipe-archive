## Ingredients

- a quart of flour
- half a pint of milk
- a spoonful of salt
- two well-beaten eggs
- a half cup of yeast
- two tablespoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Sift at night a quart of flour
2. add half a pint of milk, a spoonful of salt, two well-beaten eggs, and a half cup of yeast
3. Work it well, cover it, and set it in a warm place to rise
4. Next morning work in two tablespoonfuls of butter
5. mould the dough into rolls
6. Rub over each roll a little butter
7. bake
