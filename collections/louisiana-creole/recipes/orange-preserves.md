## Ingredients

- oranges
- fine white powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges
2. Slice nicely and weigh
3. Allow equal quantities of fine white powdered sugar
4. Place the fruit in a deep dish, and sprinkle the sugar over it, between the slices, in alternate layers
5. Let it remain over night
6. Drain off the syrup in the morning, and put it in a kettle
7. When it gets very hot put in the fruit
8. Let it cook slowly till the slices look very clear
9. Skim them out and put them in the jars
10. Fill to overflowing and then seal
