## Ingredients

- One large spoonful of hop yeast, or a yeast cake
- a pint of water
- two Irish potatoes
- flour
- a spoonful of lard

## Directions

1. put this to a pint of water, mash to this two Irish potatoes, and stir all together
2. Sift in flour until you have a stiff batter, and set it to rise
3. When it is very light, stir in a spoonful of lard, and enough flour to enable you to mould it into a loaf
4. When moulded, grease the top, and set it to rise again
5. If really light, you can now bake it; but if not, work it down again, and mould it over and let it rise again
