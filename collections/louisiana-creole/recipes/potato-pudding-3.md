## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lbs. of well mashed potatoes
- 1 lb. of butter
- 6 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1 glass of brandy
- nutmeg
- mace
- lemon

## Directions

1. Take 1 1/2 lbs. of well mashed potatoes
2. while they are warm, put in 1 lb. of butter
3. beat 6 eggs with 1/2 lb. of sugar rolled fine
4. mix all well together
5. put in 1 glass of brandy, with nutmeg, mace, or lemon
6. bake it in a nice paste
