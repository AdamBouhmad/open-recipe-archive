## Ingredients

- eggs
- sugar
- milk
- a quart of ripe fruit

## Directions

1. Make a quart of rich custard, with eggs, and sugar and milk
2. When cold, pour it on a quart of ripe fruit
3. Mash and pass it through a sieve
4. Add more sugar if required by the fruit
5. Freeze it
