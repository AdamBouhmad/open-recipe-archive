## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Tomatoes
- 1/2 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme and Bay Leaf
- 1 Pint of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- Salt and Pepper and Cayenne to Taste
- Sherry to Taste
- Chopped Parsley for Garnish

## Directions

1. Take one dozen large tomatoes, or one can, and put in a pot to boil, with one-half tablespoonful of butter
2. Add salt and pepper to taste, and one pint of water
3. Let it cook for about ten minutes and add minced thyme, parsley and bay leaf, very fine, and two cloves of garlic, minced fine
4. Let it boil, and, when well boiled, take from the fire and mash through a sieve, reducing to a pulp
5. Take a tablespoonful of flour and put in a saucepan, and add a half spoon of flour
6. When it blends and browns nicely, add the tomato juice, season nicely to taste
7. When ready to serve, add chopped parsley as a garnish
8. Serve with meat, fish or game
