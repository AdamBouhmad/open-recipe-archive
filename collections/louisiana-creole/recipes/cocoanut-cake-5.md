## Ingredients

- whites of 10 eggs
- 2 coconuts, grated
- 1 lb. of white sugar
- 1/2 lb. of sifted flour
- rose water

## Directions

1. Whip the whites of the eggs
2. Grate the nuts and add them
3. Add the sugar and flour
4. Stir this well and flavor with rose water
5. Pour into pans
6. Bake for 1/2 of an hour
