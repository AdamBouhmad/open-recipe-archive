## Ingredients

- 6 potatoes
- 4 tablespoonfuls butter or lard
- 1 pint milk
- 1 cake of compressed yeast
- 1 teaspoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful white sugar
- Sufficient flour to make a batter

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes, pare and mash them very fine through a sieve
2. Scald the milk and add the salt and sugar
3. Beat till perfectly smooth
4. Add these to the potatoes and mix well
5. Add about half a cup of flour, well-sifted several times, so that it will be very light
6. When the mixture grows tepid, add the yeast, which you will have dissolved in warm water, and beat well till you have a good batter
7. Cover the mixture with a thick cloth, and put to rise in a warm place
8. After three or four hours, when it is well-risen, add flour sufficient to make a nice, soft dough
9. Knead very lightly and quickly for about ten or fifteen minutes
10. Take up and knead lightly several times, and roll the dough out
11. Cut into little biscuits, place them in a slightly-buttered or greased pan, about two inches apart
12. Let them stand for half an hour in a warm place
13. Bake in a quick oven for twenty minutes
