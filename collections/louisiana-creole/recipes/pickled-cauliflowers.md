## Ingredients

- large, ripe, fullblown cauliflowers
- salt
- mace
- nutmeg
- best cider vinegar
- sweet oil

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves and stalks, and divide the blossom into pieces or clusters of equal size
2. Throw them into a porcelain kettle of boiling water (adding a little salt)
3. Let them simmer, and skim them well
4. When they come to a boil, take them up with a perforated skimmer, and lay them on a sieve to drain
5. Put them into stone jars (3 parts full)
6. Season with mace and nutmeg infused in sufficient of the best cider vinegar, and simmer it for 1 hour
7. When it comes to boil, take it off the fire, and pour it hot over the cauliflower in the jar, filling it quite up to the top, and adding sweet oil at the last
8. Cover it while warm, and tie leather over the top
