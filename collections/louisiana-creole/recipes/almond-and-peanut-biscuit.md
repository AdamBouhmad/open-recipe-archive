## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Sweet Almonds
- 4 Ounces of Pistachio Kernels or Peanuts
- 4 Eggs
- 8 Ounces of Powdered Sugar
- Four tablespoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Blanch, peel and pound the almonds and pistachio kernels or peanuts in a mortar till finely pulverized, adding the white of an egg, to keep the almonds from turning oily.
2. Beat the whites of the eggs to a froth, and then add the yolks, beaten separately to a cream with the sugar.
3. Beat well, and incorporate thoroughly.
4. Add four tablespoonfuls of flour, sifted well, and mix well.
5. Place in small cakes on wafer sheets of paper, and bake in a moderate oven till a very light color.
