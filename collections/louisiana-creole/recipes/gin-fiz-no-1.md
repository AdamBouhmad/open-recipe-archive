## Ingredients

- one-half tablespoonful of sugar
- a little lemon juice
- two wineglassfuls of seltzwater
- one wineglassful Tom or Holland gin
- teaspoonful of white of an egg
- ice

## Directions

1. Shake well and strain into fancy glass
