## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Malaga Grapes
- 3/4 Pound of Sugar
- 1/4 Cup of Water

## Directions

1. Wash the grapes, then cut a slit in the grape on one side, and take out the seeds.
2. Put the fruit in cold water, and let it boil.
3. Take off the fire, and let it stand uncovered for a time, and then put in cold water.
4. When cold lift them out with a skimming spoon.
5. Put the grapes in clarified sugar, which you will have boiled almost to a thread, and let them boil up once.
6. Skim them out, and put in a dish.
7. Boil the sugar to half the quantity in syrup, and pour over, and serve.
