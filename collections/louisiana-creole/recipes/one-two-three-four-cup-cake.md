## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Butter
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 4 Eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream.
2. Add the sugar.
3. Beat till very light.
4. Beat the eggs (yolks and whites together) till very light, and add them gradually to the butter and sugar, beating all the while.
5. Beat vigorously.
6. Add the sifted flour, and beat thoroughly until very light.
7. Line a round cake pan with the buttered paper, and fill with the mixture.
8. Set in a moderate oven, and bake for one hour and a quarter.
