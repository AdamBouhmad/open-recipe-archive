## Ingredients

- 2 Ounces of Chocolate
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- The Whites of 3 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla Extract

## Directions

1. Melt two ounces of chocolate in a little boiling water
2. Add two cups sugar, and stir till smooth
3. Stir in the well-beaten whites of three eggs and one teaspoonful of Vanilla Extract
4. Proceed as in Plain Icing
