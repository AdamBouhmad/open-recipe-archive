## Ingredients

- eggs
- butter
- grated cheese
- sweet milk
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs hard
2. peel and cut them in slices
3. lay them in a deep dish in close circular rows
4. Make a sauce of a tablespoonful of butter, the yolks of four eggs, a little grated cheese and half a cup of sweet milk
5. Stir this over the fire until it thickens
6. pour it over the eggs
7. strew some bread crumbs on the top
8. bake for about ten minutes
9. send to table hot
