## Ingredients

- 12 Pints of Apricot Syrup
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup
- 1 Ounce of Essence of Apricots

## Directions

1. Pare and stone the apricots, then cut them into pieces, pound and mash well and set them aside for thirty-six hours in a cool place.
2. Then strain through a bag, pressing out all the juice.
3. Cook the sugar and water till it forms the syrup called "Le Petit Casse."
4. Then add the apricot juice and mix thoroughly; let it boil for five minutes, take off the fire, let it get almost cold, add the essence, and then bottle for use.
