## Ingredients

- half a pint of cranberries
- half a pound of sugar
- a spoonful of water
- a half dozen apples
- paste

## Directions

1. Mix half a pint of cranberries with half a pound of sugar and a spoonful of water
2. Let them simmer a little until soft
3. Peel and cut thin a half dozen apples
4. Put a rim of paste around a pie plate
5. Strew in the apples
6. Pour the cranberries over the apples and cover with a nice crust
7. Bake for an hour to cook the apples
