## Ingredients

- trimmings of venison, 1/2 lb. of meat
- 1/2 pint of water
- 1 tablespoonful of whole cloves
- 1 tumbler of currant jelly
- 1 tumbler of claret
- 1/2 lb. of butter and flour rubbed together
- 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon or mace
- a little black pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. The trimmings of the venison (1/2 lb. of meat) to 1/2 pint of water; boil, with a tablespoonful of whole cloves, down to 1 pint
2. strain, and add 1 tumbler of currant jelly, ditto of claret, 1/2 lb. of butter and flour rubbed together, 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon, or mace, a little black pepper, and salt, if your taste requires
