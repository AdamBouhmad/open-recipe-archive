## Ingredients

- trimmings of venison (½ lb. of meat)
- ½ pint of water
- 1 tablespoonful of whole cloves
- 1 tumbler of currant jelly
- 1 tumbler of claret
- ½ lb. of butter and flour rubbed together
- 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon or mace
- black pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil the venison trimmings with ½ pint of water and a tablespoonful of whole cloves down to 1 pint
2. Strain the mixture
3. Add 1 tumbler of currant jelly, 1 tumbler of claret, ½ lb. of butter and flour rubbed together, 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon or mace, a little black pepper, and salt if your taste requires
