## Ingredients

- 2 Breasts of Lamb
- 1 Tablespoonful of Salt
- 1 Teaspoonful of Pepper
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Butter or Olive Oil
- Grated Bread Crumbs

## Directions

1. Take two breasts of Lamb; tie them and put them to boil in soup stock for forty-five minutes
2. Then drain well and extract all the bones
3. Press them down with a heavy weight on top
4. When thoroughly cold, cut each breast into three triangular-shaped pieces
5. Dip them in olive oil, or melted lard, or butter, and season with the salt and pepper
6. Roll each piece in fresh bread crumbs grated
7. Broil on a slow fire, allowing four minutes to each side
8. Serve with a pint of hot Macedoine or any garnish that may be desired, arranging the breast over the garnish
