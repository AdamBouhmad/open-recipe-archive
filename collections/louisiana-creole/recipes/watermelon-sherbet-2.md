## Ingredients

- 1 Ripe Watermelon
- 1/2 Pound of Pulverized Sugar
- 6 Teaspoonfuls of Sherry Wine

## Directions

1. Split a ripe watermelon in half.
2. Scoop out the center, rejecting the seeds.
3. Put the scooped fruit in a bowl.
4. Take a silver fork and pick it in small pieces.
5. Mix with a half pound of pulverized sugar.
6. Turn the mixture into a freezer and turn slowly for fifteen minutes.
7. Serve in glasses, adding a teaspoonful of Sherry to each glass.
