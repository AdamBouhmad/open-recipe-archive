## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of flour
- 1 pint of new milk
- 1 lb. of butter
- 8 eggs
- 1 1/2 grated nutmegs
- 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
- 1 1/2 wineglassful of yeast
- 1 lb. of white sugar

## Directions

1. Sift into a pan 2 lbs. of flour
2. Warm 1 pint of new milk, 1 lb. of butter
3. Stir this, if not too warm, into the flour
4. Beat 8 eggs very light, separating the yolks from the whites
5. Add 1 1/2 grated nutmegs, 1 teaspoonful of cinnamon
6. Beat these well together, adding the whites of the eggs lastly, 1 1/2 wineglassful of yeast
7. Set it to rise
8. When risen, stir in 1 lb. of white sugar, to be sifted in and mixed through
9. Set to rise in the pans in which they are to be baked
