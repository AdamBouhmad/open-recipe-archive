## Ingredients

- Five cups of flour
- 2 cups of sugar
- 1 1/2 cup of molasses
- 1 cup butter
- 1 teaspoonful of soda in 1/2 cup of sour milk
- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of ginger

## Directions

1. Mix 5 cups of flour, 2 cups of sugar, 1 1/2 cup of molasses, 1 cup butter, 1 teaspoonful of soda in 1/2 cup of sour milk, 3 eggs, and 1 tablespoonful of ginger
2. To be eaten with sauce; but just as good cold, without the sauce
