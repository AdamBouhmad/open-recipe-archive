## Ingredients

- juice of 4 oranges
- rinds of 2 oranges grated
- yolks of 8 eggs
- 1/2 pint cream

## Directions

1. Take the juice of 4 oranges, and the rinds of 2, grated, the yolks of 8 eggs
2. Mix it well together and stir it over a chafing dish of coals
3. Add 1/2 a pint of cream, and stir it all one way
