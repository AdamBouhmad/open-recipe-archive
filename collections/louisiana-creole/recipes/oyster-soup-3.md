## Ingredients

- 100 oysters
- 2 quarts of water
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 pint of rich milk or cream
- mace
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Strain the oysters through a colander and set the liquor to boil
2. When the scum has all risen and been taken off, add two quarts of water, a tablespoonful of butter, one pint of rich milk or cream, mace, nutmeg, pepper and salt to taste
3. Boil these ingredients together
4. Just before serving up, throw in the oysters
5. If the soup be too thin, stir in a little wheat flour a few minutes before serving up
