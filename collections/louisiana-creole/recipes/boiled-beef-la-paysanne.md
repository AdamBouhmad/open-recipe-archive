## Ingredients

- The Bouilli
- 5 Large Onions
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Flour
- 1 Wineglassful of Claret
- Mustard and Vinegar

## Directions

1. Hash the left-over beef.
2. Chop five large onions very fine and cook them to a golden brown in butter.
3. When nearly done, dust over them a teaspoonful of flour and moisten with a little red wine.
4. Cook the onions till done and then put in the cold hashed beef, adding a dash of French vinegar and a little mustard and serve.
