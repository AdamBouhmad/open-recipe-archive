## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 oranges
- gelatine
- whites of 2 or 3 eggs

## Directions

1. Strain the juice of 6 or 8 oranges, sweetened to taste
2. Dissolve 1 box of gelatine
3. Put the orange juice to it
4. Allow it to jelly slightly
5. Beat the whites of 2 or 3 eggs to a froth
6. Mix all well together
7. Fill moulds, and set on ice
