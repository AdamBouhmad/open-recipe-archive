## Ingredients

- 10 Ounces of Pistachio Kernels or Peanuts
- 1½ Pounds of Fine Sugar
- Whites of 3 Eggs
- 4 Drops Essence of Roses
- 2 Whites of Eggs of Royal Icing

## Directions

1. Scald, skin, wash and dry the pistachios or peanuts.
2. Pound them with two ounces of sugar, adding occasionally some of the whites of the eggs, until reduced to a pulp.
3. Then add all the sugar, gradually working it into the paste on a marble slab, being careful to have the slab and the hands very clean.
4. Roll out paste with sugar to the thickness of a quarter of an inch on the slab.
5. Spread the icing evenly over, and cut into various fanciful shapes.
6. Place the cakes on the sheet wafer or lay on baking sheet, and bake in a slow heat to a very light color.
