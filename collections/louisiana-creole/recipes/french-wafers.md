## Ingredients

- 8 Ounces of Flour
- 8 Ounces of Sifted Sugar
- Whites of 4 Eggs
- 1/2 Pint of Cream
- 1 Wineglassful of Brandy
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of the eggs to a cream and add to the flour, milk, salt and sugar
2. Work all together very light in a bowl, until you have a very smooth batter
3. Add the brandy and beat vigorously ten minutes longer
4. Have ready the wafer irons and a clear charcoal fire
5. Brush the irons with butter and place the wafers within and bake on both sides to a light brown color
6. If desired, the wafers may be rolled into fancy shapes
