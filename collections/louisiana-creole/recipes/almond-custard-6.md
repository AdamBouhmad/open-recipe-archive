## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of New Milk
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 3/4 Pound of Blanched Almonds (scalded)
- The Yolks of 4 Eggs
- Two tablespoonfuls of white powdered sugar
- The Whites of the Eggs

## Directions

1. Blanch the almonds after shelling, and chop very fine.
2. Blend the eggs and sugar, beating thoroughly, and add to the boiling milk.
3. Then add the almonds.
4. Stir well, set into cups to cool.
5. Place on top a meringue made of two tablespoonfuls of white powdered sugar and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
6. Set in the oven for a few seconds to brown, and serve cold.
