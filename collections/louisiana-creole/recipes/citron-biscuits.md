## Ingredients

- 6 Fresh Creole Eggs
- The Zest of 1 Citron
- 4 Ounces of Flour
- 12 Ounces of Powdered Sugar
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar to a cream.
2. Add the flour gradually, and the juice of the lemon and citron zest.
3. Beat well.
4. Bake on paper sheets in the form of small wafers, very thinly spread, in a moderate oven to a light color.
