## Ingredients

- A Fine Head of Green and White Cabbage
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Red Pepper Pod
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chili Pepper

## Directions

1. Select a fine white head in which the green and white are prettily mingled
2. Remove all the outside leaves and reject them
3. Cut the cabbage head into quarters and let it soak in cold water for about an hour
4. Drain well and pull off each leaf separately to discover any lurking insects
5. Throw each leaf into a pan of fresh water and drain thoroughly
6. Put into a large pot of boiling water with a pound of ham or salt pork
7. After it has been in the water ten minutes, add a teaspoonful of salt and a red pepper pod cut in two
8. Add a teaspoonful of Chili pepper
9. Cover the cabbage and boil for one hour or more until tender
