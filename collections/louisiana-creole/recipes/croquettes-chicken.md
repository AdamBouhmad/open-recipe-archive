## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- flour
- salt
- red pepper
- black pepper
- parsley
- onion
- egg
- grated bread
- lard

## Directions

1. Boil a chicken tenderly
2. When cold, chop fine
3. Put some of its liquor in pan with butter the size of a hen's egg
4. Mix in a little flour, salt, red and black pepper, parsley, and small onion
5. Add the minced chicken
6. When cold, shape the mixture
7. Dip in egg with grated bread
8. Fry in lard
