## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Figs
- 3/4 Cup of Water
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Use figs that are not quite ripe.
2. Break off the stalks, and prick them all over, and let them simmer in a syrup.
3. Then proceed in exactly the same manner as for Compote of Cherries.
