## Ingredients

- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 2 Cups of Pulverized Sugar
- ½ Cup of Cornstarch
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Milk

## Directions

1. Take the whites of two eggs, two cups of pulverized sugar, and one-half a cup of cornstarch
2. Blend the cornstarch with a little milk, to make a thick paste
3. Add the whites of the eggs, beaten light, and the pulverized sugar
4. Beat all to a stiff froth
5. Frost the cake while hot
