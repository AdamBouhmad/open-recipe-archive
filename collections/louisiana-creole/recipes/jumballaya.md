## Ingredients

- 1 lb. rice
- cold roast chicken or remnants of turkey
- slice of ham
- 1 tablespoonful lard
- 1 pint hot water

## Directions

1. Wash rice and soak it an hour
2. Cut up a cold roast chicken or the remnants of a turkey and a slice of ham, which fry in a tablespoonful of lard
3. Stir in the rice, and add slowly, while stirring in a pint of hot water
4. Cover your pot, and set where it can cook slowly
