## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Tender Quails
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Water
- 6 Truffles
- 8 Thin Strips of Bacon
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- Parsley Sprigs and Sliced Lemon to Garnish

## Directions

1. Clean the quail and butter inside and throw in a little salt and pepper.
2. Stuff with truffles, and bind the body, after rubbing, with a strip of bacon.
3. Set in the oven in a baking pan in which you have placed a tablespoonful of butter, and let it roast twenty or thirty minutes, according to size.
4. Have ready buttered toast.
5. Put the birds on the toast.
6. Add a little butter to the gravy in which they have been roasted, and a tablespoonful of water, and the juice of a lemon.
7. Let this cook for three or four minutes, strain, set on the stove for two minutes longer, and pour over the breast of the bird, so that it will soak into the bread.
8. Serve with a nice garnish of parsley and sliced lemon, and with green peas as an entree.
