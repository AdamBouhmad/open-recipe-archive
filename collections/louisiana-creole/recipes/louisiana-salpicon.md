## Ingredients

- 50 Lake Shrimp
- 1 Dozen Mushrooms
- 1 Truffle
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- 1 Glass of White Wine
- 3 Tomatoes

## Directions

1. Clean and scale the Shrimp and boil according to recipe.
2. Then follow in all particulars the recipe given for Salpicon Creole, with the exception that White Wine is used instead of milk, and a truffle is added to the minced vegetables.
3. Send to the table hot.
