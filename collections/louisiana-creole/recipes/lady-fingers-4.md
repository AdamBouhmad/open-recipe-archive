## Ingredients

- 3/4 of a Pound of Flour
- 4 Ounces of Powdered White Sugar
- 5 Eggs

## Directions

1. Put the sugar and the yolks of the eggs into a bowl and beat thoroughly till very light
2. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth and add the flour
3. Mix well and add immediately the whites of the eggs
4. Beat all together gently for a few minutes
5. Have ready a long sheet of paper, spread on a baking tin
6. Take a tablespoon and drop the batter along the paper, in lengths of four or five inches, being careful to leave an empty space of an inch between each cake
7. Set to bake in a very moderate oven
8. Bake to a very light brown, for twenty minutes, in a moderate oven
