## Ingredients

- 3 cups of loaf sugar
- 1 cup of butter
- yolks of 5 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 cup of milk
- whites of 5 eggs
- 4 cups of flour
- juice and peel of one lemon

## Directions

1. rub the butter and sugar to a cream
2. stir in the yolks of eggs well beaten
3. dissolve soda in milk
4. add the milk
5. beat the whites of the eggs to a froth
6. add them
7. sift in flour as light as possible
8. add the juice and peel of one lemon with peel grated
