## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pound of Spaghetti
- 1 Pound of Grated Parmesan Cheese
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Spaghetti a l'Italienne is prepared in the same manner as Boiled Macaroni, Italian Style.
2. Spaghetti is a more delicate form of macaroni.
