## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- ½ lb. of white sugar pounded fine
- ½ lb. of butter
- little nutmeg
- rich puff paste
- candied orange and citron

## Directions

1. Beat 8 eggs well
2. Put them in a stewpan with ½ lb. of white sugar pounded fine, the same quantity of butter, a little nutmeg
3. Set it on the fire, stirring it till it thickens
4. Put it in a basin to cool
5. Put a rich puff paste around the edges of the dish
6. Pour in the pudding and bake it in a moderate oven
7. It will cut light and clear, when you may add candied orange and citron
