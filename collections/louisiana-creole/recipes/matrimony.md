## Ingredients

- 2 dozen common sized peaches
- sugar
- 1 quart of cream or rich custard

## Directions

1. Pare and cut into small pieces 2 dozen common sized peaches
2. Cover them thickly with sugar and let them stand 3 or 4 hours
3. Beat them into a quart of cream, or a rich custard
4. If cream, sweeten it before freezing it
