## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 lb. flour sifted
- 1 lb. butter
- 1 lb. pounded loaf sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of rose water
- 1 grated nutmeg
- mixed mace and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Stir the sugar and butter to a cream
2. Beat the eggs very light
3. Put them all at once into the pan of flour, butter and sugar
4. Add the spice and rose water
5. Stir the whole with a knife
6. Take a portion of the dough and lay it on the board
7. Roll it lightly with the hands into long thin rolls
