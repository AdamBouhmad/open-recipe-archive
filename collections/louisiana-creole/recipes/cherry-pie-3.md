## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cherries
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Water

## Directions

1. Stew the cherries gently, as for a marmalade
2. Prepare the pie crust according to directions, and proceed as in directions for making pies
