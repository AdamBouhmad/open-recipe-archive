## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Sweet Almonds
- 4 Ounces of Filberts
- The Whites of 4 Eggs
- 8 Ounces of Powdered Sugar
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Flour

## Directions

1. Blanch, peel and pound the almond and filbert kernels in a mortar till finely pulverized, adding the white of an egg, to keep the almonds from turning oily.
2. Beat the whites of three eggs to a froth.
3. Add the yolks, beaten separately to a cream with the sugar.
4. Incorporate thoroughly, and beat well.
5. Add four tablespoonfuls of flour, sifted well, and mix well.
6. Place in small cakes on wafer sheets of paper.
7. Bake in a moderate oven till a very light color.
