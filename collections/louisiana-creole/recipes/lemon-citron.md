## Ingredients

- lemon peels (nice, fresh)
- water
- sugar (equal to their weight)

## Directions

1. Turn water on nice, fresh lemon peels
2. Soak them till all the white pulp can be scraped off
3. Boil till soft
4. Preserve them in their weight in sugar
