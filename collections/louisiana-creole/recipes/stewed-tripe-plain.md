## Ingredients

- tripe
- milk or milk and water
- butter
- flour
- parsley
- green onion

## Directions

1. Cut a pound of tripe in long narrow pieces, lay it in a stew-pan and add a cup of milk, or milk and water.
2. Add a piece of butter as large as a hen's egg and a tablespoonful of flour sifted in.
3. Add a bunch of parsley, and a green onion, if desired.
4. Cook slowly for nearly two hours.
