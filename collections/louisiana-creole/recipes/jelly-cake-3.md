## Ingredients

- 6 eggs beaten separately
- 0.5 lb. sugar
- 0.5 lb. butter
- 6 ounces flour
- jelly
- 1 teaspoonful yeast powder

## Directions

1. Cream the flour and butter
2. Beat the sugar and yolks together, and add to flour and butter
3. Beat well, and last add the beaten whites
4. If accustomed to using yeast powder, add 1 teaspoonful
5. Bake in shallow tin pans
6. When cool spread on the jelly
7. Pile up
8. Trim off the edges, and sift sugar over the top
