## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of best almonds
- rose water
- 1 lb. of good butter
- 1/4 lb. of white sugar
- 1 wineglass of wine and brandy mixed
- 1 wineglass of rose water
- whites and yolks of 7 eggs
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Blanch 1/4 lb. of best almonds
2. Rub them in a mortar with rose water to prevent their oiling
3. Rub them together to a smooth paste
4. Cream 1 lb. of good butter and 1/4 lb. of white sugar
5. When smooth and light, stir in the almonds
6. Add 1 wineglass of wine and brandy mixed and 1 wineglass of rose water
7. Beat separately the whites from the yolks of 7 eggs
8. First add the yolks, then the whites
9. Beat all well together
10. Line the dish with puff paste, and bake as long as a custard
