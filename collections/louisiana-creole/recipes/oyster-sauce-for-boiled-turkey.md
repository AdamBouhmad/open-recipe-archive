## Ingredients

- three dozen oysters
- half a pound of butter
- flour
- cayenne pepper
- salt
- a cup of cream

## Directions

1. Put three dozen oysters in a stewpan
2. Save their liquor in a bowl
3. Mix with the oysters half a pound of butter and flour, worked together
4. Season with cayenne pepper and salt
5. Thin this with the liquor from the oysters and a cup of cream
6. Let it boil ten minutes and serve on the turkey
