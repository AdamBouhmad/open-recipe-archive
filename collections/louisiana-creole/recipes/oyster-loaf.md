## Ingredients

- Delicate French Loaves of Bread
- 2 Dozen Oysters to a Loaf
- 1 Tablespoonful of Melted Butter
- Broiled or Creamed Oysters

## Directions

1. Take delicate French loaves of bread and cut off, lengthwise, the upper portion
2. Dig the crumbs out of the center of each piece, leaving the sides and bottom like a square box
3. Brush each corner of the box and the bottom with melted butter, and place in a quick oven to brown
4. Fill with broiled or creamed oysters
5. Cover with each other and serve
