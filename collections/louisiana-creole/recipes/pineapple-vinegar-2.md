## Ingredients

- 3 Pineapples
- 1 Quart of Pure Cider Vinegar
- Fine white sugar in the proportion of two-thirds of a pound to one quart of the juice

## Directions

1. Slice the pineapples
2. Cover with pure cider vinegar, and let them stand for three or four days
3. Mash them through a cloth, straining as long as the juice runs clear
4. To every quart of juice allow one and two-thirds pounds of fine white sugar
5. Boil all together for about ten minutes, and skim very carefully, until no scum rises upon the surface
6. Take the mixture from the fire, and when it cools somewhat bottle it
7. Put a tablespoonful of this extract in a glass of cold ice water in warm weather and serve as a summer drink
