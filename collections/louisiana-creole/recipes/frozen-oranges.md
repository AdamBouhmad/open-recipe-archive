## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Oranges
- 1/4 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Pare the oranges, and grate or cut them into fine pieces.
2. Mix well with the sugar and set aside for an hour.
3. Add the water and mix thoroughly.
4. Turn into a freezer and proceed to freeze as in Ice Cream.
