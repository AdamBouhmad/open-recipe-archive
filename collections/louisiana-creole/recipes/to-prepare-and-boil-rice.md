## Ingredients

- 1 pint rice
- 3 pints water
- 1 tablespoonful salt

## Directions

1. Wash and pick 1 pint of rice
2. Add to it 3 pints of water, and 1 tablespoonful of salt
3. Boil over a quick fire for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally
4. Pour off all, or nearly all the water (as the rice will be soft or grainy, according to the quantity of water left on it when put to steam, and the length of time allowed in the steaming)
5. Cover the vessel for 15 minutes on a very slow fire, stirring it occasionally
