## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Stale Bread
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Cup Raisins
- 1 1/2 Pints of Milk
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well, and mix, and then, when very light, add the milk.
2. If you use stale cake, add two tablespoonfuls of sugar; if you use stale bread, add half a cup of sugar.
3. Mix thoroughly.
4. Wet the bread and squeeze it.
5. Then mix in it a cup of seeded raisins.
6. Beat the egg mixture thoroughly into this, and place in the oven and bake for an hour.
7. Serve with Hard Cream, Brandy or Lemon Sauce.
