## Ingredients

- rabbits
- cold water
- butter
- onions
- celery
- cold water
- peas (green or dry)
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut one or two rabbits into joints; lay them for an hour in cold water; dry and fry them in butter until they are half done;
2. Place the meat in a saucepan with four or five onions and a head of celery cut small; add to these three parts of cold water and a cup of peas, either green or dry;
3. Season with pepper and salt, then strain and serve it. Some like it unstrained.
