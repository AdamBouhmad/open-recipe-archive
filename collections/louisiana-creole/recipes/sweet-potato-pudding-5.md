## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of sweet potatoes
- 6 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of fine sugar
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- nutmeg
- lemon rind
- 1 wineglass of old brandy
- paste
- white sugar

## Directions

1. Boil 1 lb. of sweet potatoes very tender, and press them while hot through a grater
2. add 6 eggs well beaten, 1/2 lb. of fine sugar, 1/2 lb. of butter, some grated nutmeg and lemon rind, a wineglass of old brandy
3. put a paste in the dish, and when the pudding is done, sprinkle the top with white sugar finely pulverized
