## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pound of Flour
- 1 1/2 Cups of Pulverized Sugar
- 9 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon or Pineapple Extract
- 1 Pineapple
- Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare four layers of sponge cake
2. Pare the pineapple, being careful to cut out all the eyes and the core
3. Grate the fruit very fine
4. Place a layer of the fruit over the bottom layer of the cake, sprinkle with a little powdered sugar and then spread over this a little Cream Sauce, which has been made to the consistency of Cream Puff Filling
5. Put a second layer of the cake over this, cover nicely with the grated pineapple, sugar and Cream Filling, and proceed thus till the top layer is placed over the pineapple
6. Then ice or sprinkle with white sugar
