## Ingredients

- 1 peck of damsons, or prune plums
- 3½ pints of vinegar
- 7 lbs. of brown sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of cloves, mace and cinnamon powdered

## Directions

1. Stone the damsons
2. Put into a kettle the vinegar and brown sugar
3. Boil, and skim it well
4. Put in the fruit, with the cloves, mace and cinnamon powdered
5. Boil it 2 hours
6. Take out the fruit and let it boil some time longer
7. Remove it from the fire, and when cold mix all together
