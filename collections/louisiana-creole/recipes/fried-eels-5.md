## Ingredients

- A 2-pound eel
- 2 eggs
- Mashed bread crumbs
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Hot lard

## Directions

1. Clean and skin the eel and place in boiling water and vinegar till thoroughly disgorged.
2. Cut into lengths of two inches.
3. Wipe dry with a clean towel.
4. Beat two eggs well and add mashed bread crumbs.
5. Roll the eel well in this preparation and fry in hot lard, following implicitly the directions given for frying, and remembering that the fish must always swim in the grease.
6. Drain off all grease and serve on a bed of parsley.
