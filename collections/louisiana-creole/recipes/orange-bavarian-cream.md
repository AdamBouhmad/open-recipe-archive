## Ingredients

- 6 Louisiana Oranges
- The Yolks of 5 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Cream
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1/2 Box Gelatine, or 1/2 Pint

## Directions

1. Whip the cream and prepare the gelatine according to general directions.
2. Set the milk to boil, and when it begins to boil, add the gelatine.
3. Beat the yolks of the eggs very light, and add the sugar, beating very light.
4. Strain the milk and gelatine, and add the sugar and yolks stirring constantly till well mixed.
5. Set over the fire for two minutes, and then set it away to cool.
6. When cold, pour in the orange juice, which you will strain through a sieve.
7. Set the basin in a pan of cracked ice, and stir until it begins to thicken.
8. Stir in the whipped cream very carefully, and proceed to turn into a mold to harden.
9. Always wet the mold first with cold water before turning in the cream.
10. When cold, serve with whipped cream.
