## Ingredients

- red fish
- 1 teaspoonful of mace
- 1 teaspoonful of allspice
- 1/2 teaspoonful of cloves
- 1 onion
- parsley
- 1 teacup of wine
- water
- 1 large spoonful of butter
- 3 spoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Have the scales well taken from the fish, well washed and wiped dry
2. Put the fish into the pan and half fry it
3. Chop very fine together 1 teaspoonful of mace, 1 of allspice, 1/2 of a spoonful of cloves, 1 onion, and a handful of parsley
4. Add 1 teacup of wine and as much water as you think sufficient for the gravy
5. Mix 1 large spoonful of butter with 3 spoonfuls of flour, rub them well together, then add warm water to thin it, then add all the ingredients
6. Put the fish on the strainer, and then in the pan
