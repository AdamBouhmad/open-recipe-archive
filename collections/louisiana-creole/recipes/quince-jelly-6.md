## Ingredients

- quinces
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare the quinces as before directed
2. Take off the stems and blossoms
3. Wash them clean and cut them in slices without paring
4. Fill the pan and pour in water to cover them
5. Stew them gently, putting in a little water occasionally, till they are quite soft
6. Pour them in a jelly bag
7. Let all the juice run through, pressing it
