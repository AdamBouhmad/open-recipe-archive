## Ingredients

- 1 pint and a half of good flour
- 1/2 pint of white sugar
- a heaped tablespoon of butter
- a teacup of milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls of yeast
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix yeast with the dry flour
2. Rub the sugar, butter, and yolks of eggs as in pound cake
3. Beat the whites to a stiff froth
4. Add the milk just before you put in the beaten whites
5. The flour should be added last
6. Bake immediately
