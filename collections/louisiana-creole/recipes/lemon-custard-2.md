## Ingredients

- one cup of water
- one tablespoonful of flour or corn starch
- yolks of three eggs
- one cup of brown sugar
- juice of a lemon
- whites of three eggs
- one cup of pulverized sugar

## Directions

1. Boil a cup of water and stir into it a tablespoonful of flour or corn starch
2. Beat the yolks of three eggs with a cup of brown sugar
3. Add the juice of a lemon strained and beat it up with the yolks and sugar
4. Pour this in a paste and bake it
5. While the custard is baking, take the whites of the three eggs and beat them up with a cup of pulverized sugar
6. Spread this icing on the baked custard and brown it slightly
