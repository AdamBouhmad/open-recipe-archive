## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Louisiana Molasses
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 3/4 Cup of Butter
- 2 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Vinegar
- 2 Teaspoonfuls Soda
- 1/4 Teaspoonful Each of Cloves, Allspice and Cinnamon (ground)
- 1 Grated Nutmeg
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Warm the molasses and dilute with the vinegar.
2. Beat the butter and the sugar to a cream, and then add the eggs, whites and yolks, well-beaten together.
3. Then add gradually one-half of the flour and the molasses.
4. Add the grated spices and salt.
5. Beat well.
6. Add the soda, dissolved in two tablespoonfuls of boiling water, and beat well.
7. Bake in a moderate oven from thirty to forty-five minutes.
