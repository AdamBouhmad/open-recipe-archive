## Ingredients

- 1 Strong Cup of Cafe Noir
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla
- 3/4 Pint of Gelatine
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 3/4 Cup of Water
- 1 Pint of Whipped Cream
- The Yolks of 4 Eggs

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine and whip the cream
2. After half an hour, pour it into the boiling coffee, and dissolve thoroughly
3. Add the sugar, and stir thoroughly
4. Add the milk and let it boil up once, and remove from the fire
5. Stir in the well-beaten yolks of the eggs
6. Stir steadily and carefully until all the ingredients have thoroughly blended
7. Set it on the fire to thicken slightly, stirring all the time, but be careful not to let it boil, or the eggs will curdle
8. Set aside to cool slightly; pour it into a basin, and set over cracked ice, and add the whipped cream as it thickens
9. Turn into a mold, and set in a cool place to harden
10. Serve with whipped cream
