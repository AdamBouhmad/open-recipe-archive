## Ingredients

- venison slices
- butter
- black pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the slices medium size
2. Thickly butter them
3. Sprinkle with black pepper and a little salt
4. Place the slices on a gridiron and broil them in a hurry
5. Send it to the table very hot with a little melted butter over it
