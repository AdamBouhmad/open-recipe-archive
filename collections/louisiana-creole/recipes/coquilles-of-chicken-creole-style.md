## Ingredients

- The Breast of a Roasted Chicken
- 1 Ounce of Butter
- 1 Truffle
- 4 Mushrooms
- 1 Dozen Godiveau Quenelles
- 1/2 Pint of Madeira Sauce
- Grated Bread Crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into dice-shaped pieces; take an ounce of butter and place in a saucepan; add the chicken.
2. Chop four mushrooms and one truffle and add.
3. Make a dozen small Godiveau Quenelles (see recipe), and add to the mixture in the saucepan.
4. Pour over a half pint of Madeira Sauce (see recipe), stir well and let all cook for five minutes.
5. Take a half dozen table shells and fill with this mixture.
6. Sprinkle grated fresh bread crumbs over the tops, spread a little melted butter over each and place the shells on a baking dish.
7. Place in a very hot oven and let them brown to a beautiful golden color, which will require about six minutes.
8. Place a folded napkin on a dish, set the shells upon it and send to the table hot.
