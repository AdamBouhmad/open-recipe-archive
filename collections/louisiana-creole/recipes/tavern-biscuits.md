## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of flour
- 1 lb. of brown sugar
- 4 glasses of wine
- 8 of sweet cream
- 1 lb. of butter
- 2 ounces of allspice, or 1 ounce of cinnamon, nutmeg or mace

## Directions

1. Cut out the biscuits and bake on tin sheets
