## Ingredients

- rice
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Pour on just enough cold water to prevent the rice from burning to the pot
2. Use a pot with a close fitting cover
3. Set it on a moderate fire
4. The rice is steamed rather than boiled until it is nearly done
5. Take off the cover of the pot and allow the surplus steam and water to escape
6. Add the necessary salt before boiling
