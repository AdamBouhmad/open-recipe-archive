## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of fresh butter
- 1 lb. of pounded and sifted loaf sugar
- 4 well beaten eggs
- flour
- boiling lard or clarified suet
- pounded sugar

## Directions

1. Beat to a cream 1 lb. of fresh butter
2. Mix it with the same quantity of pounded and sifted loaf sugar and 4 well beaten eggs
3. Add flour till thick enough to roll out
4. Cut the paste into oblong pieces, 4 or 5 inches in length, with a paste cutter
5. Divide the centre into 3 or 4 strips
6. Wet the edges and plait one bar over the other, so as to meet in the centre
7. Throw them into boiling lard or clarified suet
8. When fried a light brown, drain them before the fire
9. Cover them with pounded sugar
