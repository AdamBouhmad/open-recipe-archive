## Ingredients

- 3 Pounds of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Continue boiling, and dip the finger after a few moments in iced water and then in the boiling sugar, and instantly in the water again
2. The proper degree is reached when the sugar is placed between the fingers, and will not stick, or when distended it snaps hard and dry
