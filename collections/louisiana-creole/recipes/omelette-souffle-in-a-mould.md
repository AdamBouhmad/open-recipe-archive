## Ingredients

- six fresh eggs
- three spoonfuls of rice flour
- a tablespoonful of orange-flower water
- butter

## Directions

1. Break six fresh eggs, separate the whites from the yolks
2. put with the yolks three spoonfuls of rice flour and a tablespoonful of orange-flower water
3. stir these well together
4. whip the whites of the eggs to a high froth
5. mix them with the yolks
6. Pour the mixture into a buttered mould, about half full
7. bake it in a moderate oven for half an hour
8. When done turn it on to a dish and serve quickly
