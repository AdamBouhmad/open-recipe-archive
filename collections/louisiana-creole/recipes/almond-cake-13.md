## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Cups of Flour
- The Whites of Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful Lemon Extract
- 2 Pounds of Almonds
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1/2 Cup of Sugar

## Directions

1. Cream the butter and sugar
2. Add the milk, and gradually add the flour and the whites of the eggs alternately, and beat till light
3. Add the essence, and bake in jelly tins fifteen minutes
4. Take two pounds of almonds, blanch them, peel and pound to a flour
5. Beat the whites of two eggs to a froth with one-half cup of sugar, and spread on top of the bottom layer of cake as a frosting
6. Place over this a layer of the almonds, and then another layer of frosting
7. Add a layer of cake, and then go over the same process again till the top layer of cake is reached
8. Sprinkle with white sugar or ice neatly, according to taste
