## Ingredients

- chickens
- veal
- fat ham
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- white sauce
- butter
- flour
- milk or water
- mushrooms
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- paste

## Directions

1. Cut two chickens up as for frying
2. Lay some veal cut in small pieces in the bottom of your pie dish
3. Cut up over the veal a slice of fat ham
4. On this place your chickens
5. Place hard-boiled yolks of eggs in among the chicken
6. Take half a pint of white sauce, made with butter, flour, and milk or water
7. Pour this over the chickens
8. Season with a cup of chopped mushrooms, some parsley, pepper and salt
9. Cover your pie with a good paste
10. Bake for an hour or two
