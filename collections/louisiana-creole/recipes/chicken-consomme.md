## Ingredients

- 1 large chicken cut in pieces
- 3 quarts of cold water
- 1 onion
- 1 small stalk of celery
- 1 carrot
- 1 turnip
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Put the chicken into the salt and water and let it simmer gently until the scum begins to rise; then skim.
2. Add the other ingredients.
3. Boil gently for two hours, and serve with slices of toast.
