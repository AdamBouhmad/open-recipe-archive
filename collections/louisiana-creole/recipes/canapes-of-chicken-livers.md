## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Chicken Livers
- 1 Onion
- 6 Slices of Toast
- Dash of Anchovy Essence
- 1 Ounce of Butter
- Salt and Red Pepper to Taste
- Pimentos and Red Chili to Garnish

## Directions

1. Saute the Chicken Livers with a finely minced onion till tender
2. Pound them to a paste, adding first a dash of Anchovy Essence, one tablespoonful of butter, salt and red pepper to taste
3. Cut the bread the full width of the loaf and trim the edges nicely
4. Fry in butter to a golden brown
5. Take out and drain
6. Place in a silver dish, or in the dish in which they are to be served
7. Pile up the chicken liver preparation in pyramidal shape on top of the toast
8. Smooth nicely all around with a knife
9. Set in the oven for two or three minutes
10. Decorate the edges of the bread with slices of Pimentos and rings of Red Chilis
11. Serve hot
