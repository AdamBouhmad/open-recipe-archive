## Ingredients

- small lemons with thick rind
- salt
- vinegar
- brine of the lemons
- pepper
- ginger
- two ounces of mustard seed
- two cloves of garlic

## Directions

1. Take small lemons with thick rind
2. Rub them hard with a piece of flannel
3. Slit them through the rind in four quarters, but not through the pulp
4. Fill the slits with salt hard pressed in
5. Set them upright in a crock four or five days, until the salt melts
6. Turn them each day in their own liquid until they get tender
7. Make the pickle to cover them of vinegar, some of the brine of the lemons, pepper and ginger
8. Boil this pickle and skim it well
9. When cold put it over the lemons with two ounces of mustard seed and two cloves of garlic, to six lemons
