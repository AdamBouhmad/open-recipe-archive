## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. of lean veal, cut in slices
- 2 lbs. of lean ham
- 2 ounces butter
- a handful of chopped mushrooms
- 3 onions
- 1 carrot
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- rind of a lemon
- 1/2 teaspoon of mixed allspice, mace and cloves
- 4 quarts of stock
- brown roux

## Directions

1. Put 6 lbs. of lean veal, cut in slices, and 2 lbs. of lean ham into a stewpan, with 2 ounces butter, a handful of chopped mushrooms, 3 onions, one carrot, a bunch of sweet herbs, the rind of a lemon, and 1/2 teaspoon of mixed allspice, mace and cloves
2. Let it just brown at the bottom and add 4 quarts of stock
3. let it boil 3 or 4 hours
4. strain it off
5. thicken with brown roux, and boil it well for ten minutes, stirring all the time
6. pass through a tamise
