## Ingredients

- pigeons
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- flour
- butter
- soup-stock or gravy
- wine or stock

## Directions

1. Pick and wash the pigeons, stuff them with bread crumbs, parsley, pepper, salt and butter mixed.
2. Dust with flour, and put into a pan to brown.
3. Add butter and a little soup-stock or gravy.
4. Stew gently until tender.
5. Before dishing add a glass of wine if approved, if not, a little more stock, if the gravy has become too thick.
