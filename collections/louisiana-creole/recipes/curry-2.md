## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of fresh butter
- 1 medium sized onion
- chicken or mutton, lamb or veal

## Directions

1. Put 1 tablespoonful of fresh butter into a saucepan
2. Let it boil
3. Add a medium sized onion, cut into thin slices
4. Fry the onion a deep brown
5. Have ready a chicken jointed and cut up into small pieces, or 2 lbs. of mutton, lamb or veal
