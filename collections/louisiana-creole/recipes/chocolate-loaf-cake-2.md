## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1½ Cups of Sifted Flour
- ¾ Cup of Milk
- Yolks of 8 Eggs
- White of 1 Egg
- ½ Cake of Grated Chocolate
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Dissolve the chocolate in the cup of milk, and boil till thick
2. Beat the butter to a cream, and gradually add the sugar, beating constantly
3. Add the yolks of the eggs, and beat till very light
4. Sift the flour, and add the baking powder, and sift again
5. Add the flour gradually to the mixture, beating all the while
6. Add the chocolate, and beat very vigorously
7. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth, and add to the mixture
8. Add the essence, beating lightly again, and turn into a greased pan and bake in a moderate oven for three-quarters of an hour or an hour
