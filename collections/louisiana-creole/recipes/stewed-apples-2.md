## Ingredients

- 6 Apples, Large and Firm
- Sugar to Taste
- The Zest of Half an Orange
- 1 Blade Mace
- 1 Stick Ground Cinnamon
- Ground Allspice
- Half a Cup of Water

## Directions

1. Pare the apples and cut into quarters or semi-quarters, removing the cores and seeds.
2. Add half a cup of water.
3. Put them into an agate or a porcelain-lined saucepan.
4. Strew generously with sugar.
5. Let them simmer gently for half an hour.
6. Then add the zest of half an orange, grated, and the ground spices.
7. Let all simmer gently till they form a rich tender compote, that is, until not the least semblance remains of being uncooked.
8. This compote may be served hot during the meal, or it may be used as a dessert, with cream or milk.
9. Apples may be stewed whole in the same manner, only remember to extract the core with a fine knife.
