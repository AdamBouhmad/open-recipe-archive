## Ingredients

- two or three onions
- a dozen leaves of sage
- pepper and salt
- half a pint of bread crumbs
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- the yolks of four eggs
- olive oil
- a little sugar
- chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt
- a cup of the gravy from the pig
- the juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Select a pig four weeks old
2. Let the pig be prepared in the usual way by the butcher, that is scalded, drawn, etc.
3. Stuff it with a mixture of two or three onions, say half a pint when sliced and chopped, and a dozen leaves of sage, pepper and salt
4. Set this to simmer on the fire
5. Throw in half a pint of bread crumbs if the pig is small—if a large one, put a pint of crumbs—a quarter of a pound of butter, and the yolks of four eggs
6. Cook this and stuff the pig with it
7. Sew the pig up and put it in the oven to roast
8. Baste it often with a brush or swab dipped in olive oil
9. Dust a little sugar over it, and brown it evenly
10. Take off the head before serving
11. Take out the brains, put them in a stew pan
12. Add to them some chopped parsley, pepper, and salt, a cup of the gravy from the pig, and the juice of a lemon
13. Stir this over the fire, and send it to the table hot in a separate boat
