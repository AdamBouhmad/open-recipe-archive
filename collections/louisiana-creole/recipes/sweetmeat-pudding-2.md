## Ingredients

- quinces
- citron
- peaches
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 spoonful of flour
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- yolks of 10 eggs
- preserved lemon peel

## Directions

1. Make a layer of quinces, citron and peaches
2. cream 1/2 lb. of butter with a spoonful of flour
3. add 1/2 lb. of sugar, the yolks of 10 eggs
4. bake about 1/2 hour
5. preserved lemon peel is an improvement
