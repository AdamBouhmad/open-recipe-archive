## Ingredients

- 1½ Pints of Indian Meal
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Sweet Milk or Buttermilk or Hot Water
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Melted Butter, or 1 of Lard
- ¼ Teaspoonful of Soda

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs till very light, and add the cornmeal, and beat till light.
2. Add the melted butter and the milk or water.
3. If buttermilk is used, you may use the soda, dissolving it in two tablespoonfuls of boiling water.
4. Do not use the soda with the sweet milk.
5. Make a dough or batter thick enough to be spread into cakes a half inch in thickness and about five inches in diameter, the cakes being formed round.
6. Place in buttered tins, and bake in a moderate oven for about a half hour, frequently brushing across while baking with melted butter about four or five times.
7. Serve hot for lunch or tea, split and buttered, or served with fresh, sweet butter and buttermilk.
