## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 spoonfuls of yeast
- 1 pint of flour
- cold water

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well
2. Add the yeast and flour
3. Add as much water as will make a stiff batter
4. When light, bake them in hoops for breakfast
