## Ingredients

- Foies Gras
- Quarter Can of Mushrooms
- Quarter Can of Truffles
- Two Shallots
- Sprig Each of Thyme, Parsley and Bay Leaf
- Leaf of Rose Geranium
- Quarter Teaspoonful Each of Ground Cinnamon, Allspice, Cloves and Mace
- Young Sweetbread
- Quarter Cup of the Soft of Bread
- Yolk of an Egg
- Grated Bread Crumbs
- Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Half Teaspoonful of Prepared Mustard
- Milk

## Directions

1. Choose sufficient livers for the number of guests and place them in a saucepan with a tablespoonful of butter
2. Let them simmer gently and add a half can of mushrooms and a quarter of a can of truffles
3. Add two shallots, nicely minced; a sprig each of thyme, bay leaf and parsley, minced fine; salt and pepper to taste, and a half teaspoonful of prepared mustard
4. Add the leaf of one geranium, minced fine, and a quarter teaspoonful each of ground cinnamon, allspice, cloves and mace
5. Mix thoroughly and let it simmer in the juice of the mushrooms for about twenty minutes
6. Take a young sweetbread and cook according to recipe for Plain Fried Sweetbreads, and add a half cup of the soft of the bread, well moistened with milk
7. Mix this with the hashed sweetbreads, and add the yolk of an egg
8. Place this in a mortar with the foies gras and mix well
9. Turn into a pan and brush lightly with the beaten yolk of an egg, and sprinkle grated bread crumbs over
10. Set in a pan of boiling water (Bain-marie), and bake in the oven for about a half hour
