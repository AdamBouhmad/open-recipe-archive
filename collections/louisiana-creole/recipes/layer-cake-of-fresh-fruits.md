## Ingredients

- 4 Layers of White or Gold Cake
- 3 Apples
- 2/3 of a Cup of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Orange or Lemon Extract
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Make a good White Cake or Gold Cake.
2. Pare and grate three apples, and cook them with two-thirds of a cup of sugar.
3. When they come to a thick jelly, take off the stove and flavor with Lemon or Orange extract.
4. Take the whites of two eggs, beat till very light.
5. Add four tablespoonfuls of white sugar, and beat till very stiff, so stiff that the whites can stand alone.
6. Spread a layer of this on the top of the bottom layer of cake.
7. Add a layer of apples.
8. Spread another layer of frosting.
9. Cover with a layer of cake, and so continue till all is used up.
