## Ingredients

- 1 Large Pineapple
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Pare the pineapples.
2. Cut out the eyes, and then grate the fruit, or cut it very fine.
3. Add the sugar and water and stir well.
4. When the sugar is thoroughly dissolved, turn the mixture into a freezer, and proceed to freeze as in Ice Cream.
