## Ingredients

- spinach
- soda
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Wash in two or three waters, as the grit adheres very closely to spinach
2. When well washed, boil it one half hour in clear water
3. Add a little soda, if it does not look a nice green
4. When soft, drain it well and chop very fine
5. Add butter, salt if needed, and pepper to taste
6. Garnish with hard-boiled eggs cut in fancy shapes
7. Or in early spring, poach two or three eggs, and lay on the freshly cooked spinach
