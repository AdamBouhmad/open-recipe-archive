## Ingredients

- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Best Old Bourbon
- 3 Lumps of Loaf Sugar
- 1/4 of a Lemon
- Hot Water to Fill One Tumbler

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from a quarter of a lemon and dissolve with the sugar
2. Add the whisky and let all dissolve well
3. Fill with hot water; stir well, and add a little grated nutmeg, if desired, and serve hot
