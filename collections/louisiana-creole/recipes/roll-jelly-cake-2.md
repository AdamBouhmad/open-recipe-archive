## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup of sugar
- 1 cup of flour
- yeast powder
- jelly for filling

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs well
2. Add the sugar
3. Add the flour
4. Bake in a shallow square pan
5. When done, take it out and spread on the jelly
6. Roll at once
