## Ingredients

- 4 quarts of flour
- 1 lb. of brown sugar
- 2 ounces of beaten ginger
- a little allspice
- 1 lb. of butter rubbed in very fine
- 4 eggs beaten up light
- 1 quart of molasses

## Directions

1. Add the brown sugar to the flour
2. Add the ginger and allspice
3. Rub in the butter
4. Add the beaten eggs
5. Add the molasses to make it up
6. Roll it in thin cakes
7. Bake them
