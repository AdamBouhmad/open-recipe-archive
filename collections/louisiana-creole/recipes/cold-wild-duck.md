## Ingredients

- Remains of Cold Duck
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1/2 Tumbler of Currant Jelly
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Port Wine

## Directions

1. Cut the cold Wild Duck nicely in thick slices.
2. Warm the butter in the saucepan, add the wine and jelly, thoroughly blended; mix well.
3. Serve the sauce with the slices of cold duck.
4. The duck may also be served very deliciously with Currant Jelly alone, and buttered toast.
