## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of White Pulverized Sugar
- 10 Eggs
- 1 Pound Sifted Flour
- 1 Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Wineglassful of Brandy
- 1 Pound of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla, Lemon or Rose Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Wash the butter and drain, and take out a heaping teaspoonful
2. Then beat the remainder to a rich cream with the sugar, and add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs
3. Beat till very light, and add a part of the flour, into which you will have sifted the baking powder
4. Then add a part of the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, and continue alternating with the flour and the eggs till all are used up
5. Then add the grated nutmeg, the brandy and the essence, and continue beating steadily for a half hour
6. Bake in a moderate oven for one hour
7. Use the broom-wisp test. If it comes out clean and dry the cake is done; otherwise continue baking for a quarter of an hour longer
