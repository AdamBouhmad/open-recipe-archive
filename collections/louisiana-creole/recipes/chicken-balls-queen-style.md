## Ingredients

- 1 Young Chicken
- 2 Small Onions
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 4 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 2 Eggs
- 1/2 Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Make the mixture in exactly the same manner as croquettes.
2. Place the mixture in a frying pan and fry in butter, using about a tablespoonful.
3. To this add about one pint of milk.
4. Beat the chicken thoroughly in this, add a grated nutmeg, then take off the fire and add two eggs, well beaten.
5. Fill custard cups with the mixture, place in the oven setting in a pan of boiling water and covering with paper.
6. Let them bake thus as you would a cup custard for twenty minutes.
