## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 Parsnips
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Pint of Milk
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Boil the parsnips until so tender that they break easily under pressure.
2. Mash them well, after draining off all water.
3. Put a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan, and add a tablespoonful of flour.
4. Blend, without browning, and add a half Pint of milk or cream.
5. Stir well, and as the mixture begins to boil, add the parsnips.
6. Mix thoroughly, season with salt and pepper, and serve in a dish, heaping up the parsnips in pyramidal shape.
7. Serve with veal cutlets.
