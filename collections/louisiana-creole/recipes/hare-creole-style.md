## Ingredients

- 1 Fine, Tender Hare
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Large Onion
- 1 Dozen Small Onions
- 3 Tomatoes
- 1 Ounce of Minced Ham
- 2 Sprigs of Thyme
- 2 Bay Leaves
- 1/2 Glass of White Wine
- 1/2 Glass of Red Wine
- 1 1/2 Tablespoonfuls of Salt
- 1 1/2 Tablespoonfuls of Pepper
- 1 Pint of Consomme or Water
- Grated Nutmeg
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Skin, clean, draw and thoroughly wash a fine tender Hare and preserve the liver and heart
2. Cut the Hare into pieces at the joints
3. Make a marinade by taking a half glass of White Wine, one large finely-sliced onion, the thyme and bay leaves finely minced, and place in a stone jar
4. Add a half teaspoonful of grated nutmeg and a tablespoonful of salt, and place in this mixture the cut-up Hare, and let all steep for six hours
5. Lift the pieces out carefully and have ready a saucepan into which you will have placed a teaspoonful of butter
6. Add twelve small onions glazed, one ounce of ham minced fine
7. Put the Hare into the pan and let all brown nicely for about ten minutes
8. Add the flour finely rubbed, stir well and let brown
9. Add the tomatoes peeled and sliced fine, let all brown ten minutes longer
10. Add the red wine and the consomme or water and stir till it begins to boil
11. Season according to taste with salt and pepper
12. Let all cook for three quarters of an hour
13. Add the heart and liver which you will have finely chopped and thoroughly mixed together
14. Let all cook for a quarter of an hour longer and serve with toasted Croutons
