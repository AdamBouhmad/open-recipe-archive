## Ingredients

- a dozen apples
- a large cupful of brown sugar
- a teacupful of water
- the grated rind of a lemon
- one pint of milk
- four eggs
- two ounces of loaf sugar
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Peel, cut and core the apples
2. Put them in a sauce-pan with the water
3. As they heat, add the brown sugar and lemon-peel
4. When mashed and well cooked, take it off
5. Put the fruit in the bottom of a deep dish, and pour a custard of the milk, sugar and eggs, over it
6. Grate over it before baking, a little nutmeg
7. Bake in a moderate oven
