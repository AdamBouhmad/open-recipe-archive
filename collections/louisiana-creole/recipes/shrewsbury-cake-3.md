## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- three-quarters of a pound of sugar
- five well beaten eggs
- a nutmeg
- some essence
- about a quart of flour
- currants (optional)

## Directions

1. Beat to a cream half a pound of butter, and three-quarters of a pound of sugar
2. add five well beaten eggs, a nutmeg, some essence, and about a quart of flour
3. Sift the flour, mix it well
4. drop the mixture with a spoon on buttered tins
5. Add currants if you wish
