## Ingredients

- 3 pints of oysters
- 1 pint of rich cream
- 1 teaspoonful of flour
- 1 tablespoonful butter

## Directions

1. Strain oysters through a cullender
2. Put 1 pint of rich cream in a clean saucepan
3. Mix 1 teaspoonful of flour and 1 tablespoonful butter together
4. When mixed and the cream begins to heat, stir in the flour and butter slowly
5. Let it simmer for a few minutes, then add the oysters
6. Cook them ten minutes and dish hot for table
