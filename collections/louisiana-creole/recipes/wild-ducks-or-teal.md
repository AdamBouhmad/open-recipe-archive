## Ingredients

- wild ducks or teal
- butter
- lemon juice
- cayenne pepper
- sugar
- port wine

## Directions

1. Roast the birds; a duck should take about 15 minutes with a good fire; a teal will take less time
2. Baste them very frequently
3. Attend to the fire and motion of the spit
4. When you dish it, draw the knife four times down the breast
5. Have ready a little hot butter, the juice of a lemon, cayenne pepper, a little dust of sugar, and a glass of port wine
6. Pour it hot over the ducks
