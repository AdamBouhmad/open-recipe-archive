## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- a cup of milk
- whites of six eggs
- three cups of sifted flour
- one teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- half a teaspoonful of soda
- a small cocoanut

## Directions

1. Cream one cup of butter and two cups of sugar
2. add a cup of milk, the whites of six eggs, and three cups of sifted flour with one teaspoonful of cream of tartar, and half a teaspoonful of soda mixed in the flour
3. Grate a small cocoanut, dry it in a skillet over the fire by stirring it about ten minutes
4. Stir the cocoanut into the batter
5. Bake in a moderate oven about three-quarters of an hour
