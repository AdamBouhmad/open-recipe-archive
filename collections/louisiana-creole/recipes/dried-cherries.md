## Ingredients

- cherries
- sugar
- whisky

## Directions

1. Stew the cherries with a little sugar for a short time; then spread them on dishes and set them in the sun
2. As they dry pour the syrup over them, a little at a time, for 3 or 4 days; when dry, pack them away in boxes or jars, putting a layer of cherries with a layer of sugar, and a little whisky sprinkled between
