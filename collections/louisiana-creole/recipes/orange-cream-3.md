## Ingredients

- juice of four oranges
- peel of one orange
- a pint of water
- half a pound of sugar
- beaten whites of five eggs
- yolks of five eggs
- a cup of cream or sweet milk

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice of four oranges, and put it with the peel of one into a sauce-pan
2. Add to this a pint of water, half a pound of sugar, and the beaten whites of five eggs
3. Mix carefully, place it over a gentle fire, or it will curdle, stir it in one direction until it looks thick
4. Strain it through a gauze sieve
5. Add to it, when cold, the yolks of five eggs, and a cup of cream or sweet milk
6. Set it on the fire until hot enough to cook the eggs, or nearly ready to boil them
7. Take it off, stir until cold, and set it on ice, or freeze it as you choose
