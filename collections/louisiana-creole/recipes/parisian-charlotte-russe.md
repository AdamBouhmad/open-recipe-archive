## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pound of Stale Lady Fingers
- 1/2 Box of Gelatine
- 1 Cup Grated Cocoanut
- 1/2 Pound of Macaroons
- 4 Eggs
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine in cold water for about half an hour.
2. Put one-half of the cream to one side, and whip the rest.
3. Put the remaining pint to boil, and stir into it the sugar and eggs, beaten together till very light.
4. Stir about one minute, and as it thickens add the gelatine, which you will have dissolved either in a little warm water or Sherry Wine.
5. Then add the lady fingers and a teaspoonful of the vanilla; add the macaroons and the grated cocoanut.
6. Turn all this into a bowl or tin basin, place it on ice, and stir continually until it begins to harden.
7. Add the whipped cream, and stir all very carefully.
8. When well-mixed, wet a fancy dish of glass or a mold, turn the Charlotte Russe into it, and stand on the ice to harden.
