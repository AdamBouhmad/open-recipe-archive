## Ingredients

- half a pint of milk or water
- four ounces of butter
- two ounces of sugar
- five ounces of flour
- essence to taste
- three eggs
- a small pinch of soda

## Directions

1. Put half a pint of milk or water into a stew-pan over the fire, with four ounces of butter and two ounces of sugar
2. As soon as these begin to boil, withdraw the stew-pan from the fire, and stir in five ounces of flour
3. Stir well for a few minutes, add essence to taste, and, one by one, three eggs and a small pinch of soda
4. Drop this paste on a baking-sheet in small round balls (the size of a hickory nut), and bake a light brown in a quick oven
