## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- arrowroot or cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon
- 1 quart cold milk or cream
- whites of 4 to 6 eggs

## Directions

1. Thicken a quart of milk with arrowroot or cornstarch, a tablespoonful to the quart
2. Sweeten and let it boil
3. When cold, add a quart of cold milk, or cream if you have it
4. Flavor to your taste
5. When the cream is put into the freezer and is just beginning to thicken, whip up very light the whites of 4 to 6 eggs, and add this to the cream
