## Ingredients

- a sponge cake weighing about a pound
- half a pint of sherry and Madeira (mixed)
- six eggs
- a quart of milk
- half a pound of almonds

## Directions

1. Place a sponge cake weighing about a pound in a glass bowl
2. pour over it half a pint of sherry and Madeira (mixed)
3. Make a rich custard of six eggs and a quart of milk, sweeten to taste, flavor and let it cool
4. Blanch half a pound of almonds
5. stick them in the top of the sponge cake
6. pour over it the custard
