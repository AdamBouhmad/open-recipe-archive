## Ingredients

- eggs (two hard-boiled yolks)
- mustard (a tablespoonful)
- sweet cream (a tablespoonful)
- sweet oil (two tablespoonfuls)
- egg (yolk of a raw)
- salt
- sugar (a spoonful)
- vinegar (one tablespoon and a half)

## Directions

1. Take the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs, rub them to a paste in a bowl with a tablespoonful of mustard and one of sweet cream;
2. add gradually two tablespoonfuls of sweet oil; when well mixed add the yolk of a raw egg, to give the paste a delicate smoothness;
3. a little salt, a spoonful of sugar, and one tablespoon and a half of fine vinegar.
