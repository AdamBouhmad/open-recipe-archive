## Ingredients

- 1/4 lb. of dry sifted flour
- 1/4 lb. of fresh butter washed in rose water
- 1/4 lb. of sifted loaf sugar
- the yolk of an egg beaten with a tablespoonful of cream

## Directions

1. Mix the flour and sugar together
2. Rub in the butter
3. Add the egg yolk and cream mixture
4. Make it into a paste
5. Roll and cut it into small round cakes
6. Bake upon a floured tin
