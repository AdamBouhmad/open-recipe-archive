## Ingredients

- 6 Veal Cutlets
- 1 Egg
- 1 Cup of Bread Crumbs
- Parsley Sprigs
- Melted Butter or Sauce a la Ravigotte

## Directions

1. Have the cutlets as thin as possible.
2. Season well, after having beat with the rolling-pin, and roll in a well-beaten egg and then in bread crumbs.
3. Pat lightly with the hands and brush with melted butter.
4. Place on a double broiler and broil on a very slow fire till no blood exudes.
5. Serve with drawn butter sauce poured over or a Sauce a la Ravigotte.
6. Garnish the dish nicely with parsley sprigs.
