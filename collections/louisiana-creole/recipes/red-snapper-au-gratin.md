## Ingredients

- 1 Fine 3-Pound Red Snapper
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Large Onion
- 3 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Cup of Stale Bread Crumbs
- 3/4 Tea Cup of Water
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Select a fine, large fish, clean and wash thoroughly.
2. Make a dressing by taking one cup of stale bread, wet and squeezed of all water; one large onion and three sprigs of parsley.
3. Chop the onions and parsley fine and mix with the bread crumbs and fry in a spoonful of butter, seasoning well with salt and pepper.
4. Stuff the fish and sew up with a soft thread.
5. Rub the fish thoroughly with salt and pepper and butter.
6. Put small pieces of butter all over the fish and add a few pieces on the bottom of the baking pan.
7. Pour in water to the depth of two inches, cover the pan and bake on the outside of the oven, about an hour and a half.
