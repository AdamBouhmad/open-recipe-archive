## Ingredients

- fruit cake batter
- two pounds of seedless raisins
- one pound of citron
- one pound of blanched and cut almonds
- one pound of grated cocoanut
- a cup of sweet milk
- wine, brandy and nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make the batter the same as for fruit cake
2. instead of the same quantity of fruit, add two pounds of seedless raisins, one pound of citron, one of blanched and cut almonds, and one pound of grated cocoanut
3. Pour over the cocoanut a cup of sweet milk
4. Add the wine, brandy and nutmeg
