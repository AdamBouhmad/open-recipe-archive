## Ingredients

- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Indian Meal
- 1 Spoon of White Flour
- 3 Cups of Milk
- 1¼ Cups of Boiling Water
- A Pinch of Salt

## Directions

1. Bring the flour to a paste with a little cold water, and also scald the meal with a little hot water.
2. Have the water boiling in the proportion given above; add the meal, and then add the flour, stirring constantly.
3. Let it boil for about twenty minutes, and add the pinch of salt and the milk, stirring almost constantly.
4. Let all cook for ten minutes more, stirring often.
5. Serve while hot, with sugar and milk.
