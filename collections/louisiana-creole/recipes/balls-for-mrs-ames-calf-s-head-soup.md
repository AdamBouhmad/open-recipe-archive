## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound beef or veal
- 2 slices of salt pork
- lights and liver
- 6 small onions
- 1.5 large tablespoonfuls of sweet marjoram
- 1 nutmeg
- 1 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 desertspoon powdered mace
- 1 desertspoon cloves
- 8 eggs
- 3 sifted crackers
- 1/4 gill of wine
- butter the size of an egg
- 2 tablespoons salt
- flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Put beef or veal in chopping tray with 2 slices of salt pork an inch thick
2. Add some of the lights and liver, 6 small onions, 1.5 large tablespoonfuls of sweet marjoram, 1 nutmeg, 1 teaspoon black pepper, 1 desertspoon powdered mace, same of cloves, 8 eggs, 3 sifted crackers, 1/4 gill of wine, butter the size of an egg, and 2 tablespoons salt
3. Mix all well together
4. Roll the balls in flour
5. Fry slowly in lard
