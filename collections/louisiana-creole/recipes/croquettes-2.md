## Ingredients

- turkey, beef, chicken, or sweet bread
- grated bread crumbs
- grated lemon peel
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- hard boiled egg yolk
- milk
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Chop the meat very fine
2. Add grated bread crumbs
3. Season with a little grated lemon peel, salt, pepper, a very little nutmeg, and yolk of a hard boiled egg
4. Moisten the whole with enough milk to make the ingredients adhere
5. Form into cones or pear-shaped balls
6. Dip each into a batter of eggs, well beaten
7. Roll in bread crumbs
8. Fry brown in butter
9. Serve with fried parsley
