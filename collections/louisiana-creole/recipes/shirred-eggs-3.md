## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Break the eggs into a thin dish, in which they are to be served, having first buttered the bottom of the dish or pan.
2. Sprinkle them with salt and pepper.
3. Pour over a little melted butter.
4. Place in a quick oven, and let them bake until the yolks are set.
5. Serve in the dish in which they have been cooked.
