## Ingredients

- 6 Large Apples
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 4 Cloves
- 1 Stick Cinnamon
- 1 Cup Water
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Cut the apples into pieces and peel
2. Let them boil till mashed into a jelly, stirring frequently to prevent burning
3. Add the ground cloves and the stick of cinnamon, ground fine
4. Let them boil at least three-quarters of an hour, mashing as they become tender
5. Take off the fire and press them through a coarse sieve
6. Add sugar to taste and add the butter
7. Set all back on the fire and let it simmer gently for five minutes longer
8. Set to cool in a dish and serve with Roast Pork or Roast Goose
