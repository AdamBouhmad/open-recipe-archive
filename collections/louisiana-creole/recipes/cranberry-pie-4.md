## Ingredients

- 1½ Pints of Cranberries
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- Plain Pie Crust

## Directions

1. Cook the cranberries as for Cranberry Sauce
2. Do not strain the berries
3. Prepare the pie crust
4. Bake the lower crust, and fill with the fruit, and proceed as in general directions for making pies
