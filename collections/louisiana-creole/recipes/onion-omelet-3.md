## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 large onion minced very fine

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs as for a plain omelet
2. Stew the onions in the butter till quite tender
3. Stir in the omelet once
4. Let it cook as in a plain omelet
5. Roll in folds and serve hot
