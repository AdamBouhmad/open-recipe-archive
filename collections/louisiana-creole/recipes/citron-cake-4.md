## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- one pound of sugar
- one pound of flour
- eight eggs
- one pound of blanched almonds
- half a pound of sliced citron
- flour
- half teaspoonful of soda
- sweet milk
- or one tablespoonful of yeast powder

## Directions

1. Add to the batter one pound of blanched almonds, cut small, and half a pound of sliced citron dredged with flour
2. beat all up well
3. Beat in a half teaspoonful of soda, moistened with sweet milk, or if preferred, a tablespoonful of yeast powder, rubbed in the flour before mixing
4. Beat this mixture well, and bake it in a cake-pan with buttered paper in the bottom
5. cover the top of the cake with something to protect it from the heat of the stove, until the bottom is nearly done
