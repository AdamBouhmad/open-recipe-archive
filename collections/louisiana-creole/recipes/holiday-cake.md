## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of flour
- 6 ounces of butter
- 1 ounce of the best powdered ginger
- 1 ounce of carraway seeds ground
- 1 ounce of sweet pepper
- 2 lbs. of treacle
- 1 lb. of sugar
- a large teaspoonful of soda mixed in boiling water

## Directions

1. melt butter and treacle
2. mix with flour and other ingredients
3. bake in a slow oven for 2½ hours
