## Ingredients

- 3 Ounces of Grated Chocolate
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Quart of Milk
- 3/4 Cup of Powdered Sugar
- 3/4 Cup of Powdered Crackers
- 3/4 Cup of Butter
- 1 Pinch of Salt

## Directions

1. Soak the cracker crumbs in water for a few minutes, to dissolve them, and then squeeze out thoroughly.
2. Dissolve the chocolate in a little milk, and add the other ingredients.
3. Pour all into a buttered dish, and let bake for twenty minutes.
4. Then take out, and let it cool slightly.
5. Spread over the top a meringue made of one cup of powdered sugar and the whites of three eggs.
6. Set back in the oven to brown slightly for two minutes, and serve cold.
