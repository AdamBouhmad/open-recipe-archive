## Ingredients

- 50 or 100 Large, Fine Oysters
- 12 Blades of Mace
- 1 Large Tablespoonful of Allspice
- 1 Level Tablespoonful of Cloves
- 1 Pepper
- 2 Sliced Lemons
- 1/2 Pint of White Vinegar
- Salt to Taste
- 1/2 Dozen Pepper Corns
- White wine

## Directions

1. Boil the oysters in their own liquor until the edges begin to ruffle.
2. Then take a half pint of white wine
