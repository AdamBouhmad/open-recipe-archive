## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Brussels Sprouts
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chopped Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the Brussels Sprouts carefully
2. After washing, drain thoroughly and boil them in salted water for ten minutes
3. Take out of the hot water, drain and put into cold water
4. Drain again and put them in a saucepan with two tablespoonfuls of butter
5. Season according to taste with salt and pepper
6. Add a teaspoonful of chopped parsley
7. Let them cook slightly for ten minutes more, or less if very tender
8. Serve hot
