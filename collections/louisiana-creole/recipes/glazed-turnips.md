## Ingredients

- 6 Turnips of Uniform Size
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Water
- 1 Large Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt to Taste
- 1 Tablespoonful of flour, mixed in water

## Directions

1. Select fine, small and tender turnips of uniform size.
2. Top the heads and the stems, and remove the skins, but not too closely, lest they should break up when boiling.
3. Take a frying pan large enough for the turnips to lie in it, side by side.
4. Put the butter in first, and when melted add the turnips.
5. Sprinkle with the sugar and water, and season with salt to taste.
6. Set on the back of the stove, where they can simmer gently for an hour.
7. When nearly done, and tender all through, add a tablespoonful of flour, mixed in water, blended well.
8. Set in the oven, with a paper on top.
9. Let them stand for about half an hour and use as a garnish for beef, veal, etc.
