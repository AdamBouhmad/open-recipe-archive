## Ingredients

- 6 Tongues
- 1 Onion
- 1/4 of a Small Carrot, Cut Fine
- 1/4 of a Small Turnip, Cut Fine
- 1 Pint of Broth or Water
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 2 Sprigs of Thyme and Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Clean the tongues; wash well and boil in clear water for an hour and a half
2. Throw them into cold water and remove the skins
3. Cut the vegetables fine, and put them with the butter into a saucepan
4. Add a pint of broth or water, and then add the finely-minced herbs
5. Add the tongues and let them simmer gently for two hours
6. Serve hot, with the gravy poured over
