## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Sweet Cream
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered Sugar
- 6 Drops of Jamaica Rum

## Directions

1. Always set the cream on ice for several hours before using
2. Sweeten it to taste, and add a few drops of Jamaica rum
3. Have ready a second bowl, beat the cream, or, rather, whip it, as you would in beating eggs to a froth, with clear, steady strokes, using an egg beater, or, if you have a small crank churn, which is better, use it, pressing the dasher up and down very hard, and removing the cream as it rises around the dashboard
4. In the same way remove the cream as it rises, while beating with the egg beater, putting the whipped portion into the second bowl, and continuing the process of whipping until all the cream is drained
5. Set on ice, and it will keep cool, fresh and sweet and firm
