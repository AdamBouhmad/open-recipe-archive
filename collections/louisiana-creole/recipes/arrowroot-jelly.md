## Ingredients

- 1 dessertspoonful of arrowroot
- cold water
- 1 pint of boiling water
- sugar
- port or sherry wine

## Directions

1. To 1 dessertspoonful of arrowroot add as much cold water as will make it into a soft paste
2. Pour on 1 pint of boiling water
3. Stir it briskly for a few minutes until it becomes a clean, smooth jelly
4. Sweeten to the taste
5. Season with port or sherry wine
