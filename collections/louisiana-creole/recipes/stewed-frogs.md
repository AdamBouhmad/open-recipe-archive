## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Frog Legs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1/2 Pint of Water
- 1/2 Pint of Oyster Water
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Sprig of Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig of Sweet Marjoram
- 10 Allspice
- 1 Clove
- The Yolk of an Egg
- Croutons
- 2 Dozen Oysters
- Salt and Pepper
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Take the legs of one dozen frogs and prepare the same as for frying.
2. Take a tablespoonful of butter and put in a frying pan.
3. When it begins to melt, add a tablespoonful of flour and stir constantly.
4. When it begins to brown nicely, add a half pint of water and a pint of oyster water.
5. Throw in the Frog legs as it begins to boil, and add salt and pepper, a little Cayenne, a sprig of thyme, bay leaf and sweet marjoram, eight or ten allspice, one clove.
6. Let it simmer about fifteen minutes and take off the fire.
7. Have ready the yolk of a beaten egg and add, blending well, and serve immediately with garnishes of Croutons, and fried in a little butter, with oysters laid upon them.
