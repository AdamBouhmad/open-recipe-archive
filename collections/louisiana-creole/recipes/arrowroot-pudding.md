## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- arrowroot
- 6 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 1 nutmeg
- grated lemon peel
- paste
- citron

## Directions

1. Boil 1 quart of milk and make it into a thin batter with arrowroot
2. add 6 eggs, 1/2 lb. of butter, the same of sugar, 1 nutmeg and a little grated lemon peel
3. put a nice paste in the dish, and bake it nicely
4. when done, sift sugar over it, and stick slips of citron all over the top
