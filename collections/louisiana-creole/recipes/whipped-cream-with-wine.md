## Ingredients

- whites of three eggs
- one pint of cream
- four tablespoonfuls of sweet wine
- four spoonfuls of sugar
- bright jelly or light-colored marmalade
- sponge cake

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of three eggs to a froth
2. Add one pint of cream, four tablespoonfuls of sweet wine, and four spoonfuls of sugar
3. Put bright jelly, or light-colored marmalade in spots among the cream and serve sponge cake with it
