## Ingredients

- beef
- veal brisket
- okra pods
- onion
- red pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Chop a pound of beef and half a pound of veal brisket into squares an inch thick; slice three dozen okra pods, one onion, a pod of red pepper, and fry all together.
2. When brown pour in half a gallon of water; add more as required.
