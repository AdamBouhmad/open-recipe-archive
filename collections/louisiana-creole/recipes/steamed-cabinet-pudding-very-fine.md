## Ingredients

- butter
- brioche, or any kind of cold sweet roll, or Sally Lunn
- sponge cake, or macaroons
- currants, or seedless raisins
- chopped citron, or other dried fruit
- six yolks of eggs
- a pint of milk or cream
- six ounces of sugar
- a glass of brandy
- the grated rind of a lemon
- extract of lemon
- wine or hard butter sauce

## Directions

1. Butter a pudding mould, and line it with brioche, or any kind of cold sweet roll, or Sally Lunn, that has been left over.
2. Fill the mould with layers of sponge cake, or macaroons, alternately with currants, or seedless raisins, chopped citron, or other dried fruit.
3. Make a boiled custard of six yolks of eggs, a pint of milk or cream, six ounces of sugar, a glass of brandy, and the grated rind of a lemon.
4. Moisten the macaroons with extract of lemon, and then pour over the custard.
5. Steam the pudding.
6. Serve with wine or hard butter sauce beaten up with a little wine.
