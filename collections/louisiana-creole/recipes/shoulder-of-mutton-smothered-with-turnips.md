## Ingredients

- 1 Shoulder of Mutton
- 1 Carrot
- 1 Onion
- 1/2 Stalk of Celery
- 4 Cloves
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 6 Turnips
- 1 Quart of Boiling Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. If the mutton does not appear very tender the process of smothering it will make it so. It is well to beat the leg well with a rolling pin, and you will be sure of good and tender eating.
2. Season well.
3. Slice an onion and one carrot very fine; chop fine a half stalk of celery, and put these, with the shoulder of mutton, into a deep baking pot.
4. Cover well and let the mutton juice permeate the vegetables and brown them.
5. Then add the minced bay leaves and cloves; cover and let these brown, and after ten minutes add one quart of boiling water.
6. Season well again and set on a steady fire, allowing fifteen minutes to every pound.
7. An hour before serving add six whole turnips, which have been peeled and parboiled, and let these remain smothered with the mutton.
8. Serve with the turnips as a garnish.
