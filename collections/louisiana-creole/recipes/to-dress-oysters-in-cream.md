## Ingredients

- 1 quart oysters
- half pint sweet cream
- tablespoonful butter
- salt
- black pepper
- red pepper
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. After draining the oysters, add half a pint of sweet cream, a tablespoonful of butter, salt and black and red pepper
2. Place the whole in an a la braise dish
3. When nearly done, thicken it with wheat flour and serve it up hot
