## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Pecans
- The White of an Egg
- ½ Pound of Finest White Confectionery Sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the pecans in halves, being careful not to break the meat.
2. Take the white of an egg and beat well with its weight in water till it forms a cream.
3. Work in with your fingers the finest white confectionery sugar till it forms a smooth paste.
4. Take a small piece of this paste, roll it, and put it between two halves of the pecans, and then lightly roll in the paste, flattening the pecan somewhat.
5. The coating outside must be very, very light, so that the delicate brown of the pecan meat shows through.
6. Set the pecans to dry, and serve on dainty china saucers, setting a saucer to each guest.
