## Ingredients

- 6 potatoes
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 eggs
- minced parsley
- a half onion minced very fine
- powdered bread crumbs
- boiling lard

## Directions

1. Boil the potatoes, peel and mash very fine.
2. Add the butter and minced parsley, and a half onion minced very fine.
3. Add the yolks of the eggs, beaten very light, and then form the potatoes into balls and throw them for two minutes into boiling water.
4. Take out, brush with the whites of the eggs, roll lightly in powdered bread crumbs, and fry in boiling lard.
5. Drain and serve with fish, meat or any sauce.
