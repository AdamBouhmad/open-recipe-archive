## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- a teaspoonful of salt
- 1 gill of water
- 1 gill of milk
- 1 gill of good yeast

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a pan, with salt
2. Warm together the water and milk with the yeast
3. Mix into the liquid enough flour to make a thin batter
4. Stir it till quite smooth and free from lumps
5. Put a handful of flour over the top, and set it in a warm place to rise for 2 hours or more
6. When quite light, make it into a dough, with some more milk and water
7. Knead it well for 10 minutes
8. Mould it into rolls, or round balls, and bake it
