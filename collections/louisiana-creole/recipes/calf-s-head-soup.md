## Ingredients

- calf's head with eyes, teeth, and nose bone removed
- calf's liver, lights, feet and heart
- water
- salt
- 6 very small onions
- 1 coffee cup of very finely sifted sweet marjoram
- 1.5 teaspoons black pepper
- 1.5 teaspoons red pepper
- 2 tablespoons salt
- small one of mace (powdered)
- 2 nutmegs
- 1 tablespoon of cloves
- 3 tablespoons of butter
- 8 tablespoons of flour
- 4 pounds of crackers
- 1 pint wine
- 1 bottle of claret
- 6 or 8 eggs
- fresh lemon

## Directions

1. Clean the calf's head thoroughly; remove the eyes, teeth, and nose bone, making the skin smooth and white as possible
2. Clean the liver, lights, feet and heart
3. Put all these into an iron pot
4. Cover with water and add salt enough to make the scum rise, about 2 tablespoonfuls
5. Boil all until the meat cleaves from the skull bone, and the liver is tender to the touch of the fork
6. Put the meat in a separate pot, cover with water and boil until the meat slips from the bone
7. Boil the feet separately as they require so much more boiling than the head
8. Fish out the head with a long skimmer, then pour out the liquor
9. Wash the pot and strain the whole through a hair sieve into the pot
10. Separate the head from the bone, cut it into pieces of proper size to give each person
11. Divide the feet and liver also, and put into the strained liquor with 6 very small onions minced very fine, 1 coffee cup of very finely sifted sweet marjoram, 1.5 teaspoons black pepper, 1.5 teaspoons red pepper, 2 tablespoons salt, small one of mace (powdered), 2 nutmegs, 1 tablespoon of cloves, 3 tablespoons of butter mixed with 8 of flour (nicely browned), 4 pounds of crackers, 1 pint wine, and a bottle of claret
12. Let the whole simmer slowly till dinner time
13. 6 or 8 eggs cut up and put in as it goes to table, with small pieces of fresh lemon
