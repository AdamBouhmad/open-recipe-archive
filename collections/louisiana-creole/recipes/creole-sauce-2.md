## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful Worcestershire sauce
- ½ teaspoonful cayenne pepper
- 1 ounce butter
- 2 tomatoes
- 1 tablespoonful onions
- 1 tablespoonful parsley

## Directions

1. Chop the tomatoes, onions and parsley very fine
2. Boil all together 10 minutes
3. Strain
