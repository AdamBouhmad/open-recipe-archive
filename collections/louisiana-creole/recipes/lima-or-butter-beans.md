## Ingredients

- lima beans
- cold water
- boiling water
- butter
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Shell them
2. Lay them in cold water for an hour or so before cooking
3. When ready to cook, put them in a stew pan in boiling water enough to cover them
4. Let them boil fast and keep them covered while cooking
5. Examine them in an hour, and if soft, pour off nearly all the water
6. Stir in a lump of butter, some pepper and salt
