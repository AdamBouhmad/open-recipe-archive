## Ingredients

- a quart of milk
- a cup of maizena or corn starch
- cold water
- a cup of white sugar
- yolks of four eggs
- jelly or jam
- whites of four eggs
- a cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Let a quart of milk be set on to boil
2. While it is getting hot, mix a cup of maizena or corn starch with enough cold water to form it into a thick batter
3. Add to this a cup of white sugar and the yolks of four eggs
4. Take the milk off and stir eggs, maizena, and sugar, into the milk
5. Beat all together a few minutes
6. Pour the mixture into a baking dish and bake it lightly about ten minutes, or long enough only to cook the eggs
7. Take the pudding out, and while hot put over it a layer of jelly or jam
8. Beat up the whites of the eggs with a cup of sugar, put this over the jelly and brown
