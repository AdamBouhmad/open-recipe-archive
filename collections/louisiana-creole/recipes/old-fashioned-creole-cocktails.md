## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of good old Bourbon, Cognac, gin, sherry or vermouth
- 2 lumps of loaf sugar
- A piece of ice about the size of an egg
- A bit of lemon peel
- The juice of 1 orange
- 1 tablespoonful of lemon juice
- Seltzer
- Freshly-gathered mint
- A few cherries

## Directions

1. Take a large glass, put the lump sugar in, and mix with about three tablespoonfuls of seltzer, dissolving the sugar well.
2. Add the lemon peel and the juices of the orange and lemon.
3. Add one tablespoonful of good old Bourbon or Sherry or any of the above-mentioned liqueurs, according to the cocktail desired.
4. Stir well, and serve with delicate sprigs of freshly-gathered mint on top.
