## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of eel
- 1 sprig of thyme
- 1 teaspoonful of black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- A drawn butter sauce, or sauce a la Tartare

## Directions

1. Clean and skin the eel as for frying.
2. Cut in two-inch pieces and boil till disgorged.
3. Then roll in oil and pepper and salt.
4. Mix well and broil quickly over a clear fire.
5. Serve with melted or drawn butter sauce and pickles, or a sauce a la Tartare.
