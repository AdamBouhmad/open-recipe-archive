## Ingredients

- ducks
- 1 pint of good gravy
- salt
- cayenne
- 2 teaspoonfuls of fine ground rice
- 1 glass of port wine

## Directions

1. Cut the ducks into joints and lay them into a stewpan with a pint of good gravy
2. Let it come to a boil
3. As the scum rises, remove it
4. Season with salt and cayenne
5. Let it stew gently three-quarters of an hour
6. Mix smoothly 2 teaspoonfuls of fine ground rice with a glass of port wine
7. Stir it into the gravy
8. Let it have 7 or 8 minutes to amalgamate with the gravy
9. Dish and send it to table very hot
