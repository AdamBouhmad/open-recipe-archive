## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Fat Pork
- 2 Pounds of Lean Pork
- 1 Pound of Inner Lining of Stomach of Hog
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 3 Bay Leaves
- 2 Large Onions
- 1 Tablespoonful Each of Salt and Pepper
- 1 Teaspoonful of Cayenne
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chili Pepper
- 1/4 Teaspoonful Each of Mace, Cloves and Allspice, ground fine
- 1 Tablespoonful Each of Minced Thyme, Sweet Marjoram and Parsley

## Directions

1. Select the largest intestines of the hog, wash clean, disgorge and thoroughly cleanse
2. Let soak for twenty four hours in fresh water, changing the water frequently
3. Drain and dry well
4. Cut them into threadlike pieces of about one inch in length
5. Hash the pork, lean and fat, together
6. Mix thoroughly with the threads of intestines or inner stomach of the hog
7. Season highly with the salt, pepper and Cayenne and Chili pepper
8. Mince the onion and garlic and herbs as fine as possible and add to the meat
9. Add the ground spices and mix and hash all together very fine
10. Take six or eight of the large intestines that have been thoroughly soaked and disgorged
11. Fill these casings with the preparation, after scalding and drying the casings thoroughly
12. Tie into the desired lengths
13. Boil in an aromatic water with an herb bouquet, or in milk
14. Broil, or bake in the oven for eight or ten minutes
