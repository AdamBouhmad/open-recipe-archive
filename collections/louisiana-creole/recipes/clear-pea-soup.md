## Ingredients

- two quarts of good beef or veal soup stock
- a quart of young green peas
- heads of lettuce
- a sprig of mint
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Into this put a quart of young green peas, heads of lettuce, and a sprig of mint
2. Add salt and pepper to taste
