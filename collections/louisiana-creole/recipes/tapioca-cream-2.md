## Ingredients

- two teaspoonfuls of tapioca
- cold water
- a quart of milk
- yolks of three eggs
- a cup and a half of sugar
- whites of three eggs

## Directions

1. Soak two teaspoonfuls of tapioca for two hours in a little cold water
2. Boil a quart of milk, and to it add the tapioca, the yolks of three eggs, well beaten with a cup and a half of sugar
3. Give it one boil, and set it away to cool; do not boil it long, or the eggs will curdle
4. Beat the whites of the eggs, and put them on top, or boil them in a little of the milk and put it on the cream
5. Set it on ice until wanted
