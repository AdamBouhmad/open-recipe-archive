## Ingredients

- smoked ham
- spices
- sweet herbs

## Directions

1. Soak your ham all night, scrape it nicely, and boil it half an hour to make the skin tender.
2. Take it from the pot, gash it all over, introduce as much of the pounded spices as the incisions will hold, and then close the skin over the gashes and boil in the same manner, with vegetables thrown in, as in recipe for boiled ham.
