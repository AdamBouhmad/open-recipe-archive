## Ingredients

- a nice puff paste
- pie mixture
- butter

## Directions

1. Line a pie dish with a nice puff paste, rolled to twice the thickness of a dollar piece
2. Put in the pie mixture half an inch deep, and spread it to within a finger width of the edge
3. roll out a puff paste crust, turn a plate the size of the one on which the pie is made on to it
4. with a knife cut the paste around the edge of the plate
5. then take the plate off, make three small incisions with the end of the knife on each side of the middle
6. take it carefully up and cover the pie with it
7. press it lightly with the finger against the bottom crust
8. put it in a quick oven for three-quarters of an hour
