## Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful Flour
- 8 Shallots, Greens and White
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley
- juice of 1/2 of a Lemon
- 1/2 Can Mushrooms
- Consommé
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. If mushrooms are used, make a white sauce, that is, let the butter and flour blend without browning
2. Add a half cup of consommé and a half can of chopped mushrooms, the white of the shallot (chopped very fine) and the juice of half a lemon
3. If a brown sauce, add the shallots to the butter and flour, which you will have browned, using the chopped white and green of the shallots
4. Then add a half pint of consommé, and let it simmer for about an hour, and add the juice of a lemon and serve
5. The white sauce is used for fish, the brown for meats
6. Always season to taste
