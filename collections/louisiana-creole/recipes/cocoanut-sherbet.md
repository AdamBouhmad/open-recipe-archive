## Ingredients

- 2 cocoanuts
- 1/2 lb. best white sugar
- 2 quarts water

## Directions

1. After taking the milk from the nuts, break and take off the dark rind
2. Grate the white meat, and pour over it 2 quarts of water
3. Let it stand awhile
4. Strain through a strong piece of muslin, or use a fruit press, so that you abstract all the richness
5. Add the milk taken from the nuts, and the sugar
6. It is then ready for the freezer
