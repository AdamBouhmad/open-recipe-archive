## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Flour
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Cake of Compressed Yeast
- 4 Eggs
- 1/2 Pound of Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Scald the milk and add the butter.
2. When it grows lukewarm, add the yeast, dissolved in about three tablespoonfuls of warm water.
3. Add the salt and flour, and beat well for about ten minutes.
4. Then set in a warm place over night.
5. In the morning beat the yolks of the eggs well, and then the whites to a stiff froth; add the yolks and beat well, and then add the whites, and mix all thoroughly.
6. Beat till very light, let it stand for about a quarter of an hour, and then bake in a buttered pan for about twenty minutes in a quick oven.
