## Ingredients

- 1/4 Pound of Butter
- 3/4 Pound of Sugar
- 3 Pints of Flour
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Ground Ginger
- 1 Teaspoonful Ground Cinnamon
- 1 Pint of Sweet Milk or Sour Cream
- 1 Teaspoonful of Soda or Baking Powder
- eggs
- molasses

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream, and then add the sugar.
2. Beat till very light, and then beat the yolks of the eggs till creamy and add to the butter and sugar.
3. Beat very light.
4. Now add the molasses and stir well, mixing thoroughly.
5. Then add the milk and mix well into the flour, with which you will have sifted the baking powder, and beat till very smooth.
6. Then add the ground ginger and cinnamon; mix well.
7. Finally add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth; mix well and pour into two well-greased, shallow tins.
8. Bake in a moderate oven for forty minutes, using the broom wisp test before taking out of the oven.
9. Sprinkle with white sugar and serve, either hot or cold.
10. If soda is used, dissolve it in a tablespoonful of boiling water and add to the molasses; then add to the butter and sugar before adding the flour and milk.
