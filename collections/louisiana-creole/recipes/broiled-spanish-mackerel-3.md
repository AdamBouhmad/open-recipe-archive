## Ingredients

- A Spanish Mackerel, or 3 Pounds Weight
- 1 Tablespoonful of Olive Oil
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1 Sliced Lemon
- Parsley to Garnish
- Sauce à la Maître d'Hôtel
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as the Pompano, serving whole
2. Where the fish is large, split downwards, in the middle of the back, and broil in the same manner as the Pompano
3. Place on a dish garnished with parsley and slices of lemon
4. Serve with a Sauce à la Maître d'Hôtel
