## Ingredients

- 2 nice white cabbages
- salt
- water
- weak vinegar
- turmeric
- good cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Take 2 nice white cabbages and quarter them
2. If very large more than quarter them
3. Put them in salt and water to remain 24 hours
4. Boil them till tender in weak vinegar with a little turmeric in it
5. Put them in jars and pour over them good cold vinegar
6. Tie them up
