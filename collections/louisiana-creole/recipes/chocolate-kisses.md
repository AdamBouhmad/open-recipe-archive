## Ingredients

- one-half pound of sugar
- one ounce of finely-powdered chocolate
- whites of four eggs

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and chocolate together
2. Mix it with the whites of four eggs well beaten
3. Drop on buttered paper
4. Bake
