## Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful Flour
- The Juice of 1/2 a Lemon
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley
- 1 Pint of Consommé
- yolk of one egg, well beaten

## Directions

1. Put the butter and the flour in the saucepan and let them blend without burning
2. Mix well over a slow fire, and add one pint of consommé
3. Add the juice of half a lemon and the chopped parsley, and let all boil about fifteen minutes
4. When it reaches this point take off the stove and add the yolk of one egg, well beaten
5. Mix well, stirring round, and serve with boiled fish, etc.
6. Never add egg while the sauce is on the fire, as it will curdle immediately
