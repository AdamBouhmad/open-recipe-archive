## Ingredients

- 8 Grains of Tartaric Acid
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 20 Drops of Lemon Essence
- Sufficient Gum Tragacanth to Make a Paste

## Directions

1. Take eight grains of Tartaric Acid, one pound of sugar, twenty drops of Lemon Essence, and make a paste with all, adding a sufficient quantity of mucilage or Gum Tragacanth to make a paste
2. Cut out into tablets and dry in the open air
