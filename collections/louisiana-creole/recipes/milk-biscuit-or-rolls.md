## Ingredients

- a pint of milk
- half a pound of butter
- nearly two quarts of flour
- two eggs
- a cup of yeast

## Directions

1. Warm a pint of milk and half a pound of butter
2. Pour this into nearly two quarts of flour
3. Add two eggs and a cup of yeast
4. Knead it very well
5. Make into round balls
6. Flatten each one on the palm of your hand and prick it with a fork
7. Bake
