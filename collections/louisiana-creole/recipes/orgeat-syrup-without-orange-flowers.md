## Ingredients

- A pound of sugar to every pint of water
- A gill of orange water, or rose water, to every four pounds of sugar used
- Two tablespoonfuls of pure essence of bitter almonds to every four pounds of sugar used

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of a pound of sugar to every pint of water
2. Boil this a few minutes, skim it clear
3. When cold, to every four pounds of sugar used, allow a gill of orange water, or rose water, and two tablespoonfuls of pure essence of bitter almonds
4. Serve it in iced water
