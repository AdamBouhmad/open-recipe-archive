## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. of beef suet
- a bottle of the best olive oil

## Directions

1. Render 6 lbs. of beef suet
2. strain it through a hair sieve into a clean pan
3. to this add a bottle of the best olive oil
4. stir it well and then put into jars, each containing about 3 lbs.
5. cover tightly, and keep in a very cool place
