## Ingredients

- fine fat goose
- four onions
- sage leaves dried and powdered
- cupful of stale white bread crumbs
- teaspoon of black pepper
- little cayenne
- teaspoon of salt
- cup of milk or beef water
- butter
- apple sauce

## Directions

1. Draw a fine fat goose
2. Peel four onions and boil them ten minutes in plenty of water to take from them the strong taste
3. When the onions have boiled take them from the fire, chop fine, and add to them a large spoonful of sage leaves dried and powdered
4. Add a cupful of stale white bread crumbs, a teaspoon of black pepper, a little cayenne, and a teaspoon of salt
5. Mix all together with a cup of milk or beef water, and stuff the goose with it
6. Put it in the oven and brown it nicely
7. Baste often with butter
8. When done dish it with its own rich brown gravy, and send to table with a boat of apple sauce
