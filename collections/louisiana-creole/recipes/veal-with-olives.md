## Ingredients

- A Flank of Veal
- 1 Slice of Cold Boiled Ham
- 1 Grated Onion
- 1 Hard-Boiled Egg
- 1 Lemon
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Dozen Stoned Olives
- Chopped Herbs
- Bread Crumbs
- Soup Broth

## Directions

1. Get a flank of veal and cut it into strips of about four inches in length and four in width
2. Cut off sufficient to make a half cup, and chop this fine, with a slice of cold boiled ham
3. Make a mince meat, adding chopped herbs, according to taste, 1 grated onion, 1 hard-boiled egg, the juice of 1 lemon and a tablespoonful of butter, with a half cup of bread crumbs
4. Take the strips of veal, stuff them nicely with this mixture and roll over the ends, tying to prevent the farcie from escaping
5. Place a tablespoonful of butter in a frying pan, and, when it heats, add the rolls of veal
6. Let them fry for ten minutes, turning, and then add soup broth sufficient to cover them
7. Cover closely and set back on the stove, and let them simmer steadily but slowly for an hour longer
8. Place in a hot dish, pour the gravy over, seasoning highly; add about two dozen stoned olives, and pour over the rolls and serve
