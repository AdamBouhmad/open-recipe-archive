## Ingredients

- one quart of sifted flour
- three teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- one of soda
- a little salt
- two large spoonfuls of shortening
- warm water or milk

## Directions

1. To one quart of sifted flour, add three teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar and one of soda mixed in the flour, and a little salt
2. Add two large spoonfuls of shortening
3. Mix soft with warm water or milk
4. Bake
