## Ingredients

- 3 Eggs
- 1 Cup Cream
- Grated Horseradish
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1/2 Pint of Consomme or Broth

## Directions

1. Grate the horseradish in sufficient quantity for use, and place it in a saucepan with the boiling stock.
2. Let it boil about ten minutes, or less, until tender.
3. Season to taste.
4. In the meantime rub the eggs in a bowl with the cream, beating and mixing thoroughly.
5. Add these to the horseradish, stirring constantly, but do not let the sauce boil, or the horseradish will curdle.
6. Serve with roast meats or with baked fish.
