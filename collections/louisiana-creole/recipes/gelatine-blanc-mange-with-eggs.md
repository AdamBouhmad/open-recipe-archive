## Ingredients

- one quart of new milk
- half a box of gelatine
- cold water
- one cup of sugar
- four eggs
- vanilla or lemon extract

## Directions

1. Set one quart of new milk on to boil
2. Soak half a box of gelatine in cold water for ten or fifteen minutes
3. Stir the soaked gelatine into the boiling milk
4. When the gelatine is dissolved, stir into the milk one cup of sugar
5. Take the jelly from the fire, and while the mixture is very hot, stir in four eggs
6. Season with vanilla or lemon extract
7. Pour into moulds and eat with cream
