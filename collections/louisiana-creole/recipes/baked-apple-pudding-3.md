## Ingredients

- 1 pint of stewed apples
- 1 pint of cream or 2 ounces of butter
- 1 lb. of powdered sugar
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1 tablespoonful of rose water
- 1 teaspoonfal of grated lemon peel

## Directions

1. Stew apples in as little water as possible, and not long enough for the pieces to break and lose their shape
2. When cold, mix them with 1 pint of cream or 2 ounces of butter, 1 lb. of powdered sugar, 1 grated nutmeg, 1 tablespoonful of rose water, 1 teaspoonfal of grated lemon peel
