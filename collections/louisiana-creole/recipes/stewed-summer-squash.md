## Ingredients

- summer squash
- butter
- gill of cream
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Gather them while young and tender
2. Peel, cut them up, take out the seeds
3. Put them on to boil
4. Let them cook rapidly until very tender
5. Drain them well in a colander
6. Mash with a wooden spoon
7. Put this pulp in a stew pan with a small piece of butter, a gill of cream, and a little pepper and salt
8. Cook this, and stir constantly until the squash is dry
9. Serve very hot
