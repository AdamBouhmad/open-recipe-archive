## Ingredients

- 9 Eggs
- 1 Pound of Pulverized Sugar
- 3/4 Pound of Flour
- The Juice of 1 Orange or 1 Tablespoonful of Extract
- 1 Cup of Orange Marmalade
- Transparent Icing to Garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare the cake drops as directed above, with sponge or cup cake batter
2. Place thin slices of oranges cooked in syrup on the Bouchés
3. Gloss them over with Transparent Icing
4. Flavor the batter of these Bouchés with the zest of an orange or with Curaçoa Liqueur
