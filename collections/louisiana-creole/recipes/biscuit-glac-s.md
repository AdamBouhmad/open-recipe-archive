## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream
- The Yolks of 6 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla
- 1/2 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Gill of Maraschino or 1 Gill of Kirsch
- 1 Teaspoonful of Strawberry Extract
- a few drops of Cochineal

## Directions

1. Set one-half the cream to boil
2. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the sugar till they are exceedingly light
3. Take the boiling milk from the fire, and stir in while still very hot the sugar and eggs
4. Set to cool
5. When cool add the Maraschino or Kirsch, and set to freeze as Ice Cream
6. Whip the remaining cream to a very thick froth, adding the Vanilla, and stir it into the freezing cream
7. Cut paper cases or use the specially prepared molds that come for Biscuit Glacés, and fill with the mixture
8. Fill the top one inch high with a little of the cream which you will have colored with a teaspoonful of Strawberry Extract and a few drops of Cochineal
9. Pack these molds or cases in salt, and freeze for two hours longer, until they can be turned out whole
