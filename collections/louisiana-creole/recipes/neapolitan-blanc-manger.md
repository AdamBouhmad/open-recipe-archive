## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Cornstarch
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- The Yolk of 1 Egg
- The Whites of 3 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon Extract
- 1 Tablespoonful of Grated Vanilla Chocolate
- Whipped Cream

## Directions

1. First make the cornstarch blanc manger preparation, and then separate it into four parts.
2. Put the first into a mold, and then stir into it, while warm, one heaping tablespoonful of grated Vanilla chocolate.
3. Pour the white preparation into the same mold, on top of this.
4. Then a layer of the chocolate, and then the white.
5. Cover the last layer with the yolk of an egg, and set to cool and harden.
6. Serve cut into thin slices, with whipped cream.
