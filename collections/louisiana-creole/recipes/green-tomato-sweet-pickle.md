## Ingredients

- seven pounds tomatoes
- salt
- four and a half pounds of sugar
- one ounce of cinnamon
- one ounce of cloves
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice tomatoes until you have seven pounds
2. Sprinkle them with salt
3. Let them stand twenty-four hours
4. Soak them for the same length of time in fresh water to get the brine from them
5. When drained off and ready, allow four and a half pounds of sugar, one ounce of cinnamon, one ounce of cloves, and enough vinegar to cover them
6. Boil the compound together
7. Pour it over the tomatoes
8. Let them stand twenty-four hours
9. Then bring all to a boil
10. Tie away in jars
11. Keep in a cool place away from the light
