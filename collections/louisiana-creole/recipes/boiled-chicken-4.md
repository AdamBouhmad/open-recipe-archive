## Ingredients

- A Spring Chicken
- Drawn Butter Sauce
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Select a nice spring chicken, clean and singe and split down the middle of the back.
2. Season with salt and pepper, rubbing well on the inside of the chicken.
3. Place in a saucepan and cover well with water and let it simmer well for one hour if the chicken is young.
4. If the chicken is a year old and over, let it simmer for two hours, according to age.
5. When done take out of the water and place in a heated dish.
6. Pour over a Drawn Butter Sauce and garnish with chopped parsley.
