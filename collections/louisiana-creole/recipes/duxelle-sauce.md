## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Madeira Sauce
- 1/2 Glass of White Wine
- 12 Mushrooms
- 2 Shallots
- 1/2 Ounce of Beef Tongue
- 1/2 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Put half a pint of Madeira Sauce and a half glass of White wine in a saucepan.
2. Add the mushrooms, which must be chopped very fine.
3. Then add the shallots, which will have also been chopped fine and browned in butter.
4. Let this reduce slightly and add half an ounce of finely chopped cooked beef tongue.
5. Let all boil for five minutes and serve hot.
