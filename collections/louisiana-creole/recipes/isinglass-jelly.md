## Ingredients

- one pint of water
- one ounce of isinglass
- one pound of sugar
- one cup of pale wine
- rind of a lemon
- juice of a lemon
- grated rind of lemon

## Directions

1. Boil in one pint of water one ounce of isinglass
2. When well dissolved, add to it one pound of sugar and a cup of pale wine
3. When the water is boiling, add to it the rind of a lemon
4. When taken off the fire, add the juice and grated rind of lemon
5. Strain this mixture and whisk it till it begins to thicken
6. Pour it into the vessel you wish to mould it in and set in a cool place or on ice to harden
