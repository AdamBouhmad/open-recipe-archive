## Ingredients

- red snapper fish
- bacon
- white wine
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- six laurel leaves
- thyme
- sliced onions
- three cloves
- forcemeat balls or pigeons a la Gautier
- iced sweetbreads
- small glaces
- pope's eyes of a shoulder of veal
- crabs
- fowl livers
- truffles
- cock's combs
- cock's kidneys
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Clean your fish, and be careful not to damage it, and replace the roe
2. Take off the scales
3. lightly raise the skin on one side, and lard it with bacon from fin to tail
4. put it in a pan, and moisten with white wine
5. Add salt, pepper, parsley, six laurel leaves, some thyme, sliced onions and three cloves
6. cover the head with strips of bacon
7. put it into the oven, covering your fish-kettle with leaves of foolscap paper, and letting it simmer for an hour
8. When about to serve, drain it and put it on a platter
9. garnish it all round with forcemeat balls, or better, with pigeons a la Gautier, iced sweetbreads, small glaces, pope's eyes of a shoulder of veal, crabs, fowl livers, truffles, cock's combs and cock's kidneys
10. Strain the sauce through a silken sieve
11. if not sufficiently seasoned, put into a pan two spoonfuls of Spanish sauce, and two spoonfuls of the dressing of your snapper
12. let it boil down one-half
13. put your small garnishes into it, and pour the sauce around the fish
14. Serve after having jellied and browned it
