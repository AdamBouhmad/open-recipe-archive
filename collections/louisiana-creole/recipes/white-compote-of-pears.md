## Ingredients

- pears
- water
- syrup
- lemon

## Directions

1. Blanch as many pears as you intend to use, whole, with the skins on, in boiling water; take them out when soft, peel and put into cold water
2. Have your syrup ready in the stewpan; when it is boiling hot, put in the pears, with a slice of lemon, to keep them white; when thoroughly stewed take them out and serve in the syrup; they may be served hot or cold
