## Ingredients

- a tablespoonful of Irish moss
- half a pint of boiling water
- sugar
- nutmeg
- milk

## Directions

1. Wash and pick a tablespoonful of Irish moss and put it into a tin cup
2. pour on it half a pint of boiling water
3. set it on the coals for a short time
4. when it is all dissolved add sugar and nutmeg to taste
5. This may be made with milk, to resemble custard
