## Ingredients

- 4 Truffles
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 8 Croutons Fried In Butter
- Juice of a lemon

## Directions

1. Prepare the Truffles as in the recipe 'a l'Espagnole,' or simply 'a la Provençale,' that is, slice them very fine
2. Let them stew or sauter them in butter
3. Season well with salt and pepper and the clove of garlic minced very fine
4. When done, add the juice of a lemon
5. Take some slices of bread, cut in fancy shapes, and fry in butter, or toast
6. Cover each piece with some of the Ragout of Truffles, and serve hot
