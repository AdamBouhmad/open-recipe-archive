## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Cream Puff Filling
- Cream Puff Batter
- ½ Cup of Grated Almonds
- 1 Glass of Sherry Wine
- Cold Sherry Wine Sauce or French Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare a Cream Puff Batter and a Cream Puff Filling.
2. If the flavor of almonds is desired, add a few finely-grated almonds that have been steeped in Sherry Wine.
3. Have ready the frying pan, with boiling lard.
4. Roll the cream batter into small balls, and fry till they swell to the size of an egg.
5. Skim out with a skimmer, and drain.
6. When cold, split one side with a very sharp knife, and fill with the filling.
7. Close, and continue till all are filled.
8. Serve cold with Sherry Wine Sauce or Peach Sauce.
