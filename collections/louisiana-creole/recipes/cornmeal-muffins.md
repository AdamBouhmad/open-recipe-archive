## Ingredients

- 3 cups of cornmeal
- ¾ cup of sifted wheat flour
- 3 eggs well beaten
- 2 large spoonfuls of butter
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 pint of buttermilk
- a large pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Dissolve the soda in the buttermilk
2. Mix all ingredients
3. Pour into rings and bake a nice brown in the oven
