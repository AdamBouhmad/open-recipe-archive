## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- 1 Cup of Tapioca
- % Cup of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Wash the tapioca well
2. Add it to the milk and let it soak for at least two hours and a half
3. Beat the eggs separately until light
4. Add them in order to the sugar
5. Beat very light
6. Add the salt and the tapioca
7. Bake for forty-five minutes in a moderate oven
8. The pudding may be served either hot or cold
