## Ingredients

- young, freshly shelled green peas
- salt
- 1 teaspoonful pounded loaf sugar
- butter
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash peas clean
2. Put them in a bag and the bag into plenty of boiling water, with a little salt and a teaspoonful of pounded loaf sugar
3. Boil them till tender, taking from 1½ to 1 hour
4. Never let them stand in the water after they are done
5. Season them with butter, salt and pepper
