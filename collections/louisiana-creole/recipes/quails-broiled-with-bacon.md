## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Fat Quails
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Salt
- 3/4 Tablespoonful of Pepper
- 6 Slices of Bacon
- 6 Slices of Toast
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel
- Watercress and Sliced Lemon to Garnish

## Directions

1. Clean the quails, singe and wipe well.
2. Split them through the back without separating the breast and break the leg bones.
3. Rub well with salt and pepper and a little melted butter, mixed together.
4. Put the quails on a broiler and let them broil on a moderate fire for fifteen minutes, allowing seven and a half minutes to either side, and turning frequently to prevent burning.
5. Have ready a hot dish with six slices of buttered toast, lay the quail on top and pour over a little melted butter (Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel).
6. Decorate the dish with parsley sprigs, on which lay six nicely broiled slices of breakfast bacon.
