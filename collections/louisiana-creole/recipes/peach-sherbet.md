## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 dozen ripe, soft, free-stone peaches
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Get 2 or 3 dozen ripe, soft, free-stone peaches
2. Peel them and pass them through a cullender
3. Add water sufficient to weaken it
4. Sweeten to the taste
5. Freeze
