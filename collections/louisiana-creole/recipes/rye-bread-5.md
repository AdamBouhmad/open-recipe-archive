## Ingredients

- 1 Ounce of Compressed Yeast
- 2 Quarts of Water
- 1½ Ounces of Salt
- Rye and Flour Sufficient to Make a Smooth Dough
- A Few Kummel Seeds

## Directions

1. Make the dough a little stiffer than for White Bread, allowing one-half of wheat flour and one-half rye
2. Allow the same amount of yeast, and add a few Kummel seeds and a little more salt than for White Bread
3. Set to rise
4. After one rising, work again as in Bakers' Bread, divide it into loaves, and set it to rise again
5. When about twice its original size, bake in a moderate oven
