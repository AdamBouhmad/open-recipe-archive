## Ingredients

- Cherries
- Sugar - half a pound to every pound of cherries if ripe and sour, a quarter of a pound otherwise

## Directions

1. Stone the cherries
2. Allow half a pound of sugar to every pound of cherries if the cherries are ripe and sour, and a quarter of a pound otherwise
3. Set over the fire until the heat causes the juice to flow freely
4. Proceed in exactly the same manner as for Strawberry or Blackberry Jelly
