## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
- yolks of 5 hard boiled eggs
- a little cayenne pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Melt 3 tablespoonfuls of butter
2. stir into it the yolks of 5 hard boiled eggs
3. mash very smooth
4. add a little cayenne pepper and salt
