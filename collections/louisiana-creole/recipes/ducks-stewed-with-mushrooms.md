## Ingredients

- 1 Pair of Ducks
- 1 Square Inch of Ham
- 2 Onions
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Herb Bouquet, Chopped Very Fine
- 1 Glass of Claret
- 1 Cup of Water
- 1 Can of Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Prepare the Ducks for cooking exactly as in the Stewed Wild Ducks recipe and proceed to cook accordingly.
2. Immediately after adding the boiling water add a can of mushrooms, and continue cooking according to recipe.
3. The mushrooms add a delicious flavor to the dish.
4. Serve hot, using the mushrooms as a garnish.
