## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of French Toast
- 1 Dozen Anchovies
- 1 Ounce of Grated Ham
- 6 Gherkins
- 1/2 a Truffle
- 1 Tablespoonful of Salad Oil
- 1 Teaspoonful of Caper Vinegar
- 1/2 Ounce of Aspic Jelly

## Directions

1. Prepare the French toasts and trim the edges neatly
2. Chop the Anchovies very fine and mix with the ounce of grated boiled ham, and the truffle and gherkins, all minced very fine
3. Moisten this with the salad oil and vinegar, which have been well mixed
4. Spread over the toast and garnish nicely with the aspic jelly
5. Place on a folded napkin and serve
