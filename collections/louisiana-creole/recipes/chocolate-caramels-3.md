## Ingredients

- Two cups of sugar
- one cup of molasses
- one cup of milk
- one spoonful of butter
- one spoonful of flour
- half a pound of bakers' chocolate

## Directions

1. Butter your saucepan
2. Put in the sugar, molasses and milk
3. Boil fifteen minutes
4. Add butter and flour, stirred to a cream
5. Boil five minutes longer
6. Add the chocolate grated
7. Boil until quite thick
8. Butter tin flat pans
9. Pour in the mixture half an inch thick
10. Mark it in squares before it gets hard
