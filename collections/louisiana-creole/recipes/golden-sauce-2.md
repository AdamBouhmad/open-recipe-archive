## Ingredients

- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Gill of Brandy or Sherry

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar until creamy
2. Set the saucepan in a kettle of boiling water over the fire
3. Add the eggs that have been beaten well with the brandy or sherry
4. Stir until the sauce becomes a thick cream
5. Take from the fire, add the juice of a lemon and serve very hot
