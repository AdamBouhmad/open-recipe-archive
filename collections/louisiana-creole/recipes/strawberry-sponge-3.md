## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pint of Strawberries
- 1/2 Cup of Sugar
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Boiling Water
- Gelatine
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sherry Wine

## Directions

1. Stew the strawberries gently, and press them through a sieve.
2. Soak the gelatine in a cup of cold water for an hour, and then dissolve with a tablespoonful of sherry wine.
3. Pour it into the boiling water, and let it dissolve well.
4. Add the sugar, and let it thicken gently.
5. Then strain, and add the strawberries, and set it upon ice to cool.
6. Occasionally stir to prevent the fruit from settling at the bottom.
7. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth, and add them to the sponge, beating steadily till very smooth.
8. Set in a fancy bowl to harden, and serve with Vanilla Sauce poured over it.
