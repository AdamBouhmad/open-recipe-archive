## Ingredients

- clams
- clam juice
- 3 eggs
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- 1 pint of cream
- flour
- butter
- lard

## Directions

1. Strain the clams from the juice
2. Cut the clams fine
3. Beat up 3 eggs very light
4. Stir in the clams
5. Cut up the parsley with a little salt and pepper
6. Grate some nutmeg
7. Add these to the clams
8. Stir in 1 pint of cream
9. Slowly dredge in some flour until it is of the consistency of fritters
10. Have the pan hot and put in half butter and half lard
11. Let it boil
12. Drop in a spoonful of the fritter batter
13. Serve hot
