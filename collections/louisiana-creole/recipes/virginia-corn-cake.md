## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 and a half pints of boiling milk
- 1 quart of Indian meal
- a pint of wheat flour
- a tablespoonful of sugar
- a little salt
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 tablespoonful of butter in 1 and a half pints of boiling milk
2. Into this scald 1 quart of Indian meal
3. When cool, add a pint of wheat flour, a tablespoonful of sugar, and a little salt
4. Add 2 eggs well beaten
5. Bake in 2 cakes
