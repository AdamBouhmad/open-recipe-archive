## Ingredients

- 1 Calf's Head
- Yolk of 1 Egg
- 1 Lemon
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Vinegar
- Water
- Salt and Pepper
- Sauce a la Poulette

## Directions

1. Clean and prepare the calf's head as in the recipe given for Calf's Head Soup
2. Boil it according to recipe
3. Cut one lemon fine and add to the boiling calf head, which is boiled simply in water, and salt and pepper
4. Add two tablespoonfuls of good vinegar and let it cook till done
5. Make a Sauce a la Poulette
6. Put the calf's head in the sauce and let it boil for a half hour
7. Take the yolk of one egg and beat it as you would an omelet
8. Add to the calf's head and serve
