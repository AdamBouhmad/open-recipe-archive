## Ingredients

- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Cup of Powdered Sugar
- Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Gill of Brandy
- 1 Gill of Boiling Water

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar together to a cream, and add the sugar, beating until light and very white.
2. Add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, putting in one at a time.
3. Whip these well together, and add the boiling water and the brandy.
4. Set over the fire in a bain-marie and stir till creamy and no longer.
5. Serve either hot or cold.
6. Do not let it boil.
