## Ingredients

- 1 pint of new milk
- Thin rind of 1 lemon
- 6 or 8 bitter almonds
- A pinch of salt
- J of an ounce of isinglass
- Sugar to the taste

## Directions

1. Throw into a pint of new milk, the thin rind of 1 lemon, 6 or 8 bitter almonds and a pinch of salt
2. Keep it at the boiling point till strongly flavored
3. Add J of an ounce of isinglass
4. When dissolved strain the milk
5. Sugar to the taste
6. Put it in a mould, and when cold, eat it with a rich sauce
