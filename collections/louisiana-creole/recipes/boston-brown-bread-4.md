## Ingredients

- one and a half pints of Indian meal
- half a pint of wheat flour
- one cup of sweet milk
- one cup of sour milk
- a teaspoonful of soda
- three tablespoonfuls of molasses
- one tablespoonful of yeast
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Mix one and a half pints of Indian meal, half a pint of wheat flour, one cup of sweet milk, one cup of sour milk with a teaspoonful of soda in it, three tablespoonfuls of molasses, one tablespoonful of yeast, and a pinch of salt
2. Put it in a warm place to rise
3. Let it bake steadily for four hours
4. Warm by steaming it when wanted to use
