## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Currants
- 1 Pound of White Sugar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- The Soft of Bread

## Directions

1. Make a marmalade of the currants, and using the same proportion as for Cherry Charlotte.
2. Proceed exactly as for Cherry Charlotte.
