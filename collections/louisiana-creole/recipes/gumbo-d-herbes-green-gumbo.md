## Ingredients

- equal portions of young cabbage leaves
- beet leaves
- turnip leaves
- mustard leaves
- spinach
- cresses
- parsley
- green onions
- beef
- ham
- hot pepper

## Directions

1. Take equal portions of young cabbage leaves, beet leaves, turnip leaves, mustard leaves, spinach, cresses, parsley, green onions, and place them in boiling water
2. When well boiled, chop them fine
3. Make a good soup (brown gravy) in which you put beef and ham to fry a little
4. Add your vegetables (or herbs)
5. Stir often and let them fry about one hour, keeping the pot well covered
6. Add the required quantity of hot water, and let it all boil gently for about two hours
7. By adding hot pepper, you will have a real Creole Gumbo
