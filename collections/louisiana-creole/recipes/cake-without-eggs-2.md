## Ingredients

- 1 cup of butter
- 3 cups of sugar
- 1 pint of sour cream
- 1 1/2 pints of flour
- 1 lb. of chopped raisins
- 1 spoonful of soda
- spice to the taste

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake about 1 hour
