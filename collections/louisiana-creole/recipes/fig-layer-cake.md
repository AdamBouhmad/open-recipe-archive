## Ingredients

- 4 Layers of Sponge Cake
- 1 Pound of Chopped Figs
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1/2 Cup of Water

## Directions

1. Make a layer cake — any good white, sponge or cream
2. Bake in jelly tins
3. Boil one pound of chopped figs, one cup of sugar, and one-half cup of water, till the figs form a jelly
4. Spread, when cool, between the layers of cake, while they are still hot, and proceed as in Almond Layer Cake
