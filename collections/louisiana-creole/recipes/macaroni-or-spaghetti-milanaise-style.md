## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pound of Macaroni
- 1/2 Pound of Cold Boiled Ham
- 1 Pound of Grated Parmesan Cheese
- 1 Onion
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 1 Can Tomatoes
- 2 Sprigs of Thyme
- 2 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Taste
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter

## Directions

1. Boil the macaroni in water and salt as in the recipe given for Boiling Macaroni.
2. When tender, drain well through a colander.
3. While the macaroni is boiling, put a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan, and as it melts, add the grated onion.
4. Let this brown, and then add the ham which you will have minced very fine.
5. Let this brown; add the cloves of garlic, minced very fine, and the herbs, minced very fine.
6. Add almost immediately, as these begin to brown, a half can of tomatoes, or six fresh large tomatoes.
7. As it stews, add a half pound of grated Parmesan cheese, and let all stew for about ten minutes.
8. Add the macaroni or spaghetti, and let all simmer gently for about twenty minutes longer.
9. Serve hot, and pass at the same time a small plate of grated Parmesan cheese to each person.
