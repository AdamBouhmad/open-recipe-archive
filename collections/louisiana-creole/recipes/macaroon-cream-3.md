## Ingredients

- 6 Macaroons
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Orange Flower Water
- 1/2 a White Praline
- 2 Ounces of Powdered White Sugar
- The Yolks of 4 Fresh Eggs

## Directions

1. Crumble six macaroons, soak them in a pint of milk.
2. Add one tablespoonful of Orange Flower Water, in which you have put one-half of a mashed Praline.
3. Add two ounces of sugar and the yolks of four fresh Creole eggs, well beaten till light.
4. Set on the stove and let it simmer constantly till reduced almost one-half.
5. Let it cool, and serve in the glass in which it was cooled.
