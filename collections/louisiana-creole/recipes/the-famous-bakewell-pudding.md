## Ingredients

- Nice puff paste
- An inch of nice preserve or jam
- J lb. of butter
- J lb. of well boiled potatoes
- J lb. of sugar
- J ounce each of sweet and bitter almonds
- 5 eggs
- 1 tablespoon of brandy

## Directions

1. Lay a nice puff paste on plate, then an inch of nice preserve or jam
2. Mix, like pound cake, J lb. of butter, beaten to cream, J lb. of well boiled potatoes pressed through a sieve, J lb. of sugar, J ounce each of sweet and bitter almonds, both blanched and pounded
3. When beaten well together, add 5 eggs well frothed, 1 tablespoon of brandy
4. Pour over the layer of preserves and bake
