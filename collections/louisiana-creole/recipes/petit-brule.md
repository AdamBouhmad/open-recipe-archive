## Ingredients

- an ordinary-sized, thick-skinned orange
- two lumps of sugar
- cinnamon
- fine French brandy (cognac)

## Directions

1. Take an ordinary-sized, thick-skinned orange; cut through the peel entirely around the orange like the line of the equator
2. Force off the peel by passing the handle of a spoon between it and the pulp
3. Into the cup thus formed put two lumps of sugar and some cinnamon
4. Fill with fine French brandy (cognac)
5. Ignite it and pour into glasses
