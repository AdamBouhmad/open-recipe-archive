## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Cream
- 1 Pint of Flour
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 1/2 Teaspoonfuls of Yeast Powder

## Directions

1. Beat the whites and yolks of the eggs separately.
2. Then add the cream and salt, and finally the flour, into which you will have blended the yeast powder.
3. Beat well and fill the muffin ring or gem pans one-half full.
4. Place in a quick oven and bake for twenty minutes.
