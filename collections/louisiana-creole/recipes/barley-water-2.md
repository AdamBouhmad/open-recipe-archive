## Ingredients

- four large tablespoonfuls of picked and washed pearl barley
- two quarts of boiling water
- salt
- lemon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take four large tablespoonfuls of picked and washed pearl barley, and put it into a porcelain-lined kettle with two quarts of boiling water
2. Let it boil slowly until the water is reduced one half
3. Strain it and season with salt, lemon, or sugar as may be agreeable to the sick
