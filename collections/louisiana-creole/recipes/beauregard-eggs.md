## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3/4 pint of fresh milk
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of cornstarch
- salt and pepper to taste
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs for five minutes, till hard.
2. Take out of the water and cool, take off the shells, and separate the whites from the yolks.
3. Rub the yolks through a sieve, and chop the whites very fine.
4. Do not mix them.
5. Have the milk ready to boil, and rub the butter and cornstarch together, and add to the boiling milk.
6. Add the whites of the eggs, and salt and pepper to taste.
7. Prepare some buttered toast, and cover it with a layer of this white sauce.
8. Add a layer of the yolks of the eggs.
9. Add another layer of the sauce, and another layer of the yolks, and then the remainder of the sauce.
10. Sprinkle the top with a little salt and pepper.
