## Ingredients

- damsons
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the fruit in a sufficient quantity of water to cover it
2. Strain the pulp through a very coarse sieve
3. To each lb. add 4 ounces of sugar
4. Boil it till it begins to candy on the sides, then pour it into tin moulds
