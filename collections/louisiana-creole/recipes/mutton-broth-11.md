## Ingredients

- 8 pounds of the neck of the mutton
- 4 quarts of cold water
- 1/4 cup of rice
- 1 onion
- 2 large turnips
- 1 bay leaf
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Wash the neck of the mutton, or wipe it with a damp towel, and put it into the kettle with the cold water.
2. Let it come to a slow boil and skim carefully.
3. Cover well and let it simmer for about four hours.
4. Remove from the stove and pour into an earthern vessel to cool.
5. When cold, remove all the fat from the surface.
6. Return to the kettle and add the rice, the sliced turnip and the bay leaf, and season to taste.
