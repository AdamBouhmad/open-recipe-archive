## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Ounces of Fresh Bread Crumbs
- 3/4 Cup of Cold Water
- 3/4 Ounce of Butter
- 1 Cup of Cream or Milk
- 6 Whole Peppers
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Crumble the bread and place in a saucepan with the water; add the butter, salt and peppers.
2. Cook for five minutes and add the milk.
3. Cook five minutes longer, remove the peppers and serve hot.
