## Ingredients

- A piece of pumpkin
- Brown sugar and water
- A little juice of the lemon
- Mace, or other spices (optional)

## Directions

1. Peel a piece of pumpkin, and cut it in thin slices
2. Make a nice, thick syrup of brown sugar and water, and put the pumpkin into it, with a little of the juice of the lemon
3. Boil this until the pumpkin is nicely candied
4. Mace, or other spices, may be used for flavoring instead of lemon, if preferred
