## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of flour
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1/2 lb. of sugar
- 9 eggs
- 1 pint of molasses
- 1 cup of ginger
- 2 teaspoonfuls of soda

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake
