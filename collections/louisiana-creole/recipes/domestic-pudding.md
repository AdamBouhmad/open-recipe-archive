## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pints of cream
- peel of a small lemon
- crumbs of a small loaf of bread
- 1 lb. of fresh butter
- 1 teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
- 4 well beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Sweeten 1 1/2 pints of cream, and boil it with the peel of a small lemon
2. Cut the crumbs of a small loaf of bread
3. Put it in the cream
4. Boil it for 8 minutes, stirring constantly
5. When thick, add 1 lb. of fresh butter beaten to a cream
6. Add 1 teaspoonful of grated nutmeg, and 4 well beaten eggs
7. Beat all well together for some minutes
8. It may be baked or boiled
9. The water should boil quick when it is put in, and moved for a minute, to cause the ingredients to mix
10. When the pudding is done, a pan of cold water should be ready, and the pudding dipped into it, as soon as it comes out of the pot
