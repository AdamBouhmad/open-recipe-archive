## Ingredients

- 1 Chicken
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- A Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Select a fine one-year-old chicken and clean, singe and boil according to the above recipe, first having cut in joints, however.
2. In boiling always simply cover the chicken with water, otherwise you will have chicken soup, all the nutriment of the chicken being absorbed by the soup.
3. When cooked for an hour or longer, if the chicken is now very tender, take out of the saucepan and place in a dish and pour over a Cream Sauce, and serve.
