## Ingredients

- two quarts of ripe tomatoes
- four or five sweetbreads
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- three spoonfuls of flour
- a quarter of a pound of butter
- beaten yolks of three eggs

## Directions

1. Set over the fire two quarts of ripe tomatoes and stew slowly.
2. Strain through a coarse sieve.
3. Add to them four or five sweetbreads, well trimmed and soaked in warm water.
4. Season with salt and cayenne pepper.
5. Thicken with three spoonfuls of flour and a quarter of a pound of butter, mixed.
6. Cook slowly till done.
7. Just before serving stir in the beaten yolks of three eggs.
