## Ingredients

- 2 Fine, Ripe Apricots
- 2 cups of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water or Cream

## Directions

1. Pare the apricots, cut them in two, remove the stones, and cut in small pieces.
2. Mash the fruit and the sugar together, add the water or cream, and turn into the freezer and proceed to freeze as in Ice Cream.
