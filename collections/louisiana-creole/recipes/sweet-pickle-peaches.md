## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. of peaches
- 3 lbs. of sugar
- 1 quart of vinegar
- allspice
- cloves
- mace

## Directions

1. In making the syrup add allspice, cloves and mace to the taste
2. Form the syrup
3. Throw in the fruit and cook it, but not as much as usual with preserves
4. If preferred with the skins on, rub the fuzz off with a coarse napkin
