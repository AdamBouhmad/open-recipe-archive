## Ingredients

- three eggs
- a cup of milk
- a pint of boiled green corn grated
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- lard

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs with a cup of milk
2. Add a pint of boiled green corn grated
3. Throw in flour enough to make a batter thick enough to drop from a spoon
4. Add salt and pepper to taste
5. Beat it very hard
6. Drop into boiling lard one spoonful at a time
