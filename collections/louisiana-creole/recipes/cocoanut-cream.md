## Ingredients

- whites of 4 eggs
- 3 pints of milk
- cocoanut

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of 4 eggs to a stiff froth
2. Boil 3 pints of milk down to a quart and pour it on the eggs, stirring it very slowly
3. Sweeten it to the taste
4. After this has cooled, grate a cocoanut in it
5. Set it to freeze
