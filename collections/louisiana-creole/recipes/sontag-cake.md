## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of sifted flour
- 1 lb. of fresh butter washed in rose water
- 1 lb. of treacle
- 1 nutmeg grated
- a little pounded mace and cinnamon
- 1 ounce of pounded ginger
- 1 ounce of blanched sweet almonds
- 2 well beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Melt the butter with the treacle
2. When nearly cold, stir in the eggs and the rest of the ingredients
3. Mix all well together
4. Make it into round cakes
5. Bake them upon tins
