## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Peanuts
- 1 Pound of Brown Sugar
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter

## Directions

1. Shell the peanuts and break into bits
2. Set the sugar and water to boil
3. As it begins to simmer add the peanuts and the butter
4. Stir constantly and as it bubbles up once take from the fire
5. Pour from the spoon on the marble slab or a buttered plate
6. Set away to harden
