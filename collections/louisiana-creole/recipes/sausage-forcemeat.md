## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pound of Fresh Pork
- 2 Square Inches of Lean Raw Ham
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig of Parsley
- A Pinch of Grated Nutmeg
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Hash the pork; season well with salt and pepper, according to taste, adding a pinch of grated nutmeg and the chopped herbs and minced ham
2. Hash all very fine and make into small balls and use as desired
