## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- parsley
- butter
- green onion
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Beat two eggs—yolks and whites separately
2. in a bowl put a tablespoonful of water, a little parsley, a teaspoonful of butter, and a little green onion, if liked
3. beat the eggs into this
4. whisk all very rapidly for a few minutes
5. pour it into a pan, where there is a tablespoonful of butter just hot enough to color the eggs
6. cook them very slowly
7. roll up the omelet as it cooks until it is like a rolled pancake
8. pepper and salt it at the last moment of cooking
