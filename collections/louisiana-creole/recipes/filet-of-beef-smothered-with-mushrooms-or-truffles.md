## Ingredients

- 1 Filet of Beef
- 1 Can of Mushrooms or ½ Can of Truffles
- 6 Onions, sliced fine
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 2 Sprigs each of Thyme and Bay Leaf
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Pint of Hot Water
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Taste
- Sherry or White Wine

## Directions

1. Smother the beef, using a filet according to the recipe given for Smothered Beefsteak
2. After it has cooked about a half hour add one can of mushrooms and let it continue to simmer gently for an hour and a half longer
3. When ready to serve, add, if possible, a gill or a small wineglass of Sherry or White Wine and boil ten minutes longer
4. Put the filet in a dish, place the mushrooms over and around as a garnish, pour over the sauce and serve
5. If truffles are used instead of the mushrooms, add one-half can and proceed in exactly the same manner as when using the mushrooms
