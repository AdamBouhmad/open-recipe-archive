## Ingredients

- The Breasts of 2 Small Chickens, or Any Game
- 1 Ounce of Lean Ham
- 1 Gill of Madeira Sauce
- 1 Gill of Tomato Sauce
- 6 Mushrooms
- 1 Truffle

## Directions

1. Mince the breasts of the chicken or game very fine, mince the ham, truffles and mushrooms, and put all into a saucepan with a gill of Madeira Sauce and one gill of Tomato Sauce
2. Let all cook for five minutes, and then use as a garnish
