## Ingredients

- 8 or 10 Slices of Venison
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Currant Jelly
- 1 Tablespoonful of Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Slice the venison very thin in pieces about two inches long and one inch wide, and about the thickness of a silver dollar.
2. Have the chafing dish or alcohol lamp on the dining table as you sit to eat.
3. The pan must be very hot.
4. Season the meat well with salt and pepper, and ready to put into the pan.
5. Put a tablespoonful of butter into the dish.
6. Let it get very hot, without burning.
7. Put the slices of Venison in the dish.
8. In one minute turn them over.
9. Take a tablespoonful of melted butter, and blend well with a tablespoonful of Currant Jelly and a tablespoonful of water.
10. Spread this over the cooking Venison.
11. Turn again.
12. Let it cook for five minutes only, and serve very hot.
