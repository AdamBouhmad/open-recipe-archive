## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sifted White Sugar
- The Whites of 12 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Essence
- 3 Ounces of Currant, Orange or Any Preserves
- 1/2 Cup of Transparent Icing

## Directions

1. Prepare as in Creole Meringues recipe.
2. Spread some preserves thinly inside the Meringues before garnishing them with the cream.
3. Ice with Transparent Icing.
