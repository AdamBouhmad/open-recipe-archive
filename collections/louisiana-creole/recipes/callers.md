## Ingredients

- about 5 cents worth of rice
- yolks of 3 eggs
- 1 dessertspoonful of yeast powder
- enough flour to make a light batter
- whites of 3 eggs, well beaten
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Soak and pound the rice in a mortar
2. Let it stand over night
3. Add the yolks of 3 eggs
4. Put in a dessertspoonful of yeast powder
5. Add enough flour to make a light batter
6. Add the whites of the eggs well beaten
7. Have a pot of boiling lard and cook as for fritters
