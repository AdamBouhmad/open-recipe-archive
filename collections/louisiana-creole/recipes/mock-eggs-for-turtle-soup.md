## Ingredients

- 1 fresh egg
- 3 hard-boiled eggs
- about a teaspoonfull of butter

## Directions

1. Break and beat thoroughly one fresh egg
2. Take the yolks of three hard-boiled eggs, and rub them into a fine paste with about a teaspoonfull of butter
3. Mix this with the raw egg and roll into pellets of the identical size and shape of the turtle eggs
4. Let them lie in boiling water about two minutes
5. Drop into the soup
