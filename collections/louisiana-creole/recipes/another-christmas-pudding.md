## Ingredients

- One pound of raisins
- One pound of currants
- One pound of suet
- Three-quarters of a pound of bread-crumbs
- One pint of milk
- Ten eggs
- Three-quarters of a pound of citron and orange-peel mixed
- One small nutmeg
- One glass of brandy
- Flour

## Directions

1. Stone the raisins and divide them
2. Wash and dry the currants
3. Cut the peel into slices
4. Mix all these with the bread-crumbs, flour and suet chopped very fine
5. Add the grated nutmeg
6. Stir in the eggs well-beaten, the brandy, and the milk
7. When the ingredients are well blended, put it into a mould
8. Tie a floured cloth over it
9. Boil it six hours
10. When done turn it out
11. Serve with brandy and arrowroot sauce
