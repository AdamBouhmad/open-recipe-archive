## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Flour
- 1 Cup of Louisiana Molasses
- 2 Cups of Sweet Milk
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Scald the meal.
2. Beat the eggs very light, and beat the milk into them till very light.
3. Add the molasses and yeast powder, and blend well.
4. Then add the flour, mixing and beating till thoroughly blended.
5. Tie in a cloth, as you would roll a pudding, and place in a steamer, on top of a pot of boiling water, and cover first with a close cloth, and then with the steamer cover, and steam for three hours.
6. Then turn into a hot dish, and eat as a plain dessert, with a Brandy or Hard or Cream Sauce.
