## Ingredients

- 1¼ Pints of Best Jamaica Rum
- ½ of a Pint of Brandy
- 2 Quarts of Apollinaris or Seltzer Water
- 1 Pint of Lemon Juice
- 1 Pint of White Wine
- 2 Lemons, Sliced Thin
- 1½ Pounds of Sugar
- 1 Large Piece of Ice

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and water
2. Add the liquors, stir well
3. Add the lemon juice and the bits of sliced lemon
4. Set to cool and serve in small punch glasses
