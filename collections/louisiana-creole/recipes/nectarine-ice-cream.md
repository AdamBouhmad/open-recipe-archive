## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream or Milk
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Quart of Nectarines or 1 Pint Can
- 6 Eggs

## Directions

1. Peel the nectarines, stone and mash well, with half of the sugar
2. Let them stand for an hour or more, and press through a sieve
3. Set the milk to boil; beat the yolks of the eggs and the remaining half of the sugar to a cream, and beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
4. Take the milk off the fire and pour it over the sugar and yolks, stirring all the while
5. Set to cool
6. Add the mashed nectarines and sugar, and mix well; mix all thoroughly, turn into a freezer and proceed as in the general directions for making Ice Cream
