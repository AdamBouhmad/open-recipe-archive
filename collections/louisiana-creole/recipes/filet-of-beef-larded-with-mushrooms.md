## Ingredients

- 1 Filet of Beef
- ½ Can of Mushrooms
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Glace
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Water
- 1 Glass of Madeira or Sherry Wine, if desired
- ½ Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Trim and lard the beef according to the directions given in the Filet of Beef Larded recipe, and bake in the same manner—rare.
2. When it has cooked for a half hour in a quick oven, it will be done.
3. Make a sauce as follows: Take one tablespoonful of butter and one of Glace and three of water; melt the butter and add the Glace, browning nicely without burning, and stirring constantly.
4. When brown, add one glass of Madeira or Sherry wine, if desired, and one-half pint of water.
5. Season well with salt and pepper.
6. Add a half can of mushrooms, chopped very fine.
7. Stir well and let it boil about ten minutes, so as not to be too thick nor yet too thin.
8. Place the filet in a hot dish and pour the sauce over and serve hot.
