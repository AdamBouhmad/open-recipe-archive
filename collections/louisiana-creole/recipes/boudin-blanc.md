## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of the White Meat of Fowl (left over)
- 1 Pound of Lean Pork
- 1 Pound of Fat Pork
- 1 Pint of Cream
- 1/4 Cup of Soft Bread
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 1/8 Teaspoon of Ground Spices
- 1/4 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Onion
- 1 Teaspoonful Cayenne
- Salt and Pepper, 1 Teaspoonful Each

## Directions

1. Cut the meat and mince
2. Season highly with the salt and pepper and Cayenne
3. Add the minced onion and garlic and mix well
4. Mix with half a cup of the soft bread, wet and squeezed well
5. Cook all for about fifteen minutes in one pint of cream
6. When reduced take off the stove
7. Add the beaten yolks of two eggs and stir well
8. Cool the mixture
9. Fill the prepared entrails and tie either end
10. Place them in a pot containing half milk and half water
11. Boil them for about twenty minutes
12. Prick gently, place in buttered papers and broil gently
