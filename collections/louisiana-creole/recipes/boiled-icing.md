## Ingredients

- 1 and one-half lbs. of loaf sugar
- 1/2 pint of water
- whites of 7 eggs

## Directions

1. One and one-half lbs. of loaf sugar, boiled in 1/2 pint of water until it ropes
2. Beat the whites of 7 eggs to a stiff froth
3. Put the syrup on to boil, and stir it until it is milk-warm
4. Put in the eggs, and beat the whole 1 hour
