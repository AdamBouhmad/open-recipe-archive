## Ingredients

- a gallon of ripe tomatoes
- four tablespoonfuls of salt
- four tablespoonfuls of ground black pepper
- half a spoonful of ground allspice
- three spoonfuls of ground mustard
- eight pods of red pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a gallon of ripe tomatoes, skin them by pouring boiling water over them
2. Let them get cold and put them in a stew pan with four tablespoonfuls of salt, and the same of ground black pepper, half a spoonful of ground allspice, and three spoonfuls of ground mustard
3. Throw in eight pods of red pepper, and let all stew slowly until the tomatoes are soft and tender
4. Thin the mixture with enough vinegar to allow the catsup to be strained through a sieve
5. Cook it fifteen minutes, and bottle up when cold
