## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of French Toast
- 1 Dozen Anchovies
- 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 1 Ounce of Anchovy Butter

## Directions

1. Prepare six slices of French toast
2. Spread over each a little Anchovy Butter
3. Spread over this buttered toast the Anchovies and hard-boiled eggs, which have been finely minced and mixed together
4. Place on a folded napkin in a dish and serve
