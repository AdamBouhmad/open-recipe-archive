## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Soft-Shell Crabs
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Flour, Sifted
- Butter
- Sliced Lemon
- Parsley Garnish

## Directions

1. Clean the crab and wash in cold water
2. Dry with a clean towel and season well
3. Season a pint of milk with salt and black pepper, and soak the crabs in it so as to thoroughly impregnate them with the milk
4. Pat lightly with a little flour and brush over with melted butter
5. Place between the broiler and broil till a delicate brown over a slow fire, which will generally require about fifteen minutes to cook thoroughly
6. Serve on a platter, nicely garnished with parsley sprigs and lemon cut in quarters
7. Pour over the crabs a little melted butter and chopped parsley
