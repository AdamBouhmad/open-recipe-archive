## Ingredients

- 1 loaf of French bread
- 1/2 pint of milk
- 1/2 pint of milk
- 4 eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter or lard

## Directions

1. Slice a loaf of French bread and pour 1/2 pint of milk over it
2. take another 1/2 pint of milk and mix with 4 well beaten eggs, sugar and cinnamon to the taste
3. dip the slices in the mixture, and fry in butter or lard
