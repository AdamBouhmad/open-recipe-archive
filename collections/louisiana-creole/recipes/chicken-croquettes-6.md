## Ingredients

- 1 Young Chicken
- 2 Small Onions
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 4 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Large Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- Cayenne and Pepper to Taste
- 2 Eggs
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil the chicken as directed in the recipe for boiling.
2. Then, when cold, remove all the tough fibers and nerves.
3. Hash the chicken well and season with the minced vegetables and sweet herbs, mixing all thoroughly.
4. Then take a cup of the soft of the bread, wet it and squeeze, and soak in milk, in which you have beaten two eggs.
5. Mix all this with the chicken very thoroughly and season to taste.
6. When well mixed form the meat into cylindrical shapes and brush with a little butter.
7. Then roll in a beaten egg and roll again in powdered bread crumbs.
8. Fry in boiling lard and serve hot on a plate garnished with fried parsley.
