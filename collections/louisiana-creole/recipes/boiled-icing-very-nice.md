## Ingredients

- a pound of white sugar
- a cup of water
- whites of three eggs
- flour or corn starch
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Boil until very thick, a pound of white sugar in a cup of water
2. It should be as thick as for candy
3. when boiled, pour it gently on the beaten whites of three eggs
4. Beat this rapidly until well mixed with the eggs
5. flour the cake with flour or corn starch, and ice it with a knife in the usual way
6. You must use flavoring to suit your taste
