## Ingredients

- whites of ten eggs
- yolks of ten eggs
- three quarters of a pound of powdered sugar
- juice and grated rind of a lemon
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of ten eggs to a stiff froth.
2. Beat the yolks with three quarters of a pound of powdered sugar, and the juice and grated rind of a lemon.
3. Mix all together lightly.
4. Butter a thick-bottomed dish which will just hold the pudding.
5. Put it immediately in the oven, and bake it fifteen or twenty minutes.
6. Serve it just as it comes from the oven.
7. It should quiver like a golden jelly when served.
8. The oven must not be too hot, or it will scorch; the heat should be as usual to bake pies.
