## Ingredients

- 6 fresh eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- salt and pepper to taste
- parsley
- buttered toast or broiled ham

## Directions

1. Break the eggs into a saucer, one by one, and then transfer to a bowl.
2. Season well with salt and pepper.
3. Have the frying pan very hot.
4. Put into it the butter, and add immediately the eggs.
5. Keep stirring around and around and across for about three or four minutes.
6. Judge by the consistency of the egg, which must be like a thick mush as you take it from the fire.
7. Keep stirring a few seconds longer after you have taken the pan off the fire.
8. Put the eggs into a hot dish, and garnish with parsley.
9. Serve immediately with buttered toast or broiled ham.
