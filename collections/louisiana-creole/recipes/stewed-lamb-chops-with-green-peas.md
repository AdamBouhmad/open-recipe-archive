## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- butter
- water
- green peas
- sweet butter

## Directions

1. Season the chops with pepper and salt; roll in flour and fry to a pale brown.
2. When done, if the chops are very fat, pour some of it into the stock-pot and cover the chops with boiling water.
3. Parboil a pint of green peas; add them to the chops, together with a large spoonful of sweet butter.
4. Dredge in a spoonful of flour, and let all stew gently for half an hour.
