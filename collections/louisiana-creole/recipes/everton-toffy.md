## Ingredients

- one pound of brown sugar
- one-quarter of a pound of butter
- lemon or vanilla flavoring

## Directions

1. In a shallow vessel, melt together brown sugar and butter
2. Stir well together for fifteen minutes, or until the mixture becomes brittle when dropped in water
3. Add lemon or vanilla flavoring before the cooking is complete
4. Butter a flat plate
5. Pour the toffy on it to cool
6. When partly cold, mark it off in squares with a knife
