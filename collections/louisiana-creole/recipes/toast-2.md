## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of Bread
- Butter to Taste

## Directions

1. Slice the bread, neither too thick nor too thin.
2. Cut off the rough edges of the crust.
3. Hold on a fork before burning coals till nicely toasted on one side.
4. Then turn on the other, and proceed as before.
5. If dry toast is desired, the bread is not buttered.
6. If buttered toast, butter nicely, set in the oven a second and send to the table hot.
