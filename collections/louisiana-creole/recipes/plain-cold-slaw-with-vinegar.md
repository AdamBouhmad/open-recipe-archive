## Ingredients

- cabbage
- pepper
- salt
- one cup of cold, sharp vinegar
- hard-boiled eggs
- onions

## Directions

1. Chop or slice the cabbage
2. Soak the cabbage until it is cool and firm
3. Dust pepper on it
4. Throw in a little salt
5. Pour over it a cup of cold, sharp vinegar
6. Slice hard-boiled eggs and put over the cabbage when sent to table
7. Sliced onions can also be used, seasoned with salt, pepper and vinegar
8. Mix a little sweet, sliced vegetable pepper with the onions
