## Ingredients

- 1 No. 1 salted mackerel
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak a No. 1 salted mackerel over night
2. wipe it dry in the morning
3. have ready some clear, bright coals
4. heat and grease the gridiron
5. lay the mackerel on it with the flesh side down
6. when cooked turn it, by placing a dish on it
7. slip the skin side on the gridiron
8. Butter it and serve up hot
