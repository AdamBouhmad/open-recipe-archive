## Ingredients

- 3 ozs. butter
- 1 oz. of flour
- 1/2 pint of water
- boiled potatoes
- salt
- nutmeg
- pepper
- fine chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Stir butter, flour and water over the fire until it is melted into a sauce
2. Put the boiled potatoes into the sauce
3. Dish it very hot, seasoning it with salt, nutmeg and pepper
4. You may also mix fine chopped parsley with it
