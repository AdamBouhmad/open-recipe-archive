## Ingredients

- 6 Medium-Sized Trout, or 3 Large Ones
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1 Sliced Lemon and Parsley to Garnish
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Clean the fish without cutting off the head or tail
2. Rub the fish well with salt and pepper, mixed in a little sweet oil or a little butter oil
3. Broil whole over a clear, moderate fire
4. If the fish is small, broil on a quick, clear fire; if large, broil accordingly
