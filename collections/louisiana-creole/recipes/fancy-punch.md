## Ingredients

- Half a tablespoonful of sugar
- a little raspberry
- a little lemon, lime and pineapple juice
- Two parts of water to one of whiskey or brandy
- plenty of ice
- fruits in season

## Directions

1. Shake and strain in punch glass
2. put fruits in season when serving
3. use regular bar glass
