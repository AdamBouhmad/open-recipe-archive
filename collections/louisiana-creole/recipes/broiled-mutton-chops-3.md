## Ingredients

- 6 Mutton Chops
- Butter
- Salt and Pepper
- Bread Crumbs
- Chopped Parsley

## Directions

1. Season the chops well with salt and pepper and brush with melted butter and a few bread crumbs.
2. Have the gridiron very hot and place the chops upon it.
3. In a few seconds turn the chop and let this side cook.
4. The blood will be running out, and the chop is done.
5. Place on a platter, butter thickly and sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve very hot.
