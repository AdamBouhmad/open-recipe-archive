## Ingredients

- 1 peck of quinces
- 1 peck of pears
- 1 peck of peaches
- 1 peck of apples
- 3 lbs. of good sugar to each peck of fruit

## Directions

1. Pare and cut quinces in small pieces
2. Take the rinds and cores and boil them in water
3. Strain it and boil the quinces till quite soft
4. Add 1 peck of pears
5. When done, add 1 peck of peaches
6. When quite done, add 1 peck of apples
7. Add 3 lbs. of good sugar to each peck of fruit
