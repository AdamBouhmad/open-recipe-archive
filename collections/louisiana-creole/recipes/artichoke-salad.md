## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Cold Boiled Artichokes
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vinegar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chopped Parsley
- French Dressing

## Directions

1. Boil the Artichokes (See recipe)
2. When cold, peel them and cut into quarters
3. Add chopped parsley and the French dressing; mix, and serve very cold
