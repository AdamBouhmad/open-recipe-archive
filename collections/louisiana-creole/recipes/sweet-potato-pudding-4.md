## Ingredients

- One pound or a pint of hot boiled sweet potato
- Six eggs well beaten
- Three-fourths of a pound of butter
- A pound of sugar
- Grated lemon rind
- A little brandy

## Directions

1. Pass the hot boiled sweet potato through a sieve—the finer the better
2. Add six eggs well beaten, three-fourths of a pound of butter, and a pound of sugar
3. Flavor with grated lemon rind, and a little brandy
4. Make a paste around the dish
5. Pour in the sweet potato mixture
6. Bake
7. Sprinkle finely pulverized sugar over the surface of the pudding
