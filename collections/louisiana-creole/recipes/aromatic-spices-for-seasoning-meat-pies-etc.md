## Ingredients

- mace
- nutmeg
- cloves
- pepper corns
- marjoram
- thyme
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Take an ounce each of mace and nutmeg, two ounces of cloves, two of pepper corns (whole pepper will do), marjoram and thyme, each one ounce, bay leaves half an ounce.
2. Dry the herbs well first; put the spices and herbs in a paper closely folded, to keep in the aroma, and place them in a slow oven to dry for an hour, or two;
3. then pound and sift them through a sieve. Cork tightly.
