## Ingredients

- six boiled potatoes
- three tablespoonfuls of grated ham
- a little pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley
- the yolks of three eggs
- egg
- bread crumbs
- hot lard
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take six boiled potatoes
2. Add three tablespoonfuls of grated ham, a little pepper, salt, and chopped parsley, also the yolks of three eggs
3. Form into balls
4. Dip in egg and roll in bread crumbs
5. Fry in hot lard
6. Garnish with parsley
