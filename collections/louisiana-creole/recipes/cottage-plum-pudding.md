## Ingredients

- One pound and a half of flour
- Four or five eggs
- A pinch of salt
- A little nutmeg
- One pound of raisins
- Half a pound of currants
- Sugar to taste
- A little milk
- Peel of a lemon grated
- A few pieces of citron cut thin

## Directions

1. Make a thick batter with five well-beaten eggs, one pound and a half of flour, and a sufficient quantity of milk
2. Add the currants washed and picked
3. Add the raisins stoned
4. Add a little nutmeg and sugar to taste
5. Mix all well together
6. Boil it in a basin or floured cloth for quite five hours
7. The peel of a lemon grated, and a few pieces of citron cut thin may be added
