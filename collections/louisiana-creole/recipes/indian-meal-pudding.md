## Ingredients

- 8 ounces of mush
- 6 ounces of butter
- 6 ounces of sugar
- yolks of 6 eggs
- 1 white
- nutmeg
- mace
- wine

## Directions

1. Mix the butter in the mush while it is warm
2. beat the eggs very light, mix the sugar with them, and add them to the mush when cool
3. season with nutmeg and mace to the taste, also a little wine
