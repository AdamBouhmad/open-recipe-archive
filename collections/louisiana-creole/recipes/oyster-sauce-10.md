## Ingredients

- 2 dozen oysters
- The oyster water
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Herb bouquet

## Directions

1. Boil the oysters in their own water
2. Add a nice herb bouquet while boiling
3. Take a tablespoonful of butter and one of flour and put into a saucepan, and mix well without browning
4. Water this with the juice of the oysters, sufficient to make one pint; season to taste
5. Let it boil for ten or fifteen minutes, and when it reaches a thick consistency, serve with freshly added oysters, taking the old ones out
6. This is a sauce for boiled fish, etc.
