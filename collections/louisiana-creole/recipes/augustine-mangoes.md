## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of horse radish
- 1 lb. of white mustard seed
- 1 lb. of white pepper
- 1 lb. of celery seed
- 2 ounces of mace
- 2 ounces of cloves
- 2 ounces of nutmeg
- 1 lb. of garlic
- 1 lb. of turmeric
- 1 bottle of pure sweet oil
- 1 bottle of mustard
- mangoes
- boiling vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well together
2. After you have greened your mangoes for three days, wipe them dry
3. Stuff them with the prepared ingredients
4. Pour boiling vinegar over them
5. Cork them closely and put away for use
6. All the ingredients must be well beaten
