## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. of flour
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1 pint of molasses
- 6 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- juice of 1 lemon
- rind of 1 lemon
- raisins and citron

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 teaspoonful of soda in the juice of a lemon and add the rind
2. Mix all ingredients together
3. Bake in cake pans
4. Raisins and citron are a great improvement
