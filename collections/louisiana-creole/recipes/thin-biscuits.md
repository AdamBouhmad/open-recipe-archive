## Ingredients

- 2 ounces of butter
- skimmed milk
- 1 lb. of flour

## Directions

1. Warm 2 ounces of butter in as much skimmed milk as will make 1 lb. of flour into a stiff paste
2. Beat it with a rolling pin, and work it smooth
3. Roll it thin, and cut it into round biscuits
4. Prick them full of holes with a fork
5. Bake for about 6 minutes
