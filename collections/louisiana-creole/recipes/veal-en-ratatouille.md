## Ingredients

- 4 Pounds of Brisket of Veal
- 1/2 Pound of Ham
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 1/2 Can of Tomatoes, or 6 Fresh
- 4 Dozen Fresh Okra
- 2 Large Onions
- 2 Sweet Potatoes
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 2 Sprigs of Parsley
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard or Butter
- 1/2 Sweet Pepper Pod

## Directions

1. Cut the veal into pieces of about three inches in length and two in width
2. Cut the ham into dice shape, and the sweet potatoes, after peeling, into cubes of about one and a half inches
3. Add a half sweet pepper pod, if possible, being careful to extract all the seeds
4. Season the veal highly
5. Put a tablespoonful of lard into the stew pot, add the veal and let it brown nicely
6. Then add the ham, the sweet potatoes and the pepper pod, and let them simmer gently for about fifteen minutes
7. Place a tablespoonful of butter into a saucepan, and when it melts add the chopped onion
8. As they brown nicely, add the minced herbs and garlic, and then the tomatoes sliced and chopped, in their own liquor
9. Let this stew for about fifteen minutes, and then add this to the stewing veal
10. Mix thoroughly and set back on the stove, covering tightly, and let it simmer slowly and constantly, with a regular fire, for about two hours
11. Then add the okras, which have been tipped and sliced very thinly
12. Let the mixture simmer for a half hour longer, and serve
