## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Butter
- Milk
- Salt and pepper
- Whites of 2 eggs
- Egg yolks
- Gruyere cheese (optional)

## Directions

1. Bake the potatoes, and when they are done, cut off the tops, and scoop out the meat into a hot bowl
2. Mash very fine, and add the butter, the milk and salt and pepper
3. Add a little grated Gruyere cheese if desired
4. Beat all till very light, and add the beaten whites of two eggs, and stir lightly
5. Fill the potato skins with this preparation, heaping the flaky potatoes nicely on top
6. Brush over lightly with the yolks of the eggs
7. Set in the oven to brown and serve hot
