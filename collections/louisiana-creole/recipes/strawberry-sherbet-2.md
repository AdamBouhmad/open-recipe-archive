## Ingredients

- fifteen ounces of picked strawberries
- one quart of water
- one sliced lemon
- one teaspoonful of orange-flower water
- eighteen ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Crush strawberries in a mortar
2. Add to them a quart of water
3. Pour this into a basin with a sliced lemon and a teaspoonful of orange-flower water
4. Let it remain for two or three hours
5. Put eighteen ounces of sugar into another basin and cover it with a cloth
6. Pour the strawberry juice through the cloth
7. Gather up the cloth and squeeze out as much juice as possible from it
8. When the sugar is all dissolved, strain it again
9. Set the vessel containing it on ice until ready to serve
