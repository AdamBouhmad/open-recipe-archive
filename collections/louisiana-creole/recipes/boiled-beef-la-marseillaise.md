## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Small Onions
- ¾ Cup Claret
- ¾ Cup Meat Gravy
- ½ Can of Mushrooms
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- ½ of a Grated Nutmeg
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Slice the bouilli into thin, fine slices.
2. Take a dozen onions, the smallest kind, and dust over with sugar, and bake in the oven.
3. When a good color, put a little of the bouillon in the stewing pan and boil down one half.
4. Moisten with a cup of red wine and thick meat sauce, allowing half and half in proportion.
5. Then add the beef, the mushrooms, the bouquet garni, salt, pepper and a little nutmeg, and serve very hot.
