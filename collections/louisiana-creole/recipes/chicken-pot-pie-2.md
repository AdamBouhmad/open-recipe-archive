## Ingredients

- chicken
- fat salt pork
- butter
- pepper
- light crust

## Directions

1. Cut up a chicken
2. Parboil it, save the liquor it was boiled in
3. Wash out the kettle, or take another one
4. In it fry three or four slices of fat salt pork
5. Put it in the bottom of the dish in which the pie is to be made
6. Put in the chicken and the liquor
7. Also a piece of butter the size of a teacup
8. Sprinkle in some pepper
9. Cover with a light crust
10. Bake an hour
