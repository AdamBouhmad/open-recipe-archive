## Ingredients

- beef
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- onion
- mushroom or tomato catsup
- broth stock or gravy-soup
- bread

## Directions

1. Slice some beef in very thin pieces, season with pepper and salt, and shake a little flour over it.
2. Chop a medium sized onion and put it (without the beef) into a stew-pan with a tablespoonful of mushroom or tomato catsup.
3. Boil for a few minutes, then add a pint of broth stock, or gravy-soup; boil it down to half the quantity.
4. Five minutes before serving, throw in the cold sliced beef; let it boil five minutes and serve on toasted bread.
