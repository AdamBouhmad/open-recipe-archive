## Ingredients

- Left Over Sponge Cake
- 1 Glass Sherry Wine
- Brandy or Sherry Wine
- A Boiled Custard
- Blanched Lemons

## Directions

1. Saturate left-over sponge cake with Brandy or Sherry Wine
2. Take a rich Boiled Custard
3. Place the cake in a deep glass dish
4. Pour the custard over it
5. Decorate with blanched lemons
6. Serve cold
