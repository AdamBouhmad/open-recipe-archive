## Ingredients

- half a pound of sifted flour
- one ounce of butter
- a quarter of a pound of fine sugar
- one egg
- half a teaspoonful of caraway seeds
- milk enough to make a paste

## Directions

1. Rub butter and sugar into flour
2. add egg and caraway seeds
3. add milk to make a paste
4. roll out to quarter-of-an-inch thickness or thinner
5. cut in small round cakes
6. bake on tin plates in a quick oven ten or twelve minutes
