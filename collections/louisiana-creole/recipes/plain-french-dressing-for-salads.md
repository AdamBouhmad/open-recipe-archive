## Ingredients

- 3 Tablespoonfuls of the Best Olive Oil
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vinegar, According to Taste
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Black Pepper

## Directions

1. First put the oil into a small bowl.
2. Then add gradually the salt and pepper until all are thoroughly mixed.
3. Then add gradually the vinegar, stirring continually for about a minute.
4. It is now ready to pour over the salad, and remember that it must be mixed thoroughly.
