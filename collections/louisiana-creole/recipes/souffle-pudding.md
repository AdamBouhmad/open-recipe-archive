## Ingredients

- one pint of milk
- one cup of flour
- one spoonful of sugar
- a piece of butter as large as an egg
- yolks of five eggs
- whites of five eggs

## Directions

1. Scald the milk, flour, and butter together.
2. After the batter becomes cold, stir in the yolks of five eggs.
3. Just before baking, stir in the whites.
4. Bake in a quick oven, and serve with sauce.
