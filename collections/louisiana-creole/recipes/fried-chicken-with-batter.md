## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- small tablespoonful of butter
- wheat flour
- salt
- 1 pair of chickens

## Directions

1. Make a light batter with 3 eggs, a small tablespoonful of butter, a little wheat flour, and salt to the taste
2. Joint the chicken
3. Put the chicken into the batter
4. Grease the frying pan
5. Throw the mixture of chicken and batter into it and fry a good brown
