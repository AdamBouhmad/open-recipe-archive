## Ingredients

- tomatoes (small and green)
- clarified syrup
- orange (1 per 2 lbs. of tomatoes) or lemon (2 per 3 lbs. of tomatoes)

## Directions

1. Take the tomatoes while small and green
2. Put them in cold clarified syrup, with 1 orange, cut in slices, to every 2 lbs. of tomatoes
3. If very superior preserves are wanted, add, instead of the oranges, 2 fresh lemons to 3 lbs. of tomatoes
4. Simmer them over a slow fire for 2 or 3 hours
