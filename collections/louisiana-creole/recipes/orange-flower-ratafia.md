## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Orange Blossoms
- 10 Pints of Brandy
- 4 Pounds of Sugar
- 1¾ Pints of Water

## Directions

1. Steep two pounds of orange blossoms, well bruised and pounded, in ten and a half pints of good brandy for fifteen days.
2. Add a syrup made of four pounds of sugar and one and three-quarters pints of water.
3. Filter and bottle.
