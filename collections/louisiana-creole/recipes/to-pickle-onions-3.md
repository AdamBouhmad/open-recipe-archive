## Ingredients

- onions
- strong salt water
- vinegar
- whole pepper
- mustard seed

## Directions

1. Peel the onions
2. Boil some strong salt and water
3. Put it over them
4. Cover
5. Let them stand twenty-four hours
6. Take them up with a skimmer
7. Make some vinegar boiling hot
8. Put to it whole pepper and mustard seed
9. Pour it over the onions to cover them
10. When cold cover close
