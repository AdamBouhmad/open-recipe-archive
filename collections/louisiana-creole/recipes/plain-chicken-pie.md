## Ingredients

- chickens
- fat meat
- onions
- Irish potatoes
- pepper
- salt
- parsley
- sweet butter
- eggs
- milk
- crust

## Directions

1. Take two nice chickens, or more if they are small
2. Cut them up as for frying
3. Put them in a pot to stew with some slices of fat meat
4. Let them cook for half an hour
5. Add a few onions and four Irish potatoes sliced small
6. Season with pepper, salt, a little parsley, and a quarter of a pound of sweet butter
7. When it is cooked well there should be gravy enough to cover the chickens
8. If you want it very nice, beat up two eggs, and stir into the stew with half a pint of milk
9. Line a five-quart pan with a crust made like soda biscuit, only more shortening
10. Put in the chickens and gravy
11. Cover with a top crust
12. Bake until the crust is done
