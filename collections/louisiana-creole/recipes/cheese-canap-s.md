## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of Swiss Cheese
- 6 Slices of Toast
- 1 Gill of Sauce Piquante

## Directions

1. Take six slices of bread cut the whole width of the loaf, one-half inch in thickness, and hollow out one-half of the inner portion.
2. Toast this nicely and spray the inner part with Piquante Sauce.
3. Have ready six slices of toasted or baked Swiss cheese; fit a slice into each cavity in the sliced toast.
4. Set in the oven for a few minutes and serve very hot.
