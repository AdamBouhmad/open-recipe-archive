## Ingredients

- sponge cake
- wine
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls finely powdered sugar
- 1 glass sweet wine
- 1 pint cream

## Directions

1. Put some thin slices of sponge cake in the bottom of a dish
2. Pour in wine enough to soak it
3. Beat up the whites of 3 eggs very hard
4. Add to it 2 tablespoonfuls of finely powdered sugar, a glass of sweet wine, 1 pint of cream
5. Beat well and pour over the cake
