## Ingredients

- hard boiled egg
- raw veal
- pepper
- salt
- herbs (such as parsley, thyme and sage)
- bacon
- mace
- floured cloth

## Directions

1. Cut a hard boiled egg in thin slices, and place it in the middle of a bowl
2. Put a layer of raw veal cut in thin slices, and sprinkled with a mixture of pepper, salt and herbs (such as parsley, thyme and sage)
3. Place next very thin slices of bacon, and continue to put alternate layers of veal, seasoned with a little mace and of bacon, until the bowl is filled
4. Mash it down and tie a floured cloth over it very tight
5. Turn it down in a pot of hot water, and let it boil 2 hours
6. It is eaten cold
