## Ingredients

- 1/4 peck of okra
- 1/2 peck of potatoes
- shin or leg of beef
- 10 quarts of cold water
- cayenne or black pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut up in small pieces a quarter of a peck of okra
2. Skin one-half a peck of potatoes
3. Put them with a shin or leg of beef into 10 quarts of cold water
4. Boil it gently for 7 hours, skimming if well
5. Season with cayenne or black pepper and salt
