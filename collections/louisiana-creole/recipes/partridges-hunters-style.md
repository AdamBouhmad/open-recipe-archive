## Ingredients

- 3 Fine Partridges
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Finely Chopped Onion
- 12 Whole Mushrooms
- 2 Sprigs Each of Thyme and Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- ¼ Glass of Sherry or Madeira Wine
- 1 Cup of Water or Consomme
- Croutons

## Directions

1. Clean the Partridges; singe, draw and wipe well
2. Cut up the birds as you would a young chicken
3. Rub well with salt and pepper, and place in a stewpan with two tablespoonfuls of butter
4. Let them brown well on either side, about three minutes
5. Add the finely-chopped onion and carrot, and the minced herbs
6. Let these brown for two minutes
7. Add the flour and let all brown nicely
8. Add a cup of water or consommé and the wine and the chopped mushrooms
9. Cover closely and cook for fifteen minutes
10. Serve, using toasted Croutons as garnish
