## Ingredients

- Three and a half cups of flour
- one cup of sweet lard
- one teaspoonful of salt
- one teaspoonful of baking powder
- a cupful of very cold water

## Directions

1. Mix with a knife, using the hands as little as possible
2. Roll and cut after the crust is on the pie-plate
