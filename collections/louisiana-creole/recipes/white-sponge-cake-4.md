## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Pulverized White Sugar
- 1 Cup of Flour
- The Whites of 11 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla Extract

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of the eggs and the sugar to a thick froth
2. Mix the baking powder and the flour, adding the powder after the fourth sifting
3. Then add the flour gradually to the eggs, beating lightly and thoroughly
4. Add the essence, and bake for from thirty to forty-five minutes in a quick oven
