## Ingredients

- Spare Ribs
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- A Garnish of Parsley and Radish
- Butter

## Directions

1. Dredge the spare ribs lightly with salt and pepper, after having washed well and wiped dry with a coarse towel
2. Place them in the baking pan and dredge with butter
3. Place them in the oven and cover with a piece of buttered paper
4. Allow twenty minutes to every pound in cooking
5. About twenty minutes before serving take off the buttered paper, dredge again with melted butter, and let it brown nicely
6. Serve with a garnish of parsley and radishes
