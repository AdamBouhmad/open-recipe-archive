## Ingredients

- 1 pint of molasses
- ½ lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of good brown sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of ground ginger
- 1 gill of cream
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. Heat together well with a spoon: molasses, butter, brown sugar, ground ginger, and cream
2. Add as much sifted flour as will make it stiff enough to bake
3. When done, serve it up with cold sauce of sugar and butter creamed together
