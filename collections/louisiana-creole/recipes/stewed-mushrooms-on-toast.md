## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- tablespoonful of butter
- salt
- pepper
- powdered mace
- slice of bread

## Directions

1. Pull out the stems of the mushrooms, and peel them
2. Melt a tablespoonful of butter in a stew pan
3. Throw into the butter a little salt, pepper, and powdered mace (if liked)
4. Lay the mushrooms in this, upper side down
5. Stew till they are tender, which will be in about twenty minutes
6. Fry a slice of bread until it is a light brown
7. Arrange the mushrooms over it
8. Serve hot
