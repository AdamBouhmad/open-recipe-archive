## Ingredients

- fleshy part of a rump of beef
- thyme
- parsley
- salt
- small white onions
- carrots
- cloves
- allspice
- 2 teaspoonfuls of brown sugar

## Directions

1. Take the fleshy part of a rump of beef and put it on the fire early in the morning to boil
2. Skim all the grease off
3. Put in thyme, parsley and salt with the rump
4. After the soup is sufficiently boiled and the grease carefully taken off, put in the small white onions and a few carrots cut round
5. Add cloves and allspice
6. Just before you are ready to dish the soup, put two teaspoonfuls of brown sugar to turn it dark
