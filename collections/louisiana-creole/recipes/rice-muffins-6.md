## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Boiled Rice
- 1 Cup of Flour
- 3 Eggs
- 1½ Pints of Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard or Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- ½ teaspoonful of baking powder

## Directions

1. Mash the rice through a close sieve
2. Beat the yolks very light and add
3. Add the milk and butter
4. Sift in the flour and half a teaspoonful of baking powder
5. Add the sugar and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
6. Add the salt
7. Bake in muffin rings in a quick oven for about fifteen or twenty minutes
