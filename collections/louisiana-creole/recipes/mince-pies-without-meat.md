## Ingredients

- 6 lbs. of the best apples, cored and minced
- 3 lbs. of raisins stoned and minced
- 3 lbs. of hard boiled eggs, cut very fine
- 3 lbs. of powdered sugar
- ½ ounce of salt
- ½ ounce of cinnamon
- ½ ounce of nutmeg
- ½ ounce of mace
- 8 finely powdered cloves
- grated peel of 4 lemons
- juice of 2 lemons
- ½ pint of brandy
- ½ pint of wine
- 4 lbs. of currants
- candied fruit

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well together and put into a deep pan
2. Have ready, washed and dried, currants and as you make the pies add them with candied fruit
