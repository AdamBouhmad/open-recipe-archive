## Ingredients

- cold roast veal
- fat ham
- a cup of grated bread or cracker crumbs
- two well-beaten eggs
- salt
- pepper (black and red)
- yolk of egg
- fine cracker crumbs

## Directions

1. Mince cold roast veal as fine as possible.
2. Add a fourth part as much fat ham, a cup of grated bread or cracker crumbs, and two well-beaten eggs to bind the crumbs together.
3. Season with salt, and pepper (black and red).
4. Mix and form it into a loaf.
5. Glaze the outside with yolk of egg, and sprinkle over it fine cracker crumbs.
6. Bake half an hour, and serve with gravy made from the bones of the veal.
7. Serve the gravy hot.
