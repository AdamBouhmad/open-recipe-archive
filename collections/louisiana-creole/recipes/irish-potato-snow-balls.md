## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes
- the yolk of an egg
- a small piece of butter
- bread crumbs
- salt
- good lard

## Directions

1. After boiled tender, drain off the water
2. Let the potatoes steam till they break to pieces
3. Mix them with the yolk of an egg, a small piece of butter, bread crumbs, with salt to the taste
4. Form them into balls
5. Fry them in good lard or brown them in an oven
