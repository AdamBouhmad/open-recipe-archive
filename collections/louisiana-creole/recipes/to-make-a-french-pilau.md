## Ingredients

- 1 pair of fowls
- rice
- butter
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil a pair of fowls
2. When done, take them out and put the rice in the same water, first taking out some of the liquor
3. When the rice is done, butter it well
4. Cover the bottom of the dish with 1/2 of it
5. Put the fowls on it and add the remainder of the liquor
6. Cover the fowls with the other 1/2 of the rice
7. Make it smooth
8. Spread over it the yolks of 2 eggs, well beaten
9. Bake in a moderate oven
