## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. flour
- 5 lbs. sugar
- 5 lbs. butter
- 10 lbs. currants
- 5 lbs. raisins stoned and cut
- 37 eggs
- 2 ounces cloves
- 2 ounces nutmeg
- 1 ounce cinnamon
- 1 ounce mace
- 2 lbs. citron
- 0.5 pint brandy
- 0.5 pint wine
- 0.5 pint rose water

## Directions

1. Bake 5 hours or more
