## Ingredients

- 1 coffee cup of butter
- 1 1/2 coffee cups of sugar
- 1 coffee cup of milk
- 3 coffee cups of flour
- 4 eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients well together
2. Bake
