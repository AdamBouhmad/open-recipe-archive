## Ingredients

- 3 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 1 Raw Yolk of Egg
- 1 Tablespoonful of Tarragon Vinegar
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Olive Oil
- 1/8 Clove of Garlic, Minced Very Fine
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Prepared Mustard
- Salt and Cayenne to Taste
- About Two Tablespoonfuls of Spinach or Parsley Juice

## Directions

1. Make in exactly the same manner as Sauce Rémoulade
2. Color with the juice of spinach or parsley, using about two tablespoonfuls of either
