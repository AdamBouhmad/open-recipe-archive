## Ingredients

- duck
- potatoes
- butter
- onions
- parsley
- water
- giblets
- gravy
- catsup
- brown flour
- lemons

## Directions

1. Pick, draw and singe the duck; wash it out carefully and stuff it with potatoes, mashed with butter, onions, and parsley.
2. Put it down to a good fire or in a hot oven, pour in a cup of water.
3. Let it roast for half an hour if it is fat and tender, longer if tough.
4. As soon as the duck is cleaned, boil the giblets, and before serving, chop them up fine with some of the gravy from the duck, two tablespoonfuls of catsup, a lump of butter, and a little brown flour.
5. Have lemons cut on side dishes, or serve with brown duck sauce.
