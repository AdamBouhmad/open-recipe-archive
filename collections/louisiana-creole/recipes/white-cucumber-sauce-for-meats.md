## Ingredients

- four or five cucumbers
- three-quarters of a pint of veal stock
- yolks of three eggs
- cayenne pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Peel and take out the seeds from the cucumbers, cut them into very small pieces and put them into a saucepan with the stock and seasoning
2. Simmer it slowly until they are tender
3. Stir in the yolks of the eggs well beaten
4. Make it very hot, but do not let it boil
5. Serve it up quickly
