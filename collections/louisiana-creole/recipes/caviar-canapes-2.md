## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of French Toast
- ½ of a Box of Russian Caviar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the toast and cut in delicate circles
2. Put half the contents of a small box of Russian Caviars into a saucepan
3. Add two tablespoonfuls of cream
4. Heat one and a half minutes on the stove
5. Stir constantly
6. Pour this over the toast
7. Place on a dish on a folded napkin and serve
8. Alternatively, the circles of toast may be used as a foundation, the edges being spread with Anchovy butter, with an onion ring at its base
9. Fill this decorated ring with the Caviars
10. Place on a folded napkin on a dish and serve
