## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream
- % Cup of Sugar
- % Ounce of Gelatine
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Dissolve the gelatine in water
2. Mix with the milk and cream
3. Let it come to a boil
4. Add the Vanilla
5. Pour into a mold
6. Let it harden
7. Serve cold
