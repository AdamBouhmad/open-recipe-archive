## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Bread Crumbs
- 2 Cups of Chopped Apples
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1/2 Spoonful Each of Ground Spices

## Directions

1. Utilize stale bread crumbs for this pudding.
2. Butter the bottom of a baking pan, and put in a layer of the apples, which you will have stewed nicely.
3. Put over this a layer of bread crumbs, and sprinkle with sugar and dot with butter.
4. Sprinkle lightly with the spices.
5. Continue this till you have used up all the apples.
6. Put a layer of bread crumbs on top.
7. Place in a moderate oven, and bake to a nice brown.
8. Serve hot, with Cream Sauce or Hard Sauce.
