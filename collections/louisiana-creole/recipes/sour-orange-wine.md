## Ingredients

- One gallon juice of sour oranges
- Four gallons of water
- Twenty pounds of sugar
- A quart of uncooked orange juice

## Directions

1. Take one gallon juice of sour oranges, four gallons of water, and twenty pounds of sugar
2. Boil this mixture in a vessel large enough to hold it, and skim it as it boils until no more scum rises
3. Pour it into a flannel bag and strain
4. Put it in a cask, adding to it a quart of uncooked orange juice
5. Let it ferment
