## Ingredients

- yolks of five eggs
- half a pound of sugar
- half a pound of blanched almonds
- rind of a lemon
- sifted flour
- whites of two eggs

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of five eggs with half a pound of sugar
2. Add half a pound of blanched almonds, cut fine or pounded
3. Grate the rind of a lemon, mix well, and add gradually enough sifted flour to make into a dough
4. Roll out and cut in strips the length and size of the forefinger
5. wet them with the beaten white of two eggs, and bake
