## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Louisiana Oranges
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Juice of Half a Lemon
- 3/4 Cup of Water

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges, and cut them into halves crosswise, and take out the seeds and the inner pulp.
2. Put the sugar and the water on to boil, and after ten minutes add the lemon juice.
3. Then put a few of the oranges at a time into the hot syrup, and let them stew gently.
4. After a half hour take them out, lay in a dish, pour over the hot liquid syrup and serve either hot or cold.
