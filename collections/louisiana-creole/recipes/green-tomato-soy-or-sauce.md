## Ingredients

- a peck of green tomatoes
- a pint of salt
- a dozen raw onions
- two tablespoonfuls of black pepper
- two tablespoonfuls of allspice
- a quarter of a spoonful of ground mustard
- half a pound of white mustard seed
- a tablespoonful of red pepper
- strong vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice a peck of green tomatoes thin
2. salt them thoroughly, using a pint of salt
3. Let them stay in this all night
4. in the morning drain them from the salt
5. wash them in cold water
6. put them in a kettle with a dozen cut-up raw onions, two tablespoonfuls of black pepper, same of allspice, a quarter of a spoonful of ground mustard, half a pound of white mustard seed, and a tablespoonful of red pepper
7. Cover all with strong vinegar
8. boil it until it becomes like jam
9. Stir it frequently while it is boiling or it will scorch
