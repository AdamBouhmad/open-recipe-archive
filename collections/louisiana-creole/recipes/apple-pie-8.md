## Ingredients

- 4 Large Apples (tart)
- 1 Cup Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful Sweet Spices, Blended Equally
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter

## Directions

1. Pare and slice the apples nicely.
2. Then put them in a porcelain-lined saucepan, and let them stew gently, adding the water and the butter.
3. Season well with a little ground cinnamon, mace and allspice.
4. When done, take off and allow to cool a little.
5. Make a flaky pie crust (see recipe), and line the tin plates, after greasing slightly with butter, and bake the bottom crust slightly brown.
6. Take out and fill in with the apples, and then cover the top with a thin layer of crust.
7. Decorate the edges, and set in the oven to bake.
8. When a nice, delicate brown, take out and let them cool.
9. Remove from the tin plate by slightly loosening the outer edge with a knife.
10. Turn over into a china plate, sprinkle with white powdered sugar, and serve cold or hot.
