## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Fresh Peanuts
- The Zest of 1 Lemon
- 1 Gill of Water
- 1/2 Pint each of Milk and Cream
- 1 1/2 Ounces of Powdered White Sugar
- 1 Ounce of Gelatine

## Directions

1. Peel four ounces of fresh peanuts, and pound them into a flour.
2. Add the grated zest of a lemon, and a little water, sufficient to make all into a thick paste.
3. Boil equal quantities of milk and cream to equal a pint and a half, and add an ounce and a half of white powdered sugar.
4. Let it cool, and add a piece of gelatine, blended with one spoon of water.
5. Mix well, and then strain through a sieve.
6. Put the cream back on the fire to heat, and add the peanuts.
7. Let all come to a good boil.
8. Take off and set it to cool in a cool place, and serve cold.
