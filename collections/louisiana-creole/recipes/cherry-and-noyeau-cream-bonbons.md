## Ingredients

- 2 Ounces of Soaked Gum Arabic
- 2 Pounds of Sugar
- 1 Gill of Cherry Juice
- 3/4 Gill of Noyeau
- The Whites of 2 Eggs

## Directions

1. Work the gum, Noyeau and sugar to an elastic paste
2. Use the cherry juice and whites of the eggs and some of the sugar for the Royal Icing
3. Proceed as in Chocolate Cream Bonbons with the white Noyeau Cream forming the inside and the cherry-colored icing the outside
