## Ingredients

- 1 quart of good flour
- a pinch of salt
- 1 spoonful of yeast
- 2 eggs well beaten
- ½ pint of milk
- 1 ounce of butter

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, salt, yeast, eggs and milk together
2. Knead it and set it to rise
3. Next morning work in the butter
4. Make the dough into small rolls and bake them
5. The top crust should not be hard
