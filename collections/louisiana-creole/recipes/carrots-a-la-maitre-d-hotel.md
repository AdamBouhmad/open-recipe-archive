## Ingredients

- 4 Good-Sized Carrots
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 3 Sprigs Each of Thyme and Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Scrape the carrots and boil them at the same time that you are making your soup, or bouillon, leaving them whole.
2. When done and ready to serve, skim out of the soup, and place in a dish.
3. Cut them into pieces, more or less large, and then place them in a frying pan with a tablespoonful of butter, minced parsley, thyme, bay leaf.
4. Add salt and pepper to taste, and when they have cooked for about ten or fifteen minutes pour over a Sauce a la Maitre d'Hotel, and serve hot.
