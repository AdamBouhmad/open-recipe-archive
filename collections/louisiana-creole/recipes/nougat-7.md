## Ingredients

- 10 Ounces of Sifted Sugar
- 1 Pound of Shredded Almonds, Dried
- A Few Drops of Essence of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Put the almonds on a baking sheet or plate at the entrance of the oven and let them heat through and through
2. In the meanwhile put the sugar in a pan or porcelain-lined saucepan, and set on a moderate stove
3. Keep stirring with a wooden spoon until it begins to melt
4. Then quicken the stirring, and as soon as the sugar begins to form upon the surface in small white bubbles, like pearls, immediately throw in the almonds
5. Stir all gently until thoroughly mixed, and then pour out on a wet marble slab, and flatten to one-sixth of an inch in thickness
6. As soon as it begins to thicken, cut into pieces of about three inches long and two wide, or, if you have fancy molds, line them thinly with the Nougat, having previously slightly buttered them on the inside
7. Cut the edges of the Nougat even and level before it becomes cold, for then it is brittle, and breaks easily
