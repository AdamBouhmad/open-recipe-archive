## Ingredients

- fish
- butter
- parsley
- onions
- allspice
- pepper
- salt
- flour

## Directions

1. After the fish is well cleaned, take off the fins and tail
2. Cut it into 4 or 5 parts
3. Lay in a deep dish some lumps of butter, parsley and onions
4. Chop all very fine with a little allspice
5. Make a layer of fish, well seasoned with pepper and salt, flour it
6. Continue this layering until the dish is full
7. Bake 1 hour
