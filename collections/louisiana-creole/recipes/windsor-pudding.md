## Ingredients

- 1 pint of milk
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 4 or 6 eggs
- A piece of butter
- 1 ounce of almonds (half bitter)
- A little lemon peel
- Juice of 1 lemon
- i lb. of loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Thicken 1 pint of milk with 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
2. Boil and let it stand till cold
3. Add 4 or 6 eggs, a piece of butter, 1 ounce of almonds (half bitter), a little lemon peel, the juice of 1 lemon and i lb. of loaf sugar
4. Bake in cups
