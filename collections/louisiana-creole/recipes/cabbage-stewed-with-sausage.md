## Ingredients

- 2 dozen fine sausage (chaurice preferred)
- 1 head of cabbage
- 1 tablespoonful of lard
- Salt to taste
- 1 inch of red pepper pod
- 1 pound fresh pork

## Directions

1. Wash the cabbage well after having cut it into quarters and allowed it to soak half an hour
2. Cut the cabbage into shreds of about one inch in width and five in length
3. Scald the cabbage with boiling water for about fifteen minutes
4. Throw off this water and cover it again with boiling water and let it boil for twenty minutes
5. Prick each sausage in several places
6. Cut the meat into pieces of about two inches in length and one in thickness
7. Put the lard in the frying pan and fry the sausage and meat until they are about half done
8. Drain the cabbage and turn into the sausage and meat and the fat drippings
9. Cover and stew gently where it will not scorch for at least forty minutes
10. Season with salt and add an inch of red pepper pod
11. Let all simmer till the cabbage is quite done
12. Put in a dish with the sausage and meat piled in the center and the cabbage heaped around as a border
