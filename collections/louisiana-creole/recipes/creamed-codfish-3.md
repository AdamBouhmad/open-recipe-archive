## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Picked Codfish
- 1 Pint and a Half of Cream
- The Yolk of 1 Egg
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Pepper
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Soak the codfish overnight, and then let it boil about forty minutes.
2. Take off and scald again and drain, and again scald and drain, allowing it to stand each time about four or five minutes before changing the water.
3. Put one large tablespoonful of butter in the frying pan; when melted add the flour and blend, without browning.
4. Add the milk, stirring constantly until it boils, and then add the fish, seasoning highly with pepper and Cayenne.
5. Let it boil about ten minutes longer, and take off the fire.
6. Add the yolk of an egg which has been beaten thoroughly.
