## Ingredients

- a thick slice of very nice toast
- a small pint of the best West India molasses
- a heaped tablespoonful of ground ginger
- a pint of hot water

## Directions

1. Lay the toast in a pint bowl
2. Stir the ground ginger into the molasses
3. Mix the molasses with a pint of hot water
4. Pour the whole over the toast
5. Cover it with a plate for a few minutes
6. Eat it while warm, previous to going to bed
