## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Cold water
- White sugar
- Peel

## Directions

1. Quarter the oranges and take out the seeds and white strings
2. To every pound of pulp, add a cup of cold water, and let it stand thus for twenty-four hours
3. Boil some of the peel in several waters until quite tender
4. To each pound of pulp, add one-quarter of a pound of boiled peel, and one and a quarter pounds of white sugar
5. Boil this slowly until it jellies, and the bits of peel are quite transparent
