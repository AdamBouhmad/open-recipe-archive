## Ingredients

- 3 Large Cold Boiled Potatoes
- 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 9 Tablespoonfuls of Olive Oil
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Vinegar
- 1 Large Onion
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 4 Sprigs of Parsley

## Directions

1. Pare and peel the potatoes, if freshly boiled, and let them cool.
2. Prepare the salad dressing, following implicitly the directions given for plain French dressing, only here, the quantities are larger in proportion.
3. Add the vinegar, stirring constantly. A dash of mustard may be added, if desired.
4. Mince the onion very fine, and cut the potatoes into dice or slices, and mix them carefully with the onion.
5. Then add the dressing, turning the potatoes into it without breaking.
6. Sprinkle all with parsley, nicely chopped, and serve cold.
