## Ingredients

- 3 fresh lemons
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar pounded
- ½ lb. of good fresh butter
- yolks of 6 eggs
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Grate with care the oily rind of 3 fresh lemons
2. Rub this with loaf sugar pounded until perfectly incorporated with sugar
3. Add by degrees fresh butter
4. Beat very light the yolks of 6 eggs
5. Mix all well together
6. Line a dish with puff paste and put in the above mixture
7. Bake ¾ hour
8. Serve hot
