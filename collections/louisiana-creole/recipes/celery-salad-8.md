## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Crisp French Celery
- 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- French Dressing

## Directions

1. Cut the celery into pieces of about a quarter of an inch
2. Chop two hard-boiled eggs, not too fine, and mix well with the chopped celery
3. Blend all with French dressing and serve
