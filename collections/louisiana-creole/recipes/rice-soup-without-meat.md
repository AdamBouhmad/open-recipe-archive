## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Rice
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 3 Quarts of Water
- 1 Spoon of Butter
- 1 Pint of Milk
- Pepper and Salt
- Cream

## Directions

1. Wash the rice thoroughly, rubbing dry.
2. Put it in a saucepan with one pint of cold water; when swelled, add one pint of boiling water; and when it begins to get very tender, add the remaining pint of boiling water.
3. Add the pepper and salt.
4. Beat up the yolks of the eggs with a few tablespoonfuls of cream.
5. When quite smooth, stir in carefully a few spoonfuls of the boiling rice water, and then pour the eggs and cream into the saucepan, stirring very briskly.
6. Draw aside and stir for two or three minutes, but do not allow the soup to boil when once the eggs are added.
