## Ingredients

- 2 Fine Hares
- 2 Onions
- 2 Carrots
- A Half Glass of White Wine
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard or Butter
- 1 Cup of Broth or Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Take two fine Hares, clean neatly and cut the filets neatly from the rack.
2. Lard the surface carefully with fine needles.
3. Season well with salt and pepper.
4. Make a marinade with half a glass of White wine, one onion and one carrot, minced very fine.
5. Let all steep together for two hours.
6. Place the butter or lard in a baking dish with an onion and carrot, sliced fine.
7. Put the filets of Hare over this and set in the oven and let it cook for a half hour.
8. Baste frequently with the Hare's own juices.
9. Place the filets on a hot dish, add a tablespoonful of broth to the gravy in which the Hares were cooked.
10. Let all come to a boil on the stove.
11. Strain the gravy and pour over the filets.
12. Bring to the table hot and serve with a Sauce a la Poivrade.
