## Ingredients

- 1 Chicken
- 1 Onion
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme, Bay Leaf and Parsley
- Pie Paste

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the chicken into small pieces of about two inches in length and make a plain fricassee.
2. Prepare a Vol-au-Vent Paste and fill a tin pan of about two quarts with the Paste.
3. Pour in the chicken and gravy, and let it bake in the oven till the top crust is nicely browned.
4. Always bake the under crust first.
