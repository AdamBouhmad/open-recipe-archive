## Ingredients

- a tumbler of sifted flour
- a tumbler of milk
- two eggs
- a teaspoonful of yeast powder
- a teaspoonful of melted butter or lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Put a teaspoonful of yeast powder in the flour before sifting
2. beat the eggs separately
3. Mix all together
4. add a teaspoonful of melted butter or lard just before baking in little fancy pans
5. Put salt in the flour with the yeast powder
6. bake as quickly as you can
