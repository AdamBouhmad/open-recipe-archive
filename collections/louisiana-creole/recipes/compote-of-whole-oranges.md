## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Louisiana Oranges
- 1 Pound of Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the oranges, with their peel, gently in water for twenty minutes.
2. Turn the oranges very thinly and spirally, scoring out, if possible, all the inner pulp and peeling.
3. Make a syrup by boiling the sugar, and add the oranges.
4. Let them simmer for twenty minutes longer.
5. Take off, and allow the oranges to remain in their juice for several hours.
6. Dish them up in a compote dish, and pour over their syrup on the instant of sending to the table.
