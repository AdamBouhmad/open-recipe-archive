## Ingredients

- a pint of minced veal
- three heads of celery
- yolks of four hard-boiled eggs
- a tablespoonful of dry mustard
- a large spoonful of olive oil
- four tablespoonfuls of good wine vinegar
- cayenne
- salt
- parsley
- celery leaves

## Directions

1. To a pint of minced veal add three heads of celery.
2. Make a dressing of the yolks of four hard-boiled eggs, a tablespoonful of dry mustard, and a large spoonful of olive oil.
3. Beat this dressing till perfectly smooth.
4. Add to it slowly (to keep from curdling) four tablespoonfuls of good wine vinegar, a little cayenne and salt.
5. Garnish the dish with parsley and celery leaves.
