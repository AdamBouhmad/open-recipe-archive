## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pound of Dried Fruit
- 3/4 Pound of Sugar
- 3/4 Cup of Water
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Allow the dried fruit to soak in cold water a long while, say about two hours, to swell up.
2. Then boil, and afterwards pour into a syrup of boiling sugar, and proceed as in other compotes.
