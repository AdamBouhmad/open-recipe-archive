## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- ¾ Cup of Powdered Sugar
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Set the milk to boil
2. Beat the eggs and sugar to a thick cream and very light, and then stir them into the boiling milk
3. Stir over the fire till the sauce begins to thicken, and no longer
4. If you stir too long the sauce will curdle
5. Take it from the fire and add the Vanilla and serve cold
