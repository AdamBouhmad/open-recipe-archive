## Ingredients

- white cabbage
- 1 pint best cider vinegar
- 1 lb. fresh butter
- flour
- salt
- cayenne
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Take a nice, fresh, white cabbage
2. Wash and drain it and cut off the stalk
3. Shave it in small and very even shreds with a cabbage cutter or a sharp knife
4. Put it into a deep china dish
5. Take 1 pint of best cider vinegar and mix it with 1 lb. of fresh butter divided into 4 bits and rolled in flour
6. Add a small saltspoon of salt and the same quantity of cayenne
7. Stir this all well together and boil it in a saucepan
8. Have ready the yolks of 4 eggs nicely beaten
9. As soon as the mixture has boiled, take it off the fire and stir in the beaten egg
10. Pour it boiling hot over the shred cabbage and mix it well all through with a spoon
11. Set it to cool on ice or in the open air
12. It must be perfectly cold before it is placed on the table
