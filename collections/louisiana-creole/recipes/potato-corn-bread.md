## Ingredients

- 1 quart of fine cornmeal
- a pint of milk
- a half pound of sweet potatoes
- a pound of butter
- 1 pound of brown sugar
- 8 eggs

## Directions

1. Boil and mash smooth the potatoes
2. Beat the eggs and stir them into the butter
3. Add the milk, and lastly the meal
4. Beat the whole well together
5. Bake in a pan
