## Ingredients

- 12 Large Peaches
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Take twelve large peaches or one can, two cups of sugar, and one pint of water.
2. Cut the peaches fine, and mix all the ingredients together.
3. Then freeze in the ice cream freezer according to directions already given.
4. Serve with rich cream or milk.
