## Ingredients

- 6 Plantains
- % Pound of Sugar
- The Juice of Half a Lemon
- 1 Cup of Water

## Directions

1. Peel the plantains and cut into halves or quarters
2. Put the sugar and water on to boil, and, after ten minutes, add the lemon juice
3. Put the pieces of plantain, a few at a time, into the hot syrup and let them stew gently
4. After half an hour, or forty-five minutes if not tender, take them out, lay in a dish
5. Pour over the hot syrup, and serve either hot or cold
