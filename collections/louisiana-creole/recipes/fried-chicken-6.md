## Ingredients

- 1 pair young chickens
- water
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- lard
- 1/2 pint cream
- mace
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut up a pair of young chickens
2. Lay them in a pan of cold water to extract the blood
3. Wipe them dry
4. Season with pepper and salt
5. Dredge them with flour
6. Fry them in lard with both sides a rich brown
7. Take them out of the pan and keep them near the fire
8. Skim carefully the gravy in which the chickens have been fried
9. Mix it with 1/2 pint of cream
10. Season with a little mace, pepper and salt, adding some parsley
