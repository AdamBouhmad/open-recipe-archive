## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Pint of Cream
- Yolks of 3 Eggs
- 4 Ounces of Sugar
- 2 Ounces of Grated Chocolate

## Directions

1. Boil one pint of milk and one pint of cream.
2. Add, while boiling, three yolks of eggs, beaten well with four ounces of sugar.
3. Stir constantly, and let it reduce one-half.
4. Add two ounces of grated chocolate.
5. Let it boil three or four minutes.
6. Take off and serve when very cold.
