## Ingredients

- 7 pints of cherry water
- 4 ounces of orange flower water
- 4 pounds of sugar
- 1 quart of water

## Directions

1. Take seven pints of cherry water and distill it to nearly one-half
2. Add four ounces of orange flower water
3. Let it stand for four or five hours
4. Dissolve four pounds of sugar in a quart of distilled and filtered Mississippi water, and set it over the fire till well-blended and dissolved
5. Take it off, and let it cool
6. Add the cherry water, strain and bottle
