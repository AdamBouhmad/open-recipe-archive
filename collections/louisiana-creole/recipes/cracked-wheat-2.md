## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Cracked Wheat
- 1 Quart of Water
- 1 teaspoonful of Salt
- sugar and cream

## Directions

1. Soak the grain over night in a quart of cold water
2. In the morning set the kettle containing the grain in another kettle of cold water
3. Add the salt
4. Let it gradually heat and boil for at least an hour and a half
5. It should be thoroughly cooked
6. Serve with sugar and cream for breakfast
