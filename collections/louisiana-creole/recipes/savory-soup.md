## Ingredients

- 4 pounds lean beef
- 4 quarts water
- 1 onion, chopped fine
- 3 sprigs parsley
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 sprig thyme
- 1/2 cup rice
- 3 tablespoonfuls oatmeal
- 1 tablespoonful salt
- 1/4 teaspoonful pepper

## Directions

1. Put the meat in four quarts of cold water and let it simmer for three hours
2. One hour before serving, add one-half cup of rice which has been soaked in water until soft and three tablespoonfuls of oatmeal
3. Add one tablespoonful of salt and one-fourth teaspoonful of pepper
4. Add parsley, sprig of thyme and one onion chopped fine
5. Boil an hour longer and serve very hot
