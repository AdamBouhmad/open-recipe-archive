## Ingredients

- a pint of rice that has been boiled soft
- a teacup of flour
- two eggs well beaten
- a pinch of salt
- enough milk
- a tablespoonful of melted butter or lard

## Directions

1. Take a pint of rice that has been boiled soft
2. add to it a teacup of flour, two eggs well beaten, a pinch of salt, and enough milk to make a nice thick batter
3. throw into the batter a tablespoonful of melted butter or lard
4. bake on a hot griddle
