## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Butter
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Nut Meats, Cut Fine
- 2 Teaspoonfuls Baking Powder
- 1/8 Teaspoonful of Extract of Almond

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream, and add the sugar, and beat till very light
2. Then add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs, and beat till very light
3. Add the milk, and mix carefully
4. Then sift the baking powder and flour together, and add the flour, beating till very smooth
5. Add half the beaten whites, which must be of a stiff froth, and mix well
6. Then add the nuts (pecans, mashed almonds, peanuts and Brazilian nuts, mixed, and cut or mashed very fine)
7. Add the remainder of the whites of the eggs, the Almond Extract and the baking powder, and mix well
8. Pour into two square, flat pans, lined with buttered paper, and bake in a moderate oven forty-five minutes
9. Use the broom-straw test. If the straw comes out clean and dry, the cake is done; otherwise bake it a little longer
10. Keep the heat of the oven steady and moderate
