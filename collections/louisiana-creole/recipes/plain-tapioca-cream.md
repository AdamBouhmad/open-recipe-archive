## Ingredients

- pearl tapioca
- sugar
- nutmeg
- rich cream

## Directions

1. Boil the pearl tapioca as you do rice
2. When cool sweeten it to the taste
3. Grate nutmeg over it
4. Pour rich cream over it and serve
