## Ingredients

- bread dough
- jam or preserves

## Directions

1. roll out, and spreading it very thickly with any kind of jam or preserves
2. roll it over carefully, and tie up in a nicely floured square stout cloth
3. have ready a pot of boiling water, and drop in the pudding
4. keep it boiling briskly ½ hour
5. keep a kettle of water on the fire, to renew the water as it evaporates
