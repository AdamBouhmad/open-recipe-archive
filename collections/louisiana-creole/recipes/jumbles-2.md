## Ingredients

- 6 eggs, well beaten separately
- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of white sugar for rolling
- mace and orange peel to the taste
- 2 grains of soda

## Directions

1. Mix eggs, flour, sugar, and butter as you would beaten cake, except that the batter must be stiff enough to roll in cakes
2. Roll them in white sugar
3. Add mace and orange peel to taste
