## Ingredients

- Three-fourths lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of sugar
- yolks of 10 eggs and whites of 5
- 2 nutmegs

## Directions

1. Mix three-fourths lb. of butter, 1 lb. of sugar, the yolks of 10 eggs and the whites of 5, and 2 nutmegs
2. Bake either with or without paste
