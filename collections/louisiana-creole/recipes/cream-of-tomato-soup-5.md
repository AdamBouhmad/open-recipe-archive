## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Pure Tomato Juice
- 1 Gill of Rice
- 3 Onions
- 8 Allspice
- 4 Cloves
- A Sprig of Thyme
- A Pinch of Sugar to Taste
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Stew the Tomatoes for about two hours, and then extract the Juice.
2. Add the other ingredients, and boil for about an hour and a half; then strain.
3. The rice, being creamy, should now make the soup as thick as cream.
4. Serve with Croutons or Quenelles.
