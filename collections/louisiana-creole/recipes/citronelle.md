## Ingredients

- The Zest of 30 Lemons
- The Zest of 4 Oranges
- 3½ Quarts of Alcohol
- 2½ Pounds of Sugar
- ⅛ Drachm Each of Cloves and Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Grate the zests of the lemons and oranges, and grate the nutmeg and grind the cloves.
2. Mix well.
3. Pour over the alcohol, and steep for fifteen days.
4. Distill in a "bain-marie," or water bath, and add the syrup made with two and a half pounds of sugar and one quart of water.
5. Bottle and keep in a cool, shady place.
