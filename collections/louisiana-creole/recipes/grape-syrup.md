## Ingredients

- 1 Gallon Plain Syrup
- 2 Pints of Grape Juice
- 3/4 Pint of Grape or Catawba Wine

## Directions

1. Pick, wash, stem and seed the grapes; then pound and mash well, and set aside in a cool place for thirty-six hours.
2. Then strain through a bag, pressing out all the juice; make a Plain Syrup, and proceed in exactly the same manner as for Currant Syrup, and add the wine when you are about to bottle.
