## Ingredients

- 3 cups of sugar
- 1 cup of water
- 1 lb. of chocolate
- 1 cup of milk
- 1 cup of butter

## Directions

1. Dissolve the chocolate in the water
2. Add 1 cup of milk, and 1 cup of butter
3. Let it boil till it will drop from a spoon, generally about 1 hour
4. Pour in a flat dish and cut in squares
