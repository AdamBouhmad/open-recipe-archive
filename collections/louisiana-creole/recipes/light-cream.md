## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Pint of Cream
- 4 or 5 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered White Sugar
- 2 Fresh Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Orange Flower Water

## Directions

1. To one pint of milk add the same quantity of cream, and four or five tablespoonfuls of powdered white sugar.
2. Let it boil till reduced to one-half the quantity.
3. Take off the stove, and whip in the whites of two fresh Creole eggs, and, when it is as white as snow, add a half pint of whipped cream.
4. Place upon the fire, and let it simmer gently, stirring continually.
5. Add a tablespoonful of orange flower water, or other essence.
6. Take off, let it cool, and serve when cold.
