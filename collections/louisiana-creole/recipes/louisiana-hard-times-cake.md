## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- one pound of sifted sugar
- yolks of six eggs
- whites of six eggs
- one pound of flour
- two teaspoonfuls of yeast powder
- one cup of cold water
- two teaspoonfuls of brandy, wine or extract of lemon

## Directions

1. Cream half a pound of butter, with one pound of sifted sugar
2. Add to this the beaten yolks of six eggs
3. Beat this again, and set it by until you beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
4. Sift a pound of flour, and put into it two teaspoonfuls of yeast powder
5. Then pour in alternately a little flour and beaten eggs until all is used
6. Then mix in a cup of cold water and two teaspoonfuls of brandy, wine or extract of lemon
7. Butter a four-quart cake-pan or mould, have the oven ready, and pour in the mixture, and bake immediately
8. Cover the top of the cake while baking
