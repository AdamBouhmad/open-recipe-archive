## Ingredients

- hops
- water
- corn-meal
- bakers' yeast
- flour

## Directions

1. Put a cup of hops into a pint of water
2. When boiling hot, strain it over a pint of corn-meal
3. Add a teacup of bakers' yeast
4. When cool roll the dough in flour and cut it out into cakes
5. Dry them for use
