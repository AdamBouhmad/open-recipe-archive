## Ingredients

- best oranges
- water
- loaf sugar (1 1/2 lbs. per lb. of pulp)

## Directions

1. Take the best oranges and cut them in half
2. Take out the seeds and juice
3. Boil them in 3 or 4 waters (always hot) until they are tender
4. Beat them to pulp in a marble mortar and rub them through a hair sieve
5. To 1 lb. of this pulp allow 1 1/2 lbs. of loaf sugar
6. Take 1/2 of the sugar and boil it with the oranges till it becomes ropy
7. Take it from the fire, and when cold, make it up in paste with the other 1/2 of the sugar
8. Make but a little at a time, for it will dry fast
9. With a rolling pin roll them out as thin as tiffany, upon paper
10. Cut them round with a wineglass
11. Let them dry, and they will look clear
