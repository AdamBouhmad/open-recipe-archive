## Ingredients

- 1 full quart of clams
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 coffee cup of cream
- pepper
- salt
- rich paste

## Directions

1. Open clams and take off the shells
2. Chop them quite fine
3. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of butter and a coffee cup of cream
4. Season with pepper and salt
5. Bake it in a rich paste
