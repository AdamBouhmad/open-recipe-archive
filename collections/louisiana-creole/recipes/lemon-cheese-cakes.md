## Ingredients

- peel of two lemons
- half a pound of sugar
- yolks of six eggs
- one-half pound of butter
- juice of the lemons
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Boil the peel of two lemons until tender, and pound them
2. Take half a pound of sugar, the yolks of six eggs, and one-half pound of butter
3. Stir all well together
4. Add the juice of the lemons last
5. Lay puff paste in your pans
6. Fill them half full of the mixture
7. Bake lightly
