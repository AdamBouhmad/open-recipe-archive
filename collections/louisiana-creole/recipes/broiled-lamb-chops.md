## Ingredients

- 6 Lamb Chops
- Butter, Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Season the chops well with salt and pepper and brush with melted butter and a few broad crumbs.
2. Have the gridiron hot and place the chops upon it.
3. In a few seconds turn the chops and let the other sides cook.
4. Let them remain on the griddle until the chops are firm under pressure of a fork.
5. Place on a platter, butter thickly and sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve very hot.
