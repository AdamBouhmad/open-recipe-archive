## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- flour
- lard
- hot water
- wine
- walnut catsup
- pepper
- salt
- onion
- butter
- mustard

## Directions

1. Slice 8 middle sized cucumbers
2. Flour them slightly and fry a light brown in a little lard
3. Pour off the lard and add to the cucumbers 4 tablespoonfuls of hot water, 2 of wine, 2 of walnut catsup, pepper, salt, and sliced onion
4. Add a lump of butter dipped in flour
5. Stew about 15 minutes
