## Ingredients

- four eggs
- a quart of milk
- sugar
- flavoring
- baker's bread
- hot butter

## Directions

1. Beat four eggs to a quart of milk, sweeten and flavor to taste
2. Cut slices of baker's bread and steep them until thoroughly saturated
3. Fry in hot butter and serve
