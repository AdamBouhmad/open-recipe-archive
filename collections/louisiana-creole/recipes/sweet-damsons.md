## Ingredients

- 5 lbs. of fruit
- 3 lbs. of sugar
- 1 quart of vinegar
- mace
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. To 5 lbs. of fruit add 3 lbs. of sugar, 1 quart of vinegar
2. Boil the sugar and vinegar, with mace, cloves and cinnamon
