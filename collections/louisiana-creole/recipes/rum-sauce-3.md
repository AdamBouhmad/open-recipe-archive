## Ingredients

- 1 Gill of Jamaica Rum
- 6 Ounces of Granulated Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Caramel
- 1 Tablespoonful of Cornstarch
- 1 Pint of Water
- The Zest of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Put the sugar and caramel and lemon zest in a saucepan, and when it begins to boil add a tablespoonful of cornstarch diluted in four tablespoonfuls of cold water
2. Let all cook for three minutes, and then remove from the fire, and add the rum
3. Mix well, then strain through a sieve into a sauce bowl and serve hot with fruit puddings especially.
