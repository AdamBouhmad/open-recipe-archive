## Ingredients

- Cranberries
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the cranberries in cold water and pick well, rejecting all those that float on top or are in any manner over-ripe and spoiled
2. Put them in a porcelain-lined saucepan with one pint of water
3. Let them boil over a moderate fire, stirring occasionally with a wooden spoon and mashing the fruit as much as possible
4. When the berries have cooked about twenty minutes, remove the saucepan from the fire
5. Add sugar stirring in sufficient to sweeten nicely
6. Let them cook at least ten or fifteen minutes longer after adding the sugar
7. Put into an earthenware bowl and let the sauce cool
8. Never strain the sauce
9. Let them stand at least overnight or twenty-four hours in a cool place before serving
10. Serve with Roast Turkey
