## Ingredients

- a quart of milk
- one quarter of a pound of butter
- four eggs
- enough flour
- a cup of yeast

## Directions

1. To a quart of milk, one quarter of a pound of butter, four eggs, and enough flour to form a very stiff batter, add a cup of yeast
2. set it to rise three hours
3. bake in greased muffin rings
4. Split, butter, and serve them hot
