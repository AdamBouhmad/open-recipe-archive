## Ingredients

- 1 Can of Asparagus
- 1/2 Pint of Vinaigrette Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the asparagus as in the Boiled Asparagus recipe.
2. Drain and set to cool.
3. Serve with a half pint of Vinaigrette Sauce.
