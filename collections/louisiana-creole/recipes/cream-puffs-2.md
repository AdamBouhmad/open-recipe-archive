## Ingredients

- 1 cup of water
- 1 cup of best butter
- 1 cup of sifted flour
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups of milk
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of cornstarch
- fine flour
- cold milk
- vanilla

## Directions

1. Put 1 cup of water on the fire
2. When hot melt in 1 cup of best butter
3. When it boils beat in 1 cup of sifted flour
4. If lumpy, stir until perfectly smooth
5. Place it aside to cool
6. When cool, stir in 1 at a time 3 eggs without first beating them
7. Drop the batter on tins in small spoonfuls
8. Bake 1 hour in quick oven
9. For the cream put 1 1/2 cups of milk to boil
10. Beat yolks of 2 eggs very light with 4 tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
11. When the milk boils, pour it on the eggs, stirring all the while
12. Wet 2 tablespoonfuls of cornstarch in fine flour with a little cold milk
13. Add it to the custard and replace on the fire
14. Stir until quite thick
15. Flavor when cold with vanilla
16. Place the cream in the puff by lifting up the top with a knife and dropping in the cream with a small spoon
