## Ingredients

- ½ Pound of Butter
- 2 Cups Grated Cocoanut
- 5 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Milk
- ¼ Cup of Butter
- ¾ Pound of Flour
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- The Juice and Grated Zest of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a rich cream
2. Add the yolks of the eggs, and beat till very light
3. Add the milk, and the flour by degrees, beating all the while, and then add the lemon
4. Beat well
5. Add the cocoanut, and stir in carefully and well
6. Sift the baking powder over the whites of the eggs, which you will have beaten to a stiff froth, and add these, beating lightly and thoroughly
7. Have ready two loaf pans, and line them with very thin buttered paper
8. Divide the batter into two even parts, filling each pan about three-quarters full
9. Set in a moderate oven, and bake for forty-five minutes
10. When done, cover the top with finely-grated cocoanut, and sift powdered sugar over it
11. Set it to cool
