## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sifted Sugar (White Pulverized)
- The Grated Peel of 1 Lemon
- 1 Tablespoonful of Orange Flower Water
- 12 Eggs
- 3/4 Pound of Sifted Flour
- 1/4 Pound of Mashed Rice (Dried)
- 1 Tablespoonful of Baking Powder
- 4 Ounces Sweet Almonds
- 1 Ounce of Bitter Almonds

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth, after having blanched and peeled the almonds and pounded them to a fine powder in a mortar
2. Beat the yolks of the eggs very light, and gradually add the rice, which has been well-mashed
3. Mix the almonds thoroughly with the flour; then add these gradually to the sugar, beating vigorously
4. Add the grated zest of the lemon and the essence, and finally add the whites of the eggs and mix lightly and quickly
5. Butter a paper and line the bottom and sides of the pan, and bake the cake in a moderate oven for one hour
