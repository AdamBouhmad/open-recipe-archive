## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- 2 ounces cloves to every gallon of liquor
- 1 ounce of allspice to every gallon of liquor
- 1 ounce of mace to every gallon of liquor
- 1 bottle of port wine to every 3 quarts
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take a quantity of tomatoes, put them into a tub in layers with salt till all are in
2. Let them stand 24 hours
3. Strain off all the juice, squeezing the pulp well
4. Put the juice on to boil
5. Reduce it to 1/3, and to every gallon of liquor put 2 ounces cloves, 1 ounce of allspice, 1 ounce of mace, and boil it down well
6. To every 3 quarts add 1 bottle of port wine, and boil it a little longer
7. Put it away to cool and settle, then bottle it
8. If there is not enough spice, pound a fresh nutmeg and put it in
