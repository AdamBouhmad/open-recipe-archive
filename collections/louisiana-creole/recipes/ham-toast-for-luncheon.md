## Ingredients

- yolk of an egg
- a tablespoonful of sweet milk
- grated or finely chopped ham
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Beat the yolk of an egg with a tablespoonful of sweet milk.
2. Set it on the fire to warm, and thicken it with grated or finely chopped ham.
3. Let it simmer a few moments and pour it on buttered toast.
