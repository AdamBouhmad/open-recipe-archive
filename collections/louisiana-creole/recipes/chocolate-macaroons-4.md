## Ingredients

- 12 Ounces of Ground Almonds
- 1.5 Pounds of Sifted White Sugar
- 4 Ounces of Grated French Chocolate
- The Whites of 3 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Sugar

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl, and let it form a stiff paste
2. Lay the macaroons out upon the wafer sheet in the form of lady fingers
3. Bake at a very moderate heat till a light brown
