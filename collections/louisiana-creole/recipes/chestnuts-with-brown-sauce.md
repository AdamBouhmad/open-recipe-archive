## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Chestnuts
- 1 Pint of Brown Sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the chestnuts in the same manner as Stewed Chestnuts, as far as boiling
2. Make a Brown Sauce (see Sauce Espagnole)
3. Add the chestnuts to it and sauté for fifteen minutes longer
4. Let all simmer gently and serve hot
