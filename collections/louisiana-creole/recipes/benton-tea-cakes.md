## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 6 ounces of butter
- 3 spoonfuls of yeast
- new milk

## Directions

1. Rub the butter and yeast into the flour
2. Make into a paste with new milk
3. Make into biscuits
4. Prick them with a clean fork
5. Bake
