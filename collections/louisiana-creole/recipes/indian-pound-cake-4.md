## Ingredients

- 3/4 Cup of Flour
- 1 Cup of Indian Meal
- 3/4 Pound of Butter
- 3/4 Pound Sugar
- 8 Eggs
- 1 Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Teaspoonful of Cinnamon
- 3/4 Glass of Sherry Wine and Brandy, Mixed

## Directions

1. Stir the butter and sugar to a cream, and add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs
2. Beat till very light, and then add the meal and flour, well blended
3. Beat light
4. Add the spices and liquor, and finally the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
5. Beat thoroughly, but lightly, and turn into a buttered tin
6. Bake in a moderate oven for an hour and a half
