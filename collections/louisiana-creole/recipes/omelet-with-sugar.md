## Ingredients

- six eggs
- seven spoonfuls of powdered sugar
- lemon

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs, whites and yolks separately, with seven spoonfuls of powdered sugar
2. Flavor with lemon
3. bake like a pudding for ten or fifteen minutes, or just long enough to set the eggs
4. Do not bake longer as it will spoil the jelly-like consistency of the omelet
