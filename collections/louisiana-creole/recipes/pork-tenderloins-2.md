## Ingredients

- 4 Pork Tenderloins
- A tablespoonful of lard
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Have the tenderloins cut thin and split lengthwise without separating.
2. Season well with salt and pepper.
3. Have ready a very hot frying pan, place a tablespoonful of butter or lard within and add the tenderloin.
4. Turn every two minutes, not leaving them very long on either side at a time.
5. Be careful to cook through and through, smothering over a low fire.
6. Serve with Apple Sauce or Currant Jelly.
