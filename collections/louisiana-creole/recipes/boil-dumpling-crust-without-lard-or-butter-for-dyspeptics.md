## Ingredients

- a pint of flour
- salt
- boiling water

## Directions

1. Sift a pint of flour in a basin, salt it as usual
2. pour on it a fine stream of boiling water from the spout of a kettle, pour it slowly
3. mix the flour and hot water with a spoon until it is a nice soft dough that you can handle
4. pour it on the biscuit board, which should be well floured
5. give it two or three turns, and it is ready for the fruit
