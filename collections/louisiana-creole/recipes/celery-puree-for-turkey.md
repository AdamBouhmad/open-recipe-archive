## Ingredients

- six or eight heads of celery
- half a pound of butter
- white soup stock
- sugar
- pepper
- salt
- a cup of milk or cream

## Directions

1. Chop up six or eight heads of celery, boil them a few minutes
2. Drain and put them in a saucepan, with half a pound of butter, some white soup stock, a little sugar, pepper and salt
3. Cook till soft, then strain it through a sieve
4. Heat it again and add a cup of milk or cream
