## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Ears Young, Tender Green Corn
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1 Minced Onion

## Directions

1. Score the corn along each row, and then cut from the cob with a knife.
2. Press out all the pulp and corn juice from the cob.
3. Mix all and season well with salt and pepper.
4. Mince the onion fine, and blend with the lard, which you will have put into the frying pan.
5. Add the corn when the onions begin to brown slightly, and keep stirring and stirring till the grain is cooked, which will be in about fifteen or twenty minutes.
6. Serve hot.
