## Ingredients

- 8 Ripe Pomegranates
- 1 Quart of Water
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Essence of Vanilla
- 3 Drops of Cochineal

## Directions

1. Press the pips of six or eight ripe pomegranates with a wooden spoon through a strainer to produce a pint of juice.
2. Make a syrup by boiling one quart of water and one pint of sugar.
3. Add, when cooling, the pomegranate juice, a half teaspoonful of Essence of Vanilla, the juice of a lemon, and a few drops of Cochineal.
4. Proceed to freeze as directed.
