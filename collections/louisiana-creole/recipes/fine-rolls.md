## Ingredients

- half a cup of butter
- a half pint of milk
- two spoonfuls of small beer yeast or a cup of home-made yeast
- a little salt
- two pounds of flour

## Directions

1. Warm half a cup of butter in a half pint of milk
2. add two spoonfuls of small beer yeast, or a cup of home-made yeast, and a little salt
3. pour this on to two pounds of flour
4. Let it rise an hour
5. knead it
6. make into loaves or rolls
