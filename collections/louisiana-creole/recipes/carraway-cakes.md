## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of sifted loaf sugar
- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of butter
- some carraway seeds
- 3 well beaten eggs
- a little rose water

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar with the flour and butter
2. Add some carraway seeds
3. Make it into a stiff paste with the eggs and rose water
4. Roll it out thin
5. Cut it into round cakes
6. Prick them with a fork
7. Bake them upon floured tins in a quick oven
