## Ingredients

- three quarters of a pound of loaf sugar
- nearly half a pint of water
- the peel of a lemon
- eight eggs, yolks only
- four eggs, whites only
- ten ounces of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Put loaf sugar in a stew pan with nearly half a pint of water and thin-cut lemon peel
2. Let it simmer twenty minutes
3. Beat the yolks of eight eggs and the whites of four for ten minutes
4. Pour in the boiling syrup and beat it well for half an hour
5. Grease your cake pan well and put a paper in the bottom
6. Stir ten ounces of sifted flour gently into the mixture
7. Pour the batter immediately into the pan
8. Bake in a rather quick oven about half an hour
9. Have the oven in baking order before you put the flour in
