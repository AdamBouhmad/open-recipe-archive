## Ingredients

- 4 pounds of watermelon rind
- 1/2 pint of best French vinegar
- 1 pound of brown sugar
- 1 tablespoonful of mixed ground cloves, mace, cinnamon and allspice

## Directions

1. Cut the watermelon rind into strips, cook in clear water and drain.
2. To four pounds of rind take one pound of brown sugar and half a pint of vinegar, and make a syrup, to which add the ground spices.
3. Boil the watermelon rind in this syrup till quite tender.
4. Put in jars while hot and seal.
