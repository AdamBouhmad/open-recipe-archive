## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls butter
- one tablespoonful strong dry mustard
- pepper
- salt
- one-half pint oysters

## Directions

1. Take 3 tablespoonfuls of butter and one of strong, dry mustard and put them in a chopping dish
2. Add pepper and salt
3. When they commence boiling put in one-half pint of oysters without the liquor
4. Have a hot dish in readiness
5. Take out the oysters with a fork when cooked, and continue to replace them until all are done
