## Ingredients

- the juice and grated rind of six lemons
- one pint of milk or cream
- six ounces of sponge cake or macaroons
- eight yolks
- whites of four eggs
- one pound of sugar
- a little salt
- puff paste
- cut-up almonds
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix in a basin the juice and grated rind of six lemons, one pint of milk or cream, six ounces of sponge cake or macaroons, eight yolks, and the whites of four eggs (whipped to a froth), one pound of sugar, and a little salt
2. Work all these materials together for at least ten minutes
3. Put a border of puff paste around a pie-dish, then pour in the batter
4. Strew cut-up almonds over it
5. Bake
6. Sift powdered sugar over it, and serve
