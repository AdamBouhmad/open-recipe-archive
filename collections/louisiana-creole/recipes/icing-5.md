## Ingredients

- whites of 2 eggs
- 2 teacups of granulated sugar
- water
- 1 teaspoonful of citric acid
- lemon extract

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar until clear with just enough water to moisten it
2. Beat the eggs to a stiff froth
3. Pour the boiling sugar very slowly into the eggs
4. Dissolve 1 teaspoonful of citric acid in a small tablespoonful of water, putting enough in to flavor delicately
5. Add a little extract of lemon
