## Ingredients

- the whites of 6 eggs
- the yolks of 4 eggs
- the juice of half a lemon, or a spoonful of orange flower water or Kirsch
- 3 tablespoonfuls of powdered white sugar
- butter

## Directions

1. Have a baking dish ready, greased with butter
2. Be sure that the oven is very hot
3. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth
4. Beat the yolks and the sugar to a cream
5. Add the juice of half a lemon or a tablespoonful of orange flower water or Kirsch
6. Add the whites of the eggs
7. Stir carefully and heap all quickly into the baking dish
8. Bake about fifteen minutes, till the top is a delicate brown
9. Serve immediately, as it will fall if allowed to stand
