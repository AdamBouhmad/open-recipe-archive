## Ingredients

- 1 Filet of Beef
- ½ Can of Truffles
- ½ Glass of Sherry
- 1 Pint of Broth or Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Glace
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Proceed in the same manner as in the preparation of Filet of Beef Larded.
2. When it has baked for a half hour, make a sauce as follows: Put one tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan; add two tablespoonfuls of Glace and add a half wineglass of Sherry and one pint of broth or water.
3. Let it boil slowly for ten minutes, and add one-half can of truffles, chopped very fine, if a la Périgeux; if aux truffes, cut in dice.
4. Let the sauce boil slowly twenty minutes longer, and then pour over the filet, serving hot.
