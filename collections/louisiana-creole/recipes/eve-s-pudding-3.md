## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 6 apples
- 6 ounces of suet
- 6 ounces of pounded cracker
- 6 ounces of currants
- 6 ounces of white sugar
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix together 6 eggs, 6 apples chopped fine, 6 ounces of suet, 6 ounces of pounded cracker, 6 ounces of currants, 6 ounces of white sugar, a little salt and nutmeg
2. boil it 3 hours
3. serve it with wine, sugar and butter sauce
