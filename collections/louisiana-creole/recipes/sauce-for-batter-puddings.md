## Ingredients

- yolks of 6 eggs
- 4 spoonfuls of sifted sugar
- butter
- 1 pint of boiling cream
- 1 wineglass of brandy

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of 6 eggs with sugar and butter mixed together
2. Have ready a pint of boiling cream to mix with the yolks
3. Put it on the fire and stir it until it is of the consistency of sauce
4. Add to it a wineglass of brandy
