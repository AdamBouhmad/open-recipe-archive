## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Flour
- 1 Pound of Butter, or Half Butter and Half Lard
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Cup of Ice Water
- 1 Egg
- Chicken, pigeon or other meat, already fricasseed

## Directions

1. Make the Puff Paste as directed and let it stand in a cool place at least six hours.
2. Roll the paste out very thin, and then line the deep pan in which you intend to cook the chicken or meat.
3. Cut around the edge nicely with a knife.
4. Then take another small pan and cover it only half way with the paste.
5. Cut around the edges, trimming off all strings of dough.
6. Then brush the paste all around the top with a beaten egg.
7. Put it on a sheet iron or square tin, and set it on the ice till very cold.
8. Then set it in a very hot oven to bake for about half an hour.
9. Do not let it burn or scorch.
10. When done, fill the inside with chicken, pigeon or other meat, already fricasseed, with which you intend to make the Vol-au-Vent and then cover with the top crust.
