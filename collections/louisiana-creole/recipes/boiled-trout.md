## Ingredients

- large fresh trout
- salt
- boiled parsley
- melted butter
- catsup

## Directions

1. Buy large fresh trout, clean very nicely, wash them well
2. sprinkle well with salt
3. boil them for 15 minutes
4. when done, send in on a drainer, with boiled parsley over them
5. Use melted butter and catsup as a dressing
