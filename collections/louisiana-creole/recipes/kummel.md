## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of the Best Rectified Alcohol
- 30 Drops of Kummel Oil
- 1 Quart of Boiling Water
- 2 Pounds of the Finest White Loaf Sugar

## Directions

1. Pour the boiling water over the sugar and stir till dissolved.
2. Bring to a boil, and let it continue boiling from fifteen to twenty minutes.
3. Strain, and when luke warm, add the oil and the alcohol.
4. Bottle and keep for at least two months before using.
