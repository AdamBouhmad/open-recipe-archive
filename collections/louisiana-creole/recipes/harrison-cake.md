## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- 6 cups of flour
- 4 cups of sugar
- 2 cups of butter
- 1 cup of cream
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- 1 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake
3. Eat with fresh butter
