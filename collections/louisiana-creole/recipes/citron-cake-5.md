## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Flour
- 1 Cup of Butter
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 12 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Cream
- 1 Glass of Wine
- 1 Cup of Chopped Citron
- 1 Teaspoonful of Almond Extract

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Add the sugar, and beat till very light
3. Add the yolks of the eggs, and beat till very light
4. Add the cream, and mix thoroughly, and then add by degrees the citron, mixing thoroughly
5. By degrees, add the flour, well-sifted
6. Beat well
7. Add the wine, and lastly the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
8. Mix all quickly and lightly; add the almond extract, mix lightly, and then set to bake in a buttered pan in a moderate oven for one hour
