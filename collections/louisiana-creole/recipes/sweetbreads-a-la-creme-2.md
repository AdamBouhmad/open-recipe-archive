## Ingredients

- 3 pairs of sweetbreads
- 10 mushrooms
- 1.5 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1.5 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 pint of cream
- salt and white pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and parboil the sweetbreads for twenty minutes.
2. Then remove all veins and nerves, and chop the meat into pieces of about an inch and a half.
3. Chop the mushrooms very fine indeed.
4. Put the butter in a saucepan, and, when it melts, add the flour, being careful not to let it brown.
5. When perfectly smooth, add the cream and stir constantly until it boils.
6. Then add the chopped mushrooms and let them simmer for five minutes.
7. Season well to taste with salt and white pepper.
8. Then add the sweetbreads and cook for five minutes longer and serve hot.
9. At luncheons and dinners the sweetbreads a la Creme are served in small silver shells or fancy paper cases.
