## Ingredients

- one pound of good flour
- one-quarter of a pound of good sugar
- a tablespoonful of salt
- two gallons of water

## Directions

1. Boil one pound of good flour, one-quarter of a pound of good sugar, and a tablespoonful of salt in two gallons of water
2. Boil for two hours
3. bottle it for use
