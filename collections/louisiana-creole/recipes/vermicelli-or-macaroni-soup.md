## Ingredients

- a quarter of a pound of vermicelli or macaroni
- a quart of warm water
- good stock or plain veal, chicken or beef soup
- a spoonful of butter
- half a pint of stewed tomatoes

## Directions

1. Swell a quarter of a pound of vermicelli or macaroni (whichever is preferred) in a quart of warm water for one hour; then add it to some good stock or plain veal, chicken or beef soup; add a spoonful of butter and half a pint of stewed tomatoes just before the soup is served. This is a very fine soup, and is especially nourishing for delicate stomachs.
