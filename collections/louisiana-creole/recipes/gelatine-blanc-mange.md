## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- one ounce of Nelson's or Coxe's gelatine
- one cup of cold water
- half a pound of fine white sugar
- cream for serving

## Directions

1. Soak an ounce of Nelson's or Coxe's gelatine in a cup of cold water for one hour
2. Add the soaked gelatine to one quart of milk
3. Add to this half a pound of fine white sugar
4. Let it simmer very gently on the fire in a stewpan until all the gelatine is dissolved
5. Strain it and pour it in a mould
6. When it begins to thicken, put it on ice and serve it with cream
