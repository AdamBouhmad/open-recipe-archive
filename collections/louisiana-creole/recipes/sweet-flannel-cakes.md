## Ingredients

- ½ pint soft-boiled rice
- 1 teacup cream
- 1 teacup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 1 tablespoonful yeast or 1 teaspoonful pearl ash

## Directions

1. Let the rice cool
2. Add the cream, sugar, eggs, and yeast or pearl ash
3. Rub them well together
4. Bake on a griddle
