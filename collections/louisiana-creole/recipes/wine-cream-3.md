## Ingredients

- The Yolks of 8 Eggs
- 1 Cup Sugar
- 1 Quart of Madeira or Sherry Wine

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and eggs well together, and add gradually one quart of Madeira or Sherry Wine, flavored with lemon or vanilla.
2. Set the vessel in which they were mixed in another containing boiling water.
3. Let the mixture simmer until the cream is perfectly formed, and then set to cool.
4. Serve with whipped cream.
