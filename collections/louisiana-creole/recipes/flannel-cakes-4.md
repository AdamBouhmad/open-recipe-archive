## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. flour
- 4 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls yeast
- 1 pint milk
- 1 teaspoonful salt

## Directions

1. Sift 2 lbs. of flour and mix a teaspoonful of salt with the flour
2. Set the pan before the fire to rise
3. Warm the milk and stir it into the flour so as to make a stiff batter
4. Beat the eggs very light and stir them into the yeast
5. Add the eggs and yeast to the batter
6. Beat all well together
7. If it is too stiff, add a little more warm milk
8. Cover the pan closely and set it to rise near the fire
9. Bake it when quite ready
