## Ingredients

- 1 pound of cacao beans
- 2 quarts of brandy
- 1 pound of sugar
- 1 quart of water
- 1 tablespoonful of vanilla

## Directions

1. Roast the beans to a light brown and grind as you would grind coffee
2. Infuse in two quarts of brandy for six days
3. Make a syrup with the sugar and water; reduce to one-half and let it cool
4. Add the cacao and brandy
5. Flavor with vanilla
6. Strain and bottle for use
