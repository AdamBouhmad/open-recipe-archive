## Ingredients

- some tart juicy apples
- one-third as much of the prepared meat
- one pound of raisins
- a quarter of a citron
- a gill of brandy
- enough sweet cider to make the whole quite wet

## Directions

1. Pare, core, and chop, not very fine, some tart juicy apples
2. put to them one-third as much of the prepared meat
3. stone one pound of raisins, and cut a quarter of a citron in small bits
4. add a gill of brandy, and enough sweet cider to make the whole quite wet
