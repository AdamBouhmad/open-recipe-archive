## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean it as usual and split it down the back
2. Break the breast-bone with a stroke of the potato beetle
3. Spread it out flat and lay it on the gridiron over clear coals
4. Put the inside of the chicken to the fire first
5. Put a tin cover over it
6. Let it broil quickly until nearly done
7. Turn it and finish without the cover
8. When nicely browned take it on a dish
9. Season it with salt and pepper
10. Butter it freely
11. Turn it once or twice in the butter and serve it hot
