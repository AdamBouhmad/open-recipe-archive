## Ingredients

- 1½ Cups of Granulated Sugar, Sifted
- 1 Cup Sifted Flour
- The Whites of 11 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Cream of Tartar
- 1 Teaspoonful Vanilla
- ½ Teaspoonful Salt

## Directions

1. Sift the flour four times, and then put in the cream of tartar, mix well, and sift a fifth time.
2. Sift the sugar four times.
3. Have the whites of the eggs beaten to a very stiff froth, so stiff that they may be cut with a knife clearly.
4. Then add the flour by degrees, beating all the time.
5. Lastly, add the flavoring extract.
6. Do not butter the pan for this cake, but turn the mixture quickly into an ungreased Turk's head pan (a pan with a tube in the middle).
7. Bake for three-quarters of an hour in a moderate oven.
8. Test with a broom wisp.
9. When done, turn the cake upside down, resting on the tube of the pan, and let it cool.
10. The cake will fall out of the pan.
