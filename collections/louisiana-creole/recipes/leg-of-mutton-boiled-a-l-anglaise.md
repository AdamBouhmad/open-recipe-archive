## Ingredients

- a fat, fine leg of mutton
- warm water
- salt
- turnips
- cream
- butter
- pepper
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Select a fat, fine leg of mutton, put it on the fire in warm water
2. When it boils skim it, and let it simmer gently for two hours and a half
3. Throw in a tablespoonful of salt
4. When the mutton is done garnish with turnips mashed in cream, butter, pepper and salt, and send it to table with a sauce boat of caper sauce No. 11
