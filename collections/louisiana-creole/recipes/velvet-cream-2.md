## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Cream
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 6 Ounces of Sugar
- Juice of 1 Lemon
- 3 Tablespoonfuls Milk
- 1/2 Pint of Gelatine

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine in the water, and let it dissolve well.
2. Set the milk to boil, and add the powdered sugar.
3. Then dissolve the gelatine in it.
4. Take off the fire, and add, as it cools, essence of vanilla or orange, using about two tablespoonfuls.
5. Mix well.
6. Then add the whipped cream, set over the fire one minute, pour into a mold to harden, and serve cold.
