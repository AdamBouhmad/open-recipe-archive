## Ingredients

- large piece of fine fresh salmon
- flour
- fresh butter

## Directions

1. Take a large piece of fine fresh salmon, cut from the middle of the fish
2. well cleaned and carefully scaled
3. wipe it dry in a clean coarse cloth
4. dredge it with flour
5. put it on the spit, and place it before a clear bright fire
6. Baste it with fresh butter
7. roast it well — seeing that it is thoroughly done to the bone
