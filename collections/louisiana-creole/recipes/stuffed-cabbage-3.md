## Ingredients

- 1 head of fine white cabbage
- 1 dozen fine chaurice
- 2 onions
- 1 tomato
- 1 clove of garlic
- 1 square inch of ham
- 1 spoonful of butter
- Thyme, parsley and bay leaf
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Select a fine head of cabbage
2. Take away the big green leaves and select about twelve of the finest and nicest of the large white leaves
3. Put them in cold water for about an hour
4. Parboil for about twenty minutes in boiling water
5. Prepare a stuffing with a dozen fine chaurice, one onion chopped fine, a tablespoonful of butter and a tomato
6. Mince the sausage meat very fine after taking out of the cases
7. Mince fine the onion and tomato
8. Mince fine one sprig each of thyme, parsley and bay leaf
9. Add one square inch of finely-minced ham
10. Put the butter in the frying pan and as it browns add the onions
11. Let these brown and add the sausage meat and the ham
12. Mince four or five of the tender white leaves of the cabbage very fine and add
13. Add the minced clove of garlic and onion
14. Let these brown for five minutes and then let all simmer for about ten minutes
15. Take out and take each leaf of the cabbage and drain dry
16. Lay open on the table and put in each leaf equal quantities of the stuffing
17. Fold over and close nicely
18. Take slender strips of bacon and lay at the bottom of a wide and deep frying pan
19. Place the stuffed leaves on top of these
20. Place other strips of bacon on top
