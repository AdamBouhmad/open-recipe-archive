## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Blackberries
- A Plain Paste, Not Too Flaky

## Directions

1. Make a Plain Paste, which should be rather firm, to prevent the berries from escaping.
2. Roll this out to about one and a half feet in length, or one foot, according to the size of the family, and the number of guests.
3. Spread the blackberries, which you will have carefully picked, over this paste.
4. Carefully roll over and over until the entire paste is taken up.
5. Sew in a clean piece of white muslin and put into a kettle of boiling water.
6. Let it boil continuously for at least two hours and a half.
7. Take out, let it cool slightly, rip the cloth which binds it, and serve immediately with a Hard Sauce or Cream or Brandy Sauce.
