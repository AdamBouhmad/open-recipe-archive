## Ingredients

- 1 Pineapple
- The Whites of Four Eggs
- 1 Pint of Whipped Cream
- 1/4 Cup of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sherry

## Directions

1. Pare the pineapple, and grate it.
2. Beat the whites of the eggs until foamy, and then add by degrees the sifted sugar.
3. Add one tablespoonful of Sherry.
4. Stir the whipped cream, which must be very cold from standing on ice.
5. When all is very stiff beat in the pineapple, pulp and juice, adding as much as the cream and the meringue will hold without becoming too soft.
6. Place in glasses, and serve very cold.
