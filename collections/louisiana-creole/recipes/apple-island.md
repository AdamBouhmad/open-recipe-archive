## Ingredients

- whites of 6 eggs
- 4 tablespoonfuls of nicely stewed, seasoned apples
- sugar
- rose water or lemon or nutmeg
- milk

## Directions

1. To the whites of 6 eggs put 4 tablespoonfuls of nicely stewed, seasoned apples, rich with sugar, seasoned with rose water, lemon, or nutmeg
2. Beat till quite light
3. Season with milk, which is prepared like boiled custard, with the apple
4. Pour it in a dish, and put the island on it
