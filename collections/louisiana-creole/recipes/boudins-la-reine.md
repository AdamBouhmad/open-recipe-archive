## Ingredients

- 1 Young Chicken
- 2 Small Onions
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 2 Eggs
- 1/2 Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Place the mixture in a frying pan and fry in butter, using about a tablespoonful
2. Add about one pint of milk
3. Beat the chicken thoroughly in this
4. Add a grated nutmeg
5. Take off the fire and add two eggs, well beaten
6. Fill custard cups with the mixture
7. Place in the oven setting in a pan of boiling water and covering with paper
8. Bake for twenty minutes
9. Take off the paper and let them brown
10. Serve hot
