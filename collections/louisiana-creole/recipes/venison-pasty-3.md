## Ingredients

- bones, meat, etc., of venison
- pepper
- salt
- port wine
- butter
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut up and stew, or braise the parts of meat intended for the pie
2. Season with pepper, salt, port wine, butter, and if liked, mushrooms
3. Stew all until tender
4. Make a paste and finish like chicken pie
5. This is better to eat cold than hot and should be rich enough to be a solid jelly when cold
