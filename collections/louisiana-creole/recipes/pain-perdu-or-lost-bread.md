## Ingredients

- a pint of fresh milk
- a cup of sugar
- two beaten eggs
- any flavoring
- six slices from a loaf of bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Take a pint of fresh milk, and sweeten it with a cup of sugar
2. stir two beaten eggs in it
3. season with any flavoring you like
4. Cut six slices from a loaf of bread
5. soak each piece of bread a few minutes in the custard of milk and sugar
6. take the pieces out one by one, and fry them in butter made hot in a frying pan
7. pile them up and serve hot
