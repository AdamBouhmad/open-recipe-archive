## Ingredients

- 6 Stuffed Olives
- 6 Slices of Buttered Toast
- 6 Anchovies
- Capers and Minced Olive to Garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare the toast and cut into delicate circles.
2. Place on each circle a coiled Anchovy, and set a Stuffed Olive in the center of the coil.
3. Decorate lightly with Olives and Capers minced very fine, and serve.
4. Alternatively, this Canapé may be prepared by frying the circles of toast in butter, and spreading them with Anchovies pounded to a paste, and decorating on top with minced Capers and Olives.
