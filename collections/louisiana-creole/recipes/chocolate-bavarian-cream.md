## Ingredients

- 2 Large Tablespoonfuls of Chocolate
- 1 Pint Milk
- 1 Pint Cream
- 1/4 Cup Sugar
- 3/4 Pint of Gelatine
- Yolks of 4 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla or Rum
- 2 Ounces of Best French Chocolate

## Directions

1. Prepare the gelatine and cream according to general directions
2. Grate the chocolate and set the milk to boil
3. Add the grated chocolate, stirring slowly until all is dissolved
4. Take the saucepan from the fire, and add the sugar, mixing well, and the vanilla and rum
5. Turn it into a tin vessel to cool; set the vessel over cracked ice, and stir constantly till it begins to thicken
6. Stir the whipped cream in carefully, and pour the mixture into a mold, and set in a cool place to harden
7. Serve with whipped cream
