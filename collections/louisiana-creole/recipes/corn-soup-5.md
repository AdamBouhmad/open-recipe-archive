## Ingredients

- young corn
- water
- pork
- seasoning
- vegetables

## Directions

1. Take young corn and cut the ears across
2. Grate them in water, 2 ears to a pint
3. About 6 quarts will make a good tureen of soup
4. To this quantity put a piece of pork and seasoning to your taste
5. Add vegetables if you like
6. It must boil 3 hours
