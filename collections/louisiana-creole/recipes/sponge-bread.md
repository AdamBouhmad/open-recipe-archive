## Ingredients

- three quarts of wheat flour
- three quarts of boiling water
- twelve spoonfuls of family yeast, or six of brewers'
- flour to mould it
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Mix three quarts of wheat flour and three quarts of boiling water thoroughly
2. Let them remain until lukewarm
3. Add twelve spoonfuls of family yeast, or six of brewers'
4. Place it where it will be warm and keep the air from it
5. Leave it to rise
6. When it is light, work in flour to mould it, and a little salt
7. Let it stand for a second rising
8. Shape into loaves and bake
