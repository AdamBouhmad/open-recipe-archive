## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Molasses
- 1.5 Pound of Brown Sugar
- 0.75 Pound of Butter
- A Pinch of Salt
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Ginger
- 0.5 Teaspoonful of Soda
- 0.75 Pint of Milk or Water

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Work in the flour and mix well
3. Add the molasses and mix well
4. Then add the milk, into which you will have dissolved the soda thoroughly, and beat well
5. Add the ginger and continue working lightly till thoroughly mixed
6. Then roll out and cut in various fanciful shapes and bake to a light brown
