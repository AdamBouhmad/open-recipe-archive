## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- half a pound of butter
- half a pound of sugar
- half a nutmeg
- delicate paste

## Directions

1. Beat eight eggs very light
2. Add them to half a pound of butter, and the same of sugar, which have been beaten to a cream together
3. Grate in half a nutmeg
4. Set it on the fire in a stew-pan, and stir it constantly until it is hot
5. Do not leave it more than five minutes on the fire, as you only wish to slightly cook the whites of the eggs to prevent their running when put on the paste
6. Line two pie pans with delicate paste, and pour in the mixture
7. Bake in a moderate oven
8. Do not allow the top to burn, as it will, if not covered when first put in the oven
9. Cover with a pan until the bottom is cooked, and then a few moments colors the top
10. Serve it with a tart pie
