## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Indian Meal
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Make the dough as for Corn Dodgers.
2. Shape the pone of bread like the Corn Dodgers.
3. Sweep a clean place on the hearth and draw out a pile of hot ashes.
4. Place the pone of bread on top of the ashes.
5. Cover with hot ashes and let bake to a nice brown.
6. Draw the cake out of the ashes and wipe clean with a cloth till every particle of ashes that adhered in the crevices disappeared.
7. Serve hot with butter or molasses.
8. Alternatively, wrap the cake in a layer of fig leaves before baking.
