## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Fat Snipe
- 6 Slices of Buttered French Toast
- 6 Strips of Bacon
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Parsley Sprigs and Sliced Lemon to Garnish
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the bird by hand picking, singing and trussing neatly, following the Invariable Rule for Broiling Birds
2. Rub the bird well with salt and pepper, and then with melted butter
3. Tie a strip of very finely-sliced bacon around the body of the bird joining with a skewer
4. Place on a broiler over a slow fire, and let it cook for ten, fifteen or twenty, or even thirty minutes, according to the size of the bird
5. Turn frequently, so that it may cook well without burning
6. When done take off the broiler
7. Have ready always buttered French toasts, and place the birds upon them allowing a slice of toast for each bird
8. Trim away the rough edges of the toast
9. Pour over the bird a little of the juice that has run from it in broiling, and let it soak down into the toast
10. Pour over a little melted butter and chopped parsley, and lemon juice if you like
11. Garnish with sliced lemon and parsley sprigs, and bring to the table hot
