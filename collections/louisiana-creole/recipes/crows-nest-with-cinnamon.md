## Ingredients

- sour cooking apples
- sugar
- cinnamon
- lemon
- water
- light pie crust
- cream
- butter for hard sauce

## Directions

1. Cut nice sour cooking apples into a baking dish, small or large as you need.
2. Put sugar, cinnamon, and lemon over them.
3. Throw in a cup of water, and cover the dish with a crust of light pie crust.
4. Be sure to cut air-holes in the crust before putting in to bake.
5. Put it in the oven, and bake until the apples are tender.
6. Hat it with cream and sugar, or hard sauce of butter and sugar; beat together until firm enough to slice like butter.
7. Grate a little nutmeg over the sauce, if cinnamon is not liked.
