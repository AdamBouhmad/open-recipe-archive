## Ingredients

- Indian meal
- cold water
- boiling water
- a little salt
- yeast

## Directions

1. Mix Indian meal and cold water as for a thick gruel
2. Stir the mixture into boiling water
3. Let it boil 1/2 hour
4. Stir in a little salt
5. Take it from the fire and let it remain until lukewarm
6. Stir in yeast and Indian meal, making it the consistency of common dough
7. When light, take it out and put into buttered pans
8. Let it remain a few minutes
9. Bake it about 2 1/2 hours
