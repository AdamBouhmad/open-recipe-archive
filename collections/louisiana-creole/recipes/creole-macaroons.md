## Ingredients

- 12 Ounces of Shredded Almonds
- 4 Ounces of Ground Almonds
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 2 Eggs
- 4 Ounces of Wheat Flour
- The Zest of 2 Oranges

## Directions

1. Blanch and wash and dry the almonds
2. Grind four ounces
3. Shred twelve ounces by cutting the almonds lengthwise into thin shreds
4. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the whites separately, and then beat them together with the sugar, rubbing till very light and smooth
5. Add the grated zest of two Louisiana oranges, that has been rubbed on sugar
6. Incorporate all the other ingredients
7. Roll the paste out into balls about the size of an egg
8. Place them on buttered wafer paper on a baking sheet
9. Set in a moderate oven, and bake to a light color
