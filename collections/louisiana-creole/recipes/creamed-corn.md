## Ingredients

- 8 Fine Ears of Corn
- 1 Pint of Milk Cream
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1/2 Spoonful of Black Pepper
- 1 Dessertspoonful Butter

## Directions

1. Score the corn down the center of each row of grains and then cut from the cob
2. With the knife press out all the pulp from the cob, leaving the hull on the cob
3. Set a porcelain or agate saucepan on the fire
4. Put into this the corn cobs, which you will have cut into pieces
5. Cover with water and let them boil until you have extracted all the juices
6. When the liquid is reduced to about one pint, add the corn and let it boil for about twenty minutes
7. Stir in a quarter of a pint of milk, season with salt and pepper to taste
8. Add a teaspoonful of butter and serve hot
