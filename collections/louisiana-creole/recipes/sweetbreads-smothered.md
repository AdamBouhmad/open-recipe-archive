## Ingredients

- 3 Pairs of Sweetbreads
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Carrot
- 1 Onion
- 2 Sprigs of Thyme
- 2 Bay Leaves
- 1 Pint of Consomme or Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the Sweetbreads as in the recipe for "Sweetbreads Larded With Mushrooms."
2. Put them into a saucepan with two tablespoonfuls of butter, and let them brown slightly.
3. Add a finely-sliced carrot and onion and the minced herbs.
4. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
5. Cover the saucepan with a buttered paper, and then cover closely.
6. Occasionally uncover and turn the Sweetbreads till they are all browned evenly to a nice golden brown.
7. When they have reached this color, add the pint of Consomme or water and cover again and let them simmer for about twenty minutes.
8. Serve with any kind of sauce or garnish that may be desired, placing the sauce on the dish first with the dish very hot, laying the Sweetbreads over the sauce, and garnishing nicely with fried Croutons.
