## Ingredients

- large fresh water terrapin
- water
- bacon
- cloves
- allspice
- salt
- black pepper
- red pepper
- wine
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take a large fresh water terrapin and clean it
2. Place it in a digester with 2 quarts of water, a slice of bacon, 2 dozen cloves, 3 dozen allspice, salt, black and red pepper
3. Boil this for 3 or 4 hours
4. Thicken and brown it before serving
5. Throw in a glass of wine in which has been grated half a nutmeg
