## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Cherries
- 1 Lemon
- 8 Ounces of Sugar

## Directions

1. Stone the cherries and press the juice from the pulp into a porcelain vessel
2. Add a little water and the juice of one lemon
3. Stir well and let stand for two hours
4. Wash the stones well and pound them fine, and add to the cherries, and add eight ounces of sugar
5. Let all stand one hour
6. Strain the mixture and stir well and filter
7. Set on ice till ready to use
8. Serve in glasses with crushed ice
