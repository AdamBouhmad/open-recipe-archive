## Ingredients

- two pints of milk
- four eggs
- a small teacupful of yeast or a yeast cake
- a piece of butter the size of an egg
- a teaspoonful of salt
- sifted flour

## Directions

1. Take two pints of milk, four eggs, and a small teacupful of yeast, or a yeast cake
2. melt a piece of butter in a little of the milk
3. add a teaspoonful of salt
4. thicken with sifted flour until it is like buckwheat batter
5. Set it to rise for eight or ten hours
6. bake in muffin rings, or pour it like batter cakes, on a hot griddle
7. Butter them when cooked this way, just as they come from the griddle
