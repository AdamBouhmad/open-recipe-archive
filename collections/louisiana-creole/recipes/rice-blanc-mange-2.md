## Ingredients

- 1 teacupful of the best rice
- 1 pint of cold water
- 1 pint of new milk
- 1 tablespoonful of white sugar

## Directions

1. Put a teacupful of the best rice into 1 pint of cold water
2. Let it stand until the rice cracks, and looks perfectly white
3. Add 1 pint of new milk, and 1 tablespoonful of white sugar
4. Stir it, and let it gently simmer until all the milk is absorbed, stirring frequently
5. Pour it into a mould to cool
6. The rice may be flavored with vanilla, and eaten with cream
