## Ingredients

- beef brisket
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- butter
- salt
- sweet herbs
- mace
- onion
- egg
- flour

## Directions

1. Before boiling the beef make a dressing of bread crumbs, pepper, butter, salt, sweet herbs, mace, and an onion, all chopped fine and mixed with a beaten egg.
2. Put the dressing between the fat and the lean of the beef; sew it up to keep the dressing in.
3. Flour a cloth, tie the beef up tight in it, and let it boil five or six hours.
