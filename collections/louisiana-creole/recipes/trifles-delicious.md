## Ingredients

- lady fingers
- half dozen macaroons
- one cup of wine or diluted extract
- three tablespoonfuls of jelly or jam
- one pint of milk
- three eggs
- one cup of white sugar
- whites of two eggs
- one cup of white sugar
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a glass bowl, or dish, with lady fingers
2. Break up, and put also half dozen macaroons
3. Pour over them a cup of wine, or diluted extract, to moisten them
4. Put in three tablespoonfuls of jelly or jam
5. Pour over this a boiled custard, made with a pint of milk, three eggs and a cup of white sugar
6. Whip up the whites of two eggs with a cup of white sugar and lemon juice to taste
7. When it will stand alone, put it on the custard, and serve
