## Ingredients

- 3 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 1 Raw Yolk of Egg
- 1 Tablespoonful of Tarragon Vinegar
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Olive Oil
- 1/2 Clove of Garlic, Minced Very Fine
- 3/4 Teaspoonful of Prepared Mustard
- Salt and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs till hard.
2. Remove the shells and set aside the whites, which you will have crumbled fine for a garnish.
3. Put the yolks into a bowl, and mash very fine, till perfectly smooth.
4. Add the mustard, and mix well, and the seasonings of vinegar and salt and Cayenne to taste.
5. Then add the olive oil, drop by drop, working in the egg all the time, and then add the yolk of the raw egg, and work in thoroughly, till light.
6. Then add the juice of half a lemon.
7. Mix well, increasing the quantity of oil or vinegar, according to taste, very slightly.
8. It is now ready to be served with cold meats, fish or salads.
