## Ingredients

- 6 quinces
- water
- lemon peel
- sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 pint of cream
- mace
- nutmeg
- paste

## Directions

1. Take 6 quinces, pare them, cut them in quarters, and stew them with a little water and lemon peel
2. let them cook gently till soft
3. then rub them through a sieve
4. mix with sugar till very sweet
5. add 4 eggs, 1 pint of cream, mace and nutmeg
6. bake it in paste
