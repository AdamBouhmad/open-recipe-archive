## Ingredients

- 16 whites of eggs
- 1 lb. of white sugar
- ¾ lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of flour

## Directions

1. Cream butter
2. Mix the sugar
3. Put in flour and eggs alternately
4. Bake 2 hours
