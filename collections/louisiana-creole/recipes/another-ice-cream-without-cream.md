## Ingredients

- a quart of milk
- sugar
- yolks of eight eggs
- vanilla
- lemon or almond

## Directions

1. Add sugar to a quart of milk until it is very sweet
2. Let this boil on the fire
3. When it boils gently, take it off and pour it scalding hot to the beaten yolks of eight eggs
4. Stir it constantly, but never boil it as the scalding milk will cook the eggs sufficiently
5. It should also be stirred while cooking
6. Flavor with vanilla, or lemon or almond
7. If with a vanilla bean it is better to boil it in the milk before putting in the sugar
8. When the custard is cold, put it in the form or freezer
9. If you have no freezer you can make one, by using a tin kettle with a tight cover, set this in the centre of a tub that is large enough to leave a space of four or five inches around it
10. Fill the space with layers of cracked ice and coarse salt, a layer of ice last
11. Cover the whole with a woolen cover for half an hour
12. Then shake the kettle constantly, after that, until frozen
13. Cover up till wanted
