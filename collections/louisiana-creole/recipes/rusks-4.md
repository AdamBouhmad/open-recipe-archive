## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Warm Milk
- ¼ Cup of Butter
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Cake of Compressed Yeast
- Flour Sufficient to Make a Batter

## Directions

1. Dissolve the yeast in the warm milk, and then add flour sufficient to make a thin batter.
2. Set to rise over night.
3. In the morning beat the sugar and the butter to a cream, and add the eggs, well beaten.
4. Add these to the batter, beat well, add the salt, and sufficient flour to make a soft dough.
5. Then mold the dough into balls with your hands, or into small loaves.
6. Set to rise until they have reached about twice their bulk.
7. Then place in the oven and bake for thirty minutes.
