## Ingredients

- one fresh lemon
- one teacupful of sugar
- one teacupful of butter
- one large teaspoonful of wheat flour
- half a pint of boiling water
- one half nutmeg

## Directions

1. Boil a fresh lemon in plenty of water, until a straw will penetrate it
2. Cut it in slices, and each slice in quarters
3. Add a teacupful of sugar, and the same of butter, with a large teaspoonful of wheat flour worked into it
4. Put all together into a stew-pan, and stir in gradually half a pint of boiling water
5. Keep it over the fire for ten minutes, stirring it all the time
6. Serve with half a nutmeg grated over
