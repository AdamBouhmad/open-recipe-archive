## Ingredients

- 6 Frog Legs
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 3 Large Onions
- 6 Fresh Tomatoes or a Half Can
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 6 Green Sweet Peppers
- 1 Cup of Consommé or Boiling Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Wash the Frogs' legs
2. Put two tablespoonfuls of butter in a saucepan and add the legs
3. Let this brown well, being careful not to burn
4. After ten minutes of very slow cooking on a good fire, take three large onions and slice them and let them brown with the frogs
5. Add one-half dozen nice large fresh tomatoes, or a half can; cover and let these brown
6. Cook very slowly, adding salt and pepper to taste, thyme, bay leaf, and two cloves of garlic, all chopped very fine indeed
7. Let the mixture smother slowly over the fire, and add one-half dozen green sweet peppers sliced very fine, being careful to extract all the seeds
8. Stir well and let it smother twenty minutes longer, stirring frequently to prevent burning
9. When well smothered, that is, when the Frogs' legs are tender, add one cup of broth or consommé; if not, add one cup of boiling water, and let it cook again for half an hour very slowly and well covered
10. Serve hot
