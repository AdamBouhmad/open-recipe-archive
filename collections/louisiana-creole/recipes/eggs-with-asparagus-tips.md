## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of asparagus tips
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of milk
- salt and pepper
- buttered toast

## Directions

1. Boil the asparagus tips.
2. Put the eggs into a saucepan, with the butter, after seasoning well with salt and pepper, and mixing the milk.
3. Stir a second, and throw in the asparagus.
4. Proceed to scramble as in the preceding recipe.
5. Serve on buttered toast.
