## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- one and a half cups of brown sugar
- one cup of milk
- sweet or sour
- yeast powder or soda
- one cup of molasses
- four eggs
- one nutmeg
- one paning of raisins
- five cups of flour

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients, using two teaspoonfuls yeast powder if using yeast powder, or one heaping teaspoonful soda if using soda
