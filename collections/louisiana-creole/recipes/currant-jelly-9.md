## Ingredients

- Currants that are fully ripe
- 1 pound of fine white sugar to every pint of juice

## Directions

1. Take currants that are fully ripe
2. Clean, pick, wash and boil
3. To every pint of juice add one pound of fine white sugar
4. Let it boil for twenty minutes, always skimming it while on the fire
5. Do not let it remain longer than twenty minutes, or it will grow dark
6. Pour immediately into cups
