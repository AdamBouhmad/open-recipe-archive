## Ingredients

- 8 Quarts of Mushrooms
- Salted water

## Directions

1. Cook the mushrooms for three-quarters of an hour in salted water.
2. Drain lightly and can them, excluding all air.
3. Submit to the bain-marie, letting them cool gradually.
4. Keep in a cool place.
