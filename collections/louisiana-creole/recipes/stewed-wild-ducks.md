## Ingredients

- 1 Pair of Ducks
- 1 Square Inch of Ham
- 2 Onions
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Herb Bouquet Chopped Very Fine
- 1 Glass of Claret
- 1 Cup of Water
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and pick the ducks nicely.
2. Cut into joints, or stew whole, as desired.
3. Rub well with salt and pepper.
4. Chop two onions very fine.
5. Put them into the stewpan with a tablespoonful of melted butter, and let them brown slightly.
6. Add the well-seasoned ducks.
7. Let these brown well and add the one square inch of finely minced ham.
8. Add the clove of garlic and two sprigs each of thyme, parsley and one bay leaf, minced very fine.
9. Let this brown with the ducks, stirring frequently, and then pour over one good glass of claret.
10. Let this simmer for ten minutes, stirring constantly, so that it will not burn, and add one cup of boiling water.
11. Season well to taste, and let the ducks simmer well for about an hour.
