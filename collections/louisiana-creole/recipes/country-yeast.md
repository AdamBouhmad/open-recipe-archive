## Ingredients

- a large handful of hops
- 3 pints of water
- flour
- a heaping teaspoonful of salt
- a large teacupful of lively yeast

## Directions

1. Put hops into a vessel with water and boil half an hour, or until the strength is extracted
2. Strain it through a sieve on to 8 or 10 spoonfuls of flour
3. Stir until all the dry flour is moistened, or as thick as griddle cakes
4. Add a heaping teaspoonful of salt
5. When about blood-warm, stir in a large teacupful of lively yeast
