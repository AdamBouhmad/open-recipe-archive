## Ingredients

- pineapples
- white sugar (1 lb. per lb. of fruit)

## Directions

1. Pare and slice the pineapples, and to each lb. of fruit add 1 lb. of white sugar
2. Put in the jar first a layer of sugar pulverized, then a layer of pines
3. Then one of sugar and another of pines, until the jar is full
4. Tie up tight, to exclude the light and air
5. This is delicious, and keeps well
6. Not to be scalded
