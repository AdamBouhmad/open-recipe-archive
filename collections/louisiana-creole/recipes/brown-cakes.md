## Ingredients

- 2 cups of sugar
- 1 cup of butter
- 3 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of soda
- a pinch of salt
- mace to the taste
- flour enough to make a soft dough
- lard for frying
- sugar for sifting

## Directions

1. roll and cut into small cakes
2. throw them into boiling lard
3. turn them over
4. take them out with a fork, dripping all the lard from them
5. sift sugar over each one whilst hot
