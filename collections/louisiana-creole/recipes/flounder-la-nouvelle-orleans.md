## Ingredients

- A 3-pound Flounder
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 6 Allspice
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 2 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Sprig of Sweet Basil
- 1 Can of Mushrooms
- Croutons
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Select a fine, fresh Flounder, parboil, by scalding well after it has been cleansed.
2. Slit open in scores on top and put in butter and salt, and set to bake in an oven.
3. When it is two-thirds cooked, take off and pour over a cream sauce made of two tablespoonfuls of flour, one tablespoonful of butter, spice, thyme, parsley, sweet basil and bay leaf, a can of mushrooms, all chopped fine, and cook about five minutes.
4. After pouring this over the fish, put back in the oven and let it bake till done, which will be in about fifteen minutes.
5. Prepare Croutons, or crusts of bread cut in diamond shape, fry these in butter and use as a garnish for the fish.
