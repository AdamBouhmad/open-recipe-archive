## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Sago
- 8 Large Apples
- 1 Quart of Boiling Water
- Sweetened Cream

## Directions

1. Soak the Sago for half an hour in cold water, and then put it in a farina boiler and add one quart of boiling water.
2. Pare the apples, and add to the Sago, and let all boil together till very tender.
3. Sweeten to taste.
4. Then bake in a moderate oven for twenty minutes, and serve with cream.
