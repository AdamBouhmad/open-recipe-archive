## Ingredients

- 3 Kidneys
- 1 Cup of Water
- 1½ Tablespoon of Butter
- ½ Glass of White Wine
- 1 Teaspoonful of Sherry Wine
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1 Sprig Each of Chopped Parsley, Thyme and Bay Leaf, Very Fine

## Directions

1. Select perfectly fresh kidneys; wash them well, and then slice very thin
2. Season well with salt and pepper
3. Put one and a half tablespoonfuls of butter into the saucepan; when melted and very hot, add the kidneys and chopped herbs, being very careful to stir constantly and very fast, to prevent burning
4. Add a half glass of White Wine, if possible, and one cup of water or consommé
5. Let it boil up once, and the kidneys are ready to be served
