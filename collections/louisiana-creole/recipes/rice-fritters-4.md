## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Cold Rice
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Orange or Lemon Essence
- A Pinch of Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take one cup of cold rice, mash it well, and then pass through a sieve.
2. Season with the orange essence or lemon, a pinch of cinnamon, and a tablespoonful of butter.
3. Add the beaten yolks of two eggs.
4. Then make the rice into small boulettes or balls, roll in a beaten egg, fry to a golden brown, sprinkle with white sugar and serve.
