## Ingredients

- 2 dozen fresh green peppers
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1/2 onion, chopped very fine
- 1/2 inch of boiled ham
- 1 clove of garlic
- 1/2 cup of bread, wet and squeezed thoroughly
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Cayenne pepper
- Grated bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Parboil one dozen of the peppers.
2. Take off the skins and cut the topmost tip, and clean the inside of the peppers, throwing the seeds away.
3. Take a dozen or more raw peppers, cut off the stalk or stem, and clean the insides of all seeds, throwing the seeds away.
4. Chop the peppers very fine.
5. Put a tablespoonful of butter on the fire.
6. Add one-half of an onion, minced fine, and let it brown slightly in the butter.
7. Add the peppers, minced very fine, almost mashed into a jelly, and the half inch of ham, minced very fine.
8. As these brown, add the clove of garlic, minced fine.
9. When the peppers are well-cooked add one-half a cup of bread that has been wet in water and squeezed thoroughly.
10. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and let it brown.
11. Add a dash of Cayenne pepper.
12. When the stuffing is well browned and well seasoned, take from the stove and stuff the peppers.
13. Sprinkle a little grated bread crumbs on top, and a dot of butter on each pepper top.
14. Put it in the stove, let it brown, and serve hot with meats, broiled or roasted, chicken, etc.
