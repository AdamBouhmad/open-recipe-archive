## Ingredients

- 100 oysters
- 3 hard boiled eggs
- 1 large tablespoonful of flour
- butter
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 of allspice
- cayenne
- 1 large fresh lemon

## Directions

1. Drain the juice from 100 oysters
2. Pour the juice into a saucepan
3. Let it slowly simmer
4. Skim it very carefully
5. Rub the yolks of 3 hard boiled eggs and 1 large tablespoonful of flour well together
6. Stir this into the juice
7. Add small pieces of butter, a teaspoonful of salt and 1/2 of allspice, a very little cayenne, and the juice of a large fresh lemon
8. Let this simmer for 10 minutes
9. Just before dishing add the oysters
