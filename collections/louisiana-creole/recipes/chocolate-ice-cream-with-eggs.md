## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Chocolate
- 6 Ounces of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 8 Yolks of Eggs
- 1 Pint of Whipped Cream
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Dissolve the chocolate in a little water, and add it to the boiling milk
2. Take off, and pour over the eggs and sugar, which you will have beaten to a thick cream
3. Add the Vanilla, and set to cool
4. Add the remaining cream, and freeze as directed
