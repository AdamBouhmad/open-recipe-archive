## Ingredients

- fresh cod
- bread crumbs
- yolk of an egg
- salt
- pepper
- boiling lard

## Directions

1. Cut the fresh cod into slices 1 inch in thickness
2. dry it in a towel
3. have ready bread crumbs, and the yolk of an egg well beaten
4. salt and pepper the fish
5. dip each slice first in the egg and then in the bread crumbs
6. prepare boiling lard and lay them into it
7. fry till a nice brown
8. drain off all the fat from each slice and serve hot
