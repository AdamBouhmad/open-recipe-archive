## Ingredients

- 1 can of salmon
- 2 heads of crisp lettuce
- 1 cup of milk
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 tablespoonful of chopped parsley
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Sauce a la mayonnaise

## Directions

1. To one can of salmon allow two good sized heads of young lettuce.
2. Make a bed of the crisp hearts of the leaves, and tear the other leaves into small pieces with a fork, making very delicate shreds.
3. Drain the oil from the can of salmon, and separate the fish into flakes.
4. Take a cupful of boiling milk and a tablespoonful of butter and two of flour, and stir over the fire until quite smooth.
5. Add to this a tablespoonful of chopped parsley and the salmon.
6. Season with pepper and salt, and let it cook about ten minutes.
7. Set away to cool.
