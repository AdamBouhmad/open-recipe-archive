## Ingredients

- 8 Fine Ears of Corn
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Cup of Milk or Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter

## Directions

1. Boil the corn, and then cut the grains from the cob with a sharp knife.
2. Put in a saucepan and add one tablespoonful of butter.
3. Add the milk, and season nicely to taste.
4. Let the corn boil for about ten minutes and serve hot.
