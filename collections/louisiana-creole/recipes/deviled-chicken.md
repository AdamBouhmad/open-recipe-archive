## Ingredients

- 1 Chicken
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Onion
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig of Parsley
- 1/2 of a Clove of Garlic
- 1 Glass of White Wine
- 1 Pint of Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Prepared Mustard
- Salt and Pepper
- A Dash of Cayenne

## Directions

1. Boil the chicken according to the above recipe.
2. Mince the meat fine.
3. Make a sauce by putting into a saucepan one tablespoonful of butter, and as it melts add one onion, minced very fine; a sprig of minced parsley, one minced bay leaf, and a half clove of minced garlic.
4. Let it simmer gently without browning and then add one tablespoonful of flour, well sifted.
5. Mix thoroughly, and add three tablespoonfuls of vinegar or a wineglass of White Wine.
6. Stir well and add one pint of the water in which the chicken was boiled.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste and a slight dash of Cayenne.
8. Add one teaspoonful of prepared mustard.
9. Let it simmer three minutes longer, and as it comes to a boil pour over the chicken and serve.
