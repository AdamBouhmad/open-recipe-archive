## Ingredients

- six spoonfuls of flour
- six of corn meal
- a little salt
- four eggs
- a cup of milk

## Directions

1. Take six spoonfuls of flour, and six of corn meal
2. Add a little salt and sift them together
3. Make a batter with four eggs, and a cup of milk
4. Stir in the flour and meal
5. Make it a soft batter
6. Bake in small tins for breakfast
