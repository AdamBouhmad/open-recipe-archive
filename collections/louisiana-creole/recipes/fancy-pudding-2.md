## Ingredients

- A loaf of French bread sliced
- J pint of milk
- Other J pint of milk
- 4 eggs
- Sugar and cinnamon to the taste
- Lard or butter for frying

## Directions

1. Take a loaf of French bread sliced, J pint of milk poured over it
2. Mix the other J pint with 4 well beaten eggs
3. Add sugar and cinnamon to the taste
4. Dip each slice of bread in the mixture, and fry it in lard or butter
5. Serve it with a sauce of sugar, wine and nutmeg
