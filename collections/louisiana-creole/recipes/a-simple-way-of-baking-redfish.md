## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of Redfish or a Fish Weighing 4 Pounds
- 2 Large Onions
- 3 Sprigs of Parsley
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 3 Sprigs of Thyme
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- A Hollandaise Sauce

## Directions

1. When purchased in slices, clean the outer edges well of the fins, wash and then rub the fish well with salt and pepper
2. Prepare some chopped onion and parsley and lay in a deep baking pan
3. Place a layer of fish over this and then a layer of small lumps of butter and chopped parsley and onion
4. Place over this another layer of fish
5. Cover with another layer of chopped parsley, onions and butter, and place in the oven to bake
6. Bake about an hour, basting frequently, and serve with a Hollandaise Sauce
