## Ingredients

- 6 Tongues
- 1 Large Onion, Cut Fine
- 2 Carrots, Cut Fine
- 1 Herb Bouquet, Minced Very Fine
- 3 Pickles
- ¼ of a Cup of Capers
- 1 Pint of Boiling Water
- 2 Slices of Bacon

## Directions

1. Scald and blanch the tongue removing the skins.
2. Throw them into cold water, dry and piqué or lard very delicately with larding needles.
3. Season well with salt and pepper.
4. Slice the bacon into fine strips and lay in the bottom of a saucepan.
5. Place the lamb tongues over this.
6. Place on top another fine layer of bacon in very fine strips.
7. Add the minced carrots, onion, herbs, and salt and pepper again to taste.
8. Let it simmer for about fifteen minutes and then moisten with about a pint of boiling water or broth.
9. Let it cook over a slow fire about three hours.
10. Take out the tongues and place them on a hot dish.
11. Strain the sauce through a sieve, set back on the stove a few seconds, and add one-quarter of a cup of capers and three pickles, sliced fine.
12. Stir well and let it boil up once.
13. Pour over the tongues and serve.
