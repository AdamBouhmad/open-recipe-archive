## Ingredients

- slices of sponge cake
- preserve (red or black currant)
- sherry
- whites of four eggs
- powdered loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Lay slices of sponge cake at the bottom of a glass dish.
2. Spread over them a layer of preserve (red or black currant is very good for the purpose).
3. Place over that more slices of sponge cake, then another layer of jam.
4. Do this until you have filled the dish.
5. Pour over it sufficient sherry to soak the cake properly.
6. Beat up the whites of four eggs with sufficient powdered loaf sugar to make it a very stiff froth.
7. Cover the top of the cake completely with the froth, and bake.
