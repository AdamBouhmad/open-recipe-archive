## Ingredients

- half a pint of veal stock
- half a pound of chestnuts
- peel of half a lemon
- a cupful of cream or milk
- cayenne
- salt

## Directions

1. Remove the dark shell of the chestnuts, and scald them until the inner skin can be taken off
2. Put them into the saucepan with the stock, the lemon peel cut very thin, some cayenne and salt
3. Let it simmer till the chestnuts are quite soft
4. Rub it through a sieve
5. Add the seasoning and cream, and let it simmer for a few minutes, taking care it does not boil, and stirring constantly
