## Ingredients

- 1½ Quarts of Best Champagne or Claret
- 1 Quart of Strawberry Juice
- 2 Quarts of Seltzer Water
- 1 Pint of Lemon Juice
- 1 Pint of Pineapple Juice
- 1½ Pounds of Sugar
- 1 Large Piece of Ice
- 3 Dozen Fine, Ripe Strawberries

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar and water
2. Add the liquor, using either Claret or Champagne
3. Add the fruit juices and then the whole strawberries
4. Set to cool and serve in small punch glasses
