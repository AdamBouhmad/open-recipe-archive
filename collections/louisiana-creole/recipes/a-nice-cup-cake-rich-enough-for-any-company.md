## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- three cups of sugar
- five eggs
- five cups of sifted flour
- one cup of sour cream
- one teaspoonful of soda
- a glass of wine
- a little nutmeg

## Directions

1. Work butter and sugar to a cream
2. beat eggs separately
3. stir in flour
4. add sour cream and soda
5. flavor with wine and nutmeg
6. bake in quick oven in round tins
7. ice while warm
