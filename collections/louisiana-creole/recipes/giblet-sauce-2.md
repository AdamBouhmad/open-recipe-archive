## Ingredients

- The Turkey Giblets
- 1 Cup of Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Put the giblets or simply the gizzard into a saucepan and cover well with water.
2. Let them simmer as long as the turkey roasts, then cut them fine and take the turkey out of the pan on which it has been roasted.
3. Add the giblets and stir well, and then add a cup of the water in which the giblets have been boiled.
4. Season to the taste and serve in a sauce dish, pouring over the dressing when serving the turkey.
