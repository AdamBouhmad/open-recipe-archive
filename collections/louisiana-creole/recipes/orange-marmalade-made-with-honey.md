## Ingredients

- Ripe oranges
- Strained honey
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Quarter a dozen large ripe oranges
2. Remove the rind, seeds and filaments, but save all the juice
3. Put the juice and pulp into a porcelain kettle, with an equal quantity of strained honey, adding one-third as much sugar as honey
4. Boil until very thick, sweet and clear
5. When cold, put it in small jars
