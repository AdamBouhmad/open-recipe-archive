## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Large Crabs
- Sliced Stale Bread
- Butter
- Milk
- Cayenne
- Salt
- Chopped thyme
- Bay leaf
- Sliced lemon
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil according to recipe one dozen fine large crabs and pick out all the meat
2. Season nicely with salt, Cayenne and chopped thyme and bay leaf
3. Take stale bread and slice very thin
4. Lay in a little milk to moisten
5. Butter a baking pan and cover the bottom with a layer of bread
6. Put in a layer of crab meat and lay over at intervals slices of lemon cut very fine and thin
7. Dot here and there with bits of butter, and then spread over another layer of bread
8. Then another layer of crabs, and repeat till the meat is used up
9. Lay on top a thick sprinkling of bread crumbs, dotted with butter
10. Place in the oven and bake for about twenty minutes
11. Serve hot
