## Ingredients

- one-half pound of butter
- one-half pound of grated bread crumbs
- one-half pound of sugar
- the juice of two lemons with the rinds grated in
- six eggs well beaten
- a glass of brandy
- four tablespoonfuls of marmalade
- wine sauce

## Directions

1. Take one-half pound of butter, one-half pound of grated bread crumbs, one-half pound of sugar, the juice of two lemons with the rinds grated in.
2. Add six eggs well beaten, a glass of brandy and four tablespoonfuls of marmalade.
3. Steam this pudding in a mould and serve with wine sauce.
