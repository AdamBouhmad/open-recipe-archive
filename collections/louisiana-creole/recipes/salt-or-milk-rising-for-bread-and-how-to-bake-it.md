## Ingredients

- a pint of new milk
- nearly a pint of boiling water
- a teaspoonful of fine salt
- flour
- fine flour
- a little lard
- a cup of water

## Directions

1. Take a pint of new milk, stir in nearly a pint of boiling water
2. then salt it with a teaspoonful of fine salt
3. thicken this with flour enough to make a thick batter
4. Set it in a warm place to rise
5. it is ready to mix into bread
6. Mix the yeast in a soft dough with fine flour, a little lard, and a cup of water
7. mould it, and set it to rise
8. When well risen, bake it a nice brown
9. Wrap it in a damp cloth for a few minutes, and let it cool slowly before it is cut
