## Ingredients

- macaroni
- milk
- water
- salt
- grated cheese
- butter
- pepper
- mustard

## Directions

1. Boil as much macaroni as will fill the dish, in milk and water, till quite tender
2. Drain it on a sieve
3. Sprinkle a little salt over it
4. Put a layer in the dish, and one of grated cheese, with butter, pepper and mustard, until you fill the dish
5. Bake it in a quick oven, some 20 or 30 minutes
