## Ingredients

- dozen or more fine, ripe tomatoes
- butter
- stale bread
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- sugar

## Directions

1. Scald and peel about a dozen or more fine, ripe tomatoes
2. Butter a shallow baking dish, and put in the finest, without breaking them, and not quite touching
3. Fill up the space between with stale bread, buttered
4. Mash the rest of the tomatoes, and strain out all the hard parts
5. Mix with a spoonful of butter, pepper and salt
6. Pour it over the dish
7. Strew bread crumbs on the top
8. Sprinkle sugar between each layer of tomatoes
9. Bake about ½ hour
