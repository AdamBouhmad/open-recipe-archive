## Ingredients

- nicely headed cabbages
- 1 gill of salt
- cold water
- cold vinegar
- 2 tablespoonfuls of turmeric
- 2 gallons of the best vinegar
- 2 lbs. of brown sugar
- 1 tablespoonful best cayenne pepper
- 2 ounces of turmeric

## Directions

1. Quarter the cabbages
2. Put them in a porcelain skillet of cold water with 1 gill of salt, and let them boil until they may be pierced easily with a straw
3. Place them on a dish carefully to drain
4. While draining, take a jar of cold vinegar and stir in 2 tablespoonfuls of turmeric
5. Place the cabbage in this vinegar and let it remain 48 hours
6. Take 2 gallons of the best vinegar, 2 lbs. of brown sugar, 1 tablespoonful best cayenne pepper, 2 ounces of turmeric and tie them up in 6 muslin bags
7. Boil two of these bags with the sugar and pepper in the vinegar, and while boiling, pour upon the cabbage, after having been placed in a dry place with the other bags of turmeric
8. Made and fit for use in one week
