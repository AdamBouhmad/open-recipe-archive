## Ingredients

- 8 Cups of Buckwheat Flour
- 1½ Pints of Sweet Milk or Water
- ⅜ Cake of Compressed Yeast
- ⅜ Teaspoonful of Salt
- ½ Teaspoonful of Soda or Saleratus

## Directions

1. Take a small earthen crock, or a pitcher, and put the warm milk or water, or half milk and water, into it, and then add the salt and the buckwheat flour.
2. Beat till perfectly smooth, and then, when you have a stiff batter, add the yeast, dissolved in a little water.
3. Beat this till smooth, and then cover well and set to rise over night.
4. In the morning add a half teaspoonful of soda or saleratus, to remove any sourness that may have accrued during the night.
5. Bake on a griddle, like Batter Cakes.
