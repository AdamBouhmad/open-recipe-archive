## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Raspberry Juice
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup
- 1 Ounce of Raspberry Essence

## Directions

1. Pick, stem and wash the berries
2. Proceed in exactly the same manner as for Strawberry Syrup
3. Add the essence when about to bottle
