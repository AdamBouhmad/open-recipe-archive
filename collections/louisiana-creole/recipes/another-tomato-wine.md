## Ingredients

- berries or small tomatoes
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Bruise your berries, or small tomatoes
2. Measure the juice, and add two pounds of sugar to each gallon
3. Put it in a cask, adding two gallons of water to each four gallons of juice
4. Let it ferment like blackberry wine
