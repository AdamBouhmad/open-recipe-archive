## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Louisiana Molasses
- 1 Cup of Boiling Milk or Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder or Soda
- 3% Cups of Flour (Sifted)
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Cup Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Ground Ginger
- % Teaspoonful of Cloves

## Directions

1. Melt the butter.
2. Dissolve the soda in the boiling water, using about one tablespoonful, and add it to the molasses.
3. Then beat the sugar and butter to a cream, until very light, and add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs.
4. Add these to the molasses, and then stir in the cream.
5. Beat till smooth, and then add gradually the flour, beating till very light and smooth.
6. Now add the ginger and cloves, or a teaspoonful of cinnamon, and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
7. Place in a shallow cake pan, buttered, and bake in a moderate oven from thirty to forty-five minutes.
