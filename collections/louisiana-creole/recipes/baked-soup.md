## Ingredients

- beef or mutton
- vegetables
- salt
- pepper
- a pint of peas
- Patna rice
- four quarts of water

## Directions

1. Cut the beef or mutton and the vegetables in pieces, season them with salt and pepper, and put them into a jar with a pint of peas and the Patna rice. Pour in four quarts of water, cover the jar very closely, and set it in the oven to bake. When done, strain it through a sieve, and serve it very hot.
