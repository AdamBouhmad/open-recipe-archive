## Ingredients

- a tablespoonful of arrow-root
- cold water
- half a pint of boiling water
- salt
- sugar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix a tablespoonful of arrow-root, or for an infant, half as much
2. when mixed with cold water, stir in it half a pint of boiling water
3. Season with salt, sugar or nutmeg
