## Ingredients

- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Pound of Fine White Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lemon Juice
- 1 Tablespoonful of Any Kind of Essence Desired

## Directions

1. Make a Royal Icing of the finest possible white loaf sugar, sifted, and mixed or worked into a paste with the whites of eggs and lemon juice in the proportions given for Plain Icing
2. The paste must be softer than Plain Icing
3. Flavor with any kind of essence, according to fancy
