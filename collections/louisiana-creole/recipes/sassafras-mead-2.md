## Ingredients

- 4 Bunches of Sassafras Roots
- 1.25 Pints of Honey
- 3.5 Pints of Louisiana Molasses
- 1 Tablespoonful of Cream of Tartar
- 0.5 Teaspoonful of Carbonate of Soda

## Directions

1. Take the roots of sassafras, and make about two quarts of Sassafras Tea
2. Strain well
3. Set to boil again, and when it boils add one and one-half pints of honey, and three and one-half pints of Louisiana Syrup or Molasses
4. Add a tablespoonful of Cream of Tartar
5. Stir well and set to cool
6. When cool strain it
7. Take about a dozen clean bottles and fill with the mixture
8. Cork very tight, and put in a cool place
9. In a day it will be ready for use
10. When serving this Mead, take a glass and fill half full with ice water
11. Add a tablespoonful of the Mead and stir in a half teaspoonful of Carbonate of Soda
