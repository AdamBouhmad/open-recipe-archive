## Ingredients

- 3 1/2 ounces of laurel leaf
- 2 1/2 ounces of myrtle flowers
- 1 gallon of brandy
- 6 pounds of white sugar
- 3 1/4 pints of water
- 12 cloves
- 1/2 of a nutmeg

## Directions

1. Bruise or pound the bay leaves, the myrtle flowers, the coarsely-grated nutmeg and the cloves
2. Pour over them one gallon of brandy
3. Reduce over a gentle fire the sugar and water till well-dissolved
4. Let it cool
5. Mix the whole with the bay leaves and brandy, mix well, filter, put into tightly corked bottles, and preserve
