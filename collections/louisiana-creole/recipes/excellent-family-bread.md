## Ingredients

- a peck of sifted flour
- half a pint of family yeast, or a gill of brewers' yeast
- new milk, or milk and water warm
- a cup of shortening
- a teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Take a peck of sifted flour, half a pint of family yeast, or a gill of brewers' yeast
2. wet all up soft with new milk, or milk and water warm
3. Add a cup of shortening, and a teaspoonful of salt
4. Knead it faithfully, and set it in a warm place to rise
5. It is better to take the dough when risen, and work it down again; but some dislike the trouble, and bake it as soon as it rises
