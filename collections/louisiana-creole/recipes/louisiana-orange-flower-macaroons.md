## Ingredients

- a coffee cup of freshly gathered petals of the orange
- two pounds of dry, sifted white sugar
- whites of seven eggs

## Directions

1. Cut orange petals with a pair of scissors into sugar; this keeps their color fresh
2. Beat the whites of seven eggs to a stiff froth
3. Add to the orange flowers and sugar
4. Drop this mixture on white paper in small cakes
5. Bake in a slow oven; do not let them brown
