## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup of flour
- 3 cups of ground rice
- 8 apples, tart and not overripe
- 2 quarts of milk
- sugar and cinnamon
- 1/2 of the peel of an orange
- 3/4 of a grated nutmeg
- butter

## Directions

1. Pare the apples and take out the cores, leaving the apples whole.
2. Take the ground cinnamon and sugar, and mix well, and fill the cores with this mixture.
3. Boil the rice in milk till it comes to the consistency of flour, having added the grated peel of an orange and a half teaspoonful of grated nutmeg and a half cup of flour.
4. Take off the fire, and let it cool.
5. Cover each apple all over with a very thick coating of the rice, and tie each dumpling in a cloth very tightly, and put them in a pot of cold water.
6. Bring the water to a quick boil, and boil the apples for three-quarters of an hour.
7. When done, untie the cloth and place the dumplings carefully on a large dish.
8. Sprinkle each with a little grated nutmeg, put on top of each a dot of butter, set in the oven for five or ten minutes to brown, and serve with a Hard or Cream Sauce.
