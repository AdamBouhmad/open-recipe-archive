## Ingredients

- 1 box of gelatine
- 1 quart of milk
- 1 lb. of powdered sugar
- 10 eggs
- 1 pint of cold water
- 1 pint of boiling water
- vanilla
- lady fingers or sponge cake

## Directions

1. Pour over the gelatine 1 pint of cold water and allow it to soak at least 10 minutes
2. Pour over 1 pint of boiling water
3. Beat the yolks of the eggs with the sugar
4. Boil the milk in a porcelain or brass kettle
5. When milk comes to a boil, pour in the gelatine and let the mixture come to a boil again, stirring all the time
6. As soon as it boils, pour over the beaten eggs and sugar, stirring briskly to mix it well
7. Have the kettle thoroughly scraped at the bottom, then pour the whole back into it
8. Put it over a moderate fire to thicken, being careful not to let it curdle
9. The moment it begins to simmer, take it off and pour out, stirring occasionally until it cools
10. When cool, add the beaten whites and flavor with vanilla
11. Line a pan with lady fingers or sponge cake and pour in the mixture
12. Set it in the coolest place possible to congeal
13. To prevent the lady fingers floating out of their places, pour a little of the mixture in and let it congeal to fix them at the bottom and sides of the pan
14. When that is done, pour in the rest of the mixture and it will congeal within the cake
