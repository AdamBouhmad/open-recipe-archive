## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of butter
- 3/4 lb. of finely pounded white sugar
- 1/2 lb. dried and sifted flour
- 1/2 lb. of blanched sweet almonds cut into small thin pieces
- 1 well beaten egg
- a little rose water

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream
2. Mix in the sugar, flour, almonds, egg, and rose water
3. Mix all well together
4. With a spoon drop the batter upon wafer paper or tins
5. Bake
