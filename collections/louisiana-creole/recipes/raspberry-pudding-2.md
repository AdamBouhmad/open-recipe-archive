## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Raspberries
- % Pound of Powdered Sugar
- % Pound of Butter
- 1 Gill of Sherry Wine and Brandy Mixed
- 1 Stale Small Sponge Cake
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Stew the berries and proceed in the same manner as for Orange Pudding
2. Bake for about twenty minutes or half an hour after adding the fruit
3. Serve cold, with a Cream or Hard Sauce
