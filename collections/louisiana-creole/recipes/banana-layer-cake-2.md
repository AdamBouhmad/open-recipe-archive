## Ingredients

- 9 Eggs
- 3/4 Pound of Flour
- 1 1/2 Cups of Pulverized Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon or Vanilla Extract
- 3 Bananas
- Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare four layers of Sponge Cake
2. Slice the bananas lengthwise very nicely and lay on the bottom layer
3. Sprinkle with a little powdered sugar, and then spread a little Cream Sauce which has been made to the consistency of Cream Puff Filling
4. Over this put on the second layer of cake; cover nicely with the bananas and the Cream Sauce, and proceed thus till the top layer is placed over the bananas
5. Then sprinkle with white sugar or Ice, according to taste
