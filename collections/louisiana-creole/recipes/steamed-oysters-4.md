## Ingredients

- 4 Dozen Oysters
- A Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- A Dash of Cayenne

## Directions

1. Have ready a pot of boiling water
2. Drain the oysters in their shells and put them in a shallow tin pan, the bottom being perforated
3. Cover and put them over the steamer
4. Let them stand about ten minutes, put into a hot dish, season with pepper and Cayenne, and serve with drawn butter sauce
5. If one has not the perforated tin, steam the oysters in their shells by washing the shells thoroughly on the outside, placing the oysters in the steamer and covering, letting them remain about fifteen minutes over the boiling water, or until the shells open easily
