## Ingredients

- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup
- 3 Ounces of Extract of Vanilla
- 1/4 Ounce of Caramel

## Directions

1. Boil the syrup and add the Caramel
2. Drain, and then add the essence of vanilla
3. Stir well and bottle
