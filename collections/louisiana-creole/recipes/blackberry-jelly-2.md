## Ingredients

- Blackberries
- Water
- White sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the fruit till tender in a little water
2. Throw off the water, bruise and strain the fruit
3. To each pint of the juice add one pound of white sugar
4. Put it now in a preserving-pan, and boil it rapidly, but do not stir it while boiling, as that breaks the jelly
5. Skim it carefully, and when it jellies, pour it into tumblers or small jars
