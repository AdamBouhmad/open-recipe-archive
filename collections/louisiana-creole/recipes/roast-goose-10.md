## Ingredients

- 1 Young Goose
- 1 Onions
- 1 Cup of Mashed Potatoes
- 1/2 Teaspoonfuls of Thyme
- 2 Sprigs of Parsley
- 4 Apples
- 1 1/2 Tablespoonfuls of Lard
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Pick the goose by hand picking, never scald it
2. Singe and clean
3. Season well
4. For the dressing: take one cup of mashed potatoes, four apples peeled nicely and cored, and four onions, one-half teaspoonful of sage powdered well, one-half teaspoonful of thyme, and pepper and salt to taste
5. Place the apples and onions and herbs in a saucepan and add water sufficiently to cover nicely
6. Let all cook together till soft
7. Mash well and rub through a sieve
8. Add the cup of mashed potatoes and mix well, seasoning with salt and pepper
9. Stuff the body and craw, sew up and truss the goose
10. Put into the roasting pan, rubbing a half tablespoonful of lard over it and pouring over a half cup of water, boiling
11. Baste the goose very frequently, say every ten minutes
12. Roast allowing twenty-five minutes to every pound, generally requiring at least an hour and a half
13. If a Green Goose, cover from the beginning with a piece of buttered paper
14. Serve with Apple Sauce
