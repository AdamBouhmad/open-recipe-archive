## Ingredients

- 8 eggs
- ½ lb. of sugar
- ½ lb. of butter
- grated nutmeg
- rich paste for edge of dish

## Directions

1. beat up eggs
2. put them into a stewpan with sugar, butter, and grated nutmeg
3. put it on the fire, stirring it till it thickens
4. pour it into a bowl to cool
5. set a rich paste around the edge of the dish
6. pour in the pudding
7. bake it in a moderate oven
