## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Apricot Marmalade
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Gill of Water
- 1 Gill of Brandy

## Directions

1. Take four ounces of Apricot Marmalade and put into a saucepan.
2. Add the butter and water, and set on a brisk fire.
3. Stir constantly until it comes to a boil, and then add a gill of good brandy.
4. Let the mixture cook for two minutes more, and serve with baked apple dumplings, boiled dumplings, or apple pudding, as desired.
