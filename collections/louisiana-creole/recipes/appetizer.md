## Ingredients

- A dash of absinthe
- 1 teaspoonful of vermouth
- 1 dash of pepsin
- 3 tablespoonfuls of seltzer water
- Finely crushed ice

## Directions

1. Fill a glass with finely-crushed ice, and add the above ingredients.
2. Shake well together, pour into a small, delicately thin tumbler, and serve cold.
