## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Fine Hominy
- 3 Eggs
- 1/4 Cup of Melted Butter
- 3 Cups of Fresh Milk or Sour Milk
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of White Sugar
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Salt
- 1 Teaspoonful of Soda, if Sour Milk is Used
- 1/4 Cup of Flour

## Directions

1. Mash the hominy through a sieve, and then stir in the milk, salt, yeast powder and sugar.
2. Add the yolks of the eggs, beaten very light, and then the flour, well-sifted.
3. Lastly, add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, and bake in a quick oven for twenty minutes.
4. If buttermilk is used, add a half teaspoonful of soda, instead of the yeast powder.
