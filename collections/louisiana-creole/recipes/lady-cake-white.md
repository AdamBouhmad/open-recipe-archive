## Ingredients

- 1% Cups of Flour
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- % Cup of Butter
- The Whites of 4 Eggs
- % Cup of Milk
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of Peach Extract

## Directions

1. Cream the butter, add the eggs, beating the whites to a stiff froth, and mixing well.
2. Add the milk, and gradually add the flour, blending thoroughly, and beating till very light.
3. Add the essence, and bake in a moderate oven for one hour.
