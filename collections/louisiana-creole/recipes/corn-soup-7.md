## Ingredients

- 6 pounds lower ribs of beef
- 6 quarts cold water
- 1 quart sliced fresh tomatoes
- 1 quart corn sliced from the cob
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- 1 pod cayenne pepper without seeds
- salt and black pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Put the meat and water into a soup pot and as soon as the scum begins to rise, skim carefully
2. Add the tomatoes and the corncobs
3. Cook for four hours or so longer
4. Take out the corncobs and add the corn cut fine
5. Add salt and pepper to suit the taste and a pod of cayenne pepper without the seeds
6. Cook one hour longer and serve with slices of toast bread
