## Ingredients

- one dozen crabs
- one pound bread crumbs
- two tablespoons butter
- one teaspoon salt
- half teaspoon black pepper
- slice of onion
- half tablespoon chopped parsley
- thyme
- one egg
- three tablespoons lard

## Directions

1. Boil the crabs five minutes
2. Pick and chop them, adding all the ingredients
3. Shape in forms of mutton chops, and roll in the beaten egg
4. Stick in a claw
5. Put in a frying pan three tablespoons of lard
6. When boiling, put in the crabs to brown, which they will do in three minutes
7. Serve in a round dish with claws in the center
