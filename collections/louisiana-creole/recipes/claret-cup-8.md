## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Good Claret
- 1 Pony of Curacoa
- 1 Orange, Sliced
- A Dash of French Cognac
- ½ Cup of Loaf Sugar
- 1 Large Piece of Ice
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar and lemon juice
2. Add the Claret, Curacoa, Brandy and sliced orange and lemon juice
3. Put to cool
