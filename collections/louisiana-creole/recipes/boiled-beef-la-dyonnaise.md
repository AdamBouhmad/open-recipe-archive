## Ingredients

- 6 Sausages (Chaurice)
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 3 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 2 Eggs
- Bread Crumbs
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- Water
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Make sausage meat of the bouilli, adding the pork sausage, garlic, parsley and thyme.
2. Moisten some bread crumbs in water and dissolve over them two eggs, salt and pepper.
3. Chop the whole and tie it tightly in a cabbage leaf.
4. An hour before serving take out the remaining bouilli and the farci or stuffed cabbage leaf.
5. Let them cool and cut them into slices and roll these in beaten eggs, and then in bread crumbs, and fry in butter.
6. Throw over them a dash of powdered parsley and squeeze over all the juice of a lemon.
