## Ingredients

- 2 Ox Tails
- 2 Onions
- 2 Carrots
- 1 Turnip
- ½ Can of Green Peas
- ¼ Inch of Ham
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Sprigs of Thyme
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Pint of Consommé or Boiling Water
- 1 Glass of Sherry Wine or Water

## Directions

1. Cut the ox tails into pieces of three or four inches in length
2. Chop two onions fine, and put the whole into a saucepan with a tablespoonful of butter
3. Let them brown a little and add two large carrots, cut into dice, and one turnip cut the same way
4. Brown these with the ox tails
5. Add one-half inch of ham, well chopped and let it brown, and then add two sprigs of thyme and one bay leaf and one clove of garlic, chopped very fine
6. Let these ingredients all brown about two minutes over a hot fire
7. Then add one glass of Sherry wine or water
8. Let all brown two minutes longer, and add one pint of consommé or boiling water
9. Season again to taste, and add a half can of green peas
10. Let all boil until the ox tails are tender to the touch, and serve hot
