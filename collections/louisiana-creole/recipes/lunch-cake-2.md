## Ingredients

- % Cups of Sugar
- 3 Cups of Flour
- % Cup of Milk
- 2 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of Peach Extract

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and eggs to a cream, and add the milk.
2. Then add gradually the flour, into which you will have sifted the baking powder.
3. Add one tablespoonful of Peach Extract, and bake in a moderate oven for three-quarters of an hour.
