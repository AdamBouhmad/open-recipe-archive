## Ingredients

- a calf's head or half a beef's head
- a cow-heel
- black pepper
- salt
- thyme
- parsley
- garlic

## Directions

1. Boil a calf's head or half a beef's head with a cow-heel until very tender.
2. When done, pick out all the bones and chop the meat and tendons very fine.
3. Strain the liquor they were boiled in, and set it away to cool.
4. Skim off the fat and pour the jelly over the meat.
5. Season with a teaspoonful of black pepper, salt, and thyme, powdered.
6. Boil all together for a few minutes, and pour into bowls or jelly moulds.
7. Serve with parsley.
8. Add a little garlic if the flavor is liked.
