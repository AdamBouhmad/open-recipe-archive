## Ingredients

- small green sweet oranges
- thin clarified sugar

## Directions

1. Take small, green, sweet oranges, and pierce them several times with a penknife till very soft
2. Let them stand for three days in water, which must be renewed fresh every day
3. Put them in a dish after the third day, pour thin clarified sugar over them
4. Prepare a syrup as directed
5. Add the oranges when it begins to boil
6. Cook till they are tender, and proceed in the usual way
