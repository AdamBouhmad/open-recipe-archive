## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Quinces
- ¾ of a Pound of Sugar

## Directions

1. Take ripe quinces and cut them into small pieces.
2. Steam them in sufficient water to cover them and let them cook till they can be mashed with a wooden spoon.
3. When well-mashed in the water, pour in the sugar, allowing three-fourths of a pound for every pound of fruit.
4. Proceed to arrange in layers as directed above.
5. Add the mixture and cook until it assumes a bright red color.
6. Test by pouring on a saucer just sufficient to see if it will jelly.
7. When it reaches this degree of consistency, proceed to run into jars or glasses.
8. When cold it will be quite solid.
