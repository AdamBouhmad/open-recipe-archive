## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Minced Left-Over Fish
- 1 Tablespoonful of French Mustard Dressing
- 1 Gill of Sauce Piquante
- 2 Ounces of Parmesan Cheese
- 6 Slices of Toast

## Directions

1. Utilize any kind of white-fleshed Fish, Red Fish, Red Snapper, Sheephead or Trout.
2. Prepare a forcemeat and season with the French Mustard and the Piquant Sauce.
3. Spread a layer one-quarter of an inch thick over delicate strips of toast, dredge with grated Parmesan Cheese.
4. Set in the oven, and bake for five minutes and serve hot.
