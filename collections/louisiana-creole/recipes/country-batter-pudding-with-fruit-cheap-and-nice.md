## Ingredients

- one quart of buttermilk
- one egg
- one large teaspoonful of soda
- salt
- flour
- one quart of chopped fruit (pie-plant, berries, strawberries, peaches, etc.)
- sugar
- cream

## Directions

1. To a quart of buttermilk add one egg, a large teaspoonful of soda, a little salt, and flour enough to make a thick batter.
2. Pour it over a quart of chopped fruit, such as pie-plant, berries, strawberries, peaches, etc.
3. Beat it a little.
4. Tie it tightly in a bag, drop it in a kettle of hot water, and let it boil two hours.
5. Alternatively, this pudding may be poured into a cake pan and baked, if not convenient to boil it. Put in plenty of fruit.
6. Serve with sugar and cream.
