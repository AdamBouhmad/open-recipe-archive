## Ingredients

- one quart of molasses
- three gallons of rain water
- one pint of sharp yeast

## Directions

1. Mix a quart of molasses in three gallons of rain water
2. Add to this, one pint of sharp yeast
3. Let it ferment and stand four weeks; you will then have good vinegar
