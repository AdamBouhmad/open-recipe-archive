## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Dried Red Beans
- 1 Carrot
- 1 Onion
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1/4 Pound of Ham or Lean Salt Pork
- 1/2 Pint of Cream or Milk
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the beans by washing and soaking them over night, or at least five or six hours, in fresh, cold water.
2. Drain off this water and put the beans in a pot of cold water.
3. Add the ham or salt pork, and the herbs and onion and carrot, minced fine.
4. Let the water heat slowly and boil the beans until they will mash very easily under pressure.
5. Remove the beans from the fire and take out the bits of ham.
6. Press the beans through a colander.
7. Add a tablespoonful of butter as you return them to the pot in which they have been boiled, and a half pint of cream or milk, or sufficient according to quantity to make the puree of the consistency of thick starch or mashed potatoes.
8. Season with salt and pepper, and serve.
