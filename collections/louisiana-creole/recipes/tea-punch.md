## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Strong Tea
- French Brandy or Wine or Rum to Taste
- 6 Lemons
- ½ Pounds of Sugar

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from the lemons and grate the zest
2. Add this to one quart of boiling tea, and add the sugar
3. Dissolve well
4. Let it boil up three times
5. Take off the fire, and add rum, brandy or wine to taste
