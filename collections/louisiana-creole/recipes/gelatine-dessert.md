## Ingredients

- one box of Cox's gelatine
- one quart of boiling water
- one teaspoonful of tartaric acid
- four cups of sugar
- beaten whites of three eggs
- one tablespoonful of good extract of lemon

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the gelatine and stir until dissolved
2. Add tartaric acid and sugar, let dissolve and bring to a boil
3. While boiling, stir in the beaten whites of three eggs
4. Let this boil up once again and take it off the fire
5. When nearly cool, add a tablespoonful of good extract of lemon
6. Strain the mixture into moulds or cups
7. Set it in a cool place or on ice to become firm
