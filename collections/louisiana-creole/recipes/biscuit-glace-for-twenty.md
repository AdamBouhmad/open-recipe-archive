## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- pulverized sugar
- cream
- vanilla extract
- egg whites
- strawberry sherbet
- ice
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix ten yolks of eggs with one and a half pounds pulverized sugar.
2. Add half a gallon of cream and vanilla extract.
3. Beat white of eggs well if the cream is too light.
4. Place inside box within the outer box, and stuff with ice and salt.
5. Let it freeze.
6. When frozen, place red sherbet on top of biscuit to give pretty appearance.
7. Freeze in a square box and cut in small pieces.
