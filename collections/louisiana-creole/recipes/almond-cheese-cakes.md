## Ingredients

- 4 ounces of almonds
- 1 spoonful of rose water
- 4 ounces of sugar
- 1 spoonful of cream
- whites of 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Blanch and pound 4 ounces of almonds with a spoonful of rose water
2. add 4 ounces of sugar pounded, a spoonful of cream, the whites of 2 eggs well beaten
3. mix all as quick as possible, and bake it in a warm oven 20 minutes
