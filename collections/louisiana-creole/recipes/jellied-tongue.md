## Ingredients

- 1 beef tongue
- 2 calf's feet
- 4 pints of strong consomme
- 1 glass of sherry wine
- Spices
- 2 onions
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Parboil the tongue and two calf's feet.
2. Take out of the hot water and skin and clean the tongue well, and take the bones out of the calf's feet.
3. Mince two onions very fine, and fry them in a tablespoonful of butter.
4. Let them brown, and lay on these the well-seasoned tongue and calf's feet.
5. Let them simmer ten minutes, and then add one pint of consomme, and five minutes after, one glass of white wine.
6. Let these smother, keeping well covered, for an hour and a half.
7. Take the tongue out, and let the calf's feet cook and reduce a half hour longer.
8. After this add the tongue for two minutes longer.
9. Put all into a bowl or dish, and let it cool.
