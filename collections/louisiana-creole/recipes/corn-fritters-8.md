## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Corn Meal
- 1 Pint of Boiling Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 3 Eggs
- 1/2 Cup of Sugar
- Flavoring to Taste
- 1/2 Spoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the cornmeal together, and add the melted butter and the milk, stirring and beating well.
2. Add the salt and any flavoring extract preferred.
3. Lastly, add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
4. Fry in boiling lard, dropping in the lard by spoonfuls.
