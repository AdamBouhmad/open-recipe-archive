## Ingredients

- one pound best beef
- 1 pint cold water

## Directions

1. Take off all the fat and cut the lean into small pieces
2. Pour over it 1 pint of cold water and let it stand 3 or 4 hours
3. Put it over a brisk fire and let it boil 20 minutes
