## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of New Orleans Molasses
- 3/4 Cup of Sugar
- The Juice of 2 Lemons
- 3 Eggs
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 3/4 Teaspoonful Each of Nutmeg and Cinnamon
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter

## Directions

1. Blend the lemon juice and molasses well, and gradually beat in the flour, which you will have moistened with a little water
2. Add the spices and the butter, melted, and the yolks of the eggs, beaten very light with the sugar
3. Add the whites, beaten to a stiff froth
4. Mix well
5. Line the pie pans with a pie crust, bake, fill with the mixture, and bake again for half an hour
