## Ingredients

- 6 large, smooth potatoes
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- whites of 4 eggs
- salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the potatoes very clean and bake them
2. Cut lengthwise, scrape out the insides carefully, so as not to break the skins
3. Mash smoothly, adding the boiling milk, butter, seasoning and half the beaten whites of the eggs
4. Put into potato skins, place in a pan
5. Cover with remainder of whites, and bake a light brown
