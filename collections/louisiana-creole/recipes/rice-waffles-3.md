## Ingredients

- 2 cups rice
- 2 eggs
- 1 spoonful butter
- ½ pint milk

## Directions

1. Boil 2 cups of rice till quite soft
2. Make it into a thick batter with 2 eggs, 1 spoonful of butter, ½ pint of milk
3. Beat it till quite light
4. Bake in a waffle iron
