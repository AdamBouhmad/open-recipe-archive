## Ingredients

- cold potatoes sliced
- chopped eschalots
- pepper
- salt
- mustard
- oil
- tomato catsup
- vinegar
- sprigs of parsley

## Directions

1. Slice cold potatoes, add to them chopped eschalots, and season with pepper, salt, mustard, oil, tomato catsup and vinegar
2. Garnish with sprigs of parsley
