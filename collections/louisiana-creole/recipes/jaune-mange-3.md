## Ingredients

- an ounce of isinglass
- water
- the juice of two large oranges
- a gill of white wine
- the yolks of four eggs

## Directions

1. Break up and boil an ounce of isinglass in rather more than half a pint of water until it is melted
2. strain it
3. then add the juice of two large oranges, a gill of white wine, and the yolks of four eggs beaten and strained
4. sweeten to taste
5. stir it over a gentle fire till it boils up
6. dip a mould into cold water and pour the preparation into it
