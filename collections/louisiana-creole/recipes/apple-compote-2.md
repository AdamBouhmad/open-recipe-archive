## Ingredients

- three-quarters of a pound of sugar
- one cup of water
- six nice sour apples

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of three-quarters of a pound of sugar and a cup of water and let it boil
2. While you are paring and taking out the cores of six nice sour apples, keep the syrup boiling
3. Throw the apples into the syrup and let them boil for half an hour, or until transparent
4. Pour into a glass or china dish and serve for a lunch or tea
5. They are nice when served warm
