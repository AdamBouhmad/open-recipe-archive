## Ingredients

- Six large, nice apples
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 6 eggs
- juice and grated rind of 1 lemon
- whites of 3 eggs
- 3 tablespoonfuls of sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Stew 6 large, nice apples, and mash through a sieve
2. Mix in 1 lb. of butter and 1 lb. of sugar
3. Add 6 eggs beaten very light
4. Add the juice and grated rind of 1 lemon
5. Bake the pudding
6. Save out the whites of 3 eggs, beat them up with 3 tablespoonfuls of sifted sugar to a stiff froth
7. After the pudding is baked and nearly cold, lay the sugar and egg over the top
8. Set it in a cool oven for a few minutes, until it is crusted, but not browned
