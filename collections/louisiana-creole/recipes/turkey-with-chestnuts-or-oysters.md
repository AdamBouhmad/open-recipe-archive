## Ingredients

- 1 Turkey
- Chestnut or Oyster Dressing

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey in the manner indicated in Roast Turkey.
2. Stuff according to taste with either a Chestnut or Oyster Dressing.
3. Cook as in recipe for Roast Turkey.
