## Ingredients

- 1.5 pints of oysters
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 pint of water
- 3 soft crackers
- pepper
- mace
- butter the size of an egg
- 1 small onion
- celery

## Directions

1. Strain the liquor from 1.5 pints of oysters into a saucepan with 1 pint of milk and 1 pint of water
2. Add 3 soft crackers pounded fine, a little pepper, mace, butter the size of an egg, small onion, and celery chopped fine if you have it
3. Boil all together a few minutes
4. Add the oysters and let it boil up once
5. Serve immediately
