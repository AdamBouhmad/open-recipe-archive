## Ingredients

- a cocoanut
- one cup of sugar
- the white of an egg

## Directions

1. Grate a cocoanut, place it in a skillet over the fire, and stir until it is as dry as flour
2. Beat one cup of sugar and the white of an egg to a froth
3. Mix well, and make into small cakes
4. put them on buttered paper and bake
5. The oven should not be very hot
