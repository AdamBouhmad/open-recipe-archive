## Ingredients

- 1/2 ounce of Carrigean Moss
- 1 quart of new milk
- essence of bitter almonds
- 1 glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Take 1/2 ounce of Carrigean Moss (the white)
2. Put it into 1 quart of new milk
3. Reduce it, by simmering, to 1 pint
4. Flavor it with a few drops of essence of bitter almonds
5. Add 1 glass of white wine
6. Sweeten it to the taste
7. Pour it into a mould, until cold and firm
8. Serve in a glass dish
