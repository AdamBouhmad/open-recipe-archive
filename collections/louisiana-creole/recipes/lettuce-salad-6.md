## Ingredients

- 3 Heads of Lettuce
- French Dressing
- 2 Eggs

## Directions

1. Take fresh, crisp lettuce of sufficient quantity for the number to be served, three young heads being enough for six.
2. Dip in cold water examining each leaf, and pick over carefully, and select the fresh crisp leaves.
3. Place all these in a salad bowl, and garnish nicely with sliced hard-boiled egg.
4. Never dress the lettuce before bringing to the table.
5. Bring to the table, and let the sauce, always a plain French dressing or Creole dressing preferred, be made at the table.
