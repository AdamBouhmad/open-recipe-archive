## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 oysters to each person
- 3 slices of fat bacon
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- Salt and pepper
- 6 or 8 squares of buttered toast
- Olives
- Pickles
- Sliced lemon

## Directions

1. Drain the oysters well and dry with a clean towel.
2. Sprinkle them with pepper and salt, and roll in a little flour.
3. Place the bacon, cut into thin slices, in a frying pan, and let all the fat fry out.
4. Remove the bacon from the pan and place in the oysters, covering the bottom.
5. As they turn a golden brown on one side, turn over on the other.
6. Serve on squares of buttered toast, with garnishes of olives, pickles and sliced lemon.
