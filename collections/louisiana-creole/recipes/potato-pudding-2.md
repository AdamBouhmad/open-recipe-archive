## Ingredients

- 1½ lbs. well mashed potatoes
- 1 lb. butter
- 6 eggs
- ½ lb. sugar rolled fine
- 1 glass of brandy
- nutmeg, mace, or lemon

## Directions

1. While potatoes are warm, put in butter
2. Beat 6 eggs with ½ lb. of sugar rolled fine
3. Mix all well together
4. Put in 1 glass of brandy, with nutmeg, mace, or lemon
5. Bake it in a nice paste
