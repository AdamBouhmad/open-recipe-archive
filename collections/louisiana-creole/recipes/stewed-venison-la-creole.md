## Ingredients

- Venison Steaks, or Rougher Part of the Deer
- 2 Onions
- 1 Square Inch of Ham
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Clove of Garlic, Chopped Very Fine
- 1 Herb Bouquet, Chopped Fine
- 1 Glass of Claret
- 1 Cup of Water
- 1 Can of Mushrooms

## Directions

1. Cut the Venison into two-inch square pieces, and rub well with salt and pepper.
2. Chop two onions very fine, and put them in a stewpan with a tablespoonful of melted butter.
3. Let them brown slightly.
4. Add the Venison meat and let it brown slightly.
5. Add one tablespoonful of flour, and let this brown a little.
6. Chop the square inch of ham very fine, mincing it, and add.
7. Add the clove of garlic, and two sprigs each of thyme and parsley and a bay leaf, minced fine.
8. Let this brown nicely, and pour over one glass of good Claret.
9. Let this cook for ten minutes, stirring it constantly, so that it will not burn.
10. Add one cup of boiling water.
11. Stir well, season again to taste, and let it boil for thirty minutes.
12. If desired, add a can of mushrooms immediately after adding the water.
13. Serve very hot.
