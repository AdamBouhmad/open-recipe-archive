## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Grated Parmesan or Gruyere Cheese
- 1 Cup Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- Yolk of an Egg
- Salt and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Blend the flour and cheese together and add at the same time the salt and Cayenne.
2. Moisten with a well-beaten egg and one tablespoonful of melted butter, and work all gently into a paste.
3. Roll out on a biscuit board into thin strips of not more than an eighth of an inch in thickness, and cut into strips of four inches in length and one-eighth of an inch in width.
4. Place on buttered sheets of paper and bake in a very hot oven until a light brown.
5. It is very pretty to make little rings of some of the strips and pass the others through them in little bundles like gathered bound bits of straw.
