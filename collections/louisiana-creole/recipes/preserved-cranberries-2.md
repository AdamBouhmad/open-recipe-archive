## Ingredients

- cranberries
- loaf sugar (1 lb. per lb. of cranberries)
- water (1/2 pint per lb. of sugar)

## Directions

1. Wash the fruit and weigh it
2. To each lb. of cranberries allow 1 lb. of loaf sugar, dissolved, 1/2 pint of water to 1 lb. of sugar
3. Set it on the fire in a preserving kettle, boiling it 10 minutes
4. Put in the fruit and boil slowly till it is soft and a ruby color
5. Put away in glass jars, with brandied paper on the top
6. Exclude the air with kid tightly sealed over the jars
