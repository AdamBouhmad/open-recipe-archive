## Ingredients

- flour
- water
- onion
- thyme
- parsley
- cloves
- allspice
- lemon
- eggs

## Directions

1. Fry slightly, putting in a little flour before it becomes too brown, as the sauce must be a pale yellow
2. Add water to the sauce
3. Put in a little onion, thyme and parsley, chopped very fine
4. Add 2 or 3 cloves and allspice
5. Let the whole simmer till well done
6. Add the juice of a lemon, and a little of the peel
7. Stir in the yolks of 2 eggs well beaten
