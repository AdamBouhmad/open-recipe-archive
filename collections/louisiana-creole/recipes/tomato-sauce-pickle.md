## Ingredients

- one gallon of tomatoes
- one gallon of vinegar
- salt
- onions
- cloves
- pepper
- ginger
- mustard
- sugar

## Directions

1. Slice the tomatoes (green ones are firmest), and sprinkle salt between each layer
2. Let them remain thus for twelve hours, then rinse them, and put them to drain on a sieve
3. Put your vinegar to boil with a dozen onions cut up in it, season high with cloves, pepper and ginger
4. When this boils throw in your tomatoes and let them boil five minutes
5. Finish by stirring in one-quarter of a pound of mustard and a pound of sugar
6. Add a quart of vinegar and bottle it
