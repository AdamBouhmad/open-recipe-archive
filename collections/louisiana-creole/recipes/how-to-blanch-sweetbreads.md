## Ingredients

- 3 pairs of sweetbreads
- salt

## Directions

1. Select three fine pairs of sweetbreads and clean and trim nicely.
2. Soak them for at least two and a half hours in cold fresh water, pouring off the water from time to time till three separate waters have been used.
3. About three-quarters of an hour may be allowed for the first two waters.
4. Add a pinch of salt to each water.
5. After soaking for the time specified, drain the sweetbreads and place them in a saucepan of cold water and set on the fire.
6. Add a half teaspoonful of salt and let them blanch till they come to a boil.
7. Then drain and set them in cold water to freshen.
8. Drain thoroughly, press them into shape and lay on a napkin in a cool place.
9. The sweetbreads should be pressed down gently with a pound weight, in order to flatten well.
