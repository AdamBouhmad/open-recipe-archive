## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Green Peas
- 3 Small Carrots
- 1-8 of a Head of Green and White Cabbage
- A Tablespoonful of Butter
- ½ Pint of Consomme of Veal or Chicken
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Shell the peas; take three small carrots, one-eighth of a head of cabbage, and one-quarter head of lettuce, and cut into small dice-shaped pieces.
2. Put a tablespoonful of butter in a saucepan and let the dice-shaped vegetables smother for about fifteen minutes over a slow fire without browning.
3. Add the green peas and the consomme, and let all cook for a half hour, stirring frequently to prevent burning.
4. Season to taste with salt and pepper, and use a sprinkling of chopped parsley as a garnish.
