## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of grated sponge cake
- whites of 8 eggs
- yolks of 2 eggs
- 1/2 lb. of butter

## Directions

1. To 1 lb. of grated sponge cake, add the whites of 8 eggs and the yolks of 2, 1/2 lb. of butter
2. beat very light and boil it for 2 hours in a mould
