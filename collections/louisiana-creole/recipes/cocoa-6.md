## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Cocoa

## Directions

1. Set the milk to boil in a farina boiler, and moisten the Cocoa with a little milk
2. Pour it into the milk just as it begins to simmer, stirring all the while
3. Let it boil up once
4. Serve hot, with or without Whipped Cream
