## Ingredients

- 1/2 pint milk
- 6 eggs
- 6 ounces sugar
- vanilla or other essence
- 1 pint whipped cream

## Directions

1. Beat eggs and sugar till very light, with milk and flavor with vanilla or any other essence
2. Let it simmer on the fire without boiling for 16 minutes
3. When it becomes tepid, mix in 1 pint of whipped cream
4. Pour it into a mould, and surround it with ice
5. Whip up some cream and pour it into the dish after the form is turned out
