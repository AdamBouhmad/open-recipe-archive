## Ingredients

- free-stone peaches
- salt and water
- garlic
- horse radish
- mustard seed
- cloves
- ginger root
- vinegar
- cinnamon
- fresh made mustard
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Take free-stone peaches of the finest kind, let them be full grown, but not quite ripe
2. Lay them in salt and water for 2 days, covered with a board to keep them down
3. Wipe them dry
4. Cut them open, and extract the stones
5. Prepare the stuffing to the taste: minced garlic, scraped horse radish, bruised mustard seed, and cloves, a little ginger root soaked in water to soften it, and then sliced
6. Fill the peaches with the mixture
7. Tie them round, to secure the stuffing
8. Season the vinegar with broken cinnamon, fresh made mustard, a little ginger and nutmeg
9. Mix this all well together, and fill up the jar with the pickle
