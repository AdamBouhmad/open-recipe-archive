## Ingredients

- 1 Quart Young Okra
- 1 Tablespoonful Vinegar
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- A pint of water
- A teaspoonful of salt
- Tarragon vinegar

## Directions

1. Wash the okra well in cold water, and put in a porcelain-lined or agate saucepan
2. Add a pint of water and a teaspoonful of salt
3. Cover the pot, and let the okra simmer for about half an hour
4. Take from the pot, season with salt and pepper to taste
5. Pour over a tablespoonful of Tarragon vinegar and set to cool
6. Serve as a salad, and with all meats, such as daube, roast, etc.
