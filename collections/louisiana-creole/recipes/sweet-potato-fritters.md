## Ingredients

- 4 Boiled Sweet Potatoes
- 1 Glass of Milk, White Wine or Brandy
- 1 Teaspoonful of Sugar
- Juice of Lemon
- Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the boiled sweet potatoes into slender round slices, and steep them for a half hour in a little White Wine or brandy or milk
2. Make a light batter, to which you will have added a tablespoonful of sugar
3. Add the juice of a lemon to the batter
4. Take each slice at a time, dip it in the batter, and let it drop from the spoon into the boiling lard
5. When fried to a nice golden brown place on a piece of brown paper, and drain in the oven
6. Place in the dish in which they are to be served
7. Sprinkle with powdered white sugar, and serve hot
