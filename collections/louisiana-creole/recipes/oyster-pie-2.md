## Ingredients

- rich puff paste
- oysters
- eggs
- butter
- mace
- salt and pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. Put a rich puff paste in the dish
2. Lay oysters over the bottom of the dish
3. Boil some eggs hard, and put 2 or 3 pieces with butter, mace, salt and pepper
4. Shake in a little flour
5. Add more oysters and eggs, till the dish is full
6. Cover with a crust and bake
