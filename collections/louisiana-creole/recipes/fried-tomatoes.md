## Ingredients

- 1 dozen ripe tomatoes
- fresh butter
- 2 or 3 sliced green peppers
- salt
- 1 or 2 onions

## Directions

1. Peel 1 dozen ripe tomatoes, and fry them in a little fresh butter, together with 2 or 3 sliced green peppers
2. Sprinkle on them a little salt, then add 1 or 2 onions sliced, and let the whole cook thoroughly
