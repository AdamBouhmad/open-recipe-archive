## Ingredients

- The Bouilli
- 1 Teaspoonful of Powdered Saffron
- 2 Cayenne Pepper Pods
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- ¾ Cup of Bouillon
- Lettuce Leaves
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Crush the pods of two Cayenne peppers and a teaspoonful of powdered saffron and heat and brown in butter.
2. Moisten with a little bouillon.
3. Boil the sauce down, and when nearly ready to serve, thicken with a little butter.
4. Serve in a gravy dish with the Bouilli, which has been nicely and tastefully garnished with lettuce leaves on a parsley bed.
