## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Juice of 12 Oranges
- The Grated Rind of 2 Oranges, Rubbed on Sugar

## Directions

1. Boil the milk or cream in a farina boiler
2. Take off the fire, and while hot pour this over the yolks of the eggs, which have been beaten to a thick cream, with the sugar; add orange juice and rind of the oranges
3. Stir till all begins to thicken
4. Then add the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth
5. Set to cool
6. Turn into the freezer, and proceed in the manner above indicated
