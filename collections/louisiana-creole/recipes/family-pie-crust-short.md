## Ingredients

- one pound of sifted flour
- half a pound of sweet lard or beef drippings
- a dessertspoonful of salt
- cold water
- a bit of volatile salts, the size of a small nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put a pound of sifted flour into a bowl, work into it half a pound of sweet lard or beef drippings, with a dessertspoonful of salt
2. When it is thoroughly mixed put to it enough cold water to bind together
3. Flour the paste slab, or table, and rolling pin
4. Take a part of the paste and roll it to less than a quarter of an inch in thickness
5. A bit of volatile salts, the size of a small nutmeg, dissolved in a little hot water and put to the paste, will make it more light and delicate
