## Ingredients

- berries
- boiling water
- refined sugar

## Directions

1. Bruise your berries and measure them
2. To every gallon, add a quart of boiling water
3. Let this stand twenty-four hours, stirring it three or four times during this time
4. The third day strain off the juice
5. To every gallon of this strained liquor, put two pounds of refined sugar
6. Cork it tight and let it stand until cool weather
