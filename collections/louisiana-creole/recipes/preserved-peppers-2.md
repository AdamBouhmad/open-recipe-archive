## Ingredients

- small round peppers (green)
- vine leaves
- alum (a little)
- lemon or ginger
- water (1 pint per 1 1/2 lbs. of sugar)
- sugar (1 1/2 lbs.)
- white of egg (beaten to stiff froth)

## Directions

1. Take the small round peppers while they are green
2. Extract the seeds and cores with a sharp penknife
3. Put the outsides into a kettle with vine leaves and a little alum to give them firmness, and assist in keeping them green
4. Flavor with lemon or ginger
5. Allow 1 pint of water to 1 1/2 lbs. of sugar
6. Mix with it some white of egg beaten to a stiff froth
7. Boil and skim it, and when the scum ceases to rise, put in the fruit
