## Ingredients

- Five eggs
- 2 cupfuls of sugar
- a dinner plate full of peaches, peeled and cut up fine
- 3 or 4 tablespoonfuls of milk
- flour
- a pinch of salt
- a dessertspoon of butter

## Directions

1. Beat 5 eggs with 2 cupfuls of sugar, well mixed together
2. Add a dinner plate full of peaches, peeled and cut up fine
3. Add 3 or 4 tablespoonfuls of milk, and as much flour
4. Add a pinch of salt and a dessertspoon of butter
5. Bake in a slow oven
6. Eaten hot or cold
