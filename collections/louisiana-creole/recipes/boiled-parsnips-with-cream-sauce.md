## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 Parsnips
- Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Proceed exactly as above in peeling and boiling the parsnips.
2. When done, drain and put into a heated dish, and serve with a Cream Sauce poured over them.
3. Serve the parsnips with boiled salt or fresh fish or boiled corn beef.
