## Ingredients

- butter
- eggs
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put four ounces of butter into an omelet pan over the fire
2. as it begins to sputter, break the eggs into it without disturbing the yolks
3. season with pepper and salt
4. fry the eggs carefully and remove them on to the dish in which they are to be served
5. Put two ounces more butter in the pan
6. fry it of a brown color
7. put to the butter two tablespoonfuls of vinegar
8. pour it over the eggs and serve
