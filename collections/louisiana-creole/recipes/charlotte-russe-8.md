## Ingredients

- Three ounces of isinglass
- 1 quart of water
- vanilla
- yolks of 3 eggs
- white of 1 egg
- 3 pints of cream
- sponge cake
- ice

## Directions

1. Put isinglass in 1 quart of water with vanilla sufficient to flavor it
2. Boil it down to a pint, or sufficient to make a strong jelly
3. When done, strain through a sieve
4. Take the yolks of 3 eggs and the white of 1 and beat them well
5. Stir them in the jelly while hot
6. Sweeten to your taste and put it to cool
7. Take 3 pints of cream and beat it to a high froth and let it drain on a sieve
8. When the jelly is cold, stir in the froth
9. Butter the moulds and have a nice sponge cake ready to cut
10. Lay it round so as to fit close and cut the same in narrow pieces
11. Lay in the bottom so as not to move them
12. Pour in the froth and put it on the ice to cool for 3 hours
13. Have a cloth dipped in hot water and wipe over the mould
14. Turn the mould bottom upwards on the dish and take it off
15. Be careful not to make it too warm, or the shape will be spoiled
