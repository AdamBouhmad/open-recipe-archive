## Ingredients

- 8 Ripe Greengages
- 3 Ounces of Apple Marmalade
- ½ Pound of Feuillage Paste

## Directions

1. Prepare a half pound of Feuillage Paste and line six tart molds with it
2. Spread nicely on the bottom of each three ounces of Apple Marmalade
3. Take the greengages, wipe well and remove the stones
4. Cut into quartered pieces and lay nicely over the marmalade
5. Sprinkle two ounces of powdered sugar evenly over them
6. Bake in a moderate oven for twenty-five minutes
7. Remove and let cool
8. Spread apple marmalade nicely over the top and send to the table
