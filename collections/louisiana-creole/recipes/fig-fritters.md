## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Fresh Figs
- 3/4 Cup of Sugar
- Creole Fritter Batter

## Directions

1. Pare the figs, taking off every particle of the skin.
2. Then cut up nicely, sprinkle with sugar and let them stand for half an hour.
3. Then add the juice to fritter batter and proceed as in Apple Fritters.
