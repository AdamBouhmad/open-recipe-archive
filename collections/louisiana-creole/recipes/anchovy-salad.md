## Ingredients

- anchovies
- crisp lettuce
- chopped watercress
- chopped whites of eggs
- sour French Dressing

## Directions

1. Have an oval dish and arrange the anchovies drained of oil crosswise on a bed of crisp lettuce or of chopped watercress
2. Surround with a border of chopped whites of eggs and a similar border of chopped watercresses
3. Pour over all a sour French Dressing
