## Ingredients

- 1 Cupful of White Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Water
- 3 Quarts of Nicely-Popped Corn

## Directions

1. Put the butter, water and sugar into a boiler
2. Boil till it begins to almost run to candy
3. Throw in the nicely popped corn
4. Stir vigorously over the fire until the sugar is evenly distributed over the corn
5. Take the kettle from the fire and stir until it cools a little
6. If you wish, pile it into mounds and roll into balls while still hot enough for the grains to adhere
