## Ingredients

- oysters
- their own liquor
- pepper
- salt
- chopped parsley
- butter
- grated bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Lay the oysters in a shallow pan or dish with a little of their own liquor
2. Some pepper, salt, chopped parsley, butter, and grated bread crumbs
3. Have a layer of bread crumbs on the top of the pan
4. And set it in the oven to bake a light brown
5. They should be served hot with tomato or walnut catsup poured over them
