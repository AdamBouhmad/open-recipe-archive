## Ingredients

- 1/4 Cup of Butter
- 2 1/4 Cups Powdered Sugar
- 1/4 Cup of Boiling Water
- 2 Wineglasses of White Wine
- 1 Tablespoonful Grated Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream, and add the boiling water
2. Set the vessel containing the mixture into a bain-marie or saucepan of boiling water, and stir in gradually the white wine and the grated nutmeg
3. Let the sauce get hot, but do not let it boil
4. When it forms a thick cream take off, and serve either hot or cold
5. Stir all the time it is on the fire
6. Serve with any pudding.
