## Ingredients

- 1 lb. loaf of stale bread
- butter
- raisins or almonds
- currants
- sugar
- nutmeg
- lemon peel
- 4 eggs
- ½ pint of sweet cream or milk
- small glass of good brandy
- glass of lemon juice
- white wine

## Directions

1. Butter and slice very thin 1 lb. loaf of stale bread
2. Butter very well a large sized tin pudding dish or pan
3. Stick raisins or almonds in the bottom and sides of the pan gracefully
4. Put a layer of bread
5. Lay over it a layer of currants, raisins, sugar, and chopped almonds, nutmeg and grated lemon peel
6. Lay on a layer of bread, raisins, currants, etc., in alternate layers, until all is in the pan
7. Pour over it batter made of 4 eggs well beaten, ½ pint of sweet cream or milk, a small glass of good brandy, glass of lemon juice, butter and a little more sugar
8. Tie a strong double paper over the pan and place it in a boiling bottle of water, being careful that the water does not reach the top of the pan
9. Let it boil for 1½ hours
10. Turn it out on a deep dessert plate or dish
11. Pour over it boiled custard, seasoned with white wine
12. Stick a few whole blanched almonds and serve it up for table
