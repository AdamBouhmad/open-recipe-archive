## Ingredients

- three cups of fine sugar
- two cups of butter
- five cups of sifted flour
- half a pound of chopped raisins
- half a pound of dried currants
- brandy, and nutmeg, or extract of nutmeg, or lemon

## Directions

1. Combine three cups of fine sugar, two cups of butter, five cups of sifted flour, half a pound of chopped raisins, and half a pound of dried currants
2. flavor with brandy, and nutmeg, or extract of nutmeg, or lemon
3. Put the same flavor in the frosting, if the cake is to be used for special occasions
