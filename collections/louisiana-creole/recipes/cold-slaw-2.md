## Ingredients

- cabbage
- raw egg
- condensed milk
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Shave cabbage into shreds
2. Mix 1 well beaten raw egg in 1 teacup condensed milk, 1 cup vinegar
3. Boil till it thickens
4. Pour this over the cabbage
5. Sprinkle with salt
