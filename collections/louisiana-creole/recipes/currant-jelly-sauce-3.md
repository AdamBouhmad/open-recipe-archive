## Ingredients

- 1/2 Tumbler of Currant Jelly
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Gill Water
- 1 Gill of Port or Madeira Wine

## Directions

1. Melt the butter, and add the jelly, blending well, and then add the gill of wine and water.
2. Add a little salt and sugar to taste.
3. The sauce is much finer when made of wine without water, but this is a question of taste.
4. If the wine only is used, double the proportions, or according to taste.
5. This sauce is served with Venison and other game.
