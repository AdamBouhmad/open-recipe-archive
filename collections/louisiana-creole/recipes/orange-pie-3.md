## Ingredients

- juice and sliced pulp of two large oranges
- grated yellow rind of one orange
- yolks of three eggs
- a cupful of sugar
- whites of three eggs
- a cup of milk
- puff paste

## Directions

1. To the juice and sliced pulp of two large oranges, add the grated yellow rind of one orange
2. Beat the yolks of three eggs with a cupful of sugar
3. Beat the whites to a high froth and add to them a cup of milk
4. Mix all the above together
5. Have ready a nice puff paste, and bake the mixture in it
