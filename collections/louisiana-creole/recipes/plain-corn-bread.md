## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of the Finest Indian Meal
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- A Heaping Tablespoonful of Lard or Butter
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Pour just enough boiling water over the meal to scald it through and through, without making a dough or batter.
2. Stir it well, and let it grow tepid.
3. Add a melted teaspoonful of lard or butter.
4. Mix this well, and add water, and beat till the batter is very smooth.
5. Dissolve one teaspoonful of soda in a little boiling water, and add to the mixture, and grease a baking pan.
6. Turn the mixture in and bake in a quick oven for about thirty-five minutes, till a golden brown.
7. Take the bread from the pan whole, and place on a hot platter, and serve, cutting it at the table into slices about two inches long and three wide, and serving with butter.
