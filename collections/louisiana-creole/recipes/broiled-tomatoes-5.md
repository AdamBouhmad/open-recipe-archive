## Ingredients

- 6 Tomatoes
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Take six tomatoes and cut them in halves on the cross.
2. Do not peel.
3. Put them on the broiler and broil on a slow fire till tender.
4. Turn the broiler from one side to the other often, to prevent burning.
5. In about ten minutes they will be done.
6. Put on a hot dish; put a little melted butter, a little chopped parsley and the juice of a lemon on each, and serve hot.
