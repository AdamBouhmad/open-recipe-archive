## Ingredients

- a quart of nice yellow peaches
- sweet cream
- a quart of milk
- half a pound of sugar
- yolks of four eggs

## Directions

1. Peel and stone a quart of nice yellow peaches
2. Put them in a bowl, sweeten them well, and chop very fine
3. If you have sweet cream, put to the fruit a quart of it; if you have not, take a quart of milk, sweeten it with half a pound of sugar, let it boil, and when boiling, pour it on to the beaten yolks of four eggs
4. When this custard cools, you may add the chopped peaches, which should be well sweetened
5. Pour all in the freezer and set it where it can be frozen
