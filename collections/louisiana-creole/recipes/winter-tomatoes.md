## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- red pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and cut all of the hard part of the tomatoes
2. Add a little salt and red pepper
3. Let them stew until thick enough to bottle
4. Put all the bottles in an oven with straw placed around them to prevent their breaking
5. Fill the oven with lukewarm water
6. Let it boil 10 minutes
7. Cork the bottles tightly, whilst in the oven, so as to make them air tight
8. Rosin them also
