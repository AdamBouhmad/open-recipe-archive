## Ingredients

- 3 Bananas
- Fritter Batter a la Creole
- Sugar
- Sufficient Cognac or Rum to saturate

## Directions

1. Slice the banana and sprinkle with sugar, and cover with sufficient Cognac or Rum to saturate well.
2. Set them aside for half an hour, then drain, and proceed as in Apple Fritters.
