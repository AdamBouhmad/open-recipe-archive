## Ingredients

- chocolate
- cold milk
- hot milk or milk and water

## Directions

1. Scrape or grate the chocolate
2. Take a heaping tablespoonful for each cup to be served
3. Allow half a pint of milk or milk and water to each heaping spoonful of chocolate
4. Make the milk hot, rub the chocolate to a smooth paste with the cold milk
5. Stir it in the boiling milk
6. Let it boil up once and cover it
7. Set it back in a place where it will keep warm
8. Serve with toasted biscuit or rolls
9. Sweeten the chocolate unless you use the prepared chocolate
