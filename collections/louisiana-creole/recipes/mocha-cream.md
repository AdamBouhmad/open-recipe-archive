## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of the Best Mocha Coffee
- The Zest of 1 Orange
- 7 Pints of Brandy
- 4 Pounds of Sugar
- 3 Pints of Water

## Directions

1. Roast the coffee to a light brown without the least trace of burning.
2. Then grind it quickly, while it is hot, and infuse immediately in seven pints of good brandy.
3. Add the zest of the orange, cut very fine, and let it remain thus for six days.
4. Then place in a vessel in a bain-marie, and distill till reduced to one-half.
5. Dissolve the sugar and water over a slow fire, then let it cool, and mix it with the coffee mixture.
6. Filter it, bottle and preserve in a cool, shady place.
