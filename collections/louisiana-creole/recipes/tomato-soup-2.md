## Ingredients

- 1 quart milk
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- 1 dozen fine tomatoes

## Directions

1. Boil 1 quart milk
2. Thicken with 1 tablespoonful flour
3. Season with salt, pepper and a little butter
4. Stew 1 dozen fine tomatoes
5. Drain partially, strain through cullender
6. Pour the boiling milk to it
7. Boil up once, and serve
