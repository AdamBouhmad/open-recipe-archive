## Ingredients

- 3/4 of a Pound of Cold Cooled Veal
- 1 Small Onion
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 4 Sprigs of Parsley
- 1 Large Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Onion
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- Cayenne and Pepper to Taste
- Bread
- 2 Eggs
- Powdered Bread Crumbs
- Boiling Lard
- Fried Parsley

## Directions

1. Remove all the tough fibers and nerves
2. Hash the veal well and season with the minced vegetables and sweet herbs, mixing all thoroughly
3. Take a cup of the soft of the bread, wet it and squeeze, and soak in milk, in which you have beaten two eggs
4. Mix all this with the meat very thoroughly and season to taste
5. Form the meat into cylindrical shapes and brush with a little butter
6. Roll in a beaten egg and roll again in powdered bread crumbs
7. Fry in boiling lard and serve hot on a plate garnished with fried parsley
