## Ingredients

- 3/4 Peck of Fresh Young Spinach
- 1 Cupful of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter

## Directions

1. Select young and tender spinach.
2. Carefully pick it over, and reject all wilted leaves and coarse fibers and nerves.
3. Cut off the roots.
4. Wash and drain it well in cold water, and press out all the water.
5. Put the spinach in a kettle, and add one cupful of water.
6. Place on a moderate fire and let it simmer for ten minutes.
