## Ingredients

- 18 Small New Potatoes
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful Parsley
- Drawn Butter Sauce

## Directions

1. Select small, round new potatoes, about the size of a marble
2. Boil them for about half an hour or twenty minutes, according to the touch when pierced with a fork
3. Drain off water, peel quickly, and put into a saucepan, with a tablespoonful of butter
4. Let them brown lightly, and serve with Drawn Butter Sauce, in which you have put a tablespoonful of chopped parsley
