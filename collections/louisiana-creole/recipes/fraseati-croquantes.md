## Ingredients

- 8 Ounces of Flour
- 6 Ounces of Sugar
- 2 Ounces of Ground Almonds
- 2 Whole Eggs and 3 Yolks
- 1 Ounce Aniseed

## Directions

1. Break the eggs into a bowl, and beat light, and add the sugar and beat very light.
2. Add the almonds, pounded to a powder, and the aniseed.
3. Beat all together till well incorporated.
4. Add the flour and beat thoroughly.
5. Roll the paste out on a clean dry marble slab in the form of a long, thick rope.
6. Take a knife and cut it into pieces about the size of a guinea egg.
7. Roll each between the palms of your hands, and shape them into oval balls.
8. Make a slight incision into each cake by pressing the back of the knife across the surface.
9. Brush the egg over them, and sprinkle lightly with granulated sugar.
10. Bake in a moderate heat to a light color.
