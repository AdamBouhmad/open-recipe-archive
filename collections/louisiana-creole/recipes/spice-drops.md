## Ingredients

- The Yolks of 3 Eggs
- 0.75 Cup of Sweet Milk
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 1 Cup of Molasses
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Ground Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add the molasses and the milk
3. Blend well, add the spices, and the nutmeg and lemon extract, and then add the flour, sifted with the baking powder
4. Beat well for fifteen minutes, and then drop on tins lined with buttered paper
5. Bake in a quick oven to a delicate brown
