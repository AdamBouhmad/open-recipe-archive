## Ingredients

- 12 Small or Six Large Croakers
- 1 Pint of Milk
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Flour
- Parsley
- Sliced Lemon
- Grease

## Directions

1. Fill a small pan with milk, and add salt and pepper to taste
2. Roll the Croakers in the milk and then take out and roll in dry, sifted flour
3. Have ready a pan of grease that has been heated very hot without boiling
4. Drop in the Croakers and cook till brown
5. The fish must swim in the grease
6. Lift carefully out of the frying pan and serve on a bed of fried parsley, with garnishes of sliced lemon
