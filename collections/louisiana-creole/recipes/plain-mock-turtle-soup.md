## Ingredients

- a calf’s-head
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- cloves
- sweet herbs
- onions
- white wine
- force-meat balls
- a pound of lean veal
- half a pound of salt pork
- brains of the calf’s-head
- curry powder

## Directions

1. Boil a calf’s-head until very tender; take out the head, strain the liquor, and skim off the fat when cold, and keep till following day.
2. Cut up the meat of the head and brain, and add to the liquor; place over the fire, after seasoning to taste with pepper, salt, mace, cloves, sweet herbs, and onions.
3. Let it stew an hour, then add a tumbler of white wine, and it is ready for the force-meat balls.
4. For the balls, chop a pound of lean veal with half a pound of salt pork; add the brains of the calf’s-head, seasoned with pepper, salt, mace, cloves, sweet herbs, or curry powder.
5. Make into balls the size of the yolk of an egg; boil part in the soup, fry the rest for a separate dish.
