## Ingredients

- 8 onions
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of flour
- Lemon juice
- Salt and pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Boil the onions until quite tender, adding salt and pepper
2. When soft, mash well and pass through a sieve
3. Take one spoon of butter and one of flour and melt, blending together without burning, or allowing to brown
4. In this cream dissolve the puree of onions, boiling gently for ten minutes and stirring well
5. Add the juice of a lemon, a teaspoon of vinegar, and serve with cutlets of lamb, fried sweetbreads, etc.
