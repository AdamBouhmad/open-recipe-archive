## Ingredients

- A Glass of Good Rich Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Brandy or Whisky
- A Small Quantity of Crushed Ice

## Directions

1. Dissolve the whisky or brandy and sugar together
2. Mix well, and pour over the milk
3. Add a small quantity of crushed ice and serve
