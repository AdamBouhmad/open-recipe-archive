## Ingredients

- yolks of 11 eggs
- 2 cups of sugar
- 1 cup of butter
- 1 cup of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1/2 teaspoonful of soda
- 3 cups of flour
- extract of vanilla

## Directions

1. Mix yolks of 11 eggs, sugar, butter, milk, cream of tartar, soda, and flour
2. Flavor with extract of vanilla
3. Bake
