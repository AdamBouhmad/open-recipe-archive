## Ingredients

- 1 shin of veal
- 3 pounds shin of beef
- 8 quarts water
- 1 cup tomatoes
- a handful of sorrel if tomatoes are not used
- salt and pepper
- 3 onions
- 3 leeks
- 10 allspice
- 6 cloves
- 3 large carrots
- 1 head of celery
- 1 bunch of parsley

## Directions

1. Put the meat into a large soup kettle and let it come slowly to a boil
2. Draw it forward and as it begins to boil more rapidly skim as the scum rises
3. After another hour add the pepper, salt and vegetables
4. The soup should boil incessantly but gently for about eight hours
5. Take it off and strain into a large bowl and set it away in the ice box until the next day if not for immediate use
6. Remove the fat from the surface and pour off all the clear part into a saucepan and boil again for one or two hours
7. Remove it from the fire
