## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Grated Cheese (Very Rich)
- 0.5 Cup of Milk
- The Yolks of Two Eggs
- Salt and Cayenne to Taste
- bread

## Directions

1. Toast the bread nicely in square slices and cut off the crusts.
2. Butter nicely while very hot and then plunge them into a bowl of boiling milk.
3. Place them on a heated dish and stand in the oven to keep hot while you proceed to make the Rarebit.
4. Have a porcelain-lined saucepan and set a half cup of milk in it over a moderate fire.
5. When it is boiling hot, add the cheese which has been finely grated.
6. Stir unceasingly till the cheese melts, and then add the salt, Cayenne and the yolks of the eggs and pour over the toasted bread.
7. Serve hot.
