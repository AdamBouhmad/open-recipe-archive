## Ingredients

- 1 spoonful of good butter
- warm milk
- 1 1/2 lbs. of sifted flour
- 2 eggs well beaten
- a pinch of salt
- a large spoonful of good yeast

## Directions

1. Cut up butter in warm milk
2. When cool, stir in the sifted flour
3. Add eggs well beaten, salt, and yeast
4. Mix well together
5. Put it into buttered tins to rise
6. When risen, bake 1/2 hour
