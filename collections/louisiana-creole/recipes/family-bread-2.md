## Ingredients

- 1 peck of sifted flour
- ½ pint of family yeast
- new or skim milk
- a little shortening
- a teaspoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Wet the flour with milk, adding shortening and salt
2. Knead it well
3. Have the dough soft if wheat flour, and stiff if made of rye
