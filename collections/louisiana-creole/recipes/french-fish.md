## Ingredients

- boiled fish
- 1 ounce of flour
- 1 quart of milk
- 2 small peeled onions
- parsley
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 sprig of thyme
- grated nutmeg
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of pepper
- 1 pound fresh butter
- white pepper and salt

## Directions

1. Have ready a boiled fish, pick all from the bones
2. Put in stewpan 1 ounce of flour, to which add, by degrees, a quart of milk, mixing it very smoothly
3. Add 2 small peeled onions, a little parsley, a bay leaf, a sprig of thyme, tied together, a little grated nutmeg, teaspoonful of salt, teaspoonful of pepper
4. Place over fire
5. Stir in 1 pound fresh butter, and pass it through a sieve
6. Lay a little sauce on the bottom of your dish
7. Then a layer of the fish
8. Season lightly with white pepper and salt
9. Then another layer of the sauce, until the fish is used up
10. Finish with sauce
