## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes to fill a jar
- vinegar
- half an ounce of garlic sliced
- a quarter of an ounce of salt
- white pepper finely ground

## Directions

1. Take enough ripe tomatoes to fill a jar, put them in a moderate oven, and bake them until they are thoroughly soft
2. Strain them through a coarse cloth or sieve, and to every pint of juice put a pint of vinegar, half an ounce of garlic sliced, a quarter of an ounce of salt, and the same of white pepper finely ground
3. Boil it for one hour, then rub it through a sieve
4. Boil it again to the consistency of cream
5. When cold, bottle it, put a teaspoonful of sweet oil in each bottle; cork them tight, and keep in a dry place
