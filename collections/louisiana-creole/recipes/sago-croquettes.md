## Ingredients

- Equal Parts of Sago Marmalade and Rice Croquettes
- Creole Fritter Batter
- Port Wine Sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare Croquettes of Rice, adding equal parts of Sago Marmalade.
2. Dip in fritter batter, and fry to a golden brown.
3. Serve with Port Wine Sauce.
