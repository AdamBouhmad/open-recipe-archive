## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 lb. of white sugar
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of yeast dough
- lemon or bitter almond for flavoring

## Directions

1. Cream the butter with the yeast dough
2. Mix in the eggs, sugar, and flavoring
3. Bake as you would light bread
