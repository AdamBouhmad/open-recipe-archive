## Ingredients

- 2 Potatoes
- Boiling Lard
- Parsley Sprigs to Garnish

## Directions

1. Have two medium-sized potatoes, and slice them just as thin as possible.
2. Drop a few pieces at a time into the lard, and be sure to have the lard about three inches deep.
3. Stir the slices occasionally, to keep them from overlapping the others.
4. When a light brown, take out of the pan and drain on a piece of brown paper, which you have put in a colander.
5. Stand in the open oven, and continue frying.
6. As you finish the second frying turn the first from the colander into a heated dish, and so continue till all the potatoes are fried.
7. Serve hot.
8. Garnish, if you wish, with parsley sprigs.
