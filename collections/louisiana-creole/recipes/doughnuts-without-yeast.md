## Ingredients

- half a pound of butter
- a pint of sour milk or buttermilk
- three quarters of a pound of sugar
- a small teaspoonful of saleratus dissolved in a little hot water
- two well beaten eggs
- flour
- half a teaspoonful of lemon extract
- half a nutmeg grated
- boiling lard for frying

## Directions

1. Combine butter, sour milk or buttermilk, sugar, saleratus, and beaten eggs
2. Add enough flour to make a smooth dough
3. Flavor with half a teaspoonful of lemon extract and half a nutmeg grated
4. Rub a little flour over a breadboard or table
5. Roll the dough to a quarter of an inch in thickness
6. Cut it in squares, diamonds, or round cakes
7. Fry in boiling lard
8. These cakes may be made in rings and fried
