## Ingredients

- cutlets
- dry stale bread crumbs
- raw eggs
- thyme
- parsley
- marjoram
- onion
- celery leaves or seed
- 1 dozen olives
- letter paper
- lard
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Trim the cutlets nicely
2. Prepare a stuffing of dry, stale bread crumbs, mixed with the yolks of some raw eggs
3. Season highly with thyme, parsley, marjoram, onion and celery leaves or seed, etc.
4. Cut up 1 dozen olives fine, and mix in
5. Put plenty of the dressing on each cutlet, and wrap it carefully in letter paper
6. Lay them side by side in a flat, common pot
7. Add a little lard and let them sweat for 1 hour
8. Broil quickly on hot coals, and serve and eat with lemon juice
