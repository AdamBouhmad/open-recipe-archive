## Ingredients

- One pint of new milk
- quarter of a pound of grated chocolate
- one cup and a half of white sugar

## Directions

1. Boil all these together until it will pull like candy
2. Try a little, and if stiff enough to pull, pour it on a buttered dish
3. Mark it off in squares with a knife as it cools
4. It will break easily when cold
