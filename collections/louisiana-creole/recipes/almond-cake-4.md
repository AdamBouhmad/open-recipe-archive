## Ingredients

- 2 ounces of blanched bitter almonds
- 7 ounces of finely sifted flour, well dried
- 10 eggs
- 1 lb. of loaf sugar, pounded and sifted
- 2 tablespoonfuls of rose water
- whites of eggs and 24 teaspoonfuls of loaf sugar for icing
- lemon, rose water, or vanilla for seasoning

## Directions

1. Beat the almonds very fine
2. Mix with the flour, eggs, sugar, and rose water
3. Bake
4. Ice with the whites of eggs and sugar
5. Season with lemon, rose water, or vanilla
