## Ingredients

- one pint of cream
- one ounce of isinglass
- water
- vanilla bean
- four ounces of sugar
- whipped cream

## Directions

1. Take one pint of cream, whip it until stiff
2. Boil one ounce of isinglass in about a pint of water
3. Boil it until reduced to half a pint
4. Boil in this water and isinglass, a vanilla bean
5. When nearly cold, take out the bean, add four ounces of sugar
6. When this is blood warm, stir in the cream
7. Eat with whipped cream
