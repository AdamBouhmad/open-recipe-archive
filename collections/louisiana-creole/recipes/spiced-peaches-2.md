## Ingredients

- 9 lbs. of good, ripe peaches
- 4 lbs. of sugar
- 1 pint of vinegar
- cloves
- mace
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Rub peaches with a coarse towel and halve them
2. Put 4 lbs. of sugar and 1 pint of vinegar in a kettle with cloves, mace and cinnamon, each 1 ounce
3. When the syrup is formed throw in the peaches, a few at a time, so as to keep them whole
4. When clear, take out and put in more until all are done
5. Boil the syrup till quite rich
6. Pour over the peaches
