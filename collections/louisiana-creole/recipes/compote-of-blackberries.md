## Ingredients

- 1 Quart Blackberries
- 3/4 Pound of Sugar

## Directions

1. Select fine berries, but not too ripe
2. Wash them in cold water, and drain through a sieve
3. Boil the sugar, and add the berries, and let them boil up once very gently
4. Put them into a dish, pour the jelly over and serve
