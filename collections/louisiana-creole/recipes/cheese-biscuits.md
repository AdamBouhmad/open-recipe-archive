## Ingredients

- 0.5 Pound of Butter
- 0.5 Pound of Flour
- 5 Ounces of Grated Swiss Cheese
- 0.5 Tablespoonful of Mustard
- Yolks of 2 Eggs
- A Dash of Cayenne

## Directions

1. Beat the butter to a cream.
2. Add the yolks of the eggs, well beaten, and mix well.
3. Add gradually the grated cheese, mustard and Cayenne.
4. Add the flour gradually, beating in thoroughly and make a stiff dough.
5. Roll it out and cut into square or round biscuits.
6. Bake in a rather slow oven for twenty minutes and serve.
