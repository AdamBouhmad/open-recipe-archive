## Ingredients

- 2 dozen green peppers
- 1 dozen red peppers
- 1/2 large head of cabbage
- 3 tablespoonfuls of mustard seed
- 1 teaspoonful of ground cloves
- 1 tablespoonful of ground allspice
- 2 tablespoonfuls of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of salt per pepper
- cold water
- cold vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the tops from the peppers and remove the seeds
2. Put a teaspoonful of salt into each pepper, cover with cold water, and soak for twenty-four hours
3. Chop the cabbage very fine, and mix well with the ground spices, mustard seed and salt
4. When thoroughly blended, stuff the peppers with this mixture
5. Put on the tops, and stand upright in stone jars, and cover with cold vinegar
