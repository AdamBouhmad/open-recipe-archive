## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful and a half of butter
- 1 tablespoonful and a half of flour
- Half a cup of water or white broth
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Chopped gherkins or other vinegar pickles, about two or three

## Directions

1. To the recipe for white sauce add chopped gherkins, or any other vinegar pickles, using about two or three
2. Add just before serving
3. Serve with fish
