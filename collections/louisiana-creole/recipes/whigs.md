## Ingredients

- ¾ lb. butter
- ¾ lb. sugar
- 6 eggs
- 2 lbs. flour
- 1 pint new milk
- a little salt
- 1 gill good yeast

## Directions

1. Cream ¾ lb. of butter and ¾ lb. of sugar
2. When well mixed, stir in 6 well beaten eggs
3. Sift in 2 lbs. of flour, 1 pint of new milk, and a little salt
4. Mix this well together
5. Add 1 gill of good yeast
6. Bake in small tins or muffin rings
