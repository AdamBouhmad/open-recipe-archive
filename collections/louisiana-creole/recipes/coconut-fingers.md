## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Butter
- 4 Ounces of Sugar
- 3 Eggs
- 2 Ounces of Flour
- 4 Ounces of Grated Cocoanut

## Directions

1. Beat the butter, sugar and eggs together for five minutes, blending thoroughly
2. Then add the flour, and immediately after the grated cocoanut
3. Beat well, and have ready buttered tins
4. Divide the mixture into fingers of about four or five inches in length
5. Bake in a moderately quick oven for thirty minutes
6. When cool dust with powdered sugar
