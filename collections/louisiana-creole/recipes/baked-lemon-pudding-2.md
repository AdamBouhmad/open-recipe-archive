## Ingredients

- 3/4 Pound of Butter
- 3/4 Pound of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Gill of Sherry and Brandy, Mixed
- 1 Small Stale Sponge Cake
- 6 Eggs
- oranges

## Directions

1. Grate the rind of the oranges, and squeeze out the juice after removing the seeds
2. Grate the inner meat
3. Beat the butter and sugar to a thick cream, and then add the wine
4. Add these to the oranges, grated with the rind
5. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth, and the yolks separately
6. Add them in order to the mixture
7. Place in a moderately quick oven, and let it bake for about a half hour
8. When done, let it cool slightly
9. Place a meringue on top, as in Apple Meringue Pudding, and set back to bake two minutes more
10. Serve cold
