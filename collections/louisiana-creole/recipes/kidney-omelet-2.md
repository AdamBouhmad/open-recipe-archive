## Ingredients

- 3 kidneys
- 6 eggs
- 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls of butter

## Directions

1. Cut the fat from the kidneys and wash well
2. Cut into small pieces
3. Mix these with the eggs, which you will have prepared as for a plain omelet
4. Proceed as in Ham Omelet
5. Serve with Tomato Sauce
