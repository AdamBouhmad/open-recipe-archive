## Ingredients

- 12 Shallots
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vinegar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Chopped Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Sauce Velouté

## Directions

1. Chop the parsley very fine
2. Have ready a Sauce Velouté
3. Add the other ingredients
4. Mix well
5. Place in a saucepan and set in boiling water and let it heat
6. Serve hot with fish, white meats of chicken, etc
