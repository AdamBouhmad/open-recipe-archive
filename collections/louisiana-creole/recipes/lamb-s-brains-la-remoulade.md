## Ingredients

- Lamb's Brains
- 1 Pint of White Wine
- 2 Quarts of Water
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 2 Shallots
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- 6 Capers
- 3 Small Vinegar Pickles
- 1 Tablespoonful of Parsley
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Olive Oil
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Vinegar
- 1 Tablespoonful of Creole Mustard
- 1/2 Tablespoonful of Chives
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Plunge the Lamb's Brains into cold water and let them stand for an hour, changing the water several times
2. Prepare in exactly the same manner indicated for the preparation of Sheep Brains
3. After removing from the water and taking off the skin, drain of all water
4. Have ready a saucepan of boiling water and season it with salt and pepper and an herb bouquet of parsley, bay leaves and thyme
5. When the herbs begin to boil add a pint of white wine to the water, as it boils up again drop in the brains and let them cook for ten minutes
6. Remove the herb bouquet and strain the brains through a sieve
7. Place on a hot dish
8. Chop the shallots very fine, mince the garlic and mash the yolks of the eggs
9. Put the vinegar into a small saucepan and add the shallots, the garlic and let all boil till the vinegar is reduced about one-half
10. Mash the yolks of eggs in the sweet oil and cut up the capers and add all to the vinegar
11. Add the parsley and the vinegar pickles chopped fine and let all come to a boil
12. Add the chives and two teaspoonfuls of Creole mustard
13. Mix well and pour all over the brains and send to the table very hot
