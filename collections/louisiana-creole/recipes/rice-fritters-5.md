## Ingredients

- Left-over rice
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of yeast powder
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Take the left-over rice and mash very fine
2. Take three eggs, a half cup of flour, one teaspoonful of yeast powder, and sugar to taste, and beat all into a light, thick batter
3. Cook by dropping a spoonful at a time into boiling lard
