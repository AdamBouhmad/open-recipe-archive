## Ingredients

- one quart of firm clabber
- one quart of sweet cream
- white sugar
- vanilla bean
- half a cup of sweet milk

## Directions

1. Make it very sweet with white sugar
2. Flavor with vanilla bean boiled in half a cup of sweet milk
3. Churn all together ten minutes
4. Freeze in moulds, or in any ordinary freezer
