## Ingredients

- raspberries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take the weight of raspberries and sugar; pick the berries very carefully, and lay them, with the sugar, in a stone jar overnight
2. In the morning boil it until it begins to jelly; the great secret of making nice jam, and preserving the raspberry flavor, is to boil it enough
