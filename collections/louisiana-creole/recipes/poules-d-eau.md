## Ingredients

- 2 Pair of Poules d'Eau
- 6 Turnips
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 2 Onions, Chopped Fine
- 1 Square Inch of Ham, Minced Fine
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 3 Sprigs Each of Thyme and Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Before cooking parboil a few minutes if there is the slightest odor of fish
2. Add a small peeled carrot or onion to the water to absorb the flavor of fish
