## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- good crushed sugar in weight of 12 eggs
- flour in weight of 7 eggs
- juice of 2 nice lemons
- rind of 1 lemon or 4 spoonfuls of rose water

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks and whites separate
2. Add the flour and sugar
3. Stir them lightly with the juice of 2 nice lemons and the rind of 1, or 4 spoonfuls of rose water
4. Stir all well together
5. Bake on tins in a moderately hot oven
