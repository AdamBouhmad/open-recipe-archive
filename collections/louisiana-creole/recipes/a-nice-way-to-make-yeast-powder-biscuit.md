## Ingredients

- a quart of flour
- two heaping spoonfuls of yeast or baking powder
- a tablespoonful of lard
- a little salt
- water

## Directions

1. Take a quart of flour and sift it
2. Sift one pint of the flour in a tin basin
3. Sift into it two heaping spoonfuls of yeast or baking powder
4. Add to the flour a tablespoonful of lard and put this also in the basin
5. Make a nice batter with a little salt
6. Beat the flour, lard and water very briskly until it is light
7. Take down your biscuit board and sift on it the other pint of flour
8. Make a hole in the flour and pour in your batter
9. Gently stir it until it is a soft dough
10. Keep it as soft as possible
11. Roll it out and cut it with the biscuit cutter
12. Bake quickly
