## Ingredients

- 2 1/4 Pounds Flour
- 1 3/4 Pounds Sifted Sugar
- 1 1/4 Pounds Butter
- 1 Pound of Seeded and Chopped Raisins
- 1 Pound Chopped and Dried Cherries
- 1 Pound of Currants, Washed, Cleansed and Dried
- 12 Eggs
- 1 Ounce of Salt
- 1 1/2 Pounds of Shredded Orange, Lemon and Green Citron Peel, Combined
- 3/4 Pint of French Brandy
- 1/2 Gill of Caramel or Burnt Sugar Coloring
- 8 Ounces of Ground Almonds
- The Zest of 4 Oranges
- 1 Ounce of Ground Cloves
- Cinnamon, Nutmeg and Coriander Seed in Equal Proportions
- Two teaspoonfuls of baking-powder

## Directions

1. Work the butter until it becomes very creamy, using a wooden spoon.
2. Then add the sugar, working to a thick cream, and add the yolks of the eggs, well-beaten.
3. Beat till light, and add by degrees the flour, salt and two teaspoonfuls of baking-powder, working the batter all the time.
4. Then add the fruit, which will have dredged slightly with flour, working them well into the batter.
5. Add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a thick froth, and when all are well incorporated, pour the mixture into a baking pan, lined with double sheets of buttered paper, and place on top thick sheets of paper which you set in the oven.
6. Bake in a moderate oven, and keep the heat regular and steady all the time, being careful not to increase or diminish it during the baking.
7. Bake for two hours and a half.
