## Ingredients

- i lb. each of macaroons, ratifees, sponge cake, and sugar
- i pint of milk
- 1 or 2 eggs
- 1 lb. of fruit cut small (dried cherries, angelica and raisins)
- 1 pint of whipped cream

## Directions

1. Soak i lb. each of macaroons, ratifees, sponge cake, and sugar in a light custard made with i pint of milk and 1 or 2 eggs
2. When ready to freeze, add 1 lb. of fruit cut small (dried cherries, angelica and raisins)
3. Place in freezer
4. Stir until partially congealed
5. Add a pint of whipped cream, and freeze hard
