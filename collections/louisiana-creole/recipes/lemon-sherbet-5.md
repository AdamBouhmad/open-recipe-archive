## Ingredients

- The Juice of 10 Lemons
- The Grated Zest of 3 Lemons
- 1 Pint of Sugar
- 1 Quart of Water

## Directions

1. Put the sugar and water to boil.
2. Grate the zest or yellow rind of three lemons, and add to the syrup, and let it boil for five minutes.
3. Then set away to cool.
4. Extract the juice from the lemons, and grate the fruit.
5. Mix this with the syrup, and then strain through a thin muslin cloth.
6. Turn into the freezer, and proceed as in Ice Cream.
