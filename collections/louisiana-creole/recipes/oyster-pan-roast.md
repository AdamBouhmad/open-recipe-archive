## Ingredients

- A Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Sliced Lemon and Parsley to Garnish

## Directions

1. Drain the oysters, heat a deep frying pan, drop in a generous lump of butter
2. When it melts, add the oysters, covering and shaking the pan constantly over a hot fire
3. Have ready a dish well buttered and nicely garnished with parsley and lemon slices
4. When the oysters are brown, turn quickly into the dish and add salt and pepper and melted butter, into which you have dropped finely chopped parsley, and serve hot
