## Ingredients

- two tablespoonfuls of boiled rice
- a little warm water
- a tablespoonful of butter
- three or four eggs beaten light
- a quart of sweet milk
- sugar enough to make it quite sweet
- one cup of picked and seeded raisins
- nutmeg
- essence of lemon or vanilla

## Directions

1. If the rice is very dry, wash it with a little warm water
2. Put it in a pan
3. add a tablespoonful of butter, three or four eggs beaten light, a quart of sweet milk, sugar enough to make it quite sweet, and one cup of picked and seeded raisins
4. Flavor with nutmeg and essence of lemon or vanilla
5. Bake lightly
6. Do not allow it to remain in the oven long, as the milk will become watery and thus destroy the jelly-like consistency of the custard
7. The raisins may be omitted if they are objectionable
