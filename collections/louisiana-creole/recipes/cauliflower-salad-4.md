## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Boiled Cauliflower
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chopped Parsley
- 1 Teaspoonful of Tarragon Vinegar
- A French Dressing (plain)

## Directions

1. Boil the cauliflower as directed (See recipe)
2. Then separate the flowerets; mix them with parsley, and cut the remainder very fine and mix also
3. Let it cool
4. Serve with a French dressing, after adding first an extra teaspoonful of Tarragon vinegar
