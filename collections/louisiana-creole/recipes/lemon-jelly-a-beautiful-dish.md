## Ingredients

- Isinglass
- Water
- Lemon juice
- Sugar
- Rinds of lemons
- Saffron

## Directions

1. Set an ounce of isinglass in a pint of water on the stove in a stew-pan
2. Stir the isinglass until it dissolves
3. Let it boil a few minutes, then add a pint of lemon juice sweetened with a pound and a half of sugar, or a little more, if it is wished very sweet
4. Stir this in with the rinds of six lemons, and boil all together
5. After boiling for about five minutes, put a teaspoonful of saffron in to color it yellow, and strain through a flannel bag
6. Fill your jelly-glasses with it; when cool, it is a most beautiful dish for a collation
