## Ingredients

- 1 pint of new milk
- 2 or 3 bitter almonds
- a stick of cinnamon
- a piece of lemon peel
- 7 or 8 good sized lumps of sugar
- yolks of 6 eggs
- almond or rose water

## Directions

1. Put almonds, cinnamon, lemon peel, and sugar into milk
2. Let the whole simmer gently till the flavor is extracted
3. Strain and stir till cool
4. Beat the yolks of 6 eggs and mix well with the milk
5. Stir the whole over a slow fire till it is the thickness of rich cream
6. Flavor it with almond or rose water, using 1 ounce
