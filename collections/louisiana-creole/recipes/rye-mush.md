## Ingredients

- 3-4 Cup of Rye Meal
- 1 Quart of Boiling Water
- salt
- sugar and cream

## Directions

1. Sift the meal into the boiling water, and stir constantly while doing so
2. Add the salt, and continue stirring till the mixture begins to boil
3. Cover and let it cook slowly for at least an hour and a quarter
4. Serve hot for breakfast, with sugar and cream
