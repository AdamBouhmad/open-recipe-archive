## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Vanilla Cream
- 1 Pint of Pistache Ice Cream
- 1 Pint of Strawberry or Raspberry Cream

## Directions

1. Prepare the ice cream according to recipes
2. Have at hand a brick form or mold that will hold three pints
3. Lay at the bottom of the mold Pistache Ice Cream
4. Place over this the Vanilla Cream and on top of this the Raspberry or Strawberry Cream
5. Cover the mold tightly and set to freeze for two hours
6. Plunge the mold into warm water, wash off all the salt and dry
7. Carefully uncover, and unmold the cream in a cold dessert dish or on a piece of paper
8. Take a long, sharp knife, dip into warm water, cut the brick of cream through the center, and then divide the pieces into three or four parts
