## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- The Yolks of 5 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1/2 Pint of Gelatine
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla
- 1 Pint of Cream
- The Whites of 5 Eggs
- Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine and whip one pint of cream.
2. Set the milk to boil, and add the gelatine, which you will have dissolved in two tablespoonfuls of milk.
3. Have the eggs and sugar beaten very light, and, as the milk boils up, add the sugar and eggs, but do not let them boil, or the eggs will curdle.
4. Stir well, and turn the gelatine, which you will have dissolved well, into the milk.
5. Then remove from the fire and strain, and add the essence of vanilla.
6. Have the mold ready, clean and well rinsed and turn the cream into it.
7. Set it on ice to harden.
8. When ready to serve, make a cream by whipping the whites of the eggs into the cream which has been whipped with powdered sugar.
9. Turn the cream into a dish, heap this delicious meringue cream over it, and serve very cold.
