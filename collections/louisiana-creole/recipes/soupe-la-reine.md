## Ingredients

- two chickens
- water
- thyme
- sweet-bay
- parsley
- onion
- flour
- rice
- eggs
- cold milk

## Directions

1. Boil two chickens in water with thyme, sweet-bay and parsley
2. When cooked (not to pieces), take them out of the water
3. cut up the breasts in small pieces the size of dice
4. fry a few pieces of onion without coloring them
5. add a little flour and the water that the chickens were boiled in
6. add a little rice and the balance of the chickens, meat and bones, chopped fine
7. Boil all together
8. when thoroughly cooked strain through a colander and put back to boil, stirring constantly
9. When it comes to a boil remove it from the fire
10. add the beaten yolks of a few eggs and a little cold milk, stirring continually
11. Keep the soup in bain-marie
12. When ready to serve put the small pieces of the breasts in a soup-dish and pour the soup over them
