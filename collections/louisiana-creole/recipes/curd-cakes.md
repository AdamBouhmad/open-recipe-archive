## Ingredients

- 1 quart of curds
- 8 eggs (leaving out 4 whites)
- 4 tablespoonfuls of sugar
- grated nutmeg
- a little flour
- butter for frying

## Directions

1. Mix curds, eggs, sugar, nutmeg, and flour all well together
2. Heat butter in a frying-pan
3. Drop them in and fry like fritters
