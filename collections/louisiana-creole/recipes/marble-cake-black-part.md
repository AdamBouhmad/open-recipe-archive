## Ingredients

- yolks of four eggs
- one cup of brown sugar
- half a cup of molasses
- half a cup butter
- half a cup of sour milk
- one teaspoonful of soda
- plenty of all kinds of spices to suit the taste

## Directions

1. Put first black, then white, dough, until all is in
2. then bake
