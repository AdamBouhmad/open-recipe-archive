## Ingredients

- 3 Pairs of Sweetbreads
- 1 Onion
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig of Thyme
- 1/2 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 1 Teaspoonful of Prepared Mustard
- 1 Pint of Water
- Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and parboil the sweetbreads as already shown in recipe.
2. Chop an onion very fine and place it in a saucepan with a tablespoonful of butter.
3. Let them simmer without browning.
4. Add one bay leaf, one sprig of thyme, one-half clove of garlic, a teaspoonful of prepared mustard, and mix well.
5. Then add a pint of water and stir well.
6. Add the sweetbreads which have been chopped very fine and formed into little fringed balls, by patting with the hand.
7. Let them simmer for about fifteen minutes longer.
8. Serve with a Cream Sauce.
