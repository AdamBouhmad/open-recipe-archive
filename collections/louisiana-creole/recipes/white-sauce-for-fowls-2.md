## Ingredients

- 1 teacupful of milk
- 1 large spoonful of butter
- flour
- yolk of 1 egg
- 1 teaspoonful of cream
- chopped parsley

## Directions

1. Melt in a teacupful of milk, 1 large spoonful of butter kneaded in flour
2. beat up the yolk of an egg with a teaspoonful of cream
3. stir it into the butter and heat it over the fire, stirring it constantly
4. Chopped parsley improves the sauce
