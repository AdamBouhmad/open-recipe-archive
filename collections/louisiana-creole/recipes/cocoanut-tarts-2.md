## Ingredients

- 6 cocoanuts
- yolks of 10 eggs
- 1 lb. of white sugar
- 1 lb. of butter
- rose water
- nice puff paste

## Directions

1. Season cocoanut, yolks of eggs, sugar, and butter with rose water
2. Put the butter in cold and bake in a nice puff paste
