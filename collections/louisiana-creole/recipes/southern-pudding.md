## Ingredients

- nice tart apples
- sugar
- 1 lemon
- stale rusks
- milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Lay alternately in a baking dish slices of nice tart apples
2. On these sprinkle sugar, and the grated oily rind of 1 lemon, and then crumbs of stale rusks which have been soaked in milk
3. Then more slices of apples, sugar and crumbs of rusks
4. Cut very thin slices of butter and lay thinly on the top
5. Over this sift thickly pulverized sugar
6. Bake 1 hour, and send to table in the same dish
