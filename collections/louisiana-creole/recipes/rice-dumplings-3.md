## Ingredients

- ½ Pound of Mashed, Cold, Boiled Rice
- ½ Dozen Apples
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Egg
- Sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Pare the apples and take out the cores.
2. Fill the interiors with sugar and cinnamon.
3. Beat the egg and the rice together and add the flour, knead to a dough.
4. Cover the apples with a thick coating of rice.
5. Tie each in a dumpling cloth and put them in a pot of boiling water.
6. Let them boil for three-quarters of an hour.
7. When done, turn out carefully on a dish and serve with a Hard or Cream Sauce.
8. Or the dumplings may be baked in the oven.
