## Ingredients

- 3 dozen cauliflowers
- 2 teaspoonfuls of mustard per quart of vinegar
- 1/2 cup of white sugar per quart of vinegar
- salt and water

## Directions

1. Boil the cauliflower for about twenty minutes in salt and water
2. Remove and drain, and break carefully, and let them get cold
3. Pack in a jar and pour over them hot spiced vinegar which you will have strained, having first stirred into each quart of vinegar two teaspoonfuls of French mustard and a half cup of white sugar
4. Cover the cauliflower well with the mixture and bottle tight
