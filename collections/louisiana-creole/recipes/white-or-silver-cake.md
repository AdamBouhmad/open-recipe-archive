## Ingredients

- whites of 20 eggs
- 1 1/2 lbs. of flour
- 1 1/2 lbs. of sugar
- 15 ounces of butter
- a little more than 1 teaspoon of almond extract
- 1 level teaspoon of soda
- 1 good teaspoon of cream of tartar

## Directions

1. Wash thoroughly and cream butter
2. Roll sugar and add to it
3. Beat the whites of eggs very stiff and add to this
4. Sift in the flour and beat mixture well 10 or 15 minutes
5. Put in the extract
6. Last of all, pulverize soda and cream of tartar in a little flour and sift into the mixture
7. Beat as little as possible to mix well
8. Bake 1 1/2 hours, the first 15 minutes in a very moderate oven, increasing heat gradually
9. Cover the pan the first 1/2 or 3/4 of the hour
10. This makes a good large cake, or 2 small ones
