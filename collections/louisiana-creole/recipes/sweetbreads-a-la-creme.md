## Ingredients

- Sweetbreads
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Mushrooms
- Salt
- White pepper

## Directions

1. Remove all veins and nerves, and chop the meat into pieces of about an inch and a half.
2. Chop the mushrooms very fine indeed.
3. Put the butter in a saucepan, and when it melts, add the flour, being careful not to let it brown.
4. When perfectly smooth, add the milk and stir constantly until it boils.
5. Then add the chopped mushrooms and let them simmer for five minutes.
6. Season well to taste with salt and white pepper.
7. Then add the sweetbreads and cook for five minutes longer and serve hot.
