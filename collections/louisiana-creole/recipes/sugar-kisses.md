## Ingredients

- whites of three eggs
- powdered white sugar
- two or three drops of essence of lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of three eggs to a froth
2. Stir in powdered white sugar, a little at a time, till you have formed a very thick batter
3. Add two or three drops of essence of lemon
4. Wet a sheet of white paper, lay it on a tin and drop this mixture upon it in lumps about the size and shape of a walnut
5. Set them in a cool oven, and as soon as their surface is hardened, take them out and remove them from the paper with a broad-bladed knife
6. Let the oven cool still more, then place these little cakes, laying the flat part of two together, on a sieve and return them to the oven
7. Let them remain for fifteen minutes before they are done
