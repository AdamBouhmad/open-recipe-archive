## Ingredients

- 6 Potatoes
- 1 Pint Gravy or Broth
- 1 Onion
- 1 Leek
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Take cold boiled potatoes and cut into quarters, and then drain and put into a saucepan
2. Cover with any meat gravy or soup stock that may have remained, and chopped onion, chopped leek, pepper and salt
3. Set on the stove and let stew or simmer for half an hour
