## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Yolks of 8 Eggs
- A Gill of Any Kind of Liqueur

## Directions

1. Boil the milk and take from the fire
2. Add the sugar and the eggs, beaten to a thick cream
3. Add a gill of any kind of fancy liqueur
4. Turn into a mold and freeze as directed
