## Ingredients

- three quarts of molasses
- eight gallons of rain water
- good yeast, or two yeast cakes
- one sheet of common brown paper
- molasses

## Directions

1. Take three quarts of molasses, add to it eight gallons of rain water
2. Turn the mixture into a clean cask, shake it well two or three times
3. Throw in a few spoonfuls of good yeast, or two yeast cakes
4. Place the cask in a warm place, and in ten days throw in it a sheet of common brown paper, smeared with molasses; it should be torn into narrow strips
