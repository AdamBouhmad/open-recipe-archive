## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1½ pints boiling milk
- 1 quart Indian meal
- ½ pint wheat flour
- 1 tablespoonful sugar
- a little salt
- 2 eggs well beaten

## Directions

1. Dissolve 1 tablespoonful of butter in 1½ pints of boiling milk
2. Scald 1 quart of Indian meal into this
3. When cool, add ½ pint of wheat flour, a tablespoonful of sugar, a little salt, and 2 eggs well beaten
4. Bake in 2 cakes
