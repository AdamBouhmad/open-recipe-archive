## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of Bouilli
- 6 Slices of Stale Bread
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Parsley and Lettuce Leaves to Garnish

## Directions

1. Take left-over or stale bread, slice it thickly and dip in cream or milk.
2. Then dip it in the beaten whites and yolks of egg and fry in butter.
3. Cut the bouilli into slices to match the bread, dip it in the egg and fry also.
4. Serve on a dish with chopped parsley dashed over it and a garnish of parsley or lettuce leaves.
