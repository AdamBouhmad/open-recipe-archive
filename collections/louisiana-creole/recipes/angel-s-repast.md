## Ingredients

- apples
- white sugar
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Stew nice apples
2. Strain them
3. Sweeten with white sugar
4. Mix 4 whites of eggs
5. Add the stewed apples and eggs beaten to a stiff froth
6. Make a rich boiled custard
7. Pour it into a glass dish and drop on the custard the beaten apples and eggs
