## Ingredients

- two eggs
- two cups of sugar
- half a cup of butter
- one cup of sweet milk
- three cups of flour
- one teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- half a teaspoonful of soda

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients
2. Bake in squares
