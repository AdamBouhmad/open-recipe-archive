## Ingredients

- fillet of veal
- pepper
- salt
- mace
- a little water
- butter
- pounded biscuit or bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Slice pieces of the fillet of veal 1/2 an inch thick and 8 or 10 inches wide and long
2. Put them in a dish
3. Sprinkle over them on 1 side, a little pepper, salt and mace, all finely pounded
4. Roll tightly and tie them up separately
5. Put them into a stewpan with a little water and butter, and simmer down to a brown gravy
6. Stew them 1 hour
7. When they are to be served, have ready some pounded biscuit or bread crumbs, which sprinkle over them after they are put into the dish
