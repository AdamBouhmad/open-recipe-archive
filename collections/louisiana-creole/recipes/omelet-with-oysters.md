## Ingredients

- eight or ten eggs
- a gill of cream
- a tablespoonful of sweet butter
- a spoonful of chopped parsley
- pepper
- salt
- a pint of chopped oysters

## Directions

1. Break eight or ten eggs in a basin
2. whip them up well
3. add a gill of cream, a tablespoonful of sweet butter, a spoonful of chopped parsley, pepper and salt to taste
4. beat it again very light
5. stir in a pint of chopped oysters
6. when the butter is hot put in the omelet
7. When the eggs have partly set, roll the omelet in form of a cushion, which you can do by using the tin slice
8. Brown delicately
9. serve with a little melted butter or some sauce you prefer
