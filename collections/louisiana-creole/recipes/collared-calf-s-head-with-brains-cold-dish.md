## Ingredients

- half or whole calf's head
- water
- sage leaves
- an onion
- meat of head and brains
- slices of ham
- pepper
- salt
- chopped tongue
- an eschalot
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Boil half or the whole calf's head, as you require.
2. Cover it with water and let it simmer for two hours.
3. Take it up, remove the bones, and put them back into the broth.
4. Let it continue to stew, adding to it sage leaves, and an onion.
5. Cut the meat of head and brains into a stew-pan, adding to it some slices of ham, pepper and salt, the chopped tongue and an eschalot.
6. Beat the brains up with two eggs, before putting them in, which should be the last thing.
7. Let these cook two hours.
8. Pour all in a mould and fill up with the liquor from the head, which should be boiled to a jelly.
