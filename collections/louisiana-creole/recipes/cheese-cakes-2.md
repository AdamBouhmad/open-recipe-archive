## Ingredients

- 6 ounces of butter
- 6 ounces of sugar
- 4 eggs
- a glass of brandy or wine
- a little rose water
- a teaspoonful of nutmeg or mace
- a handful of currants
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Beat butter and sugar to a cream
2. Add eggs, brandy or wine, rose water, nutmeg or mace, and currants
3. Bake it in puff paste
