## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pound of Flour
- 1 Pound of Butter
- The Whites of 16 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoon-ful Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon Extract

## Directions

1. Wash the butter. Then beat it and the sugar to a fine cream.
2. Add one-quarter of the flour, well-sifted with the baking powder.
3. Then add one-quarter of the whites of the eggs, which you will have beaten to a stiff froth.
4. Continue alternating the eggs and flour till all are used.
5. Add the flavoring extract, and beat steadily for a half hour.
6. Bake in a moderate oven for one hour.
