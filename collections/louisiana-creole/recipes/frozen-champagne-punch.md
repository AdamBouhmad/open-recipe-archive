## Ingredients

- 6 Lemons
- 2 Oranges
- 1 Pound of White Powdered Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water
- % Pint of Champagne
- ½ Gill of Brandy

## Directions

1. Extract all the juice from the oranges and lemons
2. Dissolve the sugar in boiling water, and when cold add the juice
3. Freeze very hard for four or five minutes, and when firm add the liquors, turning long enough to mix well, and then proceed to freeze as in Creole Punch
4. Serve in punch glasses
