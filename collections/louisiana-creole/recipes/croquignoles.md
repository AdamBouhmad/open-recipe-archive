## Ingredients

- 8 Ounces of Sweet Almonds
- 4 Ounces of Bitter Almonds
- Whites of 4 Eggs
- 1½ Pounds of Fine Sugar

## Directions

1. Scald, skin and wash and dry the almonds, as directed in "Macarons," and then pound them into a pulp adding the whites of the eggs gradually, remembering always that they must be beaten to a stiff froth.
2. Then work in the sugar, and you will have a firm paste.
3. Drop these in small balls upon wafer paper, spread upon a baking sheet of tin or iron, in distinct and separate rows.
4. Pass a little brush, moistened with water, lightly on their surface, and bake in a very slack oven till a very light color.
