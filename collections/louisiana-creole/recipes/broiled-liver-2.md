## Ingredients

- 1 pound of beef's liver
- 1 tablespoonful of melted butter
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Chopped parsley to garnish
- Bacon slices

## Directions

1. Wash and slice the liver into thin pieces of about three inches in length and one-quarter inch in thickness.
2. Run a skewer through to prevent from curling up.
3. Season well with salt and pepper, brush lightly with butter, and place on a double broiler, stringing on the skewer with alternate slices of bacon.
4. Broil as you would a tenderloin steak for about five minutes, and serve with a sauce of melted butter and chopped parsley poured over.
