## Ingredients

- Peaches, quinces, plums, or citron, preserved
- A pound of loaf sugar
- A small cup of water

## Directions

1. Take preserved peaches, quinces, plums, or citron from the syrup, and drain them on a sieve
2. To a pound of loaf sugar, put a small cup of water
3. When it is dissolved, set it over a moderate fire, and let it boil
4. When it boils, put in the fruit to be candied, and stir continually until the sugar granulates over the fruit
5. Take it up, and dry it in a warm oven
6. If not sufficiently candied, repeat the operation
