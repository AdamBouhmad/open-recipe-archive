## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Grated Parmesan or Gruyere Cheese
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Sifted Flour
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 3 Eggs
- 1/4 Cup of Fresh Milk or Cream
- A Pinch of Grated Nutmeg
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Put the butter into a saucepan and melt, and add the flour, blending without allowing it to brown
2. Add immediately the boiling milk, and let it boil for two minutes
3. Remove from the fire and stir in the yolks of three eggs, well beaten
4. Add salt, pepper, nutmeg and the grated cheese
5. Mix all thoroughly
6. Have ready a small pudding dish, or several small souffle dishes and butter these well
7. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth and beat them into the mixture
8. Fill the dishes about two-thirds full
9. Bake in a moderate oven for about twenty-five minutes
10. Serve immediately
