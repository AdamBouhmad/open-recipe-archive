## Ingredients

- Twelve large apples
- water
- 2 whites of eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Quarter and core twelve large apples
2. Boil them in as little water as possible
3. Pass through a sieve
4. When cold, add 2 whites of eggs beaten
5. Sweeten to the taste
6. Beat all up with a spoon till quite stiff
7. Have made previously a soft custard, with the 2 yolks
8. Flavor and sweeten slightly
