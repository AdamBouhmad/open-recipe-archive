## Ingredients

- 4 pounds round of beef
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 clove
- vinegar
- lard
- 3 pigs feet
- 8 calves feet
- carrot

## Directions

1. Season 4 pounds round of beef well with salt, pepper, a little garlic, 1 bay leaf, and 1 clove
2. Lard well through and through
3. Sprinkle with a tablespoonful of vinegar and let it stand for 24 hours
4. Put the daube in a pot with a small piece of lard
5. Cover with heavy cover on a slow fire and let it smother for about 4 hours until thoroughly done
6. Take off the cover and let it fry a light brown
7. Remove from the fire
8. In a separate pot have 3 pigs feet and 8 calves feet boiling steadily till they leave the bone, skimmed well at the first boil
9. Add one carrot to boil
