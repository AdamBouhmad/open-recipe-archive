## Ingredients

- ½ Pound of the Best Flour
- ⅛ Ounce of Yeast
- ½ Cup of Sugar
- ⅛ Ounce of Salt
- 1 Pound of the Best Butter
- 12 or 8 Eggs

## Directions

1. The dough for Bullfrogs is just the same as that for the Brioche, only a half pound of flour is used, instead of a pound, and a cup of butter.
2. Bake in the same manner as Brioche.
