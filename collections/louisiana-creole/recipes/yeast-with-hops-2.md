## Ingredients

- eight large Irish potatoes
- a handful of hops
- water
- a cup of flour
- half a cup of good yeast, or a yeast cake

## Directions

1. Peel and boil eight large Irish potatoes
2. Boil a handful of hops in a little water, or in the water the potatoes were boiled in
3. mash the potatoes fine
4. strain the water from the hops over them
5. Put in a cup of flour to the potatoes before the water is poured on, as it mixes better when dry
6. mix all together and beat it
7. then put in half a cup of good yeast, or a yeast cake
