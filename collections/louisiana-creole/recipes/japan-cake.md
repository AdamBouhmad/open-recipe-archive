## Ingredients

- 3/4 lb. of sugar
- 1/2 lb. of butter
- 5 beaten eggs
- 1 teaspoonful of rose water or a nutmeg
- 1 lb. of flour
- sugar for sifting

## Directions

1. Stir together sugar and butter until white
2. Add 5 beaten eggs, rose water or nutmeg
3. Add 1 lb. of flour
4. Drop it with a large spoon on flat tins that have been buttered
5. Sift sugar over them
