## Ingredients

- potatoes
- small onion
- water
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- fresh butter
- sweet cream

## Directions

1. Pare and slice thin half a dozen fine potatoes and a small onion
2. Boil them in three pints of water till so soft that you can pulp them through a cullender
3. When returned to the pot add a very little salt and cayenne pepper
4. Add a quarter of a pound of fresh butter, divided into bits
5. Boil it 10 minutes longer
6. When you put it into the tureen, stir in 2 tablespoonfuls or more of sweet cream
