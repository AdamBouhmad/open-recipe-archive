## Ingredients

- 2 Young Eggplants
- ¾ Pint of Milk
- Salt and Pepper
- Flour

## Directions

1. Slice the eggplants nicely and thin.
2. Boil them in milk in which you have put salt and pepper to taste.
3. Pass the eggplant in flour, dusting lightly, and fry in boiling lard.
4. The eggplant must float in the lard.
5. Drain on brown paper in the mouth of the oven and serve hot.
