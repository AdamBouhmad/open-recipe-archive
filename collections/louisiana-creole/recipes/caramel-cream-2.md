## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Pint of Cream
- 4 Ounces of Powdered Sugar
- The Yolks of 3 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Caramel

## Directions

1. Boil one pint of milk and one pint of cream together.
2. Add four ounces of powdered white sugar and the yolks of three eggs, nicely beaten together till very light.
3. Add one teaspoonful of Caramel.
4. Let it reduce one-half.
5. Strain, garnish nicely with any fruits, and serve cold.
