## Ingredients

- plums
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Get plums before they are dead ripe.
2. Allow a pound of sugar to a pound of fruit, dissolve and boil the sugar and water (allowing half a pint of water to a pound).
3. Boil the syrup until it is thick, then put in the plums and boil them until they are transparent.
4. Bottle them in sealed jars.
