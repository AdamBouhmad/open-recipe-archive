## Ingredients

- one quart of milk
- six eggs
- seven tablespoonfuls of flour
- salt
- wine or lemon sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the milk
2. Stir in the eggs and flour, while the milk is nearly hot enough to boil
3. Do not let it boil when you stir in the flour, but take it off the fire, or you will curdle the eggs
4. Salt the milk slightly before boiling
5. Bake this batter half an hour
6. Eat it with wine or lemon sauce
