## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Sauce Espagnole
- 1 Glass of Madeira Wine
- 1 Glass of Mushroom Liquor
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- 1 Teaspoonful of Pepper
- Salt to Taste

## Directions

1. To one pint of Sauce Espagnole add a glass of Madeira wine and a glass of mushroom liquor, a herb bouquet and a teaspoonful of pepper.
2. Carefully remove all fat and set on the fire and cook for thirty minutes.
3. Strain and use when needed.
4. This sauce is used in all recipes where Madeira Sauce is indicated as a foundation sauce.
