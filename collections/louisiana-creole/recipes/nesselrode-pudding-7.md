## Ingredients

- 1/2 Pint of Chestnuts
- 3/4 Pint of Almonds
- 1 Pint of Cream
- 1 Pint of Boiling Water
- 1/2 Pound of Mixed French Candies of the Finest Kind
- 1/2 of a Grated Pineapples, or 1/2 of a Can
- The Yolks of Six Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Essence
- sugar
- 4 tablespoonfuls of Sherry

## Directions

1. Shell the chestnuts and throw them into boiling water to blanch
2. Then take off the brown skin, and set them in a saucepan and cover with boiling water, and let them boil for twenty minutes
3. Then drain and press them through a colander
4. Shell the almonds and blanch them for twelve minutes in boiling water
5. Then take them out and pound them, and cut the fruit into very small pieces
6. Put the sugar and the water on to boil together, and let the mixture boil for ten minutes
7. Then beat the yolks of the eggs very, very light, and add them to the boiling syrup
8. Let it boil up once, and take off, and then beat very lightly with an egg beater, till cool
9. Add all the fruits, the almonds and chestnuts, and the pint of cream, and a tablespoonful of Vanilla Essence
10. Beat well, and add four tablespoonfuls of Sherry
11. Put the pudding into a freezer to freeze and ripen, and let it stand covered with a carpet for four or five hours, or till frozen, renewing the ice and salt and draining off water as necessary
