## Ingredients

- 8 Ounces of Scalded or Split Pistachio Kernels
- 6 Ounces of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla
- A Few Drops of Prepared Cochineal
- Small bits of granulated sugar
- Seeded raisins or cleaned currants

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar till brittle, and then add the Pistachio Kernels or Peanuts and the Cochineal
2. Add the flavoring and mix gently
3. Then pour the Nougat out on a marble slab, slightly buttered
4. Flatten out the Nougat by means of a hard lemon slightly rubbed with butter to prevent sticking
5. Roll to about one-sixth of an inch in thickness, and before it cools sprinkle all over with small bits of granulated sugar, free from dust, and seeded raisins or cleaned currants
6. Press these lightly in with the fingers, and then divide the sheets of Nougat while hot into small diamond shapes, about two and a half inches long and one in width
