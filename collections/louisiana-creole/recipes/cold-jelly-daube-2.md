## Ingredients

- Four lbs. round of beef
- salt
- pepper
- a little garlic
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 clove
- a tablespoonful of vinegar
- a small piece of lard
- 3 pigs feet
- 8 calves feet
- one carrot
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Season beef well with salt, pepper, a little garlic, 1 bay leaf, and 1 clove
2. Lard well through and through
3. Sprinkle with a tablespoonful of vinegar, and let it stand for 24 hours
4. Put the daube in a pot with a small piece of lard
5. Cover with heavy cover on a slow fire, and let it smother for about 4 hours, until thoroughly done
6. Take off the cover, let it fry a light brown, then remove from the fire
7. In a separate pot, have 3 pigs feet and 8 calves feet boiling steadily till they leave the bone, skimming well at the first boil
8. Add one carrot to boil about 1 hour, then slice in length and place at bottom of bowl when the daube is to be served
9. Break 2 eggs, whites and shells to be beaten together and boiled with jelly briskly, then strain through thick flannel or cloth
10. Mix the daube gravy with jelly; let it rest about 1/2 an hour, then pour slowly over the daube
11. Set it in a cold place, and when cold, take the lard from the top with a spoon
12. With a round edge knife detach carefully the daube and turn into a round plate
13. Place the plate on top of the bowl and turn it over to avoid breaking the jelly
