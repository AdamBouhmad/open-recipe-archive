## Ingredients

- 100 River Shrimp
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Tomato Catsup
- 3 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Boil the shrimp and pick.
2. Put them into a salad dish.
3. Season well with black pepper and salt and a dash of Cayenne.
4. Add two tablespoonfuls of tomato catsup to every half pint of shrimps.
5. Garnish with lettuce leaves and hard-boiled egg and serve.
