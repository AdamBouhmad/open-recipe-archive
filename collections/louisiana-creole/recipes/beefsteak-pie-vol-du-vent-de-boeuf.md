## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cold Cooked Meat
- 2 Slices of Breakfast Bacon
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 3/4 Dozen Potatoes
- Thyme, Bay Leaf and Parsley
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Taste
- Pie Crust

## Directions

1. Make a nice pie crust.
2. Line a baking pan with this, and bake in the oven.
3. Cut the meat very fine, into dice, and season well, rubbing with the minced thyme, parsley, bay leaf, and salt and pepper.
4. Stew the meat as in Ragout de Veau a la Bourgeoise.
5. Place in the pan.
6. Dot the top with bits of butter, and place over all a layer of pie crust, decorating the edges nicely.
7. Bake to a nice brown.
8. Serve in the dish in which it was cooked, with any left-over sauce spread over the slices.
