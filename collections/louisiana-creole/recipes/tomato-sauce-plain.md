## Ingredients

- tomatoes (twelve)
- crackers (three pounded)
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and slice twelve tomatoes, pick out the seeds;
2. add three pounded crackers, salt and pepper;
3. stir twenty minutes and serve.
