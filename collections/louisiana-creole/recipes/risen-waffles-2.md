## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Flour
- 1½ Quarts of Milk
- ⅜ Cake of Compressed Yeast
- 3 Eggs
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Melted Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Scald the milk, add the butter, and let it grow tepid.
2. Sift the flour and salt together, and add the milk and butter, and finally the yeast.
3. Beat all continuously for five or ten minutes, and then cover well and set in a warm place for about two hours.
4. Beat the whites and the yolks of the eggs separately, and add first the yolks to the batter, after it has risen well, and then add the whites.
5. Beat in thoroughly, and set to rise again for about a quarter of an hour.
6. Then beat lightly and pour into a pitcher.
7. Prepare the waffle irons as indicated above, and proceed to bake in the same manner.
