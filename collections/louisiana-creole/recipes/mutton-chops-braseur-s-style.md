## Ingredients

- 6 Mutton Chops
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Juice of 1 Lemon
- 3 Shallots, Chopped Fine
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Select six thick chops from the loin of the mutton.
2. Trim neatly and season well with salt and black pepper and a dash of Cayenne.
3. Rub lightly with butter on either side and broil on a hot charcoal fire.
4. Have ready a hot dish and pour over the chops a sauce of melted butter, seasoned nicely with salt and pepper, the juice of one lemon and three minced shallots.
5. Serve hot.
