## Ingredients

- ½ pound of almonds blanched and pounded very fine
- rose or orange flower water
- yolks of 8 eggs
- whites of 4 eggs
- ½ lb. of loaf sugar
- 3 ounces of wheat flour

## Directions

1. Beat almonds with rose or orange flower water
2. Beat the yolks and whites until very light
3. Add to the almonds
4. Beat in ½ lb. of loaf sugar
5. Continue to beat while the oven is heating
6. When ready, add 3 ounces of wheat flour, which must be stirred in and not beaten
7. Butter the pans and fill them half full
8. Sprinkle a little sugar over them as you put them in the oven
9. Bake in a moderate oven
