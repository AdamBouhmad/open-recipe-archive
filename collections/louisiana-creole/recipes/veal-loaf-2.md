## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of veal
- 2 slices of salt pork
- 2 eggs
- 2 Boston crackers
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 2 teaspoonfuls of pepper
- 1 nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop veal and salt pork fine
2. Beat 2 eggs well
3. Roll 2 Boston crackers fine
4. Mix veal, salt pork, eggs, crackers, salt, pepper, and nutmeg well together
5. Bake from 2.5 to 3 hours
6. Bake well and serve cold sliced
