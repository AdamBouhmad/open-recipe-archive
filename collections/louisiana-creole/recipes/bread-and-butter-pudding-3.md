## Ingredients

- bread
- butter
- citron
- raisins
- sweetmeats
- 6 eggs
- 1 pint of milk
- salt
- nutmeg
- 1 spoonful of rose water
- sugar to the taste

## Directions

1. Cut the bread in thin slices, butter them and put a layer into a well buttered dish
2. strew citron and raisins, or sweetmeats, over it
3. then another layer of bread and fruit, and so on until the dish is filled
4. beat 6 eggs with 1 pint of milk, a little salt, nutmeg, and a spoonful of rose water
5. sweeten to the taste, and pour it over the bread
6. let it soak 1 or 2 hours before baking
7. bake 1 hour
