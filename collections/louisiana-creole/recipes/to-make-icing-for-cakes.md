## Ingredients

- whites of two eggs
- a quarter of a pound of white sugar, ground fine like flour
- extract of lemon or vanilla

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of two eggs to a froth
2. add to them a quarter of a pound of white sugar, ground fine like flour
3. flavor with extract of lemon or vanilla
4. beat it until it is light and very white
5. Beat the frosting until it may be spread smoothly on the cake
