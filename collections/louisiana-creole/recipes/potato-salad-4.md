## Ingredients

- boiled potatoes (Irish)
- 5 large hard boiled eggs
- 4 potted ham
- 2 tablespoons sweet pickle
- 2 tablespoons salt and pepper
- 2 tablespoons of Prince of Wales salad sauce
- 2 yolks (raw) and 1 cooked
- 1 tablespoon Worcester sauce
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- pepper
- sweet oil about 1 pint
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Slice boiled potatoes and hard boiled eggs
2. Mix potatoes, eggs, potted ham, sweet pickle, salt and pepper thoroughly before making the dressing
3. For the dressing, stir the oil into the raw yolk as you do for mayonnaise dressing
4. Add 2 tablespoons of Prince of Wales salad sauce
5. Add the cooked yolk
6. Add 1 tablespoon Worcester sauce
7. Add 1 teaspoon sugar and pepper to taste
8. Mix all other ingredients
9. Pour the dressing on the potatoes, 1 spoonful or more at a time
10. Mix well
11. Squeeze a few drops of lemon juice
12. Add the whites heated stiff
13. Garnish with eggs, pickles, etc., according to your taste
