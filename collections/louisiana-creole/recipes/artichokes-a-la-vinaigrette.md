## Ingredients

- Fine, Tender Young Artichokes
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Vinegar
- 1 Shallot
- The Yolk of a Hard-Boiled Egg
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Prepare and boil the artichokes as in recipe for French Artichokes Boiled.
2. Take the yolk of a hard-boiled egg, dilute it with two teaspoonfuls of vinegar, blend well, season to taste with salt and pepper.
3. Chop the shallot very, very fine, add to the mixture, and then add gradually three tablespoonfuls of Olive Oil.
4. Mix all together well.
5. Place the artichokes on a folded napkin on a dish, and send to the table with the sauce in a separate dish.
