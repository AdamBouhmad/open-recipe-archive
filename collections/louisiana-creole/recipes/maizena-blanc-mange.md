## Ingredients

- quarter of a pound of maizena
- three pints of milk
- quarter of a pound of white sugar
- lemon rind
- cream or stewed fruit for serving

## Directions

1. Put two and a half pints of the milk on to boil
2. Wet the maizena with the remaining half pint of milk
3. When the milk boils add a quarter of a pound of white sugar and some lemon rind, sliced or grated
4. Let this boil a little, then stir in the mixed maizena
5. When cooked five minutes, pour it into moulds or bowls
6. Wet the bowls first with cold water to prevent the jelly sticking to the sides
7. When firm and cold, eat it with cream or any kind of stewed fruit
