## Ingredients

- a quart of flour
- three eggs
- three-fourths of a pound of sugar
- half a cup of shortening
- a teaspoonful of soda
- buttermilk
- boiling lard for frying

## Directions

1. Mix flour, eggs, sugar, and shortening
2. Add a teaspoonful of soda and mix to a soft dough with buttermilk
3. Roll out and cut them
4. Fry in boiling lard
