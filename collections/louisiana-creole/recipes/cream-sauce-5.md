## Ingredients

- one quarter of a pound of butter
- one small tablespoonful of wheat flour
- one teaspoonful of chopped parsley
- one teaspoonful of young onions, or eschalots, chopped fine
- one saltspoonful of salt
- one saltspoonful of pepper
- one grated nutmeg
- one glass of cream, or rich milk

## Directions

1. Put a quarter of a pound of butter in a stew-pan, with a small tablespoonful of wheat flour, a teaspoonful of chopped parsley, and the same of young onions, or eschalots, chopped fine
2. Add a saltspoonful of salt, and the same of pepper, and a grated nutmeg
3. Mix these well together
4. Add a glass of cream, or rich milk
5. Set it over the fire, and stir it with a silver spoon until it is ready to boil; if it is too thick, add more milk
6. This sauce should be stirred for fifteen minutes
