## Ingredients

- 1 coffee cupful of rice
- 1 quart of new milk
- a large spoonful of butter
- a little salt
- grated nutmeg
- a little ground cinnamon
- 1 pint more of new milk
- 1 lb. of raisins

## Directions

1. Wash a coffee cupful of rice well
2. Put it into 1 quart of new milk over night to soak
3. In the morning add a large spoonful of butter, a little salt, grated nutmeg, and a little ground cinnamon, and 1 pint more of new milk
4. Put it into the oven, and when it is warm, stir the pudding without removing it from the oven
5. Stone 1 lb. of raisins, and add at this time
6. Let it cook until the rice is perfectly done
