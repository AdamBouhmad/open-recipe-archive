## Ingredients

- one quart of sifted flour
- a teacupful of sugar
- half a cup of butter
- one cupful of raisins
- two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar
- one teaspoonful of soda
- two tablespoonfuls of mixed spices
- cold water

## Directions

1. To one quart of sifted flour, add a teacupful of sugar, half a cup of butter, one cupful of raisins, two teaspoonfuls of cream of tartar, and one of soda, two tablespoonfuls of mixed spices
2. rub thoroughly together the flour, cream of tartar, soda and butter
3. stir in sufficient cold water to make a stiff batter
4. add the spices and raisins
5. pour it into a small tin pan
6. bake one hour
