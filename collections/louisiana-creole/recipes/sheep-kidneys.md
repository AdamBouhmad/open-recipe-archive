## Ingredients

- 6 Kidneys
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Lemon juice
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Slice the kidneys very thin and wash well, then scald and wipe dry.
2. Season well.
3. Pass a skewer through each kidney and brush with melted butter.
4. Place on a double broiler and cook for five minutes, allowing two and a half minutes to each side.
5. Place on a hot dish and pour over melted butter and a little lemon juice.
6. Garnish nicely with parsley and serve hot.
