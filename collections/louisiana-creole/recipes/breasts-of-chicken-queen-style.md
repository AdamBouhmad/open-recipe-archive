## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 Breasts of Chicken
- 2 Truffles
- 4 Mushrooms
- 2 Ounces of Chicken Forcemeat
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Gill of Madeira Wine
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Mushroom Liquor
- 1 Pint of Hot Sauce a la Reine

## Directions

1. Carefully remove the small filet found under the breast of each chicken and set aside on a dish for further use
2. Take a small, sharp knife and make an incision three inches long and one inch deep in the inner side of each breast
3. Season lightly with salt and pepper
4. Stuff each breast in the incision made, using two ounces of chicken forcemeat mixed with two truffles and four mushrooms, all finely minced
5. Put a tablespoonful of butter in a stewpan and lay the breasts in gently
6. Take each small filet, press gently into shape, and flatten
7. Make several small incisions and place within a fine slice of truffle, about an inch in diameter
8. Carefully lay on top of each breast lengthwise
9. Brush lightly with melted butter
10. Pour into the pan, but not over the breasts, the wine and mushroom liquor
11. Cover tightly and set in the oven for fifteen minutes
12. Send to the table hot
