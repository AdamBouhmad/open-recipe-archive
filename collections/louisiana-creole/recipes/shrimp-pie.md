## Ingredients

- picked shrimp
- 2 large slices of bread
- 2 glasses of wine
- 1 large spoonful of butter
- pepper
- salt
- nutmeg and mace

## Directions

1. Have a large plate of picked shrimp
2. Cut off the crust from 2 large slices of bread, and make the crumb into a paste with 2 glasses of wine and 1 large spoonful of butter
3. Add as much pepper, salt, nutmeg and mace, as you like
4. Mix the shrimps with the bread
5. Bake in a dish or shells
