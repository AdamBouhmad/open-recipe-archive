## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. veal
- broth from boiling veal
- ½ loaf of bread
- 3 eggs
- 2 heaping tablespoons of butter
- part of a nutmeg or powdered mace
- salt and pepper
- parsley
- thyme
- sage
- onion

## Directions

1. Boil veal till done
2. Take out the bones, skins and strings
3. Chop very fine
4. Take enough of the broth the veal was boiled in and ½ loaf of bread to make a panada as thick as mush
5. Stir into this while on the fire, 3 eggs and 2 heaping tablespoons of butter, till the whole looks like thin scrambled eggs
6. Turn this out on the meat, stirring it well
7. Add part of a nutmeg or powdered mace and salt and pepper, in fact, any seasoning to taste
8. Add a little parsley, thyme and sage chopped very fine, and a little onion chopped and fried
