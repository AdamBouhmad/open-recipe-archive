## Ingredients

- eight pounds of beef or veal
- one pound of ham or bacon
- eight onions
- pepper, salt, and sweet herbs to taste
- the liquor of a boiled turtle
- wine
- spice
- cayenne
- catsup
- the flesh of the turtle, prepared as in recipe No. 1
- sliced lemon
- whole pepper
- a bunch of parsley
- two leeks sliced
- a quarter of a pound of good sweet butter
- a tablespoonful of flour
- a lemon sliced thin

## Directions

1. Put on, at an early hour in the morning, eight pounds of beef or veal, one pound of ham or bacon, eight onions, with pepper, salt, and sweet herbs to taste
2. Make a rich soup of this, and add to it the liquor of a boiled turtle
3. Season very high with wine, spice, cayenne, and catsup
4. Put in the flesh of the turtle, prepared as in recipe No. 1—do not use the eyes or tongue
5. Let this boil
