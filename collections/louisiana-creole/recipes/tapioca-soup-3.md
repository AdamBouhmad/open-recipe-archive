## Ingredients

- 4 Ounces of Tapioca
- 3 Quarts of Broth

## Directions

1. Soak the tapioca a few hours
2. Add to three quarts of broth about forty minutes before serving
3. Stir frequently in the broth while boiling
4. Serve hot
