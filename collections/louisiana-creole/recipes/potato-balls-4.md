## Ingredients

- mashed potatoes
- the yolk of an egg
- flour
- egg
- bread crumbs
- very nice lard

## Directions

1. Mix mashed potatoes with the yolk of an egg
2. Roll them into balls in a little flour, with egg and bread crumbs
3. Fry them in very nice lard or brown them in a hot oven
