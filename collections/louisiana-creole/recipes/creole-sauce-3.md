## Ingredients

- 2 Tomatoes
- 6 Shallots
- 1 Chopped Sweet Pepper
- 1 Glass Sherry
- Salt and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Make a Tomato Sauce quite brown.
2. Add the chopped shallots and sweet pepper, and when these are browned, add one wine glass full of Sherry wine, seasoning highly.
3. Serve with meats.
