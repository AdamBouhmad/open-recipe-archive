## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sweet Almonds
- The White of 1 Egg
- 12 Ounces of Powdered Sugar

## Directions

1. Blanch, skin, wash and dry one pound of sweet almonds, and then cut them into very small pieces.
2. Add the white of one egg, beaten to a thick froth, and twelve ounces of powdered sugar.
3. When well-mixed drop upon sheets of white paper, and bake to a very light color in a moderate oven.
