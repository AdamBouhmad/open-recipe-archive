## Ingredients

- yolks of 3 eggs
- wheat flour
- whites of 3 eggs
- apples
- brandy
- sugar
- lard

## Directions

1. Beat yolks of 3 eggs with wheat flour to a batter
2. Beat whites separately and add to it
3. Pare apples, core, and cut them in slices
4. Lay them in a bowl in brandy and sugar about 3 hours before dressing
5. Dip each piece in batter and fry in lard
6. Sprinkle white sugar over them
