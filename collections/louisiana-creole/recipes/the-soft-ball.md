## Ingredients

- 3 Pounds of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water

## Directions

1. Keep the sugar boiling
2. Dip the finger into iced water and then into the boiling sugar and immediately into the water again
3. If the sugar has enough consistency to form a small ball when rolled between the finger and the thumb, the degree is reached
