## Ingredients

- Six eggs, yolks only
- 2½ dozen ladies' fingers
- 6 ounces of granulated sugar
- ½ ounce of gelatine
- 1 pint of pure sweet milk
- 1 pint of thick sweet cream
- ice and salt
- tablespoonful of extract of vanilla

## Directions

1. Put the gelatine in the milk cold and put on to boil in a bain Marie
2. Stir now and then
3. Beat the yolks of the eggs with the sugar
4. When the milk comes to a boil, add the eggs and sugar to the milk
5. Stir constantly to let the custard thicken well and to prevent curdling
6. Strain through a swiff muslin cloth and set aside in a cool place to get cold and to thicken
7. Put cream in a pan and put this in a larger pan which has been filled with ice and salt
8. When cream gets very cold, whip it up with a fork or egg beater until it is very light
9. Line mould with ladies' fingers
10. Gradually slip the cream into the custard, which must be quite thick, until all is used and the custard is free from lumps
11. Flavor with a tablespoonful of extract of vanilla
12. Pour the mixture into the mould
13. Pack in ice and salt to stand for not less than 2 hours
