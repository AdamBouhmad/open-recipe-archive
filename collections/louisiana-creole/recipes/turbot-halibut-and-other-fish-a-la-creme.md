## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- 1 quart of cream or milk
- yolks of 4 eggs
- 1 onion
- bunch of parsley
- 3 tablespoonfuls of flour
- 1 pound clarified butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Boil fish with salt and separate from bones
2. Boil 1 quart of cream or milk with the yolks of 4 eggs beaten in
3. Add 1 onion and a bunch of parsley
4. When boiled, strain the cream and heat again
5. When boiling stir in 3 tablespoonfuls of flour, perfectly smooth
6. Add 1 pound clarified butter
7. Butter a deep dish and place first a layer of fish, then of sauce, until it is full, having a layer of sauce on top
8. Cover with bread crumbs and bake 1 hour
