## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Water
- Essence or Liquor to Taste

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar
2. Remove the saucepan from the fire, and immerse it in a pan containing cold water, so that the sugar may cool quickly
3. Place a lid over the pan, to prevent the sugar forming a crust on top by contact with the air
4. Roll the sugar into balls or pellets, flatten out and work the flavoring into them
5. Set them in a box or mold and thinly cover their surface with a little starch powder to prevent forming lumps
6. Dry in a moderately warm closet for about fifteen hours
7. As you remove the bonbons place them on a dry sieve and brush off any remains of starch with a long-haired soft brush
