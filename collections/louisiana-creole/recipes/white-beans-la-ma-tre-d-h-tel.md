## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of White Beans
- 2 Sprigs of Minced Thyme and Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Sauce à la Maître d'Hôtel

## Directions

1. Prepare the white beans and cook in exactly the same manner as red beans à la Maître d'Hôtel.
2. Before adding the butter, add a minced sprig of thyme, bay leaf and parsley, and salt and pepper to taste.
3. Let them simmer for a few minutes on the fire.
4. Take off and add the juice of a lemon.
