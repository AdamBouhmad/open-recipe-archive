## Ingredients

- the weight of 10 eggs in very fine sugar
- the weight of 6 eggs in flour

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks with the flour
2. Beat the whites alone
3. By degrees, mix the whites and flour with the other ingredients
4. Bake in a quick oven
