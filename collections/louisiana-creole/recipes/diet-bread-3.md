## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of flour
- 1 lb. of sugar
- a little mace
- grated nutmeg
- a little rose water
- 6 eggs
- a pinch of salt

## Directions

1. Sift the flour, sugar, mace and nutmeg together
2. Stir well together
3. Beat the eggs very light, add salt
4. Mix all the ingredients well
5. Bake it slowly in a pan
