## Ingredients

- One-half yeast cake
- ¼ cup cold water (whole cake in cold weather)
- 1 quart lukewarm water
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- ½ cup lard
- 1 tablespoon salt
- flour

## Directions

1. beat hard with hand
2. add flour to make thick dough (use gallon tin bucket, with tight fitting cover)
3. when risen to top, add flour to make dough stiff, in bucket
4. knead on board 15 minutes, use as little flour as possible, keeping dough soft
5. make in loaves
6. cover to rise
7. when to the top of pans, bake in moderately hot oven
