## Ingredients

- 6 Beet Roots
- 2 Quarts of Water
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut off the beet tops and save for boiling or purée or gumbo.
2. Soak the beets in cold fresh water, and wash well, taking off every particle of earth that may adhere.
3. Wash them carefully, without scraping them.
4. Put the beets into a pot of cold water, covering well, and boil until tender.
5. Set them to cool.
6. When cold, slice nicely and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste, and add vinegar, and set aside for an hour, for the vinegar to penetrate thoroughly.
7. Serve as a salad.
