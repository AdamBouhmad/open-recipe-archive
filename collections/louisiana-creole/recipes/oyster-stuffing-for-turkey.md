## Ingredients

- three or four dozen nice plump oysters
- a tumblerful of bread crumbs
- a tumblyful of nice beef suet
- three eggs
- salt
- pepper
- a little butter
- a teaspoonful of mace
- some cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Take three or four dozen nice plump oysters, wash and beard them
2. Add to them a tumblerful of bread crumbs
3. Chop up a tumblerful of nice beef suet
4. Mix together, and moisten with three eggs
5. Season with salt, pepper, a little butter, a teaspoonful of mace, and some cayenne pepper
6. Roll force-meat into cakes, and fry them
7. They are pretty laid around a turkey or chicken
