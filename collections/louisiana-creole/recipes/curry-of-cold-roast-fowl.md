## Ingredients

- two large onions
- two apples
- two ounces of butter
- a dessertspoonful of curry powder or paste
- half pint of gravy or soup-stock
- one spoonful of lemon juice
- two tomatoes
- boiled rice

## Directions

1. Fry the fowl and the onions in butter to a light brown color.
2. Stew the apples, or fry them also.
3. Put all, onions, apples, gravy and fowl, with the tomatoes and lemon juice into a stewing pan.
4. Let it stew thirty minutes.
5. Serve with boiled rice. If curry paste is used instead of curry powder, no lemon is required.
