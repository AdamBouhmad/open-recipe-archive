## Ingredients

- salsify
- cold water
- olive oil
- French vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- eggs
- milk
- flour
- hog's lard
- parsley

## Directions

1. Scrape the salsify, throw it for a few moments into cold water
2. Parboil it, drain it and cut into lengths of three inches
3. Steep until cold in a bowl with two tablespoonfuls of olive oil, one of French vinegar, pepper and salt
4. Let it remain in this, occasionally turning it until ready to fry it
5. Make a batter with eggs, milk, and flour
6. Dip the salsify in this batter, and fry in hog's lard
7. Fry parsley with it and serve
