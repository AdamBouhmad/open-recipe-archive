## Ingredients

- 6 large apples
- 1 pint water
- juice of ½ a lemon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut 6 large apples in half, peel and take out the seeds; stew them in 1 pint of water, the juice of ½ a lemon and sugar
2. When the apples are sufficiently tender take them out and arrange them in the dessert dish; let the syrup keep on boiling till it is clear and rich; then pour it over the apples
