## Ingredients

- Two pounds of beef chopped fine
- one peck of apples
- two pounds of raisins
- two pounds of currants
- one pound of citron
- one-half pound of suet
- three pounds of sugar
- powdered cinnamon, cloves and nutmeg, a spoonful each
- a bottle of champagne cider

## Directions

1. Moisten with a bottle of champagne cider
2. When you bake the pies, place a spoonful of butter on each pie
3. do not put butter in the jar with the meat
