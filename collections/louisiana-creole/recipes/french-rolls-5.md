## Ingredients

- 1 quart of lukewarm milk
- 1 quart of flour
- 2 ounces of butter
- 2 eggs
- a teaspoonful of salt
- 6 spoonfuls of yeast

## Directions

1. Melt the butter
2. Add to it the eggs and salt
3. When cool, stir in the yeast, and flour sufficient to mould it
4. Set it in a warm place
5. When light, mould it into small rolls
6. Lay them on flat buttered tins, and let them remain 20 minutes before baking
