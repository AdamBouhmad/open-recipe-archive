## Ingredients

- 8 lbs. round of beef
- strips of pork
- a small piece of onion
- garlic
- salt and pepper
- 1 pint of vinegar
- water
- 1 ounce of gelatine

## Directions

1. Make incisions in the beef
2. Put strips of pork, a small piece of onion and garlic, salt and pepper into the incisions
3. Pat it away for several hours
4. Pour over it 1 pint of vinegar and let it remain all night
5. In the morning let it brown a little
6. Fill the saucepan full of water and cook it slowly 4 or 5 hours before taking it up
7. Add 1 ounce of gelatine dissolved in a little cold water
8. Pour all in a mould
9. In the morning skim off the grease
