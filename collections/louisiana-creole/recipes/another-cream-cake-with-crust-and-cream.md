## Ingredients

- three-quarter pint of water
- half a pint of butter
- three-quarters of a pound of flour
- eight eggs
- a teaspoonful of dry soda
- two pints of milk
- one cup of flour
- two cups of sugar
- four eggs
- rose water

## Directions

1. For crust: boil the water and butter together
2. while boiling stir in the flour
3. take it off and let it cool
4. then add your eggs (beaten separately), and a teaspoonful of dry soda
5. Use about a spoonful of the crust for each puff
6. bake on tins for about twenty minutes
7. For cream: while the milk is boiling add your flour, sugar and eggs (previously well beaten together)
8. let it cook until it begins to thicken
9. take it off, and flavor with rose water
10. When crust is done cut it open and put in the cream
