## Ingredients

- a loin of lamb
- butter
- anchovies
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut a loin of lamb into thin slices
2. Beat them and lay them in water to take out the blood
3. Fry them in butter
4. Make the sauce with butter, anchovies and lemon
