## Ingredients

- 6 ounces of almonds
- 1 ounce of bitter almonds
- 8 ounces of loaf sugar
- 3 pints of filtered river water
- 1/2 gill of orange flower water

## Directions

1. Blanch and peel the almonds, and soak them in cold water for several hours
2. Pound them thoroughly into a pulp, adding as you pound a half gill of orange flower water
3. Put this into an earthen vessel with eight ounces of the finest and whitest loaf sugar
4. Add three pints of clear, filtered water, and then stir all well together
5. Return to the mortar
6. Pound well and strain
7. Stir well and set the vessel on ice
8. At the end of an hour it will be ready for use
