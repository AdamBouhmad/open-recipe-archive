## Ingredients

- one-half pound of gum arabic
- one pint of water
- half a pound of fine sugar
- whites of four eggs
- powdered starch
- flavoring

## Directions

1. Dissolve gum arabic in water; strain it
2. Add sugar and place over the fire, stirring constantly till the sugar is dissolved and all is the consistency of honey
3. Add gradually the whites of four eggs, well beaten
4. Stir the mixture till it becomes somewhat thin and does not adhere to the finger
5. Pour all into a pan slightly dusted with powdered starch
6. Flavor to the taste, just before pouring out to cool
7. When cool divide into small squares
