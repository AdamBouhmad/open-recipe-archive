## Ingredients

- 8 Dozen Tomatoes
- A Bain Marie

## Directions

1. Pour boiling water over the Tomatoes to loosen the skins.
2. Then skin the Tomatoes, and bring to a hard boil for twenty minutes.
3. Then drain and can as quickly as possible, excluding all air.
4. Set in a bain-marie to cool, and keep in a cool, dark place.
