## Ingredients

- a pint of meal
- two quarts of flour
- salt
- warm water, or milk
- a cup of yeast
- a little lard

## Directions

1. Make a pint of meal into mush, then pour it onto two quarts of flour
2. when cool, add a little salt and warm water, or milk, and a cup of yeast
3. work it with a spoon, and set it by to rise until morning
4. Knead it well; yes, very well, and make it into loaves
5. place in the pan, and when light, bake it
6. Add a little lard, if liked
