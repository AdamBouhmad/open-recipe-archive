## Ingredients

- oysters
- pounded crackers
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- milk

## Directions

1. Procure any quantity of oysters desired, and place in a baking dish
2. Put alternate layers of oysters and pounded crackers
3. Season each layer with salt, pepper and butter
4. When filled, pour on enough milk to soak the crackers
5. And bake forty minutes
6. Serve hot
