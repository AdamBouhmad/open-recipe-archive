## Ingredients

- puff paste
- butter
- jam
- eggs for custard
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Line a deep pie dish with puff paste, having first buttered it thoroughly.
2. Place on this a layer of jam, then a layer of custard, then jam, then custard, until the dish is nearly full, leaving the custard layer at the top.
3. Bake for twenty minutes in a moderate oven.
4. Let the pudding cool.
5. Beat up the whites of the eggs that were used for the custard into a stiff whip with a little powdered sugar.
6. Pile the whip on as high as possible, and serve.
