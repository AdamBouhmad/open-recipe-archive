## Ingredients

- six ounces of stoned raisins
- six ounces washed and dried currants
- six ounces of bread crumbs
- six ounces of suet
- six eggs
- half a nutmeg
- half a lemon
- half a glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix six ounces of stoned raisins, six ounces washed and dried currants, six ounces of bread crumbs, six ounces of suet and six eggs
2. Flavor with half a nutmeg, half a lemon and half a glass of brandy
3. Mix all these ingredients together
4. Put the pudding into a mould, or floured cloth, and boil three hours
