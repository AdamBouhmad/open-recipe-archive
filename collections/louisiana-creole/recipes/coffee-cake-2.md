## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- one cup of molasses
- one cup of strong coffee
- two eggs
- five cups of flour
- one teaspoonful of soda
- one cup of currants
- one cup of raisins
- spice to taste

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients and mix well
2. Bake
