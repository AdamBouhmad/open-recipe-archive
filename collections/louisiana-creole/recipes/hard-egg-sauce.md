## Ingredients

- Three or two hard-boiled eggs
- Finely-minced parsley

## Directions

1. Make a White Sauce.
2. Add three or two hard-boiled eggs, chopped, but not too fine, and a little finely-minced parsley as a garnish.
3. This sauce is served with boiled fish or boiled chicken or other fowl.
