## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Cafe Noir (Black Coffee)
- 4 Eggs
- 4 Cups of Flour
- 1 Cup of Butter
- 1 Cup of Molasses
- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Seeded Raisins
- 1 Cup of Currants, Washed and Dried
- 1 Grated Nutmeg
- ¾ Teaspoonful of Ground Cinnamon
- ¾ Teaspoonful of Cloves
- ¾ Teaspoonful of Soda, Dissolved in Warm Water

## Directions

1. Wash and cream the butter
2. Add the sugar and beat till very light
3. Add the yolks of the eggs
4. Beat them into the butter and sugar till very light
5. Add the molasses, and mix thoroughly
6. Take the seeded currants and raisins and blend them with one-half of the flour and add gradually
7. Add by degrees the remainder of the flour, beating vigorously
8. Add the coffee, and mix well
9. Add the soda, dissolved in hot water
10. Add the spices, mix well, and finally add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
11. Mix thoroughly but lightly, divide into two parts, set in two greased paper-lined tins, and bake from forty-five minutes to one hour in a moderate oven
