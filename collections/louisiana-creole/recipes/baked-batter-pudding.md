## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 6 eggs
- 9 tablespoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Beat eggs separately and very light
2. Stir flour in gradually while the milk is boiling hot
3. Bake 1/2 hour in a quick oven
4. Serve with wine and sugar sauce
5. This pudding must be sent to the table as soon as it is done
