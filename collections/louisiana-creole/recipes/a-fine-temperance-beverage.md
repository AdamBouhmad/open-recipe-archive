## Ingredients

- juice of a dozen lemons
- one pound and a half of double refined sugar
- a picked quart of raspberries or strawberries
- a ripe pineapple
- ice water

## Directions

1. To the juice of a dozen lemons put one pound and a half of double refined sugar, and a picked quart of raspberries or strawberries
2. Pare a ripe pineapple and slice it, put over it half a pound of sugar
3. Stir the lemon juice with the sugar
4. Crush in the berries slightly bruise the pineapple and chop it up in small pieces
5. Put the lemon juice in a large punch bowl
6. Add to it three quarts of ice water, then put in the strawberry and pineapple juice
7. Stir it until all the sugar is dissolved, and then set it on ice
8. Serve in punch glasses
