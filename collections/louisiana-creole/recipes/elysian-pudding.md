## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of pippins
- 1 lb. of sugar
- 1 lb. of butter
- 1 fresh lemon
- 6 eggs
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Grate 1 lb. of pippins
2. Stir to a cream 1 lb. of sugar, the same of butter
3. Add the grated apples and the rind of 1 fresh lemon
4. Whip very light 6 eggs
5. Beat all well together
6. Line a dish with puff paste
7. Pour in the batter, and bake 1/2 an hour
