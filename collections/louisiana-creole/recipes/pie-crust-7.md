## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Sifted Flour
- 1 1/2 Cups of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Salt
- Nearly a Cup of Tepid Water

## Directions

1. Rub the flour and butter very lightly together, being careful not to allow it to harden under touch.
2. Keep on kneading a little butter at a time into the flour till you have used up all and the dough feels greasy to the touch.
3. Always remember to add the salt before putting in the butter.
4. Add, little by little, the water, kneading gently, till you have a nice, soft dough, lifting out the portion that is wet and continuing to knead as you mix the flour and water.
5. Always be very careful in adding the water, and never wet the flour twice in the same place.
6. Mix all together lightly with your hands when all is moistened, using always your own good judgment in adding water.
7. Do not work the dough; simply mix lightly and thoroughly.
8. Dredge the board lightly with flour.
9. If you intend to make two pies, cut the dough into four parts.
