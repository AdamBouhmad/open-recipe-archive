## Ingredients

- 4 Layers of Sponge Cake
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Pulverized Sugar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Cream
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla

## Directions

1. Make a layer sponge cake
2. Prepare a filling by beating together the yolks of two eggs, one cup of pulverized sugar, two tablespoonfuls of cream, and one teaspoonful of Vanilla
3. Beat and mix thoroughly to a thick cream, and spread between the layers, with a layer of cake on top
