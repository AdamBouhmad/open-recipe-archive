## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Finely-Grated Green Corn
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 4 Eggs
- 3/4 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1 Cup of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful of Melted Butter

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs separately, and then add the yolks, well beaten, to the corn.
2. Add the salt, melted butter and the milk, and mix well.
3. Then add sufficient flour to make a thin batter, and finally the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
4. Bake on a hot griddle, butter generously, and serve very hot.
