## Ingredients

- chicken
- salad oil
- truffles
- parsley
- chives or small green eschalots
- garlic
- pepper
- salt
- button mushrooms
- brown gravy sauce
- lemon juice
- fried bread
- crayfish

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken up as for a fricassee
2. Put it in a sauce-pan with a wineglassful of salad oil
3. Allow it to cook rather briskly for twenty minutes
4. Put in with it a quarter of a pound of truffles cut up, a bunch of parsley, six chives or small green eschalots, a bruised clove of garlic, and pepper and salt
5. Let them stew for twenty minutes
6. Pour off the oil and take out the parsley
7. If only one chicken is used, throw in half a pint of button mushrooms, a ladleful of brown gravy sauce, and the juice of a lemon
8. Garnish this dish with pieces of fried bread and large crayfish
