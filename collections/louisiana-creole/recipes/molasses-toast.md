## Ingredients

- bread
- West India molasses

## Directions

1. Make a thick slice of very nice toast, evenly browned on both sides, but not the least burnt
2. Lay it in a pint bowl
3. Pour over it a small ½ pint of the best West India molasses, having stirred into the molasses a heaped tablespoonful
