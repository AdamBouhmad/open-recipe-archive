## Ingredients

- 4 ounces of rice, well washed
- 1½ pints of rich milk or cream
- 40 sweet almonds
- 6 bitter almonds
- grated rind of a lemon or a small piece of vanilla bean
- new milk
- 2 ounces of butter
- sugar to taste
- yolks of 8 eggs beaten very light
- 5 whites of eggs
- 1 tablespoonful of sifted sugar per egg white

## Directions

1. Put rice into a milk or rice boiler containing milk or cream and let them boil gently until the rice is swollen and quite soft
2. Blanch almonds and pound them into a smooth paste
3. Grate the rind of a lemon or vanilla bean into a small quantity of new milk and boil until soft
4. Put cooked rice into a dish or pan and when cooled throw into it butter and sugar, mixing well
5. Add the almonds, beating well
6. Add the yolks of 8 eggs beaten very light
7. Butter the dish it is to be baked in
8. Beat the 5 whites of the eggs in a very light whip and add to each a tablespoonful of sifted sugar
9. The moment the oven is ready, quickly lay over the pudding the icing of eggs and bake 15 minutes
10. Serve immediately
