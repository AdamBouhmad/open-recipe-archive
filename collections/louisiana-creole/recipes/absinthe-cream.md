## Ingredients

- 7 pints of brandy
- 1/4 pound of wormwood heads
- zest of 2 lemons
- 3 oranges, thinly sliced
- 6 pounds of sugar
- 3 pints of water

## Directions

1. Pound the wormwood and infuse it for two days in the brandy with the zest of the lemons or the sliced oranges
2. Distill the liquor to one-half the quantity
3. Dissolve six pounds of the finest white sugar in three pints of water, let it cool, and mix thoroughly with the brandy and wormwood
4. Strain it and put in tightly corked bottles
