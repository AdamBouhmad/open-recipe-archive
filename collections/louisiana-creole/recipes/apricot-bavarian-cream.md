## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Fresh Apricots or 1 Pint Can
- 1/2 Box of Gelatine, or 1/2 Pint
- 1 Pint of Cold Water
- 1 Pint of Cream

## Directions

1. Soak the gelatine and whip the cream
2. If the apricots are fresh, stew them and sweeten to taste
3. If canned, press them through a colander or sieve
4. Add the gelatine, which has been dissolved in a little boiling water, and then turn into a bowl
5. Stand upon cracked ice, and stir constantly till it begins to thicken, and then add the whipped cream stirring constantly till mixed well
6. Turn into a mold to harden and serve with whipped cream
