## Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoonful of Vinegar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Prepared Mustard
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Olive Oil
- The Yolk of 1 Egg, If Desired
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Blend the mustard and the oil, adding the latter, drop by drop at first, and then proceeding more confidently.
2. Whenever the dressing appears to be curdling, add a few drops of vinegar, and work rapidly till it becomes smooth again.
3. Add the salt and pepper, and when the dressing is finished, use it for celery salad, fish, tomatoes, potatoes, etc.
