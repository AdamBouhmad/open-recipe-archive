## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Meal
- ½ Teaspoonful of Salt
- Cold water
- Cracklings

## Directions

1. Mix together 1 pint of meal, a half teaspoonful of salt, and cold water enough to make a thick batter.
2. Mix the cracklings, already fried, in the batter.
3. Pour a large tablespoonful at a time on a griddle.
4. Fry to a golden brown.
5. Serve crisp, requiring no butter or other accompaniment.
