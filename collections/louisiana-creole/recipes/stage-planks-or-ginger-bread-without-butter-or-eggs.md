## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Molasses
- 1 Cup of Sour Milk
- 1 Tablespoonful of Ground Ginger
- 1 Gill of Lard
- 3 Cups of Flour
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Soda

## Directions

1. Melt the molasses, lard and ginger together and blend well.
2. When thoroughly melted and warmed, beat for about ten minutes.
3. Then dissolve the soda in a tablespoonful of boiling water and add to the molasses.
4. Mix it thoroughly, and then add the flour, using good judgment and adding just enough of the three cups of sifted flour to make a stiff batter.
5. Beat thoroughly and vigorously.
6. Have ready several greased, shallow pans; pour the mixture into them and bake for ten minutes in a quick oven.
