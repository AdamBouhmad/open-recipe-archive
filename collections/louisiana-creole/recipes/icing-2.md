## Ingredients

- one pound of powdered or flour sugar
- whites of four eggs

## Directions

1. Put the sugar to the eggs before you beat it at all
2. beat till it is stiff
3. Spread it on the cake with a wet knife, wetting it in cold water each time you use it
4. Set it in front of the stove to dry, or in an oven with the least particle of heat
5. The cake must be nearly cold
6. You can flavor the icing with rose, orange, or lemon; if the latter, add a very small portion of grated rind
