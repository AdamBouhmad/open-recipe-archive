## Ingredients

- half a pint of thick cream
- four eggs
- a little salt
- soda
- flour
- a cup of sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix half a pint of thick cream, four eggs, a little salt, soda, and flour enough to make a stiff batter
2. If too stiff to drop nicely, thin it with a cup of sweet milk
3. Drop on tins
4. Bake in a quick oven
