## Ingredients

- two pounds of light brown sugar
- one teacup of water
- two tablespoonfuls of butter
- one tablespoonful of vinegar
- two tablespoonfuls of flavoring extract

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of light brown sugar, one teacup of water, two tablespoonfuls of butter, one of vinegar, and two of flavoring extract
2. Dissolve the sugar in the water but do not stir it
3. Set it on to boil and let it boil briskly for twenty minutes
4. Try it by dropping a spoonful in a glass of cold water
5. If cooked enough to pull, butter some dishes and pour it into them
6. When cool enough to handle, pull it until it becomes as white as cream
