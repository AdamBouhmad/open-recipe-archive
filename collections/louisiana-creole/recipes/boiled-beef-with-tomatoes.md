## Ingredients

- ½ Dozen Tomatoes
- 2 Cups of Bouillon
- ¼ Teaspoonful of Flour
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme and Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- Butter
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Take a half dozen fine, ripe tomatoes, and parboil them in butter, being careful not to let them burn.
2. Add a pinch of flour and two good cups of bouillon, a little salt and pepper, a clove of garlic, a sprig of parsley, thyme and bay leaf.
3. After two hours, take out the tomatoes and allow the beef to cook a few minutes in the sauce.
4. Then serve on a flat dish, arranging the tomatoes around the beef and under each tomato put a nice piece of buttered toast.
