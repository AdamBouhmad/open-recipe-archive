## Ingredients

- 9 Eggs
- 1 Pound of White Pulverized Sugar
- 3/4 Pound of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful of Noyau Extract
- 1 Cup of Preserved Peaches
- Transparent Icing to Garnish

## Directions

1. Prepare the drop cakes as directed in Bouchés de Dames
2. Place halves of peaches, preserved in syrup, over them
3. Gloss over with Transparent Icing, flavored with Noyau, and slightly tinged with Carmine
4. Do not allow any excess of moisture from the preserves
5. The syrup should become entirely absorbed into the bouchés before you attempt to gloss it with the icing
