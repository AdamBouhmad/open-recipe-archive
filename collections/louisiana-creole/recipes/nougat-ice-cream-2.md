## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Nougat
- 8 Eggs
- % Pound of Sugar
- 1 Quart of Cream
- 3 Drops of Essence of Peach Kernels
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Make some Nougat using about a pint
2. Bruise and mash it, and blend with one gill of orange flower water
3. Add to this the sugar and the yolks of the eggs, beaten to a thick cream
4. Set the milk to boil
5. Take from the fire and add the above ingredients, stirring well, and adding at the end the Essence of Peach Kernels, and the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
6. Then freeze in the manner above directed
