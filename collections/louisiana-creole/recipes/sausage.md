## Ingredients

- Bouilli from the day before
- Chopped parsley
- A few spices
- Salt
- Cayenne pepper
- Beef extract from bouillon
- 1 Pound of pork
- A few truffles
- 1 Wineglass of Madeira
- Entrails

## Directions

1. Take the bouilli of the day before, mince and add chopped parsley, a few spices, salt and Cayenne pepper, and a little beef extract saved from the bouillon
2. Take a pound of pork and add, mixing thoroughly
3. When the whole is well mixed, add a few truffles and a little Madeira
4. Fill some entrails that have been thoroughly cleansed with this meat and shape the sausage as one desires
5. Boil in butter and serve alone
