## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Fine Peaches
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Juice of 2 Lemons
- 1 Quart of Water

## Directions

1. Pare and cut the peaches in halves, remove the stones.
2. Cut the peaches up very fine and sprinkle with a half pound of sugar, mash well together and set aside for an hour.
3. Then set the water and the remaining half of the sugar to boil.
4. After five minutes remove from the fire and let cool.
5. Add the peaches and mix well.
6. Then press all through a fine cloth sieve, extracting all the juice from the peaches.
7. Set to freeze and serve as directed, in glasses.
