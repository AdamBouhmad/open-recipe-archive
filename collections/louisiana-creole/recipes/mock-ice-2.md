## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoonfuls good preserve
- cream
- 1/2 ounce isinglass
- 1/2 pint water

## Directions

1. Take about 3 tablespoonfuls of some good preserve
2. Rub it through a sieve with as much cream as will fill a quart mould
3. Dissolve 1/2 ounce of isinglass in 1/2 pint of water
4. When almost cold, mix it well with the cream
5. Put it into a mould
6. Set it in a cool place, and turn out the next day
