## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Malaga Grapes
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Quart of Water

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar and water five minutes
2. Mash the grapes and add all to the sugar and water
3. Set to cool
4. Press through a fine cloth, pressing hard to extract all the juice of the grapes
5. Set to freeze and serve as directed
