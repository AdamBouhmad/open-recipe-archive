## Ingredients

- four eggs
- twelve ounces of powdered and sifted sugar
- ten ounces of flour
- vanilla for flavoring
- butter
- white sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Beat the eggs and sugar together in a stewpan on the fire, until the batter feels warm to the touch
2. Remove it from the fire, and stir it thoroughly until it becomes cold
3. Add the flour and flavor with vanilla
4. Butter some paper and place it on the baking tins or pans
5. Drop the cake mixture in round or ovals on the buttered paper
6. Sift white sugar over the biscuit
7. Bake in a slow oven
