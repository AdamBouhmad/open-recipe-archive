## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Red Beans
- 1 Ounce of Butter
- 1 Onion
- 2 Cloves
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1 Glass of Claret Wine
- 6 Small Glazed Onions

## Directions

1. Pick and wash the beans and let them soak in cold water for six hours.
2. Drain thoroughly, and put in a saucepan, with sufficient fresh cold water to cover.
3. Add a tablespoonful of butter, and a medium-sized onion, with two cloves stuck in it.
4. Boil for about twenty minutes, and then add a glass of Claret.
5. Stir well, and let the beans cook for three-quarters of an hour longer, stirring frequently to keep from scorching.
6. Remove from the fire, take out the herb bouquet and onion, pour the beans into a hot dish, and decorate the edges with a half dozen small glazed onions.
7. Serve hot.
