## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Cream
- 2 Cups of Flour
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of Lemon Essence

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the sugar to a cream
2. Add the cream, and gradually add the flour and baking powder, sifted
3. Add the whites of the eggs, beat lightly, and bake in a quick oven thirty minutes
