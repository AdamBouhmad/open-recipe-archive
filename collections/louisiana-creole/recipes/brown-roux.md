## Ingredients

- butter
- browned flour

## Directions

1. Melt some butter very slowly
2. stir into it browned flour till it is a fine, even brown
3. add as much to the butter as will make it the thickness of paste, stirring well with a wooden spoon for fifteen or twenty minutes, till it is of a yellowish brown color
4. Great care must be taken, for if the fire is too hot, it will become bitter and unfit for use
5. When cold it should be thick enough to cut with a knife
