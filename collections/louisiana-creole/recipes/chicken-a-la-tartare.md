## Ingredients

- 1 Spring Chicken
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley
- 1 Tablespoonful of Thyme
- 1 Bay Leaf, Minced Fine
- 1 Chopped Onion
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Boil the chicken according to the above recipe, adding the chopped vegetables and herbs.
2. Season to taste.
3. When done, place on a hot dish, butter nicely and serve with a Sauce a la Tartare.
