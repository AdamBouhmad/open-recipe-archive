## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- brine
- one part whiskey
- three parts water
- spices

## Directions

1. Prepare your cucumbers by letting them stay a few days in brine, or if time is an object scald them in brine
2. If you have no vinegar convenient drop your scalded cucumbers into a mixture of one part whiskey and three parts water
3. Secure them carefully from the air
4. By Christmas they will be fine, firm, green pickles, and the whiskey and water will be excellent vinegar
5. Add spices after they are pickled
