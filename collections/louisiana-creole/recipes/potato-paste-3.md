## Ingredients

- mealy potatoes
- water
- 1 quart of flour

## Directions

1. Boil mealy potatoes soft
2. rub them through a sieve while hot
3. put them in a stewpan over the fire with as much water as will make them the consistency of mush
4. sift 1 quart of flour in, and make into a paste
5. knead it till very light
