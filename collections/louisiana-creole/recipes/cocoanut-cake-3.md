## Ingredients

- one cup of butter
- two cups of sugar
- three cups of flour
- four eggs
- icing
- grated cocoanut

## Directions

1. Make a batter of one cup of butter, two cups of sugar, three cups of flour and four eggs
2. Bake in jelly cake pans
3. Spread a layer of icing between each cake with grated cocoanut on top of the icing
4. finally, ornament the top with a thick layer of cocoanut
