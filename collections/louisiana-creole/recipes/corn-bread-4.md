## Ingredients

- 1 cup of cornmeal
- 1 cup of breakfast hominy (boiled)
- 1 egg
- a pinch of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of yeast powder
- sufficient sweet milk

## Directions

1. Make a soft batter with the cornmeal, hominy, egg, salt, yeast powder, and sweet milk
2. Bake quickly in a slightly larded hot pan
3. Serve instantly
