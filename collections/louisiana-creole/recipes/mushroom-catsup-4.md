## Ingredients

- fresh mushrooms
- salt
- vinegar
- pepper
- cloves

## Directions

1. Lay fresh mushrooms in a deep dish
2. strew a little salt over them, then a fresh layer of mushrooms and salt, till you get in all the mushrooms
3. Let them stay in this brine three days
4. then mash them fine
5. add to each quart a spoonful of vinegar, half a spoonful of pepper and a teaspoonful of cloves
6. pour all this in a stone jar
7. place the jar in a pot of boiling water
8. let it boil two hours
9. then strain it without squeezing the mushrooms
10. Boil the juice fifteen minutes
11. skim it well
12. let it stand a few hours to settle
13. bottle and cork it well
14. Keep it cool, or it will ferment
