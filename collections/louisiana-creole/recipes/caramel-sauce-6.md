## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Cup of Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Sherry
- 2 Ground Cloves
- 1 Stick of Cinnamon
- Grated Zest of 1/2 Lemon

## Directions

1. Put the sugar in a saucepan and let it melt and brown to a golden yellow.
2. Add the water and the wine, or essence and spices, etc., and set to cool after it has boiled three minutes.
