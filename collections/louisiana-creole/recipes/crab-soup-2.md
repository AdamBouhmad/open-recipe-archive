## Ingredients

- 18 young fat crabs
- 12 fine, ripe tomatoes
- 1 large onion
- 1 clove of garlic
- 1 spoonful of butter
- 2 spoonfuls of lard
- grated bread or crackers
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- sweet marjoram
- thyme
- half teaspoonful of lemon juice
- peel of a lemon
- water

## Directions

1. Open and cleanse the crabs of deadmen's fingers and sand
2. Cut them into 4 parts and extract the meat from the crabs and the fat from the top of the shells
3. Scald and skin 12 fine, ripe tomatoes
4. Squeeze the pulp from the seeds and juice and chop fine
5. Pour boiling water over seed and juice and strain it off, using it to make the soup with more water if required
6. Stew in soup pot one large onion and one clove of garlic in one spoonful of butter and two of lard
7. Put in the tomatoes and after stewing a few minutes, add the meat from the claws, then the crabs, and lastly the fat from the top shells
8. Sift over it grated bread or crackers
9. Season with salt, pepper, parsley, sweet marjoram, thyme, half teaspoonful of lemon juice and the peel of a lemon
10. Pour in water in which seeds were scalded and boil moderately for one hour
