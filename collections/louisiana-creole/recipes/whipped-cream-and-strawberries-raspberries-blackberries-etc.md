## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Berries
- 1 Pint of Whipped Cream
- 1 Cup of Sugar

## Directions

1. Stem the berries, sprinkle nicely with white sugar, and set aside for several hours in a cool place
2. Serve with the whipped cream, dishing the berries first in serving, and heaping the cream prettily on top
