## Ingredients

- 2 pints well boiled rice
- 1 large spoonful butter
- 1 pinch salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 pint milk
- 1 pint sifted flour
- 1 teaspoonful yeast

## Directions

1. Stir into 2 pints of well boiled rice 1 large spoonful of butter and a pinch of salt
2. When cool, add 2 eggs well beaten, 1 pint of milk, 1 pint of sifted flour, and 1 teaspoonful of yeast
3. Let it stand 2 hours before baking
