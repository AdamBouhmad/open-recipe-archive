## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- vinegar
- mace
- ginger
- whole pepper
- cloves

## Directions

1. Slice the cabbage and sprinkle with salt
2. Let it remain three days
3. Drain
4. Pour over it boiling vinegar in which you have put mace, bruised ginger, whole pepper and cloves
5. Let it remain in this until next day
6. Then give one more scald
7. It is ready to put up for use
