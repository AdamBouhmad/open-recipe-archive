## Ingredients

- cray-fishes
- bread
- onions
- salt
- black pepper
- egg
- garlic
- ham
- turnip
- carrot
- flour
- water
- tomatoes
- thyme
- sweet bay
- parsley
- rice

## Directions

1. Wash the cray-fishes, boil and drain them
2. Separate the heads from the tails
3. Clean out some of the heads, allowing two or three heads to each person
4. Peel the tails
5. Chop up a part of them, add to them some bread, onions, salt, black pepper and an egg or two
6. With this dressing, stuff the heads that you have cleaned out
7. Chop the claws and the parts adhering to them
8. Fry a little garlic, onions, ham, one turnip, one carrot, and a little flour
9. add some water, the chopped claws, a few tomatoes, thyme, sweet bay, parsley and a little rice
10. stir often to avoid scorching
11. When well boiled
