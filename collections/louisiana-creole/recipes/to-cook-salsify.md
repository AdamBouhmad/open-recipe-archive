## Ingredients

- salsify
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Boil salsify until the skin comes off easily
2. When taken off, cut the roots in pieces about the size of an oyster
3. Put into a deep vegetable dish a layer of crumbs of bread or crackers, a little salt, pepper and nutmeg, and a covering of butter
4. Then a layer of salsify
5. Put these layers alternately until the dish is filled, having crumbs of bread on the top
6. Pour in as much water as the dish will hold
7. Bake brown
