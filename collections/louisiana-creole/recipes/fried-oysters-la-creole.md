## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 oysters to each person
- 1 egg
- 1 glass of milk
- 1/2 teaspoonful of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of black pepper
- Grated cracker or bread crumbs
- Butter oil or lard
- Parsley
- Sliced lemon
- Pickle

## Directions

1. Select the firmest and largest oysters, allowing six or eight to each person.
2. Drain in a colander and dry with a soft linen towel.
3. Beat an egg thoroughly and mix with a glass of milk and a half teaspoonful of salt and pepper.
4. Mash bread crumbs or crackers in another dish.
5. Dip the oysters one by one in the milk and roll gently in the bread crumbs, patting softly with the hands.
6. Drop into a deep frying pan with sufficient lard or butter oil for the oyster to swim in it.
7. In from three to five minutes the oysters will be done.
8. Take them out with a skimmer, being careful not to break, and drain on a piece of soft brown paper.
9. Serve on a bed of fried parsley, with garnishes of sliced lemon and pickle.
