## Ingredients

- 1 Pint Mushrooms
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 1/2 Cup of Cream
- The Yolk of 1 Egg
- 1 Tablespoonful Flour
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Sherry Wine

## Directions

1. Put the mushrooms with their juice, into a saucepan, porcelain-lined or agate.
2. Let them simmer for fifteen minutes.
3. Then add one tablespoonful of butter, blended well with the same amount of flour, and mixed thoroughly with the cream.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Stir well.
6. Bring it to a good boil, and remove from the fire, and stir in the yolk of an egg, which has been beaten with Sherry Wine.
7. Serve immediately.
