## Ingredients

- one ripe cantaloupe
- vinegar
- three pounds of loaf sugar
- five pounds of cantaloupe
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- mace

## Directions

1. Take a ripe cantaloupe, quarter it, remove the seeds and cut it into pieces an inch square
2. Put the cut pieces in a stone crock
3. Pour on scalding vinegar
4. When it cools heat it again, and return it to the cantaloupe
5. Repeat this next day
6. On the fourth day take out the fruit and add fresh vinegar to cover it
7. To every quart of this vinegar add three pounds of loaf sugar, and five pounds of cantaloupe
8. Put to them nutmeg, cinnamon and mace, to taste
9. Put all in a porcelain-lined kettle and simmer until the fruit can be pierced with a straw
10. Pack it in jars and keep in a cool place
