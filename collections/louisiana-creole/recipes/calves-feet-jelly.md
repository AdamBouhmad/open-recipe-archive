## Ingredients

- two calves' feet
- one gallon of water
- whites of six eggs
- half a pint of wine
- half a pound of white sugar
- juice of four lemons
- rind of one lemon grated

## Directions

1. Take two calves' feet, add to them a gallon of water which you must reduce by boiling to a quart
2. Strain it while hot, and set away to get cold
3. When cold take off the fat, and remove any settlings which may be in the bottom
4. Melt the jelly in a stew-pan, and add to it the whites of six eggs, well beaten, half a pint of wine, half a pound of white sugar, the juice of four lemons, and rind of one grated
5. Boil this a few minutes, and pass it through a flannel strainer
