## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Watercress
- Minced Potato, if desired
- A Plain French Dressing

## Directions

1. This salad is made of watercress simply, or watercress and minced potatoes, mixed in equal quantities, and served with a French dressing.
2. It is a most healthy, light and excellent salad, especially in summer.
3. The salad is delightful without the potatoes. They may be added if desired.
