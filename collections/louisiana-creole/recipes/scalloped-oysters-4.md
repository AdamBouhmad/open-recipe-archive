## Ingredients

- 1 quart of oysters
- butter
- powdered bread crumbs or crackers
- allspice
- pepper corns
- mace
- small glass of cooking Maderia

## Directions

1. Scald 1 quart of oysters in their own liquor, and drain them
2. Butter a pan
3. Sprinkle powdered bread crumbs or crackers well over the bottom and sides
4. Put a layer of oysters, then of bread crumbs
5. Next, add small pieces of butter, a few whole allspice and pepper corns, a very little mace
6. Continue this until the pan is full
7. Cover last with crumbs
8. Add small glass of cooking Maderia
9. Bake 1 hour
