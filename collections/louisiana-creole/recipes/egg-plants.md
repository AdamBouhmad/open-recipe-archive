## Ingredients

- egg plants
- bacon
- onion
- bread crumbs
- egg

## Directions

1. Parboil them, after splitting them in half
2. Scrape out the middle
3. Chop up the middle with little slips of bacon, onion cut up small, and crumbs of bread mixed with a raw egg
4. Fill the skins with this stuffing and bake them nicely
5. Grate bread on the surface of them
