## Ingredients

- 3 Pints of Cranberry Juice
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup
- 3/4 Ounce of Soluble Essence of Lemon

## Directions

1. Proceed in exactly the same manner as for Currant Syrup, adding twice the quantity of sugar, and adding the essence after the syrup has somewhat cooled and you are about to bottle it.
