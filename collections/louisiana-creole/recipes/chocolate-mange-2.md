## Ingredients

- 3 ounces of French isinglass
- cold water
- 1 quart of new milk
- half cake of vanilla chocolate
- cream

## Directions

1. Dissolve 3 ounces of French isinglass in a very little cold water
2. Put on a quart of new milk to boil
3. Grate half cake of vanilla chocolate and stir in the milk
4. Let it simmer and add the dissolved isinglass
5. Let all simmer 5 minutes
6. Pour into a mould
7. When cold and stiff, turn out and serve with cream
