## Ingredients

- Corn
- 2 Level Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- The Yolks of 2 Eggs
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Butter
- Milk
- Buttered toast

## Directions

1. Slit the corn in two and grate from the cobs.
2. Put the cobs into boiling water and let them boil slowly about an hour, till the water is reduced to three quarts.
3. Take the cobs out and drain over the kettle.
4. Add the corn and let it boil till very soft, which will require about thirty minutes.
5. Take the soup off and press all through a sieve.
6. Season highly and set back to simmer gently, adding the flour and butter, thoroughly rubbed together.
7. Stir constantly till the soup thickens, and then add the boiling milk.
8. Cook a moment only, take off the fire, stir in the beaten yolks and serve hot with buttered toast cut in dice shape.
