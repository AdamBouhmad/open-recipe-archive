## Ingredients

- grapes
- sugar

## Directions

1. Pick the grapes from the stems; wash and drain them; mash them with a spoon; put them in the preserving kettle, and cover them closely with a large plate; boil them 10 minutes; then pour them into the jelly bag, and squeeze out the juice
2. Allow a pint of juice to 1 lb. of sugar; put the sugar and juice into the kettle, and boil them 20 minutes, skimming them well; fill the glasses while the jelly is warm, and tie them up with brandy papers
