## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cold Meat
- 1 Onion
- 1 Pint of Chopped Potatoes (Uncooked)
- 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 1/4 of a Clove of Garlic
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter or Lard
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Taste

## Directions

1. Take the remains of cold roast, stew, bouilli, steaks or fowl, and mince very fine.
2. To every quart of meat allow one onion, a quarter of a clove of garlic, chopped fine, and one pint of chopped (uncooked) potatoes, and two hard-boiled eggs, chopped fine.
3. Mix all this with the minced meat, add salt, pepper and Cayenne to taste.
4. Put into a stew pan with a tablespoonful of butter or lard, and let it simmer gently.
5. After ten minutes add a half pint of hot water.
6. Let it cook ten minutes longer and serve.
