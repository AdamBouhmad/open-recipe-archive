## Ingredients

- 1 pint of Sauce Piquante
- 3 chopped mushrooms
- 1 teaspoonful of dry mustard
- 2 teaspoonfuls of tarragon vinegar

## Directions

1. Put a half pint of very light Piquante Sauce on the fire
2. Add the mushrooms finely chopped and a teaspoonful of dry mustard which has been well diluted in two teaspoonfuls of tarragon vinegar
3. Let the sauce boil for five minutes and serve hot
