## Ingredients

- ½ lb. of stewed pumpkin
- 3 eggs
- ½ lb. of fresh butter or 1 pint of cream
- ¼ lb. of powdered white sugar
- ¼ glass of wine and brandy, mixed
- teaspoonful of mixed spice (mace and nutmeg)
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Take ½ lb. of stewed pumpkin, 3 eggs, ½ lb. of fresh butter or 1 pint of cream, ¼ lb. of powdered white sugar, ¼ glass of wine and brandy, mixed, a teaspoonful of mixed spice (mace and nutmeg), or if you prefer it, add a little cinnamon
2. Put a nice paste in the dish
3. Pour the mixture in
4. Bake it ¾ hour in a moderate oven
5. Grate sugar over it when cool
