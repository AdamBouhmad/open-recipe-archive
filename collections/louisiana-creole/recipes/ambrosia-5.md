## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Sweet Louisiana Oranges
- 1 Cup of White Sugar
- 1 Cocoanut

## Directions

1. Grate the cocoanut after paring.
2. Peel and slice the oranges, taking out the seeds.
3. Have a nice large glass dish.
4. Put first a layer of oranges in the dish, sprinkle with sugar, and then a layer of cocoanut, till all the oranges and cocoanut are used, the layer of cocoanut being the last.
5. Sprinkle with powdered white sugar, and let it stand about two hours before serving.
