## Ingredients

- beef or veal
- pepper
- salt
- ginger
- 4 eggs
- stale bread
- flour
- water
- hot water
- tomato or walnut catsup

## Directions

1. Chop the beef or veal very fine and take out all the stringy parts
2. Season it with pepper, salt and a little ginger
3. Beat up 4 eggs very light
4. Take a large plateful of stale bread and make them in balls
5. Roll them in flour and fry them
6. Take 2 tablespoonfuls of flour, mixed with cold water
7. Put some seasoning and hot water into the frying pan
8. After it has boiled throw it over the balls
9. Let it stew about 15 minutes before dishing them in tomato or walnut catsup
