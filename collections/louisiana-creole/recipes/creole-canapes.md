## Ingredients

- 1 Cup of Grated or Minced Boiled Ham
- 1 Onion
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Peeled Tomato
- 1 Minced Green Pepper
- 6 Slices of Buttered Toast
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Ounces of Parmesan Cheese
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Grate and mince only the lean portion of ham till you have a cupful
2. Put this in a saucepan with a tablespoonful of butter
3. Add the finely minced onion and garlic
4. Let this cook for three minutes
5. Add the finely-cut tomato and minced green pepper
6. Season to taste with salt and pepper
