## Ingredients

- 2 Pints of Pineapple Juice
- 1 Gallon of Plain Syrup
- 1 Ounce of Lemon Juice

## Directions

1. Pare the pineapples, cutting out the eyes and the core
2. Cut into very fine pieces and mash and pound well, or grate the pineapple
3. Set aside for thirty-six hours in a cool place
4. Strain through a fine muslin bag, pressing out all the juice
5. Make a Plain Syrup and proceed as in the General Directions for Making Fruit Syrups
