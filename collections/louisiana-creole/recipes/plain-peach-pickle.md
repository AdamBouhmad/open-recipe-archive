## Ingredients

- eight or ten fine, nearly ripe peaches
- brine
- vinegar
- whole pepper
- ginger
- mace
- mustard seed

## Directions

1. Wipe off the down with a flannel rag from eight or ten fine, nearly ripe peaches
2. Put them into brine strong enough to bear up an egg
3. In two days drain them from this brine, and scald them in boiling vinegar, and let them stay in all night
4. Next day boil in a quart of vinegar, one ounce of whole pepper, one of broken-up ginger, eight blades of mace, and two ounces of mustard seed
5. Pour this boiling on the peaches, and when cool, put them in jars, and pack away carefully in a cool place
