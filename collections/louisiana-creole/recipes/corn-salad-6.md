## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Corn Salad
- A Plain French Dressing
- Hard-Boiled Eggs
- Beets

## Directions

1. Take one pint of fresh Doucette and pare off the outer stale leaves, if there are any; cut off the roots
2. Wash the Doucette well in two waters, drain in a napkin and place in the salad bowl
3. When ready to serve add a plain French dressing but not before
4. Mix well, so that every portion will be impregnated with the dressing
5. Serve very cold with a garnish of two hard-boiled eggs, sliced or cut in quarters, or of two medium-sized beets
