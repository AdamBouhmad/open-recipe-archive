## Ingredients

- 12 large tomatoes
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- crumbs of bread

## Directions

1. Take 12 large tomatoes and take out as much of the pulp and seed as you can with convenience scoop out, without injuring the form
2. Chop up the pulp, and add to it salt, pepper, thyme, to the taste, with 1 tablespoonful of butter
3. Form this into a stuffing with crumbs of bread, and put back into the skins
4. Bake them in a pan very nicely in a moderate oven
