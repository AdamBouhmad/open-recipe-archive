## Ingredients

- butter sauce (half a pint of No. 10)
- parsley (chopped, half a cup)
- lemon juice (one)

## Directions

1. Take half a pint of butter sauce No. 10, and add half a cup of chopped parsley and the juice of one lemon.
2. Pour hot water on the parsley before chopping.
