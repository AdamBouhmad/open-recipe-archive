## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Flour
- 1 Pound of White Pulverized Sugar
- 3/4 of a Pound of Butter
- 9 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Ground Cinnamon
- 1/2 Teaspoonful Ground Allspice
- 1 Teaspoonful of Grated Nutmeg
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Ground Mace
- 1 Wineglasstul Brandy
- 1 Wineglasstul of Sherry or Madeira
- 1 Teaspoonful of Soda
- 1 Cup of Sour Cream
- 1/4 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and the sugar to a cream, and then add the well-beaten yolks of the eggs.
2. Beat till very light.
3. Add half the flour gradually, alternating with half the whites of the eggs, which you will have beaten to a thick froth.
4. Then add the spices, the brandy and the wine.
5. Now add gradually the rest of the flour, and then the remainder of the whites of the eggs.
6. Finally add the sour milk, into which you will have dissolved one teaspoonful of soda in a tablespoonful of boiling water.
7. Stir well, and turn into a buttered Turk's head tin, and bake for one hour in a moderate oven.
8. Use the broom-straw test.
