## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Fine Crabs
- 6 Ripe Tomatoes
- 1 Sprig Thyme
- 3 Sprigs Parsley
- 1 Sprig Sweet Marjoram
- 1 Large Onion
- 1 Clove Garlic
- 1 Teaspoonful Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls Lard
- 1 Lemon
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne

## Directions

1. Cleanse the crabs thoroughly and extract all the meat from the body and claws
2. Scald and skin the tomatoes, and squeeze the pulp from the seeds and juice; chop very fine
3. Pour boiling water over the seeds and juice and strain
4. Chop the onion and garlic and stew with the tablespoonful of butter and lard
5. As they begin to brown, add the tomatoes, cover, and after simmering a few minutes, add the well-seasoned meat of the crab
6. Sift over this some grated bread or crackers and season with Cayenne, sweet marjoram and thyme
7. Pour in tomato water and add about a quart or more of water
8. Let it boil moderately for about an hour
9. Add the juice of two lemons and serve
