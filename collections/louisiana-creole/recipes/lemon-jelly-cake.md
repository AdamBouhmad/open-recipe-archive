## Ingredients

- the cup cake recipe
- juice and rind of 8 lemons
- ½ lb. of butter
- 1 lb. of white sugar
- yolks of 6 eggs

## Directions

1. beat the eggs and sugar very light
2. melt the butter and pour it on the eggs and sugar
3. pour boiling water over the lemons
4. grate the outside after pressing out the juice
5. add rind and juice
6. boil slowly and stir constantly until of the consistency of blanc mange
