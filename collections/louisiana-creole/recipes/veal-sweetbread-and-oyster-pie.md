## Ingredients

- veal sweetbread
- oysters
- cream
- butter
- yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- flour
- paste

## Directions

1. The sweetbread of veal is the most delicate part of the animal
2. Boil it tender, season with pepper, salt and butter
3. Put in two dozen oysters
4. Thicken their juice with a cup of cream, a tablespoonful of butter, the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs, and a tablespoonful of flour
5. Pour all in a deep pan, and cover with paste and bake
6. If there is too much liquid, keep it to serve with the pie, if necessary, when baked
7. After baking, the pie is sometimes too dry
