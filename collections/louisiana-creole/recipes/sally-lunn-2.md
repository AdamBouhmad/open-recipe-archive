## Ingredients

- One cup of warmed sweet milk
- a cup of yeast
- one cup of sugar
- one quart of sifted flour
- four eggs
- a cup of lard and butter melted together

## Directions

1. Pour the mixture into a cake mould after it has been well beaten
2. let the mould be warmed and well greased
3. Set it to rise in a warm place
4. let it rise until very light
5. bake like a cake
6. With a sharp knife divide the cake, severing the top from the bottom crust
7. butter both
8. set the top crust down on the under half
9. bring it to table hot
