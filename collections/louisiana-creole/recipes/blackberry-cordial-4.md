## Ingredients

- fine, ripe fruit
- loaf sugar
- brandy
- cloves
- cinnamon
- hot water

## Directions

1. Select fine, ripe fruit
2. Squeeze the berries without boiling
3. To a quart of the strained juice, put a pound of loaf sugar
4. Boil it for half an hour, and add a quart of brandy, some cloves and cinnamon, when on the fire
5. If the fruit thickens too rapidly while boiling, throw in a cup of hot water
