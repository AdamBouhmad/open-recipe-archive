## Ingredients

- quarter of a pound of butter
- quarter of a pound of sifted sugar
- two eggs
- six ounces of flour
- almonds, vanilla, or lemon for flavoring
- butter for paper

## Directions

1. Beat up a quarter of a pound of butter and a quarter of a pound of sifted sugar
2. Add two eggs and six ounces of flour
3. Flavor with almonds, vanilla, or lemon
4. Butter some paper and place it on baking-sheet or pans
5. Drop the mixture in drops about the size of a nutmeg
6. Bake in a hot oven
