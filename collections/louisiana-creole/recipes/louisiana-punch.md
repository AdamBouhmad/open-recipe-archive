## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of White Burgundy Wine
- 2 Quarts of Seltzer Water
- 1 Pint of Brandy
- 1 Pint of Good Old Cognac
- 1 Pint of Curacoa
- 1 Pint of Lemon Juice
- ½ Dozen Cherries
- 2 Dozen Strawberries
- ¼ of a Sliced Pineapple
- 2 Lemons, Sliced Very Thin
- 1½ Pounds of Sugar
- 1 Large Piece of Ice

## Directions

1. Mix the Seltzer Water and the sugar
2. Add the wine, brandy, Curacoa and lemon juice
3. Stem and wash the strawberries and the cherries
4. Add, and decorate the punch with thin slices of lemon and pineapple
5. Set to cool and serve in small punch glasses
