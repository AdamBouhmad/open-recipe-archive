## Ingredients

- 1 quart of wheat flour
- 1 dessertspoonful of super-carbonate of soda
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 lb. of powdered sugar
- 1 1/2 tumblers of buttermilk

## Directions

1. Mix these ingredients, and knead them into soft dough
2. Place in a greased pan, and set it to rise in a slightly warm oven
3. When risen, bake
