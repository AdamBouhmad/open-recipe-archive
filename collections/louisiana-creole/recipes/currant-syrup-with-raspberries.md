## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Currant Juice
- 2 Pounds of Sugar
- 6 Pounds of Currants
- 1 Pound of Sour Cherries
- 1 Pound of Raspberries
- 1 1/4 Pints of Water

## Directions

1. Pick, wash, seed and dry the currants carefully.
2. Seed the cherries, and stem them and the raspberries, pound and mash them well, and let them stand for thirty-six hours in a cool place.
3. Then strain through a bag.
4. Cook the sugar and water till it forms the syrup called "Le Petit Casse," or crack, till it instantly snaps asunder when placed between the fingers, after dipping them in cold water, to enable you to test.
5. Then add the fruit syrups, and mix thoroughly.
6. Let it boil for about five minutes, take off the fire, let it get almost cold, and then put up in bottles.
