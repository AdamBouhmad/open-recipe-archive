## Ingredients

- flour
- yeast
- warm water
- warm milk
- 4 eggs

## Directions

1. Make a stiff batter with flour, yeast and warm water, at night
2. In the morning, have a little warm milk and eggs, and flour to make it sufficiently stiff to bake
