## Ingredients

- 1 Pair of Fine Ducks, French or Canvas-Back
- 1 Dozen Glazed Onions
- 2 Carrots
- 1 Square Inch of Ham
- 1 Cup of Green Peas
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 2 Sprigs Each of Thyme and Parsley
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 1 Glass of Madeira or Sherry Wine
- The Zest of 1 Lemon
- Croutons fried in butter

## Directions

1. Prepare the Ducks exactly as in the recipe for Stewed Ducks With Turnips, only the turnips are omitted.
2. Add two carrots cut into dice pieces, and twelve glazed onions and the green peas.
3. A quarter of an hour before serving add a glass of Madeira wine.
4. Serve on a hot dish, with Croutons fried in butter, using the onions as a garnish with the Croutons.
