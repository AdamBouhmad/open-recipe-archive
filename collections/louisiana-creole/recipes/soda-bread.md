## Ingredients

- 1 pound of flour
- a teaspoonful of tartaric acid
- milk or buttermilk
- a little saleratus or soda

## Directions

1. Rub the tartaric acid into the flour perfectly dry
2. Make a batter of milk or buttermilk
3. Add a little saleratus or soda
