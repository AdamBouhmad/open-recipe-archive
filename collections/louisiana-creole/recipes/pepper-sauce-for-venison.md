## Ingredients

- 1 carrot, minced fine
- 2 sprigs each of thyme and parsley
- 1 onion, minced fine
- 1 pint of consommé
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 wineglass of sherry or Madeira
- 1/2 grated lemon
- 1 small piece of celery
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoonful butter
- 1 tablespoonful flour
- A dash of cayenne
- Half glass of currant jelly

## Directions

1. Make sauce poivrade in exactly the same manner as the pepper sauce recipe
2. When it is to be served with venison add a half glass of currant jelly, and the sauce allowed to boil ten minutes longer
