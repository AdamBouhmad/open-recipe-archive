## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Louisiana Molasses
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Pound of Granulated Sugar
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Vinegar
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Soda
- The Juice of 1 Lemon

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar until it becomes quite thick when dropped into water
2. Add the molasses and the vinegar and butter
3. Boil till it hardens when dropped into cold water
4. Stir in a small half teaspoonful of bicarbonate of soda
5. Pour into buttered tins
6. As soon as it begins to cool sufficiently pull till white
7. Moisten the hands while pulling with ice water or butter
8. The sticks may be single, twisted, braided or flattened, according to taste
