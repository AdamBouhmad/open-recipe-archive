## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Milk
- The Yolks of 6 Eggs
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 3/4 Cup of Sugar
- 1 Dozen Macaroons
- Orange Juice or Extract of Orange
- Two tablespoonfuls of powdered white sugar
- The Whites of the Eggs

## Directions

1. Set the milk to boil.
2. Blend the butter and the flour, and stir into the boiling milk.
3. Beat the yolks of the eggs with half a cup of sugar, very light, and add this to the milk, stirring constantly for one minute, and take from the fire to cool.
4. Add a tablespoonful of orange Juice or extract of orange.
5. When cool, crumble one dozen macaroons over the top of the dish.
6. Make a meringue with the whites of the eggs and two tablespoonfuls of powdered white sugar.
7. Beat to a stiff froth, pile on the custard, and set in the oven for a few minutes to brown.
8. Serve very cold.
