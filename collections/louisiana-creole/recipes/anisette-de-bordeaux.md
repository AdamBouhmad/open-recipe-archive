## Ingredients

- 10 Ounces of Green Aniseed
- 4 Ounces of Star Aniseed
- 1 Ounce of Coriander
- 2 Ounces of Hyson Tea
- 1 Ounce of Fennel
- 3½ Gallons of Alcohol
- 10 Pounds of Sugar
- 7 Pints of Water

## Directions

1. Take the aniseed, coriander, fennel, and Hyson tea, and pound well.
2. Macerate or steep for fifteen days in three and a half gallons of the finest rectified alcohol.
3. Distill in a "bain-marie," or water bath, according to directions already given.
4. Make a syrup with the ten pounds of sugar and seven pints of water.
5. Mix well with the aniseed liquor and filter.
6. Bottle and keep in a cool, shady place.
