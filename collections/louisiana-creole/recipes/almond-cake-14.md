## Ingredients

- 12 eggs
- 1 pound of sifted loaf sugar
- 3/4 pound of sifted flour
- 6 ounces sweet almonds
- zest of 2 citrons
- zest of 1 orange
- 1/4 teaspoonful salt
- 1 tablespoonful of orange flower water

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs and the whites separately, beating the latter to a stiff froth.
2. Blanch the almonds, and peel them and pound them to a fine flour in a mortar.
3. Grate the zest of the oranges and lemons or citrons, and mix well.
4. Beat the sugar and the yolks of the eggs to a cream, and gradually add one-half of the flour, beating well.
5. Then add the almonds, mixing thoroughly, and, after beating vigorously, add one-half of the whites of the eggs.
6. Beat well, add the remainder of the flour, and then add the remainder of the eggs.
7. Finally add the orange flower water, and mix lightly.
