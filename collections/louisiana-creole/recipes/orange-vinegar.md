## Ingredients

- 36 Oranges
- 1 Quart of Good French Cider Vinegar
- Sugar in proportion of two-thirds of a pound to 1 quart of orange syrup

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges
2. Slice them very thin and cover with pure cider vinegar
3. Let them stand for three or four days
4. Mash them through a cloth, straining as long as the juice runs clear
5. To every quart of juice allow one and two-thirds pounds of fine white sugar
6. Boil all together for about ten minutes
7. Skim very carefully, until no scum rises upon the surface
8. Take the mixture from the fire, and when it cools somewhat, bottle it
