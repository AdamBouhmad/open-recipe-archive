## Ingredients

- 3 dozen oysters
- Thin slices of breakfast bacon
- Minced parsley
- Sauce piquante

## Directions

1. Wrap each oyster in a very thin slice of breakfast bacon.
2. Lay on a broiler over a baking pan in a hot oven.
3. Remove when the bacon is brown.
4. Each must be fastened with a wooden toothpick.
5. Serve with minced parsley and pepper sauce, or Sauce Piquante.
