## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 1 1/2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- 1 tablespoonful of cut parsley

## Directions

1. Proceed in exactly the same manner as for Plain Omelet
2. Mix a tablespoonful of cut parsley in the omelet before putting in the frying pan
