## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb. of blanched sweet almonds
- rose water
- 8 eggs
- a gill of cream
- sugar
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Beat 1/2 lb. of blanched sweet almonds with rose water
2. Beat the yolks of 8 eggs and add them, with a gill of cream, and sugar to the taste
3. Lay a sheet of puff paste in a plate or dish
4. Put the mixture on it, cover it with another sheet of paste, and bake 1 1/2 hours in a slow oven
