## Ingredients

- good yeast cake
- warm water
- sugar
- sifted flour
- lard

## Directions

1. Take a heaping spoonful of good yeast cake pounded
2. Add one-half a cup of warm water and a lump of sugar
3. Add enough sifted flour to make a thick batter
4. Set this to rise in a cool place in summer and a warm place in winter
5. It will be light and ready to use in about three hours unless it is kept very cool
6. Use a heaping spoonful for one quart of flour
7. Add half a cup of lard to make the bread better and richer
8. Grease the bread on top before baking
