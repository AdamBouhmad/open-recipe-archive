## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of fresh butter
- 1 lb. of powdered white sugar
- 2 lbs. of sifted flour
- 1 heaping tablespoonful of mixed nutmeg and cinnamon
- a small glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Rub the butter into the sifted flour crumbled fine
2. Add a heaping tablespoonful of mixed nutmeg and cinnamon
3. Moisten it entirely with butter
4. Add a small glass of brandy
5. Roll it out into a large, thick sheet
6. Cut it into round cakes about the size of saucers
7. Bake them on flat tins, slightly buttered
