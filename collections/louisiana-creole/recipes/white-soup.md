## Ingredients

- a tablespoonful of butter
- a slice of ham
- a half cup of water
- a quart of stock
- any remains of cold veal, chicken, game or rabbit roasted dry

## Directions

1. Let them simmer gently for an hour
2. If a very rich soup is desired add to the vegetables two or three quarts of good soup stock, made by boiling a beef bone in three quarts of water until the meat is tender
3. Let all boil together for half an hour
4. Add ten or twelve ripe tomatoes and a half-dozen whole peppers
5. It should cook for another hour or so
6. It must then be strained through a sieve or coarse cloth
7. Serve with toasted or fried bread cut in bits in the tureen
