## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 quart of milk
- white sugar
- extract of vanilla or nutmeg
- bread
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat together the yolks of 5 eggs
2. Add 1 quart of milk
3. Sweeten with white sugar to the taste
4. Flavor with extract of vanilla, or nutmeg
5. If baked in cups, pour cold water in a pan, and place the cups in that for the oven
6. If in a dish, cut bread very thin; butter it, and lay on the top
