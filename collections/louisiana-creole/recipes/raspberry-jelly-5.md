## Ingredients

- Raspberries
- 1/2 pound of sugar to every pound of berries

## Directions

1. To every pound of raspberries allow a half of a pound of sugar.
2. Put the berries in a porcelain-lined kettle, and cover them with the sugar and let them stand for two hours.
3. Set over a moderate fire and bring the mixture to a boiling point.
4. Skim while boiling.
5. Pour immediately into cups and glasses, and cover tightly and place in a cool dry place.
