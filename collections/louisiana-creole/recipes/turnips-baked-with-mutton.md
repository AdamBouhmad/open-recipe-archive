## Ingredients

- 6 Turnips
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Roast Mutton Drippings

## Directions

1. Boil the turnips until nearly tender.
2. Drain in a colander.
3. If very large, cut in quarters; if small, let them remain uncut.
4. Lay them on the pan, around the roasting leg of mutton, and let them bake about an hour, basting frequently with the drippings from the mutton.
5. When done, place around the leg of mutton as a garnish, and serve.
