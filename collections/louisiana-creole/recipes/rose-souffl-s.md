## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds Powdered White Sugar
- The Whites of 2 Eggs
- 1 Ounce of Orange Flower Water
- A Pinch of Carmine Powder

## Directions

1. To two pounds of powdered sugar add the whites of two eggs and one ounce of Orange Flower Water and a little pinch of Carmine Powder.
2. Make of all a firm, consistent paste, adding more sugar, if necessary.
3. Roll out on a sheet of paper, sprinkled with sugar, then cut the paste into little pieces, which you will form into balls about the size of a nut.
4. Place them on a wafer sheet on a baking sheet at a distance of two inches apart, so that they will not touch one another, should they run.
5. Bake in a moderate oven.
