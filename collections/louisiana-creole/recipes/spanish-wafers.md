## Ingredients

- 9 Ounces of Flour
- 2 Ounces of Sifted Sugar
- 3/4 Pint of Chocolate Water
- 12 Drops of Essence of Vanilla
- 2 Eggs
- 2 Gills of Cream
- 2 Ounces of French Chocolate
- 1/2 Pint of Boiling Water

## Directions

1. Prepare the chocolate water by dissolving well two ounces of French chocolate in a half pint of boiling water
2. Place the sugar, vanilla, eggs and cream in a pan, work all vigorously into a smooth, light batter
3. Add the cold Chocolate Water, beat all together well for ten minutes longer
4. Bake the wafers as directed in recipe for French Wafers, and curl in the form of cornucopias while still warm and able to retain impressions
