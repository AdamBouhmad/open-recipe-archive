## Ingredients

- one or two nice chickens
- yolks of six hard-boiled eggs to each fowl
- a cup of olive oil
- a cup of vinegar to two fowls, a half cup to one
- pepper
- salt
- vinegar
- fresh lettuce heads
- six more hard-boiled eggs
- red beets

## Directions

1. Roast one or two nice chickens, season them well, and when cooked, put them by to cool
2. Just before serving the dish, carve the fowls in small pieces, taking out all the large bones
3. Make a dressing of the yolks of six hard-boiled eggs to each fowl, mash the yolks very smooth with a wooden spoon and pour gently on them in a little stream a cup of olive oil
4. Beat the eggs all one way till they are creamed
5. Add now a cup of vinegar to two fowls, a half cup to one, pepper, salt, and drop a little vinegar on the fowl, then pour on the dressing
6. Arrange on the dish, cool, fresh lettuce heads quartered, and slice six more hard-boiled eggs over all as a garnish
7. Mix red beets with the green lettuce and yellow eggs as a handsome addition
