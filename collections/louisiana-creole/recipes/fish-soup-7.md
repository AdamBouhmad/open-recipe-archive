## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of Fish of Almost Any Variety
- 4 Onions, Chopped Fine
- 6 Tomatoes, Chopped Fine
- 1 Herb Bouquet
- Sprig of Parsley
- 1 Glass of White Wine
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Salad Oil
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 3 Pints of Water
- Cayenne pepper
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Chop the onions and fry them in the salad oil.
2. Cut the tomatoes fine and add onions, and put in all the other ingredients, except the fish, adding the flour to make a good roux.
3. When brown add the water, and, after it has boiled about a half hour, add the slices of fish.
4. When they are firm remove the herb bouquet, add Cayenne pepper, and salt and pepper to taste, and serve the fish soup in a tureen, pouring it over crusts of dried toast.
