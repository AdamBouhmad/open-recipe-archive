## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 Cups of Powdered Sugar
- 1/4 Cup Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Cornstarch
- 1 Gill of Boiling Water
- 1 Gill of White Wine or Maraschino

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream, and blend the cornstarch with a little cold water to form a paste
2. Boil the gill of water and add the cornstarch, stirring constantly as it thickens; continue stirring till transparent
3. Then add the butter and sugar, stirring well till perfectly blended, being careful to have the vessel containing the sauce in a bain-marie, or another vessel of boiling water
4. Add a gill of White Wine or Maraschino, and the Juice of a lemon or orange, or vanilla extract, according to taste
5. Serve hot with any pudding.
