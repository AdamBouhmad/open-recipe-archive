## Ingredients

- one pound of butter
- one and one-fourth pounds of white sifted sugar
- yolks of twelve eggs
- whites of twelve eggs
- a pound of sifted flour
- two pounds of currants
- two pounds of large raisins
- one pound of citron
- a quarter of a pound of sifted flour
- a grated nutmeg
- a glass of wine
- a glass of brandy

## Directions

1. Wash and cream one pound of butter
2. cream one and one-fourth pounds of white sifted sugar with the butter
3. add the yolks, beaten lightly, alternately, with the whites beaten to a stiff froth, of twelve eggs
4. stir in carefully a pound of sifted flour
5. The day before, wash and dry two pounds of currants, pick and seed two pounds of large raisins, and slice one pound of citron
6. Pour all this fruit into a large pan, and dredge it well with a quarter of a pound of sifted flour
7. stir all well into the butter
8. add a grated nutmeg, a glass of wine, and the same of brandy
9. Bake in a large cake mould very carefully, four hours
