## Ingredients

- half a cup of rice
- one quart of milk
- sugar
- yolks of four eggs
- lemon rind or essence
- nutmeg
- whites of four eggs
- one cup of white sifted sugar
- lemon, rose or vanilla for seasoning

## Directions

1. Boil half a cup of rice in a quart of milk until it is thoroughly done.
2. Sweeten to taste, and let it cool.
3. Beat in the yolks of four eggs.
4. Flavor with lemon rind or essence and nutmeg.
5. Bake in a pudding-dish.
6. When cool, pour over it the whites of your eggs, beaten with a cup of white sifted sugar.
7. Bake light brown.
8. Season to taste with lemon, rose or vanilla.
