## Ingredients

- a cup of light and warm yeast
- a cup of warm, sweet milk
- two eggs beaten
- a cup of sugar
- a spoonful of grated orange peel and nutmeg
- flour
- salt
- a lump of butter as big as an egg
- warmed butter
- a beaten egg
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take a cup of light and warm yeast, a cup of warm, sweet milk, two eggs beaten, a cup of sugar, a spoonful of grated orange peel and nutmeg
2. add to this, flour enough to make a thin batter
3. set it in a warm place to rise
4. pour this batter into sifted flour, enough to form into a rather stiff dough
5. add salt and a lump of butter as big as an egg
6. Work it thoroughly
7. set it in a tureen to rise again
8. When it is risen, flour the board and roll out the dough half an inch thick
9. smear the surface with butter
10. cut into strips about six inches long, and two inches wide
11. fold them over and over
12. lay them within an inch of each other on a warm and greased baking tin, or pan
13. swab the tops over with warmed butter and a beaten egg
14. set them to rise, which will require an hour
15. Just before you put them in the oven, sift some sugar over them
