## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Okras
- 1 Tablespoonful Butter
- 3 Tomatoes
- 1 Onion
- 1 Green Pepper
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Teaspoonful of Chopped Parsley
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Wash the okras and pare the ends
2. Place in a saucepan with one tablespoonful of butter
3. Add a finely-minced onion and clove of garlic and green pepper
4. Let all cook for six or eight minutes, and then add the three tomatoes, chopped fine; also add the juice of the tomatoes
5. Season to taste with salt and pepper; add a dash of Cayenne and a teaspoonful of chopped parsley
6. Add the okras, and let all simmer slowly for twenty minutes
7. Place in a hot, deep serving dish, and cover and send to the table
