## Ingredients

- ¾ Pound of Grated Coconut
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Melted Butter
- ¾ Pound of White Powdered Sugar
- Whites of 6 Eggs
- 1 Glass of White Wine
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Vanilla
- 1 Tablespoonful of Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Beat the sugar and butter to a light cream, and then add the wine and the essence of vanilla
2. Gradually add the coconut, beating in as lightly as possible
3. Add the scalded milk, which has been allowed to cool
4. Finally beat in lightly the whites of the eggs, which have been whipped to a very stiff froth
5. Do this latter with a few swift strokes, so that the coconut may stand out fresh and flaky, like snow
6. Bake in pie crusts which have already been set in the oven
7. Serve cold, sprinkling over white powdered sugar
