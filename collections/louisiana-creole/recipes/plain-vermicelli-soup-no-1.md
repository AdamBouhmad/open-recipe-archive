## Ingredients

- a soup-bone, weighing from two to three pounds, or a brisket of beef
- four quarts of water
- two onions
- two carrots
- two turnips
- salt to taste
- a quarter of a pound of vermicelli

## Directions

1. Put a soup-bone, weighing from two to three pounds, or a brisket of beef, into four quarts of water; add two onions, two carrots, and two turnips; salt to taste, and place over the fire to boil for three hours; then remove and strain; put back on the stove, and add a quarter of a pound of vermicelli, and let it boil till tender; serve with tomatoes.
