## Ingredients

- 100 Small Cucumbers
- Boiling brine
- 24 Whole Spices
- 1 Ounce of Mustard Seed
- 12 Small Red Peppers
- 6 Blades of Mace
- 8 Cloves
- 2 Onions
- Boiling vinegar

## Directions

1. Take about 100 small Cucumbers, and place them in a jar.
2. Cover them with boiling brine that will be strong enough to bear an egg.
3. Let them stand for several days.
4. Then take them out of the brine, wipe them well, and place them in clean jars.
5. Add two dozen whole spices, one ounce of mustard seed, and six blades of mace, eight cloves, two chopped onions, and the red peppers.
6. Cover them with boiling vinegar and cork.
7. In several weeks they will be ready for use.
