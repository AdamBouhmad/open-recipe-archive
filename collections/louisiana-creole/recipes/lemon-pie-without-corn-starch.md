## Ingredients

- a lemon
- one cup of sugar
- two tablespoonfuls water
- yolks of three eggs
- nice crust
- whites of three eggs
- a cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Take the juice and grated rind of a lemon, one cup of sugar, two tablespoonfuls water, and yolks of three eggs
2. Bake in a nice crust
3. Make an icing of the whites and a cup of sugar
4. Pour it over the pie
5. Put it back in the oven, and brown lightly
