## Ingredients

- Six eggs
- Three lemons
- Six tablespoonfuls of corn starch
- One large spoonful of butter
- One cup of sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the corn starch in a pint and a half of water
2. Stir in the butter
3. Let it get cool
4. Stir in the yolks of the eggs
5. Add the juice of the lemons, and the grated rind
6. Add one cup of sugar
7. Bake this lightly in a pudding dish
8. When cold pour it over a meringue, or icing, made with the whites of the eggs, and sufficient sugar to make a thick icing
9. Put it back in the oven
10. Let it brown lightly
