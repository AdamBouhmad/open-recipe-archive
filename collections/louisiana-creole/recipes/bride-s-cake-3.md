## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of White Pulverized Sugar
- 1 Cup of Butter
- 4 Cups of Sifted Flour
- ½ Cup of Cornstarch
- The Whites of 12 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Cup of Sweet Milk
- 1 Teaspoonful of Extract of Lemon

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of the eggs to a stiff froth.
2. Sift the flour and baking powder.
3. Sift the sugar.
4. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream.
5. Beat till very light.
6. Mix the cornstarch with the sweet milk and add to the sugar and butter, beating well.
7. Then add gradually the flour, mixing thoroughly, and beating light.
8. Add the extract of lemon, and finally the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth.
9. Bake one hour in a moderate oven, using the broom wisp test.
10. If not done, bake a little longer.
