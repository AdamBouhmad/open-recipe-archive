## Ingredients

- 8 pounds of veal cutlets
- good slice of salt pork
- 2 eggs
- 6 crackers
- butter to size of a hen's egg
- 1 tablespoonful of salt
- cayenne pepper
- sweet herbs

## Directions

1. Chop fine 8 pounds of veal cutlets with a good slice of salt pork
2. Break in 2 eggs after meat is well mixed
3. Add 6 crackers powdered fine
4. Add butter to size of a hen's egg
5. Rub in a tablespoonful of salt, cayenne pepper, and sweet herbs to your taste
6. Mix the whole thoroughly
7. Make up like a loaf of bread
8. Smooth over the top with butter melted in water
9. Bake in a moderate oven
10. When cold, cut in thin slices and serve
