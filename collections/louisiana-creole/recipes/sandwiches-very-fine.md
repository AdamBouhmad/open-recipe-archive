## Ingredients

- sweet butter
- mixed mustard
- sweet oil
- salt
- pepper
- egg yolk
- tongue or boiled ham
- bread
- parsley

## Directions

1. Take half a pound of nice sweet butter, three tablespoonfuls of mixed mustard, the same of sweet oil, a little salt, pepper and the yolk of an egg.
2. Put it over the fire and stir till it thickens; set it by to cool and chop fine some tongue or boiled ham.
3. Cut the bread thin, then spread on the dressing and over it put a layer of ham or tongue.
4. Press the slices of bread hard together, trim the edges and garnish with curled parsley.
