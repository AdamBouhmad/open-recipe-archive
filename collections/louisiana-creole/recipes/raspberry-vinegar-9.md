## Ingredients

- 2 Quarts of Raspberries
- 1 Quart of Good French Vinegar
- White sugar in the proportion of one pound to every pint of the liquor

## Directions

1. Put one quart of raspberries into a jar or deep vessel, and pour over the vinegar
2. Let them stand for twenty-four hours, and then strain through a jelly bag, and add the other quart of raspberries, and let them stand for twenty-four hours longer
3. Strain them again, and again add the fruit, repeating this operation of straining and letting stand twenty-four hours three times
4. Strain through a muslin bag, and add one pound of white sugar to every pint of the liquor
5. Boil the whole in a porcelain kettle for half an hour
6. When cold, bottle it and keep it in a cool place
