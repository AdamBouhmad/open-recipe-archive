## Ingredients

- turkey or fowl
- piece of fresh beef
- lard
- onion
- water
- 100 oysters
- oyster liquor
- pulverized fillet

## Directions

1. Take a turkey or fowl, cut it up with a piece of fresh beef
2. Put them in a pot with a little lard, an onion, and water sufficient to cook the meat
3. After they have become soft, add 100 oysters with their liquor
4. Season to your taste
5. Just before taking up the soup, warm it until it becomes mucilaginous, and add two spoonfuls of pulverized fillet
