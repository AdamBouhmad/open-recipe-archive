## Ingredients

- Cut some rare roast meat or broiled steak very fine
- A teacupful of the cut meat
- A pint and a half of boiling water
- A cracker (rolled fine)

## Directions

1. To a teacupful of the cut meat put a pint and a half of boiling water; cover it, and set it on the fire for ten minutes;
2. Season to taste.
3. Roll a cracker fine, and put in with the meat.
