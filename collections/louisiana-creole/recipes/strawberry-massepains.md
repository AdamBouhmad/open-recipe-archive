## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Sweet Almonds
- 8 Ounces of Sugar
- 6 Ounces of Crushed Strawberries

## Directions

1. Pound the almonds, and then proceed as in plain Massepains, adding the strawberries after you will have strained them through a sieve.
