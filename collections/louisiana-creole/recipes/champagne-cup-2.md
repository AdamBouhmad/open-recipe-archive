## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Champagne
- 1 Lemon, Sliced
- 1 Sliced Orange
- A Dash of Old French Cognac
- 1/2 Pint of Seltzer Water
- A Dash of Curacoa
- 1 Large Piece of Ice

## Directions

1. Mix the sugar and water; add the liqueurs, and the sliced orange and lemon, and the ice
2. Stir well and let cool
3. Serve in small, thin tumblers, and decorate with a piece of fresh green mint on top
