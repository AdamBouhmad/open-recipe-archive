## Ingredients

- 3 Pairs of Teal Ducks
- 1 Bigarade or Sour Orange
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Ground Allspice and Cloves
- Lemon Zest
- A Strip of Bacon
- Buttered Toast
- Sauce à l'Espagnole

## Directions

1. Clean the ducks and take the livers and fry them in a little melted butter.
2. Season well with salt and pepper and a slight pinch of ground allspice and cloves and the zest of a bigarade, or sour orange.
3. If the orange is not available, take the zest of a lemon.
4. Place this in the interior of the ducks, and then rub the outside well with salt and pepper.
5. Bind with a strip of bacon and place on the broiler.
6. In about thirty minutes it will be done, cooking over a slow fire, turning frequently.
7. In the meantime prepare a Sauce à l'Espagnole.
8. As soon as the birds are done pour off from the broiler all the juice that has fallen, and put this into the sauce, with the juice of two sour oranges or citrons.
9. Let it warm without boiling, pour over the birds, which you have placed on buttered toast, and serve hot.
