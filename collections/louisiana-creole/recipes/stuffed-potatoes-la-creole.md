## Ingredients

- 6 or 8 potatoes
- Chaurice (sausage)
- 1 tablespoonful of butter

## Directions

1. Select fine large potatoes, and have ready several nice Chaurice.
2. Wash and peel the potatoes.
3. Potatoes may also be stuffed after washing, by peeling carefully and scooping out the insides nicely with a spoon or sharp-pointed knife.
4. Then make a sausage forcemeat and heap up lightly on top.
5. Butter a baking dish nicely, place the potatoes upon it, and let them bake in a slow oven for a half hour or forty-five minutes, till nicely browned, and send to the table hot.
