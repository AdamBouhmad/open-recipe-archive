## Ingredients

- two pints of green shelled beans or string beans
- corn from two dozen ears
- salt
- pepper
- boiling water
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil for half an hour two pints of green shelled beans, or the same amount of string beans
2. Pour off the water
3. Cut the corn from two dozen ears, put it in the pot among the beans
4. Add salt and pepper, and cover them with boiling water
5. Let it boil for half an hour
6. Add a lump of butter as big as a hen's egg, rolled in flour
7. Let this boil up once and it is done
