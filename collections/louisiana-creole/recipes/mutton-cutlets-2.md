## Ingredients

- 4 Mutton Cutlets
- Butter
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. The cutlets are slices from the thick part of the leg of the mutton and are very excellent eating.
2. Trim off the outer skin and broil in the same manner as mutton chops.
3. They are very delicious served for dinner with a garnish of Puree of Spinach.
