## Ingredients

- A Pair of Squirrels
- 2 Onions
- 1 Square Inch of Ham
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Clove of Garlic, Chopped Very Fine
- 1 Herb Bouquet, Chopped Fine
- 2 Sprigs of Thyme
- 2 Bay Leaves
- 1 Glass of Claret
- 1 Cup of Water
- 1 Can of Mushrooms
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Skin and clean the Squirrels; wash well and cut into pieces at the joints.
2. Chop two onions very fine and put them in a stewpan with a tablespoonful of melted butter.
3. Let them brown slightly; then add the Squirrel.
4. Let it brown slightly, and then add one tablespoonful of flour, and let this brown a little.
5. Chop the square inch of ham very fine, mincing it, and add.
6. Then add the clove of garlic, and two sprigs each of thyme and parsley and a bay leaf, minced fine.
7. Let this brown nicely, and pour over one glass of good Claret.
8. Let this cook for ten minutes, stirring it constantly, so that it will not burn, and then add one cup of boiling water.
9. Stir well, season again to taste and let it boil for thirty minutes.
10. Add a can of mushrooms immediately after adding the water if desired.
11. Serve very hot.
