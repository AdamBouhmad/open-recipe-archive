## Ingredients

- 3 pints cream
- 15 eggs
- 1/2 lb. blanched almonds
- sugar
- salt
- mace or rose water

## Directions

1. Mix 3 pints of cream, 15 eggs, 1/2 lb. of blanched almonds, sugar, salt and mace, or rose water, to the taste
