## Ingredients

- two pounds of the chopped meat
- two pounds of suet free from strings or skin, and chopped fine
- two pounds of currants, picked, washed, and dried
- four pounds of peeled and chopped rich tart apples
- the juice of two lemons
- the chopped peel of one lemon
- a pint of sweet wine
- one large nutmeg grated, or teaspoonful of ground mace
- three pounds and a half of sugar
- quarter of an ounce of ground cloves, or allspice
- the same of cinnamon
- a large tablespoonful of salt

## Directions

1. Mix the whole well together
2. put it in a stone pot, or jar, cover it close, and set it in a cool place for use
3. Mix it well together again before using
