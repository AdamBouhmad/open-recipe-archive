## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- water
- butter
- toast

## Directions

1. Pick and wash the asparagus; throw aside all stalks not crisp and tender
2. Cut off nearly all the horny white parts
3. Tie the rest in neat bunches
4. Boil in salted water for twenty minutes or half an hour
5. Take it out, let it drain a minute and lay on buttered toast, the heads all one way
6. Cover with rich drawn butter sauce
