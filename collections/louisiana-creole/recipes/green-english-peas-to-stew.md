## Ingredients

- quart of green peas
- cold water
- pint of boiling water
- large lump of butter
- flour
- teaspoonful of white sugar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Shell a quart of green peas for a small mess
2. Wash them in cold water
3. Put them on to cook in a stew pan with a pint of boiling water, or enough to cover them
4. Let them cook half an hour
5. Stir in a large lump of butter rolled in flour
6. Let this cook a few minutes
7. Add a teaspoonful of white sugar, same of salt and pepper
8. Serve while hot
