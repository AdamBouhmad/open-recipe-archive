## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of nut kernels
- 1 lb. of sugar
- whites of 6 eggs
- 2 tablespoonfuls of flour

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of eggs to a stiff froth
2. Mix with nut kernels and sugar
3. Add 2 tablespoonfuls of flour
4. Bake
