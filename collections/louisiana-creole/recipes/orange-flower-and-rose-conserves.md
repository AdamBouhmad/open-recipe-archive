## Ingredients

- 8 Ounces of Rose Water
- 2 Ounces of Powdered Orange Petals
- 1 1/2 Pounds of Sugar

## Directions

1. Take two ounces of powdered orange flower petals, one and one-half pounds of sugar, and eight ounces of rose water.
2. Steep the petals in distilled water, and then add the sugar, and set to heat over a moderate fire.
3. Add the rose water.
4. When it begins to jelly turn into a jelly glass.
