## Ingredients

- 1 quart of milk
- 1/2 an ounce of moss

## Directions

1. To 1 quart of milk add 1/2 an ounce of moss
2. Boil it till thick
3. Strain it, and pour it into moulds
4. Flavor it with anything you prefer
