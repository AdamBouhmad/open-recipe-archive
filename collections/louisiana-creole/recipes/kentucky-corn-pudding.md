## Ingredients

- one dozen ears of corn
- 1 teacup milk
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 teaspoonful sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the grains down through the middle
2. Scrape out the heart
3. Mix this with 1 teacup of milk, 3 eggs, 1/2 cup of butter with 1 teaspoonful of sugar
4. Mix well and bake in a moderate oven
