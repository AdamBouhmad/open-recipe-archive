## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Creole Fritter Batter
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 2/3 Cup of Sugar

## Directions

1. Make a plain Creole batter for fruit fritters, and add the juice of a lemon, and sugar to taste.
2. Proceed as directed in cooking and serving Apple Fritters.
