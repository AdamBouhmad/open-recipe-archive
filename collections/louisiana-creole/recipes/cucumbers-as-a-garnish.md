## Ingredients

- 3 Fine Cucumbers
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vinegar
- 1 Large Onion
- Salt
- Black Pepper

## Directions

1. Peel and slice three fine cucumbers.
2. Make a marinade of one teaspoonful of salt, one of black pepper and a tablespoonful of vinegar.
3. Add one finely-sliced onion.
4. Let the cucumbers marinate in this for an hour.
5. Drain and use as a garnish for cold meats, especially cold Bouilli.
