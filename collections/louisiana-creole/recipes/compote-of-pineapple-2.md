## Ingredients

- 1 Pineapple
- % Pound of Sugar
- % Cup of Water

## Directions

1. Peel the pineapple, and cut in slices, very thin
2. Put it into a syrup made of half a pound of sugar, and let it boil up once
3. Take out, and let the syrup simmer a few minutes longer
4. Pour over the pineapple, and serve
