## Ingredients

- half a pound of vanilla chocolate
- half a pound of sugar
- yolks of eight eggs
- one pint of rich, sweet milk
- half a pint of whipped cream
- one and a half ounces of isinglass

## Directions

1. Grate half a pound of vanilla chocolate
2. Put it in a stew-pan with half a pound of sugar, the yolks of eight eggs, and one pint of rich, sweet milk
3. Stir over the fire until it begins to thicken
4. Strain through a sieve into a basin
5. Add half a pint of whipped cream, and one and a half ounces of isinglass
6. Mix well and pour into a mould
7. Set it on ice if the weather is warm
