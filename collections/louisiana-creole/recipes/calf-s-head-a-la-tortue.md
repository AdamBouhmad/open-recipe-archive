## Ingredients

- 1 Calf's Head
- 2 Large Onions
- 1 Can of Mushrooms
- 1 Lemon
- Thyme and Bay Leaf
- 1 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Wineglass of Sherry
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Pint of Consomme or Water
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- Salt and Pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Slice the onions and mince the garlic
2. Put the butter into a stew pot, and as it melts add the onions
3. Add one tablespoonful of flour, sifted well, and as this becomes brown, add one pint of consomme or water
4. Add the chopped thyme and bay leaf and the peel of one lemon, cut very fine, and the juice
5. Let all this simmer for about ten minutes
6. Cut up the calf's head and add it to the mixture
7. After fifteen minutes add a half can of mushrooms
8. In a few minutes add one small glass of Sherry wine
9. Let it all cook about ten minutes, then season well according to taste
10. Let all cook about half an hour longer
11. When ready to serve, place the calf's head in the middle of the dish, pour the gravy over and range the mushrooms around
12. Garnish with pieces of a flat omelet made from the two eggs and cut into diamond shapes, alternating with toast buttered and cut into diamond-shaped Croutons
