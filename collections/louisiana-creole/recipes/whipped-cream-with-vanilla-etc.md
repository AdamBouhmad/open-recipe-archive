## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Cream
- 2 Ounces of Powdered Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla Extract

## Directions

1. Put a pint of sweet cream into a basin, add the sugar and the essence
2. Always remember to add the essence before whipping the cream
3. Proceed to whip according to the directions given in Whipped Cream
