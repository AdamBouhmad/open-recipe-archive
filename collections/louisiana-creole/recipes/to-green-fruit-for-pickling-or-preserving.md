## Ingredients

- vine leaves
- a teaspoonful of beaten or ground alum
- water
- saleratus

## Directions

1. Put vine leaves under, between, and over the fruit in a brass kettle
2. over the leaves sprinkle a teaspoonful of beaten or ground alum
3. cover the fruit to be greened, with water
4. boil it gently with the leaves and alum
5. if not a fine green, take more leaves and dust a little saleratus over them
6. Spread them out to cool when green
7. proceed to preserve or pickle them as desired
