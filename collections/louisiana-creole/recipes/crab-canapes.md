## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of Toast
- 8 Hard-Shelled Crabs
- 1 Ounce of Butter
- 1 Small Onion
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Gill of Broth or Water
- 2 Ounces of Grated Parmesan Cheese
- 2 Ounces of Grated Swiss Cheese

## Directions

1. Boil the crabs
2. Pick out all the meat from the claws and bodies, and season nicely with salt and pepper
3. Put one ounce of butter into the saucepan, and add a finely minced onion
4. Let this cook on a slow fire for two minutes, but by no means allow either butter or onion to brown
5. Add a tablespoonful of flour, and stir constantly for a minute and a half
6. Add a gill of broth or water
7. Stir well and let this mixture cook for five minutes, stirring constantly
8. Add the crab meat and let it cook for fifteen minutes longer, stirring occasionally with a wooden spoon
9. Turn the mixture into a dish and let it cool for about a quarter of an hour
10. Put a tablespoonful of butter into a saucepan, and add immediately a tablespoonful of flour and blend well
11. Let this cook for three minutes, stirring all the time
12. Add two ounces of grated Parmesan and two ounces of grated Swiss cheese
13. Stir all well together, blending thoroughly
14. Turn into a vessel to cool
15. Cut six slices of bread the full length and width of the loaf, using preferably the Pan Bread or delicate French loaf
16. Let the thickness of each slice be about a quarter of an inch
17. Neatly trim off the crust
18. Fry the bread in a saucepan with a tablespoonful of butter till they have reached a golden brown
19. Let them cool, draining off all butter
20. Divide the crab forcemeat and the cheese separately into six equal parts
21. Place a layer of the crab forcemeat one-quarter of an inch thick on each slice of toast
22. Take the six portions of cheese and roll each into a ball-shaped form about two inches in diameter
23. Arrange them on each portion of toast nicely and equally
24. Place in a dish and brown in the oven for five minutes
25. Send the Canapes to the table hot in the same dish in which they were baked
