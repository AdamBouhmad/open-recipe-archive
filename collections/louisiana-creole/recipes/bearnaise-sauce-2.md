## Ingredients

- 6 Shallots
- 0.5 Clove of Garlic
- 0.5 Gill of French Vinegar
- 1 Tablespoonful Each of Flour and Butter
- Yolks of Four Eggs
- A Grated Nutmeg
- 0.5 Lemon's Juice
- Glace
- White Wine
- White Consomme
- Pepper and Salt

## Directions

1. Chop the shallots and mince the garlic very fine
2. Blend the butter and flour, or take a good tablespoonful of Glace, and moisten with a tablespoonful of White Wine and good white consomme, till you have about a pint
3. Set on the stove in a porcelain-lined saucepan
4. Add the pepper and salt and butter, and a quarter of a teaspoonful of grated nutmeg
5. Add half a gill of vinegar and the juice of a lemon, according to taste and acidity
6. When of the consistency approaching starch, take from the fire and add the yolks of four eggs, beaten well, stirring all the time, till you have the consistency of a thick starch
7. Serve immediately with broiled steak, broiled chops, or broiled fish
