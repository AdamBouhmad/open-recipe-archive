## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Cream
- % Pound of Sugar
- 1 Ounce of Candled Orange Flowers
- The Yolks of 3 Eggs

## Directions

1. Bruise the orange flowers, and mix and stir them with the boiling milk
2. Take from the fire, and add the sugar, and dissolve well, and then the beaten yolks of the eggs, mix well
3. Freeze in the manner above indicated
