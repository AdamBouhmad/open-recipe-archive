## Ingredients

- Three lbs. of strawberries
- 8 ounces of red currants
- 1 pint of water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Crush the strawberries and currants in a sieve and let the juice run into a deep dish or bowl
2. Pour the water over the strawberries remaining in the sieve
3. Melt the sugar in a little water over the fire and add to the juice
4. Pour it into the mould
5. The taste must be the guide for the quantity of sugar
