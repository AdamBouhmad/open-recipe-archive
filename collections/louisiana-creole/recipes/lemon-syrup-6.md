## Ingredients

- 8 Ounces of Lemon Juice
- 15 Ounces of Sugar
- 3/4 Ounce of Extract of Lemon

## Directions

1. Press the juice from the lemons and grate the zest of four into the syrup; set aside in a cool place for six or eight hours.
2. Then strain carefully through a bag.
3. Make a Plain Syrup, using the proportions of fifteen ounces of sugar to a pint and a half of water.
4. Prepare a gallon of syrup, and then proceed as in the General Directions for Making Syrups.
5. Add the essence when about to bottle.
