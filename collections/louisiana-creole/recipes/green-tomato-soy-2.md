## Ingredients

- ½ bushel of green tomatoes
- 1½ dozen of white onions
- salt
- vinegar
- sugar
- sweet oil
- box of mustard
- black pepper
- mace
- cloves
- allspice
- mustard seed
- coriander
- celery seed

## Directions

1. Take ½ bushel of green tomatoes and 1½ dozen of white onions
2. Slice them thin and put them in jars, with a tablespoonful of salt between each layer
3. Let them stand 24 hours
4. Drain off the water and boil them 10 or 15 minutes in vinegar
5. Take them out and put them again in jars, a layer of tomatoes and onions, spice, a little sugar, and a tablespoonful of sweet oil, until the jar is filled
6. Mix a box of mustard in the vinegar, and pour over them till all are covered
7. Use black pepper, mace, cloves, allspice, mustard seed, coriander and celery seed as spices to be bruised
