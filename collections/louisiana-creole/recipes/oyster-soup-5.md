## Ingredients

- 3 pints of large, fresh oysters
- 2 tablespoonfuls of butter
- flour
- a bunch of sweet herbs
- 1 saucer full of chopped celery
- 1 quart of rich milk
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take the liquor from the 3 pints of oysters, strain it, and set it on the fire to make the soup
2. Roll the butter in flour
3. Put the butter, bunch of sweet herbs, chopped celery, and rich milk into the liquor
4. As soon as the ingredients boil, take out the herbs and put in the oysters just before you send it to table
5. Season with pepper to your taste
