## Ingredients

- 6 Young Wild Pigeons
- 1 Onion
- 1 Tablespoonful of Lard
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Sprig Thyme
- 1 Sprig Bay Leaf
- 1 Sprig Parsley
- Pie Paste
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Clean and cut the young pigeons into small pieces of about two inches in length and make a plain fricassee.
2. Prepare a Vol-au-Vent Paste and fill a tin pan of about two quarts with the paste.
3. Pour in pigeons and gravy, and let bake in the oven till the top crust is nicely browned.
4. Always bake the under crust first.
5. Alternatively, after cleaning the pigeons, stuff each daintily with oyster or egg dressing and then loosen the joints with a knife without separating them.
6. Put into a stewpan and make a plain fricassee as indicated.
7. Let them cook until tender, and season with salt and pepper.
8. Fill the pie dish with the pie paste, put in the birds, pour over the gravy, cover with a crust and bake.
