## Ingredients

- Tenderloin or porterhouse steaks
- fat of the meat
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- blood from the steak
- tomato sauce or catsup

## Directions

1. Have a clear fire of coals to broil on; rub the gridiron with a little fat of the meat
2. Lay on the steak without salting, let it broil gently until one side is done
3. Turn the steak and catch the blood as you turn it, to make the gravy rich
4. If the steak is large and firm, take a quarter of a pound of butter and work into it pepper and salt
5. When the steak is done lay it on to this seasoned butter, keep it hot until the butter melts, turn the steak in it a few times
6. Put the blood with the gravy and serve hot, with tomato sauce or catsup
