## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 1 pint milk
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- the whites of six eggs
- the juice of one lemon
- two tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar
- a cream sauce

## Directions

1. Boil the rice with the milk, and add the whites of three eggs, well beaten with the sugar.
2. Stir well, and flavor with the juice of one lemon.
3. Take from the fire as it thickens well, and set in a dish to cool.
4. Form the rice into small balls of about two and a half inches square.
5. Have the rest of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth, with two tablespoonfuls of powdered sugar.
6. Cover the tops of the balls with the mixture and place in the stove to heat.
7. Let the balls remain about four minutes, without browning.
8. Take out, and serve with a Cream Sauce or just as they are.
