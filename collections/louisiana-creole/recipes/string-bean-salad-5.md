## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Cold Boiled String Beans
- French Dressing (plain)

## Directions

1. Only very young and tender beans should be used for this salad.
2. Boil as directed under the heading Vegetables.
3. Put the beans in a salad bowl and allow to cool well.
4. Serve with a plain French dressing, or, better still, a simple dressing of vinegar and a dash of Cayenne.
