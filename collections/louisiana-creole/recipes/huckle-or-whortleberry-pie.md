## Ingredients

- a quart of picked huckleberries
- pie paste
- a teacupful of brown sugar
- half a teacupful of water
- a teaspooonful of flour
- a saltspoonful of salt
- half a nutmeg

## Directions

1. Put a quart of picked huckleberries into a basin of water
2. Take off whatever floats
3. Take up the berries by the handful
4. Pick out all the stems and unripe berries, and put the rest into a dish
5. Line a buttered pie dish with a pie paste
6. Put in the berries half an inch deep
7. To a quart of berries put a teacupful of brown sugar, and half a teacupful of water
8. Dredge a teaspooonful of flour over
9. Throw in a saltspoonful of salt, and half a nutmeg grated
10. Cover the pie, cut a slit in the centre, or make several incisions on either side of it
11. Press the two crusts together around the edge
12. Trim it off neatly with a sharp knife
13. Bake in a quick oven for three-quarters of an hour
