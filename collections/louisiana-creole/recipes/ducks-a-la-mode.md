## Ingredients

- 2 fine ducks
- butter
- flour
- 1/2 pint of good gravy
- 1/2 pint of red wine
- 2 eschallots
- 1 anchovy
- sweet herbs
- lemon

## Directions

1. Cut the ducks into quarters
2. Fry them in butter a little brown
3. Pour off all the fat and throw a little flour over them
4. Add 1/2 pint of good gravy, 1/2 pint of red wine, 2 eschallots, 1 anchovy and a few sweet herbs
5. Cover them close
6. Let them stew 15 minutes
7. Take out the herbs
8. Skim off the grease and let the sauce be as thick as cream
9. Send it to table
10. Garnish it with lemon
