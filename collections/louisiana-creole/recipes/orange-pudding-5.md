## Ingredients

- one large orange of a deep color and smooth, thin rind
- 1 lime
- ½ lb. of powdered loaf sugar
- ½ lb. of fresh butter
- 8 eggs
- 1 glass of mixed wine and brandy
- 1 teaspoonfal of rose water
- 5 ounces of sifted flour

## Directions

1. Have ready a sheet of puff paste made of 5 ounces of sifted flour and ½ lb. of fresh butter
2. Lay the paste in a buttered dish
3. Trim and notch the edges
4. Put in the mixture
5. Bake it 1 hour in a moderate oven
6. Grate loaf sugar over it before you send it to table
