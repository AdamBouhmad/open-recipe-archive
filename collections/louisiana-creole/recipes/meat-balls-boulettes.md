## Ingredients

- 1 pound of raw or left-over meat
- 1 tablespoonful of butter
- 1 onion
- juice of a lemon
- salt
- pepper
- Cayenne

## Directions

1. Take one pound of steak from the upper round and mince and chop very fine.
2. Add to it one tablespoonful of lemon juice, one onion (well grated), one tablespoonful of melted butter, salt, pepper and Cayenne to taste.
