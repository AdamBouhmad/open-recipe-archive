## Ingredients

- 2 Pounds of Beef Suet
- 1 1/2 Pounds of Flour
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- The Yolks of 8 Eggs
- 1 Pound of Raisins
- 1 Pound of Currants
- 1/2 Pound of Citron
- 1 Glass of Madeira Wine
- 2 Glasses of Brandy
- 1 Nutmeg
- A Pinch of Cinnamon
- Milk

## Directions

1. Chop finely two pounds of beef suet.
2. Take one and a half pounds of flour, mix with suet; add to this one pound of sugar, the yolks of eight eggs, one pound of raisins, stoned and floured; one pound of currants, one-quarter pound citron, chopped fine; one glass Madeira wine, two glasses brandy, one nutmeg, grated, and a little cinnamon.
3. Beat the whole well together, adding milk till of proper consistency.
4. Wrap in a cloth, previously scalded and floured, leaving room for the pudding to swell; boil six hours, and serve with Brandy or Hard Sauce.
