## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Milk
- 1 Pint of Flour
- 3 Eggs
- 2 Teaspoonfuls of Baking Powder
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs separately till very light and frosty
2. Stir in the unskimmed milk and the flour that has been twice sifted with the baking powder
3. Add the whites of the eggs, beaten to a stiff froth
4. Pour the mixture into buttered cake tins
5. Bake in a quick oven till a light brown, and serve either hot or cold
