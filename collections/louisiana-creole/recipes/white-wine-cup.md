## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Best Sauterne
- 3/4 of a Sliced Lemon
- 3/4 of a Sliced Orange
- A Dash of Old French Cognac
- A Dash of Maraschino
- 1 Cup of Pineapple Juice
- 1 Large Piece of Ice
- 1 1/2 Pint of Seltzer Water
- 1/2 Cup of Sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar and the Seltzer water, add the wine and fruit juices and liquor
2. Add the cherries and sliced lemon and orange
3. Stir well
4. Let cool and serve in small, thin tumblers
5. Decorate the tops with sprigs of fresh green mint, with two or three cherries laid on top
