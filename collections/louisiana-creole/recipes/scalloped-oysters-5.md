## Ingredients

- oysters
- bread crumbs
- butter
- pepper and salt
- grated crackers or small champion biscuit
- ham

## Directions

1. Take the oysters from their liquor
2. Place them in the bottom of a dish or pan
3. Cover them with bread crumbs, butter, pepper and salt
4. Add another layer of oysters, then the bread crumbs, etc.
5. Continue this till the dish is full
6. Cover the top either with grated crackers or small champion biscuit
7. Bake about 30 minutes
8. Serve with slices of ham
