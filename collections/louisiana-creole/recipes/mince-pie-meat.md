## Ingredients

- a nice tender piece of beef which is free from gristle, skin or strings
- hot water

## Directions

1. Put the meat in hot water, enough to cover it
2. boil it gently until turning a fork in it will break it
3. set it to become cold, then take out all the bone and gristle parts
4. If the tongue is used peel off the skin, chop it very fine
