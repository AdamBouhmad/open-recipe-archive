## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 large spoonfuls of rice
- cold milk
- 1 quart of boiling milk
- 5 eggs
- lemon
- sugar to the taste

## Directions

1. Mix 2 1/2 large spoonfuls of rice in a little cold milk
2. stir it into 1 quart of boiling milk
3. let it boil 15 minutes, stirring it constantly
4. when cold, add 5 eggs, a little lemon, sugar to the taste, and bake it 1 hour
5. put a paste, or not, as you prefer, on the dish
