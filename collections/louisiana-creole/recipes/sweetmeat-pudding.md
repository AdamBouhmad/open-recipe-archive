## Ingredients

- paste
- preserves or jam or marmalade
- flour
- cloth

## Directions

1. Make the paste, roll it out in square pieces
2. Take the preserves, and put them in with a knife (any kind of jam or marmalade)
3. Have each layer different
4. Do this till you have a sufficient quantity
5. Roll it round
6. Sprinkle the cloth with flour and put the pudding on to boil
