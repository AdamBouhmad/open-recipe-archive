## Ingredients

- 6 Slices of Toast
- 1 Cup of Chicken Forcemeat
- 1 Ounce of Butter
- 1 Gill of Cream
- The Whites of 2 Hard-Boiled Eggs
- 2 Ounces of Parmesan Cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare a Chicken Forcemeat
2. Prepare six slices of toast, cut square or in circles
3. Add an ounce of butter and one gill of cream to the chicken forcemeat
4. Work well together, and then set to cool
5. Spread the toast lightly with butter
6. Spread over each slice a portion of the chicken forcemeat to the thickness of one-quarter of an inch
7. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese
8. Set in the oven and bake for five minutes
9. Decorate in the center with delicately cut pieces of the white of hard-boiled eggs
10. Serve
