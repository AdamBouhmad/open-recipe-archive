## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Pure cider vinegar
- Fine white sugar in the proportion of one and two-thirds pounds to every quart of juice

## Directions

1. Peel the oranges
2. Slice them very thin and cover with pure cider vinegar, and let them stand for three or four days
3. Mash them through a cloth, straining as long as the juice runs clear
4. To every quart of juice allow one and two-thirds pounds of fine white sugar
5. Boil all together for about ten minutes, and skim very carefully, until no scum rises upon the surface
6. Take the mixture from the fire, and when it cools somewhat, bottle it
7. Put a tablespoonful of this extract in a glass of cold ice water in warm weather
