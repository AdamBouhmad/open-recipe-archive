## Ingredients

- 1 Cup Rice
- ¾ Cup Sugar
- ¾ Grated Nutmeg
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla
- The Peel of a Quarter of an Orange, Cut In Dice

## Directions

1. Boil the rice very soft, and then add the milk, and let it come to a good boil.
2. Add the orange peel, cut in quarter dice.
3. Beat the eggs and sugar well together till very light, and add to the boiling custard.
4. Cook for one minute longer.
5. Then take from the fire, and add the vanilla and one-quarter of the grated nutmeg.
6. Place all in a dish to cool.
7. Sprinkle the top with grated nutmeg, and serve cold.
8. If you wish to have a baked custard, place the custard in a pan or in cups, set in the oven to brown, and serve hot with a Cream Sauce.
