## Ingredients

- salsify
- water
- 5 eggs
- 1 pint milk
- 1 large spoonful butter
- flour
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Scrape the salsify and lay it in water to prevent its becoming black
2. Boil it quick and mash it fine
3. Prepare a batter with 5 eggs, beaten very light with yolk and white separate
4. Add 1 pint of milk and a large spoonful of butter
5. Thicken the whole with flour to the consistency of fritter batter
6. Stir in the salsify
7. Season with pepper and salt to the taste
8. Fry them nicely
