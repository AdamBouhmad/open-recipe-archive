## Ingredients

- 12 Shallots
- 2 Cloves of Garlic
- 1 Pickle
- 1 Tablespoonful of Mustard
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vinegar
- The Yolk of an Egg
- 4 Sprigs of Chopped Parsley
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme and Bay Leaf
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Chop the shallots, greens and white all very fine, and mince the cloves of two garlics very fine
2. Put these in a bowl, and add one pickle of about three inches long, chopped very fine
3. Drain the pickle first of all water
4. Add a good bunch of parsley, chopped very fine
5. Mix all this together in a bowl, and add one tablespoonful of mustard
6. Mix well
7. Add a good tablespoonful of vinegar and salt and pepper to taste
8. Beat the yolk of an egg and mix well in the sauce
9. Serve cold, with cold meats, turkey or fowl
