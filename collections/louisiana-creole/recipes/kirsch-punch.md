## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Fine Louisiana Oranges
- 1 Gill of Kirsch
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Pineapple Syrup
- 2 Lemons
- 2 Dozen Strawberries

## Directions

1. Take fine, fresh Louisiana oranges, and press out all the juice, and mash the pulp and strain through a sieve
2. To each pint of juice allow one gill of Kirsch and two of the juice of two lemons
3. Sweeten to taste
4. Flavor with Pineapple Syrup, add whole strawberries when in season, and freeze as in Creole Punch
5. Serve in glasses
