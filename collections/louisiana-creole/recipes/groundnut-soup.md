## Ingredients

- half a pint of shelled ground nuts
- 2 spoonfuls of flour
- 1 pint of oysters
- 1 pint and a half of water
- 1 or 2 red peppers

## Directions

1. Beat up the ground nuts well
2. Add two spoonfuls of flour and mix well
3. Put to them a pint of oysters and a pint and a half of boiling water
4. Throw in a red pepper or two if small
