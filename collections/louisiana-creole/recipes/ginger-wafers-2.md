## Ingredients

- 1 Large Spoon of Ground Ginger
- 4 Ounces of Molasses
- 8 Ounces of Flour
- 8 Ounces of Sifted Sugar
- Whites of 4 Eggs
- 1/2 Pint of Cream
- 1 Wineglassful of Brandy
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vanilla Sugar

## Directions

1. Beat the whites of the eggs to a cream and add it to the flour and milk, salt and sugar, molasses and ginger.
2. Work all well together very light in a bowl, until you have a smooth batter.
3. Add the brandy and Ginger Extract and beat vigorously ten minutes longer.
4. Heat the wafer irons over a clear charcoal fire.
5. Brush the irons with butter.
6. Place the wafers within and bake on both sides to a light fawn color.
