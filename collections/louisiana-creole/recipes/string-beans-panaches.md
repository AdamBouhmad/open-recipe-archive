## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of String Beans
- 1 Pint of Lima Beans
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Boil the string beans and cut them into pieces of about three-fourths of an inch in length; mix them with an equal quantity of boiled Lima beans.
2. Put them in a saucepan with two tablespoonfuls of butter; let them saute for five minutes, and toss continually while cooking.
3. Season with salt and pepper to taste, place on a hot dish, sprinkle with finely-chopped parsley and serve hot.
