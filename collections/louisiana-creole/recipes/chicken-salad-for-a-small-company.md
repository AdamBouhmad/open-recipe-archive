## Ingredients

- four eggs
- yolks of two
- a spoonful of mixed mustard
- an ounce of sweet oil
- a saltspoonful of salt
- black pepper
- four tablespoonfuls of good vinegar
- one-half a teaspoonful of sugar
- boiled or baked chicken
- three heads of celery
- pepper
- salt
- six or seven heads of white-heart lettuce

## Directions

1. Boil four eggs hard, throw them in cold water
2. When cool, take the yolks of two in a bowl, pour over them a spoonful of mixed mustard, an ounce of sweet oil, a saltspoonful of salt, and a little black pepper
3. Mix this carefully, pouring in the oil a little at a time
4. When it is smooth, pour in four tablespoonfuls of good vinegar, and one-half a teaspoonful of sugar
5. The chicken is supposed to be already boiled or baked
6. When cold, pick all the flesh from the bones and pile it in the centre of a glass bowl, or dish
7. Mix with it three heads of celery, cut up fine, and season it with pepper and salt
8. About the time you wish it served, take six or seven heads of white-heart lettuce, split them, and place them closely around the cut-up chicken, and pour over it all the dressing
