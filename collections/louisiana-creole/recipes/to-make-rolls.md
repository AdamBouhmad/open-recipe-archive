## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of flour
- 1 lb. of butter
- 5 eggs
- 1 pint of yeast

## Directions

1. Rub butter into flour, beat up eggs and mix with it, mix in yeast, cover up to lighten
2. Then bake in rolls
