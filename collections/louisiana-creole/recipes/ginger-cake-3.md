## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Flour
- 2 Eggs
- 3/4 Cup of Milk or Sour Cream
- 1 Teaspoonful of Soda or Baking Powder
- 3/4 Cup of Butter or Lard
- 1 Tablespoonful of Ground Ginger, or 1 Tablespoonful Each of Ground Cinnamon and Ginger
- 1 1/2 Cups of Louisiana Molasses

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks of the eggs to a cream and then add the melted butter, beating till very light.
2. Sift the baking powder and the flour together, and add the ginger, mixing well.
3. Then add the milk and the molasses to the butter and eggs, mixing thoroughly.
4. When well blended, add the flour and ginger and baking powder (mixed) and beat vigorously.
5. Finally add the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth, turn into a well-greased tin, and bake in a moderate oven for forty-five minutes.
6. Use the broom wisp test before taking the cake out of the oven.
