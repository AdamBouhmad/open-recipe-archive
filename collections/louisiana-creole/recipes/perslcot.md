## Ingredients

- 10 Ounces of Bitter Almonds
- 1 Ounce of Cinnamon
- 2.5 Gallons of Alcohol or Fine Brandy
- 6 Pounds of Sugar
- 3 Quarts of Water

## Directions

1. Peel the almonds and pound and mix with the cinnamon, and steep for fifteen days in the alcohol
2. Distill in a water bath
3. Add the syrup
4. Color with Cochineal or Caramel
5. Bottle for use
