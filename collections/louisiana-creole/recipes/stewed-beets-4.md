## Ingredients

- beets
- butter
- flour
- parsley
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Boil them first
2. Scrape and slice them
3. Put them into a stewpan, with a piece of butter, rolled in flour, with finely chopped parsley, a little vinegar, salt and pepper
4. Set the pan on hot coals and let the beets stew for 1 hour
