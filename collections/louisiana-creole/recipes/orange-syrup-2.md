## Ingredients

- Ripe and thin-skinned oranges
- A pound and a quarter of white sugar to every pint of juice

## Directions

1. Select ripe and thin-skinned fruit
2. Squeeze the juice, and to every pint, add a pound and a quarter of white sugar
3. Boil it slowly, and skim as long as any scum rises
4. Take it off, let it grow cold, and bottle it
5. Secure the corks well
