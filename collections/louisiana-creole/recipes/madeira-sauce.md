## Ingredients

- 2 Gills of Espagnole Sauce or Brown Sauce
- 1 Gill of Truffles, Cut in Two
- 1 Gill of Mushrooms, Cut in Two
- 1 Glass of Madeira Wine
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Make a Sauce Espagnole and let it boil for about five minutes
2. Add salt and pepper to taste, and the mushrooms and truffles, cut in pieces
3. Let them boil for ten minutes, and then stir in the wine
4. If you have not the Madeira, use Sherry wine
5. Serve with Filet of Beef Roasted, etc.
