## Ingredients

- 2½ cups of unsifted flour
- 2 cups of sugar
- 1 cup of butter
- whites of 8 eggs
- ½ cup of sweet milk
- 2 teaspoonfuls of yeast powder

## Directions

1. sift yeast powder in the flour
2. cream butter and sugar together
3. add the whites of the eggs well beaten
4. add the flour
5. add the milk
