## Ingredients

- 2 or 3 very nice pineapples
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take 2 or 3 very nice pineapples
2. Pare and grate them into a bowl
3. Put the grated pineapple on a sieve and let it drain well
4. Press it down to get out every drop of juice
5. Weaken it as much as you please with water
6. Make it very sweet, as all things when frozen have less sweetness
