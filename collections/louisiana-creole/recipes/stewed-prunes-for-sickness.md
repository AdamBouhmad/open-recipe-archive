## Ingredients

- prunes
- water
- clear brown sugar

## Directions

1. Wash the prunes
2. put them in a stew pan
3. cover them with water
4. to each pound of prunes put a cupful of clear brown sugar
5. Cover the stew-pan and let them boil slowly, until the syrup is thick and rich
