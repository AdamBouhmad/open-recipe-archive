## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 quarts of consomme or bouillon
- salt

## Directions

1. Break the eggs and drop them one by one into boiling salted water, being careful not to allow the water to boil when once the eggs are in it.
2. Have the frying pan to one side of the stove, and cook slowly until the eggs are firm.
3. When firm, carefully remove with a spoon or perforated skimmer, and lay in cold water for a moment, until the edges are trimmed evenly.
4. Transfer to the tureen and pour the boiling soup very gently into the tureen and serve.
5. One egg and about a half pint of broth should be allowed to each person.
