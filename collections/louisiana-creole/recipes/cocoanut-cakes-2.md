## Ingredients

- a cocoanut
- half a pound loaf sugar
- two tablespoonfuls of water

## Directions

1. pare the cocoanut and grate half a pound
2. allow the same quantity of loaf sugar
3. Dissolve the sugar in two tablespoonfuls of water, place it on the fire
4. when the syrup is boiling hot, stir in the cocoanut
5. Continue to stir it until it is thick like candy
6. pour it out on a buttered pan, and cut it across in shapes, or use a round cake cutter
