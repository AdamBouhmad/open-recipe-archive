## Ingredients

- whites of 11 eggs
- 10 ounces of granulated sugar
- 5 ounces of flour
- 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1 teaspoonful of extract of vanilla

## Directions

1. Sift the mixture 4 times
2. Beat the eggs
3. Add the sugar and flour
4. Bake 40 minutes
