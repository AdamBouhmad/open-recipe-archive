## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoonfuls of good yeast
- 1 pint of lukewarm water
- a little salt
- sifted flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Put the yeast into the water
2. Add salt
3. Stir in gradually as much flour as will make a thick batter
4. Cover the pan, and set it in a warm place to rise
5. When quite light and the middle is hot, grease and set muffin rings on it, having first buttered them round the inside
6. Put a ladleful of the batter for each ring
7. Bake them over a quick fire
8. When sent to table do not cut them; split them by pulling them open with the hands
