## Ingredients

- apples
- rind of 1/2 a lemon grated
- sugar
- 1 pint milk
- 1 pint sweet cream
- yolk of 1 egg

## Directions

1. Scald as many apples as will make, when pulped, a thick layer at the bottom of a dish
2. Mix the rind of 1/2 a lemon, grated fine, and as much sugar as will sweeten to the taste
3. Mix 1 pint of milk, the same of sweet cream, and the yolk of 1 egg
4. Scald it over the fire, and stir it all the time
5. Do not let it boil
6. Add a little sugar, if required, and let it stand to cool
7. Lay over it the apples with a spoon, and then put on a whip made the day before
