## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Rich Milk
- 4 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Gill of Yeast
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- Flour

## Directions

1. Scald the milk and add the butter, and let it grow tepid.
2. Then add the yeast and dissolve well.
3. Add the salt, and now add the flour sufficient to make a light dough.
4. Set to rise, and when twice its bulk work lightly, and roll out very thin, and cut into pieces about two and a half inches square.
5. Stick well with a fork through and through, and bake in a slow oven, as you would soda crackers.
