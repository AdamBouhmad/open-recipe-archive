## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Fine Bananas
- 1/2 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pint of Sugar or Cream
- The Juice of 2 Oranges

## Directions

1. Peel the bananas, cut them very fine and mash well.
2. Add the cream or water and the orange juice, turn into the freezer and pack away in ice and rock salt.
3. Set to freeze according to directions given for freezing Ice Cream.
