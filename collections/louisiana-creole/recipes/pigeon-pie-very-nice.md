## Ingredients

- six pigeons
- their own livers
- a little bacon
- some butter
- parsley
- rolled cracker or a small piece of bread
- salt
- slices of veal or beef
- chopped parsley
- mushrooms
- pepper
- salt
- butter
- the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
- plain beef-stock or water
- gravy

## Directions

1. Truss the pigeons and stuff them with their own livers, a little bacon, some butter, parsley, and rolled cracker or a small piece of bread
2. Salt to taste
3. Cover the bottom of the baking dish with slices of veal or beef
4. Season with chopped parsley, mushrooms, pepper, salt, and butter
5. Place the pigeons on this
6. Lay between each two pigeons the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs
7. Cover with a nice pie crust
8. Parboil the pigeons a little, also the beef, before putting them in the pan
9. Keep the water they were boiled in to fill up the pie
10. Add plain beef-stock or water as the pie bakes to keep it very moist
