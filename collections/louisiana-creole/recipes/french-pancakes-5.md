## Ingredients

- 3/4 pound of flour
- 4 eggs
- 3/4 pint of cold milk
- 4 ounces of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Sift the flour into a bowl.
2. Break in the eggs; beat well, and add one ounce of powdered sugar.
3. Mix thoroughly with a spatula, and then add the cold milk, pouring it in gradually.
4. Mix well for six minutes.
5. Have ready a clean griddle; butter lightly, and when hot, drop on it about two large spoonfuls of the batter at a time.
6. Let the cakes bake two minutes on each side, and then turn and bake two minutes on the other.
7. Turn the pancake on a dish; sprinkle generously with powdered sugar.
8. Continue baking the cakes and turning on the dish, sprinkling each in turn with the powdered sugar, till all is used.
