## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. of sifted flour
- 1 lb. of powdered sugar
- 3 lbs. of butter
- 4 eggs
- ½ cup of the best brewers' yeast
- 1½ pints of milk
- 1 teaspoonful of powdered cinnamon
- 1 grated nutmeg
- 1 tablespoonful of rose water
- lard for frying
- loaf sugar for coating

## Directions

1. fry them in lard
2. grate fine loaf sugar over them
