## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Flour
- 3 Cups of Sour Milk or Warm Water
- 1 Teaspoonful of Soda
- 3 Eggs, Beaten Very Light
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt

## Directions

1. Mix the flour and meal, and pour on the milk or warm water.
2. Make a batter somewhat stiffier than for Buckwheat Cakes, and add the eggs, well-beaten, the whites and yolks separately, and finally add the soda, dissolved in a little warm water.
3. Bake on a griddle, making the cakes large and generous.
4. Serve with Louisiana Syrup.
