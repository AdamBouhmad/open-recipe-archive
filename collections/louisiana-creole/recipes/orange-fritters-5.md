## Ingredients

- 2 Large Louisiana Oranges
- Creole Fritter Batter
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. Pare the oranges, taking off every particle of the white inner skin.
2. Then slice nicely, and take out the seeds.
3. Sprinkle with sugar, and let them stand for an hour.
4. Then add the juice to the fritter batter, and proceed as in Apple Fritters.
5. Alternatively, place the oranges thus sliced over a slow fire, add a little water and several tablespoonfuls of sugar, and let them cook till you have a light marmalade.
6. Take it off, let it cool, cover the slices of orange well with the jellied juice, dip in the batter, and proceed as directed in recipe for Apple Fritters.
