## Ingredients

- A Mayonnaise Sauce
- 8 Shallots
- 1/2 Clove of Garlic
- 1 Pickle
- A Handful of Parsley, Minced Fine
- 1 Teaspoonful Mustard
- Salt, Cayenne and Black Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Prepare the Mayonnaise
2. Put in a bowl a half dozen shallots, greens and all, and chop fine
3. Add a handful of parsley, chopped fine; and the half-minced clove, and one whole pickle, well chopped
4. Mix all this together and put in a cloth and strain out the juice by pressing
5. Add this juice to the Mayonnaise, and add one teaspoonful of mustard, salt, Cayenne and black pepper to taste
6. Serve with filet of trout, etc
