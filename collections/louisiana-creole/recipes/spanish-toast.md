## Ingredients

- 1 Egg
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 1 Gill of Brandy or Rum (if desired)
- 3 Tablespoonfuls of Powdered Sugar
- ⅛ Ounce Each of Powdered Nutmeg and Cinnamon
- 6 Slices of Stale Bread
- Lard for Frying

## Directions

1. Beat the egg and sugar well, and add the milk and the rum or brandy if desired.
2. Have ready the slices of stale bread, cut in diamond shapes or square, and let them soak for about a half hour in the mixture.
3. Lay into a pan of boiling lard, and fry to a golden brown.
4. Skim out and drain on brown paper in the mouth of the oven, and place on a hot dish.
5. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and grated cinnamon and nutmeg, and serve with milk or cream.
