## Ingredients

- 1/4 Can of Truffles
- 1 Cup of Bread, Wet and Squeezed
- 1 Onion, Chopped Very Fine
- 1/8 Square Inch of Ham
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 1/2 Teaspoonfuls of Sage
- 1 Sprig Each of Thyme, Parsley and Bay Leaf, Minced Very Fine
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Proceed in exactly the same manner as for egg or oyster stuffing, using a quarter of a can of truffles chopped, instead of the oysters or egg
