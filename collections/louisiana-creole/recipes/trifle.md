## Ingredients

- 1 pint rich cream
- 4 teaspoonfuls white wine
- pounded loaf sugar

## Directions

1. Add to 1 pint of rich cream 4 teaspoonfuls of white wine
2. Sweeten it with pounded loaf sugar
3. Whisk it well and as the froth rises, lay it upon a sieve placed over a deep dish
4. As it drains, pour the cream into the pan in which it is whisked, till it is done
