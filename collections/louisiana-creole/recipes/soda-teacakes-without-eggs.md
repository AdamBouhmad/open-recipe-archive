## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- half a pound of butter
- one teaspoonful of fine soda
- one cup of milk
- half a nutmeg
- flour

## Directions

1. beat it to a cream
2. Dissolve a teaspoonful of fine soda in a cup of milk, and pour it into the batter
3. add half a nutmeg and flour to make a stiff batter
4. Bake in tin squares or a shallow pan, cook twenty minutes
