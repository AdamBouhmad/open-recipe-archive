## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Chestnuts
- 2 Cups of Sugar
- 1 Tablespoonfull of Butter

## Directions

1. Take fine, large Chestnuts, and transfix them on a hatpin or on small pointed sticks
2. Dip them in the Caramel, and then dry and Glacie them
