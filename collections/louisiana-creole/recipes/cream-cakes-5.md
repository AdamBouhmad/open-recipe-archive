## Ingredients

- butter
- 1 pint of milk
- flour

## Directions

1. Melt as much butter in a pint of milk as will make it as rich as cream
2. Make the flour into a paste with this
3. Knead it well
4. Roll it out frequently
5. Cut in squares
6. Bake on a griddle
