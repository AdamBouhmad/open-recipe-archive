## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Large Ears of Young Corn
- 1 Pint of Milk
- 4 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Salt
- 1/2 Teaspoonful of Black Pepper
- 1 Dessertspoonful of Butter

## Directions

1. Score the ears of corn down each row with a knife, and then cut from the cob.
2. With the knife press out all the pulp and corn juice, leaving the hulls in the cob.
3. Beat the yolks of the eggs well, and then add the corn.
4. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
5. Add melted butter, and then add the whites of the eggs beaten to a stiff froth.
6. Stir in carefully, and place the whole in a dish, which you will have buttered.
7. Set in the oven and bake for an hour, slowly at first, more rapidly towards the end of the last fifteen minutes.
8. Serve with roast beef, veal or lamb.
