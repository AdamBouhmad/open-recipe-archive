## Ingredients

- 3 Cups of Chopped Veal
- 1 Cup of Chopped Ham
- Salt, Pepper and Cayenne to Taste
- 1/2 Cup of Cream Sauce

## Directions

1. Utilize the left-over filet of veal and chop very fine
2. Add one cup of minced ham
3. Prepare a Cream Sauce in the proportions to make just a half cup
4. Season the chopped veal with this, add a dash of Cayenne and grated nutmeg
5. Spread upon hot buttered toast and serve at breakfast
