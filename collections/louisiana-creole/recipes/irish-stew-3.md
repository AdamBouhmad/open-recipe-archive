## Ingredients

- square, thick piece of beef from the round or sirloin
- water
- salt
- pepper
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut a square, thick piece of beef from the round or sirloin and trim off the fat
2. Put it into a stewpan with water enough to cover it
3. Season it slightly with salt and pepper
4. Let it stew slowly till tender all through
5. Then add potatoes, being first pared and cut into quarters
