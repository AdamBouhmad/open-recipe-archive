## Ingredients

- sweet breads
- cold water
- grated bread crumbs
- pepper
- salt
- lard
- hot water
- sherry
- nutmeg
- lemon juice
- flour

## Directions

1. Boil ten minutes to blanch
2. Throw them into cold water
3. Skim and lard them
4. Roll in grated bread crumbs, pepper and salt
5. Cook in boiling lard till quite tender
6. Lay them in a dish
7. Pour over the following gravy: a cup of hot water, a large wineglass of sherry, a little nutmeg and lemon juice, thickened with a tablespoonful of flour
