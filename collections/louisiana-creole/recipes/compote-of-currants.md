## Ingredients

- 1 Quart of Currants
- 3/4 Pound of Sugar
- 3/4 Cup of Water

## Directions

1. Select nice berries and pick clean and wash well in cold water, and drain them through a sieve.
2. Boil the sugar and add the berries.
3. Let them boil up once very gently.
4. Put them in a dish, pour the syrup over and serve.
