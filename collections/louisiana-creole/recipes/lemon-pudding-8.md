## Ingredients

- 1 nice lemon with a smooth thin rind
- 3 eggs
- 1 lb. of fine white sugar
- 1 lb. of fresh butter
- 1 tablespoonful of wine and brandy mixed
- 1 teaspoonful of rose water
- puff paste

## Directions

1. Grate the rind of the lemon
2. Cut it in half, and squeeze the juice of the lemon into the plate with the grated rind, taking out all the seeds
3. Mix the juice and rind well together
4. Stir the sugar and butter with a spoon till it is perfectly light and of the consistency of cream
5. Beat the eggs until they are quite smooth and as thick as a boiled custard
6. Stir the eggs gradually into the pan of butter and sugar
7. Add the seasoning by degrees; then the juice and rind of the lemon
8. Mix all the ingredients well together
9. Have ready a nice puff paste, enough to cover the edges and bottom of the dish
10. Pour the mixture in and bake the pudding 1/2 hour, in a moderate oven
