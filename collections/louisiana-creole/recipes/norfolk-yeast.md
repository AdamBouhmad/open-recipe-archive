## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of boiling water
- a large handful of hops
- 1 1/2 lbs. of flour
- a large teacupful of good yeast
- 1 teaspoonful of pearl ash per bottle

## Directions

1. Have ready 2 quarts of boiling water
2. Put into it a large handful of hops and let them boil 20 minutes
3. Sift into a pan 1 1/2 lbs. of flour
4. Strain the liquor from the hops
5. Pour half of it over the hops
6. Let the other half of the liquor stand till it is cool
7. Pour it gradually into the pan of flour, mixing it well
8. Stir into it a large teacupful of good yeast
9. Put it in bottles and cork tightly
10. In each bottle put a teaspoonful of pearl ash
