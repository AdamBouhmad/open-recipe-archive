## Ingredients

- 1 Dozen Peaches
- Sugar to Taste
- Whites of 6 Eggs
- 6 Tablespoonfuls of Sugar
- 1 Teaspoonful of Vanilla
- Rich pie crust

## Directions

1. Pare and stone the peaches, and stew according to recipe.
2. Line the bottoms of two pie pans with a rich pie crust, and then fill in with the peaches, which you will have sweetened to taste.
3. Bake in a quick oven twenty or twenty-five minutes.
4. Beat the whites of six eggs to a stiff froth with the sugar, which should be fine white powdered.
5. Add a teaspoonful of vanilla.
6. When the meringue can stand alone, cover the tops of the pies three-quarters of an inch thick, after baking, and set back in the oven and bake for two or three minutes, to a nice, delicate brown.
