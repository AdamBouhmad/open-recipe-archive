## Ingredients

- ½ Peck of Spinach
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Cup of Milk
- 6 Toasted Croutons
- Salt and Pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare the spinach as Plain Boiled Spinach, hash fine, and put in a saucepan with a tablespoonful of butter.
2. Season with salt and pepper, add a tablespoonful of flour, mixed with a cup of milk and a spoon of melted butter, and serve with toasted dice Croutons on the spinach.
