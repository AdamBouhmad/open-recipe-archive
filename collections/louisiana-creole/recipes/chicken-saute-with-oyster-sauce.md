## Ingredients

- chicken
- salt
- pepper
- flour
- oysters
- lemon juice
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Cut up the chicken as for frying
2. Roll each piece in salt, pepper, and sifted flour
3. Fry a light brown
4. Pour off most of the grease the chicken was fried in
5. In the same pan put three dozen oysters with a pint of their juice, and a spoonful of lemon juice
6. Let them simmer a few minutes
7. Serve with pieces of fried bread around the dish
