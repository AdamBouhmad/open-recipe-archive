## Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Flour
- 1 Glassful of Claret
- 1 Tablespoonful of Chopped Parsley
- 1/2 Pint of Water
- Salt, Pepper, Nutmeg, and Allspice to Taste

## Directions

1. Melt a tablespoonful of butter, stir in two tablespoonfuls of flour, and mix well until smooth.
2. Then add a wine-glassful of Claret, stirring all well.
3. To this add about half a pint of water, and season with pepper and salt and a little nutmeg and allspice.
4. Let the sauce simmer and reduce to about one-half.
5. Add parsley as a garnish and serve with boiled fish or boiled meat.
