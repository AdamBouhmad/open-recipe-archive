## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- 1 quart of flour
- 1 quart of buttermilk
- 1 large spoonful of soda
- a little salt

## Directions

1. Mix cream of tartar into flour
2. Dissolve soda in the buttermilk
3. Mix flour and buttermilk well and quickly
4. Add salt lastly
5. Pour into tins or make into rolls
6. Bake quickly
