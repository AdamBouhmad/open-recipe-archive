## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 Pound of Suet
- 1/2 Pound of Lean Veal
- 1 Tablespoonful of Flour
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1/2 Gill of Cold Milk
- 1 Teaspoonful Each of Minced Thyme and Parsley
- 1 Bay Leaf
- 2 Raw Eggs
- A Pinch of Grated Nutmeg
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Remove all the stringy tissue from the suet and pound in a mortar
2. Hash the veal well and mix with the meat
3. Take a tablespoonful of flour and blend well with half a gill of cold milk and a tablespoonful of melted butter and add to the suet and veal and blend well
4. Season highly with salt and pepper, and add a pinch of grated nutmeg
5. Add the yolks of two raw eggs and the white of one egg, and, when well blended, strain all through a sieve
6. Roll into balls and use as needed
