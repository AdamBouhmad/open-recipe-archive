## Ingredients

- 9 Eggs
- 1 Pound of White Pulverized Sugar
- 3/4 Pound of Flour
- The Juice of 1 Lemon
- 1 Cup of Mixed Preserved Fruits

## Directions

1. Prepare the cake as directed in Biscuits Glacés aux Confitures recipe.
2. Place on the baking sheet.
3. Drop different-sized small bits of fine preserves, orange, apricot, damson, red currant, etc., on the biscuit, and spread smoothly, so as to form a beautifully marbled pattern.
4. Cut out the cakes in any fanciful design.
5. Cover with Transparent Icing.
