## Ingredients

- half a cup of butter
- one and one-half cups of sugar
- two eggs
- three-quarters of a cup of sweet milk
- two cups of sifted flour
- one and one-half teaspoonfuls of soda
- one teaspoonful of cream of tartar
- one cup of pecans picked out and cut fine

## Directions

1. Bake in a small cake pan
