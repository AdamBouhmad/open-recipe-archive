## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- salt
- cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoonful brown sugar
- grated bread or cracker
- butter
- mace

## Directions

1. Peel the tomatoes, cut them in half and squeeze out the seeds
2. Put them into a stewpan, without any water
3. Add to them salt and cayenne pepper to your taste, 1 teaspoonful of brown sugar, some grated bread, or cracker, butter and mace (powdered)
4. Stew them slowly, till perfectly done
5. Send them to table very hot
