## Ingredients

- 2 ounces isinglass
- 1 pint boiling water
- bitter almonds blanched
- sweet almonds blanched
- rose water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Whip 2 ounces of isinglass, pour a pint of boiling water over it to dissolve it
2. Next day, break it up, add to it some bitter and sweet almonds blanched, beat them with rose water and sugar
3. Put it on the fire and let it boil up 5 or 6 times
4. Take it off and strain it through a muslin bag
