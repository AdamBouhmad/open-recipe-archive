## Ingredients

- juice of 6 lemons
- 2 quarts of water
- 2 lbs. of white sugar
- whites of 24 eggs

## Directions

1. Take the juice of 6 lemons to 2 quarts of water
2. Steep the rinds for some time in water and strain it
3. Add 2 lbs. of white sugar and the whites of 24 eggs
4. Beat until they will adhere to the bowl
5. Keep the freezer in motion until the cream be of the consistency of snow
