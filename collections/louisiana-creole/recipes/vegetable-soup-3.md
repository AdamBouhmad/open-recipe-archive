## Ingredients

- yellow split peas
- dried white beans
- cold water
- salt
- cayenne
- celery
- celery seed
- water
- turnips
- carrots
- parsnips
- potatoes
- onions
- salsify
- asparagus tops
- fresh butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Soak all night in cold water either two quarts of yellow split peas or two quarts of dried white beans
2. In the morning drain them
3. Season them with a very little salt and cayenne and a head of minced celery, or else a heaped tablespoonful of celery seed
4. Put them into a soup pot with four quarts of water
5. Boil them slowly till they are all dissolved
6. Stir them frequently
7. Have ready a quantity of fresh vegetables, such as turnips, carrots, parsnips, potatoes, onions, also salsify, and asparagus tops
8. Put in first the vegetables that require the longest boiling
9. Cut all vegetables into small pieces
10. Enrich the whole with some bits of fresh butter rolled in flour
11. Boil these vegetables in the soup till they are quite tender
12. Put it in a tureen and serve it up
