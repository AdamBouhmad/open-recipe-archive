## Ingredients

- 1 cupful of rice
- 1 quart of milk
- a pint of cream
- 1 cup of sugar
- 1 teaspoonful of salt
- 6 oranges

## Directions

1. Prepare the rice as in the Rice Cream Pudding recipe, boiled, but omit the fruits and use only the grated rind of an orange in making the pudding.
2. When cold, add the juice of an orange and the cream, beaten or whipped to a froth.
3. Four tablespoonfuls of wine or lemon juice may be substituted for the orange juice, according to taste.
4. Freeze the same as you would ice cream, and serve with an Orange Sauce.
