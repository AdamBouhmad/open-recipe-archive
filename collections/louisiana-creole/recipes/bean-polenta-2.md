## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Dried Beans
- 1 Tablespoonful of Molasses
- 1 Tablespoonful of Mustard
- 1 Tablespoonful of Butter
- 1 Tablespoonful of Vinegar
- Juice of 1 Lemon
- Salt and Pepper to Taste

## Directions

1. Wash two cupfuls of dried beans, having previously soaked them over night.
2. Pour off the water.
3. Put the beans in the stewpan and cover with fresh cold water, and cook the beans until tender.
4. Pour into a colander, and press the beans through.
5. Put this pulp into the stewpan, and add one tablespoonful of ready-made mustard, one tablespoonful of molasses, one of butter, one of vinegar, the juice of a lemon, and salt and pepper to taste.
6. Serve very hot, as a vegetable.
