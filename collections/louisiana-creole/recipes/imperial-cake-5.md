## Ingredients

- 1 Pound of Butter
- 1 Pound of Sugar
- 1 Pound of Flour
- The Juice and Rind of 1 Lemon
- 1 Pound of Blanched Almonds
- 1/2 Pound of Citron
- 3/4 Pound of Raisins
- 9 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoonful of Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Blanch and peel and pound the almonds in a mortar
2. Seed the raisins, and cut the citron into shreds and quarter-inch dice, very fine
3. Grate the zest of the lemon
4. Beat the butter to a cream
5. Add the sugar gradually, beating till very light
6. Add the yolks of the eggs beaten to a cream and beat till very light
7. Mix the sifted flour and baking powder with the almonds, and add them gradually to the mixture, beating vigorously
8. Add the raisins, dredging with flour, and the citron and mix well
9. Add the juice of the lemon, and finally the whites of the eggs, beaten to a thick froth
10. Turn into a buttered tin and bake for an hour in a moderate oven
11. Use the broom-wisp test before taking out of the oven
