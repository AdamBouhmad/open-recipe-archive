## Ingredients

- one gallon of whisky
- four pounds of brown sugar
- one cup of yeast
- seven gallons of water

## Directions

1. To a gallon of whisky add four pounds of brown sugar, a cup of yeast, and seven gallons of water
2. Put it into a demijohn or keg
3. If you set the vinegar in April, it will be good in November to pickle with
4. Cover the mouth of the vessel with muslin, to keep out flies or insects
5. When sharp and clear, bottle it
