## Ingredients

- Macaroni
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Always purchase the best quality of Macaroni.
2. Avoid breaking the macaroni as much as possible.
3. Immerse it whole in a large saucepan of boiling water; add a tablespoonful of salt and one of butter.
4. Let the macaroni cook from twenty to twenty-five minutes.
5. Remove from the fire and drain in a colander.
6. If not intended for immediate use, cover at once with cold water.
7. When cool, drain and use as needed.
