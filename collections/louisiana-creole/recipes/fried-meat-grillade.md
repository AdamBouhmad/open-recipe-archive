## Ingredients

- round of meat
- salt
- pepper
- tablespoonful of lard
- sliced onion
- one clove of garlic
- one chopped tomato
- parsley

## Directions

1. The round of the meat is always selected for Grillades, and one steak will serve six persons. The steak is cut into pieces of about six or eight squares and each piece is called a grillade.
2. Season well with salt and pepper, rubbing these into the meat thoroughly and letting it soak well into the fibres.
3. Have ready a hot pan, and place within a tablespoonful of lard, and, when hot, add a sliced onion and one clove of garlic, chopped very fine.
4. Let this brown, and then add one chopped tomato.
5. Place the Grillades in this, letting them soak thoroughly.
6. Cover with a tight cover, and set back, letting them fry slowly, so as to absorb all the lard and juices.
7. Serve on a hot dish, when brown, with garnishes of parsley.
