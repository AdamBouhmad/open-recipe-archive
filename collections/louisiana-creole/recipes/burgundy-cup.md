## Ingredients

- 1 Pint of Good Old Burgundy
- 1 Sliced Lemon
- 1 Orange
- A Dash of Old French Cognac
- ¾ Pint of Seltzer Water
- 1 Large Piece of Ice
- ½ Cup of Sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar and lemon and orange juice and add the liquors and water
2. Decorate with the sliced lemon and sprigs of green mint
