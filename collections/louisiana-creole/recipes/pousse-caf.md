## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoonful of anisette
- 1 teaspoonful of curacoa
- 1 teaspoonful of Benedictine or Chartreuse
- 1 teaspoonful of good old Bourbon or Cognac

## Directions

1. Mix all the above ingredients in a small glass, and serve cold, or otherwise, as preferred.
