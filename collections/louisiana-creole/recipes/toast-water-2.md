## Ingredients

- slices from a loaf of wheat bread
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cut two or three slices from a loaf of wheat bread
2. Toast them very brown
3. While hot, put them in a small pitcher, and pour over them a pint and a half of water
4. Sugar may be added if liked, but when the stomach is affected it is better without it
