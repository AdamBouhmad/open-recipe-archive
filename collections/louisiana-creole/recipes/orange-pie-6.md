## Ingredients

- 2 Fine Louisiana Oranges and Their Juice
- The Rind of 1 Orange, Grated
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- 1 Tablespoontul of Cornstarch
- 3 Eggs
- 1 Cup of Powdered Sugar
- 3/4 Teacupful of Boiling Water

## Directions

1. Beat the butter and sugar to a cream
2. Blend the cornstarch with sufficient cold water to moisten and mix well, and stir into the teacup of water, which should be boiling in a saucepan
3. Let it cook, stirring constantly, for two minutes only
4. Add the butter and sugar, and stir well
5. Remove from the fire, and add the well-beaten yolks of two eggs
6. Line the pie plates with pie crust and bake the under crust
7. Add the custard, and let it get slightly brown in the oven
8. Spread over the whites of two eggs, beaten to a stiff froth, with two tablespoonfuls of fine white powdered sugar
9. Let it bake for about three minutes longer in a quick oven, and serve cold
