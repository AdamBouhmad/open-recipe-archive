## Ingredients

- three or four dozen eggs
- scalding vinegar
- whole pepper
- cloves
- allspice
- ginger
- cloves of garlic

## Directions

1. Boil three or four dozen eggs for half an hour
2. Let them cool and then take off the shells
3. Place them in wide-mouthed jars
4. Pour over them scalding vinegar seasoned with whole pepper, cloves, or allspice, ginger, and a few cloves of garlic
5. When cold, bung them down very close
6. Let them be well covered with the vinegar
7. In a month they will be fit for use
