## Ingredients

- 1 cup of cornmeal
- 1 cup of breakfast hominy boiled
- 1 egg
- a pinch of salt
- 1 teaspoonful of yeast powder
- sweet milk

## Directions

1. Mix ingredients with sufficient milk to make a soft batter
2. Bake quickly in a slightly larded hot pan
3. Serve instantly
