## Ingredients

- raspberries
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Pick all the leaves and stems from the raspberries
2. Put them on in bell metal skillets
3. Mix a little powdered sugar with them and mash them well
4. Stew only a short time
5. Take them off and pass them well through a clean sieve to extract as much of the pulp as possible
6. To every pint of juice put 1 lb. (dead weight) of loaf sugar
7. Return it to the skillet and stew until you think it sufficiently thick, which you may discover by cooling a little
