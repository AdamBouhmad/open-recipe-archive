## Ingredients

- 1 peck of green tomatoes
- 1 dozen onions
- 1 ounce of cloves
- 1 ounce of mustard seed
- 1 tablespoonful of white mustard seed
- 1 cupful of salt
- 1/2 teaspoonful of red pepper or the pods cut into strips
- 1 ounce of cloves
- 1 ounce of black pepper
- 2 quarts of vinegar

## Directions

1. Slice the tomatoes and the onions
2. Put a layer of tomatoes and a layer of onions, and sprinkle with salt, and continue till all are used up
3. Let them stand over night
4. In the morning drain off all the liquor that has accumulated, and put the vegetables into a porcelain-lined pot with all the other ingredients, and cover with two quarts of vinegar
5. Let all simmer gently for a quarter of an hour, and then put away in stone jars, letting the vinegar cover the pickles, and boiling while hot
