## Ingredients

- 4 Layers of Pound or Cup Cake
- 1/4 Pound of Raisins
- 1/4 Pound of Citron
- 1/4 Teaspoonful Each of Ground Cloves, Cinnamon, Allspice and Nutmeg
- Frosting to Cover

## Directions

1. Make a good Pound or Cup Cake and bake in tins, taking out enough to fill three tins.
2. Add to the remaining batter one-half teaspoonful each of ground cloves, allspice, cinnamon and nutmeg, and one-quarter pound each of raisins, seeded, and citron, chopped very fine.
3. Mix thoroughly, and bake in two layers.
4. Place between the other layers, alternating with a frosting, as in Almond Layer Cake.
