## Ingredients

- 1 lb. of nice potatoes
- ½ lb. of carrots
- 1 egg yolk
- salt and pepper
- about 1 teaspoonful of butter
- fine bread crumbs
- 1 or 2 small pieces of butter

## Directions

1. Boil potatoes and carrots
2. When cooked quite tender, rub the potatoes and carrots through a wire sieve with a wooden spoon
3. Add the yolk of an egg, salt and pepper to taste, and about 1 teaspoonful of butter
4. Rub and beat all up into a thick paste
5. Have a pudding dish well buttered and scattered all over with fine bread crumbs
6. Put in the mixture
7. Add bread crumbs on the top, and 1 or 2 small pieces of butter
8. Bake for about ¾ hour, or till the crumbs are nice and brown
9. Turn out the whole on a dish and serve hot
