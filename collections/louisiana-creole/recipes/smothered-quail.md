## Ingredients

- 6 Fine Fat Quails
- 1/2 Carrot
- 1/2 of an Onion
- 3/4 Cupful of Water
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Butter
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- 6 Thin Strips of Bacon

## Directions

1. Select fine fat quails, clean, singe and wipe well.
2. Truss neatly and cover with a thin layer of bacon.
3. Place two tablespoonfuls of butter in a saucepan.
4. Place the quails in the pan and add half of an onion and carrot minced very fine.
5. Cover and let the quails brown to a nice golden color.
6. Moisten with a half cup of water and set the pan in the oven.
7. Cover with buttered paper and let the quails cook for twenty minutes.
8. Serve on a hot dish nicely garnished with parsley sprigs or lettuce leaves.
