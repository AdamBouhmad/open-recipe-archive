## Ingredients

- one egg
- one spoonful of sugar
- a small lump of butter
- a gill of yeast, or a yeast cake
- a quart of flour
- warm milk, or milk and water
- a tablespoonful of lard or butter

## Directions

1. Beat together one egg, one spoonful of sugar, a small lump of butter and a gill of yeast, or a yeast cake
2. Add to this a quart of flour, and enough warm milk, or milk and water, to form a dough
3. Work it and set it to rise
4. When it has risen, take down your bread-board and flour it well
5. Roll your dough out on the board, and spread over it a tablespoonful of lard or butter
6. Sprinkle a dust of flour over the butter
7. Roll it up into rolls and bake quickly
