## Ingredients

- 1 Lump of Sugar
- 2 Cloves
- 3 Allspice
- 2 Tablespoonfuls of Old Port Wine
- A Dash of Grated Nutmeg
- A Bit of Lemon Peel

## Directions

1. Take a small mixing glass and dissolve the sugar in a little warm water
2. Add the Allspice and Cloves, ground well
3. Add the Port Wine
4. Fill with hot water and add a bit of lemon peel
5. Stir well
6. Grate a little nutmeg on top and serve very hot
