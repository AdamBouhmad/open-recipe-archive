## Ingredients

- A Loin of Pork
- Salt and Pepper to Taste
- Parsley to Garnish
- Apple Sauce

## Directions

1. Score the loin in close lines across and down, about a half inch apart
2. Dredge well with salt and pepper and place in the oven, letting it cook slowly and long
3. Allow at least twenty-five minutes to every pound
4. Baste every five minutes for the first half hour and every ten minutes thereafter
5. When cooked thoroughly, take out of the baking pan and put in a hot serving dish
6. Garnish nicely with parsley
7. Serve with Apple Sauce and a little horseradish
