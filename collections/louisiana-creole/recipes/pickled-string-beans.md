## Ingredients

- 7 pounds of beans
- 1 quart of best cider vinegar
- 1 teaspoonful of alum
- 1 ounce of cloves
- 6 red pepper pods

## Directions

1. Take young tender beans, and cut the two extremities lightly, and string carefully.
2. Throw them into fresh water for several hours.
3. Wash well and drain, and put them into earthen jars.
4. Pour over boiling vinegar, spiced as for Tomato Pickle, and allow one-quarter of a teaspoonful of powdered alum to each quart of vinegar.
5. Cover and cork well.
6. Three days after pour off this vinegar and boil again and pour over the pickles, and let them stand again for three days.
7. Add a few cloves and pieces of red pepper pods, and bottle closely.
