## Ingredients

- egg plant
- egg
- pounded cracker
- lard
- salt

## Directions

1. Parboil egg plant
2. Slice it and dip each piece in beaten egg and roll it in pounded cracker
3. Drop it in hot lard and fry brown
4. Season with salt
