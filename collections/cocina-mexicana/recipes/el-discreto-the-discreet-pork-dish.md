## Ingredients

- Cloves of garlic, cleaned
- Parsley
- Ham
- Meat broth from the meat to be served
- Salt
- Ground spices (all varieties except saffron)
- Oil

## Directions

1. Fry clean garlic cloves and plenty of parsley with ham, all chopped
2. Add broth from the meat that will be served in this stew
3. Season with salt and ground spices of all varieties, except saffron
4. Place small portions of meat in this broth
5. When serving, add oil and salt if needed
