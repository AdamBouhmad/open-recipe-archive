## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- beef
- hot water
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- thyme
- peas (cooked)
- onions (cooked)
- mushrooms (cooked)
- carrots (cooked)
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place a piece of butter in a pot and melt it over the fire
2. Add two tablespoons of flour and let it fry and brown in the butter
3. Add the piece of beef to be stewed and keep stirring and turning it with the brown substance until it becomes firm
4. Add hot water to the stew, stirring until it boils
5. Season with salt, pepper, one bay leaf, and a little thyme
6. After boiling for one hour, add as much as desired of the following already cooked items: peas, onions, mushrooms, and carrots
7. If the stew remains white, bind it with egg yolks
