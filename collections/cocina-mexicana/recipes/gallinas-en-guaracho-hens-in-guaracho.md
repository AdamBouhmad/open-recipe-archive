## Ingredients

- Cooked hens
- Garlic
- Lard
- Broth from cooking the hens
- Whole toasted sesame
- Large yellow raw squash
- Cooked pineapple
- Parsley
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cilantro
- Cumin
- Salt
- Sugar
- Raisins
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Divide cooked hens into quarters
2. Chop fine garlic cloves and fry in lard
3. Add the broth in which the hens were cooked and whole toasted sesame
4. Place the hen quarters with pieces of large yellow raw squash, cooked pineapple and plenty of parsley
5. Add cinnamon, clove, pepper, cilantro and cumin, ground with the parsley
6. Season with appropriate salt and cook over two fires until the broth has regular consistency
7. Then add sugar with raisins and almonds
