## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Lard
- Garlic cloves
- Bread
- Black pepper
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Fish portions
- Beaten egg
- Lemon slices
- Bay leaves
- Pickled chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Roast the tomatoes in lard until they turn yellow
2. Grind them with clean garlic cloves, a piece of bread, black pepper, cloves, and cinnamon
3. Fry everything together
4. Season with salt and add vinegar with a little sugar to taste
5. Coat fish portions with beaten egg and fry them
6. Pour the sauce over the fried fish
7. Garnish with lemon slices, bay leaves cooked separately, pickled chiles, and olives
