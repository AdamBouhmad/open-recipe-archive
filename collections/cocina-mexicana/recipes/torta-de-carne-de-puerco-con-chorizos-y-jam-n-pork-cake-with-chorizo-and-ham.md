## Ingredients

- chorizo
- pork meat
- broth
- garlic
- onions
- tomatoes
- ham pieces
- salt
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- nutmeg
- capers
- raisins
- pickled capers
- almonds
- eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Simmer chorizo and pork, then add a little broth and leave on the heat until cooked.
2. Separately fry garlic, onions, tomatoes, ham pieces, salt, clove, cinnamon, pepper, and nutmeg.
3. Put everything with the meat in the pan in which the cake is to set, and add capers, raisins, pickled capers, and almonds.
4. Have the eggs well beaten and add them.
5. Placing on top of everything sugar, cinnamon, and bread crumbs, cook the cake at two heats with more heat above than below.
