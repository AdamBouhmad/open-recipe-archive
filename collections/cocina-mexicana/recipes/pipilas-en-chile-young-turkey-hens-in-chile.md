## Ingredients

- wide chiles
- roasted tomatoes
- walnuts
- spices
- raisins
- almonds
- nutmeg
- sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Toast some wide chiles and soak others, grinding all with roasted tomatoes and walnuts
2. Fry everything and season with spices
3. Add raisins, almonds and nutmeg and sesame seeds on top
