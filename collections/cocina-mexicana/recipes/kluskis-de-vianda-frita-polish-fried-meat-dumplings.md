## Ingredients

- one pound lean ham
- bread soaked in wine
- lemon peel
- salt
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Mince the ham finely
2. Mix with bread soaked in wine and drained, lemon peel, salt and pepper
3. Form the paste into flattened balls
4. Roll in breadcrumbs
5. Fry
