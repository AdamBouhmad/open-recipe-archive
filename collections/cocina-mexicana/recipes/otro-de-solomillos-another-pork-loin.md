## Ingredients

- wide pork loin
- clove
- cinnamon
- salt
- carrots
- beets
- ham
- eggs
- parsley
- lard
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Find a wide loin, open it from one edge to the center so it can unfold and then pound it a little
2. Grind clove, cinnamon and salt, and add to the meat which is placed to cook
3. Cook carrots, beets, ham and eggs, all cooked; cut these things into pieces, mince parsley very fine and fill the loin first with a layer of ham, another of carrot, another of beet and another of egg with parsley, ending with ham
4. Once the filling is in place, roll the meat so that it forms a kind of meatloaf, tie it tightly, simmer in lard and once done add sufficient water for it to cook
5. When it finishes cooking, sprinkle with vinegar
