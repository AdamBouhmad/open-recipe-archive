## Ingredients

- half a cuartillo of milk
- semolina
- orange flower water, lemon zest, or vanilla
- sugar
- four egg yolks
- four egg whites

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil and add semolina until a very thick porridge forms
2. Let it cook a little
3. Flavor with orange flower water, lemon zest, or vanilla
4. Add sugar
5. Mix in four egg yolks
6. Beat four egg whites until stiff and fold in
7. Butter a mold or pan and dust with finely grated bread
8. Pour the mixture inside
9. Finish cooking by surrounding the mold with hot ashes and gentle heat on top of the lid
10. Serve very hot and golden
