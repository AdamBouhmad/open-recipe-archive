## Ingredients

- walnuts, cleaned and toasted
- tomatoes, roasted
- broth from meat, poultry, or fish
- salt
- saffron
- spices

## Directions

1. Grind the cleaned and toasted walnuts and roasted tomatoes.
2. Fry both ingredients together.
3. Add broth from the meat, poultry, or fish that will be cooked with the nosqueado.
4. Season with salt, saffron, and other spices.
5. Add the meat and let simmer until it is completely cooked.
