## Ingredients

- veal tongue
- vinegar
- wine
- oil
- black pepper powder
- paper

## Directions

1. Wash the tongue and cook it with plenty of vinegar
2. Although it need not be very tender, skin it
3. Rub it with wine, oil, and pepper powder
4. Wrap in paper so it does not lose its aroma
