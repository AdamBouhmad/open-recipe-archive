## Ingredients

- beef brains
- water
- red wine
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- small onions
- flour
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Wash the brains, remove the clotted blood, the thin skin and the fibers, and let them soak in water for several hours.
2. Heat three quarters of wine and add the brains with onion, thyme, bay leaf, parsley, salt and pepper, letting them cook for half an hour.
3. While cooking, sift the brains; fry small onions in butter until they brown, dust with a little flour, moisten with the wine in which the brains were cooked, and add some mushrooms.
4. Place the brains on a platter and arrange the fried mixture around them.
