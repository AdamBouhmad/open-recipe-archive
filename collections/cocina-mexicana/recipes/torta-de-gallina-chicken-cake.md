## Ingredients

- Chicken cooked with pork loin, longaniza and chorizos
- Lard
- Garlic
- Onions
- Xitomates (tomatoes)
- Green chiles
- Salt
- Saffron
- Clove
- Pepper powder
- Chopped parsley
- Wine from Parras or Málaga
- Broth from cooking the chicken
- Beaten eggs
- Bread sliced and golden fried
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Capers
- Hard-boiled egg slices
- Olives
- Cured tornachilesQuiero (pickled peppers)
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken with pork loin, longaniza and chorizos
2. Divide the chicken and other cooked meats into small pieces
3. Fry the meats in lard with ground garlic, onions, xitomates and chopped green chiles
4. Season the fried mixture after it is cooked with salt, saffron, clove and pepper powder, and chopped parsley
5. Add the chicken with the other meats, adding some wine from Parras or Málaga and some broth in which they were cooked
6. Let it all boil until the liquid is consumed, taking care that the stew does not burn
7. Set apart and let it cool to incorporate this meat with beaten eggs
8. In a cazuela lined with lard, put a layer of bread slices fried in fat and sprinkled with wine, and on top another layer of chicken arranged as described
9. Add raisins, almonds, capers, hard-boiled egg slices, olives and cured tornachilesQuiero
10. Cover this layer with another of bread and egg around it and continue alternating in this arrangement until the cazuela is full, the last layer being bread, sprinkled with wine and covered with beaten egg
11. Let the cake curdle and brown at two gentle fires and serve sprinkled with sugar
