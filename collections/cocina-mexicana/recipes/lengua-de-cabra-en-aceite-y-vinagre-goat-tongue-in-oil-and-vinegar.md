## Ingredients

- goat tongues (lengua de cabra)
- hot water
- water
- salt
- oil (aceite)
- vinegar (vinagre)
- broth from cooking, one spoonful
- onion slices (rebanadas de cebolla)
- avocados (aguacates)
- chiles or chipotle in vinegar
- olives (aceitunas)
- capers (alcaparras)

## Directions

1. Soak and wash in hot water, place in a pot with water and salt and bring to boil. When done, remove from the pot and strip off the skins. Cut into small pieces and add oil, vinegar, a spoonful of the broth in which they cooked, slices of onion, avocados, chiles or chipotles in vinegar, olives, and capers.
