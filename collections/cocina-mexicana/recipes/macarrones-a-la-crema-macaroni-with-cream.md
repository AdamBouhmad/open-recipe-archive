## Ingredients

- one pound of macaroni
- butter
- salt
- one onion studded with cloves
- eight ounces of grated cheese
- a little nutmeg
- a little pepper
- some spoonfuls of cream

## Directions

1. Put one pound of macaroni in boiling water with a piece of butter, salt, and an onion studded with cloves
2. Let boil for three-quarters of an hour
3. Drain the macaroni and place in a saucepan with a little butter, eight ounces of grated cheese, a little nutmeg and pepper, and some spoonfuls of cream
4. Fry everything together
5. As soon as the macaroni begins to shred, dress and serve
