## Ingredients

- fish cooked in salted water
- eggs
- bread
- cheese
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper in powder
- parsley
- spearmint
- oil
- garlic
- hazelnuts
- bread soaked in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook fish in salted water and drain well
2. Remove bones and chop the fish
3. Mix with raw eggs, a little grated bread and cheese, cloves, cinnamon, and powdered pepper
4. Add finely chopped parsley and spearmint
5. Mix well and form balls of regular size
6. Fry the balls in oil
7. Boil in water with salt
8. Fry garlic in oil and make a sauce of ground hazelnuts with bread soaked in vinegar
9. Dissolve in some of the cooking broth of the meatballs
10. Pour the sauce into the oil with the meatballs
11. Once it has boiled several times, serve
