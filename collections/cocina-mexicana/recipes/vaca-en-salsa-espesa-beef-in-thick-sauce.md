## Ingredients

- beef, sliced thin
- butter
- flour
- water or broth
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- beaten eggs
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the beef sliced thin in a pot with a piece of butter
2. Once the butter is melted, add a good handful of flour
3. Stir and thin with a little water or broth
4. Season with salt, pepper and a small bunch of parsley
5. Before serving, add beaten eggs and a little vinegar
