## Ingredients

- 2 pounds sugar
- 1 quartillo virgin honey
- 5 egg whites per pound of sugar, beaten
- grajea (sugar pellets)
- toasted sesame seeds
- toasted almonds in small pieces
- pine nuts (optional)

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with 2 pounds of sugar
2. When at medium point, add 1 quartillo of good virgin honey
3. Allow to reach the soft-crack point
4. Remove from fire and let cool slightly
5. Add beaten egg whites in the ratio of 5 per pound of sugar while cooling, gradually
6. Beat everything with a spoon
7. Pour into a platter
8. Decorate with grajea and toasted sesame seeds on top, and toasted almonds in small pieces and pine nuts if desired
