## Ingredients

- Mackerel
- Soft roe (lechecillas)
- Butter
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Lemon juice
- Fine herbs
- Vinegar
- Oiled paper

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the mackerel, removing the soft roe
2. Cook the soft roe in a pan with butter, salt, pepper, and lemon juice
3. Mix the cooled soft roe with butter combined with finely chopped fine herbs, salt, pepper, lemon juice or a dash of vinegar
4. Fill the fish bellies with this preparation
5. Wrap each in oiled paper
6. Roast on a grill and serve in the paper
