## Ingredients

- turkey or hen
- ham, chopped or butter
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- broth (a couple of small cups)
- cloves of spice
- toasted garlic
- parsley
- hazelnuts
- egg yolk

## Directions

1. Clean the bird with a dry cloth
2. Cut into pieces and sauté with chopped ham or butter, garlic, parsley, salt, and a little pepper
3. Add a couple of small cups of broth from the pot
4. Make a sauce with cloves of spice, toasted garlic, parsley, hazelnuts, and an egg yolk
5. Dissolve it with the same broth and add to the pot to cook a while longer
6. If the sauce is not very thick, add a couple of egg yolks and spread over the top
