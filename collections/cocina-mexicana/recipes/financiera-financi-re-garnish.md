## Ingredients

- cock crests
- cock wattles
- quenelles
- mushrooms
- truffles, sliced
- artichoke bottoms, chopped
- beef sweetbreads
- poultry livers
- good sauce made with substance and strained broth, well thickened and bound
- chicken gravy

## Directions

1. Mix the cock crests and wattles, prepared as explained in their particular article, with quenelles, mushrooms, truffles in slices, chopped artichoke bottoms, beef sweetbreads, and poultry livers
2. Cook all ingredients well
3. Serve everything under a good sauce made with substance and well-strained, thick, and bound broth, or in chicken gravy
4. It can be adorned with fried toasts around it or served as a filling for timbales or puffed pastry balls
5. It can be used as an ornament for a fattened hen or other similar entries
6. It is by itself a very respectable entry
