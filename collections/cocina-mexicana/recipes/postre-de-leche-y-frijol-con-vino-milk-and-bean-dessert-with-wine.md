## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- cooked beans (prepared with water only)
- flour
- cinnamon stick
- sherry wine

## Directions

1. Sweeten two quarts of milk with two pounds of sugar
2. Mix with one cup of cooked beans that have been ground, adding a little flour
3. Incorporate well, strain, add a cinnamon stick, and bring to heat
4. When at proper consistency, add half a quart of sherry wine
5. Let boil again until it reaches the proper point, then pour into serving plates
