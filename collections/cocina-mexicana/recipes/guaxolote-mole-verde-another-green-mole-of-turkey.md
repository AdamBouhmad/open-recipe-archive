## Ingredients

- raw green chiles
- shelled walnuts
- pine nuts
- peanuts
- sesame
- peppercorns
- clove
- cinnamon
- turkey

## Directions

1. Soak raw green chiles and grind them with shelled walnuts, pine nuts, peanuts and sesame, all toasted
2. Mix everything well and fry it, adding the turkey without water or broth
3. The walnuts should be fried separately with peppercorns, clove and cinnamon, all ground
