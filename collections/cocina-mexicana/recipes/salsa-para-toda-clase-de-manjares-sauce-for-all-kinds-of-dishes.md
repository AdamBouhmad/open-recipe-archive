## Ingredients

- half a quarter liter of broth
- one glass of white wine
- salt
- pepper
- lemon peel
- bay leaf
- a little agraz juice

## Directions

1. Put one glass of white wine, salt, pepper, lemon peel, bay leaf and a little agraz juice in half a quarter liter of broth
2. Let it infuse over hot ashes for eight hours
3. After which it can be used for fish stews, vegetable dishes, etc.
