## Ingredients

- Lamb meat
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bacon
- Onion
- Water
- Parsley
- Spearmint
- Lettuce heart
- Soaked and chopped pine nuts
- All spices
- Lemon or orange juice

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces and place in a pot with salt and pepper.
2. Chop bacon into small pieces and fry well, then fry the finely chopped onion.
3. Add everything to the pot and put it to braise over low heat, stirring frequently so it does not stick.
4. After braising well for about an hour and a half, add water from a pot that has been placed over the stew pot.
5. When the meat is well cooked, add plenty of parsley, spearmint, lettuce heart, all finely chopped, soaked and chopped pine nuts, all spices, and a little lemon or orange juice.
