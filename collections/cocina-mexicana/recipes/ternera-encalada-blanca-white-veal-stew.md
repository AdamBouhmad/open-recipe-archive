## Ingredients

- roasted veal
- butter
- flour
- water
- salt
- pepper
- herbs bundle
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- small onions (optional)

## Directions

1. Cut roasted veal into small pieces and place in a saucepan with a little butter
2. Dust with flour and fry
3. When fried, dilute with sufficient water, adding salt, pepper, and a bundle of herbs
4. Let cook until the broth reduces a little
5. Dissolve egg yolks in the remaining broth and add a little vinegar
6. Small onions may also be added
