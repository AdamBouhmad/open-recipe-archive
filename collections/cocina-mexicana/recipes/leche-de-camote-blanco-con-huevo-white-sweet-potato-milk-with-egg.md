## Ingredients

- five cuartillos of milk
- three egg yolks
- one ounce of starch dissolved in milk
- four ounces of cooked and sieved white sweet potato
- two ounces of soaked and ground almonds
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Sweeten five cuartillos of milk
2. Dissolve three egg yolks in it
3. Add one ounce of starch dissolved in a little milk
4. Strain the milk thus prepared and mix with four ounces of cooked and sieved white sweet potato
5. When it is boiling, add two ounces of soaked and ground almonds
6. Allow it to reach the proper point
7. Pour into serving dishes
8. Once cold, dust with cinnamon powder
