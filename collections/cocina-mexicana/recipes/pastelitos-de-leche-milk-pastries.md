## Ingredients

- Two quarter litres milk
- Two pounds ground sugar
- Three egg yolks
- Four pounds almonds
- One handful pine nuts
- Paste filling

## Directions

1. Make the dough with two quarter litres of milk, two pounds of ground sugar, three egg yolks, four pounds of almonds and one handful of pine nuts, all well ground
2. Heat until the dough takes on a firm consistency so the pastries can be formed
3. Remove from heat and when it cools a bit, form as before and fill with any paste, preferably the following
4. Form a very well clarified syrup with six pounds of sugar
5. Mix in two pounds of white sweet potato that has no fibers
6. Grate one large coconut
7. Grind one pound of blanched almonds
8. Mix everything well and place on the fire
9. When it begins to reach cajeta consistency, add one cup of vanilla and bring to a high cajeta consistency
