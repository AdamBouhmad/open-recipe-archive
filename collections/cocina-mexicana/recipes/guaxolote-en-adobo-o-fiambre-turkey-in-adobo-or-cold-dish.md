## Ingredients

- turkey, cleaned
- salt
- peppercorns
- clove
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- fatty ham slices
- water

## Directions

1. After cleaning the turkey, sprinkle well with salt, peppercorns, clove, cinnamon and nutmeg, all ground
2. Arrange chopped garlic, onion and parsley all over it
3. Top with slices of fatty ham
4. Wrap well in a clean napkin
5. Put to boil in water
