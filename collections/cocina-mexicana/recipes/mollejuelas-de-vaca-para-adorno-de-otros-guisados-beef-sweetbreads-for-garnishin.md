## Ingredients

- Beef sweetbreads
- Ham slices
- Butter
- Onions
- Carrots
- Salt
- Pepper
- Herb bundle
- Broth

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbreads in warm water, blanch them, refresh in cold water, and drain.
2. Arrange them in a casserole between slices of ham.
3. Melt butter separately and pour over the sweetbreads, adding onions, carrots, salt, pepper, and a herb bundle.
4. Moisten with broth.
5. Once cooked this way, they are used to garnish a multitude of dishes.
