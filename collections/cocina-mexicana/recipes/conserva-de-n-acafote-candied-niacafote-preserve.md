## Ingredients

- níacafote
- lime water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make in the style of papaya preserve, cutting the pieces to regular size
2. Soak the pieces for twenty-four hours in infusion of settled lime water; test this water to ensure it coats the tongue
3. Rinse and cook, testing the cooking with a thin straw; if the straw passes through easily it is done
4. Place in cold water and then in fine clarified syrup, strained and at medium point and hot
