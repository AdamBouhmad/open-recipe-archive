## Ingredients

- black chiles
- black chile seeds
- lard
- toasted tortillas
- clove
- cinnamon
- meat
- meat broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Devein the black chiles, toast them in lard and grind them with their seeds toasted on a comal, toasted tortillas at the coals, clove and cinnamon
2. Fry the ground mixture in lard and season with salt, then add the meats to be cooked with the broth in which they were cooked, and leave to boil until it reaches a good consistency
3. Chickens turn out very good in this mole
