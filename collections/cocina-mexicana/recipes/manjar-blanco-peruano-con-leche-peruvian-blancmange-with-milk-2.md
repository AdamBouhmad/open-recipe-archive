## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of milk
- four and a half pounds of crushed sugar
- six ounces of ground and sifted starch
- one chicken breast, ground
- orange blossom water
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. In two cuartillos of milk, add four and a half pounds of crushed sugar, strain it and add six ounces of ground and sifted starch, and one ground chicken breast
2. Place over fire and stir very carefully so that it does not break
3. As soon as it separates from the pan, add orange blossom water, allowing it to boil with it
4. After it has cooled, sprinkle ground cinnamon on top
