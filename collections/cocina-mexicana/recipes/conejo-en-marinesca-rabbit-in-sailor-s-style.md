## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- rabbit
- rabbit liver
- red wine (one quarter)
- broth (two quarters)
- herb bouquet
- salt
- pepper
- small onions
- eel (optional)
- capers
- anchovy
- fried bread croutons

## Directions

1. Fry a little flour in butter until it browns
2. Add the rabbit cut into portions with its liver, letting it render a little
3. Moisten with one quarter red wine and two quarters broth
4. Add a well-sorted herb bouquet, salt, and pepper
5. Let the rabbit cook over gentle heat
6. After half an hour on the fire, add a dozen small onions that have been blanched
7. If mixing with eel, cut it into pieces and add it only when the rabbit is nearly perfectly cooked
8. Before serving, remove the herb bouquet and remove the fat from the broth
9. Add a good handful of whole capers and one chopped anchovy if available
10. Serve with fried bread croutons and broth poured over
