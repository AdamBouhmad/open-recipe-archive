## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- two egg yolks and one egg white
- half a pound of lard
- cold water
- melted butter
- minced meat, fish or sweet filling

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour add two egg yolks and one egg white, half a pound of lard and cold water
2. Make a soft dough that is rolled out adding melted butter
3. Form the empanadas and fill with minced meat, fish or sweet filling and fry
4. Sprinkle with sugar before serving
