## Ingredients

- six egg yolks
- two whole eggs
- four ounces of butter
- four ounces of ground almonds
- a little orange flower
- a little salt
- two ounces of flour
- half a pound of sugar
- six egg whites
- almonds cut in squares or strips
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Knead together six egg yolks, two whole eggs, four ounces of butter, four ounces of ground almonds, a little orange flower, a little salt, two ounces of flour, and half a pound of sugar
2. Beat six egg whites and incorporate them into the batter
3. Pour everything into a butter-greased paper box and place to bake in the oven
4. Make another composition with almonds cut in squares or strips, to which add a third of their volume in powdered sugar, moisten with beaten egg whites, and cover or fill the hollows with this mixture
5. After the mixture has been browned, when they are almost done baking, finish baking in the oven
6. Remove and cut the bizcochos according to the desired shape
