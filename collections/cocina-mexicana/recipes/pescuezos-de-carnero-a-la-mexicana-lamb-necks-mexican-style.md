## Ingredients

- Lamb necks
- Onions
- Parsnips
- Warm butter
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Pasilla chiles
- Roasted tomatoes
- Garlic
- Lard

## Directions

1. Prepare as previously described.
2. Serve over a sauce made from pasilla chiles that are soaked and ground with roasted tomatoes and a little garlic.
3. Fry the sauce in lard and season with salt.
4. Place the sauce in a separate serving dish and the lamb necks on the side.
