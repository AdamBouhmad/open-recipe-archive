## Ingredients

- pepita de calabaza (pumpkin seeds)
- manteca (lard)
- tomates (tomatoes)
- agua (water)
- caldo del guajolote (turkey broth)
- sal (salt)
- carne (meat)
- rajas de chayóte (strips of chayote)

## Directions

1. Toast pumpkin seeds in lard and then grind them
2. Cook tomatoes and grind them separately
3. Bring water to a boil in a pot and gradually add the ground pumpkin seeds, stirring with a spoon
4. Once well fried, add the tomato and let it fry
5. When fried, add the turkey broth and let it boil briefly
6. Add salt, meat, and strips of ground chayote to make it green
