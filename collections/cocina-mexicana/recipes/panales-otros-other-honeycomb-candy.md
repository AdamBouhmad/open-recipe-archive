## Ingredients

- Two cuartillos of water
- Half an arroba of sugar
- Twelve egg whites, well beaten
- Lemon juice, the amount of half an eggshell

## Directions

1. The proper point is known by inserting a popote and removing it covered; if what comes out breaks between the teeth, it is ready
2. Follow the same procedure as in the previous articles
