## Ingredients

- squabs
- tomatoes
- water
- meat broth, two cups
- giblets
- spices
- garlic, fried
- bread crumbs, soaked

## Directions

1. Clean the squabs and extract the giblets.
2. Fry a little tomato with the whole squabs until well browned, add the corresponding water and two cups of broth.
3. Grind the giblets with spices, fried garlic and soaked bread crumbs; dissolve all and add to the squabs, letting it come to a boil.
