## Ingredients

- three quarters of fennel essence
- three quarters of good wine spirit
- one jar (eighteen quarters) of the best refined Spanish brandy
- four quarters of boiled water
- four quarters of clarified syrup
- four ounces of sweet almonds
- half a quarter of milk

## Directions

1. Pour the fennel essence and wine spirit into a vessel with the best refined Spanish brandy, boiled water, and clarified syrup
2. If the mixture is too strong, add boiled water and syrup in proportion
3. Clarify with four ounces of crushed or cracked sweet almonds and half a quarter of milk
4. Filter through a cloth or felt until the fennel liqueur is perfectly clear and transparent
