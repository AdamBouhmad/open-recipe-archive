## Ingredients

- Squash
- Honey made with panela

## Directions

1. Cut the squash into pieces and remove the seeds
2. Wash in water and dry
3. Add to the honey made with panela
4. Let them boil, and as soon as they are well soaked and tender, bring the honey to mirror point and remove from heat
