## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Lard
- Parsley
- Onion
- Chiles
- Garlic
- Bacon strips
- Salt in grains
- Pepper
- White wine
- Broth from the pot
- Brandy

## Directions

1. After cleaning and preparing, flatten the breast a little and break the legs
2. In the pan put lard, parsley, onion, chiles, garlic, all finely chopped
3. In this mixture turn the turkey a few times on the grill and pass everything to another suitable pan according to its size
4. Place in it, covered at the breast with bacon strips, and well seasoned with salt in grains and pepper
5. Moisten with a glass of white wine and another of the same of broth from the pot; if you do not have white wine, it can be replaced with half a glass of good brandy
6. Cook over slow fire and add a little strained broth to thicken the sauce
