## Ingredients

- Green beans
- olive oil
- salt
- black pepper
- broth from boiled meat
- 2 or 3 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the beans well and cook them in water until perfectly tender.
2. Remove the water and place them in a pan with olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
3. Give them a few turns, then add a little broth from boiled meat and 2 or 3 egg yolks dissolved in it.
