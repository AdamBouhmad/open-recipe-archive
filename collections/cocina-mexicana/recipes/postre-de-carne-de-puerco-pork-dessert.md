## Ingredients

- One pound of pork loin, chopped fine
- Hard sponge cake, ground
- A little cinnamon
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Beaten eggs
- Butter or lard for frying
- Syrup at high point
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- Toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Chop one pound of pork loin fine and mix with hard ground sponge cake
2. Add a little cinnamon and toasted sesame seeds
3. Add everything to beaten eggs and mix well
4. Take out portions with a spoon and fry in butter or lard
5. Put in syrup at high point with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and toasted sesame seeds
