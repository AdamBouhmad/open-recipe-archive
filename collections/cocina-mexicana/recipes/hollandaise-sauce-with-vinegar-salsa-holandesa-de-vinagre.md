## Ingredients

- one glass of good vinegar
- twelve raw egg yolks
- four ounces of butter
- coarse pepper
- salt
- chili powder
- nutmeg
- pound and a half of very fresh butter

## Directions

1. Place in a saucepan with the vinegar the twelve raw egg yolks, four ounces of butter, coarse pepper, salt, chili powder, and nutmeg
2. Heat everything in a bain-marie without ceasing to stir
3. When it begins to thicken, add pound and a half of very fresh butter
4. Continue stirring until the butter is well dissolved and everything is well bound together
5. Finally strain the sauce through cheesecloth
