## Ingredients

- chiles (chiles)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- manteca (lard)
- aceite (oil)
- jitomate (tomato)
- cebolla (onion)
- ajo (garlic)
- calabacitas (small squash)
- carne de lomo (loin meat)
- longaniza (sausage)
- chorizos (chorizos)
- costillas (ribs)
- chorison (large sausage)
- lechuga (lettuce)
- rábano (radish)
- queso añejo (aged cheese)

## Directions

1. The day before, remove the veins from raw chiles and soak in salted water
2. The next day, remove that water, break apart the chiles, and fry in lard and a bit of oil with finely chopped tomato, onion, and garlic
3. Once well fried, add water, salt, and tender squash in slices
4. Once everything is cooked, remove from heat and garnish with fried loin meat, longaniza, chorizos, ribs, and chorison, all fried; and on top sliced onion, avocado, lettuce, radish, oil, and aged cheese
