## Ingredients

- one quarter of a quart of cooked milk
- half a quarter of a quart of cream
- mamey

## Directions

1. Mix one quarter of a quart of cooked milk with half a quarter of a quart of cream, dissolving them well with a small mill
2. Sweeten and beat again until it makes much foam
3. Place it on mamey
