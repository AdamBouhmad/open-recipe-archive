## Ingredients

- pichones recien nacidos (newborn squabs)
- agua hirviendo (boiling water)
- broqueta (skewer)
- vino blanco (white wine)
- peregil (parsley)
- cebolla (onion)

## Directions

1. Prepare newborn squabs by scalding them, and after emptying them while leaving the wings, head and feet, pierce each one with a skewer across the legs to prevent them from separating much when briefly blanching in boiling water
2. Once well cleaned, cook them in a pot with a glass of white wine, a small bunch of parsley and onion
