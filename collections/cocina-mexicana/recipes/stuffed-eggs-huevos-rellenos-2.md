## Ingredients

- eggs
- water
- raw eggs
- breadcrumb
- custard
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Harden eggs in water
2. After cleaning, crown them down to take part of the yolk
3. Remove the yolks, which will be ground together with two or three raw ones with a breadcrumb, soaked in custard
4. Add an equal amount of butter and season with salt and pepper
5. Well mixed all, it will be poured into the hollows left by the yolks in the cooked whites, covering them with their respective caps
6. Placing on a plate the part of the filling that remains, arrange the eggs on top and cover them with breadcrumb, sprinkled with butter, and then pass them to an oven or place them in a hornilla with a comal of fire on top, until they cook well on low heat
