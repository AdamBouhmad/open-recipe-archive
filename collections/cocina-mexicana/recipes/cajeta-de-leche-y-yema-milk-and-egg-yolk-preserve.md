## Ingredients

- 6 cuartillos of milk
- 4 pounds of fine sugar
- one stick of cinnamon
- half a pound of almonds passed through the metate with a little milk
- 22 egg yolks
- 2 or 3 custards (natillas)
- orange flower water
- olor

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar in the milk
2. Cook with its stick of cinnamon and strain the almonds that have been passed through the metate with a little milk
3. Simmer until it thickens and becomes dense
4. Remove from heat and let it cool
5. Incorporate the 22 egg yolks and 2 or 3 custards
6. Cook again until it reaches the proper point
7. Just before removing from heat, add orange flower water and olor
