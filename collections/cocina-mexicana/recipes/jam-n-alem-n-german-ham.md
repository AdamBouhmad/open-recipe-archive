## Ingredients

- very thin slices of raw ham
- slices of white bread crumb
- butter
- herbs
- mushrooms
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut very thin slices of raw ham
2. Fry slices of white bread crumb in butter
3. Line a saucepan with slices of ham
4. Layer toasted bread, fine herbs and mushrooms alternately until the saucepan is full, finishing with ham
5. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top
6. Put it in the oven for two or three hours and serve hot
