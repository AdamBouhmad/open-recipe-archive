## Ingredients

- early figs or figs
- water
- clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Choose the early figs or figs at a stage when they are neither too green nor too ripe
2. Heat water, and when it is about to come to a first boil, add the early figs or figs, reducing the heat so they maintain the same degree of heat without boiling
3. When they are in a state to be able to be peeled, remove them from the heat, peel them, and rinse in broken water
4. Add them then to clarified syrup at low point, and over gentle heat let it reach the preserve point
