## Ingredients

- Spanish sauce
- about half an egg's worth of anchovy butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Let the Spanish sauce reduce a little
2. At the moment of serving, add the anchovy butter and lemon juice to cut the saltiness that the butter might produce
3. When adding this butter to the sauce, which should be quite hot, stir it well with a spoon so that both things bind together well
