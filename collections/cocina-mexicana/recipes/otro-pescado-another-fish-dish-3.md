## Ingredients

- Oil
- Lard
- Garlic head
- Onion
- Parsley
- Tomatoes
- Chilchotes
- Clove
- Cumin
- Ancho or mulato chile
- Fried bread
- Raw fried fish
- Olives
- Capers
- Castilian vinegar
- Laurel
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Heat oil and lard together
2. Chop a head of garlic very finely and fry until golden
3. Fry half a cup of chopped onion, some chopped parsley, two or three tomatoes without skin, and split chilchotes
4. When the lard begins to release, add a little ground clove and cumin
5. Add half an ancho or mulato chile and a slice of fried ground bread
6. Once fried, add the previously fried raw fish to boil briefly in the broth
7. When placing on the platter, garnish with olives, capers, and slices of fried bread in oil
8. Before removing from the fire, add a little Castilian vinegar, laurel, and a pinch of pepper
9. Once on the platter, add a little oil
