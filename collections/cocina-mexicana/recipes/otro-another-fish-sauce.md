## Ingredients

- onions
- garlic
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- parsley
- mint
- lard
- flour
- fish broth
- olives
- capers
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop onions, garlic, tomatoes, green chiles, parsley, and mint.
2. Fry everything in lard.
3. Also fry some flour until golden and mix with the fried mixture.
4. Add a little broth from the cooked fish.
5. Add chopped olives, chopped capers, and a little vinegar.
