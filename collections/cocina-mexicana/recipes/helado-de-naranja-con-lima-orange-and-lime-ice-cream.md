## Ingredients

- twenty-two ounces white sugar
- two quartillos of clear water
- eight or nine oranges
- two lemons

## Directions

1. Dissolve twenty-two ounces of white sugar in two quartillos of clear water
2. Select eight or nine oranges and two lemons, clean them with a cloth
3. Scrape the rinds of the most fragrant and least bitter oranges
4. Cut them in half and squeeze them between thumb and forefinger with the other hand to break the small bladders where the juice is enclosed
5. Place between the palms of both hands, squeezing the rinds in opposite direction with force to break the cells that close the globules of essential oil that resides in the yellow rind
6. Strain the liquid through a fine-haired sieve and keep in a cool place
