## Ingredients

- ham, cleaned and desalted
- oil for frying
- Jerez wine or white wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. After cleaning and desalting, cut into thin slices and fry
2. When half fried, add a glass of Jerez or white wine and a spoonful of sugar
3. Cook at low heat for a while and serve on the plate with its own sauce
