## Ingredients

- eggs
- water

## Directions

1. If making many at once so they all turn out equal, fill a large pot with water to boil
2. When boiling, place eggs in a small basket and immerse inside the pot
3. This way all will finish at the same time and at the same point
4. Three minutes are sufficient
