## Ingredients

- fine sugar
- requesón (fresh cheese)
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Clarify the fine sugar well and bring it to medium point
2. Gradually add the requesón while another person beats it as one does for turrón until it breaks apart well
3. Remove from heat and beat well
4. Return to the fire, add orange flower water, and bring to cajeta point
5. Fill the molds
