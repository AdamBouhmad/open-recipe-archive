## Ingredients

- ribs
- Jerez wine or Málaga wine
- broth

## Directions

1. Cook the ribs with wine and equal quantity of broth
2. Season them
3. When cooked, remove from the casserole or saucepan
4. Place on a platter
5. Pass the sauce through a sieve and pour over the ribs
