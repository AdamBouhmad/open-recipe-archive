## Ingredients

- rice, washed and sun-dried
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron
- two or three roasted garlic cloves
- lard
- salt
- raw shrimp
- tomatoes
- onions
- garlic
- parsley
- dried chiles
- vinegar
- egg
- cooked tomatoes
- comino

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in water after washing and sun-drying it.
2. When the rice is cooked, add clove, cinnamon, pepper, saffron, and two or three roasted and ground garlic cloves.
3. Add a little lard and season with salt.
4. Let all the broth be consumed and set aside until the next day.
5. Grind raw shrimp and fry them in a pan with lard, tomatoes, onions, garlic, parsley, and dried chiles, all finely chopped and abundant.
6. Add a little vinegar.
7. Remove half of this mixture and bring to a boil with the shrimp and rice.
8. When it is almost dry, mix with beaten egg as if for frying.
9. Grease a pan with lard and pour in the rice to set it over two gentle fires.
10. When the cake has set, remove it and after it cools, cut it into pieces.
11. Grind some cooked tomatoes with a little pepper, clove, and comino.
12. Fry this with the remaining half of the mixture, season with salt, add water, sliced onions, green chiles, a little chopped parsley, and the pieces of cake.
13. When well seasoned, add a pinch of vinegar.
