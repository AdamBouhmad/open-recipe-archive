## Ingredients

- Raisins
- Butter
- Roasted tomato
- Oil
- Water
- Salt
- Rabbit
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Brown a large portion of raisins in butter and then grind them with plenty of roasted and skinned tomato
2. Fry the broth again in oil, add sufficient water and salt and the portions of cooked or roasted rabbit
3. When the broth is thick, add plenty of vinegar and after a good boil, remove it
