## Ingredients

- carp
- brown broth for cooking
- carp meat from two fish
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Select a good carp and cook it in brown broth
2. Remove all the meat, separating it from the bones, leaving pieces one to two inches long
3. Make a filling with its meat and that of another carp
4. Place the head and tail at the two ends of the fish platter, arranging the filling in the middle so it looks like a whole fish
5. Coat with egg and sprinkle breadcrumbs on top, drizzled with melted butter
6. Place immediately on hot embers, covering with a camping oven or a comal with fire on top to brown it
7. When serving, place the soft roe or any other garnish around it
