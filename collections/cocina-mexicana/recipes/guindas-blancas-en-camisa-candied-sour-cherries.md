## Ingredients

- Egg white
- Large ripe sour cherries
- Powdered sugar passed through silk sieve

## Directions

1. Beat the egg white until it rises well.
2. Select the largest and ripest cherries and remove half of the stem.
3. Coat the cherries with the beaten egg white.
4. Roll the egg-coated cherries in powdered sugar passed through a silk sieve, ensuring they are evenly covered.
5. Blow off excess powder.
6. Place them on paper sheets, separated from each other without touching.
7. Place them on a sieve in a stove until ready to serve.
