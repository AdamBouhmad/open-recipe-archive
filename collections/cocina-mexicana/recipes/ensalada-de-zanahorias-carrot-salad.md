## Ingredients

- carrot
- oil
- sliced garlic
- raw onion, finely chopped
- oil
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook and core the carrots, and chop them into small pieces; fry sliced garlic in oil, and when it is already golden, remove it; fry the carrots there, place them on a platter, stir in finely chopped raw onion, and add oil and vinegar with salt.
