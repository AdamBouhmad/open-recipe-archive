## Ingredients

- Whole onions
- Cold ham
- Chicken broth
- Salt
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Chicken quarters
- Red wine
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Fry some whole onions, and when they are already golden, add a little cold ground ham so that it browns also
2. Then add a little of the broth in which the chicken was cooked and season with salt, clove, pepper and cinnamon, all ground
3. Add the chicken quarters, and when the broth has thickened a little, thin it with a little red wine and a pinch of sugar
4. When it thickens to the proper consistency, remove from heat and serve
