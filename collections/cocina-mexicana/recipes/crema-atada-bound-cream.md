## Ingredients

- Milk
- Cream
- Sugar
- Egg whites
- Caramel

## Directions

1. In one quarter liter of milk bring another of cream to a boil, four ounces of sugar, three egg whites and a tablespoon of caramel
2. Once reduced to half, pass through a sieve and let cool
