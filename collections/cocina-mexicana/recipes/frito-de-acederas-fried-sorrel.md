## Ingredients

- beaten eggs
- flour
- sorrel leaves
- oil or very hot butter
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare beaten eggs and flour with which to coat the sorrel leaves one by one
2. Immediately put them in the skillet, which will be prepared beforehand with oil or very hot butter
3. Serve dusted with powdered sugar
