## Ingredients

- pork loin
- salt
- green chile
- tomato
- xitomatl
- garlic
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- almonds
- pine nuts
- capers
- caper berries
- tornachiles
- olives
- good white wine or vinegar
- parsley
- ham
- lard

## Directions

1. Chop a raw pork loin and cook it with its salt
2. Once cooked, chop it well again
3. Chop green chile, tomato, xitomatl and garlic very finely
4. Fry this preparation well
5. Add pepper, clove, cinnamon, almonds, pine nuts, capers, caper berries, tornachiles and olives
6. Dilute all this with good white wine or vinegar
7. Add defoliated parsley and pieces of ham
8. When well seasoned, slit another pork loin
9. Fill the openings with this mixture and fasten with a string that can be easily removed after cooking
10. Grease a casserole with lard and also grease the loin
11. Wrap in paper and cook over two gentle fires
12. Once cooked, remove the paper and strings, grease it with a little lard, and continue over two fires until it browns, to serve with the following sauce
13. For the sauce: toast a piece of bread in lard, grind it with a portion of capers
14. Chop green chile, tomato and xitomatl, and fry everything
15. Fry the caper and bread stock there
16. Grind pepper, clove and cinnamon, and add them with a little broth, seasoning with necessary salt
17. Add capers, caper berries, olives, tornachiles, vinegar and oil
18. Bathe the loins with this sauce and serve
