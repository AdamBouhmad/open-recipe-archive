## Ingredients

- pineapple
- tomatillo
- golden garlic
- bread toasted in lard
- cinnamon
- cloves
- water
- salt
- chicken or poultry quarters

## Directions

1. Grind pineapple, tomatillo, golden garlic, bread toasted in lard, cinnamon, and cloves
2. Fry everything together
3. Add the necessary water to make a sauce, already seasoned with salt
4. Place the chicken or poultry quarters in the sauce to cook
