## Ingredients

- pork loins cut into very small pieces
- ham fat and lean in equal quantities
- salt
- Málaga wine or other generous wine
- thin pork intestines

## Directions

1. Cut pork loins, ham fat and ham lean into very small pieces in equal quantities
2. Place all in a pot and season with salt
3. Moisten with Málaga wine or other generous wine so the meat is well soaked and covered
4. Incorporate everything together, cover the pot and keep in this state for one or two days depending on temperature and season
5. Fill thin pork intestines, tie at the desired distance
6. Prick with a pin so the air that they may contain comes out and they do not spoil so quickly
7. Hang to air dry in a place where the sun does not hit
