## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- ash water
- salt
- butter
- garlic
- onions
- tomatoes
- poblano chiles or tornachiles
- parsley
- saffron
- cumin
- pepper
- clove

## Directions

1. Soak chickpeas in ash water, peel them and after washing well, cook in salted water
2. When cooked, mash them with sufficient water
3. Fry garlic and onions in butter
4. Add tomatoes, poblano chiles or tornachiles and parsley, all chopped
5. Add the chickpea broth
6. Add saffron for color, cumin, pepper and clove
7. Make the soup the same way as in the previous article
