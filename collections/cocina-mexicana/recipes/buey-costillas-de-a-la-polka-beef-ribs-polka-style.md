## Ingredients

- Beef rib prepared as in previous recipe
- Garlic cloves, sliced
- Oil for frying
- Tomato sauce
- Capers
- Olives
- Stuffed onions
- Hard-boiled eggs, halved lengthwise

## Directions

1. Prepare the beef rib as in the previous recipe
2. Fry sliced garlic cloves in oil with the rib until golden, then remove the garlic
3. Place the rib on a plate
4. Pour tomato sauce with capers and olives over the top
5. Surround with stuffed onions alternated with halves of hard-boiled eggs cut lengthwise
