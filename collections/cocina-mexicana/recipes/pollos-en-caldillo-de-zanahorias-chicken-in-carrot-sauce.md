## Ingredients

- manteca
- cebolla picada
- zanahorias cocidas y molidas
- almendras tostadas
- pan frito
- ajonjolí tostado
- ajo
- clavo
- pimienta
- canela
- tomates cocidos y molidos
- pollos
- caldo

## Directions

1. Fry finely chopped onion in butter, then also fry cooked and ground carrots, toasted almonds, fried bread, toasted sesame, garlic, clove, pepper and cinnamon in the same butter
2. Add cooked and ground tomatoes to fry as well
3. Place the chickens with a little of their broth
4. Let simmer until the sauce has thickened as desired
