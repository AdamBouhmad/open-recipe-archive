## Ingredients

- cooked rabbit
- salt
- garlic
- parsley
- saffron
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- cumin ground
- cooked egg yolks
- oil
- vinegar
- raisins
- ham or boiled egg stuffing
- lard and oil for frying
- chopped onion
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- raisins
- capers
- rabbit cooking broth

## Directions

1. Cook rabbit and chop its meat, cleaned of skin and tendons
2. Mix with salt, chopped garlic and parsley, ground saffron, clove, pepper, cinnamon, and cumin
3. Add crumbled cooked egg yolks, a little oil and vinegar
4. Form meatballs with raisins, ham or boiled egg inside
5. Fry in lard mixed with oil
6. When fried, remove them
7. In the lard used for frying, add chopped onion, roasted and ground tomatoes with raisins and spices from the meatballs, capers and rabbit broth with a little oil
8. Bring this broth to a boil and add the meatballs to finish cooking and broth to reach regular consistency
