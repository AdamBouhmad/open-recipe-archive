## Ingredients

- Stockfish, cooked as indicated
- Oil
- Six medium onions, sliced into rings
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Nutmeg
- Juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Cook the fish as in the previous articles and drain it on a clean cloth
2. Fry the onion rings in oil; when they are set in the oil, remove and drain them
3. Heat gently half a quart of good oil and add salt, pepper, garlic, nutmeg and the juice of two lemons, mixing well
4. Cover the fish with this sauce
5. At the moment of serving, garnish with the fried onion rings, keeping them hot
