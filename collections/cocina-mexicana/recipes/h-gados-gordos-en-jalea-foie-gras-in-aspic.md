## Ingredients

- foie gras
- ham, grated
- earthnut truffles
- mushrooms
- tongue in scarlet
- beef udder
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare in the same manner as the previous recipe, replacing the beef udder with grated ham
2. Pound and pass the filling through a fine sieve
3. Cut earthnut truffles, mushrooms, scarlet tongue, and beef udder into dice shapes
4. Mix all these ingredients with the filling
5. Pour everything into the mold
6. Cook as described, then cool immediately
7. When ready to serve, soak the mold in warm water
8. Dry the mold
9. Cover with a plate and unmold by turning over
10. Decorate the loaf with aspic and serve
