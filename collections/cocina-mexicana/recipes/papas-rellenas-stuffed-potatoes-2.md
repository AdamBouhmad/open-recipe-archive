## Ingredients

- raw potatoes
- cooked brains
- parsley
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- tomatoes
- onion
- bread
- pork broth
- spices
- thyme

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes raw and make a small hole to fill them.
2. Prepare the filling by cooking and chopping brains, frying them in oil with a little chopped parsley, bread crumbs and a pinch of pepper.
3. Replace the cap on the potatoes and give them a light boil in a little water in a shallow pan.
4. For the sauce, roast some tomatoes and grind them with a piece of fried bread.
5. Fry onion pieces in a pan, add the tomatoes and bread, and when well fried add a little pork broth, ground spices and a little thyme.
6. Let it thicken greatly and use this broth to baste the stuffed potatoes.
7. Place on a low fire with a hot tin on top.
