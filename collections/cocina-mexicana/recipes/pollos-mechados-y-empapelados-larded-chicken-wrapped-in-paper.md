## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Thin lard strips
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Oiled papers

## Directions

1. Lard the chicken with thin strips
2. Roll in bread crumbs, sprinkling with salt and ground pepper
3. Wrap in oiled papers
4. Place on the grill taking care that the papers do not burn, as the chicken is to be carried to the table in them
