## Ingredients

- 5 pounds sugar
- 16 egg yolks, beaten
- 1.5 pounds toasted almonds, not too finely ground
- 2.5 cuartillos milk
- 2 ounces soaked and ground rice
- 1.5 ounces butter
- 1 small cup Jerez wine
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Once the syrup is clarified and cold, add the beaten egg yolks.
2. Over low heat give the first boil, then apply heat to continue boiling.
3. Mix the almonds well with the milk so no lumps remain.
4. As it thickens, add the butter, wine and cinnamon.
