## Ingredients

- pork
- lamb
- ham
- soaked bread
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- mint
- eggs
- oil
- wine
- lard
- flour
- water
- white vinegar
- oregano
- clove
- pepper
- saffron
- olives
- capers
- pickled peppers
- fried bread slices

## Directions

1. Mince pork, lamb, ham and soaked bread with onion, garlic, parsley and mint.
2. Mix all with eggs, oil and wine.
3. Form into balls and fry in lard until golden.
4. Once all are fried, fry flour in the remaining lard with minced garlic and onion.
5. Add water, white vinegar, oregano, clove, pepper and ground saffron.
6. Add olives, capers, pickled peppers and toss with the balls, adding three or four beaten eggs.
7. Season over two fires.
8. When done, serve adorned with fried bread slices.
