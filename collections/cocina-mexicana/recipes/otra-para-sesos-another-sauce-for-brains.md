## Ingredients

- finely minced onion
- finely minced parsley
- drops of lemon
- pepper
- cooked and minced egg yolks
- cooking oil

## Directions

1. Mince onion very finely
2. Mince parsley also very finely
3. Add drops of lemon
4. Add a pinch of pepper
5. Add cooked and minced egg yolks
6. Add cooking oil
7. Mix everything to form an exquisite sauce for brains and chicken breast
