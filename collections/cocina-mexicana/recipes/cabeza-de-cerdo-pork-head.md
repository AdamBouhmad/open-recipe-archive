## Ingredients

- pork head
- salt
- spices
- onions
- parsley
- garlic
- lard
- wine
- aromatic herbs
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean by scalding
2. With a knife, remove the bones without touching the skin
3. Insert inside fatty bacon seasoned with salt, spices, onions, parsley, and garlic, all finely chopped
4. Wrap in a cloth and cook in a sauce made with red wine, aromatic herbs, onions, salt, and pepper
5. Serve on a napkin over a platter
