## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one cuartillo of milk
- sponge cake (mamón)
- gragea
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Make a high-point syrup with two pounds of sugar, mix in one cuartillo of milk, and let boil until as thick as royal custard
2. Pour over layers of sponge cake and decorate with gragea and pine nuts on top
