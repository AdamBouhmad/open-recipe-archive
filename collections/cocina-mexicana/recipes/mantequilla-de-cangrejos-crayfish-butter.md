## Ingredients

- crayfish
- butter, one and a half pounds per one hundred crayfish

## Directions

1. Cook the crayfish
2. Separate the meat from the shells
3. Grind the shells until reduced to very fine powder and dried
4. Put one and a half pounds of butter for every one hundred crayfish
5. Pound everything together
6. Let cook over gentle heat
7. Pass through fine cloth over a vessel filled with very cold water
8. By this means the crayfish butter will solidify on the surface of the water
9. Form it into a compact piece and store for use
