## Ingredients

- twelve egg yolks
- half pound ground sugar
- three ounces flour
- two quartillos milk
- salt
- lard
- pasta or other filling
- sugar

## Directions

1. Mix twelve egg yolks with half pound ground sugar and three ounces flour
2. Incorporate everything by adding gradually up to two quartillos of milk, stirring well until it reaches the right consistency and adding appropriate salt
3. Grease a platter with lard and pour most of the batter into it
4. Place the pasta or other filling in the center
5. Cover with the rest of the dough
6. Place the platter on very gentle heat, covering it with a comal with fire and ensuring it is exposed to air so it browns
7. When browned, sprinkle sugar on top
