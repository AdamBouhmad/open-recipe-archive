## Ingredients

- one chicken liver
- ham
- parsley
- onions
- green chile
- cauliflower
- broth
- wine

## Directions

1. Mince the chicken liver with ham, adding parsley, onions, and green chile to make a mixture
2. Stuff the chicken with this mixture and roast it in a casserole
3. Cook the cauliflower separately, adding it with broth and wine
4. Serve by placing the stuffing around the bird
