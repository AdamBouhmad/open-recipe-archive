## Ingredients

- perch
- light broth
- anchovy
- fresh butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- flour
- water
- vinegar
- crayfish essence

## Directions

1. Cook the perch in light broth
2. Let them cool
3. Prepare and arrange them on a plate, keeping them hot
4. Make a sauce with chopped anchovy, fresh butter, salt, pepper, nutmeg, a little flour, water, and vinegar
5. Bind this sauce with crayfish essence
6. Pour over the perch and serve
