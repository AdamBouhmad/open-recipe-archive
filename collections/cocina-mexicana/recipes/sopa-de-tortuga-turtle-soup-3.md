## Ingredients

- turtle meat
- consumed broth
- pepper
- cloves
- onions
- carrots
- thyme
- bay leaf
- chicken dumplings
- parsley
- anchovies
- oil
- vinegar
- Madeira wine

## Directions

1. Cut sufficient turtle meat into pieces the size of a walnut
2. Soak the pieces
3. Add them to consumed broth with pepper, cloves, onions, carrots, thyme and bay leaf
4. Let everything cook over low heat for three or four hours
5. Meanwhile, prepare chicken dumplings seasoned with parsley and anchovies stewed in oil and vinegar
6. Poach these dumplings in consumed broth, drain them and place them on top of the turtle soup
7. Mix in three or four glasses of dry Madeira wine a few moments before serving
