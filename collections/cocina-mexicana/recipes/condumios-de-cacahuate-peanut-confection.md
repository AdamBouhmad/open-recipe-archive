## Ingredients

- One pound and a half of sugar
- One pound of shelled and ground peanuts

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the soft ball stage using one pound and a half of sugar
2. Add one pound of shelled and ground peanuts to the syrup. To avoid excessive oil release which spoils the confection quickly, do not grind the peanuts too fine but only crush them, or better yet grind them after toasting slightly
3. Place the mixture on heat and cook to the stage of cajeta
4. Remove from heat and beat
5. Pour into a box before it hardens completely
6. Cut the condumios when set
