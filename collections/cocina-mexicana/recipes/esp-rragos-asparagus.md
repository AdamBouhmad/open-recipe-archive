## Ingredients

- asparagus
- salt
- water
- butter
- broth
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut off part of the base of the asparagus and wash
2. Cook in salted water for about a quarter hour until they are crisp when broken
3. Arrange them on the serving plate
4. Pour sauce over them - if it is meat sauce, make it on the fire with strained broth, good butter, salt and pepper; if it is meatless, use white sauce
