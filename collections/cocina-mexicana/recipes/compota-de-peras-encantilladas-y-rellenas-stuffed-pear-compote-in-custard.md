## Ingredients

- mature bergamot pears without cloves
- six pounds sugar
- water
- filling

## Directions

1. Select ripe bergamot pears without cloves, peel them and hollow them out, removing the core and more
2. Weigh those that make six pounds and place them in warm honey and a little water made with six pounds of sugar, clarified and strained
3. Boil them for two or three hours and set them aside, saving them for the next day, when the same operation is repeated, and do the same another day
4. On the third day, when they have boiled a little, bring the honey to half-point stage and gradually remove the pears, placing them on a platter to drain
