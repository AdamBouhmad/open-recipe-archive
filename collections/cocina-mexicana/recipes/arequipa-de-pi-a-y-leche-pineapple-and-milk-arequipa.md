## Ingredients

- 5 cuartillos milk
- 2 pounds sugar
- 5 ounces starch
- orange blossom water
- 1 or 2 pineapples, ground and strained

## Directions

1. Add two pounds of sugar, five ounces of starch, and sufficient orange blossom water to five cuartillos of milk.
2. Strain everything.
3. Bring to a boil and add one or two ground pineapples strained according to quantity.
4. Add the juice and as soon as it separates from the pan, pour into the mold.
