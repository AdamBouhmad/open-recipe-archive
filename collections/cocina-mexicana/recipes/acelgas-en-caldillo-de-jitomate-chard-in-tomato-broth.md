## Ingredients

- pork belly strips
- tomato
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- chard
- broth
- vinegar
- saffron
- clove
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Fry some pork belly strips and remove them
2. In the same lard, also fry tomato, garlic, and parsley, all minced (those who like spice add green chiles to taste)
3. In this mixture add the chard, which has been cooked beforehand, well squeezed and minced with sufficient salt
4. When fried, add broth and a little vinegar with ground saffron, clove, and pepper, and a grating of nutmeg
