## Ingredients

- Raw calf's tongue
- Fat ham
- Garlic cloves
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Red wine
- Good vinegar
- Ginger, ground
- Sprigs of royal sage
- Butter or oil
- Chopped eyes
- Lean raw ham, minced
- Broth from cooking the tongue
- A heating cup of red wine
- Oregano
- Minced onion
- Capers
- Olives

## Directions

1. Pound the raw tongue with a stick until well tenderized
2. Cook it in water with salt
3. When half-cooked, remove and skin it
4. Lard it with fat ham, garlic cloves, clove, cinnamon, and pepper
5. Place it in a pot with red wine and good vinegar, clove, cinnamon, pepper, and ground ginger, sprigs of royal sage, and salt
6. Cook on two fires
7. When cooked, remove it
8. In another pot, heat butter or oil and fry a good portion of minced eyes and minced raw lean ham
9. When well fried, add some of the broth in which the tongue was cooked, a heating cup of red wine, some oregano, and minced onion, capers, olives, and salt
10. When seasoned properly, slice the tongue, bathe it in this broth, and serve
