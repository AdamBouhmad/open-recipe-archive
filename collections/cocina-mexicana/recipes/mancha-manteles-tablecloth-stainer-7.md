## Ingredients

- ancho chiles, deveined and toasted
- almonds
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- garlic
- roasted tomatoes
- lard
- salt
- vinegar
- oregano
- sugar
- pieces of ham
- apple
- pear
- pineapple, all cooked
- poultry and its broth

## Directions

1. Grind together deveined and toasted ancho chiles, almonds, clove, pepper, cinnamon, nutmeg, garlic, and roasted tomatoes
2. Fry in lard with salt
3. Once fried, add vinegar, oregano, sugar, pieces of ham, cooked apple, pear, and pineapple
4. Add poultry and its broth
5. The broth will be in good condition
