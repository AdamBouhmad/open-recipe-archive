## Ingredients

- onions
- oil
- sugar
- pepper
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut both ends off the onions and remove the outer layers
2. Place in a dish
3. Add oil, a little sugar, a grain of pepper and a pinch of ground cinnamon
4. Roast over a gentle fire
5. Cover the dish with an iron lid and embers on top until well browned
