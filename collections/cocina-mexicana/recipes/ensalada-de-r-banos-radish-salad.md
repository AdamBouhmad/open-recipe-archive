## Ingredients

- Radishes
- Vinegar
- Water
- Salt
- Capers
- Onion
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- Pomegranate
- Oil
- Pickled peppers

## Directions

1. Chop the radishes raw
2. Keep them in diluted vinegar for one full day with a little salt
3. Remove and squeeze without bruising them
4. Arrange them on a plate, decorating with soaked capers, chopped and blanched onion, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and pomegranate seeds
5. Add plenty of oil
6. Top with capers and sliced pickled peppers
