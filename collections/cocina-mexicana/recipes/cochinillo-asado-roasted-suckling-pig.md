## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- water
- flour
- lard
- oil

## Directions

1. Place in a caldron of water slightly warmer than lukewarm
2. After breaking the tusks, rub with the hand to remove the bristles, putting in and taking out of the water several times until all the hair is removed
3. Remove the hooves and gut it, being careful not to remove the kidneys
4. Make some cuts on the neck and four others on the loin
5. Introduce the tail between skin and meat
6. Secure the hands and feet by means of two iron skewers, passing one through the thighs and the other through the chest
7. Place for twenty-four hours in cold water, hang and let dry
8. Cover the belly with flour kneaded in lard
9. Place on the roasting spit
10. While roasting, baste with good oil
11. After removing from the fire, make other incisions on the neck
