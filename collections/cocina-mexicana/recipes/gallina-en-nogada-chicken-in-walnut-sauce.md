## Ingredients

- Forty large walnuts
- Well-roasted tomatoes
- Lard
- Two cloves of garlic
- Chicken broth
- Fine ground spices
- Chicken
- White wine glass
- Parsley

## Directions

1. After peeling forty large walnuts, toast them on a griddle
2. Grind them with well-roasted tomatoes
3. Put a casserole on the fire with lard and fry two cloves of garlic
4. Remove the garlic, and in the same lard fry the ground walnut and tomato
5. Add the broth in which the chicken was cooked, with fine ground spices and the chicken cut in pieces
6. Let it boil until it thickens
7. When removing it from the fire add a glass of white wine and chopped parsley on top
