## Ingredients

- salt cod pieces
- oil
- chopped cauliflower
- green beans
- chopped onion
- parsley
- wide chile
- roasted tomatoes
- cinnamon
- pine nuts
- toasted almonds
- raisins
- burned lard
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Boil the salt cod pieces
2. Fry chopped cauliflower, green beans, and chopped onion in oil
3. Remove this after frying
4. In the same oil, fry chopped onion, chopped parsley, a wide chile ground and mixed with roasted tomatoes and a little cinnamon
5. Once everything is fried, add pine nuts, toasted almonds, and raisins
6. Finally add the salt cod
7. Add a little burned lard
8. Garnish with chiles and olives
