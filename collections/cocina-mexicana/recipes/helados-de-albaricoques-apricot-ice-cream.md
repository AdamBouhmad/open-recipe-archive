## Ingredients

- Best ripe apricots
- One quartillo of water
- Sugar dissolved in water

## Directions

1. Choose the best ripe apricots
2. Remove the pits
3. Cut them into strips
4. Put on the fire with one quartillo of water
5. As soon as they boil, pass through a sieve
6. Sweeten with sugar dissolved in water
7. Incorporate everything well
8. Put in the sorbetera to freeze
