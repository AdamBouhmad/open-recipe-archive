## Ingredients

- beef tripe (babillas)
- salt
- garlic cloves
- ground cumin
- lard
- bread
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Wash the beef tripe and divide into regular pieces
2. Cook in water with salt, whole garlic cloves, and a little ground cumin until well cooked and white
3. Set aside the cooked tripe
4. Fry whole garlic cloves and bread pieces in lard until well browned, toasting the bread to the desired color
5. Grind together the fried garlic and toasted bread with cloves, pepper, and cinnamon
6. Put the ground mixture into a pan and add brown butter
7. Add the cooked tripe with some of its cooking broth to the lard
8. Season with salt and let it thicken slightly to the proper consistency
9. Serve garnished with fried parsley
