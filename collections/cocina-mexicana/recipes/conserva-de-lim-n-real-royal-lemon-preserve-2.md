## Ingredients

- Lemons
- Salt
- Water
- Sugar syrup (almíbar)
- Orange blossom water (agua de azahar)

## Directions

1. Clean the lemons and cook them with non-iodized salt
2. Remove and place in cold water, changing the water daily until the bitterness is completely lost
3. Place in sugar syrup, bring to a boil and remove from heat
4. Repeat this process for three days
5. On the last day, add orange blossom water and bring to the proper point if well-soaked; if not sufficiently soaked and the syrup has not reached the proper consistency, remove them to bring it to point, then return them to the syrup
