## Ingredients

- one beef leg, cut into four pieces
- three chickens
- a regular amount of whole vegetables
- concentrated broth

## Directions

1. Cut a beef leg into four pieces and put them to cook with three chickens and a regular amount of whole vegetables in a saucepan
2. Fill the saucepan with concentrated broth and skim immediately
3. Place the saucepan over gentle heat so the meat boils slowly for three or four hours until cooked
4. Strain the broth through a fine napkin so it becomes clear
