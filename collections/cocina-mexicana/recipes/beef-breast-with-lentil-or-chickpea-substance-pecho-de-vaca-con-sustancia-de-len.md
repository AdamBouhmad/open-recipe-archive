## Ingredients

- beef breast
- beef broth
- lard or ham
- mixed herbs bouquet
- salt
- lentil, chickpea, or bean substance
- spinach

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into finger-sized pieces and lard them
2. Place to cook in beef broth with half a pound of lard or ham in slices, a mixed herb bouquet and little salt
3. While the meat cooks, prepare a substance of lentils, chickpeas, or beans, tinting it green with a handful of boiled spinach that has been mashed or ground
4. Fry the substance with the tendon cooking liquid to give it body
5. If it becomes too liquid, allow it to thicken over the fire
