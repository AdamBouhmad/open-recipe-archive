## Ingredients

- 1 medium cabbage, halved
- 2 partridges, plucked and drawn
- 2 chorizos
- 6 sausages
- ham slices
- carrot wheels
- cooking broth

## Directions

1. Boil the cabbage halves in water until cooked
2. Remove and drain the cabbage, then refresh in cold water
3. Squeeze out all water from the cabbage
4. Tie each cabbage half with a plucked, drawn partridge that has been passed through flame, with legs folded and tied with string
5. Line a pot or casserole with ham slices
6. Place the cabbage with partridges in the pot along with two chorizos and six sausages
7. Layer with cabbage slices, placing on each layer half a sausage, a piece of ham, and a piece of chorizo
8. Arrange carrot wheels in the same manner
9. Strain the broth and add a little substance or strained broth
10. Let it thicken and pour over the cabbage or partridges
11. Serve
