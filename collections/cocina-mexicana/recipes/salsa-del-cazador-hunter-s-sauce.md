## Ingredients

- 1 good handful of bread crumb
- milk
- about half a pigeon egg of butter
- 15 grains of fine pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook 1 good handful of bread crumb in milk and when it has the consistency of pap, add about half a pigeon egg of butter, 15 grains of fine pepper and salt
2. Put in a sauce boat with the roasted game pieces
