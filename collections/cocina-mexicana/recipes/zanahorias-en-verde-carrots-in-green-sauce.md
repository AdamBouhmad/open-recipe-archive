## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Flour
- Parsley, chopped
- Pepper
- Salt
- Broth
- Parsley sauce
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Prepare as in the white sauce carrots
2. Add two tablespoons of flour, a little chopped parsley, pepper and salt
3. When sautéed, add broth with parsley sauce and lemon juice
