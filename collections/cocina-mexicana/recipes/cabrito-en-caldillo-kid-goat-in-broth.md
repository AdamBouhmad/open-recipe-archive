## Ingredients

- kid goat, cut into portions
- butter
- parsley, minced
- garlic cloves
- onion, minced
- water
- sour orange juice (2)
- vinegar
- clove
- ground pepper
- oregano

## Directions

1. Cut the kid goat into portions and wash in cold water
2. Grease a dish with sufficient butter and place the kid goat there, sprinkling parsley, garlic and abundant onion on top
3. Add sufficient water, the juice of two sour oranges and a splash of vinegar with clove and ground pepper and a bit of oregano
4. Cook over two fires, taking care that it does not burn and that the broth reduces
