## Ingredients

- oil
- onion
- rice
- broad chiles
- water
- salt
- pepper
- saffron

## Directions

1. Fry a little oil and onion.
2. When well sautéed, add the rice.
3. Sauté the rice a little as well.
4. Then add whole chiles, filling the pan with water.
5. Season with salt, a little pepper and saffron.
