## Ingredients

- Six pounds of sugar
- Half pound of ground white sweet potato
- Half pound of ground almonds
- A quarter of custard
- Crumbled camote
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with six pounds of sugar
2. Add half pound of ground white sweet potato and half pound of ground almonds
3. When the paste begins to set, add a quarter of custard
4. Let it acquire the proper consistency
5. Put the paste on a platter with crumbled camote and ground cinnamon on top
