## Ingredients

- Cooked carrots
- Butter
- Parsley, chopped
- Onion, chopped
- Salt
- Pepper
- Tomato sauce

## Directions

1. After cooking, cut into fillets and sauté with butter, parsley and chopped onion
2. Add a little salt and pepper and pour tomato sauce over the top
