## Ingredients

- lamb or kid liver
- lamb or kid lungs
- salt
- water
- oil or lard
- onion
- hazelnuts
- garlic
- bread crumbs, toasted
- spices
- hard egg

## Directions

1. Clean and cook with salt and water
2. Chop and fry in oil or lard with onion
3. When fried, make a sauce of hazelnuts, garlic, toasted bread crumbs and spices, dissolve everything with a little warm water, adding a finely chopped hard egg
4. Mix everything and let it cook for a while
