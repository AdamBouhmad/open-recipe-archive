## Ingredients

- butter
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- water
- pepper
- clove
- cumin
- sugar
- bread toasts
- parsley
- celery
- raisins
- almonds
- walnuts
- pine nuts
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Fry minced garlic in butter until golden, then set aside.
2. In the same butter, fry minced onion; add ground tomato and fry with the onion and garlic.
3. Add sufficient water with ground pepper, clove and cumin, and a little sugar according to taste.
4. Grease another pot with butter and make layers of bread toasts moistened with the broth prepared separately, mixing well when adding.
5. Cover the bread with very finely minced parsley and celery, raisins, almonds, walnuts, pine nuts and grated cheese, with the final layer being bread.
6. Let simmer until the soup reaches a regular consistency, then set aside.
7. When boiling stops, cover with grated cheese in a thin layer and place a hot griddle on top to brown it.
