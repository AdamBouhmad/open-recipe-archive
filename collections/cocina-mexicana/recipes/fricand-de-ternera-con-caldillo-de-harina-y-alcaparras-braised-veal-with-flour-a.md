## Ingredients

- slices of streaked ham
- slices of larded veal
- garlic
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- raw tomatoes
- whole garlic cloves
- ham pieces
- capers
- flour
- wine
- whole capers
- olives
- orange or lemon leaves

## Directions

1. Place slices of streaked ham in the bottom of a casserole
2. Arrange slices of veal larded with garlic, pepper, clove and cinnamon on top of them
3. Slice raw tomatoes and add them together with whole garlic cloves
4. Fry small pieces of ham, and grind capers with a little flour and wine, so that the sauce is not too white
5. Add ground clove, cinnamon and pepper to the sauce, whole capers, olives and orange or lemon leaves
6. Chop plenty of tomatoes and add them to the meat casserole with a little wine
7. Cook this on two fires, and when it is cooked, add the flour and caper sauce and let it boil without adding any butter
8. When it has thickened well, serve it
