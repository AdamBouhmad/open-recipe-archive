## Ingredients

- hare
- butter
- flour
- red wine
- water
- salt
- pepper
- bacon, in small squares
- small onions

## Directions

1. After removing the skin and emptying, cut by members; if it has blood set aside
2. Melt butter in the casserole, place on heat, adding a handful of flour; when the mixture is made add the hare, moistening with sufficient red wine and half water
3. Season with salt and pepper; also add small square pieces of bacon and small onions
4. Be careful to skim and degrease
