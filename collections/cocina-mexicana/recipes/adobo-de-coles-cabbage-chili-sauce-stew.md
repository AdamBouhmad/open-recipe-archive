## Ingredients

- cabbage
- ancho chile, soaked
- garlic
- cumin
- meat broth
- pork pieces
- pork ribs
- chorizo
- cooked chickpeas
- lard

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage and cook it
2. Once cooked, dry it well
3. Grind soaked ancho chile with garlic and cumin
4. Fry the chile very well and add meat broth, pork pieces, ribs, chorizo, and a few cooked and cleaned chickpeas
5. Fry the cabbage in lard in a pot and then add it to the adobo
6. They should become thick
7. They can also be made in a special way with tomato and chopped garlic, chopped onion, and ground cumin; add meat broth as with the others
