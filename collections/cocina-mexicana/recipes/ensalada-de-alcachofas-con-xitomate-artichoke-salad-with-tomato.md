## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Garlic and salt
- Xitomates
- Oil
- One quarter liter per dozen artichokes
- Whole garlic cloves
- Black pepper
- Clove
- Vinegar
- Breadcrumbs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the artichokes with garlic and salt, and cook plenty of xitomates separately, which will be ground.
2. In a pan put one quarter liter of oil for each dozen artichokes and fry whole garlic cloves in it.
3. With the garlic fried, add the xitomate, which will be left to fry well with black pepper and clove.
4. Then add the artichokes, a little vinegar, breadcrumbs, and the appropriate salt.
5. When taken to the table, all the broth that the xitomate gives should have been consumed, so that they are only moist.
6. Serve hot.
