## Ingredients

- cooked beets
- almonds
- coconut
- egg whites
- syrup
- orange blossom water
- sponge cake
- cinnamon
- pastille

## Directions

1. Grind the cooked beets with a little almond and an equal amount of coconut, each separately
2. Make egg whites beaten light and cook each paste in the syrup
3. After reducing to the proper consistency, add the necessary orange blossom water
4. Moisten the sponge cake with syrup
5. Layer each ingredient and make the last layer of beaten egg whites
6. Sprinkle cinnamon and pastille on top
