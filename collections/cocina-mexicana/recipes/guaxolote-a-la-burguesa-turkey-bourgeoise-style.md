## Ingredients

- Guaxolote (turkey)
- Mantequilla (butter) or jamón derretido (melted ham)
- Peregil (parsley)
- Cebolla (onion)
- Hongos (mushrooms)
- Ajo (garlic)
- Sal (salt)
- Pimienta gorda (black pepper)
- Lonjas de jamón (ham slices)
- Vino blanco (white wine)
- Caldo (broth)

## Directions

1. Pluck the turkey, singe it over a flame, and clean it
2. Flatten it slightly on the belly and fold in the legs
3. Half-cook it in a pan with butter or melted ham, with finely chopped parsley, onion, mushrooms, and a touch of garlic
4. Transfer to another pan with seasoning, salt, and black pepper
5. Cover the belly with ham slices
6. Moisten with equal parts white wine and broth
7. Leave to cook over low heat
8. Skim off the fat and add a little strained broth to the sauce to bind it
