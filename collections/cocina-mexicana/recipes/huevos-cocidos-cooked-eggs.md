## Ingredients

- eggs
- cold bread, grated
- white sugar
- saffron
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- wine
- almonds
- raisins
- pine nuts
- toasted sesame
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Boil the eggs until very hard and remove the yolks.
2. Beat the yolks with grated cold bread.
3. Fry each one, giving it the size desired.
4. Place the fried yolks on napkins to drain the fat and let them cool while the syrup is prepared.
5. Make a syrup with white sugar; when at the right consistency, add saffron, pepper, clove, and cinnamon to taste, a little wine, almonds, raisins, pine nuts, toasted sesame, and orange blossom water.
6. When the syrup reaches high consistency, remove from heat and cover until serving time.
7. At serving time, add the eggs and warm them.
