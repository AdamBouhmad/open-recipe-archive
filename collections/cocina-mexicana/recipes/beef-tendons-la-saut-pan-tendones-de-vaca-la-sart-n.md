## Ingredients

- beef breast tendons
- lard

## Directions

1. Lift the meat from the tendons of a beef breast and expose them
2. Cut very close to the rib bones
3. Cut the tender bones from which the tendons hang
4. Cut the tendons into small square pieces
5. Soak and lard them
6. Arrange so all pieces are equal and well-formed
7. Line a pot bottom with lard and place the tendon pieces on top
8. Cover with more lard and add a pan-full of sauce
9. Cook for four hours, observing if a skewer enters easily into them, which indicates they are well cooked
