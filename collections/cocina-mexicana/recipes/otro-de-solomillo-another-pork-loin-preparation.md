## Ingredients

- abundant onion, finely minced
- fresh parsley
- pork loin, sliced thin
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- ham slices
- lard
- tomato, ground without seeds
- water
- Sherry wine
- chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Mince abundant onion very fine and some fresh parsley and mix together
2. Cut the meat into very thin slices
3. Grind clove, cinnamon and pepper
4. Make holes in the meat, which are filled with onion and parsley and some thin slices of ham
5. Dust the holes with spices where the parsley and onion have been put
6. Once everything is arranged, put lard in a pan and fry the loin
7. Once fried, add ground tomato without seeds, stirring a lot
8. Add water; once it cooks and when it is thick, add Sherry wine, chiles, olives and capers
