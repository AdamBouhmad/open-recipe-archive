## Ingredients

- lettuces
- fine poultry stuffing
- beef pulp
- ham strips
- onions
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- bay leaf
- basil
- parsley
- eggs
- bread crumbs
- lard
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Proceed as for stuffed lettuces.
2. Drain the lettuces immediately.
3. Beat some eggs as for an omelet and coat the lettuces with them.
4. Roll them in bread crumbs and fry in lard until they are a good color.
5. Serve on a napkin garnished with fried parsley.
