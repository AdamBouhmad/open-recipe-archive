## Ingredients

- spinach
- salt brine

## Directions

1. Wash spinach well
2. Briefly place in boiling water
3. After removing, refresh well in cold water
4. Transfer to a barrel or container filled with thick salt brine, ensuring they are well covered
5. After some time, it can be taken from this preparation for all the stews mentioned above and many others
