## Ingredients

- cold-point syrup
- egg yolks
- sponge cake
- pastille
- colored pastille

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks over cold-point syrup and cook until they are very thick
2. Layer sliced sponge cake
3. Over this layer place one of paste and continue until the platter is full
4. Sprinkle each layer with syrup and colored pastille powder
