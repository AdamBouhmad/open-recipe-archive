## Ingredients

- one pound of sifted sugar
- one pound of raw or roasted peanuts, ground

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of sifted sugar with one pound of ground raw or roasted peanuts according to taste
2. Give the two things together a pass over the metate
3. Extract through a syringe and form the rings
