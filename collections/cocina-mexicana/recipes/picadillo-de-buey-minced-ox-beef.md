## Ingredients

- ox meat
- meat stock
- butter
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Roast a piece of beef on the spit, preferably the loin pulp
2. Let it cool and mince very finely
3. Clarify and reduce a few spoonfuls of red stock
4. At serving time, add the minced meat to the sauce, reheating in the oven without allowing it to boil
5. Add two ounces of fresh butter and heat on a plate with pepper and nutmeg, letting everything boil until it thickens well
6. Strain the meat or sauce through a sieve and pour over the plate
