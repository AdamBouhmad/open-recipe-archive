## Ingredients

- Pork tails
- breadcrumbs
- beaten egg
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cook the tails prepared as directed in the previous article in a frying pan
2. Let cool
3. Cover alternately with breadcrumbs or grated bread and beaten egg
4. Fry
5. Garnish for serving with a bouquet of fried parsley
