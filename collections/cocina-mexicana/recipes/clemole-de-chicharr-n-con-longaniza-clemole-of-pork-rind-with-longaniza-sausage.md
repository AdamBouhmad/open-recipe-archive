## Ingredients

- Longaniza sausage, in pieces
- Lard
- Salt
- Garlic cloves, chopped
- Dried ancho chile
- Pasilla chile
- Tomatoes, cooked
- Toasted tortillas
- Water
- Pork rind (chicharrón de carne)
- Epazote

## Directions

1. Fry pieces of longaniza in lard with salt
2. Remove the fried longaniza and fry chopped garlic cloves in the same lard until golden, then remove them
3. In the same lard, fry ancho and pasilla chiles that have been toasted, deveined, and ground with cooked tomatoes and toasted tortillas
4. Add a little water with regular-sized pieces of pork rind and the fried longaniza
5. Add a sprig of epazote
6. Let everything boil until the pork rind is cooked and the clemole reaches the proper consistency
