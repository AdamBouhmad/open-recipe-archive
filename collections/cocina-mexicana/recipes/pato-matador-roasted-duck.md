## Ingredients

- Female duck
- Paper
- Duck liver
- Lemons

## Directions

1. Pluck the duck and singe it in paper flame
2. Remove the wings and neck, cut the ends of the legs, turn them outward and tie before placing on the spit
3. Rub with its own liver and wrap in paper, which is removed when it is almost roasted
4. Serve hot with two whole lemons
