## Ingredients

- A handful of orange blossoms (azahar)
- Water
- Syrup (almíbar)
- One bottle of Champagne wine
- Fish glue (cola de pescado)

## Directions

1. Clean a handful of orange blossoms and place the petals in cold water after separating them from the pistil
2. Remove them and place in fresh water over the fire until they boil, then remove immediately
3. Drain and place in syrup and let it boil until it reaches the pearl stage (punto de aperlado bajo)
4. Once the blossoms are cooked, let the syrup cool
5. Add one bottle of Champagne wine and an equal amount of fish glue
6. Follow the same procedures as described in the previous article
