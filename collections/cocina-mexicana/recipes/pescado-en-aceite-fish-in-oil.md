## Ingredients

- fish
- oil
- garlic
- pepper
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous recipe, only instead of frying butter, fry oil with garlic, pepper, a little vinegar, and water.
2. Put the fish in this sauce, serving it very hot.
