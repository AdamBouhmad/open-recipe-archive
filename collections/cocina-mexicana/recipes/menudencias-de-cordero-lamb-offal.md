## Ingredients

- lamb head
- lamb heart
- lamb lungs
- lamb liver
- lamb feet
- ham pieces
- herb bundle
- carrot
- onions
- broth
- parsley
- onion
- thyme
- bay leaf
- garlic
- cloves
- oil

## Directions

1. Bleed and wash the head, heart, lungs, liver, and feet of lamb
2. Cook in broth with ham pieces, herb bundle, carrot, and onions
3. Make the sauce with two glasses of broth, parsley, onion, thyme, bay leaf, garlic, cloves, and a spoonful of oil
4. Simmer everything over low heat for some time
5. Strain to serve very hot in a sauce boat or over the offal arranged on a platter with the head in the center with brains exposed and surrounded by the other parts
