## Ingredients

- ribs
- fresh lard or oil
- broth
- flour

## Directions

1. Prepare and brown the ribs in a casserole or saucepan with fresh lard or oil
2. Add a little broth afterwards
3. Cover and let cook slowly after seasoning
4. When cooked, remove the ribs
5. Degrease the broth and mix with a little toasted flour
6. Give it a boil to thicken
7. Dip the ribs in the sauce and serve
