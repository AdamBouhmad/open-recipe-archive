## Ingredients

- carnero (lamb)
- manteca (lard)
- cebolla (onion)
- ajo (garlic)
- peregil (parsley)
- xitomate maduro (ripe tomato)
- caldo (broth)
- especias de todas (all spices)
- cominos (cumin)
- azafrán (saffron)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- azúcar (sugar)
- garbanzos (chickpeas)
- plátano largo (plantain)
- manzana (apple)

## Directions

1. Prepare lamb in one of the previously described ways and cut into regular pieces
2. Heat lard in a pot and fry plenty of onion, garlic, parsley and ripe tomato
3. Add the sufficient broth from cooking the offal, then add the offal itself
4. Grind all spices with a little cumin and saffron, and add to the broth
5. Add a bit of vinegar and a pinch of sugar
6. Add well-cooked chickpeas and slices of plantain and apple
7. Season carefully so it is neither watery nor too thick
