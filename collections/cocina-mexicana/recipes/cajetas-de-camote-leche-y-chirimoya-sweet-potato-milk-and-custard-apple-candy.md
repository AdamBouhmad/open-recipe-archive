## Ingredients

- One pound of sugar
- One pound of clean sweet potato
- One pound of sugar in powder
- Two quartillos of milk
- One pound of clean, pitted and ground custard apple
- One pound of sugar
- Shredded coconut (optional)

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup without lemon with one pound of sugar
2. When it reaches breaking point, set aside and add one pound of clean sweet potato mixed with one pound of powdered sugar, dissolved in two quartillos of milk, beating everything
3. Return to the fire and once it has boiled and is near cajeta point, add one pound of clean, pitted and ground custard apple and one pound of sugar, letting it boil until completely cooked
4. Custard apple may be omitted and the candy will still be good, and grated coconut may also be added when the syrup is put on
