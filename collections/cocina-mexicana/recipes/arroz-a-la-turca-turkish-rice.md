## Ingredients

- one pound of good rice
- concentrated broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Choose one pound of good rice and wash in many waters
2. Boil in plenty of water
3. Let drain and place in a saucepan with concentrated broth until it bursts
4. Moisten little by little
5. When half cooked, add enough liquid
6. Continue cooking until done
