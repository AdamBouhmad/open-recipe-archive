## Ingredients

- lard
- finely chopped garlic
- finely chopped onions
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- strips or slices of roasted and peeled green chile
- salt
- cloves
- pepper
- saffron
- cumin
- parsley, all ground
- cooked lettuce, chopped
- cooked cauliflower, chopped
- cooked artichokes, chopped
- olives
- capers
- cooked chicken, divided into quarters and fried
- vinegar
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Fry finely chopped garlic and onions in lard
2. Add roasted and ground tomatoes and strips of roasted and peeled green chile
3. When this mixture is half-fried or rendered, season with salt, ground cloves, pepper, saffron, cumin, and parsley
4. Add cooked and chopped lettuce, cauliflower, and artichokes, olives, and capers
5. Place the cooked chicken pieces, divided into quarters and fried, in this mixture
6. Add vinegar and a bit of chicken broth so it can simmer
7. Let the sauce reduce, remove from heat, and let rest before serving
