## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- salt
- broth
- parsley (optional for lean days)
- lean broth (for lean days)

## Directions

1. Make the same paste as for the soup à la Xavier in the following article, except for the parsley, in the quantity necessary according to the amount of soup to be prepared, being careful that it is very thick, so that when filling an ordinary spoon almost full, the paste can be strained with the finger, and when it falls into the boiling broth, it forms a round, long, or oval ball
2. Let the soup cook for half an hour and be careful to degrease it and ensure it is well salted
3. It can be made lean for days of abstinence, using lean broth of this kind
