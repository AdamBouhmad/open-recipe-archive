## Ingredients

- five eggs
- five ounces of powdered sugar
- half pound of ground and sifted starch
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat five eggs well with five ounces of powdered sugar until they become pure foam
2. Mix in half pound of ground and sifted starch
3. Beat a little more with the hand
4. Place greased molds, pour the necessary amount of dough
5. Place at two fires or put in the oven
