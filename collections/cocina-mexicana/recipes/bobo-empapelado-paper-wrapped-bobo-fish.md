## Ingredients

- Bobo fish
- All spices
- Butter
- Olive oil
- Five lemons
- Paper
- Red wine
- Vinegar
- Water

## Directions

1. Well desalted and cleaned bobo, grind all spices and coat the bobo inside and out with them, then with butter
2. Oil a piece of paper, squeeze five lemons over it and wrap the fish which is placed in a baking pan
3. On the fire, add red wine, vinegar and water to it and keep at two heats turning the fish
4. Once the broth is consumed, pour much olive oil into the pan so the bobo fries
