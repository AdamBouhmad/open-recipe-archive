## Ingredients

- very little lard
- plenty of oil
- a piece of bread
- a small garlic clove
- plenty of onion
- one tablespoon of Castilian vinegar
- a wide dried chile soaked since the day before
- cod broth
- parsley

## Directions

1. In very little lard and plenty of oil, fry a piece of bread and a small garlic clove; remove it
2. In the same oil, fry plenty of onion afterwards, letting it refry until it turns golden
3. Add one tablespoon of Castilian vinegar, let the vinegar dry and add a wide dried chile that has been soaked since the day before
4. Refry the stew and season with the same broth in which the cod was cooked
5. Once it has come to a boil, add the piece of bread and the garlic that was fried at the beginning after grinding it
6. When it is halfway fried, add a little chopped parsley
