## Ingredients

- rice
- lard or clarified butter, melted
- powdered sugar
- milk

## Directions

1. Wash the rice very well, spread on a cloth and place in the sun
2. Once well dry, grind very fine and pass through a sieve
3. Once well sifted, place in a vessel where it can be beaten well
4. Gradually add melted and well-clarified butter
5. Once well clarified, add very finely ground sugar and beat again until the sweet paste is very fine, as it will become sweeter when cooked
6. Once sweetened, add an equal quantity of milk
