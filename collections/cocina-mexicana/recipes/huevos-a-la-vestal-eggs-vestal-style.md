## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of cream
- two cuartillos of milk
- lemon peel
- coriander
- sugar
- sweet almonds
- two bitter almonds
- six to eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Boil two cuartillos of cream and two cuartillos of milk until reduced to half, adding a piece of lemon peel, a little coriander, and sugar to sweeten the cream.
2. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
3. Dissolve a few sweet almonds and two bitter almonds, both pounded or ground; add six to eight egg yolks, pass everything through a sieve, and cook in a bain-marie.
4. Serve these eggs as a course between dishes.
