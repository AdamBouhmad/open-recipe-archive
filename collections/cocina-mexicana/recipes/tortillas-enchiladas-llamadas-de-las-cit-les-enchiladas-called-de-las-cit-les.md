## Ingredients

- Jitomates
- Huachinango red chiles
- Lard
- Tortillas
- Pork belly in shreds

## Directions

1. Roast jitomates and grind them with huachinango red chiles and fry.
2. Separately, fry the tortillas in lard.
3. Fill them with shredded pork belly.
4. Cover them with the chile mentioned above.
