## Ingredients

- cooked hens
- onions
- lard
- parsley
- hen broth
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- nutmeg
- cilantro, toasted
- oil
- toasted sesame
- small chiles
- raisins
- almonds
- ham
- vinegar
- sugar
- white wine
- chorizo
- pork loin
- lettuce
- hard boiled eggs

## Directions

1. After the hens are well cooked, fry onion quarters in lard
2. Then drain them and in the same lard fry finely minced parsley
3. Add the broth in which the hens were cooked
4. Add back the onions that were removed
5. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper, nutmeg and toasted cilantro
6. Add this to the broth with oil and toasted sesame
7. Add minced small chiles, raisins and almonds
8. Add chopped ham, a little vinegar and a piece of sugar
9. Bring to a boil, and when thickened, if the broth is white, add ground toasted cilantro for color
10. Add a glass of white wine and taste, adjusting sugar or vinegar so that the wine, vinegar and sugar are prominent
11. Serve the hens adorned with sliced chorizo and pork loin that were fried with them and with minced parsley, whole lettuce hearts and sliced hard boiled eggs
