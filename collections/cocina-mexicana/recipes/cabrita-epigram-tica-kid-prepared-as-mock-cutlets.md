## Ingredients

- kid, one good foreleg quarter
- shoulder (espaldilla)
- breast (pecho)
- ribs (costillas)
- salt
- lard (manteca)
- eggs, beaten
- pepper

## Directions

1. Choose a good foreleg quarter and separate the shoulder, which should be tied in a saucepan right away. Cook the breast, and when it is done, place it between two casseroles with weight on top to flatten it. Cut the ribs, prepare them, and place them in a saucepan, dusting them with a little salt and sprinkling them with melted lard. Once the breast meat has cooled, cut it into pieces that imitate ribs; bread them and bathe them with beaten eggs, seasoning with pepper, salt, and melted lard; bread again a second time. Cut thin strips from the shoulder meat and do the same. When serving, fry the mock ribs in lard that has not been used for other frying, let them jump in the pan, and place them on the platter.
