## Ingredients

- Cooked cod
- Onion
- Lard
- Red tomato
- Garlic cloves
- Green chiles
- Bread
- Vinegar
- Cooked beans
- Cured chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. After cooking, cut the cod into slices and remove the bones, being careful that the pieces are not too small.
2. Fry onion with lard, well-colored tomato, garlic cloves and green chiles, all chopped.
3. The entire fried mixture should be as bulky as the fish.
4. Place in a large enough pan for the fried mixture and cod, adding a piece of bread soaked in vinegar to thicken.
5. Cover the pan and let it simmer over two low fires for more than half an hour, without stirring, until ready to serve.
