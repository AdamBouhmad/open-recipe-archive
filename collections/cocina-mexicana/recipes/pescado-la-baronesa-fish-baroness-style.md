## Ingredients

- whole fish
- egg yolks
- French oil
- salt
- vinegar
- lettuce
- radishes

## Directions

1. Cook the fish whole and place on a platter to cool.
2. When ready to serve, beat egg yolks and incorporate half a bottle of French oil, ground salt, and a little vinegar.
3. Cover the fish with this sauce.
4. Garnish with lettuce leaves and radishes.
