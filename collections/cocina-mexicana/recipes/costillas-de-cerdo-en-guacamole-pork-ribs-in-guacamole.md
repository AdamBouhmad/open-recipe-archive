## Ingredients

- pork ribs
- xitomatl, cooked
- garlic
- onion
- guajes
- broth from the pork
- salt
- green chiles
- small squash, cooked

## Directions

1. After cooking the pork ribs, cut them into small pieces
2. Grind well-cooked xitomatl
3. Fry it with chopped garlic and a little onion
4. Grind a portion of fine guajes or small ones
5. Dissolve this paste in a little broth from the pork cooking
6. Season with necessary salt
7. Add green chiles and small squash cooked in quarters
