## Ingredients

- pears, peeled
- clarified syrup
- four cups of clarified syrup
- one pound of ground almonds
- four egg yolks
- sugar powder
- cloves
- cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Peel the pears and place in clarified syrup
2. Once well strained, remove the pears and reserve the syrup to cover them, placing them before cooling in a strainer
3. In four cups of clarified syrup add one pound of ground almonds and place over heat
4. Once it thickens, remove to rest a little, then add four egg yolks and return to heat until it reaches the consistency of pulling away from the pan
5. Remove the pears which will be intact and place on a platter, pouring this paste over them until covered
6. On top of this place powdered sugar and a griddle with heat so it only browns
7. Then drive two cloves and three small cinnamon sticks into each pear
