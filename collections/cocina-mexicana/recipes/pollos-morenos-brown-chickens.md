## Ingredients

- chickens
- ham
- cinnamon
- clove
- nutmeg
- pepper
- biscuit
- broth
- salt
- wine
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Once the chickens are cooked, line a pot with ham.
2. Place half of the chickens on top, and over them another layer of ham; then the remaining chickens and then ham.
3. For four chickens, add an eighth of peeled cinnamon, clove, nutmeg and a little pepper, all ground with biscuit.
4. Add the broth in which they were cooked and sufficient salt.
5. When everything has boiled well and the meat is about to cook, add a quarter liter of wine with raisins and cleaned chopped almonds.
6. Put everything over two fires and leave until the sauce is almost consumed.
