## Ingredients

- corvina (sea bass)
- lemon slices
- parsley
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cleaning, place the whole corvina or divide it into regular-sized portions in a baking dish
2. Intersperse lemon slices and fresh parsley leaves between the portions, or if using whole corvina, place it between the lemon slices and parsley
3. Add oil in proportion and a little vinegar
4. Bake in the oven until cooked through
