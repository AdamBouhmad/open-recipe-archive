## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cloves
- nutmeg
- white wine
- sponge cake (mamón)
- pastille
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Roast ripe tomatoes in oven on a board
2. Press in a napkin so no juice remains
3. For each cuartillo of juice add half pound of white sugar with one cinnamon stick, six cloves and a piece of nutmeg all ground
4. Boil until it becomes syrup
5. Add a cup of white wine
6. Layer sponge cake and mixture
7. Top with pastille and cinnamon
