## Ingredients

- Two parts meat
- One part pork loin or pork fat
- Salt
- Ground black pepper
- Clean beef tripe

## Directions

1. Mince two parts meat and one part pork loin or pork fat together
2. Season the mixture with salt and ground black pepper so that the pepper flavor predominates
3. After everything is well incorporated, let it rest for one or two hours
4. Fill the clean beef tripe with this mixture
5. Prick the tripe at intervals to allow air to escape
6. Tie with threads at distances of one-third of a vara, or leave them at the desired size for the sausages, with the thread hanging from both ends
7. Once all are filled, pass a stick or rope through their threads and place it over a pot with boiling water
8. Allow the sausages to cook suspended in the water without touching the bottom of the pot
9. When cooked, remove them on the same stick or rope they were placed on
10. Let them cool and hang them in the air where the sun does not reach them
11. Eat them cold, roasted, or fried in lard
