## Ingredients

- grated bread or ground biscuit
- water or milk
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Boil grated bread or ground biscuit with water or milk
2. Add a little butter
3. Sweeten with sugar
4. Can be mixed with almond milk or made with ground hazelnuts in place of milk
