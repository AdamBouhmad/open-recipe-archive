## Ingredients

- rice
- eggs
- sugar
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare a quarter pound of rice as if for making a pastry, but instead of putting it in a mold, form small balls.
2. Soak them in eggs beaten with sugar.
3. Coat them with bread crumbs.
4. Soak again in egg and coat with bread crumbs a second time.
5. Fry them.
