## Ingredients

- six egg whites
- egg yolks from the six eggs
- one pound of flour
- forty cloves, ground
- one pound of white sugar, pounded

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until they rise as much as possible
2. Add the egg yolks, flour, ground cloves, and pounded white sugar
3. Beat everything with a small stick or paddle until the dough is somewhat loose and manageable
4. Using a small silver or wooden spoon, take small quantities to form little tablets like flattened nuts on paper
5. Sprinkle sesame seed on top
6. Bake in a warm oven
