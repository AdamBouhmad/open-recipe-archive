## Ingredients

- manteca
- cebolla
- xitomate picado
- rajas de chile verde mondado
- arroz
- agua
- pedacitos chicos de bagre
- sal
- pimienta fina
- yerbas para frituras

## Directions

1. Put a pot on the fire with manteca, and when it burns, fry onion, chopped tomato, and strips of peeled green chile in it
2. When everything is well fried, add the rice, after it has been thoroughly washed and dried in the sun
3. Let it brown in the manteca, and immediately add water and many small pieces of catfish
4. Season with salt
5. When the rice is half cooked, add a pinch of fine pepper
6. Put a griddle on top so the water is consumed
7. Garnish with fried herbs to serve
