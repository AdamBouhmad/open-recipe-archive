## Ingredients

- four egg whites
- lemon zest scrapings
- three or four tablespoons of fine powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat four egg whites thoroughly until they form quite a bit of foam.
2. Add some lemon zest scrapings and three or four tablespoons of fine powdered sugar.
3. Beat the mixture again until well incorporated.
4. Place on papers and follow the method explained in the previous articles.
