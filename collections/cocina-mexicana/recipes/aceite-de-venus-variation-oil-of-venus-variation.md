## Ingredients

- One ounce of anise
- One ounce of caraway
- Descaled rind of one or two oranges
- Two drams of mace
- One adarme of vanilla
- Sixteen quartillos of common aguardiente
- Four pounds of sugar
- Eight quartillos of water
- Saffron or caramel

## Directions

1. Put in infusion for five days at atmospheric temperature in sixteen quartillos of common aguardiente the anise, caraway, orange rind, mace, and vanilla
2. Proceed to distillation to draw out half of the liquor
3. Sweeten it with four pounds of sugar dissolved on the fire in eight quartillos of water
4. Mix everything exactly, filter through a cloth sleeve
5. Give it yellow color with saffron or caramel
6. Bottle it
