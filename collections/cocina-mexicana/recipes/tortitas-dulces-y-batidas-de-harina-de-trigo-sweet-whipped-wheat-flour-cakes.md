## Ingredients

- twenty-five eggs
- half a pound of lard
- half a pound of sifted sugar
- a piece of yeast the size of a pipila egg
- a handful of toasted sesame
- a little salt
- wheat flour

## Directions

1. Mix in an artesa or lebrillo twenty-five eggs, half a pound of lard, half a pound of sifted sugar, a piece of yeast the size of a pipila egg, a handful of toasted sesame, and a little salt
2. As it incorporates by hand, add little by little wheat flour without kneading, but rather lifting from the artesa and beating
3. The dough should be liquid as it should remain, then put it in small pans or appropriate molds without filling, because these cakes swell when cooking
4. Put quite a bit of raw sesame on top
5. Put to cook in a moderately warm oven, taking care that they do not burn or remain undercooked, for which they are checked with a popote
