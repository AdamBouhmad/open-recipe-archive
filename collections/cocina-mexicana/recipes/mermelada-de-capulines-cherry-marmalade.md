## Ingredients

- capulines
- two pounds of sugar per three pounds of capulines

## Directions

1. After removing the stems and pits from the capulines, dry them in a vessel over slow fire without stopping stirring
2. Clarify the sugar and add the capulines so they cook sufficiently, stirring often
3. For each three pounds of capulines need two pounds of sugar
