## Ingredients

- four to six pounds of beef
- one head of lamb
- sixteen to twenty quartillos of water
- sufficient salt
- one hen, well cleaned
- three carrots
- three turnips
- four leeks
- two onions
- one small bundle of parsley

## Directions

1. Place four to six pounds of beef, one head of lamb, sixteen to twenty quartillos of water, and sufficient salt in a pot
2. Skim the pot
3. After skimming, add one well-cleaned hen, three carrots, three turnips, four leeks, two onions, all cut in half, and a small bundle of parsley
4. Let it all simmer for at least eight hours, adding more broth as it reduces
5. Set aside and use to make the best soups
