## Ingredients

- hens
- meat loins
- ham
- chorizos
- spinach
- chickpeas
- tomatoes
- garlic
- green chiles
- raisins
- almonds
- candied citron
- hard-boiled eggs
- plantain
- vinegar
- sweet potatoes
- saffron
- spices
- oil
- capers

## Directions

1. Cook the hens with loins, ham and chorizos.
2. Cook the spinach and chickpeas separately.
3. Cut the hens into pieces, fry tomato, garlic and green chiles separately and add the well-squeezed spinach and chickpeas, then the meat.
4. Add raisins, almonds, candied citron, hard-boiled eggs, sliced plantain, a little vinegar, sweet potatoes, saffron and all spices.
5. Once seasoned properly, pour oil over the top and garnish with chiles and capers.
