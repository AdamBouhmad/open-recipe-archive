## Ingredients

- Teal ducks
- Cauliflower
- Water
- Flour
- Salt
- Butter
- Essence
- Fresh butter
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. After singeing and drawing the teal ducks, wring the legs
2. Clean and prepare the cauliflower, blanch it and cook in white broth made with water, flour, salt and butter
3. When the cauliflower is cooked, drain it and add to a good essence with fresh butter and black pepper
4. Let the broth thicken
5. Arrange the teal ducks on a plate with the cauliflower around them and pour the sauce over all
