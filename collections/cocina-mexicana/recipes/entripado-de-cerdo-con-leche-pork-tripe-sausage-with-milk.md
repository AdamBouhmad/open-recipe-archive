## Ingredients

- four yards of tripe
- twelve ounces pork fat without salt
- pound and a half of pork ribs
- seven lamb sweetbreads
- three quarts milk
- two chicken breasts
- twelve egg yolks
- onion
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- clove
- nutmeg
- whole onions
- laurel
- basil
- wine
- lard
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Parboil the pork, chicken and sweetbreads, cutting the fat into small pieces and heating it with white onion
2. Then add the pork, then the sweetbreads, and successively the chicken and chopped parsley
3. Remove from fire and season with salt, pepper, cinnamon, clove and nutmeg
4. Cook the milk with the egg yolks over gentle heat without letting it stay long on the fire, and mix with everything said
5. Fill the tripes with all this mixture
6. Pierce the filled tripes with a larding needle
7. Put them in boiling water with whole onions, parsley, laurel, basil, clove and wine to cook
8. This will take about two hours
9. Grease them with lard and roll them in bread crumbs
10. Grill them over the grate
