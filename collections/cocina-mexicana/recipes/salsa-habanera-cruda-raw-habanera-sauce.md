## Ingredients

- yellow squash cooked in the pot
- three to four garlic cloves
- three roasted tomatoes without skin
- salt
- peppercorns
- half small cup vinegar
- double amount of broth
- one small onion
- minced parsley
- one sweet chile

## Directions

1. Take cooked yellow squash from the pot
2. Crush in the metate with three to four garlic cloves, three roasted tomatoes without skin, sufficient salt, and peppercorns
3. Add half small cup of vinegar with double the amount of broth
4. Add one small onion, a little finely minced parsley, and one sweet chile
5. Keep everything raw and cold
6. This sauce is very good for vegetables
