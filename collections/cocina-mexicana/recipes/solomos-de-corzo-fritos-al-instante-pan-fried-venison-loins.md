## Ingredients

- venison loins (prepared, studded, and marinated)
- butter
- pepper sauce

## Directions

1. After preparing, studding, and marinating the loins as described in the previous article, fry in butter over very high heat
2. Arrange in the shape of a crown
3. Once set, place a good pepper sauce in the center
