## Ingredients

- woodcock meat and entrails
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Chop the woodcock meat and entrails very finely
2. Mix with salt, pepper, and lard, incorporating everything together
3. Make the toasts in the ordinary way
4. Place to cook on low heat in a pan
5. Serve with lemon juice
