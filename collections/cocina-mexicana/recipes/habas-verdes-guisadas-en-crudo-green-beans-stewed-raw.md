## Ingredients

- Green beans, raw
- Lard
- Roasted tomato, ground
- Onion, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Green chili, chopped
- Water
- Pepper
- Fresh cilantro sprigs
- Egg yolks
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the raw green beans in lard.
2. When fried, add the roasted and ground tomato mixed with chopped onion, garlic, and green chili, so that this mixture also fries.
3. Add sufficient water for the beans to cook, a little pepper, and some sprigs of fresh cilantro.
4. When the beans are cooked, add egg yolks cut with vinegar.
