## Ingredients

- Chestnuts, the most flattened
- Water
- Clarified syrup at medium consistency

## Directions

1. Select the most flattened chestnuts and remove the first shell
2. Prepare two pots with boiling water over the fire
3. In one pot place the chestnuts to give five or six boils, then remove with a skimmer and place in the other pot to finish cooking
4. Prick them with a pin; if they offer no resistance, they are ready, and remove from the heat
5. Remove one by one to strip away the inner skin or shell that remains
6. Add them to clarified syrup at medium consistency, letting them boil in it until the preserve reaches the proper point, then remove
