## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- 2 pounds veal from the kidneys
- piece of ham
- handful of soaked chickpeas
- 1 pound fresh pork
- quarter chicken
- banana with peel
- chorizo
- handful of green beans
- half cabbage
- piece of squash
- eggplant
- malanga
- Castilian squash
- 2 turnips
- 2 sweet potatoes
- 4 tomatoes
- 2 chayotes
- onion
- garlic
- cumin
- cilantro
- pepper
- saffron

## Directions

1. Prepare a medium pot with water to the middle.
2. Add a little salt, 2 pounds of veal from the kidneys, a piece of ham, a handful of soaked chickpeas, and 1 pound of fresh pork.
3. Add a quarter chicken and place to cook, being careful to remove the foam.
4. After it has boiled for about an hour, add well peeled and cleaned: a banana with its peel, a chorizo, a handful of green beans, half cabbage, a piece of squash, an eggplant, a little malanga, a piece of Castilian squash, two turnips, two sweet potatoes, four tomatoes, two chayotes, an onion and a piece of squash.
5. Let everything boil for another hour, being careful to remove any foam that rises, and for seasoning grind a little salt, two or three garlic cloves, some cumin grains and cilantro with two of pepper, and once everything is made into a paste, dissolve with broth from the pot, and add to it with two threads of crumbled saffron.
6. Let it boil a while and serve.
