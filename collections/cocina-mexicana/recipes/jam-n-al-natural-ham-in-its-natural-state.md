## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- red wine
- roots
- onions
- parsley
- fine herbs
- pepper
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Desalt the ham by placing it in water after making incisions on the surface of the lean meat and edges of the fat
2. Once desalted, wrap it in linen and tie it
3. Place in a pan of appropriate size, adding two quarts of water and two quarts of red wine
4. Add roots, onions, and a good bundle of parsley and fine herbs
5. Cook for five to six hours at very low heat
6. Let cool in the same broth
7. Remove from broth and lift the skin without removing any fat
8. Sprinkle with pepper and breadcrumbs, color with a hot iron plate and serve cold
