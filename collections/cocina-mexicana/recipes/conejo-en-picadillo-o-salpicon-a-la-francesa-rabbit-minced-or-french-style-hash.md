## Ingredients

- roasted and cooled rabbits
- Spanish sauce
- butter
- eggs
- fried croutons

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from two roasted and cooled rabbits and remove callouses
2. Chop extremely fine and bind at the moment of serving with Spanish sauce that has been reduced to thicken
3. Mix in a little butter and ensure it does not boil
4. Serve hot with soft eggs around it or fried croutons
