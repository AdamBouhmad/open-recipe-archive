## Ingredients

- milk
- semolina
- sugar
- salt

## Directions

1. Put the milk on the fire, and when it boils, scatter the semolina inside, stirring from time to time so that it does not stick nor form lumps.
2. The soup should not remain very thick, nor lacking in sugar, as the appropriate amount should be added with very little salt.
