## Ingredients

- Three or four chickens, roasted and cooked
- Two large spoonfuls of rice
- Concentrated beef broth
- Bread crusts
- Butter

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from three or four cooked roasted chickens that have been cooled
2. Pound the chicken with two large spoonfuls of rice that have been cooked for only a quarter of an hour in boiling water
3. When the chicken breasts and rice are well pounded together, dissolve the substance in good concentrated broth and pass through fine muslin
4. Moisten the fatty substance with concentrated broth so that it is neither too thick nor too watery
5. Prepare bread crusts as for common soup and a quarter of an hour before serving, moisten them with boiling concentrated broth
6. Put the remains of the chickens in the broth and let simmer over gentle heat for two hours
7. Strain the broth through fine linen or silk sieve and add the substance at the moment of serving the soup, which should be very hot and of good flavor
