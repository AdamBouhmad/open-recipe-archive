## Ingredients

- hare
- butter
- ham
- liver
- artichoke bottoms
- mushrooms
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- white wine
- sugar or thick syrup
- vinegar
- bread

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as the regular Civet de Liebre, with the difference that to this add a good piece of sugar or several spoonfuls of very thick syrup and a little vinegar, using less salt.
2. When serving, place fried croutons in butter around it.
