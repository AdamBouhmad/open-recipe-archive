## Ingredients

- Chopped garlic
- Butter
- Tomato, chopped
- Onion, chopped
- Turnip, chopped
- Rutabaga, chopped
- Carrot, chopped
- Celery, chopped
- Chard, chopped
- Lettuce, chopped
- Peas
- Beans
- Broth from the pot
- Pepper
- Clove
- Salt
- Bread slices
- Butter for frying

## Directions

1. Fry a little chopped garlic until well fried
2. In the same butter, add all the well-chopped vegetables: tomato, onion, turnip, rutabaga, carrot, celery, chard, and lettuce, as well as peas and beans
3. When well fried, add plenty of broth from the pot, pepper, clove, and salt
4. Let everything cook well
5. When sending to the table, add bread slices fried in butter to give a light boil
