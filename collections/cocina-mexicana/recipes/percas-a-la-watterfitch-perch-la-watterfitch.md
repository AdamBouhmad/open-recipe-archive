## Ingredients

- three large perch
- carp milt to replace the roe
- salted water
- parsley root
- carrot peel strips
- parsley leaves
- bay leaf
- three tablespoons of curled sauce
- four ounces of butter
- lemon quarters without peel
- salt
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Gut three large perch through the gills
2. Replace the roe with carp milt
3. Tie the heads closed
4. Place in a pot with lightly salted water, parsley root, carrot peel strips, parsley leaves, and a bay leaf
5. Let cook, and while cooking, remove the skin and scales
6. Separate the fins and place them at intervals across the body of the perch
7. Transfer the perch to another pot and pour the strained cooking liquid over them (discarding the bay leaf)
8. Add three tablespoons of curled sauce with four ounces of butter, lemon quarters without peel, salt, and black pepper to the pot
9. Add the carrot strips and parsley
10. Heat everything, ensuring the sauce is well bound
11. At serving time, drain the perch, arrange them, and pour the cooking liquid over them
