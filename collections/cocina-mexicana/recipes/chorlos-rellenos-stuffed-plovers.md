## Ingredients

- plovers
- intestines and giblets of the plovers
- bacon
- parsley
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- thin bacon slices

## Directions

1. Clean and pluck the plovers
2. Open them below the tail when cleaned and plucked to remove the intestines and giblets
3. Chop all the extracted parts with a little bacon, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper
4. Fill the plovers with this mixture
5. Cover with thin slices of bacon
6. Roast at a gentle fire
