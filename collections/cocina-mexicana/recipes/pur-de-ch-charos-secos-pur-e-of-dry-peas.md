## Ingredients

- dry peas
- onions
- carrots
- lard or butter
- broth
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Sauté onions and carrots cut in quantity proportion in a pot with lard or butter
2. After some time, add broth and a small bundle of fine herbs
3. Let it simmer for a couple of hours over very low heat
4. Pass through a sieve and season
5. Serve hot
