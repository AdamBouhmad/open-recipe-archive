## Ingredients

- onions
- butter
- spoonfuls of curly sauce
- cream, in proportion of three quartillos for thirty large onions
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare and fry the onions in butter as in the preceding article, but without letting them take color
2. When they are well broken down, add some spoonfuls of curly sauce, cream in the proportion of three quartillos for thirty large onions, and a little sugar
3. Allow everything to reduce at a very hot fire, stirring constantly
4. Pass through a cloth when it is well thick
