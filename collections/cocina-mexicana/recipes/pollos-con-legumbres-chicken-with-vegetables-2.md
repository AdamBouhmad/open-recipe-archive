## Ingredients

- lard
- ground garlic
- cooked chicken, divided into quarters
- chopped cabbage
- chopped carrots
- chopped lettuce
- chopped string beans
- peas
- small onions
- chorizos
- strips of ham
- capers
- olives
- chicken broth
- cloves
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat lard and add ground garlic
2. Place the cooked chicken, divided into quarters, in the lard
3. Add chopped cabbage, carrots, lettuce, and string beans, along with peas, small onions, chorizos, and strips of ham, all cooked
4. Add capers and olives
5. Pour in some chicken broth
6. Season with cloves, pepper, and salt
7. Let everything simmer until most of the broth is consumed
