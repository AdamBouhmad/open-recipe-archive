## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- beef slice
- veal pulp
- capon
- carrots
- turnips
- kohlrabi or parsnips
- parsley root
- celery root
- onions
- asparagus
- sorrel
- chervil
- bread crusts

## Directions

1. Place half-roasted, defatted and skinned lamb leg in a pot with beef slice, veal pulp, capon, two carrots, two turnips, two kohlrabi or parsnips, parsley root, celery root and some chopped onions
2. Let everything cook for a long time so the broth becomes well-nourished
3. In another small pot, cook three or four bunches of asparagus, some sorrel and chervil, cut, with broth from the large pot
4. Soak bread crusts or toasts on which arrange the asparagus and sorrel
5. Serve hot
