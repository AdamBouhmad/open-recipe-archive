## Ingredients

- green and white guavas
- sugar
- tejocote jelly

## Directions

1. The guava should be green and white in color; peel it, remove the seeds and grind it
2. Sieve and then weigh it; for each pound of fruit, use one pound of sugar, and for every four pounds of sugar, add half a cuartillo of tejocote jelly
3. Give the sugar a caramel point, mix it with the fruit and let it boil until you can see the bottom of the pot
4. In this state, beat with the greatest force possible until it turns white
5. Pour into small boxes, and after drying, to remove the paper, moisten the top, which also prevents them from crumbling
