## Ingredients

- one pound of rice
- six quarters of milk
- sugar to taste
- ten egg yolks
- raw milk
- orange blossom water
- carmine or small bread for pink color

## Directions

1. Wash one pound of rice with cold water, and then with hot water.
2. Spread it on a cloth or napkin to dry and then grind it very well.
3. Add six quarters of milk and sugar to taste.
4. Put on the fire, stir well, and when it boils, add ten egg yolks dissolved in raw milk and strained through a sieve, a little orange blossom water and carmine or a small piece of bread to give it a pink color.
5. When it detaches from the spoon, it is ready and pour it on a platter to serve cold.
