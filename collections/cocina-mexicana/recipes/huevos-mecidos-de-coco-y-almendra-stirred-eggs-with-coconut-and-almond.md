## Ingredients

- sugar
- grated coconut
- almonds
- white camotes
- cream
- egg yolks
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Make syrup with two and a half pounds of sugar
2. When at medium consistency, cool slightly and mix in grated coconut, one pound of almonds and four or five white camotes ground
3. Add a quartillo of cream and yolks of eighteen eggs with a little orange blossom water
