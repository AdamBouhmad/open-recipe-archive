## Ingredients

- Salt pork or fat ham
- Fat or oil for frying

## Directions

1. Cut slices of fat ham two to three inches long
2. Place them in a cooking pot over fire
3. Fry them in the fat that they release themselves
4. When they become soft like cracklings, remove them and place them in a cool place to finish frying
5. Toast them to serve
