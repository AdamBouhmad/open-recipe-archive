## Ingredients

- 1 pound flour
- some ground sugar
- some tequesquite
- lard and oil
- some yeast dissolved in water

## Directions

1. To 1 pound of flour add some ground sugar, some tequesquite, lard and oil, and some yeast dissolved in water.
2. Add water to combine and knead.
3. When ready, make as the other empanada recipes.
