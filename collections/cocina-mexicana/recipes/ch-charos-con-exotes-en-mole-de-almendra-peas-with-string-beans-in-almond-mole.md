## Ingredients

- Dried ancho chiles
- Almonds
- Toasted bread
- Lard
- Peas
- String beans, chopped
- Longaniza sausage
- Small sausages (choricitos)
- Salt
- Water

## Directions

1. Remove the veins from dried ancho chiles, soak them, and grind them with almonds and toasted bread
2. Fry in lard
3. Add the peas, chopped string beans, pieces of longaniza and small sausages that have been cooked together in water with salt, and the broth in which they were cooked
4. Let it boil until the mole thickens
