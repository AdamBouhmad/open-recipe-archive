## Ingredients

- Bitter oranges
- Water
- Sugar syrup (almíbar)
- Orange blossom water (agua de azahar)

## Directions

1. Remove the yellow part with a grater, make a small crown at the top
2. Boil changing waters and do the same as said for lemons without the precautions of air and others that only they require
3. Three days after the last boil, roll in a basin and place in the sun, changing water daily until they are no longer bitter
4. With a small handling spoon, extract only the seeds
5. Complete the preservation with the same method as the previous ones
