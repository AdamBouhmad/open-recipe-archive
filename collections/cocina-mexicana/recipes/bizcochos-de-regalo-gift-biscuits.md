## Ingredients

- six pounds of flour
- six pounds of yeast
- one and a half pounds of butter
- one pound and twelve ounces of sugar
- sixteen eggs

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Form the biscuits
3. Bake in the oven
