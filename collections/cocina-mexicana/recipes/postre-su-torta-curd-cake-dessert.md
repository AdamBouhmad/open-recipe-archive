## Ingredients

- curdled milk
- salt
- sugar
- cinnamon
- clove
- butter
- sponge cake
- milk

## Directions

1. Press the curdled milk as if making cheese
2. Pass it through a metate and add a little salt, sugar, cinnamon and clove
3. Butter a cazuela
4. Lay down a layer of the curd and another of sponge cake soaked in milk cooked with sugar and cinnamon
5. Continue laying layers until filling the platter
