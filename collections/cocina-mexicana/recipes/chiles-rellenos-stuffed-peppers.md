## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Cooked pork
- Garlic, chopped
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon, ground
- Salt
- Oregano
- Sugar
- Flour
- Eggs
- Tomatoes
- Onions
- Vinegar
- Parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Roast the chiles and remove all the skin
2. Devein them well and give them a boil with salt
3. Remove from that water, place in vinegar and salt and leave from one day to the next
4. Chop cooked pork meat
5. Fry some chopped garlic and when half-fried incorporate the meat, clove, pepper and ground cinnamon, salt, oregano and a little sugar
6. Stir everything and let cool
7. Remove the chiles from the vinegar, wash them and fill them
8. Cover them on top with a little flour, then roll them in beaten egg and fry
9. Make the broth with tomatoes, onions and garlic, all well fried; season with clove, pepper and vinegar; add water and sufficient salt
10. When the broth boils add some chopped parsley and add the chiles to give them a boil
