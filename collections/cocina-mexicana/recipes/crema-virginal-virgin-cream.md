## Ingredients

- Four ounces of rose petals
- Four ounces of orange blossoms
- Eight quartillos of brandy
- Sugar
- Water
- One ounce of reseda spirit

## Directions

1. Mix four ounces of rose petals and four ounces of orange blossoms together well
2. Leave in infusion for several hours in eight quartillos of brandy
3. Distill to obtain slightly more than half the liquor
4. Sweeten in proportion like the previous creams
5. Add one ounce of reseda spirit, stir well and filter
