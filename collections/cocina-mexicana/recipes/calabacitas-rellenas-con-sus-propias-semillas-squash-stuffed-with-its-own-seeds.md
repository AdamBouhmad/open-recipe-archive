## Ingredients

- calabacitas (squash)
- water
- salt
- garlic
- onion
- xitomates (tomatoes)
- parsley

## Directions

1. Divide the calabacitas lengthwise into two parts.
2. Cook them in salted water.
3. Remove them and with a spoon extract the insides or hearts, which are chopped a little.
4. Chop also garlic, onion, xitomates, and parsley, and fry everything together.
