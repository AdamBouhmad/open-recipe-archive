## Ingredients

- cooked egg yolk
- fried bread
- oil
- garlic
- cinnamon
- pepper
- water
- fish
- anchovy sauce
- lettuce
- bread slices

## Directions

1. Beat a cooked egg yolk with a slice of bread fried in oil, garlic, cinnamon, and pepper.
2. Fry in the same oil used for the fish, without frying too much.
3. Add a little water to this sauce.
4. Add the already-fried fish.
5. If whole, coat the inside and outside with this sauce, then coat again with anchovy sauce.
6. Do not return to the fire.
7. Place over the steam of a pot or on a hot surface.
8. Garnish the platter with fried bread slices spread with anchovy sauce.
