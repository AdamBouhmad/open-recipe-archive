## Ingredients

- pork meat cut into small pieces
- wide chile, deveined
- mulato chile, deveined
- a little pepper
- cloves
- Castilian vinegar
- a little oregano from Spain
- thyme
- whole anise
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Cut pork meat into small pieces
2. Devein a little wide chile and a little mulato chile and soak in hot water
3. Wash very well so it is not too spicy
4. Grind everything together with a little pepper and cloves
5. Remove from the metate with Castilian vinegar and place in a dish
6. Add to the chopped meat a little Spanish oregano, thyme, and whole anise, salt to taste and ground
7. If it lacks vinegar, add a little more
8. Mix everything well and fill cow intestines
9. Hang them where smoke rises, which they should receive for three days
10. Then place them in the air to finish drying, avoiding direct sun
