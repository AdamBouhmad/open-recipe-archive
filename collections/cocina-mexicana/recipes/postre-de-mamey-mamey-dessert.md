## Ingredients

- Mamey, well softened by hand
- Ground sponge cake
- Cinnamon
- Beaten eggs
- Syrup

## Directions

1. Make a dough or paste with mamey well softened by hand, ground sponge cake, cinnamon, and beaten eggs
2. Put this in syrup and let it cook over two fires
3. Decorate it as the squash dessert is decorated
