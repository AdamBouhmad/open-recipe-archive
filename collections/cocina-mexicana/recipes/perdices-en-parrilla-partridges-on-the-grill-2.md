## Ingredients

- Partridge
- Butter
- Good seasoning
- Salsa a la Pebre or orange sauce

## Directions

1. Split in two pieces along the kidneys
2. Roast and fry
3. Brown in a pan with a little butter and good seasoning, turning on both sides
4. Place on the grill, grease them
5. Serve with salsa a la Pebre or orange sauce
