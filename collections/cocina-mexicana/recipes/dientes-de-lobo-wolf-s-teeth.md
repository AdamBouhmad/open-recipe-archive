## Ingredients

- Two sheets of paper cut lengthwise
- Common puff pastry dough
- Butter
- Pasta (see PASTA entry for composition)
- Anise sugar

## Directions

1. Fold two sheets of paper cut lengthwise into a fan shape
2. Bend and arrange them on a base of common puff pastry dough
3. Butter the paper
4. Open the pleats slightly and dress with a spoonful of pasta, conducting and arranging it with your finger across the paper sheets, leaving sufficient space between them so the teeth do not touch when cooking
5. Dust with anise sugar
6. Bake in a slightly warm oven until they take on a beautiful color
7. Remove from oven and take the paper by its ends, separating and pulling the leaves away from the teeth without breaking them
