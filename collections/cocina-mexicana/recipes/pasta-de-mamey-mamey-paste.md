## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- a pound and a half of ground and sieved mamey

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the point of gathering in water with one pound of sugar
2. Off the fire, mix with a pound and a half of ground and finely sieved mamey
3. Return the mixture to the fire and as soon as it detaches perfectly from the pan, pour it out so the paste is formed
