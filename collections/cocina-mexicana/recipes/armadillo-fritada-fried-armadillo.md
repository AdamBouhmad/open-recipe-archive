## Ingredients

- armadillo meat
- salt
- onions
- garlic heads
- spearmint
- laurel
- vinegar
- sugar
- cloves
- pepper

## Directions

1. On the third day after killing the armadillo, place it in water with a little salt
2. After two or three boils, change the water and rinse in hot water
3. Grease a pan with lard and place the meat in it
4. Add two or three whole onions, two garlic heads, some spearmint and laurel
5. Cover with water and let boil until completely cooked
6. When cooked, add a little vinegar, a piece of sugar, ground cloves and pepper, and salt to taste
7. Let the liquid reduce and brown in the fat
