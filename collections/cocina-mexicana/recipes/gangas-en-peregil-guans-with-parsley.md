## Ingredients

- Guans
- oil and lard
- salt to taste
- water
- onion, sliced
- parsley, ground
- capers, ground
- clove, cinnamon and pepper
- strong vinegar
- a piece of sugar

## Directions

1. After washing the guans well, put them to fry with a little oil and lard.
2. When they begin to take color, add water in excess to cover them.
3. Add competent salt and let them cook until all the water is consumed.
4. Then add considerable sliced onion, and as soon as it is fried, add a little boiling water and grind a portion of parsley and an equal portion of capers, all ground with clove, cinnamon and pepper.
5. Add a bit of strong vinegar, a piece of sugar and let them boil until the broth thickens well.
6. Serve them in it, rolling them when bringing them to the table, with a bit of vinegar.
