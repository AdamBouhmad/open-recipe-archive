## Ingredients

- half an arroba of pork meat, loin and lean by half
- three pounds of fat
- one pound of washed and ground wide chili
- six ounces of clean garlic
- one-eighth of cumin
- salt to taste
- two quartillos of egg yolk vinegar
- one quartillo of Jerez wine
- one ounce of cinnamon
- one ounce of clove
- one ounce of pepper
- one large nutmeg

## Directions

1. For half an arroba of pork meat, loin and lean by half, add three pounds of fat
2. Mince the meat finely, put one pound of washed and ground wide chili, six ounces of clean garlic, one-eighth of cumin and salt to taste
3. Mix with two quartillos of egg yolk vinegar and one of Jerez wine
4. Let infuse for twelve hours, and the next day add spices in powder, one ounce of cinnamon, one of clove, one of pepper and one large nutmeg
5. Mix everything very well and let rest for three or four hours
6. With this fill bull tripe, giving them a regular size
7. Hang them above the brasero so they dry and do not get moldy
