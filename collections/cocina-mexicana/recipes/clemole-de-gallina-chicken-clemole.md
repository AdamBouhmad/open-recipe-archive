## Ingredients

- ancho chiles, deveined
- tortillas
- garlic, fried in lard
- toasted sesame seeds
- fresh cilantro, toasted
- cloves
- pepper
- ginger
- cumin
- cooked tomatoes
- lard
- salt
- cooked chicken
- pork papad
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Devein the ancho chiles, treat and soak them
2. Fry tortillas and garlic in lard
3. Toast sesame seeds and fresh cilantro
4. Grind together the chiles, fried tortillas, garlic, sesame seeds, cilantro, cloves, pepper, ginger, cumin, and cooked tomatoes
5. Fry this mixture in sufficient lard with salt
6. Add the cooked chicken, pork papad, and chicken broth
7. Simmer and finish as with previous recipes
8. An alternate version can be made using peanuts instead of tomatoes
