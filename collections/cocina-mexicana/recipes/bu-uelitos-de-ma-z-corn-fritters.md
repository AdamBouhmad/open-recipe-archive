## Ingredients

- corn
- water
- anise
- lard
- salt
- eggs
- syrup

## Directions

1. Grind corn dry
2. Heat water with anise, lard and salt to boiling
3. Once it crumbles, pour it into a bowl to cool
4. Add as many eggs as are needed for it to puff up
5. Fry in lard that is not very hot
6. Cook in syrup
