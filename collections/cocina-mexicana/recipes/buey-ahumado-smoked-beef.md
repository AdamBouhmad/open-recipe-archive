## Ingredients

- beef, large pieces
- fine salt
- green wood for smoke

## Directions

1. Begin by dividing it into large pieces, which are sprinkled with fine salt.
2. After two or three days, place in a press and hang it in a chimney, far from the flame so the fat does not melt.
3. Below, light a fire with green wood, which produces plenty of smoke.
4. Cut the smoked beef into very thin slices and eat it raw or cooked on toast of butter or lard.
