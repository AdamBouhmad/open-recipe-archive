## Ingredients

- green peas
- broth
- carrot rounds
- onion slices
- lard
- bread
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook the green peas in broth, adding carrot rounds and onion slices with a little lard
2. When cooked, grind them in a metate and pass the liquid through a sieve
3. Add more broth if it is too thick
4. Pour this liquid over some small pieces of fried bread
5. When they are well soaked, serve
6. If spinach juice is added, the purée will retain the green color of the legume
