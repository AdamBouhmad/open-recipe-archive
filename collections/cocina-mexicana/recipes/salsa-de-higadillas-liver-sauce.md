## Ingredients

- pork or poultry livers
- garlic
- parsley
- bread crumb
- vinegar
- onion
- broth

## Directions

1. Pound the pork or poultry livers with clean garlic and parsley and grind them with a bread crumb soaked in vinegar
2. In a pot, fry minced onion, and when it becomes golden, add the liver mixture dissolved in good vinegar, adding a little broth
3. Let everything boil on low heat until it acquires regular consistency
