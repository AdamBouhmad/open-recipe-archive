## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- one large cup of virgin honey
- half a pound of clean ground almonds
- six hard biscuits, ground

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with three pounds of sugar and one large cup of virgin honey
2. Clarify the syrup
3. Add half a pound of clean ground almonds
4. Let it boil until a small amount dropped in cold water sets
5. Remove from the fire
6. Mix in six ground hard biscuits
7. Empty into a platter
