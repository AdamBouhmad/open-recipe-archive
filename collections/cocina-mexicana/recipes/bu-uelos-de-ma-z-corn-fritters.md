## Ingredients

- Nixtamal from half a measure of corn
- Water
- Lard
- Aniseed

## Directions

1. Grind the nixtamal from half a measure of corn as if for making atole and let it settle
2. Boil a little water with lard and aniseed
3. Add the settled dough, stirring continuously until it comes away from the pan
