## Ingredients

- three or four pounds of sugar
- double quantity of rose water

## Directions

1. Pound three or four pounds of sugar in a marble mortar.
2. Pass through a horsehair sieve.
3. Dissolve in an earthenware vessel with double quantity of rose water until it obtains a certain consistency.
4. Proceed as described with the pan and needle.
