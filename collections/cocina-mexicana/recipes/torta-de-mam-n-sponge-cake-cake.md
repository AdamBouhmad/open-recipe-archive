## Ingredients

- four boxes of cold molded mamón
- one pound syrup at high point

## Directions

1. Add four boxes of cold molded mamón to one pound of syrup at high point and let it boil two or three times until it becomes paste, which is put in the cake pan and made to set over two fires or in the oven
