## Ingredients

- pork liver
- lard
- fat ham
- parsley
- onion
- pepper
- salt
- spices
- ham slices for garnish

## Directions

1. Chop very finely the pork liver, lard, fat ham, parsley, and onion
2. Season the chopped mixture with pepper, salt, and spices, with the lard and ham together forming a volume equal to the liver
3. Line the bottom of a casserole to a height of three fingers with the chopped mixture
4. Extend over it strips of well-seasoned ham
5. Put a second layer of chopped mixture
6. Continue this way until the casserole is full, covering all with ham slices as the final layer
7. Place in the oven
8. Once removed, let it cool
9. To serve the cheese, warm the mold or casserole slightly and turn it over onto a plate
