## Ingredients

- Milk, as needed
- Egg yolks, four per cup, with the white removed
- Sugar
- Cinnamon, one large piece

## Directions

1. Boil the milk well and when it boils add the sugar and a large piece of cinnamon and continue boiling until it thickens
2. Remove from heat and continue stirring so that it does not form skin
3. When cool, measure the milk and add the yolks, mix them very well with a spoon, strain, and put to cook in a water bath
4. To know when it is done, insert a straw and when it comes out clean it is done
5. Leave in the cazuela until the water cools a little, remove it, and cover the cazuela with linen so that it does not crack
