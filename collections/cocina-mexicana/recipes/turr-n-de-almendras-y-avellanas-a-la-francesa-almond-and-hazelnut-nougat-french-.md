## Ingredients

- 4 ounces almonds
- 4 ounces hazelnuts
- 2 pounds syrup prepared at strong flower point
- 3 or 4 pieces of lemon peel
- 1 egg white, beaten

## Directions

1. Soak almonds and hazelnuts in hot water to peel them
2. Transfer to cold water and drain well
3. Add well-drained nuts to 2 pounds of syrup prepared at strong flower point
4. Add 3 or 4 pieces of lemon peel
5. Stir everything with a spoon
6. Add 1 well-beaten egg white
7. Stir a second time
8. Pour into wooden boxes lined with paper
9. If the nougat is not well spongy, place it over fire for a brief time
