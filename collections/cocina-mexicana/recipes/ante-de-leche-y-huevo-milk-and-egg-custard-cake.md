## Ingredients

- three cuartillos of milk
- one pound of sugar
- eight egg yolks
- one pozuelo of flour
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Mix three cuartillos of milk with one pound of sugar, eight egg yolks, and one pozuelo of flour
2. Strain everything and put over heat until it reaches the proper consistency
3. Place over layers of sponge cake, decorating the last layer with cinnamon, raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
