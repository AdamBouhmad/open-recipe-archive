## Ingredients

- one pound clean rice, washed in cold water
- butter
- salt
- nearly half an ounce of cinnamon
- two quartillos of milk with necessary sugar
- two more quartillos of milk
- six beaten eggs
- sugar and cinnamon for syrup

## Directions

1. Put in a casserole with boiling water, a finger of butter and a little salt, one pound of clean rice washed in cold water
2. When it is close to cooking, add nearly half an ounce of cinnamon and two quartillos of milk with the necessary sugar
3. As soon as it consumes, strain two more quartillos of milk and add them
4. When the pasta is at the right point, which is known in that it does not fall when turning the casserole over, remove from the fire and empty in a platter to cool, taking care to stir it so that it does not form a skin, and afterwards add six beaten eggs when they are well risen
5. Put the cake in a pan or cake dish so that it sets over two fires, and serve with syrup made of sugar with cinnamon on top
