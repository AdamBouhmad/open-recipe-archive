## Ingredients

- veal cut in slices
- butter
- whole garlic cloves
- fine spices ground
- spices in grain
- onion
- Carlon wine
- Jerez wine
- vinegar
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut the meat in slices and place in a pot with a little butter, whole garlic cloves, ground fine spices and other spices in grain
2. Add a little onion, Carlon wine and Jerez wine, vinegar and water with sufficient salt
3. Cover the pot with brown paper and a plate on top
