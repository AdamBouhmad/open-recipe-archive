## Ingredients

- One large pineapple
- Four ounces of grated and ground coconut
- One and a half pounds of sugar at breaking point consistency

## Directions

1. Cook and grind one large pineapple
2. Mix with four ounces of grated and ground coconut in clarified syrup made with one and a half pounds of sugar at breaking point consistency
3. Achieve point higher than cajeta
4. Remove
5. Beat
6. Pour into molds
