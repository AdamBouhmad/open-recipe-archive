## Ingredients

- cooked young pigeons
- sponge cake powder
- lard
- ground sponge cake
- clove
- cinnamon
- Chinese orange juice
- white wine
- sugar
- raisins
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. After cooking the pigeons, roll them in sponge cake powder and fry them in lard, remove them from the fat and add to it more sponge cake, ground with clove and cinnamon, and then, juice of Chinese orange, white wine, a little sugar, raisins and pine nuts
2. Let the sauce boil until well seasoned and somewhat thick, placing the pigeons in it to serve
