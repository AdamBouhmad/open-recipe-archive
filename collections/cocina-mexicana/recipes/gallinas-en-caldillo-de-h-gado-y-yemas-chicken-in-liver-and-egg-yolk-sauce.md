## Ingredients

- chickens
- salt
- water
- lard
- garlic
- liver
- egg yolks
- cloves
- pepper
- cumin
- saffron
- oil
- vinegar
- olives
- small chilies

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens in salted water
2. Cut the cooked chickens into quarters and fry them
3. Once browned, set aside and fry ground garlic in the lard
4. Add cooked livers and egg yolks ground together with cloves, pepper, cumin, and saffron
5. Season the fried mixture with salt, add oil, vinegar, the fried chicken, and the cooking broth
6. Let it boil until the sauce has reduced to regular consistency
7. Add olives and small chilies before serving
