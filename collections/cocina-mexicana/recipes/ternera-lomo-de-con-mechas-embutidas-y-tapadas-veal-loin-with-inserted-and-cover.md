## Ingredients

- Veal loin, cleaned
- Ham, cut into pieces
- Garlic cloves
- Clove
- Parsley, chopped
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- Water
- Vinegar
- Small whole onions with heads
- Celery branch
- Lard
- Burned lard
- Flour, browned in lard

## Directions

1. Clean the loin and make openings with the knife point and lard them with small pieces of ham, garlic cloves and cloves, then cover the lardons and press with chopped parsley seasoned with salt and pepper powder.
2. Thus prepared, cook in water with salt, sufficient vinegar, whole small onions with heads, a celery branch and lard.
3. After cooking and with little broth, empty the pot into a suitable casserole that can contain everything, with burned lard, removing then the celery branch.
4. Add flour browned in lard and place the casserole over two fires, taking care to turn the loin so it does not burn, and let the broth reduce until it becomes so thick that it can be spread on the loin with a feather when taking it to the table.
