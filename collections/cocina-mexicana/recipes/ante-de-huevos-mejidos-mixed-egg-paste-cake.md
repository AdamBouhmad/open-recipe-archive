## Ingredients

- sugar
- egg yolks
- sponge cake (mamón)
- syrup at medium point
- wine
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- candied citron

## Directions

1. Clarify one pound of sugar and thicken to medium point
2. Cool and add six egg yolks
3. Remove the skin and stir well for good color
4. Heat until thick like atole
5. Set aside and when cold slice sponge cake
6. Soak in syrup at medium point and plenty of wine
7. Alternate layers of egg mixture and sponge cake with egg as the final layer
8. Top with cinnamon, raisins, almonds, pine nuts and candied citron
