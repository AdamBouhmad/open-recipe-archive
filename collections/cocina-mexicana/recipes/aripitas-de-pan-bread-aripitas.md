## Ingredients

- four large water bread tortas
- one pound of lard, not melted
- half pound of ground sugar
- sesame seeds
- dragée

## Directions

1. Grate four large water bread tortas
2. Mix with one pound of lard without melting and half pound of ground sugar
3. Knead everything well
4. Form the aripitas
5. Sprinkle sesame seeds and dragée on top
6. Place on papers greased with lard in a soft oven
