## Ingredients

- 3 pounds rice
- 2 ounces corn, ground before mixing
- 5.5 pounds sugar
- 8 cuartillos of milk, strained
- 1 chicken breast, ground
- almonds (optional, if desired)
- drops of orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Grind 3 pounds of rice, 2 ounces of corn (ground beforehand), and 5.5 pounds of sugar
2. Add the mixture to 8 cuartillos of strained milk
3. Place over fire, stirring constantly
4. Before it reaches the desired point, add 1 ground chicken breast, a little almond if desired, and a few drops of orange blossom water
5. The correct point is determined by dropping a small amount into cold water; it should sink to the bottom forming a small ball
