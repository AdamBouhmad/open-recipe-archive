## Ingredients

- two eggs
- six tablespoons flour
- three tablespoons sugar
- half an ounce green anise
- a little salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Knead two eggs with six tablespoons of flour, three tablespoons of sugar, half an ounce of green anise and a little salt on a very clean surface.
2. Once the dough is firm, divide it into four bands or strips of a third in length and the thickness of a finger.
3. Brush them with butter on a buttered tin sheet, making a small cut lengthwise with a knife along the breads.
4. Place them in a moderately hot oven.
5. When cooked, cut as needed for serving.
