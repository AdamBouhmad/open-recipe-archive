## Ingredients

- veal slices
- ham
- salt
- bay leaves
- oregano
- thyme
- onions
- butter
- water
- carrot slices
- chard
- almonds
- spices
- wine
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Stud veal slices with ham and place in a saucepan with a little salt, bay leaves, oregano, thyme and whole onions
2. Parboil everything together in butter, then add water and carrot slices
3. Have chard cooked separately and squeeze well; grind with almonds and fry in a saucepan with butter and a little chopped onion
4. When the meat is cooked, add the chard sauce and place the dish on a platter
5. Grind spices with wine and sprinkle the meat with this
6. Garnish with chiles and olives
