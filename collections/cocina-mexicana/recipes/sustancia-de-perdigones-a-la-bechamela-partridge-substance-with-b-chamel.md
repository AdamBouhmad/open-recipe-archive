## Ingredients

- 2 roasted partridges
- béchamel sauce

## Directions

1. Take the meat from 2 roasted partridges, removing the skin and sinews.
2. Chop and pound it, adding a tablespoon of béchamel sauce.
3. When ground, thin it with the same sauce, pass through a sieve and heat only for a moment.
4. Serve with fried eggs on top and surrounded by fried bread rounds.
