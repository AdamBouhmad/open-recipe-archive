## Ingredients

- Pears (San Juan, white, milky, brown, or bergamot varieties)
- Water
- Sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Select pears in good season of any variety, but not ripe as they would fall apart when preserved
2. Peel and place in cold water
3. Cook until half done and remove
4. When cold, prick in several places with a needle or pin (pin is better as it is not copper)
5. If bergamot pears, scrape the colored surface with a knife, leaving them white, and rinse
6. As they are pricked, place in sugar syrup and cook until they take on the preserve point
