## Ingredients

- sardines, cleaned and without bones
- flour
- oil
- garlic, finely chopped
- onion, finely chopped
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- salt
- parsley, leaves removed
- flour browned in butter
- clove
- ground pepper
- a little water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean and bone the sardines, then roll them in flour and fry in oil
2. Remove the fried sardines from the oil
3. In the same oil, fry the finely chopped garlic and onion
4. Add the roasted and ground tomatoes, season with salt after frying
5. Add the leafless parsley, flour browned in butter, clove, ground pepper, a little water and vinegar
6. Let it boil, then add the sardines to the sauce
