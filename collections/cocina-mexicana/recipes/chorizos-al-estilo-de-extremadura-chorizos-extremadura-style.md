## Ingredients

- pork meat, finely chopped (excluding loins, bacon, and ham)
- garlic
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop all the pork meat finely, separating out only the loins, bacon and ham
2. Put in a trough or basin, adding necessary garlic, pepper and salt
3. Mix everything very well
4. Let this chopped mixture rest for twenty-four hours
5. After that time, make chorizos of the desired size, being careful to press them as tightly as possible
6. Pierce them with a needle after making, with many piercings so the air comes out, as this is what damages them
7. Hang and smoke them in the same manner as morcillas (blood sausages)
