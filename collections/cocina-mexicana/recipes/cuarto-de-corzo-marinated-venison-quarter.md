## Ingredients

- venison quarter (with loin and haunch prepared and studded with fine ham strips)
- good red wine (three or four bottles)
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaves (three or four)
- cloves (six)
- thyme sprigs (six or seven)
- onions (five, sliced)
- parsley (a handful)
- chives (a handful, whole)
- pepper sauce

## Directions

1. After preparing the loin and haunch of the venison, stud with fine strips of ham
2. Place in a deep baking dish or appropriate pot
3. Add the wine, salt, pepper, bay leaves, cloves, thyme sprigs, sliced onions, parsley, and whole chives
4. Let the venison marinate in this mixture for forty-eight hours
5. When ready to cook, remove from the marinade and place on the spit
6. Roast for five quarters of an hour until cooked
7. At the moment of serving, dress and bring to the table with pepper sauce
