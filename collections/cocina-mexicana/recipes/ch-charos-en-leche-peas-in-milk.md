## Ingredients

- peas
- water
- tequesquito
- butter
- flour
- milk
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the peas in boiling water with tequesquito
2. Reduce the water by boiling
3. Melt butter and dissolve flour in milk and add to the peas
4. Then add the milk with flour and salt
