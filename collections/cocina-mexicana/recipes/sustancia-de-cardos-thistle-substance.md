## Ingredients

- Cooked thistles
- Large ruffled sauce
- Concentrated broth
- Gelatin
- Heavy cream

## Directions

1. Put cooked thistles cut into small pieces in a saucepan with three large spoonfuls of ruffled sauce and six spoonfuls of concentrated broth
2. Let everything reduce until the thistles are reduced to a paste
3. Pass through a sieve, making it as thick as possible
4. If too thick, mix in heavy cream to loosen it
5. Add a piece of gelatin the size of a walnut without boiling the paste
6. Keep hot in a bain-marie
