## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Chorizos
- Sliced pork loin
- Strips of ham
- Lard
- Garlic, finely chopped
- Onion, finely chopped
- Capers
- Roasted and ground tomatoes
- Clove
- Pepper
- Salt
- Chicken broth
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Olives
- Chiles or cured tornachiles

## Directions

1. Cook the chicken together with chorizos, sliced pork loin, and strips of ham
2. Quarter the chicken and fry it in lard, then remove it from the lard
3. In the same lard, fry finely chopped garlic and onion
4. Add ground capers and roasted tomatoes
5. Season the mixture with clove, pepper, and salt
6. Put in the cooked chorizos, ham, and pork loin, along with the fried chicken pieces and its broth
7. Add a little vinegar
8. Let everything simmer until the broth is somewhat thick
9. When removing from the heat, add oil and garnish with olives, chiles, or cured tornachiles
