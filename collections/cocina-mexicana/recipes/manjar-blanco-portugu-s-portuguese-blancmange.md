## Ingredients

- half a pound of rice
- eight cuartillos of milk
- sugar to taste
- one chicken breast, ground

## Directions

1. Soak half a pound of rice for twenty-four hours
2. Cook it without salt, wash it, grind it and dissolve it in a little milk
3. Strain it and regrind the strainings until no grain remains
4. Add it then to eight cuartillos of milk sweetened to taste, with one ground chicken breast disintegrated with a little more milk
5. Place over heat and let it reach the point of separating from the pan
