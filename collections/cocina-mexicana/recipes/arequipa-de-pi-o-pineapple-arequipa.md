## Ingredients

- 7 cuartillos milk
- 2 pounds sugar
- 4 ounces almonds
- 1 ounce starch
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 pineapple, ground and strained

## Directions

1. Add two pounds of sugar, four ounces of almonds, and one ounce of starch to seven cuartillos of milk.
2. Once it has thickened, add another pound of sugar and one ground pineapple strained through cloth.
3. Return to boil until the point is reached where the bottom of the pan is visible when stirred.
4. Pour into molds.
