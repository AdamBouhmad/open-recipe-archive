## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Water
- Vinegar
- Whole onions
- All spices
- Garlic
- Lard
- Oregano
- Toasted bread
- Roasted xitomates
- Oil

## Directions

1. Place the chickens in water with vinegar, whole onions, and all spices the night before they are to be served
2. The following day put to cook, covering the pot very well
3. Fry garlic in lard, and after frying, remove it and add a handful of oregano, toasted bread, and roasted xitomates, all ground, so it also fries
4. Pour the pot over all this, being careful to add oil when bringing to the table
