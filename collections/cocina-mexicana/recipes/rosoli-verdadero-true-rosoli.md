## Ingredients

- Twenty-four hours of rose leaves of Castile
- Sixteen quarters of common water
- Five ounces of jasmine flowers
- Four ounces of orange blossoms
- Half ounce of ground cinnamon
- Half drachm of ground cloves
- Four pounds of sugar
- Eight quarters of spirit of wine

## Directions

1. Let macerate the rose leaves for twenty-four hours in common water at atmospheric temperature
2. Add jasmine flowers, orange blossoms, ground cinnamon, and ground cloves
3. Distill everything to obtain eight quarters of liquor
4. Dissolve four pounds of sugar in the liquor
5. Add eight quarters of spirit of wine
6. Give it a crimson color
7. Pass through a strainer
8. Bottle
