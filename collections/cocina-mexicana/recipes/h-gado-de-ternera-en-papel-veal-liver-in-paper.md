## Ingredients

- veal liver
- oil
- pepper
- salt
- fine herbs
- strong paper
- lard
- bacon

## Directions

1. Cut into strips like a finger's width and place in marinade composed of oil, pepper, salt and fine herbs
2. Take strong paper and make small boxes, which are spread with lard, place inside a thin strip of bacon and fine herbs, and on top the slice of liver, and then fine herbs and another strip
3. Close the box and place on the grill, letting it roast until the paper becomes very golden, and then serve immediately
