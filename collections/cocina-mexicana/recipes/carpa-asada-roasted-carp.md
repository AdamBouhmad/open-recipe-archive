## Ingredients

- Carp
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth or stock
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Scale and clean the carp
2. Salt lightly
3. Place to roast on the spit
4. Season with pepper
5. As it roasts, baste frequently with broth
6. Add half a lemon to the pan sauce
7. Arrange the carp on a platter
8. Pour the hot sauce over it
