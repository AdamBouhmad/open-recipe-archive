## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of sugar
- 1 grated coconut
- 8 cuartillos of milk
- sticks of cinnamon
- 16 egg yolks and 6 egg whites
- 2 crumbled sponge cakes
- high-point syrup
- raisins, almonds, and pine nuts

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at mirror point with sugar
2. Mix in the grated coconut and boil until the bottom of the pan is exposed when stirred
3. Cook the milk separately with only cinnamon sticks until it is as thick as for custard
4. Mix the milk with the coconut preparation and place over heat until it reaches the point of cajeta
5. Remove from heat and add the beaten egg yolks and whites that have been beaten separately and well risen
6. Add crumbled sponge cakes
7. Bake the cake in the oven
8. When cooked, brush with high-point syrup and stud with raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
