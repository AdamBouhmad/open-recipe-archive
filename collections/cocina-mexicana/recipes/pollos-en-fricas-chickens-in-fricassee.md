## Ingredients

- onion
- parsley
- chickens
- broth
- cooked ham
- chorizos
- cinnamon
- clove
- saffron
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- lemon juice
- olives
- chiles

## Directions

1. Fry finely chopped onion, and separately also fry parsley, letting it rise in color.
2. Mix one and the other and add the chickens in quarters and the broth in which they were cooked, with slices of cooked ham, chorizos, ground cinnamon, clove and saffron and egg yolks beaten with vinegar or lemon juice.
3. Let everything boil and serve with olives and chiles.
