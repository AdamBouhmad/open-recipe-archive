## Ingredients

- one arroba of well clarified sugar
- whole quinces with skin

## Directions

1. Clarify the sugar well and strain
2. Place over fire to reach a very high point, so that it makes threads with the finger
3. Remove the pot and let it rest a little
4. Cook whole quinces with skin
5. Strain through cloth, weigh ten pounds and add to syrup
6. Once the mass is well broken down in the syrup, return the pot to the fire and let it boil a little so it does not reach too high a point
7. Remove, beat a little and put in small boxes
