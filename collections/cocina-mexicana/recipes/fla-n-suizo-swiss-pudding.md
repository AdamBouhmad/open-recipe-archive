## Ingredients

- pastry crust
- two cuartillos of cream
- four ounces of fine butter
- potato starch flour
- salt
- pepper
- half a pound of melted butter
- grated Gruyère cheese
- Parma cheese
- Neufchâtel cheese
- egg yolks
- egg whites
- thick paper
- butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Prepare the pastry crust as in the previous article
2. Boil two cuartillos of cream with four ounces of fine butter
3. Make a paste with potato starch flour instead of common flour
4. Knead this paste in a bowl with salt and pepper, half a pound of melted butter and grated Gruyère cheese, an equal amount of Parma and Neufchâtel cheese
5. Mix everything with raw egg yolks, making the paste more consistent than cake batter
6. Beat half of the egg whites whose yolks were used and fold gently into the mass
7. Pour immediately into the prepared pastry crust
8. Cover with thick buttered paper tied with string
9. Bake in a moderately hot oven
10. When cooked, arrange on a plate, remove the paper and serve
