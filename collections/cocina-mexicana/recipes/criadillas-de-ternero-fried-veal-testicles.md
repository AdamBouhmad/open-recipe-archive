## Ingredients

- veal testicles
- salt
- eggs
- lemon juice
- pepper
- raw onion
- breadcrumbs
- oil or lard

## Directions

1. Cook the testicles with their natural coverings and plenty of salt.
2. Remove the outer layers and slice the meat.
3. Fry with beaten egg or without it and serve with lemon juice, salt, pepper, and slices of raw onion.
4. Alternatively, cut into strips of one inch, roll in breadcrumbs, and fry in oil or lard to use as garnish for other dishes.
