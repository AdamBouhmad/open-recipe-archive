## Ingredients

- peas
- tequesquite
- lard
- garlic
- flour
- broth
- onion heads
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- whole chilies
- cooked pork ribs
- loin slices
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the peas with a little tequesquite.
2. Place a pot on the fire with lard and fry chopped garlic cloves in it.
3. When golden, remove them and fry a little flour in the same lard, stirring until well browned.
4. Add a little broth from the pot and two or three onion heads cut in quarters.
5. Grind a little clove, pepper, and cinnamon, and add to the broth.
6. Add the cooked peas and whole chilies, along with cooked pork ribs and loin slices.
7. Season with fine salt, and when it has thickened well, remove from heat.
