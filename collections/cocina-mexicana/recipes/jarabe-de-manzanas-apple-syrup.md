## Ingredients

- sufficient quantity of sour apples to obtain two cuartillos of juice
- two pounds of sugar
- necessary water

## Directions

1. Peel sour apples in sufficient quantity to obtain two cuartillos of juice
2. Remove the seeds, cut into pieces more or less large, and place in a matraz with two pounds of sugar and sufficient water to keep them adequately moist
