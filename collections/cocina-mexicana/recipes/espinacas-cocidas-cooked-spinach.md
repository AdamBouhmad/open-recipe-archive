## Ingredients

- spinach
- salt
- lemon juice or oil and vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach well
2. Cook in salted water
3. When cooked, let cool and drain
4. Chop and when serving, add either lemon juice or oil and vinegar, and serve
