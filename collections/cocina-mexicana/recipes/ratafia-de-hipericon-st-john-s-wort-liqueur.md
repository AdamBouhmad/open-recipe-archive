## Ingredients

- sixteen quartillos of common aguardiente
- one pound of St. John's Wort flower
- two pounds of very white sugar

## Directions

1. Keep in infusion for thirty to forty days in the warmth of the atmosphere in sixteen quartillos of common aguardiente one pound of St. John's Wort flower
2. Pass everything through a silk sieve
3. Add two pounds of very white sugar and dissolve
4. Filter the liquor through the sleeve and store in well-sealed bottles
