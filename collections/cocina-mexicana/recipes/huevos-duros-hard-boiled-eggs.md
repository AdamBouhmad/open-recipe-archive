## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cook with shell and when hard remove and discard shell
2. Cut into slices and place on a plate
3. Serve with salt, oil, vinegar and pepper
4. These are usually served this way to garnish raw salads, plates of asparagus, cauliflower, etc.
