## Ingredients

- Chickpeas
- Water with tequesquite
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Cook the chickpeas with a little tequesquite-treated water.
2. Once cooked, wash them in cold water, rubbing them until the skins are removed.
3. Drain them and grind them well.
4. Put a pot with a little water on the fire and add these ground chickpeas.
5. Season the broth with sea salt.
6. When it has thickened properly, serve.
