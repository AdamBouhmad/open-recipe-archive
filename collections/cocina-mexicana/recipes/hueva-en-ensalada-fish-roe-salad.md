## Ingredients

- Fresh roe
- Capers
- Olives
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Small squashes
- Pickled chiles
- Onion
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Place fresh roe in a cloth napkin and tie loosely like a bag
2. Place in water the night before
3. The next day, after tying as described, place the napkin in a pot and let the water boil
4. Once the roe is cooked, remove it and take off all the skin
5. Break apart the roe in a plate with a spoon
6. Chop capers, olives, parsley, oregano, small squashes, pickled chiles, and onion
7. Mix all of this with oil and vinegar and combine with the roe
