## Ingredients

- peas
- almonds with shell
- lard
- bread slices
- small onions

## Directions

1. Cook the peas.
2. Fry some shelled almonds until golden in lard.
3. Also fry some bread slices in the same lard until golden, remove both, and grind well.
4. Fry some small onions.
