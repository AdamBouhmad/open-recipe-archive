## Ingredients

- A slice of ham
- 1/4 liter white wine
- Meat broth
- A bouquet of mixed herbs
- Sprigs of cloves
- Game or roasted meat (rabbits, leveret, partridges, woodcock, or other roasted meats)
- Shallot or garlic, chopped
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Fried toast

## Directions

1. Place a slice of ham in a pot and let it brown and stick
2. Moisten with a quarter liter of white wine and a little meat broth, adding a mixed bouquet of herbs and sprigs of cloves
3. Let it simmer for an hour
4. Pass this sauce through a sieve
5. Cut up the rabbits, leverets, partridges, woodcock, or whatever other roasted meats are to be used
6. Add these to the sauce with chopped shallot or garlic, salt, and black pepper
7. Heat everything without letting it boil and serve with fried toast
