## Ingredients

- folded dough
- pastry cream
- chocolate zest

## Directions

1. Between two leaves of the dough mentioned in the previous articles, place some pastry cream mixed with chocolate zest in sufficient quantity to give it taste
2. Fry the pastry and glaze like the previous ones
