## Ingredients

- ducks
- peas
- broth
- pan essence
- spinach

## Directions

1. Prepare large ducks, tucking the legs forward
2. Cook over coals
3. Cook peas separately in broth, pass through a sieve and add pan essence
4. If the sauce is not sufficiently green, mix in some cooked and ground spinach
5. Serve the duck over the sauce.
