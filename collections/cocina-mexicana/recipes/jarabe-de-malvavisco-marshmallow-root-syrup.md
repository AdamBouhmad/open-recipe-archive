## Ingredients

- half a pound of marshmallow root
- three quartillos of water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Wash half a pound of marshmallow root very well
2. Cut it into small pieces
3. Put it on the fire in three quartillos of water
4. When it has boiled well and the water is sticky, pass it through a sieve to separate the decoction
5. Put this with sugar on the fire, and once the mixture is clarified, let it boil until it reaches the glaze point
6. Filter it through a cloth or strain it through a wet cotton cloth placed in a frame
7. After it cools, bottle it
