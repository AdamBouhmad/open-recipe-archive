## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet almonds
- water
- orange blossom water
- 1.5 pounds powdered sugar
- apricot jam or other preserves

## Directions

1. Blanch 1 pound of sweet almonds, placing them in cold water as they are blanched
2. After draining them, grind them in a marble mortar, rolling them little by little with common water and a little orange blossom water
3. When well ground, put them in a pan with 1.5 pounds of powdered sugar and put them to dry over a small heat with a gentle fire
4. When applying your finger to the paste nothing sticks, remove from the heat and let it cool on a plate dusted with sugar
5. When cold, cut into small pieces, which are rolled and rounded, leaving them the thickness of a little finger, giving them all if possible the same volume
6. Then cut them to form with them a ring in the shape of a rosette
7. Line them on an iron grill, which will have been placed in a basin
8. Make a base or ring with the same paste, spread over it a light layer of apricot jam or other preserves, and cover the base or ring with the same marzipan paste, cut in the desired shape
9. Place on the grill, glaze with very white sugar and let it drain
10. Thread the marzipans on paper sheets and put them in the oven, somewhat lively so that they take color
