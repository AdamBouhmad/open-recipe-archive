## Ingredients

- ripe pomegranates
- sufficient quantity of common water
- two pounds of white sugar, crushed

## Directions

1. Select ripe pomegranates and separate the grains
2. Crush the grains in a marble mortar
3. Add sufficient quantity of common water and let boil for several minutes in a porcelain or silver vessel
4. Strain through cloth or sieve, let rest and decant the liquid
5. Dissolve over fire two pounds of crushed white sugar in seventeen ounces of pomegranate juice prepared as described
6. Allow it to take on syrup consistency, skimming continuously
7. Once cool, store in bottles for use
