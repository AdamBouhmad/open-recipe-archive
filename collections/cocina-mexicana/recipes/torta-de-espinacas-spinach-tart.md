## Ingredients

- spinaches
- green lemon peel
- sugar as needed
- fresh butter
- salt
- puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach and wash thoroughly in abundant water
2. Scald the spinach in a pot with water on the stovetop
3. Remove immediately and drain
4. Squeeze and press the spinach between the hands
5. Pound in a mortar with green lemon peel, necessary sugar, a piece of fresh butter and a little salt
6. When well pounded, line a tart pan with puff pastry dough
7. Spread the spinach mixture as evenly as possible on top
8. Make a cord border with the same dough around the edges
9. Bake in the oven
10. When cooked, dust with scraped sugar
11. Pass a shovel made hot over the top so that it browns
12. Serve immediately
