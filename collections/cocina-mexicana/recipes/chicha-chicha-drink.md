## Ingredients

- One pineapple
- Six quartillos of water
- Tamarinds
- Two cloves
- One thick cinnamon stick
- Twelve peppercorns
- Pineapple skins, crushed

## Directions

1. Grind a pineapple
2. Put it in a pot with six quartillos of water, tamarinds, cloves, cinnamon stick, peppercorns, and crushed pineapple skins
3. Cover the pot well
4. Place in the sun and night air for three days
