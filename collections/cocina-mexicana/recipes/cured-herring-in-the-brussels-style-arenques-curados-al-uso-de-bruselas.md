## Ingredients

- Eight to ten cured herrings, divided in halves
- Butter
- Fine herbs
- Mushrooms
- Onions and garlic, finely chopped
- Pepper
- Oil
- Breadcrumbs
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Form a box from thick paper, buttered on top and bottom
2. Lay eight or ten herrings lengthwise, divided in two halves, having removed their heads, tails, bones and skin
3. Add butter kneaded with fine herbs between the two halves, plenty of mushrooms, onions and garlic, all finely chopped, pepper and a little oil
4. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs
5. Place them on the grill over a gentle fire so the paper does not burn
6. Squeeze lemon over them and serve in the same box
