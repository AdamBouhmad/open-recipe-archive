## Ingredients

- 1 pound mulato chile
- 3 wide chiles
- 2 chilpocles
- Lard
- Tomato as needed, cooked
- Salt to taste

## Directions

1. Break up and brown all the chiles in lard
2. Add the tomato, which should be cooked
3. Grind the chile and tomato together very well
4. Cut the turkey into pieces and roll it in the cazuela with the chile, everything raw
5. Put on the fire with necessary salt
6. Give the broth a few boils separately and then incorporate it with the mole and the broth in which it cooked
7. Grind clove, cinnamon and pepper, a little cilantro, one roasted garlic head, a handful of toasted sesame and a tortilla browned in lard
8. Add everything to the mole when it is almost cooked, and when it is done remove from the fire
