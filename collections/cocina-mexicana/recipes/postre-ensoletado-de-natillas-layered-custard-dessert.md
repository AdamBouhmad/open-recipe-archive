## Ingredients

- custard (natillas)
- milk
- sifted sugar
- cinnamon powder
- sponge biscuits (soletas)

## Directions

1. Beat the custard with milk, sifted sugar and cinnamon powder
2. Place in alternating layers with sponge biscuits
