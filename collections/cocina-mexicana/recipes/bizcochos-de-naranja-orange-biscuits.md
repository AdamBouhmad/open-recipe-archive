## Ingredients

- six pounds of flour
- one and a half pounds of lard
- three egg yolks
- one small cup of orange juice
- three fingers' pinch of salt, ground
- half a pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the flour, salt, and sugar in a bowl
2. Make a hole in the center of the mixture and add the lard, yolks, and orange juice
3. Mix with the hands and continue stirring with the flour
4. If it sticks, continue adding flour until forming a ball
5. Place flour on the table, give the dough the shape of a sausage and cut small rings from it
6. Wrap them in papers and put them in the oven
7. When removed, sprinkle with powdered sugar and cinnamon
