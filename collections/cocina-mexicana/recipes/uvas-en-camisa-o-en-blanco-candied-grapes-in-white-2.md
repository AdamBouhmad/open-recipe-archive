## Ingredients

- grapes in small clusters
- egg whites
- fine sugar

## Directions

1. Cut the grapes into small clusters and dip them in beaten egg whites
2. Roll them in fine sugar and blow on them so that only the amount of sugar that has stuck to the egg white remains
3. Thread them on a paper placed over a sieve, or over a wicker hurdle, or over a drying rack, and dry them at a gentle heat
