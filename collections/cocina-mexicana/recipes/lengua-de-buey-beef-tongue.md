## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- water
- onions
- cloves
- thyme
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Salt the beef tongue for twelve to fifteen days at least, turning it from one side to the other daily.
2. Then it is cooked or left to dry in the fireplace.
3. When you wish to cook it, place it in a pot full of water with some onions, two cloves, a little thyme and bay leaf, without any salt or pepper.
4. Cook slowly for six or seven hours, after which let it cool in its own broth and drain.
5. Serve whole as a cold appetizer, and in slices for a supernumerary course.
6. Care must be taken that it cooks gently, so that the broth is not greatly consumed and the tongue does not become too salty.
