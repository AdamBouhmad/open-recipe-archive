## Ingredients

- Eighteen cuartillos of milk
- Two pounds of sugar
- One cinnamon stick
- Twenty-four eggs, beaten as for frying

## Directions

1. Sweeten eighteen cuartillos of milk with two pounds of sugar
2. Strain and add a cinnamon stick
3. Heat until it becomes so thick that when stirred the bottom of the pot is revealed
4. Remove from heat and let cool
5. Mix in twenty-four beaten eggs as for frying
