## Ingredients

- a little yeast
- a cup of salted water
- 1 pound of melted and cold lard
- 3 servings of Málaga wine or other generous wine
- flour as necessary

## Directions

1. Dissolve a little yeast with a cup of salted water
2. Mix with one pound of melted and cold lard and three servings of Málaga wine or other generous wine
3. Beat until all is well incorporated
4. Gradually add flour in the necessary quantity so the dough becomes smooth and can be extended, kneading at the same time
5. Place this dough in a lard-greased bowl and cover for eight to ten hours rest
6. After this time, extend with a rolling pin on a table sprinkled with flour
7. Cut empanadas to the size needed to cover the fish or piece to be placed in it, or cut small portions for small empanadas
8. Fill with minced meat, fish, or recado alone
9. Placing what they are to contain in the center, fold and crimp the edges
10. Shape gracefully and variously, imitating with the dough the fish or piece contained inside, or other animals or flowers
