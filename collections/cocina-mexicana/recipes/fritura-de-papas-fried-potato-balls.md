## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- bread crumb
- pepper
- parsley
- butter
- eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Peel the potatoes and cook them with salt.
2. In a mortar, grind them with a piece of soaked bread crumb.
3. Place this paste in a saucepan with a pinch of pepper, very finely chopped parsley, a little butter and stir in eggs, both white and yolk, so that balls can be formed.
4. Fry the balls in lard.
5. These are served as is or with the following sauce: cook some eggs and beat the yolks with a piece of soaked bread.
6. Fry very finely chopped onion and garlic, add the cooked yolks there and broth from the pot, before it has chile and parsley, add sufficient salt and keep this sauce very hot.
7. At mealtime arrange the balls on a platter and cover them with the sauce.
