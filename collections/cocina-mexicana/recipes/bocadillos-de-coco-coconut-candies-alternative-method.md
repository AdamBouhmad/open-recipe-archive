## Ingredients

- Three pounds of sugar
- Coconut
- Brown husk

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with three pounds of sugar
2. Remove from fire when about to reach the proper point
3. Beat very well until it becomes panochita
4. Add one ground coconut after removing the brown husk
5. Return to fire until it reaches the setting point in water
6. Remove and beat again
7. Place the candies on papers with some dragée on top
