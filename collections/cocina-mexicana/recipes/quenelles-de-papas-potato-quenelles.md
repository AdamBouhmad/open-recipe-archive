## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- minced onion
- egg yolks
- egg whites

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in well-lit coals
2. Clean them and pound or mix with a piece of butter, salt, pepper, minced onion and egg yolks in proportion to the quantity of potatoes
3. Beat egg whites until they harden and mix with the rest
4. For the rest follow the same procedures as for poultry quenelles
