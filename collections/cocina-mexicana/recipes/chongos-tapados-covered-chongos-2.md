## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- four portions of butter
- sixteen eggs
- four reales of pan de la vida
- lard and butter for frying
- ground cinnamon
- sliced cheese without salt
- sugar and cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Make a half-point syrup from four pounds of sugar
2. Add four portions of butter and return to heat to increase the point, then cool
3. Beat sixteen eggs and add them to the syrup, beating it like turrón
4. Slice four reales of pan de la vida and fry in lard and butter
5. Place in a baking dish and begin layering with syrup and eggs, plenty of cinnamon, and sliced unsalted cheese
6. Continue layering until the final layer is well soaked with syrup
7. Before starting, butter the baking dish
8. Sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top
9. Bake over two slow fires
10. Insert a drinking straw to check if it is cooked and set
