## Ingredients

- chayóte (chayote)
- agua de piña (pineapple water)
- almendra martajada (pounded almond)
- azúcar machacada (crushed sugar)

## Directions

1. Grind cooked chayote and mix with pineapple water and pounded almonds, placing in crushed sugar pound for pound
2. Put over heat and give it the paste point
