## Ingredients

- 1 pound fine flour
- 1/2 pound lard
- 8 egg yolks
- almond paste for filling
- paper
- lard for frying
- high-point syrup
- sugar beads or toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of fine flour with half a pound of lard and eight egg yolks
2. From this dough make small balls the size of large walnuts, with an opening in the middle to fill them with almond paste that will have been prepared beforehand
3. After closing them, wrap each one in paper, and in this way fry them in very hot lard, not removing the paper until after frying
4. Serve them thus, or in high-point syrup, well clarified, with sugar beads or toasted sesame on top
