## Ingredients

- Onion heads
- Bread soaked in lard
- Water
- Fresh oregano
- Small chiles
- Cooked exotes (green beans)
- Clove
- Black pepper
- Fine salt

## Directions

1. Grind some onion heads with a piece of bread soaked in lard
2. Fry the ground mixture and add sufficient water with a little fresh oregano and some small chiles
3. Add the already-cooked exotes
4. Grind a little clove and black pepper and add to the stew
5. Season with necessary fine salt
6. When the sauce has taken on some consistency, remove from heat and serve
