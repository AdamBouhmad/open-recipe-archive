## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- peas
- butter
- onions
- garlic
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- parsley
- cooked egg yolks
- saffron
- cumin
- pepper
- cloves
- vinegar
- cooked and broken onions

## Directions

1. Cook pig's feet until perfectly done, debone and divide into small pieces.
2. Cook peas separately.
3. Once both are ready, fry in a pan with butter, chopped onions and garlic.
4. Fry roasted ground tomatoes, chopped parsley and crumbled cooked egg yolks in the same pan without removing the onion and garlic.
5. Once everything is fried, add the pieces of pig's feet and the broth in which they were cooked.
6. Season with salt and add saffron, cumin, pepper and cloves all ground, a little vinegar and cooked broken onions.
7. Let everything simmer until most of the broth is consumed and the stew is thick.
