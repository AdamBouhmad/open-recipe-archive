## Ingredients

- Thirty Gamboa pears or other buttery pears, almost ripe
- Water
- Eight ounces of sugar
- Juice of three lemons

## Directions

1. Use Gamboa pears or other buttery pears that are almost ripe
2. Peel thirty pears and remove the seeds and skin
3. Put them in water on the fire until they become a paste
4. Sweeten with eight ounces of sugar
5. Pass through a thick sieve
6. Add the juice of three lemons
7. Put in the sorbetera to freeze
