## Ingredients

- Flour
- Water
- Powdered sugar
- One tablespoon cooking oil
- Two tablespoons orange blossom water
- Egg whites
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Dissolve sufficient flour in water with a little powdered sugar, one tablespoon of cooking oil, and two of orange blossom water
2. Beat well
3. At the time of use, mix with a few well-beaten egg whites, making sure it is regularly thick so that after one immersion the fruit is well covered
4. Once covered, fry in very hot lard, bathing it with lard and removing it as soon as it is golden
