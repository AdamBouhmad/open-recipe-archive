## Ingredients

- half cuartillo milk
- a little more than a quarter pound starch
- aniseed
- salt
- 12 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Dissolve a little more than a quarter pound starch in half cuartillo milk
2. Strain and add a little aniseed and salt
3. Cook until it pulls away from the pan and forms a ball
4. Set aside to cool
5. When cold, add 12 egg yolks and mix well like washing so no lumps remain, adding more eggs if needed
6. Put in a piping bag and pipe in spirals
7. Finish with syrup
