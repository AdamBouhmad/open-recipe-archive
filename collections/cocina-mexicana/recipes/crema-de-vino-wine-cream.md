## Ingredients

- 8 egg yolks
- powdered sugar (sufficient quantity)
- 1 bottle Fuentidueña wine or another sweetened or aromatic wine

## Directions

1. Dissolve 8 egg yolks in sufficient powdered sugar
2. Gradually add, stirring constantly, 1 bottle of Fuentidueña wine or any other sweetened or aromatic wine
3. Cook in a bain-marie or in a pan with boiling water, as explained various times, without ceasing to stir, until the cream reaches the proper point and is well cooked
