## Ingredients

- bobo
- huevos
- alcaparras
- aceitunas
- jamón
- chorizos
- papeles
- aceite
- sal
- pimienta

## Directions

1. Clean and dry the bobo.
2. Stuff with eggs, capers and olives, and if in season add chopped ham and sausage.
3. Oil some papers and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
4. Wrap the bobo in the papers.
5. Place on a grill and turn so they brown evenly.
