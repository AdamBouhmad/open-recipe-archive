## Ingredients

- venison ribs
- vinegar
- salt
- onion
- pepper
- water
- pork meat
- lamb meat
- garlic
- tomato
- parsley
- yerba buena (mint)
- bread crumb
- broth
- eggs
- clove
- capers
- caper berries
- candied citron
- lard
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Cut the ribs and pound them on the thick part with the back of a machete or mortar
2. Place them in vinegar with salt
3. Let them drain and then parboil them with onion, pepper, water and salt
4. Prepare a picadillo (minced filling) with chopped pork and lamb meat, garlic, onions, raw tomato, parsley, yerba buena, bread crumb soaked in broth, raw eggs, clove, pepper, salt, capers, caper berries and candied citron
5. Fill the ribs with this mixture
6. Place a baking dish or cazuela prepared in advance, lined with lard and a bed of garlic, pepper, onions, parsley and bay leaves
7. Arrange the filled ribs alternating with another layer of the seasoning until the cazuela is full
8. Place over two fires and cook dry while the ribs brown
9. Once browned, add vinegar and broth
10. Continue cooking with more heat from above than below
