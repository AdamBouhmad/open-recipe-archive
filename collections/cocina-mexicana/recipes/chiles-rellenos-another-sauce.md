## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Garlic cloves
- Tomatoes, roasted
- Capers
- Salt
- Saffron
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Pepper
- Bread, toasted
- Lard
- Water from cooked meat for the picadillo
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Olives
- Small cured chiles

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles by roasting and removing seeds and veins
2. Fry halved garlic cloves in lard until golden and remove
3. Grind the garlic together with roasted tomatoes and capers
4. Return the ground mixture to the frying pan and let it simmer slightly
5. Season with salt, saffron, cinnamon, clove, pepper and toasted bread, all ground
6. Add the water in which the meat for the picadillo was cooked, some oil and vinegar
7. Let the sauce boil
8. Place the stuffed and fried chiles in it and let it thicken appropriately
9. Just before serving, add olives and small cured chiles without letting the sauce boil again to avoid off-flavors
