## Ingredients

- Good oil
- Garlic clove
- Bay leaf
- Clove of spice
- One or two anchovies
- Shallot
- Parsley
- Truffles
- Salt
- Pepper powder
- One glass of dry white wine or red wine
- Spanish sauce of substance or stock
- Bread crust
- Butter
- Fried toasts in butter

## Directions

1. Place oil, whole garlic, bay leaf and whole clove in a saucepan with one or two finely chopped anchovies, shallot and parsley
2. Add peeled, washed truffles cut into slices, macerate or render over heat for two minutes
3. Season with salt, a little pepper powder and a glass of dry white or red wine
4. Let cook until the wine is almost entirely consumed
5. Moisten with a little Spanish sauce of substance or stock
6. Let boil two minutes over gentle heat
7. Place the stew on a bread crust spread with butter and dried in the oven or over the grill
8. Remove the garlic
9. The plate can be bordered with toasts of bread fried in butter
10. If moistening the truffles with substance or stock, first add a little flour to thicken the sauce
