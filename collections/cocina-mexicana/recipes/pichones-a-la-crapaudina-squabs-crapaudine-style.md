## Ingredients

- fat squabs
- oil
- coarse pepper
- parsley
- onions
- mushrooms
- bread crumbs
- onion
- verjuice
- broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Choose the fattest squabs and tuck the legs underneath
2. If large, divide in half; if small, leave whole and open along the back, flattening them without breaking the bones too much
3. Marinate in good oil with coarse pepper, parsley, onions and mushrooms, all chopped
4. Ensure they absorb as much of the marinade as possible
5. Roll in bread crumbs
6. Place on the grill and use the rest of the marinade to baste them until they brown perfectly
7. For the sauce: place a chopped onion in a mortar with verjuice; pound both, trying to extract as much juice as possible
8. Mix the juice with broth, salt and coarse pepper
9. Heat and pour on the plate under the squabs
