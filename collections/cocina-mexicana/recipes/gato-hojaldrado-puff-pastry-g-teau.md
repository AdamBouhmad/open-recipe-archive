## Ingredients

- flour
- water
- salt
- butter

## Directions

1. Moisten about a pound of flour with water, add salt, and do not put butter, making the dough soft
2. Let rest for half an hour and then extend with the rolling pin, so that it is a finger thick
3. Then spread fresh butter on it evenly, fold in two, and press with the rolling pin
4. Incorporate the butter and repeat the same operation four or five times
5. Then make the gâteau, gild it, and cook
