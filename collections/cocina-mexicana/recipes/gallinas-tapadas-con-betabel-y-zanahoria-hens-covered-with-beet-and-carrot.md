## Ingredients

- Beets
- Carrots
- Large onions
- Hens
- Chorizo (large)
- Ham
- Capers
- Olives
- Tomato

## Directions

1. Cook beets, carrots, and large onions.
2. Chop the beets, slice the carrots, and shred the onions.
3. Cook the hens with large chorizo and ham.
4. Remove the meat from the hens, removing all large bones.
5. Slice the chorizo and cut the ham into strips.
6. Grease a casserole with lard and layer each ingredient, beginning with the roots and onions, followed by the chorizo and ham.
7. Add capers, olives, and tomato in layers.
