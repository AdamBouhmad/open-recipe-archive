## Ingredients

- One pineapple, peeled
- Two pounds of sugar
- Nine cuartillos of water
- Six large lemons or eight small ones
- A little pepper
- A little clove
- A little cinnamon
- A little fine bread

## Directions

1. Peel the pineapple and grind it with all the other ingredients.
2. Use water from the nine cuartillos to rinse the metate and then crush the pineapple skins.
3. Mix everything in a pot, also adding the peels from two lemons after they have been squeezed there.
4. Leave in the open air overnight.
5. Can be drunk the following day.
