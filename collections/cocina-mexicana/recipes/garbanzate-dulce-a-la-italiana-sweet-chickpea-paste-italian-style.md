## Ingredients

- ten quarters of milk
- three pounds of sugar
- one cup of pine nuts
- four ounces of almonds
- half cup of chickpeas, well hulled and washed

## Directions

1. Mix ten quarters of milk with three pounds of sugar.
2. Strain this mixture.
3. Grind one cup of pine nuts, four ounces of almonds, and half cup of chickpeas well hulled and washed.
4. Mix everything ground with the milk, well blended and put on the fire to reach the point of cajeta (thick syrup).
5. Pour into platters, and the next day place a comal with fire on top so it develops a crust.
