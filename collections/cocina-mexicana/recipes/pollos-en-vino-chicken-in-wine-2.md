## Ingredients

- one well-fattened chicken
- oil
- veal cut into thin slices
- wine
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chicken, sauté it over high heat in a pan with oil, generally with a little veal cut into thin slices
2. Add wine and when the bird is cooked through, remove it from heat
3. Strain the sauce through a sieve, adding a little flour dissolved in butter
