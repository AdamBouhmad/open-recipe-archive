## Ingredients

- Pineapple, large and well-ripened with healthy and leafy crown
- Water
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Select large pineapples in good season with healthy and leafy crown
2. Peel without removing the crown
3. Remove all black spots until completely clean
4. Cook in boiling water
5. If covering in slices, cut after the pineapple is cooked, making the crown unnecessary; if covering whole, place in already clarified and hot syrup
6. Bring to boil in the syrup for some time daily, for four or five days, over gentle heat, until the syrup has penetrated it perfectly
7. After that time, bring the syrup to the proper high point, leaving the pineapple there for the same number of days
8. After those days, remove and place to dry on the drying rack
