## Ingredients

- hen
- pork loin pieces
- longaniza (sausage)
- small chorizos
- lard
- onion
- garlic
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- salt
- saffron
- cloves
- pepper
- parsley
- carlon wine
- broth from the hen
- beaten eggs
- toasted bread slices
- wine
- raisins
- almonds
- capers
- cooked egg
- olives
- chile peppers

## Directions

1. Cook the hen and cut it into small pieces along with pork loin pieces, longaniza, and small chopped chorizos, all cooked with the hen
2. Fry in lard with chopped onion, garlic, tomatoes, and green chiles
3. When rendered, season with salt, saffron, cloves, pepper, and chopped parsley
4. Add the cooked hen with everything else, a little carlon wine, and broth from the hen
5. Simmer until it dries without burning
6. Remove and let cool
7. When cooled, incorporate with sufficient beaten eggs
8. In a cazuela coated with lard, pour a little beaten egg
9. Arrange a layer of bread slices toasted on the comal and sprinkled with wine
10. Over this place another layer of the hen with everything else, adding raisins, almonds, capers, cooked egg, olives, and chile peppers
11. Place another layer of bread and egg around it and continue layering until finished, with the last layer being bread
12. Sprinkle with wine, cover with more beaten egg, and finish as the previous recipe
