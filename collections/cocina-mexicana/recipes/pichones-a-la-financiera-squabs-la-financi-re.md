## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Truffles or earthen mushrooms
- Fat poultry livers
- Rooster combs
- Rooster kidneys

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the squabs as in the previous recipe
2. Arrange and place in the center of the plate a ragout of truffles or earthen mushrooms (which are the same), mixed with fat livers, rooster combs, and rooster kidneys
