## Ingredients

- twenty egg yolks
- one pound and four ounces of sugar in powder
- two common tablespoons of cinnamon
- one tablespoon of clove, both things in powder
- flour

## Directions

1. Put twenty egg yolks in a pot, beat like chocolate until very well united
2. Add the powdered sugar
3. Add the cinnamon and clove, both in powder
4. Incorporate everything with the yolks and continue beating and adding flour without stopping until making a soft dough
5. Form the mostachones on half sheets of paper and take them to the oven
