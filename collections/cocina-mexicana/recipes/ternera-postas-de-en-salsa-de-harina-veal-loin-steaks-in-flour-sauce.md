## Ingredients

- Veal loin
- Water
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Tomato
- Onion, raw
- Sliced chorizo
- Strips of ham
- Thyme
- Bay leaves
- Marjoram
- Lard
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cut the veal loin into slices
2. Cook in a deep pot with water, salt, black pepper, cloves, cinnamon, tomato and raw onion all ground together, slices of chorizo, strips of ham, thyme, bay leaves, marjoram, and lard
3. When cooked and the small amount of broth is consumed, add flour fried and browned in lard
4. Let everything season over two fires so that the steaks are left with a fried and thick sauce
