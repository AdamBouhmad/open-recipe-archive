## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Green chile
- Onions
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Avocado
- Oil
- Oregano

## Directions

1. Roast the tomatoes, peel them, and remove the seeds
2. Pound them well by hand
3. Mix with finely chopped green chile and onions
4. Season with salt and pepper
5. Add necessary vinegar
6. Add finely chopped pounded avocado
7. Drizzle with a little oil and dust with oregano powder
