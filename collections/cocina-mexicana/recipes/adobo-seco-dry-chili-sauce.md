## Ingredients

- ancho chile
- garlic
- cumin
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- pork meat
- lard
- fried plantain

## Directions

1. Take ancho chile, devein and soak in water to reduce its heat
2. Grind with garlic and cumin and add vinegar to help grind on the metate
3. Once well ground, add water and salt
4. Place pork meat in a pot and cover with the chile mixture
5. It is good to do this in the afternoon so the meat stays in infusion all night
6. In the morning, put it to cook
7. If it has fat, add little lard; add more if it does not have fat
8. Nothing should be added to thicken it as it thickens by itself
9. Adjust the amount of chile so the adobo does not lack broth; if it does, add a little more vinegar
10. Garnish with fried plantain when serving
