## Ingredients

- Fifty egg yolks
- Four ounces of sifted sugar
- One quarter ounce of white toquesquite powder
- Half a quarter of Tequila mescal or Spanish brandy
- Flour as needed

## Directions

1. Beat fifty egg yolks until they become stiff and mix with four ounces of sifted sugar, one quarter ounce of white toquesquite powder, and half a quarter of Tequila mescal or Spanish brandy, incorporating everything with the greatest lightness
2. Gradually add the flour that the eggs can absorb so that the dough remains smooth but not sticky
3. Follow the same procedures as in the previous articles
