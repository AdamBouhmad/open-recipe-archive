## Ingredients

- eight ounces sugar
- one and a half quartillos of water
- almonds from the previous recipe, prepared the same way

## Directions

1. Dissolve eight ounces of sugar in one and a half quartillos of water and add the same quantity of almonds from the previous article, prepared the same way
2. After letting them candy, set them aside
