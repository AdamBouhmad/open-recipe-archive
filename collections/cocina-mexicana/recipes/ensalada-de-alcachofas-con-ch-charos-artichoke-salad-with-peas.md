## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Oil
- Garlic cloves, peeled

## Directions

1. Cook the artichokes as described previously.
2. Remove the center leaves that form the entire spiny part.
3. Place a pan over the fire with oil and fry a large quantity of peeled garlic cloves until golden, then remove and grind them.
