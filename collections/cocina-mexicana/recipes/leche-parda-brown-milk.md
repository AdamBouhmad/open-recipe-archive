## Ingredients

- four quarts of milk
- eight egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- one lemon peel
- half an ounce of almonds with skin
- a little starch
- one ounce of ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook four quarts of milk
2. Add eight egg yolks and sugar to taste, then strain and return to heat
3. Add a lemon peel and when it reaches medium point, toast half an ounce of almonds and grind with skin and a little starch
4. When it is about to reach the proper consistency of manjar blanco, carefully add ground cinnamon powder so that no lumps form
