## Ingredients

- beans
- onion
- red chile powder
- squab meat
- chicken breast
- lard
- Jerez wine

## Directions

1. After cooked wash beans and add a small amount of fried onion, ground red chile, some pieces of squab and chicken breast
2. Should be well loaded with lard, then add a little Jerez wine
