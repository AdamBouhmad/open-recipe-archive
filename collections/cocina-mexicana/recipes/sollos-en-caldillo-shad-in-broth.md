## Ingredients

- Shad fish
- White, brown, or simple broth

## Directions

1. Remove the roe with the handle of a knife or make a sufficient opening to clean or gut it through there
2. Cut the fins and tail as cleanly as possible
3. Tie the head with thread so it does not separate
4. Cook in white, brown, or simple broth
5. Serve on a napkin garnished with parsley
