## Ingredients

- Hen
- Water
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Lard
- Flour
- Garlic
- Tomato
- Parsley (chopped)
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Cumin (ground)
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the hen and divide it into small portions.
2. Cook in water with a little salt and a dash of vinegar.
3. Brown a little flour in lard.
4. In the same lard, fry chopped garlic, tomato, and parsley, as well as ground pepper, cloves, and cumin.
5. Add the hen with its broth.
6. Season with salt and a little sugar.
7. Let the broth reach the proper consistency.
