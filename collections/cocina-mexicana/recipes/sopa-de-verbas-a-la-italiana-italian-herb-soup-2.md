## Ingredients

- sorrel
- spinach
- lettuce
- chard
- chervil
- butter
- ham or jowl
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- flour
- water or broth
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Place sorrel, spinach, lettuce, chard, chervil, butter, ham or jowl, salt, pepper and spices in a pot
2. Let everything cook gently
3. Add a little flour and moisten with water or broth
4. Beat egg yolks and mix with the herbs
5. Heat everything without boiling
6. Pour this mixture over bread soup
