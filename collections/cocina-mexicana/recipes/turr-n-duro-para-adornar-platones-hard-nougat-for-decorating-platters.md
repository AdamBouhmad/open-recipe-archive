## Ingredients

- half pound of sugar
- blanched and split almonds
- oil

## Directions

1. Toast half a pound of sugar and add blanched and split almonds with no liquid
2. Mix by placing it on a walnut board rubbed with oil and passing over it a stick also rubbed with oil
3. Once spread out and mixed, cut into pieces or strips
4. Use them to make columns, arches, trophies, or any decorations necessary to form a bouquet, as when dried it acquires incredible hardness
