## Ingredients

- Beef leg
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Shallots
- Spanish sauce (see page 765)

## Directions

1. Place the leg in a large bowl or earthenware vessel
2. Pour over it three or four quarts of good vinegar, salt, pepper, thyme, bay leaf and shallots
3. Let marinate thus for three or four days, turning it daily
4. Lard the entire surface opposite to the sweetbread or fat
5. Wrap in papers and place on the spit
6. Let it cook for four hours
7. Dress and serve roasted this way, on Spanish sauce
