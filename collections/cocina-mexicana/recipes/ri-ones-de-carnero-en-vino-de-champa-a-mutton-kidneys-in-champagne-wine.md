## Ingredients

- twelve mutton kidneys
- butter the size of an egg
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley, chopped
- mushrooms
- flour
- Champagne wine
- Spanish sauce, thick
- fine butter
- lemon juice
- fried croutons

## Directions

1. Remove the skins from twelve kidneys and cut them into slices
2. Place in a pan a piece of butter the size of an egg with the kidneys seasoned with salt, pepper, nutmeg, chopped parsley, and mushrooms
3. Fry them by sautéing over high heat, and when fried, add one tablespoon of flour and a glass of Champagne wine that has been boiled with two tablespoons of thick Spanish sauce
4. Stir over the fire without letting them boil, and at serving time add about a walnut of fine butter and lemon juice
5. Serve surrounded with fried croutons
