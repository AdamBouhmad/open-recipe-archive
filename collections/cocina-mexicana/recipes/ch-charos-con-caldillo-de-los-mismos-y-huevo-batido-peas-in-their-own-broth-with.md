## Ingredients

- tender peas
- tequesquite
- bread
- lard
- onion
- water
- clove
- pepper
- salt
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook tender peas as described in the previous recipe.
2. Remove a portion of the peas and grind with a piece of bread fried in lard.
3. Chop onion finely and fry in plenty of lard.
4. Add the ground bread and peas and fry as well.
5. Add water in a regular quantity and the whole peas, with ground clove, pepper, and necessary fine salt.
6. Let everything boil until it thickens well.
7. When ready to remove from heat, beat two or three egg yolks and add to the peas, stirring well.
8. Place the pot on hot ashes so the egg cooks.
