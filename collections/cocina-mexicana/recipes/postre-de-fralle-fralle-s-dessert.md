## Ingredients

- 1 quarter of an arroba of sugar
- 1 quarter-measure of virgin honey
- 1/2 pound of ground and sifted rice
- 4 ounces of almonds in small pieces
- toasted sesame
- slices of cooked ham
- sponge cake
- cooked chicken breasts
- egg whites
- milk
- sugar
- scent tablets
- aged cheese, ground

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with one quarter of an arroba of sugar and one quarter-measure of virgin honey
2. Clarify, strain, boil and remove from the fire when a drop cast into cold water hardens
3. As you mix in half a pound of ground and sifted rice, keep stirring constantly, and when the rice is done, add four ounces of clean almonds in small pieces and toasted sesame
4. In a cazuela or earthenware dish put a layer of slices of cooked ham, another of sponge cake, the paste next, and on top cooked chicken breasts, neither whole nor very ground
5. Over each layer put a covering of egg whites beaten in a pot with milk, sugar, plenty of scent tablets and a small piece of aged ground cheese
