## Ingredients

- Water with salt
- Pig blood
- Milk, one-third the amount of blood, cooked
- Plenty of onion
- Garlic
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Marjoram
- Parsley, all chopped very finely
- Pig fat, chopped
- A piece of sugar
- Almonds, chopped
- Raisins, chopped
- Pine nuts
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Ginger
- A little cumin, all spices ground
- Large intestines of pig

## Directions

1. Prepare a pot with a little water with salt and receive the pig blood in it
2. Beat the blood so it does not curdle and add one-third part cooked milk
3. Mix well and add plenty of chopped onion, garlic, oregano, thyme, marjoram, and parsley
4. Mix the chopped pig fat with chopped almonds, raisins, pine nuts, and clove, pepper, cinnamon, ginger, and cumin, with the spices ground
5. Mix everything very well
6. Fill the large pig intestines
