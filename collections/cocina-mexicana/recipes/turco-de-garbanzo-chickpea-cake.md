## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- ash water
- salt
- cooked egg yolks
- clove
- pepper
- lard

## Directions

1. Soak the chickpeas in ash water, peel them, wash them well, and put them to cook in salted water
2. After cooking, grind them with one third of their volume of cooked egg yolks, and clove and pepper to taste
3. Mix lard into the ground mixture to make a dough that can be ground
4. Make little tortillas on the metate and complete the operation as in the previous articles
