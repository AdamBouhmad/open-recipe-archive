## Ingredients

- Half pound of coast rice
- Eight cuartillos of milk
- Sugar to taste
- Sixteen egg yolks, well beaten
- Small cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Grind half pound of coast rice and dissolve in eight cuartillos of milk
2. Strain and sweeten to taste
3. Add sixteen well-beaten egg yolks and some small cinnamon sticks
4. Let boil until it has the common point for desserts
