## Ingredients

- One beef head
- Water
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Mixed herbs (bouquet garni)
- Two onions
- Carrots
- Parsnips

## Directions

1. Place a pot of water large enough to turn the head easily over the fire
2. When water is hot, stir and turn the head in all directions and observe if the hair can be removed
3. If it can, remove the pot from the fire, take out the head, rub it by hand, and soak in water until all hair is removed
4. Be careful not to heat the water too much so the skin does not toughen
5. After this operation, soak the head in cold water for hours
6. Remove the bones and cook as follows
7. With the head well scalded and jaws removed, dissolve a handful of flour in a pot or pan with water, bring to a boil and season
8. Season with salt, pepper, a mixed bouquet of herbs, two onions, carrots, and parsnips
9. Place the head in this and let cook for three hours or less as needed
10. When cooked, drain and uncover the brains
11. Serve with a spicy sauce or simply with oil and vinegar
