## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- twelve egg yolks, beaten
- six cuartillos of milk
- ground pine nuts
- twelve beaten eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Give three pounds of sugar a half point
2. Spin in it twelve beaten egg yolks passed through a sieve
3. Cook six cuartillos of milk to mix with the egg and some ground pine nuts
4. Knead and mix with twelve beaten eggs
5. Place in a pan between two fires with lard to cook
6. This can be made a day before eating
