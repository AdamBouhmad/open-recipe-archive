## Ingredients

- rebanadas de tomate (tomato slices)
- otras verduras y legumbres (other vegetables and legumes)
- carnes de toda clase (meats of all kinds)

## Directions

1. Layer sliced tomatoes and other vegetables and legumes over meats of any kind.
2. Cook at two heat sources.
