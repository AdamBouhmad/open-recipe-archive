## Ingredients

- Beef sweetbreads
- Thick pieces of ham for larding
- Scarlet tongue for larding
- Butter
- Marinade

## Directions

1. Lard a sweetbread with thick pieces of ham and scarlet tongue.
2. Leave it for twelve hours in a marinade prepared as in the previous article.
3. After that time, wrap it in paper coated with butter.
4. Remove the paper a little before it is completely cooked so it can take color.
5. Immediately jelly it and serve with pepper sauce.
