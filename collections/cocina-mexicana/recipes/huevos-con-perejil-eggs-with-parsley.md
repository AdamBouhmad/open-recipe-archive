## Ingredients

- parsley
- garlic
- saffron
- salt
- pepper
- mint leaf
- herbs
- eggs

## Directions

1. In a wide vessel place parsley, fried garlic, saffron, salt, pepper, a mint leaf and herbs all in branches
2. Add fried eggs
3. Bring to a boil and remove with a large skimmer
