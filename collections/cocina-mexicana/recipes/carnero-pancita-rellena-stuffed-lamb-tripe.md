## Ingredients

- menudo (lamb offal)
- tripas (tripe)
- panza (stomach)
- manitas cocidas (cooked lamb feet)
- xitomates maduros (ripe tomatoes)
- cabezas de ajo (garlic heads)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- azúcar (sugar)
- especias de todas (all spices)
- garbanzos (chickpeas)
- jamón entreverado (striped ham)
- huevos duros (hard-boiled eggs)
- tomillo (thyme)
- orégano (oregano)
- manteca (lard)
- papel blanco (white paper)
- salsa (sauce)

## Directions

1. Clean the offal and form braids with the tripe
2. Chop everything except the stomach, adding cooked and deboned lamb feet, also chopped
3. Chop ripe tomatoes and clean garlic heads, fry in a pot
4. Once fried, add the chopped meat
5. Add a bit of vinegar and sugar, grind all spices and add a good portion of well-cooked chickpeas
6. Once well fried, add plenty of sliced striped ham and chopped hard-boiled eggs
7. Sprinkle abundantly with thyme and oregano, mix everything well
8. When it has dried, remove from heat and let cool
9. Fill the stomach with this mixture, then sew and stitch it
10. In a pot of sufficient size, add plenty of lard, place white paper on the bottom and the filled stomach on top
11. Coat the top with the same lard and cook over two gentle fires, being careful to coat it repeatedly with lard
12. When it has browned, remove from heat and serve with some sauce
