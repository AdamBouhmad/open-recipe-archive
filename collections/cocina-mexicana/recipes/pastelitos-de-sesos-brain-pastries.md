## Ingredients

- Brains, well cooked
- Salt, ground
- Pepper, ground
- Stale bread, sliced

## Directions

1. After brains are well cooked and all offal removed, coat them with sufficient ground salt and pepper
2. Place between slices of stale bread, pressing so they stick well
3. Fry in very hot lard
4. As soon as they brown, remove and serve
