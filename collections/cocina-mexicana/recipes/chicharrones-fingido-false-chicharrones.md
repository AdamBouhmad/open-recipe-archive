## Ingredients

- two cups milk
- one cup flour
- four eggs
- lard for greasing and frying
- sugar syrup
- raisins, pine nuts, and almonds for garnish

## Directions

1. Mix in a bowl two cups milk, one cup flour, and four eggs, beating or kneading by hand until no grains remain
2. Pour the mixture into a frying pan greased with lard, place over coals with a comal on top
3. Once the mixture is cooked, cut into squares and mark smaller squares with a knife to imitate chicharrones, leaving the bottom layer the thickness of a finger
4. Fry in sufficiently hot lard, bathing vigorously to make them puff
5. Place in hot syrup without letting them boil, removing as soon as the syrup penetrates them
6. Ensure the syrup is thicker on top, then remove from the syrup to serve
7. Garnish with raisins, pine nuts, and almonds
