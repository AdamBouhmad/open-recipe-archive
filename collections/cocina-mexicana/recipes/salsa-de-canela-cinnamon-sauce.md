## Ingredients

- Bread
- Cinnamon sticks
- Salt
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Brown a piece of bread well over the fire
2. Add cinnamon sticks to the bread
3. Grind bread and cinnamon together
4. Season with a little salt
5. Add water so it is not too thick
6. Sweeten with sugar
