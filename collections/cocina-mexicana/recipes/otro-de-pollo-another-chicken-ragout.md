## Ingredients

- Onion, minced
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Tomatoes without seeds
- Toasted almonds
- Lard
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Chicken broth
- Chorizones
- Ham
- Pork loin, sliced
- Chicken
- Citron
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Olives
- Capers
- Chiles in vinegar
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Mince onion, garlic, parsley and oregano and fry
2. Grind tomatoes without seeds, toasted almonds in lard, cloves, cinnamon and pepper
3. Add to the mixture and fry
4. Add the broth in which the chicken was cooked
5. Add chorizones, ham, sliced pork loin and chicken
6. Add citron, raisins, almonds, olives, capers and chiles in vinegar
7. When serving, add Sherry wine
