## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one pound of grated coconut
- cinnamon sticks
- one cuartillo of cream
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with two pounds of sugar, clarify it, strain it, and let it give one boil.
2. Remove from heat, add the grated coconut and cinnamon sticks, and return to heat.
3. When it has given several boils and begins to thicken, add half a cuartillo of cream and let it reach the point of cajeta.
4. Pour onto a platter and brown with heat from above, or serve without browning and make some decorative designs or flowers on top with cinnamon powder.
