## Ingredients

- sugar
- syrup at medium point
- egg yolks
- orange blossom water
- sponge cake (mamón)
- syrup

## Directions

1. Make one pound of sugar into syrup at medium point
2. Beat two egg yolks into warm syrup until incorporated
3. Put in pot with heat and add a little orange blossom water
4. Boil until it reaches a point less than firm
5. Soak sponge cake in syrup
6. Alternate layers of sponge cake and paste
