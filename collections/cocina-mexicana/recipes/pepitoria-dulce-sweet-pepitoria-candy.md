## Ingredients

- squash seeds (toasted and cleaned)
- honey or panoche candy at high stage of hardness

## Directions

1. Toast and clean squash seeds
2. Place them in honey or panoche candy at high stage of hardness
3. After stirring well, remove small portions with a spoon
4. Place them on wafers and let them dry in the sun or in the stove
