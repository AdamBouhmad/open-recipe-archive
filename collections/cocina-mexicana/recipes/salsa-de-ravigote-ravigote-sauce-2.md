## Ingredients

- fine herbs such as parsley
- chives
- chervil
- celery
- one tablespoon capers
- one or two anchovies optional
- one raw egg yolk
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon oil
- one tablespoon vinegar

## Directions

1. Grind fine herbs in the mortar such as parsley, chives, chervil, and celery
2. Add one tablespoon of capers and if desired one or two anchovies
3. When everything is made into a paste, add one raw egg yolk
4. Add salt, pepper, one tablespoon of oil, and one tablespoon of vinegar
5. Serve this sauce cold
