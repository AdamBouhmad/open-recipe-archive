## Ingredients

- two solomillos
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- red wine
- flour or potato starch
- sugar

## Directions

1. Bone and prepare two solomillos
2. Place in marinade with salt, pepper, oil and half a glass of red wine
3. Two hours before serving, place in a pot with the marinade and trimmings
4. Cook until it takes color, moistening with a little water
5. Cook slowly until ready to serve
6. Skim the fat from the sauce and thicken with a little flour or starch in which a lump of sugar is dissolved
7. Bathe the solomillos with this sauce before serving
