## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- Six raw egg yolks
- Half a cup of hot water with proper salt dissolved in it
- Half a pound of melted lard
- A piece the size of an egg of yeast dissolved in a little hot water
- Sifted powdered sugar, as much as needed to sweeten the dough if desired

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. The sugar is optional
