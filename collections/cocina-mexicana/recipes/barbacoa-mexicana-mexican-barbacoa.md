## Ingredients

- Meat
- Chile-garlic sauce or adobo or other sauce
- Salt
- Stones
- Dry firewood
- Palm petate (woven mat)
- Maguey leaves or sticks

## Directions

1. Form a hole three-quarters deep and of competent size for the piece to be cooked
2. Put stones at the bottom
3. With dry firewood form a fire in the center so that after two or three hours the hole is well heated
4. Remove all the fire, leaving the stones
5. Moisten a new palm petate and place it over the stones
6. Place the prepared meat on top, either with chile-garlic, or adobo, or another sauce well spread and loaded with salt
7. Place another petate over the meat, sticks or maguey leaves to cover it and some hot stones so earth does not fall
8. Cover with another wet petate, a little earth on top, and enough fire over it
9. After eight or ten hours, remove the meat well cooked
