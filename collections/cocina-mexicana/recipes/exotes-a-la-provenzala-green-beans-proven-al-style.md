## Ingredients

- Onions
- Oil
- Cooked exotes (green beans)
- Parsley, chopped
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut onions into pieces and fry well in oil
2. Then add the already-cooked exotes mixed with chopped parsley, salt and pepper
3. When well fried, place on a platter
4. Pour a little vinegar in the pan in which they were fried and sprinkle the mixture over them
