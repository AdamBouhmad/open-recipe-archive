## Ingredients

- milk
- cinnamon sticks
- sugar
- a piece of fresh butter
- three egg yolks per taza
- mamón, sliced
- custard
- butter
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Cook milk with cinnamon sticks and sugar in excess
2. Remove from heat and add a piece of fresh butter and three egg yolks per taza, beating them well
3. Pour into a pan or tortera coated with butter
4. Layer sliced mamón that will be well soaked with the prepared milk, and another layer of custard
5. Cook over two gentle fires, and cover the cake on top with beaten eggs
6. Brown the eggs with a heated tile
