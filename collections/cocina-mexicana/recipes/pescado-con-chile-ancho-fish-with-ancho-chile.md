## Ingredients

- Oil
- lard
- tomato
- onion
- fish slices
- garlic
- ancho chiles
- cinnamon stick
- peppercorns
- cumin

## Directions

1. Heat oil and lard with tomato and onion in proportion.
2. Place fish slices in the frying base as it begins to boil.
3. Grind garlic cloves, ancho chiles, cinnamon stick, peppercorns, and cumin together.
4. Lower this mixture from the metate with plenty of oil.
5. Beat everything together with a fork and pour over the fish.
6. Place fish on low heat, covering the pot.
