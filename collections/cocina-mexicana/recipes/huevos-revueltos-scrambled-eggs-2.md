## Ingredients

- Butter
- Dried chiles (whole)
- Eggs
- Fine salt

## Directions

1. Burn a little butter and fry whole dried chiles in it until well toasted
2. Remove the pan from heat and add the necessary eggs, having first beaten and scrambled them
3. Mix in the necessary fine salt and stir continuously until they set
4. Serve with the whole chiles that were toasted
