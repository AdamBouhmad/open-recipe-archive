## Ingredients

- butter
- raw egg yolks
- lemon juice
- black pepper
- salt
- saffron
- nutmeg
- chiltepiquín powder

## Directions

1. Make the same as the Portuguese sauce, adding saffron, nutmeg, and chiltepiquín powder
