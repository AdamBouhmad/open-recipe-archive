## Ingredients

- Partridge meat, raw and minced
- Fattened young hen meat, raw and minced
- Capon meat, raw and minced
- Raw ham, a little
- Beef leg
- Lard
- Parsley
- Onions
- Mushrooms
- Earth truffles (criadillas)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine spices
- Garlic, a pinch
- Two egg whites
- Five or six egg yolks
- Nata (cream), a little
- Thin slices of beef pulp

## Directions

1. Mince very finely the raw meats of partridge, fattened young hen, and capon, along with a little raw ham, beef leg, and lard
2. Add parsley, onions, mushrooms, and earth truffles to the minced mixture
3. Season with salt, pepper, fine spices, and a pinch of garlic
4. Add two egg whites and five or six egg yolks and a little cream
5. Chop everything finely and incorporate well together
6. Roll this mixture into large pieces according to the desired quantity
7. Flatten thin slices of beef pulp on the table and wrap each sausage piece in these slices, making each at least as thick as an arm and of proportionate size
8. Line a pot with slices of ham
9. Place the well-wrapped sausages in the pot
10. Cover with slices of beef and other strips of ham
11. Cook over low coals (brasa) for eight to ten hours
12. Allow them to cool in the same pot
13. Remove any fat and carefully remove the wrapping meat without breaking the sausages
14. Cut into slices and serve cold
