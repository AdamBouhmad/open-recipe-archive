## Ingredients

- lamprey
- butter
- carrots
- onions
- parsley
- bay leaf
- white wine
- salt
- black pepper
- lemon juice
- fried croutons

## Directions

1. After placing the lamprey in boiling water to remove the mud or slime, as described in the previous article, empty it through the gills, being careful to remove the head at the knot so that the tail can be inserted there.
2. Put a piece of butter in a pan with sliced carrots and onions, parsley and bay leaf, frying these things until they are well rendered.
3. Then add white wine, salt and coarse pepper; when this broth is seasoned, strain it through a sieve over the lamprey, which will be placed to cook.
4. After cooked and cooled, it should have been bled properly.
5. Serve with lemon juice adorned with fried croutons in butter.
