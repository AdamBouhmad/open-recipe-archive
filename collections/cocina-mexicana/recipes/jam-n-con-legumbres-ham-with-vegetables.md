## Ingredients

- young pork ham
- salsify
- onions
- bay leaf
- thyme
- garlic
- parsley
- spinach
- green beans
- lettuce
- chicory
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Choose a young pork ham and cook it in a marinade of salsify, onions, bay leaf, thyme, garlic, and parsley until the broth becomes aspic
2. Place the ham on a deep plate, surround and cover it with cooked salsify, spinach, green beans, lettuce, onions cooked in broth, or chicory that has been soaked and cooked in broth or thick Spanish sauce
3. Pour the sauce on top, placing the end of the ham on a circular cut white paper
