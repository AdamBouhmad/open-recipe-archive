## Ingredients

- Dried cod strips, six inches long and four inches wide
- Water
- Parsley
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Beat the stockfish the day before it is to be served, cutting it into strips six inches long and four inches wide
2. Tie the strips and soak them for twenty-four hours, changing the water three or four times during this period
3. One hour before serving, place the stockfish in a pot with cold water and bring it to a boil over good heat
4. At the first boil, move the pot to the corner of the stove so it stops boiling
5. At the moment of serving, surround it with parsley and serve potatoes separately
