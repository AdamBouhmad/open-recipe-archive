## Ingredients

- mojarra fish
- lard
- garlic
- tomatoes
- onion
- parsley
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cumin
- water
- chile in vinegar
- olives
- capers
- lemon or bitter orange

## Directions

1. Clean and fry in lard until golden
2. Remove from heat and fry many finely minced garlic
3. Then fry tomatoes, onion and parsley, also chopped
4. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper and cumin
5. Add a little water to the seasoning and fry everything until it is thick and somewhat dark
6. Place the mojarra on a platter to receive the steam from the pan
7. Cover generously with the sauce so the fish is submerged
8. Chop finely chile in vinegar, olives, capers and a little lemon or bitter orange
9. Serve in this manner
