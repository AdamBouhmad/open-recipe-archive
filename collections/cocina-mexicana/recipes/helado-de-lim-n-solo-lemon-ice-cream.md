## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds white sugar
- two quartillos of very clear water
- four or six good lemons

## Directions

1. Dissolve one and a half pounds of white sugar in two quartillos of very clear water
2. Select four or six good lemons and clean them with a cloth
3. Cut them in half
4. Squeeze them in the same manner as described for the oranges
5. Strain the liquid through a fine-haired sieve and set aside in a cool place
