## Ingredients

- sesos de ternera (calf brains)
- caldo de pollo (chicken broth)
- yemas de huevos (egg yolks)

## Directions

1. Prepare and wash as the preceding recipes
2. Cook them in chicken broth, which is thickened with egg yolks at the time of serving
