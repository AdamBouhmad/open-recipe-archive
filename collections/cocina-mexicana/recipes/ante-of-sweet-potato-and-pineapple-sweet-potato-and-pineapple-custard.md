## Ingredients

- Sweet potatoes
- Pineapple
- Sugar
- Sponge cake (mamón)
- Syrup
- White wine
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind sweet potatoes and pineapple in the proportion of eight parts sweet potato to each one of pineapple
2. Mix with one and a half pounds of sugar for each pound of ground fruit
3. Place over heat until it reaches a paste-like point
4. Layer over sponge cake sprinkled with light syrup mixed with white wine
5. Dust the top layer with ground cinnamon
