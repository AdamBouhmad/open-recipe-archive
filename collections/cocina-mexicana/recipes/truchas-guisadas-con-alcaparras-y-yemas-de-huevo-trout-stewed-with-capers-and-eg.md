## Ingredients

- trout
- garlic cloves
- oil
- capers
- cloves
- pepper
- cumin
- salt
- water
- egg yolks
- lemon or vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry split garlic cloves in good oil and when golden, remove and grind together with cloves, pepper and cumin
2. Add the ground mixture to the same oil with salt and a little water
3. When the sauce comes to a boil, add clean trout pieces with capers
4. Let them cook, then thicken the sauce with beaten egg yolks cut with lemon or vinegar
