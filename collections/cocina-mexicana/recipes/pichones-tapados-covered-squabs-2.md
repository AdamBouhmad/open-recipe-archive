## Ingredients

- manteca (lard)
- peregil (parsley)
- poleo (pennyroyal)
- yerbabuena (mint)
- pichones (squabs)
- jamón (ham)
- lomo de puerco (pork loin)
- piña (pineapple)
- manzana (apple)
- cebollas (onions)
- xitomates (tomatoes)
- chiles verdes (green chiles)
- especias molidas (ground spices)
- chilitos (small chiles)
- aceitunas (olives)

## Directions

1. Line a pot with lard and add parsley, pennyroyal and mint
2. Place the squabs in quarters on top with pieces of ham and pork loin
3. Add pineapple, apple, onions, tomatoes and green chiles, all sliced, ground spices, small chiles and olives
4. Cook at two fires and serve
