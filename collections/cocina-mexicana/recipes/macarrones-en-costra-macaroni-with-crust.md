## Ingredients

- macaroni
- bread crumbs
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare the macaroni as in the previous recipe
2. Place on a plate
3. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and grated cheese
4. Allow to take color under a bell oven
