## Ingredients

- Roasted doves
- Broth from the pot
- Salt
- Peppercorns
- Verjuice
- Bread crusts, fried

## Directions

1. Take roasted and cold doves, remove the head and the interior of the belly, discard the gizzards, and grind the rest.
2. Dissolve the ground mixture in the broth from the pot, strain it through a sieve, season the strained liquid with salt, peppercorns and a little verjuice.
3. In this sauce warm the doves without letting them cook, and serve them with fried bread crusts.
