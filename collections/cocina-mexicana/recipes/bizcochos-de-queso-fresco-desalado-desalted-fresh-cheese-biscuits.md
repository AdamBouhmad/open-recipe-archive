## Ingredients

- four ounces of fresh cheese, desalted in cold water
- three pounds of melted butter, almost cold
- eighteen ounces of ground sugar
- six pounds of flour
- six egg yolks and three egg whites

## Directions

1. Desalt four ounces of fresh cheese in cold water and grind it
2. In three pounds of almost-cold melted butter, add eighteen ounces of ground sugar and beat slightly
3. Incorporate the ground cheese
4. Beat everything until it thunders and makes bubbles
5. Mix in gradually six pounds of flour
6. Add six egg yolks and three egg whites and knead
7. Form the biscuits
8. Bake in a not-very-hot oven
