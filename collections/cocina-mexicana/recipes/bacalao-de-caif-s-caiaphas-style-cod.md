## Ingredients

- Cod
- Garlic cloves
- Olive oil
- Butter
- Onion
- Chile
- Tomato
- Cooked tomatoes
- Parsley
- Bread
- Bread slices for frying
- Pickled chiles

## Directions

1. Soak the cod from the evening and the next day early, cook it until very soft
2. Remove the bones, skin and divide into small strips to add to the following sauce
3. Place equal quantities of olive oil and butter and fry some whole garlic cloves in it
4. Remove these and fry other peeled and minced garlic with abundant onion in pieces, strips of chile and very finely minced tomato
5. Meanwhile grind the garlic that was fried with a portion of cooked tomatoes, which is also added to the sauté
6. Once this is done, add a little minced parsley, a small piece of soaked and ground bread and add the cod to boil over low heat
7. At serving time, fry some bread slices and place them on top and serve in the same pan with some pickled chiles
