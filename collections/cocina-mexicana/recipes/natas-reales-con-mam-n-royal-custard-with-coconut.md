## Ingredients

- two quartillos of milk
- four ounces of butter
- eight egg yolks
- one handful of ground soaked rice
- sugar to taste
- cooked custard
- ground coconut

## Directions

1. Mix and place over fire two quartillos of milk with four ounces of butter, eight egg yolks, one handful of soaked ground rice, and sugar to taste
2. When it is near reaching the point of custard, add custard creams that will incorporate well
3. Place all in a platter and cover the top with ground coconut
