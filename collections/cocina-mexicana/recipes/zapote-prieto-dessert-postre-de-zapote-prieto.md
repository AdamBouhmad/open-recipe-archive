## Ingredients

- Zapote prieto fruits
- White wine
- Sugar
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Remove the skins from these zapotes and remove the pits
2. Mix and beat them with white wine, sugar and ground cinnamon, and pouring them into a platter, adorn the top with more cinnamon powder
