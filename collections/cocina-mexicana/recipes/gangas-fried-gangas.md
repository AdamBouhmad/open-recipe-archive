## Ingredients

- Gangas
- Lard
- Water
- Salt
- Onions, raw
- Wide chile
- Garlic, cleaned and crushed
- Vinegar
- Cloves, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Black pepper, ground
- Lard for cooking

## Directions

1. Grease a saucepan with lard
2. Place gangas and cover with water
3. Add corresponding salt, one or two raw onions, divided, one wide chile, and two or three cleaned crushed garlic cloves
4. Let boil until cooked
5. Add a little vinegar with a bit of cloves, cinnamon, and pepper
6. Place a comal on top with some lard
7. Cook over two fires until all the broth is consumed
8. Brown in the remaining grease in the pan
9. Serve alone or with one of the sauces or broths for birds
