## Ingredients

- one quarter liter of broth
- half a glass of white wine
- salt
- pepper
- lemon peel
- two bay leaves
- a little vinegar

## Directions

1. Put half a glass of white wine, salt, pepper, lemon peel, two bay leaves and a little vinegar into a quarter liter of broth
2. Leave everything to infuse over low heat for ten or twelve hours
3. Strain through a sieve to be served with all types of poultry, vegetables and fish
