## Ingredients

- beans
- salt
- oil
- garlic
- hazelnuts
- bread
- water
- vinegar
- pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook with salt and cut them
2. Add oil with fried garlic
3. Toast hazelnuts and pound them
4. Toast a little bread, soak it in water and vinegar, squeeze it well, and grind it with pepper and eggs
5. Add to the beans and let everything boil together
