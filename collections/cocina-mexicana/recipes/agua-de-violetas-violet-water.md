## Ingredients

- two handfuls of violets
- four cuartillos of water
- half a pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Gather the violets in the early morning before sunrise, because the coolness of the night will have concentrated their perfume
2. Use only the flowers
3. Add half a pound of sugar and leave in infusion for six hours
4. After this time, strain the water through a cloth
5. To drink it more pleasantly, place the glass inside snow
