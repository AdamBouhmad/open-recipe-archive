## Ingredients

- prepared quails
- butter
- sliced sausage
- sliced sausages
- ham slices
- salt
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- chopped parsley
- clove
- cinnamon
- ground pepper
- water
- white wine
- tender leaves of chamomile
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Fry the prepared quails in butter with sliced sausage, sliced sausages, ham slices, and salt
2. Once done, add roasted and ground tomatoes, chopped parsley, clove, cinnamon, and ground pepper
3. Add equal quantities of water and white wine
4. Cook until the quails are well cooked and the sauce is thick
5. Serve with tender chamomile leaves on top, chiles, and olives
