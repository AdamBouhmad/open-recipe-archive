## Ingredients

- Quarter of an arroba of apricots
- Apricot kernels
- Aguardiente
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Mace

## Directions

1. Cut a quarter of an arroba of apricots into small pieces and split the halves to extract the kernels
2. Peel the kernels and crack them
3. Put all in a proportionate bottle with one quartilla of aguardiente, half a pound of sugar, a little cinnamon, eight cloves and very little mace
4. Seal the bottle well and let the infusion happen in fifteen or twenty days, taking care to shake it frequently
5. After that time filter through a cloth and bottle, storing in the cellar or another cool place
