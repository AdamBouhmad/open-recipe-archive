## Ingredients

- pork liver
- four ounces butter
- handful parsley
- shallots
- ham
- spices
- nutmeg
- pepper
- salt
- pork kidneys
- pork brains
- pork loins

## Directions

1. Put a good pork liver, cut into slices, in a pan with four ounces of butter and a handful of chopped parsley and shallots
2. When the liver is half cooked, let it cool for a quarter hour
3. Cut ham into dice equal in amount to the liver and make a good filling with it, mixing together, seasoning with spices, nutmeg, pepper and salt, and adding what remains in the pan where the liver was fried
4. Lightly season the two kidneys, brains and loins after cutting them into wheels in the shape of small pieces
5. Arrange the pastry as described for the cold pastry from the previous article
6. Spread the filling on the interior and place the brains on the bottom, distributing the small pieces
7. Add the kidneys and cover everything with the filling
