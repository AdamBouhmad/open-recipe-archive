## Ingredients

- walnuts, cleaned
- sesame seeds, toasted
- lard
- water
- vinegar
- sugar
- cinnamon, ground

## Directions

1. Grind the cleaned walnuts with toasted sesame
2. Fry the ground mixture in lard
3. Add water, vinegar, a little sugar, and ground cinnamon
