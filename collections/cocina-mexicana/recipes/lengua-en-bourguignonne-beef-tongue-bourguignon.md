## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- thick bacon strips
- salt
- pepper
- beef broth
- white wine
- carrots
- onions

## Directions

1. Lard a well-cleaned tongue, carefully scraped of its skin, with thick seasoned bacon strips
2. Cook in a pot with beef broth, white wine, carrots and onions
3. After it has boiled for some time, cover well, reduce the heat underneath and place heat on top
4. Let it simmer slowly for about five hours
5. Strain the sauce when cooked and thicken with a Spanish sauce
6. Serve the tongue surrounded by vegetables and bathed with the sauce
