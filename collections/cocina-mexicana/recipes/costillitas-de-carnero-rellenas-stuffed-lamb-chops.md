## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- minced meat with almonds and raisins
- fried seasoning

## Directions

1. Follow the same procedures as for tomato chops Mexican style, with the difference that instead of egg and bread crumbs, place minced meat with almonds and raisins, well seasoned
2. Add a little fried seasoning on top before wrapping in papers
