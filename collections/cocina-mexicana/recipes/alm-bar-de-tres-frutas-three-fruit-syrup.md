## Ingredients

- eight ounces of strawberries
- eight ounces of cherries
- sugar

## Directions

1. Take strawberries and cherries, eight ounces of each, peeling them well
2. Crush these fruits and press their juice through a cloth or strainer
3. Reduce everything over moderate heat
4. Clarify and cook in sugar, letting it boil for some minutes
5. Remove from heat and stir until it hardens
6. Put this preserve in paper boxes; generally three pounds of sugar are needed for one pound of fruit juice
