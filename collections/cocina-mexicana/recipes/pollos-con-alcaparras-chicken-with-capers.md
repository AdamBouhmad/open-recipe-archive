## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Ham
- Chorizo
- Capers
- Onion
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Fried bread
- Lard

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous recipes
2. Once the meat is well fried, grind capers and fry them with onion
3. Add to the casserole
4. Add ground clove, cinnamon and pepper with fried bread slices in lard
5. The broth should remain thick
6. Garnish as the previous recipes
