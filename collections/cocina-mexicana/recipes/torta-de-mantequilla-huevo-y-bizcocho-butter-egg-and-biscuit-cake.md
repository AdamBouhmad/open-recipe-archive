## Ingredients

- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- eggs
- hard biscuit, ground

## Directions

1. Wash the butter very well and form it into a ball; the next day slice it
2. In a pan, layer alternately: a layer of butter, another of sugar and cinnamon, another of egg, and another of ground hard biscuit, until the pan is full
3. The first and last layers should be butter, with plenty of sugar and cinnamon on top
4. Place over two fires until it sets, which is when it pulls away from the sides
