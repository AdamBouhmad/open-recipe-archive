## Ingredients

- lard
- quarters of onion
- tomatoes
- garlic
- radishes
- beet stalks
- lettuce
- tequisquite water
- shrimp broth
- vinegar
- clove
- saffron
- carrots
- beets
- egg
- comal

## Directions

1. Put lard over the fire and add quarters of onion, tomatoes, garlic, radishes and beet stalks, all sliced
2. When half fried, add a large quantity of lettuce, more than with the other vegetables, and a little tequisquite water
3. Once everything is fried and well cooked, add a little of the broth in which the shrimp were cooked, vinegar, and ground clove and saffron, along with carrots and beets with the pieces
4. Once the stew is seasoned, mix in the cooked and whole shrimp and a little soaked and squeezed bread
5. Cover everything with beaten egg as for frying, and place a comal with fire on top to set it
