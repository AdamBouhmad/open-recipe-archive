## Ingredients

- Pork meat, minced
- Garlic, to taste
- Salt, to taste
- Wide chiles, deveined, soaked, and ground
- Red wine, sufficient quantity
- Cured pork casings
- Green wood for smoking or aromatic wood such as juniper

## Directions

1. Mince pork meat and season with garlic and salt
2. Add deveined, soaked, and ground wide chiles and sufficient red wine to well moisten the mixture
3. Leave in this state until the next day, testing at the fire to see if it is well seasoned and adding what it lacks and wine if it has dried too much
4. Fill cured pork casings with it
5. Tie the divisions and prick the sausages with a needle to let air escape
6. Hang so they can receive smoke from green wood or aromatic wood such as juniper, placed at an appropriate distance
