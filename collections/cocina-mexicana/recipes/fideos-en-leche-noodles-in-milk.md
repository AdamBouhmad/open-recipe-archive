## Ingredients

- milk
- noodles
- sugar

## Directions

1. Heat the milk and at the moment it begins to boil, add the noodles
2. Take care that they do not form into balls and stir them so they do not break apart and become reduced to paste
3. Sweeten with sugar to taste
4. Half an hour is sufficient for the noodles to burst and be well cooked
