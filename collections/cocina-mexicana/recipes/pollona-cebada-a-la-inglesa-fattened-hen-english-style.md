## Ingredients

- fattened hen
- salt
- English sauce

## Directions

1. Gather the hen by placing the legs outward and securing the wings with thread passed through
2. Place to cook in water with a pinch of salt without any other seasoning
3. When cooked, arrange on a plate pouring English sauce over it
