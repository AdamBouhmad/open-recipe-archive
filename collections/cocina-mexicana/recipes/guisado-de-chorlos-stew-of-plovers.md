## Ingredients

- Plovers
- Water
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Salt
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Onion, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Parsley, chopped
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Put a little water and plenty of vinegar to boil, a little oil, salt, clove, pepper and cinnamon all ground.
2. Leave to boil until only the butter remains.
3. While frying add onion, garlic and chopped parsley, and add breadcrumbs before serving.
