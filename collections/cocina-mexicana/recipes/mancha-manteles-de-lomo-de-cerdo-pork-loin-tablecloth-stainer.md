## Ingredients

- ancho chiles, deveined and soaked
- pine nuts
- walnuts
- cooked tomatoes
- fried bread
- clove
- cinnamon
- lard
- salt
- cooked pork loin
- sliced chorizo
- ham in strips
- broth
- sliced pear and apple
- cooked onions separated into leaves
- vinegar
- sugar

## Directions

1. Grind deveined and soaked ancho chiles with pine nuts, walnuts, cooked tomatoes, fried bread, clove, and cinnamon
2. Fry in lard with salt
3. Once ready, add the cooked pork loin with sliced chorizo and ham in strips, the broth, sliced pear and apple, cooked onions separated into leaves, vinegar, and sugar
4. It will be like the previous ones
