## Ingredients

- three dozen oysters
- butter sauce
- chopped and parboiled parsley

## Directions

1. Parboil three dozen oysters and set aside at first boil
2. After draining them, place in a pan with a little butter and parsley sauce that has been chopped and parboiled
3. Prepare this sauce the same as the thick Spanish sauce, adding to the oysters at the moment of serving four ounces of butter
