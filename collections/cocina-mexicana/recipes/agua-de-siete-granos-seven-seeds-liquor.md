## Ingredients

- one ounce caraway seeds
- one ounce fennel seeds
- one ounce green anise
- one ounce caraway
- one and a half ounces cilantro seeds
- half ounce angelica seed
- one ounce star anise or common dried anise
- one drachm of mace
- refined brandy
- three and a half pounds sugar
- fourteen quartillos of water

## Directions

1. Crush or pound in a mortar the caraway seeds, fennel, green anise, caraway, cilantro, angelica seed, star anise or common anise, and mace
2. Infuse all ingredients for eight days in a jug of refined brandy
3. Distill in a bain-marie after eight days
4. Dissolve three and a half pounds of sugar in fourteen quartillos of water
5. Mix this with the distilled liquor
6. Filter and bottle
