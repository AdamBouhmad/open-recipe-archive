## Ingredients

- cod
- butter
- onions
- flour
- salt

## Directions

1. Brown chopped onions in butter
2. Add a little flour
3. Add broth from cooking the cod
4. Let it thicken as needed
5. Place the cooked and previously prepared cod in the sauce
