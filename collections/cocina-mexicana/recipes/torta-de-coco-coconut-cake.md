## Ingredients

- One grated coconut
- Twelve eggs
- Mamón
- Half pound of lard
- Syrup
- Sherry wine
- Powdered cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat twelve eggs as if for frying
2. Mix with crumbled mamón
3. Burn half a pound of lard and cook the cake like all others over very slow heat, but when adding the paste to the lard it should be very hot
4. When cooked, add syrup, sherry wine, and powdered cinnamon
