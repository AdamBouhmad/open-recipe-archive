## Ingredients

- broth and vinegar in equal parts
- salt
- three or four minced garlic cloves
- pepper
- a bunch of parsley

## Directions

1. Take a pan and add equal parts broth and vinegar
2. Add salt, three or four finely minced garlic cloves, and pepper
3. Add a bunch of parsley and crush it
4. Put everything over heat for about one hour
