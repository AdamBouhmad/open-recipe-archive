## Ingredients

- spinach
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- onion
- avocado

## Directions

1. The spinach must be tender and soaked in water for a long time to deflame
2. Chop as you would lettuce
3. Dress with oil and vinegar and salt
4. Adorn with slices of deflamed onion, avocado, and other things customary in lettuce salads
