## Ingredients

- beef, sliced lengthwise
- walnuts
- pine nuts
- almonds
- pepper
- mint
- parsley
- oregano
- wide chilies
- cumin
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- lard
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Slice the beef lengthwise and stuff with walnuts, pine nuts, almonds, pepper, mint, parsley and oregano
2. Grind wide chilies with cumin
3. Coat the beef with the chile mixture
4. Wrap and tie the pieces with threads
5. Prepare a pot with sticks on the bottom to prevent burning
6. Arrange the meat rolls well in the pot
7. Add water and vinegar as needed to cook it, and the corresponding salt
8. Cover the pot with dough, bring to a boil and remove the next day
9. Prepare ground wide chili to coat all the rolls
10. Fry them in lard and add pepper, vinegar and toasted bread to the sauce
