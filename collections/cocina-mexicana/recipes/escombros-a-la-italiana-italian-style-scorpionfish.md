## Ingredients

- Four scorpionfish, cleaned and gutted
- Half a bottle of white wine
- Some slices of onion
- Carrot peels
- Some sprigs of parsley
- One bay leaf
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads and tails of four cleaned scorpionfish
2. Place them in a pot with half a bottle of white wine, some onion slices, carrot peels, some sprigs of parsley, one bay leaf, and sufficient salt
3. Cook until done
4. Drain the fish
5. Arrange on a plate and serve with Italian white sauce
