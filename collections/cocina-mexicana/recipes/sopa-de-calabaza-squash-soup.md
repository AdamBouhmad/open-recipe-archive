## Ingredients

- squash
- salt
- butter
- cheese
- sugar
- cinnamon
- 4 eggs
- milk

## Directions

1. Cut the squash into pieces
2. Cook in water with salt
3. When half cooked, remove, drain and cut into slices
4. Fry in butter
5. Arrange in alternating layers with fresh cheese, sprinkling all with sugar and cinnamon
6. Beat four eggs, dissolve in a quarter liter of milk, and mix both together
7. Bathe the soup with this mixture
8. Add a little fresh melted butter
9. Cook over two gentle fires until the broth is consumed but the soup is not too dry
10. Remove and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon on top
