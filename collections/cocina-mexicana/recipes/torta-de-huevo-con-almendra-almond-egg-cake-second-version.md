## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds of sugar
- one pound of ground almonds
- ten egg yolks
- a little ground cinnamon
- five toasted sponge biscuits
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with one and a half pounds of sugar, clarified and at thread stage
2. Incorporate one pound of ground almonds
3. Keep stirring over the fire until the bottom of the pan becomes visible
4. Remove from heat and add ten egg yolks, a little ground cinnamon, and five toasted sponge biscuits
5. Mix everything well
6. Pour into a pan greased with butter
7. Cook over two fires
