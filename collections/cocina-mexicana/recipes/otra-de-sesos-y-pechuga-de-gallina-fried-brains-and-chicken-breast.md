## Ingredients

- brains
- chicken breast
- spinach
- salt

## Directions

1. For the amount of brains specified, use one chicken breast and a few spinach leaves, all cooked and finely chopped.
2. Fry the spinach first, then the brains and then the chicken.
3. Once everything is fried and mixed with salt, let it cool a little so that figures of the desired shape can be formed.
