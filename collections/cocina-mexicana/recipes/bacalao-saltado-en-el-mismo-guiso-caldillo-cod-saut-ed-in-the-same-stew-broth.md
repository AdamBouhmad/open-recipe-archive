## Ingredients

- Dried ancho chile
- Garlic
- Cumin
- Black pepper
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Lard
- Water
- Cooked cod

## Directions

1. Grind soaked and deveined ancho chile with garlic, cumin, pepper, clove and cinnamon, and fry all of it.
2. Add a little water, and after giving some boils, remove from the heat, then add the already-cooked cod, making it jump, stirring in the same pan, being careful to soak well.
3. At serving time add oil and oregano on top if desired.
