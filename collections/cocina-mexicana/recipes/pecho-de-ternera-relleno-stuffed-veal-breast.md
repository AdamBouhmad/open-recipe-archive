## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- bacon slices
- filling
- onions
- carrot
- bouquet
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- white wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Separate the extremity from the side bones
2. Remove the top and bottom parts
3. Introduce cooked filling into the cavity
4. Cook the meats again
5. Place in a pot with bacon slices, breast facing up, covered with more bacon slices
6. Add onions, carrot, bouquet, salt and pepper
7. Add half broth and white wine
8. Cover the pot and cook over fire
9. When done, add some broth and lemon juice
