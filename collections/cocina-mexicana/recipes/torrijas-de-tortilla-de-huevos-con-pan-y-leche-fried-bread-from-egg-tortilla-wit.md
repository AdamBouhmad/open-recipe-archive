## Ingredients

- bread loaf
- milk
- beaten eggs
- a little butter
- beaten egg for coating
- syrup

## Directions

1. Soak a loaf of bread in milk the night before making the fried bread
2. The next day, drain in a napkin
3. Mix with plenty of beaten eggs
4. Add a little butter and make a cake
5. When cool, cut into slices
6. Fry coated with beaten egg
7. Place in syrup
