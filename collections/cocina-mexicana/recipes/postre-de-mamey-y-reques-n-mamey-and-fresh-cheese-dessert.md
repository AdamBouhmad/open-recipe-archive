## Ingredients

- Syrup at high point made with two pounds of sugar
- Three mameys, strained through a sieve
- Half the weight of the mixture in ground fresh cheese
- Mamón marquesote
- Syrup
- Ground cinnamon
- Cinnamon
- Pine nuts
- Toasted almonds

## Directions

1. Make a syrup at high point with two pounds of sugar
2. When boiling, add three mameys strained through a sieve
3. Add half the weight of the mixture in ground fresh cheese
4. Leave on the fire until it reaches the point of being thick
5. Set aside and when lukewarm, put on a platter in alternating layers with mamón marquesote
6. Sprinkle with syrup and ground cinnamon
7. Decorate the top layer with cinnamon, pine nuts, and toasted almonds
