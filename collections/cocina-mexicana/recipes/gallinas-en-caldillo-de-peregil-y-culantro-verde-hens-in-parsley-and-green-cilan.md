## Ingredients

- Cooked hens
- Lard
- Ham
- Onion
- Green cilantro
- Parsley
- Mint
- Broth from cooking the hens
- Clove
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Vinegar
- Wine
- Salt
- Fried and ground toasted bread and lard

## Directions

1. Fry the cooked hens in lard until golden
2. Add finely chopped ham, onion, green cilantro, plenty of parsley and mint
3. Once the herbs are fried, add the broth in which the hens were cooked with clove, pepper and nutmeg
4. Add vinegar and after boiling sufficiently, shortly before serving add a cup of wine
5. Let it still give a few boils
6. If the broth is not thick enough, add bread fried and toasted in lard and then ground, seasoning everything with salt
