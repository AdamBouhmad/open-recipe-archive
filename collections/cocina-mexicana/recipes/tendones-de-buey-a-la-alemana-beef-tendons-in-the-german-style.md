## Ingredients

- beef breast
- cabbage
- parsley
- onion
- garlic head
- clove spice
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- sausages
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut two or three pounds of beef breast precisely into three or four equal pieces
2. Blanch briefly in boiling water
3. Blanch half a large cabbage for more than a quarter hour
4. Cook the beef breast with a little broth, sprigs of parsley, onion, a head of garlic, two cloves of spice, a bay leaf, a little thyme and a little basil
5. After one hour add the cabbage cut in three pieces, well pressed and tied with string, with four large whole onions
6. When almost cooked, add four sausages and a little salt
