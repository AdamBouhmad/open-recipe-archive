## Ingredients

- 2 pounds beef
- 2 pounds veal
- 1 old hen
- onions
- carrots
- 2 quartillos of water
- mushrooms
- small onions
- parsley
- cloves
- bay leaf
- salt
- sustancia roja (red substance)

## Directions

1. Put beef, veal, hen, onions and carrots in a pot with 2 quartillos of water
2. Allow to reduce over high fire until gelatin begins to form
3. Transfer pan to moderate fire
4. When the gelatin begins to turn black, add a little broth
5. Add mushrooms, small onions, a bunch of parsley, cloves and bay leaf
6. Skim, season with salt and let boil three consecutive hours
7. Strain this substance and add the red substance that has been prepared separately
8. Let boil again for one hour
9. Degrease the sauce and strain through cheesecloth
