## Ingredients

- flour
- twenty-five egg yolks, beaten until hardened
- two ounces sifted sugar
- salt, as much as can be taken with three fingers
- tequesquite white powder, the same amount as salt
- one small bowl of refined Spanish aguardiente
- one small bowl of melted and cooled butter

## Directions

1. Place a mound of flour in a trough with a hole in the middle
2. Pour the beaten egg yolks into the hole along with the sifted sugar, salt, tequesquite white powder, aguardiente, and melted butter
3. Mix everything well with the surrounding flour, using only as much flour as the eggs can absorb so the dough remains soft
4. Separate any excess flour if there is any
5. Wet your hands in flour and knead the dough with your palms until it no longer sticks to them
6. Cut and shape the puchas
7. Cook and glaze them as described in other articles
