## Ingredients

- thirty or forty turnips
- salt
- water
- concentrated broth
- sugar
- German sauce
- fine butter

## Directions

1. Turn thirty or forty turnips into balls of the same size
2. Blanch them in boiling water with a little salt
3. After refreshing them in cold water, place them to cook in good concentrated broth with a little sugar
4. When they are cooked, add one good tablespoon of German sauce
5. Complete their seasoning in a water bath with a little fine butter
