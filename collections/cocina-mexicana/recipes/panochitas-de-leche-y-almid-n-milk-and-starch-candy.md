## Ingredients

- six pounds sugar
- five quartillos of milk
- four ounces starch

## Directions

1. Dissolve the sugar in the milk and strain it immediately
2. Mix in four ounces of starch dissolved in a little of the same milk
3. Put the mixture on the heat and let it boil until it reaches the cajeta point or separates from the pan when stirred
4. Remove from heat and beat until it thickens
5. Form the panochitas as in the previous article
6. Can also be made into small balls with an almond inside, shaped with pincers
