## Ingredients

- corn
- water
- salt
- one and a half jarros of milk
- two tablespoons of butter
- two cloves
- ground cinnamon
- lemon zest
- nutmeg
- sugar
- eight beaten eggs
- one tablespoon of orange blossom water
- a little anise

## Directions

1. Parboil the corn with water and salt
2. Pound well in a mortar and drain so that no water remains
3. Pass through a sieve to remove the husk
4. Mix with one and a half jarros of milk, two tablespoons of butter, two cloves, a little ground cinnamon, lemon zest and nutmeg in sufficient sugar
5. Knead well and add eight beaten eggs, one tablespoon of orange blossom water and a little anise
6. Place in the pudding mold as with the others and cook
