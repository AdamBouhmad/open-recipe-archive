## Ingredients

- cooked salt cod
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- lemon slices without skin or seeds
- peppercorns
- two tablespoons of oil
- butter the size of an egg
- bread crumbs
- lemon juice
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take a somewhat deep serving dish
2. Place in it some mashed garlic, onion, parsley, lemon slices without skin or seeds, peppercorns, two tablespoons of oil, and a piece of butter the size of an egg
3. Place the cooked salt cod on top
4. Cover with a layer of the same mixture
5. Sprinkle with bread crumbs
6. Place the casserole over low heat so it boils slowly
7. Add a little lemon juice and pepper on top
