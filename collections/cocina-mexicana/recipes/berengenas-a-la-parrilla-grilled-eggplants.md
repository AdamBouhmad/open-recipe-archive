## Ingredients

- Eggplants
- Fine salt
- Coarse pepper
- Fine oil

## Directions

1. Split the eggplants in two lengthwise
2. Keeping the skin on, remove the seeds and liquid
3. Place them on a plate
4. Sprinkle with fine salt and coarse pepper
5. Add fine oil and let them sit for about half an hour
6. Place them on the grill and roast them
7. Take care to baste them with their sauce
8. When cooked, serve them alone or with tomato sauce
