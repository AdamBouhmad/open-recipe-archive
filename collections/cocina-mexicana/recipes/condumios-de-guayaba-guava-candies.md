## Ingredients

- 1 pound guava, peeled and seeded
- 1.5 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the guavas, remove the seeds, and grind them
2. Make a clarified syrup with the sugar brought to the soft-ball stage (punto de juntar en el agua)
3. Mix off the heat
4. Add the ground guava to the syrup off the heat
5. Return the pan to the fire and allow the mixture to reach the hard-ball stage (punto de despegar del cazo o de cajeta bien marcado)
6. Remove from heat and beat
7. Before the mixture sets, pour it onto a wooden board (cajón)
8. Cut the candies into the desired size and shape
