## Ingredients

- meat from roasted birds without bones
- olive oil
- vinegar
- salt
- black pepper
- lettuce hearts
- pickled cucumbers
- chiles
- olives
- hard boiled eggs
- anchovies

## Directions

1. Take the meat without bone from roasted birds and place in a marinade of olive oil, vinegar, salt and black pepper
2. A short time later arrange on lettuce hearts divided in two parts
3. Decorate with pickled cucumbers on top, or chiles and olives, halves of hard boiled eggs cut lengthwise, and strips of anchovies, if available
