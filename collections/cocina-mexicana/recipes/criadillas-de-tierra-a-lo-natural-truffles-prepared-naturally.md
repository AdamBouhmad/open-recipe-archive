## Ingredients

- Best truffles
- Paper

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the best truffles
2. Wrap each one in five or six pieces of paper, moistened
3. Let cook between embers for more than an hour
4. Remove the papers, dry and serve hot in a napkin as a supplementary dish
