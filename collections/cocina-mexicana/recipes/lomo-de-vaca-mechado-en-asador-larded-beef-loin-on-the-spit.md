## Ingredients

- beef loin
- bacon
- oil
- onions
- parsley
- lemon juice
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Remove the nervy skin and fat, clean it properly, cutting the tip.
2. Lard it with bacon on top at the two ends, leaving the middle unlarded.
3. Place in a marinade for several days with oil, onions, parsley, lemon juice and cinnamon.
4. Arrange in the form of an 8 or round, or if not, in the form of a horseshoe.
5. Roast on a spit over good heat until it takes on good color.
6. Serve with the appropriate sauce.
