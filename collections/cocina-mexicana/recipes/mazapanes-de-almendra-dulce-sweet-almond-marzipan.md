## Ingredients

- 3 pounds sweet almonds
- egg white
- 1.5 pounds clarified sugar syrup at flowering point

## Directions

1. Blanch the sweet almonds in hot water
2. Dry them in a sieve after wiping well with a cloth
3. Grind them in a metate, adding egg white from time to time to prevent them from releasing oil
4. Once well ground, put this paste in 1.5 pounds of clarified sugar syrup at flowering point and stir well with a wooden spoon, taking care that it does not stick to the bottom of the pan
5. Know that the mass is at the right point if, touching it quickly with the back of your hand and lifting it promptly, nothing sticks to it
6. Place the paste on a tinned copper plate and dust with sugar all over, letting it rest until it cools
7. Put it on a clean board, cut with molds of the desired shape and form, transfer the cut pieces to paper placing them carefully
8. Bake at a moderate heat, and when cooked on one side, turn them over so they cook equally on both sides
