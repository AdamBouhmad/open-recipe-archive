## Ingredients

- rice
- milk
- chicken breasts, ground
- a pinch of salt
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Well wash a little rice, grind it, strain it with milk and put it to cook with the same
2. Add some ground chicken breasts, a pinch of salt and a little butter
3. When everything is cooked, add sugar according to taste
4. Let it reach its point and empty it onto the platter where it will cool
