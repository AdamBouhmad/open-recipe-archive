## Ingredients

- veal loin
- ham strips
- raisins
- almonds
- pepper grains
- clove
- cinnamon sticks
- salt
- vinegar
- water
- oregano
- lard
- broad chile
- onion
- olives
- chiles

## Directions

1. Lard the loin with strips of ham, raisins, almonds, pepper grains, clove and cinnamon sticks
2. Accommodate in a deep and compact pot
3. Add salt, vinegar, water, oregano, sufficient lard and a little broad chile that has been deveined, soaked and ground
4. Place over heat and when cooked, let it fry in its own lard as the broth is consumed, taking care to turn it
5. Place on a platter, coat with its marinade and adorn with onion slices, olives and small chiles
