## Ingredients

- Two pounds venison meat
- One pound fat ham
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Nutmeg
- Aromatic herbs

## Directions

1. Remove skin and sinews from venison, mince very finely with fat ham
2. Season with salt, pepper, spices, nutmeg, and crushed aromatic herbs
3. Wrap the mixture in caul (see ENCRESPADOS, page 296), making it the size and thickness of an egg
4. Flatten and cook on a grill
5. Serve with a spicy sauce
