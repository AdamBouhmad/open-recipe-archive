## Ingredients

- fourteen egg whites
- four ounces of flour
- sugar
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. To fourteen egg whites, add four ounces of flour.
2. Beat little by little until it breaks apart.
3. Add a pinch of sugar and beat well.
4. Fry in a pan with hot lard, stirring constantly.
5. Place on a platter and roll up like hojuelas.
