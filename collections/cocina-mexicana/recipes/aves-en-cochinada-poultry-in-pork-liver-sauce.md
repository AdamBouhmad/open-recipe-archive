## Ingredients

- Pork liver
- Toasted almonds with skin
- Toasted bread soaked in broth
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pimienta (pepper)
- Nutmeg
- Sugar
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Pound the pork liver with toasted almonds with skin and toasted bread soaked in broth
2. When well pounded, strain
3. Season with cloves, cinnamon, pepper and nutmeg
4. Give it a boil with sugar and lemon
