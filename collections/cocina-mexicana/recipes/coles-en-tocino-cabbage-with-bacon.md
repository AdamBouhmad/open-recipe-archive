## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- salt
- bacon with rind
- meat
- pepper
- roots
- parsley
- onion
- clove

## Directions

1. Wash cabbages cut in quarters, cook them a quarter hour in water
2. Cut bacon with rind into pieces and combine with the cabbages, which are removed a moment later
3. Place them in fresh water, press them and put them to cook together with the bacon and the meat desired, adding salt, pepper, roots, parsley, onion and clove
4. Once the meat and cabbages are cooked, remove them from the broth, place the meat on the platter, cover it with the cabbages and garnish with the pieces of bacon
