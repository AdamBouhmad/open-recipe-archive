## Ingredients

- rebanadas de pan de un dia para otro
- apio picado
- perejil picado
- pimienta
- nuez moscada
- clavo
- canela
- caldo
- sal

## Directions

1. Toast the bread slices from one day to the next and the following day place between each two toasts chopped and mixed celery and parsley
2. Arrange in a deep dish, sprinkle with a little pepper, nutmeg, clove and cinnamon and pour the broth seasoned with salt on top
3. Put the soup over two fires, and as soon as the broth is consumed so it is not very thick, remove it and garnish like common soup
