## Ingredients

- lamb blood
- salt
- lard
- oil
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- parsley
- oregano
- thyme
- chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Put a casserole with water and when it boils vigorously add the blood with little salt and let it cook without falling apart
2. Remove and cut into thin slices
3. Put lard and oil in the frying pan and fry the slices, or rather parboil them, since frying them makes them tough
4. Chop plenty of tomato, onion, and a little garlic and fry it in the same pan where the blood was fried
5. Add water and immediately add the blood
6. Grind clove, cinnamon, and pepper and add a little chopped parsley, oregano, and thyme
7. The broth must be extremely thick
8. Add a little oil and garnish with chiles, olives, and capers
