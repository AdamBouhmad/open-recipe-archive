## Ingredients

- Rice
- Butter or lard
- Garlic cloves (one or two heads according to rice quantity)
- Xitomatl (tomatoes)
- Onion
- Pot broth

## Directions

1. Wash the rice quickly in two or three waters so it does not soak
2. In a pot with lard fry the garlic cloves from one or two heads according to the quantity of rice, removing them after frying
3. Add the rice immediately and let it fry until it is half browned
4. Push it to one side in the same pot and on the other side add some chopped tomato and a whole head of onion, both chopped
5. When the seasoning is fried, mix it with the rice and stir until it is well browned
6. Add already seasoned pot broth and let it cook over low heat without touching it again
