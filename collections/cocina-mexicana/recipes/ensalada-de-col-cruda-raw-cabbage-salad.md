## Ingredients

- cabbage
- water
- vinegar
- oil
- onion
- olives
- capers
- chiles in vinegar
- cucumbers

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage very finely
2. Place it in the open air and change the water repeatedly so it loses its flavor
3. Season with vinegar and oil, sliced onion, olives, capers, chiles in vinegar and cucumbers if available
