## Ingredients

- two and one-half pounds of sugar
- half pound of ground almond
- twenty egg yolks
- eight tablespoons of custard
- one drachm of ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at high point with two and one-half pounds of sugar, and away from the fire add half a pound of ground almond, twenty egg yolks, eight tablespoons of custard and one drachm of ground cinnamon.
2. Incorporate everything, put it on the fire and bring to a boil without ceasing to stir until it reaches the point of coming away from the pan, then pour it into a platter or into boxes.
3. Cut into square pieces and wrapped in papers, this sweet is also usually called Queen's nougat.
4. It can be varied by putting curd cheese in place of the custard.
