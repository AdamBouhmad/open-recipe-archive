## Ingredients

- Brussels sprouts, small and green, the size of a walnut
- salt
- water
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- velvet or curled sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Remove the yellow leaves
2. Cook in salted water
3. Refresh in cold water and drain
4. Serve with a piece of butter, salt and pepper, and if desired, bind the butter by adding a spoonful of velvet or curled sauce
