## Ingredients

- fish
- oil
- garlic heads, crushed
- thyme
- bay leaf
- oregano

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and desalt it if it is salted
2. Cut into pieces that fit in a barrel
3. Fry in oil
4. After frying well, set aside and let cool
5. In fresh oil, fry crushed garlic heads, thyme, bay leaf, and oregano
