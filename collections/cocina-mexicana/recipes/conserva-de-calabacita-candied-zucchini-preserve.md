## Ingredients

- zucchini
- white sugar
- egg white
- lemon
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Remove the thin outer skin from the zucchini
2. Cut into wheels one finger's width thick
3. Remove the seeds with the tip of a knife
4. Place in clean water and stir by hand like making soap suds so they release their foam and impurities
5. Dissolve white and solid sugar in the same water to make a syrup, clarifying it well with egg white and lemon, bringing it to medium point
6. Put the syrup in a pot and add the zucchini wheels
7. Cover to preserve the aroma
8. Cook over gentle heat until the zucchini becomes soft; if it reaches the right consistency, remove it; if the syrup lacks point, remove and finish cooking by adding orange blossom water more for flavor than scent
9. Let the syrup cool
10. Pour into containers and seal when cool, as sealing while hot causes it to become sour or the syrup to thin and lose consistency
