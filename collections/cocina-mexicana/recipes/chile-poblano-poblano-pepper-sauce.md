## Ingredients

- Poblano chiles
- Roasted tomatoes
- Milk
- Cheese

## Directions

1. Grind poblano chiles and fry with roasted and skinned tomatoes but without strips
2. When well fried add milk instead of water
3. Season and add slices of cheese
