## Ingredients

- four calf's brains
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- melted butter
- breadcrumbs
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Soak four calf's brains, divide each one in half, and without blanching them, season with salt, pepper and nutmeg
2. Soak them in melted butter, coat with breadcrumbs, dip in beaten seasoned eggs and breadcrumb again
3. Fry them in a pan over moderate heat, giving them the necessary time to cook, and when done and golden in color, drain on a clean cloth
4. Arrange on a plate with a handful of fried parsley on top
