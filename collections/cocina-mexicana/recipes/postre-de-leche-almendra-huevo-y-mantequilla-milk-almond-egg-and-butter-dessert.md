## Ingredients

- milk
- ground rice
- egg yolks
- powdered sugar
- ground almonds
- butter
- biscuit or sponge cake

## Directions

1. Add a little ground rice and some egg yolks to the milk
2. Sweeten with powdered sugar, strain, and heat
3. When at medium consistency, mix in some ground almonds
4. Let simmer until the milk is nearly at its proper point, then add some butter
5. Butter the plates where the dessert will be served, pour the dessert into them, and top with crumbled biscuit or sponge cake mixed with melted butter
6. Place under a metal sheet with gentle heat to brown the top
