## Ingredients

- roasted garlic
- onions
- capers
- parsley
- oil
- hot water
- lard
- flour
- cinnamon
- clove
- white wine
- cooked beef tongue
- fried bread
- fried plantains
- hard-boiled egg slices

## Directions

1. Roast and grind garlic, onions, capers and parsley, fry everything in oil
2. Once fried, add a little hot water
3. Separately brown a little flour in lard and add to the fry
4. Do the same operation with ground cinnamon and clove
5. Add a glass of white wine to this broth
6. Put the cooked and sliced tongue in this sauce
7. Decorate with fried bread, fried plantains and slices of hard-boiled eggs
