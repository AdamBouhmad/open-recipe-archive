## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter
- flour
- substantive broth
- salt
- fried bread toasts

## Directions

1. After washing the spinach and briefly placing it in hot water, drain it well
2. Fry it in butter, dusting it with a little flour
3. When well fried, add substantive broth
4. Season with necessary salt and let it cook
5. Serve with fried bread toasts
