## Ingredients

- one pound of fine flour
- one spoonful of hard butter
- sufficient water
- one pound of beef marrow

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of fine flour with one spoonful of hard butter and sufficient water to be able to knead it
2. Add one pound of beef marrow that has been cooked to remove the small bones and ground
