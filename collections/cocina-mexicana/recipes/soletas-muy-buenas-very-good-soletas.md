## Ingredients

- twenty-two egg whites
- twenty-two egg yolks
- one pound of powdered and sifted sugar
- one pound of powdered and sifted starch
- finely ground dragée for dusting

## Directions

1. Beat twenty-two egg whites until well risen
2. Add twenty-two egg yolks once the whites are well beaten
3. After beating well, mix in one pound of ground and sifted sugar
4. When well incorporated, gradually add one pound of ground and sifted starch to avoid lumps
5. Drop equal portions with a small spoon onto paper
6. Place the papers on baking sheets and put in a moderately heated oven
7. Leave for a short time, remove, dust with finely ground dragée, and return to the oven to set well
