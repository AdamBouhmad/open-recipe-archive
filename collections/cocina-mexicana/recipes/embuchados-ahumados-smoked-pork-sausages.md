## Ingredients

- large or medium pork intestines
- pieces of onion
- white wine
- salt and pepper
- fine herbs, chopped
- spices
- pork intestines or belly
- pork fat

## Directions

1. Remove the thick end of the intestines and soak for one or two hours in hot water with onion and white wine
2. Wash well in cold water and drain
3. Dry with clean cloth and sprinkle with salt-pepper, fine chopped herbs and spices
4. Leave for two days
5. Cut to desired sausage size
6. Take pork belly, remove fat, and cut into strips the length of the sausage
7. Form the sausages seasoning with all kinds of spices and place in the prepared tripe
8. Dry at the chimney or in a smoky place
9. Store in a covered pot over very gentle heat
10. Let them release their juice
11. Add water with chopped onion and clove, two glasses of white wine, salt and pepper
12. Cook until nearly done and cool in their own broth
13. Remove and grill on the griddle and serve hot
14. Can be served whole or in slices, optionally divided in half and finished cooking in a chickpea stew
15. Serve hot, accompanied frequently with pepper sauce
