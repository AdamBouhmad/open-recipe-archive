## Ingredients

- cabbage
- salt
- butter
- ripe tomato
- garlic
- onion
- pork broth
- pepper
- cloves
- saffron
- cumin
- pork ribs
- lard
- ham
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. After cleaning the cabbages, put them to cook with a little salt
2. When cooked, remove and let cool, then chop into large pieces
3. Put a small pot on the fire with butter and fry ripe tomato, garlic and onion well chopped
4. Once well fried, add pork broth, grind a good amount of pepper, cloves, saffron and a little cumin
5. When it has boiled a little, season with fine salt and add pork ribs, pieces of lard and some slices of ham
6. Put on low heat, add a little good vinegar and let reduce until almost dry
