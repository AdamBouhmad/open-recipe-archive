## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- water
- salt
- chorizos
- tomatoes
- onions
- parsley
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron
- beet
- carrot
- eggs
- lard
- olives
- capers
- chiles
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Wash and clean the feet well, then cook with water and salt
2. Once clean, mince as if it were other meat
3. Cook chorizos and cut into slices
4. Fry tomatoes without seeds with onions and minced parsley, and let refry a lot
5. Add clove, cinnamon, pepper and strands of saffron, water and salt
6. Add the feet and let boil until the water is consumed
7. Then add the sliced chorizos
8. Cook a beet and a piece of carrot; after cooking, cut into small pieces and add with everything else
9. Let boil until the liquid is completely consumed
10. Beat eggs as for frying and add to the picadillo
11. In a separate pan with lard, when it is well fried, add the cake which is put at two heats until it sets
12. When golden, place on a platter and garnish with lettuce and onion slices
13. Whole and minced olives, capers and chiles should be added to the picadillo
