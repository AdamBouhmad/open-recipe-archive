## Ingredients

- Eight ounces of violet flowers
- One ounce of purple iris powder
- Twelve ounces of sugar
- Six quartillos of water

## Directions

1. Crush eight ounces of violet flowers in a marble mortar
2. Put them in a vessel with one ounce of purple iris powder
3. Dissolve twelve ounces of sugar in six quartillos of water
4. Pour over the flowers, taking care to seal the vessel hermetically
5. Leave in infusion for four hours
6. After which pass the mixture through a sieve and freeze
