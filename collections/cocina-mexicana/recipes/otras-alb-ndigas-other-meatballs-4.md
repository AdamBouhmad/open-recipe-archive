## Ingredients

- loin meat
- solid meat
- two or three cooked eggs
- salt
- clove
- cinnamon
- ground pepper
- aged cheese
- chopped onion
- chopped parsley
- butter
- roasted almonds
- fried bread
- tomatoes

## Directions

1. Chop the loin and solid meat
2. In a casserole mix with two or three cooked eggs, salt, clove, cinnamon and ground pepper, aged cheese, chopped onion and parsley
3. Mix well and form meatballs in a pan with butter and fry
4. In a separate casserole heat butter with chopped onion, garlic and one or two tomatoes and fry
5. Toast almonds in a comal, grind them and fry in the same casserole, then add water
6. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper and a piece of fried bread with a little chopped parsley and thyme
7. Let boil and when thick add the meatballs, let simmer a few minutes and serve
