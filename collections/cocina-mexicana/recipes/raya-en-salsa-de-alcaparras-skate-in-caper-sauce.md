## Ingredients

- Skate
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- caper sauce

## Directions

1. Remove the bile from the skate, wash it and place to cook with salt, pepper and onions
2. Clean the skate afterwards
3. Heat it with a little of the broth in which it was cooked
4. Serve with caper sauce
