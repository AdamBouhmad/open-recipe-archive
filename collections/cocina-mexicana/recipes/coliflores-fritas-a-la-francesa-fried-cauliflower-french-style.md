## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- thick white sauce
- frying batter
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower until almost done, with about one quarter of the cooking still remaining
2. Make a thick white sauce and fry the cauliflower in it
3. Allow to cool
4. Cover or soak with frying batter
5. Fry in moderately hot butter until golden
6. Remove and arrange on the plate
7. Serve
