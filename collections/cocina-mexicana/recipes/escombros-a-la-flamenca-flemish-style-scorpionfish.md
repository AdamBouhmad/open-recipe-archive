## Ingredients

- Scorpionfish
- Butter
- Parsley, chopped
- Onions, chopped
- Shallots, chopped
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Lemon juice
- Oil

## Directions

1. Clean and gut the scorpionfish, tie them by the head, and cut off the tail tip without splitting along the back
2. Fill the belly of each fish with a piece of butter mixed with chopped parsley, onions, shallots, salt, black pepper, and lemon juice
3. Wrap each fish in a sheet of buttered paper, tied at both ends with twine, and oil them well
4. Place on a grill and leave for three quarters of an hour over gentle and even heat
5. When cooked, remove the paper, arrange on a plate, and pour over them the butter from the paper
6. Serve with lemon juice
