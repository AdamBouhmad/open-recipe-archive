## Ingredients

- four ounces spirits of wine
- four drams guaiacum
- two drams quina powder

## Directions

1. Place ingredients inside a well-sealed bottle, shaking it twice a day
2. After eight days pass through a fine cloth
3. Use by putting ten or twelve drops in half a glass of warm water and rinse four or five times per week
