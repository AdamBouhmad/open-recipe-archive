## Ingredients

- Cooked fish (codfish or snook)
- Garlic
- Onions
- Tomatoes
- Butter
- Parsley
- Clove
- Pepper
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked fish
2. Mix with chopped garlic, onions, and tomatoes, all fried in butter
3. Add chopped parsley, clove, and pepper powder
4. Mix everything with beaten eggs, incorporating well
5. Make the small cakes as the previous ones
6. Serve dry or in adobo
