## Ingredients

- Lamb
- Roasted tomatoes
- Cooked tomatoes
- Raw onions
- Roasted garlic
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Green cooked chiles
- Lard
- Oil
- Wine
- Good vinegar
- Salt
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cooked small onions
- Cinnamon powder
- Small chiles
- Olives
- Cooked ham pieces (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb as is customary.
2. Grind roasted tomatoes, cooked tomatoes, raw onions, roasted garlic, oregano, thyme, and green cooked chiles.
3. Fry all ground ingredients in lard.
4. Place the lamb in this frying mixture.
5. To make the sauce, add oil, wine, and good vinegar.
6. Season with corresponding salt, clove, and pepper.
7. When at good consistency, add cooked small onions to the stew.
8. Serve with cinnamon powder on top, small chiles, and olives.
9. Optional: add pieces of cooked ham.
