## Ingredients

- small squashes
- tequezquite water
- potatoes
- peas
- beans
- mulato chiles
- garlic
- cumin
- vinegar
- oil
- thyme
- salt
- onion
- fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Cook small squashes in tequezquite water
2. Cook potatoes, peas and beans each separately
3. Soak mulato chiles, devein and grind with six garlic cloves and cumin
4. Place the chile in a dish with the cooked vegetables, considerable vinegar and oil, thyme, salt, a little chopped onion and fresh cheese dusted with a grater
