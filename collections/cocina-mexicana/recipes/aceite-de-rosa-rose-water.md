## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of rose leaves
- two quartillos of warm water
- two quartillos of good brandy
- half a pound of clarified sugar
- one ounce each of cinnamon, nutmeg peel, and cilantro

## Directions

1. Infuse one quarter pound of rose leaves in two quartillos of warm water
2. Leave for two or three days
3. Pass through a sieve
4. Add two quartillos of good brandy, half a pound of clarified sugar, one ounce each of cinnamon, nutmeg peel, and cilantro
5. Leave for at least ten days
6. Filter
