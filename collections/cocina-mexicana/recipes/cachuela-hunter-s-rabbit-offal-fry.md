## Ingredients

- Ham slices
- Rabbit livers, hearts, and kidneys
- Eggs (optional)
- Parsley, chopped
- Green chiles

## Directions

1. Place ham slices in a cazuela (earthenware pan) and fry in the fat they render
2. When the ham pieces are browned, add thin slices of raw rabbit livers, hearts, and kidneys, or those previously cooked in salted water
3. Add beaten eggs to the mixture if desired, stirring together with the fry
4. Add chopped parsley and green chiles
5. Serve once the organs are cooked through
