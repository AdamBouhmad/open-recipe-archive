## Ingredients

- Auauhtle (spirulina)
- Shrimp, cleaned
- Cilantro, dried and toasted
- Chile seeds, without veins
- Toasted bread
- Garlic cloves, roasted
- Clove, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Pepper, ground
- Eggs, beaten
- Lard
- Tlemole

## Directions

1. Toast a small amount of auauhtle well, then grind it with clean shrimp, dried toasted cilantro, chile seeds without veins, toasted bread, roasted garlic cloves, and ground clove, cinnamon and pepper
2. Mix with beaten egg prepared as if for frying and well combined, put in a buttered baking dish
3. Place to cook at two gentle fires
4. When the cake has set, divide it in the same dish into pieces
5. Add a little more lard, fry it well and cook in well-fried common tlemole
