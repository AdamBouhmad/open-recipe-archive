## Ingredients

- pork loin
- ham
- pepper
- cinnamon
- garlic cloves
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- bread crumbs
- lard

## Directions

1. Lard a pork loin with ham, pepper, cinnamon, and garlic cloves
2. Place in a pot with water, vinegar, and salt
3. Remove and place in a pot, allowing it to finish cooking over two fires until the broth is consumed
4. When ready, serve with bread crumbs toasted and browned in lard
