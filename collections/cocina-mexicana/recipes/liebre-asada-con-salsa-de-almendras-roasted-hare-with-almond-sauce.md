## Ingredients

- Hare
- oil
- onions
- almonds
- pepper
- bread
- salt
- vinegar
- oregano

## Directions

1. Golden chopped onions in oil and add ground almonds with pepper and toasted bread fried, seasoning with necessary salt and adding a little oregano
2. Place the hare, already roasted raw, in this sauce with a little vinegar, and drying it sufficiently, serve the hare with this same sauce over it
