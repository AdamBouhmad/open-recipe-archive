## Ingredients

- Four poultry breasts
- Soft bread crumbs soaked in milk
- Butter
- Four egg yolks
- Salt
- Pimienta gorda (coarse pepper)
- Nutmeg
- Two egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Scrape the meat from four poultry breasts with a knife, removing all skin and sinews
2. Grind and pass through a quenelles sieve, placing results in a plate
3. Soak soft bread crumbs in milk
4. Press well in a fine cloth or new dishcloth to extract the milk
5. Grind the bread crumbs
6. Take an equal quantity of ground bread as the separated meat
7. Add butter in the same quantity as the bread
8. Grind both until no trace of butter remains
9. Add the poultry meat and grind everything together until perfectly mixed
10. Add four egg yolks with salt, coarse pepper and a little nutmeg
11. Grind slightly to mix
12. Add two beaten egg whites to the paste
13. Before removing from the mortar, test a small amount to check seasoning
