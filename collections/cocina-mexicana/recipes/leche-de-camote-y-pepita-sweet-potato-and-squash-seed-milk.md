## Ingredients

- eight cuartillos of milk
- four ounces of cooked and sieved white sweet potato
- four ounces of ground squash seed dissolved in milk
- orange blossom water
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Sweeten eight cuartillos of milk well
2. Strain and place over heat
3. Add four ounces of cooked and sieved white sweet potato
4. Allow to boil until it thickens
5. Add four ounces of ground squash seed dissolved in a little milk
6. Allow it to reach the proper point
7. When removed from heat, sprinkle with orange blossom water, which incorporates quickly
8. Leave a moment over the heat and pour into serving dishes
9. Decorate with cinnamon powder
