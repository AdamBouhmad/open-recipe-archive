## Ingredients

- xitomates crudos
- manteca
- ajos picados
- cebollas rebanadas
- rajas de chile verde
- sal
- clavo
- azafrán
- pimienta molida
- azúcar
- vinagre
- pollos cocidos
- jamón en lonjitas
- salchicha
- longaniza
- caldo
- aceitunas
- chilitos
- tornachiles curados

## Directions

1. Grind raw tomatoes and fry in butter with chopped garlic, sliced onions and strips of green chile
2. Season with salt, clove, saffron and ground pepper, sugar and vinegar
3. Place the cooked chickens in this sauce with strips of ham, pieces of sausage and longaniza, and the broth in which they were cooked
4. Cover the cazuela with a griddle, comal or sheet of tin with heat on top
5. Let the stew season over two fires; when seasoned, add olives and small chiles or cured chiles
