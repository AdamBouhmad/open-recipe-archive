## Ingredients

- Thirty or forty chestnuts
- Butter
- Consumed broth

## Directions

1. Remove the shell from thirty or forty chestnuts and place them in a frying pan with a little butter
2. Fry and toss them over the fire until the second shell or thin skin that covers them falls off by itself
3. After cleaning them, cover them with consumed broth in a pot and let them cook for an hour and a half
4. When well cooked, pass them through a sieve
