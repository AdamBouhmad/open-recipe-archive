## Ingredients

- very red beets, not fibrous
- salt
- pepper powder
- oil
- vinegar
- raw onion, sliced

## Directions

1. Roast very red beets that are not fibrous in an oven or grill.
2. Cut them into slices, seasoning with salt, pepper powder, oil, vinegar and raw sliced onion.
