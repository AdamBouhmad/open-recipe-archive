## Ingredients

- pork loin, minced
- tomato, minced
- garlic, minced
- green chile, minced very fine
- salt
- hard-boiled egg, in whole slices
- almonds, halved
- clove
- pepper
- ginger
- parsley, leaves removed
- wine
- lard

## Directions

1. Make a picadillo of pork loin with tomato, garlic, green chile minced very fine, and fry in lard, adding sufficient salt, whole slices of hard-boiled egg, halved almonds, clove, pepper, ginger, and parsley with leaves removed.
2. Add a little wine, and stirring well, when it becomes a bit dry, place it in papers well coated with lard, tying them with twine.
3. In a pot with more lard, place them at gentle heat, taking care to turn them. As soon as you know they are cooked, set them aside, and letting them rest for some hours, remove the twines and papers, making slices of them, which rolled in flour will be fried again.
4. Serve with the following sauce, or with whichever sauce best suits: having made some bits of salt pork, grind them together with raisins and capers, then fry in the same lard left from the salt pork. Add clove, pepper, cinnamon, raisins and halved almonds, toasted sesame, cumin, a little wine, a small lump of sugar, and let it boil. If the broth remains too thin, thicken it with fried and ground bread.
