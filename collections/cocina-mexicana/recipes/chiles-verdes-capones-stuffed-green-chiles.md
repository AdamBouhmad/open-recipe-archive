## Ingredients

- Large green chiles
- Fresh cheese
- Epazote leaves
- Lard
- Salt
- Butter or lard for serving
- Thin tortillas

## Directions

1. Select large green chiles, roast them and peel them
2. Cut a little below the stem, about halfway down the chile, in such a way that the head is not removed, and remove the veins from the top
3. Crumble fresh cheese and chop some epazote leaves, mixing them together well with the cheese
4. Fill the chiles through the head with the cheese mixture
5. Fry the filled chiles in lard until they are well cooked
6. Remove and drain them
7. Dip thin tortillas in hot lard or butter seasoned with salt
8. Wrap each cooked chile in a tortilla and arrange on a platter
