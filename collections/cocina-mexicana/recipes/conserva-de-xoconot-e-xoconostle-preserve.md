## Ingredients

- Xoconostles
- Fine clarified syrup
- Coconut paste or other filling
- Orange blossom water and scent

## Directions

1. Peel and cook the xoconostles
2. Deflame like citron by changing water daily until very white and soft
3. Remove the crown and dry the seed without breaking them
4. Place in fine well-clarified syrup at medium point, filled with coconut paste or other filling
5. Bring to preserve point and add orange blossom water and scent
