## Ingredients

- two quartillos of milk
- two pounds sugar
- six ounces peeled and ground almonds

## Directions

1. Sweeten the milk with sugar and add the peeled and ground almonds
2. Let it all boil until when stirred you can see the bottom of the pan, or until it reaches the cajeta point
3. Remove from heat, beat it, and pour into molds or form the panochitas as in the previous articles
