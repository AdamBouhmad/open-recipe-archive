## Ingredients

- pork sirloin meat
- salt
- ground pepper
- a little clove
- cinnamon
- Malaga wine or equivalent

## Directions

1. Mince the sirloin meat very finely, as it is the most suitable because it is the most tender
2. Season with salt, ground pepper (some add it whole), a little clove, cinnamon and Malaga wine or equivalent
3. Knead, taste and stuff into wide pork intestine
4. Put to air for a few days
5. After that press between two wooden boards, putting weight on top or in another way as seems convenient
6. When it is well dry keep it in a cool place
