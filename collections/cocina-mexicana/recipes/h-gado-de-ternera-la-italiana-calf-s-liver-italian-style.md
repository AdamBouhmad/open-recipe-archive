## Ingredients

- veal liver pieces
- good oil
- salt
- Italian sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Sauté pieces of liver in a saucepan with good oil
2. Dust with salt, turning without breaking them
3. Drain after cooking well and place on a platter
4. Pour over an Italian sauce and lemon juice
