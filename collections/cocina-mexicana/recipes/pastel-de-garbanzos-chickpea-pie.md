## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- tequesquite water
- cold bread
- lard
- egg yolks
- tomatoes
- onions
- parsley
- oregano
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron
- cumin
- raisins
- almonds
- capers
- fish pieces
- olives
- sugar
- cinnamon for dusting

## Directions

1. Cook the chickpeas in tequesquite water, then wash with hot water and grind with a little cold bread fried in lard without toasting it, so that they remain in small grains
2. Grind some cooked egg yolks with more cold bread, and make a mass of everything
3. For the filling fry lard, tomatoes, tomatoes and onions, all chopped with parsley, oregano, clove, cinnamon, pepper, saffron and cumin (the spices ground), raisins, almonds, capers, small pieces of fish and olives
4. When everything is well fried and thick, coat a baking dish with lard and extend on the bottom and sides part of the chickpea mass, flattened like a tortilla
5. Cover the baking dish with a comal with fire and let the mass cook alone
6. Then put the sauce or hash and cover with another layer of flattened mass as said before, and cover again with the comal with fire
7. When everything is cooked, decorate the top of the pie with small figures of the same mass, made the same way as the rest, and serve sprinkled on top with ground sugar and cinnamon
