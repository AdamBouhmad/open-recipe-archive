## Ingredients

- sole fish
- parsley
- onion
- salt
- black pepper
- nutmeg
- butter
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Cut off the heads and tails, then gut and clean the fish
2. Put in a sauté pan or skillet
3. Sprinkle with chopped and washed parsley and a little chopped onion, salt, black pepper, and grated nutmeg
4. Warm or melt a piece of butter and add it on top
5. At the moment of serving, place over high heat, stir so they do not stick, and when cooked on one side, turn to the other
6. Arrange on a plate and sprinkle with Italian sauce
