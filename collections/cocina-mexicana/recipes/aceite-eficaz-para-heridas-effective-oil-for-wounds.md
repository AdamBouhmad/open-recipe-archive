## Ingredients

- one pound good olive oil
- one pound very finely ground sugar

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of good olive oil with one pound of very finely ground sugar
2. Put the mixture in a vessel and stir well before putting it on the fire, which at first should be gentle
3. When the sugar has melted increase the fire, making sure it continues to boil and move constantly
4. When it becomes a reddish color and caramelized it is a sign that the oil is nearly ready, with only ten or twelve minutes remaining
5. Apply the balm oil as hot as possible after washing the wound with warm water, soaking it in a compress which is placed on top
