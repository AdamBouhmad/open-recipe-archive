## Ingredients

- pork feet
- pork tongues
- salt
- ham strips
- small chorizo
- peas
- tender green beans
- beets
- carrots
- cauliflower
- brains
- burned lard
- minced onion
- roasted tomatoes
- ground garlic
- cloves
- pepper
- saffron
- cumin
- capers
- olives
- broth
- beaten eggs
- ground sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the pork feet and tongues with salt, strips of ham, small chorizo, peas and tender green beans, and separately cut beets, carrots, cauliflower and brains
2. In burned lard fry minced onion, roasted tomatoes and ground garlic, and once fried add the deboned feet in small pieces, and the tongues with skin removed and sliced, the ham in strips, the small chorizo divided, the beans, beets, carrots, cauliflower and brains, all in small pieces, cloves, pepper, ground saffron and cumin, capers, olives and a little broth, so that all this boils
3. Once seasoned and dry, mix in beaten eggs, and in a casserole or pan well lined with lard, pour it to place over two gentle fires, and as soon as it is set and releases from the pan, place it on a plate and sprinkle with ground sugar
