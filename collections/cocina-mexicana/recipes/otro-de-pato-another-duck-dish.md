## Ingredients

- ducks, prepared as previously described
- butter
- two or three tomatoes
- two bunches of onion
- parsley, finely chopped
- flour
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare the ducks as in the previous recipe
2. Heat butter in a casserole and add the ducks, tomatoes, bunches of onion, and finely chopped parsley
3. Fry everything together
4. Add flour and fry a little more
5. Add water
6. Grind cloves, cinnamon, and pepper and add to the pot
7. The broth should remain thick
8. Garnish as in the previous recipe
