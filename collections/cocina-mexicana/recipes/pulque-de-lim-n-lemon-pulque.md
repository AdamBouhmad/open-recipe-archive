## Ingredients

- one or two lemon peels
- lemon juice
- pulque
- sugar

## Directions

1. Scrape one or two lemon peels with a sugar lump according to quantity
2. Squeeze the juice in the proportion of one lemon for two quartillos of pulque
3. Sweeten immediately
