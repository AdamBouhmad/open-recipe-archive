## Ingredients

- One pound of sugar
- one and a half pounds of citron, grated and completely desalted

## Directions

1. To one pound of sugar add one and a half pounds of citron
2. Grate the citron and completely desalt it
3. Cook and remove bitterness
4. Keep set aside
5. Mix in ground and strained sweet potato
6. Place over heat until it reaches its proper stage
