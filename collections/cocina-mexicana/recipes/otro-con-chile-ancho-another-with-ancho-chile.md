## Ingredients

- Oil
- Lard
- Tomato
- Onion
- Fish
- Garlic
- Ancho chiles
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Cumin

## Directions

1. Heat oil and lard with tomato and onion in proportion
2. In the mixture place the fish slices
3. While they give the first boil, grind two cloves of garlic, two ancho chiles, a stick of cinnamon, some peppercorns, and a little cumin
4. Mix this from the grinding stone with plenty of oil
5. Beat everything with a fork and pour it over the fish, which will be placed on a gentle fire, covering the pan
