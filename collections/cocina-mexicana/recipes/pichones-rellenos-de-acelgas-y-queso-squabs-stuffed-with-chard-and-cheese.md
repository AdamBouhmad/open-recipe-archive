## Ingredients

- chard
- salt
- cheese, grated
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- nutmeg
- hard biscuit, ground
- butter
- squabs, cooked

## Directions

1. Cook the chard in salted water
2. Drain, chop and squeeze it
3. Mix with grated cheese, cloves, cinnamon, pepper, nutmeg powder, ground hard biscuit and a little butter, incorporating everything
4. Stuff the cooked squabs with this mixture
5. Place them in a dish rubbed with butter and salt
6. Cook at two fires, taking care to brush them with butter from time to time until they are well browned
