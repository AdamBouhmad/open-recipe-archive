## Ingredients

- Onion
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- Exotes (green beans)

## Directions

1. Cut the onion into small squares and fry in butter
2. When well fried, dust with flour and add a little broth
3. Let it boil and when partly reduced, add the exotes
4. Let them boil a little, remove from heat and serve
