## Ingredients

- pork ribs
- Spanish sauce
- tomato sauce
- butter
- gherkins

## Directions

1. Flatten as if they were veal
2. Be careful not to leave much fat
3. Place on the grill turning a few times so they cook perfectly without browning
4. Serve with Spanish sauce or with tomato sauce, with browned butter and sliced gherkins
