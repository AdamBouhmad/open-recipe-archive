## Ingredients

- tocino in pieces
- small diced onion
- leg of mutton
- salt
- pepper
- warm water
- parsley
- mint leaf
- lettuce heart
- pine nuts, soaked
- spices
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fry the bacon pieces with a little finely diced onion
2. Cut the meat into pieces and add to a pot with salt and pepper
3. Braise over gentle heat
4. Add warm water
5. When cooked, add chopped parsley, a mint leaf, lettuce heart, soaked pine nuts, and spices all finely minced
6. When serving, add a little lemon juice
