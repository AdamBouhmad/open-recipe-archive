## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs, chopped
- beef broth with substance
- eggs
- parsley, fried

## Directions

1. Reduce the potatoes to a paste as in the previous article.
2. Strain them and season with salt, pepper and chopped fine herbs.
3. Moisten with beef broth with substance.
4. Form small balls, dip them one by one in well-beaten eggs and fry them.
5. Serve adorned with fried parsley.
