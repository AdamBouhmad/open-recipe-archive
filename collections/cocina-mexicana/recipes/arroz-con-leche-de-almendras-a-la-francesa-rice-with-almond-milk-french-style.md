## Ingredients

- almonds, half a pound of sweet almonds and six bitter almonds
- milk, six cuartillos total
- rice

## Directions

1. Place the almonds in a pot with water over the fire until nearly boiling, to remove their skins
2. Once peeled, place the almonds in cold water
3. Grind the almonds in a mortar
4. Place them on the fire in a pot with two cuartillos of milk
5. Place afterward in a fine cloth
