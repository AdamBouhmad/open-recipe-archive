## Ingredients

- fattened pullets
- chickens
- partridges
- other birds
- veal picadillo
- ham slices
- lemon juice or mushroom stock

## Directions

1. Stuff the poultry with fine, well-seasoned veal picadillo
2. Line a pot or marmite with ham slices and the same picadillo mixture
3. Place the stuffed birds in the pot
4. Cook over coals in the marmite until the sauce reduces somewhat
5. Transfer to a baking dish, bread the birds, and brown in the oven
6. Serve with lemon juice or mushroom stock
