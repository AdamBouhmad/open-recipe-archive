## Ingredients

- mayonnaise
- mustard
- egg yolks
- small onions
- parsley
- capers
- pickled gherkins optional
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare a mayonnaise
2. Incorporate into it while stirring vigorously for a long time one tablespoon of mustard for every two egg yolks used
3. When the mixture is made, add three or four small onions and one tablespoon of minced parsley
4. Add some capers and if desired some minced pickled gherkins
5. Season with salt and pepper in greater quantity than for mayonnaise
