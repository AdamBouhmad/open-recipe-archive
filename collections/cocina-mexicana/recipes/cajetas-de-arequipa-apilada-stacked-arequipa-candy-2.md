## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- seven cuartillos of milk
- four ounces of peeled and ground almonds
- one ounce of sifted starch
- one pound of sugar
- one ground and strained pineapple

## Directions

1. Put two pounds of sugar in seven cuartillos of milk with four ounces of peeled and ground almond and one ounce of sifted starch
2. Put on the fire and let it reach the point
3. Then add another pound of sugar and one ground pineapple strained through a serviette
4. Let it boil until it reaches the candy point
