## Ingredients

- partridges, cleaned
- oil
- bread crumbs
- lettuce

## Directions

1. After cleaning, place raw partridges on the grill
2. Roll them with oil
3. Roll them in bread crumbs and return to the grill to brown
4. Serve with lettuce salad, not chopped
