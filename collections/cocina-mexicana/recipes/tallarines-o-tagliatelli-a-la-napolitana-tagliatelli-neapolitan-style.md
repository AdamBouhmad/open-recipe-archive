## Ingredients

- flour
- salt
- water
- butter
- grated cheese
- pepper
- cream

## Directions

1. Make a dough with flour, salt and water, extend it as thin as possible to the size of a napkin folded into four folds.
2. Cut into strips half a finger wide and place in boiling water, letting them boil several times.
3. Remove and place in cold water, draining in a sieve.
4. Once well drained, prepare as follows: place butter and grated cheese at the bottom of a plate with a layer of tallarines seasoned with pepper on top.
5. Add another layer of butter and cheese, alternating layers until all are used, then pour a glass of cream over the top.
6. Finish with another layer of cheese and cook over two fires.
