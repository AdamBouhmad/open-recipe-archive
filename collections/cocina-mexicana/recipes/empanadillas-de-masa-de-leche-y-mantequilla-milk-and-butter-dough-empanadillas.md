## Ingredients

- Two pounds of flour
- One cup of butter and lard, melted and cooled in equal parts
- Six egg yolks
- Half a small cup of anise water
- Half a small cup of settled tequesquite water
- A little salt
- Sufficient milk
- Almond paste or preserves for filling
- Ground sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of flour with one cup of butter and lard, melted and cooled in equal parts, six egg yolks, half a small cup of anise water, half a small cup of settled tequesquite water, a little salt, and sufficient milk so everything is well mixed
2. Knead until blisters form on the dough, adding milk if it dries during kneading
3. Extend with a rolling pin
4. Cut the empanadillas
5. Fill with almond paste or preserves
6. Fold and crimp
7. Fry in butter
8. When removed from the oil, dust with ground sugar and cinnamon
