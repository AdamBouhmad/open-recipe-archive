## Ingredients

- beef square with ribs
- salt
- black pepper
- mushrooms
- parsley
- scallions
- garlic
- warm butter
- bread crumbs
- clear substance or thin broth
- verjuice
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Cut a square of beef, dividing the ribs without leaving them too long
2. Place these ribs to marinate for an hour with salt, black pepper, mushrooms, parsley, scallions, a hint of garlic, and warm butter
3. Keep the marinade at hand near the ribs, cover them with bread crumbs, and place on the grill over gentle heat, rolling them with the rest of the marinade
4. When cooked and nicely browned, serve over a sauce made of clear or thin substance, two spoonfuls of verjuice, salt, and black pepper. They can also be served without sauce
