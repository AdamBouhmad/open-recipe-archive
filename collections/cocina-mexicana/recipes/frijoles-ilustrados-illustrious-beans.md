## Ingredients

- dark beans
- lard
- clove
- cinnamon
- chiles
- olives
- chorizo
- longaniza
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the dark beans without tequesquite, after cooked empty into a cazuela
2. Take a pan and fry with plenty of lard and all kitchen seasonings
3. Grind clove and cinnamon, add chiles, olives, chorizo, longaniza and a little vinegar
