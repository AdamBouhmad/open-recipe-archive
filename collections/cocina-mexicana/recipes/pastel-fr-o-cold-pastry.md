## Ingredients

- dough
- ham slices
- filling
- meat
- salt
- pepper
- ground aromatics
- ground spices

## Directions

1. Prepare the dough as in the hot pastry article
2. Cover the bottom with ham slices
3. Spread the filling over the slices
4. Place the meat on top of the filling, adding salt, pepper, ground aromatics and a little ground spices
5. Fill any gaps left by the meat with some of the same filling or sauce that was under it
6. Cover everything with ham slices
7. Raise the dough giving it an agreeable form and decorate it
8. Cover the pastry with the same dough, also decorating and glazing the cover
9. Put the pastry in a well arranged and somewhat warm oven, being careful not to brown or toast too much
10. For the filling: half fry the meat in a pan, then chop it and grind with ham, salt, pepper, spices and ground aromatics
11. For each pound of meat, one and a half pounds of ham is necessary
