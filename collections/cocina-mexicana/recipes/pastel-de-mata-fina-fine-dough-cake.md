## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- fourteen egg yolks
- one pound of butter
- half pound of ground sugar

## Directions

1. Take two pounds of flour
2. Add fourteen egg yolks and one pound of butter, adding the butter a little at a time, with half a pound of ground sugar
3. Knead everything, and the consistency of the dough should be like a pearl
4. Extend a little dough on the plate as if making tortillas and then wrap the rest of the dough around it
5. Before doing so, separate a little of the same dough and make long strips with which to form around the wrapper some braids that serve as decoration
