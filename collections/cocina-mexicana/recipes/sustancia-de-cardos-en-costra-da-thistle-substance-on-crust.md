## Ingredients

- Crumb of large bread loaves
- Butter
- Thistle substance

## Directions

1. Make slices with an incision on the edges from bread crumb
2. Toast in butter and drain
3. When the thistle substance is hot, add as much butter as a pigeon egg
4. Remove the interior crumb from the toasts
5. Replace it with the thistle substance
