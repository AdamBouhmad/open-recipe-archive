## Ingredients

- onions
- light beef and ham stock
- stronger strained stock
- mustard sauce (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the onions in the embers
2. Peel them and place in a pan
3. Moisten with light beef and ham stock
4. When they have simmered for some time, bind the stew with stronger strained stock
5. When serving, a little mustard sauce may be added, especially when this stew accompanies appetizers with onions
