## Ingredients

- plate of frijoles parralefíos
- tequesquite water
- two hard biscuits
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- eggs
- lard
- honey or panocha

## Directions

1. Take the quantity that fills a plate of frijoles parralefíos, soak in tequesquite water for twelve hours, and after that wash in two or three waters
2. Cook and grind with two hard biscuits, a little clove, cinnamon, and pepper
3. Knead everything, put it in beaten eggs, and take portions with a spoon that will be fried in lard and served with honey of panocha
