## Ingredients

- six ounces of flour
- twenty egg yolks
- competent amount of milk
- sugar to taste
- two lemons
- one stick of cinnamon
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients into the milk and break up with your hand
2. Pass everything through a sieve and place on the fire
3. Peel two lemons and add the peels when it comes to a boil with a cinnamon stick
4. Cook until it reaches the consistency of manjar blanco
5. Pour into a cold dish
6. Sprinkle sugar on top and burn with a hot iron
