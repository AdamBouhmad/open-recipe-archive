## Ingredients

- Four pounds flour
- One and a half cups melted lard
- One piece of yeast the size of an egg, dissolved in a little warm water
- One cup syrup
- One small bowl white wine
- Four eggs
- A little oil
- Aged cheese
- Cinnamon
- Sugar
- Saffron
- Cloves
- Pepper
- A little roasted cilantro
- Two or three eggs, well beaten

## Directions

1. Knead four pounds flour with one and a half cups melted lard, one piece of yeast the size of an egg dissolved in a little warm water, one cup syrup, one small bowl white wine, four eggs, and a little oil
2. After kneading well, dust with a little flour
3. Extend with a rolling pin
4. Cut the dough with a round mold to form small pastries
5. Fill them with a paste made from aged cheese, cinnamon, sugar, saffron, cloves, pepper, and a little roasted cilantro, all very well ground and mixed with two or three well beaten eggs
6. Place the quesadillas in the oven until they are cooked
