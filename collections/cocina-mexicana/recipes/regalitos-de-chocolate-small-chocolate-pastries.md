## Ingredients

- 15 meringue shells
- 1 pound chocolate
- water
- powdered sugar
- a little chocolate in the plate for serving

## Directions

1. Prepare 15 meringue shells made with the mass explained in its place
2. Dissolve 1 pound of chocolate in water
3. Place a little chocolate in the plate where they are to be served and arrange the small pastries on their side where they were filled
4. Put on the fire dusted with fine sugar
5. Cook gently for approximately half an hour
6. When glazed with the sugar, remove and serve
