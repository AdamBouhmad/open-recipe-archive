## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- one pound of butter
- one pound of powdered sugar
- four well-beaten eggs
- three ounces of sifted starch
- cold water as necessary

## Directions

1. Knead together the flour, butter, powdered sugar, well-beaten eggs, sifted starch, and cold water as needed
2. Form small cakes
3. Bake in the oven
