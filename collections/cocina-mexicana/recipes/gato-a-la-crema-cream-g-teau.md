## Ingredients

- about one pound of flour
- one quarter liter of double cream or the thickest cream that forms on the surface of milk
- a little salt
- just over half a pound of butter
- egg for brushing

## Directions

1. Place about one pound of flour on a table and make a hollow in the center
2. Pour in one quarter liter of double cream or the thickest cream from milk, and a little salt
3. Lightly work the dough and let it rest for half an hour
4. Add just over half a pound of butter
5. Knead five times as for puff pastry
6. Form one large gâteau or many small ones
7. Brush with beaten egg and bake in the oven
8. Adjust ingredient quantities proportionally based on the desired amount of gâteaux
