## Ingredients

- 4 quartillos brandy
- 0.5 ounce celery seed
- 2 quartillos water
- 1.25 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Distill the brandy with the celery seed and extract 2 quartillos of liqueur.
2. Dissolve the sugar in water, form the mixture and filter.
