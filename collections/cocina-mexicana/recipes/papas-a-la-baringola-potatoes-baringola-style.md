## Ingredients

- Medium-sized raw potatoes
- Beef or vigil broth and water
- A little oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Roots
- Some onions
- Parsley sprigs
- Oil, vinegar, salt and pepper for the sauce

## Directions

1. Peel the raw potatoes of medium size and put them to cook in beef or vigil broth and water with a little oil, salt, pepper, roots, some onions and parsley sprigs
2. When cooked and with no more broth remaining, let them fry in the oil and take on good color
3. Serve with a sauce made of oil, vinegar, salt and pepper
