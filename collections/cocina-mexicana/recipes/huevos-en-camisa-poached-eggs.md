## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- vinegar
- eggs

## Directions

1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan over high heat with a little salt and a splash of vinegar.
2. When it boils vigorously break the eggs one after another and drop them into the pan, so that the yolk falls wrapped in the white, which coagulates in less than a minute, if the water continues to boil.
3. When the yolk is already solidified, remove the egg with a skimmer.
4. Have a sauce prepared beforehand, according to the taste of each one, which is poured very hot over the eggs and served.
