## Ingredients

- puff pastry
- macaroni
- bread crumbs
- grated cheese
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut thin puff pastry into small strips
2. Wrap the strips to form small cords
3. Arrange on a mold, buttering them one by one so they form a spiral until the mold is covered with dough
4. Fill with macaroni
5. Sprinkle the top with equal parts breadcrumbs and grated cheese
6. Put the timbale in a moderately hot oven
7. Bake for three-quarters of an hour until well cooked and golden
8. Turn onto a plate and serve immediately
