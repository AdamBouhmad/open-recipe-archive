## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- onions
- five or six cloves of spice
- beef
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Put vegetables such as carrots, turnips, and onions in a saucepan, filling it only three-quarters full
2. Add five or six cloves of spice according to the amount of gelatin you want to make, being careful to use more turnips and onions than carrots
3. Place the beef on top and moisten everything with broth or water
4. Let the vegetables cook over gentle heat, using the same procedures as in the previous recipe
