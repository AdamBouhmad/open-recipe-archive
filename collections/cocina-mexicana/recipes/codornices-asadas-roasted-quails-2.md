## Ingredients

- quails
- butter
- salt
- bread crumbs
- pepper
- egg yolks
- feathers for applying

## Directions

1. Give the quails a turn in the roaster first
2. Prepare a saucepan on the fire with butter
3. Add salt, a little bread crumbs, a little pepper, and for every two quails, one egg yolk
4. Apply this mixture to them with feathers
5. Turn until golden brown
