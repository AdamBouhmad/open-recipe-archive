## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- beef or meatless broth
- potatoes, raw, peeled and in thin pieces
- salt
- pepper
- parsley and other herbs

## Directions

1. Make a roux of good color with butter and flour in the pot.
2. Dissolve it with beef or meatless broth.
3. Add the raw peeled potatoes in thin pieces.
4. Add salt, pepper, parsley sprigs and other herbs.
5. They can be served alone or with appropriate roasted meat.
