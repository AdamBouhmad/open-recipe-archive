## Ingredients

- one measure rice
- three measures water
- salt
- fresh or browned butter

## Directions

1. Pour one measure of rice into three measures of water in which salt has been dissolved
2. Boil vigorously in a well-covered pot over a very hot fire
3. When the rice has burst and is cooked, make some holes with the handle of a wooden spoon
4. Introduce fresh or browned butter through these holes
5. It penetrates and seasons the rice
6. Serve immediately after removing excess fat
