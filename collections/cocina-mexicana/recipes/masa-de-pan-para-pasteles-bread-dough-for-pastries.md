## Ingredients

- beef marrow, ground
- bread
- lard
- two or three eggs
- salt
- pitted raisins

## Directions

1. Grind beef marrow, bread, and lard together
2. After grinding well, knead and add two or three eggs, appropriate salt, and some pitted raisins
3. Grind everything well again and form the pastel as described in its place
