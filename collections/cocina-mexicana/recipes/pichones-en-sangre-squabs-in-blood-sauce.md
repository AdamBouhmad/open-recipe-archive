## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Lemon juice or vinegar
- Blood from the squabs
- 2-3 egg yolks
- 2-3 tablespoons milk

## Directions

1. Place a small amount of lemon juice or a splash of vinegar in a small dish
2. When slaughtering the squabs, allow the blood to fall into the dish
3. Prepare the squabs as for compote
4. Use the blood as a binding agent, adding 2-3 egg yolks and 2-3 tablespoons of milk
5. Pass everything through a sieve
