## Ingredients

- beef pieces
- salt

## Directions

1. Cut into slices the pieces of beef that you want to salt and arrange them in layers or beds in the salting place, in the same way as is done with pork.
2. The beef will absorb and admit as much salt as is added to it, unlike pork which only takes the salt it needs.
