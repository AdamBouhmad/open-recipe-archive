## Ingredients

- flour
- egg yolk
- rice
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make a dough like that of gaznates and a bit more loaded with flour, but not too dry.
2. Extend it with a rolling pin over flour powder, above and below; cut the wheels with a plate, according to the desired size.
3. In the middle of each one, place rice and honey as is customary.
4. Fold the other half and seal the edges.
5. Have sugar half ground and turn them in it so they take it on.
6. Place them on tin sheets and put them in a well-tempered oven.
