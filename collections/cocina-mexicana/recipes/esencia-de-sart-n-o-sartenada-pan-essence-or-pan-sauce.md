## Ingredients

- 2 pounds veal, cut into thick cubes
- 2 pounds ham, cut into thick cubes
- 2 large carrots
- 3 onions
- 1 pound butter
- juice of 3 or 4 lemons
- 3 or 4 cloves
- basil
- 2 bay leaves, crushed
- thyme
- pepper
- salt
- broth

## Directions

1. Cut veal, ham, carrots, and onions into thick cubes
2. Place everything in a large saucepan over the fire
3. Add butter, lemon juice, cloves, basil, crushed bay leaves, thyme, pepper, and salt
4. When this has been consumed, add a ladleful of broth from the bottom of the pot
5. When reduced to half by boiling, remove from the fire
6. Pour into a glazed earthenware vessel for use as needed
