## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Bay leaf
- Lemon juice
- Flour
- Onion
- Clove
- Mushrooms
- Breadcrumbs
- Egg
- Fried parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the squabs as in the previous recipe
2. Parboil them in butter with salt, pepper, nutmeg, a bay leaf, and lemon juice
3. When well parboiled, add a little flour to the butter
4. Add an onion, a clove, and chopped mushrooms
5. Allow everything to boil for a few minutes
6. Remove the squabs and reduce the sauce
7. Remove the onion and bay leaf and pour the sauce over the squabs
8. Allow everything to cool
9. Once the squabs are coated with sauce and cold, roll in breadcrumbs
10. Coat with beaten egg seasoned with salt and pepper
11. Cover a second time with breadcrumbs
12. Fry until they turn a beautiful color
13. Serve with fried parsley
