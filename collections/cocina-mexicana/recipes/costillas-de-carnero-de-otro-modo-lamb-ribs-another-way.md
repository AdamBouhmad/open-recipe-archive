## Ingredients

- Lamb ribs
- Bacon fat
- Lemon juice
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Remove the backbone, leaving all the meat possible.
2. Strike the ribs well with the knife and place on a grill to roast slowly.
3. Continuously baste with a feather dipped in bacon fat and lemon juice.
4. If a little breadcrumbs are added, it is very good for those with poor appetite.
