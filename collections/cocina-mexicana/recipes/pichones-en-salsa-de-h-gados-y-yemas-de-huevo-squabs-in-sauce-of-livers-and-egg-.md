## Ingredients

- cooked livers
- cooked egg yolks
- lard
- chopped garlic
- chopped onions
- salt
- cloves
- pepper powder
- chopped parsley
- oil
- vinegar
- squabs
- squab cooking broth
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Grind the cooked livers with cooked egg yolks
2. Fry chopped garlic and onions in lard, then add the ground mixture
3. Season the frying with salt and add powdered cloves and pepper, chopped parsley, oil and vinegar
4. Place the squabs with their cooking broth in this mixture
5. Let simmer so the broth becomes well seasoned
6. When removing from heat, add small chiles and olives
