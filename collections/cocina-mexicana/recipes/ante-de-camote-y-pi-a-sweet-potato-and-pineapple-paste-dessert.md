## Ingredients

- Sweet potato, one pound
- Pineapple, one pound
- Sugar, three pounds

## Directions

1. Grind both the sweet potato and pineapple well
2. Make syrup first, then mix in the paste
3. Put on heat until it reaches the proper point
4. Arrange layers of sponge cake as in the previous recipe
