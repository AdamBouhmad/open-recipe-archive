## Ingredients

- radishes
- marjoram
- cinnamon
- eggs
- lard
- syrup
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Slice the radishes and let them dehydrate in water for twelve to fifteen hours
2. After that time, grind them and mix with ground marjoram and cinnamon
3. Make this paste in well-beaten eggs, and mixing everything, take spoonfuls of the dough that will be fried in lard and served with syrup and cinnamon powder on top
