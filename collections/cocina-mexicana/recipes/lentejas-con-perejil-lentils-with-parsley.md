## Ingredients

- Lentils
- water
- salt
- butter
- parsley
- pepper
- fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cook in water and salt, drain and place in a pan with abundant butter, chopped parsley, salt, and a little pepper.
2. Sauté for a few minutes and serve with fried bread crusts, all very hot.
