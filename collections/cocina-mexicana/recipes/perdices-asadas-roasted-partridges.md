## Ingredients

- partridges, cleaned
- butter
- tomato, ground
- garlic, chopped
- cilantro, toasted and ground
- pepper, ground
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the whole cleaned partridges
2. Fry them in butter
3. Add ground tomato, chopped garlic, toasted and ground cilantro, ground pepper, and a little vinegar
4. Place over low heat and let consume until they brown in their own fat
