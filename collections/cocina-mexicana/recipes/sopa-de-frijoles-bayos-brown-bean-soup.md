## Ingredients

- Brown beans
- Broth
- Two carrots
- Two onions
- Two cloves
- Pot fat
- Bread slices
- Lard
- Flanders cheese or other good cheese, grated
- Cooking oil

## Directions

1. Cook these beans in broth with two carrots, two onions, two cloves and some pot fat
2. When everything is well cooked, arrange bread slices fried in lard in another tureen
3. Strain the bean broth over them and let it boil a little
4. When it begins to dry, add some grated Flanders cheese or other good cheese and a little cooking oil
5. Remove from heat and place a griddle on it for a while with little fire
