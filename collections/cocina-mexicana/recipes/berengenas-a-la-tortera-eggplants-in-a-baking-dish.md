## Ingredients

- Eggplants
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Butter

## Directions

1. Divide the eggplants in two halves
2. Remove the hearts
3. Chop these hearts very finely and add chopped parsley and garlic
4. Put everything in a saucepan with butter and let it cook
5. Arrange the eggplants in a baking dish
6. Fill them with the chopped mixture
7. Put them to cook in the oven at two heats
8. Arrange them and serve hot
