## Ingredients

- Rabbit slices
- Truffles
- Butter
- Pepper
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Reduced demi-glace or velvet sauce
- Game essence
- Egg yolks
- Toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut the rabbit slices into two-inch pieces
2. Make five or six incisions in each piece
3. Insert truffle pieces cut in semicircles into the incisions
4. Fry in butter with pepper, salt and nutmeg
5. Drain and place in a sauce made with reduced demi-glace or velvet sauce and game essence, bound with egg yolks and butter
6. Arrange for serving with buttered toasted bread around the plate
