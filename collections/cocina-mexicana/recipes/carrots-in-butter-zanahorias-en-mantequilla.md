## Ingredients

- Carrots, thick as a coin of four
- Butter
- One quarter-liter of broth
- Salt
- A pinch of sugar
- Fine herbs
- One tablespoon of curled sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the carrots that have the thickness of a coin of four, and blanch them for five minutes
2. Mix them in a saucepan with a little butter, one quarter-liter of broth, salt and a pinch of sugar, and let them boil
3. When you observe that they are almost cooked, let them reduce to gelatin, and then add a little more butter, fine herbs, and one tablespoon of curled sauce
4. Let them boil once so that everything is well incorporated, and serve them adorned with fried croutons
