## Ingredients

- 1 pound bitter almonds
- 4 egg whites
- 3 pounds powdered sugar
- additional egg whites as needed

## Directions

1. Place 1 pound of bitter almonds on a clean cloth and rub them to remove dust
2. Grind them in a marble mortar with 4 egg whites, being careful not to grind them too much and to prevent them from turning into oil
3. Transfer the ground mixture to a bowl and incorporate 3 pounds of powdered sugar
4. If the paste is too dry, add egg whites, ensuring it is neither too liquid nor too thick
5. Arrange small portions the size of a walnut on sheets of paper
6. Bake the macaroons in the oven at a very gentle and steady heat
