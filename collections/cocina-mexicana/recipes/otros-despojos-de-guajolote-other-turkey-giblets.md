## Ingredients

- Turkey giblets
- Butter or lard
- Flour
- Broth or water
- Pepper
- Salt
- Bundle of mixed herbs
- Turnips
- Fried potatoes

## Directions

1. Cut the giblets into pieces and sauté in butter
2. Dust with flour and moisten with broth or water
3. Season with pepper, salt and a bundle of mixed herbs
4. Cook, adding two turnips that have been sautéed in butter until they take color
5. Drain and garnish with fried potato slices
