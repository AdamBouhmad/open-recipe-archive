## Ingredients

- ancho chiles
- pasilla chiles
- clean peanuts
- clove
- cinnamon
- lard
- salt
- meat
- meat broth

## Directions

1. After deveining and toasting ancho and pasilla chiles, grind them together with clean peanuts, clove and cinnamon
2. Fry the ground mixture in lard and season with salt, add the meats to be cooked with the broth in which they were cooked, and leave to boil until it reaches a good consistency, so that it is neither too watery nor too thick
