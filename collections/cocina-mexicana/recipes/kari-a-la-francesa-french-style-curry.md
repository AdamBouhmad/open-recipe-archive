## Ingredients

- beef tendons or other meat
- butter
- saffron
- bay leaf
- hot chiles
- pork
- flour
- broth with mushrooms
- artichoke hearts
- pearl onions
- rice
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Prepare the meat and vegetables as in the Indian-style recipe
2. When the stew is cooked, remove the meat and mushrooms from the sauce
3. Add egg yolks to the sauce without letting them boil
4. Strain through cheesecloth
5. Pour the sauce over the stew to serve
