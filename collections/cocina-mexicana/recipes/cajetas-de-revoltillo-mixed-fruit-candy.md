## Ingredients

- equal parts grated pear, peach, and apple, cooked and sifted
- one pound of sugar for each pound of mixture

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts grated pear, peach, and apple, cooked and sifted through a sieve
2. Make a half-point syrup with one pound of sugar for each pound of mixture
3. Add the fruit mixture and cook to a point slightly higher than the wrinkle point
4. Pour into candy molds
