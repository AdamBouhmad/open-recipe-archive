## Ingredients

- Lard
- Squabs
- Ham slices
- Flour
- Ground garlic
- Chopped onion
- Water
- Fresh chopped parsley
- Ground clove
- Ground cinnamon
- Ground pepper
- Four to six egg yolks
- Lemon juice
- Chiles
- Capers

## Directions

1. Heat lard in a casserole
2. When very hot, add the squabs
3. After browning well, add ham slices
4. Sprinkle flour and ground garlic, add chopped onion
5. When fried, add water and fresh chopped parsley, ground clove, cinnamon, and pepper
6. When the meat is well cooked and broth is thick, beat four to six egg yolks with a little lemon juice
7. Add the egg mixture very slowly to prevent curdling
8. Serve with chiles and capers
