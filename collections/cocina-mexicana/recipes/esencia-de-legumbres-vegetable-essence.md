## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 pounds beef
- 1 old hen
- 1 beef shank
- 2 or 3 dozen carrots
- 2 or 3 dozen onions
- 2 or 3 dozen turnips
- 2 or 3 lettuce heads
- chervil
- a few celery stalks
- cloves

## Directions

1. Place beef, old hen, and beef shank in a proportioned pot
2. Add carrots, onions, turnips, lettuce heads, chervil, celery stalks, and cloves
3. Fill the pot with broth
4. Let it boil, proceeding as for making concentrated broth
5. When the meats are cooked, strain the essence through a sieve
6. Let it reduce a little if it does not have sufficient consistency
