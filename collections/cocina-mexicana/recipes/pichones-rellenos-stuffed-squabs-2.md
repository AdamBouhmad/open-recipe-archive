## Ingredients

- squabs
- veal
- breast of poultry
- ham
- onion, fried
- eggs
- parsley
- black pepper
- cumin, ground
- paper

## Directions

1. Remove the wings and necks from the squabs and trim the tail, making a fine minced filling of veal, breast of poultry, ham and fried onion; add eggs, parsley, pepper and ground cumin.
2. Stuff two squabs with this mixture and cross the feet over the breast so the filling does not fall out.
3. Then wrap in kraft paper and roast in a pot between two fires.
