## Ingredients

- Ducks, peeled and tail removed
- Water treated with tequesquite
- Onion tops
- Salt
- Whole onions
- Morcilla filling
- Toasted bread
- Cooked lettuce
- Lard
- Oil
- Garlic cloves, cleaned
- Duck broth
- Clove, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Pepper
- Salt
- Good vinegar
- Carrot, sliced
- Beet, sliced
- Onion
- Potatoes, cooked and fried
- Raisins
- Capers
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Peel the ducks, remove the tail, and infuse in water treated with tequesquite and onion tops for eight to ten hours.
2. Cook in water with salt and two whole onions.
3. Once cooked, remove them.
4. Grind a little morcilla filling with toasted bread and cooked lettuce.
5. Heat equal parts lard and oil in a pot, brown clean garlic cloves there, fry the ground mixture, and add broth from the cooking liquid.
6. Grind and add clove, cinnamon, little pepper, and necessary salt.
7. Once boiled, add good vinegar, and when the broth thickens, remove from heat.
8. Arrange the ducks on a platter and pour this sauce over them.
9. Garnish with sliced carrot, beet, onion, cooked and fried potatoes, raisins, capers, and almonds.
