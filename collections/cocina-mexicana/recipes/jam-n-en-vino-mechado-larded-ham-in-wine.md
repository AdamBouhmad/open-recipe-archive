## Ingredients

- ham
- cinnamon
- cloves
- whole pepper
- water
- wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean the ham and lard it with cinnamon, cloves and whole pepper
2. Place it in a pot to cook with a little water and the same amount of wine
3. Cover the pot
4. Once the ham is cooked, remove it and skin it
5. Pour coarse sugar on top, which is burned with very hot plates
6. Dust with more sugar, cloves and cinnamon, all ground
