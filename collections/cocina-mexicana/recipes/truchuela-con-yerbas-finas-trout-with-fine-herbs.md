## Ingredients

- trout
- fine herbs
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- aromatic powders
- white wine
- bread crumbs
- good cheese

## Directions

1. Gut and wash the trout and prepare it well
2. Place it on a plate with cooked fine herbs and butter
3. Add salt, pepper, nutmeg, and aromatic powders in powder form
4. Moisten with a bottle of white wine
5. Dust the trout with bread crumbs, sprinkle with melted butter, and place in the oven to cook
6. While cooking, baste it regularly with its cooking liquid
7. Cover with breadcrumbs mixed with grated good cheese, sprinkle with melted butter, and brown under a dome oven
8. Serve with Italian sauce (see ITALIANA)
