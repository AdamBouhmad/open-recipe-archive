## Ingredients

- Two egg yolks
- Salt
- Three or four drops of edible oil
- One clove of garlic
- One tablespoon of Castilian vinegar
- More oil
- Lettuce
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Place two egg yolks in a pot with a little powdered salt and beat well with a clean and dry wooden spoon.
2. Add three or four drops of edible oil and continue beating.
3. Continue adding oil in this manner until it falls in a stream.
4. Crush one clove of garlic, add it, and continue beating until the oil is incorporated with the egg.
5. Add one tablespoon of Castilian vinegar, beat again, and add more oil.
6. These operations must be done without stopping beating, as the sauce breaks very easily.
7. When it is like a purée, it is done.
8. Finely chop a little lettuce and prepare it like a salad, adding a little powdered pepper; form it in the bowl like a cone.
9. Once the fish or chicken is prepared, cooked, and deboned, place the lettuce mixture on top to cover the piece and above it the prepared sauce, to which slices of boiled egg are usually added.
