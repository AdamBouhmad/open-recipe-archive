## Ingredients

- cooked egg yolks
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- toasted sesame
- wine
- white fish
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind cooked egg yolks and season with clove, cinnamon, pepper, toasted sesame, and wine, leaving it a consistency suitable for filling fish
2. Wash the fish and coat with salt
3. Fill with the mixture
4. Cover with a batter for frying fruits
