## Ingredients

- salt
- pepper
- 2 cloves
- nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon flour
- water
- 1 piece of butter the size of a walnut

## Directions

1. Season with salt, pepper, 2 cloves and nutmeg, 1 tablespoon flour dissolved in water and a piece of butter the size of a walnut
2. Allow the sauce to form over heat, stirring and keeping it more watery than thick
3. After boiling for a little more than a quarter hour, remove from heat and mix in 1 pound of butter, sliding it in little by little and stirring always so it does not turn into oil
4. Add a little vinegar and pass through cloth over a water bath
