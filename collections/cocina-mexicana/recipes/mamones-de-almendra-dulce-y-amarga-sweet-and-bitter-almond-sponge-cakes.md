## Ingredients

- one pound almonds soaked in cold water overnight
- a handful of bitter almonds
- twenty egg yolks
- ground sugar
- sixteen egg whites
- two tablespoons ground starch
- ground biscuit

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of almonds in cold water overnight and grind well
2. Add a handful of ground bitter almonds and mix well with twenty beaten egg yolks, beaten more than for royal eggs
3. Mix in ground sugar so the mixture is heavily sweetened
4. Beat sixteen egg whites and add two tablespoons of ground starch
5. Mix everything together and add ground biscuit
6. Pour into a pan buttered with fresh butter
7. Cover the pan with a griddle with fire and cook
8. Test doneness by piercing with a straw
