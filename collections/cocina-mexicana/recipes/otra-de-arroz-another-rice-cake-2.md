## Ingredients

- cooked rice with salt
- cloves
- pepper
- cumin
- ground saffron
- onion
- garlic
- tomatoes
- green chile
- lard
- salt
- raisins
- almonds
- olives
- capers
- sliced chorizo
- ham pieces
- cooked pork loin
- beaten eggs
- sugar

## Directions

1. Cook the rice with salt until it becomes thick, and when it cools, mix in the cloves, pepper, cumin, ground saffron and onion, garlic, tomatoes and green chile, all minced very finely and fried in lard with salt, raisins, almonds, olives, capers, sliced chorizo, ham pieces and cooked pork loin, and beaten eggs
2. Incorporate everything and pour into a well-buttered casserole, place in the oven or over two gentle fires, and when it is set, remove it and sprinkle with sugar
