## Ingredients

- Lard to grease
- Dough like ravioli dough
- Flour
- Wine and sugar
- Ground meat filling
- Chickens, cooked
- Flour and lard for coating
- Artichoke bottoms
- Cauliflower, cooked
- Beaten egg for glazing
- Sugar mixed with cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grease a pot with lard and line it with a not-too-thin sheet of dough made like ravioli dough
2. Use two more ounces of flour for dusting the table and adding to the dough to make it waxy
3. Add wine and sugar to the dough
4. On this sheet place a thick layer of prepared ground meat filling
5. Arrange the chickens on top, cooked in quarters if large or whole if small
6. Roll them in flour and fry them
7. Intersperse artichoke bottoms and cooked cauliflower between them
8. Cover with another thick layer of the same filling and another sheet of dough
9. Join the edges with the dough that extends beyond the pot, seal and crimp them
10. Decorate the top with small figures or flowers of the same dough
11. Glaze with beaten egg
12. Put the pot in the oven until the cake cooks, taking care it does not burn
13. When the dough sheets are cooked, remove from the oven and before it cools, dust with sugar mixed with cinnamon
