## Ingredients

- artichokes
- bacon
- oil
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Choose tender artichokes and use only the hearts, cutting them in four pieces
2. Fry diced bacon and finely sliced bacon, and there add the artichokes
3. They are also fried in oil, coating them first in a batter made of egg yolks with a bit of sugar
