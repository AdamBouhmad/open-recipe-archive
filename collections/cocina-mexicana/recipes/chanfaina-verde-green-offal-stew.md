## Ingredients

- asadura (offal)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- ajos (garlic)
- manteca quemada (burned lard)
- peregil molido (ground parsley)
- pan (bread)
- pimienta (pepper)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- aceite (oil)

## Directions

1. Cook the offal in water with salt and mashed garlic (one clove per offal) and chop it after
2. Fry chopped garlic and onion in burned lard
3. Add plenty of ground parsley with bread fried in lard, garlic and pepper
4. When this has fried a little, add the chopped offal
5. After frying, add the cooking broth which should be strained, vinegar and abundant oil
6. Let it boil until the small broth reaches the proper consistency
