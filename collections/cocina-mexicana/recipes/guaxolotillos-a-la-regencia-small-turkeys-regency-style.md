## Ingredients

- Small turkeys
- Butter
- Thin ham
- Ham slices
- Onion
- Carrot
- Salt
- Pepper
- Bouquet garni
- Concentrated broth
- Gelatin
- Financière garnish

## Directions

1. Empty, singe and gather the birds as previously described
2. Fry in butter so their meat becomes somewhat firm
3. Lard the belly with thin ham
4. Cook in a casserole with ham slices, onion, carrot, salt, pepper and a mixed bouquet garni, moistening with concentrated broth but ensuring the larded part does not get wet
5. When cooked, untie them, cover with gelatin and serve on a financière garnish
