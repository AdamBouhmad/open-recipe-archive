## Ingredients

- cooked chickpeas
- lard
- salt
- minced ham
- ham slices
- burned lard

## Directions

1. After cooking the chickpeas, grind one part
2. Have minced ham and ham slices prepared
3. Grease a pot with lard
4. Add half of the ground chickpeas and salt
5. Add the minced ham with the whole chickpeas and ham slices
6. Top with the other half of the ground chickpeas
7. Put on the fire and baste with burned lard
