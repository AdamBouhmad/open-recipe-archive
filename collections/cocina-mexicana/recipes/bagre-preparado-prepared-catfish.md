## Ingredients

- catfish
- lemon
- lard
- tomatoes
- garlic cloves
- fresh parsley
- onion bunches
- clove
- pepper
- oil
- water
- capers
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the catfish in water and once soaked, scrape it with a knife to remove the dark parts
2. Once clean, cut it into slices without breaking it and squeeze some lemons over it
3. Wash again and dry well with a napkin, then fry in lard
4. Roast some tomatoes, peel some garlic cloves and grind them with the tomatoes and a bit of fresh parsley
5. Fry everything together with two or three bunches of whole onions
6. Once well fried, add water with a pinch of clove and pepper, and a good quantity of oil
7. Add the fish and then capers and chiles in vinegar
8. The broth should be thick
