## Ingredients

- chestnuts
- sugar for caramel syrup

## Directions

1. Remove the first skin and boil in water until the second skin can be removed
2. Let them drain and dry a little in the oven
3. Make a syrup to the point of caramel, keeping it hot over gentle heat
4. Add the chestnuts one by one, turning them with a fork or small fork
5. When removed, insert a pointed stick in each one to let it dry in a wicker basket, placing the stick between the rods so the caramel can dry in the air
