## Ingredients

- veal
- carrots
- garlic
- ham
- cloves
- pepper
- water
- vinegar
- aromatic herbs
- oil
- thyme
- marjoram
- bay leaves
- oregano from Castile
- salt

## Directions

1. Lard the veal with carrots, garlic, ham, cloves and pepper, and place it to cook with water, a little vinegar, garlic and a bundle of aromatic herbs
2. Once well cooked, remove it and roll it in a napkin, pressing it very tightly, and leave it in a press for a full day
3. Then slice it, place it in oil and vinegar of which about a quarter part will be vinegar with thyme and marjoram all ground, plenty of bay leaves, abundant oregano from Castile, garlic fried in oil and cooked carrots
4. All of this with the corresponding salt is placed in a cured pot but one without lard, cover it with a napkin and over it an inverted plate
5. In this state place the pot in the open air for eight days, after which it can be eaten
