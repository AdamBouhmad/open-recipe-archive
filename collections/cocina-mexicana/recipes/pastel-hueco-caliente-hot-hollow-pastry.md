## Ingredients

- Plegada dough (folded dough for puff pastries)
- dry flour

## Directions

1. Form the crust with the plegada dough mentioned in the previous article for puff pastries
2. fill it with dry flour
3. cook it over embers or with two fires
4. once cooked, remove the flour and fill with desired filling
