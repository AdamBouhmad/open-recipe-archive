## Ingredients

- Lettuce, finely chopped
- White lettuce hearts
- Radishes
- Cooked carrots
- Cooked beets
- Raw radishes
- Walnuts
- Pine nuts
- Candied citron
- Dried plantain
- Roasted peanuts
- Ripe plantains
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Roasted garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Jicama slices
- Lime slices
- Orange slices
- Pear slices
- Small chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Chop the lettuce very finely and soak it in water, soaking white lettuce hearts in the same water
2. Cut radishes into quarters
3. Chop cooked carrots and beets, and one or two raw radishes
4. Peel walnuts and pine nuts
5. Chop candied citron and dried plantain
6. Clean roasted peanuts
7. Slice some ripe plantains
8. Remove lettuce from water and mix everything together well
9. Make a sauce of vinegar, oil, roasted garlic molded with salt and pepper
10. Do not add this dressing to the salad until it is about to be served
11. Place in a platter and garnish with lettuce hearts, radishes, slices of jicama, lime, orange, pear, and larger pieces of dried plantain, small chiles, washed olives and capers
