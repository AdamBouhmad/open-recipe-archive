## Ingredients

- Green chile, deveined
- Xitomatl (tomato)
- Onion
- Garlic
- Bread slice
- Lard
- Broth or water
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pimienta (pepper)
- Salt
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Chop green deveined chile, xitomatl, onion and garlic very finely
2. Burn a bread slice in lard
3. Grind and fry with the chopped ingredients
4. Add broth or water
5. Season with ground cloves, cinnamon, pepper and salt and a piece of sugar
6. When thickened enough, pour over the fried poultry that will be very hot
