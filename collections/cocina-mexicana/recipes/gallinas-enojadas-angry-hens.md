## Ingredients

- Lard
- Small walnuts
- Garlic
- Cumin
- Cooked hen quarters
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry small walnuts, garlic and cumin, all ground, in lard in a casserole
2. Add the quarters of cooked hens with abundant oil and a little vinegar
3. When everything has boiled and the hens are well seasoned, remove and serve
