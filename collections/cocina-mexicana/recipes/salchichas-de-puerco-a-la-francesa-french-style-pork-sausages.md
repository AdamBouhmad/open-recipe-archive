## Ingredients

- pork meat from the best parts such as sirloin and fat or jowl in equal quantities
- capon or large chicken breasts
- beef meat
- shallots
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- fine breadcrumbs
- thin pork or sheep intestines

## Directions

1. Prepare pork meat from the best parts such as sirloin and fat or jowl in equal quantities
2. Optionally mix in capon, chicken or beef to make the hash more delicate
3. Chop everything finely
4. Add shallots, salt, pepper, spices, fine herbs and a little very fine breadcrumbs
5. Fill thin pork or sheep intestines which have been prepared, cleaned and washed well with this hash
6. Prick from place to place with a pin to release any air that may have been introduced when making them
7. When full, blend the meats with the hand and tie according to the desired size
8. To eat, place with or without paper on a grill to cook and serve fried in a pan with ham and wine as a supernumerary dish or as a garnish for other large pieces
