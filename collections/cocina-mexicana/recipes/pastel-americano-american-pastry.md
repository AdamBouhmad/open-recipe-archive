## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- one pound of dried prunes
- one pound of flour
- two whole eggs and two yolks only
- salt
- three ounces of powdered sugar
- one small cup of tequesquite water
- about twelve ounces of butter

## Directions

1. Clarify one pound of sugar and once clarified add one pound of dried prunes
2. Let it reach a high point, and when at this point remove the pits
3. Place one pound of flour on a board, make a mound and in the center put two whole eggs and two yolks only, a pinch of salt, three ounces of powdered sugar, one small cup of tequesquite water, and about twelve ounces of butter
4. Knead everything very well and let it rest a bit
5. Meanwhile butter a pan
6. Roll out one third of the dough to the thickness of a thick cigar and the shape of the pan
7. Cook over two fires and when done place the preserve and cover with another part of the dough that is also rolled out
8. Place again to brown, and when it is nearly done coat with a small amount of butter
9. Decorate with the rest of the dough as desired and add the remaining butter (a pound total)
10. Place again to finish cooking and brown well
11. Serve hot
