## Ingredients

- Roasted partridges
- Broth from a stew or soup
- White wine
- Green garlic, chopped
- Lemon zest
- Spanish sauce
- Fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Cut the limbs from roasted and cooled partridges and remove the skin.
2. Slowly heat them in broth from a stew or soup.
3. In a separate pot, boil white wine with chopped green garlic, lemon zest, and a bit of Spanish sauce, letting it reduce by half.
4. Arrange the partridge limbs on a platter, filling the gaps with fried bread crusts.
5. Pour the prepared sauce over everything.
