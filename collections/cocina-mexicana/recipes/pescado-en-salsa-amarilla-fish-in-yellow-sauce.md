## Ingredients

- Oil
- Lard
- Tomatoes
- Peppers
- Garlic
- Onion
- Water
- Clove
- Black pepper
- Tabasco pepper
- Fish
- Saffron
- Bread

## Directions

1. Shortly before serving, put a pan on the fire with equal quantities of oil and lard in proportion to the amount of fish
2. As soon as it boils, add chopped tomatoes, peppers, garlic, and onion
3. When fried, add water to cover the fish
4. When it boils hard, season with ground clove, black pepper, and ground Tabasco pepper
5. Place the fish in the broth to cook
6. Add ground saffron until it takes on a light yellow color
7. Take a slice of bread that should have been soaked beforehand and grind it again with the fish sauce
8. Add the bread to the stew and let it fry so it does not remain raw
