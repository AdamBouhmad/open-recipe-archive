## Ingredients

- Cacaloxochil flowers
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Place the flowers in water and keep them there overnight
2. Give them three boils in another water and then return them to cold water
3. Remove from there and add to clarified syrup of more than half point with the water in which they were cooked
4. Let them boil in it until they reach the preserve point
