## Ingredients

- cooked spinach
- fresh butter
- lemon zest
- almond biscuits made with bitter almonds
- sugar
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Pound the cooked spinach in a mortar with fresh butter, lemon zest, some bitter almond biscuits, sugar, and orange flower water
2. Fill the tostones with this mixture and prepare them as with the other types
