## Ingredients

- any meat
- white wine
- vinegar
- water
- ground spices
- raisins
- almonds
- capers
- pickled capers
- olives
- ham
- salt
- parsley
- sugar
- butter
- eggs
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut meat into small pieces and place in a pot with a little white wine, a little vinegar, and about half as much water.
2. Add ground spices of all kinds, raisins, almonds, capers, pickled capers, olives, and ham, all in small pieces.
3. Add if desired everything that usually goes into minced meat, without forgetting salt above all.
4. Bring to boil, then let finish cooking on gentle heat with the pot covered, stirring with the pot itself so it does not burn.
5. When everything is well cooked, remove from heat and add a little parsley leaves half-ground and a pinch of sugar.
6. Turn out the contents of the pot into a pan with butter and mix with beaten eggs in sufficient quantity.
7. Let set at two equal heats.
8. When serving, sprinkle on top with ground cinnamon and sugar, and garnish around with lettuce heads.
