## Ingredients

- Raw coffee
- Cream
- Egg yolks
- Sugar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Take a quarter pound of raw coffee for one quarter liter of cream
2. Brown it without toasting in a clean pan
3. Add it to the boiling cream, cover well and strain this infusion
4. Add egg yolks in the proportion indicated in the previous article, sugar, and a little salt
5. Finish as the preceding recipe
