## Ingredients

- Calf brains
- Flour
- Lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Butter
- Herbs, chopped
- Vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Boil the brains lightly
2. Let them cool
3. Cut into three pieces
4. Cook with flour and lard for half an hour, seasoning the sauce with salt and pepper
5. Place on a platter
6. Separately melt butter with flour, chopped herbs, salt and plenty of pepper
7. Add vinegar or lemon juice well incorporated
8. Coat the brains with this sauce when serving
