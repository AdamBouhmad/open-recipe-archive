## Ingredients

- corn
- rendered lard
- salt
- water of settled tequesquite
- flour
- fresh or aged cheese
- dried epazote
- pork cracklings fat
- chile
- brains

## Directions

1. Cook corn with little water and when cooked remove it and let it cool
2. Grind it and sieve it, and the sifted powder is mixed with a bit of melted lard
3. Add salt and a bit of water of settled tequesquite and beat so the mass is of good consistency
4. Form small tortillas with it, sprinkle a bit of flour on them, and place in the center crumbled fresh or aged cheese and a bit of dried epazote, or fill with other substances such as pork cracklings fat ground with chile, brains, etc.
5. Fold the tortilla afterward pressing the edges so it sticks and sprinkle with another bit of flour
6. Fry in very hot lard, bathing them from above so they puff up
7. When golden remove them and well drained of lard, eat them hot
