## Ingredients

- pig's feet
- egg
- sesame
- garlic
- water
- breadcrumbs
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper

## Directions

1. Clean well and let cook from the night before the day in which they are to be cooked
2. Remove the broth and cover with beaten egg as if for chiles
3. Fry sesame with chopped garlic and when golden, add water to boil well, then the feet, breadcrumbs ground with cloves, cinnamon and pepper
