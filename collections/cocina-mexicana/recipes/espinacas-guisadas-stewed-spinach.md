## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter or lard
- beef broth
- salt
- pepper
- custard (natillas), sweetened
- bread, fried

## Directions

1. Clean spinach well and place in boiling water
2. After a quarter hour, remove, drain and chop finely
3. Fry in a good amount of butter or lard
4. When fried, add a little beef broth seasoned with salt and pepper, or alternatively, add sweetened custard
5. Let boil until well cooked and serve very hot with fried bread on top
