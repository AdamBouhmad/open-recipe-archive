## Ingredients

- Carrots
- Onion, fried
- All spices
- Salt
- Hot water
- Sugar or honey
- Vinegar
- Flour

## Directions

1. Cook, cut into strips and place in a casserole with fried onion
2. Season with all spices and salt, covering with hot water
3. Add sugar or honey, or vinegar (they should be either very sweet or very sour)
4. Fry a little flour until it burns and dissolve with the carrot broth
5. Give a boil and serve cold or hot
