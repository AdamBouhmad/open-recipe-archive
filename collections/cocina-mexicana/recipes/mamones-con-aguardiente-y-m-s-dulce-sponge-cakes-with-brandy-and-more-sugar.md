## Ingredients

- eggs
- fourteen ounces sugar
- twenty ounces starch
- a small glass refined brandy

## Directions

1. Make the same as the previous mamones recipes
2. Use fourteen ounces sugar and twenty ounces starch
3. When the egg whites are risen, mix in a small glass of refined brandy
