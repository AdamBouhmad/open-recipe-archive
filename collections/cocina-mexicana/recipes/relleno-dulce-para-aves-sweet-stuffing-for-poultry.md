## Ingredients

- biscuit
- cheese
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cilantro toasted
- saffron
- sugar
- eggs

## Directions

1. Grind biscuit with a little cheese
2. Add plenty of cinnamon and pepper, little toasted cilantro, saffron and sugar, all ground
3. Beat mixed with eggs
