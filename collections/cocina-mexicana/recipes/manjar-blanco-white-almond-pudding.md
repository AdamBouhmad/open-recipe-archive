## Ingredients

- thirty-two quartillos of milk
- three pounds of rice
- one quarter pound of sugar
- chicken breast shredded
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Take the milk and put in the pan with the sugar and strain with a cloth
2. Wash the rice and dry it in the sun, then grind it dry and sift through a fine sieve
3. Stir with the milk over heat
4. Add the shredded chicken breast
5. When it reaches the right point add a bit of orange blossom water
6. To know when it is done pour on a platter and when it releases it is ready
