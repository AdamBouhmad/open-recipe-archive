## Ingredients

- perch
- onion
- carrot
- bay leaf
- parsley sprigs
- salt
- water
- butter sauce

## Directions

1. Empty the perch and remove the gills
2. Wash and tie the heads with string
3. Place in a pot with a sliced onion, a carrot, a bay leaf, some parsley sprigs, salt, and sufficient water
4. Let cook
5. When cooked, drain them
6. Remove the skin, scales, and fins
7. Fix the fins into the middle of the body at intervals
8. Keep them hot in their cooking liquid
9. At serving time, drain and cover with butter sauce
