## Ingredients

- veal liver
- ham pieces
- good oil
- thyme
- bay leaves
- parsley
- onions
- pebre sauce

## Directions

1. Lard a liver with fairly large pieces of well-seasoned ham
2. Season in good oil, thyme, bay leaf, parsley and onions
3. Wrap in greased paper after placing on the spit and securing well with skewers
4. Serve bathed and over a pebre sauce
