## Ingredients

- one catfish
- lettuce
- lard
- green chili
- xitomate (tomato)
- garlic
- onion
- ground spices
- cumin
- cooking oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After removing the phlegm from the catfish, fry it in lard.
2. Cook a finely chopped lettuce.
3. In a casserole with lard, fry chopped green chili, xitomate, garlic and onion; once fried, add the lettuce with ground spices, all of them including cumin, oil and vinegar, placing the catfish in this broth until it is well seasoned.
