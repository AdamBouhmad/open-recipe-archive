## Ingredients

- beef
- mint
- parsley
- garlic
- ham
- cloves
- pepper
- vinegar
- salt
- aguardiente
- orange
- lemon
- sour lime
- thyme
- rosemary
- parsley
- marjoram
- bay leaf
- cinnamon
- garlic
- lard

## Directions

1. Drain the beef
2. Chop mint, parsley, garlic and ham; mix and insert into the meat with a knife
3. Add cloves and pepper
4. Prepare in a dish vinegar with salt, aguardiente, small pieces of orange, lemon, sour lime in small slices, thyme, rosemary, parsley, marjoram and bay leaf
5. Grind cinnamon, cloves and pepper and add the meat to the broth
6. Let it infuse all night
7. The next day make another broth the same as before and add the meat
8. Cook it
9. When cooked, fry garlic in lard and add the meat, garnishing as before
