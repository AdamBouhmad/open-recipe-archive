## Ingredients

- roasted veal
- lard
- flour
- water
- salt
- pepper
- herbs
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- chives

## Directions

1. Cut roasted veal into small pieces and place in a saucepan with a little lard
2. Sprinkle with flour, and when fried dissolve with sufficient water adding salt, pepper, a small bundle of herbs and let cook until the broth is reduced somewhat
3. In the remaining broth dissolve egg yolks and add a little vinegar; chives may also be added
