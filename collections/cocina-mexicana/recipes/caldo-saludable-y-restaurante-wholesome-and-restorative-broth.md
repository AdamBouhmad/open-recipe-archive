## Ingredients

- twelve quartillos of water
- five parsnips
- three turnips
- four large onions
- three cloves
- one lettuce with a little parsley inside, blanched in boiling water beforehand
- one hen
- two pounds of beef shin
- three pounds of beef pulp
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Place all ingredients in a pot and cook over low heat
2. When the vegetables and meat are completely cooked, pass the broth through a silk sieve
3. Use it to make soup as desired or as a base for other dishes
