## Ingredients

- Hare
- hare liver
- other livers
- parsley
- onion
- fresh butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Once the hare is eviscerated without removing its skin, stuff it with its liver and other livers if available, with parsley and onion, all chopped very fine and crushed in a mortar with a good piece of fresh butter, salt and pepper.
2. Sew the hare's belly so that nothing escapes from the stuffing.
3. Place it on the spit and let it cook over a gentle fire until it is perfectly cooked, which is known when the skin detaches from the body.
4. Remove it from the spit, separate the rest of the skin so that it is left clean, and serve it with pepper sauce.
