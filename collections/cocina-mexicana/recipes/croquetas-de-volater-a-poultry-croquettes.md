## Ingredients

- A good piece of butter
- Parsley, minced
- Mushrooms, minced
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Cream
- Poultry stock or poultry essence
- Roasted bird meat from the previous day
- Breadcrumbs
- Beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Melt a good piece of butter
2. Add minced parsley and mushrooms with two tablespoons of flour, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
3. Let this render a little
4. Add a little cream and two large tablespoons of poultry stock or poultry essence
5. Thicken the sauce appropriately
6. Cut the meat from birds roasted the previous day into small dice
7. Mix the meat with the sauce
8. Let the stew cool
9. Form into small balls
10. Coat with breadcrumbs
11. Bread again by dipping in beaten eggs
12. Coat with breadcrumbs again
13. Fry and serve garnished with fried parsley
