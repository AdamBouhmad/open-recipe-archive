## Ingredients

- partridges, braised
- light red substance
- braising liquid from partridges

## Directions

1. Make a light red substance seasoned with the same braising liquid in which the partridges were cooked
2. Pass through a silk sieve
3. Let reduce to half to develop good flavor
4. Degrease, pass through cheesecloth
5. Pour over the partridges
