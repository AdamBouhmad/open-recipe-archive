## Ingredients

- thick and fresh mushrooms
- good oil
- salt
- coarse pepper
- garlic
- butter
- onion
- parsley
- breadcrumb shavings
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Choose thick and fresh mushrooms and cut off the upper part
2. Wash and let drain
3. Make incisions below
4. Marinate for two hours in good oil with salt, coarse pepper and garlic
5. Chop the stems and render in butter with onion, parsley and a little chopped garlic
6. Arrange the mushrooms on a fireproof plate
7. Put a little of the prepared mixture in each mushroom
8. Dust with breadcrumb shavings
9. Sprinkle with a little oil
10. Place the plate over gentle heat, covering with a camp oven
11. At the moment of serving, pour lemon juice over the mushrooms
