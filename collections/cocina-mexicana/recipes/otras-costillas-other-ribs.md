## Ingredients

- Lamb ribs
- Garlic heads
- Vinegar
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Marjoram
- Salt
- Breadcrumbs
- Parsley
- Lard

## Directions

1. Cook with garlic heads, vinegar, thyme, laurel, marjoram, and salt.
2. When cooked, roll in breadcrumbs and parsley.
3. Wrap in paper, fry in lard, and serve. The same way the tails are prepared.
