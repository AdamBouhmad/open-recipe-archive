## Ingredients

- pineapples
- syrup with scent
- orange blossom water
- biscotela
- syrup
- pastille

## Directions

1. Grind the pineapples
2. Add to syrup with scent and orange blossom water
3. Heat until it takes body
4. Layer this mixture with well-soaked biscotela in syrup
5. Bake in oven
6. Top with pastille
