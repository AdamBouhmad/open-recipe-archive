## Ingredients

- asadura cruda
- manteca
- sal
- chiles anchos desvenvados y remojados
- ajos
- cominos
- vinagre
- zumo de limón
- orégano
- agua

## Directions

1. Slice the raw offal and put to fry in lard with salt
2. While frying, add deveined and soaked wide chiles, garlic and cumin, all ground, so they fry as well
3. Then add vinegar, the juice of one lemon for each one, oregano and enough water so that by boiling the offal can cook perfectly before the sauce is consumed, for the chanfaina must be left to dry over two fires
