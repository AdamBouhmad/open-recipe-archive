## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Bacon or butter
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Broth
- Clove spices
- Toasted garlic
- Almonds
- Hazelnuts
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. After cleaning the turkey with a dry cloth, cut into pieces and sauté with chopped bacon or butter
2. Add garlic, parsley, salt and a little pepper
3. Add some broth from the pot and make a sauce with clove spices, toasted garlic, parsley, almonds, hazelnuts and an egg yolk
4. Dissolve with the same broth and add to the pot to cook a while longer
5. To prevent the sauce from being too thick, add a couple of egg yolks
