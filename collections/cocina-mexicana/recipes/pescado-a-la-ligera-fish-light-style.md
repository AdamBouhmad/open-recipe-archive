## Ingredients

- Cooked fish
- Lard
- Flour
- Onion
- Parsley
- Pepper
- Bread crumbs
- Vinegar
- Oil

## Directions

1. Fry the cooked fish in lard dusted with flour and remove
2. In the same lard fry finely minced onion and parsley
3. Add a pinch of pepper, sprinkle bread crumbs and let brown
4. Return the fish to a little vinegar and oil
5. As soon as it comes to a boil, remove from heat and serve
