## Ingredients

- Scraps, trimmings or leftovers of stockfish
- Butter
- Four onions, cut into small dice
- Flour
- Two quarts of double cream
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Clean the stockfish scraps as you would clean cod
2. Fry the four onions cut into small dice in butter without browning them much
3. Bind them with flour
4. Add two quarts of double cream and cook until the sauce acquires a consistency thick enough to coat the spoon
5. Incorporate the stockfish into this sauce and use it to garnish the puffed ball
