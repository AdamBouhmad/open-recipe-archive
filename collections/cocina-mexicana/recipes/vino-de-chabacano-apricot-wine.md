## Ingredients

- Apricots
- Spirit of wine
- White powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the fuzz from the apricots, cut them into pieces, remove the pits, and press the pulp to extract all the juice
2. Place the juice in a porcelain vessel or a large barrel if the quantity is large, cover it with cloth and let it rest for fifteen days to three weeks while fermentation occurs
3. When fermentation has finished, the liquid will have a film on the surface and exhale a strong odor; then separate the crust, decant, strain to remove any particles that may have risen during decantation, and mix with one pound of spirit of wine and two pounds of powdered white sugar for every eight quartillos of juice
4. Return to another vessel for fifteen days, filter and bottle
5. After one month it will be ready to serve
