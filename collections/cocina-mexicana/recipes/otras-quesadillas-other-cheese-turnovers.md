## Ingredients

- corn
- water
- manteca (lard)
- salt
- water from esquite (corn preparation)
- flour
- fresh or aged cheese, crumbled
- epazote (aromatic herb)

## Directions

1. Cook corn in a small amount of water.
2. Once cooked, remove from heat and let it dry.
3. Grind and sift it, and mix the powder with melted lard.
4. Add salt and a little water from esquite which should be well settled.
5. Beat well so the dough becomes compact.
6. With this dough form small tortillas which are dusted with flour and filled with fresh or aged crumbled cheese and epazote.
7. Once the tortilla is filled, fold the empty half over the other half that is filled, sealing the edges.
8. Dust again with flour and fry in very hot lard, which should also be poured over them with a spoon to prevent them from puffing up.
9. Once well fried, drain and serve immediately.
