## Ingredients

- 2 quartillos of cream
- 3 ounces of sugar
- 1 tablespoon of candied orange blossom
- 6 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Put in 2 quartillos of cream 3 ounces of sugar and 1 tablespoon of candied orange blossom
2. Bring this mixture to a boil, then allow it to cool
3. Add 6 egg yolks and stir all together
4. Pour into small cups or jars
5. Boil water in a pot and place the jars or cups in it so that water reaches only to three-quarters of their height
6. Cover the pot, place heat on top, and make the water boil very gently until the cream in the jars has set
