## Ingredients

- Chiles, soaked
- Garlic
- Small squashes
- Cauliflower
- Beets
- Carrots
- Onion
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Apples
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Oregano
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Grind the soaked chiles and garlic
2. Cook small squashes, cauliflower, beets, carrots and onion
3. Mix everything together with the ground chile
4. Add cinnamon, pepper, apples in quarters, raisins, almonds, oregano, oil and vinegar
