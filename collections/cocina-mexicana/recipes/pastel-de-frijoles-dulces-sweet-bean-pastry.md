## Ingredients

- Nixtamal, cleaned
- Stale bread
- Lard
- Salt
- Sugar to taste
- Cooked and ground beans
- Syrup
- Ground cinnamon
- Candied citron pieces
- Raisins
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Grind cleaned nixtamal and stale bread together
2. Knead with lard, without water, adding salt and sugar to taste to form the pastry
3. For the filling, add cooked and ground beans to syrup, thickening as needed
4. Add ground cinnamon and fry
5. Once fried, set aside and add pieces of candied citron, raisins, and almonds
6. Cover and cook this pastry as the others, and on top sprinkle ground sugar and cinnamon
