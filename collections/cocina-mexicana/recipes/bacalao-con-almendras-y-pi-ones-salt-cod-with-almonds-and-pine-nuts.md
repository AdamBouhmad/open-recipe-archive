## Ingredients

- well-cooked and cold salt cod
- cloth
- a little flour
- lard
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- oil
- ground almonds
- ground pine nuts
- water
- two ounces of almonds and slightly more pine nuts per pound of salt cod

## Directions

1. After the salt cod is well cooked and cold, dry it with a cloth
2. Fry it with a little flour in lard
3. For the sauce, finely chop garlic, onion, and parsley
4. Fry in oil without browning, just cooking
5. Add ground almonds and ground pine nuts separately
6. Incorporate them with the oil but do not let them fry much
7. Add a little water to season properly
8. Place the salt cod and cook over two low heats until the water is consumed
