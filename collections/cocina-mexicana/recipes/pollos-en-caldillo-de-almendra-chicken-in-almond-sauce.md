## Ingredients

- Lard
- Garlic cloves
- Almonds
- Bread
- Salt
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Cinnamon
- Raisins
- Pine nuts
- Wine
- Chicken

## Directions

1. Fry split garlic cloves in lard and once golden, remove them
2. In the same fat, add toasted almonds and ground bread, also toasted
3. Season everything with salt, cloves, pepper, and cinnamon, all ground
4. Add raisins, pine nuts, and wine
5. Place cooked chickens in this sauce
