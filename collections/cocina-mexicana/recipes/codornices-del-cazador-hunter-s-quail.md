## Ingredients

- quail
- butter
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- flour
- white wine
- broth

## Directions

1. Empty and singe the quail at the flame.
2. Place them in a pot with a little butter, a bay leaf, salt, pepper and some fine herbs.
3. Put the pot over high heat, making them jump from time to time.
4. When they resist the pressure of a finger, mix in a common spoonful of flour.
5. Add half a quarter-liter of white wine and some broth.
6. Once the sauce is well thickened, remove the quail from the heat, arrange them on a plate and serve.
