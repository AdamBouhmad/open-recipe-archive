## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- hot water
- cold water
- beef broth
- ham slices
- onions
- cloves
- ham strips (wide and thin)
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Soak the tongue in hot water, then place it in cold water.
2. Remove it and place it to cook in two large spoonfuls of broth with ham slices, a small bundle of herbs and an onion or two studded with cloves.
3. When it is nearly finished cooking, remove it from the broth, remove the skin, stuff the interior part with wide strips of ham and the exterior with thin strips.
4. Place on the roasting spit for an hour and serve with spicy sauce in a separate sauce boat.
