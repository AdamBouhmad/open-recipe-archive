## Ingredients

- partridges
- partridge liver
- bacon (scraped)
- salt
- parsley (chopped)
- onions (chopped)
- butter

## Directions

1. Make a stuffing with its liver, scraped bacon, salt, chopped parsley and onions.
2. Put this stuffing in the body and cook.
3. Then turn the legs over the breast.
4. Simmer in a pan with butter.
5. Then put in the roaster to roast.
6. Serve with the same sauce as the preceding ones.
