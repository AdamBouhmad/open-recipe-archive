## Ingredients

- one pound of rice
- milk cream
- three raw egg yolks
- ten ounces of melted butter
- sugar to taste
- cajeta (dulce de leche)

## Directions

1. Wash the rice well after soaking
2. Grind it not too finely
3. Add milk cream and place in a pot to puff up
4. Add three raw egg yolks, ten ounces of melted butter, and sugar to taste
5. Make the tamales as with the others
6. Place a little batter in water until it rises
7. Fill with cajeta and make them loosely as they will puff up in the pot
8. Leave them in the steam of the pot until they are cooked and serve hot
