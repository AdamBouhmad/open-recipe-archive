## Ingredients

- Sturgeon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Ground aromatics
- Oil
- White wine, one bottle
- Lemon juice, from one lemon
- Oil sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the sturgeon as previously stated and open it along the belly without separating the two halves.
2. Place it on a platter with salt, pepper, ground aromatics, a little oil, a bottle of white wine, and the juice of one lemon.
3. Bake it in the oven until set.
4. Let it set, dress it over its own cooking liquid, somewhat reduced.
5. Serve accompanied by a sauce boat with oil sauce.
