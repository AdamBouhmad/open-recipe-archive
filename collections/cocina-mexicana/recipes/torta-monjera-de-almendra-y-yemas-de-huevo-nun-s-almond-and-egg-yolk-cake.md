## Ingredients

- Two pounds of white sugar
- One pound of well-ground almonds
- Twelve egg whites
- One pound of sugar
- Three cups of warm water
- Three cups of warm lard
- One cup of white wine
- Twelve well-beaten eggs
- A little salt
- A little yeast dissolved in warm water
- Flour in proportion

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup with two pounds of white sugar at medium point and mix in one pound of well-ground almonds that has been prepared beforehand
2. Once at high point, lower it and let cool
3. Beat twelve egg whites until they rise well and add the paste, stirring very well, and let them rest while the dough is prepared
4. Make the dough by adding to one pound of sugar three cups of warm water, three cups of warm lard, one cup of white wine, twelve well-beaten eggs, a little salt, and a little yeast dissolved in warm water
5. Add flour in proportion so the dough is neither thick nor hard nor very soft
6. After kneading, prepare the cake pans, coat them with lard, and add the paste
7. Put a latticed arrangement of the same dough on top, place in the oven, and remove once golden
