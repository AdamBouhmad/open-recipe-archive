## Ingredients

- Sour apples in sufficient quantity to obtain two quartillos of juice
- Two pounds of sugar
- Water as necessary
- Juice of one lemon
- One common tablespoon of orange blossom water, or one coffee spoon of cinnamon spirit

## Directions

1. Peel the sour apples and remove the seeds
2. Cut them into pieces more or less large
3. Place them in a flask with two pounds of sugar and enough water to keep them sufficiently moistened
4. Place in a bain-marie of a still, exposed for several hours to the heat of boiling water
5. Let cool on the ground
6. Add the juice of one lemon and one common tablespoon of orange blossom water or one coffee spoon of cinnamon spirit
