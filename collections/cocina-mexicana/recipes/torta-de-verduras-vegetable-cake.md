## Ingredients

- chorizo
- green beans
- salsifis
- carrots
- tender green beans
- peas
- cooked chayotes
- cooked potatoes
- zucchini
- butter
- onion
- tomatoes
- garlic
- parsley
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook green beans, salsifis, and chopped carrots with chorizo in salted water, along with tender green beans and peas
2. When cooked, drain everything
3. Once dry, add the crumbled chorizo, chayotes, cooked and peeled potatoes cut into small pieces, and zucchini already stewed with butter, onion, tomatoes, garlic, and chopped parsley (which should be one-third of the rest), and these should be dried
4. Mix everything well with sufficient beaten eggs to set
5. Pour into a buttered casserole dish and finish as the previous recipes
