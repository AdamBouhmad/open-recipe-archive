## Ingredients

- garlic, fried
- pasilla chiles, deveined and toasted
- peanuts
- fried bread
- cloves
- pepper
- cumin
- lard
- water
- salt
- pork loin or backbone

## Directions

1. Grind together fried garlic, deveined and toasted pasilla chiles, peanuts, fried bread, cloves, pepper and cumin
2. Fry this mixture in lard in a pot
3. Add water and salt
4. Add pork loin or backbone cut into pieces
5. Cook until it reaches a thick broth-like consistency
