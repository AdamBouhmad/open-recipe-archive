## Ingredients

- 12 egg yolks
- flour
- melted lard
- egg white
- sugar

## Directions

1. To twelve egg yolks add as much flour as they will hold and as much melted lard, mix everything very well and beat sufficiently, adding more flour until the dough is of such consistency that it does not stick to the board.
2. Extend it afterwards with a rolling pin and cut thin cocolitos, which will be joined together in the middle with egg white, and fry them in lard that is not very hot.
3. When removed, sprinkle powdered sugar generously over them so that they remain sugared.
