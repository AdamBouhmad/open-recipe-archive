## Ingredients

- tripe
- onions
- parsley
- carrot
- bay leaf
- basil
- cloves
- salt
- chile
- water
- melted butter
- parsley
- scallions
- garlic
- salt
- pepper
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. After preparing the tripe as previously described, cook it with onions, parsley, carrot, bay leaf, basil, cloves, salt, and chile with sufficient water
2. Remove and drain, then cut into thick pieces about four fingers wide
3. Coat with melted butter mixed with parsley, scallions, finely chopped garlic, salt, and pepper
4. Serve with a spicy sauce
