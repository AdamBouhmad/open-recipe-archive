## Ingredients

- Lard
- Garlic cloves
- Tomatoes
- Raisins
- Salt
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Black pepper
- Wine
- Sugar
- Almonds
- Chicken
- Ham

## Directions

1. Fry sliced garlic cloves in lard and once golden, remove them
2. Add roasted and ground tomatoes to the fat
3. Once these are fried, add ground raisins and season with salt
4. After a short time, add cloves, cinnamon, and pepper, all ground, a glass of wine, a small lump of sugar, and sliced almonds
5. Place cooked chicken quarters in this sauce with the broth in which they were cooked and slices of cooked ham
6. Let everything boil until the sauce has regular consistency
