## Ingredients

- sugar
- twenty egg yolks
- one pound of soaked and bruised almonds
- half a quarter liter of wine
- half an ounce of cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at half-point
2. Away from heat add twenty egg yolks and one pound of soaked and bruised almonds, in such a way that they remain like broken rice
3. Return to fire without ceasing to stir until it reaches the point of cajeta, and remove it then adding half a quarter liter of wine and half an ounce of cinnamon powder
4. As soon as everything is well incorporated, empty into the plates covering with comals or metal sheets with fire to brown
