## Ingredients

- Pasilla chile
- Tomatoes
- Lard
- Spices (except saffron)
- Meat
- Salt

## Directions

1. Remove the veins from the pasilla chile and toast it.
2. Grind it with cooked tomatoes and fry in lard.
3. Add all spices except saffron, meat, and salt.
4. Let it simmer.
