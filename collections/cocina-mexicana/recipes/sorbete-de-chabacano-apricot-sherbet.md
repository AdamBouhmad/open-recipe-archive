## Ingredients

- Thirty ripe apricots
- One quartillo of water
- Half pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Select ripe apricots and remove the pit
2. Cut them into pieces and place in a pan over the fire with one quartillo of water
3. Give them a boil and pass through a sieve, making all the pulp pass through
4. Add half pound of sugar that has been dissolved in water over the fire
5. After incorporating well, let cool and freeze
