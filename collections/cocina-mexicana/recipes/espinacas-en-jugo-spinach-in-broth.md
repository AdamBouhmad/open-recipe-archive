## Ingredients

- spinach
- broth from pot or other broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Boil the spinach in salted water and place in cold water
2. Chop the spinach
3. Add to a pot with broth instead of milk
4. Cook so that it is not too thin
5. Before serving, add strained broth and roasted meat
