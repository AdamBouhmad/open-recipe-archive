## Ingredients

- five pounds of sugar
- sixteen egg yolks beaten
- pound and a half of toasted almonds not very finely ground
- two and a half quartillos of milk
- two ounces of soaked and ground rice
- an ounce and a half of butter
- a small cup of Jerez wine
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Once the syrup is clarified and cold, add the egg yolks
2. At low heat bring to first boil then add heat to continue boiling
3. Mix the almonds well with the milk so no lumps remain
4. As it thickens, add the butter, wine and cinnamon
5. Bring to gentle consistency and pour onto a platter
6. Garnish with pine nuts, almonds and cinnamon powder
