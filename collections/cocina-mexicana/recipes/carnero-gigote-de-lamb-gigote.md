## Ingredients

- menudo (lamb offal)
- sal (salt)
- manteca (lard)
- cebolla (onion)
- peregil (parsley)
- xitomate (tomato)
- harina (flour)
- culantro seco molido (dried cilantro ground)
- limón (lemon)
- agua (water)
- yemas de huevo (egg yolks)

## Directions

1. Clean the offal as previously described and cook with salt
2. Once cooked, cut into small pieces and fry in lard with plenty of chopped onion, parsley and tomato
3. When well fried, sprinkle with a little flour and ground dried cilantro
4. Turn twice with the flour and squeeze lemon juice over it
5. Add a little water from cooking the offal and season with salt
6. When thickened, add two or three egg yolks beaten with lemon
7. Remove from heat, stirring well
