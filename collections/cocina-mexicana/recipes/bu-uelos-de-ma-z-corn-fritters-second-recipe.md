## Ingredients

- niztamal, well washed
- milk
- egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- cinnamon powder
- clarified sugar
- eggs
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash niztamal well, grind, strain, and let settle
2. To one cup of settled corn add one and a half cups of milk, eight egg yolks, sugar to taste and a pinch of cinnamon
3. Mix well and cook until it reaches the right consistency, then pour into a platter to cool
4. Clarify two pounds of sugar, beat four eggs as for chiles and cut the dough into quarter portions
5. Add the egg, a pinch of flour, fry in butter like fried pastries, and place in syrup to boil
