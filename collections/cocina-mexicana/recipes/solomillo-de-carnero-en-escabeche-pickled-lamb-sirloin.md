## Ingredients

- Lamb sirloins
- Bacon strips
- Vinegar
- Aromatic herbs
- Parsley sprigs
- Onion
- Half-jelly
- Meat broth

## Directions

1. Clean the sirloins carefully and lard them with bacon strips.
2. Place in a marinade for four or five days in vinegar, aromatic herbs, parsley sprigs, and onion cut into pieces.
3. Shortly before serving, cook them in a half-jelly and coat with good-colored meat broth.
4. Serve hot.
