## Ingredients

- ancho chile
- garlic
- cumin
- lard
- cold bread, soaked
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Devein and soak a little ancho chile
2. Grind it with garlic and cumin and fry in lard
3. Grind a little cold soaked bread
4. Mix the bread with the chile
5. Put to boil; when thick, mix with beaten eggs
6. Pour into a pan or casserole with lard
7. Allow to set over two fires
8. Serve this cake dry or with tomato sauce or chile sauce
