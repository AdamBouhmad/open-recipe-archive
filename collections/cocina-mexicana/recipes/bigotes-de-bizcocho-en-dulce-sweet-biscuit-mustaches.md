## Ingredients

- two handfuls of pitted and cleaned raisins
- two biscuits
- some cooked egg yolks
- half citron
- egg yolks mixed with biscuit
- ground biscuit
- beef bone marrow lard
- sugar syrup
- wine

## Directions

1. Grind in a metate two handfuls of pitted and cleaned raisins, two biscuits, some cooked egg yolks and half citron
2. Mix the paste well and form small points
3. With other egg yolks mixed with biscuit, make small balls
4. Roll everything in ground biscuit and fry in beef bone marrow lard
5. Serve with sugar syrup and wine
