## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Ham
- Onion
- Garlic
- Spices
- Butter
- White wine
- Water

## Directions

1. Pluck and clean the partridges.
2. Brown them and place in a suitable pot.
3. Add fried ham cut into pieces, coarsely chopped onion in abundance, pounded garlic, spices of all kinds, and butter.
4. Add white wine.
5. Cook over low heat.
6. Place a small pan with water on top of the pot, separated by kraft paper.
