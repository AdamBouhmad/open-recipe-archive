## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Their livers, chopped
- Truffles or earth truffles, chopped
- Ham scrapings
- Salt and pepper
- Parsley and onions
- Two pats of butter
- Six truffles or whole earth truffles, peeled
- A small bundle of parsley
- Onions
- Half a head of garlic
- Two cloves
- Coarse pepper
- Veal slices
- Ham slices
- Broth
- One glass of Champagne wine
- Red meat essence
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. After removing the shoulder of the young partridges, flatten them slightly without breaking the bones
2. Fill them with their chopped livers, chopped earth truffles, ham scrapings, salt, pepper, parsley and onions
3. Partially cook them with two pats of butter, six whole peeled truffles or earth truffles, a small bundle of parsley, onions, half a head of garlic, two cloves, salt and coarse pepper
4. Place everything with all the ingredients over slices of veal and cover with slices of ham
5. Let them sweat for a quarter of an hour
6. Moisten with a little broth and one glass of Champagne wine
7. Let them finish cooking
8. Arrange the young partridges and whole earth truffles in the same dish in which they are to be served, keeping them hot
9. Add red meat essence to its broth and make it boil to deplete it and let it reduce to the consistency of a sauce
10. Pass it through a sieve, squeeze the juice of one lemon into it and sprinkle the young partridges and earth truffles with it, which are then served hot
