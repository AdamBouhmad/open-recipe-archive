## Ingredients

- Picadillo of pork loin with chorizo
- Lamb tongues
- Sweetbreads
- Chicken hearts
- Chicken livers
- Longaniza pieces
- Ham pieces
- Small whole eggs from fish roe
- Oregano
- Broth
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Olives

## Directions

1. Prepare a picadillo of pork loin with chorizo and add to it lamb tongues, sweetbreads, hearts and chicken livers, all chopped, small pieces of longaniza and ham, whole small eggs from fish roe, all cooked and a little oregano
2. When everything is incorporated, add a little broth to it so that it boils for some time, and season by adding raisins, almonds and olives
3. When it has dried, mix it with beaten eggs, put it in a cazuela lined with lard, cover with more egg and let the cake curdle at two fires
