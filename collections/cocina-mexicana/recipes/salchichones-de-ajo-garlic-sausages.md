## Ingredients

- Two pounds of fresh pork meat
- One pound of fat ham
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Nutmeg
- Garlic, a little
- Cleaned pork intestines
- Water
- Carrots
- Onions
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Basil
- Fresh cilantro

## Directions

1. Mince together two pounds of fresh pork meat and one pound of fat ham
2. Season the minced mixture with salt, pepper, spices, nutmeg, and a little garlic
3. Fill the very clean intestines with this mixture and tie them to the desired size
4. Smoke them for six to seven days
5. After this time, cook them in a pot of water seasoned with salt, carrots, onions, thyme, laurel, basil, and fresh cilantro for two hours
6. Allow them to cool in their broth and serve
