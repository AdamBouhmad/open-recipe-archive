## Ingredients

- one arroba of sugar
- four lemons
- water
- twenty-two pounds of grated and cooked peaches
- four cuartillos of jelly

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup with one arroba of sugar and bring to a high thick point
2. Squeeze four lemons in a jar of water
3. When the syrup is boiling, add the lemon water to make it candied
4. Remove and keep in the pan for two days until it sets
5. Mix twenty-two pounds of grated and cooked peaches with four cuartillos of jelly
6. Put on the fire until the peaches are half-cooked
7. Set aside and leave until the next day, then return to the fire and bring to the jelly point
