## Ingredients

- beef kidneys
- butter
- parsley, chopped
- shallots
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- flour
- white wine
- Spanish sauce
- lemon juice
- fried toast rounds

## Directions

1. Clean the kidneys and cut them into slices
2. Place a piece of butter in a pan with the cut kidneys, chopped parsley, shallots, mushrooms, salt, pepper, and a little nutmeg
3. Sauté the kidneys over high heat so they do not release their juice
4. Bind with a little flour, moistening them with a glass of white wine and two tablespoons of Spanish sauce
5. Remove from heat without letting them boil
6. Bind with a piece of fine butter and lemon juice
7. Serve hot with fried toast rounds arranged around them
