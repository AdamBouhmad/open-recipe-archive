## Ingredients

- neck meat from pig, mostly fatty, chopped into regular pieces
- ground salt
- wide chile for color
- powdered pepper
- oregano passed through a sieve
- blood from beef
- intestines from beef or pork

## Directions

1. Chop the neck meat of the pig into regular pieces
2. Season with ground salt, wide chile for color, a little powdered pepper, and oregano passed through a sieve
3. Knead like chorizos
4. After a couple of days, dilute the paste with beef blood
5. Stuff everything into beef or pork intestines
6. When tying the morcillas, leave them slack so they do not burst when used
7. Hang them over smoke
