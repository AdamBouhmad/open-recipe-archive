## Ingredients

- Lettuce or romaine lettuce
- Salt
- Pepper
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove all the tough and very green outer leaves from the lettuces or romaine
2. Clean the root well and cut into quarters in cold water
3. When serving, shake off the water well
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and dress either with oil alone, or with oil and vinegar seasoned with salt
