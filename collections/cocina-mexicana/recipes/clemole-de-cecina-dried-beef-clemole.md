## Ingredients

- cecina (dried beef), soaked and washed
- water
- salt
- lard
- epazote
- ancho chile
- pasilla chile
- toasted sesame seeds
- cooked tomatoes

## Directions

1. Soak and wash the cecina thoroughly
2. Cook it with water, salt, lard and epazote
3. When half cooked, fry in sufficient lard the ancho and pasilla chiles, sesame seeds, all toasted and ground together with cooked tomatoes
4. When well fried, add the contents of the cooking pot
5. Simmer until it thickens
