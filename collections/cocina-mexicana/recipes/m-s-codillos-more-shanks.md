## Ingredients

- shanks
- lemon
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- bread
- tomato
- onion
- capers
- fried bread
- green chiles
- cloves
- spices

## Directions

1. Beat the shanks
2. Spread with lemon, salt and pepper
3. Roast on a grill
4. As you turn them to spread with lard, sprinkle with bread crumbs
5. When well roasted, place on the platter
6. Boil a tomato and an onion
7. Mash them with capers, a piece of fried bread, green chiles, cloves and ground spices
8. Fry everything in lard and drizzle over the shanks
