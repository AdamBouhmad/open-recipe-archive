## Ingredients

- Cauliflower
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Olives
- Tornachiles
- Avocado
- Capers
- Black pepper
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower whole with little salt.
2. Then chop it and dry it so that the flower is visible.
3. Add the necessary vinegar and oil.
4. Garnish with olives, tornachiles, quarters of avocado, capers, and a little black pepper and salt.
