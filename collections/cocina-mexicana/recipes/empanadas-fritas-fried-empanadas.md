## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds flour
- half pound melted and cold butter
- salt water
- tequesquite
- ground meat filling
- sugar

## Directions

1. Combine one and a half pounds of flour with half pound of melted and cold butter, salt water, and settled tequesquite to soften it.
2. When it is smooth and workable, portion the dough by hand to the desired size, extend with a rolling pin, and fill with chopped meat filling.
3. Fold and seal the edges and fry in butter.
4. When removed, sprinkle with ground sugar.
