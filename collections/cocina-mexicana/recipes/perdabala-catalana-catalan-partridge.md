## Ingredients

- partridge
- butter
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- herbs
- garlic
- sour orange
- sliced bread

## Directions

1. Clean the partridge
2. Braise it in a saucepan with butter
3. Remove the partridge and to the same butter in which it fried add broth, salt, pepper, and all herbs, and let it boil again
4. Add the partridge and cook
5. While it cooks, fry two cloves of garlic separately
6. In another small saucepan prepare a sour orange cut in slices
7. A quarter hour before serving, combine everything
