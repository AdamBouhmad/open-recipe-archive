## Ingredients

- 6 quartillos good brandy
- 4 quartillos water
- 4 ounces rose spirit
- 4 ounces orange blossom spirit
- 1.5 ounces jasmine spirit
- 1 ounce reseda spirit
- 2.5 pounds superior sugar

## Directions

1. Rectify the brandy in a bain-marie.
2. Proceed with everything else as in the previous liqueur.
