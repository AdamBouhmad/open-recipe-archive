## Ingredients

- Broccoli rabe
- Salt
- Oil
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Orange juice

## Directions

1. Clean and cook the broccoli rabe as in the previous recipe
2. Place a pot on the fire with oil and fry minced garlic cloves in it
3. Remove the garlic once it has browned and fry the broccoli rabe there
4. Once fried, remove it, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and dress with oil and vinegar
5. Alternatively, orange juice may be used instead of vinegar
