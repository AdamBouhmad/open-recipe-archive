## Ingredients

- pear
- peach
- white sugar in proportion of one pound for each pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Cook these fruits separately
2. Remove the pit from one and the core from the other
3. Grind them and pass through a cloth or sieve
4. Mix in equal parts
5. Add white powdered sugar in proportion of one pound for each pound of fruit
6. Put on the fire and let reach the point of cajeta
