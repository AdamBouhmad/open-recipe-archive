## Ingredients

- calf rennet
- onions

## Directions

1. Put it to cook as stated in the previous article, and cut the onions into strips, which are fried
2. Then add the onions in a saucepan with the rennet, divided into small pieces, let it boil at a gentle fire for a few moments and serve
