## Ingredients

- egg yolks
- fresh milk
- sugar
- flavoring pastille
- sponge cake (mamón)
- syrup
- raisins
- almonds
- walnuts
- toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks with fresh milk, necessary sugar, and flavoring pastille
2. Cook until thick and pour over layers of sliced sponge cake moistened with syrup, decorating the last layer with raisins, almonds, walnuts, and toasted sesame
3. Place over hot ashes with a griddle with fire above
