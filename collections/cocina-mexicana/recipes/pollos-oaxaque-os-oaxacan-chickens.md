## Ingredients

- pork loin
- dried wide chiles, deveined and toasted
- garlic cloves
- cumin
- cinnamon
- clove
- pepper
- salt
- raw tomatoes
- lard
- paper

## Directions

1. Roast the pork loin and cut it into very small pieces.
2. Grind the dried wide chiles, several garlic cloves and a little cumin.
3. Also grind cinnamon, clove and pepper and add everything with sufficient salt.
4. Chop raw tomatoes and fry all this in lard, adding the chopped pork loin.
5. Let it season well, and use this to stuff raw chickens, which are sewn with thread.
6. Coat them with lard, wrap them in oiled paper, and put them in the oven, turning them from one side to the other until they are very cooked.
7. Then, remove the paper, sprinkle a little salt and pepper on them, and put them to brown.
