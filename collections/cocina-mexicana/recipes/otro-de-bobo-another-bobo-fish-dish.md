## Ingredients

- cebolla
- ajos
- perejil
- aceite
- manteca
- agua
- sal
- bobo
- harina
- clavo
- canela
- cominos
- pimienta
- vinagre
- orégano
- aceitunas
- alcaparras
- chiles

## Directions

1. Chop plenty of onion, garlic and parsley and grind together.
2. Fry in oil and lard, add water and salt.
3. Add the bobo whole or in portions and cook in this sauce.
4. In a pan with lard, fry a little flour until wheat-colored and pour hot over the fish, stirring the broth so no lumps form.
5. Add to the sauce ground clove, cinnamon, cumin and pepper; add oil and vinegar and let it season until the broth is of regular thickness.
6. A short time before it is done, sprinkle with oregano.
7. Garnish with olives, capers and chiles.
