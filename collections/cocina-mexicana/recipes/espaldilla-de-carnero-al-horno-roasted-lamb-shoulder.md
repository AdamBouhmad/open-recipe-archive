## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder
- onions, sliced
- Galician turnip or parsnip
- parsnip, cut in strips
- garlic head
- two cloves
- bay leaf
- basil leaves
- water or broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. If desired, lard the shoulder with thin strips
2. Place two or three sliced onions in the bottom of a casserole of appropriate size
3. Add a Galician turnip or parsnip and a parsnip cut in strips
4. Add a head of garlic, two cloves, a bay leaf, and some basil leaves
5. Pour in one quart of water, or preferably broth
6. Season with salt and pepper, using less salt if the shoulder is larded
7. Place the shoulder on top and put the casserole in the oven
8. Leave until the meat is well cooked
9. Remove and strain the sauce, mashing and pressing the vegetables well so that a clear substance forms to bind the sauce
10. Degrease the sauce and serve it over the shoulder
