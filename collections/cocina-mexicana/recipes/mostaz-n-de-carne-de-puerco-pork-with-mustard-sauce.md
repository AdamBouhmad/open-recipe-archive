## Ingredients

- mustard
- all spices
- cooked pork meat
- toasted and ground bread
- white wine
- a pinch of sugar
- olives

## Directions

1. Grind the mustard and pass it through a sieve
2. Heat it in a pan with all spices, stirring until it jumps
3. Add the already cooked pork meat and fry it as well
4. Add toasted and ground bread, white wine, and a pinch of sugar
5. Remove from heat and add olives
