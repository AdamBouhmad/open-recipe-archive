## Ingredients

- atole made with milk
- thin biscuits, ground
- lady fingers (mamones)
- melted butter
- powdered sugar
- cinnamon or syrup

## Directions

1. Make the atole with milk the day before, with a thick consistency so that the next day it can be cut into thin slices
2. Wrap these slices in ground fine biscuits
3. Layer the slices alternately with lady fingers that have melted butter poured over them
4. Place over two fires
5. When cooked, dust with powdered sugar on top and add cinnamon or syrup
