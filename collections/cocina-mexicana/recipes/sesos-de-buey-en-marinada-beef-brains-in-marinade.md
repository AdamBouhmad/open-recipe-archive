## Ingredients

- butter
- flour
- water
- pepper
- vinegar
- garlic
- clove
- parsley
- onion
- beef brains
- frying paste

## Directions

1. Make a marinade with a piece of butter kneaded with flour, a little water, pepper and vinegar, garlic, clove, parsley and onion; warm it by stirring over the fire.
2. Add the brains from one head, washed in warm water and cut into slices half a finger thick; let them marinate for two hours.
3. Drain afterwards and roll them in some frying paste, fry them and serve adorned with fried parsley.
