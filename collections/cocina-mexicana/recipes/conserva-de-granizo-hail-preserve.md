## Ingredients

- Citron, turnip, or jícama
- Salt
- Water
- White sugar
- Egg white
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Remove the skin and interior part of the citron, and from what remains cut out the granizo (small pearl-like balls)
2. Give it one boil in salted water, letting it soak for nine days and changing the cold water each day
3. If using turnip, peel it, cut out the granizo and give one boil without letting it fall apart
4. If using jícama, only peel it and cut out the granizo
5. Once prepared, add to syrup made of very white sugar, clarified with egg whites beaten in water, at medium consistency
6. Let everything boil until the syrup has the proper consistency and pour into a platter or preserve dish
