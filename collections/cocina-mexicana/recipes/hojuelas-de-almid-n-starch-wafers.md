## Ingredients

- One pound of flour flowers
- Beaten egg as for frying
- Two ounces of cold butter
- Two ounces of starch ground dry
- A little water of salt

## Directions

1. Knead one pound of flour flowers with beaten egg as for frying, two ounces of cold butter, two ounces of starch ground dry, and a little water of salt
2. When the dough can be cut with a knife, make three or four balls, coat them with butter, and let them rest half an hour
3. Extend sheets of dough with a roller
4. Cut them square or oblong with a pastry wheel, or in whatever shape is desired
5. Fry them in butter not too hot, so they do not lose their shape
6. Serve with sugar coating and beads, and sprinkled with high-point syrup
