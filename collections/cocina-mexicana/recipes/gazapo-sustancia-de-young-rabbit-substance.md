## Ingredients

- Roasted rabbit meat
- Spanish sauce
- Game essence
- Meat gelatin
- Rabbit croquettes

## Directions

1. Chop and grind the meat from a roasted rabbit after removing the skin and tendons
2. Moisten with Spanish sauce and a bit of game essence
3. Heat this preparation
4. Pass through a sieve or filter through cheesecloth
5. Add a bit of meat gelatin and arrange on a plate
6. Surround with rabbit croquettes
