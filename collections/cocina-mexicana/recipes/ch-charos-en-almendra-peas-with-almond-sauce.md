## Ingredients

- cooked peas
- almonds with shell
- butter
- bread slices
- small onions
- parsley
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry the almonds with shell until golden in butter.
2. Toast bread slices in the same butter until golden, then remove both and grind well.
3. Fry small onions, and when fried, fry the ground almonds and bread in the same pan.
4. Add a little water, chop parsley very finely and put in the pan with the cooked peas.
5. Season with necessary salt and let simmer until the broth thickens.
6. Then leave over hot coals.
