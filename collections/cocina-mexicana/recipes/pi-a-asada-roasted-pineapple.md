## Ingredients

- one pound of cooked pineapple
- half pound of grated coconut
- clarified syrup of half-point, made with three pounds of sugar
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of cooked pineapple and half a pound of grated coconut with clarified syrup of half-point, made with three pounds of sugar
2. Let everything boil until it thickens
3. Then remove it from the fire so the pineapple browns
4. When browned, decorate with ground cinnamon on top
