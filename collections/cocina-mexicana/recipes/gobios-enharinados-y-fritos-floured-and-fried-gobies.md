## Ingredients

- gobies
- flour
- tomato fried sauce

## Directions

1. Remove scales, empty and clean the gobies
2. Dust the inside with flour and roll the outside in flour
3. Fry for the same time as the plain fried gobies
4. Serve with tomato fried sauce
