## Ingredients

- six cuartillos of milk
- half a pound of clean walnuts, ground and dissolved in milk

## Directions

1. Sweeten six cuartillos of milk and strain through clean cloth
2. Place over heat
3. When it thickens, add half a pound of clean walnuts, ground and dissolved in milk
4. Incorporate well, adding more sweetness if needed
5. Allow it to reach the point of cajeta
6. Remove from heat, pour into serving dishes and decorate with ground cinnamon
