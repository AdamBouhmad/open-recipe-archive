## Ingredients

- asparagus
- water
- good butter
- béchamel sauce

## Directions

1. Cut asparagus into small pieces and blanch in boiling water
2. Drain well and fry in a saucepan with good butter
3. Add a good ladleful of béchamel sauce in the meatless version
4. Serve hot
