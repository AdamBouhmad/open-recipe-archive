## Ingredients

- Pork leg
- water
- thyme
- basil
- veal slices
- white generous wine
- broth
- two lemons
- mixed herbs
- onions
- two garlic heads
- six to eight cloves
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Desalt the leg in fresh water for two or three days
2. Parboil with a handful of thyme and basil
3. Place in a saucepan or pot lined with veal slices
4. Add white generous wine, broth, two lemons without skin in thin slices, a mixed bouquet of herbs, onions, two garlic heads, six to eight cloves, thyme, bay leaf and basil
5. Once cooked, remove and let the broth reduce to the point of gelatine
6. Sieve the gelatine
7. Pour over the leg and serve cold
