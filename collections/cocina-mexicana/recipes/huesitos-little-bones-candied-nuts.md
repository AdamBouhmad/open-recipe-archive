## Ingredients

- sugar, one pound
- virgin honey, one quarter measure
- hazelnuts, half pound
- pine nuts, half pound
- almonds, toasted, a few
- small walnut
- hard biscuit, enough to thicken
- clove
- nutmeg
- cinnamon
- egg yolks, eight
- red pastille coating, sifted

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with one pound of sugar and one quarter measure of virgin honey
2. Once boiling and at the hard ball stage, add half pound of hazelnuts, half pound of pine nuts, a few toasted almonds, small walnut, enough hard biscuit to thicken, a little clove, nutmeg and cinnamon, all ground, and eight egg yolks
3. When it reaches the point of pulling away from the pan, remove from heat
4. Once cooled, shape the mixture into date-like forms
5. Wrap them in sifted red pastille coating
