## Ingredients

- six pounds of sugar
- one pound of clean toasted and ground almonds
- fourteen egg yolks
- ten boxes or papers of toasted ground mamón
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at medium point with six pounds of sugar
2. Add one pound of clean toasted and ground almonds, fourteen egg yolks, and ten boxes or papers of toasted ground mamón
3. Put on the fire so it acquires consistency
4. Mix in ground cinnamon, which should also be sprinkled on top when brought to the table
