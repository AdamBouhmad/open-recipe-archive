## Ingredients

- Jitomates (tomatoes)
- Green chili peppers
- Tortillas
- Fresh or aged cheese
- Epazote (optional)

## Directions

1. Roast the jitomates very well and then roast some green chili peppers.
2. Knead them by hand and fry them very well.
3. Once the sauce is very well seasoned, remove it from the heat.
4. Slice tortillas into small squares and fry them until they are well browned.
5. Add them to the sauce and as soon as it boils, remove it from the heat.
6. Garnish with fresh or aged cheese according to taste.
7. You can add a sprig of epazote to the sauce.
