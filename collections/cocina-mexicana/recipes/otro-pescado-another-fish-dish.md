## Ingredients

- fish
- garlic
- cumin
- Castilian pepper
- well-ripened tomatoes
- green chile
- onion
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the fish.
2. Grind garlic, cumin, and Castilian pepper.
3. Fry with a good quantity of well-ripened tomatoes, a green chile, and onion in large pieces, mashing them with a spoon.
4. When fried, set aside.
5. Shortly before serving, coat it with a sauce made of onion cooked in large pieces, oregano sprinkled over it, oil, and vinegar.
