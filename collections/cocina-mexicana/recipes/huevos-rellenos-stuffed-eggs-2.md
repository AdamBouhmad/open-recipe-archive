## Ingredients

- eggs
- sugar
- pepper
- a little cinnamon
- egg for beating
- salt
- sugar syrup
- orange blossom water
- sugar powder
- cinnamon
- musk

## Directions

1. Cook eggs in water, peel and cut lengthwise
2. Knead the yolks with sugar, pepper and a little cinnamon until sweet
3. Fill the eggs and close them well
4. Beat eggs with a little salt
5. Fry like chiles and if peeling occurs fry again
6. Make a syrup that boils with orange blossom water and remains thick
7. Glaze the eggs, place on a platter and sprinkle sugar, cinnamon and musk on top
