## Ingredients

- beef tendons
- lettuce
- root vegetables

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the tendons as in the preceding method
2. Arrange in a crown shape and coat with jelly
3. Place a border of lettuce around them
4. In the center arrange root vegetables cut into stick or olive shapes, arranged in a pyramid
