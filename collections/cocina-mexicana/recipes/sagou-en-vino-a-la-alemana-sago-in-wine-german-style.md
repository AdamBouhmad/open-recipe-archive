## Ingredients

- sago
- water
- lemon peel
- cinnamon
- red wine
- lemon slices
- sugar

## Directions

1. After washing the sago, put it to cook in water with the peel of a lemon and cinnamon
2. When the water is reduced to half, replace the missing quantity with an equal amount of good red wine
3. Add some slices of lemon and sugar, and allow it to finish cooking
4. Serve this potage sprinkled with sugar and cinnamon
