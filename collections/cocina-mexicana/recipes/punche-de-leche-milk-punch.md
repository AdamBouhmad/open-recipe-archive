## Ingredients

- seven cuartillos of milk
- half a pound of almonds
- cinnamon sticks
- a small piece of vanilla
- a little lemon

## Directions

1. Place seven cuartillos of milk and let it give one boil
2. Sweeten to taste and mix with half a pound of almonds, not too finely ground
3. Add some cinnamon sticks, a small piece of vanilla and a little lemon
4. Place on fire to give a strong boil, remove and pour into a clean pot
