## Ingredients

- squash seeds
- lard
- cumin
- garlic
- small chiles
- tomatoes
- turkey or chicken
- broth
- salt

## Directions

1. With the squash seeds very dry, rub them with a rough cloth to remove the outer skin
2. Soak in salt water for a while and then dry in the sun so they dry again
3. In that case grind with a brazier under the metate, like cacao, until the mass is very smooth
4. Fry then in lard until small balls form
5. Then add cumin, garlic, small chiles and tomatoes, all raw and ground
6. After frying this and seasoning with salt, add the turkey, chicken, etc., divided into regular portions, with the broth in which it was cooked
7. Let boil until the pipián or mole becomes thick
8. The quantity of small chiles varies according to taste, as some want the dishes spicier than others
