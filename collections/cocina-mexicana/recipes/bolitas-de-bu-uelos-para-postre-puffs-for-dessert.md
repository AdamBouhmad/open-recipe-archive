## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- five ounces lard
- salt
- water
- twenty egg yolks
- eight egg whites
- lard for frying
- syrup
- dragée

## Directions

1. Cook one pound of flour in water with salt and five ounces of lard
2. Do not stop stirring while cooking, and when it sticks to the fingers and crackles when stirred, remove and let cool
3. Mix afterward with twenty beaten egg yolks and eight egg whites
4. With a spoon, drop portions of this dough into very hot lard
5. Place afterward in syrup and serve with dragée
