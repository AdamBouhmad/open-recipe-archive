## Ingredients

- pineapple
- salt
- tequesquite
- coconut
- jicama
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Clean the pineapple and cut into small pieces
2. Soak in salt water and tequesquite solution and cook
3. Cook the coconut, clean it, and grate it
4. Wash the jicama in hot water and grate it
5. Make fine syrup and add the pineapple, give one boil and add the coconut, give another boil and add the jicama
6. Continue boiling until everything is penetrated and well preserved and reaches proper consistency
7. Add orange blossom water
