## Ingredients

- Tomatoes and tomatillos
- Garlic
- Lard
- Broth from cooking the hens
- Ground bread
- Salt
- Cooked onions
- Chiles
- Olives
- Hen quarters
- Cooked chorizos
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Toasted cilantro
- Saffron
- Oregano
- Oil

## Directions

1. Fry ground raw tomatoes and tomatillos in lard with garlic cloves
2. Add broth from the cooked hens and thicken with ground bread, seasoning with salt
3. Add cooked onions separated into layers, chiles, olives, hen quarters, cooked chorizos
4. Add clove, cinnamon, pepper, toasted cilantro, saffron and oregano, all ground together
5. Let everything boil until the broth reaches proper consistency
6. When serving, drizzle with oil
