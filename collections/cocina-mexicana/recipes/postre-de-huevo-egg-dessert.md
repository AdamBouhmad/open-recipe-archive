## Ingredients

- Milk
- Egg yolks crumbled
- Sugar as needed
- Lard for greasing
- Syrup
- Ground dragée

## Directions

1. Add egg yolks crumbled and the necessary sugar to milk so that it remains watery
2. Put this over two fires to set in a greased pan or casserole
3. After cooking, pour syrup on top and ground dragée
