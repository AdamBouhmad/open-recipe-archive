## Ingredients

- pulpas de carnero (lamb shoulder meat)
- lomos de puerco de cabeza (pork head loin)
- alcaparras (capers)
- jamón (ham)
- peregil (parsley)
- almendras (almonds)
- ajos (garlic)
- clavo (clove)
- canela (cinnamon)
- pimienta (pepper)
- azafrán (saffron)
- xitomate (tomato)
- yemas de huevo (egg yolks)
- sal (salt)
- pasas (raisins)
- acitrón (candied citron)
- pan rallado (breadcrumbs)

## Directions

1. Chop two lamb shoulders and two pork head loins
2. Add plenty of capers, ham, parsley, a good portion of almonds and peeled garlic
3. When everything is very finely chopped, add powdered clove, cinnamon, pepper, saffron, chopped tomato and parsley
4. Mix well, then blend in four to six egg yolks and sufficient salt until a mass is formed
5. Form large balls and fill them with raisins, pieces of almonds, candied citron and beaten egg
6. Roll in breadcrumbs, fry and serve dry or in a light broth
