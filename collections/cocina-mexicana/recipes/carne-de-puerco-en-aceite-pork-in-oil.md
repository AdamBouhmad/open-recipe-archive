## Ingredients

- pork loin
- salt
- pepper
- all spices
- oil

## Directions

1. Take pork loin and slice as for beefsteak.
2. Roll in salt and pepper.
3. Place in a pot and cover with oil.
4. Add all spices, apply very low heat.
5. When cooked, remove and let cool.
6. Place in glass jars, cover with the same oil in which it cooked, and seal.
