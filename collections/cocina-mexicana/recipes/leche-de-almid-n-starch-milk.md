## Ingredients

- five cuartillos of milk
- two ounces of starch dissolved in milk
- five egg yolks
- cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Mix five cuartillos of sweetened milk with two ounces of starch dissolved in milk and five egg yolks
2. Incorporate everything well and strain
3. Place over heat and add cinnamon sticks
4. Allow to reach the proper point
