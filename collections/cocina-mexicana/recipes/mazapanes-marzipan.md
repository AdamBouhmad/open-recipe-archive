## Ingredients

- one pound of clarified sugar
- one pound of ground almonds

## Directions

1. Boil the sugar giving it the point of hard crack
2. Mix the almonds with the syrup and put on the fire until it reaches the point when tested by hand with water, and if it no longer sticks, it is ready
3. Remove from the fire and with wet hand make small balls
4. Score them like chocolate and place on wafers
5. Immediately take to the oven or place in a pan with its cover
6. As soon as they take the color of bread, remove them, peel off the wafer and place on a plate, or take a thick syrup in which you put slices of green lemons; place the marzipans on top and sprinkle them with ground sugar
