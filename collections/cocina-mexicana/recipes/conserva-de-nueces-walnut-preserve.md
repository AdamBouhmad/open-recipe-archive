## Ingredients

- Walnuts, picked before June at end of May
- Water
- Sugar syrup (almíbar)
- Orange blossom water (agua de azahar)
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper, three grains

## Directions

1. Peel from the first thin shell and puncture in three or four places, passing them side to side with something suitable, for example a spindle, large awl or punch tool
2. Place in water for nine days, changing daily
3. After, place in boiling water giving two or three boils
4. Remove when tender and drain
5. Clarify four quartillos of sugar syrup for one hundred walnuts and place in it cold
6. Bring to boil over low heat until the honey cooks
7. Remove and add orange blossom water, clove, cinnamon and three grains of pepper
