## Ingredients

- Salt
- Saltpeter
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Basil
- Balm mint
- Marjoram
- Ginger
- Texocote water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Make a brine with salt, saltpeter, thyme, laurel, basil, balm mint, marjoram, and ginger
2. Add clarified syrup at the mirror point, made with one arroba of sugar for each jar of texocote water, or eighteen cuartillos of the same
3. Return the pan to the fire and skim well without stirring the mixture
4. Let it boil until it can be divided with a spoon, which indicates the proper point
5. Skim again off the fire and let it rest a moment
6. Pour with a jug into molds or dishes
7. The next day begin to place in the sun so the jelly forms a sugared crust on top, or leave in the shade to dry without a crust on the surface
