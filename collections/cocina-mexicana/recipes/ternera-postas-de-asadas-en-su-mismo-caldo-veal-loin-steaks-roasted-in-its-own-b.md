## Ingredients

- Veal meat
- Vinegar
- Avocado leaves
- Salt
- Onion, finely chopped
- Oil
- Olives

## Directions

1. Soak the veal meat from the day before in vinegar
2. The following day, cook it with avocado leaves and the necessary salt, letting its broth be consumed and allowing it to roast
3. When roasted, slice it and serve with finely chopped onion, oil, and olives on top
