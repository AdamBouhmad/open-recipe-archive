## Ingredients

- Celery stalks
- Salt
- Oil
- Garlic
- Vinegar
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Peel the celery stalks as in the previous recipe and cook with salt
2. Before they are very cooked, remove and drain them
3. Place a pot on the fire with oil, fry sliced clean garlic cloves in it, and remove when golden
4. Add the cooked celery and a little vinegar and fry well
5. Grind the garlic that was fried and mix with the oil in which the celery was fried
6. Sprinkle the celery with salt and pepper
7. Pour the oil with garlic on top
8. Then dress with raw oil and vinegar seasoned with salt
9. Decorate with small cooked onions
