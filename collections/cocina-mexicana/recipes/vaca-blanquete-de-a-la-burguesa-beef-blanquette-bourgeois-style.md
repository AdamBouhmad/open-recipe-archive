## Ingredients

- square or loin of beef
- lard slices
- white substance
- consumed broth
- egg yolks
- fresh butter
- lemon juice
- parsley
- fried croutons

## Directions

1. Cook a square or loin of beef on the roasting spit
2. When cooked, let cool immediately
3. Carefully remove the sirloin and cut into small pieces the size of a coin
4. Place in a saucepan between slices of lard
5. Let warm for half an hour in a bain-marie in a stove
6. Clarify and reduce two ladles of white substance with a little consumed broth
7. Bind with three egg yolks
8. Add four ounces of fresh butter, the juice of one lemon, and a little minced parsley
9. Add the beef blanquette to this sauce and serve with fried croutons around it
