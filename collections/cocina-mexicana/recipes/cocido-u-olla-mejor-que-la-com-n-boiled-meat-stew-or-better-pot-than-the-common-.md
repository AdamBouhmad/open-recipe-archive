## Ingredients

- mutton
- beef jerky or pork jerky
- chorizo sausages
- longaniza sausage
- oxtail
- ham
- chickpeas
- rice
- salt
- chicken
- garlic
- onion
- parsnips
- mint

## Directions

1. From the day before, skin and desalt the mutton, place it to soak in cold water, preferably well water.
2. In another water, soak the beef or pork jerky, chorizo sausages, longaniza sausage, a good oxtail, and a piece of ham; also add the chickpeas and a good handful of rice.
3. The next morning, wash and clean the mutton well by skinning it and wash a chicken with it in cold water.
4. Put this meat in a pot with competent water; clean the oxtail, ham, and other meat well with a knife and hot water, adding everything to the pot except the ham which should remain soaking.
5. Add the soaked chickpeas and necessary salt.
6. Put the pot on the fire and remove all the foam it produces.
7. When it has finished foaming, add two whole heads of garlic, two of onion, two parsnips, and a sprig of mint.
8. After one hour of boiling with these ingredients, add half a cabbage.
