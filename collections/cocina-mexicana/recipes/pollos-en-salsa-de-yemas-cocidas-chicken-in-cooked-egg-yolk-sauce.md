## Ingredients

- burned lard
- garlic
- onions
- tomatoes, all finely chopped
- salt
- chopped parsley
- cloves
- toasted cilantro
- pepper
- cooked egg yolks, ground
- wine
- chicken broth
- cooked chicken

## Directions

1. Fry finely chopped garlic, onions, and tomatoes in burned lard and season with salt
2. Add chopped parsley, cloves, toasted cilantro, pepper, and ground cooked egg yolks
3. Add wine and the broth in which the chicken was cooked
4. Place the chicken in this sauce
5. Let simmer until it reaches the proper thickness and is well seasoned
