## Ingredients

- water
- vinegar
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook water in a pot with half vinegar and sufficient salt
2. Remove from the fire
3. Break one by one the eggs that you want to poach without breaking them or stirring
4. When the whites are set remove them, starting with the first ones, and put them in cold water
