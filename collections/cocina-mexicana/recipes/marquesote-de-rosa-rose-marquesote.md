## Ingredients

- Clarified syrup
- Egg whites
- Lemon juice
- Rose
- Grageé

## Directions

1. Clarify the syrup and beat egg whites
2. For each pound of syrup add two egg whites and beat until very stiff
3. The syrup should be almost at the taffy stage
4. For each pound of sugar add the juice of one lemon, which is added when at the proper point, which is when dipping a finger in cold water it forms a thimble shape
5. Then remove from heat and add the rose and beat strongly with all your strength
6. Put in the mold and add grageé on top
7. The rose is added according to each person's taste
