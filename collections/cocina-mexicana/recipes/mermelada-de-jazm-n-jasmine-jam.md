## Ingredients

- Jasmine flowers
- 1.5 pounds sugar per pound of jasmine flowers

## Directions

1. Make syrup at high feather stage with one and a half pounds of sugar for each pound of jasmine flowers
2. Remove from heat and when lukewarm, dissolve the jasmine flowers in it, crushed and passed through a sieve
