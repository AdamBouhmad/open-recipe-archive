## Ingredients

- jicotea
- sugar

## Directions

1. Clean and remove the heart, give one boil
2. Defleme in the sun, changing the water and tasting until it loses its flavor
3. Add to syrup and boil until penetrated and turns golden brown
4. Cool and add flavoring
