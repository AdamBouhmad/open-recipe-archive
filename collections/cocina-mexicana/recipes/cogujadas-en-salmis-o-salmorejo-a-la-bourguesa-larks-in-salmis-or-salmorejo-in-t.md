## Ingredients

- roasted larks
- bread toasts
- broth
- salt
- coarse pepper
- fine garlic
- vinegar
- fried croutons

## Directions

1. Use roasted larks that have been served at table
2. Remove the heads and contents from the body
3. Discard the gizzards and pound everything else with the toasts in a mortar or pestle
4. Dissolve the resulting paste with a little broth
5. Strain and season with salt, coarse pepper, a little pounded fine garlic, and a dash of vinegar
6. Heat the larks in this broth without boiling
7. Serve decorated with fried croutons
