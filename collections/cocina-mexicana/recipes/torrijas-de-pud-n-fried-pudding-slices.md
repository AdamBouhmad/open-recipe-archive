## Ingredients

- pudding ingredients
- eggs
- lard
- clarified syrup
- pine nuts
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix all pudding ingredients and wrap in a napkin tied very tightly
2. Hang in a pot with little water and cook by steam until set
3. Cool completely
4. Remove the crust from the surface with a knife
5. Slice or cut into appropriate pieces
6. Coat with beaten egg and fry in lard
7. Give a quick boil in clarified syrup
8. When syrup reaches conserve point, place torrijas with syrup in plates
9. Decorate with pine nuts and dust with cinnamon
