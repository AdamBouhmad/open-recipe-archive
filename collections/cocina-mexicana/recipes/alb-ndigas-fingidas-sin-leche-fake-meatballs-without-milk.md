## Ingredients

- twenty-four cooked egg yolks
- one pound of almonds, soaked and ground, sprinkled with water

## Directions

1. Make the paste by mixing well twenty-four cooked egg yolks and one pound of almonds, soaked and ground, and sprinkled with water.
