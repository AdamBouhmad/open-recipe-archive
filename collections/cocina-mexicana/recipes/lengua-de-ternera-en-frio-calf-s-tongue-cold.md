## Ingredients

- Calf's tongue
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Water
- Clove, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Black pepper, ground
- Lettuce, finely minced
- Thin onion slices
- Oil
- Salt
- Small hot peppers (optional)
- Olives (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the tongue in wine and vinegar with very little water
2. Cut it into thin slices
3. Place the slices on a platter and dust with ground clove, cinnamon, and pepper
4. Sprinkle with wine and vinegar
5. Cover with finely minced lettuce and thin onion slices
6. Pour oil over the top
7. Serve cold and, if desired, garnish with small hot peppers and olives, seasoning in all cases with salt
