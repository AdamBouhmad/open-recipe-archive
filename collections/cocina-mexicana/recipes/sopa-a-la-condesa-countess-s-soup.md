## Ingredients

- Purple beans
- Beef broth
- Two carrots
- Two onions
- Lard or fat from the pot
- Two cloves (spice)
- Bread crusts
- Butter

## Directions

1. Put purple beans to cook in broth, adding at the same time two carrots, two onions, some lard or fat from the pot, and two cloves
2. When the beans are cooked until they fall apart, pass them through a sieve to make a clear substance
3. Pour this substance over bread crusts fried in butter
