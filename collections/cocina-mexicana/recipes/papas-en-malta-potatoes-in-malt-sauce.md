## Ingredients

- cooked potatoes
- potato flour
- butter
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- anchovies (optional)
- capers (optional)

## Directions

1. Cook and cut the potatoes, keeping them as hot as possible.
2. Dissolve potato flour with butter, broth, salt and pepper over low heat.
3. Once the sauce is sufficiently thickened, add chopped anchovies or capers if desired.
4. Pour the sauce over the potatoes.
5. Serve as hot as possible.
