## Ingredients

- Eight ounces of orange blossoms
- Twelve ounces of sugar
- Eight cuartillos of water

## Directions

1. Dissolve twelve ounces of sugar with eight cuartillos of water
2. Pour boiling over the orange blossoms placed in a vessel
3. Cover well and let infuse for six hours
4. Strain through a sieve and freeze
