## Ingredients

- peels of two bitter oranges, descarnated
- game essence

## Directions

1. Place the descarnated peels of two bitter oranges (removing the green or yellow part of the peel and separating it from the white part left attached to the orange) in boiling water
2. Remove immediately from the water, let drain, and place in game essence
