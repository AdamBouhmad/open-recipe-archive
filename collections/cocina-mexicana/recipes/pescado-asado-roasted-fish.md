## Ingredients

- fish
- butter
- white wine
- bread crumbs
- parsley

## Directions

1. After scaling and cleaning well, remove the head.
2. Dry well with a napkin.
3. Place on the grill until it takes on a good golden color, with gentle heat.
4. Then in a pan or skillet, add a quarter pound of butter for every two pounds of fish.
5. Melt it, removing the foam.
6. Add half a small glass of white wine, bread crumbs, and parsley.
7. Make a sauce by bringing it to a boil a couple of times.
8. Drizzle the fish with this sauce at the time of serving.
