## Ingredients

- raw lamb
- butter
- onion
- garlic
- raw tomato
- water
- spices
- pepper
- Tabasco
- clove
- oregano
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut the raw lamb into pieces and fry it well until golden
2. Chop onion and garlic and brown in the same butter
3. Add crushed raw tomato to what is frying
4. When everything is well fried, add water so the meat cooks but it should not become watery
5. Add spices, pepper, Tabasco, clove, oregano and oil
