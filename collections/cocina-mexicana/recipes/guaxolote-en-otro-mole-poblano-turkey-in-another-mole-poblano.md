## Ingredients

- Turkey meat
- One pound of pasilla chile peppers (deveined)
- One pound of mulato chile peppers (deveined)
- Wide and pasilla chile peppers (if mulato is not available)
- One pot-full of chile seeds
- An equal amount of sesame seeds
- Pork knuckle pieces
- Pork jowl pieces
- Lard
- Water
- Salt
- One small basket of cooked tomatoes
- Half an ounce of cinnamon
- One quarter ounce of cloves
- Ten fine peppercorns
- One handful of toasted cilantro
- A little anise

## Directions

1. Two days after killing the turkey, cut it into regular pieces
2. Devein one pound of pasilla and one pound of mulato chiles, or if mulato is unavailable, use one and a half pounds of wide and half a pound of pasilla, and toast them on a comal carefully so they do not burn
3. Toast one pot-full of chile seeds and an equal amount of sesame seeds, and grind them well
4. Fry the portions of raw turkey with pork knuckle and jowl pieces
5. When well fried, add everything ground, mixed with competent water and the appropriate salt, and let it boil
6. After it has boiled for some time, grind one small basket of cooked tomatoes and add them to the pot, covering the meat with hot water so it cooks
7. When the turkey is done, grind half an ounce of cinnamon, one quarter ounce of cloves, ten fine peppercorns, one handful of toasted cilantro, and a little anise, and if there is not enough lard, burn what is needed and add it
8. Let it boil until the mole has thickened well, then serve with toasted sesame seeds on top
9. If it is not desired to make it with pork jowl and knuckle, add more lard
