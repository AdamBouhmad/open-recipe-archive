## Ingredients

- Peach
- Sugar
- Texocote jelly or fish glue

## Directions

1. Cook the peach the same way as for making peach preserve
2. Mash it by hand in its own water
3. Proceed as for the jellies of the previous articles
4. If greater consistency is desired, mix sweet texocote jelly or, better, fish glue according to the explanation given before in orange jelly
