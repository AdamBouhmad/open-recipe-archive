## Ingredients

- all scraps of poultry, cooked or raw, as available
- two pounds of beef
- one parsnip
- one onion studded with two cloves
- one small mixed bunch of herbs
- concentrated or reduced common broth

## Directions

1. Collect all the scraps of poultry and place them in a pot with the beef, parsnip, and onion studded with cloves
2. Add the mixed bunch of herbs
3. Moisten with concentrated or reduced common broth
4. Leave the pot on the fire until the meats are cooked
5. Pass the concentrated broth through a silk sieve
6. Fill a cooking pan with it up to the rim
7. Place over the edge of the stove, remove the fat, clarify with three egg whites, adding a little cold water as it boils
8. Pass through a fine cloth
9. Let it reduce or concentrate to the point of semi-gelatine
10. Keep for when needed
