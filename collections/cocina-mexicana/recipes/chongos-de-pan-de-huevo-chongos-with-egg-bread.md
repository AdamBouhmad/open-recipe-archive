## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- nine egg yolks
- fresh butter
- cold egg bread or sponge cake
- syrup

## Directions

1. Make a high-point syrup with one pound of sugar and let it cool
2. Beat nine egg yolks with a little fresh butter and mix with cold syrup, which is put on the heat to give one boil, then remove
3. In a new casserole place a layer of slices of cold egg bread or sponge cake, another of syrup, and continue this way until the casserole is full
4. Place this over two fires until the chongos are cooked
