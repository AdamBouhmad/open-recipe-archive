## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- water
- lemon juice
- butter
- curled sauce
- concentrated broth
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the mushrooms after cutting off the end of the stem, and sauté them in a little water with lemon juice
2. Chop them, squeeze them in clean cloth and fry them afterwards in butter with lemon juice until the butter converts to oil
3. Add then some spoonfuls of curled sauce and an equal amount of concentrated broth
4. Allow to reduce and add a good amount of black pepper
5. As an alternative to curled sauce, a little flour can be used, moistened with concentrated or condensed broth
