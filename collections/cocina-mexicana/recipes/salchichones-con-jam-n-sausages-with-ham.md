## Ingredients

- Equal parts leg meat and fatty pork meat
- Ham equal to one-third the weight of the meat
- All kinds of ground spices
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- White wine
- Intestines

## Directions

1. Mince equal amounts of leg meat and fatty pork
2. Add one-third ham cut into small squares and mix with the meat
3. Mix with all kinds of ground spices, lemon juice, sufficient salt, and white wine
4. Fill intestines, dividing them at short intervals with tightly tied twine
5. Cook in salted boiling water
6. Once cooked, hang in the air to dry
7. Serve grilled or fried
