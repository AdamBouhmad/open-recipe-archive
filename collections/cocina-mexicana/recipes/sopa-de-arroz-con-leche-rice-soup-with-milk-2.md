## Ingredients

- rice, well rinsed with warm water
- milk, sufficient quantity
- salt or sugar, to taste

## Directions

1. Cook the rinsed rice in sufficient milk
2. Season with salt or sugar according to taste
3. Serve immediately
4. Stir frequently during cooking
5. It can also be made with almond milk (using one quarter pound of sweet almonds and four bitter almonds to make half an azumbre of milk)
