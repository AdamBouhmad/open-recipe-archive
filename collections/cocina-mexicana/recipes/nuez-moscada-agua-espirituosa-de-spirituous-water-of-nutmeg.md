## Ingredients

- nutmeg
- refined Spanish brandy
- water
- sugar

## Directions

1. Scrape rather than grind the nutmeg
2. Distill in fourteen cuartillos of refined Spanish brandy with a little water over moderate heat, continuing until the phlegm or tails begin to fall
3. For common quality: make syrup with one and a half pounds of sugar and ten cuartillos of water
4. For double quality: make syrup with eight cuartillos of water and two pounds of sugar
5. For fine and dry quality: make syrup with twelve to sixteen cuartillos of brandy and eight cuartillos of water with one and a half pounds of sugar
6. Mix the spirit with the syrup, filter in the customary manner
7. Once clear, the operation is complete
