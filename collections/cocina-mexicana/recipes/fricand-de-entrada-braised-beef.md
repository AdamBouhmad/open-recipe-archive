## Ingredients

- a small beef cut
- fine lardons
- water
- butter
- ham scraps
- salt
- carrots
- large whole onions
- bay leaf
- a charred or roasted onion

## Directions

1. Take a small beef cut, lard it with fine lardons, and place it in a saucepan with water to cover it, adding a piece of butter, ham scraps, salt, carrots, large whole onions, and a bay leaf
2. Let it cook over low heat for three consecutive hours
3. Then pour the broth into a small saucepan with a charred or roasted onion, and let it reduce until it reaches the consistency of aspic, binding it with this essence and browning the fricandó in it
4. Place the other things on top if it is to be served alone; but if it is to be served as seasoning or sauce for a chicory, sorrel or beef tongue stew, the whole fricandó will be poured over the cooked greens
5. The fricandó can also be served without these greens with tomato sauce
