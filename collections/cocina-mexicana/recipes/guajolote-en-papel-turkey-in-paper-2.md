## Ingredients

- turkey
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- sugar
- lemon juice
- cinnamon
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean the turkey and hang in the open air for twenty-four hours
2. Cook in a pot and when half-cooked, place on a roasting spit with butter and a dusting of salt and pepper
3. Insert cloves and wrap in brown paper, leaving to roast over gentle heat
4. When roasted, make a sauce with a little sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, pepper and broth, and drizzle over it
