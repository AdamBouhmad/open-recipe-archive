## Ingredients

- huacamote
- milk
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Grind huacamote and dissolve with milk (four cuartillos of milk to four large huacamotes)
2. Strain and add sugar and orange blossom water
3. Place in a dish over the hot coals and set a comal on top so that it curdles
