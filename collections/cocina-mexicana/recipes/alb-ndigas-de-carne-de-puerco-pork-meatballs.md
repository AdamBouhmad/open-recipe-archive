## Ingredients

- lomo and lean pork meat
- clove powder
- cinnamon
- cumin
- saffron, ground and mixed with garlic
- minced herbabuena (mint)
- lard
- raw egg

## Directions

1. Mince together loin and lean pork meat
2. Grind in a metate, removing scraps and tendons as they appear
3. Mix the ground meat with clove powder, cinnamon, cumin, saffron ground with garlic and minced herbabuena
4. Mix well with some lard and a raw egg
5. Form into meatballs
6. Cook in broth with salt, parsley and herbabuena
7. When half-cooked, place in a tomato broth with onion, parsley and minced herbabuena, green chiles and deflemmed lemon
8. Thicken with ground almonds
9. When the broth is sufficiently thick, serve
