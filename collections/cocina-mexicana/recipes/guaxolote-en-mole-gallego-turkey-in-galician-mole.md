## Ingredients

- Wide dried chile pepper (deveined)
- Toasted almonds (small amount)
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Nutmeg
- Lard
- Salt
- Hot water
- A small piece of sugar
- Raw turkey meat

## Directions

1. Fry the deveined wide chile pepper and grind it with a few toasted almonds, toasted sesame seeds, and nutmeg, all three in equal quantity
2. Fry everything in lard, add the necessary salt, a little hot water, and a small piece of sugar
3. Place the raw turkey in pieces and let it cook until the sauce thickens well
