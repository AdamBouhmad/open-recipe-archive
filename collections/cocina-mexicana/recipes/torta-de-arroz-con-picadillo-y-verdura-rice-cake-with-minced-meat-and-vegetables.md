## Ingredients

- meat
- rice
- salt
- cumin
- saffron
- butter
- garlic
- onions
- thyme
- parsley
- mint
- ginger
- chile strips
- olives

## Directions

1. Mince and cook the meat.
2. Cook the rice and season with salt, cumin, and saffron until it comes out very dry.
3. Butter a pan with your hand.
4. Place in it a layer of rice, another of garlic, onions, thyme, parsley, mint, ginger, cumin, chile strips, and olives, all fried, and another on top of minced meat.
5. Cover everything with rice.
6. Cook with little heat below and much heat above.
