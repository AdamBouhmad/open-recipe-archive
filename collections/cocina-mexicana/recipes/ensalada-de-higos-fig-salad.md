## Ingredients

- green figs
- straw
- cinnamon
- ground pepper
- syrup at medium point
- white wine
- raisins
- almonds
- sugar
- citron
- clean walnuts
- ground cinnamon
- sesame seeds in grain
- long plantain

## Directions

1. Cut the green figs into four parts and place them to cook with a handful of straw on top; once cooked, wash them with hot water so they cool, and with settled ash to remove their stickiness.
2. Add to the figs ground cinnamon and pepper, and with syrup at medium point place them on the fire to give one boil.
3. After removing them, keep them at rest for two days without touching them, and then drain the syrup, pouring over the figs on a platter white wine, raisins, almonds, sugar, citron, clean walnuts and ground cinnamon, sesame seeds in grain and long plantain.
