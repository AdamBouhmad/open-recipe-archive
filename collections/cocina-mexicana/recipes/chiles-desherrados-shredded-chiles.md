## Ingredients

- Green poblano chiles, tornachiles, or small chiles that are dried and called chilaca
- Lard
- Onion
- Hot water
- Epazote branches
- Fine salt
- Aged cheese
- Fresh cheese
- Avocado
- Oil, optional

## Directions

1. Roast green poblano chiles, tornachiles, or dried chilacas, and peel them
2. Shred the chiles
3. Heat lard in a cooking vessel over the fire and fry them well
4. Once fried, slice an onion and add it to the chiles
5. Moisten with a little hot water and add epazote branches
6. Season with fine salt and crumble a little aged cheese into it
7. Let it boil until the water is consumed and the chile is fried
8. Remove from the fire and place in a small dish
9. Slice good fresh cheese over it, add slices of peeled avocado, and if desired, a little oil
