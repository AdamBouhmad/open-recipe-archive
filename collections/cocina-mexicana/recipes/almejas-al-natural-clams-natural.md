## Ingredients

- clams
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the clams and make them open as will be described ahead.
2. Once clean and one shell removed, wash them until the water runs clear.
3. Add only the water necessary for them to cook.
4. Add a little butter and parsley.
5. Once they boil, serve them, adding lemon juice if deemed convenient.
