## Ingredients

- brains
- pig feet
- chorizo
- pork loin slices
- chicken
- peas
- beets
- spinach
- eggs
- brains sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare brains, pig feet, chorizo, sliced pork loins, chicken, peas, beets, and fine spinach.
2. Mix very well with beaten eggs.
3. Cook the cake at two heats as with the previous ones.
4. For the sauce, mix tomatoes with garlic and fine spices, all ground, and fry with onions.
5. Add pine nuts, raisins, almonds, cooked onions, chilote, olives, salt, and a pinch of sweetness.
6. Top with sesame seeds and serve in a sauce boat.
