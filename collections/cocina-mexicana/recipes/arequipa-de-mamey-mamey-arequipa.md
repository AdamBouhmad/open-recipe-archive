## Ingredients

- 1 cuartillo milk
- 1 pound sugar
- 0.5 mamey fruit, ground

## Directions

1. Add one pound of sugar to one cuartillo of milk.
2. When it reaches half point, add the ground mamey.
3. If the mamey has fibers, strain it through cloth mixed with a little milk.
