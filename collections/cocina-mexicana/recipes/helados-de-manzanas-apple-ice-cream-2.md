## Ingredients

- twenty bittersweet apples
- a little water
- twelve ounces of powdered sugar or syrup
- juice of two lemons

## Directions

1. Choose twenty bittersweet apples
2. Peel them, cut into quarters more or less large
3. Remove the core and seeds
4. Place over gentle heat with a little water and allow to cook until reduced to a fine paste
5. Pour into a hair sieve placed over a pan so it strains there
6. Mix with twelve ounces of powdered sugar or syrup
7. Make the mixture with care, cool it
8. Squeeze the juice of two lemons over it and freeze
