## Ingredients

- Cooked and cut lamb offal
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic
- Butter
- Lettuce leaves
- Offal broth
- Cooked chickpeas
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Pepper
- Oil
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. With the offal cooked and cut into small pieces, chop plenty of tomato and onion with a little garlic
2. Fry in butter, add some chopped lettuce leaves
3. When well fried, add the offal with some broth in which it was cooked and some well cooked chickpeas
4. Grind cinnamon, clove and pepper
5. The sauce should be thick, when serving add oil and garnish with chiles, olives and capers
