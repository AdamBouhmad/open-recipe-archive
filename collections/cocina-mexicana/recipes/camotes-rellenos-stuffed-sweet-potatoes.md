## Ingredients

- small white sweet potatoes
- picadillo of pork meat
- egg
- lard

## Directions

1. Choose small, white sweet potatoes without fibers
2. Once cooked, divide them lengthwise and between the two halves place picadillo of pork meat
3. Coat with beaten egg and fry in lard
4. Serve dry or in a tomato broth like that of stuffed chiles, or in nogada sauce
