## Ingredients

- Three beef shank bones from the leg of cow
- Two chickens
- Four carrots
- Four onions, one of which studded with two cloves
- A large bunch of parsley and small onions
- One tablespoon of concentrated broth
- Twenty mushrooms, sautéed cold in water with lemon
- Butter
- Wheat flour

## Directions

1. Place the beef shanks, chickens, carrots, onions (one studded with cloves), and the bunch of parsley and small onions in a saucepan
2. Add one tablespoon of concentrated broth and place the saucepan over high heat, taking care to skim the ingredients well
3. When the broth has reduced, as known by the large bubbles when boiling, add concentrated broth, keeping it well liquid and not allowing it to take on too dark a color
4. Fill the saucepan with concentrated broth, carefully skimming it well, and when it boils, move it to a corner or edge of the stove
5. Make a white substance by melting butter in a saucepan and adding wheat flour as much as it can absorb, stirring constantly over the fire without letting it take on color
6. Add the twenty mushrooms sautéed cold in water with lemon to the white substance, mixing them well with the hot white substance
7. Dilute this white substance with the small amount of broth from the sauce, then pour it over the ingredients
8. Let the sauce boil in the corner of the stove, skimming it well, and after an hour and a half, remove the fat
9. Once the ingredients are cooked, pass the sauce through a fine sieve, ensuring it remains as white as possible
10. Use the sauce as needed
