## Ingredients

- Wild boar leg
- Ham strips
- Spices
- Aromatics
- Salt
- Pepper
- Ginger
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Basil
- Large onions
- Parsley
- Small headed onions
- Six bottles of white wine
- Equal quantity of water
- Six carrots
- Six large onions
- Five cloves
- Parsley bunch with small onions
- Gelatin
- Breadcrumbs (optional)

## Directions

1. Singe the wild boar leg well at the flame and scald it
2. Debone it down to the elbow
3. Lard it with thick strips of ham seasoned with ground spices and aromatics
4. Place it in a bowl with salt, pepper, ginger, thyme, laurel, basil, large onions, parsley and small headed onions
5. Leave to marinate for four or five days
6. When ready to cook, remove the aromatics from the interior
7. Wrap in clean cloth, tying it like a piece of beef
8. Place in a suitable vessel for cooking over coals with the brine from marinating, six bottles of white wine, equal quantity of water, six carrots, six large onions, five cloves of spice, a bunch of parsley with small onions, and salt if needed
9. Cook over gentle fire for six hours
10. Test in various places to ensure it is well cooked
11. Leave half an hour in its cooking liquid away from the fire; if not well cooked, let it boil another half hour more
12. Leave with its crust, or if desired, cover with breadcrumbs, or if the leg is fatty, remove the crust leaving it white
13. Cover with gelatin, ensuring it has a fine form
