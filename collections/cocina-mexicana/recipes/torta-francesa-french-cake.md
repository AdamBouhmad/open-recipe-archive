## Ingredients

- Tongue, cooked
- Pork feet, cooked
- Ham, cooked
- Pork loin, cooked
- Tomato picadillo
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- A little sugar
- Eggs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Tongue, pork feet, ham and loin, all well cooked and chopped, make a picadillo of tomato, add the meat with raisins, almonds, pine nuts and a little sugar; beat some eggs and mix everything, cooking it over two fires in a skillet spread with butter.
