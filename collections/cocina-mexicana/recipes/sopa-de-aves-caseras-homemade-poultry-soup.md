## Ingredients

- poultry scraps
- bread
- broth

## Directions

1. Cook the poultry scraps after being cut into pieces in a pot
2. When they are well cooked, remove them and grind them with bread crumbs, which are dissolved with their broth
3. Pass everything through a strainer
4. Soak the soup with the broth in which the poultry was cooked, and pour the substance we mentioned on top
