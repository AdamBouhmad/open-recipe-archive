## Ingredients

- Black figs, not yet ripe
- Clarified syrup at medium point
- Ground and sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and cook the black figs that are not yet ripe
2. Cover them as they come out of the water so air does not strike them, as they would be damaged
3. Let them drain covered
4. Place them in clarified syrup at medium point to give them a boil each day over low heat until they are well penetrated
5. To preserve, roll them in ground and sifted sugar
