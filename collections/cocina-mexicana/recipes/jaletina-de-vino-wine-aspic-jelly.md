## Ingredients

- veal feet
- sugar
- sweet oranges
- cinnamon
- brandy
- muscatel wine
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook three veal feet until they fall apart completely; strain the broth
2. Measure eight cuartillos and remove the fat by straining through a damp napkin with hot broth
3. Place one pound and nine ounces of sugar, two sweet oranges with peel cut into pieces and one peeled, a little cinnamon in sticks, half a cuartillo of brandy, half of muscatel wine, and four eggs with shell
4. Place the pot on active heat without flame, because the brandy will ignite; when it comes to a boil, add the broth to boil quickly
5. Once boiling, pour it into the filter, being careful to pour out what comes through coated; when it cools, put a little of everything that remained above in the pot, taking the same care, heat it and return to the filter
6. To remove from the mold, place it in hot water for a moment and turn it over
