## Ingredients

- Eighteen eggs, separated
- One pound of sugar
- One pound of melted and warm butter
- Three handfuls of white yeast
- A little warm water with settled tequesquite
- Flour as needed
- Egg white for glazing
- Coarse sugar for sprinkling

## Directions

1. Beat the eighteen eggs, keeping whites and yolks separate
2. Join them together and mix with one pound of sugar, one pound of melted warm butter, three handfuls of white yeast, and a little warm water with settled tequesquite
3. Add as much flour as can be moistened with this mixture
4. Knead everything together
5. Form the rosquetes
6. Place them in the sun for a while to air dry
7. Coat the top with egg white
8. Sprinkle with coarse sugar passed through a fine sieve
9. Bake in the oven until cooked
