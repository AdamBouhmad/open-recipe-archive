## Ingredients

- One turtle
- Onion chives
- Parsley
- A little tomato
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Pepper
- Four egg yolks
- Flour
- Water
- Half a bottle of Port wine, Madeira, or Sherry
- Half a bottle of Bordeaux wine
- Egg slices for garnish

## Directions

1. Remove the turtle from its shell and cook it, then once cooked, chop it, leaving everything in the broth in which it was cooked.
2. Fry the soffritto composed of onion chives, parsley and a little tomato.
3. When fried, add the chopped meat and the broth, and put everything to cook on a gentle fire.
4. Grind a little cinnamon, clove and pepper and add it to the broth.
5. Then grind four egg yolks, make a broth composed of water and flour, and if it does not turn out thick enough, fry more flour which will be added to the broth with half a bottle of Port, Madeira or Sherry wine and half a bottle of Bordeaux wine.
6. When serving the soup at the table, garnish it with egg slices.
