## Ingredients

- four quartillos of milk
- eight egg yolks
- orange blossom leaves
- cinnamon sticks
- half pound of soaked and ground almonds
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Sweeten four quartillos of milk and mix with eight egg yolks, straining after they are well dissolved
2. Put on the fire and add some orange blossom leaves, only to give it flavor, not so many that they turn the milk green, and some cinnamon sticks
3. When it has thickened from boiling, remove the orange blossom leaves and add half pound of soaked and ground almonds
4. Let the milk boil a little more and once it reaches a good consistency, remove the cinnamon sticks, pour into the plates and decorate with cinnamon powder on top
