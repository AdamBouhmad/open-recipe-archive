## Ingredients

- Several oranges
- Water
- Syrup at low point or clarified
- Batter for frying (see pasta para freír) or batter for fritters to cover fruits (page 106)
- Butter
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the colored part of the peel from several oranges and leave the white part
2. Divide them into quarters and blanch in boiling water for about a quarter hour
3. Drain them, remove the seeds and place the quarters thus prepared to cook in syrup at low point or simply clarified, letting them boil over low heat until the syrup rises almost to the point of caramel
4. Remove from heat and when cold, coat them in some batter for frying or batter for fritters to cover fruits and fry in butter until they turn golden and acquire a beautiful color
5. Remove them, dust with sugar and serve
