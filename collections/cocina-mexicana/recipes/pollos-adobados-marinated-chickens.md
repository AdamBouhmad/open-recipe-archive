## Ingredients

- chickens
- wine
- vinegar
- spices
- beaten egg
- nutmeg scraping
- grated cheese
- oil

## Directions

1. The day before cooking, put the chickens in a sauce made of equal parts wine and vinegar and whole spices.
2. The next day cook them in the same sauce.
3. When they are very cooked, fry them with beaten egg and serve with nutmeg scraping, grated cheese and oil.
