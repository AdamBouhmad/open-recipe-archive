## Ingredients

- Salt in grains
- Thyme
- Sage
- Laurel leaves
- Cloves
- Pepper grains
- Pork meat
- Ground salt
- Cold water

## Directions

1. Place a wooden trough or artisan box with a hole in its lower part on a wooden bench or tripod, and attach a tube for draining.
2. Cover the bottom of the trough with a layer of salt grains, thyme, sage, and laurel leaves with some cloves and pepper grains.
3. On this layer place the meat to cover it, beginning with the largest pieces.
4. Rub the meat well with ground salt on both sides and cover it with the salt mixed with the aromatic plants, and conclude with a layer of these same substances, which must completely cover the pieces of meat.
5. Fill the trough with a layer of salt and another of meat; the last layer must be of salt and substances.
6. Once done, pour over it one or two liters of cold water distributed evenly.
7. This filters through the meat, dissolves the salt and eventually drains out.
