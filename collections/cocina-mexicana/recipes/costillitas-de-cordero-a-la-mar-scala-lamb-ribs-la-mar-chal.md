## Ingredients

- lamb ribs
- bread crumbs
- german sauce
- egg yolks
- butter
- aspic or gelatin

## Directions

1. Prepare as lamb breast à la maríscala with the difference that the ribs do not need to be cooked over coals but covered with raw bread crumbs and placed on the grill
