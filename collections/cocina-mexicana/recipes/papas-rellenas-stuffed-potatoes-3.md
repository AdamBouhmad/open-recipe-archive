## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Cooked meat or minced ham
- Egg
- Onion
- Ham
- Parsley
- Bread crumb
- Butter
- Onion
- Broth or water

## Directions

1. Wash and peel the potatoes and make a hole in the middle with a knife and fill it with a stuffing made of cooked meat or minced ham, egg, onion, ham, parsley and bread crumb all well mixed
2. Before filling, the inside of the potato should be spread with butter
3. Once stuffed, put the potatoes in a saucepan with butter and onion to fry a little and add some broth or water, making a sauce
