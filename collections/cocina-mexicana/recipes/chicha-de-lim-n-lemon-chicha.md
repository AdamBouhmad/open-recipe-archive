## Ingredients

- Three peeled and ground pineapples
- Juice of twenty-four lemons
- Half an ounce of cinnamon
- Two pounds of sugar
- A little clove
- A little bread for color

## Directions

1. For three peeled and ground pineapples, add the juice of twenty-four lemons, half an ounce of cinnamon, two pounds of sugar, and a little clove.
2. Let rest for one day.
3. The following day, strain and add sugar until sweet to the palate.
4. Add a little bread to give it color.
