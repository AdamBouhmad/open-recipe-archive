## Ingredients

- butter
- six eggs
- small pieces of crumb from cold bread
- Spanish sauce, reduced or somewhat consumed

## Directions

1. Warm a little butter on a plate and break six eggs over it, placing the plate over hot coals
2. Cut small dice from the crumb of cold bread and fry them in butter; when it has turned reddish, drain the toasted bread pieces, scatter or spread them over the eggs and cover with a hot campaign oven
3. Once the eggs are cooked, pour Spanish sauce over them, reduced or somewhat consumed, and serve
