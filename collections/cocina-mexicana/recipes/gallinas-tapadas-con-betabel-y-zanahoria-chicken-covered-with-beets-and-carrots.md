## Ingredients

- beets
- carrots
- large onions
- chickens
- chorizos
- ham
- lard
- capers
- olives
- cured tornachile peppers
- hard-boiled eggs
- saffron
- cloves
- pepper
- wine
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Cook beets, carrots, and large onions
2. Mince the beets, slice the carrots, and separate the onion layers
3. Cook the chickens with chorizos and ham
4. Cut the cooked chickens into pieces and remove all large bones
5. Slice the chorizos and cut strips of ham
6. Grease a casserole dish with lard
7. Layer the vegetables, chorizos, and ham, beginning with the roots and onions
8. Add capers, olives, quarters of cured tornachiies, slices of hard-boiled egg, ground saffron, cloves, and pepper to the layers
9. Place the chicken pieces on top and cover with layers of the same ingredients in reverse order, so the onions and roots are on top
10. Pour wine mixed with chicken broth around the sides of the casserole in moderate quantity
11. Add burned lard over the final layer
12. Simmer over two fires, stirring occasionally with the casserole to avoid breaking up the dish
13. When somewhat dry, carefully empty the contents into the serving dish
