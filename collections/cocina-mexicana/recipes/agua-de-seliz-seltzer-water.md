## Ingredients

- water
- one drachm of tartaric acid
- one drachm of bicarbonate of soda

## Directions

1. Fill a bottle with water to the neck, which will be stoppered with a stopper that closes hermetically
2. Add one drachm of tartaric acid and one drachm of bicarbonate of soda to the water
3. Stopper the bottle immediately upon adding the ingredients and tie the stopper with a cord
4. After a quarter hour it can be drunk
5. It can also be drunk by adding a spoonful of currant syrup or any other syrup, and then it takes on a taste similar to Champagne wine
