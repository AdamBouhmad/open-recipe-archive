## Ingredients

- barley
- fresh butter
- salt
- almonds with a little sugar (optional)
- melon seeds and cucumber seeds, peeled (optional for cooling effect)

## Directions

1. Wash and peel the barley well
2. Bring it to a gentle boil in water for five or six hours until it has reduced to a creamy consistency
3. From the beginning add a little fresh butter
4. At the end, or when it is finishing cooking, add a little salt
5. If a more pleasant broth is desired, mix in some almonds with a little sugar; but if it is to be cooling, add peeled melon and cucumber seeds
