## Ingredients

- a piece of bread
- lard
- a few beans
- pepper
- clove
- beans
- water
- vinegar
- oregano
- salt
- aged cheese, ground
- oil
- small chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Toast a piece of bread in lard and grind it with a few beans, pepper, and clove.
2. Fry everything, then add the beans with equal proportions of water and vinegar according to the quantity of beans.
3. Let them boil, adding oregano and salt.
4. When bringing to the table, put aged ground cheese, oil, small chiles, and olives on top.
