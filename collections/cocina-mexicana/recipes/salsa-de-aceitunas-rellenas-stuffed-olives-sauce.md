## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound stuffed olives
- thick Spanish sauce
- 2 tablespoons oil

## Directions

1. Drop 1/2 pound stuffed olives into boiling water and remove immediately
2. After draining them, add them to thick Spanish sauce
3. Place in a water bath
4. At the moment of serving over the dishes that call for it, add 2 tablespoons of oil
