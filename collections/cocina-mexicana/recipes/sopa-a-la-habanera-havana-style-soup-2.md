## Ingredients

- bread
- garlic
- parsley
- grated cheese
- almonds or hazelnuts
- broth
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare the bread by first making it into long, thin strips
2. After toasting them, place them in a cooking pot, arranging them in layers, mixing between them chopped garlic, parsley, and grated cheese
3. Crush some toasted almonds or hazelnuts and add broth from the pot
4. Bring to a boil and add some cloves, or if preferred, cinnamon
5. Once cooked, place half in the soup
