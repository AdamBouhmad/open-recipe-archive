## Ingredients

- half pound of flour
- one pound of butter
- salt
- eight egg yolks
- sifted sugar as needed for regular sweetness

## Directions

1. Knead half a pound of flour with one pound of butter, with salt, eight egg yolks, and sifted sugar in the amount necessary so that the dough becomes regularly sweet
2. After kneading, form the rings which are flattened over paper
3. Place them between two griddles at two gentle fires
4. Once they are known to be cooked, remove them from the fire
