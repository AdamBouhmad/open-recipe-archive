## Ingredients

- potatoes, cooked and peeled
- onions, chopped
- mushrooms, chopped
- butter
- flour
- broth
- salt
- coarse pepper
- egg yolks
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Peel and slice the potatoes cooked in salted water, or better as indicated for salad à la française.
2. Place them in a pot with onions and mushrooms, all chopped, and a good piece of butter.
3. Macerate over the fire and add a little flour.
4. Turn them well on all sides and moisten with broth, seasoning with salt and coarse pepper.
5. Let them cook and once the sauce has been sufficiently consumed, bind it with egg yolks.
6. Add lemon juice or vinegar at the moment of serving.
