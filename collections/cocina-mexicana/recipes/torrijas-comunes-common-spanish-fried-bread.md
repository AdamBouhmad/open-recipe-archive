## Ingredients

- cold bread slices or sponge cake
- butter or lard
- wine
- beaten egg
- clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Take slices of cold bread or sponge cake
2. Spread with a little butter or lard, or soak in wine
3. Coat with beaten egg
4. Fry in butter or lard
5. Place in clarified syrup
