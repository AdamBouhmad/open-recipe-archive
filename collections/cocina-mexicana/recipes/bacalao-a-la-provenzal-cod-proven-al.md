## Ingredients

- cooked cod
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- pepper
- nutmeg
- oil
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Divide the cooked cod into slices and place in a dish or pan
2. Prepare the pan beforehand with butter mixed with finely chopped parsley, onion and garlic
3. Add pepper and nutmeg and a spoonful of oil on top
4. Cover with the same mixture of butter and seasonings
5. Sprinkle again with another spoonful of oil
6. Cover the entire surface with bread crumbs soaked in oil
7. Place over two gentle fires and serve
