## Ingredients

- Tendons from a trussed bird
- Bread crumbs
- Cream
- Egg yolk
- Bread crumbs for coating
- Beaten egg
- Fried parsley for serving

## Directions

1. Remove the tendons from a trussed bird and mince them finely
2. Mix with bread crumbs soaked in cream and egg yolk
3. Beat and season the mixture well
4. Form into small balls
5. Wrap them in bread crumbs
6. Moisten them in beaten egg and bread again
7. Fry them and serve in pyramids with fried parsley underneath
