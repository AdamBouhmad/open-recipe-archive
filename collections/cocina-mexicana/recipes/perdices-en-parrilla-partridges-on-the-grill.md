## Ingredients

- partridges
- butter
- seasoning
- sauce a la pebre or orange sauce

## Directions

1. Cut in two pieces by the kidneys.
2. Flatten after roasting and frying.
3. Simmer in a pan with a little butter and good seasoning, being careful to turn them on both sides.
4. Then place on the grill, grease and serve with a sauce a la pebre or orange sauce.
