## Ingredients

- Squash, not too firm
- Pepper
- Salt
- Tomatoes, chopped
- Raw oil
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Choose squash that is not too firm and cut into thin slices
2. Place on the bottom of a casserole, sprinkle with pepper, salt and chopped tomatoes, removing the skin first by placing them over coals
3. Place other slices in the same manner, bathe with raw oil and place over two fires
4. It is good to add breadcrumbs on top
