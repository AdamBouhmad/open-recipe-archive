## Ingredients

- equal quantities of turnips and carrots
- somewhat less onions
- celery
- leeks
- lettuce
- parsley
- butter
- broth
- sugar
- black pepper
- thin bread slices
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. Cut turnips and carrots into equal dice.
2. Cut onions, celery, and leeks into smaller dice.
3. Chop lettuce and parsley coarsely.
4. Fry carrots in butter first.
5. When half-fried, add the turnips.
6. A few moments later, add the onions, leeks, and celery.
7. When everything is well-softened and good color, add the herbs and fry them together with the vegetables.
8. Moisten everything with broth with a little sugar.
9. Let cook over gentle heat.
10. When the vegetables are well-cooked, arrange them in layers in a dish, alternating vegetables with thin bread slices.
11. Sprinkle coarse pepper over each layer.
12. Wet everything with the broth in which the vegetables were cooked.
13. Place the soup on hot coals to form a crust.
14. Serve with broth in a tureen on the side.
