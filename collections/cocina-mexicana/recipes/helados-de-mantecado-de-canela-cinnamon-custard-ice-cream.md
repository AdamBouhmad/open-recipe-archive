## Ingredients

- Two quartillos of milk
- One pound of white sugar
- Half ounce of powdered cinnamon
- Eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Sweeten two quartillos of milk with one pound of white sugar
2. Put to boil with half ounce of powdered cinnamon
3. Remove after it has boiled a little
4. Allow to cool
5. Mix in eight egg yolks, stirring well to incorporate
6. Return the mixture to the heat and let boil until it takes the consistency of atole
7. Allow to cool again
8. Remove the cream and freeze
