## Ingredients

- Thirty-two egg yolks
- Four ounces very finely ground sugar
- One piece of yeast the size of an egg
- Lukewarm water
- A pinch of salt
- One small cup white wine
- Four ounces hot lard
- Flour

## Directions

1. Beat thirty-two egg yolks with four ounces of very finely ground sugar and one piece of yeast the size of an egg, dissolved in a little lukewarm water
2. After the yolks are well beaten with the sugar and yeast, add a pinch of salt, one small cup of white wine and four ounces of very hot lard, being careful to mix all this well
3. Add flour until absorbed, so the dough is neither loose nor hard
4. Extend the dough with a rolling pin, making sure the sheet is neither very thin nor thick
5. Cut strips two fingers wide, and cut them to the desired size for the rosquetes
6. Seal the points together and form the little rings
7. Fry them in lard or butter until golden
8. Remove them and roll them in sugar and cinnamon
