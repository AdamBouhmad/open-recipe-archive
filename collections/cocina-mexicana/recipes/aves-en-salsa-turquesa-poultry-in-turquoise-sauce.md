## Ingredients

- Almond shells
- Garlic cloves
- Bread slices
- Pimienta (pepper)
- Cinnamon
- Lard
- Poultry
- Broth
- Salt

## Directions

1. Fry almond shells, garlic cloves and bread slices in lard
2. Grind everything very well with pepper and cinnamon
3. Fry the ground mixture again
4. Place the poultry in it
5. Add broth and season with salt
6. Let it thicken as needed
