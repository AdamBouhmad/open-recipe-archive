## Ingredients

- Three pounds of squash
- six quartillos of milk
- two and a half pounds of white sugar

## Directions

1. Clarify two and a half pounds of white sugar to a high stage
2. Add three pounds of cooked and ground squash and let it boil a few times with the sugar
3. Add half of the milk and once it has boiled add the remaining half
4. Cook until it reaches the proper stage and add orange blossom water
