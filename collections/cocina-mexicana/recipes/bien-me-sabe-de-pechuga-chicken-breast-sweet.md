## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- one pound of clean ground almond
- one chicken breast
- paper for wrapping

## Directions

1. Make a syrup clarified and strained well with four pounds of sugar
2. Let it boil until it reaches the mirror point
3. Add then one pound of clean ground almond, to which let it boil once so that it becomes paste
4. After well washed, wrap a chicken breast in paper, wet it and place it in the embers to cook
5. Once cooked, grind it, and with a little ground biscuit, put it in a cuartillo of milk and stir with the said paste
6. Place on fire and let it take the point of cajeta
