## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of milk
- one pound sugar
- one cup rice
- two cups cooked chickpeas
- eight egg yolks
- one cup Jerez wine

## Directions

1. Grind the rice and cooked chickpeas very finely
2. Dissolve in the milk with the sugar and strain
3. Add eight egg yolks and place on heat
4. Once thickened, add one cup Jerez wine
5. It should not be very thick
