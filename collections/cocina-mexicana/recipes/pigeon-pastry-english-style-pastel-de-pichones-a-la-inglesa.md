## Ingredients

- three pigeons
- pigeon offal (livers, gizzards, heads, wings)
- mixed herbs
- basil
- loose ham sliced thin
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- broth
- six egg yolks
- dough

## Directions

1. Pluck the three pigeons well, empty them, and pass them through flame
2. Cut off the necks, legs, and wings
3. Place them in a pot with their offal such as livers, gizzards, heads, and wings, but not the legs
4. Add a mixed herb bouquet, a little basil, loose ham cut into small slices, and a piece of butter
5. Season with salt and coarse pepper
6. Add broth and let them boil until almost cooked
7. Remove from heat, let cool, and place in a deep vessel with their gravy and six egg yolks beaten until hardened
8. Cover everything with a dough lid, sealing the edges with the base, brown it, and insert the pigeon legs through it
9. Finish baking the pastry and serve as is
