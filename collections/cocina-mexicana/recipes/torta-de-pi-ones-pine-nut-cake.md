## Ingredients

- Pine nuts of good color
- Six ounces of sugar
- Milk
- Six beaten eggs
- Lard
- Sheets of dough
- Pieces of beef marrow
- Syrup
- Egg threads
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel pine nuts of good color and after grinding them to a pound weight, mix them with six ounces of sugar
2. Cook a little milk until it becomes somewhat thick after boiling, and dissolve the pine nuts in it so that it is not watery
3. Mix with six beaten eggs
4. Coat a pot with lard and put a sheet of dough at the bottom
5. Pour the paste there and add inside the pot, distributed, some small pieces of beef marrow, very well washed and bled, which should remain submerged in the paste
6. Put another sheet of dough on top, sprinkle with melted lard, and put between two fires
7. Recognizing with a stick that the cake is cooked, cut it into pieces and pour syrup over it
8. To decorate it, put egg threads, raisins, almonds, and ground cinnamon
