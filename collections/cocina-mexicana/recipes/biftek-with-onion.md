## Ingredients

- prepared beefsteak
- butter
- salt
- onion, finely chopped

## Directions

1. After preparing the meat, place it in a pan with butter, salt, and finely chopped onion
2. Let it roast
3. Add the sauce and let it reduce in the heat for some time
4. Remove from heat and serve on a plate
