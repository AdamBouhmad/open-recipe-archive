## Ingredients

- apples
- aguardiente
- sugar
- green lemon peel
- orange flower water
- flour
- warm water
- melted butter
- two beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Cut apples into four pieces and remove the skin and seeds
2. Soak them for three hours in aguardiente, sugar, green lemon peel, and orange flower water
3. Drain them in a sieve or cloth
4. Make a paste with flour, warm water, melted butter, and two beaten eggs, thick enough to coat the apples well
5. Cover each piece with the paste
6. Fry in boiling butter until golden in color
7. Dust with sugar as they come out and pass a spatula heated in the coals over the top
8. Peach fritters are made the same way
