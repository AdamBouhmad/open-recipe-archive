## Ingredients

- four egg whites
- a pinch of flour
- one cup yeast water
- six egg yolks
- a little flour
- half a cup of sugar cane brandy
- lard for coating the pan
- red wine
- high-point syrup (almíbar)
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat four egg whites with a pinch of flour until well risen
2. Mix in one cup of yeast water, six egg yolks, a little flour, and half a cup of sugar cane brandy
3. Mix the batter well and pour into a pan coated with lard
4. Cook over two fires until the batter sets, which can be checked by inserting a straw; if it comes out clean, it is done
5. Remove and let cool
6. Once cold, open with a knife, scoring it on the cut side in the manner that pork rinds or belly rinds are marked
7. Fry the pieces
8. Pour a little red wine over them, high-point syrup, and a generous amount of ground cinnamon where the score is made
