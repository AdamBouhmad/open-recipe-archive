## Ingredients

- Two pounds sugar
- Thirty egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a medium-point syrup from two pounds sugar
2. Add thirty well-beaten egg yolks
3. Place over heat until it boils vigorously
4. Proceed as in the previous recipes
