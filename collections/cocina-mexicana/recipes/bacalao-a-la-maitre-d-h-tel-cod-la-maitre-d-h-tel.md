## Ingredients

- Cod
- Milk
- Water
- Maitre-d'hôtel sauce
- Lemon

## Directions

1. Soak in equal parts water and milk, then scald immediately to remove the skin more easily
2. After well prepared, cook in cold water
3. Turn when it begins to boil and remove promptly
4. Drain well and place on the platter, cover with a maitre-d'hôtel sauce in which a few drops of lemon are added and serve immediately
