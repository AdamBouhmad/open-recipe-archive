## Ingredients

- Rabbit
- Onion
- Parsley
- Butter
- Water
- Salt
- Oil

## Directions

1. After the rabbit boils without removing it, remove the skin, empty the pot, skin the rabbit and wash it very well
2. Divide into good portions, chop the onion coarsely and fry it and the rabbit in plenty of oil
3. When it takes color, remove it
4. In another pan fry much sliced onion and finely chopped parsley; add a little butter, water and the necessary salt
5. Add the portions of fried rabbit and the onion with which it was fried, let it boil until cooked
6. When the broth has thickened, remove it, add raw oil and let it rest
