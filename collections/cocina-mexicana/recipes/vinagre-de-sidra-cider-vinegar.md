## Ingredients

- One medium barrel full of cider or pear cider
- One and a half pounds of leavening made with rye flour

## Directions

1. For a medium barrel full of cider or pear cider, prepare one and a half pounds of leavening made with rye flour
2. If you do not wish to use the vinegar for leavening, let it sour on its own
3. Once it has soured, dissolve it with warm water and introduce it through the hole of the barrel
4. Stir everything well with a stick
5. Place the barrel in a warm place or room like a kitchen
6. Use the vinegar after ten or twelve days
7. As soon as it is of good taste, transfer it and bottle it, because otherwise it will turn sour and spoil, becoming unpleasant in taste, which is necessary to avoid
8. If pear wine or pear cider is used for this, better vinegar will be obtained, as it will be stronger and more pleasant to look at
