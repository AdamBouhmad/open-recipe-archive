## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter or margarine
- potatoes
- breadcrumbs
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Clean and wash all the leaves thoroughly
2. Add spinach to boiling water and keep for about a quarter hour
3. Remove and let drain
4. Some chop it immediately, but current good table practice is to keep it whole so it retains all its juice and flavor
5. After cooking and draining, fry in butter or margarine with cooked sliced potatoes, rolled in breadcrumbs or without them, but always with the corresponding salt and a little pepper
6. When serving, add oil and vinegar
