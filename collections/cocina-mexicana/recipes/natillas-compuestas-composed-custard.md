## Ingredients

- milk
- coconut
- powdered sugar
- ground cinnamon (optional)

## Directions

1. After the milk has been cooked, allow it to cool in basins
2. Collect the creams with a spoon and place on a plate over coconut
3. Cover with powdered sugar and serve
4. A little ground cinnamon may be added on top if desired
