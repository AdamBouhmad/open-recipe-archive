## Ingredients

- almonds
- egg whites
- sugar

## Directions

1. Soak the almonds, peel them, open them and dry them in the sun
2. The next day grind them well on a metate, rolling them with egg whites at medium beat
3. Then grind sugar and add two egg whites without beating, and mix this with the almonds while kneading it
4. Do this until the paste is in condition to come out of the syringe
5. Divide the product lengthwise and form the rings
6. Place them on paper, dust them with sugar and put them in the oven where they will remain until golden
