## Ingredients

- Cooked marinade
- Champagne wine
- Stock from any cooking
- Truffles

## Directions

1. Make a cooked marinade (see marinade)
2. Moisten with Champagne wine and stock from any cooking
3. Once cooked and to taste, strain through a sieve
4. Place truffles in it to cook, which will take about half an hour
5. Let drain, dry well and serve in a napkin
