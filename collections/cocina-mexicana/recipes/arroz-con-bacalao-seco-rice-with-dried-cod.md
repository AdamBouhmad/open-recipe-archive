## Ingredients

- about one pound dried cod
- oil
- two or three cloves garlic
- tomato
- parsley
- rice
- fresh chile
- saffron
- pepper
- clove

## Directions

1. Toast the dried cod over fire, and when it softens, shred it finely, removing the bones, then wash it in two changes of cold water
2. Place a pan, cazuela or pot over fire with oil, in which fry two or three cloves of garlic, tomato and parsley
3. When fried, add the well-drained cod, fresh chile, saffron, pepper and clove
4. Add the rice, give it two turns, then add hot water and cook as in previous recipes
5. You may also add fried or roasted, peeled chiles
