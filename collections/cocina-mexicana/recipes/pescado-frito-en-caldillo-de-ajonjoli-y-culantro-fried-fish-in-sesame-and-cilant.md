## Ingredients

- green chile
- tomato
- tomato
- garlic
- sesame
- cilantro
- bread
- lard
- fish broth
- clove
- cinnamon
- salt
- oregano
- small onions
- small chiles
- olives
- avocado
- parsley

## Directions

1. Chop green chile, tomato, tomato and garlic very finely and fry well.
2. Grind a little sesame and cilantro with a piece of bread fried in lard, loosen with fish broth, add clove and cinnamon, and fry the fish in oil.
3. Place the cooked fish on a plate, bathe with this broth and place in the steam of a pot.
4. When serving, garnish with small chiles and olives, sliced avocado, oil and very finely chopped parsley.
