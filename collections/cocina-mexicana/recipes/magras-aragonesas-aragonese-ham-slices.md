## Ingredients

- one pound of ham of the best quality
- oil
- lard
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- large olives
- capers
- very ripe tomatoes
- clove
- pepper
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Take one pound of the best quality ham, wash it well and scrub it with a small brush
2. Scrape it with a knife so it releases all the red color
3. Cook it in a pot with water
4. Cut it into slices the size of a cigar
5. In a separate pot, heat oil and lard and fry garlic and onion with a little parsley until sautéed
6. Take large olives, chop them finely and fry them with capers
7. Take very ripe tomatoes, remove the seeds and chop them raw, add them to the pot and fry well
8. Grind clove and pepper and add to the mixture
9. When seasoned, add the ham slices and if more oil is needed, add it
10. Let it thicken
11. Garnish with large olives, capers, large capers, and fried bread
