## Ingredients

- Cooked and cut potatoes
- Potato flour
- Butter
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Anchovy, minced
- Capers

## Directions

1. Once the potatoes are cooked and cut, keep them as hot as possible
2. Dissolve potato flour with butter, broth, salt and pepper over low heat
3. As soon as this sauce is sufficiently thickened, add if desired some minced anchovy or capers, and with it water the potatoes
4. Serve as hot as possible
