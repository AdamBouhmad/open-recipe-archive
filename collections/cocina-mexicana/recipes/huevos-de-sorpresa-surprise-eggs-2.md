## Ingredients

- twelve eggs
- chocolate cream
- orange cream or coffee cream

## Directions

1. Make a small hole at both ends of twelve eggs, through which a straw is inserted to burst the yolk
2. Blow from one end so the eggshells empty, then place them in water and rinse them with water until they are very clean
3. Let them drain and dry in the air, covering one of the two small holes with flour mixed with an egg yolk
4. When they are dry, fill six shells with chocolate cream by means of a small proportioned funnel, and the other six with orange cream or coffee cream
