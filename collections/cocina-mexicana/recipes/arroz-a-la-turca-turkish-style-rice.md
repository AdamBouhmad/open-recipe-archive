## Ingredients

- 1 pound of good rice
- butter
- garlic, coarsely chopped
- concentrated broth
- saffron powder
- raw tomato, ground
- clove
- black pepper
- a small amount of saffron, ground
- whole poblano chiles, roasted and peeled

## Directions

1. Choose 1 pound of good rice and wash it in many waters
2. After having boiled it in sufficient water, let it drain
3. Put it in a pot with concentrated broth until it bursts, moistening it little by little
4. When it is half cooked, add a little saffron powder, a little butter, and bone marrow from beef broth or remains of fried poultry
5. Mix everything together and let it finish cooking so that it remains whole and does not fall apart
6. When you want to gild it, add a little raw ground tomato; fried tomato will be added with clove, black pepper and a little ground saffron
7. Add some whole roasted and peeled poblano chiles
8. Cover the pot and put it over low heat
9. Let it sit without stirring until it has dried out
10. Then remove it from the heat and let it rest in the steam of a boiling pot
11. Serve in a tureen or platter with concentrated broth on the side
