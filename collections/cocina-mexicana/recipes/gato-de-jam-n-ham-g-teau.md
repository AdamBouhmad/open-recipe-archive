## Ingredients

- fat ham
- water
- puff pastry dough
- salt
- egg

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of fat ham, which are put to desalt in water
2. Make a puff pastry dough with less salt than is ordinarily used
3. Form the gâteau with it, checkerboarding the edges
4. Place on a sheet pan, gild
5. Cover with the ham slices already drained, trimmed, and separated from the rind or skin
