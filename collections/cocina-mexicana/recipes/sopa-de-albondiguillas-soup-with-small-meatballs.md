## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one quarter of a cuartillo of milk
- two ounces of fresh butter
- salt and pepper
- flour

## Directions

1. In a bowl, put the four eggs, the quarter of a cuartillo of milk, two ounces of fresh butter, a little salt and pepper
2. Beat everything together, mixing in a little flour, until a consistent dough is formed
3. Make the small meatballs the size of an olive
4. Dust them with flour and fry them in butter
5. Place them in the soup tureen and pour the broth over them
