## Ingredients

- one pound flour
- one pound good butter
- twelve egg yolks
- one pound sugar finely ground into powder
- twelve egg whites
- one cup almond liqueur from bitter almonds

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with one pound of good butter
2. Beat twelve egg yolks and mix them in, beating at the same time with one pound of finely ground sugar
3. Beat the twelve egg whites in the same way
4. Following the beating slowly, add the eggs little by little to the flour and butter mixture
5. Once all is united, continue beating for two hours
6. Finally add one cup of bitter almond liqueur and beat with it for another half hour
