## Ingredients

- kidneys
- tomatoes
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- lard
- oil
- vinegar
- bread

## Directions

1. Cook the kidneys as before and slice them
2. Roast some tomatoes and grind them with clove, cinnamon, and pepper
3. Fry everything in lard and oil
4. Add a little vinegar, then the kidneys in the sauce
5. Let them season, ensuring the sauce is thick
6. Garnish with fried bread
