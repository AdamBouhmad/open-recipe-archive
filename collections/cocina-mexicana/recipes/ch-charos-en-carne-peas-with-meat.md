## Ingredients

- meat
- water
- Castilian vinegar
- garlic
- onion
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- oregano
- oil
- peas

## Directions

1. Cook the meat with water and Castilian vinegar
2. Separately, cook peeled garlic in pure vinegar
3. Fry garlic and very finely chopped onion and add cloves, cinnamon, pepper, powdered oregano, and a little oil
4. Add the cooked meat and peas and season
