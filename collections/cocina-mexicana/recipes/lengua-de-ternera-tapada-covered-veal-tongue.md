## Ingredients

- butter, melted
- onion slices
- garlic
- tomatoes, chopped
- green chile strips
- veal tongue, cooked and skinned, sliced thin
- black pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- capers
- olives
- Malaga wine or Parras wine
- broth

## Directions

1. Render thin slices of onion, garlic, and chopped tomatoes and strips of green chile in melted butter
2. Butter another cooking vessel and place a layer of the rendered mixture
3. Add a layer of thin slices of cooked, skinned veal tongue, dusting with pepper, cloves, and cinnamon
4. Place raisins, almonds, capers, and olives on top
5. Continue alternating layers of tomato and onion mixture and tongue slices until the vessel is full
6. Carefully pour equal quantities of broth and Malaga or Parras wine over the layers
7. Place in the oven or over two fires until the broth is reduced, leaving only enough so the dish does not burn
