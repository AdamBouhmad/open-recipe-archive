## Ingredients

- two mulato chiles
- butter
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- fried and ground bread
- fish

## Directions

1. Fry two mulato chiles in butter and grind them
2. Fry garlic until transparent
3. Then fry onion and tomato
4. Add the chile
5. Add a piece of fried and ground bread to thicken
6. Then add the fish
