## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter
- earthnuts (criadillas de tierra)
- parsley
- garlic
- mushrooms
- flour
- broth
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Fry the mushrooms in butter
2. Add earthnuts, chopped parsley and garlic and other finely chopped mushrooms
3. Dust everything with flour
4. Add a little broth
5. Let cook
6. When almost cooked, place over two gentle fires until the broth is consumed
7. Remove, add a little breadcrumb and reheat over two very gentle fires
