## Ingredients

- Chile seeds
- Dried ancho chiles
- Lard
- Small, thick oval tortillas (memelitas)
- Salt
- Ground pepper
- Small onions with tops, chopped
- Cooked duck, cut in quarters

## Directions

1. Toast the chile seeds and ancho chiles
2. Grind everything together and fry in lard so that the sauce becomes thick
3. Soak small thick oval tortillas in the sauce
4. As they are placed on the plate, sprinkle with salt, ground pepper and chopped small onions with their tops
5. On top place quarters of cooked duck
