## Ingredients

- Mutton meat
- Bread soaked in milk
- Cooked potatoes
- Fine herbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Egg yolks
- Breadcrumbs
- Picante sauce or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Chop the meat and add a crumb of bread soaked in milk, cooked potatoes, fine herbs, salt, pepper and two or three egg yolks.
2. Mix everything well.
3. Form the meatballs.
4. Coat with breadcrumbs for frying.
5. Serve with a picante sauce or tomato sauce.
