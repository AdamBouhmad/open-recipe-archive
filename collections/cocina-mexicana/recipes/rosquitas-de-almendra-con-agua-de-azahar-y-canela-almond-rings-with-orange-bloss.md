## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- one pound of sugar
- orange blossom water
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of almonds in water and grind them
2. Grind one pound of sugar
3. Incorporate both, mixing them with a little orange blossom water and ground cinnamon until a soft dough is made
4. Form the rings which are baked on papers in a gentle oven, not very hot, until they brown and turn brown
