## Ingredients

- Duck, cleaned
- Parsley, small bunch
- Onion
- Garlic head
- Cloves, two
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Basil
- Culantro, fresh
- Onion, sliced
- Carrot
- Parsnip

## Directions

1. Clean the duck, pass it over a flame to burn the down, and position the legs as if the duck were lying down.
2. Place in a pot of its exact size with a small bunch of parsley, onion, a head of garlic, two cloves, thyme, bay leaf, basil, and some fresh culantro.
3. Add sliced onion, carrot, and parsnip.
