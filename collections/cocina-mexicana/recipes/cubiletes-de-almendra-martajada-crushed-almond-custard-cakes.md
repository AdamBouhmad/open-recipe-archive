## Ingredients

- Almonds, ground without releasing oil
- Sugar, sifted to taste
- Butter

## Directions

1. Grind almonds so they do not release oil, though some graininess may remain
2. Mix with sifted sugar to taste
3. Fill butter-lined custard cakes and place immediately in the oven
4. After cooling, boil in syrup
