## Ingredients

- Two pounds of good truffles
- Six slices of ham
- A little carrot peel
- One large onion
- One bay leaf
- A little powdered aromatics
- Salt to taste
- Half a bottle of Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Wash and brush two pounds of good truffles, clean them and place them in a pot with six slices of ham, a little carrot peel, one large onion sliced, one bay leaf and a little powdered aromatics, with sufficient salt
2. Moisten everything with half a bottle of Champagne wine and cover the truffles with ham slices
3. Let them cook until the broth becomes gelatinous, then let cool and place them with their stew in the tart
4. Make the tart the same way as French minced tart (see ahead), proceeding the same in everything and serving without opening it
