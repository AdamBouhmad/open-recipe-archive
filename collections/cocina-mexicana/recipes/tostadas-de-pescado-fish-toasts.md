## Ingredients

- carp meat
- parsley
- green lemon rind
- bitter almond biscuit
- butter
- salt
- egg yolks
- bread crumbs
- cream

## Directions

1. Chop carp meat with parsley and green lemon rind
2. Mash everything with bitter almond biscuit, some butter and salt
3. Add some egg yolks and bread crumbs soaked in cream
4. Place this filling on the toasts
5. Place in an oven or pan to take color
