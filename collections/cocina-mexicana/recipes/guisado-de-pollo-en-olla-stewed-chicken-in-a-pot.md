## Ingredients

- Chicken with offal
- Lard
- Tomatoes, without seeds
- Onion in wedges
- Garlic, crushed
- Ham, sliced
- Bay leaves
- Thyme
- Oregano
- Carboy wine
- Water
- Salt
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Chiles
- Capers
- Olives

## Directions

1. Clean the animals well in hot water, then rinse in cold water
2. Put a pot on the fire with lard
3. Add the chickens and offal to the pot
4. Mince one or two tomatoes without seeds, two or three bunches of onion in wedges, crushed garlic, sliced ham and bay leaves
5. Add a little thyme and oregano
6. Fry everything together
7. Add carboy wine and enough water to cook
8. Add salt, cloves, cinnamon and pepper
9. Cover the pot and bring to a boil until the broth reduces
10. Garnish with chiles, capers and olives
