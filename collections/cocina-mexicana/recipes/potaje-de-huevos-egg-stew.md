## Ingredients

- eggs
- parsley, chopped
- egg yolks
- onion, chopped
- water
- pepper
- salt
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs and remove the shells and cut in half
2. Grind a little chopped parsley with egg yolks
3. Fry a little chopped onion
4. Add water with a pinch of pepper and salt to taste
5. Place the eggs in the pot being careful they do not break apart
6. When seasoned, add oil and serve
