## Ingredients

- one pound of rice
- one chicken breast
- twelve cuartillos of milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of rice and cook one chicken breast without salt
2. The next day grind the rice very well until no bran remains
3. Add it to twelve cuartillos of milk, dissolve it and strain it
4. Sweeten immediately and put it on the fire to cook
5. Grind the breast, dissolve it in a little milk that will have been separated, and mix it with the milk and rice as soon as it begins to thicken, which is when it pulls away from the pan when stirred
6. Then empty it into the platters in which it is to be taken to the table
