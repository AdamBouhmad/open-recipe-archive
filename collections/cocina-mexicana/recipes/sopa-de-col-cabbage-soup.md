## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Broth from the pot
- Ham strips
- Garlic, minced
- Onion, minced
- Parsley, minced
- Black pepper
- Carrots, sliced thin
- Fried bread

## Directions

1. Mince the cabbage and place to cook. When half-cooked, drain this water and add broth from the pot and strips of ham.
2. Mince the garlic, onion, and parsley finely. Fry the garlic and onion with a little black pepper.
3. When fried, add the parsley to sauté and immediately add some of the broth in which the cabbage is cooking, which should also have thinly sliced carrots.
4. Let it come to a boil and turn the pot with everything in it. Add some black pepper and when serving, place fried bread in the soup bowl and pour the mixture over it.
