## Ingredients

- robalo, desalted
- lard
- oil
- jitomate without seeds
- three bundles of onion
- garlic head
- parsley
- thyme
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- saffron
- water
- vinegar
- capers
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Desalt the robalo and after drying with a cloth fry in lard and oil
2. Chop plenty of jitomate without seeds, three bundles of onion and a head of garlic, all very finely, plenty of parsley, chopping each thing separately
3. Heat oil and lard in a casserole
4. Parboil first all the garlic, then the onion and then the jitomate with the parsley; fry this last part a lot
5. Add a little thyme, grind cloves, pepper, cinnamon and saffron
6. Let a little water and vinegar boil until it thickens
7. Add some capers and put the fish to boil over low heat
8. Use plenty of oil and to serve decorate with chiles, capers and olives
