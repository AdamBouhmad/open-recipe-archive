## Ingredients

- lard
- salt
- chopped garlic
- green chiles in strips
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- cooked poultry livers
- saffron
- cloves
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- cooked small onions
- squabs
- squab cooking broth

## Directions

1. Fry chopped garlic and green chile strips in lard with salt
2. Add roasted ground tomatoes mixed with cooked poultry livers, saffron, cloves and pepper
3. Add oil, vinegar, cooked small onions, the squabs and their cooking broth
4. Let everything simmer until the sauce reaches a good consistency, neither too thick nor too thin
