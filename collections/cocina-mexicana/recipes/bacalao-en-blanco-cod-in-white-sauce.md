## Ingredients

- cod
- water
- lard
- nutmeg
- flour
- pepper
- milk

## Directions

1. Cook the cod after cleaning it well
2. When about to boil, skim it and remove from heat, cover it well, leaving it for a quarter of an hour
3. Then take it out of the water and drain it
4. Put a little lard in a casserole, nutmeg, flour and pepper
5. Dissolve everything in a little milk, then add the cod so that it takes on the flavor of this stew and serve
