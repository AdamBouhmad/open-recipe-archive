## Ingredients

- parsley, chopped
- small onions, chopped and washed in equal quantity to the parsley
- 2 tablespoons flour
- half a pound of butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- 2 glasses of milk

## Directions

1. Place the chopped parsley and onions in a saucepan with the flour, butter, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
2. Moisten everything with two glasses of milk
3. Let it boil for about fifteen to twenty minutes, stirring constantly without stopping
4. This sauce is served over certain fish, such as cod, turbot, etc.
