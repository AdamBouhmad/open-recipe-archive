## Ingredients

- beet
- sugar
- raisins
- cleaned and toasted almonds
- figs
- toasted sesame seeds
- good vinegar
- sugar
- sweet muscatel wine or other generous wine

## Directions

1. Cook the beet with a piece of sugar, and once peeled, cut it into small dice.
2. Divide some raisins and remove their pits; chop clean, toasted almonds; also chop some figs, and sprinkle toasted sesame seeds over everything.
3. To some good vinegar add enough sugar to neutralize the acidity, and stir all the chopped ingredients there, taking care that not too much liquid remains.
4. Let it sit this way for two or three hours in infusion, and after placing it on the platter, when serving, bathe it with sweet muscatel wine or other generous wine, and sprinkle more toasted sesame seeds over the top.
