## Ingredients

- beef ears
- marinade
- frying batter
- oil
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the ears as in previous articles
2. Cook them in white broth
3. Drain after cooking and divide lengthwise into two, three or four strips
4. A quarter hour before serving, add marinade on top
5. Drain, coat with frying batter
6. Fry in not-very-hot oil
7. When they take good color, remove and drain on clean linen
8. Arrange on plate with a handful of fried parsley piled on top in pyramid form
