## Ingredients

- small artichokes
- water
- vinaigrette
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Choose the smallest artichokes, cut them into four pieces, remove the first leaves, cut the upper part of the others and the stem
2. Cook in water and serve in cold water with vinaigrette, salt and pepper
