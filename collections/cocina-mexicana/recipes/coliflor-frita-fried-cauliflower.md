## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- parsley
- vinegar
- water
- egg
- oil

## Directions

1. Keep in a marinade of salt, parsley and vinegar for a short while
2. Then cook with water and salt
3. Let drain well and coat in beaten egg before frying in oil
