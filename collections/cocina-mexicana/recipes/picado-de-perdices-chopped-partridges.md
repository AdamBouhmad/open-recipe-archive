## Ingredients

- partridges
- butter
- chiles
- parsley
- garlic
- flour (a spoonful)
- broth
- white wine
- bread (toasted slices)

## Directions

1. After removing membranes and tendons, coat and roast.
2. Then mince the meat very finely.
3. Simmer in a pan with butter, chiles, parsley and garlic.
4. Add a spoonful of flour.
5. When everything is well mixed add broth and white wine.
6. Put the minced meat in this sauce and cook without boiling.
7. Serve with slices of toasted bread.
