## Ingredients

- lamb ribs
- butter
- béchamel sauce
- tongue slices
- onions
- concentrated broth
- fried toast

## Directions

1. Fry the ribs and remove the butter from the pan
2. Add two large spoonfuls of béchamel sauce to the pan and turn the ribs in it
3. Arrange slices of tongue at scarlet between circles of onions braised in concentrated broth
4. Place on top of the ribs
5. Arrange the ribs around a plate with a round fried toast in the center
6. Place the rib bone on the toast
7. Pour the remaining béchamel over the top. Ten ribs suffice for one course
