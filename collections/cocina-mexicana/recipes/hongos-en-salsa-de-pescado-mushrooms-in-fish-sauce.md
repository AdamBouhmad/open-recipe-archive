## Ingredients

- garlic cloves
- bread
- oil
- butter
- epazote
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- water

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves and a piece of bread in oil and butter; once fried, grind with plenty of epazote.
2. Fry all this in plenty of oil, add mushrooms that will already be cooked and sliced, a little salt, pepper, and water to season.
