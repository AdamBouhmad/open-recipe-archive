## Ingredients

- puff pastry dough
- lard
- butter
- garlic
- onions
- parsley
- oregano
- ground capers
- wine
- vinegar
- whole capers
- caperberries
- olives
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. From the puff pastry dough separate two pieces, one to cover the bottom of the baking dish coated with butter and another to cover the filling which will be prepared thus
2. Fry the pompano alone in lard
3. In butter fry garlic, onions, parsley and oregano, all chopped finely and capers ground with wine
4. After everything is fried add vinegar, the fried pompano, whole capers, caperberries and olives
5. Place this filling in the baking dish lined with the mass, brush with some feathers of beaten egg, cover with the other piece of dough and bake in the oven
