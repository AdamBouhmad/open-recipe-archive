## Ingredients

- one and a half pounds sugar
- twelve bergamot pears cooked and peeled
- one quarter liter custard sauce
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a strained and clarified syrup with one and a half pounds of sugar
2. When cold, place in it twelve cooked and peeled bergamot pears and put them over low heat until well penetrated
3. Remove them and add one quarter liter of custard sauce to the syrup, beating them together
4. Return the pears there so they boil once, then pour them into the platter
5. Add cinnamon on top
