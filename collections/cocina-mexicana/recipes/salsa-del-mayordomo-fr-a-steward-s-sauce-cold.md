## Ingredients

- water
- flour
- butter
- parsley
- small onions
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice or vinegar

## Directions

1. Prepare as the bound steward's sauce but without water, kneading all the ingredients without heating them
2. At the moment of serving, place below and above the viands, fish or vegetables
