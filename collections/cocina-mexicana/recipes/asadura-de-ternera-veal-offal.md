## Ingredients

- veal offal
- water
- salt
- onions
- flour
- lard or butter
- broth or water
- spices
- fine herbs, chopped
- egg yolks
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. Let the offal bleed in warm water, then pass it to boiling water.
2. When cool, cut into small pieces and drain well.
3. Cook with quartered onions and necessary salt.
4. When cooked, remove from heat, drain well, dust with flour and stir.
5. Fry in lard or butter, turning and stirring frequently.
6. Moisten with broth or water and season with spices, salt, and chopped fine herbs.
7. When the broth has thickened somewhat, remove from heat and let cool.
8. Bind with beaten egg yolks and a little good vinegar.
9. Return to very gentle heat without boiling, just to cook the egg a bit, stirring continuously so it does not curdle.
