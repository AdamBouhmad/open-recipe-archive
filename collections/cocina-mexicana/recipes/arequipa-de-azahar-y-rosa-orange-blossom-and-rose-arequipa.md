## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- four quarts of milk
- three ounces of starch
- half pound of ground almonds

## Directions

1. Place two pounds of sugar in four quarts of milk
2. When dissolved, add three ounces of starch and half pound of ground almonds
3. Cook over heat until it reaches the desired consistency, which should be thick enough that a line drawn in it takes a moment to disappear
