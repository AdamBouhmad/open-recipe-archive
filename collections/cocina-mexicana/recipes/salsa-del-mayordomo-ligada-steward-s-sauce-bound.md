## Ingredients

- one glass of water
- one tablespoon of flour
- half a pound of butter
- parsley
- small chopped onions
- salt
- coarse pepper
- juice of one lemon

## Directions

1. Pour into a saucepan one glass of water with one tablespoon of flour, half a pound of butter, chopped parsley and small onions, salt and coarse pepper
2. Put everything on the heat, stirring incessantly and adding the juice of one lemon
3. It should have the appearance of a white sauce and be neither too thick nor too thin
4. Instead of lemon, a little verjuice or vinegar juice can be used
