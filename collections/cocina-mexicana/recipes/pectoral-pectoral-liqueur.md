## Ingredients

- 6 quartillos brandy
- peels from 4 sour oranges
- 1.5 drachms fine broken cinnamon
- 1 drachm nutmeg peel
- 4 quartillos distilled water
- 2 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Put the orange peels, cinnamon and nutmeg to infuse in the brandy for eight days and filter afterwards to extract about 4 quartillos of liqueur.
2. Dissolve the sugar in water, form the mixture and filter.
