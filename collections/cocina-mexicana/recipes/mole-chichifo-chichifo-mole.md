## Ingredients

- Chile mulato, deveined and toasted
- Cooked tomato
- Raw garlic
- Avocado leaves
- Pork meat

## Directions

1. Grind chile mulato deveined and toasted, cooked tomato, and raw garlic
2. Fry and add one or two avocado leaves and pork meat
