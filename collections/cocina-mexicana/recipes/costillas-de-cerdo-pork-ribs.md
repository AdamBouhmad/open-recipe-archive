## Ingredients

- pork ribs
- broth
- thyme
- mint
- parsley
- aromatic herbs
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour
- white wine
- onion
- garlic head
- clove

## Directions

1. Cook the ribs with broth and a bundle of thyme, mint, parsley and other aromatic herbs, salt and pepper
2. In a casserole put butter and flour
3. Brown with broth, white wine, parsley, onion, half a head of garlic and clove
4. When cooked pour the sauce over the ribs and serve
