## Ingredients

- pipila (young turkey)
- ham strips
- bacon slices
- butter
- broth

## Directions

1. Remove the entrails and clean and prepare the bird
2. If very fat, cover with a layer of lean ham slices; if not, lard with strips of fat ham and cover with buttered paper, which is removed when half-cooked to allow it to brown
3. Serve moistened with broth
