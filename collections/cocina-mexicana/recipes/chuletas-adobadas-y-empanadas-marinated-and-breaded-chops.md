## Ingredients

- Lamb loin slices
- orange juice
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- breadcrumbs
- oregano

## Directions

1. Make medium-sized slices of lamb loin and leave them from one day to another in a marinade made with one cup of orange juice, another of oil and two of vinegar with ground pepper and salt
2. The next day, remove them from the marinade and cook them on the grill
3. Roll them in breadcrumbs with ground salt and pepper
4. Return them to the grill to roast
5. Serve them in onion sauce with oil, vinegar and oregano
