## Ingredients

- veal, bled and cleaned
- salt
- two thirds dried wide chiles
- one third pasilla chiles
- two heads of garlic, cleaned
- cumin
- oregano
- lard
- good vinegar
- olives
- chiles in vinegar
- raw onion

## Directions

1. Bleed and clean the veal, then cook in water with salt.
2. Devein and soak two parts wide chiles and one part pasilla.
3. Grind with two clean heads of garlic, a little cumin and oregano.
4. Fry this sauce in a little lard, dissolve with good vinegar and add necessary salt.
5. Place the meat there and let boil until the broth is consumed, so that it becomes a paste.
6. Serve adorned with olives, chiles in vinegar, and slices of raw onion with the rings separated.
