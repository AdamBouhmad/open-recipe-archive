## Ingredients

- coles dough
- butter (less than standard)
- flour
- egg yolk for gilding
- mold with oil
- syrup at crack point

## Directions

1. Prepare the coles dough as for the zampabocas
2. Divide into three perfectly equal sheets
3. As needed for decorating molds, gild and repeat baking until the secadillos are a good color and very dry
4. Oil the mold to be used
5. Take the secadillos one by one with the tip of a skewer, thread them from below
6. Dip in syrup at crack point that has been prepared
7. Begin by placing one in the middle of the mold
8. Continue placing the rest around it until reaching the edge, making them touch one another
9. After this operation, flip the mold upside down, turning the pastries right-side out
10. Garnish on a napkin and serve
