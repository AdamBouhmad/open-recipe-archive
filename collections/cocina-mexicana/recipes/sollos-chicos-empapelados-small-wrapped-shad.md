## Ingredients

- Small shad fish
- Paper
- Lard (mantecada)

## Directions

1. Wash the fish
2. Remove the scales
3. Empty/gut them
4. Wrap each in a sheet of paper with lard
5. Roast on the griddle (planilla)
6. When cooked, open to remove the roe and soft roe that could be harmful if eaten
7. Replace with a little butter mixed with parsley, pepper, and salt
8. Serve as is or dusted with flour and fried
