## Ingredients

- one raw egg yolk
- salt
- juice of one lemon
- olive oil
- a quarter of a liter
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place one raw egg yolk in a small bowl with a little salt and the juice of one lemon
2. Stir and turn continuously with a wooden spoon
3. Add oil in a thin stream while stirring
4. Add a little vinegar as the sauce thickens or curdles
5. Continue adding oil in this manner until a quarter liter is absorbed, taking care to add sufficient salt
6. Serve white, left as it naturally becomes after the above steps, or serve green by adding spinach green (see spinach green, page 322)
7. This sauce is used for cold fish appetizers or for salads of legumes cooked in salted water
8. It can also be made to curdle or congeal with snow or with calf brains in place of the raw yolk
