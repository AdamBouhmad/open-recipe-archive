## Ingredients

- meat from game, either furred or feathered
- fat ham
- shelled almonds or hazelnuts
- pine nuts or chestnuts
- some crusts of bread
- salt
- spices
- fine herbs
- butter or lard
- mutton broth or other meat broth

## Directions

1. Mince the meat finely with fat ham, shelled almonds or hazelnuts, pine nuts or chestnuts, or some crusts of bread, seasoning with fine salt, spices and a few fine herbs
2. If the meat is raw, cook it in butter or lard; if it is already cooked, then form the tortilla of eggs as usual, and the minced mixture should be soaked with some mutton broth or other meat broth, and all the minced mixture should be poured over the tortilla before it sets
3. When it has set well, transfer it to the platter without breaking it, and make sure the sauce is on top
