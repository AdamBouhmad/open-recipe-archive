## Ingredients

- two breasts
- two quarts of milk
- sugar to taste
- cinnamon to taste
- eight egg yolks with whites
- butter
- raisins
- almonds
- crumbled biscuits

## Directions

1. Boil two quarts of milk with sugar and cinnamon to taste
2. Add eight egg yolks with whites and mix well
3. Spread the pudding mold with butter and put on the bottom raisins, almonds and crumbled biscuits, then only biscuit and then raisins and almonds and so on until full
4. On the last layer put only raisins and almonds and with a spoon gradually pour the milk so it soaks well
5. Cook over two fires
6. For this pudding use the following sauce: Melt butter and add flour so it takes the color of wheat, powdered sugar and a cup of Sherry wine
7. Remove from fire and pour this sauce at the base of the pudding and also on top
