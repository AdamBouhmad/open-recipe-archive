## Ingredients

- butter
- boned beef shoulder
- water or broth
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- brandy

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter and place the boned shoulder inside.
2. Give it several turns over a very active heat.
3. Add a little water or broth, about a small half glass, salt, pepper, fine herbs and a few drops of brandy.
4. Cook everything over low heat.
