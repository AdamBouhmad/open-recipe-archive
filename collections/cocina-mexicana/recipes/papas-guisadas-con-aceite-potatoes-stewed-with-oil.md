## Ingredients

- tomatoes, chopped finely
- onions
- green poblano chiles without veins
- lard
- flour
- large potatoes, sliced
- oil
- Tabasco pepper
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop finely tomatoes, onions, and green poblano chiles
2. Fry in plenty of lard
3. Add a little flour and stir so the sauce is neither thick nor watery
4. Add large sliced potatoes, plenty of oil, and a pinch of Tabasco pepper
5. When it has boiled a little, add a bit of good vinegar
6. Let it season and then let it rest in the steam of a pot
