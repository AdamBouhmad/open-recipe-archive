## Ingredients

- 1 pound of lard
- half a pound of powdered sugar
- 5 egg yolks
- salt
- flour as needed
- filling: minced recado, blancmange, or other milk sweet
- egg white for glazing

## Directions

1. Place in a bowl one pound of lard, half a pound of powdered sugar, five egg yolks, and corresponding salt
2. Beat all with a spoon in circular motions until a foam-like mixture forms
3. Mix in flour without kneading, just combining well with the hands
4. Form small tortillas with this mixture in the hands without flattening
5. Fill with minced recado, blancmange, or other milk sweet, folding in the usual manner
6. Glaze with egg white and sugar
7. Bake in the oven or in a covered pot over embers with a comal on top
8. Serve sprinkled with powdered sugar
