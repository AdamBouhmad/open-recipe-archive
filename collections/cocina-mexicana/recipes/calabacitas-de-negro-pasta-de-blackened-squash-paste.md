## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- three blackened squashes, cleaned of shells and seeds
- four ounces of almonds, ground

## Directions

1. Dissolve four pounds of sugar over heat
2. Add three blackened squashes, cleaned of shells and seeds, ground together with four ounces of almonds
3. Stir everything well together
4. Place over fire until it reaches the point of cajeta (a fudge-like consistency)
5. Pour into a platter
