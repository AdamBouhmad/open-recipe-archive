## Ingredients

- White sapotes, not too ripe
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Select sapotes that are not very ripe
2. Peel them
3. Remove the pits
4. Soak them for a short time in settled lime water while the syrup clarifies
5. Add them to the syrup
6. Cook over low heat until it reaches the proper consistency
