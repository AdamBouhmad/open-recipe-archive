## Ingredients

- Lard
- Flour
- Garlic
- Onion
- Hot water
- Capers
- Pepper
- Clove
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Peas
- Raw fish

## Directions

1. Heat lard well and fry flour until very golden
2. Add chopped garlic and onion
3. Once everything is fried, add hot water and stir well to prevent it from breaking
4. If it thickens too much, add more water while continuing to stir until the broth is thin
5. Add chopped capers, pepper, clove, parsley, oregano, and cooked peas
6. Add raw fish to cook in this broth
