## Ingredients

- Fish
- Salt
- Pepper
- Flour
- Lemon
- Orange
- Lard

## Directions

1. Clean and remove bones, let it air dry
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Roll in flour until well coated
4. Sprinkle with lemon and orange juice
5. Fry in lard
6. Serve without doing anything else
