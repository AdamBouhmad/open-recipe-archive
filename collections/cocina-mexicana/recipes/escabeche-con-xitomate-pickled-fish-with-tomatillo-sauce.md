## Ingredients

- Tomatillos
- Lard
- Garlic cloves, cleaned
- Bread, a piece
- Pepper
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Sugar, a little
- Fish portions
- Egg, beaten
- Lemon slices
- Bay leaves, cooked separately
- Tomatillos, pickled
- Olives

## Directions

1. Roast tomatillos in lard until they turn yellow and grind with cleaned garlic cloves, a piece of bread, pepper, clove, and cinnamon.
2. Fry everything together, season with salt, and add vinegar with a little sugar to taste.
3. Pour this sauce over portions of fish that have been coated with beaten egg and fried, with lemon slices and bay leaves cooked separately on top, and pickled tomatillos and olives.
