## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt
- garlic, minced
- oil
- butter
- vinegar
- olives
- chiles in vinegar
- onion, minced
- pepper
- oregano from China

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower with salt
2. Fry the minced garlic in oil and butter
3. Add the cauliflower there and fry well
4. Place on a platter
5. Pour over it oil, vinegar, olives, chiles in vinegar, minced onion, a little salt, pepper and a little oregano from China sprinkled over the top
