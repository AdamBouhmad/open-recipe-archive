## Ingredients

- corn cooked in water and dried
- manteca (butter)
- water of tequisquite settled
- mantequilla (butter)
- pedacillos de chile tostado (pieces of toasted chile)
- queso (cheese, optional)

## Directions

1. Cook the corn in water and dry it well, then grind it
2. Add butter and a little settled tequisquite water to form a thick dough
3. Make long oval tortillas and place them on the comal
4. When they have formed a crust, mark them with a knife leaving a small border
5. Lift the crust from the center and remove some dough
6. Fill with butter and pieces of toasted chile (cheese can also be added and sprinkled)
7. Place back on the comal until well cooked
8. Alternatively, fry in butter taking care not to brown them so they do not harden
