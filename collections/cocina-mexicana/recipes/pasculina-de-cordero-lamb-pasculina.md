## Ingredients

- lamb head
- lamb feet
- lamb liver
- lamb lungs
- mushrooms
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook and prepare the head and feet of lamb as done for beef
2. Add the liver and lungs
3. Cut the feet into small pieces
4. Arrange the liver and lungs in a mushroom dish or other preparation
5. Add a liaison of egg yolks
6. Place everything over the head
