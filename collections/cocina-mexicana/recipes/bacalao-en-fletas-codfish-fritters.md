## Ingredients

- Desalted and cooked codfish
- Butter
- Garlic
- Onion
- Tomato (xitomate)
- Castilian pepper powder
- Good oil
- Parsley
- Flour
- Beaten eggs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Desalt the codfish, cook without falling apart, and remove the bones, leaving it in regular-sized thin slices.
2. Fry in butter with minced garlic, onion, and tomato, and while frying add a little Castilian pepper powder.
3. Place the fish there, then add good oil and abundant minced parsley.
4. After cooking a while on the fire, remove and mix with a little flour, incorporating everything with beaten eggs and seasoning with salt according to how salty the codfish is.
5. With a silver spoon, take portions and make balls or fritters, which will be fried in butter kept hot on the fire in a pot, and after frying are served either plain or with some fish sauce or in the sauce from the previous recipe.
