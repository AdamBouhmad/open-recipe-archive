## Ingredients

- a pound and a half of sugar
- one hundred chestnuts
- a little orange blossom

## Directions

1. Place in a pan a clarified syrup made with a pound and a half of sugar with one hundred chestnuts prepared the same as for compote
2. Let everything boil, mixing in a little orange blossom, until it detaches from the pan and stirring continuously
