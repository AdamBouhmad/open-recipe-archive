## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- Chilchotes
- Butter
- White atole
- Cheese

## Directions

1. Roast tomatoes and remove skin, then grind and fry in butter with strips of chilchotes
2. When well fried add white atole instead of water
3. Let season and add sliced cheese
