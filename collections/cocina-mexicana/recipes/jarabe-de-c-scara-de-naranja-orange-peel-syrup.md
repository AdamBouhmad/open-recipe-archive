## Ingredients

- six ounces of fresh yellow orange peels
- two quartillos of warm water at sixty degrees
- white sugar
- sugar lumps rubbed on orange peel exterior

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as the lemon peel syrup, using orange peel instead of lemon peel
