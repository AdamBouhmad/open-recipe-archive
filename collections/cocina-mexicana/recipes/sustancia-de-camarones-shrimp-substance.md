## Ingredients

- Fresh shrimp
- Butter

## Directions

1. Procure very fresh shrimp and after having split them, separate the white meat from the tail and legs and chop these, setting them aside on a plate
2. Pound the remaining parts that were not reserved, including the trimmings, meat and eggs that are usually found in their shells, with a piece of fine butter
3. Pass through a sieve and place to heat in a saucepan in a water bath, adding the chopped meats that had been set aside
