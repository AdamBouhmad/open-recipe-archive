## Ingredients

- twelve mutton kidneys
- butter
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare twelve kidneys by removing the skin and dividing them in two parts
2. Place in a pan with butter, salt, and pepper
3. Prepare and cook the same as the mutton kidneys from the previous articles
