## Ingredients

- thrushes
- paper
- butter
- bread crumb
- salt
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. After cleaning, coat the thrushes, cover with paper, and put to roast
2. When tender, coat with butter so they brown
3. Add a crumb of bread and ground salt
4. Serve with spicy sauce
