## Ingredients

- shad, carp, eels, barbel and other fish
- eggs for omelet
- mushrooms
- truffles or earth truffles
- parsley
- onions
- bread crumb soaked in milk
- butter
- egg yolks
- salt
- pepper
- spices

## Directions

1. Clean and debone shad, carp, eels, barbel and other fish
2. Chop them finely and very small
3. Mix this minced fish with an omelet of eggs not very well cooked
4. Add mushrooms, truffles or earth truffles, parsley, onions, a bread crumb soaked in milk, a little butter and egg yolks
5. Chop everything before mixing with the already minced fish
6. After both minces are mixed, make a stuffing with them
7. Season with salt, pepper and spices
8. Cook it to serve alone, or to stuff other fish such as sole and carp on the backbone, or make small sausages with it, or stuff cabbages, suckling pigs, croquettes and all things susceptible to it
