## Ingredients

- Calf's tongue
- vinegar
- wine
- oil
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. After washing, cook with plenty of vinegar
2. Although it should not be very soft, peel it
3. Rub with wine, oil and pepper powder
4. Wrap in paper so it does not lose its aroma
5. The next day serve cold, accompanied with some sauce
