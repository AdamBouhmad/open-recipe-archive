## Ingredients

- Lamb pulp
- Ham
- Garlic cloves
- Lard
- Garlic heads
- Parsley
- Mint
- Black pepper
- Cinnamon
- Cloves, ground
- Ham pieces
- Salt
- Wine

## Directions

1. Lard the lamb pulp with ham strips and garlic cloves
2. Place in a pot with lard to fry
3. Add minced garlic heads, minced parsley, minced mint, ground black pepper, ground cinnamon, ground cloves, ham pieces, and sufficient salt
4. Pour in wine only, without any water
5. Cover the pot as described in other recipes, or with a napkin, placing a casserole with water on top
6. Let the meat cook in this manner
