## Ingredients

- half an ounce of green lemon zest
- half of orange flower preserve
- half of apricot preserve
- three ounces of powdered sugar
- four egg whites, well beaten

## Directions

1. Make a paste with half an ounce of green lemon zest, half of orange flower preserve, and half of apricot preserve, crushing everything and passing it through a silk sieve
2. Add three ounces of powdered sugar and four well-beaten egg whites to the mixture
3. Place the batter in paper boxes
4. Once the bizcochos are baked, coat them with powdered sugar beaten with one egg white and decorate with sugar pearls
