## Ingredients

- one quarter liter of vinegar
- parsley
- shallots
- pickled gherkins, chopped
- capers
- grapes or currants
- pepper
- chili powder
- nutmeg
- a little meat gelatin
- two tablespoons butter sauce
- two tablespoons concentrated broth
- a little anchovy butter

## Directions

1. Put parsley, shallots, and chopped pickled gherkins, capers, grapes or currants, pepper, chili powder, nutmeg, and a little meat gelatin into a quarter liter of vinegar
2. Let everything boil until it reduces to gelatin consistency
3. Moisten then with two tablespoons of butter sauce, another two tablespoons of concentrated broth, and a little anchovy butter
