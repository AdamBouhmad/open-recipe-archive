## Ingredients

- nineteen eggs
- three cups melted and cold butter
- one pound ground sugar
- orange blossom water
- flour
- milk
- raisins
- pine nuts
- almonds
- walnuts

## Directions

1. In a bowl, place nineteen eggs and three cups of melted and cold butter, one pound of ground sugar, and a bit of orange blossom water.
2. Gradually mix in flour until a workable dough forms.
3. On a floured table, knead it until smooth, then extend with a rolling pin.
4. Cut as before, fill with some milk, adding raisins, pine nuts, almonds, and walnuts chopped; fold and finish as before, sprinkling with sugar or sesame seeds.
5. Bake in the oven.
