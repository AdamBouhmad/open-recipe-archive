## Ingredients

- Hare
- flour
- powdered sugar
- melted butter
- cherry jelly
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Roast the hare the same way as in the previous article.
2. Mix equal parts of flour and powdered sugar.
3. Sprinkle the hare with melted butter and sprinkle it on top with the flour and sugar mixture.
4. Serve immediately after giving it a few turns on the spit.
5. You can also pour over the hare, thus prepared, a sauce composed of cherry jelly and Spanish sauce, boiling the two things together.
