## Ingredients

- purslane
- ground cumin
- bread soaked in liquid
- cooked pork
- whole green chiles

## Directions

1. Put all ingredients in the pot
2. Add the cooked pork with whole green chiles
3. Let everything season and ensure there is sufficient broth
