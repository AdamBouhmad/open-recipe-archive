## Ingredients

- walnuts
- syrup at high point
- pignoli (pine nuts)
- raisins
- almonds
- toasted sesame (ajonjolí)
- cinnamon
- egg yolks, beaten
- thick syrup
- molded cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel the walnuts, soak them, toast them without burning, grind them and add them to syrup at high point
2. On a platter greased with butter, place a sheet of paper and on top a layer of the walnuts with syrup, another of pine nuts, raisins, almonds, toasted sesame, cinnamon and other flavoring spices as desired, and the last of beaten egg yolks, and this can be repeated in the same order as long as the last is always of beaten yolks
3. Cook in the oven or over two fires, and if the quarters of the cake are desired to be covered, they are put in very thick syrup and then rolled in molded cinnamon
4. Cook the cake over two fires
