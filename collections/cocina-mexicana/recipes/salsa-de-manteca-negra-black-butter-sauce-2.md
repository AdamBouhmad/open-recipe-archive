## Ingredients

- Butter
- Parsley
- Vinegar
- Salt

## Directions

1. In a pan or pot fry butter until it becomes almost black
2. Then also fry in it a little parsley, after frying add vinegar and salt
3. Let it cook near the heat for a moment
