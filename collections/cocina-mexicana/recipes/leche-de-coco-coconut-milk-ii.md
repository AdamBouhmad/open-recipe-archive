## Ingredients

- coconut meat
- hot water
- egg yolks
- one pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Scrape the meat of one coconut and grind it with one cup of hot water
2. Break it up and squeeze through a strainer and return the strainer with hot water and the milk that comes out
3. For each cup of strained milk add four egg yolks
4. Clarify one pound of sugar and when it reaches medium point let it cool
5. Add two cups of coconut milk, mix well and return to heat to reach the consistency of soft eggs
