## Ingredients

- milk
- bread
- salt or sugar

## Directions

1. Bring the milk to a boil
2. Sweeten it or season it with salt
3. Pour it over the bread prepared for soup
4. Alternatively, a liaison of egg yolks can be added to the milk, in which case put the milk back on the fire, stir gently when adding the yolks, and remove when it becomes thick enough to coat the back of a spoon
