## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Butter
- A spoonful of flour
- A little water
- Pepper
- Minced parsley
- Egg yolks
- A little lemon juice
- A little cold water

## Directions

1. Cook the potatoes and slice them thin
2. Take a little butter and put it in a pan, add a spoonful of flour
3. Once it has simmered a little, add a little water, and once incorporated add the potatoes, pepper and minced parsley
4. Once it has simmered a little, remove from the fire and incorporate egg yolks, a little lemon juice and a little cold water
