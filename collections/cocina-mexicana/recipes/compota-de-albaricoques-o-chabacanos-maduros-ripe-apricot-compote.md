## Ingredients

- Ripe apricots, recently cut from the tree or some time after
- Water
- Clarified syrup of low point
- Almonds from the apricots, peeled

## Directions

1. Do not peel if recently cut from the tree; some time later cut them and remove the stone
2. Place them in water over fire, and when they feel soft when touched with a finger, refresh them in cold water
3. Drain and dry them, and place them in clarified syrup of low point, allowing them to boil three or four times
4. Skim them, and if the syrup is not well cooked, bring it to the proper point separately, then pour it over the apricots
5. In this second cooking of the syrup, add the almonds from the apricots, which should already be peeled
6. When everything is cold, arrange it in a compote dish and serve
