## Ingredients

- Egg yolks
- Egg whites
- Water
- Saffron or marigold flower

## Directions

1. For a consistent and good egg wash, dilute one egg white with two or three yolks and beat together as if making an omelette
2. For a pale or dull egg wash, use only egg yolks, which must be diluted in water
3. Apply to pastries or pastels using a feather, soft brush, feather brush, or pig bristles
4. If egg cannot be used for the wash, soak a little saffron or marigold flower (also called calendula or flower of the dead) in milk as an alternative
5.  Fish eggs can also be used in Lent in places where eggs, poultry, and dairy products are not eaten
