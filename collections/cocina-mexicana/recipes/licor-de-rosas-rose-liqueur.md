## Ingredients

- 4 quartillos brandy
- 3 pounds selected rose petals
- 1.5 quartillos distilled water
- 0.5 pound double rose water
- 12 ounces sugar

## Directions

1. Distill the rose petals in the brandy to extract 4 quartillos of liqueur.
2. Dissolve the sugar cold in the rose water, form the mixture, tint with cochineal and filter the liqueur.
