## Ingredients

- seven cups of nixtamal
- one and a half pounds of lard
- twelve egg yolks
- salt
- one and a half pounds of clarified sugar at mirror point
- two Chiantla cheeses

## Directions

1. Grind seven cups of nixtamal, wash it very well and grind without sun-drying
2. Sift and knead with one and a half pounds of lard and twelve egg yolks with little salt
3. Once incorporated, add a little cold water, being careful not to make it too watery
4. Prepare one and a half pounds of clarified sugar at mirror point and add to the masa
5. Beat a little with the hand to reach a regular thickness
6. Pass two Chiantla cheeses through a metate and mix well with the masa
7. Let rest to make the tamales
8. Note: beat the masa with hand, not spoon, and do not beat too much so the cold lard does not come out
9. If the cheese is salty, do not add salt; if it is well-seasoned, add a little salt
10. To cook, place with the head up and the folded part down
