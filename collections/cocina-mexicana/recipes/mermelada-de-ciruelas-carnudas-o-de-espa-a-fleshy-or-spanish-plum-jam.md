## Ingredients

- Best plums
- Water
- Sugar equal in weight to the thickened jam

## Directions

1. Select the best plums and cook in a little water
2. Press them, letting the jam thicken over the fire
3. Dissolve in syrup at breaking point, made with as much sugar as the weight of the thickened or dry jam
4. Heat afterwards letting the syrup crackle but without boiling, and when cooled pour into jars
