## Ingredients

- four small trout of equal size
- carp quenelles
- truffles
- mushrooms
- light broth
- bread crumbs
- beaten eggs
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Clean and wash four small trout of equal size, drain them, and fill with a mixture of carp quenelles, truffles cut into large dice, and mushrooms
2. Tie the heads and cook in light broth as previously described in an earlier article
3. When cooking is complete, cool the trout, drain them, and roll twice in bread crumbs, coating each with beaten eggs
4. At the moment of serving, fry until golden brown
5. Arrange on a plate over tomato sauce French style (see page 778)
