## Ingredients

- Half a dry chile
- Garlic
- Water
- Bread slices
- Grated Flemish or La Barca cheese

## Directions

1. Soak half a dry chile and grind it with garlic, strain through a sieve, and not too watery, fry it
2. When fried, add plenty of water and bread slices as in the previous soups
3. When the soup has thickened as needed, sprinkle with grated Flemish or La Barca cheese
4. Remove from the fire and place a griddle with embers on top for a while
5. Remove and serve
