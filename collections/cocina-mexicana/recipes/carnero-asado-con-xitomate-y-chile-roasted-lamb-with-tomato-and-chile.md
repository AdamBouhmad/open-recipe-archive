## Ingredients

- lamb quarter, cut into regular portions
- water
- vinegar
- three broad chiles, deveined and washed
- three green chiles, peeled and deveined
- five ripe tomatoes of regular size
- two garlic heads, cleaned
- ginger
- clove
- cumin, ground
- thyme sprigs
- marjoram sprigs
- bay leaves
- Chinese oregano leaves
- salt
- fat ham slices
- lemon juice
- oil
- onion slices, leached in water

## Directions

1. Cut a quarter of lamb into regular portions, drain blood and remove excess fat well, and place in a deep bowl
2. Cover with water and vinegar
3. Add three broad deveined and well-washed chiles, three green chiles peeled and deveined, five ripe tomatoes of regular size, two clean garlic heads, a little ginger, clove and cumin, all ground
4. Add sprigs of thyme and marjoram, and leaves of bay and Chinese oregano
5. Add sufficient salt and let infuse for a whole day
6. The following day, place over heat adding slices of fat ham
7. Simmer over regular fire until the broth is consumed
8. Then brush with lemon juice and oil, and place over two gentle fires, turning occasionally so it browns evenly and brushing with oil using a feather
9. Once dry, serve the portions with onion slices leached in water
