## Ingredients

- manzanas cocidas
- bizcocho tostado
- canela
- pimienta
- manteca
- sal
- vinagre
- azúcar
- pollos
- caldo
- pasas
- almendras
- yemas de huevo cocidas y molidas

## Directions

1. After the apples are cooked, remove the cores and peels and grind with toasted biscuit, cinnamon and pepper
2. Fry in butter with salt, then add a little vinegar and a lump of sugar
3. Place the chickens with their broth
4. Add raisins and almonds
5. Thicken the sauce with cooked and ground egg yolks
6. Let simmer until well seasoned
