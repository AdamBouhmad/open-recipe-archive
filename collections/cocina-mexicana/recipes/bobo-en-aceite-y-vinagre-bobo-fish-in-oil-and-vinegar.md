## Ingredients

- bobo
- agua
- aceite
- vinagre
- perejil
- cebolla
- aguacate
- chiles

## Directions

1. Soak the bobo and remove the scales.
2. Place a pot with water over heat and when very hot add the fish to half-cook.
3. Remove from water and dry with a napkin.
4. Place on a platter and bathe with a sauce made with oil, vinegar, plenty of chopped parsley and finely chopped onion.
5. Garnish with avocado and chiles in vinegar.
