## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- cooking broth
- 2 glasses of red or white wine
- small onions
- lard
- flour
- fried bread

## Directions

1. Once the tongue is cooked, remove its skin
2. Cut into rounds and strain the broth in which it cooked, removing the fat
3. Put everything in a pan with 2 glasses of red or white wine
4. Fry small onions in lard with a little flour and reduce the sauce to the right consistency
5. Place over pieces of fried bread and pour the sauce over it
