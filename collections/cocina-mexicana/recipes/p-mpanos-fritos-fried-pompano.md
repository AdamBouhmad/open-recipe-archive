## Ingredients

- pompano, cleaned
- oil
- garlic, ground
- onions, ground
- parsley, chopped
- flour
- lard
- oil

## Directions

1. Fry the cleaned pompano in oil with ground garlic, ground onions, and chopped parsley
2. When dry, remove and set aside
3. Roll in flour
4. Fry again in lard mixed with oil
