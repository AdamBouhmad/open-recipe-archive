## Ingredients

- Seville olives
- capers
- small birds
- larger birds
- chestnuts
- sausage meat
- small onions
- kid meat (for larding)
- carrot slices
- ham in small pieces
- bread crumbs
- milk
- eggs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Begin by filling a Seville olive with capers, and use it to stuff a small bird
2. Stuff a larger bird with this small bird, and continue progressively with as many birds as desired
3. Take care to fill the gaps between one bird and another with chestnuts and sausage meat mixed together
4. Arrange them in a suitable vessel with small round onions larded with kid meat, carrot slices, and small pieces of ham
5. Remove the membranes and tendons from the roasted bird meat and cut into strips and place in a syrup of proper consistency of candied peel, when available, once cooled a little
6. Once well incorporated into the syrup, empty into molds, and when cold cut into small tablets
7. Form small balls or figures, roll them in bread crumbs, coat with milk and egg, fry with parsley, and serve
