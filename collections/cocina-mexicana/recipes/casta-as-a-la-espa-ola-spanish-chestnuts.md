## Ingredients

- fifty chestnuts
- two ounces of butter
- four spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- two quarter-measures of concentrated broth
- one bay leaf
- a little nutmeg zest

## Directions

1. Peel fifty chestnuts in hot water as if they were almonds
2. Carefully remove all the second skin, as any remaining would make the dish bitter
3. Put them in a pot with two ounces of butter, four spoonfuls of Spanish sauce, two quarter-measures of concentrated broth, one bay leaf and a little nutmeg zest
4. Let them boil for half an hour
5. Remove them from the broth and place in another pot
6. Let the broth reduce and strain through a sieve over the chestnuts
7. Keep them hot in a water bath
8. At the moment of boiling, place them on a plate with the sauce below
