## Ingredients

- sole fish
- butter
- parsley
- onion
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- light sauce
- verjuice

## Directions

1. After gutting and washing the sole well, make an incision along the back lengthwise
2. Melt good butter and place it in the plate where the fish will be served, with chopped parsley, onion, and mushrooms
3. Season with salt and pepper
4. Place the sole on top and cover with the same ingredients that were placed below
5. Cover the plate well and place it on a small stove over low heat to cook
6. When done, serve with light sauce and a small amount of verjuice
7. A little grated bread can be added and the plate placed over low heat under a cloche oven
