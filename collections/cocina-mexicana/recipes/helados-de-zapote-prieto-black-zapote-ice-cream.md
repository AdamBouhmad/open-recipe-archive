## Ingredients

- Black zapotes
- Water
- Powdered sugar
- Málaga wine or other generous wine

## Directions

1. Remove the skins and pits from the zapotes
2. Pass the pulp through a thick ayate cloth
3. Dissolve in very little water
4. Sweeten with powdered sugar
5. Mix with Málaga wine or another generous wine
6. Put in the sorbetera to freeze
