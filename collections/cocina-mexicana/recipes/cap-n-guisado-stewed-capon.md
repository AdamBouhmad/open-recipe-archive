## Ingredients

- 1 capon
- lard
- salt
- minced garlic
- onions
- chopped tomatoes
- poblano chiles or tornachiles, shredded
- artichoke bottoms, chopped
- peas
- green fava beans
- saffron
- cumin
- pepper
- clove
- fried and ground bread
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Divide the capon into proportionate portions and fry them in lard with salt, turning them so they fry evenly on both sides.
2. While frying, add minced garlic, onions and chopped tomatoes and shredded poblano chiles or tornachiles according to the season; when everything is fried, add water to make a small broth, adding saffron, cumin, pepper and clove all ground, chopped artichoke bottoms, peas and green fava beans.
3. When everything is cooked, thicken the broth with fried and ground bread, adding a little vinegar: when taken to the table add oil, capers, raisins, almonds and saffron.
