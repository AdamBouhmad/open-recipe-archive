## Ingredients

- large chard stems
- salt
- eggs
- flour
- sugar and cinnamon

## Directions

1. From the larger stems remove the thin membrane and cut into small pieces
2. Cook with salt, and after draining well
3. Coat the stems with a mixture of eggs and flour
4. Fry in skillet, serving with sugar and cinnamon
