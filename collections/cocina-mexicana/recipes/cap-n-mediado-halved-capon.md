## Ingredients

- capon
- bacon pieces
- garlic
- broth
- flour
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- sauce

## Directions

1. Place a capon studded with bacon pieces and some garlic in a casserole
2. After browning, add broth and when cooked through, remove the capon and strain the sauce
3. Return the capon in less quantity of the sauce
4. Place on a platter to cool
5. Cover with a sauce made of butter and flour
6. Cover with breadcrumbs, place in the oven or on the grill to form a crust, and serve with a spicy sauce
