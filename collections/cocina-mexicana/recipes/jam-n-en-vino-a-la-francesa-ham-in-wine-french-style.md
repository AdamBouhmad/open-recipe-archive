## Ingredients

- ham
- water
- wine
- carrots
- onions
- thyme
- laurel
- cloves
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Soak the ham for three days in cold water
2. Let it drain
3. Wrap it in clean white linen
4. Cook it in equal parts wine and water with carrots, onions, thyme, laurel, cloves and a small bundle of fine herbs
5. It is well cooked when it can be easily pierced with a larding needle
6. Once cooked, remove the center bone
7. When completely cold, lift the top and cover it again with fat, bread scrapings and fine herbs
