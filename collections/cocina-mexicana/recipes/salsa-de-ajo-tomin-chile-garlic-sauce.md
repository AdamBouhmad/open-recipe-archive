## Ingredients

- Wide chiles
- Comino seeds
- Bread crumb
- Good vinegar
- Finely chopped onion

## Directions

1. Devein wide chiles, soak them, then grind with a few comino seeds and a bread crumb
2. Dissolve this mixture in good vinegar
3. Add very finely chopped onion
