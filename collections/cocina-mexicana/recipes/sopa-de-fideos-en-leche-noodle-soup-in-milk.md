## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- noodles
- butter

## Directions

1. Boil the milk with sugar and cinnamon
2. Strain and return to the fire
3. Add the noodles which should have been fried in butter beforehand
4. Boil until well cooked, taking care that the soup does not thicken
