## Ingredients

- Veal head
- Lemon
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley bundle
- Herbs
- Onions
- Carrots
- Bread crumbs
- Flour
- Parsley
- Onions

## Directions

1. Scald the head to remove hair
2. Place in cold water for twenty-four hours to remove all impurities
3. Remove the bones of the lower jaw and the end of the snout, leaving only the skull
4. Rub with lemon
5. Wrap in cloth and cook in salted and peppered water with a bundle of parsley and herbs, onions, carrots, bread crumbs, and flour
6. When cooked, remove and let drain
7. Carefully remove the skull bones and expose the brains
8. Serve garnished with chopped parsley and onions
9. Each person seasons it on their plate to taste with salt, pepper, oil, and vinegar
