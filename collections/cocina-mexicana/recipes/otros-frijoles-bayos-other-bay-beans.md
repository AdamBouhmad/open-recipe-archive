## Ingredients

- Bay beans
- fried onion
- ground red chile
- pieces of squab
- pieces of chicken breast
- butter
- Jerez wine

## Directions

1. After cooked, wash the beans.
2. Add some fried onion and ground red chile, pieces of squab and chicken breast.
3. They should be loaded with butter.
4. Add a little Jerez wine.
