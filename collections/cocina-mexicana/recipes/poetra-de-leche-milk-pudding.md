## Ingredients

- 20 cuartillos milk
- 80 hard egg yolks, ground
- 6 ounces almonds
- 4 pounds starch
- sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Take twenty cuartillos of milk, and to each cuartillo add four hard egg yolks, ground.
2. To the total quantity of milk add six ounces of almonds and four pounds of starch.
3. Grind everything together.
4. Place over low heat without ceasing to stir, adding adequate sugar and orange blossom water.
5. Cook until it reaches the consistency of manjar blanco (blancmange).
6. Pour into platters and sprinkle cinnamon powder on top.
