## Ingredients

- Butter
- A little saffron
- Two green chiles, well crushed
- A little broth
- Salsa

## Directions

1. Place butter, a little saffron and two well-crushed green chiles in a saucepan
2. Put on the fire to fry a little
3. Add a little broth and an equal amount of sauce
4. Let it reduce until it reaches a regular consistency
5. Pass through a strainer
6. Put on heat
7. When about to serve, add a little butter
