## Ingredients

- rabbit meat, chopped
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- veal offal
- flour
- bread crumbs
- egg, beaten

## Directions

1. Mince the meat with bacon, salt, pepper, veal offal and some flour, make small portions, and these wrap with bread crumbs, dip in beaten egg and coat with bread again
2. Fry and bring to the table
