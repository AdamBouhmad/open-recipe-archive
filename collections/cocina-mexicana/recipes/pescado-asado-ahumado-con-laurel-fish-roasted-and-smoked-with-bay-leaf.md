## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- pepper
- lemon
- oil
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. After cleaning the fish, dry it well.
2. Rub with salt, pepper, lemon and oil.
3. Place on a grill, and under it place bay leaf on the fire from time to time so it receives the smoke.
4. Turn frequently and rub with oil, lemon and a little pepper until it is golden.
5. Serve with chopped onion and parsley.
