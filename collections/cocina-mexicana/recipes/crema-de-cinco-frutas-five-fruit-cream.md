## Ingredients

- Peels of two bergamot limes, cut very thin
- Peels of two bitter oranges, cut very thin
- Peels of two citrons, cut very thin
- Peels of two lemons, cut very thin
- Peels of three Chinese oranges, cut very thin
- Sixteen quartillos of brandy of twenty to twenty-four degrees
- Four pounds of white sugar
- Eight quartillos of water

## Directions

1. Cut the peels very thin from the citrus fruits
2. Leave in infusion for eight days at room temperature in brandy
3. Distill until slightly more than half the liquor is obtained
4. Dissolve four pounds of white sugar in eight quartillos of water over heat
5. After cooling, mix exactly with the distilled liquor
6. Filter through a bag and store in well-sealed bottles
