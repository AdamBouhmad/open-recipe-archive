## Ingredients

- mulato chile, deveined and soaked
- tomato
- water
- salt
- clove
- ground pepper
- peas and beans, cooked
- sweet potato and pineapple, in pieces
- pork meat
- sugar
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Devein and soak mulato chile, then grind with tomato
2. Fry well and add water, salt, clove, and ground pepper
3. Add cooked peas and beans, sweet potato and pineapple in pieces
4. Add pork meat, a piece of sugar, and a little vinegar
5. Let season
