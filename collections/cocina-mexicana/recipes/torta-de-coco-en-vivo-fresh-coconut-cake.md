## Ingredients

- Eighteen egg yolks
- Eight egg whites
- One grated coconut
- Two ounces of ground biscuit
- Lard
- Four pounds of sugar for syrup
- One quarter cuartillo of Jerez wine

## Directions

1. Beat eighteen egg yolks separately
2. Beat eight egg whites separately
3. Combine the well-beaten yolks and whites
4. Mix with one grated coconut and two ounces of ground biscuit
5. Pour into a pot greased with lard
6. Place this pot inside another pot with boiling water
7. Cover with a comal or tin sheet with fire on top
8. When a straw inserted comes out clean, the cake is cooked
9. Remove from the pot and wrap in a napkin
10. When cold, place in clarified low-point syrup made with four pounds of sugar
11. Simmer in the syrup and when removing from heat, add one quarter cuartillo of Jerez wine
