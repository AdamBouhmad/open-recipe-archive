## Ingredients

- capon
- broth
- rice

## Directions

1. After cleaning, emptying, and removing pin feathers, tie the thighs with legs turned inward
2. Place breast-down in a pan full of broth
3. When well-skimmed, add a pound and a half of rice well cleaned and unwashed
4. Cover the pan and let simmer over low heat about two hours
5. Place the capon on a platter, drain the broth from the rice, finish preparing the rice with butter well-seasoned, and pour over the bird; if too thick, add a little broth
