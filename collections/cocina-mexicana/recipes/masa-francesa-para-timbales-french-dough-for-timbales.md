## Ingredients

- one quarter-pint of flour
- a little water
- twelve ounces of butter
- two egg yolks
- a handful of salt

## Directions

1. Place flour on a table with a hole in the middle
2. Add water, butter, egg yolks, and salt to the hole
3. Knead until it hardens, but without becoming very firm
4. Fold or pleat three times
