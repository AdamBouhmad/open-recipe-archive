## Ingredients

- Veal breast
- Butter
- Flour
- Salt
- Pepper
- Meat broth
- Parsley
- Peas

## Directions

1. Cut into small pieces
2. Lightly fry with butter, sprinkling with flour, salt, and pepper
3. Moisten with a good glass of broth, adding parsley
4. Cook for one hour
5. Then add the peas
6. When everything is well cooked, skim the sauce and serve
