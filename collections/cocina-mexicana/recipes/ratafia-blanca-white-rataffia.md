## Ingredients

- one hundred apricot or chabacano kernels (almonds and stones)
- eight cuartillos of refined aguardiente
- two cuartillos of water
- one pound of sugar
- ten grains of white pepper
- eight or ten cloves
- two small sticks of cinnamon the size of a finger

## Directions

1. Coarsely crush one hundred apricot or chabacano kernels (almonds and stones) in a mortar
2. Put them in a large bottle with eight cuartillos of refined aguardiente, two cuartillos of water, one pound of sugar, ten grains of white pepper, eight or ten cloves, and two small sticks of cinnamon the size of a finger
3. Seal the bottle well and place it in the sun for fifteen to twenty days so that the heat develops the perfume of the ingredients
4. After this time, clarify the liquor by filtering it through a cloth and pour it into bottles, taking care to seal them well
