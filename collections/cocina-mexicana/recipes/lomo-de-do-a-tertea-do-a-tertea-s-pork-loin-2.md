## Ingredients

- two pork loins
- salt
- two large peeled garlic heads
- lard

## Directions

1. Coat two pork loins with salt and let sit for two days, turning occasionally.
2. After two days, grind two large peeled garlic heads and mix with a little salt.
3. Coat the loins well with the garlic mixture, ensuring it penetrates well.
4. Cover generously with lard and place in the oven.
5. Cook for a little over one hour.
6. Optional: serve with the following sauce: In a little lard, add a handful of flour, but first add some chopped onion and parsley; once transparent, add the flour just until it is partially cooked; add a little water; just before serving, add an egg yolk diluted in a little vinegar and do not heat further with the egg as it will cook.
