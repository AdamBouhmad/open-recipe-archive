## Ingredients

- Orange blossoms
- Lemon juice
- 2.5 pounds sugar per pound of clean orange blossoms

## Directions

1. Let the orange blossoms boil for a few moments to soften them
2. Throw them into fresh hot water with lemon juice and let them return to a boil until they break easily between the fingers
3. Remove and place in cold water also with lemon juice
4. Let them sit, then press in a napkin and crush them
5. Place over the fire without boiling, dissolving in syrup at the blown stage
6. Dissolve in syrup and allow some boils
