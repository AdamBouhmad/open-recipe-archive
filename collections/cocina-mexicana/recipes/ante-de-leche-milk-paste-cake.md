## Ingredients

- milk
- cinnamon
- clove
- orange blossom water
- milk cream
- biscotela
- syrup at medium point
- cinnamon for topping

## Directions

1. Boil the milk until it becomes thick
2. Skim off cream according to desired amount
3. Mix in cinnamon and a bit of clove
4. Add orange blossom water and cream
5. Slice the biscotela
6. Soak it in syrup at medium point
7. Place in dish in layers of biscotela and milk
8. Bake in oven until it sets
9. Top with cinnamon
