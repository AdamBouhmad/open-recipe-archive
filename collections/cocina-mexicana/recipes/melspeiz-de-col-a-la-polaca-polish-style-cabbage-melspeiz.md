## Ingredients

- flour (two handfuls)
- water
- two eggs
- melted butter
- salt
- two cabbage heads (white)
- pepper
- five eggs
- two tablespoons curdled milk
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make a thick dough with two handfuls of flour, water, two eggs, a little melted butter and salt, and let it rest
2. Blanch two cabbage heads in boiling water, chop them very finely and cook with pepper and salt
3. Let cool and mix with five eggs, two tablespoons of curdled milk and a little nutmeg
4. Spread the dough very thin and wide, arrange the small piles of chopped cabbage on it, and fold the dough covering the filling
5. Cut the dough around each pile of cabbage in a way that small pieces like sacks or French toasts are formed
