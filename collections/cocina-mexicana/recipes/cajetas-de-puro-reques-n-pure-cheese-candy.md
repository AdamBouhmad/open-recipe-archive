## Ingredients

- Cheese (requesón) in equal quantity to the sugar used
- Sugar equal in pounds to the cheese

## Directions

1. Make syrup with as many pounds of sugar as there will be pounds of cheese
2. After clarifying, let it boil until it reaches breaking point
3. Then without removing the pan from the fire, add the ground cheese, not stopping to beat until it reaches cajeta point
4. When pouring it out, continue beating much
5. Some separate the pan from the fire to add the cheese, but immediately return it to the fire and proceed as stated
