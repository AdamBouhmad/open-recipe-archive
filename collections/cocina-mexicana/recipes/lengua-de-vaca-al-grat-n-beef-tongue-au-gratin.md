## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- beef broth
- vinegar
- small pickled cucumbers
- parsley
- small onion
- chervil
- salt
- pepper
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Roast the tongue and then cut it into thin rounds
2. In a plate or serving dish, place a little broth and a splash of vinegar
3. Add finely chopped small pickled cucumbers, parsley, small onion, and chervil, seasoned with salt, pepper and a little bread crumbs
4. Arrange the tongue slices on top and sprinkle the same condiments over them, covering everything with bread crumbs
5. Place the dish on a low heat and let it cook until it sticks to the bottom
6. Serve, pouring a little broth over it
