## Ingredients

- rabbit, completely deboned
- calf or beef tripe
- pork lard or beef udder
- onions
- salt
- fine spices
- chopped shallots
- nutmeg, grated
- basil powder

## Directions

1. Completely debone a good-smelling rabbit and cut into strips
2. Cut calf or beef tripe into strips
3. Add pork lard or beef udder cut the same way
4. Add onions cut the same way
5. Cook everything over the fire until half done
6. Season with salt, fine spices, chopped shallots, grated nutmeg and basil powder
