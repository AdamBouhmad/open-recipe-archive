## Ingredients

- Four pounds and twelve ounces of clarified white sugar at alfeñique stage
- one pound of ground and shredded sweet potato
- two pounds of Castilian squash
- eighteen egg yolks
- four ounces of ground almond
- one quartillo of milk

## Directions

1. Clarify four pounds and twelve ounces of white sugar to alfeñique stage
2. Mix in one pound of ground and shredded sweet potato and two pounds of Castilian squash
3. Add eighteen egg yolks and four ounces of ground almond
4. Add one quartillo of milk
5. Cook everything together, stirring in the clarified syrup
6. When cool, return to heat until it reaches the candy stage
