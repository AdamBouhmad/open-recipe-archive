## Ingredients

- squash
- fried onion
- mashed garlic
- pepper
- salt
- tomato
- parsley
- mint
- cooked oil

## Directions

1. Cut squash into large pieces and place in a pot with fried onion, mashed garlic, pepper, salt, and tomato.
2. Turn until fried, then add a handful of parsley and mint.
3. Add cooked oil and pepper.
