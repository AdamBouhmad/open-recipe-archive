## Ingredients

- 1 pound mulato chile
- Tomato, a little
- 1 to 2 garlic heads
- A little toasted sesame
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Sugar to taste
- Salt to taste
- 1 chocolate tablet
- Meat as desired
- Water

## Directions

1. Remove veins from the chile and toast until it takes on a dark color
2. Toast the seeds until they are dark
3. Roast a little tomato and 1 to 2 garlic heads
4. Grind everything together
5. Fry very well
6. Add sugar and salt to taste
7. Add chocolate tablet, meat as desired, water and season
