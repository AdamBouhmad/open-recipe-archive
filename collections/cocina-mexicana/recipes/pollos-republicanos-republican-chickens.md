## Ingredients

- a little mamey
- plenty of raisins
- almonds
- water
- salt
- lard
- chickens

## Directions

1. Grind a little mamey with plenty of raisins and almonds.
2. Mix with a little water and fry until well browned.
3. Add more water and corresponding salt.
4. Parboil the chickens in lard and place them in the sauce and let boil until tender and the sauce thickens.
