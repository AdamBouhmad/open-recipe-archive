## Ingredients

- rice
- hot water
- cold water
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Clean the rice thoroughly to remove small hulls and foreign matter
2. Wash it many times in hot water, rubbing with the hands
3. Wash it a final time in cold water
4. Boil immediately in a small amount of broth
5. Keep moistening as it absorbs liquid and thickens
6. The rice must burst well but not become mushy
7. It is sufficiently cooked when the grains break apart between the fingers without effort
8. Add enough broth so the soup is neither too thin nor too thick
9. Calculate one ounce of rice per person
