## Ingredients

- half a measure of flour
- one cup of water
- half a measure of butter
- four eggs
- some grated nutmeg
- chicken or egg broth

## Directions

1. Put flour in a pot or skillet
2. Add one cup of water and half a measure of butter and bring to a boil
3. Once boiling, add the flour and stir very well
4. Remove from heat and let cool
5. Add four eggs and some grated nutmeg
6. With one small spoonful add the dough to chicken or egg broth a short while before serving
