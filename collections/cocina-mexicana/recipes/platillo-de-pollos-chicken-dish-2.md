## Ingredients

- three chickens
- wine
- vinegar
- water
- ham slices
- cloves
- cinnamon
- toasted almonds
- pepper
- ginger
- salt
- lard
- tornasolé peppers
- olives
- capers
- caperberries

## Directions

1. Put three quartered chickens to cook in one and a half quart of wine, another quart of vinegar and two quarts of water
2. Add ham slices, cloves and cinnamon ground with toasted almonds and a little pepper and ginger
3. Season with sufficient salt and add a tablespoon of lard
4. Cover the pot as for a stew and put to cook over gentle fire
5. When the chickens are very tender, uncover and add tornasolé peppers, olives, capers and caperberries
