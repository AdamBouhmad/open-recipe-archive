## Ingredients

- pasilla chiles
- fried bread
- garlic
- cumin
- lard
- salt
- cooked string beans
- longaniza
- papada
- cooked potatoes
- broth

## Directions

1. Toast pasilla chiles and grind them with fried bread, garlic and cumin and fry the ground mixture in lard with salt
2. Add to this frying the cooked, shredded and chopped string beans, pieces of longaniza and papada also cooked and the broth in which these were cooked
3. Add the cooked, peeled and sliced potatoes and let everything boil until the cooking liquid becomes seasoned and of regular consistency
