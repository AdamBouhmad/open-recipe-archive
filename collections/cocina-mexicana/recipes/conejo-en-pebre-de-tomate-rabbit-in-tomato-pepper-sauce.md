## Ingredients

- rabbit
- water
- salt
- lard
- garlic cloves
- raw tomato ground fine
- rabbit cooking broth
- cinnamon
- pepper
- bread crumb
- onion quarters
- ham slices
- chorizos
- capers
- oregano
- vinegar
- olives
- pickled tomatillos
- pickled zucchini
- good oil

## Directions

1. Clean the rabbit and cook with water and salt
2. Fry garlic cloves in lard in a pot and remove them
3. Fry ground raw tomato in the same pot with salt
4. Add sufficient broth from cooking the rabbit and rabbit in portions
5. Add cinnamon, pepper, and bread crumb ground together
6. Add onion quarters, ham slices, chorizos, capers, and much oregano
7. Add a little vinegar
8. When seasoned, add olives, pickled tomatillos, and pickled zucchini
9. Before removing from heat, add good oil
