## Ingredients

- kid backbone (espinazo)
- warm water
- salt
- mulato chiles (chiles mulatos)
- cumin (cominos)
- garlic (cabeza de ajos), half a head, raw
- lard (manteca)
- lettuce (lechuga)

## Directions

1. Wash the backbone with warm water and put it to boil in salted water. Soak and devein some mulato chiles and grind them with cumin and half a head of raw garlic and salt. Remove the backbone from the pot and let it drain. Coat it well with the chile mixture and fry in lard. Serve with lettuce salad.
