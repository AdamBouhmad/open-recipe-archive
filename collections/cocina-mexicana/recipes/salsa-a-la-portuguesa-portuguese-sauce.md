## Ingredients

- 4 ounces of butter
- 2 raw egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
- black pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Place the butter and raw egg yolks in a saucepan with lemon juice, pepper, and salt
2. Place the saucepan over moderate heat, being careful to stir the sauce constantly without stopping, as otherwise it will curdle
3. Once it is somewhat warm, lift some of the sauce with a spoon and let it fall from a certain height back into the same saucepan
4. Stir and beat vigorously so that the butter binds with the yolks, being careful to make this sauce only at the moment of serving
5. If it becomes too thick, add a little water
