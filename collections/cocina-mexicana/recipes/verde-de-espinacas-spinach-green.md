## Ingredients

- spinach bundle
- parsley
- onion stems
- water

## Directions

1. Blanch in boiling water a bundle of spinach in which a little parsley and some onion stems have been placed
2. Refresh in cold water and press well with the hands
3. Grind or mill and pass through a sieve
4. If this green becomes too thick, mix cold broth with it and use it as an ingredient in sauces and stews
