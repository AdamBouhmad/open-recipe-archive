## Ingredients

- cow udder
- good broth
- carrots
- onions
- thyme
- bay leaf
- cloves
- peppercorns

## Directions

1. Wash and soak a cow udder, parboil it, and then put it to cook in a braising vessel with good broth, some carrots, onions, thyme, bay leaf, cloves, and peppercorns
2. Prepare the cooked udder as the rennet of the same cow is prepared
