## Ingredients

- Timbale pastry dough
- Pastry sheets three lines thick
- Filling
- Sauce

## Directions

1. Line a pan with timbale pastry dough using sheets three lines thick
2. Place the filling in the center
3. Cover with a sheet of the same dough
4. Seal the edges well
5. Place the pan on hot coals with a griddle and fire on top
6. When the dough has taken color, uncover by removing the top layer and add the sauce
