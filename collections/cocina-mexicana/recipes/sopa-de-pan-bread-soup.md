## Ingredients

- Dry or toasted bread crusts, not burned
- Broth, sufficient to soak the bread
- Additional hot broth for serving
- Some vegetables

## Directions

1. In a suitable soup pot, cut dry or toasted bread crusts, but not burned ones
2. Pour over them enough broth to soak them
3. At the time of serving, pour over another bit of broth, well heated, covering it with some vegetables
4. Never cook the bread in the broth, as this bad habit removes its flavor
