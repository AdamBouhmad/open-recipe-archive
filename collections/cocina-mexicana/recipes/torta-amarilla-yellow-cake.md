## Ingredients

- beaten egg yolks
- very finely ground almonds
- chopped almonds
- raisins
- walnuts
- pine nuts
- syrup at point
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Mix beaten egg yolks with very finely ground almonds and other chopped almonds, raisins, walnuts, and pine nuts
2. After the cake has set in the usual way, arrange it on a plate
3. Brush with syrup at point and orange blossom water
