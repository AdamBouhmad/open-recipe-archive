## Ingredients

- butter
- oil
- chopped garlic
- tomato
- parsley
- rice
- water

## Directions

1. Fry in butter and oil chopped garlic, tomato and parsley and there brown the rice
2. Once it is browned, add a bit of water and cover until it dries
3. Do not wash the rice, only clean it
