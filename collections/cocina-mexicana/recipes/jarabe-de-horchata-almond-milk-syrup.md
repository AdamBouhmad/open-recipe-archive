## Ingredients

- one pound of sweet almonds
- four ounces of bitter almonds
- cold water
- lemon peel
- two pounds of sugar
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Place one pound of sweet almonds and four ounces of bitter almonds in cold water for sufficient time to easily remove their skins
2. Grind them, adding a little water and lemon peel from time to time
3. Once the paste is ready and thinned with half its weight of water, press strongly between two cloths through tightly woven linen
4. Return the paste to the mortar with its water and press again
5. Make syrup at crack stage with two pounds of sugar in an earthenware vessel
6. Remove from heat, add the almond milk and return to fire, stirring until it has boiled for several minutes
7. Remove from heat again, let cool, add orange blossom water and strain all through cloth or filter
8. Fill bottles and store, checking from time to time since the almond oil, being lighter, rises to the surface and appears to divide it in two parts; stir to preserve the exact mixture and prevent souring
