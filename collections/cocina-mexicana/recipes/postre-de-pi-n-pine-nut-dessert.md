## Ingredients

- One cuartillo of pine nuts
- One pound of almonds
- Forty to fifty large walnuts
- Four cuartillos of milk
- Syrup as needed
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind the pine nuts, almonds, and forty to fifty large walnuts well
2. Mix with four cuartillos of milk and the necessary syrup
3. Put on the fire until it reaches the point of white confection
4. Empty into a platter and sprinkle ground cinnamon on top
