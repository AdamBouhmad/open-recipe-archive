## Ingredients

- butter
- salt
- eggs
- butter
- milk or cream

## Directions

1. Line a deep plate with butter, sprinkling with a little salt
2. Place eggs on top
3. Dissolve a little butter with a spoonful of milk or cream
4. When well heated pour over the eggs
5. Cover well placing the plate over low heat
6. Serve
