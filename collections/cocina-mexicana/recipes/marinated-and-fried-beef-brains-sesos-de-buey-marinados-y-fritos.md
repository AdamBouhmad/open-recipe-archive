## Ingredients

- beef brains
- marinade
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- frying batter
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the brains as for black butter brains
2. Blanch them, then cook in a marinade
3. When cooked, divide into pieces and season with salt and pepper
4. Place in vinegar so they are covered
5. Drain them and immediately wrap in frying batter
6. Fry carefully so the oil is not too hot
7. Serve surrounded with fried parsley
