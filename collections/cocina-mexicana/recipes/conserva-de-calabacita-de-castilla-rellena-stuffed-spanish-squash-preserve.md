## Ingredients

- Spanish squash
- Lime water, settled
- Clarified syrup at medium point
- Coconut preserve or other preserve
- Cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Leave the squash whole for stuffing, cutting only one wheel from the trunk end to hollow it
2. Peel the entire squash carefully without damaging the flesh
3. Follow all procedures from the previous article until the preserve is complete
4. Carefully remove the cooked squash
5. Fill with coconut preserve or another preserve
6. Cover with the small wheel that was removed and has been cooked
7. Secure with small pieces of cinnamon
8. Give it another boil in the syrup
