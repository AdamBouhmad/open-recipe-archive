## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- cabbage
- dried oregano
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak the chickpeas in water from one day to the next
2. On the following day, cook them
3. Add a little dried oregano
4. When they have boiled well, add a little vinegar and let them thicken over strong heat
5. Then place them in the steam of a boiling pot
