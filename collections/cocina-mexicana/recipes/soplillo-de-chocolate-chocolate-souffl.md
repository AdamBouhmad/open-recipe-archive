## Ingredients

- two ounces of chocolate
- a little water
- half a spoonful of potato starch
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- four egg yolks
- six egg whites
- sugar for glazing

## Directions

1. Put two ounces of chocolate in a pot and dissolve it with a little water
2. Add half a spoonful of potato starch, four ounces of powdered sugar and four egg yolks
3. Beat six egg whites and mix them with the composition
4. Pour the soufflé into a silver pot and put it to cook in the oven or under a camp oven
5. Glaze with sugar and serve
