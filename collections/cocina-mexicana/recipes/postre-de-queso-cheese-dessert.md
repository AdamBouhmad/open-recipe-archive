## Ingredients

- fresh cheese
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 handful of flour
- lard for frying
- honey
- sugar beads
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Put fresh cheese to soak, then grind it very finely, put it on a table, add three egg yolks and a handful of flour
2. Stir, knead and make some curious little figures or small rings that are fried in lard, then sprinkled with honey and arranged on a platter, decorating with sugar beads and cinnamon
