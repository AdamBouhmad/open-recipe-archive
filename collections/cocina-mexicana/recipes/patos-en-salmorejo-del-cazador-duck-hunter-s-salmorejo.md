## Ingredients

- cooked roasted ducks
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- cup of Bordeaux wine
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Remove the breasts and legs from ducks that have been roasted
2. Divide the carcasses or skeletons into pieces
3. Place everything in a casserole with salt, pepper and a little oil, a cup of Bordeaux wine and the juice of a lemon
4. Fry everything, tossing it together and serve.
