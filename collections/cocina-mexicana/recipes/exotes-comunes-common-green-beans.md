## Ingredients

- Tender exotes (green beans)
- Water
- Salt
- Tierra salt or tequesite

## Directions

1. Select very tender exotes, wash them well, cut off both ends and remove the strings where the pod joins
2. Bring water with salt or a little tequesite to a boil in a pot
3. Add the exotes and cook until well done
4. Remove the pot from heat, take out the exotes, refresh them and wash in cold water
5. They are then ready to use in various preparations
