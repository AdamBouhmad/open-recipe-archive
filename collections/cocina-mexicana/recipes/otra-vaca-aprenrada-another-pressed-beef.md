## Ingredients

- meat
- current vinegar
- sour orange juice
- salt
- lard
- sliced onion
- oregano
- thyme
- marjoram
- ham slices
- chorizos
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- ginger
- a little grated nutmeg
- water
- Jerez wine
- small chiles
- onion

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into slices and put in a dish with a little current vinegar, sour orange juice, and a little salt.
2. Leave in infusion two or three days and then remove and press.
3. Coat a dish with lard and place a layer of meat and another of sliced onion, oregano, thyme, marjoram, ham slices, and chorizos.
4. Add cloves, cinnamon, pepper, ginger, and a little grated nutmeg, and let everything fry together.
5. Add water and Jerez wine in proportion to the meat.
6. Let cook over two fires and it should remain like adobo.
7. Garnish with small chiles, onion, etc.
