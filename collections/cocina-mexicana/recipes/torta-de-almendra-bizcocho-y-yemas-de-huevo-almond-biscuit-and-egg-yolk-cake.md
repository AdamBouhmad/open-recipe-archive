## Ingredients

- Twenty egg yolks
- Four egg whites
- One pound of finely ground almonds
- A little biscuit

## Directions

1. Beat twenty egg yolks until hard and mix with four whites, also beaten as for turrón
2. Stir in one pound of very finely ground almonds and, adding a little biscuit and incorporating well, put in a lard-coated pan, which will be placed over embers, covered with a comal and more embers on top
3. Cut the cake into small pieces, let them boil briefly in syrup, add white wine and decorate with cinnamon, raisins and pine nuts on top
