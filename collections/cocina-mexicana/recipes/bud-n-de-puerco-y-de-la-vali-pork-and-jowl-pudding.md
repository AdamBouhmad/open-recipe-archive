## Ingredients

- onion, chopped
- water or lard or jowl
- lard cut into dice-shaped pieces
- three-fourths blood
- one part cream (fresh milk fat)
- fine salt
- cinnamon
- pepper
- clove
- ten egg yolks
- chopped rabbit liver
- pork tripe, eight inches long

## Directions

1. Chop onion and cook it with a little water or lard or jowl.
2. When it is well cooked and only the fat remains, divide a piece of lard into dice-shaped pieces and add them to the pan with the onion.
3. Mix in three-fourths blood and one part cream, season with fine salt and spices of cinnamon, pepper, and clove.
4. Add ten egg yolks and mix well.
5. If the mixture is still very liquid, add chopped rabbit liver.
6. Heat over gentle heat, stirring and mixing constantly without letting it become too hot.
7. Fill pork tripe about eight inches long, leaving one-third empty so the pudding does not burst when cooking.
8. Conclude the operation as described for the pork pudding.
