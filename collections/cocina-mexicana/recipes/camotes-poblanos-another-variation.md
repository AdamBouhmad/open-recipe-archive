## Ingredients

- three pounds sweet potato
- two pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Use three pounds of sweet potato to two pounds of sugar and proceed as in the previous recipes, with the difference that to shape the candies, wet your hands instead of holding powdered sugar in them
2. Place them on a board and let them dry in the sun
