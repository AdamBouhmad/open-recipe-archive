## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Ham
- Lard
- Salt
- Sausage
- Longaniza
- Wine
- Water
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Flour

## Directions

1. Lard the chickens with thin strips of ham
2. Fry them quickly in a pot with lard and salt, together with pieces of longaniza and sausage
3. Cover all the meats with wine and very little water
4. Add raisins, almonds, cloves, and cinnamon
5. Cover the pot and put it on the fire to cook the chickens, turning them
6. When cooked, if there is too much broth, thicken it with flour browned in lard
