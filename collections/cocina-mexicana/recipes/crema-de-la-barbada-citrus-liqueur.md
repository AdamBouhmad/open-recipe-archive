## Ingredients

- One jug of brandy
- Rind of six choice citrons, cleaned of flesh
- Half an ounce of cinnamon
- Half an ounce of mace
- Six quartillos of river water
- Three and a half pounds of refined sugar
- One quartillo of orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Place the cleaned citron rinds, cinnamon, and mace in infusion in the jug of brandy for eight days, keeping the vessel well covered
2. After that time, distill in a bain-marie to extract nine quartillos of liqueur
3. Dissolve the sugar in six quartillos of river water over heat
4. After the syrup has cooled, add one quartillo of orange blossom water
5. Mix with the distilled brandy
6. Filter the liqueur and bottle it
