## Ingredients

- bread crumb from a soft roll
- water
- pepper
- salt
- a little butter
- egg yolks
- cream

## Directions

1. Put bread crumb from a soft roll in a pan with water, pepper, salt and a little butter
2. Let it all boil over gentle heat until the bread is well softened
3. Make a liaison of egg yolks and cream
4. Mix the liaison with the panetela when it is no longer boiling
5. Serve
