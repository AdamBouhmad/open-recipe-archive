## Ingredients

- Ricotta cheese
- Syrup
- Cloves
- Ground cinnamon
- Bruised almonds
- Hard biscuit, ground
- Twelve egg yolks
- Six egg whites, beaten

## Directions

1. Cook the ricotta with syrup, a little clove, ground cinnamon, bruised almonds, and ground hard biscuit
2. Mix everything with twelve yolks and six beaten egg whites
3. Take portions with a spoon and fry in very hot lard, letting them puff as much as they want
4. Serve with ground cinnamon on top
