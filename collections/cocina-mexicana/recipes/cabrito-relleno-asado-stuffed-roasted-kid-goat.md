## Ingredients

- kid goat
- butter
- ground garlic
- salt
- pork loin
- water
- chorizo
- tomatoes, minced
- onions, minced
- clove
- pepper
- saffron
- cumin
- parsley, minced
- broth
- raisins
- sliced almonds
- pine nuts
- capers
- ham, diced
- hard-boiled eggs
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Skin without removing the head, and removing the entrails, hang it, allowing it to ripen until the next day
2. After washing and drying, anoint outside and inside with butter, ground garlic and salt
3. Prepare the filling: mince raw pork loin and cook with water, salt and a chorizo
4. Fry in butter minced garlic, tomatoes and onions, and when done add the cooked pork loin and chorizo meat
5. Season with ground clove, pepper, saffron and cumin, and minced parsley
6. Moisten with a little broth and add raisins, sliced almonds, pine nuts and capers, cooking until the broth reduces
7. Add small diced squares of ham and hard-boiled eggs, and a little vinegar and oil with necessary salt
8. Fill the kid goat with this mixture, sew the belly opening, place in a dish with butter mixed with ground garlic and salt, and cook over two fires or in the oven, which should not be very hot
