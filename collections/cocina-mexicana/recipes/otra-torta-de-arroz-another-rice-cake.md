## Ingredients

- four ounces rice, soaked overnight
- one pineapple, grated
- necessary sugar
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron
- six egg whites
- two egg yolks
- butter
- syrup with spices
- colored tablet (pastilla colorada)

## Directions

1. Soak rice overnight; to four ounces add grated pineapple with necessary sugar
2. Cook all together and add clove, cinnamon, pepper and saffron
3. When cool, add six beaten egg whites and two beaten yolks
4. Cook in butter over two fires until it sets
5. Squeeze and place in syrup with all the spices
6. Boil until set and top with a colored tablet
