## Ingredients

- young rabbits
- oil
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- garlic
- bay leaf
- earthen truffles
- mushrooms
- game essence
- tomato sauce
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Divide the young rabbits into pieces and put them on the fire in oil with salt, pepper, nutmeg, garlic and bay leaf
2. When cooked, remove some of the oil from the pan, the garlic and the bay leaf
3. Add earthen truffles, mushrooms, a little game essence and tomato sauce
4. Let everything boil, add lemon juice, season and serve
