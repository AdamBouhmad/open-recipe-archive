## Ingredients

- Sugar
- Almonds or cooked egg yolks or other items to be glazed

## Directions

1. Prepare a quantity of sugar syrup according to what will be glazed, clarifying it
2. Let it boil until taking a little with the fingers, it cracks between them
3. Once the syrup reaches this point, remove the pan from the heat, place it among straw, wrap it well and keep it covered
4. Add almonds, cooked egg yolks, or whatever you wish to glaze, immediately covering the pan so that air cannot reach the syrup
5. Leave it this way until it cools completely
6. Break the crust that will have formed on the yolks or items by one side
7. Drain the honey from them and carefully remove them
8. Rinse in lukewarm water and let drain
