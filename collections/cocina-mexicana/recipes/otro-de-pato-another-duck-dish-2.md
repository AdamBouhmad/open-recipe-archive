## Ingredients

- ducks, prepared as previously described
- butter
- garlic, finely chopped
- parsley, finely chopped
- onion, finely chopped
- ham slices
- thin turnips
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- water
- turnip slices
- fried bread slices
- Jerez wine

## Directions

1. Prepare a small casserole with butter
2. Chop garlic, parsley, and onion, and fry together with ham slices and the duck
3. Chop thin turnips and fry them with butter
4. Together with cloves, cinnamon, and pepper, add them to the pot where everything is cooking
5. Add water for seasoning
6. Slice other turnips and add them
7. Fry bread slices, grind them, and add to the pot
8. The dish should be thick
9. Before removing from heat, add Jerez wine
10. Garnish as in the previous recipes
