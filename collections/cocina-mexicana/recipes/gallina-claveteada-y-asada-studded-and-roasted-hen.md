## Ingredients

- hen
- cinnamon
- cloves
- pepper
- ham
- raisins
- almonds
- chiles
- garlic
- cumin
- vinegar
- wine
- water
- lard
- salt
- tomato
- parsley
- spices

## Directions

1. Clean the hen and divide into quarters
2. Stud the quarters with cinnamon, cloves, pepper, ham, raisins and almonds
3. Grind well broad chiles that have been soaked and cleaned with garlic and cumin
4. Dissolve the ground mixture with vinegar and wine
5. Marinate the studded hen quarters in this adobo from one day to the next
6. The following day add a little water to the sauce and cook the hen in it with lard and little salt so that when the adobo is consumed it is not too salty
7. When well cooked and the broth is consumed put to brown
8. Serve with fried tomato sauce with garlic, parsley and spices
