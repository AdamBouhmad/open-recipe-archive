## Ingredients

- Yellow limes
- Salt
- Hot water
- Sugar syrup (almíbar)
- One pound sugar per six limes

## Directions

1. The limes must be yellow; peel them well without removing the stem
2. Cook with a little salt, and as soon as they are done, remove and place in hot water without salt for two days, changing the water twice daily
3. Do not place in cold water until the limes are soft
4. For six limes, use one pound of sugar; the honey must be cold and the limes cold when they meet
5. Place over gentle heat
6. The honey must have enough water so that it is not necessary to add more later
7. Do not remove the insides; break the shell as it is and leave it as the lime is
