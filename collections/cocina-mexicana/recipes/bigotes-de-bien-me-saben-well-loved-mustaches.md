## Ingredients

- four reales of milk
- two ounces almonds
- aniseed
- sugar to taste
- twelve egg yolks
- cinnamon sticks
- hard biscuit powder
- egg whites
- butter for frying
- powdered sugar for serving

## Directions

1. Soak the almonds in cold water, and when well soaked, remove the skins and grind very finely along with the rice
2. Dissolve the ground almonds, rice, and egg yolks in a little milk and strain through a sieve
3. Add cinnamon sticks and place over heat until it pulls away well from the pan
4. Remove from heat and let cool completely
5. Once cold, remove the cinnamon and form the mixture into mustache shapes
6. Roll in hard biscuit powder, then in combined egg yolk and egg whites, and fry in butter
7. Serve with powdered sugar sprinkled on top
