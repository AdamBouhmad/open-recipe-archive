## Ingredients

- two pounds dough
- flour
- egg
- optional: rabbit, larks or other meats
- stew

## Directions

1. Mold or arrange two pounds of dough into a well rounded form
2. Flatten evenly with a rolling pin
3. Fold three inches of dough at the edge between the fingers, avoiding creases or wrinkles
4. Raise to a convenient height
5. Make the size of an appetizer plate, garnishing if desired with what will go inside, such as rabbit or larks, or if not, fill the interior of the pastry with flour only
6. Cover and decorate by brushing with a broken and beaten egg using a feather brush
7. Put into a preheated oven
8. Be careful not to brown too much
9. When cooked, empty by removing the interior dough and replace with a stew
10. To make a raised crust to cover it, use the same procedures and place its crust cover on the pastry
