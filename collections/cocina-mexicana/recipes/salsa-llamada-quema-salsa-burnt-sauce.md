## Ingredients

- soft part of a mollet bread of half a pound, or of a soft bread of equal weight
- milk
- twenty grains of black pepper
- salt
- amount of fine butter equal to one egg

## Directions

1. Soak the soft part of a mollet bread of half a pound, or of a soft bread of equal weight in milk and let it dry with the milk
2. Then cook it until it has the consistency of a thick pap
3. Add twenty grains of black pepper and sufficient salt, completing the operation by adding an amount of fine butter equal to one egg
4. Serve in a sauce boat next to the dishes or partridges
