## Ingredients

- Onion
- fine herbs
- butter
- flour
- water or broth
- lentils
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Brown pieces of onion and chopped fine herbs in butter.
2. Add flour; when the mixture is formed, moisten with water and better yet with broth.
3. Add the lentils cooked in water and drained as before.
4. Add salt and pepper, sauté the same way and serve.
