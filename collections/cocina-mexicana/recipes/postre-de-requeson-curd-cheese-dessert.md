## Ingredients

- curd cheese
- sugar
- ground cinnamon
- coconut paste
- egg
- clarified syrup
- orange blossom water
- raisins
- almonds
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix the curd cheese with sugar and ground cinnamon, and make small tortillas from it by hand that are wrapped with coconut paste brought to a high point on the inside
2. Roll them in beaten egg, fry them and put them in clarified syrup that has orange blossom water, decorating with raisins, almonds and cinnamon
