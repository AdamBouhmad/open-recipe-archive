## Ingredients

- rice
- small amount of water
- lemon peel
- milk
- sugar
- three eggs
- two ounces ground almonds
- piece of marrow (tuétano)

## Directions

1. Cook the rice in a small amount of water with lemon peel
2. Then add milk and sugar to finish cooking
3. Beat three eggs as for frying
4. Mix in the rice, two ounces of ground almonds and a piece of marrow
