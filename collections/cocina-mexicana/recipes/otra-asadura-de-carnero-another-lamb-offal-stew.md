## Ingredients

- lamb lungs, sliced
- lard
- salt
- tomato
- onion
- hot water
- pepper
- oil

## Directions

1. Slice the lungs into slices the thickness of a finger, put in a pan with plenty of lard and a little salt
2. Chop tomato in a quantity equal to one third of the offal, and an equal part of onion, and cook everything together over low heat without water until fried
3. Then add hot water in proportioned quantity, a pinch of pepper and cooking oil
4. Bring to a boil and let the water be consumed; the broth should remain thick
