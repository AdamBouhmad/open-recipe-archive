## Ingredients

- Six carrots
- Six turnips
- Onions
- Cabbage
- One foot of celery
- One glass of water
- Four ounces of lard or fresh butter
- Small bundle of parsley
- One quarter cuartillo of cooked chickpeas or beans
- Water for the necessary broth
- Salt for seasoning

## Directions

1. Cut six carrots into thin halves and six turnips, and chop the onions, cabbage, and celery very fine.
2. Put this in a pot adding a glass of water and four ounces of lard or fresh butter and a small bundle of parsley.
3. Boil everything until the water is consumed and add one quarter cuartillo of cooked chickpeas or beans and enough water to obtain the necessary broth.
4. Let them cook for three hours and, having seasoned them properly, pass the broth through a sieve or strainer.
5. With it, soups can be prepared for Fridays and vigils in the same manner as those prepared with meat broth.
