## Ingredients

- rice
- milk
- sugar
- a little orange blossom water
- cinnamon
- butter
- egg yolks
- sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Cook rice with milk, sugar, a little orange blossom water, cinnamon and butter
2. When cooked, add egg yolks and pour into a dish to cool
3. Form balls the size of an egg
4. Fry and dust with sugar
