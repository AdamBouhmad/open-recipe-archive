## Ingredients

- Guaxolote (turkey)
- Picadillo de toda clase de viandas (hash of various meats)
- Salchichas (sausages)
- Castañas asadas (roasted chestnuts)

## Directions

1. Prepare the turkey with garnish and place it on the spit
2. It requires an hour and a half to roast perfectly
3. It can be stuffed with hash of all kinds of meats mixed with sausages, and if desired, with roasted chestnuts
