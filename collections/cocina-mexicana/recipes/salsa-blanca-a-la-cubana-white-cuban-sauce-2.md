## Ingredients

- pieces of veal cooked the day before
- essence of poultry
- broth from the pot
- four egg yolks
- parsley
- butter

## Directions

1. Take pieces of veal cooked the day before and cut into small pieces
2. Add essence of poultry with broth from the pot
3. Bring to a boil in a pan
4. Add four egg yolks, a little parsley, and butter
5. Cook over low heat until the sauce is bound
