## Ingredients

- hen
- bacon strips
- salt
- vinegar
- watercress

## Directions

1. Lard the hen with bacon strips
2. Wrap it in greased paper
3. Place it on the spit
4. When almost roasted, remove the paper so it takes color
5. Baste the bacon strips
6. If possible, place it on a platter of watercress
7. Sprinkle with salt and a little vinegar
8. Serve drizzled with gravy
