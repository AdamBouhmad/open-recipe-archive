## Ingredients

- Six quartillos of milk
- Half pound of blanched almonds
- Half pound of ground almonds
- Three pounds of white sugar
- Half pound of ground rice, strained with the milk
- Twelve egg yolks, well drained and dissolved in a little cold milk

## Directions

1. Combine six quartillos of milk with half pound of blanched almonds, half pound of ground almonds, three pounds of white sugar, half pound of ground rice strained with the milk, and twelve egg yolks dissolved in a little cold milk
2. Cook everything and bring it to the point of cajeta, adding more sugar if needed
