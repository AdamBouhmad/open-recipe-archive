## Ingredients

- Dough
- Minced meat filling (beef)
- Fried mushrooms
- Artichoke hearts
- Spanish sauce, thick

## Directions

1. Mold a little dough, extend it until the size of an appetizer plate, and place this base on a tart pan of the same size
2. Spread a little minced meat filling in the middle of the sheet of dough, and place on top a few fried and drained mushrooms
3. Add some artichoke hearts divided into four or six parts, and roll the remaining filling like sausages of convenient thickness, placing them on top of the other garnishes and around them, so that everything forms a kind of dome or somewhat flattened ball
4. Make another sheet of dough somewhat larger than the first, moisten the edge of this one; place the second on top to form the cover and seal it at the edges with the first
5. Trim the edges and dampen the tart, place a false cover of puff pastry dough, brush it with egg wash, and put it in the oven to cook
6. When it is done, remove the cover, trim it, add a good thick Spanish sauce, and serve
