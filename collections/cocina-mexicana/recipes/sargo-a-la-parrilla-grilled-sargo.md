## Ingredients

- sargo fish
- oil
- salt
- coarse pepper

## Directions

1. After emptying and cleaning the fish thoroughly, make some incisions on each side
2. Place to marinate in oil with salt and coarse pepper for one hour before grilling
3. Place on the grill and sprinkle from time to time with its marinade until perfectly cooked
4. Serve with chopped sauce (see reinforced chopped sauce)
