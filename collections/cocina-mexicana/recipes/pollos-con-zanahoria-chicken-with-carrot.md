## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Ham
- Chorizo
- Carrot
- Lard
- Thyme
- Water
- Oil
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Sherry wine
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Cut chicken into quarters and place in a casserole with ham and chorizo slices, carrot slices, a spoonful of lard and a little thyme
2. Fry everything very well
3. Add water
4. Add oil and grind clove, cinnamon and pepper
5. Add a little sherry wine before serving
6. Garnish the platter with chiles, olives and capers
