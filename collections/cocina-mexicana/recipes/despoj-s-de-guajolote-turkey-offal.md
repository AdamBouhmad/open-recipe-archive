## Ingredients

- turkey offal
- salt
- butter
- a little flour
- green chile
- cinnamon
- parsley
- ham, finely chopped

## Directions

1. Cook the turkey offal with salt
2. After bringing to a boil, remove from heat and fry in butter with a little flour
3. Add green chile, cinnamon, parsley, and finely chopped ham
4. Let cook over low heat
5. Serve after two hours
