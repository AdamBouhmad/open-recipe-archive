## Ingredients

- one pound fresh lard
- one pound ground sugar
- one pound flour
- a little salt
- one quarter ounce ground cinnamon
- sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Take one pound of fresh lard and knead it with one pound of ground sugar and one pound of flour, adding a little salt.
2. Knead with the hands until the dough has no lumps or rough spots, and when it is smooth, divide it into equal quantities.
3. To one portion add one quarter ounce of ground cinnamon and mix well, then spread on paper to a thickness of one finger's width.
4. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top, then place the other portion of dough on top and sprinkle more sesame seeds over it.
5. Place between two comales (griddles) to cook, and when half-cooked, make cuts with a knife so the heat penetrates and it cooks well.
6. When cooked, break into small pieces, wrap in paper, and serve.
