## Ingredients

- Cooked and peeled potatoes
- Butter
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Eggs
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Grind the cooked and peeled potatoes with butter so that the paste is not too watery but rather firm
2. Add egg yolks to give it a good color
3. Season with the appropriate amount of salt
4. Make small balls, shapes like mustaches, or little figures as desired
5. Bathe them with half-beaten egg, mixing the white with the yolk
6. Roll them in breadcrumbs
7. Fry them and serve
