## Ingredients

- slices of beef or veal round
- bay leaves
- fish salt
- ground salt
- salted anchovies
- good oil

## Directions

1. Cut slices of beef or veal round (the latter is better)
2. Place in boiling water in which bay leaves and fish salt have been added
3. After the meat has soaked for two hours, remove and drain
4. Dust again with ground salt and beat with a round stick until the meat is well penetrated with salt
5. In the bottom of the vessel where the meat is to be placed, put some salted anchovies
6. Place the prepared beef or veal on top
7. Fill the vessel with good oil
8. Serve as a supplementary dish
