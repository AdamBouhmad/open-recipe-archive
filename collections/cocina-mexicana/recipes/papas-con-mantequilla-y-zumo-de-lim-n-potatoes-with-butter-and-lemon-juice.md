## Ingredients

- potatoes, cooked in a hermetically sealed pot
- butter
- parsley, chopped very fine
- shallots, chopped very fine
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut into slices the potatoes cooked in a hermetically sealed pot, as for salad à la française.
2. Place them in a pot with a good piece of butter, very finely chopped parsley and shallots, salt, pepper and lemon juice.
3. Mix everything together and bind in a pot over gentle heat.
4. Serve as quickly as possible.
