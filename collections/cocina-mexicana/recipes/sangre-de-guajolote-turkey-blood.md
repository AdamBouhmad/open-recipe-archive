## Ingredients

- Salt
- Turkey blood
- Lard
- Lettuce
- Oil
- Onion slices
- Pepper
- Chiles

## Directions

1. Put salt on a plate and collect the turkey blood into it
2. When curdled, heat lard in a pan
3. Add the whole blood as it is
4. Turn it like an omelet
5. When fried, place on a plate with lettuce, oil, onion slices, salt, and pepper
6. Garnish with chiles
