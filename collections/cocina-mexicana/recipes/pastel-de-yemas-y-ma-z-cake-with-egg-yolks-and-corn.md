## Ingredients

- corn
- sugar
- six cooked and ground egg yolks
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Wash the corn and trim the ends
2. Put in the sun to dry
3. Grind and sift through an ayate
4. Grind sugar
5. To about half an almud of corn, add six cooked and ground egg yolks with melted butter
6. Make it like the previous recipe, placing it in a casserole dish greased with butter and cooking covered with fire on top
7. Fill with desired filling
