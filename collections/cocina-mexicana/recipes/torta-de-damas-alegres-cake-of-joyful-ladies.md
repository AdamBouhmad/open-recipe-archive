## Ingredients

- one pound almonds
- one pound sugar
- orange blossom water
- seven beaten eggs
- butter or lard
- raisins
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. After soaking one pound of almonds in hot water, clean and grind with another pound of sugar, rolling it with orange blossom water
2. Mix with seven beaten eggs and put in a skillet or pan oiled with butter or lard
3. Let it set over two fires or in the oven
4. Serve decorated with raisins and pine nuts
