## Ingredients

- veal
- fat bacon
- garlic
- parsley
- pepper
- lemon juice
- salt
- water
- lard
- white wine or aguardiente
- oil

## Directions

1. Cut veal into pieces of about half a pound and place in a cazuela with heat above and below so it roasts slowly
2. When half-roasted, fry fat bacon and put in the cazuela with crushed garlic, parsley, pepper, lemon juice, salt and a small amount of water
3. Stir from time to time so it browns on all sides
4. If the veal is fatty, roast it dusted first with salt, and when half-roasted remove the moisture from the fire and add a little lard and white wine or aguardiente; if there is no lard, good oil may be used
