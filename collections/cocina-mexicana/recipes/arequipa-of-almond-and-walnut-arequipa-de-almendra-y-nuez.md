## Ingredients

- Four quartillos of milk
- Three pounds of white sugar
- Ground almonds, very finely ground and moistened with water during grinding to prevent oiliness
- Half a cup of walnut, well ground and dissolved in a little milk

## Directions

1. Cook four quartillos of milk and sweeten it afterwards with three pounds of white sugar
2. Let it come to a boil and set aside to cool
3. Pour it into another pan without passing the sediment, being careful not to stir it
4. Add very finely ground almonds that have been moistened with water while grinding
5. Put everything over the fire until it thickens, then add half a cup of ground walnut dissolved in a little milk
6. Allow it to reach the proper point and pour out of the pan
