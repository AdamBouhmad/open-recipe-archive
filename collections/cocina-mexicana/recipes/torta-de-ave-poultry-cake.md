## Ingredients

- poultry of any kind
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- fine herbs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Any kind of poultry that is used should be cut into two pieces or more, scald it and reheat it in butter with salt, pepper, spices, fine herbs and a bundle of parsley
2. Place it on the base of the cake
3. The poultry giblets are prepared the same way
