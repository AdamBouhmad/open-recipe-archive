## Ingredients

- six eggs
- one pound flour
- half pound ground sugar
- four ounces cooked beef marrow
- butter
- pine nuts
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Beat six eggs and mix with one pound of flour, half pound of ground sugar, four ounces of cooked beef marrow, and butter.
2. Butter a pan or dish and place the cake in it to cook.
3. Decorate on top with pine nuts, raisins, and almonds, toasted after cleaning.
