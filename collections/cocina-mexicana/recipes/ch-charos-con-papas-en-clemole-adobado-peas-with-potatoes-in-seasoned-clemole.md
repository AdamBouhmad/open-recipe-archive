## Ingredients

- Peas
- Small round onions (cebolli­tas cabezonas)
- Potatoes
- Dried ancho chiles
- Tomatoes, cooked
- Lard
- Salt
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the peas, small onions, and potatoes separately, then peel and chop the potatoes
2. Grind the deveined and soaked ancho chiles with cooked tomatoes
3. Fry in lard and season with salt
4. Add the peas, potatoes, and small onions with oregano, thyme, vinegar, and the water in which the onions were cooked
5. Let everything boil until the broth reduces slightly; it should not be very thick
