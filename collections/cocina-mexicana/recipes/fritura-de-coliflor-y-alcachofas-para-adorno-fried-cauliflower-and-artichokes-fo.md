## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- artichokes
- butter
- good eating oil
- garlic
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflowers and artichokes.
2. Remove the hearts from the artichokes and the florets from the cauliflower.
3. Put a piece of butter and twice as much good eating oil in a pan, clean two or three garlic cloves half crushed, fry until they are browning, and add the pieces of cauliflower and artichoke hearts.
4. Taking care that they do not fall apart, fry until they are lightly browned, remove one and the other from the pan, and sprinkling them with pepper and salt, place them on the soup or stews that they should garnish.
