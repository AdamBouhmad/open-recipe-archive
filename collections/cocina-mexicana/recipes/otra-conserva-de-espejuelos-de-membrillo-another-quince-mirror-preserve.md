## Ingredients

- Quince slices
- Peach jelly
- Apple jelly
- Orange blossom water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Complete the operation as described in the previous article
2. Without removing the pot from heat, add five quarts of peach jelly and one quart of apple jelly for each six pounds of quince
3. Let it return to the point of forming a thin film on a plate of water
4. Remove from heat
5. Add a little orange blossom water
6. Pour into glass jars or other vessels for storage
