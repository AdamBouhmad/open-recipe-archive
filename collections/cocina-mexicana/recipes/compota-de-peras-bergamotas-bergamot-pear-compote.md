## Ingredients

- whole pears with skin
- water
- sugar
- lemon strip

## Directions

1. Cook the pears whole in their skin in boiling water
2. When slightly cooked, peel them whole or cut in half and place in cold water
3. Cook sugar in a pan with half a quarter liter of water
4. Add the pears with a lemon strip
5. After they are well cooked and the syrup reaches the proper point, serve cold or hot
