## Ingredients

- Veal loins
- Ham strips
- Garlic cloves, split lengthwise
- Cinnamon sticks
- Pepper grains
- Clove
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Thyme
- Oregano
- Water

## Directions

1. Lard the loins very densely with ham strips, garlic cloves split lengthwise, cinnamon sticks and grains of pepper and clove.
2. Place in a casserole and cover with vinegar, adding salt, thyme and oregano, and leave in this marinade until the next day.
3. On that day add water and place to cook until the broth is consumed and they remain completely dry, taking care to turn them so they do not stick or burn.
