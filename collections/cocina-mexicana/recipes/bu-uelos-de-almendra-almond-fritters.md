## Ingredients

- almonds
- sugar
- salt
- cinnamon
- orange blossom water
- flour
- lard
- syrup
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Grind the almonds after peeling them, strain them like atole and cook the same way with sugar and a little salt
2. When cold, add a little cinnamon and some orange blossom water
3. Knead with proportioned flour and lard until very soft
4. Make the fritters, fry in lard, and put them in syrup with cinnamon, raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
