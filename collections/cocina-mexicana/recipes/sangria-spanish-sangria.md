## Ingredients

- equal parts orange drink made from sour orange
- red wine covered, or Carlon
- optionally: lemonade instead of orange drink
- optionally: snow or ice

## Directions

1. Mix the orange drink and red wine in equal parts
2. When mixing, raise your hand as high as possible so that as the liquid falls it forms foam, with which the sangria is drunk
3. Less wine may be added according to the particular taste of those drinking it
4. Lemonade may substitute the orange drink, and if it is snow-chilled, the sangria will be better
