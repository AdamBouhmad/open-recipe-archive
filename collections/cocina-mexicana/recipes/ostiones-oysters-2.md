## Ingredients

- hardtack bread
- pepper
- onion
- parsley
- oil
- lard
- vinegar
- thyme
- bay leaves
- oysters

## Directions

1. Grind a little hardtack bread and some pepper.
2. Fry finely chopped onion and very finely chopped parsley and parboil in oil and lard.
3. After parboiling, add two tablespoons of bread with pepper and parsley.
4. Add vinegar, thyme, and a few bay leaves.
5. Wash the oysters well to remove all brine and place in the sauce.
6. Let them simmer for a while, remove from heat, and add oil.
