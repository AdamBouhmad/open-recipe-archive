## Ingredients

- Green chile
- Lard
- Tortillas
- Fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Make a thick green chile with abundant lard
2. In a baking dish or skillet, place a layer of this chile, another of tortillas first dipped in hot lard, and another of sliced fresh cheese, another of chile and so on successively until filling the dish
3. Remove the heat from the stove and leave it on an ember bed, there place the baking dish and put heat on top so it sets like a cake
