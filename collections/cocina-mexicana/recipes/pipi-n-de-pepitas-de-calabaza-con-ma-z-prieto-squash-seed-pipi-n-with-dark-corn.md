## Ingredients

- squash seeds
- dark corn
- wide chile
- lard
- salt
- broth of meats or birds
- shrimp, roots or fruits

## Directions

1. Toast the seeds and corn and grind together with the wide chile sufficient to give color
2. Strain everything and fry in lard with salt
3. Add immediately the broth of the meats or birds to be cooked in this pipián, or the shrimp, roots or fruits desired, chopped or sliced
4. Allow the sauce to thicken afterwards
