## Ingredients

- fat livers
- lard
- some mushrooms
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the livers in the pan
2. Immediately chop them with lard, some mushrooms, fine herbs, salt and pepper
3. Dress the toasted slices in the common manner
