## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- a little orange blossom water
- sixteen egg yolks
- six pounds of ground and sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of almonds with a little orange blossom water
2. Add sixteen egg yolks and six pounds of ground and sifted sugar
3. Form the rings and bake them in the oven on tin sheets
