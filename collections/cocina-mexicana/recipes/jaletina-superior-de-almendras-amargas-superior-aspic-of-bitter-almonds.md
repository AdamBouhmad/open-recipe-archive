## Ingredients

- Legs of mutton, veal, or beef, cleaned and with hair removed
- Bitter almonds, ground
- Water
- Sugar to taste

## Directions

1. Clean the meat thoroughly by removing hair and cleaning the legs
2. Cook without salt, skimming carefully and thoroughly until the meat almost falls apart, which is when the aspic is at the proper point for setting
3. Strain the aspic
4. Mix the strained aspic with a small amount of ground bitter almond mixed and dissolved in water like almond milk, and a small amount of sugar, both to taste
5. Pour the aspic into molds
6. Cool until it sets completely
7. To unmold, dip the tip of a knife in hot water and use it to loosen the aspic from the sides of the mold
8. If the aspic is very adhered to the bottom, pass the mold quickly through hot water to allow it to release without softening the aspic
9. Optional: instead of almond milk, wine, spirits, or any other liquor may be mixed in
