## Ingredients

- cod, desalted, soaked and cooked
- breadcrumbs
- garlic, peeled
- chopped parsley
- fine pepper
- clove
- saffron
- beaten eggs
- salt
- vinegar
- oil
- fried bread
- egg yolks
- cumin
- culantro
- whole pepper
- finely chopped onion

## Directions

1. Desalt, soak, and cook the cod, then remove bones and chop well
2. Mix with breadcrumbs, peeled garlic, chopped parsley, fine pepper, molded clove and saffron, beaten eggs, and salt
3. Season the mixture well
4. Form meatballs with a spoon moistened in vinegar
5. Fry in very hot oil as they are formed
6. For the broth, grind a little fried bread, egg yolks, saffron, cumin, and culantro; add whole clove and pepper, chopped parsley, and a little finely chopped and fried onion
7. When the broth is well seasoned, add the meatballs to finish cooking until tender
