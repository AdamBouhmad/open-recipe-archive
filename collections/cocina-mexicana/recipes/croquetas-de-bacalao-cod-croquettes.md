## Ingredients

- cooked cod fish, cut into small cubes
- a piece of butter
- a little flour
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg, grated
- egg, beaten
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cook the fish as described in the cod article and cut it into small cubes
2. Put a piece of butter, a little flour, salt, pepper, and grated nutmeg in a saucepan
3. Place over heat, stirring and turning constantly
4. Pour this sauce over the cod, mixing everything perfectly
5. Proceed as for poultry croquettes: form into croquettes, coat with beaten egg and bread crumbs
6. Fry in hot oil until golden
7. Drain on clean cloth or a colander
8. Serve as an intermediate course
