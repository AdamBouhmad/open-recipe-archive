## Ingredients

- peeled and crushed almonds
- candied lemon peel without flesh
- syrup at the point of joining in water, or powdered sugar
- egg white

## Directions

1. Make a paste with peeled and crushed almonds, candied lemon peel without flesh, and syrup at the point of joining in water or powdered sugar
2. Sprinkle the paste with egg white
3. Dry everything over low heat
4. Divide this paste into two parts: make a cake with the larger part and small figures like hearts, little palms, flowers, etc. with the other part to decorate the cake, which are called secadillos
