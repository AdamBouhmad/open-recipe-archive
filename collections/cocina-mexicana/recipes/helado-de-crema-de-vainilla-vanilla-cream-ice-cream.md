## Ingredients

- Six egg yolks
- Three quarters of a pound of sugar
- A piece of vanilla
- Two and a half cuartillos of cream

## Directions

1. Place six egg yolks, three quarters of a pound of sugar and a piece of vanilla in a pan and beat well
2. Then place on the fire with two and a half cuartillos of cream and stir much until it adheres to the spoon, without letting it boil
3. Cool and freeze
