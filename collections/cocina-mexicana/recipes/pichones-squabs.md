## Ingredients

- squabs
- salt
- lard
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- chopped parsley
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- capers
- broth from cooking the squabs
- vinegar
- olives
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook the squabs in salted water
2. Fry them in lard, then remove from the fat
3. Fry roasted ground tomatoes and chopped parsley in the lard
4. Add salt, ground cloves, cinnamon and pepper to the mixture
5. Add capers, the fried squabs, the broth in which they were cooked, and a little vinegar
6. Let simmer until the sauce becomes somewhat thick
7. When removing from heat, add olives and oil
