## Ingredients

- small whole cooked onions
- small pickles
- anchovy strips
- aromatic herbs, chopped
- pieces of marinated tuna
- vinegar
- oil
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- capers
- stuffed olives

## Directions

1. Arrange the small whole cooked onions and small pickles in a dish
2. Add the anchovy strips, chopped aromatic herbs, and pieces of marinated tuna
3. Season as is customary
4. Adorn with hard-boiled egg yolks, capers, and stuffed olives
