## Ingredients

- butter
- breadcrumbs
- fine herbs, minced
- cooked beef pulp
- salt

## Directions

1. Spread butter in a plate with breadcrumbs and minced fine herbs, and make very thin slices of cooked beef pulp seasoned with salt.
2. Place one layer of them on top of the plate, and then another layer of herbs and pieces of butter.
