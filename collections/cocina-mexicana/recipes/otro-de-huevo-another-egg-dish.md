## Ingredients

- eggs
- tomato without seeds, chopped
- onion, finely chopped
- garlic cloves, two or three
- parsley
- cooked egg yolk
- salt
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron threads
- water
- thyme
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook the eggs as before
2. Fry the garlic first, then the onion, and finally the parsley with the tomato
3. Fry with a cooked egg yolk and salt to taste
4. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper and saffron threads
5. Add water and when it is boiling place the eggs with thyme and oil on top and serve
