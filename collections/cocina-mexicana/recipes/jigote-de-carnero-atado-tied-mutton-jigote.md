## Ingredients

- good piece of mutton haunch, somewhat lean
- tocino slices for larding
- vinegar
- defatted broth
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- chopped scallions
- garlic cloves
- oil

## Directions

1. Remove the outer skin from the meat
2. Lard with tocino slices
3. Submerge in a marinade of equal parts vinegar and defatted broth with good seasoning of salt, pepper, fine herbs, chopped scallions and garlic cloves
4. Leave the meat two hours in this sauce, turning several times to absorb
5. Remove and drain the meat
6. Dry and rub all over with a little oil
7. Place on a spit in front of a good fire
8. Continuously baste with its own juices and serve with the defatted juices
