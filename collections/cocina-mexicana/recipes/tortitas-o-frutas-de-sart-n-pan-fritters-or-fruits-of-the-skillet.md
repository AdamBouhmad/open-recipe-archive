## Ingredients

- One cup of water
- One pound of sugar
- One pound of lard
- Three egg yolks
- Salt, the amount that fits in three fingers
- Sufficient flour to make a dough like biscuit dough

## Directions

1. Mix one cup of water, one pound of sugar, one pound of lard, three egg yolks, salt, and sufficient flour to make a dough like biscuit dough
2. Form small round tortillas with it, place them on papers, and put them to cook in a very mild oven
