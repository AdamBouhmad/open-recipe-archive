## Ingredients

- güaxolote (turkey)
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- nutmeg
- garlic, chopped
- onion
- parsley
- ham slices
- water
- wine
- bitter oranges or limes, sliced
- garlic cloves
- onions
- bay leaf
- thyme
- avocado leaves

## Directions

1. After cleaning the turkey, dust it well with salt, pepper, clove, cinnamon and nutmeg, all ground together.
2. Arrange chopped garlic, onion and parsley all over it, and on top place slices of fat ham.
3. Wrap it well in a clean napkin and boil it in water.
4. After an hour and a half, remove it and make openings with a knife.
5. Lard it with ham, raw garlic, cloves, cinnamon, pepper, salt and avocado leaves.
6. Place it afterwards in a clay pot and add a proportionate amount of wine, sliced bitter oranges or limes, garlic cloves and onions cut into quarters.
7. Add a little bay leaf and thyme, cover the pot and place it on the fire.
8. Once cooked, remove it and serve in regular pieces, or alone with slices of blanched onion and lettuce hearts, or with adobo sauce and grated cheese on top, or with a little vinegar and much oil with sufficient salt and pepper.
