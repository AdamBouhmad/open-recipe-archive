## Ingredients

- Fresh walnuts
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Choose fresh walnuts and remove the skin
2. Drop them in water and grind well on a clean metate
3. Make syrup and add the ground walnuts
4. Cook until it pulls away from the pot
5. Pour into paper boxes cut to desired size
