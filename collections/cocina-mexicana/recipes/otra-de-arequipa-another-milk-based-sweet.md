## Ingredients

- 5 cuartillos of milk
- 2.5 pounds of sugar
- 1 ounce and 4 ounces of starch
- 1 ounce of unground almonds
- one stick of cinnamon
- one cleaned and ground pineapple
- orange flower water
- olor (aromatic flavoring)
- a little bread crumb

## Directions

1. Heat the sweetened milk with its stick of cinnamon
2. Add the starch
3. Once thick, add the unground almonds to cook until it reaches the proper point
4. Add the cleaned and ground pineapple, stirring continuously
5. Give it its proper point
6. Just before removing from heat, add orange flower water, olor, and a little bread crumb
7. As soon as it is removed from heat, place in pans, fill the molds, let them cool, and set them in the sun
