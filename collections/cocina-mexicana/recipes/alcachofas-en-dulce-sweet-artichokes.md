## Ingredients

- artichokes
- broth
- salt
- fried ham
- butter
- onion
- sugar

## Directions

1. Trim and clean the artichokes
2. Cook in broth and salt
3. Drain them
4. Top with fried ham, butter, and fried chopped onion
5. Sprinkle a little sugar on top
6. Let simmer over gentle heat for several boils
