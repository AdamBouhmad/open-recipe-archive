## Ingredients

- capers, minced
- parsley, minced
- salt
- ground pepper
- oil
- white wine

## Directions

1. Mince capers and parsley, and mix them with salt and ground pepper, stirring everything together with oil.
2. After cleaning the bobo fish, stuff them with this mixture.
3. Wrap them in paper and place them in a platter or casserole with oil and white wine.
4. Let them cook over gentle heat until they are browned.
5. Once roasted, unwrap them and place them on another platter with more oil, ground pepper, capers, olives, split tornachiles and minced parsley on top.
6. While being brought to the table, keep them over the steam of a boiling pot.
