## Ingredients

- Half a bottle of white wine
- Half a cuartillo of vinegar
- Juice of two lemons
- Three ounces of salt
- Half an ounce of black pepper
- A little nutmeg and mace
- Four cloves
- Four bay leaves
- A little thyme
- A fairly thick bunch of parsley
- One garlic head
- Ten tender garlic cloves, crushed
- One ounce of tender mushrooms

## Directions

1. In a pot over heat, add half a bottle of white wine, half a cuartillo of vinegar, juice of two lemons, three ounces of salt, half an ounce of black pepper, a little nutmeg and mace, four cloves, four bay leaves, a little thyme, a thick bunch of parsley, one garlic head, ten tender crushed garlic cloves, and one ounce of tender mushrooms
2. When it is about to boil, reduce heat and leave over hot ashes for six or seven hours
3. Pass through a sieve and filter
4. Store in well-sealed bottles and use as needed
