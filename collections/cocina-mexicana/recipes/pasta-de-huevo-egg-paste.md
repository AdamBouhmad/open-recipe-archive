## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- six egg whites
- two ounces of very finely ground almond

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with one pound of sugar that is clarified and brought to the point of gathering in water
2. Set aside and beat with care so it does not curdle
3. Once it has cooled a little, mix in six well-beaten egg whites, having let the oil drain off, and two ounces of very finely ground almond
4. When everything is well incorporated, return to the fire to cook the egg, without ceasing to move
5. Set aside again and beat until it has the proper consistency
