## Ingredients

- Five or six large onions
- Lard
- Calf sweetbread, cut into pieces
- Flour
- Beef broth, defatted
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Brown five or six large onions cut into pieces in lard until they take on a good golden color
2. Add the sweetbread cut into pieces to the pan
3. Stir for a few minutes avoiding the lard from hardening too much
4. Add a piece of flour, a cup of defatted broth and a spoonful of vinegar
5. Serve very hot
