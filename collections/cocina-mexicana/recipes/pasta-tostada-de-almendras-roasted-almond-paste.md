## Ingredients

- One ounce of sweet blanched ground almonds
- A little egg white
- Orange blossom water
- Twelve ounces of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the ground almonds with a little egg white and orange blossom water
2. Place over gentle heat
3. Gradually mix in the powdered sugar little by little while stirring constantly until everything is well incorporated
4. Pour onto a small box lined with paper
5. Cut with a knife into desired shapes
