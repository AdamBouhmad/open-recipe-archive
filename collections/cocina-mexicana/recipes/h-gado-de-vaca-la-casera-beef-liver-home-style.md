## Ingredients

- Beef liver
- Bacon pieces
- Bacon slices
- Veal scraps
- Roots
- Garlic
- Bay leaf
- Clove
- Broth
- White wine
- Butter

## Directions

1. Lard the liver across with large pieces of well-seasoned bacon
2. Place in a casserole on bacon slices with veal scraps, roots, garlic, bay leaf, clove, good broth from the pot, and half a bottle of white wine
3. Skim everything, cover with other bacon slices, seal the casserole hermetically, and place fire on top also
4. Ensure it does not boil at great rolling boils
5. After cooking, strain the sauce, take half of it, reduce it by binding with butter, and serve with this sauce
