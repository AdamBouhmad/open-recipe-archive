## Ingredients

- four egg yolks
- flour
- salt
- well-rendered lard
- egg white

## Directions

1. Mix four egg yolks with the flour they will take up, salt and well-rendered lard
2. Put some reeds to fry and clean them well, make a thick tortilla with moderate rolling pin
3. Mold and cut on the reed, seal the two ends with egg white
4. Fry the tubes in lard and remove them to be eaten without needing to fill them
