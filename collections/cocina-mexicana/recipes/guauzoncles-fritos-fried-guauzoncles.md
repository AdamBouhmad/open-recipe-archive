## Ingredients

- Guauzoncle sprouts
- Fresh cheese or aged cheese
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Pepper
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Remove all the leaves from the guauzoncle sprouts and the thickest part of the stems.
2. Boil them in salted water from the earth.
3. When cooked, remove and refresh them in clean water.
4. Squeeze them out.
5. Grind fresh or aged cheese and mix it with a little bread crumbs.
6. Place a small amount of this mixture between two layers of guauzoncle.
7. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
8. Dip in beaten egg and fry.
9. Serve fried with salt and pepper.
