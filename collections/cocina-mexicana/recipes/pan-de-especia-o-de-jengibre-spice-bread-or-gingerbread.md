## Ingredients

- honey made from unrefined sugar or panela
- almonds
- crushed cloves
- nutmeg
- pepper
- ginger
- candied or covered lemon peel
- flour

## Directions

1. Make honey from unrefined sugar or panela and skim it.
2. Mix in almonds divided into quarters, crushed cloves, nutmeg, pepper, ginger and candied lemon peel cut into strips.
3. Knead everything with as much flour as necessary so that the dough becomes thick.
4. Form small loaves in molds or without them.
5. Place them in the oven to bake, browning them with thinned honey.
