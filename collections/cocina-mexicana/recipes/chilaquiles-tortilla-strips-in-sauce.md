## Ingredients

- tortillas
- manteca
- sal
- harina
- huevo
- picadillo de carne
- clara de huevo
- mole o pipián
- costillas
- lomo

## Directions

1. Make tortillas of lard and salt, about four or five fingers long and one finger thick, well cooked on the griddle
2. Split each one in half lengthwise
3. Remove all the crumb and fill with ground meat
4. Close the halves joining them at the edges with egg white
5. Dust with flour and coat in beaten egg
6. Fry in lard, then add to mole or pipián which should be ready prepared
7. Eat alone or with ribs and loin
