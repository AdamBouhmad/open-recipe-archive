## Ingredients

- Four beef feet
- Verjuice
- Butter and flour paste
- Salt
- Pepper
- Shallot
- Broth
- Anchovy
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cook four beef feet in water.
2. Drain and place in a casserole with two tablespoons of verjuice, a piece of butter kneaded with flour, salt, pepper, chopped shallot and a quarter liter of broth.
3. Let simmer gently for half an hour.
4. Before serving, add a chopped anchovy dissolved well in its broth and a handful of chopped parsley.
5. If the sauce is too sharp, add a little more verjuice.
