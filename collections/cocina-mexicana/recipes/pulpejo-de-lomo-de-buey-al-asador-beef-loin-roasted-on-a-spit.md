## Ingredients

- Beef loin cut
- Thin ham for larding
- Good oil
- Pepper
- Bay leaf
- Onion slices
- Butter-covered paper
- Vinegar
- Garlic
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cut and wash the beef loin
2. Lard it with thin ham and let it marinate for at least twelve hours in good oil with pepper, bay leaf and onion slices
3. Remove from marinade and place on the spit, taking care to cover the larded part with butter-covered paper
4. Cook over lively fire so that it does not lose its juices
5. Set aside while still slightly bloody and serve with a sauce made from the juice of the meat, a little vinegar, garlic, salt and pepper, served separately in a sauce boat
