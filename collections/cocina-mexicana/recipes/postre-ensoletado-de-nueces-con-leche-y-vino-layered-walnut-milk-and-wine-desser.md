## Ingredients

- walnut paste with milk
- egg yolks
- sifted sugar
- sponge biscuits (soletas)
- wine
- walnut paste
- raisins
- pine nuts
- whole almonds
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Prepare a walnut paste with milk (see walnut paste recipe), but without letting it dry completely
2. In a serving dish, place a layer of egg yolks beaten with sifted sugar until hardened, and another of walnut paste
3. Place on top another layer of sponge biscuits sprinkled with wine, and another of walnut paste
4. Alternate one and the other until the dish is filled, covering the last layer with a layer of beaten egg yolks like those placed at the bottom
5. Place over two gentle fires and when the dessert is set, remove and decorate with raisins, pine nuts, whole almonds and cinnamon
