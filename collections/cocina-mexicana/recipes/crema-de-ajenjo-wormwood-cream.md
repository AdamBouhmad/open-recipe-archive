## Ingredients

- 8 quartillos brandy
- 0.5 pound fresh wormwood shoots
- peels of lemons or oranges
- 4 quartillos water
- 3 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Distill the brandy with the wormwood and peels to extract 4 quartillos of liqueur.
2. Dissolve the sugar in water put on the fire.
3. When it is about to boil, form the mixture, filter and store in bottles.
