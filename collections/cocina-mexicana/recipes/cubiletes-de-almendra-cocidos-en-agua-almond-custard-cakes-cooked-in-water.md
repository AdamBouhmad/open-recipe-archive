## Ingredients

- Ten eggs, separated
- Four ounces of clean almonds, soaked and ground
- Four ounces of powdered sugar, sifted
- Water

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites first, then the yolks until the mixture stiffens
2. Mix with four ounces of clean soaked ground almonds and equal amount of powdered sifted sugar
3. Pour into molds or cups up to half their height
4. Place in boiling water, following the same procedures as the previous article
