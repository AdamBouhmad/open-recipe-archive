## Ingredients

- Veal sweetbreads
- Bacon strips
- Good sauce
- Sorrel or tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Lard the sweetbreads with bacon strips after preparation
2. Cook in the oven with a good sauce reduction for three quarters of an hour
3. Brown well
4. Place over sorrel or in tomato sauce
