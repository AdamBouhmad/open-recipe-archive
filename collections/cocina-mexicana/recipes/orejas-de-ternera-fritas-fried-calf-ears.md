## Ingredients

- Prepared and cooked calf ears
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper in grain
- Batter
- Oil for frying
- Fried parsley

## Directions

1. Cut prepared and cooked ears lengthwise into four pieces
2. Place in marinade with vinegar, salt and peppercorns
3. Coat in batter
4. Fry them, spreading them quickly in the pan and turning them
5. Drain them
6. Serve covered with fried parsley
