## Ingredients

- 2 rabbits, boned
- ham
- diced potatoes
- salt
- carrot
- onion
- bay leaf
- parsley
- thyme
- peppercorns
- wine
- broth or water

## Directions

1. Bone two rabbits well
2. Lard one rabbit with ham
3. Chop the other rabbit with an equal piece of ham coarsely
4. Season the chopped mixture with salt and diced potatoes
5. Fill the whole rabbit with this stuffing, interspersing strips of larded ham throughout
6. Once filled, close and wrap in a clean thin cloth
7. Place in a pan lined with ham
8. Arrange carrot pieces, quartered onions, bay leaf, parsley, thyme, peppercorns, and salt around it
9. Add equal parts wine and broth or water
10. Add the broken rabbit bones
