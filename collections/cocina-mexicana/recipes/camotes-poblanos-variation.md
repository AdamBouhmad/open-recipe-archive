## Ingredients

- three pounds white sugar, ground and sifted
- two pounds white sweet potato, ground and sifted

## Directions

1. Mix three pounds of white sugar, ground and sifted, with two pounds of white sweet potato, also ground and sifted
2. Put everything over the fire and proceed as in the previous recipe
