## Ingredients

- branches of fresh fennel
- several tablespoons of butter sauce
- equal amount of ruffled sauce
- salt
- coarse pepper
- nutmeg
- butter

## Directions

1. Chop fennel branches as finely as possible
2. Blanch, refresh, and drain them
3. Mix several tablespoons of butter sauce with equal amount of ruffled sauce
4. Add salt, coarse pepper, and nutmeg to the mixture
5. Knead the chopped fennel with a little butter and add to the sauce
6. Mix everything well and serve
