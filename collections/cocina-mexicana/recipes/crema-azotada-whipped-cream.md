## Ingredients

- Cream
- Powdered sugar in a proportional amount
- A little orange blossom

## Directions

1. Put the cream in a pot with the proportional amount of powdered sugar and a little orange blossom
2. Beat all with a whisk made of peeled wicker twigs
3. When the mixture has puffed up well, let it rest a moment
4. Lift it immediately with a skimmer and arrange it on the plate in the form of a pyramid
5. Garnish with small strips of candied lemon or orange peel
6. Serve
