## Ingredients

- 2 quarts of tea infusion
- 8 to 10 ounces of sugar
- 2 quarts of aguardiente or rum, or 1 quart of each
- 12 to 15 drops of essential oil of lemon
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Make two quarts of tea infusion
2. Add eight to ten ounces of sugar
3. Strain and mix with two quarts of aguardiente or rum, or one quart of each
4. Add twelve to fifteen drops of essential lemon oil, taking care to incorporate it in a coffee spoon of powdered sugar
5. Filter through paper placed in a funnel, allowing one or two days for the filtration to complete slowly
6. Pour into two well-sealed bottles
7. Heat in a water bath when desired to drink
