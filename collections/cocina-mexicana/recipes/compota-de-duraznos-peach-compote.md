## Ingredients

- Peaches
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut into two pieces
2. Remove the stones
3. Soak to peel them
4. Make the syrup as for currants
5. Put the peaches in it, dry them, let the syrup thicken
6. Water them with it in the serving dish
7. Serve cold
