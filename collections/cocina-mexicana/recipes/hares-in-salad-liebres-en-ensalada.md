## Ingredients

- Hares
- Oil
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Small chili peppers
- Olives
- Capers
- Oregano powder

## Directions

1. Roast the hares over raw heat
2. Fry them in oil
3. Season with salt
4. Add a little vinegar
5. To serve, add oil and vinegar
6. Garnish with small chili peppers, olives, capers, and oregano powder
