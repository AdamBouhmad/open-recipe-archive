## Ingredients

- peas
- potatoes
- small onions
- wide chiles
- cooked tomatoes
- salt
- oregano
- thyme
- vinegar
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook separately peas, potatoes and small onions, peeling and chopping the latter
2. Fry in lard the wide soaked and ground chiles with cooked tomatoes and season with salt
3. Add oregano, thyme and vinegar and the water in which the onions were cooked, which is also added to the sauce with the peas and chopped potatoes
4. After boiling everything together and once the cooking liquid has been sufficiently consumed without becoming too thick, remove and serve
