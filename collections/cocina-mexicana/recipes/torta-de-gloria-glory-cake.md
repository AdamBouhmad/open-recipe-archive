## Ingredients

- rice cooked with milk
- pastry dough
- eggs
- lard (manteca)
- syrup (almíbar)

## Directions

1. Make thick rice cooked with milk, either ground or whole
2. Prepare pastry dough to taste
3. Beat eggs
4. Butter a pan and cover the bottom with egg slices until the bottom cannot be seen
5. Alternate layers of rice and pastry
6. Make the last layer of egg
7. Cook over two fires
8. When cooked, break into small pieces and place in syrup
