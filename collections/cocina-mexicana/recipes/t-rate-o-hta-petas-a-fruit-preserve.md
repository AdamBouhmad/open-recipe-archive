## Ingredients

- Fruit
- Cold water
- White solid sugar syrup well clarified

## Directions

1. Carefully remove the thin skin and place in cold water
2. Drain and place in the sugar syrup
3. Remove the seeds that come loose with the boiling using a metal spoon and remove from heat as soon as preserved
