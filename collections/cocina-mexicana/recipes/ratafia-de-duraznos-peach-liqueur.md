## Ingredients

- sufficient quantity of good ripe peaches
- four quartillos of juice
- almonds cracked and cleaned of their shells
- twelve quartillos of common aguardiente
- two pounds of sugar

## Directions

1. Choose a sufficient quantity of good ripe peaches, remove the pits and crush them
2. Wrap in cloth, place in the press and extract four quartillos of juice
3. Add the cracked and cleaned almonds
4. Leave in infusion for forty to sixty days in the warmth of the atmosphere in an areniscous stone jug or in a narrow-necked carboy or bottle, having mixed with twelve quartillos of common aguardiente and dissolved two pounds of sugar
5. After this time decant the liquor
6. When the sugar is well dissolved, filter again and bottle
