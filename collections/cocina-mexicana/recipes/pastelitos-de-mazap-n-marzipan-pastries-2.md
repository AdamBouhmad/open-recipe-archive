## Ingredients

- One pound almonds
- One and one half pounds sugar
- Marzipan
- Mint
- Fresh cheese
- Egg yolks
- Egg whites
- Rosewater
- White sugar, finely sifted and ground

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of almonds and one and one half pounds of sugar
2. Mix this with marzipan, mint, fresh cheese and egg yolks
3. After everything is well mashed, form the pastries in the manner of quesadillas
4. Bake on a board on a wafer in a low oven
5. Glaze by brushing with the following icing using a feather
6. Beat egg whites with rosewater, mixing in white sugar that has been very well sifted and ground, and a little of the remaining ingredients
