## Ingredients

- robalo, well desalted
- lard
- oil
- onion
- vinegar
- pepper
- thyme
- fresh parsley
- chiles in vinegar
- capers

## Directions

1. Desalt the robalo well, divide into portions, dry with a cloth, fry in lard and oil
2. Slice a good amount of onion and fry in the same oil in which the fish was fried, taking care that the onion is cooked not golden
3. Add more oil, a little vinegar, a pinch of pepper and a little thyme
4. Place the fish to give a small boil, place on a platter and decorate with fresh parsley, chiles in vinegar, and capers
