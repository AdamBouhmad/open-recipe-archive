## Ingredients

- dried ancho chiles
- garlic cloves, roasted or raw
- cumin seeds
- bread crumb soaked in vinegar
- lard
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- aged cheese, cut into small pieces
- onions, cooked
- nopal paddles, cleaned and cooked
- dried oregano

## Directions

1. Soak the ancho chiles and grind them with roasted or raw garlic cloves, a few cumin seeds, and a bread crumb soaked in vinegar.
2. Fry this mixture in lard, and while frying, add sufficient water, salt, a little vinegar, small pieces of aged cheese, and onions cooked and cut into quarters.
3. Add the cleaned nopal paddles that have been cooked with charcoal and cut as described in previous articles, and let them simmer.
4. If desired, sprinkle dried oregano, crumbled between the hands, over the top.
