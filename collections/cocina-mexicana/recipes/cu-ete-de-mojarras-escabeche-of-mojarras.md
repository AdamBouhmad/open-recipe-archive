## Ingredients

- mojarras fish, cleaned and without scales
- garlic cloves, peeled
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- good vinegar
- ground pepper
- cloves
- bay leaves
- thyme
- Chinese oregano
- two orange leaves
- limes
- lemon peels and rinds
- oil

## Directions

1. Clean the mojarras and remove scales, then dry them
2. Fry peeled garlic cloves in oil until golden
3. Coat the mojarras in flour, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and fry in the oil after removing the garlic until they are golden
4. Drain well from the oil and let cool
5. Grind the golden garlic and dissolve it in plenty of good vinegar, adding a little ground pepper and cloves
6. Heat the vinegar with bay leaves, plenty of thyme, Chinese oregano, about two orange leaves, slices of limes and lemon peels and rinds
7. After one or two boils, heat oil and fry the vinegar with all that has been added, then set aside
8. Layer in the barrel: one layer of mojarras, another of oregano, raw thyme and some bay leaves; add over it part of the prepared mixture cooked in vinegar and some of the broth
9. Continue layering until the barrel is full, filling well with raw oil and covering it to use after eight days
