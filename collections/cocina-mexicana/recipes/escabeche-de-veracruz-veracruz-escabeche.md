## Ingredients

- fish
- lard
- water and vinegar in equal parts
- salt
- toasted cilantro, ground
- cinnamon
- pepper
- bay leaves
- oil
- Seville olives
- chopped onion
- lemon halves (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the fish and cut it into slices while raw
2. Fry in lard until well cooked and golden
3. Boil water and vinegar in equal parts with salt
4. Add ground toasted cilantro, cinnamon, and pepper in proportional quantities to the broth
5. Add bay leaves and bring to a boil in a cooking pot
6. When the fish is cold, arrange the slices in a barrel or pot
7. Sprinkle well with ground spices of the same kind added to the broth
8. Pour the warm broth over it
9. To each layer of fish add broth, bay leaf, spices, and a small cup of oil
10. When serving, add Seville olives and chopped onion
11. Optionally add lemon halves before sealing the pot
