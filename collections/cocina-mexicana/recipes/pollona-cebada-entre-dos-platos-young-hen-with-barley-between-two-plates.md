## Ingredients

- one young hen, passed through flame, emptied and trussed
- a piece of butter
- salt
- pepper
- parsley
- onions
- mushrooms
- garlic
- slices of beef
- strips of ham
- agraz juice

## Directions

1. Pass the young hen through flame, empty and truss it
2. Parboil it in a pot with a piece of butter, salt, pepper, parsley, onions, mushrooms, and garlic, all chopped
3. Line another pot with slices of beef
4. Place the hen with its seasoning on top
5. Cover with strips of ham
6. Cook over hot coals
7. When cooked, degrease the sauce, pass through a sieve, mix with a spoonful of strained broth and a splash of agraz juice
8. Ensure the mixture tastes good
9. Pour over the hen and cover with another plate to serve
