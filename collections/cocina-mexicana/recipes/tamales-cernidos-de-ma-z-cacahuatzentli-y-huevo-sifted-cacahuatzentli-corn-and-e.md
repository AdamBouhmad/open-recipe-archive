## Ingredients

- three pounds of cacahuatzentli corn, cooked and sifted
- half pound of melted lard
- eight egg yolks
- sugar as needed
- a little warm water
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- pieces of walnut
- sesame

## Directions

1. Mix for the mass three pounds of cacahuatzentli corn cooked and sifted as indicated in the previous articles, half a pound of melted lard, eight egg yolks, the necessary sugar according to whether you want them more or less sweet, and a little warm water
2. The mass should be neither watery nor thick, and beat it until it makes foam
3. Put inside the tamales plenty of cinnamon, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, pieces of walnut and sesame
4. Form and cook them the same as the others
