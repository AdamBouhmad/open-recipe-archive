## Ingredients

- Tomato
- Onion
- Olive oil
- Butter
- Olives
- Cumin
- Bobo fish
- Breadcrumbs
- Capers

## Directions

1. Mince tomato and onion and put in a pot with olive oil and butter to fry
2. When almost fried, add split olives and cumin to finish frying
3. Then add the bobo, which is sprinkled with breadcrumbs and ground
4. Let the bobo fry well, and when brought to the table add olive oil, olives and capers
