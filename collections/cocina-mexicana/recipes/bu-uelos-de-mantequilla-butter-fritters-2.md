## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- anise water
- butter
- salt
- yeast
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. To half a pound of flour use eight or ten eggs with whites and yolks; add anise water, butter and a pinch of salt; very little yeast that is kneaded well until it forms air pockets
2. Extend it on a cloth with a rolling pin, wrap it like a cigar with plenty of butter spread on the table
3. Put to hang and form tubes in napkins; once they are formed, cut with a knife into little pieces and fry pieces standing upright, stirring with a stick so they form little balls
4. Place on plates and add syrup with cinnamon and orange blossom water with the syrup
