## Ingredients

- Dried chipotles
- Mulato chiles
- Pasilla chiles
- Jitomates
- Lard
- Pork broth
- Tortillas
- Scrambled eggs
- Fried pork meat
- Chorizos
- Longaniza
- Aged cheese

## Directions

1. Take the chipotles, burn them.
2. Also take mulato and pasilla chiles in equal amounts to the chipotle used.
3. Toast these two kinds of chile and then soak them.
4. Grind all three types of chile.
5. Roast sufficient jitomates and grind them.
6. Put a pot with a little lard on the heat, and once it is burned, add the chile and fry it well.
7. Then incorporate the jitomate and add the pork broth until it is well thickened and season with salt to taste.
8. Take a frying pan, add lard until it burns, and dip the tortillas one by one.
9. Remove them one by one and dip them in the chile.
10. Fill them with already prepared scrambled eggs and place them on a plate, stacking them.
11. Finally, cover them completely with the chile sauce.
12. Garnish with fried pork meat, chorizos, longaniza, and aged cheese on top.
