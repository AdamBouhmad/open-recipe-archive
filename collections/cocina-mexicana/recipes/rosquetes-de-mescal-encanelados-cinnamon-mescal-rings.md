## Ingredients

- Flour (one cup per six egg yolks)
- Six egg yolks per cup of flour
- Mescal or refined brandy
- White tequesquite powder
- Lard or butter for frying
- Sugar and cinnamon for coating

## Directions

1. For each cup of cake flour, add six egg yolks and mix
2. Gradually incorporate a small glass of mescal or refined brandy
3. Add the amount of white tequesquite powder that can be taken with three fingers
4. Knead everything very well
5. Beat the dough until it reaches a point that is neither too hard nor too soft
6. Form the rosquetes
7. Let them air dry a little
8. Fry in lard or butter until golden
9. Drain and roll in sugar and cinnamon
