## Ingredients

- Young hare loins
- Salt
- Pepper
- Melted butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the young hare loins in the form of ribs
2. Boil the skeleton or front frame of the same animal to extract the ribs
3. Place each rib, well cleaned, in each prepared piece of loin so that they have exactly the shape of ribs
4. Dust with salt and pepper
5. Dip in melted butter
6. Roll in breadcrumbs
7. Cook on the grill
8. Serve with tomato sauce underneath
