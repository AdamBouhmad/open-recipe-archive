## Ingredients

- potato
- bread
- oil or lard
- sliced garlic
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- raw onion, sliced
- oregano
- pepper powder
- aged cheese, crumbled
- hard-boiled eggs, sliced

## Directions

1. After the potatoes are cooked, slice them, roll them in bread crumbs, and fry them in oil or lard where sliced garlic has browned, which should then be removed.
2. Once placed on the platter, add salt, oil, vinegar, raw sliced onion, oregano, pepper powder, crumbled aged cheese, and also sliced hard-boiled eggs.
