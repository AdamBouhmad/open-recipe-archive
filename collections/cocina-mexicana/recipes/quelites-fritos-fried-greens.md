## Ingredients

- quelites (wild greens)
- tequesquite
- lard
- ripe tomatoes
- onion
- garlic
- chile strips
- salt
- bread fried in lard

## Directions

1. After removing the roots and washing the quelites, cook with a little tequesquite
2. Heat a clay pot with lard over the fire and fry ripe tomatoes cut coarsely, onions, and garlic
3. Add the quelites after washing them with a little water and some chile strips
4. Season with salt
5. When well fried, serve with slices of bread fried in lard
