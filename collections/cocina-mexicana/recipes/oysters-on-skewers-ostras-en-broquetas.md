## Ingredients

- oysters
- appropriate sauce
- mushrooms
- breadcrumbs
- butter
- beaten and seasoned eggs

## Directions

1. Parboil the oysters in their water
2. Place them in an appropriate sauce mixed with mushrooms
3. Thread them on skewers, alternating one oyster and one mushroom
4. Coat twice with breadcrumbs, the first time with butter and the second with beaten and seasoned eggs
5. Fry and serve
