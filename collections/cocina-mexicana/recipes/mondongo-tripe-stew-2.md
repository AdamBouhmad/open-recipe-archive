## Ingredients

- tripe, cleaned and washed
- vinegar
- lemon
- salt
- bay leaf
- corn, cut in wheels
- thyme
- parsley, finely chopped
- bread crumbs, toasted
- garlic
- bacon
- spices
- chile

## Directions

1. After cleaning well and washing with two or three waters, drain and leave in a marinade composed of vinegar, lemon, salt and a bay leaf for a couple of hours, then remove and drain
2. Cut into pieces and cook with corn cut into wheels, thyme and finely chopped parsley
3. When almost cooked, make a sauce with toasted bread crumbs, garlic, bacon, spices and chile
4. Finish cooking in a pot, stirring well and serve
