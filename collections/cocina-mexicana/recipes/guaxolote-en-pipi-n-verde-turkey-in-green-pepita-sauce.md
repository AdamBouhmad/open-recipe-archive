## Ingredients

- green chile
- a little tomato
- pumpkin seeds
- necessary broth
- cooked and shredded turkey
- pork meat, optional
- salt
- sprigs of green cilantro

## Directions

1. Grind green chile with a little tomato and fry it well
2. Dissolve the pumpkin seeds, prepared the same way as in the previous article, in a little broth and fry it also
3. Add the necessary broth, the cooked and shredded turkey
4. If desired, add pork meat
5. A little before finishing, season with appropriate salt and sprigs of green cilantro
6. When it thickens enough, finish cooking
