## Ingredients

- Chilpotle
- Raw jitomates (tomatoes)
- Lard
- Water
- Salt
- Epazote
- Tortillas, toasted and ground
- Chicken or other fowl
- Pork meat
- Broth
- Hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Toast chilpotle letting it get well roasted, remove veins and wash very well, changing water several times if you want it not to be spicy
2. Once well soaked, grind it together with raw jitomates, fry in lard and add water, salt, some sprigs of epazote, and some well-toasted and ground tortillas
3. Then add chicken or the fowl desired, fried raw
4. Once cooked, remove and fill with the following filling: fry pork meat, put it to boil in water with salt
5. When cooked, remove the water and fry with lard, adding mole broth and some hard-boiled eggs with the yolks whole and the whites chopped
6. When the filling is dry, fill the fowl and place on platters, covering them with thick mole broth
