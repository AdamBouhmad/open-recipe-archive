## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs (optional)
- water
- butter
- rabbit or beef
- salt
- pepper
- thyme
- bay leaf
- parsley
- onions

## Directions

1. Place flour on a table and make a hollow in the center
2. Break two eggs in the hollow, or omit this step if desired
3. Add a glass of water and a piece of butter
4. Gather the flour to form dough and flatten it three times
5. Cut a piece of dough and press and flatten it to a thickness of three lines
6. Cut one or several strips from this dough
7. Moisten the edges of a deep plate and fit the dough strips on top
8. Place a rabbit divided into portions on the plate, already prepared
9. Season with salt, pepper, thyme, bay leaf, chopped parsley and onions
10. Add a piece of butter or good fat
11. Form a lid with the remaining dough
12. Brush with a brush the edges that will receive the lid
13. Place the lid on top, pressing with fingers on the edges so they stick
14. Brush with a brush dipped in egg
15. Place in the oven and bake for about one hour
16. Instead of rabbit, slices of beef or cow meat may be used
