## Ingredients

- pork loin
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- water
- oil
- oregano
- ground pepper
- raw ham
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- salt
- thyme
- bay leaf
- Castilian vinegar
- Jerez wine
- lettuce
- radishes
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Slice the loin into thin slices less than a finger wide.
2. Chop much parsley, onion and garlic, place in a pot of water.
3. Add oil, oregano, ground pepper and raw ham; fry all this together with the loin.
4. Prepare roasted and ground tomatoes, fry in the same oil in which the meat and seasonings were fried.
5. Add water with little salt, oregano, thyme, crumbled bay leaf, and Castilian vinegar.
6. After it comes to a boil, add half a cuartillo of Jerez wine; if it thickens too much, add more wine but not water as it will break.
7. Garnish with lettuce, radishes, chiles and olives.
