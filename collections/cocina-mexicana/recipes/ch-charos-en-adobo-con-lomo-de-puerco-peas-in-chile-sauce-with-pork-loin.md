## Ingredients

- Pork loin
- Dried ancho chiles
- Fried bread in lard
- Garlic
- Cumin
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Peas
- Small sausages (chericitos)

## Directions

1. Remove the veins from dried ancho chiles and soak them
2. Grind the soaked chiles with fried bread in lard, garlic, and cumin
3. Fry everything and season with salt
4. Add vinegar and the broth in which the pork loin was cooked
5. Add the cooked pork loin, peas, and small sausages that have been cooked beforehand
6. Let it boil until the sauce reaches a regular consistency
