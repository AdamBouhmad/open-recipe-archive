## Ingredients

- quinces without skin or core
- two pounds of quince
- three pounds of crushed sugar

## Directions

1. Cook quinces without skin or core, grind and sift through a horsehair sieve
2. Take two pounds of this and add three pounds of crushed sugar in a pot and place over fire until it reaches the point of coming away in water
3. Remove from fire and beat without getting lumpy
4. Pour warm into pots
5. Peaches and apples are made the same way, but peaches are ground well and to each pound and a half add one pound of sugar, and the point at which it comes away from the pot and does not fall from the spoon
