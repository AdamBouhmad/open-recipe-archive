## Ingredients

- young rabbit members
- white wine
- broth
- mixed herbs bundle
- salt
- coarse pepper
- egg
- breadcrumbs
- fat

## Directions

1. Divide the young rabbit into members and cook with half a quart of white wine, broth, a mixed herb bundle, salt and coarse pepper
2. Let the sauce reduce so it adheres to the meat and allow it to cool
3. Thread the pieces of young rabbit onto small wooden skewers
4. Coat with beaten egg, bread, moisten in fat to bread a second time
5. Put on the grill until they take on a beautiful golden color
6. Serve in the same skewers
