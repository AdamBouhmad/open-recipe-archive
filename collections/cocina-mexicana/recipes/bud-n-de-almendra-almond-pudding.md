## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- ten egg yolks
- four cloves
- twenty bitter almonds
- half pound of ground sugar
- butter
- sponge cake

## Directions

1. Beat the almonds with ten egg yolks and four cloves, each thing separately
2. Add the almonds with twenty bitter almonds, all well ground, half pound of ground sugar
3. Mix this and beat for more than a quarter hour
4. Have a casserole spread with butter with a bit of sponge cake
5. Pour the beaten mixture there and place in the oven with low heat, and if you want it to brown, place a paper on top
6. To know when it is cooked, insert a straw and when it comes out clean it is done
7. Turn out onto a plate and sprinkle sugar on top
