## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- butter
- salt
- sugar
- fat

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour, two eggs minus one white, two butters, a little salt, ground sugar and a little fat; knead this well as for pastry
2. With a rolling pin extend it and with a small glass cut into rounds and then turn the glass over at the base
3. Cut so they remain like butter biscuits
4. Then fry in fat and place on a serving plate, add point syrup and cinnamon in powder
