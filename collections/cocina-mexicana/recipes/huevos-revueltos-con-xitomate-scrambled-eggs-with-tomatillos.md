## Ingredients

- Butter
- Garlic (minced)
- Onion (minced)
- Parsley (minced)
- Tomatillos (raw, minced)
- Eggs
- Fine salt
- Chorizo (sliced and fried)
- Longaniza (sliced and fried)
- Ham (sliced and fried)
- Pork cracklings (small pieces)
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. In a pan with butter on the fire, fry minced garlic, onion, parsley, and raw tomatillo, all finely chopped
2. Add the beaten eggs seasoned with fine salt
3. Stir much so they do not join together
4. If desired, add slices of fried chorizo, longaniza, and ham, and also small pieces of pork cracklings
5. When removing from heat, sprinkle with salt and pepper
6. These eggs make a very good filling for roasted birds, fish, pastries, or empanadas
