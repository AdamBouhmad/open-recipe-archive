## Ingredients

- doves
- tomato
- garlic
- onion
- toasted bread
- butter
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- thyme
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- water
- cooked chickpeas

## Directions

1. Clean and wash the doves
2. Fry chopped tomato, garlic, and onion
3. When half fried, add the doves to parboil
4. Add bread toasted in butter, clove, cinnamon, and a small amount of pepper, all ground
5. Add thyme, bay leaf, and marjoram
6. Add enough water for cooking
7. When cooked and reduced to a regular thickness, add cooked chickpeas to serve
