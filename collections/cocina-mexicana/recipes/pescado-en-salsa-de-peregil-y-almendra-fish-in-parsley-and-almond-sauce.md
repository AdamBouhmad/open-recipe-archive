## Ingredients

- parsley
- green chile
- garlic
- almonds
- toasted bread
- lard
- fish broth
- cloves
- pepper
- cinnamon
- salt
- oil
- vinegar
- capers
- olives
- tornachiles

## Directions

1. Chop plenty of parsley, green chile, and garlic finely
2. Grind almonds with toasted bread in lard
3. Fry the garlic first
4. When golden, add the rest of the mixture
5. When fried, add the bread with almonds
6. Add a little unsalted fish broth
7. Grind cloves, pepper, and cinnamon and add to the broth and season with salt
8. When thickened, add oil, vinegar, capers, olives, and tornachiles
9. Bathe the fish with this sauce
