## Ingredients

- almond cake
- egg yolks
- butter
- syrup at medium point
- pine nuts
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut the almond cake into slices
2. Coat with beaten egg yolks and butter
3. Place on plates
4. Bathe with syrup at medium point
5. Decorate with pine nuts and dust with cinnamon
