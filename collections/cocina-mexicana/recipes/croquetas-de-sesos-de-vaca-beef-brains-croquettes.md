## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- truffles
- a little white wine
- German sauce
- curly parsley sauce
- a little butter
- parsley
- lemon juice
- beef brains
- bread crumbs
- grated cheese
- melted butter

## Directions

1. Put mushrooms, truffles, and a little white wine in a saucepan
2. Let everything reduce to the consistency of aspic
3. Add some spoonfuls of German sauce, a little curly parsley sauce, a little butter, parsley, and lemon juice
4. When this broth is boiling, add the brains, divided into ten portions each
5. When everything is well mixed, fill shells with the mixture
6. Dust with bread crumbs and grated cheese
7. Lightly sprinkle with melted butter
8. Place under a bell oven until perfectly golden
