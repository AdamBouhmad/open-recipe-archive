## Ingredients

- red chiles
- bread
- lard
- roasted and cleaned almonds
- melon seeds
- broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Devein red chiles and toast them well in lard
2. Toast a piece of bread and grind both together well
3. Grind separately a handful of roasted and cleaned almonds and another of melon seeds
4. Thin this in a little broth and strain everything
5. Fry the ground chile with bread, and when well fried, add the strained broth of almonds and melon seeds
6. Let it boil, and when it has thickened, season with necessary salt
7. Remove from heat and pour this pipián over the roasted poultry
