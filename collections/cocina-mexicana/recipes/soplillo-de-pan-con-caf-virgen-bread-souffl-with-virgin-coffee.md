## Ingredients

- three cuartillos of milk
- six ounces of sugar
- four ounces of coffee, somewhat broken up
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Bring three cuartillos of milk to boil with six ounces of sugar
2. Toast four ounces of somewhat broken up coffee
3. Upon removing the coffee from the pan in which it was toasted, immediately add it to the milk, covering the pot with a lid
4. Strain immediately through a strainer over bread crumbs
5. Let cool and proceed as explained in the previous article
