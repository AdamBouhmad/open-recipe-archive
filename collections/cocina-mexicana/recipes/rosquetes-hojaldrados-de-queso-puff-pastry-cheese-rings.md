## Ingredients

- One pound flour
- Two eggs
- One tablespoon melted and cooled lard
- Half pound fresh unsalted cheese, grated
- A little white tequesquite water settled
- Water as needed
- Butter

## Directions

1. Make a pile on a table with one pound of flour and make a hollow in the middle, where you add two eggs, one tablespoon of melted and cooled lard, half a pound of fresh unsalted cheese grated, a little white tequesquite water settled, and the water necessary to bind the flour, which should remain soft
2. Dust the table with flour and on it extend the dough with the rolling pin as much as possible
3. Spread with butter, fold and spread again, extend a second time with the rolling pin, and repeat this operation two or three times, leaving it in the last fold the thickness of a coin's edge
4. Cut or remove the rosquetes in the same manner as the previous article
5. Fry them in lard
6. Arrange them on a plate
