## Ingredients

- Ten egg yolks
- One pound clarified syrup
- Medium-point consistency
- Biscuit cakes (biscotelas)
- Syrup for sprinkling
- Ground cinnamon
- Egg whites

## Directions

1. Thoroughly beat ten egg yolks and strain through a cloth
2. Mix into one pound of clarified syrup when cold and at medium-point
3. Place over heat to reach a paste-like consistency
4. Pour over sponge cake sprinkled with syrup
5. Dust with ground cinnamon
6. Coat the top layer with egg whites
