## Ingredients

- brains
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- flour

## Directions

1. Remove the membrane from the brains and crumble them by hand.
2. Mix with fine salt and pepper.
3. Coat a pan with butter and add the mixture.
4. Place the pan in the heat of a water bath and do not move it at all until a sort of cake forms.
5. In a large pan, heat flour until it becomes reddish and add the mixture to finish cooking.
