## Ingredients

- old partridge
- fifty superior chestnuts
- good broth
- bread

## Directions

1. Roast an old recently killed partridge
2. Roast and clean fifty superior chestnuts well
3. Cook the chestnuts in good broth
4. Remove the skin from the partridge and bone it
5. Grind the partridge meat thoroughly in a mortar
6. Drain the chestnuts and add them to the mortar with the ground partridge meat
7. Grind and mix both together
8. Press through a sieve to strain
9. Boil bread soup over gentle heat
10. Mix with the paste and proceed as for lentil substance soup
