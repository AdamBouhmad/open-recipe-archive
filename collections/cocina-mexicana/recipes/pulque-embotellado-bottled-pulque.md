## Ingredients

- fresh pulque
- aguamiel
- sugar

## Directions

1. Bottle the fresh pulque as it comes, adding half a pozuelo of aguamiel
2. Seal with a well-fitted stopper
3. Cover the bottle opening with a piece of badana leather tied securely
4. After twenty-four hours you will have a pulque as strong and flavorful as wine
5. If aguamiel is not available, substitute with a small piece of sugar placed inside the bottle to ferment
