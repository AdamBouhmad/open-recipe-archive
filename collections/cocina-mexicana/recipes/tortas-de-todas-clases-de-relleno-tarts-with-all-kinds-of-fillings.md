## Ingredients

- Half pound or twelve ounces of meat (beef or game), without bones or fibers
- An equal amount of good beef fat
- Parsley
- Onion
- Mushrooms
- Fine salt
- A little mixed spices
- Two eggs
- A little flour
- Ham slices
- A good red substance or a stew of crests, mushrooms, or beef tenderloin

## Directions

1. Chop the meat well and grind it with an equal amount of good beef fat, parsley, onion and mushrooms, all chopped very fine, and season with fine salt and a little mixed spices
2. When everything is well mixed, add two eggs and moisten in a mortar, adding a few drops of water from time to time
3. Line a tart pan with a sheet of dough and garnish with balls of filling, rolling them on a table with a little flour
4. Cover with ham slices and with another sheet of dough on top, as indicated in the previous articles, and add also a good red substance or some stew of crests, mushrooms, beef tenderloin, etc.
