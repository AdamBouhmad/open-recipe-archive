## Ingredients

- artichokes
- fried bacon
- salt
- parsley
- green garlic
- pepper

## Directions

1. After cleaning and removing the outer leaves, trim the tips and cut through the stem to introduce a bit of fried bacon mixed with salt, parsley, green garlic and a bit of pepper
2. Place them in a cazuela between two fires, turning them, and although on the outside they appear hard, on the inside they will be tender and tasty
