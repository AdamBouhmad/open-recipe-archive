## Ingredients

- Solid veal
- Vinegar or lemon juice
- Lard
- Water
- Salt
- Capers
- Garlic cloves
- Bread
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Take a piece of solid veal and soak it in vinegar or lemon juice for a while.
2. Parboil it in lard.
3. Once browned, add water and salt.
4. Crush capers, garlic cloves, and a piece of bread soaked in vinegar together and add to the meat.
5. Cook over heat until it dries.
