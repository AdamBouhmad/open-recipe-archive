## Ingredients

- Beef rib prepared for braising
- One quarter Málaga wine
- One quarter broth
- Ham for larding, heavily seasoned with spices
- Thirty onions

## Directions

1. Pour one quarter Málaga wine and one quarter broth over the rib prepared for braising with ham larding heavily seasoned with spices
2. When half cooked, add thirty onions
3. When the beef is well cooked, remove it and carefully dry it of fat
4. Arrange on the serving plate with onions around it
5. Pour a good sauce of beautiful color over it
