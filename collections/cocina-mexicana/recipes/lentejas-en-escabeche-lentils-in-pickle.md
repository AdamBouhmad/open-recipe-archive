## Ingredients

- lentils
- salt
- lard
- garlic

## Directions

1. Soak the lentils from the day before
2. On the day they are to be cooked, put them to cook with salt
3. When cooked, drain them
4. Place a pot with lard on the heat and fry clean peeled and chopped garlic until browned
5. Add two or three onion heads (recipe continues beyond visible text)
