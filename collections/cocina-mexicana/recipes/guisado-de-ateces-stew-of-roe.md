## Ingredients

- fish roe
- biscuit bread
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- oil
- vinegar
- chiles
- onion
- olives

## Directions

1. Wash the roe and slice it
2. Grind a piece of biscuit bread with a bit of salt and pepper and roll the slices in this powder
3. Fry them in lard over low heat
4. Place them on a platter with oil and vinegar and decorate with chiles, onion and olives
