## Ingredients

- one goose
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- dried wide chiles, deveined and soaked
- a little ground cumin
- some grated nutmeg

## Directions

1. After the goose is half-cooked in salted water, dust it with more salt and pepper, wrap it in papers well coated with lard and place it on the grill
2. Let it roast, turning it frequently, and when it is tender, remove the paper
3. Grind some wide dried chiles that have been deveined and soaked along with a little ground cumin and some grated nutmeg
4. Prepare the goose with this mixture, tie it with thread and serve
