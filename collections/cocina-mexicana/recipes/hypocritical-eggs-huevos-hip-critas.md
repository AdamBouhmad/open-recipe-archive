## Ingredients

- thin slices of ham
- butter
- fine salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut some thin slices of ham
2. Fry them in butter, and when they are cooked, arrange them at the bottom of a plate and pour the butter in which they cooked over them
3. Break and place eggs on top
4. Dust with fine salt, pepper and nutmeg
5. Put on very low heat to set, and when they are set, take them to the table
