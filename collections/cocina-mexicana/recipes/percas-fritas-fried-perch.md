## Ingredients

- perch
- water
- salt
- oil
- onions
- parsley
- lemon juice
- flour

## Directions

1. Scald the perch
2. Empty, remove gills, and wash
3. Make incisions on the sides
4. Marinate with salt, a little oil, onions, parsley, and lemon juice
5. Drain
6. Roll in flour
7. Fry until golden brown
