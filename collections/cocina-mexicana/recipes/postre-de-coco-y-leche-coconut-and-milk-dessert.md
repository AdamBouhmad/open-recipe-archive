## Ingredients

- 2 coconuts
- 10 egg yolks
- 2 quarter-measures of milk
- little or no sugar
- sugar beads
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make a preserve brought to a high point with two coconuts
2. Beat ten egg yolks until they are ready for confections, and cook two quarter-measures of milk as for atole, without sugar or with very little, until it has consumed a third part or a little more
3. Keep these three things separate until the next day, when you mix the yolks with the preserve, and then this with the milk, emptying everything into a silver platter that will be put over two fires, and it will be decorated with sugar beads and cinnamon
