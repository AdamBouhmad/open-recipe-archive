## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- tench
- mushrooms
- salt
- pepper
- onions
- cloves
- caldo
- white wine
- red substance
- asparagus
- artichokes

## Directions

1. Brown flour in hot butter and fry the pieces of tench there
2. Add mushrooms, salt, pepper, and a small bundle of onions studded with cloves
3. Moisten everything with equal parts broth and boiled white wine
4. Bind with a little red substance
5. Asparagus and artichokes may be mixed in the seasoning after being parboiled
