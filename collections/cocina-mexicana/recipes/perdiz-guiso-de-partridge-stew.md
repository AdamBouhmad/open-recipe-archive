## Ingredients

- 1 partridge
- 3 equal parts water
- 3 equal parts vinegar
- 3 equal parts oil
- 1 large onion
- 2 or 3 bay leaves
- 6 grains black pepper

## Directions

1. Cover the pot with a casserole filled with water
2. Bring to a boil over low heat until the partridge is cooked
