## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- custard (natillas)
- sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare as in the English-style spinach, using slightly less butter
2. When cooking, moisten with custard
3. When removing from heat, add more custard and sprinkle with sugar
