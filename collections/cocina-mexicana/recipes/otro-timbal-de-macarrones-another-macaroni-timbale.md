## Ingredients

- half a pound of macaroni
- one ounce of lard
- salt and pepper
- water
- half a pound of Gruyere cheese, grated
- one ounce of butter

## Directions

1. Put half a pound of macaroni in a suitable pan with one ounce of lard, salt, pepper, and sufficient water to cover it
2. When the water is consumed, grate half a pound of Gruyere cheese and add it to the macaroni with one ounce of butter
3. Beat until the cheese is well incorporated
