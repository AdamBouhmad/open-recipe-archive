## Ingredients

- one pound clean and ground almonds
- one and a half pounds molded sugar
- two roasted hen breasts
- three cups of natillas (custard)
- some molded cinnamon
- a piece of butter
- a piece of mamón (sponge cake) crumbled
- eggs as needed

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of clean and ground almonds with one and a half pounds of molded sugar, two roasted hen breasts, three cups of natillas, some molded cinnamon, a piece of butter, a crumbled piece of mamón, and the eggs necessary for everything to rise
2. Put in a pan or casserole with butter and let it set over two fires
