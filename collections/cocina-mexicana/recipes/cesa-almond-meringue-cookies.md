## Ingredients

- six egg whites
- four ounces powdered sugar
- four ounces sweet almonds, reduced to paste
- fine sugar for dusting
- whipped cream or preserves for filling

## Directions

1. Beat six egg whites with four ounces of powdered sugar and evaporate them over hot coals, stirring continuously.
2. Add four ounces of sweet almonds reduced to paste and mix everything together.
3. Form the meringue into round or oval shapes the size of a spoon, leaving them hollow inside.
4. Dust with fine sugar and place in the oven.
5. When they have risen, remove them, split them in half, fill with whipped cream or preserves, and cover again.
