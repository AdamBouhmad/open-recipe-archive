## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- six eggs
- three eggs without whites
- one small cup of tequesquite water
- juice of half a sweet orange
- a little wine
- lard for frying
- syrup

## Directions

1. Mix two pounds of flour, six eggs (three of which without whites), one small cup of tequesquite water, juice of half a sweet orange, and a little wine.
2. Knead until the mixture bubbles (until it thunders).
3. Form a ball, wrap in a napkin, place briefly in the sun, then extend with a palote.
4. Cut long, wide strips using a carretilla (pastry wheel).
5. Fry in lard that is not very hot so they remain white.
6. Coat with syrup.
