## Ingredients

- Green chiles of the season
- Oil
- Butter
- Garlic cloves, ground
- A little water
- Oil
- Cooked potatoes

## Directions

1. Remove the veins from green chiles of the season, grind them very fine, fry them in oil and butter adding some ground garlic cloves
2. Once well fried add a little water so it seasons, add more oil on top, and put in the cooked potatoes cut into quarters in this sauce
