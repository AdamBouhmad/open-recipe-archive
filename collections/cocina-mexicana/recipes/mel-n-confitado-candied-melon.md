## Ingredients

- melon pulp
- sugar
- vinegar
- cinnamon
- clove

## Directions

1. After removing the skin from the melon flesh, stud it with cinnamon and clove
2. Preserve the meat with sugar and vinegar, making a much appreciated, very healthy and appetizing compote
3. It is eaten with its syrup and can be preserved for many years
