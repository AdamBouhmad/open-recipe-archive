## Ingredients

- Chicken with its broth
- Lard
- Chopped onion
- Ground parsley
- Toasted bread toasts browned on a griddle or grill
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Capers
- Oil
- Olives

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onion in lard
2. Then fry ground parsley mixed with toasted bread browned on a griddle or grill, clove, pepper, cumin, and salt
3. Once everything is well fried, place the chicken with its broth in the pan
4. Add vinegar, capers, oil, and olives
5. Simmer until cooked
