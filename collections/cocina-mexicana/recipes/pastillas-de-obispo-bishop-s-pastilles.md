## Ingredients

- one pound royal sugar
- four ounces gum arabic
- episcopal spirit
- a little carmine and indigo

## Directions

1. Prepare the sugar and gum as previously described
2. Use episcopal spirit to dissolve the sugar
3. Add a little carmine and indigo to give it a violet color
