## Ingredients

- meat, cut into small pieces
- oil or lard
- chopped onion
- sweet pepper
- garlic
- pepper
- tomatoes, peeled and chopped
- salt
- hot water or pot broth
- bay leaf
- potatoes, cut into thick pieces

## Directions

1. Cut meat into small pieces and sauté in a pan with oil or lard
2. Add chopped onion, sweet pepper, garlic, pepper and tomatoes (after removing skin)
3. Add salt and cook over low heat with its own broth, stirring occasionally
4. Add hot water or pot broth, bay leaf and potatoes cut into thick pieces
5. Let it cook the necessary time
