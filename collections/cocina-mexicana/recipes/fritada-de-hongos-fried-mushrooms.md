## Ingredients

- Mushrooms, cooked
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Parsley
- Chopped onion
- Vinegar
- Eggs

## Directions

1. Put salt, pepper, a little nutmeg, parsley, finely chopped onion, and one or two spoonfuls of vinegar in a pan or casserole.
2. Add the previously cooked mushrooms.
3. Put everything over gentle heat and let it boil for a quarter hour.
4. At the moment of serving, add an egg liaison.
