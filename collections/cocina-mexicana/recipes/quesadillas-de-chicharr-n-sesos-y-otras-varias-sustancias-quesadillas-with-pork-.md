## Ingredients

- thin tortillas
- pork cracklings fat ground
- chile pasilla toasted
- epazote chopped
- salt
- lard
- brains
- ahuautle
- squash flowers

## Directions

1. Form and fill thin tortillas or tortilla dough with ground pork cracklings fat, a bit of toasted pasilla chile, some chopped epazote and necessary salt
2. Fold and seal well when made of dough and fry in lard or place on a comal trying to eat them freshly made
3. These quesadillas can be filled with various substances such as brains, ahuautle, well-chopped squash flowers, and can serve as a light breakfast
