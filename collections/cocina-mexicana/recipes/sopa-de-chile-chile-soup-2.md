## Ingredients

- Lard
- Tender onion slices
- Almost hard bread pieces
- Three to four wide chiles
- Salt

## Directions

1. Put a pot on the fire with lard and let it burn, then fry tender onion slices in it
2. Add pieces of almost hard bread, broken by hand
3. Moisten with a sauce made from three or four wide deveined and washed chiles, ground and dissolved in sufficient water
4. Season with salt and after the soup boils once, remove it, let the covered pot rest, and serve hot
