## Ingredients

- cooked salt cod
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- parsley
- beaten eggs
- fish broth
- oil
- fried garlic
- crushed bread crust
- saffron

## Directions

1. Chop the cooked salt cod and mix with bread crumbs, salt, pepper, crushed garlic, and parsley
2. Add beaten eggs in proportion to achieve regular consistency
3. After mixing well, form balls
4. Wrap in bread crumbs and fry
5. They can be cooked afterwards in fish broth with oil, fried garlic, and a bread crust crushed with parsley, pepper, and a little saffron
6. Pour this mixture over the small meatballs
