## Ingredients

- beef square (cuadrado de vaca)
- fine thin lard
- braising pan
- pieces of lard or ham
- meat scraps
- carrots
- onions
- herb bouquet
- concentrated broth
- gelatin
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare the piece well and remove the sinews and skin covering the loin
2. Lard it with fine thin lard
3. Tie it so it does not lose its shape
4. Place in a braising pan with pieces of lard or ham, meat scraps, carrots, onions, and a mixed herb bouquet
5. Moisten with concentrated broth and let it simmer for three hours over two fires
6. Drain and untie the meat
7. Cover with gelatin and arrange on French or Spanish tomato sauce, or on cucumbers, chicory, or spinach
