## Ingredients

- Two medium cabbages
- Half pound of beef
- One pound of fatty ham
- Salt
- Whole pepper
- Cilantro
- Cumin
- Ginger
- Saffron
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Black pepper
- Grated nutmeg
- Seven or eight egg yolks
- Ham slices
- Parsnips
- Onions
- Mixed herbs
- Broth

## Directions

1. Parboil two medium cabbages for twenty minutes in boiling salted water
2. Refresh in cold water, let drain, and remove the core
3. Chop half a pound of beef and one pound of fatty ham, seasoning with salt, whole pepper, a little cilantro, cumin, ginger, saffron, cinnamon, clove, black pepper, all ground, and grated nutmeg
4. Mix in seven or eight egg yolks and use this mixture to stuff the cabbages, tying them very well
5. Line a cooking vessel with ham slices and add some parsnips, onions, and a mixed herb bundle, placing the cabbages on top and moistening with undefatted broth
6. Cook over low heat for an hour and a half
7. Drain the cabbages afterwards, press them slightly to squeeze out liquid, untie, and serve with Spanish sauce underneath
