## Ingredients

- Six quartillos of fresh milk
- Four ounces of clean orange blossoms
- Four quartillos of rectified brandy
- Four pounds of white sugar, crushed
- Eight quartillos of common water
- Sixteen quartillos of white, clean Champagne wine

## Directions

1. Heat six quartillos of fresh milk in a pot, adding four ounces of clean orange blossoms
2. After one or two boils, pour into an earthenware or porcelain bowl
3. When cool, add four quartillos of rectified brandy, stir well and filter through a bag to separate the blossoms and any skin that has formed
4. Dissolve four pounds of crushed white sugar in eight quartillos of water over heat
5. Cool and mix with sixteen quartillos of Champagne wine
6. Combine with the milk mixture and filter together
