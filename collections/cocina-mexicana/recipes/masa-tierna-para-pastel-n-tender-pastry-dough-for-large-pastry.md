## Ingredients

- Flour
- Lard, butter, or cold oil
- Salt

## Directions

1. Moisten the flour with lard, butter, or cold oil
2. Dissolve the proper amount of salt in a very small amount of water
3. Mix well
4. Form pastries from this dough
