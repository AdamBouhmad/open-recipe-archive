## Ingredients

- eight quartillos of milk
- one quarter arroba of white sugar
- seven ounces of ground rice strained with milk
- cinnamon sticks
- half pound of soaked and ground almonds
- one ounce of starch
- caramel
- gold leaf or cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Sweeten eight quartillos of milk with one quarter arroba of white sugar
2. Mix with seven ounces of rice, ground and strained with milk, and put on the fire to boil, adding some cinnamon sticks and taking care that it does not stick
3. After boiling a little, add half pound of soaked almonds ground with one ounce of starch, having dissolved these in a little milk
4. Let everything boil until it reaches the point where, poured a little onto a plate and blown on to cool quickly, it runs to the side when the plate is tilted, leaving it clean
5. Set aside then, and having removed the cinnamon sticks, pour into the plates in which it is to be served
6. Decorate the milk after cooling with small figures or designs of gold leaf, which look very good under the caramel; but if these golden ornaments are to be omitted, put the caramel without them, making the design with the caramel itself, and putting crushed tablet or cinnamon in the spaces left by the ornaments
