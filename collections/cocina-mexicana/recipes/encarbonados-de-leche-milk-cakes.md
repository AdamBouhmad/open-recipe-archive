## Ingredients

- nixtamal
- milk
- sugar
- nutmeg or chopped almond
- cinnamon sticks
- flour
- butter
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Mix a handful of well-ground nixtamal with four cuartillos of milk and sweeten
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Cook with a little grated nutmeg or chopped almond and some cinnamon sticks, giving it a higher consistency than that of manjar blanco
4. When it reaches the proper consistency, cool it well on a platter
5. Cut small pieces with a knife, removing them carefully so they do not break
6. Place them on another platter dusted with very fine flour
7. Put them in a pan on the fire with well-foamed butter, being careful that they do not fall apart or burn
8. Serve on a platter with ground cinnamon on top
