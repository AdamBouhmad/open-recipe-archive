## Ingredients

- one catfish
- buttered paper
- breadcrumbs
- onion
- garlic
- xitomate (tomato)
- parsley
- bay leaves
- cooking oil
- salt
- clove powder
- pepper powder
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After washing the catfish well, dry it thoroughly.
2. In a casserole place a buttered paper, roll the catfish in breadcrumbs and place it on the paper.
3. Chop onion, garlic, xitomate, plenty of parsley and a few bay leaves; mix everything very well with plenty of oil, and season with the corresponding salt and a powder of clove and pepper in equal quantities.
4. Place at two fires, and when it is well cooked, let it rest in the heat, and when serving pour lemon juice over it.
