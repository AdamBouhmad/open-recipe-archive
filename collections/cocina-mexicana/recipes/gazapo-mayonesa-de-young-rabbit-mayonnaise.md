## Ingredients

- Roasted young rabbit
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Reinforced sauce
- Mayonnaise
- Capers
- Anchovies
- Cucumbers
- Meat gelatin

## Directions

1. Divide a roasted rabbit into pieces
2. While cold, toss and mix with oil, vinegar and reinforced sauce
3. Place on a plate and cover with mayonnaise
4. Garnish with capers, anchovies, cucumbers, etc.
5. Surround everything with a border or ring of meat gelatin
