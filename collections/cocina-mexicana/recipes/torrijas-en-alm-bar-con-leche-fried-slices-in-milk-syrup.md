## Ingredients

- cold sponge cake slices
- beaten eggs
- butter
- sugar
- milk
- sesame seeds
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make syrup with milk instead of water
2. Prepare torrijas with slices of cold sponge cake, coat with beaten egg, and fry in butter
3. Boil the fried slices in the milk syrup
4. Serve sprinkled with toasted sesame seeds and dusted with cinnamon
