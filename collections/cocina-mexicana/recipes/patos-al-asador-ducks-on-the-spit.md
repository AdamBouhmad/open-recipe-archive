## Ingredients

- Ducks, cleaned
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Oil
- Lemon juice
- Water

## Directions

1. Clean the ducks as described in the previous recipe.
2. Place them on the spit sprinkled with salt and pepper.
3. Once cooked, prepare a sauce of oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and water.
4. Baste the ducks inside and out with the sauce and serve.
