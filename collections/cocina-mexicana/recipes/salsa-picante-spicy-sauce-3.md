## Ingredients

- Two or three leeks or wild garlic
- A little butter
- One quarter of a glass of vinegar
- One ladle of Spanish sauce
- Gherkins in vinegar
- Capers in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the leeks or wild garlic very finely
2. Fry them with a little butter
3. Add one quarter of a glass of vinegar and let it reduce
4. Add one ladle of Spanish sauce
5. Let everything cook slowly for one quarter of an hour at the corner of the stove
6. Remove the grease
7. At the time of serving, add finely cut gherkins and capers in vinegar
