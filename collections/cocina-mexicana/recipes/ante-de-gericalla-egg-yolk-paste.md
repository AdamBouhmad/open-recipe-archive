## Ingredients

- Egg yolks
- Syrup of medium point, cold
- Syrup
- Ground pastille

## Directions

1. Beat egg yolks with cold medium-point syrup until very thick
2. Place the layers as in all the antes
3. Sprinkle each one with syrup and ground pastille
