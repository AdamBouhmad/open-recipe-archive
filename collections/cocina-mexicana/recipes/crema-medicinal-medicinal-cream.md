## Ingredients

- two egg yolks
- one ounce of ground sugar
- one tablespoon orange blossom water
- half a cuartillo of very hot water

## Directions

1. Place the egg yolks, ground sugar, and orange blossom water in a regular cup
2. Beat until everything is well incorporated
3. Without stopping beating, add the very hot water
4. This cream is used by the sick as an excellent pectoral remedy
