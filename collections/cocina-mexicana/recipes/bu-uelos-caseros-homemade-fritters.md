## Ingredients

- flour of the finest quality
- water
- salt
- egg whites, beaten
- sugar
- one tablespoon of oil
- one or two tablespoons of orange flower water

## Directions

1. Mix flour of the finest quality with water, salt, beaten egg whites, sugar, one tablespoon of oil and one or two tablespoons of orange flower water
2. Let it sit until it reaches the right consistency
3. Work the dough as much as possible
4. Drop into the hot oil pan in small, thin portions, making a small hole in the middle with your finger
5. The oil must be boiling; when toasted on one side, flip to the other side until it has the same color
6. Serve with sugar or honey on top
