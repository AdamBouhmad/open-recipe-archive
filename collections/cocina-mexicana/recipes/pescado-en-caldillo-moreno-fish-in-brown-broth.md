## Ingredients

- fish, cut into small pieces
- slices of carrots and onions
- garlic
- parsley
- thyme
- basil
- salt
- equal parts water and red wine

## Directions

1. Add sliced carrots and onions, garlic, parsley, thyme, basil, and salt to the cut fish
2. Add equal parts water and red wine
3. Cook together
