## Ingredients

- two pounds flour
- one pound fresh butter
- water

## Directions

1. Mix flour with butter and water, adding water little by little until the dough is sufficiently soft
2. Form balls and flatten them with a roller, dusting the table with flour so they do not stick
3. Give them one inch of thickness
4. Gild with egg
5. Bake in the oven
