## Ingredients

- Garlic cloves
- Cumin
- Water
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Oregano
- Ground pepper
- Fresh fish
- Olives
- Tornachiles
- Capers

## Directions

1. Grind toasted garlic cloves with cumin, dissolve in a little water with salt, and add oil, vinegar, oregano and ground pepper
2. Place the fresh fish there and let it cook over two fires, taking care to turn it from time to time so it cooks evenly
3. When all the water has been consumed, remove from heat and add olives, tornachiles and capers, and stir everything together to serve
