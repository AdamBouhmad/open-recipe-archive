## Ingredients

- spinach
- butter
- milk
- sugar
- lemon zest
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. When the spinach is fried with only the butter, add a little milk, necessary sugar and lemon zest
2. Also make a delicious dish if you add a little orange flower water
