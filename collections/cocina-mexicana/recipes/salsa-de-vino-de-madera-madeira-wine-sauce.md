## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon of flour
- 1 glass of dry Madeira wine
- some tablespoons of concentrated stock
- butter
- salt
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Dissolve the flour in a glass of dry Madeira wine
2. Add some tablespoons of concentrated stock, a little butter, salt, and nutmeg
