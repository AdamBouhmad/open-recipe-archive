## Ingredients

- one pound of coffee
- two quartillos of boiling water
- clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Roast one pound of coffee until it has a golden color
2. Grind it and put it in an earthenware or porcelain vessel
3. Pour two quartillos of boiling water over it
4. Stir well with a wooden spoon or spatula
5. Cover the vessel and keep it until the next morning
6. Decant the liquid
7. Press the grounds or dregs in a press and add the resulting liquid to the other clarified liquid
8. Mix it with clarified syrup in double the quantity of the coffee tincture (two parts syrup to one part coffee water)
9. Put it on the fire until it reaches the breaking point in water
10. Set it aside, let it cool slightly and pour it into bottles
