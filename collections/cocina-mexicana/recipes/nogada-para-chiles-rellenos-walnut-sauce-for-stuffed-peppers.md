## Ingredients

- Fresh walnuts, cleaned and skinned
- A little pepper
- Bread soaked in vinegar
- Good vinegar
- Fine salt
- Cooking oil
- Pomegranate seeds (optional garnish)

## Directions

1. Grind the fresh walnuts that are well cleaned and skinned
2. Add a little pepper and some bread that has been soaked in vinegar to the walnuts
3. Grind everything well together
4. Season with good vinegar
5. Add fine salt and cooking oil
6. Leave the sauce quite thick
7. Warm the peppers and extend them on a platter while hot
8. Cover them with the seasoned nogada sauce
9. If desired, sprinkle pomegranate seeds on top for garnish
10. Note: The nogada will turn dark if salt is added long before serving, so it is better to season it almost when bringing it to the table, or leave it without salt so each person can season it to their taste
