## Ingredients

- Fruit
- Syrup

## Directions

1. Allow the syrup with the fruit to thicken until the fruit begins to stick to the pot
2. Stir with the skimmer within the same syrup until it acquires a golden color
3. Place a plate on the compote, turn the pot upside down, and empty it onto the plate
4. Transfer cleanly to the compote dish, unless it is served on the same plate hot or cold; however, hot compotes are better
