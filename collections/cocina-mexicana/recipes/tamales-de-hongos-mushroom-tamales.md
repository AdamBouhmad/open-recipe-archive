## Ingredients

- masa (as for chile tamales)
- mushrooms
- lard
- fresh chiles
- epazote
- salt
- mulato chiles
- or for alternate filling: mulato chiles, epazote, salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the masa the same as for chile tamales
2. For the filling: cook the mushrooms in a pot
3. Put lard in a casserole and place the drained mushrooms there
4. Slit some fresh chiles, remove veins and place where the mushrooms are with a little deshojado epazote and salt
5. When dry, use this to fill the tamales
6. Alternatively: cook the mushrooms as above, toast eight to ten mulato chiles, remove veins and grind
7. Fry in lard, add a little deshojado epazote and its corresponding salt
8. Should look like the mole of the other chile tamales
9. Add the cooked mushrooms and proceed as with the other tamales
