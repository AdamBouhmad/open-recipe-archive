## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- butter
- bread crumbs
- salt
- pepper
- eggs
- Flanders cheese
- macaroni
- Spanish sauce
- Madeira wine
- roast juice

## Directions

1. Prepare the ribs carefully. Coat them with melted butter and cover with bread crumbs seasoned with salt and pepper.
2. Beat eggs in proportion, yolk and white, and dip the breaded ribs in them, then bread them again with bread crumbs and Flanders cheese. Load this second breading heavily.
3. Melt butter or margarine in a pan and fry the ribs in it until they take on a good color.
4. Place them then on a platter with macaroni and cover everything with Spanish sauce mixed with Madeira wine and a few spoonfuls of roast juice.
