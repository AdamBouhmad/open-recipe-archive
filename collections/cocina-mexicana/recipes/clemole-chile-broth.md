## Ingredients

- Chile ancho, deveined and toasted
- Cooked tomato
- Roasted garlic head
- Toasted tortilla, then soaked
- Pork meat
- Epazote

## Directions

1. Grind chile ancho deveined and toasted, cooked tomato, a roasted garlic head, and a toasted tortilla that has been soaked
2. Fry with pork meat, a sprig of epazote and season
