## Ingredients

- 3 pounds of veal
- clove, cinnamon, ham, and whole garlic, rolled in salt and pepper
- ½ pound of chopped ham
- 3 cloves of garlic
- aromatic herbs and parsley
- 2 small cups of hot water
- broth from the pot, strained
- macaroni
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Soak the veal and lard it with clove, cinnamon, whole ham, and whole garlic rolled in salt and pepper
2. Place the chopped ham, garlic, aromatic herbs, and parsley in the pot
3. Cook the meat over low heat, turning it as well as the ham so it cooks well
4. When the ham is melted, before the meat burns, add two small cups of hot water to the pot
5. As it reduces, add strained broth from the pot and then the macaroni
6. The macaroni should be cooked in boiling water first, then left a while in the broth to thicken slightly
7. Once ready, place in a serving dish and cover with grated cheese
