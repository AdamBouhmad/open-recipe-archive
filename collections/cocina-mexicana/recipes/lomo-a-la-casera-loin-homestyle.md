## Ingredients

- Loin
- Bacon
- Lemon slices
- Onion slices, raw
- Oil
- Salt
- Pepper
- Paper
- Lard
- Broth or water
- White wine
- Carrots
- Onions

## Directions

1. Lard the thickest part of the loin with bacon and spread on a platter
2. Place five or six pieces of lemon and as many slices of raw onion on top
3. Sprinkle generously with oil so the meat becomes well impregnated and the oil coats around it
4. Dust with salt and pepper
5. After a quarter hour turn the meat so it soaks on both sides and season again
6. Remove the meat from the marinade and wrap in paper greased with lard and tied with string tightly
7. Place in a pan to cook with the rest of the marinade
8. When the meat is almost completely cooked, remove the paper wrapper
9. Finish cooking on low heat
10. Remove the meat when completely cooked
11. Add to the pan half a cup of broth or water, half a glass of white wine, some carrots and some onions
12. When serving pour this sauce over the loin with the juice of a lemon
