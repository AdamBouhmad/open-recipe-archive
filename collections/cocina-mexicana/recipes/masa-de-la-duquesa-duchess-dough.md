## Ingredients

- two quarter-pints of cream
- one common spoonful of orange-blossom water
- two ounces of sugar
- four ounces of butter
- a little salt
- flour
- eggs

## Directions

1. Place cream in a pan and bring to a boil
2. Add orange-blossom water, sugar, butter, and salt
3. Mix in flour as described in the article on cooked dough for French marquesote
4. Add eggs little by little in small quantities, stirring the paste with a wooden spoon
5. Leave it as consistent as the cauliflower paste
6. Give it the desired shape, sprinkling flour on the dough and rolling it to make duchess rolls
7. Place on a platform with a spoon
8. Bake in the oven after the puff pastry and glaze with sugar
