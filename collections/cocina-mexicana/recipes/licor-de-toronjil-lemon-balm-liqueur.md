## Ingredients

- Six to eight drops of lemon balm essence
- One quarter liter of alcohol or rectified spirit of wine
- Very clarified simple syrup of high or low concentration according to preference
- Cochineal for coloring

## Directions

1. Add six to eight drops of lemon balm essence to each quarter liter of alcohol or rectified spirit of wine
2. Let it rest one or two days
3. After this time, sweeten with very clarified simple syrup of high or low concentration according to preference
4. Color with cochineal to imitate the color of the lemon balm flower
5. Filter the liquor
6. Bottle it, giving it the name of 'Kiss of Melissa'
