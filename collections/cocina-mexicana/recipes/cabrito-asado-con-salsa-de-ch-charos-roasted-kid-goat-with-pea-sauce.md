## Ingredients

- kid goat
- oil
- salt
- egg yolks
- garlic cloves
- onion, minced
- butter
- water
- fresh peas
- nutmeg
- clove
- cinnamon
- ground pepper
- kid goat lungs or offal

## Directions

1. After cleaning, place to roast, anointing with oil and salt
2. Once roasted, anoint with beaten egg yolks
3. Fry garlic cloves and minced onion in butter, add a little water, fresh peas, nutmeg, clove, cinnamon and ground pepper
4. Mince finely the lungs or offal and stew with spices and peas to serve as sauce for the kid goat
