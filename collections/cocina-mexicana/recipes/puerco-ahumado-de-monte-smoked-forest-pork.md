## Ingredients

- pork meat
- salt
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Wash the pork meat well and make cuts lengthwise, but without separating the strips
2. Salt it; and so that it releases the water from the brine, leave it in the sun for a day
3. Then arrange it by layers on some hooks
4. Put it over fire, throwing bay leaves on the coals so they produce smoke
5. Leave the meat over the fire until it browns, and afterwards use it as desired
