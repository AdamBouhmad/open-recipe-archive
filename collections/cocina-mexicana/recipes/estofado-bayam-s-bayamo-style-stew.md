## Ingredients

- two pounds of beef leg, cut into crosswise pieces
- salt
- four ounces of butter
- half a glass of dry wine
- water, double the quantity of wine
- three or four small whole onions
- a couple of bay leaves
- one small cinnamon stick
- six garlic cloves

## Directions

1. Take two pounds from a beef leg, cut into crosswise pieces; place to cook in a pot with the necessary salt, and being cooked, remove and fry in a pan with four ounces of butter.
2. When fried, add half a glass of dry wine, double quantity of water, three or four whole small onions, a couple of bay leaves, one small cinnamon stick, and six garlic cloves.
