## Ingredients

- veal tongues
- water
- salt
- butter
- garlic, ground
- broth from cooking the tongues
- bay leaf
- thyme
- lettuce leaves
- olives
- small chiles
- cured chiles

## Directions

1. Wash and cook the tongues
2. Skin them and place in a cooking vessel with butter, ground garlic, their broth, bay leaf, and thyme
3. Let all the broth be consumed over two fires, turning the tongues occasionally, until they are roasted in their own butter
4. When serving, remove the bay leaf and thyme, arrange on lettuce leaves, and garnish with olives, small chiles, and cured chiles
