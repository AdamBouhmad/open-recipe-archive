## Ingredients

- Pork meat
- Chorizos
- Olemole
- Tortillas
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Abundant lard

## Directions

1. Cook the pork meat; when it is half cooked, add the chorizos.
2. Once everything is cooked together, fry it and set aside from the heat.
3. Prepare the olemole separately, as well as tortillas and toasted sesame seeds.
4. Place a layer of meat and cover it with tortillas and sprinkle with sesame seeds on top.
5. Pour the olemole broth and a handful of sesame seeds over it.
6. Repeat this operation successively, coating the tortillas with abundant lard because the tortillas absorb a lot.
7. Let it heat well and serve.
