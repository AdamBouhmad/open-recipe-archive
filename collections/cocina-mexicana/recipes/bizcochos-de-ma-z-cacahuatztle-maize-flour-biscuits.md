## Ingredients

- four pounds of maize, ground and sifted
- one and a half pounds of powdered sugar
- one pound four ounces of cold butter
- fifteen egg yolks
- seven egg whites

## Directions

1. Grind four pounds of maize, sift it, and mix with one and a half pounds of powdered sugar
2. Knead with one pound four ounces of cold butter, fifteen egg yolks, and seven egg whites, so that the dough is neither very loose nor very stiff
3. Form the biscuits with it
4. Place on parchment papers and put in the oven at good temperature
