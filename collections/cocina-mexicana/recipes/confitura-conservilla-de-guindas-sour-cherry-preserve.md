## Ingredients

- Sour cherries
- Half a pound of powdered sugar for each pound of fruit
- A very small amount of lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place the pitted sour cherries in a vessel that has not contained grease, taking care to damage them as little as possible and not to waste the juice.
2. Add half a pound of powdered sugar for each pound of fruit.
3. Add a very small amount of lemon juice.
4. Cook over high heat, stirring the cherries gently.
5. When the cherries are cooked and at the proper point, remove from heat and follow the same procedures as for other French preserves.
