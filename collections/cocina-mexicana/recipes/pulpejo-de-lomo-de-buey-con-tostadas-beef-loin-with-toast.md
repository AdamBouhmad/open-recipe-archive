## Ingredients

- Slices of beef loin from the previous day
- Broth
- Bread slices
- Butter
- Broth or stock
- Parsley butter
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut slices of leftover beef loin and warm them gently in broth without boiling
2. Fry bread slices in butter until they turn a good color and are the same size as the meat slices
3. Arrange on the plate in a crown shape, alternating one slice of meat with one piece of toast
4. Place in the center a little stock or broth, butter mixed with parsley and lemon juice
