## Ingredients

- butter
- six eggs
- three or four tablespoons of strained broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Melt a little butter to coat the inside of six copper molds or timbales.
2. Dissolve six eggs (whites and yolks) in three or four tablespoons of strained broth.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Strain through cheesecloth and pour into the molds without filling them completely.
5. Cook in a bain-marie, making the water boil gently.
6. When they have set, pass a knife carefully around the molds to loosen the eggs.
7. Turn out onto a plate and serve with some watery substance or light sauce.
