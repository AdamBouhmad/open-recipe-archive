## Ingredients

- eight egg yolks
- four cuartillos of milk
- sugar as needed
- a handful of starch
- sponge cake layers
- toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Place eight egg yolks in four cuartillos of milk with the necessary sugar and a handful of starch
2. Cook everything until it has the consistency of atole
3. Pour into a platter over layers of sponge cake, alternating layers until full
4. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds over the top
5. Serve as before
