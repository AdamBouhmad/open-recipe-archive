## Ingredients

- 4 ounces of soaked almonds, not very finely ground
- 4 ounces of sifted sugar
- half a pound of melted and cooled butter
- half a pound of fresh cheese, ground
- 12 eggs, beaten and well risen, whites and yolks separated
- cinnamon powder to taste
- butter
- clarified syrup at medium point
- 1 pound and a half of sugar
- raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and candied citron

## Directions

1. Mix well the soaked almonds, sifted sugar, melted and cooled butter, ground fresh cheese, well-risen beaten eggs with whites and yolks separated, and cinnamon powder in sufficient quantity to give the cake the desired color
2. When everything is well incorporated and no lumps of almond remain, pour the batter onto one or two sheets of buttered paper prepared in a buttered pan
3. Bake at two gentle fires until set
4. When cooked, remove the cake from the pan with the same papers and drain any remaining butter
5. Carefully replace the cake, removing the papers
6. Pierce it with a popote (reed or straw) all the way through to the bottom
7. Pour clarified syrup at medium point made with a pound and a half of sugar over it
8. Simmer gently until the syrup is consumed and the cake is dry
9. Serve studded with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and candied citron
10. It can also be made without syrup, using instead sifted sugar and two ounces of toasted and ground sponge cake
11. Both versions can have a crust made with biscuit, sugar, cinnamon, and butter
