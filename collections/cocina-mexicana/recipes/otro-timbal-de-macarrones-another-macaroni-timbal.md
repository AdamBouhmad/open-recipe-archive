## Ingredients

- half a pound of macaroni
- one ounce of lard
- salt
- pepper
- sufficient water to cover
- half a pound of Gruyere cheese
- one ounce of lard
- lard for the pan
- pasta dough two lines thick

## Directions

1. Put the macaroni in a proportionate saucepan with one ounce of lard, salt, pepper and sufficient water to cover it
2. When the water has been consumed, grate half a pound of Gruyere cheese and add it to the macaroni with one ounce of lard
3. Beat until the cheese is well melted
4. In another saucepan, put lard and coat it well with it
5. Line it with a paste two lines thick throughout
6. Put the macaroni inside, covering it with a layer of the same paste and thickness
7. Cook over low heat, with heat from above and below, for half an hour
8. Turn it out onto the plate on which it is to be served
