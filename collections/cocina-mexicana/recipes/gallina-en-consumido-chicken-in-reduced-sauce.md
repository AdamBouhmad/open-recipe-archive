## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Whole tomatoes
- Clean garlic
- Chopped parsley
- All spices
- Water
- Salt
- Wine
- Sugar
- Olives
- Raisins
- Capers

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chickens, fill them with whole tomatoes, clean garlic, chopped parsley and all spices
2. Put them to cook in water with salt, and keep them over two fires until the broth has been consumed and the chicken is glazed
3. After remove everything that had been stuffed, grind it, dampen it with wine, add a little sugar
4. Add the chicken and let it season in this broth
5. Decorate with olives, raisins and capers for serving
