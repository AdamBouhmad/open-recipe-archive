## Ingredients

- Ham
- Lard (manteca)
- Tomatoes (xitomate)
- Onions
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Hot water
- Salt
- Squabs (raw)
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Málaga wine

## Directions

1. Cut ham into small dice and fry until golden
2. Add more lard and fry the finely chopped tomatoes, onions, parsley, and garlic
3. After frying everything well, add hot water, salt, and raw squabs
4. Let boil until they cook
5. Season with ground cloves, cinnamon, and pepper
6. Let the broth reduce until thickened
7. Serve adding a little Málaga wine
