## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- forty egg yolks
- a quarter of a quart of mezcal
- a bit of cold lard
- sifted sugar
- cloves
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix three pounds of flour with forty egg yolks, a quarter of a quart of mezcal and a bit of cold lard
2. Stir everything
3. Make the rings and fry them in very hot lard
4. When removed from the frying, roll them in sifted sugar mixed with clove and ground cinnamon
