## Ingredients

- Twenty-five egg yolks
- One and a half pounds of sifted sugar
- Equal quantity of ground curd cheese
- Raisins
- Pine nuts
- Pieces of almond
- Pieces of walnut
- Toasted and ground sponge cake in the quantity necessary to form the paste
- Butter for coating the mold

## Directions

1. Beat twenty-five egg yolks until firm
2. Mix with one and a half pounds of sifted sugar and equal quantity of ground curd cheese
3. Add raisins, pine nuts, pieces of almond, and pieces of walnut
4. Add toasted and ground sponge cake in the quantity necessary to form the paste
5. Pour into a mold coated with butter without filling it completely
6. Bake in the oven as the previous ones, being careful not to let it dry out by giving it air
