## Ingredients

- cooked squash
- beaten eggs
- ground sponge cake
- sifted sugar
- pepper powder
- lard
- syrup
- toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Cook the squash, grind it, pass through a sieve or napkin
2. Mix with beaten eggs in proportion of two eggs per cup of squash
3. Add ground sponge cake as needed for consistency, sifted sugar, and a little pepper powder
4. Form the torrijas and fry in lard
5. Boil in syrup
6. Decorate with toasted sesame seeds
