## Ingredients

- larger pork intestines
- pork meat
- lard in small pieces
- salt
- pepper
- aromatic plants

## Directions

1. Choose the larger pork intestines and after washing them and keeping them in cold water for twenty-four hours, let them drain and dry.
2. Cut them lengthwise along with the meat also cut the same way, and with lard in small pieces.
3. Add salt, pepper, and crushed aromatic plants, with which composition fill another intestine, making it of convenient thickness.
4. Tie at both ends and place to salt.
