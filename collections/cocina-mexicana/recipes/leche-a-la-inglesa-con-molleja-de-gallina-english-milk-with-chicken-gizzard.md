## Ingredients

- Three cuartillos of milk
- Three or four chicken gizzard skins, clean and finely ground

## Directions

1. Cook three cuartillos of milk and let it cool
2. Sweeten to taste
3. Add three or four clean chicken gizzard skins that have been finely ground
4. Strain through a thick napkin
5. Pour into a serving dish placed over hot coals
6. Cover with more hot coals so it sets without boiling
7. Garnish finally with spun eggs
