## Ingredients

- manteca
- cebollas rebanadas
- ajos enteros
- carnero o vaca en pedazos menudos
- huevos
- jamón picado
- caldo de la olla
- chícharos tiernos
- trozos de ave

## Directions

1. Put manteca to fry according to the quantity of soup, and add sliced onions, whole garlic cloves, mutton or beef in small pieces, some eggs and chopped ham
2. When it has taken on the color of a roast, add pot broth, tender peas well cooked and some pieces of bird
