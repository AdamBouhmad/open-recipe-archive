## Ingredients

- lard
- onion, chopped
- tomato, chopped
- water
- mixed spices
- salt
- egg, beaten
- olives
- small chiles, chopped
- hard-boiled egg, in pieces
- garlic
- bread, fried

## Directions

1. Fry chopped onion and tomato in lard
2. Add a little water, mixed spices, and salt
3. Add beaten egg and stir everything until it resembles scrambled eggs and fries together
4. Add olives, chopped small chiles, and hard-boiled egg pieces
5. Fill the cleaned prepared fish with this mixture
6. Close them, coat with beaten egg, and fry
7. Serve as is or in a broth made by frying chopped garlic, onion, and tomato, thickening with bread fried in lard, and adding all spices
