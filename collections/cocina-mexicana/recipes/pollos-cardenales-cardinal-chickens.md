## Ingredients

- beets
- bread
- garlic
- cooked chicken
- chiles
- chorizos
- ham
- plantain slices
- sweet potato slices
- capers
- raisins
- almonds
- olives
- red wine
- sugar

## Directions

1. Grind beets with a small piece of bread and strain.
2. Fry half a garlic clove, and ground, add the strained beet, in which you will place the cooked chicken with chiles, chorizos, ham, slices of plantain and sweet potato, capers, raisins, almonds and olives, letting everything boil over the fire.
3. When removing from heat, add red wine and a little sugar.
