## Ingredients

- mullet
- tomatoes
- onions
- oregano
- pepper
- salt
- olives
- capers
- oil
- avocado
- chiles

## Directions

1. Wash the mullet well and cook it
2. Boil whole tomatoes and whole onions
3. Mix them together after removing the skin from the tomatoes
4. Add oregano and pepper powder, salt, olives, a handful of capers and oil
5. When serving the fish, coat it with this sauce
6. Decorate with avocado and chiles
