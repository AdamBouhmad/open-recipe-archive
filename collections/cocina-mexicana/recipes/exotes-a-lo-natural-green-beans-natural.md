## Ingredients

- Water
- Salt
- Exotes (green beans)
- Butter
- Black pepper
- Parsley, chopped
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Put water with salt in a pot over heat, and when it is boiling, add the cleaned exotes with their ends and strings removed and washed
2. Cook until they become soft and yield to the touch of your fingers
3. Remove them, let them drain in a colander and then refresh them immediately in cold water
4. Put a piece of butter in a saucepan and add the exotes with salt, black pepper, parsley that has been chopped and scalded in boiling water, and lemon juice
5. Bind everything together by shaking the saucepan in all directions without using a spoon, and serve hot
