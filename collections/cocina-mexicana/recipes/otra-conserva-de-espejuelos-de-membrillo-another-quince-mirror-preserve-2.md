## Ingredients

- Peeled quinces
- Boiling water
- Sea salt water
- Clarified syrup
- One pound sugar for each pound of quince

## Directions

1. Peel the quinces and cut into very thin slices
2. Add to boiling water
3. Once cooked, transfer to fresh water with sea salt, changing both three times
4. Add to clarified syrup
5. Keep on heat until it reaches point, having been made with one pound sugar for each pound of quince
