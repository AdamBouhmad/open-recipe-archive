## Ingredients

- Calf ears
- Bacon slices
- Parsley
- Onions
- Lemon slices
- Beef broth
- White wine
- Paper
- Grease
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. After cleaning and passing through flame, scald the ears and place in cold water
2. Cook in water or in a pan lined with bacon slices, with parsley, onions and some lemon slices
3. Add beef broth and good white wine
4. Cover the ears with other bacon strips and on top with greased paper
5. After an hour and a half they will be cooked
6. Remove and drain them
7. Tie the tips as if they were onions
8. Serve with Italian sauce
