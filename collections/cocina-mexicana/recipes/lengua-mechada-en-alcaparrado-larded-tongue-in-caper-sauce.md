## Ingredients

- Calf's tongue
- Butter
- Capers, ground
- Fried bread
- Garlic
- Wine
- Clove, ground
- Black pepper, ground
- Small cooked onions

## Directions

1. Prepare, cook, and lard the tongue as in the previous recipe
2. Brown it in butter
3. Add capers ground with fried bread and garlic
4. Fry everything together
5. Moisten with wine
6. Season with ground clove and pepper
7. Add small cooked onions
8. Let the broth thicken on two fires
