## Ingredients

- beef ribs
- seasoned breadcrumbs
- butter
- scarlet tongue (cooked)
- large onion
- gelatin
- Spanish sauce or butter and flour liaison
- salt

## Directions

1. Coat the ribs well with seasoned breadcrumbs and sauté in butter
2. Cut cooked scarlet tongue into wheels
3. Cut large onion into wheels, removing the interior part and replacing it with tongue wheels
4. Cover the ribs with these wheels of tongue embedded in the onion wheels
5. Cook the ribs as described in previous articles
6. Drain them and cover all visible parts of the ribs and the tongue wheel with gelatin, without touching the onion wheel
7. Arrange on a plate with the broth in which they cooked, thickened by adding a little Spanish sauce or a light liaison of butter and flour moistened with the rib broth, and left to thicken
