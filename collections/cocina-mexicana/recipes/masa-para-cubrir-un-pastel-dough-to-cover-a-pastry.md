## Ingredients

- 1 pound of flour
- 4 whole eggs
- 4 ounces of lard
- 5 ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Knead 1 pound of flour with 4 whole eggs, 4 ounces of lard, and 5 ounces of sugar with all fingers without adding water
2. Extend with a rolling pin on a board, leaving it slightly thicker than a peso coin
3. Butter the inside of a cooking pot and cover it well with this dough
4. Fill according to directions given elsewhere
5. Cover with another layer of the same dough after filling
