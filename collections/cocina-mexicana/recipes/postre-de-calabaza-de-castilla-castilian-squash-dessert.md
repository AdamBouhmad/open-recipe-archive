## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one coconut, ground
- squash
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar
2. Grind the coconut and boil the squash
3. Weigh one pound of squash and grind it
4. When the syrup is thick, add the coconut to boil and then add the squash
5. Stir until it pulls from the pan
6. Turn out onto a dish and sprinkle cinnamon on top
