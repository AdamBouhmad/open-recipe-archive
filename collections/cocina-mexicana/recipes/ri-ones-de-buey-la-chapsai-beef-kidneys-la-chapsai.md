## Ingredients

- beef kidneys
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- flour
- white wine
- aguardiente
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Butter a pan and place in it kidney slices cut into rounds, all of the same thickness and equal on all sides
2. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg, and begin frying over moderate heat
3. Turn the rounds so they heat on both sides
4. Incorporate one tablespoon of flour equal in volume to the butter used to grease the pan
5. Divide this sauce into many parts and place over the kidneys, stirring everything together
6. Add a glass of white wine and another of aguardiente
7. Add a little chopped parsley, empty the pan, and serve the kidneys
