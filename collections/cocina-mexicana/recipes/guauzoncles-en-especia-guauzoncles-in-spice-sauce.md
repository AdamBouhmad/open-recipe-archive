## Ingredients

- Guauzoncles, fried
- Lard
- Garlic
- Onions
- Roasted tomato
- Broth or water
- Salt
- All spices
- Cumin
- Bread

## Directions

1. Fry garlic and onions in lard.
2. Add roasted and ground tomato.
3. Pour broth or water into the mixture.
4. Season with salt and spices, using a very small quantity of cumin.
5. Thicken the broth with soaked and ground bread.
6. Add the fried guauzoncles.
7. Boil everything until the broth is well seasoned and of regular consistency.
