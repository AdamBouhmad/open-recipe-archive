## Ingredients

- four quartillos of syrup at high point
- two quartillos of virgin honey
- toasted and ground bread in necessary quantity
- ground sesame seeds
- crushed almonds
- powder of clove
- cinnamon
- ginger
- dragee
- cinnamon powder
- small confits
- clean and toasted peanuts
- pieces of walnut

## Directions

1. Add the honey to the syrup
2. Mix in toasted and ground bread in necessary quantity
3. Add ground sesame seeds, crushed almonds and powder of clove, cinnamon and ginger to taste
4. Once everything is well incorporated and the paste has the proper consistency, pour into plates or boxes
5. Decorate on top with dragee, cinnamon powder, small confits, clean and toasted peanuts split in halves, and pieces of walnut
