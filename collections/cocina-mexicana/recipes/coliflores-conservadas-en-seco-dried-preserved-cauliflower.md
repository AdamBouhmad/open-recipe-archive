## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean the flower head of all its leaves
2. Divide into three or four parts according to size, opening perpendicularly on the side
3. Place in lightly salted water
4. Remove after a few minutes and submerge in boiling water
5. Keep in hot water for no more than two minutes
6. Remove from water and drain well on wicker
7. Place wicker in the sun to complete drying
8. Finish drying by placing in a moderately warm oven
9. Store the cauliflowers in sacks or paper bags, or in white wooden boxes
