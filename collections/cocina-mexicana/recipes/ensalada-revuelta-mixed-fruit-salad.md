## Ingredients

- bergamot pears
- quince
- quince
- sugar
- cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- vinegar sweetened with sugar or muscatel wine

## Directions

1. Peel bergamot pears, quince, and quince
2. Remove the cores and chop into small squares
3. Cook in water with a little sugar and some cinnamon strips
4. When half-cooked, remove from fire and drain
5. Add pitted and chopped raisins, clean and toasted almonds and pine nuts
6. Sprinkle with cinnamon powder, moistening with vinegar sweetened with sugar or muscatel wine
