## Ingredients

- butter, melted
- parsley, finely chopped
- salt
- pepper
- one glass of cream
- one tablespoon of flour
- cooked eggs

## Directions

1. To the melted butter add finely chopped parsley, salt, pepper, and a glass of cream in which a tablespoon of flour has been previously dissolved.
2. When the sauce is ready, place the cooked eggs cut into rounds in it.
