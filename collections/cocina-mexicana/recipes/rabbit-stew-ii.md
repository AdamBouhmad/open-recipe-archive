## Ingredients

- onion
- garlic
- ham
- rabbit
- water
- clove
- cinnamon
- pimientos, ground
- thyme
- laurel
- oregano
- bread
- oil
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop considerable onion and garlic, cut thin strips of ham and fry everything
2. Skin the rabbit, cut into raw pieces, fry with the above, add water, clove, cinnamon and ground pimientos, thyme, laurel and oregano
3. Thicken with fried bread ground and when serving add oil and chiles in vinegar
