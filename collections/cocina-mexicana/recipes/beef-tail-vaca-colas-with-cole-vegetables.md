## Ingredients

- Beef tails
- Cole vegetables
- Parsley
- Small onions
- Nutmeg
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Simmer the beef tails with a bouquet of parsley, small onions, and nutmeg.
2. Moisten with broth and add salt and coarse pepper.
3. Let simmer gently until the tails are cooked.
4. Remove the tails from the pot, drain, and wipe off the fat.
5. Arrange interspersed with the cole vegetables and tallow on top.
6. Sprinkle well with thick Spanish sauce of good flavor.
