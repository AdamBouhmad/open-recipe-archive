## Ingredients

- beef ribs
- four ounces pork fat with hair
- butter

## Directions

1. Arrange the ribs well and place at the bottom of a pot four ounces of fatty pork, divided into slices, a little butter, and the ribs on top
2. Leave to cook over gentle heat in their own substance, stirring continuously, and when cooked, arrange on a plate
