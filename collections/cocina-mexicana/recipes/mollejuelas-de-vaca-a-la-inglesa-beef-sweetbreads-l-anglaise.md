## Ingredients

- Beef sweetbreads
- Semi-gelatin
- Egg yolks
- Butter
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Soak the sweetbreads, blanch and cook in a semi-gelatin, then let cool and drain.
2. Beat egg yolks with melted butter and coat the sweetbreads twice with this mixture, successively covering them with bread crumbs.
