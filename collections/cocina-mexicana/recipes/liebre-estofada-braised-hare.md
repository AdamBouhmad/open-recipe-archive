## Ingredients

- Hare
- vinegar
- water
- blood of the hare
- offal of the hare
- lard
- garlic
- chorizo
- onion
- sherry wine
- almonds
- sugar
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- flour

## Directions

1. After emptying and skinning the hare, divide it into quarters and place it to bleed in water with vinegar, adding the blood that has been saved and all the offal
2. Remove the hare and offal from the liquid and render or half-fry them in a pot with lard
3. Add ground garlic, slices of chorizo and onion
4. When everything is fried, moisten with sherry wine and the water with vinegar and blood in which the hare was bled, having strained it first
5. Add ground almonds, a lump of sugar and clove, ground with pepper and cinnamon
6. Cover the pot and let the hare cook over gentle heat
7. When cooked, thicken the sauce with flour browned in lard
