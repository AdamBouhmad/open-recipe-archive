## Ingredients

- Beef tendons
- Meat jelly
- Macaroni à la Neapolitan

## Directions

1. Cut the tendons into small square or round pieces.
2. Cook them in white (in salted water).
3. Drain and place in a sauté pan with meat jelly.
4. Heat so that the tendons are well covered with the jelly.
5. Prepare macaroni à la Neapolitan separately.
6. Place the tendons on top of the macaroni to serve.
