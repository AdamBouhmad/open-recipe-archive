## Ingredients

- one duck, well prepared and arranged
- butter
- half a pound of olives

## Directions

1. Heat butter in a saucepan
2. Turn the duck a few times in the butter
3. Add half a pound of olives to the saucepan where the duck is
4. Let sit for a few minutes
5. Serve the duck surrounded by the olives
