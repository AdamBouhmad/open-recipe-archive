## Ingredients

- Stuffed chiles
- Onions
- Salted water
- Epazote branches
- Cooking oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles as described previously
2. Slice onions and fry them after the chiles have been fried
3. Add salted water and place the chiles in the broth so they cook well with epazote branches
4. When they are well cooked, remove them and serve with the broth, adding a little cooking oil
