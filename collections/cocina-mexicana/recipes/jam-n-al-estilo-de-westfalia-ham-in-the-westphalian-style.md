## Ingredients

- Ham legs separated from the pork
- Pure saltpeter
- Spirit of wine
- Juniper berries, ground or crushed
- Juniper wood for smoking

## Directions

1. Separate the ham legs from the pork body
2. Salt them sufficiently with pure saltpeter
3. Place under a press and leave for eight days
4. Soak with spirit of wine in which juniper berries have been steeped in infusion, ground or crushed
5. After this operation, smoke and dry them with the smoke of juniper wood, burned for this purpose in a suitable location
