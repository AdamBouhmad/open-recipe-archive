## Ingredients

- rice
- butter
- chickens
- middle loin
- longaniza
- chorizos
- ham
- garlic
- tomato
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron
- fresh chiles or huauchínangos

## Directions

1. Wash the rice very well and put it to dry in the sun
2. Put butter in a cazuela and there add the rice until it browns
3. Cook in a pot the chickens, middle loin, longaniza, chorizos and ham
4. Once the rice is fried, spread it as well as you can on the bottom, and on top place a layer of chickens, chorizos, etc., another of rice, another of chickens and so continue until finished, arranging that the last be of rice
5. Fry garlic and tomato in plenty of butter, and then add a bit of broth in which the meat was cooked
6. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper and saffron; place the pot on the fire and over it pour a bit of this spiced broth, which you continue doing until the rice cooks
7. For it to be quicker, place it between two fires; once it has almost no broth, toast some fresh chiles or huauchínangos, remove their veins, fill them with the same rice and bury them in the rest
8. Serve this way
