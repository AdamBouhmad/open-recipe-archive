## Ingredients

- large onions
- salt
- picadillo (minced meat filling)
- flour
- eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. Take large onions and cut off the ends, then cook well in salted water
2. When cooked, remove and cool
3. Carefully separate the layers, trying to keep them as whole as possible
4. Having prepared a picadillo as for chiles, fill the onion layers
5. Cover well with layers the portion of picadillo that has been placed
6. Dust with flour, dip in beaten egg and fry in lard
7. Afterwards place in a small broth like that made for chiles, or serve covered with nogada sauce, or garnish vegetable stews with them
