## Ingredients

- Lamb shoulder
- Bacon strips
- Meat trimmings
- Carrots
- Onion
- Bouquet garni
- Meat broth
- Chicory or other vegetables

## Directions

1. Break the bone on top with a knife and roast on a spit prepared with bacon strips.
2. Add any meat trimmings, carrots, onion, and a bouquet garni, pouring broth over it.
3. Serve with its cooking reduced, or over chicory or other vegetables, with whatever spices are desired.
