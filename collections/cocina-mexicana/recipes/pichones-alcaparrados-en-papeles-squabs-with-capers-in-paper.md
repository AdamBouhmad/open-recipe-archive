## Ingredients

- squabs, cooked
- oil
- salt
- capers, ground
- almonds, ground
- bread crumbs
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Fry the cooked squabs in oil with salt
2. Brush them well and cover with a thick sauce made with ground capers and almonds, bread crumbs and pepper powder
3. Wrap them in oiled papers
4. Place on the grill to brown
