## Ingredients

- twelve ounces of lemon juice
- twenty-four ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Take twelve ounces of lemon juice and twenty-four ounces of sugar
2. Let the lemon juice rest in a vessel for twenty-four hours
3. Strain and cook the sugar
4. Add the juice and after stirring and bringing to a boil, remove from heat and bottle
