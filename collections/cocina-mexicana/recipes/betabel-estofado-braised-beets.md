## Ingredients

- beets
- water
- salt
- onion
- oil
- pepper

## Directions

1. Scrape and clean the beets
2. Give them a boil with water and salt, reserving the water
3. Cut into slices
4. Sauté with onion pieces, oil, and a few peppercorns
5. Add some of the reserved red cooking water
6. Let the broth reduce and serve
