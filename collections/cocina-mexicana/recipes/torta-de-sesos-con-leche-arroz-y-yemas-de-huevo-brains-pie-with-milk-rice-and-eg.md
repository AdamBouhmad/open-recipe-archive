## Ingredients

- sheep brains
- salt
- water
- milk
- white sugar
- whole rice
- egg yolks
- cinnamon
- cloves
- eggs
- butter
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the sheep brains in water with salt, and after cooking clean them well
2. For each brain, cook three quartillos of milk with white sugar, mixed with a handful of whole rice well washed and sun-dried, with two egg yolks dissolved in the same milk, one cinnamon stick and a few cloves
3. Once the rice is cooked, dissolve the brains in it
4. Beat twelve eggs for each brain, first the whites and then the yolks, and after stirring, break the mixture formed of the brains, rice and milk into them
5. Butter a pie dish, pour the said composition into it, and cook at two gentle fires, taking care to add butter from time to time around the edges
6. Once the pie is set, serve sprinkled with ground cinnamon
