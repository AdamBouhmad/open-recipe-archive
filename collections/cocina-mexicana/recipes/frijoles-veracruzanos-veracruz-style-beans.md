## Ingredients

- serrano beans
- water
- lard
- garlic
- oil
- onion
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Wash beans to remove dust and cook in water with a little lard; when cooked set aside
2. In a cazuela fry sliced garlic cloves in oil, remove from oil once golden
3. In the same oil fry a small amount of chopped onion
4. Add the beans with the water they cooked in
5. Set aside two or three spoonfuls of beans to grind
6. Add the cooked beans to the cazuela with lard, onion slices, the ground beans and salt
7. Let the broth reduce and re-fry the beans
8. While re-frying with plenty of lard mash with a spoon and gradually add breadcrumbs, re-fry until they form a molote
9. Serve very hot
