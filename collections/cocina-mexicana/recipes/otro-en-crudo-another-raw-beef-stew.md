## Ingredients

- lard
- sliced garlic
- sliced onion
- chopped tomato
- parsley
- mint leaves
- raw beef slices, as thin as possible
- ham slices
- Castilian vinegar
- water
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- Jerez wine
- chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Heat lard in a casserole and add sliced garlic, sliced onion and chopped tomato
2. Add parsley and mint leaves and fry everything
3. Add thin slices of raw beef and ham slices
4. Fry and then add Castilian vinegar and water
5. Grind cloves, cinnamon and pepper
6. Before serving, add a bit of Jerez wine and garnish with chiles, olives and capers
