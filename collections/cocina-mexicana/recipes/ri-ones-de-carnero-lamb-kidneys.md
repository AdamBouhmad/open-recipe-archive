## Ingredients

- lamb kidneys
- salt
- pepper
- lard
- garlic
- parsley
- vinegar
- strong wine

## Directions

1. Open the kidneys in half and season with salt and pepper
2. Pierce them with a larding needle and place on the grill
3. When roasted, place in a casserole with lard
4. Make a sauce of garlic and parsley crushed and diluted with a little vinegar
5. They can also be cooked with a glass of strong wine
