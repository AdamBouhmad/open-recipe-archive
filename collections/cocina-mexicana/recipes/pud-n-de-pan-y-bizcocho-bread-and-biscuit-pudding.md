## Ingredients

- four cuartillos of well-sweetened milk
- six cakes of cold soup bread
- twelve hard biscuits
- one bowl of curd cheese
- half a pound of butter
- three egg yolks
- one egg white

## Directions

1. Mix six cakes of cold soup bread and twelve hard biscuits with four cuartillos of well-sweetened milk, one bowl of curd cheese, half a pound of butter, three egg yolks and one egg white
2. Soak everything in the milk and break it up well with a spoon
3. Pour into molds greased with fresh butter, cook in them and then cool before serving the pudding
