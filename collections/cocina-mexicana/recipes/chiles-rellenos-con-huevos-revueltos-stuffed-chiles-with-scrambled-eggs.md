## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Scrambled eggs
- Flour
- Beaten egg
- Lard
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Tomatoes, roasted and well-ripened
- Water
- Onion quarters

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles as described
2. Fill the chiles with scrambled eggs instead of meat picadillo
3. Coat with flour and dip in beaten egg
4. Fry the chiles
5. Fry chopped garlic and parsley in lard with roasted ground tomatoes
6. Add water and let the sauce boil until the tomato is well cooked
7. Add clean onion quarters and ground spices with necessary salt
8. Place the stuffed chiles in the sauce and let it reduce to regular consistency
