## Ingredients

- Peas
- Lettuce hearts
- Wood sorrel
- Oil or ingredients for the seasoning of other stews

## Directions

1. Add peas to the pot when it is boiling with some lettuce hearts and wood sorrel
2. Add the seasoning used for other stews
3. If the peas are dried, cook them the same way as chickpeas
