## Ingredients

- One and a half pounds of huacamote
- Eight pounds of almond
- Clarified syrup made with six pounds of sugar
- One quartillo of cream

## Directions

1. Grind one and a half pounds of huacamote and eight pounds of almond
2. Add to clarified syrup made with six pounds of sugar
3. When it is about to set, remove and let cool a little
4. Add one quartillo of cream and return to the fire until it takes the proper consistency
5. Arrange as with the other pastes
