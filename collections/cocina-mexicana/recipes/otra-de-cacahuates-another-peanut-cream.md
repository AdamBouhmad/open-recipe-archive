## Ingredients

- two ounces of toasted and cleaned peanuts
- a little lemon peel
- water
- milk
- sugar

## Directions

1. Grind two ounces of toasted and cleaned peanuts and a little lemon peel, adding a small amount of water
2. Add to milk prepared as described in the previous recipes
3. Complete the operation in the same manner
4. Garnish this cream with the same toasted peanuts
