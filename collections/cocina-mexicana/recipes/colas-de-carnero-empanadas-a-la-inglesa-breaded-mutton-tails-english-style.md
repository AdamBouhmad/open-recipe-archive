## Ingredients

- mutton tails
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook at the braise and when cooked, drain and season with salt and pepper
2. Melt a piece of butter in a pot and place the tails there to coat them perfectly
3. Roll them in cold crumbled or grated breadcrumbs
4. Break four eggs over the butter, beating everything together and coat the tails with this, taking care that they remain soaked on all sides
5. Rolling them again in the breadcrumbs, they will be perfectly covered with compact breadcrumb coating and retain their shape
6. Half an hour before serving, place them on the grill over gentle heat, covering them with a hot dome oven so they take on good color
7. Dress them on a plate with clear substance below
