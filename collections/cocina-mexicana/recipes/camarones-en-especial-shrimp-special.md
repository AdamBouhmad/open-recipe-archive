## Ingredients

- cleaned and well-cooked shrimp
- lard
- cooked tomatoes, without seeds and ground
- chopped garlic
- potatoes cooked separately
- chickpeas
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- saffron
- cumin
- parsley, chopped
- small cooked chard or similar greens
- bread, soaked
- small whole chiles
- salt

## Directions

1. Fry cleaned and well-cooked shrimp in lard with cooked, seeded and ground tomatoes and chopped garlic
2. When it has fried well, add potatoes cooked separately and chickpeas
3. Add the shrimp and add clove, cinnamon, pepper, saffron and a little cumin, chopped parsley and small cooked greens
4. Thicken this with a piece of soaked bread
5. Season with whole small chiles and salt as needed
