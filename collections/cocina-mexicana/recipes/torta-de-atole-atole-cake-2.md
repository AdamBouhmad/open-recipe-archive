## Ingredients

- milk
- atole
- fine biscuits (bizcochos)
- sponge cake (mamones)
- butter
- sugar

## Directions

1. Make the atole with milk from the night before with high consistency so that the next day it can be cut into thin slices
2. Wrap these slices in finely ground biscuits
3. Make a layer of these slices and another of sponge cakes with melted butter on top
4. Cook over two fires, and when done, add sugar
