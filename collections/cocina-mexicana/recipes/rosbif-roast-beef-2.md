## Ingredients

- beef sirloin
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Take a piece of beef and cut it lengthwise to the desired size, and pound it well.
2. Place a quantity of butter and pork meat in a pan.
3. Coat the meat in a little flour, and when the butter is hot, place the meat in it.
4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, turning from time to time.
5. Serve hot.
