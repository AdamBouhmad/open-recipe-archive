## Ingredients

- lamb shoulder or similar cuts, studded
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- lard
- thyme
- marjoram
- cloves
- cinnamon
- sugar
- spirits (cane, mescal, or Catalan)
- roasted tomatoes
- cooked lamb liver

## Directions

1. Stud the lamb cuts and place them in a pot with water, salt, a little vinegar, lard, thyme, marjoram, cloves, cinnamon, sugar, and spirits (whether cane, mescal, or Catalan)
2. Cover the pot and cook until the meat is done and the broth is consumed
3. Empty the contents into a cazuela (earthenware dish)
4. Prepare a sauce by roasting tomatoes and cooking lamb liver, then grinding both and frying them
5. Add this sauce to the meat and let it simmer together until the sauce is consumed and the dish becomes dry
