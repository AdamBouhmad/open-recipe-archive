## Ingredients

- eggs
- flour
- milk
- sugar
- orange blossom water
- vanilla
- lemon zest
- butter

## Directions

1. Mix eggs with flour to form a thick dough
2. Dilute the dough with milk until it reaches the consistency of a thick porridge
3. Add sugar, orange blossom water, vanilla, lemon zest, or any other flavoring at hand
4. Butter a baking dish and pour in the mixture
5. Bake quickly over brisk heat with coals placed on top of a cover
6. The mixture will rise like a soufflé and should be served hot immediately after baking
