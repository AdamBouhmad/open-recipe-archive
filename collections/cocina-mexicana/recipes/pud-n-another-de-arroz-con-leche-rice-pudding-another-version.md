## Ingredients

- common rice pudding
- clove
- cinnamon
- saffron
- egg yolks
- a little washed butter
- clean almonds
- raisins
- pine nuts
- walnuts
- hard biscuit cake, ground
- custard cream
- sugar
- cinnamon
- sesame

## Directions

1. Base this on common rice pudding; when it is half cooked, add ground clove, cinnamon and saffron
2. Separate a portion without the spices, let it cool and mix with egg yolks, then combine it with the other portion so they boil together with a little washed butter until all is cooked, being careful it does not fall apart
3. Remove from heat and add clean almonds cut into pieces, raisins, pine nuts and walnuts
4. Grease a baking dish or pan with butter and place a layer of rice, then a layer of ground hard biscuit above it, then a layer of plain custard cream covered with powder of sugar and cinnamon
5. Continue in this order until the dish is full, making sure the last layer is rice with a little washed butter on top
6. Let it brown at two gentle fires; if you want the pudding covered, beat egg yolks well and mix with a little milk, ground almonds and sugar, place this on top and let it set with a comal with hot coals
7. Decorate finally with toasted sesame, raisins, almonds and pine nuts
