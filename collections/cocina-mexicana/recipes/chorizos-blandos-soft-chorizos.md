## Ingredients

- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- marjoram
- laurel
- fine oregano
- meat without much fat
- one quartillo of Jerez wine per pound of meat

## Directions

1. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper, marjoram, laurel and fine oregano
2. Mince meat that does not have much fat and pass it through the metate, adding for each pound of meat one quartillo of Jerez wine
3. Mix everything and fill the tripe
4. Prick them with a pin, put them in hot water and let them boil
