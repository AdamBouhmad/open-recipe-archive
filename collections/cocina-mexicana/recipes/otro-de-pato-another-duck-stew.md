## Ingredients

- Ducks
- Lard
- Tomatoes
- Onion bunches
- Parsley
- Flour
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Follow the same method as the previous recipe until where it says to put lard in a pan
2. When hot, add the ducks, two or three tomatoes and two bunches of onion and a little chopped parsley, and this is added with the meat
3. Once everything is fried, add flour and let fry a little more
4. Then add water, grind clove, cinnamon and pepper, add to the stew and garnish as in the previous recipe
5. The broth should be thin
