## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- mint
- parsley
- garlic cloves, sliced
- pepper and cloves, ground
- salt
- ham, sliced thickly
- water
- four parts vinegar
- half a bottle of red wine
- pepper and cloves, whole

## Directions

1. Take the leg and remove the shank
2. Take mint, parsley, sliced garlic cloves, ground pepper and cloves, salt in powder, and ham sliced into thick pieces
3. Stud the leg like an estofado with all these ingredients
4. To do it better, make small openings between intervals with the tip of a knife
5. Place in a casserole that is not too wide
6. Add the necessary amount of water, four parts vinegar, and half a bottle of red wine, some pepper and whole cloves
7. Cook over two fires until it is like roasted meat
