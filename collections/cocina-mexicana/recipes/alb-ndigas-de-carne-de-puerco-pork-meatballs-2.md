## Ingredients

- Pork loin and lean pork meat
- Clove powder
- Cinnamon
- Cumin

## Directions

1. Mince together pork loin and lean pork meat
2. Grind in a grinding stone, removing scraps and tendons as they appear
3. Mix the ground meat with clove powder, cinnamon and cumin
