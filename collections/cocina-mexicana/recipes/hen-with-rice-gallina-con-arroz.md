## Ingredients

- hen
- water
- salt
- thyme
- bay leaf
- cloves
- three onions
- two beets
- ham
- rice (about half a pound)

## Directions

1. Cook the hen in a saucepan with water to cover, salt, a little thyme, bay leaf, cloves, three onions, two beets, and ham in pieces
2. When it is almost done, remove the onions and beets
3. Adjust the broth for the rice and add about half a pound of rice
4. When the rice is cooked, set it aside and place the hen on top
5. Serve the hen on top of the rice
