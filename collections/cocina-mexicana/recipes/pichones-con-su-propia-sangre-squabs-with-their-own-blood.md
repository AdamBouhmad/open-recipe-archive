## Ingredients

- Creole vinegar
- Squabs
- Lard
- Onion, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Ham slices
- Chorizo
- Ground cinnamon
- Ground pepper
- Thyme
- Fried bread slices
- Jerez wine
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Place creole vinegar in a casserole and bleed the squabs into it to collect the blood
2. Heat lard in a casserole, add chopped onion and garlic
3. When well fried, dissolve the blood and strain it, then fry it in the casserole with the onion and garlic
4. Add the whole squabs with ham slices and chorizo
5. Fry everything very well
6. Add ground cinnamon, pepper, thyme, and fried bread slices
7. The broth should be thick
8. Add Jerez wine
9. Garnish with chiles, olives, and capers
