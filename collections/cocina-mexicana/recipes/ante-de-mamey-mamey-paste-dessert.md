## Ingredients

- Mamey
- Syrup at medium point
- White wine
- Spices
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Sesame seeds
- Sponge cake

## Directions

1. Grind the mamey and add it to the syrup at medium point for six boilings
2. Add white wine, spices, raisins, almonds, and sesame seeds
3. Pour over sponge cake
