## Ingredients

- almonds
- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- sponge cake, sliced and toasted

## Directions

1. Soak the almonds and the next day grind them
2. Dissolve in milk and strain, then grind again until no grains remain
3. Mix with milk sweetened with sugar and strained
4. Boil with a little cinnamon until it begins to thicken
5. Pour over toasted slices of sponge cake
6. Cook the soup without letting it boil much so the sponge cake does not fall apart
7. Serve with cinnamon on top
