## Ingredients

- sugar from a loaf with the most black vein
- lemon
- egg white

## Directions

1. Choose sugar from a loaf that has the most black vein and make a syrup at medium point
2. Clarify it with lemon and egg white
3. Keep until the next day
4. Taking out small portions, put them to boil until they have the point of breaking in water
5. Throw them in a moist box and lift them on a smooth stone or marble, stretching them until very white
6. Form the figures that are desired
