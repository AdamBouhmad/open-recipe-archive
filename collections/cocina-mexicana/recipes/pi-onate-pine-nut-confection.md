## Ingredients

- Two pounds of sugar
- Six egg yolks
- Half a pound of ground pine nuts
- One soft cheese ground
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar and add six egg yolks
2. Place to boil for a few minutes, then add half a pound of ground pine nuts and one ground soft cheese
3. Stir everything together and add a pinch of cinnamon
4. Give it the point of cajeta
