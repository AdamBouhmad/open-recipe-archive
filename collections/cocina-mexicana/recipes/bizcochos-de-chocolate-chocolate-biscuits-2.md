## Ingredients

- six fresh eggs
- one and a half ounces of chocolate, ground to fine powder
- a pinch of vanilla
- six ounces of flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Separate the eggs, putting the yolks in one bowl and the whites in another
2. In the bowl with the yolks, add the ground chocolate powder and vanilla
3. Beat everything very well for at least a quarter of an hour
4. Add the well-beaten egg whites
5. Once well incorporated, add six ounces of flour, stirring always so it mixes
6. Place the biscuits on papers spread with butter
7. Sprinkle powdered sugar on them
8. Put them in the oven
