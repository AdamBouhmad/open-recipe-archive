## Ingredients

- fat ham
- beef without fibers or tendons
- salt
- pepper
- lard or butter
- bread crumb soaked in broth
- mushrooms
- egg yolks
- flour

## Directions

1. Cut fat ham and beef without fibers or tendons into short pieces
2. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and pass them through lard or butter
3. When both are cooked, let them cool, chop them
4. While chopping, add a little bread crumb soaked in broth
5. Add finely chopped mushrooms fried in butter
6. Knead with egg yolks, from which small meatballs or small squares can be formed
7. Roll them in flour and fry them
8. Serve to accompany dishes
9. This stuffing can be used to stuff game birds or fish
