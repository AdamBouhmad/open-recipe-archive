## Ingredients

- Lettuce
- Watercress leaves
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Salt

## Directions

1. Chop the lettuce as usual
2. Chop also some watercress leaves (referring to the plant and not the flower)
3. With its corresponding oil and vinegar it acquires a particular taste and is very healthful
