## Ingredients

- tender calabacitas (squash)
- wide chiles
- water
- garlic cloves
- cumin
- cloves
- bread
- vinegar
- fine salt
- onion
- oregano
- cooking oil

## Directions

1. Seed the wide chiles well and soak them in cold water.
2. After washing them well, grind them with garlic cloves, cumin, cloves, and bread soaked in vinegar.
3. When all is ground, season with fine salt as needed.
4. Add a little chopped onion and make the sauce more or less thick by adding good vinegar.
5. Coat the cooked calabacitas with this sauce and crumble cheese on top.
6. Sprinkle with oregano and cooking oil.
