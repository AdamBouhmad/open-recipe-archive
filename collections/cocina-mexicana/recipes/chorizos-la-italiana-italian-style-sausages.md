## Ingredients

- lean pork
- one quarter part ordinary salt pork
- spices
- salt
- cilantro
- anise powder
- white wine
- hot pork blood
- meat strands from the animal head

## Directions

1. Chop lean pork with one quarter part ordinary salt pork and season with spices, salt, cilantro, and anise powder.
2. Pour white wine and an equal amount of still-hot pork blood over this mixture.
3. Make strands with the meat from the animal head and introduce them with everything else in the intestines.
4. Tie to a convenient size.
5. Cook and place in smoke.
