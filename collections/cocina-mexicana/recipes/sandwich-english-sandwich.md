## Ingredients

- small, very thin slices of cold bread
- butter
- very thin slices of beef sirloin, cooked ham, tongue, poultry, or game
- fine forcemeat, poultry substance, and excellent Italian cheese

## Directions

1. Lightly spread butter on the bread slices
2. On some slices, arrange a very thin slice of beef sirloin, cooked ham, tongue, poultry, game, or fine forcemeat with poultry substance and excellent Italian cheese
3. Cover these slices with the other buttered slices
4. Press together to reduce their volume
5. Serve
