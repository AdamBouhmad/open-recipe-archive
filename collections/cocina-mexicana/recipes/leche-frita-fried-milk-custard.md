## Ingredients

- diez y seis yemas de huevo (sixteen egg yolks)
- cuatro cuartillos de leche muy endulzada (four quarts of very sweetened milk)
- hojas de naranjo (orange blossoms)
- cuatro onzas de almendra, remojada, molida y desleída en una poca de leche (four ounces of soaked, ground almond dissolved in a little milk)
- bizcocho molido (ground sponge cake)
- azúcar en polvo (powdered sugar)
- huevo batido (beaten egg)
- mantequilla quemada (browned butter)
- polvo de canela (cinnamon powder)

## Directions

1. Add sixteen egg yolks to four quarts of very sweetened milk
2. Strain immediately and place over heat
3. Once it begins to boil, add some orange blossoms
4. When it becomes thick, add four ounces of soaked, ground almond dissolved in a little milk
5. Allow it to reach a higher consistency than cajeta
6. Pour into deep plates over ground sponge cake
7. Allow to cool until the next day
8. When cold, divide with a knife into pieces of desired size and shape
9. Cover pieces with powdered sugar, or coat with beaten egg
10. Roll immediately in ground sponge cake
11. Fry both types in browned butter
12. As they are removed from the butter, roll the egg-coated ones in cinnamon and sugar powder, and those fried covered with sugar in cinnamon alone
13. Place on serving platters or plates
