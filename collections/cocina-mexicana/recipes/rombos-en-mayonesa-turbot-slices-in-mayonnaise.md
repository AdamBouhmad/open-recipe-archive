## Ingredients

- Leftover turbot slices
- Salt
- Coarse pepper
- Reinforced herb sauce
- Oil
- Tarragon vinegar
- Hard-boiled eggs
- Anchovy strips
- Gherkins
- Tarragon leaves
- Capers
- Aspic toasts
- Mayonnaise or white or green provençale sauce

## Directions

1. Take the slices of turbot that have been left over at the table and remove the skins
2. Dress them, cut them into wheels or in the shape of hearts, and place them in a dish
3. Season with salt, coarse pepper, reinforced herb sauce, oil and tarragon vinegar
4. Arrange the turbot slices in the shape of crowns on the plate with a border of hard-boiled eggs, decorating with strips of anchovy, gherkins, tarragon leaves and capers
5. Place some nice aspic toasts around the plate and in the center of the turbot slices, a mayonnaise or white or green provençale sauce
