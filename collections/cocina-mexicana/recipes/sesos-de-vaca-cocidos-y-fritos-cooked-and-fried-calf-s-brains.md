## Ingredients

- calf's brains
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- frying batter
- lard or butter
- fried parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare as the butler's brains from the previous articles, cook them and divide in half
2. Place them in a dish with fine salt, a little pepper and vinegar
3. At the moment of serving, drain them, coat with frying batter (see pages 106 and 514), and fry in lard or butter that is not too hot
4. After frying, drain on clean cloth and arrange on a plate with a bouquet of fried parsley, arranged in pyramid form
