## Ingredients

- sardines marinated in oil
- lemon
- chopped parsley
- buttered paper
- oil for frying
- a bunch of parsley
- two eggs
- a good handful of shelled pine nuts
- garlic cloves
- butter
- water
- capers

## Directions

1. Marinate the sardines in oil and anoint them with lemon and chopped parsley
2. Wrap each sardine individually in buttered paper
3. Fry them in oil until well fried
4. Remove the papers and place the sardines on a platter
5. Prepare the sauce: boil a bunch of parsley and two eggs, toast a good handful of shelled pine nuts and grind all together
6. Fry garlic cloves in butter until golden, remove them, and fry the parsley with everything else in the same butter
7. Add a little water and the fried garlic minced very finely
8. When it has thickened well and just before finishing, squeeze a lemon over it
9. Pour the sauce over the sardines
10. Garnish with capers
