## Ingredients

- Roasted young partridges
- Salt and pepper
- Shallots, chopped
- Parsley, chopped
- Lemon rind, grated
- Lemon juice
- One glass of white wine

## Directions

1. When roasted young partridges have been served at table, they can be made into salmis at the same table in view of the guests
2. Break them apart and dress their pieces on a plate
3. Place the plate over a burner or apparatus with spirits
4. Season with salt, pepper, chopped shallots and parsley, grated lemon rind and lemon juice
5. Moisten everything with one glass of white wine
6. Let the salmis boil for several minutes
