## Ingredients

- Lard
- Parsley
- Onion
- Sesame
- Cilantro
- Black pepper
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Ginger
- Chicken
- Red wine
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Capers
- Olives
- Tornachiles
- Salt

## Directions

1. Grease a pot with lard and make a layer of chopped parsley, sliced onion, toasted sesame, dry cilantro, and all the spices ground together
2. Place the chicken quarters on top, fried raw
3. Add a quarter liter of wine, another of vinegar, half a quarter liter of oil, raisins, almonds in quarters, capers, olives, cured tornachiles, and appropriate salt
4. Cover the pot tightly and let it boil over gentle heat until the chickens are cooked
