## Ingredients

- pumpkin seeds, toasted on comal
- bread, toasted
- toasted ancho chile
- lard
- salt
- meat and its broth
- toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Toast pumpkin seeds on comal, taking care they do not overcook, and toast bread likewise
2. Grind the seeds with toasted ancho chile to give it color
3. Once strained, add the bread also ground
4. Fry in sufficient lard with salt
5. When ready, add the meats and their broth
6. Once it has thickened, remove from heat and sprinkle toasted sesame on top
