## Ingredients

- pork loin
- pork shoulder
- hard biscuits
- spices
- beaten eggs
- lard
- syrup
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook and mince finely one loin and one shoulder of pork; mix them with hard biscuits well ground, spices to taste and beaten eggs, leaving it of a regular consistency, neither watery nor thick; make small sticks and fry them in lard, then put syrup and ground cinnamon on top.
