## Ingredients

- One pound of flour
- A little salt
- A little water
- Four eggs
- Half pound of lard
- Fifty Spanish-style plums
- Half pound of well-mashed panocha
- A little cinnamon
- One peeled lemon zest
- Thread or string for tying
- Butter for coating the cloth

## Directions

1. Place one pound of flour on a table with a little salt
2. Add a little water, four eggs, and half pound of lard
3. Moisten the dough so that it becomes somewhat firm or tight
4. Knead and extend with a rolling pin
5. Arrange it on a napkin coated with butter
6. In the middle of the dough place fifty Spanish-style plums with half pound of well-mashed panocha, a little cinnamon, and one peeled lemon zest
7. Wrap the dough in the form of a ball or pellet and tie with threads as tightly as possible to prevent water from penetrating
8. Place the pudding to boil in a pot or cauldron with boiling water
9. Leave it in this state for about an hour and a half or a little more
10. At the moment of serving, drain it, cut the threads, remove the napkin from the paste, and pour it onto a deep plate
11. Be very careful not to damage or break it when removing the napkin
