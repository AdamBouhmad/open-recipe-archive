## Ingredients

- beef sirloin
- salt
- pepper
- small onions
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook in the roaster with great heat.
2. Remove from the roaster when cooking has caused it to lose the reddish color of the interior.
3. It is then considered cooked, although in reality it is half raw.
4. Serve on the side in a gravy boat the juice without fat, with some chopped small onions, a strong seasoning of salt and pepper, and a few drops of vinegar.
