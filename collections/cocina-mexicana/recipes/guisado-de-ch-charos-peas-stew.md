## Ingredients

- peas
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- oil
- onion
- oregano
- water
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. After cooking the peas with a little white salt, grind one-third of them with a little pepper
2. Fry this with butter, oil, finely chopped onion, and oregano
3. Once fried, add water and sea salt with the whole peas
4. After seasoning, let cool and stir in three egg yolks, being careful not to break up the egg when heating
