## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- onion
- green chiles
- garlic
- raisins
- capers
- clean almonds
- cooked fish pieces
- parsley
- diluted vinegar
- egg whites
- flour
- egg yolks
- lard
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook tomatoes and chop finely
2. Chop onion finely and clean green chiles
3. Slice garlic, raisins, caperberries and clean almonds
4. Fry everything together
5. When well fried, add cooked fish pieces, capers, chopped parsley and a little diluted vinegar so the sauce is thick
6. Beat egg whites very well until they thicken with a little flour
7. Mix in the yolks, ensuring the egg becomes very thick both from beating and flour
8. Grease the molds inside with lard, add some of the egg mixture, making a small hole in the center
9. Add the filling so the sides remain covered with egg
10. Add a larger piece of fish, covering it with more filling, always ensuring it is surrounded by egg
11. Cover everything well with the egg and cook at two very gentle fires
12. When well set, sprinkle sugar and cinnamon on top
