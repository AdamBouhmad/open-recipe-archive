## Ingredients

- Ducks, cleaned
- Oil and lard in equal parts
- Onion, minced
- Garlic, minced
- Tomatillos, minced
- Black pepper, whole
- Water
- Salt
- Turnips, cleaned and sliced
- Bread, well browned in lard
- Garlic cloves
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the raw ducks in equal parts oil and lard.
2. Once fried, add minced onion, garlic, and tomatillos, whole black pepper, and sufficient water and salt.
3. When nearly cooked, add cleaned, sliced turnips.
4. Once cooked, grind well-browned bread in lard with two garlic cloves in a little vinegar.
5. Add to the ducks and leave over the fire until the broth thickens well.
