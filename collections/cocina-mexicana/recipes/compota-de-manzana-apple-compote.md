## Ingredients

- Apples
- Water
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Cut apples into two or four pieces depending on size
2. Remove the cores
3. Cook in a little water and sufficient sugar
4. Do not wait for it to become marmalade
5. Water with its own broth after reduction
6. Only peel queen apples; for other varieties, lightly prick the skin
