## Ingredients

- Green apricots
- Water
- Soda, two handfuls
- Linen cloth

## Directions

1. Boil water in a pot for an instant with two handfuls of soda
2. Place the apricots there and remove them after the first boil
3. Wash them well, prick them with a pin and place them in cold water
4. Return them to boiling water to blanch them and remove from the fire
5. Wrap them with linen cloth and place them in
