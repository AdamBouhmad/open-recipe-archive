## Ingredients

- offal, in large pieces
- water
- tomato
- onion
- garlic
- lard
- clove, ground
- pepper
- parsley
- oregano
- thyme
- cooked ham, sliced
- chiles
- olives

## Directions

1. Cut the offal into large pieces and cook in a pot
2. Once cooked, cut into pieces, chop tomato, onion and garlic, and fry with plenty of lard
3. Once fried, add the offal, grind clove, pepper, parsley, oregano and thyme, chop everything well, put over heat until very thick
4. Garnish with slices of cooked ham, chiles and olives
