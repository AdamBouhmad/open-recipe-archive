## Ingredients

- lard
- lamb meat
- salt
- onion
- water

## Directions

1. Grease a pan or bowl with lard and arrange the meat for putting in a hot oven
2. Add salt, a little onion and enough water to cover the meat
3. This operation is done at night, and the following morning remove from the oven and remove the broth
4. Add fat and the lard it had, putting embers on the sides of the oven, and turning the meat, leave until it finishes browning
5. In the absence of an oven, roast over two fires, being careful to turn the meat and not break it when it is very tender
