## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Ham
- Chorizo
- Onion
- Garlic
- Lard
- Water
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Egg yolks
- Almonds with shell
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Cut chicken into quarters and place in a casserole with ham and chorizo pieces, chopped onion and garlic with a spoonful of lard
2. Fry until cooked
3. Add water in small quantity with oregano and thyme
4. Grind four to six egg yolks with almonds including shell, fried in lard
5. Add to the casserole
6. Add ground clove, cinnamon and pepper
7. The broth should remain thick
8. Garnish with chiles, olives and capers
