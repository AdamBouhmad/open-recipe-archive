## Ingredients

- 1 quart flour
- 1/2 pound powdered sugar
- very little butter
- salt
- a little orange blossom water
- 2 eggs

## Directions

1. Mix flour with powdered sugar, butter, salt, orange blossom water, and eggs
2. Moisten everything together so the batter is very consistent
3. Knead with a rolling pin or wooden stick
4. Use to make small houses, rocks, etc.
5. Bake in a gentle or warm oven
