## Ingredients

- turnips
- butter
- cream
- sugar
- broth
- gelatin
- broth passed through a sieve

## Directions

1. After cutting the turnips into pieces more or less thick, blanch them
2. Refresh and drain them, then place them immediately to fry in butter
3. Add cream, a little sugar and one tablespoon of broth and let this sauce reduce
4. Remove the turnips from the frying to the bottom as they brown
5. Arrange them on a plate, pouring over them the gelatin that has been loosened and four or five tablespoons of broth passed through a sieve
