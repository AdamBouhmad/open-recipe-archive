## Ingredients

- four ounces melon seeds
- two ounces sweet almonds
- three quartillos of water
- six ounces sugar
- a little grated lemon rind

## Directions

1. Blanch four ounces of melon seeds and two ounces of sweet almonds and grind together in a mortar, rolling them from time to time with a little water so the oil does not form
2. Mix afterwards with three quartillos of water and strain everything with expression through a cloth
3. Add six ounces of sugar and a little grated lemon rind, letting it steep in infusion for half an hour and ensuring the sugar dissolves well
4. Strain again as before and place to freeze in the ice cream maker
