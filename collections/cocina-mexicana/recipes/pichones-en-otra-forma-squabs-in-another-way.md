## Ingredients

- squabs
- ham, slices
- meat broth
- fine herbs
- garlic
- parsley
- black pepper
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Place the squabs in a pot between two slices of ham with meat broth and fine herbs.
2. When cooked, make a sauce with garlic, parsley and pepper, all dissolved in vinegar and poured over top.
