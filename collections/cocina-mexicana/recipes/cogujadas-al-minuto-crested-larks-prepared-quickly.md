## Ingredients

- crested larks, plucked, emptied and cleaned
- butter
- salt
- mushrooms
- shallot
- parsley, chopped
- a small spoonful of flour
- a quarter liter of white wine
- a little broth
- fried croutons for garnish

## Directions

1. Place the plucked, emptied, and cleaned crested larks in a pan with butter and salt
2. Fry until they brown
3. Add chopped mushrooms, shallot, and parsley
4. Once they are well rendered, add a small spoonful of flour, a quarter liter of white wine, and a little broth
5. As soon as they begin to boil, remove from heat and serve adorned with fried croutons
