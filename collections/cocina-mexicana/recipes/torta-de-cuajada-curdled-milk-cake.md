## Ingredients

- Two or three pounds of curdled milk (cuajada)
- Sugar powder
- Syrup at preserve point
- Ground cinnamon
- Mamón made with ground starch
- Butter
- Scented pastille

## Directions

1. Put two or three pounds of cuajada in a sieve and grind it with a molinillo until it breaks down
2. Sweeten with sugar powder
3. In a small pan greased with butter, place a layer of cuajada
4. Sprinkle with preserve-point syrup and cinnamon powder
5. Place another thin layer of mamón made with ground starch
6. Sprinkle with syrup and cinnamon, cover with a thin layer of butter
7. Alternate layers until finishing with one of cuajada
8. Cook over two fires
9. Serve hot dusted with cinnamon powder and scented pastille
