## Ingredients

- a handful of flowers
- three-quarters of a quart of water
- half a pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Crush a handful of flowers and add to three-quarters of a quart of water with half a pound of sugar, mixing well
2. Once the sugar is well dissolved, strain through fine linen and freeze in a sorbet maker
