## Ingredients

- large peas
- tequezquite
- warm water
- lard
- salt
- ancho chile
- garlic
- cumin
- pork pieces
- pork ribs

## Directions

1. Clean the large peas and put them to cook in hot water with a little tequezquite
2. Do not add cold water
3. Once cooked, add warm water and drain three times until the water is clear
4. Add more hot water with a little lard and salt
5. Season until they become thick
6. Soak ancho chile, grind it with garlic and cumin
7. Fry pieces of pork and ribs, then fry the chile very well
8. Add the large peas to season and become thick
