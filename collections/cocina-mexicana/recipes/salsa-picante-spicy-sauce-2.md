## Ingredients

- half a quart of vinegar
- 2 chiltepiquines, ground
- a little fine pepper
- 1 bay leaf
- thyme
- 3 tablespoons of Spanish sauce
- 2 tablespoons of stock

## Directions

1. Put the vinegar, ground chiltepiquines, fine pepper, bay leaf, and thyme in a saucepan
2. Let half of it reduce
3. Then mix in three tablespoons of Spanish sauce and two of stock
4. Let it reduce greatly until it becomes like a thin paste
5. It should have a strong flavor and can be made with red substance instead of Spanish sauce
