## Ingredients

- beef sweetbreads, chopped
- fat ham
- garlic cloves
- some onions
- five or six small hot chiles
- a little nutmeg
- dry Madeira wine
- concentrated or reduced broth

## Directions

1. Chop the beef sweetbreads, ham, and garlic cloves
2. Place all in a cooking pan with some onions, five or six small hot chiles, and a little nutmeg
3. Moisten with dry Madeira wine and concentrated or reduced broth
4. Bring to a very strong boil until it reaches the point of gelatine
5. Place the pan over moderate heat, and as the gelatine begins to set, moisten it with broth and pass through white cloth
