## Ingredients

- bustard
- ham, seasoned with salt and pepper
- nutmeg, grated
- laurel
- lard, pounded
- ham slices

## Directions

1. Empty the bustard and beat its belly and break the leg bones
2. Cover with ham seasoned with salt and pepper, grated nutmeg and laurel
3. Put plenty of pounded lard in the pie and ham slices, because bustard meat is very dry
4. Form the pie as is customary
5. Let it cook for at least four hours
