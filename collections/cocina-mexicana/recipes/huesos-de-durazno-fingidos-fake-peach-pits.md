## Ingredients

- almonds
- pasta for frutillas
- cochineal or bread for coloring
- marmalade of cherries
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Place an almond in the middle of some pasta and make a ball
2. shape into a peach pit form using appropriate molds
3. dye the pasta with cochineal or bread to imitate the color of the pit
4. alternatively, dry cherry marmalade and mix with powdered sugar to make a paste, mold with the almond
5. dry the pits in the stove and keep in a dry place
