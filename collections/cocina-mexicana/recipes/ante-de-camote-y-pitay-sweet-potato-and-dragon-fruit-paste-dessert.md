## Ingredients

- White sweet potato without fibers
- Syrup at medium point
- Dragon fruit
- Orange blossom water
- Sponge cake
- Wine and syrup for moistening
- Raisins and almonds
- Sugar, three pounds per two pounds of sweet potato

## Directions

1. Cook the white sweet potato and grind it well on a metate
2. With the syrup at medium point well clarified, mix in the sweet potato
3. Once it has boiled several times, add a little dragon fruit until it takes color
4. Add a little orange blossom water and put on heat until it takes body
5. Arrange layers of sponge cake sprinkled with wine and syrup, and another of this paste
6. Decorate with raisins and almonds, maintaining the proportion of three pounds of sugar per two pounds of sweet potato
