## Ingredients

- lomo de cerdo
- ajos
- chiles anchos
- sal
- mejorana
- orégano
- laurel
- vinagre
- agua
- manteca
- azúcar
- rabanitos
- aceitunas
- chilitos

## Directions

1. Slice the loin and marinate with garlic, deveined soaked and ground ancho chiles, salt, marjoram, oregano, bay leaf and vinegar
2. The next day, cook adding the precise amount of water for the meat to cook, sufficient lard, and a piece of sugar
3. When cooked it will be dry like all adobo
4. To serve, garnish with small minced radishes opened in water, olives and small chiles
