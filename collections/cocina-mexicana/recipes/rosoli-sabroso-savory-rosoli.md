## Ingredients

- Two quarters of spirit of wine or well-aged brandy
- One quarter of warm water
- One and a half pounds of clarified syrup at low feather point
- Half lemon
- Anise
- Cinnamon
- Cilantro
- Mace

## Directions

1. Mix two quarters of spirit of wine or well-aged brandy with one quarter of warm water
2. Add one and a half pounds of clarified syrup at low feather point
3. Add half lemon, anise, cinnamon, cilantro, and mace
4. Let everything infuse for only four days
5. After that time, filter through a strainer
6. Once purified, it is ready to be drunk
