## Ingredients

- A few almonds without skin
- Cumin
- Corresponding salt
- A little water
- A little oil

## Directions

1. Grind a few almonds without skin, cumin, and the corresponding salt
2. Remove the ground mixture from the molcajete with a little water
3. Add a little oil to it
4. Mix everything together
5. Cover the peppers with this sauce
6. Add a little oil on top
