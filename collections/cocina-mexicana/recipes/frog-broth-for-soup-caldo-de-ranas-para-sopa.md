## Ingredients

- Frog legs and backbones from fifty frogs
- Water
- Carrots
- Leeks
- Turnips
- Parsnips
- Celery
- Toasted onion
- Butter

## Directions

1. Wash the frog legs and backbones from fifty frogs
2. Place in a pot with sufficient water to make soup
3. Add carrots, leeks, turnips, parsnips, a little celery, a toasted onion, and a good piece of butter
4. Let cook over low heat for about four or five hours
5. Use this broth to make soup
