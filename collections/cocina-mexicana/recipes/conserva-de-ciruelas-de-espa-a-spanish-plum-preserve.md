## Ingredients

- Spanish plums
- Water
- Syrup at blown consistency
- Syrup at pearlescent consistency

## Directions

1. As the plums are peeled, place them in cold water
2. Pass them through boiling water and moderate the heat, letting them cook gently until they begin to turn green
3. Remove from heat and let cool in their water, then place in cold water again
4. Make syrup at blown consistency, place the well-drained plums in it, bring to a boil over high heat and skim
5. Remove from heat and let cool in the syrup, then return to heat, keeping it on the fire until the syrup reaches pearlescent point and pour into jars or pots that should not be covered until the preserve has cooled well
6. Note: Gather plums when they begin to ripen so they do not fall apart when cooked. Some prefer to prick them with a pin so the syrup penetrates without letting them boil in it
