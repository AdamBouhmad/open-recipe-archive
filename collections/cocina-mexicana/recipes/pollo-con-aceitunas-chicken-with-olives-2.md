## Ingredients

- Chicken, cut into quarters
- Ham slices
- Chorizos
- Lard, one tablespoon
- Water
- Onion
- Garlic, minced
- Olives without pits
- Fresh parsley
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Fried bread slices in lard
- Oregano
- Thyme
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into quarters and add ham slices and chorizos with one tablespoon of lard
2. Fry well until browned
3. Add a little water
4. In another pan, fry some onion and minced garlic
5. Grind some pitted olives with fresh parsley
6. When well ground, add to the pot with the meat and fry very well
7. Add water
8. Grind cloves, cinnamon, pepper and some fried bread slices in lard
9. Add a little oregano and thyme
10. The broth should be thick
11. Garnish the platter with chiles, olives and capers
