## Ingredients

- Veal loin
- Sliced onion
- Oregano
- Orange peel from China orange
- Capers, ground
- Fried bread, ground
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Thyme
- Laurel

## Directions

1. Arrange the loin as in the previous article and place to cook adding sliced onion, oregano and a little orange peel from China orange.
2. When emptied from the pot into the casserole, and separated the thyme, laurel and orange peel, add capers ground with fried bread and a little oil and vinegar, letting it dry and fry over two fires in its own sauce.
