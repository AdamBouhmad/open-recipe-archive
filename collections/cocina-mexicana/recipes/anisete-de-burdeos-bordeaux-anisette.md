## Ingredients

- 8 quartillos brandy
- 3 ounces green anise
- 2 ounces dry anise
- 0.5 ounce coriander
- 0.5 ounce fennel
- 4 quartillos water
- 3 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. After cracking the seeds, add them with the brandy over a bain-marie to extract 4 quartillos of liqueur; do not let the flame pass through.
2. Dissolve the sugar in water, form the mixture, filter and bottle as with the previous liqueurs.
