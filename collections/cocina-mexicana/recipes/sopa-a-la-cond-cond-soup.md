## Ingredients

- purple beans
- meat broth
- two carrots
- two onions
- pot fat or grease
- two cloves
- bread crusts
- butter

## Directions

1. Cook purple beans in broth
2. Add two carrots, two onions, pot fat or grease, and two cloves
3. When the beans are cooked until they fall apart, pass through a sieve
4. Make a clear substance
5. Pour over bread crusts fried in butter
