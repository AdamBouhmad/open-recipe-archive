## Ingredients

- almonds
- cooked egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon
- clove
- beaten egg
- lard
- syrup
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Soak and grind almonds with an equal volume of cooked egg yolks
2. Mix with sugar, cinnamon and clove, all in powder form and to taste
3. Form the fried bread pieces from this paste
4. Coat with beaten egg and fry
5. Place in good-quality syrup and decorate with pine nuts and sprinkle with cinnamon
