## Ingredients

- leg of venison
- parsley
- ham
- pepper
- clove
- lard
- bay leaf
- tomato
- garlic
- sherry wine

## Directions

1. Debone the leg of venison and slice it
2. Lard it with parsley and ham seasoned with pepper and powdered clove
3. Make additional slits with a knife tip and season these openings with the same spices
4. Line a cazuela with lard and place the larded slices in it
5. Add some bay leaves on top, sliced tomatoes, and one whole head of garlic
6. Pour one cup of sherry wine over everything
7. Cover the cazuela with a comal with heat on top
8. Cook over two fires until the meat is done
