## Ingredients

- lamb chops
- butter
- broth
- salt
- coarse pepper
- chopped shallot
- egg yolks
- nutmeg
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Put the chops in a pan with a piece of butter and fry over moderate heat, turning them from time to time until well cooked
2. Remove the chops from the pan to drain the fat, leaving about half a tablespoon in the pan
3. Add broth and at the same time salt, coarse pepper and chopped shallot
4. When what remained in the pan has come loose from boiling, return the chops with three egg yolks, causing the sauce to bind without boiling
5. At the time of serving add a little grated nutmeg and a splash of vinegar
