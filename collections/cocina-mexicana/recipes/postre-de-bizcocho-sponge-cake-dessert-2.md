## Ingredients

- Equal parts flour and ground sponge cake
- Toasted and ground sesame seeds
- Raisins
- Almonds in small pieces
- Fresh melted butter
- Beaten eggs
- Lard for greasing the pan
- Syrup at high point, well clarified

## Directions

1. Mix equal parts flour and ground sponge cake together well
2. Add toasted and ground sesame seeds, raisins, and almonds in small pieces
3. Add fresh melted butter and beaten eggs
4. Grease a frying pan or casserole with lard and pour in the mixture
5. Let it cook and set over two fires
6. After cooking, pass it through syrup at high point that is well clarified
