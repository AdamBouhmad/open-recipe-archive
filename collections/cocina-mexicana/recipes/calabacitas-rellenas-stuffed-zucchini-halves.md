## Ingredients

- zucchini
- breadcrumbs
- cheese
- salt
- clove
- oil
- egg
- lard

## Directions

1. After cooking, split in half
2. Remove the insides and mix them with breadcrumbs, a little cheese, salt, a pinch of clove and oil
3. Fill the zucchini halves with this mixture
4. Place in a baking pan greased with lard
5. Pour a little beaten egg over each one
6. Bake between two fires until the egg is golden and serve dry
