## Ingredients

- Pork ears
- butter
- twelve large onions
- flour
- half a quarter-liter of vinegar
- one quarter-liter of broth
- salt
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the pork ears with seasoning equal to that of the head and let them cool
2. Divide into small strips and place in a saucepan
3. Cut twelve large onions into half-circles
4. Fry the onions in butter until golden
5. If no sauce is prepared, mix one tablespoon of flour well with the fried onions
6. Add half a quarter-liter of vinegar, one quarter-liter of broth, salt and black pepper
7. Let them boil a few times
8. Pour this sauce over the strips of pork ears
9. Fry everything together and keep hot without boiling until serving time
