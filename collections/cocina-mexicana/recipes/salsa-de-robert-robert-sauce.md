## Ingredients

- butter
- one tablespoon of flour
- four chopped onions
- a piece of butter
- salt
- pepper
- one tablespoon of broth
- one tablespoon of vinegar
- a little mustard

## Directions

1. Put butter with flour and let it brown
2. Add four chopped onions, a piece of butter, salt, pepper and one tablespoon of broth
3. Keep over heat for a quarter hour
4. Before serving, add a tablespoon of vinegar and a little mustard
5. Stir well until everything is dissolved
