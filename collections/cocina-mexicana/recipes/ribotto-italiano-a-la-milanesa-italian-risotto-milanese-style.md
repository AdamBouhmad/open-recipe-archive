## Ingredients

- half a large onion
- butter
- one pound of rice
- broth as needed
- saffron
- Parma cheese or other good cheese, grated
- butter
- white pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Chop half a large onion and fry it gently in a pot with butter until golden
2. Add one pound of rice and the broth as needed to make it burst and serve with a certain consistency, thicker than for ordinary soup
3. Add a little saffron, taking care that it does not stick
4. When the rice has burst according to each person's preference (the Milanese eat it half-cooked), mix in grated Parma cheese or other good cheese and a little butter
5. Remove from heat, stir well and serve, seasoned to taste with white pepper and a little nutmeg
