## Ingredients

- turnips
- cabbage quarters
- parsnips
- carrots
- onions
- duck
- broth
- ham
- herb bundle
- salt

## Directions

1. Parboil for seven or eight minutes: turnips, cabbage quarters, parsnips, carrots and onions, all cut and trimmed neatly
2. Place in a pot with a duck divided into quarters, good broth, a piece of ham cut into slices that are hung from the skin and tied with thread, an assorted herb bundle and a little salt
3. When everything is cooked, arrange the duck with the ham and vegetables around it
4. Skim the broth, bind it with a little concentrated broth and pour it over the duck and vegetables, ensuring the sauce is not too thick and has good flavor.
