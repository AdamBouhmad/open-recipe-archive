## Ingredients

- Two pounds of fine sugar
- half quartillo of virgin honey
- egg white for clarification
- lemon
- fourteen egg whites, well beaten

## Directions

1. Make a syrup from two pounds of fine sugar and half quartillo of virgin honey
2. Clarify with egg white and lemon, strain through cloth, and cook to caramel or panochita stage at a very high point
3. Remove from heat and add a few drops of lemon
4. Have ready fourteen egg whites beaten as much as possible
5. Add less than half of the beaten egg whites little by little while beating vigorously without stopping
6. Place over heat, beating constantly without ceasing until cooked
7. Test and if cooked, remove from heat, add another portion of egg whites, beat, and cook in the same manner
8. Lower heat, add the final egg whites without the oil from their setting, beat and cook as the first and second time
9. The point is reached when it sounds hollow like tamal dough and the bottom of the pot is clean and clear like the stage for cajeta
10. Stir in toasted sesame, raisins, pine nuts and other ingredients as desired or as in the alfajor recipe
