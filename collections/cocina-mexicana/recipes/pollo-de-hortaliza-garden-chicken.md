## Ingredients

- chicken
- oil
- salt
- garlic
- saffron
- agraz or vinegar
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Cut into quarters and fry in a pan with oil.
2. Prepare a pot with water seasoned with salt and crush some garlic with saffron, agraz or a little vinegar, dissolving well with water from the pot, and add everything to it with a handful of grated bread, letting it boil a couple of times.
3. Have the chicken pieces in a pan or baking dish and pour the sauce over it and let them cook a little taking care to stir them frequently; it is a quick and tasty stew.
