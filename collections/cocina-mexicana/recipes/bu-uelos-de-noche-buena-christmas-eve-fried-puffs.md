## Ingredients

- 3 pounds flour
- 12 eggs with whites
- a little more than a cuartillo pulque
- hot water with aniseed
- yeast

## Directions

1. Add to the flour hot water with aniseed that was cooked with the aniseed
2. Dissolve a little yeast in warm water, strain and add everything together and knead well until combined
3. Beat against the metate until it puffs up
4. Butter a pot and let the dough sit in it
5. As it pulls away from the pot, take small pieces and stretch over the knee on a napkin until they reach the necessary size
6. Fry and remove with a stick, putting in syrup or honey with sprinkles
