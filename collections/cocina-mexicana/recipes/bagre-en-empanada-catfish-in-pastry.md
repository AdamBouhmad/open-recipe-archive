## Ingredients

- one large catfish
- cinnamon sticks
- garlic cloves
- aguacate or orange leaves
- vinegar
- clove powder
- cinnamon powder
- pepper powder
- saffron, ground
- cooking oil drops
- generous wine
- semolina dough made from fine grain or bran
- lard

## Directions

1. Clean the large catfish, remove the bones, and stuff it with cinnamon sticks, pieces of garlic, and pieces of aguacate or orange leaves.
2. Make a vinegar broth with clove powder, cinnamon and pepper; add a little ground saffron and a few drops of cooking oil; mix everything well, dissolve in generous wine, and place the fish in this infusion from one day to the next.
3. Form a dough of semolina made from fine grain or bran, and from it make a pastry large enough to contain the fish.
4. Grease the pastry leaves inside and out with lard so that the edges can be sealed; place the fish in it, pour the broth made with wine over it, close the pastry well forming a seal around the edges, and place it in the oven at a gentle heat.
5. When it is time to eat it, remove the top from the pastry and place it on the platter carefully so that it does not break.
