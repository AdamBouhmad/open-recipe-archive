## Ingredients

- one cold loaf of bread
- milk
- twelve eggs
- one pound of fresh butter
- one pound of ground sugar
- half ounce of cinnamon
- raisins
- almonds
- citron

## Directions

1. Soak one cold loaf of bread in milk
2. Beat twelve eggs as if to fry them, and with them beat one pound of fresh warm butter
3. Remove the bread from the milk and break it apart well to mix it with the eggs
4. Add one pound of well-ground sugar, half ounce of cinnamon, raisins, almonds and citron
5. Extend a napkin coated with hard butter, pour everything into it, and tying the four corners, tie it and place in a pot of boiling water to cook
6. When it is firm like a tamale, remove and make slices that are covered with sugar and ground cinnamon
