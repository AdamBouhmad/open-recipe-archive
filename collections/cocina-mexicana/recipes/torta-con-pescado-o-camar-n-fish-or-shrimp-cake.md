## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onion
- Garlic
- Tomatoes
- Green chiles
- Saffron
- Clove
- Cumin
- Black pepper
- Parsley
- Fish or shrimp
- Cooking water
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Eggs
- Sugar
- Rice
- Pear
- Peach
- Quince
- Apple
- Cooked zucchini slices

## Directions

1. Fry in butter finely chopped onion, garlic, tomatoes and green chiles
2. Add saffron, clove, cumin, ground black pepper and chopped parsley
3. Add the fish or shrimp, a little of the cooking water, raisins, almonds, oil and vinegar
4. When well seasoned and dry, remove and incorporate sufficient beaten eggs and sugar
5. In a pan greased with butter, place a thin layer of rice, then a layer of fish or shrimp
6. Add pieces of pear, peach, quince, apple and sliced cooked zucchini
7. Continue layering with rice as the last layer
8. Top with more beaten egg and finish as in the previous recipes
