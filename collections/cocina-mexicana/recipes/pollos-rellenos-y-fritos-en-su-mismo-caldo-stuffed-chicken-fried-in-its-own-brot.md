## Ingredients

- Two chickens
- Picadillo (see picadillo of pork meat for fillings, page 643)
- Mushrooms
- Cow udder
- Four tablespoons of béchamel sauce (see béchamel sauce)
- Whole pepper
- Broth from the chicken

## Directions

1. Clean the chickens and fill them with picadillo.
2. Cook them and let them cool afterwards.
3. Remove the breasts, making a hole lengthwise through the chicken, and cut them into dice-shaped pieces.
4. Mix the diced breast meat with mushrooms cut the same way, and as much as one cow udder, cooked and prepared in the same manner.
5. Put everything in a saucepan with four tablespoons of béchamel sauce in which a little whole pepper has been dissolved, and keep the stew hot in a bain-marie.
6. Heat the chickens in a saucepan with a little of their broth, and at the moment of serving, drain them, arrange them on a plate and place the breast mixture inside the chickens, taking care that it is not too watery.
7. Pour a little loose béchamel sauce over the chickens and serve.
