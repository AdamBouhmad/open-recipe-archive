## Ingredients

- onion heads
- oil
- lard
- vinegar
- salt
- bay leaf
- thyme
- garlic, crushed
- nutmeg
- clove
- pepper
- catfish

## Directions

1. Fry the onion heads in oil and lard in a cazuela (earthen pot)
2. Once they have taken color, add vinegar, salt, bay leaf, thyme, crushed garlic, nutmeg, clove and pepper
3. Place the catfish in this sauce and let it cook
4. Before serving, baste it with oil
