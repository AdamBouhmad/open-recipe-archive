## Ingredients

- four cups of liqueurs or wine
- eight cups of jelly
- proportioned sugar
- one ounce of fish glue

## Directions

1. Use four cups of liqueurs or wine for eight cups of jelly, proportioned sugar, and one ounce of fish glue.
2. Proceed as in the preceding articles.
