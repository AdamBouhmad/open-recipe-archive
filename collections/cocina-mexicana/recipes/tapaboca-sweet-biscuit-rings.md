## Ingredients

- harina (flour)
- manteca (lard)
- azúcar (sugar)

## Directions

1. Make ordinary biscuit dough, typically made with poor quality flour, very little lard, and more sugar.
2. Form the dough into rosquillas (rings) of various sizes.
3. Bake them in the oven on metal sheets.
