## Ingredients

- ground almonds, well dried
- powdered sugar in equal quantity
- beaten egg whites
- orange flower water drops

## Directions

1. Knead ground dry almonds with equal quantity of powdered sugar, moistening little by little with beaten egg whites and some drops of orange flower water, more or less according to desired thickness
2. Evaporate this dough over low heat in a pan
3. Place on bread on the table to cool before using
