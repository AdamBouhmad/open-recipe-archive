## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- epazote
- onion
- sesame
- white bread
- almonds
- water
- lard
- oil
- vinegar
- oregano
- capers
- olives
- chiles

## Directions

1. Cook the fish with salt, epazote and onion.
2. Toast a little sesame and grind with a piece of white bread soaked and a portion of clean almonds.
3. Beat this with a little water and boiling lard, and when it thickens well, fry in lard.
4. When well fried add sufficient salt, oil, vinegar and oregano.
5. Place a little of this broth on a plate, set the fish on top and bathe with the same sauce, adding more oil, vinegar and oregano, and garnish with soaked capers, olives and chiles.
