## Ingredients

- trout
- salt
- cream sauce

## Directions

1. After gutting and washing the trout, prepare well-salted water and pour it into a fish kettle
2. Once the fish is placed in the kettle, allow it to cook over low heat
3. Dress the fish and serve it with cream sauce (see page 779)
