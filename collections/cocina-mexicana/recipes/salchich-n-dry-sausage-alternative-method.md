## Ingredients

- lean short pork meat
- half its weight in beef shreds
- half its weight in bacon
- five ounces of salt per every six pounds of prepared meat
- ground and broken pepper
- three-eighths of niter

## Directions

1. Choose lean short pork meat and add half its weight in beef shreds and half its weight in bacon, which will be cut into pieces while the other two are minced together
2. Season adding five ounces of salt for every six pounds of prepared meat, ground and broken pepper and three-eighths of niter, whose mixture will be blended as well as possible
3. The next morning fill the veal intestines or other widest veal intestines you can get, crushing the meat well with a wooden mallet
4. When well full tie them strongly
5. Put them in a cauldron, letting them bathe in salt mixed with an equal part of niter for eight days
6. After that remove to smoke and bathe with wine dregs in which sage, thyme, laurel and basil have been boiled
7. When dry wrap them in paper to preserve them in ash
