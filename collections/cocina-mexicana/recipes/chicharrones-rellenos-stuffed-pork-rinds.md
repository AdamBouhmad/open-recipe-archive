## Ingredients

- pork rinds, sponged
- water with tequisquite
- minced filling
- lard
- salt
- red wine

## Directions

1. Soak the sponged pork rinds in tequisquite water
2. Wash them
3. Fill them with the minced filling so they have the shape of a pillow
4. Cook them with lard and salt
5. When the broth is consumed, add more red wine so the rinds brown
6. Slice them and serve hot
