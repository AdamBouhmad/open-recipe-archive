## Ingredients

- garlic cloves
- lard
- water
- six testicles
- one cup chopped onion
- half cup washed and chopped tomatoes
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- parsley
- thyme
- cooked ham
- capers
- olives
- oil

## Directions

1. Chop some garlic finely, put a little lard and water in a casserole and fry there until the water is consumed
2. Fry six testicles with one cup of chopped onion, half cup of washed and chopped tomatoes so that the seeds fall out
3. Once fried, chop the testicles and add to the mixture with one spoonful of the broth in which they were cooked
4. Add ground clove, pepper and cinnamon, a little chopped parsley, thyme, sliced cooked ham, capers, olives and a little oil
