## Ingredients

- fresh pork meat covered with fat
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Divide the fresh pork meat covered with fat into pieces
2. Put all these pieces with a small quantity of water in a pan over very hot fire
3. Let the fat melt and the water evaporate
4. Then salt the frying, keeping it on the fire, stirring continuously and pressing the meat pieces with the skimmer as much as possible until the complete evaporation of water and the cracklings remain well cooked and dark in color
5. Take them out, drain them and serve
6. Purify and save the remaining lard for use
