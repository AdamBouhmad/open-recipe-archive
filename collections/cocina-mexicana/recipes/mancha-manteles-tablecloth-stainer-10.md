## Ingredients

- pine nuts
- tomatoes
- garlic
- toasted bread
- clove
- cinnamon
- soaked ancho chiles
- lard
- salt
- poultry and its broth
- cooked small onions
- vinegar
- sugar
- apples
- olives

## Directions

1. Grind together pine nuts, tomatoes, garlic, toasted bread, clove, cinnamon, and soaked ancho chiles
2. Fry in lard with salt
3. Once ready, add the poultry, its broth, cooked small onions, vinegar, sugar, and apples
4. When removing from heat, add olives
