## Ingredients

- peach
- water
- sugar (one and a half libra for each dozen peaches)
- peeled and ground almond (four onzas)
- cream of milk (half a cuartillo)
- egg yolks (twelve), beaten

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches and remove the pits if desired
2. Give them a boil in water and place them in clarified syrup made with a libra and a half of sugar for each dozen peaches
3. When the syrup has penetrated them well, remove them and leave them in the same honey until the following day, when they are taken out and reserved in an appropriate vessel
4. Put the syrup alone on the fire so it reaches the high point of preserve
5. Remove from heat and let cool, then stir in four onzas of peeled and ground almond, half a cuartillo of cream of milk, and twelve beaten egg yolks
6. Return to the fire so it reaches the point of cajeta, and add the peaches to the paste for them to give one boil, then removing them and letting the paste recover the point of cajeta it had
7. Place the peaches on a platter, pour the paste over them, and brown them with a comal with fire from above
