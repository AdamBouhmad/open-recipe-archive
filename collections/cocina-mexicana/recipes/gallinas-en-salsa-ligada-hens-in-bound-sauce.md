## Ingredients

- Lard
- Ground tomatillos and garlic
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Cilantro
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Parras wine
- Hen quarters
- Egg yolks
- Lemon juice
- Ham pieces

## Directions

1. Fry ground tomatillos and garlic in lard
2. Add clove, pepper, cinnamon and cilantro, all ground, vinegar, a little sugar and Parras wine
3. Place the hen quarters in this broth
4. Beat egg yolks with lemon juice and add to the dish with small pieces of ham
5. Let everything simmer together over gentle heat
