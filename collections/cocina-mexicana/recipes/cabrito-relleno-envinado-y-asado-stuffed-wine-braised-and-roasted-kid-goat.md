## Ingredients

- kid goat
- ground meat
- salt
- lard
- ground garlic
- liver of the kid goat
- roasted almonds
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- ginger
- wine
- cooked egg slices
- parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare as in the previous recipe and cook as directed
2. After cooking, make a sauce with the cooked liver of the same kid goat ground with roasted almonds
3. Fry the sauce in lard and season with salt, clove, pepper, cinnamon and ginger
4. Moisten with wine
5. Return to the oven so the sauce dries, taking care it does not burn
6. Garnish on top with slices of cooked egg, minced parsley and quarters of white roman lettuce or curly celery in water
