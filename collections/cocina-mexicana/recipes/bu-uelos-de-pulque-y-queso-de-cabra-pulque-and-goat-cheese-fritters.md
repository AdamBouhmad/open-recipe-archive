## Ingredients

- four pounds of flour
- a little pulque
- pound and a half of butter
- twenty-four egg yolks
- a little anise water
- a little tequesquite (lime)
- one or half goat cheese, according to size
- fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Knead four pounds of flour with a little pulque, pound and a half of butter, twenty-four egg yolks, a little anise water and tequesquite, one or half goat cheese
2. After everything is well kneaded, beat the dough against a wooden tray or table until it shows eyes, which is the sign of being ready
3. After keeping it some time in the sun, make the fritters
4. Crumble fresh cheese, desalting it in water for about an hour
5. Press out the water with a napkin and, leaving it well aired, grind it and gradually mix flour in sufficient quantity to form a dough
6. Make very thin little tortillas from the dough
7. When all the tortillas made from the dough are ready, put them in very hot lard and keep basting them without ceasing with the same lard by means of a little spoon, which is done gently, for with the blow of the lard they will rise, and it is necessary to care that they do not break
