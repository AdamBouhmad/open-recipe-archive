## Ingredients

- longaniza sausage
- butter
- onions
- tomatoes
- lean ham
- fat ham
- pepper
- clove
- salt
- white wine
- beaten eggs
- chile powder

## Directions

1. Remove the meat from the longaniza casing and fry it in butter
2. Remove from the butter and fry in it chopped onions and tomatoes, and pieces of lean and fat ham
3. When the ham is golden, add pepper, clove, salt and a little white wine
4. Put the longaniza back in the pan, stir everything together, remove from heat and mix with beaten eggs as for frying
5. Butter a pie dish, pour everything in, and cook the pie to set at gentle heat
6. When it is set on the bottom, put heat in a griddle on top; when the pie is well set, serve with chile powder on the plate
