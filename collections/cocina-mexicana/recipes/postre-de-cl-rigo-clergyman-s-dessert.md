## Ingredients

- 4 quarter-measures of milk
- 6 purple camotes
- 3 ounces of almonds
- sugar to taste
- orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Mix four quarter-measures of milk, six purple camotes, three ounces of almonds and sugar to taste, all ground
2. When well dissolved, put over the fire so that it reaches the point of manjar blanco
3. In that case add orange blossom water
4. Put alone on the plate or over sponge cake
