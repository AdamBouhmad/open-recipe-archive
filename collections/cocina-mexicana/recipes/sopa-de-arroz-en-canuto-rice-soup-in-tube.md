## Ingredients

- Rice
- Beef marrow
- Onion
- Garlic
- Butter
- Salt
- Broth or water
- Two egg yolks

## Directions

1. After soaking and defleeming the rice well, place a pot on the fire with beef marrow
2. Fry finely chopped onion and garlic in it, and when it has browned, add the rice, sufficient butter, and salt
3. Stir the rice, and when browned add a little broth or water
4. When it is consumed, add another little bit of liquid, and continue adding liquid thus until the rice cooks well
5. Then add two beaten egg yolks and place over two low fires
6. Stir well so the egg does not curdle, and keep it this way until time to serve
