## Ingredients

- cod
- oil
- potatoes
- water
- garlic
- parsley
- pepper
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Soak the cod, then cut into pieces and fry in oil
2. When well browned add water and some potatoes and let everything cook
3. Grind fried garlic, parsley, pepper and a bit of bread to thicken the broth
4. Serve after a while
