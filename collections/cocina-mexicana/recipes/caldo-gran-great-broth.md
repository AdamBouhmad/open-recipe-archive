## Ingredients

- a piece of beef, more or less large
- whole garlic cloves
- roasted onions
- pennyroyal
- a sprig of fresh cilantro
- toasted and ground cilantro with the other spices
- cumin
- turnips
- parsnips
- leeks

## Directions

1. Cook a piece of beef in water
2. After skimming, season with whole garlic cloves, roasted onions, pennyroyal, fresh cilantro sprig, and toasted and ground cilantro mixed with the other spices and cumin
3. Add the ordinary vegetables: turnips, parsnips, and leeks
4. When the meat is well cooked, pass the broth through a sieve and keep it for use as needed
5. Very good soups can also be made with this broth
