## Ingredients

- corn
- anise water, boiled
- water with settled teqesquite
- cold lard, beaten

## Directions

1. Sift the corn and place in a batea (large wooden trough)
2. Add boiled anise water and settled teqesquite water, all cold
3. Beat much with both hands
4. Add beaten cold lard and clean water
