## Ingredients

- beef or lamb tongue
- salt
- boiling water
- Castile vinegar
- 4 to 6 garlic cloves, peeled
- onions, in quarters
- wide chile, deveined
- pepper, ground
- cinnamon
- bay leaves
- butter, size of one egg
- bread slices, toasted in butter
- equal parts broth and tongue cooking broth
- other cooked substances, ground and strained

## Directions

1. Clean the tongue well and cook with necessary salt
2. When almost cooked, add equal quantities of boiling water and Castile vinegar
3. Skin the tongue and cut into one-inch pieces
4. Add four or six peeled garlic cloves, the same number of onions in quarters, one wide deveined chile, pepper ground and cinnamon, bay leaves and a little salt, and add a piece of butter the size of an egg
5. Cover the pot and let everything boil until almost cooked
6. Fry the tongue pieces cold and coated with beaten egg, taking care they do not mix together
7. Arrange necessary toasted bread slices fried in butter
8. Bathe with equal parts broth from the pot and the broth in which the tongue was cooked, mixed with the other substances that were also cooked, ground and strained through a sieve
9. When almost all the broth is consumed, remove and decorate with the fried tongue pieces
