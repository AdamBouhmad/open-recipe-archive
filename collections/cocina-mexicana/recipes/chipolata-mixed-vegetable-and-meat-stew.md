## Ingredients

- two dozen carrots
- two dozen turnips
- equal quantity of chestnuts
- equal quantity of onions
- a little sugar
- concentrated broth
- twelve small sausages
- twelve pieces of ham
- two dozen mushrooms
- several spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
- consummate broth or cooking stock

## Directions

1. Parboil two dozen carrots and the same number of turnips, cutting and turning them into the shape of olives
2. Prepare an equal quantity of chestnuts and the same of onions
3. Cook them together with a little sugar in concentrated broth
4. Cook separately twelve small sausages and the same number of pieces of ham
5. Put everything in a pot with two dozen mushrooms and several spoonfuls of Spanish sauce
6. Moisten with consummate broth or cooking stock
7. Let it reduce to the proper point, being careful to skim it well and clarify it afterward
