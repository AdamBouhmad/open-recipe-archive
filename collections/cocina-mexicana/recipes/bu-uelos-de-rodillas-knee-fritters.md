## Ingredients

- One pound of fine flour
- Eight egg yolks
- A little salt
- A little warm water
- White tequesquite settled
- A few drops of lemon
- A little raw lard
- A small piece of yeast dissolved in warm water
- Half pozuelo of aniseed water
- One whole pozuelo of brandy

## Directions

1. Add the egg yolks to the flour with salt, warm water, tequesquite, lemon drops, raw lard, yeast dissolved in warm water, aniseed water, and brandy
2. Mix everything well and beat the dough against a metate until it forms eyes or blisters and comes completely away from the stone
3. Form the fritters and fry them in lard
