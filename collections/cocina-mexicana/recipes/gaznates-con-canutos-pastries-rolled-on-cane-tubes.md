## Ingredients

- flour
- water with salt
- lard for frying
- filling: custard, huevos moles, or spun sugar threads
- syrup
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Make dough as for hojuelas, knead well, and stretch it.
2. Wrap around cane tubes and fry in lard.
3. Once cooled, remove the tubes.
4. Fill with custard, huevos moles, or spun sugar threads.
5. Top with syrup, raisins, and almonds.
