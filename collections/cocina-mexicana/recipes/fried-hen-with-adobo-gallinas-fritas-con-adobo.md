## Ingredients

- hen, boned and cooked the day before, cut into pieces
- vinegar
- broth
- salt
- parsley
- onions
- garlic, crushed
- bay leaf
- flour
- oil for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare an adobo with vinegar, broth, salt, parsley, onions, crushed garlic, and bay leaf
2. Place the pieces of boned and cooked hen in the adobo
3. Keep them in the adobo for two or more hours over hot coals
4. Remove and drain them
5. Coat in flour
6. Fry them
7. Serve with fried parsley
