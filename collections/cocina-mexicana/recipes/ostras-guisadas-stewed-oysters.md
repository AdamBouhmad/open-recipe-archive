## Ingredients

- oysters
- chopped garlic
- oil
- breadcrumbs
- clove
- pepper
- water
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Soak the oysters in hot water for a day
2. Remove them from the water and clean off the mucilage in the middle and the small extremities around the edges
3. Fry chopped garlic in oil
4. Add a handful of breadcrumbs until well browned, with a little clove and pepper
5. Add water and a few drops of lemon juice
6. Place the oysters there to cook
