## Ingredients

- Lamb meat
- Pork loin from the head
- Bread soaked in vinegar
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Saffron
- Cumin
- Salt
- Parsley
- Grated biscuit
- Egg yolks
- Garlic
- Toasted bread
- Ripe tomatoes
- Vinegar
- Lard
- Roasted lamb
- Broth or water
- Cooked onions
- Olives
- Chilies
- Oregano

## Directions

1. Take two parts lamb meat and one part pork loin from the head and mince very well.
2. After mincing, add a piece of bread soaked in vinegar and grind everything together very well, removing all tendons and skin that resist grinding.
3. Once ground, regrind with clove, pepper, cinnamon, saffron, and a little cumin.
4. Add salt according to taste and mix with well-chopped parsley and a little grated biscuit.
5. Form into balls the size of a walnut with an egg yolk in the center of each.
6. Fry in lard until golden.
7. Make a sauce by sautéing garlic cloves until golden and remove; toast a piece of bread; roast ripe tomatoes and grind everything with a little vinegar; fry in the lard in which the garlic browned.
8. Once well fried, grind clove, pepper, cinnamon, and cumin; add the roasted lamb and turn in the frying mixture.
9. Add sufficient cooking oil, some meat broth or water, cooked onion pieces, olives, chilies, and a little oregano.
10. Let it thicken sufficiently.
