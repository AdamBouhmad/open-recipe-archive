## Ingredients

- Black salsify roots, prepared and cooked
- White sauce, well seasoned
- Frying batter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Vinegar
- Egg, beaten
- Flour
- Lemon slices
- Tomato broth

## Directions

1. Prepare and cook the salsify as described in the previous recipe and let drain
2. Make a well-seasoned white sauce and fry the salsify in it
3. Place immediately in a dish to cool
4. At the moment of serving, coat with frying batter and fry
5. Optionally, dip briefly in a bowl with salt, pepper, and vinegar before battering
6. Alternatively, coat with beaten egg after dusting with flour, and serve fried dry with salt, pepper, and lemon slices on the side, or in tomato broth
