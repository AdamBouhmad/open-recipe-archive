## Ingredients

- one pound sweet almonds soaked in cold water overnight
- a handful of bitter almonds
- twenty egg yolks
- ground sugar powder
- sixteen egg whites
- two tablespoons ground starch
- ground biscuit
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak one pound of sweet almonds in cold water overnight
2. Grind well and mix with a handful of ground bitter almonds
3. Mix everything with twenty egg yolks, beaten more than for royal eggs
4. Add sugar powder so the mixture is heavily sweetened
5. Add sixteen beaten egg whites and two tablespoons ground starch
6. Mix everything with ground biscuit
7. Pour into a buttered pan
8. Cook at two fires until a straw inserted comes out clean
