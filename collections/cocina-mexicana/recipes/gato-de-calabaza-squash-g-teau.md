## Ingredients

- squash
- milk
- butter
- potato starch
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the squash and cut into small pieces, which are put to cook in a pot with a little milk
2. When cooked, press with cloth to extract the liquid that would make the gâteau too watery
3. Cook more with butter, adding potato starch, dissolved in milk with sugar
4. Let boil gently
5. When it has the proper consistency, remove and let cool, continuing the operation as in the following article
