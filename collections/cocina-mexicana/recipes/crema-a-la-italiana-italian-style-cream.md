## Ingredients

- tres cuartillos de leche (three quarters of milk)
- cáscara de limón verde (green lemon rind)
- cilantro (coriander)
- canela (cinnamon)
- cinco onzas de azúcar (five ounces of sugar)
- dos granos de sal (two grains of salt)
- harina (flour)
- seis yemas de huevo (six egg yolks)

## Directions

1. Boil three quarters of milk in a saucepan
2. Add a small amount of green lemon rind, which can be taken with three fingers of coriander, a small piece of cinnamon and five ounces of sugar with two grains of salt
3. Continue boiling until the milk is reduced to half
4. Let cool a little
5. In another saucepan, dissolve a small amount of flour, about a scant coffee spoonful, in six egg yolks
6. Pour the cream there little by little, stirring at the same time
7. Pass through a sieve onto the plate in which it should be served, allowing it to set in a bain-marie
8. Before taking it to the table, pass a red-hot shovel over it so that it takes on color
