## Ingredients

- Chopped parsley
- Half of a ground nutmeg
- Juice of lemon or sour orange
- Egg yolks
- Seasoned broth

## Directions

1. Place chopped parsley, half a ground nutmeg, and lemon or sour orange juice in a pot
2. Add egg yolks one at a time, stirring immediately so they do not curdle
3. When three egg yolks per portion have been added, place over gentle heat
4. Immediately add seasoned broth, stirring constantly until thickened
5. Remove from heat and strain into a sauce dish
