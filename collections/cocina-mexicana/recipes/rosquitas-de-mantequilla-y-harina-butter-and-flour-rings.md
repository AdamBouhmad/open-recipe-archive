## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- half pound of lard
- half pound of butter
- half pound of sifted sugar
- eight egg yolks
- a small measure of warm water

## Directions

1. Incorporate with two pounds of flour half a pound of lard, half of butter, the same of sifted sugar, eight egg yolks and a small measure of warm water
2. Once everything is well mixed and kneaded, form the rings
3. Sprinkle cracked sugar on top and place them on papers
4. Put them in the oven to toast
