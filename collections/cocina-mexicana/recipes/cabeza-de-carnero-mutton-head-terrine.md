## Ingredients

- Cooked mutton head
- Pork loin
- Tomato
- Garlic
- Onion
- Oil
- Butter
- Lemon
- Bread
- Pepper

## Directions

1. After the head is cooked, remove all the bones.
2. The meat, brains and tongue, along with a piece of cooked pork loin, is chopped into small pieces.
3. Fry a little chopped tomato, garlic and onion in oil and butter.
4. Once fried, mix in the meat and set aside.
5. Grease a casserole with butter.
6. Spread the meat in it like a cake, making sure it is hollow in the center.
7. Pour a few drops of lemon juice over the top, grated bread mixed with ground pepper in the dry, a little more oil and butter.
8. Place between two fires until browned.
