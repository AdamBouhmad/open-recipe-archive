## Ingredients

- Sponge cake pieces
- Egg
- Syrup of medium point with sufficient wine
- Almonds
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Hollow out some sponge cake pieces from the top
2. Fill them with any paste
3. Fry them in beaten egg
4. Place them in syrup of medium point with sufficient wine
5. Decorate with almonds, cinnamon, etc.
