## Ingredients

- garlic cloves
- wide chiles, deveined
- walnuts, cleaned
- peanuts, cleaned
- lard
- oil
- meat, poultry, or fish
- broth

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves halved and one or two wide deveined chiles in lard with oil; the chiles are only to provide color to the nogada
2. Grind the garlic and chiles together with walnuts and peanuts in equal parts
3. Fry everything in the same oil and lard in which the garlic and chile were fried
4. Add the meats, poultry or fish and the broth in which they were cooked to this frying
5. Let it thicken as needed until it has the consistency of nogada
