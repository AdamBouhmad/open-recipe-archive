## Ingredients

- four egg yolks
- one pound of ground sugar
- two pounds of lard
- two cups of water
- salt
- three pounds of flour
- orange juice
- oil

## Directions

1. Beat four egg yolks in a tub with one pound of ground sugar and two pounds of lard
2. Bring two cups of water to boil with a pinch of salt, remove from heat, and when the water is warm, add the mixture and continue beating until it no longer sticks to the hand
3. Then add three pounds of flour and knead everything very well to roll out
4. Add orange juice and oil
5. Cut the empanadas before adding the minced filling
