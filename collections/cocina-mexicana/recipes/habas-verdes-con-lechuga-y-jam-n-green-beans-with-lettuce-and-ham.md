## Ingredients

- Small, tender green beans
- Water
- Salt
- Lettuce, chopped
- Lard
- Ham slices
- Garlic, chopped
- Onions, chopped
- Broth
- Clove
- Pepper
- Poached eggs (optional)

## Directions

1. Choose small and tender beans and cook them in salted water, also cooking chopped lettuce.
2. In a separate pot, fry ham slices in plenty of lard until they become like chicharrones (crispy bits); remove the ham from the lard.
3. Fry very finely chopped garlic and onions in the same lard.
4. Add the cooked and well-drained beans and lettuce with a little salt, and fry.
5. Cover with broth, add the fried ham, and ground clove and pepper.
6. Let everything simmer until most of the broth is consumed.
7. If desired, serve with poached eggs: when the broth is simmering, break the eggs and drop them in one side so they set and cook without stirring or mixing with the beans.
