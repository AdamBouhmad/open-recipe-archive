## Ingredients

- six chayotes
- water
- sugar
- cinnamon
- four eggs
- four tablespoons butter
- ground biscuit
- lard

## Directions

1. Prepare six chayotes that are in season and blanch with water
2. Drain and let cool
3. Split lengthwise in half
4. Extract the inner pulp carefully so that the shell remains intact
5. Make a mass from the extracted pulp and add a cup of ground white sugar, a little cinnamon, four beaten eggs, and four tablespoons of butter
6. Once well mixed, fill the shells and dust the top with ground biscuit
7. Heat lard in a clay pot, cover with a comal with coals on top and let them brown
8. Remove from heat and let cool a little before serving
