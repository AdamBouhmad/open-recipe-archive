## Ingredients

- Eight or ten wide chiles
- Cloves
- Dried coriander
- Good vinegar
- Veal meat
- Lemon slices
- Sour lime
- Oregano
- Salt
- Ham
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Spices except saffron
- Hard egg yolk
- Fine breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Devein eight or ten wide chiles, wash them changing the water several times, and grind with two or three cloves and toasted dried coriander
2. Dilute this paste in good vinegar and place the veal meat there, adding slices of lemon and sour lime, a handful of oregano, and plenty of salt
3. The next day remove the veal pulps from this marinade and place to cook after larding with ham, clove and cinnamon, in good vinegar and with all spices ground, except saffron
4. When the meat is nearly cooked, bind the broth with a hard egg yolk crumbled and a little fine breadcrumbs, setting aside as soon as cooked
