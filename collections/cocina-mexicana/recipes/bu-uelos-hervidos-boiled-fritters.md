## Ingredients

- water
- aniseed
- salt
- lard
- one pound of fine flour
- sixteen eggs

## Directions

1. Boil a little water in a small pot with a little aniseed, salt, and lard
2. When boiling, gradually add one pound of fine flour so it does not form lumps, and leave it until it reaches the right consistency, which is known by taking a little bit of dough and beating it in the hand; if it does not stick, then it is ready
3. Remove from heat and pour on a napkin to cool
4. Then knead with sixteen eggs and take portions with the spoon that will cook in plenty of lard, but not very hot
