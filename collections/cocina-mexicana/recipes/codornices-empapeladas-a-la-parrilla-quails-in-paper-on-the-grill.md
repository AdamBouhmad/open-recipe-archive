## Ingredients

- quails
- salt
- water
- ham
- oil
- breadcrumbs
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Cook the quails in salted water
2. Drain them and lard with ham
3. Brush with oil and cover with breadcrumbs mixed with salt and pepper powder
4. Wrap in well-oiled papers
5. Place on a grill over very gentle heat so the papers do not burn
6. Once they are golden, remove and serve
