## Ingredients

- 1-2 pounds of peas
- butter
- parsley bouquet
- onions
- sugar
- salt water
- flour

## Directions

1. Wash the peas and mash them in a pot with butter
2. Add a bunch of parsley, onions, and a little sugar
3. Place over good heat and moisten with clear, light salt water
4. When the water has reduced and the peas are cooked, remove the parsley
5. Take off the heat and thicken with a good piece of butter and a pinch of flour
