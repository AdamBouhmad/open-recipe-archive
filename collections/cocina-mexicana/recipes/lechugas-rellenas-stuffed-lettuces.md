## Ingredients

- lettuces
- fine poultry stuffing
- two pounds beef pulp
- ham strips
- sliced onions
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- cloves
- bay leaf
- basil
- parsley
- whole onions

## Directions

1. Parboil the lettuces for a moment; drain them and open the leaves without removing the stem until reaching the heart.
2. Remove the heart and in its place put a piece of fine poultry stuffing and tie the lettuces.
3. Slice two pounds of beef pulp and line a pan with it and with strips of ham and sliced onions.
4. Let them sweat on the fire and add a little flour.
5. When it begins to stick, stir it with a spoon on the burner so it browns a little.
6. Moisten with broth and stock in equal parts, and season with salt, pepper, cloves, bay leaf, basil, parsley and whole onions.
7. Place the stuffed lettuces in a pot; put the cooked mixture on top, add the broth and let the lettuces cook.
