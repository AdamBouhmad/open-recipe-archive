## Ingredients

- Two parts pork loin
- One part mutton pulp
- One quarter almonds, peeled
- Piece of bread
- Four egg yolks
- Nutmeg
- Pepper
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Jerez wine
- Two eggs with whites
- Salt
- Parsley
- Mint
- Tomato broth
- Onion
- Parsley
- Mint
- Green chiles
- Squeezed lemon
- Ground almonds

## Directions

1. Mince and grind well two parts pork loin and one part mutton pulp together with one quarter peeled almonds, a piece of bread and four egg yolks
2. Grind separately nutmeg, pepper, clove and cinnamon
3. Mix everything, adding a little Jerez wine and two eggs with their whites
4. Mix well and form the meatballs, filled as in the previous article
5. Cook with salt, a little parsley and mint
6. When half cooked, place in a tomato broth with minced onion, parsley and mint, green chiles and squeezed lemon, thickening with ground almonds
7. When the broth is sufficiently thick, they can be served
