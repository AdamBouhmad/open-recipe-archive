## Ingredients

- Turkey giblets (wings, feet, neck, and gizzard)
- Lard
- Bundle of parsley
- Onion
- Garlic
- Two cloves
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Flour
- Broth from the pot or substance broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Turnips

## Directions

1. After well blanching and removing quills from all parts, place in a pan with lard, a small bundle of parsley, onion, garlic, two cloves, thyme and bay leaf
2. Place on fire and add a good spoonful of flour, add broth from the pot or substance broth, season as needed and add some turnips, which are first sautéed in the pan until they have a golden color
3. Put them to cook, and if the sauce is too thick, let it reduce on the fire
