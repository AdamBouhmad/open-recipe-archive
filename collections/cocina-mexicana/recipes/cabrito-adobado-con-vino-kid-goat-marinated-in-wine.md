## Ingredients

- hind quarter of kid goat
- thick bacon strips
- wine
- broth
- onions
- garlic
- thyme
- laurel
- mixed herbs
- salt
- spices

## Directions

1. Lard the hind quarter with thick strips
2. Prepare a marinade with equal parts wine and broth, with onions, garlic, thyme, laurel, a mixed herb bundle, salt and spices
3. Add bacon slices to the marinade
4. Leave marinating for five or six hours over the fire
5. When well cooked, serve with a spicy sauce made from the same broth strained through a sieve
