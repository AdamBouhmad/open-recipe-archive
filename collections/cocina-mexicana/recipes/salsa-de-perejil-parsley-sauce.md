## Ingredients

- parsley leaves
- garlic
- bread crumb
- vinegar
- salt
- sugar
- cold water

## Directions

1. Gather a handful of parsley leaves, three or four peeled garlic cloves and a piece of cold bread crumb soaked in vinegar
2. Grind everything
3. Lower it from the metate with vinegar, adding as much as necessary so that it is not too thick
4. Season with salt and sugar, trying to use the latter to reduce the strength of the vinegar, and also add a little cold water
