## Ingredients

- Beaten egg yolks
- Toasted and ground mamón
- Sifted sugar
- Cinnamon powder
- Melted and cold butter
- Water

## Directions

1. Make a dough of proper consistency by mixing beaten egg yolks with toasted ground mamón, sifted sugar, cinnamon powder and melted cold butter
2. Taste the dough to ensure it is well seasoned, adjusting cinnamon and sugar to taste
3. Pour into molds or cups without filling more than three-quarters of their height
4. Cover with a pot lid or a small cake pan with heat on top
5. Test doneness using a straw, and when cooked, remove and let cool
6. If made in molds, unmold and serve with syrup and cinnamon powder; if made in cups, serve in the same cups dusted with cinnamon
