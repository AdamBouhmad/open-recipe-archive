## Ingredients

- Chiles (dried red chiles), most colorful
- Tomatillos (green tomatoes)
- Chicken
- Epazote
- Corn dough (masa)
- Water

## Directions

1. Put three chiles to cook with half a cup of tomatillos
2. Boil a chicken and then remove veins from the chiles, grind them with the tomatillos and fry everything in a small cazuela
3. Once well fried, add to the pot in which the chicken is boiling
4. Add a small sprig of epazote
5. If it is too spicy, dissolve a little corn dough in water and add about a cup of it and keep sufficient broth
