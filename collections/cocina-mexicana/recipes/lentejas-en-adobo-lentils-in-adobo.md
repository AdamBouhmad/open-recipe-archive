## Ingredients

- lentils
- wide chiles
- butter
- water
- salt
- clove
- pepper
- cumin
- bread
- vinegar
- onions

## Directions

1. Cook the lentils as in the previous recipe.
2. Remove the veins from the wide chiles and toast them well.
3. Grind them and fry in a little butter.
4. Add a little water and season with the necessary salt.
5. Then add the lentils and grind a little clove, pepper, cumin and a piece of bread soaked in vinegar.
6. Pour all the ground mixture into the pot so that it boils until it is seasoned and then add another little bit of vinegar.
7. When it has thickened, add two or three onions cut into quarters and let them boil a few times.
