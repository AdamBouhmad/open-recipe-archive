## Ingredients

- fresh butter
- parsley, chopped
- onion, chopped
- mushrooms, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- blackfish

## Directions

1. Melt a little fresh butter in a pot, adding chopped parsley, onion, and mushrooms, with salt and pepper.
2. Place the blackfish there with the necessary preparations and season with the same ingredients that were put underneath.
3. Cover the pot well and let cook over gentle fire.
4. Serve with its broth, which should not be allowed to thicken, adding a splash of verjuice.
