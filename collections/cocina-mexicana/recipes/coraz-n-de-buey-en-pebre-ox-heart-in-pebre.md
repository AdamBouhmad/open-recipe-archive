## Ingredients

- ox heart
- oil
- onions
- parsley
- lemon juice
- cinnamon
- clove
- pepper
- ginger
- nutmeg
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Slice the ox heart and marinate for many days in oil with onions, parsley, lemon juice, cinnamon, clove, pepper, ginger, nutmeg and bay leaf
2. At the moment of serving, grill on the grill and place on pebre broth
