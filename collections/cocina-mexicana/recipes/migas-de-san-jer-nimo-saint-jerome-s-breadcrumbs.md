## Ingredients

- one loaf of cold bread
- half a pound of lard
- four crushed biscuits
- sugar
- cinnamon powder
- red wine
- toasted sesame seeds
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Soak a loaf of cold bread and then squeeze it out
2. Gradually make it absorb half a pound of lard and four crushed biscuits
3. Mix in sugar and cinnamon powder
4. As it dries, sprinkle with red wine; it should remain juicy
5. Add toasted sesame seeds, raisins, and almonds
