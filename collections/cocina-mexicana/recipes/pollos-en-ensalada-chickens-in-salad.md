## Ingredients

- chickens
- pork loin
- salt
- whole spices
- lettuce
- beets
- carrots
- tomatoes
- garlic
- onions
- green chiles
- parsley
- broth
- bread
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens with pork loin, salt and whole spices.
2. Chop lettuce not very finely, also cooked beets and carrots.
3. Chop tomatoes, garlic, onions, green chiles and parsley.
4. Pour the broth in which the chickens were cooked, slice the pork loin, quarter the chickens and add them to the broth with the chopped lettuce, carrots and beets, and sliced fruits.
5. Let everything boil, and near the end, add fine spices, ground with a piece of bread and a little salt.
6. When serving, add plenty of oil and vinegar.
