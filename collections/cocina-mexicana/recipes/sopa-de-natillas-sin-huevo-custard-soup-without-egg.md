## Ingredients

- butter
- mamon, sliced and cold
- milk, boiled
- sugar
- cinnamon
- custard

## Directions

1. Butter a pot well
2. Make layers alternating slices of cold mamon sprinkled with milk boiled with sugar and cinnamon, and layers of custard, continuing in this order until the last layer is custard
3. Add more sweetened milk
4. Cook the soup over hot coals
5. Serve dusted with cinnamon
