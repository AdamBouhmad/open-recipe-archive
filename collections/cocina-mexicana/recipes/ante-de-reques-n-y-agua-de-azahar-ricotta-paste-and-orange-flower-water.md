## Ingredients

- half an ounce of ground cinnamon
- two medium ricottas
- three pounds of clarified syrup
- orange flower water
- mamey slices
- egg white

## Directions

1. Add half an ounce of ground cinnamon and two medium ricottas to three pounds of clarified syrup, separating a little of which
2. Beat and place to cook over heat
3. To the separated syrup add a little orange flower water
4. With it wet the mamey slices, with which layers will be made, alternating them with others of ricotta
5. On the last one place syrup with beaten egg whites, which will be cooked by brushing with ground cinnamon with a comal with heat
