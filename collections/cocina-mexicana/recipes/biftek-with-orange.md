## Ingredients

- beef pulp, sliced very thin
- water
- butter
- oil
- vinegar
- juice of three or four cooked oranges
- orange slices
- ham slices
- two wide chiles
- garlic
- bay leaf
- thyme
- oregano
- salt
- pepper
- achiote
- clove
- cinnamon
- capers
- olives

## Directions

1. Slice the beef very thin
2. Prepare a dish with plenty of water with butter, a little oil and vinegar, juice of three or four cooked oranges, sliced ham, two wide chiles, garlic, bay leaf, thyme, oregano, salt, pepper, a piece of achiote, clove and cinnamon
3. Heat until the meat partially cooks
4. When ready to serve, add capers and olives
