## Ingredients

- Lard
- Oil
- Garlic cloves, halved
- One or two wide red chiles, deveined
- Cleaned walnuts
- Cleaned peanuts
- Meat, poultry, or fish
- Broth in which the meat was cooked

## Directions

1. Fry halved garlic cloves and one or two wide red chiles that are deveined in lard with oil; these serve only to give color to the nogada
2. Grind the garlic and chiles together with cleaned walnuts and cleaned peanuts in equal parts
3. Fry everything in the same oil and lard in which the garlic and chile were fried
4. In this frying put the meats, poultry or fish and the broth in which they were cooked
5. Let it thicken as needed until it has the consistency of nogada
