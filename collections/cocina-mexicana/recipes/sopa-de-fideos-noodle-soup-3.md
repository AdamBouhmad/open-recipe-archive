## Ingredients

- broth from the pot or meat essence
- fideos (thin noodles), 1 ounce per person

## Directions

1. Bring the broth from the pot or meat essence to a boil
2. Add the fideos after breaking them so they don't clump together
3. Cook for at most ten minutes
4. Remove and let them continue boiling slowly at the edge of the heat
5. Stir frequently and ensure they don't cook too much
