## Ingredients

- mojarra fish
- oil or lard
- garlic
- egg yolks, cooked and ground
- capers, ground
- water
- oil
- clove
- pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the mojarra in oil or lard
2. Separately, fry minced garlic with cooked and ground egg yolks and ground capers
3. Once this sauce is fried, add water to it
4. It should be thick and when serving, add oil, clove and pepper
