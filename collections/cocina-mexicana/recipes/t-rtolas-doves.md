## Ingredients

- Doves
- Tomato, chopped
- Garlic
- Onion
- Bread, fried in butter
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Thyme
- Bay leaf
- Marjoram
- Water
- Cooked peas

## Directions

1. Eviscerate and wash the doves.
2. Fry chopped tomato, garlic and onion; once half-fried, place the doves there to parboil as well and add some bread fried in butter, clove, cinnamon and pepper in small quantity, all ground, thyme, bay leaf and marjoram.
3. Add sufficient water for cooking and once cooked and of regular thickness, mix in cooked peas to serve.
