## Ingredients

- pork meat
- salt
- lard
- green plantains

## Directions

1. Take the pork meat and salt it well; after it has taken the salt, cut it into regular pieces
2. Fry it in lard in a saucepan; once it is fried, remove it and place it on a platter
3. In the same fat from the saucepan, add green plantains cut into slices
4. When they are fried, place them on top of the pieces of pork meat and serve with white rice
