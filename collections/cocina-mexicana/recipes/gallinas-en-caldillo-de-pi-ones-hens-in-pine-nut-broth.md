## Ingredients

- Pine nuts (cleaned)
- Lard
- Jerez wine
- Hen broth
- Ground cinnamon
- Flour (browned in lard)
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Toasted sesame
- Cooked hen quarters
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Grind clean pine nuts and fry in lard.
2. Add Jerez wine and a little of the broth in which the hens were cooked.
3. Add ground cinnamon, flour browned in lard, raisins, almonds, and toasted sesame.
4. Add the cooked hen quarters and a little sugar.
5. Let everything boil so the broth is not too thick.
