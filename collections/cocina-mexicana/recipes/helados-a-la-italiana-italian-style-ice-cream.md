## Ingredients

- four cuartillos of double cream
- one quarter of coffee
- ten fresh eggs
- five egg whites
- half a pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Boil four cuartillos of double cream
2. Remove from heat and keep in a corner of the oven in a pan
3. Add one quarter of coffee, cover and leave in infusion for two hours
4. Separate the whites and yolks of ten fresh eggs and beat only five whites
5. Strain the custard through a sieve and mix with the eggs
6. Add half a pound of sugar
7. Allow everything to cook over gentle heat until it thickens
8. Remove, pass through a sieve, cool and freeze
