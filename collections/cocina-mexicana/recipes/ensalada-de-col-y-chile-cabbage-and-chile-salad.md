## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Garlic cloves
- Salt
- Oil
- Chiles, soaked
- Vinegar
- Black pepper powder
- Oregano

## Directions

1. Cook the cabbage whole with garlic and salt.
2. While cooking, slice the cabbage.
3. Place sufficient oil in a pot and brown sliced garlic, then fry the cabbage slices in it.
4. Prepare a wide cloth soaked in vinegar.
5. In a platter, arrange layers alternating: cabbage slices, fried garlic, soaked chiles, cabbage, fried garlic, and so on until full, ensuring the last layer is cabbage.
6. Sprinkle with vinegar and powder of black pepper and oregano.
