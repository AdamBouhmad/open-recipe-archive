## Ingredients

- Partridge
- Lemon slices without rind or seeds
- Bacon strips
- Greased paper
- Clear juice or lemon or orange juice

## Directions

1. After plucking, cleaning and preparing the partridge, cover the breast with lemon slices without rind or seeds
2. Cover with bacon strips
3. Wrap in greased paper
4. Place on the spit
5. Serve with clear juice or lemon or orange juice
