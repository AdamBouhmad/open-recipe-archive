## Ingredients

- potatoes
- eggs
- butter
- breadcrumbs
- fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare the potatoes as for fries with egg and everything
2. Butter a saucepan, add breadcrumbs and place paper on the bottom to prevent burning
3. Place the potato there to cook over two very gentle fires
4. If desired, a little fresh kneaded cheese can be added, or it can be made without it
