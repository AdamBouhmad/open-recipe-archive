## Ingredients

- cebolla
- ajo
- manteca
- agua
- cominos
- clavos
- canela
- pimienta molida
- orégano
- perejil picado
- alcaparras
- aceite
- tostadas de pan
- huevos estrellados
- tajadas de queso

## Directions

1. Fry onion and garlic chopped until they brown and add water with a few cumin, cloves, cinnamon and pepper, all ground
2. Add oregano and chopped parsley, capers and more manteca, letting it boil
3. Arrange the bread toasts in a pot and gradually add this broth, cooking the soup over two fires
4. Dress with eggs fried on top, slices of cheese, and more oil
5. It can serve on days of abstinence
