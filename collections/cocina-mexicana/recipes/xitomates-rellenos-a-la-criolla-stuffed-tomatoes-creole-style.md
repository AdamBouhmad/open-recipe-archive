## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- sausage meat
- onion head
- parsley
- chives
- saffron
- breadcrumb scraping

## Directions

1. After removing the seeds, fill them with a tasty sauce (see sauces section), or simply with sausage meat, which is mixed with an onion head, parsley, chives, and saffron, all chopped; place them to cook on a grill, or better yet in a covered baker under a camp oven, with enough breadcrumb scraping.
2. At the moment of serving in the same baker in which they were cooked, squeeze lemon juice over them, and they make an excellent intermediate course.
