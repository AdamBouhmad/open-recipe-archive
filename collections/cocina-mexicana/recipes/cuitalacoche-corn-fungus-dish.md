## Ingredients

- cuitalacoche
- salt
- water
- lard
- onion
- epazote leaves
- chipotle strips
- fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Ready them and cook in water with salt
2. Once cooked, remove by draining the water and fry with plenty of lard and plenty of finely minced onion
3. Add epazote leaves, chipotle strips, and fresh cheese slices
