## Ingredients

- one pound of very white sugar, ground
- one pound of flour
- eight egg whites and nine egg yolks
- half an ounce of cinnamon
- twelve cloves, ground

## Directions

1. Sift one pound of very white ground sugar into a bowl and mix it with another pound of flour
2. Add eight egg whites and nine egg yolks, half an ounce of cinnamon and twelve ground cloves
3. Stir everything and beat until the dough becomes very smooth
4. Using a spoon, place small portions on papers
5. Put in the oven
