## Ingredients

- Chito (hominy)
- Water
- Salt
- Tomatoes
- Large chiles
- Epazote
- Cumin
- Garlic
- Lard
- Guaje pods

## Directions

1. Soak the chito in hot water for a while
2. Wash it and put it to cook in a pot with water and salt
3. Peel, wash, and grind tomatoes with two or three large chiles, a sprig of epazote, cumin, and three or four garlic cloves
4. Add lard to a cazuela and fry this mixture
5. Add the meat with broth and let it season
6. Take a bundle of guaje pods, tie them, and add them to the mole, letting them boil for a while
