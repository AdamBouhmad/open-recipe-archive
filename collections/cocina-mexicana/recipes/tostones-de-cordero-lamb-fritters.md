## Ingredients

- lamb meat cut into small dice
- mushrooms
- truffles
- tomato sauce
- puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Cut roasted lamb meat into small dice form, having carefully removed all skin and sinews
2. Cut mushrooms the same way
3. Place everything in a thick tomato sauce
4. When this mixture is cold, divide it into small portions, each the size of a walnut, and wrap each in puff pastry dough
5. Prick the dough lightly with the tip of a knife
6. Fry the tostones and serve with fried parsley
