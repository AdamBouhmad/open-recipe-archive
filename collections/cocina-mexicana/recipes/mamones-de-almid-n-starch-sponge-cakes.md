## Ingredients

- twenty-four eggs
- ground cinnamon sugar
- eighteen ounces ground starch

## Directions

1. Beat twenty-four eggs with the egg whites and yolks separated until they are stiff
2. Mix the whites with two handfuls of sifted sugar
3. When well incorporated, add the yolks, mixing near the oven
4. Add eighteen ounces ground starch while beating with one hand and pouring starch with the other so lumps do not form
5. When incorporated, pour into paper boxes and put in a well-tempered oven, known by putting in a little wool which browns quickly
