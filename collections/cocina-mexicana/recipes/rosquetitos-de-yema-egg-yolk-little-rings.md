## Ingredients

- One pound flour
- Forty egg yolks, well beaten
- One quarter of a quartern of brandy
- Four egg whites, also beaten
- Four ounces ground sugar

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour add forty well-beaten egg yolks with one quarter of a quartern of brandy
2. Then add four beaten egg whites and four ounces of ground sugar
3. When the eggs are well beaten, gradually mix in one pound of flour
4. Knead the dough until it becomes firm
5. Form the rosquetes and place them in a hot oven, leaving them there until they turn golden
