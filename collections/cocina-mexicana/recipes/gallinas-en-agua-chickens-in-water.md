## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Water
- Vinegar
- Whole onions
- All spices

## Directions

1. Soak chickens in water, vinegar, whole onions and all spices the night before they are to be served
2. The following day put them to cook, covering the pot very well
3. Fry garlic in lard, and after frying remove it
4. In the same lard add a handful of oregano, toasted bread and roasted tomatoes, all ground, so that it fries also
5. Pour the pot contents over all this
6. Be careful to add oil when bringing it to the table
