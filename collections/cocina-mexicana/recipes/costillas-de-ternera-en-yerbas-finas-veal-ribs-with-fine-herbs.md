## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- butter
- salt
- spices
- fine herbs
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Place the ribs in a pan with butter, salt and spices. Toss them in the butter for five minutes. Have the fine herbs prepared and very finely chopped, sprinkle half over the ribs, turn them to the other side and sprinkle the other half. When they are roasted, season them with lemon juice.
