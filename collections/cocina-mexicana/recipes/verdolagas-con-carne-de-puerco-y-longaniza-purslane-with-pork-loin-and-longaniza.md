## Ingredients

- pork loin
- longaniza
- seasoning base fried
- purslane
- clove
- pepper
- saffron
- ground cumin
- broth from pork
- cooking water from purslane

## Directions

1. Cook pork loin and longaniza together
2. Mix with the fried seasoning base and purslane as described in the previous article
3. Season everything with ground clove, pepper, saffron, and cumin
4. Moisten with some of the broth in which the pork was cooked and some of the water in which the purslane was cooked
