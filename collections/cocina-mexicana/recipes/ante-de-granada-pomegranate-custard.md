## Ingredients

- cold bread slices
- pomegranate juice
- sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make slices of cold bread
2. Mix pomegranate juice with sugar and sprinkle over the slices
3. Layer one layer of slices and another of pomegranate
4. Place in the steam of the pot so that it stays juicy and scatter cinnamon on top
