## Ingredients

- Eight eggs
- Half a pound of very finely ground sugar
- Half a pound of well-ground starch
- A little lard for greasing the molds

## Directions

1. In a vessel without grease, beat eight eggs with half a pound of very finely ground sugar
2. When the egg is forced by hard beating, add half a pound of well-ground starch
3. Once well mixed, take iron molds or sheet metal molds, greased with a little lard, and pour the egg mixture in, being careful not to fill the molds too full so they do not overflow much when they puff up
4. Put in a well-tempered oven, and if you don't have one, use a pot placed on the fire with a comal on top
