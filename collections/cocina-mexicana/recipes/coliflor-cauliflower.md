## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- oil
- garlic
- vinegar
- bay leaf
- oregano
- thyme
- salt
- pepper powder
- capers
- olives
- chiles in vinegar
- mulato chile
- water
- roasted tomato
- fried garlic
- peppercorns

## Directions

1. Cook the cauliflower separating the florets so that it cooks divided
2. When cold, remove the water and let it drain
3. Fry garlic sliced thinly in oil and when fried lower it to cool
4. Add enough vinegar, bay leaf, oregano, thyme, salt, pepper powder
5. Return to heat and when seasoned with sufficient salt lower it and add the cauliflower well drained and let it boil a little
6. Separate from fire, add capers, olives, chiles in vinegar and more oil
7. Take it cold
8. For it to be in adobo, grind one or more mulato chiles according to the quantity, soaked in water, wash and grind with a roasted tomato, some fried garlic, about twenty peppercorns
9. Fry all this in oil, add a little vinegar, season, add salt to taste, more oil, olives and capers
10. Add the cauliflower in this thick sauce and take it cold
