## Ingredients

- ½ pound of Gruyère cheese
- macaroni
- fish or meat broth (or braised broth, which is better)
- spices

## Directions

1. Grate half of the cheese and cut the other half into small pieces
2. In the bottom of a pan, place a light layer of grated cheese and another of macaroni
3. Add another layer of cheese pieces, then another of macaroni, alternating until the cheese is finished
4. Pour fish or meat broth (or braised broth, if available) over it
5. Cook until the broth is consumed
6. Add spices and serve this soup with little broth and very thick
