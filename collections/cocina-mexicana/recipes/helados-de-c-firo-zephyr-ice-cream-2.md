## Ingredients

- Zest of one grapefruit
- Zest of one orange
- Grated zest of half a lemon
- One vanilla pod
- One and a half quartillos of milk
- Eight ounces of cream
- Twelve ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the zest of grapefruit and orange with the grated zest of half a lemon
2. Cut the vanilla pod into small pieces
3. Put the mixture to boil with one and a half quartillos of milk, eight ounces of cream and twelve ounces of sugar
4. Stir until the mixture becomes sufficiently thick
5. Remove from heat and after cooling, freeze in the sorbetera
