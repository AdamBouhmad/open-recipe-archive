## Ingredients

- four ounces of angelica
- four quartillos of aguardiente
- one pound of sugar
- one quartillo of water
- a little cinnamon
- a little clove

## Directions

1. Remove leaves from angelica branches and divide into pieces more or less large
2. For four ounces of angelica place four quartillos of aguardiente, one pound of sugar, one quartillo of water, a little cinnamon and a little clove
3. Leave in infusion for two months in a jug
4. After this time clarify and bottle
