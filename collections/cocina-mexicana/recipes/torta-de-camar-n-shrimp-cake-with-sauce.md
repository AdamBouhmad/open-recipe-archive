## Ingredients

- Shrimp
- Bread
- Eggs
- Mulato chile
- Tomatoes
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Soak the shrimp in hot water, clean them and grind them dry with a piece of toasted bread
2. Beat the egg and make the cake, sprinkling water over it while stirring
3. Toast the mulato chile, cook with tomatoes, grind everything and fry
4. When it is boiling, add the cake and cooked and chopped potatoes
