## Ingredients

- flour
- anise
- butter
- salt
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cook the flour as for polladas with a little anise and butter, less salt, until it is like paste and forms like noodles in the pan, away from the heat and set to cool
2. Once it is cold, beat the necessary eggs and add the cooked flour
3. Beat well until it is dissolved with no lumps remaining
4. Take a pan with butter that is not very hot, beat with a spoon so that they fry
5. Then place on a plate with glaze on top
6. When the flour is cooking, once it is ready so it does not stick to the pan, add a little butter and set to cook thick
