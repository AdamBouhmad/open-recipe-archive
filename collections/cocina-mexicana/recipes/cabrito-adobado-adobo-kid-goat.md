## Ingredients

- kid goat
- broad chile, deveined and soaked
- bread soaked in vinegar
- cumin
- garlic

## Directions

1. Place in a dish or pan with adobo made from broad chile deveined, soaked and ground, with bread soaked in vinegar, cumin and garlic
2. Place in the oven or roast over two fires
