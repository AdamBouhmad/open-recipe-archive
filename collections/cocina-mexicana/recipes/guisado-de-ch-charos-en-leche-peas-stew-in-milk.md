## Ingredients

- peas
- butter
- flour
- cooked milk
- pepper
- water
- cauliflower

## Directions

1. Cook the peas in a pot and add butter
2. Once cooked, chop them
3. Add a little flour, not to brown it but just to pass it through the butter
4. Add cooked milk instead of water and a little powdered pepper
5. Add a bit of water and if possible make it with butter
6. Small pieces of cauliflower can be added to the sauce
