## Ingredients

- lettuce
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- nutmeg powder
- flour
- milk
- bread crusts

## Directions

1. Remove all tough leaves and veins from the largest ones
2. Wash well
3. Blanch in boiling salted water
4. Refresh in cold water
5. Drain and squeeze well
6. Chop very fine
7. Cook using the same procedure as sorrel in broth
