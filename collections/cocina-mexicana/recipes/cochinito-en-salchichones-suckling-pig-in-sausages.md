## Ingredients

- suckling pig
- ham
- capers
- spices
- wide chile powder
- salt
- oregano
- wine
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts

## Directions

1. Debone it carefully so it does not break
2. Cut into strips and fill the intestines with ham, capers and all spices, whole or ground, wide chile powder, salt and oregano
3. Sprinkle the meat with wine and add raisins, almonds and pine nuts
