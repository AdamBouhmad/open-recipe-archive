## Ingredients

- 3 cups starch
- 3 cups milk
- 6 egg yolks and 1 egg white per cup of milk

## Directions

1. Regrind the starch and pass through a sieve
2. Measure 3 cups milk and put in a pan with 3 cups starch
3. Cook and stir until it reaches the point where it can be grabbed by hand without sticking
4. Wrap the dough in a napkin
5. Prepare 6 egg yolks and 1 white for each cup of milk and beat very well
6. When they no longer form foam, combine with the dough
7. Heat butter, make the puffs and fry one by one over low heat
8. Dip in syrup with flavors and sprinkle with sprinkles on top
