## Ingredients

- eggs
- cheese
- bread
- cheese
- pepper
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. Make a crust composed of half cheese and half bread
2. Break eggs over it
3. Sprinkle with cheese, pepper and nutmeg
4. Place over low heat, browning with a hot iron plate
