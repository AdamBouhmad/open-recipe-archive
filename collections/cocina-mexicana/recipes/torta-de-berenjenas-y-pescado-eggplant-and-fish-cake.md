## Ingredients

- eggplant
- fish
- beaten eggs
- aged cheese, grated
- bread
- xitomate, fried

## Directions

1. Cook eggplant and cut into pieces
2. Cook fish and cut into pieces
3. Make a picadillo with both ingredients
4. Add fried xitomates and sliced hard-boiled eggs
5. Mix all with beaten eggs, grated aged cheese and bread
6. Cook like the previous cake, dry or with sauce
