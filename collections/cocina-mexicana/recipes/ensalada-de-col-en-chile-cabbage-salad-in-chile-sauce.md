## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- White salt
- Dried chile peppers, deveined and well washed
- Garlic cloves, roasted
- Cumin
- Good vinegar
- Onion, very finely chopped
- Black pepper, ground
- Clove, ground
- Oregano
- Chiles in vinegar
- Olives

## Directions

1. Cook the cabbage with white salt, then chop it into pieces not too fine, and wash it.
2. Place in a serving dish and form a sauce as follows: grind dried chile peppers that have been deveined and well washed with roasted garlic cloves and some cumin.
3. Dilute the paste in good vinegar and season with the appropriate salt.
4. Finely chop onion and add to the sauce.
5. Dust the cabbage well with ground pepper and clove.
6. Mix well with the sauce.
7. When serving, garnish with oregano, chiles in vinegar, and olives.
