## Ingredients

- Veal offal
- bacon
- onion
- garlic, one clove
- broth or water
- bay leaf
- garlic
- spices
- poultry offal

## Directions

1. Take veal offal and a bit of bacon
2. Cut everything into small dice pieces
3. Fry an onion and one garlic clove
4. When softened a bit, add broth or water
5. Add bay leaf, garlic and spices
6. Can add some poultry offal
