## Ingredients

- oysters in shells
- water from the oysters
- butter
- parsley
- mushrooms
- cooked potatoes
- garlic
- flour
- broth
- wine
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Make an opening and detach the oysters from the shell
2. Cook them for a few minutes in the water they contained inside
3. Let them drain
4. Fry parsley, mushrooms, cooked potatoes, and garlic in butter
5. Add a tablespoon of flour, broth and wine, and let it thicken
6. Select and clean the largest shells
7. Arrange four to six cooked oysters on each shell
8. Pour the fried mixture over them
9. Cover with breadcrumbs
10. Sprinkle with butter
11. Place on a grill over gentle heat
12. Brown them in a camp oven or with a comal over coals
