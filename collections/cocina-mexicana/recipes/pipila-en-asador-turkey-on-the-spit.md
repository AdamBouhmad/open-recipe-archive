## Ingredients

- Turkey
- Slices of lean ham
- Strips of fat ham
- Broth
- Lard-soaked paper

## Directions

1. Remove the innards, clean and prepare the turkey
2. Cover with a layer of slices of lean ham if very fat, and if not, lard it with strips of fat ham
3. Cover with paper greased with lard, which is removed when half cooked so it takes on good color
4. Serve drizzled with broth
