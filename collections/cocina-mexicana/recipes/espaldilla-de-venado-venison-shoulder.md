## Ingredients

- venison shoulder
- ham
- bread crumbs
- broth
- egg
- pepper
- nutmeg
- salt
- parsley
- tender onions
- carrots
- bouquet garni
- white wine

## Directions

1. Bone entirely and separate a portion of its meat to make the filling, for which it will be chopped very finely with an equal amount of ham and a little bread soaked in broth and already dry.
2. Grind everything together adding an egg, and after another without leaving the ground; seasoning with pepper, nutmeg, salt, parsley and chopped tender onions.
3. When perfectly mixed, extend the shoulder sprinkling it with a little pepper and salt and spread over it a layer of filling and some bacon strips.
4. Roll and tie at both ends like a thick sausage, placing in a casserole on the bottom of which have been placed carrots, a composed bouquet, the remains and offal of the boning, half a bottle of white wine, a little salt.
