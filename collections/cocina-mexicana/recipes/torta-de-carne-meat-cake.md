## Ingredients

- Minced meat
- Pieces of longaniza
- Sausage
- Ham
- Small chorizo
- Olives
- Raisins
- Whole almonds
- Cinnamon powder
- Eggs
- Butter
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare a minced meat mixture as in the previous recipes
2. Add pieces of longaniza, sausage, ham, small chorizo, olives, raisins, whole almonds, and plenty of cinnamon powder
3. Mix everything with beaten eggs sufficient to set the cake
4. Pour into a pan greased with butter and even it out
5. Add more egg on top, spread with butter, and cook over two gentle fires
6. Watch that it does not burn, and once set, remove and sprinkle ground sugar on top
