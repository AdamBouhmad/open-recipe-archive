## Ingredients

- veal ribs
- vinegar or lemon
- butter
- bread crumbs
- parsley
- pepper
- salt
- ham

## Directions

1. Soak the ribs for a while in an infusion with vinegar or lemon.
2. Coat paper with butter and sprinkle generously with ground bread, minced parsley, pepper, salt and some slices of ham. Place the ribs on top of this, cover them with more parsley, bread and ham, wrap them in the paper and place them on the grill.
