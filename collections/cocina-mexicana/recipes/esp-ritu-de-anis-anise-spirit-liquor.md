## Ingredients

- half pound anise
- half pound angelica seeds
- four pounds common spirit of wine
- two pounds river water

## Directions

1. Grind half a pound of anise and another half of angelica seeds
2. Let them macerate for several hours in four pounds of common spirit of wine
3. Add two pounds of river water
4. Put everything in the still to distill, obtaining four pounds of liquor
