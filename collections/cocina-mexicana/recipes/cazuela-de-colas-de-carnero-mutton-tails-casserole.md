## Ingredients

- mutton tails
- turkey wings
- chestnuts
- parsnips
- onions
- mushrooms
- small sausages or chorizos

## Directions

1. Place in good broth the tails, an equal amount of deboned and prepared turkey wings, chestnuts, parsnips, onions, mushrooms and twice as many small sausages or chorizos as the tails and wings, so that there is one part of tails, another of wings and two of chorizos
2. When all is cooked, dress in a casserole large enough to be brought to the table
3. Reduce the cooking broth, skim the fat, pour it well hot into the casserole and serve
