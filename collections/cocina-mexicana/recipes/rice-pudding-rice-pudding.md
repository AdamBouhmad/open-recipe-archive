## Ingredients

- broken rice
- butter
- salt
- pepper
- melted butter for serving
- honey or syrup for serving

## Directions

1. Make the same mixture as for Indian Pudding using broken rice instead of cornmeal
2. Cook in the same manner
3. Serve with melted butter and honey or syrup
