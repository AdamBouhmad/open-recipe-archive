## Ingredients

- peas
- green beans
- butter
- garlic
- onions
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- cooked potatoes
- cloves
- pepper
- capers
- beaten egg

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as the peas with green beans and green chile, putting cooked, peeled and chopped potatoes in place of the green beans, or simply adding the potatoes without removing the green beans, and making the mixture with all three things.
