## Ingredients

- Goose
- Lard
- Garlic
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Brush them with a sauce made of lard, crushed garlic, salt, pepper and lemon juice
2. After roasting, sprinkle with warm broth
