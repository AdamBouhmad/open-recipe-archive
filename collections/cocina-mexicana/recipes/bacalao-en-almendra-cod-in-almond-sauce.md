## Ingredients

- Desalted and soaked cod
- Almonds
- Spices
- Black pepper grains
- Whole cloves
- Parsley
- Roasted garlic

## Directions

1. After the cod is well desalted and soaked, place it to half-cook in slices, in this state let it cool to dress it with ground almonds, ground spices, some grains of fine pepper, whole cloves, chopped parsley and roasted garlic.
