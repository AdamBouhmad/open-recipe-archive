## Ingredients

- oysters
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- lard
- cooking broth
- toasted bread, ground
- pepper
- clove
- oil

## Directions

1. Desalt and cook the oysters
2. Chop a considerable amount of onion, garlic, and parsley, which place in a pan with lard over heat
3. Let macerate without frying or browning
4. Add the oysters with a little of the broth in which they were cooked
5. Thicken it with toasted bread ground dry like for alfajor
6. Season with pepper and clove
7. Add a little oil
