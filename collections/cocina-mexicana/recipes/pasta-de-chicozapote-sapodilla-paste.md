## Ingredients

- ripe chicozapotes
- sugar of equal weight to the fruit pulp

## Directions

1. Select well-ripened chicozapotes, grind them, and pass through a fine bristle sieve
2. Mix the pulp with sugar of equal weight, ground, and place on the fire
3. Stir until it reaches the point of detaching from the pan, then pour into small dishes or paper boxes
