## Ingredients

- much onion
- parsley
- many garlic cloves
- oil
- tomatoes
- cod
- water
- capers
- olives
- chiles

## Directions

1. Slice much onion, chop parsley, peel many garlic cloves and fry everything in oil
2. Roast tomatoes and break them up by hand
3. Fry them in the onion
4. Add the cod with a little water
5. When removing from heat, add a good quantity of oil
6. Then add capers, olives and chiles
