## Ingredients

- veal
- bacon
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- parsley
- thyme
- oil
- white paper
- lard
- vinegar
- flour

## Directions

1. Choose a good piece; lard it with bacon seasoning it with salt, pepper and fine herbs, and place it on a platter, where it will be kept for two hours in marinade with parsley, finely chopped thyme, pepper and a little oil
2. When it has taken on the flavor, place it on the spit, pouring all the seasoning over it, wrapping it well with two sheets of white paper spread with lard, and let it roast slowly and gently over low fire
3. Afterwards remove the paper and lift with a knife all the little herbs that have remained stuck between the paper and the meat, which should be placed in a small pot with a little of its own fat, a splash of vinegar, a little lard and flour, salt and pepper
4. Let it all come together to serve it under the roasted piece as sauce
