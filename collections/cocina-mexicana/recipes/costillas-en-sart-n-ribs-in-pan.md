## Ingredients

- ribs
- juice from the ribs
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- fresh garlic
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- nutmeg

## Directions

1. After boiling the ribs over low heat, place them to drain in the saucepan containing the juice they have released
2. Add broth, season with salt, pepper, and fresh garlic finely chopped
3. Return to the heat and add three beaten egg yolks, a little vinegar, a little grated nutmeg
4. Take care that it does not boil
