## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Butter
- Broth
- White wine
- Lemon juice
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Leave the young partridges in their butter
2. Make a lightly red substance with broth and half a bottle of white wine
3. Pour this over the young partridges with its broth
4. When the broth thickens, degrease as much as possible, allowing it to reach the consistency of sauce
5. Ensure it has the necessary salt for good taste
