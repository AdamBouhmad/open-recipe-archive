## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- seven egg yolks
- half an ounce of raw lard
- sugar or salt as needed
- warm water as sufficient to soften the dough

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour, seven egg yolks, half an ounce of raw lard, sugar or salt as needed, and warm water sufficient to soften the dough
