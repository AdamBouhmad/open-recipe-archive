## Ingredients

- three pounds superfine sugar
- three ounces good powdered coffee
- one quarter pint water
- a small piece of isinglass

## Directions

1. Prepare three pounds of superfine sugar as previously described
2. Bring three ounces of good powdered coffee to a boil in one quarter pint of water
3. Strain the decoction through a sleeve into which a small piece of isinglass has been placed to remove the sediment
4. When the liquid is cold and clear, mix it with the sugar to form the pastilles
5. Observe that only as much mixture as can be kept hot while working should be placed in the pan at once, as cooling would damage the operation
