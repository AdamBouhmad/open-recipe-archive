## Ingredients

- 3 pounds apples, cooked and cleaned
- 2 pounds sugar
- Cinnamon and clove, ground
- 1 pound washed butter
- Egg whites, beaten
- Syrup

## Directions

1. Place 3 pounds of cooked and cleaned apples in syrup made with 2 pounds of sugar, with a little ground cinnamon and clove and 1 pound of washed butter
2. Put everything over fire until it takes the consistency of paste
3. Pour over layers of sponge cake, sprinkled with syrup and white wine
4. Coat the last layer with beaten egg whites mixed with syrup over the fire until they cook
5. Beat again after cooling
6. Spread over the paste and dust with ground cinnamon
