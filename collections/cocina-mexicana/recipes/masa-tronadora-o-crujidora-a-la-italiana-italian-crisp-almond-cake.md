## Ingredients

- one pound of peeled almonds
- two ounces of orange-blossom water
- lemon peel
- one and a half pounds of powdered sugar
- egg white

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of peeled almonds with two ounces of orange-blossom water and lemon peel
2. Grind together, sprinkling occasionally with egg white
3. Make a clarified syrup with one and a half pounds of powdered sugar and boil until it reaches the soft ball stage
4. Remove the pan from the heat and add the almond paste, mixing well
5. Return the pan to the fire on very low heat, stirring the paste until it separates from the pan
6. Pour onto a plate sprinkled with sugar
7. When cool, form rolls or bases from which you will make the cake or gatò in the desired shape
8. Bake in the oven
