## Ingredients

- 2 pounds white sugar
- 1 quartillo virgin honey
- egg whites
- 2 pounds peeled almonds, half-toasted

## Directions

1. Clarify the sugar and honey together with egg whites
2. Reach the point of alfeñique, which is when sugar breaks inside water
3. Beat 1 egg white and mix with 2 pounds peeled and half-toasted almonds
4. Proceed as in the previous article
