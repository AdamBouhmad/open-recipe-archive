## Ingredients

- Cooked hare
- oil
- garlic
- roasted tomatoes
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- cooked egg yolks
- broth or hot water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the quarters of cooked hare in oil and, removing them, fry garlic and roasted tomatoes and chopped parsley in the same oil
2. When everything is fried, season with salt and pepper
3. Place the hare quarters with cooked and ground egg yolks, moisten with broth or hot water and a little vinegar, letting it boil until the sauce is of good consistency
