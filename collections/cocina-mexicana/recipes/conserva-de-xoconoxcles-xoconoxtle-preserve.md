## Ingredients

- Xoconoxcles
- Salt
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Scrape them with a tezoncle stone until the skin is removed, and being well clean, remove the crowns with a knife and hollow them out through that opening
2. Place them to cook with a little salt
3. Once cooked, add them to cold water, which should be changed from time to time until they have completely lost all their sourness
4. Then place them over low heat with the clarified syrup that has been prepared
5. Let it reach the proper point
