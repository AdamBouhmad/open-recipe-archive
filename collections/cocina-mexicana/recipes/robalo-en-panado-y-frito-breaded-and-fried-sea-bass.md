## Ingredients

- Sea bass, whole if small or in portions if large
- Flour
- Wine
- Egg yolks
- Salt
- Lard for frying

## Directions

1. Make a somewhat loose batter with flour, wine, egg yolks and salt
2. Cover the sea bass with this batter, whole if small, or in portions if large
3. Fry in lard
