## Ingredients

- two pounds of sifted sugar
- one pound of fine flour
- one pound of soaked and well-ground almonds
- half pound of orange preserve (candied orange, drained from syrup and ground)
- three quarters ounce of ground cinnamon
- a small amount of grated nutmeg
- water

## Directions

1. Mix together the sifted sugar, fine flour, ground almonds, candied orange, ground cinnamon, and grated nutmeg
2. Knead everything by rolling with water until the dough becomes manageable
3. Place the dough on a plate dusted with flour and let it rest for two hours
4. After two hours, test the dough by making one pastry and baking it in a well-tempered oven to see if it is ready; if it puffs up, the dough is good, and you may make the remaining pastries in whatever shape you desire
5. If the test pastry spreads, it is a sign the dough lacks flour, so add more flour
6. Once the pastries are made, glaze them as described in the previous article
