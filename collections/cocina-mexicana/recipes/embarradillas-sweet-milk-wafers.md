## Ingredients

- one cuartillo of milk
- half a cuartillo of cream
- two panochas of one pound each
- white wafers

## Directions

1. Mix one cuartillo of milk with half a cuartillo of cream
2. Use two panochas of one pound each in place of sugar
3. Pour the mixture into white wafers
4. Fold them like quesadillas
