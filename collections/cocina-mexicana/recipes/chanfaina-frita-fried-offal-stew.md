- alcaparras

## Directions

1. Thoroughly wash the offal and cook in water with vinegar, salt, a smashed head of garlic, an onion split in a cross, two bay leaves, thyme and marjoram
2. Fry in a good amount of lard the chopped onions and two cloves of garlic
3. While frying, remove the garlic and grind it with one large tomato for each offal, adding both ground things to the lard to fry as well
4. Then chop the offal and add it to the frying, and when well fried, add a small cup of oil, cinnamon, pepper, clove and cumin, these spices ground, and vinegar to taste
5. Let everything fry more, and then add two eggs cut up in vinegar
6. To serve, garnish with olives, cured small chiles and some capers
