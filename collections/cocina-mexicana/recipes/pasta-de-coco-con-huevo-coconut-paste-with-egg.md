## Ingredients

- two coconuts
- three pounds of sugar
- nine egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make the paste as in the previous article
2. Before the paste reaches point, mix in nine egg yolks, beaten medium
3. Let it boil until the bottom of the pan is visible when stirred
