## Ingredients

- pork loin, sliced
- lard
- onion
- garlic
- tomato
- ancho chiles
- spices
- pineapple
- sweet potato
- banana
- chorizo

## Directions

1. Slice pork loin and cook
2. Once cooked, place lard in a casserole
3. Chop much onion, garlic, and tomato
4. Grind two ancho chiles and add spices
5. Add pineapple, sweet potato, and banana
6. Add chorizo
