## Ingredients

- one pound of citron segments
- four pounds of sugar
- water in which the citrons were separated

## Directions

1. Add the sugar to the citron segments in the same water in which the citrons were separated
2. Once the syrup is clarified, crush the segments and add them
3. When it has acquired a sour taste, strain it again and bring it to the high point of syrup consistency
