## Ingredients

- pears
- sugar
- orange blossom water
- cloves
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cook the pears with their skin until not too soft
2. Peel and place in cold water, changing the water several times so they remain white
3. Add to cold syrup and bring to a violent boil so they do not turn red
4. Add orange blossom water, some cloves, and cinnamon strips
