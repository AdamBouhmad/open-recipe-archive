## Ingredients

- rice
- milk
- salt
- eggs
- sugar
- custard
- lard
- hardtack biscuit
- pine nuts
- raisins
- toasted almonds

## Directions

1. Cook the rice with milk as in the previous recipe until it has thickened well
2. Mix with a dozen eggs beaten as for frying, add plenty of ground sugar so the sweetness stands out, and add a quart of thick custard, mixing everything well
3. Grease a mold with lard, dust with hard, ground biscuit so it takes on all the sides of the pot
4. Add the rice with the egg and put over two gentle heats
5. After a while, add on top a thin layer of a couple of eggs beaten with plenty of grated biscuit and put the comal back on it so it forms a crust, ensuring both the heat above and below are gentle to avoid burning
6. When the cake has set well, adorn with pine nuts, raisins, and toasted almonds and dust with sugar
