## Ingredients

- lamb trotters
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- mixed herbs
- cooked meat
- beef fat
- bread crumbs
- milk
- parsley
- onion
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook eight lamb feet in water, then simmer for half an hour over gentle heat with a little broth, salt, pepper and a mixed herb bouquet
2. Bone them as much as possible
3. Replace the bones with the filling described below
4. If desired to fry, coat with beaten egg, dust with breadcrumbs and fry in hot fat until golden, serving immediately upon removal from the pan
5. If serving without frying, soak in hot butter, cover with breadcrumbs and brown in a camp oven on a plate, drain the fat and serve plain or with a clear substantial sauce
6. For the filling: mince a small piece of cooked meat and an equal amount of beef fat, and a little bread crumb soaked in milk and pressed or dried afterwards, season the mixture with salt, pepper, minced parsley and onion, and bind with three egg yolks
