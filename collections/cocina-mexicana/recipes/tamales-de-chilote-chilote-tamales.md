## Ingredients

- sugar
- tomato
- parsley
- salt
- pork meat broth
- clove
- cinnamon
- Castilian vinegar
- capers
- raisins
- almonds
- olives
- chiles in vinegar
- cooked cheese, sliced

## Directions

1. Place a casserole on the fire with sugar and let it heat
2. Chop tomato and parsley, add salt and fry
3. Once fried, add pork meat broth
4. Grind clove and cinnamon and add to the mixture
5. Add a piece of sugar and Castilian vinegar
6. Add capers, raisins, almonds, olives, chiles in vinegar, and sliced cooked cheese
