## Ingredients

- 4 ducks
- salt
- pepper
- forcemeat
- gelatin

## Directions

1. Remove the breasts and legs with their skin from four ducks
2. Bone them, place on clean linen and season with salt and coarse pepper
3. Place a little forcemeat on the meats and sew in the form of a ball or globe
4. Cut the legs below the joint and cook in a pan
5. When cooked, allow to cool between two dishes or casserole lids
6. Untie, arrange and reheat in a half-set gelatin
7. Arrange on the plate in the form of a crown, cover with gelatin and place a macedoine in the center.
