## Ingredients

- half a quarter of flour
- one pound of melted lard
- two egg yolks
- tequesquite
- water
- two panels of sugar

## Directions

1. Add one pound of melted lard to the flour in a pan
2. Dissolve two egg yolks and incorporate the flour, mixing in the lard
3. Dissolve the amount of tequesquite that fits between four fingers in a cup of hot water
4. Once the dough is kneaded, add this to the dough and knead again like washing clothes
5. Make long, thin rolls the thickness of a finger
6. Cut into small pieces with a knife
7. Bake two panels in the oven
8. Throw them in a cup of water
9. Put on the heat and when they break apart and the boiling starts, remove from heat
10. Add the little balls and form the muéganos
