## Ingredients

- White sweet potato, cooked
- Flour
- Egg yolks
- Lard for frying
- Syrup

## Directions

1. After the sweet potato is cooked, peel and grind it, removing all the fibers
2. Mix with flour and add egg yolks
3. Knead well until it becomes spongy
4. Form the fritters as desired
5. Fry in lard and serve with syrup
