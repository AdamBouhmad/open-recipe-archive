## Ingredients

- Young partridges, roasted
- One glass of white wine
- Spanish sauce
- Shallots, chopped
- Parsley, chopped
- Salt and pepper
- Bread toasts, fried in butter and crushed
- Red meat essence or Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. After the young partridges are roasted and cooled, break them apart and place their pieces in a casserole
2. Break the bones of the carcass and place them in another casserole with one glass of white wine, a little Spanish sauce, chopped shallots and parsley, salt and pepper
3. Make this sauce thicken over a very lively fire
4. Pass it through a sieve and pour it over the pieces of the young partridges
5. Heat them in it without boiling
6. Arrange this sauce by mixing in toasts of bread, fried in butter and crushed
7. If Spanish sauce is not available, it can be replaced with red meat essence, in which the remains of the young partridges are fried, seasoning as indicated and moistening with broth and white wine in equal quantities; let the broth thicken and pass through a sieve
