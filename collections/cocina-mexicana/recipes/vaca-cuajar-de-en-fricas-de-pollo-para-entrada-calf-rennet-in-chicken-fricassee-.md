## Ingredients

- thick fatty pieces of calf rennet
- onion
- garlic
- clove of spice
- butter
- flour
- broth
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scrape, clean very carefully and wash in many waters of boiling water the pieces of thick and very fatty rennet
2. Soak them in cold water and put them to cook in water with sliced onion, garlic and cloves of spice
3. Fry everything in butter with a handful of flour, moisten with a little broth, thicken the sauce with egg yolks, and serve
