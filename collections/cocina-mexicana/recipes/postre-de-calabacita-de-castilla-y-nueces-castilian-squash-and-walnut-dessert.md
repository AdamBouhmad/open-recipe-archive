## Ingredients

- One medium squash
- Eighty large walnuts or double the amount of small ones
- Syrup made from three pounds of sugar
- Mamón
- White wine

## Directions

1. Cook a medium squash and grind it with eighty large walnuts or double the amount of small ones
2. Put in the syrup made from three pounds of sugar
3. Put everything on the fire until it reaches the point of paste
4. Spread over layers of mamón sprinkled with white wine
5. Stud the top as in other desserts
