## Ingredients

- Pineapple
- Pineapple preserve syrup
- Texocote jelly or fish glue

## Directions

1. After making pineapple preserve as described in its place, remove the pineapple that is to be left covered
2. From the syrup mix one and a half cuartillo with one of sweet texocote jelly, or with fish glue, to give it the consistency of jelly
3. Let it boil and when it has reached the point so that the jelly can be divided with a spoon
4. Remove from fire, skim, let rest a moment and pour into molds or dishes in which it is to be served
