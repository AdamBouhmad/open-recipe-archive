## Ingredients

- Fresh pork meat
- Lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- White wine
- Pork blood
- Half ounce of cinnamon and clove, ground together
- Thick strips of pork
- Pork intestines
- Broth
- A small bunch of parsley
- Onions
- One head of garlic
- Thyme
- Laurel
- Basil

## Directions

1. Mince together fresh pork meat and lard, seasoning with salt and pepper
2. Sprinkle this minced mixture with white wine and pork blood, and add half an ounce of cinnamon and clove ground together
3. Take thick strips from the pork and dust well with spices, and distribute among the minced mixture
4. Put everything in the pork intestine, and when full, tie at both ends
5. Put to cook for three hours with broth, little salt, a small bunch of parsley, onions, one head of garlic, thyme, laurel and basil
6. Once the sausage is cooked, smoke it in the chimney, leaving it hanging until it is tight and hard to the extreme
