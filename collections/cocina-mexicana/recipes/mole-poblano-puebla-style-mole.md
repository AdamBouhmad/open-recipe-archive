## Ingredients

- Half a pound of wide chiles (chiles anchos)
- Half a pound of pasilla chiles
- Lard
- Culantro (cilantro), about one handful or slightly less
- Half a quart of cooked tomatoes
- Half a head of roasted garlic
- Pepper
- Clove
- Toasted sesame (ajonjolí)

## Directions

1. Toast in lard half a pound of wide chiles and another half pound of pasilla chiles, taking care that they do not burn.
2. Toast also in the lard about a handful or slightly less of culantro.
3. Grind both things with half a quart of cooked tomatoes, half a head of roasted garlic, and a little pepper, clove, and toasted sesame.
4. The meat that is to be stewed is fried raw and then added to the mole, which is seasoned according to taste.
