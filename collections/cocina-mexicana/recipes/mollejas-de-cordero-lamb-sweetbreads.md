## Ingredients

- lamb sweetbreads
- cold water
- broth, one cup
- pepper
- salt
- small onions
- nutmeg
- lard, a proportioned quantity
- flour
- three eggs
- parsley, finely chopped
- lemon juice
- peas

## Directions

1. After cleaning, place in cold water for some time
2. Put in a dish with one cup of broth, a little pepper, salt, small onions and nutmeg
3. Mix well with a proportioned quantity of flour-dusted lard and leave on low heat for half an hour
4. Beat three eggs with finely chopped parsley and nutmeg
5. Add lemon juice and some peas
