## Ingredients

- Four well-beaten eggs
- One pound of ground sugar
- One pound of ground starch
- A little flour
- Sesame seeds
- Ground sugar for topping

## Directions

1. To four well-beaten eggs, add one pound of ground sugar
2. When the paste is very thick, add one pound of ground starch and a little flour
3. Mix everything and pour into paper boxes, sprinkling sesame seeds on top
4. Put in a well-heated oven
5. To make soletas with the same batter, place small portions on paper or sheet metal and dust with ground sugar before baking
6. To make covered mamones, dip them in thick hot syrup, remove quickly, and roll in ground cinnamon
7. They can also be made toasted in slices
