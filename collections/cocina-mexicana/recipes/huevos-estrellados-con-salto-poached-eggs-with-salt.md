## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- vinegar
- eggs
- pepper
- vinegar or lemon juice

## Directions

1. Fill three-quarters of the pot with water and salt and a little vinegar, placing it at the edge of the stove
2. Break the eggs into it without breaking them, always keeping the water boiling
3. Let them set
4. Remove them with a slotted spoon, and if they have some consistency put them in cold water
5. Change the water, and a little before serving heat them
6. Dry them on a white cloth and place on a plate
7. Sprinkle each egg with a pinch of pepper and a drop of vinegar or lemon juice
