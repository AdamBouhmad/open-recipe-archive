## Ingredients

- Apples
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Peel the apples and remove the seeds
2. Put them in a clay pot on a layer of powdered sugar
3. Place more powdered sugar in the heart of each apple
4. Cook in the oven or under a dome oven
5. Dust with sugar and serve hot
