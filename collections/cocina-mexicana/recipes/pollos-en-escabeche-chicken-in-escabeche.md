## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Flour
- Lard
- Garlic cloves
- Water
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Saffron
- Cilantro
- Brown sugar
- Orange leaves
- Lime

## Directions

1. Once the chickens are cooked, divide them into regular portions
2. Dust with flour or roll them in it
3. Also fry some chopped garlic cloves in lard and add some water and vinegar with necessary salt
4. Add to the sauce a little saffron and dry cilantro, both ground, a small piece of brown sugar, some orange leaves, and slices of sour lime cooked separately
5. Place the fried chicken quarters in this escabeche and let everything boil until the sauce thickens a little
