## Ingredients

- flour
- lard
- fine herbs or onions
- broth or water
- cooked lentils
- salt
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Make a light roux with flour browned in lard
2. Add finely chopped fine herbs or onions cut into small squares
3. Fry with the flour
4. Moisten with broth or water and let everything dissolve
5. Add the already cooked lentils with salt and black pepper
6. Serve hot
