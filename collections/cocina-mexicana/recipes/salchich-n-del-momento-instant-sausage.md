## Ingredients

- Pork meat
- Cloves
- Cinnamon
- Saffron
- Cumin
- Wine
- Vinegar
- Lard
- Buttered paper

## Directions

1. Mince and cook the pork meat
2. Mince again and add cloves, cinnamon, saffron, cumin, wine, vinegar, and lard
3. Season over heat
4. Wrap in buttered paper and coat with a little lard on the outside
5. Roast over two fires
