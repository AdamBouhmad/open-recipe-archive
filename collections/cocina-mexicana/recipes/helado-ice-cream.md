## Ingredients

- Vegetable and fruit juices or composition to be frozen
- Ice, crushed
- Salt

## Directions

1. Pour the composition to be frozen into tin freezing vessels, surrounding them with crushed ice mixed with salt
2. Place the freezing vessel inside a bucket with ice between it and the bucket
3. Stir until it freezes
4. With a wooden spatula, stir the liquid occasionally to remove what adheres to the sides
