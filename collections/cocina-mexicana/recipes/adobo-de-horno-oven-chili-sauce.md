## Ingredients

- ancho chile
- mulato chile
- garlic
- cumin
- pork loin, raw
- lard

## Directions

1. Devein and soak equal quantities of ancho and mulato chile
2. Grind with garlic and cumin
3. Coat the raw pork loins very well with this mixture
4. Place them in a buttered pan and put in a moderate oven until cooked
5. If desired, serve them whole, garnoned with salad, or in slices, letting them dry completely
