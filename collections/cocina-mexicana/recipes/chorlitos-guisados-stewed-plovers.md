## Ingredients

- Plovers
- 2 whole heads of garlic
- 2 dried mulato or red chiles, well cleaned
- Salt
- Water
- Vinegar
- Oil
- Capers, desalted
- Parsley
- Kid goat or veal liver, grated
- Clove
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. After cleaning thoroughly, cook the plovers with two whole heads of garlic, two dried mulato or red chiles very well cleaned, salt, and equal amounts of water and vinegar
2. When cooked, remove the broth and let it cool
3. When cool, strain it to collect the fat it has released
4. Place this fat in a pot with a little oil and fry the plovers there
5. When they are almost golden, increase the oil and fry with them a good portion of desalted capers ground with a handful of parsley leaves and a little grated kid goat or veal liver
6. Increase with broth from the cooking of the plovers and some ground clove and cinnamon
7. When it is almost finished thickening, season with salt and serve
