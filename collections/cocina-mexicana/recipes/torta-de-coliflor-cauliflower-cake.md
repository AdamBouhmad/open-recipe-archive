## Ingredients

- Cauliflower, cooked
- Biscuit, ground
- Aged cheese
- Eggs, beaten
- Lard

## Directions

1. Follow all the procedures of the potato cake recipe, with the only difference that cooked cauliflower is used instead of potatoes
2. These cakes are usually served in spice broth, although they may also be cooked in chile
