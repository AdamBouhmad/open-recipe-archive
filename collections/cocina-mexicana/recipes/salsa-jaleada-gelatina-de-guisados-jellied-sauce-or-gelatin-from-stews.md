## Ingredients

- cooking liquid from stews
- butter

## Directions

1. Strain the cooking liquid (the broth produced by the meats as they cook) through a fine napkin or silk sieve, being careful that it becomes very clear
2. Let it reduce immediately in a large saucepan over high heat
3. When it becomes as thick as a sauce, or the gelatin adheres to the spoon, pour it into a smaller saucepan
4. Place it in a bain-marie or over low heat so it stays warm until serving time, when a small piece of fresh butter will be added to correct the saltiness
