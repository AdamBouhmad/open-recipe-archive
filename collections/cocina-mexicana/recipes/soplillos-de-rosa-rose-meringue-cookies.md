## Ingredients

- three pounds powdered sugar
- two egg whites
- one ounce orange flower water
- a little carmine powder

## Directions

1. Beat together the powdered sugar with the egg whites, adding little by little the orange flower water and carmine powder
2. Make a consistent and fairly firm paste
3. Roll the paste on a table dusted with sugar
4. Cut into small pieces the size of hazelnuts
5. Place on sheets of paper six lines apart so they do not touch
6. Put in the oven until they rise
