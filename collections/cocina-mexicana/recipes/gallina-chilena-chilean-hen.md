## Ingredients

- hen, cut into pieces
- potatoes
- garlic
- onions
- boiling water
- egg yolks, a pair
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the hen cut into pieces, and after browning well, add potatoes to also brown, with some garlic and onions.
2. Then pour boiling water and let cook.
3. Before serving, dissolve a pair of egg yolks with a little vinegar, taking care that it does not curdle.
4. If desired, some peas or a little rice can be added.
