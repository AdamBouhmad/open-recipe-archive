## Ingredients

- eggs
- salt
- water or milk
- butter

## Directions

1. Break the necessary eggs into a saucepan, season with fine salt and add a little water or milk
2. Beat everything together
3. The more beaten the eggs, the lighter the cake will be
4. Melt butter in a pan without letting it get too hot
