## Ingredients

- four eggs
- one pound sugar powder
- one pound ground starch
- a little flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Beat well four eggs with one pound sugar powder
2. When the mixture is very thick, add one pound ground starch and a little flour
3. Mix everything and pour into buttered paper boxes
4. Bake in the oven, dusting with sifted sugar on top
