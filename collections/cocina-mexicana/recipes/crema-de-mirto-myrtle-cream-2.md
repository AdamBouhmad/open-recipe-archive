## Ingredients

- six ounces of myrtle flower, or in its absence, leaves of the same shrub
- one ounce of peach leaves
- one quarter part of a nutmeg, in coarse powder
- sixteen cuartillos of brandy

## Directions

1. Mix six ounces of myrtle flower, or in its absence, leaves of the same shrub, with one ounce of peach leaves and one quarter part of a nutmeg in coarse powder
2. Place all in infusion at atmospheric temperature in sixteen cuartillos of brandy for a very short time
3. Distill in a water bath to extract half of the liquor
4. Mix the obtained spirit well with essence of mignonette
5. Filter and store
