## Ingredients

- muffin bread
- fresh butter
- egg yolks
- poultry breasts
- good cheese
- two eggs
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- concentrated good broth

## Directions

1. Make a paste with muffin bread, fresh butter and egg yolks
2. Let it dry and then cool
3. Then grind it with poultry breasts, fresh butter, good cheese, two eggs, salt, pepper and nutmeg
4. Bring good concentrated broth to a boil
5. Shape the quenelles with a small spoon and drop them into the broth
6. Serve this soup with good grated cheese on the side
