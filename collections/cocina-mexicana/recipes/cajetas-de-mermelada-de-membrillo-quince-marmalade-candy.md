## Ingredients

- quince, not too ripe, peeled and cored
- salt
- twenty-nine pounds of sugar for every arroba of quince

## Directions

1. Put peeled and cored quince in a pot of hot water with salt
2. Leave overnight and the next day wash and cook
3. Put in a pot with warm water and let sit until the next day, then drain in a basket
4. Grind thoroughly, pass through a very fine cloth, and put in clarified syrup at ball point made with twenty-nine pounds of sugar for every arroba of quince
5. The point is known when it bubbles with smoke
6. When it reaches this point, remove from heat, beat in the pan, and fill the candy molds
