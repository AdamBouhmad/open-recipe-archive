## Ingredients

- carrots
- turnips
- kohlrabi or parsnips
- leeks
- asparagus tips
- onions
- lettuce
- sorrel
- chervil
- chard
- peas
- green beans
- butter
- broth
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut into small thin strips carrots, turnips, kohlrabi or parsnips, leeks, asparagus tips and onions
2. Chop lettuce, sorrel, chervil and chard and mix all with peas and green beans
3. Cook halfway in butter
4. Moisten with fat broth or lean and finish cooking
5. Add any substance or sauce, salt and pepper and serve without bread or with it, having added it before so it soaks
