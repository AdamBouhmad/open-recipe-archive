## Ingredients

- squabs
- salt
- eye (presumably garlic or onion)
- parsley
- tomatoes
- apple slices
- morcón slices
- cloves
- pepper powder
- raisins
- capers
- flour
- lard
- oil

## Directions

1. Pass the squabs over flame to singe the down
2. Clean and empty them, taking care not to waste the blood
3. Cook them with the blood, broth foam, necessary water, eye, parsley and tomatoes all chopped, apple and morcón slices, salt, cloves and pepper powder, raisins and capers
4. When the squabs are cooked, remove them from their broth
5. Roll them in flour and fry in lard
6. Add their broth and the things that cooked with them
7. Thicken the broth with bread fried in lard and darkened in color
8. Add oil and let boil until everything is perfectly seasoned
