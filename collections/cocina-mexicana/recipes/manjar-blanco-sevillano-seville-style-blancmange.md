## Ingredients

- 30 cuartillos of milk
- 0.5 chicken breast
- 2.5 pounds ground and sifted rice
- 180 egg yolks
- 1 pound well-washed butter

## Directions

1. Combine 30 cuartillos of milk with 0.5 chicken breast, 2.5 pounds of ground and sifted rice, and 180 egg yolks
2. Cook together
3. When it reaches medium point, add 1 pound of well-washed butter
4. Let everything boil until it reaches the proper consistency
