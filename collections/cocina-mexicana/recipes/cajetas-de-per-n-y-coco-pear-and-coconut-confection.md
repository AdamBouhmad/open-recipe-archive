## Ingredients

- a quarter arroba of sugar
- two grated and ground coconuts
- one pound of cooked pear, passed through a fine cloth or through a loose hair sieve

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with a quarter arroba of sugar and clarify it
2. Mix in two grated and ground coconuts and one pound of cooked pear, passed through a fine cloth or through a loose hair sieve
3. Let it reach the point of cajeta, being careful to stir it
4. Remove from fire and pour into molds
