## Ingredients

- poultry or game (preferably fat chickens, pheasants, or partridges)
- lettuce, chopped
- lettuce hearts
- radishes
- anchovy strips
- pickles, thinly sliced
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook the poultry or game on a spit; when cold, cut into strips and debone carefully
2. Extend the strips over chopped lettuce placed at the bottom of the plate
3. Make compartments or divisions marked by lettuce hearts and other garnishes such as radishes
4. Season like other salads
5. Adorn with anchovy strips and very thin slices of pickles
