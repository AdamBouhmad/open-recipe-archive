## Ingredients

- Duck
- Lard
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Onion
- Ham slices
- Turnips
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Fried bread
- Sherry wine

## Directions

1. Follow the same as the previous ones
2. Heat a small pan with lard, mince garlic, parsley and onion, slices of ham, and fry everything together with the duck
3. Slice thin turnips and fry them with lard, together with clove, cinnamon and pepper and add once more to where everything is
4. Add water to season it, slice more turnips and add them
5. Fry slices of bread, grind and add to it
6. The stew should be thick and before taking off the fire add a glass of Sherry
7. Garnish as in the previous ones
