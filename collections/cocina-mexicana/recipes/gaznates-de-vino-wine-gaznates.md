## Ingredients

- four handfuls flour
- nine egg yolks
- warm salted water
- warm tequesquite water
- small cup sherry wine
- optional mezcal

## Directions

1. Knead four handfuls of flour with nine egg yolks, a little warm salted water, warm tequesquite water and a small cup of sherry wine
2. When the dough blisters, let it rest and extend it very thin with a rolling pin
3. Cut and seal the gaznates and fry in a pot
4. Mezcal can be used instead of wine
