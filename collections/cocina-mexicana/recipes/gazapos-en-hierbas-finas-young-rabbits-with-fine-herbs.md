## Ingredients

- young rabbits
- parsley
- onions
- mushrooms
- garlic
- butter
- thyme
- laurel
- basil powder
- flour
- white wine
- meat broth
- salt
- peppercorns

## Directions

1. Divide rabbits into portions and place in a pan
2. Add parsley, onions, mushrooms, and garlic all chopped, a piece of butter, thyme, laurel, and basil powder
3. Place everything on the fire together
4. Add a little flour
5. Moisten with a glass of white wine and a little meat broth
6. Season with salt and peppercorns
7. Let the rabbits cook and reduce the sauce to sauce consistency
8. When ready to serve, take the livers that cooked in the fricassee, crush them, and add to the sauce
