## Ingredients

- corn (nixtamal, dry)
- pork meat broth
- teqesquite water
- salt, ground
- lard
- clean water

## Directions

1. Make the nixtamal dry, sift it
2. Add the broth from pork meat and a little teqesquite water and ground salt
3. Beat much and add cold lard on top
4. Make with clean water
5. To two almudes of corn add three pounds of lard
