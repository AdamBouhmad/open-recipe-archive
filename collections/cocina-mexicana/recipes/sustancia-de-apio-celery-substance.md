## Ingredients

- thick celery roots
- fresh butter
- salt
- flour
- cream or broth
- sugar

## Directions

1. Peel and wash the thick celery roots, cook and pass through a strainer.
2. Put fresh butter in a saucepan and add the substance with salt, a tablespoon of flour, cream or broth, and a small piece of sugar.
