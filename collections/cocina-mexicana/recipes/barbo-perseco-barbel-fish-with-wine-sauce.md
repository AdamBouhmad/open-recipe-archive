## Ingredients

- Barbel fish
- Wine
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Fine herbs
- Mushrooms
- Good butter
- Fried bread slices
- Crayfish

## Directions

1. After emptying and removing the scales, place to cook in a pan with wine, salt, black pepper, fine herbs, mushrooms and good butter
2. Once the fish is cooked, separate the wine and reduce it by half over heat
3. Thicken with kneaded butter
4. Serve the barbel with this sauce, with fried toast and crayfish
