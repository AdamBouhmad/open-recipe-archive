## Ingredients

- Cow head
- Dried chiles
- Garlic cloves
- Onion pieces
- Fatty pork meat or ham
- Good vinegar
- Salt
- Ginger
- All kinds of spices
- Lime pieces
- Fresh hide or clean palm mat

## Directions

1. Remove the horns from the head at the root
2. Pierce the hide in all places and stuff it with whole dried chiles, garlic cloves, pieces of onion, and fatty pork or ham that has been pickled for ten or twelve hours in a chile-garlic mixture made of good vinegar, salt, ginger and all kinds of spices with pieces of lime
3. Stuff the tongue as much as possible
4. Through the throat insert the garlic-cumin, plugging it so it does not come out
5. Wrap the head either in a piece of fresh hide or in a clean palm mat that has been soaked, and tie it with some strings of maguey twine
6. Form a pit as explained before, adding dry wood and fire
7. When the stones have turned red, sprinkle them with water and arrange the head as directed
8. Place on top some of the hot stones and the embers that have been removed from the pit
9. Cover with earth until full and make a good fire of wood on top, which should last from the beginning of the night until the following morning, at the hour when the barbacoa is to be eaten
10. It should be served hot
