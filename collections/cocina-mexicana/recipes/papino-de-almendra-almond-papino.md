## Ingredients

- almonds, peeled and ground
- good-point syrup
- mamón
- dragée (sugar-coated balls)

## Directions

1. Peel and grind the almonds and mix them with good-point syrup.
2. Place in layers alternating with layers of mamón, sprinkling the top layer with dragée.
