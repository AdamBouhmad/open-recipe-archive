## Ingredients

- five pounds of flour
- nine egg yolks and three egg whites
- ten ounces of ground sugar
- one pound and two ounces of butter
- one pound of yeast
- salt to taste
- water as needed

## Directions

1. Mix five pounds of flour with nine egg yolks and three egg whites, ten ounces of ground sugar, one pound and two ounces of butter, one pound of yeast, salt to taste, and water as needed
2. Knead forcefully until smooth
3. Form the biscuits
4. Place in the sun until they puff up
5. Bake in the oven
