## Ingredients

- sugar
- egg yolks
- wine
- sugar
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. With well-clarified syrup and at a melcocha point, add well-beaten egg yolks and beat again until it becomes sugared
2. Add a little wine and once sugared, form small balls and roll them in sugar and ground cinnamon
