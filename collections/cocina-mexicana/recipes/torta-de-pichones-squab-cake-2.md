## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Eggs
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Make as in the previous recipe with the difference of using squabs instead of chickens
2. Once the egg has set, spread with butter
3. Add a layer of breadcrumbs mixed with sugar
4. Return to heat to brown, adding more butter on top
