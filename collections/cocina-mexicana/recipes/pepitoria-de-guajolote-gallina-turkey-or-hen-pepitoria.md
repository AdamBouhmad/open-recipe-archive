## Ingredients

- one turkey or hen
- chopped ham or lard
- garlic
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- two cups of broth from the pot
- clove spice
- toasted garlic
- hazelnuts
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clean the bird with a dry napkin and cut into pieces
2. Sauté with chopped ham or lard, garlic, parsley, salt and a little pepper
3. Add two cups of broth from the pot
4. Make a sauce with clove spice, toasted garlic, parsley, hazelnuts and one egg yolk
5. Dissolve the sauce with the same broth and add it to the pot to cook for a while
6. If the sauce is not very thick, add a couple of egg yolks and spread them on top
