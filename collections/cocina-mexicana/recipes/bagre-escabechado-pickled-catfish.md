## Ingredients

- one catfish, cut into portions
- lard
- garlic, chopped
- one cup of clean pine nuts
- twelve cloves
- fine pepper
- Tabasco pepper
- ginger
- vinegar
- salt
- lime or cooked lemon slices
- fried garlic cloves

## Directions

1. Clean the catfish and cut it into portions, fry it in lard with chopped garlic.
2. Form a sauce by grinding one cup of clean pine nuts, twelve cloves, an equal amount of fine pepper, an equal amount of Tabasco pepper and a piece of ginger, and when everything is very well ground dissolve it in vinegar, and add the necessary salt; strain it.
3. Once the fish is cold, add this broth with slices of cooked lime or lemon, adding in the layers of fish that you place, fried garlic cloves.
4. Cover it and it will last for many days.
