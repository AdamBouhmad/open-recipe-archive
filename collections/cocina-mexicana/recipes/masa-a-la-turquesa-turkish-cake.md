## Ingredients

- 1 pound sweet shelled almonds
- 1 pound flour
- 1/2 pound butter
- 12 ounces sugar
- a little saffron
- eggs as needed

## Directions

1. Grind together the almonds, flour, butter, sugar, and saffron
2. While grinding, add eggs gradually until the batter becomes soft and smooth
3. Grease a paddle or platform with butter
4. Place and spread the batter on it
5. Bake in a gentle oven
