## Ingredients

- five pounds of flour
- one quarter liter of white wine
- lard
- syrup

## Directions

1. Make the dough with five pounds of flour, one quarter liter of white wine, and a little lard
2. After kneading, extend with a roller on a table, cutting the fritters long with the pastry wheel and curling them with the hand so they have the shape of a fan
3. Fry and put in syrup
