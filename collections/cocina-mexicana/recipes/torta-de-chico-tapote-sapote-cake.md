## Ingredients

- Chico tapote
- Cloves
- Pepper
- Egg whites
- Egg yolks
- Mamón
- Syrup
- Pastille

## Directions

1. Clean and place in a napkin and squeeze
2. Grind cloves and a little pepper, and for each chico tapote three egg whites and six yolks
3. Dissolve half a mamón and mix everything
4. Once drained, add to the syrup with its pastille on top
