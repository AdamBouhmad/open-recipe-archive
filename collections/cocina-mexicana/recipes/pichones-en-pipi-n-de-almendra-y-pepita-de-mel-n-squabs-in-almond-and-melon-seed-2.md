## Ingredients

- almendras (blanched almonds)
- pepitas de melón (melon seeds)
- pan (bread)
- chiles anchos (wide dried chiles)
- manteca (lard)
- caldo (broth)
- pichones (squabs)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- canela molida (ground cinnamon)

## Directions

1. After blanching the almonds, toast them and brown a piece of bread in lard
2. Fry the wide dried chiles, destemmed, until toasted without burning, and grind them well
3. Grind the melon seeds separately, strain them well, and then fry the chile in lard
4. Once well fried, add the almond and melon seed broth to fry also
5. Add water or broth and the squabs cooked in salted water, letting it boil until the pipián has thickened
6. Dust with ground cinnamon, and just before serving, add salt, as adding it earlier would break the pipián
