## Ingredients

- beef tendons
- tomato sauce

## Directions

1. Prepare as in the preceding method, with the difference that after placing in a crown shape, place tomato sauce in the middle
2. They may be prepared this way with different sauces which give their names to the dishes, such as tendons with asparagus tips, lentil substance, etc.
