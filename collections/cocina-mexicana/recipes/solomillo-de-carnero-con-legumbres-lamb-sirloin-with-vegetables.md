## Ingredients

- Lamb shoulder
- Bacon strips
- Spinach
- Green beans
- Cauliflower
- Beans
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Take a lamb shoulder and after removing the bones, lard it with bacon strips and place on the spit.
2. Once roasted, serve, garnishing the plate with vegetables such as spinach, green beans, cauliflower, beans, potatoes, etc.
