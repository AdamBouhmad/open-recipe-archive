## Ingredients

- milk
- cinnamon
- sugar
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Cook good milk with cinnamon sticks and sufficient sugar
2. Once well reduced, set aside and let cool
3. Mix three egg yolks for each cup of milk, or add another yolk if the yolks are pale or small
4. Strain and follow the procedures of the previous articles
