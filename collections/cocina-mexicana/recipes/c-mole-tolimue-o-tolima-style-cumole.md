## Ingredients

- one chicken
- one real of pork loin
- longanizas
- chorizos
- morcillas dulces (sweet blood sausage)
- pasilla chile, deveined
- sesame seeds, toasted
- almonds with skin
- peeled calabaza seeds
- peanuts
- lard
- chicken broth
- salt

## Directions

1. Toast in lard half a real of deveined pasilla chile
2. Toast sesame seeds, almonds with skin, peeled calabaza seeds and peanuts
3. Fry this mixture in lard until the fat releases
4. Add the well-ground chile
5. Once everything is well fried, add the chicken, other meats, broth and salt
6. Season to taste
