## Ingredients

- pork loin
- salt
- vinegar
- herbabuena
- oregano
- wine
- two broad dried chiles, deveined
- saffron
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- raw eggs
- raisins or almonds
- ham or hard-boiled egg
- parsley leaves
- ground biscuit
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Mince the pork loin and cook with salt, a little vinegar, herbabuena and oregano
2. Once cooked, mince the meat again
3. Mix with a little wine, vinegar, two broad dried chiles (deveined, soaked and ground) with saffron
4. Grind separately clove, cinnamon and pepper, and add to the mixture
5. Add a pair of raw eggs and form the dough
6. Form balls larger than a walnut, placing in the center raisins or almonds, ham or hard-boiled egg, and one or two parsley leaves
7. Roll in ground biscuit, dip in beaten eggs, and fry
8. Place in a spiced broth or clemole, or better yet in a tomato sauce ground with wine or vinegar and spices in due proportion
