## Ingredients

- sliced lemons with white pith removed
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- parsley
- tarragon
- chile powder

## Directions

1. Cut slices of lemons from which the white pith has been removed
2. Place them in a vessel with oil, vinegar, salt and pepper, minced garlic, parsley and tarragon, and a little chile powder
3. Mix everything well
4. This sauce can be served for grilled fish
