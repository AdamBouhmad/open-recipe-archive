## Ingredients

- Turkey giblets
- Salt
- Lard
- Flour
- Green chile
- Cinnamon
- Parsley
- Ham

## Directions

1. Cook with salt, and after giving a boil, fry with lard and a little flour
2. Add a little green chile, cinnamon, parsley and chopped ham
3. Leave to cook over slow fire and serve after two hours
