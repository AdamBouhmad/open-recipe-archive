## Ingredients

- Chou paste dough
- Eggs
- Frangipane (franpipan), stewed or seasoned
- Puff pastry scraps
- Flour

## Directions

1. Make a chou paste dough in the common manner
2. Wet with eggs so it is not too liquid
3. Add a little seasoned or stewed frangipane
4. Extend or flatten with a rolling pin
5. Cut some rounds with a pasta cutter of three and a half inches
6. Place the prepared dough with egg on these rounds, forming three-cornered hats
7. Lightly brown on top and put in the oven
8. When cooked, arrange and serve
