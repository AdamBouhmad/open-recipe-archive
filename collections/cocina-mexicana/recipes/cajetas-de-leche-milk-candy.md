## Ingredients

- one pound of rice
- sixteen cuartillos of milk
- eight pounds of sugar
- half a pound of peeled and ground almonds

## Directions

1. Wash and dry one pound of rice one day before making the candy
2. Grind it dry the next day so the powder is very fine
3. Dissolve in eight cuartillos of milk and strain through a cloth
4. In other eight cuartillos of milk, add eight pounds of well-ground sugar, grinding until it dissolves well, and strain through the same cloth
5. Mix everything and put on the fire until it thickens a little
6. Add half a pound of peeled almond ground with a little milk
7. Then bring it to the candy point
