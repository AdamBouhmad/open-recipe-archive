## Ingredients

- Fresh butter
- Jitomates (tomatoes), cut into four pieces
- Some onions, cut into pieces
- Salt
- Pepper
- A little fat-free broth

## Directions

1. Fry the jitomates cut into four pieces with some onions also cut into pieces in fresh butter with good seasoning of salt and pepper.
2. When the jitomates are well fried, add a little fat-free broth and finish cooking over low heat.
3. Strain the sauce through a fine sieve or colander, pressing it through with a spoon.
4. If it is too thin, reduce it over low heat, being careful as it sticks easily to the pot.
5. If it seems too thick, thin it with broth.
