## Ingredients

- tench
- salt
- flour
- lemon juice
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare as described in previous articles
2. Open along the back
3. Dust with salt and flour
4. Add the juice of some lemons
5. Fry in butter until golden
6. Serve dry
