## Ingredients

- squabs, well cleaned
- water
- tomatoes, finely chopped
- salt
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- raw lard
- white wine

## Directions

1. Place the squabs in a pot with only the water necessary for them to cook
2. Add finely chopped tomatoes, salt, cloves, cinnamon, pepper, plenty of raw lard and a little white wine
3. Cover the pot and let the squabs cook until all the broth is consumed
4. Serve immediately
