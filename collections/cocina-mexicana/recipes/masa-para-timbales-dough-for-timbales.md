## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- water
- four tablespoons of cooking oil
- one quarter of butter or lard
- two egg yolks
- salt

## Directions

1. Place one pound of flour on a table and make a hollow in the middle
2. Add a little water, four tablespoons of cooking oil, one quarter of butter or lard, two egg yolks, and salt
3. Knead everything together and add flour so that the dough becomes very firm
