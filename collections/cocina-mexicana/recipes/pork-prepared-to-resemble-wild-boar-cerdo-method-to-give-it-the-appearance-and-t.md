## Ingredients

- Young, not very fat pig
- vinegar
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- garlic
- sage
- cilantro
- salt
- juniper berries
- pepper
- clove
- parsley
- onion
- clover
- tansy or peppermint
- green citrus rind

## Directions

1. Choose a young pig that is not very fat and tender, which can be recognized by how easily the skin tears when stretched
2. If to be served whole, twist the legs like a suckling pig; if to be served divided, cut the ribs and loins
3. Leave these pieces for eight days to marinate in vinegar seasoned with thyme, bay leaf, basil, garlic, sage, cilantro, salt, juniper berries, pepper, clove, parsley, onion, clover, and some branches of tansy or peppermint, and green citrus rind
4. After eight days the pork will have the color and taste of wild boar
