## Ingredients

- tuna
- salt
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Empty the tuna as soon as it comes out of the water
2. Divide into pieces
3. Place on grill to roast
4. Fry in oil, seasoning with salt and pepper
5. Finally, place in wooden casks with more oil and a little vinegar
