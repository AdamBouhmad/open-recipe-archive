## Ingredients

- fish
- eggs
- parsley
- onion
- sugar
- oil
- flour
- vinegar
- water
- tomato

## Directions

1. After washing well, separate the bones and skin, and chop the meat very finely.
2. Beat a sufficient quantity of eggs with parsley and fried, crushed onion, adding a pinch of sugar.
3. Mix the fish with this mixture and form the meatballs.
4. Fry them in oil.
5. If you want to serve them with sauce, add a little flour and a few drops of vinegar to the same oil in which they were fried.
6. Mix well and add a little warm water.
7. To thicken this sauce, grind two meatballs with some fried onion or some fried tomato.
