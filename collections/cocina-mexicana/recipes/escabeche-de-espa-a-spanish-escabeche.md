## Ingredients

- fish
- good quality oil
- raw oil
- vinegar in regular quantity
- salt
- pepper
- clove
- bay leaves
- fine herbs

## Directions

1. Partially cook the fish on grills
2. Fry it in good oil
3. Place it in barrels or small casks with a sauce made of raw oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, clove, bay leaves, and fine herbs
4. Ensure the fish is covered with the sauce
5. Seal the barrels well
6. If much time passes before eating, add sauce to each barrel equivalent to what has been consumed
