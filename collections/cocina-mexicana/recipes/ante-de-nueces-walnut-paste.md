## Ingredients

- Large walnuts
- Syrup (medium point)
- Red pastille
- Plenty of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind large walnuts
2. Mix with syrup at medium point, red pastille and plenty of cinnamon
3. When the paste reaches medium point, arrange in layers over mamones
