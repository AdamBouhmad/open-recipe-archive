## Ingredients

- Kidneys
- Salt
- Lemon
- Onion
- Garlic
- Lard
- Flour
- Carlon wine
- Jerez wine
- Parsley
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut the kidneys into pieces
2. Give them a bath of salt and lemon and let sit for a while
3. Chop onion and garlic, fry in lard without browning and then add the kidneys
4. Add a little flour to the mixture to brown with the seasoning
5. Cover so the kidneys cook without adding water
6. After they have boiled, add half a glass of carlon wine, the same amount of Jerez wine, chopped parsley and a little vinegar
7. Cover again and let cook
