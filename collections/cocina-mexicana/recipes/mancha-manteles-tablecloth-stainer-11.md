## Ingredients

- almonds
- pumpkin seeds
- chile seeds
- sesame, all toasted
- roasted tomatoes
- soaked ancho chiles
- lard
- salt
- pork loin
- small chorizos
- longaniza, all cooked
- zucchini
- vinegar
- sugar
- broth
- olives

## Directions

1. Grind and fry almonds, toasted pumpkin seeds, chile seeds, and sesame with roasted tomatoes and soaked ancho chiles in lard with salt
2. Once ready, add pork loin, small chorizos, and longaniza, all cooked, zucchini, vinegar, sugar, and broth
3. When removing from heat, add olives
