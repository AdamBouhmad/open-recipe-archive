## Ingredients

- squash
- onion
- parsley
- spearmint leaves
- salt
- tomatoes
- oil
- pepper

## Directions

1. Cut into large pieces
2. Place in a pot with fried onion, parsley, spearmint leaves, salt and tomatoes
3. Turn frequently until braised
4. If cooked with water and salt, season with raw oil and pepper
