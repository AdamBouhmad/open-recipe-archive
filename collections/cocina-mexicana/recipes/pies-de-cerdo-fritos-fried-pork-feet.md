## Ingredients

- Pork feet
- Salt
- Breadcrumbs
- Ground black pepper
- Lard
- Sesame seeds, toasted

## Directions

1. Clean the feet and cook them with salt until well done.
2. Remove the bones without breaking the feet into pieces.
3. Grate a lot of bread and mix it with ground pepper.
4. Roll the cooked feet well in the breadcrumb mixture.
5. Grease a pot with lard and place a layer of feet.
6. Sprinkle with more grated bread and pepper.
7. Place another layer of feet, sprinkling with a little ground salt.
8. Continue this way until finished.
9. Pour melted lard over everything and cook over two gentle fires until they brown slightly.
10. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds on top and serve.
