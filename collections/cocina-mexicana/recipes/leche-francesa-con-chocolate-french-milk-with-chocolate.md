## Ingredients

- A little milk
- Flour
- Cinnamon sticks
- One tablet of chocolate

## Directions

1. Sweeten a little milk and thicken with flour
2. Strain through a sieve and heat with cinnamon sticks
3. When it is near the consistency of light manjar blanco, add one tablet of chocolate
4. Let it reach the proper consistency and pour into serving plates
