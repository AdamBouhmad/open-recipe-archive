## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Sardines
- Butter or bacon
- Tomatoes
- Lemon juice
- Pepper
- Salt
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the partridges.
2. Insert two cleaned sardines inside each bird.
3. Roast with butter or bacon.
4. If tomatoes are available, place them on top or add lemon juice, a pinch of pepper, salt, and parsley.
5. Roast between two fires.
6. Remove the sardines before serving.
