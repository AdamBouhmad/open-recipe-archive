## Ingredients

- sugar
- ground almonds
- egg yolks
- virgin honey

## Directions

1. Put one pound of sugar to make a syrup at ball point, and once it is thus, mix in half a pound of finely ground almonds
2. Let it reach a cajeta point and remove from heat to cool
3. Add twenty egg yolks, return to heat for the egg to cook, and then add a spoonful of virgin honey
