## Ingredients

- lettuces
- fresh butter
- flour
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lemon juice
- vinegar

## Directions

1. After cleaning the lettuces and tying them with thread, drain them well.
2. Put a pan on the fire with fresh butter in which a good spoonful of flour has been dissolved.
3. Let it boil for a while and then season with salt, pepper and nutmeg, adding lemon juice and vinegar.
4. When they have boiled for a quarter hour, they can be eaten.
