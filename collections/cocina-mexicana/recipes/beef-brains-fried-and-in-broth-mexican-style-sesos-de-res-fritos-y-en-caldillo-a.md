## Ingredients

- beef brains
- lemon juice
- onions
- parsley
- bay leaf
- salt
- eggs
- lime slices

## Directions

1. Place brains in warm water to clean them of veins and the thin skin covering them
2. Then place in boiling water and after a few minutes remove and place in cold water
3. Add the juice of a large lemon or two small ones, a couple of onions in quarters, a bouquet of parsley, a little bay leaf and fine salt
4. Bring to a boil and when cooked, remove and slice when cold
5. Coat the slices with beaten eggs, fry and serve with salt and pepper and lemon slices, or cook them with the tomato broth of stuffed peppers, increasing a little the amount of cumin and fried chopped parsley
