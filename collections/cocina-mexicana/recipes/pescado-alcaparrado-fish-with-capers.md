## Ingredients

- capers
- bread
- garlic
- lard
- oil
- cooked fish

## Directions

1. Grind a good amount of capers with soaked bread and peeled garlic cloves.
2. Fry everything in lard and oil.
3. Once fried, incorporate the already-cooked fish.
4. If whole, place on a platter and sprinkle entirely with this sauce.
