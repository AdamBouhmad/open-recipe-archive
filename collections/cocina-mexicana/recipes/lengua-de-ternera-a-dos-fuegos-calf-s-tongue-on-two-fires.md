## Ingredients

- Calf's tongue
- Lean ham, cut into small pieces
- Fat ham, sliced
- Onions, cut into small pieces
- Carrots, cut into small pieces
- Mushrooms, cut into small pieces
- Parsley
- Bay leaf
- Black pepper
- Salt
- One glass of broth
- One glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Peel the tongue and lard it with small pieces of lean ham
2. Arrange it in a pot lined with slices of fat ham
3. Add the onions, carrots, mushrooms, parsley, and bay leaf, seasoned with pepper and salt
4. Moisten with one glass of broth and one glass of white wine
5. Cover with more slices of fat ham
6. Place the pot on gentle heat and put burning coals on the lid or on a comal
7. After six hours, remove the tongue when cooked
8. Divide it lengthwise into two parts and serve with a spicy sauce
