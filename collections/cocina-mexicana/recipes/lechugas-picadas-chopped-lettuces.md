## Ingredients

- lettuces
- salt
- water
- four ounces butter
- pepper
- flour
- broth
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Parboil well-washed lettuces in salted water, keeping only the most tender parts.
2. Remove from hot water and place in fresh water, and when cooled, chop and put in a pan with four ounces of butter, salt and a small amount of pepper.
3. When the lettuces have fried somewhat, add a small amount of flour proportionate to the lettuces.
4. Mix everything well, add broth and let boil for a quarter hour.
5. Arrange on a plate with toasted bread around it and serve.
