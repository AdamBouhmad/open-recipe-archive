## Ingredients

- ripe tomatoes, ground
- clean garlic cloves, chopped
- onions, sliced
- lard
- cooked chickpeas
- water
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind ripe tomatoes, chop clean garlic cloves, slice onions, and fry everything in lard.
2. Add cooked chickpeas as mentioned in previous articles, and then add the necessary water, seasoned with corresponding salt.
