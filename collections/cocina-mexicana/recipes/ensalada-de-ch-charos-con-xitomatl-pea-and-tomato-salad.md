## Ingredients

- peas
- green beans
- small squash
- tomatoes
- green chiles
- vinegar
- salt
- small onions
- avocado

## Directions

1. Cook peas, green beans and very small squash
2. Shell the beans
3. Cook tomatoes and after removing the skin blend with some green chiles
4. Dissolve this in vinegar and add sufficient salt
5. Mix all the cooked vegetables with this broth
6. Add cooked small onions and avocado pieces
