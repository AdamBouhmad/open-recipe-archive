## Ingredients

- squash
- salt
- dried wide chile
- cumin
- garlic
- vinegar
- oregano
- oil
- cheese

## Directions

1. Put squash to cook with salt and let cool after cooking
2. Grind a quarter portion of soaked and deveined dried wide chile, cumin and half a head of garlic
3. Add vinegar to thin it and incorporate the squash with a little oregano
4. When serving add oil and cheese
