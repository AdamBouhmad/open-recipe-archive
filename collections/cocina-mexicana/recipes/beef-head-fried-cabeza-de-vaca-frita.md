## Ingredients

- One beef head, cooked naturally or otherwise
- Marinade
- Frying batter

## Directions

1. Cook a beef head naturally or another way
2. Cut into medium pieces
3. Place in a dish and pour marinade over, ensuring all pieces are well soaked in the liquid
4. Make a frying batter
5. After draining, coat the pieces with batter
6. Fry, noting the oil does not need to be very hot
