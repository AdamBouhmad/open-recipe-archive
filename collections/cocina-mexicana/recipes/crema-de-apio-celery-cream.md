## Ingredients

- two quartillos of water
- two celery feet, washed and cut into pieces
- four quartillos of cream
- four ounces of sugar
- a little cilantro
- cinnamon
- lemon peel
- one tablespoon of orange-flower water

## Directions

1. Cook two celery feet washed and cut into pieces in a pot with two quartillos of water
2. Strain the broth through a sieve
3. Add four quartillos of cream, four ounces of sugar, a little cilantro and cinnamon, lemon peel, and one tablespoon of orange-flower water
4. Allow to boil until it consumes half
5. Set aside and add finely chopped poultry sweetbreads while the cream is still warm
6. Mix everything, strain, place over coals, cover with a comal with fire, and when it reaches the proper point, cool on snow
