## Ingredients

- One real of fresh cheese
- Two hard biscuits
- Five eggs
- Panela honey of medium point

## Directions

1. Dissolve the fresh cheese, grind the biscuits and mix them together
2. Beat five eggs
3. Make panela honey of medium point
4. Once everything is mixed with the egg, add it to the honey until it sets like a cake
5. Remove slightly from the fire and add the egg on top and place a griddle with little heat on it
