## Ingredients

- larks
- cooked forcemeat
- fried breadcrumbs
- ham slices
- buttered paper
- mushrooms or sauce

## Directions

1. Open the larks along the spine and debone them
2. Fill them with cooked forcemeat
3. Place a bed of the same forcemeat in a platter or baking dish
4. Arrange the larks in a circle on top, covering the spaces between with forcemeat, showing only the upper part of the breast in the center of the platter
5. Cover with wheels of fried breadcrumbs
6. Cover the entire dish with ham slices and buttered paper
7. Place in a campaign oven or between two fires until cooked
8. Remove the bread and ham slices and serve with mushrooms or with one of the corresponding sauces
