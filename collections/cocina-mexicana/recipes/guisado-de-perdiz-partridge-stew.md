## Ingredients

- partridges
- grill
- oil
- whole garlic heads
- parsley
- whole cloves
- whole peppercorns
- sour orange juice
- ham slice
- hot water
- flour

## Directions

1. Well clean and wash the partridges
2. Put them to brown on the grill on both sides
3. When browned, put them in a pot with oil, two whole heads of garlic, plenty of chopped parsley, six whole cloves, six whole peppercorns, and sour orange juice according to the quantity so it is semi-dark
4. Add a slice of ham and put on the fire to fry
5. When the parsley is fried, add hot water to cook
6. When half cooked, add flour to thicken, stirring so it does not form lumps
7. Serve thick
