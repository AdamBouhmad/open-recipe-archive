## Ingredients

- Veal pulps
- Pork jowl
- Lard
- Salt
- Small onions
- Garlic cloves
- Cloves
- Ground cinnamon
- Nutmeg
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Wine
- Water
- Fried and ground toast

## Directions

1. Bleed the veal pulps, divide into proportional pieces, and lard with pork jowl
2. Place in a pot with lard, salt, small onions, and minced garlic cloves
3. Add cloves, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, bay leaf, thyme, and sufficient wine and water
4. Cover the pot well and place on the fire
5. Let the veal cook, thickening the broth with fried and ground toast if too much remains after the meat is cooked
