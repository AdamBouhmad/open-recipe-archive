## Ingredients

- 1 young rabbit, cleaned and emptied
- butter
- ground spices
- nutmeg
- salt
- peppercorns
- parsley
- shallots

## Directions

1. Divide the rabbit into portions, removing the offal and patting dry to remove all blood
2. Put a piece of butter in a pan and when slightly hot, add the rabbit with ground spices, nutmeg, salt, and peppercorns
3. Place the pan over high heat
4. When the portions are browned, add finely minced parsley and shallots
5. Leave the pan on the fire for another three or four minutes
6. Remove the rabbit and serve immediately
