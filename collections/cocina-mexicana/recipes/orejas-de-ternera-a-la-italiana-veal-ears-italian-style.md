## Ingredients

- Veal ears
- Bacon slices
- Parsley
- Onions
- Lemon slices
- Meat broth
- White wine
- Greased paper
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the veal ears and pass them through flame
2. Scald them and place in cold water
3. Cook them in water or in a covered pot lined with bacon slices, with parsley, onions, and lemon slices
4. Add meat broth and good white wine
5. Cover the ears with other bacon slices and top with greased paper
6. Cook for an hour and a half until tender
7. Remove and drain the ears, then tie the tips as if they were onions
8. Serve with Italian sauce
