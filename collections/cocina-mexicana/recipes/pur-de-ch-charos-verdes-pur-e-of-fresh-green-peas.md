## Ingredients

- two pounds of fresh green peas
- salt
- sugar
- broth
- bread

## Directions

1. Cook the green peas in boiling water with salt over high heat so they do not lose their color
2. After cooking, drain the peas and place them in cold water, passing them through a sieve or tamiz
3. Put them in a clean saucepan with a little sugar
4. Add sufficient broth so it is neither too clear nor too thick
5. Serve very hot with some small pieces of fried bread
