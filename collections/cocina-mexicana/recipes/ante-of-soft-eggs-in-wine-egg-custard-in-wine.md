## Ingredients

- One caldron cup of wine
- Two cups cold syrup at point
- Fifteen egg yolks
- Sponge cake (mamón), sliced
- Sugar
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix one caldron cup of wine with two cups of cold syrup at point
2. Beat fifteen egg yolks in this mixture
3. Place over heat and stir until it reaches a paste-like point
4. Pour over layers of sliced sponge cake
5. Dust the top layer with sugar and ground cinnamon
