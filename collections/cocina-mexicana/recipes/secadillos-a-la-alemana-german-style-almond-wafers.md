## Ingredients

- one pound sweet almonds
- twelve ounces powdered sugar
- a little candied orange blossom
- egg whites
- virgin wax
- a little oil

## Directions

1. Peel one pound of sweet almonds and cut them into thin slices, thinner than for nogado
2. Place them in a vessel with twelve ounces of powdered sugar and a little candied orange blossom, and knead with egg whites
3. Rub the tin sheets with virgin wax and a little oil
4. Spread the paste on top as thin as possible and place in a moderately hot oven
5. When the secadillos are half cooked, remove from the oven and cut into very equal square pieces
6. Return to the oven for a moment, and when removed, shape them as desired
7. Once cold, place in a sieve and dry in the stove until serving time
