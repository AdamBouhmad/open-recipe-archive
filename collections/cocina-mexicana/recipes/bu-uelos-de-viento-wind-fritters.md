## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- anise
- butter
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. For each pound of flour, use twelve eggs
2. Put the flour in a pan, add to the pound of flour its anise, a little butter, its salt, a little less than three cups of water
3. Cook until it forms clumps, take a thin piece and press it on your hand; the butter should already be fried
4. Then lower the heat and gradually dissolve it in warm butter and fry
