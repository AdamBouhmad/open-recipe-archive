## Ingredients

- Juice of ripe oranges
- Brandy
- Sugar
- Sponge cake
- Eight eggs, beaten
- Pastille and dragées

## Directions

1. Add brandy and sugar to the orange juice until it is sweet and sour
2. Add sponge cake until it absorbs the liquid
3. Garnish with eight beaten eggs
4. Top with pastille and dragées
