## Ingredients

- Water
- Two handfuls of soda
- Green apricots
- Clarified syrup
- Orange peel
- Orange juice

## Directions

1. Boil water in a pot with two handfuls of soda
2. Add the apricots and remove from heat after the first boil
3. Wash them well and prick with a pin
4. Place in cold water
5. Return to boiling water to blanch them and remove from heat
6. Wrap in cloth, place in cold water, and drain on a sieve
7. Leave covered and give them one boil in clarified syrup
8. Remove from heat and leave in the syrup for one or two hours
9. Drain and increase the syrup temperature slightly, adding the blanched orange peel and juice of one orange
10. Give the apricots a second boil covered
11. Let them cool in an appropriate vessel
12. Place in compote dishes, passing the syrup through clean cloth over them
