## Ingredients

- Half a lobe of beef liver
- Six cuartillos of water
- Six to eight turnips, sliced
- Two or three sprigs of parsley
- A dozen jujubes

## Directions

1. Soak in water and bleed half a lobe of beef liver, and cut like chanfaina into small pieces.
2. Put it in a pot with six cuartillos of water, six to eight sliced turnips, two or three sprigs of parsley, and a dozen jujubes.
3. Bring to a boil and skim.
4. Let one third be consumed or reduce to four cuartillos, and pass through a silk sieve.
