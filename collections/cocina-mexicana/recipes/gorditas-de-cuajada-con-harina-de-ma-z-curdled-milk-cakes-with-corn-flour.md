## Ingredients

- Two calderas of corn flour (prepared as in previous recipe)
- Two calderas of curdled milk (well pressed, drained, and ground)
- Sugar (sifted, to taste)
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Anise (all ground)
- Egg yolks (as needed)

## Directions

1. Prepare the corn flour as in the previous recipe
2. Measure two calderas of corn flour and two calderas of curdled milk that has been well pressed, drained, and ground
3. Mix both ingredients together
4. Add to taste: sifted sugar, cinnamon, clove, and ground anise
5. Add the necessary egg yolks to moisten the dough
6. Form the dough by incorporating all ingredients without kneading
7. Place small portions on orange leaves or papers
8. Transfer to tin sheets and place in the oven to bake
