## Ingredients

- milk corresponding to one pound flour
- one pound flour
- twelve eggs
- anise water
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. Add the milk corresponding to one pound of flour, which will be mixed with it and with twelve eggs (whites and yolks)
2. Put on the fire until it has taken the convenient point, and then also add anise water and a little piece of sugar, kneading well until it comes away from the pan
3. Put them in the syringe adding lard so they come loose
