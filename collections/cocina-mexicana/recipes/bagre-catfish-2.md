## Ingredients

- bagre
- water
- lemon
- lard
- jitomates

## Directions

1. Soak in water and once soaked scrape with a knife to remove the dark part
2. Once clean cut into slices without dividing it and squeeze some lemons on it
3. Wash again and dry well with a cloth and then fry in lard
4. Roast some jitomates
