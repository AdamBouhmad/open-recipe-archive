## Ingredients

- 1/2 pound sugar
- 1/2 pound virgin honey
- 5 egg whites, beaten
- 1/4 pound purified or Holland sugar in small lumps
- fragrant water or citrus peel (citron, lime, orange, or lemon)

## Directions

1. Clarify the sugar and honey separately
2. Mix both together and incorporate 5 beaten egg whites when they reach the flower point
3. Continue beating and stirring until reaching the caramel point
4. Add 1/4 pound of peeled almonds in small pieces, well-dried in the stove or over a hot comal but not toasted
5. Add 1/4 pound of purified or Holland sugar in small lumps, moistened with fragrant water or rubbed one by one with citrus peel
6. Incorporate everything with a spoon, stirring strongly
7. Remove from fire promptly to avoid overdoing the caramel point
8. Pour immediately onto white wafers spread on a table over paper
9. Extend the paste with a roller to 2 or 3 fingers thick
10. Cut into pieces by dropping a sharp knife blade vertically onto the paste
11. Store in a dry place or serve immediately
