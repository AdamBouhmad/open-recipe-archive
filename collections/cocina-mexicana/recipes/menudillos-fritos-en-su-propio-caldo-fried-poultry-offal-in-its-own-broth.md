## Ingredients

- poultry offal (cleaned)
- cold water
- lard
- eggs
- salt

## Directions

1. After cleaning, place the offal in a pan with cold water, lard, eggs, and a little salt.
2. Let it boil until the water is consumed, leaving only the lard.
3. Fry in the remaining lard.
4. Arrange on a plate and serve, or use to garnish other dishes.
