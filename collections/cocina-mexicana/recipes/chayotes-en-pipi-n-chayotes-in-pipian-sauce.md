## Ingredients

- Chayotes, cooked
- Ingredients for pipían sauce (see pipían)

## Directions

1. Cut the cooked chayotes into small cubes.
2. Add them to a pot with the other ingredients that make up the pipián.
3. Let it simmer together until it reaches the proper consistency.
