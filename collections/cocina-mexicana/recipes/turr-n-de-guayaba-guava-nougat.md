## Ingredients

- five pounds of sugar
- three and a half pounds of cooked and strained guava

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the soft ball stage with five pounds of sugar and mix with three and a half pounds of cooked and strained guava, stirring and letting it reach the cajeta point, which is when when beaten the bottom of the pan is visible
2. Remove from heat, pour into boxes with wafers or paper, level with wet hands, and let it air until the next day when it is exposed to the sun, cooled and wrapped in papers
