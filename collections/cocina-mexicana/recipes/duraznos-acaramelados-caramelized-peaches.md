## Ingredients

- raw peaches or peaches preserved in brandy
- sugar syrup at caramel point

## Directions

1. Use raw peaches or candied peaches in brandy, letting them drain
2. immerse them in syrup at caramel point
3. thread them on small skewers, arranged at the joints of the drying rack or on the hoops of a drying basket
4. dry them in the stove
5. arrange in pyramid form or as desired for serving, making it pleasing to the eye
