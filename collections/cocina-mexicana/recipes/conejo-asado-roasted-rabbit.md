## Ingredients

- Rabbit
- Butter
- Oil
- Onion
- Water
- Salt
- Good vinegar
- Parsley
- Tomatoes
- Bread
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Oregano

## Directions

1. After skinning the rabbit, cut it into good portions and fry them in equal parts of butter and oil with plenty of sliced onion
2. When well fried, add water, necessary salt and a little good vinegar, and let it boil until the rabbit is very tender
3. Cooked, remove it and fry again with oil and chopped onion
4. Grind a portion of parsley leaves with three or four well-roasted tomatoes and a slice of bread browned in butter; fry this together with the rabbit
5. Add broth from the same water in which it cooked and the onion; then grind cloves and pepper, not too sparingly, add them and let it thicken, adding a little more salt
6. Also chop a little fresh oregano and when thick, add raw oil, letting it rest in the heat
7. This broth should remain very thick
