## Ingredients

- one brain
- onion
- parsley
- oregano
- French oil
- butter
- sardines
- ground pepper
- grated cheese
- one large cup of water
- one small spoonful of French oil
- flour (same quantity as water)
- two eggs

## Directions

1. Cook a brain and then chop it very well
2. Chop some onion, parsley and oregano
3. Fry everything in French oil and butter
4. Once the onion is golden, add the brains and sardines (with spines removed and torn apart), a little ground pepper and grated cheese
5. Put one large cup of water in a pot with one small spoonful of French oil and bring to a boil
6. Add the same quantity of flour little by little so it does not form lumps
7. Once it is a little cool, incorporate two well-beaten eggs with both white and yolk
8. Check the dough by taking it between your fingers so it does not stick; make the empanadas the size of a peso and thin
9. Fill them with the aforementioned filling so they are like little empanadas and put them to air dry on a sheet
