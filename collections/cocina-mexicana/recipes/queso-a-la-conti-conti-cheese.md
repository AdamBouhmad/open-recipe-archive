## Ingredients

- four quarters of a quart of cream
- lemon zest
- orange flower water
- powdered sugar
- egg yolks
- cream

## Directions

1. Boil and let reduce the same quantity of cream from the previous article, with the same ingredients and powdered sugar
2. Bind immediately with some egg yolks and curdle over embers
3. Then drain in a cheese strainer and serve with cream around it and sugar
