## Ingredients

- five or six anchovies
- butter, equal amount to anchovy meat

## Directions

1. Wash the anchovies well
2. Remove the meat from the anchovies
3. Pound the meat well
4. Pass it through a fine hair sieve or strainer without adding any liquid
5. Mix and combine with an equal amount of butter
6. Use in dishes that require anchovy butter
