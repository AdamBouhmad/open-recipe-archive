## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- half a pound of melted butter
- half a pound of yeast
- fifteen eggs
- half a pound of ground sugar
- five additional eggs
- one pozuelo of orange blossom water
- cooked milk

## Directions

1. Mix three pounds of flour with half a pound of melted butter and half a pound of yeast until well incorporated
2. Add fifteen eggs and half a pound of ground sugar and knead well
3. Let rest in a warm place for three to four hours until the dough rises
4. Add five more eggs and one pozuelo of orange blossom water
5. Moisten the dough with cooked milk instead of water and knead well again
6. Form the biscuits on paper when the dough is neither hard nor too moist
7. Bake in the oven
