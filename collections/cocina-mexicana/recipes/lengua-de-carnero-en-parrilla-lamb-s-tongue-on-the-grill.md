## Ingredients

- cooked lamb's tongue
- two or three onions
- lard
- flour
- broth or white wine
- garlic
- parsley
- chives
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cook the tongues as previously described
2. Slice two or three onions and fry them in lard with a small handful of flour
3. Soak in broth or white wine, adding finely minced garlic, parsley, chives, salt and pepper
4. Add lemon juice and place on the grill until cooked through
