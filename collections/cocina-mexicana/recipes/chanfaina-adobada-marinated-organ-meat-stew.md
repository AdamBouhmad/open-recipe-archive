## Ingredients

- chiles anchos
- ajos
- cominos
- pimienta
- clavos
- pan
- vinagre
- manteca
- sal
- bofes
- hígados
- jamón
- longaniza
- chorizos
- pasas
- almendras
- caldo de la asadura
- aceite
- tornachiles

## Directions

1. Devein and soak two ancho chiles, then grind with garlic, cumin, pepper, cloves and bread soaked in vinegar
2. Fry all this in sufficient lard with salt
3. Add lungs and livers in small pieces, strips of ham, pieces of longaniza and small chorizos, all cooked together
4. Once fried, add raisins, almonds, vinegar, and strained broth from the roasted meat
5. Cook over two fires until it becomes thick and without broth
6. When removing from heat, add oil and quarter the small chiles
