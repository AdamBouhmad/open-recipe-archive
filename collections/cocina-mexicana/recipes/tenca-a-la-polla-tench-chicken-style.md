## Ingredients

- tench
- butter
- flour
- white wine
- salt
- black pepper
- bay leaf
- parsley
- chervil
- mushrooms
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Briefly immerse the tench in a pot of nearly boiling water and remove it
2. Remove the slime and scales with a knife
3. Divide into pieces and soak in water to remove excess fat
4. Place in a pan with heated butter and fry and sauté the tench portions
5. Add a spoonful of flour and mix with the rest
6. Moisten with a bottle of white wine
7. Add salt, black pepper, a bay leaf, a small bundle of parsley and chervil, other small bunches of cabbage-like parsley, and mushrooms
8. Allow the stew to season over moderately brisk heat
9. When everything is cooked, add a liaison of three egg yolks
10. Remove the bay leaf before serving and taste to check the seasoning
