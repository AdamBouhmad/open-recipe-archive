## Ingredients

- Guava skins
- Pulque
- Cinnamon, finely powdered
- Sugar to sweeten

## Directions

1. Take only the skins of the guavas, grind them well
2. Dissolve this mixture in the pulque and sweeten it
3. Add a little cinnamon made into very fine powder
4. Let ferment for three or four hours
5. Strain and serve
