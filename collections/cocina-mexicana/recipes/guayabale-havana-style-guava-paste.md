## Ingredients

- guavas
- sugar
- water

## Directions

1. Take a few guavas, peel them, cut them in half and remove all the insides with a spoon
2. Rinse the shells in cold water and place the insides in a vessel
3. Once clean, weigh the shells; for four pounds of guava, clarify five pounds of white sugar
4. Take one pound of the four pounds of guava and cut it into pieces, then grind the remaining three pounds
5. First add to the syrup to cook, then grind; when it reaches medium point, strain the insides through a sieve, and add all the jelly that comes out with a small cup of virgin honey
6. When it is ready to set point, take a small guinea plantain, chop it into pieces and add it; the point should be such that when you insert the paddle, the bottom of the pot is uncovered so it cuts well
7. Remove from heat and add small amounts of half-crushed sugar, about half a pound, which is most proper, and add more sugar on top
8. Pour into boxes and place in the sun
