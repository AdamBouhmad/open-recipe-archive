## Ingredients

- two handfuls of almonds
- egg white
- orange-blossom water
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Heat the almonds to peel them
2. As you peel them, place in cold water
3. Dry them immediately and pound in a mortar
4. Roll them occasionally with a little egg white and orange-blossom water beaten together to prevent the almonds from becoming oily
5. Grind well, and pass through a sieve if necessary so the paste is not grainy or lumpy
6. Place this paste in a pan and dry it with powdered sugar as done with common paste until it becomes manageable
7. Form a roll and let it rest for some time
8. Remove a base or cake that you put to dry in the oven in a cake pan
