## Ingredients

- Peaches, peeled
- 12 ounces sugar per pound of fruit
- Ground almonds from the peach stones

## Directions

1. Peel the peaches and cut them into thin slices
2. Put them to cook in a pan with twelve ounces of sugar for each pound of fruit
3. When the jam is close to reaching its setting point, mix in the ground almonds from inside the stones
