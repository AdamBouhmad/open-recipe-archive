## Ingredients

- Watermelon at good ripeness
- Lime or ash water, settled
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Select watermelons at good ripeness; use the red flesh for the table
2. Use only the white part of the slices
3. Remove the green skin
4. Cut the pieces into desired size and shape, or make them into flowers, stars, or other figures
5. As they are cut, place in settled lime or ash water
6. After two or three hours, remove and wash well in natural water
7. Cook and then prick in various places with a needle so the syrup will penetrate
8. Allow to boil in the syrup for some time over several days
9. Place in the sun to blanch
10. Finally, bring the syrup to preserve point
