## Ingredients

- beef loin
- jowl bacon, finely larded
- bay leaf
- parsley
- onion
- clove
- pepper
- nutmeg
- lard
- flour

## Directions

1. Take a piece of loin and lard it thickly with jowl bacon
2. Cook with a bunch of bay leaf, another of parsley, one or two onions, clove, pepper and a nutmeg
3. When cooked, remove from heat
4. Let the broth reduce if there is much
5. Remove all the leaves to leave the meat very clean
6. In the remaining lard add a little flour and let it take on a dark color
7. Add the meat to this
