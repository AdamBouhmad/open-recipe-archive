## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Sugar
- Fish glue (cola de pescado)

## Directions

1. Squeeze the juice from the oranges through a silk sieve
2. Let it rest and strain it
3. Clarify the syrup and pass it through a sieve after having mixed orange peels with it
4. Add the fish glue and the strained orange juice
5. Pour the jelly into small jars, or if preferred, into clean orange peels cut in half crosswise, surrounded with powdered sugar
6. Test the consistency by placing a little fish glue on a spoon over sugar to know if it has enough consistency; if not, add more
