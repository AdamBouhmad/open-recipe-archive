## Ingredients

- fat livers
- lard
- mushrooms
- fine herbs
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the livers in a pan
2. Chop immediately with lard, some mushrooms, fine herbs, salt and pepper
3. Season the toasts in the common way
