## Ingredients

- cucumbers
- ham slices
- carrots
- onions
- allspice
- cloves
- meat broth

## Directions

1. Cut cucumbers into small ovals and blanch for ten minutes
2. Let them cool and drain
3. Place ham slices in the bottom of a pot
4. Add cucumbers on top, cover with more ham
5. Add carrots, onions, allspice, and two cloves
6. Cook for half an hour
7. Prepare bread soup and place cucumbers on top
8. Pass the cooking broth through a silk sieve, degrease it, and pour over the soup
9. Alternatively, cook the blanched cucumbers in broth for three quarters of an hour and place on bread soup
