## Ingredients

- one arroba of honey
- eighteen cuartillos of water
- five pounds of white Spanish chalk, finely ground
- peels of three lemons
- two and a half pounds of clarifying charcoal
- six beaten eggs
- eighteen cuartillos of water

## Directions

1. Put the honey with eighteen cuartillos of water in a pot that can hold two arrobas
2. Dissolve the honey over fire
3. When dissolved, add five pounds of finely ground white Spanish chalk with the peels of three lemons
4. Stir carefully with a skimming spoon so it does not stick to the bottom of the pot
5. After some time, add two and a half pounds of clarifying charcoal and continue stirring
6. When the honey is about to boil, sprinkle it with a mixture of six beaten eggs and eighteen cuartillos of water until it resembles a sponge
7. Pass it through a cloth strainer as many times as necessary to give it proper clarity
8. Wash and drain the cloth one or two days before clarifying more honey, using a mixture of three eggs and water until it reaches ten degrees
