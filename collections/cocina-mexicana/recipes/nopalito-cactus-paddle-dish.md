## Ingredients

- nopal (cactus paddles)
- tequesquite
- garlic
- onion
- salt
- lard
- ancho chiles
- oil
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Remove all spines and the lower end of the cactus leaf
2. Cut into slices like green beans or into small squares
3. Cook in tequesquite water so they release their slime
4. Once cooked, fry with minced garlic and onion
5. When golden, add necessary salt
6. Add whole ancho chiles to the lard
7. Let the broth reduce and finish by adding oil and vinegar
