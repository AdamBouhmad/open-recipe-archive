## Ingredients

- oranges
- pulque
- cinnamon
- sugar

## Directions

1. Squeeze the oranges into the pulque
2. Strain immediately
3. Sweeten with sugar that has been rubbed on the orange peels to capture their aroma
4. Dust with cinnamon
