## Ingredients

- 2 eggs' worth of pastry cream
- 4 ounces powdered sugar
- 1 raw egg yolk
- a few Corinth raisins
- an equal amount of pine nuts
- candied or covered lemon rind in very small pieces
- a little powdered cinnamon
- double rose water
- a few drops of orange blossom water or lemon juice
- puff pastry dough

## Directions

1. Place the pastry cream, sugar, raw egg yolk, raisins, pine nuts, lemon rind, cinnamon, and rose water in a bowl
2. Mix everything together
3. Add a few drops of orange blossom water or lemon juice
4. Make two rounds of puff pastry the thickness and size of a plate
5. Place the mixture on one of the rounds and cover with the other round
6. Ensure the edges are well sealed
7. Place in the oven immediately
8. Bake for approximately half an hour
9. When half-cooked, dust with sugar and sprinkle with a few drops of plain water or orange blossom water
10. Return to the oven briefly to glaze the sugar
11. When removed, dust again with sugar and serve
