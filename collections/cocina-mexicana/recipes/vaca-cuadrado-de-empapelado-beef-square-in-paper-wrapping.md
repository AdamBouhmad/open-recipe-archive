## Ingredients

- square cut of beef
- scarlet tongue in strips
- butter
- parsley
- shallots
- mushrooms
- truffles
- garlic
- thyme
- bay leaf
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- brandy
- white wine
- German sauce
- fresh butter
- fine herbs
- ham slices
- oiled paper

## Directions

1. Prepare a square of beef as for roasting
2. Lard it with medium strips of scarlet tongue
3. Place in a saucepan with a large piece of butter, parsley, shallots, mushrooms, truffles, garlic, thyme, bay leaf, salt, pepper and nutmeg
4. Let cook for one hour
5. Remove the square and add to the herbs the remaining butter in the pan, a little brandy and a bottle of white wine
6. Let this mixture reduce to a jelly
7. Add to this two spoonfuls of German sauce and a bit of very fresh butter
8. Pour all this over the beef square and let cool
9. Garnish well with fine herbs and thin slices of ham
10. Wrap in sheets of oiled paper held with string
11. Place on the grill over hot fire
12. When the paper takes on good color, serve the beef in the paper, removing the string
13. Can serve separately a thick Spanish sauce
