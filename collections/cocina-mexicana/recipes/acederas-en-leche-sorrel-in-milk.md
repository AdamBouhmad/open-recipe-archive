## Ingredients

- sorrel
- butter
- one or two eggs, according to quantity
- flour
- milk

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous recipe and fry equally in butter
2. Break apart one or two eggs, according to the quantity, mix with flour adding another egg
3. Dissolve everything in milk, then pour this mixture onto the sorrel, which is stirred well so the mixture is complete
4. After cooking as the previous recipe, put on the platter and use in the same way
