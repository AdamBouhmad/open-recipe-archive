## Ingredients

- Purple sweet potato
- Pineapple ground
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Grind the purple sweet potato and mix with competent ground pineapple
2. Make syrup at half point and in it squeeze through a serviette the juice of the sweet potato and ground pineapple
3. Add flavorings and give it the point of jelly
