## Ingredients

- Desalted codfish
- Salt
- Three or four dried chiles
- Portion of peeled toasted almonds
- Cumin
- Pepper
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Butter
- Water from cooking the codfish
- Good vinegar
- Oregano
- Oil
- Large olives
- Pickled chiles

## Directions

1. After thoroughly desalting, put the codfish to cook with salt.
2. Grind three or four dried chiles, a portion of peeled toasted almonds, cumin, pepper, clove and cinnamon.
3. Fry in butter until the butter rises above; add a little of the water in which the codfish cooked, and the deboned fish.
4. Once it has boiled a couple of times, add a little good vinegar, abundant oregano and oil, large olives and pickled chiles.
5. Let it rest and serve.
