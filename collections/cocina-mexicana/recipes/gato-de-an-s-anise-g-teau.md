## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- two ounces of ground anise
- two ounces of ground star anise
- one egg white
- cream

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup with two pounds of sugar to the soft crack stage
2. Add two ounces of ground anise and two ounces of ground star anise
3. Remove from heat and mix in one egg white beaten with cream
4. Stir until the syrup rises again
5. Pour into molds or boxes
