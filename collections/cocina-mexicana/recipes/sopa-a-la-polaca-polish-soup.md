## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Water
- Beef broth
- Fennel

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes in water and after cooking cut them into equal round slices
2. Boil a handful of chopped fennel in broth for a quarter of an hour
3. Pour this broth over the potatoes that will have been placed in the soup tureen, arranging it so that there are enough potatoes to substitute for bread
