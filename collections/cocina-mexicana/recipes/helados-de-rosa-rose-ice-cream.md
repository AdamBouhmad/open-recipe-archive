## Ingredients

- rose petals, clean
- water
- sugar (at least one-half pound for each four quartillos of water)

## Directions

1. Crush clean rose petals and steep them in water.
2. Transfer the mixture back and forth between vessels many times so that the water becomes better impregnated with the perfume.
3. Strain through a sieve.
4. Add sugar in proportion, at least one-half pound for each four quartillos of water, because cold concentrates flavors.
5. Place the mixture in the ice cream churn and proceed as for other ice creams.
