## Ingredients

- Tomatoes
- onion
- veal
- ham
- parsley
- butter
- bread crumbs
- broth

## Directions

1. Remove the top lid from the tomatoes and scoop out the insides.
2. Chop onion, veal, ham, and parsley; mix everything together and fry well.
3. Use this to fill the tomatoes.
4. Grease a baking dish or pan with butter and place the stuffed tomatoes in it.
5. Sprinkle the remaining minced mixture with a little bread crumbs over the top, add a little broth, and cook over two fires (top and bottom heat).
