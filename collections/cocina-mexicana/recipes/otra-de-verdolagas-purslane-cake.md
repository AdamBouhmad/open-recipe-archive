## Ingredients

- purslane
- chorizo
- ham
- longaniza
- chickpeas
- egg yolks
- sugar
- cinnamon
- cloves
- butter
- beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Stew the purslane somewhat dry and with cooked and chopped chorizo, ham, and longaniza, make a paste of peeled cooked chickpeas and cooked egg yolks
2. Grind everything and incorporate with sugar, cinnamon, and cloves to taste
3. In a buttered casserole, place a layer of paste, another of the purslane, and the last of the paste, which should be well buttered
4. Place over two fires to set, and after browned, sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
5. Another version is made with a layer of beaten egg, another of the paste, and another of the purslane, with the last layer of egg to bind the sides
6. Once set, sprinkle with ground sugar
