## Ingredients

- Three pounds of flour
- One pound of sugar
- Six egg yolks and three egg whites
- Four small cups of white wine
- Nearly half a pound of melted butter
- One piece of yeast the size of an egg, dissolved in one cup of anise water

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Knead and work the dough until when cut with a knife it makes small holes
3. In this case, extend it and cut the hojuelas
4. Fry them in butter
