## Ingredients

- bagre
- oil
- wide chiles, soaked
- almonds
- oil
- radishes, chopped
- onion, sliced
- olives
- tornachildren, divided

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the bagre and fry it in oil and remove from the pan
2. In the same oil fry wide chiles that have been soaked and ground with almonds
3. When it is fried and not too thick add the portions of bagre
4. Cover with the marinade adding oil and place over two fires
5. When it has reduced, remove carefully and place on the platter covering with the marinade
6. Decorate with chopped radishes, sliced onion, olives and divided tornachildren
