## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- one quarter measure of virgin honey
- fourteen egg yolks, well beaten
- fourteen egg whites
- toasted sesame seeds
- toasted almonds
- pine nuts
- hazelnuts
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Add one quarter measure of virgin honey to four pounds of sugar and clarify together
2. Remove from heat at the soft-crack stage and let cool
3. Once cool, add fourteen well-beaten egg yolks
4. Return to low heat, stirring constantly or beating without stopping
5. As soon as the yolks are well cooked, remove from heat and add the fourteen egg whites
6. Return to heat until it reaches the proper consistency
7. Add toasted sesame seeds, toasted almonds, clean pine nuts, hazelnuts, and cinnamon powder
8. Pour into plates or small boxes
