## Ingredients

- Buñuelos from previous recipe
- White honey or half-point panocha

## Directions

1. After breaking apart the buñuelos from the previous recipe, fry them a little more
2. Drizzle white honey or half-point panocha on top
3. Separate them once the topping is thick
4. These are called humble fritters
