## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground walnut
- 1.5 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Grind the walnuts
2. Make a clarified syrup with the sugar brought to the soft-ball stage as described in previous recipes
3. Mix the ground walnuts with the syrup
4. Place the pan over the fire
5. Bring the candy to the hard-ball stage (punto de cajeta)
6. Remove from heat and beat
7. Pour onto a board to cool
8. For best results with this candy, use piloncillo or unrefined cane sugar instead of refined sugar
