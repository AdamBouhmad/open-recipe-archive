## Ingredients

- White fish
- Capers
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Salt
- Flour
- Lard
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Olives
- Small chiles

## Directions

1. Scale, clean and wash the fish very well
2. Bathe with a thick sauce made with capers, clove, cinnamon, pepper and salt
3. Once well coated, cover with flour and fry in lard
4. Serve with oil, vinegar, olives and small chiles
