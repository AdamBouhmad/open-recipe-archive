## Ingredients

- flour
- eggs
- brandy
- salt
- oil
- orange blossom water
- milk
- water
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of flour with six eggs, one tablespoon of brandy, a good pinch of salt, one tablespoon of oil, and two tablespoons of orange blossom water
2. Mix milk and water in equal quantities and use to make the batter liquid until it has the consistency of a thick porridge or atole
3. Heat a clear fire with fine charcoal and place a pan on it
4. Melt a small piece of lard (or butter or oil if necessary, though lard is better) in the pan
5. Pour one ladle of batter, spreading it so it covers the entire bottom of the pan, making a very thin tortilla
6. When cooked on one side, flip to cook the other side
7. Serve very hot
