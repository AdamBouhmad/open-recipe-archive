## Ingredients

- sesos (brains)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- huevos (eggs)
- harina (flour)
- pan rallado (breadcrumbs)
- perejil (parsley)
- cebollas (onions)
- ajos (garlic)
- manteca (lard)
- canela en polvo (cinnamon powder)

## Directions

1. Clean the brains well and remove the membrane that covers them
2. Cook them in a casserole with water and salt
3. When cooked, remove and cut into slices not too large
4. Bathe them in eggs beaten with flour and a little breadcrumbs, parsley, onions and garlic all chopped very finely
5. Put them to fry with lard until they begin to brown
6. Then sprinkle a little cinnamon powder on top and serve
