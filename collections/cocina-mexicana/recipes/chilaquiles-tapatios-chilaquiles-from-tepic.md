## Ingredients

- green or red chile
- pork meat
- chorizo
- thin tortillas
- toasted sesame seeds
- lard

## Directions

1. Prepare green or red chile
2. Cook pork meat and chorizo and fry them
3. Place a layer of this meat and cover with thin tortillas
4. Bathe with chile that has plenty of lard
5. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds
6. Continue multiplying the layers until the platter is full, making sure to add plenty of lard
