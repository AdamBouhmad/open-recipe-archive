## Ingredients

- three pork loins with fat
- three egg yolks
- one egg white
- spices

## Directions

1. Chop three pork loins with their fat finely
2. Add three egg yolks and one egg white and spices
3. Fill intestines with this mixture
4. These must be eaten immediately as they cannot be kept
