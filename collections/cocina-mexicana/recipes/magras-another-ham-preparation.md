## Ingredients

- ham
- thyme
- bay leaf
- marjoram
- garlic
- vinegar
- tomato
- onion
- parsley
- water

## Directions

1. Cook the ham with thyme, bay leaf, marjoram, mashed garlic and a little vinegar
2. When cooked, slice it
3. Fry plenty of tomato, onion, garlic and parsley well
4. Halfway through frying, add the ham and let it fry well to season
5. Add water from the same water in which the ham cooked
6. Let it thicken
