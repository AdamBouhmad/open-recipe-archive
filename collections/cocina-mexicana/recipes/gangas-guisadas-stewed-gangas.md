## Ingredients

- Gangas, well cleaned
- Whole garlic heads
- Mulato or wide chiles, well cleaned
- Salt
- Water
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Place cleaned gangas to cook with two whole garlic heads, two mulato or wide chiles well cleaned, corresponding salt, some water and some vinegar
2. When cooked, drain the broth and let cool
3. When cool, skim to collect the fat that has rendered
4. Place this fat in a saucepan
