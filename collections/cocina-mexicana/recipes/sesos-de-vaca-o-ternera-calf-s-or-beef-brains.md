## Ingredients

- calf's or beef brains
- warm water
- cold water
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- bay leaf
- thyme
- garlic
- parsley

## Directions

1. Place the brains in warm water to clean them of blood and the membrane surrounding them.
2. Leave them in infusion in cold water for two hours.
3. Then place them in clean water and bring to a boil, adding a little vinegar, salt, pepper, a bay leaf, thyme, garlic, and parsley.
4. Half an hour is enough for them to cook.
