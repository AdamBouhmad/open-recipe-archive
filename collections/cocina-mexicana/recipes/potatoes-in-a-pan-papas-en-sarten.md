## Ingredients

- potatoes
- butter or lard
- spinach, meat filling, or old rags, instead of fried bread crusts (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Peel and cut into thin slices after cooking.
2. Place in a pan with very little butter or lard.
3. Turn until they take on a deep color.
4. Serve without sauce.
5. For garnish, spinach, meat filling, or old rags can be used instead of fried bread crusts.
