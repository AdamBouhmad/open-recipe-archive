## Ingredients

- veal loin from the leg
- orange juice
- water
- ham strips
- vinegar
- bay leaves
- thyme
- garlic
- spices
- lard
- carrot slices
- oil
- greens
- garlic
- onion
- cumin
- carrot wheels

## Directions

1. Shape the veal loins into rounds like cheeses
2. Soak in orange juice and water
3. The next day dry with a cloth
4. Stud with long strips of ham
5. Cook in a pot with equal parts water and vinegar, bay leaves, thyme, crushed garlic, abundant spices, a tablespoon of lard and carrot slices
6. When the meat is cooked, coat with oil and lard
7. Place at two fires to brown lightly
8. Serve in a sauce of green quelites seasoned with garlic, onion, cumin and a small amount of water, as it should remain dry
9. When placing the meat in the sauce, garnish with carrot wheels
