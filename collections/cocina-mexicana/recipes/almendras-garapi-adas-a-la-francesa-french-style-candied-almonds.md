## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- four ounces of water
- one pound of crushed sugar
- lemon zest

## Directions

1. Remove the shells from one pound of almonds and divide them into four parts lengthwise.
2. Place on heat in a proportionate pot with four ounces of water and one pound of crushed sugar.
3. When the almonds begin to crackle and jump, remove from heat.
4. Stir and agitate strongly with a spatula or wooden spoon to granulate them.
5. Add lemon zest from the first outer skin.
6. Cover the heat and place the pot back on, stirring its contents continuously until it takes a caramel color.
7. Place a layer of dragée on the bottom of a plate and extend another layer of almonds on top, continuing this way until finished.
8. Have it removed in the oven or keep it in a warm, dry room.
9. Pistachios, peanuts and hazelnuts are candied in the same way.
