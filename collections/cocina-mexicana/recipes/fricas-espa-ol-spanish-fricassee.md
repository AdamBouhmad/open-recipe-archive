## Ingredients

- chicken
- wine sherries
- chorizo or sausage slices
- raisins
- almonds

## Directions

1. Proceed in everything as in the French fricassee.
2. When it is already done and ready to be removed from the heat, add to it a little sherry wine according to the quantity, slices of chorizo or sausages, raisins and almonds.
