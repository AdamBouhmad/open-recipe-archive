## Ingredients

- salt cod pieces
- onion
- garlic
- pepper
- saffron

## Directions

1. Prepare the salt cod pieces
2. Chop onion and fry it
3. Crush garlic, pepper, and saffron
4. Mix everything together with the onion
5. Sprinkle the mixture over the salt cod at serving time
