## Ingredients

- Ripe bergamot pears
- 1 pound sugar per pound of fruit

## Directions

1. Select ripe pears, peel them and cook until very soft
2. Pass through a sieve pressing them strongly
3. Let dry over the fire
4. When well thickened, dissolve in syrup at high feather stage, which is heated without boiling
