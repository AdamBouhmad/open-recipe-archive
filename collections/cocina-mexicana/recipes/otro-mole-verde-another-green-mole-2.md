## Ingredients

- Tomatillos (green tomatoes)
- Epazote
- Green chile
- Lard
- Water
- Onions
- Peas
- Green beans
- String beans
- Cooked pork ribs
- Beans

## Directions

1. Roast a good proportion of tomatillos and grind them with a good bundle of epazote and green chile
2. Fry in lard with sufficient water and onions in sections
3. Incorporate peas, green beans, and string beans, all raw
4. Add cooked pork ribs and beans, leaving it on low heat
