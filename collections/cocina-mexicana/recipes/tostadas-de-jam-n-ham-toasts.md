## Ingredients

- ham slices
- water
- beef substance
- ham substance
- vinegar
- black pepper
- butter

## Directions

1. Cut equal slices of ham
2. Soak in water for two hours to desalt
3. Fry in the same fat that renders from the ham, as for making pork crackling
4. When they stick, moisten with beef and ham substance
5. Let this sauce boil a little, remove fat and pass through a sieve
6. Add a splash of vinegar and some black pepper
7. Cut toasts the size of the ham slices and fry in butter until golden
8. Layer together with the ham and coat with the sauce on top
