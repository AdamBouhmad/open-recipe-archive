## Ingredients

- pork meat
- veal
- mutton
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- oregano
- thyme
- milk
- bread
- ham
- eggs
- raisins
- almonds
- capers
- Sherry wine
- salt
- garlic
- flour
- bay leaves
- parsley
- onion
- lard

## Directions

1. Mix pork, veal and mutton meat and remove the skins
2. Grind clove, cinnamon, pepper, oregano and thyme
3. Grind on the metate with milk and a piece of bread soaked beforehand in milk and mix with the meat together with the spices, minced ham, raw egg, raisins, almonds, capers, two raw egg yolks and Sherry wine
4. Put all this in a napkin in the form of a ball and in the middle place a hard-boiled egg studded with strips of ham, tie it in the napkin and put it to cook in salted water and the skins that were removed from the meat
5. Cover and from time to time poke it with a fork to know when the meat is cooked
6. Then untie the napkin so the meat cools a little
7. Brown half a head of garlic that is partly crushed and when well browned add two tablespoons of flour to the lard; when it is browned strain it in the water in which the large meatball was cooked, and add more spices, which are taken from the same ones added to the meat; add half a cup of Sherry wine, two or three bay leaves, parsley leaves, plenty of sliced onion and the large meatball so it finishes cooking
8. Do not move the large meatball more than with a fork to turn it and let the broth thicken a little
