## Ingredients

- Cooked brains
- Liver pieces
- Raw ham pieces
- Eggs
- Lemon rind
- Cloves
- Salt
- Pepper
- Breadcrumbs or ground bread
- Lard

## Directions

1. Use silver skewers, or thick straws if silver ones are not available
2. String pieces of cooked brains, small pieces of liver and raw ham onto the skewers or straws so that they are perfectly covered
3. Beat an egg with its shell, yolk, grated lemon rind, cloves, salt and pepper mixed together
4. Bathe the skewers with this mixture
5. Cover them with breadcrumbs or ground bread
6. Fry them in lard
7. When the fritters are well browned, place them on a platter and serve
