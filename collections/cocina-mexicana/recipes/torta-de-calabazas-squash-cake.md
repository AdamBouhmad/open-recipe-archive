## Ingredients

- Squash
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Cinnamon
- Sesame seeds
- Candied citron
- Eggs
- Lard

## Directions

1. Chop the squash and cook it
2. When cold, squeeze it well
3. Add raisins, almonds, cinnamon, sesame seeds, and candied citron
4. Beat the necessary eggs as if for frying
5. Mix everything together, coating the skillet with lard and place over two fires
