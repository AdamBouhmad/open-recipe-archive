## Ingredients

- Lard
- Tomatoes, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Onion, chopped
- Water
- Fine salt
- Large dried chiles, whole
- Aged or fresh cheese
- Eggs
- Hard-cooked egg yolks
- Epazote leaves
- Fresh cheese for garnish

## Directions

1. Fry chopped tomato, garlic, and onion in lard until the tomato is well fried
2. Add water and the appropriate amount of fine salt
3. Once it boils a little, add whole large dried chiles and pieces or slices of aged or fresh cheese
4. While boiling strongly, break eggs into the broth, adding hard-cooked egg yolks and epazote leaves
5. Let it boil a little more and remove from heat
6. Garnish with slices of fresh cheese
