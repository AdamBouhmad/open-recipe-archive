## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- garlic
- tomato
- parsley
- artichokes
- peas
- chile huauchinango
- pepper
- clove
- saffron
- rice
- broth

## Directions

1. Clean and cut three or four chickens into pieces.
2. Fry them well in a pan or earthen pot with butter.
3. Add one or two cloves of garlic, tomato, parsley, artichokes and peas.
4. Sauté everything well.
5. Add a little chile huauchinango, pepper, clove and a little saffron.
6. Add the rice and after two turns cover well with broth.
7. Cook over high heat following the previous method.
8. Can be made with hens, partridges, etc., but when using tough birds or meats, do not add the rice until they are cooked.
