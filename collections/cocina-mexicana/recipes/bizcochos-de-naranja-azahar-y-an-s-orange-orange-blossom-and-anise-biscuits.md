## Ingredients

- fifteen egg whites
- half a pound of well-ground sugar
- fifteen egg yolks
- three large cups of leavening
- juice of half an orange
- half a pound of very hot lard
- a little

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites with the half pound of well-ground sugar
2. When well risen, add the same number of egg yolks which are also beaten until everything is very thick
3. Add three large cups of leavening, the juice of half an orange, half a pound of very hot lard, and a little
