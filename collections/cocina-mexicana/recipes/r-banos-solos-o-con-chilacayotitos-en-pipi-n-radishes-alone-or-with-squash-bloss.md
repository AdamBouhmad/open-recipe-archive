## Ingredients

- radishes
- chilacayotitos (young squash)
- salt
- water
- pipián sauce

## Directions

1. Cut the radishes into rounds or in the shape of dice and place them to cook in water with salt
2. Cook the chilacayotitos separately if they are somewhat tough, since if they are very tender, which is the most flavorful, they cook in the same pipián into which they are added raw and chopped
3. Once the radishes are cooked, add them to the pipián, which is prepared and seasoned in the common manner
4. The radishes alone or the chilacayotitos can be prepared in this way without accompanying one another
