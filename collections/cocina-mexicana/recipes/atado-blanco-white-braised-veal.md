## Ingredients

- Raw meat
- Water
- Vinegar
- Ham
- Ground thyme
- Cumin
- Clove
- Garlic heads
- Lard

## Directions

1. Place the raw meat in a pot with plenty of water, vinegar, ham, ground thyme, cumin, clove, and garlic heads.
2. Place over heat, add lard, and let it boil until the water is consumed, placing it between two fires.
