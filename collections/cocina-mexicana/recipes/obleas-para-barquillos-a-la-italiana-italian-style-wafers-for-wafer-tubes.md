## Ingredients

- eight eggs
- fourteen ounces of powdered sugar
- one pound of flour
- six ounces of cream
- six ounces of milk
- one ounce of orange blossom water
- zest of one lemon

## Directions

1. Beat well eight eggs with fourteen ounces of powdered sugar and one pound of flour
2. Add six ounces of cream, the same amount of milk, one ounce of orange blossom water and the zest of one lemon
3. Mix everything well until it forms no lumps whatsoever
4. Heat the wafer iron and make the wafers as common ones
