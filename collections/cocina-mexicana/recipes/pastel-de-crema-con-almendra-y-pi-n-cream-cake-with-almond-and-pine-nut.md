## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- two egg yolks and three egg whites
- a little yeast dissolved in settled tequesquite water
- one small cup of butter
- another small cup of lard, both melted
- half a small cup of Malaga wine
- juice of two sour limes
- powdered sugar in amount sufficient to stand out
- water as needed
- half a pound of almonds, soaked, peeled and ground
- half a pound of pine nuts
- one large cup of milk cream
- six egg yolks
- one pound of powdered sugar
- one quarter of an ounce of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make the dough with one pound of flour, two egg yolks and three egg whites, a little yeast dissolved in settled tequesquite water, one small cup of butter and another of lard, both melted, half a small cup of Malaga wine, the juice of two sour limes, powdered sugar in amount sufficient to stand out, and the water needed
2. Proceed as in the previous article
3. For the filling, mix half a pound of almonds, soaked, peeled and ground with another half pound of pine nuts, one large cup of milk cream, six egg yolks, one pound of powdered sugar and one quarter of an ounce of cinnamon, all well incorporated
4. Fill the cake with this mixture
5. Finish the same as in the previous article
