## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs, chopped
- cream or milk
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Proceed exactly as for the Dutch-style potatoes, with the difference that instead of moistening the paste with beef broth, do so with cream or milk.
2. Add a little powdered sugar instead of pepper.
