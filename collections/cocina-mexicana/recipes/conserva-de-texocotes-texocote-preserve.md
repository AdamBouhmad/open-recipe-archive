## Ingredients

- Texocotes (Mexican hawthorn fruits)
- Water
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Cook the texocotes in water until softened and remove to hot water as they are pitted
2. Return them to hot water to remove the skins, then place in a little water so they finish cooking and remain soft
3. Wash them well in warm water three or four times
4. Let them boil well in clarified syrup and let rest until the next day
5. Return to the fire so the preserve reaches the proper point, which should be high
