## Ingredients

- beef, chopped into walnut-sized pieces
- bacon
- onion
- pepper
- water
- salt
- clove
- cinnamon
- parsley
- saffron
- egg yolks
- vinegar
- broth

## Directions

1. Chop the meat into walnut-sized pieces
2. Fry bacon, then add onion and everything to brown in the pan with pepper
3. Place in a pot over low heat
4. Add water for cooking and turn occasionally
5. Season with salt, clove, cinnamon, a handful of parsley and a little saffron
6. If making a quick sauce, dissolve egg yolks in a small amount of vinegar
7. Beat with warm broth so they do not curdle
8. Add to the stew, ensuring it does not boil
9. Serve
