## Ingredients

- ham
- hot water
- milk
- sugar
- fresh rosemary

## Directions

1. Wash the ham well in hot water
2. Cook it in sufficient milk with six pounds of sugar and a small bundle with fresh rosemary
3. When it has cooked and the white skin has formed on it, remove it
4. With a comal with fire form a crust, or with powder pastilles or dragées, or sugar and cinnamon
