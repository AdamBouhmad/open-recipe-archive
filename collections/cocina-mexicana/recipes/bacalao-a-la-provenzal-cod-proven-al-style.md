## Ingredients

- Cod
- Butter
- Onion
- Parsley
- Garlic
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Olive oil
- Bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Desalt the cod and then cook it, cut into slices and put in a dish buttered, add finely chopped onion, parsley and garlic, adding pepper and nutmeg, and on top of this put more cod, which is bathed in oil, and over this another layer equal to the first that was put in the dish, on the cod which should remain on top, put a layer of bread crumbs soaked in oil and put to fry at two gentle fires until it takes color
