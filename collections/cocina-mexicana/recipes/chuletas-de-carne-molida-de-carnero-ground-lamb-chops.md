## Ingredients

- Ground lamb
- spices
- water
- salt
- egg
- flour
- white wine
- vinegar
- sugar
- pepper

## Directions

1. Grind the meat with all kinds of spices and make some small elongated patties
2. Put them to cook in a pan with water and salt, letting them partially cook until the water is consumed
3. Fry them afterward with beaten egg
4. Place them lastly in a light broth made with fried flour, white wine, vinegar, a little sugar and a little pepper
