## Ingredients

- pork foot
- pork loin
- tongue
- ham
- chorizos
- beets
- cauliflower
- chickpeas
- tomatoes
- fried garlic
- saffron
- pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon
- breadcrumbs
- eggs
- chiles
- olives
- almonds
- pine nuts
- capers

## Directions

1. After cooking the pork foot, loin, and tongue, chop them into small pieces
2. Mix with ground breadcrumbs
3. Beat the eggs and mix everything together, adding chiles, olives, almonds, pine nuts, capers, and all the ingredients
4. Place over two fires
5. Once set, serve with sauce
