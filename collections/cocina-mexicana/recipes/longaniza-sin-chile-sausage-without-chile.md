## Ingredients

- pork loin
- pepper
- clove
- ginger
- cumin
- vinegar
- water
- salt
- pork intestines, thin and clean

## Directions

1. Cut the pork loin meat as in previous recipes
2. Grind pepper, clove, ginger and plenty of cumin
3. Dissolve these spices in diluted vinegar, add sufficient salt and place the meat there to infuse for twenty-four hours
4. After infusing, fill the thin, well-cleaned intestines and hang the sausage to dry
