## Ingredients

- Milk
- Cinnamon stick
- Egg yolks
- Sugar
- Ground almond
- Butter
- Mamón
- Egg
- Powdered cinnamon
- Raisins
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Cook the milk very well with a cinnamon stick until it thickens
2. Let cool and add egg yolks, sugar, and ground almond
3. Coat a skillet with butter and place a layer of mamón and another of custard until full
4. Add beaten egg on top and over it sugar and powdered cinnamon with raisins and almonds
