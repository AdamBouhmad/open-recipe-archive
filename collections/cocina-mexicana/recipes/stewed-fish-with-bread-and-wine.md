## Ingredients

- Fish
- Seasoning vegetables
- Parsley
- Lard
- Toasted bread
- Butter
- Ground pepper
- Ground clove
- Bay leaves
- Wine
- Oil

## Directions

1. Chop all the seasoning ingredients and plenty of parsley very finely
2. After frying well, add toasted bread fried in butter, ground pepper and clove, bay leaves, plenty of wine and a little oil
3. This stew is very good for catfish
