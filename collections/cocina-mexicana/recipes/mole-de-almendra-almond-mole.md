## Ingredients

- Chile
- Almonds, ground
- Cooked meat
- Chorizo
- Capers
- Olives
- Chile strips
- Almonds

## Directions

1. Grind the chile and fry it with all the seasonings
2. Add the ground almonds
3. Once fried, add the cooked meat, chorizo, capers, olives, chile strips, and almonds
