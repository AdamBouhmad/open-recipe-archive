## Ingredients

- A piece of loin worth three ribs with two removed leaving one large rib
- Thick strips of ham well seasoned for larding
- A beef shank
- A beef foot
- Onions
- Parsnips
- A head of garlic
- Few aromatics

## Directions

1. Take a piece of loin worth three ribs, remove two ribs leaving only one large rib
2. Lard the large rib with thick strips of well-seasoned ham
3. Place it to braise with a beef shank and beef foot, onions, parsnips, a head of garlic, and few aromatics
4. After five or six hours, or when well cooked, drain on a plate to cool well
5. Prepare lightly and reheat immediately in its own cooking liquid
6. Strain and reduce the liquid to proper consistency
7. Turn occasionally so it takes on equal color on both sides and acquires the taste of the sauce
8. Arrange on a plate surrounded by glazed onions or parsnips cut like little sticks
