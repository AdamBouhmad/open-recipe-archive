## Ingredients

- lean ham
- seven whole eggs
- seven egg yolks
- sugar syrup
- little cinnamon
- lard
- puff pastry flour

## Directions

1. Take more lean than fat, enough for a quarter-size casserole, and once cooked grind seven whole eggs and seven yolks together
2. Add sugar syrup, a little cinnamon with lard and the puff pastry made from flour to make the base
