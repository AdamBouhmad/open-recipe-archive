## Ingredients

- Flour
- Milk
- Sugar
- Cinnamon powder
- Egg yolks
- Fried bread crusts or toasted bread slices or crushed hardtack

## Directions

1. Toast the flour in a pan dry and without butter
2. While still hot, dissolve it in sufficient milk for a soup
3. Add sugar and cinnamon powder
4. Put the mixture to cook without ceasing to stir
5. At the moment of serving, thicken it with some egg yolks
6. Pour everything over fried bread crusts, toasted bread slices grilled, or crushed hardtack
