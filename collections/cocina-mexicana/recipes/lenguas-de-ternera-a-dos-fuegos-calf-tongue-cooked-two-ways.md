## Ingredients

- One calf tongue
- Pieces of lean ham
- Slices of fat ham
- Onions
- Carrots
- Mushrooms
- Parsley
- Laurel
- Pepper
- Salt
- One glass of broth
- One glass of white wine

## Directions

1. Remove the skin from the tongue and insert pieces of lean ham throughout.
2. Place in a pot lined with slices of fat ham.
3. Add onions, carrots, and mushrooms, all cut into small pieces, parsley, and laurel, pepper, and salt.
4. Moisten with one glass of broth and another of white wine.
5. Cover with more slices of fat ham, place the pot on gentle fire with hot coals on the lid or in a comal.
6. Cook until tender.
