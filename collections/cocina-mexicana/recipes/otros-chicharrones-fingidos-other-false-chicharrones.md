## Ingredients

- one cup milk
- one cup flour
- eggs, as needed for frying
- sweetener to taste
- lard
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Mix one cup milk and one cup flour well together
2. Beat eggs suitable for frying and combine with the flour mixture, blending well with sweetener to taste
3. Grease a baking dish with lard, pour in the mixture and bake at two fires
4. When cooked, remove and let cool
5. Cut crosswise and divide into quarters, making cuts like in true chicharrones
6. Place in point sugar syrup and let boil briefly
