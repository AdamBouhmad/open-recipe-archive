## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- one coconut, well ground
- four quartillos of milk
- half pound of sugar
- four ounces of peeled and ground almond
- eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Clarify a syrup made with one pound of sugar.
2. Add one coconut well ground to the syrup and place over heat, letting it boil until it forms a consistent paste like that used for filling gasnates.
3. Sweeten four quartillos of milk with half a pound of sugar, place over heat, and when it comes to a boil, add four ounces of peeled and ground almond like atole.
4. Mix the milk with the paste and let boil a little, then remove from heat.
5. When cooled, add eight beaten egg yolks as for huevos reales.
6. Return to heat so the egg cooks, and pour onto a platter, browning as described in the preceding articles.
