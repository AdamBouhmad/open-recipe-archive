## Ingredients

- Cow feet (as many as desired for the amount of aspic to be made)
- One good rooster, plucked, singed over flame, and cleaned
- Sufficient water
- Sugar in proportion
- Cinnamon sticks
- Two or three cloves
- Two or three lemon peels, cleaned of white pith
- Four or five egg whites
- Juice of the lemons whose peels were used

## Directions

1. Prepare the cow feet by washing and cleaning them thoroughly
2. Pluck, singe over flame, and clean the rooster
3. Place both the cow feet and rooster in a pot with sufficient water
4. Bring to a boil and skim carefully and thoroughly
5. When the meats are completely broken down, the aspic is at the right point, without needing to be overly strong
6. Strain through fine cloth or linen and remove the fat thoroughly
7. Add sugar in proportion, cinnamon sticks, two or three cloves, and two or three cleaned lemon peels
8. Allow the aspic to cook for some time with these ingredients
9. In the meantime, beat four or five egg whites until they harden
10. Mix the juice of the lemons into the beaten egg whites
11. Add this mixture to the aspic, stirring it occasionally over the heat
12. Allow it to come to a boil until it rises, approaching the point of overflowing from the edges
13. Filter through fine cloth or felt until it is very clear
14. Place in a cold location to set
15. Optional: color the aspic using cochineal or kermes for red, beet juice for crimson, tournesol dye for violet, egg yolks for yellow, cooked chard or spinach for green, or almond milk for white
