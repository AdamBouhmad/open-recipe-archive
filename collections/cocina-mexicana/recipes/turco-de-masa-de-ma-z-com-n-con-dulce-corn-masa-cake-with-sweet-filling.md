## Ingredients

- two medium plates of corn flour
- twelve raw egg yolks
- a little anise water
- sugar to taste
- necessary lard

## Directions

1. Rub and remove the nixtamal, put it to dry in the sun, then grind it and sift it through a sifter
2. Mix two medium plates of this flour with twelve raw egg yolks, a little anise water, sugar to taste and the necessary lard, so that the dough is in good condition
3. Beat this dough so that it can be ground, and little tortillas are made from it on the metate
4. Form the turco the same as the previous ones
5. Cook at two fires
6. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top
