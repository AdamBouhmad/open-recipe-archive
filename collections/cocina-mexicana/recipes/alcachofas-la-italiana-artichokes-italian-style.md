## Ingredients

- artichokes
- lemon juice
- water
- salt
- Italian sauce

## Directions

1. Divide into four equal parts
2. Remove the heart and rub with lemon juice
3. Cook with water, salt, and a little lemon juice
4. Drain and serve with Italian sauce
