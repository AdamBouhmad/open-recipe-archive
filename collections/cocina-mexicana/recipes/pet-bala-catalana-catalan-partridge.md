## Ingredients

- Partridge
- Butter
- Broth
- Salt
- Pepper
- Herbs
- Garlic
- Sour orange
- Lemon slices

## Directions

1. Clean the partridge and brown it in a pan with butter
2. Remove the partridge and add broth, salt, pepper and herbs to the same butter
3. Return the partridge and let it cook
4. Separately fry two portions of garlic
5. In another small pan, cut a sour orange into slices
6. A quarter hour before serving, mix everything together to finish cooking
