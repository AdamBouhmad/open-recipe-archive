## Ingredients

- one pound of bitter almonds
- four egg whites
- three pounds of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Place one pound of bitter almonds in clean cloth, rubbing to remove dust
2. Grind in a marble mortar with four egg whites, being careful not to grind too much so they do not become oil
3. Once ground, place in a bowl and incorporate three pounds of powdered sugar
4. If the paste is too dry, add egg whites as needed, ensuring it is neither too liquid nor too thick
5. Arrange on paper sheets in small portions the size of a walnut
6. Bake in the oven
