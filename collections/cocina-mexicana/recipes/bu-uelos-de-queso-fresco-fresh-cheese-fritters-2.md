## Ingredients

- four fresh cheeses
- egg yolks
- hot lard
- honey

## Directions

1. Take four fresh cheeses, remove the rind and knead like bread
2. Put it in a clean bowl and add egg yolks, as many as are needed
3. So that it becomes white, add hot lard; if hard, add more yolks
4. Then put them in the honey
