## Ingredients

- One pound mutton pulp
- Half a pound pork meat
- Half a pound fat ham
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Peeled and chopped almonds
- Raisins
- Olives
- Capers
- Pine nuts
- Raw eggs
- Flour
- Butter
- Tomatoes
- Dried walnuts
- Almonds
- Sherry wine
- Chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Chop the mutton pulp together with pork meat and fat ham until it becomes fatty.
2. Mix in ground clove, pepper and cinnamon, peeled and chopped almonds, raisins, olives, capers and pine nuts.
3. Knead everything with two raw eggs.
4. Form long meatballs, coat with flour and fry in butter.
5. For the broth: roast some tomatoes and grind them with dried walnuts and almonds.
6. After frying this, add unfried pine nuts, clove, and cinnamon, then fry it all together.
7. Add enough water for the meatballs to cook.
8. Cook the meatballs in this broth.
9. When cooked and removing from heat, add a little Sherry wine and cover the casserole so the meat does not evaporate.
10. Garnish with chiles in vinegar and olives.
