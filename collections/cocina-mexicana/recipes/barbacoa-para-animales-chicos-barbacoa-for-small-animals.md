## Ingredients

- Small animal
- Dry firewood
- Small pebbles
- Green leaves
- Coconut shells

## Directions

1. Kill the animal by pressing strongly with the hand on the snout and nose, an operation that lasts a little more than a quarter hour
2. During this time, open a hole in earth about one foot deep in which light a fire
3. To heat it put layers alternately of firewood and small pebbles
4. Hold the animal over the flames for some time and scraping with a knife remove all the hair or bristles, the same as if it had been plucked in boiling water
5. Open it and remove the entrails, which will be washed immediately with care and placed in coconut shells, as well as the blood which will be collected at the time of opening the animal
6. When the hole is well heated remove the fire and place at the bottom some of the pebbles that were not hot enough
7. Cover them with green leaves on which place the animal with its entrails
8. Extend over it another layer of green leaves and hot stones, covering the hole with stones
9. Open it after about four hours and remove the animal very well roasted, which is an excellent meal
