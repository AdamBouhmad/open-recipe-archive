## Ingredients

- Artichokes
- Cold water
- Egg batter
- Lard

## Directions

1. Clean the artichokes, divide them into pieces and soak them for a little time in cold water.
2. Remove them and drain well.
3. Coat them in egg batter, or dip them in some frying paste, and fry them in sufficient lard until they take on color.
