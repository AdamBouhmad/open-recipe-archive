## Ingredients

- Twelve egg whites
- Sixteen egg yolks
- One pound of flour in its finest form

## Directions

1. Beat twelve egg whites and sixteen egg yolks until the batter is white and thick
2. Then add one pound of fine flour little by little, so it does not become lumpy or muddled
3. Make the mamones long like biscotti, or round
4. To give them a crust, beat eighteen eggs and add them on top
