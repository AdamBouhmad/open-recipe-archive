## Ingredients

- lamb kidneys
- small spit
- lard
- fine herbs
- anchovy butter
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. After cleaning and splitting them well without separating them completely, place them on a small spit so they stay open
2. Pour a little lard in the bottom of a casserole and place the kidneys inside
3. Set everything over high heat
4. When they are done, place inside them a small ball of melted lard mixed with fine herbs or anchovy butter and a little lemon juice
