## Ingredients

- Two plump, good-sized chickens
- Their livers
- Grated ham
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Onions
- Butter
- Lemon or sour orange slices
- Ham
- Paper
- Vegetables such as spinach, mushrooms, peas, cardoons, small onions, roots, cucumbers, or olives

## Directions

1. Select two plump and good-sized chickens, pluck, clean and empty them.
2. Fill them with their livers, chopped and mixed with grated ham, salt, pepper, chopped parsley and onions.
3. Cross their legs and blanch them in butter.
4. Place lemon or sour orange slices on the breast so it remains white, and wrap them in ham and paper.
5. Put them to cook in the roasting spit.
6. Once cooked, serve them with some vegetable stew, such as spinach, mushrooms, peas, cardoons, small onions, roots, cucumbers or olives, which absolutely depends on the taste of the house master or the guests.
