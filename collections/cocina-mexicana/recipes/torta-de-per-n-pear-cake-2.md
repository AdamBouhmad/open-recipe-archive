## Ingredients

- Pears
- Mamón (a type of fruit), half-toasted and crushed
- Sugar, sifted
- Six eggs per pound of paste

## Directions

1. Cook the pears and remove their cores and skins
2. Grind them and let them drain until no water remains
3. Mix with an equal volume of half-toasted and crushed mamón
4. Add salt and pepper to taste
5. Mix in the necessary sifted sugar to make the paste flavorful
6. Beat the egg whites and yolks separately until stiff
7. Incorporate them into the paste
8. Pour the mixture into a pan greased with lard or butter
9. Let it set over two fires
10. Proceed as in previous articles
