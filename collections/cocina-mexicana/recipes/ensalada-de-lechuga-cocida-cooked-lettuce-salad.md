## Ingredients

- Lettuce
- Salt
- Cold water
- Vinegar
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the leaves from the lettuces and wash them very well
2. Gather and tie them, forming bundles of regular thickness
3. Cook them with white salt
4. Once well cooked, remove and place in cold water to refresh, washing them without breaking them apart
5. After refreshing, drain well and press them so all the water comes out without crushing them
6. Untie them and place the bundles of lettuce in a platter and moisten with good vinegar, sweetened with a little sugar and tempered with water
