## Ingredients

- venison
- pepper
- garlic
- orange or lemon juice
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. Cut the meat into pieces and place in a casserole with pepper, garlic, salt, butter and necessary water.
2. When the meat is almost tender, uncover the casserole so it dries and only a regular sauce remains.
3. When removed from heat, squeeze a lemon or bitter orange over it.
