## Ingredients

- four quartillos of milk
- twelve egg yolks
- cinnamon sticks
- orange leaves
- sponge biscuits (soletas)
- beaten custard (natillas)
- sifted sugar
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Sweeten four quartillos of milk and mix in twelve egg yolks
2. Dissolve the yolks well, then strain the milk and place it over heat
3. Add some cinnamon sticks and orange leaves
4. Allow to boil until it reaches the point of setting, stirring occasionally
5. In the serving dishes, make layers: one of the cooked milk, another of sponge biscuits, another of beaten custard with sifted sugar and cinnamon powder
6. Continue in this order until the dishes are filled, ensuring the final layer is milk
7. After cooling, dust with cinnamon powder
