## Ingredients

- whole eggs
- flour
- milk
- sugar
- orange flower water
- vanilla
- lemon zest or other flavoring of choice
- butter

## Directions

1. Dissolve whole eggs in flour to make a thick paste
2. Thin it gradually with milk, sugar, orange flower water, vanilla, lemon zest or other flavoring of choice until it reaches the consistency of a thick batter
3. Butter a baking pan and pour in this batter
4. Cook quickly over a lively fire and under a bell oven
5. The paste will rise like a soufflé
