## Ingredients

- Hares
- water
- salt
- oil
- onion
- cooked capers
- pepper
- vinegar
- flour
- lard
- hare broth
- olives
- pickled chile peppers

## Directions

1. Cook the hares in salted water and divide into portions
2. Fry in oil and set aside
3. In the same oil, also fry thin slices of onion, then add cooked and ground capers, which after being fried are seasoned with salt and pepper, adding vinegar, flour browned in lard and broth from the pot
4. Place the hares in this sauce and let boil until it is almost completely consumed, leaving the hare portions nearly dry
5. Arrange on a platter and cover with what remains in the pot from the sauce, thinning it with a little oil
6. Decorate the plate with olives and pieces of pickled chiles
