## Ingredients

- garlic cloves
- butter
- ham slices
- pork jowl cooked and cut into dice
- salt
- ground pepper
- thyme
- marjoram
- broth
- flour browned in lard
- sliced turnips

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves cut into pieces in butter, and once they are golden remove them
2. In the same butter fry some slices of ham and cooked pork jowl already cut in the form of dice
3. Add salt, ground pepper, thyme, marjoram, broth and flour browned in lard
4. Once this sauce is boiling, add the sliced turnips for them to cook
5. When they are cooked and the sauce has thickened, serve
