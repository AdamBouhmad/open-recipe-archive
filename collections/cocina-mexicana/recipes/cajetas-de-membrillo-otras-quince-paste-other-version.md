## Ingredients

- membrillos (quinces)
- azúcar machacada (crushed sugar)

## Directions

1. Cook the quinces without skin or core, grind them, sift through a hair sieve and add crushed sugar in the proportion of three pounds for each two pounds of quince
2. Place this over heat until it reaches the point of detaching from the pan
3. Then remove from heat, beat without coating it, and place warm in the small boxes
