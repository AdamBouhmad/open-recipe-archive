## Ingredients

- cooked salt cod
- oil
- garlic
- green or hot chile
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Cook the salt cod and drain it well
2. Place it on plates
3. Fry oil with garlic
4. Add green or hot chile
5. Add the same amount of vinegar as oil
6. Pour this sauce over the salt cod at serving time
