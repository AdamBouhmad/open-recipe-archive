## Ingredients

- woodcocks
- butter, about half an egg's worth
- juice of three lemons
- one tablespoon of common measure of chopped shallots
- about three-quarters of a quarter-liter of white wine
- pepper
- salt
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Quarter the woodcocks and place them on a plate set over a small heater or brazier with spirits
2. Add a piece of butter about half an egg in size, the juice of three lemons, one tablespoon of chopped shallots, about three-quarters of a quarter-liter of white wine, pepper and salt
3. Sprinkle everything with breadcrumbs and let cook for ten minutes, turning it carefully
4. After this time, remove the plate from the heater and distribute the almorejo among the guests
