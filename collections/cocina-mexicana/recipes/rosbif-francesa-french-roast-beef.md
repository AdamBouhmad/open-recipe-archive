## Ingredients

- beef loin
- superior oil
- salt
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- bay leaf
- chopped small onions

## Directions

1. Soak the loin in a marinade for twelve hours before putting it on the roaster.
2. The marinade is composed of superior oil, well seasoned with salt, pepper, chopped parsley, a bay leaf and chopped small onions.
3. Turn the loin from time to time in the marinade so that all its faces are soaked.
4. Roast for two or three hours, according to volume.
5. Do not remove from the heat until cooking is advanced enough that the center remains somewhat reddish, but not bloody.
6. Serve with the same sauce used in English roast beef.
