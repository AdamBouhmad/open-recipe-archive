## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- fresh butter (half pound)
- six egg yolks
- four ounces of soaked and ground almonds
- sponge biscuits (soletas)
- butter
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the point of conserve with one pound of sugar
2. Remove from heat and add half pound of fresh butter and six beaten egg yolks
3. Return to fire and gradually mix in four ounces of soaked and ground almonds
4. When it reaches the point of cajeta (when stirring shows the bottom of the pan), remove from heat and place a little in a deep plate lined with butter
5. Place a layer of sponge biscuits on top and another of the paste until the plate is full, with the last layer being paste brushed with butter
6. Place over two gentle fires to set
7. After cooling, dust with cinnamon
