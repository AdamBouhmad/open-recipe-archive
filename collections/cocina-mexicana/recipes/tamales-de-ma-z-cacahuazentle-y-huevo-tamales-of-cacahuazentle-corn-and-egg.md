## Ingredients

- Three pounds of cooked and ground cacahuazentle corn
- Half pound of melted lard
- Eight egg yolks
- Sugar to taste
- Warm water
- Canela
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Pine nuts
- Walnut pieces
- Sesame seeds
- Corn leaves

## Directions

1. Mix for the dough three pounds of cooked, ground and sifted cacahuazentle corn, half pound of melted lard, eight egg yolks, necessary sugar according to desired sweetness, and a little warm water
2. The dough should be neither watery nor thick
3. Beat until it makes foam
4. Place inside the tamales considerable canela, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, walnut pieces and sesame seeds
5. Form and cook the same as the others
