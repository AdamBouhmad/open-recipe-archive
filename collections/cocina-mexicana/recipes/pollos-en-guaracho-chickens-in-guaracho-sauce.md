## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic cloves
- chicken broth
- toasted sesame seeds
- pieces of raw Castilian squash
- cleaned raisins
- finely chopped parsley
- cloves
- cinnamon
- ground pepper
- salt
- cooked chicken quarters
- sugar
- white wine
- cleaned and chopped almonds

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves in lard
2. Add the broth in which the chickens were cooked, toasted sesame seeds, pieces of raw Castilian squash, cleaned raisins, finely chopped parsley, cloves, cinnamon and ground pepper, and sufficient salt
3. Add the cooked chicken quarters
4. Cook over two fires
5. When the broth is well seasoned, add a piece of sugar, a glass of white wine, cleaned and chopped raisins and almonds
