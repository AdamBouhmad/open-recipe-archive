## Ingredients

- calabacitas (squash)
- water
- salt
- lettuce
- cauliflower
- carrot
- beet
- oil
- vinegar
- nogada sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the calabacitas in salted water.
2. Remove a slice from the bottom so they can sit well after being filled.
3. From the top where the flower is, remove another slice to be able to remove the heart, insides and seeds.
4. Fill with lettuce, cauliflower, carrot, and beet, all cooked, chopped, and seasoned as for salad with salt, oil, and vinegar.
5. Cover with the same slice that was removed from the top.
6. Place on a plate and cover with nogada sauce.
