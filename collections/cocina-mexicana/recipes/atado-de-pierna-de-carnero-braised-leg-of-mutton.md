## Ingredients

- Mutton pulp
- Ham strips
- Water or vinegar
- Lard
- Tomato
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Garlic head
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Lard the mutton pulp with strips of ham and leave it from the day before in an infusion of water or vinegar.
2. The next day, put lard in a pot and parboil the meat.
3. Add water, a chopped tomato, clove, cinnamon, and pepper.
4. Crush a garlic head and add it to the pot in which the meat is to boil.
5. Let it cook down until only a sauce remains in which the meat will fry.
6. Garnish with cold whole potatoes or slices, which should be allowed to cook in the same broth.
