## Ingredients

- small lettuces
- salt
- ham slices
- carrot
- onions
- bundle of fine herbs
- beaten egg or frying batter
- flour
- water from cooking
- cloves
- pepper
- nutmeg
- whole small green peppers

## Directions

1. Choose small lettuces and after cleaning, tie them with thread.
2. Put them to cook with salt, slices of ham, carrot, and sliced onions and a bundle of fine herbs.
3. When they have cooked, remove them and press well.
4. Coat them with beaten egg or other frying batter, or with just flour.
5. Put them back in a little of the water in which they cooked, adding the ham, carrots, onions and other substances that are in the broth.
6. Season with cloves, pepper and nutmeg, and when the broth is thick and has whole small green peppers, pour it over the lettuces and serve.
