## Ingredients

- Calf's tongue
- Salt
- Ham, cut into pieces
- Egg, beaten
- Butter
- Tomatoes, chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Onions, chopped
- Parsley, chopped
- Water
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Black pepper
- Saffron
- Cumin
- Bread crumb
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. Pound the tongue with a stick and cook it with salt
2. When half-cooked, remove and skin it
3. Return it to cook with a piece of ham until done
4. Remove and slice both the tongue and ham
5. Beat egg and dip the slices in it, then fry in butter
6. In another pot with butter, fry the tomatoes, garlic, onions, and parsley, all chopped
7. Add a little water and grind clove, cinnamon, pepper, saffron, cumin, and a bread crumb soaked in vinegar
8. Season the broth with salt and add the fried tongue and ham slices
9. When the broth has thickened sufficiently, remove from heat and serve
