## Ingredients

- hare with skin
- oil
- water
- salt
- bread
- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- dried chiles, wide

## Directions

1. Cook the hare with its skin, and after two or three boils, remove the skin.
2. Fry in oil until it takes color.
3. Add boiling water and salt and cook until done.
4. Divide into regular portions.
5. In a pot with oil, fry a good slice of bread until golden and remove.
6. Fry plenty of sliced onion and garlic in the same oil.
7. When the onion is slightly browned, add to the fried bread, ground with much parsley and four to six wide soaked and deveined chiles.
8. Add sufficient salt and the hare in pieces.
9. Let this broth boil until well thickened.
10. Serve in plates with raw oil on top.
