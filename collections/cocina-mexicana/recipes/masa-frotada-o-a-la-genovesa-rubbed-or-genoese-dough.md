## Ingredients

- one quarter-pint of flour
- a little salt
- eight egg yolks and four egg whites
- twelve ounces of sugar
- zest of two or three lemons
- half a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Place flour on the table with a hole in the middle
2. Add salt, eggs, and pieces of sugar to the hole
3. Rub and scrape the zest of two or three lemons
4. Moisten all the sugar with a rolling pin and reduce to powder, mixing with the dough
5. Add half a pound of butter
6. Knead everything well together and rub with the palm of the hand two or three times
7. Knead again, gather together and let rest
8. Cut into a strip shape, roll until round and the thickness of the little finger
9. Divide into pieces of equal size
10. Form S shapes or other similar figures, crimping at one end and dividing at the other
11. Place on buttered platforms or paddles
12. Brown and bake in an oven that should be hotter than for sponge cake
13. Serve as small side dishes
