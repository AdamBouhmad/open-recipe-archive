## Ingredients

- kid goat, cut into regular pieces and well-cooked
- butter
- garlic cloves
- salt
- ground pepper
- bay leaves
- butter
- water

## Directions

1. After the kid goat is cut into regular pieces, washed and well cooked, place a dish with butter on the fire
2. Partially cook some garlic cloves in the butter, remove them after frying
3. Gradually add the kid goat so that it browns
4. Then place together in the dish and add salt, ground pepper, bay leaves, a little butter and proportioned water so that it reduces and the kid goat remains in the butter
