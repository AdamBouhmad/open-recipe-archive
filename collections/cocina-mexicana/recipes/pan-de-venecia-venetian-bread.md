## Ingredients

- one pound of rice, well washed
- two quarts of milk
- one pound of very white sugar
- three well-beaten egg whites
- butter
- ground biscuit

## Directions

1. Break the well-washed rice in a metate
2. Add two quarts of milk, one pound of very white sugar, and three well-beaten egg whites
3. In a dish greased with butter and the bottom sprinkled with a little ground biscuit, pour the paste
4. Beat it very well
5. Place in the oven or at two fires until it sets
6. Serve with sugar and cinnamon on top
