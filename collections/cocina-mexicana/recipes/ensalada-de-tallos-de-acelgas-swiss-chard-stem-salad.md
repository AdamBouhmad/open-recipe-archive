## Ingredients

- Swiss chard with large leaves
- salt
- water
- oil
- garlic
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Choose Swiss chard with large leaves which have better stems
2. Cook only the stems in salted water, cut into pieces of one inch or a little more
3. Fry afterwards in oil with garlic and season with salt, oil and vinegar
