## Ingredients

- hare with skin
- bread, toasted
- cumin
- garlic
- pepper
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- paper
- lard

## Directions

1. Cook the hare with its skin, and when the skin loosens, remove it.
2. Make a sauce of toasted bread, cumin, garlic and pepper, ground all together and dissolved in oil with a little vinegar.
3. Add sufficient salt and mix, making sure the broth is well thickened.
4. Coat the hare inside and out with this sauce.
5. Place on the grill wrapped in buttered paper.
6. Keep at mild fire until cooked.
7. Remove papers and coat again.
8. Place in oven until browned.
