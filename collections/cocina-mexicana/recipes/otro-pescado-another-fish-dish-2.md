## Ingredients

- fish
- garlic
- cumin
- Castilian pepper
- tomatoes
- green chile
- onion
- oregano
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry the fish.
2. Grind garlic, cumin, and Castilian pepper and fry with a good amount of well-ripened tomatoes, a green chile, and onion in large pieces, crushing with the spoon.
3. When well fried, set aside.
4. Just before serving, coat with a sauce made of cooked onion in large pieces, sprinkled oregano, oil, and vinegar.
