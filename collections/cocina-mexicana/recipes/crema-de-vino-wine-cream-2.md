## Ingredients

- Egg yolks
- Powdered sugar
- Wine from Valdepeñas or Málaga

## Directions

1. Beat eight or ten egg yolks with sufficient powdered sugar, and to the beaten mixture add little by little, without stopping stirring, a bottle of Valdepeñas or Málaga wine, according to preference, sweetened and aromatic
2. Cook everything without stopping to stir, until the cream is perfectly bound
