## Ingredients

- milk
- egg yolks
- sugar
- butter
- marquesote (sponge cake)
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Mix as much milk as needed with egg yolks and sugar until it becomes as thick as jericalla
2. Butter a platter
3. Place a layer of marquesote with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and cinnamon
4. Place another layer of milk on top
5. Continue filling the platter in this manner
6. Place at gentle heat until you observe that the milk has penetrated well into the marquesote
7. Dust the top with ground sugar and cinnamon
