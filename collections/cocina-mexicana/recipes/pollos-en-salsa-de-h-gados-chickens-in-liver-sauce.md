## Ingredients

- cooked chickens
- oil
- pepper
- lemon juice
- green chile
- ripe tomato
- sheep liver
- broth from cooking the chickens
- toasted bread
- lard
- clove and cinnamon
- salt to taste
- cooked ham
- vinegar
- tornachilos
- olives

## Directions

1. After the chickens are cooked, coat them with oil, pepper, and lemon juice.
2. Put them on a grill to roast and remove them before they brown.
3. Chop green chile and ripe tomato.
4. Fry a sheep liver, and once well fried, grind it.
5. Combine the ground liver with the chopped mixture and fry everything well.
6. Add the broth in which the chickens were cooked and toasted bread fried in lard and ground with a little clove, cinnamon, and sufficient salt.
7. Place the chickens in the sauce and when it comes to a boil, add cooked ham in pieces, vinegar, tornachilos, and olives.
8. Once everything is seasoned and the broth thickens, remove from heat.
