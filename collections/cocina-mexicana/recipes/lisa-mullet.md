## Ingredients

- garlic
- fish
- ground spices
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper
- laurel
- oregano
- orange leaves
- Castilian vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. For each four mullets, use two heads of garlic
2. Add ground spices in powder: cloves, cinnamon and pepper in abundance, laurel, oregano, orange leaves, Castilian vinegar and oil until the fish is covered
3. Put everything to cook in a covered pot set over another pot
