## Ingredients

- half an ounce of fine cinnamon
- tomato
- garlic
- all spices except saffron and cumin
- red wine
- water from boiling the cinnamon
- a piece of sugar
- toasted sesame
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- pieces of candied citron

## Directions

1. Boil half an ounce of fine cinnamon in a little water.
2. Grind it with tomato, garlic, and all spices except saffron and cumin.
3. Add a little red wine, the water in which the cinnamon boiled, and a piece of sugar.
4. Add toasted sesame, raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and pieces of candied citron.
5. Let it boil well, and when seasoned, add the chicken in pieces and serve.
