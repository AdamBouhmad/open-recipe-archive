## Ingredients

- two pounds of flour
- half pound of fresh butter
- half pound of powdered sugar
- eight egg yolks
- one small bowl of water

## Directions

1. For two pounds of flour, add half a pound of fresh butter, half a pound of powdered sugar, eight egg yolks, and one small bowl of water
2. Knead everything and when the dough reaches regular consistency
3. Form the biscuits
4. Place on papers and put in the oven until they bake completely
