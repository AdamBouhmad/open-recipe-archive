## Ingredients

- sliced bread or sponge cake
- wine or other liquor
- beaten eggs
- lard or oil
- syrup

## Directions

1. Soak slices of bread or sponge cake in wine or other liquor
2. Coat with beaten eggs
3. Fry in lard or oil
4. Serve in syrup
