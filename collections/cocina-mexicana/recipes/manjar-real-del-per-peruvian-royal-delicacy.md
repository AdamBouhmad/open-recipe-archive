## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one pound of almonds
- one chicken breast
- clarified syrup at medium point

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at medium point with two pounds of sugar
2. Away from the fire, mix in one pound of ground almonds and one ground chicken breast
3. Return to the fire and keep there until it reaches the candy stage
