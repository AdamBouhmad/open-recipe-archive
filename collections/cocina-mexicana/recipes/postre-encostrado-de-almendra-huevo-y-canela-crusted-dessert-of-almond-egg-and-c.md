## Ingredients

- four pounds of sugar
- one pound of soaked, clean, ground almonds
- forty egg yolks
- half an ounce of ground cinnamon
- butter
- ground sponge cake
- sugar
- ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at half-point with four pounds of sugar
2. Away from heat add one pound of soaked, cleaned, and ground almonds
3. Put on fire, and as soon as it has thickened a little, remove it and mix in forty egg yolks
4. Return to heat, and as soon as it is at the point of detaching from the pan, remove it again and stir in half an ounce of ground cinnamon
5. Empty into a platter buttered, cover with sponge cake, sugar, and cinnamon, all ground, bathe with melted butter, and put gentle fire on top to form the crust
