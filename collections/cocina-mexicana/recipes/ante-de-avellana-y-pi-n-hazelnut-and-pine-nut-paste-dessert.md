## Ingredients

- Hazelnuts, one pound
- Pine nuts, one pound
- Sugar, pound and a half
- Cinnamon powder, half ounce
- Sherry wine
- Sponge cake
- Almonds and raisins for decoration

## Directions

1. Make the syrup first, well clarified
2. Grind the pine nuts and hazelnuts together
3. Dissolve in the syrup and once it has boiled several times, stir in the cinnamon powder and a little Sherry wine
4. Once it reaches the proper point, place a layer of sponge cake and another of paste, rolling it like the others
5. Decorate with paste, almonds, and raisins
