## Ingredients

- bofes o livianos (lungs)
- hígados (livers)
- agua (water)
- manteca (lard)
- sal (salt)
- cebolla (onion)
- peregil (parsley)
- ajo (garlic)
- pimienta (pepper)
- clavo (cloves)
- canela (cinnamon)
- chilchotes (small chiles)

## Directions

1. Purchase the offal from the slaughterhouse
2. Cook the offal with livers in a little water with lard and salt
3. Fry sliced onion in lard being careful not to brown it
4. Remove the onion from the lard and add the offal and livers cut into small pieces
5. Add plenty of ground parsley, roasted garlic cloves that are also ground, pepper, clove, cinnamon, the fried onion slices, and two or three roasted small chiles
6. Season well and serve
