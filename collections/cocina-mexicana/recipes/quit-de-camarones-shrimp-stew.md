## Ingredients

- lard
- French oil
- onion
- parsley
- garlic
- tomato
- shrimp
- olives
- capers
- pepper
- soaked bread
- vinegar
- salt

## Directions

1. Heat a bit of lard
2. When hot, add a bit of French oil, plenty of finely chopped onion, parsley, garlic and tomato
3. Once the mixture is well fried, add the shrimp with olives, capers, ground pepper, soaked bread and a bit of vinegar
4. Let them season with salt, aiming for it to be somewhat salty
