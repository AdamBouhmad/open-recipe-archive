## Ingredients

- six pounds flour
- one pound butter
- twenty-four eggs
- one cuartillo warm milk
- one bowl yeast dissolved in white tequesquite water
- half bowl orange juice
- half bowl salt in grain
- one bowl anise water
- one bowl powdered sugar
- one large slice of cooked pumpkin with dark color

## Directions

1. Mix gradually six pounds of flour with one pound of butter, twenty-four eggs, one cuartillo of warm milk, one bowl of yeast dissolved in white tequesquite water, half bowl of orange juice, half bowl of salt in grain, one bowl of anise water, one bowl of powdered sugar, and one large slice of cooked pumpkin with dark color
2. Mix all this in parts and then knead much until it makes bubbles
3. Cover and put in the sun for two hours
4. After that divide the dough into equal pieces and stretch it in the usual way that puff makers do, taking care that the sheets are very thin and do not break
5. Fry one by one in a pan or stretched tart pan with plenty of lard
