## Ingredients

- Potatoes cooked a l'étuvée
- A good piece of butter
- Salt
- Pepper in grains
- A good piece of butter
- Parsley, garlic, all minced very fine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. Cut cooked potatoes into pieces and put them in a saucepan with a good piece of butter, salt and pepper in grains
2. Sauté them from time to time over a good fire
3. Then braise them with a sauce made of a good piece of butter, parsley, garlic, all minced very fine, salt, pepper and lemon juice, all kneaded together in a saucepan over low heat
4. Serve this stew as promptly as possible
