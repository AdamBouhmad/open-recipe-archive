## Ingredients

- beef
- two onions
- some spice
- one head of garlic, well peeled
- some bay leaves and oregano
- one glass of wine
- salt
- parsley
- one small cup of oil
- a little more than half or a little more of brandy

## Directions

1. Place the meat, two onions, and a little spice in a pot, one head of garlic well peeled, some bay leaves and oregano, one glass of wine, salt, parsley, and one small cup of oil, a little more than half or a little more of brandy.
2. Let it cook over low heat, stirring from time to time and placing a piece of brown paper in the mouth of the pot, and its lid on top.
