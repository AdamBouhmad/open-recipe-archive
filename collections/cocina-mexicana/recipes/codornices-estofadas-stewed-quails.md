## Ingredients

- cleaned and emptied quails
- burned butter
- sliced sausage
- salt
- chopped onion
- clove
- cinnamon
- ground pepper
- parsley
- raisins
- chopped almonds
- Malaga wine
- flour
- butter

## Directions

1. Clean and empty the quails
2. In a pot with burned butter, partially cook the quails with sliced sausage and necessary salt
3. Add chopped onion and let it brown
4. Once browned, add ground clove, cinnamon, and pepper, parsley, raisins, chopped almonds, and Malaga wine instead of water
5. Cover the pot, sealing the joints well, until the quails are cooked
6. Thicken the sauce afterward with flour browned in butter
7. Pour the sauce over the quails and serve
