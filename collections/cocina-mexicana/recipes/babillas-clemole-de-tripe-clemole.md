## Ingredients

- tripe (babillas)
- lard
- roasted tomatoes
- roasted chiles
- whole small zucchini or sliced zucchini
- hoja santa sprigs

## Directions

1. Fry roasted tomatoes and roasted, deveined chiles in lard, using three-quarters wide chiles and one-quarter pasilla chiles, ground together
2. When well fried, add the prepared and cooked tripe as described in the previous article
3. Add broth from the cooking liquid
4. Add whole or sliced zucchini depending on size
5. Add sprigs of hoja santa
6. Season with salt and let it reach the proper consistency
