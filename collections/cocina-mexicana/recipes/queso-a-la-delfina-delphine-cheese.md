## Ingredients

- cream
- sugar
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Boil the cream with sugar and orange flower water, let it cool immediately and beat until it has risen very well
2. Drain in a strainer with cloth, or in a willow-rod basket, or mold of the shape you wish to give the cheese
3. When well drained, pour into the serving dish in which it is to be served
