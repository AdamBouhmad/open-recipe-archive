## Ingredients

- one cuartillo of flour
- three eggs
- one quarter of butter
- a little salt
- cream
- one tablespoon of Catalan brandy

## Directions

1. Make a dough with flour, eggs, butter, salt, cream, and Catalan brandy
2. Let the dough rest for three hours
3. Thin it out with a rolling pin to a regular thickness
4. Cut into desired shapes
5. Fry them
6. Dust both sides with sugar
