## Ingredients

- Milk
- Cinnamon
- Egg yolks

## Directions

1. Sweeten milk and strain.
2. Add cinnamon and five egg yolks for each cooking pan of milk.
3. Pour into a bowl and set the jericalla in a water bath with fire above, or in the steam of a pot, always covered with a griddle with coals.
4. Sprinkle cinnamon on top when serving.
