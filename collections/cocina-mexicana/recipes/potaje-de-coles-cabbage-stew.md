## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Salt
- Oil or lard
- Pepper
- Nutmeg powder
- Cream of milk

## Directions

1. After thoroughly washing, cook cabbage with salt
2. Cool and drain, cutting into thick pieces
3. Reheat with oil or lard, salt, pepper and nutmeg powder
4. Then add cream of milk and cook over low heat until the salt reduces and the cabbage is well bound
