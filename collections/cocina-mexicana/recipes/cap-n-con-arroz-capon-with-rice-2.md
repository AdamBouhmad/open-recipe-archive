## Ingredients

- capon
- water
- salt
- thyme
- bay leaf
- clove
- three large onions
- parsnip
- ham

## Directions

1. Truss and tie the capon so it maintains a graceful position while cooking.
2. Place in a casserole with water that almost entirely covers it, adding salt, very little thyme and bay leaf, one clove, three large onions, a parsnip and ham fat.
3. Let them cook well in this broth, turning from time to time.
