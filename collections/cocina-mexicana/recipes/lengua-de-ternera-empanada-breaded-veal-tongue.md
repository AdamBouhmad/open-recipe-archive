## Ingredients

- veal tongue
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- bay leaf
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Soak the tongue in water to bleed
2. Cook it in water with salt and vinegar
3. Skin it, add a little ground bay leaf, roll in bread crumbs, and fry
4. Serve with sauce and cold
