## Ingredients

- rice
- salt
- milk
- white clarified sugar syrup
- flour
- lard

## Directions

1. After washing the rice in two or three waters so it does not break, place in milk with a little salt
2. Add white clarified sugar syrup until the rice cooks well
3. Once cooked, let it consume and thicken greatly
4. Place in a platter and the next day, take portions of this rice and form whatever figures desired
5. Roll in flour, then fry in lard until golden and sprinkle with sugar over them
