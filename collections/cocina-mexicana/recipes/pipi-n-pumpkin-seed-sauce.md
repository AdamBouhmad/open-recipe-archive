## Ingredients

- pumpkin seeds, dried
- water
- salt
- cumin
- garlic
- small green chiles
- tomatoes
- lard
- cooked turkey
- turkey broth

## Directions

1. Once the pumpkin seeds are dry, rub them with a cloth so the chaff falls off
2. Soak in salted water for a while
3. Once removed, let them dry in the sun
4. Once dry, grind like chocolate with fire under the metate until completely ground
5. Fry in sufficient lard until becoming like small balls
6. Then add cumin, garlic, small green chiles, and tomatoes, all raw and ground
7. Once fried with salt, add the cooked turkey and its broth
8. Boil until somewhat thick. The chile will be whatever desired, as it is only to give a slight subtle heat
