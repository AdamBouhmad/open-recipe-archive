## Ingredients

- one cup flour
- one cup milk
- four eggs
- lard for greasing and frying
- sugar syrup

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with the milk first, then add the eggs and mix until well combined
2. Grease a frying pan with lard and place it in a pot of boiling water
3. Pour the mixture into the pan until it reaches the thickness of a cigar butt and place a small comal on top over low heat to set
4. Once set, remove and divide into pieces like meat chicharrones
5. Fry in warm lard, continuously basting with the spoon so they puff up and brown
6. Make a light sugar syrup and pour over them
7. Serve hot
