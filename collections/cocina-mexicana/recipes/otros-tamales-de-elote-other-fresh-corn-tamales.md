## Ingredients

- The most tender fresh corn ears
- Milk
- Sugar
- Salt
- Twelve ounces of lard for 25 to 30 ears

## Directions

1. Choose the most tender corn ears.
2. Remove the heads with a knife and separate the best husks.
3. Cut the kernels and grind well, washing them from the metate with a little milk.
4. Add sugar and salt to taste.
5. Use twelve ounces of lard for twenty-five to thirty ears.
