## Ingredients

- snipe
- water
- salt
- onions
- lard
- tomatoes
- garlic
- pepper
- cilantro
- broth

## Directions

1. After cleaning them well, removing entrails and washing, cook them in water with salt and 2 or 3 onions
2. Once cooked, fry them in lard
3. In the same lard fry minced tomatoes, minced garlic, a little pepper and ground cilantro with the necessary salt
4. Moisten the mixture with a little broth from the cooking of the snipe and brown at low heat on both sides
5. Serve alone or with the sauce that best suits, among those of tomato or chile
