## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- twenty ounces of grated and ground coconut
- cinnamon sticks
- sixteen egg yolks
- four ounces of ground almond
- one cuartillo of cream
- butter
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with the sugar, clarify it, strain it, and bring it to a boil until it reaches medium point.
2. Remove from heat and add the grated and ground coconut and cinnamon sticks.
3. Return to heat and when it begins to take on a paste consistency, remove from heat and mix in the egg yolks without beating, ground almond, and cream.
4. Return to heat and let it reach the point of cajeta (a thick candy-like consistency).
5. Remove the cinnamon sticks and pour onto a plate, brush with butter using a feather, and place under a camp oven to brown.
6. When serving, dust with ground cinnamon.
