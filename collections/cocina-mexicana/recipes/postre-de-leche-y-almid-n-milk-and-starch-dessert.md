## Ingredients

- starch, two tablespoons
- milk, five cuartillos
- sugar
- orange leaves
- cinnamon sticks
- egg whites, eight

## Directions

1. Dissolve two tablespoons of starch in five cuartillos of milk and sweeten with sugar so that it becomes loaded
2. Add some orange leaves and a few cinnamon sticks
3. Place immediately over fire until it reaches a point higher than cream
4. After removing the dessert from the fire, add and stir eight egg whites
5. Place a comal with fire on top
