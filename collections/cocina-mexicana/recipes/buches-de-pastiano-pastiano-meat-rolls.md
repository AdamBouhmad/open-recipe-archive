## Ingredients

- Equal parts of lamb and pork meat
- Raisins
- Sesame
- Pepper
- Chopped apples
- Clove
- Pine nuts
- Cinnamon
- Saffron
- Garlic
- Lard
- Paper cartridges
- Roasted tomato
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Mint
- Spices

## Directions

1. Chop lamb and pork meat as if for meatballs
2. Add to the chopped meat raisins, sesame, pepper, chopped apples, clove, pine nuts, cinnamon, saffron and garlic
3. Beat everything very well and knead with lard
4. Make paper cartridges the size of cigar tubes and fill them
5. Cook over two fires
6. While they cook, roast some very red tomato
7. Chop the roasted tomato with garlic, parsley and mint
8. Make a sauce with this and spices from those added to the meat
9. Place the meat rolls in the sauce
