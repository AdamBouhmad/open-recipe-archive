## Ingredients

- turkey giblets prepared as previously described
- butter
- turnips
- a tablespoon of flour
- broth from the giblets
- a lump of sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare the giblets as previously described and cook them as for pipila in adobo
2. After cooking, fry some turnips in butter until they take on a good color and remove from the butter
3. In the same butter, add a tablespoon of flour and make a paste
4. Dissolve this mixture in the broth from the giblets
5. Let cook until it thickens, then strain
6. Cook the turnips in this sauce
7. Add a lump of sugar
8. Place the giblets on a platter, garnish and cover with the turnips
9. Pour the sauce over everything
