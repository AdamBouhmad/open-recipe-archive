## Ingredients

- Six quartillos (about 1.5 liters) of milk
- Sufficient sugar
- Cinnamon sticks
- Butter
- More than half a large cold biscuit
- Twenty-five egg yolks

## Directions

1. Heat six quartillos of milk with sufficient sugar and cinnamon sticks, then let it cool
2. Coat a pot well with butter that is not new
3. Crumble more than half of a large cold biscuit into the pot
4. Pour the milk into the pot, crumble more biscuit, and add pieces of butter until completing one pound, accounting for what was used to coat the pot
5. Before pouring the milk into it, dissolve twenty-five egg yolks
6. Strain through a sieve and sweeten a little more than usual
7. Pour into the pot and put the cake in the oven to brown, or cook it over coals, and after it has set, cover it with a comal with fire on top
