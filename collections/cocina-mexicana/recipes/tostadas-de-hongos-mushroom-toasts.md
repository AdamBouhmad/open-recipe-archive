## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- butter
- parsley
- broth
- flour
- salt
- egg yolks
- bread slices

## Directions

1. After cleaning the mushrooms, slice them thickly
2. Fry in butter with a little parsley
3. Moisten with good broth and add butter mixed with a little flour
4. Let season over gentle heat adding necessary fine salt
5. When well cooked, bind with egg yolks
6. Arrange thin toasted slices of fried bread on a platter
7. Place the mushrooms on each toast and serve
