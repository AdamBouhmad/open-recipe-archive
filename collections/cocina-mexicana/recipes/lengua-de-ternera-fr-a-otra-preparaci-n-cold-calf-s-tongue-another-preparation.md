## Ingredients

- Well-salted calf's tongue
- vinegar
- water
- bay leaf
- Tabasco pepper
- fine pepper
- oil
- salt

## Directions

1. Keep the tongue for twelve hours in vinegar
2. Cook afterwards in water, precisely in the quantity needed for cooking, with bay leaf, Tabasco pepper, fine pepper and vinegar
3. Let rest for twelve hours after cooking
4. Then peel, dry, anoint with oil and rub with powder of the two peppers
5. Wrap in paper and serve cold with some sauce
