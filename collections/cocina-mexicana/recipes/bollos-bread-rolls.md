## Ingredients

- One and a half pounds flour
- Half ounce lard
- A little flour and warm water
- Half pound flour
- Half ounce salt
- Half pound lard
- Six eggs

## Directions

1. Place one and a half pounds of flour on a clean board
2. Mix with half ounce of lard, a little flour and warm water
3. Form a consistent paste and fold it upon itself
4. Add to this paste half pound of flour, half ounce of salt, half pound of lard and six eggs
5. Unite everything to the fermented paste
6. Knead again
7. Let it rest for ten hours
8. Form the rolls from this dough
