## Ingredients

- Butter
- Onions
- Cream from cooked milk
- Sugar
- Six quartillos of blood (four from pork and two from beef)
- Two quartillos of the thickest cream possible
- Half a pound of rice with milk unsweetened
- Three pounds of pork fat or jowl cut in dice form
- Salt
- Pepper
- Spices
- Fresh cilantro
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cook onions in butter in more or less quantity according to what you want to make, letting them cook until they fall apart
2. Add a good cup of cooked milk cream, taking care if fresh that they are very thick
3. Add a lump of sugar the size of a walnut
4. Have onion substance equivalent to half a pound of rice and let it be very white
5. Put this onion substance or broth into six quartillos of blood, four of pork and two of beef
6. Add two quartillos of the thickest cream possible
7. Add half a pound of rice with unsweetened milk
8. Add three pounds of pork fat or jowl cut in dice form
9. Add salt, pepper, spices and a bit of fresh cilantro and finely chopped parsley
10. Mix everything perfectly and taste with your finger to ensure it is well seasoned
11. Fill the cleaned intestines using a funnel, taking care not to fill them too much so they don't burst when cooking, and blow them before filling in case they have holes
12. Tie the ends and place the puddings in a pot with hot unsalted water
13. They should cook without boiling, and when the water begins to rumble, prick them from time to time with a pin
14. If blood runs out, they are not yet done
15. They are done when they feel firm
16. Set them aside to cool and give them a few pricks after cooling to fry them in a pan with lard, which is the same as putting them on a grill
