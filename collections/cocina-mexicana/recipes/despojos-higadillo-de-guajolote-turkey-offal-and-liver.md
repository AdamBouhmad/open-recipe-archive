## Ingredients

- turkey offal and liver
- butter
- flour
- water
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- green onions
- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- verjuice or vinegar
- chiles
- potatoes
- bread

## Directions

1. Cut the offal and liver into pieces and soak in cold water for one or two hours so that the meat becomes white
2. Drain well and place in a pot with a piece of butter and a spoonful of flour, stirring until the butter has melted
3. Moisten with a glass of water and add salt, pepper, grated nutmeg, a bouquet of parsley and green onions
4. Place the offal in this mixture and let cook for three quarters of an hour
5. Separately, mix three egg yolks and lemon juice, verjuice or a splash of vinegar into the sauce
6. When the offal is nearly cooked, add some chiles in strips and continue cooking, covering the pot with brown paper
7. Garnish with fried potato slices and toasted bread slices
