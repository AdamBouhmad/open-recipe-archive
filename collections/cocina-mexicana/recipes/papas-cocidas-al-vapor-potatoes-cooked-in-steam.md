## Ingredients

- potatoes
- water

## Directions

1. Place them in a pot with very little water at the bottom and on a layer formed with shingles or wicker twigs
2. Cover the pot with a thick cloth, placing on top a pot with water that fits the mouth of the pot so as to prevent evaporation
3. Let them cook in this manner, which achieves them remaining whole and becoming softer than if cooked another way
