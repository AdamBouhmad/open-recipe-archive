## Ingredients

- pan francés or pan de mollete (French bread or soft bread)
- manteca, mantequilla, or aceite (lard, butter, or oil)
- sal fina (fine salt)

## Directions

1. Cut the upper part of the French bread or soft bread loaf with a knife, leaving the crust according to the size of what is to be placed on top or what the dish requires
2. Fry the bread in lard, butter, or oil seasoned with fine salt, according to what the stew that the coscorrones are to garnish requires
3. Take care that they remain well browned, without burning on any part
