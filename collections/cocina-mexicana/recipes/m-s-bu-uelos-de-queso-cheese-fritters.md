## Ingredients

- fresh cheese
- salt (if needed)
- cold bread, grated
- egg
- lard for frying

## Directions

1. Grind fresh cheese, and if it does not have salt, add and mix it with grated cold bread
2. Well incorporated, beat with egg
3. Remove small portions with a spoon and fry in lard
