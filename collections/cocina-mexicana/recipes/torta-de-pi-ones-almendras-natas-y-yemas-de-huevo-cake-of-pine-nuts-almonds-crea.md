## Ingredients

- One pound of cleaned pine nuts
- One pound of almonds without shell, soaked for at least six hours
- Two pounds of sugar
- Half pound of custard cream (natillas)
- Six cooked egg yolks
- Half ounce of cinnamon
- A little clove
- A little nutmeg
- Lard
- A little flour
- Pastry dough for lattice

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of cleaned pine nuts and one pound of soaked almonds
2. Mix with two pounds of sugar
3. Add half pound of custard cream and six cooked egg yolks
4. Add half ounce of ground cinnamon, a little clove, and a little nutmeg, all ground
5. Pour into a pan or pot with lard and a little flour
6. Form a lattice of pastry dough on top
7. Bake in the oven until cooked
