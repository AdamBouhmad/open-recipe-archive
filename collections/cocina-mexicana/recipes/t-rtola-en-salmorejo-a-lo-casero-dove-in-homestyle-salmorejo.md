## Ingredients

- roasted and cold doves
- broth from the pot
- salt
- whole peppercorns
- vinegar
- fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Take roasted and cold doves
2. Remove the head and interior of the belly; discard the gizzards
3. Grind the rest
4. Dissolve the ground meat in the broth from the pot
5. Strain through a sieve
6. Season the strained mixture with salt, whole peppercorns, and a little vinegar
7. In this sauce, heat the doves without letting them cook
8. Serve with fried bread crusts
