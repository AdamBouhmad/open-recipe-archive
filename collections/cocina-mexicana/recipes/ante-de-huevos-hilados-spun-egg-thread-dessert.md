## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- sixteen egg yolks
- sponge cake slices

## Directions

1. With the syrup prepared from two pounds of sugar, use sixteen egg yolks, beaten very well.
2. When the syrup is at medium consistency and boiling vigorously, pour the yolks through a small jug with a hole, and the stream coming out should be circulated throughout the syrup so the yolks become threaded.
3. Make slices of sponge cake, place them in the serving dish, and pour the eggs over them in this manner.
4. Alternate layers of sponge cake and eggs.
5. If a more delicate and soft result is desired, between layers of sponge cake add the eggs and pieces of whipped cream, making it as full as desired.
6. Bathe it with abundant syrup and orange blossom water.
