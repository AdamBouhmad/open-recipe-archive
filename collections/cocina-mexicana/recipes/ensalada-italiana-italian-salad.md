## Ingredients

- celery
- endive
- chicory
- lettuce
- chervil
- watercress
- pomegranate seeds
- sugar
- hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. Combine various vegetables: celery, endive, chicory, and lettuce
2. Garnish with various herbs such as chervil, watercress, and others of this class
3. Add pomegranate seeds with sugar
4. Many garnish the platter with hard-boiled eggs
