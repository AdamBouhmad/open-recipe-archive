## Ingredients

- 6 pasta macaroons (including 2 of bitter almond)
- 4 cuartillos milk
- 1 tablespoon candied orange blossoms
- 2 ounces sugar
- 4 egg yolks

## Directions

1. Mix 6 macaroons of pasta, choosing 2 to be of bitter almonds, and dissolve them in 4 cuartillos of milk
2. Add 1 tablespoon of candied orange blossoms, 2 ounces of sugar, and 4 egg yolks
3. Cook the cream stirring continuously
4. Pass through a sieve and let cool in the dish in which it should be served
