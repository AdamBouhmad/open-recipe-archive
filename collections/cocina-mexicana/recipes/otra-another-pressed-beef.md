## Ingredients

- large pieces of pulp
- ham
- garlic cloves
- pepper
- whole cloves
- good current vinegar
- a little water
- crushed garlic heads
- oregano
- thyme
- bay leaf
- Tabasco peppers
- salt
- lettuce leaves
- oil
- vinegar
- pepper powder
- oregano
- thyme
- onion slices
- olives
- capers
- chiles in vinegar

## Directions

1. Cut large pieces of pulp and stud with plenty of ham, garlic cloves, pepper, and whole cloves.
2. Put in a pot with plenty of good current vinegar and a little water.
3. Add crushed garlic heads, oregano, thyme, bay leaf, Tabasco peppers, and corresponding salt.
4. Cover the pot so it does not evaporate and bring to a boil.
5. Once the meat is cooked, remove and place on a platter and take to the open air, which is sufficient for it to become like pressed.
6. To eat, cut into very thin slices and place on lettuce leaves and add oil, vinegar, a pinch of pepper, oregano, thyme, onion slices, olives, capers, and chiles in vinegar.
