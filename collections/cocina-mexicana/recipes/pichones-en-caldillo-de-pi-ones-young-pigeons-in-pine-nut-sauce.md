## Ingredients

- cooked young pigeons
- lard
- clean pine nuts
- cinnamon
- vinegar
- broth from cooking the pigeons
- bread browned in lard
- sweet wine

## Directions

1. The cooked pigeons are fried in lard, from which they are then removed
2. Add to the fat to also fry, clean pine nuts ground with cinnamon and a little vinegar
3. Then place the pigeons and the broth in which they were cooked, a little bread browned in lard and ground, and sweet wine, letting the sauce boil so it is not watery
