## Ingredients

- veal offal
- garlic
- vinegar
- salt
- cooked ham
- pepper
- clove
- thyme
- oregano
- pork caul
- lard
- wine
- spices

## Directions

1. Clean the offal well and remove the trachea or esophagus
2. Coat all parts with ground garlic mixed with vinegar and salt
3. Lard the entire piece with strips of cooked ham rolled in salt, pepper, clove, thyme and oregano in powder form
4. In a casserole dish coated with lard, spread a sheet of pork caul that has also been buttered and dusted with the same spices and aromatics used for the ham strips
5. Arrange the offal on top with the lights, kidneys, liver and heart separated, ensuring all parts are well wrapped in the caul and will not come loose even if turned
6. Add a little wine, lard and the same spices used inside
7. Place in the oven to cook, turning it to ensure even cooking
8. When the broth is consumed, let it fry in its own fat
