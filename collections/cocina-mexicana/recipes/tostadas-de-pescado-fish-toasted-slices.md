## Ingredients

- carp meat
- parsley
- green lemon rind
- almond biscuits made with bitter almonds
- a little butter
- salt
- egg yolks
- bread crumb soaked in cream

## Directions

1. Chop carp meat with parsley and green lemon rind
2. Pound everything together with almond biscuits made with bitter almonds, a little butter and salt, some egg yolks and bread crumb soaked in cream
3. Place this filling on the toasted slices and put them in the oven or in a baking pan so they take color
4. Cover with sugar and glaze by passing a red-hot shovel over them
5. Serve as side dishes or garnishes for other stews
