## Ingredients

- figs
- water
- syrup

## Directions

1. Remove the skin well from the figs and place them in a pot with boiling water
2. As soon as they come to a boil again, remove them, dry them well, and place them in syrup
3. After the syrup has penetrated the figs, let it reach the proper point and remove from heat
