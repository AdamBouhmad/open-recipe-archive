## Ingredients

- mixed lean and fat pork meat
- salt
- pepper
- clove, ginger and nutmeg (optional)

## Directions

1. Take mixed lean and fat pork meat, mince it and season with salt and pepper
2. Some add clove, ginger and nutmeg, but this is not most common
3. Fill wide pork or veal intestines and cook them in the cauldron
4. When cooked hang them to remove them
5. This sausage can also be made with lean meat, and then it is given the name of lean butifarra
