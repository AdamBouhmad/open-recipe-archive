## Ingredients

- One pound of tender veal
- Garlic clove
- Egg yolk
- Breadcrumbs
- Pepper powder
- Lemon juice
- Salt
- Lard

## Directions

1. Chop and grind one pound of tender veal with a garlic clove
2. Mix in an egg yolk, breadcrumbs, pepper powder, a few drops of lemon juice, and sufficient salt
3. When well mixed, grind it at the edge of the metate so the meat strands hang like totopostles
4. Fry in lard
5. Serve alone or with oil and vinegar
