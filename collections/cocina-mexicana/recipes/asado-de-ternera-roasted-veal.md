## Ingredients

- Veal pulp
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon
- Lard

## Directions

1. Take veal pulp and rub it with salt, pepper, and lemon.
2. Heat a little lard and brown the meat slightly.
3. Cover it perfectly without adding water.
4. Turn the meat frequently and drain the moisture from the lid until the meat is tender.
5. If the sauce has been consumed by the time it reaches the table, add a little hot water and place it briefly on the heat so it has a dark sauce.
6. Serve it with slices of fried bread.
