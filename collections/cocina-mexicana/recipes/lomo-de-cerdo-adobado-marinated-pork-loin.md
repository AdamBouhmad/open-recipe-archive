## Ingredients

- chiles anchos
- chiles pasillas
- ajos
- cominos
- jengibre
- tomillo
- orégano
- vino
- vinagre
- lomo de cerdo
- agua
- manteca
- aceitunas
- chilitos

## Directions

1. Make an adobo sauce from deveined and soaked ancho and pasilla chiles, ground together with sufficient garlic, cumin, ginger, thyme, oregano, wine and vinegar
2. Marinate the loin, whether whole or in portions, in this adobo
3. The next day, add water and lard, and cook over two fires as with the previous recipes
4. When ready to serve, garnish with olives and small chiles
