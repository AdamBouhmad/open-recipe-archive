## Ingredients

- butter
- oil
- cold bread slices
- garlic
- raw egg yolks
- broth
- onion
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- capers

## Directions

1. Place a pan on the fire with butter and oil
2. Fry sliced cold bread, garlic cloves and two or three raw egg yolks in it
3. Grind everything and dissolve in broth from the pot
4. Fry minced garlic and onion, and add the broth seasoned with necessary salt and pepper, a little vinegar and sliced capers
5. Let it thicken on low heat, and when thickened, pass it to the sauce boat
