## Ingredients

- tongues
- ham
- garlic
- pepper
- cloves
- onion
- thyme
- oregano
- Castilian vinegar
- wine
- water
- salt
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Spike the whole tongues with ham, garlic, pepper and cloves
2. Place in a pot with plenty of sliced onion, thyme and oregano, Castilian vinegar, an equal amount of warm wine and water with salt
3. Grind cloves, cinnamon and pepper, cover the pot so it does not evaporate until the tongue cooks
4. The broth should be thick and serve with olives and chiles
