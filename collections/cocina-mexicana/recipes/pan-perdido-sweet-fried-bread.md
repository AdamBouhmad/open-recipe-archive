## Ingredients

- five or six eggs
- one to one and a half tablespoon orange blossom water
- good brandy
- dried lemon zest
- bread slices
- sugar

## Directions

1. In an earthenware dish or deep plate, beat five or six eggs well.
2. Add one to one and a half tablespoon of orange blossom water, good brandy and dried lemon zest in sufficient quantities to properly perfume the mixture.
3. Continue stirring and beating everything together.
4. Cut bread slices in the form of toasts, three to four lines thick.
5. For plating, cut these slices into the shape of stars, flowers, disks, etc.
6. Place the bread in the dish containing the mixture, leaving it there the necessary time so it can soak well.
7. Fry the bread afterwards and serve hot, arranging the slices as if they were fritters and dust with sufficient sugar.
