## Ingredients

- Three pounds of cacahuazentle corn
- Half pound of melted lard
- Eight egg yolks
- Sugar
- Warm water
- Cinnamon
- Raisins
- Pine nuts
- Almonds
- Walnuts
- Sesame

## Directions

1. Beat all ingredients without making foam.
2. Add plenty of cinnamon, raisins, pine nuts, almonds, walnuts, and sesame.
3. Cook in leaves as with the previous tamales.
4. Cover the pot with zacate while they cook.
5. Rice tamales are made the same way.
