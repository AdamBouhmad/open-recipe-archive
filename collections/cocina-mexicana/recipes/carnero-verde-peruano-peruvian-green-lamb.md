## Ingredients

- One or two pounds of lamb
- Oregano
- Ground pepper
- Salt
- Orange or lemon juice
- Lard
- Green chile
- Garlic
- Onions
- Hot water
- Parsley
- Spearmint
- Toasted almonds
- All spices

## Directions

1. Prepare a wide plate and cut the lamb into regular pieces, season with oregano, ground pepper, salt, and a few drops of orange or lemon juice, leaving it for a quarter hour to absorb the marinade flavor.
2. In the meantime, fry in a casserole in four ounces of lard a green chile, some garlic, and three onions.
3. Add the meat and braise with low heat.
4. After one hour add a little hot water so it does not burn.
5. When the meat is cooked, add finely chopped parsley and spearmint.
6. Pound toasted almonds in the metate, add a little of all spices, season, and let it cook a while longer, resulting in an exquisite stew.
