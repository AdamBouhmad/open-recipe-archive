## Ingredients

- Six pounds of red currants
- Three pounds of white currants
- One pound of raspberries
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Mix well and press six pounds of red currants, three of white and one of raspberries in cloth, extracting all possible juice
2. Place this juice on the fire in a cauldron, adding one pound of sugar for each pound of juice
3. When it has boiled and taken on regular consistency, pass through a sieve
4. Fill glasses or small jars and leave exposed to air for several days in a dry place
