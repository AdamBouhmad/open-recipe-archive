## Ingredients

- three or four pounds of beef
- four ounces of ham in slices
- onion slices
- carrot slices
- parsnip slices
- one onion studded with cloves
- one chicken divided into quarters
- some mushrooms
- one head of garlic
- other vegetables as desired
- a little broth
- very good broth

## Directions

1. Place the beef, ham slices, onion slices, carrot slices, and parsnip slices in a cooking pot
2. Add the onion studded with cloves, the chicken divided into quarters, mushrooms, head of garlic, and other vegetables as desired
3. Pour in a little broth and cover the pot well
4. Cook at very high heat at first, then over hot coals, gradually increasing the fire little by little
5. When everything begins to stick, moisten with very good broth and let it cook gently
6. Take care that everything becomes tender and has body without being too salty
