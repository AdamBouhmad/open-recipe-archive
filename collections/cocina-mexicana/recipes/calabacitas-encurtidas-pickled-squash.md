## Ingredients

- small calabacitas (squash)
- water
- vinegar
- salt from the earth
- strong vinegar
- garlic heads
- fine salt
- good oil
- thyme

## Directions

1. Take the small calabacitas and cook them in a little water that contains one third vinegar, adding some salt from the earth.
2. When they have cooked, place a pot on the fire with strong vinegar and crushed garlic heads, adding fine salt.
3. When the vinegar is boiling, add the calabacitas to it and cover the pot.
4. Let them cool; once cooled, drain them and transfer to a vessel with good oil and plenty of thyme.
5. These calabacitas are used to adorn various dishes.
