## Ingredients

- Onions
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Oregano
- Pomegranate
- Avocado
- Lettuce

## Directions

1. Remove the tails and crowns from the onions and cook them with salt.
2. Arrange them whole or separated into leaves on a platter.
3. Add oil, vinegar, oregano, pomegranate broken into seeds, and avocado in quarters with skin or peeled into pieces mixed with the onion.
4. Garnish with lettuce on top.
