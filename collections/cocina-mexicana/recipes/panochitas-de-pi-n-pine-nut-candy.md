## Ingredients

- sugar
- clarified syrup
- ground or whole pine nuts

## Directions

1. Make in the same way as panochitas de cacahuate, using ground or whole pine nuts in place of peanuts
2. Pine nut candy made with ground pine nuts does not include sesame seeds
