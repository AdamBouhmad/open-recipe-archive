## Ingredients

- sliced stale bread
- lard
- broth from the pot, seasoned and boiled
- cilantro, toasted and ground
- cooked brains
- salt
- pepper powder
- grated bread
- beaten egg
- or cooked kidney slices
- grated bread
- salt
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Toast the sliced stale bread in a comal and arrange in a casserole dish brushed with lard
2. Pour the seasoned broth over it, which has been boiled with toasted and ground cilantro in the quantity necessary so its flavor stands out
3. Place the casserole over hot coals with fire above it
4. Once it has boiled sufficiently so the bread is neither raw nor falling apart, remove from heat
5. Decorate with cooked brains seasoned with salt and pepper powder, rolled in grated bread, coated with beaten egg and fried; or with slices of cooked kidneys rolled in grated bread, seasoned with salt and pepper powder and fried in lard
