## Ingredients

- One rabbit
- Rabbit, beef, or lamb caul
- Beef lard or udder
- Onions
- Salt
- Fine spices
- Shallots
- Nutmeg, grated
- Dried basil powder
- Clean intestines
- Beef broth
- Two quartillos of white wine
- Bundle of fine herbs

## Directions

1. Completely bone a rabbit of good quality and cut it into strips with a piece of rabbit, beef, or lamb caul, with lard or beef udder
2. Cut the onions the same way and leave everything on the fire until half cooked
3. Season then with salt, fine spices, chopped shallots, grated nutmeg and basil powder
4. Put this mixture into clean intestines and form the sausages as described in previous articles
5. Cook in beef broth with two quartillos of white wine and a bundle of fine herbs
6. Allow to cool in the same cooking liquid
7. Coat with the fat from the cooking liquid, cover with breadcrumbs, grill on a griddle and serve as a supplementary dish
