## Ingredients

- Twelve ounces of soaked and finely ground almonds
- Two pounds of sifted sugar
- Carmine dye
- Lemon juice
- Ground cinnamon
- Ground clove
- Sugar for dusting

## Directions

1. Mix the almonds with the sifted sugar so that the almonds are perfectly incorporated.
2. Divide the paste into three equal portions: leave one in its natural color, tint the second with carmine dissolved in a little lemon juice, and the third with ground cinnamon, adding very little ground clove to enhance the flavor.
3. Extend each paste separately with a rolling pin on a sugar-dusted table.
4. Stack the pastes one on top of the other so that the carmine paste is in the middle.
5. Shape them to resemble a whole ham, round, or in pieces of whatever form desired.
