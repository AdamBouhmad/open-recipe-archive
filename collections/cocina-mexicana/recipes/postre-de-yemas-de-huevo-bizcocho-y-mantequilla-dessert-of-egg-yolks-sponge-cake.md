## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- egg yolks
- sponge cake
- butter
- cinnamon
- sesame seeds, toasted

## Directions

1. Cook milk with sugar
2. Beat egg yolks and mix with the cooked milk
3. Take a cold slab of the best sponge cake and cut it into slices
4. In a dish, lay down a layer of slices and on top of each slice place layers of butter, adding cinnamon and toasted sesame seeds to each one, until filling the platter
5. Once well layered high, pour the milk with egg yolks over it until well soaked
6. Place fire above and below, both not too intense, and leave until it sets like a cake
7. Decorate with toasted sesame seeds and ground cinnamon on top
