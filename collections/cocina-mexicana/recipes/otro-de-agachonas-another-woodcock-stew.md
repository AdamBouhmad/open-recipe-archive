## Ingredients

- Woodcock
- Butter
- Innards, gizzards and livers of the woodcock
- Ham in pieces
- Onion, finely chopped
- Garlic, chopped
- Red wine
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Thyme

## Directions

1. Pluck and open the woodcock and save the innards, gizzards and livers.
2. Put butter in a casserole and place the woodcock with pieces of ham, plenty of finely chopped onion, a little chopped garlic.
3. The innards, gizzards and liver, after being washed and ground are fried and placed in the casserole with a little red wine, clove, cinnamon, pepper and a little thyme.
