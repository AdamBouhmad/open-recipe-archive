## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar
- six well-beaten egg whites
- maraschino liqueur (optional)

## Directions

1. Make a syrup with half a pound of sugar at the blown stage.
2. Add six well-beaten egg whites to it, mixing with a wooden spoon.
3. Add desired flavoring or, if making with maraschino, add half a glass to the mixture.
4. Proceed as for ordinary meringues, with the only difference being to make them smaller, placing them in the oven on a sheet covered with paper.
