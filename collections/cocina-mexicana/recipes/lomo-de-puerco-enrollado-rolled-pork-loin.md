## Ingredients

- pork loin
- onion
- parsley
- ham slices
- ground pepper
- bread crumbs
- lard
- broth
- tomato
- garlic
- cumin
- clove

## Directions

1. Open the loin down the middle.
2. Chop onion, parsley and some ham slices with ground pepper and a little bread crumbs.
3. Once all is mixed, roll the loin three times and sew so the filling does not come out.
4. Cook and when done, coat well with lard and fry.
5. Place in a pot and reheat with a sauce made from broth, onion, tomato and garlic; fry this well and add to the pot.
6. Add cumin and clove, thickening with bread crumbs.
7. Garnish with chiles, olives and capers.
