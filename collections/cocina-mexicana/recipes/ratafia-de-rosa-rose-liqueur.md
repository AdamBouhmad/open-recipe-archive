## Ingredients

- Twenty-six white or colored roses or four ounces of their petals
- Hot water
- Aguardiente
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Put in infusion twenty-six white or colored roses or four ounces of their petals in two quartillos of hot water and leave for two days in this state
2. After this pass the infusion through cloth squeezing it and mix with as much aguardiente as there is of the decoction
3. Add half a pound of clarified syrup for each four quartillos of liquor
4. Pour it into a bottle, cover and let rest for twelve or fifteen days, after which filter through a cloth so it clarifies
