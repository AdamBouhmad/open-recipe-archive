## Ingredients

- Sugar
- Milk
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Nutmeg
- Nuts
- Almonds
- Hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Make a syrup of medium consistency with white sugar and clarify it well
2. Mix three quarters of this syrup with the remainder of ten quarters of milk that have been boiled separately
3. Heat the mixture until it reaches the consistency of cajeta
4. When off the heat, add powder of cinnamon, clove and nutmeg according to taste
5. Add small pieces of walnuts, almonds and hazelnuts
