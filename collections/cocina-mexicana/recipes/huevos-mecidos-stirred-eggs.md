## Ingredients

- sugar
- grated coconut
- camote (sweet potato)
- almonds
- raisins
- cream
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make syrup with six pounds of sugar
2. Mix in one pound of grated coconut, one pound of camote and one pound of almonds, all ground
3. When this reaches medium consistency, remove from heat and add ground raisins dissolved in the syrup
4. Return to fire, and when the paste begins to rise in consistency, add a quartillo of cream
5. After boiling with it, remove from fire and let cool
6. Add sixteen well-beaten egg yolks and return to heat to reach the proper consistency, which should be that of cajeta
7. If it is to serve as ante, give it a lower consistency and pour over slices of mamones moistened in white wine
