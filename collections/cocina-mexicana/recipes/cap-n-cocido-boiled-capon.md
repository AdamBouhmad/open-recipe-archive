## Ingredients

- capon
- lemon juice
- bacon slices
- onions
- garlic
- chopped bacon
- broth

## Directions

1. After preparing, sprinkle with lemon juice
2. Cover with bacon slices and cook over low heat with onions, garlic, chopped bacon, and the capon's own neck
3. Moisten with broth and serve
