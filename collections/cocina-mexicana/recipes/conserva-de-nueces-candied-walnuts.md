## Ingredients

- Walnuts, gathered at the end of May
- Honey, five quartillos for every one hundred walnuts

## Directions

1. Gather walnuts at the end of May
2. Peel away the thin outer shells
3. Pierce them through completely with a basting needle, making five punctures in each walnut
4. Keep them in water for nine days, changing the water daily
5. After this period, place them in boiling water until they become tender, which will happen in two or three boilings
6. Remove, drain, and allow to cool
7. Place over gentle heat with five quartillos of honey for every one hundred walnuts, and allow to reach the proper point
