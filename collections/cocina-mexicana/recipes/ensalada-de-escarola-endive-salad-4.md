## Ingredients

- endive
- oil
- vinegar
- salt
- garlic (optional)
- pepper (optional)
- bread crumb (optional)
- raw tomatoes (optional)
- onion (optional)

## Directions

1. Clean the endive
2. Dress with oil, vinegar, and salt
3. Optionally add crushed garlic mixed into the vinegar
4. Optionally add pepper and bread crumb to thicken
5. Optionally add raw chopped tomatoes with a little onion, salt, and oil
