## Ingredients

- White beans
- Broth
- Carrots
- Onions
- Cloves
- Salt
- Butter
- Bread toasts

## Directions

1. Make it the same as lentil essence soup
2. After seasoning with salt, add a little butter, stirring much
3. Once seasoned, place over bread toasts
