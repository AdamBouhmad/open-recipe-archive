## Ingredients

- six pounds of fine flour
- six ounces of butter
- five ounces of sugar
- eggs as the dough will take
- six egg whites for the frosting
- powdered sugar
- half a lime or sour orange
- orange blossom water
- candied fruit granules

## Directions

1. Mix six pounds of fine flour, six ounces of butter, five ounces of sugar, and as many eggs as the dough will take, setting aside six egg whites for the frosting
2. Knead everything
3. Form rosquettes and drop them into a pot of boiling water
4. Leave them there until they rise from the bottom and float on the surface
5. Remove and let cool while heating the oven
6. Once the oven is warm, place the rosquettes inside and remove as soon as they bake
7. Spread the frosting made from the six egg whites, powdered sugar, half a lime or sour orange, and a little orange blossom water
8. Dust with candied fruit granules and return to the oven to dry
9. They are also good without frosting
