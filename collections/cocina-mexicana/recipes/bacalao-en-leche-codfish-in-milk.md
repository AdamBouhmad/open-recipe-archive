## Ingredients

- Well desalted codfish
- Bread
- Oil
- Butter
- Milk
- Salt

## Directions

1. After thoroughly desalting, cook and debone.
2. Fry some slices of bread in oil, grind them and dissolve in melted butter.
3. Bathe the codfish in this mixture, put it in milk seasoned with necessary salt, and cook until the broth thickens sufficiently.
4. Remove from heat and serve.
