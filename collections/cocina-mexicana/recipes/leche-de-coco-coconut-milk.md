## Ingredients

- coconut
- milk
- sugar
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Peel the coconut of its inner shell and grind it by sprinkling with milk so that the mass can be strained like atole, which should be done immediately
2. Regrind the strainings until no grain remains and sweeten the strained liquid, using plenty of sugar
3. Strain again through cloth and pour into a pan, which is placed on the fire for the milk to boil, taking care to stir so it does not stick
4. When it reaches the point where upon stirring the bottom of the pan is visible, like for cajetas, remove from the fire and after resting a few moments, quickly mix in the egg yolks necessary to give it color, which should be somewhat bright
5. Return to the fire to bring it to a slightly higher point, and after cooling, pour into the platter in which it is to be served, adorned with cinnamon powder
