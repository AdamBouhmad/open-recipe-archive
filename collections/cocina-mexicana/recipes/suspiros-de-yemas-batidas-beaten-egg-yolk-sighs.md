## Ingredients

- Egg yolks
- Sifted sugar, as much as needed

## Directions

1. Beat the egg yolks until they become stiff
2. As you beat them, gradually mix in sifted sugar in the amount needed so the paste becomes consistent
3. The mixture should be such that it can be dropped with a spoon onto sifted sugar without running
4. Place the sighs in a gentle oven and let them cook and brown
