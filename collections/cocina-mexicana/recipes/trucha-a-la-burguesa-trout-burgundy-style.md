## Ingredients

- trout
- one bottle white wine
- three onions
- mixed herb bundle
- clove
- two heads of garlic
- bay leaf
- thyme
- basil
- butter
- flour
- parsley

## Directions

1. Let the trout salt for one hour
2. Put it to cook with one bottle of white wine, three onions, a mixed herb bundle, clove, two heads of garlic, bay leaf, thyme, basil and butter kneaded with flour
3. Bring to a boil over high heat
4. Remove the onions and mixed herb bundle
5. Serve with its sauce; a quarter hour is sufficient for its complete cooking
6. When serving, sprinkle with chopped parsley on top
