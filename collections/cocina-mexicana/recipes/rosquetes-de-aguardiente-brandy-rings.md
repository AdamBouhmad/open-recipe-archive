## Ingredients

- Three pounds of cake flour
- Three egg whites
- Thirty egg yolks
- Four ounces of butter
- Four ounces of ground sugar
- One small glass of Spanish brandy

## Directions

1. Beat the egg whites until very thick and stiff
2. Beat the thirty egg yolks together
3. Mix the egg whites and yolks with as much sugar as they will hold
4. Add half a lime or sour orange and a little orange blossom water
5. Knead everything until it crackles
6. Form small rosquetes and arrange them on buttered paper or tin sheets
7. Bake in the oven
8. When cooked, dust with pearl sugar and return to the oven to dry
9. They can also be made without icing
