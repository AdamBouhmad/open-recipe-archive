## Ingredients

- Three eggs
- A little salt
- One quartillo of milk
- Sifted flour
- Lard

## Directions

1. Beat three eggs in a bowl with a little salt
2. Add one quartillo of milk and mix well
3. Add sifted flour, beating everything by hand until the dough is formed like common fritters
4. Make the fritters and fry them in lard with moderate heat
