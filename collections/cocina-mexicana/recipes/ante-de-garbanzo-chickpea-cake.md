## Ingredients

- chickpeas
- pine nuts
- syrup
- egg yolks
- ground almonds

## Directions

1. Cook the chickpeas and remove their skins
2. Grind them together with pine nuts
3. Sift the pine nuts and chickpeas through a sieve
4. Add them to well-clarified syrup
5. Add some egg yolks and ground almonds
6. Cook until it takes body
