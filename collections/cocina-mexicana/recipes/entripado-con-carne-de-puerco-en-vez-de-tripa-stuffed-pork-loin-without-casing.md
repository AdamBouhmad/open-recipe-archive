## Ingredients

- pork loin meat, cooked and chopped
- salt
- beaten eggs
- fried chorizos
- chopped ham
- raisins
- almonds, in small pieces
- ground spices

## Directions

1. Cook and chop the pork loin meat, add salt and beaten eggs
2. Extend this mass and place fried chorizos, chopped ham, raisins, and small pieces of almonds and ground spices on it
3. Wrap the meat, forming a pillow shape, and tie it so it does not fall apart when cooking
4. Cook in water with a little salt
5. To serve, cut into slices that are fried or cooked in xitomate broth
