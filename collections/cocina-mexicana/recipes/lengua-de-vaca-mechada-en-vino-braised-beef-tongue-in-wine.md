## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- lard
- fried bread
- garlic
- wine
- cloves
- ground pepper
- small cooked onions

## Directions

1. Lard the tongue as described in the previous article
2. Parboil it in lard
3. Add fried and ground bread with garlic
4. When the mixture is fried, add wine, cloves, and ground pepper with small cooked onions
5. Let the sauce reduce until thicker than in the preceding article
