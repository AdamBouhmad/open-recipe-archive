## Ingredients

- jicama
- carrots
- syrup at medium point
- sponge cake (mamón)
- paste
- pastille
- sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Slice very thinly
2. Mix with chopped carrots
3. Add to syrup at medium point
4. Keep with aromatics exposed to air
5. Grind everything
6. Layer sponge cake and paste
7. Top each layer with pastille and sesame seeds
