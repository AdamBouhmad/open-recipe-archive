## Ingredients

- Fried garlic
- Capers
- Parsley
- Lard
- Salt
- Chicken broth
- Vinegar
- Fried flour
- Clove
- Pepper
- Oil
- Chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Grind together fried garlic, capers, and parsley
2. Fry the ground mixture in lard with salt
3. Put in the chicken with its broth
4. Add vinegar, fried flour, ground clove, and pepper
5. Let everything simmer until completely seasoned
6. When removing from the heat, add oil, chiles, and olives
