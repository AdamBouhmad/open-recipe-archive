## Ingredients

- milk
- cream
- panochitas (brown sugar loaves)
- obleas (wafers)

## Directions

1. Mix one quarter of milk with half a quarter of cream
2. use two one-pound panochitas in place of sugar for this quantity
3. pour into white wafers, folding them like quesadillas
