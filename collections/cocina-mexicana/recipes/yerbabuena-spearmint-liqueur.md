## Ingredients

- 2 handfuls of freshly picked and cultivated spearmint
- 12 cuartillos of aguardiente
- 2 cuartillos of water
- 3 and a half pounds of sugar
- 7 cuartillos of water for the syrup

## Directions

1. Place the spearmint in infusion for several days in the aguardiente and water
2. Distill in the common manner
3. Sweeten with sugar dissolved in water
4. Filter through a cloth (manga)
5. If an oily liqueur is desired, sweeten with syrup cooked over fire
