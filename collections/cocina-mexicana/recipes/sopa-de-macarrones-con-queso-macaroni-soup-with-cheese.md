## Ingredients

- macaroni
- broth
- cheese (Flemish and Gruyère)
- braise broth (optional, but better)

## Directions

1. Add the macaroni to the boiling broth
2. Stir and skim continuously
3. Let them cook for a quarter hour
4. Then remove and let them boil slowly over low heat until well cooked
5. Before serving, sprinkle grated Flemish and Gruyère cheese on top
6. The soup should be thick
7. Grated cheese can also be served on a separate plate, which is preferable
8. Macaroni is very tasty cooked with braised broth
