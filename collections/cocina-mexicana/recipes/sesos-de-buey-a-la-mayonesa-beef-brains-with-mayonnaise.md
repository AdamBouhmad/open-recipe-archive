## Ingredients

- beef brains
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- white wine
- mayonnaise
- toast
- gelatin

## Directions

1. Blanch in boiling water with vinegar and salt four portions of brains that have been cleaned and soaked in cold water.
2. Refresh and cook in a marinade with white wine.
3. When the brains are cooked, allow them to cool, cut them in half, giving all the same shape.
4. After arranging them in a crown on the plate, coat with green or white mayonnaise.
5. Place two good slices of gelatin toast on the edge of the plate.
