## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- thirty egg yolks
- one pound of almond
- half a quartillo of virgin honey
- twelve hard biscuits
- half an ounce of cloves
- half an ounce of cinnamon, all ground

## Directions

1. Mix all the ingredients together
2. Cook to the point where the mixture pulls away from the pan
3. Let cool
4. Store until the next day
5. Shape into small sticks in the form of dates
6. Roll in cinnamon
