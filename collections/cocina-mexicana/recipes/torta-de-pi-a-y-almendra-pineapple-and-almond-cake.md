## Ingredients

- one pound of cooked and ground pineapple
- one pound of ground sugar
- two ounces of almonds
- two egg yolks
- two pounds of sugar
- sponge cake
- eggs

## Directions

1. Mix one pound of cooked and ground pineapple with one pound of ground sugar
2. Make another paste by grinding two ounces of almonds with two egg yolks, and mix with two pounds of sugar
3. Layer in the following order: one layer of sponge cake, one of pineapple, one of almonds, and the last of eggs
4. It turns out better if the first layer is also of eggs
