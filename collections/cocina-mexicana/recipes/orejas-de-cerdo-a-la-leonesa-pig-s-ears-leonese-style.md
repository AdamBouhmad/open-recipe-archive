## Ingredients

- pig's ears
- onions
- lard
- flour
- broth
- vinegar
- lemon
- bread

## Directions

1. In a sauce of onions cut into pieces and passed through lard, place the ears cut into strips
2. Add a little flour and moisten with broth
3. Reduce
4. When serving, add a few drops of vinegar or lemon juice
5. If desired, serve with sliced fried bread
