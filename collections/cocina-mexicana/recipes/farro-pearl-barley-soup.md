## Ingredients

- barley or spelt or oats
- meat broth or vegetarian broth
- almonds

## Directions

1. After half-grinding any of these grains and removing the hulls, wash three times in warm water
2. Place to cook with meat broth or vegetarian broth as desired
3. When half-cooked, add some almonds
