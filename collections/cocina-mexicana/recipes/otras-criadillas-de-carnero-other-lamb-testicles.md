## Ingredients

- lamb testicles
- peas
- onion
- tomato
- parsley
- lard
- chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Cook the testicles with peas
2. Mince onion and tomato with a little parsley
3. Fry in a cazuela with lard
4. Add the peas, and before they finish cooking add the testicles
5. The broth should remain sparse, garnish with chiles, olives and capers
