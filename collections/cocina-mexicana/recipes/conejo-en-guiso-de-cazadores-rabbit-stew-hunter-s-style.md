## Ingredients

- butter (one quarter pound)
- flour (two heaping tablespoons)
- rabbit
- white wine (one and a half bottles)
- mushrooms
- lean ham
- herb bouquet
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Put butter and flour in a pot, letting the flour brown
2. Add the cleaned, cleaned of entrails, and regularly cut rabbit portions to macerate or render in the frying
3. Moisten with one and a half bottles of white wine
4. Add mushrooms and lean ham that have been partially cooked in another vessel, and a mixed herb bouquet
5. Let the stew season over lively heat until it has reduced somewhat
6. Add salt and pepper
7. Remove the fat from the broth and ensure it is neither too thick nor too watery
8. Taste to verify it has sufficient salt
9. Remove the herb bouquet and serve
