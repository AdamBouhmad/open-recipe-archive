## Ingredients

- red bean flowers
- salt
- aged cheese
- eggs
- lard
- garlic
- onion
- tomato
- water
- pepper
- clove
- comino
- saffron
- green chiles
- camote (sweet potato)
- apples
- plantain

## Directions

1. Remove the center membrane from the bean flowers and cook them with salt, ensuring the flowers are tender.
2. When well cooked, remove from the water they were cooked in and rinse in fresh water.
3. Remove from water and drain.
4. Chop them the same as meat.
5. Grind a little aged cheese and mix with the chopped flowers.
6. Mix both with beaten eggs as if for frying.
7. Heat a little lard in a pan, pour in the egg, and cook over two fires until well set.
8. Remove from heat and put another pan with lard over the fire.
9. In it, fry chopped garlic and onion in quarters.
10. Then fry raw ground tomato and add a little water.
11. Add necessary salt, pepper, cloves, comino, and saffron, all well ground.
12. Put the pieces of cake in this broth, adding whole green chiles and slices of sweet potato, apples, and plantain.
13. When everything is well seasoned, remove from heat and serve.
