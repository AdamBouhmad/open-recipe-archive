## Ingredients

- Gangas (plucked, cleaned, and eviscerated)
- Salt
- Water
- Giblets from the gangas
- Heads of the gangas
- Pan toasted in lard
- Bread, toasted in lard
- Cloves, ground
- Black pepper, ground
- Cinnamon, ground

## Directions

1. Cook plucked and cleaned gangas in salted water
2. Grind all the giblets together with the heads and bread toasted in lard
3. Fry all the ground mixture
4. Place the gangas in the same pan
5. Add the cooking broth with ground cloves, pepper, and cinnamon
6. Let everything boil until the sauce becomes moderately thick
7. Remove and serve
