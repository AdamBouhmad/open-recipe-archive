## Ingredients

- Rice
- Water with salt
- Cinnamon sticks
- Sugar

## Directions

1. After washing the rice in several waters, put it to cook in water with salt.
2. Add some cinnamon sticks, and when it is half cooked, sweeten it with sugar.
3. This rice broth is not served thick.
