## Ingredients

- Hare loin and rear section
- hare liver
- butter
- shallots
- white wine
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- vinegar
- hare blood

## Directions

1. Usually the loin with the rear part of the hare is what is roasted, because the front part is preferable in civet.
2. It is necessary to lard the fleshy parts and leave the piece in the spit for at most one hour.
3. For the sauce, crush the liver, which is rendered or lightly fried for just an instant in butter with finely chopped shallots.
4. Moisten with white wine and broth, adding salt, pepper, a splash of vinegar and the hare's blood, which will have been set aside.
5. It is also served with a clear and piquant sauce, composed of roasting juice or substance with vinegar and fried shallots.
