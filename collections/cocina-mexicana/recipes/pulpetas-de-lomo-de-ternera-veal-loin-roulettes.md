## Ingredients

- veal loin meat
- vinegar
- pepper
- salt
- garlic
- bay leaves

## Directions

1. Prepare the loin roulettes, sprinkle with vinegar, dust with ground pepper and salt, and coat with ground garlic and powdered bay leaves.
