## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Thin lard strips
- Toasted ancho chiles, deveined and soaked
- Garlic
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Lard
- Water
- Marjoram sprigs
- Oil
- Olives
- Large capers
- Cured small chiles
- Lettuce leaves

## Directions

1. After cleaning the chicken, lard with thin strips of lard
2. Place in a casserole
3. Add toasted, deveined, soaked, and ground ancho chiles mixed with garlic, clove, pepper, and cinnamon
4. Add salt and vinegar
5. Let rest closed overnight
6. The next day, add lard, water, and marjoram sprigs
7. Place to cook over two fires, being careful not to touch them, until well cooked and fried and roasted in their own adobo
8. Arrange on a plate over lettuce leaves, drizzle with oil, and serve adorned with olives, large capers, and cured small chiles
