## Ingredients

- Pineapple
- Tequesquite
- Sweet potato
- Jícama
- Clarified syrup
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Peel the pineapple, cut into pieces and cook with tequesquite
2. Leave in cold water overnight, and the next day put to boil with clarified syrup
3. Do the same with the sweet potato and jícama separately
4. Afterwards, unite all three together, adding a little orange blossom water so they give one boil together
