## Ingredients

- tuna
- eels
- anchovies
- marinade in lean meat
- onion slices
- lemon
- small round onions
- pepper
- salt
- bay leaf
- one pound of butter
- red sauce

## Directions

1. Stuff the tuna with a good slice of eels and anchovies
2. Place on the spit
3. Roll it as it cooks with marinade prepared in the pan with onion slices and lemon, small round onions, pepper, salt, bay leaf, and a pound of butter
4. Immediately remove the fat from this marinade or sauce
5. Mix with the great red sauce
6. Pour over the tuna
