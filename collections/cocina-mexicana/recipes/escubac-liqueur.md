## Ingredients

- One jug of refined brandy
- Four cuartillos of water
- Six cuartillos of refined brandy
- One ounce of saffron
- Peels from four lemons, cleaned of flesh
- Two ounces of cilantro
- Two drams of cinnamon
- One dram of mace
- Twelve cloves
- One ounce of bitter almonds
- Four pounds of sugar
- Twelve cuartillos of water

## Directions

1. Dilute one jug of refined brandy with four cuartillos of water
2. In six cuartillos of refined brandy, infuse one ounce of saffron to extract the tincture
3. Pour the diluted brandy into a large bottle with the cleaned lemon peels, cilantro, cinnamon, mace, cloves, and bitter almonds, all ground
4. Leave in infusion for eight days
5. After this time, add the saffron and its tincture (passed through a sieve), saving some for coloring the liqueur
6. Distill in a bain-marie in the ordinary manner
7. Dissolve four pounds of sugar in twelve cuartillos of water, clarifying the syrup perfectly
8. Sweeten the distilled product with this syrup, mix with the saffron tincture, and filter through a cloth
