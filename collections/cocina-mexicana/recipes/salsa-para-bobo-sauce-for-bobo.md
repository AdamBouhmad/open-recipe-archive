## Ingredients

- onion
- garlic
- parsley
- oregano
- egg yolks
- fried bread
- clove
- pepper
- cumin
- broth from the bobo
- oil
- capers
- olives
- chiles

## Directions

1. Mince onion, garlic, parsley, and oregano and fry them together
2. Mix in some egg yolks and a piece of fried bread
3. Add clove, pepper, and cumin
4. Add a little broth from the bobo, oil, and capers
5. When serving at the table, garnish with olives and chiles
