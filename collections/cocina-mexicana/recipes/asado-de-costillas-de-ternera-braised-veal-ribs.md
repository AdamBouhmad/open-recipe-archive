## Ingredients

- Veal ribs
- Lard
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion ends
- Water
- Potatoes

## Directions

1. Fry the ribs in a pot with plenty of lard, salt, pepper, and onion ends.
2. Let them fry until they take on the color of a roast.
3. Add sufficient water for the meat to cook.
4. Cover the pot and place over gentle heat.
5. When the meat is almost cooked, add potatoes to cook with it.
6. The sauce should be very reduced when served.
