## Ingredients

- 2 onions
- 8 carrots
- 2 turnips
- butter or lard
- broth

## Directions

1. Peel, wash, and cut two onions, eight carrots, and two turnips.
2. Place in boiling water for a quarter hour over low heat.
3. Refresh and drain.
4. Sauté with butter or lard, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking to the bottom of the pot.
5. When soft, moisten with broth and simmer gently at the edge of the stove.
6. Once cooked, pass through a sieve, heat, and serve.
