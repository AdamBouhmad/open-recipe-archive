## Ingredients

- One quartillo of common broth
- Half quartillo of white wine
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lemon peel
- Two bay leaves
- A little vinegar

## Directions

1. Mix in a quartillo of common broth half of white wine, seasoning with salt, pepper, lemon peel, two bay leaves and a little vinegar
2. Leave in infusion over continuous low heat for ten or twelve hours
3. Pass through a sieve so that it serves for all kinds of birds, vegetables and fish
4. It has the advantage of being able to be preserved without alteration for many days
