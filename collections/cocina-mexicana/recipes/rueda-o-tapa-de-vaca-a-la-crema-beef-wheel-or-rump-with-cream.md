## Ingredients

- Beef pulp or wheel
- Ham
- Salt
- Spices
- Parsley
- Small onions
- Mushrooms
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth
- White wine
- Egg yolks
- Cream

## Directions

1. Cut the pulp or wheel into pieces the size of an egg
2. Lard each piece by running through it a thick strip of ham
3. Season with salt, spices, parsley, minced small onions and mushrooms
4. Put in a saucepan with a little butter and parboil over fire
5. Add a little flour mixed with broth and white wine
6. Let the beef cook until done and the broth is very thick
7. Add a liaison of three egg yolks mixed in cream
8. Let everything incorporate over the fire
