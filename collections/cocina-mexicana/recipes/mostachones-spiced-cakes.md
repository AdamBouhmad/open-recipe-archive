## Ingredients

- one pound of ground and sifted sugar
- ten eggs, five with yolk
- thirty or more cloves for eating, according to taste, very finely ground
- one pound and four ounces of flour
- cinnamon powder, which can be omitted if desired

## Directions

1. Put the sugar in a pot and add the ten eggs
2. Beat, and when they are incorporated add the finely ground cloves
3. Add the flour and beat again, adding cinnamon powder
4. Form the mostachones on paper and put them immediately in a temperate oven
