## Ingredients

- one cuartillo of milk
- sugar
- one stick of cinnamon
- salt
- half a loaf of bread, crumbled
- egg yolk
- some whole raisins
- toasted almonds
- lemon zest
- one stick of cinnamon
- two cloves
- six beaten eggs
- two tablespoons of butter
- half a glass of generous wine

## Directions

1. Cook one cuartillo of milk with necessary sugar, a stick of cinnamon and a little salt
2. Add half a loaf of bread, crumbled
3. Add egg yolk, some whole raisins, toasted almonds, lemon zest, one stick of cinnamon, and two cloves
4. Add six beaten eggs, two tablespoons of butter and half a glass of generous wine
5. Mix everything very well
6. Coat the mold with butter
7. Pour the mixture inside and place it over two gentle fires
8. When cooked, empty it onto a platter
9. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar powder on top and decorate with candied almonds
