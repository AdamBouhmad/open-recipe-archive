## Ingredients

- 1 coconut, well ground
- 1 handful of almonds, ground
- sugar to taste
- egg yolks (optional, for yellow color)
- small piece of sponge cake (optional, for rose color)

## Directions

1. Mix one coconut, well ground, with a handful of ground almonds and sugar to taste
2. Put everything over the fire until it reaches the point of manjar blanco
3. If you wish it yellow in color, add egg yolks; or a small piece of sponge cake if it is to have a rose color
4. Empty into a platter
