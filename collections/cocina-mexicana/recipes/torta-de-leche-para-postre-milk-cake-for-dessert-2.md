## Ingredients

- milk
- eggs
- sugar
- cinnamon
- butter

## Directions

1. Curdle the milk as for cheese, press it, pass through a metate
2. Add the egg yolks sufficient to moisten the dough, with corresponding sugar and a little cinnamon
3. Have a pan buttered in which to place said dough
