## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of milk
- twelve egg yolks
- sugar to taste
- a little orange blossom water
- a handful of ground almonds
- sponge cake moistened with milk
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- cinnamon, whole or ground

## Directions

1. Add twelve egg yolks to two cuartillos of milk with sugar to taste, orange blossom water, and a handful of ground almonds
2. After mixing, cook until it has the consistency of atole
3. Pour into a platter over layers of sponge cake moistened with milk
4. Decorate the top with raisins, almonds, pine nuts, and cinnamon, whole or ground
5. Serve as the previous recipe
