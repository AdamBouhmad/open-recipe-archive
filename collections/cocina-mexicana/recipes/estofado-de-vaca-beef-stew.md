## Ingredients

- beef, cut into regular pieces
- two or three onions
- one clove
- one head of garlic, unpeeled
- some bay leaves and oregano
- one glass of wine
- parsley
- one small cup of oil per pound of meat

## Directions

1. Place the meat cut into regular pieces in a pot with two or three onions and one clove, one head of garlic unpeeled, some bay leaves and oregano, one glass of wine, parsley, and one small cup of oil per pound of meat.
2. Brown everything over low heat and stir frequently.
3. Place a piece of brown paper in the mouth of the pot and a cup of water on top.
