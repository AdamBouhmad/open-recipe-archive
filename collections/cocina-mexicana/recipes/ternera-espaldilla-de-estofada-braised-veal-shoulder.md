## Ingredients

- veal shoulder
- fat ham, sliced
- salt
- pepper
- lemon slices
- carrot pieces
- onion heads
- fine herb sprigs
- meat broth
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. Cover the bottom of a casserole or pan with slices of fat ham.
2. Bone the veal shoulder and place over the ham slices.
3. Add competent salt and pepper, then place lemon slices on top with pieces of carrot, onion heads, and sprigs of fine herbs.
4. Add sufficient meat broth with a little good vinegar.
5. Cook over two gentle heats.
6. When cooked, remove and strain the broth, then degrease when cool.
7. Return the meat alone to two heats, adding the broth and taking care to turn it from one side to the other.
8. When the broth has been consumed, remove from heat and serve.
