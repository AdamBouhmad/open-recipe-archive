## Ingredients

- One pound sugar
- One quarter litre water
- Half pound fresh butter
- Mamones, well crumbled
- Wine
- Cinnamon
- One pound flour
- One piece of butter, melted
- Salt
- Fresh butter for greasing

## Directions

1. Dissolve one pound of sugar in one quarter litre of water
2. Heat and when the syrup reaches the proper point, add half a pound of fresh butter
3. Add well crumbled mamones, a little wine and a little cinnamon
4. For the dough, add to one pound of flour one piece of melted butter and salt, mixing well and kneading until very smooth
5. Cover the bottom of a plate greased with fresh butter with this dough
6. Place the aforementioned paste on top
7. Cover with the dough
8. Bake over two fires
9. When cooked, remove and serve cold
