## Ingredients

- six cuartillos of milk
- egg yolks
- ground rice
- orange juice
- cinnamon sticks

## Directions

1. Make as the custard apple milk with egg, being careful not to add the orange juice until the mixture reaches the proper point to prevent it from curdling
