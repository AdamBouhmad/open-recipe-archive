## Ingredients

- Almonds
- Hot water
- Egg whites, beaten
- Sugar
- Sifted sugar powder

## Directions

1. Soak the almonds in hot water so that they can be peeled well
2. After peeling, place them in cold water, washing them several times
3. Dry them well in a cloth and grind them, lowering them from the metate with beaten egg whites to prevent them from forming oil
4. Once ground, have ready well-clarified syrup that is at the sheet point
5. For each pound of sugar that has been clarified, add one pound of ground almond, mix very well
6. When well incorporated, place on the fire, stirring constantly so it does not stick
7. When shaking the spoon quickly the dough does not stick, it is ready; remove from fire and empty it onto a plate sprinkled with sifted sugar until it has cooled well
8. Form pieces from it and put them in a tube or syringe that has a hole at the bottom in the shape of a star, or as desired, so that when the dough is pressed it comes out and the rosquillas are cut to the desired size by joining the two points together
9. After the rosquillas are formed, place them on papers that will be put in the oven at gentle heat, making sure they do not brown too much, and remove them from the oven as soon as they come off the paper
10. If you want to give them another flavor, you can add to the almond dough, before mixing it with the flour, juice or peel of orange, lemon or citron
