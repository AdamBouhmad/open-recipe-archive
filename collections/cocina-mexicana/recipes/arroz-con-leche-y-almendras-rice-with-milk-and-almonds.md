## Ingredients

- almendras limpias (clean almonds)
- arroz lavado y cocido (washed and cooked rice)
- leche (milk)
- azúcar (sugar)
- canela (cinnamon)
- grajea molida (ground dragée)

## Directions

1. Grind clean almonds and washed and cooked rice in equal parts
2. Add them gradually to milk
3. Strain, regrind the remaining strainings and incorporate everything
4. Sweeten according to taste and put to boil in a pot, stirring continuously until it reaches the consistency of manjar blanco
5. Empty into the dishes in which it is to be served and sprinkle sugar, cinnamon, and ground dragée on top
