## Ingredients

- Beef palate
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Olive oil

## Directions

1. Place salt, vinegar, and pepper on a plate
2. Beat everything together
3. Add finely chopped fine herbs, garlic, and parsley
4. Add three spoonfuls of oil
5. Cook the palate pieces in thin slices in this mixture
