## Ingredients

- Cooked beef pulp
- Butter
- Breadcrumbs
- Fine herbs, finely chopped
- Salt
- Capers
- Tender squash, pickled

## Directions

1. Spread butter in a platter with breadcrumbs and finely chopped fine herbs
2. Make very thin slices of cooked beef pulp with salt
3. Place one layer of meat slices on top of the platter
4. Then place another layer of herbs, pieces of butter and breadcrumbs
5. Place the platter on a grill over very gentle fire
6. Place a comal on top with also very gentle fire
7. Keep the platter until the meat develops a crust
8. Serve at the table adorned with capers and tender pickled squash
