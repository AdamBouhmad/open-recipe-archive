## Ingredients

- beef abomasum
- onion slices
- garlic
- clove
- butter
- flour
- broth
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Scrape and clean the abomasum pieces carefully and wash them in several boiling waters
2. Soak them in cold water
3. Cook them in water with onion slices, garlic and clove
4. Fry them in butter with a little flour
5. Add a little broth and thicken the sauce with egg yolks
