## Ingredients

- artichokes
- bread crumbs
- ground almonds
- salt
- coarse pepper powder
- garlic, minced
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook artichokes as directed, and remove the fuzzy center.
2. Fill them with a mixture composed of bread crumbs, ground almonds, salt, and coarse pepper powder.
3. Cover the filling with the small leaves that had been removed to remove the fuzz.
4. Place a tortera with oil over gentle heat, fry minced garlic in it. When it has browned, arrange the artichokes there, pouring oil through the center, and cover them with a comal that has little heat.
5. When they are fried, sprinkle a little bread crumbs over them, and with the broth consumed, serve with salt and coarse pepper powder.
