## Ingredients

- spinach
- oil
- garlic
- shrimp, cleaned

## Directions

1. Prepare the spinach the same way as the Mexican-style spinach, with the sole difference that the tomato is omitted
2. Fry in oil and when cooking, add cleaned shrimp
