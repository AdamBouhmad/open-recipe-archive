## Ingredients

- butter
- toasted and ground cilantro
- roasted garlic cloves
- chicken broth
- chickens
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- saffron
- salt
- cooked onions
- toasted sesame
- good vinegar

## Directions

1. Heat a casserole with butter and fry a handful of toasted and ground cilantro for each chicken.
2. Grind roasted garlic cloves and dissolve in a little of the broth in which the chickens were cooked.
3. Pour this broth over the fried cilantro and add the chickens in quarters, well cooked.
4. Grind clove, pepper, cinnamon, and saffron, and season with these and corresponding salt.
5. Fry cooked onions separately and add to the casserole with a little whole toasted sesame.
6. After a few boils, add a little good vinegar, adjust seasoning, remove from heat, and let rest.
