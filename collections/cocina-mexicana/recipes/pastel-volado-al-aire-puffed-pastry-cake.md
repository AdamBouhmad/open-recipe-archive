## Ingredients

- Folded pastry dough for base
- Puff pastry dough with five or six folds or turns
- Water

## Directions

1. Form a base or foundation of folded pastry dough of regular thickness and appropriate size
2. Form another sheet of puff pastry dough that has been given five or six folds or turns
3. Moisten the base of the folded dough
4. Place the puff pastry sheet on top
5. Roll with the rolling pin so it sits well
6. Trim to give it a pleasing form, either square, round, or oval
7. Using a mold of attractive figure and eight or ten lines smaller than the dough, cut the dough by pressing the mold so that only the puff pastry on top is cut, leaving the base of folded pastry intact with the circle cut by the mold remaining in place
8. Bake in a hot oven or place over two fires
9. When the pastry is cooked, remove it carefully and lift off the figure marked by the mold
10. Fill the hollow with filling and cover with the top piece that was removed
