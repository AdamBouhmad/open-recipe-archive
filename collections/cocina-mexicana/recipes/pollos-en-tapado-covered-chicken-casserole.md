## Ingredients

- manteca
- sal
- xitomate rebanado
- cebolla rebanada
- ajos picados
- chile verde en tiras
- calabacitas tiernas sancochadas
- pollos cocidos deshuesados despedazados
- jamón en lonjitas
- salchicha
- salchichón
- chorizo
- mollejas
- hígados
- huevo cocido
- pimienta
- clavo
- canela en polvo
- pasas
- almendras
- aceitunas
- tornachiles curados
- peregil deshojado
- aceite
- caldo

## Directions

1. Sauté sliced tomato, sliced onion, chopped garlic and strips of green chile in butter with salt
2. Grease a cazuela with butter and make a layer of this mixture, then a layer of tender sautéed squash slices, then a layer of cooked, boned and shredded chicken mixed with strips of ham, sausage, salchichón, chorizo, sweetbreads, livers and cooked egg, all in small pieces
3. Season with salt, pepper, clove and ground cinnamon, add raisins, almonds, olives, quarters of cured chiles, parsley and plenty of oil
4. Cover with another layer of the mixture half-fried, another of squash, and alternate the remaining ingredients until the cazuela is full, with the mixture on top
5. Carefully pour the broth in which the chickens were cooked so the liquid sinks in, then brush the surface with oil
6. Place the cazuela over two fires, moving it from time to time with both hands so it does not burn, and let simmer until the sauce is entirely consumed
