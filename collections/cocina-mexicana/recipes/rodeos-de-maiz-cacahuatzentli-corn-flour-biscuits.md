## Ingredients

- Six pounds and four ounces of this corn made into flour and passed through a sieve
- Twenty egg yolks and four egg whites
- Two pounds of sugar
- Two and one half pounds of lard

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients
2. Do the same as in the previous articles
