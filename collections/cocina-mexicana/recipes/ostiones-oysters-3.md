## Ingredients

- garlic
- oil
- breadcrumbs
- parsley
- pepper
- lemon juice
- oysters
- oyster broth

## Directions

1. Chop garlic very finely and fry in oil over low heat so it cooks well.
2. Add breadcrumbs and when well fried add very finely chopped parsley, powdered pepper, and lemon juice with more oil.
3. Add the oysters with their own broth but in small quantity, as they should remain dry.
