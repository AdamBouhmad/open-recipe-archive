## Ingredients

- oil
- vinegar
- whole onions
- head of garlic
- whole peppercorns

## Directions

1. Use equal quantities of oil and vinegar
2. Add two whole onions, one head of garlic, and four whole peppercorns
3. Put in a pot, covering with paper and placing a cup of water on top
4. Stir occasionally with the same pot
