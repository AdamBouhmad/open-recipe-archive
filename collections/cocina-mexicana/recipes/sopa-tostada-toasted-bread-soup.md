## Ingredients

- bread crusts or slices of toasted bread
- broth

## Directions

1. Take bread crusts or slices of toasted bread and place them in a cooking pot with a little broth over very low heat
2. Let the broth be consumed and the slices stick a little
3. Then separate them by adding new broth
4. Transfer to the soup tureen and serve in broth
