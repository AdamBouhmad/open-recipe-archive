## Ingredients

- coconut
- water
- eight egg yolks
- one pound of sugar
- Jerez wine

## Directions

1. Grind the coconut, sprinkle with water and squeeze through a napkin
2. Add eight egg yolks mixed with milk and dissolve them
3. Clarify one pound of sugar and when at medium point remove from heat
4. Add the coconut and return to heat to cook to the proper point
5. Add Jerez wine
