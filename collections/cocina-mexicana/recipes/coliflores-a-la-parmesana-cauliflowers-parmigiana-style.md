## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- white sauce
- grated cheese
- bread crumbs
- butter

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous ones and pour white sauce over them, adding grated cheese to the sauce
2. Cover with a layer of bread and grated cheese
3. Brown in melted butter and add another layer of bread and cheese
4. A quarter hour before serving place on a platter in a covered oven so they form a crust and take color
