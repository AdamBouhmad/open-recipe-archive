## Ingredients

- thin slices of fatty ham
- puff pastry dough with less salt than usual

## Directions

1. Cut thin slices of fatty ham and soak them in water to desalt
2. Make a puff pastry dough with less salt than is customarily used
3. Form the gâteau with the dough, scalloping the edges
4. Place on a baking sheet, brush with egg wash, and bake until golden
5. Cover with the ham slices, already drained, trimmed, and separated from the rind or skin
