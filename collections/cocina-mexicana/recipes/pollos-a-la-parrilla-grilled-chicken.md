## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Salt
- Lard
- Garlic, ground

## Directions

1. After cleaning, rub the chicken with salt inside and outside
2. Coat with lard and ground garlic
3. Place on the grill over moderate heat
4. Take care to turn them and coat them frequently with lard
5. Once cooked and golden, serve
