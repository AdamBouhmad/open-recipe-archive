## Ingredients

- Six cuartillos of water
- Half pound of beef pulp, cut into small square pieces
- Two or three lettuces
- A little parsley
- Chicory, if desired

## Directions

1. Bring to boil in six cuartillos of water half a pound of beef pulp, cut into small square pieces, with two or three lettuces and a little parsley, and if desired, a little wild chicory.
2. Let one third of the broth be consumed and pass the remainder through a silk sieve.
