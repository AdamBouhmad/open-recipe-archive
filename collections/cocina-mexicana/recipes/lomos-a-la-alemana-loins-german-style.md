## Ingredients

- loin
- ham strips
- pepper
- salt
- flour
- butter
- water
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Slice into somewhat thick slices, lard with thick strips of ham and after adding ground pepper, salt and flour, fry in butter and let cook for about a quarter hour, adding water little by little
2. When the sauce is thick, remove from heat and serve with olives and capers
