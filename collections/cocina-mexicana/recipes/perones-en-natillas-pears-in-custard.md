## Ingredients

- Pears
- Cloves
- Cinnamon sticks
- Clarified syrup
- Ground almonds
- Custard
- Egg yolks
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Peel the pears, divide them in half, remove their cores, and stud them with cloves and cinnamon sticks.
2. Place them in clarified syrup and bring to a boil until it reaches the consistency of a preserve, carefully turning them so they do not break.
3. When the pears are cooked and the jelly is at a lower point, remove them and arrange them on plates to form a bed, which is covered with its jelly mixed with ground almonds, custard, and beaten egg yolks, making a loose paste.
4. Arrange as many layers as fit in the plates, which should be deep, with the last layer being the paste.
5. Dust on top with ground cinnamon, forming flowers or other designs with it.
