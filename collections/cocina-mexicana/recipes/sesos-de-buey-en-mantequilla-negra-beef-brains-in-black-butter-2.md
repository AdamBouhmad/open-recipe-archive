## Ingredients

- beef brains
- water
- lard or ham slices
- bay leaves
- onion slices
- carrot slices
- parsley
- green onions
- white wine
- meat broth
- black butter

## Directions

1. Remove the clotted blood, the small skin, and the fibers that cover the brains.
2. Soak for two hours in warm water.
3. Cook between two slices of lard or ham, with bay leaves, onion and carrot slices, a bundle of parsley and green onions, a glass of white wine, and meat broth.
4. Allow to boil for half an hour over low heat and drain.
5. Add black butter underneath and serve.
