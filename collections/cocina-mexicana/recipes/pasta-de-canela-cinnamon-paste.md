## Ingredients

- Powdered cinnamon
- Syrup at water-joining point consistency in greater or lesser quantity as desired

## Directions

1. Mix powdered cinnamon with syrup at water-joining point consistency off the heat in the desired quantity for color intensity
2. Return to heat and let it simmer a little
3. Remove from heat
4. Beat it
5. Pour into molds
