## Ingredients

- Fresh pork meat, minced
- Lard, minced
- Salt
- Pepper
- White wine
- Pork blood
- Cinnamon, half an ounce, ground
- Cloves, half an ounce, ground
- Thick strips of pork
- Spices
- Pork intestine

## Directions

1. Mince together fresh pork meat and lard
2. Season with salt and pepper
3. Sprinkle this minced mixture with white wine and pork blood
4. Add half an ounce each of ground cinnamon and cloves
5. Cut thick strips from the pork and dust them well with spices
6. Distribute these strips among the minced mixture
7. Put everything into pork intestine, and when full, tie both ends
