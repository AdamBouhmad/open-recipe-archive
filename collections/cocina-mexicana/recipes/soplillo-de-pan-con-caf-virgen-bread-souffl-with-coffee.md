## Ingredients

- milk, three quarts
- sugar, six ounces
- coffee, four ounces, somewhat broken

## Directions

1. Boil three quarts of milk with six ounces of sugar
2. Toast four ounces of somewhat broken coffee
3. Immediately upon removing from the pan, pour into the milk and cover the pot
