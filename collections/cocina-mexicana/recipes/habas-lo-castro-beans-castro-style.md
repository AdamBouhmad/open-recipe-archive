## Ingredients

- beans in pods
- water
- butter
- flour
- broth
- salt
- pepper
- savory
- egg yolks
- sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the pods and cook in boiling water to shed the skin
2. Drain and toss with butter in a pot adding a little flour
3. Moisten with broth from the pot and add salt, pepper, and chopped savory
4. After a few moments, bind with egg yolks and a little sugar, stirring vigorously, and serve
