## Ingredients

- Folded dough
- Puff pastry dough with five or six turns
- Filling (not specified in recipe)

## Directions

1. Form a base or bottom of folded dough of regular thickness and convenient size
2. Form another sheet of puff pastry dough that has been given five or six turns or folds
3. Moisten the bottom of the folded dough and place the puff pastry sheet on top
4. Pass the rolling pin so it settles well, then trim to give it an attractive shape, whether square, round or oval
5. With a graceful-figured mold that is eight or ten lines smaller in extension than the dough, cut the dough by fitting the mold in such a way that it does not penetrate or cut more than the puff pastry that forms the upper layer, leaving intact the folded dough of the bottom below, and the wheel cut by the mold remains attached in its place
6. Put in a hot oven or place at two fires, and when the dough is cooked, remove it
7. Carefully remove the figure marked by the mold, and add the filling in the hollow that results, and cover or not with the marked part that had been removed
