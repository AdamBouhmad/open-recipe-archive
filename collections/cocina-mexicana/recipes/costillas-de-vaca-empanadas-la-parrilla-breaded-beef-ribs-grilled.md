## Ingredients

- beef ribs
- salt
- black pepper
- butter
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare the ribs and season with a little salt and black pepper
2. Melt a little butter and dip the ribs one by one in it
3. Remove them from the butter and place in a pot containing bread crumbs, turn them there, and remove to coat completely with more crumbs
4. Half an hour before serving, place on the grill over gentle heat so the bread does not brown too much
5. When cooked, dress with a clear sauce underneath
