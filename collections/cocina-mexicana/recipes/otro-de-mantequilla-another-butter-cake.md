## Ingredients

- thin slices of bread
- butter
- milk
- sugar
- raisins
- sesame seeds
- cinnamon
- dragée
- eight egg whites
- sugar for topping

## Directions

1. Make thin slices of bread and fry them in butter
2. Pour cooked milk sweetened with sugar over the bread slices
3. Mix in raisins and everything else as above, sprinkling sesame, cinnamon, and dragée on top
4. Make the puff pastry with eight well-beaten egg whites
5. Sprinkle sugar on top and place over two fires until it sets
