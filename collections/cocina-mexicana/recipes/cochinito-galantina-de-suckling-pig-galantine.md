## Ingredients

- Suckling pig
- Two pounds of beef liver, chopped
- Two pounds of fat ham, chopped
- Two eggs
- Salt
- Spices
- Ham strips
- Game meat in thin strips
- Poultry in thin strips
- Criadillas de tierra slices
- Linen cloth

## Directions

1. Scald it, singe it at the flame and debone it up to the head, which should not be removed.
2. Chop separately two pounds of beef liver and two pounds of fat ham, and mix them afterward, adding two eggs, salt and spices.
3. Extend the flattened suckling pig on a table and cover it with half of the said chopped mixture.
4. Place on top as much as desired of strips of ham, game and poultry in thin slices, and slices of criadillas de tierra, covering all with the other remaining half of the chopped mixture.
5. Sew the belly skin and give the suckling pig its natural form.
6. Wrap it in linen and tie it in such a way that it does not decompose or lose its shape.
