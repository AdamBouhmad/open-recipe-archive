## Ingredients

- six pounds of sugar
- four pounds of sweet potato, peeled and ground
- two pounds of pineapple, peeled and ground

## Directions

1. Make syrup with six pounds of sugar and bring to point
2. Mix in four pounds of sweet potato and two pounds of pineapple, both peeled and ground
3. Allow to recover the point of cajeta
