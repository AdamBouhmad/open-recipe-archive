## Ingredients

- pork meat, mixed lean and fat
- pepper
- small amount of hot chili

## Directions

1. Mince pork meat that is mixed with lean and fat until it becomes a paste
2. Season as you would chorizo, adding pepper
3. Use a small amount of hot chili
