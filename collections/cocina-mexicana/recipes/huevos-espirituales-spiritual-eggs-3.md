## Ingredients

- two egg yolks
- wine or very hot spirits
- powdered white sugar
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat the yolks in a cup
2. Gradually add very hot wine or spirits while beating
3. Add powdered white sugar
4. Beat very well and add a pinch of cinnamon
5. When the cup is full it can be drunk
