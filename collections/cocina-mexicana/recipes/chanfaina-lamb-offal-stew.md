## Ingredients

- lamb liver, in pieces
- lamb lungs, in pieces
- cooked lamb blood, in pieces
- lard
- onions
- garlic
- spices
- parsley, finely chopped
- chile
- water
- flour
- eggs, optional

## Directions

1. Sauté the pieces of liver, lungs and cooked blood in lard, adding onions, garlic, spices, finely chopped parsley and chile
2. After everything is well sautéed, add water and bring to a boil
3. Fry flour, dissolve it with water once well fried, and add it to the chanfaina to thicken the broth
4. If desired, you can add a beaten egg mixture
