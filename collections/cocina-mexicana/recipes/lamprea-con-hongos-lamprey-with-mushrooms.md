## Ingredients

- lamprey
- mushrooms
- butter
- mixed herbs bundle
- parsley
- onion
- garlic head
- clove
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- flour
- red wine
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Place a lamprey divided into pieces in a pan with mushrooms, good butter, a mixed bundle of herbs, parsley, onion, garlic head, clove, thyme, bay leaf, basil, a little flour and two glasses of red wine, salt and pepper
2. Let the broth reduce somewhat and bind with the lamprey's blood
3. Serve
