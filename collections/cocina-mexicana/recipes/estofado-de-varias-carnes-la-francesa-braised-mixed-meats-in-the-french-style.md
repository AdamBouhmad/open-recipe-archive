## Ingredients

- beef breast or any other meat, well flattened with a mallet
- good broth or water
- two ounces beef marrow or butter
- salt
- three or four cloves
- a little pepper
- one bay leaf
- one sprig of thyme
- one onion
- two ounces fatty pork meat
- asparagus
- artichoke bottoms
- mushrooms

## Directions

1. Place the well-flattened meat in a cazuela or pot with good broth or water that rises three fingers above the meat to cover it
2. Add two ounces of beef marrow or butter, salt, three or four cloves, a little pepper, one bay leaf, one sprig of thyme, one onion and two ounces of fatty pork meat
3. Let everything cook together, turning the meat from time to time
4. When the meat is half cooked, asparagus broken into pieces, two or three artichoke bottoms and mushrooms may be added
5. When everything is perfectly cooked and the broth is of good flavor, neither too watery nor too thick, serve
