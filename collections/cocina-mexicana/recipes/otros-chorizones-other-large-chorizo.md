## Ingredients

- one arroba of pork meat from loin or backbone
- one pound of wide chile soaked and well ground
- half pound of salt
- one head of garlic

## Directions

1. Chop the meat and chile finely
2. Grind the salt and garlic well
3. Mix together with a little water
4. Leave in adobo for twelve hours and taste
5. Add whatever is missing
6. Fill intestines with the mixture and let them air dry
