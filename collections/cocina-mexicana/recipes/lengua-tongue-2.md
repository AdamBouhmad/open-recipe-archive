## Ingredients

- capers
- tomatoes
- butter
- fine spices
- tongue, cooked

## Directions

1. Fry capers
2. Roast tomatoes
3. Brown in butter
4. Grind everything and fry in butter and oil
5. Add ground fine spices
6. When the sauce is ready, add the cooked tongue in slices and let rest
