## Ingredients

- beef meat
- beef broth
- onion or parsley
- small onion bulbs (cabezonas)
- capers
- anchovies
- garlic, finely minced
- salt
- black pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the beef meat in a pot and cut into very thin slices.
2. In the same serving dish, add two spoonfuls of substantive beef broth with onion or parsley, small onion bulbs, capers, anchovies if available, and finely minced garlic, seasoning with salt and black pepper.
3. Arrange the beef slices on top and cover with the same ingredients placed below.
4. Cover the plate and place to boil over a medium flame for half an hour, more or less.
5. Serve with a light sauce.
