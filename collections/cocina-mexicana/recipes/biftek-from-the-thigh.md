## Ingredients

- slices from the thigh, half an inch thick
- grease
- butter
- pepper
- onion, finely chopped
- water

## Directions

1. Heat the grill until very hot and coat with grease, then place on high heat
2. Place the meat slices on it and let them roast until they begin to turn dark color, then turn to the other side so they take the same color
3. Place on a hot plate and let sit for a while, placing a layer of butter between slice and slice
4. Add a little pepper and place back on the grill until well cooked
5. Chop an onion very fine and add to a spoonful of hot water, then pour over the meat before serving
