## Ingredients

- butter
- garlic cloves
- tomatoes, roasted
- cooked chickpeas, hulled
- clove
- pepper
- cumin
- onion, chopped
- hot water
- salt
- oil
- fried toast

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves in butter and then grind them with roasted tomatoes, cooked hulled chickpeas, clove, pepper, and cumin
2. In the butter in which the garlic was fried, fry chopped onion
3. Add the ground mixture, which will also be fried
4. Add hot water, salt and oil
5. Once this broth has boiled, soak the fried toast placed in a pot greased with butter with it
6. Let the soup cook over two gentle fires
