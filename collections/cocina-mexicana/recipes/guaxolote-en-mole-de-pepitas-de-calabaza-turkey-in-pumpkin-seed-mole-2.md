## Ingredients

- Pumpkin seeds
- Salt water
- Lard
- Toasted tree nuts
- Cacahuates (peanuts)
- Wide chile peppers
- Turkey broth
- Turkey meat
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Black pepper

## Directions

1. Sprinkle the pumpkin seeds with salted water and toast them on a comal, then grind them
2. Brown the toasted tree nuts, peanuts, and wide dried chile peppers in lard, then grind them
3. Fry all the ground mixture together in sufficient lard
4. Add the broth in which the turkey has been cooked, and the turkey pieces
5. Season with the corresponding salt and sufficient cinnamon, cloves, and black pepper, all ground
6. Let it thicken and serve
