## Ingredients

- nopales (cactus paddles)
- tequesquite
- lard
- onion
- water
- epazote leaves
- pasilla chiles
- oil

## Directions

1. Clean the nopales and cut lengthwise into strips
2. Cook in tequesquite water in a pot
3. Heat lard in a large amount in a clay pot and fry the nopales until golden with onion wheels and adequate water
4. Add epazote leaves and pasilla chiles
5. Allow to season
6. When finishing, add plenty of oil
