## Ingredients

- Spinach
- Butter
- Salt
- Nutmeg
- Frangipane

## Directions

1. Make a ball of partridge-cooked and very green spinach, and parboil it in butter with a little salt and nutmeg
2. Mix it with the frangipane and finish the operation as explained in the article of frangipane tart (see ahead)
