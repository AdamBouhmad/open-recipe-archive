## Ingredients

- Fifteen hard-boiled eggs
- Cream sauce
- Parsley, minced
- Onion, minced
- Breadcrumbs
- Beaten eggs

## Directions

1. Cut the white and yolk of fifteen hard-boiled eggs into small square pieces
2. Place in a saucepan
3. Make a cream sauce with which to mix finely minced and washed parsley and onion
4. Pour the sauce over the eggs so they become somewhat bound
5. Mix well with the sauce
6. Once cooled, scoop out portions with a full common spoon and place on a baking sheet
7. Shape them as croquettes by rolling in breadcrumbs
8. Coat with beaten egg
9. Bread a second time
10. At the moment of serving, place in very hot oil
