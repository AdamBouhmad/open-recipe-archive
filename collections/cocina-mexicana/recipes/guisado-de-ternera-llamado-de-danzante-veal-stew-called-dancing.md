## Ingredients

- veal
- green chile
- soaked chickpeas
- ham
- fine ground spices
- lard
- vinegar
- parsley

## Directions

1. Make the meat into small pieces and place in a pot with full accompaniment, slices of green chile, soaked chickpeas, pieces of ham, fine ground spices, lard, vinegar and parsley
2. Put everything on the fire over low heat, cover the mouth of the pot with a cazuela that has water so that in case the stew dries up water from this same vessel is added to it and it is stirred with the same pot
