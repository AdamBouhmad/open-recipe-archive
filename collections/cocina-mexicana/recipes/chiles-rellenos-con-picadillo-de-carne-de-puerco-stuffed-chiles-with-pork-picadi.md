## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Pork picadillo
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Tomatoes, roasted and ground
- Water
- All ground spices
- Salt
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Plantain slices
- Apple slices
- Camote slices
- Pomegranate seeds
- Flour
- Beaten egg
- Oil or lard for frying

## Directions

1. Prepare the chiles by roasting them and removing the seeds and veins
2. Coat them in flour and dip in beaten egg
3. Fry the chiles until golden
4. In lard, fry chopped garlic and parsley with roasted ground tomatoes
5. Add sufficient water and bring the sauce to a boil
6. Add ground spices and necessary salt to the sauce
7. Place the fried chiles in the sauce and let it thicken
8. Season with good vinegar and a little sugar to soften the acidity of the tomato
9. If desired, add slices of plantain, apple, camote or other fruits
10. When serving, garnish the platter with pomegranate seeds
