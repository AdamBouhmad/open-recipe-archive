## Ingredients

- Spanish squash
- Lime water, settled
- Clarified syrup at medium point

## Directions

1. Cut the squash into wheels and peel carefully without damaging the flesh
2. Remove the insides and place in settled lime water to firm up
3. Remove and wash in two or three clean waters, being careful not to break them
4. Blanch in boiling water and let drain
5. Place in clarified syrup at medium point
6. Boil for some time over three consecutive days to allow the syrup to penetrate
7. On the third day, raise the syrup to preserve point and transfer to preserve jars
