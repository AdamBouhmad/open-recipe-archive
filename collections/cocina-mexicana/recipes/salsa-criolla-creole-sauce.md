## Ingredients

- three tablespoons butter
- garlic
- pepper
- cumin
- ground capers
- toasted flour
- salt
- water

## Directions

1. Heat a pan with three tablespoons butter
2. Add minced garlic, pepper, and ground cumin
3. When half fried, add ground capers
4. Add a little toasted flour and the necessary salt
5. Add water and simmer
6. This sauce is very good for fish, squab, and other poultry
