## Ingredients

- one pound of butter per pound of flour
- cold water
- salt

## Directions

1. Mix the dough well soft, adding cold water
2. Flatten with the rolling pin until it reaches the thickness of a coin
3. Coat the surface well with butter
4. Fold in half and roll with the rolling pin again
5. Repeat coating with butter and folding as many times as leaves are to be given to the dough - at least three
