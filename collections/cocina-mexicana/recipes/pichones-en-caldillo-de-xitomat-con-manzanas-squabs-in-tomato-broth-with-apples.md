## Ingredients

- squabs
- ham cut into dice
- longaniza pieces
- chorizo slices
- apple slices
- whole small headed onions
- lard
- chile strips or tornachiles
- garlic
- roasted tomatoes
- cloves
- pepper
- chopped parsley
- fried and ground bread
- oil

## Directions

1. Cook the squabs with diced ham, longaniza pieces, chorizo slices, apple slices and small whole onions
2. Fry chile or tornachile strips with garlic and roasted tomatoes with cloves and pepper, all ground
3. Add chopped parsley
4. Pour the pot of cooked squabs with their broth and the other cooked ingredients into this mixture
5. Let simmer briefly
6. If the broth is too thin, thicken it with fried ground bread
7. In either case, add oil so the squabs simmer well
