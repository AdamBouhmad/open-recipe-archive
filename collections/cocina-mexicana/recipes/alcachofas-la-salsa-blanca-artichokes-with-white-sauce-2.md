## Ingredients

- artichokes
- white sauce

## Directions

1. Clean the artichokes and cook them
2. Place in cold water as with the previous recipe
3. Before serving, reheat them by placing in boiling water
4. Remove the inner part
5. Place in a dish and cover the hollow with white sauce
6. The sauce may also be served separately in a sauce boat
