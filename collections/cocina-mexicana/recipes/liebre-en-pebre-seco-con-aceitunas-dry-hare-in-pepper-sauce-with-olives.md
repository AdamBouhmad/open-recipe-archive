## Ingredients

- Hares
- water
- salt
- oil
- onion
- pitted olives
- bread
- ginger
- nutmeg
- lettuce

## Directions

1. Cook the hares in salted water and divide into portions
2. Fry in oil and set aside
3. In the same oil, fry thin slices of onion, then add pitted olives and bread fried and ground with ginger and nutmeg
4. Place the hare in this sauce and let boil until the portions are dry
5. Serve on lettuce leaves
