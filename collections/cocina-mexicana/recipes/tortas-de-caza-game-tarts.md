## Ingredients

- rabbit or hare or woodcock or woodcocks or partridges
- fine herbs
- salt
- spices
- ham slices
- butter
- dough
- egg for brushing
- good sauce with good broth or strained broth
- orange juice
- stew of beef sweetbread and mushrooms or sliced truffles

## Directions

1. For rabbit: divide its members and break the bones a little with the machete along the loin
2. For hare: remove all bones which can be reserved for a civet, and only put the meat in the tart
3. For woodcock: for one tart use two birds, divide into quarters, mince the inner part and incorporate with the intended minced filling
4. For woodcocks or snipe or partridges: remove the legs and neck and empty them; make a filling with the intestines or mix with the minced meat
5. Place the game on the bottom layer of dough in the tart pan with a bunch of fine herbs, salt, spices, ham slices and butter
6. Cover with another sheet of dough as explained in the previous article
7. When cooked and drained, add a good sauce made with good substance or strained broth
8. Squeeze the juice of two oranges into the sauce
9. Or if there is a good stew instead of sauce, either of beef sweetbread and mushrooms or of sliced truffles, so much the better for the tart which will be more esteemed
10. Always add the juice of one lemon when serving, since game requires a little piquancy or sharpness
