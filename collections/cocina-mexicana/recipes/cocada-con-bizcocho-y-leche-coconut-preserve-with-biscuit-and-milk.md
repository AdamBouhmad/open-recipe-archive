## Ingredients

- one pound of sugar
- six egg yolks per coconut
- grated and ground coconut
- hard biscuit
- cinnamon
- butter

## Directions

1. Clarify the syrup and bring it to medium point, then remove from heat and let cool.
2. Add the egg yolks and return to heat until the egg is cooked.
3. Add the grated and ground coconut and keep on heat until the mixture reaches the point where the bottom of the pan becomes visible, then remove from heat and pour onto a platter.
4. Sprinkle hard biscuit ground with cinnamon over the top and add a little melted butter with a feather.
5. Place a griddle with heat on top to brown it, ensuring no empty spaces remain without biscuit.
