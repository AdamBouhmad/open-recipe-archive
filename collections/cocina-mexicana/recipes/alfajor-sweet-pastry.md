## Ingredients

- Sugar
- Honey
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Pepper
- Biscuit
- Sesame seeds
- Pine nuts
- Almonds
- Walnuts

## Directions

1. Make a syrup and cook until it reaches the thread stage
2. When the paste is cold, empty it into a platter, small boxes or paper cones
3. In the first two cases, decorate the surface with toasted sesame seeds, pine nuts, almonds, walnuts in quarters, and a little cinnamon powder
