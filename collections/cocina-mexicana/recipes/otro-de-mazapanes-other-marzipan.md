## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- one or two egg whites, beaten
- one pound of sugar
- honey

## Directions

1. Grind the almonds, coating the grinding stone with one or two beaten egg whites so it does not become oily
2. Clarify the sugar giving it the point of mirror gloss, add the almonds there and when it detaches from the pot, set it aside for the next day
3. Make balls with wet hands, place on wafers and flatten a little, making a cross in the middle with a knife dipped in water
4. Send to the oven
5. If one wants to coat them, it can be done by clarifying honey giving it a point of mirror gloss, well high
