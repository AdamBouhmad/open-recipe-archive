## Ingredients

- rice
- egg
- pork loin, minced
- flour
- spiced broth

## Directions

1. Wash the rice and cook until the liquid is consumed
2. Set aside and bind with a little beaten egg
3. Form meatballs with minced pork loin inside
4. Dust with flour, fry, and cook in spiced broth
