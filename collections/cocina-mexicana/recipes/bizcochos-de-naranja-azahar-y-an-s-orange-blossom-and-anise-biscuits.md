## Ingredients

- fifteen egg whites
- half a pound of finely ground sugar
- fifteen egg yolks
- three calderas cups of yeast
- juice of half an orange
- half a pound of very hot butter
- a little clean anise
- one cuartillo of orange blossom water
- flour as needed for proper consistency

## Directions

1. Beat the fifteen egg whites with half a pound of finely ground sugar until well risen
2. Add the fifteen egg yolks and beat together until the mixture is thick and well incorporated
3. Add three calderas cups of yeast, the juice of half an orange, half a pound of very hot butter, a little clean anise, and one cuartillo of orange blossom water, mixing well
4. Mix in flour gradually until the dough reaches a consistency that is neither hard nor too soft
5. Form the biscuits and let rest for eight to ten hours
6. Bake in a well-heated oven
