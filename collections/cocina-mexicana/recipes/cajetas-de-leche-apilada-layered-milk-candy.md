## Ingredients

- Eight pounds of sugar
- Sixteen quartillos of milk
- Two ounces of cornstarch dissolved in milk and strained
- One pineapple, cooked in water, peeled, cleaned and ground
- Two ounces of cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Dissolve eight pounds of sugar in sixteen quartillos of milk over the fire
2. Strain the sweetened milk and return to the fire, adding two ounces of cornstarch dissolved in milk and strained
3. Bring to cajeta point
4. Then add one pineapple cooked in water, peeled, cleaned and ground, letting it return to cajeta point
5. Set aside from the fire and when pouring into the molds, quickly incorporate two ounces of cinnamon powder
