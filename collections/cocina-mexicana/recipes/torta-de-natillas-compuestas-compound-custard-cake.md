## Ingredients

- Butter
- Mamón (sponge cake)
- Two quartillos of cooked and sweetened milk
- Four calderas of custard
- Eight egg yolks
- Sugar
- Ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grease a platter with butter
2. Place a layer of mamón
3. Moisten with two quartillos of well-cooked and sweetened milk
4. Mix four calderas of custard with eight egg yolks
5. Prepare with sugar and cinnamon
6. Pour a little custard over the mamón
7. Place more mamón on top and another layer of custard
8. Always finish with a layer of custard
9. Dust with ground sugar and cinnamon
10. Place the platter over hot coals and cover with a griddle or similar
11. Add more hot coals on top
