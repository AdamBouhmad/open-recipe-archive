## Ingredients

- Half a cuarta of sugar
- Four cuartillos of milk

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at high point with half a cuarta of sugar
2. Lower heat and let cool
3. Mix in four cuartillos of milk
4. Return to fire until candies form on wafers
5. Press them to achieve regular thickness
6. Mark with a thimble and cut
