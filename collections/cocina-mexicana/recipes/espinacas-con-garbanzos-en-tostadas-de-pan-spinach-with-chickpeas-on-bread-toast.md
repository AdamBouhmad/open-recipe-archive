## Ingredients

- spinach
- earth salt
- chickpeas, soaked from the day before
- lard
- onion, chopped
- garlic, peeled and ground
- bread soaked
- egg yolks
- bread, sliced and toasted on a comal or fried in lard

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach and cook it in water with a little earth salt
2. When the greens have released, cook the soaked chickpeas in the same water
3. Fry a little chopped onion in lard and add the cooked spinach and chickpeas with their broth
4. Add garlic peeled and ground with soaked bread and egg yolks, according to the quantity of spinach
5. Let everything boil until well thickened
6. Arrange on slices of bread toasted on a comal or fried in lard, being careful in both cases that nothing burns
