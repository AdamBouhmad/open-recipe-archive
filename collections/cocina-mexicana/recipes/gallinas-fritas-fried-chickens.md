## Ingredients

- Chickens
- Aromatic herbs
- Garlic
- Onions
- Salt
- Breadcrumbs
- Pepper
- Lard

## Directions

1. Cook the chickens with aromatic herbs, garlic and onions and convenient salt, covering the pot well
2. When the chickens are cooked, roll them in breadcrumbs
3. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper over them
4. Brown them in lard
