## Ingredients

- butter
- marrow
- milk
- sugar
- cream
- egg yolks
- cinnamon
- canela

## Directions

1. Butter a plate and place a layer of marrow on it, moistened with two portions of well-cooked and sweetened milk
2. Mix three cups full of cream with eight egg yolks, composed with sugar and cinnamon, and place a little over the marrow
3. Place another layer of marrow on top and another of cream, with the cream always being the last layer, sprinkled with ground sugar and cinnamon
4. Place the plate over hot coals, covered with a comal or tin sheet, place more hot coals on top and let the cake set
