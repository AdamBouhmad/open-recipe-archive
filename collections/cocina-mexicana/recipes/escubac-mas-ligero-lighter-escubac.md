## Ingredients

- Three-quarters of an ounce of saffron
- Twenty-four grains of mace or nutmeg bark
- Peel of one orange
- Peel of two lemons
- Sixteen cuartillos of common brandy
- Four pounds of sugar
- Eight cuartillos of water

## Directions

1. Infuse for eight days at room temperature three-quarters of an ounce of saffron, twenty-four grains of mace, orange peel, and lemon peels in sixteen cuartillos of common brandy
2. Distill everything in a bain-marie to obtain slightly more than half the liqueur
3. Dissolve four pounds of sugar in eight cuartillos of water
4. Mix everything well, filter through a cloth, and store in well-sealed bottles
