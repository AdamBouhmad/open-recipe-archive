## Ingredients

- black chiles
- garlic
- vinegar
- salt
- pepper
- cinnamon
- onion

## Directions

1. Remove veins from the black chiles, toast them and grind them with garlic cloves
2. Lower with good vinegar and season with salt
3. Grind four peppercorns and a little cinnamon
4. Well mixed, mince the onion and mix it with the sauce
5. Place this in the sauce boat
