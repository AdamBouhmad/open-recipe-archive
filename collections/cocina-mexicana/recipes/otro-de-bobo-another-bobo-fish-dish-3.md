## Ingredients

- bobo
- agua
- limón
- aceite
- vinagre
- orégano
- cebolla
- aceitunas
- alcaparras
- chiles

## Directions

1. Once the bobo is scaled, place in a pot with little water, some lemon slices, oil and vinegar and let it come to a boil.
2. When placed on the platter, bathe with its own broth and oil and dust with oregano, cooked onions, olives, capers and chiles.
