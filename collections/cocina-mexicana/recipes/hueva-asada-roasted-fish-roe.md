## Ingredients

- fish roe
- water
- paper
- lard
- onion
- oil
- pickled chiles
- capers
- parsley
- olives
- oregano
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Soak the roe in water from one day to the next
2. Dry it carefully so it does not break
3. Place a paper on the grill greased with lard on top
4. Place the roe on the fire, also greasing it with lard on top and leave it until it is well soaked and golden
5. Place it on the plate with onion, oil and pickled chiles
6. It can also be made as a salad by cooking it inside a cloth, but the water must be boiling when adding it and it should not be too tight in the cloth
7. Remove the skin, cut into slices and place on a plate with chopped capers, fine parsley, olives, oregano, pickled chiles, chopped onion, oil and vinegar
