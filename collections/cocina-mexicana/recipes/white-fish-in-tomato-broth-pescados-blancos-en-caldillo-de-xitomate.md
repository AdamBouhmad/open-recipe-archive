## Ingredients

- garlic
- tomatoes
- bread, soaked
- white fish
- vinegar
- pepper
- cilantro
- oregano
- onion, in pieces
- cooked chiles, in pieces
- oil

## Directions

1. Crush garlic and tomatoes
2. Mix with well-ground soaked bread
3. Cook the fish in this mixture with vinegar, pepper, cilantro, oregano, pieces of onion, and cooked chiles
4. At the time of serving, add oil
