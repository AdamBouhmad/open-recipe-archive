## Ingredients

- Squabs
- Ham slices
- Chorizo
- Lard
- Onion, chopped
- Garlic cloves
- Dried cilantro
- Water
- Ground clove
- Ground cinnamon
- Ground pepper
- Fried bread slices
- Thyme
- Jerez wine
- Chiles
- Olives
- Capers

## Directions

1. Place squabs in a casserole with ham slices, chorizo, and a spoonful of lard
2. Chop plenty of onion and garlic cloves
3. Fry dried cilantro in lard
4. When onion and meat are fried, add the cilantro to fry everything together
5. Add water, ground clove, ground cinnamon, ground pepper, fried bread slices (all ground), a sprig of thyme, and Jerez wine
6. Keep the broth thick
7. Garnish with chiles, olives, and capers
