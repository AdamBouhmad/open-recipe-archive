## Ingredients

- Beef sirloin
- Pieces of pork fat from the breast
- Salt
- Spices
- Onions
- Parsnips or tomatoes
- Herb bouquet

## Directions

1. Partially cook the sirloin in a pot with pieces of pork fat
2. Make a red sauce with the same fat after removing the sirloin
3. Return the sirloin to the pot with the fat, spices, salt, onions, parsnips or tomatoes and a herb bouquet
4. Let it cook four hours over low fire
5. Remove the fat and serve
