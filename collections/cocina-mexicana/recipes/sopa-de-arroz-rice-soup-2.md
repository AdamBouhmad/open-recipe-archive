## Ingredients

- rice
- lard
- garlic clove, chopped
- broth

## Directions

1. Wash the rice, drain it well and spread it out to dry in the sun
2. In a pan with lard over fire, fry the chopped garlic cloves and once they have browned well, add the rice so it also fries and browns
3. Then add the necessary broth until it is well cooked, then let it dry by placing a comal with gentle fire on top
