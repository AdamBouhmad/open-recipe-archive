## Ingredients

- beef, veal, or mutton
- butter or oil
- ground pepper
- half a glass of vinegar
- broth from the pot or water
- one whole clove
- one tomato
- one head of garlic
- one bay leaf
- onion
- flour
- cold water
- peeled potatoes or thick potato slices

## Directions

1. Place a pot or pan over the heat with butter or oil; when hot, sauté the meat.
2. When well browned, add a little ground pepper, half a glass of vinegar, and cover with broth or water.
3. Add one whole clove, one tomato, one head of garlic, and one bay leaf.
4. If the meat is beef, being tougher, do not add the onion until it is half-cooked; but if it is veal or lamb, add plenty of onion right away, seasoning it.
5. When cooked, dissolve a little flour in cold water and add it to thicken the sauce and let it boil several times until the flour is dissolved.
6. Peeled potatoes or thick potato slices are often added as well.
