## Ingredients

- Twelve cuartillos of good white Castilian vinegar
- One and a half pounds of tarragon leaves, wilted in the shade

## Directions

1. Pour the twelve cuartillos of good white Castilian vinegar into a demijohn
2. Wilt the tarragon leaves in the shade, taking great care to spread them out so they do not scald
3. Once the leaves have become limp, add them to the demijohn with the vinegar
4. Add a small amount (as specified in the continuation of the recipe)
