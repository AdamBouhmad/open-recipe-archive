## Ingredients

- Partridges
- Oil
- Onion
- Garlic
- Spices
- White wine or vinegar
- Water

## Directions

1. Brown the partridges in a suitable pot with oil, coarsely chopped onion, a whole head of garlic, and spices.
2. Once well browned, add white wine or vinegar.
3. Cover the pot with kraft paper.
4. Place a small pan filled with water on top.
