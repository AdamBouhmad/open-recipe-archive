## Ingredients

- Sea bream
- Oil
- Garlic
- Sour oranges
- Vinegar
- Water
- Cloves
- Saffron
- Pepper
- Salt
- Butter

## Directions

1. Cut off the head, gut the fish and remove the scales
2. Divide into portions and fry in oil with a few cloves of garlic
3. Remove when golden and let cool
4. Squeeze twelve sour oranges to mix their juice with a quarter liter of vinegar and two liters of water
5. Add to this liquid clove, saffron and pepper, ground with the necessary salt
6. Place on the fire to season without boiling
7. When seasoned, remove and let cool
8. Put the pieces of sea bream in this escabeche
9. Cover the vessel or barrel very well in which it is kept
10. Serve fried or hot, with no other dressing than garlic fried in butter
