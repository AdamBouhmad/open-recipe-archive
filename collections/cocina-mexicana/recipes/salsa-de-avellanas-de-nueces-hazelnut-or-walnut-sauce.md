## Ingredients

- Hazelnuts or walnuts
- A few grains of garlic
- Cumin
- One cooked egg yolk
- White broth or water

## Directions

1. Make absolutely the same way as the pine nut sauce
2. Ensure the whiteness stands out along with the taste of the fruit
3. Avoid loading it with spices, which would make it stimulating and would not let its pastiness stand out
