## Ingredients

- ajo
- manteca
- pimienta
- cebolla picada
- caldo suficiente
- hojas de orégano
- mejorana
- tomillo
- pan bien tostado
- perejil picado
- aceite
- manteca quemada
- huevos duros
- limón
- hojas de romita

## Directions

1. Chop some garlic cloves and brown them in manteca, then grind them with a little pepper
2. Adding chopped onion, fry everything
3. Add sufficient broth and while boiling add some leaves of oregano, marjoram and thyme
4. Place well-toasted bread slices and let boil a little, the soup should be very brothy
5. Away from the fire, add a little chopped parsley, thyme and marjoram and a little oil and burned manteca
6. To serve, garnish with slices of hard-boiled eggs, lemon and leaves of romita
