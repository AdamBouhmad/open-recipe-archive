## Ingredients

- 2 pounds white beans or haricots
- pieces of bacon
- 1 onion
- carrot
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley
- 2 cloves

## Directions

1. Clean and wash two pounds of white beans or haricots.
2. Cook in cold water with small pieces of bacon, one onion, carrot, bay leaf, thyme, parsley, and two cloves.
3. When cooked, remove the bacon, legumes, and water.
4. Pass through a sieve or strainer with broth.
5. Season, reheat, and serve with small pieces of fried bread.
