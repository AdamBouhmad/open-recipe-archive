## Ingredients

- half a bottle of dry Madeira wine
- twenty large mushrooms
- as many rounded earth truffles
- two chili peppers
- a little tomato
- one ounce of beef gelatin
- four tablespoons of Spanish sauce
- two tablespoons of concentrated beef extract
- twenty rooster combs and kidneys
- twenty quenelles moistened in the spoon
- twelve sweetbreads of veal or lamb, whole or sliced

## Directions

1. Put half a bottle of dry Madeira wine in a saucepan with twenty large mushrooms, as many rounded earth truffles, two chili peppers, a little tomato, and one ounce of beef gelatin
2. Let everything reduce to a gelatin consistency
3. Moisten immediately with four tablespoons of Spanish sauce
4. After two tablespoons of concentrated beef extract have been stirred in, make the sauce boil
5. Skim it and let it reduce over the heat
6. Arrange the mushrooms and earth truffles in a dish and pour the sauce over them
7. You can add to this sauce twenty rooster combs and kidneys, as many quenelles moistened in the spoon, and twelve sweetbreads of veal or lamb, whole or sliced as preferred
