## Ingredients

- Cabbage
- Filling

## Directions

1. After washing the cabbage well, boil for a quarter hour in water
2. Remove and place in fresh cold water
3. When cooled, squeeze gently with the hand without breaking the leaves
4. Remove the leaves one by one and place a little filling on each
5. Reassemble the leaves one on top of another as if the cabbage were whole
6. Tie in all parts and cook over coals
7. Divide afterwards into two halves, place on a plate, and pour good strained broth or meat essence over the top to serve
