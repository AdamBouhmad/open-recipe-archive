## Ingredients

- two cuartillos of milk
- twelve egg yolks
- sugar and cinnamon to taste
- sponge cake (mamón), sliced
- comal with fire for browning

## Directions

1. Grind together two cuartillos of milk with twelve egg yolks, sugar, and cinnamon to taste
2. Strain everything
3. Cook until it reaches a paste consistency
4. Remove from heat and pour over layers of sliced sponge cake
5. Allow to set in the steam of a pot
6. Brown with a comal heated over fire on top
