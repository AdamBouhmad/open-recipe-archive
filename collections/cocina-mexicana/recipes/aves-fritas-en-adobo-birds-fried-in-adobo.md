## Ingredients

- Bird pieces
- Vinegar
- Broth
- Salt
- Parsley
- Onions
- Garlic
- Bay leaf

## Directions

1. Prepare the adobo with vinegar, broth, salt, parsley, onions, crushed garlic and bay leaf
2. Place the boneless pieces of bird in the adobo
