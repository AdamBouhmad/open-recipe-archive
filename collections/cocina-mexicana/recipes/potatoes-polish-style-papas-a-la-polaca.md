## Ingredients

- potatoes, well washed
- water
- butter
- two large onions in four pieces
- thyme
- bay leaf
- clove
- salt
- pepper in grains

## Directions

1. Put well-washed potatoes in water with a little butter, two large onions cut in four pieces, thyme, bay leaf, clove, salt and peppercorns.
2. Let cook until a finger can be pressed inside.
3. Place in a sieve to drain.
4. Peel and divide in two or three parts.
5. Cover with white sauce or capers.
