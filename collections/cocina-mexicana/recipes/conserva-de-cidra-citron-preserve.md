## Ingredients

- Citron
- Syrup (almíbar) at low point

## Directions

1. Peel the citron and cut into pieces or small figures carved with a knife or other suitable instrument
2. Cook in water until they acquire the proper degree for preserves
3. Set aside and let cool, then change the water, repeating this three times daily (morning and afternoon) for three days so it loses all its unpleasant bitterness, leaving it exposed to sun and night air
4. After this time, drain it, dry it and place it in clarified syrup at medium point, giving it several boils
5. The next day return it to the fire so the syrup reaches preserve point
