## Ingredients

- bread
- water
- lard or oil
- garlic
- pepper
- salt

## Directions

1. Break bread into small pieces and sprinkle with a little water to moisten it
2. Place lard or oil in a pan or pot and add garlic
3. When it is golden, add a little pepper and salt
4. Then add the previously prepared bread and a few spoonfuls of water on top
5. Stir constantly until golden and serve hot
