## Ingredients

- six cuartillos of milk
- twelve egg yolks
- two ounces of starch dissolved in milk
- pulp of custard apple
- sugar
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Do the same as the egg custard apple milk, but without mixing egg and adding instead two ounces of starch dissolved in milk
2. Add custard apple pulp as needed for flavor
3. Add sugar if needed
4. Allow to boil to recover the proper point and pour into serving dishes
