## Ingredients

- salt-cured tuna
- water
- portions
- beaten egg
- soup or salad

## Directions

1. Wash the tuna in many waters to desalt it
2. Cut into portions
3. Cover with beaten egg
4. Fry
5. Serve over soup or salad
