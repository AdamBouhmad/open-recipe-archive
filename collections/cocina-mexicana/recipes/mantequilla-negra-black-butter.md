## Ingredients

- half a glass of vinegar
- salt and pepper
- half a pound of butter

## Directions

1. Pour half a glass of vinegar with salt and pepper into a saucepan and let it boil several times
2. In another saucepan, put half a pound of butter and let it burn until almost black
3. Allow it to rest
4. Pour it into the other saucepan over the vinegar
5. Keep hot and use in dishes that require it
