## Ingredients

- one pound of flour
- four ounces of lard
- three egg yolks and two egg whites
- three ounces of sugar
- lukewarm water

## Directions

1. Make a dough with one pound of flour, four ounces of lard, three egg yolks and two egg whites, and three ounces of sugar, mixing with lukewarm water
2. Form the tortillas
3. Cook them on a comal or in the oven
