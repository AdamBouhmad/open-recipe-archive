## Ingredients

- Cooked and peeled pork tongues
- water
- salt
- vinegar
- chorizo slices
- longaniza pieces
- ham slices
- wide chile soaked and ground with garlic
- clove
- cumin
- lard
- parsley leaves
- small chiles
- olives
- sliced hard-boiled eggs

## Directions

1. After cooking and peeling, divide into regular-sized pieces and place in a pot with water, salt, vinegar, chorizo slices, longaniza pieces and ham slices
2. Add wide chile soaked and ground with garlic, clove and cumin
3. Let cook in this marinade, and when done, empty everything into a pot with burned lard, frying it
4. Then add water from the first cooking of the tongues
5. Let the small broth reduce over two fires
6. For serving decorate with parsley leaves, small chiles, olives and sliced hard-boiled eggs
