## Ingredients

- Salted cod
- Fresh water
- Butter

## Directions

1. To desalt it, place in cold water, which should be changed frequently over the course of twenty-four hours, and then boil over the fire for fifteen to twenty minutes.
2. Do not bring to the table until a quarter hour has passed after removing it from the heat, although during that time it can be kept in the water in which it was cooked, with the dish covered.
