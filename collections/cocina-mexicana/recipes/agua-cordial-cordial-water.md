## Ingredients

- Aguardiente
- Lemon peels, defatted, from fifteen very fresh lemons
- Cinnamon, fine
- Coriander, broken up
- Sugar
- Water

## Directions

1. In a jar of aguardiente, place the defatted peels of fifteen very fresh lemons with half an ounce of fine cinnamon and four ounces of broken coriander
2. Leave in infusion for eight days
3. After that time, distill and then mix with three and a half pounds of sugar, dissolved in ten and a half quarts of water
4. Filter through a cloth and bottle
