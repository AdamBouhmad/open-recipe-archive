## Ingredients

- telera bread roll
- pickled chiles
- olives
- sardines
- three or four avocados
- pasilla chile pieces
- chopped onion
- vinegar (optional)

## Directions

1. Open a telera roll and proceed as in the previous recipe
2. Chop the olives and chiles carefully
3. Shred the sardines with a carving knife
4. Add peeled avocado pieces and pasilla chile pieces
5. Add chopped onion and a little vinegar if desired
6. Combine all ingredients well
