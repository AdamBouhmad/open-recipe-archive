## Ingredients

- turkey
- butter
- veal slices
- bacon strips
- broth
- pepper
- salt
- herbs

## Directions

1. After singeing and gutting the turkey, twist its legs and pass through butter
2. Place slices of veal in the bottom of a pot and set the bird on top with the breast down, covering it with strips of bacon
3. Add broth, seasoning with pepper, salt and a bouquet of herbs
4. Cook over gentle heat and when done, strain the broth through a sieve, remove the fat and serve by pouring it over the turkey
