## Ingredients

- quail
- ham slices
- concentrated broth
- wooden wine
- tongue à l'écarlate
- sauce à la financiera

## Directions

1. Cook the quail as described for quail English style, omitting the brains.
2. Arrange them by placing between each quail a piece of tongue à l'écarlate cut in the shape of a crest.
3. Pour sauce à la financiera on top.
