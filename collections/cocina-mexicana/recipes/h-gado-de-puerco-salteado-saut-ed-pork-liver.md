## Ingredients

- pork liver
- lard
- salt
- pepper
- fine herbs
- chives
- flour
- juice
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry in lard, cut into thin slices of equal thickness
2. Make sure to fry on both sides
3. Once fried, dust with salt mixed with pepper
4. Remove from the pan as soon as the slices are well fried
5. In the sauce in which they cooked, add a handful of fine herbs chopped with chives
6. Add half a spoonful of flour, a few spoonfuls of juice, and a little vinegar
7. When the sauce is of good consistency, add the liver slices
8. Let them soak well and serve very hot
