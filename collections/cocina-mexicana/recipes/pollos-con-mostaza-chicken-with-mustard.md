## Ingredients

- Chicken
- Ham
- Chorizo
- Lard
- Water
- Onion
- Garlic
- Mustard

## Directions

1. Cut chicken into quarters and add ham and chorizo slices with a spoonful of lard
2. Fry well and add water
3. In a separate pan, fry a little onion and chopped garlic
4. Grind a little mustard, removing the pungency the same way as for sauce
5. Grind well and add to the fried onion
