## Ingredients

- eggs
- powdered sugar
- orange flower water
- milk
- sugar
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Break the eggs and separate the whites and yolks
2. Beat the whites until they form much foam
3. Add powdered sugar and a little orange flower water
4. In a separate pot cook sufficient milk also with sugar and orange flower water
5. When the milk boils add the whites by spoonfuls
6. Remove them one by one and let them drain on a sieve
7. Remove half of the milk and beat the yolks
8. Add the yolks to the milk
9. Stir with a wooden spoon and remove from heat as soon as they are well bound
10. Place the whites in the plate and pour the sauce passed through a strainer on top
11. Ordinarily twelve eggs and half a pound of sugar are used for half an azumbre of milk
