## Ingredients

- purslane
- lard
- garlic
- onion
- tomatillo
- green chile
- clove
- fresh cheese
- water from cooking the purslane
- jocoque or butter

## Directions

1. Clean the purslane
2. Cook purslane in salted water
3. Drain in a colander
4. Fry lard, garlic, onion, tomatillo, and green chile all chopped in a pot
5. When the seasoning base is half fried, add the drained purslane
6. Season with salt and finish frying the seasoning base with the purslane
7. Add ground clove, crumbled fresh cheese, and some of the water in which the purslane was cooked
8. When seasoned, remove from heat and add a little jocoque or butter, being careful that neither should boil
