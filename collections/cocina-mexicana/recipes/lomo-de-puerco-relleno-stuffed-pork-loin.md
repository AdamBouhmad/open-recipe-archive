## Ingredients

- pork loin
- water
- vinegar
- cooked chard
- tomato
- garlic
- onion
- parsley
- aromatic herbs
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- lard
- cumin
- salt

## Directions

1. Pound the loin and soak in water and vinegar for a while.
2. Chop cooked chard, tomato, garlic, onion, parsley and aromatic herbs; add clove, pepper and ground cinnamon; fry very well in lard and also grind a little cumin.
3. Open the loin lengthwise and fill with the mixture.
4. Sew with thread.
5. Parboil in lard and there add water, salt and more of the filling; cook with little water so it dries well.
6. Turn it to prevent sticking.
