## Ingredients

- white paper
- melted butter
- butter
- bread crumb
- parsley
- onion
- garlic
- salt
- black pepper
- eggs

## Directions

1. Cut a large sheet of white paper into small equal squares and fold them to form little boxes.
2. Butter inside and outside with melted butter.
3. Mix another good piece of butter with half a handful of bread crumb, parsley, onion, a touch of garlic, salt, and black pepper.
4. Distribute this mixture in the bottom of the boxes.
5. Break one egg in each.
6. Season from above with a little salt and fine pepper.
7. Place the boxes on gentle heat on the grill, passing a red-hot shovel over them, and ensure the yolks remain half-soft.
8. Serve in the same boxes.
