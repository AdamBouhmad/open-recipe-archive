## Ingredients

- white sweet potato
- flour
- egg yolks
- lard
- syrup

## Directions

1. After white sweet potato is cooked, peel it and grind it, removing all the fibers
2. Mix with flour and add egg yolks, knead much until it is fluffy, and then form the fritters as best suits
3. Fry in lard and serve with syrup
