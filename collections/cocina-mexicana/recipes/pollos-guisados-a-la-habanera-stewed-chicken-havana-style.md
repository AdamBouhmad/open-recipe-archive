## Ingredients

- chickens
- lard
- ham slices
- green chiles
- parsley
- tomatoes
- garlic
- onions
- cinnamon
- pepper
- cloves
- vinegar
- raisins
- toasted almonds
- capers
- fried plantain slices
- broth

## Directions

1. Pluck the chickens well and singe them with a flaming paper to remove remaining down
2. Open them and remove the entrails, clean well, and cut into quarters
3. Fry in lard with ham slices
4. When half-browned, add chopped green chiles, parsley, tomatoes, garlic, and onions
5. Add cinnamon, pepper, ground cloves, and a little vinegar
6. When the chickens are half-cooked, add raisins, toasted mashed almonds, whole capers, fried plantain slices, and two cups of broth
7. Let simmer until the sauce is thick
