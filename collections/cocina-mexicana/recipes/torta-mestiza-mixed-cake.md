## Ingredients

- eggs
- almonds
- pine nuts
- walnuts
- hazelnuts
- milk
- butter
- sugar
- cinnamon
- syrup

## Directions

1. Beat eggs according to the quantity of cake desired, using more yolks than whites
2. Grind separately almonds, pine nuts, walnuts, and hazelnuts with a little milk so they can be ground
3. Mix each of these things separately with a bit of beaten egg
4. Butter a pan and add a little beaten egg, then layer one portion of each mixture and cover with the remaining egg in small quantity
5. Place the cake in the oven, and once cooked, divide the torta into quarters and place in well-clarified syrup of good consistency
