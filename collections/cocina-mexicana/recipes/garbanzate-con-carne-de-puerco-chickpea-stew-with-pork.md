## Ingredients

- Pork in regular-sized portions
- clean water
- chickpeas
- green chiles, whole
- pieces of cured meat
- ham, ground
- longaniza sausage
- chorizo
- turnips in pieces
- tomato
- onion, chopped
- garlic, chopped
- green chile, chopped
- ancho chile, ground
- saffron, ground
- lemon juice
- fried bread, ground
- salt to taste

## Directions

1. Put the pork in clean water with chickpeas, pieces of cured meat and whole green chiles on the fire.
2. When the meats and chickpeas are cooked, remove some of the chickpeas to grind them.
3. Return the ground chickpeas after mixing with ham, longaniza, chorizo and turnips in pieces, tomato, onion, garlic and green chile, all chopped, and everything fried apart with a bit of ground ancho chile and saffron.
4. Add the juice of a lemon, a fried and ground piece of toast, and season with corresponding salt.
5. For serving at the table, place the portions of pork in the middle of the platter, placing the other meats and turnips around them, and pouring the chickpeas and thick broth over, which will have remained as sauce.
6. Garnish with fried toast around.
