## Ingredients

- Three merlos (blackfish)
- Fine herbs
- Butter
- Salt
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Bread crumbs
- White wine

## Directions

1. Prepare three merlos as in previous articles
2. Place them on a silver plate with fine herbs and butter
3. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg
4. Dust with bread crumbs or ground bread
5. Sprinkle with butter
6. Moisten with white wine
7. Cook in a small stove covered with a dome oven
8. Serve with sauce or light broth
