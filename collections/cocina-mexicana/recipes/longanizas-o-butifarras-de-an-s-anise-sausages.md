## Ingredients

- Three parts meat
- One part pork loin or pork fat
- Salt
- Clove
- Black pepper
- Roasted cilantro
- Whole roasted anise

## Directions

1. Mince three parts meat and one part pork loin or pork fat
2. Season the mixture with salt, clove, pepper, and roasted cilantro, all in powder form, and whole roasted anise
3. After everything is well incorporated, complete the operation as in the preceding article on Longanizas o Butifarras para Camino
