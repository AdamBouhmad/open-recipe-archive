## Ingredients

- One cuartillo of milk
- One pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Sweeten the milk with sugar, strain it and bring to boil until it sounds like taffeta
2. Pour into a bowl or leave in the same pot
3. Beat well, and when cold, shape into flowers or other forms
