## Ingredients

- Thirty apricots (for six persons)
- Red wine (quantity to fill the tureen)
- Sugar
- Cinnamon
- Starch (if needed)
- Bread croutons fried in butter

## Directions

1. Open thirty apricots and break the stones
2. Put all in an earthenware pot over the fire until the fruit is reduced to jam
3. Place a strainer over the soup tureen and pass the apricots through with the help of a spoon until only the broken stones and skins remain
4. Pour as much red wine as the tureen can hold
5. Add sugar and cinnamon in sufficient quantity
6. If the mixture is somewhat watery, thicken it with some spoonfuls of starch
7. Add fried bread croutons in butter
