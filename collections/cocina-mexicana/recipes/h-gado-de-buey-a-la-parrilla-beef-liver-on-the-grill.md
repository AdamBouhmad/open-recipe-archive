## Ingredients

- beef liver
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the liver into thin slices, which are placed on the grill.
2. Dust with salt and pepper and turn to the other side, doing the same, letting them cook very little.
3. Serve two slices at a time, one on top of the other, placing between them a small ball of butter kneaded with parsley.
