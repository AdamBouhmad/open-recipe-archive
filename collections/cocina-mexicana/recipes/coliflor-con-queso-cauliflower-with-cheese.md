## Ingredients

- cauliflower
- grated cheese

## Directions

1. Prepare as the previous one, pass to a platter whose bottom is first covered with a layer of grated cheese, which is also added over the entire surface of the cauliflower
2. Place the platter on the hearth with gentle fire below and much heat on top so the cheese melts and sticks to the cauliflower
3. Serve very hot
