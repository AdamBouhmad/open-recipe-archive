## Ingredients

- one pound of almond
- one pound of sugar
- half pound of preserved lemon
- one ounce of cinnamon powder
- sifted sugar
- cinnamon powder
- orange flower water

## Directions

1. Peel one pound of almond in hot water and grind it by rolling with water so it does not become oily
2. Mix away from the heat with clarified syrup at the soft-ball stage made with one pound of sugar
3. Add half a pound of preserved lemon, removed from its syrup, drained and ground, and one ounce of cinnamon powder
4. Return the mixture to the heat and when it has such consistency that it does not stick to the hands, remove again
5. Empty onto a plate to cool
6. Form the mostachones into whatever shape is desired and place on papers
7. Glaze by beating sifted sugar, cinnamon powder, and orange flower water until the mixture forms threads when lifted
8. Coat the mostachones with a brush
9. Cover with a comal with a little heat to dry them
10. When cold, store in a humid place so they do not harden if they are not to be served immediately
