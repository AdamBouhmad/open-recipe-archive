## Ingredients

- cabbage
- tequesuete
- anise
- butter
- garlic
- cloves
- cinnamon
- saffron
- cumin
- wine
- good vinegar
- plantain
- sweet potato
- olives
- tornachiles

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage coarsely and put to cook with a little settled tequesuete and anise
2. When the cabbage is cooked, fry clean garlic cloves in butter
3. Grind cloves, cinnamon, saffron and cumin and add everything, then add the cabbages with a good portion of wine and another of good vinegar
4. When it is reducing, add slices of plantain and sweet potato, olives and tornachiles
