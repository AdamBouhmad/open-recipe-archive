## Ingredients

- cooked peas
- salt
- ripe tomatoes
- two heads of garlic
- handful of tomatoes
- handful of parsley
- butter
- flour
- one and a half cuartillo of milk
- eight to ten hard-boiled eggs
- Tabasco pepper powder
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cook peas with a little salt.
2. Chop ripe tomatoes, clean garlic heads, tomatoes and parsley very finely.
3. Mix this minced mixture and fry well in butter.
4. Add a little flour, and once fried, add one and a half cuartillo of milk and eight or ten hard-boiled eggs chopped finely, and a little Tabasco pepper powder.
5. Once everything has thickened slightly, add the cooked peas and a glass of white wine.
6. Let everything simmer until it has thickened, remove the pan from heat and cover so it rests.
