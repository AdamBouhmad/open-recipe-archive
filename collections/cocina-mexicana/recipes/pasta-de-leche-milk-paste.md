## Ingredients

- half a pocillo of ground rice
- two cuartillos of milk
- a pound and a half of sugar

## Directions

1. Dissolve half a pocillo of ground rice in two cuartillos of milk sweetened with a pound and a half of sugar
2. Strain the mixture
3. Place on the fire and let it boil until the paste has the consistency of almond paste
4. Set aside and beat, shaping fruits or small figures, which are left to air dry to give them the appropriate color
