## Ingredients

- Truffles
- Butter
- Half a quarter-liter of Jerez wine
- Two tablespoons of Spanish sauce thickened

## Directions

1. Cut truffles into slices or dice and place in a saucepan over gentle heat with a piece of butter
2. As soon as they release their juice, add half a quarter-liter of Jerez wine and two tablespoons of thickened Spanish sauce
3. Let cook over gentle heat
4. When cooked, skim the broth and add it on top with a small piece of butter
