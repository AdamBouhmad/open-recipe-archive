## Ingredients

- mutton rib rack
- breadcrumbs
- pepper
- salt
- oil or lard
- lemon juice

## Directions

1. Take a well-aged mutton rib rack and remove the large bone at the extremity
2. Skin and clean it well, removing all tendons
3. Flatten it with a wet cleaver
4. Remove all the ribs, leaving only the largest one so that a piece of rib remains uncovered to hold it
5. Fry in oil or lard until fried
6. Roll in breadcrumbs and sprinkle with sufficient pepper and salt
7. Place on the grill over gentle fire, adding a little lemon juice from time to time until it forms a crust
