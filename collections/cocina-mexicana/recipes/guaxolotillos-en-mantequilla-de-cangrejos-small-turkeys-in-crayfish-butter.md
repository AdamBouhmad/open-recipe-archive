## Ingredients

- Small turkeys
- Crayfish butter sauce

## Directions

1. Empty and singe them over the flame, gathering the haunches with thread and leaving the feet outside
2. Cook in a good sauté pan
3. Dress and untie them, and pour the crayfish butter sauce over them
