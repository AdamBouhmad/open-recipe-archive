## Ingredients

- zucchini
- eggs
- ground meat
- garlic
- parsley
- breadcrumbs
- hazelnut sauce
- toasted bread

## Directions

1. Cut off the stem and crown
2. With a skewer, drill through the middle to remove the insides
3. Make the filling with eight eggs per pound of seasoned ground meat with garlic, parsley, and breadcrumbs
4. Fill the hollows with this mixture
5. Cook for two hours with broth from the ground meat or stew, taking care they do not fall apart
6. After cooking, pour hazelnut sauce with a little toasted and soaked bread, eggs and spices
7. Shake the pan so they do not scorch and serve hot
8. Poultry meat, sausage, and particularly chicken breast are good for the filling
