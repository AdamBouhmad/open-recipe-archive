## Ingredients

- One ounce of tuberose flowers, cleaned of plant leaves
- Six quartillos of water
- Twelve ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Put one ounce of clean tuberose flowers in a glass
2. Pour six quartillos of water over them, in which twelve ounces of sugar have been dissolved on the fire
3. Dissolve the sugar so that it only warms, without boiling or heating much
4. Keep the flowers in infusion for four hours, stirring the glass from time to time
5. Pass the mixture through a thick cloth
6. Put in the sorbetera to freeze
