## Ingredients

- Beef landrecilla (fatty beef)
- Velouté sauce
- Egg yolks
- Butter
- Beef landrecilla essence
- Breadcrumbs
- Beaten eggs
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Prepare the same as poultry croquettes
2. Use beef landrecilla essence to moisten the sauce instead of poultry essence
3. Use landrecilla meat cut into small dice instead of bird meat
4. Add the fat from the landrecilla
5. Mix with the sauce
6. Let cool
7. Form into balls and coat with breadcrumbs
8. Bread by dipping in beaten eggs
9. Coat with breadcrumbs again
10. Fry and serve with fried parsley
