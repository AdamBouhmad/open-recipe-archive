## Ingredients

- Fish roe
- Hardtack bread
- Salt
- Pepper
- Lard
- Oil
- Vinegar
- Chiles
- Onion
- Olives

## Directions

1. Wash the roe and slice it
2. Grind a piece of hardtack bread with a little salt and pepper
3. Roll the roe slices in this powder
4. Fry in lard over a gentle fire
5. Place on a platter with oil and vinegar
6. Garnish with chiles, onion, and olives
