## Ingredients

- Puff pastry dough
- Filling of choice
- Egg

## Directions

1. With puff pastry dough extended as described in previous articles with three or five turns, form a base
2. Cut in round or oval figures
3. Place pieces of desired filling in the center
4. Cover with other rounds or ovals of the same size
5. Press the edges to seal them well
6. Brush with egg
7. Bake in the oven or cook with two fires
