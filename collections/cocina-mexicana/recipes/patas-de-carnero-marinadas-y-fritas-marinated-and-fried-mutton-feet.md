## Ingredients

- Mutton feet
- Vinegar
- Salt
- Pepper
- Fine herbs
- Small onions
- Water
- Flour
- Butter or oil
- Egg white
- Parsley

## Directions

1. Cook and fry the feet as in the previous recipe.
2. Remove from the casserole and drain.
3. Once completely cold, place in a marinade composed of vinegar, salt, pepper, fine herbs and chopped small onions.
4. A quarter hour before frying, remove from the marinade.
5. Separately prepare a sauce of water, flour and a regular proportion of butter or oil, with salt and pepper, and a beaten egg white to make foam.
6. Roll the feet in this sauce and fry without browning.
7. When serving, sprinkle chopped parsley on top.
