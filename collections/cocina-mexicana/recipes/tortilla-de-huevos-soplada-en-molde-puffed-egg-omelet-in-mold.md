## Ingredients

- six eggs
- three tablespoons of fine powdered sugar
- one tablespoon of potato starch
- four crushed macaroons
- a little salt
- glazed orange blossom, powdered

## Directions

1. Crack six eggs, separating whites from yolks.
2. Add to the yolks three tablespoons of fine powdered sugar, one tablespoon of potato starch, four crushed macaroons, a little salt, and a little powdered glazed orange blossom.
