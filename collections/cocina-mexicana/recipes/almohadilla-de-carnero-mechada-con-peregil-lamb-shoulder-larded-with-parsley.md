## Ingredients

- Lamb shoulder
- Fresh parsley sprigs

## Directions

1. Properly cut the lamb shoulder, removing the lower flaps or skins beneath the loins
2. Lard it throughout with very fresh parsley sprigs
3. Place it to roast on the spit
4. When the parsley is well dried, sprinkle with hot lard, which should be prepared beforehand
5. Continue sprinkling from time to time until the shoulder is cooked
6. Add a little broth or stock to a pan with some chopped onions, salt, and coarse pepper
7. Heat and serve the shoulder over this sauce
