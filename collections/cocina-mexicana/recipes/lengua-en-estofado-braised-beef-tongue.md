## Ingredients

- beef tongue
- water
- vinegar
- salt
- oregano
- thyme
- bay leaves
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- garlic
- ham
- pickled chiles
- olives
- capers

## Directions

1. Put the tongue in boiling water to remove the skin well
2. Once well peeled, wash the throat with the same hot water
3. Once washed, put in cold water and wash again
4. Clean and dry with cloth, place on a cutting block and beat with a mallet or stone to tenderize
5. Lard with clove, cinnamon, pepper, garlic and ham
6. Put in a pot to cook with water, vinegar, salt, oregano, thyme and bay leaves
7. Cook overnight on low heat, covering the pot well so it reduces until little broth remains
8. Decorate with chiles, olives and capers
