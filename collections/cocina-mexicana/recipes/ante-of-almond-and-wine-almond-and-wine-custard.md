## Ingredients

- Four pounds sugar
- Clarified syrup
- One pound ground almonds
- Marquesote cake
- Syrup and wine
- Toasted sesame seeds
- Ground cinnamon
- Pine nuts
- Almonds (for garnish)

## Directions

1. Make very clarified syrup with four pounds sugar
2. When it reaches espejuelo point, add one pound of ground almonds
3. Let it boil until it returns to the same point
4. Remove from heat
5. Place over marquesote cake moistened with syrup and wine
6. Sprinkle each layer with toasted sesame seeds and ground cinnamon
7. Garnish the top with pine nuts and almonds
