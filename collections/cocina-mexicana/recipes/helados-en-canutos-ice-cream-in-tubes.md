## Ingredients

- sugar as needed
- three quartillos of milk
- seven egg yolks
- two ounces ground cinnamon

## Directions

1. Dissolve the corresponding sugar in three quartillos of milk and strain
2. Add seven egg yolks beaten with two ounces of ground cinnamon, mixing well
3. Cook everything until it has the consistency of atole
4. Remove from heat, let cool and pour into tubes, sealing the joints with sealing wax or Campeche wax alone
