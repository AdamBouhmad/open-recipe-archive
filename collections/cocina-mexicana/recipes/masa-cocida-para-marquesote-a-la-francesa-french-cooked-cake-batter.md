## Ingredients

- 1 quart water
- 2 ounces butter
- 1 lemon rind
- a little salt
- 1 quart flour, sifted through silk
- eggs as needed
- candied fruits or preserves, diluted

## Directions

1. Put water, butter, lemon rind, and salt in a saucepan
2. Heat the mixture until it begins to boil
3. Sift flour over the saucepan and add as much as it can absorb
4. When the batter becomes very thick, let it cook, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon
5. Allow to cool
6. Break eggs into it one at a time, mixing each thoroughly, adding more until the batter becomes soft
7. Grease a large saucepan with butter, sized so the batter fills only one quarter of its height initially, as it will fill completely when cooked
8. Place in a hotter oven than for ordinary cakes
9. The cake should be somewhat dry when done
10. Remove from the saucepan and coat the interior with diluted candied fruits or preserves
