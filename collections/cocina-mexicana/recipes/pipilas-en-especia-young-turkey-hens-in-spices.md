## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic cloves
- flour
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- young turkey hens
- broth
- saffron
- clove
- cinnamon
- salt
- oregano
- small chiles
- olives
- onion

## Directions

1. Fry some garlic cloves in lard and brown a little flour in the same place
2. Fry also roasted and ground tomatoes
3. Put in this sauce the young turkey hens divided into regular portions and the broth in which they were cooked
4. Season everything with saffron, clove, cinnamon, salt, oregano, small chiles, olives and quarters of onion
