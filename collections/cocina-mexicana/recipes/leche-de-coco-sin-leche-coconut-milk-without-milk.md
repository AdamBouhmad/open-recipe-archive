## Ingredients

- coconut
- water
- clarified syrup of more than half point
- sugar
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Grate the coconut, then grind it and throw it into boiling water that has been prepared beforehand
2. Strain through a napkin, discarding the pulp, and mix with clarified syrup of more than half point and cold, in proportion of one pound of sugar for each coconut
3. Stir well and add six egg yolks for each pound of sugar and beat much until everything is well incorporated
4. Put the mixture on the fire and let it boil until it reaches the point of separating from the pan and the consistency of milk
