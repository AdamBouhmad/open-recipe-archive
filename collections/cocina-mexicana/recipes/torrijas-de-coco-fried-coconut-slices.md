## Ingredients

- cold sponge cake slices
- thick coconut conserve
- beaten eggs
- lard
- syrup
- Spanish brandy
- cinnamon
- almonds
- raisins

## Directions

1. Place a small amount of thick coconut conserve between two slices of cold sponge cake
2. Coat with beaten egg
3. Fry in lard
4. Boil in syrup mixed with Spanish brandy and cinnamon
5. Serve decorated with almonds and raisins
