## Ingredients

- potatoes
- milk
- orange flower water
- sugar
- egg yolks, beaten
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Cook potatoes whole and peel them
2. Mash them and make a thick dough with milk, orange flower water, sugar, and beaten egg yolks
3. Form into small balls and fry
4. Serve with sugar and a pinch of cinnamon
