## Ingredients

- two quartillos of milk
- two quartillos of custard
- three egg yolks
- four ounces of powdered sugar
- two ounces of scraped chocolate

## Directions

1. Bring two quartillos of milk and two quartillos of custard to a boil together with three egg yolks and four ounces of powdered sugar
2. Dissolve everything well, stirring continuously
3. When it has consumed half its volume, add two ounces of scraped chocolate
4. Allow to boil for a few minutes, strain, and serve when completely cold
