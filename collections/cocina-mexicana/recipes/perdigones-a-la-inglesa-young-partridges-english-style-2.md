## Ingredients

- Young partridges
- Oil
- Salt and pepper
- Spicy sauce or butter for frying

## Directions

1. After cleaning the young partridges and passing them through flames, gut them
2. Divide them lengthwise and tuck their feet
3. Flatten well with a mallet
4. Brush with oil and season with salt and pepper
5. Place on a grill over high heat until roasted
6. Serve with spicy sauce or fried afterwards in butter
