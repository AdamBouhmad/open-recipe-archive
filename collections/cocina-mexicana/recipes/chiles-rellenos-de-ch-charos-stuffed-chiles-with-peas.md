## Ingredients

- Chiles
- Peas
- Exotes or potatoes
- Eggs, semi-beaten
- Flour
- Beaten egg
- Lard
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Tomatoes
- Flour browned in lard
- Small zucchini slices
- Salt

## Directions

1. Prepare the peas with exotes or potatoes as previously described
2. Reduce the sauce more than usual and thicken with semi-beaten eggs
3. When the eggs have set, fill the chiles with this mixture
4. Coat the chiles with flour and dip in beaten egg
5. Fry the chiles
6. Make the sauce as in previous articles but use flour browned in lard instead of fried bread
7. Add slices of zucchini or whole very small ones
8. Place the stuffed chiles in the sauce
