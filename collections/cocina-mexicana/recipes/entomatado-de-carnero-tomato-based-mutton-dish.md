## Ingredients

- tomatoes
- xitomate
- raw onions
- thyme
- ginger
- clove
- pepper
- green chiles
- roasted garlic cloves
- oregano
- nutmeg
- wine
- good vinegar
- mutton meat
- broth from cooking the meat
- small whole onions, cooked separately
- ground cinnamon
- small chiles
- acerninas

## Directions

1. Grind the tomatoes with a little xitomate, raw onions, thyme, ginger, clove, pepper, green chiles, roasted garlic cloves, and a little oregano
2. Fry all the ground mixture
3. Add nutmeg shavings and make a light broth, adding wine, good vinegar, the mutton meat, and a little of its cooking broth
4. Let it thicken as needed
5. Shortly before it finishes seasoning, add the small whole cooked onions set apart
6. Serve sprinkled with ground cinnamon and adorned with small chiles and acerninas
