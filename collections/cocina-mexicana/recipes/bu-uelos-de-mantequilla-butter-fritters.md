## Ingredients

- flour
- butter
- anise water
- salt

## Directions

1. To one pound of flour add a good piece of butter that is to be melted
2. Add a little anise water and salt and knead well
3. Extend the dough on a table and with the mouth of a small glass cut out half moons
4. Fry in butter
5. Then make a thick syrup and put the fritters in it
