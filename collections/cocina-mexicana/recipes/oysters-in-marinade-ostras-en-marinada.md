## Ingredients

- oysters
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- batter or frying paste

## Directions

1. Parboil the oysters and place between two very clean and dry cloths for half an hour
2. Then marinate them with lemon juice, salt, pepper, and nutmeg
3. Coat them in batter or frying paste
4. Fry them
