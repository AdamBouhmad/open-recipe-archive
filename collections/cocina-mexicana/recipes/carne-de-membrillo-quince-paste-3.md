## Ingredients

- Quince juice residue
- Powdered sugar

## Directions

1. After the juice is drained, the residue remaining in the sieve is mixed with powdered sugar
2. Knead and flatten with a rolling pin
3. Cut with the rim of a glass turned upside down
4. Place these circles on paper to roast in the oven
5. Keep them twelve hours in the oven
6. Afterwards wrap them in paper and place them in jars
