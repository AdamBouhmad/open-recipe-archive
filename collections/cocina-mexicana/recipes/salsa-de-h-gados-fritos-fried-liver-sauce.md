## Ingredients

- livers
- bread
- garlic
- onion
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- clove
- pepper
- parsley
- broth
- salt
- vinegar
- oil

## Directions

1. After the livers are well cooked, fry them with a piece of bread, garlic and minced onion
2. Remove the fried ingredients from the pot and grind well with the yolks of two hard-boiled eggs, some cloves, pepper and parsley
3. Dissolve this well-ground mass in broth from the pot, seasoning with salt and adding a little vinegar, and if desired, some oil
4. When properly thickened, the sauce is done
