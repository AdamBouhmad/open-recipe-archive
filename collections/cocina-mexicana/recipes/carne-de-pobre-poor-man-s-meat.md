## Ingredients

- bread
- lard
- ancho chile
- roasted tomatoes
- broth
- cumin
- ay ocotes

## Directions

1. Toast bread in lard
2. Grind with ancho chile and roasted and fried tomatoes
3. Mix this sauce
4. Add broth and season
5. Add cumin and ay ocotes
