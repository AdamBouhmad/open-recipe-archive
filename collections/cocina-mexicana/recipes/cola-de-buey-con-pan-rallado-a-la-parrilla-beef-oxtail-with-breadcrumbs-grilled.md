## Ingredients

- beef oxtail
- water
- salt
- butter
- pepper
- breadcrumbs
- spicy sauce

## Directions

1. Cook the oxtail in a pot with water and salt.
2. Melt butter seasoned with salt and pepper, soak the oxtail in it after drying with a cloth, and then roll it all over with breadcrumbs.
3. Dip again in the butter and roll over the breadcrumbs, then place on the grill.
4. Serve immediately over some spicy sauce.
