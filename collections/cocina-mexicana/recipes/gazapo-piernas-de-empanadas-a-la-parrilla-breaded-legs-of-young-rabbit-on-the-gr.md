## Ingredients

- Rabbit legs
- Bread crumbs
- Melted butter

## Directions

1. Debone the legs
2. Cover with bread crumbs and baste with melted butter
3. Roll again in bread crumbs
4. Cook on the grill
5. Serve on devil sauce
