## Ingredients

- Six pounds sugar
- Six pounds sour cherries with stems cut in half

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup of strong flower point with six pounds of sugar
2. Add six pounds of sour cherries, with stems cut in half, keeping them whole with their pits
3. Give ten or twelve boils with the pot covered
4. Remove from the fire and let rest for half an hour
5. Return to the fire until the syrup reaches strong flower point
6. Retire and store in glass vessels or glazed clay pots
