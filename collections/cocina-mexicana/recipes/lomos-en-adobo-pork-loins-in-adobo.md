## Ingredients

- pork loin
- dry epazote
- spearmint
- oregano
- four garlic cloves
- pepper
- cumin
- bay leaf
- cinnamon
- Castilian vinegar
- salt
- ancho chile
- mulato chile
- lard

## Directions

1. Take a loin and slice it.
2. Add some dry epazote, spearmint and oregano.
3. Grind four garlic cloves, pepper, cumin, bay leaf, cinnamon, Castilian vinegar and the corresponding salt.
4. Grind ancho chile and soaked mulato chile without toasting, and let infuse from one day to the next.
5. The following day, fry in lard.
