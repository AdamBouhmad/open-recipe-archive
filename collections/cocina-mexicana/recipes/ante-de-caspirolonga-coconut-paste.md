## Ingredients

- Two grated or ground coconuts
- Clarified syrup of medium point made with three pounds of sugar
- Ten egg yolks
- A little white wine
- Orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Add two grated or ground coconuts to clarified syrup of medium point made with three pounds of sugar
2. Once everything has come to a boil, remove and let cool
3. Add then ten egg yolks, a little white wine and orange blossom water
4. Return to the fire, and taking the consistency of paste, pour into a platter and brown with a griddle with fire
