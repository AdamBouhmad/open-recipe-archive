## Ingredients

- Twenty-four egg yolks, well beaten
- One pound of ground almonds
- One pound of white sugar
- Lard

## Directions

1. Beat the twenty-four egg yolks well
2. Add one pound of very finely ground almonds and one pound of white sugar
3. Beat everything together
4. Pour into a cooking pot with lard
5. Sprinkle sugar on top
6. Cook over two fires
