## Ingredients

- 2 quarts water
- 4 ounces butter
- 1 lemon rind
- 2 ounces sugar
- a little salt
- flour as needed
- sugar or candied fruits for finishing

## Directions

1. Put butter, lemon rind, sugar, and salt in a saucepan with the water
2. When the water is about to boil, add flour
3. Make the batter as in the preceding article, but with more consistency so it can be handled better
4. Shape it as desired, often in the form of a cabbage or its leaves, from which it takes its name
5. Bake as in the marquesote recipe
6. Coat with sugar or add candied fruits inside
