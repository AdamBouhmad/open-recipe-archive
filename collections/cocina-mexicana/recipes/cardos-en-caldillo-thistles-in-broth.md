## Ingredients

- Thistles
- Meat broth
- Flour
- Pepper
- Nutmeg
- Fine herbs
- Butter

## Directions

1. Remove all leaves and white parts only
2. Peel the stalk and cook in a little meat broth
3. Dissolve a tablespoon of flour in the broth with a little pepper and nutmeg, adding a bundle of fine herbs
4. When cooked, drain and dust with flour
5. Fry in butter
6. Moisten with concentrated broth or cooking broth
7. Fry well, then soak for half an hour in the broth
8. Arrange on a platter and cover with any sauce
