## Ingredients

- pasta de pepita ó de almendra (seed or almond paste)
- almíbar de punto de juntar (sugar syrup at soft-ball stage)
- agua (water)
- bizcocho tostado y molido (toasted and ground biscuit)
- clavo (clove)
- pimienta (pepper)
- canela (cinnamon)
- pasas (raisins)
- piñones (pine nuts)
- almendras (almonds)
- nueces (walnuts)
- ajonjolí tostado (toasted sesame)
- huevo batido (beaten egg)
- manteca (lard)

## Directions

1. Spread the seed or almond paste on a table
2. Cut pieces large enough to imitate chiles
3. Prepare a consistent filling outside the heat made with sugar syrup at soft-ball stage in water and toasted ground biscuit, seasoned with powdered clove, pepper, and cinnamon
4. Add chopped raisins, pine nuts, almonds, walnuts, and toasted sesame
5. Place the filling in the paste pieces
6. Wrap them giving them the shape of chiles
7. Coat with beaten egg
8. Fry following the same procedures as the previous recipe
