## Ingredients

- 1 ounce finely powdered sugar
- a little candied orange blossom (moistened and dissolved in 1 tablespoon water)
- cream or milk

## Directions

1. Put 1 ounce of finely powdered sugar in a pan without water and melt it over a small fire
2. When dissolved and at the breaking point, observe carefully the moment when it becomes a reddish or somewhat high yellow color
3. Then add a little candied orange blossom, moistened and dissolved in 1 tablespoon of water
4. Incorporate everything with cream or milk and conclude the operation as in white tea cream
