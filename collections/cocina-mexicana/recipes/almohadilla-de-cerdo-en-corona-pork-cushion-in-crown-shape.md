## Ingredients

- fresh pork shoulder, legs, and sirloin bones
- sausage meat
- bread crumbs
- egg yolks
- small onions
- butter
- white wine

## Directions

1. Remove from two pieces of fresh pork the shoulder blades, legs, and sirloin bones, and tie them in crown shape so the ribs remain on the outside and the sirloins on the inside
2. Line the interior of the crown with sausage meat mixed with bread crumbs and some egg yolks
3. Arrange these pork quarters on an appropriate pan or sheet and place in the oven for two hours
4. Meanwhile, fry small round onions in butter until they brown, then cook in white wine, reducing to gelatin
5. Remove the crown from the oven and lift from the pan with a large spatula or the lid of a pot
6. Arrange on a plate, placing the onions in the middle, sprinkling and setting the cushion
