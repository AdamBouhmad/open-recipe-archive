## Ingredients

- birds
- pumpkin pieces
- tin pieces
- leek pieces
- chamomile pieces
- squash pieces
- garlic
- onions
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- salt
- wine
- canela

## Directions

1. Place in an earthenware pot pieces of pumpkin, tin, leek, chamomile, and squash if available, with chopped garlic and onions, clove, cinnamon, pepper, salt and sufficient wine, and the birds
2. Cover the pot as for stewing, and when serving add cinnamon
