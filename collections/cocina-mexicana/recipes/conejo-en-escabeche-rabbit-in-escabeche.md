## Ingredients

- rabbit
- bacon
- raw ham
- oil
- thyme
- laurel
- parsley, chopped

## Directions

1. Remove the bones, and lard with bacon and raw ham, close and tie with thread
2. Let braise with oil, thyme and laurel, and when it no longer releases juice, remove and drain, cutting into small pieces that can fit through wide-mouthed bottles
3. When no more fits in one of these, fill with oil and cover with moistened parchment; can be conserved this way for a long time
4. Serve in wheels with chopped parsley and oil
