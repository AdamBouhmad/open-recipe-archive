## Ingredients

- milk
- butter
- sponge cake, toasted in the oven or on a griddle
- curd cheese, crumbled
- raisins
- pine nuts
- almonds, cleaned and broken
- walnuts, cleaned and broken
- toasted sesame seeds
- syrup
- egg yolks
- ground cinnamon
- sifted sugar

## Directions

1. Prepare as for the previous chongos, but before the syrup reaches medium point and off the flame, mix three egg yolks per pound of sugar into it
2. Toast the sponge cake in the oven or on a griddle
3. After the chongos are cooked (but always soft and not watery), and when the boiling has subsided off the flame, cover well with egg yolks beaten and mixed with ground cinnamon and sifted sugar
4. Butter them and cover with a griddle with flame on top to brown the batter
5. When cold, garnish with ground cinnamon and the rest as described in the previous article
6. Can also be made with fresh cheese instead of curd cheese
