## Ingredients

- arroz (rice)
- mantequilla derretida (melted butter)
- azúcar en polvo (powdered sugar)

## Directions

1. Wash the rice in two or three waters and let it drain, then dry it in the sun.
2. When well dried, grind it very well and weigh it.
3. Add half a pound of melted butter for each pound of rice.
4. Beat it well, moistening the masa with warm water if necessary, and continue beating until it reaches the proper point, which is known when a small ball formed from the masa floats on water.
5. Add half a pound of powdered sugar for each pound of rice and mix well.
6. Form the tamales, which can be filled with bienmesabe, manjarblanco, or any other paste.
7. Cook them by steam like the others.
