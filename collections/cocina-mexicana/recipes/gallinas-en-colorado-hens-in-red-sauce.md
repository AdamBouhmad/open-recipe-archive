## Ingredients

- beetroot
- wide chiles
- garlic
- lard
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- ginger
- cooked carrots
- cooked hen

## Directions

1. Cook beetroot and grind it on a metate
2. Soak wide chiles and grind them separately without the skin
3. Toast garlic in lard and remove it
4. In the same lard, add the ground beetroot and chile
5. Add clove, cinnamon, pepper, a little ginger, cooked and finely minced carrots, and the cooked hen
