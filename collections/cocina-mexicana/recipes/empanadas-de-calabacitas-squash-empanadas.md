## Ingredients

- Small squash
- Onions
- Tomatoes
- Garlic
- Green chile
- Salt
- Lard
- Cinnamon
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Saffron
- Dried cilantro

## Directions

1. Peel the squash and chop them, also chop onions, tomatoes, garlic and green chile
2. Cook everything with sufficient salt and plenty of lard
3. When cooked, add a little cinnamon, cloves, black pepper, saffron and dried cilantro, all ground and dissolved in vinegar
