## Ingredients

- nixtamal (corn prepared with lime)
- butter

## Directions

1. Wash the nixtamal and grind it much more than for common tortillas
2. Take small portions of the dough, and on a clean metate, form them with the mano del metate or metate hand, leaving them with wrinkles or small horizontal steps at equal distances as they fall
3. As they dry a little, fry and toast them in hot butter, in which state they are called totoposdes from the Mexican word totopochtic
