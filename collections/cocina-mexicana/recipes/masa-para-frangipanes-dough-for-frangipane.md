## Ingredients

- two or three eggs
- flour
- milk
- salt
- sugar
- candied orange blossom
- almond paste, ground

## Directions

1. Place eggs in a pot with as much flour as they can absorb
2. When well dissolved, finish moistening with milk
3. Let cook for a quarter hour, stirring constantly
4. Add a little salt, sugar, candied orange blossom, and ground almond paste
