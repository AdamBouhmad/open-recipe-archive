## Ingredients

- lard
- garlic cloves
- ancho and pasilla chiles, deveined and soaked
- cooked tomatoes
- toasted bread
- clove
- salt
- cooked onions, separated into leaves
- raisins
- almonds
- sugar
- cooked poultry with sausage
- saucisson, sliced
- broth
- olives
- small chiles

## Directions

1. Fry garlic cloves in lard, and once golden grind together with deveined and soaked ancho and pasilla chiles, cooked tomatoes, toasted bread, and cloves
2. Fry all ground together in lard with salt
3. Once ready, add cooked onions separated into leaves, raisins, almonds, a piece of sugar, the poultry cooked with saucisson sliced, the broth
4. When ready to serve, add olives and small chiles
