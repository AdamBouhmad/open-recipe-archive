## Ingredients

- two pounds of common bread dough
- half pound of ground sugar
- seven egg yolks
- four egg whites
- four ounces of yeast
- half pound of butter

## Directions

1. Mix the common bread dough with the ground sugar, egg yolks, egg whites, yeast, and butter
2. Beat the dough until it makes bubbles
3. Place in paper boxes, higher than those for mamón
4. Grease with butter
5. Let it air dry
6. Place in the oven, taking care that it does not burn
