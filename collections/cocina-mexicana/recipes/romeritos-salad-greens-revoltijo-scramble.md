## Ingredients

- Romeritos (salad greens)
- White tequesquite (alkali)
- Sesame seed pipián sauce (see pipián de ajonjolí, page 661)
- Cooked potatoes, sliced
- Cooked nopal pieces
- Whole shrimp with some of their cooking broth
- Pieces of aguahirtle cake (see torta de aguahirtle)

## Directions

1. Wash the romeritos and cook them in water with a little white tequesquite
2. After cooked, squeeze them by pressing between the hands and put them in a pot
3. Prepare the sesame seed pipián sauce (see pipián de ajonjolí, page 661)
4. Add the romeritos to the prepared pipián with cooked sliced potatoes, cooked and chopped nopal pieces, whole shrimp with some of their cooking broth, and pieces of aguahirtle cake
5. Let everything boil together until the pipián reaches the proper consistency, being careful not to add new broth so it does not break
6. Alternative: sometimes this scramble is made with mole instead, grinding soaked ancho chile with cooked tomatoes and toasted bread, frying the ground mixture in lard with salt, then adding the romeritos with the other ingredients and necessary broth from the shrimp cooking liquid, letting it boil until perfectly seasoned
