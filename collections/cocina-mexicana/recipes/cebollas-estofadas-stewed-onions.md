## Ingredients

- pork or veal
- tomatoes
- clove
- cinnamon
- pepper
- large onions
- water
- vinegar

## Directions

1. Fry pieces of pork or veal in a small pot
2. Add roasted and ground tomatoes with clove, cinnamon and pepper
3. Fry everything very well
4. When well fried, add large onions to parboil them
5. Add water so they cook and add vinegar
6. Remove the meat and serve
