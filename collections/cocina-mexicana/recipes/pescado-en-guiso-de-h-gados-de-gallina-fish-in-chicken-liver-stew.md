## Ingredients

- onion
- roasted and peeled green chile
- very ripe tomato
- tomato
- garlic
- oil
- lard
- chicken livers
- bread
- vinegar
- fish broth
- pepper
- clove
- cinnamon
- salt
- olives
- chiles

## Directions

1. Chop onion, roasted and peeled green chile, very ripe tomato, tomato and garlic, all ground.
2. Fry in equal parts oil and lard.
3. Grind chicken livers with a piece of bread soaked in vinegar, and when the mixture is well fried, mix it in.
4. Loosen everything with broth in which the fish was cooked, add ground pepper, clove and cinnamon, and when very thick, add necessary salt and portions of fish.
5. When finishing, add a little vinegar and oil, olives and chiles.
