## Ingredients

- Four pounds of sugar
- Two cuartillos of white Castilian vinegar

## Directions

1. Make a clarified syrup at the blown-sugar stage with four pounds of sugar
2. Add two cuartillos of white Castilian vinegar
3. At the first boil, remove from heat and pour into a basin
4. Let it cool and bottle immediately
