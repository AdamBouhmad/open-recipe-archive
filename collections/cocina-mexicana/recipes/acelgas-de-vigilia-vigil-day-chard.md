## Ingredients

- Chard
- Shrimp, whole cooked and cleaned
- Chickpeas or potatoes (optional)
- Oil

## Directions

1. Prepare the chard in the same manner as the main chard recipe, with the exception of the bacon pieces
2. In their place, add whole cooked and cleaned shrimp
3. Garnish with chickpeas or potatoes, or prepare without them
4. Add oil
