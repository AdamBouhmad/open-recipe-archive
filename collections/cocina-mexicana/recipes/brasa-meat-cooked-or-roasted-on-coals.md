## Ingredients

- A piece of meat
- Slices of fat or ham, or meat
- A small mixed bunch of herbs
- Parsnips or tomato
- Onions
- Spices
- One glass of white wine
- One glass of broth

## Directions

1. Cover the piece of meat you want to cook with slices of fat or ham, or meat
2. Add a small mixed bunch of herbs, parsnips or tomato, onions and spices
3. Add one glass of white wine and one glass of broth, according to the size of the piece to be cooked and bathed
4. Cook slowly over two gentle fires for many hours until cooking is complete, verifying the process gradually
