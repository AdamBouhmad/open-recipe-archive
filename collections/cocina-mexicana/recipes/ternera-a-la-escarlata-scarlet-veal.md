## Ingredients

- Veal leg
- Ham
- Salt
- Spices
- Thyme
- Basil
- Laurel
- Cloves
- Garlic
- Onion slices
- Linen cloth
- Aromatic herbs
- Spanish sauce

## Directions

1. Take a leg and debone it
2. Lard with ham and coat generously with salt and spices
3. Place in a pot with thyme, basil, laurel, cloves, chopped garlic, and onion slices
4. Cover well with linen cloth so air does not penetrate
5. After three or four days turn it and leave for the same amount of time well covered
6. Remove, wrap in linen cloth which is sewn, then cook in water with onions and a bouquet of aromatic herbs
7. Can be served hot with Spanish sauce
