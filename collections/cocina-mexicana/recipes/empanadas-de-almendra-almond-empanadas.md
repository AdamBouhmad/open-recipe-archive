## Ingredients

- 1 pound of almonds
- 1 pound of sugar
- orange blossom water
- flour
- eggs
- butter
- a little white wine
- lard for frying
- optional: syrup with a little cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of almonds with another pound of sugar, adding orange blossom water while grinding until the paste becomes very smooth
2. Knead flour with eggs, butter, and a little white wine
3. Form empanadas with the dough
4. Fill them with the almond paste
5. Fry in a pan with very hot lard
6. Serve dry or in syrup with a little cinnamon
