## Ingredients

- Compact turnips, not raw
- Lime water, settled
- Clarified syrup at low point

## Directions

1. Select compact turnips that are not raw
2. Peel and place in settled lime water for one or two hours
3. Wash in two or three natural waters
4. Place in clarified syrup at low point
5. Allow to boil until it acquires the consistency of a preserve
