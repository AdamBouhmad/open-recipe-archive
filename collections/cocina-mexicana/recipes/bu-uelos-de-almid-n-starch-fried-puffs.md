## Ingredients

- 3 cups sifted starch
- 3 cups milk
- 6 egg yolks and 1 egg white per cup of milk

## Directions

1. Cook the starch and milk together, stirring until at the right point, which is tested by touching it and if it does not stick to the fingers, it is ready
2. Wrap in a napkin while beating the eggs
3. For each cup add 6 egg yolks and 1 white, beat very well so no lumps remain
4. Fry over low heat and pour syrup and sprinkles on top
