## Ingredients

- four pounds of flour
- eight eggs
- half a pound of lard
- one cup of orange blossom water
- sugar very ground as much as necessary
- one pound of strong leavening
- toasted sesame seeds

## Directions

1. Mix the flour with the eggs, lard, orange blossom water, and necessary sugar
2. Add the strong leavening
3. Knead well
4. When forming the biscuits add toasted sesame seeds
5. Put them in the sun
6. When the dough has risen, put them in the oven until they bake
