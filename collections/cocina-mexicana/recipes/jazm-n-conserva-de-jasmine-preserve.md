## Ingredients

- fresh, recently opened jasmine flowers
- high-point syrup

## Directions

1. Place the flowers away from the heat in high-point syrup
2. Leave them there for twenty-four hours
3. Give them several boils in the same syrup until it reaches the low feather point
4. Let the preserve cool and store it in jars or new pots, sealed hermetically
