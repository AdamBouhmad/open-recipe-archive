## Ingredients

- Corn kernels (elote)
- Sugar
- Lard
- Anise
- Corn husks
- Water

## Directions

1. Take the corn and grind it
2. Add sugar, lard and anise to taste
3. Mix so everything combines
4. Form the small tamales
5. Place corn cobs at the bottom of a pot and some husks over them
6. Add water until covered
7. Place the tamales on top
8. Cover the pot and cook until they are done
