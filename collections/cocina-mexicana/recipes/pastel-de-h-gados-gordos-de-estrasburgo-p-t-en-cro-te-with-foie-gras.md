## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ham
- 1 pound fat ham
- salt
- pepper
- spices
- crushed aromatics
- 2 pounds pastry dough
- fat livers
- truffles
- butter
- bay leaf

## Directions

1. Mince and pound together 1 pound of lean ham and 1 pound of fat ham
2. Season this filling with salt, pepper, spices and crushed aromatics
3. Prepare a pastry mold with 2 pounds of dough, giving it a tall and narrow shape
4. Spread the filling on the bottom and line everything with ham slices
5. Season the fat livers with salt, pepper and spices and garnish them with the filling and truffles
6. Place these livers in the center of the pastry which has been filled with the filling and truffles
7. Place a piece of butter, ham slices and a bay leaf on top
8. Complete the operation as indicated for cold pastry in previous articles
9. Bake in the oven until cooked through
