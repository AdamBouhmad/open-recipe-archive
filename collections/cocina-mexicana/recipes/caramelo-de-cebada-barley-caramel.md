## Ingredients

- barley
- water
- sugar
- lemon
- sweet almond oil

## Directions

1. Take the desired amount of barley and cook it in clear water
2. Once the water has absorbed all its substance, strain through a sieve and pour the strained liquid into clarified sugar syrup
3. Let it cook over strong heat until it reaches the caramel stage
4. Pour into a tin plate or dish oiled with a little sweet almond oil so it does not stick
5. Once it begins to harden, cut with a knife into pieces of the desired size and shape, wrap in paper and store for use
