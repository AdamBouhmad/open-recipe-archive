## Ingredients

- six ounces of sweet almonds
- six ounces of bitter almonds
- one pound of sugar

## Directions

1. Make them the same way as the sweet almond marzipan, with the difference that for six ounces of sweet almonds, add six ounces of bitter almonds and one pound of sugar
