## Ingredients

- sugared water or honey
- cinnamon
- clove
- nutmeg
- white wine

## Directions

1. Prepare water sweetened with sugar or honey, aromatized with cinnamon, clove, or nutmeg, or with a little of all three spices.
2. Mix in equal parts with white wine.
