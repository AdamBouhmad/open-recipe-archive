## Ingredients

- Eight and a half quartillos of milk
- half arroba of fine sugar, strained and cooked
- one small cinnamon stick
- sufficient ground almond
- one sweet potato or mamey, shredded and passed through a metate and strainer

## Directions

1. Cook eight and a half quartillos of milk with half arroba of fine strained sugar and one small cinnamon stick until well sweetened
2. When it reaches medium stage, add sufficient ground almond
3. After the almond has cooked a short while, add one sweet potato or mamey that has been shredded and passed through a metate and strainer
