## Ingredients

- Handful of flour
- Two eggs
- Sufficient salt
- Water
- Lard to grease
- Requesón (curd cheese)
- Chard or cooked onions
- Oil or lard for frying
- Fourteen to sixteen eggs
- Oil or lard

## Directions

1. Form a dough with a handful of flour, two eggs, sufficient salt, and water
2. Knead it and extend it with a rolling pin to form a sheet
3. Grease a baking pan with lard and line it with the dough sheet
4. Grease it with lard and put a well-spread layer of requesón on top
5. Over this place another layer of minced chard or onions
6. Over this mincing pour fourteen or sixteen broken eggs
7. Put another layer of the dough sheet, well greased with lard
8. Multiply the layers until finished
9. Cover everything with one or two layers of the same dough well greased with oil
10. Put the cake in the oven or over two fires until it sets
