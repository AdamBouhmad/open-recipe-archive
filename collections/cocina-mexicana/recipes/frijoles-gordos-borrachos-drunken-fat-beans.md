## Ingredients

- fat beans
- water
- lard
- chopped onion
- beans with their broth
- toasted and ground dried broad chile
- salt
- pulque
- aged cheese, ground

## Directions

1. Cook the beans without tequesquite after washing in natural water, and when cooked and thick, fry chopped onion in lard.
2. Add the beans with their broth, toasted and ground dried broad chile, and the necessary salt, letting them become well refried.
3. Add pulque instead of water and aged ground cheese, making them boil again until well seasoned.
