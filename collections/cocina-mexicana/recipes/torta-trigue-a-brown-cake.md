## Ingredients

- eight quarter liters of milk
- three ounces of cornstarch
- handful of wheat
- handful of almonds
- one ounce of cinnamon

## Directions

1. Add three ounces of cornstarch to eight quarter liters of milk, stirring well until dissolved
2. Add a handful of wheat and another of almonds, both ground, and one ounce of cinnamon, and strain everything
3. Put on the fire and continue as indicated
