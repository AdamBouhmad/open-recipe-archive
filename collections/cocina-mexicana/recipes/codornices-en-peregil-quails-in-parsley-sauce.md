## Ingredients

- Quails
- Lard
- Minced seasoning base
- Parsley, ground and chopped
- Egg yolks
- Clove
- Pepper
- Salt
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. After washing the quails well, place them in a saucepan with a bit of lard, some minced seasoning base, parsley ground and also chopped, egg yolks, clove and pepper, the necessary salt, oil and vinegar.
2. Cover the pot with a comal.
3. Remove when tender.
