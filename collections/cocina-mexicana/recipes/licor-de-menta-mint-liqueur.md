## Ingredients

- 8 quartillos brandy
- 12 ounces fresh mint
- peels of 3 lemons
- 4 quartillos distilled water
- 1 drachm pure mint essence
- 3 pounds sugar

## Directions

1. Distill the mint with the brandy and peels to extract about 4 quartillos of liqueur, in which dissolve the mint essence.
2. Dissolve the sugar with the water on the fire, let cool, make the mixture and filter.
