## Ingredients

- Small sturgeon
- Light broth or light sauce for fish
- Ham, grated or butter
- Abundant aromatics
- Salt
- Italian sauce
- Butter

## Directions

1. Choose a small sturgeon and clean it by removing the entrails through the gills and anus, removing the gills.
2. Lift some cuts or planks from both sides by introducing the knife between the flesh.
3. After washing it well and draining it, arrange it in a fish platter with a good light broth (either with grated ham or with butter).
4. Season with abundant aromatics and salt, and place it to cook over two fires, sprinkling it frequently.
5. After cooking, let it drain and serve with Italian sauce mixed with its own broth, letting this consume into it, and adding a piece of butter.
6. This sauce is served separately in a sauce boat.
