## Ingredients

- cooked cod
- butter
- parsley
- onions
- salt
- whole pepper
- lemon

## Directions

1. After cooking and draining the cod, place it in a pan with some butter, parsley and chopped onions, salt and whole pepper
2. Melt the butter over fire and once well mixed and the cod thoroughly impregnated with it, serve with lemon juice
