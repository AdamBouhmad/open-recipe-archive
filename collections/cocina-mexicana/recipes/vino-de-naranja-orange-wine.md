## Ingredients

- Oranges
- Sugar
- Aguardiente
- Water (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare as with Vino de Limón; but since its juice is less acidic than that of lemon, reduce the quantity of sugar, and perhaps it would not be inappropriate in certain cases to add a little water to the juice and increase the dose of aguardiente
