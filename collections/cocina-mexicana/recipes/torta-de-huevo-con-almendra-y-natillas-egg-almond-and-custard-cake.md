## Ingredients

- one quarter pound of good, clean almonds, coarsely ground
- a little orange blossom water
- six beaten eggs
- one teacup of custard
- a little fine ground sponge biscuit
- ground sugar to taste
- butter for greasing
- ground sponge biscuit for dusting

## Directions

1. Grind one quarter pound of good, clean almonds, leaving it somewhat coarse
2. Sprinkle with a little orange blossom water
3. Mix with six eggs beaten for cooking
4. Incorporate one teacup of custard, a little fine ground sponge biscuit, and sugar to taste
5. Grease a pot with a little butter and dust with finely ground sponge biscuit
6. Pour in the almond mixture and dust the top with ground sugar
7. Brush with butter and allow to set over two fires
