## Ingredients

- ripe plantains
- beaten eggs
- lard
- syrup
- toasted sesame seeds
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Slice ripe plantains lengthwise
2. Coat each slice with well-beaten egg
3. Fry in lard
4. Boil in syrup
5. Decorate with toasted sesame seeds and dust with cinnamon
