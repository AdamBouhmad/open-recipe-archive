## Ingredients

- veal pulp
- roasted tomatoes
- finely chopped onions
- oregano
- vinegar
- oil
- olives, pitted
- small chiles in vinegar, chopped
- roasted poblano or cuaresmeño chile strips
- capers

## Directions

1. Cook the veal pulp well.
2. Shred the cooked veal.
3. Grind roasted tomatoes in sufficient quantity.
4. Add finely chopped onions, oregano, vinegar, considerable oil, olives halved, small chiles in vinegar chopped, strips of roasted poblano or cuaresmeño chiles, and capers.
5. Mix all with the shredded meat.
6. Place on a plate.
