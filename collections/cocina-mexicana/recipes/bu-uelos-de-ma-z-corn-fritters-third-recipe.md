## Ingredients

- corn
- salt
- butter
- whole anise
- eggs

## Directions

1. Wash corn well until very white and strain as for atole
2. Let settle overnight and the next day discard the water and the sediment that remains
3. Add salt and butter, cook in a clay pot without ceasing to stir
4. Once a crust forms on the bottom, scrape the dough to remove it all; spread a little butter on it and continue this way until the dough is cooked
5. When it can be grasped and after it has cooled a little, add some whole anise seeds and eggs with yolks and whites until it is clear the dough will not take more
6. Once this is done fry the little rings and pour honey over them; for these, sugarcane honey is better; for the others, sugar honey
