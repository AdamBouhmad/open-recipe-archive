## Ingredients

- Rice
- Milk
- Salt
- Sugar syrup with clarified white sugar
- Flour
- Lard
- Powdered sugar
- Powdered cinnamon
- Orange leaves

## Directions

1. Wash the rice in two or three waters so it does not break
2. Place it in milk with a little salt
3. Add sugar syrup made from well-clarified white sugar until the rice cooks well
4. Once cooked, let it reduce until it becomes very thick
5. Pour it onto a platter
6. The next day, take portions of this rice and form the shapes desired
7. Roll them in flour
8. Fry them in lard until they are golden
9. Sprinkle powdered sugar and cinnamon on them
10. Place them on a platter over orange leaves and cover with more orange leaves
