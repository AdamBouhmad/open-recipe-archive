## Ingredients

- hare, cleaned
- lard or oil
- salt
- garlic
- onion
- black pepper grains
- cloves
- hot pepper, crushed
- rice
- water

## Directions

1. Put a pot with lard or oil, salt, garlic, a little onion, grains of black pepper, cloves and crushed hot pepper.
2. Add the rice after it has been well moistened with water and moisten it again.
3. When fried, add water in such a way that the rice remains whole.
4. Serve when cooked.
