## Ingredients

- Ham slices
- Butter
- Roasted tomatoes, ground
- Garlic
- Chorizo, sliced
- Clove, ground
- Black pepper, ground
- Cinnamon, ground
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Wine
- Salt
- Prepared larded calf's tongue

## Directions

1. Fry ham slices in butter and remove when done
2. In the same butter, fry roasted tomatoes ground together with garlic
3. Add the fried ham and chorizo slices, ground clove, pepper, and cinnamon, raisins, almonds, wine, and salt
4. Place the prepared tongue on top, arranged as in the previous recipe
5. Let the broth thicken on two fires
