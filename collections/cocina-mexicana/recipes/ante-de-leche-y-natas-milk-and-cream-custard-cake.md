## Ingredients

- milk
- ground cinnamon
- whole cloves
- a little orange flower water
- sponge cake (mamón)
- syrup
- ground flavoring pastille

## Directions

1. Boil milk and gradually remove the cream, adding it back when very thick
2. Add sufficient ground cinnamon so its color stands out, some whole cloves, and a little orange flower water
3. Layer over sponge cake moistened in syrup and place in the oven or over two fires so that it sets
4. Sprinkle with ground flavoring pastille
