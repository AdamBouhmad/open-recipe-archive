## Ingredients

- pork offal
- ham
- spices
- thyme
- salt
- vinegar
- pork caul
- squash seed powder, peeled, toasted and ground
- lard

## Directions

1. Lard the offal as you would meat for a stew, with ham and spices
2. Add thyme, salt and vinegar on top
3. Dust a pork caul with ground squash seed powder
4. Wrap the offal in it
5. Place it in a casserole greased with lard
6. Bake in the oven until cooked
