## Ingredients

- crumb of fresh bread
- cream
- butter
- lemon zest
- sugar
- Corinth raisins

## Directions

1. Take the crumb of fresh bread
2. Pour it into boiling cream
3. Stir while it boils
4. Add a piece of butter, lemon zest, sugar if desired, and Corinth raisins to the cream
5. Finish in the same manner as the rice cake
