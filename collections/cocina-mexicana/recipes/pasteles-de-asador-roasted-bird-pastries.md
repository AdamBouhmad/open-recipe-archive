## Ingredients

- Rice flour, about half a pound
- Eight to ten egg yolks
- Half a pound of sugar
- Wine as needed
- Squab or similar bird
- Salt
- Fine spices, ground
- Lard
- Sifted sugar
- Cinnamon

## Directions

1. Beat rice flour with eight or ten egg yolks and half a pound of sugar
2. Moisten with necessary wine to make it spreadable and mix everything well
3. Take a squab or equal bird, remove the neck and wings cleanly
4. Coat with salt and finely ground fine spices
5. Wrap in paper greased with lard and spit-roast over fire until cooked
6. Remove the paper and let it brown
7. Coat with the flour and egg mixture, place by the fire
8. As it dries, alternately apply baths of lard and batters using feathers until the bird is well covered
9. Give a final bath of lard, and when dry, dust with sifted sugar mixed with cinnamon
10. Serve
