## Ingredients

- clean chickens
- ham slices
- pepper
- cloves
- cinnamon
- garlic
- water
- pork picadillo
- lard
- sliced garlic

## Directions

1. After cleaning well, put chickens in a deep pot with ham slices, pepper, cloves, cinnamon, garlic and a little water
2. Let them cook over two fires
3. Remove and drain in a napkin
4. Fill with pork picadillo
5. Put them in a pot with lard and sliced garlic
6. Fry until golden
