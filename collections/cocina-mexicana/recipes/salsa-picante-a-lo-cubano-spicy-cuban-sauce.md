## Ingredients

- juice of two lemons
- half glass vinegar
- spicy dates finely minced
- pepper
- half bunch of parsley
- four or five garlic cloves
- one bay leaf
- small quantity of cumin
- two cups broth with substance
- four tablespoons habanera sauce
- toasted flour optional

## Directions

1. Express the juice of two lemons
2. Mix with half glass of vinegar
3. Add finely minced spicy dates
4. Add a pinch of pepper, half bunch of parsley, four or five garlic cloves, one bay leaf, and a small quantity of cumin
5. Put everything in a pan and bring to boil for the time necessary until it reduces to half
6. Then add about two cups of broth with substance and four tablespoons of habanera sauce
7. Let it cook a little to thicken
8. If you want it to thicken faster, add a little toasted flour
