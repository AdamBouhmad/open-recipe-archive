## Ingredients

- half a pound of sugar made into clarified syrup of medium point
- sixteen beaten egg yolks
- milk
- almonds finely ground

## Directions

1. Add the beaten egg yolks to the syrup
2. Place over heat stirring constantly
3. Cook until it releases from the pan sides
4. Can also be made adding milk instead of water and some finely ground almonds
5. Shape the mixture into small egg forms
6. Roll in powdered sugar
7. Once cooled place each on separate papers
