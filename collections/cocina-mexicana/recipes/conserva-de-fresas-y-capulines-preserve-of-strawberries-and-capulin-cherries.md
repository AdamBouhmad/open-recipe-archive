## Ingredients

- Half pound of selected strawberries
- Half pound of capulines
- Three pounds of sugar for every pound of juice

## Directions

1. Remove stones and stems from the fruit
2. Crush all the fruit well
3. Press the juice through a cloth or sieve
4. Reduce over moderate heat
5. Clarify and cook in sugar
6. Let boil for several minutes, stirring and removing until it hardens
7. Put in paper boxes or prepared jars
