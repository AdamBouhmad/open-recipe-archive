## Ingredients

- Four quartillos of slightly sweetened milk
- Two quartillos of flour
- Butter
- Eggs
- Biscuit or toasted and ground bread

## Directions

1. Mix four quartillos of slightly sweetened milk with two quartillos of flour
2. Strain through a sieve
3. Place over heat until it reaches the consistency of cajeta, or until the paste pulls away from the pan when stirred
4. Remove from fire and add half a tablespoon of butter
5. Pour onto a flat platter or clean table, giving it the thickness desired for the fritters
6. Cut the fritters after the paste has cooled
7. Bathe them with half-beaten egg
8. Roll them in ground biscuit or toasted and ground bread
9. Fry them
10. They can be served immediately, but they are better if left from one day to the next
