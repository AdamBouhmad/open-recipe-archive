## Ingredients

- Cooked xitomatl (tomatoes)
- Pimienta (pepper)
- Cloves
- Cumin
- Oil
- Small cooked onions
- Oregano
- Salt
- Cooked or roasted poultry or fish
- Parsley, chopped very finely
- Chiles
- Olives

## Directions

1. Grind cooked xitomatl with pepper, cloves and cumin
2. Add oil and beat well
3. Separate small cooked onions and sprinkle with sufficient oregano
4. Season with salt
5. With this broth coat the cooked or roasted poultry or fish and place in the steam of a pot
6. When serving, pour oil over the top
7. Sprinkle with finely chopped parsley, chiles, olives and other garnishes
