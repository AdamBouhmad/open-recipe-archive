## Ingredients

- three pounds of sugar
- one grated coconut
- half a pineapple, ground
- six egg yolks, lightly beaten

## Directions

1. Clarify three pounds of sugar, strain and place over heat
2. Have a peeled and grated coconut ready
3. Let the honey come to a boil two or three times and add the coconut
4. When it becomes half thick, add the ground pineapple and keep over heat until when stirred the bottom of the pan is visible
5. Remove from heat and let stand until the next day, then add six lightly beaten egg yolks
6. Stir with the paste until well incorporated
7. Place over heat and stir constantly until the bottom of the pan is visible again
8. Pour onto platters and immediately place a griddle with heat on top to brown
