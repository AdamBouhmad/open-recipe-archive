## Ingredients

- pineapple juice
- six beaten eggs
- one and a half cups of sugar
- two sticks of ground cinnamon
- three tablespoons of butter
- one panetela
- half a glass of Malaga or Muscatel wine

## Directions

1. Use the same ingredients as for the coconut pudding, made in the same manner
2. The only difference is that instead of coconut milk, use pineapple juice and add half a glass of Malaga or Muscatel wine to the dough
