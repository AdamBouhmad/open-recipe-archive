## Ingredients

- 8 lemons
- 5 quartillos of spring water
- 8 ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Scrape eight lemons as finely as possible; place the scrapings in five quartillos of spring water for two hours, to give it flavor
2. Cut the lemon pulp in half and squeeze the juice into the same water, and killing the lemon scraping with eight ounces of sugar, mix everything with the water, and stir with the spatula or spoon over the fire
3. When the sugar is well melted, pass the mixture through a very clean cloth, and proceed as for the ice creams of the previous article
