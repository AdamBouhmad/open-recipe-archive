## Ingredients

- half pound cooked rice
- half pound pork loin cooked with sweets and chopped as for pastel
- ten beaten eggs
- sugar
- clove
- cinnamon
- saffron
- ham
- sausages, cooked

## Directions

1. Mix half pound of cooked rice with another half of pork loin, cooked with sweets and chopped as for pastel, with ten beaten eggs, first the whites and then the yolks, with sugar, clove, cinnamon and saffron, all ground, and finally with cooked ham and sausages
2. Put in a casserole greased with butter and let it cook over two fires, adding afterwards on top a little beaten egg white
3. Serve dry
