## Ingredients

- Young tender rabbit
- Parsley
- Shallots
- Mushrooms
- Garlic
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Butter
- Ham
- White oiled paper

## Directions

1. Scald a tender young rabbit and divide it into portions, removing the largest bones
2. Marinate with minced parsley, shallots, mushrooms and a bit of garlic, salt, black pepper and butter
3. Wrap each piece with its seasoning and a thin slice of ham in white oiled paper
4. Cook over very gentle heat on a grill, placing another sheet of greased paper underneath the wrapped pieces
5. Serve without removing the paper
