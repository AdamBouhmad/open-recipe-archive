## Ingredients

- Lamb necks
- Onions
- Parsnips
- Warm butter
- Bread crumbs
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Cook the lamb necks over coals (see braising method) with onions and parsnips, etc.
2. Bread them by dipping in warm butter and rolling successively in crumbled bread crumbs mixed with salt and pepper.
3. Place on the grill and serve with their own juice.
