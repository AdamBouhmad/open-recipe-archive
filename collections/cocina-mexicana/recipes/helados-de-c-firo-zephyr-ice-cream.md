## Ingredients

- pomelo scrapings
- orange scrapings
- lemon peel scrapings
- vanilla
- 1.5 quartillos of milk
- 8 ounces of cream
- 12 ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Mix the scrapings of a pomelo and an orange, with the grated peel of a lemon, cut into small pieces a vanilla, and place to boil with a quartillo and a half of milk, eight ounces of cream and twelve ounces of sugar, stirring until the mixture becomes quite thick
2. Remove it then and after being cold, freeze it
