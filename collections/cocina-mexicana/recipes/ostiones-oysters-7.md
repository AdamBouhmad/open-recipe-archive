## Ingredients

- tomato
- onion
- parsley
- butter
- Spanish oil
- capers
- olives
- oysters
- salt

## Directions

1. Chop finely much tomato, onion, and parsley.
2. For one can of oysters use two ounces of butter to fry; when browned incorporate half a cup of Spanish oil.
3. When very hot fry the chopped mixture in this blend, adding many capers and olives.
4. When all is fried add the oysters with salt if needed and leave on low heat to thicken.
