## Ingredients

- calf rennet
- butter
- mushrooms
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- egg yolks
- lemon juice
- fried croutons

## Directions

1. Cook the rennet as stated for the Provençale style, cut it into small rounds and put it in a saucepan with butter, mushrooms, minced parsley, salt, pepper and nutmeg
2. Let everything boil and then thicken with egg yolks; add lemon juice and dress with fried croutons on the edge of the plate
