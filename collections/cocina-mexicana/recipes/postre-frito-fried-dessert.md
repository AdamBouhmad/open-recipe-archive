## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon
- flour
- lard or butter

## Directions

1. Make the common milk dessert, but brought to a very high point, and empty it into a shallow dish, keeping it for the next day
2. Cut slices of the dessert from it, roll them in flour, fry them in lard or butter, arrange them on the platter and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
