## Ingredients

- pine nuts
- roasted tomatoes
- lard
- salt
- cloves
- cinnamon
- pepper, all ground
- wine
- chicken broth
- vinegar
- cooked chicken

## Directions

1. Grind pine nuts and roasted tomatoes together
2. Fry them in lard with salt
3. Add ground cloves, cinnamon, and pepper
4. Add wine, chicken broth, and a bit of vinegar
5. Place the cooked chicken in this sauce
6. Let simmer until everything is well seasoned
