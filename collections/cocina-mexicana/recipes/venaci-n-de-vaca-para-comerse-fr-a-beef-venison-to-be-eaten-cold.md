## Ingredients

- Beef leg pulp
- Butter
- Oil
- Bay leaves
- Garlic, crushed
- Peppercorns, whole

## Directions

1. Divide the pulp of a beef leg into three pieces
2. Bruise with the flat part of a cleaver without grinding the meat
3. Clean without water so no skin remains
4. Put to parboil in a pot with butter and oil, three bay leaves, crushed garlic and whole peppercorns
