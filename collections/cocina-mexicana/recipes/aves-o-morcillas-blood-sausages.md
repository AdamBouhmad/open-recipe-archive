## Ingredients

- blood
- fat, minced
- onion and tomato, minced
- dried pasilla chile, toasted
- nutmeg
- peeled and split almonds
- pine nuts
- peanuts
- raisins
- spices

## Directions

1. Place blood, finely minced fat, and minced onion and tomato in a pan
2. Rinse the tomatoes slightly to remove the seeds
3. Mix everything very well and add a pinch of toasted dried pasilla chile, a piece of nutmeg, peeled and split almonds, pine nuts, peanuts, raisins, and ground spices
4. Fill casings loosely
5. String on a stick and place in a pan with boiling water
6. Prick with a pin so they do not burst
