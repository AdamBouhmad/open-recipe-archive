## Ingredients

- venison
- butter
- garlic
- sherry wine
- bay leaves
- peppercorns
- potatoes

## Directions

1. Place a little butter in a casserole and add the meat with necessary salt and two or three crushed garlic cloves, a little sherry wine, bay leaves and peppercorns.
2. Place in the oven after seasoning inside and outside and adding peeled raw potatoes.
