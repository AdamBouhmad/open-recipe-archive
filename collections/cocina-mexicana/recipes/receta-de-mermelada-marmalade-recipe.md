## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- lemon
- one pound of quince, cooked and sifted
- two apples, cooked and sifted

## Directions

1. Clarify two pounds of sugar with lemon
2. Give it caramel point and remove from heat
3. When it has cooled a little, add one pound of quince and two apples, all cooked and sifted through a sieve
4. Beat well until it incorporates into the syrup without putting it on the fire
5. Once incorporated, put in glass or glazed vessel and place in the sun to whiten
6. It will last up to one year
