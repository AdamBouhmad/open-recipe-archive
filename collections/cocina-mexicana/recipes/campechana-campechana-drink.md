## Ingredients

- Twelve quartillos of pulque
- Six quartillos of water
- One unit of white panela (cane sugar)
- Cloves, peppercorns, and cinnamon (ground)
- Half a pineapple

## Directions

1. Grind all ingredients well together
2. Let ferment
3. Strain
