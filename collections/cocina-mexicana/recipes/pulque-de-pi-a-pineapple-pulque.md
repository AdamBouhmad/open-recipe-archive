## Ingredients

- pineapples
- pulque
- cinnamon
- pepper
- clove

## Directions

1. Peel and grind the pineapples
2. Then grind them again with the peels
3. Strain through a sieve with the pulque
4. Sweeten to taste
5. Add small amounts of cinnamon, pepper and clove
6. The greater the quantity of pineapple, the better the pulque turns out
