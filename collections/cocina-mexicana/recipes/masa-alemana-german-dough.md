## Ingredients

- flour (divided into four parts)
- one-third of the flour weight in butter
- one-sixth of the flour weight in sugar
- one-sixth of the flour weight in currants or Muscatel raisins
- one-sixth of the flour weight in almonds cut into strips
- salt
- cream
- egg

## Directions

1. Set aside one-quarter of the flour to serve as leavening for the other three-quarters
2. Place flour in a bowl with butter, sugar, currants or Muscatel raisins, and sliced almonds
3. Salt this dough as done with roll dough
4. Mix with a little cream and egg
5. When smooth, add the leavening and mix everything well
6. Place in a buttered mold
7. Let rise or ferment for many hours
8. Bake in the oven like rolls
