## Ingredients

- carrots
- jicama
- syrup
- ground sponge cake
- cinnamon
- toasted sesame

## Directions

1. Core the carrots, and chopped very fine mix them with an equal amount of grated jicama
2. Put everything in syrup with ground sponge cake, and let it take the appropriate point
3. Empty into a platter and decorate with cinnamon and toasted sesame on top
