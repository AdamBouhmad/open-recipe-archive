## Ingredients

- four ripe pineapples
- one pound of sugar
- clarified syrup
- juice of two lemons
- one glass of water

## Directions

1. Peel four ripe pineapples
2. Grind them and add to clarified syrup at the point where a thread forms when a small amount is taken between two fingers
3. The syrup is made with one pound of sugar
4. Leave in infusion for three hours to absorb the aroma of the fruit well
5. Add the juice of two lemons
6. Strain through muslin or a sieve, pressing with a wooden spatula to extract all the pulp from the pineapples
7. Add one glass of water
8. Freeze in molds of the same shape as the pineapple, in the same manner as explained
