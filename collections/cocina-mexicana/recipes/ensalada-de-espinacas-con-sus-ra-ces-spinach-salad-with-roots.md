## Ingredients

- spinach
- oil
- garlic
- vinegar
- water
- pine nuts
- raisins

## Directions

1. Clean the spinach well and wash, but leave the roots on
2. Fry in oil with some clean, chopped garlic cloves
3. When fried, add a little vinegar diluted with boiling water
4. Add toasted pine nuts and pitted raisins
5. Let it season and serve hot
