## Ingredients

- two quartillos of milk
- cinnamon sticks
- sugar
- egg yolks
- water

## Directions

1. Bring two quartillos of milk to boil with cinnamon sticks and sweeten to taste
2. Set aside about one large cup and dissolve a little syrup in it, then mix with the rest
3. Allow to cool, then return to heat until it thickens
4. Remove from heat and let cool completely
5. Mix in several egg yolks that have been broken but not beaten, to give a good yellow color
6. Return to fire until it thickens enough to form a skin
7. When very cold, cover the milk well with finely ground sugar
8. Burn the top with a hot iron blade
