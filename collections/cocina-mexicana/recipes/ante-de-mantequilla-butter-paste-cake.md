## Ingredients

- frozen butter
- cold water
- sponge cake (mamón)
- honey at point
- red pastille powder
- milk
- egg yolks
- sweetener
- orange blossom water
- starch
- sesame seeds, toasted
- almonds
- candied citron
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Melt the frozen butter and freeze it again in cold water
2. Place over sponge cake
3. Pour honey at point over it and sprinkle with red pastille powder
4. Add twelve egg yolks to one cuartillo of milk with sweetener, orange blossom water and a little starch, stirring
5. Cook until it becomes thick like atole
6. Alternate layers of sponge cake and toasted sesame seeds in the dish
7. Top with almonds, candied citron and cinnamon
