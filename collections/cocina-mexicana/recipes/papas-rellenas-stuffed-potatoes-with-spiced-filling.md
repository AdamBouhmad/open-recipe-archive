## Ingredients

- Whole potatoes with skin
- Garlic, onion and parsley minced very fine
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- A little oregano, all ground
- Brains minced
- A little oil
- Tomato
- Onion
- Garlic
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- A little vinegar
- Pork broth

## Directions

1. Cook the whole potatoes with skin and then peel them, remove a small crown and with a knife scoop out the paste to fill them as follows
2. Fry minced garlic, onion and parsley very fine, add ground clove, cinnamon, pepper and a little oregano
3. In this preparation add some minced brains and a little oil and fill the potatoes, covering them with the small crown that was removed
4. Put them on the serving platter and pour over them the following sauce which should already be made
5. Fry tomato, onion and garlic, dust with clove and cinnamon, add a little vinegar and pork broth, cook until seasoned and when serving the potatoes pour it over them
