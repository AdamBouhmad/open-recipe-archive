## Ingredients

- Two mutton pulps
- Two pork head loins
- Capers
- Fatty ham
- Parsley
- Portion of almonds
- Peeled garlic cloves
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper
- Saffron
- Tomato
- Parsley, minced
- Four to six egg yolks
- Salt
- Raisins
- Almond pieces
- Candied citron pieces
- Boiled egg pieces
- Ham pieces
- Breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Mince two mutton pulps and two pork head loins
2. Add abundant capers, fatty ham, parsley, a portion of almonds and peeled garlic cloves
3. Mince again with these additions
4. Add clove, cinnamon, pepper and saffron all ground, tomato and minced parsley
5. Mix everything, adding four to six egg yolks and the proper salt until forming a dough
6. Make large balls filled with raisins, pieces of almond, candied citron, boiled egg and ham
7. Cook in boiling water, and when well cooked, bathe with beaten egg yolks
8. Roll in breadcrumbs, fry, and serve dry or in broth
