## Ingredients

- red cabbage
- 6 pears or sour apples per cabbage
- 4 ounces of butter
- salt
- pepper
- a quarter liter of water

## Directions

1. Wash and slice the cabbage
2. Clean 6 pears or sour apples for each cabbage
3. Cook the two things together for one hour over a slow fire in a pan with 4 ounces of butter, salt, pepper, and a quarter liter of water
