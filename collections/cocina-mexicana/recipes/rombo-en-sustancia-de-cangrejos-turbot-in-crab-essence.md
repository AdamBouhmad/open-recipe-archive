## Ingredients

- Turbot
- Two or three pounds of sliced beef
- Ham
- Salt
- Pepper
- Parsley
- Fine herbs
- Onions studded with cloves
- Two bay leaves
- Fresh butter
- Flour
- Beef broth
- One bottle of Champagne or other wine
- Crab stew

## Directions

1. Place two or three pounds of sliced beef larded with ham in a pan with salt and pepper, a small bundle of parsley, fine herbs, onions studded with cloves, and two bay leaves
2. Sweat them over the fire, and when everything is stuck, add fresh butter with a little flour
3. Having made this red base thus, moisten with broth and scrape the bottom with the spoon
4. Wrap the turbot in ham slices and place to cook with a bottle of Champagne or other wine and with the beef base, placing the same beef on top
5. When the turbot is cooked, let it season over the embers, then dress it in the fish platter and serve with crab stew on top, bound with the substance of the crabs themselves
