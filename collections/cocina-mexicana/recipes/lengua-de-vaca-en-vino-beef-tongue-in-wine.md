## Ingredients

- Beef tongue
- Salt
- Small whole onions
- Cooked ham
- Ham slices
- Sausage slices
- Chorizo, cooked
- Cloves
- Black pepper
- Cinnamon, ground
- Parsley
- Oregano
- Flour, browned in lard
- Capers
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Málaga or Parras wine
- Broth from cooking the tongue

## Directions

1. Beat the tongue against a stone, wash thoroughly, and place to cook with salt
2. Once cooked, remove the skin and divide into regular portions, allowing one or two portions per plate
3. Lard the portions with cooked ham or raw lard
4. Arrange in a casserole with small whole onions cooked separately, ham slices, sausage slices, cooked chorizo, cloves, ground pepper and cinnamon, parsley leaves, some oregano, flour browned in lard, capers, raisins, and almonds
5. Cover the tongue with all these ingredients, add Málaga or Parras wine and a little of the broth in which the tongue was cooked
6. Let the broth reduce until somewhat thick and serve the tongue with it
