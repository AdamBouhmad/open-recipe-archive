## Ingredients

- cooked tongue
- adobo from pork loin recipe
- lard
- tongue broth

## Directions

1. Once cooked, skinned and sliced
2. Marinate with the adobo from the pork loin recipe
3. Burn lard in a cazuela and fry everything
4. Add tongue broth
5. Let consume over two fires
