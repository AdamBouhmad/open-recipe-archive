## Ingredients

- four egg yolks
- one cup flour
- a little salt
- two cups milk
- lard for coating the pan

## Directions

1. Mix four egg yolks with one cup of flour and a little salt
2. Beat the mixture and incorporate two cups of milk
3. Ensure everything is well blended and without lumps
4. Pour into a pan coated with lard
5. Proceed as explained in the following article
