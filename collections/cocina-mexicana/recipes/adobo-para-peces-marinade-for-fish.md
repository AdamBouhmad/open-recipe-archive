## Ingredients

- fish
- salt
- spices
- onion
- lemon juice or vinegar
- flour
- parsley

## Directions

1. Cut the fish into pieces or slices, place in a pan with salt, spices, chopped onion, lemon juice or vinegar.
2. Simmer until cooked, stirring over gentle heat.
3. Remove the pieces of fish, roll them in flour and fry them with parsley.
