## Ingredients

- Beef sweetbreads
- Spanish sauce (see page 765)

## Directions

1. Prepare the same way as in the previous article
2. Use Spanish sauce in place of the German sauce
3. These sweetbreads prepared this way are commonly used as garnishes for other stews
