## Ingredients

- Bread loaf without butter, four fingers high
- Chicken breasts, cooked
- Milk
- Carrot slices
- Butter or lard
- Flour
- Salt

## Directions

1. Make a bread loaf that is not made with butter, four fingers high, which is kept for three days wrapped in a napkin.
2. After this time, cut it into strips four fingers wide and remove the crusts from top and bottom.
3. Hollow it out so that the crumb removed can serve as a lid for one of the boxes, arranging it conveniently.
4. As the boxes and lids are made, brown them in butter on all sides and let them drain.
5. Boil milk in a quantity proportional to the boxes to be filled, with carrot slices, which are removed from the milk as soon as they have imparted their flavor.
6. Put a pan on the fire with butter and add flour in proportional quantity, and before it browns, incorporate the milk and very finely chopped cooked chicken breasts that have been prepared beforehand, taking care that they remain well white.
