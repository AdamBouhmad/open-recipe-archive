## Ingredients

- artichokes
- salt
- water
- fresh cheese
- pepper

## Directions

1. Wash the artichokes well
2. Place in a pot with water and salt to cook
3. Once cooked, remove from water and drain
4. Remove the thick dry leaves
5. Mash a little fresh cheese
6. Grind a little pepper powder
7. Mix with the cheese and stuff the artichokes by opening the leaves then closing them again
