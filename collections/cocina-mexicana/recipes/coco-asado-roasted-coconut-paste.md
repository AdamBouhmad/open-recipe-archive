## Ingredients

- One and a half pounds of sugar
- Half a pound of ground coconut
- Eight egg yolks

## Directions

1. Make a small conserve or paste by mixing the clarified sugar syrup made with one and a half pounds of sugar with half a pound of ground coconut and eight egg yolks
2. When it reaches the point of cajeta (a certain consistency), empty it onto a plate
3. Brown it with a comal or tin sheet with heat from above
4. Dust with ground cinnamon
