## Ingredients

- small head onions
- water
- meat broth
- sugar
- bread soup

## Directions

1. Clean the small head onions carefully in the amount needed
2. Blanch them
3. Cook them immediately in broth
4. Add a little sugar
5. When sufficiently cooked, place on prepared bread soup
