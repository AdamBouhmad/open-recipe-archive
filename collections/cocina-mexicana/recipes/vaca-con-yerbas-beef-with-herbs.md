## Ingredients

- cooked beef
- lard with fine herbs chopped
- breadcrumbs
- fine herbs
- lard

## Directions

1. In a dish put lard with fine chopped herbs and breadcrumbs
2. Place cooked beef cut in small pieces as possible
3. Add fine herbs and small pieces of lard on top
4. Another layer of breadcrumbs
5. Expose to moderate heat covered with an iron lid
6. Place coals on top
7. When the piece is cooked, serve
8. Also add pickles or capers
