## Ingredients

- one glass of milk
- two ounces of sugar
- six egg yolks
- three egg whites
- one tablespoon of orange blossom water
- powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Boil a glass of milk with two ounces of sugar and skim the froth.
2. Beat together six egg yolks and three egg whites with one tablespoon of orange blossom water and a little powdered sugar.
3. When the milk stops boiling, pour it gently over the eggs in a cup or shallow plate, stirring continuously.
4. Place this plate or cup in a pan of boiling water, cover with a comal with heat.
5. When the eggs are set, remove them, let them cool, dust with sugar, and caramelize with a hot shovel.
