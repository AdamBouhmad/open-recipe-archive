## Ingredients

- slices of beef pulp
- lemon juice
- salt
- pepper
- butter
- onion, finely minced
- garlic, finely minced
- broth
- lemon juice
- pepper powder

## Directions

1. Arrange the slices of pulp as described for sirloin
2. Coat with lemon juice and sprinkle with salt and pepper
3. Place a casserole over the heat with butter and fry the slices in it with finely minced onion and garlic
4. Cover with broth and place in the oven or cook over two fires until the broth is consumed in both cases
5. Add a few drops of lemon juice and sprinkle with pepper powder on top
