## Ingredients

- sesos (brains)
- sal (salt)
- vinagre (vinegar)
- ajos (garlic)
- manteca (lard)
- cebollas (onions)
- vino blanco (white wine)
- caldo del puchero (pot broth)
- perejil (parsley)
- yerbabuena (mint)
- plátanos fritos (fried plantains)

## Directions

1. Clean the brains well with salt and vinegar
2. Place them in a casserole in which six or seven garlic cloves have been previously fried with lard and four whole onions until both are seen to be golden
3. Cook the brains and add half a glass of white wine and an equal amount of pot broth, a small bundle of parsley and some mint leaves
4. Let the herbs give their flavor to the brains by cooking a little
5. Season with salt and serve
6. Garnish with fried plantains cut into wheels
