## Ingredients

- six fresh eggs
- one ounce of fine chocolate powder
- four ounces of flour
- ten ounces of powdered sugar

## Directions

1. Beat and mix well in a mortar six fresh eggs, one ounce of fine chocolate powder, four ounces of flour, and ten ounces of powdered sugar
2. Once the batter is made, arrange it on papers or in molds to bake in the oven
