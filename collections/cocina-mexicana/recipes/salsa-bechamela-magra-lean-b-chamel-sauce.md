## Ingredients

- 12 quartillos of cream
- 4 tablespoons of wheat flour
- 2 or 3 shallots
- 1 carrot cut into strips
- Scraped nutmeg
- A good amount of whole pepper
- Salt
- 1 small bunch of mixed herbs
- Mushrooms or mushroom trimmings if available
- 2 glasses of water
- A very small piece of sugar
- A little butter to mix in at serving time
- A little cream if needed to thin it out

## Directions

1. Bring 12 quartillos of cream to a boil
2. Dissolve 4 tablespoons of common wheat flour into the cream
3. Place the pot over a high heat so the sauce does not take color, and stir constantly until it becomes thick enough for your intended use
4. Prepare separately: put 2 or 3 shallots, 1 carrot cut into strips, scraped nutmeg, a good amount of whole pepper, salt, a small mixed bunch of herbs, and mushrooms or their trimmings in another pot
5. Make this preparation boil with 2 glasses of water while the milk is reducing
6. When the milk has reduced to the proper point, incorporate the other preparation gradually and slowly, as adding it all at once would curdle it
7. Add a very small piece of sugar, because it is necessary that its taste not be perceived
8. Keep a little butter warm in a bain-marie to be mixed into the sauce at the time of serving
9. If the sauce becomes too thick, add a little cream
