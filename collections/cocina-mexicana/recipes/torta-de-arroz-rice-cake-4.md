## Ingredients

- half pound rice
- quarter liter cream
- piece of butter
- outer peel of lemon
- salt
- six egg yolks
- sugar
- four egg whites
- one or two spoonfuls orange blossom water
- butter for pan
- bread crumbs

## Directions

1. Put half pound of rice to cook and gradually add quarter liter of cream and a piece of butter, the outer peel of lemon and salt
2. When the rice is open and very thick, remove the lemon and let cool in another vessel
3. Add six beaten egg yolks with sugar and four beaten egg whites with one or two spoonfuls of orange blossom water
4. Grease a pan or mold of the desired shape with butter, dusting with bread crumbs
5. Pour in the rice and place on a stove with high heat on its cover
6. When the cake has taken sufficient color, turn it over onto a plate
7. In the same manner are made cakes of noodles, semolina, etc.
