## Ingredients

- oil
- vinegar (equal quantity to oil)
- onions (two whole)
- garlic (one head)
- peppercorns (four whole)

## Directions

1. Put in a pot with equal quantities of oil and vinegar, two whole onions, one head of garlic, four whole peppercorns.
2. Cover the pot with paper and put a cup of water on top.
3. Stir occasionally with the same pot.
