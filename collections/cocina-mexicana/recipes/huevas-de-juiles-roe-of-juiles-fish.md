## Ingredients

- juiles roe
- oil
- garlic
- parsley
- capers
- onion
- water
- salt
- pepper

## Directions

1. Fry the raw roe in oil with garlic
2. Once fried, set aside
3. Grind parsley and an equal amount of rinsed capers
4. Fry in oil, add sliced onion
5. Add a little water and once thickened, add the roe
6. When well seasoned, serve
7. Alternative: some eat it raw, mixed with oil, vinegar, pepper, salt and parsley
