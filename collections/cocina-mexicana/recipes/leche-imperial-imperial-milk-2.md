## Ingredients

- two quartillos of milk
- eighteen to twenty egg yolks
- half pound of ground almonds
- two tablespoons of starch
- sugar

## Directions

1. To two quartillos of milk add eighteen to twenty egg yolks, half pound of ground almonds and two tablespoons of starch
2. Sweeten to taste
3. Break up the starch and eggs in the milk, strain it
4. After straining, add the ground almonds dissolved in a little milk
5. Place over heat and cook to the consistency of burned milk
