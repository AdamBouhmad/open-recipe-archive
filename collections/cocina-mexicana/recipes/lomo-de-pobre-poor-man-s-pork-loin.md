## Ingredients

- pork loin
- lard
- ground spices
- cooked tomatoes
- water
- salt
- Málaga wine

## Directions

1. Fry in lard and mix with ground spices and a good portion of cooked tomatoes.
2. Add necessary water and salt.
3. When tender, let dry, adding a little Málaga wine.
