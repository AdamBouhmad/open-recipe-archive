## Ingredients

- chicken breasts
- salt
- chopped garlic
- chopped parsley
- saffron
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- ground cumin
- cooked egg yolks
- oil
- vinegar
- lard
- raisins
- ham or hard-boiled egg (filling)
- onion, chopped
- roasted and ground tomatoes
- capers
- chicken broth

## Directions

1. Prepare and season chicken breasts as in the previous article
2. Mix with chopped garlic, chopped parsley, saffron, clove, pepper, cinnamon, ground cumin, and cooked and dissolved egg yolks
3. Add a little oil and vinegar
4. Form the meatballs with raisins, ham, or hard-boiled egg inside
5. Fry in lard
6. Remove and prepare the broth by frying chopped onion in the same lard, adding roasted and ground tomatoes with raisins, the same spices used in the meat, capers, and chicken broth with a little oil
7. When the broth is boiling, add the meatballs to finish cooking, without allowing the broth to reduce much
