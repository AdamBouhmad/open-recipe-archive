## Ingredients

- flour
- milk
- eggs
- salt
- pepper
- fruits of all kinds in slices
- sugar dissolved in butter
- honey
- all sorts of spices

## Directions

1. Make as you would the Meal-Pudding but adding sliced fruits of all kinds
2. Serve with sugar dissolved in butter and honey, with all sorts of spices added
