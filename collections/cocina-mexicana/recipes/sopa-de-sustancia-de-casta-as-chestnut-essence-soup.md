## Ingredients

- Five to six dozen chestnuts
- Salt
- Four ounces of sugar
- Four ounces of bread crumb
- Broth
- Bread toasts
- Butter
- Fine salt

## Directions

1. Cook five to six dozen chestnuts with a little salt and four ounces of sugar
2. When well cooked, separate two dozen, and the rest remove the shells and clean well
3. Grind with a piece of bread crumb of about four ounces, well dissolved in broth
4. Strain through a cloth, return this broth to the heat, seasoning to taste with fine salt
5. Having boiled so it is not very thick, pour over bread toasts fried in butter
6. After the soup gives a short boil, remove from heat putting on top the twenty-four chestnuts separated and well cleaned
