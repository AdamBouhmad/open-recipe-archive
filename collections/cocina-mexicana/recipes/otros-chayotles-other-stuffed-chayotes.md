## Ingredients

- jitomate (tomato)
- cebolla (onion)
- ajo (garlic)
- agua (water)
- sal (salt)
- epazote (Mexican herb)
- chilpocle (smoked chile)
- chayotles

## Directions

1. Chop the tomato very finely, double the quantity of onion, and a few garlic cloves; fry all this
2. Once well fried, add sufficient water, salt, a sprig of epazote, and a fried chilpocle
3. Chop the chayotles into small cubes, add to the sauce and let season
