## Ingredients

- white mushrooms
- lemon juice
- water
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Turn or round a sufficient quantity of mushrooms
2. Place mushrooms in water mixed with lemon juice
3. Put in a pot over fire with a good piece of butter and the juice of one lemon
4. Allow to boil for five minutes
5. Store in an earthenware vessel for use in galantines as indicated in their respective places
