## Ingredients

- Lamb
- oil
- melted lard
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Let the piece of lamb from which the chops are to be cut rest
2. Remove the bone, skin, tendons and fat
3. Cut the slices, flatten them with a wet cleaver, and round them with the knife
4. Moisten them afterward in oil or melted lard, bread them and cook them on the grill
