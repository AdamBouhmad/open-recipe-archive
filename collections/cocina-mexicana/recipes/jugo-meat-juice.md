## Ingredients

- medium onions
- medium carrots
- meat and poultry scraps
- half a quartillo of water or broth
- clove of spice

## Directions

1. Place onions and carrots cut into rounds in a pot
2. Add meat and poultry scraps on top with half a quartillo of water or broth
3. Cook over high heat until the color becomes golden
4. Add boiling water and a clove of spice
5. Let it cook for one and a half hours, being careful to squeeze the meat well so the juices are released
6. Pass the juice through a sieve
7. Pour it into another vessel and return the meats to the pot, which will give it good flavor
