## Ingredients

- Whole ripe apricots
- Water
- Clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Open them as much as necessary to remove the stone
2. Prick them with a pin and place them in water over fire so they soften
3. Place them afterwards in cold water, let them drain and place them in clarified syrup that has been cooked beforehand and is boiling
4. After some more boilings, allow them to cool, drain them and place them in the compote dishes
