## Ingredients

- Calf mesentery (cuajar)
- ham
- chorizo
- parsley
- green onions
- garlic
- pork tripe, thin
- ham pieces
- chorizo pieces
- water
- vinegar
- red chile

## Directions

1. Divide the calf mesentery into pieces so that each one is filled with a small piece of ham, chorizo, parsley, green onions and garlic, all finely chopped
2. Tie the filling with the thinnest pork tripe so that it does not fall apart when cooking
3. Cook it in a pot with pieces of ham, chorizo, water, sufficient vinegar and red chile, keeping the pot covered and over low heat until the filling becomes tender
