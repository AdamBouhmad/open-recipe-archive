## Ingredients

- venison meat portions
- ham strips
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- cloves
- cinnamon sticks
- ancho chiles
- cilantro
- wine
- vinegar
- oregano
- lime
- water
- lard

## Directions

1. After degreasing and rinsing well, lard with ham strips rolled in salt and ground pepper, garlic, cloves and cinnamon strips
2. Place in a cazuela and adobo with toasted, deveined and ground ancho chiles combined with garlic, cloves, pepper, cinnamon, cumin, toasted cilantro, all ground and incorporated with wine, vinegar, salt, oregano and sliced lime
3. The next day, place in a pot, add necessary water and lard
4. Put over fire
5. When cooked, burn more lard in a cazuela and finish as described for the previous recipe
