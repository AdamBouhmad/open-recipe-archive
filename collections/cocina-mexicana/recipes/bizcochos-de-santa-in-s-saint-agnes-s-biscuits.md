## Ingredients

- three pounds of flour
- half a pound of ground and sifted sugar
- five eggs, two with whites and three without
- one pound of lard
- one cup of settled tequesquite water

## Directions

1. Beat everything so it incorporates
2. Mix in the flour and knead
3. Extend with a rolling pin
4. Cut the biscuits with a bay leaf mold
5. Bake in the oven
