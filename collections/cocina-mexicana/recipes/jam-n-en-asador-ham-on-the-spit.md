## Ingredients

- ham
- white wine
- onions
- carrots
- thyme
- bay leaf
- breadcrumbs

## Directions

1. Prepare and place in a marinade of good white wine with onions, sliced carrots, thyme and bay leaf
2. Keep the vessel hermetically sealed with a cloth and lid or another vessel of equal size
3. Place on the spit and while roasting, baste with the same marinade
4. When half cooked, remove the skin, dust with breadcrumbs and return to the fire to brown
5. Serve with the marinade, which should be strained and reduced to sauce consistency
