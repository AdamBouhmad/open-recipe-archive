## Ingredients

- Garlic cloves
- Lard
- Vinegar
- Wine
- Fine pepper
- Tabasco pepper
- Clove
- Cumin
- Bay leaves
- Dry oregano
- Oil

## Directions

1. Fry a few cloves of garlic in lard, set them aside and add twice as much vinegar and one part wine
2. Add fine pepper and Tabasco pepper, ground clove and cumin, all ground, bay leaves and dry oregano
3. Place the fish there in infusion for some time, and when cold, add oil and serve
