## Ingredients

- tomatillos
- butter
- poblano chiles
- degreased broth
- hard-boiled egg yolks
- salt

## Directions

1. Roast four or six tomatillos, remove the seeds and grind them
2. Place a pot on the fire with butter and fry them
3. Separately, fry three or four roasted and cleaned poblano chiles
4. When already cooked, remove the pot from the fire
5. Add degreased broth from the pot to the fried tomatillo, and add the chiles sliced without seeds and two hard-boiled egg yolks crumbled and also dissolved in broth
6. Season everything with the necessary salt
7. When it has thickened appropriately, remove from the fire
