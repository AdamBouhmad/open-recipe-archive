## Ingredients

- raw young pigeons
- lard
- chorizo
- ham strips
- salt
- pineapple
- garlic
- tomato
- clove
- cinnamon
- sugar
- white wine
- water

## Directions

1. Fry the raw pigeons in lard with chorizo, ham strips and salt
2. Add then pineapple, garlic, tomato, clove and cinnamon, all ground, a lump of sugar, white wine and a little water, and let them cook in this sauce, which should consume almost entirely
