## Ingredients

- eight quartillos of milk
- three pounds of white sugar
- one pocilio of soaked rice
- one pound of almonds
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. To eight quartillos of milk add three pounds of white sugar in powder and one pocilio of soaked rice
2. Grind this and strain in the same milk
3. Soak one pound of almonds, grind it and set aside half pound; roast and finely grind the rest
4. Strain with the same milk
5. Place a pan over heat with this milk and let it cook until it thickens
6. When done, pour half pound of almonds that was set aside into the dishes, cut into thin strips and add thickly to the dishes
7. Grind sugar and sprinkle the milk on top
