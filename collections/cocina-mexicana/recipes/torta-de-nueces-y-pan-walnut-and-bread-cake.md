## Ingredients

- walnuts, cleaned and ground
- sugar
- pepper
- clove
- cold grated bread
- salt
- citron pieces
- raisins
- almonds
- beaten eggs
- cinnamon, molded

## Directions

1. After cleaning the walnuts and grinding them, mix them with sugar, pepper, clove, cold grated bread, salt, citron pieces, raisins and almonds
2. Incorporate everything with beaten eggs and put in a pan or casserole greased with butter, leaving it to set over two fires, and dusting the cake on top with molded sugar and cinnamon
