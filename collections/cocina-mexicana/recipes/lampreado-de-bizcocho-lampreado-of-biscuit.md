## Ingredients

- biscuits or biscuit bread
- hard cheese
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- saffron
- eggs
- eggs
- syrup

## Directions

1. Grind biscuits or biscuit bread with hard cheese, and two parts of biscuit, add another of raisins, almonds, pine nuts and saffron; stirring everything, beat with eggs, so that the dough does not become too loose.
2. Make the small pastries and fry them, trying to make them well-filled; keep beaten eggs on hand to give them another layer, or as many as desired, and finishing frying, put the sauce on them as in the previous article.
