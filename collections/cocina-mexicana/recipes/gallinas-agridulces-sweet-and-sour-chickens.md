## Ingredients

- Water
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Pepper
- Cinnamon
- Clove
- Ham slices
- Chorizos
- Raw chickens

## Directions

1. Put water in a pot with a little vinegar, sugar, pepper, cinnamon and clove, all ground
2. Add ham slices, chorizos and raw chickens, covering the pot
3. Leave everything infusing from one day to the next
4. The following day cook the chickens and thicken the broth with toasted bread in lard
5. When serving sprinkle toasted sesame seed over the top
