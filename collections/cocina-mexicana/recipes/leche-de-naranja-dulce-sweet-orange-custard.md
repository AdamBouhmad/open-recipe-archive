## Ingredients

- leche (milk)
- naranja dulce (sweet orange)
- zumo de naranja (orange juice)

## Directions

1. Prepare as the cherimoya custard with egg
2. Take care not to add the orange juice until the mixture reaches the proper point, to prevent it from curdling
