## Ingredients

- one pound of fat raw ham
- half pound of almond
- sugar syrup
- two egg yolks
- ground biscuit
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Grind well one pound of fat raw ham on the metate
2. Also grind half pound of almond and add both to sugar syrup at high point
3. After boiling a little, add two egg yolks and a little ground biscuit
4. At a point lower than that of cajeta, but that comes off from the pan, remove from heat
5. Color it with cinnamon, form the pastries and fill them
