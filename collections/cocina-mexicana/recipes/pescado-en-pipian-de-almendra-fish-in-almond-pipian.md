## Ingredients

- almonds
- bread
- lard
- red chiles
- melon seeds
- fish broth
- salt
- clove
- pepper
- onion

## Directions

1. Clean the almonds, toast and grind them with a piece of bread fried in lard and red chiles toasted in it.
2. Add some melon seeds, and after grinding everything well, strain it, loosening with broth in which the fish was cooked.
3. Season with necessary salt, fry everything again, adding a little clove and pepper, and add the cooked fish, garnishing with sliced raw onion.
