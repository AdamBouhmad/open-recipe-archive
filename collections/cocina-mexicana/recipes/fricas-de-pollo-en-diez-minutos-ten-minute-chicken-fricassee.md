## Ingredients

- chicken
- butter
- mushrooms
- parsley
- shallots
- salt
- ground spices
- white wine

## Directions

1. Cut the chicken into pieces and fry the portions in butter, in which they will be cooked very briefly.
2. Remove them and add to the remaining butter in the pot: chopped mushrooms, parsley, and shallots, salt and ground spices.
3. Fry all of this for an instant and add a glass of white wine, letting the sauce thicken over high heat.
4. Add the chicken portions so they heat through, without letting the sauce boil, and arrange on a plate to serve.
