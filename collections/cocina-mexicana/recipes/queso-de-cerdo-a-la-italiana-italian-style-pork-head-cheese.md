## Ingredients

- one pig's liver
- fat (two-thirds of the liver's weight)
- jowl (one-third of the liver's weight)
- pepper
- salt
- spices
- thyme
- sage
- laurel
- basil
- parsley
- culantro
- anise
- nutmeg
- caul fat

## Directions

1. Mash a pig's liver with two-thirds of its weight of fat and one-third of jowl; mix well and season with pepper, salt, spices, thyme, sage, laurel, basil, chopped parsley, culantro and crushed anise, and scraped nutmeg.
2. Cover the sides and bottom of a tin mold with caul fat; place the chopped mixture on top and cover everything with slices of fat, putting it to cook in the oven.
3. When cooked, let it cool in the mold and remove it by soaking it in boiling water.
