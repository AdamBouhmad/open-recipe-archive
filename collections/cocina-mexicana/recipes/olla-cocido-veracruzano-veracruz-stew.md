## Ingredients

- 2 jarros cold water
- 2 pounds veal
- 2 pounds beef
- piece of Veracruz bacon
- additional water
- half cabbage
- lettuce
- 2 or 3 turnips
- equal quantity of carrots
- handful of soaked chickpeas
- turkey giblets
- half chicken
- some roasted lamb bones
- parsley
- 3 cloves
- roasted onion

## Directions

1. Take a pot containing about 2 jarros of cold water.
2. Place in it 2 pounds of veal, 2 pounds of beef and a piece of Veracruz bacon.
3. Place over low heat, adding 2 more jarros of water once the first ones have lost their coldness.
4. Increase the heat to produce foam; remove it as it rises until no more appears.
5. After boiling for about eight hours over equal heat, when it has been cooking for four hours, add half cabbage, a lettuce, two or three turnips, equal quantity of carrots, a handful of soaked chickpeas, giblets of a turkey, half a chicken and some roasted lamb bones, a little parsley, three cloves and a roasted onion, and add hot water if needed, until the eight hours mentioned are complete.
