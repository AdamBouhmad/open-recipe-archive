## Ingredients

- small thin tortillas
- aged or fresh cheese

## Directions

1. Crumble aged or fresh cheese into small thin tortillas
2. Fold them and pass them through the edges with fine and clean threads or some string so the cheese does not come out
3. Place them over the fire until the cheese melts
4. When melted, eat them hot, because cold they lose all their flavor
