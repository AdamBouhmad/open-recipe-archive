## Ingredients

- Pomegranates
- Three quarters (cuartillos) of pomegranate juice
- Twelve ounces of sugar

## Directions

1. Squeeze the pomegranates through a cloth
2. Measure the resulting juice
3. Mix three quarters of juice with twelve ounces of sugar
4. Put it to cook
5. The jelly is at its point when a little dropped on a plate can be lifted without it sticking
