## Ingredients

- leg of mutton
- ham
- broth or common substance
- salt
- pepper
- fresh eggs
- fried toast

## Directions

1. Roast a leg of mutton on the spit and let it cool
2. Take the best meats from it and mince them finely
3. Let a slice of ham sweat and stick in the pot
4. Moisten with strained broth or common substance
5. Remove the ham immediately and add the minced meat
6. Let it heat without boiling, season to taste
7. Dress on a plate and adorn with fresh eggs, interspersed with fried toast
