## Ingredients

- beef loin
- ham
- garlic
- spices
- salt
- water
- vinegar
- olives
- capers
- oil
- chiles in vinegar
- ground pepper
- onion

## Directions

1. Wash the beef loin well
2. Slice it and stud with ham, garlic and all spices
3. Soak in salted water for one day
4. The next day soak in vinegar
5. On the third day have all this together with the meat
6. Press the meat
7. Once pressed, cook in a covered pot
8. When the meat is cooked, slice it
9. Place on a platter
10. Add vinegar, olives, capers, oil, chiles in vinegar, ground pepper, and sliced onion
11. Serve
