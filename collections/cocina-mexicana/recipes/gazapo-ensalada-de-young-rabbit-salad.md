## Ingredients

- Cooked rabbit
- Hard boiled eggs
- Lettuce hearts
- Capers
- Anchovies
- Cucumbers

## Directions

1. Prepare the rabbit cooked as described in the mayonnaise recipe
2. Surround with hard boiled eggs and lettuce hearts
3. Garnish the salad with the same ingredients mentioned for the mayonnaise, except for the gelatin
