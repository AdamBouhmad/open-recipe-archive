## Ingredients

- Meat
- Clove
- Pepper
- Cumin
- Garlic
- Vinegar
- Raisins, almonds, and pine nuts (optional)

## Directions

1. Chop the meat and parboil it
2. Grind it with clove, pepper, cumin, and garlic, and knead it with vinegar
3. If desired, add raisins, almonds, and pine nuts
4. Wrap the minced meat in paper cartridges or fill intestines like sausages
5. Fry them alone or with egg
6. Serve with any desired sauce
