## Ingredients

- one pound of almonds
- one and a half pounds of sugar
- marzipan
- mint
- fresh cheese
- egg yolks

## Directions

1. Grind one pound of almonds and one and a half pounds of sugar
2. Mix this with marzipan, mint, fresh cheese and egg yolks
3. After everything is well crushed, form the pastries in the manner of quesadillas
4. Bake on a board over wafer at a gentle oven heat
5. Gloss them by spreading with a feather the following icing
6. Beat some egg whites with rose water, mixing very well sifted and ground white sugar and a little cinnamon
