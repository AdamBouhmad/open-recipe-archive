## Ingredients

- Catfish portions, fried
- Onion
- Green chile
- Garlic
- Very ripe tomato
- Water
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- Clove
- Cinnamon
- Pepper

## Directions

1. After frying the catfish portions, chop onion, green chile, garlic, and very ripe tomato very fine
2. Fry it and add water, a splash of vinegar, a lump of sugar, a little clove, cinnamon, and pepper powder
3. Cook until the sauce is very thick
