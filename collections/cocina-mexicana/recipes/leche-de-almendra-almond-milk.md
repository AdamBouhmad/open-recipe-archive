## Ingredients

- Milk
- Ground and sieved rice
- Ground almonds
- Egg yolks
- Sugar to taste
- A little orange blossom water

## Directions

1. Mix milk with ground and sieved rice, ground almonds, egg yolks, and sugar to taste
2. Heat until it reaches the proper consistency
3. Before it reaches the final consistency, add a little orange blossom water
