## Ingredients

- pork head, cleaned
- fine salt
- coarse pepper
- thyme
- bay leaf
- basil
- six cloves
- cilantro
- half nutmeg
- two heads of garlic
- four shallots
- handful of parsley leaves
- vinegar
- ham slices
- parsley leaves
- white wine
- onion
- turnips
- parsnips
- carrot
- tomillo
- albahaca
- head of garlic

## Directions

1. Bone the pork head carefully
2. Remove all meat and fat without cutting the skin
3. Cut the meat and fat into very thin strips, keeping them separate
4. Cut the ears into strips as well
5. Season everything on both sides with fine salt, coarse pepper, thyme, bay leaf, basil, six cloves, a little cilantro, half nutmeg all ground, and finely chopped remaining items; two chopped heads of garlic and four chopped shallots and less than a handful of whole parsley leaves
6. Place the head skin in a round casserole
7. Arrange over it the strips of meat mixed with fat forming a layer with vinegar, some ham slices and parsley leaves arranged carefully
8. Continue this way until full and sew the skin, which will be folded like a bag, wrapping it in a clean cloth that is tied tightly with twine
9. Place this brawn in a pot exactly its size
10. Let it cook for six or seven hours with broth, four quartillos of white wine, onion, turnips, parsnips, carrot, thyme, bay leaf, basil, a head of garlic, salt and pepper
11. When cooked, strain and place in a round vessel of its size, covering with a lid with a heavy weight on top to give it the desired shape until it is cold
12. Serve as an intermediate course
