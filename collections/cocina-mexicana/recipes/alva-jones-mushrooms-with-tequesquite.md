## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- hot water
- tequesquite
- warm water
- butter
- salt

## Directions

1. Clean mushrooms and cook with hot water and a little tequesquite, without wetting them in any way with cold water; once cooked, pour warm water and drain three times until the water is very clean.
2. Leave them with little water, add a spoonful of butter with a little salt and season until they become thick.
