## Ingredients

- potatoes
- salt
- bread crumb soaked
- pepper
- minced parsley
- butter
- eggs
- lard

## Directions

1. After peeling the potatoes, cook them with salt; in a stew grind them with a piece of soaked bread crumb
2. Place this paste in a pan with a powder of pepper, very minced parsley, a little butter and beat eggs into it with both white and yolk so that balls can be formed, which are fried in lard
3. These are served as is or with the following sauce: cook some eggs and beat the yolks with a small piece of soaked bread, fry minced onion and garlic, add the cooked yolks and broth from the pot
