## Ingredients

- milk
- sugar
- cinnamon sticks
- egg yolks (three for each quartillo of milk)
- butter
- beaten eggs with sifted sugar
- cold mamón
- raisins
- pine nuts
- almond pieces
- walnut pieces
- cinnamon powder

## Directions

1. Boil milk with sugar and cinnamon sticks, then strain and mix with egg yolks in proportion of three yolks per quartillo
2. Return to fire, stirring until it becomes as thick as cold atole, then remove
3. In a well-buttered serving dish, make a layer of eggs beaten with sifted sugar until hardened
4. On top place a layer of cold mamón, then one of raisins, pine nuts, almond pieces and walnut pieces, then one of cooked milk
5. Alternate in this order until the dish is filled, but place beaten eggs only around the edges so they bind with the first and last layers
6. The last layer should also be beaten egg so that after cooking the dessert appears coated and fried
7. Cover with this beaten egg and place the dish over two gentle fires to set
8. When set, sprinkle sifted sugar and cinnamon powder on top
