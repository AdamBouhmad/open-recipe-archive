## Ingredients

- half pound butter
- six eggs
- six large spoonfuls cheese à la crème
- nutmeg
- salt
- sugar
- breadcrumbs
- cream, if needed

## Directions

1. Mix together at once the butter, eggs, cheese à la crème, nutmeg, salt, sugar, breadcrumbs and cream if needed to soften the dough
2. Flour the hands and form the dough into well-rounded balls
3. Cook in salted water
4. Drain well
5. Brown in hot butter
6. Serve
