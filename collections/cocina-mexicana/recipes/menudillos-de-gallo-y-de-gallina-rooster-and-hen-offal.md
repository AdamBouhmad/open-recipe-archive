## Ingredients

- rooster crests (in sufficient quantity)
- warm water
- hot water
- salt
- white broth
- rooster kidneys
- rooster livers
- cold water

## Directions

1. Place crests in warm water to bleed; when bled, put in very hot water (but not boiling, hot enough to keep your finger in it).
2. Stir, and when the skin loosens, remove from water and rub with a handful of salt in a cloth.
3. Rub well until the skins are completely loosened, then soak again in cold water.
4. After one hour, cook and braise in white broth.
5. For kidneys: after bleeding, simply boil in white broth for half an hour.
6. For livers: bleed in cold water, then blanch in water that is nearly but not quite boiling so they do not harden.
7. When they acquire some consistency, refresh in cold water, prepare and drain.
8. These prepared offals serve to garnish soups or other dishes.
