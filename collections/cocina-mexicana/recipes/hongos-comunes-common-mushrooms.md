## Ingredients

- mushrooms
- water
- garlic heads
- salt

## Directions

1. After cleaning and washing the mushrooms well, put them to cook in water with crushed garlic heads and salt
2. After the mushrooms are very well cooked, they can be stewed in mole, adding pork ribs, or likewise in pipián, in ajocomino or in white sauce, frying sliced onion, then the mushrooms, and when fried, add one or two large chiles that boil with them
