## Ingredients

- pork feet
- salt
- sweetbreads
- sheep brains
- garlic
- tomatoes
- onions
- butter
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- vinegar
- egg whites
- egg yolks
- flour
- eggs

## Directions

1. After cleaning well, cook the pork feet with salt
2. Cook sweetbreads and sheep brains separately, and remove the skins
3. Chop garlic finely, tomatoes and onions, fry them in butter and set aside from heat
4. Debone the pork feet, chop coarsely, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and fry
5. Add the fried tomato, season with a little clove, pepper and ground cinnamon, and moisten with a little of the broth in which the feet were cooked, adding a little vinegar
6. When everything is seasoned and the broth has dried, remove from heat, beat egg whites, and when thickened well, stir the yolks with a little flour
7. Pour the chopped pork feet mixture into the egg and stir
8. Put everything in a buttered pie dish in the following order: first a layer of sliced brains, and on top a little of the egg forming another very thin layer; then another of sliced sweetbreads, and over it another thick layer of the chopped pork feet; alternate the layers this way, covering at last with one or two beaten eggs, and cook the pie at two gentle fires until set
9. Serve with a sauce made from chopped tomato and sliced garlic
