## Ingredients

- Robust, black suckling pig
- Sage
- Thyme
- Wormwood
- Fresh cilantro
- Pepper sauce

## Directions

1. Choose a robust suckling pig of black color.
2. Prepare it the same as the boar, and marinate it in the same way; but adding only small sage, thyme, wormwood, and fresh cilantro to the marinade, letting it sit for five or six days.
3. After this time, place it to cook on the roasting spit and serve with a good pepper sauce.
