## Ingredients

- raw offal
- onion, several bunches, sliced
- tomato
- parsley
- ham, sliced
- lard
- water
- clove, ground
- cinnamon
- pepper
- Jerez wine, a little
- chiles
- capers
- olives

## Directions

1. Chop the raw offal
2. Slice several bunches of onion, a tomato and parsley; take some slices of ham, and put everything together in a dish with plenty of lard
3. Once fried, add water, ground clove, cinnamon, pepper and a little Jerez wine
4. Garnish with chiles, capers and olives
