## Ingredients

- One young goose with white fat
- The goose's liver, chopped
- Butter
- One onion in small pieces
- Sufficient quantity of chestnuts prepared as for chestnut purée
- Salt
- Pepper

## Directions

1. Prepare as stated.
2. Sauté the chopped liver in butter with an onion in small pieces, add sufficient quantity of chestnuts prepared as for chestnut purée.
3. Season this mixture to good taste and fill the body of the goose with it, fold the rump inside and sew it along the belly and by the vent.
4. Place on the spit five and three-quarter hours before serving.
5. Observe the same degree of roasting as if it were not stuffed.
