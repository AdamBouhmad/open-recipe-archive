## Ingredients

- white lettuces
- salt
- lard
- ground garlic
- chile filling
- small herbs

## Directions

1. Choose white lettuces and cut the stem near where the small leaves begin, removing the larger ones on top.
2. Put them in the sun to lose freshness and wilt, then wash and put to cook in water with salt, lard and ground garlic.
3. When cooked, remove from water and drain with the stems pointing up, carefully turning some leaves so they don't detach from the stem.
4. In the hollow formed by the turned leaves, fill with the same minced chile filling, and interweave the leaves so they form a ball until reaching the smallest leaves that cannot be turned.
5. Serve in the same sauce as the filled chiles with the same garnishes.
