## Ingredients

- kid goat
- ground meat
- salt
- lard
- ground garlic
- liver of the kid goat
- roasted almonds
- clove
- pepper
- cinnamon
- ginger
- wine
- cooked egg slices
- parsley

## Directions

1. Fill the kid goat with the ground meat mixture seasoned with necessary salt
2. Sew closed the opening of the belly
3. Place in a casserole with lard mixed with ground garlic and salt
4. Cook over two fires or in the oven, which should not be too hot
