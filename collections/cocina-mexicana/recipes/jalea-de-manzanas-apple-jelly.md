## Ingredients

- best quality apples
- water
- one pound of sugar per pound of cooked apples
- candied lemon peels

## Directions

1. Choose the best apples and peel and cut them
2. Remove the cores
3. Place in a vessel on the fire with sufficient water until very thick
4. Pour through clean cloth and let drain
5. Put one pound of sugar per pound of cooked apples in a vessel
6. Pour into jars and cover with candied lemon peels
7. For now cover with simple paper moistened in brandy, and two days later with white paper
8. Store in a cool place, but without humidity
