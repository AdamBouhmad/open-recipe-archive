## Ingredients

- Large, green beans
- Lard
- Garlic
- Onions
- Tomatoes
- Tomatoes (xitomates)
- Parsley, chopped
- Broth
- Egg yolks, cooked
- Fried bread toasts
- Saffron
- Cumin
- Cilantro, toasted
- Clove
- Pepper
- Oil
- Vinegar

## Directions

1. After the large green beans are cooked, remove their skin.
2. Fry in lard the finely chopped garlic, onions, tomatoes, and xitomates.
3. When everything is well fried, add the beans so they fry partially with chopped parsley.
4. Add broth, which is seasoned and thickened with cooked egg yolks, fried bread toasts, saffron, cumin, toasted cilantro, clove, and pepper, all ground, along with oil and vinegar.
5. Allow to simmer until fully seasoned, but they should not be too thick.
