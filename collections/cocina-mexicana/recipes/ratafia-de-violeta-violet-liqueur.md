## Ingredients

- half ounce of Florentine iris root
- four quartillos of spirit of wine
- two ounces of litmus in pan
- equal quantity of well-clarified syrup

## Directions

1. Put in infusion for twelve hours half an ounce of Florentine iris root in four quartillos of spirit of wine
2. Then add two ounces of litmus in pan and leave the liquor at rest until it has acquired a beautiful purple color
3. Strain
4. Mix with equal quantity of well-clarified syrup and filter
