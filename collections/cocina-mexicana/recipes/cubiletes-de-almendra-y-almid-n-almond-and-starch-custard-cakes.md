## Ingredients

- Fourteen egg yolks
- Seven egg whites
- Half pound of clean soaked almonds, not too finely ground
- Four ounces of sifted starch
- One fistful of sifted sugar
- Syrup
- Cinnamon
- Candy dragées, pine nuts, almonds, walnuts, raisins, or hazelnuts

## Directions

1. Beat fourteen egg yolks and seven egg whites separately until stiffened
2. Mix together and combine with half pound of clean soaked almonds, four ounces of sifted starch, and one fistful of sifted sugar
3. Test the dough by placing a small amount in the oven; if it does not puff but collapses, add a little more starch to the mass
4. When it reaches the proper point, pour into molds and place in a not very hot oven, at the temperature needed to bake mamones
5. Test the oven temperature by placing a little bran in it; if it browns immediately, the temperature is right
6. When the custard cakes are cooked, pierce them with a straw so the syrup penetrates
7. Make syrup in advance at half point and boil with cinnamon, then place the cakes in it and let them absorb the syrup on low heat until all the syrup is consumed
8. Arrange on a plate and decorate with dragées and pine nuts, almonds, walnuts, raisins or hazelnuts
