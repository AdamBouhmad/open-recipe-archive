## Ingredients

- verjuice, nearly ripe
- some pears or peeled apples

## Directions

1. Select verjuice nearly ripe, remove the seeds, and place on the fire to burst, stirring with a spatula
2. Add some peeled pears or apples divided into pieces
3. When everything is well dissolved, pass through a fine bristle sieve so that only the skins and pits remain
4. Place this pulp in a pan on the fire to dry as much as necessary to acquire consistency, then weigh it
5. Make a syrup with an equal amount of sugar as the weight of the fruit
6. When the syrup reaches the high blown point, add the pulp and let it boil until it reaches point, stirring constantly with a spatula that must not be removed at all
7. When the paste detaches perfectly from the bottom of the pan or the pot, it is a sign that it is done and it is then poured into tin molds or paper boxes, which are dried in the stove, dusting them with fine sugar
