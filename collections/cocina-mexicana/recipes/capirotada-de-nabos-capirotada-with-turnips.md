## Ingredients

- bread toasts
- turnips
- water
- salt
- garlic
- chickpeas
- cooked ham
- breadcrumbs
- pepper
- burnt butter

## Directions

1. Cook turnips in salted water with garlic.
2. Make the sauce with broth poured over a mixture of ground garlic with cooked and ground chickpeas.
3. Layer in this order: bread toasts, turnips, slices of cooked ham, and cooked whole chickpeas.
4. Do not use cheese.
5. When sufficiently thickened, cover with breadcrumbs, add salt, pepper and burnt butter on top.
6. Place a hot griddle on top to brown it.
