## Ingredients

- a plate of raw tomatoes
- a plate of toasted cacao shell
- a toasted tortilla
- four or six cloves
- a stick of cinnamon
- thirty-two ancho chiles
- lard
- turkey or meat broth
- meat
- salt

## Directions

1. Grind separately a plate of raw tomatoes, another of toasted cacao shell, a toasted tortilla, four or six cloves and a stick of cinnamon
2. Fry first the ground tomato and then place the other things so they also fry
3. Then add thirty-two ancho chiles, toasted in lard and ground, with the broth of the turkey or meat to be cooked in this mole and the same meat divided into regular pieces
4. Leave on the fire to season and thicken the broth, adding the necessary salt
