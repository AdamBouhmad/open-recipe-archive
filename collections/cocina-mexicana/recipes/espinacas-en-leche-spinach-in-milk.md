## Ingredients

- spinach
- water
- lard
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg powder
- flour
- milk
- fresh butter
- fried bread crusts

## Directions

1. Peel and wash, cook in boiling water and place in cold water, chop
2. Put in the saucepan with lard, salt, pepper and powdered nutmeg
3. Put on the stove on good heat
4. Dust with a little flour and moisten with milk
5. Finish reducing with a good piece of fresh butter
6. Serve surrounded by small fried bread crusts and hot
