## Ingredients

- Lamb head
- Liver
- Lungs
- Feet
- Heart
- Hot water
- Salt
- Egg yolks
- Lard
- Lemon juice

## Directions

1. These include the head, liver, lungs, feet, and heart; remove the jaws and snout from the head, cut the offal into slices, drain the blood, and wash well with hot water.
2. Then place in a casserole and cook with salt.
3. A moment before serving, drain them, and except for the head, add to the rest a spicy sauce or whatever is preferred, with two beaten egg yolks, a little lard, and lemon juice.
4. Place this stew in a large platter.
