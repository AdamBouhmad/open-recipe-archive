## Ingredients

- two pounds of sugar
- one pound of walnuts
- ten egg yolks
- cinnamon

## Directions

1. Make clarified syrup at half-point with two pounds of sugar
2. Away from heat, mix in one pound of clean, ground walnuts
3. Return to fire, and before it reaches its point remove again from heat and add ten egg yolks, which are stirred so they incorporate well
4. Raise to fire again and allow it to reach the point of cajeta, browning afterwards when emptied into the platter with fire from above, and decorating after cooling with ground cinnamon
