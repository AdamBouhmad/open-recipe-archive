## Ingredients

- menudo (tripe), from lamb or beef
- salt
- butter
- onions, finely chopped
- green chiles, finely chopped
- garlic, roasted and ground
- tomatoes, roasted and ground
- bread slices, fried and browned
- saffron
- cumin
- cilantro, toasted
- clove
- pepper, ground
- grated cheese
- bread crumbs
- parsley

## Directions

1. Clean the menudo very well and cook it perfectly in salted water
2. Fry onions and green chiles finely chopped in butter, and add roasted and ground garlic and tomatoes
3. Add the menudo cut into small pieces
4. Add saffron, cumin, toasted cilantro, clove and ground pepper
5. Add some of the broth in which the menudo was cooked
6. Let it boil and when somewhat dry, remove from heat
7. In another pot greased with butter, make a layer of fried and browned bread slices
8. Add a layer of menudo pieces prepared as described, sprinkling with grated cheese and a little pepper
9. Alternate the layers until ending with a bread layer
10. Add the remaining menudo broth or the quantity needed for the soup
11. When cooked, remove from heat
12. Cover with bread crumbs
13. Bathe with burned butter
14. Cover with a comal with heat so it browns
15. Serve with parsley leaves sprinkled on top
