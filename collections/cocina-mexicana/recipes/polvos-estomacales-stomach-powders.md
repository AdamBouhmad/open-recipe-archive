## Ingredients

- eight grains quina powder
- three grains rhubarb powder
- one grain Lemery's ethiops
- one grain ammoniac salt

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Take this dose before eating or in the first spoonful of soup in case of stomach weakness
