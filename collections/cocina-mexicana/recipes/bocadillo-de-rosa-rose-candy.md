## Ingredients

- Two pounds of well-clarified syrup
- One pound of fresh walnut, ground
- A little orange-blossom water
- Cinnamon, if desired

## Directions

1. To the clarified syrup, add one pound of fresh ground walnut
2. Bring to the setting point in water
3. Add a little orange-blossom water, remove from fire, beat and place the candies on paper with cinnamon on top if desired
