## Ingredients

- cooked chickens
- butter
- garlic cloves
- green chiles
- beet
- tomato
- carrot
- chicken broth
- fine ground spices
- oil
- vinegar
- ham slices
- salt
- a little sugar

## Directions

1. After cooking the chickens, fry garlic cloves in butter, remove them, and place the chicken quarters in the butter.
2. Fry the chicken, then add ground green chiles, beet, and tomato.
3. Dice and add a carrot.
4. Add chicken broth with fine ground spices, a little oil and vinegar, ham slices, and salt.
5. When seasoned, add a little sugar and let thicken reasonably, then remove from heat and serve.
