## Ingredients

- calf rennet
- lard or grated ham
- carrots
- onions
- bay leaf
- thyme
- parsley
- garlic
- cloves
- chili
- salt
- peppercorns
- concentrated broth
- white wine
- onions
- oil
- bread crusts

## Directions

1. After scraping, scraping, washing in many waters and parboiling the thickest parts of the rennet, put them to cook with lard or grated ham, carrots, onions, bay leaf, thyme, parsley, garlic, cloves, chili, salt and peppercorns, moistening everything with a few spoonfuls of concentrated broth without degreasing, and a bottle of white wine, and let everything boil gently for seven to eight consecutive hours
2. Then put the sauce to cool without removing the rennet; and then cut some onions into strips, which are fried in oil with minced garlic and parsley; then add the rennet with its sauce and immediately dress it with bread crusts, moistened in oil, dusted with salt and pepper, and toasted on the grill
