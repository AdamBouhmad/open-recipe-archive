## Ingredients

- Four to six beets
- One large ripe tomato
- Pepper grains
- Cloves
- Two cloves of garlic
- Two ancho chiles deveined and well soaked
- Lard
- Chicken broth
- Salt
- Chorizos
- Chiles in vinegar
- Olives
- Raisins
- Almonds
- Capers
- Beet slices
- Fruit slices such as pineapple, sweet potato, apple or pear

## Directions

1. Cook four to six beets, and when peeled, grind half of them with one large ripe tomato, some pepper grains, cloves, two cloves of garlic and two deveined and well-soaked ancho chiles
2. Fry all this ground in lard
3. Add the chicken broth with sufficient salt, the chicken and some cooked chorizos, chiles in vinegar, olives, raisins, almonds, capers, beet slices and slices of fruits such as pineapple, sweet potato, apple or pear
