## Ingredients

- Fava beans
- Butter
- Flour
- Broth from the pot
- Salt
- Pepper
- Savory, chopped
- Egg yolks
- Sugar

## Directions

1. Remove the pods and cook in boiling water so they shed their skins
2. Drain and give them several turns of butter in a saucepan, adding a little flour
3. Moisten with broth from the pot, and add salt, pepper and chopped savory
4. After a few moments, bind with egg yolks and a little sugar, stirring vigorously
5. Serve
