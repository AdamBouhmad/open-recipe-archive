## Ingredients

- catfish, prepared and cleaned
- oil
- lemons, sliced
- garlic cloves, peeled
- thyme
- marjoram
- bay leaves
- pepper
- cloves
- ginger, ground
- salt
- vinegar
- water

## Directions

1. Prepare and clean the catfish as described in articles about this fish
2. Wash it, divide it into pieces about four inches long or slightly more
3. Fry in oil and let cool
4. In a suitable vessel, prepare the escabeche by calculating sufficient quantity to fill the barrel after the fish is placed: fry sliced lemons in it until they release all their juice, adding peeled garlic cloves strung on a stick, thyme, marjoram and bay leaves, ground pepper, cloves and ginger, with sufficient salt in proportion to the quantity of fish
5. Add vinegar diluted with water according to how strong it is, letting it boil until it incorporates well with the escabeche
6. Set aside and let cool completely
7. When cooled, place the fish in the barrels, which will be covered with the second lid, tightening the hoops
8. Through the hole in the lid, add enough of the prepared oil to fill it exactly
9. If the fish is kept in pots or other vessels, ensure the escabeche covers it completely
