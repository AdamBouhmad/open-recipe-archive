## Ingredients

- String beans
- Carrots
- Fresh fava beans
- Peas
- Chorizo
- Chayotes
- Potatoes
- Squash
- Lard
- Xitomate (tomato)
- Onion
- Garlic
- Chopped parsley
- Beaten eggs
- Salt

## Directions

1. Cook in salted water with chorizo: string beans, sliced carrots, fresh fava beans and peas
2. Drain them, and when dry mix with the meat of the cooked chorizo, with chayotes and potatoes cooked separately and chopped very fine, and small squash stewed with lard, xitomate, onion, garlic and chopped parsley, these last ingredients composing one third of all the other ingredients
3. Mix everything with sufficient beaten eggs and proceed as for the other cakes
