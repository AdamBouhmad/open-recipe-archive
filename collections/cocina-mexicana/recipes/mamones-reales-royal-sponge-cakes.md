## Ingredients

- mamones cut in half
- almond paste or coconut paste
- egg yolks not beaten
- syrup at half point
- wine
- raisins
- almonds
- pine nuts
- toasted sesame seeds
- dragée and cinnamon

## Directions

1. Cut the mamones in half, place almond paste or coconut paste in the middle, coat with egg yolks that are burst and not beaten, and place them in syrup at half point, boiling vigorously. When cooked, remove them and arrange on a plate, where they are sprinkled with wine, studded with raisins, almonds and pine nuts, sprinkled with toasted sesame seeds and dusted with dragée and cinnamon on top.
